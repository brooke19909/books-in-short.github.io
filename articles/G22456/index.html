Rest in Karlovy Vary with treatment: reviews and photos

The German poet Johann Wolfgang Goethe, the brilliant composer Ludwig Van Beethoven, the mysterious Italian Giacomo Casanova, the Russian Tsar Peter the Great, Ivan Sergeyevich Turgenev, Yuri Gagarin, Leonid Brezhnev - all these famous people could meet in one place that each of them once visited. them for treatment and relaxation - in Karlovy Vary.

How did the sources come about?

The city is located among the hills in a unique beauty valley, at the confluence of two rivers, Teply and Ogra. According to legends, in the 14th century, the king of Bohemia, Charles IV (aka the emperor of the Roman Empire) discovered a healing spring while hunting a deer. The wounded animal plunged into a small body of water and rushed to run with new strength, hiding from its pursuers. The ruler was astounded and gave the order to found a city where, with the help of wonderful water, he and the nobles regained health and regained strength. The city was named after him, Carlsbad (later renamed Karlovy Vary), and the symbol was a deer or chamois.

To whom and why it is useful to visit

Today, rest in Karlovy Vary for recovery is chosen by people with digestive tract problems and excess weight, diseases of the spine, joints, to restore the nervous system. Modern methods of treating with water from thermal springs are based on many years of experience, works and research of balneologists. Mineral water is used internally for therapeutic purposes; it is used to make water massage and take baths.

Sources of the city are arranged in small groups:

  • Three - in the Garden Colonnade: Serpent Spring, Park, Freedom.
  • Six - in the Mill Colonnade: Rock, Libushe, Prince Wenceslas I, Prince Wenceslas II, Mill Spring (radon), Mermaid.
  • Two - in the Market Colonnade: Source, Source of Charles IV.
  • Two - in the Castle Colonnade: Lower and Upper Castle.
  • One is in the Geyser Colonnade (Vrzhidlo).

The chemical composition is similar in all sources. For those who remember the periodic table, it is carbon dioxide-sulphate-sodium water with calcium, lithium iron, bromine, potassium. The water in the springs differs in temperature (from 30 ¬∞ C to 72 ¬∞ C) and carbon monoxide content (0.3 - 0.7 g / l), which has a different effect on the body and is taken into account when prescribing treatment.

Sources are not a panacea, moreover, under certain conditions, treatment is prohibited. Contraindications are:

  • the presence of the disease in an acute form,
  • infections
  • alcoholism and drug addiction,
  • malignant tumors
  • acute heart failure.

But even in such cases it is not forbidden to come to rest in Karlovy Vary and enjoy the atmosphere of this city. For people who prefer outdoor activities - golf, tennis, horse riding, cycling. These classes will help to relax and improve the body, because the air is also saturated with healing minerals.

What to do with the child

Have you come to rest in Karlovy Vary with your children? This is its pluses. If stories about magic water and healing air, which give health, did not add optimism to your offspring, organize an entertainment program for him in Karlovy Vary with outings in the area.

Located near the Czech-German border, the distance to Karlovy Vary is 72 kilometers, but the trip is worth it. Behind the gate you find yourself in the world of German fairy tales. Especially for men (and not only)

While mothers relax in the spa and have fun with children, dads can diversify their holidays in Karlovy Vary by stopping by the European Center for Natural Beer Baths, which is located just six kilometers from the city along a picturesque forest road along the river. The interior of the first half of the 19th century was recreated in the building. In the courtyard there is a reservoir with carps, which even an inexperienced fisherman can easily catch. Nearby is another pond with the trout that lives in it. The catch is baked according to an old recipe in a beech wood stove.

Beer, which is brewed here on German equipment, is poured into special wooden containers for procedures. Beer heated to a comfortable temperature brings an amazing effect: the skin becomes soft, the body is saturated with vitamins, acids and trace elements. Toxins are removed and a person is charged with energy. Beer baths are recommended for strengthening the circulatory system, cardiac activity, weight loss and restoration of potency.
