Beeline, Family tariff plan: description of benefits

Sharing communication services - now it is possible with the help of tariff plans from Beeline. By connecting a special tariff plan (hereinafter TP), which involves the addition of additional numbers, you can share the packages of minutes, text messages, and Internet traffic with your close people, friends, and colleagues. Moreover, the amount of communication services that is needed is determined individually by the subscriber when connecting Beeline services.

The tariff plan “Family” is represented by several variations: for economical / optimal / intensive communication. It is enough to choose any of them, add additional numbers and you can start making calls, using the Internet and sending SMS messages to any subscribers (not only Beeline). The tariff plan “Family” is an excellent choice for those who want not only to save on expenses for cellular communications, but also to monitor the activity of additional numbers. What are the conditions for connecting this tariff, what advantages it provides - this will be discussed in this article.





The line of tariff plans "Everything!"

As mentioned earlier, for the family, you can choose the necessary tariff plan for the volume of included minutes / megabytes / messages. The line "Everything!" contains 4 options: for 500, 800, 1200 and 1800 rubles. The higher the subscription fee, the “larger” the packages provided under this tariff. Also, the cost is reflected in the number of rooms that can be tied to the main.

The following important facts should be noted right away:

  • The connection and use conditions may vary in different regions of the country. For example, the “Family” tariff plan (Beeline, Moscow and the region) allows you to share all available packages only for a subscriber. a fee of 800 rubles. While for Kazan subscribers, such conditions can be obtained by connecting a tariff plan for 500 rubles per month.
  • Even if the main number is blocked (due to lack of funds or other circumstances), the communication services for the connected numbers will be available normally.
  • The provision of service packages that are included in the selected tariff is carried out for all subscribers tied to the main number equally. This means that it is impossible to limit, for example, for a particular family member using the Internet, having allocated a certain amount of traffic for him.




Features of the tariffs of the line "Everything!"

  • Abon the fee according to the tariff plan is debited in advance, once a month (on the day of connection / transition to the selected TP).
  • The funds are written off according to the tariff plan from the number to which add. numbers (it is considered the main one).
  • Only the home region numbers can be selected as additional numbers.
  • If there is no money in the account of the main number at the time of writing off the monthly payment, other subscribers will have to pay minutes, SMS and Internet, according to their traffic (“Everything for the family”).




How it works?

Detailed information about what conditions are proposed for the above TP options should be found on the official website of your Beeline company region. The tariff plan “Family” is connected, like any other tariff, through the office (if we are talking about purchasing a new SIM card), through the Internet self-service system, through technical support specialists, via a mobile phone (entering relevant requests and commands). Consider one of the most convenient ways to manage your account - through your personal account on the resource of the mobile operator. Before you begin to change the tariff plan, you must certainly fulfill the following conditions:

  • Visit the Beeline website (the “Family” tariff plan, a description of the options of which can be found in the “Rates” section) and determine how much services may be required.
  • Top up the account balance by the amount that will be obtained after adding up the subscription fee for the selected TP + 100 rubles (this amount is charged when switching to any tariff plan of the “Everything!” Line), so if we are interested in the tariff plan for 800 rubles, then to the account need to be paid - 900 rubles (subscription fee is charged at the time of connection to the tariff).
  • Refuse from the number of options and services connected to the number, which to one degree or another duplicate the communication services included in the selected TP (for example, if unlimited Internet is connected to the number, then you can refuse it, because the tariff plan includes megabytes included).




The order of connection (transition) to TP “Everything!”

After replenishing the account, you should go to your personal account (or dial the appropriate command on the phone, you can see it on the official resource of the mobile operator), select the tariff of interest, familiarize yourself with its terms again and connect. Then you need to move on to adding numbers. This process is simple, just follow the prompts of the Internet service. After specifying the numbers, the specified subscribers will receive an SMS message to confirm the binding to the common TP.





Beeline: Family tariff plan (benefits)

Advantages of the line of tariff plans “Everything!” obvious:

  • the ability to share included service packages with other numbers;
  • general balance (you can not deposit money to other numbers, it’s enough just to be “in plus”, the main thing is to top up the balance of the main account on time);
  • profitability (by choosing a tariff plan with the required volume of minutes, messages or the Internet, you can provide yourself and your family with communication services).

Conclusion

TP line “Everything!” became truly popular with all Beeline subscribers. The tariff plan “Family 07”, which was previously available in some regions of our country (for example, in Tatarstan) and allowed you to communicate with your “favorite” numbers at a reduced cost, cannot compare with the offers of the mobile operator we considered earlier for profitability. Using TP options from the "Everything!" Line It is equally available for different devices (tablets, smartphones, phones) of one subscriber.
