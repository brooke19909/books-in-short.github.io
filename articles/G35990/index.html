Popular hotels in Kirov. Vyatka

The city of Kirov is not a fashionable resort or a treasury of historical attractions. This is an ordinary provincial city, almost 900 kilometers away from Moscow. But Kirov is also a kind of capital, but not state, but industrial. This city is often called the peat and fur capital of Russia.

Every year, tens of thousands of tourists and business people come to the city to the eyeballs filling the Kirov hotels. Vyatka is most popular for its unique location and impeccable service.

Where is Vyatka located?

The hotel is located next to the Kirov Park, on the well-groomed paths of which it is especially pleasant to stroll on summer evenings. The birds singing and the rustle of leaves are heard in the rooms, and the Solovievsky ponds help maintain coolness even on the most stuffy days.



The road from the bus station through the park, next to which is the hotel "Vyatka" (Kirov), takes no more than five minutes on foot. Direct access to the hotel from the train station is via trolleybuses No. 3 and No. 6, as well as buses No. 9, 12, 53, 60, 61. A transfer can be arranged by prior arrangement.

Number of rooms

The cozy hotel in Kirov "Vyatka" offers its guests comfortable rooms of various categories. These are standard budget rooms, there are 83, and luxurious suites that can accommodate a small delegation or a family of travelers.

Standart room

Despite the budget cost of living, and it is 1650 rubles, this room has everything to make your stay in the city as comfortable as possible. A comfortable bed, refrigerator, kettle, TV will satisfy the most demanding guests.

Single Superior Rooms

The hotel has 23 rooms of this category. In addition to standard amenities such as a TV, fridge and kettle, this room features a wider bed. In the season when many hotels in Kirov are crowded, Vyatka can accommodate an extra bed in superior rooms. The cost of stay is 2100 rubles.



Single comfort

This room is particularly comfortable. In addition to a wide bed and a standard set of amenities, the room is equipped with air conditioning, which is very relevant in the hot summer months. The hotel has seven Comfort rooms and offers them at prices from 2300 to 2800 rubles per day.

The double standard room, of which the hotel has 44, has enough spaces to make guests feel comfortable and comfortable. The rooms are equipped with functional furniture, a TV will help to brighten up the evening time, and food and drinks in the refrigerator will keep their freshness for a long time. You can stay in this room for only 2600 rubles per day.

Junior Suite

An ideal option for business travelers is a junior suite consisting of one or 2 rooms. It clearly distinguishes the working and sleeping areas. You can choose a room with one wide bed or two single beds. The furniture of this room allows four guests to comfortably accommodate in it.

And the main thing that leaves far behind other Kirov hotels - Vyatka has installed a jacuzzi in the bathrooms for the comfort of its guests! In addition to the required TV, refrigerator, air conditioning is available. Stay in this room will cost from 2800 to 3000 rubles per day.

Suite

Guests of even the highest level will be satisfied with the level of comfort provided in deluxe rooms. Impeccable service, friendly attitude to guests - all this is provided by the hotel "Vyatka", the city of Kirov. Guest reviews confirm the quality of service. And what can I say - after all, it was in this hotel that Vladimir Putin stayed.

The room consists of three rooms, warm floors and a jacuzzi help to quickly remove relaxation after a busy day. The maximum cost is 5900 rubles.

Nutrition

Breakfast is not included in the price and is available for a fee, which is 300 rubles for a buffet meal. For those who prefer continental breakfasts, meals are organized in a cafe on the eighth floor. You can also order lunch or dinner throughout the day.



Additional services

Not many Kirov hotels have their own gym . Vyatka allows its guests to keep fit for free. Also, at any convenient time, guests can use the services of a beauty salon.

For tourists and business travelers coming to Kirov, this hotel would be the best option. And for many of those who have already stayed at Vyatka and regularly come to the region, this hotel will become a truly second home.
