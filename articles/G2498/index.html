Hotels: Belomorsk (Karelia), Hotel "Gandvik"

Rest in Karelia promises an unforgettable experience, a meeting with the discreet, but surprisingly good and attractive nature of the north. Going here, you can be sure of quality service and comfortable accommodation in guest houses and hotels of the republic. The development of the hotel business in Karelia ensures that a vacation spent here in summer or winter will become a good memory for any vacationer, will cause a desire to return to this region again and visit local hotels.





Belomorsk is a small Karelian town. Thanks to archaeological finds and a rich history, it is very attractive to many tourists.

Hotels of Belomorsk (Karelia)

To accommodate guests in large Russian cities, there are hotels of various categories and prices. Visitors have the opportunity to choose the most suitable hotels for themselves. Belomorsk also provides for a comfortable stay for travelers and business travelers with everything that is needed. But it cannot be said that visitors have ample opportunity here.





There are few hotels in the city. In the center are Gandvik and White Sea, two main hotels. Belomorsk offers its guests free Wi-Fi access, which is a rarity, since mobile Internet does not work well in the city. In season, the cost of living in the rooms of Belomorsk hotels is quite high: about 2500 - 3000 rubles (without breakfast). Therefore, some travelers prefer accommodation at camp sites or choose private hotels.

Belomorsk can offer lovers of budget accommodation a popular Vyg camp site located in the center. At the entrance to the city there is a car camp that allows you to save on accommodation and increase mobility of movement. Relatively recently opened the institution "Starchina" (guest house), located at the confluence of the Vyg River with the White Sea. According to reviews, friendly staff work here, good breakfasts are served. On the Kola highway, not far from the turn to Belomorsk, travelers are also awaiting the Pushnoy Motel.





If you believe the reviews, the living conditions in Belomorsk hotels are quite acceptable. The best hotel in the city is the Gandvik Hotel.

Description

Welcomes Belomorsk warmly and hospitably. Hotel "Gandvik" offers quality food for them, as well as upscale living and recreation conditions. Thanks to the competence and responsibility of the staff, residents are guaranteed comfort and safety.





The hotel has a landscaped surrounding area, a summer cafe, a parking lot, a park area, a modern promenade. The hotel is located at a distance of 250 m from the river Vyg. A 5-minute drive from it is the railway station. Wi-Fi is available on site (free service). The front desk and bar are open 24 hours a day. Conference facilities, a lounge (shared) and ticket service are available.

The hotel provides guests with the opportunity to relax from the hustle, chat with friends or retire with a loved one. The rooms are decorated in a classic style. All guest rooms are equipped with TV. Each bathroom has a hairdryer. Guests can appreciate the proposed options for accommodation: economy, comfort, junior suite, suite - only 44 cozy rooms for a truly comfortable stay.

Services

At the hotel, guests can take advantage of:

  • beauty and health studio;
  • Laundry
  • resistant souvenirs;
  • business center;
  • luggage storage room;
  • ticket office;
  • Wi-Fi
  • order minor repairs, washing and ironing.

At any time, guests can purchase a souvenir for themselves and their loved ones, thanks to which the memory of visiting Karelia and the impressions received during the trip will be preserved for a long time. At the hotel you can book and purchase a ticket for the Sapphire motor ship to visit Fr. Solovki (excursion).

The Pomeranian Bar offers an extensive menu consisting of salads, appetizers, alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, first and second courses, desserts and ice cream. In addition, aromatic coffee mixes and delicious cocktails can be offered by the bartender. The hotel restaurant "Pomeranian cuisine" is designed for comfortable accommodation of 30-40 people (max. Accommodation - up to 70 people). Visitors here expect a rich menu, attentive staff, a cozy atmosphere and interesting dance and entertainment programs.

Those who come to Belomorsk by train have no reason to worry about transport: hotel staff delivers cars to the station, and the driver greets the arrivals on the platform. Thus, guests are no longer required to overpay for a city taxi.

Wi-Fi (wireless) is available on site and in rooms.
