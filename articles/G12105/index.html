Where on the “Android” clipboard and what is it?

Where is the clipboard on the Android? Before answering this question, it is worth understanding what it is and how it functions. Modern mobile devices are developing very quickly and are practically not inferior to computers of the beginning of the zero years in their characteristics. Such a hurricane speed of development also implies a complication of devices, despite the fact that manufacturers claim the opposite. And now let's proceed to the answer to the question: “Where is the clipboard on the Android?”

What it is

The clipboard is usually used to copy information from one source to another. For example, you need to copy text (image, photo) from an Internet browser and paste it into an application designed for communication on a social network. Such technologies have been used for a long time.



How it works

Many people think that the clipboard is a separate program or a third-party application. However, this statement is not entirely true. The clipboard, as a rule, is a service and part of the operating system, regardless of which device you have in front of you. Computers, smartphones, tablets and, possibly, other mechanisms that use data entry have it.



The principle of operation is associated with the operating system, as well as RAM. The data that you copy is cached, in other words, written inside RAM. If you do not delve deeply into technical aspects and computer science, then such an operation provides speed and data exchange between different applications.

Where is the clipboard on the Android?

Well, here we come to the most important answer to the question. It is important to note that most modern smartphones have touch screens. Therefore, to use this function, you need to pinch the text on the display, after which a special area will appear, which allows you to select certain fragments. After the fragments have been selected, just click the screen again. As a result of this, a menu appears with the suggested options: “Copy text”, “Paste text”, “Cut text”, etc. Thus, now you know where the clipboard is on “Android”. “Samsung” is this or another manufacturer - it doesn’t matter, the principle of functioning is the same for everyone.



Depending on the version of Android, the symbols may look like pictograms, text, various icons. They may also vary in size, shape, etc. Some Android smartphones may have a service that allows you to view the clipboard using an additional menu, and the data in it is not overwritten, but saved in separate fragments. Some text editors on personal computers have similar functionality.

Summary

Thus, we answered the question "Where is the clipboard on the Android?". As a summary, you can return to the topic of other devices.

Most likely, every day you use such a function on a personal computer or laptop, which is a banal copy and paste of text. For example, on Windows it is invoked by the commands “Ctrl + c” and “Ctrl + v”. According to a similar principle, the clipboard in the Android phone also works, where instead of a mouse and keyboard commands, touch input and additional menus are provided on the screen.

If you have not fully figured out your smartphone, then the best option would be to visit the official website of the manufacturer and familiarize yourself with the instructions that come with the device. There, the operation of the clipboard should be described in more detail, as well as all the commands for using it.

Most likely, most readers already understood how to use this function, but simply did not know what it was called. However, many of us master various gadgets intuitively, without hesitation, and very rarely open instructions.
