Should I buy a Prestigio phone? Review of the best models and reviews about them

Sometimes there are quite good phones of little-known brands. Attract value and functionality in such devices. But is it worth buying such smartphones?

Design





The appearance of Prestigio devices is quite interesting and remarkable. Each device has its own design and stands out among its brothers.

Confuses the appearance of only a great similarity with popular smartphones. It seems that for its devices the company takes several parts from well-known models and combines them.

For example, Grace X3 is reminiscent of the design of many devices of the well-known Samsung company. Moreover, if you remove the logo of the company, then the Prestigio cell phone will be quite difficult to distinguish from a more popular brand.

Of course, such a similarity is only on hand for the budget apparatus. In fact, the Prestigio phone implements the design of flagships in a cheaper segment of the market.

It will be difficult for users in this regard to reproach the company, because the devices turn out to be quite interesting in appearance and bear little resemblance to cheap smartphones made in China.

Camera





In addition to design, the Prestigio phone sometimes imitates other more advanced brothers in other aspects. The cameras installed on the device periodically imitate the same market leader, Samsung. So, the 5453 Duo got an 8 megapixel eye, and in fact it is an analog of five megapixels of a well-known company.

Sometimes the Prestigio mobile phone is equipped with more powerful cameras, as in the case of 3530. As many as 13 megapixels are installed there, and the front one will also please the owner with the presence of 5 megapixels.

Naturally, there are exceptions. The 3453 model has only 2 megapixels, but given its cost, this does not look very important.

It is impossible to say that cameras in the company's devices are their weak point, on the contrary, this is one of the strengths of the company's smartphones. Few budget mobile phones can boast of such characteristics.

Filling





In its category, you can confidently consider the Prestigio Multiphone as a fairly advanced device. The filling of some representatives is quite capable of competing even in the middle class of devices.

For example, the phone Prestigio Grace 7557 is able to surprise many users with its filling. This smartphone, which is quite noticeable both in design and in ability, will satisfy many requests of even sophisticated users.

The device became the owner of as many as eight cores, each of which has a frequency of 1.7 GHz. Naturally, the MTK processor is used, as in most Chinese devices. Despite its origin from the Middle Kingdom and low cost, the used part has proved itself very well.

In addition to an excellent processor, the smartphone has as much as 2 GB of RAM. For a representative of budget devices, this is an excellent indicator.

Of course, there are cheaper devices with lower characteristics. Although the Grace 7557 has a low price, it can rightfully be considered one of the flagships of the company.

The company sells a solid filling even in the cheapest devices. An example of this is the 3503, which has 1.2 GHz and 512 RAM.

It is possible that many smartphones released by the company do not reach 7557 in terms of hardware, but they can still satisfy many of the owner‚Äôs requests.

Display





The weakest point in the cheapest representatives is the screen of the device. And the matter is not in the small diagonal, but in resolution. Setting even 4 inches, the company sometimes neglects the rest of the parameters.

So, the phone Prestigio 3453 equipped with TFT-technology. And everything would be fine, but the lack of pixels leads to graininess of the image.

A similar flaw was fixed in more advanced models. Already in 7557 modern Super Amoled technology and the best resolution are used.

When choosing a device, you should definitely study the characteristics of the screen. Since not all devices can satisfy the display quality.

Battery

The main problem with smartphones manufactured by this company is the battery. At first glance, the batteries used in the devices have a considerable capacity, but the duration of the operation fails.

Using a not-so-demanding MTK processor should solve this problem, but it still exists.

The device discharges incredibly quickly, although the battery parameters correspond to the same Lenovo. The approximate capacity even in the weakest phones is 2000 maH, but it seems that in reality it is only 1500.

Accordingly, the duration of operation with active use is only 3-4 hours. It may seem that this is quite normal, but it is not always possible to additionally charge the device.

Compared to the same Lenovo, which have a more advanced filling and a similar battery capacity, the operating time is simply ridiculous.

Repairs





An unpleasant situation that the owner of Prestigio may face is a breakdown. However, the problem is not the damage to the device itself, but the search for spare parts for it. Most workshops simply do not have parts for the devices of this company.

The only way out of this situation is a long wait for the appearance of the required in the warehouse or an order from abroad.

Advantages

If you evaluate the most successful devices from the lineup, you can add up to the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhether to become the owner of Prestigio.

An interesting design of the devices should be noted. And no matter what the cost of the device, each smartphone stands out, and this is already pleasing. Even among popular mobile phones, it is difficult to find such a wide variety. The catchy look, as well as the individuality of each phone, highlight the brand against the general background.

No less enjoyable is the presence of a solid camera. In top models, such as 3530, it will be as much as 13 megapixels. Moreover, the Prestigio phone has all the necessary data to increase the quality of pictures.

It is rather difficult to ignore the screens of some devices. The use of modern technology and the development of small things allow the display to compete with well-known brands.

And a more significant advantage of Prestigio is the price set on the phone. Almost all models can be safely attributed to cheap devices worthy of attention.

disadvantages

There are also disadvantages that slightly worsen the overall picture.

For the most part this is an incredible battery drain. It seems that the standard battery for all does not satisfy all the needs of the device. Constant dependence on charging overshadows the impression of the company's smartphones. Perhaps replacing the battery with an analog with a larger capacity will solve the problem, but a standard battery is not enough.

The painful point in the lowest priced devices is their screen. Outdated technology and noticeable pixels do not improve the impression when used. Behavior in the sun will also not delight the owner.

Reviews





According to users, a lot of both positive and negative sides has a Prestigio phone. Reviews come down to completely different opinions.

Some owners have problems with freezing the system, while for others on the same device everything works perfectly. It seems that a similar situation arises due to the firmware version.

The vast majority of Prestigio owners complain about the short battery life, but many budget devices have this problem. Unfortunately, the Prestigio phone, reviews of which can be found, most likely, will also have such a disadvantage.

In general, device owners are inclined to believe that the cost and functionality fully cover minor flaws. The phones of the company are quite suitable for all users.

Total

Smartphones of the company show themselves from the best side as a budget option. The average functionality is quite suitable for solving many problems.
