sights

Relaxing in Club Nena Hotel HV-1 (photos are given in the article), you can visit the famous city of Side with its attractions. This is a real historical open-air museum. The city has been known since the seventh century BC. But at the same time, most of the historical monuments preserved in Side dates from the second century BC.

Along the perimeter of the city, the walls of an ancient fortress are preserved, which remind of past times. No less interesting for tourists is the Nymphaeum Fountain. The ancient Greeks built sanctuaries, dedicating them to water nymphs. They looked like semicircular buildings three stories high. In Side, unlike other cities, a lot of historical monuments have been preserved.



Near the city, tourists can enjoy the Manavgat waterfall, which was artificially created during the construction of the dam. More recently, this place could be visited for free, but now everything has changed. The waterfall has one feature - its height is two meters, but its width is forty meters.

In the northwestern part of the city are the remains of an ancient aqueduct. It was once built to deliver water from a river in a mountainous area. For those times, it's just an awesome design.

In the old district of Side, the Agora has survived to this day. This is something like a market square, where trade was going on and people gathered. Of course, many details have suffered greatly from time to time, but even in this form this place is simply amazing.

In Side, you should definitely visit the ancient amphitheater built by the Greeks. Here, once there was a gladiatorial battle, and later on this place was a temple in the open. The statues that once stood in the amphitheater have long gone to museums. And in the ruins of an ancient theater, opera performances are sometimes arranged.



A must-see place for tourists is the port of Side. Even in ancient times, the harbor flourished due to the trade in slaves that pirates brought here. Side later turned into a port with international trade. And now, on the coast, the remains of the oldest moles have been preserved. From the port you can go for a walk along the river.

One of the favorite places for tourists is the Green Canyon. Here, beautiful vegetation, turquoise water and majestic mountains - all this creates stunning landscapes.
