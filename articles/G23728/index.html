The favorable climate of Mui Ne Resort

Potential guests of Vietnam are often stopped by the rainy climate of this country. After all, no one wants to enjoy a vacation during a month and a half rain. However, do not rely on prejudices inspired by Hollywood films! Guest reviews of the local economy hotel (with 3 stars) - Canary Beach Resort 3 *, indicate excellent resort weather. None of them have complaints of precipitation!

Let's make it clear. The climate of Vietnam from mid-October to mid-December is really rainy. But we want to reassure tourists traveling to the Phan Thiet resort. Near this 250-thousandth city, the capital of the resort industry, the Canary Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Mui Ne) is located. The rain front does not pass into the Fantiet zone. Guest rooms

Hotel rooms 3 * Canary Beach Resort are spacious. The family rooms are furnished with two double beds, armchairs, tables, bedside tables, a wardrobe. They are equipped with air conditioning, safe, TV, minibar, electric kettle, hairdryer.

Catering

The food is tasty and varied. It is provided by a hotel restaurant and bar.

The restaurant is built in the middle of a picturesque garden of coconut trees. Here, in addition to Vietnamese technology for the preparation of seafood, international cuisine familiar to Europeans is practiced. The menu has a lot of French dishes.

For lunch and dinner, the menu includes seafood, meat and fish main dishes. Lots of vegetable salads. It lacks a variety of pastries, juices, fruits.

Prices for paid drinks and dishes of the restaurant and bar (not included in the all-inclusive system) are 2-2.5 times higher than in shops and shops of the village.

Beach

The beach of the 3 * Canary Beach Resort is neat, clean, with convenient entry into the water. Its water area is located in Mui Ne Bay, so the sea is calm here. In some reviews, tourists write that the water of the South China Sea is cloudy. This is not entirely accurate. It's all about the tide rolling on the sandy shore. The waves, capturing sand, form a sand curtain in the coastal strip. A few meters from the coast, the water is much cleaner. And after a few dozen - perfect purity.



Right on the beach, guests of the Canary Beach Resort 3 * (hotel photos are presented in the review) have the opportunity to enjoy tasting exotic fish and grilled seafood.
