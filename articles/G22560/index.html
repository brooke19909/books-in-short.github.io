Tasiana Star 4 * (Limassol, Cyprus): hotel description, services, reviews

Cyprus is one of the most beautiful and sunny islands of the Mediterranean Sea. Every year, thousands of tourists from all over the world come here on vacation. Cyprus attracts both with its rich excursion - after all, the goddess of love Aphrodite herself was born in the coastal waters, went ashore, fell in love with these places and stayed here to live - and with gorgeous beaches and snow-white hotels of different categories along the coast, buried in tropical greens. Cyprus, by the way, is a rather expensive tourist destination, and not everyone can afford to live in local five-star hotels. Therefore, those who like comfort, but want to get it at affordable prices, it is better to choose among 4-star options that do not belong to global hotel brands. These include, for example, Tasiana Star 4 * (Cyprus / Limassol). The advantage of this hotel is its location in close proximity to the shore (beach) and entertainment centers.



Holidays in Cyprus

Once on the island, tourists freeze from the beauty of the natural landscape: mountains, tropical greenery and flowers, the gold of beaches and the enchanting turquoise water surface ... The modern architecture of the big cities - Nicosia, Larnaca and Limassol - is also impressive. Nevertheless, being in any corner of the island, you feel the atmosphere around you, which is inherent only in resort towns. The air in Cyprus is also unique, as if saturated with the smells of citrus fruits, olives, tropical exotic flowers, to which the brackish aroma of the Mediterranean Sea is mixed. This is more felt by those tourists who relax in coastal hotels, such as the aforementioned Tasiana Star 4 * (Limassol). Leaving the hotel, after 5-7 minutes you can plunge into the gentle sea, and then comfortably sit on a sun lounger or directly on the sun warmed by the sun and enjoy plenty of sunbathing. Despite the fact that the best time to visit the island is from the beginning of May to the end of October, Cyprus does not lose its relevance throughout the year. It's never cold here. Of course, swimming in the sea in December or January will not work, but the beautiful panorama from the window, flowering evergreen trees and bushes will certainly cheer you up, and most hotels have outdoor and indoor pools with heated water. And winter is an ideal time for excursions, because there are no queues at the ticket offices in front of the sights, and the sun does not bake anymore. By the way, many hotels, including Tasiana Star 4 *, have tour desks. They will organize an exciting program for you, provide transport and meals during the mini-trip.
