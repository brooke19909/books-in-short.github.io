Calabria

A beautiful sunny land with a clear and crystal-clear sea, mountains as if covered with green velvet, and small coastal towns with their unforgettable charms. All of this is Calabria. Tourists bring photos of her beauties from a trip to share landscapes that are indescribably verbally with friends. There are not many people here, Calabria is not like the north of Italy, resembling a buzzing hive. It still boasts pristine nature, beautiful air, ancient towns with a traditional way of life. Location

The hotel is fifty-five kilometers from Lamezia Terme Airport. Usually from there the hotel organizes transfers for groups. From the hotel Le Rosette Resort 4 * straight to the sea about a hundred meters. But since you are unlikely to jump into the water directly from the high bank, you will have to get to the beach by a roundabout road. And this is about four hundred meters. About a quarter of an hour you need to go to the nearest village of Parlegia, where there are cafes, bars and shops. Many tourists called the hotel location successful not only because of its proximity to the beach and the village, but also because it fully allows you to appreciate the beauty of nature spread around. The sunsets are especially beautiful here. About five kilometers from here to the town of Tropea, where the famous beach, depicted in all the photographs of Calabria, is located, it can be said, a visiting card of the region.



Territory

Hotel Le Rosette Resort 4 * is fifteen cottages in one and two floors high, built in the same style. They are located in a very large and beautiful garden. Its territory is very picturesque, with panoramic views of the sea and mountains, which vacationers often mention in their responses. Very clean air. The hotel is surrounded by the greenery of the park: there are palm trees, roses, oleanders, huge cacti, and even a small pine grove. Everywhere the smell of flowers reigns, and alleys and arbours beckon to walk and relax in the shade of trees.



Residence

Hotel Le Rosette Resort 4 * has one hundred and fifteen rooms. Among them there are standard, triple, family rooms, as well as premises of the highest category. The rooms are air-conditioned with balconies or patios with terraces. It has all the amenities - telephone, TV (projection panels, digital), mini-bar. Rooms are cleaned every day and towels are changed. The bathroom has a hairdryer and bathrobe. The rooms, although small, but comfortable. The last repair was recently, the furniture is new, and all the equipment works well. The guests write that they feel very comfortable and secluded, and not like in the "Soviet" boarding houses, where they live on each other "on their heads." Nothing disappears from the rooms, so many do not even use safes.

Service

In this hotel, as tourists write, you can find all the services that the best hotels of Calabria usually offer. Firstly, it is a transfer from both the airport and the train station in Tropea. Registration of vacationers is carried out around the clock. You can use the safe to store valuables, but for a fee. These services, for which you have to spend extra money, include Internet access. There is also a laundry, parking, tennis court. Bicycle rental is available. And children and adults are entertained by animators. The evening shows in the amphitheater are very beautiful here, although they are held only in Italian. A playground is open for young guests, and in the summer there is also a mini club. It can be visited by children from six to twelve years old. If you want to invite a nanny to look after a child, then this service needs to be pre-ordered and paid extra. Most hotel services are included in the price of the so-called club card, which must be purchased by vacationers upon arrival. Here you can also stay with pets, but only small in size.
