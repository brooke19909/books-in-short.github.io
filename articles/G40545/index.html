Lindos Princess Beach Hotel (Lardos, Greece): hotel description, services, reviews

In Greece there are many different interesting resorts. There are cities for noisy and youthful recreation, places that are especially loved by people taking children on vacation. And also in Greece there are quiet and calm resorts with an indescribable atmosphere, conducive to relaxation. It is in this place that Lindos Princess Beach Hotel is located.





general information

In 2002, in the small resort village of Lardos, the opening of a beautiful hotel complex, made in the traditional Greek style, took place. Lindos Princess Beach Hotel quickly fell in love with tourists. In 2007, it was even decided to increase the number of rooms to 575.

It is very quiet here, and all the nearest attractions are concentrated in Lindos - it is about 3-4 minutes drive from the village. But the airport will have to get about half an hour by car.

What is in the hotel?

In terms of services, Lindos Princess Beach Hotel has everything that can come in handy. Guests have access to free private parking and Wi-Fi, luggage storage, ticket service, dry cleaning and laundry services, a tour desk and reception.

The hotel also has everything you need for official events. Three meeting rooms, a small business center. And 3 conference rooms - for 80, 100 and 400 people. Inside, there are several shops (fur, jewelry, souvenir and food), car rental and a doctor‚Äôs office. Doctors, by the way, can be called to the room if such a need arose.

About the staff, you can also say a few words. Real professionals work here. And besides, speakers of six different languages. There are employees who speak Greek, English, German, French, Italian and Russian.





Recreation and Sports

Lindos Princess Beach Hotel has a lot to do. Here you can play bocce and futsal, table tennis, billiards, darts, do archery or water sports on the hotel's beach. In the evening, the hotel complex hosts all kinds of shows and entertainment events. In addition, guests have the opportunity to do water aerobics and work out in the gym. There is also a tennis court and minigolf, everything you need for water polo, basketball and volleyball on the beach. By the way, often on the territory of the hotel instead of show programs they organize various theme parties.

Did the guests bring their children with them? You don‚Äôt have to worry that they will be bored. There are two children's pools with rides and slides, a playground and a mini club that accepts all children aged four to twelve. There they are supervised by nannies and carers. Also for kids organized a children's disco.

SPA

Lindos Princess, like any other good hotel, has its own spa and wellness center. It has several treatment and massage rooms. The list of services is quite wide. But various massages, body treatments, and also pedicures and manicures are especially popular. Also, many people like to visit SPA-baths, a Turkish bath, a steam room and an indoor pool.

It is worth noting that several highly qualified doctors work in the center. They will help customers decide on the most suitable procedure for them and their body.

And also only persons over 16 are allowed to enter the SPA.





About nutrition

Lindos Princess has four restaurants serving guests. The main one is the establishment called Spondi, which serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Thallasa serves excellent seafood. But it is open only for dinner. Like Osteria, but only this is a restaurant of Italian cuisine. And the beachfront Aeolos Restaurant offers only light snacks and drinks. By the way, there is still a bar called Dionysos.

The hotel serves its guests on an all-inclusive basis. In fact, this is another of its advantages. As travelers who have been here assure, the food in the restaurants here is very tasty. Standard breakfasts - cereals, omelets, fried eggs, boiled eggs, fried sausages, sausages, mushrooms, onion rings. They also serve all kinds of desserts - jams, cookies, dried fruits, yogurts, cottage cheese, honey pies, jelly, souffl√©, cakes.

There is also dietary food - stuffed vegetables, stews, baked low-fat meat (even lamb is cooked), salads. In general, Lindos Princess Beach Hotel 4 * is a real paradise for lovers of delicious food. Even true gourmets will find on the menu what they like.

Bungalow

Family apartments

Many people do not mind relaxing at sea with their children. For such guests the hotel has a separate category of rooms. The family apartments have a living area of ‚Äã‚Äã30 square meters. m. The living space is divided into two rooms - a bedroom and a living room. One has a double bed. And in the other - two folding sofas. Suites

Lindos Princess Beach Hotel also has apartments of this category. Take, for example, a family suite. It is intended for those people who want to relax at sea, without denying anything to themselves, even in terms of apartments.

These rooms are 45 square meters. m. The suites have two bedrooms, which are connected by a corridor. Inside there is one large bright bathroom. The highlight of these apartments is that the view from them opens exclusively to the sea. For many people, this nuance is of great importance. And a week's worth of living in such apartments is approximately 95 tons. (for 2 adults and 2 children).

But the best option is not this family room with a balcony. The most expensive apartments are called executive suites. Their area is 50 square meters. m. Inside, in a spacious bedroom decorated in exquisite colors, there is a king-size bed. What is most interesting, there are two such rooms in the suite. Each has its own plasma TV. The bathroom, however, is one. But there is a jacuzzi and a shower in it. The view from the window, of course, on the sea. This apartment can accommodate two adults and, if necessary, 2 children.

Organizational moments

In Lindos Princess 4 * tours are sold out pretty quickly. Therefore, if you want to get there, it is better to secure a room in advance, using the early booking. It will only be necessary to provide personal credit card details. Suitable "Visa", "American Express" or "Master Card". The card needs to be used only your own. Because when registering, you will need to submit it along with your ID. The owner must be alone.

Check-in starts at 14:00, check-out lasts until noon. You also need to know that the hotel's restaurants have a dress code. Beachwear, slippers and slippers are strictly prohibited. Men should dress in long trousers, and girls in dresses, sundresses, etc.

What else do guests say?

If you look at the reviews left about your stay at Lindos Princess Beach Hotel, you can see that this hotel is really as good as it is presented in the standard descriptions. But not for everyone. This hotel is definitely not suitable for people who prefer a quiet holiday. Yes, the place where the hotel is located is calm. But the hotel itself is quite noisy. This is because the vast majority of tourists go here with their children. Anyway, in the summer. In winter there are fewer tourists, which, in principle, is logical. Although the climate allows you to relax here even in January.

By the way, guests are advised to rent a car and drive around the island on their own. In Rhodes there are such picturesque places where excursions are not conducted. But you can see for yourself much more interesting sights and outlandish landscapes.

However, this is a good hotel complex for those people who want to have fun from the heart in pleasant conditions, buy plenty at sea and not deny anything in terms of food. If someone dreams of such a vacation, then it is worth going here on your vacation. No one will feel sorry. Numerous reviews confirm this.
