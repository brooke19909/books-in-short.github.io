Parktronics for cars: types, specifications, installation and reviews

In modern urban conditions, driving a car requires considerable skill - personal transport is becoming more and more every day, and freedom for maneuver is becoming less. And if the drivers still manage to ride, then putting the car in the parking lot or just stopping near the store is already a difficult task requiring a good eye and speed of decision making. Just to alleviate this problem, parking sensors are designed for cars. Today we will talk about them in more detail.

What is it needed for

Parktronic, or parking radar, is used to alert the driver that his car has driven too close to another car or obstacle. The device is quite simple, but very useful in situations with limited visibility and in cramped places. In addition, modern cars, due to the fact that almost all of them are front-wheel drive, have a long front overhang and a sloping hood, which is often not visible from the driver's seat.

In addition, for the sake of design refinement, the rear window is made small in size, especially in sedans (the edge of the trunk is not visible there). All this creates discomfort and the need to "estimate" the distance to the foreign object "by eye". Sometimes this does not always end successfully. As a result, scratches from close contacts appear on the bumpers.

How it works

Structurally, parking sensors is an ultrasonic radar, the sensors of which are simultaneously emitters. They generate ultrasonic waves and receive their reflections.

From the return time of the reflected signal, the device determines the distance to the obstacle. Most modern cars are still equipped with this simple device at the factory. But you can install it on any old cars. The only question is to find out what parking sensors are for cars.

Kinds

The system consists of several sensors, a switching unit and an information panel. Sensors can be located on the front and / or rear bumper - in the most problematic, sought-after areas mentioned above. Sensors for each zone can be from two to four. Two pieces that are located in the corners - the most economical and cheapest option.

But in the center, a blind zone forms , which is not controlled in any way. There is a risk of crashing into, say, a pipe sticking low out of the ground. Three sensors on the bumper are already a more reliable solution. But the best option is four elements at an equal distance from each other. Thus, the entire bumper area is fully controlled. According to owners, such a system is the most common and convenient.

Warning systems

The information screen, in fact, is a warning system for the driver about the presence of an obstacle. Structurally, it is a box with a screen on which the car and sensors located on the bumpers are symbolically depicted, depending on the configuration. In addition to the characters on the screen, an additional sound signal can be used . Thus, there are three types of alerts:

  • Sound.
  • Optical
  • Character.

The first view is the simplest. If there is an obstacle in the range of the radar (from 2.5 m to 25 cm), an intermittent signal starts to sound. As the obstacle (or vehicle) approaches, the signal begins to increase. With close proximity to the bumper, the sound becomes continuous. Using such a system, it is convenient to park by ear, but if there is a problem with hearing or loud music is playing, then the benefits of parking sensors are significantly reduced.

The next system is optical. It is a strip of LEDs of different colors, from green to red, in accordance with the proximity of the object. These lanes are located on the right and left of the information screen and correspond to the location of obstacles on the respective sides of the vehicle. Digital display - a screen on which numbers are displayed - the distance in meters to the object. The numbers are often duplicated by sound indications and LED strips. The system has the ability to include various options. In some situations, digital display is not too necessary, or, conversely, when driving in a traffic jam, it is advisable to turn off the sound warning, otherwise the parking sensors will constantly beep. Owner reviews indicate that all three systems are equally convenient and the comfort of use often depends on driving experience.

Models

The types of parking sensors for cars are very diverse, but they are all divided into different groups, which should be mentioned:

  • The first group consists of parking sensors for a car, the indicators of which are built into the rear-view mirror and are strips of columns of horizontal dashes of different colors. They will light up one by one as the car approaches the obstacle.
  • The second, more advanced option is parking sensors, combined with a rear view camera. For such a model, the information display is a digital screen on which a picture is displayed from a camera located at the back (and sometimes - in front). Unfortunately, the camera lens can get dirty in bad weather, which will not happen with ordinary sensors - they are not afraid of such weather changes.
  • The most modern models of parking sensors have not point, but tape sensors. Such sensors are very easy to install and do not stand out so much on the bumper, so they are becoming more widespread.

Modern parking sensors for cars are often equipped with additional features. Usually they carry redundant and unnecessary information (clock, compass). When choosing a suitable parking sensors model, it is better to focus on the model with maximum functionality and ease of use.

Installation

Currently, installing parking sensors on a car is a fairly common service in specialized service centers. The work is not too complicated and is mainly associated with pulling wires from the sensors to the switching unit and the information screen.

Holes for sensors are also drilled in the bumpers. If you install parking sensors on a car, the price will greatly depend on the installation kit, as well as on the type and size of the car. Self-assembly

Of course, you can save and install parking sensors on the car yourself (in the instructions everything is described in sufficient detail). However, this work is connected with the dismantling-assembly of the cabin, and often - and the removal of the bumper. In addition, to connect the device you will need to crash into the on-board electrical system.

Therefore, before starting work, it is advisable to estimate your skills.

Conclusion

As you can see, parking sensors for cars - a popular and popular option. In the modern rhythm of life, it is moving from the category of luxury into a necessary item that greatly facilitates life.
