Orka Sunlife Resort & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Fethiye, Oludeniz): hotel infrastructure, room description, service, reviews

Turkish resorts attract tourists with a high level of service and relatively low cost of living, when compared, for example, with European countries. At the same time, there are so many coastal cities and towns that you can come here on vacation every summer and discover something new. For example, the new resort Orka Sunlife Resort & Spa 5 * (Turkey) is built right on the side of the mountain, so it offers beautiful views of the bay of the Mediterranean Sea. We will talk about it in more detail in this article, and also consider the reviews of former guests.

More about where this complex is located

Located in southwestern Turkey, Fethiye resort is considered one of the most unusual holiday destinations in the country. And all thanks to its picturesque natural landscapes - low mountains stretch along the coast, which are almost completely covered with trees. Only a few independent travelers came here a few years ago, since there were no large complexes here. But now the resort is developing rapidly, so on its territory not only first-class large hotels are built, but all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay has been created. At the same time, the Fethiye authorities managed to keep the nature of this land intact.

It is worthwhile to clarify that Orka Sunlife Resort & Spa is located almost 7 km from the central part of Fethiye. It would be more correct to say that the hotel is located in the village of Oludeniz, which is considered a national park in Turkey. Its center is 700 meters from the complex, so the noise from the roads and infrastructure does not reach the hotel. The main attraction of Fethiye, the Lycian Trail, is approximately 500 meters away.

Perhaps the most significant drawback, according to tourists, is the remoteness of the hotel from the beach. Since it is located on a mountainside, the distance to the sea is more than 2.5 km. To reach the coast on foot, you need to spend a lot of time, so the hotel has prepared a special free bus for tourists.

The nearest airport accepting flights from Russia is located near the city of Dalaman. It is almost 60 km from Oludeniz, so it can take about 1-2 hours to get to the hotel.
