Transport Bank: feedback from depositors and employees. Rating and Reliability of Transport Bank

In order to maintain the national economy in the railway transport industry, back in 1994, AMFI Bank was established. After a while, his administrative and organizational form was changed to CB Transportniy Bank, LLC, reviews of which are still positive. The commercial organization is small in size and is focused on serving customers from Moscow and the region. The territorial bank works with such cities of the country as St. Petersburg and Yekaterinburg, Perm, Smolensk and Belgorod.

A significant achievement in the work of the financial institution was the introduction of a service called Internet banking. Key Points in History





The financial institution easily survived the crisis of 2008 , and the main merit belongs to the financier Shishkhanov. It was necessary to spend at least two years to restore the bank's indicators to the pre-crisis level. A significant role in this matter was played by the new president of the institution, Lomanov, who was appointed to the post in 2008. Until 2011, a 99% stake held by the Lomanov spouses. After the sale of shares, Transport Bank, reviews of which in most situations have a positive connotation, continued to service the broad-based group of companies New Transport Technologies. At the moment, several influential individuals are the owners of the bank at the same time. These are Serebryannikova and Sorokin, Mazanova, Genetliu and Fez.

A bit about achievements and ratings





Transportniy Bank in 2013 made a significant financial breakthrough and increased its assets to 23 billion 600 million. This amounts to almost 2.5 times the increase in capital available at that time. The expansion was achieved thanks to the active attraction of both private and commercial funds. In 2012, the financial institution's net profit reached 96 million rubles. The authorized capital in 2013 amounted to only 800 million. It was during this period that the RIA Expert rating agency awarded the institution a credit rating of A level. This characterizes the institution solely on the positive side, speaks of its stability, a high level of reliability and trust. There was a positive outlook for the future. According to the latest official data, the volume of assets amounted to 29 321 395 thousand rubles. Transportniy Bank, whose rating is very good, took 144th place in Russia for this value. The amount of capital is equal to 2,402,169 thousand rubles, which corresponds to 195 place among Russian banks. The volume of loans is 20 626 429 thousand rubles, and deposits - 17 633 608 thousand rubles, which corresponds to 122 and 113 places. The total profit for the last year is 382,027 thousand rubles, which made it possible to reach 144 places in the ranking. According to the evaluation of the online project BankStars.ru, the financial institution received 4 stars. As for such well-known rating agencies as Moody's, Fitch and S&P, they did not appreciate the reliability of Transportniy Bank, just bypassing it with their attention.

Recent work changes





Despite the fact that by the end of 2014, Transportny Bank, whose feedback from depositors always testified to its fulfillment of its obligations, was deemed reliable, today some malfunctions can be observed in its operation. In particular, some clients talk about delays in payroll, which no one talks about. There is a cessation of accepting deposits, including replenishment of existing ones. This order was issued by the Central Bank on April 16 for a period of up to 6 months. All contracts, according to representatives of the financial institution, which were signed before April 16, will be executed in accordance with the regulations. This includes the calculation of interest on deposits and their withdrawal.

What do customers and bank employees say?

Transportniy Bank faced certain difficulties, but this did not affect its fulfillment of its obligations. Most of the reviews about the financial institution still speak about it from a positive side. According to employees of the institute, nothing in their work has changed. Based on the analysis of this information, we can talk about the bank's performance, despite certain obstacles that, in principle, had to be faced by almost every institution offering its services within the financial sector in 2015.
