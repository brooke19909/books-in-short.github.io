The recreation center "Syabry", Minsk region: description, features and reviews

Today, not only local residents, but also guests from neighboring countries and far abroad come to recreation centers in Belarus. It is comfortable here, for relatively little money you can actively and interestingly spend a vacation or a weekend. Most of the suburban complexes are located in picturesque places near water bodies and have developed infrastructure for good rest and entertainment. One of such hospitable places near Minsk is the recreation center "Syabry".

general information

The year-round recreation center "Syabry" is located in the village of Semkovo, which is 9 kilometers from the Minsk Ring Road. Accommodation

"Syabry" (recreation center near Minsk) offers guests accommodation in two luxury cottages and six junior cottages. All houses are built in a rustic style, centrally heated and equipped with warm floors. The total housing stock of the suburban complex is 14 double rooms with the possibility of installing additional beds.

Junior Suite Rooms

A comfortable junior suite occupies half of a two-story cottage.

On the ground floor there is a lounge, where a refrigerator, TV, microwave, electric kettle, dishes are installed; bathroom with shower, toilet and hairdryer. Slippers and a bathrobe are prepared for each guest.

On the second floor there is a bedroom with a huge bed and a TV.





Suites

Chic deluxe room occupies a full cottage. On the ground floor there is a bar equipped with a refrigerator, microwave, kettle and kitchenware; spacious living room with TV, leather furniture; En suite bathroom with Jacuzzi. As in the junior suite, there is a bathrobe and slippers for every vacationer. On the second floor there is a bedroom with air conditioning, comfortable armchairs, a TV and an electric fireplace.

The room can deliver dishes from the restaurant.

Nutrition

The recreation center "Syabry" near Minsk has a restaurant in the hunting style and the adjacent dome according to the European model. The hunting lodge is divided into 4 small rooms and in total is ready to accommodate more than 60 guests:

  • wine room (26 seats) with a TV;

  • central hall (16 seats) with fireplace and plasma TV;

  • hunting room (10 places);

  • farmhouse (10 seats).

In the dome, a fully glazed hall on the street, up to 85 people will feel comfortable. There are 2 plasma panels and musical equipment. It is this room that guests prefer for corporate events and personal celebrations. People say that it is impossible to find a better place to hold a holiday. The panoramic glazing of the hall provides a unique opportunity to admire the magnificent nature from any place. But the strongest impression you can get when you are inside the dome during rain or snow.





The restaurant's menu offers dishes of not only national cuisine, but also European. It is also proposed to try and evaluate dishes prepared according to traditional Caucasian recipes, or try the trout that you just personally caught from a stocked lake and expertly prepared by a chef in a restaurant in the Syabry complex.

Recreation center: active sports and entertainment

Guests who come here on vacation can rent one of two artificial tennis courts and a soccer field. Balls and rackets for games do not have to be carried with you; you can rent them. Guests staying in a deluxe cottage enjoy free use of all facilities.

True lovers of hot temperatures will not disregard the Russian bathhouse with a spacious steam room and swimming pool, the Finnish sauna with a font, a hammam and a black-style bathhouse.





The three-story building of the Russian bath is designed for a company of up to 10 people. On the ground floor there is a steam room with brooms, a swimming pool. On the second floor there is a relaxation room with a table, TV, microwave, fridge and kettle. Here you can order food and drinks from the restaurant. On the third floor - Russian billiards and sofas.

Finnish sauna accommodates 4 people. Here, in addition to a shower room and a dry steam room with a font, there is also a room equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.

For fans of fishing in the territory, a special stocked pond was created and comfortable conditions for fishing: convenient descent to the water, toilet, parking for a car. If you wish, you can rent pavilions located around the perimeter of the lake. The rental price includes a picnic set.

Also, the recreation center "Syabry" invites its guests to test their accuracy on a dash or try their luck on slot machines.

In addition, within walking distance are equipped beaches. All guests are delighted with their stay here, they are satisfied with the service, the cleanliness of the territory, and the opportunity to have fun.

Cost of rest

Payment for accommodation and services in the Syabry complex (recreation center, Minsk) is made in Belarusian rubles in cash or by credit card. It is also possible to pay for services using ERIP and WEBPAY payment services.

Providing a double junior suite for a day will cost approximately 5500-8500 Russian rubles (depending on the number of guests and the age of children). The price for accommodation in a luxury cottage is about 12 500-19 500 Russian rubles per day (also depends on the number of people living in the room and their age).





For regular customers and guests renting cottages, discounts and promotional offers. There is an opportunity to purchase a subscription. This fact is also very pleasant to vacationers.
