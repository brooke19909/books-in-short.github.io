"Barskaya estate" of the Tver region: recreation, prices, photos and reviews

Tver land is famous for its cultural and historical heritage, a huge number of archaeological structures and centuries-old monuments are concentrated on its vast expanses. Every year, tourist flows increase. It offers a variety of hotel complexes, resorts and pensions, built in the most picturesque places of the region. Almost every second hotel provides transfer services and has a children's corner on its territory.

Here you can find a complex for a secluded holiday, a romantic weekend, family vacations and active pastimes. The Barskaya Usadba luxury hotel of the Tver Region, which was opened not so long ago, was especially popular - in 2009, the Pension is a place of relaxation leisure, as it is located at a distance from the metropolis in the middle of a forest and endless fields.

Location and Short Description

The Barskaya Manor complex (Tver) is located in a picturesque outback, on the banks of the Volga River, only 55 km from the central part of the city. But even in a certain distance from civilization, a favorable infrastructure has been created here, contributing to a comfortable suburban vacation. The hotel has a beautiful landscaped area, decorated with a fountain, flat alleys and artificially created islands with flowering shrubs.

The management constantly maintains the high European status of the tourist complex. Improves its services, trains and retrains personnel. Impeccability reigns here even in small things. Housing stock

As for accommodation, the hotel "Barskaya Usadba" offers several accommodation options: economy rooms, deluxe rooms and individual VIP cottages with individual amenities. VIP-houses are located at a distance from the main building, there are only 10 of them in the territory. Near the villas there is parking for cars.

There is a cozy sleeping area, a spacious living room, an equipped kitchen, a small entrance hall with a dressing room and a bathroom in the cottages of the Barskaya Usadba complex (Tver Region). Prices for accommodation in a guest double house, taking into account three meals a day, range from 5000 r. - on weekdays, on weekends - from 7 thousand p. Standard room will cost much cheaper (within 2000 p.)

All rooms are decorated in warm colors, equipped with a bathroom (a set of hygiene products is available). The windows offer stunning views of the park area and the forest. High-speed internet, TV and telephone are available.

About nutrition

The main trump card of the hotel is the excellent cuisine. Hotel "Barskaya estate" of the Tver region takes care of its guests, therefore, carefully develops a food system. Meals are served in a cozy buffet style dining room. The diet is dominated by national, eastern and European dishes.

Skilled chefs surprise guests with culinary delights: stuffed pike perch, fried milk pig and other delicacies. Sweet tooth will be delighted with fresh pastries. You will appreciate the luxurious restaurant with an exclusive renovation and a varied menu. The large hall of the institution accepts 200 people. There are regular dance evenings and special events.

Guests can enjoy a homely atmosphere and aromatic coffee at the 24-hour bar. There is also a phyto-bar with a rich assortment of ice cream, tea varieties and oxygen cocktails in the adjacent territory . Tents with gazebos are equipped, where everyone can cook their own meat on the grill with their own hands.

Leisure and service

The Barskaya Manor complex (Tver) is proud of its Aquacenter. It consists of a large swimming pool with heated floors. A separate safe pool is built specifically for the youngest visitors. For adults, a beach area with sun loungers is organized. In the center there is a jacuzzi, a Finnish sauna with hot and dry steam.

A hammam (Turkish bath) works with heated walls, floors and deck chairs. It has a relaxation area with a TV, billiards and a bathroom. For lovers of this steam room, a Russian bath with brooms and an ice pool is provided. This is where you can cleanse the body of toxins and salts.

For health and beauty, a spa is provided, built in the form of a ship cabin. A beautiful view of the inhabitants of the sea world opens from the porthole. The center offers a variety of body wraps, peels, beauty treatments, aromatherapy and massage.

For children

With special attention and care, the hotel "Barskaya Usadba" of the Tver region refers to the guys. For their leisure, a zoo with real animals and birds has been created here. The inhabitants of the farmstead are foxes, squirrels, iguanas, crocodiles and other outlandish pets.

Kids can have fun and actively in the club "Cartoons". This is something like a kindergarten, only without a kitchen area. Experienced teachers work in it, involved in drawing, modeling, outdoor games with children. The club has many educational toys, balls and other children's items. Children include animated films, organize sports contests, competitions and entertainment programs. For children, guided tours of the Museum of Russian Fairy Tales are held.

Business services

At a high level, business leisure is organized for corporate performances, parties and competitions. Spacious and technically equipped conference room for 300 people. Here you can conduct all kinds of seminars, presentations, weddings and name days.

The Barskaya Usadba Hotel in the Tver Region has a VIP room for private conferences and negotiations. The hotel can arrange receptions and business lunches upon request. There is a spacious hall for ballroom dancing and physical training with mirrored walls.

Entertainment programs

A chic entertainment program is prepared for the guests of the complex. Exciting parties with exciting shows and performances are held at the hotel "Barskaya estate" (Tver region).
