Plantronics M55 Bluetooth Headset Review

Plantronics M55 is an inexpensive headset. Usually a similar set of functions is found in higher-class models. It should also be noted that the adapter is included.

Design, construction

Plantronics M55 is similar to the M25. The differences are only in the design and scope of delivery. There is a compact adapter, as well as a cigarette lighter, which received a USB input. The headset is compact. The front panel is made of glossy black plastic.





In the reviews you can find some comments regarding this material. According to a number of users, it is easily scratched and fingerprints remain on the surface. At the ends there is a black perforated plastic. The speaker covers the mesh. The perforated panel makes the appearance of the device very unusual. There is a microUSB input on the back. It is not covered by a plug. Build is good. Nothing creaks during compression.

Way of wearing, comfort

The Plantronics M55 headset can be worn with or without a special bow. The glued nozzle is replaced here with a regular, removable, with a protrusion. It is conveniently located in the ear. The Plantronics M55 headset under a hat or with long hair is completely invisible. Everything is in order for this model with ergonomics. The device can be worn for a long time without discomfort. The front panel has a multi-function button. It allows you to switch between multiple lines, enable pairing mode, answer calls. At the end there is a power button. This solution is very convenient, it allows you to quickly put the device into sleep mode. As a result, battery resources are consumed much more slowly.





At the opposite end is the volume button. This process occurs in cycles. There are no problems with management. Everything is organized logically. The indicator light is located on the front panel. It is able to burn red. For example, this happens when pairing mode is activated. The claimed battery life is 11 hours of continuous conversation. While waiting, the device can remain on for 11 days. If you turn off the charged device, it is able to save energy for about 5 months. These indicators are very decent. The voice quality of this device is also good. No pairing problems are observed. Everything is correct and fast.

conclusions

We have already described the features of the Plantronics M55 headset. Reviews on this device will be considered later. Users attribute to their strengths getting rid of tightly fixed nozzles, improved design and functionality compared to previous models of this manufacturer. Music lovers also in their reviews praised the multimedia qualities of this device.





In the comments, as a strong point of this model, the possibility of simultaneous work with two phones is often mentioned. The presence of voice prompts, the owners also recorded to the pluses. In general, the audience has a positive attitude towards the device, there are several minor comments regarding the appearance. Now you know everything you need about the Plantronics M55 headset.
