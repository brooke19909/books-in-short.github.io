Hotel Staccoli 3 * (Rimini) - photos, descriptions, prices and reviews

Rimini is one of the most popular resorts not only in Italy, but also on the entire Adriatic coast. Hundreds of thousands of tourists come here every year in search of a magnificent beach and sightseeing vacation. In addition, this place also attracts shoppers. This city boasts a superbly developed tourist infrastructure. There are entertainment for every taste, as well as a wide hotel base. If you want to have a great rest in Rimini (Italy) and at the same time do not plan to spend a lot of time in your room, and also want to find a budget hotel, then pay attention to Hotel Staccoli 3 *. We, in turn, will take a closer look at this hotel today and find out what impressions our compatriots left in it.

Where is?

Hotel Staccoli is located on the Adriatic coast in the Italian city of Rimini, in the Bellariva district. The distance to the resort town center is only two kilometers. Bellariva has a well-developed tourist infrastructure, represented by hotels for every taste, numerous restaurants offering dishes of both Italian and international cuisine, bars with a wide selection of drinks, shops, souvenir shops, etc. In addition, in just half an hour you can walk to the center of Rimini. Staccoli (Bellariva) 3 * is located on the second coastline. It is several hundred meters away from the nearest beach. Hotel guests can walk to the sea in leisurely steps in just ten minutes. As for the proximity of the airport, it is located in the city of Rimini. Therefore, after arriving in this air harbor, the journey to the hotel will take you no more than half an hour.





Transport

The Italian city of Rimini is a fairly large transport hub. So, as mentioned above, there is its own international airport. He bears the name of Federico Fellini. The city also has a train station. By the way, the train is one of the most convenient means of transportation when traveling both in Italy and in neighboring countries. There is also a bus service. From the airport to the Staccoli 3 * hotel, if you book a tour through a travel agency, you will most likely be provided with a transfer by comfortable bus or car. If you make the trip yourself, then from the air harbor to the center of Rimini can be reached either by rail or by city bus. And from there you can get directly to the hotel by another bus or taxi. The distance from the city center to "Stakkoli" is only two kilometers, so a taxi will not cost you dearly.

Staccoli 3 * (Rimini): photo and description

"Staccoli" is a small three-star hotel located near the center of Rimini, Italy, a major resort town and literally a few steps from the main shopping street of this city Regina Elena. The hotel consists of one building, which includes 28 rooms of a standard type. All of them are quite compact, but equipped with everything necessary. This hotel is perfect for active people who spend most of their time on vacation outside the walls of the hotel.





Posting Rules

In accordance with the rules adopted in Staccoli 3 * (Italy), the settlement of tourists is carried out starting from noon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier (in case of an early flight), then if you have free rooms, you will not be tired of waiting in vain, but will be placed immediately after arrival. On the day of departure, the occupied room must be vacated before 10 a.m. Moreover, if you have not previously paid for the entire trip with a tour operator, you should pay the full price for the entire period of stay at Stakkoli. The easiest way to do this is with a plastic card. The hotel accepts cards of many large payment systems (Mastercard, Visa, Maestro and others).

Special conditions

The Italian hotel Staccoli 3 * (Rimini) is always happy for tourists staying with children. However, due to the small hotel rooms, extra beds are not available. Thus, it is recommended to book a room in advance with the required number of beds. As for travelers with pets, their accommodation at the hotel is possible. Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the three-star Hotel Staccoli (Rimini, Italy) has 28 rooms. All of them are standard and are designed for one, two, three or four guests. Since there is no possibility of installing extra beds, it is recommended to book rooms with the number of beds you already need. All rooms of the Staccoli 3 * hotel (Rimini), traveler reviews confirm this, although small, they have everything you need for your stay. So, in addition to sleeping places, they have their own bathroom with a shower and toiletries, a wardrobe, a desk, individual air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a telephone, a safe and a balcony. The rooms have ceramic tiles. Rooms are cleaned every day. Sheets and towels are changed twice a week. If desired, all hotel guests can order breakfast in the room.

Nutrition

When booking a room at Staccoli 3 * hotel, you can choose one of the following types of food: breakfast only, half board and full board. All meals are held in the hotel restaurant. A buffet is served here, serving both Italian and international dishes. The hotel also has an American Bar with a wide selection of wines, cocktails and other alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Here you will be served tea or coffee. Packed lunches are available upon request.





Beach vacation

As already mentioned, Staccoli 3 * hotel is located within walking distance from the sea. The beaches here are municipal, so you will have to pay for renting sunbeds and umbrellas. However, you can stay free of charge in the territory not occupied by sun loungers.

Entertainment and infrastructure

Since Stakkoli is a very small hotel, it cannot provide any special entertainment for guests. Mostly people who come to Rimini spend little time in the walls of the hotel. So, tourists relax here on the magnificent beaches, make walks, make purchases and, of course, go on excursions to numerous attractions.





Room price

As for the cost of accommodation in Staccoli, it is more than attractive. So, a day in a single room will cost you from 1400 rubles, and a double - from 1800 rubles.
