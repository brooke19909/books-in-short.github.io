Attractions and attractions of Nebug

With the onset of summer, everyone dreams of relaxing on the seashore. And immediately the question arises: where exactly to go, so that the vacation will remain in your memory for a long time as an unforgettable and interesting adventure? As a rule, choosing a resort town or village, people pay attention to types of entertainment and local attractions. In this respect, the sky is an ideal place.

Village History

In 1864, 50 Cossack families settled on the Black Sea coast and founded a Cossack village. In memory of the first settlers in the village, the Cossack Kuren was organized, which fully conveys the atmosphere of that time. Gradually, the village grew, and now it is a popular resort village, which attracts vacationers from all over Russia.





Sights of Nebug and its environs

Nebug is an amazingly beautiful place surrounded by mountain slopes with lush vegetation. A small river of the same name flows through the village, known to the locals as the Bull's Eye.

Vacationers here are waiting for a wide variety of entertainment, such as descent along mountain rivers, rock climbing, diving. In addition to entertainment, local attractions also attract attention. Nebug is primarily famous for its water park.

Waterpark "Dolphin"

The local water park is known for the fact that there are a lot of green spaces on its territory: cypresses, palm trees and beautiful exotic flowers are adjacent to simple Russian birches. The highest slides are also located here - this distinguishes the local water park from similar entertainment centers in other resort cities. Another distinguishing feature of the "Dolphin" - work at night. This is a real paradise for fans of nightlife. Water libraries, enchanting shows, fireworks - all this is arranged by the owners of the water park for their visitors.





Dolphinarium "Aqua-World"

If you come to rest in the village with children, you should definitely visit the local dolphinarium. An interesting show with these amazing marine inhabitants will not leave anyone indifferent. Feel free to advise your friends to visit the village of Nebug. Sights, photos of which are presented on the page, amaze with their diversity.

Dolmens

In addition to swimming in the clear sea, vacationers are attracted by unusual attractions. Nebug is famous for its dolmens and waterfalls. A group of 13 dolmens is a living reminder of the ancient Adyghe settlements. Where these amazing structures came from is still unknown, but definitely worth a look at them.

Waterfalls

In the valley of the Nebug River, you can admire the waterfalls of stunning beauty. Near the largest of them, which is called "Blue", there is a small lake. Thanks to its amazing color, the waterfall got its name. Waterfalls surround the beautiful mountain slopes covered with greenery. This amazing place gives visitors peace and tranquility. It will most of all please lovers of a relaxing holiday who have arrived in Nebug. Sights and entertainment are so diverse that everyone will surely find something of their own.





Mount Lysa

In the village of Nebug, Lysaya Mountain rises , the peak of which is covered with snow almost all year round. Here the origin of the river Cossack and Podlysaya. At the top of the mountain is a survey point. It offers beautiful views of the sea and alpine meadows on the slopes of the mountain.

Entertainment for vacationers in the village

If you are not a fan of excursions and natural attractions, Nebug will delight you with a whole arsenal of all kinds of entertainment. Even the most fastidious vacationers will surely find here what they like.

Nebuga Beach

For those who like to soak up the sun and plunge into the transparent deep sea, Nebug is an ideal choice. Clean pebble beaches stretch along the coast. Here you will find a lot of entertainment on the water - riding a banana and a tablet, water skiing, jet ski, parachuting. Even with a large influx of vacationers, you can always find a free place for your family.





Jipping

Fans of outdoor activities will not remain idle. Riding jeeps along difficult routes will give you an unforgettable experience and a positive charge of energy for a long time. You can either fully rent a car, or go on a trip in a group, having reserved a place for yourself in advance. Payment can be made both in cash and by bank transfer.

Diving

In the village of Nebug there is a diving club. Experienced instructors work here, who organize trial dives for beginners and whole diving trips for experienced diving enthusiasts .

ATV rides

Complex and exciting ATV routes are suitable for lovers of extreme relaxation. The beauty of the surrounding landscape and the crossing of mountain rivers will leave stunning memories. Experienced drivers organize unforgettable trips to the most beautiful places in the village and its environs.





Boat trips

Boat trip to the mountain Kiseleva - one of the most popular attractions in the village. After enjoying the beauty of the green slopes, you can take a dip in the bay of White Rock, which is famous for its clear transparent water. Very often dolphins come to this bay, so there is a high probability of meeting these amazing marine inhabitants here.





Excursion to Abkhazia

Almost daily in Nebug, specially for vacationers, they organize a sightseeing tour to Abkhazia. This amazingly beautiful republic attracts millions of tourists. Hospitable locals will gladly treat you to the delicacies of their national cuisine, and guides will show you the most beautiful places in Abkhazia - the resorts of Gagra, Pitsunda, Lake Ritsa, New Athos Monastery and New Athos Cave.

To summarize, I would like to wish all fans of relaxation on the coast must visit the village of Nebug. Sights and entertainments (see the photo in the article) that you read about are far from a complete list.
