Entertainment in Vityazevo - what are they?

How to have fun in Vityazevo? Entertainment will help in this matter. And what is interesting in the village? What specific entertainment in Vityazevo can be visited by both adults and children?

Interesting information

Of course, all events of this kind will be described in the most detailed way, but the history of the resort village is so interesting that it simply will not be possible to get around it. Previously, this place had a completely different name, which was directly connected with the Greeks inhabiting it - Pontus of Euxinus. The new name - Vityazevo - the village received the last name of a major who became famous during the Russian-Turkish war. Vityazevo is called by the locals little Athens, because many houses have decorations in the form of statues and ornaments from ancient times. The facades of buildings, their furnishings inside resemble the ancient Hellas. But a characteristic feature of this resort village is that the mass of civilized benefits for a person are adjacent to the absence of city noise.

Entertainment

Entertainment in Vityazevo is very diverse. For adults, there are special kitesurfing schools, water skis and parachutes, and swimming masks are available for rent . Those who prefer to rest on land will also have something to do: horseback riding routes, cycling trips, mountain rivers.

Paralia Embankment and Lunapark

What other entertainment is there in Vityazevo? The most unforgettable place in the village is the promenade of Paralia. There is a lunapark. The most extensive list of attractions will be presented here. The recently opened water park is decorated in the spirit of the historical past of the village - in the Greek style. What did the creators of these two parks come up with! Whirlpools and falls, artificial rivers and labyrinths ...





Those who come to enjoy the beauty of the resort town will be glad that they will not pay so much and will go to the Byzantium amusement park in Vityazevo. It presents a large number of interesting attractions that will appeal not only to kids, but also to adults.

Fun for kids

Children's entertainment in Vityazevo is quite diverse. Sandy beaches have an even clean bottom, clear water and a shallow depth. You can walk several tens of meters before the sea begins to reach the level of the chest and shoulders of an adult.

The climate here is not traditional marine, but dry, which you can easily adapt to. A huge plus is that the sea and all beaches are well-groomed, as the village authorities very strictly follow this. In addition, all means for bathing children, awnings, umbrellas, sun loungers, sunbeds will be offered to vacationers at affordable prices. All kinds of children's slides, cars and trampolines are on every beach. There is even a beach kindergarten.

Experienced educators watch the kids. Cafes offer a special children's menu, in shops you can find many cheap things for a child. Guest houses are equipped with separate kitchens where fastidious housewives and mothers cook their own meals. You can visit with children two parks with exciting entertainment, a terrarium. More activities nearby

In Anapa, which is only 11 kilometers from Vityazevo, you can go to the water park, dolphinarium, oceanarium, nature reserve and penguinarium. All vacationers are invited to visit farms for growing ostriches and crocodiles.

Water activities





Wherever vacationers go, they are sure to take with them a lot of the most positive impressions and emotions. Athletes will enjoy beach soccer, volleyball, swimming. Fans of extreme sports will try their hand riding a "banana", catamarans and jet skis. Those who would not mind tasting strong drinks can head to the wine cellars. There they will be provided with the best Kuban wines for tasting. Small passenger ships will hospitably offer the pleasure of a boat trip to the cries of gulls. Almost everyone who chooses such a mini-trip manages to observe dolphins swimming and frolicking nearby.
