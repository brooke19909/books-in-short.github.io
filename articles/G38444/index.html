Kuchugury guest houses: an overview

A person is able to work for a long time, spend tremendous energy on the way to his goal. Part of life goes to hard and exhausting work. But the body is not an artificial mechanism, and from time to time it needs rest. One of the great ways to give the body peace of mind, and soul inspiration, is travel. Even regular trips to a not very long distance contribute to the increase of strength and relaxation.

It is even better if there is a sea near this place, because nothing is so exciting and fascinating as its quiet noise and breeze. Krasnodar Territory gives you the opportunity to experience all the delights of a holiday by the sea. Holiday

Just two hundred meters from the sea, in the suburbs is the guest house Holiday. This place opens its doors in early May and says goodbye to vacationers in September. The main building is represented by a two-story building, in which guests are waiting for comfortable and cozy rooms. Vacationers are invited to stay in rooms of different levels of comfort and price category.

Standard is a one-room room on the second floor, which has access to a balcony, a bathroom, beds (single and double), a dressing and coffee tables, a wardrobe, a mirror, a TV and a minibar. Also in Holiday there are two-room apartments, which are designed for four people, the room equipment is complemented by a sofa bed and another TV. All of them are quite spacious, and therefore for a certain amount there is the possibility of installing an extra bed. Kuchugury (guest houses and leisure opportunities in this place provide a wonderful pastime) fill the summer with bright emotions.

Lavrio

The village of Kuchugury, whose guest houses are very diverse, boasts Lavrio Guest House. The working hours of this place are from June to September. Often the choice falls precisely on Lavrio because of its convenient location: vacationers in just a few minutes can get to the beach, souvenir stall market, public transport, grocery and hardware stores. Accommodation in this guest house is made in rooms of two levels of comfort - standard and luxury.



The first is designed for two or three people and is equipped with beds, bedside tables, a combined wardrobe, a mirror, a table and chairs, a bathroom, a TV, a climate system. The second can be a temporary home for a family or company of four. The first room of the deluxe room has single beds, a TV, a chest of drawers and a mirror, the second has a double bed with bedside tables, a TV, a sofa, a wardrobe with a built-in refrigerator and a bathroom. Lavrio Guesthouse has a children's playground, a coffee bar and parking.

"Azov"

Guest house "Azovsky" (Kuchugury) every season replenishes the number of good reviews of its vacationers. It is located in the private sector of the village and only three hundred meters from the sea, allows you to enjoy its beauty and grandeur. The territory of the guest house is quite large: there are cottages, small gazebos that are braided with grape branches, a large number of flowers and fruit trees. At this place, both small families and friendly companies find great relaxation.

Cottages in the guest house "Azov" have different equipment and price. Vacationers can stay in two-, three- and four-bed houses, each of which has a separate kitchen and shower with hot and cold water. Also on site there is a cafe-bar for a fun evening, a playground for children and parking.

"Natalie"

In a beautiful area near the amusement park and the central beach is the guest house "Natalie", which opens the doors to vacationers and allows them to get a portion of excellent relaxation.



The main two-story building includes ten rooms with all amenities: refrigerators, satellite TV, bathrooms and split systems. In the summer, you can rent such a number for the amount of 1200 to 2400 rubles. The kitchen is located on the ground floor and is equipped with a sink, gas stoves, microwave, work desks. Near each room there are tables, chairs and benches. Also, vacationers can spend an evening in a cafe-bar, which is located near the "Natalie" (Kuchugury). Guest houses, prices for which can fluctuate in different frames, are an opportunity to forget about external problems and just relax to the sound of the sea.
