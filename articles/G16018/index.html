Children's rest in the camp "Diamond" in Ruza and Voronezh

Parents employed at work tend to send their child to the camp or sanatorium during the summer holidays. How to choose a camp that is ideal for both parents and baby?





Choosing a place for a child's summer vacation

Camp selection criteria:

  1. Cost. By the way, you can save on the price of a ticket by receiving compensation, or if the child wins it in the competition.
  2. Terms of delivery of the child to the place of rest. It is necessary to determine in advance whether the transfer is included in the price.
  3. The place where the camp is located. It is better if the camp is located in the same climatic zone where the child lives - then the adaptation period will not be required.
  4. Whether the child will be interested in the camp depends on the shift program.

Located in a picturesque forest near Voronezh on the banks of the Usmanka River, the Almaz children's camp hosts 4 shifts of 170 people each in the summer. Children from 5 years old are taken to the camp.

Almaz begins work on June 1, the cost of vouchers for 21 days in 2017 was 15,800 - 28,000 rubles.
