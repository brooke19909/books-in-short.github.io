The recreation center "Dubrava" (Nugush) - a great weekend break

The recreation center "Dubrava" (Nugush) is located in one of the most beautiful places in Bashkortostan. It is located on the shore of an artificial lake. This is the Nugush reservoir, surrounded by wonderful beaches and forest. The lake is located on the territory of the Bashkiria National Natural Park.

The nearest town to the base is Meleuz (50 km). From there you need to get to the village of Nugush. Then you should get to the lake and ride along its shore. If you are without a car, then you will get to Meleuz by regular bus. And then you have to get a taxi or a ride. This is a departmental base intended for recreation mainly by employees of the Federal Penitentiary Service of Russia. However, it also accepts ordinary tourists (subject to availability). In this article we will tell you about the conditions of rest at this base and the impressions of people who have been there.

Residence

The recreation center "Dubrava" (Nugush) offers tourists accommodation in wooden bungalows or cottages. Some of the rooms are "suites" equipped with all amenities. The houses are one- and two-story. They are designed for four and six people. Near the residential buildings are toilets, showers and access to the beach.

In total, there are almost forty different buildings intended for tourists on the territory of the base. The price for accommodation on weekdays (depending on the level of comfort) is 1000-1300 rubles per day per person, and on weekends - 1500-1800 rubles. Bed linens are provided. In the simplest house there are two bunk sound wooden beds, bedside tables and a refrigerator. Near the bungalow are arbors and litter bins with garbage bags.

The recreation center "Dubrava" (Nugush): reviews

Tourists are most often delighted with their stay in this boarding house. Clean air, beautiful landscapes and a sense of freedom - these advantages were highlighted by vacationers in their reviews. Many people really like the fortified wall near the pier, stylized as the Middle Ages. Therefore, in the photo of the recreation center "Dubrava" (Nugush) you can often see these beautiful towers.

It is allowed to jump from the pier. Many people choose a boat trip through the gorges among the mountains with parking lots (for swimming and relaxing) in quiet backwaters and mountain streams.
