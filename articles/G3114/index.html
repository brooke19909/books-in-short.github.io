Pehor textile is a good choice for hand and machine knitting

Pechora yarn has always been famous for its naturalness and quality. Products from this yarn are durable. Working with her is a pleasure. Soft and pleasant to the touch, multi-colored and beautiful, pehora yarn is used for knitting children's clothes, sweaters, dresses, wraps, hats, blouses, coats. It produces interesting and original products.

Types of pehor yarn

Pehor textile makes yarn from natural materials: cashmere, silk, alpaca, linen, cotton, wool.

Each of them has its own unique properties.

Linen threads allow you to be resistant to wear, durable and convenient to use. Of these, you can knit dresses, blouses that can be worn in hot weather. In linen products, a person does not sweat and feels comfortable and easy. Linen material is hypoallergenic, so it is suitable for people suffering from various types of allergies. It also has antibacterial and antifungal abilities.

Products associated with llama wool - alpaca, are very soft and light. From it you can knit capes, blouses, sweaters and much more.

Cashmere is made from the wool of mountain goats. It usually knits warm sweaters, hats, sweaters. You can tie a coat. The main property of cashmere is that it perfectly stores heat and protects from winds. Cashmere does not cause allergies.

Patterns from pehor yarn are embossed and voluminous. Knitting with yarn "Pehor Textile" is a dream for knitters. You can use both knitting needles and a hook. The hinges are easy to dial, do not interlock and gently knit. Pecherka products are hygroscopic and do not prick, which is especially important for young children.

The value of pehor yarn

Pehor textile is highly regarded among needlewomen, because it makes yarn of different colors and shades, structure and size. A huge selection of colors allows you to connect exactly what you want. It is produced using unique Italian technology. Products made of pechora yarn do not deform, and the color does not change, remains saturated and bright. The only drawback is that the product dries for a long time after washing.
