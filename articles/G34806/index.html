Kurgan region, "Bear Lake" (health resort)

In the summer, many have only one dream - to be on the lake under the sprawling trees and enjoy the peace, tranquility and surrounding beauty. Such moments restore strength, harmonize the inner world and give the opportunity to move on. It is for you in the Kurgan region that there is a stunningly beautiful, as if born from a legend, "Bear Lake". The resort receives its guests all year round, because every season is beautiful in its own way. Summer gives you the opportunity to sunbathe and swim, autumn gives a wonderful panorama of the crimson forests, and winter gives you the opportunity to temper and breathe transparent, ringing from purity air.
