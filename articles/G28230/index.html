Choose patterns of pumpkin dolls

We decided to start sewing exclusive interior heroes: elegant young ladies, cute bunnies and bears? In the article you will find ready-made patterns of pumpkin heads, as well as information on how to create patterns yourself.

Characteristics of Pumpkin Heads

Patterns of pumpkin-head dolls differ from other species in the number of parts for making the head. These heroes are characterized by a certain tailoring and, accordingly, the shape of the resulting element. The head is made round in the form of an even ball or with a convex nose, and the pattern of this part looks like a petal. Usually four or six pieces make such petals.



Materials and Tools

To make patterns of pumpkin head dolls, you need the following:

  • Thin paper (millimeter or any other).
  • Pencil.
  • Ruler.
  • Scissors.

You can also take a sample, template or photo of the finished product that you want to repeat. To sew an interior doll, you will need:

  • The fabric is beige or white for the head and torso.
  • Dyes to give a flesh-colored white fabric (tea, coffee can be used).
  • Patterns.
  • Fabric for the performance of clothing.
  • Pins
  • Tailor's chalk.
  • Scissors.
  • Needle with thread.
  • Sewing machine.
  • Filler (synthetic winterizer, holofiber, foam rubber).
  • Yarn for making doll hair.
  • Threads for embroidering faces or acrylic paints and PVA glue for drawing them.
  • Jewelry to create an image.

How to make a pattern yourself

First, decide how many individual elements you will sew your character. The body may consist of one part, or all parts are performed separately and then stitched together. The easiest way to make a head in the form of a ball is to use a piece of elastic nylon, for example, part of golf or tights. In this case, you will quickly make a pumpkin head doll. Pattern for the head is not required at all. To sew all the details from the cotton fabric, you will have to cut out the elements of the head, from which the ball (petals) or a voluminous part with a nose will be sewn.





With all the other parts, there will be no problems. They are easy to make.

How to sew a pumpkin head doll

Patterns, as you see, you can take ready-made or create yourself. Consider briefly the sequence of creating the product itself. So, the work goes as follows:

  1. Paper parts templates.
  2. Lay out the elements on the fabric optimally sparingly.
  3. Pin with pins.
  4. Circle the workpieces along the contour and draw seam allowances.
  5. Cut out all elements of the product.
  6. Stitch each part on the left side, while leaving a hole for turning out and stuffing.
  7. Turn out the stitched element, fill it with a synthetic winterizer or other filler, sew a hole.
  8. When all the elements of the doll are prepared, combine them into a single whole.
  9. Embroider or paint with pre-coated acrylics with PVA glue as a primer on your face.
  10. For human dolls, make a hairstyle, for example, from yarn of a suitable color.
  11. Sew separately removable clothing or sew non-removable items directly onto the body blank.
  12. If necessary, decorate with additional decor (tie ribbons, glue bows, beads, give a flower, a basket, etc. to the pens or paws).

The doll is ready to decorate the interior of your home or go to visit your relatives and friends.

As you can see, making patterns of pumpkin heads on their own is not at all difficult. So if you decide to sew an exclusive thing, you can not use a ready-made template, but develop it for yourself.
