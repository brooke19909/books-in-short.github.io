Hotel Dassia Margarita 2 * Corfu, Greece: reviews

Many citizens of our country plan their holidays in advance. The main attention should be paid to the choice of hotel. It depends on what impressions tourists bring home with them. Greece is one of the best countries for organizing a beach holiday. There are also many interesting sights. Inspecting the surroundings, you can immerse yourself in the local flavor.

One of the good options for a vacation can be Dassia Margarita 2 * . This hotel is known to tourists from our country. Reviews of people who have been here will be interesting to learn for everyone who plans to come to sunny Greece. This will allow us to conclude that it is advisable to purchase a tour to this beach resort.

general characteristics

Hotel Dassia Margarita 2 * is located on the picturesque coast of Corfu. It belongs to Greece. This 2-star hotel is located in Dassia. The distance from the center of the island to the hotel is only 3 km, which allows you to walk to it. The hotel was built close to the center of the village of Dassia, just 500 m.



The hotel was built in 1989 on the second coastline. The last time the restoration was carried out in 2006. The hotel is relatively close to the airport. It is 12 km from the hotel.

This small hotel is suitable for budget holidays. The clean beach, the well-kept territory of the hotel and the friendly staff allow you to have a good rest here for different categories of tourists. Traveling around the Greek island, you can learn a lot of new and interesting things. This will be a lot of impressions.

Hotel Specialization

Dassia Margarita Hotel 2 * (Corfu) is a small, quiet hotel. It is attractive for budget travelers. However, the quality of service, the organization of all the nuances of relaxation here are high. Despite the fact that the hotel is two-star, it can compete in comfort with many more famous resorts.



Within its walls, both couples and youth companies rest. You can relax with the child. However, the rooms do not have enough space to accommodate a large family. Therefore, this option is more suitable for small companies.

Beach holidays here are great. The warm sea, a large number of sunny days, stable weather attract many vacationers to this island. It is a quiet, cozy island. Walking on foot, cycling, as well as a romantic getaway here work best. This affects the main specialization of the tourist complex.

Cost

Tours from Moscow and other cities of our country to the hotel Dassia Margarita 2 *, Corfu (Corfu) are organized by various travel agencies. You can choose a suitable offer. This will organize the rest correctly. Some tourists travel on their own. However, at the same time it will be necessary to solve a lot of organizational issues. To fully enjoy your vacation, it is recommended to purchase the appropriate tour.

Price may vary significantly depending on the seasonality of the tour. Last minute packages are also sold at a good discount.

Rooms

Dassia Margarita Hotel 2 * offers to relax in one of 37 cozy rooms. 2 rooms can accommodate 1 person. The remaining 35 rooms have 2 beds. Each room has a balcony. It has a view of the garden, the hotel grounds.



The room has satellite TV, TV, air conditioning and telephone. There is also a small kitchen. It has a refrigerator. There is a kettle and kitchenware for making hot drinks. You can store valuables in a safe. It is rented for a fee.

The room has a bathroom. It includes a shower, toilet and washbasin. There is also a hairdryer. All toiletries must be taken with you. Towels, slippers and bathrobes are also not provided. The room is regularly cleaned. Bed linens are also changed. Room service is available.

Beach and food

Dassia Margarita 2 * (Corfu, Greece) does not have its own beach. The hotel is built on the second line from the coast. You can relax on the city beach. It is 500 meters from the tourist complex. The beach is sandy, quite clean. On the beach you can rent sunbeds, umbrellas for a fee.



The water here is clean and warm in season. There is enough space for all vacationers. If you wish, you can enjoy the sun and relax by the pool, which is built on site. There is a place for installing sunbeds. The sunbathing terrace is small, but its territory is enough to comfortably accommodate hotel guests.

Meals are organized on a buffet basis. Breakfast is served. There is a bar and restaurant on site. It serves international and local cuisine. There are also restaurants and cafes in the center. You can go for a walk here.

Service

Dassia Margarita 2 * (Corfu) offers various services for its visitors. The service here is well organized. Reception is open around the clock. Check-in is at 14:00. Leaving the room on the day of departure will be required at 11:00. There are rooms for people with disabilities.



Free parking is available on site. There is a currency exchange, as well as a laundry service. Safe at the reception paid. Wireless internet is available on site. Bike rental is also available. They can be stored in a special room. Payment is made both in cash and non-cash.

In general, the hotel is designed for a relaxing pastime. Bicycle and car rental will allow you to explore the surroundings. It has a special atmosphere. Greece on the island of Corfu is revealed in all its glory. There is where to walk, what to see.

Entertainment

Reviews about Dassia Margarita 2 * indicate the presence of an insufficiently wide range of entertainment for guests on site. There is an outdoor pool, near which tourists rarely relax. The water in it is clean, the area around is well-groomed.



There is a medical office on site. There is also a lounge. You can play golf. Only bicycles can be rented out of sports equipment.

But outside the hotel you can see a lot of attractions. Many local plants and trees grow around. Olives fascinate local tourists. The beach is very beautiful. It is located in a picturesque bay. There are various activities for adults and children on the beach. There is also a children's playground on site. However, it is much more interesting to go for a walk in the center of the village or the island itself.

Entertainment outside the hotel

Dassia Margarita 2 * has a quiet, well-groomed territory. For those visitors to the resort who like to travel, want to get new experiences, it is recommended to go to the center of the village. You can walk or rent a bicycle, car.

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many different attractions. In total, about 50 architectural monuments can be seen on the island. There is a city park. There are 5 exhibitions and museums on the island. The traffic intersection is convenient, which makes it easy to move around the island.

There are bars, restaurants, shopping centers near the hotel. Here you can also buy souvenirs in memory of the rest. In total, 11 beaches have been created on the island. You can go for a walk in the afternoon or evening. A special, unique atmosphere reigns here. Many people remember this vacation as measured, but interesting.

Negative reviews

Almost always reviews about Dassia Margarita 2 * (Corfu) are positive. However, there are a small number of negative statements. Their total weight is about 5%.

This hotel cannot provide a high level of comfort for its guests. The rooms have poor drainage. It may even smell sewage. There are also many mosquitoes on the island. They do not let you sleep at night, walk along the street in the evening. Therefore, you must take funds from mosquitoes with you.

Doors slam loudly. Some rooms do not have a handle on the back. Therefore, guests are forced to slam the door. The audibility of the hotel is also high. You can hear the corridors being cleaned early in the morning. It is recommended to choose rooms with odd numbers. There is no smell of sewage.

Positive reviews

Mostly reviews about Dassia Margarita 2 * positive. Many guests claim that they were not looking for enhanced service in this vacation. Undemanding tourists, this hotel is quite suitable. Such vacationers spend most of the time on the beach, in the center of the island.

The staff at the hotel is friendly, ready at any time to resolve issues arising from guests. There is a quick check-in. In some cases, this is possible even before 14:00. Vacationers even noted that they were fed free of charge in anticipation of the settlement.

People come here not to spend most of their time on site. People here mostly only spend the night. The food is varied. Breakfast includes light but nutritious and wholesome meals. In general, this vacation is remembered by tourists as a positive experience.

A romantic, relaxing holiday here works best. Hiking, cycling, local and international cuisine, entertainment for various categories of tourists will not leave anyone indifferent. Outside the hotel there are many attractions. You can go shopping, shopping centers. Beautiful nature, picturesque bays and a unique Greek flavor allow you to relax, gain vigor for the whole next working year.

Having considered the features of rest at the Dassia Margarita hotel , one can note a decent service. For the undemanding tourist, all conditions for recreation are created here. People come here to see the beauties of Corfu. Being at the hotel all the time will be uninteresting and inappropriate.
