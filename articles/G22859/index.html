How to remove a graphic key: methods and tips

What if you forget the unlock pattern on your Android phone? Resetting to the factory settings is undesirable, since for sure you have very important data that cannot be lost. How to remove the pattern on Android without data loss?

If you're lucky, you can remember him after several attempts, relying on the memory of his hands. But what if you absolutely do not know how to enter it? Is there a way to remove the pattern key without losing data? There are several methods to unlock Android without resetting to factory settings.

Solution # 1: try using ADB (Android Debug Bridge)

The method only works if USB debugging was enabled on your Android phone or tablet before you forgot your password. In addition, it is required that you allow and trust the computer with the installed program before trying to reset the key on the device. Even worse, this method will not necessarily work for devices with encryption enabled. However, if you are lucky and your settings meet these requirements, you can easily unlock the password. How to remove the pattern from Android in this way? This is done like this:

  • Connect your Android phone to your computer via USB.
  • Open a terminal window in the ADB installation directory on the computer.
  • Enter the following command: adb shell rm /data/system/gesture.key.
  • Restart your Android device and you will see that the secure lock screen has temporarily disappeared.

Set a new key (PIN or password) before restarting the phone again. Otherwise, your device will be blocked again.
