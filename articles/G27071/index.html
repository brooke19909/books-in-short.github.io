Full board, or Full board

Novice travelers, buying permits for resorts, are wondering what does full board mean. This is one of the five main forms of food in hotels. Experienced tourists do not always prefer full board, although at first glance this form is quite attractive. How cost-effective this is for specific travelers is worth a look. In general, in the hotel industry, the guest house means a form of food, which is included in the price. Each type of food in resort hotels has an abbreviation. These are international designations that will be relevant in any country, wherever you go.





Forms of food in hotels

Firstly, you can refuse power, that is, use only the number (OB or RO). Secondly, you can take only breakfast (BB), which, as a rule, is a buffet with hot and cold dishes (in different countries the dishes are very different from each other). Thirdly, half board, which can be from breakfast and lunch or from breakfast and dinner (HB). Fourth, meals are full board, that is, you take three meals a day (FB). The main meals of the form of nutrition "full board"

This service involves three meals a day: breakfast, lunch and dinner. At breakfast, vacationers receive hot coffee or tea, in some hotels also juice. But at lunch and dinner, drinks are supposed to be paid separately. There is an extended full board option when soft drinks are included in the diet, but these are rare cases. Most often there are jugs of water on the tables, the rest must be paid extra. Full board with 4 meals a day is available. All these details must be clarified by a tour operator who sells you a ticket. In addition, it is worth knowing that, as a rule, hotels are not supposed to bring food and drinks so that vacationers buy them at local establishments. Of course, they will not search you, therefore, just do not advertise your actions.





Forms of service in hotels

Service in various hotels involves two forms: a buffet and service by waiters. It is impossible to choose them, since this is the accepted order of service in a particular hotel. The most common "buffet", especially in the resorts of Turkey and Egypt. Vacationers serve themselves. Dishes stand on one large table on large plates and trays. Everyone comes up and imposes on himself as much as he wants. Drinks can be served either right there or at the bar. The second option is more characteristic of European hotels. In this case, the visitors are served by the waiter, but according to the established menu. But it is worth noting that there are always several positions, that is, there is a choice.

When to Choose a Full Board

This form of food should be chosen if you want to spend your vacation passively, but are not interested in drinks from hotels, that is, you want to walk to local restaurants and bars on your own or do not drink alcohol. Three meals a day relieves you of money spent on food. If you plan to go on excursions, as well as independently travel around the district, it is better to choose only breakfast or half board. And during trips and trips you will independently choose an institution where you would like to try one or another dish of one of the cuisines of the world. Full board will be relevant if you are planning a beach holiday near the hotel without excursions.
