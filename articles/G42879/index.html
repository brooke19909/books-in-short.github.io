Saving virtues

The manor was so good that in the troubled year of 1918 it was not burned and plundered, but a state museum was created on its territory. Thanks to the responsible attitude of the authorities, in the Ostankino Palace even now you can hear the repertoire of the famous Sheremetyevo serf theater and see the authentic interiors of those times. This is the only theater building in Russia that has been preserved in its entirety: stage, hall, dressing rooms, engine room, etc.
