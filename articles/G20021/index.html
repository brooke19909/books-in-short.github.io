How to make money on HYIPs are the secrets of success. Features of HYIP projects

High Yield Investment Program (HYIP) is a high-yield investment option. It is most successful for those who want to make a profit, investing available money and not wasting time in vain. One of the basic rules for working on such a system is package diversification. Observing the key aspects of a profitable investment, through HYIP you can earn more than decent amounts. But neglect of the rules leads to the loss of everything invested.

Basic rules

As world practice and HYIP Monitoring show, the best results are achievable if a person simultaneously chooses ten, or even twenty projects, between which he distributes his finances. By combining and sharing funds, you can guarantee the stability of financial income. Some of the selected projects will probably be sources of loss. Pros due to others will help offset these disadvantages.

In order for the earnings on HYIP projects to be reliable and high, you need to be able to notice such a system in time and become a member of it. Only early entry is the only opportunity that allows you to achieve a good result. The sooner you managed to become a member, the more likely it is to successfully profit.

Types and money

Several varieties of hypes are known. The division into categories is determined by the level of profit. The classification according to the degree of return on high, medium, low is accepted. The higher the level of potential profit, the greater the risk inherent in such a project. It is much easier to make money if you select a project with a profitability of small or medium degree. However, do not underestimate the risks. Even with a very small level of promised profits, you can be in the red. The higher the likelihood of such an outcome, the later the investor enters the work. Every project like HYIP has several stages of development. The first is an investment that provides a payback. Then the project reaches the stage of growth, after which it passes to the stage of satiation.

First, the creators of hype offer those interested a new, yet promising, but completely unclear project, for which excellent opportunities may open up in the future - or may not appear. If a potential investor invests money at this stage, he thereby provides himself with the maximum level of support. First deposits, first investors - that is fundamentally important for any undertaking such as HYIP. After investing the first funds, the project "gets on its feet", acquires stability, becomes popular and in demand. Constantly adding new and new members who bring their money.

Growth and regression

HYIP development and the first two stages of the development of such a project can last months, sometimes even years. Do not count on the continued existence and successful operation of the system. Sooner or later, a period of decline begins, always fatal for HYIP. This is the time when new infusions become rarer. The program, which previously had huge cash reserves, is getting poorer. The administration, analyzing the situation, decides: it is time to close the enterprise.

An investor who appreciated a new hype and entered it at the very dawn of the project has a maximum of opportunities to get good money. It is necessary to become a participant when the system is just beginning to develop, when stability looms ahead, although achievable, but in the future. In this case, you can earn a decent amount and quickly exit the system, as soon as the decline becomes critically close.

Strategies and Mistakes

Some not very successful investors cannot find new hypes on time. They prefer to enter such projects late, constantly delay the moment of depositing money, and then the moment of withdrawal. This is a classic mistake that leads to the loss of everything invested. Almost all newcomers to the HYIP theme do it. Having made a mistake once, twice, third, without realizing what the mistake was, such people begin to spread the belief that HYIP is nothing more than a hoax. Many, taught by their own bitter experience, convince: it is impossible to find a HYIP, from which it is really possible to make a profit.

As you can conclude from the description of the experience of those to whom the promotion of hype gave only financial losses, basically such people initially look for a very long time, choosing the optimal direction for investing. Such persons seek maximum information about the program they are interested in, literally break through the virtual web, trying to find the feedback of those who have already taken part in the system. Only after discovering such opinions and stories, they decide to invest. At this point, the system is already reaching a stable level - and this is only the best option. It is more likely that it will already be in a decline phase. As can be seen from the above, the first prospects of work are most promising, that is, the time when no stories about a specific HYIP still exist. According to experienced investors, if you are interested in HYIP, you need to understand: just an hour of delay can already change the situation. Every hour of the existence of the project makes it closer to the time of death.

To find out on your own what the possible profitability of the project is, how much you can get by working with HYIP, how to implement it, you need to be up to date with the latest news from the investment market. There are specialized news publications in which informational bulletins are published about all new projects and opportunities that have appeared on the market. Tracking them 24/7, you can find the new HYIP in time and become a member at the moment when it is as promising as possible.

Payback

Translation high (height, high) hints at the possibility of making good profits. The moment when the investment is fully returned to the investor is called the payback day. Such a strategy minimizes the risks of significant losses. If HYIP collapses after reaching a breakeven point, the investor avoids losses. If it was not possible to withdraw the profit and all the earnings lost, this is estimated as the loss of someone else's money, so it is not taken into account.

When choosing a system for calculating the payback moment, you need to pay attention to whether the profit received is automatically added to the deposit or not.

Counting

If there is no calculator at hand, you can quickly calculate on your own when the HYIP project will pay off. Suppose a system promises a rate of return of 30% per month. Accordingly, the hundredth day of participation will be a payback moment. On this day, you need to withdraw from the system the entire amount that was invested at the beginning of the work.

Such people admit: working responsibly, they earn good profits without much difficulty. Choosing how to work, it is worth focusing on the responses of just such experienced investors.

On examples

Consider how and how much you can get, guided by the described rules of how to make money on HYIPs. Imagine an investor has 200 dollars in stock. He found a start-up project whose interest rate is one percent daily. Suppose the system does not support automatic reinvestment. In the first month, you can earn $ 60. The same will be the profit in the second and third month. After another 10 days, you can get $ 20. In just one hundred days, the participant will become the owner of earnings of $ 200. At this point, you need to withdraw the money invested in the system. Having reached the payback point, having regained the body of the deposit, you can continue to work without much panic.

The break-even point is reachable on the 167th day if HYIP offers the participant a profit of 0.6% daily. For 0.7%, this time period reaches 142 days, for 0.8% 125 days. For 0.9%, it is estimated at 111 days. If profit is more than one-tenth of a percent daily, break-even is achievable by the 91st day. For 1.2%, it is estimated at 83 days, and for 1.3%, the payback period is 77 days. When learning how to make money on hypes, you need to remember: the best results are provided to those who entered the system when it was first created. You do not need to invest anything else until you manage to return the initial funds. Some systems support the refback service. It allows you to reduce the payback period by 10 days, sometimes even more.

If you managed to successfully reach the payback point, you need to analyze how healthy HYIP is on this day, and how promising it is, how actively it is moving towards the time of death. Money: yesterday, today, tomorrow

In order for HYIP to be the source of the maximum financial advantage for a person with a little risk, it is often necessary to derive profit. This is the third key rule of anyone interested in working with HYIP. Profit should be withdrawn as often as possible. The project may seem promising, reliable and stable, from the point of view of the investor, but no one with absolute accuracy will predict what will happen in the next minute. Even administrators, creators, cannot say exactly what awaits HYIP after a short time. Even if the investor has very small reserves of free time, he should allocate several minutes daily to withdraw the funds received. Some do this several times a day, others do it once a day. According to experienced investors who have worked a lot with HYIP, withdrawing profits less than once a day is fraught with negative consequences.

Usually withdrawing money from HYIP requires only a few minutes - no more than half an hour. Most have a ten-minute time period for which you can estimate how much you managed to earn and withdraw this money. You need to visit the investor‚Äôs personal account in the project and fill out an application for withdrawing money. Experienced investors can share stories of the loss of tens of thousands of dollars due to the lack of a timely application for withdrawal of money.

Strategies

There are several options. One of the most famous is Hit and Run. The main idea is short-term investments of large sums in the project. The investor withdraws all his profits, receives a deposit back, as soon as possible under the terms of tariffs. When choosing options, they analyze HYIP, study different tariffs, determine which one is the shortest. It is under such a program that funds are introduced from the system. As soon as this period expires, a person pulls out everything that is possible from HYIP and sets off in search of a new promising option. There is no re-investment opportunity for such a system. Once again, nothing is invested in the same system. No profit is left in the account. The essence is in the introduction and the most rapid withdrawal of money. This strategy is optimal if the attention was drawn to high-yield HYIP. It is effective if you start cooperation, when the project has just started, is still untwisted.

The second popular option is a profitable investment. It is somewhat similar to the previously described. As soon as the opportunity arises, the participant completely returns the main deposit. Profit earned while working with the HYIP project is left inside. It will be a source of further financial advantage. Such a strategy is considered by many to be the most competent and associated with a minimum of risks. It suits most potential investors. The system allows you to minimize the risk of your own money, which is withdrawn as soon as possible. True, there are disadvantages. For example, profits are reduced as a person takes back a deposit, storing only the pluses received in HYIP.
