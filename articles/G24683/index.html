Traveling in the Leningrad region, many tourists call on the city of Kingisepp to admire the majestic architecture of the five-domed Catherine Cathedral. Therefore, the urgent question is where you can spend the night inexpensively. You can choose a hostel in Kingisepp, where the necessary amenities are created for you at an affordable price.

Hotel Rus-Kingisepp

There is a supermarket within walking distance of the hotel, and the Luga River is just 500 meters away. Free private parking is available here for car owners. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi. There is a shared kitchen with an electric kettle, microwave, fridge and washing machine. There is also a dining area. Lockers are provided. A continental breakfast is served in the Rus Kingisepp Hostel's lounge. Luggage storage is also available, as well as a laptop safe. Upon request, an airport shuttle can be arranged for you. The hotel has a hairdresser. Reception is open 24 hours a day. To accommodate guests, several options are offered:

  • general ten-bed room for men and women - 500 rubles per day per person;
  • common eight-bed room - 550 rubles per day per person;
  • general six-bedded room - 600 rubles per person.

Room rates include breakfast. All rooms are equipped with bunk beds with bedside rugs. Each place has a separate curtain. Mosquito nets are installed on the windows. Complimentary toiletries are provided. The hostel is located in Kingisepp at the address: 2-ya Liniya Street, 64 Building.
