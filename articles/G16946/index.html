Hotel Turtle Beach Resort 4 *, India, Goa: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Goa is a piece of paradise located on the sparkling waters of the Arabian Sea and attracting more than 2 million vacationers each year. Pristine sands, swaying palm trees, breathtaking sunsets, laid-back life, affordable fruits and seafood - all this makes you fall in love with the resort once and for all.

Goa offers many types of housing: from bungalows to rental houses for tourists, but the most popular lodging is hotels. One of them opened in February 2015 and is called Turtle Beach Resort Morjim 4 *. Now more!

Title

The Treebo concept is quality housing at affordable prices.
Hotel Turtle Beach Resort Morjim 4 * is located in India, Goa, on the beach of Morjim, which is famous for sea turtles, sailing here from November to February in order to lay eggs. Therefore, the second name of the beach is Turtle beach (turtle beach), the hotel is named similarly.

general information

The hotel is located in North Goa (Morjim). The distance to the beach area is 700 meters, to the Dabolim airport - 55 kilometers, to the nearest railway station Karmali - 15 kilometers.



The hotel area is small - three buildings on four floors, as well as:

  • pool with free sun loungers;
  • tables and free board games;
  • room with table tennis;
  • gym with a treadmill;
  • free parking;
  • 24-hour reception;
  • two restaurants with an extensive menu that includes dishes of different cuisines;
  • two bars;
  • garden;
  • conference room for 50 people.

Rooms

The hotel provides rooms of three categories: standard, deluxe and premium. All are air-conditioned. Buffet breakfasts and daily housekeeping, as well as Wi-Fi, are included. Rooms can be rented via the Internet, travel agency or directly arriving at the hotel, but in the latter case there is a risk that there will be no free rooms.

Standard rooms are fully equipped with all necessary amenities, which are a guarantee of quality from Treebo. Clean and comfortable beds with duvets, tea and coffee, satellite TV, personal hygiene products. Cozy and well-equipped rooms will delight any traveler.
Price per night - 4099 rupees.



Deluxe rooms are larger than standard. All services that are offered in standard rooms are included in deluxe. There is also a safe, windows overlook the pool. Price per night - 4599 rupees.

Premium rooms are the largest in size, they have a guest area with leather armchairs. The price per night for such a room in the Turtle Beach Resort 4 * hotel (India, Goa) is 5099 rupees.

In addition, a refrigerator is provided for rent, the cost per day is 200 rupees. Laundry facilities are also available.

Interior

The interior of the hotel is made in a modern style. Bright rooms, there are elements of wood on the walls. The rooms are minimally furnished, not overloaded with excess furniture, create the impression of coziness and comfort. But there is one significant minus - the functionality is not thought out: there are few shelves and surfaces for storing things.





The territory is landscaped. Facades of buildings and surrounding areas, like rooms, are designed in light colors: white tables, deckchairs made of light wood.

Wooden elements are an integral part of the design in connection with the concept of the hotel. Beach

The sandy beach of Morjim is located 700 meters from the hotel - 5-7 minutes walk through the courtyards or 2 minutes by scooter. It is also commonly called ‚ÄúIndian Rublevka‚Äù because of the large number of Russian tourists and high prices for food, hotels and souvenirs. Excursions

From the hotel Turtle Beach Resort 4 * (Goa) you can go on many exciting excursions.

One of the main attractions of Morjim beach is the turtle beach. You need to visit it from November to February, this is the period of time when olive turtles come here . Unfortunately, this species is considered endangered, and therefore is under the protection of the government, therefore it is forbidden to approach and cross the protected area, you can only watch from afar, but it's worth it.

We also advise you not to spare money and go on an excursion to the Dudhsagar Falls, which will certainly be offered upon arrival at the hotel. The tour takes a whole day, the program includes: visiting a waterfall, bathing with an elephant and an interesting excursion to a spice plantation, where they will not only tell and show a lot of interesting things, but also provide a buffet, treat local moonshine with fennel made from cashew nuts.



Adventure lovers will always be happy to offer a trip to Hampi, a village located among the ruins of Vijayanagara. There you can watch a beautiful sunrise and sunset, as well as visit the many temples preserved from ancient times.
