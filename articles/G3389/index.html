Holiday Village "Anchor" (Nizhnekamsk): description, services, reviews

The recreation center "Anchor" (Nizhnekamsk) is a wonderful place located in the ecologically clean region of the Republic of Tatarstan on the banks of the river Pros. The leaders of the camp site made every effort so that every vacationer was cozy and comfortable. The invigorating air, the expanses of the river, the unique picturesque nature in combination with high-quality service and developed infrastructure will give you a boost of vital energy and a lot of positive emotions.





Description

Comfortable accommodation in log-furnished comfortable houses, a country bathhouse, a cozy restaurant with a wide selection of dishes of Russian and national cuisine - all this is Ankor recreation center. Nizhnekamsk has always fascinated vacationers with its unique nature and clean air, but not all camp sites can organize a vacation with the proper service. Here, at your service are several warm houses with all conveniences with two, six and twelve-bed accommodation, which are equipped with a kitchenette with the necessary utensils and appliances, satellite TV, modern furniture, a shower and a toilet.





The bathhouse includes a spacious relaxation room, fireplace, satellite TV, there is a veranda with access to the lake. In winter, you can plunge into the hole.

For large companies on the river bank there is a huge tent with wind protection, which can accommodate 150 people, there is a summer cafe and a dance floor.

For lovers of swimming and sunbathing - a well-maintained clean beach with a sandy beach.

The management of the tourist base also took care of outdoor enthusiasts, especially for them there is paintball, a sports field, various water activities (catamarans, banana, cheesecakes), you can rent buggies, and in winter time skates, skis, snowmobiles and snowmobiles are available for hire. .

Fishermen will be delighted with fishing, animal lovers will appreciate their own stable.

For trainings and business meetings there is an equipped conference room.

Services

The recreation center in Nizhnekamsk "Anchor" is ready to offer vacationers many services:

  • protected area;
  • fishing organization;
  • horseback riding;
  • rental of catamarans, boats, bicycles, skis and skates;
  • a restaurant;
  • sauna and country bath;
  • billiards, paintball, shooting gallery;
  • sport games;
  • seminars, banquets, weddings, anniversaries and corporate parties (with individual entertainment program).




About company

Address: recreation center "Anchor", Nizhnekamsk, village Boroksky Cordon (near the B / O "Dubravushka").

The organization carries out its work all year round, on weekdays from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Saturday and Sunday is a day off.

The capacity of the houses is 65 people. The cost per day varies from 1000 to 7 500 rubles. You can pay in cash or by credit card.

Guest reviews

The recreation center "Anchor" (Nizhnekamsk) has extremely positive reviews . Vacationers really like a clean beach, convenient entry into the river, children are completely delighted with water rides and horses.

Guests who managed to attend a banquet here are satisfied with the quality of service, the organization of the festival, the cozy atmosphere of the restaurant. They express gratitude to the cooks for their quality work, excellent cooking.





On the official website of the recreation center you can find many positive reviews about the houses, baths, paintball. People who have been here at least once dream of returning again. Anchor is a great vacation, polite and caring staff, high-quality service and, of course, magnificent nature.
