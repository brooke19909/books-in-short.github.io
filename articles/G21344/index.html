New money in Russia (photo)

In the near future, Russia will acquire new bills in denominations of 200 and 2 thousand rubles. This news was announced on April 12, 2016 on the official website of the Central Bank. But it will not be banknotes prepared for the Olympics in Sochi, but full-fledged monetary units, which will now be able to use the citizens of Russia.

The Central Bank has not issued new money since 2001, but now this resonant decision has been made, which should benefit people by facilitating all financial transactions conducted with cash. Often there were complaints from citizens that the calculation using existing bills is inconvenient.





Why do we need new bills?

The answer to this question was given by a political and statesman - Elvira Nabiullina, who said that the introduction of new banknote values ‚Äã‚Äãshould significantly facilitate the calculations of citizens of the whole country. The fact is that most operations are carried out in the ranges from 100 to 500 rubles, as well as from 1000 to 5000 rubles. Now, intermediate values ‚Äã‚Äãwill help to settle, which will make payments more simple and understandable.

At first, the regulator could not decide whether to choose a value of 200 and 2 thousand rubles, or 300 and 3 thousand rubles. Most likely, the decision was made based on the worldwide trend, because in many currencies there is a deuce: 2 dollars, 200 euros, 200 hryvnias and so on.

How inflation and the release of new money are related

So far, Nabiullina believes that a reasonable issue of currency can accept a situation in which inflation does not rise above six percent. So far, the analysis revealed that the Bank of Russia is doing everything possible to reduce inflation to four percent by the end of 2017. Until recently, the key rate on loans was 11 percent, at the moment this number has fallen by 0.5 percent, that is, the rate has become 10.5 percent. This is due to the fact that a monetary policy is being pursued, which should also positively affect the value of the currency.





At the moment, in economic terms, Russia is starting to stabilize, which can be seen in the dynamics of GDP in the first quarter of 2016. This is partly achieved by reducing fluctuations in oil prices. The Bank of Russia also confirms these statements, adding that positive trends will not go side by side with increasing inflation.

All this is aimed at discouraging the consumer ability of citizens, because this is how it turns out to slow down price increases to a minimum. The head of the regulator added that the issue will not affect the money supply in any way, leaving it the same, because shabby banknotes will go out of circulation and will be replaced by new banknotes. Thus, it will be possible to achieve that the issued money does not require additional costs.

Is this information true?

Absolutely. All available printing presses that are ready to go will be launched. In fact, all the machines are working now, because at the moment the budget deficit is covered thanks to the available funds in the Reserve Fund.

It also organizes the creation of a financial pillow that can protect the entire Central Bank from collapse. Foreign currency is purchased from the Ministry of Finance. The whole operation pays off thanks to the release of new money.

At the moment, equity financing is only in small amounts, as Oleg Vyugin put it. Now the deficit is being financed from the Reserve Fund, but in fact - the issue. So far, the Central Bank cannot sell enough foreign exchange reserves received from the fund.





Note Design

New money will be in the hands of Russian citizens as early as 2017, but until this time the design will remain uncertain. It is necessary to conduct a vote, according to the results of which the most suitable ‚Äúperson‚Äù will be selected for new banknotes.

Logically, then the new banknotes, as well as the old ones, should use the symbols of the Russian regions. That's what the Central Bank wants to do, said Elvira Nabiullina. So the design will remain roughly general, so the new money will be able to fit confidently into the existing picture.

In the summer of this year, 49 cities were selected that may appear on new banknotes. At the moment, about a million Russians took part in the vote, for whom the design of new money turned out to be important and interesting.

Each character had to collect more than five thousand votes in order to pass to the second stage, in which the winner will also be selected by popular vote. That is why it will not be possible to find a photo with new money until the vote ends.
