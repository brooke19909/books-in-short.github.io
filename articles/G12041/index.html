Stylish convertible vest with knitting needles. Schemes and Description

Increasingly, in stores and fashion magazines you can see the word "transformer". A transformer can be a dress that has many variations of wearing, harem pants, a scarf pipe that turns into a bolero, or a vest. Such things are original and multifunctional. But such clothes can not only be purchased in stores, but also made with your own hands, for example, knit. Let us consider in more detail how to knit a transformer vest with knitting needles (diagrams and description are attached).





How to wear?

Why is such a vest called a transformer? The fact is that in addition to the usual vest, this thing can be worn in many ways. For example, like a scarf-bactus, draping it around the neck, or like a shawl, throwing it over your shoulders.

A transformer vest (you can see a photo of it in the article) will look great in several wearing options:

  • Like a regular elongated asymmetric vest, without a fastener, with flowing sides.
  • Like a shortened sleeveless jacket, if you tightly wrap the edges around the waist and tie it in a knot.
  • Like an elongated sleeveless jacket, if you fix one edge on the opposite shoulder and stab it with a brooch.

If you execute the model in two colors, the product can be draped around the face in different shades.





Selection of yarn and tools

What yarn to knit a transformer vest with knitting needles? Schemes and descriptions usually recommend a specific yarn for each particular model, but in fact you can safely experiment.

Thick woolen yarn is perfect for a winter product. It is very warm, the thing will look stylish, but then the edges of the vest will not flow as if the product was made of thinner yarn. Although there is a way out of this situation: if you prefer bulky yarn, choose knitting needles one or two sizes larger than recommended, then the transformer vest will be not only warm, but soft and airy.

For a stylish accessory for spring and early autumn, thin woolen or thicker cotton yarn is suitable. A thread with lurex or small sequins will look very beautiful. The product can be connected with an openwork pattern, then it can be worn in the summer.

Pattern selection

Such a complex and original thing as a transformer vest, knitted with needles, it is not necessary to overload it with a complex pattern:

  1. For thick bulky yarn, especially if it is melange yarn, the garter stitch is perfect - this is when in each row all the loops are knitted face.
  2. Simple pearl knitting also looks beautiful. In the first row, alternate one front and one wrong loop, and in the second row, each front knit the wrong loop, and vice versa.

These patterns are good in that they are two-sided, so no matter how you tie the transformer vest, it will look neat.

If a simple monotonous knitting is for you, then you can decorate the product with braids, aranes or lace. The image shows a diagram of a simple but interesting pattern with interweaving. The pattern repeat is seven loops and twenty seven rows.





Pattern and description

Consider how to knit a scarf vest (transformer) with knitting needles (diagrams and description will be further). Sectional dyeing yarn consisting of wool, mohair and acrylic or polyester (about 400 g in size 36-42) is suitable for work. Knitting needles are those recommended by the manufacturer of yarn.

A knitted transformer vest with knitting needles (diagrams and a description, as well as a pattern confirm this) is a triangle with slots-armholes in the middle.





  1. Let's get started with a set of seven loops.
  2. Next, knit five rows of garter stitch.
  3. From the sixth row, start adding one loop on both sides in every second row. You can tie the entire product with a garter stitch or alternate it with a facial or pearl stitch.
  4. Additions can be made from a broach or by knitting two from the edge loop (one for the back wall, the other for the front).
  5. After 90 cm from the start of work, leave room for armholes. Divide the fabric in half, after 16 cm from the middle, close several loops for the armholes and continue to knit in the opposite direction, not forgetting the addition on one side of the product. After knit the middle part, and then the third part. Having knitted the height of the armhole, again work on one canvas. After 20-30 cm, close the loop.

A stylish knitted transformer vest with knitting needles, the schemes and description for which we examined are ready! Wear it over a shirt, turtleneck, or dress. It looks great both with strict office things, and with everyday and even romantic ones.
