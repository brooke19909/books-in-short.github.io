Duration of maternity leave

The amount of benefits is calculated taking into account the number of days spent on maternity leave. On the basis of an order issued by the Ministry of Health and Social Development, paid leave is:

  • In the case of a single pregnancy, one hundred and forty days.
  • In the presence of complicated one-year pregnancy - up to one hundred fifty-six days.
  • Against the background of preterm birth (from the twenty second to the thirty week) - one hundred fifty six days.
  • With the adoption of a baby up to three months - one hundred and ten days.
  • In the case of multiple bearing - one hundred ninety-four days.

The basis for accrual is a disability certificate, which can be taken at the antenatal clinic, starting from the thirtieth week of pregnancy (from the twenty-eighth - with a multiple form).
