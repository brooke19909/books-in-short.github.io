An interesting men's cardigan with knitting needles: diagram, photo, description

With the onset of autumn and a quick cooling, knitting patterns for men's jumpers become extremely relevant for many knitters. Trying to choose the right model and pattern, you can easily get confused. Especially if the one for whom the entire project is being started cannot clearly articulate his requirements. In this case, it is best (to save your time and nerves) to offer the man several simple options for cutting a sweater, choosing universal color schemes.



He can agree to a classic men's knit cardigan (the scheme of which will be quite simple for a knitter).

Yarn: colors, composition, thickness

It's no secret that many men prefer conservatism when it comes to choosing clothes. Of course, there are nonconformists who like light green jeans and bright pink shirts, but now we will not talk about them.

In this article, two models of simple but very interesting jumpers will be considered. Each of them can suit a man of almost any age: beloved, father, younger brother. In addition, these things will be very practical, since they can be worn at work, at school, and in bowling.

It will take at least 800 grams of yarn to knit a men's cardigan with knitting needles. The schemes are designed for threads of medium thickness (about 250-350 m / 100 grams). Some men, even in winter, prefer exclusively cotton products, since they are hot in woolen ones. This is especially true if they are the happy owners of quality winter jackets.

Getting to work

First of all, you need to bind a control sample. This is an obligatory step for all craftswomen who want to make a man's jumper with knitting needles the first time and without blooming. The pattern scheme is important for the size of the paintings: if there are harnesses, they must be present on the control sample. Otherwise, the finished product will be less than planned.

Scythe - timeless classic

Making a collar

The designer used a fairly common and beloved type of collar to design this men's cardigan with knitting needles (schemes for this part are needed).

The collar is made in the following way:

  1. Loop calculation is done.
  2. The required number of elements is drawn on circular or straight knitting needles.
  3. A fabric with a height of 8-10 cm is knitted. Almost any elastic band (1: 1, 2: 2, 3: 3, French, English and others) can serve as a pattern. The peculiarity of this detail is that its edges do not have to be even: they are consistently expanded and then narrowed. The addition of loops occurs in each front row from two sides (two loops on one row). When the collar is half knitted, you need to start reducing the number of elements. This is done in the same way as before this addition. As a result, when the part is folded in half and sewn to the roll-out of the neck, a collar of the desired shape will be obtained.
  4. When fixing parts folded in half to roll out, its edges are superimposed on each other.

The finished product can only be steamed with steam from the iron and presented to the happy future owner.
