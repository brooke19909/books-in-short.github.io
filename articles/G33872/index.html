Moscow, water park "Aqua-Yuna": photos, prices and reviews

Summer is all year round, there is a lot of warmth and light, waterfalls and splashes of water from all sides, people in swimsuits and with smiles on their faces, the sounds of laughter, fun and joy - all this is a real picture, and you can not enjoy it anywhere on exotic islands, but in Moscow. Such an opportunity to residents and guests of the capital, as well as to nearby cities, is provided by the Aqua-Yuna water park - the most beautiful part of the Yuna-Life country club. Its doors opened to visitors in April 2011 and have not closed since then, regardless of weather, season or any other factors. Here you can arrange a hyperactive vacation, or just relax by the pool, sipping a delicious cocktail. Let's see what interesting the water park can offer its guests.





Features

Today it is one of the best, largest (3000 square meters) and modern places of water recreation in the entire capital. The water park "Aqua Yuna" belongs to the country club, which is already beloved by many Muscovites, located just eight kilometers from Moscow. Getting here quickly and easily, which was one of the reasons for the high popularity of the place, but far from the most important. The first thing to say is the infrastructure of the water park. At the disposal of visitors is the whole variety of facilities necessary for a quality holiday, both passive and active. Here you can swim in the pool, ride on all kinds of slides or surf, relax in the hot tub, visit spa treatments or sweat in the sauna. Hungry quality bar with a wide selection of food and drinks. And for those who want more, there are many interesting offers for an additional fee.

The second important feature of the water park is its safety level. In particular, this applies to the water purification system - it consists of three stages, at the end of which an additional ultraviolet disinfection is performed. It is absolutely safe for health and allows you to provide the maximum degree of protection for visitors from the risk of any allergic reactions or infections. Thirdly, it is comfortable here both in terms of temperature conditions (air and water), and in terms of service. The staff of the water park is attentive to any needs of the guests and does everything so that the rest of visitors is a success.





Aqua-zone "Aqua-Yun"

This part of the club is represented by the following objects:

  • a large 15-meter pool, plus two smaller ones in the main zone and one in the SPA center;
  • water slides (there are nine in all);
  • artificial wave for surf;
  • Russian bath (sauna) and hammam;
  • bathtub with hydromassage;
  • geysers and waterfalls;
  • water guns.

The entire water park "Aqua Yuna" is divided into two main zones - for children and adults. It is convenient and safe - parents may not worry that their baby will climb the coolest and most dangerous hill. For added security, order watchers are in the aqua zone.





Entertainment for adults

For those over the age of sixteen, five slides are available for individual descent, located in the adult area. Their height varies from four to nine meters, and the speed of descent varies from seven to fourteen meters per second. The steepest and longest (80 meters) is called the Black Hole. Drive off with an average speed of 24 km / h.

Adults will also like the jacuzzi of the water park, Finnish sauna or hammam (for those who like hot things), as well as a spa center for complete relaxation. Have a good time talking or just watch what is happening around in a comfortable area by the pool. Do not forget to order some refreshing drink at the bar, which also offers a variety of snacks and more hearty dishes. If you want something new, go study to surf on an artificial wave.





Artificial wave

This is a fairly new phenomenon of sports tourism, which made it possible to surf without the need to fly to exotic islands and calculate the seasonality of waves. Now you can do this type of water sports much cheaper and within your own place of residence. Flowrider - the only surfing simulator in Moscow - is located in the institution "Aqua-Yuna". The water park (the photo clearly shows the appearance of this building) invites beginners to try their hand at the new image of a surfer gliding along artificial waves, and professionals to maintain and develop their existing skills.

The wave generated by the simulator reaches a width of 10 meters and a height of more than a meter. Classes on it are not only exciting, but also useful - they allow you to tighten the muscles of the legs, buttocks, abs and oblique muscles of the abdomen. In addition, regular training will help improve coordination. If then you decide to go surfing, you can show amazing results.

Ticket price and additional features for visitors to "Aqua Yuna"

A number of basic entertainment is already included in the cost of visiting the water park - this is all that is located in the aqua zone, as well as a jacuzzi, saunas and billiards. Entrance ticket for an adult costs 1,500 rubles, for children under 14 years of age - 700. In addition, you will need to leave a deposit for the keys to the locker - 300 rubles, which are returned upon exit.

There are several additional services that guests can use for a fee. This includes visits to the solarium and spa treatments, as well as classes in the fitness center at the club. Sports enthusiasts will love the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãplaying futsal or running with a racket on the tennis court.





"Aqua Yuna" (water park): reviews

Numerous visitors like it very much. The proximity to Moscow and its convenient location allow you to quickly get to the place at any time and have a good rest. Guests note carefully thought out infrastructure, good pools and quality slides. Parents like that there is a separate area for children - so they can fully enjoy their vacation, without worrying about the safety of the child.

Prices also do not cause any dissatisfaction on the part of visitors. For a water park near the capital, the cost of visiting is very affordable. In addition, many people like to arrange holidays here, especially children's events: water, slides, treats - what else do kids need for joy and laughter? Especially a lot of positive feedback received a surfing simulator. Artificial wave has become one of the main entertainment for adult guests.





Water park location

Just eight kilometers from the capital, in the Mytishchensky district of the Moscow region, the water part of the Yuna-LIFE country club, Aqua Yuna (water park), is conveniently located. The address of this place is the village "Red Hill", the ninth estate. Both buses and electric trains come here. Many guests come to the club in their own cars. Finding a place is pretty easy.

If you decide not to use the services of agencies (which offer travel arrangements for an additional fee), you can independently come to the Aqua Yuna water park. How to get here by car? On Dmitrovskoe highway you need to go 8 kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road, after the village of "Kapustino" turn left. From Leningradskoye Shosse - in the direction of Sheremetyevo-2 and then another 10 minutes (from the airport).

Buses depart from the Altufyevo metro station, and electric trains depart from the Savelovsky station (Sheremetyevskaya station).

Finally

Perhaps the best water park in Moscow, Aqua Yuna, pleases its guests all year round. Here everyone will find entertainment for themselves, and for small money by metric standards. For those who can and want to spend more, such an opportunity also exists - a number of additional services will appeal to visitors with its variety (from spa to tennis courts) and quality. Be sure to visit this place on one of your free days.
