Fantasia Hotel Deluxe (Turkey / Kemer / Camyuva): hotel description and reviews

The resort complex Fantasia Hotel Deluxe is part of one of the largest and most respected companies in Turkey, Ulusoy Holding. The hotel was opened in 2005, when the company's experience in the service industry reached 70 years, and in the tourism industry - 35 years. Thus, the institution has formed a stable tradition of service, thanks to which thousands of tourists from all over the world enjoy quality holidays.

Hotel Restaurants

Fantasia Hotel Deluxe Marmaris cares not only about comfortable living, but also about quality food for its guests. All gastronomic requests can be fully satisfied in the restaurants of the resort complex:

  • Nostalgie is the main hotel restaurant. It serves breakfast, lunch and dinner as a buffet, as well as a night snack buffet. The spacious dining room is distinguished by a festive decoration with a predominance of gold. Guests can also enjoy al fresco dining thanks to the large outdoor terrace.
  • "A La Turka" - a restaurant of traditional Turkish cuisine. It works in the warm summer season. The institution operates on an open terrace with a breathtaking view of the sea. In addition to authentic dishes, you can also enjoy Turkish music.
  • Prelude is an outdoor terrace restaurant overlooking the pool. The institution specializes in European cuisine.
  • Allegra is an outdoor restaurant located next to the pool. The chef will prepare for you the best fish dishes in the open air. The institution has a pleasant rustic atmosphere, due to the large number of wooden elements of decor.
