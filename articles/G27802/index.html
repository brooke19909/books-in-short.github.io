Edele Hotel 3 *, Nha Trang: hotel reviews

If you plan to relax in Nha Trang (Vietnam) and are looking for a comfortable, modern, but at the same time inexpensive hotel, then consider the Edele Hotel 3 * as an option. We will talk about what this hotel is, as well as what impressions tourists from Russia have left after visiting it.



Location

Hotel "Edel" can safely boast of a very good location. So, it is located just five hundred meters from the center of Nha Trang. Therefore, in the vicinity of it there are a large number of entertainment facilities and other tourist infrastructure facilities. The distance to the beach is only four hundred meters. The nearest airport is Cam Ranh, 27 kilometers away. So upon arrival, you can quickly reach the hotel.



General description, photo

Edele Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) was built in 2014. It is a high-rise modern building, equipped for the convenience of guests with high-speed elevators. The hotel's housing stock includes 98 superior, deluxe, senior deluxe and premier suites. Room sizes range from 26 to 35 square meters. meters. All of them, regardless of type, are equipped with comfortable furniture, a TV, a telephone, a minibar, a bathroom, a hairdryer, air conditioning, a safe. Balconies are not available in all rooms. An electric kettle and tea and coffee making facilities are provided.

The hotel has a restaurant offering guests both breakfast (included in the price) and meals throughout the day (for an additional fee). The Edel rooftop features a small pool with a sun terrace, which offers magnificent views of the sea and surroundings. The hotel also has parking, a 24-hour front desk, a tour desk, laundry service, dry cleaning, luggage storage and a small gym.

Cleaning

This item did not cause any particular complaints from the guests of the Edel Hotel. So, according to them, the maids here do a good job of their duties. If you, having arrived in your room, find that you have been poorly cleaned, then inform the reception at the reception. You can be sure that from now on the maids will do their job better. In general, cleaning is done here every day. Every day, maids renew towels and replenish supplies of drinking water, the contents of the minibar and bath accessories. Bed linen is changed a couple of times a week.



Check in, check out

According to travelers, this hotel does not have to rely on the opportunity to get the keys to your room before two in the afternoon. If you arrive at Edel earlier, you can leave things at the reception and go to a restaurant, pool, take a walk in the surroundings or go straight to the beach. After you are given the keys to the apartment, the hotel clerk will deliver your luggage as well.

As for the day of departure, you should release your room before 12 noon. If you plan to leave the hotel later, you can extend your stay by several hours at an additional cost.



Location

This point, according to most tourists, is an indisputable advantage of the Edele Hotel 3 * (Vietnam). After all, it is located within walking distance from most shopping and entertainment facilities in the center of Nha Trang. So, in the vicinity of the hotel there are a lot of cafes, bars, night clubs, travel agencies, car rental points, beauty salons, shops, souvenir shops, etc.

Nutrition

Room rates at Etel Hotel include breakfast only. They are served in buffet style in a restaurant located on the second floor of the hotel. According to our compatriots, in the mornings there is always something to eat. Therefore, you will not stay hungry. So, there are always soups, dishes of meat, poultry or fish, rice, delicious pastries, boiled and fried eggs, sausage and cheese slices, fresh vegetables and fruits. Guests can also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant, but at an additional cost. Here you will be offered dishes of both national and European cuisine more familiar to us.

However, most tourists preferred to eat outside the hotel during the day - near the beach, in the city center or on excursions. According to travelers, the food here is delicious almost everywhere, and the prices are pleasantly pleasing. Moreover, if you get tired of a rather specific local cuisine, then as a variety you can eat more familiar dishes, as well as fast food.



Beach vacation

Many tourists have chosen the hotel Edele Hotel 3 *, among other things, and because of its proximity to the sea. So, hotel guests can walk to the nearest beach in just five minutes. At the same time, your path will go through a lot of outlets and cafes, where you can buy everything you need, as well as a snack if you wish.

The beach here is in municipal ownership. Therefore, for the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas will have to pay. However, if you wish, you can sit on the mat brought with you, laying it directly on the sand. By the way, beach towels for Edel guests are issued at the hotel. Therefore, you do not need to take them with you from home. The beach here is quite large, clean, with a sandy entry into the water. True, the sea is often troubled. Therefore, vacationers are not always able to fully swim.
