Park-hotel "Philippe" (Kaliningrad): description with photo, overview of services, reviews

Park Hotel "Philip" in Kaliningrad is a fashionable country hotel in the territory of the Amber Region, which is very popular among those who want to relax as far as possible from the bustle of the city. It offers a large selection of diverse rooms, a spa complex with a 20-meter pool, a gourmet restaurant with a cook menu with experience in Michelin restaurants. On the territory of the Philip Park Hotel in Kaliningrad there is a large landscaped area, a lake with fish and swans, an ideal venue for a large-scale celebration. They like to celebrate weddings, graduations, anniversaries.

Features

Among the distinguishing features of the Philip Hotel in Kaliningrad is an amazingly beautiful natural park. It has a nice shadow from the trees, neat paths, a large lake.

You can relax in the women's and men's saunas, take a Charcot's shower or pour yourself cold water from the tub. A real Russian bath is equipped on the lake. The 20-meter pool offers hydromassage and aero massage facilities. It offers guests a professional manual massage, which can fill anyone with energy.

For those who want to maintain a healthy lifestyle, there is a gym with power and exercise bikes, a treadmill.

Banquets are regularly held here. The restaurant at the Park-Hotel Philippe in Kaliningrad is ready to provide a high level of service and excellent cuisine. Weddings are held regularly. A summer tent with amazing aerial decoration is installed on the shore of the lake, a view of the water surface opens from the cozy hall of the restaurant, which makes the Philippe Hotel in Kaliningrad one of the most attractive venues for celebrations.

How to get there

It is noteworthy that the hotel itself is located not in Kaliningrad, but in its suburbs, in the territory of Bagrationovsky district. The exact address is the village of Novoselovo, 11k Berlin Highway.

The hotel is located near the Polish border in the south of the capital of the Amber Region.

Hotel Philippe in Kaliningrad accepts credit cards, cash, or bank transfers.

Judging by the photo from the Philip Park Hotel in Kaliningrad, one of the most popular services is a Russian bath. The bath is located directly on the water. Here you can fully relieve stress and fatigue.

Customer Impressions

There are quite a lot of positive reviews about the Park-Hotel Philippe in Kaliningrad. The guests say that the creators of this hotel have thought through everything to the smallest detail. It will be possible to fully relax here.

Parents can calmly leave their children in the playroom with professional animators, and you can go to the bathhouse or restaurant. There are enough places to take a walk, enjoying the unique nature around. Many who have spent time here are sure to come back again and again.

Guests emphasize that although the price of the rooms may seem quite high, the price includes visits to the sauna and pool, as well as a nutritious and delicious continental breakfast. The lake has a bewitching promenade, quality furniture, new bedding. It is noticeable that the guests here are not going to save.

Couples with children prefer to come here to celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, other memorable and important dates. A large and luxurious room, in which many prefer to stay until the morning after a plentiful and hearty dinner, leaves a positive impression. In addition, the hotel has a huge landscaped area, which you can enjoy walking around, enjoying the evening scenery. Moreover, you can safely go on such a vacation with the children, since the game room with the animator works for them from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. During this time, parents can, without rushing anywhere, go for dinner, and then take a leisurely stroll throughout the park hotel.

Negative

Negative reviews about the Philip Hotel in Kaliningrad are also found. However, it is recognized that most of these claims are minor.

For example, guests complain about slow Wi-Fi, but at the same time emphasize that mobile communications work well. In some rooms there is a characteristic smell of repair, which confirms that the rooms are constantly updated.

Claims are expressed by guests and to blankets and pillows of disgusting quality. It is amazing when in such a fashionable and expensive hotel bedding is made from synthetics.

For many it turns out to be a minus and the fact that the hotel is located right next to the highway. There are no warnings about this either on the hotel website or in other sources. You can find out about this only from guest reviews. The consolation is that the track is not very busy, in addition, behind a protective shield.
