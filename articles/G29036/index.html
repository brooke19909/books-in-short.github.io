Sochi seaport and its infrastructure

The seaport of Sochi is not just a place where ships come and where they depart. This is a special architectural project with its own characteristics and attractions. And, of course, this is the only way to get to any place by water.





History of occurrence

At the end of the 19th and the beginning of the 20th century in the city of Sochi, the port, which can be observed now, was absent. Then ships and other vessels were forced to stop at the raid. Cargo and passengers were transported ashore through a boat crossing. The building of the marine station, which exists today, in Sochi appeared in 1995. The project was led by architect K. S. Alabyan. The building, adjacent to the sea port of Sochi, reaches a height of 71 m. It is crowned with a spire with a five-pointed star. In general, I want to note the original design in which this project was implemented. One cannot help but pay attention to the following: sculptures set in three tiers flaunt around the tower. They represent 4 seasons and 4 cardinal points. Symbolically enough. There is also a fountain on the territory, next to which there is a sculpture of the goddess of navigation. The sea port of Sochi is very beautiful, especially at night - in the dark, it is illuminated from all sides by bright lights. Many photographers are inspired by the views from these places. Therefore, they remove the port of Sochi - the photos are extraordinary.





The only one in all of Russia

It's true. Sochi Seaport is the only specialized passenger port in the entire Russian Federation. It was created in 1934. Full name - Sochi Commercial Sea Port. This is a commercial enterprise that works towards the development of regular passenger shipping. His activities also include cruise tourism, carried out along the Black Sea coast of the Russian Federation.

Service

Citizens arriving at the seaport of Sochi to go from there on water transport are served at the passenger terminal. It consists of an international modern pavilion with a capacity of up to 350 people in one hour. Passengers are given the opportunity to visit a restaurant or cafe. There is also a duty free shop. And it should be noted that the port of Sochi Imeretinsky has immigration, customs and border services. Vessels from other cities of Russia, Turkey, Ukraine, Greece, France, England, Panama, Malta and even the Bahamas come here all year round . All ships are serviced around the clock. The port service is at the highest level, the staff provides all types of services that passengers need.
