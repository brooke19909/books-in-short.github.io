Knitted baby hat is an important part of the wardrobe

In cold weather, warm things cannot be dispensed with, especially for children. At the same time, young fashionistas should have several models of hats of various colors and styles at their disposal. In this case, the child will be dressed ‚Äúaccording to the weather‚Äù each time, and the mother will not have to make a difficult choice between a very dense hat and a very thin one. If you have several hats, choosing the most suitable one will not be difficult. Especially if the knitted children's hat is made of natural yarn.



In this product, the child will be comfortable at any time of the year. However, the threads must have a suitable composition and thickness. You should not make a hat from linen for the winter or knit a charming panama hat from wool. In the first case, the child may freeze, and in the second, it will be hot for him, even if you select a thin thread. When making a summer hat, it is better to choose thinner cotton yarn. It is preferable to carry out the work by hook No. 1 - 1.5. In this case, the density of knitting will be optimal so that the product holds its shape well. At the same time, it will not be very thick. Many mothers carry out the field of children's hats with an openwork pattern. Such a finished product will need to be starched so that it does not change its shape during prolonged wear.



It is also very important to choose the right model. Do not think that if a knitted baby hat looks very good on one child, it will also be perfect for another. Each baby is individual, this must be taken into account. If in one model the child will feel very comfortable, then wearing another will give him certain inconvenience. Therefore, when choosing a new hat, you must definitely ask the baby for an opinion. If he feels discomfort, it is better to refuse to wear such a thing. It is also very important that the knitted baby hat is in harmony with the rest of the clothes.

Models for girls are presented in a wide range of colors. If desired, you can knit a panama hat or a hat of bright red or pink, as well as any other color. A knitted children's hat designed for a girl is almost never found in black colors. An exception may be winter models made from woolen yarn. But even in this case, they must be decorated in such a way that the finished product looked very beautiful and festive. For example, many mothers knit animal-shaped caps in this color. A winter hat that resembles a black cat will look very unusual on a girl, especially if complemented by a similar scarf and purse.



Hats for girls for the summer, as a rule, are made using a hook, but a hat for a boy with knitting needles is knitted most often. In the latter case, the product is quite dense and beautiful, even if the model itself is quite simple. You can always decorate it by tying a funny machine or a bright helicopter. It is also possible contrasting finish, for example, when the base is made in black, and on the edge of the panama trimmed with white thread. Such a hat for a boy will look good in the wardrobe of any baby.
