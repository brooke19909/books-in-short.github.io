What is a dispute?



As a matter of fact, this term is interpreted in its literal sense, so it is not necessary to give a full interpretation. One has only to say that the dispute involves early completion of the order with the stipulated full or partial return of funds to the buyer. In the process of resolving the dispute by peaceful means, it is proposed to carry out monetary settlements between the buyer and the supplier by mutual agreement of the parties. If the offer is accepted, then the order is considered completed, and the re-challenge is not provided.
Except in those cases when the order came with a defect or the wrong product was ordered, the opening of a dispute can be used for emergency protection of your money in the absence of contact with the seller and the completion of the order time. The button for opening a dispute is not unlimited. It becomes available only six days after the publication of the order number and becomes inactive with the end of the automatic confirmation of the order. After that, a refund with "Aliexpress" becomes impossible.

How long can the argument last?



On the site "Aliexpress" when placing an order, a timer starts, counting the rest of the time until the order is completed. Until the end of the timer, you can open a dispute. The dispute automatically becomes a claim after 15 days. Next, the claim is considered by mediators.
