Hotel Alexander House Hotel 4 * (Greece): photos and reviews

Alexander House Hotel is a beachfront hotel located on the seafront, close to the famous Agia Pelagia Beach. The hotel is not very large and rather resembles a guest house or a summer cottage. Nevertheless, it has a fairly decent and high-quality range of services that fully meets the European "four". And the atmosphere here reigns homely, just family. Tourists, including those from Russia, love Alexander House Hotel for good and quality food, interesting entertainment and excursion programs, as well as friendly and friendly staff. The hotel was built in 1993, but in 2004 a complete renovation was carried out and the entire interior was updated. Therefore, the hotel is fully consistent with modern standards.



Agia Pelagia

This resort village, where Alexander House is located, is located not too far from Heraklion, has a well-developed tourist infrastructure. There are bars and authentic taverns, shops and souvenir shops. However, clubs and discos in this village you will not find. It is located on the north coast of Crete. This village is famous for its picturesque bay and sandy beach. The terrain is located so that the coast is protected from the north winds. Therefore, there are almost never big waves here. In the Middle Ages there was a monastery of St. Pelagia (hence the name), but to this day its building has not been preserved. Only the ruins and the foundation remained. And in ancient times, in these places was the ancient city of Apollonia. Therefore, archaeological excavations are often conducted here. In just a few years, the village itself has transformed from a provincial fishing village into a fashionable resort with good infrastructure.



How to get and what's nearby

The hotel is located some thirty kilometers from Heraklion Airport. Therefore, getting here is not so difficult. Fifty meters from the Alexander House Hotel there is a bus stop. It is worth going out of the gate, and you will understand that it is easy enough to get from here to Heraklion or Rethymnon. If you travel on your own, then by bus you can very easily get to the hotel from the airport. Transfer takes less than an hour. A bus ride is about the same time. Not far from the hotel is the iconic Kapsis Hotel for fans of the Eagle and Tails program. Nearby are beautiful interesting beaches and bays, both equipped and wild. Around the hotel there are many supermarkets where you can buy water, fruits and everything that you are missing.

Territory

Since the Alexander House Hotel (Crete) is built on a hillside from where it descends on terraces, there are a lot of stairs. Therefore, it is not very suitable for people with disabilities or mothers with strollers. The territory of the hotel is not very large, even somewhere in the chamber. But if you come with a small child, then at least he will not go anywhere far. Nevertheless, there is a beautiful garden with banana palm trees, araucaria. There are flowers everywhere. Greenery surrounds the enclosure around the perimeter. There is also a courtyard, many flowers here. Some plants have signs with inscriptions, so the hotel park is at the same time a botanical garden. The hotel building has a closed shape and is built like a fort. When you go out of the gate, streets lead to the beach among beautiful old courtyards planted with lemon and pomegranate trees.

Staff

The staff of the Alexander House Hotel (Crete) for the most part are friendly and cheerful. So many tourists write in their reviews. The staff is sociable, friendly, but, on the other hand, not at all intrusive. Cleaning, waiters, cooks, head waiter - each of them has a special approach to customers. They will help to solve all issues with food, and with accommodation, and with cleaning. If you rent a car, then at the reception you will be given a map and even draw the desired route. There is no staff turnover at the hotel, people have been working here for many years and are interested in their reputation. Often, tourists part with them almost friends.

Sea holiday

The beach is 150 meters away. It is sandy and pebble, sloping into the water, smooth. True, vacationers say that it is a bit narrow. To get to it, you need to pass several small shops. Since the beaches in Crete are municipal, then umbrellas and sunbeds are paid. They cost about two euros a day, and people collect money in uniform. However, they may not be suitable for payment - it depends on the day of the week and on the number of vacationers. Towels for sunbathing can be borrowed at the hotel. If you come with your umbrella and bedding, then relax on the beach for free. There are always places for sunbathing. The water is very clean, waves are rare. Along the beach there is a promenade, there are many taverns on it. In the evening you can take a walk and listen to live Greek music to the sound of the waves. The Alexander House Hotel (Greece) also has a freshwater pool. The area for children is separated separately. The pool is always clean and does not smell bleach. It is small, but rather deep - more than three meters. Since the beach is municipal, all soft drinks, cocktails and so on are at your disposal only on site. This means that on the beach you will have to buy all this in nearby stores. You can get these services by the pool for free.
