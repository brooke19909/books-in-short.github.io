Hotel Bayside Katsaras 4 * (Rhodes, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Bayside Katsaras 4 * is a luxurious resort establishment that will allow you to enjoy all the delights of the Greek island of Rhodes. A warm climate and unique nature will be complemented by comfortable living conditions and excellent service.





Brief description of the hotel

The hotel complex Bayside Katsaras 4 * opened its doors to travelers in 1997. Since then, he underwent several reconstructions. Now it is a modern hotel that meets international standards of service. From May to October, you can fully relax in this resort.

Hotel Bayside Katsaras 4 * is not able to boast a large territory. Nevertheless, here guests will find everything that is necessary for a good rest. The main objects of the hotel are a three-story building and a huge pool of an original form.

Hotel rooms

Hotel Bayside Katsaras 4 * has 70 comfortable rooms. The following accommodation options are available to guests:

  • Standard rooms have single, double and triple variations. They are equipped with one and a half orthopedic beds, bedside tables, a workplace, a built-in wardrobe. There is a private exit to the balcony (through a huge glass door through which a lot of sunlight penetrates the room.
  • Family rooms are two-room apartments. The bedroom has a large bed and a pair of twin. The second room is a living room combined with a kitchen area.
  • Suite is a luxury apartment, which is a spacious studio. The bar counter divides the areas of the bedroom and living room. The large plasma TV is located so that it is convenient to watch both from the bed and from the sofa.

It is worth noting that the interior design of all rooms of this hotel is designed in the same style. The walls are painted white, which visually expands the space, and furniture and decorative elements are mainly made of wood.
