Making a Headpiece of the Thunder God

First you need to prepare the basis for creating a helmet - inflate the ball and fix it. Further, the work will consist in processing paper and laying it on a ball.

Making Thor's helmet using the papier-m√¢ch√© technique:

  1. Tear a newspaper into small pieces and in a separate pile - white paper.
  2. First you need to take pieces of white paper, dip in water, which is poured into a ceramic container, and lay on the ball.
  3. Thus, you need to lay out one layer. Wait until the pulp dries. Approximately 2 hours are needed.
  4. The paper is opened with a thin layer of glue and again left to dry.
  5. The next step will be the application of newsprint soaked in water. It is necessary to form several layers.
  6. Leave to dry and glue again.

  8. Leave to dry for a day.
  9. The wings, which should protrude above the helmet and partially cover the face, can be cut out of cardboard.

  11. The wings are attached to the sides with the help of a thread and a needle.

When the do-it-yourself helmet of Thor is already ready, you need to start finishing. Coat the base with paint. At the base of the relief, cover the paper with sparkles, but first you need to outline these folds with gray gouache.
