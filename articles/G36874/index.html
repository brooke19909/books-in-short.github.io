By car to the Crimea: tips of an experienced tourist

Distant wanderings have always attracted adventure lovers and new experiences. And especially if this is a trip to one of the most fantastic and charming corners of our nature - to the Crimea. Despite the difficulties of organizing such a trip, it will be an unforgettable and vivid memory in life.

Car owners prefer the option of traveling by car to the Crimea. It is very good for those who plan to visit various interesting places, visit dozens of attractions and relax richly and beautifully. In addition, your car is freedom of choice and lability; you can easily change the route and go to another place at any time.

Travel Organization: Key Aspects



Before you start the engine and rush towards new adventures, you need to be well prepared for such a long journey. The first, and most important, part of this preparation is the technical inspection of the car, the elimination of all malfunctions and problems. Nothing can spoil a vacation like unexpected breakdowns on the way, which delay tourists for a long time and entail unnecessary chores and expenses.

The second important aspect is the detailed route planning. Even those who love adventure and impromptu make at least a rough plan of action to avoid fatigue and inconvenience on the road. Features of the route, possible complications and problems



A trip to the Crimea by car has its own characteristics and nuances. It is better to know them in advance, so that later you do not feel discomfort along the way. Here are the main ones:

  • The specifics of mountain roads. Crimean mountains are very dangerous for inexperienced drivers, here are full of complex serpentines and narrow sections. The speed here is limited to 40, and sometimes up to 20 km per hour.
  • Car service and refueling. In Crimea, in principle, there are no problems with this, but the quality of service is not always satisfactory. It is better to stop at those stations that are part of large network companies.
  • Night traffic. It is better to plan the time so that at the stage of moving the mountain range there is morning or time after lunch. It is dangerous to ride here at night.
  • Campings and parking lots. In order not to look for where to stay for the night in a particular place of the peninsula, stock up on a map of campsites and tent camps with the ability to park your car. So you save time for more interesting walks and excursions.

The most picturesque corners of Crimea



Traveling by car to the Crimea can be arranged in such a way as to see many interesting and beautiful places and attractions. There are simply an incredible amount of them, so you need to think through the route very carefully. It is difficult to single out the most beautiful and unforgettable corners of the peninsula. Here are just a few of them:

  • Yalta Palace, Ai-Petrinskoe Plateau and the Grand Canyon of Crimea.
  • Sevastopol and its picturesque bays, ancient Khersones, Balaklava and cave cities and temples.
  • Treasures of South Demerdzhi, Ghost Valley and beautiful waterfalls.
  • The fabulous bays of the New World, Sudak and its ancient fortress on the cliff.
  • Fantastic cape Meganom, a magical aura of the steppe expanses and coastal cliffs.

And there is the Swallow's Nest and Nikitinsky Botanical Gardens, palaces and fortresses, cozy towns, mysterious caves and turbulent mountain rivers. Crimea hides hundreds of treasures in its depths - look for them together with your friends, traveling on four wheels all over the peninsula!
