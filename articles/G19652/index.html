Reviewed Huawei Honor 4C. Description of the smartphone, specifications

It can be seen that for the successful sales of their smartphones, Huawei tried a lot of strategies. One of them is the allocation of an independent line of devices under the new brand. In particular, we are talking about the phone Huawei Honor 4C. We will consider it in today's review.





In our country, Honor is associated with the Huawei brand and, of course, does not independently advance among consumers; while the company itself strongly advocates this product as a separate development, an independent brand that produces devices.

Some experts on the mobile market believe that this was a promotional price aimed at popularizing a smartphone, its advertising, so to speak; but at the same time, the price did not rise in the end.

The device is presented in the hottest category of 5-inch smartphones to date. With a resolution of 1280 by 720 pixels, the phone has a good display density (294 dpi). In addition, there is also the ability to customize the screen display to your liking. To do this, the phone menu provides for a number of options, each of which can be configured.

CPU





Regarding Huawei devices (Honor 4C 8 GB also applies), it should be noted that they are equipped with "hardware", individually developed by the company for its products. We are talking about HiSilicon Kirin 620, running on 8 cores. Their clock frequency is 1.2 GHz. And paired with it, the graphic engine (Mali T-450 MP4) also functions.

Due to this connection, the device can really be called quite nimble in terms of interaction on a daily level. That is, the smartphone does not fail if you scroll through the menu on it, open certain applications, leaving others in the background , and so on.

It is important to note that some performance problems can be found on "bulky" (in terms of graphics) games. This is where you need to be prepared for the fact that the phone may simply not play any of them.

Communication





The official name of the device we are describing is Huawei Honor 4C Dual Sim. Already from it we can conclude that the smartphone has the function of supporting work simultaneously with two SIM cards, which, of course, gives the user advanced communication capabilities.

Both cards can receive a signal in 2G / 3G / 4G format. In addition to conventional mobile communications, there is also support for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth-signal. In addition to all of the above, one can name the navigation abilities of the smartphone, support for GPS and GLONASS systems.
