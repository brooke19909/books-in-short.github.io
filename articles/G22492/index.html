Hotel "Giraffe Potapych", Golubitskaya: reviews and photos

The village of Golubitskaya is located in the Krasnodar Territory, 38 km from Anapa and 36 km from Vityazevo. This small resort village is growing every year. All new hotels appear in it. "Giraffe Potapych" in Golubitskaya was created for family holidays. Every guest is taken care of here, providing excellent living conditions.

Where is it, how to get there

The hotel is located on the street. Shady, 5. You can get to Golubitskaya by train, plane or private car. If you move from Moscow, it is better to choose a fast train that runs 24 hours to Anapa. He has a minimum amount of time at the stations. The train leaves from the Kazan station and passes with stops in Ryazan, Lipetsk, Voronezh, Rostov.





By personal transport it is better to choose a route that passes through the Korenovsky district. There are better quality roads along it, in addition, fewer settlements have to pass on them. The hotel provides a transfer from the railway station and airport - cars with child car seats. From Anapa, the road to the hotel takes approximately 1 hour.

Accommodations

The hotel complex "Giraffe Potapych" in Golubitskaya presents rooms of different levels of comfort with a certain number of people.

  • "Poppies" - a family 1-room room with a bunk bed and a folding large sofa. The room has air conditioning, TV, table and chairs, wardrobe. The room has a small space under the kitchen. There is a crock-pot, an electric kettle, a set of dishes and a sink. The room is equipped with shower and toilet.
  • "Katyusha" - consists of two rooms. The living room has a folding sofa, table and chairs, air conditioning, wardrobe, TV. The bedroom has two 1.5 beds and a bedside table. Allocated a special place for the kitchen. It has the same equipment as in the Maki room, but additionally has a small electric stove. A shower and toilet are installed.
  • The "watermelon" room has exactly the same arrangement as the "Katyusha", only consists of one spacious room and has its own balcony.
  • "Strawberry" - consists of two rooms. The living room has a large sofa, air conditioning, TV, table and chairs. The bedroom has 3 single beds. Equipped with a small kitchen with the necessary appliances. There is a shower and toilet.
  • "Purple" and "Iris" - rooms are equipped in the likeness of "Watermelon", only without balconies.




In all rooms, the decor matches their name. The rooms also have refrigerators and laundry and cleaning facilities. Guests can use the internet for free.

Freestanding cottage

On the territory of the "Giraffe Potapych" in Golubitskaya in 2014 a two-story house was built. It has a high level of comfort and is designed for up to 9 people.

This accommodation option is ideal for a company of two families with children. Each will have a separate room and a common living room for pleasant gatherings. The house has everything you need to stay with young children:

  • Chairs for feeding.
  • Cots with sides.
  • Pots and special toilet seats.
  • Toys

On the ground floor there is a folding sofa and an armchair that transforms into a full-fledged berth. There is also a TV, dining table and chairs. The kitchen area is equipped with a slow cooker, 2-burner electric stove, small fridge, kettle, necessary utensils.





On the ground floor there is a small room with a double bed, shower and toilet. On the second - a spacious bedroom with one large and two 1.5 beds. Each floor has air conditioning.

Area for children

In the "Giraffe Potapyche" in Golubitskaya, mainly families with children of different ages have a rest. The administration did everything possible so that the kids were always busy and active.

For this, the complex has a playground for games. It has a slide, a swing, sports equipment and trampolines of different sizes. The games area provides many toys on various subjects.





The room also has a games room. In bad weather, animators are engaged there with children from 3 to 14 years old. They hold creative workshops and organize various competitions.

There is also a small library with a wide range of children's art books. Adult vacationers will also be able to pick up something to read for themselves.

Entertainment

The administration has been procuring all kinds of accessories since the winter for a fun holiday for its guests. Here are offered:

  • Bicycles for rent.
  • Face painting.
  • Table games.
  • Mini disco.
  • Contests and quests.
  • Cooking classes.
  • Thematic show programs.

A team of animators works daily with tourists. They entertain children, and in the evenings arrange programs with the participation of all family members. Rest outside the complex

Hotel "Giraffe Potapych" (Golubitskaya) is a 5-minute quiet walk from the pebble beach. It has all kinds of water activities:

  • Riding a banana and buns.
  • Slides.
  • Trampolines.
  • Cafe.

There is a small dolphinarium in the village, and in the center of the village there is a spacious amusement park. You can eat in the small cafe "Cruise", which is a 3-minute walk from the hotel.

For hotel guests, excursions to Anapa and coming proud are often organized in special bureaus to get acquainted with their attractions.

Hotel "Giraffe Potapych" in Golubitskaya, reviews

On various Internet resources there are many comments about the hotel. Tourists with children choose this holiday complex based on the employment of children in the evenings.

As for the arrangement of rooms, the guests have practically no complaints. They note in the reviews that in the "Giraffe Potapyche" (Golubitskaya) the rooms are always clean and comfortable. Tourists are happy with the presence of a multicooker. Thus, if desired, you can cook yourself, especially for kids.





The guests also point out that the animators are doing great with children and from 17.00 parents can relax, because the guys are busy and are supervised.

Many rooms have windows and balconies overlooking the playground. Therefore, children can walk there at any time, and parents from the rooms easily watch them. Guests note that the staff is very responsible and friendly. Bed linen is changed on time, cleaning is carried out efficiently.

Adult visitors have the opportunity to spend time with neighbors in a designated area. Tourists are also pleased with the proximity of the beach to the hotel. They calmly go there with their children and back after an active bath, the kids do not have time to get tired along the way.

Many guests come to this hotel more than once, because it is difficult to find the best vacation for your family.
