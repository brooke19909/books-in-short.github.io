Murcia (Spain) - a charming Mediterranean resort

Clean sandy beaches, huge orchards, contrasting landscapes, interesting sights, Murcia is famous for its rich history. Spain can rightfully be proud of this wonderful resort, also called the country's large greenhouse. Comfortable hotels here are adjacent to luxurious gardens and medieval ruins. Murcia is an autonomous region located in the southeast of the state between Valencia, Andalusia and Castile La Mancha.



In the south of the province, mountains stretch to the sea right up to Cape Palos, forming rocky shores. In the northern part you can find the Mar Menor lagoon with islands of volcanic origin - these are the most beautiful places that affect all travelers Murcia. Spain is a major supplier of fruits and vegetables in Europe. This autonomous region has the largest number of orchards in the country, and many flowers are grown. And this despite the fact that it rains here only in the mountains, and even then rarely. Local residents solved this problem by installing stations for processing sea water into fresh water.

Thanks to the preserved ancient sources, it is known that in 825, Murcia was founded on the site of the Roman settlement by the Arabs. Spain receives positive reviews from visiting tourists due to interesting historical and architectural monuments. Murcia has acquired a rich Arab heritage. The city reached a special peak in the 18th century, so so many baroque architectural monuments have survived to this day . The rule of the Arabs is reminiscent of small courtyards, winding narrow streets, the ruins of the walls and the Castillo de Monteagudo fortress, built in the 11th century.



The cathedral in the Gothic style is an iconic place in the city of Murcia. Spain is famous for its many delightful masterpieces of architecture, and this is one of them. The cathedral was built on the foundation of a Muslim mosque in the XIV-XVI centuries. The facade of the building is made in the Baroque style, and the 92-meter tower is in the spirit of the Renaissance. It is also worth visiting the city of Cartagena, which has an ancient history, convenient parking for ships and an amazingly beautiful bay. This commercial port at different times was of great interest to Muslims, Romans, Byzantines, Visigoths, Castilians and just pirates. In Cartagena, the first submarine sailing in the Mediterranean Sea was installed on a pedestal.

Sunny and warm weather, delightful sandy beaches will be remembered by tourists for Murcia. Spain is a resort country, so there is everything for a comfortable stay. Many travelers like to relax on La Manga - a long sand spit that separates the lagoon of Mar Menor from the sea. Since the depth of the bay is less than 2 m, the water here is always warm, and even has a high concentration of iodine and salt. La Manga is ideal for treatment, family and outdoor activities.



Costa Calida is also very proud of the city of Murcia (Spain). It is on this coast that the popular resort of La Manga del Mar Menor is located, as well as the salt lake Mar Menor. This place attracts lovers of water sports. Holidays in Murcia for a long time will be remembered for sunny days and fun activities.
