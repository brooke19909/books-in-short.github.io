It all started with a grove

Sokolniki is a great place, which is ideal for any type of vacation. Here, they will feel great and find something to do both youth and the older generation. Little children did not go unnoticed, for whom there are quite a lot of entertainments. This park is one of the oldest in Moscow and one of the largest in Europe. The history of its creation goes back to the distant 15th century. He appeared even earlier than Victory Park in Moscow.





Once upon a time there was the most ordinary grove in which it was customary to carry out the royal hunt for falcons, hence the name - Sokolniki. Now it is a landscaped park with alleys and ennobled plantings.

A vacation spot for everyone

Sokolniki Park was created for people with different interests. Here, cyclists and scooters move along special paths, and for those who are walking, there are alleys that are lined with stone or pavers. By the way, both rollers and a bicycle can be rented in the park, so it is not necessary to carry all the equipment with you. If you decide to take a walk in the winter, then skiing and ice skating are popular here.





There are several venues where organized concerts are held, fans of extreme relaxation can try their hand at endurance and climb a climbing wall or go karting. In the park you can shoot archery or crossbow, play tennis or billiards, and if you want, then swim in the pool. For sports enthusiasts, fitness facilities are equipped here. Children will be interested in horseback riding or ponies.

And Sokolniki Park is a paradise for children. There are multiple swing-carousels, attractions, playgrounds, including a games room in the open. You can go for a walk in a small zoo or jump on plenty of trampolines.

The diversity that is inherent in this park is quite difficult to describe, so it's best to see everything with your own eyes.

Resting with the whole family

Children's parks in Moscow are full of their diversity in terms of entertainment.
