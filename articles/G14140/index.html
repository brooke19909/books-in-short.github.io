Best San Diego 3 (Spain, Salou): photos and reviews of tourists

Spain is a popular tourist country not only among Europeans, but also residents of the CIS countries. Rest here will cost a little more than in Turkey, Greece or Bulgaria, but there will be much more impressions from it. In resort towns along the coast, both luxury hotels and small hotels for budget accommodation have been built. One of the latest is the Best San Diego 3 * hotel. Photos, a detailed description of rooms and infrastructure, as well as reviews of tourists can be seen in our article.

Holidays in Salou, Spain

To begin with, it is worth noting that Salou is often called the most Russian resort in Spain, because it is here that tourists from the CIS countries often come. This small town is located on the northeast coast of the country near the Costa Dorada. The high season begins here in late April-mid-May and lasts until late autumn. The resort is ideal for couples with young children, but young people will not be bored here. Salou is also considered the center of gastronomic tourism, because there are a huge number of restaurants and bars offering guests dishes of Mediterranean and Spanish cuisine.

All the beaches of the city are distinguished by their cleanliness. The advantage is that there are no jellyfish at all in the sea, and the entrance to the water is sandy and gentle, therefore suitable for children. At the same time, you will not find large shopping centers in Salou, however, souvenirs can always be bought in small shops located along the coast. This is a very compact resort - all the attractions are concentrated along the promenade of King Jaime. There are illuminated singing fountains, a monarch statue covered in gilding, as well as Catalan luxury villas. A large amusement park is open for children, and young people can relax in the many nightclubs that can be found on the coast.

General Hotel Information

Hotel Best San Diego 3 * in Salou is the place for an inexpensive but comfortable stay. It is suitable for those who like to live in the busy center of the resort or simply do not want to spend a lot of time on daily walks to shops, the beach or bars. The complex was built in 1974, but has since been seriously repaired several times. The last update was completed in 2016, so do not worry that you will come across a room with old furniture or fixtures.

The hotel grounds are also ennobled. It has a huge garden, private pool and sunbathing area. In total, the complex offers 252 rooms. All of them are located in a large six-story residential building. Both in internal and external design shades of white prevail.

The Best San Diego 3 * complex (Spain, Salou) accepts tourists all year round. Qualified staff works here, who speaks not only Spanish and English, but also Ukrainian, Russian and Polish. Therefore, the settlement and resolution of any contentious issues will not cause you any problems. Registration of guests starts at noon local time and is carried out throughout the day, until midnight. Guests under 12 years old are free of charge. However, you cannot come with animals here. You will need to leave your rooms after the holidays early in the morning - until 10:00.

We have already noted above that the Best San Diego 3 * hotel has a favorable location. Now let's talk about it in more detail. The hotel is conveniently located between the beach area and the town center. The coast is just 200 meters away. Therefore, tourists can get there in about 5-10 minutes. The central part is approximately the same. There is a bus stop 150 meters from the hotel, from where you can leave for other major cities in Spain, including Barcelona. A supermarket, pharmacy and ATM are 200-250 meters away. If you want to visit a restaurant or a night club, then you have to walk about 200 meters.

What rooms are in the hotel Best San Diego 3 *?

Since this hotel is considered a budget option for recreation, the rooms here do not differ in exquisite decoration and luxurious design. In turn, they will be placed on the sofa, and not on the bed. No extra beds are available. Each room has a bedroom with a separate living area, a small bathroom and an open balcony. It has a dining set. The windows, as a rule, overlook the pool and neighboring buildings, but from a distance you can see a strip of coast.

Beach holidays at the hotel

When choosing this hotel for relaxation, it is worth knowing that Best San Diego 3 * (Spain) does not have a private stretch of coast. Guests have access to the city beach, which is 200 meters from the complex. Entrance to it is free, but tourists will have to spend money on additional services. For example, they will have to pay a daily rent for a sunbed and sunshade. Water activities are also available for a fee, for example, motor boat rental, banana rides, windsurfing and diving. But playing beach volleyball is completely free.

However, tourists who do not want to spend money should not be upset. They can come to the beach with their own towels or relax by the outdoor pool next to the hotel. It is filled with fresh water and does not heat, but it has a large area and depth, so it is suitable even for adults. Next to it is a sun deck equipped with comfortable sun loungers with mattresses, as well as umbrellas that protect from the sun.

Hotel criticism

Negative reviews about Best San Diego 3 * are quite common. However, guests still recommend a vacation in this place, and some even say that they would like to return here again. But before buying a ticket, you should still pay attention to the following disadvantages:
