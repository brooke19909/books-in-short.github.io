New year in Russia. Where to go? 5 great places!

Which of us does not like New Year? This is an excellent occasion to meet with dear people, friends and relatives, have a good rest and have fun. Long New Year holidays are the best time to get out of the tenacious clutches of a big city and meet the holiday differently from everyone else. So, 5 places where you can interestingly celebrate New Year in Russia.

Great Ustyug





The city has always attracted many tourists. Than? Of course, the fact that this is the estate of Santa Claus. You have the opportunity to become a participant in a magical celebration, have a wonderful time in a winter fairy tale, recharge with new energy for the coming year. Children will not have to be bored: Grandfather's estate is huge and interesting - here you can ride on a troika, watch fabulous performances, go on excursions to the reserved forest and much more. Adults will also have something to do: a sauna, a quality bathhouse, a billiard room, snowmobiles, bars, an equipped ski run ... perhaps this will be your best New Year in Russia. Where else to go?

Kremlin tree

Tickets for this magnificent Moscow festival are always sorted out quickly, so you should hurry up to buy them at the box office of the Kremlin Palace or just order on the website. This is a real dream of many children, and adults will be able to visit a fairy tale, to remember childhood.

New Year Golden Ring Tours





In the winter holidays, many of our compatriots think about where interesting tours could be made. Russia traditionally celebrates New Year's Eve merrily and on a grand scale. The majestic cities of the Golden Ring are eight places where you can perfectly meet the holiday. New Year in Russia - where to go? Pereyaslavl-Zalessky, Ivanovo, Sergiev Posad, Vladimir, Suzdal, Kostroma, Yaroslavl, Rostov the Great - each of them is cozy in its own way, crowned with domes burning in the sun, rich in stunning examples of applied art. They are the real business cards of Russian culture. Learn more about Russia!

Mysterious New Year in Russia. Where to go? Baikal!

The deepest and cleanest lake in the world is beautiful not only in summer but also in winter. At this time, Baikal is covered with a thick blue-blue crust of ice, which gives a chance to see the clean bottom of the lake. Tourists who find themselves on Lake Baikal on New Year's Eve often do not sit in warm hotel rooms, but go to celebrate New Year on the island of Olkhon. This place is rich in legends and traditions. For example, locals say that the grave of the great Genghis Khan is located here. The length of Olkhon is 70 km, it is full of attractions, the main of which are the Shamanka rock or the Kurykan Wall. As soon as the strait freezes, you can drive a car along the magical coast of Lake Baikal in winter, go to the fish market, buy souvenirs from natural materials.

New year in Russia. Where to go? To the capital of the holiday - Vologda!



This holiday is celebrated here with a traditional Russian scale. You will have the opportunity to participate in this Russian festivities and learn more not only about Vologda and the region, but also about Russia as a whole. Tourists will be interested to see wooden and stone architecture, monasteries, cathedrals, museums. From here you can go to Veliky Ustyug. A tour to Vologda for the New Year and Christmas is very interesting and more economical than a trip, for example, to Finland.
