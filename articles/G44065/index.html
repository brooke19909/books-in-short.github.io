Pension "Flight" (Alushta): description and reviews

It is impossible to imagine a more picturesque place on earth than the Southern Crimea - Alushta. Pension "Flight" is located just in this part of the peninsula. It is located just a hundred meters from the Black Sea coast. Here, both young people and families with children love to relax, because in this area all the conditions for an optimal vacation are created. This Crimean city has a favorable climate, pebble beaches, healing air, and a well-developed tourist infrastructure. All this in general attracts travelers to this hotel complex. We learn a lot of interesting things about this institution from the article below.

Description

Boarding house "Flight" (Alushta) is a resort and health institution that was founded forty-five years ago. It employs exclusively qualified personnel. The territory of the complex is landscaped and well-groomed, and all kinds of exotic plants grow around the building.



This dispensary is located in the very center of the resort, just a few steps from the promenade. In the park area is Pension "Flight" (Alushta). Photos taken on his own small pebble beach show that it is equipped with awnings, fresh water showers, sunbeds and a first-aid post.

This resort complex attracts vacationers not only with its wonderful and convenient location, but also with a large selection of comfortable rooms at affordable prices.

Accommodation

Pension "Flight" (Alushta) consists of one four-story building, which can accommodate two hundred and sixty guests. All apartments have views of the sea, the promenade or the landscaped garden.

The double and triple rooms have the following amenities: washbasin, comfortable beds, bedside tables, wardrobe and TV.

Shower, toilet and fridge are in single rooms or in some apartments for two people. In addition, there are also junior suites. The cost of living starts from 1300 rubles per person, it includes three meals a day in the dining room of the dispensary on the ground floor. An afternoon snack is also included in the children's menu.





Infrastructure

Pension "Flight" (Alushta) has on its territory sports grounds, a library room with interesting literature, a rental point for various equipment for swimming, luggage storage and parking. In addition, in his case you can connect to free Wi-Fi.

Beautiful women will undoubtedly like the fact that the complex still has a beauty salon, massage and beauty parlor.

What can I do?

Not a single tourist had to be bored yet, having come to rest in the boarding house "Flight" (Alushta). Reviews of people who spent their holidays there indicate that the dispensary has a lot of entertainment.

It organizes boat trips on catamarans, yachts, jet skis and motor ships. In addition, in the territory of the dispensary there is a medical and health center where you can go through all kinds of procedures.

Fans of traveling can use the services of a tour desk and drive to the sights of Crimea. For fans of nightlife near the resort there are many restaurants, cafes, bars and discos.





The opinion of many vacationers

Over the entire period of existence, many tourists in their walls took the boarding house "Flight" (Alushta). Reviews about it are extremely positive. Its guests like the fact that the dispensary is very conveniently located, and next to it there are a dolphinarium, an aquarium, a miniature park and the central promenade.

The territory of the resort complex is very beautiful and its own beach is located just two minutes walk, unlike other hotels in the city. Another advantage of this pension, according to vacationers, is its excellent cuisine. Here they feed always wonderful, tasty and satisfying.

The rooms are clean and comfortable with all necessary furniture. The staff of the wellness center is very friendly and helpful, which makes the rest even more comfortable and enjoyable. And, of course, the main advantage of this dispensary is its relatively low prices for such an ideal vacation.
