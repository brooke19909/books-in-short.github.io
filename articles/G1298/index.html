Crown Resort Ela Maris 3 - Pernera Bay

Description: Crown Resort Ela Maris 3 is a wonderful three-star hotel located in the cozy Pernera Bay, which occupies the territory between Protaras, which is only 3 kilometers away, and Paralimni. The hotel is 300 meters from the sea. It is built in a modern style and is popular for its excellent leisure, quality service and developed infrastructure.



Crown Resort Ela Maris 3 consists of three four-story buildings, the construction of which was completed in 1990. In 2000, reconstruction of buildings was carried out. 41 km. Larnaca International Airport (LCA) is located 5.7 km from Ayia Napa, 1.7 km from Sunrise Beach and 2.5 km from Trinity Beach. Immediately at the hotel there is a public transport stop that allows you to get to any part of the city and explore all the best attractions and interesting places of the city.



Rooms: The Crown Resort Ela Maris 3 Foundation consists of 171 rooms, of which 50 have views of the coast. Air conditioning, hair dryer, television, refrigerator. Internet and in-room safe for an extra charge. Each has a balcony and a kitchenette with a small cooking stove.

By type, numbers are divided into:

  • STUDIO (41) - there are two beds, all bath accessories are free of charge during the stay.
  • One Bedroom Apartment (130) is a two-room apartment with a door between the rooms.

A microwave and highchair for babies are available upon request.

Meals: Crown Resort Ela Maris provides three meals a day to its guests BB. There is a children's menu. You can visit another restaurant (Crown Restaurant), which prepares dishes according to recipes of international cuisine, a pool bar (for a fee), a snack bar.

Beach: There is no private beach. The sandy Pernera city beach is 300 meters from Crown Resort Ela Maris 3. Beach towels, umbrellas and chaise lounges for a fee. On the beach vacationers delivers a bus that leaves every 20 minutes.

Additional information: The hotel has equipped its guests with a spacious conference room for up to 50 people. There is an outdoor pool, outdoor parking for cars, laundry, Internet in public areas Crown Resort Ela Maris 3. Reviews show that there are all conditions for sports entertainment. For sports activities there is a free gym, a volleyball court, tennis court, rent of rackets and balls, bicycles. You can visit the diving center, billiards club. In the evening, all kinds of entertainment programs are held for vacationers.

Reception is round-the-clock. At the front desk there is always a fresh press for vacationers. There is also a safe for storing valuables of guests during their holidays. Registration of those who come to rest is after 14.00, and those who are already leaving - before noon. If you check in too late or leave too early, additional charges may apply.
