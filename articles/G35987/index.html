Samsung 5250 smartphone: quality and affordability in one device

Samsung 5250, like any other mobile device based on the BADA platform, belonged to the most affordable gadgets in its time. This is indicated by the cost of the device, and its technical parameters, and even equipment. It is about this most popular mobile solution to this software platform that will be discussed in this article.

Technical specifications

Modest even by the standards of 2010 (and it was then that device was officially introduced) the hardware components in the Samsung 5250. The device's characteristics indicate the presence of a single-core central processing unit with a clock frequency of only 667 MHz. The capacity of the internal drive is only 100 MB. The maximum size of the installed memory card in this situation can be equal to 16 GB. The list of wireless interfaces consists of Wi-Fi, GPS and bluetooth. The device is also equipped with transmitters of cellular networks of the 2nd (GSM 900/1800) and 3rd (UMTS) generations.





Camera

At the moment, a 3.2 megapixel sensor (just such is installed in the only camera of this device) provides a very, very low quality photo and video. But do not forget that this smartphone was officially introduced in 2010. At that time, the capabilities of this camera were fully consistent with the segment of budget solutions. Other features of this camera include the presence of 2X digital zoom and automatic white balance selection.

Autonomy

Samsung 5250 was equipped with a removable battery with a capacity of 1200 mAh. Taking into account the diagonal of the screen of 3.2 inches, its resolution (only 240 x 400), relatively modest technical parameters and high optimization of system software, the phone owner could confidently count on 3 days of the gadget's operation under average load. A more intensive use reduced this time to 1-1.5 days. Well, in the maximum battery saving mode, you could stretch 4-5 days.
