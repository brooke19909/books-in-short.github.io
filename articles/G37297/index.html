Anapa: the history of the city and the origin of its name

The history of this superb resort began 12 millennia ago. Just such an age was established for the oldest archaeological research that has been discovered in this area. During its development, the land has changed rulers many times, and now it is a recognized sanatorium in Russia. The first name of the settlement is Sindika, or Sinda harbor. It was given by the name of the tribal settlement, which lived here before our era.
