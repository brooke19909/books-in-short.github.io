Convenient wireless headset for mobile electronics

Headphones have long been a constant companion of music lovers and mobile electronics. As a rule, these devices are equipped with a long cord, which can interfere with movement. It became much more convenient to use them with the advent of Bluetooth technology, which eliminates the need for wires. For example, you can answer a call without removing the phone from your pocket or bag.

Device specifications



For sports, a wireless headset is suitable like no other. Headphones are very light, well-fitting, perfectly protected from ambient sounds. The device is compatible with absolutely any electronics: it can be connected to a smartphone, tablet, computer, phone. For example, the Logitech H800 model is capable of transmitting stereo sound with high quality and can operate on battery for 6 hours. The buttons located on the headphone allow you to easily select recordings, adjust the sound volume, and also switch to microphone mute or receive calls. Even at a distance of 12 meters from the electronic device (smartphone, computer), the wireless headset provides impeccable sound transmission, as a nanodeceiver is inserted into it. The headphone membranes have laser settings, there is an equalizer. The device comes in handy on the go. This is facilitated by the folding headset design. If a microphone bothers you, for example, while watching a movie on a computer, its position can be adjusted by moving it behind the headband. For recharging and connecting to a PC, a USB cable is included with the device.
