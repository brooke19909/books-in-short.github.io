Hotels of Medvezhyegorsk. Description and prices

Medvezhyegorsk is a city located on the coast of Lake Onega. It attracts tourists not only with its natural wealth and attractions. Unique healing springs of mineral waters have been found in Medvezhyegorsk, and the local climate even has a therapeutic effect. People come here for different purposes. Someone to work, and someone to rest. Both need housing. Below will be presented the hotels of Medvezhyegorsk, prices for accommodation in each of them and guest reviews.





"Little Bear"

Medvezhyegorsk hotels may seem non-standard, but this is their attractiveness. Based on numerous reviews, the rating opens the Malaya Medvezhka country club located on the shores of Lake Onega. This place is notable for the fact that on its territory there are several cottages and mini-hotels, and each is unique in its own way. For a family holiday in the silence of nature - this is the best place. The club operates both in winter and summer. This is one of the best ski resorts in Karelia. The club consists of eight comfortable cottages and mini-hotels. There is a Russian bathhouse, pavilions, each of which has a barbecue and a fish smokehouse, a coffee shop with karaoke. The club is just a 10-minute drive from the Bear Mountain ski station. Here you can rent ski equipment and go to equipped tracks. For guests with children there is a gentle slope 400 m high.





Living conditions and prices

All cottages are built from local valuable pine species and lined with shungite, a natural stone with healing properties. These are unique mini-hotels. Medvezhyegorsk is famous for such cozy hotels for vacationers.

Accommodation in the cottage "Dream" is designed for 6 people. There are 2 extra. places. On the ground floor there is a sauna, shower and pool. There is also a toilet and a small gym. On 1-2 floors there are bedrooms, a kitchen with everything you need and a toilet room. In the courtyard there is a Russian stove, a gazebo with barbecue. The cost of renting a cottage per day is from 20,000 rubles to 25,000 rubles, depending on the season. There are also three cottages with 10 beds, costing from 15,000 to 17,000 rubles. The cost of 4 local cottages is from 8,000 to 10,000 rubles per day. It is possible to rent only part of the cottage.

The hotels of Medvezhyegorsk provide their guests with the opportunity to plunge into the pleasant atmosphere of a comfortable stay.

Hotel "Onega"





It is located in the very center of the city. The old four-story building has 65 rooms for 148 people. The rooms are distinguished by the number of beds, as well as amenities. The whiter ones have conveniences inside, the cheaper ones use a shared shower and toilet. Each room has a TV and radio. The hotel has a bar and restaurant. The prices here are quite affordable. The cheapest room costs about 500 rubles per day, the most expensive - up to 4000 per day. In expensive apartments there is a possibility of accommodation with animals.

The hotels of Medvezhyegorsk, designed for recreation, are a combination of high quality service, comfort and friendliness of the staff.
