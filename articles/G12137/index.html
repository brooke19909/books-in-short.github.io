How to get there

Just a kilometer from the Volga River is the park-hotel Dubna. The hotel is located at 5 Zhukovsky Street.

You can get here as follows:

  • From Moscow from the Savelovsky station you can get to Dubna by bus or electric train (the "Sick Volga" stop). Next, it is most convenient to get by taxi (about 100 rubles). You can also get to the hotel by buses No. 1, 11 or minibus No. 12. Get off at the Melody stop. Then it remains to walk a couple of minutes through the park.
  • If you are coming from Moscow by car, you need to follow the Dmitrovskoe highway to Yakhroma. After the traffic light, you need to turn onto Dmitrov's bypass road, at the end of which you will see a monument with a tank. After 300 meters, turn right and follow signs for Dubna. Arriving in the city, get to the monument to the creators of aviation technology, turn right, and after 200 meters you will see the hotel.
  • If you are coming from Tver by car, drive along the Leningradskoye Shosse to turn onto Konakovo. After passing through this locality, follow the signs to Dubna. 200 meters after the monument to the creators of aviation technology, you will see the hotel.

Catering establishments

The Dubna Park Hotel will delight its guests with delicious food and quality drinks. "Camelot" - a cozy restaurant, decorated in accordance with the ideas about the atmosphere of the Middle Ages. The dining rooms are decorated in a knightly style and decorated with designer wooden furniture, shields, weapons, fireplaces, paintings, stucco moldings. The dining room can accommodate up to 50 guests at a time. The chef will arrange a real feast for guests with modern and antique dishes. You can book a table by phone, whose number is available on the official website.

Another favorite place for guests of the Dubna Park Hotel in Russia is the Why Not sports bar. In the cozy room there is a large screen for watching broadcasts of sports matches. You can also play billiards here. There is a separate room for karaoke lovers and an outdoor area with barbecue. You can also book a table by phone.

Reservation Features

You can book a room in the park hotel "Dubna" through the form on the official website or by phone. Two options are possible:

  • For early bookings, the guest is required to pay the cost of one day of stay for three days. Only after that, a room reservation will be considered guaranteed.
  • If the room is booked for the next three days, an oral guarantee by telephone will be sufficient.

In case of cancellation of the reservation or eviction before the declared time, the administration reserves the right to collect a fine from the client in the amount of the daily cost of living.
