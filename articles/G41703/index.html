Omsk hotels. The best living conditions in the city

The city of Omsk, which is the largest administrative, industrial and cultural center, is visited annually by tens of thousands of guests. For them, a well-developed hotel chain has been created and successfully operates in the city. Omsk hotels are comfortable apartments for every taste and budget.

Hotel Ibis



The hotel has a convenient location close to major traffic intersections, close to the city center. The number of rooms includes one hundred and sixty-eight standard rooms with one or two beds. The comfortable rooms are equipped with furniture, household appliances (TV, refrigerator and air conditioning). Throughout the hotel provided the opportunity to access the Internet.

Hotel "Ibis" (Omsk) is not only a place to stay, but also a modern tourist and entertainment complex. The hotel guests can visit the gym, sauna and swim in the beautiful pool.

Dining is organized in the restaurant of the ibis hotel (Omsk). There are also several bars for guests to spend time on. For corporate events (conferences, seminars) there are several conference rooms with a capacity of twenty to seventy people.

Hotel Malibu





Omsk, as a rule, is visited by business people with a business visit. The best option for them is to stay in the central part of the city. This is where the Malibu Hotel is located. The hotel rooms are distinguished by a modern original design and a high level of sound insulation. The Malibu Hotel (Omsk) not only has a convenient location and comfortable living conditions, but also provides its guests with the opportunity to have fun in their free time.

Guests are catering in the hotel restaurant, whose menu includes a variety of dishes of European and Russian cuisines. For entertainment, the hotel offers access to the spa, pool and gym. Near the hotel there are numerous cafes, restaurants and night clubs where you can spend your leisure time.

What to do in the hotel?

On the third and fifth floors there are cafes offering visitors dishes of Russian, Eastern and European cuisines. Restaurant service at the request of guests can make a special menu. Lovers of outdoor activities await the bowling located on the fifth floor. Some hotels in Omsk provide good facilities for guests with children. For younger guests, the Flagman Hotel has a cafe and an entertainment center.

Hotel Mayak





The hotel is located in the historic city center. Nearby are the largest cultural institutions, such as the Drama Theater, Museum of History and Local Lore, River Station, Philharmonic Concert Hall, Musical Theater. The distance of the hotel from the railway station is five kilometers. The airport is six kilometers away. Choosing the central hotels of Omsk for your stay, you can not only relax well, but also see some local attractions.

The Mayak Hotel consists of two large buildings and belongs to the three-star category. The number of rooms consists of one hundred and twenty rooms, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The bathroom in the room is equipped with modern fixtures. The room has telephones with access to urban and intercity communications.

Food and other services

Meals are provided in the restaurant "Salty & Marshmallow". Breakfast is included. Kurosawa Sushi Bar and Lobby Bar are also available.
The security of guests' stay at the hotel is ensured by professional security and 24-hour video surveillance. For storage of valuables and luggage, the services of using a safe deposit box and luggage room are provided.

A machine for shoeshine and the ability to use the ironing room on each floor will help you look neat. The hotel also provides ticket, flower, taxi and car rental services. There is an internet room and a fitness room. The hotel has three modern conference rooms (Mayak, Irtysh and Om) ranging in size from twenty-three to forty-two square meters. Each of them can accommodate twenty-five people. Their equipment has no analogues in the city and will pleasantly amaze the most experienced client.





The aforementioned Omsk hotels are the best choice for city guests visiting the successfully developing modern metropolis with various goals. The level of service in hotels in the city varies depending on their category and the set of services provided, room design. Guests need to pay attention to their location, given the proximity to attractions, administrative buildings.
