Climate

The favorable climate of Thailand, in the conditions of which three crops grow in a year, does not limit the holiday season. Water in the coastal waters of the Andaman Sea is warm all year, like boiled milk. The climate of Phuket is determined by the constant monsoon here. The annual temperature difference here is minimal: from 35 0 C in April to 25 0 C in December.

Accordingly, the Karon Living Room Hotel 2 * hotel under such weather conditions is available for vacationers year-round, in any of the seasons: dry (here it starts in November and lasts until April) and wet (from May to October).

It is for the island of Phuket that seasonality is conditional due to the least rainfall for Thailand. The rainy season here is characterized by their amazing transience: tourists, having waited for an abundant tropical rainfall, which rarely lasts more than an hour, as soon as a wave of a magic wand again sees the sun and the blue sky above them. In such weather, beach vacations are not disturbed, and tourists who fly to the Land of Smiles are less exposed to heat.
