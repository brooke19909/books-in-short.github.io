Popular hotels in Kramatorsk: Sapphire, Kramatorsk, Nirvana

Restaurant and hotel complex "Sapphire"





Cozy hotel located 10 minutes from the city center, next to the forks. The territory is equipped with parking, which is under 24-hour security. The guests and partners are provided with refurbished and elegant rooms equipped with air conditioning, internet and satellite TV.

The complex is focused on the business audience of tourists, like the rest of Kramatorsk hotels. For a comfortable presentation, there is a conference room with technical equipment. In the presence of large banquet facilities for ceremonial and business meetings. Sapphire Hotel is also a chic restaurant with national cuisine and a friendly atmosphere. Room service is available.

Hotel Kramatorsk





Kramatorsk hotels are suitable for versatile leisure. We bring to your attention the Kramatorsk complex, which is successfully concentrated in the central part of the city close to legal, administrative and cultural and entertainment institutions. The multi-storey building is under guard.

On the top floor there is a real casino with slot machines. After an active day, you will find comfortable rooms with a private bathroom and modern furniture. You can satisfy your hunger in a luxurious restaurant or bar.

Hotel Nirvana





Kramatorsk hotels are distinguished by affordable pricing and high service. These include Nirvana. Located a short drive from the train station.
