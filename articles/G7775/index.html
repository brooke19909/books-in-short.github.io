The best beaches of Saki in Crimea - overview, features and reviews of tourists

Saki is a resort city in Crimea. The coastline of the amazing sandy beaches of Saki extends over a length of more than four and a half km and goes far beyond the horizon. In addition, the city is known as a therapeutic mud resort.



City of Saki

Well equipped for tourists, the city is located four to five kilometers from the Black Sea. The usual beaches of Saki, which almost all start in shallow water, must be reached by bus or minibus. They go often. Very important is the salty shallow (only one and a half meters) Lake Saki. It was formed after the end of the ice age, and the natural depression was filled with sea water.

At the bottom, silt was deposited, which was preserved with salt. Over millions of years, most of the water has evaporated, and what is left is called brine. In it, a layer of salt is five meters. Do not rush to go on an expensive trip to the Dead Sea. Saki Lake is twice as rich in minerals and minerals. No wonder the city has about a quarter of the population - people with disabilities who need constant treatment. The territories are equipped with numerous ramps, so that its guests will be easy to move around. Healthy companions and relatives of patients, when they rest from the procedures, will find entertainment and relaxation on the many beaches of Saki.

Golden beaches

The coastline is just a paradise for those who take their children on a trip. Clean and spacious Saki beaches have a gently shelving bottom. Tent camps and unorganized campsites line up near some. Tourists can stay for a day or stay longer if you like it. The sea is usually clear, the water is clear, unless, of course, it storms.
