Hotel Zen Phaselis Princess Resort

Antalya was once a small and inconspicuous Mediterranean town. It was quiet here, and few people knew about such a place at all. Now it is a resort city where you can have a good vacation. The beachfront is dotted with numerous hotels and tourists. There are many hotel complexes in Antalya, so it is very difficult for inexperienced tourists to choose from them. It is especially difficult to decide on a five-star hotel, because there are so many of them! And where better to go with the children? Where to stay? We advise you to pay attention to the five-star Turkish hotel complex Zen Phaselis Princess Resort.



Hotel location

So, if you decide to become a guest of the hotel, you will like its favorable location. The nearest airport is only 74 km away, the town of Antalya, the center of tourism, is only 60 km away, Kemer is 16 km away, and Phaselis and Olympos are only 5 and 25 km away respectively. So, from this place you can visit any city and visit the main attractions of Turkey, get to know the culture, chat with local residents.

The hotel is surrounded by greenery. Beautiful scenic spots and the seashore, the majestic Tahtali mountains are a real resort paradise.



Zen Phaselis Princess Resort - rest on the highest level!

The family-friendly complex was opened in 1988, and since then it has been constantly updated. For example, the last restoration was made in 2011. Now the building is in a beautiful and impeccable form. Two buildings, a huge hotel area, several pools and lots of greenery - this is what the Zen Phaselis Princess Resort leisure complex is . The hotel has 427 rooms of various types. Interestingly, several rooms are located away from the hotel, but there is a bar, pool, cafeteria. Guests will enjoy all the privileges of Ultra All Inclusive. Standard rooms are usually designed for a family of two people, and economy rooms can accommodate from 3 to 6 people. Each room is equipped with a temperature controller, hairdryer, minibar, satellite TV, bathroom and shower, safe, balcony and terrace. The apartments have new, strong and beautiful furniture, paintings, as well as soft Turkish carpets. And for those traveling with their kids, the hotel provides another cot for free.



All Inclusive System

This Zen Phaselis Princess Resort 5 system is very convenient. Guests will have access to several bars: the Lobby bar, the Captain bar, a pool bar, as well as a disco bar, a bar on a hot beach, a service bar, a zen cafe. The chef will delight guests with the cuisine and skill. From seven in the morning until eleven in the evening you can enjoy delicious food: salads, ice cream, desserts, pastries, broths, soups, main dishes and much more. The ladies at this hotel run the risk of ruining their figure, because they cook here very tasty! Children will enjoy the entertainment program of animators, as well as special areas with decorations and swimming pools with water slides. Parents will be able to enjoy their water rides and discos. For sports lovers there is the opportunity to do aerobics, water aerobics, as well as water and motor sports. Women will be pleased with the presence in the Zen Phaselis Princess Resort SPA-center, hairdresser, shopping center, as well as saunas. Such a vacation will be the best for you and your children. Zen Phaselis Princess Resort is a hotel complex that will turn your stay into a real fairy tale!
