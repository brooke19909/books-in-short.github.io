Park Inn by Radisson 4 El Sheikh Resort: review, description, features and reviews

Sharm el-Sheikh has been the most popular resort city of Egypt for many years. Every year, thousands of our compatriots come here to rest here. If you are one of them and prefer to stay in comfortable, but not very expensive hotels, we suggest that you consider the four-star Park In Bai Radisson as an option for accommodation.



Location

Park Inn By Radisson 4 * is located on the Red Sea in Nabq Bay. Naami Bay is 24 kilometers away, and the nearest airport is Sharm El Sheikh, 8 kilometers away. The hotel complex has its own beach. It is 500 meters from the hotel. There is a shuttle service.

Park Inn By Radisson 4 *: traveler reviews from Russia

Just like tourists from other countries of the world, our compatriots today are very attentive to the issue of choosing a hotel. So, they not only study the official description of hotels of interest and ask the operators of travel agencies for advice, but also read the comments of other people who recently had a rest in a particular place. This allows you to get a more complete and reliable picture. After all, no one wants to be disappointed on a long-awaited vacation. In this regard, we bring to your attention the generalized reviews of our compatriots regarding their stay at the four-star Park In Bai Radisson hotel. Just note that most of the guests were satisfied with their choice and can recommend it as a suitable place to stay for other tourists. But we learn about everything in more detail.



Hotel itself

On the whole, our compatriots were satisfied with the accommodation at the Park Inn By Radisson 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh). So, according to them, the rooms here are very spacious, clean and bright. Of course, they are not furnished as luxuriously as in five-star hotels, but all the furniture and appliances are in good working condition. If you suddenly find a malfunction, then you should immediately contact the reception, and the problem will be quickly fixed. As for cleaning, guests note that for Egypt it is quite high quality. In addition, many guests noted that the cleaners almost every day built swan towels, flowers and other figures on beds from towels. Especially this fact pleased the kids.

Many tourists in their reviews note the huge and well-planned territory of the hotel. According to them, there is everything you need for a great vacation - and several swimming pools with sun terraces and bars, and a water park with slides, where both children and adults, sports grounds, and a spa are happy to frolic and much more.



Kitchen

As a rule, travelers rarely come to a consensus about food. However , the Park Inn By Radisson 4 * hotel in this sense can be called a pleasant exception. So, most of the guests were satisfied with the quality of food. According to them, the choice of dishes here is quite diverse, and everything is prepared very tasty. Many praise the grilled meat that cooks in the evenings on the street. In addition, our compatriots say that there are dishes for every taste. So, neither a child, nor a dieter, nor a seasoning lover will remain hungry. There were no complaints about the quality of drinks either.



Beach vacation

According to our compatriots, despite the fact that the Park Inn Radisson Resort 4 * hotel, which we are reviewing, is on the second line, it‚Äôs very close to the sea. So, walking to the beach can be reached in just 7 minutes. In addition, a bus runs between the hotel and the sea all day. A trip on it will take literally one minute. So even older people and families with small children will be comfortable getting to the beach and then returning to the hotel. You can enter the sea here either directly from the shore or from the pier. In the second case, you will immediately find yourself next to a densely populated coral reef. In addition, guests recommend in the morning not to rush to the beach. The fact is that in the morning there are often waves at sea, so swimming is prohibited. After lunch, the sea calms down and you can have a great time. As for the beach itself, despite the fact that it belongs to two hotels, our compatriots found it quite spacious. According to them, sun loungers are always enough here.



Comments on entertainment at the hotel complex

According to tourists, you can have a good time not only on the beach, but also in the hotel itself. So, our compatriots note that in the Park Inn By Radisson Sharm 4 * there are excellent pools. Guests also liked that there is a rather large water park with a lot of water slides. Both children and adults came here with pleasure.

The whole day a team of animators works at the hotel. However, as tourists from Russia and other CIS countries note, its representatives could not be called inventive. So, according to them, the entertainment program was always the same. This also applies to evening performances. Here you should not count on the show of invited artists. In addition, guests complained that the hotel did not have its own club, although there was a room for it. So, if you want to visit a disco, then you can go to the city, accompanied by animators. Tourists used this opportunity, however, according to them, it would be nice to organize a club in a hotel complex.
