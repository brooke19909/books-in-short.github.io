"Nemo" dolphinarium in Feodosia: photos, schedule and prices

Dolphins are animals that are unique in many ways. Often, they are considered one of the main symbols of the sea. Do not underestimate the mental abilities of dolphins. If desired, they can be taught to perform a variety of tricks. Visit the dolphinarium in Feodosia and get to know these amazing animals closer!

Dolphin performance - an entertainment program that does not have age restrictions!

Performances involving animals are most often considered entertainment designed for children and adolescents. We hasten to convince you - the dolphinarium in Feodosia "Nemo" is a place where adult spectators will not be bored.





Everyone is interested in admiring the grace of marine animals and appreciating the coordinated work of trainers and dolphins. Very often people come to performances in dolphinariums as whole families. Even the smallest children are ready to carefully monitor what is happening in the water arena. Nemo is a multidisciplinary entertainment and educational center. Here you can watch a vivid show, as well as learn a lot about the inhabitants of the seas.

Dolphinarium artists in Feodosia

Four bottlenose dolphins participate in the performances of the Nemo Dolphinarium: Oscar, Eve, Alpha and Zaika. The most colorful and interesting show is made by two more artists - Fleur and Terry - sea lions from South America. During the show, animals demonstrate the most unexpected talents. Dolphins enjoy jumping, dancing, playing with a trainer and even singing.

Show programs and additional services

During the tourist season, the dolphinarium in Feodosia delights residents and visitors of the city with three daily performances daily, except Monday. In the evening, you can visit the unique "night" romantic program. For those who want to learn more about marine animals, an educational and entertainment show called "Meet the Dolphins" is offered. Classes are held for small groups, they include a lecture and observation of dolphins.





In addition, each guest can touch the dolphinarium artists with their own hands and even swim with them. Nemo in Feodosia offers its visitors dolphin therapy sessions, as well as swimming with dolphins and diving classes. When ordering any of the listed services, a memory photo with marine artists is provided for free. Each spectator of a day show can take pictures with dolphins (for an additional fee). Today "Nemo" in Feodosia is not only a dolphinarium, but also an aquarium. You can visit the exhibition of marine animals immediately after watching the presentation of dolphins.
