The main resort of the country

Sochi is not only a long city, but also very happy. After all, there is so much sea, sun and beautiful scenery!

The city is rightly called the "summer capital of Russia." The resort has a whole list of favorable climatic factors: it is a warm sea, beautiful beaches, mild climate, clean mountain air, as well as the presence of healing mineral springs and healing mud.





The main wealth of the resort city of Sochi is the sea and a unique climate. The latter was formed not without the help of the mountains of the Caucasus Range. They protected a narrow strip of the coast from cyclones and anticyclones with a powerful wall. The average annual temperature in Sochi is +14.2 degrees. Here, even the most insignificant frosts in winter are considered abnormal.

Administratively, the city of Sochi is divided into four districts: Central, Lazarevsky, Khostinsky and Adler, bordering the unrecognized state - Abkhazia. The main Russian resort has ten sister cities abroad. Among them is California Long Beach.

Ancient and heroic Volgograd

Volgograd is a large city in Russia, which emerged as a fortress in 1589. First of all, it is interesting for its many historical and architectural monuments. Many foreign tourists who are interested in Russia include this city in their travel itinerary.





Volgograd (aka Stalingrad) is a city with a heroic past. History remembers at least four great battles for this place on the map: in 1670, 1708, 1774 and 1942. The last battle for Volgograd, which was fought between the Soviet and fascist troops, was the bloodiest and most fierce. During the Battle of Stalingrad , at least two million people were killed! About a million bombs were dropped on the city itself. But he, like a Phoenix, rose from the ashes ...

Today Volgograd is the largest industrial, transport and cultural center of Russia. In addition, he is much in what is the first and most-most. For example, in Volgograd :
