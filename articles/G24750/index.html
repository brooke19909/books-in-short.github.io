Hotel Seagull Hotel Beldibi 4 *, Turkey, Kemer: photos, reviews of tourists

Holidays in Turkey are still the most economical and popular among our tourists. Someone prefers five-star complexes, and someone and more budget options. In our article we want to talk about one of the four-star complexes of the famous Kemer resort. Seagull Hotel Beldibi 4 *, located in the village of Beldibi, offers its guests an economical vacation and good conditions. Like any institution, the hotel has its advantages and disadvantages, which we want to tell about.

A bit about the hotel ...

Seagull Hotel Beldibi 4 * has long been successfully hosting tourists on its territory. The hotel was built back in 1995 and has since been open to all comers. The hotel complex has a fairly impressive size, its area is about 10 thousand square meters. The last repair of the establishment was carried out in 2007.

As we have already said, the hotel is located in the suburbs of the Kemer resort, or rather, in the village of Beldibi. The hotel is 45 km from the international airport. Such a good location of the institution allows tourists not only to enjoy all the delights of a sea holiday, but also to see the historical places of Kemer.



One of the advantages of Seagull Hotel Beldibi 4 * is its proximity to the sea. The hotel is built on the coast, so its guests do not need to spend precious time relaxing on the road to the beach.

Number of rooms

The room fund of Seagull Hotel Beldibi 4 * is represented by only two categories - these are family apartments and standards. By the way, according to tourists themselves, these are the most popular types of rooms, since most vacationers prefer budget accommodation options. Well, couples simply can not do without a functional two-room suite, in which there will be enough space for both adults and children.

Standard apartments consist of a bathroom and one bedroom. Unfortunately, these rooms do not have a balcony. But they are equipped with safes, minibars, air conditioning, telephones, wireless Internet. Among the standards, there is one for people with special needs.

Family rooms are a bit more spacious, their area varies between 28-35 square meters. The apartments are divided by a partition into two bedrooms: for children and adults. Family rooms are identical to the standard.

In general, the number of rooms is not very diverse, but for a four-star hotel this is quite a normal situation, since such establishments are focused on more budget apartments. It is worth noting that the minimum cost of a tour for two guests at Seagull Hotel Beldibi 4 * (Turkey, Beldibi) starts for seven days from 55 thousand rubles.

Attractions Beldibi

Outwardly, Beldibi is not much different from other Turkish villages on the Mediterranean coast. Its main street, stretching along the sea, is named after the famous hero - Ataturk. Along the street stretched shopping malls, cafes, and numerous hotels.

There is no promenade familiar to any resort in Belbidi. There is only a beautiful road that stretches along the Ataturk Park. Walking around the village, you can admire the mosque. The building, although not ancient, was built according to all the canons of oriental craftsmanship. The main attraction of the resort can be called stunningly beautiful mountains. Coniferous vegetation, breathtaking landscapes and mountain gorges - all this is Beldibi.



To see the local beauty, you should go along the bed of the Beldibi River to the mountain gorge. However, such a trip can only be made as part of an excursion group. because in the local forests live mountain boars. Tourists who are interested in ancient monuments will be interested to see the Beldibi cave, found in 1959. It preserved ancient drawings. Currently, the excavations of the village have become a popular tourist attraction. There is also a beautiful waterfall nearby.
