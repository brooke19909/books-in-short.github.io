Sofia, Bulgaria: description and attractions

Sofia (Bulgaria) is not only the largest city in the country, but also its capital. A large number of tourists visit the local attractions annually. Travelers are attracted to this city by the beautiful architecture of historical buildings, harmoniously combined with modern infrastructure.

Sofia (Bulgaria) is considered the cultural center of the state. The population here is more than one and a half million. At the same time, there are about 20 educational institutions providing higher education in the city. Sofia will not leave indifferent those who are interested in Bulgaria, because in this city you can get acquainted with the culture and traditions of the local population.

In the capital, travelers can visit the Philharmonic, enjoy the sounds of classical music at the conservatory. In addition, tourists are encouraged to visit local museums. One of the largest is Historical. Also interesting are the exhibits in the Ethnographic, Archaeological, Zoological Museums.





The amazing location of the city of Sofia (Bulgaria). Photos of the capital show the beauty of not only the local architecture, but also the nature that spans around.

The metropolis is located at the foot of Vitosha Mountain, the slopes of which in the 20th century were turned into a National Park.

In the city itself there are also a lot of parks and squares. Lovers of greenery will be delighted with the local vegetation.

One of the most ancient buildings of the capital is the church of St. Sophia.

It belongs to the 5-6 centuries, and it is considered the hallmark of the modern city. All travelers are primarily sent on an excursion to this temple.

Of interest to tourists is the church of St. George. The unique atmosphere and beautiful murals and murals will appeal to almost all travelers interested in the history of Bulgaria. The city also has Turkish mosques, the walls of which are several centuries old.





The most beautiful boulevard is considered Boulevard Vitosha, which begins near the cathedral. Past numerous shopping galleries, it reaches directly to the Palace of Culture Square. The city of Sofia (Bulgaria) is considered one of the most beautiful European cities. Tourists are advised to visit the quarter on the southwestern outskirts, called Boyana. It is adjacent directly to the foot of Vitosha, and here is a small church, which UNESCO has included in the list of world heritage. The murals here date from the 13th century.





The mountain itself is a great place for a winter holiday. It has hotels with developed infrastructure, lifts, viewing platforms. After the onset of the season, this area is quite lively. Tourists come to Vitosha from all over the world.

Sofia (Bulgaria) annually attracts a huge number of travelers. And not in vain, because all the conditions for cultural relaxation, walking in the most beautiful places and exploring the history of the country are created here.
