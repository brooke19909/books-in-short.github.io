The four-star Saritas Hotel (Turkey / Alanya) is a great option for a budget holiday

Everyone needs an annual vacation by the sea, where you can forget about the hectic everyday life, work and everyday problems. But not everyone can afford expensive tours abroad - it is not at all necessary to be a millionaire in order to spend a vacation with high quality and profit, for example, in Turkey. This resort is very popular all over the world, because it has a developed tourism industry, interesting and numerous sightseeing routes, as well as quality service.





We offer you an inexpensive “Saritas” hotel (Turkey), founded in 1995 in the bosom of wildlife, at the edge of the sea, surrounded by a tropical park in the small resort village of Obagol, 3 km from the center of Alanya. The complex is famous for its hospitality and first-class amenities. Visitors are greeted as the most expensive and long-awaited people.

Saritas Hotel (Turkey) is a series of 7-story buildings in a beautiful bright design, which are surrounded by outdoor pools. Couples with children, retirees and business travelers who combine work and leisure will enjoy it here. The contingent of tourists consists mainly of Germans and Russians. The atmosphere in the guesthouse is friendly and serene.





Number of rooms

A comfortable stay awaits you in the hotel rooms. Room size - from 16 m2. For large companies and families with children, more spacious rooms are offered. Regardless of class, all rooms are decorated in a calm color scheme, which sets in a positive mood. In personal use a bathroom with hygiene products.

There is a small balcony with an amazing panorama of a flowering garden with palm trees or the sea. The amenities include air conditioning, satellite TV, a telephone line, a bar with drinks (for a fee). You can use the safe - for a fee. The price includes daily cleaning and a change of bed (scheduled).





Nutrition

Three meals a day awaits guests at the hotel. Saritas Hotel (Turkey) has restaurants and bars in the adjacent area. Qualified chefs work in the establishments who are happy to prepare exotic dishes of regional, national and international cuisine for guests. Connoisseurs of elite alcoholic beverages will be pleasantly surprised by the wide selection of wine, cognac and whiskey.

Beach





Saritas Hotel (Turkey) is famous for its cleanest private beach and warm azure sea. He receives only praise from tourists. The coastline is quite wide, equipped with sun loungers and sunshades. Those who want to get a shot of adrenaline can surf, dive, parachute, ride a banana or scooter.

Hotel Description

It features large open-air pools with one water slide. Sports activities are offered: billiards, pinball, table tennis, darts, volleyball and football. Available transport rental, beauty salon, souvenir shops, sauna, massage room.





In addition, Saritas Hotel (Turkey) has a television room, travel agency, laundry service, post office, exchange office and a disco club. There is a conference room with 100 seats and wireless Internet. There is a play area for children, a development center and a shallow pool. In the daytime, the kids are entertained by a team of fun animators and clowns.

It is not necessary to have a lot of money for a quality and informative vacation. It is Turkey (Alanya) that offers interesting excursions, entertainment venues and a lot of pleasant things at an affordable price . Hotel Saritas is one of the best places for people with low incomes.
