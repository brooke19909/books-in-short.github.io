Les Colombes 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Club Hotel Le Colomb 3 rises on the coastline enveloping the northern part of Hammamet. It is equidistant from the cities of Hammamet and Nabeul and is only 6 kilometers from both. Tunis Carthage Airport is 65 kilometers from Club Hotel Les Colombes 3. The hotel complex is adjacent to the private picturesque sandy beach of Tunisia. In addition, two more beaches are adjacent to it - Omar Khayyam and Bel Azur.

Description of Hotel Club Les Colombes 3 *

The Club Hotel Les Colombes 3 (Hammamet) is family oriented. In it for children spend a lot of leisure activities. With the onset of school holidays in the hotel complex opens a mini-club for children. The hotel has a pottery workshop. Potters involve children and adults in a free workshop.





The club hotel is equipped with four pools - two outdoor, indoor heated and children's. The sea, framed by a gorgeous beach, is one hundred meters from the hotel. At the beach, visitors use sun loungers and parasols for free. They have access to the Turkish bath, jacuzzi, massage room and sauna.

Three rooms work for them: reading room, TV and conference room. The club-hotel has a room with slot machines, a gift shop. Guests can use the services of a hairdresser, currency exchange, medical center, free parking and laundry.

Prices for hotel accommodation and additional services in it are affordable. One night for two adults without children in a standard room (meals - half board) costs 5200-5300 rubles. Two adults (without children), settling in standard apartments on an all-inclusive basis, pay for accommodation at the rate of 6800 for one night.

Room facilities



The air-conditioned apartments are equipped with a bathroom, toilet and telephone, TV, high-speed Wi-Fi and a safe. From the rooms there is access to a balcony from which sea views open. Room service is excellent. They are cleaned daily, and free towels are provided. Bed linen is changed twice a week. Hygiene products in the bathroom do not need to be paid extra.

Club Les Colombes 3 places a lot of emphasis on sports activities. On the beach, vacationers enjoy water skiing, bicycles and scooters. They are engaged in windsurfing and parachuting, sailing.
