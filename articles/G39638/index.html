Services

If the guests have a desire to relax in the fresh air, then you can visit one of the pools. There are two of them in the hotel. Nearby is the sun terrace. In addition, there is the opportunity to play table tennis, and a massage parlor is open for lovers of cosmetic services. Service and maintenance

At the hotel, guests are treated with respect and attention. The staff is always ready to help in resolving any issues that a guest has. Upon personal request, food and drinks can be delivered to your room. If a guest is used to driving his own car and even on vacation cannot give up this habit, he can rent a car - this feature also provides Lucy Beach Hotel 5 *.

The registration desk, which records arrivals and departures, is constantly working, so potential guests can not worry about having to wait a long time for check-in. Ironing and dry cleaning services are also available. Laundry service is available. There is even a fax point at the hotel. By the way, there you can make photocopies of documents, if necessary. It should be noted that Lucy Beach Hotel 5 * (Greece) is not only a hotel complex, but also a venue for various official and entertainment events. The hotel has two rooms - for banquets and for conferences, which are regularly rented by the organizers of various events.





Customized services

At the request of guests, the administration can provide a safe, personal air conditioning, turn on the heating. In addition, the hotel has rooms designed for people with disabilities.
