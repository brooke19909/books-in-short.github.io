Sea, beach and water activities

The coast of the Red Sea is really far from the Delta Sharm Resort 4 * hotel. Nevertheless, the hotel has its own beach in Naama Bay. Delta Sharm Resort 4 * does not have a pontoon, and there is a reef, but tourists are delighted nonetheless. The beach is gentle, good for children or those who like to splash in warm water.
