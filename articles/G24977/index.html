Geographical position





The administrative-territorial unit and the small village of the same name are located in the southern part of the Indonesian island. In addition to its exclusively tourist focus, the village is also famous for others. It is the largest fishing center in Bali. Jimbaran is washed by the Indian Ocean, and in the east the isthmus connects it with the Bukit Peninsula. Just 5 km from the village there is an international Balinese airport.

The vast majority of the local population are Balinese, religion is Hinduism. The place is crowded due to tourists arriving in these places year-round.

Facts from the story

It is not possible to say exactly when Jimbaran was founded. The first written records of him in Balinese chronicles date back to the 15th century. They make it clear that the settlement arose several decades before, at a time when the southern part of the island belonged to the kingdom of Majapahit.

Initially, the economic structure of the settlement was a typical fishing village. Tourists appeared in these places for the first time in the 1920s, they arrived from the Netherlands. During World War II, representatives of the American and European intelligentsia and creative people lived in Jimbaran for a long time.

The development of the tourism industry was interrupted for a long time due to the Japanese occupation, the subsequent war for independence and the political isolation of the country. The revival and growth of interest in the island began in the 1970s, then the corresponding infrastructure began to develop.

Jimbaran Hotels

The resort sector of the economy is leading not only for Jimbaran, but also for the whole country. This means that here you will find absolutely everything for a comfortable stay, including great beaches, restaurants and cafes, markets and even attractions.

One of the main advantages of the village is the variety of accommodation options in it. The choice is so great that you can find guest houses and hostels for the budget-conscious tourist and lodging for a person who is willing to spend the lion's share of the trip budget on the luxury and beauty of five-star hotels. Holidays for every taste and wallet provide many opportunities.

In Jimbaran, there is the Four Seasons Resort Bali at Jimbaran Bay, owned by the famous hotel chain around the world. The luxurious complex, located in the mountains, has comfortable rooms with stunning sea views. He will give his guests an unforgettable experience.

When buying tickets to Bali, do not forget about the cheapest accommodation option - hostels and guest houses. The cost of one night in them starts from 2-3 thousand rubles.

Jimbaran Beach is magnificent. Clean and spacious, with azure-colored sea coast. Calm coastal waters, a low wave, bottom topography and a rich underwater world are an ideal place for those who are just starting to learn the basics of diving and surfing. Bali, Jimbaran: Local Market

Of course, the main attraction of Jimbaran is its beaches with beautiful sunsets and fish restaurants. People come here not for architecture, night clubs or ancient history (although it is), but for relaxation and quiet relaxation.

But lying on the beach, you see, is also a little boring. Moreover, how, if not in the process of walking, to enjoy the surrounding beauty and local color.





The first step is to go to the fish market. The village is the center of fisheries, and it would be an unforgivable mistake not to look at all the amazing creatures that local people are pulling in from the sea. The bulk of buyers are representatives of restaurants, but there are tourists. The best time to visit is early morning. You can try a lot, and it will cost less than in a restaurant.

Uluwatu Temple

Uluwatu Temple is one of the six most significant temples of the island of Bali. Jimbaran is located nearby, so you can get to the sights on your own by a rented car or moped. What is one of the key temples of the island good for?

Firstly, its incredible location on top of a cliff, at an altitude of 70 meters above sea level. It offers stunning views directly to the Indian Ocean. If you are lucky, then you can get to the magnificent festive ceremonies, which are held every six months, or to daily performances - dances "ketchak". According to the chronicles, the temple was founded a thousand years ago.





You should be careful on the way to the temple. The monkeys who came from the jungle are very curious and without any twinges of conscience steal glasses and cameras from gape tourists.

Tourists reviews

The village with beaches and luxury hotels is a popular holiday destination, so there are always plenty of tourists here. Many of them in their reviews after the trip noted that a huge flow of people from all over the world spoiled local residents, the service at times very high prices, to say the least, is lame.

Most travelers, according to popular tourist portals, speak of Jimbaran as a paradise. Among the main advantages include its location, clean beaches and the beauty of nature. The admiration of holidaymakers is also caused by local gastronomic pleasures. Fish market, restaurants, cafes, street food - all this is worthy of attention and tasting.





Jimbaran (reviews of tourists confirm this) is a place for a relaxing holiday, for example, during a honeymoon or a romantic trip, as well as holidays with children. There is no that vibrant and active nightlife with parties and dancing, as in the center of the island.
