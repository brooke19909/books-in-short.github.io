When is the best time to relax in Vietnam?

This country is mountainous, and the climate here is predominantly subtropical. Therefore, lovers to soak up the sun in the winter will not be very good here, except in the south. It is there that in December the most favorable holiday season begins. Until May, in the southern regions there is practically no rain. But the time of rest in central Vietnam, where the Nha Trang resort is located, is completely different. In winter it rains and is rather cold, almost like in Europe. But in May summer begins, and the humid heat reigns. You can come here in February, and the most comfortable stay is until September. Then heavy rains begin, and it is not at all interesting to spend a vacation in this way. Although there are, of course, tourists who are in love with Vietnam and are ready to live here all year round, only moving from one resort to another.



Holidays in Nha Trang

This city is not for nothing called the "beach capital" of Vietnam. A cheerful and cheerful resort annually attracts tens of thousands of people. The landscapes here are just like on murals or in advertising clips. Tourists can not stop looking at the white sand and palm trees hanging over it, green islands with fanciful cliffs rising from the sea. Great diving and colorful fish await diving enthusiasts. All this is Nha Trang. Vietnam, whose photo is often adorned with offers from travel agencies, is represented mainly by pictures of the beaches of this resort. Nightlife with its noisy parties and dances until the drop is also Nha Trang. Would you like to get healthy? At your service are SPA salons, peeling with fish, baths with therapeutic mud and many other secrets that tourists describe in their reviews. And if you are interested in the ancient culture of the country, then you can admire the ancient Cham towers, which have been towering right in the city center for many centuries.

Although Nha Trang is a very famous resort, there are not many expensive and fashionable hotels here. Several luxurious fives, two of which are not in the city, but in the distance - one on the island of entertainment, and the second in a special bay. A couple of "fours" right on the seashore (some even have fenced beaches for guests). According to the responses of tourists, Nha Trang 3-star hotels often have pools, amazing service, as well as ‚ÄúFrench‚Äù full-wall windows, from which you can see the sea directly from the bed where you are resting. There are about fifty such hotels in the town. But the most popular, both among Russian tourists and among the local population, are the so-called low-category hotels - two-star, or even less star-shaped. These hotels are inferior to three rubles in size, the cheapest rooms are without windows or with bars. But there are all the services there - bike rental, travel agencies, cleaning and ironing. These mini-hotels are very simple and convenient, they work mainly in the season, and in their maintenance they are absolutely not inferior to their dear brothers. So tourists are increasingly choosing this kind of budget housing.

Sea

From Indochine Hotel Nha Trang 2 you can reach the resort's central beach in five minutes. A beautiful boulevard planted with coconut trees stretched along it . Equipped beach - there are sunbeds, umbrellas. It is municipal, but very clean. Directly on the spot can arrange water, beer, pizza, cocktails, ice cream. The beach is very safe, nothing disappears, you can safely leave things and swim. The water is very clean and clear. There are no waves in the morning, but after lunch there may be wind. Opposite the beach is Winperl, an amusement park that spans an entire island. Nha Trang is very famous for this wonderful place. There is a water park, an oceanarium, and a show of seals with dolphins, and one of the best sand beaches that are used for body peeling. The island can be reached by cable car, laid directly across the sea - this is an interesting and very beautiful sight.
