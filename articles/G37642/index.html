Autumn compositions for every taste

Any time of the year generously gives us its colors. So, autumn compositions will be able to decorate not only our apartment or office, but also a cafe, restaurant, club ... Everywhere we want to feel comfortable and relaxed, natural materials should be used in the decor.

Of course, the easiest way to do this is at home. But for a restaurant or cafe, autumn compositions are usually developed by a florist or professional designer. However, the implementation of them will not require large time or financial costs. After all, materials can be found even in the nearest park.

So, what is required for our autumn compositions to decorate and revitalize rooms intended for relaxation or work? They do not have to be complicated at all. All you need - leaves, twigs, cones, tree bark, fruits - you can collect in a forest or park. Excessive decor - for example, gilding or silvering - is completely unnecessary. Of the additional materials, it will be enough to stock up with red and green ribbons, braid, sparkles in the spray, glue.

Autumn compositions by themselves are quite bright thanks to natural colors. Twigs of mountain ash with berries, chrysanthemums, dried summer flowers , combined with a modest decor of earth color, with transparent glass vessels can work wonders. Orange physalis boxes, young cones, yellow and green acorns look bright and attractive. It is especially important to create a climate, an aura of comfort and warmth in those places where people come - in cafes, restaurants, offices. Customers are subconsciously tuned to a more positive mood by calm music, the smell of coffee, the autumn composition, the photo of which we present below. By the way, in addition to ornamental shrubs with berries, apples, chestnuts, nuts, and ivy leaves look original and attractive in bouquets. Autumn compositions can be placed in glass vessels, in porcelain vases and even ... in pumpkins.

Although Halloween is mostly associated with these fruits, their bizarre shape makes florists increasingly use them in their creations. Dried slices of fruit, shells of all kinds of nuts, as well as ears of wheat, rye, decorative corn, sunflowers, cones - from bright to muted colors - the choice of materials is as huge as never before in the fall. Center the candles in discreet beige or cream tones.

The craft itself - an autumn composition - can be on a large dish or in a vase, on a piece of wood bark or a decorative tray. Additional coloring is not required, it is the dried leaves and flowers, with their charm of wilting, that will give the interior a unique flavor. If you want bright accents, rosehip branches or clusters of mountain ash, sunflowers and chrysanthemums are useful.

Maybe not very original, but autumn compositions with reeds look natural and elegant. They are suitable for almost any interior - from the restrained classic to the modern avant-garde.
