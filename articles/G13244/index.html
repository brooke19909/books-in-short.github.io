To Moscow from Ukhta and back: ways of quick or inexpensive travel

The city of Ukhta is the second most populated in the Komi Republic. There is a good transport connection between Ukhta and the capital of the country, so residents of both cities can easily reach their destination.

Trains and buses go to Moscow from Ukhta, planes fly, and can be reached by private car. It remains to choose the best mode of transport and go!

Distance Moscow-Ukhta

If you measure the distance on a ruler, you get only 1270 km. That is how many kilometers separates Moscow and Ukhta.

However, in fact, roads stretched for 1,630 km. If you go on foot, you will have to overcome 1540 km.

On the road by car

If you are planning to go to Moscow from Ukhta by car, you need to get the best route.

The distance will be 1632 km, you will have to spend on the road, depending on driving style and traffic density, from 20 to 23 hours. Traveling by car has its advantages and allows you to independently determine:

  • departure time;
  • route selection;
  • number and frequency of stops;
  • the possibility of deviation from the route;
  • the presence of fellow travelers.

From Ukhta to the nearby city of Syktyvkar, the R-176 highway leads, along the entire route (more than 300 km) there are four gas stations.

Having reached the city of Kurchatov, then you can choose and make your own route:

  • Continue on P-176 through Kirov to the city of Kotelnich, where take to the right and go onto the P-243 highway. She leads through Kostroma, Yaroslavl and Sergiev Posad to Moscow. Experienced travelers note that the Kirov-Kostroma segment does not differ in high-quality road surface, but a small number of settlements allows you to move quickly. From Kostroma to Yaroslavl, a well-groomed road is laid.
  • Turn right from Kurchatov and go through Ilyinsko-Podomskoye, Kotlas, Veliky Ustyug, Totma, and in Vologda go onto the M-8 highway.
  • In the city of Kotelnich turn left and take the R-176. On the road, there are such settlements as Yaransk, Uren. In Nizhny Novgorod, the highway will go into the M-7, along it enter the capital through Vladimir.

If between Moscow and Ukhta the distance is difficult to overcome in one day, or there is no shift driver, then near large cities (Yaroslavl, Kirov) you can stop for a vacation in numerous motels and hotels.

Railway connection between Ukhta and Moscow

It is convenient to travel from Ukhta to the capital and back by train. On the way you will have to spend (depending on the composition) from 27 to 31 hours. The following flights depart from Ukhta to the capital (buying tickets, you need to specify the schedule):

  • at 01:25, the corporate team 021N "Polar Arrow";
  • at 01:25 a train arriving from Labytnangi, 209M;
  • at 05:09, train 223 from Sosnogorsk;
  • at 06:21 the corporate staff of Vorkuta 041M;
  • at 10:33 again from Vorkuta 375;
  • at 16:07 Vorkuta squad 287.

All flights, except 287, arrive at the Yaroslavsky station. Flight 287 I arrives in Belorussian.

From Moscow to Ukhta all flights are in the afternoon:

  • at 12:50;
  • at 13:05;
  • at 20:35;
  • at 21:50.

In trains, fares are set according to the level of comfort:

  • seats from 1533 rubles .;
  • reserved seat from 2500 rubles .;
  • coupe from 3822 to 5800 rubles. (depending on the composition);
  • Lux (only in 021N) almost 9 thousand rubles.

When buying a ticket, you can check if the train has air conditioning, a restaurant car, sockets and other amenities.

Air travel

The fastest way to get from Ukhta to Moscow and back is by plane. There are enough flights in this direction to choose a convenient time of departure and arrival. You will have to spend only two hours on the road.

From Domodedovo (Moscow) to Ukhta:

  • flight KO 750, departure at 10:40 (does not run on weekends);
  • KO 707 at 22.05 (does not fly on Saturday).

From Ukhta planes of the Komiaviatrans Airlines fly out:

  • at 07:30 flight KO708;
  • at 15:55 flight UT180 or U4180.

The cost of the flight is from 4241 rubles and above.

By bus from Ukhta to the capital

A good option is to go to Moscow from Ukhta by bus. However, it must be taken into account that there is no direct bus service, you will have to do a transfer in Kirov. Traveling without taking into account the waiting time for a transfer takes about 20 hours. Buses arrive in Moscow at the Teply Stan metro station.

The fare is 2500-3000 rubles.

You can get from the city of Ukhta, located in the north of the Komi Republic, to Moscow in a variety of ways, it all depends on the financial capabilities and time that you can spend on the road.
