Italy. San Remo - city of lights

What could be better than relaxing on the Ligurian Sea? In Italy, many different attractions are concentrated that are interesting in their own way, they can not be found equal anywhere. San Remo is one of the most beautiful cities in the country. It is located on the seafront, is considered one of the most expensive and popular resorts. The city is especially beautiful at night. Once upon a time, members of the royal families, noble people and aristocrats came to enjoy the indescribable scenery and atmosphere of the resort.



It is Italy, San Remo that is famous for its unique places for relaxation. The city often hosts contests and celebrations of a global scale, as well as festivals, car races, fights and much more. One of the hallmarks of this place is the annual flower fair. It is here that you can see rare plant species, some of which are grown in private households. Italy (San Remo in particular) is full of ancient castles, palaces, buildings. Against this background, various competitions, fashion shows and other events seem more successful and vibrant.

The Italian resort began to be popular in the nineteenth century. Then San Remo did not fall silent day or night. Since then, every tourist dreamed of visiting the "city of lights." The most popular casino is San Remo, as well as old quarters and picturesque streets. In the city you can see many villas of outstanding personalities. It is best to move around the resort on foot, as it is small in size and there are a lot of people who want to visit it. San Remo (Italy), reviews of which are positive and colorful, is famous for its numerous boutiques, vibrant parks, gardens, restaurants, azure beaches. In this regard, every traveler will be able to find something to his taste: rest in a relaxed atmosphere or wild entertainment from morning to night. In the city you can often get to another holiday or fashion show, as well as sailing regattas, rowing competitions and tennis tournaments.



Italy (San Remo - the most popular and expensive resort in the country) is famous for its unsurpassed cuisine and friendly population. In many cities you can visit wonderful beaches. For example, in San Remo you can visit the pebble coast, soak up in specially equipped places and visit interesting excursions. Tourists should definitely admire the Cathedral of Christ the Savior, which was opened in 1913. The building is decorated with stone carvings, tiles and other decorative elements. In any store, a map of San Remo (Italy) is sold, with which you can easily navigate and get to the desired place.



If the trip is planned for a beach holiday, it is best to go to the resort in July and August. During the off-season, you can visit various excursions and walks (of course, it is best to do this in the company of a guide). Italy (San Remo) is warm and beautiful even in winter. Basically, the air temperature does not fall below thirteen degrees Celsius. This is a great option for a winter holiday for residents of Russia, Ukraine and more northern countries.
