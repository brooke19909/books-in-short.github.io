How to make a rose out of paper - step by step description, schemes and ideas

Rose is one of the most loved by many flowers. Often it is placed on greeting cards, pictures, used in decorating the premises for the ceremony. How to make a rose out of paper? Many people think that the process of making such a beautiful flower is very complex and time-consuming, do not even dare to start its production, replacing with simpler, in their opinion, colors.

In this article, we will introduce the reader to how to make a rose out of paper with simple and understandable methods. There are several of them. Consider them all along with step-by-step manufacturing instructions. After reading the text of the article, everything will become clear to you. You will not only learn how easy it is to make a rose out of paper, but you will also be happy to put this knowledge into practice.

Manufacturing methods

There are several simple methods for making such a beautiful flower. The easiest option is to twist the roses from a long strip of colored double-sided paper, in which one side remains flat and the outer edge is cut out by a wavy line. After cutting, it remains only to curl the workpiece around the stick or any other rod.

You can apply another method of composing a flower from individual petals. How to make a flower - a rose from paper? You can cut individual petals of different sizes according to the patterns and make a flower by gluing elements to the middle.

Some masters cut a pattern from several petals at once. This is faster since a large number of petals are cut out by folding paper in several layers.

The product is assembled either on a sheet of cardboard, or put on a thin wire or wooden stick. It turns out a voluminous flower that can be put in a vase or arrange a gift bouquet.

Next, we will examine in more detail how to make a rose out of paper on your own.

Folding a flower from a strip

To make a flower like in the photo below, you need to cut a square out of bright double-sided colored paper. Then, with a simple pencil, starting from the middle, draw a line in a spiral. Try to keep the width of the strip equal in any part of it. Then, with scissors, a strip is carefully cut along the drawn line.

Twisting starts from the outer edge to the center point. How easy it is to make a rose out of paper, you already know. The last turn is attached to the glue gun. To prevent the rose from breaking up when carrying it, it is better to strengthen the base by sticking the craft on a small circle in the center. It remains only to cut out a carved leaf from dark green paper and stick it next to the flower.

Location options

This option can be attached to a greeting card or glued to the cover of a photo album. Of the colors made in this way, you can make a wall panel or decorate a photo frame. A rose attached to a thin stalk will look beautiful. It can be created from a wooden skewer, thin wire, a cocktail tube or a simple twig from a tree or shrub.

Room decoration

For a holiday with roses, collected from thin strips of paper, you can decorate not only the walls of the room. A garland of roses made of different types and colors of paper looks beautiful. Necessarily it should be dense sheets. The color scheme can be varied.

Looks beautiful flowers made not only from double-sided colored paper, but also collected from the analogue with a printed print. So that the flowers on the rope do not pile up, but are evenly distributed along the entire length, you need to make two holes on the outside of the roses, and when pulling the rope, you need to tie a knot in front of each of them.

Template making

From the square sheets of double-sided colored paper by folding, you can cut several petals at once. First, the square is folded in half, then four times, then the template blank is folded diagonally, a triangle is obtained, in which the corner is cut off in a semicircle and the external acute angle is processed in the same way. It turns out a workpiece from the same petals. How to make a rose out of paper? To make a flower, it is necessary that the parts have a different number of petals. In the third frame in the photo below, it is seen that a different amount of extra petals is cut from each blank. One to four. The ends of the parts are fastened to PVA glue.

The edges of the petals are bent outward. To do this, you can use a round pencil or felt-tip pen. When the templates are prepared, the assembly of the flower begins. First, one petal glued around it is glued to the rod of wire. Then a small part is attached, then a larger one. With each layer, the number of petals increases. They are placed with a shift in a checkerboard pattern. So the petals will not overlap each other.

In the end, it remains to cut a leaf of green color along the contours. Looks nice option corrugated manufacturing. For this, the leaf is folded with an "accordion". Folds must be ironed carefully.

How to make a rose from corrugated paper?

Several squares of different colors are cut. Then you need to choose the core on which the corrugated paper is wound. The sheet is not completely wrapped. Be sure to leave 5 or 6 cm on the connection of the ends of the craft. Then it is pulled together by pressing. When 5 petals are made, you can start creating a flower. How to make a rose out of paper in stages, read further in the article.

A strip of green corrugated paper is first wound on a thin stick. This will be the stalk for the flower. Also, you first need to cut the leaf.

Making roses from corrugated paper is easy with your own hands. It is necessary in turn to wind the free ends of the elements on the rod. They are located with an offset so that each petal is clearly visible. At the end of a wide strip of green paper, all the windings on the top of the wire are fastened together. You can cut sharp sepals, so the flower will look even more beautiful. Lastly, a leaf is glued. It can be carved.

Now you know how to make a big rose from corrugated paper. Next, consider another interesting option for making such a beautiful flower, but with a surprise inside.

How to make a rose from corrugated paper, read on. First, a green strip of corrugated paper is wound around the wire. This is a stalk on which a candy wrapper with a candy is first attached. Its edges can be fixed with transparent tape. Then cut out several large circles with an elongated lower part. When wrapping them on a stalk, they are glued around the wire to a PVA or glue gun. Then sepals with sharp ends are cut out and a wide strip of green paper is wound around a bundle of paper. It turns out a thickening, as on a natural living flower.

Flower composition

How to make a flower - a rose from paper? There is another option for the manufacture of such crafts. Individual petals are cut according to the drawn patterns. In the photo below you can see how such a detail looks. An incision is made in the center from below, then the lower edges are shifted to each other and glued together. Thus, a volumetric lobe is obtained.

All parts are placed in layers. Each subsequent row consists of smaller petals. They are placed with a lateral shift, so that all elements are clearly visible and do not overlap each other.

Wavy petals

You already know how to make a rose out of paper in stages, but there are many patterns for cutting the petals of this beautiful and magnificent flower.

The photo shows how a wavy version of the cut petals looks like. However, making them right out of paper is half the battle. The main thing is to beautifully bend the edges of each element. To do this, you can use a round pencil, felt-tip pen or pen. On the left side, the corner bends inward, and on the right side bends outward. When glued, the flower turns out to be real, because all the petals are effectively bent in different directions.

Making large flowers

Often when decorating rooms for ceremonies, such as weddings or anniversaries, there is a need for the manufacture of flowers of huge sizes. How to make a big rose out of paper? Manufacturing methods are no different from standard size crafts. The only thing that is difficult is the manufacture of large-sized petals. The paper is used thick, so they use a glue gun to connect the petals.

But there is also a plus in the large sizes of each element. The advantage lies in the ease of creating a petal of any shape. You can make the part completely wavy, with beautiful bends or cut the petal with sharp corners.

Such large crafts are located not only on the walls of the room, but they also decorate chairs for a wedding feast, they are placed on separate tables, decorate doors and arches for a wedding ceremony. Such large roses look very solemn, but, of course, a lot of material will go away, so the cost of such a decor is significant.
