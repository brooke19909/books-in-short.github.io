Outboard electric motor - the best solution for the fisherman

Today, to ensure successful fishing, you will need to use an outboard electric motor. This device will allow serious fishermen to climb even in hard-to-reach places, which will ensure a good catch.



The design of the electric motor was amenable to improvement over many years. Applied power devices varied from 12 to 24 or 36 volts.

The determining factor when buying a product is the size of the boat. A powerful device will require a more advanced shuttle, since only one battery for the engine can weigh more than 30 kg.

A boat electric motor has many advantages over the gasoline version. Firstly, this device is much quieter. Secondly, fishermen have the opportunity to conveniently maneuver the boat. Depending on the drive, only a slight movement of the arm or leg is enough for the ship to sail in the right direction.

Before installing the device on the front of the boat, you must make sure that its bow has enough space, while the bottom surface should be flat. You can also install the device at the stern of the vessel: this allows you to make a boat of any type.

This engine arrangement is best suited for canoes or small skies. For a ship up to five meters in size, you can also purchase a 12-volt boat electric motor. However, you must consider how much fish will be caught. For long swim it is better to use a motor with 36 volts.

When calculating the power of the unit, one should also determine the number of people who will be in the boat. If you have to fish alone, then a simpler engine is enough, if you are going to fish with a company, then a weak device may not be able to bring the shuttle to planing. The weight of the outboard engine also plays an important role and makes it necessary to take into account the number of accompanying people.



A more powerful electric motor for the boat has considerable weight, and the battery also has a large mass. Thus, moving the device one to the ship out of the car and vice versa, and also installing it on the transom can be quite a challenge.

You should not choose an excessively powerful engine, since the operation of an inflatable boat with such an aggregate can be simply dangerous. In addition, when using a high-performance device, there is a risk of damage to the transom, which will lead to repair of the vessel.

Choosing an engine, you can immediately say that imported devices are in many ways superior to domestic ones, and some craftsmen assemble their own boat electric motor.

You should not especially think about choosing a brand, since the quality and functionality of all leading manufacturers are approximately the same. Although Japanese and American models are slightly better in their characteristics than European counterparts.
