Vintage Hotel "New World" (Crimea). Reviews, photos

Crimea is an amazing corner of the planet, which is supposedly specially created for enjoying relaxation on the shores of the gentle sea. Among the many local resorts, a completely special place is occupied by the village of New World. It has been known for over 100 years as a center of Crimean winemaking.

It was there that in 2012 the New World vintage hotel was built. He was assigned the category of ‚Äú4 stars‚Äù, by the way, quite deservedly, because there you can organize an excellent vacation, including the whole family, along with young children.





Description

Vintage Hotel "New World" is a chic 4-storey mansion with a unique wine cellar, which contains the best wines from around the world, including those produced in the Crimea, as well as a fish restaurant and a cozy lounge bar. The hotel demonstrates a unique archive of photographs taken at the end of the 19th century, when the village was actively landscaped at the expense of Prince Lev Golitsyn.

Vintage Hotel "New World" is an ideal place for a honeymoon or a romantic getaway together with your loved one. In addition, the hotel can organize a variety of corporate events, which for a long time will be remembered by their participants.

Unlike most hotels in Crimea, in the "New World" guests can not have problems with the lack of cold or hot water, since the first desalination plant on the peninsula was recently commissioned there.

Wedding organization

Vintage Hotel "New World" (Sudak) is an excellent place for romantic weddings. The hotel has a team of professionals who has extensive experience in organizing such events. They are ready to fulfill your every whim. You can entrust them with the organization of an exit ceremony on the seashore or in the Chaliapin Grotto, as well as a classic wedding with a banquet at the highest level. Excursions

Vintage Hotel "New World", reviews of which are mostly positive, organizes excursions to local attractions for its guests. For example, they are waiting for a walk to the estate of Prince Golitsyn, which today houses the museum of the history of the famous plant of Crimean champagne wines, and a visit to the Grotto Chaliapin. In addition, they can go to the city of Sudak and explore the medieval Genoese fortress. No less interesting will be an excursion to the village of Uyutnoye, where the ancient Temple of the Twelve Apostles is located and the powerful Tower of Frederico Astagvere, which once served to observe the ships passing by.

Vintage Hotel "New World", Crimea: reviews

Almost everyone who rested at this hotel left there with the best possible experience. This is evidenced by numerous reviews, among which it is difficult to find a hint of at least some complaints about the quality of service, the work of cooks or the location of the hotel.

Especially much praise can be heard about the design of the rooms, the arrangement of the territory and the chic view that opens from the panoramic balconies.

The only drawback that some guests noted was the lack of their own beach, but it is compensated by the fact that guests are given special cards for free use of sun loungers.

Stocks

The hotel, including a restaurant and a champagne bar, is open throughout the year. On the occasion of various holidays, such as March 8, February 23, St. Valentina and others, banquets are organized there and / or discounts on room reservations are provided. In the vintage hotel "New World" you can also royally celebrate the New Year. In the program: a banquet in the hall of the Golitsyn estate, which will be accompanied by a show program with the participation of Santa Claus and the Snow Maiden, a laser show on Mount Kush-Kaya and excursions to the sights of the Sudak region.

How to get to the New World vintage hotel

The hotel (Novyi Svet) is located at 3 Naberezhnaya Street.

From the airport, bus or train station of the city of Simferopol, you can get there by cars of different categories provided by the hotel (if the transfer service was previously ordered).

Otherwise, from the capital of Crimea you should get to the city of Sudak by regular bus and then take a fixed-route taxi to the village of Novyi Svet.





Weather in the New World

From the end of May to the end of September, the village usually has wonderful sunny weather. The water temperature in Novosvet Bay during this period varies from 22 to 27 degrees above zero. It rains very rarely. As for air temperature during the day, in summer this indicator fluctuates between +29 ... +34 o C.

You can relax in the New World even in September, since in the first half of the month it is quite hot there (+23 ... +29 ).
