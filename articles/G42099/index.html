Anastasia Hotel, Cyprus: overview, rooms, beach and traveler reviews

Cyprus is not the most popular holiday destination among Russian tourists. Many prefer cheaper resorts in Greece, but in vain. Cyprus has its own zest in the form of clean beaches, rich excursion programs and quality service. Perhaps the only drawback to trips here may be their high cost. Nevertheless, if you still decide to go on vacation to the island, then pay attention to the hotel in Cyprus "Anastasia Beach 4 *".

Location of Anastasia

The complex is located in the southeast of the island of Cyprus, in the small tourist village of Protaras. This place is famous for its clean sandy beaches and small rocky bays. The area is remote from the club life of the resort of Cyprus, so it is suitable for lovers of a quiet holiday with your family. Also, prices are lower than in other resorts of the island. At the same time, the town is located near the famous center of nightlife in Cyprus - Ayia Napa.





The Anastasia Hotel (Cyprus) itself is built right on Marlita Beach. The center of Protaras is only 15 minutes away. The sandy beach is located 200 meters from the hotel, so the road to it will take 5-10 minutes on the strength. You can get here from the nearest Larnaca airport, which is 65 kilometers away. A trip by car on smooth roads will take no more than an hour. The pebble beach of Kalamies is 1 kilometer from the hotel. Attractions lovers will also have something to see. 5 km away is the Agia Napa Monastery, as well as the Cavo Greco National Park. Tourists with children should visit the Protaras Ocean Aquarium, located 3 kilometers from the hotel.

General Hotel Information

"Anastasia" was built in 1998, during the development of the resort of Protaras. It's hard to believe, but a few decades ago there was a virgin forest, where only date trees and shrubs grew. Hotel Anastasia (Cyprus) has one large four-story building, which has almost 200 rooms. It should be borne in mind that the hotel staff speaks only English and Greek, so there may be problems with the understanding of Russian-speaking tourists.





For guests, mainly multi-bed rooms are offered. The hotel is designed for families, so children with all ages are welcome here. And animals, on the contrary, are not welcome here. Their placement is strictly prohibited even at an additional cost. Check-in at 14:00 local time. At the same time, tourists are often settled in a neighboring hotel for 1-2 days. Eviction takes place no later than noon. Mostly British, Poles, Germans, Greeks have a rest here, but Russian tourists are not so common.

Rooms and their equipment

The main building of the building has 190 rooms. Among them are family rooms and bridal apartments. Each room is soundproofed from the others, so guests will not hear their very noisy neighbors. The rooms have a good, modern renovation, designed in a classic style. All have spacious, furnished balconies, and some have a private terrace. The windows mostly overlook the sea, but it is possible to view both the surroundings and the water park.





Also, the Anastasia Hotel (Cyprus) offers guests a host of amenities to ensure a comfortable stay in the rooms. We list the main ones:

  • Bathroom with shower. The hotel provides personal hygiene products, a hairdryer, and towels, bathrobes and slippers.
  • TV with satellite television. Depending on the room, modern flat models and old tube ones can be found. A radio is also provided.
  • Small kitchenette: fridge, electric kettle, coffee maker, dishes. Also in the room there is a minibar, the filling of which is paid.
  • A small electronic safe for storing documents and valuables.
  • Direct telephone for communication with the reception or making international calls.
  • Individual air conditioning.
  • Ironing facilities.

Food at Anastasia

Good food can surprise Cyprus. Hotel Anastasia Beach offers its guests an all-inclusive service. Tourists who bought it can eat without restriction on the hotel territory, as well as receive free drinks, including alcohol. True, it does not include breakfast, so you will have to pay for it separately. In the morning, a classic English breakfast is served: scrambled eggs, tea, pastries, omelets, different types of cereals. Day and evening, Greek and European dishes are prepared. There is always meat on the table, as well as a lot of fish. Fruits and vegetables are also served without fail. Among the drinks, tourists are offered sparkling and mineral water, juices, 4 types of beer, red and white wine and strong alcoholic drinks. Upon request, a diet or children's menu can be prepared.





The following restaurants and bars are open on site every day:

  • restaurant "Athena";
  • cafeteria "Artemis";
  • Hermes bar
  • pool bar.

Hotel infrastructure

On the territory of the complex, tourists can find everything that may be needed for a comfortable stay on the island of Cyprus (Protaras). Hotel "Anastasia 4 *" offers its guests a developed network of infrastructure facilities. Consider the main ones:

  • A business center that provides fax and photocopying services for tourists. For official events here is a private conference room. The center provides paid courier delivery.
  • Wireless paid internet. The cost of one day of use is 10 euros.
  • Free private parking. Luggage storage at the reception.
  • Currency exchanger that allows you to convert rubles to euros at the most favorable rate.
  • Dry cleaning and laundry services include laundry, ironing. There is also a trouser press.
  • Modern elevator, doorman services. The hotel has all the amenities for people with disabilities.

Organization of leisure guests

"Anastasia" is a hotel (Cyprus, Protaras), which can offer its guests a whole range of entertainment: both free and paid. We list the most interesting of them:

  • Several pools: both open and closed. Some of them can heat water on cold days.
  • Animation and evening entertainment: live music, contests, shows, folk dance lessons.
  • Organization of excursions to major cities of Cyprus (Nicosia, Ayia Napa), as well as to popular attractions of the island.
  • Paid water park for both adults and children. Hotel guests receive substantial discounts on admission.
  • Private beach with free towels and sun loungers. For money, tourists can rent equipment for water sports, as well as go diving.
  • Playgrounds (table and classic tennis, darts, billiards). Fitness room with its own modern fitness equipment.
  • The spa offers guests such paid services: massage, jacuzzi, steam room, sauna.
  • A beauty salon that will create a wonderful evening look for you. Qualified hairdressers also work here.
  • Gambling machines for adults and a video game salon for children.

Relax in a hotel with children

For young guests the hotel "Anastasia" (Cyprus) also prepared a lot of entertainment. Among them are a children's mini-pool, a games room, and an animation program. Parents can always leave their child in a kids club, where animators will occupy him with interesting games, educational programs and competitions. Children can spend time outdoors by playing on a playground equipped with modern swings. For older children there is a TV room and a video game room.





Comfortable conditions are provided for families with small children. When checking in with a child up to two years old, the hotel will supplement the room for free with a baby crib. And in the restaurant for the convenience of feeding the kids there are high chairs. Paid tourists can always hire a round-the-clock nanny who will sit with their child while they, for example, go on an excursion.

Positive feedback from tourists

Before choosing a voucher, read reviews on the Anastasia Hotel in Cyprus. Every year many Russian tourists stay here. Here are the advantages of the hotel they note:

  • delicious dinners that will especially appeal to lovers of meat and seafood;
  • serve good alcohol, including a variety of beer, make delicious cocktails;
  • a large pool, there is always free space on the sun terrace, and there are always sun loungers and sun loungers;
  • Friendly and sociable staff who understands the Russian language and is also attentive to children;
  • a good water park on site, many different slides for people of any height and build, and lifeguards really follow people.




Guest reviews about the hotel

But, considering the Anastasia Hotel (Protaras, Cyprus), reviews of which are mostly positive, it is worth considering the negative opinions of guests. Tourists note the following disadvantages:

  • On arrival, tourists can often be transferred for several days to another hotel, because Anastasia does not have enough places;
  • monotonous nutrition, consisting mainly of fast food;
  • very noisy in the evenings due to animation and a restaurant with live music;
  • poor private beach with constant wet sand, so you have to go to neighboring ones;
  • all animation is conducted in English only;
  • very slow and expensive internet;
  • all sockets in the hotel are in English, so you have to buy adapters in the store.

Should I choose Anastasia?

Choosing this hotel for relaxation, it is worth paying attention that it is more designed for English tourists. They are populated here primarily, and all events are held in English. But the staff understands Russian and can even help solve your problems. It is also worth considering that temporary check-in at another hotel is a normal practice on the island of Cyprus. The Anastasia 4 * Hotel, reviews of which note this pattern, is no exception.

Despite these shortcomings, Anastasia is perfect for a quiet family vacation, for older couples, as well as for small friendly companies.
