Cooking sweet gifts - do-it-yourself bouquets of sweets in a basket

Flowers and sweets are those gifts that will be glad for both a little girl and a young girl, and every female representative. These two presentations can be combined into one original composition in the form of a bouquet of sweets. Today, such gifts are relevant and in demand. And you do not need to order it from the masters. We will teach you how to make bouquets of sweets with your own hands - in a basket. The following article is devoted to this direction in needlework.



Composition of sweets. Where to start execution?

To make a bouquet of sweets in a basket, you will need such materials and tools:

  • caramel sweets;
  • wooden skewers (10 cm long);
  • corrugated paper green and any other bright color;
  • basket;
  • PVA glue, hot gun;
  • kapron threads ;
  • braid or tape;
  • narrow tape;
  • wrapping paper or mesh;
  • soft thin wire;
  • Styrofoam;
  • sisal;
  • scissors.

We learn to make bouquets of sweets with our own hands in a basket: the design of flowers and leaves

From corrugated paper of bright color (two-color can be used), cut out 10 rectangular parts 3x8 cm in size. Round the upper edges of these blanks. The middle parts are slightly stretched to form a recess. Fasten 3-4 caramels with each other using a thread for one of the ‚Äútails‚Äù of the wrapper. It turns out a bunch. Put the stick between the sweets and carefully wrap the thread or tape again. If you want a larger flower, tie 6-7 candies. We bind the petals under the sweets, laying one by one and attaching with tape. It turns out a flower, in the middle of which there are sweets, and around them are paper petals. Wrap the stick-stick and the place of attachment of all parts with green corrugated paper and fix its edge with tape or glue. Sweet flower is ready. Similarly, perform the required number of such elements.



The implementation of products such as bouquets of sweets with their own hands in a basket, suggests the presence of sweet "buds" and "leaves". To execute them, wrap caramel in a square piece of corrugated paper and tie it in one tail. It turns out an unbroken bud. Attach it to a wire, which is then wrapped in green paper.

The leaflet is made as follows: wrap a long narrow strip of paper on one edge to a "twig" of wire. Wrap the other edge by extending a blade of scissors over it. The sweet details of the composition are ready.



Basket design

How to fix such products as bouquets of sweets with their own hands in a wicker basis? In a basket, lay out wrapping paper or a floral grid, beautifully straightening the ends to the outside. Center a foam piece of suitable size, attach it with a glue gun. Insert flowers into the base. Close all voids and the surface of the foam with sisal fiber. From a ribbon or decorative braid, make a bow and attach it to the handle of the basket. You can decorate the basket with decorative butterflies, ladybugs, artificial flowers.

Presentations such as bouquets of sweets in a basket will bring aesthetic pleasure to the hero of the occasion, and will please him with their taste. Make and give presents with pleasure!
