"Bristol" is a hotel of St. Petersburg. Description of rooms, reviews

A lot has been written and said about St. Petersburg - a city with a rich history and one of the centers of world culture. Nevertheless, those who have not seen its many monuments of architecture, stunning palaces and cathedrals with their beauty, cannot appreciate its greatness.



Apparently, this is why armies of tourists come to the Northern capital from all over the world to not only see all these famous creations with their own eyes, but also to walk along its streets and touch the historical heritage.

Where to stay

The opportunity to have a great vacation or business trip is provided by numerous modern hotels in St. Petersburg. Today, the tourism industry in the city is highly developed. The excellent infrastructure that characterizes many hotels in St. Petersburg, the high level of services provided, service and quality service - all this has caused the number of visitors to the Northern capital to increase year after year. City hotels are presented in various categories. There are also inexpensive mini-hotels or hostels, and fashionable five-star apartments. This makes it possible for every guest who arrives in St. Petersburg to choose a suitable place to stay that will meet his needs and capabilities.

Hotel Features of St. Petersburg

Many hotels in St. Petersburg are located in historical buildings that are architectural monuments. In addition, most of them are located in central areas, which provides guests with convenient mobility and walking access to numerous attractions, including museums, parks, temples, cathedrals, unique architectural structures and sculptures. In general, the main feature of most hotels in St. Petersburg is their location.



Some of them, located in ancient buildings, sometimes cause a feeling of touching history. Many hotels have preserved elements of antiquity. For example, during the reconstruction of the Aston Hotel, architects managed to preserve a staircase dating from the nineteenth century. And there are many such hotels in St. Petersburg. The Bristol Hotel is no exception. In general, judging by the reviews of tourists, one can return to the Northern capital more than once, each time admiring it in a new way.

Infrastructure

The updated Bristol is a hotel (St. Petersburg) that offers its customers relatively low prices with a fairly high quality of service. Its modern, stylish rooms provide everything you need for a comfortable stay - leisure or work. Guests have the opportunity to use the guarded parking lot located at the hotel for personal vehicles free of charge.

Additional amenities include a concierge service and translation service. Reception works without interruption. Visa support is provided here, a transfer is arranged.

If necessary, everyone can order excursion tours with the services of an interpreter and a guide on the ground floor of the hotel, rent a car, order tickets to museums and events, book a table in restaurants. ATMs are installed in the lobby, there is also a left-luggage office, a laundry.

Businessmen

On the ground floor there is a conference room with amenities for business meetings, negotiations and even educational seminars. It is designed for a maximum of fifty people.



The cost of renting a conference room includes wireless Internet, arranging three types of furniture according to the selected scheme, as well as providing elements for navigation around the hotel.

The Bristol Hotel (St. Petersburg) also has a business center in its infrastructure where there is all the necessary office equipment - a scanner, a photocopier, and fax communication is available . A coffee break or business lunch can be arranged for participants upon request.

Housing stock

Bristol, the prices at which, depending on the occupancy of the hotel, can vary, has one hundred and nineteen comfortable rooms of the standard (single and double) categories, superior - twin / double, as well as deluxe. All are equipped with LCD televisions, there are also paid safes, minibars, and telephones. Bristol is fully heated and air-conditioned. The rooms are equipped with manually adjustable split systems, and there is also free Wi-Fi. The standard category rooms have beds with bedside tables, a wardrobe, a desk with a chair, a luggage rack. The floor is carpeted. There is also an electric kettle and necessary utensils.



In the double rooms of the superior category, in addition to the above furniture, there is also a fold-out sofa. Comfortable deluxe rooms - a bedroom with a combined living room - have an improved interior and some additional amenities. They have a double wide bed, upholstered furniture, a full-length mirror, a coffee machine.

The combined bathrooms have bathrobes, slippers and a hair dryer. All toiletries and towels are provided.

Price

The room rates for the month of December at the hotel are as follows. The price of living in the standard category in a single room is two thousand, in a double room - 2650 rubles. Superior rooms will cost tourists 2950 for double occupancy, while deluxe rooms will cost four and a half thousand. Starting from December 30, the price policy of the Bristol Hotel changes in the direction of a rise in price. The price includes a continental buffet breakfast. There is no fee for pre-booking a room in Bristol, and the prices for accommodation, according to the rules of the hotel, are subject to change without prior notice to customers.
