Hotel Dome Resort 3 *: description, services, reviews

Dome Resort is located on Phuket Island, off the west coast of Thailand. It is aimed at adult tourists without children. The national composition of the guests is mixed. Guests from all over the world stay at the hotel. More than a million tourists relax here every year. In Thailand, the hotel is known under the brand name Villa Botany Kata Beach 3 *.





Location and hotel services

The hotel is located at: 83100, 98, M.4, Kata Beach, T. Karon Muang, Phuket, Thailand. The hotel is a 45-minute drive from the airport. The distance from the airport to the hotel complex is 40 km. A 15-minute drive away is a chain of supermarkets and souvenir shops. Jungceylon Shopping Center is a 20-minute drive away. Free private parking is available for motorists. Hotel Dome Resort 3 * offers motor scooter and motorbike rental services. Thanks to the rented transport you can quickly explore the surroundings and local attractions. A variety of excursion programs are also available for an extra charge.

Dome Resort has a Mezza bar and restaurant on site. Hotel guests can enjoy Thai and international dishes. Food and drinks can be ordered in the room. Free services are provided for cooks, laundry, maid, tour guides, administrators. The staff speaks English and Thai. The hotel has a 24-hour reception. Pets are not allowed. Check-in is from 14:00, check-out is until 12:00.





Room features

Hotel type Dome Resort - cottage complex. The hotel offers its customers 45 comfortable rooms. You can choose superior double rooms with two single or one double bed. Couples with children book grand deluxe rooms with one double and two single beds. Extra beds are available upon request. The number of rooms does not exceed 50 pieces. The rooms are located in a bungalow and a large three-story building. Each guest chooses the most optimal option for him. The building is located away from the main complex, in a quiet comfortable place. Bungalows are located in the center, around a small pond. They are surrounded by a superb tropical garden. Each room at Dome Resort Kata Beach has a private balcony or terrace. It offers rooms with a bath or shower. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, fan, refrigerator, TV-satellite, minibar, computer or laptop. Free Wi-Fi is provided in each room. Internet is also available in all public areas of the hotel. You can choose a room with a view of the lake or the green spaces. Hotel rooms include daily linen and towels.





Superb beach

Only 300 meters from the sea is Dome Resort. Kata Beach is a public private beach. The hotel is located on the second coastline. Many guests admired the clear water and white sand. Umbrellas and sunbeds can be rented for a fee. If guests rented sunbeds and went away, then the workers will carefully monitor that the paid places are not taken by other guests. The sandy beach is very clean. There are no broken glass, shells and sharp stones. The length of the beach is more than 4 km, the width of the coastline is 30 m. There are restaurants and cafes near the road. You can buy refreshments and local dishes from them. The beach does not have sharp ebbs and flows. The descent into the water is gentle. The bottom of the sea is sandy, shallow. There is an opportunity to swim near the shore. Large and dangerous waves are very rare. The beach is surrounded by cliffs buried in dense vegetation.





Guest reviews

Quiet garden with a small patio, friendly staff, excellent service, close proximity to the market, shops, beach - all this is offered by the Dome Resort hotel. Reviews of tourists are diverse. Both positive and negative opinions are present. Many guests complained about the lack of sound insulation. Poor plumbing and old bedding were highlighted. But most guests describe the hotel complex as a large, cozy hotel with excellent service and location. You can have a great time in the outdoor pool with a jacuzzi. Each bungalow has its own table with chairs. The Dome Resort comes with two bottles of water daily. Valuables can be deposited in individual safes located in the hotel lobby.
