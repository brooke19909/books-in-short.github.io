Where is the reservoir located?

In the Izobilnensky district of the Stavropol Territory. It was formed after the installation of the dam on the Bolshoi Yegorlyk river. That is, the reservoir is not only a favorite tourist destination, it is also used as energy for the Stavropol State District Power Station, Novotroitskaya Hydroelectric Power Station, as well as for water supply, irrigation and fish farming. That is, the object is extremely important. A distinctive feature - it does not freeze in winter. Therefore, many waterfowl winters here, and athletes are engaged in open water.
