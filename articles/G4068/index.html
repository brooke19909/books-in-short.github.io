Turkish Kemer: water park invites tourists

In recent years, Turkey has become a kind of Mecca for Russian tourists. And this is not surprising: a short flight, Russian-speaking staff in hotels, all-inclusive packages, affordable prices. Such a vacation can afford even not very well-off Russian family. Be sure to visit the famous water park in Kemer! Photos of him are presented below.

Turkish Kemer is one of the popular centers of resort tourism. Having tasted all the charms of a variety of services and entertainment at the hotel, I want to walk around the city and visit local attractions. For example, a water park. Kemer, and indeed the entire Turkish coast, is famous for its water attractions.



In the resort of Kemer, the water park is spread over a vast area of ​​six thousand square meters. m with staff of more than 750 people. "Water World" began its active work in the field of entertainment in the late 90s of the last century, and since then the number of people wishing to visit it has been increasing every season.



In the city of Kemer, the water park "Water World" is a favorite vacation spot for residents and guests of the resort, both adults and children of different ages. A variety of attractions will not leave anyone indifferent, there are a great many of them for every taste and age.

Perhaps it is worth starting the description with the most extreme entertainment in Kemer. The water park offers extreme attractions: the Black Hole beckons fearless researchers with its 92-meter unknown, and the Crazy River fully lives up to its name. The Typhoon and Freefall descents tickle the nerves no less.



Having experienced the thrill to the fullest, you will want to relax in a comfortable sun lounger on the edge of the pool under the shade of tropical palm trees ... Or maybe it is better to dip a tired body in a jacuzzi and enjoy the quiet sound of a waterfall? The choice is yours.

For young visitors to the water park, there are also many interesting activities. A specially created "Kids Club", spread over an area of ​​500 square meters. m, hospitably opened its doors to curious boys and girls. A children's pool with slides, toys and entertainment will certainly attract their attention.



Hungry, hurry up to eat in the restaurants and cafes of the Water World. Fast food bars are open for those who want a quick bite. You can have lunch both indoors and in special outdoor areas. You can drink soft drinks and try desserts right by the pool, without being distracted for a long time from your favorite entertainment.

Fans of traditional Turkish cuisine will be able to appreciate the impeccable work of cooks, choosing their favorite dish from a huge assortment of menus. By the way, special dishes are updated hourly. The Dolphin bar differs from other cafes in its special vitamin menu, which can restore your strength in a matter of minutes.

All guests of the water park receive free insurance at the entrance, guaranteeing a safe and active holiday. In the resort of Kemer, the water park "Water World" is open daily from 6 am to 10 pm, but most attractions and pools begin full-fledged work only from 10 am. You will have to pay $ 9 for a ticket, after 14.00 - $ 12.
