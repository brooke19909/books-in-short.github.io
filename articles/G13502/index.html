Hotels with a sandy beach in Greece - the best choice for families with children

A vacation with a small child is always an exciting and reverent journey, especially if it is planned abroad. For example, today hotels in Greece with a sandy beach are very popular, where a huge number of tourists come every year. What can this resort country offer?

When choosing a place to stay with children, all parents proceed from several principles: favorable climatic conditions, white sand on the coast, good food, including the availability of a children's menu, animation. Almost all hotels in Greece with a sandy beach meet these requirements. The islands of Crete, Corfu and Rhodes are considered the best for family vacations, but Peloponnese is also ready to offer a huge selection of hotels. In all these institutions, special attention is given to young guests, in many of them souvenirs are given to children. As a rule, on the territory there are children's pools equipped with slides, swings and playgrounds. Greece: the best hotel for children





Where to stay in this beautiful country with a mild and favorable climate and developed infrastructure? Many tourists have praised the Aldemar Cretan Village Hotel in Crete. Here for you and your children a team of animators will work, entertaining events are constantly held. In addition, children can use the games room, playground, two pools, which are located on the territory of the hotel in the open air. Upon request, parents can receive a cot for babies, a baby monitor, high chairs, and also provide babysitting services. True, you will have to pay extra for this.

If you are interested in hotels of Greece with a sandy beach, but with a maximum of amenities for children, pay attention to the hotel Capsis Ruby Red Regal. There is a mini-club for children from 5 to 12 years old, they can spend time on the playground or in the pool with water slides. At the request of parents, strollers, chairs, cribs are provided, and a children's menu is prepared. If you need a nanny for a certain time, you can also pay extra for her services. A distinctive feature of the hotel is the presence of an amusement park, which is made in the spirit of the ancient architecture of the palaces of Crete.





Excellent hotels in Greece with a sandy beach are located on the island of Rhodes. For example, Aldemar Paradise Village has two children's pools, a restaurant specially equipped for them. Animation programs are held every day, as well as the opportunity to engage in water sports on the beach. Hotel Olympos Beach is located in a prestigious beach area, has a green area. Children can use the pool with water slides, a playground, a mini club. In addition, as with many Greek hotels, babysitting services are offered.
