Ionian Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece): address, photos and reviews of tourists

If you are looking for a place for a relaxing and secluded holiday, you can safely buy a tour to the Peloponnese. Its advantages are beautiful nature, clear sea and well-groomed beaches. The abundance of attractions attracts tourists who prefer cultural and sightseeing leisure. Arriving here, you can save a lot by staying at the Ionian Beach Bungalows Resort Hotel. What advantages does it have over other hotels?

Location and important information

The hotel is away from the bustle of the city, but still has a favorable location. The hotel has the following address: EO Kato Achaias Araxou, Kato Achaia 252 00, Greece. Araxos Airport is located only 8 km from the complex, so the road to it will take 10-20 minutes by bus.

The nearest village is 4 km from Ionian Beach Hotel (Peloponnese). This is the tourist city of Kato Achaea, where tourists can visit many cafes, nightclubs, bars, restaurants and Greek taverns. There are shops, souvenir shops and tour desks. Travelers prefer to buy cosmetics with the addition of olive oil, jewelry and decor items here.

The large city of Patra is 24 km away. You can get to it by bus or taxi.





Ionian Beach Hotel 3 * is located on a vast landscaped area, which hosts 82 separate bungalows. The complex is located right on the beach, so the distance to the sandy beach here is only 100 meters. It was built in 1974, although the last renovation here was completed in 2016. This hotel welcomes tourists with pets. Kids are free of charge in a crib and playpen. High chairs are always at the restaurant. Most often, Greek families with children stop here looking for a budget place to stay.

Infrastructure and entertainment

The infrastructure of the complex Ionian Beach Bungalows Resort Hotel 3 * is well developed. Here you will find all the necessary services and entertainment that a fastidious tourist may require. We list the main services and services that are provided here:

  • a luggage room in which tourists can leave large bags and suitcases;
  • Wireless internet is available free of charge in all common areas;
  • a doctor who works on call and accepts only for an additional fee;
  • an outdoor pool filled with fresh water that works in fine weather;
  • a sunbathing area near the pool, which provides free sunbeds, umbrellas and mattresses for guests;
  • currency exchange point;
  • car parking;
  • mini golf course;

Positive reviews

What reviews does the Ionian Beach Hotel receive? Most often, guests are satisfied with the rest in this place. A small cost of living is fully paid off by the provided service. In their reviews, they describe the following advantages of the hotel:

  • high-quality and regular cleaning of rooms;
  • varied and tasty food, which is suitable for young children and people suffering from diseases of the digestive system;
  • modern equipment in the rooms, for example, a new air conditioning with a remote control or a flat-screen TV;
  • there are always free sunbeds on the beach and pool;
  • on the territory of the complex there are many fruit trees that actively bear fruit in the summer.




Negative reviews

Sometimes Ionian Beach Hotel receives negative reviews. Some tourists in their comments describe only minor flaws, while others criticize the complex. We list the main disadvantages of the hotel, which are most often noted by guests:

  • There are no infrastructure facilities near the hotel;
  • except for one administrator, the hotel has no Russian-speaking staff;
  • old repairs and shabby furniture in the rooms;
  • children's animation is carried out only in Greek;
  • On weekends, local tourists come to the hotel, who make a lot of noise in the evenings.

This budget hotel will be a good option for travelers who love a lazy vacation. It will also suit you if you have your own car with which you can easily move around the country. The complex is aimed at adult travelers, the elderly or families with children.
