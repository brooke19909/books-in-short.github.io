We sew ourselves: a dress pattern for a girl

With the advent of a hot summer, many mothers begin to think about how to sew a new thing on their beloved child. There are a huge number of models of summer dresses for girls - beautiful and light.

If you wish, you can find good patterns for children and interesting ideas for clothing. One of these ideas is a summer dress-sundress.

Such a product can be sewn by any girl who does not even have a talent for needlework. You can complete it in just a few hours. A free dress pattern for a girl is a rather rare thing, but some professional tailors still share their invaluable experience and unique ideas.

The sundress with which we will try to sew is the same in front and behind. The pattern is designed for a two-year-old child. So, take a square piece of fabric, you can have a big scarf, if you are faced with sewing for the first time and are afraid to spoil the material. For this product, wool or cotton material, that is, natural, is best suited. Make sure that your fabric is at least 33 cm long and at least 35 cm wide. First you need to give the cut the desired shape. To do this, measure 13 cm exactly in the middle along the top edge of the fabric and make

marks with soap or a pencil. Draw lines from these marks to the bottom edges of the fabric. Thus, you will receive the form of a sundress. Then you need to make an armhole with a length of 9.5 cm and a width of 5 cm. Dotted line indicate the middle of the dress vertically - and the dress pattern for the girl is ready!

Now we begin the sewing process. Measure about 3 cm at the top of the dress and make loops for the ribbon, then connect the front and back of the sundress. Pass the ribbon and tie a bow. Nice summer dress is ready.

In this case, you must certainly correct the pattern so that the dress fits perfectly.
