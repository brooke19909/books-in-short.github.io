Comet Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Marmaris): hotel description, reviews

Marmaris in Turkey enjoys the fame of a fun and youthful resort. A street of bars, a beautiful promenade, discos and nightclubs - there is something to do besides sunbathing on the shores of the Aegean Sea. Many tourists choose first-line hotels in Marmaris to be closer to the beaches. But this arrangement is immediately reflected in the price of a hotel room. Therefore, budget tourists prefer not to spend money on such trifles as walking distance to the sea, and are looking for housing in blocks away from the waterfront. As a rule, hotels there lure customers not only with a low price, but also with a higher level of service. And if you do not mind walking to the sea (or use the free shuttle service to the beach, which is also included in the list of services of such hotels), we suggest that you consider the Comet Deluxe Hotel 4 * accommodation option in Marmaris. Its description was made possible thanks to the numerous reviews that we carefully analyzed.



Hotel Location

This hotel is located in the new quarter of Armutalan. The city center with Bar Street, Atlantic Sea Park, the market and other entertainment can be reached by shuttle bus (four kilometers). And in the immediate vicinity of the Comet Deluxe Hotel 4 * there are such interesting places of Marmaris as the Karasan Point Center shopping center, the amphitheater and the Aqua Dream water park. Dalaman International Airport is eighty kilometers from the hotel. A tourist will have to get to the hotel from it in less than an hour. Reviews say that if it were not for the distance from the sea, it would be a fabulous place. But if you have healthy legs and you are not averse to walking (especially since a walk in Marmaris is always fun), you are right here. A shuttle bus to the center costs two liras (less than one US dollar). But reviews urge you to avoid the city beach - it's crowded and a bit dirty.



Territory, building

Hotel Comet Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) is small. It has an almost family atmosphere. Tourists say that on their second day, they get the impression that they came to stay with their friendly relatives. The territory of the hotel is small, compact. There are two outdoor pools with slides, one of which operates year-round. They have a sun terrace full of sun loungers and parasols. There is also a playground and parking - free for hotel guests. The administration is trying to create maximum comfort for its guests. Everywhere flower beds and neat paths. The hotel itself consists of one three-story building with an attic. It is noteworthy that all rooms are equipped with balconies, even those that are under the roof. What tourists say about food

The food at the Comet Deluxe Hotel 4 * is very tasty and varied. Dishes are repeated only at breakfast, which is understandable: every person has already established habits regarding morning meals. But for lunch and dinner, guests are offered a variety of delights of Turkish cuisine. Adherents of traditional European food can also find dishes for themselves. Reviews report that every day they give either meat or grilled fish. There are always a lot of fresh vegetables and fruits - melons, watermelons, apples, pears, plums, peaches. Turkish sweets are beyond praise. But each dinner, along with baklava and other pastries, served a large cake decorated as for a wedding. Children liked ice cream and fresh juices. Tourists speak well about drinks at the bar. The waiters are very helpful, there were no queues at the restaurant. There was always enough food, regardless of when you come to the meal - to its beginning or to the end.



Beach, Pools

To compensate its guests the distance from the sea, the hotel Comet Deluxe 4 * launched a free shuttle to the coast. The beach rented by the hotel is called Nirvana Beach - after the cafe. It is located in the Icmeler region. The beach is sandy, much cleaner and more pleasant than the coast in the city center. And the Icmeler district itself is very beautiful. The only negative is the transfer. The bus leaves the hotel at a quarter past ten in the morning and returns at five. Thus, you need to choose: either the sea or lunch. But before the transfer there was no transfer at all, and hotel guests had to spend money on minibuses or walk through the city on foot. Another compensation for the distance from the beach is the pools. There are two of them - an adult and a child. Both have water slides. Despite its modest grounds, the hotel has a sun terrace. After analyzing the reviews, we can conclude that most tourists traveled to the beach on a free bus, returned for lunch by minibus, and then relaxed in the pool.

Services

Hotel Comet Deluxe Hotel 4 * fully lives up to its four stars. For guests here, all conditions are created for a vacation full of pleasant emotions. Ultra All Inclusive includes a hookah, bicycle rental (some tourists used to get to the beaches), airport shuttle, Turkish hammam, spa facilities. There is excellent Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, you can chat with friends on Skype. The reception is open 24 hours a day and its staff are fluent in English. A baby cot and a professional nanny are available upon request. At the reception you can book tours of Marmaris and the surrounding area. Parking is free for guests of the hotel.
