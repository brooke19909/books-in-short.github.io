Should I go to the Maldives in September

The Republic of Maldives, in other words simply the Maldives, is located on the atolls of the Indian Ocean and is one of the South Asian states with the capital in the city of Male.





Oasis in the indian ocean

The Maldives is famous for its magnificent beach expanses, clear water, corals and a rich underwater world. According to many tourists, the visibility of the water in depth is at least 40 meters. For this reason, the islands attract the attention of scuba divers from around the world.





For vacationers, the most attractive time to relax in the Maldives is the period from November to December. Even in September, in this beautiful corner of the planet there are bright green vegetation and comfortable conditions for relaxation.

September holiday in the Maldives

The Maldives in September is very moody with its weather. This is due to the fact that in the first half of the month begins the period of heavy rainfall and raging storms traditional for this time. Heavy rainfall does not prevent a decrease in temperature. Therefore, warm weather in September standardly pleases tourists. The air temperature does not fall below 28 degrees heat, and the water temperature is at least 26 degrees heat.





At this time, the southwest wind of Hulhangu brings heavy rainfall, sea waves and high humidity in the air. Despite the high humidity, the continuous sea breeze helps vacationers get an excellent golden tan.





Holidays in the Maldives in September are chosen by noisy youth companies who are only happy with such weather conditions. They get a million impressions and unforgettable experiences. Despite the fact that in the September period there is always a lot of rain, there are rare days without them. It is on such unique days that even with very high humidity, tourists enjoy the hot sun, although they do not have time to dry all their things.





It is interesting that in most cases it rains at night, and hot and dry weather continues to delight during the day. Even when short-term rain begins during the day, all vacationers simply enjoy such a pleasant change in weather. While in September in the Maldives, one should not forget that here is a very treacherous sun, which even in heavy rain and bad weather can cause significant harm to the skin of a vacationer.





Annoying insects Maldivian holiday

Vacationers always want comfort and pleasant enjoyment of their holidays, but often flying surprises await him. In September, the Maldives is rich in annoying insects, which are becoming more and more precisely due to increased humidity. Of the insects in September, the most annoying are not mosquitoes, but numerous ants that crawl into the bungalows and even completely closed houses. They are fought with in the Maldives with special sprays and mixtures. In addition, in September in the Maldives you can get tired of the close attention of local bumblebees, which are two times more than the Russian representatives of this family.





Despite this, there are only positive reviews about trips to the Maldives in September, because, according to tourists, nothing, even such harmful insects, can spoil a wonderful vacation in such a beautiful corner of the world.





Active holidays in the Maldives in September

The Maldives in September is very popular for all fans of windsurfing and surfing. For these types of extreme vacations, the September weather in Male is very comfortable, especially since this city often hosts world surfing tournaments. Their time coincides with the onset of the fall season, so the cost of living in hotels during this period of time increases sharply. On all beaches of the Maldives , special schools have been opened and are actively functioning to teach these types of outdoor activities. Everyone is taught the initial skills of taming the waves, which in the absence of a storm are completely safe.





Unlike surf lovers who want to go diving in September in the Maldives are few. This is because in the rainy season all the common places for immersion in water are uninteresting due to poor visibility. However, some diving fans come to the Maldives in September, and not in any other month. This is because it is in this month and only in the channel near Ari Atoll that you can admire the game of giant whales in the water.





Popularity of holidays in the Maldives

The Maldives is located in the tropical zone, therefore, despite the traditional autumn rains, quite comfortable and warm climatic conditions prevail here. You can swim in the sea even at night, as the water at its temperature of at least 26 degrees is very pleasant. The ongoing tours to the Maldives in September convince that the rainy season on the islands does not scare a lot of tourists who want to relax away from the city's routine.





Maldives attracts many tourists with white beaches, unique weather, crystal clear sea water. If you carefully examine the reviews of tourists available about the Maldives in September, we can conclude that the islands have established themselves as a priority place for a relaxing holiday, even in the autumn season.





7 reasons to visit the Maldives in September

  1. Remember the spectacular view of the islands during the rainy season.
  2. Get a great golden brown tan.
  3. Enjoy wallowing on the beach with the purest white sand.
  4. Take a swim at night in crystal clear warm water.
  5. Sign up for a surfing school and experience the joy of conquering the waves.
  6. Go diving and see a game of whales underwater.
  7. To be comforted that the quality of rest justifies the cost of the trip.
