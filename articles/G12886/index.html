Prime Family Club 4 * (Turkey / Antalya) - photos, prices and reviews

Side, located seventy kilometers from Antalya, is not only an ancient maritime city, but also a combination of the grandeur of nature with architectural monuments and the modern rhythm of spa life. Recently, it began to attract more and more tourists who are satisfied with everything here: the crystal clear sea, and even sandy beaches, and developed infrastructure.



Resort Description

Side is famous for its landscapes. It is surrounded by mountains, the plains of which harmoniously merge with golden beaches. The best hotels of the city are located directly on the beach. In Side, you can relax for a tourist with any income. There are five-star luxury hotels and apart-hotels with rooms with a kitchen.

The city consists of several areas. Colakli, for example, is built up by modern upscale hotels, there are many restaurants, discos, bars. This area is more suitable for youth outdoor activities.

Kumkey, unlike Cholakli, couples with children choose to relax. There are family hotels where you can stay with kids. It is here that Prime Family Club 4 is located. The reviews about staying at this hotel, left by Russian tourists, are mostly only positive. Our compatriots like it a lot.



Location

Prime Family Club 4 was built relatively recently - in 2008. The territory on which it stands, although quite large, but thought out to the smallest detail. All the necessary infrastructure components for a comfortable stay are quite compact. Even the rooms farthest from the restaurant can be reached in two to three minutes.

The hotel consists of several three-story cottages. The nearest airport in Antalya is seventy kilometers away.

The territory is fully equipped. Around the pools is a large garden. Signs are everywhere.

There is a large supermarket near the Prime Family Club 4 hotel (Turkey). Within walking distance there are many inexpensive cafes serving excellent black coffee and delicious baklava, restaurants. Three hundred meters from the gate there is a real eastern market where you can cheaply buy fish and fresh fruit.



Infrastructure

Being a four-star, Prime Family Club 4 (Side) has a well-developed infrastructure, which some local hotels with a higher category often cannot boast of.

In its main building for vacationers, there is a hairdresser, an office for emergency medical care, laundry, porter services, and dry cleaning. There are several shops on the territory, including an inexpensive souvenir kiosk. There are also coffee machines, juice machines, as well as ATMs.

The hotel has its own free outdoor parking, which is not required to be booked in advance. In order to use the private closed parking, you need to pay extra.

Hotel Policies

The registration desk of Prime Family Club 4 (Turkey) is open around the clock. Settlement is carried out very quickly, you have to wait a maximum of fifteen minutes. There, at the receptionist, each settling tourist is provided with a listing of the services that are offered at the hotel. A photocopy machine and fax machine are also installed here, there is also a depository safe with a paid left-luggage office.

In all hotel areas, including the pool, free internet connection is possible.



There, at the registration desk, you can order a taxi, rent a car or sign up for excursions. If you wish, you can arrange a transfer to the airport.

Upon departure, vacationers, as required by hotel rules, must vacate the room they occupy before twelve o'clock in the afternoon. Handing over the keys, you must fully pay for both accommodation and the services that tourists used.

For convenience, you can close your account not only in cash, but also with plastic cards of many payment systems. Those who prefer cashless payments, in order to avoid misunderstandings, should discuss in advance with the administration the possibility of this type of payment.

Number of rooms

Hotel Prime Family Club 4 by the number of guests is classified as average. In its three-story buildings, two hundred and twenty rooms. They all open onto an indoor pool, beyond which you can see the azure sea, or the garden and amphitheater.

Best of all, they help to learn about the pros and cons of the number of rooms in Prime Family Club 4 (Turkey) reviews of those people who were already in it and were not too lazy to share their impressions upon arrival home.



The categories of rooms that are offered for check-in at this hotel are as follows: Standard, Family Prime and Suite. Standard rooms have an area of ​​thirty square meters. The maximum number of residents is two adults and one child.

The two-room family suite accommodates three adults and one child. Extra beds and bedding are available upon request. If desired, maids bring an iron and ironing board to the room.



The rooms, renovated in soothing colors, are equipped with comfortable single or wide double new beds, a safe, a minibar in which two bottles of water are free, a desk for work, nightlights, bedside tables.

On the wall hangs a flat-screen TV showing three Russian-language channels. For using the safe and minibar you need to pay extra. There is also a large makeup mirror. Parquet flooring. The entire hotel, including rooms, is air-conditioned.

In front of the rooms on the second and third floors there are small furnished balconies with chairs and a coffee table. The windows on the ground floor open directly to the terrace in front of the building, where there are sun loungers. If you wish, you can sunbathe right on the lawn in front of the room.

Rooms are cleaned daily, maids clean it unobtrusively, bed linen changes after three days.

Prices

The cost of living begins with two and a half thousand rubles a day. Depending on the season, prices vary. For the category "Prime" tourists will have to pay from four and a half, for a suite - from five and a half thousand rubles per day.

The bathrooms in the hotel are combined: they have a shower, a modern washbasin and a toilet. There are also hair dryers and slippers. All the most necessary for personal hygiene bath accessories are updated every morning.

Nutrition

Everyone who comes to Side, Prime Family Club 4, uses the all-inclusive concept. This system includes a variety of food buffets, which are set on schedule in the main restaurant of the hotel complex. The menu is constantly full of vegetable dishes, there are always several types of meat and fish, which is especially tasty in this resort town.

For lunch and dinner, fruits are always offered: apples and oranges, watermelon and melon, grapes, bananas, etc. A lot of delicious oriental sweets. In the evenings, guests can visit the hotel's other à la carte restaurant. Those who wish are offered room service.



Bars

On the territory of Prime Family Club 4, the food reviews in which Russian tourists have left only positive, in addition to the main restaurants and a la carte restaurants, there are also three bars: in the lobby with a wide selection of drinks from ten in the morning until midnight, by the pool and on the beach.

Throughout the day, guests are offered a drink, and from five in the evening - coffee and tea with Turkish pastries.

Beach

The distance from Prime Family Club 4 * to the seashore is about seven hundred meters. You can walk to the beach in just five minutes. The territory of its own swimming area is landscaped and green. There is a bar on the beach where you can have a great meal. Shelving, umbrellas and mattresses are free of charge.

There is also a volleyball court, there is a toilet and shower. Sunset at sea is convenient and easy, there are no algae in the water. There is animation on the beach, and there are also many opportunities for water activities.

Pool

Prime Family Club 4, a photo of the pool from which testifies to the impressive size of the reservoir, offers its vacationers a lot of entertainment. Those who do not want to go to the sea every day can spend time comfortably and sunbathe directly on its territory. A pool with two slides and a children's section is the pride of this hotel complex. It is always clean water. But the most important thing is the huge number of sun loungers arranged around, as well as a bar where you can taste any drinks, including locally produced alcohol, for free. From four o’clock, the little guests are offered delicious ice cream.

Pool water changes every other day. For those wishing every morning there is a group of water aerobics.

For children

Since the four-star hotel complex Prime Family Club 4 * is positioned as intended mainly for family vacations, much is provided for a comfortable stay with children of almost all age categories. If the client wants to place a child under two years of age in an extra crib or in existing beds - up to twelve years old, then you do not need to pay extra for this.

In addition, the hotel offers a kids club and a playground for its little vacationers. At the restaurant, upon request, you can receive dishes from a special menu for babies, as well as a high chair. For the convenience of parents there is such a paid service as a "nanny".

The shallow pool provided for children is very convenient for swimming kids. A low slide is arranged here.

Entertainment

In addition to swimming in the pool and swimming in the sea, as well as relaxing and sunbathing on the terraces in front of the buildings, the hotel has the opportunity to visit the sauna or hammam - Turkish bath. Guests can enjoy a massage or make beauty treatments. In addition, there is a beauty salon on the territory where you can do manicures and pedicures, as well as a new hairstyle.

In Prime Family Club 4 * all day until late in the evening funny animators work. They are working hard to make the leisure of the hotel guests interesting. In the evenings, the amphitheater hosts shows with guest artists and competitions. Lovers of outdoor activities can play beach volleyball, ping pong, darts, billiards, attend classes in water aerobics or a gym. And the beach has everything you need for water sports.
