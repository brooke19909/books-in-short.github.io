Many experts advise tourists to go on vacation to Dresden. The fact is that this German city is still being rebuilt and reconstructed, therefore it does not experience a large influx of foreign guests, like many other hotels in Germany. Here you can relax inexpensively and efficiently in the calm atmosphere of traditional German calm and poise. You can leisurely take excursions to the many Dresden churches and museums, explore the palaces or relax in the comfort provided by small and cozy German hotels. The Hilton Hotel (Dresden) is a modern and stylish complex located in the heart of the Old Town, close to all significant sights of Dresden. The hotel offers its guests to relax in the fitness centers and gyms, relax with a glass of delicious local beer in the bar or brasserie, taste several delicious German dishes in upscale restaurants, as well as visit the solarium, sauna and steam bath.
