Modeling and sewing clothes for Baby Bon

One of the most popular baby dolls today is Baby Bon. Girls around the world dream of such a toy. The baby doll, which can be wrapped in soft diapers and blankets, rolled in a stroller, sing songs, tell tales and, of course, dress up, has rightfully become the favorite of little nannies. Where can I get quality clothes for my daughter‚Äôs darling? There are few options: outfits can be purchased at the toy store or sew clothes for Baby Bon yourself.



Material selection

First of all, it should be noted that the coloring of the fabric for the baby doll should be with children's prints. That she will look harmoniously in such products. As for the texture, it will be easier to work with knitted fabrics (fleece, footer, velor, interlock or cooler). And as a diaper and blankets, you can use flannel, velsoft or terry.

Sewing options

So, how to sew Baby Bon clothes? The easiest way is to alter existing children's things. They do it this way: they put a thing on a doll and sew along the contour of the body with a marking seam, thereby separating all that is superfluous. Then, having removed the clothes from the doll, a line is drawn on the sewing machine on the basting and they cut off all the excess. In this way, you can alter the sliders, overalls, little men, vests, blouses and hats. If you design clothing for Baby Bon yourself, then first you need to make some measurements and develop patterns.



How to make clothes patterns

Patterns of clothes for Baby Bon are done by the standards of a baby doll. To make them, you will need to measure the circumference of the chest, the circumference of the tummy, the growth of the pupa, the length of the arms and legs and the width of the shoulders. For a hat you need to know the volume of the head. Basic clothing patterns can also be made by tracing the contours of the doll on paper. After that, a couple of centimeters are added to the contours for a free fit and allowances for seams. Designing clothes for Baby Bon begins with a sketch of the future product, where all shaped lines, folds and seams are defined. After that, they are transferred to the main template and cut. Then you need to redraw the details on the fabric, cut them and, adding allowances for the seams, begin assembly.

How to stitch details

To make things look neat, cuts are best handled on a sewing machine. However, if one is not available, this task can easily be handled manually. The seams are first sketched, and then stitched with a sewing buttonhole stitch. As fasteners for clothes for Baby Bon, you can use Velcro, sewn buttons, zippers and buttons. You can also decorate to your liking.

Ideas for outfits

Baby clothing sets may well come from baby kits.

Beautiful velor dresses with a strip of lace on the bottom of the skirt will look good with a thin cap and tight-fitting sliders. Beautiful overalls-men will come out of the colored fleece, to the kit of which you can make an interesting hat with a knot or ears. Terry blouses with a hood will also look good, on which a muzzle of a teddy bear or a cat will harmoniously fit. In addition to clothing, you can also sew a bib, socks or booties, which will be a great addition to the image of the baby.
