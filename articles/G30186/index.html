Vitaly Kolesnik - a man in an iron mask

Kolesnik Vitaliy was born in Ust-Kamenogorsk (Kazakhstan) on August 20, 1979. Pupil of the hockey school "Torpedo". For a long time he played in attack. But, as the hockey player himself says, he always wanted to stand on goal. As a child, he often went to hockey with his father, and he liked to watch goalkeepers. One day, the coach invited him to try himself in this role. When Nikolai Myshagin headed the Torpedo, Kolesnik played the main goalkeeper for three seasons.

Over the ocean

For two months he played at the Lowell Monsters Farm Club. In early December 2005, they invited to Colorado, where Vitaly Kolesnik made his debut in the NHL against Boston. The goalkeeper has three victories in the NHL. But as Vitaly himself says, the most memorable is the game with the New Jersey Devils. Then he repelled seven shot-goal shots. In a match against the Phoenix, says the hockey player, he showed a not very good game. Replaced already in the fourth minute. And soon the hockey player went to the AHL.

Over the year, an athlete in America has learned a lot: tactical training, attitude to work, physical and psychological stability. So says Vitaly Kolesnik. The hockey player believes that everything is completely different there. Not like in Europe. The speed, the number of throws, the intensity of the game are much higher. You need to quickly pack up and react, as there is much less time to make a decision.

At first, it was difficult to understand the nuances of the new rules. Then I got used to it. He does not regret anything - this is a great school for the goalkeeper. In the spring of 2006, they offered to play again at the farm club, this did not suit, as he dreamed of playing at the highest level.
