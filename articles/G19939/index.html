How much to fly from Moscow to Yerevan by direct flight

You can fly from Moscow to the capital of Armenia by direct flight from any airport in the Moscow region, except for Zhukovsky. Five airlines work in this direction, at least ten more carriers that are part of alliances with them sell tickets for joint flights. From our article you will learn how much to fly from Moscow to Yerevan by plane.

Flight from Sheremetyevo

Flights to Yerevan from Sheremetyevo are carried out by Aeroflot and North Wind. The Russian national carrier sends aircrafts to the capital of Armenia up to six times a day, and there are two flights to the North Wind - in the morning and afternoon. The duration of the flight from 2 hours 45 minutes to 3 hours. The time it takes to fly from Moscow to Yerevan in time for a particular flight depends on the day of the week; on days of low airport load, travel time according to plan is less than during peak periods.

The distance in a straight line (by air) from one airport to another is slightly more than 1800 km. Due to the fact that flights over the territory of Moscow are prohibited, the airway actually passes around the city, extending the flight by an average of ten minutes. Flights are operated by Airbus and Boeing.

Domodedovo-Zvartnots

Siberia Airlines (S7), Red Wings, and Ural Airlines fly to Yerevan from an airborne south of the others. From four to five flights a day lasting 2 hours 45 minutes - 2 hours 55 minutes on Airbus aircraft of models 319, 320 or 321.

The distance from Domodedovo to Yerevan in a straight line is almost a hundred kilometers less than from Sheremetyevo, and flights on this route are shorter by 5-10 minutes. You can find out exactly how to fly from Moscow to Yerevan from the flight schedule for the desired date.

Flight to Yerevan from Vnukovo

During official events with the participation of the country's top officials and distinguished guests, the flight time from Moscow to Yerevan from Vnukovo Airport may be different. Government jets use the same lane as civilian ships and may make unexpected changes to the regular flight schedule. Such delays are an exception to the rule, but the travel time to Yerevan can be several tens of minutes longer due to the fact that the airport accepts flights of statesmen.

The only carrier for passengers to Armenia from Vnukovo is the Uteyr company based in it.
