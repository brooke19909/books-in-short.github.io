Hotel Vasilia 4 * (Corfu, Greece): description and reviews

Vasilia 4 * (Corfu) is not just a hotel, but a whole resort village right on the sea. Although it was built a long time ago, back in the nineties of the last century, this complex does not lose popularity among tourists. After all, the last repair was there only in 2015. The clear sea, picturesque beaches, beautiful virgin nature, excellent service - all this attracts travelers exploring the Greek islands, families with children, as well as those seeking relaxation and solitude. The hotel is part of the huge Gelina Village Resort complex.



Aharavi

This resort area is located in the north of the island. Here is the longest beach, the warmest water and most of all vegetation. Golden sand with small pebbles by the sea and oranges, pomegranates and almonds in the distance - this is the portrait of this area where the Vasilia 4 * hotel is located (Corfu island, Acharavi). Although the coast has been known since ancient times, and even mentioned in Greek myths, this village practically does not appear in history. But people come here not for the sake of sights, but thanks to the amazing scenery and, of course, the sea. Nature is the main thing to see here.

Where is the hotel located and how to get there

Hotel Vasilia 4 * (Corfu) is located in the resort of Acharavi, only three hundred meters from the center. The island's capital and airport are thirty-seven kilometers from the hotel. Near the hotel there is a mini market where you can buy fruits, sweets, ice cream. A bus runs to neighboring villages, the schedule of which hangs in the lobby at the reception. Tickets can be bought directly from the conductor. Buses, including to the capital Corfu, stop one hundred and fifty meters near the hotel. Near the hotel is the famous water park "Hydropolis". Guests have the right to go here free of charge five times a week.



Infrastructure and service

Hotel Vasilia 4 * (Corfu) is built in the style that many call traditional Greek. Some say that it is architecture in the manner of a Roman villa. The hotel consists of two-three-story villas and the main building, surrounded by a landscaped multi-tiered garden with paths and ladders. The name of each house is associated with Greek myths. In the main building there is a restaurant and a lobby with administration. Of all the neighboring hotels, only Vasiliya has such a huge territory, a lot of greenery and so rich vegetation around. In this recreation complex there is a paid parking, shops with beach goods and souvenirs, a currency exchange, an ATM. A room where tourists leave their luggage at a late check-out or early arrival also belongs to the same types of paid services. There are many services and entertainment for children - a games room, a soft-covered playground, a babysitter, a mini club, and a small zoo. The hotel has good animators who arrange performances in the amphitheater, and in the evenings there is a disco. Very good conditions for those who are fond of sports or just love outdoor activities: tennis courts, volleyball, basketball, beach soccer, as well as many activities on the water - aerobics, diving, windsurfing, skiing, and much more. And for wellness you can visit the spa and wellness center.



Rooms

Many guest rooms at Vasilia 4 * Hotel (Corfu) are located in the main building and are standard rooms. The villas are suites and apartments. There are about four hundred and fifty rooms in the hotel, and Vasily owns more than sixty. They are spacious and at the same time comfortable. The rooms are furnished, air-conditioned. The room has everything you need - satellite TV, a hairdryer, a fridge, a safe, a bathroom with a bath or shower. Wi-Fi in the room - for a fee. The studio (thirty-five meters) is equipped with a kitchenette. They are designed for two guests. Standard apartments are forty-six meters in size. Four people can live there, there is a bedroom and a living room. The suites are the most luxurious apartments. In addition to the kitchen area and living room, they have two more bedrooms with bathrooms. Everywhere there are balconies with sea views. Very beautiful, especially in the evenings. Sometimes even mainland Greece is visible.

The Penelope's restaurant is very beautiful and stylish, it is especially pleasant to spend romantic evenings with a view of the sea. The menu is varied, the food is prepared at home. For those who love the sea

If you came to the hotel Vasilia 4 * (Corfu) to sunbathe and swim, then this place is just for you. The beach at the hotel is public, sandy with interspersed small pebbles, is located several tens of meters from any hotel building. You can go to the beaches near other neighboring hotels. Moreover, the coast in the Aharavi region stretches for eight kilometers. The entrance to the water is gentle, very convenient for children. The water is clean and the warmest on the island. Sunbathing is comfortable, not hot - such a pleasant climate here. The beach is equipped, including a shower. In addition, the hotel garden has two swimming pools with solariums, sun loungers and parasols. At the main is a bar. Near each artificial reservoir there are children's zones. On the beach, sunbeds and other accessories are free. Towels are secured (ten euros).



Island trips

Tourists who stayed at the hotel Vasilia 4 *, about. Corfu (Corfu), Acharavi (Acharavi), usually primarily visit the capital, ancient Kerkyra, the surrounding monasteries, and also go on boat trips. The island is not very big, so even if you have a few days of vacation, you can always explore all the surroundings. Transport here is excellent, but, of course, renting a car gives you more options. You are free on your schedule and are free to stop wherever you wish. Of the interesting excursions that can be made from Acharavi, tourists note a trip to Mount Pantokrator - the highest on the island, from where you can even see Italy and Albania - with a church on top. Of course, the most picturesque place in the vicinity is Paleokastritsa. True, swimming there is cooler, but the water is just an amazing color. And young people who want to hang out and try the local nightlife will not be disappointed. You do not even need to leave Akharavi for this. In the resort town everyone will find entertainment to their liking. At the same time, the presence of discos and clubs does not interfere with the rest of the rest.
