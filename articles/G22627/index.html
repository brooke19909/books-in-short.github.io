Cheap iPhone is not cheap enough

Gadgets manufactured by Apple have always been and remain at the peak of popularity. Apple products are a mark of quality for all time. But, unfortunately, it is not available to everyone because of its high cost. As everyone already knows, Apple decided to rectify the situation and release a cheap iPhone. More precisely, the budget version of its famous smartphone. This article will discuss the characteristics, name and appearance of the new gadget.



Title

Apple's cheap iPhone is called the iPhone 5C. There were many versions. For example, Color, Casual, PlastiC and others. Ultimately, the company still settled on 5C. "C" means "colorful." This translates to "color." Thanks to what the phone appears before us not as cheap, but as a color and bright device.

Housing

Unlike previous models, the cheap iPhone has a plastic case. Thanks to this, it is much cheaper than all previous ones. After all, the cost for plastic cases is much less than for expensive aluminum plates. When creating 5C, Apple offers its customers plastic cases of various colors. In addition to standard white and black, green, pink, yellow and blue are released. Front 5C - an exact copy of Iphone 5. It feels like holding an Iphone 5 in your hand, only in a bright color case.

Screen

The diagonal of the screen of the new iPhone is 4 inches. Screen resolution - 1136x640 megapixels. The picture quality is excellent, like all smartphones from Apple. It is impossible to distinguish pixels with the naked eye. An undoubted plus is a very realistic color rendering. To find fault with the screen is simply unrealistic.



"Iron"

The heart of this smartphone is the Apple A6 processor. This is a fairly powerful processor, which is already used in the iPhone 5 model . Camera

As always, Apple has a good camera. Without all kinds of extra ultrapixels and showing off. A cheap iPhone has exactly the same photo module as the original Iphone 5 - 8 megapixels, with flash, auto focus, image stabilization, FullHD-video recording and f / 2.4 aperture. The pictures are bright and saturated. The front camera of the phone is the same as its predecessor - 1.2 megapixels.



Price

So we got to the most important thing. As practice shows, a budget iPhone cannot be purchased cheaply. Despite the promises of the company, their cheap Iphone is not cheap enough. The price for it is not so small, especially in Russia and the CIS countries. Buy Iphone 5C can be for 400-500 dollars! Despite the fact that he has a plastic case and he is going to China.
