Club Hotel Sunbel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): photos and reviews of tourists

It's no secret that every year many compatriots try to brighten up everyday life with a fabulous vacation by the sea. And quite often the place for such a fun vacation becomes Turkey, which, by the way, is one of the most popular countries among Russian tourists. Of course, there are a lot of resort areas on the territory of which all the conditions for a comfortable, interesting and fun holiday are created.

One of the most popular resort cities in Turkey is Kemer. It is here that the small but very cozy hotel complex called Club Hotel Sunbel is located. It is natural that before the trip, many tourists try to find the maximum amount of information about this place. Where is the hotel located? Can I come here with a child? What service and living conditions does the hotel provide? What kind of reviews do holidaymakers who have already managed to see all the charms of this place put aside? Answers to these questions will be useful to many readers.

Where is the hotel located?





The beautiful Club Hotel Sunbel is located on the picturesque seashore, in the resort village called Beldibi. This is a quiet place that is perfect for a relaxing, leisurely vacation with all amenities. Naturally, if you wish, you can get to other settlements. For example, just a kilometer from the hotel building there is another small village Goynuk. The distance to the center of Kemer with all its attractions is about 15 kilometers - you can get here by taxi or rented transport. By the way, there are several shops, entertainment and shopping centers in Beldibi, so you are unlikely to feel cut off from the environment.

The nearest international airport is located on the territory of the large and famous resort town of Antalya - the distance to it is 45 kilometers. Nevertheless, it is not worth worrying about this, as the hotel, as well as travel agencies, provides tourists with a transfer.

Brief description of infrastructure and territory

As already mentioned, the Club Hotel Sunbel 4 * hotel is located almost on the seashore. It can be considered relatively small, since the total area is 5500 square meters. Nevertheless, the entire territory is well equipped and well maintained. In the center is a multi-storey building, where, in fact, the rooms are located. In the courtyard there is a swimming pool, sports and children's playgrounds. The exterior, as well as the interior design of the building, is a successful combination of modern architecture and oriental culture. The territory is decorated with palm trees and exotic flowers, there are places for relaxation and walking. The small area in the hotel is compensated by the atmosphere of real home comfort and genuine hospitality.

It is worth saying that the hotel was built in 1996. And in 2003, a complete reconstruction of both buildings and the entire territory was carried out. Today the hotel meets all international standards. By the way, almost every year the hotel arranges certain cosmetic repairs in order to create the most comfortable conditions for guests.

What type of food does the hotel offer?





Like almost all hotels in Turkey, the Club Hotel Sunbel 4 * complex operates on an all-inclusive basis. This means that guests are provided with full three meals a day. The hotel's main restaurant serves generous buffets. Good cuisine is just what Club Hotel Sunbel boasts. Reviews of tourists indicate that the restaurant always has enough space, even with the maximum occupancy of the hotel. The menu here is quite diverse. Breakfast includes scrambled eggs, pancakes and other delicious dishes. For lunch and dinner, you will have much more choice: meat and fish dishes, vegetable salads, cold and hot snacks, an abundance of goodies, fresh fruit and, of course, delicious homemade ice cream.

By the way, grilled dishes are prepared for guests every day. And you can have dinner on the outdoor terrace by the pool, which is also very convenient. Naturally, the hotel has bars, in particular near the pool and in the lobby. Here you can get light snacks and desserts, coffee, tea, sweet and carbonated drinks, as well as alcohol produced in Turkey. Beach and water recreation for tourists





What conditions for a beach holiday can the Club Hotel Sunbel offer? Kemer is a place that is ideal for water recreation. The hotel owns its own small section of the beach, which is located in close proximity - only 50 m. The beach here is sand and pebble. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient, although it is still recommended to acquire special rubber shoes. There is a sandbank for bathing children. Also nearby is the pier of a neighboring hotel, which nonetheless you can freely use.

Comfortable sun loungers complete with sun umbrellas are arranged in even rows on the shore. Here you can swim, sunbathe, chat and just relax, enjoying the beauty of nature and the sound of the sea.

Naturally, fans of a more active holiday will not have to sadly spend time, since there are all the opportunities on the coast to do various water sports and just have fun. On the shore for a relatively small price you can rent a canoe or boat, ride a catamaran or take a short boat trip. There is also a scuba diving school where you can not only rent the necessary equipment, but also take a quick training course and complete your first dive in the company of professionals. Also, tourists will have the opportunity to do parasailing or go skiing. In a word, each vacationer will find a suitable occupation for himself in the territory of the Club Hotel Sunbel hotel complex. Turkey is really a wonderful country for a beach holiday.
