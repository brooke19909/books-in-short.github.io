Ekaterina Frolova - famous actress

Russian cinema is rich in talented male and female actors.

The new star of Russian cinema

In 2005, another movie star appeared on TV screens - Ekaterina Frolova. She graduated in 2004 from the St. Petersburg Academy of Theater Arts. Then she began working at the Riga Russian Theater.



In 2005, director Artyom Antonov noticed a young, promising actress. He invited her to star in the movie Half Moon as a nurse. This film describes the events of World War II. In the story, the captured Germans are forced to build a tower for the Russians. The main character, Grigory Anokhin, is directed to lead the German brigade. He will have to face not only the hostility of the locals towards the Germans, but also with himself.

Although the role of Frolova in this film is insignificant, but she played just fine.

In 2006, Ekaterina Frolova starred in a cameo role in the film "Opera-2. Chronicles of the slaughter department. " The plot of this series tells the story of a serial maniac who kills women of different ages by strangling them with a black stocking.

In 2009-2013, Ekaterina Frolova starred in the serial film "Fire." This is a Latvian film where the actress played the wife of Felix. The plot of the series revolves around life in a hotel.
