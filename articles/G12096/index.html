How to make paper pockets for little things

In our pockets we usually put a variety of small things: coins, keys, notes and other items. To use this simple invention of mankind is a common thing, because it is very convenient. But sometimes there are not enough pockets, and then the real hell of things scattered everywhere begins. For example, you need an additional insert for a notebook or for storing papers. There is a way out when you know how to make. Consider how to make paper pockets, the most affordable material.





Simple pocket envelope

Made very quickly from a square sheet of paper. It is based on the principle of adding angles to the center. Let‚Äôs take a closer look.

First, find the middle. Fold the paper in half or use a ruler. Expand the sheet. Fold the corners opposite each other to the intended line. In principle, you can wrap the edges with an overlap. Already done half of how to make a pocket out of paper.

Now bend another corner (bottom or top). It should lie so that they can close the central part. Stick it with other edges with glue or double-sided tape.

Bend the remaining corner down. Decorate the envelope as desired and fix in its rightful place. Now you can use the pocket!

The original way to fold the envelope





For such crafts, you will also need a square sheet. Fold the paper diagonally. Fold one top corner to the outside. It should touch the edge of the sheet and not go beyond its borders. When folding, rely on the instructions in the picture, it shows how to make paper pockets.

Now take the corner from the edge. Wrap it inward. It should slightly go beyond the upper corner, which was bent for the first time. Do the same with the other edge. Craft at this stage should resemble a house.

Let's make a pocket, thanks to which the envelope will be kept closed. We bend a small corner from the last folded part. Now we unfold it and form a rhombus from it.

We bend the other corner of the craft, which remains at the top, and put it into the resulting rhombic pocket. Decorate the envelope as desired.

How to make a pocket of paper roomy





The finished pocket has the following dimensions: width - 10 centimeters, height - 15 centimeters. Parameters, of course, can be changed as desired.

On colored paper, draw a square with sides of 16 centimeters.

From the bottom into the square we retreat one centimeter, put the points with a pencil in several places. Laying the line.

Now from the left edge we make three such indents. They should also be one centimeter wide. On the right side we do the same. Thanks to such allowances, the liner will turn out to be capacious. How to make paper pockets more spacious? Just draw more allowances.

Fold the side indents along the lines with an accordion on one side, then on the other side. Now bend the bottom allowance up. And return it to its original position. On the bottom strip you can see small squares. Put glue on them. The allowance is folded up again. Wait for the glue to set.

Now this pocket can be put, for example, in a notebook. Just apply glue on the sides of the workpiece and press it against the sheet. We admire the result!

If you are thinking how to make paper pockets on a stand, use this method. Just do not forget to make a semicircular cut at the top of the sheet so that it is more convenient to use the insert.

How to make a heart-shaped pocket

Such an envelope can be used not only as a pocket in a notebook. In the craft, you can put a small sweet present, and then give the heart to a dear person on Valentine's Day. In the process, rely on the picture, it will show how to make a beautiful pocket from paper.





Fold the paper diagonally and then unfold. Bend one corner to the center, and the opposite to the very edge.

Now lift the free edges up. Turn the blank over to the other side. Fold all sharp corners to form a heart. For reliability, the crafts can be glue a little. Decorate, for example, with rhinestones, flowers made of paper or fabric. The heart-shaped insert is ready!

Now you know how to make paper pockets in different ways. And remember that a neat bend is the key to an attractive craft.
