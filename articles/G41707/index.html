Option for roaming from Megafon - Travel without worries

Going on a trip around the country, subscribers have to think about how much the cellular services will cost outside the home area. For those who do not plan lengthy negotiations and are ready to do a few minutes on the day of communication with their relatives and friends, the connection of additional services will not be relevant. Otherwise, you should study the list of proposed options and choose the one that is more suitable. Among these services is the offer from Megafon Travel without worries. In the current article, this option for travel will be discussed in more detail.

Thus, for active communication, the mentioned option will be a rather successful solution. It should also be noted that, according to the terms of use of the option, the preferential cost of services is not available when in the Crimea and in the city of Sevastopol.

The cost of communication services without connecting services

The service "Megaphone" "Travel without worries" is not required to connect. You can use the communication in roaming without additional options. It should be borne in mind that all incoming calls will be charged at 9 rubles per minute, a similar cost will also be set for outgoing calls. For some tariff plans with the included number of minutes, it is possible to use prepaid packages when roaming in the country.





Managing the Megafon Travel without cares service

You can activate the option through a personal web account, however, as well as turning it off. In addition, through a short number and sending SMS, this procedure can also be performed.

  • Via USSD service: * 186 * 1 #
  • Via SMS service: an empty message to number 0500991 (if an empty message cannot be sent - the device does not allow this, then you can specify any character or letter in the text).

To disable Travel without worries (Megaphone), dial * 186 * 0 # or send a message to the number that was used to send SMS for activation with the word STOP. Upon arrival to your area, you should deactivate the service in order to avoid additional charges from the account.
