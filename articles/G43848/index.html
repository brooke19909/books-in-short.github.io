Felt Beer Coasters

First you need to decide on the design of the stand. The most popular theme for the kitchen, of course, is fruit. Stands are made in the form of strawberries, apples, etc. Such a soft and cozy material as felt can make your kitchen more comfortable and sweeter. In addition, to sew a similar product is a couple of trifles.

To make a stand from felt for a mug with your own hands in the form of an orange you will need:

  • felt white and orange;
  • needle;
  • sewing threads;
  • scissors;
  • pencil;
  • compass;
  • paper for a pattern.

It takes about half an hour to make. In order to make a stand for a mug with your own hands, you first need to draw two circles on the paper with a diameter of about 10 cm and 9 cm. From a orange felt, cut a circle of a larger diameter, from white - the one that is smaller.

You can take other colors, and then you get a fantasy fruit. Triangles should also be cut from orange felt. They should be sewn to the white circle with a seam ‚Äúover the edge‚Äù, imitating slices of an orange cut in half. Then you need to connect the two parts together. Do-it-yourself mug stand ready! Now the tea party will be much nicer.

Wooden bead stand

There are many other materials from which you can make no less beautiful coasters. For work, you will need wooden beads and a strong thread. Yes, from the most ordinary balls you can make a real work of art. After all, a stand made of this material will look very beautiful.

The number of beads depends on their diameter. The smaller they are in size, the more they will be needed.

So, first you need to string the beads on a thread and lock it in a ring. The resulting circumference in size should be slightly larger than the bottom of the cup. Then from the beads you need to make another ring - a smaller one so that it fits inside the first one. If necessary, you can make another third circle, which will be less than the second. But here it all depends on the size of the beads - after all, sometimes two rings are quite enough. Next, you need to thread the resulting circles with each other so that the inside does not fly out. So easily you can make a stand under the mug with your own hands. This is a very simple and quick version of its manufacture!

DIY computer cup mug stand

With the advent of flash cards, floppy disks and CDs are a thing of the past. Many of the houses left their whole mountains - both empty and filled with films or music. No need to discard discs. After all, from them you can make wonderful coasters for a hot mug. To do this, you will need:

  • 2 unnecessary disks;
  • glue;
  • putty;
  • small spatula;
  • napkins for decoupage;
  • degreasing solvent;
  • scissors;
  • sandpaper;
  • acrylic lacquer;
  • brush.

First you need to glue two disks together. Let them dry. Then putty to coat the hole in the middle. After it dries, treat the disk with sandpaper. Degrease the surface. Take a napkin for decoupage, cut and glue two circles to fit the disks. After the glue dries, you need to cover the product with varnish.

Do-it-yourself stand for a hot mug is ready! You can have a tea party!
