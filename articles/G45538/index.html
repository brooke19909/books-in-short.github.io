Bergamo, Italy: attractions with photos and reviews of tourists

Italy is a country of unsurpassed traditional cuisine, temperamental people and a warm sea. Every year this state is not deprived of the attention of tourists. There is no seasonal framework, a good pastime will be provided both in winter and in summer, since there are more than enough sights, in particular in Bergamo.

Road to the past

The founders of this ancient, ancient city are the Celts. The name did not happen by chance, the locals worshiped the god Bergamus, and from there the name went.



Since the VI century, the city has become a center for Herzegovina under Charles the Great. This was followed by the great battles with the ruins and Guelphs, their people were united, but removed from power by all means and tried to gain independence.

Closer to the 15th century, the urban industry began to transform, trade and the production of fabrics went up.

Nowadays, tourists have different associations with this place, for example, tea with bergamot, the character of the famous Italian comedy Harlequin and local dances.

Climatic conditions

The weather in Bergamo is quite stable. There is no particular temperature difference and significant precipitation. Type of climate - Mediterranean. The average temperature in the summer is + 25. The average temperature in the winter season is + 7.

Given all these features, we can say that the climate is comfortable enough for excursions at any time of the year, and no rain will prevent you from enjoying the beauties of this beautiful ancient city.

Discover the world of beauty

Do you know what to see in Bergamo? The sights of this area will satisfy all taste needs, regardless of whether you are a lover of paintings or good music.



When ordering an excursion in this beautiful city, it is worth paying attention to the fact that it is divided into two parts. Upper Bergamo is located on a hill and personifies the atmosphere of past centuries. The lower part gives a ticket to the world of more modern Italian architecture.

The place that is most often in demand among tourists is Piazza Vecchif Square. Architectural monuments remind of those times when the city was under the influence of the Venetian authorities.

Not far from the square is the Colleoni chapel, made in the Renaissance style.
When searching for something unusual or just walking around the city, you should look into the Carrara art gallery, which is located in the lower part of Bergamo, where paintings by famous Italian artists are stored .

Those who cannot imagine their life without music should visit the Opera Theater. It was built in the early 18th century by the then-famous composer Gataeno Donizetti. If you travel in the summer season, you can get to the music festival, which brings together famous singers from all over the world.

And this is only a small part of what you can see in Bergamo.



The sights of this city are not limited; for more detailed information, you should contact the guide directly. Also, before departure, it is worth checking with the tour operator how to navigate the area upon arrival.

Italian city lights

Romantic Italy is a country with unusually delicious cuisine. In order to relax after long excursions, you should run to some restaurant or cafe.

The most popular places among tourists who came to rest on their own or with their families:

1. Colleoni & dell'Angelo - a luxurious restaurant that has gathered everything you need to demonstrate real, traditional, Italian cuisine. Experienced organizers will help organize your meal or celebration at the highest level.

2. Da Mimmo - A traditional Italian cafe that specializes in high-quality pizza and pasta. This place has become the most visited in the city due to affordable prices.

3. Bierschenke Marienplatz - this establishment is different from all the others, as German cuisine is represented here . This cafe is suitable for those who are a true fan of meat dishes.

Of course, there are much more such places in the city, as well as attractions, but here it all depends on your individual wishes.

Bergamo is a city of delicious temptations.

Comfortable rest

Next, consider what a good vacation is impossible without - these are hotels in Bergamo. Italy is famous for its marvelous nature. Wonderful vegetation adorns the country, surrounded by mountain systems. This is where you can get real relaxation.



According to tourists' reviews, there were definitely some of the best hotels at affordable prices that could provide a truly comfortable stay after hours of excursions and walks.

1. La Valletta Relais - the rooms of this hotel are imbued with a cozy atmosphere. Outside the window there is an excellent panoramic view, which makes it possible to feel the Italian spirit. Price: from 75 euros.

2. Entro Le Mura - an inexpensive hotel that contains only 6 rooms, which is the main advantage, there is no unnecessary fuss. In summer, breakfast is served in a beautiful courtyard with a charming garden, and in winter - by a blazing fireplace. Price: from 48 euros.

3. Gombi Hotel - a 4-star hotel with a modern interior. It is located in the very center of Bergamo, which makes it possible to quickly get to it from the airport or train station. Price: from 113 euros.

Travel agencies can offer you a lot of variations, but before making the final choice, look at the reviews of those who have already visited Bergamo hotels. Italy, although a European country, but extra caution will not hurt.
