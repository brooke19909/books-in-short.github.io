Hand clutch: master class and patterns

The hand clutch is a popular fixture dating back to the 15th century. It is a part of women's clothing, it looks like a cylinder in which hands are hidden during the cold.

Coupling History

We associate this product with past centuries. How often do we look at paintings that depict ladies hiding their hands in couplings.

Apparently, the hand clutch was invented in Italy. It is not very cold here, but here they know a lot about luxury; it is not for nothing that the famous Venetian carnival appeared in this country .

The famous painter Titian painted his paintings, depicting languid ladies with sable fur couplings on them, and his brother Cesare Vecellio said the following about them: ‚ÄúVenetians are charming: in summer they wear beautiful gloves, and in winter - couplings‚Äù.



Sewing the sleeve into the hands was then a tricky business. It was necessary not only to pick up beautiful materials, such as, for example, swan fluff, but also to decorate it with gold buttons, as well as crystal jewelry.

Coupling as a decoration of the nobility

An accessory such as a clutch on his hand soon became a piece of jewelry for the English queen. Her clutch was made of silver brocade, it was embroidered with gold and pearls, on it were patterns depicting birds, trees and forests, on it were beautiful buttons.

This product could replace a handbag. There were many pockets in it, inside of which one could put various ladies' little things, such as powder, perfume, a mirror, lipstick and so on.



And there was also a tradition of aromatizing the coupling. Noble ladies added flavor to the clutch, which made them unique. Couplings everywhere were worn by men who dressed in rather feminine clothes in the 17th century. This product becomes an integral part of the court toilet, which cannot do without a clutch.

In the 18th century, the clutch on the arm becomes impractical. At the court, it is small, made of expensive materials and decorated with expensive jewelry, but it does not warm your hands. Then the couplings were made of satin, and decorated with fur.

In the 19th century, couplings were also popular, and there were a variety of models. There was still a fashion to wear small dogs in couplings. Men at that time wore warm clutches in which it was possible to hide a bandoleer for hunting.



In the 20th century, people did not have time for couplings; they spun in a whirlwind of events. But at the same time, women again sought to be elegant, and these wardrobe items began to appear. Leading world fashion designers began to introduce them into their collections.

Hand clutch now

She was forgotten for a long time, but recently she has become popular again. The hand clutch is especially convenient for those who walk with a child in cold weather. As a rule, inside such a product there is warm fur and a layer of artificial fluff. This clutch can be worn on the handle of the stroller, and it will help you warm your hands in cold weather. But it can also be used to put on a car seat, to lay on a sled or on a stroller.

What are hand couplings

These products in their form are fused and separate. Hartan (a clutch for hands) is most often one-piece, and it is suitable for a stroller with a single handle. There is a manual clutch separate, it looks like mittens. These are two products in one. Suitable for strollers with separate handles.



Outside, the coupling is usually covered with a moisture-resistant material, it can be raincoat fabric, bologna fabric. Inside, she may have faux or natural fur. The coupling can be used not only on a wheelchair. For example, when it's cold, you can put it on a swing seat on the street or on a bench.

Clutches made from natural fur are popular. Such a hand clutch just looks luxurious.

Hand clutch pattern

It is not difficult to make such a product on hand by yourself, and if you are interested in a clutch for hands, its pattern is simple. Usually it is sewn from raincoat fabric and faux fur. But you can take a synthetic winterizer, real fur, velor, fleece. Fleece is a warm material that will warm your hands in any weather.



The pattern is done this way: first, from paper or from film, we make a part measuring 45 by 45 centimeters. We also cut out a pocket measuring 28 by 11 centimeters, a pocket valve measuring 11 by 10 centimeters, a small pocket strap.

How to sew a hand clutch, preparation

If you are interested in how to sew a sleeve on a hand, then this is not difficult. For work, you need faux fur, you can mink fur, you need a fixing tape, synthetic winterizer, good tailor's scissors. The scissors you use must have sharp blades so you can cut a flat ribbon with them. Also, if you want to sew a sleeve for your hands, you need to have threads and a needle. You can use a sewing machine, but for sewing the coupling you will need to purchase a special needle for the sewing machine. This needle will sew fur, and you can purchase it in special hardware stores.

Sewing workshop

If you like the clutch for hands, then the pattern for it is done in a matter of minutes. You just need to cut a rectangle of the right size out of paper. Then this rectangle is applied to the faux fur, and a similar one is cut out of this material. Then you need to cut the same rectangle from the synthetic winterizer.



Now the faux fur is fastened with a synthetic winterizer on a sewing machine. One side remains uncrosslinked. We take a long ribbon and sew it to the edges of the coupling. It will be fastened at your waist, and you can always carry the sleeve with you. It can be decorated with rhinestones, feathers, artificial flowers. You can attach pockets to it in which you can store such necessary trifles as lipstick, perfume, cosmetic bag, wallet and even a mobile phone.

How to knit a hand clutch with knitting needles

If you have some knitting skills, then you may have a knitted clutch for hands. In winter, it will serve as an excellent adaptation for riding a stroller, especially if the stroller has an iron handle.

The coupling is so well knitted. For work, you will need threads, about three hundred grams, and knitting needles that are thicker than thread. It is not necessary to take new threads, you can dissolve old things. The children's hand clutch is well knitted with knitting needles made of yarn, which is half wool and half acrylic.



The pattern for such a product can be taken uncomplicated, it can be a handkerchief, two-on-two elastic, a checkerboard. The size of such a clutch is about twenty to twenty centimeters. No longer required, given that the knitted fabric stretches.

Buttons are sewn along the edges of the coupling so that it can then be attached to the handle of the stroller. Such a knitted product can also be used as a blanket for a child, and as a mat on a swing. Knitted clutch for hands will be your salvation not only in winter but also in summer in cold weather. You can knit it from multi-colored threads to make it beautiful.
