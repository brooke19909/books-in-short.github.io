Hotel Novia Gelidonya Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): photos and reviews of tourists

If you love the sea and the sun, and also want to enjoy quality service, go to the Novia Gelidonya Hotel resort. Here you will find everything that a modern tourist may need, and Kemer, in turn, will delight you with beautiful nature, original culture and lots of attractions.





General Hotel Information

Novia Gelidonya Hotel Resort is 7063 sq. M. m. Moreover, almost the entire territory, free from buildings, planted with palm trees, flowers and other plants. This hotel is a complex of three four-story buildings.

Despite the fact that the Novia Gelidonya Hotel was founded and began its work in 1994, it is quite modern and meets all standards and requirements. Considering that major repairs were made in 2012, there is a beautiful and elegant interior, new comfortable furniture, as well as the latest technical equipment.





Resort Location

The Novia Gelidonya Hotel 4 boasts a convenient location. Turkey, Kemer, Ataturk Boulevard, 07980 - the address of this resort. It is about 150 meters from a luxurious beach. Thanks to the proximity to the famous Taurus Mountains, you can always enjoy their views. To be in the city center, you have to overcome a little less than a kilometer. The famous Goynuk Canyon is 11 km from the hotel, and the ancient city of Phaselis, popular with tourists, is about 15 km. Antalya International Airport is 50 km away.

Number of rooms

The Novia Gelidonya Hotel has 170 comfortable rooms. They are conditionally divided into the following categories:

  • Standard rooms are simple and concise. It can accommodate two or three guests in twin beds, as well as additional guests on folding beds.
  • Family apartments are designed for families with children or large companies. In fact, these are two standard rooms connected by an interior door. In addition, each of them has an individual bathroom. One of the rooms has a balcony.
  • If you like luxury, look for a luxury apartment. Here, guests have access to a brightly decorated bedroom, as well as a living room with a huge glass door leading to the terrace. The marble bathroom has a jacuzzi.
  • A special category includes eco-numbers. Their decoration is almost completely made of natural materials. The apartments are located on the ground floor, and therefore they have access to a terrace with a luxurious garden.

Accommodations

Given that the Novia Gelidonya Hotel 4 * was relatively recently renovated and reconstructed, guests can use the most modern equipment in its rooms. The following amenities are available to guests:

  • modern air conditioning will allow guests to make the air temperature in the room cool enough to hide from the Turkish heat;
  • on the balcony or terrace there is garden furniture, as well as clothesline;
  • the bathroom, in addition to all the necessary plumbing, is also equipped with a hairdryer and bath accessories;
  • the satellite channel package also contains several Russian-language ones;
  • landline telephone allows guests to solve organizational issues with the reception without leaving their room;
  • if guests do not want to pay an additional fee for filling the minibar, then they can use it as a refrigerator;
  • the room has a safe, which guests can use for a fee.

In the Novia Gelidonya Hotel 4 *, special attention is paid to room care. Maids perform thorough cleaning every day, and bed linen and towels are replaced every two days.

All Inclusive

Novia Gelidonya Hotel offers its guests an all-inclusive break. If we talk about nutrition, then it implies the following points:

  • from 7:00 to 10:00, guests are invited to have breakfast in the main restaurant (in buffet style);
  • from noon to 2 p.m. lunch is held;
  • also from 12:00 to 16:00, guests can eat a huge assortment of snacks in the lobby bar;
  • from 16:00 to 17:00, guests can enjoy coffee and cakes in the restaurant;
  • from 19:00 to 21:00, guests have the opportunity to enjoy themed dinners that are not repeated for the entire period of rest;
  • from 22:00 to 23:00, guests can satisfy their hunger with traditional Turkish soups;
  • Guests can enjoy local and foreign drinks in the hotel bars.




Additional All-Inclusive Services

You can get a lot of pleasant experiences if you go on vacation at the Novia Gelidonya Hotel. Turkey boasts a huge number of resorts that operate on an all-inclusive system. In this hotel, in addition to food, this concept includes the following services:

  • a visit to a traditional Turkish hammam;
  • aromatic sauna, which will help relieve stress and improve well-being;
  • training in a modern gym;
  • use of wireless Internet in all public areas of the hotel (over 600 MB extra charge);
  • use of sun loungers, mattresses and parasols on the beach and near the pool;
  • playing table tennis, darts, checkers, chess and backgammon;
  • participation in the entertainment program from animators;
  • visiting the open-air cinema.




Hotel Bars

In order for the rest to be even brighter and more fun, tourists just need quality drinks and cocktails. So, in the Novia Gelidonya Hotel Kemer you can visit the following bars:

  • One of the most favorite guest houses is the bar, located next to the pool. Here they have the opportunity to try a lot of popular and brand new cocktails. Hamburgers and french fries are also served here to satisfy guests.
  • A cozy and elegant place can be considered a lobby bar. The institution located in the lobby has a special atmosphere that gives peace and tranquility. The highlight of the bar can be considered a huge variety of coffee varieties and a large assortment of confectionery.
  • If you love spending days at sea, then you will certainly be pleased with the presence of a cozy bar on the beach. It mainly serves beer, sweet sodas and ice cream cocktails. If you do not want to go to a restaurant for lunch, have a snack with traditional Turkish cakes, barbecue or pizza.
  • The disco bar is an ideal place for nightlife lovers. Here every evening guest DJs perform.




Hotel infrastructure

Going on vacation to Novia Gelidonya Hotel 4 * (Kemer), you will get everything that a person needs for a good rest. On the territory you will find the following infrastructure objects:

  • large swimming pool with clean fresh water in the open air (there is equipment for heating);
  • almost the entire territory is occupied by an exotic garden;
  • bike rental service;
  • a playground for children located in a palm garden;
  • parking zone, which is guarded around the clock;
  • a business center with a large number of items of office equipment;
  • car rental service, as well as mopeds;
  • the gift shop presents a huge selection of products from local craftsmen;
  • a beauty salon where you can make a manicure, hairstyle and get a consultation with a cosmetologist;
  • At the reception you can get an answer to your questions at any time.




Useful information for guests

A great place for a sea holiday, tourists consider Kemer. Novia Gelidonya Hotel is characterized by some features that travelers will find useful:

  • check-in and check-out of the guests, respectively, after 2:00 p.m. and before noon (if you have free apartments, you can be given the service of early registration or late eviction);
  • Children under 6 years old stay free of charge on existing beds, and children under 2 years old are provided with special cots;
  • The daily cost of an extra bed is 70% of the price of living in a single guest room;
  • The hotel does not offer the possibility of pets;
  • You can use a plastic card to pay for the services received;
  • All payments must be made using the same credit card that was used for booking;

Positive reviews

A lot of positive impressions and bright moments gives travelers a resort hotel Novia Gelidonya Hotel 4 *. Reviews about this place include positive comments as follows:

  • the hotel is located very conveniently relative to the sea, as well as near the city center;
  • the hotel has its own well-kept beach, which is kept in perfect cleanliness;
  • high-quality daily cleaning (even if you do not leave a tip, the room will always be clean);
  • bartenders very quickly, politely and efficiently serve guests;
  • beautiful animation with a lot of sporting events and incendiary concerts (neither adults nor children have to be bored);
  • the pool practically does not smell of bleach;
  • in the dining room they serve a lot of fresh fruits (peaches, oranges, bananas, figs, apples and much more);
  • the buffet menu always has meat and fish;
  • despite the fact that the replacement of bed linen is announced every 2 days, in fact, it is carried out daily;
  • the rooms are very beautiful and bright, and given the recent renovation, all the equipment is new and fully operational;
  • the air conditioner copes with the functions assigned to it.




Negative reviews

Unfortunately, the rest is often overshadowed by flaws and some negative aspects that Novia Gelidonya Hotel is not without. Traveler reviews indicate the following negative aspects of this hotel:

  • the dishes in the dining room are washed very poorly, but because it is all in stains and greasy to the touch;
  • despite the fact that there are many refrigerators with free drinking water in the territory, they are almost always closed;
  • wireless internet is very slow;
  • local alcohol of very poor quality;
  • despite the fact that the hotel operates on an All Inclusive system, you will have to pay $ 1 per day for using beach towels;
  • dishes in the restaurant are repeated day by day;
  • despite the fact that the bathrooms have showers with doors, all the same the whole floor will be flooded with water, as the joints leak;
  • the room does not have a large chandelier that would provide normal lighting (you have to be content with a light near the mirror and two sconces above the bed);
  • Clubs of tobacco and hookah smoke soar throughout the territory;
  • if you come to a free sauna or hammam, employees will persistently impose paid services on you.

General impression

If we talk about the general impression that develops after visiting this hotel, then it is quite positive. At first glance, there is everything you need for a good rest. These are comfortable rooms with freshly renovated and new furniture, and a beautiful area with a huge amount of greenery, and a large pool, and a private beach within walking distance. Here you can enjoy both sea and city vacations, because in order to get to the center of Kemer, you need to walk just a few minutes. Well, of course, do not underestimate the concept of "All Inclusive", in accordance with which you get good nutrition and a number of additional services.

The bulk of the negative aspects are associated with the work of the hotel staff. Of course, there are no complaints about the maids, but the administration is indifferent to the comments and requests of the guests. In addition, organizational and financial confusion constantly arises. So, for example, in your account you can see those services that you did not even think to use.
