Sights of Kazan

Kazan is a city rich in cultural events and historical places with a million people. It has 34 state museums, many private exhibitions and galleries. Along with historical places, the city is actively building modern centers. Festivals of international level are held here - Chaliapinsky, Nureyevsky, Rachmaninovsky. In Kazan, under the auspices of UNESCO, the world's first Institute for a Culture of Peace was created.



For visiting visitors to cultural events and their immediate participants, the Ibis Hotel (Kazan) becomes a temporary home, reviews about which are preserved in the book of honored guests of the hotel.
