So that negative moments do not spoil your vacation, in advance learn about the disadvantages inherent in the Dream Phuket Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel. Reviews focus on the following flaws:

  • Air conditioning in the guest rooms is right above the bed. Because of this, you have to turn off the device at night so as not to catch a cold.
  • The location is not very convenient for those who do not rent vehicles. And beaches, and infrastructure facilities, and attractions are removed from here at a decent distance.
  • From Friday to Sunday, you have to pay an additional fee for visiting the beach. Moreover, the cost is quite high, and there is no explanation about this.
