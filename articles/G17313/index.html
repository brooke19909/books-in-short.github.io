State standard specification bleached calico: fabric properties

Today, calico fabric is very popular and is in great demand. Sheets are used to make bedding sets, bathrobes and costumes for medical workers, sets for newborns (which include diapers, hats, vests, romper suits), and in ancient times they sewed underwear for soldiers from this fabric.

This matter began to be brought to Russia from Asia in the 16th century. The fabric is loved because it is easy to wash and affordable. Bleached calico has differences from other types of fabric in that the density of weaving of the fibers is from 105 to 140 grams per g / m2. The article describes in detail the fabric, lists the scope of its application and properties.

What kind of material is white and where is it used?

This superb canvas is unique in origin, produced only by the best manufacturers. This dense cotton fabric with plain weave has the name coarse calico bleached by GOST. Because of such snow-white nature, it is used for sewing gateways for military uniforms, for napkins on kitchen tables, tablecloths in dining rooms.

Tailors also make clothes in the atelier custom-made from this material. Sew skirts and petticoats, summer dresses, pajamas, nightgowns, men's shirts, handkerchiefs. From bleached calico we get wonderful lambrequins and curtains for windows, bedspreads (top made of calico, quilted with synthetic winterizer), decorative pillows, mattresses and much more, which everyone needs.

White color in things

White clothing carries health, good luck, cleansing and longevity. It also means a symbol of innocence, spirituality, purity and freedom. So people thought in antiquity, so we think today.

White products are a sign of social status (earlier this spoke of nobility, nobility of greatness and welfare). White-collar workers meant belonging to the intelligentsia, white shirts, suits and interiors - this is a sign of belonging to a wealthy class.

Fabric advantages

Coarse calico has the following advantages:

  • High practical quality: it is used for different purposes (both in the creation of various products, so in everyday life). Quickly does not crumple and does not get dirty. The fabric is also easy to wash and iron.
  • Hygienic.
  • Easy.
  • Eco-friendly.
  • Hygroscopic.
  • Breathable.
  • Wear resistant.

Purpose and use of fabric

The above are the benefits of a material that includes cotton. It is precisely on these indicators that bleached calico fabric is valued among clothing manufacturers. Stitched products from this material last a long time, and they are also very pleasant to the body. They are very comfortable and comfortable to wear. Such clothes and bedding from a simple fabric with additional decoration will cost a little cheaper than from more expensive fabrics.

Wearing such clothes is very good for health, because it consists of a cloth of cotton threads (natural medium fiber), which are made using the well-known technology of carded spinning. In a fabric such as bleached calico, the threads are thick and tightly bound, they are made from pure raw materials.

How to distinguish cotton from some materials?

You can distinguish it using two methods of verification:

  1. Combustion. All fabrics of natural origin, where there is cotton, burn well. This spreads the smell of burning paper. At the end of burning smolders.
  2. Tactile and visual impression. When you touch cotton matter, it gives a feeling of warmth and softness. The fabric has the ability to wrinkle easily; when processed, it can be drained without difficulty.

The simplest methods that make it possible to recognize material from other synthetic species have been given above. Choosing a bleached calico, you will be satisfied, because it meets the needs, aesthetic needs of the buyer, as it is a durable and comfortable material. The fabric in which cotton is present is very practical and beautiful.
