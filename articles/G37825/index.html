How to win blackjack - tips for beginners

In order to know how to win blackjack, a player needs to learn several aspects of this card game.

Game basics

All card games have strict rules, which are strictly forbidden to be violated. Therefore, those who set themselves the goal of an indispensable win, the rules must be studied thoroughly. There are not many of them in this game.

Blackjack is played with a 52-card deck. In gambling establishments, so that an attentive player can not count the points remaining in the deck, they mostly play six-deck blackjack. But there are places where this is considered insufficient. There you can meet the game with eight decks.

The dealer deals two cards face down to each player. The dealer hands himself one card (first) open. The maximum winning combination is 21 points. Maps have the following numerical values:

  • Ace - 1 or 11, depending on which value is preferable.
  • The main cards (Jack, Queen, King) - 10 points.
  • Lower cards (from 2 to 10) - points correspond to the numbering of the card.

Card suit in this game has no semantic meaning. Only the face value of the card is important. Your cards do not compete with partners on the table, but directly only with dealer cards. The sum of the points of your cards is called "hand".
