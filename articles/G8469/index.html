CJSC "Nadezhda": employee reviews

Nadezhda Closed Joint-Stock Insurance Company (CJSC) collects numerous reviews, because under the license of Rosstrakhnadzor it is engaged in twenty-two types of insurance, in the same list the most popular type is CTP. The clientele for this reason is very extensive. Reviews about CJSC Nadezhda can be sorted into three approximately equal parts - two from customers, where there are both negative and positive, the third part - reviews of employees. This article will be devoted to their review.





Statutory fund, ratings

Employees of CJSC Nadezhda leave reviews on the Internet, judging by the texts, most often mandatory as part of their work. In particular, information about the company is given there, rather little is written about working conditions (except for those who quit), but a lot is written about activities and types of insurance services. There you can find out, for example, that the authorized capital of the company is 103,000,000 rubles, therefore, reviews of CJSC Nadezhda note that the company is held among the 500 largest insurers in Russia. Financially, this insurance company is quite stable, since the volume of insurance reserves is sufficient - 227 million rubles.

Judging by the reviews of the insurance company, Russian rating agencies assigned Nadezhda CJSC a first-level rating, rather reliable - "BBB +". This article will examine the geography of the presence of this company in Russia, and it is quite wide. Its development began from a small regional organization with a residence permit in Krasnoyarsk, and after a while it grew into a large national insurer, represented in nine regions of the country. Today there are eleven branches and almost eighty representative offices. Reviews about CJSC Nadezhda Krasnoyarsk are mostly favorable.

Krasnoyarsk

Already in the distant 1994, reviews of employees of Nadezhda CJSC noted the opening of a subsidiary of a medical focus, which began operating as an insurance medical institution under a state license for the provision of compulsory medical insurance (MHI) services. In those days, its activities were limited only to the Krasnoyarsk Territory and the Republic of Khakassia (which, in principle, is already very much - the territories are simply vast). For compulsory health insurance, reviews about the company NAO Nadezhda began to be received in large numbers almost immediately.

And today MCO "Nadezhda" is the leading medical insurance institution in these territories, more than one and a half million people are insured. Naturally, the huge amount of feedback received about the insurance company CJSC Nadezhda is due to the activities of the subsidiary. Only one product receives more responses. This is CTP. The reviews of Nadezhda CJSC from employees indicate that, as the only local insurer, the company has more than twenty percent of the clientele in this region.

Credentials

CJSC "Nadezhda" has the right to provide insurance services of many types provided for by Law No. 4015-1 of 1992 of the Russian Federation. This is insurance of people against illnesses and accidents, medical insurance. This insurance covers all land transport (railway - a separate point), as well as air and water. Insurance of various transported goods.

Widely used is the type of agricultural insurance - crop, garden, forest, animals. Property insurance of individuals and legal entities is very popular. And of course, insurance for owners of vehicles, rail, air and water transport. Also, organizations operating them can insure their hazardous industries. Business and financial risks and much more are insured.

Milestones

The year of birth of Nadezhda CJSC is 1991, it was then that in October the company was awarded a certificate stating that the state has registered this joint-stock company. Already in 1993, a medical "daughter" appeared, and therefore the company became an insurance group serving millions of customers. At the same time, the rating established sixteenth place in the country among fifty-eight companies involved in compulsory medical insurance.

The following year, the active development of new areas began: property and pet insurance, voluntary medical insurance, accident insurance. Then, legal entities began to apply to the company. A type of cargo insurance has appeared. In 1995, a group of companies breaks into prestigious employers. Even before OSAGO began to be involved here, the agent network was developing very quickly - in 1995 their number exceeded one thousand.
