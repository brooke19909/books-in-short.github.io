Ski club Guy Severin: description, services. The best ski centers in Russia

The steep bank of the Moscow River became a pilgrimage area in the winter. These places were discovered back in 1948. The first track tested the champion of alpine skiing in Moscow. Thanks to this, a ski resort was founded here , which bears the name of the very champion - Guy Severin.

general description



The structure of the ski resort "Chulkovo" (Guy Severin) includes four slopes. The first is considered the main one, there is a sharp drop in altitude, steep occasions and a winding road. The track is not intended for beginners, it is designed for real professionals. No less difficult is the second track, which is called "Forest". The thing is that her path is very difficult, so she obeys only very experienced skiers. Track number 3 was created for children and people who are just mastering the basics of skiing, there are no complicated turns or danger zones. The fourth track is called "Tubing". It is also a training area where you can work out with a personal ski instructor. Therefore, the main thing is to have a desire to learn how to ride, and everything else can be organized by staff. You only need to come to the ski club Guy Severin.

Training



Throughout the day on the slope you can watch the photographer and videographer. Shooting is constantly conducted here, after which a special bank of photographs of the entire resort is created. Thus, each guest can choose the appropriate photos for their personal album. In addition to the spontaneous photo shoot, you can contact the photographer and order individual shooting. As a result, the guest will receive unique and professional pictures of his vacation.

On the basis of the resort, a children's and youth school of sports skiing was created. Back in Soviet times, it was considered one of the most prestigious. It is here that you can get the fundamental skills of professional skiing. For some time, the school stopped its work, but since 2005 it has been functioning and training young staff again. At the moment, classes are held not only with children, but also with adults. Ski Club Guy Severin is famous for its qualified staff. Professional teachers have a high level of skill, are looking for an individual approach to each student, which gives wonderful results in the end.

Security

Particular attention is paid to security. All disputed places have been identified. It is there that special nets are provided, and in case of a fall there are mats for soft landing. Each track is illuminated, so it is impossible to get lost. The city takes care of the comfort of guests. Ski Club Guy Severin has a developed infrastructure.

Infrastructure

Near the tracks there is a rental center where you can take any equipment for recreation. Rental of adult sets costs 600 rubles. per hour, and for children under 13 years old - 450 rubles for the same time. There is also a medical assistance point nearby. All services there are absolutely free, so in case of an unsuccessful fall or hypothermia, you can contact a medical center where they will provide the necessary assistance. There is also a specially equipped room where you can warm up, drink tea and change clothes. The administration took care of all the necessary factors convenient for the tourist.

For beginners who do not yet have their own equipment, there is a store called Snowball. Here you can buy any products for skiing. It has everything from costumes to professional skiing. The store presents goods of famous brands. Since purchases in such stores are not cheap, the customer is given the opportunity to conduct some kind of product test. That is, any equipment can be tested first, and then confirm the purchase. The ski club Guy Severin is located extremely favorably.

Getting to the resort is easy. It can be a bus or a personal car. For all questions of interest, it is better to immediately contact the administration.

Novosibirsk ski center "Inya"





Ski club "Inya" is located in the Oktyabrsky district of the city of Novosibirsk. The tracks are located on the southern slope of the Key Kamyshensky plateau above the river Inya. An incredible view opens above the pond, therefore, they not only ride here, but also conduct photo shoots.

On the basis of this club, two tracks for beginners were created, everything was done as comfortably as possible, so study will be easy for everyone. A special Carving ski simulator is also designed for inexperienced skiers to train them in accordance with all the latest technologies. It is this simulator that will allow you to learn a beginner‚Äôs course in record time. Therefore, the guests of this club are attracted by such a prospect.

In addition to the slopes, a small winter town has been created on the territory. There are two very cozy cafes where you can always warm up with warm drinks. The food is served exclusively Russian cuisine. Some dishes are quite high in calories. It is rather a necessity, since skiing requires a lot of energy. The complex also includes 10 comfortable rooms, so you can stay here for a few days. The parking zone accommodates 300 cars at a time.

Ski club "Kant"



This club offers its guests training in any winter sport. Therefore, you can try not only skiing, but also much more. But if, nevertheless, the guest arrived for the sake of skiing, then 18 tracks of varying difficulty were created here, which attracts even professionals. The center is located in Moscow at the address Elektrolitny Proezd, 7.

"Yakhroma" - a club for skiers



Ski club "Yakhroma" has the closest location to Moscow. It takes about 30 minutes to get there. This attracts many guests. A large base involves a huge amount of entertainment, fully thought-out leisure and a high level of service. Here both a beginner and a true professional will find something to do.
