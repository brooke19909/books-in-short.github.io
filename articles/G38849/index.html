5230 Nokia: specifications, prices, photos

Nokia has launched its most famous devices related to the Lumia line, relatively recently. Prior to that, the manufacturer of smartphones in the arsenal had phones with much simpler characteristics. general description





Immediately after its release, the model was positioned as an innovative solution, but very soon it became a typical representative of the budget class of the company's products. If you believe the data in the Internet media, the phone was released in 2009 at a price of 149 euros - for the simple version, and 225 euros - for the device with the additional Nokia Comes With Music service. If we talk about official sales in our country, only the more expensive Nokia 5230 model could be found in stores. The characteristic of the service shows that this is a musical addition that gave the user access to listening to their favorite music. Again, at the time of the presentation of the device, its functions were quite new.

Housing

Since, as already noted above, the Nokia 5230 model (the characteristics confirm this) is a relatively budget option, it is not surprising that it was offered on the market in a plastic case. However, as shown by customer recommendations, the material here is quite pleasant to the touch, which leaves a positive impression.

The Nokia 5230 device, the characteristics of which we present at the moment, comes on the market in several variations. They differ among themselves depending on which panel we are talking about. The front of the phone is available on the market in black and white, while the back is decorated with black, red, pink, blue or light green cover - the choice of the buyer. Such a variety definitely allows you to personalize your phone ‚Äúfor yourself‚Äù, which will clearly appeal to a young audience of users of the Nokia 5230 model.

Display





So, it‚Äôs worth mentioning right away - the screen here, like on old keyboard smartphones, belongs to the TFT type. Its resolution is only 360x640 pixels, which looks ridiculous compared to modern devices. The sensor on this model is installed resistive, so there will be problems with simultaneously pressing several points on the screen.

Filling

Given the age of the device, the technical equipment here is also difficult to call advanced - there is a processor with a frequency of 434 MHz, which has a RAM of 128 megabytes. Although this is not much, the phone was able to play the games of that time. Developers, by the way, judging by the reviews, quite qualitatively optimized the Nokia 5230.

The characteristics of the WiFi module are not mentioned, for this reason we conclude that such a function is not provided here. But the device supports mobile Internet. In addition, Nokia has a physical memory of 78 MB (with the possibility of increasing it due to a memory card).





It is also important to mention the camera on the phone that this review is dedicated to. Of course, the resolution in it is not large enough to photograph the text (only 2 megapixels); but already this was a good achievement at that time for the Nokia 5230.

The characteristic of the camera is nothing compared to the accelerometer - an option that allows you to determine the position of the device in space. It is very often used, as we know, in games and applications.

operating system

By the way, regarding the software and OS for the model - of course, not modern Android or iOS is installed here. The model is Symbian 9.4, which coped relatively well with the tasks set for Nokia 5230. The GPS feature shows that the device is capable of receiving a signal at 20 stations with the A-GPS option connected. Thanks to such an OS, a large number of entertainment content was already available on the Internet at that time. Thus, it is impossible to say that a device that does not work on Android or iOS is capable of anything.

Total

Of course, the Nokia 5230 model can not be compared with modern budget phones for the reason that it was released back in 2009.
