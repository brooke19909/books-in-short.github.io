Sunrise Island View - a dream or reality?

Description: Sunrise Island View is located in a romantic bay called Sharks Bay, which is famous for its beautiful coral reef. Nearby, just three kilometers away, is the Sharm El Sheikh Airport. At the exit of the hotel is a small pretty village of Soho.





Rooms: Altogether, Sunrise Island View has 492 guestrooms. They can be standard, with a living room, two rooms or two bedrooms at once. All rooms have a private bathroom, safe, balcony, etc. This hotel has special rooms for non-smokers. Cleaning is done regularly, and in order to significantly improve its quality, you should tip and be sure to indicate what should be removed.

Meals: The hotel has three own restaurants. Mostly tourists offer a buffet. Two additional restaurants offer national and Italian cuisine. Guests can sample dishes from different countries. Meat dishes are presented in a wide assortment. You can also enjoy the various fruits that Sunrise Hotel offers with the generosity of a hospitable host. Egypt is a country where fruits such as persimmons, tangerines, dates, strawberries, grapes, etc. are grown.

If necessary, you can order special food for children, as well as for those who need diet food.

The range of alcoholic beverages in Sunrise Island View is quite large. Those who wish can order both light drinks and stronger ones.





Beach: A wide sandy beach is only 30 meters away. There are a sufficient number of beach accessories, a lot of water entertainments, guests can take a boat ride, a banana, and parachute.





Useful information: This hotel has various sports facilities. Those who wish can play beach volleyball, golf, soccer, etc. There is a gym. Extreme lovers can try their hand at diving.

In the evenings, a disco is available for guests. Viewers will be interested in watching a colorful fire show.

The hotel has a kids club where children spend time under the guidance of professional educators. Children can play outdoor games, bowling, draw and play with peers. In the evenings, a children's disco is especially for the children. Toddlers love spending their time in children's pools on slides.

Sunrise Island has four pools. The first with a slide and a water bar is located near the main restaurant. The second - with heating and slide - is designed for children. In this pool, gymnastics and water aerobics are performed in the morning . At the beach there is a third pool with music, a bar and a waterfall. The last pool is aimed at an adult audience.

Numerous shops are located on site. There is a spa center for guests.

Digest: A luxurious garden with canals, waterfalls and bridges gives the Sunrise Island View Hotel a fabulous, heavenly atmosphere, as hotel guests often mention in reviews. The territory is well-groomed, many different trees and flowers are planted on it. Of particular interest are the compositions of stones and bushes. In the dark, the area is decoratively highlighted.

Most tourists are happy with their stay at Sunrise Island View. Most guests consider the service, food and infrastructure to be excellent.
