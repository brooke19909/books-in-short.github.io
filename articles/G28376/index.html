Blue Wave Suite Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): room description, photos, services and reviews

If you want to relax with family or friends in an atmosphere of carefree and fun, then you should definitely go to the Blue Wave Suite Hotel Resort. Here you will be surrounded by the attention and care of the staff, and you will be able to take advantage of the mass of services and amenities.





Hotel info

This luxury hotel was built in 1999. Since then, it has gained wide popularity among travelers. Given that the reconstruction was carried out in 2014, the territory and number of rooms look completely updated. It is worth noting that only the main building underwent major repairs.

Blue Wave Suite Hotel has a small area (about 7000 sq. M.). It houses the main four-story building, as well as six small detached blocks. Despite the compactness of the territory and the large number of vacationers, the hotel has absolutely no crowding, which is ensured by a successful layout.

This hotel provides all the features and amenities that will make your stay comfortable and complete. Pools, the beach, restaurants, dry cleaning, laundry, a gym are not a complete list of infrastructure facilities of the hotel.

Hotel Location

The guests of the resort hotel will certainly appreciate the quality service, convenient location and warm weather. Alanya is only a few kilometers from the hotel (about 20 minutes drive). The nearest airport is just over 100 km from the hotel. It is worth noting that the sea harbor of Alanya, located near the hotel, provides direct communication with Cyprus, the distance to which is no more than 5 km. The road to the beach will not take you 10 minutes.

Resort Rooms

Comfortable accommodation provides its guests with the Blue Wave Suite Hotel. You can choose the following options for placement:

  • Economy rooms are ideal for those tourists who do not like to spend a lot of time indoors, and spend most of the day on excursions and by the sea. The small room has two twin beds, a desk and nightstands.
  • Standard rooms are equipped with a large bed or two twin beds. It is also possible to install an additional clamshell. There is a small sitting area with a soft armchair and a floor lamp.
  • The suites are spacious and have an elegant interior. The room is decorated in calm colors, which contributes to a good rest. In addition to the bedroom, there is a living room area with upholstered furniture. It is worth noting that the room is decorated with bright paintings.
  • The hotel has special rooms for disabled guests. For the comfort of people with disabilities, rooms are equipped with wide doorways, as well as comfortable handrails.




Guest Room Amenities

All rooms at Blue Wave Suite Hotel Resort are decorated in an elegant, contemporary style and equipped with the latest technology. In your apartment you can take advantage of a wide range of amenities. It is worth noting the following:

  • satellite television with many music, news and entertainment channels (also provided in Russian);
  • mini-bar with fridge (alcohol and carbonated drinks are paid separately);
  • individual air conditioning system that helps you maintain a cool air temperature in the room;
  • reliable code safe that will ensure the safety of your valuables;
  • thanks to the presence of a landline telephone, you can, without leaving your room, make an order in a restaurant, as well as contact the administration with a request or comment;
  • the bathroom has a modern shower, as well as an electric hairdryer and a full set of bath accessories;
  • rooms have access to a private balcony with plastic chairs and clothesline.

Close attention is paid to housekeeping. The apartments of Blue Wave Suite Hotel 4 * are cleaned daily, and bed linen is changed twice a week.





Services for guests

Each guest of the hotel will be surrounded by care and attention because travelers are provided with a huge number of services. Free service includes the following items:

  • the use of comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas to protect from the sun both on the beach and around the pool;
  • rental of special equipment for people with disabilities;
  • use of wireless Internet in public areas;
  • taxi call at the appointed time;
  • room service during the day.

Part of the services provided to guests at an additional cost. The hotel Blue Wave Suite Hotel 4 * includes the following:

  • rent a car, motorcycle or bike;
  • use of the safe box at the reception;
  • room service at night;
  • currency exchange operations (in addition to exchange differences, a commission is also charged);
  • transfer to the airport or any other designated point;
  • washing and ironing;
  • delivery of food and drinks to the apartment from the restaurant;
  • calling a doctor to the hotel or directly to the room.

Despite the fact that the list of services is not the widest, at the hotel you will not feel left out. A small number of services is more than compensated by the attention and friendliness of qualified personnel.

Entertainment and outdoor activities

Warm Turkish weather has a fun and active pastime. Alanya can offer travelers plenty of opportunities for vibrant entertainment. In particular, in this hotel you will have access to the following leisure activities:

  • evening animated shows, which include dance and vocal numbers, as well as fun contests and a disco;
  • own well-groomed beach with sunbeds, sunshades and other amenities;
  • children can have fun in the mini club;
  • in the night club you can enjoy compositions from invited DJs, as well as incendiary dances;
  • a Turkish bath will help to relax and improve well-being;
  • in the garden, as well as in some rooms there are ping-pong tables;
  • in the open air there is a playground with modern attractions and a safe cover;
  • if you want to improve your health, get rid of cellulite or just relax, be sure to visit the massage parlor;
  • to keep fit, exercise regularly in the gym;
  • outdoor pools with water slides.




Useful information for tourists

One of the most attractive destinations for lovers of sea holidays is Turkey. Blue Wave Suite Hotel 4 * has some internal rules that tourists should familiarize themselves with:

  • check-in and check-out of the guests is carried out, respectively, at two in the afternoon and before noon (if you have available rooms, you can use the services of early registration or late check-out);
  • children stay in the apartment for free if they do not need extra beds;
  • The hotel does not offer the possibility of staying with pets;
  • You can use a plastic card to pay for received services through the terminal;
  • throughout the hotel (with the exception of specially designated places) smoking is prohibited;
  • if with your arrival at the hotel you decide to celebrate a wedding or a wedding anniversary, then as a presentation you will be provided with fruits, sweets and champagne in your room;
  • birthday people are given a birthday cake (can be served in a restaurant or delivered to a room);
  • if you relax in this hotel more than two times, then automatically become a regular guest, which gives you the right to upgrade the room category with a sufficient number of free places.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, in the Blue Wave Suite 4 * hotel you may encounter a number of negative points. They are reflected in negative reviews as follows:

  • the hotel does not give out free beach towels, and so you have to bring your own or buy new ones at the supermarket;
  • the sea is located far from the hotel, despite the fact that this is the first line;
  • there are no catering establishments on the beach, and therefore, to have a bite to eat, you will have to return to the hotel;
  • rather monotonous food, which bothers after a few days;
  • the animation is rather sluggish;
  • It is worth noting that the view from the windows of the rooms facing the courtyard is very unpleasant - garbage cans, fittings, bricks and other hotel vital products;
  • for the use of air conditioning you have to leave an impressive deposit of $ 20;
  • a very small shower, in which it will be inconvenient for people of large physique to bathe;
  • all entertainment after eleven in the evening is paid;
  • very little food that can be fed to babies;
  • old air conditioners can not cope with their task;
  • plumbing requires replacement;
  • a lot of noise from the disco, which is held after dinner;
  • in rooms, wireless internet is very slow;
  • tourists are not informed that only one of the 6 buildings of the hotel has been repaired, and therefore, most likely, you will have to live in not the best conditions;
  • When cleaning maids do not pay enough time to the bathroom (the toilet does not wash and the mirror is not wiped);
  • the entry into the sea is covered with soft sand, but after a couple of meters sharp stones begin;
  • for such a large number of vacationers (about 700 people) the pool is too small;
  • if you are booking a two-room suite, please note that the air conditioner will be installed only in one of the bedrooms, and in the second it will be very stuffy;
  • use of the safe is possible only at an additional cost;
  • stained bath towels;
  • the hotel is quite far from the airport, and therefore the transfer can stretch for several tiring hours;
  • the hotel has an incredibly small area, which is almost completely occupied by buildings and pools (there is nowhere to go for a walk);
  • despite a well-functioning reservation system, the hotel has more guests than the number of available rooms, and therefore some tourists are accommodated in neighboring "triples";
  • the buffet menu contains very specific Turkish soups, which may not be to the taste of the European;
  • a limit is set for the use of wireless Internet (free of charge 200 MB per day);
  • low-quality powder juices;
  • on the way to the hotel you will have to cross a busy roadway, on which there is no pedestrian crossing;
  • the hotel has very expensive excursions (it is better to buy them from local guides);
  • if you leave the hotel early, lunch box will not be provided to you;
  • huge lines are always lined up to the counter where dishes from the chef are served, and therefore not all guests manage to try it;
  • in the first days of your stay, finding a hotel beach is quite a challenge.

General impression

For most travelers, one of the most favorite tourist destinations is Turkey. Hotel Blue Wave Suite Hotel 4 * many choose because of affordable prices. Be prepared for the fact that the road from the airport to the hotel will take you several hours. Nevertheless, a positive impression of the institution compensates for all the inconvenience. You will certainly enjoy the elegant interior of the hall, the cleanliness of the territory and the comfort of the rooms. Speaking of rooms, it is worth noting that, with the exception of the main building, they are quite outdated. But if you are not going to spend the whole day indoors, then most likely do not pay attention to it.

The food in this hotel deserves all praise, despite the fact that it is pretty monotonous - a wide range allows you to constantly experiment. The hotel is called a real sweet tooth paradise. This is due to the huge amount of confectionery served in the restaurant. The only thing that disappoints is not very high-quality drinks. If we talk about animation, then it is represented by only two employees. Nevertheless, their energy and enthusiasm is enough to amuse all vacationers. Speaking of the beach, it is worth noting that it is clean, well maintained and well equipped. The only thing that spoils the impression is its remoteness from the hotel.
