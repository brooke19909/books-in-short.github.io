Club "Grand Michel" - bowling in Saratov

The game of bowling implies a high coordination of movements, a well-developed eye, coordination of body movements. You can play alone or in a large company. Holding a birthday or corporate event at a bowling alley is a great idea.

Location

The sports and entertainment club Grand Michel is located in the center of Saratov, on Kirova Avenue. In the immediate vicinity there is a parking lot with lots of parking spaces.

The club is a very popular holiday destination both among local residents and visitors. Here you can have a great time in the company of those closest to you: visit a restaurant, have fun on the dance floor, which can simultaneously accommodate up to 200 people, play billiards and bowling.

Advantages over other bowling centers

Modern equipment, qualified instructors, professional equipment - all this you can find in the bowling in the "Grand Michel" (Saratov). A high level of customer service is a priority for management and staff.

It features 6 upscale game paths. Each of them is equipped with modern AMF equipment, thanks to which prompts are issued on TV screens, they can be used to improve your results and the quality of the game. All the equipment necessary for the game, including special shoes, is included in the rental price.

Just a couple of hours of bowling at the Grand Michel (Saratov) is enough to feel like a professional player participating in world-class competitions.

Cost of visiting

The cost of one hour of the game depends on the day of the week and time of day. The cheapest rates on weekdays from 12:00 to 18:00 - 650 rubles. On weekends and holidays, the cost of one hour of track rental can reach 900 rubles.

An undoubted advantage is that a bowling bar is organized, where visitors are offered a variety of Japanese and European dishes, prepared by reservation.

In addition to the rental service for bowling alleys, guests are offered professionally made reaming of balls according to an individual order, repairs, as well as grinding.

It is best to reserve places and time in advance, reserve a track in order to play bowling at the Grand Michel in Saratov. By phone 8 (452) 27-98-41, you can contact the administrator and he will answer all your questions.
