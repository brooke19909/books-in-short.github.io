About the recording loop and fragment length



Since the memory in the recorder is limited, and the recording should not stop, the rewriting of old information is the solution. That is, while the memory was free, the video was recorded in the normal mode, but as soon as it was full, the new recording begins to overlap with the old one from the very beginning.

Recording is not a continuous stream. It is divided into files of a certain duration. Most of the models break the recording into fragments for 1, 2, 3, 5, 10, 15 minutes. There are devices that write everything in one large file, but this is not the best idea. In some registrars, you can independently change the duration of the saved video fragments. But there are instances where this parameter is strictly prescribed in the device code. It will be useful to study the reviews on the purchased DVR: which is better?

What is the length of the fragment to choose? Depends on the circumstances. Mode 1 and 2 minutes can immediately be thrown back. Digging into a heap of one-minute files is a pleasure below average. If trips take place within the city limits, then a mode of 3 or 5 minutes is quite suitable. If the trip promises to be long, for example, an excursion to interesting places, you can choose the 15-minute mode. Then there will be something to see and remember.
