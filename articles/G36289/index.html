Okeanos Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): hotel description and reviews

Want to spend an unforgettable summer vacation and discover stunning Cyprus? Do you like to relax in comfort, but do not want to overpay for a hotel? In this case, the three-star Okeanos Beach Hotel, located in the popular tourist city of Ayia Napa, can be a suitable option for you.





Location

So in the vicinity of the hotel you will find a lot of entertainment facilities, as well as restaurants, bars, shops, travel agencies, etc. The nearest beach is just a hundred meters away. The hotel is 36 kilometers from Larnaca Airport , and 172 kilometers from Paphos Air Harbor. Nicosia is 85 kilometers from the property.





Cyprus, Okeanos Beach Hotel 3 *: photo and review

Okeanos is a small, cozy hotel, offering its guests a calm measured relaxation on the shores of the picturesque sea. The hotel's housing stock includes 45 standard rooms. All apartments are newly renovated with modern furniture and appliances. Each room has air conditioning, flat-screen TV (satellite channels in different languages), minibar (for a fee), telephone, free safe, hairdryer, bathroom and balcony. Everywhere in the hotel, including the rooms, there is Wi-Fi. Guests can use it for free.

Cleaning is carried out every day while vacationers are not in the room. Bed sheets are updated three times a week, and towels as needed.

The hotel has an outdoor pool (there is a section for children and a jacuzzi), a sun terrace with sunbeds and umbrellas, a restaurant (indoor and patio), a bar, parking, and a conference room for up to 60 people. The reception is open 24 hours a day. You can also rent a car at the hotel or book an excursion to a place of interest to you.





Okeanos Beach Hotel 3 *: reviews and comments from tourists

Every person planning a vacation wants everything to go perfectly while on vacation. Indeed, most of us only once a year can break away from everyday work and relax on the seashore. It is not surprising that people want to get a charge of positive emotions, vivid impressions and memories that will brighten up gray everyday life in the cold season. In this regard, modern tourists try very carefully to approach the issue of choosing a hotel. After all, the overall impressions of foreign vacations will largely depend on how comfortable the hotel will be. According to experienced travelers, special attention when planning a vacation should be given to studying the comments of those people who have already visited the place you are interested in. Based on their reviews, you can make a more complete and closer to reality view of a particular hotel, which will help you make the right choice. After all, former guests do not try to embellish the hotel in which they lived, as representatives of travel agencies can do. On the contrary, people want to share information not only about advantages, but also about disadvantages. So you will know almost exactly what awaits you in a particular hotel, if you decide to stay in it.

Today we offer to find out together what impressions our 3-star Cyprus hotel Okeanos Beach Hotel has left for our compatriots. Reviews of former guests, we note right away, are mostly positive and even sometimes enthusiastic.





Rating

From the foregoing, the obvious conclusion suggests that the hotel we are considering is fully justifiable. Confirmation of this is its rating. It is based on the assessment of guests on different points. So, the rating of Oceanos Beach is 4.1 on a five-point scale. Tourists praised the infrastructure, service, room equipment, location and the nearest beach. Some dissatisfaction concerned only the organization of leisure for the youngest travelers. But we propose to find out everything in order.

Location

Almost all tourists who commented on Okeanos Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) mention the excellent location of the hotel. So, according to them, it is located very close to the sea. So, the nearest beach can be reached in just a couple of minutes. The hotel is in a quiet and picturesque location. At the same time, the city center with lots of entertainment can be reached on foot in just ten minutes. In addition, a small port is located next to the hotel. From here you can go on a fascinating sea excursion.

Hotel itself

Judging by the comments they left, travelers really liked the fact that Oceanos is a small hotel. So, even when it is almost full, here you will not meet crowds of tourists, lines in the restaurant and the hype around the sun loungers by the pool. Thus, this hotel is perfect for a relaxing leisurely and relaxed vacation on the shores of the azure sea. As for the hotel‚Äôs own territory, it is small. However, there is a pool with a section for children and a jacuzzi, as well as a sun terrace with sun loungers and a restaurant patio.



Housing stock

As follows from the reviews of tourists, they were very pleased with the rooms offered to them at Okeanos Beach Hotel (Ayia Napa). So, the apartments here are very bright and pleasantly decorated. They can not be called too spacious, but being in them is very comfortable. Also, guests mention comfortable high-quality furniture and modern appliances. In the rooms everything is working properly. If, nevertheless, something breaks, then inform the administrator at the reception and the problem will be fixed very quickly. An indisputable plus is the fact that almost all apartments have at least partial sea views. So, even while in your room, you can enjoy the azure waters.

As for the equipment of the apartments, there is everything you need for a comfortable stay during your vacation. So, for example, many guests noted with pleasure the fact of having a free safe and working perfectly not only in the rooms, but also throughout the hotel wireless Internet.

Staff

Judging by their comments, our compatriots were very pleased with the hotel staff. So, they note that the staff here are very friendly, helpful, coping with their duties and just pleasant in communication. And this applies to absolutely everyone - from managers and waiters and maids.

As for the knowledge of foreign languages, the majority of Oceanos employees do not speak Russian. However, you can always find a Russian-speaking administrator at the reception, who, if necessary, will help you to resolve any problem or answer your question.

Hotel Policies

Accommodation of newly arrived guests in Okeanos Beach Hotel 3 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) is carried out strictly after two in the afternoon. According to some tourists, they were slightly surprised by such strict rules. Indeed, in most hotels, especially when it comes to the all-inclusive concept, the bracelets for new arrivals were worn immediately, regardless of the time of arrival, even if they could be accommodated a bit later. At Oceanos, you will have to wait two hours, and only then can you use the infrastructure. However, for most travelers this did not become a serious problem. After all, you can always leave things for storage at the reception and go for a snack in a cafe next door or go straight to the beach. On the last day of your stay, you should leave the room before noon.

Beach

According to the vast majority of our compatriots who chose the three-star Okeanos Beach Hotel, their vacation at sea was a success! So, literally a hundred meters from the hotel there is a large clean beach equipped with everything necessary. It offers vacationers there are 10 pairs of sunbeds belonging to the hotel. Accordingly, its guests can use them for free. As for entering the water, the bottom is stony right next to the hotel. However, you can reach the depths along a specially laid path from bags filled with sand. So no difficulties should arise here. In addition, just a five-minute walk from the hotel there is an excellent beach with a sandy sunset in the sea. However, here you already have to pay for using sunbeds. But you can always sit on an unoccupied chaise lounge area, laying a mat. By the way, tourists pay attention that beach towels are not provided for guests at Okeanos . Therefore, it makes sense to capture them from home, or to buy it already upon arrival in Cyprus.

Entertainment

Since the hotel "Oceanos" is more aimed at a relaxing holiday, special fun is not offered to guests here. However, for tourists there is an outdoor pool with a sun terrace, TV lounge, gym, sauna. In addition, guests can play billiards or table tennis. As for the animation, in the form in which it is present in many hotels in Turkey, you will not find it here. So, in the evenings sometimes live music is played and Cypriot evenings are held. At the same time, vacationers do not lack entertainment. After all, the hotel is very close to the city center with lots of clubs, bars and other places where you can have a great time.

In addition, experienced travelers advise everyone not to limit themselves to beach vacations and walks in the vicinity, but be sure to go on excursions. Moreover, you can explore new places both independently and as part of an organized group. In the first case, you can rent a car. This can be done either directly at the hotel or in one of the agencies in excess of those available in Ayia Napa. In this case, you can plan your route and time yourself. As for organized excursions, you can also order them by contacting the Oceanos Reception. In addition, the city has many travel agencies offering the same services.
