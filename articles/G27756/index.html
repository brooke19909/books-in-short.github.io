Number of rooms

Aquapark Jungle Hotel consists of a main two-story building and one-story and two-story chalets. The hotel has 860 rooms, among which there are standards and family rooms. If necessary, you can order a connection apartment.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, hairdryers, balconies, telephones, safes, minibars, toiletries, bathrooms, satellite channels, modern TVs, wardrobes, electric kettles and other amenities.

The standards are for 2 adults and 2 children. Family apartments are more spacious, therefore they can accommodate up to 6 people.

Rooms are cleaned daily, as is linen change. Room service is available for guests for a fee.
