Hotel Anthea Hotel Apts 3 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus)

Ayia Napa is a large sea city on the island of Cyprus. It has an amazing history and is famous for its nature reserve. The status of the resort received at the end of the XX century. Beautiful sandy beaches attracted tourists to these parts, the settlement began to actively develop. Today, Ayia Napa boasts a lively nightlife and plenty of entertainment.

In captivity of centuries





The city is a favorite vacation spot not only for foreign tourists, but also for local residents. On Fridays, island hotels are flooded with Cypriots who spend their weekends on the coast. The resort has such a large number of bars and discos that it is often compared to Ibiza.

The whole galaxy of beaches of the island is awarded the international sign "Blue Flag". This suggests that the water in the waters of Cyprus does not contain harmful substances. Swimming in it is safe. The coastline is regularly cleaned and put in order. The local sunsets are the hallmark of Ayia Napa. They color the high southern sky in orange, crimson and purple tones.
