Radio "Pioneer" 2 Din: characteristics and reviews

Pioneer is the largest producer of multimedia equipment. Most famous for its radio 1 Dean and 2 Dean, which will be discussed. When choosing 2 Din car radio, it is worth considering the size of the display, functionality and appearance. At the Pioneer models he is at the highest level.

Short description

2 Din Pioneer radios most often have a 7-inch display, and there are also 6-inch models. They have a bluetooth module, in some there is the possibility of distributing wifi. There is a built-in navigation system, media players and much more.

Description

Review 2 Din car radio "Pioneer" 170 (Pioneer AVH-170) should start with the fact that it is available in two versions - with red backlight and green backlight (AVH-170G). The package includes the radio itself, a shaft for installation in the car body, a decorative frame, an external USB cable for more convenient use of USB devices, as well as an ISO cable and connector.

Connecting 2 Din Pioneer radios is not difficult. The shaft is installed in the hole of the head unit, then through the connector that comes with the kit, it is connected to the power supply of the car and to the speaker wires. If desired, you can connect a USB drive to the back, since the USB connector is located there.

Aux input, connector for connecting a rear view camera, connector for controlling buttons on the steering wheel - all this is located on the rear panel.

On the front panel is a large six-inch touch screen. On top of it is a disk drive, microphone. On the left of the display are buttons for volume control, song switching, mute and display.

2 Din Pioneer radio tape recorder has a large number of settings, a bright display and excellent saturation. 7 equalizer settings are available to the user. You can also configure them yourself.

Description

The design of this 2 Din Pioneer radio is at the highest level. It supports almost all popular formats, even the WAW format, which practically does not take up space on the device.

Unlike its previous models, this radio tape recorder supports synchronization with devices on the Android operating system, there is also a karaoke function, which no other version has. Instead of the old equalizer, there is a 13-band, the sound quality of which can be adjusted for each listener. You can adjust each speaker, also adjust the bass level.

It supports this radio and devices with IOS, playing their media format (AAC). Using the bluetooth module, you can connect 2 devices to the radio without loss of radio control functions on each. Thanks to a remote microphone that can be attached to the body near the steering wheel, the device can be used as a headset for talking.

Touch screen, diagonal - 6 inches, takes up the whole place, is surrounded only by the display frame. On the left of the screen are the control buttons, highlighted in green. At the back there is a connector for connecting a rear view camera, an adapter for controlling buttons on the steering wheel, a main connector, as well as a USB and RCA input. Also located is a connector for an antenna that amplifies the signal built-in.

The screen is very bright, coated with anti-reflective coating, has more than 200 thousand colors. The functionality of this radio is very solid: from the equalizer settings to watching movies and listening to Internet radio using a smartphone connected to it.

The menu itself is understandable even without a manual; such a radio tape recorder will look great in many cars without spoiling their appearance. The price of this radio tape recorder ranges from 17 to 20 thousand rubles (250-300 dollars).
