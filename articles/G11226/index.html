Waterpark in Montenegro: description of a hotel with water activities

When choosing a travel option, a significant factor is the proximity of the sea, pond or water activities. The combination of the sea, forests with mountain air, a large number of sunny days creates that healing and relaxing effect, which allows you to maintain health and mood for a long time after rest.

One of the best options in this case is Montenegro. Its majestic nature, clear waters of the Adriatic, rich Slavic cuisine attract travelers who are tired of the bustle of the city and megacities. The four-star Hotel Mediteran, on the territory of which the only water park in Montenegro is located, is perfect for a good holiday in this hospitable country.

Description of hotel with water park

The hotel is located in the village of Becici, located three kilometers from Budva, one of the most famous resorts in Montenegro.

This is a beach hotel located on the first coastline 200 meters from the sea and less than 40 kilometers from the international airport. Therefore, a short shuttle will quickly bring travelers tired of the flight to the hotel rooms.

Reconstructed in 2004, the Hotel Mediteran offers guests more than 200 rooms of various levels of categories. The complex has a playground, aniamation, water park. In Montenegro, this is a rather rare occurrence, since recreation is more often oriented to the adult population.

Accommodation and meals

The Meridian Hotel offers guests accommodation in rooms of nine categories. Depending on the taste, the level of solvency of the client and taking into account individual requests, you can choose a standard level number, a prestigious one or eat Marlene Dietrich in an elegant style solution.

Regardless of the cost, each room is equipped with everything necessary for a full stay in the hotel: there is all furniture, air conditioning, a bathroom with toiletries, a minibar, a TV, a safe. The rooms - balconies with sea views, water park. In Montenegro Great Slavic cuisine. The hotel offers the concept of a breakfast buffet or half board.

Entertainment

The only hotel with a water park in Montenegro is the pride of the country. On the territory of more than seven thousand square meters there are several cascading pools, a jacuzzi, a plateau for sunbathing and attractions.

Water park in Montenegro it is available for residents of other hotels, in addition, a special shuttle service has been organized for visitors from other resorts of the country to visit it. It is necessary to purchase an entrance ticket, the cost of which is low - 15-30 euros. The park also has courts and playgrounds for sports.

Vacation Reviews

Holidays in Montenegro appeal to most travelers. The benevolence of the population, the charm of the natural landscape, the transparency of the waters of the Adriatic Sea - all these are the advantages of the tourist region.

However, comparing travel with holidays in Turkey and other European resorts, vacationers note a low level of development of hotel infrastructure. Of course, in comparison with the water megacomplexes, which are numerous in Turkish hotels, the only water park in Montenegro (Budva) looks quite modest .

Reviews of tourists note the advantages of the few tourists, the compactness of the complex, well-groomed territory.

Fans of a relaxing holiday return again and again to this wonderful country, which retained an indisputable advantage in its shortcomings - it did not allow the entertainment industry to absorb the natural richness and diversity of the landscape.
