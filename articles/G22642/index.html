Five best beaches of Barnaul

What does every person want in the summer? Of course, visit a cool place, sunbathe, swim in a clean reservoir, enjoy the fresh air and beautiful vegetation. All this can be obtained on the beach of Barnaul. Which place to choose? There may be several options.

Beach in the city





Not so long ago, the most amazing city beach opened in Barnaul, which is located on Enthusiasts Street 12c. The main feature is that it is located directly in the park, in the city, and you can get to it by public transport. Visitors like the fact that on this beach in Barnaul there is a special artificial surface and then you do not have to wash off the hated sand. There is also a children's area - a small Italian pool with heating function.

Another amazing feature is the presence of two bars, in water and on land, you can buy a cool drink right while swimming.

Solar





More popular among locals enjoys the beach "Sunny" in Barnaul. And there are several reasons for this:

  1. On its territory there is a large body of water - a spacious lake, with crystal clear water without a muddy bottom and vegetation.
  2. On the shore there are more than 600 sun loungers and many umbrellas that create a shadow.
  3. This place has gained popularity because of the availability of spacious sports grounds for playing soccer and volleyball.
  4. There is also a small children's area with attractions.
  5. Cozy cafes, located right on the beach, allow you to sit in silence, drink a cool drink and eat ice cream. Alcohol is prohibited in the complex. Vigilant guards for violations of the rules may be asked to leave the territory.
  6. Here the infrastructure is excellently developed: parking, left-luggage office for storage of things, cabins for changing clothes and much more.
  7. Cleaning is carried out regularly.

Sunny Beach in Barnaul is located in the village of the same name. People can stay in this place from 10.00 to 21.00. It is worth noting that admission is paid: 300 rubles for children and 400 for adults.

Urban

Right on the island of Pomazkin is the beach, which locals nicknamed "Urban". Despite the fact that his visit is free, there are always free places for sunbathing. You do not need to arrive in the early morning in order to settle in the sun. There is a bus stop near the beach that can be reached by public transport. Flights are operated every 15 minutes. It is very convenient for those people who do not have their own car.





The infrastructure here is well developed: there are toilets, showers and changing rooms. There are also several paid services that visitors can use if they wish. These include a trampoline complex, rental of air mattresses and boats.

Water world





The favorite place for all young people is the Water World beach in Barnaul. The advantages of this place can be listed endlessly. To a greater extent, visitors like a rich entertainment program that will not let you get bored. There is a volleyball and soccer field. Often, the administrators of the complex recruit two teams and arrange competitions, for participation in which you can get a memorable gift. Every week, various contests and quizzes are held here. Weekend evenings are beach parties. On the beach in Barnaul, located at 39a Pravoberezhniy Trakt, there is a bar that serves the most delicious milkshakes. There is also a small tent where you can rent water rides for the whole family. Entrance to the complex is paid, the cost is from 50 to 100 rubles.

Adriatic

Adriatica is a new beach in Barnaul, which is located at ul. Popova, 167a. It is designed specifically for those people who love an active lifestyle. Every day, with modern musical compositions, there are sports classes in water aerobics and swimming. They are conducted by qualified instructors. The complex has a medical service that can provide first aid if necessary. If desired, you can buy a swimsuit, slates, a towel and other necessary summer accessories. This place is not popular because of the expensive entrance, the cost of which is 400 rubles. Carrying drinks and food with you is prohibited. Everything you have to buy at a local bar at inflated prices.
