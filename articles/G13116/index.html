Oukitel K4000: review, specifications, reviews

Oukitel has released a surprisingly robust and functional device. With the K4000, you can literally hammer in nails. However, what else is the device capable of?

Design





Phone Oukitel K4000 got the most ordinary appearance. The manufacturer abandoned the catchy decisions and made the device in a standard style. However, against the background of state employees, the device still looks very solid and elegant.

The device is made of metal and, of course, plastic. Although the usual materials are used, the device received as much as 204 grams of weight. Perhaps this is due to strong glass designed to protect or insert metal, but the device is heavy.

Significant weight greatly affects the use. With prolonged use, the hand gets tired. The oleophobic coating, which does not allow the phone to slip out, brightens up the situation a bit. It also protects the device from fingerprints and dust.

Oukitel K4000 external parts turned out to be familiar. Overview of the front side made it possible to detect sensors, earpiece, screen, front-end, controls. The left side of the phone is empty, and on the right is the volume control along with the power button. The bottom end is designed for a microphone and a USB jack, and the top only for a headset jack.

The design and the small number of colors did not improve. The K4000 model is delivered only in black and white options.

Camera

Display

Oukitel K4000 became the owner of a five-inch screen. The number of ppi did not fail: as much as 294 pixels. On a budget, a resolution of 1280 by 720 is a rare occurrence. It provides high ppi and high-quality image.

In the sun, the behavior of the screen is quite acceptable. There is a slight loss of brightness, but this is not critical for the Oukitel K4000. The overview of angles is also improved due to the IPS matrix.

There is also additional protection, or rather Gorilla Glass 2.5D glass. The display resists impacts without any problems. Leaving a scratch will also be very difficult.

Hardware

The MTK chip was chosen for the role of the processor in the K4000 - the expected solution for a budget device. The processor has four cores, each of which runs on one gigahertz of frequency.

The video accelerator of the device was the Mali-T720 chip. The device received two gigabytes of RAM. Native memory was also enough. The user was provided with 16 gigabytes, as well as the ability to install a flash drive.

Autonomy

The smartphone boasts not only its strength, but also the duration of the work. The manufacturer equipped his brainchild with a battery for as much as 4000 maH.

Using the device for 5-6 hours a day, the user can not worry about charging for two days. Such autonomy is difficult to find even among flagships. Oukitel really thought out every little thing in its creation.

Price





Cost has drawn considerable attention to the Oukitel K4000. A market review reports that the price ranges from 9 to 11 thousand rubles. For a shock-resistant and functional phone, the cost looks more than affordable.
