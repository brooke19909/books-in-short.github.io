Plantronics Wireless Headset: Connectivity and Customer Reviews

Wireless technologies span different areas of life. Perhaps the most promising direction for the development of this concept is the Bluetooth headset, which has already won many fans and continues to progress. A lot of companies are represented in this area, therefore, the choice among connoisseurs of convenient gadgets is extensive - it is enough to determine the requirements for functionality and design. However, as practice shows, outwardly simple devices can cause a lot of trouble in operation. To rid yourself of such problems and expand the capabilities of a mobile device, you should initially select ergonomic and reliable models. In particular, Plantronics' wireless headset is one of those gadgets. The company develops not just functional add-ons for phones, but also gives them a stylish design.





Headset device

The device is a compact addition to the phone, through which the sound is broadcast wirelessly. Accordingly, the technical stuffing and controls are subordinate to the implementation of this task. So, in a standard design, such a device contains: a volume control, buttons for managing calls, indicators, a power switch and a connector for recharging. Thanks to this equipment, Plantronics Bluetooth headset allows you to conveniently receive and end calls, monitor the charge level, adjust the volume level, etc. Some models are equipped with additional functions, including voice dialing.

Plantronics Product Range

Plantronics was one of the first companies to adopt technologies that enable wireless communication through the Bluetooth module. To date, her assortment includes the whole Voyager family, which contains three main models. So, as a basic solution, you should consider the Voyager Legend option. This is a versatile Plantronics headset that combines functionality, attractive design and usability. Among the features of the gadget stand out: impeccable sound quality, the presence of the possibility of voice commands, a triple microphone and ease of wearing and handling.





Modification of the Legend UC can be seen as a continuation of the base model, but with a bias towards professional purposes. This advantage is realized due to wider settings, the availability of sensors, the possibility of using additional chargers and synchronization with a wide range of devices - from a computer to a phone. Also, for those who place high demands on such equipment, the Plantronics headset is available in Focus UC design. In this case, all standard features and controls are retained, but the emphasis is on more efficient noise reduction.

Connection

Before first use, the owner must pair the headset with a compatible device. This task is carried out in three stages, upon completion of which the necessary settings will be carried out. So, now you can move on to the question of how to connect a Plantronics headset to the target device. First of all, the headset is turned on, it is set to synchronization mode. The readiness of the device for pairing will be indicated by a characteristic flashing of indicators. This is followed by the second stage, during which it is necessary to activate the Bluetooth action in the phone or computer itself.





At the final stage, the wireless gadget is directly connected to the main device. To do this, start the search mode for Bluetooth devices in the phone, smartphone or computer itself and find the desired model in the list of found names. Once synchronization is confirmed, the Plantronics headset will be used as a valid accessory for the device. However, a password may be required at the time of confirmation. Headset control

In terms of management, everything is simple. The main buttons and controls have already been noted, through which the user can make calls, end them, adjust the volume, etc. The Plantronics modern Bluetooth headset does not limit its capabilities. For example, through voice control, you can receive calls by saying the word Answer. An even more technological solution involves the use of space sensors. The task of such a sensor is to recognize the moment when the user puts the headset on the ear. Then the call will be automatically received.





Operation tips

Despite the complexity of the technical implementation of the gadget, it does not guarantee perfect signal transmission. But adherence to optimal performance will help following simple tips. For example, the developers note that stable synchronization is possible only if the Plantronics headset is no more than 10 meters from the phone. If the connection is lost, it can be restored by pressing the call button or via the Bluetooth control settings in the phone itself. It is also worth listening to the signals that the headset transmits. They, in particular, help to timely charge the gadget or learn about the termination of pairing.

Positive headset reviews

The most positive feedback was taken by the ergonomics of the headset. This also applies to materials for the manufacture of the device, and design with a design, and the implementation of controls. Based on their usage practices, owners emphasize that the Plantronics headset sits comfortably in your ear and requires minimal involvement in the management of key functions. The advantages of a key operational parameter - sound transmission - also stand out. Moreover, good audibility is noted at both ends of the connection. In addition, the headset allows you to listen to music from your phone in wireless player mode, which is not available in every model from other manufacturers.





Negative reviews

Many consider the implementation of the volume control to be an offense. Although there are no complaints about audibility, adjustment can be inconvenient due to its cyclical nature. That is, after reaching a minimum or maximum, a reverse setting follows with an increase or decrease in volume, respectively. The lack of full Russification is another minus for which Plantronics headsets are criticized. Reviews, for example, note that almost all voice prompts sound in English. There are also certain complaints about the quality of voice control, but the perfect operation of this function has not yet been observed in any of the existing headsets of this type.





Conclusion

If we put aside the functional and technical advantages of such devices, then stylistic qualities will come to the fore. Still, the Plantronics phone headset is worthy of attention as a designer accessory that can complement the media image. The company offers models in different colors, but they all differ in the original style of execution. True, large sizes have become a characteristic feature of the models of this brand, while other manufacturers, on the contrary, are striving to follow the path of minimizing dimensions. This nuance is not particularly affected by operational performance, but the appearance of a large gadget on the head still scares the female audience a little.
