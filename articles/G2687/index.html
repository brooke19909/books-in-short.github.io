Kouvola (Finland): the main attractions of the town

Just 100 km from the Russian-Finnish border is the small town of Kouvola. Finland annually receives hundreds of thousands of tourists, but most of all among them are Russians. Almost daily, trains running from St. Petersburg bring hundreds of travelers to Kouvola who wish to have a good rest, have fun and sightseeing.
