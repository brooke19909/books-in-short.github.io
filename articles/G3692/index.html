Guest House "Nadezhda", Gelendzhik: services and guest reviews

In the summer, millions of tourists flock to Gelendzhik. This beautiful resort city has everything for a good rest: a warm sea, well-maintained beaches, comfortable conditions for staying and overnight for city guests, night clubs and many other establishments providing a pleasant and memorable leisure.



Guest House Nadezhda, Gelendzhik

In all resort cities located on the Black Sea, in the summer, guest houses begin to work actively and receive their guests. This type of housing for guests of the resort is very popular in Gelendzhik. Room reservation begins long before the start of the vacation period.

In 2012, one of the many complexes, the Nadezhda Guest House, was built and commissioned. Gelendzhik, Landing, 25 - the address of its location. This place is located in the microdistrict of the city of Thin Cape, the infrastructure of which allows you to organize rest in a civilized and comfortable way. There are comfortable beaches to choose from - sandy, rocky or wild. Cozy cafes and budget canteens, shops, entertainment venues are looking forward to vacationers every season. Guest House "Nadezhda" (Gelendzhik) has a convenient location near a public transport stop, which allows its guests to be mobile in Gelendzhik.



Comfort in the guest house "Nadezhda"

The guest house offers comfortable Standard rooms. Both lonely vacationers and couples can live in them. Guest House "Nadezhda" (Gelendzhik) willingly accommodates families with children of any age, for which extra beds are arranged in the rooms. For the convenience of large companies arriving on vacation, the owners of the house will take into account the wishes and offer check-in in neighboring rooms.

The guest rooms have ergonomic beds, bedding and bedding, which the hosts update for every season. Of furniture there are:

  • spacious wardrobes for clothes;
  • chairs and a table;
  • Some rooms have armchairs.

The rooms have private bathrooms and showers, hot water is served daily. Leisure in the room will help organize the availability of wireless Internet, which guests can use for free. The provision of guest rooms with household appliances - a refrigerator and a modern plasma TV with cable channels can increase the comfort of the conditions in the guest house. Equally important is the availability of air conditioning in the rooms - in the southern climate the presence of a split system is simply necessary.



On the ground floor of this guest house, the hosts equipped a kitchen for self-catering. Near the hotel there is a small free car park where guests can leave their car. The area around the guest house is landscaped, there are a lot of greenery and flowers, there is a relaxation area, there is a barbecue for guests to use. Children can play in the comfortable playground.

Additional services for guests

Guest house "Nadezhda" (Gelendzhik) on Desantnaya, in addition to accommodation, offers its guests other services for which its owners will charge an additional fee.

A cafe is organized here, the design and decoration of which has a pleasant rest. The menu offers dishes of various cuisines. Guests can be offered comprehensive three meals a day. The cost of such a service will be cheaper than regular visits to other cafes and restaurants in the city.

On check-out and check-in days, the property offers a shuttle service to or from the airport. This service allows you to save travel time and comfortably get to the place of rest.

Interesting leisure activities will be facilitated by excursions - sea or car. They are willingly organized by the owners of the house.



Guest House Nadezhda, Gelendzhik: guest reviews

Rest will be very calm if you come here with your family. Inexpensive prices and comfort in the rooms are the main feature of this place.
