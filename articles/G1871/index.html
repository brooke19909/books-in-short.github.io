Maritim Club Hotel Alantur 5 * (Turkey / Alanya) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The coast of Alanya is famous all over the world for its clean sandy beaches and warm sea waves. And today, many Russian travelers choose this place for the upcoming vacation. Especially popular is the large hotel complex Maritim Club Hotel Alantur. Over the years, the hotel managed to earn a lot of positive reviews from both tourists and critics. So what conditions await the traveler here?

Location of the hotel complex





The large Maritim Club Hotel Alantur is located on the picturesque shores of the Mediterranean Sea, surrounded by lush forests and mountains. The nearest village is located about 1000 meters - this is a cozy village called Kestel.

The undoubted advantage of the hotel is its proximity to one of the most famous resort cities called Alanya. The distance to the city center with all its attractions is only five kilometers - it can easily be reached by taxi or public transport. The nearest international airport is located in the city of Antalya - the distance is about 130 kilometers, so you should prepare for a long road.
