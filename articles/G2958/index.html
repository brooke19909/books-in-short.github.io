Mobile phone Nokia 6030

Mobile phone Nokia 6030 is developed on the basis of the largest company of the same name. Nokia has always been famous for reliable, durable equipment, thanks to which it has gained wide popularity among consumers.

The Nokia 6030 is a low-cost mobile gadget that fans of classic and minimalist will appreciate. This phone is designed only for making calls; it does not support additional advanced functions.

The mobile device was launched in 2005. The model is focused on the demand for those years.

  • size - 104 x 44 x 18 mm;

The phone is convenient in the hand, the keyboard is easy to press, the buttons are conveniently located, fingers will not slip. The menu does not cause difficulties, everything is pretty well designed.
