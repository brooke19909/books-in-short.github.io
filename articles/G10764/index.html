How to Finish Knitting: Tutorials for Beginners

One of the most entertaining activities, which very often becomes a favorite hobby, is knitting. This hobby passed through many centuries, absorbed the color and skills of different nations and is no less in demand in the modern world. If you started to learn knitting lessons for beginners, this article will help you.

Even today, a do-it-yourself thing will be a wonderful gift for relatives and friends. For example, warm socks or an original scarf. And it will look exclusive, the second such thing you will not meet. Beginner knitters have many questions, and one of the most common: "How to finish knitting?"

Knitting is an art that is mastered not only by women, but also by men. In addition to doing useful work and knitting a beautiful thing, you also relax without realizing it. In order to enter a state of calm balance, you need to take knitting and spend the evening in your favorite chair.

Here we will demonstrate how to finish knitting. This must be done when you need to complete the knitted product, and on the spoke there are many loops that need to be closed. You will need to close the hinges , for example, for a pocket, shoulder bevel or neckline.

There are several methods of how to finish knitting : with a knitting hook, with a needle and a knit stitch, but today we will talk about a simple method that does not require other skills. To do this, we will use knitting needles, and each time we will put the eyelets on one another.

Step 1. Knitting needles should be taken in the left hand, and an empty one in the right. The working thread should be knitting, as when knitting the front surface.



Step 2. The first two loops need to be knitted.



Step 3. Pass the left knitting needle from left to right into the front wall of the outer loop on the right knitting needle. This will be the furthest eyelet from the beginning of the right knitting needle. Take a closer look, the photo will show how this should happen.



Step 4. With the left knitting needle, thread this loop over the second and lower it from the right knitting needle. With this, we closed one loop, and left the second on the right knitting needle.



Step 5. The next loop to knit.

Step 6. Repeat all the steps above until you close all loose loops. There will be one loop on the right needle. The working thread must be cut off, leaving the tip of 10 cm, and stretched into this last loop. Pull the end of the thread to tighten the loop.



A little tip: if you try patterns with knitting needles for beginners, then you need to close the loops according to the pattern, in other words, the front ones are the front ones and the wrong ones are the wrong ones. Very often, novice lovers close the knitting loops very tight, and this last row turns out to be inelastic. To avoid this, take one knitting needle a size larger.

Perhaps this method of how to finish knitting, you did not like, try to close the knitting with a knitting hook, and when you get comfortable in knitting, try a knitted stitch, you will use it again and again. But this is a completely different story.
