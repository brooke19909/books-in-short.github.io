Hotel 3 * Cozy Beach Hotel, Thailand, Pattaya: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Tourists planning a vacation to Thailand should pay attention to the 3 * Cozy Beach Hotel. In this hotel complex they are able to create comfortable conditions for their guests to stay. Many vacationers who have visited the hotel earlier leave positive reviews. According to them, people of any age will have a good vacation in this place.

Where is the hotel situated?

Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * has a very good location. It is located in one of the most beautiful cities in Thailand - Pattaya. The hotel is located in the southern part of the city, on the shores of the Gulf of Thailand. It is far from the airport. The distance is 133 km. Tourists are delivered to their place of residence in 2-3 hours.





3 * Cozy Beach Hotel is located 4 km from the city center. This arrangement has a great advantage. If you wish, you can spend your holidays quietly and calmly, or plunge into the vibrant city life that reigns in the central streets of Pattaya. The famous Bali Hai Pier is only 2.3 km from Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * (Pattaya). Reviews of tourists confirm that this is a very beautiful place, which is worth a visit to every connoisseur of luxurious views of wildlife. Here you can have a photo shoot.

Hotel Description

Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) is a complex consisting of four multi-storey buildings constructed in a modern urban style. It is located on a steep hill, thanks to which the territory offers an enchanting view of Clif Bay. There are also various buildings made in the Thai style. The interior of the hotel will also delight guests with its modern design. The hall is quite spacious. While waiting for check-in, guests here will be pleased to relax after the road. You can sit on fairly comfortable soft corners.

What is remarkable about the hotel area?

Like all hotels located in the tropical zone, Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * (Thailand) is surrounded by lush greenery. The aroma from the flowers spreads throughout the territory, which many find very pleasant. But for those who suffer from allergies, it is better to take medicines that protect them from troubles with them on a trip.





The hotel has two pools. In one of them there is a jacuzzi, which can be used by all guests for free. You can relax comfortably on the sunbeds that are located around the pools. For hotel guests they are provided free of charge. You can choose a place under umbrellas or sunbathe, enjoying the warmth of the tropical sun.

The territory owned by the hotel does not differ in huge area, so here they learned to use any free space. On the roofs of the complexes are equipped with various sites for entertainment.

At first glance, you can understand that the territory is carefully looked after. There is no rubbish or dirt anywhere. Pools are also kept clean.

Number of rooms

For guests arriving to rest in Thailand, Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * can offer 480 rooms. Tourists, based on personal experience, are advised to book rooms in the 14-story building of The Grand Wing. Here all apartments have a balcony, which cannot be said about the rooms of Cozy Wing complex. They are also much more spacious and furnished with newer furniture. Guestrooms have pool, hotel garden, or sea views. It is best to admire the beauties of the local area from the highest floors. According to hotel guests, the view is amazing.





Equally comfortable are the rooms located in the Cozy Beach Resort & SPA Ocean Wing building. They also have balconies and ample space.

Tourists are not very flattering about the condition of the rooms located in Cozy Wing and The Pool Wing. These are small rooms measuring just 23 m 2 . The furniture here has long been time to replace. Renovation would also be nice, as the rooms look rather outdated. But you can still live in them.

Room decor and service

Each room, regardless of category, has everything that you may need during your stay. The beds in the hotel are quite comfortable, they are quite comfortable to sleep on. In some rooms, the condition of the furniture is a bit shabby, but it is kept clean. There is enough space in the cabinets and bedside tables to lay out all things.

The rooms also have a minibar. Drinks that are present in it can be used only at an additional cost. If desired, you can order food or drinks at restaurants that deliver to your room. Such a service is provided at the hotel for money.

The rooms have individual air conditioning. This is very convenient, since everyone can set the most suitable temperature for themselves. They also have a safe, telephone and TV.

Bathrooms have a shower. The more respectable rooms have the opportunity to take a bath. Toiletries are constantly replenished. Each bathroom has a hairdryer.

Regarding housekeeping of the Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * hotel, tourists leave different reviews. Most vacationers do not blame its quality even in the absence of a tip. Cleaners tidy every corner of the room daily. But, according to other tourists, in the rooms you can often stumble on dust even after the so-called wet cleaning. Towels are changed every day.

Infrastructure Features

Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) offers its guests a number of different services that are provided for free or at a certain cost. Administrators are present around the clock in the hotel lobby, so you can contact them at any time of the day. The hotel has staff who are fluent in Russian. All employees treat the guests kindly and respectfully. If you encounter any problems, hotel guests can count on a quick fix.





At the reception there is a left-luggage office, which guests of the 3 * Cozy Beach Hotel can use for free. For information on local attractions, please contact the tour desk.

Since the hotel often accommodates people who have arrived in Thailand on business, they are given the opportunity to take advantage of the conference rooms, which have everything you need to organize business meetings.

The hotel has a beauty salon, which for a fee offers a list of various services. They are doing a delicious Thai massage. There is also the opportunity to visit the sauna.

Nutrition

There are three systems to eat at the hotel. First, only breakfast will be served here. If you choose the half-board system, you will be waited for breakfast and dinner. There is also the opportunity to choose accommodation with three meals a day.

About how guests are fed at Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * (Thailand), vacationers respond differently, but in most cases the quality of the dishes is quite satisfactory. The variety of menus is also pleasing. Fruits are always present in sufficient quantities.





At the hotel you can eat at the restaurant, which offers its visitors dishes of Chinese and European cuisine. Many guests note that the food quality is excellent.

The hotel has 2 bars where you can enjoy delicious cocktails. It also offers a variety of snacks. You can eat in the local cafeterias, which are very close to the hotel. The prices here are quite reasonable. Food can also be bought from street vendors. But in this case, in order to eat, you have to go back to your room.

Entertainment

The Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * hotel offers various possibilities to spend an entertaining time. Here you can play billiards or table tennis. The hotel has a special platform for playing indoor soccer or basketball. For those who wish, a fitness room offers their services.

Many tourists really like water sports, which are easy to do for an additional fee. Everyone can surf the waves on a jet ski, try surfing or see the beauty of the underwater sea world by diving.





Entertainment programs are held at the 3 * Cozy Beach Hotel very rarely. But since the city center is nearby, you can visit local nightclubs.

You can spend exciting time on various excursions. Thailand is a country in which there are a lot of historical monuments and magnificent temples. Many tourists note in their reviews the beauty of the nearby islands. There you can admire the tropical flora or the magnificent inhabitants of the deep sea.

Beach holidays at the hotel

The beach is just 100 meters away. To get to it, you need to go down the special stairs. Despite the proximity of the sea, it is not so easy for older people to get to it, because the stairs are quite steep. In addition, many vacationers claim that there is a lot of garbage around it in the tropical thickets, which greatly spoils the view.





The beach belongs to the city, so there are always a lot of vacationers. Cleaning is not often carried out, and in the sand you can find a lot of different debris, which does not contribute to a good rest. Coloring all the shortcomings is swimming in the warm sea. Near the coast is quite small, which allows children to play enough in the water.

Towels are offered to guests at Cozy Beach Hotel 3 *. It is possible to settle on a sunbed under an umbrella only at an additional cost. You can hide from the sun under the trees growing near the beach.

What do tourists say about Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * (Pattaya)?

In general, many tourists were satisfied with their stay at the hotel. The condition of the local beach and the quality of the rooms in Cozy Wing and The Pool Wing buildings spoil the impression a bit. But still, the beauty of the area, the warm sea and the efforts of the staff will make every vacationer return home with a lot of positive impressions.
