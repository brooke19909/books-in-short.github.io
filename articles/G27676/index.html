Sunrise Beach 4 * (Cyprus, Protaras): rating, photos and reviews

Are you planning a vacation in Cyprus? Have you chosen the city of Protaras as your vacation spot? Looking for a comfortable hotel on the first coastline with excellent service and comfortable rooms? In this case, be sure to pay attention to the four-star Sunrise Beach.



Cyprus, hotel Sunrise Beach 4 *: photos and description

Sunrise Beach is located on the beach in the center of Protaras, on one of the best beaches in the country. Larnaca Airport is 55 kilometers away. Ayia Napa Resort is a ten-minute drive away.

Sunrise Beach is a comfortable and welcoming hotel that will appeal equally to family tourists with children, as well as older people and youth. He is part of the Sunrise Hotels network. In 2008, the hotel was completely renovated and equipped with modern furniture and appliances.

The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 235 comfortable rooms of the categories Standard, Superior, Family. The apartments have magnificent sea views. Some rooms have direct pool access. Regardless of the category, all apartments are spacious, pleasantly decorated and equipped with all necessary furniture and appliances of excellent quality. So, here you will find a TV with cable channels, a telephone, a safe (free), a hairdryer, a refrigerator with a minibar. Rooms feature furnished balconies.

On the territory of "Sunrise Beach" there is a large pool with a huge number of sunbeds. The hotel offers guests use of parking, hairdresser, beauty salon, conference room. On the territory there is a store with the necessary goods and souvenirs. Also, guests can visit the gym, spa, take children to a mini club, play table tennis. Throughout the hotel and on the beach there is access to free Internet.

Sunrise Beach guests can also partially use the infrastructure of neighboring hotels included in the same chain. So, for example, you can visit one of the restaurants in the buffet or √† la carte format, as well as several bars with a wide selection of drinks for every taste.

In general, with regard to meals, when booking accommodation you can pay for the option ‚ÄúHalf Board‚Äù. This will allow you to enjoy free breakfast and dinner in the hotel restaurant, which offers stunning views of the sea. If necessary, you can always change dinner for lunch.

Visiting other Sunrise Hotel chain restaurants is possible for a fee. Despite this, many guests recommend having dinner at least once in each of them. After all, here you will be offered excellent dishes with seafood, as well as dishes of Italian, Asian, Mediterranean and other cuisines of the world.



Sunrise Beach 4 * (Protaras): traveler reviews

As you know, while traveling to another country, a hotel for tourists becomes literally the second home. And even those people who consider themselves completely not picky, probably still want to live in a comfortable and clean room with freshly renovated, comfortable furniture, properly working appliances, as well as count on the hospitable and attentive attitude of the staff. This is especially important if you plan to spend a few days or even weeks at the hotel, or if you travel with children.

An important factor is the location of the hotel. Indeed, not all tourists are ready for long walks to the sea under the scorching summer sun. There are many very important factors, so the choice of a hotel for your summer vacation must be treated very carefully and responsibly. After all, this can greatly affect your travel experience.

Many experienced tourists are sure that the analysis of the reviews of former guests of a particular hotel helps to make the right choice. After all, in their comments they try to share reliable information not only about the merits, which are often already obvious, but also about the possible shortcomings that they had to deal with during their holidays. Thus, you can determine for yourself whether a particular hotel is suitable for you, or whether to continue the search.

Today we offer to find out together about the impression our compatriots had on visiting the four-star Sunrise Beach Hotel in Protaras, Cyprus.



Rating

Speaking about the opinions of tourists about this or that hotel, it makes sense to mention the assessment based on their comments. So, the rating of the Sunrise Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras) under consideration is quite high and is 4.6 points out of the maximum five. This state of affairs indicates that most hotel guests were not completely disappointed in their choice of accommodation in Cyprus.

Moreover, almost all the guests highly appreciated the comfort of the rooms, the quality of the services provided, the location and infrastructure of the hotel. In their opinion, Sunrise Beach is a great combination of price and quality. Well, now we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the opinions of our compatriots about rest in this hotel.



Location

Judging by the reviews of tourists from Russia and other countries of the former Soviet Union, most of them were very pleased with the location of Sunrise Beach 4 * (Cyprus). The hotel is located in the very center of Protaras, so in its vicinity there are lots of various entertainment and shopping establishments, as well as, of course, cafes and restaurants. Guests will always find how to spend time. In addition, travelers are pleased to note the fact that Sunrise Beach is literally a few meters from the public beach. Therefore, guests can walk from the room to the sea in just a couple of minutes, which is very convenient for vacationers with small children, for senior citizens, as well as for people with disabilities.



Hotel Territory

Tourists who left 4 * reviews about Sunrise Beach note that this hotel has a rather large green and very well-groomed territory. There is a large pool with comfortable sun loungers and parasols. A big plus is the fact that guests can sit on the hotel sunbeds and go swimming in the beach, for which you just need to cross the promenade. Thus, you do not have to spend money on renting sun loungers on the beach. An indisputable advantage is also the travelers call the opportunity to use part of the infrastructure of neighboring hotels included in the same Sunrise chain. So, for example, many parents enjoyed visiting the kids club at the five-star Pearl Hotel.



Housing stock

As for the rooms of Sunrise Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras), they are, according to the guests, very spacious and pleasantly decorated. Our compatriots claim that everything looks quite decent for a four-star hotel. The rooms are very comfortable, with high-quality comfortable furniture, properly working modern equipment.

A big plus, many tourists considered the availability of a free safe in each room. The spacious balconies have a table, chairs and a clothes dryer, which is very convenient. Guests pay attention that the rooms have a paid minibar located in the refrigerator. Its contents are checked every day by hotel employees. However, you can always put in the fridge the drinks and food you bought. The main thing is not to extract the contents of the minibar, if you do not intend to pay for it.

As for some details, tourists in their reviews mentioned that the TVs in the rooms do not have a USB input. Therefore, if you planned to watch movies or programs brought on a USB flash drive, this will not work.

As for the bathroom, here, according to vacationers, there is everything you need. The plumbing itself is in excellent condition. No complaints about problems in her work were noted, there were no interruptions in water. However, if something suddenly fails you, after contacting the reception the breakdown will be fixed as soon as possible.



Service and staff

According to our compatriots, the Sunrise Beach 4 * hotel (Protaras, Cyprus) can rightfully be proud of its employees. The staff here are very friendly, attentive, helpful. You will always be listened to and will do everything possible to solve any problem. In addition, our compatriots were very pleased with the fact that many hotel workers speak Russian. So, you will definitely not have any difficulties in communicating here.

As for housekeeping, it, according to former guests of Sunrise Beach, is carried out every day. Moreover, the maids work very efficiently, not limited to rubbing dust in prominent places. Bed linen at the hotel is changed every three days. Also, as necessary, maids will replenish hygiene supplies (shampoos, gels, toilet paper, shower cap, etc.) and update towels.



Infrastructure

Since today the Internet is very firmly entrenched in the life of almost every person, many tourists are interested in the question of the availability of the hotel, where they plan to stay on vacation, Wi-Fi. After all, this will allow you to always stay in touch with your family and friends who have remained at home, and also, if necessary, to solve work issues or have fun. I must say that Sunrise Beach will not disappoint its guests in this regard. After all, according to compatriots who have been here, the entire territory of the hotel (including rooms) perfectly catches wireless Internet. In addition, Wi-Fi from the hotel can be used without problems on the beach.

Nutrition

When booking a room in Sunrise Beach, many tourists choose the Half Board option. It includes breakfast and dinner included in the price. A big plus is the fact that you can always change dinner for breakfast if you wish (for example, if you plan to go on an excursion in the evening).

In many reviews, you can find a mention that the hotel's restaurant offers magnificent views of the sea. So you can enjoy your meal and enjoy the azure surface. As for the choice of dishes, according to the guests, their assortment is not as great as, for example, in Turkish hotels offering buffet meals. However, all the food is cooked very high quality and delicious from the freshest products. In addition, the menu is constantly alternating, so you do not have to eat the same thing every day.

So, for example, vacationers recall that they gladly ate cheese of various kinds (Roquefort, Dor Blue, Mozzarella), sausage slices, Greek salad, shrimp salad and others, seafood, meat of different varieties (in including lamb). You can always taste delicious fresh pastries and great desserts. Tourists also recall a large selection of various fruits and vegetables. In a word, it is impossible to stay hungry here. Former hotel guests are warned that drinks are chargeable during dinner. However, their prices are quite affordable, and the quality is very decent. Tourists are advised to definitely try the local varieties of wine.

In the comments of former guests of Sunrise Beach, you can also find a mention that there is no children's menu in the hotel restaurant. But tourists who came to rest with children, without problems, got out of the situation. Young travelers enjoyed various side dishes, soups, fruits and vegetables. However, parents need to keep this situation in mind, as in most restaurants in Cyprus (not only in the hotel) meat dishes are served with a large number of different spices, which may not appeal to children.



Sea

As you know, most travelers staying in Cyprus, expect a great beach holiday. Judging by the reviews of our compatriots, the four-star Sunrise Beach Hotel will not disappoint its guests in this regard. As already mentioned, the hotel is located on the seashore. Only a promenade separates it from the excellent public beach. Moreover, the hotel has a large number of comfortable sun loungers, so guests do not need to pay extra for the rental of sunbeds on the beach. By the way, upon arrival at Sunrise Beach 4 * (Protaras, Cyprus), you will be secured with sun loungers and a parasol, so you will not need to take up space. As for beach towels, they are given to guests here for free. You can change them as often as you like.

As for the beaches, there are two of them near the hotel. Moreover, both of them are considered one of the best in Cyprus. In addition, they are assigned a blue flag. We are talking about the beaches of Sunrise and Fig Three Bay. The hotel is literally a dozen meters from the first. Fig Three Bay Beach is approximately 300 meters away. You can reach it by a convenient boardwalk. Near the beaches there are restaurants, cafes, playgrounds, shops and even an amusement park. The beaches themselves are very clean, with lots of sunbeds. True, their rent requires additional payment.

The sea in this place, many vacationers call truly fabulous. Experienced tourists claim that it is prettier only in Cuba or in the Dominican Republic.

Entertainment

According to the guests, most of the time, of course, they spent at sea. Here you can not only sunbathe and swim, but also engage in various water sports (for an additional fee). Also, many tourists enjoyed spending time by the pool.

Travelers accustomed to an active lifestyle did not refuse to play tennis, visit the gym, walk around the surroundings and, of course, go on various excursions, the choice of which in Cyprus is very large.
