Creative lessons: how to make a dress of leaves for an autumn ball

Traditionally, an interesting competition is held in schools, gymnasiums and lyceums - the autumn ball. And, as usual, his queen is chosen. Naturally, the contestants will have to compete for the crown: read poetry, dance, demonstrate original costumes.

Autumn Dress Technology



There are many options for how to make a dress of leaves for an autumn ball. For example, in this way. An existing outfit is taken, preferably a simple straight cut. A newspaper is carefully made for him. You can only cover the skirt with paper. Now pick up more colorful foliage. Try to pick up fresh, bright, slightly dried. In this case, your dress from the leaves for the autumn ball will turn out elegant, colorful. As for tree species, of course, first of all, use maple and aspen leaves. They fully convey the festive atmosphere of this time of year. Yes, and look very textured, spectacular. Linden is distinguished by delicate lemon yellowness; birch leaves sparkle with real gold coins. Do not neglect brown shades, they will come in handy. At the autumn ball, it is customary to decorate yourself. Instead of beads and earrings, other jewelry, also use improvised materials: pick up clusters of mountain ash, viburnum, twigs with rosehip berries and berries separately. From all this wealth you will make magnificent jewelry corresponding to the high status of the Queen. When the preparation is finished, you can begin to build your dress from leaves for the autumn ball!

From student to queen



Let's start with the bodice. After choosing small birch and willow leaves, wash them on the chest, sleeves. Follow an invented pattern or do it in random order. Do not overdo it, because you will wear beads on the neck - mountain ash or rose hips. One of the main principles of how to make a dress of leaves with a taste that is not lurid is to observe a sense of proportion. But it is quite acceptable, except for beads, to pin a small rowan bunch onto the chest. And twist more twigs into the hair, stick it into the hairdo. Now a skirt. She will have to work harder. What dresses from leaves can turn out, photos will prompt you. Glue the leaves densely on a paper base, you can even impose one on the other.

Let the edges of the leaves remain free, then they will look more natural. Since you are going to become the queen of the ball, you can sometimes spray your outfit from the spray can with car paint - bronze or golden. In this case, you sparkle in the full sense of the word! Having fixed the main background in this way, proceed to the decor. For accessories, again take the berries, pieces of moss, glue them in a picturesque mess. Adjust the autumn flowers in some places . For these purposes, asters are primarily suitable - they well retain a fresh look without water for a long time.

Having finished work, carefully examine the result, correct the shortcomings, dress up and boldly go to the ball: you are guaranteed a victory!
