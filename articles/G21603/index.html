Hotel Bamboo Village Beach Resort & SPA 4 *, Vietnam: overview, description, features and reviews

Phan Thiet is one of the resorts that deserves attention. Not so long ago, Vietnam became a tourist center of Southeast Asia, its resorts experienced hard times until the early 2000s. Today it is not.

Why do tourists go there?

The nature of Vietnam, its climate and the cleanliness of the coastline attract many travelers. Most tourists come to the country from China. Every year, the growth of visitors to Vietnam amounted to hundreds of thousands of people.

In addition to a beach holiday, tourists come to study the history of the country and attractions. Traveling to Vietnam is relatively safe and affordable. In the 2000s, the country's infrastructure improved, including hotel complexes. One of them is Bamboo Village Beach Resort & SPA 4 * (Phan Thiet).

Hotel Description

Phan Thiet Resort is a city in southern Vietnam. A large number of hotels of different price categories are concentrated here.





Hotel Bamboo Village Beach Resort & SPA 4 * is located in the center of resort life, in an area called Mui Ne. This is a place of tranquility on the shores of the South China Sea.

The complex itself is located on the territory of a large tropical garden in the shade of coconut palms and among the abundance of flowering plants. Everything here has a pleasant and long rest.

The number of rooms at Bamboo Village Beach Resort & SPA includes bungalows and rooms decorated in Vietnamese style, a total of 147 pieces.

The hotel complex is a laureate of various prizes both domestically and abroad. The hotel has beautiful swimming pools, a SPA center, a meditation room.

Services

Children at the hotel

What about those tourists who came to rest at the Bamboo Village Beach Resort & SPA with their children? Would little travelers be bored? These questions always worry parents.

Of course, the most interesting thing for children will be at the water. This applies to both the pool area and the beach. Keeping track of the kids is a must. The Phan Thiet Sea is often troubled.

Those who need to leave their babies for a short time are provided with additional babysitting services. Also, children will be interested in a special outdoor play area.

Beach

This is the place where vacationers from all over the world strive. The beach in Mui Ne is not sandy and quite clean. The hotel staff ensures that vacationers have enough umbrellas and sunbeds.





The sand on the beach is grayish, shallow, the sea is clean. As a rule, in Phan Thiet, winds are frequent. This is due to geographical location. Relaxing on the beach by the hotel is a real pleasure. You can enjoy a cocktail at the bar or take a sunbath. Also here all the conditions are created for surfing.

People often come to the beach to meet the sunrises and see off the sun. The view here is very beautiful!

Transport issues

You can rent a bicycle or car to get around. It all depends on the duration and distance of the trip. Transport rental is available directly at Bamboo Village Beach Resort & SPA.

Usually, having arrived at this resort, a tourist does not immediately decide to rent a car. The fact is that the road from the airport to the hotel is quite long. However, the craving for adventure is sometimes overpowering. You should not go on long trips, but you can see the nearest attractions.

Resort Attractions

Attractions attract most tourists. There are a lot of them in Phan Thiet. Particular attention should be paid to traveling to Mount Taku, where you can see the statue of the reclining Buddha. The length of the monument is 49 meters. This is the largest Buddha statue in all of Vietnam. It is located on a mountain, where you have to climb.

Another memorable trip could be the road to the sand dunes and to the island of Rum. After exploring the white dunes, the guides definitely recommend to go to the lotus lake. These magnificent flowers bloom here all year round and are pleasing to the eye.

Those who want to take pictures in a picturesque place should definitely visit the fishing village. There are a lot of beautiful boats that have become a symbol of this place.

Red Canyon is a visiting card of the region. Travelers are also brought here. This is a very beautiful place, which was formed by the merger of sand of various colors.





On the outskirts of the city, an ancient structure - Cham towers - has been preserved . For those who profess Hinduism, these objects are cult.

Another tour is a review of the oldest lighthouse in Southeast Asia called Kega. It is only forty kilometers away, and here you can also take a picture against the backdrop of an amazing bay.

Tour programs can be booked at the hotel‚Äôs tour desk or in outdoor tents. It is completely safe and often costs slightly less. When traveling, dress appropriately. In temples it is required to cover your knees and shoulders, sports shoes will be relevant in the dunes and on the canyon.
