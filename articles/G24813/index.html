Phuket hotel Tune Hotel Pattaya 3 *: location, number of rooms, photos and reviews of tourists

The Tune Hotel Pattaya 3 * hotel can be recommended to tourists who want an interesting and inexpensive vacation in exotic Thailand.

It is located in one of the best resort cities of the southeast coast of the Gulf of Thailand - Pattaya. This is a world-recognized tranquil holiday destination for couples and families with children. In addition to a good-quality hotel class economy class, vacationers are expected to recover on the shores of the azure South China Sea. They offer delightful sandy beaches, a rich underwater world, picturesque mountains, lush greenery of the jungle, a wide range of local fruits.

Thailand, a paradise of the earth, famous for its fertile subtropical climate, softened by monsoons, is a unique place on Earth where summer reigns all year round and several crops ripen here a year. You can relax here year-round, but still the period from November to February (the so-called cool period) is considered the best time for tourists to visit the country.

Description of the hotel in Pattaya

The hotel complex itself is located in the second line, not far from the beach. The distance from it to the entertainment and shopping districts is about 4 km. The comfort and friendliness of the staff is remembered by its guests Tune Hotel Pattaya 3 *. Reviews of 95% of guests about it are positive! Customers like that when they check into the room they are not required to make a deposit.

Basic economy class services are provided to guests: comfortable accommodation, a beach and one-time meals (breakfast). The rooms are small, such as standard. They do not open with a key, but with a chip card. Smoking is prohibited. The smoking area is equipped in the lobby; those who violate this procedure must pay a fine.

There is no balcony in the room, but the bathroom pleases with its impressive size and the set of ergonomic plumbing and hygiene products present in it. The latter are represented by shampoo, conditioner, shower gel, toothpaste. This equipment is carefully maintained by the staff.

Services - For guests

Present in the room appliances are new, in good condition. According to guest reviews, TV, air conditioning, hairdryer, telephone work in the best way. Each room also has a free safe. Wi-Fi is remarkably functioning on the territory of the hotel complex, which allows you to watch online movies and even play computer online games. Especially for guests right in the lobby are three free personal computers.



There is also a minus: as in most Thai economies, there is no refrigerator or minibar in the rooms of the Tune Hotel Pattaya 3 *. Reviews of hotel guests about food in the hotel indicate its minimalism. Hotel cafe TOM & TOMS treats guests only with classic breakfasts. The food is light and nutritious: scrambled eggs, sausages, sausages, bacon, salads, granola. Lunch and dinner are organized by guests on their own, at their discretion. Shops with fixed prices are attractive with a wide selection of food for guests of the Tune Hotel Pattaya 3 * hotel. Pattaya, as you know, is not the cheapest place in Thailand to buy European food at commercial prices.

How to organize your meals for hotel guests

As we already mentioned, the hotel only partially solves the problem of food for its guests. Buying food for lunch and dinner is the concern of the tourists themselves. Where to go for groceries? The necessary shops are nearby, a 5-7 minute walk. The best (according to resorts reviews) price / quality ratio is offered by Makro store. The Big C store provides the largest assortment of products . Ready-made food, which is enough to reheat, is sold at the Tesco store. Some tourists buy food there.

However, vacationers who have rejected prejudices about exotic Asian cuisine, supposedly not suitable for residents of Western countries, are in the most advantageous position. A good option for guests of the Tune Hotel 3 *, following the example of the Thais themselves, is to eat in local street cafes called "macaques." There are many of them in Pattaya. The most popular food that holidaymakers order at the macaque is chicken skewers, sausages, octopuses, sprinkled with sweet and sour sauce. The price of one barbecue (in terms of) is 15 rubles.

By the way, according to reviews of tourists - guests of the Tune Hotel, there have been no cases of poisoning among these visiting lovers of such street food. On the contrary, holidaymakers write with satisfaction about their tasting of one or another national dish.

Thai Recommended Europeans
