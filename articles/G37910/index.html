The best numbers

Indeed, each room of the Villa Nina Hotel (Lazarevskoye) is gorgeous and comfortable. The apartments amaze the eyes of an experienced tourist. It is here that you can relax carefree, enjoy the picturesque views of the sea coast, the green nature of the village. And today it is the only hotel that does not charge for sunbathing in the hot summer.

The hotel rooms have everything you need to live and enjoy your vacation: TV, telephone, shower and toilet, Internet and satellite TV. Cleaning the rooms is carried out at the request of customers. Linen change - every three days or on demand.

The administration offers tourists and guests such rooms: deluxe, junior suite, standard. Luxury apartments come in two types: one-bedroom and a suite with kitchenette.





"Standard" by standard

Most often, the Standard room is one of the most popular among tourists. It has everything that a client needs for a comfortable stay. It has 2 beds (double and single), air conditioning, small refrigerator, hairdryer. Of course, there are several sets of bed linen, a set of towels.

Suite and Junior Suite

As for the Junior Suite, the possibilities for a comfortable stay of guests are significantly improved. The same set is provided for customers as for the standard room, but bath accessories, shoe polishing sponges, and napkins are added. Junior Suite Room - larger, more modern and comfortable.

Few customers can afford a Suite. Hotel Villa Nina (Lazarevskoye) offers customers only the best Luxe rooms in several variations.

For example, the corner Suite room is designed for 2 people (1 bed), there is a sofa, necessary equipment and furniture, bath accessories, several bathrobes, slippers, a set of bath towels and one foot towel. The same suite has a suite with a kitchen.

No less luxurious is the two-room Suite. It has 2 beds, furniture and appliances, 4 large bath towels, 4 small ones, one towel for washing feet, 4 bathrobes (according to season), 4 pairs of slippers.

Each room is designed in such a way that it is possible to equip an extra bed. 2 berths can be arranged only in the Standard room, as the area allows it. Children under 14 years old - berth for free.





A few words about nutrition

Until 22:00, each client of the hotel complex can have a tasty and inexpensive meal in the cafe-dining room. On request, you can order meals directly to your room. Near the hotel there are various places where you can also have lunch.

Additional hotel services

The hotel administration, at the request of the client, can meet at the railway station (farewell) "Lazarevskoye" on a free basis. From other places, meeting and seeing off the client to the hotel are paid.

Room service is available around the clock. If tourists settle with the children, for their convenience, the hotel has a nanny.

In addition, customers can take advantage of laundry services, a sauna with a pool. For their convenience, there is a parking lot. If you wish, you can include excursions in your leisure program, ride ATVs, organize a picnic in nature, take a walk around the village.

How to book a hotel room

In order to book a room at the Villa Nina Hotel (Lazarevskoye), 20% of its cost must be paid. If payment is not made 30 days prior to check-in, the reservation is automatically withdrawn. You need to pay the rest of the room before 12 noon, check-in starts at 13:30, check-out is until 12 noon. In case of cancellation of the reservation 10 days before the appointed settlement, the prepayment for the room will not be refunded by the hotel. From 15 days the deposited funds are returned (50% of the amount). The price per room largely depends on the season of settlement (in the summer, the cost of a room is slightly more expensive than in other seasons). If you prematurely book a room, you can save on payment.
