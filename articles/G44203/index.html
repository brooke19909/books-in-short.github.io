A little bit about the company

Alpine Electronics is a Japanese company that has been producing audio devices for cars since 1967. During this time there was not a single criticism in terms of quality for the products of this manufacturer. The main industry is head units (car radios). Players from the Japanese - it's just a storehouse of new technologies and nice "chips" for car enthusiasts. At one time, it was this company that developed the first remote control radio for mounting on the steering wheel. Alpine CDE-9880R continues the tradition of the legendary Japanese manufacturer and boasts quality and advanced technology.





The only trouble is that the products of this company have not gained immense popularity in Russia and the CIS countries. Affects the high cost of the final product. But high quality is worth it. In Russia there is only one official distributor of Alpin equipment. And this is not very good, because, in addition to car radios, the company produces acoustic systems, LCD screens, amplifiers, receivers, DVD players (for cars), navigators and much more. Specifications

Alpine CDE-9880R has the following specifications. The frequency range is from 5 to 20,000 Hertz. This is a very decent result for the car radio. Rated power - 50 watts. That is, the radio tape recorder can be completely used without an amplifier. The number of channels - 4. The amplifier mode is stereo. There are separate inputs for connecting the receiver, subwoofer and DVD player. Auto Connectors - Standard DIN. This makes it compatible. But due to the dimensions of the device, it will be necessary to expand the port for the radio on some cars (especially domestic). The signal-to-noise ratio is 105 decibels. This is a good result.





CD player specifications are standard for such devices. He can read audio formats MP3, WMA, AAC. Readable Disc Standards - CD-R, CD-RW. Nothing supernatural. For the money, you could already add the ability to read DVDs. But this is just the Alpine CDE-9880R. Separate tuning of each channel also takes place.
