Viking Club Hotel 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt): hotel description and reviews of tourists

Viking club

Located on the second line from the sea, this hotel is a complex of fifteen two-story snow-white cottages built in the style of a traditional village. It was built in 2007. The last major overhaul was carried out two years ago.

The hotel is positioned as a good place for young people who are not particularly demanding on comfort and can spend their leisure time outside the territory, in a city where there are many entertainment venues.

Territory

Built across the highway from the world famous Naama Bay, Viking Club Hotel 4 * is located ten minutes from the airport. This feature is noted in many reviews as one of the pluses, as those who come here with young children who have poor road tolerance get to the hotel very quickly and check in.

The territory of the complex is small. It is fully landscaped. Exotic tropical plants, many date palms, alluring sparkling with their emerald foliage, have a pleasant pastime in the afternoon. And in the evenings, vacationers can stroll along the promenade, which is only five hundred meters away.

Infrastructure

Viking Club Hotel 4 *, reviews of which are mostly positive, offers residents a very wide range of services. The 24-hour reception desk has the ability to arrange a transfer, call a taxi, rent a vehicle and the necessary equipment for diving, rent a depository safe. Here you need to agree in advance about a bus taking to one of the two beaches.

On the territory of the Viking Club Hotel 4 * there are two medium pools, one of which is heated. There are many free sun loungers and umbrellas around. There is also a bar.

For children

For kids, the Viking Club 4 * hotel offers an outdoor playground and a room where little guests can engage. In addition, they have a special menu in the restaurant and a high stool for feeding. Parents can request an extra bed and bedding for babies upon request.

Entertainment

Management at Viking Club Hotel 4 * is aimed at making both children and adults comfortable in it. There is a good animation, as evidenced by the reviews. In the evenings, adults, twice a week organize shows, discos and theme nights.

Viking Club 4 *: reviews

Many of those who have stayed at this hotel complex consider it appropriate for the declared category. The rooms in it are spacious and bright, equipped with basic necessities. The staff cleans them unobtrusively, quickly solving all the problems that arise with guests. The hotel area, although not very large, but well-groomed, completely landscaped.

Especially a lot of positive reviews about the possibilities for diving. The sea is calm and always clear, and the underwater world is so beautiful that it is amazing. Many tourists enthusiastically respond to the good nature and benevolence of the staff, combined with a cozy atmosphere.

However, one review is the same: the cost of living at this hotel is very affordable.
