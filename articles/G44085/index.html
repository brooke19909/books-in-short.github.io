Sylva Hotel 2 (Cyprus / Limassol): photos and reviews of tourists

The island of Cyprus for many years has been one of the most popular holiday destinations for Russian tourists. Thanks to the mild Mediterranean climate, the holiday season lasts almost all year round. Local resorts are known for a decent level of service: a good rest in Cyprus is possible even in inexpensive two- or three-star hotels. In addition, tourists who are focused on beach holidays and sightseeing, most often only need a place for an overnight stay and luggage storage.

Traveler reviews of cheap Cretan hotels can be found on almost any travel site. Sylva Hotel 2 *, located in Limassol, the second largest Cretan resort, collected a lot of positive characteristics. What attracts tourists to this Cretan hotel? What living conditions can Sylva Hotel 2 * offer (Cyprus)? Photos of the hotel and rooms, see below in the article.





Hotel info

Sylva Hotel 2 * has been welcoming guests since 2009. The hotel is known for its good location: the building was built almost in the city center, and the beach, a tourist area with restaurants and shops, and the most popular objects of city excursions are within walking distance.

The main "calling card" of the hotel is the original design of ethnic-style rooms. The territory is fenced, very cozy and picturesque, a lot of greenery and flowers.

Infrastructure

Hotel Sylva Hotel 2 * is a six-story building with 100 hotel rooms in a small equipped area. In addition, the hotel infrastructure includes the following facilities:

  • A restaurant.
  • Lobby bar.
  • Pool and plunge pool.
  • Currency exchange point.
  • Safe (at the reception).
  • Car parking, car and bicycle rental.
  • Laundry.
  • Conference hall.
  • Elevator.
  • Tour agency.
  • Television lounge and leisure room: table tennis, billiards.
  • Outdoor terrace.

High-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.





Beach

The hotel is built on the second coastline. There is no separate hotel beach, but the nearest free beach can be reached on foot in just 5 minutes - the distance to the sea is only 300 m. From most tourists staying at Sylva Hotel 2 *, reviews about local beaches are very positive. Many travelers, first vacationing in Limassol, are surprised by the unusual color of sand on the beach: instead of white or yellow, sea sand due to the high content of silicon is a grayish tint. In addition, tourists often tell the following about local beaches:

  • The beaches and sea water in Limassol and the surrounding cities are clean.
  • All beach equipment is paid, but renting it for recreation is not necessary. Many tourists enjoyed relaxing on the sand in the shade of trees.
  • Sunset at sea is gentle and safe. Near the shore, the depth is shallow.
  • Water activities and sports are available on the beach. You can use the services of instructors from the diving school, try your hand at windsurfing, water skiing, canoeing and more.

The conclusion that can be drawn by studying the reviews of former guests of Sylva Hotel 2 *: Limassol is an ideal place for a beach holiday for the whole family. In the reviews there are no complaints of cramped beaches, lack of sunbeds, obsessive merchants and other things that can spoil the impression of the resort.





Paid services

In any hotel, there are a number of services designed to provide additional comfort to guests, but not included in the price. What services can I get at Sylva Hotel 2 * for an additional fee?

  • Meals in the hotel restaurant (included at the request of the guest in payment for accommodation when booking).
  • Mini fridge.
  • Safe, minibar.
  • Transport rental and organization of sightseeing trips.
  • Hair dryer (at reception).
  • High speed internet access.
  • Massage room.
  • Access to satellite television.
  • Babysitting service.

About staff

The main staff of Sylva Hotel 2 * are employees from India and Bangladesh. There are Russian employees, for example, a clerk at the reception. In addition to Greek, the staff speaks English, Russian, Italian.

Among the positive qualities of employees from India or local residents, as a rule, they call courtesy, willingness to patiently listen to the guest and understand the problem, courtesy, smile and friendliness. At the same time, there are often complaints of unhurriedness, slowness, and a somewhat detached attitude to one's duties. In particular, the cleaning of the room is not carried out very carefully, but the request of customers to correct the defects is carried out politely and with a smile.

Oddly enough, Russian-speaking tourists have, oddly enough, Russian staff. Guests express dissatisfaction with the sullen look and incompetence, although they acknowledge that having a person with "knowledge of the language" greatly simplifies life at the hotel.

Nutrition

Meals in the hotel restaurant must be ordered in advance when booking. The choice of guests are offered the system of "Breakfast" or "Half Board". Dishes are served on the basis of the "buffet". In most reviews, hotel guests indicated that they chose to pay only for breakfast. This is explained by the fact that in the city of Limassol there is a huge selection of cafes, bars and restaurants of various price categories, offering tourists the opportunity to taste traditional Greek dishes or cuisine from around the world.

About food in Sylva Hotel 2 * (Cyprus), guest reviews say almost the same thing:

  • The hotel restaurant is quite clean, no gross violations of sanitary standards.
  • Meals are served freshly prepared. For breakfast every day, the composition of the buffet changes.
  • The set of dishes is quite traditional: eggs, slices, coffee, vegetables, sometimes fruits, granola, toasts, tea and more. Some reviews call the choice of food "not rich." However, experienced guests say that it is better to arrive early for breakfast, then the choice of dishes will be quite acceptable.

Hotel guests who prefer to pay only breakfast, talk about a wide selection of cafes and restaurants near the hotel, in which the service is aimed at Russian-speaking tourists.





Vacation with children

For guests coming to rest with children, the hotel provides additional services and equipment:

  • Possibility to install a cot in the room and a baby chair in the restaurant.
  • Baby stroller rental.
  • Babysitting service.
  • Children's mini-pool.

Information on the cost of all services can be obtained at the reception. In addition, within walking distance from the hotel there are many eating establishments offering a special children's menu, and in the city there are many interesting entertainments for parents and children.

Limassol is known for its park, which is inhabited by exotic animals: from tropical birds to African elephants. The city has two water parks with water attractions for adults and children of different ages.

Excursions

A lot of attention in the reviews is given to the organization of excursion trips for hotel guests. It is no secret that there are many interesting historical sites in Crete for viewing: monuments of antiquity, medieval architecture, reminiscent of the times when Limassol was an important seaport or was under the rule of invaders.

There are several ways to explore local attractions. The easiest is to book a sightseeing tour of the main historical places of Cyprus at a travel agency on site or with any city tour operator.
