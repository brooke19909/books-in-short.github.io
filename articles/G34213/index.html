Ski resorts in Serbia. Tourists reviews

Russian tourists who prefer outdoor activities in winter are increasingly turning their eyes to friendly Serbia. It is here that for a relatively small fee you can enjoy an interesting excursion program, improve health at local resorts and, of course, enjoy plenty of skiing. In this article we will tell you what ski resorts in Serbia are worth visiting in the first place, as well as about the features of relaxation in this wonderful country.



Winter serbia

The attractiveness of this region for Russian tourists lies in the following factors: a short flight, which saves valuable vacation time, a European level of service at fairly low prices, and common Slavic roots with the Serbian people, which make you feel at home. The ski resorts in Serbia cannot be called the best in Europe, but thanks to the efforts of the tourism business, they are getting better and better every year. New tracks for skiing appear, new services and entertainment for guests are being developed. Here, beginners from Russia will feel easy and free thanks to affordable ski schools and Russian-speaking instructors.

Serbia Ski resort Kopaonik

The largest mountain range in the country is happy to welcome both beginners and experienced athletes. Beautiful landscapes, clean air, healing springs and dense forests will not leave indifferent even an experienced traveler. Typically, tourists get to these places from Belgrade by car or bus. Here you can stay in one of the many hotels, a tourist complex or in apartments. Like other ski resorts in Serbia, the winter season Kopaonik opens in November, and ends in March.



Resort Trails

Entertainment at this resort is provided for both beginners who have recently embarked on skis and experts. Here you can find slopes of any difficulty, places for night skiing, as well as ideal cross-country ski tracks. For children, their own slopes and lifts are provided here, and attentive teachers will be happy to answer all questions of the kids. By purchasing a single ski pass, athletes can ride on more than twenty tracks.

Attractions and attractions of Kopaonik

Since the guests of the resort live almost at the foot of the mountains, they do not have to spend a lot of time to get to the places of skiing. But they can afford to relax in one of the bars of the village, dine in a restaurant, go shopping or go to an incendiary party in a night club. Ski resorts in Serbia are usually located in very picturesque places, and Kopaonik is no exception to this rule. Many trails lead from the village to the mountains of Tara, Zlatibor, Gocha and Brezovitsa. Stara Planina

This highest mountain in eastern Serbia has earned the title of one of the best resorts in the country due to the pristine nature, beautiful landscapes, developed infrastructure and a high level of service. For five months (from November to March) lovers of outdoor activities from many countries flock here. Despite the fact that Stara Planina is a fairly young resort, here you can find many slopes of varying complexity, a night skiing track, a ski school and even a kindergarten. Like many ski resorts in Serbia, Stara Planina is designed for families with children. Therefore, here you will find an interesting excursion program, a lot of entertainment for all ages, the opportunity to improve skiing skills, as well as an easy and enjoyable vacation from everyday worries.



Holidays in Stara Planina

This ski resort is located on the border between Bulgaria and Serbia. For tourists, the region is interesting for its rich culture and national traditions. Folk crafts flourish here, and everyone can bring home wonderful souvenirs. Local restaurants surprise guests with hospitality, low prices and large serving sizes. History buffs can visit the attractions located around the resort. Ancient monasteries, cathedrals and archaeological sites will not leave indifferent any connoisseur of beauty.



Tourists reviews

Only positive impressions from travelers who visited the ski resorts of Serbia. Reviews of Russian tourists are full of vivid impressions and positive emotions. Most vacationers agree that local routes are not too suitable for professionals. But for average skiers, children and beginner athletes, local conditions are perfectly suited. Those who prefer a more relaxing holiday will find many hiking and cross-country skiing trails. Fans of mountaineering, hunting and winter fishing will also not be left without work.
