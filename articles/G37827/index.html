Positive reviews

  • good nutrition (although the range of dishes is small);
  • maids conscientiously fulfill their duties (they clean even on balconies, which can not be found in all hotels);
  • if there are wet towels in the bathroom (even if you did not put them on the floor), they must be replaced with dry ones;
  • the territory, although small, but very beautiful and well-groomed, there is where to walk, relax and take pictures;
  • quick registration and check-in procedure (this will take you no more than five to ten minutes);
  • if the hotel has enough free rooms, no one will force you to wait for the checkout time, but will check in immediately;
  • the buffet menu has a huge amount of fresh vegetables and fruits locally produced;
  • the waiters are very smart, polite and do their best to please the guests, patiently respond to all requests and comments;
  • employees work in good faith, regardless of whether you tip them (no one even hints at a reward);
  • clean warm sea;
  • garbage is constantly removed on the beach;

Negative reviews

Not everything is perfect in the Titan Select Hotel resort . Reviews will help you navigate the shortcomings of the institution and prepare yourself in advance for possible unpleasant moments:

  • music is rarely played in catering establishments, and therefore it is boring;
  • there are a lot of sharp stones on the beach, and slippery slabs at the entrance to the water, which is especially inconvenient for those who rest with children;
  • the hammam is far from the present (there is not enough high temperature, not very beautiful design and rather dirty);
  • spa managers are too intrusive to offer guests services, which is rather unpleasant;
  • the bedding is old, all in small holes and unwashed stains;
  • on the official website it is indicated that the beach is 200-250 meters, but in fact this distance is about 600 meters;
  • after 9 o‚Äôclock in the morning it is impossible to find a free sunbed on the beach, and all because there are much fewer places than guests at the hotel;
  • ice cream is not included in the All Inclusive concept; it is available only at an additional cost;
  • tasteless local wine in bars and restaurants;
  • it is felt that the owners of the hotel are trying to save on everything (both on repairs, and on equipment of rooms, and on food, and on the number of staff);
  • Wireless Internet practically does not work (there are only chances to "catch" a signal at the reception and near the pool);
  • sun loungers on the beach do not wipe, umbrellas are also very dirty;
  • old leaky beach towels (if possible it is better to take your own from home);
  • there are no coolers or refrigerators with drinking water anywhere in the territory, which is unacceptable for a five-star hotel);
  • the bathroom often has the smell of sewage, as well as damp enough;
  • upon arrival at the hotel, it may turn out that your rooms are busy, and therefore you will have to spend a few days or the whole vacation in another room;

  • when you leave the room, all devices are turned off (including air conditioning);

  • drinks in the bar are poured into plastic glasses, which is unacceptable for a hotel of such a high category;
  • there is no children's menu in the dining room;
  • there is no hygienic makeup in the bathroom;
  • there is no tea set, you have to constantly go down to the bar for hot drinks;
  • There is a busy highway next to the hotel, which makes a lot of noise and dust;
  • the windows and balcony doors in the rooms do not close very tightly, which is why noise is heard from the street;
  • the buffet menu has very few meat dishes (mostly a bird);
  • the staff practically does not speak Russian (the language barrier is especially noticeable during communication with the waiters);
  • the restaurant lacks cutlery, and therefore guests often have to eat with plastic spoons and forks;
  • the beach is always very crowded, as it belongs to two hotels of the chain;
  • due to poor sound insulation in the room you can hear everything that happens through the wall.

General impression

After examining the reviews of travelers, we can confidently say that this hotel does not correspond to five-star status. In terms of service, this is a maximum of four. Although, if it is sensible to estimate the price for a tour to this resort complex, full compliance with the level of service is noted. The hotel is suitable for lovers of a quiet and relaxing holiday, as well as active tourists who prefer to spend all their free time on the beach and on excursions.
