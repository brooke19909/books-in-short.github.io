Hotel Complex "Golden Wing", Novokuznetsk: address, description, reviews

The Zolotoy Kryol hotel complex in Novokuznetsk invites you to take a break from city noise and have a good time in comfortable rooms of residents and guests of the city. This is a small hotel located near the central part of the city. And although the highways are very close, homely rooms will not leave anyone indifferent.

Description

Looking at the hotels of Novokuznetsk, pay attention to the Golden Wing hotel. All rooms of the complex have their own design solution. Each room has a work desk, so you can combine your rest with the solution of current working moments. The hotel has a restaurant with excellent cuisine and a cafe-bar. Hotel guests can order food and drinks in their rooms. On the ground floor there are three saunas, where guests of the hotel, as well as citizens can relax and improve their health. In the courtyard of the hotel in the summer, vacationers have the opportunity to swim in the large outdoor heated pool with a relaxation area. The "Golden Wing" is being monitored around the clock, security guards are on duty. The entire perimeter of the building is equipped with video cameras, in emergency cases you can use the panic button. The hotel consists of two buildings. The main building of the Golden Wing hotel complex in Novokuznetsk is located at 38 Bugareva Street. The mini-hotel is located on 51 Lenin Street.

Accommodation for guests in the main building

The rooms in the Golden Wing hotel complex on Bugareva Street occupy the second and third floors and are presented in several categories.

  • Standard room (one or two people can stay) - the cost per day is 1200 rubles.
  • Standard room - for 1500 rubles per day.
  • Junior Suite (there is one and two rooms) - the price for one night is 1900 rubles.
  • Room "Cedar" (the room has an infrared sauna) - the cost is 1900 rubles per day.
  • Accommodation in a room with a hanging bed will cost guests 2500 rubles.
  • The cost of deluxe rooms with a jacuzzi bath is 2500 and 3000 rubles per day.
  • Room with its own sauna and jacuzzi, designed for four people, will cost guests 3100 rubles.
  • Room with a jacuzzi and a sauna for six people costs 4000 rubles per day.
  • Three-room deluxe room - the price per night is 4500 rubles.

A honeymoon room is also provided. I would like to draw the attention of potential guests of the Golden Wing hotel in Novokuznetsk to the fact that the cost of living here is calculated not only for a day, but also for 12 hours, 6, 3, and 1. If you had to delay later than the estimated time, then not at all be sure to pay another day. In this hotel it is enough to pay extra for each subsequent hour.

Accommodation at the mini-hotel

In a building on Lenin Street, guests can stay in five comfortable rooms.

  • Single room - its cost is 1900 rubles per day.
  • Double one-room number - its price is 2250 rubles per night.
  • Two-room number - the cost is 2250 rubles per day.
  • Two deluxe rooms with a jacuzzi bath - accommodation in such a room will cost 3,500 rubles per day.

The fee for staying in a mini-hotel also depends on the number of hours spent in the room.

Hotel complex in Novokuznetsk "Golden Wing". Room Amenities

The hotel offers its guests all the conditions for a comfortable stay. Each room has the following amenities:

  • flat screen tv;
  • telephone;
  • refrigerator;
  • mini bar;
  • a bathroom;
  • free wireless internet.

As a tribute to modern fashion, there is a room with a bathroom, which is separated from the main room by a transparent glass wall. In the deluxe room, the jacuzzi and the living area are not separated.

Additional services

For the convenience of guests in the Novokuznetsk "Golden Wing" provides a number of services.

  • Hotel visitors can park their cars in a secure parking lot.
  • For entertainment, a large billiard room is equipped for guests. In addition to a billiard table with a quality coating, here you will find the necessary accessories, dining areas, a large plasma-screen TV. Meals and drinks can be ordered directly from the hotel bar.
  • There are three saunas in the building on Bugareva Street and one on Lenin Street.
  • Spacious and comfortable banquet room for 45 people. Everything is provided in it for holding family celebrations, business lunches, office parties, weddings. For newlyweds, after a wedding banquet, the room is provided free of charge as a gift.
  • In summer, guests can swim in the outdoor pool.
  • The restaurant will delight guests with delicious dishes of Russian and European cuisine. Business lunches are available.
  • When ordering a taxi from the hotel, customers receive a discount.

Saunas

In the saunas of Novokuznetsk hotels on Bugarev and Lenin, you can relax with your family, with friends, and also hold a confidential meeting.

  • The infrared sauna in the cedar room is especially useful for both children and adults in the cold season. Thanks to volatile, which stand out with wooden walls, the air in the room becomes the same as in the cedar forest. In addition to the bactericidal properties of volatile, cedar room helps relieve stress and nervous tension.
  • Sauna No. 1, designed for 15 people, has two lounges, a steam room and two pools, a large relaxation area. One of the pools includes heating, a geyser and air massage.
  • Sauna No. 2 is designed for ten people, consists of two illuminated pools, two lounges, a steam room and a large dining area.
  • Sauna No. 3 or VIP-sauna is also designed for ten people and consists of a large swimming pool, steam room, jacuzzi, two lounges, a large dining area.

Hotel in Novokuznetsk "Golden Wing". Reviews

Hotel guests leave objective reviews about their stay here that take into account both positive and negative aspects.

Among the positive aspects, guests note a pleasant attentive staff, good service in a cafe, the presence of disposable cosmetic accessories.

Most guests agree that the price of hotel accommodation is affordable.

Guests also enjoy the varied design of their rooms.

Of the negative comments, I would like to note not always the good work of WI-FI, poor sound insulation.

Some guests complained about the dampness in the rooms.

Also among the shortcomings, guests consider the inability to eat in a cafe during a banquet.
