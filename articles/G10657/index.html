Hotel Horizon Beach Resort 3 *, Goa: overview, description, features and reviews

If you have long dreamed of getting to know the colorful and distinctive culture, as well as enjoying the natural beauties of North Goa, then you should definitely go to the Horizon Beach Resort 3 * hotel.





Hotel info

Hotel Horizon Beach Resort 3 * is located in the central part of a city such as Calangute, where all the main objects of active and fiery resort life are concentrated. Nearby is a large nightclub, as well as the local bazaar where you can very cheaply buy original souvenirs in memory of India. It is important that the hotel is located near the beach.

The rooms of the Horizon Beach Resort 3 * hotel, although modest, are quite comfortable, and their interior is made in accordance with Indian traditions. The hotel also has its own swimming pool and massage parlor, where you can completely relax and restore vital energy.

Hotel Horizon Beach Resort 3 * has a developed infrastructure, so guests will not have to be bored. You can get acquainted with local culinary specialties in the restaurant, and you can book a sightseeing tour at the tour desk.





Guest rooms

Guests at Horizon Beach Resort 3 * (India) are invited to stay in one of the 50 rooms, each of which belongs to the standard category. One part of the rooms is equipped with a large double bed, and the other a pair of single.

Each room has all the amenities that will allow guests to feel the comfort of home. So, in the rooms there are:

  • air conditioning for individual regulation of air temperature in the room;
  • TV connected to the cable network (there is one Russian channel);
  • in each number there is a telephone by which you can contact the administration at any time of the day or make an international call;
  • a small balcony that overlooks the pool or the city street;
  • small fridge ;
  • a set including an electric kettle, dishes, as well as coffee and tea;
  • safe with electronic combination lock;
  • upon request, you will be provided with iron and ironing board (in the order of numbers).




Infrastructure and service

The guests of the Horizon Beach Resort 3 * (Calangute) Resort are provided with the following options for living and recreation:

  • green garden with exotic flowers and trees for walks;
  • a clean outdoor pool with fresh water, around which there are many sun loungers and umbrellas to protect from the sun;
  • parking area with video surveillance and 24-hour security;
  • Reception, which is open around the clock to receive guests, as well as solving problems and disputes arising during a stay at the hotel;
  • a beauty salon where you can get hairdressing services and undergo traditional local beauty treatments;
  • you can wash and iron things using the laundry service;
  • wireless Internet;
  • medical assistance is available if necessary.

Helpful information

Like any other tourist hotel, the Horizon Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Goa) has its own internal rules that govern the stay and rest of guests. The following main points can be distinguished:

  • no matter what time you arrive at the hotel, check-in and check-in only starts after 2:00 p.m. (the exception is when the hotel is not full and you can buy an early check-in service);
  • you need to move out of your room before 12:00 or pay for each hour of an additional stay at the hotel;
  • children under 6 years old stay in the room for free if they do not require an extra bed (for this age category they are not provided);
  • It is possible that no more than one additional guest is accommodated in the room with a sleeping place;
  • The daily cost of an extra bed for a child 6-12 years old is 1400 rupees;
  • pets must not be brought to the hotel;
  • at the hotel you can pay in cash or by credit card;
  • each guest is required to present a photo identification upon arrival, as well as a plastic credit card, on which funds are blocked as a deposit.




Loyalty program and bonuses for guests

A demonstration of hospitality, as well as respect for guests at the Horizon Beach Resort 3 * hotel (India, North Goa) is a system of pleasant compliments that covers the following categories of guests:

  • newlyweds receive a twenty percent discount on accommodation, as well as all other hotel services;
  • guests celebrating their birthday at the hotel also receive a certain discount on certain types of services;
  • regular coupons were also awarded to the discount coupon, which includes those tourists who have visited the hotel two or more times;
  • each and every guest arriving at this hotel on vacation receives a nice compliment in the form of a welcome refreshing drink.

It is important to note that those guests who buy tickets through travel companies cannot participate in the bonus program. A compliment can only be received by those guests who, at the time of booking, contacted the hotel directly and indicated their desire to participate in the loyalty program. Also, when arriving at the hotel, do not forget to remind the hotel administration of your right to receive a compliment.





Positive reviews

In general, it is worth noting that tourists highly appreciate the efforts of the staff of the Horizon Beach Resort 3 * hotel. Reviews note a lot of positive points, which is expressed in gratitude comments posted on specialized resources:

  • spacious and cozy rooms, which have all the necessary furniture, appliances and other items for a comfortable and complete stay;
  • the hotel did not notice insects and other traditional animals for the area;
  • on the balconies there is a table and two chairs, so you can have lunch here or just admire the surroundings with a cup of coffee;
  • every night live music in the restaurant;
  • high level of staff training;
  • daily quality housekeeping;
  • the hotel is located in such a way that the noise of a busy street and the buzz of transport practically do not reach the ears of guests;
  • the balcony door has its own lock, which is locked from the inside, which increases the feeling of security;
  • the hotel has a quiet and calm atmosphere;
  • interesting evening performances with invited artists of different genres;

  • a very small area with a tiny courtyard, which is almost completely occupied by the pool (there is no place for walking);
