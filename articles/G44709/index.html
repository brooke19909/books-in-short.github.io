Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club HV-1 is an excellent hotel for measured relaxation

Description: The exotic hotel Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club HV-1 was built in nineteen eighty-ninth year, and cosmetic repairs were carried out for the last time in two thousand and eleventh. The hotel is located in one of the districts of the resort village of Goynuk. The friendly hotel staff, speaking German, English, Russian and Turkish, impeccably takes care of customers who have chosen the amazing country of Turkey for their holidays or vacations. The hotel is only eight kilometers from the city called Kemer. Tourists can reach the international airport by car in just half an hour.



Rooms: The numerous Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club HV-1 cottages have four hundred and eighteen rooms for accommodation, of which three hundred and forty-one are standard doubles and seventy-seven are family rooms for four people. The rooms are very nice looking. The rooms have all the amenities that no tourist can do without during their holidays - these are spacious soft beds, air conditioning, satellite TV with Russian and music channels, a telephone through which you can reach any country, terrace or balcony, as well as a free mini -bar constantly replenished with drinks. In the bathroom, tourists will find a toilet, washbasin, shower, towels, hairdryer. Thoroughly cleaned rooms are provided daily, and bed linen is changed three times a week. Cots are available upon request. Pets are not allowed.

Meals: On vacation at the Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club HV1 tourists will be pleasantly surprised by the all-inclusive system, as well as a restaurant serving the highest quality and mouth-watering dishes of local and international cuisines. All of them are diligently prepared by professional chefs. Delicious light snacks, coffee, soft drinks and various cocktails are available at the bars and snack bars located in the hotel and by the pool.



Beach: Equipped sand and pebble beach is a personal property of Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club HV-1. Free mattresses, umbrellas, sun loungers, towels are provided. The beach also offers a lot of water activities.

Information for guests: All guests of Ulusoy Kemer Holiday Club HV-1 can visit the pool, sauna, Turkish hammam, fitness center, spa, jacuzzi, disco. At the hotel, tourists are free to play basketball, billiards, darts, beach volleyball, boccia, squash, futsal, chess, tennis and table tennis. Any tourist has the opportunity to rent a bicycle or car. The following services are provided to all clients: hairdresser, doctor, masseur, nanny, laundry and dry cleaning. Entertainment programs and shows are arranged every evening. Restaurants and cafeterias are nearby.



Reviews: The welcoming Ulusoy Kemer Holiday has an excellent reputation and is an ideal option for a relaxing, measured vacation. The hotel does not leave indifferent neither the family, nor the elderly and business people, nor girls and boys, because its wide range of services meets the needs of various categories of tourists, and smiling staff is in a hurry to help in any situation.
