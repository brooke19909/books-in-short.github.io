Recreation center "Lokomotiv": description of the institution and reviews of tourists

The recreation center "Lokomotiv" is a new country club, which opened a couple of years ago in the Argayash district of the Chelyabinsk region. In a fairly short time, the institution has become a fairly popular place where not only locals, but also guests of the South Ural region of Russia spend their free time with pleasure.

Detailed description

Hundreds of tourists from different parts of the country come to the Chelyabinsk region annually. People who like the local nature with its hilly plains, mountain ranges, as well as numerous rivers and lakes tend here. Visitors are welcomed all year round by tourist hotels and health centers, of which there are more than one hundred and twenty in the region. Among them, the Lokomotiv recreation center is very popular. It is located thirty kilometers from Chelyabinsk, in a picturesque location on the shores of Lake Kum-Kul.





The territory of the institution is quite large. For tourists, there are 9 cozy houses with all amenities and the same number of summer-type buildings. The rooms accommodate two or more people. The houses are equipped with brand new furniture and necessary appliances. All guests receive fresh bedding upon check-in. The recreation center "Lokomotiv" is relatively new. It has been functioning for only two years. But this time was enough to make the institution one of the most visited in the region.

Service and entertainment

The recreation center "Lokomotiv" offers its guests the most comfortable conditions. On the territory there are washbasins, showers, bathrooms and even a bathhouse. Residents of a metropolis often come here in large companies to have a good weekend after a hard working week. Here you can find something for every taste. This country club will especially appeal to fishing enthusiasts. They will be pleasantly surprised, because in the local lake there are a lot of very different fish (perch, crucian carp, mackerel, minnow, roach, whitefish, peled and ripus). You can cook a rich catch yourself. For this, there is a special brazier zone near each house. And those who are not good at cooking can send fish to the kitchen, where the local chef will cook any dish from it to order. Some lovers of country holidays come here even as families. For this, the camp site has everything you need. When parents enjoy nature, kids can enjoy spending time on a well-maintained playground.

Accommodations

All year round, lovers of pristine nature are warmly welcomed by the Lokomotiv recreation center. Kum-Kul is one of the most important sights of these places. In summer, guests can enjoy relaxing on the comfortable, comfortable beach. For those who want to sunbathe, sun loungers and umbrellas are installed here. And there are so many that enough for everyone. The visitors are especially impressed by the clear water of the local lake. Swimming in it is a pleasure. And after an active holiday, you can have a good time in your house, where no one bothers to enjoy the fresh air and silence. The Lokomotiv recreation center (Kum-Kul) provides tourists with rooms of two categories:

  1. Standard. There are beds in the room (depending on the number of guests). The hallway has a kitchenette with a kettle, fridge, microwave and a minimum set of dishes.
  2. Suite Unlike the previous room, the rooms have comfortable furniture and a TV.




Guests usually eat at a local restaurant. And in the house, if you wish, you can warm up ready-made food or make tea.

How to get there

Where exactly is the Lokomotiv recreation center located, and how do potential visitors get to the place? In principle, this is not difficult, since a specially paved asphalt road leads from the main highway to the country club itself. First, moving along Sverdlovsky Avenue, you need to leave the city. This will take some time given traffic jams. Then you need to move along the M36 and turn left after the roundabout. Behind should remain lakes Kosargi and Small Kisegach. Along the way, you can admire the picturesque landscape of the local plains and forests. Right after Kara-Kumlyak Lake, on the right there will be a ramp onto the asphalt road, which will lead directly to the country club.





By car, this path will take no more than half an hour, depending on traffic density at different times of the day. Visitors who do not have a private car can agree with the base management on the possibility of transportation in both directions. Transfer services are paid extra if there is an application from the customer.

Opinions of visitors

Numerous visitors really like the Lokomotiv recreation center. Reviews about this place are mostly positive. Many consider it an ideal place for a budget holiday.





Of course, after all, a day in such an institution per person costs from 500 rubles. This amount includes:

  • residence;
  • parking;
  • beach;
  • sports grounds and equipment;
  • use of equipment of the barbecue area.

Moreover, children under 5 years old are provided with free space. Adults will have to pay 350 rubles per day for this. The houses are usually clean. Cleaning is done on schedule. Polite staff is always ready to provide any service to visitors. According to most of them, rest in such a place gives not only health and good mood, but also a huge charge of vivacity and positive. But not everything is as perfect as we would like. As in any other establishment, guests also have skirmishes with attendants. And sometimes they go too emotional on both sides. But this is difficult to avoid in the service sector.
