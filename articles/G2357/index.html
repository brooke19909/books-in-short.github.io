Requirements for staff

The most important component of the hotel business is the culture of staff behavior. Inexpensive hotels in Moscow are read by a large number of specialists with higher education in the tourism sector. Careful selection is carried out by managers, as they know the subtleties that must be paid attention to when hiring a person.



The behavior of a hotel employee is divided into:

  1. Contact with the guest.
  2. Contact with staff.

Regardless of the number of stars assigned to the mini-hotel, service should always be at the highest level. The employee knows speech etiquette, the rules for addressing guests, and knows how to correctly and competently express his thoughts.

Rules of conduct for staff:

  1. An employee in this field should be ready to provide services to clients around the clock.
  2. Express a positive attitude towards guests.
  3. Smile and eye contact when meeting.
  4. The ability to tactfully inform the guest even about unpleasant news.

  6. Guest problems are resolved as quickly as possible.
