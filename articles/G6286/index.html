Terrace Beach Resort Hotel All Incl (Turkey, Side) - review, description and reviews of tourists

Many tourists will remember the rest at the Terrace Beach Resort Hotel (all inclusive) for a long time, since during its course guests receive a lot of positive emotions. Many tourists consider the low prices for hotel accommodation to be a pleasant moment - a week of relaxation for two here will cost only about 55,000 rubles, which most travelers consider to be low cost for the excellent conditions that are provided here.

general information

The hotel is often categorized as one that is ideal for families. All this is connected, first of all, with the fact that tourists staying here with children or in a large company will not feel any discomfort - for such cases, appropriate conditions are provided here.

Terrace Beach Resort Hotel was built in 1990. However, at the moment it has a rather fresh and modern look, which was achieved thanks to periodic reconstructions and renovation processes. One of the largest was last held here in 2010. At this time, the ventilation system was completely replaced, the rooms were renovated and individual climate equipment was installed.

Location

Terrace Beach Resort Hotel 5 * is located in the luxurious resort area, known throughout the world - Side, in the village of Kumkey. It is located at a short distance from the city center - 4 km, which means that it is within easy reach of the entertainment infrastructure. This is very often used by guests, almost daily going to local night clubs, restaurants, cafes, karaoke and other interesting places.

The nearest major airport is in Antalya. In order to get to the hotel from it, tourists need to overcome as much as 65 km. That is why, without fail, a transfer service is included in the standard package of tickets.

Rooms

The entire hotel fund consists of 270 apartments, which, depending on the number of places for guests, are divided into two categories: "Standard" and "Family". Each of them has a personal restroom, where there is a place for bathing, represented by a bath or shower, a sink and a bathroom. All the plumbing that is available here is in harmony with the installed cupboards, where guests will find soapy bathing accessories, as well as bathrobes and slippers.

Each apartment has air conditioning installed. It is with its help that tourists have the opportunity to independently adjust the air temperature in the room to the desired level.

"Standard"

In the fund Terrace Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Side), most of them are rooms of the "Standard" class, which are designed for two guests. An extra bed can be added if necessary. The room consists of one room, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is 22 sq.m.

Reviews of Terrace Beach Resort Hotel 5 * often say that the Standard rooms are quite bright, which creates a feeling of lightness when you stay in them. All this is associated with the use of bright colors in the interior of the apartment - they are represented by a harmonious combination of white and blue. There are also large windows from the ceiling to the floor, from which vacationers can watch magnificent views of the sea and gardens.

As for the furniture, the room has a large double bed, which in some apartments is replaced by two single beds. A bedside table made of light wood is mandatory installed near the beds, and there is also a wardrobe for clothes. The apartments set a small area for relaxation, made in the form of a pair of chairs and a small round table with curved legs. Here, guests recommend drinking coffee or tea, which can be prepared using the electric kettle in the room. There is also a TV.

If desired, tourists can request the installation of a safe or minibar in their room, however, a daily fee will have to be paid for using these items.



"Family"

Terrace Beach Resort Hotel has a small number of apartments designed to accommodate more guests - up to 4 people. As a rule, tourists prefer to stay in them, vacationing with friends or entire families. If necessary, a cot can be installed in the apartment, which is completely free, upon request.

The room area consists of one room with 4 beds: two in the form of a large bed and two in the form of a bunk bed. Here, a large plasma TV is installed for guests with the ability to watch a huge number of television channels, among which, as guests note, there are many Russian speakers. In all other respects, the furniture set is fully consistent with what is provided in the Standard Apartments.

As for the additional equipment, the room has a minibar, which is filled with cool water at the time of guests' arrival, and also, if desired, a safe can be installed here - for it you will have to pay a daily fee of about 1.5 euros.

Nutrition

Terrace Beach Resort Hotel offers its guests excellent food. According to vacationers, a buffet is served here every day, which guests staying for a package can visit for free. They note that at any time of the day interesting dishes of Turkish and European cuisines are served here, which are originally made out. Guests are advised to pay special attention to meat dishes cooked over an open fire.



The Terrace Beach Resort Hotel (Side) has another restaurant that does not have a buffet, guests can only visit it by reservation. It offers original dishes prepared in the best traditions of Mediterranean cuisine. Tourists note that seafood is especially tasty here. In addition, in the main hall of the restaurant there is a large aquarium where shellfish swim. Visitors can independently choose for themselves fresh seafood and indicate the desired method of its preparation - such an original opportunity in the restaurant attracts the attention of many vacationers.

In addition to restaurants, the Terrace Beach Resort Hotel has two bars, one of which is located in the building, and the second is by the pool. And in one, and in another there is the opportunity to taste cocktails from the chic bar menu, which presents a wide range of drinks. You can visit these establishments from 10 a.m. to midnight.



Conditions for children

Reviews of Terrace Beach Resort Hotel often say that this place is perfect for relaxing in the company of children. Such comments are related to the fact that a number of conditions have been created for the kids here, contributing to a comfortable stay not only for them, but also for their parents.

In particular, many parents note on the positive side the possibility of installing a crib in apartments, and for free. They are also pleased with the presence of a children's club and professional babysitting services. All employees who work with children are fluent in Russian, which is good news for customers.

In the open area, some conditions are also provided. In particular, this is a small pool - part of the water zone, as well as a playground.



Sport

Lovers of an active lifestyle very often share their comments about visiting the gym located in the main building of the hotel. They talk about the fact that the gym is equipped with several tens of simulators, with the help of which it is possible to stimulate different muscles, doing hard work to strengthen them.

Terrace Beach Resort has several outdoor areas, one of which is a tennis court. An additional fee is required to visit it. On a free basis, it seems possible to visit volleyball and basketball courts, which are also popular.

Spa

The spa, which for a short time many guests record as one of their favorite places in the hotel, is divided into two parts, one of which is free to visit, which can not be said of the other.

For payment according to the price list, tourists can go for massage treatments, which are carried out in a separate room, as well as in the spa center, which offers a wide range of cosmetic services. As for the free visit, it is provided in the hammam and sauna, which are combined into a large thermal part of the spa area.

Aquazone

Guests of Terrace Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Side), who are fans of water procedures, prefer to visit the large water zone, which is located on the territory adjacent to the main building of the hotel. There is a considerable size playground, fully furnished with sun loungers and umbrellas. In order to allow guests to cool down during sunbathing, a bar is installed here.

In the central part of the aquazone there is a large pool, the total area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is almost 1,000 sq.m. It lacks a heating system - this process is carried out under the influence of the sun. There are three slides in the pool, which guests often mention in their reviews.



Beach

A visit to the beach is one of the favorite activities of tourists who are at the resort. This hotel has access to the coastline of the Mediterranean Sea, where the water is always clean and very warm. In the comments about the rest, travelers often talk about its improvement and the good work of the beach staff.



Sun loungers, umbrellas, towels and mattresses can be taken here completely free of charge, which is positively appreciated by hotel guests. The only negative is that due to the weather conditions of the area, this zone does not work in winter, however, as tourists say, during this period you can have fun in the indoor pool, which is located in the building.
