The main attractions of St. Petersburg and Adler. How to get from one city to another?

Summer is the most beautiful season! Where does everyone want to spend their vacation? Of course, on the shores of the clean sea, sandy beach and under the rays of the warm sun! Many people come from St. Petersburg to Adler in order to fully relax. They often travel in the opposite direction to get acquainted with the most outstanding sights of the cultural capital. Fortunately, there are several ways to create a route from one point to another.

Five reasons to visit St. Petersburg

Every year, crowds of people from all over the world come to amazing St. Petersburg. What attracts them to this place? There are five main reasons to visit this city.

  1. Go to the Hermitage. This ancient museum should be visited by everyone who has visited St. Petersburg. It houses more than 100 exhibition halls of various subjects. It is worth highlighting this trip all day, because you will not be able to quickly enjoy all its beauties.
  2. Another reason to come to St. Petersburg from Adler and any other city is a walk along Peterhof. In this place you can enjoy the view of the most beautiful fountains, ancient architecture and picturesque nature.
  3. The city has a huge amount of entertainment for children: a water park, a dolphinarium, amusement parks, an aquarium and special exhibitions for kids.
  4. St. Petersburg is also a creative place. It is recommended to visit the theater in order to get acquainted with productions with the participation of the most talented actors.
  5. At night, you should come to Nevsky Prospect to see how bridges are being built. This is an incredibly romantic sight.




It is not necessary to visit any places at all. The cultural capital of Russia has a huge number of monuments, beautiful parks and ancient architecture. It is enough to come here only to enjoy the view of the historical streets.

Five reasons to visit Adler

There are five main reasons, referring to which people come to Adler from St. Petersburg and anywhere else in the world.





  1. View on the mountains. This picturesque landscape will charm every tourist.
  2. Plunge into the purest Black Sea.
  3. It will be interesting for young children to visit the monkey house, in which representatives of more than ten breeds of these cute animals live.
  4. Only here you can enjoy real Caucasian cuisine.
  5. The whole family is recommended to go to the dolphinarium. An interesting show, which is organized in it, will capture the spirit of a person of absolutely any age category.

Adler is a great resort in Russia. In order to come to this place, there is no need to make a passport, visa and learn a foreign language. There will be no less positive impressions from this place than from foreign vacations.

Fastest way

The fastest route from St. Petersburg to Adler and vice versa is by plane. Currently, there are several companies that allow you to make a flight. The total travel time is 3 hours 20 minutes. All airports are located near the city center. Nearby there are stops for public transport. At the exit from it are taxi drivers and offer their services. There will be no problems with how to get to the right place.

The minimum ticket price for one person is 4600 rubles. In the summer, a lot of hype, in this regard, it is recommended to buy tickets in advance. Prices may rise during this period.

The most extreme way

Moving from St. Petersburg to Adler by car is the most extreme way. The total distance from one point to another along the highway will be more than 2340 kilometers. If you go without stops, traffic jams and rest, then the approximate travel time is 30 hours.





Typically, two drivers combine to cover this distance and drive the vehicles in turn. If this is not possible, then you need to think through your route in advance, providing places for an overnight stay, a snack and refueling the car. On the way, there will be several traffic police posts where inspectors may ask for baggage to be shown.





Airplane, train and car are the three most popular modes of transport, they can be reached from St. Petersburg to Adler. There is another option - the bus.
