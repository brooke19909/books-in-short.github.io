Description of trampoline parks of Kirov

Not only in large cities of Russia, but also in the province, many entertainment complexes are being built every year. So, in the city of Kirov, located almost 1000 kilometers from the capital, there are several fascinating centers of development and recreation for children and adults. Recently, trampoline centers have become very popular, where people with different levels of physical fitness can feel a sense of flight and weightlessness.





In this article we will consider two trampoline parks of Kirov: the first - "Mario" and the second - "Pit Stop". Both centers are very popular in the city, especially families with children who love them. It‚Äôs great when the whole family spends fun time. The complexes allow children and their parents to have fun together, receive hormones of happiness and restore strength.

In addition, trampolining is not just a game, it is health! During classes, muscles are strengthened, coordination is improved, the vestibular apparatus is trained. During jumps, people have improved blood circulation and the functioning of important organs, stress is also relieved, and mood is lifted.

The first trampoline park in Kirov - "Mario"

Initially, when the park appeared in the city (2015), it was called Kanga. But later, at the numerous requests of the townspeople, the center created a new design similar to the Super Mario game and was given the same name.





The complex is a large hall with trampolines, which are separated by paths of soft mats. There are also holes filled with colored foam cubes, there is a separate area with sports trampolines where people with experience and certain skills train. Athletes who practice various tricks are usually involved here.

In total, the complex has 26 trampolines of various sizes, two foam pits, a soft sports track and several balance boards. All equipment is modern and certified. In the hall there are always instructors who teach visitors how to jump and group correctly. Also, CCTV cameras are installed around the perimeter of the room.

In addition to the main hall, Mario has an equipped dressing room with lockers for clothes. There is a room designed for the holidays, equipped with tables and chairs.

Acrobatic and fitness groups are organized in the park, there is the possibility of individual lessons.

The cost of one hour of jumping is 300 rubles, two hours - 520 rubles. Children from three to eleven years jump for 200 rubles per hour.

"Pit stop"

"PitStop" in Kirov is similar to the first trampoline park. There are also many trampolines, a foam pit and a room for celebrations (you can rent the whole park). There is the possibility of free jumping, individual and group exercises. The building has free parking for cars.

Park Addresses

  • Trampoline park "Mario" in Kirov is located on Lenin Street in the building at number 1B (near the hotel "Bliss").

Park Hours:

  • on weekdays from 10:00 to 22:00;

  • PitStop is located at 24B Proizvodstvennaya Street (in the south-west of the city). From Monday to Friday, the center is open from 10am to 10pm. On Saturday and Sunday from 9am to 11pm.

Visiting Rules

According to the rules of trampoline parks in Kirov, they can be visited from any age, but children under 5 years old must be on trampolines under the supervision of their parents. Visitors should wear sportswear and socks on their feet. Before visiting, you need to remove watches, bracelets, chains, glasses and other jewelry. In addition, you should not bring drinks and food with you to the trampolines.
