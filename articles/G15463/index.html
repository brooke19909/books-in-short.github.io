Patterns of women's underpants: from patterns to finished products

Underwear is the most intimate element of the wardrobe. Having gone through many years of transformation, having experienced all the changes in tastes, attitudes and moral principles, the linen has taken on the form we are used to.

Varieties of female models of cowards

Women's shorts have recently acquired a familiar and familiar look for us. A few decades ago, the most common model was knickers - voluminous shorts from the waist to the knee or mid-thigh. Modern industry offers women a huge variety of models of underpants: slips, thongs, bikinis, tankinis, Brazilians, culottes, shorts.

Sew linen not only from natural fabrics - most models are elastic, the material in the composition has a large proportion of artificial fibers, which, however, does not affect the environmental friendliness and benefit of wearing such clothes.

Popular models

Two models are most popular among women: slip-on pants, as well as shorts.

Slips are comfortable for everyday wear. A high cut on the hips allows you to wear them with dresses or sundresses with a slit, and fully closed buttocks provide their owner with comfort. In such underwear you can not be afraid that the wind will flaunt the intimate parts of the body.

Shorts are comfortable and even more closed. They are preferred to wear under a sports uniform, jeans, leggings. The length of this model of linen can vary: underpants can cover the buttocks or leave them half open. In addition, not only tight-fitting models are widespread, but also more free ones, richly decorated with ruffles, lace and multilayer ribbons.

Sizing





The finished pattern of women's underpants most often consists of two symmetrical parts and a lining on the gusset. To build a correct and competent pattern, you need to take measurements.

Cutting and sewing

After constructing a pattern of women's panties, they begin to cut the fabric. Here you need to be careful so as not to spoil the model. How to sew women's underpants? The pattern is transferred to the fabric. For shorts, it is best for beginner needlewomen to take material that does not stretch too much. If there is experience in sewing, then it must be taken into account that the pattern must be transferred to the fabric so that the stretching is along the fabric in the horizontal direction. In this case, the model will sit perfectly on the hips.
