a brief description of

Baltym is a lake located in the Sverdlovsk Region 3 km from Verkhnyaya Pyshma. This is a very beautiful and picturesque place that attracts tourists not only from Yekaterinburg and nearby cities, but also from all over the world.

The parameters of the lake are 4 kilometers long and 2.6 wide. The main bottom area is silty sediments. This factor greatly affects the transparency of water. Officially, the average depth is fixed at around 3-3.5 m. However, there are places where it reaches 5-6.5 m.

Baltym is a saucer-shaped lake, slightly elongated towards the south. On one part of the reservoir, the shores are entirely composed of mixed forests. Here you can meet such representatives of the plant world as fir, pine, spruce, birch. There are rocky shores and sandy beaches where the water is quite clear.

Coastline

On the south and north sides of the coast are completely covered with swamps and reed beds. From the east, there are sandy embankments on which beaches suitable for swimming are equipped. And abundant thickets of grass are observed along the entire coastline of the Baltim reservoir.

The lake (Yekaterinburg is 20 km) is of great interest to the local population. On its territory, namely, on the eastern side, there are a lot of different recreation centers for workers of the Uralmashzavod, Shakhtospetsmontazh and Uralelectrotyazhmash. In addition, there is a registered farm for lovers of hunting and fishing. In the southwest of the lake there are pioneer camps and motels. You can see the water station, which is located on the southeast coast, as well as the yacht club of the Ural Mining and Metallurgical Company.



Animal world

Baltym is a lake that is famous for a large number of diverse fish. Here live a chebak, ruff, silver crucian carp, bream, carp, roach, tench, perch, burbot, pike, goldfish. In the forests located on the shore of the reservoir, a lot of animals live, and predators are often found. Permanent residents of these places are lynx, wolf, wolverine, squirrel, elk. Above the surface of the water you can find a huge number of waterfowl. Some species of animals are listed in the Red Book.

Baltim Lake: recreation centers

There are a lot of recreation centers for vacationers, in which there are all conditions for a comfortable stay.

  • The recreation center "Fun" is located right in the forest among tall pines. There is a two-story building, designed for 17 rooms. There is a cafe on site and for your convenience there are gazebos.
  • The base "Forest Fairy Tale" is perfect for a vacation with the whole family, as it has baths, beaches, gazebos, a pier for those who like to go fishing and a great children's town.
  • Base "Fairy Lukomorye", located in a birch grove. On the territory there is a playground, sauna and car parking.
