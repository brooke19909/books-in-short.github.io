Easiest recipe

For those who want to know how to bake rye bread in a bread machine, an easy recipe is suitable:

1. Put in the bowl:

  • 750 g of flour;
  • 70 g of yeast;
  • 2.5 cups of water;
  • 40 g butter;
  • 2 chicken eggs;
  • 1 tbsp. l of salt.

2. Choose an accelerated baking mode, frying the crust - medium.

3. When the appliance signals the cooking of bread, let it stand for 10-15 minutes without opening the lid. After baking, you can serve to the table.

Bun with poppy seeds

For owners of such a multifunctional device, it is important to know not only how to bake rye bread in a bread machine, but also recipes for other delicious pastry products. Sweet desserts and buns. This baking is loved not only by children, but also by adults.



To prepare delicious buns with poppy seeds, you must:

1. Substitute the dough. To do this, put in the bowl:

  • two glasses of flour;
  • dry yeast packet;
  • a glass of milk;
  • 30 g butter;
  • half a glass of sugar;
  • a spoonful of salt.

2. Set the "Dough" mode.

3. While it is preparing, soak the poppy in water for 1 hour (half a glass).

4. When the dough arrives, take it out and roll it out.

5. Put the filling of poppy seeds and sugar (half a glass).

6. Roll rolls.

7. Put them in a bowl on top of each other.

8. Bake the baking program.

Jam in the bread maker

In this technique, you can cook not only delicious pastries, but also make jam. It is easy to do. Since there is no need to monitor the sweet composition so that it does not run away.

Watermelon Peel Jam

For cooking, you need to take 1 kg of watermelon peels and sugar. Finely chop the crusts and mix with sugar. Put in a bread machine. Set the "Jam" mode, after disconnecting repeat this mode after an hour.

Orange jam

For cooking, you need 4 large oranges, the juice of one lemon, a glass of sugar and cinnamon to taste. Peel the oranges, cut into cubes. In a bowl, mix all the components and set the jam mode.

Cucumber jam

Unusual cucumber jam can be prepared in a Rolsen RBM-1160 bread machine. Necessary components:

  • cucumbers 1 kg;
  • 1 lemon
  • ginger root 20 g;
  • 2 - 2.5 tbsp. Sahara.

Wash and peel the cucumbers, cut into thin strips. Grind lemon through a meat grinder. Peel ginger, grate (size optional). Mix everything in a bowl and set the jam mode.
