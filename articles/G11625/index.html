Military style photo shoot - bold, bold and exciting

A military-style photo shoot is a unique and exciting opportunity to demonstrate rigor, audacity, but at the same time naturalness and femininity. The military style puts on display clear lines, a certain color scheme and unlimited possibilities for all kinds of combinations.

Style story



The style arose after the First World War, when the life of ordinary people was just beginning to be restored, and only the material that was used to sew the military uniform was available. Gradually, light industry resumed production, but after World War II, history repeated itself again. As a separate and distinctive trend, the military style arose only in the 60s of the last century. In the USA, such clothes were worn in protest against the war. This idea was picked up by designers, and in this way the military style began to gain its popularity around the world.

Various styles

In its relatively short history, the military style went through several stages. The first stage is the birth of style in the 60s in the USA. Wearing a military uniform showed a protest against the war and the rebellious soul of its owner. They wore such clothes especially carelessly - with unbuttoned buttons, a half-tightened belt and with pants several sizes larger.

The second stage is the camouflage style of the 80s. The military camouflage print of gray-green-brown shades is in fashion. Almost everything was painted in such colors, from shoes and accessories to interior design.

And finally, the last direction, which is still alive today, is the modern style of military. Its characteristic features are the possibility of combination and a slight hint of the image of wartime.

Military style photo shoot: how to choose the right place for filming

A photo session can be organized in two ways - in the studio, with the appropriate scenery or with the subsequent processing of photos, or a thematic trip. The second option will look more impressive and bright. It can be a forest, destroyed buildings or an old quarry. You can find some abandoned village, then the photo shoot will acquire a post-apocalyptic hue.



If you want to realize a certain idea, you can agree in advance on shooting at the training ground near real tanks and guns, or rent a similar service in a historical museum. A military-style photo shoot, the photos of which were made using powerful technology in the background, will not only make the shooting more authentic, but will also bring a lot of positive emotions to its participants.

Additional military paraphernalia and decor

To create the necessary thematic atmosphere, you can also use a variety of additional attributes. It can be flags, helmets, a variety of weapons, gas masks, as well as bowlers, a bonfire and walkie-talkies. You can use the net, tents with camouflage print, trenches, etc.



The choice of decor depends primarily on the specific purpose and image in which the military-style photo shoot will be executed. If we are talking about the Second World War, modern military operations or in a fictitious post-apocalyptic form, then the attributes should correspond to this situation.

Military photo shoot - clothes, hairstyle and makeup

One of the main components of high-quality thematic photography is also the corresponding image, which is built using clothes and a single style of hairstyle and makeup.



As for clothing, preference should be given to the characteristic military coloring and a certain set of additional ammunition. However, you can use ordinary sportswear, detailing it with some military attributes - it all depends on the imagination and the specific situation. If desired, ordinary men's or women's clothing may also have a military look.

Military - a style that also involves a certain hairstyle and makeup. The best option for women will be a spikelet, bun or hair collected in a tail. Makeup should be as natural and natural as possible. If you want to turn into an antihero, then you can use bright lipstick and black contrast eyeliner. As a thematic option, you can also draw on the face and the corresponding camouflage in the form of black or dark green stripes.

Modern photo studios also offer a variety of military-style photo shoots based on specific thematic images (pilot, tanker, sailor), as well as on the plots of popular films, such as Die Hard, Pearl Harbor, etc. You can also offer your own unique version of photography, many studios will gladly go to meet the client.

Such thematic photography allows you to take a fresh look at yourself, embody different images. It will also be a great opportunity to have fun with friends and acquaintances in a non-standard setting and get a huge amount of positive emotions and pleasant memories.
