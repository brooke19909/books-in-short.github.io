How to make a mermaid tail yourself

Children very often try to transform into images of fairy-tale heroes. A sincere desire to be like their idols prompts them to put on the colorful costume of their favorite character. And, as often happens in children's fairy tales, wizards or fairies fulfill the desire, the roles of which in real life are played by us, adult fathers and mothers.

The easiest way to satisfy the needs of a little dreamer is to buy a costume of a fairy-tale hero. Although it’s easy to sew such an outfit on your own, of course, if you have some sewing skills, it’s easy. So, let's try to please the little dreamer with a luxurious mermaid outfit.

Original swim suit

We, as young children, were content to admire the heroine of the famous fairy tale by H. H. Andersen and try to be like her using impromptu outfits. Modern fans of the fabulous inhabitants of the seabed tend to "plunge" into the underwater world in the literal sense of the word. And we have no choice but to make the tail of a mermaid, allowing the baby to swim in a luxurious suit. To do this, we use a product made of elastic material in the form of flippers, which can be bought in a specialized store. From a suitable fabric (with the effect of "stretch") we sew a tail, to the base of which we fasten the fin. To learn how to make a mermaid’s tail, we suggest you get acquainted with the entertaining process of creating a fabulous costume in stages.

We are preparing a pattern

Creating a template requires a minimum amount of time. To do this, it is enough to know such parameters as the length of the proposed product, the circumference of the hips and waist. An even more convenient and no less reliable way to create a pattern:

  • spread out a sufficiently large sheet of paper;
  • lay on her a potential "mermaid";
  • circle the body contour with a pencil, leaving a couple of centimeters for fabric allowance;
  • cut a pattern along the contour.

Sewing a suit

Using the template, cut the tail out of the selected fabric. We cut out the details and sew them. We make out the belt with elastic. By the way, it can provide a perfect fit of the costume if you sew it in several places to the wrong side of the tail. Perfect fit can be achieved by attaching elastic tights to the inner surface of the product.

Mermaid fin tail



When creating the most important part of the costume, you can use, as mentioned above, the finished fin. But if desired, it can be cut out of thin flexible plastic, and glued to it comfortable rubber sandals of the child. An impromptu fin can be inserted into the finished tail of the mermaid. But this option will give the baby a lot of trouble when donning. We show you how to make the mermaid’s tail for swimming as comfortable as possible. To do this, we connect the fin and a long skirt in the shape of a "mermaid" with a disconnecting zipper.

Mermaid Carnival Costume

In the question "how to make the mermaid’s tail suitable for swimming", it is necessary to take into account mainly the requirements for the fabric from which the bathing outfit is made: the ability to dry, elasticity, resistance to ultraviolet radiation and salt water. Features of the carnival costume are spectacularity, brightness and the ability to attract attention. Therefore, it is appropriate to use various fabrics and decorative elements.



I suggest one of the options on how to make a do-it- yourself mermaid costume, which I personally tried. In particular, tailoring. For the base of the fins, I chose a synthetic material that is used as a substrate for the laminate. It’s not that I consciously searched for it, it was just that at that moment nothing more suitable was at hand. Cutting out two squares of 50x50 cm from it, I duplicated the base with a smooth satin. Having folded equilateral rectangles by an angle, I received two wedge-shaped details. Stitching a long mermaid-shaped skirt, left two symmetrical cuts along the side seams 50 cm long. Tail blanks were sewn to the free edges of the cuts so that the vertex of the right angle of the fin part coincided with the hem line. At the end, she designed the tail with lush folds of tulle with an iridescent surface. If you are faced with the question: “How to make a mermaid’s tail yourself?”, Then this method will make it as quick and effective as possible.
