Theme photoset - what is it?

The word "photoset" appeared not so long ago. Today, this phenomenon is gaining popularity, and many are striving to preserve the memory of the most vivid moments of life in the form of a series of art photographs designed into an album. Many fashion photographers offer their customers a service such as photoset. What is it that differs from other shooting genres? Let's try to figure everything out in order.

Looking forward to a happy event? Need a photoset!

A pleasant expectation of joyful life events must be supplemented by the troubles of organizing the shooting. It will be great to review beautiful pictures and show them to friends! The main feature of the photoset is the combination of several genres: portrait and subject shooting, landscape. Therefore, the pictures will remind not only about the faces of the main characters of the event, but also about such trifles as festive attributes, the color of the sky, the sun's rays - about everything that accompanied the happy event. Therefore, you need to take care of all the little things! After all, photosets, the ideas of which are thought out in advance, are much more vivid.

Wedding photo shoot

In memory of the day of the creation of the family, you can order several studio portraits from a professional and instruct someone from your friends to carry out reportage filming of the feast. But there is another way - a large wedding photoset. What it is? This is a series of photographs with the newlyweds, giving the most complete picture not only of the holiday itself, but also of the feelings of two people. Bride's shoes, waiting for the mistress, resting on a pillow ring, a bouquet in a tender hand - all these little things should not remain behind the scenes!





But the photographer‚Äôs main focus, of course, is on kisses and hugs!

Memory of a happy childhood

Tenderness, sincere smiles, wide-open surprised eyes, hands of faithful comrades, a thirst for adventure - all these are indispensable attributes of shooting children. Sometimes you don‚Äôt even need to wait for the holiday to arrange a children's photoset! What is this, understandable and true - childhood itself is a great adventure, so why not take off your favorite child without any reason?





Children's photosets, whose ideas are often tied to their favorite fairy tales, require appropriate attributes and costume elements. Therefore, it is worth considering the outfits of small models in advance.

Lovestory - a love story

A very popular destination for photo shoots is shooting couples in love. Sincerity of feelings will not hide even from an impartial lens.





Often such a survey is carried out before the wedding. Why not arrange it just like that? After all, for lovers of photoshoot - what is it in the first place? Of course, a memory for life! Or maybe even the first heirloom.





The main thing in shooting a love story is sincerity and naturalness. An abundance of props is not required here. And the lights of the city at night, a country meadow, the usual roof of a high-rise building can be a wonderful background for shooting ‚Äî anything, because the main thing is that your loved one is nearby.

Interesting position

The tummy, in which a new life grows, changes shape every day. What is no reason to invite a photographer?

Just think over everything you need in advance! Little shoes on the tummy or a huge bow tied on a rounded waist, you will not surprise anyone. But dad‚Äôs gentle hands are an immortal classic. The older children are looking very interesting in the frame, waiting for replenishment together with their parents: they can listen to the sounds, gently kiss the tummy or even draw on it.

Theme photoset for large and small

Why not make your favorite fairy tale the theme of the photo shoot? By the way, such a shot by a good photographer can be a wonderful gift for a teenager, adult girl or young parents.





And how to bring a fairy tale to life? Of course, with the help of props. For example, to shoot on the theme of "Alice in Wonderland" we stock up with playing cards, watches, roses, tea cups and, of course, fairy-tale books.





By the way, in one vein, you can shoot both kids and older model photosets.





How to prepare for a photoset?

In addition to clothing and paraphernalia, it is worth considering makeup. The face should not glare and shine. It is better to walk on it with matting powder. Overuse with makeup is not worth it, especially if the model is very young. Everything necessary for shooting should be packed compactly and accurately so that there is no mess on the set . A photoset on the street, whose ideas do not imply excessive solemnity, can do without props at all. And most importantly - a smile will decorate any face, especially if it is from the heart!
