Hotel Luxury Bahia Principe Ambar 5 * (Dominican Republic / Punta Cana): photos and reviews of tourists

In our article we want to talk about Luxury Bahia Principe Ambar 5 * in the Dominican Republic. The complex is part of the famous Bahia Principe Hotels & Resorts.

A bit about the hotel ...

Luxury Bahia Principe Ambar 5 Hotel * is thirty-five kilometers from Punta Cana Airport. The road by car takes no more than forty minutes. And to the airport of La Romana - seventy kilometers.

Hotel address: Republica Dominicana, Panta Cana, Play Bavaro.

The complex was opened in 2007. It is located on a huge territory, and in the vicinity there are hotels: Grand Bahia Principe Punta Cana 5 *, Grand Bahia Principe Bavaro 5 *, Grand Bahia Principe Turcuesa 5 *, Luxure Bahia Principe Esmeralda 5 *.



Currently, the complex is undergoing some changes, and from November 1, 2016 it will be divided into two separate hotels: Luxury Bahia Principe Ambar Blue 5 * and Luxure Bahia Principe Ambar Green 5 *.

What is a good hotel?

What attracts tourists to the hotel Luxury Bahia Principe Ambar 5 *? Guest reviews speak for themselves. Its main advantage is its location. Punta Cana is a very small region in the southeast of the Dominican Republic, which has long acquired the status of a resort. It is here on the coast that the Luxury Bahia Principe Ambar 5 * hotel is located.

This land is valued for endless white beaches with the finest grains of sand. Eternal summer reigns here. The tropical climate gives a temperature from 28 to 30 degrees year round. Perhaps this is the only place in the world where there are beautiful bounty-style beaches. All this beauty is complemented by three national parks, reptiles, mangroves, tropical birds, all kinds of animals, and, of course, wonderful fish restaurants.

Number of rooms

Luxury Bahia Principe Ambar 5 * hotel represents eleven three-storeyed villas. In total, the complex has 964 rooms for adults. This clarification is not just. The fact is that the hotel complex does not accept children under eighteen years of age.

All suites are Junior Suite Deluxe. The rooms are equipped with two large four-poster beds. The living room has a sofa bed. The bathroom is fully equipped with everything you need, as well as a shower and spa bath. The apartments have air conditioning, fan, hairdryer, alarm clock, telephone, slippers and bathrobe, safe, coffee maker, plasma TV, ironing board and iron, terrace, pillows to choose from, beach towels, minibar (replenished daily). Each room is served by a major.



In the arsenal of the hotel there are several apartments for people with disabilities.

Sightseeing Tours

Dominican Republic is an absolutely amazing country. In order to know at least a small part of it, you need to escape a little from a wonderful beach holiday and go on an excursion. This will help the hotel Luxury Bahia Principe Ambar 5 *. The tours offered by vacationers are amazing and beautiful: you can go to the deserted Macau beach or swim with dolphins. What to choose is up to the tourists themselves.



One of the most popular trips is visiting Santo Domingo. This is the most famous and ancient city of America, which was founded in 1496 by the brother of Columbus. This is where the history of the New World began.

During the walk, tourists can see modern Santo Domingo, the presidential palace, the colonial city, which is declared a UNESCO heritage.

Gourmets should definitely go to the small fishing village of El Cortecito, located next to Punta Cana. She is known for her restaurants serving dishes made from freshly caught fish.

For guests of the country it will be very interesting to visit the show ‚ÄúTrocalissimo‚Äù. This is the most famous show throughout the Dominican Republic.

Among the tourists, the Costa del Barn area is popular, which translates as "amber coast". This place is famous for amber deposits. Here you can also see the ruins of the house of Columbus, the church of San Felipe.

One of the main attractions of the Dominican Republic is the lighthouse of Columbus. However, this is not the lighthouse that would be customary to see on the seashore. This is a museum that stores the remains of the great navigator. The building is completely unlike a lighthouse, in shape it resembles a cross and is made of white marble.

Of interest to visitors is not the building itself, but those artifacts that are stored within its walls.



You definitely need to see the A. Bermudez National Park, which was created in 1956. It is located in the center of the island in the province of Santiago. The park is bordered by other reserves. It is here that the three highest peaks of the Dominican Republic are located. In addition, the twelve most important rivers of the country originate here. Deep gorges, alpine meadows, fast rivers, beautiful landscapes attract many connoisseurs of natural beauty. For fans of extreme sports there are also entertainment, for example, to climb the Duarte peak. And for naturalist photographers, this region is just a real find, because an endless number of amazing birds live in the local jungle.

The local relief features created a large number of stunning landscapes. The foothills of the mountains are richest in vegetation, which led to the formation of real jungle. Cedars, palm trees, creepers, juniper, almonds, mahogany, dates, olive, ebony grow here.

But a national park called Los Aities will be appreciated by ecotourism fans. It was created in 1976 and is located on the Saman Peninsula. The park is covered with mangrove swamps, cotton and palm trees, beautiful green islands and mysterious caves with ancient rock inscriptions. The peculiarity of this place is a unique alternation of sections of wild untouched nature with a landscaped area with good infrastructure (restaurants, hotels, bars).

In the south of the Pedernales Peninsula, the Haragua National Reserve is located. This is the largest park in the Caribbean. He is famous for the fact that turtles have been living on its islands for a long time to extend the genus. They need local desert beaches in order to bury masonry in the sand. Turtles living in the reserve are rare species and are protected by law.

Samana Peninsula attracts lovers of lonely relaxation. Here, untouched nature is everywhere (clean rivers, coconut groves, caves and waterfalls) and desert beaches. And in the sea waves you can see manatees and dolphins, in January and March - even humpback whales.
