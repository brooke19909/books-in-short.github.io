Hotels in Slovakia - the best choice for vacation



Many roads lead to this country, and all because it is located in the very center of Europe. Slovakia has not been separately perceived for a long time, since for a long time it existed inseparably with the Czech Republic. But today it is safe to say that Slovakia is a special, beautiful country in its own way. The unusual nature, a large number of attractions, the most diverse opportunities for outdoor activities and hotels in Slovakia - all this makes the country open for tourists.

Slovakia is famous for its ski resorts, medical places that more and more people choose for their holidays. The country has all the conditions for an active pastime: those who wish can ride catamarans, go hiking in the mountains, ski down the mountain slope, go fishing or go looking for interesting and unusual things. You can also afford a peaceful and relaxing holiday, because the nature of Slovakia is ready to help you retire to a place where there are no cars and crowds of people.

Hotels in Slovakia deserve the most flattering words in their direction. Of course, they can not be compared with the best world hotels, but accommodation in them remains at a fairly good level. Want to spend your holidays in Slovakia? Then you should definitely get acquainted with the best hotels of the country.

Forton Hotel 3 *

The hotel is located in one of the most prestigious resorts in Slovenia - the village of Stara Lesna, in the eastern part of the High Tatras. Just 4 km from the hotel is another popular Black Stork Golf Resort. The hotel has 22 rooms and 10 apartments equipped with toilet, bathroom, satellite TV, Internet access, safe and tea and coffee making facilities. There is a restaurant, a fitness center, a barbecue area, a conference room, a beauty salon, an illuminated tennis court and a library.

Grand Smokovec Hotel 4 *

Many hotels in Slovakia are made in the "modern" style, such is the Grand Smokovec, located in the town of Stary Smokovec. Near the hotel there is a lift to Gribienok. Rooms for every taste are equipped with a minibar, TV, TV, bathroom, telephone and radio. The hotel has a wine cellar, caf√©, restaurant, indoor pool, solarium, beauty salon and gym.

Grand Hotel Strand 4 *

This is one of the oldest hotels in Slovakia, built in the early 20th century. Some time ago, he underwent reconstruction, during which he acquired a modern appearance and interiors. The hotel has 45 rooms, including one presidential apartment. The rooms are equipped with everything that can come in handy during your vacation. Their services include a restaurant, a summer terrace, a balneocenter, a nightclub bar, rooms for Internet access and much more.

Grand Jasna Hotel 4 *

Grand Jasna is considered one of the most luxurious hotels in Slovakia, located at an altitude of 110 meters above sea level. Near the hotel there is a cable car that takes tourists to the top of the mountain slope. The rooms are decorated in a modern style and are equipped with a bathroom, toilet, minibar, telephone and TV. The hotel has two restaurants, an Internet cafe, several bars, a disco, a fitness center, slot machines, a hairdresser and a ski rental shop.

It should be noted that hotels in Slovakia are predominantly three-star and four-star. But the level of customer service they have is not worse, this is evidenced by the numerous reviews of hotels in Slovakia of those who have already stayed in them. Among several five-star hotels, it should be noted Arcadia 5 * , located just 10 km from Bratislava National Airport. Superior rooms with bathroom, LCD TV, DVD player, minibar, high-speed Internet and air conditioning.
