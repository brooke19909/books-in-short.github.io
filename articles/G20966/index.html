Brief description of the territory and infrastructure

What does Golden 5 Diamond 5 look like ? Hurghada is a resort famous for its luxury hotels. And this hotel is part of a hotel complex called Golden 5 City, along with six others. The hotel complex was opened in 2003. And already in 2008 a complete restoration was carried out. The hotel meets all international standards, famous for its comfort and hospitality. The Golden 5 Diamond Hotel itself consists of the main building, 8 one-story and 9 two-story bungalows built in oriental style.

What type of food does the hotel offer?

Of course, for many travelers, the issue of nutrition is quite important. So what does the Golden 5 Diamond 5 hotel complex offer? Tours to this hotel include full three meals a day, as well as Turkish-made snacks and drinks, which can be obtained in the bars and cafes of the complex throughout the day.





Three meals a day at the main restaurant serve buffet for guests. The room is designed for a simultaneous stay of up to 260 people. According to reviews, the menu here is quite varied - appetizers, vegetables, meat and fish dishes, as well as Turkish sweets and delicious desserts, are served in sufficient quantities. It is worth noting that guests of the Golden 5 Diamond Hotel can eat in any of the common restaurants of the huge hotel complex.

There are several bars near the pools, as well as in the water park and in the lobby - here you will be offered light snacks, coffee, tea, soft drinks and alcoholic beverages made in Turkey.

Beach holidays for travelers





The hotel, of course, has a private stretch of coast, and quite impressive in size. The 1,500-meter-long beach belongs to the entire Golden 5 Diamond City complex. The distance to it is approximately 200-300 meters, depending on the location of the residential building - you can get here in a few minutes. The beach is sandy and pebble, with a gentle slope into the sea and a coastal coral reef, therefore tourists are advised to purchase appropriate shoes.

There are sun loungers and umbrellas on the shore, as well as wicker fences that make your stay more comfortable. There are a sufficient number of sun loungers, even taking into account the large influx of tourists.

Naturally, fans of more active leisure will find something to do. On the beach there are several areas for volleyball and other team games. It is possible to rent a boat, canoe or catamaran. Of course, you can ride a banana, a boat, water skiing or do parasailing. Constantly guided tours on boats and yachts along the coast.

Do not forget that there is a lively coral reef, which impresses with a variety of marine inhabitants. You can enjoy the bright colors of nature, swimming with a mask. There is also a diving school on the beach where you can rent the necessary equipment. For those tourists who do not yet have experience in diving, they conduct training courses, and they are offered to dive the first dive in company with a professional.
