Lermontovo, "Oak Grove": a relaxing and inexpensive outdoor recreation

One of the most popular places for summer holidays is the sea coast. Those who are going to spend a vacation in nature are attentive to the choice of hotels. After all, on what hotel vacationers will live in, their mood and impression of a summer trip to the sea depends. One of the hotels, which is a popular holiday destination, is described in the article.

Hotel in Lermontovo Oak Grove

This establishment is located in a village near the city of Tuapse. It represents a house on four floors. Not far from the hotel there is a shop, catering establishments, as well as a water park. Guests staying at the hotel can feel alone with nature, enjoy their stay in a picturesque area, away from the bustle of the city. To relax guests are provided gazebos and equipment for the preparation of barbecue.



Oak Grove Hotel in Lermontovo is located in the middle of the forest, and guests can enjoy the beautiful clean air and silence.

Service

Visitors are provided with accommodation, in which there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The rooms accommodate from two to four vacationers. All numbers belong to the class economy. The hotel has fountains, parking, pool. This allows people to relax and plunge into the atmosphere of relaxation.

The hotel's cafe offers a large selection of Russian, Eastern and European dishes.

For those who visit the hotel in Lermontovo "Oak Grove" is not the first time, a discount system is provided. Paid services include meeting guests, washing clothes, ordering dishes.

Accommodation

Visitors live in rooms. The hotel has several varieties of rooms:

  1. Double rooms. These are rooms equipped with a refrigerator, TV, bed, wardrobe, shower and toilet. Also, each of them has air conditioning, a mirror and a chest of drawers.
  2. Rooms designed for three people. These are large rooms with balconies. The furniture in these rooms is bright, there are mirrors on the walls. The rooms have a shower, toilet and bath.
  3. Rooms for four people. These are rooms with three beds, a wardrobe, a table and a chest of drawers. They have a refrigerator, air conditioning.
  4. Superior rooms for two persons. These are rooms consisting of a lounge and a bedroom. They are equipped with good appliances. The rooms have a bed for two people, a sofa, a wardrobe and a nightstand.

Each guest who comes to the hotel in Lermontovo "Oak Grove" can choose the appropriate option for accommodation.

Guest opinions about the establishment

Unfortunately, the quality of service and the leisure opportunities provided by this organization are not for everyone. However, there are positive reviews about the Oak Grove Hotel in Lermontovo. Visitors who are satisfied with the quality of their vacation claim that they liked the attitude of the staff towards customers. Hotel workers are ready to meet guests at the station at any time, help them to bring their luggage, stay in the room and solve all problems. Tourists are pleased that the area near the hotel is perfect for a relaxing holiday, and for gatherings with friends. Another advantage of the hotel is its beautiful nature and clean air. Tourists also liked the quality of food in the cafe.
