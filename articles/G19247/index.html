Hostels of Vilnius: addresses, description of rooms, services provided, reviews

Inveterate travelers know that holidays in Europe are very expensive, Vilnius is no exception. Lithuania - the southernmost country in the Baltic states, used to be a member of the USSR. The capital of Lithuania is Vilnius. This city is located near the border with Belarus, it is known throughout the world thanks to the Baroque buildings, the Renaissance. Vilnius often receives tourists, therefore hotels in it are quite expensive. How to save on housing in this city? Simply, just find an inexpensive hostel. A hostel is a hotel where you can spend the night for little money. It is clean, there are many tourists, so it is very easy to make acquaintances. So, let's look at the best hostels of Vilnius.

B & B & B & B & B

B & B & B & B & B is one of the cheapest hostels in Vilnius. It is located in an old house on the outskirts of the Old Town. Here you can enjoy the incredible architecture, stroll along the pretty streets and buy interesting souvenirs.

The hostel is made in a modern style, the main color of the lobby is black, the rooms, on the contrary, are made in white. This hotel provides different room categories.

  1. Bed in 6-, 8-, 14-bed room. The rooms are shared, for men and for women. The room has a microwave, small kitchen, iron, refrigerator. It offers city views. The cost of a bed is 660 rubles.
  2. Double room, separate. Well suited for those who want to retire and relax from people. The room has a large bed, seating area, kitchen. It is worth saying that the bathroom is shared. The cost of the room is 1800 rubles.

Guests note that free Wi-Fi is available at this hostel in Vilnius, and everyone can visit the indoor skate park. Free entertainment is provided for guests. There is a restaurant on the ground floor serving brunch, lunch and dinner. Of the positive guests note the cleanliness of rooms and the friendliness of the staff. Address: Kauno g. 5, Nauviamestis, LT-03215 Vilnius, Lithuania.
