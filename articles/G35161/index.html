Hotel Volga (Kazan): address, description, reviews

If you want to enjoy comfortable accommodation and quality service, then you should choose the Volga Hotel for yourself. Kazan is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, and therefore, to complement the vivid experience, the hotel should be appropriate.





Brief description of the hotel and tourist complex

The Volga Hotel offers its guests a wide variety of accommodation options. Kazan is visited by both budget tourists and respectable businessmen, and in this institution everyone will find a room to their taste and wallet. It is worth noting that regardless of the category, all apartments have a pleasant, concise design, and are also equipped with all the necessary amenities that will make your stay in the hotel as comfortable as possible.

Hotel "Volga" (Kazan) is one of the oldest in the city, which determines the existence of strong traditions and high quality service. That is why this accommodation option is often chosen by famous people. It is worth noting that here guests are offered many amenities, among which it is worth noting the availability of wireless Internet, private parking, as well as a traditional Russian bath.

A good vacation is simply impossible to imagine without the quality food that the Volga Hotel can offer you. Kazan can boast of a large number of restaurants, but you do not have to look for a good institution for a long time, because it is located directly within the walls of the hotel. Here you will find the best dishes of Russian cuisine, as well as an international menu.





Hotel Location

Hotel "Volga" (Kazan) is located in the central part of the city, which is of the greatest interest to both tourists and business people. Also in close proximity to the institution is the railway station (it is quite possible to walk to it on foot). Popular tourist attractions such as the Kremlin and Syuyumbike Tower are within walking distance . There are a lot of urban infrastructure nearby: shops, restaurants, entertainment venues and business centers. The hotel is located at 1 Said-Galeeva Street.

The amenities provided in the apartment

Volga Hotel (Kazan) offers its guests the following amenities in their apartments:

  • comfortable beds with modern orthopedic mattresses;

Helpful information

This hotel has the following rules:

  • hotel guests are accommodated around the clock;
  • on the day of departure, you must leave your room before noon, otherwise payment will be charged for every hour spent in the room in excess of the standard;
  • each guest is required to fill out a registration card with passport and other personal data;
  • after the fourth visit to the hotel, the client is transferred to the status of permanent;
  • children under 7 years old stay in the room for free if they do not need an extra bed;
  • The price includes breakfast;
  • if one guest wishes to stay in a double room, then payment for accommodation will be charged in full;
  • upon prior agreement with the administration, pets are allowed;
  • in the case when a tourist group is accommodated in the hotel, all responsibility lies with the head, which is regulated by the relevant agreement;
  • if during the day there are no utilities, the cost of living is reduced by 10%.

If you have any questions regarding the rules of residence, in accordance with which the Volga Hotel (Kazan) operates, the telephone number is 8-800-333-03-49.





Positive reviews

Many tourists visit the Volga Hotel (Kazan). Reviews about this place are characterized by the following positive comments:

  • incredibly good location near many attractions;
  • good breakfast buffet;
  • the staff is friendly and very professional;
  • there are economical accommodation options;
  • on each floor there is a cooler with drinking water, as well as an iron and ironing board;
  • the rooms are very clean, bright and comfortable;
  • good TVs with excellent image quality.




Negative reviews

There are a number of negative points:

  • the rooms have poor sound insulation, and therefore sounds from the street and from neighboring rooms will interfere with your relaxation;
  • bedding is quite old and untidy (there are stains that do not wash);
  • despite the fact that there is an option to order food in the room, the restaurant menu is not provided in the room;
  • paid parking.

General impression

It is worth noting that the Volga Hotel is simply an ideal place for tourists, because it is located in the city center near the main cultural attractions. In addition, there are rooms in different price categories: from the budget (with a shower located on the floor) to the representative. The price includes breakfast. He is modest enough, but enough to satisfy his hunger. In addition, the hotel offers a wide range of services, among which the most popular are the sauna and massage.
