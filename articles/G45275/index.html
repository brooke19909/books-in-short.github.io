Photo and description of the rooms: what should a tourist count on?





According to reviews, the Hilton Dubai The Walk Hotel 4 * hotel offers really large and spacious rooms with a modern, comfortable atmosphere. Each room has a large balcony. Naturally, guests can count on household appliances, which are simply necessary for the real comfort of a tourist.

Catering conditions for guests





Good food is the key to a pleasant stay. What does The Hilton The Walk offer its guests? In fact, tourists are free to independently choose a convenient meal plan, whether it is only breakfast or three meals a day. A sumptuous buffet is organized for guests at the main restaurant. Reviews say that the menu here is rich and varied - it includes light snacks, fresh vegetables and lots of fruits, cheese and meat cuts, fish dishes and different types of meat. Do not forget about fresh pastries and delicious desserts.

In total, the hotel has five restaurants and six bars, each of which has its own "highlight". For example, the Bice Restaurant serves the most delicious Italian cuisine. But the Pergola restaurant offers international cuisine, and guests can enjoy dinner on the outdoor terrace, almost on the roof of a high-rise building.

Distance to the beach, water recreation





Dubai is a city that is perfect for shopping, sightseeing and beach holidays. The Hilton The Walk hotel complex is located on the second coastline - you can reach one of the most picturesque beaches of the city in a few minutes. This stretch of coast is private - only guests of two neighboring hotels have a rest here.

Naturally, you can count on comfortable sunbeds with mattresses, sun umbrellas and clean beach towels. There is a bar on the beach serving cocktails, chilled drinks and light, tasty snacks. And on the shore there is a special safe children's corner, where children of all ages bathe and have fun.

Fans of outdoor activities are not bored here either, because a sports club is equipped for guests. For a small fee, you can rent a catamaran, boat or canoe, or rent fins for swimming. Tourists also like to go scuba diving, windsurfing and sailing - on the beach you can not only get the necessary equipment, but also take some lessons from experienced instructors. Of course, tourists are offered exciting water excursions. In a word, nobody has to be bored here.

Description of additional service





Naturally, for the maximum comfort, a modern tourist needs some additional services. After all, it is always unpleasant if the vacation is overshadowed by small household problems. Guests at the Hilton Dubai The Walk Hotel can count on a full service.

For example, dry cleaning and laundry services are available on site. Here you can easily exchange currency, and the exchange rate is sometimes more profitable than at banks. In public areas there is constant wireless Internet access, which, of course, is very important for many travelers. There is no medical office at the hotel, but in case of problems, employees will immediately call you a qualified specialist.

The hotel territory has a parking with a modern security system, which is convenient for people traveling by their own transport. By the way, in the city you can easily rent a car or motorcycle - this facilitates movement around the city and its environs.

It's no secret that people often come to the city not only for relaxation - many travelers have business goals. For them, a business center with cutting-edge equipment is equipped. Guests can use a computer, printer, fax and other equipment. On the territory of two neighboring hotels there are four conference rooms of different capacities with all the necessary video and audio equipment. The hotel is often used for business events, meetings, seminars, training conferences, etc. There is also a separate banquet room, which is used to organize and conduct various celebrations, up to weddings.
