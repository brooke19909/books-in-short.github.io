Shopping in Rhodes - Time Spent Usefully

Do not argue that shopping in Rhodes, of course, loses in importance to the same occupation in the Spanish or Italian resorts, but every lover to go shopping can spend a lot of time in local shopping centers and markets with great benefit.





Rhodes itself has a characteristic feature. It is divided into two different parts: this is the old city, which is surrounded by fortress walls and is called the ‚ÄúCity of the Knights‚Äù, and the new Rhodes, built after the capture of the whole island by Turkish troops.

If you want to buy souvenirs, then you need to pave your way in the old part. Almost all of it (except Ippoton Street) is a large and motley market, located under the open sky. In this wonderful place you can probably buy any souvenir in memory of the beautiful island and the ancient city. T-shirts with the symbols of Rhodes, various statuettes of ancient Greek heroes and gods, beautiful models of ancient weapons, replicas of ships, postcards, books and much more - all this you will find here.





A clear price for a particular product on the island simply does not exist. In every cozy shop you will be able to bargain in order to reduce the cost of products that you like.

If shopping on the island of Rhodes will tire you a little, then do not worry! In the old part of the city there are a large number of various cafes and restaurants. They will help you relax and get a new boost of strength and energy, to continue your shopping in Rhodes and to get a lot of different things and trinkets.

Not far from the old part is the famous city market. With all his looks, he beckons again to arrange shopping in Rhodes. By the way, this place is more reminiscent of the Turkish market, where you can always get the freshest vegetables, fruits, meat and fish.





Immediately after the market itself, trading districts start where you can always find clothes and shoes for yourself. This place is characterized as the center of the so-called New Rhodes. It is worth saying that it is very compact, and you can take a peek at one day in every store in the city. There are several attractive shoe multi-boutiques in the city, where Italian shoes are presented to buyers, pleasant department stores selling both men's and women's clothes, and, of course, a huge variety of small shops with more accurate prices will make shopping in Rhodes an unforgettable experience!

There is, however, one more place where you can stock up on good souvenirs - the village of Lindos. Local craftsmen make exact copies of the beautiful plates of Lindos, which glorified the village throughout Europe in ancient times. Moreover, here you can always buy good jewelry to your taste. You are unlikely to regret it if you shop in Rhodes. The reviews of people who have already visited this beautiful place are extremely positive!
