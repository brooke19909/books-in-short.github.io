Floss double weave

To create such an accessory, you need a couple of threads of different colors. Undoubtedly, this is the simplest bauble made of floss, the rest contain two or more threads of different shades. Sit back and prepare your tools for work.

The first question that you will have is: "How to start a bauble of floss?" Take the ends of the strips and fasten them together.

  • Bend the thread of the first color so that a loop forms.
  • Fold the second in the same way. Now we pass one thread into another.
  • Drag the loop into which the second thread was threaded.
  • According to the principle of operation already described, we fold the loop from the tightened tape and thread it into the already created hole.
  • In the same way, we pull the free edge and fix it.
  • We do the same, repeating actions one by one.
  • We weave a bauble from floss threads until we get the required length.
  • As a result, a beautiful pigtail will begin to form before your eyes.




No one guarantees that you get a good result the first time. You will need repeated training before you knit a bauble made of floss you can beautifully and quickly. Be patient.

So, you have made the necessary length of the braid, the following question arises: "How to fix a bauble from a floss?" In order to fix the result directly on the hand, you will need some tools, depending on the choice of mounting method.

Triple Mouline Weave

How to make a bauble from a mouline thread, for sure everyone already understood. Simple bracelets from two components are easy to weave. But what if you need to make an accessory of three ribbons? The following description will be a triple bauble of floss thread . The patterns of its weaving are quite simple, but, like any others, require some skill.

  • Take three threads of different colors and fix them. The method that describes how to weave a bauble from a mouline thread with two threads will not work for three. In double weaving, the tool and work are held in hands. Three threads are quite difficult to weave in this way. Therefore, you will need a regular pin or paper clip.
  • Fasten the ends of the work sturdy and fix it on a book or similar flat surface. So creating a bracelet will be much easier for you.
  • Weaving will go on the principle of creating a braid. Surely every girl at least once in her life had to deal with braids, so it will not be difficult to create such a fenka.
  • Take the right thread in your hand and put it on the next one, which is on the left. Next, we take the leftmost one and impose it on the next one, which is to the right.
  • Thus, all available strings are intertwined gradually.

Based on the description, you should understand how to braid a bauble from a mouline thread . The length of the created work is selected in accordance with the size of the hands of the future owner.
