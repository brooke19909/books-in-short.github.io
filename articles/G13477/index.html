Holiday Inn Sharjah 5 (UAE / Sharjah)

Description. The modern 14-story building of Holiday Inn Sharjah 5 was built in 1980 in the central part of Sharjah, 10 minutes from the airport. The hotel is well located in relation to the main attractions of the city. Not far from the building are the Oceanarium, large retail stores, the gold market, the National Park, business centers and inexpensive cafes.



Holiday Inn belongs to the Holiday-Inn Hotels & Resorts chain, which is famous for its excellent service. The hotel has everything you need for a quality and relaxed holiday. A shuttle service to and from the airport and city beach is provided. Without even leaving the guesthouse, customers can have fun, actively and have fun: swim in the pool, play various games, watch movies and play sports in the comfortable gym.

License plate fund. The Holiday Inn Sharjah has 5,180 rooms: Suite, Single-Bedded, Double-Bedded. The main part of the rooms with views of the city center. Extra rollaway beds are available. Every day you will find quality service and a change of bedding. It features a personal air conditioning system, satellite TV, free newspapers and the Internet.



It also has a landline telephone, a bar with free mineral water, an electric kettle, dishes, ironing equipment, a coffee machine and a safe. The private bathroom has a bidet, bath amenities, scales and a hairdryer. If desired, customers can order food in the room.

Nutrition. Depending on the length of stay, you can choose the appropriate food system : BB, HB, FB. At Holiday Inn Sharjah 5 buffet food. In the main restaurant, from 6 to 11 in the morning a morning meal is formed. Drinks are not included in the total price. A separate menu is available for young children.

Guests can choose from several restaurants with international, international, Iranian and Mexican cuisines, as well as bars and cafes, which offer a wide range of different dishes, pastries and snacks.



Beach. The public sandy beach is approximately 3 km from Holiday Inn Sharjah 5. Vacationers can get to it by a free bus (round trip). Beach - according to vacationers - clean, sparsely populated and well-groomed. In the presence of beach amenities, showers, water activities and a cafe.

Additional Information. The hotel features a rooftop outdoor pool with city views. The second indoor pool is directly in the building, it is heated. For children, a zone in the adult pool is allocated. Also for children there is a small club where children can have fun and cognitively spend time. To help parents, a professional nanny is offered to look after the child during your absence.

Business travelers will be satisfied with three conference rooms with a maximum capacity of 800 seats. For small groups there are small halls with round tables, it will be convenient and comfortable to hold negotiations and meetings here.

For sports and leisure: fitness center, wellness center, massage room, sauna, tennis court, squash, billiard room. The hotel has parking (free for guests), laundry / dry cleaning department, change office, shops.

Digest. Are you dreaming of a good rest and fruitful work in a pleasant environment, while not overpaying the extra money? Then welcome to the Holiday Inn Sharjah 5. Reviews of this guesthouse are mostly positive. The 50% audience of the hotel consists of business travelers and couples who value coziness, comfort and tranquility.

Hotel guests are satisfied with delicious food: the menu offers various delicacies and desserts, which are difficult to refuse. Tidy rooms are tastefully decorated, each of them has the necessary items and equipment. Positive feedback about the work of the staff is a respectful and friendly address to all customers, especially to children. Every tourist is valued here.
