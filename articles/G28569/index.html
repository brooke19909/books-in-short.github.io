Palmet Resort & SPA Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Kemer)

As we all know well, for quite some time now, Turkey has been one of the favorite places for summer holidays for both Russians and citizens of other states that were part of the former Soviet Union. This country with its many resorts offers hotels for every taste and wallet size, as well as a wide variety of entertainment. Today we decided to learn more about one of the hotel complexes located in the tourist region of Turkey - Kemer, under the name Palmet Resort & SPA Hotel 5 *. We will find out that this hotel offers its dear guests how much it costs to stay in it, and we will also figure out what impressions our compatriots left here.





Where is the hotel Palmet Resort & SPA Hotel

This hotel complex is located on the shores of the azure Mediterranean Sea, seven hundred meters from the center of Kemer. The nearest airport is Antalya Airport, 55 km away. So after arrival, you can reach the hotel in just an hour.

What is the hotel complex "Palmet Resort & SPA"

This hotel is a truly luxurious place for a great holiday for both family people with children of different ages and youth. There are also all conditions for business events: conferences, symposia, meetings, etc. The hotel boasts a unique decoration in the form of drawings and mosaics that were created many centuries ago by the inhabitants of ancient Antalya.

For the first time, Palmet Resort & SPA Hotel 5 * opened its doors to tourists in 2007. The hotel invites you to stay in one of 164 rooms, the vast majority of which have magnificent sea views. The hotel complex covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äã18 thousand square meters.





Posting Rules

According to the internal rules adopted by the Palmet Resort & SPA hotel, newly arriving guests in their rooms are booked after 14:00. If you arrived earlier than the specified hour, then in the event that your room has already been vacated by previous guests and prepared by the maintenance staff, you will be settled immediately. If your room is not ready yet, then you can leave your things in the luggage storage room and go for a walk around the territory, for lunch or straight to the pool or the beach. On the day of departure, you must leave the hotel before noon. Then you need to pay for accommodation, as well as for using additional services (if you, of course, resorted to them). If you have already fully paid the cost of accommodation at the Palmet Resort & SPA Hotel 5 * to a travel agency, then at check out you will only have to pay extra for additional services provided. You can pay at the hotel both in cash and with plastic cards of the Mastercard, Visa or Maestro systems.

Since Palmet Resort & SPA 5 * is for the most part family-friendly, children of all ages are allowed here. In this case, if in your room you put a child under six years old on an existing bed, then you will not have to pay extra for its placement. Also, if you place a baby up to two years old in a crib, it will also be free. Please note that the maximum number of extra beds in a room is one or two cribs. The need to provide this service must be notified to Palmet Resort & SPA in advance.

As for the placement of guests traveling in the company of pets at the hotel, we are forced to disappoint them. So, accommodation in the Palmet Resort 5 * guests with four-legged pets is not allowed.





Number of rooms

The room base of the hotel complex "Palmet Resort & SPA 5 *" is represented by 164 comfortable rooms. At the same time, 132 rooms offer magnificent views of the sea and the pool. The windows of the remaining 32 rooms overlook the hotel‚Äôs parkland. 143 rooms from the total number are standard (the area is 30 square meters) and are designed to accommodate 2 (+ 1) guests. The hotel also has 12 family rooms (50 square meters, two rooms, can accommodate up to 2 + 2 people) and 9 family suites (72 square meters, two rooms, can accommodate up to 2 + 2 guests).

Each room has a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer, central air conditioning, satellite TV with two channels broadcasting in Russian, safe, telephone, minibar and balcony. All rooms have carpeted floors. Round the clock you have the opportunity to order room service. Cleaning is done daily, and linen is changed three times a week.

Nutrition

Dining at Palmet Resort (Kemer) is all-inclusive. So, experienced chefs in the main restaurant of the hotel serve a buffet for breakfast (from 7:30 to 10 am), lunch (from 12:30 to 14:00) and dinner (from 7 to 9 pm). Here you can choose from a wide variety of dishes, both Turkish and international cuisine, as well as taste delicious desserts and fresh vegetables.

In addition, the hotel "Palmet Resort & SPA 5 *" has a √† la carte restaurant. Here you can enjoy delicious Italian and Turkish dishes, as well as sushi and other seafood dishes. At the same time, it is possible to book a table with a view of the sea, pond or pool.

Also on the territory of the hotel complex Palmet Resort Hotel 5 * there are several bars:

  • a snack bar located on the beach and offering hotel guests snacks, salads and sweets;
  • Lobby bar, where you can taste local and foreign drinks;
  • pool bar where you can refresh yourself with your favorite drinks throughout the day;
  • Mare Nastrum bar, offering hotel guests to enjoy various drinks of both Turkish and foreign production in a special, "elite" atmosphere;
  • vitamin bar, which offers a variety of fruits, juices and teas.




Sea beach

The hotel complex Palmet Resort 5 * has its own beach. Here the hotel guests are provided with sun loungers, umbrellas, mattresses, as well as everything necessary for water sports. The beach itself is pebble, and the entry into the water is gentle.

Infrastructure

The hotel "Palmet Resort & SPA" (Kemer) has all the conditions for a comfortable stay of guests. For example, the reception here is open 24 hours a day without interruptions. Therefore, if you arrive at the hotel late, or if you have a question that needs to be resolved immediately, you always have the opportunity to contact the reception, where the on-duty administrator will help to resolve all problems.

The complex "Palmet Resort & SPA 5 *" has ample parking. Therefore, if you arrive at the Palmet Resort by your own car or rent a car already in Turkey, then you will not have any difficulties with parking.

In addition, in the hotel complex you can use the safe at the reception, exchange currency, rent a car, buy souvenirs and everything you need to relax in the shops, update your hair style, visit the hairdresser, order room service, and connect to wireless Internet. The hotel also has a medical office where a qualified doctor is always ready to provide medical assistance if necessary.

In addition, the hotel complex Palmet Resort 5 * has a conference room for 100 people and equipped with equipment necessary for seminars, conferences, meetings and other business events.





Entertainment and sports

In addition to swimming in pools and relaxing on the beach, at the Palmet Resort & SPA you can play billiards, darts, beach volleyball, engage in motorized and non-motorized water sports, take several scuba diving lessons, attend a gym or classes in aerobics.

If you want to relax, then head to the SPA center, sauna, Turkish bath (hammam), beauty parlor or for a massage.

For the youngest guests of the hotel there is a children's club, as well as a playground, a mini pool, children's animation and a mini disco. In addition, for a fee you can use the services of a nanny.

Cost of living

As for the cost of living in a hotel, it cannot be called low. So, a five-day accommodation for two at the peak of the season will cost you in the amount of 70 thousand rubles.





Palmet Resort & SPA Hotel 5 *: tourist comments

It is no secret that for many travelers one of the most important factors when choosing a hotel is the presence of positive comments from people who have already visited it. Therefore, we decided to summarize a number of reviews of our compatriots who stayed during their holidays in the hotel complex "Palmet Resort & SPA" in Kemer (Turkey).

So, as for the rooms, then all the tourists agreed that they are decorated very well, and the furniture and sanitary ware in them are new and in good condition. The rooms are spacious and bright, and the furnishings are new and stylish. Bed linens were always fresh and clean. Many note the excellent view from the window. There were no complaints about cleaning the rooms, as it was carried out daily, and the minibar was always filled. Bed linen was also changed regularly every day, and towels - as needed, so that they were always clean. Toiletries (shampoos, soap, etc.) were regularly replenished. At the same time, quite often the cleaners made various cute figures on the beds for the guests. Moreover, as the guests noticed, the hotel staff pleased the guests in a similar way, regardless of whether they left a tip or not.

The only thing that caused a number of complaints from our compatriots was the condition of the air conditioners in the rooms. Often they worked weakly and could not cope with their function, and therefore the rooms were stuffy and excessively humid. Therefore, guests had to complain about the reception and call the master. According to many tourists, a five-star hotel should pay more attention to such things.

The design of the Palmet Resort Hotel 5 * itself was appreciated by many travelers. So, guests especially remembered the beautiful fountains, antique statues at the entrance, as well as an amphitheater decorated in the same style, where entertainment events took place in the evenings.

However, a number of complaints from guests this year was caused by problems with checking into a hotel. So, upon arrival at the Palmet Resort 5 * hotel, some of our compatriots were informed that, despite the paid reservation they had in their hands, there were no free rooms in the hotel complex. Of course, tourists were not enthusiastic about such news. However, to the credit of the hotel administration, it should be recognized that travelers for one night were also provided with accommodation in a good hotel. The next day they were accommodated in booked rooms at Palmet Resort & SPA. They were also given a large basket of fruit and wine, as well as the possibility of using the safe in the room for free. Nevertheless, this situation left an unpleasant impression on tourists, especially those who arrived on vacation with young children.

Many guests were satisfied with the territory of the hotel complex Palmet Resort 5 *. It is medium in size, very clean and green. There are many great places for walking and photo shoots. Also, most tourists appreciated the location of the hotel. After all, the center of Kemer can be reached on foot in just 15 minutes, and near the hotel there are a lot of shops, shops, bars, nightclubs, etc.





An exceptionally favorable impression was also left by the staff of the Palmet Resort & SPA hotel. All the staff here are very friendly, helpful and quick. In restaurants, waiters always worked very quickly: dirty dishes were removed from the tables almost instantly, and tablecloths were replaced with clean ones. The bartenders also worked great. The only minor drawback, some guests considered the fact that few of the staff understand the Russian language. However, they all speak excellent English, therefore, remembering the elementary school curriculum, it is quite possible to explain.

Tourists really liked the beach of the hotel Palmet Resort SPA 5 *. It is quite wide and clean, there is a pier and a convenient entry into the water. Despite the pebble beach, the pebbles are small, so there is no inconvenience. Sun beds are new and comfortable. Also, there is always a sufficient number of sun loungers and umbrellas for all hotel guests. The sea is clear, azure, very warm and pleasant. Tourists also noticed that several lifeguards are constantly on the beach, so you can not worry about your safety in the water.

As for food at Palmet Resort & SPA, here the opinions of vacationers diverged. So, part of the tourists found the food offered to them at the hotel quite tasty and varied, as well as very beautifully decorated. They were satisfied with the quality and selection of soups, side dishes, salads, as well as traditional Turkish sweets. Also satisfied with the quality of food, guests celebrate delicious meat and fish that the chefs at the Palmet Resort Hotel (Kemer) grilled. In addition, many appreciated even once a week evening Turkish cuisine with a large selection of national dishes. The variety of fruits, which were always fresh and well washed, also pleased the guests. They also liked the fact that waiters and bartenders poured wine here from bottles, and cocktails are made exclusively from quality ingredients.

Our other compatriots were dissatisfied with the choice of dishes. In their opinion, a five-star hotel should offer guests a greater variety of meat dishes, as well as fruits and drinks. However, be that as it may, no one remained hungry at Palmet Resort & SPA.

As for the √† la carte fish restaurant, many celebrate its excellent dishes, its good location on the seashore, as well as a pleasant and romantic atmosphere. Also, without exception, all tourists respond positively to snack bars, where you can have a bite to eat in between main meals. Almost everyone liked the pizza, Turkish flatbread, sandwiches, french fries that they ordered here.

Not all tourists were satisfied with the animation at the Palmet Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *. So, according to some tourists, it was mainly designed for children. Nevertheless, another part of tourists assures that throughout the day you could play boccia, volleyball, water polo and do aerobics. In the evenings, on the territory of the hotel in the amphitheater various funny shows with funny contests and incendiary discos were regularly held.

In general, most of our compatriots, who have ever spent their holidays in the hotel complex "Palmet Resort & Spa", agreed that the hotel is good and quite consistent with its value. Many of the tourists are ready to return here again as part of a new trip to Turkey and will advise the Palmet Resort Hotel 5 * to their friends and relatives.
