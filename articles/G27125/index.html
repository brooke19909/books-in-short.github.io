Guest house "Santa Barbara", Adler: description, service, reviews

In the life of every person there comes a period when everything seems gray and mundane, and the routine is swallowed up with the head. It is at this moment that it is worth pausing and giving the body time to "reboot". Nothing invigorates a person, does not give a greater surge of energy, like relaxation. The noise of the sea and the sun can make us feel carefree and independent of all problems. An ideal option for such a vacation is Adler - one of the urban areas of Sochi. Despite the colorful city and its cultural and entertainment program, first of all, the vacationer is interested in the conditions in which he will live. Against the backdrop of a huge number of hotels, it is worth highlighting the guest house "Santa Barbara" in Adler. With his incredibly beautiful appearance, he makes you fall in love with yourself at first sight.

Location

Santa Barbara Guest House is very well located in relation to the main infrastructures. If you go 10-12 minutes, you can see the beach, which is especially popular with both locals and vacationers. The house is also located just a 10-minute walk from the train station in Adler. The local beach is pebble. Its length is about 15 km, which allows all tourists to comfortably swim, sunbathe and enjoy their vacation. Address of Santa Barbara Guest House: Adler, 24 Medova Street.

Sochi airport is located in the city, so tourists can easily get here even from remote regions of the country. If travelers are tired of the monotony of this area, then you can easily go on an excursion to Sochi (distance is 30 km). The main advantage is that from the train station in Adler the staff of the guest house picks up vacationers and takes them to their destination in comfort (however, for a fee).

You can get to the city center by electric trains, pleasing with their exact schedule, or by inexpensive buses that operate without a specific schedule. Departure takes place from Sochi airport every 20-40 minutes. Minibus taxi number 105 will quickly and safely deliver tourists to the bus station of the city.

Number of rooms

The impressions of the Santa Barbara Guest House in Adler depend not only on a good location, good food and a lot of entertainment, but also on the room and its arrangement. The hotel provides a wide selection of apartments with amenities: there are double, triple and quadruple rooms. Room justifies its price. The interior at first glance may seem modest, but it is quite pleasant.

The rooms have everything you need, namely:

  • Wi-Fi
  • TV;
  • air conditioning;
  • bathroom;
  • round-the-clock water supply.

The rooms are spacious, sunny and very comfortable. A hairdryer and iron can be requested at the reception, where you will be given a courtesy at any time of the day. Simplicity and comfort bribe visitors to the Santa Barbara guest house in Adler, and the view from the window to the courtyard, surrounded by many exotic plants, can not leave indifferent any guest who has been there.

Prices

The pricing policy for Santa Barbara Guesthouse in Adler depends on the month of vacation. If we talk about the beginning of the season, then in May prices are pleasing for their democracy: a double room with all amenities will cost tourists about 1000-1200 rubles, a triple room - 1500-1650 rubles. If you come here with a big company, you can safely take four-bed apartments, the price of which is 3800-4000 rubles. In high season, the cost of living increases, but usually does not exceed 5000 rubles. It is also worth noting that prices depend on breakfast, lunch and dinner, which are included at an additional cost. The cost of such a vacation may seem great to some, but everyone knows that you need to pay for quality service.

Service

Each of us on vacation wants to relax, throw all the problems overboard and enjoy a fabulous vacation. The service of the Santa Barbara guesthouse in Adler helps in this, as it provides a huge list of services that will help make your stay here as comfortable as possible. It works around the clock, as tourists are always welcome here. Young visitors under 5 years old stay free of charge, but without a separate seat. Children from 5-12 years old can check in with a 30% discount. The depth of the pool, which is located on the territory of the guest house, is designed for both adults and toddlers.

The service of the guest house "Santa Barbara" offers an exclusive barbecue, which will be prepared quickly and very tasty. Reviews about it only increase your appetite, since tourists who have tasted this dish are delighted. Cleaning takes place regularly, bed linen is changed every 5 days. An additional advantage is the presence of a store in the house where you can buy everything you need and not waste time traveling to the supermarket.

Entertainment

"Santa Barbara" is a place where you can spend time calmly or actively relax. On the basis of the guest house there is a barbecue, which is accessible to all guests. Therefore, here you can arrange picnics in a pleasant atmosphere. If you want to learn more about the surroundings or enjoy the sights, the hotel offers various excursions.

Near the pool there are sun loungers that are in great demand among the guests. Since Adler is a seaside resort, there are many water activities here. Nearby there is a water park, a dolphinarium, so children will definitely not be bored. In the evening, you can enjoy a glass of wine in the restaurant, which is located next to the guest house.

Reviews

When people choose a place to stay, they usually trust the previous guests who have already lived there. Reviews for the Santa Barbara Guest House (Adler) are mostly positive. Tourists are satisfied with the service, prices, interior. You can hear a lot of good words about comfortable rooms with a beautiful view, possibilities for relaxing with children, a lot of entertainment and pastime options.

You can not ignore the food, which is an important part of quality vacation. Guests are satisfied with the quality of the prepared dishes. The atmosphere prevailing in the hotel makes you feel at home, and the care and attention with which guests are surrounded cannot leave indifferent any heart.
