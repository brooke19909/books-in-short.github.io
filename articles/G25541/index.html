How to install a motion sensor with a lamp. LED lamp with motion sensor

The motion sensor with a lamp is used to turn on or off the light in a certain period of time. As a rule, such devices are installed in the dark. The sensor is triggered when a moving object appears in the set zone. The operation zone is set using a special control device. The motion sensor with a lamp is a completely new and economical equipment. It also serves to improve the level of lighting not only on the streets, but also indoors.

General information

In this equipment, you can use not only standard incandescent lamps, but also almost any other kind. In some cases, a motion sensor with a lamp should not be installed.

For example, it is not mounted in front of the fan, as it will trigger from the movement of the blades or on the carriageway, where automobile vehicles passing by will be an irritant. For more accurate work, there should not be any building structures, trees, engineering devices for heating and ventilation near it.

LED lamp with motion sensor

It is one of the most economical sources of lighting. This has been proven thanks to many studies. This lamp with a motion sensor is a profitable union. Today, there are many devices that serve to illuminate the street or premises when a person appears. The system turns on in the dark and is able to control the lighting power. LED lamps are equipped with a dimmer. This device is used to create comfort indoors. That is, energy saving is not its advantage. A significant amount of energy is required for functioning. Another thing is the combination of a lamp with a sensor.





A LED lamp with a motion sensor consumes much less electricity, as it turns on and off for a certain time. The main place of application is large industrial enterprises. The device is also installed in the entrances of apartment buildings, since one motion sensor can be connected to a large number of lamps. In addition, the installation of such equipment is quite simple. Many studies have proven that the use of a motion sensor with LED lamps is the best option.

Advantages and disadvantages

Among the positive aspects, it is worth highlighting lower energy consumption than other lamps, as well as small overall dimensions. I must say that the power of the LED element is an order of magnitude higher than incandescent lamps almost 7 times. Many users note the high cost of LED devices. That is why they are not very profitable to use. But this drawback is not significant.

Energy-saving lamps with motion sensor

This type of device got its name due to the competent combination of power consumption and light flux. Similar elements are designed to conserve electricity. In addition, their service life is much higher than that of other lamps. They are used in large industrial enterprises, where it is necessary to achieve less electricity consumption. But this does not end its scope. An energy-saving lamp with a motion sensor is used to illuminate streets, porches and other rooms.

Advantages and disadvantages of energy-saving lamps

Key benefits include:

  • uniform distribution of light;
  • smooth on and off;
  • various color executions;
  • the main part of the lamp does not heat up.




The only drawback is the failure with frequent on or off. Therefore, before using the device with the sensor, it is necessary to select the appropriate energy saving.

Connection

A motion sensor with a lamp is connected to a conventional network, like a switch. That is, the network closes automatically. Sometimes an additional switch is mounted in the circuit. This is done if it is necessary to ensure the inclusion of light for a certain time. The principle of operation is the parallel operation of the sensor.

If it is necessary to provide light to a large room, then several motion controllers are used. At the same time, they should be on opposite sides of the room, but ensure that all lamps work. In order not to cause a short circuit, it is necessary to work from one phase. A magnetic switch can be included in this circuit. It serves to illuminate long streets. That is, the operation of the entire street depends on one sensor.

Devices with halogen lamps

The advantages of such lamps is the high brightness of the light with the directional movement of the light flux. They are not energy efficient. The only thing you can save on is on their replacement, since they are characterized by durability. Halogen elements are produced in various designs. The most popular model is a linear halogen lamp. To install it requires a lot of space.





As a rule, the installation of such devices is carried out under the ceiling. They are not subject to mechanical stress. The main scope is street. They can not be installed indoors, as high brightness will interfere with any action.

How to connect?

Connecting a lamp with a motion sensor does not take much time and money. The devices are connected to a regular household electrical network with a voltage of 220 V. There are also battery-powered models. The serial connection diagram is indicated on the box or on the device itself. So, there are two connection sockets on the sensor - phase and zero, which are indicated by the letters L and N, respectively. The luminaire with the lamp itself is connected to L. For the device to turn on, it is necessary to supply current to these connectors. The wires are interconnected by terminals.





If you make a mistake in connecting, the motion sensor will not break. It just won't function. The wire should be so long that it reaches the nearest power source. If there is no outlet nearby, you can use the carry, while the performance of the device will not change.

Device check

Modern appliances have a special indicator. It lights up when the sensor is powered by the mains. If the indicator does not work after applying voltage, then this indicates a breakdown of the sensor or supply socket. There are models that begin to function after 30 seconds. During this time, you can check the health of the equipment without fully connecting it to the mains.

Customization

The regulation of the movement of the motion sensor with a lamp occurs using a special toggle switch, which is located on the body. Depending on the model, there may be a different number of them. For ease of adjustment, the setting symbols are located near the toggle switch. As a rule, these are letter indicators. On the case there is an arrow showing the direction of rotation of the toggle switch.





A parameter such as lighting power must be selected before installation. This is because the sensor is installed under the ceiling, and it is rather difficult to carry out further changes in the indicators of illumination.
