The interiors of the hotel complex are designed in a classic style. Historically luxuriously decorated suites are made in the best traditions of pre-revolutionary St. Petersburg. Hotel "Galunov"

Among the best hotels in the city, it is worth highlighting the Galunov complex, located a seven-minute walk from Uprising Square. Despite the fact that only four stars flaunt on the signboard of the establishment, its rating according to the "Bucking" version is 9.7. The hotel captivates guests with a warm, homely atmosphere. It is located within the walls of a historic building. Once upon a time in this area was the Old Alexander Market. In 1880, during the construction work on the trading area, a tenement house was built by order of the merchant of the first guild Galunov. The construction was carried out on a grand scale.

The new building occupied the entire block. All work on the construction of the house was completed in twenty years. Living in such apartments was truly prestigious, because the rooms were decorated with German fireplaces, carpets were in the front door, and a doorman was always on duty on the stairs. The house even had a bathroom and toilet. Filippovskaya bakery was a landmark of the quarter, from which bread was famous throughout the city. After many decades, the apartment building has turned into one of the best hotels in the city, offering beautiful rooms and homely warm service.

Solo Sokos Hotel Palace Bridge

Solo Sokos Hotel Palace Bridge is another hotel on the list of the best places in the city. It is located on Vasilyevsky Island, just 15 minutes from Nevsky Prospekt and the Hermitage. The advantage of the establishment is its convenient location and the availability of its own spa center. Hotel guests can visit the indoor pool, sauna complex and fitness club at any time. The spacious apartments of the hotel are equipped with modern appliances, and the bathrooms are decorated with marble. The hotel has cafes and restaurants. The establishment will be an excellent choice for those guests who appreciate spa resorts.

The praise of tourists about the hotel allows him to recommend it to guests of the Northern capital. According to the version of the Bucking website, the rating of the hotel complex is 8.8 points.

Hotel Kempinski Moika 22

The beautiful Kempinski Hotel Moika 22 is located in the heart of the city, on the embankment of the Moika River. Opposite the hotel are such famous sights as the Hermitage and Palace Square. The institution is located just five minutes walk from the iconic places of the city and Nevsky Prospect. The friendly hotel staff is always happy guests. The room fund of the institution is represented by 200 apartments, among which there are luxurious suites.

The hotel invites guests to have a good time and relax while rejuvenating in the fitness and spa center. Jacuzzi, saunas and face and body care programs allow you to put yourself in order. According to tourists' reviews, it is a good spa that distinguishes the institution from other hotels in the city. Especially highly appreciated by the guests are the beautiful rooms of the complex, made in a marine style with golden accents. According to the version of the Bucking website, the hotel rating is 9.6 points, which is the best characteristic for an institution.

Mini-hotel "Big 19"
