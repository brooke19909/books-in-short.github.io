How to get by train from Kiev to Adler?

The approximate distance between Kiev and Adler is 1416 km. This is a fairly large number. Therefore, the optimal vehicle for overcoming it is the train. What features does the route have in this direction? The answer to this question can be obtained by reading the article.





Why exactly the train?

Why is it recommended to move by train from Adler to Kiev? There are several main advantages of this vehicle:

  • The road between these two points promises to be picturesque. During the trip, you can watch from the window for a unique and extraordinary nature.
  • There is an opportunity to save. After all, the cost of buying tickets for this vehicle will be lower compared to an airplane or the cost of fuel for a car.
  • It is very convenient to ride with the whole family. After all, you can redeem a whole coupe and fully enjoy the trip.
  • The train is quite spacious. There is no need to sit all the way in one position, which can negatively affect your health.
  • There are favorable discounts for the preferential category of citizens.

It makes sense to choose this particular vehicle when it is necessary to overcome a long distance, for example, from Kiev to Adler.

Negative moments

However, there are disadvantages:

  • In the summer, there is a high flow of tourists, respectively, there may be problems with buying a ticket for this type of vehicle.
  • This is the longest route from Kiev to Adler.
  • On some flights there is a low level of service, which causes hostility among many passengers.

Possible travel options

There are several routes for the Kiev - Adler train, in each version the starting point is the Kiev-Passenger station, and the final Central station of the southern city of Russia.

  • The fastest way to overcome this distance is to make a transplant in Moscow. The total travel time, therefore, will take only 2 days 3 hours and 11 minutes. Exactly at midnight, the departure of flight number 090K. You will also need to make a transplant at 471M. It is worth noting that these are branded compounds equipped with air conditioning, high-quality sanitary facilities and all the amenities for travelers.
  • Two trains numbered 047 and 094 move in the same direction with a change in Bryansk. Many tourists prefer this route because of the convenience, as you will have to make a short transfer at only one point. The flight departs at 3:19, the total travel time is 2 days 4 hours and 55 minutes.
  • The longest train follows at number 302B, through the city of Zhlobin. It will take you 3 days 11 hours and 16 minutes to get there. Departure is at 2 in the morning.

As it turned out, almost all kinds of Kiev-Adler trains leave at night, that is, the passenger can sleep the first part of the way. Unfortunately, there is no direct flight between these two points. You can only take advantage of the option of a transfer in one of three cities.

About ticket purchase





It is recommended to buy tickets in advance in this direction at the box office at least 1 month before the expected date of travel. Otherwise, tickets may not be enough. You can pre-book them on the official website, but this method is not reliable. The approximate ticket price per person is from 4,300 rubles. This figure can vary significantly depending on the type of train, place in the composition and period of travel. It is worth considering that in summer prices are much lower. There are discounts for preferential categories of citizens. For example, if a child under 10 years old does not need extra bed, then he can travel accompanied by his parent absolutely free. You can buy for him a separate seat in the car at a reduced price.

Alternatives

In addition to the train, the same distance can be overcome by plane. This will significantly save time. Approximately a flight lasts no more than 10 hours. It is worth noting that the departure of the flight is carried out only 1 time per week. Therefore, buying a ticket for him is quite problematic. There are several options with transfers. This is the most expensive way to travel. The minimum ticket price per person will be 11,450 rubles.





You can overcome this path by car in 20 hours and 55 minutes. It includes many forks. It is recommended to bring a navigator with you. A free option is cycling or walking. But it is unlikely that anyone would venture to cover a considerable distance in this way.

Unfortunately, there are no direct bus services in this direction, and it is quite difficult to choose an option with transfers.





Many people on vacation plan to make a global move from Kiev to Adler. What is the best way to do this? Of course, by train. This vehicle is the most comfortable, safe and cheapest. If desired, you can use other options.
