Hotel "StandArt" (Moscow): reviews, address and photo

Design hotel "StandArt" offers its guests to stay in luxurious apartments. In addition, a huge range of services is available to guests here.





Brief description of the hotel

If you are accustomed to a luxurious vacation, the hotel "StandArt" will provide you with this opportunity. Here you can enjoy not only a luxurious interior, but also such popular services as a Russian bath or a traditional Turkish hammam. Guests will also have the opportunity to swim in the indoor pool or have a massage.

Particularly noteworthy are the rooms that the StandardArt hotel offers its guests. Each of them is a unique design development, which will provide not only a high level of comfort, but also aesthetic pleasure. In addition, it is worth noting that all apartments have a full list of amenities.

Please note that the hotel "StandArt" provides guests with the opportunity to fully eat. It serves dishes of different peoples of the world, which can be tasted not only in the restaurant, but also make an order directly in the room. It will be good news for you that the chef of this institution has twice been awarded a Michelin star.





Hotel location

This hotel is located in the very center of Moscow. After walking for a quarter of an hour, you will find yourself in the famous Red Square. Also within easy reach are famous attractions such as the Eliseevsky department store and Pushkin Square. The address of the hotel "StandArt" - 2 Strastnoy Boulevard .





Hotel Rooms

If you are accustomed to luxury and comfort, then pay attention to such an option of accommodation as "StandArt" (hotel, Moscow). Here you can find apartments of the following categories:

  • Classic rooms are designed to accommodate two guests in 2 single beds or one large. These apartments overlook the courtyard. It is worth noting that, in addition to the sleeping area, there is also a working corner and a cosmetic table.
  • Deluxe apartments, like the previous ones, can accommodate two guests in two single or one large bed. A rather interesting design decision was the mirror, which with the click of a button turns into a TV. As for the view from the window, you can choose the option with a city landscape or facing the courtyard.
  • Executive room category "Suite" consists of two spacious areas, one of which is provided under the bedroom, and the second - under the living room. The large panoramic window offers incredibly beautiful views of the city.




The amenities provided in the apartment

If you like a comfortable stay, then you will certainly be pleased with the wide range of amenities provided by our guests "Standard" (hotel, Moscow). It is about the following:

  • Individual air conditioning system to create a comfortable microclimate in the rooms.
  • Reliable safe with electronic combination lock, which will ensure the safety of your belongings.
  • In the bathroom, in addition to the bath or shower (depending on the room category), terry bathrobes, slippers, a set of towels, cosmetic sets and an electric hairdryer are also provided.
  • The TV is connected to a satellite dish with many channels (there are also paid programs).
  • A desktop that can also be used as a cosmetic (there is a large mirror).
  • A carpet is laid on the floor, thanks to which you can walk barefoot without feeling the cold.
  • Convenient handrails are installed near the bathroom, which are responsible for comfort and safety.
  • There is free wireless Internet, as well as a cable network for desktop computers.
  • A minibar is filled daily with bottled drinking water.
  • Each room has a folding bed, which is used to accommodate additional guests.
  • A landline telephone, which serves both to communicate with the reception and to make calls to other cities.
  • Large wardrobe.




Hotel Infrastructure

Many travelers choose the hotel "StandArt" (Moscow) for their stay . Employee reviews note that the following services are most popular:

  • Small indoor pool.
  • Gym with the most modern equipment.
  • Wellness center where you can undergo spa treatments for beauty and well-being.
  • To relieve emotional stress, as well as muscle fatigue, visit the Turkish hammam or traditional Russian bath.
  • You can use the wireless Internet throughout the territory for free.
  • The restaurant offers the best international cuisine.
  • The reception desk is open around the clock to check in and check in, as well as advising guests.
  • In the laundry, your clothes are washed and ironed as soon as possible.
  • A conference room is provided for business events and seminars.
  • You can check in your luggage to the left luggage office.
  • Your holidays and celebrations will be held at the highest level in the banquet hall of the hotel.
  • For an additional fee, you can order a shuttle service to the desired communication center.
  • An elevator will comfortably deliver you to the upper floors.




Positive reviews

One of the best accommodation options in the capital is "StandArt" (hotel, Moscow). Reviews about this place contain the following positive comments:

  • very beautiful design of the bathroom, as well as new and high-quality plumbing;
  • high-quality cosmetics in an individual disposable set;
  • comfortable large bed with high-quality orthopedic mattress;
  • convenient location in the central part of the city;
  • excellent cuisine in the restaurant;
  • the rooms are very large and incredibly beautifully decorated;
  • beautiful city views from the windows of the rooms, which are located on the upper floors.

Negative reviews

Not only positive, but also negative comments are characterized by the hotel "StandArt". Reviews of it contain the following negative comments:

  • the smell of chemicals in the corridors (apparently it is poison for mice or insects);
  • there are no handles on the windows, and therefore they do not open;
  • road works are constantly carried out near the hotel, which greatly complicates the access;
  • the staff is too loose and cheeky in relation to the guests;
  • breakfasts are too expensive, it is much cheaper to eat in neighboring establishments;
  • despite the fact that the booking indicates a late check out, it is refused even if there are free rooms;
  • the smell of cigarettes in the rooms.

conclusions

If you have developed a habit of luxury and comfort, then, arriving in Moscow, feel free to choose the "StandArt" hotel for your accommodation. Its main feature is the original design of the apartments. In addition, due to their spacious area, they accommodate a large number of amenities. As for breakfast, it is better to take a room without it, because its cost is quite large, and the quality is no better than in any other nearby institution.

The high cost of living at the hotel is due not only to the luxurious design, but also to its location in the central part of the city. Nevertheless, if we talk about the service and the level of service, then there are some questions. It cannot but please a huge list of quality services for a good rest. But speaking directly about employees, they lack knowledge regarding communication with guests.
