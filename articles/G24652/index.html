Hotel PalumboKendwa (Tanzania, Kendwa): photos and reviews of tourists

What is Zanzibar: archipelago, island or city? And the first, and second, and third! Zanzibar is an archipelago belonging to the African state of Tanzania and lying just 35 kilometers from its mainland coast. Washed by the warm Indian Ocean, these paradise islands have a tropical climate and are ready to receive tourists all year round. But travelers from Russia have only recently discovered this country.

The archipelago consists of a whole placer of islands, but only two of them are large enough: Unguja and Pemba. The first is often called Zanzibar. On this island is the capital of the archipelago. This is the "Stone City", Stone Town, which is often called Zanzibar. The tourism industry has long flourished on the island of Unguja. Five-star hotels lined up on the coast.

According to its characteristics, the slightly smaller island of Pemba is in no way inferior to Unguja (Zanzibar). But the tourism industry there is still underdeveloped. Pemba attracts romantically inclined travelers who long for unity with nature. This does not mean that there are no good hotels. But still, most tourists prefer Zanzibar hotels. In this article we will talk about one of them - PalumboKendwa.





Zanzibar Island: holiday features

Reviews complain that in Zanzbar, that is, Unguja, you will no longer see "real Africa." Of course, the white beaches are clean, the hotels are gorgeous, the underwater world is amazing with a variety of species, but this surroundings are indistinguishable from the resorts of Costa Azahar, Maldives, Dominican Republic and similar places. But on the northern island of the archipelago, Pemba, preserved African exoticism. Impenetrable jungle, real, not theatrical villages of the natives, pristine nature and rich underwater world. But the tourism industry is in its infancy.

And the city of Kendwa in the north of Zanzibar Island combines all the advantages of Unguji and Pemba. It is in this city that the PalumboKendwa hotel we are describing is located. The most beautiful resort Kenva can boast of is the beach of the same name. He is considered the best in the entire archipelago. Naturally, people go to Kendwa beyond the beach and the sea. By the way, the ebbs and flows here are minimal and almost imperceptible.

At the hotel, vacationers will find a small shop with all sorts of things, a restaurant and a bar. The entrance to the hotel is carefully guarded. Tourists rated the hotel grounds as excellent.





Where holidaymakers settle

The three-story building of the PalumboKendwa Hotel, although large in appearance, contains only 67 rooms. They, in addition to spaciousness (two, three or four people), have subdivisions into categories. This is a standard (balcony overlooks the garden), sea view (sea view), junior suite and suite. Each room has a covered balcony with a coffee table and two armchairs. Double rooms have wide beds. The bedrooms for more guests are complemented by sofas. All these lodges are equipped with beautiful canopies or canopies from mosquitoes. In general, the rooms, if we consider the photographs taken by vacationers, are very original, stylishly decorated with elements of African aesthetics. The bathrooms are also beautiful. Hot water, unlike other Zanzibar hotels, is available around the clock in Palumbo Kendva. Reviews claim the rooms are spacious. Tourists recommend that when checking in, ask for rooms on the upper floors, since on the first they are dark.





What's in the rooms

Old reviews of the PalumboKendwa hotel (Zanzibar) indicate that fans are struggling with African heat. But after renovation, all hotel rooms have acquired a new generation of air conditioners. They are very effective and silent. A TV with satellite channels is provided for entertainment. In order not to go down to the reception desk to solve various issues, there is a telephone. There is also a minibar in the room. He, prompt reviews, can serve as a refrigerator. But the filling of the minibar is paid, with the exception of bottled drinking water. An electric kettle is attached that reviews are highly praised. The room has a safe, spacious enough to accommodate a laptop. In bathrooms, vacationers will find a hairdryer. Cosmetic and toiletries are replenished during cleaning, once a day. The maids do their job just fine. Many reviews praise the hotel for the cleanliness of the rooms and grounds.

To be honest, the PalumboKendwa hotel is located far from the sea - five hundred meters. You will have to walk to the hotel‚Äôs own beach on foot through the village of Kendva for about fifteen minutes. Therefore, the hotel provides a free shuttle service. Pool

Hotel infrastructure PalumboKendwa, Zanzibar

This hotel carries four stars on its sign. Of course, in Tanzania this is a very conventional designation. But reviews say that the hotel administration clearly did not pull off the ceiling and did not suck out a finger. Hotel services are fully consistent with its category. The reception desk is open around the clock, and its staff speak English and Italian. One of the hotel managers can talk with Slavic tourists in Ukrainian. The hotel has a shared lounge with a library. There is also: a gift shop, currency exchange, car rental office, tour desk. Transfer to the beach is free, anywhere in the island - for money. What shocked tourists most about the hotel was wifi. It pulls everywhere, in the rooms inclusive, and it is free. If one of the guests has a birthday, the staff bakes a cake and gives it to the birthday boy with congratulations.

Entertainment

There is nothing special to do in the small town of Kendwa. All entertainments are somehow connected with the sea. Diving, snorkeling, sea fishing, walking on the famous Nangwi beach (five kilometers) - this is all that you can do in the daylight. Excursions to Stone Town, a spice plantation, an island of giant turtles, and the unique Hosani jungle are also offered. But their reviews are recommended to be ordered from local, and not from your tour operator. So it will be cheaper at times. As for entertainment after sunset, they are all - only on the territory of the PalumboKendwa hotel (Zanzibar). Animators (both for adults and for children) work during the day. But since at this time everything is on the beach, and almost no one is by the pool, their activity is hardly visible. But in the evening the guys give all their might. In addition, the administration often invites professional artists. Tourists mention Masai dances, gymnasts, fire shows, etc.
