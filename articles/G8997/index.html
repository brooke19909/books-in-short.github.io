Why is it impossible to reproduce in space animals and people?

Why is it impossible to reproduce in space? This question has worried scientists for many decades. What is the reason? There have been many studies that have confirmed that reproduction in space is impossible. Of course, conception can happen. But will the baby be born healthy?



First research

So why is it impossible to reproduce in space? The first studies were conducted back in the days of the USSR. The first participants in the experiments were rats. It so happened that these animals became pioneers. Rats that became pregnant in space gave rise to viable offspring. However, adults died very quickly after the cubs were born. Returning to Earth, the cubs experienced certain difficulties. First of all, the cubs could not move normally, since they were formed in zero gravity.

Many animals have traveled to space. It just so happened that monkeys and dogs saw our planet from orbit much earlier than humans. The French sent even cats into space. The first animals that circled the moon were turtles.

All experiments were conducted to get answers to many questions. And this concerned not only the possibility of flying in zero gravity. The experiments were supposed to answer the question of why reproduction in space is impossible.

Radiation in space

NASA experts conducted many tests and proved that it is impossible to reproduce in space. Animals that were conceived in zero gravity, not only could not move normally, they were unable to give birth to offspring. This proved that dreams of settling and conquering distant planets will remain dreams until mankind invents anti-radiation protection for spaceships.
