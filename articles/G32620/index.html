Hotel in Ryazan "Stable yard": reviews of tourists

"Stable yard" offers comfortable accommodation for those guests who value comfort, silence and proximity to nature. Everything here is decorated in a warm home style that everyone will like.





Brief description of the hotel

"Stable yard" is not just a hotel, but also a whole complex, which includes many infrastructure facilities. Here you can visit the steam room, join an active lifestyle, take advantage of free parking spaces and wireless Internet, as well as a huge number of services. A nice addition to your relaxation will be swimming in a clean indoor pool.

Speaking about the rooms of the Konyushenny Dvor Hotel, it is worth noting that elements inherent in the classical style prevail in their design. Some apartments are decorated in a pleasant pastoral style. As for amenities, in your room you will find everything you need to feel as relaxed and comfortable as in your own home.

Special attention deserves food in the guest house "Stable yard". The restaurant is focused on traditional Russian dishes. In the tavern you can enjoy alcoholic drinks, as well as original snacks. If necessary, your order will be delivered directly to your room.





Hotel Location

The Konyushenny Dvor Hotel (Ryazan) is located in a picturesque cottage village, not far from the central part of the city. Nearby is a large walking park, as well as the train station. The distance to Domodedovo International Airport is about 180 kilometers.

Guest rooms

Guest House "Konyushenny Dvor" (Ryazan) offers its guests to stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard rooms are just one room, which can be designed for both double and single occupancy. It has all the necessary furniture, appliances, as well as other amenities. Due to the presence of a large window, the room becomes bright, and due to this, it visually looks more spacious.
  • Junior Suite is a spacious luxurious room decorated in a cozy and attractive country style. In addition to the bedroom, there is also a small living area, which is represented by a soft sofa upholstered with delicate floral patterns. It is impossible to walk past the fireplace, which gives the atmosphere of the room a feeling of home warmth and coziness.
  • Suite is a huge room, which consists of a bedroom and a living room. The second, in addition to upholstered furniture, is also equipped with a real fireplace. The bedroom has a huge double bed, as well as a large wardrobe, which easily fits all your belongings.
  • VIP rooms are detached houses, each of which has two floors. A distinctive feature of these cottages can be considered that one of the walls is completely glass, and therefore you can always enjoy the picturesque views of the pond. In addition, thanks to such a design solution, there is always a lot of light in the house.

It is worth noting that one of the most popular accommodation options in Ryazan is the Konyushenny Dvor. Prices for accommodation in apartments start from 4000 rubles.





The amenities provided in the apartment

Ryazan hotels are distinguished by hospitality and quality service. As for the Konyushenny Dvor, in its apartments you can take advantage of a number of amenities:

  • individual air conditioning system, due to which the temperature in your room will not depend on the weather outside;
  • A modern wide-screen flat-screen TV that gives you the ability to watch many cable and satellite channels;
  • thanks to the ability to play videos from external media, you can always watch your favorite movies;
  • landline telephone is designed not only for communication with the administration, but also has access to intercity and international lines;
  • thanks to the presence of a music center in the room, you can enjoy your favorite tunes at any time;
  • there is always drinking water in the minibar, and after making an additional fee it will also be filled with alcohol and sweet fizzy drinks;
  • an electric kettle will allow you to treat yourself to a hot drink at any time;
  • the bathroom has a hairdryer, a full range of bath and cosmetic accessories, as well as hygiene products.




Hotel facilities

When exploring Ryazan hotels, be sure to pay attention to the infrastructure that one or another establishment can offer you. Speaking about the Stables, it is worth noting the following objects:

  • the billiard room always has a pleasant friendly atmosphere;
  • After an active excursion or a busy working day, be sure to visit the sauna, where you can relax and put your thoughts in order;
  • the establishment that the Konyushenny Dvor can be proud of is a restaurant specializing in the preparation of traditional Russian dishes;
  • for the youngest guests in the shade of the green garden a modern playground is equipped;

Hotel Policies

Many guests of the city choose for themselves such an accommodation option as the Stable Yard. The hotel has some rules that govern accommodation in it:

  • When planning your trip, keep in mind that check-in starts no earlier than two in the afternoon, but on the day of departure, the check-out time is set at noon;
  • children under the age of two years stay in the room free of charge if they do not need an extra bed;
  • the cost of a daily rental crib is 100 rubles;
  • an extra bed for a child under 12 years old will cost 500 rubles;
  • a clamshell for an adult guest will cost 1,500 rubles for each night;
  • guests cannot bring animals to the hotel, otherwise the administration has the full right to refuse to accommodate them;
  • you can pay for the services provided to you by credit card;
  • if you need documents for tax reporting, then, upon prior request, employees will prepare them for you.

Many enthusiastic comments can be found on specialized resources about the Konyushenny Dvor hotel. Tourist reviews contain the following information:

  • the hotel is located in a very picturesque place;
  • wonderful breakfasts are served in the restaurant - everything is very satisfying and incredibly tasty;
  • all rooms are very comfortable and quite nicely decorated;
  • the staff is very polite and attentive, charges guests with a positive mood;
  • the territory is very clean, beautiful and well-groomed;

General impression

It is worth noting that the Konyushenny Dvor is one of the most popular hotels in Ryazan. Here, urban comfort and country coziness are incredibly successfully combined. Despite the fact that the cost of living is quite high, many tourists and business people choose this particular accommodation option. You have a unique opportunity to relax in a green corner of nature with its own pond and clean forest air. And if you want to be at the epicenter of urban life, then you have to overcome a relatively short distance to a huge park - about half an hour by walking.
