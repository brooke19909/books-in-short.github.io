Hotel "Green Parrot" in Adler: photos, reviews

Lovers of relaxation on the Black Sea are invited to their private hotel "Green Parrot". Adler , in which the hotel is located, has a developed infrastructure and many attractions. The hotel was built in 1999, it is located near the resort town, where the main attractions of Adler are concentrated.

Description

The Green Parrot Hotel in Adler is a modern building with original architecture. It is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The cozy green courtyard with its exotic garden and many flowers has comfortable gazebos, benches, swings and an indoor pool filled with fresh water. The pool water is heated, and highlighted in the evenings. The hotel also has a children's playground and a sun terrace. Also has its own cafe. The city beach is two hundred meters from the hotel. Therefore, getting to it does not take much time. The beach is pebble, well-maintained. Here you can rent the necessary equipment (sun loungers, umbrellas, etc.). It offers guests a variety of water activities. For a fee, you can use the beaches of nearby resorts.

For guests arriving by car, free parking is provided. Near the hotel, a two-three minute walk, are the nearest dining room and grocery store. Also nearby are La Luna Restaurant, Deja Vue Cafe Bar, Magnit Supermarket and Market. The train station is a five-minute drive from the hotel, and the airport can be reached in 15 minutes. The Green Parrot Hotel is open from April 1 to October 31. Every year cosmetic repairs are made. The hotel has 30 rooms. Check-in is at 12.00 on the day of arrival, the estimated time is 12.00.





Accommodation

Private hotel "Green Parrot" in Adler offers its guests three categories of rooms. The cost of living is calculated per person, taking into account the length of stay at the hotel 10 days / 9 nights for the summer season.

  • Double rooms are standard. The price is from 7900 to 9900 rubles, depending on the season. Extra beds are not provided.
  • Double rooms standard +. The cost is from 8,100 to 10,700 rubles per day, depending on the period of stay. The rooms provide an extra bed at a cost of 5,000 to 5,800 rubles per day for one person.
  • Triple rooms are standard. The price of the main place ranges from 6900 to 9700, there is an additional place, the price for it is from 5000 to 5600 rubles per day.

For the placement of children under 5 years old without providing a place, utilities are charged in the amount of 200 rubles per day. The price includes the use of the pool, as well as accident insurance. There are family rooms, as well as non-smoking.





Facilities

The rooms of the Green Parrot Hotel in Adler provide everything for a comfortable stay of vacationers. Each room has the following amenities:

  • TV (cable TV);
  • air conditioning;
  • refrigerator;
  • electric kettle;
  • hair dryer;
  • shower and toilet;
  • Free toiletries.

Flooring - porcelain. The rooms with extra bed are equipped with an armchair bed. Rooms are cleaned daily. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.





Services

The Green Parrot Hotel in Adler provides its guests with both basic and additional services.

  • The hotel has an indoor heated pool with sun loungers.
  • For younger visitors, there is a children's playground on the street, and there is a games room in the guest house.
  • The hotel has designated smoking areas.
  • Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.
  • Laundry facilities are available for a fee.
  • If necessary, the hotel has an ironing board.
  • The hotel provides a transfer from or to the airport (additional charge).
  • A city shuttle service is also available for a fee.
  • For walks in the courtyard there is a beautiful garden with palm trees.
  • The hotel has its own cafe.
  • At the request of tourists, breakfast can be included in the price. It also provides a comprehensive 3 meals a day (and also optional).
  • At the request of vacationers, a taxi call is provided.
  • Reception is open 24 hours a day.




sights

If you want to not only sunbathe on the shores of the warm sea, but also have fun spending your time, choose the city of Adler for relaxation . The Green Parrot Hotel is conveniently located so that you can visit various attractions.

  • Amphibius Waterpark is 300 meters from the hotel.
  • A little further, at a distance of about one kilometer, is the Dolphinarium "Water Area".
  • If you want to visit the Sochi Oceanarium, you will have to overcome 1.3 kilometers.
  • 4.7-4.8 kilometers from the hotel are the Adler District Museum of History and St. Sarkis Cathedral.
  • If you wish, with excursions you can visit the Caucasian State Natural Biosphere Reserve, Agursky waterfalls, Sochi arboretum.




Positive feedback from travelers about the Green Parrot hotel

Of great interest to vacationers is the Green Parrot Hotel (Adler). Photos on official sites, unfortunately, do not always convey all the information about the institution, so we recommend that you read the reviews of hotel guests. Among the positive comments worth noting:

  • Friendly hostess, polite and helpful staff.
  • Very beautiful and well-groomed area, there are swings and gazebos, you can sit in the evening, breathe fresh air.
  • Some rooms have very beautiful mountain views.
  • The hotel has a good pool with sunbeds. Pool water is always clean. There are toilets and showers near the pool.
  • On the territory there is a cafe where you can eat three times a day. They cook very tasty, a large selection of dishes, comfortable atmosphere, always clean inside. The prices in the cafe are affordable. And the staff is very friendly.
  • Very close is a large water park where you can spend the whole day.
  • Also nearby is the coastline, where in the evening you can take a walk, sit in a cafe or go to souvenir shops.
  • The rooms are quiet, the windows are plastic. To prevent noise from the street, vacationers prefer not to open windows, but to turn on air conditioning.
  • The hotel is silent at night.
  • The sea on the near beach is very clean.




Adler Hotel "Green Parrot". Negative reviews

Unfortunately, some vacationers found a number of shortcomings in the hotel:

  • The second floor is completely under a plastic canopy, so from the window you can see only the courtyard.
  • All hotel rooms face the sunny side, so neither curtains nor air conditioning can save you from the heat.
  • Some vacationers complained that the room was cleaned only on the fifth day.
  • Many complain that the air conditioners in the rooms are outdated modifications, so they practically did not fulfill their function.
  • There are also a number of complaints that the road to the sea goes past garbage cans, which is not very comfortable.
  • It is bad that the time for swimming in the pool is limited to 20.00.
  • Also, in some rooms have never changed, neither bed nor towels.
  • From the lighting in one of the rooms there was only a chandelier, there was no sconce and table lamps, which created inconvenience in the evening.
  • On the shared balcony, one folding dryer is designed for several rooms.

Conclusion

If you are interested in the Green Parrot Hotel in Adler, reviews from tourists will help you make a decision. The hotel is quiet and comfortable, has a closed area, great for families with children. The traffic intersection is very convenient, both the railway station and the airport are nearby. The hotel is located two kilometers from the center of Adler at the address: st.
