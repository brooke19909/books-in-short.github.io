"Dropshot" - a plan of attack on the USSR. Cold war

After fascist Germany was defeated, the United States was so frightened by the power of the Soviet Army that it was forced to develop a special strategy - "Dropshot". The plan of attack on the USSR and the Allies intended to stop their subsequent invasion of Western Europe, the Middle East and Japan.

Americans get atomic weapons

For the first time, the Dropshot US plan was announced at the White House after the Potsdam Conference, which was attended by the leaders of the victorious states: the USA, Great Britain and the USSR. Truman arrived at the meeting in high spirits: on the eve of the test launches of atomic warheads were carried out. He became the head of a nuclear state.

Plan in detail

The first references available to publicity of the world public appeared in 1978. The American specialist A. Brown, working on the secrets of World War II, published a number of documents confirming that the United States did develop the DropShot strategy - an attack plan on the USSR. The scheme of action of the American "liberation" army should have been as follows.

As mentioned above, hostilities were to begin on January 1, 1957.





  1. In the short term, it was planned to drop 300 nuclear weapons and 250,000 tons of conventional bombs and shells into the territory of the Soviet Union. As a result of the bombing, it was planned to destroy at least 85% of the country's industry, up to 96% of the industry of the countries friendly to the Union and 6.7 million of the state‚Äôs population.
  2. The next step is the landing of NATO ground forces. It was planned to attract 250 divisions to the attack, of which the Allied forces were numbered 38 units. The occupation operations were to be supported by aviation, in the amount of 5 armies (7400 aircraft). At the same time, all sea and ocean communications should be captured by the NATO Navy.
  3. The third step of Operation Dropshot is a plan to destroy the USSR and erase it from the political map of the world. By this was meant the use of all known types of weapons: atomic, small arms, chemical, radiological and biological.
  4. The final stage is the division of the occupied territory into 4 zones and the deployment of NATO troops in major cities. As stated in the documents: "Pay special attention to the physical destruction of the Communists."




Crashed dreams

The Americans were unable to implement their Dropshot strategy; the plan of attack on the USSR was not implemented thanks to one event. 09/03/1949 the pilot of an American bomber flying over the Pacific Ocean, using instruments, recorded a sharp increase in radioactivity in the upper atmosphere. After processing the data, the Pentagon was extremely disappointed: Stalin was testing atomic bombs.

Truman's reaction to the message did not follow, so he was discouraged. Only after some time did the information about this appear in the press. The government was afraid of an inadequate reaction in the form of panic among the common population. Pentagon scientists found a way out of the situation by proposing to the president the development of a new, more destructive hydrogen bomb. It must be in the arsenal of the United States to pacify the Soviets.

Despite the difficult financial and economic situation, the Soviet Union was only 4 years behind the Americans in creating the atomic bomb!
