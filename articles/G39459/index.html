Hotel Cinnamon Bey 5 * Deluxe, Sri Lanka: photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Cinnamon Bey ex Chaaya Bey 5 * is located in Beruwela, near Bentota (about 7 km), in the southwestern part of the island. Hotel address: Cinnamon Bey, Moragalla, 12070 Bentota, Sri Lanka. You can get here from the city of Colombo, located at a distance of 55 km from the resort of Beruwela. The duration of the journey by bus is approximately 1 hour. The cost of the trip is about $ 0.8. Beruwela can also be reached by train. The place is located on the highway Colombo - Matara. The duration of the trip will be 1.5 hours, the ticket will cost $ 0.5. The distance to the international airport is approximately 86 km.





Hotel location

Beruwela is a port for large vessels and small fishing vessels. It is also the site of the first Muslim colonies of the island that arose here in the X century. Beruwela became a resort place not so long ago. In a short period, an excellent tourist infrastructure has developed here with resort hotels along the coast. The most popular beach is Moragalla. This place is ideal for a relaxing family vacation.

Resort Cinnamon Bey 5 * (Sri Lanka) is located in one minute walk from the beach. The hotel is surrounded by coconut palms and smooth bright green meadows. Clear sand, clear sea and coconuts create an excellent relaxing atmosphere. The entire hotel complex covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äã4.5 hectares.





Climate features

You can come to the Cinnamon Bey 5 * hotel (Sri Lanka) at any time of the year, but the December ‚Äì March season will be the best period. It is dry here at this time of the year. The tropical climate of the country allows you to be in it at any time of the year. The air temperature ranges from +27 ... + 30 degrees. But between May and September, the south and west sides of the island are subject to monsoon rains, which occur mostly at night. During this period, air humidity rises.

Hotel rooms

Hotel Cinnamon Bey Beruwala 5 * (Sri Lanka) offers all its visitors spacious and bright rooms with terraces or balconies. Guests can choose a room for smokers or non-smokers. The number of rooms in the hotel is 200. All of them are equipped with air conditioning, equipped with safes and minibars. Each room has satellite TV. All bathrooms have a shower.

In accordance with their preferences and financial capabilities, guests can choose:

  • deluxe double room with one bed;
  • Superior double room with one large bed or two single beds;
  • deluxe triple room;
  • Superior Triple Room.

The hotel has three classes of rooms:

  1. Superior Room (170 rooms).
  2. Deluxe Room (28 rooms).
  3. Suite (2 rooms).




Room facilities and layout

Superior Room-rooms are located in the left wing of the three-story building of the hotel. These rooms offer panoramic views of the surroundings - the ocean and the garden. The bathroom is equipped with a shower. Rooms are 39 square meters. m

Deluxe Rooms are located in a separate wing of the hotel called Deluxe Wing. The hygiene rooms in these rooms have bathtubs. Deluxe guests can use the pool located in the Deluxe Wing area. Rooms are 49 square meters. m

The suites are located on the upper level of the Deluxe Wing. Rooms are 134 square meters. m. They include a dining area, living space. Hygiene rooms are equipped with bathtubs. In addition, there is a private pool on the outdoor terrace, which overlooks the ocean. Residents of this class of rooms can use the pool located in the Deluxe Wing zone.

All rooms are comfortable and decorated in a Turkish style. Regardless of the category, each room has:

Room service is open around the clock. Every day, all rooms are cleaned.





Accommodation with children

At the Cinnamon Bey Hotel 5 * Deluxe, you can come with children of any age. All children under 5 years old stay free of charge when using existing bedding. Free accommodation for children under 5 years old is also possible when using the hotel's cots.

For a child of 5-12 years old, you can install an extra bed in the room. In this case, you will have to pay for it in the amount of $ 50 for one night. The maximum number of extra beds or baby cots is 1. in the room.

Extra beds or cots are available upon request when booking. The hotel must confirm the provision of this service. Beds are an extra service and are not included in the total price of the hotel. They must be paid separately on the spot.

Cinnamon Bey 5 * Deluxe Hotel has a children's pool. A babysitting service is available upon request for children (surcharge).





Hotel facilities

The hotel has a Green Globe certificate, issued only to those hotel complexes that pay special attention to the conservation of raw materials, make a great contribution to the development of local communities and protect the natural environment. Cinnamon Bey also has ISO 14000 certificates (the standard for environmental management system requirements) and ISO 22000 (the standard for safe food products).

The complex has an outdoor pool, an amazing garden and 6 restaurants. This resort is the only place after Colombo on the island, offering its guests so many restaurants. Even the most sophisticated tourist will find an institution that matches his preferences.

Guests of the Cinnamon Bey Hotel 5 * Deluxe (Sri Lanka) can use the outdoor pool. Towels, sun loungers and mattresses are provided free of charge. Wellness programs are also available (you can undergo Ayurvedic treatment). You can use the fitness services, visit the spa and sauna, go to the steam bath and massage.

Laundry and ironing services are available on site.

The hotel offers a shuttle service from the airport and vice versa (extra charge). Guests can also rent a car. Near the hotel there is free parking.

Internet access is provided from anywhere in the hotel. Wi-fi connection is free.

If necessary, guests can use the service "doctor on call" (paid).

Cinnamon Bey has one meeting room. It can accommodate 300 people.





Nutrition

The main meal is a buffet. According to tourists, breakfast at the hotel is very diverse, with a large selection of fruits and dishes for every taste. It should be borne in mind that under the laws of Sri Lanka, it is forbidden to serve and sell alcohol on Full Moon Poya Day - the full moon day. This happens once a month.

All Inclusive - the package expires on the day of departure from the hotel, at 12.00 h. All food ordered after 12.00 becomes paid. The package does not apply to special dinners and promotional evenings.

Please note that the prices in the restaurant menu are excluding taxes. You must add + 24% to the indicated price.

Many guests note that the best option for them was a package with breakfast included. Many guests enjoyed eating at the Sunflower Cafe.

Entertainment, sports

You should not expect noisy and dizzy parties from the Cinnamon Bey Hotel. It is crowded here, but guests come to this hotel for their originality and the opportunity to be among the beautiful nature, which in itself is considered a tourist attraction. Here you can spend a quiet and leisurely vacation enjoying the ocean, visiting interesting excursions and restoring your health. The local population is very friendly to tourists. Excursions

In Beruwela itself, you can visit one of the island‚Äôs oldest mosques, called Kechimalay.

You can go to Bentota and go on a tour of the Bentota Ganga River. There is also a famous turtle reserve. One of the closest Buddhist attractions is the Kande Viharaya Temple (Kande Viharaya Temple). The temple was built in 1734 and is considered the most revered place of Sri Lanka. Thousands of devotees flock here daily to perform rituals. The most important element of the temple is the giant Buddha statue, considered the tallest (48 m) of all the seated Buddhist statues in the world. It was installed in 2006. When visiting the complex, you should take into account that you will have to walk barefoot on its territory, so it is better to go there in the morning. After lunch, the sand becomes hot. Clothing should also be appropriate, not beachwear. Dresses should be below the knees so as not to offend the feelings of believers. Here you can feed the temple elephants. The temple complex is 2.5 km from the hotel.

6.6 km from the hotel, Bawa Gardens is a beautiful park laid by the outstanding local sculptor and architect Bevis Bava. Lunuganga Manor is 7.5 km from the hotel. Now it houses a boutique hotel surrounded by tropical plants, cinnamon plantations and sprawling trees.
