Applique fox: how to make yourself

Application is a great opportunity for children's creativity and self-expression. Children can perform some work options on their own. More complex applications will require the presence and help of parents.

What will be needed for the application

A suitable option for crafts is applique fox. This beautiful animal is often found in fairy tales and cartoons.

Before starting work, you need to prepare everything you need in advance:

  • The basis for the application. It may be a sheet of paper, cardboard, cloth or other item.
  • What the fox will be made of. You can use colored paper, pieces of fabric, fur or leather, plasticine, various leaves. The list can be very long, it all depends on the imagination and idea.
  • Scissors. For children, it is advisable to prepare safe, with rounded ends.
  • A simple pencil for preparing sketch or templates.
  • PVA glue.
  • Decor elements: beads, multi-colored threads, sequins, small bows or flowers, etc.

This is the main list of materials for applique foxes. In fact, plain paper and colored pencils are enough for the simplest option. It will just be necessary to colorize everything in the right colors.

DIY step-by-step application manufacturing

When everything is ready, you can get to work.

  • You must first think about how the image will look, and its location on the basis. Schematically mark it with a simple pencil.
  • If desired, the base can be given color with paints, pencils or colored paper. So the crafts will be more vivid.
  • To apply the fox out of paper, it is enough to draw each element individually and carefully cut it out. Then stick to the base, according to the marking.
  • If you work with a fabric, it is easier to pre-prepare a template from thick cardboard. And already cut the part according to the template.
  • It is advisable to first cut out all parts of the application completely. Attach them to the base and make sure that everything is done correctly. Only after that stick them.
  • When the fox applique is ready, you can decorate it with additional elements. For example, use beads for the nose and eyes. Or stick a bow in your ear.

Types of possible fox applications

There are several options for crafts:

  1. Depending on the location. The application of the fox can be placed on a piece of paper, cardboard or for decoration cards. Can be placed on clothes as decoration.
  2. Also, the applique can become an element of decor for a children's handbag, casket or other favorite item.
  3. Depending on the image of the animal itself. It can be the whole fox or just its muzzle.
