Small air conditioners are our mobile helpers

Small air conditioners are perfect for those people who often move from one place to another. For example, from a summer residence - to an apartment. Still often small air conditioners are bought if it is simply not possible to install an external cooling unit from the outside, especially if you live in an old building.



How it works?

The most interesting thing is that the principle of operation of such a miracle of technology is quite simple. Small air conditioners have 4 openings for taking air and letting it out. Air enters the device from the room. This is the process of cooling the condenser (also often called hot heat transfer). In it, the collected air begins to heat up and, with a hose, goes outside. Above the outlet in the upper part of the device is an intake part. It also provides filters to clean the air. A clean and pleasant whiff of the desired coolness can be controlled and guided by the blinds that the small air conditioners are equipped with. This is the principle of operation of such devices. They consist of a monoblock equipped with wheels, and a special outlet hose. It is usually brought to a window, door or loggia in order to divert warm air from the room.



Issue price

Today you can find small air conditioners in almost any store. They differ both in a set of functions and in price. Such devices are divided by the speed of both heating and cooling. For example, a portable air conditioner EAM -14 E / R can be purchased within 19 000 rubles. The power of its cooling is 4.1 kW, it is heated with the help of 1.8 kW. The small mobile air conditioner AEG KP - 07, the cost of which starts at 12,000 rubles, has a cooling power of 2.3 kW. From this we can conclude that the EAM -14 E / R model will be more effective over a large area. It is worth noting that mobile cooling devices are designed for small rooms up to 40 square meters. By the way, the smallest air conditioner can have several operating modes. In addition to the standard cooling / heating functions, it has a number of additional options.

For example, this small device can work to drain the air, its ventilation, or at night. Also, almost all air conditioners of well-known manufacturers are equipped with a timer, touch control, auto restart, a set of filters. It's no secret that today many manufacturers of household equipment are concerned about energy-saving technologies. Therefore, in 2011, a unique Dantex air conditioner was presented. There are 4 bends in its heat exchanger, which can significantly save energy. In addition, like standard split systems, mobile air conditioners have additional adjustment of the air flow direction. The device is controlled using the remote control.

And finally

Remember that in a mobile air conditioning system, condensate must be systematically removed. It is usually assembled at the very bottom of this device. However, if you forget about this procedure, the air conditioner will remind you of it, just by disconnecting.
