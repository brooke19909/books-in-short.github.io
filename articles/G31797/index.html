Millennium Palace Hotel - Paradise Holidays in Beldibi

Description: Millennium Palace Hotel occupies a territory in a very beautiful place, at the very foot of the mountain, in the small village of Beldibi. Only 400 meters to the sea. Vacationers here can enjoy the picturesque surroundings throughout the rest and breathe in the purest mountain-sea air. It is separated from the airport of Antalya by a distance of 35 kilometers, and to get to the city of Kemer, it is necessary to overcome a distance of 13 kilometers. This is approximately 10-15 minutes drive.



The Millennium Palace Hotel has a four-star status and includes one 5-story building, built in 2006. For the convenience of tourists, an elevator is installed. The living conditions here are fully consistent with the classiness of the hotel. The hotel area is rich in green spaces. Its area is 6000 square meters. meters. Recommended for those vacationers who will be able to appreciate the comfort of home and prefer a relaxing holiday.



Rooms: City Based   Kemer, Millennium Palace offers accommodation in one of 113 rooms, including 99 standard, 6 suites, 2 family and 6 small suites. All are superbly equipped. The air-conditioned rooms have a bathtub, sink, toilet, hairdryer. A telephone, a work desk, a safe (for a fee), and a TV are installed. There is satellite TV, among the numerous channels of which there are two Russian-speaking and a music channel. Each room has a minibar (empty), it is filled for an additional charge. From the terrace or balcony offer beautiful scenic views. Some rooms overlook the sea, and the second part overlook the most beautiful mountains. Pets are allowed, the weight of which does not exceed 3 kg.



Meal Plan: Millennium Palace Hotel provides its guests with all-inclusive meals, which is valid from 10.00 to 22.00. Served as a buffet. After this time, snacks and drinks are purchased at an additional cost. Upon reservation, meals can be served directly in the room. The hotel has an open restaurant, which is ready to accept 200 visitors at the same time, and a closed restaurant for 300 people. In addition to restaurants, there is a bar in the lobby, at the disco and by the pool.



Beaches: There is an excellent pebble beach across the road from the Millennium Palace Hotel. It is approximately 400 meters from the hotel. Its length is 40 meters. It is well equipped. There are changing cabins and showers. Beach equipment is provided free of charge. Here you can take a fascinating boat trip, go water skiing or a water bike.

Information for holidaymakers: For guests there is a beautiful outdoor pool with a water slide. There is a hairdresser, currency exchange. Car rental is available if necessary. This allows you to solve not only your work issues, but also see the sights of the surroundings and nearby cities.



On the territory of the Millennium Palace Hotel there is free parking, shops. Vacationers are invited to use the luggage storage room. While here, you can do aerobics, play billiards, volleyball or table tennis. For those who keep themselves in shape, an excellent gym is open. In addition, you can play soccer or volleyball. There is a bicycle rental for a fascinating walk. Massage (charge) or Turkish bath (charge) will help you relax. Various entertainment programs are held throughout the day. And in the evening, a disco awaits everyone. In addition, excellent conditions for children are created.

Reviews: Hotel Millennium is located in a beautiful location near the beach. It has a very beautiful area with numerous green spaces and alleys. The rooms are spacious and decorated in light colors. The staff is attentive and polite, there are several employees who speak Russian. The food is varied and very tasty.
