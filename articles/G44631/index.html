From Antiquity to the Middle Ages

How Barcelona appeared, a city in Spain, is not exactly known. There are two versions of the occurrence. One relates us to the Greek myths of Hercules. The second is somewhat closer on the scale to our days and leads to the third century BC. At this time, Roman troops conquered the city of Laye and equipped it as a fortress with a center located on the mountain. The nearby Tarracon was significantly larger and more important than a small fortification.



Over time, thanks to the beautiful harbor, open sea access, the city began to develop. Riches accumulated, and with them the importance increased. Own coins appeared. By the fifth century, Barsino, named after the commander, pushes large neighbors and occupies one of the leading positions.

Modern history

Barcelona has survived more than one war and succeeded by several rulers: Charles III, Philip V, General Suchet, Napoleon. During this time, it is difficult to say that the city had stable development and prosperity.

Most enterprises appeared during the industrial revolution - in the 19th century. At that time, the city managed to somewhat weaken the tight control of the Spanish authorities. The protective wall erected in the Middle Ages was demolished. The fortress turned into a beautiful city park.
