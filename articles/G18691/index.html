How to connect "Zero Without Borders" from MTS. Benefits and Pitfalls

The “Zero Without Borders” option of MTS allows you to make preferential calls on trips around the world. What are the benefits of this option? Incoming and outgoing calls to Russia for 0 rubles. The service is valid abroad. Let's consider the advantages of the option and how to connect “Zero Without Borders” from MTS.

Service cost

The cost of the service is 125 rubles per day. Cash is debited daily from the balance of the phone number. In order to stop debiting funds, the option should be disabled. If the subscriber does not have enough funds on the balance, the option will be suspended. Once the balance is replenished by a sufficient amount, the option will resume. It is important to note that if the option is not connected, you need to check the additional conditions, which are described below.

How to connect and disconnect

How to activate the “Zero without borders” service from MTS? This question is asked by many subscribers of the operator, traveling abroad of Russia. In order to connect “Zero Without Borders” from MTS, you must enter the following request: * 111 * 4444 #. Next, press the call (green button on the keyboard of the mobile device).

However, this is not the only way to connect “Zero Without Borders” from MTS. You can also do this using your personal account on the operator’s website. In the "Options" section, select the one you need and click the "Connect" button.

Cost of incoming and outgoing calls

Subscribers who activate the option get the opportunity to make calls to Russia from a number of countries for 0 rubles. 1 hour of outgoing calls to Russia is free. After this time, calls will be charged at the following rate: 25 rubles per 1 minute. The tariff is valid for calls to Russia made from countries such as Taiwan, Morocco, Egypt, Kuwait, Estonia, Switzerland, Saudi Arabia, Great Britain, USA, Italy, Bulgaria, Lithuania and others. A complete list is published on the operator’s website.

Outgoing calls from Tunisia to Russia in the first 60 minutes are not charged. Starting from 61 minutes, the cost will be 25 rubles for 60 seconds of conversation. All Inbox: 50 rubles per 1 minute.

Cost of calls in other countries

If the subscriber is located in any other country not specified above, incoming and outgoing calls to Russia will be charged as follows. All inbox will be free for the first 10 minutes. every call. Starting from 11 minutes, the subscriber will need to pay 25 rubles for each minute of the conversation.

Outgoing calls to Russia will be charged as follows. The first minute of conversation on the cost of roaming in this particular country in which the subscriber is located. From the second and 5 minutes of each conversation, 25 rubles in 60 seconds will be deducted from the balance. Starting from 6 minutes, the call will again be charged according to the roaming conditions of a particular country.

It is important to note that this option is not applied when registering a subscriber in the following countries: South Ossetia, Iran, Algeria, Turkmenistan, Haiti, Saint Lucia, Andorra, Cayman Islands, Maldives, Jamaica. It also does not work when the subscriber is on ships and aircraft, even if the subscriber is actually located in other countries. A complete list of countries where the option does not work is published on the website of the telecom operator.

Additional terms

Having dealt with the question of how to connect “Zero Without Borders” from MTS, it is also worth mentioning that the option under consideration is available exclusively to customers of the operator, who are private individuals.

Not all archived operator tariffs support this option. If the installation is not performed, customers are advised to connect the option “Zero without borders around the world”.

If the subscriber is in roaming and has activated the “Zero without borders” option, then during the calendar month, starting from 201 minutes of conversation, all incoming calls will be charged at the following cost: 25 rubles per 1 minute of conversation. In order to find out how many minutes have already been reached, the subscriber can dial * 419 * 1233 #, then press the call button.

It is important to note that options involving discounts on incoming and outgoing calls of a roaming subscriber are mutually exclusive. That is, by connecting one option, you cannot use the other at the same time. Such products include “Zero without borders around the world”, “Close countries”, “Foreign countries” and others.

The option “Zero without borders” will be available only if the subscriber has previously connected the following services: “Intern. and national roaming ”,“ Intern. access". Or, the subscriber can choose the “Easy Roaming and International Access” service. In order to check whether these services are connected, you need to contact the MTS communication salon, call the customer support service at 0890 or check it yourself in your personal account on the MTS website.

We examined in detail how to connect “Zero Without Borders” from MTS, as well as what advantages and limitations the service has. If an error occurs when connecting the option, the subscriber should contact the customer support service of the MTS operator.
