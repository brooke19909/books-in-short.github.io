Side Breeze Hotel (Monachus Park) 5 * (Turkey, Side, Colakli): description, service, reviews

Turkey has long been a leader among residents of post-Soviet countries as a place to relax. Here at an affordable cost you can get an excellent hotel room with an all-inclusive service. Tourists who come here on vacation can completely forget about domestic problems and issues and enjoy a vacation. Side Breeze Hotel (Monachus Park) 5 * refers to such complexes, and the tourist, crossing the threshold of the hotel, begins an unforgettable vacation from that moment.

Where is it and how to get there?

The hotel is located in Side in the resort village of Cholakli. There is no own airport. Tourists come here by taxi from the transport hubs of Antalya. The airport is 60 km from the hotel. Such a trip will cost vacationers about 70-80 dollars. You can also book a transfer in advance at the agency, which will cost $ 10-20 less.





If tourists are looking for a budget option, then you can take a public bus to the Antalya bus station and transfer there to a minibus to Manavgat. From there go dolmushi directly to Cholakli. Such a trip will take twice as long as a taxi.

When is it better to come on vacation?

In this region, the climate is subtropical. Summer is very hot and dry, and winter is cold and rainy. In Side it is better to come on vacation from May to October. At this time, the sea is already warm enough, and the temperature on the street makes it possible to sunbathe and go on excursions.

Fans of beach vacations should choose the time of high season - July-August. During this period, the highest heat indicators are set on the thermometer and, the water in the sea warms up to 30 ¬∞ C.

Tourists who love outdoor activities and want to visit as many interesting sights of the region as possible, it is better to choose the first autumn months to relax. At this time, the heat is not so annoying holidaymakers and does not force to hide in the rooms at lunchtime or look for a shadow.

Side Breeze Hotel (Monachus Park) 5 *: description

The hotel is located in the center of the resort village. The complex consists of three buildings on 4 floors. The complex is 8 km from Side. Therefore, guests can take a taxi to the city at any time for an excursion or to shop.

The hotel territory is as green as possible. Outside the complex, such plantings will not have to be seen, since in this area there are frequent droughts in the summer.



Side Breeze Hotel (Monachus Park) 5 * is divided into several relaxation areas. The interests of every vacationer of any age are provided here. The hotel has two rooms "standard" for people with disabilities.

Description of rooms

The hotel has three categories of rooms equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay:

  • standard - has a large double or two single beds, a shower and toilet in the room, split system, hairdryer, TV, safe for an extra charge, telephone, balcony with sea view;
  • family - one double bed and a bunk for children, private shower and toilet, hairdryer, air conditioning, TV, balcony with sea view;
  • apartments - a two-room suite, a large double and two single beds, armchairs, TV, shower and toilet, a balcony with beautiful sea views.




All rooms have laminate flooring. The minibar is filled with soft drinks upon arrival. Then only drinking water is added there. Other drinks must be paid extra.

Restaurants

The complex has several establishments where you can eat and drink a variety of drinks. The main restaurant provides full buffet meals for guests. Themed evenings and a variety of show programs are arranged regularly.

The beach restaurant begins its work with the opening of the season - May 1 and ends to serve customers in late October. It serves drinks from 10:30 to 17:00 daily.

Ala Carte restaurants are located on site. One of them is "Olive Garden" (Italian cuisine), and the second is "Lagos" (fish dishes). It serves delicious dishes prepared on the grill in front of guests in front of their eyes.

Dining at the hotel

The hotel belongs to the category of "all inclusive", so tourists are provided with full breakfasts, lunches and dinners. In the main restaurant, a 3-time balanced buffet-style meal is served on schedule.

A variety of dishes allows you to satisfy the taste of any gourmet. There is a children's menu. In each meal there are a choice of meat and fish dishes. Unlike other complexes, Side Breeze Hotel 5 * uses not only chicken meat, but also beef and lamb in the tourism diet.

Side Breeze Hotel (Monachus Park) 5 *: service

The hotel has professional staff. Here, guests are always treated kindly, listened carefully and resolved as quickly as possible all questions about life.
