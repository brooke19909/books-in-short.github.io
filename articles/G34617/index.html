LCD "Emerald" (Troitsk): description, apartments, reviews

For most people, buying an apartment in Moscow is a dream and at the same time inadmissible luxury. And the environment, the endless bustle in this case are a drawback of metropolitan options. That is why it is now very popular to purchase modern residential complexes and housing estates, rebuilt in the suburbs and New Moscow. One of them is the Emerald LCD in Troitsk. The project is interesting, of course, but what can it offer potential residents?

about the project

Twenty low-rise buildings erected using monolithic-brick technology - this is the Emerald LCD in Troitsk. Unique patented technologies and certified materials guarantee the reliability and durability of all buildings. The developer offers comfortable apartments, represented by a variety of layouts, as well as townhouses with a small house area, a garage and a separate entrance. And all this is not far from the Moscow Ring Road.

Location

LCD "Emerald" (Troitsk) is located just 18 km from the Moscow Ring Road, you can get to it both on Kaluga and Kiev highway. The location is a strong point of the project, it will be convenient for those who work in the capital, because the road will be comfortable and fast both on personal cars and public transport. New Moscow is a promising direction, so in the near future a metro station will appear here, which will only improve the transport situation.

Property Options

The developer offers a choice of various real estate options: from spacious apartments to full-fledged townhouses. By the way, the latter have been especially popular over the past few years, as they give a unique opportunity to avoid a huge number of neighbors, enjoying the peace, comfort and other advantages of country life.

All apartments in the LCD "Emerald" in Troitsk are rented with a finishing finish, including the installation of the front door, glazing of balconies and loggias, electrical wiring and all necessary communications. In multi-unit buildings, there is a place for a concierge or security post, and a spacious lobby is designed.

Infrastructure

How developed is the infrastructure in the Emerald LCD in Troitsk? Reviews indicate that there is everything you need in everyday life. A shopping and entertainment complex with grocery stores, service industry organizations, bank branches, as well as a cafe and a fitness center will appear on the territory of the complex.

The complex is focused on families, including with young children. While parents will work, the kids will be in kindergarten, designed for 120 people. A preschool educational institution is located on the territory of the complex under round-the-clock video surveillance, providing a high level of security to pupils.

Service technicians monitor the smooth operation of all communications and utilities. All residents of the LCD "Emerald" in Troitsk will have access to the developed infrastructure of the city.
