Where is the warm sea in September? Summer warm vacation in the fall!

In fact, a vacation in September has many advantages. One of them, which, of course, will please many, is that prices in this period are much lower than at the height of summer. In addition, the peak of heat is already left behind, that is, you will not languish from the heat. This is partly why September is traditionally considered the best time to relax with children. It remains only to find out where the warm sea is in September - and forward, towards unforgettable beach adventures!

Tunisia

Tunisia is extremely attractive to tourists in every way. Firstly, the climate here is extremely mild, and the beautifully equipped beaches on the shores of the warm Mediterranean Sea are perfect for the rest of the family. Almost all hotels are located a few steps from the sea, which is very convenient. The cost of rest by modern standards remains quite affordable. In addition, here you can appreciate all the advantages of thalassotherapy - therapeutic procedures with the use of sea water, algae and mud.

Morocco

Another country where the warm sea in September is a common thing. Morocco is ideal for both active and passive recreation. In addition to a beach holiday, you will get an excellent opportunity to go on excursions to historical sights, for example, in Casablanca, Marrakesh or Essaouira. You can even go here with children: for young travelers in Moroccan cities and resorts there are many playgrounds and entertainment centers.

Italy

Italy is very good at the beginning of autumn. Here you can not only fully enjoy swimming in warmed sea water and relax on the picturesque beaches, but also visit many historical and cultural monuments. Among the most popular resorts where the warm sea in September and a lot of entertainment for every taste - Liguria, the Adriatic coast, the Riviera de Ulisse, the Venetian Riviera and, of course, the islands (Sicily, Sardinia and Ischia).

Brazil
