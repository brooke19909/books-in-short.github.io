Craft "Snowman from Plastic Cups" with your own hands in 30 minutes!

Festive interior decoration helps to tune in a positive way and creates an indescribable magical atmosphere. Want to decorate your house with large pieces at minimal cost? The best craft for you is a snowman made of plastic cups. Even a child can make such a sculpture with his own hands; no special skills are required.

Materials and Tools

The basis of this craft is plastic cups. The easiest way is to buy new ones in large packages, but you can and diligently collect used ones throughout the year, washing them thoroughly. A snowman made of standard-sized plastic cups consists of at least 300 individual elements. Consider this when preparing material. Moreover, it is desirable to use the same cups for each ball - so the joints will be stronger, and the finished figure will look neater.





Another option is to choose different types of glasses for each of the balls. Accordingly, for the lower one you need to choose larger ones, and for the upper one - a smaller diameter. Cups are connected using a conventional stationery stapler. Stock up on enough brackets - they will need at least one standard package. A fabric, a scarf, a hat or a New Year's cap, ping-pong balls are all elements of the decor of your sculpture. Do you want to make the most beautiful snowman out of plastic cups? Manufacturing technology

A stable figure will turn out if you make it out of two balls (head and body). Start spreading the lower hemisphere. To do this, connect 25 cups in a neat circle. Repeat the next row, and lay out the third level in a checkerboard pattern. Continue to collect the cups in the same sequence, forming a volume ball. However, you do not need to close it, leave a small hole in the upper row - it will be more convenient to fix the second workpiece. The lower ball consists of 5-7 rows.





Put the hemisphere aside and start assembling the head, it is done on the same principle as the body, in the first row there should be 17 cups.
