Hotel Anyavee Railay Resort 3 * (Thailand, Krabi): description, traveler reviews

Krabi Province, located in southern Thailand, is today considered one of the most beautiful and, most importantly, pristine areas in the country. Its population is small, and the influx of tourists here even during peak season is not the same as in other regions, for example, in Phuket, however, this resort is considered one of the most popular destinations. The Krabi coast, starting from the southern island of Lanta and ending with northern Phang Ng, is a series of picturesque limestone rocky mountains, overgrown with palm trees and exotic plants, and pristine beaches.



Holidays in Krabi

Vacationing in this paradise will be interesting for almost all categories of vacationers: the shallow warm sea, numerous national parks, beautiful lakes, amazing animals found on hiking trails, kayaks, diving with fame throughout the world, surrounded by magnificent colored fish and starfish ... This is not a complete list of what can be seen on Krabi.

The province is positioned by tour operators as one of the most successful and at the same time expensive vacation spots. Every year thousands of travelers come here who equally want to get acquainted with the rich and mysterious Thai culture, personally see the most interesting places on Krabi and, of course, get their ‚Äúportion‚Äù of the sun, sunbathing on a clean sand and swimming in the gentle sea. At the same time, there are not so many beaches. Apparently, therefore, in the high season they literally do not bump into them, because there are plenty of people who want to relax.

The most popular beach - Railay - is surrounded by a rocky cape. It can only be reached by sea transport. Here, beautiful snow-white sand, several viewing platforms and caves and a real lagoon. The beach is especially popular among young lovers and honeymooners who come here on their honeymoon. There are no roads, cars do not drive, but there is a jungle.

Another beach - Klong Muang - is perfect for those who avoid noisy companies and want to relax in peace and quiet. Nearby, several hotels have been built with high "stardom", designed specifically for such a tourist.



Hotel selection

The infrastructure of the Krabi province is unevenly developed. There are still deserted beaches here, you can settle in detached bungalows that are practically isolated from the whole world, taste the local cuisine, which is based on rice and soy, fruits, coconut milk and, of course, seafood.

Getting to Krabi is very easy. A modern international airport has been built here, from which regular transport departs. Almost all hotels in Krabi operate with a transfer. Reseeding on a boat, tourists swim to any island that attracts attention, on which 2-3 hotels are built on the strength, and where, except for electric generators, nothing reminds of civilization anymore.

Far from the small cost of tourist trips to Krabi does not scare the Russians at all. Experienced travelers know that a huge advantage of staying here is a wide choice of hotel. You can choose the type of accommodation for almost any wallet and taste. If you wish, you can stay in a chic five-star apartment, or you can simply rent a bungalow on the beach or go to a relatively inexpensive hotel with a 3 * category, for example, Anyavee Railay Resort.



Description

Located on the Pranang Peninsula, on the first line, this hotel complex is lost among coconut trees. It is built on a large territory and is a cascade of bungalows descending to the water, surrounded by a tropical garden. To get to Anyavee Railay Resort, tourists make a rather exciting trip by car - from the international airport in Krabi to the ferry. Transfer by water on the boat takes another ten minutes - and here is an amazing hotel complex.

Thanks to the convenient location of Anyavee Railay Resort, guests can easily explore popular attractions, such as Pranang Cave, Orchid Garden, pearl farm, National Park, waterfalls and hot springs, mangroves, etc. The hotel was built in 2005. More recently, it has been renovated.

Infrastructure

Having crossed the threshold of Anyavee Railay Resort, vacationers immediately plunge into the unique atmosphere that prevails here. In two minutes walk from the administrative building there is a sea with clear turquoise water, a beach with white sand and majestic cliffs. Exactly such a picture is observed by visitors. A fairly spacious beach on both sides as if bordered by giant rocks, overgrown with bushes and trees. They, according to extreme sports fans, are a real challenge for climbers. Only the bravest ones manage to climb a steep wall directly from the sea.



Diving enthusiasts will appreciate the colorful coral reefs. The infrastructure of Anyavee Railay Resort 3 * includes concierge services, a garden, a 24-hour front desk. Hotel guests can use luggage storage, laundry and ironing services, fax or print documents.

If desired and upon request, personal registration is possible. The hotel has smoking and disabled rooms. Pets are not allowed.

Nutrition

In Anyavee Railay Resort 3 * there are three food systems: "breakfast", "half board" and "full board". The choice is at the discretion of the guests. The main restaurant is located right on the beach. Here tourists can plunge into the warm and cozy atmosphere. The seductive smells and aromas of the Thai cuisine of the pre-ordered restaurant will not leave anyone indifferent. In an effort to satisfy the needs of customers, the chef constantly improvises and creates real culinary masterpieces.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has a bar. This is a great place to sit comfortably in an armchair and enjoy the refreshments offered here while enjoying the sea breeze.

Services for children

For young guests Anyavee Railay Resort 3 * (Krabi) provides a children's section in the pool. Upon request, parents can get a cot in the room or invite a paid nanny.

It is possible to purchase excursion tours at the registration desk. Many Russians remember elephant safaris and bike rides.
