DIY photo book: beautiful design of unforgettable moments of life

The first photo books appeared in Europe and quickly became popular and in demand. With the original design, wide-format shots glued together in a special way are an excellent way of design.





Photobook is the best place for photos

With the help of a photo book you can beautifully and originally create digital pictures. This will help organize a large number of photos and capture happy moments of life on colorful pages. There are many reasons for such a creative impulse: a wedding, a trip, the birth of a baby, a graduation party, an evening of meetings, and much more.

You can capture the family history, thematic photoshoot, achievements and successes of the child in an interesting way, beautifully arrange a variety of creative works or drawings. A self-made photo book is also a great gift for friends or relatives.

Having decided on the topic, it is worth thinking about design and design, as well as size and format. The book can be ordered from professionals, or you can make it yourself, make this activity an exciting hobby or even turn it into a profitable business.

Another thing is a photo book, which you can arrange as you like, select colors and backgrounds, frames and interesting templates, the number and size of pictures. Holding it in your hands is much nicer than flipping through a regular album or watching photos on a computer monitor.

"Photoshop" to help you

How to make a photo book with your own hands? There are a huge number of sites and programs offering assistance in creating and printing. The process of working with the simplest of them consists in dragging and dropping photos into the editor, selecting the background and design frames. The famous Photoshop photo editor is an excellent program for editing digital pictures, as well as creating all kinds of collages and photo books. However, some knowledge, skills and abilities when working with this editor should be acquired. However, any skill comes with experience.





How else do you make a photo book with your own hands? Luma Pix Foto Fusion is also useful and easy to use. It allows you to create a book from the proposed photos automatically. The editor contains a huge number of colorful ready-made templates, a lot of ways to decorate and transform images. The program is mastered quite simply with the help of video tutorials. There are also programs that do not require activation. Their download is offered by sites involved in printing photo books. These include Myfotobooks, Fotoboo, Imagebook, Printbook. The master class of this process inspires creativity.





The electronic layout of the photo book is ready. What's next?

When the project is ready, carefully reviewed and verified, it is time to choose further actions. Option number 1 - sending the book to print and order binding with further mailing. Of course, this pleasure is worth a lot, but the result of such work is impressive.

Option number 2 - print pictures and glue the book yourself, as well as choose a beautiful soft or hard cover. In this case, it is best to use self-adhesive paper for printing photos, and for decor to buy sets of stickers. Both events are good, but a photobook created with soul and strength from beginning to end causes special feelings, although it involves a laborious process.



Tips & Tricks

If your creative choice is a self-made photo book, then before its design should carefully study the capabilities of the selected editor. Practically all of them have the function of creating a collage, choosing a design, cropping, and so on. To make a beautiful background, you can search for pictures of the picturesque area, various natural landscapes.

The cover of the photo book deserves special attention. It usually puts the best and favorite photo from the list. Binding options can be very diverse. On the pages of the book you can place notes and comments on pictures. So even more interesting will be a do-it-yourself photo book. Album templates are not limited to helping to beautifully capture moments from life. Samples can be used to create a portfolio or catalog to advertise any product.
