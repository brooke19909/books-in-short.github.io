Hotel Nikos Hotel 2 * (Greece, Halkidiki): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Greece is a hospitable country, every tourist can find here rest to their liking - beach, sports, medical or historical and educational. Beautiful beaches and clear sea, picturesque cliffs and many attractions attract travelers from all over the world.

Malia Resort

On the Greek island of Crete, a half hour drive from its administrative center of Heraklion, is the resort of Malia. This small town, with an indigenous population of about four thousand people, is an attractive and popular place. There is a lot of sunshine all year round. The swimming season is quite long: it begins in May, ends in October and is accompanied by warm weather without significant precipitation.





The beaches of Malia are excellent - there are clean sea and sand. Within the city, they are equipped with all the necessary beach attributes - sunbeds, umbrellas, water attraction complexes, entertainment for adults and children. Outside the city there are wild beaches, they are less crowded, have beautiful landscapes.

The city of Malia is a predominantly youth resort. Many bars, discos, restaurants and nightclubs attract young people here. Families with children often come here. Depending on the needs and financial opportunities, guests of the resort are offered a variety of hotels to stay.

Nikos Hotel 2 *, Malia

Half an hour walk from the center of Malia, in a quiet and comfortable place, is Nikos Hotel 2 *. It occupies one four-story building and has 39 rooms for guests. The hotel belongs to the category of "two stars". It offers spacious studio rooms (average area - about 22 square meters) with balconies. Maximum occupancy is designed for three people. An outdoor swimming pool is on site. The city beach of Malia is less than a kilometer from the hotel, shared with it by the road. A distance of no more than one kilometer separates Nikos Hotel 2 * from the supermarket, restaurants, bars and entertainment venues of the city.



Attractive and transport location Nikos Hotel 2 *. Crete, Heraklion is a popular destination. You can get here by plane, during the holiday season there are many charter flights from many cities of the world landing at Heraklion Airport. Tourists will deliver a taxi to the city of Malia, which can be booked in advance, or a regular passenger bus. A bus stop is located near the hotel. For an additional fee, the Nikos Hotel 2 * administration will offer a comfortable transfer from the airport to the hotel (or vice versa).

Hotel rooms

Single tourists, numerous groups of guests, couples without children and with children - Greece is waiting for and welcomes any category of travelers. Nikos Hotel 2 * is no exception. There are single (eight of them) and double rooms. Many of the rooms designed for two are equipped with an extra bed; the hotel has the opportunity to put a cot in the room. Architectural decisions during the design and construction of the hotel made it possible to fill every room with sun, light and comfort. The rooms have a pleasant design, modern materials are used for their decoration, including beautiful and environmentally friendly ceramic tiles. Guest rooms have the necessary furniture, a refrigerator and an electric kettle. Air conditioning is available at an additional cost.

Each room has a private bathroom with a bathtub and shower, and guests are offered a hairdryer. Of hygiene items there is toilet paper.

Guests can choose between smoking and non-smoking rooms. Housekeeping is organized two to three times a week. In the same period of time, guests will be replaced with bed linen and towels.



Facilities at Nikos Hotel 2 *

Hotel guests are offered many necessary and enjoyable services.

Tourists who want to organize their vacation without using a car can conveniently and safely leave it near the hotel - there is free private parking here. For an additional fee, the hotel will help organize the storage of cash, important documents, jewelry and other valuables in a safe. For bulky items and hand luggage there is a paid left-luggage office. It will help to rent a car or bike.



The hotel has paid Wi-Fi in common areas and outdoors, as well as fixed Internet access (wired). Guests can use the services of a business center - this is facsimile and photocopying of necessary documents.

For organizing interesting leisure activities, there is a games room, a pool table and table football, a relaxation area with satellite TV, and computers with Internet access.

If necessary, in Nikos Hotel 2 * you can get qualified first aid for free.

Leisure

The beach is within walking distance of Nikos Hotel 2 *. Crete is famous for its sandy beaches, the best of them are in the resort of Malia. There is clean sand, the entrance to the water is uniform and gentle, the sea water is clear and warm, there are umbrellas and sunbeds - all this has a comfortable and pleasant rest. To make the vacation more active, the beaches are equipped with children's and sports grounds, which offer many attractions, slides, slopes. There is a catamaran rental on the beach, and banana rides are organized. There are numerous bars with soft drinks, ice cream and light snacks.

Reservation

You can book a hotel room through numerous travel agencies and on the websites of organizations that provide hotel reservation services. It is possible to cancel the reservation free of charge two weeks before the expected arrival for most rooms of Nikos Hotel 2 *. Prepayment for reservation is not charged.

Cost of living

Sufficiently comfortable living conditions and affordable prices are offered to guests by Nikos Hotel 2 *, Greece. Malia is an expensive resort, so many tourists prefer the Nikos Hotel for the reason that the cost of rooms is quite low. On average, the cost of living for one night per person is about 1800 rubles. For early bookings, the cost of two people for nine nights is less than thirty thousand rubles (the price includes breakfast). When living in a room of a child under the age of twelve, without providing him with an extra bed, no fee will be charged. Children under two years stay free of charge with their parents, they are provided with a berth - a cot.
