general description

The Tele2 contact center, like many customer service services of other telecom operators, allows you to quickly and completely free of charge get the information you are interested in. According to the terms of the consultation, information is provided only to the owner of the number, that is, the person to whom the SIM card is registered. In practice, it turns out that it is enough to know who the number is issued for. After all, it often happens like this - SIM cards were bought, for example, by a husband for his wife and daughter and, accordingly, are officially listed for him. Moreover, it is not at all necessary that the wife will not be able to clarify the data of interest to her on her SIM card and contact the Tele2 contact center.

You can call the customer support center not only from the SIM card that is served by this service provider. Calls from international roaming

No money will be charged for negotiations with a specialist, but on condition that the call is made from the SIM card of this operator.

Before traveling to other countries, you need to make sure that roaming is provided there and familiarize yourself with tariffing information. Please note that for each country the prices are different.
