Crochet shawls: schemes or descriptions?

Each needlewoman has her own professional secrets. Someone can create, relying solely on the scheme. Another craftswoman must have a description that will describe in detail what and how to do. This order of things seems quite common when it comes to knitting clothes. In this case, it is necessary to strictly follow the manufacturing sequence. Especially when it comes to creating clothes that should have a strictly designated size. And if we are talking about crocheting a shawl - are schemes or descriptions better? It rarely sets certain size requirements. Therefore, we will try to figure this out.



Probably, while studying thematic sites, many noticed that it seems that the knitting pattern is the same, but the size of the finished product is different. At the same time, needlewomen necessarily indicate the threads from which they created this stylish accessory. It often happens that the yarn differs slightly in thickness, but the size of the finished product varies by 5-10 centimeters. And this is essential.

The reason for this lies in the fact that the craftswoman did not have a description in which the density of knitting was indicated. We are talking about the number of loops and rows that fall every 10 cm. Therefore, keep in mind that if you decide to crochet a shawl, there may not be enough patterns. Especially if the size of the finished product is important for you. In such a situation, the manufacture of a sample is an important component for the execution of a shawl crocheted, the circuit of which you have available.



You can tie a free-form sample yourself and calculate how many rows and loops will be for every 10 cm. This will initially determine the size of the work ahead and what you get after it is completed. So that the crochet shawl, the scheme of which you have, fully meets your expectations, start work from the very place from which you will needlework in the future. Especially if the pattern is complex enough for you. In this case, knitting, and you will definitely notice it, will advance much faster, delighting you and bringing pleasure. At the same time, you can master the pattern on threads easier, and then make a sample from the yarn from which you will create.



Today, crochet shawls are very popular and in demand. Schemes for free can easily be found. They periodically appear also in thematic magazines devoted to this topic. In addition, many experienced needlewomen prepare special workshops, which in their visibility can significantly surpass the diagram and description combined. In this case, step by step photos are printed that allow you to best understand the sequence and features of manufacturing any, even the most complex model.

Thus, if you decide to crochet your shawls, the diagrams or description do not really matter. The main thing is to understand how a specific pattern is formed, and then you will not have problems in the process of creating this fashionable and stylish accessory.
