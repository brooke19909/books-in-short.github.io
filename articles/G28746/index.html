Construction technology

The residential complex "Newton" (Chelyabinsk) is being built according to the method of monolithic-frame construction, the external walls are made of three-layer, brick, with a layer of insulation, the interior is also brick, but in one layer. The facades are made with spectacular panoramic glazing. In the entrance groups it is planned to allocate a place to store bicycles and separately for strollers. Concierge is also available. Elevators in buildings are installed by OTIS. Manufacturers report that they are fast and silent.

A novelty for Chelyabinsk is the mechanical supply and ventilation ventilation, which will be used in the apartments of the complex. As conceived by the designers, such a system will allow to maintain clean air and rationally use the exhaust removed from the apartments.
