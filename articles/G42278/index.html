Sanatorium "Homeland", Sochi: review, description, features and reviews

You can talk about the resort city of Sochi endlessly. This is one of the favorite places of many residents of our country, where they come in the summer and even in the winter. A magnificent combination of sea and mountain air, a large number of interesting sights, a huge selection of accommodation options - all this is in Sochi.

It is worth visiting at least once in a lifetime in this sea city of the Krasnodar Territory. And so that your vacation here will leave a truly indelible impression, you should choose the magnificent Grand Hotel and Spa Rodina (Sochi) for your stay, which will transfer every guest to the atmosphere of incredible luxury luxury.

A variety of resorts in Sochi

Big Sochi is a real storehouse of spa holidays on the Black Sea. There are a lot of these recreational facilities, because the local climate is perfect for treating many diseases.





Most of the sanatoriums were built here during the Soviet Union, but to this day they operate at full capacity and warmly welcome each new guest. They successfully treat a variety of diseases with the help of various procedures, because the medical equipment is very good. To improve the health of patients, therapeutic mud and mineral waters are also used, which this region is rich in.

Sometimes the choice of a sanatorium in Sochi can be very difficult, because each of them is a great place for both relaxing and improving health. It is worth starting from your preferences, the location of the health resort and the reviews about it of those who have already undergone treatment in it.

Grand Hotel Location

The former sanatorium Rodina (Sochi) was built in the very center of the resort. Nevertheless, this is a hotel located on the first coastline, because its large territory surrounded by greenery smoothly descends to the sea itself and the equipped beach.

How can a tourist find a hotel

Finding the Grand Hotel and Spa Rodina (Sochi) among the many hotels in the city is easy. It is located on Vinohradnaya Street, at number 33.

This place can be reached on foot from the railway station of the city of Sochi - such a walk will take only 20 minutes. You can also use minibuses at numbers 25, 17 and 6 and get off at the stop "Sanatorium" Krasmashevsky ".

Sochi will have to go from Sochi International Airport either by the Airport-Sochi express bus, or by bus number 124 to the same train station, and then get to the hotel in the manner described above. You can also take a taxi or book a transfer at the hotel.
