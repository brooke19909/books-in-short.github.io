ZTE Blade V8 Lite: specifications and description

Screen and camera

Want to have fun working on your smartphone? Then pay attention to the screen, or rather its characteristics. The ZTE Blade V8 Lite has a 5-inch display with a matrix made by IPS technology. The image quality is excellent. Color is good, saturation is at an optimal level. The picture is displayed in HD format. The pixel density is average (294 ppi), but this is enough to not see the breakdown into "squares".

But users will not be able to enjoy the cameras. The manufacturer sold 5 and 8 megapixel modules. Expect professional shots not worth it. The main camera is equipped with a flash and autofocus, but they do not greatly affect the result. In poor lighting, photos are blurry, with poor detail and lack of color.



ZTE Blade V8 Lite: Performance Features

This model did not differ in a powerful platform. The developers installed the mid-range MT6750 processor of the Chinese company MediaTek. At first it may seem that the 8-core chip is capable of much, but in practice, not everything is so rosy. Computing modules accelerate to 1500 MHz. The maximum device - applications with medium requirements. Most likely, it will not work to install and play 3D games at high settings, you will have to sacrifice the quality of the graphics. By the way, the Mali-T860MP2 video card is responsible for the latter. The amount of RAM is two gigabytes. This parameter is average, although for the price category, which includes the ZTE Blade V8 Lite Blue, the characteristics could be better.

The developers integrated 16 GB of memory. Some volume is already occupied by system folders. Given the versatility of the smartphone, the remaining storage may not be enough, so the user is invited to expand it to 256 GB by installing a USB flash drive.
