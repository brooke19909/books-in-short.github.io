Tsandripsh

One of the most diverse and at the same time typical resorts of Abkhazia is Tsandripsh. For a number of years, many Russians have preferred a vacation to Abkhazia instead of well-known resorts. Tsandripsh without intermediaries is cheap and also beautiful.

This village has an extensive mountain and coastal tourist infrastructure, has a large number of places of entertainment and special attractions, beautiful pebble beaches. The local colorful nature provides perfect conditions for year-round recreation and restoration of health in the village of Tsandripsha. People with different health conditions have a rest here, but the healing air, sun and sea are suitable for everyone. The resort, tourists say in reviews, can be called one of the most beautiful on the coast.

In Tsandripsha - one of the centers of events in the past - there are various archaeological and ancient historical monuments, for example:

  • tsandripsha ancient basilica;
  • Khashup stone fortress;
  • tsandripshsky early medieval temple.




On the territory of the settlement and in the places nearest to it, scientists constantly find signs of ancient human civilizations. The current village was clearly significant even many years ago, which is proved by finds from ancient marble, which decorated the buildings of those years. Then Tsandripsh was a lively center of pilgrimage, which many believers went to.

In addition, among the Russians who arrived to rest in Tsandripsh, there are many lovers of pristine nature. They enthusiastically go rafting along the purest mountain rivers or visit the beautiful canyon Khashupse.

Housing

The housing stock of the village is able to satisfy any requirements of tourists coming to Tsandripsh for a vacation. A variety of boarding houses and hotels, motels and recreation centers provide the maximum number of services. However, rest in Abkhazia in Tsandripsha in the private sector without intermediaries will cost much cheaper. Separate small houses can be found in the private sector at different prices than in the resort area.

The resort village is located on the banks of the Haupsche River. At the entrance to the village of Tsandripsh you can admire the beautiful white cliffs - one of the attractions of Abkhazia. You can arrive at the resort both by road and by rail. The village of Tsandripsha (Abkhazia) has its own railway station.

The climate in the village is warm, Mediterranean. It rains regularly, but it does not last long. The swimming season begins in May and allows vacationers to swim in the velvet waves until mid-autumn.

The population of Tsandripsha is small - about 5 thousand people. About half of them are Armenians, the rest are Russians, Abkhazians and Ukrainians.





How to relax

Most Russians come to Abkhazia for beach tourism and settle in the private sector. You can swim comfortably in the sea for almost six months - from late spring to mid-autumn. Rest in Abkhazia without intermediaries is very inexpensive. Prices for accommodation and food are significantly lower than in other Russian resorts. But the quality of beaches and coastal areas is very high.

In addition to a pleasant beach holiday, the country provides the opportunity to undergo spa treatment. There are large sanatoriums and pensions in the cities of Gagra, Pitsunda, Sukhum.

The potential of sightseeing and outdoor activities in Abkhazia is practically inexhaustible: the legendary Ritsa Lake, the oldest Christian churches of the first centuries of our era, the New Athos cave, fascinating waterfalls and stormy ice mountain rivers.

It is best to arrive in Abkhazia by car, this will give the opportunity to see the whole country with its innumerable attractions and beautiful landscapes.
