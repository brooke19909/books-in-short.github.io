L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Kemer, Camyuva): description of rooms, entertainment, service, reviews

In case of a desire to go to rest at sea abroad, many Russian tourists opt for Turkey. Within it there is a considerable number of luxury resorts where hotels with the best living and service conditions are presented to the attention of vacationers. One of these is L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * in Kemer. It is here that many tour operators recommend to go on vacation, because here, according to them, you can feel like a real king.





Location

L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * is located in Kemer - in one of the largest resort cities in Turkey, in one of its quietest areas. It is this condition that determines most of the lodging category - they are usually people of age and couples who want to spend their holidays in seclusion.

This hotel is located on the very coast of the warm and clean Mediterranean Sea, where a magnificent mild climate reigns. Literally 60 kilometers from L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Camyuva) is the center of Antalya.

general information

The hotel was built relatively recently - in 2007. Over the entire period of its existence, major repairs have already been made in the territory and in the buildings (in 2013), as a result of which the interior has been refreshed and living conditions have significantly improved.

L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Kemer) occupies a relatively small territory - only 16 thousand square meters. m, which is not much compared to other resorts of this kind located in the neighborhood. On its territory there is a huge amount of entertainment for guests of different categories, however they are placed very compactly.

Rooms

When describing the rooms of L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 *, one should definitely mention that they are located very compactly, in three different types of buildings. The main fund is located in a large bright four-story building, on the territory of which there are other important infrastructure: bars, meeting rooms, restaurants, clubs, etc. The second part of the apartment is concentrated in another building called Annex, which also consists of four tiers. The attention of the guests is another category of buildings - six luxury villas, each of which is a two-story building.

All apartments available here are furnished in the best possible way. The interior of each room was designed by professional designers who created an environment with a high degree of comfort.

Each building of the hotel is fully equipped with climatic equipment, the action of which rescues from heat in the summer and creates warm conditions in the cool period. Each apartment has a telephone, a safe for storing small items, and a TV connected to a satellite dish. Many of them have balconies with wonderful views of the sea and gardens.

Main building

The rooms located in the main building of the L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * are divided into two categories: standard and family.

In the standard category, there are 191 rooms, two of which are designed for people with physical disabilities (they are equipped with custom-made furniture). Each room of this type is very spacious, they have modern furniture designed for a certain number of guests (from 2 to 4 people). There are beds (double or single), and some have an additional folding sofa, which can serve as an extra bed. As for furniture, the standard rooms have a certain number of beds (from 3 to 5), and if necessary, a cot is provided.





Annex enclosure

This is a separate building, within which there is a number of rooms, consisting of standard class apartments in a classic style. Their total number is 110 pieces. All the apartments presented here are small in size - about 22-25 square meters. m, therefore it is impossible to accommodate a large number of guests in them - a maximum of 3 persons.

The rooms in the Annex are very bright and feature modern, custom-made furniture. There are cabinets for storing personal belongings, plasma TVs, as well as bedside tables. Each room has air conditioning, allowing guests to independently adjust the temperature in the room.

The Annex Standard rooms have individual bathrooms, which have all the necessary plumbing, a bathtub, a hairdryer, and bath accessories.





Villas

Guests who wish to stay away from the rest of the hotel guests and completely immerse themselves in thoughts can stay in any of the six villas that are on the territory of L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Camyuva). Each of them is a beautiful two-story building. All of them are located on the same perimeter of the common territory, at a short distance from the main building - about 800 meters.

The building has three sleeping areas, the same number of bathrooms, as well as a living area, furnished with upholstered furniture and electronics. Guests who rent a villa for themselves can cook their own meals in the equipped kitchen.

As for the conditions, then at L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * villas the service is provided by a team of workers: maids, security guards, gardeners and technical staff. Near each of them there is a small pool in which guests can swim, also there are outdoor terraces for sunbathing.

Conditions for children

Often, tour operators present this hotel as an ideal place for a family vacation, so they often come here with small children. The hotel owners have provided a number of conditions for their comfortable stay in the territory of L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Kemer).

There is a well-equipped children's club where parents can leave their children from 4 to 12 years old - here they will be looked after by professional animators and nannies. Also on the territory of this recreation area there are several playgrounds where children can engage in active sports and play with their peers.

An open-air mini-pool has been created, which is equipped with all the safety standards provided for small guests. Each restaurant and cafe has several special comfortable chairs that can accommodate kids and choose bright, colorful dishes from a separate special menu.

As for accommodation, for guests with small children in the room provided a separate bed or stroller.

Entertainment

L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * is a place where guests will definitely not be bored, as a team of professional animators always works for them. Every evening, tourists can take part in incendiary discos and competitions. From the morning until lunchtime, recreational sports programs and competitions are held near the pools.

The hotel often organizes excursions to local attractions, in which groups of guests take part. Some tour operators include their price in advance in the price of the ticket, in other cases, the guest can independently pay for their participation in it (optional).





Sport

Adherents of a healthy lifestyle living in L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * in Turkey can play sports while on vacation. To do this, there is an excellent hall in which a large number of simulators are installed. Women enjoy the fitness room and aerobics classes, which are held at the hotel under the guidance of instructors.

Guests who prefer team sports can head to specially equipped football, basketball or volleyball fields. In addition, there is a large table tennis room, as well as a golf course, which can be visited for free.





Spa area

In the main building of the hotel is equipped with a wonderful spa area, which is located on a fairly large area. Its structure includes a Turkish hammam, as well as a sauna. In addition, guests have the opportunity to visit the massage room or beautician. Visiting these areas is completely free, and all hotel guests who have reached the age of 14 years can take this opportunity.

Pools

The hotel has several aquazones where guests can truly relax in the blue pool, and then bask in the sun on comfortable sun loungers under beach umbrellas. Not far from each pool there is a bar where guests can enjoy cold drinks, lemonade, freshly squeezed juice, as well as light and healthy salads.





Near the main building there are two large pools of 200 square meters. m area each. There is also a small water park, which consists of two slides designed for outdoor activities for both adults and children. A mini-pool has been built for small guests. Under the roof of the building there is an indoor pool, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is also 200 square meters. m, you can find it in the spa area.





Beach

The hotel has its own beach, which is literally 50 meters from the main four-story building. In order to get on it, you must cross the road. The total length of the coastline is not small - it is 100 meters. The descent into the water is gentle - it is not only very convenient for adults, but also safer for children.

The beach of L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * is completely pebbled, it is clean and well-maintained - there are absolutely all conditions so that every vacationer can feel in a state of complete comfort. On its territory there are several locker rooms, toilets, as well as a huge number of sun loungers and umbrellas, which guests can use for free. Towels and air mattresses are provided here, and small bars serving refreshing drinks are offered to the attention of tourists.

Here, every tourist is offered a series of water activities. Surfing and water skiing are especially popular. Fans to explore the beauty of the underwater world will not miss the opportunity to dive underwater with scuba gear, and extreme lovers will certainly fly into the sky by parachute.

Restaurants

Dining at L'Oceanica Beach Resort Hotel 5 * is carried out in local restaurants and bars, which are located in the main building and Annex. The main restaurant serves a daily buffet, and also serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. The main building also has two establishments serving Turkish and seafood dishes.

The bars, which are several on site, serve a variety of drinks, including alcoholic, coffee, tea, and light snacks.
