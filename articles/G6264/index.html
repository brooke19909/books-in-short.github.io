How to activate the Crossroads card via the Internet, by phone

Perekrestok is a supermarket chain that was founded back in 1995 in Moscow. Today it includes 387 stores in different regions of the country. How to draw up a card

To participate in the program, you must purchase goods in the supermarket chain "Crossroads" and "Green Crossroads" in the amount of 500 rubles. The card is issued by the cashier free of charge. It is sealed in an envelope containing a form. It must be filled out and transferred to the cashier at the next visit. This is one of the ways to activate the Crossroads card (Nizhny Novgorod).

Earning and writing off points

Bonuses are credited to the account when paying for any goods in the Perekrestok chain of stores, provided that a club card is presented. The number of points awarded depends on the amount of the purchase:

1. Up to 2 thousand rubles. - 1 bonus for every 10 rubles spent.

2. From 2 to 5 thousand rubles. - 5 points for every 10 rubles.

3. More than 5000 rubles. - 10 bonuses.

The accumulated points can then be used to pay for goods in the same stores. Conversion occurs at the rate: 10 bonuses = 1 ruble. To write off the discount, it is necessary to inform the cashier before payment how much you want to pay off at the expense of points.
