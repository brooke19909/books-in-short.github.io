Amshensky Yard (Sochi): comfort and gorgeous landscapes

After a busy working year, anyone is looking forward to a long-awaited vacation. Now more and more people are eager to spend an interesting time in their own country. An original and beautiful place to relax is the village of Lazarevskoye near Sochi. Here is the guest house "Amshensky Dvor".

Hotel Features

The location of the complex was chosen very well. "Amshensky Dvor" (Sochi) is located near the mountain river Psezuapse. The windows offer a gorgeous mountain landscape. Beaches by the sea are a 20-30 minute walk. Vacationers in the evenings enjoy swimming in the river.

The hotel is located in a quiet place. There is no city bustle and noise. The cozy courtyard, fenced by a high fence, makes it possible to calmly relax and enjoy the peace. The area around the guest house is ennobled and surrounded by greenery.

From the window of the dining room you can watch the ostriches that live at the hotel. Children really like this sight. The complex has wooden carvings of animals. A beautiful fountain is constantly working and places for relaxation are equipped:

  • benches;
  • arbors;
  • little tables.




A lot of space is occupied by beautiful green lawns. Children can play on them. On the territory there is a small parking lot for guests' cars.

Accommodations

"Amshensky Dvor" (Sochi) offers its visitors rooms of different comfort and capacity:

  • 2-seater;
  • 3-seater;
  • 4-seater;
  • Suite

All rooms are equipped with modern and comfortable furniture and have amenities inside. The rooms have:

  • TV
  • split systems;
  • electric kettle;
  • refrigerator;
  • glasses with a decanter;
  • iron can be taken from the maid.




Room cleaning is carried out at the first request of the client. Sheets are changed every 3 days. For an additional payment, it is possible to provide additional space for a child under 12 years old with a 25% discount.

The attendants are always friendly and strive to fulfill any adequate request from customers.

Entertainment and food

Amshensky Dvor (Sochi) can offer its guests a delicious and expanded menu in its restaurant or dining room. Near the hotel there are several shops where you can purchase the necessary goods and products.





The complex has an outdoor restaurant in the evening. The bands play live music here. Guests at the hotel can enjoy a sauna or play billiards. Picnic lovers are provided with barbecue facilities. You can spend time in your free time playing board games.

In the evening, guests can sing karaoke. Amshensky Dvor (hotel, Sochi) is located near the Nautilus water park. There is also a dolphinarium and an amusement park. Within a 10-minute walk there is a market where you can buy souvenirs and fresh fruit. Fans of beautiful places can book an excursion to the "Crab Gorge". You need to be prepared that you have to walk a lot.

The coast can be reached by bus, which runs from the bus stop near the hotel every hour. A memorable vacation will provide its visitors with the Amshen Yard (Sochi). Guest reviews are mostly positive. Minor flaws and situations, the staff tries to immediately correct. Customer requests are always taken into account. Therefore, tourists are ready to come here every year.
