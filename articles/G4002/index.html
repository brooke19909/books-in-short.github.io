How to turn off the answering machine on Megafon: managing the Voice Mail service

The service "Voice Mail" from the company "Megaphone" is an additional option and provides the subscriber with a number of advantages. Firstly, it is an opportunity to always stay in touch with important people, even if the battery is low on a mobile phone or there is no way to answer a call. The subscriber will be able to leave a voice message and transmit the information you need.

Secondly, the assistant in the form of voicemail will save the received messages, which you can listen to at any time, either through a mobile device or from a computer. If you have already managed to use this service and are now puzzled by the question of how to turn off the answering machine on Megafon, we recommend that you read this article.
