"Planet Zero", "Beeline." Planet Zero tariff: reviews

It is no secret that mobile services between countries are charged according to a completely different principle, unlike conversations within the state. This is normal, since the operators do not provide them on their own, by their own means, but in agreement with other service providers. Since all this rests on the financial relationship between the companies, the cost of calls for subscribers can vary. Therefore, if you try hard, you can find tariff plans that will be more beneficial for calls abroad.

One of them is the Planet Zero tariff. Beeline, one of the most popular mobile operators in Russia, provides it specifically for subscribers located abroad who want to talk with their relatives and friends in the Russian Federation. Read why this plan is so beneficial and what conditions it provides for, read in our article.





General fare conditions

The fact that the Planet Zero tariff plan (Beeline is its supplier) is beneficial for people abroad, the operator indicates on its official website and in any promotional materials related to the service. First of all, users can see a bright headline informing that incoming from the Russian Federation to numbers served on this plan are absolutely free.

Of course, for domestic communication, it looks wild; Do operators charge you for calling you? But if we talk about roaming, then this practice is absolutely normal. When the subscriber is in another country and they call him, most often he pays for the call. You can also recall the heyday of the era of mobile communications, when you had to pay for an incoming call even in your region. Therefore, in fact, this feature of the Planet Zero plan, Beeline, has made quite profitable for the subscriber. True, it is important to mention one limitation - we are talking about the first 20 minutes of the conversation. Further conversations will be charged at a rate of 10 rubles per minute for the first category of countries. We talk in more detail about what this means.





Cost of services for subscribers in "popular" countries

Given the different features of the agreements between mobile operators of other countries and Beeline, Planet Zero (how to connect the tariff, we will describe below) implies an unequal cost of services in each country. Therefore, all regions of the world were divided into several groups.

The first includes Europe, the CIS countries and some popular destinations for Russian subscribers, such as Egypt, China, the USA or Thailand. They have their own tariff - for one day of use you need to pay 60 rubles; in addition, the first 20 minutes of the incoming call are free, after which the cost of each reaches 10 rubles. As for the outgoing call, it will cost the subscriber 20 rubles per minute. SMS messages from these countries to Russia will cost 7 rubles.





Communication prices for other countries

In addition to the group of countries mentioned above, there is one more. It brings together some of the remaining regions, which are obviously less popular for domestic users of mobile services, therefore, Beeline (Planet Zero) roaming in them will cost more. In particular, the daily fee for a subscriber is 100 rubles, and incoming calls from Russia after the 20th minute rise in price to 15 rubles. At the same time, the price of each minute of an outgoing conversation rises to as much as 45 rubles. SMS messages rise in price insignificantly - up to 9 rubles.

The list of countries that fall into this category is longer. This includes, for example, Israel, India, Japan, Africa and the Middle East. The higher price of services, obviously, is explained by lower demand of subscribers in these areas. At the same time, the Planet Zero tariff ‚ÄúBeeline‚Äù makes it more profitable than some packages of services of other operators. Therefore, if you like (or have to) travel, this package deserves your attention. As you can see, it extends its effect even to exotic states.
