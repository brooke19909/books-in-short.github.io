Pension "Dubna", Alushta: description, services, reviews and photos

In June 1967, in Crimea, on its southern coast, the doors for vacationers opened in a new international health resort - a new cozy boarding house "Dubna" (Alushta) opened as a unit in the structure of JINR (Joint Institute for Nuclear Research). Here, scientists and their families not only rested. Using the Dubna boarding house, Alushta hosted congresses, symposia, meetings and seminars on nuclear research, in which representatives of more than twenty countries of the world participated.





Today

Now this is a health complex, where comfortable accommodation is available, providing good rest, mood, vitality and a high level of restoration of the health of vacationers there. Pension "Dubna" (Alushta) can offer not only comfortable rooms, but also full-fledged treatment, since a medical center and special dietary establishments work great there. In addition, there are several sports venues, conference rooms, a movie and concert hall, a dance floor, a beautifully landscaped beach and much more.

Pension "Dubna" (Alushta) can accept more than three hundred people at a time. All buildings are located in a picturesque park area, where a wonderful dream and a wonderful quiet holiday. The rooms are comfortable, they have all the necessary amenities. The entire territory of the boarding house is equipped with a wireless Internet network. Electricity, hot and cold water are distributed in a boarding house in an autonomous mode. Passenger vehicles work great. The boarding house has a certificate of compliance with the quality of service of Gosstandart of the Russian Federation.





"Professor's Corner"

This is the territory of Alushta, as if created specifically for walking, relaxing and creating a good mood, and the best in this regard, even on the scale of the entire southern coast of Crimea. Visitors here once certainly become regulars, coming here every year - it is impossible not to fall in love with this land. Back in the eighties of the nineteenth century, mansions and summer residences of famous people began to appear here, and, as a rule, most of them are representatives of science, and this is where the name comes from.

There are still magnificent old-timers of the resort, palaces of scientists who, coming on vacation, enjoyed studying the geology, history, fauna and flora of the Crimea. Here we can mention the professor of geology N. A. Golovkinsky, who stood at the origins of creating such a paradise as the "Professor's Corner", which only the best resorts in Switzerland and Italy can compare with. Wild cliffs covered with subtropical vegetation at the very blue water of the vast Black Sea cannot but cause delight.





"Work Area"

Alushta after the revolution began to change rapidly, like the whole country. The spirit of the time dictated new conditions of freedom, equality and fraternity. In 1923, near the Mount Kastel was built in peak of the "Professor" rest house "Work Area". Alushta now received vacationers not only from among well-off people, but also from ordinary steelmakers, miners, miners, farmers, teachers from all over the country.

Both the local and the visitors liked the new name so much that it took root quickly. Moreover, the whole area adjacent to the holiday home began to be called so. Gradually, the entire western outskirts of the city began to be called that way. Truly unforgettable impressions await everyone who has chosen Crimea for holidays. "Professor's Corner" is the most picturesque part of it. The seven-kilometer beautifully designed promenade with the Almond Grove water park, with Kastel night club soaring into the sky like a spaceship on take-off (where visitors can enjoy a bowling alley, an excellent dance floor, cafes, bars and European cuisine).

All of them belong to health camps, boarding houses, sanatoriums. On the right is a long city beach with fine sand. You can compare the beaches that Crimea offers. "Professor's Corner" will certainly be in the lead of any rating. They are all well equipped: a wide assortment of rentals, mandatory cabins and showers. From the sun you can hide under the awnings.

There are a lot of small enterprises where you can have a great meal or quench your thirst: canteens, cafes, restaurants, bars. They are so numerous that they cannot be counted. Many shops of various profiles are open, especially souvenir shops, as well as a market of local seasonal products that fully satisfy all the needs of vacationers, even if three meals a day are not included in their leisure options. And, of course, a lot of entertainment for every age and taste. Parks established in the nineteenth century with unique tropical plants, a water park, beach vacations and a huge number of interesting excursions.

Excursions

The southern coast of Crimea is so rich in sights, magnificent natural landscapes that few were able to fully explore all this. Vacationers in Alushta have the opportunity to visit any corner of the Crimea - tours are constantly provided, their choice is huge.

Almost everyone chooses a sightseeing tour, visits the amazing Crimean mountains, not similar to any mountain range in the world, very many go to Yalta, Sevastopol, Simferopol to museums and memorial places. But near Alushta there is something to see.

Details

This is, first of all, boat trips. The boat is demonstrated to guests almost the entire south of the Crimean coast. You can go diving and endlessly be amazed at the beauty of the underwater world. For the curious, there are excursions to the Aluston fortress, which is partially preserved from the sixth century and has a unique history. This is a rural Gothic temple of Alushta (All Saints), built in the Old English style. This is a full-flowing and quite powerful waterfall Dzhur-Dzhur (translated "babbling forever").
