Resort "Arshan"

This complex includes two hospitals at once - the sanatorium "Sayany" and "Edelweiss" (children's year-round recreation camp). Arshan Resort is located in a beautiful area - at the foot of the Sayan Mountains, on the banks of the Kyngarga River with its twelve waterfalls. In spring, you can climb up to the alpine lawns covered with poppies and herbs, admire the flowering of rosemary in summer, and the surrounding snow-capped peaks in winter.





According to its characteristics, this balneological and mountain-climatic resort with its carbonic waters has the same therapeutic indicators as those in Kislovodsk, Truskavets and Morshyn, Baden-Baden and Leukerbad.

The healing sources include carbon dioxide, sulfate-bicarbonate-magnesium-calcium, siliceous waters belonging to class 1, and sulfide mud. Their temperature also varies - from +11 (considered cold) to +45 degrees. Water is used for baths and drinking.
