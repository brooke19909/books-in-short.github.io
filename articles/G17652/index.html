Hotel complex "Bogatyr": description of rooms, services and reviews

Rest in Adler is a gentle sun and a clear warm sea, mountain air and pebble beaches, subtropical nature and a unique climate, as well as a sufficiently developed service. The Bogatyr hotel complex in Sochi Park is one of the most popular holiday destinations and families with children who dream of visiting a medieval castle, and young couples seeking solitude, and business people who want a break from the high-speed rhythm of life or, conversely, hold a fruitful meeting in an informal setting.

Description

The elegant four-star hotel complex Bogatyr (Adler) received its first visitors in the summer of 2014. In a short time, the hotel has become a symbol of luxury and comfort on the Black Sea coast of Greater Sochi. This was facilitated by its favorable location, the high level of service provided, a wide range of services, friendly staff and modern room equipment.

Accommodation

The hotel complex "Bogatyr" (Adler) consists of 278 rooms (100 of which overlook the sea), decorated in the medieval style of the castle, but equipped with all modern conveniences:

  • air conditioning;
  • TV with satellite channels;
  • wireless internet with free access;
  • telephone;
  • mini bar;
  • safe;
  • set for coffee and tea;
  • communication button with a hotel employee;
  • round-the-clock room service;
  • Private bathroom with Pascal Morabito toiletries;
  • hair dryer.




Daily cleaning is required. Change of linen and towels according to the schedule or at the request of the guest.

Standart room

The standard room (31-36 square meters) includes: a large double bed, a bunk bed or a sofa for children. Some rooms have a balcony overlooking the patio. The maximum number of guests in a room is four, of which two are adults, the rest are children. Some rooms are designed to accommodate guests with disabilities.

There is an additional opportunity to combine two neighboring standards into a spacious family room.

The hotel complex "Bogatyr" in the standard version costs from 4000 to 8900 rubles per day (season affects). This also includes:

  • Buffet breakfast
  • wireless internet connection;
  • kids club;
  • parking of cars in a guarded parking lot.

Payments for visits to the SPA complex are additionally carried out at special rates.

Superior

Superior room (37-43 square meters) is suitable for a family that loves space, but does not want to overpay for excesses. Here, instead of a matrimonial bed, two single beds can be installed, and for children a bunk bed or a comfortable sofa is prepared. There are rooms with a balcony and sea views.

The cost of living is 5000-9900 rubles per day. When paying for accommodation in this category of rooms, guests receive free additional services:

  • breakfast ("buffet");
  • Wi-Fi connection
  • use of the safe;
  • children visiting the Carousel game club;
  • parking.

Free use of the SPA center is not included in the room rate in this room, but the Bogatyr hotel complex (information from the official website) provides its guests with a special discount.





Junior Suite

Junior Suite is 46-55 square meters, designed for comfortable family or friendly accommodation of three adults. To create the coziness and uniqueness of the room, many wooden objects, expensive fabrics, and carpets are used in the design. The interior style is reminiscent of the Middle Ages with elements of a fairy kingdom. In some apartments there is a balcony, there is one room designed for people with limited mobility.

Room rates range from 6,000 to 10,900 rubles per day and depend on the season. The range of services included in the price is similar to the superior category.

"Suite"

Room "suite" (60-63 square meters) - this is, first of all, spaciousness and unlimited possibilities for a comfortable stay. When decorating the suite, preference was given to the classical style, high-quality upholstered furniture and a French bed. A distinctive feature of the room is the presence of a dressing room and a guest toilet. The Suite is designed to accommodate two adults. Its cost is from 16,000 rubles per day.

Those who have chosen a suite and Bogatyr hotel complex will receive the following services free of charge:

  • Buffet breakfast
  • educational and game center for children;
  • safe and dressing room;
  • laundry;
  • separate parking of personal vehicles;
  • SPA-center.

For guests with disabilities in this category there is one room.

Queen's Suite

Room "Queen's Suite" (70-78 square meters) may be worthy of residence of royal persons of the Middle Ages. In its center is a huge bed with a wooden headboard and ottoman at the feet. For a comfortable leisure time - armchairs and a small table. For dressing - a personal dressing room. For guests - an individual toilet room.

Room rate is from 18 000 rubles. Having settled in the "Queen's Suite", guests also receive for free:

  • Buffet breakfast
  • wireless Internet;
  • child Center;
  • individual safe;
  • laundry service;
  • separate parking;
  • visit to the wellness center.




"King's Suite"

King Suite Suite (130 square meters) is located on the tenth floor. From its windows stunning views of the mountains and the endless expanse of the sea open to the eye. In this two-room apartment, without interfering with each other, four people can accommodate at the same time. Here, handmade furniture of a shade of dark aquamarine is installed, there are two bathrooms, a separate dressing room.

Only three suites are available. In the season they are better to book in advance, as they are extremely popular with lovers of luxury and comfort.

Accommodation in the "King's Suite" will cost from 20,000 rubles per day. The range of free additional services is similar to the "suites" category.

"Royal Suite"

The Royal Suite (242 square meters) is available in the hotel in a single copy and occupies two floors. It is designed to live true connoisseurs of beauty and grace.

On the ground floor there is a large luxurious living room and personal work area. On the upper - two bedrooms with large French beds, private bathrooms and individual dressing rooms. The room has a terrace from which you can admire the mountain ranges and the bay for a long time.

The room rate is from 40,000 rubles per day, the maximum number of guests is four people.

For guests of the suite "royal" is free of charge:

  • breakfast;
  • SPA complex;
  • guarded parking spaces in the parking lot;
  • personal account for massage;
  • free internet and a compliment from the hotel.

Nutrition

In the off-season, the Bogatyr hotel complex includes only breakfast in the tour price. In season, the hotel is ready to offer guests a choice of food:

  • breakfast
  • full board;
  • half board.




In any case, it will be buffet- style meals on the ground floor in the Ballada restaurant, with a capacity of 185 people. The restaurant is a spacious room in the old style with an outdoor terrace. For the design of the dining room, ceiling structures in the form of a vault, lamps with horns, burgundy jacquard tablecloths and carved dark wood chairs are used. All this helps to feel the spirit of the Middle Ages.

The menu of the restaurant is carefully designed so that here you can choose the right dishes and families with kids, and lovers of meat, and vegetarians.

It is not necessary to be a hotel guest to taste the famous masterpieces of the restaurant. Everyone can visit this place. Here you can organize a wedding and other celebrations.

Another, no less cozy restaurant "Sadko" is located on the 2nd floor of the hotel. Here guests can enjoy authentic Russian cuisine. The menu also has a large selection of dishes prepared according to European recipes.

The interior is decorated in white and red colors. The hall is decorated with hanging chandeliers and arches made of stone.

There is a respectable lobby bar in the lobby of the Bogatyr hotel complex, which was decorated in an eclectic style: oriental sofas, mosaic on the floor, potted plants and lighting, creating an atmosphere of comfort and relaxation.

The lobby bar is open to visitors at any time of the day.
