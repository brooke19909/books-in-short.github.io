Smartphone "Samsung J3" (2016): features and reviews

But such unflattering reviews do not stop the manufacturer, and every year new gadgets appear on the world market. So, the Samsung J3 smartphone appeared on the shelves of numerous stores in 2016. Case Feature

If before everyone was genuinely enthusiastic about non-separable cases, now more than ever in the price of a device with a removable back cover. By the way, the Samsung J3 2016 smartphone is just one of them.

About dimensions

Owner reviews of the Samsung J3 (2016) are often filled with laudatory words in the direction of the size of the smartphone. Although the gadget has a classic five-inch screen, there is no feeling that the device is bulky or large. Everything in it is so tiny and so neatly arranged that you do not want to scold him. If you use the language of numbers, then 7.9 * 71.0 * 142.3 millimeters - these are the dimensions of the device. The phone is lightweight and comfortable enough. Some users say that they initially even suspected it was a different model. The gadget seemed too small.

Screen

Consider the characteristics and reviews of the "Samsung J3" (2016 release) in terms of image and screen in principle. It is believed that for a budget device - this is a pretty good option. But who, when buying a new smartphone, wants to think that something is not good enough in him? Perhaps that is why it is worth finding out what is remarkable about the screen of this device.

It‚Äôs worth starting with the fact that it is made using a special technology. It is called Super AMOLED. These displays are deep black. In their creation, technology is used, which implies the use of organic LEDs and an active matrix consisting of thin-film transistors. In the people it is customary to call the matrix TFT.

If we consider this screen from the practicality side, then almost all reviews emphasize that this smartphone is ideal for watching movies. It correctly conveys colors, and the image practically does not distort.

However, looking at the gadget from different angles, you can see the pixels. True, for this it is necessary to choose the desired brightness. So we can assume that the screen is more than good for those who are not prone to skepticism.

Some are indignant that the gadget does not have an oleophobic coating. Such an outrage is justified, because even cheap Chinese smartphones have this coverage. It is because of its absence that the smartphone screen perfectly remembers your fingers, which visually makes it cheaper. Traces of sweat were never attractive.

The viewing angles of the smartphone are quite wide. This means that no matter how you twist the device, on any side the image will be pleasing to the eye.

It is strange, of course, that the screen is not protected by a special mineral glass, as it is in many other models, considered to be "non-flagships." And even more surprisingly, the gadget does not have automatic brightness control.

Talk about the numbers. According to the manufacturer, the screen resolution with a diagonal of 5 inches is 1280 x 720 pixels. This means that the average density of dots is 294 units. For a smartphone of this class, this is an excellent result.

According to test data, the screen has a strange imposed restriction. It consists in the fact that the maximum number of touches of the display is two. For a modern smartphone, this is very strange, because most, if not all models of this class, are designed for 5 touches. Why such an artificial reduction was made is incomprehensible.

There are few satisfied screen settings. You cannot select a work profile. Therefore, the screen itself is not bad, but in order to justify its price, the manufacturer, apparently, deliberately worsened this model.

Communication and wireless technology

The budget gadget from a well-known manufacturer, despite its relatively low cost, has received a full set of necessary modules, which makes it convenient to use it for everyday tasks. So, this model supports 4th generation networks, which is a necessity in modern conditions, when high-quality Internet is needed not only for entertainment, but also for using all kinds of instant messengers with the ability to make voice and video calls.

You can connect additional peripherals to the device, such as fitness bracelets or wireless headsets using Bluetooth version 4.0. The module installed in the smartphone supports the ability to transmit high-quality stereo audio, which will allow you to use a suitable headset not only for conversations, but also for listening to your favorite music.

The negative sides of the model

The main disadvantage of the model at the moment is the already outdated version of the operating system. While the 8th Android is coming out, the manufacturer still has not provided an official update until at least the 6th. And it is unlikely to do this in the future, because the model is quite old. Namely because of this, some users are faced with the fact that modern applications are not supported by this gadget.

However, these are not all the shortcomings of Samsung J3 (2016), and the reviews of the owners note at least three more unpleasant features. The first of them is a small amount of memory, both operational and internal. There is practically no space to save user programs and files, so installing a memory card is a must.

The second minus is the low quality of the pictures. Although the cameras on Samsung phones have long been famous for their thought-out optics and good post-processing of photos by the system itself, this model is not so simple. Many users note that the images are blurry, without focus or with overexposed areas.

The third negative feature is manifested if you have to repair the device due to a fall or other damage. Parts are very expensive, and it does not make sense to change a broken display and sensor, since together with the cost of work, almost the price of a new smartphone is obtained. In competing manufacturers, for example, Xiaomi, the cost of such work and details comes out many times lower.

Conclusion

After reviewing the specifications, photos and reviews of the Samsung J3 (2016), we can conclude that this model is intended primarily for those who are fans of the Korean brand. It is more expensive than the proposals of competitors, and has an impressive list of disadvantages. However, despite them, a nice design and reliability may be to the taste of some people who want to get a branded device for a small price.
