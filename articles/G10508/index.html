Nomos-Bank: reviews of specialists and customers

The collapse of the USSR gave rise to not only many small and independent states, but also a huge number of banking organizations. A striking example of one of them is Nomos-Bank, reviews of which allow you to monitor its rapid development.



This organization began work in the Russian credit market in 1993, and since 2005 it has been a member of the state deposit insurance system. For quite some time this financial institution was oriented towards large corporate business, however today it is a universal private bank with a huge assortment of financial products for both private and corporate clients. Currently, Nomos-Bank, whose ATMs can be found in most cities of the Russian Federation, is developing four areas of priority:

  • retail services;
  • corporate block of programs;
  • investment block of programs;
  • private banking.


The main clients of the bank at the moment are various medium and large shipbuilding enterprises, machine-building organizations, as well as facilities of the nuclear, food, gold mining and light industries. In addition, foreign trade companies use his services. Nomos Bank, reviews of which predict a bright future for him, is a leader in the field of financing large structures.

In addition to providing its services to corporate clients, the organization is actively developing consumer lending. The size of the loan, which can be issued here, is quite large - 2 million rubles. You can get them both as a target loan and as a non- target , but the interest rate on them is exactly the same, despite advertising that claims an individual approach to each client, 16.9% per annum. This, to put it mildly, is surprising for such a serious institution as Nomos-Bank, reviews of which say the opposite, but its programs have several significant advantages:

  • lack of hidden fees;
  • no additional payments;
  • no need to pay a down payment or provide a collateral object.


Such obvious advantages allow attracting a huge number of consumers to Nomos-Bank. Moscow has three large representative offices of this financial organization: the main office and two branches. However, before going to the bank to obtain a consumer loan, you should carefully check all the information about it. Firstly, a single rate for any loan should alert the skeptical user. After all, if a bank allows itself to contradict its advertising on one issue, then there may be differences in others. It is worthwhile to carefully read all the clauses of the proposed contract before putting your signature on it. It should be remembered that bank employees are required to provide all the necessary information in a timely manner and do not have the right to withhold it from the client. Nomos-Bank, reviews of which make it possible to form a very good opinion, can help in a difficult situation, but it is always worth 100% to know what it will take to fulfill the contract.
