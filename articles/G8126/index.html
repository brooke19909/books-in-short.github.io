Omega Beach (Sevastopol, Gagarinsky District): description, how to get, reviews. Beach holidays in Sevastopol

A comfortable rest in the city, a shallow warm sea, a gentle beach, the necessary infrastructure, including lifeguards, doctors and police posts, are all Omega Beach in Sevastopol.

Description

This resting place is located in the western part of Kruglaya Bay, in the Gagarinsky district of the city. Its second name, Omega (known since the mid-19th century), is more popular. The bay is very similar to this capital letter of the Greek alphabet.



The beach is sandy and shallow, only about 5-8 meters. Only the middle part of the coast is clayey with insignificant stones. A small layer of water warms up quickly, so the bottom is covered almost all summer with a thin layer of algae, which during the surf or storm form a beautiful fluffy green carpet on the beach.

On one of the protrusions of the coast, Cape Peschanoy, the water is much cleaner and more transparent. This place was chosen by divers and diving enthusiasts. The opening views are said to be of stunning beauty. There is also a natural mud bath here. Sludge deposits help relieve pain in the joints, smooth the skin and raise the overall tone of the body.

The beach is almost 30 meters wide and stretches over 700 meters.

Infrastructure

There are many different attractions open here, delights in water and trampolines delight children. There are several cafes with menus for every taste and budget. In the evening, discos are held on the beach and often live music is played. For lovers of outdoor activities on the beach in Omega Bay (Sevastopol), rental points are open. You can always rent jet skis, catamarans, beach accessories, etc.

On the opposite shore of the bay, the private Yacht Club South offers guests to rent a yacht or windsurfing equipment. For fishing you can rent a long boat.

On the beach "Omega" (Sevastopol) flights are organized on kite and hang glider.

Around the bay there are many residential buildings in which you can always find a room or a turnkey apartment for every taste and budget. There are several hotels within walking distance.





At a distance of one public transport stop there are two markets with fresh local fruits, vegetables and wines, as well as supermarket chains and small shops of souvenirs and beach items.

Promenade

There is a well-organized recreation area. It has many restaurants, fast food outlets, cafes and bars. For kids and teenagers, although adults will not refuse to have fun, various attractions (ball, slides, etc.) are installed, including Crazy Cart, game labyrinths, playgrounds, all kinds of swings and carousels. There are rental bicycles, gyro scooters, etc.

"Paddling pool"

This is the beginning of the beach area in Omega Bay (Sevastopol). Unworthy swimmers, families with small children and elderly people have a rest here. The bottom and shore are mostly sandy with small interspersed small pebbles. The entrance to the water is gentle. But an adult, to go into the sea at least to the waist, you need to walk about ten meters. On the other hand, the places here are unpredictable: there are such hollows that a child can leave with his head.
