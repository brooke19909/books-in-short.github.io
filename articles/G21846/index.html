"Asrin Beach" (Turkey) - an economical vacation in the village of Avsallar

Those who prefer a quiet and economical vacation are advised to pay attention to the Asrin Beach Hotel (Turkey), which has a 4-star category. It is located away from the bustle of the city, 2 kilometers from the resort village of Avsallar, in the Turkler region. The center of the nearest resort town of Alanya is 20 kilometers away, while Antalya International Airport is 90 kilometers away. Rooms

Asrin Beach (Turkey) offers its guests 187 rooms, including 2 for the disabled and 34 for non-smokers. Pets are not allowed. All rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning and have ceramic coating. There is a bathroom, hairdryer, TV (2 Russian-language channels). A telephone is installed, a safe (for a fee). Each room has a minibar in which only water is free. All other drinks from the bar are at extra cost. Upon prior request, one rollaway bed is available (if necessary). All rooms have direct or partial sea views. Every day cleaning is done. Bed linen is changed 2 times in seven days.



Nutrition

Like most hotels Turkey is famous for (Alanya), Asrin Beach Hotel for guests provides food All Inclusiv, which is valid from 7.30 to 23.30. It is served as a buffet in the main hotel restaurant, which capacity is 560 people. There is a summer terrace. High chairs are provided for little vacationers. It should be noted that imported drinks and freshly squeezed juices are served at an additional cost. In addition to the main restaurant, there are several bars.



Beach

Most hotels of this category, located on the first coastline, have their own beach. Among them is the hotel that Turkey offers its guests - Asrin Beach (photo below). The hotel owns a sand and pebble beach, whose length is 200 meters. It is across the street from the hotel. Beach equipment is provided free of charge. There are changing cabins, showers, and a pier. A number of paid entertainment is offered, including riding a scooter, banana, canoe.



Information for vacationers

Asrin Beach Hotel (Turkey) has a developed infrastructure for an excellent holiday. An outdoor pool and an indoor pool were built on its territory, two water slides were installed . For those who need to combine leisure with work, there is a conference room equipped with modern technology, the capacity of which is 75 people. Guests are offered a number of services, including a hairdresser, laundry, dry cleaning.

To create an excellent holiday, the Asrin Beach Hotel (Turkey) offers its guests a number of entertainments, including a sauna, a Turkish bath, darts, table tennis, aerobics. There is a volleyball court on the beach. An animation group works throughout the day. For an additional fee, you can play billiards, get the services of a qualified massage therapist, rent a car, visit a hairdresser or beauty salon.

In addition, the hotel has excellent facilities for younger guests. For them there is a swimming pool, a mini club, an equipped playground.
