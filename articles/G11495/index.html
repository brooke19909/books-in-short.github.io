Ski resorts in Russia. Entertainment complex "Beaver Log", Krasnoyarsk

If, by the will of fate, you find yourself in the city of Krasnoyarsk, be sure to visit the "Beaver Log" (fanpark). This all-season complex will delight you with its unique natural landscapes, amazing entertainment programs and a high level of service. In the article we will get acquainted with the types of services that are provided here.





general information

The Bobrovy Log complex (Krasnoyarsk) is located just a 20-minute drive from the city center and borders on the Stolby state reserve. This recreation area operates year-round - visitors are given a unique opportunity to relax here not only in the summer, but also in winter. It is worth noting that this recreation area in 2011 celebrated its first anniversary - 5 years from the date of opening. During this time, the all-season complex "Beaver Log" (Krasnoyarsk) managed to gain world fame and significantly expand the range of its services.

Winter activities





The Bobrovy Log complex (Krasnoyarsk), the photo of which is presented in the article, has been working in the ski format for 6 months. The duration of the work period is provided with the help of modern snow formation systems. They work in automatic mode. As a result, the Bobrovy Log complex (Krasnoyarsk) guarantees visitors a stable snow cover during not only the winter, but also several spring months. More precisely, the ski season begins in November, and ends only in April. In addition, the park has at its disposal a number of specialized equipment for preparing tracks from artificial snow. The territory of the complex is equipped with 14 slopes. Both beginner skiers and professionals in this area will be able to relax actively. For this purpose, tracks of 3 levels were created: easy, difficult and extreme. The total length of the slopes is 10 km, and elevations reach 350 meters. For beginners in this area, a training track is equipped. What is important: even children can master the basics of skiing on it. To this end, the slope is equipped with toys and a children's elevator.

Winter services





The main slopes are equipped with chairlift, tow and towing lifts. Moreover, some slopes can be used even in the dark, as they are equipped with sports lighting. It is worth noting that it is not necessary to have equipment with you. Visitors are given the opportunity to rent any equipment, up to ice rinks. Those who wish to learn skiing can use the services of an instructor. And little guests, while their parents are having fun on the snowy slopes, can comfortably and have fun at the kids club.

Summer activities in the Bobrovy Log complex





Fanpark in the warm season, as well as in the off-season, offers visitors a range of entertainment. So, everyone can take a guided tour on a chairlift. Fans of extreme sports are invited to have fun on unique attractions for Russia. Especially for children, a set of playgrounds has been created. Visitors who simply want to relax in the lap of nature are provided with picnic arbors. There are also many cafes, restaurants and bars, there is a SPA salon. In addition, you can have an amazing time on a quiet horse ride. Guests can also enjoy mountain biking. The all-season Beaver Log complex is equipped with a multifunctional platform that can accommodate over 15 thousand people at a time. Due to its size, not only sporting events are held here , but also entertainment events. So, the city authorities annually organize citywide holidays here.

Entertainment industry

Most visitors to the all-weather complex cannot go past two extreme attractions. One of them, called Rodelban, is a combination of a roller coaster and bobsled. It is worth noting that a fairly high speed develops here during the descent, and the braking process occurs manually. In addition, at the highest point of the route, the entire city is completely visible. By the way, this attraction has the largest length among its counterparts (3400 m). The second entertainment was called Ziprider. It should be noted that this fun is beyond the power of every person. Its essence lies in the fact that the visitor is dressed in special equipment, to which a cable is attached. With the help of such equipment, a person makes a dizzying flight at a speed of up to 100 km / h. If desired, 4 people can ride at the same time. Interestingly, in the world there are only four of these attractions.
