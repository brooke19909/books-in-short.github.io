Hotel Description

The 5 * Festival Riviera Resort hotel complex, founded in 2008, offers its guests to enjoy a quality vacation on the warm sea coast. This hotel belongs to the well-known Sunrise family, which in itself is a guarantee of excellent service.

Hotel Festival Riviera Resort 5 * (Hurghada) consists of two five-story buildings located respectively on the first and second coastline. Guests have the opportunity to visit the wellness center, spend time playing golf, and diving with professional instructors.





Hotel location

Hotel Festival Riviera Resort 5 * (Hurghada) is located 15 km north of the city center. The famous El Nuna resort is just 10 km away. From Hurghada International Airport to the hotel you will have to travel only 20 km by taxi or bus, which will take about 15-20 minutes of your time. The distance from the buildings located on the first coastline to the sea is not more than 200 meters. The nearest large supermarket and cafe are 3 kilometers away, and you will have to overcome 10 kilometers to the local market and restaurants.
