Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * (Alanya, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Turkey is one of the most popular countries visited by tourists during their holidays. Such activity is associated with a high level of service and affordable prices for accommodation and meals. Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * is a favorite vacation spot for thousands of tourists who come here more than once. They say that having been here once, it is impossible not to return again.

Where is it located and how does it work?

Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * is located in Alanya in the city of Avsallar. The distance to the major airport of Antalya is about 100 km. You can fly directly to Alanya - the hotel is 63 km away.

Getting to the hotel complex on your own by public transport is uncomfortable and long. The best option would be to use the transfer service provided by the hotel.

The complex operates throughout the year. Depending on the season, prices will vary in one direction or another for vouchers. Also in the cool season, only the indoor pool is open.

Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *: description

The hotel occupies a very large territory. There are two hotels here. The complex can simultaneously accommodate about 8,000 people. Basically, the hotel rests residents of Russia, Ukraine, Germany and Turkey.

The building was built in the form of a small wave. In the evening, a huge number of lights and lights illuminate throughout the complex. Therefore, from afar it seems that you get to the New Year's show.





The entire territory is landscaped as much as possible. Gardeners take care of the plantings around the clock. Everywhere laid walkways with decorative street decorations. A lot of places to relax in the shade of trees have been built on the territory.

Check-in officially starts from 14.00 daily, and you must leave before 12.00 from the room. If tourists come earlier or need to leave later, then at the reception there is a left-luggage office where you can leave things for a while and go for a walk or the sea.

Accommodations

In Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *, guests are provided with several types of rooms, different in terms of the possible number of residents and comfort class:

  • Standard double - room size 36 m 2 , has one double and one single bed, parquet floor, wardrobe, seating area, minibar, TV with Russian cable channels, safe, toilet, shower, hairdryer, mini-set of hygiene items, electric kettle , air conditioning, balcony with garden, sea, pool or city view;
  • Family room - two double beds and one single, wardrobe, TV with satellite channels, parquet floor, air conditioning, electric kettle, hairdryer, minibar, safe, air conditioning, toilet and shower, toiletries, a balcony with sea or garden view, pool, city;
  • Suite with jacuzzi - two-room suite - bedroom and living room, two folding sofas, double bed, small table with soft chairs, wardrobe, TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, safe, hairdryer, minibar, shower and toilet, bathroom with hydromassage, electric kettle , a set of hygiene supplies, bathrobes and slippers.




All rooms have a telephone and free internet. Available service: wake-up call.

Food in the complex

Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * in Alanya refers to all-inclusive hotels. The price includes not only accommodation, but also food, entertainment, service on the territory of the complex.

The hotel has a large dining-restaurant. Here, for each meal, special racks serve a huge variety of dishes. Tourists can take their favorite food in unlimited quantities. That is, the restaurant operates on a buffet basis.

The menu offers all kinds of meat and fish dishes. Seafood is served in sufficient quantities. A variety of cereals, potatoes, pasta are offered for garnish.

The menu offers hot and cold soups. Dining at the hotel is popular for its desserts. All kinds of pastries and cakes satisfy the taste of sweet lovers. Also at the racks there is always a wide variety of all kinds of fruits.

For children, the cooks of the restaurant prepare a special menu. It includes boiled meat of dietary types, steamed fish, dairy products. The restaurant always has several electric kettles that parents can use to prepare baby food.

Beach

Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * in Turkey is located on the first coastline. The road to the sea takes no more than 10-15 minutes. The hotel has its own beach, on which only guests of the complex can relax.

The road to it goes along the decorated sidewalks. The beach is equipped with sunbeds, parasols. Towels and, if necessary, air mattresses are provided. There is a bar on the beach. It presents a whole day a variety of fruits that all vacationers can take. Also in the refrigerators are refreshing drinks and water for all guests of the complex.





Hotel staff clean the sandy beach daily. Therefore, children and adults can freely play here without shoes walking on warm and clean sand. The entrance to the sea is quite gentle, which is very convenient for vacationers with small children.

Pools

Tours in Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * are also very popular due to the presence of a large number of areas for water entertainment in its territory. The complex has 10 pools of different sizes and purpose:

  • indoor - works by season;
  • 7 open;
  • 2 with entertainment - water slides, hydromassage, fountains.

Among this variety there are children's swimming areas. One pool with a shallow depth, in the center with low slides, is intended only for swimming children. Also in others there are specially fenced areas with a shallow depth for recreation and entertainment of children.





Pools are located throughout the complex. In the evening, the original lights are lit in them and you can admire the saturated blue color of the water and the patterns at the bottom.

Around the pools there are plenty of comfortable sun loungers. Sun umbrellas are available here. The coating in this area is constructed with anti-slip properties.

Restaurants

On the territory there are several mini-and snack bars in the Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * . The service in them is at a good level. Bartenders prepare a variety of non-alcoholic and alcoholic cocktails. Children are provided with dairy drinks. At certain times, snacks and desserts for snacks are served here. For customers, these services are provided free of charge.

There is also a small cafe on the beach. Here at the disposal of customers are constantly fresh fruits and soft drinks. Vacationers are given a variety of ice creams.

In the main restaurant, you can order a festive menu and cake for any occasion. There is also the possibility of dinner for a fee on the menu, which differs from the main meal in the complex.

Wellness and relaxation treatments

Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * has a wide range of spa treatments. Vacationers of the complex can take advantage of a variety of wellness treatments:

  • steam room;
  • phototherapy;
  • wrap;
  • body care;
  • manicure and pedicure;
  • cosmetic services;
  • massages;
  • hair care;
  • makeup.




On the territory there are all conditions for playing sports. Tourists can visit:

  • fitness;
  • Yoga
  • aqua fitness;
  • cycling
  • darts;
  • table and tennis;
  • billiards;
  • volleyball and futsal.

Here you can rent all the necessary equipment for practicing the above sports.

Entertainment

Guests throughout the rest are involved in all kinds of events and shows in the Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * . Prices for such tours depend on the season and on average cost from 40,000 rubles for two people per week of rest.

The hotel has a team of animators who entertain both children and adults. Every evening, various performances, contests, concerts are arranged.

With young tourists active games, creative classes, quests and at lunchtime are held. The complex has a kids club. In it, children from 4 to 14 years can spend interesting time with animators at any time throughout the day.





The hotel has several playgrounds with a variety of ladders, mazes, swings. There is a special coating that will protect babies from injuries in the event of an accidental fall.

At the reception, tourists can book an excursion program for attractions of interest to themselves. At night, the hotel has a disco with a DJ. This service is paid separately.

The hotel has a shared spacious lounge with comfortable furniture and a large flat-screen TV. Here guests can communicate in a relaxed atmosphere among themselves and read the latest press over a cup of aromatic coffee.

There is also a restaurant in the evening for guests with a 16+ age restriction. It also hosts special entertainment programs for guests, including strip dancing.

Lonicera Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *: tourist reviews

Numerous comments on various Internet resources indicate the popularity of this hotel complex among tourists from different countries. Mostly customers are satisfied with the living conditions and cleanliness of the rooms.

Opinions were somewhat divided regarding nutrition. The quantity and variety of dishes are sufficient. There are complaints from tourists about the abundance of fried and deep-fried food. Such food is heavy enough for the stomach and is of little use.

Hotel guests also indicate that it is necessary to book sunbeds on the beach in the front rows in the morning, otherwise you can stay without a place under a canopy. Almost all guests are satisfied with the work of snack bars. They note that only sometimes quite large queues are lined up in them.





The guests of the complex are very pleased with the presence of a zone for self-preparation of freshly squeezed juices. There are always a lot of different fruits and modern equipment.

The work of animators does not always satisfy the needs of guests. Tourists often notice that recreational activities are prepared in haste and are not designed for different ages of vacationers.
