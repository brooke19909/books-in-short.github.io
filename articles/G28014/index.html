The Mystery crock-pot is a small miracle in your kitchen

The Mystery crock-pot is an excellent appliance designed to facilitate the work in the kitchen. He perfectly cooks many dishes, saving his master from the need to endlessly stand at the stove. In this article we will consider the most popular models of Mystery.

Model MCM-1010

The Mystery crock-pot of the MSM-1010 brand was one of the first models released

this company. Its power is 900 watts. The volume of the bowl is 5 liters, which makes it possible to provide a family with high-quality hot food of medium size. This model has 8 programs that allow stewing, boiling, baking and frying. Many users will positively appreciate the steaming function. A special plastic grill for a double boiler is included with this multicooker. This model has a pleasant unobtrusive design, so it fits well into the kitchen interior.

Model MCM-1016

The Mystery crock-pot of this model has a smaller bowl volume and weaker power than the previous one - 3 L and 650 W, respectively. It has 8 automatic modes. For the convenience of users, there is a special display on which the cooking process is displayed. This is an inexpensive and functional model, it is suitable for a small family.

Model MCM-1017

The Mystery 1017 crock-pot is the most original and functional model from the line

this company. She has nine programs, and the highlight is the rice cooking program in four different versions at once: slow, fast cooking, pilaf and rice with a crust. The body of this model is streamlined, made of white plastic. In front is a colorful control panel, which displays a convenient Russified menu.

Cooking modes

The Mystery crock-pot has a very convenient program - Steaming. With its help, you can easily cook diet dishes in a special basket, such as vegetables, fish, meatballs, dumplings, etc. Also, in the steamer mode, you can heat ready-made dishes.

"Extinguishing" - this program is suitable for any product. Selected ingredients languish for a long time at the right temperature. In this mode, large whole pieces of meat are perfectly stewed, turning into tender boiled pork. This function will help to cook aspic, tasty stew, stuffed tomatoes or peppers.
