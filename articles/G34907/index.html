Location

In Side, a resort that has begun to be mastered for a long time, the first lane is completely occupied by five-star hotels. In the city itself there are small hotels with a tiny territory. It is designed for budget tourists. And for those who appreciate the middle ground (in Turkey these are four-star hotels), new quarters are provided. They often contain apartment blocks. Remoteness from the sea, such hotels compensate their residents with a free shuttle to the beach. These include the Kentia Apart Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side). Side, more precisely, the historic city center, is located nearby. You just need to go down the street. But near the hotel there is urban infrastructure. The territory of the hotel Kentia Apart Hotel 4 * (Side)

Side began to acquire "apartment complex" type hotels in the mid-nineties. This hotel was built in 1999. The last renovation she went in 2014. The complex consists of four 3-5-story houses. There are lifts everywhere. Between the buildings is a lagoon pool. There is also a sun terrace, lined with sunbeds and umbrellas. The territory of the hotel is small, but everything is thought out to the smallest detail. Tourists write that all services are at hand and there is no feeling of crowding or a "stone bag". Thanks to the efforts of gardeners at the Kentia Apart Hotel 4 *, you feel like in a blooming oasis. The territory also fits a mini market, a restaurant and two bars. The buildings have parking - free for clientele. Judging by the reviews, the pool is set so that most of the day the sun shines there.

Beach and its alternative

At least one kilometer from the coast is the Kentia Apart Hotel 4 * (Turkey). Reviews do not really favor the nearby beach. This is a narrow strip of sand filled by the people in season to the eyeballs. It is better to take advantage of the free transfer from the hotel. But sunbeds and umbrellas are paid there. The hotel itself has a spacious swimming pool filled with fresh water. Around it is a sun terrace.
