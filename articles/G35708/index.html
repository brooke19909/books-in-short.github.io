Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5: traveler reviews

Kyzyl Agach - a small Turkish resort village located near the city of Side. This southern village is famous for its clean sandy beaches and simply superb family hotels. For example, tourists can rent rooms in Kyzylag in the hotel Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 *. Reviews about this rather large recreation center on the Internet are mostly good.

Where is the hotel located

This hotel is located on the very edge of the village. Side is approximately 15 km from it. The nearest airport is 80 km from Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 *, in Antalya. Tourists get to this complex by operator transfer buses in about 2 hours. Such a distant location from the international air hub, vacationers are not a plus of this hotel, of course. Since the hotel is located on the edge of the village, tourists are brought here last.





About 10 km from the hotel there is another resort town popular with vacationers, including Russians, - Manavgat. Guests of the complex can get to Side by bus if desired. True, despite the fact that this city is not too far away, it takes about 40 minutes to travel to it by public transport. In addition, after 7 pm, buses from Kyzylagach to Side run very rarely.

General description of the hotel

By hotel standards, the Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * m can be considered quite new. It was built in 2015. Its convenience for living, according to most travelers, is determined by its novelty as well. The total area of ​​the hotel is 70 thousand m 2 . The hotel accepts guests in two six-story buildings of the original building.

Reception at this hotel operates around the clock. That is, guests can contact the administrators with their problems at any time of the night or day. Pets are not allowed in this hotel. Such a service is not provided here even for a fee.





The courtyard of this complex has its own. Judging by the stories of tourists, the hotel grounds look very attractive. In the courtyard of the hotel there are many beautiful tropical trees. The lawns are also broken here. A beautiful territory is, according to many travelers, one of the advantages of the Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 *. The photos presented on the page confirm this unconditionally.

Hotel stay: rooms

This complex offers Kyzylagach guests a little less than 500 comfortable rooms. If necessary, here you can book rooms of a standard class, family or suite. There is also one room designed specifically for the disabled. All rooms for rent at Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Kizilagac) include:

  • TV

  • telephone;

  • safe;

  • mini bar.

In showers, tourists can use hairdryers. The minibar at this hotel offers non-alcoholic drinks only. The refrigerator is replenished daily by the hotel staff. The bar is staffed here, judging by the reviews, very good.





This hotel also has a service such as Wi-Fi. Tourists can spend up to 50 MB for free. Air conditioning at the hotel is centralized. The rooms in this complex have beautiful laminate flooring.

Rooms: guest opinion

The rooms, rented out at Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 *, traveler reviews deserved just excellent. According to many vacationers , the rooms in this hotel are offered to guests spacious and quite comfortable. Since this hotel is new, its rooms look not only cozy, but also neat and beautiful. The hotel bar, as tour operators claim, is replenished daily. Household appliances in the apartments of the hotel are completely new, modern and working. The rooms are equipped with furniture, judging by the reviews, comfortable.

Maids clean the rooms of this hotel, as most of its guests believe , quite carefully and often. Towels and bedding are never dirty here either.

The majority of residents attribute to some drawbacks to hotel rooms only that safes can be used exclusively for a fee. Trips to Turkey with booking rooms in this hotel are already quite expensive. Therefore, many tourists believe that the safes in the rooms could be free.

In addition, tourists at this hotel can order photos as a keepsake. This small feature, of course, also adds to the hotel attractiveness in the eyes of vacationers. Paid at the hotel Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Turkey), I can also play bowling or tennis. Travelers and tourists staying at the hotel can take elevators to the floor. Services for children

As already mentioned, most Kyzylagach hotels are very suitable for families. Many tourists come to Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Kizilagac) with children. For young guests, as well as for adults, there are many services. Of course, there is also a kids club in this hotel. Babysitting services at this hotel, as well as at any other in Europe or Asia, are paid.

The hotel’s restaurant has a children's menu for young guests. Unfortunately there is no paddling pool at this hotel. However, children can swim in the adult pool. Here, a shallow zone is separated for them. In addition to the children's room, the hotel also has a teenager club. Teenagers from 11 to 15 years can visit it. Of course, children's animations are also held at the hotel. Entertain the little guests at this hotel artists in Russian.

Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 *: reviews of the infrastructure of the complex

The quality of tourist services provided at this hotel was very good. Its territory is planned quite ergonomically. All infrastructure elements in the hotel are located compactly, and finding them if necessary will not be completely difficult. The pools in this complex are large and deep. So swimming in them, judging by the reviews, is convenient. And the water in the hotel pools is usually clean.

The slides at this hotel are praised by absolutely all travelers. At least, children's attractions of this type in the hotel, as many of its guests note, are quite safe. On adult slides, experienced tourists are advised to ride carefully. Here, unfortunately, you can get injured.

Not too good reviews about Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * tourists have only in terms of cleaning the territory. In the courtyard of the hotel, unfortunately, you can see quite a lot of garbage. In the mornings, guests are sometimes even bothered by a not too pleasant smell.

Animations in this recreation complex are standard. Judging by the reviews, nothing original is offered to vacationers here. This complex was once built for German tourists. And they, as you know, are not too big lovers of animation. Recently, Russian vacationers are increasingly visiting the hotel. But under them the hotel’s service, apparently, has not yet been managed.

The elevators in this hotel, as many vacationers note, are working properly. However, at the peak of the season, tenants often have to stand in line in order to climb them to the floor. According to many guests, elevators in such a large hotel could have been provided and more.

In general, tourists evaluate the work of this hotel, judging by the reviews from the Internet, as quite tolerable. On the special websites of tour operators, in the "Reviews" section, the ratings of Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * are approximately 3.5-4.5 out of 5 possible. And this, of course, is quite a lot.

Staff Travel Reviews

In general, tourists rate the hotel as quite comfortable. However, up to 5 stars, according to most vacationers, he still does not reach a bit. In any case, the staff at this hotel is working, judging by the reviews, it is quite friendly. Domestic tourists in this hotel are polite and helpful, like any other, including from richer countries. So, our compatriots in this hotel do not have any special problems with communicating with employees. In an extreme case, the Russians can use some kind of software translator on a smartphone.





Dining at the hotel

The hotel dining room reviews of domestic tourists earned pretty good. The food in the main restaurant of the hotel is served quite tasty. The crowds in the dining room of the hotel are mainly only at peak season. At other times, guests can eat here without a crush and queues.

The Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Manavgat, Kyzylgach) operates, like most other five-star hotels, on an all-inclusive basis. Guests who book rooms here receive free regular and brunch. Lunches and dinners are served on the buffet. Also included in the price are night soup, ice cream and sweets during certain hours.

In addition to the dining room, this hotel also has several good, according to many guests, bars. One of them is located right on the beach. Here you can also have a good snack at any time. Many guests even go to the main dining room only in the morning, having lunch and dinner at this bar. Meals here are also served free of charge to hotel residents.

Once a week, hotel guests have the right to visit the à la carte restaurant on its territory . If desired, residents of the complex can choose, for example, fish or Turkish.

Beach Hotels

The Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * hotel (Side, Kyzylgach) is located quite close to the sea. The distance from its pool to the beach is only about 300 meters. The hotel guests have to go to the sea first along the paved path, and then along the bridge. The latter is thrown over the river, in which fish and turtles live. If desired, the residents of the hotel can rest in the gazebo along the way.

The hotel-owned beach itself has earned good traveler reviews. It is significant in size, and therefore there are almost no crowds here. One of the undoubted advantages of the Sunmelia Beach Resort Hotel Spa 5 * in comparison with many other complexes in Turkey is, according to many vacationers, the presence of a large number of sun loungers on the beach. Hotel guests use loungers for free. At the same time, there are enough of them even at the peak of the season for everyone. Among other things, judging by the reviews, the sun loungers on the beach are also comfortable (with a grid instead of a mattress and very light). Sun loungers are installed on the hotel beach not under umbrellas, but under a common awning. This, according to many travelers, is also very convenient.

Of course, beach towels are also provided at the hotel. This service at the hotel is also free. Tourists can pick up towels at the spa.
