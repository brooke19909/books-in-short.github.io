Deductions

True, there are some income tax benefits. Most often, this is the so-called deduction for children. This is a peculiar process of reducing the tax base. As a result, your income is also reduced, which means that deductions from wages to tax services will also be less.

Income tax on a child is an important point that parents use. Especially when the salary is already low. Even the smallest cost savings here will be very helpful. For whom

Who has the opportunity to reduce income tax? If there is a child, then we can say that all parents. The question is different - how much. And is there always this opportunity? After all, almost all of us are parents. But we do not get deductions without exception.

And it is right. After all, you can reduce the income tax if you have only minor children. Or as long as the child is learning. That is, he is a cadet or full-time student. It should be borne in mind that the age of an adult child should not exceed 24 years. It is not so difficult to understand.

In addition, the parent's annual income cannot exceed 280,000 rubles. If it is higher (in practice - more than 23 thousand per month), then you are not supposed to have any deductions. Guardians, adoptive parents and their spouses are also entitled to this benefit. How much and when should citizens be entitled?

About deductions

The income tax on minor children, as we have already found out, is reduced. All because the deduction implies a certain understatement of the tax rate. But for what amount? How much is supposed to citizens in a particular case? Let's consider in more detail.

It all depends on the number of children in the family. The more there are, the higher the deduction. And for kids who are also considered disabled, special rules apply. Need specifics?



If you have one child, then a deduction of 1,400 rubles from monthly wages is required. The second minor is subject to a similar rule. The third and subsequent children allow you to receive 3 thousand benefits. This also applies to disabled people of groups 1 and 2. In some exceptional cases, they are entitled from 6,000 to 12,000 rubles of deduction. But these are special cases, which in practice are generally rare. So, paying attention to them makes no sense.

Documents (standard)

Income tax on a salary with one child (or several) is reduced when applying for a deduction. The benefit is usually provided to parents at the place of work or in tax services. The first option is more relevant.

To do this, you will need to present a statement of the established form and some package of documents. It all depends on the specific situation as a whole. Especially if the parents of the child are divorced, as well as married to other citizens. They are all entitled to a deduction from income.

What will have to be presented for registration of benefits? For starters, help 2-PIT. Codes

The last thing that remains to be understood is what codes need to be indicated in the 2-NDFL certificate in this or that case. After all, the deduction for income tax has its own "pointers".
