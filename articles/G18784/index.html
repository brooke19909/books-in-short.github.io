The resort of Gelendzhik. Hotels near the sea

Do you like relaxing by the sea? You are invited to the resort city of Gelendzhik. Hotels near the sea will offer you comfortable conditions and affordable prices.

Atlas Hotel

If you are interested in hotels near the sea in the city of Gelendzhik , pay attention to the Atlas Hotel. The beach is just a five minute walk from the hotel. Guests with cars are offered free parking. There is a playground for children. Bike rental is available. The rooms have TV, air conditioning, private bathroom, wireless internet. If you are considering hotels in Gelendzhik with a swimming pool near the sea , the Atlas hotel is suitable for you. The hotel has a swimming pool with a bar, a sun terrace, and a sauna. There is a cafe. Rooms are available in the following categories:

  • double - 2700 rubles per day;
  • triple - 3300 rubles;
  • four-seater - 3800 rubles per one night;
  • Studio - 4000 rubles;
  • apartments with a kitchen - 4500 rubles per day;
  • Suite with two bedrooms - 5000 rubles;
  • three-bedroom suite - 6000 rubles per day.

Pets are not allowed. Guests at the hotel can use the free airport shuttle service. Atlas Hotel is located on Gogol Street, 17.





Hotel Gelendzhik

If you prefer full board for your holidays, the Gelendzhik Hotel is invited to you with meals, next to the sea. By prior arrangement, breakfast, lunch or dinner can be delivered directly to your room. If you want to cook yourself, you can use the shared kitchen. There is a grocery delivery service. The hotel is only a hundred meters from the beach, it is approximately two minutes walk. At your disposal also - barbecue, terrace for relaxation. There is a free car park. For a fee, you can rent bicycles and fishing equipment. Equipped with a playground on the street, there is also a games room in the room. Each room has a TV, air conditioning, private bathroom, free Wi-Fi. Guests are offered the following room categories:

  • double standard - 3500 rubles per day;
  • double comfort - 5000 rubles per day;
  • Suite - 6000 rubles per night.

Some rooms have a balcony or terrace. Cozy rooms feature modern furniture. The hotel allows pets. The service is paid. Transfer is paid separately. Hotel Gelendzhik is located on 1b Gornaya Street.





Gelendzhik hotels near the sea. Hotel "Orlitsa"

From the Orlitsa Hotel to the Gelendzhik Embankment is just two minutes walk. If you are interested in Gelendzhik hotels with a pool near the sea, Orlitsa Hotel is a very good option. There is a playground for children on site. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and TV. Free Wi-Fi is available. Each room has a bathroom, a balcony, beautiful comfortable furniture. To accommodate guests are offered rooms of several categories:

  • double standard - the cost is 5000 rubles per day;
  • family room - the price for it will be 6500 rubles per day for two people and 7200 rubles if three people live;
  • Junior suite category - the cost is 8800 rubles per day.

The price includes breakfast. Guests are provided with a shuttle upon request, paid separately. Pets are not allowed. Hotel "Orlitsa" is located at: st. Lunacharsky, house 133a / 1.
