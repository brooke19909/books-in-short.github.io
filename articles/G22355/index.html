Dense knitting pattern: description and patterns

Winter is already over, but things related with knitting needles have not lost their relevance, because it can be not only sweaters, hats and scarves, but also light tunics, sweaters, tops and even swimwear for sea trips. It all depends on the imagination of the craftswoman, experience with patterns and the ability to combine them. A dense knitting pattern will never go out of fashion, as it is basic and should be in the piggy bank of every lover of knitting. And it‚Äôs better if there are several of them.

Types of patterns

Depending on the prevailing main motive, patterns can be dense (for example, the front surface), convex (weaving, elastic bands, etc.), and can be loose and openwork - representing a lace fabric, light and weightless.

If braids predominate in the pattern, then these are Arani, or Irish patterns. They differ not only in density, but also in volume. Look very advantageous on sweaters, sweaters, cardigans.

You liked the pattern on which animal or plant elements are intertwined in different shades, or maybe it's geometric shapes? Congratulations, your choice fell on jacquard. A very authentic, interesting way to decorate the future product. With it, you can focus on a specific part, or you can depict a whole panel. Especially if you learn the method of hidden thread pulling from the back of the work. This opens up a world of possibilities.

Even a simple combination of front and back loops can give unusual results. The work becomes embossed, and due to different depths, the yarn color will also change in different sections of the canvas. This will add extra zest to the product.





Training

First, to get a tight knitting pattern, you need to choose the right yarn and knitting needles. It is recommended that you link several test cases to make sure that the pattern is exactly what you need.

The size of the tools is directly related to the thickness of the thread, because if the diameter of the knitting needle is too small, the fabric will be tightened and the pattern will be deformed. And vice versa, large knitting needles and relatively thin yarn will give a honeycomb, openwork pattern, even where it should not be. Therefore, when buying yarn, it is necessary to take into account information provided by the manufacturer about the recommended size of knitting needles and / or hook.

Plain surface

The lightest of dense patterns is a simple surface. It is an alternation of rows of seamy and facial loops. The edge of such a fabric is bent outwards, so you need to knit an elastic band or hem it. But on some styles, such an illusion of elongation looks very advantageous. It all depends on what exactly you knit and to whom.

Cutting the armhole and neck is also not difficult with facial knitting. Dense patterns with knitting needles, the schemes of which will be presented later, are more difficult in terms of decreasing or adding loops, since the pattern is broken. But in this case, you can safely experiment.

Wrong and front



If you want to make a pattern, but do not know how to knit a large number of threads, then the wrong loops will come to your aid. On a smooth canvas they look like an impression, creating the impression of volume. In addition, if you correctly approach the process of stitching parts, you can do a two-sided thing with a convex pattern on one side and concave on the other.

Braids



A separate view of dense patterns is when a braid is tied over a front or back fabric. It is formed from an even number of loops that cross in front or at work. To do this, use an auxiliary spoke. This is an easy option.

But patterns of dense knitting with needles usually do not stop there. They can consist of several separate weaves, or combine into one large braid, divided in the center by a purl loop for a more convex pattern.

Jacquard



Another tight knit that is well suited for children's things, mittens, hats, all warm and bright - jacquard. There are already ready-made combinations of flowers and ornaments, but this dense knitting pattern can be invented on your own. Any pattern for embroidery is enough for him. Copy the fragment several times and the pattern is ready.

Since this method of knitting comes from northern Europe, the color palette should be in the Scandinavian style: white, beige, blue, brown, red. But this is not a mandatory rule, so the choice is always yours.

Difficult weaving



There are dense patterns with knitting needles, the patterns of which contain a large number of crossed loops. But this does not mean that the canvas will be like a net. Most likely, tiers or layers will appear on the finished product, although the knitting was solid. This effect is achieved by looping one on top of the other. But to use such schemes, you must already have experience with knitting needles or take a master class.

A few words in the end

We have provided you with general information, the knowledge of which involves knitting. Dense patterns, the schemes of which are placed in this article, can be combined, modified, improved as you like. Do not be afraid to experiment, please yourself and your loved ones.

It is important to remember that patterns are dense. With knitting needles, with diagrams and explanations, it is not so easy to catch the balance of the tension of the threads so that there is no deformation, and the pattern looked exactly as it should. To do this, it is recommended to knit several samples using different numbers of knitting needles and choose the most suitable. In addition, this will help in the calculation of loops.

A dense knitting pattern is appropriate on any product and fits almost all yarn available now in the market and in specialized stores.
