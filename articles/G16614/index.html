What are some good H4 bulbs? What H4 halogen lamps to choose?

The roadway should be well lit both during the day and at night. Therefore, car owners are required to pay maximum attention to headlight lighting. The bulbs in the H1 and H2 sockets are hopelessly outdated. Today, they have been replaced by improved modifications of lighting devices. Most often, 4 and 7 generation bulbs are used for head lighting. They are more economical and have a long service life.





To determine which H4 bulbs are good, it is worth considering their varieties. To date, there are three types of such light sources.

Halogen lamps

Now halogens are used most often. Most motorists consider the most favorable ratio of their price and quality. Devices of this type are bulbs made of refractory glass. Inside is a halogen gas, in turn, it contains a filament (spiral) through which voltage is applied. Due to this, the temperature rises in the flask, the gas heats up and a glow occurs.

Halogen products with base H4 shine with a bright white light. They contain not one thread, but two. The first is responsible for high beams. The second is for the neighbor.





If we talk about which H4 bulbs are good, then it is worth paying attention that halogens have several significant disadvantages:

  1. If one of the spirals fails, you will have to change the entire bulb.
  2. Products are very hot, so they can not be used with plastic optics.
  3. Lamps are quickly damaged by vibration.

Most often, motorists are interested in which bulbs are better - H4 halogens or biksenon. The second type clearly wins. Consider why.

Biksenon

Bi-xenon lighting devices are very different from halogen analogues. They also represent a flask filled with gas. However, in this case, not 2 spirals are located inside the lamp, but two electrodes projecting an electric arc between themselves.

If you use lighting products with a base H4, then they additionally have a magnet and a special screen. Thanks to these elements switching of a high beam and a passing light is carried out.





When choosing which H4 bulbs are good, you should pay attention to the benefits of Bi-Xenon. Such products:

  • They are characterized by increased light output, which is several times higher than the same indicators for halogens. This increases the illuminated area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe roadway.
  • Not afraid of vibrations. Due to the fact that there is no incandescent thread in the device, the products are characterized by increased reliability.
  • They do not heat up, so they can be installed in modern plastic headlights.

However, when deciding which H4 halogen lamps to choose, it is worth paying attention to the disadvantages of these lighting devices:

  • High cost.
  • The difficulty of self-replacement.
  • The need for extremely fine tuning.

Because of these disadvantages, LED lighting is increasingly preferred.

LEDs

LED lighting devices are the most promising and versatile. In the process of testing, such products show the best results.

LEDs withstand loads, do not overheat and do not fail after the first bump. At the same time, they work equally well in conditions of heat and severe cold. Among other things, LED devices have a huge work resource.





The only drawback is that LEDs are not suitable for every optics. Since LED devices form a light beam, they require special lenses.

Nevertheless, if we talk about which H4 bulbs shine better and burn out less often, then it is worth giving preference to LED lighting.

Weatherproof lamp

Of course, every driver wants to purchase the most versatile vehicle lighting. However, today a rather wide selection of H4 bulbs is on sale. Which are better?

In fact, it all depends on the frequency of use of the car and weather conditions. If you have to ride on wet asphalt every day in fog or rain, it is better not to choose lamps with diffused white light. Such lighting is almost invisible in bad weather.

On sale there are special devices for such cases - all-weather bulbs. A special coloring coating is applied to the flask of these products, due to which the luminous flux becomes yellow. This color is clearly visible on wet asphalt, so the roadway is clearly visible both during the day and at dusk.

Which bulbs are better: H4 or H7?

If we talk about these two socles, then we will consider their main differences:

  • In terms of the working life of the product, 7 modifications outperform H4 lamps almost twice.
  • In the lamps of version 4, 2 spirals are installed, while the more modern analogue has one.
  • The seventh option is usually used with free-form headlights.

However, it is very difficult to single out one type of lighting device. It all depends on the particular car. For example, consider the Volkswagen Transportet. What bulbs are good in T5? H4 for dipped and main beam. Other modifications to this car are not provided.

Therefore, before buying this or that lighting element, it is recommended to check with the technical characteristics of the machine.
