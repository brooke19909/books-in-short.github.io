How to sew shorts with your own hands?

How to sew shorts on a pattern, with ease and without difficulty? First of all, you need to follow the entire sequence of technology to create a drawing. It is important not to forget to take into account the increase in freedom of fit, which provide movement and breathing. Consider the process of performing work on a specific example.

Measurements

How to make a pattern of women's shorts? To do this, you need measurements:

  • ST - half waist.
  • SB - half hips.
  • PT - increase at the waist.
  • PB is an increase in the straight line of the hips.

For example, take the following measurements:

  • ST = 38 cm.
  • Sat = 48 cm.
  • Fri = 1 cm.
  • PB = 1 cm.

Tracing paper pattern

Now we transfer the drawing of shorts to tracing paper, do not forget to mark the control points with letters. Then we begin the simulation.

Zipper and other operations

With the help of sewing operations, you can make a zipper, and you need to iron everything.

It is important to know how to sew shorts where loops will be present. This operation is not complicated. The main thing is that their location is uniform along the belt. It is necessary to prepare five loops from a strip of fabric.

After that, they should be swept to the upper cuts of the halves of the shorts at the seams of the tucks. It is necessary to make the middle back seam to the edge of the top of the product.

Belt processing

Stitch the belt parts together. After that, sew a belt to the upper section of the shorts.

When the belt is ready, the belt loops must be turned up, their ends bent and stitched along the belt along the upper edge. On the right side at the end of the belt, process the loop. This completes the sewing of the product.
