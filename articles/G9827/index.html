Philips 1871 juicer

Today, a juicer can be found in the kitchen of almost every housewife. Such a device will be especially useful for families with children, because babies constantly need a large number of healthy vitamins that contain fruits and vegetables, and, accordingly, juice from them.

But even now, some squeeze the juice in an uncomfortable and rather laborious method, rubbing the fruit on a grater and then squeezing the liquid out of the pulp using gauze. This process takes a lot of time, it is impractical, and besides, small pieces of pulp are always present in the finished drink, which not everyone likes. I think that it is understandable why such an electrical appliance is popular in the household appliance market.

Philips 1871 juicer

Philips is currently one of the leaders in the market for electrical appliances. Under this brand, tens of thousands of units of various equipment are produced every year: food processors, toasters, bread machines, mixers, blenders, irons, waffle irons, electric kettles, vacuum cleaners, hair dryers, razors , curling irons , tongs and much more. The topic of this article is the Philips juicer. This juicer can rightly be called the flagship of this type of product from Philips. What is it made of?

Feed chamber, lid, pusher for fruit, filter and container for juice, pulp container, drive shaft, retainer (movable), electric motor block, mode switch, compartment for storing the cord, two removable nozzles, juice jug with a capacity of 1500 milliliters, lid for a jug with a section for separating the pulp. Consider the design. Philips juicer is made in one color - gray metal. This color is discreet and can easily fit into the interior of your kitchen. The case is made of steel (stainless). Among the main advantages, one can single out the material from which the jug and lid are made. This material was developed to replace expensive and high-quality acrylic. It has a strong glossy surface, high chemical resistance, high temperature stability, high rigidity. The filter, which has a Philips juicer, as well as the case, is made of stainless steel.
Lid for jug, container for squeezed pulp and pusher - made of polypropylene. This material is very often used in the manufacture of products for the food industry, therefore it is safe and does not harm the health of the consumer. Philips juicer will be a good option for people who have children in the house. Firstly, the cord connected to the outlet can be easily hidden in the housing compartment, and secondly, the parts are securely attached to each other, preventing them from getting hurt. Philips juicer has a 700 Watt motor. Its capabilities are enough for a quiet work, even with very hard fruits. In order to throw not too large fruits, you will not have to cut the fruit thanks to the eight-centimeter hole.

Philips juicer: price

In different stores of household appliances, the price for this product is different. It ranges in scale from 1300 to 1700 hryvnia (5200 - 6800 rubles). For a juicer with such power and a stainless steel case, this is an affordable price.
