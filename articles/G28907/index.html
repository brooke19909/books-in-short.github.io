Karlovy Vary. How to get to the resort

Karlovy Vary is located just one hundred and thirty kilometers west of Prague, from where you can quickly get to the resort via a European level motorway. With equal success, this can be done by rail. Karlovy Vary has two railway stations, one of which receives trains from the capital, and the other serves the German direction. And about five kilometers from the city there is an international airport that receives flights from all major cities of Russia.
