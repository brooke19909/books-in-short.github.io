The best hotels of Goa: reviews and photos

Choosing a place for a pleasant holiday in Goa, you need to pay attention that there are two different resort regions - southern and northern. The southern part offers tourists excellent hotels in 4 stars or 5. They are designed for guests with high incomes. Those who have a more modest affluence, you can settle in a three- or four-star, there is also a decent level of service and conditions. All hotels in India, which are located in the northern part, are simpler and more democratic than in the south. As a rule, they have 2-3 stars and are cottages or bungalows.

As for the southern part of Goa, it is a rather comfortable place for tourists to relax. The excellent climate, tropical nature and proximity to the sea open up unlimited opportunities for a comfortable stay in an area with highly developed infrastructure. The most popular hotels in South Goa will be presented below.

Taj Exotica Goa

The hotel is located in a park of wonderful beauty that attracts tourists from all over the world. The hotel is housed in a Mediterranean-style building. Comfortable rooms are located along the lines of their own beach. The rooms themselves will surprise guests with the modern design of the rooms, excellent furniture, the presence of household and media equipment. And some apartments radiate chic: marble floors and countertops, a bar, a private mini-pool, a jacuzzi. All windows offer beautiful views of the fabulous well-kept garden and the sea.

The hotel has a well-developed infrastructure: spa, tennis, sports ground, golf course, six restaurants. All this contributes to positive emotions and good mood of guests.

The hotel has 24-hour room service. Skilled workers offer babysitting services for children, wardrobe care. For people with disabilities, there are all the necessary conditions. High-quality professional service will fill the rest with a pleasant memory of paradise places. In this article, the best hotels of Goa are represented by another wonderful hotel complex.

Planet Hollywood Beach Goa

The five-star hotel is located near Warda Beach, which is famous for its light, soft sand and unusually beautiful color of the sea coast. The most experienced traveler will be able to evaluate the quality of service and living conditions at the hotel. For guests there are spacious comfortable rooms with bathrooms, new high-quality furniture, with good air conditioning and household appliances.

Around is a well-groomed area with a swimming pool. Guests can visit the fitness center, playground, restaurant, cafe, use the parking for your car. Vacationers can also enjoy delicious food from the local and other cuisines of Fame Restaurant. In the morning, guests can enjoy a full buffet breakfast, while lunch and dinner can be ordered in the restaurant's menu. You should try aromatic coffee in the nice Planet Dailies cafe, where the visitor has several types of this drink to choose from. For guests' services, fitness centers and spas are open, where you can not only relax and get wellness or beauty treatments, but also maintain your body in great shape. Snow-white sandy beaches and emerald vegetation allows you to truly enjoy your vacation. At the end of the day you can admire the stunning colors of the sea and sunset.

Almost all five-star Goa hotels in India offer excellent leisure facilities. The next hotel complex was no exception.

Leele goa

Located in the midst of a lush garden and blue lagoons, the excellent five-star Leele Goa Hotel will help you enjoy your vacation. Guests can use the swimming pool, tennis courts, golf course, sports and playgrounds.

The hotel serves a full buffet breakfast. Morning coffee can be enjoyed sitting on a cozy balcony with a paradise view of the sea.

At the hotel you can choose a program of excursions, enjoy Ayurvedic massage and other health and beauty treatments in the spa. Beach, pool, yoga classes - all for the services of guests. In addition to everything, you can glance at one of the seven restaurants located next to the hotel. There you can order local, European dishes. Evening - time for romantic sunsets, visits to night clubs. The next day will meet a stunning sunrise in South Goa.

There are many ways to organize leisure: you can spend it with children at the playgrounds, have a refreshing cocktail by the pool, just enjoy the sun while swimming in the sea, or visit the spa.

Kenilworth Resort & SPA, Goa

This hotel (Resort Goa), stylized under the era of the Portuguese conquerors, welcomes its guests in comfortable rooms. All of them are furnished with comfortable furniture and household appliances, equipped with equipment for adjusting the room temperature, the bathrooms and the bathroom are clean.

High-income guests will appreciate the luxury and view from the room in the Deluxe room. In the restaurants Mallika and Portofino you can enjoy delicious food of national or Italian cuisine. You can look into the lobby bar, which will appreciate discounts in the daytime.

To maintain a high level of quality, prestige and fame, the hotel invites its guests to take advantage of additional entertainment: a romantic boat ride under the moon on the Arabian Sea or exciting fishing will not leave you indifferent. For children, the "dolphin cruise" service will be interesting, during which they can get acquainted with the life of dolphins in their usual habitat.

Travel Comments on Vacation

Reviews of Goa hotels are mostly positive. The high-quality service was remembered by tourists along with a pleasant, vibrant pastime in India, South Goa. The magical Indian state invites you to visit the excellent hotels of North Goa. Each of them is saturated with traditional color, fun and just heavenly enjoyment.

Goa is a place where everyone gets a piece of happiness. Many hotel complexes are well adapted for families with children, many tourists like this. Adult leisure is also well organized. Goa is considered a beach resort and a place of entertainment, but there are also excursion programs here. The abundance of exoticism is especially pleasing to travelers.

Vivanta by Taj Panaji

Relaxation, like in paradise, a unique experience - all this at the Vivanta by Taj Panaji, with rooms overlooking the river and beautiful mountains. The hotel has a rooftop pool, spa, gym, restaurants, bars with delicious food and drinks.

The room has a huge forging, minibar, TV. You can walk to the sea in 10 minutes on tropical terrain.

Hotels in North Goa can also offer vacationers a lot of interesting things.

Grand hyatt goa

Having visited the excellent Grand Hyatt Goa Hotel, which is located in North Goa in the very center of the city, you can spend an unforgettable vacation. It has a capacity of 314 rooms. The beach is six minutes from the most comfortable hotel in the northern part. The hotel has a twenty-meter pool.

Guests have the opportunity to try local and other cuisines of the world in seven restaurants. Delicious snacks can be enjoyed on the private beach. The large and bright rooms at the hotel have a colorful design, equipped with a comfortable bed, a modern cable TV and a coffee maker. In the room you can relax and splash around in the bathroom. And having ordered a cocktail in your room, you can not only enjoy the drink, but also have a beautiful view from the balcony.

Goa marriott resort & spa

If you are looking for a good hotel in North Goa, so that it is as close to the sea, then you need to Goa Marriott Resort & Spa. A couple of minutes from it is the beach. You could even say that the hotel is located on the beach of Goa. This is a five-star hotel complex, which has a swimming pool, casino, spa. Near the hotel there are three restaurants to choose from, where they can try dishes of different cuisines of the world. It also offers clean, bright rooms with windows over breathtaking, exotic locations. The room is equipped with a safe, an apparatus for making coffee, tea, a private bathroom, a modern TV, a large and very comfortable bed.

To spend time, you can take a walk yourself or use the services of excursions (they can be ordered at the hotel), do sports in the fitness center, and enjoy spa treatments.

Acron Waterfront Resort

The small Acron Waterfront Resort is considered one of the best hotels in North Goa. It has only 29 rooms, but there is everything for an unforgettable vacation.

To get to the beach, you need to walk only a hundred meters. The hotel also has a swimming pool. At the spa, massage services are available.

The bar has a huge selection of different drinks; the hotel restaurant serves Chinese, Indian and other cuisines of the world. Private cars can be parked for free at the hotel. To all this, it is worth adding a professional, pleasant service to hotel staff.

Seashell Vivalls The Villa Resort

The rooms are equipped with a comfortable large bed, a modern TV, a bathroom with all necessary cosmetics and more. Some rooms have a seating area to relax in after a busy day. Living in rooms whose colorful design is made in local traditions is unlikely to leave anyone indifferent!

All rooms at Seashell Vivalls The Villa Resort overlook the tropical garden and sea. Sitting on the balcony, admiring the beauties, taking your time to drink coffee or just enjoy the warm breeze - all this makes the rest unforgettable. The beach can easily be reached in 10 minutes on foot. The airport is 35 km from the hotel. By the way, for those who like to travel independently, the hotel provides car rental services.

Taj Fort Aguada Resort & Spa, Goa

The Vivanta by Taj Fort Aguada is simply huge! The area covers about 30 hectares of fabulous landscape. A considerable selection of rooms is 145, many of them with bewitching views of the Arabian Sea. Each room has a private balcony, wooden furniture, air conditioning, which plays a significant role for a comfortable stay, as well as a modern TV. In addition to everything, you can soak in the bath.
