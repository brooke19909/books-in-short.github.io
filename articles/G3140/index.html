Features

Hotpoint-Ariston washing machines have distinctive features. They are the application of innovative technologies. The most equipped to date is the Aqualtis series. In it, the manufacturer improved the protection and self-diagnosis systems, thanks to which a malfunction can be noticed in time. And this, in turn, significantly reduces repair costs. Although spare parts for Ariston washing machines are sold throughout Russia, and there are no particular problems with their purchase, but still the cost is not very low.

The attention of consumers deserves the Auto Dose system. Such equipment made it possible to produce devices with a high level of energy saving - class A +++ . Water consumption is also reduced by about 30%. Intelligent control independently doses the amount of powder and conditioner. A sensor is installed in the detergent drawer. He informs the owner that the powder in the cell is running out.

Care Technology - a system that allows the device to independently determine the weight of the laundry being washed. Also, using this technology, the device is able to adapt to any type of fabric.

Many Ariston models are marked with Ecotech, as they are environmentally friendly.

Error codes for washing machines Ariston

All modern models are equipped with self-diagnosis. For convenience, manufacturers programmed errors with special codes. This is necessary in order to pay attention to the malfunction in time and accordingly save on repairs. Information is displayed. In order to understand it, you need to know the decoding of the codes. In Ariston washing machines, errors are indicated by the English letter F and serial numbers.

F01 - a short circuit has occurred.

F02 - problems with the engine.

F03 - the temperature sensor or problems with the heater relay are out of order.

F04 - damage to the water level sensor.

F05 - malfunction of the drain pump.

F06 - problems with the control panel (violation in pressing buttons).

F07 - The heater is not covered by water.

F08 - Damage to the sensor of the heating element.

F09 - problems with electronics (non-volatile memory).

F10 - Faulty water level sensor.

F11 - problems connecting the pump.

F12 - gap between the display module and the controller.

F13 - malfunction in the control circuit of the drying process.

F14 - drying does not turn on.

F15 - violation of the drying mode.

F17 - problems with closing the hatch door.

F18 - There are problems in the microprocessor.

These error codes are provided for those Hotpoint-Ariston washing machines that have a display. Models without it can also carry out diagnostics, however they are programmed to inform the owner by flashing certain buttons. The signal will be repeated several times after a certain period of time. The exact information about the codes for each model is contained in the instructions, which should be included.

It is important to remember that the repair of such devices can only be carried out by a person with a special education. Therefore, without knowledge, it is not recommended to carry out repairs yourself. This may cause the device to fail completely.
