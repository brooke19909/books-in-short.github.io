Service

The registration service is open around the clock and serves guests as arrivals and departures. The staff speaks Russian, which is very convenient for tourists and hotel guests. Subject to availability, express service is practiced. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms. For this, special places are equipped. Luggage storage and safety deposit boxes are available.

Rules of residence and design in the guest house

Check-in starts at 12-00, check out the next day at 13-00 or at the request of tourists.

It is possible to cancel the reservation without penalties only 20 days before arrival. In other cases, that is, later than this period or no check-in at all, the penalty will be charged in full, and this is 100% prepayment.

Accommodation and placement of children in rooms

Guest house "Mamaika" (Sochi) offers the possibility of accommodation with children. Children under 5 years old with extra bed are free of charge. Staying children under 12 years with an extra bed costs 500 rubles per night. Older children and adults will have to pay 800 rubles per night for extra beds. Only one extra bed is allowed in the rooms and this must be agreed with the hotel staff in advance. Pets can also be accommodated in the room for a fee and as agreed with the hotel. Various credit cards are accepted at the hotel.
