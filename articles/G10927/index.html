How to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Internet: instructions for customers

Sberbank customers can fully enjoy all the opportunities that open before them. They have access to mobile banking, they can use the online service, receive money at ATMs and make payments through terminals.

Use of services





Many are interested in how to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Internet. Only those people who are already users of the online service can do this. The catch is that the card must already be registered in the mobile banking system so that the client can use the Sberbank Online service.

But if earlier on the question of how to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Internet, the management replied that this was impossible, now they have added such a service. Now make it real without leaving your home.

Activate Mobile Banking



In order to be able to fully use the various services, it is necessary that you have a special online service from Sberbank connected. He is able to significantly simplify the life of each client.

If this is done, then it will be easy for you to figure out how to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Internet. First you need to go to the website of the specified financial institution, and then through a special form enter into online banking. To do this, you will need to remember your permanent password and enter the identifier. They can be ordered at any ATM or payment terminal. Also, customers have the opportunity to get it by phone support.

Having entered your personal account, you need to see the menu. There should be a section "Mobile Banking" (MB). By clicking on “Connection Details”, you will see a window that opens. Intuitively, you can understand how to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Internet using a card number.

In the form provided (pop-up window), you must specify which package you want to choose - “economical” or “full”. After that, you will need to enter your phone number and card number (it can be either debit or credit), on which you want to be able to monitor operations. After filling in all the necessary fields, you need to click the "Confirm" button and wait until SMS arrives. The code from it will need to be entered in the special field “SMS-password” and confirm the operation.

As soon as the application is accepted, the system will automatically redirect you to the main page of your personal account.

Checking the service



In some cases, there are situations that the client does not receive a message stating that he is connected to mobile banking. But everyone has the opportunity to check whether he is functioning. After you have figured out how to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Internet without leaving your home, you can try to use the specified service. To do this, you can send a message to the number 900 with the text “Balance ****”, where **** is the last digit in your card number. If you get a response, then the service is connected. If not, then wait a little longer. Usually its activation takes place within 24 hours from the moment the application was submitted.

But you can disable it only at the bank branch. It is impossible to do this via telephone, Sberbank Online service, terminals or ATMs.

Tariffs for using the service



Before you figure out how to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Internet, you need to familiarize yourself with how much you have to pay. Each client has a choice: he can connect a “full” or “economical” package of services. Depending on this, the board will depend.

It is worth knowing that the "economical" package provides quite limited opportunities. The client can block or unblock the card, make payments by organizations for free. If you decide to find out the balance, then 3 rubles will be charged from the card account. To get acquainted with the history of recent operations, you will need to pay 15 rubles.

For those who activate the full package, 60 rubles will be debited from the account every month. for Standart MasterCard and Visa Classic or 30 rubles. for Maestro or Electron. But privileged owners of gold cards can use the service for free. A full package allows you to use all available services and perform any operations.

Potential opportunities

Each client who has figured out how to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Internet receives a lot of advantages. So, using a telephone, he can:

- unlock / block the card;

- find out the balance of funds and view recent transactions;

- view a list of cards connected to mobile banking;

- recharge your own or any other mobile phone;

- make a charity payment;

- transfer funds;

- pay for the services of companies.

Other connection ways



If you just recently began to use the services of Sberbank and have not yet figured out all the features offered, then it is better to contact the department, where a specially trained employee will help you understand all the details of banking connection. To do this, you need to bring your passport and bank card with you. But do not come back if you forget the plastic. An employee can take all the necessary information from the database after identifying your identity.

You can also connect informing by phone through a terminal or ATM. Having inserted the card into any of these devices, you need to select the option “Connect mobile banking”, and then follow the instructions that will appear on the screen.

Some consider it a more acceptable way to activate the service. This can be done simply by calling the bank hotline at 8-800-555-5550.

In addition, not everyone wants to activate the service via the Internet, because they believe that this requires a specific provider. But this is not so. The above instructions can be used even if you want to learn how to connect a mobile bank (Sberbank) via the Megafon Internet.
