Holidays in Boutique Paradise 3 - a truly paradise vacation!

For those who, on a tourist trip or on their own, lightly, decided to go on vacation to Thailand, the main problem is to find an inexpensive, but really convenient and comfortable hotel.  



Description. Hotel Boutique Paradise 3 is perhaps the most convenient place to stay in Pattaya, considering the price / quality ratio. The hotel belongs to the urban type, corresponds to the class of "3 stars" and meets almost all modern requirements of comfort and service. It is located in the center of Pattaya, about a hundred kilometers from the airport. Nearby the Boutique Paradise 3 You can visit famous shopping centers and supermarkets. The most popular of them are considered "Garden Royal", "Big C", "Central" and others. Here you can buy goods of different price categories, but of good quality, ranging from local exotic souvenirs to clothing, household appliances and electronics, etc. In addition, literally a stone's throw from the Boutique Paradise 3 there are small markets and bazaars with fruits, vegetables and homemade dishes, which are prepared in front of tourists. For those who like to taste local food and drinks, restaurants and cozy cafes, which are numerous near the hotel, will hospitably open their doors.

The rooms.

Boutique Paradise Hotel 3 has 170 rooms of various categories of comfort - from standard well-appointed rooms to luxury and VIP rooms. All rooms are spacious, bright, sunny, with wide windows from the floor. The size of the rooms depends on the class of the room. Cozy modern furnishings, traditional Thai rugs and tapestries on the walls give the housing an original national flavor. Fresh linens, elegant sleeping sets have a pleasant rest. The rooms are equipped with wide-screen TVs with satellite TV, free Internet access, a minibar, a safe, an electric kettle and tea set, and are also connected to a central air conditioning. The suites and VIP rooms have separate air conditioning and soundproofing systems.

Each room has a bathroom with shower / bath and a bathroom. The technique works fine. Bathrooms feature towels and toiletries, hair dryers. The rooms are cleaned every day, bath accessories and bed linen are changed. Upon request, you can order breakfast or drinks, fruits in the rooms.





Nutrition. Every morning, guests of the hotel are offered breakfast with local and European dishes. A bar and restaurant on site await visitors from morning until late at night.

Beach. A sandy beach, comfortable, with a high degree of comfort, is located about two hundred meters from the hotel. Here is a real paradise for adults and children.

Additional Information. In addition to the basic service, the hotel provides a number of additional services. For those who want to hold a conference or other social event, a business center is equipped with all the necessary equipment. Elevator, currency exchange office work around the clock. Secured parking, outdoor pool, gift shop, tour desk - everything that vacationers may need is taken into account in the service. In addition, the hotel provides transfers from the station and the airport.
