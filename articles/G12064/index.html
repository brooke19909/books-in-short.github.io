Night club "Mod", St. Petersburg: photos and reviews

St. Petersburg is a graceful city. Love the northern capital of a person of any age category, without exception. The kid will be delighted with the attractions in local parks, the aquarium and the water park. Mature tourists will enjoy the greatest architecture, historical sites and attractions. What to do for the younger generation? For these, there are many entertainment centers. It is recommended to look at the Mod club in St. Petersburg. A good impression of this wonderful city will be guaranteed.

brief information





The project of the Mod club in St. Petersburg was created over several years. The developers adhered to one goal - to create a universal entertainment complex in which people with a wide variety of interests could relax. Now he works in three main areas:

  • modern theater;
  • musicians coming from all over the world;
  • live performances of the most famous and completely unknown artists.

When the project was still at the planning stage, it had the working name Modernist. The creators wanted to break stereotypes by creating a universal club, unique in its direction. In this name they reduced to three letters Mod, considering this option more convenient for visitors.

Description

One of the most popular is the Mod club in St. Petersburg. Photos of the institution are presented in our article. They show that the halls are always crowded. This club has a great location - in the very center of the city. Near it there are public transport stops, a metro station and ample parking. The entire entertainment complex can be divided into several main areas:





  • Concert hall.
  • Dance floor.
  • Summer terrace.
  • Pizzeria.

On its territory there are three bars where you can buy a tasty drink at an affordable price. At each site, modern equipment is installed. The total capacity of the Mod club in St. Petersburg is 800 people.

Additionally organized a VIP zone. Here people can sit in a relaxed atmosphere, celebrate an important event and even conduct business negotiations.

All visitors are provided with free internet. On the territory of the institution there is an age restriction. Entrance is prohibited to persons under 18 years of age.

About the schedule

The club is open daily from 16:00 to 6:00 in standard mode. Live concerts and other recreational activities usually take place on Friday or Saturday.





Themed events are often organized here: New Year's parties for children and adults, mask shows, holidays dedicated to March 8 or February 23. Information about upcoming parties is published on the official website or on the social network. Usually, on these days a thematic dress code is entered. If desired, you can coordinate with the administration of your own celebration.

Every Thursday, at 18:00, a guest guest, Ilyas Muratov, a contemporary artist known throughout the world, comes to the Mod club in St. Petersburg. He creates his works in front of a large audience.

Price policy

The first thing that excites any person who plans to visit the entertainment complex is how much money does he need to take with him? On weekdays and some weekends, admission is free. It is important to please the security guard and pass face control. Paid entrance is created only on those days when stars of national pop and world level appear on the stage. The cost of one ticket can be from 150 to 5 thousand rubles.





The bar has the best prices. For example, the Sambuca drink can be bought at a price of only 200 rubles. A shot of Putinka vodka costs 50 rubles. At the same price you can buy Campari. The price is for 50 grams of the drink. Not everyone comes to the club to drink alcohol. Sober-minded people can enjoy a cup of aromatic coffee for 80 rubles.

Employee Reviews





Every month, the official website publishes fresh vacancies to the Mod club in St. Petersburg. Administrators invite new employees to the post: cook, pizza maker, waiter, bartender and dishwasher. Salaries range from 25 to 50 thousand rubles. The main condition is a pleasant appearance and a desire to work. No bad habits are welcome. Existing employees highlight several positive points:

  • Flexible working hours.
  • A friendly team always meets in the entertainment center.
  • There is an opportunity to earn good tips.
  • You can go to all concerts for free, and many employees even manage to chat with the stars.
  • Salary is always paid on time. Several times a year, employees receive bonuses.
  • Official employment, payment of leave and sick leave.

Negative about this place is extremely rare. Some workers do not like the noisy atmosphere, the need to work at night and a large crowd.

How to get there

We should also talk about how to get to the Mod club in St. Petersburg. As mentioned earlier, it is located in the very center of the city. Accordingly, the route will be extremely simple:

  1. Wherever you are, walk to the nearest metro station.
  2. The final stop will be Nevsky Prospect, Admiralteyskaya or Gostiny Dvor stations.
  3. Next, you need to turn off, moving along Griboedov Avenue.
  4. You will need to go past the museum of M. M. Zoshchenko and the Benois Wing.
  5. On the left side of the road there will be a gray building of ancient design, inside which you can see the arch. This is the famous club "Mod" in St. Petersburg. Now it remains to go past the arch into the courtyard and find the entrance.

The journey time from Nevsky Prospekt station is 5 minutes, from Admiralteyskaya 15 minutes, from Gostiny Dvor 25 minutes.

In addition to the metro, you can get to this place by your own car, by bus number 3, 3M, 3MB, 7, 22, 24, 27, 191 or trolley bus number 1, 5, 7, 10, 11 and 22.
