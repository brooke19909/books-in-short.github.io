Hotel Rayaburi Hotel Patong 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is an amazing country with a rich culture and breathtaking nature. Every year the number of tourists in the state only increases. Travelers from all over the world come here. Many of them even spend several months here.

Of course, several resort towns with the most developed tourist infrastructure can be distinguished in the country. One of the popular places is the island of Phuket. Here, tourists are waiting for dozens or even hundreds of hotels, pensions and guest houses for every taste. A popular place is considered the hotel Rayaburi Hotel Patong 3 *.

During vacation planning, everyone is trying to find as much information as possible. Travelers are interested in the location of the hotel and its proximity to the beach. Important points are the conditions of food and accommodation, as well as the availability of amenities for the child. These data will be relevant for many tourists choosing a vacation destination.

Where to look for a hotel complex? Location and its advantages

According to reviews, the location of the hotel complex Rayaburi Hotel Patong 3 * is one of the important advantages. It is located almost in the center of the Patong resort area, just a few meters from the municipal beach of the same name.





This is a calm area, populated mainly by foreign tourists. Nevertheless, nearby the hotel there are shops, cafes, restaurants, entertainment centers - in short, you can find a lot of interesting activities without feeling torn from the world. By the way, in just a few minutes you can walk to a public transport stop, and from there you can already go to any point in the vicinity. The airport can be reached in 45 minutes. By the way, travel agencies, as well as the hotel itself, offer their customers a convenient shuttle service, so you should not worry about the road.

Nearby is the famous Bangla Road, which is the center of entertainment and commerce in the city.

What does the hotel look like? Key facts about the territory

What does the hotel complex Rayaburi Hotel Patong 3 * look like? Description and infrastructure of the hotel, of course, are important for many tourists. After all, if you have to spend a week (or more) in the same place, you want a certain degree of comfort.

The hotel consists of a large multi-story building designed in a unique Thai style. Even looking at a modern boutique hotel is a pleasure, especially in the evening, when not only the building, but the whole territory is highlighted with bright lights. The internal design corresponds to the external - everywhere there is interesting stucco molding, bright accessories, high-quality wooden furniture and ecological fittings. All this, of course, is complemented by modern technology.

The hotel area is small but beautifully furnished. There are pools, playgrounds for sports and recreation. The territory itself resembles an exotic garden - everywhere there are trees and flowering bushes, bright flower beds, benches and walking paths, traditional Thai decorative elements, in a word, all so that you feel in a real oriental fairy tale.

By the way, the RayaBuri Hotel Patong 3 * hotel was opened not so long ago - in 2008. Of course, from time to time there are reconstructions in order to provide tourists with maximum comfort during their holidays.

How many rooms are there in a hotel?

What accommodation conditions can the hotel complex Rayaburi Hotel Patong 3 * offer? Reviews of tourists suggest that the rooms here are very spacious, cozy and comfortable. By the way, this hotel can rightfully be considered small, since it has 58 living rooms.

For the most part these are standard rooms of different capacities - depending on your needs, you can choose a room for one, two or three people. In addition, there are rooms with a high level of comfort. At your service are spacious apartments, which in addition to the bedroom and living room have a fully equipped kitchenette with a dining table, a spacious refrigerator, a sink, which is necessary for cooking with household appliances and utensils.

This is exactly what the rooms on the territory of the hotel Rayaburi Hotel Patong 3 * look like. Guest reviews suggest that the rooms are cleaned every day, during which bath towels are changed. You can really feel at home here.

What kind of food should I count on?

Bright memorable vacation necessarily includes good food. Arriving in another country, a person, as a rule, wants to enjoy the delights of local cuisine. What scheme can the guests of the Rayaburi Hotel Patong 3 * hotel count on?





Paying for accommodation, you automatically get the right to free breakfast. They are buffet-style in a hotel restaurant. The menu here is quite diverse - there are light snacks, salads, fresh vegetables and fruits, cereals and omelets, in short, everything that is traditionally served for breakfast in hotels.

You can eat in the main restaurant - they serve lunches and dinners, but food is already prepared to order and, accordingly, is paid separately. By the way, the local restaurant is considered the best in the district - not only guests, but also tourists from other hotels and local residents come here. Chefs are real magicians who delight guests with masterpieces of international, Mediterranean and traditional Thai cuisine.

In addition, the hotel has a bar serving good drinks, aromatic coffee and light desserts. Here you can always relax, enjoying a delicious cocktail, stylish design and a relaxed atmosphere.

Food is unlikely to turn into a problem. If for one reason or another you cannot visit the local restaurant, there are many cafes and other catering establishments in the area. Do not forget that many rooms have a well-equipped kitchenette, which means you can cook yourself.

Where is the beach? Recreation and entertainment on the water

For most tourists, Thailand is associated with beautiful beaches. As already mentioned, the territory of the hotel Rayaburi Hotel Patong 3 * goes directly to the seashore, namely to the popular Patong beach. This is one of the most beautiful and lively places on the island.





According to reviews, the coast is clean, and the entrance to the sea is very convenient. Since this beach is public, rent of sunbeds and umbrellas is paid separately (prices are quite reasonable). There are sports facilities on the beach, as well as bars where you can enjoy chilled drinks or even dine.

Fans of outdoor activities will not have to be bored either, as there is a sports center on the shore. Here you can easily rent a boat, canoe or pedalo. Travelers have the opportunity to ride a boat, water skiing, banana, try sailing and windsurfing. If you wish, you can go scuba diving, because the sea world here is amazing variety and bright colors.
