Volumetric hat with pompom

Remember, knitting is an art, and each needlewoman herself chooses a suitable pattern for her. In this description, a simple pattern is used in which the reverse and front loops alternate. Thanks to an interesting combination of loops, the volumetric cap with knitting needles has an interesting shape. And most importantly - it will be warm.





First of all, we prepare yarn, knitting needles. We knit a sample, calculate the required number of loops.

We collect loops and close them in a circle. How to knit a volume head with knitting needles? Pattern Pattern:

1-7 row - we knit everything with the wrong loops.

8-14 row - we knit all the loops face.

Now we alternate every 7 rows of the front and back loops.

Some needlewomen call this pattern ‚Äúcaterpillar‚Äù.

Loop reduction can occur in several ways. For example, divide the total number of loops into 4 parts. Now in every second row it is necessary to cut 4 loops through the same distance. When about 10-12 stitches remain on the knitting needles, the cap can be pulled off. To do this, the working thread must be pulled through all the loops and tightened well. Now the working thread must be well fixed on the wrong side.

From the remaining threads you can make a pompom, which in the future must be sewn to the cap. You can also use fur pompons purchased at needlework stores. Often they are sold with ribbons. They fix this element of decor from the wrong side. When washing it can be easily removed.

Hat with lapel

Especially popular today are caps with a lapel, knitted with English gum. They are called hats-takori. It is also a fairly lightweight pattern that a beginner knitter can handle.

It is also necessary to pick up knitting needles, yarn. For this model, it is better to use mohair, kid mohair or angora. Knitting occurs on circular knitting needles.





Description of the pattern for volume caps with knitting needles. The scheme of the English gum:

1 r.: 1 front, double crochet, remove the next loop as the wrong one. Repeat until the end of the row.

2 r.: Nakid, remove the loop as the wrong one and knit 2 loops of the wrong one. We knit to the end.

3 r.: 2 loops we knit the front, yarn, remove the loop as the wrong one. We knit to the end.

Alternating the second and third row, we knit a cap of 140-160 rows.

Many fashionistas prefer an elongated crown in a takori hat. To form it, you should knit the next 4 rows of fabric with a regular 1x1 elastic band. Now we knit the entire fabric with facial loops, while we knit 1 front, we knit the next two together. So alternate to the end of the row. Now we knit 2 rows of facial.

After every 2 facial we knit together. We tighten the remaining loops well and gently hide the thread on the wrong side. Volumetric cap with mohair spokes is ready.
