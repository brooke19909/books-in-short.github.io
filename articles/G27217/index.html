Dombay hotels: overview, description, photos and reviews

The Dombay Valley is located at an altitude of more than 1600 meters above sea level. It is located in the southern part of the Karachay-Cherkess Republic and is one of the most popular ski resorts in Russia.

The region‚Äôs infrastructure is represented by lifts, slope preparation systems, and track lighting. Dombay hotels are concentrated near the cable cars. These are large modern hotels and private cottages aimed at budget travelers.

Welcome!

A distinctive feature of the resort is its compactness. All of its tracks are within walking distance from residential areas. There are also cafes and eateries, ski equipment rental points. A walk from one end of the village to the other takes no more than half an hour. Riding in these parts begins in late November, and ends in mid-May. At the same time, the functioning of Dombay hotels is year-round.

In the summer, they receive tourists who come to enjoy the local natural beauties. In a few minutes walk from the village, mountain rivers, which feed the flood meadows, carry their waters. It offers horseback riding, cycling, boat rafting and catamaran trips. The administration of Dombai hotels offers an abundance of excursions for every taste and budget.

A bit of history

The first ski resort in the Dombay Valley was opened at the beginning of the 20th century. Up to this point, the local village was a tiny mountain village. That he remained after the advent of skiers. Everything changed after the collapse of the USSR. New modern hotels were built. Landscaped slopes appeared in Dombay, new routes were developed. The resort began to develop and grow.

Today, the ski complexes of the valley host international competitions and championships in freestyle, snowboarding, skiing, paragliding.

Transport accessibility

If you believe the reviews, then the easiest way to get to the hotels in the village of Dombay is to use the services of taxi drivers. The cost of the trip is 3000 rubles. Youth companies choose a bus transfer organized by Gulliver. One ticket from Mineralnye Vody Airport will cost about 800 rubles. Travel time does not exceed 5 hours. Alternatively, you can consider renting a car. Rental agencies are located in Mineralnye Vody. Standard rate - 1500 rubles per day.

The nearest railway stations are in Mineralnye Vody, Pyatigorsk, Nevinnomyssk. The train journey from Moscow takes about a day. A taxi to the Dombay Valley costs 3,000 rubles. Shuttle buses run. Comfortable liners go to the resort from the capital. Buses are equipped with toilets, air conditioning, TVs. A ticket costs about 3,500 rubles. Almost thirty hours will have to be spent on the road.

Residence

The Snow Leopard Hotel leads the list of the most popular tourist accommodation facilities on Dombay. The complex operates year-round. It features an indoor pool with water heating, a sauna, bicycle rental and a tour desk. Standard rooms and premium apartments are available. Minimum rate is 3500 per night.

The hotel is located in a nature reserve. The ski slopes and cafes are within easy reach. The nearest chair-lift is 850 meters away. Services of the Snow Leopard Hotel in Dombay:

  • non-smoking rooms;
  • room service;
