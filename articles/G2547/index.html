Kusadasi Golf & Spa Resort (Kusadasi): description and reviews of tourists

If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation in Turkey and at the same time stay in a luxurious five-star hotel, then the Kusadasi Golf & Spa Resort (Kusadasi) will be an excellent option. The fact that this hotel complex offers its guests, and we will talk further.



Location

This magnificent hotel is located in the village of Soke, 17 kilometers from the city of Kusadasi, on the coast of the Aegean Sea. Izmir Airport is 80 kilometers away. Thus, the journey from the air harbor to Kusadasi Golf & Spa Resort will take about an hour and a half. The location of this hotel is considered by many to be very successful if you plan to travel to the sights of Turkey. So, the distance to Ephesus, Didim and Miletus from here is quite small. Also next to the hotel complex is an 18-hole golf course.
