Do-it-yourself paper making aster: different ways

With paper-made asters, you can decorate any room with your own hands, beautiful magnificent flowers are obtained. Crafts can be made both from plain colored paper, and from corrugated. Will be needed for work: scissors, PVA glue or double-sided tape, paper strips. If asters will stand on a shelf in a vase, then material will also be needed for the stems. You can use green thin cocktail tubes or even twigs of a bush, wooden skewers or thick wire. For wrapping the core, corrugated green paper is best. Make leaves out of it.

DIY handmade paper astra

Stripes of A4 paper, 5-6 cm wide, are cut from double-sided paper of different colors for the printer. One edge of it is cut with thin strips or simple scissors, or specially designed for this purpose. They have several blades, so the cutting process is much faster and more accurate.

Do-it-yourself notches for making paper asters from paper are not completely made. A small strip of about 1 cm is left to curl paper into a skein. For convenience, use some kind of rod, for example, a piece of wire or a toothpick. In order for the skein to not turn around, its edge is glued to the PVA to the last turn. You can use a piece of double-sided tape, as in the photo above. At the end of the work, the petals of the aster from paper made with their own hands are bent in different directions. It turns out a spectacular flower that you can simply stick on cardboard and make a birthday card by adding a couple of leaflets around it. If you want to put the bouquet in a vase, instead of a toothpick, immediately take a long wire that will act as a stem.

Voluminous flowers

Using the method described above, you can make large magnificent asters with your own hands from corrugated paper. The main flower is also cut out from a long rectangle, only the edges of one of its sides are cut not by straws, but by a wide "noodle". Then there will be scrupulous and painstaking work on cutting corners on each part. The pointed petals of an aster should turn out.

When everything is ready, the bottom rectangle is twisted around the rod and the edge is glued in the same way to the last turn. Further work continues on the creation of sepals. A circle of green corrugated paper is cut out, and the edges are cut into large petals without affecting the central part of the craft. In the end, you can attach another small circle for strength.

Another option for crafts

You can create a beautiful flower from a whole pack of the finest tissue paper. The same small rectangles of different colors are cut. It is advisable to select close shades for the flower itself - pink + burgundy, yellow + orange, white + beige, etc. Several green rectangles should be put below. It will be sepals.
