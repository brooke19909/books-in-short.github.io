Cynthiana Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus / Paphos): hotel description and reviews of tourists

In one of the most beautiful corners of Greece, the Cynthiana Beach Hotel 3 * Resort is located. Cyprus is not only luxurious beaches and beautiful nature, but also a distinctive culture. Here, travelers can enjoy all the facets of a Mediterranean holiday.





Hotel Cynthiana Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus): address

The hotel is located on a picturesque secluded cape in close proximity to the sea coast. Paphos, Coral Bay, 8100 - the address where tourists can find this institution. It is only 7 km from the main street of the city, which is rich in historical monuments. Distance from the international airports of Paphos and Larnaca, respectively, 20 and 165 km.





Hotel Cynthiana Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus): how to get

Arriving at one of the international airports, guests are certainly interested in how to get to their destination. Speaking of Paphos air harbor, less than an hour will take you from the road to the Cynthiana Beach Hotel 3 * . Cyprus is not such a big island, and therefore you cannot get lost. The route is as follows:

  • First you need to go onto the A6 highway and drive along it for about 7 km;
  • Having stopped in the city limits of Paphos, you need to turn left and continue on a two-lane road;
  • after three traffic lights you need to turn towards Danais Avenue and drive 2 km;
  • after passing one intersection, you need to take the roundabout, and then onto the two-lane road to Tombs of the Kings;
  • after 9 km (at the seventh intersection) you need to turn left, where the hotel is located.

From Limassol Airport, you will get to Cynthiana Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus) as follows:

  • following the signs, you need to go onto the A1 highway, which leads to Limassol;
  • stopping in the city, it is worth following the signs that indicate the path to Paphos;
  • when you get to the city, you should be guided by the route that was described earlier.

Accommodation

To accommodate guests provided 230 comfortable rooms. You can enjoy traditional Greek motifs at Cynthiana Beach Hotel 3 * (Cyprus). Photos show concisely designed rooms, in which the emphasis is on natural materials. So, guests are available the following accommodation options:

  • Standard rooms are compact, bright rooms. Depending on the preferences of the guests, a room with double or twin beds can be provided. Luxurious views of the garden or the sea can be enjoyed from the balcony.
  • Family rooms can be either one-room or two-room. Large and twin beds are available for guests. There is a small seating area, represented by a coffee table and soft chairs. It has a private furnished balcony with sea views.
  • The suite consists of two rooms. The main advantage of the bedroom is its huge panoramic window, which occupies almost the entire wall. thus, falling asleep and waking up, you will enjoy the sea. The hotel is equipped with comfortable folding sofas. Also has its own porch.
  • All apartments are located on the ground floor, and therefore have direct access to the pool through the terrace. In addition to the bedroom and living room, there is also a kitchen with appliances and utensils. Separately allocated dining area.
