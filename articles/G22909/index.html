Design

In terms of design, this smartphone is very similar to the iPhone 6 or 6s. Most of the front panel area is occupied by a diagonal display, like the iPhone 6s plus - 5 and a half inches. Above it is the earpiece, a small peephole of the front camera and a number of sensors. At the bottom, unlike most devices under the Android OS, there is only one mechanical button (another common feature with "apple" gadgets). Outwardly, it looks more like a mechanical button in Samsung Galaxy S6, but according to the principle of operation, it fully and completely matches smartphones that run iOS. The remaining mechanical buttons are grouped on the left side of the smartphone. The volume controls and the power button are displayed here. On the upper side of the device is a standard wired audio port and a microphone, which provides suppression of external noise during a conversation. At the bottom is a talking microphone, micro-USB and a loud speaker. Moreover, the latter, like the latest generation of iPhone, is hidden behind a stylish lattice of round holes. A slot for installing SIM cards or an external drive is displayed on the right side of the device. On the back side there is the main camera, the logo of the manufacturer and a single LED (allows the camera to take pictures in low light). The case of this smartphone can be in the following colors: gray, white, blue and pink. The most affordable and practical of them is Meizu M2 Note 16Gb Gray. Reviews highlight its availability (lower cost than in other cases) and resistance to the appearance of possible pollution and damage (with this color of the case they are not very noticeable). The blue color of the case is aimed at a youth audience, which in this way will certainly want to express its individuality. Well, white and pink options are perfect for the weak half of humanity.

CPU

One of the best mid-range processor solutions is equipped with the Meizu M2 Note. Reviews give it a fairly high level of performance. All this is true for MT6753. This is an 8-core chip with support for 64-bit computing. All its modules are built on the basis of architecture codenamed "Cortex A53." This silicon crystal is manufactured according to the standards of the 28-nm process technology. Its highest frequency can reach 1.3 GHz. The strengths of this processor solution are multi-threaded tasks. But even with not entirely optimized software, this CPU feels great. Therefore, the newfound owner of this device certainly does not have to worry about a lack of performance in the foreseeable future.





Video accelerator

Another strong point is the graphics accelerator in Meizu M2 Note. The reviews of the owners of this device highlight its decent level of performance. More specifically, the gadget is based on the Mali-T720MP3 video accelerator. He does not have any special problems with launching even the most demanding software. At the same time, the image on the display is displayed in 1080p format and it is certainly impossible to distinguish individual pixels with the ordinary eye.

Memory

The impressive capacity of the built-in drive in the basic configuration of Meizu M2 Note is 16Gb. User reviews indicate that part of this disk space is occupied by system software - about 3 GB. The rest of the space the user can use to store personal information or to install application software. You can also increase the amount of internal memory by installing an external drive (its maximum capacity can reach 128 GB) in the smartphone Meizu M2 Note 16 GB. Reviews in this case highlight one important feature of this device - a second SIM card slot is used to install an external drive. Therefore, you have to make a choice: either two operators, or increased disk space with one SIM card. There is also a more advanced version of this device already with 32 GB on board. But its cost is much higher. And, as experience shows, the basic 16 GB for most users will be clearly enough for comfortable work. The RAM in this device is 2 GB. Smartphone cameras

A very good main camera is equipped with a Meizu M2 Note phone . Reviews highlight the impeccable quality of photos and videos obtained with its use. She has a 13 megapixel sensor. There is also autofocus technology and, as previously noted, single backlight. The viewing angle of the main camera is 300 degrees. All this in total allows you to get very very high quality photos. Battery: its capacity and capabilities

The Meizu M2 Note phone boasts good autonomy. Reviews indicate 2 days of reliable operation of the device on one charge with an average load. For a device with a 5.5-inch display and an 8-core CPU, this is an excellent indicator. It should be noted right away that the battery in this device is non-removable. And the case is not collapsible. So in the event of a battery breakdown, you certainly cannot do without a visit to the service center. But, on the other hand, the capacity of the complete battery is 3100 mAh, and it is manufactured by Sony Corporation. Therefore, it is difficult to believe in possible problems associated with its operation. The battery life will be reduced to 12-14 hours if you run a demanding and resource-intensive toy on the gadget. Well, in the highest saving mode, you can count on 3 days of work on one charge of the built-in battery.
