Karaganda hotels: addresses, description, reviews

Coal, found in virgin land in 1833, became the reason for the emergence of a large industrial center on this site. The coal mines, built by the Russians, and then the French and British, concentrated a large amount of labor around them. Today, there is a beautiful modern city of Karaganda.

From the first settlements to a powerful industrial center

To work in the mines, residents of the nearest villages were involved. As the extraction of workers began to increase, there was a shortage. This problem was solved by exiled convicts who worked in the faces for free.



Coal mining, activated in the 30s of the XX century, led to the formation of settlements from adobe huts that sheltered dispossessed citizens and their families. The houses settled down, collapsed, new ones grew in their place. Overgrown settlements began to form in villages. In 1931, a mining village, with the light hand of those in power, acquired the status of a working village.

The settlement continued to grow, build, and develop its infrastructure, in connection with which it was upgraded and received a new status - the city of Karaganda.

During the war with Germany, the city became poor in thousands of young, healthy, energetic people. The reduction in the number of engineering workers led to the merger of 66 enterprises into the Karagandaugol production association.

The time of the end of the 20th century was difficult for Karaganda. The "Third Country Stoker" hibernated in anticipation of its finest hour. However, this does not in any way affect the cultural development of the city.

Hotels - decoration of Karaganda

Monuments, palaces of culture, rest houses, stadiums and theaters, sports palaces - all this functions, raising the cultural level of the city residents. Theaters and museums attract tourists who check into Karaganda hotels and visit the cultural centers of the city. Karaganda has something to surprise you!
