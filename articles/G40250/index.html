TRC "Zanevsky Cascade" on Ladoga - a vacation spot for the whole family

In the recent past, the most popular trading floors in big cities were markets. It was there, in one place, it was possible to purchase a variety of basic necessities, products, clothes, shoes and much more. The assortment of goods was huge and depended on the size of the market and its focus. An alternative to the market was freestanding shops and supermarkets, which were mainly narrowly focused: bookstores, grocery, clothing, footwear, flower, haberdashery.





In recent years, one of the most popular and sought-after places among the population has become shopping centers, which united on its territory numerous shops and a convenient space for a relaxing shopping and leisure. The Zanevsky Cascade on Ladoga was opened 10 years ago, in 2003. During this time, the complex underwent numerous changes both in layout and in content. The Zanevsky Cascade on Ladozhskaya is a typical shopping and entertainment center of its kind. A huge space of space is given for various stores where you can purchase almost all categories of goods: from jewelry and household appliances to children's goods and products. However, not only a large selection of shops attracts many visitors to the Zanevsky Cascade on Ladoga. The cinema of the famous Kronverk-Cinema network, located on the top floor of the shopping center, awaits visitors from the beginning of the complex until late at night. The latest movies and animations invite visitors to relax after a shopping trip and relax in a relaxed atmosphere. Slot machines located near the cinema will help you have fun while waiting for your session.





Zanevsky Cascade on Ladozhskaya offers visitors a wide selection of fast food restaurants where you can have a bite to eat and gain strength to continue shopping. The choice of dishes and the variety of cuisines will satisfy any request. The assortment of restaurants with Japanese cuisine, sandwiches, burgers, soups and pancakes.

"Zanevsky Cascade" on Ladoga for many residents of nearby houses has become a great place to spend a weekend with the whole family. Convenient, comfortable, bright space allows leisurely shopping in a relaxed atmosphere. There is no need to spend time and energy on trips to various shops in search of necessary goods - now everything can be purchased in one place.





A wide selection of stores of various categories of goods is the main advantage of the Zanevsky Cascade shopping center on Ladoga. Electronics and household appliances stores, a book club and a grocery store, casual and luxury clothes of various brands and brands, haberdashery and furniture stores, children's goods, accessories, jewelry and fur products - all this can be found and selected here. Salons of mobile communications and household services will allow visitors to the mall to resolve the issue of payment of payments, utilities , repair shoes or return dry cleaning. Visitors can cash money at numerous ATMs, among which there are all the main banks of the city.
