"Express" (boarding house), Schastlivtsevo: reviews of tourists and photos

Pension "Express" (Arabatskaya arrow) is located in the Genic district of Kherson region of Ukraine. This is a very popular holiday destination, especially for children. The resort's resort area is located two kilometers from the village of Schastlivtsevo. The town of Genichesk is 20 km away. This guesthouse is equipped according to the standards of modern leisure requirements. There is a pool and animation. The large territory remaining from Soviet times is completely rebuilt and ennobled. By international standards, this is the first line from the sea. Not far from the boarding house are a salt lake and a hot radon spring. You can use them for free.



Holidays in Schastlivtsevo

On the spit, the Arabat Spit is a small, but very famous resort since the days of the USSR. This is the village of Schastlivtsevo. Since it is located in the center of the spit, it was even called the golden middle of the Arabat arrow. Territory and infrastructure. Services

"Express" - a large boarding house. Its territory is almost eight hectares. There is a beautiful park. There are many trees and flowers, including rare ones. Vacationers in their reviews praise the grooming and cleanliness. There is a lake where swans swim, a mini-zoo for children. The main building, where the administration and reception is located, has a first-aid post, as well as a billiard room. There are many playgrounds for children with different types of trampolines, a pool, kiosks where you can buy not only souvenirs and accessories for a beach holiday, but also vegetables and fruits. In the guesthouse you can sign up for excursions, visit the spa, where there is a good sauna and massage treatments, as well as get a haircut, make a hairstyle and manicure. The boarding house has its own paid parking. In high season animators work. In the evenings, discos for children are organized. On the territory it catches a good wi-fi. To go to the sea at most about five minutes. It depends on where the case is located. Some rooms are twenty meters from the sea. Free pick-up from Genichesk is available.
