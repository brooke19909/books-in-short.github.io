Sound

Certain questions arise about the organization of the sound subsystem in Sony KDL-43WE755. Reviews of some owners indicate an insufficient level of power of its sound, which is limited to only 10 watts. The manufacturer included 2 speakers in it. The nominal power value of each of them is 5 watts. Most competing solutions are capable of delivering sound at the level of 16-20 watts. Therefore, with such a comparison, the hero of this article loses slightly to his direct competitors.

A unique way to improve the quality of the speaker system is Dolby Digital technology. It is activated in the software options. Its essence boils down to the fact that the speakers go into the volumetric mode of the sound signal, and this significantly improves its quality.

It is also possible to connect external speakers to this multimedia center. And for these purposes, you can use both the 3.5 mm jack and the optical SPDIF port. But support for the technology of wireless connection to external stereo systems using Bluetooth in this case is not provided for the reason that there is no corresponding transmitter.
