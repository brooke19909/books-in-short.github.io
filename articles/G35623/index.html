Feature LG P705 Optimus L7

Smartphone LG P705, the characteristics of which will be considered in the article, appeared on sale five years ago (2012). At that time, he could safely claim the title of flagship. The developers of the company made a lot of efforts in order to release a unique gadget, which was predicted to become a bestseller in the mobile device market. And indeed, at the start of sales there was a stir. In the first week alone, about 5 thousand units were sold. This model interested those buyers who would like to purchase a high-quality and functional smartphone at an affordable price.



A few words about design

Before considering the characteristics of the LG Optimus P705, it is necessary to briefly highlight the features of the external design. First of all, users paid attention to the sizes. If there are no features in the length and width (125.5 √ó 67 mm) of the case, then the thickness of the device caused a lot of applause. In 2012, thin gadgets were rare. LG introduced the buyer a smartphone, whose thickness was 8.8 mm.

It should be noted that the phone looks stylish in appearance. The case itself is made of plastic. The back cover has a matte surface, which significantly reduced the level of soiling. Thanks to the special relief pattern, the panel is practically not scratched, and the gadget itself does not slip in the hand. The manufacturer used a plastic edging as a side frame. The main plate is decorated with a metal plate.

There is no great variety in colors. The buyer is presented only with classic options - white and black.



LG P705 Phone: Screen and Camera Features

Of course, the main feature of this model is a 4.3-inch screen. Thanks to a good resolution (800 √ó 480 px) and high-quality IPS-matrix, LG Optimus L7 has become a favorite. The developers have provided color rendering settings. The user can choose a natural, calm or rich range. This phone supports up to seven touches made simultaneously. The sensor is quite sensitive. The brightness range is enough for comfortable work on the street in any weather. However, the viewing angles can hardly be called wide, since when tilted, the contrast decreases markedly.

In the LG P705, the main camera feature also impressed customers. 5-megapixel sensor allows you to take high-quality pictures. Auto focus works at high speed. Complemented by the characteristics and flash. The level of detail is pretty good, so even moving objects appear crisp in the photo. The developers have provided all the standard options.

But a lot of laudatory odes were not addressed to the front camera. It is based on a 1.3 megapixel sensor, which is only suitable for video communication.



LG P705: performance feature

What, if not the hardware "stuffing", best describes the gadget? Given that the model was released five years ago, its equipment was quite impressive. To ensure the health of the phone, the developers installed the Snapdragon processor, model MSM7227A. It is based on one computing module, which is capable of delivering power of 1000 MHz. Paired with the main installed Adreno 200 graphics card.

These parameters are also complemented by a 512 MB RAM. This "filling" allows you to quickly work smartphone LG P705. The phone feature also includes the size of the internal memory. There is nothing impressive about this parameter. The user is provided with four gigabytes of storage, which can be increased up to 32 GB by installing an external drive.

The Korean device is running Android 4 (ICS). On top of the platform is the proprietary Optimus 3.0 interface. Its indisputable advantage is that the user chooses which applications to install.

The developers also provided all the necessary communications. The device implements: navigation module, Bluetooth, USB and Wi-Fi.

Autonomy

The indisputable advantage of the LG P705 is the characteristic of the battery. The device has a battery of 1700 milliamps per hour. The menu has a special option that allows you to efficiently consume battery power. In order to restore the battery life, it will take about 2.5 hours if you use a charger.

Battery life:

  • when watching a video - about 4 hours;
  • in audio player mode - up to 27 hours;
  • work on the Internet - 7-10 hours




Conclusion

Summing up, I want to note that in the smartphone the balance between price and quality is optimally maintained. Stylish design and reasonable price (at the start of sales about 14 thousand rubles) made this model one of the most popular. Its main advantages are high-quality screen, autonomy, narrow body. There are no critical flaws in the phone. The only thing that can be attributed to the weaknesses is the small size of the built-in memory and the low resolution for recording videos.
