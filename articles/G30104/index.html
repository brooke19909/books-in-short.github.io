The rest that Hurghada offers: "Mamluk" - a hotel where there is no time to be bored!

Magnificent Hurghada ... "Mamluk" - a hotel located directly on the shores of the Red Sea. The city center is 20 km, the airport is 15 km. The complex was recently opened (in 2007) and has 530 rooms (it can accommodate 1,050 guests). Near the hotel there is a water park "Titanic", and a little further - a water park "Jungle". Crossing the road, you can walk around the Cleopatra Bazaar. In 10 minutes by taxi you can get to Hurghada. The cost of the trip is $ 5.

Egypt, Hurghada. Mamluk: accommodation

The rooms are spacious and comfortable and up to standard. Each is equipped with air conditioning, shower and safe, TV (with several Russian channels), a spacious balcony or a beautiful terrace overlooking the Red Sea. Cleaning and linen changes are provided daily. The hotel has 432 standard rooms (41 sq. M each), 90 family rooms (52 sq. M) and 6 deluxe rooms (66 sq. M.). The standard has a double bed, if necessary, put a children's folding. Family rooms have a living room and a bedroom. Pets are not allowed in the rooms.

Territory

The territory is combined with the neighboring hotel - Garden. Vacationers have at their disposal 2 outdoor pools, one of which is heated and has a special children's section, a playground, dry cleaning, water slides, a hairdresser, a beauty salon, spa, car rental, currency exchange, a conference room, and an Internet cafe. In addition, you can visit a diving club, ride horses, play golf, surf, ride a banana or a pedalo. Vacationers can have a great time in one of the three restaurants (Shahrazad - European cuisine, Asia - Asian cuisine and Tex Mex - Mexican cuisine). There are 4 bars, 1 hookah bar and 1 pub on the territory.

Nutrition

Restaurants and bars are open around the clock using the ultra all-inclusive system. Alcohol is not served only from 6 to 10 in the morning. The menu is full of variety: meat, fish, seafood, an abundance of fruit, ice cream, freshly squeezed juices. Each of the hotel's three restaurants has children's and dietary tables. Guests of the Mamluk complex can dine in the restaurants of the neighboring hotel for free, which makes the rest even more comfortable.

Animation

The animation team consists of people from different countries, including Russia. In the afternoon they occupy vacationers with aerobics, horse riding, aqua aerobics, a game of billiards and other entertainments. In the evening, discos, foam parties, fire shows and entertainment programs are held. Animators can offer vacationers to go on one of the tours in Egypt, which are often organized from the Hurghada region. Mamluk is a venue for frequent volleyball, beach soccer, darts, table tennis and mini-golf competitions. Anyone can take part in them.

Hurghada, Mamluk. Children's leisure

Children of all ages will find something to do at the hotel.
