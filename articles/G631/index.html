Interest-free loan to an employee: registration procedure, tax consequences, postings

The practice of obtaining the status of lender and borrower by the employer and employee is currently quite common. This is beneficial because it helps to save interest on the individual. For the enterprise, this creates a significant plus, attracting the best personnel, and becomes one of the factors for increasing competitiveness. True, not everything is so simple. You need to know the rules for issuing money on credit, as well as the correct execution of the process.

Relevance of the issue

Many business leaders sooner or later think about whether it is possible to issue an interest-free loan to an employee. The fact is that a situation in which money suddenly became necessary can happen in the life of any person. The classic option is to contact a credit office or ask for help at a pawnshop. They are available to many, but disadvantageous due to the high interest rate. For a person, the option of receiving money without overpayments at the time of return is much more pleasant and convenient. The employer has the right to meet the employer and give him the desired amount. Typically, the decision does not require much time, is not accompanied by the calculation of interest, and the very conditions for providing money are milder than in the case of working with banks.

In order for an interest-free loan to an organization employee not to end with problems for one of the parties or both, it is necessary to declare the work as an agreement in which all details are fixed. The contract is the basis for lending money. It stipulates the age. You must understand that this format of the loan is completely legal, but only after signing by all interested parties becomes legally significant.

Features

In order for the interest-free loan from the founder to be lawful and correct, an agreement must be made in writing. Current legislation clearly stipulates the need to draw up an agreement on paper. If a person asks the company for money in order to spend it on acquiring a home, he can receive the sums received from the total mass of net profit. True, taxes must first be paid. By the way, in our country most often a loan of this format is offered to employees of the Ministry of Emergencies. It is both an encouraging measure and a means of support. Some enterprises interest-free loan money to employees in order to keep them in service for as long as possible. This is practiced only when the frame is really valuable.

About terminology

Is the type of issue of a loan to the employee from the cash desk under consideration, and what is generally hidden under the concept to which the material is dedicated? To deal with this, you should turn to the wording of laws governing our daily lives. The legislation sets an interest-free loan as transferring a certain financial mass to a person to achieve various goals. In this case, the employee does not have to pay interest for using the money entrusted to him. The employer has the right to set a small percentage, but it cannot be more substantial than the official inflation rate in the state.

An interest-free loan from the founder is not always in the form of money. You can transfer property, if such is in the possession of the enterprise. The law stipulates the maximum amount that an employee is given: 50 minimum wages or less. The money received in this way cannot be spent on doing business. Refunds are allowed both in parts and the entire amount at once. The legislation stipulates the possibility of settlements on debts earlier than this is established by the original agreement. The recipient is required to pay tax on the amount that he was given. The company does not need to pay taxes on the money that it receives back from the borrower. This is due to the fact that there is no interest that would provide the company with a revenue component.

Tax nuances

The current legislation requires a written agreement. The requirements of normative acts are such that when formulating a contract, it is necessary in detail and clearly, without using ambiguous wording, to fix the absence of interest in favor of the enterprise. Only in this case, the company will not have to pay taxes after giving the person a loan.

The personal income tax and the interest-free loan to the employee are closely related, since the second automatically entails the obligation to pay the first. Money may be spent on the acquisition of real estate. In this case, an individual has the right to apply to the Federal Tax Service or to the accounting department of the enterprise in which it is employed in order to draw up a property deduction. Article 809 of the Code governing civil relations within our country gives the right to provide employees with commodity or cash loans - at the discretion of those entering into an agreement. Section 812 of the same code of laws establishes the possibility of challenging an agreement. This is allowed only in the case when a person can provide evidence that he did not receive the money stipulated by the contract. In the presence of phenomena proving this fact, we can talk about the loss of the contract validity.

Step by step and time limit

In order for everything to be legal and legal, it is necessary to draw up a fixed interest-free loan agreement. In general, the entire process of providing a person with money by agreement can be divided into several successive steps. First, a person draws up an application, sends it to the name of the owner of the company. In the text of the document, a person asks for a certain amount, specifying the reasons for which he needs money. It is very important to indicate exactly how much money a person needs. The head considers the application and makes a decision on it. If it turns out to be positive, they call the employee for a meeting in order to agree on the provisions and conditions for concluding an agreement. The responsible person prepares the necessary documentation that accompanies the process of lending money.

When drafting an agreement, it is necessary to record in the document personal information about the person asking for money, and the general data of the enterprise providing the requested. The manager or other employee who is entrusted with such responsibility signs an agreement, fixes the fact of signing the enterprise with a seal. This is a relatively simple procedure, especially when management has nothing against giving money to hired people.

Why do we need money?

In order for the interest-free loan to the employee to be drawn up correctly, you need to take into account the goals for which the money is intended. All such persons must indicate in a statement, which asks to provide finance on credit. Most often in practice, people ask for money to buy a house or a car. You can ask the company for a loan in order to have funds for treating a rare disease or getting an education at a higher educational institution. Rules for registration
