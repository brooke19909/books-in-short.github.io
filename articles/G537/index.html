Minsk hotels: overview, description, reviews

The capital of the Republic of Belarus, Minsk, is a large and beautiful modern city worthy of the attention of travelers. Many business meetings, scientific symposia, and sports competitions are held here. The hotels of Minsk offer all their guests comfortable and convenient accommodation.

Hotel "Belarus"

Hotel "Belarus" is a symbol of the city of Minsk. Its building has an original architectural solution and is the most recognizable in the capital of the republic. The hotel has a category of 3 stars. The hotel has its own restaurant with panoramic city views. The restaurant features Belarusian and European cuisine. Parking space is provided free of charge. Pool and fitness center services are charged extra. The hotel "Belarus" provides conditions for accommodating people with disabilities. This is one of the best hotels in Minsk in the city center. Of the attractions within a five-minute walk from the hotel is the Church of St. Mary Magdalene and the Svisloch River, and on foot you can walk to the Sports Palace and Victory Park. The hotel offers the following accommodation options for guests of the capital of Belarus :

  • single standard room;
  • standard double room with one double or two single beds;
  • double superior room with a large double bed;
  • apartments;
  • family apartment;
  • suite.

Free Wi-Fi access is available. Pets allowed.

Hotel Minsk

Hotel "Minsk" has a category of 4 stars. It is located in the historical and cultural central part of Minsk. Not far from the hotel are the Belarusian National Art Museum and the Dynamo Stadium. And fans of theatrical art can visit the nearby Gorky National Academic Drama Theater in the evening. In 10 minutes you will reach the Central Railway Station of the Belarusian capital. Meals are organized in two elegant restaurants. In the morning, the hotel serves a buffet breakfast. Each room has its own original design. You can park your car in free parking. Accessible facilities are available for people with disabilities. An ATM is installed on site, a currency exchange service is also available. To accommodate guests are offered rooms of several categories:

  • single;
  • double with two single beds;
  • double with one double bed;
  • double superior;
  • suite;
  • Superior Suite
  • apartments.

All rooms have free Wi-Fi access. By the quantity and quality of the services provided, the Minsk Hotel is one of the best hotels in Minsk.

Only 300 meters you need to go to the National Art Museum of the Republic of Belarus and the Yanka Kupala Theater, 500 meters to the Belarusian State Circus and 0.7 kilometers to Gorky Park. Minsk hotels: Renaissance Minsk Hotel

Renaissance Minsk Hotel is a magnificent modern architectural ensemble with chic panoramic windows. The city center is just 250 meters away. After a little more than a kilometer, guests can admire the church of St. Simeon and St. Helena, as well as Pishchalovo Castle. Free parking space, access to wireless Internet. Pets are allowed at no extra charge. An on-site ATM machine and currency exchange service are also available. Tourists can use the services of the fitness center, indoor pool, sauna, and wellness spa. Accommodation in rooms of several categories is offered:

  • double standard;
  • double club;
  • Junior Suite
  • club deluxe;
  • corner suite.

Buffet breakfast is included in the price. Among the 5-star Minsk hotels, the Renaissance Minsk Hotel is considered one of the most attractive options.

Hotel Comfort-House

If you want to settle in silence, away from city noise, the Comfort-House mini-hotel offers its services. This is one of the hotels in Minsk with breakfast included in the price. Located Comfort-House seven kilometers from the central part of the city. Each room has an individual design solution. Guests can cook their own meals in the equipped kitchen. Breakfast includes homemade national dishes. The hotel is surrounded by greenery, on its territory guests can use the sun terrace, gazebos, take a walk in the garden. There is a sauna and pool. In winter, skiing is organized for you. Parking is free. The following numbers are offered:

  • double standard;
  • Junior Suite
  • Superior Suite
  • luxury comfort;
  • Suite with spa bath.

Free Wi-Fi access is available.

Hotel "40 Years of Victory"

If you are interested in inexpensive hotels in the city center of Minsk, pay attention to the 40 Years of Victory hotel. A kilometer meters away are the Belarusian State Circus and Victory Square. Free parking is available on site. The guests are offered the following rooms:

  • single superior;
  • double with shared bathroom;
  • double standard;
  • luxury room;
  • Superior Suite;
  • family.

There is free Wi-Fi.

Yubileiny Hotel

This hotel is also located in the central part of the capital of Belarus. In just 10 minutes on foot you will reach the Minsk Cathedral. Yubileiny is one of those hotels in Minsk that has a casino and a bowling alley. There is a restaurant and two bars. Parking space is paid extra. Guests are accommodated in the rooms of the following categories:

  • single;
  • double with two separate beds;
  • business double with one bed;
  • suite;
  • Executive Suite.

Buffet breakfast is included in the room rate. Free WIFI is provided.

Hotel Sputnik

Among the inexpensive hotels in Minsk, one can also note the Sputnik hotel. It offers free parking, a sauna and a billiard room. There is a restaurant in which European cuisine is presented. The large panoramic windows of the restaurant offer beautiful views of the city. The following categories of rooms are presented for accommodation:

  • single;
  • double with two beds;
  • double with one bed;
  • double superior;
  • family;
  • Suite

Breakfast is included. If desired, you can rent a room without food. Free Wi-Fi is available.

Other Minsk hotels

There are many more interesting accommodation options in Minsk hotels. An overview of some of them is given below.

  • Garni Hotel is a historical part of Minsk. Nearby are the Palace of the Republic and Freedom Square. There is a restaurant and parking. Free internet is available.
  • Among the hotels in Minsk in the city center, the Planet hotel stands out inexpensively. Nearby attractions include Sports Palace and Victory Park. There are many cafes and shops nearby.
  • Hotel "Slavyanskaya" has an interesting modern architecture, a few minutes walk from it is the cultural and sports complex "Minsk-Arena".
