Negative reviews

Quite a popular place to relax is the resort complex "Hilton" (UAE). Nevertheless, it is characterized by some negative points. Based on the reviews of the guests, the following can be distinguished:

  • upon arrival they require a rather impressive amount of deposit, but there is an opportunity to agree on its reduction;
  • for each spent day in the hotel an additional tourist fee is charged (the amount, although small, but this is an unpleasant surprise);
  • there are few seafood on the buffet menu;
  • in the dining room for drinks you need to pay an additional fee;
  • the beach is located in the lagoon, which is not blown by the wind, and therefore it is very hot and stuffy there;
  • if you arrive at the hotel late and are late for dinner, then you will not be fed;
  • a lot of jellyfish, starfish, crustaceans and other animals live in the sea (although for someone who likes to watch the underwater world, this may seem an advantage);
  • if you come to the Emirates during the period of fasting, then be prepared for the fact that you will not find alcohol not only in the hotel, but also in supermarkets;
  • the Russian-speaking receptionist does not very politely treat guests;
  • it is very inconvenient that in most cases cleaning is done in the afternoon;
  • overpriced in the hotel restaurants.
