Patrick Wilson - biography, interesting facts

Patrick Wilson is a famous theater and film actor. This smiling and quite popular person often flickers on the screens of movie theaters and TVs. Although he never became a star of the first magnitude, his filmography includes more than 10 successful projects awarded with various film awards.

The life path of the future actor began in 1973, in the small American town of Norfolk, Virginia. His parents were professionally involved in music. Patrick was able to graduate from the prestigious Shorecrest Preparatory School, after which he became a student at Carnegie Mellon University, located in Pittsburgh. Theatrical career

Since 1996, Wilson has been a theater actor. Together with small troupes, he takes part in his first productions, struggling in small roles. The gifted actor quickly achieved well-deserved success, and in 2001 he received his first theatrical award "TONY", and in 2002 the second award.
