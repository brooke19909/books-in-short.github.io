One of the best hotels in Tunisia - Solarus Golden Beach

Paradise holidays in Tunisia



Tunisia is an exotic country with centuries-old culture. Its ancient capital, Carthage, was one of the most influential cities of the East of ancient times. In many cities of Tunisia there are various historical attractions: fortresses and forts, palaces, temples and mosques, ancient monuments. Therefore, lovers of excursions will find here a lot of interesting and exciting. Fans of beach vacations will be able to enjoy plenty of relaxation on the white sandy beaches, swim in the azure waters of the Mediterranean Sea. At the service of vacationers there are many luxury hotels of the highest category. These include the Club Hotel Solarus Golden Beach. By the way, here, on the coast, the climate is typically Mediterranean, warm water and pleasant air temperature, without the sweltering heat. However, for those who still want to visit the famous Sahara desert, as well as see all the sights, it is recommended to come to Tunisia from May to June or from September to November. Indeed, in the south of the country in summer the temperature reaches 45 degrees.

Solarus Golden Beach Hotel 4 *



This club complex is one of the most hospitable hotels on the coast of Tunisia. It is located in one of the districts of the resort town of Monastir. Hotel Solarus Golden Beach is built in an oriental style, but the interiors of the rooms fully comply with European standards. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. For vacationers, all the conditions for a high-class vacation are provided: quality service, cleanliness, disciplined staff. Fans of delicious food can appreciate the exquisite dishes prepared by the cooks of Solarus Golden Beach restaurant for vacationers. On the territory of the complex you can find a lot of interesting and diverse entertainments for both children and adults. Young people will not regret that they chose this hotel as a place to stay. However, the hotel administration took care of the little vacationers most of all. Even a gentle entry into the sea testifies to this. Family rooms are equipped with special cots, even two-level for guests with two children. The Solarus Golden Beach's restaurant has a children's menu especially for the kids, and high chairs are provided at the table for their convenience. As for adult nutrition, here they feed simply excellently. The menu consists of local and foreign dishes. Everyone can find dishes to their liking.

Fun for kids

For small vacationers on the territory of the hotel, animators organize various entertainment events: animations, sports and game competitions, contests and quizzes, and bright show programs. Moreover, specialists with pedagogical education and psychologists are involved with children. There are games rooms and a mini club where children can sing and dance on an impromptu stage.



Entertainment for adults

For adults, there are many sports activities. Since the hotel is located in a green area, there are golf courses. There are football and volleyball courts, tennis courts, two swimming pools, one of which is indoor, a fitness club and a gym. And for those who want to get complete relaxation, there is a well-equipped spa. Here you can undergo thalassotherapy and high-quality massage, relax in the sauna or in the Turkish bath.

Conclusion

It seems that all of the above is worthy of choosing Solarus Golden Beach 4 * as the place for your vacation . Tunisia will welcome you with open arms and show what constitutes real oriental hospitality.
