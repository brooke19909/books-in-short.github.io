What is the decile ratio?

In the analysis of the effectiveness of public administration, the issue of the standard of living of the population is important. The country's authorities exercise control over its useful resources, subsoil content and gross product of people's activities. It is the state that organizes the distribution of wealth between citizens. Why are people rich in minerals in some countries

are dissatisfied with the standard of their life? And other states that do not have such resources do not know what popular indignation is. How to find a criterion for comparing distribution measures of material wealth?

What is the decile ratio?

For these purposes in sociology a number of tools are used. One of them is the decile coefficient of differentiation of incomes of the population. It is calculated by comparing income statistics for 10% of the richest population and income data for the same number of poor citizens. In science, it is believed that approaching the coefficient to 10 creates the conditions for social unrest in the country. The people will begin to express their indignation by the current government, and riots may occur.
