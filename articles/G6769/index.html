The beaches of Adler (reviews of them are presented in the article) are quite long. They occupy 16 kilometers of the coast. In the largest areas, the coastal strip reaches a width of 75 kilometers. Very well-groomed beaches are those located in the first two neighborhoods of Adler. They differ in spacious areas. Here you can find areas with small or large pebbles, as well as with sand.
