Hotel Delta Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Bodrum, Yalikavak): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

The long-awaited vacation for many people is a way to increase immunity, get exciting emotions, unforgettable memories and a charge of new strength for the coming year. Choosing Turkey, travelers combine a number of these desires with comfortable accommodation and a pleasant mild climate.

The title of the capital of the party life of the state is the city of Bodrum, which combines overnight secluded bays and picturesque mountain landscapes. It is here that the five-star Delta Beach Resort Hotel is located, which hospitably welcomes each guest, providing the opportunity to fully relax and enjoy pastime on the shores of the Aegean Sea.

Accommodation

The room fund of the Delta Beach Resort Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey, Yalikavak) consists of 228 rooms, among which the following categories are distinguished:

  • Standard.
  • Family
  • Promo

The standard class dormitory area is 31 square meters, while the family room is more spacious and has two bedrooms separated by a door - up to 44 squares. The latter - without a balcony, can be located on the ground floor. The interior of the rooms has a simple classic design. The range of light tones prevails, all shades of white.

The floors are covered with quality ceramic tiles with marble patterns. This adds chicness and gloss to the design. The walls are decorated with paintings. The illumination is sufficient, it is realized with the help of wall sconces, floor lamps and spotlights. French glazing allows the first rays of the sun to penetrate into the chambers, awakening sleeping guests with their warmth.

Delta Beach Resort Hotel 5 * receives positive reviews for the fact that it is always clean, at home comfortable and cozy. Cleaning up is a daily routine carried out by maids. For lovers of sleep, but for those who wish to wake up at the scheduled time, you can use the service "Wake-up call". Room service is available 24 hours a day.





Accommodations

The furniture set is standard: beds, bedside tables, countertops, chairs, coffee tables, armchairs and wardrobes. An individual split system allows you to maintain the required temperature. The presence of a TV with Russian-language channels will brighten up the evening stay in the apartment. The phone provides instant communication with the reception. A carefree stay is facilitated by a safe, which is free to use.

When checking in, the minibar is replenished with a number of soft drinks and drinking water. Further updates require additional fees. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

The balconies are equipped with a plastic table and chairs, which allows you to meet the sunrises and see off the sunsets with beautiful views of the surrounding landscapes, the endless sea and the starry sky.

The bathroom has an expensive finish. Contains a bath or shower, sink, mirror and hairdryer. The presence of snow-white bathrobes and disposable slippers contributes to the feeling of being at home. There is a complete set of personal hygiene products and cosmetic accessories.





Supply system

According to the concept followed by the Delta Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (you can see the photo in the article), a five-time meal is organized according to the buffet system. A range of international dishes is offered. Excellent chefs invest a piece of their soul in every culinary masterpiece. The capacity of the main restaurant is 400 people. There is an outdoor terrace. Delta Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Bodrum) reviews about food receive extremely laudatory. As tourists note, the food here is fresh and amazingly tasty.

It is possible to quench hunger in a la carte restaurants: Mezaluna - specializes in Italian dishes, and Guelt - a fish restaurant. They are open for visits in the evening, require prior reservation of the table and the execution of the dress code. Free admission to each of them is available only once a week.

The system of bars eliminates thirst due to their ubiquitous location. The lobby bar is open around the clock. The snack bar will delight you with its mouth-watering snacks, pastries and ice cream. In addition, there is a pool bar on the beach.

Freshly squeezed fresh juice, imported alcohol and Turkish coffee require an additional payment.





Beach

One of the highlights of this resort complex is a surprisingly rich and varied beach area. Here everyone will find a corner where it will be convenient and comfortable to take sun baths and find a dark tan body.

The beach is sandy, has a gentle entrance to the sea. Infrastructure includes locker rooms, showers, toilet and bar. The towel dispenser is free of charge. Numerous sun loungers with mattresses contribute to a pleasant stay. For lovers of shady spaces - umbrellas and awnings.

In addition, the coastal area is equipped with a pier and a pontoon, where there are comfortable tents, a bar and the ability to dive into the deep waters.

The range of entertainment is incredibly diverse. Trampoline complexes on the water will please not only kids, but also their parents. You can go water skiing, scooter or ring, go to the open sea, go snorkeling or diving. Volleyball field daily brings together friendly teams that are fighting for victory.





Aquazone

Since swimming is an important component of active and eventful relaxation, which allows you to improve your health and develop your body, restore important vital processes and simply rejuvenate, there are three unheated outdoor pools filled with fresh water on the territory. They are open for swimming until 19:00. The maximum area is 300 square meters. Indoor pool - heated, area - 100 squares. Children's pools allow babies to splash around freely, spending their energy under the constant supervision of their parents.

There are two bright water slopes that work depending on weather conditions. Skating with them diversifies the time spent in the fresh air, and gives a lot of positive emotions.

Leisure can be diversified by playing water polo or training in water aerobics. Every day, experienced instructors offer simple exercises to maintain beautiful muscle shape and tone.

Toddler Leisure

According to numerous guests, the leisure of the kids here is planned to the smallest detail, for which all parents are grateful, without exception. In addition to water activities, children are invited to visit the mini club, which is open during the day. Here, under the guidance of a qualified educator, you can play board games, collect puzzles or a constructor, and play numerous toys. Cheerful animators every second give kids a feast of the soul, conducting various sports competitions, competitions for ingenuity and dexterity. Children will remember the bright time spent at the Delta Beach Resort for a long time, because good memories last forever.

For outdoor games, there are playgrounds with swings, slides, labyrinths and sandboxes. In the evening, mini-discos are held. Babysitting services are available. Highchairs - in the public domain, located in the restaurant.





Pastime adults

The hotel also receives reviews of the positive plan for the organization of leisure activities for each of the tourists. In order to fill life with positive emotions, guests are invited to visit the Turkish bath or sauna. Relaxation of every cell of the body, removal from existing problems and restoration of health are what the spa is ready to present to its visitors. However, for lovers of physical activity it is proposed to visit the gym, equipped with all necessary and modern equipment. Tennis and table tennis, billiards, darts, bocce - something that you can have fun and do in your free time.

A visit to the amphitheater in the evening is a good tradition for every guest. Amazing shows, dance numbers and entertaining contests with the participation of the audience will give a lot of joy and fun to vacationers.
