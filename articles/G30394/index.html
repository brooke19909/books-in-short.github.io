Why is it worth buying an apartment in a new building.

     If you decide to buy an apartment in a new building, you should understand that you are becoming its first owner, so you get a completely clean pedigree of this housing. In addition, you can avoid the many legal problems that usually arise when buying an old home.
In addition, a great advantage in buying an apartment in new buildings near the metro Pionerskaya is that you yourself can choose any apartment on any floor, also with a good view from the window. You must admit that it is quite difficult to find an apartment that suits your requirements from old housing.
However, there is another big plus of buying housing in new buildings, which is the cost is much lower in contrast to old housing. As a rule, the developers themselves offer very profitable promotions at the time of the sale of apartments and if you bought an apartment in this way, then you managed to save a very decent amount of money that could be spent on the improvement of a new apartment.
Also, developers can offer you to create your own project for the layout of the premises, that is, in this version we have only load-bearing walls, but the internal partitions can be arranged according to your consideration, of course, you should consult with specialists and find the best option. You can make a large kitchen or bathroom, the future guest room can also become more spacious, in the old apartment there are simply no such opportunities to redo the layout. Construction agents can additionally offer you repair services from manufacturers, and you can also save on this, because all construction materials will be delivered at wholesale prices, that is, much cheaper. Buying a new apartment, many owners can not imagine what will be the design of their housing. And in this matter they will be able to help you, offering you the best designers who perfectly cope with such tasks.
Before making a final decision, buying an apartment should find out at what stage of construction this new building is located. In such cases, it is best to visit the new building on your own and at the same time evaluate how to blow out the work. Be sure to pay attention to important functioning systems, which include sewage, water or heating. All this should be new, and function without any problems. To all this, we can summarize that buying an apartment in new buildings is a very profitable option having a lot of advantages in comparison, if you take a house with a long history.
