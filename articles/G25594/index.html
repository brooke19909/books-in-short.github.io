Hotels in Nizhnekamsk: addresses and reviews

Nizhnekamsk is a city in the Republic of Tatarstan, located in the bend of the Kama River. This is an experimental locality. It was formed as a result of working out new methods of city planning. Today it is an industrial cultural and sports center of the republic. Three times he received the title of the most comfortable city in Russia. If you are planning a visit to Nizhnekamsk, the hotels will provide you with comfortable living conditions and a wide range of quality services.
