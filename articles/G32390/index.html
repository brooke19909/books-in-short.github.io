White movement: causes of defeat in the Civil War

Instead of joining



Oddly enough, but the historical division into "red", "white" and "green" during the war years was practically absent. What is the reason for this? At a time when a terrible discord occurs, it is difficult for a person to unambiguously join one of the parties. For one "ideological" follower of a monarchy or revolution, there are a hundred people "waiting". And this is normal, since such a situation has existed at all times and under any authority.

Easily changed the side of the conflict, not only loners, but even entire military units! Moreover, many moved back and forth several times during the war.

In addition, it is not worth considering that the entire territory of the former Russian Empire suddenly turned into the burning crucible of the revolution: initially the red and white were small islands surrounded by whole seas of an absolutely inert peasant mass.
