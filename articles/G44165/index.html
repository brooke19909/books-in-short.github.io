Hostels in Novy Urengoy: overview and photos

Reception is open 24 hours a day. The rooms have heating. An elevator is available on site. In addition, there are shops next to it where you can buy everything you need. Equipped parking, reservation is not needed.

In the reviews they write that the settlement is fast, you can arrive before midnight. The location is convenient. The rooms are clean.
