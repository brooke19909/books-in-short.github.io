Cherkessk: attractions, history

Karachay-Cherkessia is famous for its beautiful mountains, on which dense deciduous forests grow. In addition, the republic will delight tourists with mirror-free lakes and rivers, nature reserves. The capital in this region is Cherkessk, the sights of which are also quite diverse.

Karachay-Cherkess Reserve

This place will undoubtedly appeal to lovers of historical, cultural and architectural monuments in combination with the majestic nature. In the Karachay-Cherkess nature reserve, you can look at Mount Shoan, on which the building of the Shoanin temple is built . The style of this church is East Byzantine. In addition, on the Kalezhskaya mountain, located near the city itself, you can see the remains of the Khumarinsky settlement. When the kingdom of Alan was still in this territory, this place was occupied by the fortified city of Shchimar. Today, from the ancient buildings there is only a wall that surrounds the twelve turrets.





Sights of the city of Cherkessk are quite diverse. So, you can visit the exhibitions of the museum of the defenders of the Caucasus passes, which blocked the path (towards the Black Sea) of the fascist invaders during the Second World War. There is also a mass grave. The building of the Krasnogorsk watchtower served as a guard post, which was located next to the Nikolaev fortification. These events date back to when the region was engulfed in the Caucasian War. Until now, the Sentin Church is located in the reserve, the frescoes on the walls of which are preserved in excellent condition.

Cultural institutions of the city

Cherkessk, the sights of which we are considering, is known for the fact that many representatives of different nationalities live in the city. This was reflected in the cultural life of the region.

If you want to usefully spend your leisure time in the city, visit the performances of the Russian Drama and Comedy Theater or the Circassian Drama Theater. M. Akova. You can listen to classical music in the building of the State Philharmonic.





If you are interested in the history of the republic, you need to look at the exposition of the Karachay-Cherkess Museum of Local Lore. The construction in which it is located was built quite originally and is an excellent example of architectural trends at the beginning of the last century. The Art Gallery of Cherkessk is one of the first buildings in the city.

Temples of Cherkessk

In the city you can also go to the liturgy, which is held in the church of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker, the Cathedral of St. George and many others. If you come to Cherkessk, the sights of the region will surprise you with their historical value. For example, the building of the wooden church of the Intercession of the Holy Virgin belongs to the first half of the 18th century. It was built on the coast of the Don, but it was not once moved until it was decided to put it in Cherkessk itself.





Nature reserves

Not far from the city you can see the transparent expanse of Batalpashinsky lakes, which became famous as the Circassian Sea. In addition, you will be surprised at the antiquities of the Ust-Dzhegutsky barrows. It was in them that researchers found many ornaments made of bronze, as well as ceramics with patterns characteristic of the third and second millennium BC.

Historical monuments and pristine nature - all these are the sights of Cherkessk. You can take a souvenir photo at one of the republic‚Äôs resorts that offer tourists mountain climbing, skiing or climbing one of the high cliffs. If you are a lover of mountain hikes, you will love the ridges and dense forests where you can pick mushrooms, berries and herbs. In addition, a trip to the lakes and glaciers of the Sophia Range will leave an unforgettable experience. Be sure to visit the places of the Khutyi, Alibek and Sofrudzhin waterfalls, and also go to the Amanauz gorge, Turyev lake and the Alibek glacier. You will have a wonderful rest in nature, breathing in clean mountain air or sipping mineral water, which is so good for your health.





Souvenirs

If you bring something from the trip, you will remember longer how hospitable Cherkessk was. Sightseeing is not all that the city is famous for. Here you must buy a knitted shawl or sweater, as well as a fur figure or slippers. An exotic souvenir will be a lamb or wolf skin, as well as a silver horn, a dagger or an item of metal utensils decorated with engraving. In Cherkessk you can buy beautiful jewelry with precious stones.

Minerals and rocks bought in one of the souvenir shops will remind of the majestic nature of the region. All this will not allow you to immediately forget about the fabulous beauty of the Karachay-Cherkess Republic upon arrival home.
