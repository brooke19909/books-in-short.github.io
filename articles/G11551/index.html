Skiing in Finland. Popular resorts

Every year, thousands of tourists from all over the world come to Finland for the beautiful northern nature, the opportunity to go fishing in one of the many lakes, a rich tourist program and, of course, skiing. Local routes can not boast of dizzying slopes and sudden changes. However, they are maintained in excellent condition and will be interesting not only to novice athletes. In this article you will learn about what Finland can offer winter tours. Alpine skiing, spending the night in a hut in the middle of a winter forest, a Finnish sauna, northern lights and New Year's Eve are just a few of the reasons that attract vacationers to this amazing country.



Levy

This ski resort is located in the west of Lapland and has been repeatedly recognized as the best resort in the country. Levi is more like an alpine ski center than a traditional Finnish base. Thanks to its compact development, developed infrastructure and Finland‚Äôs only gondola lift , thousands of tourists come here every year. Alpine skiing in Finland is mainly a sport for beginners and beginner athletes, however in Levi you will find 28 tracks of increased difficulty. Here, it will be interesting for snowboarders to visit a special park with a halfpipe, and for lovers of flat skis - to ride along their own tracks. Beginners have the opportunity to attend a school for beginner skiers and learn the basics of skiing. Also here you will find Russian-speaking instructors who will help adults and children to get comfortable on the local slopes.



Skiing in Finland. Resort Luosto

Tourists come here in order to visit one of the most popular ski centers in the country. Here they can ride on tracks of varying degrees of difficulty, as well as enjoy the views of the nature of Pyhyatunturi Park. Each resort descent is equipped with the latest technology, all necessary safety measures are taken into account. Skiing in Finland can be called your favorite national sport. It is not surprising that local people enjoy riding wherever weather and conditions permit. In Luosto you will not only find seven slopes and lighted trails, but also ride a dog or reindeer. If desired, tourists can go on an excursion to the residence of Santa Claus or visit the famous amethyst mine.



Himos: skiing in Finland, reviews of tourists

Vacationers claim that this resort is a great place not only for winter but also for summer holidays. Here you can see how athletes take part in one of the many international competitions in winter sports, as well as skiing or snowboarding. The resort offers 17 slopes, each of which is equipped with snow cannons and illuminated at night. Everyone can visit the ski school and take some lessons from an experienced instructor. Vacationers say that not only an active lifestyle, but also entertainment at the resort helps them relax. For example, there are nightclubs, restaurants, as well as a public pool with various relaxing treatments.

Vuokatti

This ski resort is known for the fact that it often hosts freestyle competitions. Vuokatti also gained popularity thanks to a specially equipped tunnel for snowboarding. The optimum temperature in it is maintained not only in winter, but also in summer. Couples with children come here because they can find a lot of entertainment here. In addition to practicing traditional sports, they can ride dog sledding, deer, snowmobiles or snowmobiles. Parents claim that they are attracted here by the measured pace of the resort, an abundance of entertainment for children, as well as many simple sites for skiing.



Tahko

The famous center of winter sports is located near the town of Nilsia. The European Snowboard Championships are regularly held here, as well as the Nordic ski championships. On 20 slopes, there are about 17 illuminated high-altitude trails for skiing, many trails for children, as well as a park for snowboarders. Cross-country skiing enthusiasts will be delighted by an extensive network of 64-km trails. For children there is a free ski lift, children's descent and a mountain for sledding.

Skiing in Finland in winter is not the only activity of vacationers. Tourists who are tired of outdoor activities will be able to entertain themselves with ice fishing, snowshoeing or visiting a car driving school in winter.

Fans of outdoor activities claim that Finland is one of the most beautiful places for winter holidays . Alpine skiing, resorts, sports centers and entertainment complexes allow you to have a great vacation and replenish your piggy bank of impressions until the next season.
