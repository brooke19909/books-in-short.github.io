Unforgettable holidays in Anapa: the sun, the sea and Golden Sands (Vityazevo)

Golden Sands (Vityazevo) is a new modern three-story hotel located in one of the resort villages of Anapa. Nearby is a pedestrian zone, a huge advantage is the proximity to the sea and the sandy beach, which is only five minutes walk. The successful location of the resort complex provides stunning views of the sea landscape on one side of the hotel and the beautiful and cozy courtyard with a swimming pool on the other.





A comfortable stay on the Black Sea

There is everything that constitutes a good vacation, everything for which people go to the sea: hotels located close to comfortable beaches, all sorts of joys of resort life, including attractions, open-air discos, cafes, restaurants, bars for every taste.





Golden Sands (Vityazevo) is not only a beach paradise, there are a lot of places worthy of attention. For example, the promenade and entertainment center "Paralia", whose design is made in the ancient Greek style. For this, the inhabitants of the village called their homeland little Athens. Paralia starts from Yuzhny Avenue and extends one kilometer along the coast. In addition to architectural features, this place for entertainment is the center of the day and night resort life of Vityazevo.

Sea, sun, golden sand

Vityazevo is one of the most popular regions of the Black Sea resort of Anapa, which is rightly called a beach paradise. Clean and equipped sandy beaches and a gently sloping bottom at the entrance to the sea provide a safe and comfortable stay for children and not only. Active beach activities, such as bananas, catamarans, jet skis and parachutes, will not let lovers to relax and sunbathe on the beach.

The climate is not marked by sharp temperature changes. In high season, rain is quite rare. The air temperature is approximately 30 ¬∞ C, and coastal water is heated to 22 ¬∞ C. Vacations in this place are especially popular during all the summer months, as well as in September.





The choice of entertainment for every taste

Holidays in Vityazevo, located 14 km from the resort city of Anapa, involves a wide range of different types of outdoor activities. Interesting classes can be found for couples with children, and young people who prefer to relax to the fullest. There are many excursions, and you can also go to the dolphinarium, admire the waterfalls in Lagonaki, and visit all kinds of attractions.





Tour organizers offer underwater diving and fishing, windsurfing. At a local factory you can taste more than 20 varieties of wine and appreciate their unique taste and aroma.

Tourists are offered a large number of shops, cafes, restaurants, shops selling souvenirs, attractions and an aquarium with predators from distant Africa. Even many vacationers from Anapa come to stay in the evening in Vityazevo. The main health resort in these parts is rightly called the estuary and its healing mineral mud. What is not there in these parts, even volcanoes, among which the most famous is called Tizdar.

Hotel Golden Sands: infrastructure and food

The guest complex has a very well-developed infrastructure, which provides additional opportunities for hotel guests. Not far from it there are market rows, a sports complex, Paralia entertainment center, a bowling alley, many shops, bars and cafes.

Meals are organized in the dining room on the territory of the resort complex and includes a comprehensive 3-time menu. There is also a cafe and barbecue facilities nearby. This comfortable place will provide you with unforgettable moments in the vastness of sunny Anapa. The atmosphere of the hotel promotes outdoor activities. The residents have at their disposal a spacious clean beach, and in the courtyard of the hotel there is a swimming pool for adults with a small area reserved especially for children.
