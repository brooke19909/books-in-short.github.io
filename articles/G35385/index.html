LCD "Legend Park" in Tyumen: location, infrastructure, developer, reviews

Residential complex "Legend Park" in Tyumen is a modern residential complex consisting of seven 19-story houses, the construction of which is carried out in the territory of the Tyumen Sloboda district. Many people are interested in the comments about this residential complex, since it attracts with low prices and interesting infrastructure, which the developer offers. In this article we will appreciate all the pros and cons of this housing.

About the complex

LCD "Legend Park" in Tyumen is being built at the intersection of the Moscow highway with Zakaluzhskaya street. It is relatively close to the city center, it can be reached by car in about a quarter of an hour.

The concept of the quarter is to try to create the most comfortable living space, which will not be limited only to the apartment. At least the developer himself claims to have set himself such a task. In this regard, the description of the Legenda Park residential complex always pays a lot of attention to large and spacious zones and public spaces, which should form a fundamentally new understanding of the neighborhood, and contribute to an interesting joint pastime between neighbors, and for this you will not have to leave the territory of the residential complex.

Specifications

LCD "Legend Park" in Tyumen is a house that the builder is renting with a finishing finish. The apartments have plastic windows, ramps are installed in the entrances, all the communications necessary for a full life are made - Internet, telephone, cable TV.

The apartments already have electricity and water meters, a reliable metal door, and a video intercom. Most apartments have storage rooms, fire alarms, passenger and freight elevators.

The developer paid due attention to the arrangement of the yard. It offers residents sports grounds, bicycle paths, ample parking for more than three hundred places.

Benefits

Speaking about the advantages of the Legenda Park residential complex, many say that the microdistrict is considered environmentally friendly, although it is located very close to the city center - it is very convenient.

There is a developed infrastructure on the territory of the housing complex: several public transport routes go to the new buildings, it is convenient to get here by car. Within walking distance are a kindergarten, a new comprehensive school, a clinic for adults and children.

The great advantage of the Legenda Park residential complex in Tyumen is that it used advanced modern technologies during construction, which allowed to form a fundamentally new approach to the formation and creation of a living environment. For example, the project provides innovative solutions that can ensure the safe improvement of the area.

Parking for everyone

Apartments in the complex of the most diverse SMART-planning are able to satisfy the needs of any families and single people. On the territory of the residential complex "Legend Park" the concept "Yard without cars" is implemented - the whole house area is intended for walks and leisure.

The project provides a parking space for each apartment and a number of guest cars, so you won‚Äôt have problems with where to leave the car. An important positive point is the presence of pantries, which allow compact storage of things, such as bulky autumn or winter clothes and shoes, in one place.

Safety and Improvement

On the territory of the residential complex you do not have to worry about your safety; you can safely send your child for a walk in the yard. The entire territory of the LCD is fenced and is under round-the-clock video surveillance.

Video intercoms were held in each apartment, so you will not only immediately hear who came to you, but also see the guest on the monitor.

After the construction is completed, a comprehensive improvement will be carried out, which will turn the residential quarter into a full-fledged park. About eight hundred shrubs and trees will be planted throughout the territory, there will be a pedestrian boulevard with barbecue areas, gazebos for relaxing, amazingly beautiful lighting.

Infrastructure

New buildings are located on the territory of a fairly young and rapidly developing microdistrict near the Moscow highway. In walking distance from here are a school, kindergartens and a clinic.

The transport infrastructure of the Legenda Park residential complex attracts many potential buyers of real estate in the area. In the immediate vicinity there is a roundabout with a lot of public transport, which allows you to quickly get to anywhere in the city.

A large number of commercial premises are provided on the territory of the quarter, for which areas on the first floors of new houses under construction were allocated in advance. Therefore, shops, cafes, beauty salons and a fitness center are located within walking distance.

A stone's throw

When developing the layout of the area, it was carefully thought out so that the territory of the residential complex was convenient for both adults and children. In particular, it provides for the appearance of three entertainment areas for children of different ages, a skate park, bicycle paths, and playgrounds for workout.

Clinic No. 5 is a ten-minute public transport ride from the new buildings, and the major Continent and Columbus shopping and entertainment centers can be reached on foot. Recently, a new gymnasium has been opened on the territory of the Tyumen Sloboda, capable of accepting more than a thousand students. Near the residential complex there are several municipal and private kindergartens, as well as a private elementary school called "Euro gymnasium".

House Features

The developer is building reliable monolithic-frame houses, which are considered very durable. In addition, they are convenient for redevelopment.

The applied technology called "Wet facade" helps to avoid major repairs for more than twenty years. The whole complex is connected to a single telemetry system, which allows you to automatically collect all the necessary information from the meters.

In the apartments themselves, communications are placed as thoughtfully as possible. The wiring is made so that there are simply no risers and pipes in a visible place. At the construction stage, equity holders can agree on a particular type of finish in which the apartment will be rented. They are designed for different willingness to move. It can be a rough finish, fine, standard, standard + or comfort.

Potential buyers should be attracted by the increased ceiling height in the apartment, which reaches almost three meters, which allows you to create in each room a lot more space than exists in such a typical living space. In addition to the pantries provided for storing things, there are balconies that can be skillfully turned into stylish and comfortable additional functional spaces.

Where is?

Location LCD "Legend Park" is considered very convenient. The microdistrict in the district is developing rapidly. It is especially convenient in terms of transport accessibility, as in the immediate vicinity there is a roundabout.

Highways laid in this area are not loaded, even a bypass road, which allows you to get to any part of the city in a relatively small amount of time.

A network of public transport has been developed. For example, nearby is bus stop number 39, which is located near a residential quarter.

In the neighborhood are several new residential areas of new buildings. For example, "Intelligence Quarter."

Developer

The construction of this residential complex is carried out by the Meridian group of companies. This is a well-known developer in Tyumen who has been working in the local market since 2007.

He considers the key standards in his professional activity to be the manufacturability and reliability of construction solutions, combined with responsibility to his partners and customers. Great importance is attached to the continuous application and development of modern construction and finishing technologies.

The company emphasizes that they conclude contracts with participants in shared construction in full compliance with 412 federal law. Interest holders may not worry about the safety of their money, since in this case they are protected by the Mutual Insurance Company of developers. This is a public structure operating at the federal level.

The contractor of the Legenda Park residential complex in Tyumen is well known in the region, many trust it, since the company has a large number of completed projects on time, and tens of thousands of square meters of housing have already been commissioned. Among them are the Siberian Meridian, Solnechny, Sovremennik, New Cape, Dobry, Znamensky, Zarechny.

The company is headed by the manager Ivan Nikolayevich Druganov, the post of construction director is Denis Vadimovich Gulakov, and the personnel director is Svetlana Aleksandrovna Slivkina.

Negative

At the same time, there are also negative reviews about the residential complex. The main negative point is that for many LCDs it is still too far from the center. However, the choice has to be made in favor of these new buildings because of already too attractive prices, since the secondary market offers real estate at inflated prices.
