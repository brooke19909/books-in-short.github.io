Recreation centers, Rostov. Reviews, addresses, prices. Rest in the Rostov region

It is good when the weekend, vacation or holidays can be spent in a beautiful quiet place, but not far from home. Not every settlement has the ability to provide its residents with a choice of accommodation and entertainment in picturesque places, which can not be said about cities that have their own recreation centers (Rostov-on-Don, for example).

"Blue Wave"

The Blue Wave Recreation Center is a real hotel-type entertainment complex located in Rostov on the left bank of the Don at 17 Levoberezhnaya Street. It has a vast territory on which there is a cozy hotel that welcomes guests year-round.



At the request of customers, you can book a room of the desired category for any occasion (1-2-seater "standard", deluxe and junior suite), whether it is a weekend, an anniversary celebration or a vacation with children during school holidays. Both adults and kids can have a good time here. For those who like privacy, renting a separate cottage is suitable.

For the older generation recreation center "Blue Wave" offers a restaurant, bar and banquet hall. Pergolas in the barbecue area allow you to enjoy a relaxing holiday with the preparation of barbecue in a warm company, and those who wish to fight in the billiards can enjoy spending time in the billiard room.

To restore strength, you can use the steam room in the sauna, and then plunge into the waters of the Don on the equipped beach. Children will surely enjoy a boat ride that you can rent to feel like a real captain.

The cost of rest at the base depends on the category of the room and can cost from 1100 rubles per day, and the cottage - from 1600 rubles.

"Pole"

The most popular place for Rostovites is the left bank of the Don. Recreation facilities (Rostov-on-Don is rich in them on this side of the river) are represented by such centers as Aelita, High Bank, Kholodok, Pole and many others.

Base "Pole" is especially suitable for spending holidays with family or friends. Here you can rent a hotel room for a different number of people, but each of them will have all the amenities, comfortable furniture and a TV. The room rate depends on the number of guests and can range from 700 rubles to 1400 rubles per day.

Lovers of privacy are waiting for comfortable cottages, ready to accommodate up to 6 guests at once. The cost of living in them is only 700 rubles per person per day. Upholstered furniture, a fully equipped kitchen and all the amenities make these houses a place where you can relax from the hustle and bustle of your company.



For those who like to spend their free time actively, Polyus (recreation center, Rostov-on-Don, 94 Levoberezhnaya) offers sports grounds and an outdoor pool, which children will especially enjoy.

For customers who prefer to work in an environment of beautiful scenery, there is an equipped conference room. At the guests' disposal are a cafe, a bar and a banquet hall. In summer, you can spend time on the beach, which belonged to the recreation center, and a Russian bathhouse and gazebos with barbecue facilities are a good reason to spend time with friends.

Green Island

There is another attraction that Rostov-on-Don can boast of - Green Island. The real island, to which the pontoon bridge leads , can be visited on foot or by bicycle for free, and by car you must pay the fare.

In addition to the fact that tourist camps are located on it, it is a favorite place for picnics near Rostovites. Unfortunately, the territory of the island (except for the sites where the recreation facilities are located) is very littered, which makes it difficult to spend quality and full time there, which is why it is used for cycling and hiking.



If someone is interested in recreation centers with a swimming pool (Rostov-on-Don), then Green Island can please guests with their availability.

"Sperm whale"

For a vacation or summer vacation with the whole family, the summer holiday resort "Sperm Whale" is suitable. It is located at Rostov-on-Don, Zeleny Ostrov, Okruzhnaya street, 20. It is here that the most picturesque part of the island is located.

At the disposal of customers are fully equipped hotel rooms with air conditioning and upholstered furniture. The European restaurant will satisfy even the most demanding gourmet. The recreation center has an active rest, as it has its own sports ground, an outdoor pool and a marina with boats and catamarans.



While fishing enthusiasts are waiting for their catch, sitting in a boat or boat, other guests can enjoy sunbathing on the equipped beach. A Russian bath will help relieve stress and cleanse the body after a hard week or outdoor activities.

The cost of living in "Sperm Whale" is quite affordable, only 1000-1500 rubles. day.

Komat

For the sake of profit, summer recreation centers are often converted for year-round maintenance. Rostov-on-Don, with its huge selection of entertainment centers on the banks of the Don, did not escape these changes, although the Komat camp site remains an exclusively summer vacation destination.

Here from May to September, customers will find cozy two-story houses for up to 6 people. The cottages have everything you need for a relaxing holiday with all amenities and a fully equipped kitchen for those who wish not to depend on the cafe or restaurant menu.

The green territory of the camp site has the potential to prepare delicious barbecue in a cozy gazebo with barbecue facilities or soak up the comfortable beach. In the sauna, you can relax and then plunge into the pool with clean water to restore vitality.

The Komat camp site is located in Rostov-on-Don at the address Levoberezhnaya, 61. The cost of living in the house is from 2500-3000 rubles. day.

"Pine"

One of the advantages that Pine has (a recreation center, Rostov-on-Don) is the price. In the summer, you can rent a separate house for only 500 rubles. person per day. Each house is equipped with everything necessary for living and is located 50 meters from a well-maintained beach.

In winter, guests are welcomed into comfortable hotel rooms with all amenities. On the territory of the base there is a billiard room and a sauna where you can relax with friends.

Pine will be an ideal venue for celebrations of various sizes. Specially equipped summer and winter terraces and a banquet hall amid a beautiful landscape will make any holiday unforgettable.

The base is located at Chemordachka, 41.

"Don health resort"

Rostov-on-Don is famous not only for its recreation facilities, but also for health resorts, where you can solve many health problems or conduct preventive treatment.

Located on the banks of the Don (Levoberezhnaya street, 2), the sanatorium is located within the city, so it can be easily reached at any time of the year.

The sanatorium helps people in the treatment of:

  • of cardio-vascular system;
  • gastrointestinal tract;
  • diseases of the lungs and bronchi;
  • nervous system;
  • diseases caused by professional activities.

Patients are accommodated in comfortable rooms with a different number of beds. Each guest can choose a suitable complex for the treatment or prevention of diseases, the passage of which is designed for a period of 3 to 18 days. The cost of the course depends on its intensity and focus and ranges from 8,000 to 32,000 rubles.



At the disposal of the boarding house guests are a cinema hall, a dining room, a billiard room, a gym, a disco and much more. In order for time to pass unnoticed, each patient can choose a lesson for himself.

Don hospitality

Recreation facilities (Rostov-on-Don provides a large number of them to choose from) are located in the most picturesque places on the banks of the Don. This allows not only residents of Rostov to use their holidays comfortably and comfortably on weekends, but also residents of the whole country to spend their holidays here.



Clean beaches, well-groomed territory, regular landscaping and a warm welcome make all recreation centers a favorite place to spend time at any time of the year. If you wish, you can choose a base with European cuisine or native Russian, or you can just do a trip to cook kebabs on the riverbank and go fishing.

Whatever the reason for visiting any camp site on the Don, customers are always warmly welcomed and served by its employees. Reasonable prices, pleasant service and beautiful nature are a good reason to spend time at one of the recreation centers of Rostov-on-Don.
