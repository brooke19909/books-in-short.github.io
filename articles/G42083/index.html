Amazing and most interesting facts about the New Year

Every night from December 31 to January 1, we celebrate a wonderful and bright holiday, which is characterized by specific customs in different parts of the world. Incredibly informative and fascinating are interesting facts about the New Year and Christmas. In different countries, these glorious holidays are celebrated at different times. So it was before.

In ancient times, Babylon was distinguished by the fact that this holiday was celebrated there in the spring. In a sense, this is quite justified, because nature "wakes up" after the cold weather and really lives in a new way. At this time, the king gathered his closest servants and family, leaving the city. The lands remained at the full disposal of ordinary people, and they could arrange a celebration at their discretion. Citizens had fun from the heart, deprived of the society of their ruler. After all, a curious gift, and most importantly - befitting the wisest of kings.

How diverse it is - this New Year!

If you search and carefully study the history of this holiday, you can find the most diverse and interesting facts about the New Year.





One entertaining custom is available in Micronesia. Residents of these lands celebrate the New Year, as we do, on January 1, but still they have something that is not the same as ours. Since life on a bright holiday needs to be started from scratch, on this day all the inhabitants are given new names, and in their ears they tell their relatives. Accepting the secret, close people beat the drums in order to drive away the listening spirits with a thundering sound.

As a holiday in Russia is celebrated

Interesting facts about the New Year in Russia are also available. In the first half of the last millennium, this holiday was celebrated on March 1, from the fourteenth century it began to be met on September 1. And finally, at the end of the sixteenth century, the date of the celebration of the merry triumph becomes that which is to this day - January 1.

In Russia, the New Year is a holiday that has incorporated the traditions of the Slavs, Christians, peoples of Western Europe and the East. On the night of December 31 to January 1, people gather with their families and close friends. The atmosphere of love, positive and faith in the best reigns. The whole country sends an impulse to the Universe, which in the future should attract prosperity in their lives. The president congratulates citizens on television screens. All this happens against the backdrop of a beautiful Christmas tree, bright decorations, interesting costumes, sparklers, excellent cuisine and a splash of champagne. When the chimes strike, everyone makes the most secret desires.





Tree - an integral symbol of Christmas

Interesting facts about the New Year in London can delight and enrich erudition. In England, there is a custom associated with a tree that is used for Christmas. This tradition came here from the Vikings. Wood was prepared and sawn for Christmas, it was left to dry for a whole year.

When the holiday came, the tree was brought into the house and thrown into the flame of the hearth. Then the fire was carefully watched. Long burning meant that next year would be successful for this house. If the log was rotten, it was considered a bad sign.

A very good Christian tradition is to plant a living Christmas tree. Indeed, it is very merciful to the tree, in the best traditions of Christianity. Noble impulses can sometimes harm people. Scientists say that fungi can stay in living fir trees. Once in a warm room, they begin to multiply and secrete spores that are not very useful for humans. From here cough appears, breathing is difficult, insomnia may occur, a person feels lethargic, may get bronchitis or pneumonia.

So be careful. Process the tree properly or buy not a living Christmas tree, but an artificial one. You can create a Christmas mood without cutting down trees. The main thing is faith in a fairy tale and a positive attitude, aspiration of thoughts for a better future.
