Ice Palace "Vityaz" in Podolsk: address and mode of operation

In Podolsk (Moscow region), various sports facilities were opened. Thanks to them, the population can lead a healthy and more active lifestyle. In addition, citizens come to the centers to watch competitions in various sports. The Vityaz Ice Palace is quite in demand. He is regularly visited by many people. He has already managed to become one of the best sports complexes in the country.

General information

The sports complex appeared in 2000. Not far from it is a large green area. Near the building you can see a monument named after the building itself. It was created by the famous sculptor V. Klykov. The Vityaz Ice Palace in Podolsk is regularly used as the home arena of the Russian Knights Youth Club. When the KHL matches are held here, then in the arena you can see other popular hockey teams.

For spectators, more than 5,000 thousand seats are provided. In addition to the modern scoreboard, there is also a video cube. On it, viewers can see the latest sports news. It transmits both textual, digital and video information. The complex has high-quality equipment for air conditioning. There is a special video surveillance system. The sports center is equipped with high-quality equipment for transmitting light and sound.

In addition to the main ice arena, the complex has a training area where hockey players prepare for competitions. The main site is fenced with protective sides made of high-quality plastic, as well as high-strength glass. The construction often hosts concerts of famous artists. Citizens can get to the performances of O. Buzova, E. Temnikova and other stars.

Where is

Sports complex built in Podolsk, Moscow region. Its exact address is Kirova Street, Building 63, Building A. Nearby is Dubki Park. You can navigate to it to quickly find a building.

There are several ways to get to the sports facility. In addition to the car, it is also possible to arrive by public transport. Get off at the stop "Square Podolsky cadets." To her go:

  • Trolleybuses 1, 2, 3, 5.
  • Buses 5, 6, 9, 15, 18, 20, 21, 24, 63, 65, 406k, 417, 520k, 1004, 1007, 1024, 1026, 1028, 1032, 1033, 1034, 1036, 1048, 1050, 1077 , 1246k.
  • Minibuses 9k, 22, 27, 28, 410, 1245k.

Also in the Ice Palace "Vityaz" is available to come by rail. You need to go to the station called "Kutuzovskaya". From it to the complex are buses 5 and 6.

Mode of operation

You can visit the arena every day. She works seven days a week. The Vityaz Ice Palace opens at 7-00 a.m. and closes at 23-30. The ticket office sells tickets on weekdays until 19-00, and on weekends - until 18-00. You can find out about the work of individual sports sections directly on the spot or by phone, which is indicated on the official website.

Additional Information

The Vityaz ice palace in Podolsk is well known to many citizens. They come here to skate. Mass skating takes place on days when matches do not pass on ice. Therefore, it is recommended to find out the time of the sessions in advance. Tickets for the rink cost from 250 rubles. In the absence of your skates, you can rent them here. One skating - 200 rubles, and the amount of the deposit is 1000 rubles.

In the complex you can sign up for figure skating. There are several types of sections. Children from three and a half years to six are recruited to sports groups. In the amateur group take from 6 to 16 years. There are also just sections for those who like figure skating. They can register people of any age.

In addition to the main arena, the complex has other places for sports. For visitors there are 2 tennis courts under the roof, a gym and a gym. Open its own photo and video studio. Lovers of outdoor activities are invited to visit the bowling center. In the Vityaz Ice Palace, the palace comes to fitness classes. There are separate groups for adults and the younger generation.
