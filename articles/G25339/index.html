Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki, Kassandra, Greece)

Kassandra Peninsula is considered the most densely populated region of Halkidiki. A huge number of tourists gather here in season. Kallithea, in turn, is the most popular and, as they say today, a party resort on the peninsula. However, not everywhere is noisy and crowded. Around there are many quiet, densely forested places, the existence of which is not even known to those who come here for many years in a row to rest.

Kallithea is located on the east coast of the stunning Kassandra Peninsula. In fine weather, the peak of Mount Athos and neighboring Sithonia are visible from here. The resort is famous all over the world for its white sand beaches and clear water, as well as night clubs and the opportunity to have fun on vacation.

In the summer months, Kallithea hotels are literally crowded with tourists. Despite the fact that there are a lot of hotels, both five-star and small, economy class, rooms need to be booked in advance.





Holidays in Kallithea (Kassandra)

Accommodation prices depend on the level of service and amenities that the hotels offer, as well as their proximity to the sea and the popular attractions of the resort. For those who value comfort above all else, apartments are offered in premium hotels in Kallithea. This accommodation is especially convenient when tourists travel with the whole family.

Those who are looking for a good hotel with relatively low prices are recommended to pay attention to cheap hotels, for example, Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki / Kassandra). Kallithea - the heart of the peninsula with its clubs and discos - is two kilometers away, which is convenient for those who prefer a measured and relaxing holiday.

Location

Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki) is built on a hill surrounded by a beautiful garden. Around - majestic mountains covered with evergreen vegetation. A five-minute walk extends the blue sea.

Due to the excellent location of the hotel, tourists can enjoy marvelous sea views from their rooms. The serenity of the area where the Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 * is located makes it possible to completely relax.

Nearby is the fishing village of Afitos, where there are many inexpensive taverns that serve delicious seafood. The nearest airport, located in Thessaloniki, is eighty kilometers away.



Territory

The hotel was built in 1988. The last major renovation in it was carried out only two years ago.

The territory of the Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki, Kassandra) is completely planted with roses and palm trees. It is small, but well-maintained and thought out to the smallest detail. Weaving paths, waterfalls and traditional Greek two-story cottages descend steps down the coast. This place will be very popular with those who prefer calm and silence in relaxation. Peacocks walk across the territory, everywhere there are benches, sitting on which, you can enjoy sea sunsets.

Hotel infrastructure

Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki-Kassandra, Kallithea), although it is considered a three-star, however, it offers a range of services that correspond to a higher category. The hotel has a hairdresser and a paid medical office, there is a lobby, dry cleaning. Vacationers can use the laundry and porter services. There are several small shops on site, including an inexpensive souvenir kiosk. There are ATMs and coffee machines in the lobby.

A well-equipped small conference room is offered to clients combining leisure and work. It is designed for one-time accommodation of one hundred and fifty people.



The registration desk located in the main building serves guests around the clock. Check-in at Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 *, whose address is: Kassandra 630 77 Halkidiki, Greece, is carried out as quickly as possible. Judging by the reviews left by vacationers, the room is provided for fifteen to twenty minutes. In the same place, at the receptionist, the entrant is provided with a listing of all the services that the hotel can offer, including the operating schedule of the restaurant and bars. Fax and photocopying services are also installed here, there are also paid depository safes and a left-luggage office.

There are no wheelchair accessible rooms at the hotel. Accommodation with pets must be agreed with the manager when booking rooms. The hotel offers free Wi-Fi. Smoking is not allowed on site.

At the registration desk you can arrange a transfer to the airport, order a taxi. Bicycles and cars are also available for rent, and there is a tour desk.

Nutrition

Everyone who arrives at the Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 * hotel is served on a half-board and all-inclusive basis. Scheduled buffets are served at the Pella Restaurant. For dessert, fruit is always served: oranges, apples, grapes, etc. In the evenings, it is possible to visit the restaurant by reservation. There is a special barbecue menu. Beach

Vacationers in their reviews often mention the sea at the Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 *. And no wonder, because the beach on this part of the coast is considered one of the best on the peninsula. You can reach it by crossing the motorway at the transition with a traffic light. The territory of the bathing area is one in three neighboring hotels. White sand, clear water - all this makes the rest on this part of the coast unforgettable.

There is a bar on the beach, and racks, trestle beds and umbrellas are available at an additional cost. There is also a volleyball court, toilet and shower. The entrance to the water is gentle.

Pool

On the territory of the Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 *, convenient and well thought out to the smallest detail, most of which are positive reviews, there is a small pool with sea water, with a section for children. Around there are always free sun loungers under umbrellas, for which you do not need to pay. There is also a bar. Alcohol in it is issued only at the tables. At four in the afternoon, children are offered free ice cream. Water in the pool is changed every other day.

Entertainment

Since the Macedonian Sun Hotel 3 * is more positioned as a family, the management in it is aimed at making both children and their parents comfortable to rest. On the territory there is a good, judging by the reviews, children's animation. In the evenings, discotheques and theme nights are organized in the open amphitheater for adults. Vacationers at the Macedonian Sun can use for free all the services offered by the neighboring Palini or Atos Palace hotels, which are part of the same hotel chain.

You can play table tennis and darts, mini-golf, tennis or billiards at any convenient time. In the evenings, the restaurant plays live music. The nearby Pallini Beach has a nightclub.

Those who prefer outdoor activities can go diving or windsurfing, go water skiing or canoeing, parachute. There are many water activities on the beach, there is also a licensed windsurfing school, the necessary equipment for which you can rent.

Young people leading a nocturnal lifestyle can go to Kallithea, where there are many interesting places for entertainment.

The shallow compartment of the pool for children is very convenient and safe.
