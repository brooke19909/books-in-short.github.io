May Flower Apart Hotel (Turkey / Alanya): description and reviews

For many tourists considering holiday options, Turkey comes to mind first. This country has already managed to become famous for its huge number of hotels, high-class service, plenty of entertainment and reasonably affordable prices.

It is no secret that one of the most famous and crowded resorts in Turkey is the city of Alanya. The choice of hotels here is simply huge - tourists can settle in a chic five-star hotel, as well as in a small, cozy guesthouse. And often travelers stay at the May Flower Apart Hotel. This hotel managed to earn a good reputation and high ratings on international travel sites. Naturally, before booking it will not hurt to get acquainted with some additional information.

Hotel Location Data





Every tourist who at least once organized a vacation abroad knows that in the first place you need to pay attention to the location. Where is the May Flower App Hotel Apart? Alanya, almost the very center of the resort town - this is where many compatriot travelers prefer to relax.

This is a fairly lively area, around which there are dozens of other hotels, as well as cafes, shops, Turkish restaurants. The distance to the beach is about a kilometer, which is very good. By the way, within walking distance of tourists a large water park, museums and lots of attractions. Gazipasa Airport is 39 km away. Antalya International Airport (this is where tourists most often land) is 135 km away.

What does the territory look like? Brief infrastructure description

May Flower App Hotel Apart has been welcoming guests since 1994. During this time, he managed to collect a lot of positive reviews. By the way, in 2008 there was a complete reconstruction, and not so long ago, and cosmetic repairs, so today the hotel pleases its guests with modern amenities.

The hotel is a modern nine-story building with large panoramic windows and spacious balconies. Of course, the hotel has its own territory. Despite the small area, sports and recreation areas, terraces and even a small private garden are placed in the courtyard of the hotel complex. By the way, in their reviews, travelers note the cleanliness and tidiness of the hotel area.

Description and photos of rooms





May Flower Apart Hotel is a place famous for its comfort. All rooms are bright and clean, with freshly renovated and modern furnishings. From the windows you can enjoy views of the garden and the surrounding area, as well as observe life in the courtyard.

Of course, guests are provided with much-needed household appliances. An undoubted advantage is the presence of your own kitchen with a stove, microwave, kettle and even your own coffee maker. Naturally, the set includes dishes and everything you need for cooking. The bedroom has a large-screen TV. For an additional fee (small), guests use a telephone (international service is provided) and a safe. Of course, the rooms are equipped with modern air conditioning.

Part of the room is reserved for the bathroom. Tourists can use the shower. By the way, no one complains about problems with hot water. You can count on bath towels (they are delivered during cleaning) and basic personal hygiene products. As former guests say, all rooms are kept in almost perfect cleanliness - the maids conduct wet cleaning daily, towels and bed linen are also constantly changing.

Food: what to expect for a traveler





When choosing a place to stay, you should definitely consider the issue of nutrition, because even an impression of the most vivid entertainment can fade on an empty stomach. As already mentioned, the hotel complex is located in the city center, which means that there are numerous cafes and restaurants nearby for every taste.

But there is something that the May Flower Apart Hotel has to offer. Reviews of tourists contain information that the food in the hotel restaurant is good. When booking a room, travelers prefer to pay for a convenient all-inclusive scheme. Meals are served in the spacious restaurant, where buffets are served for tourists. The menu is quite diverse, because guests are offered meat and fish dishes, numerous snacks and salads, fresh fruits and real Turkish goodies.

By the way, the restaurant offers a diet menu, however, dishes are prepared only by prior request. If you wish, the staff delivers light snacks for breakfast directly to your room. There is a cozy bar on the territory of the hotel, where you can always pleasantly pass the time. And in a local cafe, visitors are offered snacks, homemade ice cream and delicious desserts.

Relax on the beach: entertainment and additional amenities on the beach





The hotel complex May Flower Apart Hotel is located in the city center, which means that you have to walk to the beach. The coast is located about a kilometer from the hotel building. In fact, this is not so much - the walk will take no more than 10-15 minutes.

You have to relax on a public beach, which means that you will have to pay extra for additional amenities (for example, the use of umbrellas and sun loungers). It is worth noting that the prices here are quite affordable. By the way, on the seashore there are several bars and cafes, as well as a water sports center where you can rent water transport.

The beach here is clean and beautiful, and waves rarely rise. There is a large volleyball court, attractions for children. If you wish, you can go water skiing or a boat, try parasailing, windsurfing and other water sports.

Additional amenities for guests





Choosing the hotel complex May Flower Apart Hotel as a temporary refuge, the tourist can be sure of the presence of all the benefits of modern civilization. Throughout the entire hotel, including living rooms, guests can use the Internet access, and reviews indicate that the speed and quality of the connection here is really at its best. If you did not bring a laptop or other equipment with you, then you can have a good time in a cozy Internet cafe.

If desired, clothes can be handed over for washing or dry cleaning - these services are paid separately. In case of health problems, you should contact the reception, where they can call a doctor. By the way, employees can also arrange a shuttle service or call a local taxi.

It is easy to rent a motorcycle or car in the city, which can then be left in the hotel parking lot. By the way, guests can use the parking absolutely free.

How do parents rest with their children? Additional amenities

Of course, tourists who are going on a trip with a child are interested in the availability of additional amenities, because kids need entertainment. What should I count on when staying at May Flower Apart Hotel? Alanya (Alanya) - a wonderful place, which is ideal for families.

Parents with children are usually accommodated in the apartment, although an extra bed can be delivered to the studios without any problems. Food will also please you, because among the abundance of dishes you can easily pick up something nutritious and healthy for a child of almost any age.
