Hotels of the Golden Ring: famous and non-standard hotel establishments of the route

One of the most popular tourist routes in Russia is the Golden Ring. Not only Russians, but also foreign guests are happy to go this way to admire the beauty of architectural objects, to visit unique historical places.

But since there are many who want to touch the past of the country, everyone is primarily interested in the issue of living and everyday life. Recently, the arrangement of hotels on the route has been given serious attention, and today the hotels of the Golden Ring are comfortable establishments equipped with the needs of a modern tourist. Further - about the most popular hotels that are in demand.

"Kuptsov House"





This hotel, like other three-star Golden Ring hotels, is a budget option. The institution is located in Yaroslavl, almost in the center of the city. Nearby are interesting objects for travelers - the Yaroslavl Kremlin, the Church of Elijah the Prophet, the academic theater, the buildings of the Transfiguration Monastery and other important sights.

The hotel offers 20 rooms equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay: a work desk, air conditioning, minibar, TV and DVD.

"Cathedral Sloboda"

For those who are looking for a non-standard rest, such Golden Ring hotels as the Cathedral Sloboda hotel are suitable. Moreover, this property is difficult to call a hotel, since it includes 13 houses. And not new, but well restored, but retaining the entire old interior. Starting with a Russian stove and ending with windows and doors, everything here is old.

The additional service also corresponds to: so that the guests rest from the road, disperse, gain impressions, similar Golden Ring hotels offer them the following types of entertainment - horse riding, boat rides, small forest walks, mushroom or berry trips. The choice of entertainment depends on the time of year and the taste of the vacationers themselves.

"Art Hotel"





Considering the hotels of the Golden Ring of Russia, one cannot but mention this hotel, located in the center of Pereslavl-Zalessky. This is a large estate, where at least 12 people can live freely at the same time. The owner of the institution is a rather famous artist. She not only prepares breakfast for guests, but also invites her to visit her art gallery, where anyone can take drawing lessons from her.

The furnishings in the guest rooms correspond to the taste of the hostess - unique wooden furniture is installed everywhere. Bathrooms are equipped with a shower, in addition, there is a Russian bath. In the garden there is a gazebo and cages with rabbits, which cause constant delight in children.

Hotels with pool

For those who cannot imagine their vacation without swimming, the Golden Ring of Russia hotels with a swimming pool open their doors. This is more than a hundred hotel establishments of a high level of comfort. Among them, the most famous and sought after are:





  1. "Nikolaev Posad". Located in Suzdal. This is a huge establishment with 133 rooms, where a large number of services is provided - Turkish bath, babysitting services, spa, 24-hour fitness. In addition, the hotel has three restaurants, a conference room, a sports ground. Nearby (distance - less than 700 m) there are many attractions, including the Alexander and Pokrovsky monasteries.
  2. "Russian Manchester." Location - Ivanovo. The quality of the rooms and the level of service are quite high. Spacious rooms have a modern design and full technical equipment, and additional services are quite diverse and in a large assortment. Many guests of the establishment are interested in why it has such a name. Ivanovo, like the English Manchester, is the center of the textile industry, so the owners of the establishment decided to emphasize this feature of their region, calling the hotel "Russian Manchester".
