History

This hotel in the center of Moscow - 4 stars, "Pushkin" - is located in Nastasinsky Lane, and from the windows of its rooms a gorgeous view of Tverskaya Street opens. The building in which the hotel is located today was built in the period 1883-1891.





The second floor of the historic mansion was a small theater named after Komissarzhevskaya, the leader of which was the brother of Vera Fedorovna - architect Fedor. It was this man who raised the theatrical banner of romanticism in the pre-revolutionary period. In addition, there was also a school-studio, where they trained universal actors with stage vocals and other data.

Of course, at this stage the story did not end, but only began, but this is not in this article. By the way, not so long ago, in the building where the modern hotel "Pushkin" is located, complex construction and reconstruction works were carried out, so the mansion became simply unrecognizable.
