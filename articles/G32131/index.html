Best hostels in Tbilisi: photos and descriptions and reviews

Georgia is a state located in the western part of Transcaucasia on the eastern coast of the Black Sea. It applies equally to the Near East and the Middle East.

Tbilisi is the capital of Georgia and the largest historical and economic center. The city is located on the banks of the Kura River, it was built in the V century at the crossroads of trade routes.

Now here several types of tourism are developing rapidly: mountain and sea. Tbilisi has a huge number of historical monuments, Orthodox cathedrals and monasteries. On some streets you can plunge into ancient life, and all thanks to the architecture. Most importantly, you can relax here for very little money. You can spend the night, for example, in one of the hostels. Unfortunately, not all of them are good, so now we will talk about the seven best hostels in Tbilisi.

M42 Hostel

This Tbilisi hostel is located on top of a picturesque hill. Very close is the Tbilisi funicular. For its guests, the institution provides 29 rooms of various categories:

  • Double room with economy category. It is located on the top floor, there is a TV and a fan. You can enjoy mountain or city views from the windows. Number is executed in saturated green color, the area is 16 m². The price is from 1750 rubles.
  • Bed in a dormitory room. The room accommodates 8 people, its area is 20 m², it is made in bright light shades. The price is from 520 rubles.
  • In addition, there is a double room with a balcony and a dormitory room for 8 people, as well as rooms for 6 and 4 people.

Internet is available on site, a delicious breakfast can be purchased for 250 rubles, this hostel in Tbilisi organizes a shuttle service for you and provides a free parking space. Guests, judging by the reviews, really liked its location, as well as the ability to cook outdoors.

Fox hostel

Fox Hostel is a hostel in the center of Tbilisi. Next to it is the famous Opera and Ballet Theater, the Rustaveli Theater and Freedom Square.

The hostel is quite small because it has only 5 rooms:

  1. This triple room is 15 m² in beige colors. This room has a private entrance and bathroom, as well as a balcony and air conditioning. The price is from 1900 rubles.
  2. Bed in 6-bed room. This room is common for women and men. Its area is 15 m², the price is from 500 rubles.

The hotel has a small, but very nice terrace, Internet access is provided. There is also a 24-hour reception. Hotel guests liked the location, which they talk about in reviews, because this area is filled with restaurants and cafes with national cuisine. Guests also appreciated the courtesy and friendliness of the workers of this hostel in Tbilisi.

Milana22

This hostel in Tbilisi will cost inexpensively, although it is located almost in the center, in the Sololaki area, close to local attractions, such as Freedom Square. Since the hotel is not very large, it has only 10 rooms.

Family Room is the only type of room in the hotel. It accommodates up to 6 adults. It has 3 single, 1 double, 2 bunk beds. The area is 35 m²; the room offers a magnificent view of the mountains and surroundings. They also include a private bathroom, air conditioning, a kitchen with all necessary utensils, a hairdryer and even board games.

It is worth saying that free Wi-Fi is available on site, in addition, the hostel provides breakfast. Guests can also have breakfast on site or enjoy the beauties in the small garden. Judging by the reviews, many of them loved the friendly atmosphere and cleanliness of the rooms.

Panorama ECO-Hotel

This is another place where the rooms are inexpensive. The hostel in the center of Tbilisi is located just 500 meters from the metro station. In addition, within walking distance of the hippodrome park and the stadium named after Mikhail Meskhi. The hostel is rather big; it has 15 rooms of different categories.

  • Double Room. This is an economy room for up to 2 adults. Its area is 15 m². It is made in deep blue, bright red and juicy green. This room features a minibar, cable TV, a private bathroom, a terrace with garden views. The cost is from 2600 rubles.
  • Double standard room. It is made in black and white, an area of ​​22 m². It has air conditioning and a fridge. The price is from 2900 rubles.

The price includes a very good breakfast. And in addition, the hostel provides a free shuttle service for its guests. Vacationers like the location and view of the rooms.

Budget Hostel Zodiac

Budget Hostel Zodiac is one of the best hostels in Tbilisi. It is located in the Vake district, 2.3 km from Turtle Lake, 2 km from the Tbilisi Opera and Ballet Theater.

The hostel is small but very cozy, it has only 9 rooms:

  1. Double room (pictured). Private room for privacy and tranquility. Its area is 20 m², made in delicate pink and in a calm beige color. The rooms offer stunning views of the city and surroundings. The price is from 1300 rubles.
  2. Bed in a dormitory room. The room accommodates 8 people, its area is 20 m², the price is from 260 rubles. Bathroom and kitchen are shared.
  3. Bed in a dormitory room. The room already accommodates 6 people, it is made in rich red and bright yellow. The price is from 300 rubles.

There is also a superior double room.

On the territory there is a picturesque garden, a small terrace, Internet access, and an excellent breakfast is included in the price. Judging by the reviews, the hotel guests loved the garden, the atmosphere and the friendliness of the staff.

Dvizh Hostel Eli Spali

Dvizh Hostel Eli Spali is a bright and interesting hostel in the very center of Tbilisi. It is located near the Rose Revolution Square, Samaya Garden and Tbilisi Concert Hall.

The hostel is medium in size, with a total of 13 rooms:

  • Double Room. This is a separate deluxe room. It is made in the Baroque style, in calm gray and white shades. Area 15 m². This room features a game console, laptop, iron, fan and a private bathroom. The price is from 2200 rubles.
  • Standart room. Room accommodates 3 adults with an area of ​​20 m². Made in bright and rich colors. The price is from 2600 rubles.

In addition, guests can rent a private double room with a garden view or a bed in a 6-bed room.

For its guests the hotel provides a private parking space, organizes a shuttle service. Wi-Fi and a good bar are on site. Vacationers like the location and availability of a game console in the room.

Like Hostel Tbilisi
