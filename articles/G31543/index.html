Waterpark "Rodeo Drive" in St. Petersburg: address, opening hours, reviews

TC "Rodeo Drive" is located in St. Petersburg, on Kultury Avenue. A shopping center is open daily. Along with shops, restaurants and children's play areas, the Rodeo Drive water park operates in it. The shopping complex is equipped with convenient car entrances and spacious parking.

How to get there

Next to it are public transport. Bus routes No. K-188, K-278, trams No. 9, 20 run past the shopping center. The nearest metro station is Ozerki. A little further from the Rodeo Drive water park is the Prospekt Enlightenment. A free branded bus runs from the entrance to the lobby of the Ozerki metro station. The first flight leaves at 11:00, the last at 21:45. The interval of movement is a quarter of an hour.

About the complex

The shopping center offers a wide range of services to visitors. It includes a visit to the water park "Rodeo Drive", car parking, food court area, bicycle parking. As well as ATMs, ramps for people with disabilities, toilets. TC starts working at 10:00. Doors of shopping galleries close at 22:00.

First impression

Visitors to the water complex believe that the water park is small. It can not be compared with European or metropolitan counterparts. In general, guests remain dissatisfied with the quality of service and maintenance. Some note dirt in showers and complain of a musty smell and dampness in locker rooms.

Water activities

For kids in the water park there are small slides with small and warm pools. The most popular among children are the water attractions "Grotto", "Multislide", "Hydrotube". There is an open slide for descent on inflatable "cheesecakes".

Reviews

  • chipped tiles in pools;

  • tasteless food in the bar;
  • lack of medical personnel;
  • small area.

Infrastructure

The water complex has Turkish and Russian steam rooms, Finnish saunas, fonts and a jacuzzi. It is possible to visit the bath center without paying entrance to the Rodeo Drive water park. In this case, the price of the admission ticket is 420 rubles. The subscription is valid for three hours. No towel rental. You need to have rubber slippers, replaceable clothes, a swimsuit, a bathrobe with you.

Contacts

The address of the Rodeo Drive water park: St. Petersburg city, Culture Avenue, Building 1, Rodeo Drive shopping center. The nearest metro stations are Ozerki and Prospekt Prosvescheniya.

Cost

On weekdays, a visit to the water complex for adults will cost 780 rubles for three hours, subject to visits from 10:00 to 16:00. At this time, a children's ticket costs 570 rubles. Pupils up to fourteen years old are eligible for the discount. Between 16:00 and 20:30, prices at Rodeo Drive Water Park are 870 rubles for three hours for adults and 660 for children. Entrance after 20:30 to all categories of visitors will cost 700 rubles.

At weekends, rates are higher. If you arrive between 09:00 and 20:30, a ticket for an adult will cost 960 rubles, for a child under fourteen years old 780. For entry after 20:30 they ask 700 rubles. Weekends include not only Saturday and Sunday, but also holidays and school holidays. The exception is weekdays during the summer months. Prices at Rodeo Drive Water Park may be increased. The cost of excess time for one minute is five rubles.

Paying the entrance to the water center, the client receives the right to visit the bath area an unlimited number of times. Children under seven are always allowed free of charge. The cash desk closes at 21:30. Tickets can be purchased after 09:00. The water complex is open until 23:00. When buying tickets, you must present a passport or birth certificate. All people under the age of fourteen go to the Rodeo Drive water park in St. Petersburg, accompanied by adults.

How to pay

When paying for a visit to the water zone, customers receive plastic bracelets. They are equipped with electronic key rings. These chips allow you to open and lock lockers for clothes, pass through the turnstiles. An electronic bracelet is a kind of electronic wallet, the balance of which the administration pays 1,500 rubles.

When choosing paid services, credit funds are written off. You can pay off the debt after leaving the Rodeo Drive water park (St. Petersburg) at the box office. For the loss of the electronic bracelet, the visitor must pay 300 rubles.

Gift certificates

The cost of the card is 960 and 780 rubles per adult and child, respectively. The gift certificate is valid for twelve months. The card holder has the right to enter the water entertainment zone without waiting in line.

Fifteen workouts in the evening will cost 5,000 rubles. This amount includes a lesson of forty-five minutes and two hours of visiting the water entertainment zone. Ten lessons cost 3000 rubles. Evening training starts at 21:00. Morning classes start at 12:00. They are held on Wednesdays.

All subscriptions are registered. Missed trainings for any reason are paid, but the card is not renewed.
