GOSTI

Opens the rating of the most popular - night club GOSTI. On the evening of Friday and Saturday, many lovers of noisy parties and incendiary dance rhythms gather here. There is convenient parking near the club. At the entrance, visitors will need to pass face control. The security guards do not allow dubious persons, teenagers under 18 years of age (so just in case, take your passport with you) and guests, in torn and dirty clothes, to an impressive appearance. After passing the guard, you will find yourself in a world of frantic fun.

The club has very beautiful interiors, for privacy lovers there are VIP tables that need to be booked in advance. Large dance floor accommodates all lovers of dance. The club has recently changed all lighting equipment and sound equipment, this allows guests to fully enjoy the tracks from the 90s. Themed parties and concerts of popular artists are often held. If you get tired of dancing, you can go to the bar.

You will be offered a wide selection of dishes and snacks of European cuisine, as well as a huge amount of alcoholic drinks and cocktails. The average bill is from 700 rubles.

This night club in Stavropol is located at: Dovatortsev, 51b. Open from Friday to Sunday from 23:00 to 06:00.

"Forbes" - a night club in Stavropol

This is one of the favorite vacation spots of local youth. Once in Stavropol for the first time, go relax in the Forbes club. You will definitely like a good mood, pleasant emotions, funny contests and a show program. The owners of the club thought over the interior for a long time to make it original and unlike other entertainment venues. There is a special photo zone, which is decorated with beautiful, tall plants and a luxurious sofa.

You can ask the photographer to take professional photos. The club is divided into several zones: VIP (with comfortable tables and soft sofas, you can also smoke hookah), a dance room (spacious dance floor with a large stage) and a bar (with a huge selection of delicious food and alcoholic drinks). It is in this night club of Stavropol that the best DJs play not only in the city, but throughout Russia.

You can relax at Forbes from Friday to Sunday from 5 p.m. to 5 a.m., and on weekdays (Tuesday-Wednesday) from 6 p.m. to 5 a.m. He is waiting for guests at the address: Dovatortsev, 49b.

Do you want to visit this nightclub in Stavropol? You can do this on Friday and Saturday from 17:00 to 05:00, as well as right in the middle of the work week: from Monday to Thursday from 19:00 to 05:00.
