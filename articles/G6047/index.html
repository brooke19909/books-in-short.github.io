Acquaintance with the SEC "City Park" in Saransk

City Park in Saransk was built in 2015. Today it is one of the most popular shopping and entertainment complexes in the capital of the Republic of Mordovia, and also managed to occupy an important place in the cultural life of citizens. And this is well-deserved: the shopping center has good transport accessibility, a large number of shops are open for successful shopping, there is a sports club, a whole floor of entertainment areas and a cafe, which is especially important for visitors with children.

Getting to the City Park in Saransk is easy. Shopping and entertainment center is located in the modern microdistrict of the city - Yubileiny. Nearby, about 10 minutes walk, public transport. For visitors using private cars there is a spacious two-level covered parking. The exact address of the institution: st. Volgograd, d. 71.

The City Park shopping center in Saransk became the place where the first Lenta hypermarket was opened in the city. It is located on the ground floor and occupies a vast territory, thereby attracting even more interest from citizens. It should be noted that it was City Park that became the place where many popular brands, such as H&M, decided to open stores.

The shopping center was created with care for visitors, which can be caught in every little thing. It is comfortable to be here, you can find an interesting lesson for any age. The calling card of the City Park center in Saransk is Madagascar. This is an entertainment complex, which we will talk about in more detail.

Cinema

At the disposal of visitors of the City Park in Saransk is a cinema. This is a multi-screen, well-equipped cinema with modern equipment, which is a pleasure to watch movies in.

If you want to go to the cinema with your whole family, come to the City Park in Saransk. The schedule in the cinema is designed in such a way that it is convenient to visit it with children of any age and without them - from 10 am to 3 am. There are always promotions and special offers. There is a cafe in the park with delicious popcorn and drinks. There is an interesting offer for a children's birthday.

Sport Club

"Fitness Life" is a modern sports club, which is equipped with a full-fledged strength training room with a large area for cardio, as well as a room for dancing and aerobics. It offers visitors all the necessary equipment: steps, fitballs, metballs and much more. Strip plastic training is also underway. The club has the opportunity to purchase sports nutrition.

Cafe

After long purchases, there is often a desire to eat. In City Park there are various areas that sell fast food, sushi and rolls, European dishes, ice cream and other sweets. All these cafes and restaurants are different, including in terms of interior decoration.
