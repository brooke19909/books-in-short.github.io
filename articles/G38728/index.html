Euphoria Hotel Tekirova (Turkey / Kemer): Travel Photos and Reviews

Due to its favorable location on the Mediterranean coast, Tekirova has become increasingly popular with Russians over the years. The reason for this is a certain isolation of the resort from noisy tourist centers. In addition, it is located only seventy kilometers from the Antalya airport, which is especially attractive for those who spend holidays with their families and young children who do not tolerate the transfer.



Tekirova belongs to the areas of the famous Turkish resort of Kemer, so hotel service is up to standard. And due to its remoteness from the bustling center, this small village is considered a great place for a family vacation. In addition, thanks to the developed infrastructure that Kemer possesses, Tekirova in particular, every tourist, even the smallest, will be able to find entertainment and activity for everyone.

Majestically located at the foot of the Taurus Mountains, the resort fascinates with its nature. Here, even in summer, you can see snow on the tops of the mountains, and the clear azure and very gentle sea is beyond praise.

Rest in Kemer

This place attracts avid sports enthusiasts who like excellent centers for active activities, including diving. And the convenience of the beaches and the smooth coastline create the most favorable conditions for relaxing with the kids. Active tourists - fans of adrenaline, can go rafting on fast mountain rivers, visit the best Turkish water parks with attractions that can turn the head of even an experienced extreme.



Young people thirsting for nightly entertainment in Kemer can visit clubs and cafes, dance floors and restaurants. Here you can dive headlong into shopping, as prices in resort shops are moderate, and the assortment is quite diverse.

general information

The hotel is located near Kemer in a small seaside village with a population of three and a half thousand people. Despite such compactness, the town of Tekirova has long been popular with tourists, including Russians. Many of them stay at the Euphoria Hotel Tekirova 5, which is considered the best in the region.

The old name for this hotel is Corinthia Club Hotel Tekirova. It stands on the first line of the Mediterranean coast and has its own beach. The hotel is surrounded by the snow-capped peaks of the Toros mountain range, the foot of which is buried in coniferous forests. In combination with the sea air and magnificent surrounding nature, rest in this local "Riviera" not only relaxes, but also treats.

The hotel was built in 1995, since then it has been renovated several times. In 2011, a major renovation was held here. The nearest airport is seventy kilometers away. The center of Kemer, where there are many entertainment venues, is only ten minutes walk.



There is a bus stop in front of the hotel, from which shuttles depart every fifteen minutes in the direction of Antalya, Phaselis and Camyuva. There are always taxis there, ready to take in any direction.
