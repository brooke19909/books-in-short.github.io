How to make money on Yandex.Music: myths and reality

Today on the Internet there are many different ways to make money. For example, you can perform various tasks, open your own business or create advertising platforms. So far, the Yandex.Music service is a little-known method, which you can earn money in two ways: using a special system by viewing ads or, if you are a musician, thanks to donations from listeners through the pages of your releases. Let's look at each type of earnings separately and see what kind of service it is in general.

Yandex.Music

Today there is a wide selection of services for listening to tracks of your favorite artists. The largest portal of the Russian Internet, Yandex, did not bypass this area, having created the Music service system, which works on any device with various operating systems. All you need to listen to audio is an Internet connection.





On the main page of the service are recommendations individually for you, there is also a switch by genre. The service, before you understand how to make money on Yandex.Music, will require you to enter a username and password from your mailbox on this portal.

Musical compositions can be found through the search bar, they are distributed by genre, artist, album. The service has the ability to create playlists and save audio recordings to your hard drive.

How to make money on Yandex.Music by listening to tracks

If you search on the expanses of the Russian Internet, then two paid systems of this type are offered. They help to figure out how to make money on Yandex.Music, by providing certain methods of viewing advertising while listening to audio recordings or by translating the audio track into a text document. This niche is still poorly understood, and two courses are offered - from Yana Sorokina and YandexMusic 2.0. Both services require payment for their use and look very doubtful. That is why we can assume that if you do not know how to make money on Yandex.Music, then most likely other users of the service do not know this, since this is impossible.

Earnings for the artist

If you are a musician and release your own audio albums, no matter what genre, whether it is rap or electronic tracks, you have the opportunity to post your release on the Yandex.Music service. How to make money listening to music, if you are the author of it? There are several ways, let's look at them.





Firstly, if a listener visits the page of your album on the service, he will see a donation button for the artist. Money sent to the system will be sent to your wallet. In this case, after placing the release, you must create a Yandex.Money wallet so that you can transfer the received funds to the card.

The second type of earning is holding your own concerts. This can be done after you have gained popularity through the "Music" service on the Yandex portal. Of course, it is important to devote time to your PR, organization of performances, collaboration with popular performers.

Brand Advertising





You can get money to the musician and other means. Another way to make money for the performer is to advertise various products in their tracks. But there are pitfalls: not all albums can get into the service, and it is also very difficult to find advertisers who will pay for mentioning their brand in the text of the composition. Such songs can serve as an example: Flesh Smile - "Pepsi Cola", Oxxxymiron & LSP - "Madness", Pharaoh & Buleavard depo - "5 minutes ago". These three rap tracks mention certain brands of goods that apparently paid for it.
