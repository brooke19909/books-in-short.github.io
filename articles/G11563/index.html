Hotels in Moscow near the metro: overview, features and reviews

Moscow is a large metropolis - the capital of Russia. Tourists from all over the world come here in large numbers here all year round. Someone goes for a walk and see the main attractions of Moscow: Red Square and Spasskaya Tower. Many come for work and important negotiations. The first question that arises for guests of the capital is related to comfortable living. After all, this is one of the important components of a successful trip. There are a lot of accommodation options in Moscow. This is a hotel room, daily apartments, and hostels. This article will provide an overview of Moscow hotels near the metro.

Katerina Park

One of the best hotels in the city. Here you will find comfort and coziness, and all this at attractively low prices. One of the main advantages: convenient location. The hotel is located near the metro station "Prague" (Moscow). Just 5 minutes walk. A lot of transport runs to the city center, and you can drive to Red Square in 20 minutes. Also nearby are Vnukovo and Domodedovo airports.

The modern hotel building offers its guests 239 rooms of the following categories.

  1. King-bed - standard with a double bed. It can comfortably accommodate two people. The room is equipped with all necessary furniture and appliances. There is air conditioning and a minibar.
  2. TwinBad - a room with two twin beds. In the same way as in the previous version, it is designed for two persons. The difference is that in the rooms of this category there is a small safe.
  3. Suite - a room with a large double bed and a sofa. This option is perfect for a company of 3 people. The room consists of two rooms equipped with the most modern furniture.

Near the hotel there is convenient parking and a wonderful park, which will be appreciated by lovers of hiking. The complex has an excellent cafe with a large selection of delicious dishes. You can also relax in the sauna and play billiards. For people leading a healthy lifestyle, a wonderful fitness club with modern exercise machines is equipped here. Room rates: from 4,000 per day (standard) and 6,000 (lux). It includes a buffet breakfast. Address: Kirovograd, 11.

Hotel "Mandarin"

A popular option for city guests. It is well suited for those who are looking for a cheap hotel in Moscow and close to the metro. After all, the nearest Krasnoselskaya station is only 8 minutes at a slow pace. Hotel Mandarin is located in an old manor: once there lived a family of Count Golitsyn. The interior of the interior is striking in its grace. It seems as if you are in the royal palace. There are chic carpets and many beautiful flowers in the corridors. You can choose a room in the following categories: standard, studio and suite. Price: from 3,500 per day.

Residents in suites include breakfast. Each room has comfortable beds, large plasma TVs, minibars and air conditioning. Bathrooms are equipped with all shower facilities. Hotel maids carefully monitor the cleanliness and order of the rooms. Another advantage of the Mandarin Hotel is the availability of convenient parking, the possibility of unlimited internet access and Swedish breakfasts. Each guest can enjoy a wellness massage and work out in the gym. In the evening, it is pleasant to spend time with family or friends in an excellent restaurant located on the hotel premises. Having decided to stay at this Moscow hotel near the Krasnoselskaya metro station, be sure to book a room in advance. Address: Olkhovskaya, 23.

Hotel "Sultan 5"

Not far from the Belorussky railway station is an excellent accommodation option. At the Sultan 5 Hotel you will have a good time and you can enjoy the atmosphere of calm and comfort. This is one of the best hotels near the Belorusskaya metro station in Moscow. The rooms have large soft carpets and beautiful paintings. All furniture and appliances are only the most modern. Each room has a comfortable bed, plasma TV, telephone for communication with the administrator.

The number of rooms is represented by the following categories: economy class, standard and comfort. The cost is quite low, when compared with other Moscow hotels, from 2,500 per day. There is a service ordering food directly to your room. The disadvantages of "Sultan 5" include the lack of parking. And to the pluses - the opportunity to get acquainted with the sights of the city as part of an excursion group, with which the guide works at the hotel, as well as the fact that the hotel is located near the metro in Moscow. And this plays an important role. Address: Skakovaya, 36.

"Marseilles"

This small and homely mini-hotel in Moscow (near the Mayakovskaya metro station) will be a good option for those who want to find a hotel in the center for a moderate fee. Marseille is located in the Tverskoy district of the city at the address 1st Tverskaya ‚Äì Yamskaya, 9. Supermarkets, shopping centers, cafes and restaurants are within walking distance.

In total, the mini-hotel has 5 rooms:

  • economy class (they have two separate beds and a shower);
  • standard (you can choose either the option with a double bed, or with individual);
  • Suite (a family of 3 can comfortably accommodate here).

The walls are painted in pleasant pastel colors, large windows fill the space of the rooms with sunlight, everywhere cleanliness and comfort reign. Each room has a comfortable soft bed, a table, a wardrobe, a large TV and air conditioning. If you came to Moscow with a pet, then, as agreed with the hotel administrator, he can live with you. This is undoubtedly a big plus, as many hotels are not allowed with four-legged pets. Also, the advantages of Marseille include:

  • the presence in the library with a large selection of books of different genres;
  • free parking;
  • inexpensive restaurant with delicious home cooking;
  • the possibility of unlimited use of the Internet.

Price per day: from 2,500. This is quite inexpensive for a mini-hotel in Moscow near the metro.

Noosphere

Recently, hostels are gaining more and more popularity among guests of the capital. This is a kind of mini-hotels with rather comfortable living conditions and unrealistically low prices. For example, the hostel "Noosphere". Located in a prime location, it is surrounded by various attractions. Among them: Vorobyovy Gory, Gorky Park, Drama Theater "Vernadsky 13". There are also many cafes and a large shopping center "Capitol". From here you can get anywhere in the city. After all, next to the Universitet metro hotel (Moscow).

Inside the hostel is very comfortable and clean. Large shared kitchen, which has all the necessary equipment for cooking, as well as sets of dishes. Having prepared to eat, you can enjoy your masterpiece by going to the spacious dining area. And then go to the living room with a large TV, various board games and interesting books. There are always a lot of people in hostels, a lively atmosphere reigns here. You can pay for the bed in the common room (there are 4-, 6-, 8- and 12-seater). Or choose an option without a lot of outsiders - double room.

Price: from 750 rubles per day. The hostel is waiting for visitors at 15 Vernadsky Avenue.

Hotel Marco

Many guests of the capital who managed to live in this hotel consider it the best. They come here again and advise the Marco Hotel to their friends. The rooms are decorated in a classic style. Chocolate and white colors predominate. The walls are decorated with beautiful paintings. Also in each room there are vases with interesting plants.

In total, the hotel has 10 rooms: double with a shared bed, double with two separate beds and a suite. Each room has upholstered furniture, a modern TV, telephone and air conditioning. Room rates: from 3,900 per day. The hotel is located at 6. Garibaldi. It is near the Novye Cheryomushki metro station.

"Sretenskaya"

A unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the atmosphere of everyday life from Russian folk tales, gives its guest the hotel "Sretenskaya". There is no such interior in any hotel in Moscow. Hand-made stucco molding, forged handrails, carved oak furniture and the main feature is the winter garden. It is so nice to relax here at the end of a difficult day. Residents of the hotel will be able to visit the restaurant with the best dishes of Russian cuisine, work out in the fitness room or lie in the therapeutic sauna.

Rooms are cleaned daily. You can choose: standard, junior suite and suite. They vary in size. There are two rooms in the junior suites, three in the suite. These rooms also have minibars. Cost: from 5 000 rubles per day. The hotel is located in Moscow near the metro Chistye Prudy, at Sretenka, 15.

Rules of residence

Each hotel in Moscow has certain rules of conduct, the violation of which may lead to the fact that guests are asked to leave the hotel. Observe them carefully, and then your vacation will not overshadow anything!

  1. Flammable objects must not be carried into the area.
  2. No smoking in rooms. There are special places on the street for this, not far from the hotel.
  3. Many hotels do not allow pets. This opportunity needs to be discussed with the administrator.
  4. Respect the staff and other hotel guests.
