Zoo in Bolsherechye: from the school zoo to the state zoo

The Bolsherechensk Zoo is the main attraction in the Omsk region for animal lovers. Today, you can spend the whole day here with the whole family, watching the diverse representatives of the fauna brought from around the world. When was the zoo in Bolsherechye founded, and how to get here on a tour these days?

From the "corner of nature" to the largest zoo in the region

The Bolsherechensk Zoo is currently the only settlement zoo in our country. Many of its visitors are surprised why such an object was built not in the regional center, but in an ordinary village. In fact, in the Bolshereche Zoo appeared completely by accident. In 1984, a zoo corner was created in the village school number 2. The project was handled by a group of enthusiastic students led by a biology teacher. The first inhabitants of the zoo are familiar to all pets and wild animals found with injuries and unable to survive in their natural habitat. The school project was liked by Valery Solomatin, who was then the chairman of the district executive committee. Thanks to his assistance, the zoo corner a few years after its creation received its own territory, and then the status of the state. In Bolsherechye, the zoo was decided to be located in a very picturesque place - the floodplain of the Bolshaya River. Here he is today.

Cost of visit and opening hours

The zoo is open daily, from Tuesday to Sunday inclusive, Monday is a day off. In the summer season, you can watch animals from 10.00 to 18.00, and in the winter - from 10.00 to 16.00. Children under 4 years old can attend the free zoo. Monthly campaigns are also held, during which large families can get acquainted with the inhabitants of the zoo without payment. How much are entrance fees for visitors who do not have benefits? Children aged 4 to 12 years can look at animals for only 100 rubles. All guests over 12 years old are equated with the category of "adults", the entrance for them costs 250 rubles. In Bolshereche, the zoo has an operating mode that is convenient for everyone. As in many similar organizations, there are much fewer visitors on weekdays than on weekends. If you want to watch all the animals without haste, you should come to the zoo no later than 2 hours before its closure.

How to get to the Bolsherechensky Zoo?

The exact address of the zoo is Omsk Region, the village of Bolsherechye, Sovetov Street, 67. The distance from the regional center to this facility is about 200 kilometers. Be sure to visit the local zoo during a trip to Omsk. In Bolshereche, the zoo boasts well-groomed territory and an interesting collection of animals. An excursion to this place will give a lot of positive emotions and will be remembered for a long time. By public transport, to Bolsherechye from Omsk can be reached by suburban buses. The stop in the village is made by the routes following from Omsk to the following settlements: Ust-Ishim, Tevriz, Znamenka, Tara. From the bus station in Bolsherechye to the zoo can be reached on foot in 10 minutes. You can get there by private car by plotting the map on the geographical coordinates 56.091607; 74.639103.
