Crafts from droppers: step-by-step instructions for weaving

Very often in Soviet times, unusual craftsmen caught sight of fish, frogs, robots, etc., which were made from the most common droppers. What did not invent the craftsmen lying in the hospital wards. The tubes were stained with iodine, brilliant green or potassium permanganate.



Craftsmen constantly improved their skills and created a wide variety of crafts from droppers: trinkets, pendants, pens and fish in this case are not the limit of perfection. Now this ability can be called endangered. He was replaced by ready-made more elegant kits for weaving from plastic threads with additional accessories, but the principle remained unchanged.

What can be made from droppers

Talented craftsmen create entire installations from droppers. However, newcomers to this business should first of all study the schemes and try themselves in making the simplest crafts from a dropper, the instruction in this case will become the main assistant.

Funny little devil from a dropper

These baubles were made very often. Everything depended on a flight of imagination and sleight of hand. A fascinating process could brighten up the time spent in the hospital. The main thing is to find a dropper.

The easiest way to make a handle is to make square weaving around the paste, and you're done. In the same way, you can make a cute little devil, adding to the weaving head, legs, arms and tail.



Crafts from a dropper: a workshop on the manufacture of a devil

You can get a naughty little devil by strictly following the step-by-step recommendations:

  • The first step is to make pens and legs. To do this, cut off two pieces of a dropper of the required length. After that, using small scissors, the plastic tubes are cut in a spiral, moving in a circle. This work should begin, stepping back from the beginning about two cm, the tip is excised with scissors, imitating fingers. Indentation is first needed in order to attach parts to the body.
  • The torso and the head of the little devil are woven the same in the form of a Chinese knot. Two tubes are laid crosswise, four working tips are obtained. The first tube is placed on the second from above, the second on the third from above, the third on the first down and into the loop. The tips are laid in a square, they all need to be pulled a little into the knot. Similar manipulations are repeated until the details are of the required length. Creating such crafts from droppers, the main thing is to remember to insert the pens and legs in the right places.
  • The head is attached to the body with a needle from a dropper, removing its tip. The part can be planted right in the center or slightly pushed forward.
  • Finishing work, the remaining ends on the body are cut, and on the head two ends are cut to the root, and horns are made from the others, cutting them diagonally.

DIY do-it-yourself dropper crafts: goldfish

About twenty years ago, fish from droppers could be found everywhere. But, unfortunately, time passed, and the goldfish sailed away. However, you can nostalgic and recall these simple crafts, in addition, a similar master class can be used for classes with children, they will be delighted with funny figures.



Instructions for making fish

  1. Similar crafts from droppers can be made independently. First of all, you need to make a foundation for which two tubes of 35 cm long are used.
  2. One of the tubes is cut on both sides. On the one hand - into eight strips of 10 cm long, on the other - into four strips of fifteen cm. The second tube is similarly cut.
  3. Next, you need to make an eye, in the future, when weaving, you need to consider its size. For this, a wheel is used that adjusts the frequency of the drops, as well as two tubes five and three cm long.
  4. Tubes are cut along the entire length, a fringe is made on a small one - it will serve as eyelashes.
  5. Cilia are placed on the ringlet, after which a long tube is wound around the resulting base. It should be borne in mind that the cut part will be twisted, so it must be wound in such a way that the strip does not bend toward the eye, but out.
  6. After that, you should start making the base of the fish. The long tube from the second dropper needs to be cut along the entire length, in addition two tubes that were previously prepared will be needed. They are wrapped in the resulting strip several times, thereby fixing together. After that, both tubes are wrapped in turn, using weaving in the form of a figure eight.
  7. After that, the two base tubes are twisted together, creating the shape of a fish, taking into account the place in the eye. The remaining ponytails need to be fixed, for this they are sent along the sides towards the tail and capture the strip when weaving the figure eight.
  8. It remains to insert the eye and fix it with the help of additional eights.

Creating such crafts from droppers, you should think about additional decorative elements. You can make a crown by also cutting a part from a dropper. The tail and fins are curled with scissors, and the goldfish is ready.
