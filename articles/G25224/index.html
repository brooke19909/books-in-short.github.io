Hotel Memory Boutique Hotel 4 * (Greece, Hersonissos, Crete): reviews and photos

Memory Boutique Hotel 4 * is a modern, recently refurbished hotel complex located in Hersonissos. This city is located in the north of Crete. This small, cozy hotel has been liked by many lovers of good relaxation, comfortable rooms and decent service. All this is really present here. However, first things first.

Memory Boutique Hotel 4 * is in a good location. The airport is located relatively close to it: only 25 kilometers. This is far enough so that the planes do not interfere with the rest of tourists with their take-offs and landings, but quite close - you can get there in 20 minutes by car.
