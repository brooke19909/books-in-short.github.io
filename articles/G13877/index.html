Hotel Ariane Apts 3 (Greece, Crete)

The comfortable resort complex Ariane Apts invites you to visit fans of a romantic and active holiday. The hotel management offers its visitors a rich infrastructure, improved living conditions on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, a variety of entertainment, designed for a wide age audience.





Hotel Ariane Apts is one of the most modern and prestigious in Crete. Due to its convenient location, tourists will have the opportunity to independently explore the local attractions of the resort and visit the significant and majestic sites of the country. Attentive and friendly staff will gladly provide a huge range of both service and entertainment services.





During its existence (since 1990), the Ariane Apts 3 Hersonissos complex has proved itself to be the best. Every year, millions of foreigners come here from different countries to enjoy a measured rest, enjoy the picturesque nature and snorkel. The hotel has a lot of vacationers with small children, as the guesthouse has all the amenities for a fun and comfortable stay with the kids.

You do not have to worry about food and entertainment for children, as the hotel staff has already done this for you. Young people will not get bored here either, because within walking distance there are night clubs and discos where you can have fun and relax and recharge with a positive. The best way to get to the complex is from the airport of Heraklion, which is only 28 km from the village of Hersonissos.





Number of rooms

The stylish three-story building offers 46 spacious apartments of completely different categories. For couples with children, bedroom apartments are best suited - 60 m2 with separate sleeping and living areas. This luxurious room also has a kitchen with appliances, a balcony and a bathroom.

Each air-conditioned room at Ariane Apts 3 has a private shower and toilet. In more expensive apartments you will find a TV, safe, telephone. For children, the hotel staff will provide a separate bed. From the windows of their rooms, clients can enjoy stunning views of the promenade and the sea.





Nutrition

Visitors to the complex, at their discretion, can choose for themselves an acceptable type of food: half board or all inclusive. The menu is fully balanced and provides a wide selection of different dishes for absolutely every taste. Also, guests can always have a tasty meal and have a good time in a cozy restaurant or cafe (comfortable chairs for children).

Beach

Less than 100 meters from the building of the complex there is a public sand and pebble beach with paid amenities (deck chairs, umbrellas, mattresses). There is a water entertainment center and a diving club on the coast.





Additional Information

In addition to the exotic greenery and stylish building, the beautiful landscaped grounds of Ariane Apts Guesthouse include an outdoor pool, a comfortable terrace with benches and a large car park. For guests there is a medical office, a 24-hour reception, a laundry department and wireless Internet (free).

Parents of babies can always use the services of an experienced nanny (for a fee) and a games room if they wish. And outdoor enthusiasts will love the billiard room, table tennis and sports fields. In addition, a Russian bathhouse and a Turkish sauna (hammam) are open in the building.

Digest

The prestigious and modern tourist complex Ariane Apts, according to most tourists, is great for a budget holiday with your family. Good conditions, friendly staff and lots of amazing entertainment will give you an unforgettable experience and a storm of positive emotions.

If you are looking for an inexpensive and comfortable hotel in Greece, we recommend that you visit this guesthouse. The biggest advantages of the complex are its proximity to the beach and the central part of the island, comfortable rooms with private facilities, friendly staff, and delicious food.
