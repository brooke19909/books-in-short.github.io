The recreation center "Tungus" (Nakhodka): resorts of Primorye

Nakhodka is a city in the Primorsky Territory, which can rightfully be considered one of the most popular places of mass tourism. Every year a huge number of people come here from different parts of our country to enjoy the beauties of the local nature. All kinds of specialized institutions are open here to accommodate numerous visitors, among which the Tungus recreation center (Nakhodka) is noticeably distinguished. Judging by the reviews of tourists, it really deserves special attention.

Location of the institution

The recreation center "Tungus" (Nakhodka) is located almost at the very extreme point of the Difficult peninsula. Here, the pristine untouched beauty of the coastal hills and modern tourist infrastructure are quite harmoniously combined.





People come here to enjoy the silence and pleasant coolness of the local forests, breathe in the fresh clean air and listen to the enchanting birdsong. This wealth is abundant here. And how pleasant it is to meet sunsets and sunrises, admiring the unique sea landscapes. The recreation center "Tungus" (Nakhodka) is a place where you can really relax your soul away from all the worries and annoying city noise. People who want to forget about business and merge with nature, enjoying every minute of such unity, seek this place. It is not difficult to do this, since the base works, in fact, all year round.

Accommodations

The tourist complex has a two-level location. On a hill in an oak forest there are residential houses, and below are two beaches (sandy and rocky). A narrow path 70 meters long leads to the first. The second is a little further. Yes, and getting to it is more difficult. You must go down the steep stairs. The recreation center "Tungus" (Nakhodka) offers tourists various living conditions. Here for visitors there is:

  1. Hotel with double and quadruple rooms. Each of them has a bed, refrigerator, TV, air conditioning, as well as a separate bathroom with shower and toilet.
  2. Small VIP houses. This is a one-room premise with an open veranda and a bathroom, in which a modern shower cabin and an individual water heater are installed.
  3. Large VIP houses. They have a living room and two separate bedrooms. Log houses without additional interior decoration create a special flavor. It is complemented by simple but very comfortable furniture, made in a rustic style.
  4. Housing "Town House" with "economy rooms", which are designed for 3 or 4 people. The room has only beds, a table, stools and a small refrigerator. And additional amenities (bathroom) are located in the base.

Due to this diversity, every tourist can choose a room to his liking.

Opinions of visitors

Hundreds of tourists annually host the recreation center "Tungus" (Nakhodka). Reviews of tourists about visiting this place are mostly positive. Firstly, all without exception note the indescribable beauty of the local nature.





This simply paradise on the seashore with a cozy beach framed by picturesque pristine forests cannot but arouse admiration. Secondly, it is worth noting the friendly and caring staff who daily tries to make the guests of the base comfortable and as pleasant as possible. In addition, security is provided for visitors. Therefore, all guests during the holidays feel as protected as possible. But there are a number of negative points that are also worth mentioning. There were cases when tourists complained, for example, about an untimely change of linen. Yes, and sometimes it was necessary to cover his guests independently. Room cleaning also sometimes needed to be reminded. Although this service is initially included in the price of the permit. But these are just special cases, on which you should not judge the general level of the offered service. In general, according to most tourists, rest at this base is worth the money spent on it.

Service offered

What does the recreation center "Tungus" (Nakhodka) offer its many visitors? Everything here is designed so that guests can spend their free time with maximum comfort:

  1. For your stay there are cozy houses or rooms. They can be booked in advance by first reading the price list.
  2. For visitors with children there is a playground with slides, sandpits and swings on site.
  3. A spacious pool with a transparent roof allows you to take water treatments in any weather.
  4. Two beaches allow each resident to choose the most convenient vacation option. It all depends on personal preference. On the sandy beach you can calmly sunbathe and swim, enjoying the light pleasant sea breeze. So basically couples with children rest. The rocky beach is popular with those who like diving.
  5. The forest here is a real paradise for mushroom pickers. Such walks leave a lasting impression.
  6. Those who wish can ride horses or ATVs for an additional fee, as well as rent an air mattress.




The base staff tries to do everything so that visitors are satisfied with the rest.

Rest in Primorye

Today, many managed to get enough rest in the Crimea, the Caucasus or various exotic countries. Nowadays, one cannot surprise anyone with this. Therefore, every year there are more and more people who want to relax, enjoying the beauty of their native forests. This opportunity is fully provided by the recreation center "Tungus" (Nakhodka). Primorsky Krai has for some time become a place of true pilgrimage for tourists.





People come here even from the central regions of our country, having traveled tens of thousands of kilometers. And those who live nearby can spend their days off here. After all, there is nothing better after a hard working week than to be in a fabulous corner, where there is no city noise and fuss. You can calmly wander in the forest, breathing fresh air in full, or sunbathe on the beach, enjoying the warm sun and the sounds of the surf. Some people like to come here in large companies to celebrate, for example, a birthday or any other celebration. To do this, you can even ask a local chef to prepare amazing national dishes from fresh lamb. Such a holiday will undoubtedly remain in the memory for a long time. Recently, more and more people have appeared who are beginning to understand and appreciate just this type of relaxation.
