Cross stitch camomiles. Schemes of different difficulty levels

Flowers are able to decorate life and make it brighter. Fresh flowers give as a gift. Not a single holiday can do without them. One of the most popular plants on the planet is chamomile. It refers to the field gifts of flora. A sweet and delicate flower rarely leaves anyone indifferent.

Cross Stitch

Meadow daisies are a symbol of freshness and chastity. Such flowers add coziness and positiveness when they are present on kitchen or room textiles as embroidered decorations.

The applique will look great on a linen tablecloth. Daisies on cross-stitch look very succinctly. The main thing to remember: flower petals are always white, so the fabric should not merge with them. It is best to take flax without bleaching. Combinations with other wildflowers look good. For instance:

  • blue cornflowers, shading the whiteness of daisies;
  • lilac flowers of oregano, beautifully combined with greens;
  • yellow Hypericum flowers, emphasizing the brightness of the middle of the main flower, etc.

It is very easy to choose patterns for cross-stitching daisies for tacks, napkins, hand towels. Textiles with a similar pattern fit perfectly into the village style. As for clothes and accessories, daisies look very nice on fabric handbags, sundresses, and children's clothes for girls.

Cross-stitch looks beautiful on textile bracelets and light ladies' hats. There are many uses for such art. For example, you can embroider a postcard to relatives with a pleasant wish or with a drawing for the holiday. Cross-stitch daisies look beautiful on dark fabric items.

What is the best place to start?

In order to start embroidering with a cross, you can use a simple pattern that depicts a small bush consisting of a large flower, several ovaries and greenery. It is better to start a simple cross stitch pattern from the center of a large flower. Such a technique will help to detect errors in time and correct them. To facilitate the work, in the early stages you can use a special canvas. For embroidery of white flowers you need to take the material of a dark color.
