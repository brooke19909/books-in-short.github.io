Rayan Hotel 4 * (UAE / Sharjah): description, photos and reviews

UAE - a magical Arab country in which you can see real miracles. Every year millions of tourists come here to get in touch with the culture of the East, as well as to see luxury that is not found anywhere else in the world. Here you can also make a grandiose shopping. In addition, the Emirates are one of the popular tourist destinations for the purpose of beach holidays, especially in winter. Of course, most tourists go to Dubai, but there are other emirates where it is very comfortable and interesting, for example, Sharjah. Sharjah Rayan Hotel 4 * is an excellent hotel that is suitable for those who want to get maximum comfort, but at a reasonable price.



Sharjah Anti-Dubai

This emirate is the opposite of the capital of world shopping. This is a completely "dry" emirate with strict morals. It is forbidden to drink alcohol, wearing frank outfits, cheeky behavior, etc. There are even non-smoking hotels, for example, Rayan Hotel Sharjah 4 *. Sharjah is rich in its excursion program, and those who come here know about it and are ready to deny themselves some unhealthy pleasures for this. Sharjah is the cultural capital of the country. There are many unique museums, theaters and galleries, a visit to which will bring you a lot of positive emotions and will restore the picture of the past. Beautiful and noteworthy are modern architecture, skyscrapers, a futuristic picture of the city, original fountains, and, of course, the eastern market - a world of wonders. By the way, Sharjah borders on Dubai and, if you wish, you can reach the capital of shopping in a matter of minutes. As for natural resources, this is the only emirate that is located on the shores of both the Persian and the Gulf of Oman, by the way, the latter belongs to the Indian Ocean basin.

General overview of hotels Sharjah

Here, in comparison with Dubai, there are much fewer hotels. Only 12 are coastal: 9 on the shores of the Persian Gulf and 3 on the Khaled Gulf. All other hotels are urban, and the aforementioned Rayan Hotel Sharjah 4 * (UAE / Sharjah) applies to them. However, tourists can always have a fruitful time on the beach of any of the hotels with access to the sea. In addition, there are many inexpensive hotels in Sharjah that have pretty good service, especially considering the price-quality ratio. Nevertheless, the most popular are still 4-star hotels such as Rayan Hotel 4 *.



We're going to the Ryan Hotel: how to get there?

In Sharjah, there is an international airport where planes arrive from all over the world, including from Russia. However, you can also take flights to Dubai or to the capital of the country - Abu Dhabi. However, it is most convenient to fly to Sharjah Airport - this is the basis for low-cost flights, in addition, it is only 15 minutes from the city line. A taxi will take you to the hotel in 7-8 minutes, and you will only have to pay 50 AED. However, for guests of the Rayan Hotel 4 * hotel, a shuttle service is always organized. This means that you will be met at the airport upon arrival and comfortably delivered to the hotel.

Rayan Hotel Sharjah 4 *: general description

As already noted, this hotel belongs to the urban type, is a four-star with a high level of service and is designed for non-smokers. This means that in all public areas and in most rooms, smoking is strictly prohibited. However, it has smoking rooms. In terms of soundproofing, the hotel can put 5 points.



Location

Rayan Hotel 4 * is located in the emirate of Sharjah, a 15-minute drive from the international airport. To Dubai - 25 min. drive. Sharjah Mega Mall is 550 meters away. Exact hotel address: United Arab Emirates, Sharjah city, Al Wahda. The nearest beach can be reached either by taxi or by bus.

Number of rooms

The Rayan Hotel 4 (Sharjah / United Arab Emirates) has 196 comfortable rooms of various categories (single and double rooms of the standard category, single and double deluxe rooms, executive suite and junior suite, junior suites, family rooms), the cost of which ranges from 3, 5 thousand to 7000 rubles. As you can see, the prices, although not low, are affordable, especially considering the degree of comfort.

By the way, among the rooms there are rooms for people with disabilities, and, as already noted, the hotel has rooms for smokers. All residential premises at the Rayan Hotel 4 * are furnished with modern and very comfortable furniture. In all, without exception, there are plasma TVs, free Internet, air conditioning, a safe and a minibar with soft drinks, a well-equipped bathroom with a bath or shower, a hairdryer and a full set of toiletries. Room service is available around the clock, cleaning and linen change are daily, you can order concierge services, a fresh newspaper, etc.

Rayan Hotel Sharjah 4 *: reviews and ratings of tourists

If you read the comments of tourists who have already visited this hotel, it immediately becomes clear that this hotel is rather good than bad. And it all starts with the staff. Tourists say that all the hotel staff are very friendly. They are very responsible for their duties, cleaning is carried out carefully, they never forget to refresh towels, change linen, etc. According to the stories of tourists, there are no minibars in the rooms, and there is only a refrigerator where you can store purchased drinks and food.

There is dissatisfaction with the scarcity of television channels. However, do not forget that you are in an Arab country, and Rayan Hotel 4 * is no exception. The reviews also say that from the side of the neighboring mosque several times a day there are calls for the beginning of prayer (prayer). However, this is a normal occurrence for this country, and this must be reconciled. And tourists complain about the not very convenient location of the hotel. We are talking about its remoteness from the coast. Guests have to take a taxi to the beach. Of course, this is not very convenient.
