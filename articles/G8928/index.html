How to learn to play poker on your own?

By reviewing many films, delving into the history of various countries, we begin to understand that card games have existed since ancient times. They played, play, and will always play ... Such an activity has a place anywhere, in any team. The most popular and widespread game for centuries has been poker. This is not only an interesting pastime, but also a good option for additional income. Let's try to understand how to learn how to play poker, being a beginner in this field.



A bit of history

The exact date of the emergence of poker as a single game is currently unknown. There are many versions and assumptions regarding the origin of this entertainment, but they are all so contradictory that one can only guess where the truth is and where the lie ...



The progenitor of poker can be called the primero game, or la prime, popular in European countries. It was the beginning of the 16th century. Italy, Spain, France were overwhelmed with excitement, they began to bet in card entertainments ... In the 18th century, references to a game similar to today's poker appeared. Five cards participated here, and bluffing became an integral part of the then English bragg, German pochshpil, French so far. Not only courtiers and people of high ranks were fond of such a game, but also ordinary peasants, and even sailors.

World famous

They started playing poker for money from the moment it appeared. Experiencing and at the same time amusing, each gambler tried to deceive his rivals and receive any material reward for this.

This game became especially popular in America in the middle of the 19th century. The rules have been changed, a deck of 52 cards has already been used. Then in the USA, poker began to be considered a national game.





Since the middle of the 2nd century, a wave of excitement has covered the whole world. In every country poker has been modified and supplemented, whole clubs have been formed. Slavic countries, where it became fashionable to play Russian poker, were no exception.

Pleasure or earnings?

If you have a question about how to learn how to play poker, then first of all you should decide what goal you are pursuing. And already here it is important not to make the main mistake ... Players with experience claim that poker is a whole art. Knowing all the winning combinations is not enough. It is simply impossible to play poker for money without talent, special skills and cheating techniques.

All newcomers should come to terms with the idea that they will first have to incur significant material losses, and only with time, with the acquisition of "card experience", the game will begin to reciprocate and bring a certain profit. The phrase "Beginners are lucky" does not always work here!

What is necessary for a successful game

Many people wonder how to learn how to play poker quickly and without significant monetary losses. To answer it, you should list the main qualities that a real gambling ace should have:

How interesting is playing poker with a computer?

In the modern world, machines in many industries replace people. They did not ignore human entertainment. Such a great card game, like poker, is held today even without real rivals. You can compete with a machine that does not know how to bluff and does not give out any emotions.



Everyone who has tried playing poker with a computer will confirm that it is extremely difficult to get real pleasure from such a process. However, to acquire the skills of the game is quite possible. As a training option, such a process is 100% suitable.

Today there are quite a lot of specialized sites where absolutely real people play poker, only they are not at the same table in the same room, but in one virtual world. Such entertainment is quite suitable as one of the options for entertaining pastime.
