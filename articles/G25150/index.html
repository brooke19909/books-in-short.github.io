Oasis Beach Club 4 Hv hotel: description and reviews

Description: Oasis Beach Club 4 Hv Hotel is perfect for both family and youth recreation. This is a wonderful club-style hotel, which offers its guests not only a pleasant atmosphere and comfortable rooms, but also the opportunity to have fun and enjoy the exquisite nature.





The hotel complex Oasis Beach Club 4 Hv (Turkey) is a huge territory on which there are nine large three-story buildings. In addition, there are large pools with warm water, playgrounds for games and recreation, as well as shops, restaurants and other premises.

Oasis Beach Club 4 Hv Hotel is located in one of the most beautiful and cozy resort areas called Konakla. The famous city of Alanya is only 12 kilometers away. The international airport is a little further, 115 kilometers from the hotel.

Rooms: As already mentioned, this hotel is quite large - in its territory there are 1150 rooms of standard club type. All rooms stand out for their beautiful decor, comfortable furniture and excellent condition.

The room has a private bathroom. Here you will find a comfortable bathtub, as well as a toilet, washbasin, shower and toiletries.

The room itself is also well equipped. You can watch the usual satellite channels on TV and make long-distance and international calls. There is also air conditioning, which will help maintain a pleasant cool temperature in the room. A minibar will offer a large selection of delicious drinks.

By the way, from your room you can go to the balcony, which offers beautiful views of the beach and the sea or the courtyard. Room service is available around the clock. If you wish, you can order the service "alarm clock".





Meals : Club Hotel Oasis Beach Club 4 Hv operates on a standard all-inclusive system. Three times a day, hotel guests are invited to a generous table. Exotic fruits, delicious seafood, traditional Turkish snacks and sweets await you here. There are two excellent restaurants with a luxurious oriental atmosphere and a really large selection of dishes.

Worth a visit and local bars. Here you can taste exquisite wines, exotic cocktails, as well as real Turkish coffee with ice cream and sweets.

Beach : Oasis Beach Club 4 Hv has its own sandy beach, which can be reached in a few minutes. A sufficient number of sun loungers and umbrellas are located here for guests, which can be used for free. Naturally, there are playgrounds for beach volleyball, as well as the opportunity to ride on a boat, boat and catamaran. If you wish, you can enjoy the beauty of the underwater world - there is a training program for diving, as well as all the necessary equipment.

Additional information : Hotel Oasis Beach Club 4 Hv offers its customers everything you need for a really good vacation. To begin with, it is worth noting that there is parking, a currency exchange and the ability to rent a car.





As already mentioned, the complex has several large pools interconnected. In one of them are equipped with excellent water slides that will deliver many pleasant minutes to both children and their parents. There is also a fitness club and gym, as well as excellent massage rooms. You can always visit the bathhouse or sauna.

The hotel has its own animation group, which constantly organizes fun shows, contests and concerts.

Reviews: In fact, tourists quite often choose the Oasis Beach Club 4 Hv hotel. Reviews about this place are for the most part positive. First of all, guests note the excellent living conditions - the rooms are spacious, comfortable and kept clean. In addition, they praise generous food and a variety of dishes, as well as impeccable service.
