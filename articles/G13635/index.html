Cableway Olympus in Gelendzhik: reviews and address

Funiculars are a quick way to travel, as well as one of the most entertaining attractions. In an open chair, you fly over the abyss and inspect the surroundings from a bird's eye view. An unforgettable adventure, especially for those who are afraid of heights. In this case, the tourist also receives a fair dose of adrenaline. Whether you want to experience these feelings again is up to you. But definitely worth a try. The Olympus cable car in Gelendzhik is one of the most popular attractions. From here you can shoot the city. Photos are very beautiful and bright, they will keep pleasant memories for many years to come.



Intriguing title

As we all remember from mythology, this is the mountain on which the gods lived. The Olympus cable car in Gelendzhik is a half-hour flight above the clouds and stunning views, after which tourists find themselves in an unforgettable amusement park. The described immediately echoes in consciousness with a picture of paradise, and the name becomes completely understandable. The cable car is only the first stage of adventure. An unforgettable day awaits you further. Plan ahead for more free time in advance, you can stay here until the evening, and definitely not get tired.

And again on the road

Our next stop is the Olympus cable car (Gelendzhik). How to get to it, we will now consider. A free bus is coming from the city. You can use your own transport, then your path will lie along the bypass road. Follow the signs and do not get lost. Just near the bypass road begins the park "Olympus". You can leave the car right at the entrance. If you are interested in Safari Park, then go to the crosswalk with the corresponding inscription.



Adventure begin

The cable car in Gelendzhik is known to everyone, you can ask any passerby and get an answer. Exact address: Kupriyanova Slit, Building 1. A magnificent park awaits you at the top, but tourists begin to surprise tourists even at the foot of the mountain. A bowling club is open here so that you can have fun and interesting time while waiting for the trip. The cost of the game is relatively low, so you can afford it. There is also a recreational attraction. Tourists can drink mineral water for free and even swim in it. A stocked pool will give fans the opportunity to sit with a fishing rod. Or you can go to a crocodile farm and look at the reptiles nearby.



Description

The Olympus cable car in Gelendzhik is one of the most interesting attractions. Many compare it with a similar one in Sochi, which leads to Krasnaya Polyana. First of all, it is shorter, but the views that open to tourists are hardly less picturesque.

The Olympus cableway in Gelendzhik is a semblance of a funicular. Only here are open benches with iron stops and a footrest. From the outside it looks scary enough when people soar over the abyss, without any visible insurance. In fact, it is almost impossible to get out of the seats, even if you try hard. The speed is small, no rocking is provided. Judging by the reviews, even tourists with a fear of heights and those who hit the funicular in bad weather do not refuse to visit again. Considering the opening views, it is difficult not to forget about all these little things.



Pay attention to the weather.

It is advisable to get acquainted with weather forecasts in advance. The fact is that the cable car (Olimp Park, Gelendzhik) runs at high altitude. On a foggy and windy day, pleasure can be spoiled by the fact that visibility will be much worse. Photos will turn out not so bright, and sharp gusts of wind can frighten even the most daring traveler. After heavy rain, there may be dampness and haze, due to which visibility deteriorates sharply.

It should be borne in mind that at altitude it is completely uncomfortable on a cool day, and, conversely, the sun's rays bake in the heat. Therefore, be sure to stock up on things based on the weather. Hats with fastening, so as not to be blown away by the wind, umbrellas, sunglasses and warm clothes, try to foresee what you might need from this. Be sure to choose closed shoes and comfortable clothes, because you do not go to a restaurant, but to a park, where you expect to walk a lot.

Funicular Features

There are two tracks here, so we want to tell you about both. However, in order not to choose, it is better to ride on one and the other. First of all, we are interested in the Olympus cableway (Gelendzhik). Opening hours: from 8:00 to 02:00. Night trips are an adventure for an amateur, but they are in demand every year. However, the most beautiful views are here in the morning and evening hours. They are also good because at this time it is not very hot. This is important, because on a hot day, traveling under the scorching sun can be a big load, especially if there are health problems.

The length of this track is 1140 meters, and it appeared later than the second one, located in the Safari Park. In Olympus, it runs a little lower, and the journey itself will take less time. However, both paths will show you stunningly beautiful places of the Markotkh Range.



Advantages of the cable car in the Olympus Park

On both tracks, children under 5 years old can ride for free, professional photographs are taken and there are viewing platforms with stunning views. The Olympus cable car (Gelendzhik), which every tourist must reach, has an advantage primarily in terms of operating time. The ticket price includes a visit to the amusement park, as well as an excursion. Finally, the cost of a children's ticket is attractive. If the child is 5 to 12 years old, you get a ticket for 250 rubles. For many tourists, the Olympus cable car (Gelendzhik) remained the most beautiful in memory. Reviews emphasize that even in bad weather, the spectacular views in its spectacle completely overshadowed all the inconvenience. Although for the first time it can be a little scary.
