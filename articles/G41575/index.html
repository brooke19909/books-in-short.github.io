The concept and types of social norms accepted in society

The concept and classification of social norms begin from the moment when they are clearly distinguished from the second type of norms - technical.

The rules of society are laws governing the relationship between human behavior and the actions of an organization. They help society function harmoniously and appropriately, achieve together what a person cannot achieve alone.

Social norms arose a long time ago, developed in parallel with the development of human society and were transmitted from one generation to another. The reason for their appearance was the need to control the behavior of people using rules common to most.

The concept and types of social norms depend on two criteria:

- the method of their creation, occurrence;
- ways to protect them from violations.

The concept and signs of social norms:

Firstly, these are established rules of behavior in society. The state, organizations or collective of people indicate how people should behave, what actions to perform, and which not. In accordance with these patterns, they correct their own behavior, they are equal.
