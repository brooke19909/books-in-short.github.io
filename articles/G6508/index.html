LCD "Zodiac" (Moscow): description, reviews

Moscow - the capital of our country and a huge metropolis - does not cease to grow and be built up with more new and modern residential complexes. One of these is the Zodiac LCD.





Location of the complex

The Leader construction company has chosen a place on the Volokolamsk Highway in the Pokrovskoye ‚Äì Streshnevo area for the construction of a high-rise building of the Zodiac residential complex. This is a new business class building in the original architectural design. After completion of construction in 2011, the complex immediately won first place in the nomination ‚ÄúThe Best Business Class Complex in Moscow‚Äù.





Its residents who do not have their own vehicles can get to the metro stations Schukinskaya or Tushinskaya, which are located at a distance of just over one kilometer from the Zodiac residential complex. Moscow involves a lot of transport opportunities, including trains. Not far from the complex is the stop "Pokrovskoye-Streshnevo". Thus, you can get to the residential complex quite easily, both on personal and public transport, fixed-route taxi or electric train.

Description of the complex

LCD "Zodiac" is made in the form of three monolithic-brick towers, interconnected by galleries in the middle part. Two of the towers - twenty-six floors and one - thirty. All the windows of the complex overlook the park and the Moscow River, next to which the building was built.





For the convenience and comfort of residents with children, a kindergarten was immediately created with the house. In addition, there are almost all objects of social infrastructure: a pharmacy, a shop, and dry cleaning. The developer did not ignore the owners of cars. For them, an underground three-level parking for four hundred and eighty places and an open guest parking are provided.

Parents can not worry about the safety of their children, as the local area is fenced and guarded. In addition, it is equipped with a video surveillance and access control system. Guest parking is also under guard.

LCD "Zodiac" is equipped with passenger and freight elevators, for the convenience of residents descending and ascending directly from the underground parking. So there is no need to go out. Facades of the complex are ventilated. According to the plan of the developer, they are highlighted in the evening, which emphasizes the individuality of the project and its class.

House territory

The residents of the complex have a place to take a walk in their own home area in their free time . The developer provided for its landscape design, including decorative paths, landscaping. Playgrounds have been built. The complex immediately became serviced by the management company.

Construction technology

LCD "Zodiac" embodies the best achievements of building innovations. It is made using a monolithic-frame technology using the most modern engineering achievements. The energy supply and water supply systems in the building are autonomous, which allows us not to depend on seasonal shutdowns of heat and not to experience inconvenience from interruptions in water.

Apartments LCD "Zodiac" offers both standard layout and with rounded walls. Part of the complex itself is rounded. The developer also provided for business-class housing - twelve exclusive penthouses. All residential premises are equipped with telecommunication and fire extinguishing systems.

Ecological situation

Despite the close proximity to the Volokolamsk highway, the Zodiak LCD, the reviews about the comfort of living in it and the clean air are true. Near the complex there is a large park Pokrovskoye-Streshnevo, several small parks. Green spaces in them create a favorable ecological environment. Also nearby is the Khimka River and the Canal named after Moscow.

Housing estate infrastructure

The complex is located at some distance from the metro stations "Tushinskaya", "Schukinskaya", which can be reached both by personal transport and by taxi. The owners of the first can take advantage of Volokolamsk highway, passing almost next to the building.





The rest of the infrastructure is also well developed. A few meters away is a hospital. Across the street there is a secondary school and one more nearby. In addition to the kindergarten located in the residential complex itself, there are also similar institutions in the district. There is nothing to say about parks and recreation areas. There are a lot of shops and hypermarkets in the district.

When moving or acquiring an apartment in the Zodiac residential complex, there is no need to break away from your usual lifestyle. Since the complex gives its residents all the opportunities for a comfortable existence. For those who work in the center of the metropolis, getting to work will be as convenient as from their previous place of residence. Children will make new friends in comfortable nearby schools. And the parks will give the joy of unhurried evening walks or a pleasant pastime on the picnic with the whole family.
