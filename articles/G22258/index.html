The luxury tax did not appear out of nowhere. On the contrary, it arose with specific intentions and a very long time ago. People who owned luxurious estates, expensive carriages, thoroughbred dogs ... they had to send a certain sum of money to state rulers every year. Odds and calculation

So, before listing the list of cars (luxury tax, as you could already understand, affected many models), you need to tell something about how the amount is calculated and, in principle, what kind of process it is.

In order for the law to be executed in accordance with all the rules, a clear classification was developed. She progressively increases the tax. Less age and more car value - higher tax.

The minimum bar was the mark of three million rubles. From 3 to 5, to be precise. People who bought a car for this amount in the year when they introduced the tax, began to pay a transport tax increased by one and a half times. The lucky ones who became owners of this model a year earlier were more fortunate. Their tax increased only 1.3 times. Owners of the same car with a 3-year experience (that is, the car was purchased the year before last) were completely lucky. The amount of tax for them increased by only 10 percent. And those cars, whose age is more than three years (and if they cost less than 5 million rubles), have ceased to be considered a luxury.

So, the first bar is from 3 to 5 million rubles. The second is higher, from five to ten million. And in the first five years, cars worth that kind of money will be taxed 2 times. But the most expensive category are models, for which the owner gave more than 10 million rubles. Their age can be 10 years or less. In this case, the transport tax increases three times.
