How to find out who the SIM card is registered for: tips and tricks

Today we have to figure out how to find out to whom the SIM card is registered . This task involves some preparation. The investigator will have to tune in to the high probability of failure in obtaining the relevant data. After all, information about the owners of phone numbers just do not spread. There are a number of exceptions, but you do not need to rely on them.

Information Acquisition Methods

How to find out to whom the SIM card is registered? Today, there are many ways to help cope with this task. And a little later we will consider the most common options.

For example, you can learn such techniques:

  • search directories of telephone numbers;
  • re-purchase of base numbers;
  • contacting your mobile operator directly;
  • network data search through third-party verification services;
  • getting information from social networks.

In addition, you can turn to the help of "My Account" on the website of a mobile network operator. All these tricks help to find the owner of the SIM card.

Phonebook

How to find out to whom the SIM card is registered? The first trick is to search for data in the telephone directory. Typically, these books include landline phone numbers, but you can find the base with mobile phones.

The disadvantage of this technique is the rapid obsolescence of information. It is also worth preparing for spending - directories are not free.

Important: information is searched either by the number of the caller or by the name of the citizen. It is advisable to know where the phone is registered. This will help speed up the procedure.

"My Account" and sites of operators

How to find out to whom the SIM card is registered? As already mentioned, a person can easily use the "My Account" on the website of a mobile operator. But first, you have to find out who offered the communication services. This task will not cause any trouble. At the beginning of the combination, specialized network directories determine the network operator.

What's next? Now you need:

  1. Open the official page of the cellular company.
  2. Log in to "My Account" using the appropriate number.
  3. View user profile.

Typically, this technique is used in families. If a person has access to a SIM card, determining its owner will be as easy as shelling pears.

Office call

Where is the mobile number registered? Relevant information is distributed without much hassle. For example, a person can look at the code of a mobile phone. With its help, the region of registration and the company offering cellular services are determined.

It is possible to obtain information about the owner of the SIM card in the office of the respective operator. But such a technique is rarely realized. Operators do not disclose information about their customers. This is possible in exceptional cases.

For example, if the owner of a particular phone is being sought. For example, relatives or government agencies. Then it is enough to contact the office of the cellular company with a passport and certificates indicating the presence of good reason for checking the owner of the phone.

Third Party Services

Sometimes it becomes necessary to find out where the mobile phone number is registered. As we said, this is not the most difficult operation. And they approach it differently.

Quite often, citizens use various inspection sites to translate ideas into reality. Enough:

Sometimes through such web resources it is suggested to see not only where the SIM card is registered, but also information about the operator.
