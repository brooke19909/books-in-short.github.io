How to weave a wreath and do it in accordance with all the rules?

Colorful wreaths of field herbs or elegant wreaths on the door, which have become a familiar home decoration during the New Year holidays ... They can either be purchased ready-made or made independently. We offer a small lesson on how to weave a wreath so that it turns out to be strong and for a long time delighted you with its luxurious appearance.



Wreath on the door

As a rule, spruce branches, as well as various berries (viburnum or mountain ash) are used for their manufacture. You can also take natural cones, dried leaves as a decorative material, or add artificial decorations to the finished product. It all depends on your imagination and the interior of the room in which the wreath will be located. How to weave a wreath on the door? To do this, you need to make a base. It can be made from branches of any tree that is flexible. Willow or young shoots of birch are perfect. Cut the branches, and then soak them for several hours in warm water. During this time, they will become much softer, and you can easily turn them into a ring. That it will become the basis for the wreath. It should be fixed using tape or thin rope. You should not make it too tight, because in this case it will be difficult to decorate. At the same time, the base should be composed of a sufficiently large number of branches. After you manage to complete the first part, we begin to decorate. Add branches of spruce or fir, which will be the first layer of the wreath. They should completely cover the base. Then you need to include decorative elements in the composition and optionally add a satin or polypropylene ribbon. That's all. Now you know how to weave a wreath on the door.

Summer wreath





One of the most popular types. It can be made in minutes in nature or in the park. Of course, if it is not forbidden to pick wildflowers there. How to weave wreaths on the head? There are two ways to do this. In the first case, make the basis, as for a Christmas wreath, and then decorate with ordinary flowers. In the second case, you can weave a wreath in the same way as usually done with dandelions. Choose plants with long and flexible stems. Leave three or four as the base, and then impose each subsequent perpendicular to the base and crosswise encircle the base itself. Then place the stems parallel to the base. And so with each subsequent flower. In order to complete such a product, you only need to connect the last flower with the first. And your luxurious and elegant wreath is ready.

Herb wreath

How to weave a wreath of herbs? The technology is no different from that used for weaving Christmas decorations on the door.





In the same way, first you need to make a base, and then weave various herbs into it. A riot of colors and juicy greens will create a real folk wreath. And now you know how to weave a wreath of herbs. Therefore, each outdoor trip will be able to delight your friends and relatives with colorful and elegant wreaths. Such crafts deliver special joy to children. What could be better than the opportunity to independently assemble plants for a wreath ?!
