Equipment

The package includes the Nokia 5310 itself, a battery (with a capacity of 860 mAh), and a charger. This is for the classics. Now let's look at the add-ons. Among them, you can find a wired stereo headset of average quality. Also included is a cable standard MicroUSB - USB. Depending on the country in which the phone is purchased, the equipment may be supplemented by a microSD card (2 gigabytes). Well, the package ends with a disk on which there is software, and a flyer on which you can find data to enter the Nokia Music Store service.

Dimensions

The dimensions of the device are averaged for such a segment. In all three planes they are 103.8 x 44.7 x 9.9 mm. At the same time, the mass of the device is only 71 grams. Let's face it: the phone is quite thin. If someone had experience with a Sony Ericsson W 880i phone, he will immediately notice the similarity of the two devices in this regard.





The device does not stick out at all in the pockets. He lies there calmly, comfortably. It is quite possible to carry it in pockets and, most likely, is even necessary.

Color schemes

In this regard, the Finnish manufacturer did not bother much. The device is available on the market in just two colors. But this time, not white and black, but blue and red. Probably what is needed for the music line. The corresponding colors were allocated and the area that runs along the perimeter of the screen. There are inserts made of metal panels.

The name of the company printed on the rear surface of the device is engraved in the same color.





Production material

The case of Nokia 5310 XpressMusic is made of plastic, of course. Another, in principle, would be naive to expect. The material is opaque to the touch. It has a dark gray color close to graphite. In general, it should be noted that plastic is not so bad. On the contrary, it is of good quality. That allows you to operate the device for a long time.

The back cover is made in a dot. That's just these points to wipe quite simply. And after that, the presentation of the device deteriorates sharply, since the back cover begins to seem completely worn out. The rest of the case is quite durable and resistant to mechanical stress.

Left side

There is a connector on which you need to connect the charger. This is a 2 millimeter standard network adapter.

Top end

There is a power management key. It is intended to turn the device on and off. Nearby, there is a 3.5 mm jack, in which you need to connect a wired stereo headset to listen to music or radio. Well, the MicroUSB standard connector complements the ensemble, with which the phone can be synchronized with a personal computer or laptop. For protection, it is covered with a plastic plug.

Rear panel

It has a camera eye (2 megapixel resolution). Unfortunately, the company's engineers did not add an extra key to the phone, with which you could control the shooting of objects.





Right side

There is only a key with which you can adjust the volume of music and video. It also allows you to transfer the device from one sound mode to another.

Front Panel

There is a light sensor. It helps to automatically adjust the level of the backlight of the screen, as well as the keyboard. He is brought closer to the spoken speaker, or rather, to his right. Here, not far from the keyboard, is a microphone.
