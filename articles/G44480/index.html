What yarn to choose for children's things

Choosing quality materials is crucial when it comes to knitting for children. If the product is intended to warm the baby, then the yarn should contain at least 40% wool, angora or mohair. The stores can offer material labeled "Baby" and 100% acrylic. Do not be tempted by such cheap solutions, the child will either freeze or sweat. Synthetics are not suitable for making clothes, it can only be used for knitting toys, pillows and other decorative items.

When buying threads, you should focus on the composition, as well as on the appearance:

  • Stiffness (should not be prickly).
  • Twist density (the tighter the twisted, the better).
  • The quality of the thread itself and its color (without thin or too wide areas, nodules, pale or faded spots).

How to tie a sweater with a jacquard yoke

To complete this model, basic knitting skills are sufficient. The main pattern is the front surface, the elastic - 2x2. The pattern for knitting a jacquard pattern is also developed in two versions (for a jacket of small and large size).



Work begins from below:

  1. Loops are made for the manufacture of the back part and knit with 5-6 cm elastic.
  2. Then they move to the front surface and continue to work until they reach the armhole.
  3. Here, the open loops are transferred to a thick thread and lay the part.
  4. Start manufacturing shelves and sleeves. They are also tied to armholes and stop.
  5. On circular knitting needles, loops of all parts are typed in the right order: left shelf, left sleeve, back, right sleeve, right shelf.
  6. They knit a yoke according to the scheme, timely performing the reduction of loops.
  7. Loops are gathered along the lateral edge of the shelves and the straps are knitted. Holes are made on the left side (buttonholes).
  8. Loops are gathered along the neckline and a collar is knitted.

At the last stage, all parts are sewn together, buttons are fixed and the product is steamed.

This poncho with a hood will come in handy during off-season periods when the weather is unpredictable and can change dramatically throughout the day.



Despite the fact that the ornament looks quite complex, it is extremely easy to knit. Here you need to know only the principles of forming the front and back loops, since the pattern is just a combination of them. Difficulties may arise in the manufacture of "cones". These elements can be crocheted separately and sewn or done as follows:

  • Knit one loop of the front.
  • Turn over the work, knit this loop from the wrong.
  • Turn the work again and repeat steps 1 and 2.
  • Return to knitting the rest of the row.

Making a hood

Then the craftswoman needs to determine where the beginning of the hood will be. From this place you should begin to perform straight and return rows, not circular ones.
