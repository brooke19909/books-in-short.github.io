Flowers made of money - bright and original!

Often we think about what to give to loved ones for this or that holiday. After all, I want to not just make a gift, but to present a thing that will be original and unique. Today, handmade gifts, such as hand made, have become especially valued. They not only look original, but also leave an unforgettable trace of pleasant impressions on the congratulated person.





Money as a gift is a win-win option. For many holidays, we give postcards and envelopes with notes. But what if you approach the design of a presentation with imagination and invest a piece of yourself? Making flowers from money with your own hands will not be difficult, and the originality of the gift will break all records. Together with the desired gift you will present a bunch of positive emotions and delight the hero of the occasion.

Who should I give?

Such a sweet and extravagant surprise can be presented to anyone, and hardly anyone will remain indifferent. Giving flowers from money to a woman, you kill two birds with one stone at a time, simultaneously presenting a bouquet of flowers and a main gift. Handing a respectable man a money tree, you express your congratulations and best wishes.



Performance technique

Let's consider in more detail how to make a flower from money. First, let's decide on the material. As you know, money is different in its color scheme. Therefore, the color of your flower will depend on the merits of the bill. For a beautiful design, you will need a satin ribbon, wire to create a flower trunk and green material to give the stem a natural color.

Flowers made of money in the form of roses are the easiest to make. To form a rose you will need about three bills.

1. Take the first bill, pinch in the center, so that we get two petals. Secure with a thin wire.
2. Identical work is done with the rest. Petals are stacked one into another.
3. Next, the wire coming from three bills, twist, forming a stalk.
4. And the most interesting is the final design of the flower. Wrap the stalk of wire with green material and tie a beautiful ribbon.

It must be borne in mind that the composition "flowers from money" will be disassembled sooner or later. Money will go away for its intended purpose. Therefore, when creating an original gift you need to be extremely careful and not damage the bills.



Bright and interesting!

Flowers from money - a universal gift. You can present them to almost any celebration. Anniversary, birthday, March 8, wedding or other significant event - such a gift will always be appropriate. Even modern brides are in great demand such bouquets, and this is not surprising. After all, such flowers are always diverse. A simple single rose, several flowers or a huge composition - it is always bright and interesting! In society, originality has always been appreciated. Sugary and ordinary sooner or later bothers, and something unusual brings a lot of positive emotions to our lives. There is no limit to fantasy, so create and surprise your friends and relatives, and you will always be in the spotlight!
