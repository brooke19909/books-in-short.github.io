Original toys from simple materials: baby dolls from nylon socks. Two workshops

Nylon socks can be used not only for their intended purpose. These wardrobe items make wonderful soft toys. The technology of their manufacture is quite simple, everyone will be able to master it. This article presents master classes that describe how baby dolls are made from nylon socks. We invite you to familiarize yourself with them and perform a cute soft doll for the daughter-daughter.



Preparatory stage

In the photo you see similar toys. What is needed in order to learn how to create them with your own hands? We learn about this from the list of materials and tools necessary for work:

  • one flesh-colored or pink nylon sock;
  • hollofiber or sintepon;
  • needle;
  • sewing threads;
  • knitted colored sock;
  • narrow satin ribbon;
  • small buttons (2-3 pieces);
  • two beads of black, brown, blue or green;
  • cosmetic blush.

Baby doll from a sock: a master class. Stage of pattern and sewing details

We lay the nylon sock on the table. We make two small lumps from the filler, put them in the sock - in the lower part (the one that is worn on the toes). We dress these balls with thread. Cut off the lumps. Thus, we have completed the details of the pens. Align that edge of the sock in a straight line. Wrap it with a thread. It turns out a kapron bag. We fill it with holofiber (filler) and sew up the hole from the bottom with a neat, hidden seam. We rewind this part in the middle with a thread, thus forming the head and body. To make the nubs from kapron socks look aesthetically pleasing, for work, prepare a thread that is suitable in color to the base material. On the head in the middle we pick up a small lump of filler with our fingers and sew small stitches around it. It turns out a tidy nose. In the right places we fasten the bead eyes. Sewing them, we pull the thread a little to get a recess. Thanks to these actions, the facial features of the pupa will look more natural. Ears are marked with stitches on the sides of the head. In black threads we embroider eyelashes and eyebrows, and in pink - a smile. You can arrange freckles, from this little baby will become even prettier. On the back of the torso, we perform the buttocks, pulling the capron together with several stitches. Tint the cheeks with cosmetic dry blush.



Making clothes for a soft doll

So that the baby dolls from socks, made with their own hands, are not naked, we perform outfits for them. The material for this work will serve as a knitted multi-colored sock. We will first make a headdress out of it. Cut off the bottom of the sock. Sew this part along the edge and tighten it slightly. We put a hat on the doll‚Äôs head. Next, cut the cuffs and put it on the body. We tuck and hem the bottom edge. Elastic band a little tighten with stitches in the neck. We dress the dress with a satin ribbon, forming a belt. Sew on buttons. We fix the lumps-handles. The toy is ready. Such baby dolls from kapron socks will surely appeal to both the girl and the boy.



Doll from socks (option number 2). Preparation of materials for creativity

This model of the toy is made from an ordinary sock, preferably multi-colored. In addition to him, such materials will be needed in the work:

  • filler;
  • a flap of light plain fabric (knitwear or cotton);
  • scissors;
  • needle;
  • sewing threads;
  • two ribbons about 20 centimeters long;
  • dry powder of pink color;
  • thread floss ;
  • two beads of dark color.

Description of the soft doll manufacturing process

We learn about how to make a baby doll out of a sock from the following description. We cut off the lower part of the textile part up to the heel itself. From this blank we will form the body. We fill it with filler to the very top. We select the legs by stitching a strip of stitches in the lower part in the middle. On the sides in the same way we make pens. We make out the upper edge with stitches and tighten, fix the thread with a knot and cut it off. The little body is ready, put it aside. Next, we perform the face of the pupa. From light fabric we cut two circles and sew them on the wrong side. In this case, leave a hole about 2 centimeters in size. We turn the workpiece through it and fill it with a synthetic winterizer (or other material). We sew a hole with a secret seam . Next, we fasten the eyes, embroider the thread of the nose, mouth, bangs with thread. Powder cheeks with blush.

Sew the head to the details of the body. We tie a bow made of a ribbon on the neck. From the rest of the sock, cut the cuffs. Bandage it in the gum with a ribbon. Turn the bottom edge of the workpiece. As a result, we get a hat. We put it on the head of the baby doll. That's all, the soft little doll is ready to become your kids' favorite toy.

From the presented workshops, you learned the technology by which pups are made of nylon socks. Make such figures with your own hands, the creative process will bring you pleasure and positive emotions. Well, the joy of the kids who received such a toy as a gift, there simply will not be a limit.
