Popular beaches of Novosibirsk: "Boomerang", "Remix", "Lighthouse", "Locomotive"

With the advent of the warm season, the most popular beaches in Novosibirsk open: Boomerang, Remix, Mayak, Lokomotiv. How do they differ from others, and what do city residents think about the service?



Summer is a time of warm sand, blue water and family vacations. If it is not possible to travel to warm countries, the beach will be the place to spend a weekend. Residents of the city highlight such selection criteria as service, cleanliness and safety.

Boomerang Beach in Novosibirsk

This place is popular among residents. Beach "Boomerang" in Novosibirsk is one of the best. It is located on the banks of the Ob reservoir.

The sandy coast is equipped with everything necessary for relaxation. There are umbrellas that will help you hide from the sun. Summer sofas provide an opportunity to lie down, drink cocktails or eat ice cream in comfort.

On the territory of the beach "Boomerang" in Novosibirsk there are cafes, bars, barbecue areas. A playground will be a great solution for families.

For visitors there is a catamaran rental point, as well as water slides.

The beach is clean, surrounded by pine trees. Nature and water create the perfect place for a summer vacation.



The beach "Boomerang" in Novosibirsk is not only warm sand. This is the place where you can have a great time with friends and celebrate the holiday.

A feature of the beach is that here you can not only relax, but also go in for sports. There is a rental of fitness equipment, a football field is available for rent. Bold and active experienced surfing masters will teach you how to ride the crest of a wave.

Remix

This beach is considered a recreation park. It is located on the shore of the Gulf of Berdsk. This place is ideal for families.

Remix Beach in Novosibirsk is equipped with two large pools with clean and warm water. There are 800 sun loungers on the sand, where you can sit to sunbathe and relax.

The motor ship on the beach is a restaurant with delicious dishes and warm atmosphere. It can hold a wedding or birthday.

A large game complex is installed for children. This gives parents the opportunity to relax or swim. There is also a small pool with a slide for younger guests of the park.



Visitors note a low ticket price - only 250 rubles per person. At the entrance there is a large paid parking where you can park your car.

"Lighthouse"

The beach is located on Plotinnaya street, 1/1. It exists on the basis of the Lighthouse Yacht Club. The beach in Novosibirsk is popular despite the entrance fee. The cost of tickets for the whole day at the weekend: adults - 600 rubles, children - 400, up to 12 years old - free of charge.

The beach is equipped with a swimming pool, deck chairs with mattresses, large beach umbrellas. Music plays quietly, so it will not interfere with those people who came to this place to relax.

Animators work for children, they organize various contests and relay races. Little guests will not have time to get bored.

For visitors there is the opportunity to ride not only on a boat, but also on a yacht. There is a cafe on the beach.



"Locomotive"

On the territory of Zaeltsovsky Park there is a private beach, which the townspeople loved for their comfort. This place allows you to combine leisure and other activities for children and adults.

Not far from Lokomotiv beach there is a zoo and a children's railway. In the park you can ride a bike, roller skate or run.

The beach has a large pool. It is possible to lie on the warm sand. Separately created a terrace with comfortable sun loungers. For children, there is a small pool where they can swim and splash.

The cost of a ticket on the beach depends on the recreation area and ranges from 100 to 300 rubles per hour. Visitors note that this place is very cozy, here you can relax after a working day.

Rules for visiting the beach

Many residents of the city on warm days spend time in parks and on the banks of the river. Simple rules for visiting the beach will help make spending time useful and safe.

On a hot day, choose a place so that there is no direct sunlight. The best option is a beach umbrella. This will help prevent sunstroke or overheating.

It is imperative to apply sunscreen to the skin.

Pets are not allowed on the beach.

Bathe in strictly designated areas or pool.

In public places, such as the beach, it is not recommended to speak loudly or listen to music.

If you want to have a picnic, you must not forget to clean up the trash and do not disturb people around you.

Smoking lying on the sand or in a sun lounger is strictly prohibited. All beaches are equipped with special places and ashtrays.

Fulfillment of such simple rules will make relaxation better, and also preserve the purity of nature.

The beaches of Novosibirsk - "Boomerang", "Remix", "Lighthouse", "Lokomotiv" and others open their doors with the arrival of heat. Every summer, they delight residents and tourists with clean water, warm sand and a variety of entertainment.
