An exotic country is the dream of all tourists. Overview of exotic countries

To leave noisy megalopolises with gray winter days and plunge into the world of bliss in a tropical paradise - which can be more attractive than a trip to exotic countries of the world. You will be swept by unusual flora and fauna, unfamiliar and interesting traditions of the natives, mystical traces of antiquity.

Such a vacation will be filled with vivid, unforgettable impressions, it will definitely add the spice to your life. Any corner of our planet can surprise with its originality. Picturesque places and open spaces will conquer with their uniqueness and primitiveness.

Two types of relaxation

All exotic countries for recreation can be divided into two categories. The first can be attributed to states that are interesting to everyone that nature itself created miraculously: its unusual landscapes, majestic mountains, sandy seas or proud, powerful waterfalls. The second category is countries with non-standard traditions, a variety of cultures and all kinds of folk customs. Everything that has been created by mankind over the centuries and distinguishes peoples and countries from each other.



Unlimited possibilities of modern resorts

Traveling to exotic countries, you will plunge into a completely different reality, and locals will hospitably meet and accommodate their guests in modern comfortable hotels. Rest is possible for every taste: it can be a day excursion to local attractions. You may be more comfortable if you just soak up the sun on the white sandy beaches. Fans of outdoor activities will touch ancient civilizations and take part in mysterious rituals. Adore thrills can spend time doing rafting, surfing or diving.

You can choose tasting tours, participate in the collection of tea, and then comprehend the mystery of tea drinking. Fans of fishing can order a master class from local fishermen or join the preparation of cane rum. This is just a small part of the possibilities that you can try while traveling to exotic countries.

Choosing a tour is a very difficult task, because you want to visit the most amazing country, with exclusive attractions. On the one hand, the forest jungle beckon, on the other, it is interesting to know all the subtleties of the civilization of the East.



Holidays in Palau

If you want to get in touch with tropical nature, look at rare animals and sunbathe under the Pacific sun, then a vacation in Palau will be the most impressive.

The exotic country of Palau is located in the Pacific Ocean and consists of underwater peaks. Guests can witness the unusual underwater world, which includes about one and a half thousand species of fish, seven hundred species of coral, all kinds of mollusks and marine mammals. It is in Palau that rare species of animals live, for example, manatees. In the green jungle monkeys dominate, especially the huge number of birds and insects is surprising. But the main feature of the islands is the absence of poisonous animals, so the rest will not only be wonderful, but also absolutely safe.

An exotic country attracts tourists with its nature reserves, adventurers are attracted by the legends of sunken ships. Interesting and mysterious sights of Palau are under water: these are the Ngemelis Wall, Big Drop, the underwater caves of Siis Tannel and Chandelier Cave.

Palau is truly a paradise for a romantic trip for newlyweds and the best place for families.



Legendary japan

An exotic country that almost everyone wants to visit is Japan. It is a state with traditions that have been honored for many centuries. To assess the identity and development of eastern civilization is possible only by visiting it. The beauty of Japan is revealed at any time of the year, the natural landscape has not left anyone indifferent. Thousands of guests come to the famous sakura blossom, wishing to see an extraordinary miracle.

This state combines modern cities and provincial villages with picturesque landscapes. Japan is famous for its architectural monuments of the second millennium.

In this country you can taste not only national cuisine, but also dishes of all the peoples of the world, made in the manner of talented Japanese chefs. Only in this country can extreme people tickle their nerves and try a dish of poisonous fish that only selected masters can cook correctly.



Mysterious Peru

An unusual journey in the wake of the ancient Inca civilization is represented by the exotic country of Peru. It is filled with the mysterious heritage of a prehistoric state. Even the most skeptical tourists will be surprised at the monoliths in the Peruvian temple, ancient instruments for astronomical research, and the giant drawings on the Nazca plateau are generally hit on the spot with their incomprehensible origin.

Also in Peru are ancient cities with mysterious buildings. The virgin jungle forests attract tourists. This country has the deepest canyon in the world. Visiting the extraordinary Lake Titicaca is not indifferent.

Gourmets will be amazed by the diverse cuisine, which has absorbed the best sources of culinary experience of other countries. It is worth noting that the Peruvian cuisine is listed in the Guinness Book of Records, so any dish in Peru is a culinary masterpiece.



Dream vacation

The most exotic countries will help make your vacation unforgettable and exclusive. After the trip, you will walk for a long time under the impression, revise the photo and plan the next trip to some distant exotic civilization.
