The decoration of the bouquet. Making bouquets of tulips. Making bouquets of fresh flowers

Many people love roses, chrysanthemums, orchids and gladioli, but not a single flower can be compared with beautiful spring tulips. Unfortunately, most often they are sold without special decorations, simply wrapped in cellophane. But the design of a bouquet of tulips can be a truly fascinating action. At the same time, its creator will show his sincere attitude to the person to whom he intended.

Making bouquets of fresh flowers



When using tulips to create gift bouquets, you should choose only the freshest flowers with unblown buds. The design of a bouquet of them is especially relevant in the spring, when everyone wants to feel the awakening of nature. These non-standard, bright and delicate flowers have long been one of the most beloved objects of work of breeders. Long gone are the days when only red or yellow tulips could be found on the shelves. The palette of colors and shapes of the latest varieties is simply amazing. You can already find almost black and purple tulips.

These flowers are perfectly combined with such types of plants as roses, ranunculi, hydrangeas, hyacinths, freesia, eustoma, calla lilies, and in any combination. Due to the fact that they have rather long stems, cascading and round bouquets are perfectly made from them. You can also use various portbuketnitsy. Making a bouquet of tulips will not require much work, since they themselves are a wonderful gift.

Reason for a bouquet of tulips



It is customary to give such compositions to events and holidays of a romantic nature (marriage, first meeting, Valentine's Day, March 8, birth of a child, etc.). They are irreplaceable also on a date. Moreover, the shorter the period of romantic meetings, the more tender you should choose a bouquet. So, pink, white, lilac or orange tulips, tied with a delicate ribbon, are perfect for this case. Do not forget that the stem of these flowers, unlike prickly roses, will not be able to injure the girl. At the same time, to maintain the beautiful appearance of the bouquet, you will not need a tall and massive vase, but any glass vessel with water is quite suitable. A bouquet of fragrant and delicate tulips will always cheer you up.

In the case when partners have long been flaming romantic feelings, you can present bright red flowers. Making tulip bouquets with bright hearts or other decorative accessories can overpower anyone, since the leaves of these flowers themselves are an excellent background for buds and various decor.

Making bouquets of flowers for the bride



What other flower can so unobtrusively emphasize the tender beauty of a beautiful bride, if not a tulip? At the same time, it goes well with some other plants. Making bouquets of fresh flowers will not only give a special zest to the wedding ceremony, but it will also save a rather impressive amount of money, since not everyone can afford compositions of traditional roses or expensive orchids.

Most often, in such bouquets, buds of the most delicate shades are used: white, pale pink, lilac. Often, florists, on the contrary, choose a combination of contrasting tones for the bride. So, a bouquet of white tulips looks very impressive , surrounded by, for example, red, purple or raspberry buds. Such a composition looks especially beautiful against a background of bright green leaves. A combination of terry and simple buds looks very impressive.

Bouquet for the birthday



It would be very opportune to have a bouquet of tulips at the birthday of a friend or relative. A wide selection of different shapes and colors of these colors allows you to create a huge variety of compositions. They can be monophonic (consisting of tulips of the same shade) or variegated, in which you can use a variety of shades of buds. It should be borne in mind that the design of bouquets (the photo can be seen in the article), consisting of several shades, will require more flowers, since only in this way will the whole beauty of this composition manifest itself. A small gift in the form of several bright tulips, created by yourself, will emphasize your personality and save money on a more expensive present.

Dutch tulips

Recently, Dutch tulips have been in great demand. Their distinctive feature can be considered a variety of shapes and colors, as well as a rather strong stem, which, unlike some domestic varieties, allows the flower to remain in its best form for much longer (the heads do not go awry). Also characteristic features of the Dutch tulips include a large spread in flower size, shape and color. This allows you to turn the design of the bouquet into a fascinating procedure in which you can use different varieties that only emphasize the beauty of each other.

Choice of color tulips



Some people attach great importance to the color of the buds. So, individual ladies under no circumstances will not be happy with yellow tulips, because they have a strong stereotype that these flowers predict separation. Most men who are not sophisticated in floristry prefer to choose buds of various red shades. Such a feature, most likely, speaks of human conservatism, although such a bouquet would be more than appropriate for official events. The most creative, as a rule, young people choose flowers of the most unexpected shades, often combining buds of different colors. Bright orange tulips are ideal for business partners, as this color symbolizes the desire for success and successful business.

A very unusual and original gift can be a bouquet of almost black buds. In fact, the color of such a tulip is dark purple, but it is so saturated that it seems almost black. How much is kept a bouquet of tulips

With proper care, the composition of these flowers can stand in good condition for 5-6 days. To do this, change the water in the vase daily, and cut the stems by about 1 cm, but only under the stream of water (so that air does not get in). To prevent the buds from blooming prematurely, these flowers are kept in a cool place.
