SMS loans

Credit denial

After you fill out a special form on the company's website, employees, as mentioned above, consider your candidacy as a potential borrower. Of course, situations happen when at this stage a person is refused. Many are interested in what could have been the reason and how not to get it the next time.

So, the most common reasons when a client receives a refusal from SMS-finance (reviews, as well as information on the company‚Äôs website confirm this) are a damaged credit history of the client, active debt on another loan and an attempted fraudulent activity. The first two points are very simple: if you have debt in the company, you will not be given a loan again. As for bad credit history, it is checked by forming an appeal to a special bureau. As for fraud, we are talking about trying to take a loan and hide from the company, somehow falsify documents, and so on.
