Crocheted cardigan - a universal thing

The jacket is the most versatile thing in a wardrobe. Each woman has several models available for various occasions. It can be worn with trousers and a skirt. It looks no less harmonious with a sarafan or dress. Correctly selected composition of the yarn will allow you to make a crocheted knitted jacket , which will be ideal for the winter season. In the case of choosing very thin threads, you can create a weightless openwork model for the summer period. At the same time, wool, linen, cotton, viscose, as well as yarn of any other composition can be used for work. The choice will depend on personal preference and the desired effect.



The thickness of the hook can be very diverse. If you choose a thick enough one (number 5 or more), you can create a very warm product. It will be a great alternative to an autumn coat in the case of a fairly tight knit. A thin hook will allow you to become the owner of a light openwork model. Such a thing will be very relevant in the summer. However, it will take much longer to knit, although yarn (by weight) will require relatively less.





It is worth noting that a crocheted knitted jacket can have the most diverse style. If the needlewoman does not have rich enough experience, it is better to give preference to a direct model. It will be easy to knit such a product, since the manufacturing process will not require unnecessary addition or reduction of loops. Well, if the experience is already large enough, you can create a more refined model that will look best on a woman with a specific type of physique.

Crocheting cardigans allows you to create a wide variety of sizes. In this way, you can make a product for both fairly fragile girls and larger women. The main thing is to choose the right pattern that will hide the possible flaws of the figure and emphasize its merits. The same principle must be adhered to if a model is selected for a more adult woman or a very young girl. Youth items will be inappropriate for respectable ladies. At the same time, many strict classic models can add age to young girls.





Crocheting involves several possible options for creating jackets. If the work is performed using the Irish lace technique, the individual elements are initially made, which are then connected to each other using a grid or directly. Such work requires certain skills and special knowledge. It is much simpler to crochet a knitted jacket by creating a single piece of fabric. In this case, the jacket is made much faster. At the same time, even a beginner needlewoman will be able to knit it. Special attention deserves the choice of colors. If desired, you can create a crocheted cardigan not only from monophonic, but also from multi-colored yarn. Moreover, the manufacturing sequence can also be very diverse.
