Sights of Africa: amazing African continent

Africa is the second largest continent and, possibly, the ancestral home of mankind. A combination of unbridled nature and civilization, skyscrapers of South Africa and hundreds of wild tribes of Swaziland. This continent cannot but attract with its simplicity and mystery at the same time. Crowds of tourists annually seek to visit the sights of Africa.

Unique Africa

About one billion people live in Africa, and its territory is divided into 55 countries. Not all corners of this continent are ready to open to a foreign guest. True, recently the tourism potential of African countries is developing and opening up previously hidden sights of Africa. Fans of the hot sun and beaches, thrill seekers - everyone can find something for themselves here.

The most common places for visiting tourists are the islands that belonged to the African continent. The amazingly rich nature of Madagascar, the jungle of Mauritius, the Canary beaches and the incredible beauty of Seychelles open up their spaces to those who want to find their piece of paradise.

Continental Africa also has a few trump cards. Morocco and Tunisia are almost inferior to the islands. In addition to sandy beaches, these countries are known for their medieval Arab cities. And in Morocco there is even a blue city. Listing the sights of Africa, it is impossible to miss the Egyptian pyramids and ancient Carthage.





Connoisseurs of natural wealth usually do not miss the opportunity to visit one of the national parks in Tanzania, Kenya or Rwanda. Some even go to visit the African desert of Namibia, Algeria. Here is the largest desert on Earth - the Sahara, which is located within eleven African countries.

Cradle of humanity

Africa is often called the cradle of life or the cradle of humanity, and this is by no means due to the diversity of its nature. Scientists have long agreed with this term and even contributed to its occurrence, because it was in the African expanses that the oldest human remains were discovered. Many researchers believe that from the African continent began the further settlement of our ancestors throughout the Earth.

Now the cradle of mankind is called a plot of land about 470 square meters in size 50 kilometers from Johannesburg. There are more than 30 archaeological sites where the bones of ancient people were found. The cradle is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site. Mostly the remains were found in caves. Bones of various mammals were found along with human remains.





Temples of Cote DʻIvoire

Côte d'Ivoire, or Ivory Coast, once a French colony, acquired several cathedrals as a legacy. The Catholic Cathedral of Our Lady of the World - Notre Dame de la Paix - is the most famous. It is located in the capital of the state in the city of Yamaskura and is listed in the Book of Records as the largest cathedral in the world. Its prototype was St. Peter's Basilica in the Vatican. The dome of the cathedral rises 158 meters, however, the cathedral itself is not very spacious and significantly inferior to the Vatican.

The Cathedral of Our Lady of the World is another pro-European cathedral and is completely unrelated to African culture. Mosques are more unique and colorful here. In the city of Kauara there is a Sudanese type mosque, erected in the XVII century. The Sudanese type of mosque is interesting for its special architectural details that adapt the temple to the local climate. A similar adobe mosque was preserved in Mali. The mosque in the city of Kong also dates from the 17th century, it is considered a national treasure of the country.





Crown of Tanzania - Kilimanjaro

Africa is especially famous for its highest mountain. Among the plains of Tanzania, this “sparkling mountain” rises 5890 meters. Climbing a mountain you can experience all types of climates of our planet. At the bottom of the mountain lies a savannah with many interesting inhabitants from monkeys to lions and leopards. Mountain streams flow down from melting peaks covered with snow.

There are many routes that are designed for both professionals and amateur tourists who decide to conquer Kilimanjaro. Africa in these places reveals its true essence and character. Near the mountain there are two national parks: Tarangire and Serengeti. The latter is even one of the largest reserves in the world.





Conclusion

It is impossible to briefly list all the attractions of Africa. This continent has a huge number of interesting natural and architectural objects. Wildlife, originality represented by numerous savannas, deserts. Unique animals in the open spaces for visitors to national parks and reserves. It seems that only here you can feel something real and uncreated.
