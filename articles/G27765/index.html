How the Interuniversity Campus (MSG) lives in St. Petersburg

Every year, children from all cities of Russia come to study in St. Petersburg. Leading universities of this wonderful city make it possible to become certified specialists. The inter-university campus (MSG) is becoming the second home for students.

A bit of history

MSH in St. Petersburg began to be built in 1964 in a wasteland, among the marshy soils of the city of Leningrad. On March 5, 1966, the grand opening of the long-awaited complex took place.

Time flew and the town developed rapidly. A cultural and leisure and sports center has opened, construction of new houses for more than 3,500 places has continued.

How to get there

MSG of St. Petersburg is located in the green zone. It is surrounded by two parks - "Victory" and "Aviators" at Novoizmalovsky Prospekt 16, building 1. From the metro station "Victory Park" walk only 10 minutes.

Infrastructure

The interuniversity campus is not an ordinary dormitory. It accommodates more than 9,000 students from 34 universities in St. Petersburg.

Student buildings here are called houses. These are ten equipped 10-story buildings and two administration buildings. In the last three buildings of the town are a student clinic, two laundries with a self-service system and a library. MSG management, as well as the institution of continuing education, are provided with computer rooms.

Security

MSH is the safest place to stay for both local and nonresident students. Along the perimeter of the building, they are protected by metal fences, and 120 video surveillance cameras allow you to monitor the safety of the town as much as possible. Entrance is through checkpoints only with special electronic cards.

MSG in St. Petersburg is equipped with a modern fire safety system, an alarm system and smoke and heat sensors . Security personnel provide it during the day, and from 22:00 the night controller monitors the territory.

Hostel

The hostel of the MSG of St. Petersburg is equipped with furnished rooms, which are designed for 3 or 4 people. Each floor is equipped with its own toilet and shower stalls. Also for convenience, at the other end of the corridors is a kitchen with tables, sinks and stoves.

Sometimes in the newspapers and social networks there are not quite positive reviews about the hostel of the MSG of St. Petersburg. A common cause of conflicts and misunderstandings is very strict control at checkpoints and malfunctioning key cards.

Nuances of student service

On the territory of the MSG of St. Petersburg there are 3 laundries. One is located in the first economic building, and the other on the second. Each laundry room is equipped with over 80 washing machines. Students come to wash their things themselves.

So that students can always eat on time, student cafes work in all houses. These establishments operate from 8:00 to 21:00.

About sport

A complex of 15 gyms has been built on the territory of MSG in St. Petersburg, the total area of ​​which is 22,000 m 2 . It is designed to engage in various sports: football, basketball, volleyball, handball and even tennis. If the student wants to lead an active sporting lifestyle, then there is an equipped fitness room.

The 50-meter 8-lap pool, which students and teachers call the Olympic Reserve, is a well-deserved pride of the St. Petersburg MSG.

The town boasts sports achievements of its inhabitants. This is actively promoted by the Student Cultural and Sports Center, which includes a diverse number of circles, sections, events of various nature, which can fully reveal the potential of many students.

Concert hall

In order to enable students to realize their creative ideas, a concert hall measuring 9 X 5 m with a visual sector of 900 seats is located on the territory of the MSG of St. Petersburg. The hall is equipped with the necessary equipment for creative evenings, concerts, performances and even parties.

MSG newspaper

On the initiative of the students themselves, in 2006 a monthly multi-circulation publication “MSG - Our Student House” was created on the campus’s territory. But after 9 years, the newspaper became so popular that it changed to a magazine format and changed its name - "They will understand the MSG."

Over the issues of the journal are working students and journalist educators. Media noted that the magazine is incredibly diverse in terms of styles and genres. The student life of the town is so saturated that each issue of the magazine becomes a whole firework of stories and events.

So say the students

Very bright and colorful reviews about MSG in St. Petersburg are left by those who have personally visited there. All students who study here describe their feelings using the concepts of “fun” and “sea of ​​positive”.

The campus is a great opportunity to make new friends, socialize, feel the creative upsurge and the sea of ​​mutual understanding. MSG is a city in a city and a student house in St. Petersburg.
