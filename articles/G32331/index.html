Headbands with knitting needles: diagrams, models, photos

Knitting a headband on a head is a task that, without exaggeration, can be mastered by almost all knitters. Many articles on knitting contain a similar statement, but here it is really justified. By and large, the bandage is a strip of knitted fabric, sewn to the side or made round and without a seam at all.



What yarn can be used for dressings

As a rule, we knit a bandage on the head with knitting needles to insulate in the off-season. When caps are removed, but the weather is still too cold to completely exclude hats from your wardrobe, a minimized version of the cap is put into operation.

Dressings cover your head, covering your ears and forehead, providing the necessary warmth and without wrinkling your hair. The material for knitting dressings should be chosen the same as for hats: with a high content of wool or angora. Natural fibers in the thread should be at least 30%, otherwise the accessory simply will not perform its task.

The thickness of the yarn can be absolutely any. True, threads thicker than 100 m / 100 grams give the knitting a certain roughness. If this is not a planned effect, then it is better to choose something thinner. As with many other knitwear, yarn with a thickness of 250-300 m / 100 grams should be considered optimal.

The simplest dressing models

To quickly tie a functional and practical dressing, you can apply elementary elastic 1: 1 or 2: 2. They are bilateral, which excludes the presence of the front and the wrong side. They also give the canvas a certain density. The disadvantage of these dressings can be called their unoriginality and the lack of "zest".

Also, a honeycomb or rice pattern is often chosen to knit headbands with knitting needles. The patterns of these ornaments are extremely popular.

Depending on the thickness of the yarn, 12-18 loops are drawn on the knitting needles and work according to the selected scheme, forming an even fabric. When it reaches 50 cm in length, work is stopped.

Knitting: Headband with knitting needles

To create such an accessory, you need to dial 16 loops on the knitting needles (including hem). Each first loop is removed, and the last is always the front.

Knitting Stages:

  1. 1 LITSP, 1 DIF, 1 LITS, 1 DIF, 6 LITS, 1 DIF, 1 LITSP, 1 DIF, 1 LITS.
  2. According to the drawing.
  3. 1 SPEI, 1 LITSP, 2 SPIs, 6 LITSP, 2 SPIs, 1 LITSP, 1 SPI.
  4. According to the drawing.
  5. Repeat row 1-4.
  6. 1 LITSP, 1 SPI, 1 LIPP, 1 SPI, 6 LICPs cross to the right, 1 SPI, 1 LICP, 1 SPI, 1 LICP.
  7. According to the drawing.
  8. Repeat rapport from the 1st to the 11th row until the canvas is equal to 50 cm in height.

At this stage, all the loops must be closed and the edges of the dressing sewn from the wrong side. On both sides, tie the edges with a "crustacean step".
