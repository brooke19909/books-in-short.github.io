Cheap Moscow hotels: addresses, photos and reviews

Have you ever dreamed of visiting the capital, seeing Red Square with your own eyes, walking along the ancient streets that have seen a lot in their lifetime. Many tourists are kept away from excursions to the capital by the high cost of hotel accommodation. Today we decided to help you, and prepared a list of available options for accommodation. These are not five-star apartments, but cheap Moscow hotels, which, however, are distinguished by comfortable living conditions. You understand well here that cheap does not mean bad.

Hostel "3 Penguins"

Very convenient for those traveling by rail. Not all cheap Moscow hotels are located so conveniently. Of course, for a long trip, the hostel is not suitable, but if you need to spend the night one or two nights, then this is the best option for the price-quality ratio. Address Pyatnitskaya street, d. 20, p. 2. Within walking distance of hundreds of bars, cafes and restaurants. Public transport is just a 10-minute walk away. Metro stop just 100 meters away. All rooms are equipped with: shared bathroom, safe. Free internet is available. Judging by the reviews, this is an ideal option for a single traveler, student or tourist on a budget. The cost of living is from 600 rubles per day.

Hotel RAMS

Cheap Moscow hotels are not only hostels. If you are looking for a complete room for a reasonable price, then we will help you make the right choice. Quadruple room with bunk beds will cost 800 rubles per day. Double, superior room is a little more expensive, 900 rubles per day.

It is located at Baltic, 10/2. The rooms have everything you need to spend a few days normally. In addition, the RAMS has ample opportunities to conduct various business events. The modern conference room seats up to 30 people, and is suitable for seminars and trainings, presentations and negotiations. Cheap Moscow hotels rarely please with a wide selection of dishes in a cafe or dining room. But this does not apply to RAMS. Between the second and third floors there is a cozy cafe where you will be offered rich borsch and home-made pancakes, salads and pies.

Mini-Hotel Bonotel

A very comfortable, small hotel is located on Stariye Kuzminki Street. Here you can find a variety of accommodation options. For students, you can offer a place in a quadruple room for 400 rubles. There are full double rooms for 2000 per day. But that is not all. Take any cheap hotels in Moscow, the best they can offer you is an overnight stay. Bonotel is located in the Kuzminsky park in the Golitsyn estate. You yourself will see that this is the bottom of the most comfortable places in the capital. Here you can walk for your pleasure. There is a small zoo on the territory, an annual flower festival is held. A large number of sports and development facilities will make your vacation truly unforgettable. It can accommodate up to 80 people at a time.

Mini-hotel "Happiness"

Continuing to list cheap Moscow hotels, one cannot but say about this small but very comfortable hotel. It is located within walking distance from the Crocus Expo exhibition center. The proximity to the Myakino and Strogino metro stations makes the communication between the hotel and the city center very convenient and economical. Address: Myakininskaya Street 3. Judging by the reviews, here you will find simple and very comfortable rooms, spacious and comfortable. There is a set of necessary furniture: bed, equipped kitchen, sofa. Moreover, the cost of rooms starts at 1,500 rubles.

Hotel "Maxima" (Irbis)

Cheap hotels in Moscow are not only simple and affordable. It is also stylish, modern, comfortable and unforgettable. Such is the rest at the Maxima Hotel. Some remoteness from the center is compensated by a low price, but at the same time getting from the city center is not difficult. The cost of renting rooms starts at 1300 rubles .

A comfortable hotel room can be selected for every taste. But especially tourists remember the local breakfast in Swedish, which corresponds to the best European samples. You can get to the historical center of Moscow, the botanical garden or the Luzhniki stadium from here easily and without any problems.

Each air-conditioned room has a safe, hairdryer and TV, as well as a shower. In addition, there is a fitness center, swimming pool, massage and beauty salon, sauna and massage, spa and wellness center.

Hotel Moscow Style

In addition to affordable housing, we will tell you about the cheapest hotels in Moscow. One of the most attractive hotels for budget travelers is located at ul. Tverskaya, d. 15, apt. 80. Moscow Style is the choice of a lowly tourist, for whom the main thing is a roof over their heads. Nearby you will find a huge number of various bars, pizzerias and other entertainments. Public transport is just 10 minutes on foot. Tverskaya-Pushkinskaya Metro Station is 200 meters away .

Judging by the reviews, the rooms have a modest interior, but at the same time perfect cleanliness. Each room has a safe and TV, guests have access to high speed internet. And the most important thing is the cost of a room from 600 rubles per day. At the same time, according to visitors' reviews, it deserves a rating of 8 out of 10 possible. Many cheap hotels in Moscow would not refuse such an assessment.

Hotel "Sunrise"

In order to choose what suits you, it is very important to immediately determine the selection criteria. Looking for cheap mini-hotels in Moscow? To be sure to downtown? Then the hotel "Sunrise" is just the perfect choice. It is located at Altufevskoe highway, 2 . From here you can get to anywhere in the city in just a few minutes, see all its monuments and secrets. Smolenskaya metro station is just a 2-minute walk away.

Let's devote a few words to the internal arrangement of rooms. Each of them has a TV and a private bathroom, toilet and safe. The cost of renting rooms starts at 1100 rubles. At the same time, the interior of the hotel itself is luxurious and bright, at first glance it will easily pass for a five-star one.

Hotel at night

We do not always have the opportunity to walk around the capital for our pleasure. What if you need to spend here only one night, and at the same time want to save on a hotel? Of course, choose a budget option. For example, Avita on Montazhnaya. This is a cheap hotel in Moscow for the night, which will offer you rooms starting from 1500 rubles. It is located in the vicinity of the Shchelkovskaya metro station, a 30-minute drive from the center.

The capacity of cozy rooms is from 2 to 4 guests, therefore, you can choose the option for yourself, regardless of whether you are traveling alone or with family. All rooms are equipped with a kitchenette, private bathroom. Facilities include a shared kitchen and laundry facilities, and free internet. Nearby is Izmailovsky Park, which is a favorite place for walking.

Instead of a conclusion

Any of the above hotels is the best option for a student on a budget. All of them have a high rating and excellent reviews of former guests. Despite attractive prices, the hotels offer excellent conditions for your comfortable stay. On the one hand - no pretentiousness and excesses, on the other - all the conditions for a normal life.
