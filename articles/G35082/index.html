How to weave clothes from rubber bands for dolls: history and instructions

Rainbow Loom - colored rubber bands, a universal material from which you can weave absolutely everything - from a bracelet to a dress for an adult girl. And we will dwell in more detail on how to weave clothes from rubber bands for dolls.

Doll rubber bracelet

Before moving on to something more serious, you need to practice on a simple bracelet for a doll.

Take the rubber band and twist it onto the hook four turns. The same thing needs to be done with three more colorful rubber bands. Then take the fifth one and carefully throw these four wound onto it.

To prevent the creation from blooming, put it on a pencil or pen. And on the hook, do the same operation with the new five rubber bands. Then simply connect the elastic bands on the handle to those on the hook. You can delight your favorite doll with a new accessory!

Clothes from rubber bands for dolls: how to weave

Having trained, you can safely proceed to something more serious and grandiose - creating a dress for the doll. In this case, do not get hooked - you will need a special machine.
