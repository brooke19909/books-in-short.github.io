With the approaching holiday season, Kiev tourists begin to dreamily plan their future vacation. Some plan to relax in the subtropical coastal climate. Others prefer mineral springs of Svalyava, Morshyn, Truskavets.





However, according to connoisseurs of resort recreation, the best recreation centers in the Kiev region have no less therapeutic potential than the hotels of the above-mentioned resorts. Responses of vacationers there indicate that you can not go so far from the megalopolis for wellness procedures and healing holidays in nature.

Location

Among the rating places of resort recreation in the Kiev region there is a forest recreation center "Glebovka", located at Vyshgorod district, the tract Zeleny Bor, 9. The distance from the center of Kiev to it is about 35 minutes by car.

For those who want to relax on it, it is presented in detail on specialized sites for holidaymakers. Judging by the reviews, the recreation center "Glebovka" is popular with Kiev. Contacts, as well as all the information necessary for booking rooms, can be obtained in three ways:

About the recreation center

This picturesque recreation center was built on the picturesque shore of the Kiev Sea near the ancient Vyshgorod (residence of Princess Olga). The reviews of people who rested on it tell of this wonderful place with an unusually clean air saturated with volatile pine forests, located on the picturesque coast of an impressive expanse of water, inhabited by fish, favored by birds.





In the summer, locals near the base in a small market sell inexpensive forest delicacies - freshly picked berries and mushrooms. Some vacationers often come here with inflatable boats and fishing equipment. The base is good for relaxation, communication with nature, beach pastime.

The recreation center "Lesnaya" provides its guests with two hotel buildings with a total capacity of 21 rooms ("Golden Stream", "Trefoil"), 31 comfortable Finnish wooden cottages (standard (cosiness), classic (middle), VIP), environmentally friendly, made from glued coniferous beams cedar-spruce-pine. Such houses are resistant to moisture, have excellent thermal insulation and are tight at the joints. The territory of the recreation center (and this is no less than six hectares) is very well-groomed. Wonderful stepped and paved terraces are convenient for walking. Paths are laid on the territory of the base, playgrounds are equipped.

Infrastructure

The recreation center provides its guests with facilities for seminars, receptions, banquets, seminars, delicious food, a well-maintained beach, and a guarded parking lot. Here you can organize a corporate party for 50 people without embarrassing other guests (the spacious territory of the base allows you to do this).





In the guest houses themselves there is a fireplace zone, a sauna with a good steam room, shower, a place for lounge relaxation, modern appliances are installed.

Vacationers are free to use the indoor 25-meter heated pool. Floating in it through the euro-glass opens a view of the Kiev Sea. Around the pool there is a comfortable area for lounge - relaxation.

The beach area is landscaped and in sufficiently equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas. The wide sandy strip is well-groomed and regularly cleaned. Vacationers on it create the illusion of being on the coast. It is created by comfort, gentle golden sand and the water of the Kiev Sea, the opposite shore of which is hidden behind the horizon.

Nutrition

Glebovka (a recreation center near Vyshhorod) provides food for vacationers in an equipped restaurant complex. The last includes a banquet hall, designed for the simultaneous service of a hundred people; 2 terraces accommodating 30 people.





Guests of Glebovka are treated to dishes of everyday and traditional Ukrainian cuisine. However, there is nothing to hide, gourmets who prefer the best price / quality ratio prefer to eat at a nearby recreation center.

Leisure

Wonderful outdoor recreation promises its guests "Glebovka" (recreation center). Reviews indicate a good full-fledged rest for guests. In the summer season, the beach, fishing, cycling, mushroom and berry picking are wonderful here. Guests have the opportunity to swim in the pool, take their leisure time by playing billiards, table tennis, darts.

The territory of the base with comfortable terraces and the healing pine air saturated with volcanoes contribute to the popularity of hiking.

Holidaymakers notes

However, judging by the reviews, the resort company described by us is not famous for the international level of service. Admittedly, Glebovka (a recreation center) has not yet revealed its potential.

Services, according to the guests, are provided by insufficiently motivated and trained personnel. We look forward to seeing that in the near future the rating of the base on a ten-point system will increase from the current level of 3.8 to at least 7. Guest reviews contain comments on the quality of room cleaning and low standards of service.





The recreation center "Glebovka" is so far more suited for organizing short-term weekend breaks or corporate events than for long-term resorts. The fairness of the above proves that the gratitude to the address sounded exclusively from short-term guests.

Conclusion

Who needs a forest recreation center (Glebovka)? Citizens who want to breathe full oxygen and shake off asphalt dust. Here you can organize a seminar, training or conference, relax as a team, take a steam bath, relax in nature with children. In the presence of a friendly company, a pre-planned vacation in the conditions of the local nature is simply wonderful.
