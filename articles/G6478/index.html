Natural and Man-Made Attractions of the United States

Millions of tourists visiting one of the largest countries in the world admire the incredible natural wonders and monuments created by human hands. The sights of the United States of America will surprise even the most discerning travelers, dreaming of a new meeting with historical places immediately after the trip.

It is impossible to talk about all the interesting corners of a huge country, so we will focus on cultural and natural sites, which have long been considered the hallmarks of the United States.

Disneyland

The first Disneyland in the world, opened in 1955, is a huge amusement park and real attractions of the United States of America, where tourists from all over the world seek. An unforgettable family holiday center, built for children, is loved by people of all ages. There you can feel like a child and visit the colorful castle of Sleeping Beauty, visit the small island of Peter Pan, plunge into the adventure world of Indiana Jones.



The American "Disneyland" (California) became the prototype of all theme parks of this kind. It is with amazing care that the fairy-tale world of children's cartoons is recreated, filled with incredible attractions and colorful parades. Grand Canyon

The most impressive sights of the United States of America are not always created by human hands. The Grand Canyon, located in Arizona, is an excellent example of the erosion of the Colorado River, which has crashed into limestone on a nearby plateau for centuries.

The ancient rocks of slates and granites lying at the base of the natural monument are painted in reddish color and formed about two billion years ago. The muddy waters of the Colorado River with a suspension of sand and clay have the same shade.

National park

In 1919, a national park was created here, annually hosting more than five million tourists a year. The Grand Canyon of Colorado is an amazing natural miracle that looks from afar as a flat surface, and only at the very edge of the plateau is a gorge with a bewitching depth that expands annually expanding. Travelers love to photograph the abyss of the canyon, after washing the rocks filled with the most bizarre relief forms in which everyone guesses something different.



Surprisingly, the canyon changes color depending on the lighting: dark colors are replaced with the appearance of the first rays of the sun with iridescent pink and bluish shades.
