Strezhevoy city, Tomsk region

The city of Strezhevoy is located in the north-west of the Tomsk region and is part of the Aleksandrovsky district. It is located near the right tributary of the majestic river Ob. Strezhevoy is a rather young city, recently he was only 45 years old. The nearest railway station from this settlement is Nizhnevartovsk (68 km). Two kilometers from the town is the local airport.



Historical essay

The city of Strezhevoy (Tomsk region) was built in the 30s of the last century. At that time, it was a small village where special settlers settled. At that time, repressed citizens, mainly the so-called fists, belonged to this category. Later, in 1966, the village of Strezhevoy was founded near this village. By 1967, he received the status of an urban-type settlement, and in 1978 - officially the status of a city. The city of Strezhevoy, Tomsk Oblast, became a city of oil workers who came here on duty to service rigs located within a radius of 400 km.

The name of the settlement comes from the Russian word “rod”, which means steep coast, rapid, channel, channel, rapids. It is worth noting that during the construction of the city it was proposed to name it differently - Neftegrad. However, the authorities decided that Strezhevoy sounds a lot more interesting, and there are already quite a lot of cities with the prefix "neft", so they settled on this more original and melodic name. The inhabitants of the town also liked this idea more.



Modern look

Despite the fact that Strezhevoy belongs to the Tomsk region, he is nevertheless closer to the Khanty-Mansiysk District. The city has virtually no high-rise buildings. There are 10 micro-districts here; in the near future they plan to build the eleventh. But in general, this town can be bypassed in less than one day.

Economic development

Strezhevoy (Tomsk Oblast) is a city where oil workers work for Tomskneft, which is owned on a parity basis by Gazprom Neft and Rosneft. Previously, Tomskneft was the property of Yukos. There is also a center for oil and gas production of the Sovetsko-Sosninskoye field. The city of Strezhevoy (Tomsk region) supplies oil to the neighboring Kuzbass region. In the village there is a small oil refinery.

Within the city there are also food industry enterprises. The bakery is engaged in baking bakery and confectionery products. There are also meat processing enterprises where sausages and various meat delicacies are made.



Interesting places

Among the attractions of the city is the Museum of Tomsk oil. In this unique institution you will be told about the development of an oil field in the northern part of the Tomsk region. Also in the city there is another museum - the museum of local lore, where you can learn about the history of the region in all its details. If you visit Strezhevoy, admire the monument “From here Neftegrad begins”, the stele of Victory and the monument to the founders of the city.

That, perhaps, is all that Mr. Strezhevoy can offer his guests. Landmarks of the region are also the picturesque bank of the Ob River.

Weather

Strezhevoy is distinguished by severe environmental conditions. The climate of the region is sharply continental, refers to the 2nd extremely uncomfortable zone of people. And although geographically it cannot be attributed to the Far North, due to weather conditions it is equated to such places. This region is also called the zone of northern taiga, ultraviolet deficit and swamps.



Education

Strezhevoy (Tomsk Oblast) is traditionally considered the city of oil industry workers, which is why a Training Center has been opened here, where training in oil specialties is conducted. There are also several children's institutions here: a center for further education, an art school, a ballroom dance club, a folk dance ensemble, and the mystification ensemble. Every year in March, the Sovremennik Palace hosts a concert entitled “On the Wave of the City”. Strezhevoy (Tomsk Region) can offer the smallest fun in the smallest children's entertainment centers "Broadway", "Planet", "Oscar" and "Develop!" For youth, a sports club "Paratrooper", an ice rink, several stadiums, an environmental education center and health centers "Burovik", "Neftyanik" and "Cedar" were organized.

The first school in the city of Strezhevoy (Tomsk region) began its work in 1969 (since 2012 it has been renamed to gymnasium). In total, there are currently 8 schools, one evening school and 11 kindergartens.

Development prospects

Strezhevoy belongs to single-industry towns, where the main industry is oil refining and oil production. In the near future it is planned to build a bridge over the Wahu River, this will contribute to the development of this northern town, where there is a huge reserve of gas and oil fields. The appearance of the bridge will also facilitate Strezhevoy's trade with other regions, and this will give a significant impetus to development. Strezhevoy, Tomsk Region (photo can be viewed below) is not only a city of oil workers, but a city of great expectations of our huge country.



Strezhevoy as a tourist route

Have you decided to visit Strezhevoy? The sights of this region will be of more interest to people who are interested in learning about the development of the oil industry of our country, as well as those who are fascinated by the charm of the Russian taiga. There are several comfortable hotels in this town, so there should be no problems with accommodation.

When is the best time to travel to this region? Keep in mind that winter lasts six months here. Snow falls already in September, and in October severe frosts already stand. Autumn and spring do not last very long. Therefore, the best option is the summer months. But at the same time, this region is beautiful in its own way in every season, so if you are attracted to the harsh northern regions, then you can safely come here. Once again, we recall the address: Russia, Tomsk Region, Strezhevoy.
