How it all began

The history of this amazing castle began not quite usually in the fall of 2006. At this time, the construction of the sports complex ‚ÄúAqua-Mir‚Äù was completed here, which included a swimming pool, baths of various types, a winter garden, a small room in the form of an old galleon. At this time, two young artists worked in Khryashchevka - Max Rakhmanov and Alexey Cheboksarinov. It is their idea to make a hotel in the form of a Gothic castle. From that moment, the fate of the building depended on the owner of the complex. I must admit that the customer was interested in this idea. The work of architects

When the time came to involve architects in the project, the task was not easy. It was supposed to be not just professionals in their field, but people who would be inspired by the idea and come to the solution of a difficult task in a non-standard way, with soul and creativity. It should be noted that the owner of the complex Oleg Kuzichkin knows how to infect people with his ideas. Thanks to his efforts, talented architects Yuri Guryanov and Valery Seglin joined the project. In the spring of 2007, large-scale construction of the hotel building began.

In the process, new ideas appeared that improved the original project, made it more interesting. When the facade was close to completion, there was an urgent need to develop its design. This work was headed by Max Rakhmanov, who attracted like-minded people to this interesting business.

It includes the main building (hotel), outbuilding (kitchen), entertainment area. The roof of the entertainment area is lined with stone and resembles a courtyard of a medieval European castle. It is designed for outdoor events. It quite comfortably accommodates 300 guests.

"Garibaldi Castle" (Khryashchevka, Tolyatti), a photo of which can be seen in the advertising booklets of travel agencies, took its main building to the hotel. The remaining floors are reserved for VIP rooms. In the outbuilding there is a kitchen and other office space.
