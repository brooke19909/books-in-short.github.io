The best hotels in Cyprus for families with children: photos and reviews of tourists

Cyprus is rightfully considered one of the most beautiful and ancient islands of the Mediterranean. The warm sea, magnificent sandy beaches and picturesque nature, annually attract millions of tourists from all over the globe here to rest.

Cyprus is an ideal holiday destination, where summer lasts five months, the tourist beach season begins in May and ends in early November. August and September are the months during which the peak of the influx of vacationers falls.

Even the most demanding travel enthusiasts cannot remain indifferent, making excursions to the ancient cities and the ruins of the fortresses built by the Byzantines. On this fabulous island, for a very reasonable fee, everyone can enjoy excellent Greek cuisine, admire the magnificent mountain scenery, sipping tart local wine.

What to visit with children in Cyprus

The whole family can spend time on the island all year round. And where else to go with children, if not to Cyprus, where there is a mild climate, a gentle clean sea, beautiful sandy beaches and a huge amount of entertainment. Typically, for most people, Cyprus causes associations with a serene pastime at beaches, discos or bars.

However, besides the sea and entertainment, Cyprus can offer its guests many more interesting activities or places to visit. On an island in a relatively small area, the largest number of ancient monuments is collected, such as the ruins of ancient temples, monasteries built by the Byzantines and medieval architecture.

In addition to educational excursions back centuries, you will be offered to take mountain walks, visit zoos, water parks, an aquarium and many other interesting places. With children, you must definitely watch the grand show "Dancing Fountains", which are located in the very center of Protaras.

All this will leave a lasting impression for a lifetime for both an adult and a small tourist.
