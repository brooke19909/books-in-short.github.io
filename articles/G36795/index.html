Nice beach, Crimea. Crimea resorts with sandy beaches. The best sandy beaches of Crimea

What must be in a decent resort? Of course a nice beach. Crimea is very, very famous for this. If all of them are listed, it will take more than one day, so we will talk about the best of them, those where all tourists dream of coming for a week or two. The sandy beach is good because both adults and children can relax here, as well as people who want to learn or already know how to swim.




Beaches of Donuzlav

Someone, not afraid of big words, will say with confidence: this is the best that is in the Crimea. And partly it will be true. But it is worth saying that sandy deposits not only contribute to the fact that people come there to rest, they also protect the coastline from collapses. For this simple reason, it is forbidden to use this sand as a building material. It is from here that the best beaches of Crimea originate. There are many vacationers here in season, however, free space can be found without problems. It will be useful to say that this beach stretches in two directions. On one is Evpatoria and Saki, and on the other is Olenevka and the Black Sea. If you want a more relaxing holiday, then feel free to go along the second route, if you like youth relaxation, then go the way to Yevpatoriya.

The best sandy beaches of Crimea for families with children





If you came to rest with your child in the Crimea, you should know that the place should be not only clean, but also warm. Kalamitsky Bay is well suited , where the water is clear and warm, and the beaches are made of clean sand. Evpatoria is also a good option, since the beach descends to the water gradually, without sharp cliffs, and this is what we need. Of course, you can relax with children in other places, but safety will be lower, and parents should be constantly on the alert. It can hardly be called a complete rest. You can still visit Saki, where also for the most part shallow and well-warmed beaches.

The symbol of the resort city of Feodosia

Many probably heard that in Crimea there is a Golden Beach. It is located in Feodosia, about six kilometers from the city center. Why gold, you ask? Yes, everything is simple, in the sun it has such a bright yellow color as that of real gold. Many tourists come here to relax under the warm rays of the sun on the clean sand. The width of the sand line is more than 200 meters, and the length is more than 3 kilometers, this is it, Golden Beach. Crimea is a very large peninsula, and there are plenty of places to relax, and, of course, this is the place where you must visit at least once in your life. Here you will rest from the hustle or bustle of the city, you will find here only a lot of vacationers. Golden Beach is public, you can get here for free. There are many cafes and restaurants on the territory, as well as various entertainments for both teenagers and adults. Every season, Golden Beach hosts programs for children and even performances by illusionists.

Sand lovers on the west coast

There are most of all sandy beaches, and if you want to take the best from this resort, then be sure to come here. Although this does not mean at all that in other places of Crimea something is worse. Healing sand baths are one of the advantages that should make you come to Saki or Yevpatoriya. Here you can treat the musculoskeletal system, lung diseases, disorders of the nervous system and much more.





Of course, there are also contraindications. For example, if you have insufficient blood circulation and heat treatments are contraindicated, it is strongly recommended not to abuse such treatment, although a few minutes in water and in the sun will not interfere with you. The procedure itself is extremely simple. You need to lie on your back and fall asleep in the sand. The layer should be 5-7 cm, the heart is left uncovered. In about ten minutes you can take a sea bath, the duration of which is about 10 minutes. The procedure is repeated several times a day.

Conclusion
