Defrost system

This model lacks such a popular function as No Frost. But speaking specifically about the defrosting system of the Bosch KGS39XW20R refrigerator , user reviews in most cases are positive. The manufacturer used the patented Low Frost technology. Therefore, we can conclude that the cameras will have to be defrosted in manual mode. In accordance with the instructions attached to the Bosch KGS39XW20R refrigerator, this should not be done more often than once every 6-8 months. And the process itself does not take much time.

The owner of the device will have to disconnect the device from the network, unload the products and wash the compartments. Ice does not form in the upper chamber, as in old Soviet apparatuses. In addition, the refrigerator with the lower Bosch KGS39XW20R freezer, thanks to two stand-alone compressors, allows you to defrost the compartments individually. Also, this feature is convenient when leaving for a long time. The refrigerator compartment can be turned off to save energy. Since many people have frosts stored in the lower part for a year, the operation of this camera can be left.

Advantages and disadvantages of two compressors

Since the Bosch KGS39XW20R refrigerator involves the operation of two compressors, it is necessary to find out whether such a purchase is beneficial, whether there are good reasons to purchase this equipment.

It is important to understand that not all manufacturers have similar models in the new line. The main advantage of this innovation is the uniform load distribution. After all, each compressor performs its role. The first controls and ensures the operation of the refrigerator compartment, and the second - freezers.

Other benefits include:

  1. Long service life, as the load is evenly distributed and each compressor is responsible for a specific compartment
  2. Convenient temperature setting system. If necessary, you can turn off one of the chambers, for example, for defrosting, washing or in case of vacation.




It is worth noting that Bosch KGS39XW20R repair, according to user reviews, is more expensive, even if one of the compressors fails. Since most often you have to change both motors. As a rule, masters recommend thinking about buying a new refrigerator. But do not put this fact above the rest when choosing a model. The company uses high-quality components, so repairs may not be required during the entire operational period.

The presence or absence of the ability to change the location of the shelves

A convenient storage system is organized inside the refrigerator. In the refrigerator compartment, 5 main shelves are fixed, which can be changed in height.
