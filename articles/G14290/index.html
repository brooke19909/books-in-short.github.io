LCD "Marine Gorgippia" in Anapa: reviews

Anapa is a sunny city located on the Black Sea coast. Comfortable climate, wide coastline with fine sand, warm and gentle sea make this city a favorite resort, especially for families with small children. Unfortunately, in Russia there are not many comfortable resort towns with a sandy beach and convenient entry into the sea, which is why Anapa receives hundreds of thousands of vacationers during the season. Real estate here is very popular. If you relax every year in Anapa, it's time to think about buying a property. LCD "Marine Gorgippia" is an excellent residential complex, which takes into account the requirements of most modern residents. Our task - within the framework of this review, to evaluate the project from all sides and answer the question of how suitable it is for living and recreation in the southern city. Our review is useful not only for vacationers, but also for those who live in the city all year round.





about the project

The concept of the residential complex "Marine Gorgippia" (Anapa) is thought out literally to the smallest detail. All efforts of the developer were made to create a modern complex with the most comfortable living conditions. For the construction of the residential complex, one of the most beautiful, picturesque and environmentally friendly places of the city of Anapa was chosen. The city is actively being built up, its borders are expanding. The new complex "Marine Gorgippia" will appear in a new residential neighborhood, spared from the old buildings. Nearby there are no industrial and agricultural enterprises, it provides the necessary ecology.

Water supply, electricity, gas - all the necessary communications are connected to residential buildings. Accordingly, there will be no difficulties with servicing the microdistrict; hot water and heat will be supplied without any interruptions.

Developer

For children

During the planning of the residential complex "Gorgippia Morskaya" (Anapa), great attention was paid to childhood. The complex is ideal for families with small children, and all because excellent conditions for them are created here. For active and outdoor games, playgrounds with slides, benches, sandboxes, funny houses and swings are intended. For sports there are excellent sports fields with artificial grass. In addition, the project includes the construction of a kindergarten and a comprehensive school, in which there will be enough places for all children from new buildings.





Infrastructure

How comfortable is it to live on the territory of the Sea Gorgippia complex (Anapa)? Reviews of the first real estate buyers confirm that many of them gave preference to this complex because of the development of its infrastructure. This is a new residential neighborhood, in the territory of which, in addition to kindergarten and school, there will be the necessary shops, service sector organizations.

The builder, judging by the numerous reviews of the residents, took into account the fact that almost every modern family has a personal car that needs a parking space. Wide courtyards with parking will provide each car owner with free space.

Apartments, layouts

The developer offered a variety of layouts for every taste. All apartments in the residential complex "Gorgippia Morskaya" are rented with a fine finish, which includes painting the walls and wallpapering, cement screed on the floor and linoleum with heat and sound insulation, the ceilings are decorated with modern water-based paint. The walls and floor in the bathrooms are lined with ceramic tiles, all the necessary plumbing and metering devices are installed. A sink and stove will appear in the kitchen. A great option for those who are not ready to wait and intend to call into their new family nest right after learning the keys.





You can buy an apartment in the LCD at a price of 42,000 per square meter. The developer offers many mortgage programs, which can be issued on favorable terms.

Is it worth buying

Reviews of the first buyers and those who managed to visit the construction site can characterize the project, give it the most objective assessment.

To summarize

LCD "Gorgippia Morskaya" is an excellent economy class housing project in Anapa. The developer was able to offer excellent conditions at a very affordable price.
