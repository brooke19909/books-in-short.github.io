Ice rink "Crystal" (Murom): description, address and schedule

Virtually every city has ice skating rinks. They are always in great demand, as many people prefer to have fun and actively spend their free time. The rink at the “Crystal” in Murom is liked not only by the younger generation, but also by adults. In the palace you can have a great time with your family, arrange a date or just go for fun.

general information

Crystal rink in Murom has been operating since 2011. He appeared thanks to the hockey player V. Tretyak. Today this place is popular among people of different ages. It hosts mass ice skating, competitions and hockey tournaments. Also, the site is designed to teach figure skating and hockey. About 50 people can be in classes here, and much more during amateur skating. Comfortable stands are equipped for the audience. It accommodates 350 people. For people with disabilities there is an elevator and a lift.

The skating rink has the necessary protection in the form of a grid and glass. Children are trained by two trainers. Toddlers and teenagers come to learn figure skating, playing hockey, speed skating and other sports.

In addition to practicing on ice, there are other sections in the palace for a healthy lifestyle. Recording is open for aerobics and choreography. The building also has a gym, there is a special area where you can play table tennis.

A cafe is open for all guests. Visitors to the rink can take a break by ordering hot tea or cocoa. Entrance to the rink will cost adults 70 rubles, and children and students 35 rubles. If necessary, you can rent skates - for this you will need a passport and 40 rubles.

Address

Finding a popular place for entertainment is easy. It was built next to the Losev Stadium and the park of the 50th anniversary of Soviet power. The ice rink "Crystal" (Murom) is located at the following address: Tikhomirova Boulevard, 15. It can be reached by the following transport:

  • To the stop "Ice Palace" Crystal "" - by buses No. 2l or No. 17l.
  • To the stop "Shop Crocodile" - by bus number 16 or number 18.

Mode of operation

Crystal ice rink schedule in Murom: every day from 8:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. The skate rental service operates on the same schedule.

Mass skating sessions take place several times a day: 14:00, 16:40, 17:10, 18:10, 19:20. On weekends, many sessions begin at 8 a.m. Depending on the time of the year and day of the week, schedule changes are possible, so you should look for more accurate information on the official portal.
