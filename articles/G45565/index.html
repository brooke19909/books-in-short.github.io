About hotel

Hotel Limas Hotel 3 * (Crete) is located, perhaps, in one of the most beautiful and picturesque areas of the island, near the famous village of Pigi, only 70 meters from the coast. The nearest village Rethymno is a resort town with developed infrastructure, you can get to it by regular bus, which regularly runs along the route. Travel time is 30-40 minutes. Very often, this hotel is chosen by families with young children who prioritize proximity to the airport. By local standards, the hotel is small - only 20 rooms. All tourists note that the owners of this mini-hotel are very welcoming and friendly people.





Population

All tourists and vacationers note that as soon as they get to the threshold of the 3 * hotel - Limas Hotel 3 * (Crete, Rethymnon, Greece) they are struck by the kindness of the owners. These are truly radiant, bright people, creating even in a private hotel an atmosphere of home comfort, warmth and care. If tourists for some reason arrive at the hotel before checkout time, if there are free rooms, they will be settled in the shortest possible time. Upon arrival, all guests will find a pleasant surprise - a huge basket of fresh fruit.

Rooms

All vacationers of Limas Hotel Rethimno 3 * can count on accommodation in one of 20 rooms of the category "Studio" and "Apartment". So, what can vacationers expect from a Greek 3 * hotel?





The hotel rooms are bright and spacious - this was noted by all tourists who preferred Limas Hotel 3 *. Reviews confirm the presence of a separate bathroom with a hairdryer and a set of necessary bath accessories. All vacationers, counting on freshness and coolness in the room, of course, will see air conditioning here, and quite effective and powerful. But its operation is carried out only at an additional cost. For a surcharge, you can use the safe for storing valuables and documents, as well as a minibar. Their technical equipment in the rooms there is a refrigerator, TV with satellite channels. You can spend evenings on an excellent balcony, which offers stunning views of the local beauty, and there are many of them.

Traces of fresh renovation are felt in the rooms: the walls are painted, the furniture is new and modern - everything is quite modest, budget, but at the same time clean and comfortable. There is not much furniture, but there is everything that is really needed on vacation: wide beds with comfortable mattresses, bedside tables, a table with a mirror, a chair. All those who choose apartments will get not only a full bed and a bathroom, but also a small kitchen equipped with necessary appliances. There is also a dining table with chairs and comfortable sofas where you can sit watching TV. Apartments are an excellent choice for tourists who are planning a vacation with their family or a large company: there are enough beds, space and light.





Among the shortcomings of the hotel, first of all, I would like to note the failure of the sewage system. Namely, in the season when the hotel is ‚Äúcrammed‚Äù by vacationers, private blockages occur. To do this, it is quite enough to get paper or sand in the sewer. In general, the hotel is quite quiet, soundproofing at a decent level: nothing will interfere with your relaxation and disturb your peace.

Room service

Rooms are cleaned daily during the absence of vacationers. Towels and bed linen are changed only a few times during the entire stay. Do not forget that this is a 3 * hotel, so it is stupid to expect anything supernatural from it.

Nutrition

Meals according to the "breakfast" and "half board" in the own restaurant can be offered to all vacationers by the Limas Hotel 3 * (Crete, Rethymnon). Rethymno is a place with amazing cuisine, so you should not refuse to visit local restaurants and cafes, even if breakfast and dinner are included in the price.

Now for the food in the hotel. It does not differ in variety. For breakfast you can see eggs, yoghurts, dry cereal with milk, several types of meat and cheese slices, some vegetables, drinks. Dinners are more varied: you can see meat, sometimes fish. tourists complain about the lack of seafood, fresh vegetables, herbs and even seasonal fruits. If you do not take a room with a kitchen, it is better to refuse food and eat at local restaurants - the prices are reasonable, they cook very tasty. Moreover, on the beach you can always buy soft drinks, ice cream, as well as a snack of boiled corn - a favorite treat of children.





Service

Limas Hotel 3 * has a bicycle rental service: in fact, many vacationers rent this type of transport and go on a trip around the island. The places here are really beautiful, picturesque, so not only adults, but also the youngest tourists will enjoy the trip.

Wireless high-speed internet is available throughout the hotel. Tourists note that even in the rooms the speed is excellent: it is enough to communicate on social networks, view news and search for important information. A car rental point is located across the street from the hotel - it is very convenient for those who want to go around the whole island and visit its most beautiful places, for example, coves and coves surrounded by greenery.

Entertainment on site

The presence of a swimming pool on the territory of the Limas Hotel 3 * is an excellent addition to its main advantages. Of course, it‚Äôs stupid to expect that if there is a gorgeous sea within walking distance, someone will splash around in the pool, but sometimes vacationers have a good time here. Sun beds and umbrellas are free for all hotel guests.





All lovers of outdoor games can go to the tennis court, fight with their room in beach volleyball or football - there are a lot of vacationers here in the season, so there is how to organize your leisure time with benefits for body and health. The hotel administration also took care of the children - an excellent playground is intended for them. Children are happy to frolic here, and parents can not worry about the organization of their leisure.

Beach

The beach is just a few minutes away from the Limas Hotel 3 * (Crete). Photos of tourists who are attached to almost every review confirm that the beach here is not the best. Yes, the coast is wide, the sand is fine, the sea is clean, but entering the water can be difficult. If you want to see really gorgeous beaches, which are described by many guidebooks in Greece, go to Rethymnon - it is only 8 km away - here you will really find snow-white sand, a gentle slope into the water and a quiet calm sea - a paradise for lovers of beach holidays and kids.





The beach is well-maintained: for a fee you can use sun loungers and umbrellas, rent water activities, go fishing or diving on the open sea - there are plenty of entertainments here. And one more thing: in season the sun is quite active here, so you should not neglect the use of products with a high protection factor, as well as be sure to cover your head.

To summarize

Limas Hotel 3 * is an excellent hotel for a relaxing family vacation, including with young children. If you are accustomed to a vibrant nightlife, planning to visit bars, restaurants and noisy discos with incendiary music, this place is definitely not suitable for you. If you want to enjoy the peace, tranquility and measured relaxation, to stay away from the bustle of noisy cities, feel free to book a room here and start packing your bags.

This option will surely appeal to all those tourists who are looking for a budget option, but at the same time rely on comfortable living conditions and good service.
