Crocheted mittens: schemes with a description

In winter, you can‚Äôt do without such an accessory as mittens. Why not use the acquired skill and crochet the mittens? The schemes for them are very different. In addition, you can decorate them for every taste.





Tied in a circle with imitation elastic bands

Before crocheting mittens , you need to choose the right yarn. These will look spectacular if you take a thread with a characteristic of 100 grams per 400 meters and stop at two close shades.

Suitable for such a yarn would be a hook number 3.5. If the accessory is planned to be used in the winter, then wool must be present in the composition of the yarn. And the more, the better.

You need to start crocheting mittens (schemes will help) with a ring of 5 air loops. Then alternate the thread, knitting in one shade only one row. You get a pattern that looks like a chessboard.

In the 1st row, after three lifting loops, you need to connect 11 columns with a crochet. By the way, the number of lifting loops will be the same for all rows of this figure.

2nd row: double crochet at the base of the lifting chain, then from the top of each column of the previous row, tie a regular double crochet and another before work.

In the 3rd row, the pattern of the second is repeated. The 4th row consists of alternating columns before and after work. All the following rows completely repeat the fourth. There should be so many to reach the cut for the thumb. Here, in the next row, it is supposed to make a pass from the chain formed by 13 loops. Then continue the circular pattern to the wrist.

Now it‚Äôs the turn to crochet the thumb of the mittens. Its schemes will repeat the main pattern. The main difficulty lies in recruiting the initial row.

The thread for this is supposed to be fixed in the inner part of the section, to make three lifting loops and two unbound knit stitches, which then close with one loop. It will turn out to be filled with a corner connecting the finger with the mittens.

Then tie 11 columns according to the pattern of the mittens. Repeat the triple double crochet, closed with one loop in the other corner, and again tie the columns behind and before work according to the picture. Continue to knit a circular pattern to the end of the finger. From above, connect the incision using single crochet posts.





How to decorate such mittens

They themselves are already self-sufficient. Therefore, if such a crocheted mittens (patterns come in handy) will be worn by a woman or a man, then it is better to leave everything as it is.

But for a teenager-girl or a child-preschooler it is better to sew a suitable decoration. It can be a crocheted flower, or cute pompoms.

The main thing is that children's mittens crocheted are to their owner's liking.





Related of two halves

Despite the unusual execution, it is not difficult to knit such mittens crochet. For starters, there is still a recommendation to choose thick yarn. Such that about 100 grams of weight accounted for about 120 meters. The hook for such a thread should be at number 6.

You need to start crocheting mittens (for beginners) with a chain of loops. For the specified characteristics of the yarn, there should be so many that it is equal to the length from the wrist to the end of the little finger.

Knit double crochet on a chain. At the top of the chain, make five of these columns from one base - this will be a curve for the fingers. Then tie a series of columns with a crochet on the other side of the chain. The first row is finished.

The second repeats the first. Only the increase in rounding should be done gradually. It is recommended to divide it into three times two columns from one base. If the thread is thick enough and crocheted children's mittens, then this amount may be enough. Otherwise, it is necessary to tie so many similar rows so that the width of the product covers the palm.

Now you need to tie another half of the mittens. Now they need to be folded together and tied with crochets along the entire length, leaving a cut for the thumb.

In a circle of the slot for the cuff, tie the rows: the first - from single crochet, then the second with double crochet, decorate with a series of lush columns and finish with a crochet step.

The finger starts to crochet with crochets. Continue the same rows to the desired length. To get the finger with a bevel, you need to decrease one loop on the inside of the circle, and add one on the outside. This will produce the same number of loops in each row. Then make a rounding and fasten the thread.





Mittens decoration option: kittens

Now, how to crochet mittens is understandable. It remains to consider decoration for children. The girl will like the faces of kittens on mittens.

For them, you have to connect two triangles for the ears. Their base should not be less than 5 loops. From the first row, immediately begin the reduction of loops. Complete with three columns associated with one vertex. Such triangles-ears need 4 pieces.

Sew them to the mittens of the girl. Use green beads for the eye, and make the nose out of a large pink button. Fix a few black dense threads for the antennae under the nose.





Mittens decoration option: bears

For him, you need to tie circles-ears. And the same circle for the nose. Only its size should be much smaller.

The muzzle is oval. To do this, the initial chain does not need to be locked in a ring, but simply tied at once on both sides. The muzzle for decorating the mittens should be larger than the ears. For eyes, use small black beads.
