Reviews for the hotel Dreams Beach Resort 5 * (Marsa Alam)

Resort Dreams Beach Resort 5 * (Marsa Alam) is an unforgettable vacation in an atmosphere of comfort and luxury, amidst green gardens and tropical beaches.





Hotel Description

The luxury hotel Dreams Beach Resort 5 * (Marsa Alam) is the best place for those who are used to absolute comfort and appreciate high-quality service. Here you will find spacious design rooms, friendly staff, and blue pools, and bars with unlimited drinks for every taste.

Depending on which category of room you are staying in, a charming seascape or a beautiful view of the pool will open in front of you. The apartments have everything that a tourist may need while relaxing. Also the Dreams Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Marsa Alam) boasts a rich tourist infrastructure.

Going on vacation to this resort complex, you can be sure that you will find yourself in a real oasis. In Dreams Beach Resort 5 * (Marsa Alam) you can enjoy a leisurely and imposing pastime on the beach or by the pool, as well as immerse yourself in the atmosphere of active events and sports games.





Where is the hotel situated

The hotel complex Dreams Beach Resort (Marsa Alam) 5 * is located in sunny Egypt, in the resort town of Marsa Alam of the same name . It is located about 40 km (which is about an hour away by bus or taxi) from the nearest international airport.

Room Reviews

Accommodation in 230 comfortable rooms offers its guests a resort hotel Dreams Beach Resort (Marsa Alam) 5 *. The photos taken by tourists indicate that all apartments are quite spacious and bright, and are also characterized by a sophisticated and elegant design.

Standard rooms are slightly different in size and are designed to receive one, two or three guests. They are a bedroom with single or large king beds. There is also a dressing table and a large mirror above it. In addition, without fail there is a bathroom and a small but very cozy balcony.

For large families or companies of tourists, family-type rooms are provided. They are two combined bedrooms, as well as two bathrooms, which makes living in apartments even more comfortable. Most often, one of the rooms is used as a nursery, and the second is occupied by parents.

The best rooms of the Dreams Beach Resort (Marsa Alam) 5 * hotel are suites. This is a huge bedroom, which is separated by an interior door from a small bright living room. The rooms have a lot of beautiful designer furniture, as well as all kinds of decor.
