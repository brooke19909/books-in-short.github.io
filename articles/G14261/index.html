Storage of shoes in the hallway: ideas

One of the most needed pieces of furniture in the hallway is the shoe rack. After all, without it, it‚Äôs quite difficult to keep the corridor neat. Even in a small family there are quite a lot of shoes. There is one that is actively used. Another is stored until a suitable season. That is why it is necessary to carefully consider the storage of shoes. This will not only create a comfortable atmosphere in the hallway. Thanks to thoughtful storage, the shoes will last a long time.





Design features

Is it possible to store shoes with clothes? This issue is constantly being actively discussed. Most people conclude that it is better to provide a separate place for boots and sneakers. After all, it is much more convenient and more correct.

The main arguments in favor of a separate storage system are the following points:

  1. Shoes emit a specific odor that can spread to clothing.
  2. Boots are often an excellent bait for moths.
  3. Sneakers, boots are a dusty and dirty part of the wardrobe. Therefore, it is better to keep them separate.
  4. If the storage of shoes is made in the "native" boxes, then in the closet takes up a lot of space.

Varieties of shoe racks

Those who decide to provide a separate place for the dustiest part of the wardrobe, need to determine what type of this furniture suits them.

There are several varieties of shoe racks:

  1. Open . This is the most common type of furniture designed for shoes. It has both advantages and disadvantages. Open storage of shoes in the hallway is an opportunity to quickly dry it. Therefore, unpleasant odors will not occur. But this design does not protect sandals and boots from dust. Over time, the shoe rack can turn into a rather dirty piece of furniture.
  2. Closed . Most often this is a locker, a bench, an ottoman. A shoe rack with a seat has become very common. This option solves several problems at once in a small hallway. After all, this is not only a place to store. The soft seat allows you to comfortably shoe. The pros and cons of such furniture are completely opposite to those described above. A closed cabinet does not allow boots and boots to dry properly. Accordingly, an unpleasant odor cannot be avoided. But at the same time everything is completely protected from dust. To provide at least a little ventilation, it is recommended to consider additional openings in the box.
  3. Slim . This is a special kind of shoe rack. Such furniture is very narrow and high. This is the choice of owners of small apartments. The width of the shoe is about 20 cm. It has folding shelves that open at an angle of 30-90 degrees. The smaller the angle, the greater the inconvenience of getting shoes. Unfortunately, it does not provide for the storage of various accessories. Therefore, for the brushes will have to find another place. And the most important minus of slim shoes is its instability. Therefore, when placing such furniture, additional fastenings to the wall should be provided.




Materials for shoes

This piece of furniture is easy to make yourself. You can have a variety of ideas, if you need to streamline the storage of shoes, ideas. With your own hands you can create quite unique items. In this case, it is not necessary to be a furniture master.

But before you consider the process of manufacturing shoes, you should focus on the materials.

You can make a storage system from:

  1. Thin chromed metal tubes. The constructions from them are light and very compact. This is a great option for a small hallway. The shoe rack has good ventilation. But there is a drawback. Dirty shoes can only be stored on the bottom shelf.
  2. Natural wood. These shoes look solid. Most often they are used for halls, designed in the classical style. The disadvantage of wooden models is their high susceptibility to moisture. Shoes can be put in such a design only in a well-dried condition.
  3. Rattan . Despite its belonging to the ‚Äúwooden‚Äù products, the shoe rack is very practical. It is resistant to moisture, well ventilated.
  4. MDF . This is the most common material for making shoes. It has several advantages, such as low price, excellent view, good quality.
  5. Plastic . Furniture made of this material is lightweight. She is quite mobile. Most often, plastic is used in the country.




So, the question arose sharply before you, how to think over the storage of shoes in the hallway (options).

With your own hands you can make a variety of models. A few good decisions will help you choose the most optimal way.

Water pipe shelf

In the house there will always be objects that can no longer be used for their intended purpose. A wonderful shelf can be created from plastic water pipes. The manufacturing process is simple, and saving the family budget is substantial.

Working process:

  1. Initially, make a drawing taking into account all the necessary dimensions.
  2. You will need crossbars and fittings. Therefore, if they are not in the workshop, then it is necessary to purchase.
  3. Plastic pipes must be cut into cylinders of the same size. The length should match the size of the shoes.
  4. Now the cylinders are attached to each other. You can give any shape to the future shoebox - a triangle, a hexagon.
  5. On the sides, the structure is fastened with a crossbar.
  6. Do not forget to paint it.
  7. It remains to screw the finished product to the wall.

This is a lightweight design that provides for open storage of shoes.



Ladder shoe rack

Look around - you may have great options on how to arrange your shoe storage. Ideas that come to your head can be a great solution.

For example, if you have an old stepladder, then it can become an excellent shoe shelf. Such a design is able to create even a beginner master.

Work process:

  1. Saw a piece of unnecessary stepladder. Its dimensions should correspond to the size of the future product. Choose the number of steps as desired.
  2. The ladder should be cleaned well and sanded thoroughly.
  3. The edges of the product should be fixed to the wall.
  4. Hooks are mounted on the crossbars. On them the shoes will be stored in limbo.




Pallet use

As you can see, there are a variety of ideas, if you need to think about storing shoes in the hallway.

The original option may be the pallet. It must be cleaned, painted, varnished. This design is mounted vertically on the wall. Shoes are perfectly stored in the slots.

But this option is more suitable for a country house.

Great organizer

Even a child can make it independently. You will need a piece of dense tissue. Pockets for shoes are sewn onto it. Such an organizer can be attached to the wall or to the crossbar.





But this design has a significant drawback. Keep shoes in it only clean and dry.
