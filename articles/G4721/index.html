Hotels in Saratov: rating, addresses, room selection, booking, quality of service, additional services and visitor reviews

Hotels in Saratov are located in different areas of the city. Therefore, tourists can choose a suitable accommodation option regarding the location, price and comfort level of rooms.

The article provides a list of the most popular hotels in Saratov. He will help you make the right choice of places for temporary residence in the city.

"Slovakia"

This hotel is located on the street. Lermontov, 30. The hotel is located almost on the banks of the Volga. The rooms offer beautiful views of the area. In the institution, customers can choose from rooms of different levels of comfort:

  • budget double room with 2 single beds;
  • Suite
  • deluxe suite;
  • Executive Suite
  • exclusive suite;
  • single economy class;
  • standard.

All air-conditioned rooms have a private toilet and a bathroom. The rooms are modernly renovated in a classic style. Each room has a TV, telephone, wardrobes, desks and chairs. Superior rooms have safes.

The hotel provides customers with the services of a business center. It has all the necessary office equipment. This Saratov hotel has two restaurants with European and Russian cuisine.

Guests can take advantage of free internet throughout, and an ATM in the lobby. The hotel provides a shuttle service for a fee.

"Venice"

This place belongs to the best hotels in Saratov. The hotel is furnished in a Venetian style. This special atmosphere is already felt at the entrance to the building. Beautiful metal stairs and paintings depicting Italian streets mentally send guests to this romantic country.

The hotel offers customers a choice of the following rooms:

  • double standard with a large bed;
  • comfort with a double bed;
  • Junior Suite
  • Suite with Jacuzzi
  • single standard and comfort;

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, and free internet. Hotel Saratov has a small restaurant and bar. It serves dishes from Italian and Russian cuisine.

The hotel has a room for honeymooners. It created a very romantic atmosphere typical of Venice. A young couple will surely remember for their whole life their first wedding night spent in Venice.

"Pioneer Suite"

This business hotel in Saratov is located on the avenue to them. S. M. Kirova, 15/1. The building at first sight is striking in its size. Here guests are offered rooms of different prices and comfort levels:

  • single (economy);
  • double (standard);
  • studio (one-room);
  • Junior Suite (Business);
  • Junior Suite
  • apartments (one-room);
  • luxury (business);
  • apartments (two rooms);
  • VIP suite.

All rooms are renovated in a classic style. For the equipment of the rooms, natural wood furniture is used. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, small refrigerators, electric kettles, and some have safes.

Each room has its own bathroom and bathroom with all necessary hygiene items. The price includes breakfast, which is served in one of the restaurants. The average price per night in a hotel is 2500-3000 rubles.

The hotel has a large business center. There is a conference room with all the necessary equipment. After conducting business negotiations at the request of customers in one of the restaurants can be held a buffet banquet.

"Visit"

This mini-hotel in Saratov is located on the street. Soviet, 6a. The guest house has 8 equipped rooms. Here, guests can choose one of the options below:

  • double (comfort);
  • Suite
  • apartments.

Tourists who like not to endure excessive fuss like to stay here. Near the hotel there is a beautiful park, in which in the evening you can safely walk after a busy day.

The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay:

  • TV
  • split system;
  • refrigerator;
  • mini bar;
  • telephone;
  • safe;
  • free Internet.

Each room has a spacious private bathroom and bath. Here, customers are provided with all the necessary hygiene items. The hotel has a small but cozy cafe. Breakfast is served here and is included in the price.

Park-Hotel "Cherry Mountain"

This hotel is located on Prospect 2 Pharmacy 11. The complex occupies a fairly vast territory. They are maximally landscaped and resembles a park area. The whole territory is guarded around the clock.

The hotel offers the following categories of rooms at the choice of clients:

  • double and triple standard;
  • Junior Suite
  • junior suite family;
  • Suite

All rooms have air conditioning, TV, telephone, safe, comfortable furniture. Guests can use the free internet throughout. The rooms have a toilet and spacious bathtubs. Guests are provided with disposable hygiene kits.

The rooms have large windows that offer beautiful views of the local landscapes. The approximate average cost of rooms per day is from 2800 to 3500 rubles. The price includes a delicious breakfast served at the local restaurant.

Guests of the complex can visit the on-site sauna, large pool, spa. The park hotel has a modern bowling room with several lanes and a bar.

The hotel has several restaurants with large banquet rooms, a conference center.

Hotel Chain in Saratov "Mirage"

A large company operates in the city, which contains several complexes at once.

  1. Hotel Oscar is located on the street. Working, 112 a. The institution is located in the city center, so many attractions and business centers are within walking distance from it. The hotel has 41 rooms. All rooms are decorated in a classic style and equipped with all necessary furniture and appliances. The complex has a restaurant with a banquet hall and a business center.
  2. The hotel and recreation complex "Sokol" is located on the street. Lunar, 27a. The hotel has 92 rooms from economy to luxury. There is a park area around the complex. The hotel has a conference room and a restaurant. Hotel guests can visit the spa with a large pool.
  3. The hotel complex "Acropolis" is located on the street. Vokzalnaya, 2. This area is somewhat remote from the center and is quiet from the roads. Guests can choose from cozy rooms with an original design. The ceilings here are made in the form of a vault of heaven. The complex has a restaurant serving freshly baked pastries. The complex has a Russian bathhouse and a Turkish hammam.
  4. The hotel complex "Mirage" is located in the Leninsky district on the street. Kamskaya, 1. There are 33 rooms at an affordable price. The center has a spa complex, gym, massage rooms, billiards, a solarium and a hairdresser. The price includes a nutritious breakfast organized in a local buffet restaurant. The spacious banquet rooms often host various celebrations.

The hotel chain also includes several suburban complexes.

Hotel reviews in Saratov

About the hotels described in the article, there are mostly positive reviews on various sites. The rating of these hotels is higher than 9 points out of 10. Guests note that the rooms are always clean and comfortable.

Particular attention is paid by tourists to the gorgeous views from the window of the Slovakia Hotel. In the complexes of the Mirage network, tourists like a wide range of entertainment moments.

In Venice, guests celebrate delicious food in the restaurant and original design. Park Hotel is more suitable for relaxation. Here, tourists like to stroll around in the evening.

Of the negative comments, only those related to the cost per night can be noted. But taking into account the quality of service and the level of comfort of the rooms, prices can be considered adequate.
