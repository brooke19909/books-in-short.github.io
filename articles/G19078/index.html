Antalya is one of the most famous and backward centers of tourist pilgrimage. Unless, of course, not the most popular. Here, beach vacations are most harmoniously combined with informative excursion programs and active sports.

However, calling Antalya a resort is not entirely true. This is a whole tourist complex, which includes the popular resorts of Alanya, cozy Side, fashionable Belek, the famous Kemer, as well as a number of small towns - Aks, Gyunyuk, Beldibi, Tekirova, Chamyuva, Kirish.

Luxurious Antalya hotels, perfect cleanliness and landscaping, stunning landscapes of the coastal strip make the resort so attractive for vacationers. Here are the warmest beaches and the most diverse recreational and cultural holidays. What else do you need from a seaside resort?

As a resort town, Antalya specializes in providing tourist services. Therefore, Antalya hotels are diverse and almost perfect in terms of service and amenities. In them you can enjoy solitude and silence or plunge into the energetic wave of discos. All the choice of vacationers.

Hotels in Turkey Antalya can be chosen for every taste: from luxury to the most economical options. There are few of them in the city, but the suburbs impress with their quantity and variety.



General recommendations for choosing a hotel



If you want mountains, pine groves and beautiful sea views nearby, hotels you need to choose a hotel in or near Kemer, Belbidi, Tekirova and Goynuk.

If you prefer orange groves, then book a hotel in Manavgat or Side. However, it is not so beautiful here. Because near the groves there will certainly be desert territories that will not please the eye.

The best and most expensive Antalya hotels are located on the first coastline, a few tens of meters from the sea. These are 4 and 5 star hotels for the most demanding.

3 and 2 star hotels are more democratic. Living in them is not affordable, and the conditions and service are pleasing to a high level. Local hotels of not even the highest price rates and stars are characterized by amazing conditions, many times higher than the expectations of those who come to Turkey for the first time. A feature of Turkish hotels is that here, booking a hotel in advance, you can not be afraid to make mistakes and disappointment. Any hotel here is great. Almost every hotel has at its disposal terminals with Internet access, which is convenient to use as a communication channel with the house.

In general, it is generally recognized that the most optimal price and quality hotels in Antalya are 4-star hotels. They provide an ideal price-quality ratio and practically do not give way to five-star ones (except for the price). We do not recommend taking 2-star hotels - you can remain disappointed, although, in general, they are quite good. The level of service will be quite decent and will be fully justified by modest prices.
