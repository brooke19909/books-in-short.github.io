The Dodecanisi archipelago (that is how it sounds in Greek) is located in the Aegean Sea near the Turkish coast. Location

Corali Hotel 4 * is located just 1.8 km from the picturesque village of Tigaki. The capital city of Kos is about 11 km from here, and Hippocrates International Airport is 15 km away. There are two terminals operating there, and planes from Germany, Italy, the Netherlands and other EU countries arrive in season. Tourists from Russia to Athens need to change to either local planes or a ferry. The flight time from Moscow to the Greek capital is 3.5 hours, and then to the island another 50 minutes. The ferry trip will last about 14 hours. From October to the end of April, international flights are canceled, they remain only from Athens, Rhodes and Crete. Ferries run year-round. To reach them to Kos is possible not only from Athens, but also from many other Greek islands.

Hotel Description

Corali Hotel 4 * is a medium-sized hotel, representing one building and several pretty bungalows.

All buildings are located in an unusually beautiful landscaped area, where the paths in the emerald green grass are tiled, affectionately waving palm branches, and flowers are everywhere full of flowers. Adult guests will undoubtedly be pleased with two large pools with comfortable sunbeds and wide umbrellas, tennis courts, a fitness room, sports grounds and free parking. Children will be interested in the playground with slides, swings and carousels. There are for children and two separate children's pools. The hotel's infrastructure also includes a restaurant, two bars and a small shop.

Rooms

Corali Hotel 4 * offers to accommodate 180 standard rooms, designed for one, two and three people. There are also two-bedroom apartments and a small kitchen. All rooms have a balcony or terrace, furnished with a table and several chairs, TV, telephone, small refrigerator, safe and hygiene room, which provides soap, shampoo, toilet paper. There is no carpet on the floor, and it is not needed, because in summer it is already hot in the summer. Rooms are cleaned regularly at the allotted time (usually from morning until 15:00). Sheets, pillowcases are changed every two days, and towels daily. There is no great luxury in the rooms, but all the furniture and appliances are of excellent quality and functionality. The windows overlook the surroundings and the hotel grounds.
