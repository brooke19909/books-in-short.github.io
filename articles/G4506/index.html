Hotel Dobrudja 3 (Bulgaria, Albena): location, service and reviews

The resort town of Albena has gained fame as a fashionable and modern complex in Bulgaria. The area is popular with natural resources, healing mineral waters, thalassotherapy and mud therapy. Mostly people with gastrointestinal disorders and heart diseases come here. Highly appreciated by the resort are parents with children, athletes and fans of a measured and serene pastime.

The dense forests, picturesque landscapes and the pristine beaches of the Black Sea coast contribute to a full rest. Since 1960, the green resort has been actively developing and is overgrown with hotel complexes. In the coastal zone, more than fifty boarding houses function, which also perform a recreational function. We offer you to get acquainted with one of the many hotels called Dobrudja 3 - a magnificent modern hotel amid a unique paradise nature.



General information

This is the largest complex in its category. A huge 17-story building rises in the center of the resort area, surrounded by coniferous vegetation. From the windows of the hotel you can see almost the entire town with snow-white beaches and outstanding objects. High-rise is a certain guideline for confused customers, because it can be seen for several kilometers.

Hotel Dobrudja 3 (Bulgaria) is ideally located relative to the airport (40 km), bus station (700 m) and the famous resort of Balchik - 15 km. Near the residential building are concentrated entertainment venues. The complex provides quality services for business travelers and couples. The hotel has a balneotherapy center "Medica". In the medical center you can undergo treatment, adjust the weight and bring the emotional state in order.



Number of rooms

At Dobrudja 3 you can choose either a standard room or comfortable apartments with a balcony and a seating area. Most of the rooms are equipped with air conditioning. For personal use, guests have a bath and a toilet. A TV with cable channels is available. Guests can rent a fridge, hairdryer and safe. The rooms have a small bar with paid drinks. Daily scheduled cleaning is available.

Nutrition

Hotel Dobrudja 3 does not work on an all-inclusive basis. Two meals a day are offered in the hotel's dining room. The building on the 17th floor has a panoramic restaurant with stunning city views. There is a cafeteria and a 24-hour bar. Each institution has professional chefs offering to try the delicacies of Chinese, European and national cuisines.



Coast

About 200 meters from the residential building of the Dobrudja 3 hotel (Bulgaria, Albena) there is a public sandy beach. You can walk to the coastline or take the comfortable bus, which runs there and back daily. Each number is assigned: a free sun umbrella and two sun loungers. On the coast there is a volleyball net, there is a rental point for sports equipment.

Infrastructure

Hotel Dobrudja 3 has a medical center offering tourists a course of therapeutic procedures. Before the appointment of certain therapeutic sessions, a narrow-profile doctor examines and consults you. The complex specializes in paraffin therapy, magnetotherapy, hydrotherapy, mud therapy and hydrotherapy.





The spa invites you to truly relax with your body and soul. Experienced craftsmen work here who will correctly tell you about your problems and advise wellness programs based on individual indicators. In the center there is a sauna, an indoor pool, a Turkish bath, massage rooms, a jacuzzi and a solarium.

The hotel has an open-air pool (with necessary amenities). In the courtyard there are playgrounds for active games of football, volleyball, mini-golf, a billiard room and a bowling alley. To keep fit, there is a gym. Daily aerobics for women. Near the pension there is a large tennis court and a riding school.

Late in the evening, entertainment events and dances for all age groups are organized. Equipped with hotel parking, laundry and meeting rooms. For tourists there is a tour agency, an exchange office and a rental car. Areas for children are equipped: a pool and a playground.

To summarize: the hotel is aimed at an unbiased audience of people who come to rest for new experiences, and not for luxury.

The Dobrudja 3 complex (Bulgaria) receives mixed reviews: most of the visitors were delighted with attentive service, delicious food and a central location. The rest of the tourists did not quite like the rooms and furnishings throughout the boarding house. In general, this is a good option for a budget holiday. It is calm, clean and comfortable. Much depends on the mood and personal perception. Have a nice and positive vacation!
