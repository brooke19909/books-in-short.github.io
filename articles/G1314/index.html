Philippines. Reviews of tourists say that you need to go!

Philippines ... Reviews of tourists say that this is a great place for all travelers and you must visit this country without fail. Why? There are actually many reasons. But among the main ones, I would like to highlight the wonderful cuisine, exotic nature, beautiful resorts, amazing ultramodern cities with skyscrapers and a good climate.

Section 1. Philippines. Reviews of tourists. General information



Many tourists come here every year, although holidays here are considered relatively expensive. Couples, eco-tourists, divers - here everyone finds a lot of interesting things. You can spend time on several islands - combined tours give this opportunity.

The capital is named Manila (Philippines). Reviews unanimously agree that this is a huge conglomerate, which includes 18 cities. In the central one, Metro Manila, you can dine in the fine restaurant located on the street, the decoration of which resembles old Spain. It is recommended to walk along the promenade and relax in one of the establishments, where you should definitely order a delicious national dish. Local restaurants also offer eastern and western dishes. Seafood is prepared taking into account the taste of each individual tourist!

Section 2. Philippines. Reviews of tourists. Features of rest



The Philippines has a hot tropical climate and high humidity. In May, the rainy season begins, which is very long and ends only in November. The Philippines has a relatively dry season between December and April. The climate feature of resorts located in the center of the archipelago is the absence of a rainy season. When planning a trip to this country, it should be noted that climatic conditions change throughout the year. In this regard, three seasons can be distinguished: from March to May - warm, from June to November - rainy, from December to February - cold.

Reviews of tourists about the Philippines indicate that there is excellent communication between the islands, both on ferries and on airplanes. In cities, you can conveniently travel by bus, taxi and rail. Jeepney - minibus, which in the past was a military jeep. This is a very popular form of transport. Exotic version - tricycle rickshaws. There are also motorcycles equipped with passenger strollers.

Earthquakes, floods and volcanic eruptions are dangerous. However, do not refuse to travel to this exotic country because of the likelihood of natural disasters - after all, destructive natural phenomena do not happen so often. Usually you have to wait until the message resumes due to the cancellation of aircraft flights. The rest in this country is safe for tourists.

Section 3. Philippines. Reviews of tourists. sights



Diving in the Philippines is great! The tropical sea, coral reefs, invertebrates, sponges, a variety of fish - all this appears to the enthusiastic snorkeling tourists.

Fearless surfers find excellent conditions for their exciting activities on the beaches of this country. On the island of Shiragao, the World Surfing Championship is held in September-October.

There is so much entertainment in the Philippines that no one will be bored! Those who are interested in culture are waiting for museums, exhibitions, ancient monuments of architecture.
