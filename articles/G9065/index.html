Master class: hat-hat for the girl for the winter

This is the most popular and practical hat for children. In such a hat frosts, a cold wind and snowfall will not be scary. And most importantly, the problem of all mothers has been solved - there is a scarf. Many adults also wear it during walks in the harsh winter season. In this article we want to share a master class. It will tell you how to fit a hat-helmet for a girl. You will need a couple of free evenings. Getting down.





Knitting a hat-hat for a girl

For work you will need:

  • ordinary knitting needles No. 3;
  • wool yarn (100 grams);
  • hook number 3;
  • your skillful pens;
  • circular knitting needles No. 3.

What patterns will be used during knitting

We will perform:

  • Hosiery knitting. The back loops in the front rows are knitted.
  • The scythe. Knits according to the following pattern. In the first row we collect facial loops. In the second - wrong side. We knit the third row like this. We leave three loops on the auxiliary spoke. Next, we collect three more. Grab loops from the auxiliary knitting needle. Thus, we knit crossed loops. In the fourth, we recruit the wrong ones. We knit the fifth row with facial loops. In the sixth - on the wrong side. Knit the seventh row as the first.
  • Garter stitch.





Master class: how to tie a hat-helmet for a girl

We begin to knit the bar. She will walk along the face contour. We take knitting needles No. 3 and we gain seven loops. We knit 125 rows of garter stitch . When finished, close the loop. Cut the thread. Next, take a hook and knit them crochet. We knit again. We knit forty-three rows in order of the following loops: one edge; two garter stitches; two facial smoothness; six "oblique"; two facial smoothness; four handkerchief viscous; two facial smoothness; two handkerchief viscous; one edge. Having knitted everything, we will divide our product into three parts. In the middle we will gain 36 loops, and on the sides - 35.

In the 46th, we remove the first loop and do not knit. We knit loops according to the previous scheme, and we knit the thirty-sixth along with the next. Turn over knitting again. It should be a "cap". We continue to knit in this way to the 51st row. Next, we make symmetrical increases in the middle loops. Then we start adding two loops in each sixth row. The hat-helmet for the girl is almost ready. There is very little left. So, we decrease until there are sixteen loops in the middle. We knit the cap when the side patterns end. Then close the loop.





Now proceed to the neck. We collect air loops. We connect and knit in a circle three rows. Then we make the increase. We knit to the length you need. The product is processed by edge viscous. The hat-helmet for the girl is ready. You can decorate with pompons or crochet your ears. To make a pompom, cut a strip of cardboard. Wrap with thread. Cut one end and tie the other.
