Sanatoria in Tatarstan: reviews. The most popular sanatorium in Tatarstan

When planning a vacation, more and more Russians prefer the resort areas of the Volga region. Tatarstan gained great popularity. It has excellent climatic conditions, magnificent flora and fauna. The treatment provided by the sanatoriums in Tatarstan is compared with a stay in Switzerland. Health complexes of the republic annually attract thousands of vacationers from all over the country. What did the health resorts of Tatarstan deserve such attention? Reviews of many vacationers are filled not only with impressions of the local nature. Guests who have been here enthusiastically talk about the treatment they received in the health facilities of the republic.

One of the popular places is considered the sanatorium "Santa". Tatarstan is famous for its sources of mineral waters. Widely used and therapeutic mud. The sanatorium (Tatarstan mainly has establishments of this category) began its existence in 1984. It is located in the village of Vasilyevo. He will be discussed further.



General information

At the very beginning, on its territory there were only a few rooms: two comfortable sleeping buildings, a small dining room and a medical department. Over time, gradually developing and expanding, the sanatorium began to accommodate a larger number of people who wanted to relax and restore health. To date, Sosnovy Bor (sanatorium, Tatarstan) has grown significantly, occupying almost 14.2 hectares. Vacationers can comfortably sit in seven bedroom buildings. For those who care about their health, there is a whole sports complex. The large hall of the sanatorium is designed to watch movie shows and listen to concert performances. A hairdresser, laundry, garages for cars, a cafe-dining room - all this is created for a convenient and pleasant pastime for visitors.

The nature surrounding the sanatorium of Tatarstan cannot but rejoice. Thanks to the air and the smell of conifers (spruce, cedar, pine), the body will rest and gain strength. Cultural life and rewards

Sosnovy Bor takes an active part in the public and cultural life of Tatarstan. He does not bypass specialized forums and exhibitions, receiving diplomas and certificates. Separately, a complex of therapeutic procedures is noted. This Tatarstan sanatorium is known for its work as part of an international specialized performance. Comfort

In "Sosnovy Bor" everything is provided for a relaxing and cozy stay for vacationers. In six dormitory buildings, 300 people can work wonderfully.
