Iron Port, Albatross: what does this recreation center offer tourists?

Iron Port (Ukraine, Kherson region, Golopristan district) became known as a resort in the 90s of the last century. Now it is a favorite vacation spot for residents of this country, as well as citizens of neighboring countries, mainly Moldovans, Belarusians and Russians. The secret of the popularity of the Iron Port lies in the clear Black Sea, clean air, watered by the aromas of the steppe, a favorable ecological environment and rapidly developing tourist infrastructure. The former agricultural village is rapidly turning into a noisy and fun resort. Every year new guest houses, pensions and mini-hotels open there. Summer Holidays, Iron Port

Albatross is not the only guesthouse in this resort. The village is spread over five kilometers along the sea, and near the golden beaches almost fifty bases rise. Some of them were built in the 1980s - at the dawn of the resort era of the Iron Port. Many of these buildings need renovation. Recreation centers are not newer located on the first line. But they have a more developed infrastructure, the living conditions in them are better. Therefore, it is worth deciding in advance what is best for you: living in a dull room with a bedside table and mesh beds by the sea or relaxing in a comfortable room at the base with a pool, but a little away from the beach. The second option is also encouraged by the fact that in the evenings the majority of holidaymakers go for a walk on the promenade - a pedestrian road that separates the beaches from the boarding houses of such a resort as the Iron Port. "Albatross" is located two hundred meters from the sea. Walking to the beach is only five to seven minutes, but calm nights await you.



Infrastructure

The recreation center is located in the center of the resort called Iron Port. Albatross is just one hundred and fifty meters from the bus stop. Nearby there is a food market, shops, a pharmacy. The recreation center consists of two buildings separated by a road. Both sites are fenced, guarded. Initially, the guests received the only two-story building. Around it were planted flower beds, built a playground and a bar. In 2007, the recreation center bought another piece of land and built a new three-story building on it. A pool and cafe soon appeared near him, and a well-groomed courtyard was laid out. On two sites there is a guarded paid parking. Thus, the Albatros recreation center in the Iron Port was able to attract both budget vacationers (for them there are rooms in the old building) and customers with high requirements (they are being housed in a new building). It should be noted that the cost of living in the resort depends on the season. The highest prices are July-August.
