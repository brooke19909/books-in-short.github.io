How to collect origami "Tank"

February 23 is approaching. What to give men on this day? To date, paper crafts have become popular. For example, you can make an origami tank, weapon. It will be great to collect this craft with the children and give it to dad and grandfather. We want to bring to your attention several master classes in origami "Tank".

Scheme 1

You will need:

- glue;

- ruler;

- one sheet of colored paper;

- a simple pencil;



- scissors.

Working process

Take a piece of paper. Draw a square and rectangle with a ruler. Cut out. Then we take the resulting square sheet. Bend in half. Bend both sides to the middle. Spread it out. Turn the sheet ninety degrees. We bend the craft in half and straighten it. Turn over again. Make two folds outward. So we outlined the future details of the tank. Bend in half again and two corners on the sides. Expand them to make two triangles. Turn the product over. Turn the bottom of the tank along the fold line. Getting to the manufacture of the barrel. Take the previously prepared rectangular sheet.


Bend at a distance ¬º. Turn over. With a pencil, draw two dotted lines of equal width along the entire length of the sheet. We bend at the top left corner to one of the lines. Straighten. Do the same on the other side. Insert the barrel into the tank. For strength, you can stick it. Ready crafts. Such a tank can decorate a card for Defender's Day.

Origami "Tank T-34": scheme 2 and photo

You will need:

- triangular modules (six hundred pieces);

- four matchboxes;

- scissors;

- used case from lipstick.

How to make origami "Tank": scheme

First, we will make wheels for the tank.

It is necessary to make only seven of them. Six wheels will go to the tracks and one to the tower. We prepare triangular modules. You will need seventy-two parts of green on one wheel, six on each of yellow and brown colors. Twenty-four green modules are assembled in three rows. Insert yellow and brown in the middle of the wheel. Use your hands to shape to prevent the product from breaking up. Make six more. Now let's get down to the caterpillar. Collect seven modules, then six and alternate thirty rows in this way. Lock in the ring. But do not forget to insert three wheels inside. Make the second "caterpillar" in the same way. We collect the tower. We take boxes from matches. First cut the hole. Then we glue the boxes in two rows. Paste with paper. Prepare a wooden rounded blank or you can take used lipstick. We return to the manufacture of origami "Tank". We collect seven rows of six and seven modules in turn. This is for the front of the tank, and for the back - five rows. We continue to make the tower. To assemble it, you will need two and three modules in turn (fifteen rows). Insert into the tower. Finish origami "Tank". The addition technique is quite simple, and the child can handle it. With its outlines, the resulting model resembles the Soviet T-34 tank.
