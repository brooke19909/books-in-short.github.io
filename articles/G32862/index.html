Huawei Honor 6A Smartphone: Features and Model Review

The Honor line from the Chinese brand Huawei has always been distinguished by a democratic pricing policy. But sometimes a large number of compromise solutions repelled consumers from buying. Here we are talking about the weakest Mediatech processors in the series, plastic cases, a very small battery capacity and the absence of normal sensors, not to mention a fingerprint scanner.





But the characteristics of the Huawei Honor 6A phone are a completely different story. The new model received a solid metal case, a good "stuffing" in the form of a Qualcomm processor, 2 GB of RAM and 16 GB for the needs of the user, as well as an attractive 13 megapixel camera and a fingerprint sensor. Moreover, despite such impressive technical characteristics, the Huawei Honor 6A can be bought for only 9,000 rubles. In addition, the mass of positive reviews on large online trading platforms will further encourage buying.

So, the subject of today's review is the Huawei Honor 6A smartphone . Characteristics of the model, its advantages and disadvantages, as well as the feasibility of acquiring the model will be discussed in this article.

Performance

The performance is checked by the time-tested set of chipsets in the form of the Snapdragon 430 processor and the Adreno 505 accelerator. Yes, the characteristics of Huawei Honor 6A do not shine with something outstanding, but for ordinary needs such a tandem is enough.

Two gigabytes of RAM is enough to run any modern games, but in "heavy" applications you will have to reset the graphics settings to the minimum value, if any, will run Adreno 505. The internal memory is only 16 GB, but it can be expanded up to 128 GB using external SD media.

Offline work

The device has a good 3020 mAh battery for a budget gadget. With a standard load, the charge is enough for a day's work. If you get involved in games and watching videos, then after 5-6 hours the gadget will start asking for a power outlet.





It will also be useful to note that a lot of energy-saving modes are included in the stock firmware, where everyone can choose the best option for themselves, up to disconnecting everything and everything, leaving only calls and SMS.

To summarize

Our respondent is not without flaws. A critical shortcoming is the display matrix with its faded image. But the model has much more advantages. Here, an attractive metal case, a fingerprint sensor, a smart processor for a budget model, as well as good battery life with good cameras.

The model is worth the money and is one of the most attractive options in the ratio of price and quality.
