So, let's figure out how to throw money from Motive to MTS.

  1. Open the PayJet app.
  2. Sign up with your phone number.
  3. Choose a money transfer service. In this case, select "Transfer money to MTS."

  5. Click the "Pay" button.
  6. Wait for the SMS with the code.
  7. Enter the code in the appropriate line.

If you did everything right, you can check your balance, the money should have been transferred.

Transfer within the subscriber network

You need to create a message. In the recipient line, enter the number 1080, and in the text field, enter the following: recipient number_amount of funds sent. For clarity, we can give an example. Suppose you want to send 200 rubles to the number 95063154789. You need to enter the following in the text field: 95063154789 200. Note that there is a space between the number and the amount.
