Fashionista Tips: DIY Summer Skirt

In the summertime, with its warm, and often very hot weather, you want to dress accordingly: easy, free and relaxed. What if not to wear a skirt in hot summer days? This piece of clothing is unusually feminine, moreover, it is comfortable to tolerate the heat in it. The skirt helps to emphasize all the pluses of the figure and hide its cons. It is important to choose the right style and material.





Models of summer skirts are very diverse. This is the eternal mini, which is chosen by young and slender girls, denim models are especially popular. And this is quite justified, because such a part of the wardrobe is very convenient and practical. Still at the peak of popularity, maxi skirts or ‚Äúon the floor‚Äù, as they are also called. Such models are chosen by fashionable women, forming the style of the fatal beauty in their image. Classic pencil skirts and simply midi length are also relevant. The styles "tulip" or "barrel", "year" are not for everyone, therefore they are less common. Models "sun" and "half-sun" - a great option for a skirt for women.





Summer fabrics

For the hot season, it is better to choose chiffon, satin, staple, cambric, lace and other natural fabrics. A small amount of synthetics is allowed, but it is better to refuse completely artificial materials, so as not to feel like in a sauna.

DIY summer skirt? Simply!

Those who know how to sew, it is much easier to choose their own clothes. After all, the possession of the necessary skills of cutting and sewing allows you to design almost any thing. In this case, the model will exactly fit the figure, and it will not have to be customized or sutured. For those who are just trying themselves as a seamstress, you can offer to make a simple model. So, the summer skirt with your own hands will be on an elastic band, slightly flared, and the length can be adjusted to your liking. First of all, you need to determine the footage of the fabric. As a rule, the canvases have a width of 120-150 cm. The length must be taken equal to the length of the skirt plus 15 cm to the waist and hem. As a pattern, you can use one of your things, which is just right and sits well. Given the necessary allowance for the bottom of the lapel, cut off the canvas skirt. We sew side cuts on both sides. It turns out the "pipe" - the main part. For the belt, cut off a strip equal to the circumference of the hips and another 5 cm, fold, sew, after inserting the elastic, collect in a ring. Now you need to sew the belt to the main part. A summer skirt with your own hands can be slightly pleated, then you need to pick up the fabric, sewing to the belt. The next step is the bottom hem. the lapel should be small - 2-3 cm. The DIY summer skirt is ready. An elastic belt suggests

wearing a skirt at home or outdoors. Stricter models require a belt and zipper.

Color schemes

The color of the skirts this summer is unusually bright. These are neon colors: light green, pink, orange, blue. The classics are still relevant: black and white. As well as beige, gray and all pastel colors. They wear skirts with "Turkish cucumbers" and gypsy motifs, polka dots and a strip of various thicknesses. Experiment and create your skirt, which no one else will have.
