What is crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding is a mechanism for attracting financing aimed at supporting a business, selling a product, holding events, etc. There are three models for the type of remuneration for a sponsor:

  • non-reward donation;
  • Kickstarter model - receiving non-financial rewards;
  • crowdinvesting - financial reward.

Crowdfunding will avoid the participation of exchanges, funds and banks in the process of obtaining financing. This procedure has been greatly simplified and has become more democratic. Of particular importance is the personal interest of the sponsor.

What is a crowdfunding platform?

A crowdfunding platform is a platform used to host and promote relevant projects on the Internet. It is a specialized service for posting ideas. The platform provides legal and financial aspects. In addition, it will facilitate the interaction of participants and help in promotion.





Features of crowdfunding platforms

It is worth noting the three main roles of the participants on the site. They are the author of the project, curator and sponsor. Crowdfunding platform allows you to curate any organization that is interested in supporting the project. This includes the provision of technical equipment, facilities, equipment and sponsorship of an advertising campaign. From the site, the authors represented project managers.

Crowdfunding platforms include a project creation system. It contains data on the amount and time of the project, its description, the availability of rewards, a presentation in video format, as well as information about the author and linking to a bank account. It is worth noting that various payment systems are integrated into the site, which depends on the specific service.

Most resources have a social component. This means that you can access the site using an account in a certain social network, and information about friends who are also registered on the site is also automatically indicated. The user has the opportunity to monitor the activity of other participants, as well as receive notifications about projects created or financed by them. Crowdfunding platform allows you to communicate with the author of the project you like.

Crowdfunding platform "Planet"

Planet crowdfunding platform was launched in 2011 as a test version. Financing of this type was tested on the BI-2 group. Thus, they managed to collect about 1.2 million rubles that were used to release the Spirit album. This project was not a charity, but a prepayment for the disks that the participants received without intermediaries.

This crowdfunding platform eliminates the interaction between the authors of projects and their audience. This also leads to significant savings in money and effort. Recently, there has been an increase in the number of users, as well as an expansion in the scale of financing.
