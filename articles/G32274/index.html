Zemsky Cathedral. History of development

Zemsky Cathedral is an important stage in the development of Russia. Vecherical orders have always existed in our country, but there have been periods when all independence has been reproached. And he revived the opportunity to collect most of the estates Ivan the Terrible.

The Zemsky Sobor included representatives of the following estates: the boyar Duma, whose members participated in the Zemsky Sobor in its entirety; consecrated cathedral, from them were only the highest church hierarchs; elected people from servicemen, Moscow and city nobility, archers, Cossacks and Pushkars; elected from the posadskoy population (Chernoshosny and Sloboda) and from the cloth hundreds and living room. The cathedral of the first convocation was called the "cathedral of reconciliation."

The first Zemsky Sobor was convened in order to acquaint the meeting participants with the reforms of the new organ of Ivan the Terrible - the Elected Council. As you know, the reforms of the Rada included the creation of a streltsy army, the introduction of a new criminal, as well as the development of the command system and the strengthening of central authority.



Over time, the functions of the estate-representative body also developed. So, by the end of the 16th century, participants in this meeting received the right to approve the accession to the throne of a new monarch. In those years, it was decided that the new rulers receive the approval of the people, which was expressed by the Zemsky Sobor.

The history of the development of this body is multifaceted: there were periods when its role was completely lost, and there were those when the fate of the state depended on its decision. The Time of Troubles is a prime example of the latter period.
