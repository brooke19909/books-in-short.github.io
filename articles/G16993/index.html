Where in Egypt is better to relax? Some interesting places

Holidays in Egypt - this is undoubtedly the dream of everyone who has never been there. Tourists who managed to get to know him want to enjoy his hospitality and mystery again. Booking a ticket at the moment is not a problem, only you need to know about where in Egypt it is better to relax in order to get real pleasure from the trip.



Sharm El Sheikh

This marvelous resort rightfully bears the title of the most prestigious, its beauty is simply mesmerizing. It is located on the Sinai Peninsula (in its southern part), the water temperature does not drop below 20 degrees year-round, the air warms up to 25 and higher, which allows you to swim and sunbathe the whole season. The diving enthusiasts will enjoy the rich underwater world, and everything you need for it can be purchased at any specialized store. The resort is rightfully considered one of those places where it is better to relax in Egypt. There are many entertainments here: excursions to the monastery of St. Catherine, desert jeep safari, golf clubs, tennis courts, racehorses, night discos and various show performances.



Makadi Bay

This resort is located away from the bustling Hurghada, its hotels are located on the coast, near which there are no settlements. The beaches are large and sandy, dotted with many small shells. For convenience, it is recommended to purchase special shoes that allow you to comfortably walk on the sand and enter the water. Makadi Bay will be appreciated by tourists who, when asked about where in Egypt it is better to relax, answer: "Where it is calm and calm." In this case, this is just an option for them, since entertainment, discos and shows are only near hotels, and behind them there is only nature, desert and isolation from the real world.

Hurghada

This bustling city is located on the shores of the Red Sea and is suitable for recreation at any time of the year due to climatic conditions. If you do not like diving, you can admire the world of the deep sea from a submarine through its porthole. You can visit the Coptic Church and just enjoy the life that is in full swing at this resort. Where in Egypt is better to relax? Hurghada is a good option for lovers of a rich, vibrant and stormy vacation. Many multilingual tourists will not let you get bored, and nightly entertainment is unlikely to leave you indifferent.



Before you finally start looking for vacation packages near the Red Sea, you need to decide on the purpose of the trip. This is very important, because you should ultimately get what you wanted. Think about whether you want to have fun all night long or, conversely, want to pacify in the silence and pristine nature. Or maybe the main purpose of your trip is to visit historical values. The choice of the place where it is better for you to relax in Egypt will depend on your preferences.

Each of the described resorts is in its own way unique and unique. Having visited it at least once, I want to return there again and again. If possible, it is advisable to find them all in turn, because only by comparing one place with another, you can make the final choice and firmly answer the question of where to go to Egypt to relax is best for you. Finding a suitable resort, you can easily make it your favorite vacation spot for your entire family.
