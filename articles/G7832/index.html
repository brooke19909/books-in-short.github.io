Krasnodar - Gelendzhik - a route for rest

Many, flying to the Krasnodar Territory, dream of visiting the resort town of Gelendzhik. After wandering through the streets of Krasnodar, visiting its entertainment venues, attractions and shops, vacationers begin to wonder how to get to Gelendzhik. For those who are interested in the Krasnodar - Gelendzhik route, how to get to the resort is a very important question that requires an immediate answer. We will try to satisfy your curiosity and help you quickly get to the beautiful Black Sea with its golden beaches and clear water.

So, there are only 4 ways to get there. Consider each of them.





Bus

The easiest and cheapest way. Buses on the route Krasnodar - Gelendzhik run quite often from the bus station "Krasnodar-1". If we keep in mind both transit flights and those that come from Krasnodar itself, the average departure frequency is once per hour. If you are planning to go to the sea in high season, it is better to take tickets in advance. Until July and after September, you can purchase a ticket at the ticket office of the bus station immediately before departure.

Buses engaged in transportation on the route Krasnodar - Gelendzhik, comfortable, with soft chairs, air conditioning. The cost of a ticket is on average 300-400 rubles per person. Children under 3 years old are transported free of charge, children under 12 years old travel on a children's ticket.

Travel time by bus - from 3.40 to 4.20 hours, depending on the parking time in various villages and cities. Most buses travel through Novorossiysk.





Airplane

Airports Gelendzhik, Krasnodar, the distance between which in a straight line is only 100 with a few kilometers, do not have a direct connection. Therefore, if suddenly you want to get to Gelendzhik from Krasnodar by plane, keep in mind that you will have to do this with transfers. Most flights are offered through Moscow. Taking into account connecting flights, the journey will take from 16 hours, its cost will be from 10 thousand rubles. In a word, the flight Krasnodar - Gelendzhik is a very unsuccessful thought, expensive and time-consuming.





Taxi

The fastest way to get to the resort. Today, the cost of transportation is 2.5-3.5 thousand rubles for transportation on the route Krasnodar - Gelendzhik. The route that you take (via Novorossiysk and Kabardinka or Goryachiy Klyuch and Dzhugbu) will depend on your agreement with the driver. Nevertheless, most taxi drivers prefer to move through the Hot Key and Jugbu. In this case, the journey will take from 2.5 to 3 hours. The exact time will depend on the condition of the roads and time of day.





Personal car

The most convenient and relatively inexpensive way. In this case, the most interesting. After all, you will not depend on other people's plans, on how much from Krasnodar to Gelendzhik, on the weather, traffic jams and other things. Just because you make the route yourself. If there is a traffic jam on the road, you can either go round it or drive into the nearest grove, to a river, to the mountains, just to the village you like, having examined it more closely, having rested and waiting for the traffic jam. On the road from Krasnodar to Gelendzhik, whatever path you choose, there are a lot of interesting villages and sights that you can visit only by personal transport. The Gelendzhik - Krasnodar route, the distance between the end points of which is only 180-190 km on different routes, can be easily overcome in 2-3 hours. However, in fact it will be much more interesting, more fun and informative to travel this path in a day or two in order to have time to visit caves, hot springs, interesting churches in the mountains, lotus lakes.

If you are interested in outdoor activities in Gelendzhik, it is most convenient to go to the resort either by taxi or by bus. In this case, it will be more convenient to take delivery from tour companies upon arrival in Gelendzhik. In the city you will be offered interesting car excursions to the main attractions of the resort - the sacred ancient dolmens, waterfalls on the Zhane River, observation platforms on the mountains around the city. There are several companies offering horseback trips from one hour to a whole day. Inside Gelendzhik itself it is convenient to use a taxi or move around on foot. Be sure to visit the safari park, dolphinarium, oceanarium, the small town of Kabardinka with a magnificent Old Park. Well, the resort offers a huge number of bars, restaurants, boat trips, entertainment for children and adults to vacationers.
