How to charge your phone if the socket is broken: several ways to charge without a charger

A mobile phone has so firmly entered our lives that many no longer imagine how they could do without it. Each owner is familiar with the situation when you need to make an important call, or receive a message, and the mobile phone‚Äôs battery is treacherously discharged, or worse, there is charging, but nothing happens. How to charge the phone if the socket is broken?





Method one

This method of how to charge the phone if the socket is broken is the easiest and does not require any skills at all. True, the battery of the mobile phone will suffer and will not be able to withstand repetition. All that is required is to pull out the battery and hit it hard against something solid, such as a table or stone. You can make 1 or 2 calls.

Method two: the frog to the rescue

The next way to charge the phone if the charging socket is broken is very expensive and does not justify the cost, but it is suitable if you or your loved ones have a ‚Äúfrog‚Äù. This is a universal charger with a special groove where the mobile battery is inserted. This method has its drawbacks:

  1. In terms of cost, the "frog" significantly exceeds the cost in comparison with a service center.
  2. You will not be able to use the phone until the battery is fully charged.
  3. Some user reviews of the device noted battery failure during charging.




Method 3: direct charging without using a socket

If you have some skills in working with electricity, then this method of charging the phone, if the socket is broken, can be done at home. Step-by-step instruction:

  1. Remove the battery from the mobile phone.
  2. Unplug the charger first .
  3. Remove insulation from the charger wire by exposing the wires.
  4. Stripping the cores. Find the plus and minus.
  5. Find the desired terminals on the battery. Observe the polarity of the wires and the device itself.
  6. Fix the cores, after which you can plug in the power outlet.

Important! This method can not be used if you are alone. There must be someone who can help you with a possible electric shock.





The fourth way

The described method will tell you how to urgently charge the phone if the socket is broken. Take an ordinary knife and heat it over a fire. Attach it to the battery. By increasing the temperature, the battery will come to its senses for a short while. This method is best used only when the call is more important than the cost of the battery. Follow the rule: when heating the battery, act quickly and accurately, otherwise it may swell. Heating the battery itself will make it possible to make a call lasting about 5 minutes, but the battery will have to be replaced.

Hike way to charge your phone

Previous methods described how to charge the phone at home if the socket is broken and there is no ‚Äúfrog‚Äù, but what if the situation turned out to be the exact opposite: urgently need to call in the country or on a hike? And in this case there is a way to make a call, if, of course, the right materials are at hand. In order to make the charger yourself, you will need several metal plates, a small piece of copper wire, as well as salt water. If you are going camping, for example, you can take everything you need in advance in case of an emergency. The described method will be able to recharge the phone by 5%.

The plates are buried in the ground and wrapped with copper wire. The construction is poured with salt water. The charger is ready. In case there is no iron, it can be obtained as follows: metal pins are inserted into existing products, for example, in lemons or apples and wrapped with wire.





Is it possible to take extreme measures

The following methods of charging the phone, if the socket is broken and there is no "frog", it is recommended to use it only in extreme cases, they can damage the battery. Otherwise, it is better not to spare time and take the mobile phone to the service.

Self-replacement jacks or service center?

Can I fix the charging socket myself? Theoretically, yes. But do not forget about the functions that this connector performs:

  1. Files are transferred from the phone to the computer and vice versa through the charging slot. This is perhaps the most important function, in addition to charging the gadget, given how much important data can be stored in the memory card.
  2. Through the charging socket, you can charge the phone directly from the computer using a special cord that comes with the mobile.
  3. Through the charging connector, you can connect your mobile to a TV to view photos and videos.
  4. Some other features.

Charge your iPhone without using a charger

If you are the owner of an iPhone, then the hiking method described above will not be useful to you. Alternative sources have come on the market that can convert environmental energy into electricity. Only use them without visiting the salon will fail.



If you do not have experience in repairing cell phones, it is better not to undertake the repair on your own, as careless movement can finally finish off your smartphone, which costs much more than the services of service centers. In addition, you can only aggravate the breakdown and repairs will cost more than the amount that could have been originally.

If the jack is ok but there is no charge

If the jack is OK and the phone is not charging, there may be a malfunction in the charger. In this case, you can charge the mobile phone via usb port by connecting it to a computer or tablet.

How to choose a service center

It is best to give the phone to a brand service of the same brand as the device. There must be service certificates. View the reviews of the selected service on the site.
