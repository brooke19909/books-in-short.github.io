How to make a DIY Chinese fan

Fashion for oriental culture has been observed among Europeans for a long time. And pay attention to external attributes. In the hands of Chinese beauties, vibrant webs flutter like butterflies. How to create a Chinese fan with your own hands will tell you the article presented to your attention.

It will take a little of your time and imagination to make it. This item appeared in the Middle Kingdom, the first mention of it dates back to the eighth or second centuries BC. Later, the fashion spread to him in Japan, where they began to experiment with its shape, created folding fans, from planks.





Before you make a fan yourself, you need to familiarize yourself with the traditions associated with it. Initially, these gizmos were made of feathers, a wooden handle was attached from below, later they used cloth, paper, reed and lotus. Often exquisite objects were decorated with calligraphy, pictures, and silk embroidery was used. These were real works of art. It is these weights that have taken root at the emperor‚Äôs court. The shape of the fan was round or oval.





It is quite simple to make an oriental fan with your own hands. This will require the following materials: paper (simple and for decoupage), braid, glue, bamboo skewers, nippers and scissors. It is necessary to mark the outline of the fan on paper. Then cut 2 identical parts with scissors. They must be combined and slightly bent in half. This is necessary to mark the middle of the product for drawing a pattern.





Making a fan with your own hands is a very exciting and useful thing, because at the end of the process you will become the owner of a beautiful little thing. From decoupage paper you need to cut a couple of parts similar to the previous ones, but they will be slightly smaller than them. Napkins or tissue paper are also suitable for this purpose. Next, you need to return to the blanks of white paper. They must be glued from the center of the future; bamboo skewers, which should be equally spaced from each other, are plowed to the edges.

To make a fan, made with your own hands, turned out to be truly elegant, you need to make sure that all the details are done carefully. After the sticks are glued, they need to put a second blank of white paper on them. For better fixation, they must be pressed for twenty minutes with some heavy object. A book may be suitable for this. Then it is necessary to align the sticks with pliers, and then bend the resulting structure in different directions.

To resemble the Chinese fan created, do it yourself very carefully to glue decoupage paper with the corresponding pattern. The handle can be made of thick cardboard. A piece of plywood will also work. From the selected material, it is necessary to cut out the necessary shape and glue it with superglue to the fan on both sides. Now we can say that your eastern fan is ready.

Until now, the fan is part of the culture of Japan, and in China there is even a special holiday dedicated to it, which is held in Shandong in late November. This item is a symbol of happiness and prosperity.
