Yeysk city: attractions and interesting places

On the picturesque peninsula at the base of the Yeisk Spit, there is a small resort town of Yeysk, which is washed by the waters of the warm and gentle Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov. The city gained its popularity due to climatic conditions favorable for recreation, magnificent sandy beaches, numerous recreation centers and a number of sanatorium-resort medical institutions. But this is not all that the resort town of Yeysk is famous for. Attractions play a significant role in choosing a vacation spot. After all, everyone wants to leave a lot of unforgettable impressions about their vacation.



What interesting is there in the city of Yeysk? The sights of this settlement are, first of all, unique places created by nature itself. So, to the north-west of the resort there is an amazing natural reserve - Dolgaya Spit, which is considered the longest on the entire peninsula, the length reaches 8 kilometers. It is rightly called the pearl of the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov. Here are excellent shell beaches, rich flora and fauna. This place attracts numerous windsurfers and kayserfers.



We continue to get acquainted with the most interesting places in the city of Yeysk. Attractions can be complemented by such a unique place as Lake Khan, located 40 kilometers from the resort. It is a closed shallow shallow reservoir with therapeutic mud. Its maximum depth is slightly less than a meter. In dry periods, it dries completely, showing its salty bottom, and then is again filled with water. There is also a source with iodine-bromine and hydrogen sulfide mineral waters.



In addition to natural unique places, there are a number of architectural structures that are popular with tourists in Yeysk. Sights of this category are various religious buildings, including the Holy Vvedensky Church. It rises on Shkolnaya street. The construction of the building was carried out in 1915 for the funds donated by local residents. Money was sorely lacking, so the temple was completed only in 2003, after the charity fund of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker took up it. The construction is a single-domed building, which is lined with natural stone. The interior of the temple is decorated with a carved iconostasis. A bell tower is attached to the main part.

The sights of Yeysk can be supplemented by a no less beautiful architectural structure - the Cathedral of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker. The building is famous for its rich decoration. The main hall features beautiful icons. There is a souvenir shop at the temple. Religious buildings can be supplemented with such as the Church of St. Michael the Archangel. This building is the first stone cathedral in the city, the date of construction of which is considered to be 1865. Despite the fact that it has long lost its original appearance as a result of fires and destruction, the church is very popular among believers. There is a Sunday school at the temple. And near the temple there is a beautiful park with an unusual waterfall.



And this is not all the attractions of the city of Yeysk. The city has a huge number of monuments, monuments, museums, parks, including the monument to Bondarchuk. Other places of interest include the dolphinarium, the Nemo water park, the Shark Reef aquarium, and the Yeisk Patriot armored boat.
