Regional insurance: "Asko", Chelyabinsk

In addition to large federal companies that engage in activities such as insurance, there are regional ones. So, Chelyabinsk is known for its regional company YuzhUral-Asko. We will talk about it later in the article.

General Company Information

This company is a leader in the insurance market in the South Urals region. The beginning of its activity dates back to 1990. From a small staff, she eventually grew into a large company. "YuzhUral-Asko" is engaged in many types of insurance, such as compulsory motor third-party liability insurance, property, transport, civil liability, accident insurance, VHI insurance.





The central office of the company is in Chelyabinsk. The list of representative offices is quite large; you can see it on the official website of the company and choose a suitable place where it will be convenient to carry out insurance at Asko. Chelyabinsk (the addresses of company offices are also located on insurance policies) is not the only place where Asko is located. It should be noted that the company has representative offices in the Chelyabinsk and Kurgan regions. And also in the city of Yekaterinburg, Krasnodar Territory, the city of Rostov-on-Don and other cities of Russia.
