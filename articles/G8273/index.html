PC Broadband Speaker System: Top Models, Review and Reviews

Working, playing and watching movies on a personal computer without acoustics is boring and inconvenient. Even laptop owners sometimes think that a home speaker system will be very helpful. The computer market is quite densely saturated with speakers and all kinds of music gadgets, so the choice is not always obvious.





Let's try to identify a list that includes the best speakers in 5.1 format. When compiling the rating, the opinions of specialists in this field and the reviews of ordinary equipment owners were taken into account.

The best speakers for your computer:

  • Sven IHOO T100U;
  • Logitech Z906;
  • Microlab FC 730.

Consider each model in more detail, noting the main advantages and disadvantages of the system.

Sven IHOO T100U

This broadband speaker system looks versatile and traditional. Engineers did not become wise and use some kind of know-how, but simply built cases from high-quality plywood. It turned out simple, but quite acceptable, especially since such a simple design will fit into the interior of almost any room.





The Sven home speaker system is equipped with a convenient remote control from which you can control basic parameters, including adjusting the high and low frequency lines. The system also has additional functionality in the person of an FM tuner and a music player with the ability to store data on an SD card or USB flash drive.

Characteristics of equipment and opinions of owners

The total power of the speaker system reaches 150 watts, and due to this, the sound at the output is very loud, and without any whistle or cacophony. The number of speakers provides excellent panning for the system, but effective sounding requires smart placement in the right places.

Advantages of the model:

  • universal speaker design;
  • good power;
  • the ability to connect an SD card and flash drive;
  • FM tuner;
  • clear and detailed sound.

Disadvantages:

  • The brand price for home speakers is too high.

Reviews about the model are left mostly in a positive way. Owners appreciated the high power of the acoustics and the excellent sound output. Some, of course, complain about the cost of equipment, but experts convince us that the balance of price and quality is fully maintained.

Estimated cost - 15 000 rubles.

Logitech Z906

Logitech Broadband Speaker System is designed in strict "spy" range. The subwoofer, black as night, along with the same speakers, as if trying once again not to catch the eye of the owner. Nevertheless, many experts and users consider this color correct. Just look at the ordinary performance of the system unit, keyboard or the same monitor - everything is painted black. And therefore acoustics simply merges with other desktop items.





Logitech's broadband speaker system is equipped with a convenient and fully functional remote control with a large number of buttons that allow you to adjust most of the basic parameters.

Distinctive features of the system

As for the sound, there are absolutely no complaints here. The sound output is quite loud and really clear. Neighbors will often remember you with an unkind word for quality bass. If not for the very high price, then the system can be advised to almost any owner of a personal computer.





Also, the owners in their reviews note the absence of even additional, but very useful functions, such as those that the previous respondent had (FM tuner and independent player). It turns out that the device just stands idle when the computer is turned off.

Advantages of the model:

  • very high power for equipment of this class - 500 W;
  • clear and detailed sound output;
  • built-in system decoders (DTS and Dolby Digital);
  • high-quality and deep bass;
  • relatively small dimensions for computer peripherals;
  • long and durable cables.

Minuses:

  • Impossible price for many PC users;
  • ambiguous speaker design;
  • lack of additional functionality.
  • Estimated cost - 35 000 rubles.

Microlab FC 730

This broadband speaker system was designed specifically for use in conjunction with a personal computer (although some quite successfully use it with a TV). Sensibly calibrated dimensions and number of columns provide excellent panning.





The relatively small size will allow you to move from room to room without any problems. The good power of the equipment is also pleasing, but nevertheless, the bass, coupled with other frequencies, even at maximum volume, will not prevent you from comfortably coexisting with your neighbors.

Model Features and Owner Reviews

The kit includes a remote control that can adjust most of the basic parameters. Most often, it gathers dust somewhere on the sidelines, because on a separate amplifier there are convenient wheels for adjusting the sound. By the way, it will not be superfluous to notice that the amplifier is very different from the gamut of the remaining speakers and has its own, albeit specific, but original color.

The main advantages of the model:

  • quite loud, detailed and clear sound;
  • long and durable cables;
  • universal design and small dimensions of the speakers;
  • the presence of a separate amplifier and its original appearance;
  • deep and high quality bass.

Disadvantages of the system:

  • the upper frequencies are poorly designed (additional adjustment by an equalizer is necessary).

The owners warmly accepted the FC 730 model from Mikrolab. In their reviews, they appreciated the sound quality of the system and the appearance of the equipment. Many were pleased with the bass and the quality of the mid frequencies, but to listen to classical compositions, you need to mess with the equalizer to fine-tune the upper tones. Estimated cost - 10 000 rubles.
