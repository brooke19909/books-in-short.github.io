Nokia E71 - a detailed review of the phone

Today we will take a closer look at the Nokia E71 phone. The device fits perfectly in the pocket of a shirt or trousers. Case material is one of the important features of this model.

Design

The size of the Nokia E71 is 114x57x10 mm and weighs 127 grams. In this case, metal is used for the back cover, as well as glossy plastic, painted type. This approach made it possible to almost completely eliminate abrasion coatings. Thus, we are dealing with an incredibly strong structure that does not wear out even after a long time. On the front surface there is a chrome trim. There are scratches on the plastic. They are noticeable upon careful examination. However, they are not visible from a short distance. The back cover is equipped with a dot pattern. It prevents the appearance of scuffs.





Engineers of Nokia E71 have worked out the design of the device with unsurpassed skill. The back cover, for example, is fixed on a pair of spring contacts. A similar system has proven itself. Latching ribbed. You can find them by touch. The infrared port is on the left side. The microSD slot and microUSB connector are hidden by rubber plugs. On the right there is a 2.5 mm audio output. Here are the volume buttons, as well as the voice services button. Below there is a strap hole and a 2 mm charger connector. On the top panel is the power key and speaker hole.

Display

Nokia E71 has a 2.36-inch screen with QVGA resolution. The display displays 16 million colors. The picture is bright. It is clearly visible under various conditions. In the sun, the screen behaves with dignity. There is a mirror substrate. The display shows up to eight lines of text. The font is quite readable. In some modes, the number of lines can go up to 14.





Keyboard

Nokia E71 has an unusual input device. This model is equipped with a QWERTY keyboard. In this case, typing can be done with one hand. The width of the phone is quite comfortable. With one finger you can reach all the keys. The relatively small size of the keyboard led to the fact that the buttons are installed close. Therefore, erroneous keystrokes on adjacent keys are possible. Blind dialing on the device is very complicated.

The buttons are made of plastic. The keys are voluminous, have a protruding hemisphere. However, clicking on them is easier with a fingernail rather than a fingertip. In this model, the local alphabet is selected as the main one. English is the second language here, and the corresponding letters are placed on the top row, and also highlighted in blue. The letters "B and S" the developers combined with the "F and E". To copy text, you need to translate the register, press Ctrl, and then perform the necessary action.





As for the Russian language, for him all the letters were carried out on the keys, among them there is a solid sign. On the bottom row there are frequently used buttons - a comma and a period. The backlighting is white. It is relatively uniform. The notation is clearly visible. You cannot call the keyboard too suitable for dialing numbers. The numeric keys are crowded, the symbols merge. The branded feature of the device is four separate function keys. They are responsible for access to mail, phone book and calendar. Buttons support two types of taps - short and long.

User opinions

So we figured out the main features of the Nokia E71 phone. Reviews about him are very different, and now we will try to highlight the main thing from them. The advantages of users, as a rule, include reliability, stability, usability, long battery life, build quality. Among the shortcomings, the refusal to update the Symbian platform is mentioned.
