Network hotels: the best hotels, description of rooms, services, infrastructure, features, photos

Vacation, office need, wedding or other event - the question arises of choosing a hotel in another, and often unfamiliar, country. Such a "home away from home" can both become the best memory of a vacation and completely ruin it.

To study offers, consider photos, read reviews of real tourists, it is possible these days literally without leaving home. However, there are thousands of hotels, the opinions of vacationers are sometimes polar different. How not to get lost in this variety?

Hotel Stars

The "star" of the hotel is for many the very first selection criterion.

But for starters, it‚Äôs worth deciding not on the level of the hotel, but on its purpose. Imagine if you are going to relax in a beach resort, but have ended up in a city hotel focused on business travelers. It can be luxurious, "starry" and comfortable, but still will not meet your expectations.

All hotels are conditionally divided into 3 categories.

  • Resorts. Hotels for rest. Usually located on the seashore, river or lake, outside the city, have a large adjacent territory with many areas for rest and relaxation.
  • City & Business. City hotels. Suitable for tourists who want to explore the sights of the city or organize business meetings. They are located not just within the city, but within walking distance from the historical center, or other infrastructure.
  • Transit Hotels located directly on the territory of airports, train stations and other transport hubs. Convenient for transit passengers. They may also have the category of ‚Äúbusiness‚Äù with the necessary set of services.

Only having decided on the purpose of the trip, you can proceed to the choice of the level of the hotel.

In Europe, stars are assigned to a hotel by a special commission that considers the applicant according to 21 criteria. It includes the size of the rooms, the frequency of cleaning, and the availability of additional amenities: a bar, restaurant, spa, gym. Personnel qualifications are also evaluated separately. The presence of such an ‚Äúoptional‚Äù unit as a doorman, porter or concierge may bring an extra star to the hotel.

Nevertheless, this categorization is very arbitrary. Of course, in a five-star hotel you will receive a guarantee of comfort, and the amenities will certainly not be on the floor. But even the "five" may not live up to your expectations.

In an unfamiliar country, it's better to trust familiar brands.

Monobrand Features

A hotel chain is not just a name, but also a certain quality standard. Moreover, management monitors this quality uncompromisingly. After all, only one negative experience is the loss of a client for the entire network. The management team usually has clear criteria for design, the size of the rooms, and the range of services. Personnel training is also centralized. Therefore, if you enjoyed your stay in one of the hotels in the chain, you can safely book a room in another.

It is also worth considering that the usual categorization by stars is the European system. For example, Asia and some southern countries have their own criteria for assigning stars. Therefore, the hotel may surprise you, both pleasantly and not very.

Turning to a familiar brand, you will at least avoid disappointment.

Pleasant persistence bonus

Wherever and for what purpose you go, hotels of famous chains will take you with maximum comfort.

Probably all well-known networks offer their own loyalty program. Be sure to join the club. Membership in it is usually free, and significant savings.

Firstly, hotels inform about special discounts, promotions and hot offers. Secondly, a membership card usually gives a constant discount even in high season. Often hotels give loyal customers gifts in the form of services, flowers and sweets in their rooms, free transfers, dinners and much more.

You should never be afraid to ask for a room a class higher, or to require relocation if something does not suit you. Hotel managers are more willing to meet regular customers.

And remember: one dissatisfied client is a loss for the entire network. Even if something has overshadowed your stay, branded hotels are more likely to listen and accept your complaint. And, of course, they will try to correct the situation.
