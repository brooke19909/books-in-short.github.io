Yuryev-Polsky. Sights and history of the city

In the central part of Russia, between large megacities, there are many beautiful small towns with a unique atmosphere and their own way of life. There is no fuss in them, the rhythm of life is measured and smooth, and the residents are friendly and welcoming. One of these cities is Yuryev-Polsky. Once in it, I want to linger and plunge headlong into this indescribable atmosphere, appreciate the tranquility and beauty of ancient architecture.



Yuryev-Polsky, whose sights must be seen at least once, is located in the vast Vladimir region. The city was founded in 1152 by Prince Yuri Dolgoruky at the intersection of two rivers, surrounded by endless fields. It was thanks to this that he was so named - Yuryev-Polsky.

What must be visited by a tourist who has arrived for the first time in Yuryev-Polsky? Sights, the map of which, in principle, is not needed, are located within the main square of the city, limited by earthen ramparts. Some buildings in the city center are also worth a visit.

So, the journey begins with the most important square in the city of Yuryev-Polsky - Sovetskaya. It is here that you can see the old Trading Rows, which have been preserved since the 19th century, as well as the Mikhailo-Arkhangelsk Monastery. The complex of buildings was originally erected in the 13th century. They have not survived to our time, since most of them suffered during the Tatar-Mongol yoke. After the destruction, the premises were empty for a long time and ruined, until in the XVIII century the monastery was rebuilt. Everyone who comes to Yuryev-Polsky is worth a visit to the Mikhailo-Arkhangelsk Monastery. The sights of this place are the Theological and Znamenskaya refectory churches and St. Michael the Archangel Cathedral.





Right here, on Sovetskaya Square, guests of the city have the opportunity to admire the stunning beauty of the Cathedral of St. George the Victorious. The white-stone building is striking in its grandeur and is a typical monument of ancient architecture. It is this building - the symbol of the city of Yuryev-Polsky. Sights are given a significant place in many excursions that never pass by. The cathedral building was created in the XII century and is famous for its indescribable beauty carvings on white stone. The people, animals and birds depicted on the walls of the building make up real paintings that can be viewed for a very long time. The beauty and accuracy of the work by unknown authors are amazing, as well as the scope and scale of the idea.



Many churches are available for visiting in the city of Yuryev-Polsky. Sights, among which it is worth visiting the Intercession and Nikitsky Church, are typical examples of Central Russian architecture of ancient times. Tourists who come to the city should definitely walk along the territory of the central square, go around the most popular places, walk along the preserved ancient ramparts, which offer a picturesque view of the city. Walks will bring real pleasure to lovers of ancient architecture, will be remembered for immersion in a leisurely atmosphere of life, as well as the silence and tranquility of nature.
