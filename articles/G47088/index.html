Some visitors may be unhappy with the large number of people in restaurants, the periodic lack of free tables and the excessive brilliance of waiters, especially during peak hours. After all, having the opportunity to see with your own eyes how food is cooked is a privilege of true connoisseurs of good cuisine.

Many people, having visited the restaurant once, become regular customers, come again and regularly order food at home. Such guests choose this place to celebrate their birthdays, memorable dates and other important events. This is the main criterion for the success of the institution, good service, stable quality and the right pricing policy. The fact that, being anywhere in the city, you can have a good time and have a tasty meal without unpleasant surprises and inconveniences, is the main advantage of the Tokyo City restaurant chain.
