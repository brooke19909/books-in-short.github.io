Open Dolphinarium in Yeysk

Everyone's favorite dolphinarium in Yeysk appeared first on the Azov coast. The opening took place in 2006. Now it is a favorite vacation spot of indigenous people and visitors. Is the dolphinarium (Yeisk) really so good ? Visitors leave only positive reviews . It is the only open dolphinarium throughout the Azov region. But do not be afraid to sunbathe or get a sunstroke - the stands are reliably protected by awnings. Here you will see the most diverse representatives of marine fauna. The main artists here are the Black Sea bottlenose dolphins Rem and Zorro.





You will also be able to see the Far Eastern beluga whales Miru and Elu, the Pacific walrus Varu and the northern fur seals Dus and Varu. They look forward to the inhabitants of Yeysk and visitors to their unforgettable performances. After all, these charming artists are real professionals. They invariably gather full stands that hold up to five hundred people, and each performance ends with a full house! Every year the program of performances is updated and becomes more interesting and exciting.

Dolphinarium in Yeysk is located on the Taganrog embankment, near Kamenka. This modern, picturesque building cannot be overlooked while driving along Schmidt Street.

How to get there

By car: enter the city of Yeysk, drive to the Taganrog embankment, turn right, two hundred meters straight, on the left hand - a dolphinarium.

By city transport: fixed-route taxi No. 4 and No. 7 towards the beach, stop "Dolphinarium".

Description of the institution and performances

Performances take place from May until the onset of autumn. The leading clown is here.

A huge pool with clear water is waiting for you! The hospitable dolphinarium in Yeysk offers its visitors many treats, such as ice cream, cakes, a variety of drinks, cotton candy and much more. The performance lasts one hour and includes many interesting and breathtaking rooms. Tailed artists diligently entertain the audience, work in a duet with trainers, juggle with various objects, soar high above the water, perform many acrobatic stunts, and the most amazing is that they draw real pictures on a piece of whatman paper! By the way, after the performance, an auction is announced at them, and anyone can buy a work of art he likes.

In addition, the dolphinarium in Yeysk is one of the few places where all performances can be filmed and photographed absolutely free.

All dolphinarium artists are really in love with their work and grateful spectators. Therefore, you can not only watch them in the show. After it, you can spend time in the pool with dolphins, swim and take joint photos with them or with a funny clown. For a fee, respectively.
