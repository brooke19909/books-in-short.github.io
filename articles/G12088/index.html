The most silent washing machines: make a choice

Long gone are the days when the washing process gave housewives tremendous trouble. There is no longer any need to soak huge sheets and duvet covers for the night or to tear off the skin of your hands until blood, getting rid of stains on collars and cuffs of shirts. Developments by scientists have made it possible to take a fresh look at the washing process. Every year, global manufacturers of household appliances present to potential consumers new improved models of various equipment that can ease household chores. Among them, not the least important place is occupied by silent washing machines.





We buy the device

If you decide to purchase the device from a snap, then it is unlikely that you will be able to choose an acceptable option that will please its owner for a long time. There are a huge number of criteria, guided by which you can get the perfect model. The most common sign that almost all consumers want to see is the noiseless operation of the device. Moreover, not every model of even a brand company has this quality.

Buying a silent washing machine is not as easy as it might seem at first glance. Even if the advertisement claims that this equipment does not cause any sound discomfort from its work to the buyer, you should not trust one hundred percent. Browse other options and read customer reviews.





Specifications

How to understand which washing machine is the most silent? The easiest way is to look in the instruction manual. There, a mandatory point will be the noise level. This parameter is measured in decibels (dB). Thanks to the described criterion, it is possible to easily compare among themselves the selected specimens. However, for a thorough understanding of this technical characteristic of a particular model, it is necessary to take into account the operating mode of the equipment. So, during the spin cycle, the noise level in different versions can range from 59 to 83 dB. At the same time, the indicator was measured on devices engaged in spinning at maximum revolutions and at maximum rotation speed. In this sample, silent washing machines are combined in a separate class, the noise level indicator in which is less than 70 dB.





Normal operation

However, some devices that are selected according to the above criteria produce monstrous sounds when performing standard procedures. Therefore, you should also pay attention to the noise level during normal washing. The minimum value of this indicator is 39 dB. The maximum value should not exceed 76 units. However, it is worth remembering that even the most silent washing machine reproduces the main noise precisely during the spin cycle. Therefore, if the pump makes various loud noises when collecting water, then the matter is most likely in this instance, and not in the entire model line. What is noteworthy, some service centers are happy to help resolve such a malfunction of your assistant.





Quiet equipment processes

Almost all manufacturers put special stickers and inscriptions on their silent washing machines that tell about the specifics of the devices. However, in addition to stickers, this class of equipment should also have some internal modifications that are different from others. So, the main characteristic of any device of this variation is improved sound insulation. In this case, the entire inner part of the body must be lined with special sound-absorbing material. In addition, all silent washing machines have an engine that is controlled at high frequency.
