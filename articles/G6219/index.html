War Communism and the New Economic Policy (Comparison): Differences and Similarities in the Economic Sphere

The policy of "war communism" was carried out by the Soviet government from 1918 to 1921. The main essence of the behavior of the authorities in those years is a complete infringement of the rights of the peasantry, as well as the inhabitants of most cities. Since 1922, due to the fact that the economic situation in the country has become critical, the government is changing its approach to working with the population, introducing a new economic policy (NEP). Each phenomenon of the period of "war communism" corresponds to the opposite in the years of the NEP. It is very easy to make a comparison.
