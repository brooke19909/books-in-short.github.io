Review Panasonic HC X810 Camcorder

Panasonic has long had a reputation as one of the most advanced manufacturers of cameras and camcorders. This is not surprising, since the company's products always correspond to the latest trends in the market of such equipment. This applies to both professional devices and modifications of the middle class.





The Panasonic HC X810 camcorder, an overview of which is presented in this article, belongs to the budget segment. At the same time, the device boasts the presence of a whole list of advanced functions in the field of amateur video recording.

Appearance

The casing is made of matte black plastic. The design of the Panasonic HC X810 camcorder can be called standard, as for such devices. In front is the lens, auto focus backlight, LED and built-in flash. Here, the developers placed the grooves that are needed to mount the 3D lens. On the right side, you can see the input socket for recharging and a belt handle, while the opposite side serves as the location of the touch screen with a rotary mechanism. Under the screen is a button on / off the camera, microUSB, mini-HDMI and AV connectors, as well as a lever with which the battery for the Panasonic HC X810 is removed. Below is a slot for mounting the device on a tripod, as well as a compartment for memory cards. On the back of the model is a button for directly starting video recording and a mode switch.

Ergonomics

The dimensions of the device are 134x68x63 millimeters. In this case, the device weighs only 405 grams. As evidenced by numerous user reviews, the Panasonic camcorder is very comfortable in the hand and does not slip out even with prolonged shooting.





Like most other devices from this manufacturer, the build quality is at a high level. In terms of ease of use, even trifles are thought out in the model. Key controls are arranged in such a way that the operator is provided with quick access to all the buttons and keys necessary to make changes to the shooting parameters.

Control

Most of the video shooting options in the Panasonic HC X810 camera can be set by the user using a three-inch touch screen. According to user reviews, the screen responds quickly to touch, providing a smooth transition to the necessary menu items. The interface is simple and straightforward. Even a novice can quickly figure out the basic functions and modes of operation of the device. Several buttons are located directly on the case. They are designed to turn on (off) and start video directly.

Modes

Since the model is intended primarily for amateurs, the developers did not install many complex modes on it. Their set is minimal, but quite sufficient for novice users. This ultimately had a positive effect on the cost of the device. The easiest is automatic intelligent mode. When it is activated, the camcorder itself selects all the shooting parameters depending on the external conditions.





Its complete opposite can be called manual mode. In this case, the user is provided with full access to all recording characteristics. He regulates and installs them independently. In scene mode, the device is configured depending on the scene specified by the operator. This Panasonic camcorder is also capable of taking pictures. At the same time, a function such as a freeze frame is available to the user. To use it, just press the photograph key during video recording.

Well, the last mode is the playback of the captured material through the touch screen. In addition, the model is equipped with many additional features designed to simplify the process of working with it.

Shooting quality

The device is equipped with a 16 megapixel sensor. It creates videos in Full HD format. The resolution is 1920 √ó 1080 pixels, and the maximum frequency is 50 frames per second. This can be called quite high performance, given what price segment the Panasonic HC X810 belongs to. Characteristics of contrast, brightness and color rendition of the picture are at a high level.





It should be noted that this applies only to the optimal shooting conditions. Unfortunately, such outstanding results are not typical for videos created in low light. If the ISO indicator exceeds the mark of 800, noise and blur appear on them, the detail deteriorates. The presence of the MOS System Pro sensor system, which should correct the situation, does not help much. The zoom level also cannot be attributed to the strengths of this camcorder.

Pros and cons

The advantages of the Panasonic HC X810 model include a compact and ergonomic design, acceptable quality of videos shot under optimal conditions, the ability to shoot 3D images, the presence of a stabilization system that allows you to compensate for device vibrations, many additional recording functions, as well as a relatively low cost. Along with this, there are several drawbacks. In this case, we are talking about a weak optical zoom, low quality video in low light, a narrow range of modes.

Conclusion

Representatives of the manufacturer position the Panasonic HC X810 as a camcorder, which is perfect not only for beginners, but also for experienced users. Many of the features that are available in the arsenal of the device can turn video into fun entertainment.





However, do not forget that this model is a budget option. To reduce its cost, the developers sacrificed some features of the device. The result was the presence of the disadvantages described above. On the other hand, none of them can be called critical, and most users of video equipment in this price segment face similar problems.
