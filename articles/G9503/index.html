Alanya Region, Utopia Hotel - the whole world at your feet

Utopia World Hotel, Alanya (Utopia Hotel for short) is a huge, stunningly luxurious hotel complex designed for a chic vacation. It is located on a cliff more than a hundred meters high. These are the spurs of the Toros Mountains. At this place, the ancient city of Siedra once raged with life. Its picturesque ruins are part of a huge hotel park. The hotel is located near the city of Alanya - it is only twenty kilometers away. Antalya Airport is over 150 km away.



Alanya, Hotel Utopia. Territory and living conditions

The hotel has about 560 rooms. For a vast territory of 120 square kilometers, this is not a very populous hotel, but it was created as a palace. You should feel the exclusivity of your vacation. Guests are accommodated in the main seven-story building with three elevators and twenty-six villas. Both the main building and the cottages have standard rooms (for two to four people). In addition, the hotel has two-story rooms with 2 bedrooms for six guests, suites with a jacuzzi for 4-6 people (60-70 square meters), as well as royal suites for 8 customers (128 sq.m.). Sea views are not available in all rooms, but the landscapes of the garden and mountains are also beautiful. This is Turkey, Alanya. The Utopia Hotel, the photo of which is given in this article, confirms that the area is extremely picturesque. In addition, each room has Internet access, a minibar is filled, and there is individually controlled air conditioning.

Alanya, Hotel Utopia. Services, entertainment and food



Internet access via a router, bath procedures (Russian steam room and Turkish hammam, Finnish sauna, as well as a jacuzzi) are provided to guests without payment. By the way, the fitness center is also located in a very picturesque place. Imagine you work on simulators and at the same time admire the sea from a hundred-meter height. Aerobics, dancing (step), tennis and billiards, soccer and basketball - all these services are also free of charge for guests. And here is a massage, a sauna with sea views, diving and motor water activities - for the money.



Ultra All Inclusive is the formula by which its guests eat at this luxury hotel. What does it mean? Alanya, Hotel Utopia. Beach, pool and sea

The coast at the hotel is partially composed of pebbles, but there is also sand. You can ride to the beach in an elevator whose shaft is broken into the rock. The length of the coast with sun loungers, deck chairs and umbrellas is a quarter of a kilometer. There are also two piers. There are several pools in the complex - the main one (almost one and a half kilometers in area), a miniature, special pond for relaxation (by the way, it is open until two at night), with a waterfall - you can try it in mini-rafting. For children open water park with four slides. And for the young guests there are three outdoor and indoor pools.

This is only a very brief description of the various services that the Utopia Hotel (Alanya) complex has. Prices for all these pleasures available to hotel guests start at 19,000 rubles per night.
