Detailed instructions on how to remove Yandex.Money wallet

With the advent of electronic wallets, it has become even more convenient to make purchases in online stores, to replenish the accounts of a mobile phone, not only your own, but, if necessary, your friends or relatives.





Why else need a virtual wallet?

Thanks to the virtual wallet, you can not worry about the security of your bank card, because in this case there is no need to pay everything only with it and enter a secret PIN code with every purchase. Of course, few people from the first days of use think about how to remove Yandex.Wallet. This question arises much later, when the need to use this system disappears. It should be noted that among active visitors to online stores, this Yandex service is quite popular. So, virtual money is used for purchases, payment of receipts, airline tickets and other services. But now the day has come when you (Yandex virtual money) are no longer needed, because, for example, you have become a client of another payment system. So, how to remove Yandex.Money wallet? Step-by-step instructions are presented below.

Yandex.Wallet removal steps

There are several ways to get rid of an irrelevant account in the Yandex.Money system. The first is to contact the technical support service with this question. But before that, you need to withdraw funds from the wallet if they remain in the account, since after deleting the electronic account access to money will be closed. The technical support specialist will tell the client in detail about this, to which the client will ask about how to remove the Yandex.Money wallet. It is worth noting that it is impossible to completely hide the entire history of monetary transactions and transactions from the system; at the request of the relevant structures, data on the electronic wallet will still be received, even if this payment tool is inactive by this time. The history of payment management stores all operations and cash flows, whether it is topping up, withdrawing or transferring money.





Yandex.Wallet removal algorithm

Another way to remove the Yandex.Money wallet, if it is no longer relevant, is as follows. First you need to enter your email, enter your username and password in a special field. On the top line you will find the headings “Search”, “Money”, “Mail”. You need to select the "Money" tab. The inscription https: // money appears in the address bar of the browser. yandex. ru /, in it the word “money” needs to be deleted, and instead of it enter the word “passport”, then press the “Enter” key. After completing this action, a page with three tabs will appear on the monitor screen:

  • personal data of the client;
  • Customer billing information
  • social profile.

Select the tab “Personal data”. On the right you will see the information and phone number of the client entered during registration. There is also a button “Delete account”, by clicking on which, you can delete the electronic wallet. After that, a warning appears on the monitor screen stating that you are about to delete your Yandex.Wallet. To confirm the removal of the settlement system, you will need to enter the current password from the mailbox assigned to this account in the window. Then click the “Delete Account” button again.





Summary and Conclusion

From this article, you learned about how to remove the Yandex.Money wallet. After this operation, access to the wallet will be closed. Even if funds are left on it, it will be impossible to use them. It should be understood that it will not be possible to delete only one wallet, it will be deleted along with the mailbox. Therefore, if the client expects to send letters from this mailbox, then you should not delete the account anyway. It is enough to simply withdraw the remaining funds from it and not use it if it is no longer needed. We hope that after reading this article, the question of how to remove Yandex.Money wallet will no longer cause difficulties.
