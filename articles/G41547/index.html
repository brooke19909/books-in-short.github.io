Reprofiling

After the 1917 revolution , the monastery was no longer used for its intended purpose. Convicts under various articles began to serve their sentences in the monastery. At first, it was a general labor correctional colony . Since 1994, only prisoners sentenced to life imprisonment were sent to this institution. The choice of this place fell by chance: the impressive thickness of the monastery walls reliably guards cruel criminals from the outside world.

Tierra del Fuego today is a place of serving the sentence of two hundred killers. Many of them ruined more than one life, and with particular rigidity. Visiting journalists are always impressed by the cards on the doors of the cells: from them you can learn about all the crimes of prisoners. At this moment, it is difficult not to succumb to a comprehensive fear, because being among such cruel people is psychologically difficult.

There are three criminals in each cell. During infrequent walks, these people are necessarily handcuffed, they move around with their hands behind their backs, in a bent position. Sometimes they leave their cells in order to undergo a medical examination. The prisoners work daily for an hour and a half - they sew gloves.

The colony has a psychologist. It helps criminals overcome difficult moments. Many prisoners came to God. It is noteworthy that all the icons in the local confession chapel are made by the hands of the convicted.
