DIY crafts from plastic bottles. What could be easier?

If a person wants to create, then nothing can stop him: neither a lack of time, nor a lack of money for materials, nor a bad mood. Moreover, having shown a little imagination, you can create a true masterpiece from any materials at hand. For example, you can make crafts. Do it yourself from plastic bottles create a lot of different things.



Surprisingly easy and fast you can learn to make absolutely any kind of objects. Using disposable containers, which are found in abundance in any house or apartment, you can build a high chair or ottoman, curtain, cabinet, vase, wash basin, flowers, candle holders, lampshade. And also - stylish jewelry, buttons, pockets for small things, animals, caskets, a fence ... You won‚Äôt list everything. From such a hobby there is another positive point. Making crafts with our own hands from plastic bottles, we contribute to the preservation of the purity of our mother nature and the world around us. After all, we do not litter our planet with garbage that decomposes for about a hundred years. We turn it into a stylish and necessary interior item, decorate our Earth. And also raise the mood to other people who see what can be created from an ordinary and familiar object.



Crafts from plastic bottles with their own hands - this is real creativity, which can bring a lot of pleasure to the author himself. The material itself can be very different in size and shape, color and capacity. Combining them with each other, as a result you get a real designer thing that will cause admiration, and sometimes envy.

Feel like a real sculptor and make New Year's crafts with your own hands from plastic bottles. Just imagine how much joy this activity will bring to your little one. And what kind of original Christmas tree will you have if you dress it with funny gnomes, balls, garlands, bells and icicles. And you can create a tree itself, saving a green forest beauty from cruel felling.

In recent years, DIY crafts made of plastic bottles have become especially popular. You will not recognize your summer cottage! After all, this light and plastic material is suitable for arranging beautiful, functional and funny flower beds, a garden windmill, a drinking bowl for birds, a washstand, and an irrigation system. Also, the ubiquitous bottles are suitable for growing seedlings, for use as a watering can. By stringing them on sticks or strong threads stretched parallel to the ground, you get a stylish fence.



How to do DIY crafts from plastic bottles? First of all, you need to stock up on enough material. Not only mineral water, juice or Coca-Cola containers are suitable. You can use large containers and cans, bottles of household chemicals and detergents. And craftsmen use disposable tableware, boxes of plastic made from sweets, lids of different calibers. Then we draw on the basis of, for example, a flower and cut it out. To ensure that the edges of the product are not sharp, you need to melt them. This is done using a candle, a soldering iron or sand, heated in a pan. Your workpiece is slightly rounded and takes on an incredible shape. At first, the work will seem difficult, but over time you will get the necessary dexterity. Next, we connect several such blanks with wire, insert a bead-center in the center. In the same way, we also create leaves.

Use your imagination, draw inspiration from photos of crafts, study at master classes that are on the Internet. It is possible that very soon your skill will grow so much that friends and acquaintances will ask you to sell your masterpiece.
