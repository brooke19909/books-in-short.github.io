Crete, Kyknos Beach Hotel Bungalows 4 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If you plan to spend your summer vacation on the Greek island of Crete, and at the same time want to stay in one of the comfortable hotels that hundreds of our compatriots have already appreciated, then the Kyknos Beach Hotel Bungalows 4 * will be an excellent choice. We offer to learn more about what awaits guests here.

Description, location and photo of the hotel

This hotel has at its disposal 134 rooms. 32 of them are located in the main building, and 102 - in cozy small bungalows. There are both standard and family rooms. Each of them has everything you need: a bathroom, air conditioning, refrigerator, hairdryer, safe, TV, etc. Most of the rooms have a balcony. When booking accommodation in Kiknos Beach, you can choose the type of food at your discretion: "all inclusive" or half board. It features a large restaurant and three bars.

As for the location, the hotel is just a couple of hundred meters from the sea. So the path to the beach will take no more than five minutes. The hotel is located between the resorts of Malia and Stalis. Hersonnisos is only 8 kilometers away from Kyknos Beach Hotel Bungalows 4 *. Heraklion (airport) is 35 kilometers away. So upon arrival on the road to the hotel, it will take you no more than half an hour.

Impression of the kitchen and hotel staff

According to the vast majority of guests, Kyknos Beach Hotel Bungalows 4 *, the food here is just great. For lunch and dinner, there is always a lot of vegetables (both fresh and stewed), meat dishes, seafood, desserts, fresh fruits. Our compatriots considered the hotel staff polite, welcoming and pleasant people. Mostly everyone here speaks English, German and Greek. However, if necessary, you can find Russian-speaking staff (for example, one of the cooks and waiters in a restaurant). So there will be no misunderstanding.





Kyknos Beach Hotel Bungalows 4 * (Crete): comments on beach vacations and entertainment

This hotel has no private beach (as well as most hotels in Greece). Almost all the beaches on the island are in municipal ownership. You can walk to the nearest of them from Kiknos Beach in five minutes. The beaches are sandy, the sea is warm. Entering shallow water. Sun beds are paid (6-8 euros), but you can always stay in a free area on a towel or mat brought with you. You can buy them in one of the shops in the vicinity of the hotel. Here you can buy a folding solar umbrella.

As for entertainment in the territory of Kyknos Beach Hotel Bungalows 4 *, here are equipped with two swimming pools, as well as a "paddling pool" for kids. The water is clean. According to the guests, no one had any skin irritations after bathing. The bar by the large pool delighted guests with a wide variety of quality drinks and excellently prepared cocktails. Also, all with kind words recall the cheerful bartenders. As for the sunbathing terrace, it is recommended that guests take up sunbeds early if possible. There is no animation at the hotel. You can play tennis or table tennis.

At the hotel there is a kids club (located near the small pool). Here, according to tourists who arrived on vacation with their families, you can leave the child for several hours. There is also a playground.
