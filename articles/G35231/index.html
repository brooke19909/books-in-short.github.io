The most beautiful places in Georgia: description

Georgia is a popular tourist destination for travelers from all over the world. This country is considered the embodiment of all the beauties of the Caucasus. Incredible nature, mild climate, amazing culture, wonderful sights - all this awaits you during a trip to Georgia. One of the most wonderful countries of the post-Soviet space will not leave anyone indifferent. You will learn about what beautiful places in Georgia should be visited by every traveler from our article.

Old Town in Tbilisi

Tbilisi is one of the most beautiful cities in Georgia, it is called the soul and heart of this country. The historic district of Tbilisi is a place that takes more than one hour to explore. The Old Town is a part of the settlement, once surrounded by a fortress wall, fragments of which can be seen on Pushkin Street and Baratashvili Avenue.

Most of the buildings in the historical district of Tbilisi are masterpieces of architecture: numerous neat churches, low elegant houses, narrow streets and architectural monuments, preserved to this day, will allow you to plunge into the atmosphere of incredible color. Even people who are far from art will be delighted with a walk through the distinctive and vibrant Old Town, if you believe the numerous reviews of tourists about this attraction.

Narikala fortress in Tbilisi

The most beautiful places of Georgia are concentrated in its historical center - a city called Tbilisi. Although we talked about the Old Town, we consider it necessary to highlight the most striking sights of this settlement. And the first place that, according to tourists' reviews, you need to visit while traveling in Georgia is the ancient fortress of Narikala.

This building is an internationally recognized symbol of Tbilisi. The attraction is located on Mount Mtatsminda, namely - on the Sololaksky Range. Climbing to its top, you will see an amazing panorama of the city. The ascent to the fortress cannot be called easy, however, if we turn to the ancient belief, people do not feel tired at all, going up to the fortress. It is believed that the attraction is located in those places where the Great Silk Road once ran.

Sulfur baths in Tbilisi

Experienced travelers are advised to visit the sulfur baths that were erected in the city center in the 17th century. They immediately gained great fame. Firstly, the construction is made in the Persian style. Secondly, it is believed that in the baths there is a healing source. Warm water, which contains a large amount of hydrogen sulfide, enters the baths from the ground. Similar structures were erected in England and in Italy.

Gabriadze Clock in Tbilisi

This attraction was named in honor of Rezo Gabriadze, a cult director who has such works as Kin-Dza-Dza, Mimino and Do not Cry. The puppet theater tower personifies the spirit of Georgia. Hourly music plays here, at the same time a door opens under the roof. An angel with golden wings appears, he knocks on a bell with a hammer.

Moreover, every day you can watch the curtain of a small theater open. A Georgian folk song is played, to which short ideas about human life are shown. According to traveler reviews, these performances compel viewers to experience the full range of emotions.

Funicular of Tbilisi

If you want to see all the most beautiful places in Georgia at the same time, you should go for a walk on the funicular. By cable car you will reach the Mtatsminda plateau, on the territory of which the largest amusement park in Tbilisi is located - the Bombora entertainment complex. Tourists in their reviews note that the cabins of the funicular are transparent, which means that the city offers breathtaking panoramic views.

Metekhi Church in Tbilisi

One of the most beautiful places in Georgia is Metekhi Church, which is a must-have item on any tourist route. The construction is recognizable thanks to the bronze equestrian statue created by the sculptor E. Amashukeli. According to travelers, it‚Äôs simply impossible not to notice the church.

The temple is located on a hill on the banks of the Kura River. Built several centuries ago, he witnessed the centuries-old history of the city. According to one version, the church was built during the reign of Vakhtang Gorgasali.

Jean Chardin Street

You could see this street in numerous photographs from blogs about tourism. It is considered one of the most beautiful places in Georgia. It is located in the quarter where artisans once worked. The street got its name in honor of Jean de Chardin - a French traveler who lived in the 17-18 centuries.

This street is known as the refuge of the intelligentsia of Tbilisi. Here you will find numerous restaurants and cafes, beauty salons and boutiques, archaeological exhibits and art centers. This street is considered the most European in the whole city. It is a prime example of how modernity can coexist with antiquity.

Rustaveli Avenue

One of the most interesting places is the main street of Tbilisi, which originates from Freedom Square. Currently, it is on this street that the main state, cultural, public facilities and business centers are concentrated.

On both sides of the avenue you can see plane trees. The name was given to the street in honor of the famous poet Shota Rustaveli, who lived and worked in Georgia. Tourists note that on this street you can find anything, because it is one of the busiest avenues of the city.

Signagi

This town is an ideal place for a romantic trip. Lovers come here to see the mountains (beautiful places of Georgia are often surrounded by them). In Signagi, according to reviews, you can enjoy the view of amazing landscapes. Panoramas of the Alazani Valley will turn your head. Follow the advice of tourists and be sure to make time for a walk around the city: red roofs, tiled, old pavements, cozy houses - all this will make an unforgettable impression on you!

Jvari Monastery in Mtskheta

We continue to list the most beautiful places in Georgia. Mtskheta is the first Georgian capital, it is also considered the heart of this civilization. The settlement was founded in the Bronze Age, at present it is one of the most important cultural sites of Georgia. The city is located on ancient trade routes. It is located just 20 km from Tbilisi.

Jvari Monastery, or, as it is also called, the Monastery of the Holy Cross, was erected in the 6th century. It is located on a hill, at the confluence of the Mtkvari and Aragvi rivers. Currently, the brightest attraction of Mtskheta is included in the UNESCO World Heritage List. A visit to the ancient structure allows you to feel the atmosphere of antiquity, as tourists write about in their reviews.

Wine Cellars in Kakheti

Kakheti is a spacious wine region. At first glance, visiting it is optional, because there are no transit roads, sea and mountains, the climate is dry. However, experienced travelers are advised to visit the local wine cellars for every person who wants to know the real Georgia.

In the past, Kakheti was an independent kingdom, but is now part of Georgia. The administrative capital of the region is Telavi. Kakheti gained great popularity as a tourist region after it began to organize wine tours here.

According to reviews, such excursions allow you to learn a lot about the wine production process. They will tell you about grape varieties that grow in Georgia, maybe you will have a chance to taste real Georgian wine.

Okatse Canyon, Kutaisi

Georgia has a very well developed tourism infrastructure. Travelers who wish to experience a sense of adrenaline can go for a walk along the Okatse Canyon, which is located just 50 km from Kutaisi. According to reviews, you can overcome the path along the canyon along the coast. But some tourists use a suspension bridge, the length of which exceeds 1 km. The bridge is located at an altitude of about 100 m, directly above the precipice. The suspension road ends with an observation deck where you can take unusual photos.

Svaneti Towers

We continue the listing of the most beautiful places in Georgia. Where to go to those people who want to see the oldest sights of the country? You should visit the towers of Svaneti. They are located in the historical region in the north-west of the state, surrounded by the Main Caucasus and Svaneti ranges. In addition, nearby is the village of Ushguli, which is sometimes called the eighth wonder of the world. The settlement is considered the highest in Europe. Tourists who have been here, at an altitude of 2300 m above sea level, note that the views of the surrounding nature are fascinating.

Gergeti Church in Kazbegi

This building is located at the foot of Kazbek, next to the Georgian Military Highway. This architectural complex was built in the 14th century. It includes the Holy Trinity Cathedral, a bell tower and living quarters. Travelers advise visiting the Gergeti church in the winter season, because it is during this period that you can fully appreciate the beauty of the untouched nature of Georgia (photos of the most beautiful places in the country are presented in our article).

Darial Gorge

This natural attraction is located on the border of Georgia with Russia. In the past, it was called the "Gate of the Caucasus." The gorge near the village of Stepantsminda begins, and ends in Upper Lars. In the narrowest place is an Orthodox church, the visit of which is definitely worth making time.

Mountain Adjara

Beautiful places of Georgia, photos of which you can find in our article, are in another region - Adjara. The cleanest air, rich vegetation, picturesque sea, majestic mountains, ancient fortresses - all this awaits you while traveling in Adjara. According to reviews, it is worth coming here in order to see the stone bridge of Queen Tamara, which crosses a mountain river. Surprising is the fact that the structure has no supports at all.

Batumi

This city is one of the most beautiful places in Georgia. Where to go if you are in Batumi for the first time? Tourists are advised to go to Neptune Square and Miracle Park. Be sure to visit Europe Square and the Seaport, Alphabet Tower and the Astronomical Clock. According to reviews, in Batumi you can find everything you want, including clean pebble beaches, restaurants and casinos, night clubs and a botanical garden. Unique resorts nearby are:

  • Ureki - a place with healing sands.
  • Kabuleti is a resort that is ideal for families. Here is a children's amusement park "Tsitsinatela."
  • Tsikhisdziri is a place where water sports lovers come: divers and divers.

Manor Chavchavadze

Where are beautiful places in Georgia (photos are presented in our article) related to art and culture? One of them is located in Kakheti. The estate belonged to Alexander Chavchavadze - a military general and a famous poet. He restored the building, built the Italian palace and even set up a picturesque garden. Once upon a time, such eminent writers as Alexander Pushkin, Mikhail Lermontov and Alexander Griboedov came to these parts. The wedding of the latter took place here!

Tourists can visit the estate-museum, the interiors of which were recreated with amazing accuracy. The museum was opened in 1946, it contains valuable exhibits, including personal belongings of the owners of the house. According to reviews, museum tours are very exciting. People who visited the Chavchavadze estate significantly expand their horizons.
