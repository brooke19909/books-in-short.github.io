Mehtap Beach Hotel (Turkey, Marmaris): hotel description, services, reviews

Marmaris has long been the most sought after Turkish resort. He is loved for its mild climate, for its excellent location in a lovely cove, sheltered from winds interfering with beach vacations, for the beauty of nature reigning around, for unique sights, for dozens of nightclubs, discos, restaurants, other entertainment facilities and for the holiday atmosphere reigning here is always. The Mehtap Beach Hotel 4 * hotel will help to make your stay in Marmaris even more memorable, pleasant and carefree, and quite affordable prices for tours with reasonably good service will certainly appeal to everyone who chooses this hotel among many others.

Location

Mehtap Beach Hotel is in a convenient location for those tourists who are traveling to Marmaris to relax on the sea and have fun 100 percent. On the one hand, the city is washed by the waters of two Mediterranean seas and the Aegean, because it is here that their conditional border passes. On the other hand, mountains surround it, preventing the penetration of cold air. All this makes your stay in Marmaris comfortable in summer and winter.

Mehtap Beach Hotel is located almost in the center of the bay and the city, close to many significant objects of its infrastructure. So, to the water park "Atlantis" from him two hundred meters. About the same or a little more to many bars and restaurants located on the famous tourist street Bar Street. A little further, about a 12-minute walk, is the Lukum factory and Turgut waterfall. Also very nearby there are many architectural monuments, a beautiful garden, a promenade, and the beach is only 30 meters away. Tourists can stroll to the Youth Square with the most beautiful fountains in Turkey, to the famous Caravanserai, the ancient amphitheater, port, Crazy Daisy nightclub and many other objects for every taste.

How to get there

Marmaris is located 95 km from the city of Dalaman, near which there is an international airport of the same name. In the summer there fly planes from some cities of Russia (Moscow, Samara, St. Petersburg, Novosibirsk, Yekaterinburg) and Ukraine (Kiev, Lviv, Odessa). Arriving in Dalaman, organized tourists (who bought tickets to travel agencies) are delivered to Mehtap Beach Hotel by comfortable transfers. Travel time, taking into account the delivery of people to other hotels, is about 2 hours. Tourists traveling to rest, bypassing travel agencies, can get from Dalaman to Marmaris by taxi, paying $ 200, or by bus, which takes you to a bus station located in another part of the city. From there, the easiest way to get to the hotel is by taxi, spending no more than $ 20.



Description

Mehtap Beach Hotel was built in 1989, so the term of his work in the tourism business is solid. The last major overhaul was carried out here in 2012, so the territory, rooms and all infrastructure are in good condition. In the brochures, this hotel is positioned as a four-star, but many tourists believe that its category is three stars, and believe that it is consistent with this. The hotel area is small, only 2 thousand square meters. On it stands a five-story building of a single building with a porch of several steps and a ramp. Nearby there is a small terrace with tables, acting as a cafe in the fresh air. There is almost no parking space. In the backyard there is a small area paved with tiles. It has a pool, sun loungers and umbrellas. There is a bar nearby. That’s the whole territory.

Infrastructure

Mehtap Beach Hotel 4 * meets its guests with a spacious lobby, where several sets of upholstered furniture are installed. There is a reception, a lobby bar, a library desk and a small shop with beach accessories. The reception is serviced around the clock, the staff speaks English and Turkish, some employees have a little understanding of Russian. At the reception you can arrange a doctor’s visit, put things and valuables in storage, order laundry services, call a taxi, buy sightseeing tours. One part of the foyer wall is made with huge panoramic windows that overlook the sea. And on the terrace, tourists can not only admire the endless blue distances, but also feel the exciting marine aroma, because there are literally a few steps to the water's edge.



Rooms

Mehtap Beach Hotel has 80 guestrooms. Their categories:

- Standard with an area of ​​up to 16 squares, designed for a maximum of 3 people.

- Suit room with an area of ​​up to 20 squares, designed for 3 people maximum.

- Superior area of ​​20 squares. The room is divided into two rooms without doors, can accommodate up to 4 people. Extra beds, including cots, are not available at the hotel.

All rooms have a similar design, based on the principle of minimalism, all are equipped with balconies where plastic furniture is located. The windows in each room are huge (in some they occupy two walls at once), they overlook the sea. It can be in different angles, which depends on the location of the room (the central part of the building or the end). Equipment - beds, a wardrobe, a table with a mirror and a mini fridge, air conditioning, a modern TV (there is only one channel in Russian), a safe (for a fee). The hygiene room has a shower, hairdryer, washbasin and toilet. Hygiene products are provided upon check-in. In the future, only toilet paper is replenished. Wi-Fi works well in almost every room. Cleaning is done according to the schedule set in the hotel. According to tourists, the rooms are quite comfortable, quite suitable for relaxing after a busy day.



Nutrition

Most travel agencies offer trips to Mehtap Beach Hotel 4 * with the Al power system, but, as practice shows, it is more convenient to take tours of BB or HB systems here. At less than 20 meters from the hotel there is a promenade with lots of cafes and taverns for every taste. Nearby are the streets and alleys, where also at every step there are small and large establishments where you can eat what you want. For those tourists who have chosen the type of food Al, in the central and only restaurant of the hotel there are breakfasts, lunches and dinners according to the "self-service" system. According to guests, the breakfast menu is modest and includes scrambled eggs or boiled eggs, vegetables in salad and sliced, cheese, jam, butter, coffee, tea. For lunch, soups, mashed potatoes, pasta, rice, fried potatoes, Turkish and European salads, raw vegetables in slices, chicken, nuggets, fish, fruits (mainly watermelon), pastries are offered. For dinner, the menu is about the same. The bars in the infrastructure of the hotel 2 - in the lobby and near the pool. From drinks, you can order beer, water, packaged juice, sprite, and cola for free. During the day, a snack is arranged for guests, where pizza and flat cakes are offered.

The hotel restaurant has two zones - closed with air conditioning and open on the terrace.



Stay with children

Mehtap Beach Hotel 4 * is mainly oriented towards youth. Marmaris is a fun, noisy resort where the festive atmosphere reigns uninterrupted. Relaxing with young children, especially in the city center, is very difficult. There is practically nothing in the hotel for young guests. All that couples can attract here is the proximity of the sea.

Leisure for adults

Since Mehtap Beach is located in the center of the resort, it is designed to ensure that guests entertain themselves. The hotel has an outdoor pool with a rather small area and a depth of 1.65 meters. According to the guests, it is very little in demand, despite the fact that there are comfortable sunbeds with mattresses and umbrellas around. There is a billiard room in the lobby, which can be used at an additional cost. There are no other entertainments. Animation is also not provided.





Beach

The proximity of the sea is the main advantage of the Mehtap Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey). Marmaris tourists love for the beauty of nature, climate, shopping, excellent spa business, but a beach holiday for everyone remains in the first place. Marmaris is located in a bay, some of which belong to the Aegean Sea, others of the Mediterranean. In fact, it is in the Marmaris region that an invisible border passes (officially it is located a little closer to Dalaman), separating these two huge water basins. For a beach holiday, this is important, since the waters of the Aegean Sea, you can say, are cool, warm only by the beginning of June, and by the end of September they manage to cool down. The waters of the Mediterranean Sea are much friendlier, which is why the swimming season on their coast opens in May. Marmaris is in an intermediate position. Here, at the end of spring, the water temperature does not exceed +20, and the warmest is from June to September. But the islet, located at the entrance to the bay, closes it from the restless sea winds.

If the weather is all right, a beach holiday at Mehtap Beach is a great success. In fact, guests coming out of the hotel door immediately get to the beach. It is quite large in length, so the desired place where you can settle in with comfort is always there. Coverage of the beach is small pebbles, the entry into the water is smooth, umbrellas and sunbeds are free. The disadvantage of the beach is the lack of quality cleaning. Those who wish to relax with great comfort can proceed to the neighboring beaches belonging to the coastal cafe. There is more order on them, but you have to pay or buy drinks for the sunbed and umbrella.



Additional Information

Great for all unassuming active tourists, the Mehtap Beach Hotel. Turkey is a diverse and multifaceted country. Her Marmaris resort is chosen by those who are on vacation looking for an enchanting holiday, not peace. The location of this hotel in the very center of a fun-filled life is a big minus for those who are on vacation looking for peace and harmony with nature.

From the first minutes of your stay, Mehtap Beach has an atmosphere of lightness and ease. Here, nobody imposes anything on anyone, the staff does not adhere to the dress code, does not see if guests take out food from the restaurant, just as they do not prohibit bringing their own drinks and products.

For leisure activities, a free shuttle to the city center leaves at the hotel every day at 11 pm, and returns at 3 a.m.

Active tourists can buy a lot of interesting excursions to the islands and the surrounding area from their tour operator or at any city tour desk.

Mehtap Beach Hotel: reviews

Hotel rating in various travel agencies is from 5.5 to 6.5 out of 10 possible points, and the average rating is “good”. The advantages marked by guests:

- An ideal location for outdoor activities;

- maximum proximity of the sea;

-low prices;

- unobtrusive service;

- friendly staff;

- functional rooms (everything works well, there is always hot water).

Noted disadvantages:

- too noisy, it’s impossible to sleep well;

- the hotel's beach is dirty;

- cleaning of rooms of poor quality;

- food is monotonous, there are practically no alcoholic drinks according to the Al system.
