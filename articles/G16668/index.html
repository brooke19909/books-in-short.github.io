How to choose a storage water heater "Ariston" (100 liters): reviews

The only drawback of storage boilers is the obligatory presence of pressure in the pipe with cold water. Otherwise, in its absence, it is not possible to use hot, passing through the heating tank.

There are various options in stores. They differ in design, technical characteristics, control method and, of course, volume. For example, the most popular appliances are 100 liters. Device

Accumulative electric water heater "Ariston" per 100 liters consists of the following parts. Let's look at them.

Protective systems

Each storage water heater "Ariston" (100 liters) is equipped with protection systems. First of all, the manufacturer installed a special device that does not allow the device to turn on without water. Also an important option is the protective shutdown function. The electric part of the boiler is made in such a way as to completely prevent accidental electric shock. More expensive options have bacterial protection. It is also very important to pay attention to the safety valve. Installation of the device without it is strictly prohibited. It is this device that helps reduce internal pressure, which, with a record increase, can even lead to an explosion.
