The hotel complex Villa AL Khalidiah 3 (UAE) is located near the tourist center of Sharjah. Next to it are key attractions and a walking area. Tourists visit the ethnographic museum, get acquainted with the expositions of the historical museum, examine the halls in the maritime museum.

The giant aquarium, located on the shores of the bay, is the main attraction of Sharjah. The aquarium is populated by 250 species of marine animals. It is home to miniature fish, seahorses, moray eels, sharks. A huge underwater kingdom opens before the audience behind huge glasses. The guests get the impression that they are at the bottom of the real ocean. The second floor of the aquarium is occupied by a museum, the exposition of which contains rare specimens that inhabit the water column of the ocean. The aquarium has a cafeteria and a small shop selling souvenirs.
