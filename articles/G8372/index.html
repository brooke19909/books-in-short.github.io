  1. Take a balloon. Better tight.
  2. Insert the funnel inside. If not, you can cut the plastic bottle and pull the tail of the ball on the neck. Pour flour through this funnel.
  3. Press the contents of the ball tightly. This can be done with a pen, pencil, or a wand. The main thing is not to tear the ball itself.
  4. The size of our Kapitoshka may be different. It depends on the amount of flour.
  5. When the ball has become the size we need, release the excess air and tie the tail onto a knot (without using threads). The excess can be cut off if desired.
  6. It's time to make our Kapitoshka face. For this, draw a marker with eyes, nose, and mouth. You can glue the "hair" of woolen threads.
  7. Here you go! Homemade transforming toy is ready.
