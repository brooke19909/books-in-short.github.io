Clinical Examination Value





We examined in the section above what function peroxisomes perform. Now a little about the clinical purpose of these organelles. Now let's talk a little about a disease called Zellweger syndrome. This is a rather serious disease, it is caused by a violation of protein imports. Consequently, peroxisomes are empty, in the cell and in the body as a whole, acute insufficiency of these organelles is detected. People who suffer from these disorders experience difficulties with the work of some important organs, this may include:

  • brain;
  • liver;
  • the kidneys.

Children who are born with this syndrome cannot live long and are forced to leave our world after a very short time. This disease is associated with a mutation of this organoid. Less dangerous forms of the disease are also possible.
