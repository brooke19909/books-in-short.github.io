How to make a passepartout with your own hands

Making a picture, photograph or embroidery in a baguette will require the use of a passport. Of course, you can do without it, but successfully matched to the color of the mat, its impeccable execution will make our image sound in a new, more complete and interesting way. In addition, the mat is able to harmoniously combine the decorated image with interior details .



You can buy a passe-partout in finished form, but it is not at all difficult to make a passe-partout with your own hands. First you need to decide on the color, then buy cardboard in the baguette workshop . To make a passport, you will need to cut a neat window in the sheet of this cardboard. The surface on which we will work should be strong enough and resilient, and be sure to be perfectly flat. In addition, it should be noted that in the process of cutting there is a threat of damage to it with a knife.

When making a passepartout with your own hands, you can use the usual well-ground knife, but if possible, it is better to purchase a special cutter . Using it, you can make an oblique cut (45 degrees) on the inside of the frame, which will give the image a perspective and a sense of depth, and the work will look professional and elegant.

It should not be forgotten that the passe-partout for embroidery, photography or painting should overlap the image by at least 5 mm around the entire perimeter. When cutting, you should be very careful, the cleanliness and accuracy of the cut must be observed.



As for the tools with which the manufacture of the mat is made, it should be noted that working with the simplest and cheapest cutter is quite complicated, without certain skills it is unlikely to be able to do the job accurately. Much better results can be obtained by using a cutter in which there is a special slot for the knife blade. One of the most convenient and affordable accessories for making a passe-partout with your own hands is the Logan 4000 cutter, which has a fixed angle of inclination of interchangeable blades.

The device for the convenience of the user is equipped with control marks that define the beginning and end of the cut. To fix the required field sizes, a movable graduated rod is used.
