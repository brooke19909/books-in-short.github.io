Zoo

What else can Novosibirsk offer ? Where to go with the kids on the weekend? One of the most fascinating options is to visit the local zoo. About 10,000 mammals, birds and amphibians are represented here. To see the most outlandish animals, just book an excursion program.

Planetarium



Where can I go in Novosibirsk on the weekend? A great idea for an exciting and informative pastime with the whole family can be a visit to the city planetarium. The building of the latter consists of several modern observatories, interconnected by galleries.

In the main hall of the planetarium, visitors will enjoy entertaining documentaries about space on a full-dome screen. With a clear starry sky, an observatory becomes available for customers of the institution, where you can view in detail the most distant astronomical bodies using a telescope.
