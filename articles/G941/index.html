Where is that street?

Uniquely positive customers evaluate the location of the hotel. It takes no more than 40 minutes to get from the airport to it, and no more than 500 m to walk from the resort center to the hotel. The road to the sea from the room does not take more than 5-7 minutes and passes by cafes, shops and a large market - in general, it is interesting. In addition, near the resort is the city of Nessebar, where you can stroll along the old streets and get acquainted with the sights, feeling the spirit of old Bulgaria. You can get there by bus or tourist train.

Let's go to the rooms!

When choosing a room in which you intend to spend at least 10-12 days, it is better not to consider the first floor: many vacationers complained about dampness, including bedding and towels, as well as the smell of mold. In addition, it is better to disagree with a room whose windows do not look outside the hotel, as animations from neighboring hotels do not create comfortable sleeping conditions. As many guests note, the balcony facing the courtyard greatly adds to the calm.



But with thin "plywood" walls, unfortunately, nothing can be done. Everything and everyone is heard: slamming doors, children, lovers, snoring grandmothers. According to reviews, in Hrizantema 4 * Sunny Beach is definitely not for people who are sensitive to such situations.

The rooms themselves fully meet the concept of standard in terms of comfort and quality of installed furniture. The trouble is the periodically turning off air conditioner, so in the hot summer it is better not to take rooms in the main building, because the sun peeks in them most often.

But the security in the rooms is all right. Abandoned phones, tablets and documents always remained in their places.
