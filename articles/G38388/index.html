Greece beaches: endless list of the best

On the territory of Hellas there is an incredible number of beaches. Many of them are quite remarkable. That is why this state regularly gets into the TOP-3 countries with the best coasts in the world. So, the beaches of Greece, which deserve special attention.

Crete. Balos Beach



Wonderful Greece, Crete, whose beaches annually fall in love with thousands of tourists ... Become one of them - allow yourself to relax on the coast, washed by the cleanest waters. Balos Bay is really something supernatural. Here merge into one three seas and 12 shades of water. Sea turtles, Gramvousa island, a riveting look, and the beauty of the bay itself ... The list is endless. It is not for nothing that this place was once chosen by Prince Charles and Princess Diana for their honeymoon. It is most convenient to visit the bay on ships that regularly depart from the port of Kavonisi Kissamos.

Navagio (Zakynthos)



Of all the Greek islands, Zakynthos is the greenest. Probably every third person in the world saw this photo, which is often found on Greek postcards, posters and advertising booklets. It is possible that this is the most beautiful beach in the world. In order to see such beauty yourself, you need to swim to it by boat or by car.

Elafonisos (Crete)



Sunny Greece, the beach, pink sand , shallow and clear sea - what could be better? This place is located 100 meters from the coastline of Crete. Pinkish sand is the result of a merger of ground corals and fragments of shells. Three seas are connected here : the Ionian, Cretan and Libyan. If you come here and stay for the whole day, you will have the opportunity to contemplate 18 shades of water. Elafonisos is located near Balos, so if you are traveling by car in Crete, this place should be a bullet on the map of your route.





Egremni (Lefkada)

Many of Greece's beaches are beautiful, but this one was chosen to participate in the ranking of the best in Europe. Few people know about this place. Egremni is white coarse soft sand and turquoise water. You can go down to the beach along 347 steps lying on a cliff 150 meters high. Due to its remoteness and relatively difficult descent along a steep staircase, one gets the feeling that nature allowed Egremni to select vacationers for himself.

Myrtos (Kefalonia)



Not so long ago, having analyzed all the beaches of Greece, the Ministry of Tourism and Culture of Greece recognized Myrtos as the most beautiful of them. This place is located on the west coast of Kefalonia. A well-groomed coast, stunning landscape, turquoise water - all this attracts lovers of beauty from around the world.

Plaka (Naxos)

It turns out that not all the beaches of Greece have opened to Russian tourists. Such, for example, as Sarakiniko (Peloponnese) and Plaka on the island of Naxoso, are one of the most beloved among the Greeks. The second is three miles long. The coastline is not processed, so here you can feel yourself in the role of Robinson Crusoe. Virgin beauty, lots of places for secluded relaxation, an excellent bottom and an endless sand strip - what else is needed for a good holiday?
