Rating of narrow washing machines: sizes, specifications, reliability, reviews

Today, owners of small apartments are faced with the problem of the inability to install a standard size washing machine in the bathroom or even in the kitchen . In this case, most of the world famous manufacturers offer to purchase special narrow models. Such varieties allow installation to be carried out even in a room with limited dimensions. The rating of narrow washing machines will be discussed later.

General characteristics

Before considering the rating of the best narrow washing machines, you should pay attention to important aspects of the choice of household appliances. It is customary to attribute models to this group of equipment that differ from standard ones in less depth. In this case, the width of the machine is usually standard.

In other words, models that also have a frontal glass sunroof fall into the category of narrow ones, but there is less camera for loading things into them. It is 40-42 cm. In standard machines, the depth is 44-47 cm. The main parameters of this technique follow from this. Narrow models are characterized by smaller:

  • spin speed;
  • the weight of things at maximum load;
  • power;
  • electricity consumption.

Visually, the presented models have no other changes. When choosing, it is worth considering that a different category of equipment is on sale today. These are supercompact washing machines. Their depth can vary from 29 to 36 cm.

Selection recommendations

The rating of narrow washing machines should definitely be considered before going to the store. However, there are a number of parameters that need attention. Narrow cars can be of two types:

  • Standard. The system provides a minimum number of functions, basic functionality.
  • Extended functionality. There are many functions, some of them are indispensable. This, for example, can be drying, steaming, washing down jackets, woolen or silk items, as well as antiseptic programs.

The main parameters that you should pay attention to are the tank depth, the spin and energy class, the washing quality and the spin speed.

You should definitely get acquainted not only with the reliability rating of narrow washing machines, but also with consumer reviews. This will allow you to choose the best option. Reliable household appliances will be durable, functional and will not require periodic repairs.

Going to the store, you need to measure the dimensions of the free space where you plan to install the machine. For each measurement, it is worth making a margin of 1-2 cm. It should be borne in mind that all narrow models are built-in. They have a removable lid, which allows you to freely install the unit in the kitchen, under the sink.
