Communism off the coast of America: time difference with Cuba

Cuba has always seemed to us, the former Soviet citizens, something incredible. Any tourist at the mere mention of Cuba sees sunny beaches, fogged bottles of rum and fragrant cigars. But is it only necessary to know, going to the famous Island of Freedom?

Cuban voyage

Holidays on a beautiful sunny island can be the best memory of a tourist who will warm him with long winter evenings and delight him in the midst of the daily bustle of a working day. Definitely, Cuba is worth a visit.

But before any trip, it is necessary to collect brief information about the country to which you are buying a tour. Ignorance of some simple things can significantly complicate the trip. For example, the banal time difference with Cuba can cause serious ailments for an unprepared traveler. A preliminary study of the list of things allowed for export from the country will save you from problems at customs.

How to get to Liberty Island?

Moscow and Cuba are separated by more than nine thousand kilometers. This fact means that the flight to Havana, which cordially accepts all flights from Russia, will not be very fast. But due to the fact that there is a time difference with Cuba, the journey does not seem endless. On average, a direct flight delivers a tourist to the snow-white Cuban beaches in twelve hours. And this is the shortest route.

In other cases, a connecting flight will take about twenty hours. Not everyone can withstand such a trip, so Cuba has not yet become a place of mass pilgrimage for tourists from Russia.

Cuba: time difference with Moscow

December and January is the time when tourists flock to Cuban land. And although the time difference with Cuba is significant in most countries, this does not prevent travelers from dreaming of a vacation on the sunny island coast.

If you fly to Liberty Island from Moscow, then get ready for a temporary difference of eight hours. When night is in Cuba, morning is already approaching in Moscow. Such a serious malfunction in the biological clock can cause headaches, increased fatigue and decreased immunity. As a result, the long-awaited vacation will go down the drain. But this is not what people are going on for such a long journey.





So that the time difference with Cuba does not become a cause of poor health, try to be prepared to change the biological clock. To do this, it is worth bringing the hours of awakening and going to bed to Cuban time in advance. Also, on the first day of rest, it is necessary to categorically exclude the intake of alcohol and various power engineers. Compliance with these basic rules will make the Cuban voyage the most beautiful and amazing adventure in your life.
