Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Marmaris): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Marmaris is an incredibly picturesque area in Turkey, where tourists can attract ancient sights that have been preserved since ancient times, as well as beautiful surroundings. Local hotels also offer incredible living conditions that go well with the low prices of travel packages. One of such hotels is Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 *.

general information

This hotel was built in 1999, as well as many other types of holiday destinations in the area. On its relatively small territory, almost every year, at a time when there are no tourists, repairs are underway aimed at maintaining the general condition of the building and the square on which it is located. One of the last large-scale renovations was made in 2015, during which the appearance of the case was updated, making it more modern.

Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 * is perfect for tourists of different categories. As the guests note in their reviews, it has conditions for children, which makes the rest of the whole family more comfortable; the company of friends feels great here, and for young couples in love there are numbers in which they can feel complete privacy and fully enjoy each other.



Location

The hotel is located almost in the center of Marmaris, which is located on the southwest coast of exotic Turkey. Here, almost all year round, real summer reigns, for which high air temperatures are characteristic, significantly mitigated by the influence of the air masses of the Mediterranean. The center of Marmaris is only a five-minute drive from the hotel - this is where the center of all entertainment is located, where there are a huge number of night clubs and other entertainment venues. At 850 meters from Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) is Long Beach - a favorite place for tourists, where there is not only a beautiful beach, but also many cafes, restaurants, bars, karaoke and clubs.

As for transport hubs, the nearest international airport is located in the city of Dalaman, the distance to which is 90 kilometers - it is easy to overcome it by car, for which the hotel provides a transfer service, and its payment is mandatory included in the price of the ticket.



Rooms

In total, the hotel’s fund has 155 apartments that offer excellent living conditions and, depending on them, are divided into three categories: Family, Suit and Standard. Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) has air conditioning in all rooms, thanks to which guests can create suitable temperature conditions, regardless of the weather outside.

The rooms also have private bathrooms, fully equipped with plumbing and additional furniture in the form of cabinets and decor - mirrors. In the cabinets there are sets of bath accessories, the number of which is equal to the number of guests, as well as bathrobes, slippers and bath towels.



Standard

The Standard apartments in Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 *, located on a total area of ​​40 square meters. m, there is a rather spacious room, which is designed for a couple of guests. For them, there is one large European-style bed. If necessary, the possible number of guests in the room can be increased by another two people - a couple of beds in this case will be provided on a very comfortable folding sofa.

The room also has three cabinets, two of which are bedside, and one is a place for storing clothes, which simultaneously serves as a surface for installing a TV. The apartments have a tea substation, with which it seems possible to prepare hot drinks. Experienced guests recommend drinking them on the furnished balcony, which is available in the room - from them, according to them, offers stunning views of the surroundings of Marmaris.



Family

Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 * has a small number of rooms designed to accommodate tourists with entire families. Here, according to vacationers, it’s quite comfortable to live with children of any age. According to the rules of the hotel, if necessary, parents can request a cot in the room if a child is available - it is provided completely free of charge.

The rooms Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 * Family, located on an area of ​​60 square meters. m, there is a division into two rooms. Each of them has the same furniture set, which without fail includes two berths (single or double beds), a pair of nightstands, a clothes hanger or a small wardrobe, as well as a TV connected to the satellite broadcasting system. If desired, a minibar can be installed in the room, which is filled with cool water every day, as well as a safe. The use of such items is paid. Some guests prefer to use a telephone that has access to an international communication line - calls are also paid.



Suit

Apartments of this class offer vacationers the conditions for a superior stay, however they are presented on a very small area - 30 sq. M. m. Often these numbers are chosen by tourists who came to rest together, together with a loved one. Their interior, according to vacationers, creates an atmosphere of romance and intimacy, ideal, for example, for a honeymoon.

There is only one room in which there is a large double bed. On the positive side, guests note the presence in the apartment of a large number of dense textiles, which gives a certain sophistication to the overall picture of the interior. Guests staying here can use the tea substation for making coffee, a telephone, a minibar, and, if necessary, a safe. There is a large plasma TV with the ability to broadcast Russian-language channels.



Conditions for children

In the reviews of Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), tourists very often mention that the hotel has created excellent conditions so that travelers can relax here with their families, which include small children. Parents note with positive comments that there is the possibility of free cots for guests aged 4 to 12 years, and this is done completely free of charge. They also recommend babysitting services, which are provided here hourly and for a fee.

As for the conditions provided in the open area, among them there is a small-sized pool with a shallow depth, equipped with all the safety requirements for children, and there is also a large playground where kids can play or just spend their time actively.

Nutrition

Fans of delicious food often respond positively to Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 *. According to them, it is here that you can eat very tasty, and in quite pleasant conditions.

Every day, in the large hall of the main restaurant for tourists living on the all-inclusive concept, a buffet is set up here. It presents only the most exquisite dishes, traditional for European and Turkish cuisines. If desired, guests can request a children's and diet menu, which they also regard as a positive circumstance - this is reflected in the reviews of Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 *.



Spa and sports

The spa area attracts the attention of fans of relaxation. It is here, according to vacationers, that real massage masters work who are able to immerse the client in a state of complete relaxation. In addition to massage, they can offer a huge range of cosmetic procedures, which, according to tourists, have an amazing effect.

If desired, guests can always visit the sauna and hammam, which are part of a beautiful thermal complex. Most tourists who have been here recommend using the services of parmesisters, who by their work will certainly make the soaring process more comfortable.

As for sports guests, for them, the Cosmopolitan Resort Hotel 4 * has a medium-sized gym, in which everyone can absolutely freely exercise their figure and monitor their health. In addition to the sports hall, there are several playgrounds for street games, as well as a tennis court.

Inside the main building of the hotel there are several rooms in which fans of billiards and table tennis gather.

Beach

Many comments say that the hotel is quite far from the beach line. According to many, this is not considered the best factor. However, at the same time, tourists are pleased that it is a five-minute walk from the most luxurious Marmaris beach - Long Beach. It is here that you can not only relax, but also have fun, because throughout its territory there are a large number of nightclubs, karaoke, restaurants and other institutions in which travelers are very fond of having fun.

In the comments about the rest, tourists recommend using the services of a comfortable minibus, which delivers all tourists to the beach, and for free. In addition, each guest who goes there is invited to use a bath towel for temporary use. On the beach, everyone can rent a suitable deck chair and umbrella.

Along the coastline there is a huge amount of entertainment, among which there are many sports. Here, subject to payment for the services of an instructor, you can master the skills of surfing, water skiing, scooter, as well as learn diving. According to the guests, the instructors here are quite professional, who are attentive to each client.

Here, according to tourists, it is very convenient to relax with children. They talk about the fact that the coast in this area is rather gentle, and the descent into the sea is gradual. In addition, tourists are pleased with the temperature of the water, which is kept here throughout the resort period - it is optimal for bathing kids.
