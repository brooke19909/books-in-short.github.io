The most beautiful places in Italy: overview, description and interesting facts

One of the most popular countries in the world, famous for its cultural, historical, natural treasures, delicious cuisine and excellent shopping, is a must see. To tell about the most beautiful places in Italy is not an easy task, because there is an incredible number of miraculous and man-made sights, the memories of which remain in the soul forever.

A country that gazes

The fabulous peninsula, rightfully considered a real open-air museum, preserves the treasures of the great Roman Empire, the heyday of the Renaissance, the dark times of the Holy Inquisition. The homeland of the greatest thinkers, artists and sculptors, each corner of which represents the exquisite luxury of wonderful landscapes, will surprise even the most sophisticated tourists. A country that delights the eyes, fascinates and falls in love at first sight.

Paradise Valley Valle d'Aosta

The Aosta Valley, which attracts tourists, boasts a comfortable climate and a favorable geographical position. Located in the Alps, in the north of the country, it is included in the list of the most beautiful places in Italy. Not only outdoor enthusiasts who love winter sports, but also connoisseurs of luxurious natural landscapes hurry to the ski resort with hot thermal springs.



In a paradise town, protected by the state, there are about 120 ancient knight's castles, amazing power and grandeur. Many architectural monuments are open to visitors who want to plunge into the Middle Ages.

Picturesque Lake Lago di Como

One of the most beautiful places in Northern Italy is Lake Como, on the banks of which small, but picturesque towns and villages have grown. The deep and huge pond has long attracted the attention of creative people and travelers from different countries. The natural pearl, located in Lombardy, is an amazing combination of azure water, in which the reflecting sun's rays play merrily, and pacifying mountain landscapes, immersing the atmosphere of bliss. It seems that the blue sky, which fell so low, can be reached with your hands.



The beautiful lake, sheltered on all sides by the peaks of the Alps, is protected from the cold northern winds and welcomes guests all year round. The miraculous monument, which has become a favorite vacation spot for Europeans, has been actively built up to attract even more travelers. A delightful corner, where every visitor takes a camera, invites everyone who wants to improve their health, because the wonderful climate, fresh air and the healing power of water truly work wonders.

Grotte di Frasassi - Underworld

Continuing the conversation about the most beautiful places in Italy, one cannot ignore another natural miracle - the mysterious grottoes of Thracassi. A geological monument, which consists of several karst caves, in which water sculpted bizarre sculptures, tourists call a fantastic corner of a sunny country. Entering the underground kingdom on the Apennines, opened only 46 years ago, amazed guests find themselves in a completely different world, where it seems that space is changing.



Fairy-tale caves, spanning three kilometers, include unlike grottoes with giant stalactites and stalagmites, whose age exceeds 150 thousand years. An unusual labyrinth, striking the imagination, abounds with limestone formations of amazing colors: from pale pink to dazzling blue. A bright natural attraction has made known to the whole world an area called Ancona.

Multicolored Manarola

You can talk about the amazing beauties of colorful Venice, charming Milan, vibrant Naples, majestic Rome for a very long time, but there are also not so famous cities, from whose breathtaking views it freezes. The tourist map of the country includes not only popular, but also unfamiliar to most vacationers settlements, opening a completely different Italy, devoid of artificial gloss. Unfortunately, few people know about them, and it is necessary to correct this annoying oversight. One of these cities is a miniature Manarola with unique architecture.



Included in the list of the most beautiful places in Italy, it can be called one of the most photogenic. The fishing village in the north of the country, in the province of La Spezia, admires the colorful, like a toy, houses built directly on the slopes of the rocks. An old city buried in verdure on the shores of the Ligurian Sea takes you to a real fairy tale in which you want to stay forever. Famous for winemaking and fishing, he catches the eyes of visitors with bright houses painted in all colors of the rainbow.

Medieval Sant`Agata de`Goti

Tourists who want to explore the most beautiful places in Italy, go to a small town, comfortably located on the steep bank of the Martorano River, in the region of Campania. The original village, dotted with houses that were built during the Middle Ages, admires the fantastic scenery. Cute dwellings growing out of the rocks merge with them in a single color scheme. Similar to swallow's nests, they are the hallmark of the provincial Sant'Agata de Gothi, surrounded by dense greenery.

A special, very intimate atmosphere reigns here, and many ancient sights add a certain charm to the city. As tourists admit, this corner, in which a leisurely life flows, is as if carved from the distant past.

Cave City Matera

One of the most beautiful places in Italy is located in the south of the country, in the Basilicata region. An unusual city, the historical part of which is houses, hollowed out in a cave, since 1993, is protected by UNESCO. Dwellings (sassi) with cut windows, doorways and roofs that became floors are located on the steep wall of the famous Canyon Gravina. Interconnected by winding staircases, acting as narrow streets, they remained inhabited until the end of the 20th century.



Now you can admire the very modest cave houses, where whole families huddled in unsanitary conditions that are not very comfortable. The ancient streets of Matera often become decorations for biblical-themed films. In tuff, underground churches with wall paintings that have not lost their brightness are also cut down. A walk through the city inhabited from the Neolithic era, nicknamed the "second Bethlehem", is an exciting journey into past centuries.

Unforgettable holidays in Liguria

Tourists who are interested in the most beautiful places on the coast of Italy are sure to visit Liguria - a small region of the country, whose beautiful beaches will not leave anyone indifferent. The unique geographical location, mild climate and warm sea made it the most popular corner among foreign guests.

Romantic San Remo, famous for its wonderful architecture, the ancient city of Alassio, which has turned into a famous resort, combining modernity and medieval monuments of Rapallo, perched on the cliff of Vernazza, attract millions of guests all year round. However, many tourists unanimously call the fabulously beautiful Portofino a true paradise, whose population in the high season increases several times.

Portofino - Dolce Vita style city

The miniature city, awarded special prizes for clean beaches and clear sea water, was founded by the Roman emperor. The former fishing village quickly turned into a prestigious resort, considered the most beautiful holiday destination in Italy. Hollywood stars and famous musicians buy real estate, the value of which is growing every year, and it is not for nothing that local residents joke that one window in the city costs a million euros.



The respectable Portofino with picturesque landscapes and an aristocratic atmosphere is ideal for tourists who dream of a relaxing vacation on the beach and at the same time longing to immerse themselves in a rich social life. A quiet city, where there is harmony between man and the environment, is so perfect that it seems unrealistic. Leaving a vivid impression and devoid of noisy nightclubs, it was created for a relaxing holiday. Any construction is prohibited here, and therefore Portofino looks the same as in the 19th century.

Praiano - the most beautiful place in Italy on the sea

The little-known pearl of the Amalfi coast, located on a steep cliff facing the sea, seems to be divorced from civilization. In Praiano, founded in ancient times, a serene and measured life reigns, which will appeal to tourists tired of the noise of megacities. In a small town where about two thousand people live, vacationers come, dreaming of loneliness and unity with mother nature. The hallmark of the miniature resort are cute houses carved in stone.



This is a rather "wild" place, due to the mountains and two undeveloped beaches. The sensation of swimming, according to guests, is simply crazy: from the sea, gigantic rocks look awesome. Here they enjoy the fresh air, in which the scents of pine are floating, and the unusual silence, the calming sound of the sea and stunning panoramas.

A country that opens in a new way

Unfortunately, the most beautiful cities and the best places in Italy cannot be described in the article and not seen in one trip to the country of olive oil and delicious sparkling wine. The real experience of meeting her exceeds all expectations. Here, every guest who has traveled to a real fairy tale will find something that will leave a bright mark on his soul.

You can come here countless times, constantly discovering something new. A time-lost country, which is called the cradle of world civilization, takes millions of tourists in their arms, hurrying after new experiences and dreaming to see its most beautiful corners.
