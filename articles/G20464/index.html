Alanya: Hotel Banana - a paradise for relaxation

Going on vacation? Do not know where to go and how to spend your vacation? Choose the Turkish resort of Alanya. Hotel Banana is an excellent solution for the relaxation of those who wish to enjoy the sea, the sun and comfort.

Location

One of the chic and excellent resorts that Turkey has is Alanya. Hotel Banana is located on the seafront, just 2.5 km from the city center. The nearest airport is in the city of Antalya, which is only 140 kilometers away. The infrastructure near the hotel is well developed; there is a shopping center, a hospital, bars and restaurants.



Building and rooms

Hotel Banana (Alanya) is a seven-story building, built in 2004. There are 22 excellent studio rooms in the building with all the comfortable conditions, as much as possible here can accommodate two people. The suites, and there are 82 in the hotel, have a bedroom, a living room, and bathrooms. These apartments are designed for two to three people. In addition to the building, the Banana Hotel (Alanya) has two two-story cottages. They were built in the late sixties, but thanks to the restoration work carried out in 2008, the buildings and rooms are now in excellent condition and with a fashionable design. The apartments of the complex are equipped with air conditioning, safe, telephone, TV, minibar.





Nutrition

The Banana Hotel provides all-inclusive meals. Also on site are a restaurant with national Turkish cuisine and a restaurant serving European cuisine, where you can spend unforgettable evenings and enjoy delicious dishes.

Hotel Territory

Alanya is rich in picturesque nature and enchanting landscapes. Hotel "Banana" - a paradise, immersed in a riot of Mediterranean greenery. The entire territory of the holiday complex is beautiful and well maintained. It includes two fresh water pools and slides, clothing stores, jewelry and souvenir shops, a mini market, and a night club. For guests' convenience, laundry and dry cleaning services are provided. You can relax from the hot sun in the hotel in a cozy coffee house, and the night club will be a great place for entertainment for lovers of dancing and parties. An ideal place for families with children is the resort of Alanya, the Banana Hotel has everything for this condition. There are children's sections for the kids in the pool, there is a playground for games, a mini-club works where interesting animations and entertainment programs are held. If necessary, a cot will be added to the room, so this hotel will be comfortable for you and your baby.



Hotel beach

The best beach areas in Turkey are famous for Alanya. Hotel Banana has its own beach, which is only thirty meters away, you just have to go through the underpass across the road. The beach here is covered with large pebbles, vacationers can take advantage of free sun loungers, umbrellas, towels.

Services and entertainment

The Banana Hotel has all the conditions for outdoor activities; there are volleyball and table tennis courts on the territory, and aerobics classes are free of charge. Services offered include a hairdresser, massage, Turkish bath, billiards and computer games.
