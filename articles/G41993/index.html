What you need to start knitting

Before starting knitting, you need to know general concepts. It is not necessary to study textbooks or books on knitting, in which everything is very difficult to describe. Often it is the complexity of the process description that repels novice craftswomen.

In order to knit a female pullover with knitting needles and needles are required. If you want to knit a sweater warm and thick, then appropriate threads will be required. For knitting an elegant openwork pullover, the necessary thin threads.

Yarn calculation

Perhaps, for some, the calculation process will seem complicated, but this is far from the case. The simplest calculation that many knitters use is based on the length of the thread. This indicator is indicated on each glomerulus. For example, in order to knit a female pullover with knitting needles of average size 46, you will need a kilometer of yarn. That is, if it is indicated on the skein that it is 250 meters, then 4 such skeins will be required. To increase the size or depending on the characteristics of the model, one additional skein of yarn is added. The same goes for decrease. For example, if the model involves a short sleeve, then you can buy yarn one skein less.

And you can do the tricky thing in the calculation: take your sweater, which used similar threads, and weigh it on the scales. The resulting weight is the required amount of yarn. It is better to stock up on an extra ball when buying, because the colors in batches may vary, and if there is not enough yarn, the new ball may differ in shade.

Knitting needles

Knitting needles are selected depending on the yarn. Usually, on glomeruli, manufacturers indicate which number should be taken in order to use this yarn. If you take the knitting needles of the smallest indicated number, then the knitting will turn out more dense. If you use large knitting needles, you will get a more friable and elastic fabric.

Many knitters use this technique: you need to put a piece of yarn and a knitting needle on the table. Their thickness should approximately coincide.





Sample

Before knitting a female pullover with knitting needles, you will need to knit a sample. Tying the sample is necessary to calculate the required number of loops and evaluate the behavior of the yarn after washing. A set of an arbitrary number of loops is made on the knitting needles. Approximately, the width of the sample should be at least 10 cm, so that it is convenient to calculate how many loops in this width are contained. Knit a sample you need the same viscous, which is planned to knit the future pullover. If the front surface is used , then the sample is knitted with it. If openwork or arana will be used, then it is enough to use one pattern repeat in the sample. After receiving the square-shaped sample, measurements are taken. The number of loops contained in 10 cm of knitting is calculated, excluding edge loops. Next, the sample is washed and dried. After drying, calculations and measurements are performed again. This is necessary in order to find out if the yarn does not sit after washing, otherwise after the finished product is tied and washed, it may not be enough for its owner.

Now you need to take your measurements and determine the size. After this, the manufacture of a pattern or its schematic representation will be required. You can find a pullover pattern in the magazine. Now you can start knitting.

Product Finishing

Finishing is very important when knitting, because the final result depends on it. The product, when properly selected, is neat and tidy. The quality of its implementation depends on the time that the product will be operated.

Product seams can be joined with a needle or crochet. Needle connection involves laying the seam "back with a needle" along the edges of the product. And you can make hook connections by knitting a seam from half columns along the edges of the connected parts. You will get a neat pigtail that will provide a reliable connection of parts. Ponytails remaining after a set of loops are crocheted into the seams of the product.





After knitting is completed, the product must be moistened and laid on a flat surface for complete drying. As a rule, after washing, the loops straighten and become more even and accurate. This is especially noticeable on smooth plain yarn. Please note that yarn made from natural fibers can sit if it is washed in too hot water. Usually, yarn producers point to the clew of the particular care of it. It is best to wash knitted items with hands in warm, soapy water.

Finished knitted product can be decorated at will. For example, beads, beads, rhinestones look very impressive. You can patch decorative elements around the edges, collar or cuffs. Whatever decor was chosen, independent knitting implies complete freedom of choice, since this process is creative. The main thing is not to overdo it with decorations so that the pullover does not look like a Christmas tree.
