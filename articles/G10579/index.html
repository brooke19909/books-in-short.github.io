Game evolution





From sunny India, chess over time began to be imported into other countries. So, the Chinese called chess "xiangqi", the Japanese - "shogi", the people of Thailand - "makruk". Only in Persia did the current name of chess start. The Arabs called their ruler a shah, which is why the chess king was also called that.

The rules and names changed, the evolution of chess took place. They abandoned the dice, and the number of players decreased to two people. The color of the figures has become traditionally black and white. The name of the chess pieces remains unchanged. Some of the chess pieces have changed their name. So, Raja became the Shah. Since there were two kings, it was customary to weaken one of them and make a queen. The Persians also introduced the final result of the game - checkmate to the king. In the Persian language, the word chess means "the shah is dead."

The game went a long way until it reached Russia. Chess did not come to us from Europe. It is believed that Tajiks brought chess to Russia in the 9th century BC. That is why the name of the figures in chess are literally translated from Arabic and Persian. And already in the XI century, the rules of the game of chess came to Russia.

Chess set
