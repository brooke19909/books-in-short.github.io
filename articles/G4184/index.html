Bases and hotels of Gorny Altai

Mountain Altai among the popular holiday destinations in Russia is second only to the Black Sea coast. The beauty of pearl lakes, green hills, picturesque rivers with waterfalls, caves and gorges, lost in the rocks, intoxicates with its beauty. Anyone who once visited the "Siberian Alps" will never forget them. Then this place beckons and calls to itself, beautiful memories haunt us, and again we begin to choose one of the comfortable hotels in the Altai Mountains.

Determine the area

In the first place is Maima. It is in this area that places are concentrated that are in demand among couples and youth. This direction is distinguished by massive flows of tourists, it is conveniently located in the north-west of Gorny Altai and is the first on the way from Biysk. There is a warm lake Aya, and on the border of the area there are amazing Tavdinsky caves. A visit to them does not require special training and equipment, they are fully equipped for sightseeing shows.





In order to see the picturesque Kamyshlinsky waterfall, tourists rush to the Shebalinsky district. On its territory there is a natural monument "Clean meadow", as well as the only bison nursery outside the Urals.

The Chemal district is a little further, it stretches along the left bank of the Katun for 3000 km. People come here to see the beautiful hydroelectric power station, located among the cedar forests growing among the rocks and mountain rivers. A large number of hotels in the Altai Mountains is located near the village of Katun.

Many tourists come here "savages" in their own cars, stop in the summer in a bungalow that can be rented from private owners. Practice shows that such a vacation is even more expensive due to unbalanced food in different roadside cafes and restaurants. In addition, it is not quite comfortable, especially when traveling with a small child.

Rating of the best hotels in Gorny Altai

On one of the most popular booking sites, top hotels, recreation centers and other tourist complexes of this region were compiled based on almost 5,000 tourist reviews. The first place was the modern eco-hotel "Altika" in the Ust-Munsky district near the village of Manzherok with the lake of the same name. The complex is very quiet and calm, designed for family vacations.

Panoramic windows offer unforgettable views of Katun, the main water river in the region. Its main advantages are a wide range of wellness services in the SPA complex (for example, phytobarrel and pantotherapy), comfortable rooms from environmentally friendly materials and a large swimming pool. By the way, wellness treatments (baths, saunas, pools, antlers and aromatherapy) are offered in many hotels in the Altai Mountains.





Tourists praise the Edelweiss complex near the beautiful village of Artybash. It is often visited with children who like a spacious green area in a secluded place and a wonderful zoo.

Most hotels in Gorny Altai have a high level of service, an excellent location near various natural attractions and friendly staff.

"Solar" meets tourists all year round!

This complex near the village of Katun is in demand not only among travelers who choose outdoor activities, but also among business tourists. The hotel "Solnechnaya" in the Altai Mountains often hosts corporate events and meetings. For these purposes, a spacious conference room is equipped with modern furniture and appliances, which are necessary for holding trainings, seminars, team buildings, etc.





The three-story complex has comfortable rooms with excellent repair, comfortable beds, LCD TVs, Wi-Fi, and bathrooms. Guests can use the sauna, and in the evening, friendly companies gather in cozy gazebos to fry the barbecue in the fresh air and have a good time.

"Vega" - beach holidays and comfortable accommodation

The b / o is located near Lake Aya, on a well-maintained beach which you can sunbathe, and in warm and clear water, children are allowed to frolic all day. The picturesque shores of Katun are just 100 meters from the comfortable four-story building of the Vega Hotel (Altai Mountains).





Vacationers can comfortably settle in good rooms, taste delicious dishes in the dining-restaurant. Horse riding in the surroundings of the town is very popular here, the hotel staff will help organize rafting in Katun or go on any exciting excursion.

"Yartsev's estate" - rest for every taste!

Among hotels in Chemal (Altai Mountains), this b / o is very popular among tourists. It is favorably distinguished by a very beautiful area with bright wooden houses, ennobled paths, around which there are many flower beds, trees and neatly trimmed shrubs.





It offers comfortable rooms with a modern interior. Complete meals are offered in the wonderful restaurant. Children have a great time on the playground with a trampoline, swimming in the pool. Near the hotel there is a water park. Vacationers are delighted with the magnificent baths of the complex. There are a lot of equipment for hire (boats, boats, catamarans, quads). The staff offers a wide variety of excursion routes.

"Mountain Tale" - a paradise for children and adults

This complex is called the best in terms of price and quality among the bases and hotels of the Altai Mountains. The b / c specializes in family vacations with children, so the rooms are very carefully decorated, the food is balanced, leisure is thought out.





Reasonable pricing policy of the hotel "Mountain Fairy Tale" and a wide range of services provide excellent results: every year the number of guests is growing steadily. On the beautiful territory there is a small water park, in which both large and small children are happy to rest.

Setting priorities

This is the main criterion for choosing a hotel. First of all, you need to decide what you need most. A lot of b / o is located on the right and left banks of the Katun, away from the hustle and bustle of major highways, in an ecologically clean place. If you plan hiking or more extreme hiking, it is worth staying in remote alpine regions.

Beach holidays are good near the lakes Aya, Teletskoye. It is impossible to swim in Katun: the river is mountainous and very cold, the temperature does not rise above 12-14 ¬∞ C even in the hottest months. But grayling, taimen, Siberian char, burbot, etc. are well caught in the river. Try to ensure that within walking distance there is what you want from the rest.

The choice of accommodation is a very responsible task, because the quality of rest depends on proper sleep and nutrition. Hotels on the banks of the turquoise Katun or Lake Teletskoye, the village of Manzherok, Chemal and in many other picturesque places of the Altai Mountains will give you an unforgettable vacation that will be remembered for a lifetime.
