Suspension of construction

The construction of the building stopped in 1992, although its launch was planned in 1989. This global world project was to open for the 13th World Festival of Youth and Students.

The construction was discontinued due to problems associated with a lack of financial resources, as well as a lack of building materials, many of which were simply substandard.

For the construction of the hotel, it was planned to spend about $ 300 million of investments after it became clear that the construction would have to be frozen.

However, while this issue was in limbo, a more familiar structure was built on the banks of the Tendongan River, which, of course, was incomparable with the planned project, and its construction took much less cost.



In addition, there were two more reasons as a result of which the completion of the construction was doomed to failure - famine widespread throughout the DPRK and insufficient electricity. Unfortunately, all the efforts, time and money spent on the construction by architects and initiators, led only to a shattered dream.

Thus, only the main part of this legendary building was built, but the windows, communications and all necessary equipment were not brought to a victorious outcome and installed.
