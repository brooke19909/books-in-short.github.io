Which processor is better for a smartphone: a review of models and reviews

The processor is the heart not only for computers, but also for smartphones. This small chip should have high performance, support all the latest applications. There are many models on the market, but which processor is better for a smartphone to choose? What to look at? When buying a mobile device, you need to consider several criteria.

Number of cores





Here, each user should focus only on their requests. So, there are people who do not need to work with a smartphone in a huge number of cores. For example, some very good processor models for mobile devices with a small number of cores show quite good performance when working with many applications. Here you can highlight the device company Apple, which in the design of phones uses its own development. Their devices with dual-core processors work flawlessly with applications based on their operating system.

Of course, the development of modern technology does not stand still. Many manufacturers equip their smartphone models with processors with 8 cores. Here you can get confused among these very cores. The question may arise: so which is the best processor for a smartphone to choose?

Kernels and Opportunities

With the number of cores, everything is quite easy. Previously, all manufacturers of devices for computers pursued an increase in frequency, and all commands on devices were executed one after another. Then the time came when the commands began to be executed in parallel. The frequency of the processor is far from unlimited, and each core has a specific configuration that allows you to perform several functions at the same time, that is, in parallel. The best models are capable of performing several million different operations at the same time. And all this became possible due to the presence of not one, but several cores of the processor. Previously, data streams occupied a queue for their processing, which greatly affected the speed of the device, but now there is no such problem.

Frequency

Even now, many users think that the most important parameter is the processor frequency. Which is better? For a smartphone, this feature is, of course, important. But as already noted, thanks to the parallel execution of commands today, it has shifted to a secondary plan. It is more important to purchase a smartphone with a large number of processor cores, which can provide comfort in the process of working with the device. But we can recommend choosing those devices on which the frequency is not lower than 1 gigahertz. And the kernel will take care of the rest.

What is the best processor for a smartphone? In general, the mobile version of this device has not so many differences from the older computer "brother". The main differences are size, power and clock speed. And today, some models of mobile chips are more powerful than computer ones.

Mobile Processor Manufacturers

Which processor manufacturer is best for a smartphone? Here you can make a kind of rating, which will lead Qualcomm. This market giant was founded back in 1985. At first, the company produced telephones, navigation modules, as well as wireless devices. And after it became one of the largest manufacturers of mobile processors. She licensed ARM technology and bought the rights to manufacture the A8 core, on the basis of which her own chip was created.

Qualcomm Processors





The top models are called Snapdragon, and they are installed only on flagship mobile devices. And if you ask which processor is better for a smartphone on Android, many will say that this is a chip from Qualcomm, since the performance here is impressive. The latest models are capable of supporting 4K video and have 8 cores. Working with devices based on them is a pleasure.

Mediatek Processors





Second in the ranking, you can put the Chinese company Mediatek. It is it that is distinguished when answering the question of which is the best processor for a smartphone. The company was founded in 1997 and was engaged in data storage modules and wireless technologies at that time.

This company takes not only the quality of the products, but also the quantity, since its processors are installed in all devices that are budget-friendly. It was Mediatek who developed the first ten-core processor, which is the world's most powerful single-chip chip. The performance of the model is amazing, since it is more powerful than many laptops.

Apple processors





When asked about what type of processor is best for a smartphone, fans of the "apple" technology will immediately answer that from Apple. The company has always been known for its isolation. Everything that they do is hidden from the eyes of strangers. It is known that processors for tablets and smartphones are developed internally. Models are assigned an index with a serial number. The latest models of devices are equipped with very powerful mobile processors. Despite the fact that they have only two cores and one coprocessor, the performance is still impressive.

Intel Mobile Processors





Intel has long been at the top of the ranking of mobile processors for laptops, but so far in the field of smartphones and tablets. But the fact that smartphones from Asus are equipped with processors just from Intel, suggests that the company is moving in the right direction. At the moment, the most productive chip has 4 cores with a frequency of 2.33 GHz and support for proprietary graphics. It will easily give odds to competitors from Apple and Qualcomm.

But still, on the whole, the line of mobile processors from this company is not impressive because of its high cost, not very high-quality support and the presence of all kinds of problems.

Nvidia Chips





The company Nvidia, which is known to everyone for its video cards for computer games, is in its main field an unconditional leader. With the mobile sphere, not everything is so smooth. Their Tegra processors have very good potential, but for some reason leading manufacturers are not in a hurry to buy them in large quantities. Undoubtedly, gamers will appreciate their hardware. But the majority of smartphone users are girls, youth and businessmen, who are unlikely to want to play a cool toy with ultra-high settings on their device. In addition, the company's processors are very expensive.

Reviews

What is the best processor for a smartphone? Any such rating will be controversial, since each chip from the previously listed is perfect for its duties.





So, processors from Nvidia were created specifically for games, and according to reviews, they simply have no equal in this area. Snapdragon is in demand among those users who want to receive high-quality images and a smart interface on their smartphone. Intel processors are praised for their very good cost-effectiveness. For each goal, its own processor is suitable. Well, if you want everything at once, then you need to choose the most expensive product on the market that will cope with all the tasks.
