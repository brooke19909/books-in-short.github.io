Hotel Eleonora Boutique Hotel (Greece, Hersonissos): overview, features, rooms and reviews of tourists

At one of the most popular resorts of Crete - Hersonissos, located next to Heraklion , the tourist infrastructure is developed, of course, just great. In any case, staying in hotels here is not too expensive. At the same time, the rooms themselves in local hotels for tourists are rented quite comfortable. For example, quite good reviews were earned by the Eleonora Boutique Hotel located in this city .

Where is the complex located

This hotel was built in the suburbs - in the village of Anissaras . And if Hersonissos itself is considered a youth resort, then this small village is mainly visited by family tourists and lovers of a quiet beach holiday. Located Eleonora Boutique Hotel in Anissaras near the sea. The coastline is 300 meters away.





Hersonissos itself is 1 km from the hotel. This hotel is located in a very quiet area. All that guests of the hotel can hear when they are in its territory or in the rooms is the bleating of sheep during the day and the singing of roosters in the morning. Domestic tourists arriving to Crete, having rented rooms at the Eleonora Boutique Hotel, usually to Heraklion Airport. From here, vacationers should first get to the very capital of Crete. Buses run from Heraklion to Hersonissos quite often.

Hotel Features

On the websites of tour operators, the Eleonora Boutique Hotel 3 * hotel (Crete, Greece, Heraklion) is usually marked as a three- or four-star hotel . However, this information, unfortunately, is not entirely correct. In fact, Eleonora Boutique is nothing more than a private mini-hotel. That is, this complex cannot have official stardom in principle.





Check-out time at this hotel is 14 hours. However, tourists at the hotel usually do not have to wait too long to prepare rooms for arrivals in the morning. Judging by the reviews, they settle in the rooms of vacationers here immediately upon arrival. This hotel is named for its mistress - Eleanor. The owner of the complex usually meets the guests arriving in person.

They accept residents in this hotel in one low-rise building. Around the hotel building is a picturesque garden. Here guests can take refuge in the shade during the day or take a walk in the evening. One of the features of this complex is that it creates conditions for recreation, including for people with disabilities. Children at the hotel can stay from any age.

What kind of housing is provided

In total, this hotel is equipped with 18 comfortable rooms. If you wish, tourists can rent standard, family or superior rooms in the Eleonora Boutique Hotel . The views from the windows of the hotel apartments can open onto the pool or sea.





All rooms rented at the hotel include:

  • air conditioners;
  • mini fridges;
  • safes.

Shower facilities include complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer.

Hotel Infrastructure

Despite the fact that the Eleonora Boutique Hotel 4 * is a small hotel , there are a lot of amenities on its territory for guests. For example, tenants can leave rented cars here in free parking. In addition, Wi-Fi is offered at the hotel. There is also no need to pay for this service.





At the request of tourists, the hotel can arrange an airport shuttle. Every week, the hotel hosts barbecue evenings at the hotel. There is also a small pool in this hotel. Next to it are sun loungers of a convenient design. Of course, guests can use sun loungers at the hotel for free.

Service Reviews

Of course, in terms of infrastructure development for 4 stars, this mini-hotel, according to tourists, hardly pulls. But still, according to vacationers, it is quite convenient to relax here. The owner of the hotel, Eleanor, is doing everything possible to create a friendly, homely atmosphere on its territory. The owner of the hotel always goes towards her guests and, within reason, fulfills all their wishes related to increasing the comfort of living.

Hotel pool vacationers are usually praised. According to travelers, it is very large for such a small hotel. The guests have no complaints about the administration of the complex regarding the cleanliness of the water in the pool. It is actually very pleasant to plunge into it on a hot day.

Dining at the hotel

Most often, hotel guests at Eleonora Boutique Hotel 3 * rent rooms here with breakfast. The dining room of the hotel has earned very good reviews from tourists. The food here is delicious and fresh. Regarding the variety of breakfasts, tenants also have no complaints about the Eleonora Boutique Hotel . In addition to eggs or omelet, in the morning guests can take cottage cheese, ham, cheese, tomatoes, muffins, donuts in the dining room. From drinks for breakfast, residents are offered juices, tea, coffee.





A fairly varied menu is provided at the hotel for dinner and lunch. Residents who rented rooms in the complex with breakfast only can order cooking in the afternoon or in the evening.

What is a hotel beach?

On the sites of tour operators it is mentioned that the sea is located 300 meters from the Eleonora Boutique Hotel 3 * (Crete) . In fact, this is indeed so. However, directly opposite the hotel there is no possibility to swim, unfortunately. Sharp stones prevent vacationers from entering the water here. The nearest equipped beach is about a kilometer from the complex. Most often, guests visit the beach, which belongs to the nearby Aldemar Hotel.

Cost of living
