Gee Money Bank: customer reviews



GM Money Bank, reviews of which can be found on forums of the corresponding subject, came to the Russian credit market almost immediately after the collapse of the Soviet Union. This financial institution is a division of the credit giant General Electric, founded in the 30s of the XX century. She was mainly engaged in providing consumer loans to individuals for the purchase of various types of household appliances.

Today, "Money Bank", the addresses of which can be found on the relevant information resources, is mainly engaged in issuing various credit cards to its customers . And although the way of lending has changed, the main direction of the financial organization has not changed. She is the same as the last 70 years, provides consumer loans.

GE Money Bank - basic services

Today, the main list of services provided by a financial institution includes the following operations:

  • issuing bank credit cards;
  • individual loans;
  • car loans;
  • mortgage credit lending;
  • cooperation with large retail chains.


GM Money Bank, which reviews, by the way, is not always flattering, offers its customers a wide range of loan programs. It can be both a car and real estate or securities. In principle, almost any material value can act as a guarantor of loan repayment by the client. In addition, JM Money Bank, whose expert reviews put it on a par with the largest Russian financial institutions, usually requires the borrower to take out insurance in case of delay in the loan program.
