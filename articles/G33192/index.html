Hotel Malin Patong Hotel (Thailand / Phuket): description, traveler reviews

Thailand is a stunning exotic country that attracts many tourists every year. Phuket is considered one of the most respectable resorts in the country, but there are hotels of various categories on the island. In our article we want to talk about one of the budgetary institutions - Malin Patong Hotel.

A bit about the hotel ...

Malin Patong Hotel is located on the island of Phuket, 37 kilometers from the international airport Phuket International Airoport, so tourists will not be at all difficult to get to it. The four-story hotel building is just an eight-minute walk from the famous Patong Beach. The hotel is a relatively young institution, since it was built in 2009 and has since been hosting tourists. The complex is located in a good area, near it there are numerous nightlife and shops.



Malin Patong Hotel attracts tourists primarily with its affordable prices and convenient location. The hotel provides guests with the most comfortable conditions for a carefree vacation.

The apartments are equipped with everything that guests may need for a comfortable stay. All rooms have a flat-screen TV, minibar, safe, wireless internet, air conditioning, DVD player, satellite channels, chairs, desk, wardrobe, bedside tables, balcony.

Bathrooms are equipped with towels, toiletries, towels, slippers. The rooms have a service, cleaning is done daily, and linen and towels are changed on schedule.



The windows of the hotel apartments have mountain or courtyard views.

Dining at the hotel

Malin Patong 3 * offers its guests only breakfast or accommodation without meals. The hotel has a bar, there is a restaurant offering tourists Asian, international and local dishes. Also, visitors can order delicious seafood and freshly squeezed juices at the restaurant.

A ten minute drive from Malin Patong 3 * on the sea coast is one of the oldest institutions in the city - Savoey Seafood. The restaurant was founded back in 1988. Here, visitors are offered delicious seafood - mussels, lobsters, tiger prawns, crabs, etc. The restaurant begins to receive guests from six in the evening. No need to book a seat. The average cost of dinner in this wonderful place is 1500-2000 baht.

Hotel infrastructure

Malin Patong (Phuket) is a small hotel in the center of Phuket, within walking distance of many cafes, restaurants, shops. Guests will certainly enjoy the original design of the hotel with a touch of local flavor. The institution offers round-the-clock service. An outdoor pool and jacuzzi, a spa tub are on site. It offers - wireless internet, parking. The reception service operates around the clock. The hotel does not accept tourists with animals.



In the complex you can always order a transfer, taxi, rent a car or rent a scooter, use the laundry services, a safe and a currency exchange office.

  1. Check-in is at 12.00.
  2. Departure - at 10.00.

One way transfer fee is 800 baht. An extra bed is available at an extra charge. The building has an elevator for the convenience of guests. The hotel staff speak several languages, they are always ready to assist guests in any matter, including booking tickets.

Sports and Entertainment

Malin Patong Hotel (ex. Mussee Patong Hotel) is equipped with an excellent pool with a jacuzzi on the roof of the building, as well as a comfortable relaxation area around it with umbrellas and sunbeds. Vacationers can enjoy water sports on the coast. The hotel has a tour desk, where everyone can choose suitable tours for themselves.

Beach

Malin Patong is a hotel located right on the coast. To get to the beach, just cross the road. Therefore, the complex is an excellent place for sea holidays. The public beach has sand cover and convenient entry into the sea. Fans of scuba diving expect a surprise in the form of a rich underwater world. The coast is equipped with paid umbrellas and sunbeds.

A little bit about the resort ...

Phuket is an amazing picturesque island where you can find beautiful places. Here everyone will be able to find for themselves what he was going here for. One is interested in beach vacations, the other hangouts and entertainments, sea sports and exotic attractions. The island has everything that tourists can only wish for.



There are many beaches in Phuket and each of them has its own differences. The best of them are located on the west coast of the island. The ideal option is to travel the entire coast and choose the best place for yourself.

Tourism on the island began to develop not so long ago, only fifty years ago. Over the years, the tropical island is very popular with tourists around the world. Phuket is not only accessible by plane. The island is connected to the mainland by two parallel bridges, one of which has long been pedestrian and rather serves as a symbol of the resort.

The main attractions ...

Phuket is a great place for diving. Along the coast of the island, dozens of diving centers, schools, equipment rental facilities have been built. In such institutions, trips for professionals are organized and beginners are trained.

In the south of the island is a statue of the Big Buddha, whose height is 45 meters. At its base, they plan to build a temple. You can get to it from the hotel by taxi or any other rented transport (the cost of a taxi is just over 500 baht). The road to the statue passes through huge hevea plantations (latex is produced from the juice of the plant). Big Buddha is located on a mountain whose height is more than 350 meters. The famous attraction is also an observation deck. It offers stunning views of the entire island.

For those vacationers who are not supporters of diving, but want to see the inhabitants of the sea element, an aquarium works. Of course, it cannot be called the largest aquarium, but a sufficient number of underwater inhabitants live in it. The oceanarium is divided into thematic sections, and its main pride is a multi-meter tunnel.



One of the favorite tourist destinations is the Sirinat National Park, which occupies about eight thousand hectares. On its territory there are three landscaped beaches, pristine rainforests and the sea.

The park has a water park "Splash of the jungle." The complex offers extreme slides, attractions, pools. For holidaymakers organize excursions to the mangroves. Hiking trails laid in the most beautiful places.

As a rule, women do not miss the opportunity to visit one of the local pearl farms, which grow beautiful jewelry in artificial conditions. One such place is Nakha Farm. You can get to it by boat, which in itself is an unusual adventure. And local guides talk in detail about the process of forming pearls. On the farm with your own eyes you can see all the stages of pearl making. At the end of the tour, tourists are offered to purchase their favorite jewelry.

One of the most impressive places of the whole island is the Phuket Botanical Garden. It was opened only in 2012. Currently, its area is about 10 thousand square meters. Its entire territory is decorated in the national Thai style, and is also decorated with all kinds of exhibits, decor elements, fountains and carvings created by skilled craftsmen. Guests can see unique palm trees, hundreds of plant varieties, dozens of different cacti, as well as stroll through fragrant gardens with beautiful lotuses on ponds and lilies.

Malin Patong: reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to discuss the reviews of those vacationers who managed to visit it. This complex, according to guests, can be recommended as an excellent budget hotel. Malin Patong Hotel has a very favorable location. Distance to the sea no more than 500 meters. Bangla Street, the main nightlife, is a twenty-minute walk from the hotel. It has a large number of shops, cafes, bars, restaurants, and in the evenings the street is even closed for vehicles.



Near the complex is located Patong Beach. Resort hotels are very diverse in terms of pricing, but finding a decent hotel with good rooms and low prices is not so simple. The hotel offers its guests good rooms with everything you need. Since the complex is away from the hectic nightlife, it is not as noisy as in other hotels. But still, sometimes extraneous sounds interfere with the guests. Not all tourists are satisfied that some apartments overlook the courtyard, or rather, their windows look at the facade of another hotel. But overall the rooms are nice and spacious, with normal furniture. There are no ants or insects in the apartment.

According to tourists, breakfast at the hotel is very scarce, and therefore it is not necessary to purchase tours in which they are included. Opposite the hotel is the Mali market, where you can buy food, things, and cheap meals very cheaply. All vacationers do this - they don‚Äôt go to cafes and restaurants, but go to the market, where there is a wide range of delicious dishes. There are no nutritional problems. You can also purchase lobsters and other freshest seafood on the market.

The hotel is very attentive and nice staff. Of course, no one speaks Russian, but English can be explained. Settlement is always fast. If tourists do not like the room, then the administration makes concessions and willingly changes the apartment. The rooms are cleaned daily and with high quality. Linen and towels also change regularly, sometimes even maids make beautiful figures on the beds. In general, the apartments are kept clean and tidy.



The hotel has a local guide offering interesting trips. Near the complex there is also a travel agency, in which a Russian girl works, she will advise you that it is worth visiting for beginners.

Instead of an afterword

Malin Patong Hotel is a good three star hotel that can be recommended to tourists. It is suitable for those who are planning a beach-sea vacation or an entertainment tour. The complex is conveniently located relative to the coast, but at the same time not far from the cultural center. Therefore, it is good not only for family vacations, but also for friendly companies.
