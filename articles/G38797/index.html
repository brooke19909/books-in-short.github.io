Headphones Plantronics: the best models

Plantronics is a well-known global brand that produces various audio equipment. The company was founded back in 1961 in the United States. A year later, she was already producing headsets for NASA pilots and astronauts. Known around the world in that the first person to set foot on the moon used Plantronics equipment.

Headphones of the American company are widespread among the civilian population. The company was able to enter the European market in 1986, having opened production in the UK. Plantronics is now the largest concern that manufactures headsets for devices and collaborates with many well-known brands.

Plantronics BackBeat PRO

Headphones Plantronics BackBeat Pro - a wireless solution for lovers of quality sound and style. Made in invoice form. That is, they fit snugly to the ears and head. To make the case, high-quality plastic is used. In contact with the ears - skin. The manufacturer endowed Plantronics BackBeat Pro headphones with an active noise reduction system. It does not allow extra sounds to distract from playing music.





The model received Bluetooth. But you can connect headphones to other devices through a cable. It seamlessly interfaces with devices that have a 4.0 version of the wireless interface. The working radius is 100 meters, which is an excellent indicator. The Plantronics BackBeat Pro microphone headphones allow you to make calls. A lithium-ion battery is installed, providing 24 hours of talk time.

Plantronics GameCom 388

Headphones Plantronics GameCom 388 designed for gamers. They have great sound and a swivel microphone. Made from quality materials. They are quite large but comfortable. The ear pads are wide, made of skin that is pleasant to the touch. Connect to a personal computer or other device via cable.

The design provided for a good microphone, which can be turned off if necessary. There is noise reduction for superior communication quality. On the head, Plantronics GameCom 388 headphones are fixed with a soft-trimmed bezel. The jack for connection is located on the left earphone and has the form of a 3.5 mm Jack. There is a built-in volume control.
