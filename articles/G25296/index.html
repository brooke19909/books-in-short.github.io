Winter hat for a newborn: comfortable and beautiful

The main advantage of a handmade hat is that you can choose the yarn you want according to the texture and color and knit the product to the right size. And the other positive side: exactly the same will not be with anyone.

Instruction manual

We take one hundred grams of yarn, knitting needles No. 3, a ribbon. To create a product such as a winter hat for a newborn, you need to dial 73 loops and knit two rows of facial stitch. Then we knit in this way: yarn, two loops together front. So we get holes for the satin ribbon. The next two rows are knitted with front stitch. Row: yarn, two loops together front. You need to continue with an openwork pattern. When the canvas is 12 centimeters high, you need to remove seventeen loops, evenly knitting two loops through the same number of loops. 56 stitches should remain on the knitting needles We knit two rows of smooth surface and add seventeen loops. Then four rows of front stitch and a row of loops we knit like this: yarn and two loops together front. Now again we knit two rows of front stitch. We close all the loops.

The final stage

Now you need to assemble and sew a hat. We turn off the edge along the second row of holes, we get cloves, they need to be fixed. Through the first row of holes we pass the ribbon, which will fulfill the function of ties. In the last row of holes, we also insert the tape and tighten, it turns out the bottom of the cap.

Hat with ears

Another winter hat for a newborn fits with ears. A cap with ear flaps is the best option, it will cover the whole head and ears. You can also easily make it yourself. Such a hat fits in fairly simple patterns: facial smoothness and garter stitch .



Work Stages

We take 100 grams of mohair / acrylic yarn, knitting needles No. 2. We knit 123 loops on the knitting needles and knit 28 rows with a garter stitch. Then 45 rows fit into the front surface. Now close the loops. We knit a strip knitted with a garter stitch in half and hem, this will be the edge. Now do the back seam. The upper edge of the cap is divided into about 4 parts and the ends of the parts are connected in the center. Sew from the center to the edge. It turned out 4 seams. We make two pompons and sew on the top of the head. Now do the ears. 13 loops should be retreated from the back seam and 23 loops should be drawn along the edge. We knit a garter stitch and in all rows we remove one loop on each side. When there are only three loops left, we knit a lace of the desired length without breaking the thread. In a similar way we knit an eye on the other hand.

Such a winter hat for a newborn will warm your child and will be a wonderful decoration for his head.
