Pharmacological abortion service

As you know, abortion is always a stress for the body of any woman. However, unlike abortion through surgery, taking medications that promote fetal rejection is considered a safer option. At the Aphrodite Clinic in Petrozavodsk, patients who decide to have an abortion are encouraged to use it with drugs.

Taking such medications does not imply anesthesia, as in the case of surgery. In addition, with pharmacological abortion, damage to the uterus by various instruments does not occur, the risk of infection and incomplete extraction of the fetus is minimized. The drugs that are used in this institution are modern, they do not upset the hormonal balance in the body. Clinic staff do not advocate abortion. They argue that before deciding on an abortion, every woman should think carefully about this step. However, if the patient nevertheless needs it, she is given the opportunity to terminate the pregnancy in the safest way. The cost of medical abortion in the clinic varies from 4,500 to 10,000 rubles. The price includes laboratory tests, consultations and examinations of the doctor, taking medications and ultrasounds.

The specialist also advises clients with psychosomatic problems, pregnant women and their relatives, as well as young mothers. Couples who have been diagnosed with problems with conception, disorders of sexual functions are turning to a psychologist. The specialist works with cancer patients and their family members. A person who suffers from difficulties in his personal life, problems with his relatives, work colleagues can also turn to a psychologist. The price for the services of this specialist varies from 700 to 1400 rubles.
