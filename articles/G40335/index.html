On the shore of the Gulf of Finland

A thirty-minute drive from St. Petersburg is the city of Sestroretsk. This is a resort with healing mud and mineral water, which is located on the shores of the Gulf of Finland.

It is not surprising that it was in Sestroretsk that the Aquaclub & Hotel Voda was built, representing a new type of recreation. Water rides for children and adults, SPA elements, restaurants and fashionable parties - the club concept is ideal for family or friendly holidays.

Hotel "Water"

Voda Aquaclub Hotel has a small number of rooms, which presents the categories of standard, de luxe and one superior room.

According to guests, the main advantage of the hotel is its unusual design. The minimalistic interior of the rooms is successfully complemented by backlit panels depicting fragments from comics. Artists and designers seem to provoke you to go beyond the usual things.



The guests from the bathrooms with rain showers and lights, which instead of walls are separated from the main space with just a glass partition, experience a slight shock. However, everyone gets used to the new environment pretty quickly.

Issue price

Holidays with accommodation in Voda Aquaclub Hotel will be very expensive. On weekends, you will pay 8,000 rubles for a standard double room, and 12,000 rubles for a de luxe room.

In addition to accommodation, the price includes:

- breakfast at the Mizu Dry restaurant;

- unlimited visits to Aquatrium;

- Wi-Fi;

- four bottles of drinking water;

- taxi ordering;

- parking space.

Upon request, a cot and a medical kit are available. Extra bed for an adult costs 4000 rubles, for a child (from 4 to 14 years old) - 3000 rubles.

The rooms have everything you need for a good rest: a large TV, air conditioning, a luxurious double bed, a hairdryer, a minibar and a small workplace.

Guest opinions

As we have said, many guests liked the creative design of their rooms. Transparent showers have long been remembered by couples in love, but guests with children experience some inconvenience.

High-quality bedding, comfortable beds and room equipment in general leave a pleasant impression.

The ambiguous opinion of the guests was formed regarding the work of the restaurant during breakfast. A small selection of food on the menu and just one serving of a hot drink (tea or coffee) - such restrictions do not correspond to the declared status of the Voda Hotel.

Voda Aquaclub Hotel is located in the immediate vicinity of the highway (700 meters from the Gulf of Finland), and the noise of the track sometimes causes inconvenience to vacationers. Travelers agree that you can live in a hotel no more than 2-3 days.

Aquatrium

Still want to know what a Voda aquaclub is? The basis of the entire complex is a huge pool (area - 600 sq. M) in a room with 12-meter ceilings. The feeling of a tropical beach is created due to comfortable air temperature (+31 degrees) and water (+30 degrees), and the musical accompaniment changes depending on the time of day.



For maximum relaxation, the pool is equipped with sixteen sun loungers with air massage. Hydromassage nozzles are also installed around the perimeter. An unusual experience promises vacationers a visit to a small grotto.

And finally, geysers. In the center of the pool above them are installed handrails with RGB-illumination, which give a kind of feeling of flying.

In the thermal zone for vacationers, a cozy Finnish sauna is equipped . However, much more interesting is the hammam, the design of which is also created using LED backlighting. Warm steam allows the lungs to breathe freely, opens the pores and saturates the body with moisture. The gentle temperature and unusual interior attract even the youngest guests of Aquaclub & Hotel Voda.

Thrill lovers will definitely appreciate the snow fountain for contrasting ice wipes after visiting two saunas.

Separately, it is worth noting the quality of the water in the pool. The bowl is made of stainless steel, this prevents the formation of rust and any organic compounds. In addition, the minimum amount of chlorine is used for water purification, which limits the negative impact on the body and ensures the absence of an unpleasant odor. Every month, the water is tested for quality: the Center for Hygiene and Epidemiology in St. Petersburg takes samples and, based on the examination, draws up a conclusion that we are ready to submit to you for review at any time.

Reviews

They come to the Voda Aquaclub Hotel in order to relax their body and soul. According to visitors, the incredible atmosphere created in the water complex is conducive to relaxation.



Large swimming pool, hydromassage, deck chairs, comfortable changing rooms and showers - all the details were worked out when designing the complex, so visitors come back here again.

The concept of the aquaclub is slightly different from traditional water parks, so some vacationers do not have enough noisy attractions.

The prices for single visits, according to reviews, are quite acceptable: an adult ticket for the whole day on Saturday (until 18:00) or Sunday will cost 2000 rubles. In addition, you can pay for the rental of a bathrobe (200 rubles) or a solarium (3 minutes - 60 rubles).

On sale there are also subscriptions and gift certificates for a one-time visit.

Termalia hall

No SPA complex can be imagined without a specific set of beauty procedures. Termalia Hall is a territory of comfort and limitless relaxation. There is a separate dressing room and a large Japanese-style room where you can relax and even meditate with relaxing music.

Services Termalia Hall:

  1. Soaring. In a cedar bath, you are ready to offer classic soaring, with lemon, with brooms and ice, as well as honey and salt. Cost: from 1500 to 2500 rubles.
  2. Peelings (soap, coffee, mango, coconut and others). Cost: 1500 rubles.
  3. Wraps (with chocolate, coconut, seaweed or sea mud). Cost: 4900 rubles.
  4. Massage (classic, honey, lymphatic drainage, stone massage, relaxing, sports). Cost: from 800 to 2800 rubles.

Drop dance

Hotel-aqua club Voda (Sestroretsk, Russia) offers guests a unique entertainment - foam parties Drop Dance. Signature tropical cocktails are served in the private bar, and Go-Go girls are energized and in a good mood.

Every Saturday, the aquaclub organizes thematic events and raffles of gift certificates and valuable prizes. You are waiting for a safe cover on the dance floor, a live rain wall, a laser-light show, foam and music news from the best DJs in Moscow and St. Petersburg. A visit to Drop Dance is included in the ticket price. Entrance to parties is paid separately. A professional photographer always works at events, and the results of his work can be seen in the official aquaclub groups on the Web.



Restaurant Mizu

After all the water entertainments, Voda - aquaclub, 3 * hotel (Russia, St. Petersburg) - offers to restore strength in the only water restaurant Mizu.

Mizu Dry is located in the reception area and serves breakfast. The second platform - Mizu Wet - is open for vacationers in the water area. A special terrace in the very center of the pool is divided into two parts: on the lower tier there is a lounge zone, and the upper part is designed for various events.

The combination of European and Japanese cuisine, as well as the skill of the chef and the unusual presentation of dishes - the Mizu restaurant is well known outside Sestroretsk. Signature sushi menu, freshly squeezed juices or light fitness salads - here any visitor will find something to their taste. For children there is a special menu with a dessert made from natural ice cream with fresh fruits.

Vacationers give Mizu restaurants only the best ratings. Breakfast on the Dry venue is served √† la carte until 2:00 p.m., which is especially convenient for those who like to sleep. The taste and quality of the food leave only good impressions, and the main comments concern high prices.

Private events

Aquaclub & Hotel Voda has everything you need for private events. Children's party, birthday party, hen or stag parties, presentations, shows and corporate events - the club space allows you to easily accommodate up to 250 people.

Making a banquet menu with the chef Mizu, animation, room decoration and music - a personal manager is ready to take care of all the arrangements for organizing the event.

Depending on the number of invitees, it is possible to rent one of four sites:

- Mizu Dry;

- Drop Dance;

- Mizu Wet;

- Termalia Hall.
