Where to go in Pskov: attractions, their description and photos

One of the important cultural and tourist centers of Russia is Pskov. Where to go for a tourist in this oldest city is described in this article. In Pskov, there are many architectural complexes, temples, chapels and cathedrals. Many of them can be visited through excursions. In the article, we will talk about the most attractive, and sometimes unusual places in this city.

Dovmontov city

Dovmontov city is an extension supplementing the Pskov Kremlin. Initially, this structure was not part of his ensemble. Fragments of ten cathedrals were discovered in Dovmontov city. There is a small museum exposition with a model of the Pskov Kremlin. Of particular interest is the composition of the battle on Lake Peipsi.



Museums

The Two Captains Museum tells about the family of the writer Kaverin, the creation of the novel of the same name and the first explorers of the North. In it you can see the part from the mammoth tusk, airplane ski from the Nord-Ost, the whale‚Äôs vertebra and many other interesting exhibits.

Museum "Honey Farm" - another attraction of Pskov. This is the farmstead of engineer Glazov. He invented a new generation of biostrator hives. The museum promotes a healthy lifestyle and longevity that can be achieved with bee products. Glazov shares unique old recipes on how to use fruits and berries for natural healthy drinks.

The Honey Farm Museum consists of seven separate buildings. Nearby is an existing apiary. The estate exhibits peasant life 19-20 centuries. And in the apiary you can see the various buildings of the hives - from rarities to modern ones. There is also a separate author's shop, which sells thematic books, honey, propolis and much more.





The railway museum tells the story of transport. Expositions are in three thematic rooms. In front of the entrance is a large semaphore and wheeled trolley. There are a number of other equally interesting museums. Many of them are included in excursion plans.

Where to go in Pskov: Chambers

The Pagankins' Chambers are a medieval monument of Russian architecture that began its history in the 17th century. The building was built by order of the merchant, whose surname became the name of the chambers. The museum complex includes an exposition dedicated to the Pskov region during the Second World War, the House of Masson and the gallery of paintings. Separately exhibited items found in treasures hidden by wealthy merchants.

Order chambers were also built in the 17th century. It was the duty station of city officials. At first the chambers were wooden, but after numerous fires they were built of stone. The walls are 2 meters thick. The chambers have five branches, in which courts of various kinds were run. And in the cellars were criminals in blocks.





The chambers of the Menshikovs, old wealthy merchants, have been preserved on the Romanov Hill. The first building was built in the middle of the 17th century. The remaining buildings were completed by the sons of the merchant. The chambers are interconnected by an underground passage.

Rattle tower

Where else can I go in Pskov? One of the most interesting places in the city is the Gremyachaya tower. It was built in 1525 on the right bank of the river. Pskov. The tower is covered in many legends. It resembles the architecture of medieval Italian cities. In ancient times, the tower was a fortification. It has 6 tiers and many small loopholes.

There is a secret hole in the dungeon leading to the river. The tower is only 20 m high, but a whole garrison was freely located inside. The legends told by the guides feature the ghosts of the princes, artisans with a demonic escort, sleeping beauties and countless treasures.





Galleries

For art lovers, there are many places to go in Pskov. For example, the Gallery of Naive Art is included in the Pskov Museum-Reserve and is located in the Menshikov Chambers. It contains a variety of works by artists and sculptors of homemade art. About four hundred paintings and fifteen sculptures of the author.

At first, the Gallery was located in only one hall, then it began to grow, and as a result, in many Menshikov chambers you can admire unique works of art. Exhibited works (statuettes, paintings, etc.) were collected for 40 years.

Planetarium

The Pskov Planetarium is a unique place in the city. It is located in the former church of the Nativity of the Virgin. There is a star room in the planetarium, there are telescopes and projectors. The dome is designed for the projection of the starry sky. The planetarium hosts a variety of programs with visual effects. On the second floor there are lectures and demonstrations of planets. The creation of galaxies, constellations, etc. is told and shown.

If the question arose of where to go with children in Pskov, then this, of course, is a planetarium. In addition to the above, it has special cognitive programs with fairy-tale characters. And visual examples will help students not only in the educational process, but also significantly expand their horizons.

Excursions

There are many excursion programs in Pskov. For all tastes. Excursion tickets are sold at seven locations throughout the city. Two of these are located directly in the Pskov Kremlin. Sightseeing tours can be purchased through travel agencies. Their cost depends on the season and the saturation of the program.

Escape quests

When the working week ends, the question arises, where to go in Pskov on the weekend? One of the great leisure options is real-time quests. Sign up for them in advance. The bottom line is to go through the whole quest and get back in real time. Plots can be varied. Many are styled as popular films. The most important thing is intelligence and ingenuity. Quests are held by teams of two to five people.

It can be a great vacation for a family with teens. Passage - without the use of any force. Extraordinary thinking and intelligence develops perfectly. You need to book such quests in advance, at a convenient time. The only condition is not to be late, because time is very important here.

Parks and attractions

One of the interesting sights of Pskov is the arboretum park. It is located near the Mirozhsky monastery. Trees of many species and species are planted in the park. There is a small lake in this natural area. And in the middle is an artificially created island. It also planted some types of trees.



Through the river Mirozhka, which flows next to the park, paved dams and pedestrian bridges. All this forms a picturesque artificial reservoir. The park has attractions, benches. And lit by lanterns in the evening, it becomes fantastically beautiful.

Where to go in Pskov for rides? Fans of such entertainment can visit the city park. Pushkin. You can visit it at any time of the year. The park has not only attractions, but also dance floors. The Botanical Garden has been created. You can ride a ferris wheel. There are several other parks, each of which has its own "highlight".

Youth clubs

For fans of club entertainment, there is also where to go in the evening in Pskov. For example, in Bingo or R-16. These clubs are open every night. They host various show programs. You can relax from the noise and dancing in the billiards room. And for those who like to try themselves as a singer, there is karaoke. In restaurants, you can order dishes not only from European, but also from Chinese cuisine.
