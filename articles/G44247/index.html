Smartphone Huawei G6: review, specifications, firmware, price and reviews

The Huawei G6 is a great mid-range smartphone. It stands out against the background of similar devices with an excellent combination of low cost and a good level of performance. These opportunities will be considered in the framework of this material.





Options, appearance and usability

We will begin the review of Huawei G6 with a complete set and convenience of work on this smart phone. This unit cannot boast of something unusual in terms of equipment. Its boxed version includes the following accessories:

  • A modest entry-level speaker system.
  • Charger.
  • USB / microUSB adapter cord.
  • Smartphone with built-in battery.

Among the list of documents there are a multilingual user manual and, of course, a warranty card. But the external flash drive will have to be purchased separately, since it is not in the basic configuration. The body of the device is made of ordinary plastic with a matte finish. Its dimensions are 131.2 x 65.3 mm. The lock button and volume rocker are located on the right edge of the smart phone. Below is the microUSB standard port. In the lower left corner is a classic audio jack. Three standard touch buttons are displayed on the bottom of the front panel, and the top edge of the smartphone is not busy.





Hardware stuffing

Sufficiently powerful hardware in the Huawei G6. First of all, you need to allocate a central processor. In this case, we are talking about the MCM8226 chip from Kualkom. It belongs to the Snapdragon 400 family. It is based on 4 cores based on the architecture codenamed Cortex A7. The maximum possible clock frequency at which they can operate is 1.2 GHz. The second important component in this device is the Adreno 305 video card . The amount of installed RAM is 1 GB, and the built-in storage capacity is 4 GB, of which the user can use only 1 GB, which is not enough for today. As a result, the normal operation of this gadget is impossible without a flash card. As noted earlier, it will have to be purchased separately. This smartphone can address a maximum of 32 GB. All this in total allows us to say that this gadget can easily cope with any task at the moment.

Cameras

There will be an incomplete review of the Huawei G6, if you don‚Äôt touch the cameras. There are two of them in this device, as expected. The matrix of the main camera is based on an 8 megapixel sensor. An autofocus and image stabilization system is also provided. The developers did not forget about the LED backlight. The number of program settings is not so great, but still, the quality of the photos received with its help is not satisfactory, although it cannot boast of special quality. There is also a front camera for making video calls. She has a 5 megapixel matrix, and she copes with this task perfectly.





Battery and its features

Very modest, as for such a device, the battery capacity of 2000 mA / h in the Huawei G6. Responses of owners such a decision of engineers is pretty criticized. With a mediocre load, this value is enough for 2 days of battery life, provided that some of the parameters (for example, screen brightness) are brought to a minimum. In reality, this figure is even worse and amounts to only 8-12 hours, which is very small. In general, the autonomy of this smartphone model leaves much to be desired. Another problem is that the battery is soldered into the device. As a result, the only reasonable solution to this problem is an external battery that connects to the microUSB port.

Software

Today this device is running Android with version 4.3. On sale, this phone appeared in May 2014, there is no reason to expect updates for it. Therefore, you need to be content with what is already available. There is also a special software add-in of its own design in the Huawei G6. The firmware is supplemented by Emotion UI. It has many useful widgets. First of all, this is the weather forecast. The second - faster access to the photo gallery. In addition, there is quick access to contacts and audio recordings. For the rest, the set of applications and utilities is standard: ordinary OS applications and programs from Google. And, of course, international social services.





Data exchange

Support for multiple interfaces implemented in the Huawei G6. There is everything for comfortable work on this gadget. The smartphone supports the following methods of transmitting information :

  • There is only one SIM card slot. But all data transfer standards are supported. That is, the smartphone can work in all types of mobile networks: GSM, WCDMA and LTE. In the latter case, the data transfer rate can reach 150 Mbit / s, which allows you to upload any amount of information to this device.
  • It supports the most used technology for transferring impressive amounts of data - Wi-Fi. The data transfer rate in this case is similar to LTE.
  • There is also bluetooth - an ideal solution for those cases when you need to urgently transfer a small file to a similar device.
  • Navigation can be carried out either using GPS (the corresponding transmitter is installed in the gadget) or using the a-GPS system (in this case, mobile networks are used to determine the location).
  • There is also a regular jack for connecting an external sound system. It is located in the lower right corner of the gadget. A very controversial decision: under certain circumstances, you can break off the pin of the speaker system.
  • The last wired interface is microUSB. It allows you to charge the battery. And when connected to a personal computer, it also allows you to exchange data.

Price and reviews

Everything previously described is just the technical specifications of the Huawei G6. Reviews of real owners are sometimes much more useful. Now it‚Äôs not difficult to find them. Most of them indicate such advantages:

  • Very productive hardware platform.
  • The software runs smoothly and there are no glitches.
  • Flexible customization of the interface, which is provided by the proprietary add-in "Emotion UI".

But his shortcomings are as follows:

  • Autonomy (on a single charge with a minimum load, the smartphone lasts a day).
  • Inconveniently located ports are 3.5 mm and microUSB.

In principle, the Huawei G6 has completely turned out. The price for it is currently $ 200. In general, the ideal ratio of cost and performance.





Summary

If you need a smartphone with good functionality and affordable price, then you can safely turn your eyes towards the Huawei G6. Although this device has been on sale for a long time, its characteristics are still relevant, and it can solve almost all problems today.
