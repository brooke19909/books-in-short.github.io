Entertainment and beaches of Koktebel

One of the best places of rest in Crimea has always been Koktebel, although this is just an urban-type settlement. However, there are both large pensions and small hotels. In fact, there is housing for every taste and budget. In addition, without leaving the village, you can find entertainment for extreme lovers, as well as for those who prefer a family pastime. But everyone who has ever rested there will surely remember the incredibly clean beaches of Koktebel.



The resort village is located in a bay near the extinct volcano Kara-Dag. This volcano is one of the most beautiful natural attractions of Crimea. Its bizarre shapes resemble the king, queen and numerous retinue. But the most famous formations of the Kara-Dag mountain range are the Golden, or Devil's, gates. And all this magnificence can be enjoyed simply by visiting the beaches of Koktebel.

The beaches stretch along the entire village. The sea here (due to its location in the bay) is always clean and transparent. Despite the fact that the coast is pebble, as in most of the Crimea, the bottom itself is sandy. Not only does this make swimming enjoyable, but also a comfortable water temperature from June to October. In addition, almost all the beaches of Koktebel are free, with developed infrastructure. At the service of vacationers sun loungers, umbrellas and swimming mattresses are provided for a fee.



The most famous beaches of Koktebel are the beaches of the boarding houses "Blue Bay" and "Primorye". They are located in the central part of the village, near the coastal park. But they are popular not only because of this. Every day the beaches are cleaned of garbage, and garbage is cleaned not only on the coast, but also in the water. Drinks and light snacks are available on site. Various water activities are organized for vacationers.

But for many extreme people who go on vacation to the Crimea (Koktebel), beaches are not the most important thing. Near this village, on Klementyev‚Äôs mountain, there is the Koktebel glider center, in which everyone can fly a hang glider or parachute jump. Unique natural conditions allow you to literally freeze in the air and enjoy the opened beauty. All jumps are carried out under the strict guidance of instructors.



In addition to flights, there are other entertainments. On the territory of the village itself there is a water park, a dolphinarium and dinoteria. Without leaving Koktebel, you can have a great time. But if you still want to visit other places, you can book any tour of the Crimea or, if time permits, go by bus to Sudak or Feodosia.

In addition, the festival of jazz and ethnic music "Jazz-Koktebel" is held in Koktebel. Every year he gathers more than 20 thousand fans from around the world. And this is no coincidence. Many jazz artists are happy to take part in it. The popularity of the festival is growing every year, so it is recommended to purchase a subscription to attend concerts in advance.

This is what Koktebel is famous for. Here you can relax not only with your body, but also with your soul. As already mentioned, this is one of the cleanest places in the Crimea. Come to Koktebel (Crimea), a photo of the beach and the clear sea will then warm your soul for a long time to come.
