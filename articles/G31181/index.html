How many km from Lipetsk to Moscow, how to get and save time

How many km are from Lipetsk to Moscow? This indicator differs depending on the chosen way of travel. Let's try to figure out how best to get to the capital of Russia in such a way as to save the maximum amount of time and money, as well as spend the trip in relative comfort.

How to get there by car

How many km from Lipetsk to Moscow are along the motorway? Approximately 470. It is recommended to go to the M-4 highway, and then north according to the signs, without changing the direction of movement.

The distance indicator can be increased if you go on city roads and go around paid sections.

It may seem to some that such maneuvers will save time and money, but this is not so. And that's why:

  • To avoid paid sites, you will have to call in settlements, and this will lead to inevitably getting into traffic jams or heavy traffic.
  • The quality of municipal roads is poor, no one has canceled the cost of depreciating a car.
  • The maximum speed on the toll road is 110 km / h, and not on city roads - from 40 to 60 km / h. Many cameras allow you to get a guaranteed fine. How many km from Lipetsk to Moscow need to be driven at such a speed that the journey turns into a difficult test?

Travel time is 5-6 hours.

Travel by bus

Just on the free sections of the road there are regular buses from Lipetsk. They depart several times a day and move along the route Lipetsk-Yelets-Moscow.

This option is not very convenient for several reasons:

  • not the most comfortable buses;
  • you have to go from 6 to 8 hours;
  • the final destination is Domodedovo Airport, and this is Moscow Region, not Moscow.

The average ticket price is 800-1000 rubles.

Aircraft - an alternative to ground transportation

Lipetsk is a fairly large city with serious business activity. Perhaps this is what caused regular direct flights to the capital, and even several times a day.

It will take only one hour for the plane to cover almost 500 km from Lipetsk to Moscow. How much time and effort is saved at the same time, you should not even mention.

The average ticket price ranges from 4 to 5 thousand rubles. A very substantial fee for comfort.

Summing up, we can say that the plane is the fastest and most expensive way, the bus is the longest and cheapest, and autotrip on your car is the golden mean. Those who are closer to this option should look for travel companions through a special service. This will help save money on gasoline and improve the quality of the trip with adequate companions.

How many km from Lipetsk to Moscow is not so important when new impressions and a new city are ahead. So go ahead - towards adventure.
