Hotel Chanalai Romantica Resort 4 *, about. Phuket, Thailand: description, features and interesting facts

Thailand, namely Phuket, is one of the most popular resorts. Every year thousands of tourists flock here who want to have a good time on the beach, enjoying the exotic nature and mild climate. Naturally, a huge number of hotels of different sizes and categories have been built on the territory of the city. One of the popular holiday destinations is the Chanalai Romantica Resort.

Naturally, a person going on a trip tries to find the maximum amount of useful information. So what is this hotel and what quality of accommodation can a tourist expect?

What does the hotel look like? Location





Of course, travelers are primarily interested in the location. The Chanalai Romantica Resort 4 * hotel is built on a picturesque coast, near the center of the resort town of Kata. Within walking distance you will have a variety of shops and cafes. Patong's most famous Thai beach is a 20-minute drive away.

The hotel complex Chanalai Romantica Resort (ex Tropical Kata Resort) consists of three buildings. The hotel area is small but cozy and goes almost to the beach itself. This place is very popular among tourists from all over the world.

Hotel Chanalai Romantica Resort (Phuket): what do the rooms look like?





The hotel complex is considered quite large and consists of 209 rooms. Travelers can choose a comfortable standard room or a more luxurious deluxe room.

Regardless of the category, you can count on comfort and all the necessary amenities in the room. The rooms are beautifully furnished and furnished in an interesting Thai style. Naturally, you will have at your disposal a full range of household appliances, including a telephone, a TV (you can watch satellite channels) and a powerful air conditioning system.

The minibar is filled with drinking water, the remaining drinks are brought to the room upon request. Food can be stored in a small refrigerator. There is a small coffee maker, an electric kettle and a set for making hot drinks. Guests can also use the safe and internet access.

There is a spacious bathroom with a shower, a washbasin, a toilet and a hairdryer. Also, guests are provided with clean towels, stocks of which are replenished every day. Reviews indicate that the rooms are in perfect cleanliness - maids come every day. By the way, bed linen is changed several times a week.

Dining at the hotel





Food is an important part of a good quality stay. What can the Chanalai Romantica Resort offer? When you pay for your stay, you are entitled to daily breakfast in the main restaurant. Buffets are served here in the mornings, and the menu, as evidenced by the reviews, is quite diverse.

There are other catering facilities at the hotel where you can eat for a reasonable price. Morakot and Manee restaurants offer traditional Thai dishes, as well as more familiar European dishes. There is also a summer cafe where you can have a snack and enjoy aromatic coffee in the open air, admiring the view of the sea. The grill bar is also constantly working, where you can try meat and vegetable dishes. The hotel also has two bars. Here, guests can relax in a pleasant atmosphere, enjoy strong drinks, order a delicious dessert.

Where is the beach located?





The Chanalai Romantica Resort Kata Beach hotel complex is located almost on the seashore - the distance to the beach is only 50 meters. This is a private stretch of the coast, so you can count on comfortable sun loungers, umbrellas, towels and chilled drinks.

Reviews say that the beach here is very clean, comfortable and well maintained. There is a shallow area where children can splash around. Naturally, guests have the opportunity to engage in more active types of recreation, including boat trips and boating, water skiing and a banana, sailing , etc.

And by car in 20 minutes you can drive to one of the largest and busiest beaches in Phuket called Patong. This beach is perfect for travelers who love busy places, exciting entertainment and nightly parties on the beach.

The list of additional services





Hotel guests can count on certain additional services, without which it is difficult to imagine a truly comfortable stay. For example, in public areas there is access to the Internet. Also, guests can use the local exchange office with a fairly favorable rate. If necessary, hotel staff can arrange a shuttle service or help you call a taxi.

A gift and souvenir shop is located on site. There is a business center with the necessary equipment, as well as a spacious conference room for 100 people. By the way, the room is used not only for business events, but also for the organization of celebrations, including weddings. The hotel also has professional florists and photographers.

There is no medical center on the hotel premises, but in case of problems, a qualified doctor is called to the guests.

How do hotel guests spend their time?

Of course, on the territory of the town there are many entertainment venues. Nevertheless, you can find yourself an interesting lesson in the Chanalai Romantica Resort hotel.

In the courtyard there is a swimming pool with a children's section, where guests often spend time. On the terrace you can relax and sunbathe, because it offers comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas. Tourists can also use the jacuzzi. There are several massage rooms where customers are offered high-quality Thai massage. There is a SPA salon, which offers a large number of cosmetology, relaxing and wellness treatments.

You can have a good time playing billiards. Guests can use the local large library as well as the games room. And do not forget that Thailand is a beautiful country with ancient history, and therefore is perfect for sightseeing holidays. At the hotel you can help you plan a trip or purchase a ready-made tour of the surroundings.

Hotel Chanalai Romantica Resort (ex Tropical Kata 4 *): what do tourists say?





Of course, the opinion of travelers who have already managed to spend some time at the hotel is very valuable. What do they say about Chanalai Romantic Resort? In fact, tourists respond positively about this place. The hotel is perfect for a truly comfortable holiday on the beach, as it provides travelers with everything they need.

Beautiful area, proximity to the beach, delicious food and comfortable rooms - what else is needed for a pleasant pastime? And the cost of living is not so high, given the quality of service. By the way, the hotel staff are polite and welcoming, they do everything possible to make guests feel at home. Workers are fluent in English. Tourists recommend this place for a fun holiday on the exotic Thai coast.
