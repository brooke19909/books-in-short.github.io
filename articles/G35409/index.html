The recreation center "Far Far Away" (Pskov region): reviews, prices

The nature of the surroundings of the city of Pskov is conducive to the development of tourism in this region. Modern hotels, tourist complexes and recreation centers of the Pskov region offer vacationers the organization of their leisure in the picturesque corners of the region.

One of the most beautiful places in the Pskov region is Lake Peipsi. The eastern shore of this huge reservoir belongs to our country, and the northern and western - to Estonia.

Tourist complex "Far Far Away"

The shores of Lake Peipsi are very loved by Russian tourists. In these places, magnificent nature, unique forests, striking a riot of colors, but the most important highlight is the purest water of the lake.

Amid all this grandeur on the shore Crystal Chui Lake is the recreation center "Far Far Away. This heavenly place is surrounded by a noble pine forest, which is abundantly filled with edible mushrooms and delicious berries.





The nearest town is located forty kilometers from the base, it is the city of Gdov. From Pskov to the recreation center is about an hour's drive, the distance is about eighty kilometers. On holidays and weekends, local urban and rural residents of nearby villages come to the recreation center to relax from city noise and plunge into the bosom of nature.

Description of the recreation center

The recreation center is a comfortable cottage town with a developed infrastructure. This island of civilization among the indescribable beauty of wildlife really resembles a fairy kingdom.





All cottages are built of wood and saturated with the smell of needles. All of them are similar to each other, but each has its own characteristics. Some of the log cabins are one-story, others are on two floors. The houses are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay - shower, toilet, high-quality furniture, kitchens, televisions and refrigerators. The houses are heated with real fireplaces and electric heaters.

The infrastructure of the recreation center

The recreation center has a restaurant with a large terrace, a grill bar, 2 conference rooms, a billiard room, a sports and sauna complex, children's rooms with animators, and equipment rental.

On the territory of the base there are volleyball courts, tennis tables, a dance floor and a children's town and a real zoo. The recreation center "Far Far Away" will not let anyone get bored, everyone will find entertainment for the soul.





The territory of the recreation center in summer is somewhat different from winter decoration. In winter, on the territory of the base, there are some charms - they fill a large skating rink, arrange ski slopes, and large snow slides are being built.

Accommodation for vacationers

At the same time, the recreation center "Far Far Away" can take one hundred and seventy people vacationers.

For lovers of delights and elite relaxation, the so-called VIP cottage is rented, which can accommodate sixteen people, twelve in the main places and four more on additional ones. The cottage has two floors, its total area is more than three hundred square meters.

The atmosphere in the VIP cottage is luxurious. On the ground floor there is a kitchen and a huge hall with large sofas with a plasma TV and stereo system. Right below is a chic billiard room, a sauna with a jacuzzi and two toilets with showers. The second floor - a zone of bedrooms, there are six in total, they are all of different capacities, equipped with quality furniture. From the second floor there is access to a balcony with a picturesque view of the forest and lake. Near the cottage there is a huge gazebo, a barbecue area and a large relaxation area.





The rest of the cottages are standard. They are designed for a different number of tourists (from two to 12 people). Each of them has amenities, fireplace, living room, bedrooms and kitchen. Near each house there is a cozy gazebo and barbecue.

Accommodation prices depend on capacity, season and number of days of arrival. So, for example, a VIP cottage per day costs 20,000 rubles, 4 days from Monday to Thursday - 50,000 rubles, 3 days from Friday to Monday - 50,000 rubles, and a week - 100,000 rubles.

Ordinary cottages in this price range: a house for 6 people in the summer costs 8,000 rubles per day, in the off-season it costs 6,500 rubles, a week in the summer this house will cost 30,000 rubles to vacationers, and at other times of the year - 18,000 rubles.

The recreation center "Far Far Away" also offers accommodation in double and triple rooms with hotel-type amenities that are rented in one of the cottages. Room rates range from 1000 to 2000 rubles per day, depending on capacity and season.
