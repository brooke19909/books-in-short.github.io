Camp "Todes" in Bulgaria: description, activities and entertainment, living conditions

Camp "Todes" in Bulgaria is located in the resort of Albena. Its doors are open for both children and their parents. Classes in choreography are held according to a specially developed unique program. Groups are formed according to the degree of preparation of the participants. In addition to dancing, children and adults, many other entertainments await. Learn more about everything from the article.

Description

In 2017, the Todes camp in Bulgaria celebrated its fifteen years of existence. The resort of Albena, in whose territory it is located, is located on the picturesque coast of the Black Sea Bay, 45 kilometers from the city of Varna. It is recognized as environmentally friendly.

Albena is the third most important resort in Bulgaria. It features wide sandy beaches and convenient access to the sea. The water temperature in summer is always comfortable. Ebbs and flows are practically absent, which favors a vacation with children. Over two hundred meters from the coast, the depth of the sea does not exceed one and a half meters.

Activities and Activities

Highly qualified teachers and soloists of the famous ballet "Todes" work in the camp. All of them undergo special training for working with children. Thanks to the unique program of classes developed for each level of training separately, children prepare a spectacular concert program for one season, which is shown to guests and parents at the end of the holiday. A similar technique helps boys and girls get to know different dance styles.

Accommodations

The shift can last 14, 21 or more days, depending on the choice of vacationers. Accommodation in rooms - two to three people.

The rooms are comfortable, with all amenities, located in cozy houses. For children with parents equipped apartments. There is a pool in the camp. Wi-Fi is available. Three meals a day - buffet. Quality medical care is provided.

In the camp "Todes" in Bulgaria, all conditions are created for a comfortable and fun holiday for children and their parents. And the picturesque nature, mild climate and warm sea will make vacations unforgettable.
