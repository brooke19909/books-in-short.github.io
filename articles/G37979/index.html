Hotel Thalassa Sousse 4 * (Tunisia, Sousse): photos and reviews of tourists

Modern Tunisia is one of the most popular destinations among Russian tourists. They are attracted to beaches of pure sand, a rich excursion program, high quality service and relatively inexpensive trips. Budget tours are provided by the Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel (Tunisia). But is it worth recommending it for a vacation?

Important hotel information

There is an extensive garden in the shade of which numerous cozy bungalows built in the Arabian style are scattered. Two main three-story buildings are located near the coast. In total, the complex has 487 comfortable rooms, most of which accommodate about 2-3 people. The entire hotel is equipped with ramps for wheelchairs and strollers. The hotel staff hardly speaks Russian, so you should practice your conversational English or French before relaxing.





Tourists begin to settle in the hotel Thalassa Sousse 4 * (Tunisia, Sousse) strictly from 14:00. If you arrived earlier, you can go to the beach, leaving things in the locker. It is forbidden to come to the hotel with pets. For small children a discount on accommodation. Tourists whose vacation has come to an end should leave their apartments before noon. The last redecoration of all buildings and rooms was completed in 2013.

Where is the hotel located?

The hotel is part of the tourist resort of Sousse, which is famous for its rich nightlife. In the city center, tourists can find many nightclubs and bars. Discos are also held in the open air. The most popular of them, Bora Bora, is considered the largest in Africa. But for lovers of a relaxing holiday there is entertainment. For example, thalassotherapy centers or golf courses. Tourists interested in the cultural heritage of the country can buy excursions to the ancient monuments of Tunisia. So, the medieval Medina is 4 km away from the Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel (Tunisia).

The complex is located away from the noisy center, so it is well suited for families with children. You can get here from Monastir International Airport, which is 22 km from the resort. The hotel is located on the first beach line, so the distance to the sea is less than 100 meters. Also nearby are Hannibal Park and Aqua Palace.

Hotel Rooms

Travelers who decide to stay at Thalassa Sousse Resort Aquapark 4 * (Tunisia, Sousse) are waiting for 487 cozy rooms that are decorated in bright summer colors. Most of the apartments (287) are located in the main building of the hotel, while the rest are located in small bungalows. All of them are designed for accommodation from 1 to 4 adults. There are spacious rooms that are perfect for families with children. All rooms have a small balcony. The apartments on the ground floor are replaced by terraces. The floor is tiled. The design of the rooms combines classic and minimalism.





In addition to beds, each room has a certain set of furniture. It includes: a desk and a coffee table, a chair, a mirror, bedside tables. The balcony also has a dining set. The bathroom in the rooms is combined. There is a bath with shower, washbasin, large mirror, hairdryer, cosmetics and towels. Daily housekeeping is provided. Bed linen is changed 2 times a week.

More about room amenities

Also, all rooms of the Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel (Tunisia) are equipped with amenities that allow you to make your stay more comfortable. All tourists can use the following equipment:

  • Individual air conditioning. With it, guests can maintain a cool room temperature even on the hottest days.
  • Direct telephone. It is used to communicate with staff, as well as to order additional services or international calls.
  • Wireless Internet. Its connection is not included in the price.
  • Refrigerator for storing food or drinks. Provided for a fee. The approximate cost of its use is 15 dinars per day.
  • Safe. Used to store documents, jewelry, money or phones.
  • TV. Some rooms still have old picture tubes. Satellite channels are connected, including Russian-language ones.

Complex infrastructure

Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * (Tunisia) gets good reviews. Many tourists appreciate the developed infrastructure of the hotel, providing a comfortable stay. For its guests, the complex offers the following facilities and services:

  • Own amphitheater. In the evenings, animators hold entertaining shows for tourists.
  • Doctor's office. The cost of visiting him is not included in the insurance, so you will have to pay separately for each appointment.
  • Currency exchange point.
  • Beauty saloon. Guests can undergo a course of cosmetic procedures for the face, make-up, manicure and pedicure.
  • Barbershop. Male, female and children's masters work.
  • Laundry. It offers tourists paid cleaning services for clothes and shoes.
  • Shops on site. Guests can buy souvenirs, food, alcohol and tobacco, newspapers and cosmetics.
  • Car parking. Car rental is chargeable.
  • Internet corner. His visit is paid separately. The cost is approximately 5 dinars per hour.
  • Business center. Equipped with several conference rooms and a meeting room.




Hotel entertainment program

Tourists who love a quiet pastime can soak up the snow-white beach of the Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel (Tunisia). But for travelers who prefer outdoor activities, there is a lot of entertainment. The complex offers the following leisure activities:

  • Two pools: outdoor and indoor heated. Sun beds, mattresses and umbrellas are provided. There are water slides for adults and children.
  • Water activities on the beach: parasailing, windsurfing, diving, water polo. Tourists can ride a catamaran, canoe, banana, water skiing.
  • An animation program that includes sports, entertainment and cultural events.
  • Spa. It offers wellness services: hammam, jacuzzi, thalassotherapy, massage, peeling, therapeutic mud.
  • Fitness Centre. There is a paid gym. Mass classes are held in aerobics and water gymnastics.
  • Sports games: volleyball, table and classic tennis, basketball, golf.
  • Billiard room.
  • Archery. There are also tutorials for beginners.
  • Entertainment shows and discos with live music, karaoke contests and dance lessons.
  • Organization of excursions to the monuments of Tunisian culture.




Conditions for children

The Thalassa Sousse 4 * complex (Tunisia) has prepared a lot of entertainment for tourists with children. For younger guests there are 17 water slides where they can ride with their parents. There is also a private children's pool. For outdoor games, a playground with a swing and a sandbox was built. There is a mini-club for children from the age of four. For them, educational and entertaining lessons, contests and games are held. There is also a teenage club for older children.

For babies up to two years a free cot is provided in the room, and in restaurants parents can feed the child in a high chair.





Positive feedback: hotel advantages

Visitors to the Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel (Tunisia) leave different reviews . Most of them are usually positive. They celebrate the following benefits of relaxation here:

  • A gentle entry into the sea. It is shallow, so it is perfect for children and for tourists who do not know how to swim.
  • Good location. There are several supermarkets and restaurants nearby. It takes only 2-3 minutes to get to the city center by taxi.
  • Clean pool. Water does not smell like bleach, and the area near it is well-groomed. There are many sunbeds, so you do not need to occupy them in the early morning.
  • Plumbing in the rooms works well, nothing leaks.
  • Thoroughly cleaned apartments. Well washed floors, regularly change bedding. For a tip, employees beautifully lay out sheets, leave flowers in vases.
  • A varied diet. Every day at the buffet there are several types of meat, seafood, including shrimp and mussels.
  • There are no insects in the rooms.




Negative reviews: hotel flaws

Like any other complex, the hotel Thalassa Sousse 4 * (Tunisia) receives negative reviews. Tourists are unhappy with the following shortcomings of the hotel:

  • The restaurant does not have enough seats and utensils for all guests. To dine, tourists have to stand in line.
  • Neglect of the staff towards the guests. They do everything extremely slowly, do not seek to help with solving problems.
  • A lot of jellyfish swim in the sea, so burns constantly remain on the legs and arms.
  • The beach is poorly cleaned. There are a lot of cigarette butts, plastic glasses and other rubbish in the sand. The urns here are always crowded.
  • Tasty food in the main restaurant. The fried eggs are greatly overcooked, spicy sauces and spices are added to all dishes.

Instead of an afterword

We can say that the Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel (Tunisia), the photo of which is presented above, is perfect for a budget holiday. Nevertheless, many tourists note that the complex does not correspond to four stars. There are significant flaws here that can ruin your vacation experience. In general, the hotel can be recommended to families with children who really like the local water park. The youth here will also not be bored.
