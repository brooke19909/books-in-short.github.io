What are the circuits on microcontrollers?

Now there are quite a few different devices and devices that allow a person to freely create. These are paints, and paper with a pen, and a microcontroller. Everyone chooses what is closer to him. And what electronic circuits on microcontrollers can be built? From the point of view of amateur electronics, almost any.

What can be done on microcontrollers?



These small control devices, despite their nondescript parameters (if compared with a computer) offer ample opportunities for their application. You can make complex and simple circuits on microcontrollers. But in the end, it comes down to two main areas:

  1. Creation of robots.
  2. Creation of automated systems.

Robots



This area is developing more as an entertainment. No, of course, there are industrial robots that work on microcontrollers, but you can rarely see something like this in a home environment. Most often, electronics lovers create such mechanisms specifically for entertainment. If we talk about which circuits on microcontrollers can be cited as an example, then we must first recall the models of transport (usually ground). But in addition to cars, these devices assemble their own quadrocopters, walking devices and ships.
