Turkish Aegean resorts - a paradise that no one knows

Turkey is a country that few modern Russians have visited. However, the bulk of tourists are sent only to the Mediterranean coast, to the famous Antalya, Side, Alanya and other cities. However, few people visit Turkish resorts on the Aegean , and in vain. It is about these bewitching and unforgettable places that we will now talk about.



As everyone knows, this wonderful southern country is washed immediately by three seas: the Black, Mediterranean and Aegean. Alas, the tourist infrastructure on the shores of the first is practically undeveloped, since, according to local residents, this is too cold a region. With the Mediterranean Sea, everything is clear - this is the majority resting place. There are chic hotels, and shops, and parks, and everything that you might need during your vacation. But the map of Turkey does not end there. Aegean resorts - this is one of the most mysterious and enchanting attractions of this country.



It is mainly worth noting that the western coast of the Asia Minor Peninsula is the center of quiet and picturesque harbors. All resorts of Turkey on the Aegean are very comfortable and beautiful, but these are not all the advantages that they can boast of. The main difference of this region is the presence of a wind called imbat, which blows from the sea. Thanks to him, the region is becoming not as sultry and arid as in other cities in Turkey. Also, it is this natural aspect that allows windsurfing to all tourists who decide to spend their holidays here.





As mentioned earlier, bays are a virtue that streams all resorts of the Aegean Sea. In Turkey, the number of picturesque bays is very large, and almost each of them has its own characteristics. Among them are both open harbors, in which high waves rise, necessary for surfing, and small bays, ideal for families with children. By the way, it is in this region of the country that the influence of the Greek style is felt. In ancient times, the modern resorts of Turkey on the Aegean were repeatedly conquered by Greek commanders, and Hellenistic buildings were erected on these lands. Some of them have survived to the present day and are now living historical monuments for us.

Despite the fact that the resorts of Turkey on the Aegean are not as popular as other regions of the country, the infrastructure here is excellently developed. A huge number of water parks, shopping centers, restaurants and nightclubs will not make even the most picky tourist bored. Entertainment here, as they say, for every taste, and provides for both relaxing with children and holding a honeymoon. It is only necessary to warn the tour operator in advance of their plans, and he will provide you with the necessary and useful program.

Resorts in Turkey on the Aegean Sea have another significant advantage - they are all located on the first coastline. It's just that in this region there is such a landscape that does not allow to build residential complexes in several rows. Therefore, no matter what room you check in, you will in any case enjoy the sea, getting out of bed in the morning, and, of course, you can reach it in a matter of minutes.
