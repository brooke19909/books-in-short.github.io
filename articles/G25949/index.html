Harbor Island, Bahamas: Travel Photos and Reviews

Harbor Island is located off the northeast coast of Eluthera. We can safely say that this is the pearl of the Bahamas. In addition, it was Harbor who was one of the first to be populated. Here you will completely plunge into the world of the tropics and the noise of the Caribbean Sea. Although this part of the Bahamas has been mastered for a long time, the tourism business is still rapidly developing in Harbor. Here you can find a lot of attractive hotels, restaurants, attractions and other places to stay.

Beaches





Visiting the Bahamas is the dream of every person, especially girls. This place is very often visited by newlyweds. It is quiet, comfortable, calm and very beautiful. The nature on the islands is truly fabulous. And for sports lovers here is the cleanest place for diving. But the pink beach on Harbor Island (Bahamas) deserves special attention. In some sources, it is called Pink Sands Beach.

This name came from crushed shells whose shells have bright pink or red tones. These shells are called foraminifera. Such unicellular organisms are large clusters on the bottom of reefs and in caves on the ocean floor. Also, a huge amount of red corals can be seen near the coast. Particles of reefs and foraminifers mix with sand. From this, the beach area acquires an unusual shade. Harbor Island (Bahamas) for this reason is the most popular among tourists. But the rest here can not be called cheap. But there are also reasons for this.





The beach is always in perfect condition. Thanks to the coral reefs that protect the coast from the onslaught of the Atlantic waves, there is no debris on the coast. The sand is always clean, smooth and soft. It is convenient to sunbathe, run, jump and you can safely swim. A huge number of palm trees grow on the beach, due to this there is always a place where you can hide from the blazing sun. Fans of diving here are very lucky, as the island of Harbor (Bahamas) is considered an ideal option for diving. The water in these places is very clean, you can consider every corner of the seabed.

Professionals and water diving enthusiasts from all over the world come here specially in search of adventure. The beach is gaining such popularity that many stars of show business travel to Harbor Island (Bahamas) to relax and enjoy the beauty of the local tropics. Some celebrities admire these beaches so much that they even buy real estate here. Walking around the neighborhood, you can see the houses of Keith Richards, Robin Williams, Susan Sarandon. On the pages of fashion magazines you can often see beautiful models on the background of a sunny beach. In most cases, they are photographed on the Pink Sands Beach.

Tips and comments




What say vacationers who are lucky to visit the island of Harbor (Bahamas)? Guest reviews are very important. Reading them, you can already make an objective idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe upcoming vacation. Below are tips for regular visitors to these paradise places.

As tourists note, to see all corners of the Bahamas, you can go for a ferry ride and stop in the wildest areas of the Bahamas. In addition to the beach, you must visit the island of Eleuthera. According to vacationers, his main pride is the "Glass Window". This name was given to a rocky strip 10 meters wide, which separates the Caribbean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean. If you decide to go there you need to carefully consider the weather. The waves of the Atlantic Ocean can surge and take away not only human lives, but also vehicles. Due to periodic hurricanes, you can often observe the destroyed roads, which are constantly being repaired.

Tourists with admiration tell that on the left bank there is a natural depression, from which water sometimes rises. This is an amazing sight.

Tourists note the attention of another unforgettable place on the island of North Eleuthera - the Cave of the Priest. At the beginning of the 18th century, people settled in this place. According to legend, there was a shipwreck in which European mariners were hit. Among the survivors was a priest who volunteered to read prayers, and since then this place has been considered holy and clean.

In Dunmore Town, according to vacationers, the following buildings deserve special attention - the old Sugar Mill, an old prison built in the 18th century, the shipyard and the summer residence of the Governor of the Bahamas.
