Exploitation

Now we turn to checking the efficiency of the furnace, or rather, how the cooking process is arranged here. Warm up

In order to warm up the dish, you need to place it inside the oven on a plate. Important: the plate should not contain patterns or other patterns that are applied with bright and shiny paint. This is described in more detail in the manual. Further, to reheat food, you can press the "+30 sec" button and then the "Start" button several times. This is the easiest way.





In addition, you can select the microwave power by clicking on the appropriate button on the panel. Microwave allows you to choose at what power the heating will occur - 800 W, 700 W, 400 W. It is worth remembering that the lower the power, the less energy the microwave will ‚Äúeat‚Äù, but, unfortunately, the same 400 or a little more W is not always enough for the necessary heating. This must be taken into account. After the power has been selected, you need to set the time using the "Min", "10 sec." Or "10 min." Buttons and press the "Start" button.

Defrost

Defrosting is also carried out in two ways. The first is just to place the frozen product in a deep plate inside the oven, set the timer (or select the power, and then set the time) and click on "Start". Everything is very simple.

The second method allows you to use the so-called accelerated defrosting. First, the product is placed inside the oven in a deep plate where melted ice will flow.





Secondly, you need to click on the ‚ÄúAccelerated Defrost‚Äù button on the control panel and select the corresponding mode with additional presses. For example, if you need to defrost a fish, then you should press the ‚ÄúQuick Defrost‚Äù button until the number 3 appears on the display.

Thirdly, it remains only to select the weight of the product using the "100 G" key and then click on "Start". The microwave will independently select the right time for defrosting and the required power. Very comfortably.

Overview of Modes

And now the most interesting thing is an overview of cooking modes in the Samsung ME83KRS-2 microwave oven. There are 4 of them: "Russian cuisine", "Diet menu", "Soup / Porridge" and "Drinks".





"Russian kitchen"

The first menu contains as many as 18 programs for automatic cooking. The cooking method can be found in the instructions, everything is described in detail there. The most interesting from this menu look dishes of fish and poultry. Also worth noting is the ability to quickly bake potatoes. In short, the "Russian Cuisine" allows you to cook a hearty and quick dinner.

"Diet menu"

In the second mode, 10 recipes are collected. Basically, these are vegetables and some cereals. This menu will be useful to those who adhere to any diets or fasting. In this mode, it is convenient to cook breakfasts.





"Soup / Porridge"

Using a microwave Samsung ME83KRS-2 you can cook a very good breakfast of oatmeal. The "Soup / Porridge" menu allows you to make a serving of oatmeal cooked, as on a stove, in just 6 minutes. In addition, in the mode there are programs for cooking borsch, several types of soups, hodgepodge and more.

"Beverages"

The last mode is drinks. The choice is small, just dried fruit compote and cranberry juice, but it's still better than nothing. Compote, by the way, is quite good. Not like freshly brewed, but tasty.
