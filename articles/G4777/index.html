Where to relax abroad in the winter? A fabulous winter vacation for every taste

Unfortunately, not everyone succeeds in taking a vacation in the warm season. However, the cold winter season opens up much more opportunities for you, because you are able to completely plan your vacation depending on your own preferences. So where to relax abroad in the winter?



In pursuit of warmth

In this regard, Egypt is considered a classic of the genre with its attractive weather, almost unchanged at different times of the year, many excellent hotels with excellent service, adequate prices and rich culture. The second most popular place is occupied by the UAE, where, among other things, you can enjoy great shopping (especially if you go here closer to February, when the famous shopping festival is held in Dubai). When deciding where to relax abroad in the winter, you can opt for Thailand. In addition to the exotic nature and guaranteed vivid impressions, you will receive another nice bonus - for Russian citizens, entry into this country is visa-free. Other options, no less interesting in all respects, are Goa (India), Cuba, Sri Lanka, Vietnam, Spain, Dominican Republic and the Canary Islands. All these countries welcome their guests with the warm rays of the sun, well-warmed sea waters and comfortable hotels for every taste.

Winter is 100%

Does a beach holiday in the midst of winter seem boring and uninteresting to you? Then, when planning where to relax abroad in the winter, you can choose one of many other options. Austria is traditionally a favorite destination for ski lovers among magical snowy landscapes. Superbly equipped slopes, high-quality equipment and experienced instructors will help you get the most out of your trip.





Another interesting option where to relax in Europe in winter is Switzerland. Surrounded by the fabulous beauty of the mountains, this country will allow you to enjoy one of any popular winter sports, try out traditional hot fondue, as well as the famous chocolate as a dessert. If you are planning a family winter vacation, Finland is the best fit for you. In spirit, this country is extremely close to Russia, at least thanks to its famous ‚Äúbathhouse‚Äù tradition. And it is here that the world-famous residence of Santa Claus is located. Extreme lovers will find a real paradise on earth, going to Iceland, which has long been considered the best place for climbers.

Choosing where to relax abroad in the winter, be sure to pay attention to tours to Germany. This country is interesting in itself, and on the eve of Christmas and New Year, it becomes completely similar to the illustration from an old postcard. Going to Germany, do not miss the opportunity to visit the traditional Christmas market. In addition, those who do not know where to relax in the winter abroad can find magnificent ski resorts and explore the memorable places of such countries as France, the Czech Republic, Turkey and Bulgaria. And, of course, a vacation spent in the Italian Alps will be unforgettable.





Whatever choice you make, you will not be disappointed and get a unique chance to see familiar countries in a completely different light!
