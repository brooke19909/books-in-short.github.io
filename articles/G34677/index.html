How to set up a satellite dish yourself

Information technology is currently striking at a rapid pace of development, providing more and more opportunities for modern man. Despite the gradual development of Internet television, satellite broadcasting remains in first place in terms of popularity and broadcast quality, since it can give a clear picture of a digital image at any time of the day or night. That is why many people are thinking about how to set up a satellite dish and is it possible to do this on their own. The answer here will be in the affirmative, since it is quite accessible to every person, subject to a careful study of the technology for setting up satellite equipment.

So, first of all, it should be remembered that the most popular satellites that broadcast today 73 Russian-language channels are Sirius, Amos and Hotbird. The main advantage of installing a dish is its price, because sooner or later you will fully pay for the cost of satellite equipment. Indeed, most television channels are broadcast without encoding and therefore completely free.

How to set up a satellite dish, and what is needed for this? To highlight this issue, we start with a kit of a standard set of satellite dishes:

  1. Mirror (plate) - serves to receive a signal from a satellite, and can have sizes from 60 to 129 cm in diameter.
  2. Converter - is a device for transcoding the signal to the frequency with which the following device works - the receiver.
  3. DVC - Receiver - directly receives the decrypted signal from the converter and transmits it to your digital device (TV, plasma, and so on). In addition, DiSEq (disek), a device for connecting several converters into a single unit, as well as cables and brackets, are also included in the standard delivery package. And, of course, you should not forget about high-quality mounting devices, with the help of which you will need to direct the dish to the satellite in a south-westerly direction.

There are many manuals where you can find the answer to the question of how to set up a satellite dish yourself, however, if you analyze many of them, then all the work can be reduced to several stages:

1. Preparation of equipment for installation. At this stage, according to the instructions, you assemble the plate itself by attaching brackets and all other technical parts to it.
