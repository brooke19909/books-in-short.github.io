Fethiye, Turkey: prices, tours and reviews from tourists

Turkey is one of the few countries that can be proud of its ecological diversity. This is the only country that combines notes of Asia, Africa and Europe. Among this stunning nature in the picturesque greenery of the mountains lies the small resort town of Fethiye. Thanks to the fresh air of pine and cedar forests, developed hotel infrastructure and clean ecology, thousands of tourists want to spend their holidays here. Tours in Fethiye (Turkey) are very popular with both couples and young people. People who prefer a relaxing holiday come here. He even got his name from tourists - the turquoise coast.



Geographical information, or Where is Fethiye

If you find Fethiye on the map, you will see that the resort is located in the southwestern part of Turkey, at the foot of Uludag (Mazi Olympus), in the amazingly beautiful bay of the Aegean Sea, which is hidden among the mountains covered with coniferous forests. 40 kilometers away is Dalaman Airport. It is 200 kilometers from the famous resort town of Antaliego, and 135 kilometers from Marmaris. And 11 kilometers from the city is the wonderful Oludeniz Bay, where about 50 resort hotels are located.



Bit of history

The first settlement on the territory of modern Fitkhiye (Turkey) was founded by Greek sailors in the 5th century and was called Telmessos (City of Light). The city flourished at a rapid pace and by the end of this century had become a major port of economic importance. This could not fail to attract the attention of neighboring states, and it was constantly conquered by the people of the Achmenids, then the Romans, then Christians and Byzantines. When in 1390 the city became part of the Ottoman Empire, it received a new name - Meghri. The resort got its current name in 1914 in honor of the first military pilot Fethi Bey. Due to the fact that the city belonged to different peoples, each of which left a certain mark, there were a huge number of ancient unique sights, but two strong earthquakes that occurred in 1856-1957. destroyed most of them. In its original form, only an ensemble of rock tombs located around the city has been preserved. The most beautiful of them is the tomb of the 3-4th centuries. BC with the name "Garden of Almond Trees."



Climate and weather resort town

The weather of Fethiye also has a significant impact on the number of visitors to this city. People come here because of the mild microclimate, which is due to the proximity of mountains and the bay. In summer, the air temperature is 30-34 degrees Celsius, which allows you to enjoy plenty of beach vacations. July is considered the hottest, on some days of which the air temperature warms up to 40 degrees Celsius. The most favorable period for rest are May-June and August-September. During this period, the air warms up to 30 degrees Celsius in the afternoon, and water - up to 25-27 degrees Celsius. May and September are characterized by cooler nights, which distinguishes Fethiye from other resorts in Turkey. This is due to the fact that the Aegean Sea is much colder than the Mediterranean. In winter, January and February are considered the coldest months. The air temperature here drops to 12 degrees Celsius. As the reviews of vacationers show, during this period people come here who want to see all the attractions of the resort. The most unfavorable for visiting is December, since during this period the greatest amount of precipitation falls.



Tours in Fethiye

Holidays in Fethiye (Turkey) is very popular with tourists. They are attracted here not by archaeological sites, but by magnificent landscapes. People come here to see the beautiful and amazing local nature: spruce forests, pine forests, flowering acacia, myrtle, laurel. Here you can see the rarest plant - the Frankinsky tree. The coast in the vicinity of Fethiye is severely indented, which led to the formation of numerous bays and islands. In addition to excellent landscapes, the resort has a developed infrastructure that allows you to enjoy your vacation in this city as much as possible. Here are excellent beaches and a huge number of various entertainments that make your vacation unique and unforgettable.



The beaches of the "turquoise coast"

As mentioned above, Fethiye has excellent beaches, most of which are municipal. Here is one of the largest pebble-sand beaches called Calis, which stretches for several kilometers, combining several secluded bays along it in one chain. This is one of the best ecological beaches in Turkey. If you walk along the coastline, you can see a wide sand spit that goes into the sea. The beach of this bay is called "Cleopatra". A little south of it is a paradise with unspoilt nature called "Oludeniz" with its Blue Lagoon. Most of the beaches here are paid, only Belgegiz is free. Unlike many resorts in the country, all beaches are clean. That is why many people seek vacation in Fethiye (Turkey). The sea, as a rule, is completely calm here in the morning, and by noon a small breeze appears. A steady fresh sea wind allows you to spend on the beach even the hottest hours of the day.



Entertainment

Holidays in a city such as Fethiye (Turkey) will give you the opportunity to fly on a steam plane. From a bird's eye view opens up a beautiful panorama of the coast with numerous scattered islands. Thanks to the steep steep slopes, which are many in the vicinity of the city, you can fly over the meadows and forests on a hang glider. It offers vacationers rafting. Extreme lovers can take part in a jeep safari. Rest will not be complete without boat trips on a yacht. In addition, Fethiye has not only magnificent sea and beaches. There are many underwater tuff caves, especially near Oludeniz. This is a paradise for diving enthusiasts.



Accommodation during the holidays

An important role during the rest is played by comfortable accommodation. Like numerous tourist cities, the resort of Fethiye (Turkey) offers its guests private pensions and excellent hotels of various categories, where, regardless of class, tourists will enjoy high-class service. Conventionally, the resort of Fethiye can be divided into two parts. In one, the best Turkish hotels are located, such as Club Hotel Letoonia, Hill Side Beach and others that offer an elite expensive vacation. Each of the complexes is located in a separate bay, so all the entertainment is concentrated on its territory. The second part of Fethiye is Oludeniz Bay, where about 50 hotels of various classes are concentrated. Here you can choose a club hotel, and a quiet one, designed for families. But all of them are famous for their favor for Russian tourists, so in each of them there is a translator from Russian. Many of the hotels have fitness rooms, discoclubs and TV salons. All hotels provide a varied diet, mainly on an all-inclusive basis. In the evening, entertaining evenings and a variety of shows are held. Especially popular with tourists are additional services, including a Turkish bath, billiards, bowling, massage and others. During leisure excursion trips are organized. The hotel staff is very attentive and polite with their guests.



Holiday Prices

The cost of the tour in Fethiye (Turkey) at different times of the year is significantly different. In addition, an important role is played by the category of the hotel when placing. On average, all tours offered are 8 days long. The most popular, as the reviews show, is a tour with accommodation at Akdeniz Beach Hotel Oludeniz 3 *. Its cost is from 489 USD per person. Tours with accommodation at Tropicana Oludeniz Hotel 3 *, Club Pink Palace 3 * have about the same cost. One of the leaders among tourists was a tour with accommodation at Orient Resort Hotel 4 *, the cost of which, taking into account air travel, is from 370 USD. For those who want to relax with the best comfort, it is recommended to pay attention to the tour with accommodation at Hillside Beach Club 4 *, the duration of which is, on average, 7 nights. Its cost for 2 adults is 3640-3820 USD. Hot tours to Fethiye are very popular, as their cost quite often decreases by 57-59%.



Attractions Fethiye

In addition to beach recreation and entertainment, Fethiye (Turkey) has many interesting places and structures that are popular with tourists. In the thickness of the mountain cliffs, you can see huge Lycian sarcophagi having an unusual shape. It will be interesting to visit the tomb of Amintas, which is carved into the rock in the form of a small temple. Nearby is an old mosque, the construction of which dates back to the 18th century. Many excavations were made here, the findings of which are stored in the city museum. The ancient amphitheater, which testifies to the fact that the Romans once lived on this earth, deserves attention. And the ruins of a medieval castle tell of the Rhodes knights. But this is not all that can be seen while relaxing in Fethiye (Turkey). Reviews of tourists tell a lot about the Valley of the Butterflies, which attracts with its unique beauty and lush vegetation. And going to the mountain village of Yuzumlu, located near the resort, you can visit the stone house, built in the Ottoman style. In this settlement, ancient rites and customs have been preserved. In the vicinity of Fethiye there are many ruins of ancient cities.





Shopping

To diversify your stay in the wonderful resort town of Fethiye (Turkey), a shopping trip will help. As in many Turkish cities, there is a huge selection of handmade carpet products. Tourists can purchase chic openwork gold jewelry and leather goods. Famous oriental sweets are especially popular among city guests.

How to get to the resort

50 kilometers from Fithiye is the International Dalaman Airport. In addition, Fethiye operates flights from Izmir, Istanbul, Antalya and Ankara.
