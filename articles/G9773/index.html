Hotel Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 *, Greece, Peloponnese: photos, reviews

Greece is a country that is loved by many tourists from Russia and from neighboring countries. Its territory has a mild, pleasant climate that attracts travelers. In the midst of the spa season, it is sunny and has a high temperature. However, despite this, vacationers do not languish from the heat, thanks to the Mediterranean air masses that envelop the Peloponnese peninsula.

Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * is a hotel that many Russian tour operators recommend for relaxation. Its great popularity is due to the fact that highly qualified personnel work here, who responsibly performs the work entrusted to him, providing the most comfortable conditions for each tourist to live. In addition, many travelers in their reviews note that they were attracted by the low cost of the trip.



Location

Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * is located on the Peloponnese, on the coastline washed by the Mediterranean Sea (Patras Current). It is located in a quiet and secluded place, which is ideal for a romantic holiday for two. At a considerable distance from it is a lively village - the city of Patra. The nearest international airport is 10 kilometers from the hotel, which provides easy access for tourists.

general information

In the official description of the hotel Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * it is said that this vacation spot was built in 1991. Since then, up to now, repair work has been regularly carried out on its territory and in buildings, for which non-resort time is chosen. The hotel is ready to receive guests throughout the resort period, which lasts from May to October - at a time when the air temperature is as hot as possible and the water in the sea is warmed to the optimum level.



Rooms

The total number of apartments in the hotel is small - 50. All of them are located in a large two-story building, and some are located in small bungalows, which are also comfortably furnished.

Guests often attach photos of Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * to their reviews, which show the stunning interior decoration of the apartments. As they say in their comments, living in these rooms is very comfortable, here you can tune in to an excellent vacation and relaxation.

All apartments that are available in the hotel are presented in one category - standard. They are designed to populate a different number of people (from 1 to 3).



Rooms in the building

The apartments, which are located in the main building of the Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 *, offer beautiful views of the mountains or the sea and gardens. Each of them has modern air conditioners, with which you can easily adjust the temperature conditions in the room to optimal. The attendants regularly change towels and linen, as well as clean the room - three times a week.

As for the furniture equipment, the required number of beds in the form of beds with hard mattresses has been installed, there are cabinets for storing personal belongings, as well as bedside tables on which vacationers can put small items. Tourists can use a mechanical safe for storing valuables, as well as a refrigerator for free. Here on the wall there is a large plasma TV with the ability to view satellite channels, there is also a telephone.

All apartments have a private bathroom with shower, toilet and sink. Guests are provided with a complete set of bath accessories, as well as a hairdryer for drying hair.



Bungalow rooms

Each bungalow in Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * is an individual, separate one-story room, which overlooks the sea, the pool or the garden. There is a bedroom with a large double bed. Some bungalows are divided into two rooms - each of them has one of the same beds. Apartments of the second type are designed to accommodate four people in them, and often they are chosen for a family vacation, often with a child.

All bungalows have a private bathroom with amenities and bath amenities. It also has personal air conditioning.



Living conditions for children

In a quiet place in which Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * (Greece, Peloponnese) is located, families often come to relax, and even with small children. That is why the hotel has created a number of conditions that improve the living of this category of guests. In particular, there is a small pool designed for bathing babies, which is part of a large one. Children can also spend time on a playground specially equipped for outdoor activities.

Parents of the youngest guests of the hotel have the opportunity to take a crib, this can be done on request. During a visit to a local cafe, children can be seated on special comfortable chairs; a personal menu is offered here, which includes bright and mouth-watering dishes.

Nutrition

Tourists vacationing at the Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * have the opportunity to eat in a local cafe, where gourmets are presented with delicious and nutritious dishes of Mediterranean, European and Greek cuisine. For guests who rest on an all-inclusive basis, a daily buffet is organized here, where, according to guest reviews, original snacks and main dishes are presented.

The hotel also has one restaurant, which can be visited only on a fee basis (the dishes served in it are not included in the price of the all-inclusive package), as well as a bar located by the pool.



Pool

The hotel has one outdoor pool, which is divided into two zones: for adults and for children. This place is equipped taking into account all safety standards, water is regularly cleaned here, so it is always crystal clear. Around the pool there is a large sun terrace, equipped with comfortable sun loungers and beach umbrellas, which save vacationers from direct sunlight. Nearby there is a bar where guests can enjoy light snacks, salads, choose some delicious desserts for themselves or sip refreshments and bright cocktails.

Sport

Fans of a sports lifestyle in their reviews of Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * (Peloponnese) share comments on the large hall, which has several simulators designed to train different muscle groups. Tourists are also pleased with the fact that his visit is completely free. If desired, vacationers can visit the tennis court.

Beach

The beach line that runs along the hotel is washed by the clean and warm waters of the Mediterranean Sea. The coast is literally ten meters from the hotel and is closed to outsiders. Tourists who relax here have an excellent opportunity to enjoy a magnificent view of the environment in the form of mountains, a garden and endless sea open spaces.

The beach is always clean and beautiful, everything is arranged in such a way that it is convenient for guests to relax. In particular, for greater comfort of guests there are several locker rooms, showers, as well as public toilets. Tourists can sit on comfortable sun loungers under huge umbrellas, but rent will have to be paid for their use.



Services

For all vacationers in Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 * a wide range of services is provided. In particular, all guests can take the opportunity to get advice from the administrator, who is located around the clock at the reception (reception). Tourists note that many of them speak Russian, which greatly facilitates communication for guests from Russia and the CIS countries. Here you can also get a fresh press in your room free of charge, and for a fee, it seems possible to rent a luggage storage room.

Hotel guests can always exchange currency at a special point or withdraw cash from plastic cards using an ATM. For guests with cars there is a free secure parking. By the way, a car can be rented here, but its cost is paid according to a separate price list.

All interested tourists can use the opportunity of free access to the Internet (Wi-Fi), which is available throughout the hotel. With this service, vacationers always stay in touch with their family and friends.



Guest reviews

In social networks and on thematic sites you can find a lot of reviews about Niforeika Beach Hotel Bungalows 3 *, left by tourists here. Often, colorful photographs taken on the territory of the hotel and in its rooms are attached to them.

So, guests in their comments share their impressions gained during the rest. They talk about the fact that the rooms of the main building are very comfortable, here you can comfortably sit on very comfortable beds and use modern furniture. The general atmosphere here is homely, which makes for a pleasant holiday. Parents vacationing with young children note that the hotel is not very designed for children, but nevertheless some conditions for this category of guests are provided.

As for infrastructure, travelers celebrate deliciously cooked food in restaurants and a wonderful buffet from which guests are treated. Here you can paddle time in the pool with warm water or on a clean beach.

The general conditions of tourists are pleasing, so often they plan to return to this hotel again and again, and also recommend that their relatives and friends relax here.
