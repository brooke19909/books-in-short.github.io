Women's poncho with knitting needles. Schemes and recommendations

With the onset of cold weather, I want to wrap ourselves in warm voluminous knitted things. One such thing is a poncho. It does not constrain movements, is combined with almost any clothing and for several seasons has not left the catwalks. There are a lot of variations of models: from frankly ethnic to youth and even sexual. The poncho can be dense, with voluminous braids and fringe, shortened with wide sleeves or light and delicate.

Even a novice needlewoman will have no difficulty knitting a poncho for herself . Patterns can be found both in magazines or on knitting sites, or come up with yourself. Consider what models are fashionable capes, patterns for them and patterns.

Poncho Story

Homeland Poncho - South America. This element of traditional Native American clothing was made from a rectangular piece of fabric. In the center there was an opening for the head, and the embroidery itself, which was characteristic of a particular tribe, and fringe along the hem decorated the canvas. The Europeans who mastered the continent, then really liked this cloak, and they gladly brought it to their wardrobe.





In the 60s of the last century, the poncho was at the peak of fashion. It was worn by a bohemian: artists, musicians and simply fashionable loafers. Complemented with wide-brimmed hats, high boots, boots or flared trousers. Just at that time, poncho knitting needles appeared. Patterns were transmitted to each other, supplemented and reached our time. And since today the 60s is back in fashion, it's time to knit yourself an trendy poncho.

How to knit a poncho?

How to knit a poncho with needles, if not a lot of experience in this type of needlework? There are several options for simple styles. Depending on the model, the cape can be knitted on both circular and straight knitting needles. The knitted model does not require stitching. To do this, dial the number of loops for the hole on top. The head hole can be wide and fall off one shoulder, V-shaped or with a dense high elastic collar. Having decided on the neck, continue to knit in a circle, making additions in each row so that the product looks like a circle when unfolded. The length of the product can be any: a shortened cloak to the elbow, to the middle of the thigh or longer. This knitting poncho for women is one of the easiest and fastest. Especially if you work with thick bulk yarn.





Another model of poncho knitting needles for beginners is knitted from rectangles with a decorated neckline and shoulder seams. This option is good because you do not need to do the reduction and addition of loops, but you can completely concentrate on the figure.

Warm poncho

Knitting winter and demi-season poncho for women made of warm wool yarn. If you decide to use 100% wool, choose one that does not prick, it will cost more, but it will not cause irritation on the skin. Also, for a warm wrap, yarn made of wool and acrylic or mohair is suitable. The winter model can be decorated with a jacquard pattern, for example, imitating the ethnic patterns of the Indian tribes, or Scandinavian patterns with deers, snowflakes and Christmas trees.





Arana and braids are another option for decorating a poncho with knitting needles. Braid patterns are fairly easy to read, and even a novice can handle them, but if you are not confident in your abilities, it is better to start with simpler weaves. To knit arana, you will need additional knitting needles.

On some demi-season models there are sleeves, so the hands remain warm. Sleeves can be connected as a smooth surface, repeating the pattern of the canvas, and an elastic band, echoing with the collar.

Summer poncho

Knitting poncho with knitting needles for women is an occupation not only in the winter, but also in the summer. The cloak can be not only cozy warm, but also light, and even beach. To do this, you need to choose a thin yarn, for example, cotton. Viscose is suitable for the beach model, as it dries quickly and is not as heavy as cotton.





What pattern to choose for knitting a summer poncho with knitting needles? Schemes with openwork patterns, from alternations of yarn and two loops connected together, are perfect. This can be an imitation of a large or small mesh, a complex pattern, for example, with flowers. The pattern for the summer poncho can be seen at the shawls and stoles.

To better understand how to knit a poncho with knitting needles, you just need to get started. You will be surprised how easy and enjoyable this process is.
