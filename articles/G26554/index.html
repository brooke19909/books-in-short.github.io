How to put the promised payment on MTS

The easiest way to activate the service is the USSD command. On a mobile phone, you need to dial the combination * 111 * 123 # and the call key and then follow the prompts that pop up on the screen. Through the operator

There is another opportunity to put the promised payment on MTS in Russia. How to do it:

How much is it

Three days after the activation of the service, the amount of payment and a small commission are debited. If the "Promised payment" was not more than 30 rubles, then the commission is not charged - the service is considered free. In other cases, the fee depends on the amount requested:

  • from 31 to 99 rubles - seven rubles;
  • from 100 to 199 rubles - ten rubles;
  • from 200 to 499 rubles - twenty-five rubles;
  • more than five hundred rubles - fifty rubles.

If on the balance sheet at the time of writing off the payment there is no necessary amount of money, then a service fee will be charged at the time of replenishment of the account.

Many users of communication services are interested in the question of how to put the promised payment on MTS with a minus. The ways of connecting the service are the same as with a positive balance.
