88 Hotel Patong Phuket 3 *: description and photo

If you decide to relax in the country of smiles in Thailand and are looking for a budget option to stay within walking distance from the main tourist attractions and the sea, then 88 Hotel Patong Phuket 3 * may suit you.



Location

This hotel is located in Phuket (Thailand), in a residential quarter of Patong. The tourist infrastructure center - Bangla Road - can be reached in just 10 minutes on foot. Patong Beach is also within walking distance. So, according to hotel guests, you can walk to it with a leisurely step in 12-15 minutes. In the vicinity of 88 Hotel Patong Phuket 3 * there are many cafes, restaurants, bars, shops, a shopping center, a market, companies offering excursions and rental cars and motorbikes, and much more.



General information, photo

88 Hotel (Patong Beach, Thailand) is a city hotel. So he does not have his own territory. In total, the hotel building has 8 floors, the last of which is equipped with an outdoor small pool with a sun terrace and several sunbeds. So if you have no desire to go to the sea, then you can refresh yourself without leaving the hotel.

The housing stock of the hotel in question consists of 30 comfortable cozy rooms. For the convenience of guests, the hotel building is equipped with an elevator. In addition to the rooms and the pool, there is a 24-hour reception desk, an Internet cafe and a dining room.

It is worth noting that the hotel 88 Hotel Patong Phuket 3 * reviews from tourists receive mostly positive. This is evidenced by his rating, which is 4.2 points out of five maximum possible. According to our compatriots who have been here, the hotel is perfect for active travelers who prefer to spend most of their time on the beach, excursions and walks in the vicinity.



Rooms

The apartments in this hotel as a whole, the guests were quite satisfied. According to them, they have everything you need: comfortable beds, spacious cabinets, a TV with cable channels, a refrigerator, air conditioning, a hairdryer and a bathroom. Tiled flooring. Please note that there are no safes in the rooms. However, experienced tourists are not advised to leave valuables unattended. Therefore, it will be safest to deposit them in a safe at the reception.

As already mentioned, the hotel has a total of 30 rooms. They are represented by the following categories: standard, superior (have a balcony) and deluxe (with a separate bedroom). Our compatriots staying here recommend to settle on the upper floors, if possible. This will provide you with a more beautiful view from the window. In addition, the noise coming from the street will less disturb you.

Also note that upon check-in you will be asked to make a deposit of 500 baht. This amount will be returned to you upon departure.

As for cleaning, it is carried out every day. Maids change towels and bedding several times a week. Also, as they are spent, they replenish personal hygiene products in the bathroom.



Nutrition

The cost of living at 88 Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Patong, Thailand) includes breakfast only. They are held in the hotel's dining room from 8 to 11 in the morning. Breakfast here, according to tourists, monotonous. So, in the morning you can eat omelet, scrambled eggs, sausages, bacon, ham, cornflakes, toast, coffee and orange juice. During the day, travelers can eat in a cafe in the vicinity of the hotel, especially since there is no shortage of them here. The food is delicious everywhere, according to our compatriots, and the prices are pleasantly pleasing. Also, you can always have a bite to eat, having bought, for example, pancakes with banana filling, in macaroons (mobile food stalls). In addition, experienced travelers are advised to visit the local market. Here you can buy the freshest seafood, which you will immediately cook for a small surcharge.

Sea

As already mentioned, Patong Beach from 88 Hotel Patong Phuket 3 * can be reached in a leisurely step in a quarter of an hour. The beach here is municipal, quite large and clean. Sun loungers and parasols can be rented all day for an extra charge. There are a lot of cafes and bars on the beach where you can have a bite to eat and purchase drinks if you wish. Experienced tourists are also advised to visit other beaches in Phuket (Kata, Kata Noi, Karon and others). You can do this on a tuk-tuk or by renting a motobike. As for beach towels, they are issued free of charge at the hotel.
