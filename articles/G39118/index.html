A brief overview of the smartphone "Nokia 1320"

The official debut of the Nokia Lumia 1320 smartphone, a review of which is presented in this article, took place in Abu Dhabi last year. As stated by representatives of the manufacturer during the presentation, in this device, the developers sacrificed high-quality filling for the sake of mass and budget.



general description

The dimensions of the device are quite impressive and are 164.2x85.9x9.8 mm in height, width and thickness, respectively. Due to such a large size, using a smartphone with one hand is almost impossible. As for its weight, it is equal to 220 grams. The case of the model is made of matte plastic, which was a pretty good solution for the Nokia Lumia 1320. The reviews of its owners have become a clear confirmation that the smartphone fits comfortably in the hand and does not slip out of it. The novelty is available in black, white, yellow and red. On the rear panel you can see the speaker, flash and lens. It should be noted that the device is equipped with a battery, which cannot be removed. Camera

The model is equipped with a camera with a resolution of five megapixels. It was inherited from the Lumia 625 smartphone. Equipment

A pretty good performance smartphone provides a Snapdragon-400 processor, which consists of two cores. Each of them operates at a frequency of 1.7 GHz. The device has 8 gigabytes of stationary memory. It should be noted that about a quarter of it is required to meet the needs of the system, and therefore this part of the space is inaccessible to the user. Be that as it may, the Nokia 1320 provides a slot for installing additional media. The size of RAM in this model is 1 gigabyte. The novelty supports almost all types of modern wireless connections. In general, thanks to such technical equipment, demanding applications are launched and work without too much delay.



Offline work

As already noted above, the model is equipped with a stationary battery. Its capacity is 3400 mAh. Given the far from the most advanced characteristics of the device, a full charge lasts about three days in standby mode, for one hour of continuous conversations, or three to four hours of using the Internet. Even some of the flagship smartphones cannot boast of such an indicator.

Sound

The Nokia 1320 has just one speaker. It is located on the back of the device. Two small tubercles are provided on the sides of it, which serve to protect the speaker from pollution and salinization. As with incoming calls, and when listening to music, the sound is smooth and loud. There are no special complaints about the telephone speaker. After all, the interlocutor is heard clearly.

The music player has exactly the same settings and functions as similar applications from more expensive modifications from this manufacturer. Due to the fact that the audio capabilities of the device, the developers decided not to cut back, the recorded compositions and radio sound without any distortion, including in the headphones.
