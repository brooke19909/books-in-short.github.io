TM Air-O-Swiss: a brief overview of the lineup

A Swiss company specializing in the manufacture of humidifiers has introduced three main types of devices to the market:

- traditional;

- ultrasound;

- steam.

The simplest and most inexpensive models have a simple design, which is based on the principle of cold evaporation. The most common are AOS E2441, AOS E2241, AOS E2251. Their design consists of a fan and a filter that humidifies the air. The indisputable advantage of such models is silent operation. They are ideal for installation in rooms such as the bedroom and the nursery.

The modern ultrasonic humidifier Air-O-Swiss stands out against the general background of the models, as it has many advantages. Like the first option, it does not emit extraneous sounds at all during operation. This effect is achieved by minimizing the amount of steam. It is worth paying attention to the high power of such devices and ease of operation. They are completely safe for children. The humidifier itself has a fairly compact size and is equipped with sensors that automatically control the humidity level in the room.





Steam devices are slightly different from the above. First of all, the fact that the principle of their work is to heat the water. Such humidifiers can be used in rooms with a large area, for example, in greenhouses, museums, libraries. Due to its high performance, it is easy to maintain a moisture level in the range of 60-70%. However, these devices have pronounced disadvantages. This is the noise they emit during operation, the lack of humidity control and the consumption of large amounts of electricity.

Highlights When Choosing Humidifiers

A person who does not have detailed information about humidifiers needs to know the main points that will come in handy when purchasing devices of this type. Recommendations are selected based on customer reviews.

The first thing that is most important when buying a device is the technical parameters. What exactly is included here:

  • tank volume - its capacity is directly proportional to the duration of continuous operation of the device;
  • functionality, for example, the absence or presence of automatic control of the humidity level or the option of self-switching off when the water is completely evaporated;
  • performance - selected depending on the size of the premises;
  • noise level is a very important parameter, especially if a humidifier is needed for rooms intended for relaxation.




The second stage is the selection of additional functions. Here TM Air-O-Swiss did not stint. Almost all products of this brand have the following options:

  • humidity sensor;

  • hydrometer;

  • auto power off;
  • neutralization of allergens;
  • ionization.
