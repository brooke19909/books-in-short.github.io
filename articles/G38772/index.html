Hotel Exotica Hotel by Zante Plaza 4 * Greece: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Hotel Exotica Hotel by Zante Plaza 4 * invites fans of exotic holidays to its territory who prefer excellent conditions at a low cost.

Many tour operators offer this place for all those who want to spend time with their family, which may include small children. Often it is chosen as the venue for a romantic trip with a loved one.



general information

Built in the late 90s, the hotel was completely renovated in the winter of 2016. At present, in its relatively small territory there are five modern three-story buildings, in which the entire number of rooms is located. During the last repair work, furniture filling was completely updated in his rooms and high-quality repairs were made, which the guests had already managed to turn their attention to. According to them, the internal atmosphere prevailing in the apartments sets up every tourist who is relaxing here to have a rather pleasant pastime.

Location

Exotica Hotel by Zante Plaza 4 * is located in Greece, in the central part of the small village of Kalamaki, next to another resort town - Laganas. This is a fairly quiet place where there is no noisy entertainment. This, as noted by travelers, contributes to a favorable rest with young children, whose peace easily breaks any noise.

The nearest airport is located three kilometers from the Exotica Hotel by Zante Plaza 4 *, which, of course, is popular with tourists - from it you can reach the recreation center in a short time.

Beach

The hotel is quite far from the beach - 600 meters, it does not have its own private territory on it.

Tourists living in the Exotica Hotel & Spa by Zante Plaza (Greece) very often tell about their impressions received during the visit to the city beach, which, according to them, is quite well arranged for relaxation. It is quite clean, the banks here are not steep, and throughout the territory there are evenly arranged amenities in the form of public toilets, shower cabins and locker rooms.

If desired, tourists can rent sun loungers and umbrellas - for them rent is paid. There is also a rental point for towels and air mattresses. Prices

The approximate cost of a ticket to the hotel is often reflected in the comments left by tourists that rested in it. On average, it is about 35-40 thousand rubles. As a rule, this price includes transportation costs on both sides, all medical insurance necessary for entry into Greece, as well as a weekly rental of a double room. If desired, tourists can take advantage of additional services that are provided here, however this is not included in the price of the tourist package.

Some operators may offer to include in the package price the cost of a tour of local attractions, but experienced travelers do not recommend this, since in Greece their cost is much cheaper.
