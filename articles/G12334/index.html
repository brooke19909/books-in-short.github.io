The best books of Alexander Elder

Alexander Elder is a well-known professional trader, consultant and expert in exchange trading. The author of many articles and books on this topic. Elder's 1993 work, entitled "How to Play and Win on the Exchange," became an international bestseller (translated into 12 languages) and has survived several editions. In a professional environment, the book has received great recognition. But this is not the only work of the trader that is worthy of attention. The article will feature the best books by Alexander Elder. So let's get started.

"How to play and win on the stock exchange"

This most popular work of a trader has already been mentioned. According to Elder, success in the stock market game depends on three factors: methods, psychology and risk control. At the beginning of the book, Alexander shows that the keys to success lie in psychology. A trader teaches how to develop discipline and avoid emotional traps. Then he talks about the search algorithm for profitable deals using charts, computer indicators and other analysis tools. Based on this information, readers will be able to develop their own system by combining different methods. And in the end, Elder will teach you how to properly manage funds in a brokerage account.

Being a successful trader, Alexander not only reveals the secrets of skill, but also offers 200 exchange tasks with solutions. Trading methods in the futures, stock, foreign exchange and other markets are presented in a very accessible form. Readers will learn how to achieve success in the stock market game, save money during failures and significantly increase capital in profitable trading sessions. Few trading books can teach such things. We go further.

"Basics of exchange trading"

This book by Alexander Elder considers work in the financial markets from the very basics to the professional level. Tells in detail about the construction of indicators. Alexander pays special attention to the interpretation of the market from the perspective of the psychological interaction of traders. The doctor also introduced the "Three Screens" system.

This trading book perfectly describes what is going on in the mind of the exchange and how it solves the problems that arise in front of all market participants. Therefore, it is worth taking into service all the ideas listed in it and adapt them to your own trading style.
