Water park "Dzhubga" - reviews of tourists, features and interesting facts

Krasnodar Territory - warm, welcoming, gladly accepts guests at any time. For summer entertainment tourists are provided with a lot of opportunities, including water rides. Rest in the water park "Dzhubga" is available from May to September. The recreation area is open and is located near the Black Sea.

Description

In 2009, the water park "Dzhubga" was opened in the Krasnodar Territory. The name of the place of entertainment was given by the resort village, in which one of the largest complexes of water attractions for all tastes and temperament was built. The park spreads over 2.6 hectares. The capacity of the recreation area is 2 thousand people at a time. The open-air water-entertaining ensemble operates from May to September, giving adults and children a lot of positive emotions and outdoor activities with extreme impressions.

In the water park "Dzhubga" to the delight of visitors there are 16 water slides for an adult audience and 12 slopes for children. In addition to them, the park housed pools with additional features, a jacuzzi, food zones, showers and much more. The area around the pools is equipped with sun loungers, recreation areas. The architectural ensemble is complemented by fabulous towers and heroes, animal sculptures, copies of ancient statues and so on.

Vacationers are delivered to the water park by buses and fixed-route taxis; for motorists, free parking for 500 places is equipped. Safety of stay on the slides is provided by instructors telling about safety measures for behavior on one or another hill. A security team is responsible for the order throughout the aquazone. For guests equipped with showers in the form of fantastic crowbars and a free sanitary area.





Entertainment for adults

Would you like to make a trip to the water park "Dzhubga"? Everyone needs to study the reviews about this place before traveling. The water park in the village of Dzhubga was erected by Turkish experts, some types of slides were developed specifically for this project. Entertainment for adults is presented in a wide variety. The demand is for a large slide that resembles a butterfly in its structure with the name "Tsunami". Its height is 10 meters.

The descent along it resembles the movement of a pendulum - a special bot alternately raises the tourist on one side, then on the other, the scope gradually decreases and the visitor enters the pool. This attraction is considered the most constructively complex, and the safety equipment at it is of the highest level - lifeguards are watching the movement of everyone going down the hill. They take the lucky one in the pool. Children under 12 years old are not allowed to enter the hill. No less exciting is the Ufil Flying Bords slide with a large number of intricate bends, sharp descents, high climbs and high speed.

The popular attraction "Big Hall" with a height of 112 meters. The slide is equipped with a tunnel with a 30-meter closed section, where a tourist is waiting for a light show and extreme water flight. The fastest slide is Kamikaze; for less active vacationers, Rafting Slide and Body Slyde are offered. These attractions are equipped with twists and turns, but their passage is connected with the pleasure of flying, without tickling nerves and only with pleasant emotions.

Pools and meals

The largest pool in the Dzhubga is the Lazy River. It is stretched along almost the entire perimeter of the water park and is equipped with a slow flow simulation mechanism. Conveniently located on a swimming vehicle, the tourist travels, exploring all local attractions.

For more active users, swimming pools with wave simulations from calm to meter height are offered. In the water park you can go surfing, for which there is a body of water with special equipment that imitates the waves, quite suitable for beginners and professionals.





In the afternoon, the main pool of the aquazone becomes the venue for a fun foam party, where the main participants are children and adolescents. Everyone who is tired and wants to relax, sunbathe and take a nap, is invited by comfortable sun loungers and the generous Black Sea sun.

The issue of nutrition has been quite successfully resolved - it is proposed to use a cocktail bar with a wide selection of soft drinks and alcoholic drinks. Also on the map of the institution are presented teas, coffee, chocolate and much more.

Nearby there is a pizzeria, in the menu of which you can find salads, several types of pizza, burgers, french fries and much more. The menu of the third restaurant offers traditional dishes of southern and Russian cuisine with a wide selection of salads, snacks, a variety of soups and much more.

Service Rating

Many vacationers, in search of a variety of leisure activities, make a trip to the water park "Dzhubga". Reviews of tourists are mainly focused on entertainment, but services are not left without attention. Positive assessments went to the maintenance staff: cleanliness was noted in all rooms and in the recreation area. Most visitors liked the organization of the process and the availability of safety regulations.

About it is written on the stands near each attraction and on the entrance ticket to the water park "Dzhubga". Rental services for swimming equipment seemed too high and unjustified for the majority, since not everyone had enough bots for comfortable descent from the hills, and deck chairs were often busy. As a result, it was completely incomprehensible to many how to still soak up the sun calmly.





There are significant comments on the rides that Dzhubga Waterpark is proud of. Reviews of vacationers warn that on some slides, even extreme, there are poorly docked parts that form a small step. With a high speed of descent, these shortcomings can lead to serious injuries and some tourists have already seen this.

The reviews also indicate that even on children's slides you can periodically run into the sharp edges of some connecting element or mount. There are real cases when visitors received head injuries when flying out of a closed attraction pipe. Such cases occur infrequently, but vacationers recommend being vigilant.

Many stories are dedicated to instructors and rescuers. Vacationers say that their care was polite and persistent, which saved many children and adults from injuries. Some tourists noted that the staff sometimes does not fulfill their duties or is neglected. It is noted that in most cases, instructors try to shift their own responsibility for the life and health of children to their parents, sometimes without apparent necessity.

In general, most tourists really liked the Dzhubga water park. Reviews and impressions of vacationers are filled with vivid descriptions of emotions, photographs taken by a professional photographer, and wishes to those who were not in this recreation area. Everyone advises to take the time to try all the water rides on their own.
