"Varliana" (Lazarevskoye): unforgettable emotions

Sochi is a popular city for many tourists. Finding decent housing at an affordable price is not easy here. "Varliana" (Lazarevskoye) is a great place to relax as a family, as well as a friendly company or a lone traveler.

Location Features

The complex is located in the village of Lazarevskoye on the street. Sedova, 14. The sea in the immediate vicinity of the hotel, and the height above it is 20 m. The hotel provides a transfer of guests from the airport or railway stations of Sochi. This service must be agreed upon when booking the room or a few days before arrival.

Near the complex you can find cafes and restaurants. Entertainment activities and attractions are also located close to the hotel. Varliana (Lazarevskoye) is located near a pharmacy and various shops - the acquisition of necessary goods will not be difficult.

Accommodations

The complex provides rooms of various capacities and comforts:

  • economy (1st building) - the minimum number of necessary furniture and a fan, located on the floor;
  • with amenities (building 2) - there is a necessary set of furniture in each room; the room has a folding chair for resting the child and a fan or air conditioning;
  • Superior (building 3) - two- or three-room rooms with all amenities, spacious balconies with a table and chairs overlooking the sea.




All rooms have modern and comfortable furniture. The rooms are cleaned every 5 days with the replacement of bed linen. The price includes not only accommodation, but also the use of a playground for children, a swimming pool and parking.

"Varliana" (Lazarevskoye) on its territory has a large number of green spaces and bright flower beds. On the territory there are beautiful walking paths with unusual small decorative figures. In the space provided there are comfortable sunbeds for sunbathing and air baths.





You can book a room by phone.

"Varliana" (Lazarevskoye): reviews and entertainment

The hotel complex has two swimming pools. One is for bathing children, and the other is for adults. The purity and treatment of water under special control by the staff. On the territory there is a modern playground for children to play.





Near gazebos and tables for relaxation, there are fountains of different designs and complexity. Hotel "Varliana" (Lazarevskoye) was created for families with children of different ages. There is where to frolic kids and enjoy the silence of adults.

According to tourists' reviews, there is a cozy cafe-dining room with delicious home and European cuisine. Upon request, a diet menu is available. Here you can buy all kinds of snacks and sweets for children. A good assortment of all kinds of drinks is also provided. The bar is open until 24.00.

Tourists note interesting show programs at the Varliana Hotel (Lazarevskoye), which are held here every 5-7 days. In the evening, you can have fun and dance in the open air or enjoy the multi-level fountains with colorful lights. The territory of the complex is clean and comfortable.
