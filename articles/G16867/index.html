Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay - Sochi Paradise

Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay is a great place to spend your holidays. It is located in Adler, and if you want to relax at sea, sunbathe and just enjoy the good atmosphere, it is worth coming here.





A must-see place

Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay, the private sector of which is considered one of the most popular places among tourists, stretches along the Black Sea coast, next to the Psou and Mzymta rivers . This is a very picturesque place. It is also called the Imereti Valley. The valley is located under Adler (the most comfortable region of Sochi), and its total territory is about 1300 hectares. The Imereti Lowland is a very important wetland that must be preserved as a natural value. Moreover, this is a unique place not only by Russian standards, but also by international standards.





Interesting Facts

Nizhneimeretinskaya bay has many other features. More than a hundred years ago (namely in 1911), the tsarist government of this territory was given the special status of a protected natural zone. It should also be noted that Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay has some geomorphological and climatic features that differ from those inherent in Adler and Sochi. In fact, it was they who influenced the formation of unique biological diversity throughout its territory. And these are reptiles, and amphibians and, of course, birds with insects. In general, this place is a real paradise for connoisseurs of natural values.

A couple of words should be said about the climate. There is high humidity, warm air - while resting in this place, you can not worry about the bad weather and uncomfortable climatic conditions. True, in the last month of summer it can be hot (for people who are not accustomed to the south) - the thermometer column sometimes exceeds 30 degrees.





Location

Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay begins from the place where Tsimlyanskaya Street ends. The district infrastructure was rather poorly developed until the early 2010s, but then, when preparations for the Olympic Games began, it was significantly improved and improved. But what attracted tourists so much up to this point has remained unchanged, namely, a wide beach strip. Indeed, the beaches in these places are indescribable - pebble and sand, there is a place to relax for every taste. In this area there are a large number of different recreation centers, private hotels, hotels, guest houses - so there is where to stay. Moreover, at fairly reasonable prices. Unity with nature

Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay (Adler) is a place significantly different from the rest of Sochi. Firstly, it is distinguished by naturalness and pristine nature. There are not even breakwaters on the beaches. And the sea, by the way, is cleaner than in Sochi itself. Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay, the photo of which demonstrates stunning sea views and amazing southern vegetation, is an ideal vacation spot for those people who dream of breaking out of a metropolis to some quiet and comfortable place to enjoy peace and quiet. Lovers of wild beaches have no place here, because they are not here, and lovers of noisy relaxation and parties should also go to another district of Sochi (in the central or in Adler, for example). But in order to be alone with nature and restore harmony in your soul, the option here is just perfect.
