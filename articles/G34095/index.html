Wonderful Andersengrad in Sosnovy Bor

The city of Andersengrad in Sosnovy Bor appeared not so long ago, only 30 years ago. The reason for its construction was the anniversary of the famous writer Hans Christian Andersen, in whose honor this wonderful and beautiful world of childhood, creativity and games was named.



The city of Andersengrad in Sosnovy Bor is an interesting and unusual way to spend a weekend with a child. On the territory of about 2 hectares a real city was erected, which will certainly impress not only children, but also adults. Inside there is a large number of various figures depicting the heroes of your favorite fairy tales.

Andersengrad in Sosnovy Bor is a real fairy-tale city in which there is something to see. On its territory you can see the real Town Hall tower, bastions, the Tin Soldier tower, the Little Mermaid monument and many interesting structures. Also in the reserve is a summer theater, on the stage of which children's performances are often held. Adults and children can relax and have a meal at the Little Mermaid cafe.

The fabulous city of Andersengrad in Sosnovy Bor is decorated with many colorful illustrations, panels, stained-glass windows. Interesting and beautiful decor adds a fabulous atmosphere and allows you to plunge headlong into the world of childhood. The roofs of houses and towers are decorated with beautiful brick tiles and decorated with weathercocks. Beautiful carved and forged figures will show the exact direction of the wind at any time. The clock tower is located on the town hall tower, and the fairytale city also has its own cannons, an underground passage and a suspension bridge.



Andersengrad in Sosnovy Bor is famous for its colorful and interesting children's parties and performances, which number up to 300 a year. All events are educational and developmental in nature. They are aimed at awakening the interests of children in painting, theater, music, as well as general aesthetic education. Visitors can become participants in grandiose festivals, theatrical productions, music concerts and matinees, fairs and attractions. On the territory of a fabulous city for children, a real road with a gas station has been developed. Kids can ride scooters and bicycles.

How can adults and children get to Andersengrad in Sosnovy Bor? The cost for visiting the fabulous city is not charged. You can get there by driving to Sosnovy Bor in any convenient way. It is located directly in the city between the streets of Solnechnaya and 50 years of October.

So how is it easier and faster to get to Andersengrad? How to get to the fabulous city? By car, drive towards Sosnovy Bor along the Peterhof Highway. The same way can be done on minibus No. 401, which leaves from the Avtovo metro station. The second option is an electric train from the Baltic Station. It is necessary to get to the Kalishche station, from where bus number 5 runs to Sosnovy Bor.
