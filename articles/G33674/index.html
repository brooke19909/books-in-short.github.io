How to make a fly agaric hat for a children's party with your own hands?

Kindergarten teachers sometimes pose difficult tasks to their parents. The most bewildering of most mothers and fathers is the requirement to make some thematic fancy dress. And if clothes for a particular image can still be picked up in the wardrobe of a child, then not every parent knows how to make a fly agaric hat or cat ears for a matinee.





The option is simple: from cardboard and paper

If the matinee is soon, and there is no time to sew a complicated hat for the mushroom, try to make it from improvised stationery materials. A fly agaric hat made of paper or cardboard will turn out no worse than a textile one if you make a little effort. Cut a circle from a Whatman paper of a suitable diameter. Make a cut in it in the radius and glue it in such a way that you get a low cone. Another option is to make 4-5 incisions and glue them all with a slight spacing. Wait for the workpiece to dry and paint it red. Your cap of fly agaric from paper is almost ready, it remains only to attach the ties and stick on the top of the circles of white paper. In the same technique, a hat can be made immediately from red cardboard. Attention - be sure to arrange the fitting at the procurement stage. And only if the size of the headgear suits you, go to the decor.





Remaking an old hat

It is very simple to make a fly agaric hat from some unnecessary carnival dress. Most importantly, the original hat should be suitable for the child in size and have hard margins. The base must be sheathed with foam, giving it the shape of a mushroom. You can do this arbitrarily by attaching individual small pieces of material to the fields of the hat. The workpiece is ready, and you like its shape. How to make a fly agaric hat from such a base? Everything is very simple - sheathe the underside with a white or light beige fabric. The top of the headgear should be covered with red material. The final step is to attach an elastic band or tape to fix the headgear and not to forget about the decorative circles of white fabric. Useful advice - you can also decorate a headpiece with elements cut out of white paper.

We sew according to all the rules

For those who at least know a little sewing and are ready to spend some time making a fancy dress, our instructions on how to make a fly agaric hat from fabric and improvised materials will come in handy. You will need foamed polyethylene, it can be purchased at hardware stores. On this material, we draw a circle whose size is equal to the diameter of the future hat, and in its center a second smaller circle equal to the circumference of the child‚Äôs head. We cut out this detail and make several more of the same material. We fasten individual elements together. Next, we need to give a bulge to the top of the cap. To do this, we lay foam layers in layers. An alternative is to create the correct shape: make cuts and glue the foamed polyethylene, as in the version with a hat made of cardboard.

We trim the resulting workpiece with a red and light cloth. Sew the circles on top. Your hat is ready, you just have to sew the ties. If you don‚Äôt like the way the headdress sits on the child, try to sew the base-cylinder into the inner hole. It is very simple to make it: take a piece of foamed polyethylene, cut a strip from it, the length of which will be equal to the girth of the child's head. Fasten the resulting rectangle into a ring, tighten with a cloth and sew it inside the hat.





Original ideas

A fly agaric hat in the kindergarten can be made in a combination technique. To make such a headgear, make a base from thick cardboard, and then cover it with a cloth. An interesting idea for those who have an unnecessary soft hat with ties - instead of individual ribbons or elastic bands, you can sew it into a hard fly agaric hat. You can give the required shape to the fields using thick, fairly rigid foam rubber. How to sew a fly agaric hat using the patchwork technique? To do this, you will have to draw in advance a pattern of the upper red circle with white spots sewn into it. Useful advice: remember the real fly agarics, do they always have even specks on hats? Try to make circles that are not quite perfect in shape, add asymmetric spots.





How to decorate a hat?

A ready-made fly agaric hat can be made even more interesting. Most often, such costumes of parents are asked to be made for the holiday of autumn. Accordingly, the decor is appropriate : autumn colored leaves, "stuck" to the hat. You can also decorate such a hat with figures of insects. Now you know how to make a fly agaric hat with your own hands. But before you get to work, consult with a kindergarten teacher or art director. Often parents are required to make hats as similar to each other as possible.
