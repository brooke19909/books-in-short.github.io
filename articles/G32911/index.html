Hotel The Phulin Resort 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Phuket attracts travelers from all over the world with its beaches, luxurious resorts, interesting culture and sincere Thai hospitality. Naturally, the tourist has plenty to choose from, because in the city there are many hotels of different sizes and categories. One of them is The Phulin Resort 3 *. This place has a good reputation. So what can a guest expect?

Brief description of the hotel and location





The Phulin Resort is a small, picturesque hotel located in the western part of Phuket - in a quiet resort area called Karon, just a few minutes walk from the seashore. Patong's more lively and famous beach is about 5 km away. The center of Phuket is located about 10 km from the hotel - it can easily be reached by taxi or public transport. Phuket International Airport is 45 km away, although you should not worry, as the hotel offers a shuttle service to its customers.

The Phulin Resort Hotel itself is relatively small, consists of several three-story buildings, a garden and a spacious courtyard. This place is ideal for a relaxing holiday on the picturesque seashore.

Hotel The Phulin Resort 3 *: what rooms does it offer?





This is a medium sized hotel. It consists of 105 rooms of the class "standard" and "luxury", and there are rooms of different capacities. Guests will enjoy a wonderful view from the window, as well as comfortable furniture and pleasant interior decoration. Some rooms have a spacious balcony.

Naturally, tourists can count on a set of equipment, without which, of course, it is almost impossible to imagine a truly pleasant vacation. You can skip the evening watching TV channels (there is a connection to satellite TV, there are some English and Russian-language channels). The deluxe rooms have DVD players. The air conditioning system will provide the most comfortable temperature conditions. Guests have access to a small safe and secure lock. There is also a minibar with a small refrigerator, however, it is replenished for free only with drinking water. The remaining drinks must be paid separately. By the way, the refrigerator can also be used for food storage.

The bathroom with modern fixtures will please you. Here, tourists can take a shower at any time. There is also a powerful hair dryer for drying hair. Of course, guests can expect a daily change of towels.

Dining for hotel guests





The Phulin Resort also includes free daily breakfast. In the mornings, guests are invited to a cozy restaurant, where buffet tables are already set. According to reviews, the choice of dishes for breakfast is greater in comparison with other hotel restaurants of the same price category. They offer omelets, pancakes, toasts, sandwiches, cereals, drinks and fresh pastries. If you wish, you can have lunch, dinner and a pleasant time here, but at an additional cost. Also nearby are many cafes of various categories - you can choose a suitable place for you in terms of quality and price. In addition, tourists can visit the restaurants of neighboring hotels - the prices here are mostly reasonable.

Where to look for a beach? Holidays by the sea





Reviews of The Phulin Resort 3 * hotel indicate that one of its advantages is its proximity to the beach. The distance to it is 500 m - a path leads to the shore, and the distance can be covered in 10-15 minutes.

It‚Äôs worth saying right away that the beach is public. On the other hand, reviews indicate that the coast here is calm and not as crowded as, for example, Patong. There is a convenient sandy entry into the sea, and on the beach the sand is soft, clean. True, the availability of appropriate shoes is still worth taking care of. For a small fee, you can rent a comfortable chaise lounge with an umbrella for almost the whole day. There are a lot of cafes on the beach where you can dine and enjoy the masterpieces of local cuisine. Naturally, tourists have the opportunity to engage in more active forms of recreation and even water sports.

The list of additional services and entertainment for guests

Naturally, travelers can count on a number of other services. For example, the hotel has ample parking, which guests can use for free (by the way, rented transport is one of the favorite means of travel for travelers, since the prices are relatively low). Here you can exchange currency, use the laundry services.





Almost throughout the hotel there is Internet access. There is a small conference room, as well as a well-equipped business center. Here vacationers can use computer and office equipment, send a fax or letter (there is a post office), rent a laptop, audio and video equipment.

As for entertainment, almost the entire courtyard is reserved for a pool of clean fresh water. There is a compartment for bathing children, a spacious sun terrace, on which deck chairs and umbrellas are located. For a small fee, you can use the services of a doctor, as well as a professional massage therapist (reviews indicate that Thai massage is worth trying on yourself). An excursion bureau is constantly operating at the hotel, where you can pick up and buy an interesting excursion or get additional information about the sights of the city.

Hotel The Phulin Resort 3 *: tourist reviews

Naturally, the opinion of travelers who have already managed to visit one place or another is a valuable source of information. How do tourists respond to The Phulin Resort? Reviews are positive. Guests celebrate a large territory and a pleasant interior design. The rooms are quite spacious, bright and clean - it is always pleasant to relax here, enjoying the coolness and pleasant views and windows. Maids clean the rooms very carefully. By the way, the staff here are kind, friendly and helpful. It is worth considering that most of them communicate in English - a problem can arise with Russian here.

An undoubted advantage is the proximity to one of the most beautiful beaches in Phuket. And since the hotel is located on the second line, the area is calm, and the prices in cafes, pharmacies and shops are lower. This place is popular with fans of beach and relaxing family vacations.
