Interface and Apple Carplay

Among the main reasons for buying the Pioneer SPH-DA120, user reviews call it compatibility with the CarPlay application. This is an evolving software platform on Apple iOS devices, not on the receiver itself. Therefore, no matter how non-original the receiver may be, it will always work the same way, no matter what the seller says about this!

Over time, the platform‚Äôs capabilities change through regular iOS updates. The same can be said about compatible applications, which are also periodically updated by developers, opening up new perspectives and improving CarPlay functionality.

Therefore, it makes no sense to talk about the platform here - what is relevant today, with the next update, may become outdated.

The Pioneer SPH-DA120 interface is not quite convenient. The overview is closed by a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãicons and options that immediately clog the main screen. Wireless bluetooth audio

If wireless CarPlay is required, then it is not here. But there is an alternative that the Alpine iLX-700 lacks, and that is built-in Bluetooth. After pairing with a smartphone, you can broadcast sound to the car speakers without the need to connect cables. This will not give everything that Apple offers in wireless CarPlay, but the sound is 99% of the device‚Äôs operating time, and AppRadio provides this opportunity, unlike its competitors in the same price range. Bluetooth audio can be controlled through the built-in music player, which displays meta data such as title, artist, album and genre. Directories are navigated using either the buttons on the front panel or the controls on the steering wheel, unless, of course, they are compatible.





User settings

For users, adaptation is important, and the Pioneer SPH-DA120 reviews call it a device with a number of features that help fit into the interior of the car. In the "Themes" section, you can select a background, as well as upload your own images from a USB drive. In the Lighting section, you can choose the color of the touch buttons on the side panel or define it using the color wheel in accordance with the tone of the interior. In the "Themes" subsection, you can choose the color of the interface and the color palette of the main screen.

Pioneer SPH-DA120: Overview of Multimedia Options

The SPH-DA120 supports many formats, surpassing its budget rivals in this. Two USB ports allow you to have one spare connector for connecting a USB drive with many files. What if I watch videos in DivX, H.264, MPEG4 and MKV formats? Of course, this can be done using the built-in media player. Need a slide show of images and photos? No problems. And if there is another video source, then you can connect it through the HDMI port on the rear panel.

Like other receivers compatible with CarPlay, the Pioneer SPH-DA120 supports a rear view camera, displaying the image on the screen with the help of the parking lot. However, today this feature has become the standard.

Audio settings

Among the sought-after features that the Pioneer SPH-DA120 car radio has, the feedback from music lovers is called pre-installed and customizable equalizers. They will allow you to achieve the desired sound or, after connecting the microphone, independently measure the acoustics of the car and automatically adjust the sound level.

Another standard feature supported is MIXTRAX, a technology for creating non-stop mixes of excerpts from an audio library with DJ effects. True, users listening to online audio are deprived of the opportunity to ever experience it.





Advantages and disadvantages

The choice of budget devices that support CarPlay is quite limited. It depends on the desires of the owners, as well as on the type of multimedia that you plan to use. Pioneer has the maximum number of connector types in its price category. Two USB ports will allow you to connect both iPhone and an additional media with video and graphics. The HDMI connector opens up more possibilities for multimedia, and the equalizer and graphics settings will please many.

At the same time, those who just want to connect their iPhone to use CarPlay may be tempted to purchase the Alpine iLX-700 because of its slightly larger screen, attractive appearance and convenience. CarPlay already uses the side of the display, so its large width reduces the inconvenience of the Apple interface.

The quality of the Pioneer screen seems lower with the same number of dots with Alpine (800 √ó 480) and its smaller size. Although such permission in the era of retina displays is a crime. Car radios that cost more than a thousand dollars still come with the same low definition.





Verdict

The main advantage of Pioneer is its support for streaming audio via Bluetooth. This simple function, which is found today in most media centers, opens up the possibility of wireless sound transmission without the need to get your phone out of your pocket and allows passengers to become a DJ during the trip.

It all comes down to individual preferences. If setting a color theme, watching movies or images is important, and there is a need for Bluetooth audio, then this model is very good for its price and worthy of being a member of Apple CarPlay. But for many Alpine owners with a wide screen and a clean interface, this is not enough, although the lack of Bluetooth is depressing.
