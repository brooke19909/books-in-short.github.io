Joseph Goebbels: quotes, statements, worldview position

For many years, it separates us from the events of 1945, when fascism, as an ideological doctrine that triumphed in individual states, was destroyed. However, the voices of his ideological inspirers still do not stop. Among them is Joseph Goebbels, whose quotes can still be found in books, in films, and on the Internet.

So what was this German speaker talking about, a master of propaganda and lies? Let's try to briefly answer this question.

Who is Goebbels?

The name and surname of this politician does not inspire fear for modern young people, but meanwhile some dozens of years ago, the surname of this leader of the German people was a household name.

He was a very famous figure in Nazi Germany, an associate of Hitler, who was responsible for propaganda and public education in the Reich. A brilliant speaker, Goebbels could talk for hours. Millions of people listened to him, millions of people believed his words. However, the propagandist himself in his letters and conversations with loved ones often allowed himself such words addressed to the Fuhrer or the party that ordinary citizens of the country could not allow.

Goebbels was certainly known as a smart, erudite and talented person, however, being a convinced Nazi, he caused a huge number of misfortunes to many peoples on earth, including his own German. The crimes of this man are truly terrible. However, he did not survive the Reich, and his death in 1945 was shameful and terrible.





Joseph Goebbels: quotes that went down in history

Goebbels spoke a lot in public. Many of his statements have been preserved for us by film, some of his associates and colleagues illuminated something in the memoirs, but we will consider only some of the expressions of this person, having previously classified them.

  • His quotes are about the new Germany.
  • About politics.
  • About the Russians.

So, with regard to quotes about Germany, Goebbels was a consistent supporter of the fascist idea, according to which, the future of the country should be determined by a strong state, headed by clean, racially clean people.

This will be a totalitarian state with the right to intervene in the private life of a simple person. For example, Joseph Goebbels noted that the goal of a nationalist revolution is precisely a totalitarian state.

Goebbels brought a lot of evil to the German people with these words, his quotes about the essence of the Nazi state are still studied by historians around the world, trying to understand what role this man played in unleashing the world war.

He spoke of the dictatorship of the nationalist idea, which should be the engine of social progress. The politician said that the will of the people should be in harmony with the will of the great leader, while an experienced dictator should be able to keep his people in check.
