How to remove blue screen on iPhone 5S? Reasons and solutions

The blue screen on the iPhone 5S is not news. Many owners experience this issue on their Apple gadgets. It is noteworthy that the previous generation of phones (iPhone 4, 4S) worked properly, and very rarely this happened to him. But 5, 5s, 6 and 6 Plus have become problematic gadgets. It was they who came from the risk zone.





Symptoms

Symptoms of this breakdown can be completely different, but the result is always the same - a non-working display. Most often, an apple burns on the iPhone 5S, then a blue screen, after which the device does not respond to pressing. In this state, it cannot be used in any way: either reset the settings or reflash.

Causes

There is no definite reason why something like this happens. This malfunction may be due to improper operation of the phone, its crash, failure of any part, incorrect replacement of a spare part in a service center, etc. Often, a blue screen appears on the iPhone 5S as a result of an attempt to repair it on its own. Users unknowingly open the lid, and under it is a thin ribbon cable. If you cut it off or break the contact, it could very well lead to a similar result - a blue screen on the iPhone 5S and a reboot.





Users say that the screen may or may not work. This suggests that the contacts can be interconnected (the screen works) and disconnected (the screen does not work), for example, in any position of the device or at a certain temperature. Often, if you put the device in the freezer, the restored iPhone 5S can be removed after 5-10 minutes. Under the influence of temperature, the contacts can connect in some way, but this is not for a long period of time.

What to do?

Like any other technique, iPhones also break, and if this happens, you need to contact a service center. Although this is commonplace, often service centers are indispensable. But you can also try to fix the gadget manually. So, let's say you saw a blue screen on the iPhone 5S. What to do and how to solve this problem? The very first and obvious step is to try resetting your account login. This rarely helps, but you need to try. This is done by connecting the gadget to a computer and using iTunes.

Will flashing help?

Most likely, the miracle will not happen, and the problem cannot be fixed. Therefore, the following is a flashing of the device. A small percentage of iPhones came to life precisely after a flashing. But you need to understand that the firmware solves software problems, and the blue screen is most likely related to the hardware. Therefore, it is possible that after flashing the blue screen on the iPhone 5S will again make itself felt even if the firmware helps.
