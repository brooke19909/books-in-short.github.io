Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * (United Arab Emirates, Ajman): description, service, reviews

Beach Hotel Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * is a private resort with all modern amenities. This hotel is so unique that travelers visiting the UAE specially come for excursions. It belongs to the group of travel companies of the NMN. However, one of the owners of the hotel is Sheikh Sharjah. This hotel has earned admiring reviews from many guests for true Arabian hospitality, service at the highest international standards, excellent rooms and apartments, luxurious cuisine and a cozy, homely atmosphere of true relaxation. Although this hotel is located in the developing emirate, the infrastructure of which cannot be compared with Dubai or Abu Dhabi, Ajman hotels are absolutely not inferior to these cities. But the prices there are much lower. In this article we will not only provide a description of the Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 *, but also consider how tourists rate this hotel.



When is the best time to go to the UAE?

The UAE is good in that the tourist season here lasts all year round. True, there are months when the temperature, due to the desert location of the country, rises to 50-60 degrees Celsius. Therefore, many are afraid to visit the United Arab Emirates in the summer, especially with children. However, due to the very dry climate of the country, the heat is not felt so much. In addition, this is a low season in the UAE. There are almost no people, and it is natural that all the attention of the hotel staff is concentrated on you and your family. The sea resembles fresh milk, its temperature rises above thirty, and sometimes you just do not want to get out of it for hours. High season here is spring and autumn.

The weather in the UAE in September, for example, is one of the most comfortable for the year. In winter it is somewhat cool, the temperature drops to 25 degrees Celsius. But it was at this time that it was good to go on different excursions, to visit museums and exhibitions. The spring is a bit hotter, and the temperature of the water and air are almost the same. We can say that at this time the velvet season begins. And the weather in the UAE in September will allow you to feel the heat of this summer (but not the same as in July or August), the warm sea and real beach relaxation. And the prices at this time still remain low. Because September is a good month for those who want to extend their summer.





Ajman - a small but promising principality

This is one of the most modest and calm emirates in the country. True, it is just starting to be built, and therefore it is considered not particularly rich. Many foreign workers come here. True, the prospects, including with regard to recreation and tourism, are huge. It is also the smallest emirate that fits between Sharjah and Dubai. But here there is a beautiful pristine nature, pristine beaches and water in the sea, palm trees everywhere. The hotel staff is polite and welcoming, and the atmosphere seems to be created for relaxation, coziness and comfort. Locals often have picnics right on the beach when evening comes. Privacy and a private beach are the hallmarks of many hotels, especially five-star hotels, such as the Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * (Ajman). Opened many restaurants and hookahs with panoramic sea views. And there is also a port for traditional Arab dhow boats - it is even considered a local attraction.

Lovers of antiquities are strongly encouraged to local museum of local lore with an excellent collection of weapons and ancient manuscripts. And near the city there are mineral springs.

Location

Resort Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * is located on the shores of the Arabian Gulf. From any of its points open very picturesque views. It is located not far from two international hubs - 22 kilometers from Dubai, and 11 from Sharjah. But if you are flying on a charter flight, then know that they are all transferred to suburban airports, and it takes them much longer to travel to the hotel, about two hours. And so the transfer from the hotel takes 45-50 minutes, if without traffic jams. If the groups are large, then the hotel provides a large bus, and if there are few people, then good quality cars. Near the hotel there is a beautiful promenade. When walking on it, they say "towards Dubai" or "Sharjah." The fact is that it really is so located and stretches almost from one emirate to another. Well, if you move a little from the sea, then there are a lot of shops and supermarkets, including those with high-quality and cheap children's toys. You can also buy souvenirs and sweets. If you only have breakfast, then next to the hotel there are fish and Chinese restaurants. They are inexpensive and with good food.



Hotel Territory

This hotel captivates you as soon as you get here. Spacious hall, with columns decorated in local color. The building itself is a real palace, like from a fairy tale. Many colors, light, and the decoration of the space is amazing. The architecture is very beautiful, at the same time modern and traditional. The building is made in a semicircle, so that the shadow falls on the guests as much as possible. Inside, it is also finished with high quality. The hotel Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * has an underground parking for up to 250 cars. You can leave scooters and bicycles there. Several shops are located on site, including jewelry and jewelry, and a currency exchange. But the course at the hotel is not very good. Among the sellers there are Russians, you can explain and even bargain for discounts. The hotel territory is a masterpiece of landscape art. It is small, but well maintained and original.

In addition to greenery, flowers and trees, there are many luxurious lawns where you can indulge in relaxation or meditation, and not just on the grass, but not sofas and armchairs made by the best modern designers. The hotel park is located on terraces with beautiful panoramic views where you can walk during the day and run in the morning. It is not at all noisy here - birds are singing, the sea and the wind are rustling in palm trees ... In a word, a real oasis.



Where do guests stay at the resort?

In the hotel Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * there are 254 rooms of various categories. These are standard rooms, Suites, suites and apartments. There are also club rooms and several types of residences, including two-room and royal .. Each room has a flat-screen LCD TV, a safe, a minibar (fridge), a kettle and a coffee machine, as well as a large selection of these bags in bags. In the bathroom there is a hairdryer, very high-quality toiletries, bathrobes and slippers. Soap, gels, conditioners, creams from Trussardi. An iron and ironing board are also featured in the rooms. The beds are very comfortable, the linen is snow-white. However, the sockets are not European, but triple, so you need to have an adapter with you. Free wifi catches in rooms of any category.

There are 5 * non-smoking rooms at the Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel, and three rooms are designed for people with disabilities. During the resettlement, guests are served cocktails, and children are given nuts and sweets. The air conditioning in the room is very cold, so if you go hot from the beach, then at least for children, it is better to wear warm sweaters or sweaters.

Nutrition

The main restaurant of Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * is called "Ankor Bistro". They prepare and serve dishes of different nations, including European countries. In this restaurant you can order three meals a day, half board or just breakfast. There is a specialized institution "Gourmet" with Arabic cuisine. Tourists very much appreciated the Dragon's Lair restaurant, where you can try Thai and Japanese dishes. The food there is not only tasty, but also unusual. The hotel has three bars - in the lobby (Ankor Cafe), by the pool (Ash Two O) and on the roof (Nujum Lounge). Alcohol is not served or sold at the hotel, and you need to remember this if you book tours to this hotel. However, in Ajman you can buy alcohol in stores - this is almost the only emirate where this is not prohibited. And in the rooms you can well drink alcohol, no one will blame you. They cook very tasty, and tourists claim that the choice of food is so huge that even their eyes run wide. Fresh, pickled and grilled vegetables, fish, including salted, many types of meat, nuts, a variety of fruits (watermelons, melons, pineapples), juices and pastries. Almost every day, salads with seafood - squid, shrimp, octopus. The confectioner is a French chef who bakes amazing desserts. Many say that in the heat of two meals a day is enough, especially since the food is very hearty and delicious.



Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 *: service

In this hotel, as in most "fives", you can hand over clothes for washing or cleaning. There is a business center where guests can use Apple brand computers. There it is also possible to Xerox or laminate documents, send a fax. Babysitting services at Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * are classified as paid. If you want to see what real shopping in the Emirates is, book a transfer to the malls of Sharjah or Dubai. He is free at this hotel. Internet is available not only in the rooms, but throughout and even on the beach. However, it does not have a very high speed. If you need a better connection to fly, then it will cost you an additional fee.

If you need a representative car, such as a limousine, this service will also be provided here. The employees are constantly smiling, talking to you kindly. The staff is very attentive. There are Russian-speaking workers. As soon as you tell about your desires, they are immediately fulfilled. Even if you want to switch to another number. The staff will always respond to any requests from you, they are very caring and attentive to the guests. Whether you leave a tip or not, this will not affect the quality of service at all. Water at the hotel is free - they bring three bottles to the room every day, and you can even dial in coolers on site. This is a rarity for the UAE: as a rule, here in hotels people pay extra for water.

Leisure

What else has Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * prepared for tourists? There is not much entertainment here. But there is a SPA center with a sauna, a jacuzzi and a hammam, and various types of massage - European and Asian, relaxing and therapeutic. Guests can use the gym with excellent equipment. Hammam and sauna, as is customary in the Emirates, are separate for men and women. The gym is excellent, it works around the clock, and even with sea views. Kids Club at Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 - one of the best in the resort. For the main entertainment, you can go to Dubai and Sharjah at least every day, using the free shuttle service. Just need to pre-register with the administrator. Drive a little over half an hour. In Dubai, singing fountains are usually watched, the Burj Khalifa, they ride the metro to Marina. Spouses or lovers can enjoy a romantic candlelit dinner on the beach. If you have a birthday, they will bring a deliciously delicious cake to your room, and create a congratulatory inscription on the sand by the sea so that this is the first thing you see when you look out the window. In the event that you reserve a hotel for the New Year, you will be amazed not only by the abundance of all goodies, but also by the huge Christmas tree in the lobby three stories high, as well as the amazing atmosphere that prevails throughout the hotel.



Sea holiday

The beach at Bahi Ajman Palace Hotel 5 * is private, sandy. No one else comes here. To get to the sea, you just need to go through the pool area. Towels are provided, sunbeds and umbrellas can be used free of charge. There are very comfortable sun loungers. And sunbeds are huge beds with mattresses. The beach is not only beautifully equipped, but also provides guests with various sports and leisure activities on the water. You can book a boat trip or a yacht. A children's room is open on the beach. True, there are not very many toys, but the kids have somewhere to rest during the hot hours. Sunset in the sea is good, shallow. The sand is white, the water is clear, crabs on the breakwaters, you can watch the fish. There are no shells, and rescuers work fine. A green lawn with large umbrellas is also reserved for the beach area. On the territory there is an outdoor pool, in which the water is heated. For children there is a small "paddling pool". It is located in the shade so that the kids do not overheat. Children there are very comfortable. There are few people on the beach, a maximum of several dozen people.
