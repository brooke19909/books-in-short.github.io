Hotel Khaolak Emerald Beach Resort & Spa 4 * (Thailand / Khao Lak): overview, features and reviews

But there are places in the Kingdom of Smiles that are completely dedicated to relaxation. And if you get tired at your work so much that on vacation you just want to lie in a languid bliss near a tropical lagoon, you need to go on vacation to Khao Lak. This resort is located on the Andaman Sea, in the province of Phang Nga. Nearby is the island of Phuket - the exact opposite of Khao Laku. So those who crave fun and drive need to overcome only 80 kilometers to be in the epicenter of the nightlife of Patong. More relaxed tourists Khao Lak offers to enjoy the natural beauties: waterfalls, rocky bays, mangrove forests. This resort specializes in spa services, massages and yoga. When to go to Khao Lak Resort

Khao in Thai means "island". This small piece of land lies not far from the mainland, in the Andaman Sea. In climatic terms, it is located at the junction of two zones. In winter, or rather from November to early March, tropical winds reign over the island. This is the most favorable time to relax, the so-called high season. During this period, dry, clear sky, calm sea. The temperature is quite comfortable for Europeans. In the afternoon, the air in the shade warms up to +28 degrees, and the water in the sea - up to +26. In the spring months, Khao Lak is still dry. But it gets very hot. The thermometer column on some days reaches +35 degrees in the shade. Khaolak Emerald Beach Resort & Spa is open all year. But it should be remembered that from the beginning of June to the end of October the island falls under the influence of monsoons, which bring precipitation. In the rainy season, the sea is often worried. But temperature indicators remain high - up to +33 degrees. Heat in the wet period is much more difficult to tolerate, since high humidity creates the effect of a Turkish bath.

Hotel location in Khao Lak

Khaolak Emerald Beach Resort & Spa is located in a secluded area, a bit away from the resort's urban infrastructure. This arrangement has its pros and cons. Let's start with the latter. Shops, restaurants, massage parlors and a small market are nearby, but prices are much higher than in the village. To buy cheaper products and services, you need to walk the road for about fifteen minutes. Sangteo (pickup with two benches for passengers) to the central beach of Nang Thon - 150 baht, to Bang Niang, the evening market - 250 baht.

Now about the pros of the location. The hotel is literally a few steps from the virgin rainforest, right on the beach. A five-minute drive takes guests to the gates of Koh Lak Lam Lu National Park. This attraction of the island with a diverse exotic fauna, flora, granite cliffs and waterfalls is a must. To make up for the inconvenience of the remoteness of the hotel from the city infrastructure, the hotel provides a free shuttle service to the center and the evening market.
