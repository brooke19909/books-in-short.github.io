Psychology of management

Management psychology is a branch of psychological science that studies personality and social groups in the management system of a work collective; it is designed to analyze personal and group functions in order to solve assigned tasks.

Moreover, a distinctive feature of the psychology of management is that the object of its study is concentrated on the organized activities of people. Management relations coordinate the joint activities of people, making it logical and organized, which helps to achieve higher production results.
