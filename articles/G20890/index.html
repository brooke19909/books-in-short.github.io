Smartphone review Fly 4404. Specifications and reviews

Among the many manufacturers of budget devices, Fly should be noted. The company does not pursue a supernova design or a powerful filling, but produces a lot of inexpensive devices. What the company gets, you can understand by the example of IQ4404.

Design





The appearance of the company's smartphones will not be able to particularly surprise. Due to the imitation of leaders, the design is somewhat boring. The Fly IQ 4404 phone was no exception. The front end imitates Samsung, while the rear looks very similar to HTC.

Fly 4404 is made of plastic, and not of the worst quality. The assembly will also please the user, there are no gaps in the device. There may be a slight creak when squeezing the device, but this is not critical.

Variations in the color of the case are very scarce. Owners are offered only a white and black device.

The front panel sheltered the display, sensors, speaker, touch buttons and a camera. The back is also the expected picture in the form of a speaker, a main camera, a company logo and, of course, a flash.

The power button is located on the right side, and the volume control is on the left. The bottom end got a USB-connector and a microphone. Above, respectively, a headphone jack.

The design does not shine with special originality, but it cannot be called too boring. Appearance is quite acceptable for a cheap device. The smartphone also weighs not very much, only 132 grams.

Screen





The Fly 4404 got a quite suitable 4.5-inch screen. Improves the impression of the display and the applied IPS-matrix, significantly increasing viewing angles. However, the pixel resolution failed: only 854 by 480. Of course, such characteristics are used in almost all state employees, but I wanted the best.

The brightness in the sun dims a little, although in general the screen behavior of the device is excellent. Only emerging pixels are confused, but this is logical at 218 ppi.

For an inexpensive device, the display turned out great. The user should not have any special complaints about the image.

Camera





Removes Fly 4404 rather mediocre. The reason for this lies in the installed camera with 5 megapixels. Although the device has functions that enhance the photo, such as autofocus and detailed settings, this does not save the situation.

The resolution of 2560 by 1920 slightly improves the quality of images. The device lacks sharpness a bit to reach the cameras of modern state employees.

Filling

The Fly IQ 4404 uses the MTK processor, the Chinese favorite. Actually, due to the use of this particular part of the device, it turns out so cheap. The good news is that the processor has two cores. Each of them operates at a frequency of 1.2 GHz. Such characteristics allow you to use the device as efficiently as possible.

But the RAM in Fly IQ 4404 has not so bright indicators. They equipped the smartphone with 512 megabytes. This is considered the minimum indicator for devices on Android.

Lame in Fly 4404 and internal memory. It is only 4 GB, and only 2.5 is available for use. The rest of the memory is occupied by the system. The only way out is to increase due to the flash drive. You can put a card up to 32 GB in your smartphone.

System

The device operates under the guidance of "Android" 4.2. The system allows you to install all the necessary applications and even not very demanding games on your phone.

Problems in the operation of the device should not arise due to the filling, providing good performance. In addition, updating the system to a newer version is not difficult.

Communication

The device is available all the necessary standards for GSM-communication and even 3G. In addition, the device works with two SIM cards.

Unfortunately, there is only one radio module in the device, and the simultaneous use of two cards is impossible. When you call the second SIM card will automatically turn off.

Naturally, the Fly IQ 4404 Spark phone has features such as bluetooth, mobile Internet and Wi-Fi.

Battery





Equipped a smartphone with a battery capacity of 1750 maH. After evaluating the performance and functionality of the device, we can conclude that this is not enough. The 7 hours of active work indicated by the company actually come down to 3-4. The duration depends on the functions used.

Disabling applications and Internet connections will help reduce battery consumption.

Price

The smartphone turned out to be quite cheap for a device with such good characteristics. You can buy Fly IQ 4404 Spark for about 4 thousand rubles. Most likely, the price will be even lower, because the device has already been discontinued.

In comparison with the more popular brands, the Fly 4404, considering the filling and cost, looks very tempting.
