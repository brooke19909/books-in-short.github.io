Perfect option

These two words fully characterize this hotel complex. Pension "Southern Night" (Vardan) is not without reason receiving positive reviews . Accommodation

Each person, about to come to rest, is worried about where he will be accommodated. In this case, there is no need to worry. The hotel's room fund provides apartments fully equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable life. Soft comfortable beds, spacious wardrobes, tables, stylish decoration - all of these are different rooms that you can rent by deciding to come to the Southern Night Pension (Vardane). Entertainment

Arriving in Vardan (boarding house "Southern Night"), people want to not only enjoy the delights of the local beach, but also have fun and relax. Infrastructure and additional services
