Hotel "Eurasia" (Tyumen): address, description of rooms, infrastructure, photos and reviews

Business Hotel "Eurasia" in Tyumen is an ideal accommodation option for those who arrived in the city for business purposes. Tourists will also like this place, because a convenient location in the historical center of the city opens up great opportunities for exploring the sights.

If you want to become part of the friendly team of the Eurasia Hotel in Tyumen, vacancies will certainly interest you. Most of all, the institution needs qualified waiters and kitchen workers. Also vacancies of managers and technical personnel are constantly updated.

Location

In the very center of the city is the hotel "Eurasia" in Tyumen. The address of the establishment is Sovetskaya Street, 20. It is only 300 meters from the Tura River and 12 kilometers from Roshchino Airport.

The contacts of the Eurasia Hotel in Tyumen are known to many citizens and guests of the city.

Catering establishments

There are several catering establishments in the Eurasia Hotel. Among them:

  • Mint is a panoramic summer restaurant located on the roof of the hotel. On the spacious terrace for up to 120 people, you can enjoy your favorite dishes, listening to live music and admiring the views of the city. Themed parties and gastronomic festivals are also regularly held here.
  • Orient Express is a cozy restaurant located on the second floor of the hotel. The dining room for up to 80 people has an elegant and at the same time harmonious interior. In addition to classic European dishes, Siberian and Asian dishes are also presented here. Breakfast and business lunches are also served here. And in this institution it is possible to hold banquets from 8 to 40 people.
  • Lungo is a patisserie where you can buy desserts for a holiday, corporate event, or just for tea drinking. You can choose from the presented products or custom-made cooking.
  • The Noble Nest is a banquet restaurant, the interior of which is made in the style of the XIX century. Designer furniture, luxurious textiles and exquisite tableware are all presented in the dining room for up to 120 people. This institution holds private and corporate events.
  • Lobby bar - a cozy place, equipped in the lobby of the hotel. This is a great place for friendly meetings, business negotiations or just a relaxing pastime. Here you can drink alcohol, soft drinks and hot drinks, enjoy sandwiches, fresh pastries and ice cream, as well as read the latest press, watch TV (news or sports).

Positive reviews

You can hear a lot of good things about the Eurasia Hotel in Tyumen. Positive reviews are as follows:

  • convenient location in the central part of the city;
  • good underground parking;
  • good, hearty breakfasts with excellent, natural coffee;
  • perfectly clean linens and bath towels;
  • quick registration and check-in procedure;
  • the minibar is regularly replenished with drinking water;
  • attentive and friendly staff who are always ready to help;
  • if possible, populate before the specified time, without requiring an additional fee;
  • a very beautiful winter garden with a fountain, fish and birds;
  • free minibar with juice and chocolates;
  • good sauna;
  • a good set of toiletries in the bathroom;
  • good wireless internet speed;
  • the parrots in the winter garden are completely tame (if you live in the atrium, they will often fly into your room);
  • a good gym with a lot of modern simulators and other equipment.

Negative reviews

Not without flaws. Here are some negative reviews worth focusing on:

  • many dishes from the restaurant menu are in fact not available;
  • the spa area is open only until 22:00, which is very inconvenient for guests who spend almost all day at work, and can only relax late in the evening;
  • not the best quality of cleaning;
  • during conferences or banquets, noise from them spreads practically throughout the hotel;
  • the room is not aired before the guests check in, and therefore there is initially an unpleasant, musty smell;
  • there are few shelves in the bathroom to arrange bath accessories and cosmetics;
  • in the lobby bar, many chairs are dirty;
  • in the cold season, the air conditioning system does not work, and in the rooms it is very stuffy (especially in the atrium, whose windows overlook the winter garden).
