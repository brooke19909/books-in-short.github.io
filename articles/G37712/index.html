The most romantic cities in the world

There are many beautiful places on our planet, and some of them are literally created for lovers. Let's talk about the most romantic cities in the world, where couples can go on their honeymoon or any other time.

Magic Paris



The brilliant capital of France is literally filled with the scent of love. It is enhanced by the unique taste of French wine and fresh oysters, which can be tasted in any restaurant. The Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Gallery, the Champs Elysees, the Luxembourg Gardens await lovers. A stunning panorama of the city in all the splendor of its classical architecture will open a walk along the Seine. The most romantic cities in Europe and, incidentally, the whole world can not be compared with Paris, rightfully called the main city for loving hearts.

Cozy Copenhagen



For many years now, the wondrous Little Mermaid has been attracting lovers to her, for the sake of love she has given up her immortality. A bronze figure of a fairy-tale heroine is installed in the port of the Danish capital on the Langelinho embankment. The fame of it is spread all over the world by severe sailors who came with their ships to the port of Copenhagen. Since then, couples come to the capital of Denmark from different parts of the world every year. They come to the Little Mermaid and ask her to help keep love forever and prevent her from fading away. That is why, overlooking the most romantic cities, one cannot but mention Copenhagen.

Strict St. Petersburg



In June and early July, the city on the Neva especially attracts lovers. The reason for this is the white nights, turning the northern capital of Russia into a fabulous place. At this time, there are a lot of festivals, concerts. The city founded by Peter the Great is perfect for honeymooners. All lovers seek to visit the world famous Kisses Bridge, because, according to legend, those who kiss on it will never be able to part.

Golden Prague



The most romantic cities are also represented by the capital of the Czech Republic. Its medieval charm with magnificent castles, ancient monasteries, beautiful cathedrals, houses with tiled roofs attracts loving couples. It will be pleasant to wander through the city winding streets, take a walk along the Charles Bridge, go to a quiet restaurant, stand under the astronomical clocks famous on the whole planet on Old Town Square, counting down happy moments of life.

Free New York



Speaking about the most romantic cities, it should be said separately about New York - a city that, like a feeling of love, gives people freedom. Skyscrapers, looking up, give the feeling of flying, just the way that people in love experience. Couples who come to New York love to walk in city parks, enjoy the panorama of the Brooklyn Bridge, spend evenings in the charming restaurants of Manhattan.

Fascinating Venice



To discuss the most romantic cities and not to mention Venice would be a crime. This is a dream city that allows lovers to forget about the mortality of the world. Venetian masks, graceful dolls, lace flicker in shop windows when couples walk along the narrow medieval streets. And fast gondolas immerse their passengers in the world of love and romance.
