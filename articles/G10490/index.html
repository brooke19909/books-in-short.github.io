How to find a stolen smartphone: methods and tips

For most people, a mobile device is a repository of personal data, personal information and an indispensable device for solving everyday problems. Sometimes, due to forgetfulness and carelessness, the phone is lost or falls into the hands of attackers. In such cases, it is often a pity not so much the lost device, but the files that it contains. If you take appropriate measures and know how to act in this situation, you can find a stolen smartphone by IMEI or another way as soon as possible.

We are looking for a switched off phone

Not always the person who found the phone, seeks to return to its owner. If the gadget was stolen, then the attacker immediately removes the SIM card and disconnects the smartphone. This significantly reduces the ability to search for a mobile device.

Often people ask, can I find a smartphone if it was stolen and turned off? If the cell is not active, it is best to immediately contact the appropriate authorities and write a theft statement. This will require some data. It is necessary to take a check from the store and a box from the device, if they are preserved, as well as a passport. At the police station, talk about the trouble and write a statement indicating the identification data, more precisely, the IMEI code, which is unique for each device.

In the past few years, the number of phone thefts has decreased significantly, due to the affordable price of smartphones, as a rule, theft is more affected by expensive gadgets. Police officers will take measures to find the device, but statistics are such that they are rarely found. If the device is discharged or turned off, but the SIM card remains inside, the provider determines the location of the smartphone and returns it to the owner.

  • Windows Phone

Law enforcement officers send a request to the court for permission to oblige the operator to find the stolen phone on their own by IMEI. Using satellites, the mobile operator can find the stolen device by identifier, this option is available exclusively from the provider. To do this, a court order is required.

Often on the Internet, there are sites offering to find a stolen phone on their own by IMEI, but third-party resources do not have such capabilities. If, after sending the request, a third-party resource asks to make payment, this is a usual deception of money. Information databases on the code are stored exclusively by the mobile operator; they cannot be freely available. Entering IMEI data of a lost phone allows other users to find out if they are buying a stolen device.

By number

According to the local legislation of the Russian Federation, when buying a SIM card, an agreement is necessarily concluded, usually one number is used from the mobile operator. Often people are interested in whether it is possible to find a stolen smartphone if it is turned off by the SIM card number. Unfortunately, it is impossible to find a smartphone by the SIM number. Such capabilities and technological equipment are available exclusively from the mobile operator, but, having made such a request, the company employees will refuse. It is not a human factor, but the high cost of the procedure; at present, it is not among the standard ones and requires technical resources and time costs. Searching for a device makes sense only if it was released in a limited quantity and is expensive.

There are resources on the World Wide Web with offers to find the lost device for free if it was stolen. You should not be fooled by such offers - this is another myth, not one site has such capabilities. As an exception, official web pages from manufacturers of Samsung and Apple devices are highlighted, which attach a personal account to the device and search through their satellite channel (geolocation), if activated.
