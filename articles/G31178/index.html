Pattaya's tropical parks and the greenest hotel in the city

Pattaya, where thousands of tourists flock annually, is not rich in cultural and historical values. But the locals did everything possible to turn the city into a magical place where you can relax and have fun for every taste. The expanses of the sea are admirable, the parks of Pattaya beckon with exotic beauties, the fiery shows excite the blood, and the cozy hotels promise a carefree vacation. There are enough tourist complexes here. But there is one paradise in the city in which you want to stay forever.

Pattaya, Green Park

For those who want to immediately plunge into the fabulous atmosphere of the rainforest, a great choice will be accommodation in the greenest hotel in Pattaya Green Park.





Sitting here, you can forget about the bustling city beaches and distant parks of Pattaya. There is a lagoon pool with bridges, and a well-groomed picturesque area, where dozens of species of trees and hundreds of different flowers grow. There are chameleons on the territory, squirrels jumping in the branches, parrots and other exotic birds flutter.

Let's go to the rooms!

The hotel was built in 1995, during which time it was reconstructed several times. Recent improvements were made in 2013. There are several three-story buildings and one-story bungalows.

The hotel has 193 rooms. Most of them overlook the pool, but there are views of the garden and the city. The interior is original and tasteful. The design used Thai style, all the furniture is new and functional.

Room features:





  • Bathroom with bath or shower, hairdryer and toiletries.
  • Terrace or balcony.
  • TV with satellite channels.
  • Air conditioning.
  • Phone, radio.
  • Safe.
  • Kettle and coffee maker.

Type of food - full board, service - around the clock. Umbrellas, chaise lounges, towels - it is free.

Paid services:

  • Transfer.
  • Luggage storage.
  • Mini bar.
  • Room service.
  • A beauty salon and shops are located on site.
  • Providing a printer, fax, Internet access.
  • Organization of excursions to other cities and provinces, to the islands and parks of Pattaya.
  • Banquets, business meetings.
  • Car rent.

Away from noise

The location is very good: in the northern part of Pattaya Bay, it is always calm, and the center is only 10 minutes on foot. The nearest attractions and parks in Pattaya are Mini Siam, a complex of miniatures of masterpieces of world architecture, the world famous Alcazar transvestite show, the Tiffany's Show similar to it and the local administrative center.

The path to the sea takes 5 minutes and passes through a quiet side street, which ends with a beautiful staircase. City beach, umbrellas and deck chairs on it are paid.





The level of service is very high. For younger visitors there is a cot (on request), babysitting services (for a fee), a children's pool and a special menu in the restaurant.

The pool bar offers a wide range of desserts and drinks. The restaurant serves gourmet international and Thai dishes.

Entertainment

On site you can indulge in a Thai massage, sunbathe in the sun loungers or take a swim in the magnificent outdoor pool.

Outside the complex, guests of the resort will find many interesting places that you can visit on your own. This is the Village of elephants, and the zoo "Khao Keo", and such unusual exotic parks in Pattaya as the "Three Kingdoms" and "Nong Nooch".

To see the surroundings from a bird's eye view, you should climb to the best observation deck of the city. It is part of the Pattaya Park complex. Democratic prices: 200 baht per person. To dine in a rotating panoramic restaurant, you will have to pay only 500 baht: the price includes a lift, a buffet and a descent in an extreme booth or trailer.
