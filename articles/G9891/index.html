Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria): description and reviews

Sunny and hospitable Bulgaria can become an unforgettable beach holiday destination for many tourists. Beach resorts with comfortable hotels of various categories follow each other along the entire Black Sea coast of the country, but each of them offers excellent accommodation conditions, excellent food and interesting leisure activities.

Among other resorts with its silence and tranquility stands out a place located in the vicinity of Burgas, called St. Vlas. If you opt for this young, but already famous for many of its wonderful beaches resort, then a five-star Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria, St. Vlas) can be an excellent option for all-inclusive accommodation. The hotel is located in close proximity to the sea.

Short description of the hotel Moonlight (Bulgaria)

The hotel complex consists of two multi-storey buildings located opposite each other. The hotel itself was built relatively recently: the first building met its guests in May 2013, the second was opened in the summer of 2016.





The Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria) provides guests with comfortable accommodation in modern rooms of various categories, buffet-style meals organized in its own restaurant, indoor and indoor pools with a cocktail bar, spa treatments. Lovers of outdoor activities can enjoy volleyball, billiards, table tennis, darts, speed skating on the water surface on extreme modes of transport.

Young guests will not remain indifferent to entertainments at the equipped playground, swimming in the children's section of the outdoor pool and evening mini-disco.

All additional services offered to guests can be found at any time at the reception in the first building of the hotel. Reception staff are fluent in English and also understand Russian.

Location

The Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria) is located a 10-minute walk from the center of Sveti Vlas, while the Sunny Beach Luna Amusement Park and Platinum Casino are just 2 kilometers from the hotel complex.

There is a bus stop within walking distance from the hotel, from where you can easily reach the famous large resort Sunny City (4 km) and the ancient city of Nessebar (8 km), which is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Program.

The distance from the international airport "Burgas" is 30 kilometers. This distance hotel guests can easily overcome in 25-35 minutes by car.

Number of rooms

The number of rooms is represented by 3 types of rooms:

  • standard double rooms with the ability to install one extra bed;
  • studios that can comfortably accommodate up to four people;
  • apartments for two to four guests.




Regardless of the category, each room is equipped with: a balcony, air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone, mini fridge, safe, convenient closet. Private bathrooms come with a shower or bathtub, guest bathrobes, towels and toiletries.

In addition to the above, the apartment has a guest room and bedroom, as well as an additional bathroom.





Cleaning is carried out regularly.

The hotel was built in such a way that almost all of the rooms have a beautiful view of the Black Sea, only a few rooms overlook the mountains.

Looking through the reviews about the Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria), which are left by tourists who visited it in 2016, you will notice that most of them are advised to settle in the newly built second building. In the new building, according to guests, the rooms are much more comfortable.

Nutrition

Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria) operates on an all-inclusive basis. The restaurant's menu is varied, it actively includes dishes of national cuisine. According to guest reviews, they cook here deliciously and a lot.

The restaurant is large, there are enough tables for visitors, so there are no queues and problems with the place.





The attendants of the hall and kitchen are always neatly and cleanly dressed, polite and friendly.

There is an opportunity to dine outdoors, comfortably sitting at a table by the pool.

During the hours when the restaurant is closed, you can have a light snack and a cup of tea / coffee in the pool bar.

Infrastructure

The Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria) provides its guests with free parking for personal vehicles, conference room services, currency exchange, the opportunity to relax in free sun loungers by the large outdoor pool with a children's area, swim in the indoor pool, use the services of the SPA complex and beauty center , enjoy a Turkish bath or sauna, undergo a massage, relieve stress in the jacuzzi, work out in the fitness center.





The hotel complex has a very small playground, a golf sector and a games room. From sports entertainment - this is billiards, beach volleyball, table tennis.

In the evening, a children's disco is organized for young guests, for adults - its own animation, a program that will not leave anyone indifferent.

The hotel "Moonlight" (Bulgaria) offers its guests free Wi-Fi throughout its territory.

Beach and sea

The Moonlight Hotel Complex is located 10 meters from its own equipped beach. In order to get on it, just leave the hotel and go down the path or stairs.

The beach itself near the hotel is quite narrow, but a little further becomes wider. Its coating consists of fine sand, the entrance to the water is smooth, you need to go to the depth for a while.





For an additional fee, beach chairs and umbrellas can be rented on the beach, but beach towels are provided for free, with a deposit.

Holiday Benefits

"Moonlight" is a hotel (Bulgaria), reviews of which are mostly positive, offering its guests a wonderful trip to the Black Sea coast. Tourists who have rested here sincerely thank the hotel staff for their warm hospitality, excellent and delicious cuisine, constant cleanliness and freshness in the rooms, for an individual approach to each client and the created atmosphere of comfort and coziness.

In their comments, guests emphasize the quality of the Wi-Fi signal, its extensive coverage area and availability even during peak load times.

They left pleasant memories with tourists and Friday Bulgarian dinners with incredible dances, live sound, as well as evening animations for children and adults.





The hotel, according to many, was simply created for lovers of a quiet and relaxing holiday surrounded by nature. And for incendiary parties you can easily go to the neighboring Sunny Beach.

Finally, a convenient and gentle entrance to the sea is the best option for families with small children and people who feel unsure of themselves in the water.

Disadvantages of the hotel, according to some vacationers

In general, the Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria, St. Vlas) has collected positive reviews and is rated by guests on a five-point scale, if not by 5 points, then by 4.5 accurately. However, some guests noted a number of shortcomings, which staff clearly need to work on:

  • The hotel territory is not fenced, which allows outsiders to enter it.
  • On the sunbeds and grounds there can be people without bracelets (a sign of a hotel guest), for some reason the hotel staff does not pay attention to them.
  • Inconvenient descent to the sea for the elderly and with disabilities.
  • Narrow beach in the immediate vicinity of the hotel, lack of free sunbeds and umbrellas.

In conclusion, we can say that according to information from tour operators and individuals, the five-star Moonlight Hotel (Bulgaria) is popular with guests of the resort of St. Vlas. In this regard, it is recommended to book rooms for the summer season in advance.
