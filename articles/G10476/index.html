"Olive Beach" in Gorky Park - an oasis in the metropolis

Gorky Park today is a favorite place of all Muscovites. It attracts metropolitan residents from all over the metropolis to take a walk, go for a morning run, spend time with family, attend lectures that are constantly held in the park, or simply enjoy the beauty of nature and marvelous landscapes. Due to the successful combination of forests and modern infrastructure, thousands of people gather on a weekly basis in the park. There is a lot of controversy about its recent reconstruction: someone believes that modern design solutions have mutilated the historical memorial of Moscow, while others rejoice at new attractions and a European look of the park. But, as they say, there are no comrades for the taste and color. One thing is definitely not going to leave visitors indifferent - restaurants in Gorky Park. Well, who will refuse to relax on the verandas of restaurants after a long walk or exhausting run? Everyone wants to drink fresh lemonade or have a snack (by the way, the choice of cuisines is very rich). Restaurant "Olive Beach" in Gorky Park - just such a place.

Olive oasis



The restaurant offers its visitors not only delicious dinners on the outdoor terrace, but also the opportunity to sunbathe. You can dine under the shade of olive trees, carefully planted in the restaurant. At your choice - dishes of European and Japanese cuisines, as well as refreshing drinks that will help get rid of thirst. "Olive beach" in Gorky Park is not in vain in its name has the word "beach", because the restaurant will welcome those who wish to sunbathe. Watching the course of the Moscow River, sipping a refreshing cocktail from a straw, you can at least lie all day on a sun lounger and at the same time have a good rest. The entrance fee to the restaurant is 1000 rubles, this also includes the cost of renting a deck chair. Yoga classes are often held at the restaurant: fresh river air is excellent for meditation. Even if you like to do yoga alone, the restaurant terrace is perfect for this purpose. The "Olive Beach" in Gorky Park will pleasantly surprise you with its interior. The plank floor, like that of a grandmother in the village, and light wicker furniture make you feel like you are really on vacation in a country house.

Country holidays in the center of the metropolis



The "olive beach" in Gorky Park will certainly become your favorite place, but to diversify your leisure time, visit other restaurants in the park. The interior of the rest of the establishments is a bit similar to the design of the Olive Beach, but the creators of the park strove for this - the atmosphere of the village house by the lake with simple but elegant furniture successfully fits into the landscape of the park and pleases the eye of visitors. So, if you are walking with children, go to the Droplet: while you have dinner, your offspring can enjoy games on the playground near the restaurant. For lovers of seafood, a real find will be the "Fisherman's House". In addition to delicious fish dishes, the institution offers you to catch your own lunch in the lake. Your catch may be a silver carp or even trout.

Do not sit at home!



Walking, workshops, restaurants "Droplet", "Fisherman's House", "Olive Beach" in Gorky Park - all this and more you can find in the heart of Moscow. You should not spend your weekend and time after work at home. Just drive to the Gorky Park metro station (Culture Park) - and enjoy your vacation!
