Location

Otium Hotel Amphoras Sharm 5 * was built on the very shore of the Red Sea on the small hill of Om el Sid in Amphoras Bay, one of the most popular among diving fans, since two centuries ago the Greek galleon with amphora sank there. The largest international airport in Egypt, where planes arrive from Russia, is located near the city of Sharm El Sheikh. It is only 18 km from the hotel, so the journey by transport provided as part of the transfer takes no more than 30 minutes. The central part of Sharm el-Sheikh is much closer - only 10 km, you can get there by shuttle bus or taxi. At 7 km from the hotel is located one of the most beautiful and, perhaps, the most tourist bays - Naama Bay, famous for not only picturesque views, but also developed infrastructure. Otium Hotel Amphoras Sharm 5 * in this beautiful place of the coast is not alone. It is surrounded by other hotels on the left and on the right. A plus in the location is the presence of shops with Egyptian goods and a supermarket literally opposite the hotel, where you can inexpensively buy the necessary things.

Sea

Egypt is famous for its beautiful, warm even in winter and rich in exotic fish Red Sea. Otium Hotel Amphoras Sharm 5 *, although located near the coast, but, as mentioned above, is located on a hill. Therefore, to get to the beach, you need to use the elevator or stairs. This does not create any particular inconvenience, since the elevators always work properly, roomy and comfortable. The beach at the hotel is sandy, quite long. Sunbeds with mattresses and fungus umbrellas from the sun are always available. The Red Sea is known for having many corals in it even off the coast. It is interesting to watch them and the life of its beautiful inhabitants, but they create some problems for water procedures. To create maximum comfort for vacationers, two piers are built on the beach from which you can descend into the water. One of them is always on duty by rescuers who do not refuse to take some things for storage (for example, telephones) while tourists are swimming.
