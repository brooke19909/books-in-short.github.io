Ostas 3 *, Prague: hotel description, service, reviews

The hotel is located on Orebitskaya street, which belongs to the Prague 2 district. This is the central quarter. On its territory are concentrated a lot of attractions and entertainment complexes. It stretches along the picturesque coast of the Vltava River. Borders on Prague 1 in the north.

Captured by history





Its area is small, but of great interest to travelers. Business Card - New Place and Vysehrad. Today, the area is considered an important cultural and commercial center of the city. The winding streets of the quarter are covered with open terraces of coffee houses and pubs. Local chefs are good at cooking meat.

On the eve of the holidays, fairs and festivities are held in the squares of Prague 2. The route of marchings of mummers stretches past the buildings of the National Museum, Podskalsky Customs, past monuments and art galleries. The quarter is home to the National Theater and State Opera. Jazz and music clubs of the Czech capital are hidden in the back streets of the district.

Winter holidays





On the eve of Christmas celebrations, the Ostas 3 * hotel (Prague) becomes especially crowded. Rooms are booked in advance, no rooms are available in November. December brings cloudy and cold days to the city. The capital suddenly becomes gloomy and pale. This will last until the first days of December. Already in the first half of the month it will be lit up by a myriad of neon lights.

A tiny sparkle and a web of garlands become full owners in Prague. The metropolis is preparing for the celebration of Christmas. Closer to the celebration, the city will be covered with fluffy white snow. Tours in Prague for the New Year begin to be sold in the summer. They are in high demand not only among Russians, but also among citizens of the USA, Japan, China, Germany and other European powers.

During this period, the air temperature is -3 ° C, at night drops to -5 ° C. Humidity exceeds 90%. Almost every day it snows or rains. Gradually, the metropolis turns into a fabulous town. Therefore, tours to Prague for the New Year are in high demand among tourists. In December, the streets of the capital are flooded with youth and couples in love. Families with children and single travelers come to the Czech Republic.

Seasons





In spring, the city blooms with tender pastel garden flowers. The buds swell and open in March, and in April the first greens appear on the trees. The humidity level is gradually reduced. In the courtyards of Prague houses viburnum blooms. The fragrance of trembling magnolias is in the parks. The gardens are buried in the bright yellow petals of the broom and in the delicate lace of sakura.

In May, the daily temperature reaches 18 ° C. Crowds of tourists flock to the metropolis again. Prices in hotels after a slight lull begin to skyrocket. Ostas 3 * (Prague) is no exception. The closer to summer, the less available rooms. With the onset of June, the first heat arrives in the Czech capital. The thermometer column reaches 23 ° C. In July and August - 25 ° C.

According to tourists, the most beautiful time in these parts is autumn. Even an elegant winter can not compete with her. Numerous squares try on golden brocade. The emerald carpet is replaced by a yellow mat. The colored grasses of the hills give the metropolis a special charm.

Key points





Ostas 3 * (Prague) was erected in 1932. The number of rooms does not exceed 50. Next to it is a tram stop. Buses run within a five-minute walk. The road to the very center of the city takes no more than a quarter of an hour. The nearest metro station is Florenc.

The hotel has a fashionable restaurant and comfortable apartments. All guests enjoy free internet access. Meals are carried out according to the buffet system. The price of a ticket to Ostas 3 * (Prague) includes breakfast. The organization of lunches and dinners for tourist groups.

Family suites and non-smoking rooms are available. The front desk is open 24 hours a day. The elevator is working. The hotel laundry offers its services. Registration of new customers occurs from 14:00 to 24:00. You need to leave the room before 11:00. Ostas Hotel 3 * accommodates children of any age. Extra beds are not available.

Pets are not allowed in the hotel. The cash desk of the hotel accepts cash and plastic cards Visa, MasterCard, Maestro, American Express.

Gastronomic card





Located on the territory of Ostas 3 * restaurant offers hearty and delicious breakfasts. Meals are held in the main hall. The service is carried out according to the "Buffet" principle. Travelers celebrate excellent coffee, which is brewed in the bar. It serves pastries, fruits, sandwiches with cheese and smoked meats, cereals, cereals and granola. The assortment of tea and juices.

The restaurant's profile is European cuisine. The nearest catering facilities are located a short drive from the hotel. The BARFuD Bistro is famous for its large portions. In addition, you can have lunch in the taverns of the neighboring hotels Amadeus, City Lounge, Victoria, and Hilton Prague. There are food delivery points at home. Tourists order pizza, Asian noodles, sushi, rolls and meat products.

Neighborhood





Just a couple of minutes walk from the hotel is the Breakout Prague quest center. The entertainment complex opens at 09:00 and ends at 22:30. On average, it takes two hours to complete one game. Participants are offered a variety of stories. The program in the spirit of the communist past of the country is in great demand.

The military museum exhibited samples of modern and Soviet technology. Separate expositions are dedicated to weapons and uniforms. Admission is free. In the near future it will be closed for large-scale reconstruction, which will last until 2020.

Cimrman English Theater puts on comic and satirical performances. Its uniqueness lies in the fact that the artists of the theater took the traditional Czech humor, which was translated into English, as the basis of their work. Tourists really like the surroundings. They are delighted with the performances and recommend the theater to their friends. This is a great place to relax in the company of loved ones.

Reviews

Considering room types in Ostas 3 *, many visitors make a mistake. Traveling alone with a child, choose a double suite. But in vain! Much more comfortable in apartments for three adults. It should be borne in mind that the audibility in the hotel is excellent. The rooms overlooking the carriageway are bright and comfortable, but in the morning it can be noisy.

Those rooms, from the windows of which you can see the courtyard, are damp and dark. This is the best option for those who value peace and quiet. In winter, these rooms are cold. Since the smoking area is located on the open terrace between the second and third levels, it is better to settle in far away from this part of the building. The hotel has five floors. Otherwise, all rooms are almost the same.

Russians do not recommend suites 21 and 31. But 27 are praised. If you need an iron, hairdryer or electric kettle, you need to contact the reception desk. Reception staff never fail. Some tourists were unsatisfied with the quality of cosmetic supplies. According to them, soap and shampoo very dry the skin and can cause allergic reactions.
