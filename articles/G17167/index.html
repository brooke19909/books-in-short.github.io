Faulty washing machine. Possible malfunctions of the washing machine

The washing machine has a habit of failing. Often the owner does not know what the cause of the breakdown is, and quickly grabs the phone to call the wizard. In principle, everything is true. But the problem may not be so big, and it will be completely possible to eliminate it by our own efforts. But in order not to aggravate the situation, you should know exactly what to repair. So, the topic of our conversation today is "Faulty washing machine." We will discuss the main reasons for the failure of the unit and ways to eliminate breakdowns.





Common problems

About 90% of washing machines break down due to their illiterate installation or improper operation.

As a rule, housewives complain of such problems:

  • clogged drain system;
  • drum overload;
  • the machine does not turn on;
  • water does not heat up;
  • problems with pouring water into the car;
  • strong vibration or abnormal sounds;
  • torn pens and other factors.

More often, the repair shop receives equipment with a clogged drain system. And all because small foreign objects get there, which we forget or simply do not consider it necessary to remove dirty clothes from the pockets. They pose a serious danger to technology: they damage the tank, pump and other elements. To remove them, you often have to disassemble the unit.

Torn handles often fall into the category ‚ÄúPossible malfunctions of the washing machine‚Äù. This is due to the fact that the hatch of the washing machine is equipped with a locking system. This block is removed 3 minutes after the end of the machine. But some people stubbornly do not want to remember this fact and make every effort to open the door, as a result of which they tear off the handle.

No less often, malfunctions arise due to problems with the cord or outlet.

Troubleshooting Methods

Owners of this kind of equipment should know what actions should be taken in case of a breakdown. It doesn‚Äôt matter what brand of unit you have - LG, Indesit, Bosch, Samsung washing machine ... Faults of various models are of a general nature. So, we will analyze the most common ones.

  • Problem : The machine does not drain water.

Reason : clogged drain system.

What to do : contact the master.

  • Problem : equipment does not heat water.

Reason : The heating element has burned out.

What to do : call the wizard.

  • Problem : The machine does not turn on.

Reason : malfunction of a button, socket or surge protector; malfunctioning of the hatch lock device; unit control unit is out of order.

What to do : it is necessary to check the socket with a tester or other device; if it is working, you must contact the workshop.

  • Problem: the operation of the equipment is accompanied by extraneous sounds.

Reason : foreign objects getting into the drum.

What to do : inspect the drum and remove solid objects; if this cannot be done on your own, specialist assistance will be required.

  • Problem : Water is visible under the machine.

Reason : tank is leaking, the fill / drain system is faulty, damage to the manhole cuffs.

What to do : contact the master.

  • Problem : The drum does not spin.

Reason : drive belt is torn; problems with the engine or control system.

What to do : contact the workshop.

In addition to all of the above, a malfunction of the washing machine often occurs due to improper installation. The equipment should be placed only on a flat surface and do not forget to remove the transport bolts.

Samsung Faults

A washing machine of this brand may have malfunctions typical for products of other manufacturers:

  • the drum rotates unevenly;
  • heating elements break down;
  • there is water under the machine;
  • rusting body.




This list of issues can be expanded. But the Samsung company produces washing equipment, which is equipped with an electronic display. The malfunction code of the washing machine is displayed on the screen. Knowing its designation, you can quickly identify the cause of the breakdown and take appropriate measures. So, what are the numbers on the scoreboard telling us:

  • E 1 - error in the water inlet.
  • E 2 - error draining water.
  • E 3 - the tank is full of water.
  • DE, DOOR - the hatch door is open or not tightly closed.
  • E 4 - the imbalance of the laundry placed in the drum.
  • E 7 - the water level sensor is defective.
  • E 8 - the temperature regime of water does not correspond to the norm.
  • E 9 - water leakage.

Repair of the Samsung washing machine

To reset the error code, the unit must be turned off and on again. When contacting the workshop, specialists should be informed of the error number, then they will advise how to fix the breakdown and quickly select the necessary spare parts for repair.





It should be noted that the Samsung washing machine, the malfunctions of which we described just above, is well repairable. And if it is properly exploited, then it rarely breaks. But if trouble has happened, you have to fix it. Do not rush to call neighbor Vasya, who at least knows how to hold a screwdriver in his hands. All the same, you do not need this tool. So, let's discuss what to do in the event of a breakdown.

With error E1, it is necessary to check the presence of water in the water supply and the pressure level. If there is no problem with this, you need to contact a repair specialist.

Errors E 2, E 3 also require inspection by the master.

To eliminate the DE, DOOR error, you must re-close the hatch or check the health of the hatch device and the electronic controller.

Error E 4 is eliminated by a uniform distribution of linen in the drum, including an increase in its volume. If after that the machine still does not want to function, it is necessary to contact specialists.

With errors E 7, E 8, E 9, the washing equipment should be inspected by the master.





LG malfunctions

This brand has established itself well, therefore, in many houses you can see the units of the mentioned manufacturer. But, like any technique, it can fail. Failures of the LG washing machine are typical:

  • Rattling caused by foreign objects entering the drum or pump.
  • Knocking (may indicate a large amount of loaded laundry).
  • Vibration (occurs due to the installation of equipment on an uneven surface or uneven distribution of laundry in the drum).
  • Water leakage (observed due to clogged drain pipes and poor connection of the hose to the tap or washing machine).
  • The drum does not rotate - the reason lies in the poorly closed door.
  • The machine does not turn on (the water tap may have been shut off or the power cord is not connected to the outlet).




LG troubleshooting

The products of this company may have the same problems as the Samsung washing machine. In some cases, its malfunctions can also be eliminated independently. But more often you still have to turn to specialists for help. So, what do housewives usually complain about, and what to do in such situations?

If an excessive amount of foam forms during washing using the LG technique, you need to reduce the amount of powder to fall asleep or change its type.

When water slowly enters the washing machine, this may indicate insufficient pressure. Check whether the tap is open and the intake hose is not squeezed.

Failures of the LG washing machine are often associated with problems with the filter of the drainage pump. In order for the machine to last for a long time, it is necessary to periodically check its condition. It can hold small items when washing clothes and even threads.

If the water leaves the machine too slowly, check the drainage hose and filter for clogging.

Zanussi Challenges

The Zanussi washing machine, the malfunctions of which, as a rule, are associated with the natural wear of parts or hidden defects, rarely needs repair. No wonder the equipment of this brand is included in the group of the most reliable equipment. But individual breakdowns can be observed. The most common:

  • bearing wear;
  • clogged filter;
  • stopping the collection of washing powder;
  • shutdown in spontaneous mode;
  • engine problems.

Often, owners of equipment complain of incomplete drainage of water at the end of the wash. This is due to a clogged drain filter.

Troubleshooting



If the malfunction of the washing machine is caused by a clogged drain filter, it can be cleaned on its own. When the blockage is eliminated, it is necessary to check the operability of the equipment. If the problem persists, pay attention to the drain pump winding and check the operation of the electronic controller.

But if the drive motor fails, then you can not do without the help of a specialist. Such a breakdown occurs due to a short circuit on the control triac or as a result of failure of the coil of the tachogenerator sensor.

By the way, the Zanussi washing machine detects malfunctions using a self-diagnosis system. It allows you to automatically check the status of components, equipment parts and displays an error code on the monitor screen.

Bosch Common Problems

The technique of this brand is distinguished by quality and ease of use. Bosch washing machine malfunctions also shows on the electronic display. To know their cause and method of elimination, you must remember what this or that error code means:

  • F01 / F16 is associated with a malfunction in the sunroof lock.
  • F02 / F17 - water supply problem.
  • F03 / F18 - malfunction of the drain system.
  • F04 - water leak.
  • F19 / F22 - The heater is defective.
  • F20 - a problem with the temperature sensor or TENA relay.
  • F21 - malfunctions in the engine.
  • F23 - problems in aquastop.
  • F25 - breakdown of the aquasensor.
  • F26 / F27 - malfunction of the pressure switch.
  • F28 / F29 - Problem with the water flow sensor.
  • F40 - malfunction in the voltage supply.
  • F63 - module failure.

Bosch owners often complain about a malfunction of the washing machine, such as clogged drain pipes. The cause of this failure is a manufacturing defect and improper operation of the equipment.

For any problem, it is better to contact the masters for help.
