How to make amulets and amulets with your own hands?

Beliefs that success in life's affairs come from specially created objects have been around since ancient times. Each nation has its own customs of making charms from the evil eye and the influence of dark forces. If you want to test your skills and make amulets with your own hands, then you can easily achieve your goal. The case is fascinating and simple enough.





To give a little thing "magical properties", it is necessary to consider who it is intended for. It is necessary to make a talisman only on the growing moon or on the new moon. It also matters the day of the week. For example, the environment is under the auspices of Mercury, so it is suitable for creating an amulet that attracts good luck in business. Friday is dedicated to Venus. On this day, you can weave an amulet with your own hands for love, to contribute to luck in romantic affairs. Further days and planets are distributed as follows. Tuesday corresponds to the warlike Mars, Thursday - to the leader of Jupiter. It is also necessary to check with the astrological clock, because each of them is given the name of a particular planet. Engaged in needlework, you need to mentally say to yourself what purpose the talisman will serve. The direction of your desire should be one: love, wealth, family well-being. Mental mood is also very important, it should be positive. The most diverse materials can be useful: semiprecious stones, yarn, beads, pieces of leather, matter. Everything that is attractive to you. When the amulets and charms made with their own hands are finished, they must always be carried with them.





Now consider specific examples. One of the most affordable options is to make a bracelet. For it, it is necessary to select stones or beads devoid of chips or cracks. Their number in the talisman should be odd, but not more than five. The most suitable stone is amber, it accumulates energy in itself. Then all prepared elements are strung on a thread (the largest stone is placed in the center) and its ends are connected. This amulet will protect you.





The amulets embroidered with their own hands served for the same purpose. Since ancient times, women made them; they decorated clothes, household items with symbols. For example, used solar signs, runes. Modern craftswomen can decorate a purse, pendant, keychain in the same way. At the same time, attention should be paid to the quality of cross-stitch. On the wrong side, the threads should not overlap and it is important that there are no knots. Otherwise, they will become an obstacle to the embodiment of your desire. If you need amulets that attract money, use your own hands to draw Othel and Fehu runes on a piece of leather, a plaque, or embroider runes on the fabric. In this case, it is necessary to give preference to red and gold color.

A talisman made in the form of a bag will help in romantic affairs. It is very easy to make it from a small square of fabric, along the perimeter of which you need to skip the ties. You can put any item in it that you associate with love relationships: dried flower petals, candle cinders , etc. Thus, you will create individually configured amulets, with your own hands you will attract good luck and happiness.
