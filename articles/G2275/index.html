MTS 982T smartphone: review and owner reviews

MTS has proposed a new model of a budget smartphone - 982T. Like all other phones manufactured under this brand, the communicator is blocked for using SIM cards of other operators. Up to ten attempts to unlock the phone are provided. However, with the use of special programs, it is still possible to connect to operators other than MTS. In this case, you get an inexpensive smartphone and do not overpay for often unnecessary additions to the device, and you do not need to invest additional funds in the competition and advertising of manufacturers. Mobile phones from telecom operators are often very successful. Is such a smartphone MTS 982T, we can find out if we test it. Details below.

MTS 982T: appearance review



The packing box is made of red cardboard, it has the usual rectangular shape with the image of a smartphone, the logo of a mobile operator, the name of the phone and a brief description of the characteristics of the MTS 982T. The device comes with a charger, a headset and a USB cable. Taking a smartphone in our hands, we can hardly call it thin: the thickness of the case is 12.3 mm. In general, a mobile phone with a four-inch display is quite compact. The case is made with inexpensive plastic, but the touch is quite pleasant. To protect against damage to the screen, a metal rim runs along its entire perimeter. At the bottom of the display are three familiar function buttons on the touch controls. On the top of the smartphone there are mechanical keys to lock the screen and adjust the volume. The back of the case is entirely made of matte plastic. The speaker and camera of the device are reliably recessed into the case and are framed by protective frames made of metal. On the bottom there are holes for charging and connecting to a computer, and on the top we can see a jack for connecting headphones or a stereo headset.

MTS 982T: quality reviews



For such an inexpensive phone, quite high-quality materials were used, the assembly is not satisfactory. The back cover is removable, manufacturers have tried to securely attach it to the case. To open the device, you have to work hard. But this feature is rather useful, because when using a smartphone less dust and moisture will get inside.

Performance and Features



The four-inch display of the mobile device has a resolution of 800x480 pixels. This is quite enough for daily work, because the text is viewed on such a small screen quite clearly. The brightness can be adjusted independently, in direct sunlight on the phone, the parameters are displayed to the maximum. The contrast and color rendition of the display are not satisfactory; using the phone is quite convenient.

The MTS 982T smartphone is fast enough when it comes to launching applications, turning pages, or using the Internet. With this mobile device, of course, it is impossible to launch heavy games, but this is not required of him for his price category.

The 1400 mAh battery on the MTS 982T mobile phone is removable, without recharging you can manage up to fourteen hours of active use of the device (calls, sending messages, chatting on social networks, web surfing). When watching videos or playing games continuously, the battery will last for six hours.

The smartphone supports wireless connection, bluetooth 4.0, radio, can work on the Internet using 3G.

At the maximum allowable load, the mobile device does not heat up, which indicates a good balance of the phone.

The smartphone is programmed to support MTS only SIM cards, but if necessary, with the help of special programs it can be adapted to the use of other operators.

Photography



The camera of the MTS 982T smartphone supports a 3.2 megapixel module and a flash. This is enough to take high-quality pictures for social networks. The device interface is simple, without using additional built-in functions. There are practically no settings for photo and video shooting, you can especially not bother and shoot in automatic mode.

Thus, this budget smartphone fully pays for the cost with its functionality.
