Hotel Central Hotel Prague 3 * Prague: overview, description and reviews

The capital of the Czech Republic is not in vain called the "golden". Prague, combining European comfort and service, ancient architecture, colorful local traditions and delicious national cuisine, is rightfully considered one of the most popular tourist destinations. Even a ten-day trip may not be enough to have time to explore all the old and modern sights of the city and its environs, wander around the famous places for shopping, taste local desserts and famous varieties of Czech beer. Therefore, it is so important to find a comfortable hotel for your stay, located near the largest number of historical places that Zlata Prague is known for. Prague Central Hotel Prague 3 * - a hotel in the center of the Czech capital, annually collecting a lot of positive reviews on Russian forums for tourists. Why is this hotel so popular with our compatriots? How do the guests of the old capital's hotel evaluate the conditions of accommodation and rest at Central Hotel Prague 3 * (Prague 1)?





Address, Location

Central Hotel Prague 3 * is located in the Old Town (Prague 1 district ), in the center of the historical part of the capital city. Within walking distance from the hotel are the famous Powder Gate and the old Old Town Square - the place where most sightseeing tours traditionally begin. Hotel guests are available taxi parking, all types of public transport. There is a metro station nearby.

The address of the Central Hotel Prague 3 *: Czech Republic, Prague 1, Rubna street, No. 8.

Description

Central Hotel Prague 3 * (Prague) is a three-star hotel, the history of which lasts for almost a century. The seven-story hotel building was built in the early thirties of the last century. It offers guests 50 rooms of different levels of comfort.

In 2008, the old building was renovated with the replacement of equipment and furniture. The hotel positions itself as an inexpensive hotel for a relaxing family vacation.





Infrastructure

Infrastructure Central Hotel Prague 3 * includes:

  • Reception 24-hour reception.
  • A restaurant.
  • Lobby bar.
  • Beauty saloon.
  • Laundry / dry cleaning / ironing.
  • Car parking.
  • Elevator.
  • Smoking area.

The hotel has free Wi-Fi access. Several superior rooms on the 6th floor of the hotel have a balcony-terrace with city views. When checking in, you need to pay a city tax.





Paid services

For the convenience of guests, the administration of the Central Hotel hotel can offer guests a number of additional services that are not included in the room rate:

  • Room service.
  • Rental of various vehicles.
  • Organization of excursions at the hotel agency.
  • Banquet and conference room rental.
  • Transfer (flight from the airport and back).
  • Baby-sitter (nanny).
  • Parking for guests.
  • Minibar (not available in every room).
  • Accommodation with pets (by prior reservation).




Conditions for relaxing with children

The administration of the metropolitan hotel Central Hotel Prague (Prague 1) 3 * separately emphasizes its status as a family hotel. Here, guests are provided with the entire range of services for families with children that is standard for European countries:

  • A child under the age of 6 years stays in the hotel room for free (without providing a separate bed for sleeping).
  • For children up to the age of three, a cot is provided for free in the room upon request; the hotel restaurant has high children's chairs.
  • The breakfast menu at the hotel contains dishes suitable for baby food: juices, fruits, muffins, granola, pastries, milk and more.
  • If necessary, the hotel can provide professional babysitting services for an additional fee.

Rooms: interior, equipment, services

Hotel guests are offered two types of rooms:

  • Standard deluxe, 20 m¬≤, designed to accommodate from 1 to 3 guests.
  • Superior Standard, 30 m¬≤, for 3-4 guests.

Each room is equipped with:

  • Beds by the number of guests, chairs, table.
  • Safe (included in the room rate).
  • Bathroom (shower or bath, toilet).
  • A satellite TV is provided.
  • Central air conditioning.
  • Hair dryer.

  • Radio.

Important! The hotel does not have rooms, facilities and equipment for people with disabilities.

Reviews on room quality and service level

About accommodation in Central Hotel Prague 3 * reviews for the most part are quite positive. Guests satisfied with their stay at the hotel are much more than those who left negative reviews. What positive impressions do the hotel guests share?

  • The rooms are clean. The rooms are very spacious and well lit.
  • Room equipment corresponds to the three-star status of the hotel. The rooms are quite decent furniture, although not the most fashionable interior.
  • The bathroom is very large and spacious. Although sometimes it is not complete without minor flaws. For example, the absence of a waterproof curtain. By the way, not a single complaint about the lack of water in the hotel, cold or hot, was found.
  • The room is cleaned daily, hygiene items (gel, soap, toilet paper, etc.) are laid out, bedding is changed.
  • From the windows and balconies of the hotel there is a very beautiful view of the Old Town. If the windows overlook the Town Hall, a loud, melodic bell ringing of a tower clock is heard every hour.
  • All equipment in the room (hairdryer, refrigerator, TV, kettle) is working properly. On TV, if you have time to watch TV shows, you can find at least 6 channels in Russian.




What were dissatisfied with the Russian guests staying in this Prague hotel?

  • High audibility in the rooms. As acknowledged by the guests, literally hear telephone conversations in the next room.
  • Not too new towels.
  • Modest interior.
  • Cool room temperature.

Nutrition

The price of accommodation in the Central Hotel Prague 3 * includes breakfast buffet. Every day, guests are offered a choice of dishes from the usual European hotel menu: pastries, slices for vegetable salads and fruits, fresh juices, eggs and fried eggs, several types of sausages and cheeses, honey, jams, tea, granola and more. The restaurant has a coffee machine for making aromatic coffee beans. In addition, a lobby bar with an excellent drinks and snacks card welcomes visitors on site.





Tourists also have no problems organizing lunch or breakfast in the city. Near the hotel, as in any other part of Prague, there are many small and large cafes, restaurants and famous Czech pubs with a good selection of snacks. In addition, you can purchase food a few meters from the hotel, in the supermarket TC "Kotva".

Staff

A special mention, perhaps, deserves the work of employees of the Central Hotel Prague 3 * (Prague). Reviews of Russians visiting this Czech hotel range from enthusiastic to sharply negative. The tourist who left positive feedback about the work of the staff most often tells the following:

  • Many are happy with the lack of a language barrier. While most of our compatriots in African or Asian hotels have to communicate their requests to staff through Russian-speaking guides and travel agents, then at Central Hotel Prague 3 * you can easily find an employee who speaks Russian - both at the reception and among the maids. In addition, receptionists speak English.
  • Hotel workers are often described as polite and welcoming people.
  • The overwhelming majority of former guests were satisfied with the level of room service and the diligence of the staff.

Entertainment and leisure

There are no special entertainments or events at the hotel. But they are not particularly necessary. As unanimously the reviews of former guests of Central Hotel Prague 3 * (Czech Republic, Prague), excursions to the famous Karlovy Vary or small nearby towns, day trips to Germany or Austria will completely fill the days of relaxation and make your vacation unforgettable.

In addition, many attractions are located near the Central Hotel, right in the Old Town. For this reason, most tourists prefer instead of organized paid excursions to buy a guide to the Czech capital and travel on their own, by public transport or on foot.
