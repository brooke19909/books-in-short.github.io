The city is a tourist city, because its attractions cause admiration and delight among Russian and foreign tourists. In this article we will consider the most popular hotels in Azov, which are recommended for vacationers to stop.

Amaks Hotel

Amax Hotel with its cozy atmosphere is considered a great place to relax and conduct seminars, conferences, negotiations. This hotel will help you organize meetings, meals and accommodation. Fans of pair procedures will appreciate the real Russian bathhouse located at the institution.

Hotel "Amax" gives its guests the opportunity to combine relaxation with a party in billiards. It is also worth noting that for guests with poor health there is a computer diagnostics room and a dental office. The guests who arrived here by private car will not have problems with parking: the territory has its own parking lot.

Guest House "Azov"

Hotel "Azov" offers its guests a variety of amenities, including: free private parking on site and free internet. Each room is equipped with a TV. A kettle is at guests' disposal. A 24-hour reception desk is open here. Not far from here is Rostov-on-Don, a little further - Taganrog.





Hotel Alpha

This institution is perfect for businessmen, honeymooners and tourists who get acquainted with the sights of Azov. For each guest of the city, the hotel will gladly offer a room that will fully correspond to its tastes and income level. Throughout the year, friendly staff will be happy to meet you and tell you about all the sights of the city that are worth seeing.

"Sunny"

This economy class Azov hotel has a very advantageous location - in the city center on Petrovskaya Square, near the descent to the Azovka River. Here, guests are provided with 3 suites, 31 double, 9 single, one five-seater 2-room, 7 triple, in addition, 4- and 6-seater rooms equipped with comfortable bunk beds. Moreover, each room has a refrigerator, telephone, shower, TV, toilet.





Hotel Scher Hof

We continue to consider the hotels in Azov. Overlooking the outdoor pool, this complex is located in the city center, near Rostov-on-Don. Here guests have at their disposal a SPA center, an outdoor pool, a restaurant, a private beach and a bar. Free private parking is available on site. Other amenities include a TV with many channels. The nearest airport is 32 kilometers away.

Grand hotel soho

Of course, and this is not all Azov hotels. Soho Grand Hotel offers a restaurant throughout the year, free Wi-Fi throughout and free parking. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV and air conditioning, while some have stunning views of the city or river. All rooms have a bathroom with a large bathtub. Slippers and bathrobes are also provided. The reception is open 24 hours a day.





Hotel Prestige

Pets are allowed in this small establishment with free internet. Free private parking is available on site. Room amenities include a TV. Guests can always use the shared kitchen. You can also play billiards here.
