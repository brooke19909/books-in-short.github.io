Hotel "Continental", Belgorod: photos and reviews of tourists

The Continental Hotel (Belgorod) offers comfortable accommodation to people who have arrived on a business trip, or to ordinary tourists.





Brief description of the hotel

If you often travel on business trips, then the Continental Hotel is just perfect for you. Belgorod provides great business opportunities, and this hotel will provide comfortable accommodation. Here, in luxuriously decorated apartments, you will feel real home comfort, because for this all necessary amenities are provided, as well as a set of household appliances.

All services that you may need on a business trip will be provided by the Continental Hotel (Belgorod). Guests really like the lobby, where you can enjoy a cup of coffee in a relaxed atmosphere, as well as the games room with a pool table, darts and other entertainment. There is also wireless internet and a gym. In addition, everything in this hotel is provided for the convenience of persons with disabilities.

Restaurants - this is what the Continental Hotel (Belgorod) can be proud of. The coffee house is very popular, where, among other things, a huge selection of confectionery is presented. Also visit the restaurant on the terrace, where you can not only taste the most delicious dishes of Russian cuisine, but also enjoy the beautiful panoramas of the city.





Hotel Location

The Continental Hotel is about 2 kilometers from the train station, but the airport will have to cover a distance of about 9 kilometers. Very close are such popular tourist sites as Cathedral Square, the Kursk Bulge Museum, as well as the huge Victory Park. Since this is the central part of the city, a huge number of shops, restaurants and entertainment venues are located nearby.

Room Amenities

Continental Business Hotel provides the following amenities in guest apartments:

  • The door is opened using a special electronic key, which simultaneously serves as an activator of all household functions in the room;
  • due to the fact that the room has an individual air conditioning, you can independently adjust the air temperature;
  • free wireless internet access;
  • modern TV, which gives you the opportunity to view a huge number of satellite channels;
  • there is always drinking water in the minibar, and for an additional fee it is also filled with alcohol and refreshments;
  • in each room there is a small safe that will ensure the safety of your valuables and documents;
  • landline telephone is connected to both domestic and international lines; all pillows and blankets are made of hypoallergenic materials;
  • the bathroom is equipped with a shower, as well as a hairdryer, bath accessories and a set of cosmetics;
  • There is an electric kettle, a service, as well as tea, coffee and sugar bags.

Hotel services "Continental"

Arriving at this hotel, guests can expect to receive a wide range of services:

  • 24-hour reception, the staff of which is always ready to help you;
  • if necessary, for an additional fee, it is possible to abolish some bureaucratic procedures, thereby reducing the duration of registration;
  • You can use cashless terminals or withdraw funds from a card using an ATM;
  • the concierge will meet you at the entrance, help you bring your suitcases to your room, and also familiarize yourself with the layout of the hotel and provide background information on its infrastructure facilities.

The provision of additional services, such as the delivery of food and newspapers in the room, as well as the organization of excursions and other things, are discussed individually.





Hotel Infrastructure

Due to the developed infrastructure, guests can enjoy a wide variety of services at the hotel. So, it is worth noting especially the work of the following objects:

  • a large terrace so that you can enjoy the views of the city;
  • in the sauna you can relax both physically and emotionally;
  • thanks to the work of the fitness center, you will always be in excellent physical shape;
  • in the games room for organizing your leisure time, billiards, table tennis, as well as darts and other entertainment are provided;
  • in the massage room, specialists in therapeutic as well as relaxing massage work;

  • free wireless internet;
  • next to the hotel there is a private parking zone, which can be used by each guest;
  • the conference room has all the conditions for business events;
  • at the hairdresser you can do a manicure, a spectacular hairstyle or undergo cosmetic procedures;
  • your celebrations can be held in a beautifully decorated banquet room;
  • On the territory of the hotel there are shops with groceries, household chemicals, as well as souvenirs.




Positive reviews

The services of the Continental Hotel cause a lot of positive feedback from guests:

  • the hotel staff treat each guest as an important person, which is incredibly nice;
  • the hotel rooms are very well planned, and therefore the accommodation in them is incredibly comfortable;
  • the breakfast included in the price is very tasty and satisfying;
  • if you need any hygiene items, the hotel staff will quickly and absolutely free provide you with them;
  • very comfortable orthopedic mattresses on beds;
  • simply excellent quality of food in the restaurant;
  • very convenient location of the hotel;
  • good parking with convenient access and 24-hour security;
  • there are many pillows of different sizes on the bed, and therefore you can choose the one that suits you;
  • unobtrusive music in a restaurant that will make your meal even more enjoyable.

General impression

The hotel business is endless competition, and therefore each hotel seeks to take its place in the sun. The Continental Hotel is positioning itself as a luxury hotel for sophisticated guests. It is worth noting that the types of services in the hotel are the most numerous, and therefore each of the guests will find something to fill their leisure with. Nevertheless, this hotel is quite expensive. This is due not only to the luxurious design, but also to the fact that the hotel is located in the very center of the city. With all this, the hotel staff should be a little quick.
