DIY doormats for newborns: what you need to know

The developing mat has already become an integral attribute in the arsenal of children's entertainment. It belongs to the category of educational gaming tools that contribute to the manifestation and development of intelligence, creativity, imagination, the establishment of causal relationships, concentration, the development of motor skills, learning ability and curiosity. Many parents after the birth of the baby prefer to buy developing mats for newborns.

Of course, not all of them do it with their own hands, but the number of people who want to do it is steadily growing. The reasons for all are different, but the main thing is to give your baby the best. Unfortunately, purchased rugs very often cannot boast of rich functionality, in addition, sometimes the quality of materials also raises questions. The cost of these products is also not in last place: sometimes there simply is no money, or they can find more important uses (food, clothes, strollers, etc.).

Developmental mats for newborns

Do it yourself is not easy, but possible. In principle, any mom (or dad) can handle such a task, although it seems to many that this is not feasible. In the end, you will make do-it-yourself developmental mats for newborns for your own child, and not for an exhibition.

Much more important is not the aesthetic factor, but the love and soul embedded in the product. But one must also strive for visual beauty and accuracy so that a child develops good taste from childhood. So, where to start if you decide to make do-it-yourself developmental mats for newborns? First, think about the theme or plot of your future creation. It can be one storyline or a combination of several. For example, you decided to sew a rug on the theme of "Farm" for the study of animals by the baby. But there you can "enter" another farmer's family, which will also participate in the training process. In general, the subject can be different, it is important that it is interesting for the baby and has a semantic load.

Developmental mat for newborns. Photos, tips for work

First, make a sketch of your creation, determine its size. Then proceed to audit the bins in order to find all kinds of fabrics there in the form of scraps or unnecessary things.

Collect everything you find, discard too shabby or suspicious materials. Remember that the fabric must be safe, preferably natural and clean. Yes, everything that you select for work, it is better to wash before starting work, so that later there will be no surprises with the finished rug. If you have animals, then cut them out of some fabric where they are painted, or cut them yourself. Remember that the texture should be different: smooth, rough, convex, soft, dense, etc. As a filler, you can use foam rubber or synthetic winterizer. The arcs on the mat are mainly made from a cut plastic children's hoop. Sewn parts must hold tight, so take the time and thread. If you use accessories (locks, dogs, buttons), then it must also be securely attached. Children really like to open pockets, doors, windows, so foresee this and be sure to do them. If any items are removable, then buy special Velcro in the store. That's about how you can make a developmental mat for newborns. The feedback from those who use hand-made rugs is invariably positive.
