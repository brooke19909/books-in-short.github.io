Hotel Best Maritim 3 * (Spain / Costa Dorada): photos, reviews

Our article will interest those tourists who are planning a vacation in Spain, but are looking for cheap and good hotels. We want to talk about one of these budget complexes. Best Maritim 3 * (Spain) is a three-star hotel located in the Cambrils area of ​​Vilafortuni.

A bit about the hotel ...

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain) is located on the first line and consists of one four-story building. The hotel is 112 kilometers from the airport in Barcelona and 12 kilometers from the airport in Reus. The city of Tarragona is located 14 kilometers from the complex.



The hotel was built in 1983 and is currently located in the center of the tourist area of ​​Cambrils. As we already mentioned, the hotel has a three-star status, but in terms of quality of service and a range of services, it is approaching a four-star one. The last reconstruction of the complex was carried out in 1998.

Number of rooms

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain) is one of the best hotels in its category in the entire Costa Dorada. In his arsenal there are numbers of the following categories:

  1. Standard room. Some apartments have a beautiful sea view. These rooms have an area of ​​22 square meters. m. In total, the hotel has 456 apartments of this category.
  2. Master suite - three-room rooms with two bedrooms and a living room with an area of ​​about 80 square meters. m. The apartment can comfortably accommodate six people. The complex has eight such rooms.
  3. Junior suite - one-room rooms with a sleeping and living area. The area of ​​the apartment is about 802 square meters. m
  4. Eight rooms for people with disabilities.

Dining at the hotel

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain) serves food to guests in a buffet format. The main restaurant serves Spanish and international dishes.



Guests of the complex can optionally choose the food option convenient for them: half board, full board and continental breakfast. The hotel also has two cafe bars and a snack bar. Service in the restaurant is organized at a high level.

Complex infrastructure

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain), the photo of which is given in the article, has a well-developed infrastructure, thanks to which tourists have at hand everything that is needed for a complete comfortable stay. The complex has two swimming pools (indoor and outdoor with fresh water), car rental, business center, currency exchange, TV lounge, gym, beauty salon, sauna.

Sports and Entertainment

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain, Cambrils) offers tourists to visit the gym, tennis courts, play billiards, table tennis, visit the massage room, hairdresser, medical center, shopping gallery, internet cafe, game machines. During the day, the hotel hosts sporting events (archery, darts, water aerobics, petanque), and shows in the evenings are organized.

Beach

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain, Costa Dorada, Cambrils) does not have its own beach, like all European hotels. Guests can use the nearby municipal beach. It is equipped with paid sunbeds and umbrellas. The poolside hotel has a free relaxation area. In addition, tourists can relax on the terrace with hammocks.

Children's holiday

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain, Cambrils, Costa Dorada) takes care of the rest of children. On its territory there is a mini-club in which animators are occupied by young guests. In the evenings, a children's disco is organized for the kids. In addition, the hotel has a playground with attractions.

A little bit about the resort ...

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain, Costa Daurada) is located near the small resort town of Cambrils, near Tarragona and Salou. In the past, the city was an old fishing port and is now a recreation area with a ten-kilometer beach. The main advantage of the resort is the sea, the pristine coast and a relaxing holiday.



Some local hotels are located directly on the beach. Every day, local fishermen bring fresh fish to the restaurants of the complexes.

Cambrils has an area of ​​more than 35 square meters. km Conventionally, the town is divided into three parts: port (with hotels and entertainment centers), historical (with medieval quarters and old houses) and Villa Fortuni - a territory built specifically for vacationers. Cambrils combines modern tourism and national traditions. People of different incomes come to the resort to relax.

The city is located on the Mediterranean Sea, so there is a mild climate without sudden temperature changes. Pyrenees and Catalan mountains reliably protect the coast from cyclones and winds. The proximity of the mountains affects the temperature. On the coast - 150 sunny days a year, however, there is never a debilitating heat (+26). In winter, the temperature does not fall below +13 degrees. The season lasts from May to the end of October.

Cambrils beaches

The coastline of the resort has a length of about ten kilometers. It is divided into 8 beaches: Cavet, Vilafortuni, Esquirol, Riera, Llosa, Horta Santa Maria, Cap de Sant Pere, Ardica. In different periods, they were all awarded the Blue Flag.



On the most popular of them there is a yacht club, a sailing school, a windsurfing club. All the beaches of the city have a gentle entry into the sea, so the resort is suitable for families with kids. Each of them is equipped with changing cabins, playgrounds, toilets, bars, places for sports, here you can also rent umbrellas and sunbeds.

Resort Attractions

Almost all the attractions of Cambrils are located in the port quarter and the Old Town. The most important of them is Park-Sama. This ensemble with unique architecture was designed in 1982 by Josepe Fonser. It consists of a park and a colonial-style palace. The building looks amazing surrounded by Mediterranean and tropical gardens, in the center of which is an artificial lake.

Tourists are interested in Villa Fortuna Castle, which was built in the 12th century. In the port, in the tower, exhibitions of artworks devoted to the history of the city are often held.

Historical sights include the medieval convent of Convento de Escurnalbu, the chapel of De La Verge del Cami, the church of Iglesias de Santa Maria. Tourists are always interested in the emerald museum, where you can not only learn a lot of interesting things about how stones are mined, but also purchase products from them.

Sightseeing trips

Resting in Best Maritim 3 *, Superior (Spain, Costa Dorada, makes a lasting impression) or another category - it doesn’t matter, you can visit the neighboring resorts: La Pinedo and Salou. Each of these cities has its own beautiful places. In Salou, for example, you can walk along the promenade along the sea, whose length is 6.5 km. The city is famous for its golf clubs and the Port Aventura theme park. In La Pineda, there is the Aquapolis water park with a dolphinarium, pools and slides.



Opposite Cambrils there is another resort area - Montbrio del Camp, it is known for healing thermal springs. Hotels usually offer trips to Barcelona. Yachts for boat trips also depart from Cambrils.

Best Maritim 3 * (Spain): reviews

Speaking about the hotel, we turn to the reviews of vacationers who have already managed to visit it in order to understand how they evaluate this budget complex. According to tourists, the Best Maritim 3 * hotel (Spain) has an excellent location, being on the coast. Only a bicycle path separates the hotel from the beach. The complex will certainly appeal to beach lovers. It is convenient to live here even with the youngest children, since you don’t need to go anywhere far. Everything that tourists need is located either in the hotel or in the immediate vicinity.

What rooms does Best Maritim 3 * (Spain) offer? Guest reviews regarding the apartments are approximately the same. Based on them, some conclusions can be drawn. Some of the rooms in the hotel are freshly renovated, while others would be nice to refresh. All are clean and well maintained, but furnished with slightly outdated furniture. The beds are equipped with good orthopedic mattresses. For children, the hotel gives out clamshells, which many do not like. Of course, they are good, but still this is not a bed.

All appliances in the apartments are new, plumbing is in good condition. The hotel is built in the shape of the letter "P". Naturally, there are rooms in noisy places and in quieter ones. Guests do not recommend lodging in apartments, near which there are extracts from the restaurant, because the buzz from them interferes with them all day and ends no earlier than 23.00. Good views from the rooms overlooking the pool.

Three-room apartments are very spacious and comfortable, but the bedrooms have no windows. More precisely, they exist, but they go to completely unexpected places (on the next balcony or in the public corridor).



Maids are cleaned daily. There are disputes regarding its quality, but in general there are no serious complaints. Towels are also changed daily. The bedding is also beautiful, without flaws.

Nutrition Reviews

The food at Best Maritim 3 * deserves special praise. Reviews of tourists say that the food in the hotel corresponds to four stars. The main restaurant serves a varied buffet. For breakfast, food is usually simpler. But at the same time, all kinds of drinks are served free of charge, including champagne. For dinner and lunch, a noticeable variety appears on the tables. Especially delicious are seafood and meat cooked with you on the grill. Very often themed nights are organized in the restaurant: Mexican, Catalan, etc. At lunch and in the evening, drinks are not included in the price of the meal, they must be paid separately. In general, the food is very tasty and good. When leaving for excursions, you can order a lunch package, but its quality is much different from the food in the restaurant.

The hotel is always crowded with people, not even in high season. The restaurant sometimes does not cope with the influx of people, so in the evenings there are lines. The waiters are fast and always happy with the visitors.

General impression of the hotel

As we mentioned, the hotel is located on the coast. To get to the beach, you just need to cross the bike path. The sandy coast of the resort is beautiful and wide, with a gentle entry into the sea. The water is very clean and warm, sometimes algae nails to the shore, but there are very few of them. Sun beds and umbrellas on the coast are paid, since the beach is municipal. However, all vacationers are located in the hotel's free recreation area by the pool, taking up towels in the early morning. And go swimming in the sea. In such a simple way, you can avoid unnecessary expenses.



In the daytime and evening, the hotel has an unobtrusive animation. Everyone can visit water aerobics, play darts and board games. Children really like the evening mini-disco.

There is a stop right in front of the hotel. From here you can leave by bus to the nearest cities or to Barcelona. At the same time, it is completely optional to purchase a tour from local guides. Also nearby is Salou Station, which can be reached on foot. Good transport interchange makes it possible for those tourists who want to see the nearest resorts to travel actively, and not just spend time on the coast.

Near the hotel there are several shops where you can buy wine, groceries and everything you need for tourists. There are also cafes and restaurants here for those who want to try the food of not only the chef of the main restaurant. According to vacationers, the town is very quiet and calm, life in it freezes after 22.00.

There are several excursion bureaus near the hotel where you can book a trip. There are local guides at the hotel. The cost of excursions is very different, it is difficult to say where it is more profitable to purchase them. In general, you can independently see Salou, Barcelona and other cities using public transport services. So, for example, a trip to Barcelona will cost only 9 euros, which is much cheaper than group tours.

The hotel staff deserves special attention, thanks to which the institution has a decent level of service. Literally a few people speak Russian at the hotel, and even that is bad. Most of them understand English well. Therefore, one can always explain. The language barrier is not observed. The staff is attentive and responsive, trying to help at the slightest hint. There is no need to ask for something several times. It is thanks to the employees that such a positive opinion is formed about the hotel.

The hotel complex can safely be considered a good option for a budget holiday. Perhaps this is due to the occupancy of the hotel. There are always many tourists here, even in October and September. Among the guests of the complex there are many Europeans (Germans, French), Russians can be met not so often.

Instead of an afterword

Good reviews at the Best Maritim 3 * hotel (Spain) give reason to recommend it as a great economy class complex for those tourists who cannot afford a more expensive tour or do not want to overpay extra money. I would like to note that the hotel has its pros and cons (we talked about them in the article). What is worth paying more attention to, tourists decide. However, the great popularity and high attendance of the complex speak for themselves. Moreover, the complex is located on the coast, which is very important for a beach-sea vacation.
