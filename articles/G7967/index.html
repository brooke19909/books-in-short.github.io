Club Pasha Beach Hotel (Turkey, Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists

The quiet cozy Club Pasha Beach Hotel complex (formerly called My Dream Beach) opened in 2000 on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea in the tranquil tourist village of Mahmutlar, 125 km from the airport. It is famous for unsurpassed cleanliness, professional service and high service. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is about 4,500 square meters. m. It has swimming pools, recreation areas and entertainment venues.





The hotel accepts people suffering from any physical ailments. Favorable conditions for comfortable living and convenient movement around the territory are created here. The cozy center is more suitable for a relaxing and relaxed holiday with children. But young people are unlikely to like it, because there are no noisy disco clubs.

Near Club Pasha Beach Hotel 3 * a lot of shops are concentrated, on the weekends a market opens. The cost of living in this hotel is much lower than in others, and in terms of quality of service, it is not inferior even to 4-star ones. The hotel has a well-thought out children's program: fun animation with clowns, an outdoor pool and playgrounds equipped with playground equipment. Adult tourists will also find what to do with themselves.

The number of rooms includes 188 rooms of different categories: Standard, Family, Suite. All rooms are spacious, cozy and fully suitable for a comfortable stay. The rooms have a double bed, a bathroom is allocated. On request, rollaway beds and cots are added.

The rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning, direct dial telephone, color TV with cable television. For a fee, tourists can always use the minibar and safe. Some rooms have balconies overlooking the coast. The rooms are cleaned daily at certain hours (the bed is changed at least three times every seven days).





Nutrition. Club Pasha Beach Hotel offers an all-inclusive concept. A children's menu has been developed (high chairs are provided). This system includes snacks and drinks of different strengths. Ice cream and juices are not included in the total price. By appointment, guests can use the international restaurant. The hotel has another restaurant that does not require recording. There are 3 bars and a buffet with fresh pastries and real Turkish coffee.

The beach . Club Pasha Beach Hotel has its own sandy stretch of beach. It is located 150 meters from the building. To get to the shore, guests must cross the underpass. In free use - air mattresses, sun visors and comfortable sun loungers. Until the evening, a beach bar serves customers. From water entertainment: windsurfing, canoeing, catamarans, parasailing, diving, scooters, water skiing.





Additional Information. On a large landscaped area is an outdoor pool with water slides. Sun loungers are built around the perimeter of the pool and there is an excellent terrace where you can spend time behind a book. We did not forget about the little guests, for them the hotel has a safe, shallow pool with slides, a games area with swings, a club is open.

In the lounge for guests there is access to wireless Internet. In the building there is a sauna, massage room, beauty salon, hammam, bathhouse. Active leisure includes: tennis court, billiards, chess, darts, volleyball, mini-golf, aerobics, futsal, gymnastics.

It offers tourists a shopping center, dry cleaning, laundry, exchange office, fax, amphitheater, TV lounge, rental of transport and sports equipment, a conference room.

Digest. Do you dream to spend a vacation on the Mediterranean coast, to breathe a breath of fresh sea air? Head to Club Pasha Beach Hotel. Guest reviews are mostly enthusiastic. Almost everyone was satisfied with the amazingly delicious food: food is served all day and in large quantities. During the season, they offer certain varieties of fruits, also on the menu are fresh pastry and aromatic teas.

Nice clean rooms have a good rest. The rooms are cleaned properly. The guests liked the water slides, shopping shops with a huge assortment of products, a landscaped area with beautiful green shrubs and palm trees. Friendly staff was also noted.
