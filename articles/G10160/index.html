How to get to the city?

As a rule, vacationers come here by train, which should be called the most budgetary way to get to Primorsko-Akhtarsk. This type of transport involves a route to Anapa, Novorossiysk or Krasnodar.

In addition, there is another way to travel, but it is more expensive. This is a trip by plane to another city (for example, Krasnodar), from where buses travel. You can choose a route based on the price category of the ticket.
