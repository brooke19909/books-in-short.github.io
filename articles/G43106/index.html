The best sandy beaches of Abkhazia

Abkhazia is an amazing place of incredible beauty. The unique and fertile nature, the healing climate, and the very special atmosphere of this region make a vacation here unforgettable. All these qualities of the Soul Country, as locals call it, are perfect for families with children. Including for the first acquaintance with the sea, especially since it is much cleaner here than in the Russian Black Sea resorts located not so far. And since bathing in azure water is the main reason why people go to Abkhazia, it is worthwhile to learn more directly about the beaches, because this is an inseparable attribute of such a vacation. And if adults can lie on a pebble or even on a rocky surface, then little tomboys certainly need to run, and to jump over stones, you see, is not a pleasant occupation. That is why the sandy beaches of Abkhazia are popular among families with children. Basically, the coastline is pebble or sand and pebble, although if you try, you can find clean sand. Consider the main beaches in some resorts of this amazing country.





Sukhum

A city with ancient history, the capital of the Country of Soul. There are few vacationers on the beaches, so you can not be afraid to remain without a place in the sun. Basically, large and small pebbles are found. However, those who are looking for the sandy beach in Abkhazia, too, may well get what they want.

The most popular with locals and visitors of the city is Sinop Beach. It is located in the southwest of Sukhum. The center can be reached by public transport in 5 minutes, on foot - in about half an hour. There is an entrance for vehicles, here you can leave the car. Wide enough (over 50 meters). A gentle entrance to the sea is exactly what children need. On the territory there are cafes, snack bars, shower, toilet, a variety of water activities are offered.





You can also note the beaches belonging to the sanatoriums "Strategic Missile Forces" and "MBO". You cannot completely call them sandy, because closer to the water there is still a strip of pebbles. However, these are the most comfortable holiday destinations with sun loungers, parasols, cafes and beach equipment rental points. But these sandy beaches of Abkhazia are not municipal property. Only guests of sanatoriums can use them freely. However, this does not mean that you cannot get there if you are not resting in a health resort of a country called Abkhazia. Sandy beach - the private sector, or rather its guests, can also visit. Only vacationers will have to pay entrance fees. However, the price is rather purely symbolic.

Pitsunda

This settlement is perfectly suited for families with children. It has amazingly clean air thanks to the relict Pitsunda pine grove and the cleanest sea on the entire coast. Basically, the coastal strip is pebbled. But there is, perhaps, the best sandy beach in Abkhazia. More precisely, it is located not in Pitsunda itself, but 6 km from it, in the resort village of Rybozavod. Very spacious, there is always where to put a rug. And if you get up early and come to the morning sun, then here you can see how dolphins are splashing. There is another big plus at this place: Pitsunda Bay protects the coast, and therefore there are no strong winds and high waves, the sea is mostly calm and quiet.



In addition, while relaxing in the vicinity of Pitsunda, it makes sense to turn your attention to the sandy beaches of Abkhazia in the village of Agarki and the village of Ldzaa (Lizawa). Of course, they do not look like the comfortable beaches of foreign resorts, and they are far from the Russian coast in terms of comfort and infrastructure. However, in silence, tranquility and beauty, they may well compete.

Gagra

Gagra can rightly be called a youth resort. This is where most vacationers seek. And in the old and new parts of the city, the beaches are mostly pebble. However, in Stara Gagra there is a very good beach belonging to the sanatorium “Solnechny”. Strictly speaking, it is also not quite sandy, rather, sandy and pebble. Entrance to it is paid. The good thing is that here, as in Pitsunda, the sea is not very worried. Gagra Range protects against high waves of tourists.





New Athos

A quiet, peaceful resort, there are few vacationers here, so families with young children love this place very much. The sea is clean and warm, gentle entry into the water. But, the truth, the phrase "sandy beaches of Abkhazia" is not about this resort. Here, mostly pebbles. However, if you choose not public beaches, but rather be content with the wild ones, in the vicinity of New Athos it is quite possible to find sand and pebble cover. Of course, you don’t have to dream about infrastructure, but you can fully enjoy the delightful nature. There is also a sandy and pebble beach belonging to the territory of the boarding house “At the monastery”. Despite the fact that this is a private territory, admission is free, and vacationers are few in number, so there is enough space for everyone.

Finally





If you are thinking whether to choose a vacation in Abkhazia, a sandy beach should not be the only criterion. In any case, visiting the Land of Souls is a must. After all, this is an unforgettable place in which it is very easy to fall in love.
