Envelopes for a wedding for money do-it-yourself money in the style of provence: master class, photo

Increasingly, people prefer to give money as gifts. This is especially common among those invited to a wedding. In order for the newlyweds to remember your gift, we suggest you make envelopes for money with your own hands.

Tools and materials

To make envelopes for a wedding for your own money, you will need:





  1. Ready envelope.
  2. Beautiful paper (whole sheets and leftovers). Suitable for decoupage paper, with sparkles, wallpaper and so on.
  3. Cardboard.
  4. Glue.
  5. Scissors.
  6. Ruler.
  7. Pencil.
  8. Decorative elements (bows, sparkles, rhinestones, figurines of the bride and groom, pieces of fabric and so on).

How to make envelope for money?

An envelope for money can be bought or made. If you will buy ready-made, then choose one-color and such that there is nothing on it (for example, decorative elements). Pure white envelopes can be found in the mail or in stores with goods for creativity.

To make envelopes for the wedding for your own money, you need to prepare the basis. It can be of two types:

  1. The envelope that opens on top.
  2. The envelope that opens to the side.

First way:



  1. Take a sheet of cardboard and draw a layout with a pencil and ruler, as shown in the illustration below. Remember that the width of the finished envelope must be at least eight centimeters, and the length must be at least sixteen.
  2. Then cut the layout and carefully glue the parts together.

The second way:





  1. Take a cardboard and cut a rectangle 20-25 centimeters long and 17-18 centimeters wide.
  2. Fold the layout so that the edges of the paper join on one side of the envelope.
  3. Fold the envelope to the sides.
  4. Glue the envelope on all sides except one.

When the base is ready, you can start decorating the envelope for the wedding money with your own hands.

Scrapbooking

Scrapbooking is a special kind of needlework, the purpose of which is to create albums, postcards, envelopes and frames from paper in collage form. In one craft, as a rule, at least five types of paper and various elements are used. The work is voluminous and very interesting.





Master class "How to make an envelope for money" in scrapbooking technique:

  1. Take plain paper and make an envelope out of it.
  2. Cut two rectangles from scrapbooking paper or designer cardboard, two millimeters less than an envelope.
  3. Take another sheet of decorative paper and cut one rectangle, one millimeter smaller than the previous one.
  4. Glue the first two rectangles on both sides of the envelope, then attach the third rectangle on the front side.
  5. Cut paper from lace or buy a finished item in a scrapbooking store. Glue the decor in the center of the envelope.
  6. Cut a strip of ribbon or lace. Its length should be no more than an envelope. Glue the tape with the glue gun on top of the envelope.
  7. Glue several flowers on the ribbon, different in their shape and color.
  8. You can add a few more decorative elements: flowers cut out of paper, figures (keys, hearts, and so on), inscriptions.

The money envelope using scrapbooking technique is ready!

Envelope in the style of Provence

Do-it-yourself envelope for wedding money in a Provence style is a very tender gift.

The main features of this style:

  • A specific color palette: white, cream, milk, beige, as well as light pastel shades of lavender, turquoise, blue and green colors.
  • Floral motifs in patterns.
  • A sprig of lavender.
  • Lace.
  • Ornate lettering.

Possible options for making DIY wedding envelopes for money in the style of Provence:





  1. Take the lace and stick it on the front of the envelope.
  2. Tie the envelope with twine, and in place of the bow, attach the dried sprig of lavender.
  3. Tape the envelope with floral paper.
  4. If you want to use a minimum of decor, then immediately make an envelope from the design cardboard. It is better to choose with a geometric pattern. Then only a beautiful inscription from above will be enough.
  5. Take two sheets of designer cardboard with the same pattern, but different in tone. Make an envelope from one, and cut a thin strip from the other. Glue it to the front of the envelope. Decorate it with a beautiful inscription.
  6. Make an envelope from designer cardboard. Decorate the valve with artificial flowers and beads that stick together easily with a glue gun.

Envelope with butterfly

Such a very delicate envelope for money for a wedding with your own hands (photo below) with a minimum of decor, but very beautiful and original, can be made using a paper butterfly.





To do this, make an envelope from a beautiful designer cardboard. Next, take a sheet of cardboard or thick paper. The length should be enough to wrap around the envelope.

In the center of the paper, draw a large butterfly - from five to eight centimeters. Inside the moth, draw a few more contours. Draw lines on both sides of the butterfly. Cut the stripes and the figure along the contour.

With a clerical knife, alternately cut the inner contours of the butterfly. As a result, you should get a voluminous moth, as in the picture above.

Connect the strips together with glue and put the jewelry on the envelope. Beads can be glued to the center of the butterfly.

Postal envelope

Do-it-yourself decorated envelopes for wedding money will be original.

Workshop on their manufacture:





  1. Cut out a cardboard layout of one side of the envelope, as in the figure above.
  2. Take beautiful paper (with sequins, for decoupage and so on).
  3. Attach the layout to the selected paper and circle it.
  4. Cut along the contour.
  5. Take the mailing envelope and insert the cut out part into it.
  6. Glue the inside of the envelope. It is more convenient to do with glue-pencil.
  7. Decorate the envelope from the outside. For example, as in the previous master class.




The mail envelope is ready for money!

Other possible envelope design ideas

How else can you decorate envelopes for a wedding for money with your own hands?





  1. Make an envelope of light-colored paper . Take ink, gel pens or paints and draw patterns with your own hands.
  2. Take a figured hole punch with the shape of butterflies, flowers, angels and so on. Knock out the figures from decorative paper and glue them in a chaotic manner on the envelope.
  3. Give the envelope flap its original shape. To do this, take special wavy scissors or a border. Using these tools, you can make lace on paper.
  4. Instead of paper, use photos of newlyweds and their relatives printed on plain paper.
