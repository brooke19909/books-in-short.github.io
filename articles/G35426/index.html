Supra M72KG

The first hero of this short review is the Supra M72KG tablet - a model that is a typical representative of the budget price segment, since the average price of the tablet is 3000 - 4300 rubles. We proceed to consider the main technical characteristics of the device.

  • Supra M72KG runs on the basis of the most popular operating system Google Android, the installed firmware version is 4.4 Kit Kat.

  • A dual-core processor, coupled with 512 megabytes of RAM, will provide the opportunity to work with the system comfortably, without freezes and jerks.
  • GPS support will also be a pleasant surprise - this means that you can insert a SIM card into the device and use it as an ordinary mobile phone, as well as as a navigator.

Supra 14AG: Gadget Feature

Supra 14AG is slightly different from the previous model, taking a stable position as a mid-range device. The cost of the tablet ranges from seven to nine thousand Russian rubles. What determines the cost, we will consider in the technical specifications below.
