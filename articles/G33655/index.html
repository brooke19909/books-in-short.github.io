Knitted clothes are warm, comfortable, beautiful. In the wardrobe of each person there are things connected with knitting needles or crochet, on a typewriter or manually. Unchanging in a changing fashion

Knitting is one of the most common and ancient types of needlework. Over the millennia of its existence as a way to create the necessary garments, knitting in itself has not changed too much. The materials have changed, and the loops, front and back, remained the same as hundreds of centuries ago. The functionality of knitted things has also changed - from those saving from the cold, they have also become a decorative element of style. The basic principle

Needleworkers who can knit or crochet can allow in their wardrobe to have more than one hat that they like. You can find knitted hats with a description and patterns on the pages of women's magazines.

Perhaps, the main thing in a hat for every day is convenience, then beauty and correspondence to fashionable trends. So, in order to make it convenient in the headdress, it is necessary to observe the appropriate size. The loops should be calculated by tying the sample from the same threads, with the same knitting needles and the same pattern with which the cap will be created. Yes, knitwear has a universal texture that can stretch, depending on the method of knitting. But still, a comfortable and beautiful hat should be of a suitable size.
