Wolf: cross stitch pattern. Description and practical recommendations

If you look at the rich collections of experienced needlewomen, it becomes clear that such a character as a gray wolf is very popular. A cross stitch pattern depicting a lone forest resident can be found in the binder of any magazine devoted to needlework. What attracts this wayward beast?

At the peak of popularity





With the image of a wolf, the majority associates gloomy ideas: night, moon, snow, loneliness, but most importantly - eternal freedom. In the look of the gray handsome man, who so often becomes the evil hero of children's fairy tales, it is as if through neglect of human ideals, contempt for the crowd, the true individuality of a natural loner. That is why the wolf becomes such a frequent visitor to embroidered paintings . The cross stitch pattern, developed by a famous designer and equipped with a full set of threads, canvas and embroidery needles, will be a wonderful gift for a passionate needlewoman.

Practical tips

To make the finished work look neat and beautiful, adhere to the following recommendations:

  • Experienced craftswomen recommend embroidering from the center of the fabric up and to the sides, and only then - down.
  • If the diagram depicts a beast in the winter forest or simply against the background of snowdrifts, you should first work on the animal, then on areas of dark shades. Lastly, snow and other fragments of white color are embroidered.
  • Often, when there is a wolf in the picture, the cross-stitch pattern is provided with additional decorative elements: for example, a ‚Äúback to the needle‚Äù stitch or a monastic stitch made using beads. As a rule, in this way, circuit designers recommend highlighting amazing wolf eyes. If there are beads in your set or if the back needle stitch is indicated in the pattern , do the decor after finishing the main work. Even if the gray wolf itself is already ready, the cross-stitch pattern involves decorating the image only at the end of the last cross.

Thread or beads?

Perhaps you would like to present a beautiful handmade picture to someone as a gift? Cross stitch pattern "Wolf" will be a great option: do it yourself or, if a relative or girlfriend also loves needlework, make a complete set for embroidery. In ready-made sets from well-known manufacturers, you can find whole stories exclusively from beads. If you used to embroider exclusively with a cross, feel free to master a new technique: many craftswomen believe that it is much easier to embroider with beads than with mouline thread.
