LCD "Imperial Mytishchi": reviews, description

Not far from the village of Pogorelki, seven kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road, the Imperial Mytishchi Residential Complex is being built. The reviews speak of low monolithic-brick buildings of varying number of storeys - from four to six floors, where you can judge by the finished buildings the exterior decoration, which is very impressive. Reviews mention the modern style of the complex "Imperial Mytishchi" repeatedly: the lining is high-quality, the latest technologies that preserve thermal insulation, promising durability.

Apartments are offered from the developer, which will be described in detail below. There are three, two, one-room, and the layout options are varied, as the reviews say.
