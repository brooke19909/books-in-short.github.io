Hotel Bodrum Beach Resort 4 * Turkey, Bodrum: photos and reviews of tourists

The part of Turkey bordering Greece is washed by the waters of the Aegean Sea. It is there that the famous Bodrum resort is located. It occupies fourteen bays, in which hotels and small settlements are located. The city of Bodrum itself rises in the heart of the resort.

It will take only an hour to explore the entire peninsula from the inside. If you move along the coast, then the road can take 2.5 hours.

Bodrum - the "party" paradise





Bodrum is considered an ideal place for parties. It is not without reason that they call it the capital of nightly adventures. At a short distance from each other there are numerous bars, restaurants, night clubs and discos.

All young people seek, if not to live in Bodrum, then at least relax here. Those who prefer a relaxing holiday on the beach, it is better to choose a quieter place. Active tourists from France, England and Scandinavia mainly come here. In these places it is very windy, so the resort is ideal for surfers and divers.

In the city itself, decent housing is not easy to find. But only 10 minutes away in picturesque bays, luxurious hotels rise. In season, minibuses run to each of them from the city center. Only the most remote ones will have to get a taxi late at night.

Weather

As already mentioned, it is quite windy, but not hot at all. Temperature even in the midst of summer rarely exceeds 30 degrees.

In Bodrum, you can rarely find abandoned beaches. Almost all of them belong to hotels. It can be pebble embankments or a concrete area with an artificial embankment of sand.

Of the 14 bays located in the vicinity of Bodrum, the most famous are Gumbet and Bitez.

Hotel complexes

If you look at the resort from above, it seems that Bodrum occupies a niche around which hotels are located at different levels. More expensive complexes are equipped with special elevators that allow you to go down to the beaches. There are both budget hotels and luxury hotels. Most of them provide for relaxation with children.

One of the resort's most popular hotels is Bodrum Beach Resort. Its description and reviews of tourists will be presented below.

Service is available around the clock. Bed linen is changed three times a week. Clean the rooms every day. In the bathroom there is a shower, a large mirror, towels, a bathroom, a washbasin, a hairdryer and toiletries.

Tourist note

The Bodrum Beach Resort 4 * hotel, undoubtedly, deserves attention, but there are some points that should be considered when planning a vacation in Turkey.

If a trip to the resort is carried out with the help of a tour operator, then you need to prepare for the fact that you will be accommodated in the "Russian" building. It has the cheapest rooms. There may be mold on the walls, and cobwebs, and an unpleasant smell. And the view from the window leaves much to be desired. But this is all the flaws of a budget holiday. Either you have to pay, or endure and enjoy at least such conditions. This is not the fault of the Bodrum Beach Resort. The testimonies of the victims suggest that most often this is simply negligent work of tour operators. They seduce tourists with good prices, but forget to tell about the conditions. You can fix the error on the spot, but you will have to pay a little extra. The hotel staff will gladly relocate you to the best room with a chic view. There are enough of them.
