Sirenis Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Side, Kumkoy): description, service, reviews

Kumkoy is a Mediterranean resort in Turkey. It is popular not only among families traveling with children, but also among young couples. It is surrounded by long sandy beaches. Along them an endless string of high-rise hotel complexes.

Away by the sea





Sirenis Hotel is a typical premium hotel. It is located near the coastline. Around it are concentrated nightclubs and restaurants. Life in these parts flows measured and leisurely. Guests wake up for dinner and go to bed after midnight.

The beaches of the region are clean and even shallows. Thanks to the active movement of air masses, Kumkoy attracts adherents of water sports. The swimming season at Sirenis Hotel opens in May. This month the water warms up to +23 ° C. In June, it reaches 26 ° C, in July - 27 ° C of heat.

It's already cooler in August. Feels the approach of autumn. Therefore, the temperature of the water area is +26 ° C, in September - +24 ° C, and in October - +22 ° C. The last vacationers leave the hotel in November.

Key points





The hotel was built in 2004. After ten years of operation, it was completely repaired and modernized. It covers an area exceeding 29,000 square meters. The staff at Sirenis Hotel does not speak Russian. The staff speaks Turkish, English and German. The hotel is represented by one residential building. Its height is six floors.

Vacationers appreciated the large territory of the complex. It abounds in flower beds and flower beds, alleys and park areas. Her gardeners carefully look after her. The leafy crown of tropical trees creates an extensive shadow. The guests with pleasure spend the sultry hours on the winding paths of the square.

Clients are served by a 24-hour accommodation service. New guests are registered at lunch time. Early check-in possible. Sirenis Hotel is aimed at the elderly, families with young children, newlyweds and people with disabilities. The national composition of holidaymakers is represented by residents of Turkey, citizens of Germany, Russia, Belarus, Kazakhstan. Type of holiday offered - beach.

Hotel location

The hotel is located three kilometers from the bustling resort of Side. The nearest airport is Antalya. Travel time from the passenger terminal to Kumkoy is one hour. The residential building is five hundred meters away from the Mediterranean Sea. During the day, the Sirenis Hotel bus runs to the beach and back. You do not need to purchase a ticket.

The center of the municipality is within easy reach, just five minutes by taxi. Three kilometers from the hotel rise the famous ruins of the ancient city. Their visit is a must for any excursion program. The nearest large village is Manavgat. Regular buses go to it.

The city attracts tourists with magnificent natural parks, priceless monuments of ancient architecture, a variety of shops, cafes and restaurants. Twice a week, an oriental bazaar operates in it. Its counters are choked with fresh fruits and vegetables, herbs, cheeses, delicacies and meat. They also sell household utensils, decor items, clothes and souvenirs.

Accommodation





On six floors of Sirenis Hotel 5 * there are 416 comfortable rooms. Most travelers live in standard rooms. The number of standard apartments is 391. The area is 18 square meters. Maximum occupancy is 3 adults. There are 12 rooms with bunk beds. They are designed for 4 guests and occupy 28 m².

Family suites in Sirenis Hotel 5 * consist of two bedrooms, which are separated by a door. They accommodate three adults and one child. The number of Family Room is 53, the area is 25 m². In high season, vacationers are offered promotional rooms. Their difference is low cost.

Each living room has beds, a table, chairs, a mirror, a TV. The bathroom contains cosmetic and bath accessories, shower, washbasin, toilet, hairdryer. The apartment has a balcony. The use of a minibar, safe and telephone at Sirenis Hotel (Turkey) is made for a fee.





The central air conditioning system is responsible for the indoor microclimate. A laminate board is laid on the floor. In the package of TV channels there is one Russian-speaking. On the day of arrival, mineral water in the refrigerator is provided free of charge.

The rooms are cleaned every day. They vacuum clean, dust, add hygiene items. Bed linen, judging by the reviews of the Sirenis Hotel, is changed three times a week.

Gastronomy

The hotel catering is organized according to the All Inclusive system. Meals are organized according to the rules of the buffet. Schedule of the main dining room:

  • 07:30 - 10:00 (breakfast);
  • 10:00 - 10:30 (lunch);
  • 12:30 - 14:00 (lunch);
  • 19:00 - 21:00 (dinner);
  • 23:30 - 00:00 (nightly meal).

The price includes local drinks. You must pay extra for imported alcohol, soft drinks and bottled soda. In the daytime, guests are served by the bars of the Sirenis Hotel (Kumkoy). The bistro in the lobby is open from 10:00 to 24:00, the snack bar by the pool - from 10:00 to 24:00, an outdoor cafe on the beach - from 10:00 to 18:00.

Visits to the Ottoman Restaurant and Italian cuisine are by appointment. Service is not included in the tour price. One free visit is allowed. Institutions open at 19:00. Recent visitors leave after 21:00. The calculation is made in cash or plastic cards Visa, MasterCard.

Hotel Territory

Description Sirenis Hotel says that the hotel has everything you need for a fun and eventful stay. The amphitheater stage hosts family meetings and music evenings daily. Performers are invited artists. Gymnasts, acrobats, dancers show their talents.

The hotel’s medical center hosts therapists, pediatricians, masseurs and cosmetologists. There is a currency exchange office next to the reception desk. His course is not the most profitable. Service Sirenis Hotel is represented by dry cleaning services, laundry, hairdresser, nail studio. There are grocery stores, souvenir shops, kiosks selling swimming equipment and spa supplies.

Car parking is located opposite the main building. Entrance to it is free. No reservation is required. At the car rental you can borrow a car for a while. The tour desk offers a wide selection of sightseeing and study tours. The agency's arsenal includes short routes and multi-day trips.

Sports and recreation





The entertainment team at Sirenis Hotel is responsible for the animation team. She holds fun competitions and relay races. Organizes leisure for adults and children, collects tourists for morning exercises, invites you to participate in aerobics training, and in oriental dance lessons. Entrance to the gym is free.

For the use of equipment, diving equipment, tables for playing billiards will have to fork out. A visit to the Turkish steam bath, bath and sauna is included in the standard package of services. The hotel has swimming pools. Water slides are on schedule. At noon you can play darts, table tennis, and bocce.

Kids are invited to the kids club. It accepts young travelers who are already four years old. The upper age limit is 12 years. Children under the supervision of educators learn to sing and dance. They sculpt and paint. A platform is open for them, on which swings, carousels, horizontal bars, ladders for climbing are installed.

On the coast, guests ride on bananas, tablets, catamarans and canoes. Stretched net for playing beach volleyball. Tournaments and team competitions are held on the site. Everyone can take part.

Positive reviews about the rest





The Russians were satisfied with the service at the hotel. They note the friendly staff, the delicate and attentive attitude of the reception and reception staff. Food, according to most, was at its best. The menu included meat and fish dishes. The food was not too salted and too spicy. Spice was added in moderation. For young children, parents took broths, boiled pasta, mashed potatoes, rice and milk porridge.

To bring to readiness or warm milk or a mixture, vacationers used a microwave installed in the restaurant hall. In June, there were no lines in the dining room. Sun loungers, umbrellas and towels were enough for everyone. The children were delighted with the cocktails that were prepared in the bars.

Rooms were cleaned every day. They didn’t ask for any surcharge, they didn’t extort a tip. Information desk employees took an active part in solving problems. They called medical staff, contacted representatives of insurance companies.

Negative impressions of vacationers

In high season, hotel staff made a lot of mistakes. Our compatriots were accommodated in rooms that did not correspond to the categories stated in the tour package. The issue of changing the room was not always decided in favor of the guests. Travelers had to be content with apartments for the disabled.

The bartenders spoke rudely to the children. Sometimes they refused to pour them lemonade, soda or tea. In August, the dining room was full. People had to independently search for empty tables and chairs. Technical employees did not help or organize cleaning.

Hotel surroundings

The abundance of gastronomic facilities in the neighboring town is a short drive away. Karma, Dedem, Side, Ozde restaurants invite you to taste gourmet cuisine. You can eat inexpensive and tasty in the eateries Meshur, Panorama, Side Viking, Kucukoglu.

In the municipality, Turkish cuisine is most widely represented. Mediterranean dishes are also available. The assortment includes pizza, steaks, burgers, confectionery. Cooking work. Lunch is especially noisy and crowded at Q Beach. The institution treats seafood. It is perfect for romantic meetings and family feasts.

The Serenade offers Mexican dishes generously seasoned with spices and hot pepper. The Sultan has developed a vegetarian menu. You can try traditional Norwegian treats in Norden. For fish it is recommended to come to the "Estuary". But the most delicious ice cream - in the "Bardo".

Excursions





For lovers of informative and eventful relaxation, the hotel offers a variety of travels. At their service are not only convenient tourist buses, but also yachts, jeeps and boats. A visit to Cappadocia is a must for any review program. This region surprises with the pristine beauty of nature.

The cost of visiting the Mamure fortress and walking along the streets of the ancient city for a group of four adult tourists is 14,500 rubles. ATV safari will cost 1160 rubles. per person.
