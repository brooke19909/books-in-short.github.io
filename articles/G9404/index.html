Hotel Club Sidera

Description

The five-star Club Sidera hotel, part of the Kirman Hotels hotel chain, is built on one of the magnificent Turkish coasts in the town of Karaburun.

The resorts of Alanya and Manavgat are 37 and 23 kilometers respectively. The hotel is marked by a quality standard. There is a bus stop nearby, and there is always a taxi at the front door. The territory on which Club Sidera is located occupies almost 15,000 square meters. The last renovation of the hotel building was carried out in 2006. The hotel infrastructure includes two small conference rooms, a medical office, a beauty salon, a reading room, a shopping center, a currency exchange, a game salon, and dry cleaning. A 24-hour front desk can arrange transfers. There are also rental points for sports equipment and car rental. The airport is a 40-minute drive away.

Beach



Club Sidera is a hotel that is built on the first line of the sea. Everything you need (umbrellas, sunbeds and deck chairs) on the beach is free. There are also changing cabins and showers. The entrance to the sea is convenient, there is no need for special shoes. The hotel's beach is marked with the Blue Flag.

Additional Information

Pets are not welcome at Club Sidera. For children there is a children's section in the pool, a playground, babysitting services on request, as well as chairs in the restaurant and an in-room crib. Adults can occupy themselves with all water sports, including parachuting, banana riding, water skiing. Here on the beach you can play volleyball. The hotel has built two outdoor pools with slides. There you can play boccia, futsal, tennis. Guests can visit the massage room, Turkish bath or fitness club. On the seashore you can rent a catamaran or a yacht. The hotel has a very fun animation, including for children.
