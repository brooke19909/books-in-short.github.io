Black Sea Apartment (Sunny Beach) - the perfect place to relax abroad

Want to visit Sunny Beach? Sunny Beach Black Sea Apartment is an ideal place to relax in Bulgaria. You do not have to spend time on the road to the beach if you choose Black Sea Apartment as your accommodation. Sunny Beach is in close proximity to the hotel, and you will have to overcome the path to the beach within 100 meters, which will take no more than 5 minutes. Black Sea Apartment (Sunny Beach) is located in the first line from the sea and is a mini-hotel. In addition to comfortable accommodation, each of the guests can absolutely enjoy panoramic sea views from a spacious balcony. You can cool off in the outdoor pool at the Black Sea Apartment (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach).





Black Sea Apartment: about the hotel

The hotel consists of several complexes. The floors of the main Black Sea Resort building are 4 floors. Here are accommodated rooms for guests. Accommodation is possible in rooms with a capacity of 1-3 people, where 2-6 beds. The hotel has 22 fully equipped and fully equipped rooms.





In addition to accommodating guests in comfortable rooms, tourists are accommodated according to the BB or FB meal plan (choice of vacationer, indicated when booking). Dining at the hotel is organized on the basis of its own national restaurant. The menu features colorful, rich dishes of Bulgarian cuisine and a varied European menu, an extensive wine list.

Between sleep and relaxing on the beach (Sunny Beach), you can enjoy relaxation in the spa at the hotel or tighten your shape in the gym. It offers aerobics and fitness.

Hotel check-in rules

Check-in at the hotel is from 14:00 to 22:00. Late check-in is possible, but for such a service you will have to pay an additional fee. Check-out is until 12:00 p.m.

Detailed information on booking and prepayment of the selected room depends on the category of apartments. You can monitor the available dates for booking and familiarize yourself with the conditions for the provision of services in more detail on the official website of the hotel or using the online service for selecting a place to stay during your vacation.





No pets are allowed in the hotel.

For families with children, extra bed is available upon request. There is an extra charge for the service and the maximum number of extra beds is 2. There are special cots in the reserve of the hotel complex.

Hotel internal infrastructure

Living in the Black Sea Apartment (Sunny Beach), which are part of the tourist complex, you can count on comfortable accommodation in a standard type room with a mounted air conditioning system.

On the territory of the complex by the pool, there is a bar where you can calmly sit in the evening, and nearby is an Italian restaurant. Vacationers can use the laundry facilities at an additional cost.





Upon request, it is possible to arrange a transfer from the hotel to the airport of Burgas.

Guests traveling by car can leave their vehicles in a secure parking lot at the hotel.
