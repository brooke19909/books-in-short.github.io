Where is the hotel located

The village of Kadriye has long been a suburb of Belek. However, she retains her personality. Everything here is imbued with the spirit of the same "luxury". Emerald lawns stretch golf courses. On the vast, more than a million square meters, territory of one of them, "Kariya Golf Club", and our hotel is located. Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort is only thirty-five kilometers from Antalya International Airport . You can get there (if your package does not include a transfer) by minibus-dolmusha. The neighborhood at Regnum Karia Golf & Spa Resort is appropriate. Such brilliant hotels as Riu Kaya Belek and Sentido Zeynep are located in Kadriye. Tourists who know a lot about quality rest stay in them. If you are bored with swinging a club at your own golf club, you can change the atmosphere. Nearby are the lawns with holes "Kaya Igles" and "Antalya."



Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort: prices

Rest class "luxury" and requires significant investment. The price range for the most modest number "standard" ranges from fifteen to forty-six thousand rubles. But there are nuances. Very often, the Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort 5 * hotel announces promotions and very attractive offers. For example, with early booking you can get a double room for twelve thousand rubles per night. But that is not all. Last minute vouchers give you the opportunity to relax in a luxury hotel for literally 30 percent of the cost of living. It should be noted that the hotel opened only in April 2014, so frequent promotions are designed to make it known among potential customers. Many tourists have already taken advantage of advantageous offers. Now in Turkey is not the season. But is it really so important if you offer accommodation in a palace with heated pools? A week for two at the end of February in the Luxer Room will cost one thousand nine hundred dollars. This price includes a flight to Antalya and back, a transfer to Belek and insurance.





Where do guests live?

So that the reader can make an opinion about the luxury of the rooms in the Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort 5 *, we will describe the "modest" standard room (most of the tourists who left reviews stayed there). It is called the Luxurious Room and can be of two types: overlooking the golf course or the sea. First, the area. Two people are invited to live in fifty square meters. And to this room there is also a furnished balcony of 12 m 2 . Expensive bedding, a split system, a flat-screen TV are not all signs of luxury.





Toiletries are put from the company "Bulgari". And bathrobes are provided not only for adults, but also for children. There are family rooms with access to a huge roof terrace. The rooms of the suites and villa categories shine with special chic. They are not located in a high-rise building, but in one-story cottages. They have their own heated pool, jacuzzi, garden. The guests of these residences are provided with a lot of privileges (butler and butler services, a la carte restaurant, and for those living in the crown villa there is also a helicopter transfer from Antalya). And without exception, upon arrival, they give a bottle of champagne and a basket of fruit.





Nutrition

Most vacationers used the all-inclusive system at the Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort. Main meals were served at the Gourmet restaurant. This is a stunning luxury room. And it adjoins a huge outdoor terrace. Gourmet has not just a children's menu, but a whole restaurant for young travelers. And for the very little ones there is a Baby Corner. This is a room where you can cook for babies. In addition to the main buffet restaurant, there are five more a la carte restaurants: Turkish (Ottoman), Italian (Tetramonto), fish (Sea Horse), Asian (Shu Fang) ) and international ("Kariya") cuisines. From the reviews of tourists, we can conclude that the food in this hotel is as luxurious as everything else. The products are very high quality, and the dishes were created by true craftsmen.



Beach, pools, water park

Entertainment at the Regnum Carya Golf & Spa Resort can be listed for a very long time. Naturally, there is animation (both adult and children's). But you will not surprise spoiled customers with it. But the laser show, the singing fountains in the pools, the fireworks lasting ten minutes on the beach - are there any similar entertainments in other hotels? On the private sandy beach of the hotel it is proposed to sunbathe in the pavilions, under umbrellas, under awnings.

The pools amaze with magnificence and variety. There is also an artificial wave where you can even ride a board. And in the water park, a river flows from the hills, along which you can go kayaking. For children there is a Chelsea football team. And adults can take golf lessons for a fee. The spa now also will not surprise anyone. But its content is important. The hotel has a cryo sauna and other amazing facial and body treatments.
