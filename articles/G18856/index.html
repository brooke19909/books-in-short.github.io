Hotel Turkler Artemis 4 * - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

One of the most visited regions of the Republic of Turkey by our compatriots for several decades is Alania. Tourists come here not only because of the great climate, warm sea and the mass of attractions, but also thanks to the developed hotel base and the presence of entertainment for every taste. If you decide to spend your summer vacation in Alanya, then as a accommodation, consider the hotel complex Turkler Artemis 4 *. Today we have prepared for you detailed information about this four-star hotel, and also found out whether tourists from Russia and other countries of the former USSR liked the stay here.

The location of the hotel complex

Turkler Artemis Hotel 4 * hotel is located in one of the most developed tourist regions of Turkey - Alanya - in a small resort village called Turkler. The nearest air harbor is located in Antalya and is 110 kilometers away from the hotel complex. After landing at the airport, your journey to the hotel will take a couple of hours. The distance to the resort center - the city of Alanya - is about twenty kilometers. Turkler Artemis Hotel 4 * is located on the second line and has its own sand and pebble beach, which will have to go about 350 meters.

Hotel Description

This hotel complex took its first guests in 2006. The last large-scale reconstruction was carried out here in 2011. Previously, the hotel was called Verola Beach Hotel, and since 2012 it has been renamed Turkler Artemis 4 *. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãits own territory is nine thousand square meters. The hotel complex has 95 comfortable standard and family rooms. It has everything you need for an excellent holiday for both young people and family tourists with children and older people.





Hotel Policies

In accordance with the rules adopted by Alanya Turkler Artemis Hotel 4 *, check-in for arriving guests is carried out, as in most other hotels, after 14:00. If you arrive at the hotel complex earlier than the indicated time, then if you have available rooms of the category you have reserved, you will be settled immediately. But at the peak of the tourist season, the hotel is often full, as they say, "to the eyeballs." In this situation, in case of early arrival, you will most likely be asked to wait until the previous guests have vacated the room and prepare the hotel maids for check-in. At the same time, you can leave your things in a special room and take a walk around the hotel, have lunch, swim in the pool or go straight to the sea.

As for the discharge from the hotel, it is necessary to vacate the room and hand over the keys before noon. At the same time, you should pay for your stay, as well as for using any additional services, if you have resorted to them. You can pay either in cash or by credit cards of the Mastercard and Visa systems. If you have a card of another payment system, then the possibility of using it in Turkler Artemis 4 * should be checked with the hotel in advance.





Accommodation with children and pets

The four-star Turkler Artemis Hotel welcomes guests with children of all ages. If in your room you place a baby under 2 years old on the provided crib, or a child from 2 to 12 years old on existing beds, you will not have to pay extra. For placement on an extra bed an older child or adult will have to pay 25 euros for each day of stay. Please note that in each room there is the possibility of placing only one extra or baby cot. This service is available upon prior request.

As for tourists who are used to traveling in the company of their kitties or dogs, they will be disappointed. Indeed, in accordance with the rules of Turkler Artemis 4 * (Alanya), no pets are allowed on its territory.

What are hotel rooms

As already mentioned, the Turkler Artemis hotel complex has 95 comfortable rooms located in a multi-storey building equipped with an elevator for the convenience of guests. The number of rooms of Turkler Artemis 4 * consists of 12 corner, 77 standard and 6 family rooms. Each of them is equipped with individual air conditioning, satellite TV, safe, minibar with fridge, telephone, bathroom with hairdryer, Wi-Fi and a balcony. All rooms have carpeted floors. Rooms are cleaned every day. Also, as necessary, maids replenish supplies of bath and toilet accessories. Sheets and towels are changed three times a week. Hotel guests have the opportunity to order room service.





Restaurants, bars

Dining at the Turkler Artemis 4 * hotel complex (Turkey) is organized according to the all-inclusive system, popular among our compatriots. So, the main restaurant of the hotel three times a day offers you dishes, snacks and desserts of national and international cuisines in a buffet format. It should be noted that the food in Turkler Artemis 4 * (reviews of tourists only confirm this) is quite diverse, tasty and of high quality.

During the day, in the four bars of the hotel (lobby bar, pool bar, beach bar and snack bar), you can enjoy your favorite alcoholic and soft drinks, tea and coffee, as well as a snack between breakfast, lunch and dinner.





Beach and sea

The four-star hotel complex "Turkler Artemis" is located on the second coastline. The hotel's private beach is 350 meters away. On the way to it you need to cross the road. This will not cause difficulties, since there is an underground passage. On the beach, guests of the Turkler Artemis Hotel 4 * can use sunbeds, mattresses and parasols for free. There is also a bar where you can enjoy your favorite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks and various snacks. In addition, the beach has everything for water sports.

Entertainment

Turkler Artemis Hotel 4 * has its own outdoor pool. Near it there is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas. There is also a bar where you can order a variety of soft and alcoholic drinks. For the convenience of the smallest travelers, the hotel also has a children's pool. In addition, there is a playground for the smallest, as well as a mini club.

In addition to swimming in the pool, tourists have the opportunity to play table tennis, billiards or darts. There are also animators at the hotel throughout the day and evening.





What infrastructure does the hotel complex have?

At the four-star Turkler Artemis Hotel (Alanya) you will find everything you need for an excellent vacation and pastime. So, the reception is open 24 hours a day. And if you are forced to check into the hotel at off-hours, or if you have any problems that require immediate resolution, you can always find a receptionist at the reception who will help you. The hotel also has a luggage storage room where you can leave your suitcases and bags if necessary.

Guests of Alanya Turkler Artemis Hotel 4 * have the opportunity to use the services of dry cleaning, laundry, babysitting. The hotel also has a hairdresser, beauty salon, car rental, games room and shop where you can buy souvenirs and everything you need for a beach holiday. If you feel unwell, you can call a doctor. To do this, you should contact the reception.

In addition, the hotel complex has its own parking. Using it for guests is free. So, if you want to go on vacation in your car or rent a car in Turkey, then you will not have any problems with parking.

Cost of living in the four-star hotel complex "Turkler Artemis" (Alanya)

As for the prices of tours to the Turkler Artemis Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey), they can be called average. So, a seven-day stay in this hotel complex in September will cost you in the amount of twelve thousand rubles. At the height of the tourist season (July-August), this cost will be an order of magnitude higher.





Turkler Artemis Hotel 4 *: reviews of tourists from Russia

Since it is very important for many tourists at the stage of choosing a place to stay on vacation to find out the opinions of those people who have already been here, we summarized the comments of our compatriots staying at the four-star Turkler Artemis hotel in Alanya. In general, most of the reviews were positive. But in fairness it should be noted that some tourists were very unhappy with their choice of hotel. We offer to understand the situation in more detail.

First of all, many tourists care about the condition of the hotel rooms themselves. So, our compatriots found the rooms at Turkler Artemis spacious, fairly clean and well furnished. However, some travelers complained about the old plumbing. As for the cleaning of the rooms, it was carried out efficiently and regularly. There were also no problems with the timely change of bed linen and towels.

In most cases, the issue of nutrition becomes controversial. The Turkler Artemis Hotel was no exception. Most of the guests of this hotel complex found the food quite acceptable, the choice of dishes in the restaurant was quite diverse, and the food quality was good. Some tourists note many fruits and vegetables. The other part of travelers, by contrast, is convinced that a four-star hotel should offer its guests a more varied menu with a large number of meat and fish dishes. However, in general, no one here remained hungry.

Many complaints from the guests of the Turkler Artemis Hotel in Alanya caused a hotel beach. So, many tourists were unhappy that the sunbeds and umbrellas here are quite old and mostly broken. Also, many left an unpleasant impression of entering the water. After all, the bottom here is quite rocky, and some vacationers even got hurt.

As for the service and work of the hotel staff, there are practically no complaints at this point. So, our compatriots really liked that almost all the employees at the reception speak excellent Russian. The waiters in the restaurant are fast and always friendly. The same goes for maids and other employees.

But on the issue of animation, the opinions of tourists again diverged. So, someone called the animators the true masters of their craft, who made the rest of the hotel guests more diverse and interesting. Someone found the entertainment program boring and monotonous. It is possible that the hotel just changed the team of animators. So some of the tourists were more fortunate with the entertainment program.

In general, most of the travelers were completely satisfied with their stay at Turkler Artemis (Alanya). They do not exclude the possibility of coming to this hotel again and are ready to recommend it to all friends.
