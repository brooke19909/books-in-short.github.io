Sanatorium "Ocean": photos and reviews

Russia is famous for its abundance of sanatoriums. The natural features of the regions are such that they can provide significant relief to the condition of patients in the shortest possible time. Add to this modern medical support and an abundance of wellness procedures - and get an excellent alternative to visiting a clinic or hospital. Today we consider the sanatorium "Ocean" of the Primorsky Territory. It has been known since Soviet times, but today it remains popular and competitive.



Holidays by the sea

If you dream of blue open spaces and shady parks, magnificent fountains and sunny beaches, then you will definitely like it here. The sanatorium "Ocean" is a wonderful health resort of the Primorsky Territory. Who visited once, he will forever love these places. The reason is that at the same time you enjoy your vacation in a paradise on the seashore and undergo an excellent course of treatment. A huge selection of procedures and recreational activities gives everyone the opportunity to spend an unforgettable and healthy vacation.

Region features

Sanatorium "Ocean" comfortably hid in the forest, where nothing will distract you from a relaxing holiday. This is a real paradise, where the number of sunny days exceeds 250 per year, and the percentage of windless reaches 40. In addition to the general charm of the region, the health resort has its own infrastructure. On the territory of the sanatorium you will be provided with everything you need, there is also an excellent beach.

Sanatorium "Ocean" is buried in a picturesque forest. Entering the gate, you find yourself in an amazing world where pristine nature is combined with modern infrastructure, coziness and comfort. In the center is a picturesque spring. Many vacationers consider it mandatory to scoop up cold water from it in the morning, which seems to revive. And children will definitely like the unique, colorful playground for games.



Residence

Sanatorium "Ocean" provides its customers the opportunity to conveniently settle in a comfortable building. It was built in the 80s of the last century, but after overhaul it is again ready to receive guests. Tourists note that the rooms are quite simple, but very comfortable. Beds and nightstands are located here, at the request of guests, you can put a cot.

You can choose from cozy comfortable rooms for one or two tourists, as well as suites. Guests celebrate cleanliness and comfort, attractive interior, calm colors, beautiful curtains and modern furniture. There is everything you need for high spirits and a sense of comfort.



Guest meals

This is an important issue for those who are planning to spend their holidays here. Sanatorium "Ocean" (Vladivostok) offers its guests to use the services of the dining room. Four meals a day are waiting for you. A cozy room and homely atmosphere will make your stay even more enjoyable.

Military sanatorium "Ocean" takes into account the importance of therapeutic diets, as well as individual characteristics and tastes of each person, therefore, the dining room is open upon reservation. From the extensive list you can choose your favorite dishes.

Clinical nutrition is a prerequisite without which it is impossible to get rid of chronic diseases. Therefore, the therapist will first diagnose and then prescribe one of the existing nutrition systems. On diets No. 1, No. 5 and No. 9, patients receive a second breakfast at 12:00. This is usually boiled milk. For diet No. 9, an afternoon snack is provided - an additional snack. The features of nutrition include the use of seafood, which positively affects the patient's condition.



Diagnostic and advisory services

The sanatorium is distinguished by modern equipment and qualified specialists. There is a functional diagnostics office and a laboratory department, an X-ray and ultrasound, an endoscopic office. At the service of each patient is a gynecologist and gastroenterologist, psychotherapist and reflexologist, dentist. Some of the procedures are included in the price of the ticket, the rest can be selected and paid on the spot.

During the rest, you can undergo a full examination and get advice from a treating specialist, as well as a referral to narrow-profile diagnostics (if the situation requires it).

The main methods of treatment

Today, the Okeansky sanatorium of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation is able to accept up to 500 patients at a time. This is a multidisciplinary, medical and preventive institution, whose doctors over the years have accumulated considerable experience in the correction of various diseases. The main methods are climatotherapy and hydrotherapy, psycho- and reflexotherapy, hardware physiotherapeutic treatment, special nutrition and much more.

Rest in the sanatorium is interesting at any time of the year. Even in winter, guests will have the opportunity to swim in sea water. For this, there is a huge pool. His visit in combination with other unique procedures perfectly helps to restore health.

Cardiovascular Recovery

This is one of the priority areas. Here, rheumatism and cardiosclerosis, hypertension and neurosis, heart defects are corrected successfully. To restore health, climatotherapy and thalassotherapy, mud therapy and balneotherapy, specialized physical education and aromatherapy are used. In addition, being in nature will be very useful for the patient. Most of the time he will be able to be in the fresh air, listen to the splashing of the waves and swim in the sea, all this helps a speedy recovery.



Entertainment

On the territory there are cafes and bars, cinemas and a library, a billiard room. For the most athletic, there is a huge hall with a set of all the necessary equipment for various games. There are other entertainments:

  • Summer and winter fishing is a favorite pastime for guests of the sanatorium "Ocean" (Vladivostok). Reviews say that here you can get an indispensable experience. In the summer you can go to sea and try to get something from its depths. In winter, ice fishing awaits you. At this time, Amur freezes, and, having drilled a hole, the craftsmen raise a good "crop" from the bottom.
  • You are waiting for an unforgettable trip to Vladivostok, where you can visit historical places and museums, theaters. There is a huge number of reserved corners, each of which is worth a visit.
  • The resort organizes city tours and the most protected corners of the Primorsky Territory.

Instead of a conclusion

A multidisciplinary sanatorium is waiting for its guests all year round. Regardless of the weather outside, it is always warm and comfortable here. The cost of living is from 1810 rubles per person per day in an ordinary one-room apartment. Similar accommodation in superior room - 1950 rubles. Two-room suite will cost 2030 rubles. per day. This is the average price in the region, given that the price includes food, treatment and accommodation. Judging by the reviews, the resort is very decent. Here you can not only have a great time, but also thoroughly heal. Regular visitors note good nutrition, attentive attitude and professionalism of the staff, as well as a variety of medical procedures, each of which can ensure good health at least for the coming year.
