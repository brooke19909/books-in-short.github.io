Turkey, Kemer: the best hotels. Kemer: 5 stars, 1 line. Description, rating, review and visitor reviews

Turkey is a country with a unique history and picturesque sea coast. A place of affordable, but comfortable rest for tourists from many countries of the world. A favorite resort is Kemer, with its mild climate, warm Adriatic Sea, excellent level of service and many attractions. What is the rating of Kemer hotels 5 stars 1st line of the sea coast? Where is it better to stay and how not to make a mistake with the choice of hotel? We are reading an article.





Why choose Kemer?

Kemer is one of the most popular resorts of the Antalya coast of Turkey. Due to the proximity of mountains that protect against winds, the climate is mild: winters are not cold, and summers are moderately hot. Pine and cedar forests make the air healing, and the proximity of the sea fills it with a unique aroma. Picturesque landscapes and the ruins of medieval fortresses make Kemer interesting for tourists who prefer bathing in the sea for sightseeing. Well, for those who are going to enjoy bathing, sunbathing and relaxing without worries, Kemer is truly a paradise! Hotels of various levels make the resort affordable and attractive for people with a wide range of incomes. Here those who are looking for a budget vacation and those who prefer an all-inclusive system and a high level of service will be able to stay here.





What are they, the best hotels of Kemer, 5 stars on the first line, reviews of tourists about them and recommendations of professionals - all the answers in the following sections of the article.

Economy Kemer Hotels

Basically, resorts in Turkey, in particular Kemer, offer hotels at the level of 3-5 stars. What is the difference? She is very significant. 3 star hotels are very cheap, but the price will most likely be accommodation, breakfast and pool access. Most of the rooms of such hotels have long been in need of repair, and the food is pretty monotonous. They are located a kilometer or more from the sea coast. An ideal option for those who want to save on accommodation and plan only to spend the night in a booked hotel, spending the rest of the time and money to get acquainted with the culture of the country.

5 Star and Luxury Kemer Hotels

4 star hotels offer more comfortable rooms, they are usually closer to the sea, their infrastructure is richer, and food is more diverse. Among these hotels you can find very good ones at an affordable price.

Those who are going to relax in comfort and do not want to take care of anything during their vacation should choose only 5 star hotels. 5-star Kemer hotels - on the first line, with a huge territory and a lot of entertainment, a pool complex and its own beach and, of course, with an all-inclusive system. More details about them are below.





Well, for the most demanding guests who are not going to save on vacation, but want to see the highest level of service in return, Luxury hotels are suitable - they usually offer separate villas where the guest will have his own pool and a lot of other luxury amenities.

Tourists reviews

Our rating will be based on the reviews of tourists who chose these hotels for relaxation : Kemer, 5 stars, 1 line, all inclusive system. What do people say after rest? Basically, tourists are satisfied with their holidays in Turkey: although the cost of such a vacation is above average, but it involves a lot of services and entertainment included in it. These are comfortable rooms, and a wonderful territory, and organized leisure (both for adults and for children), and a varied menu. If complaints arise, they are usually connected with the bustle due to the large number of vacationers (which once again proves the popularity of the chosen hotel), the lack of sunbeds on the beach or the queue to the pool slides.
