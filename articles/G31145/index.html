A good washing machine: what is it?

At the moment, it is difficult to choose any model of household appliances. This is true for both large and small equipment, because the market has a huge variety of models, which makes the choice very complicated. How to understand such a wide variety of functional features, as well as individual washing programs? If you are interested in a good washing machine, then it is worth considering some points.



The most important criterion for her choice is the mass of dry laundry loaded. The larger the load, the more profitable will be the acquisition of this model. In the drum, designed to load 3.5-4 kilograms of laundry, it will all be crumpled, so it does not wash as it should. That is why a good washing machine is designed for 5-6 kilograms of laundry or more. In this case, every thing extends perfectly even with partial load. The engine load will be minimal, which will positively affect its operation. After all, the engine is the first to fail.



A good washing machine should have an optimal spin power setting. There is no direct correlation: the higher it is, the better. The spin is measured in the number of revolutions of the drum per minute. You need to be careful, because different types of linen require different washing modes. For example, for a hand wash program or for wool, this value should not exceed 400 revolutions per minute, since a high spin speed can simply damage such a fabric, which will make things very unusable very quickly. At the same time, fabrics such as cotton and linen are quite well wrung out at 1000-1200 rpm. At the same time, they will not lose their properties. Usually it is necessary to look at the composition of the fabric, as it is often combined, which dictates certain washing conditions. That is why a good washing machine, the reviews you liked about, should provide the opportunity to select a specific spin mode. Well, if the step of the selected power is 100 revolutions. So you can set the mode that most closely matches any fabric. Budget models have a drawback - the power level is already programmed for each washing mode, and it cannot be changed. It can only be turned off.


If you are interested in a good washing machine, then the important point here is the presence of additional functions. These include the degree of contamination of the laundry, quick washing, the delay in the start of washing, silver nano, aqua-stop. These features can be found in different models from various manufacturers. For example, the silver nano program is found in some devices from Samsung, it allows you to disinfect water. Aqua-stop is a program that is typical for manufacturers such as Electrolux and Bosch. If the water pressure rises at some point, this function will prevent leakage. The best washing machine allows you to set the degree of contamination of clothes, so you can set additional parameters in the washing program, after which the time spent on the whole process can decrease or increase, depending on the circumstances.

There are a number of parameters that depend on individual preferences. Here we are talking about the color and shape of the unit, the type of load, embeddedness and other parameters that do not affect the performance of the machine.
