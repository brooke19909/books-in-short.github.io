The main attractions of Dagestan

Dagestan has always attracted attention with the beauty of its nature and the identity of culture. The republic is located at the junction of Asia and Europe, therefore, it adopted the traditions and customs of these two parts of the world. Relaxing here is one pleasure, because you do not need to choose between the sea, mountains and forest, there is everything here. Silence, calm, clean air contribute to relaxation. Sights of Dagestan will not let you get bored, so the time intended for relaxation will be spent fun and with benefit.



It provides unique opportunities to get acquainted with the culture of one of the most ancient peoples, to look at architectural and historical monuments belonging to different eras, to admire the local landscapes. Lush rivers, alpine auls, flowering meadows, high mountains, clean beaches, the Caspian Sea - this is Dagestan. The republic welcomes its guests very cordially, so you should not be afraid of hostility from the local residents, they are very hospitable and friendly.

For a comfortable stay, it is best to choose pensions or hotels located in the capital of Dagestan. The sights of Makhachkala will not let you get bored, because this is one of the most picturesque cities in the Caucasus. A huge number of entertainment and cultural institutions, mountains, hot mineral springs, the sea will allow not only a good rest, but also improve your body. It is also worth visiting Derbent - the oldest cultural center of the republic. It has a favorable climate, beautiful nature, many monuments of architecture and history. It was from here that the traditions of Islam, artistic craft, art, and writing spread.



The main attractions of Dagestan are, of course, the landscapes of mountain peaks, dense forests, meadows and the sea. You should definitely visit the Samur forest, which is a real gem of the southern part of the republic. This is the only subtropical forest in all of Russia. The grass here sometimes reaches the belt, vines are found at every step. On the seashore there are excellent places to relax, near a forest, clear spring water, a beach.

Architectural sights of Dagestan should be sought in the village of Kubachi. The empty ledges, the stone cascades of the hut are somewhat reminiscent of a waterfall. In some places, the huts have grown together, creating a structure of several floors. Here you can familiarize yourself with the techniques of coinage and notching for precious stones. Gunib is considered the most beautiful place. The climate here is very mild, which is in no way inferior to Teberd, Davos or Abastumani. There are practically no winds, a lot of sun, clean air - all this has a beneficial effect on human health.



Tobot waterfall, Addal mountain, Sary-Kum dune, Aeolian city, Sulak Canyon, Mount Shalbuzdag - these are not all the sights of Dagestan. What are the beautiful beaches washed by the waters of the Caspian Sea, beautiful caves, fortresses built several centuries ago! In the republic, you can even find cave paintings painted on stones in the IV century BC. e. and preserved until today. Architecture lovers are interested in looking at watchtowers and battle towers. Since the people constantly fought with someone, the buildings are scattered throughout the republic. The trip to Dagestan will be remembered for a long time, because there is something to do and what to see.
