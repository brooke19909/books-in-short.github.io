Travel to Tel Aviv

For many decades, the "Promised Land" (Israel) has attracted thousands of tourists and pilgrims from all over the world. This amazing country combines biblical miracles and modern achievements of the Israelites, who were fifty years old enough to turn it into a blooming oasis. The rapidly growing cities, excellent quality roads and man-made forests amaze the imagination of every person who visited Israel. But, having arrived in Israel, one cannot fail to visit the capital of the state of Tel Aviv. He is rightfully included in the list of the best resorts in Europe.
