Planetarium in Saratov: description, services and prices

The planetarium in the city of Saratov is a center of culture and education, where visitors are invited to plunge into the beautiful world of space and see a model of the starry sky, constellations and galaxies, regardless of the time of day or climatic conditions. There are lectures on various topics. A planetarium is an organization that is always interesting to visit for people of different ages, both for children and adults. The features of this institution are described in the article.

Activities and activities

The planetarium in Saratov, located at the address: Chernyshevsky street, 177/181, is one of the institutions that is often visited by groups of schoolchildren accompanied by teachers.

The organization holds informative classes, demonstrations of various films, computer graphics, as well as other materials on space, the formation of galaxies, stars, planets, astronomical science.

An excursion to such an institution helps teachers to turn ordinary school lessons into a pleasant, interesting and useful pleasure. Such classes are characterized by understandability, brightness, clarity of presentation of the material. Thanks to these lectures, students can learn a lot about the laws of life in the universe.

In the Saratov Planetarium on Chernyshevsky Street, visitors are offered about seventy different classes devoted to the natural sciences. They are focused not only on children, but also on adults who are interested in the cosmos, phenomena and laws of the universe.

Events dedicated to various topics and events

The institution offers a variety of entertainment programs for children of almost all ages. One of the most popular events are matinees dedicated to a certain topic. They are intended for younger students. Groups of students, accompanied by teachers, visit the planetarium in Saratov on Chernyshevsky Street on September 1, at the beginning of the school year.

There are also events dedicated to events such as Cosmonautics Day, Sun Day, New Year.

Solemn events for children must certainly be memorable, cheerful, vibrant and fabulous. Employees of the institution try to organize the holidays in such a way that children of any age categories feel cozy, comfortable, they have fun and interesting.

In the Saratov Planetarium, prices for group visits (students accompanied by teachers) range from 100 to 120 rubles. For other categories of customers, the cost of services is 200 rubles. If visitors want to shoot any planetarium premises with a camera, they must pay 150 rubles.

Birthday

Relatives of the birthday man, especially if this is a child, try to do everything possible so that his holiday is bright, cheerful and memorable. The planetarium in Saratov offers services for holding such a solemn event on site. Little birthday people can make an interesting journey into space.

And when they return to Earth, delightful surprises from Astrologer await them.

In addition, the participants of the celebration are offered various games, a children's disco with lighting equipment, tasty treats (for an additional fee). Such an event can be attended not only by small guests, but also by their relatives.

Gala events at the planetarium for adult visitors

It's no secret that almost any couple dreams of a romantic date. The planetarium in Saratov offers original events for lovers. This is a date under the starry sky. Such a romantic event will undoubtedly become one of the most memorable, bright and colorful days spent with a loved one.

The planetarium in Saratov also organizes events for newlyweds.
