Insurance business and its essence

Then the property and slaves were insured against their possible destruction by various forces of nature or from other circumstances. So gradually an association of people interested in this process took place. There was an insurance business.

This is one of the main institutions of the economy. The most widespread insurance business was in the conditions of market relations.

Insurance is a prerequisite for the growth of the welfare of the nation. It makes it possible to stabilize the system of social and pension benefits.
