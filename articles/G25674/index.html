History of the Moneybux project



At the end of 2012, on the Internet in forums and blogs, a powerful advertising campaign began to promote a project that allows you to earn money while sitting at home at the computer. The platform was called Moneybux and had the address moneybox.com. The creators of the platform positioned it as a reliable project, where you can earn good money. To do this, it was only necessary to read letters, watch videos, register on other sites. All this was supposed to provide a stable daily income. And the majority believed, because many, especially novice Internet users, have no network experience; newcomers are willing to follow the administrators of such business projects. In addition, the level of payment for simple actions attracted many lovers of quick and easy money.

What happened next? The principles of the Moneybux website: registration
