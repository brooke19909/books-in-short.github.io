Knit crochet napkins: master class "original hot stand"

It is believed that the manufacture of napkins allows you to hone any knitting techniques, and also helps to learn to understand various patterns. You can start your first acquaintance with a hook precisely with the implementation of such openwork products that will become a unique decoration of the interior.

Making round and rectangular napkins will allow the master to acquire the necessary skills in using the hook. Learning how to knit, you can create other unique and beautiful things - bedspreads, tablecloths, pillows, etc. In this article we would like to share with you a step-by-step master class on how to make an openwork hot stand.

We crochet napkins: a step-by-step master class for beginners



We offer you to make a bright and elegant craft that will not only decorate the kitchen, but will also serve as an excellent hot stand. For work, you will need yarn in two colors (white and green), a four-millimeter hook, scissors and a needle with a large eye for embroidery. And, of course, do not forget about the excellent mood! So, we crochet napkins as follows. First, take the thread of the main color and make a ‚Äúmagic ring‚Äù. We carry out two air loops. Next, we knit double crochet posts in a ring. There should be 11 pcs. After that, we connect the ring, tighten it and perform one single crochet in the first loop of the row. The magic ring allows you to make the central hole minimal, which gives special accuracy to the product. The second row begins with three air loops. In the same row we knit a single crochet. Next, in each loop of the first row we knit two with a crochet. Thus, you should get them 24. Close the row with a single crochet, knitting it in the first loop of the row. We crochet napkins on. The third row begins with the implementation of three VP for lifting. After that, we continue to work according to the scheme: two double crochet, then one double crochet and again two double crochet (each element in a new row). As a result, you get 36 loops. Complete the series with a single crochet. Begin the fourth row with three air loops. Perform double crochets - 2 pcs., One double crochet and again double crochets - 2 pcs. As a result, their number will increase to 48. Close the row with a single crochet, knitting it in the initial loop of the row.

We crochet openwork napkins: schemes of the final stage



The fifth row will start with three air loops. We knit one single crochet in the same row. After that, we perform two air loops. Next, we knit two double crochet in the same loop of the previous row. Skip two rows. Again, make two double crochet stitches. Thus, we complete the entire fifth row. That's all, we can finish our knitting by stretching the working thread through the loop and cutting it. Our napkin is almost ready.

Crochet: decoration of the finished product

In order for our craft to become more attractive and elegant, we take a green thread. We will extend the hook in any row, preferably closer to the outer edge, and extend the air loop. Next, skip the hook in the loop of the next row and make another one. We crochet (napkins) by analogy, until we return to the first loop. In order to finish knitting, we will stretch the thread and cut it. That's all, our charming do-it-yourself do-it-yourself handicraft is ready.
