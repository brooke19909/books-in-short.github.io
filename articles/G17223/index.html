Nubia Aqua Beach Resort 5 * (Egypt / Hurghada): photos and reviews of tourists

Hurghada is a picturesque corner of Egypt, which is famous for its warm climate, gentle sea waves and clean long beaches. Thanks to this, every year thousands of tourists from all over the world come to this city. Naturally, there are many hotels for every taste. One of them is the hotel Nubia Aqua Beach Resort 5 *. So how close is the complex to the sea? What living conditions should you expect? Are there any conditions for children's relaxation? What reviews do travelers who have already managed to visit this place leave?

Location Description





The large Nubia Aqua Beach Resort 5 * hotel is located very close to the crystal clear waters of the White Sea, in a picturesque lagoon, which is famous all over the world for its white sandy beaches. Hurghada city center with all its attractions and entertainment facilities is 11 kilometers away. The international airport is 17 km from the hotel complex. By the way, tourists are provided with a transfer from the airport, and they will not have to go that long.

Description of the conditions for children's rest

Since many parents take their children on a trip, the interesting question for them is what conditions the Nubia Aqua Beach Resort 5 * hotel can offer. Traveler reviews indicate that children of different ages feel quite comfortable at the hotel and can always find an interesting lesson.

For example, there is a small outdoor pool. And in the winter, a separate section for children works in a heated pool for adults. You can have a good time at the playground. The mini-club, where teachers and animators are constantly engaged with children from 4 to 12 years old, works every day. In the evenings, the guys arrange real discos. For a fee, your child will be able to ride an electric car or brighten up leisure time for video games.

Additional service for guests





Hotel Nubia Aqua Beach Resort 5 * offers its guests some paid and free services. In particular, there is a laundry service, which guests can use for a fee. There is also a spacious parking of vehicles (nearby, by the way, you can rent a bicycle, motorcycle or car without problems).

On its territory there is a large supermarket where you can buy groceries and household items, as well as several stores that offer various goods, ranging from souvenirs to jewelry and clothes. There is also a photo studio where you can order a photo shoot from an experienced photographer.

Leisure and entertainment on site





Is there anything to do on the territory of the hotel complex Nubia Aqua Beach Resort 5 *? Reviews suggest that no one will be bored here. To begin with, it is worth saying that there are four pools of different sizes, including one with a heating system. There are small water slides.
