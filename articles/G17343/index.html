Among them is the Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *), built on the outskirts of the city of Protaras.



Description

This aparthotel is located two hundred meters from the sea, on the first line from Lombardi Beach. There are many shops, restaurants and bars around. A kilometer away is another swimming area - the sandy Fig Tree. Ayia Napa Resort is six kilometers away. Twenty meters from the main building there is a bus stop. The nearest airport (Larnaca) is a forty-minute drive away.

Location

The Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *) has a large and well-groomed area. It is literally planted with trees and shrubs. The hotel is a storey building of the main building and several small bungalows in front of which are green lawns. You can often see frolicing children here, as many prefer Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *, Cyprus) precisely because of its family-oriented nature. The hotel has its own parking lot, which residents should not pay for.

Infrastructure

The reception desk, located on the first floor of the main floor building, is open around the clock. Here you can reserve a depository safe, use a left-luggage office and get a full map of the hotel area with a list of all the services provided in it. Those who did not order a return transfer on the tour can arrange it right on the spot. Here you can reproduce documents or send a fax.



The hotel has laundry facilities, concierge services and a tour desk. Having resorted to the help of the registrar, you can call a taxi. ATMs are installed in the lobby, there is a convenience for currency exchange. For those who arrive earlier than the scheduled time, there is a lobby - a living room with a large TV.

Housing stock

Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *) is the average largest hotel. Its rooms are equipped in a four-story building and in two-level bungalows. This aparthotel offers both standard and open-plan apartments. Each room has a balcony overlooking the garden or pool, as well as the sea. All accommodation types include a kitchenette and dining area, fridge and microwave. Extras include wooden furniture, a TV and a telephone.

In total, Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *) has sixty-six rooms located in a floor building, as well as forty-four bungalows. Tourists must pay extra for using a small safe in the rooms, as well as for connecting to the wireless Internet. The family-category apartments have upholstered furniture. Bathrooms combined. The bungalows have bathtubs and showers are located in the floor building. All hygiene products are provided free of charge. After daily cleaning of the rooms, the maids update them.



Nutrition

Hotel Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *) provides meals only according to the concept of "continental breakfast." In the morning, a breakfast buffet is served for guests. The bar serves coffee and drinks throughout the day. Lunch or dinner can be ordered √† la carte in the restaurant, which serves local or international dishes. The reservation clerk must be notified in advance of the reservation. Those who wish can have lunch or dinner at one of the many cafes or restaurants in the city. To do this, there you can order a round-trip transfer. This service is paid.

For children

Like most hotels located in the Cyprus resort of Protaras, Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *) tour operators are positioned as a family. Therefore, there are always many children for whom all the necessary conditions are created. Parents for babies can get high chairs in the restaurant for easy feeding, as well as an extra folding bed. The hotel has a shallow paddling pool. On the beach, the entrance to the water is convenient for the kids and there is no need for special rubber shoes.

Additional Information

On the territory of Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *) there is a small outdoor freshwater pool. Around him there are always a lot of sunbeds and umbrellas. There is also a bar serving drinks and coffee. For freshly squeezed juices tourists will have to pay on the spot.



In addition, guests can spend leisure time by renting a pool table. There is no need to pay for table tennis or beach volleyball . The hotel has a library and a sauna. A lot of entertainment is presented on the municipal beach. Here trestle beds, sunbeds and umbrellas are issued for payment. The hotel has facilities for disabled guests and rooms for smokers.

Beach

The nearest bathing area is a five-minute walk. The road passes through the highway, no underground passage is provided. The beach is sandy. The entrance to the water is convenient, there is no pontoon.

rules

As in many other hotels, at Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *), check-out is set at noon, and check-in at two in the afternoon. If there is a free room, registration is carried out even after hours. The rooms are suitable for children of any age. In this case, the placement of one child under two years of age is free. The maximum number of children's cots / cribs in a room is one.

Tourists who come with pets are not allowed into the hotel. For convenience, those who wish can pay with credit cards. Russian-speaking staff works in the restaurant and at the reception of Maistros Hotel Apts (Class A 4 *).

Reviews

Those who rested in this hotel, basically everything was fine. Tourists should understand that they are going to an apart-hotel, in which you need to either cook yourself or eat at nearby restaurants or cafes. For independent cooking, there are all conditions: a toaster and microwave, a full set of dishes, an electric kettle.



The breakfast included in the price is good enough. The menu includes dishes for different tastes. No one calls breakfasts monotonous, since there are at least twenty kinds of food on the table. Cereals and several types of yogurts are served daily, which, judging by the reviews, our compatriots really liked, canned and fresh fruits, ham, assorted cheese, fried bacon, stewed beans and much more.

Many positive impressions of the Russians left the staff. Especially a lot of good words have been said to the receptionists who, without any surcharges, can, upon request, change the reserved number to the best.

Among the minuses of this hotel, some vacationers call the additional fee that they charge at the hotel for the inclusion of air conditioning and Internet. The rooms have balconies on which tourists really enjoyed sitting and watching the magnificent sunset.
