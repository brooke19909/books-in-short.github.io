Unlimited Internet on MTS: connection to a phone, tablet or computer

Mobile Internet today is no less popular service among subscribers than a package of minutes for conversations or SMS messages. The vast majority of people use smartphones, which gain tremendous opportunities if you connect to unlimited Internet.

On MTS.ru, like on the website of any other operator, there are descriptions and characteristics of all tariff plans, including those related to unlimited Internet. We will talk about them in this article.

To begin with, we note one condition, namely, speed. The fact is that many tariff plans involve unlimited Internet. Day or night?





Another important comment on the question of how to connect unlimited Internet to MTS is the time of day. The fact is that some tariff plans have more loyal conditions for subscribers who are interested in the Internet at night. For example, the Smart Nonstop tariff, which costs 650 rubles per month, offers the subscriber an unlimited connection at night, while during the day a different amount of data is activated, limited to 10 GB per month.

Another set of tariff plans is the Internet, which can be connected as part of the Unified Internet service. Here, on the Maxi option, the user is given 12 GB day and night (which are considered separately); and on the Internet VIP package at night, unlimited MTS Internet is available (on a phone, tablet or computer - no difference); whereas in the daytime, again, the traffic account is maintained according to the 30-gigabyte tariff (per month). The cost of service on this package is 1200 rubles.

Conditional Unlimited

To look for a suitable tariff plan, you need to understand how the grid is arranged for the MTS operator as a whole. There are two categories of packages - designed for smartphones (which also include minutes for conversations and message packages for communication); as well as those that are suitable for tablets with personal computers. The second includes tariffs consisting purely of Internet traffic.





To find conditionally unlimited Internet for a month, MTS makes it easy. Among the plans for a smartphone is the already mentioned Smart Nonstop. A reserve of 10 GB for a month in the afternoon is enough for comfortable communication on social networks, online games, viewing sites. If a person needs to upload a video or a movie, he would be able to upload at night (from 1 a.m. to 7 a.m.).

Another option is the Internet VIP, which provides 30 GB during the day and night unlimited Internet. Service "Unified Internet"

An interesting service was launched by MTS for those who would like to order mobile Internet in half with other subscribers. The operator allows you to share traffic regardless of the distance at which users are located. It is enough to register in the system one.mts.ru, after which you will get access to manage your resources. You can distribute the Internet to no more than 5 people (besides yourself).

The benefit of the opportunity to use such a service is obvious - now you can fold with friends and order a more expensive tariff, getting enough traffic at a lower cost. Plus, the distribution procedure itself, as stated on the company's website, is extremely simple. You can understand what you need to do for this, simply by looking at the special instructions.
