Hotel Natural Park Resort 3 * (Thailand / Pattaya): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Natural Park Resort 3 * is a magical place, because almost its entire territory is a picturesque green park. The history of the hotel dates back to 1990. During this time, its own traditions and high standards of service were developed . Nevertheless, a long period of operation had an impact on the condition of the hotel, slightly aging it.





Description of the hotel complex

Hotel Natural Park Resort 3 * (Pattaya) is a fabulous place, which is located in the shade of trees in a dense green garden. It is located at some distance from the objects of urban life, as well as from the main local attractions.

Natural Park Resort 3 * (Pattaya) compensates for its remoteness from the city with a huge range of services and high-quality service that meets the highest requirements and standards. It has incredibly comfortable rooms, as well as helpful and friendly staff who are always ready to fulfill your request or answer questions.

Natural Park Resort 3 * hotel provides such wide opportunities for entertainment and relaxation that they can satisfy the needs of even the most fastidious traveler. Here, excellent cuisine from the chefs of the hotel, many wellness services, as well as active entertainment.





Hotel Location

The hotel is located in the world famous resort city of Pattaya, in the south of Thailand. The hotel is located in a quite secluded place, 10 km from the city center, which makes it just perfect for a relaxing holiday. The city beach is located very close to the resort - literally across the road.

What the hotel offers

The resort complex Natural Park Resort 3 * can offer its guests the following services and services for recreation:

  • a large outdoor pool with clean fresh water, which is regularly disinfected with bleach and other special reagents;
  • two large cozy restaurants, one of which is ready to introduce you to all the features of local cooking, and the second prepares international dishes;
  • in bars you can drink alcohol or refreshing sugary drinks, as well as enjoy ice cream and a mouth-watering snack;
  • at the reception, absolutely free, you can deposit your valuables into a safe;
  • in the business center you will have access to computer equipment, and you can also use a photocopy, fax and scanner for your documents;
  • Wi-Fi is free in the lobby, next to the reception;
  • a small shop will delight you with a huge variety of souvenirs and accessories in a local colorful style;
  • You will certainly be delighted with the massage that the masters working in this hotel can do for you;
  • if you have a business meeting or seminar planned, then you have the opportunity to use the conference room for these purposes.




Apartments for hotel guests

Resort Natural Park Resort 3 * has 167 rooms, which can be divided into the following categories:

  • A standard bungalow is a small detached house that can accommodate two guests. It consists of a small bedroom with a king-size bed or a pair of single beds. Also on the territory of the hotel there are several bungalows, which consist of 2, 3 and 4 bedrooms, designed for large families or companies. It is important that you have your own spacious terrace with wicker furniture and a tumble dryer.
  • Superior room is a room located in a common building. It is one-room and is a bedroom, designed for two vacationers with a child (can accommodate in an extra bed). It has a small balcony from which you can enjoy the green landscapes of the garden.
  • Junior Suite - This is a spacious room located on the ground floor. Its feature is access to a huge private terrace, which is surrounded by green gardens. It has all the necessary garden furniture, as well as a canopy that protects you from rain.
  • The presidential number is striking in its size. It consists of five bedrooms, each of which has its own bathroom. Each of them is designed for two guests. Thus, these apartments are just perfect for families or large groups. There is also a large outdoor terrace for relaxing in the fresh air.
  • Like the presidential room, family apartments are arranged, which also have several bedrooms and bathrooms, as well as a terrace on the street. The difference is a spacious and comfortable living room, as well as a dining room and kitchen for family dinners.
  • Several bungalows are located in the wooded part of the park. Every two houses are interconnected by a corridor. A special feature is the national Thai style, which was followed by designers when designing these apartments.




Room Amenities

The rooms of the Natural Park Resort 3 * hotel have the following amenities necessary for guests:

  • a small balcony or a spacious outdoor terrace where you can relax while sitting in wicker chairs, as well as dry clothes on a rope;
  • modern air conditioning (split system), which allows you to regulate the air temperature in the apartments at your discretion;
  • a TV with cable channels, most of which are musical (there are several Russian entertainment and news programs);
  • using your phone you can not only contact the administration, but also make calls outside the host country;
  • the minibar will be filled daily, subject to the order and payment for this service at the reception;
  • You have the opportunity to store your valuables in an electronic safe;
  • The bathroom includes a shower and a hairdryer.




Entertainment and outdoor activities

The guests of the Natural Park Resort 3 * hotel complex (Pattaya) can fill their leisure time thanks to the following services:

  • you can swim in the cleanest pool, as well as sunbathe on comfortable sunbeds located near it;
  • for the smallest, a so-called paddling pool with water slides is also provided;
  • relieve stress and cleanse your soul and body by visiting the sauna;
  • on a public beach, hotel staff provide training in marine sports, as well as rent equipment;
  • certainly use the services of full-time masseurs;
  • if the weather is cloudy, then you can buy a chocolate tan in a solarium;
  • for walks around the hotel there is a huge green garden;
  • in the shade of the trees is a playground with swings and a sandbox;
  • You can get an unforgettable experience using the services of the spa.




Bonuses for guests

As a sign of respect to its guests, Natural Park Resort 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand) prepared for them some pleasant surprises:

  • If the newlyweds arrive at the hotel, then subject to the availability of free apartments, they will be accommodated in a superior room. Also in the room guests will find a romantic floral decor and figures of towels.
  • If the hotel has birthday parties, they will certainly receive a delicious holiday cake with candles as a gift from the establishment.
  • Guests who wish to commemorate their wedding anniversary with a trip to the resort are entitled to a choice of cake or plate of fruit. Also a pleasant surprise will be the romantic design of the room.
  • Regular guests, depending on the number of visits, are entitled to enjoy many privileges. This can be a plate of fruit, and upgrading the category of apartments, as well as many other features.

To participate in the loyalty program, do not forget to inform about your intention to receive a bonus even during the reservation or purchase of the tour.

Positive reviews

The best illustration of the level of service at Natural Park Resort 3 * - reviews left by its guests. They note the following positive points:

  • beautiful and comfortable rooms, made in the traditional local style;
  • well maintained pool;
  • a huge garden, which is constantly and carefully looked after;
  • a good gym with a large set of sports equipment;
  • hearty breakfasts that fit perfectly into the concept of healthy eating;
  • the room is regularly and thoroughly cleaned;
  • supplies of disposable cosmetics and hygiene items are constantly replenished.




Negative reviews

Among tourists, there is some disagreement regarding the rest in the Natural Park Resort 3 * (Pattaya). Reviews contain such negative comments:

  • the hotel is located very far from the city center and entertainment facilities;
  • upon arrival, there are often inconsistencies with the number of guests arriving and the availability of rooms;
  • breakfasts are held very early - around 6 in the morning;
  • construction is underway nearby, so you will have to forget about a relaxing holiday.
