This is a very important stage, the appearance of a thing will largely depend on the quality of its implementation. To do this, you need to know how to sew the knitted parts correctly so as not to spoil the product. Many needlewomen use a mattress seam in their work , which is traditionally performed on the front side. In this case, the density of the work performed will not be of particular importance.



It is worth noting that it is important to learn not only about how to sew knitted parts, but also about how to properly prepare them for this procedure. Do not start joining the individual elements immediately after closing the loops. It is first necessary to wash and dry the canvas, fixing it in a tense position. At the same time, the contours of each part must be formed in strict accordance with the previously constructed pattern. In this case, the finished product will ideally fit the size and better keep its shape while wearing.

To form a sweater with sleeves, you also need to know how to sew the knitted parts correctly. True, in this case it is better to use a lowercase seam. To do this, first marks the middle of the sleeve, which at the highest point is connected to the shoulder seam. The product must be turned inside out. Seams must be laid in the immediate vicinity of the edge loops. The result is a fairly strong connection. True, the seam with this method of execution is not flat, but voluminous.

Sometimes the junction of two different elements is the main decoration of the product. To make the finished thing look beautiful, you need to know how to sew knitted parts with a hook. In this case, forming the connecting posts, you can get a very beautiful seam.



No less careful you need to act by stitching crocheted dolls. The seam should be not only inconspicuous, but also strong enough. In this case, crocheted dolls will be able to survive the most diverse effects from the side of the child, without losing their appearance and preserving all available parts of the body. Work must begin on the wrong side, tightly connecting the edges of the canvas. After the seam is almost formed, it is necessary to turn the part to the front side and fill it with foam rubber or other soft material, which is desirable to compact as much as possible. Then the body of the doll will keep its shape well. After that, you can complete the work begun. Such a toy will be able to compete with purchased analogues.
