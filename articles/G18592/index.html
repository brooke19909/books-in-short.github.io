Holidays in October in Tunisia: traveler reviews

The velvet season is considered one of the best periods for relaxation, because the sun is not so scorching, crowds of tourists do not interfere with sightseeing, and the sea is still warm and gentle. If the vacation took place in the fall, then why not spend a vacation in October in Tunisia? Reviews of tourists about this country are only the most positive, however, the trip must be planned for the first two decades of the month, because then the rainy season will begin. In October, the air temperature is kept at +25 ¬∞ , and water - +20 ¬∞ . At night, the thermometer drops sharply, so you should take light warm things with you: sweater, sweater, cardigan, jacket.





Rest in October in Tunisia seems to many a fairy tale, because there is a huge selection of beaches with white sand. You can calmly sunbathe under the gentle rays of the sun, visit the proposed excursions and at the same time not be exhausted from the heat. Tourists who prefer outdoor activities will certainly enjoy this country. In addition to visiting architectural and historical monuments, Tunisia offers a two-day excursion program to get acquainted with the local oases, the Sahara desert, salt lakes, and the Roman amphitheater El Jem.



Traveling to the cities of the country also makes an unforgettable vacation in October in Tunisia. Reviews of tourists cause a desire to independently explore the cultural and historical heritage of this state. Each city has its own atmosphere, unique attractions. Cool autumn days can be spent on shopping in local shops and bazaars. Shopping will not only entertain, acquaint you with oriental flavor, but will also make an unforgettable vacation in October in Tunisia. Reviews on Tunisian products are the most positive. Here you can buy beautiful carpets, fabrics, spices, gold and silver jewelry, ceramic dishes and much more.

Exotic lovers will also like their October vacation in Tunisia. Reviews of trips in the Sahara by jeep are only the best. You can ride a camel along the dunes, visit the mountain gorges, green oases. Many will enjoy riding ATVs, scuba diving, golf, tennis, horseback riding on Arabian horses, sailing. Tourists can also visit water parks and attractions. Thalassotherapy (treatment with mud and algae) is ideal for those who want to improve their health.



In October, dates are just collected, so travelers have a unique opportunity to buy fresh fruits. For active tourists, Tunisia has prepared many entertainment programs. Rest (October 2013 turned out to be very warm) in this country will appeal to both adults and children. Local beaches are sandy, they have a gentle slope, making it very convenient to enter the water. Even the little ones will feel comfortable. Young people will like a huge selection of entertainment venues, the nightlife in Tunisia is very eventful. There are many clubs, bars, discos, there is a casino, and open-air light and sound shows are held. Tunisia always welcomes guests; the velvet season will bring many pleasant surprises to each traveler.
