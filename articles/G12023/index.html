Wireless Speakers for Bluetooth Phone

When you listen to music on your mobile, don't like the playback quality? Or maybe you want the sound of an MP3 player? Then you need to purchase a wireless speaker for the phone, with which the audio files from the mobile device will sparkle with new colors.

A compact portable speaker will replace the built-in speakers on mobile devices. A portable sound system increases the volume of the music, converting the amplifier signal into surround sound.

How to choose wireless bluetooth speakers for your phone

In order to guess with a purchase, be sure to think about why you need a column:

  • Spend more fun with friends
  • listen to music outside the home;
  • add mood while playing sports;
  • listen to music on trips, hikes, walks;
  • brighten up the time in the country, beach or on a picnic.

If you plan to regularly listen to good quality music outside the home, it is important to think about the right choice and the following options:

  • food - batteries, batteries, as well as the ability to connect to the network;
  • power - from 2 W for a single-band system, and from 14 to 200 W - for speakers with a subwoofer.

Useful Options

Usually the larger the device, the better the sound quality, but there are models that use an external surface (for example, a table top) as a resonator. This greatly improves sound at low frequencies.

The price of a portable wireless speaker for the phone depends on the functionality of the device and the characteristics of the speakers. In addition, the cost will be affected by additional features and functions:

  • FM tuner - when the device can be used as a standalone radio player;
  • screen - broadcast frequency, song name, time;
  • the presence of connectors and "sockets" for compact media - the speaker will be able to play music recorded on a microSD card or flash drive;
  • for communication via smartphone or Skype - speakerphone speakerphone;
  • protection against interference caused by a telephone signal;
  • equalizer.
