Hotels in Yarovoy "Rus" and "Khutorok": description of hotels

There are few who know the Lakes Bolshoy and Maloe Yarovoye, which are located in the city of the same name. These are mud-healing salt lakes located in the Kulundinskaya steppe. In terms of their medicinal qualities, they are in no way inferior to foreign ones.

Provincial city with great potential

The climate in Yarovoy is continental, so in summer the air temperature does not drop below twenty-five degrees. Nature and air of pristine purity. The city is located far from large industrial centers and highways, so the products of processing plants and exhaust gases of cars do not reach it. However, the transport connection to the city is good. You can get there by train or by bus. If you go by your own transport, then there will be no problems either. All hotels in Yarovoy have their own parking.

Spring is a modern provincial city, which has all the amenities for tourists, but there is no annoying noise of nightclubs. A combination of European quality and legendary Altai hospitality. This is a summer resort, and therefore many residents rent their apartments to vacationers. Many hotels in Yarovoy also work only in the summer.



Summer rest

One of the hotels for summer living is the Khutorok Hotel (Yarovoye). Being within walking distance from the beach and from the water park, it nevertheless has its own fenced area with video surveillance, which allows its guests to feel protected. The hotel has six buildings, in which twenty-three comfortable rooms are located: from double to quadruple. All have a TV, an electric kettle, a wardrobe and an obligatory bathroom with hot and cold water.

For visitors who want to relax in extra comfort, a separate two-story wooden cottage is offered.



Children under five years old are accommodated for free. Each cottage has a terrace where you can relax and enjoy the evening sunset. In the courtyard of the hotel there is a fountain, benches are installed. An agreement has been concluded with the summer cafe located a hundred meters away from the hotel. The second cafe is located on the territory of the Stagecoach Motel, which is also located nearby.

Trips to the city center

All hotels in Yarovoy have affordable room rates, and their choice depends only on customer requests. Transportation of visitors to the city center is carried out on modern comfortable buses from Omsk, Novosibirsk, Kemerovo and Barnaul. If you wish, you can call a taxi. Many hotels and hotels deliver holidaymakers with their own transport.

Long stay

This option is offered to its guests by the Rus Hotel (Yarovoye). Located in the city center, this hotel is of great interest to guests. It offers not only year-round living, which is convenient when undergoing medical procedures, but also has its own cafe and cooking. The number of rooms in the hotel is small and therefore a preliminary reservation is recommended.



Prices depend on the comfort of the room and the number of guests staying. For convenience, all rooms have a double or two single beds, a sofa, a wardrobe, bedside tables, a table, a refrigerator, a TV and a sanitary room with hot and cold water. Children under five years stay free of charge. You can take extra space.

This is a variant of a hotel in Yarovoy that can be used for long stays. If a person who has arrived in the city has not found free places in hotels, then you can always contact the center for the resettlement of city guests.

In Yarovoye, in addition to the mud cure lake, there are historical and architectural monuments that you can visit in your free time. Frequent performances by pop artists are organized.
