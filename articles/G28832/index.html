The best hotels in Naro-Fominsk: description, addresses

Naro-Fominsk is a small town near Moscow. It does not have such an accumulation of attractions, infrastructure objects as in Moscow, but quite often businessmen and business travelers come here. The city boasts excellent transport links with the capital, so getting from here to anywhere in Moscow will not be the slightest difficulty. If you find yourself in Moscow, but cannot afford expensive metropolitan hotels or you need to spend a few days in a town near Moscow, the best hotels in Naro-Fominsk will be an excellent option for you.

"Voyage"

Do you want to be in a homely cozy place, enjoy fully comfortable, impeccable service? Give your preference to the Voyage Hotel in Naro-Fominsk. All those city guests who chose this option for their stay recommend it to their friends and acquaintances.





Forty-seven comfortable rooms for every taste: here you will be equally comfortable with a couple in love, family with children or work colleagues. The rooms are equipped with modern comfortable appliances, a TV, a refrigerator, a telephone, a bathroom with a set of bath towels of cosmetics - that guarantees a great stay. "Sports"

The Sportivnaya Hotel (Naro-Fominsk) attracts guests with its name - many want to understand what it has in common with those conditions that are provided to vacationers.

Democratic, even budget, price is the main advantage of this hotel. A daily stay in a standard double room will cost only 1,500 rubles. For the money, guests can use modern furniture, a TV, a fridge and an en suite bathroom in their room.

The hotel is quite small, it is better to book rooms in it in advance, so that upon arrival you will not come across a lack of free places. Modern furniture, a TV, an electric kettle in the room, a private bathroom - that is the standard set that provides comfort during your stay.

"Vega"

Are you interested in the best hotels in Naro-Fominsk? Check out the hotel called Vega. This is a decent option. Spacious rooms of various categories with modern renovation and comfortable furniture, a TV, a fridge and a bathroom are what all guests can count on.
