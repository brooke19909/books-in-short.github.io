Hotel Gouvia Hotel 3 * (Greece / Corfu): photos and reviews of tourists

Gouvia Hotel 3 * is a small cozy hotel that has been operating since 1990. It is located in a quiet lagoon, which is reliably protected from wind and city noise. There is everything for family or outdoor activities, including the friendly atmosphere created by the staff of the institution.

The city center is 6 km away and the international airport is just 7 km away.

Catering establishments

The management of the resort hotel Gouvia Hotel 3 * took care of the quality nutrition of its guests. Corfu has quite interesting culinary traditions. Guests can visit the following catering establishments:

  • Misos is the main restaurant of this hotel. It is decorated in an antique style with a large number of decorative elements and greenery. Here guests can order a comprehensive three meals a day. There is also the possibility of service on the "a la carte" menu. There are Greek, Mediterranean and international cuisines.
  • The main restaurant is a cozy room decorated in a Mediterranean style. A delicious and hearty breakfast buffet is served here every morning. There are both cold snacks and hot dishes. There is also a separate buffet with diet dishes and kitchen appliances for preparing baby food.
  • The main bar is located in the lobby area. It is characterized by a bright design, in which there are oriental notes. This is a great place to watch sports matches or friendly gatherings. In addition to a wide range of drinks, there are also all kinds of snacks.
  • The pool bar is located on the outdoor terrace in the shade of the garden. He specializes in cocktails and cold drinks. Pizza and hamburgers are also served here at lunchtime.

Negative reviews

Do not forget about the negative aspects that may accompany your stay at the Gouvia Hotel 3 * (Corfu Island, Gouvia). Reviews say the following:

  • cramped rooms, and especially the bathrooms equipped in them;
  • the quality of housekeeping is very mediocre;
  • breakfast is constantly repeated;
  • too rocky beach and uncomfortable entrance to the sea;
  • the hotel staff practically does not understand Russian, and therefore, before the trip, you should learn basic phrases in English;
  • for the use of air conditioning you need to pay an additional 5 euros per day;
  • drinks are not included in the price of dinner, and therefore you will have to pay for them separately;
  • too long check-in procedure;
  • the rooms do not have any dishes (even glasses);
  • if you have booked a room in advance (for example, for half a year), then the reception staff may require a surcharge related to price increases.

General impression

Given the ratio of price and quality, on the positive side, you can characterize the hotel Gouvia Hotel 3 * (Corfu). Reviews note a convenient location that provides a relaxed atmosphere and quick access to infrastructure facilities. The main advantage of this institution can be considered a huge Olympic pool, which is kept in perfect cleanliness. As for the negative, it is worth noting cramped rooms with outdated interiors and poor-quality repairs.
