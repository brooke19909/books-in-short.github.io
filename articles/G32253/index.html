5 * Hotels: Hilton Fujairah Resort, UAE, Fujairah. Reviews, hotel description

Hotel 5 * Hilton Fujairah Resort - a chic resort complex, which is located next to the famous Hajar Mountain.





Hotel Description

The 5 * Hilton Fujairah Resort hotel offers its guests to stay in chic apartments or spacious chalets. There is everything a modern tourist needs: a sleeping place, a work area, a bathroom, and all kinds of equipment to ensure a sufficient level of comfort.

Guests of the 5 * Hilton Fujairah Resort have many opportunities for entertainment, relaxation and fun. On the beach you can go in for sea sports, fish, as well as ride a banana, canoe and other attractions. There are also many opportunities for sports lovers and other activities related to an active lifestyle.

You can also visit a number of bars, where a lot of drinks are always available, and there is also the opportunity to watch sports matches.





Resort Location

Emirate, in which the Hilton Fujairah Resort 5 * hotel complex is located - Fujairah. This is a beautiful and very picturesque place at the foot of Hajar Mountain on the very shore of the Indian Ocean. Having traveled by car or bus for just a few minutes, you will find yourself in the city center or the Old Fort, beloved by tourists. 25 km from the hotel are the beautiful waterfalls of Wadi El Vouraya, which you definitely need to visit. The journey from Dubai Airport to the hotel will take about an hour of your time.





Hotel rooms

Many tourists dream of getting to the UAE. Hilton Fujairah Resort 5 * offers its guests some excellent accommodation options:

  • Hilton Room with a double bed. There is also a modification of these apartments with a larger area and, accordingly, capacity. Guests are very fond of these rooms because their windows offer incredibly beautiful views of the sea coast.
  • The hotel has several family suites that are designed to accommodate four guests. There are two bedrooms, each of which is combined with its own bathroom.
  • King Hilton Apartments are for two guests. There is also the possibility of checking into a room with a child who is provided with an extra bed.
  • Twin rooms are small in size and have a double bed. This is perhaps the best option for a romantic getaway, so these apartments are often booked by the newlyweds for a honeymoon.
  • The chalets are located near the beach and can offer accommodation in double or single beds. The standard capacity is two guests, but depending on the size of the apartment, you can set another 2 or 3 extra beds. Each chalet also has its own access to the sea and private car parking.




Guest apartment features

In each of the rooms of the Hilton Fujairah Resort 5 * hotel (UAE, Fujairah) you have the opportunity to take advantage of the following amenities:

  • a modern large TV that broadcasts dozens of satellite channels (Russian-language programs are also provided);
  • an air conditioner with an individual control mode for air temperature and microclimate in the room;
  • mini-bar, which for a fee is filled with alcohol and carbonated drinks (drinking water is provided free of charge, and its supplies are replenished daily);
  • thanks to the availability of a telephone, you can solve issues with the administration without leaving your number, as well as make a call on an international line;
  • there is a built-in ironing board in the closet , as well as an iron so that you can put your things in order at any time;
  • in order to always be able to enjoy aromatic tea or coffee, the room provides an appropriate set, as well as an electric heater for water;
  • the toilet is combined with a shower, there is also a hairdryer, bath accessories and disposable packaging of cosmetics.

Hotel infrastructure

The Hilton Fujairah Resort 5 * hotel offers tourists to use the following objects of resort infrastructure:

  • a clean beach, which is constantly looked after by hotel workers (sun loungers, umbrellas and other necessary equipment are provided free of charge);
  • a fresh water pool will appeal to those who do not like noisy waves;
  • the gym has the most modern equipment for those who follow their figure;
  • the reception is open around the clock, so you can be sure that you can get answers to your questions at any time;
  • a beauty salon provides a number of hairdressing and cosmetology services;
  • dry cleaning will help to cope with stains on your clothes;
  • there is a large green garden for walking;
  • You can sunbathe and enjoy the views on the large outdoor terrace;
  • in the room fund there are apartments specially designed for non-smoking guests, as well as those that are equipped in accordance with all the needs of the disabled;
  • you can climb to the upper floors by elevator;
  • next to the reception there is a currency exchange office;
  • You can purchase souvenirs in stores;
  • it is possible to rent a car to independently explore all the surrounding attractions;
  • there is a network of restaurants, bars, as well as a night club on the territory;
  • since smoking is prohibited in almost the entire hotel, special zones have been allocated for this purpose;
  • if necessary, you can be provided with a transfer to the airport or in the opposite direction;
  • There are several halls for banquets, as well as business meetings;
  • There is a playground for children in the garden.




Rules of residence

The hotel has the following rules:

  • check-in and check-in are only possible after 15:00, and you need to leave the room on the day of departure no later than noon;
  • Children under 12 years old stay in the room free of charge, provided that they are accommodated in the beds in the room;
  • the cost of an extra bed for one night is 180 dinars;
  • For children under 6 years old, meals are provided free of charge, and for children under the age of 12 years, a 50% discount applies to restaurants in the hotel.

Positive reviews

Many comments can be found about the hotel Hilton Fujairah Resort 5 *. Reviews give a fairly positive idea about this institution:

  • own sandy beach, where it is always very clean;
  • the staff is very competent and polite, it is also important that some employees speak Russian;
  • the restaurant menu is decent enough, the assortment is large, everything is prepared very tasty;
  • a beautiful garden where many interesting exotic plants grow;
  • the rooms are quite spacious and equipped with everything necessary for life;
  • cleaning is carried out every day, while it is thorough enough;
  • a clean well-kept pool, from which there is practically no smell of bleach.




Negative reviews

Tourists have a number of comments and complaints about the work of this hotel complex:

  • a very small assortment of drinks (both alcoholic and non-alcoholic);
  • it is possible to drink alcohol in the bar only in the afternoon;
  • the appearance of the hotel is affected by its age - almost everything requires repair or replacement;
  • Outlets at the hotel are not provided for European standards, so you need to bring an adapter with you;
  • there is practically no entertainment for either children or adults.
