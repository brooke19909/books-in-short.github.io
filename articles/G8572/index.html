Ozerninskoe reservoir - a fishing place

With an increase in the level of urbanization, there are more and more people wishing to leave for the weekend or on vacation away from a noisy, dusty city, from unnecessary conversations and a large number of friends.



Far from the hustle and bustle

Where to? To nature, to the forest, to a reservoir. For example, Muscovites who want to get a little distracted from routine work may well choose the Ozerninskoye Reservoir as their goal. It was created in 1967 during the construction of a dam on the Ozerna River. Since then, this place has been a favorite vacation spot for residents of the capital and the Moscow region, and this is not at all surprising. Clean air, woodlands and a large number of fish - the dream of an avid angler.

The reservoir stretched for 29 km. Its width does not exceed 2 km. The shores are heavily indented, a large number of bays are found. A map of the depths of the Ozerninsky reservoir suggests that the bottom is uneven. There are many shallow areas - no more than 3 m. The maximum reaches 20 meters. And on average, the depth is 5-7 meters. The bottom itself consists of clay, sand and silt.

Things to do?

The construction of the dam served as the beginning of the establishment of a fish farm. Trout, grass carp, eel, peled, silver carp, sturgeon, bester settled here. Of course, now hardly anyone can say for sure whether these species are found in this pond, but the fact that fishing here is successful at any time of the year is a fact. The Ozerninsky reservoir allows lovers of fresh fish soup to catch a bream, pike, pikeperch, perch, roach, scam. Here are just fishing here is possible only on trips for a fee. In addition, some rules apply, the control over the implementation of which is quite strict. For example, live bait can not be brought from other reservoirs, in winter it is allowed to have only 5 girders for one fisherman, it is not allowed to use boats with gasoline engines.

Perhaps it is thanks to such strict rules that fishermen are not left without a catch either in winter or in summer. Therefore, it is definitely worth coming to the Ozerninskoe reservoir. Rest with tents allows you to choose a place away from other tourists. Although those who are not embarrassed by society, and those who do not like a "wild" vacation, can stay at the fishing base "Remyanitsa". In general, here you can also rent a boat and purchase a fishing permit itself.
