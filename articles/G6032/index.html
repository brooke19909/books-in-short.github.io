Dress in the Greek style with your own hands. Express option



Soon the time will come for bright and interesting holidays that will require you to have an appropriate appearance. Of course, you can buy a costume, but the best thing is to make it yourself. However, what if you do not have special sewing talents ?! Here this article will come to the rescue, which will tell you about how to create a beautiful dress in the Greek style with your own hands, spending a minimum of time and supplies.

Dress in the Greek style with your own hands. Manufacturing sequence

Step 1

Honestly ... This is the most difficult stage.

You need to go to the nearest fabric store and find some good fabrics of white, cream or dark purple colors.





Why such a color scheme ?!

In ancient Greece, white was, so to speak, traditional. He was present in the clothes of all the inhabitants. Dark purple fabrics were allowed to be used only by members of the royal family. In truth, women do not have the right to wear a toga, but no one knows about it. Despite these rules, you can choose the color that you like best. The main thing is that a dress in the Greek style, sewn with your own hands, brings you joy and satisfaction.

Step 2

Measure the fabric from your shoulder to the floor. Such strips of fabric will need two pieces.





Step 3

As a rule, fabrics have a width of 45-54 cm. If you think that it will be too wide for you, then you can cut it according to your parameters. But be careful at this stage - even if you do not want your suit to hang on you, then it also should not be too narrow, so it will lose sight!

Step 4

A dress in the Greek style can be sewn without a sewing machine. In a fabric store, find nice gold-colored cords to create a belt. If you have wide, metallic shades of the belt, then they also fit perfectly into the image of the Greek goddess.





Step 5

In addition, you need to find glue for the fabric. Ask the seller if they have any fabric adhesives - this will replace the sewing process.

Step 6

If you want to make the costume even brighter and visually more interesting, then buy a braid to trim the edges of the dress in a Greek design.





Step 7

A dress in the Greek style with your own hands will also require two large brooches from you. If you want, you can make one home by cutting a round piece of cardboard or a rigid base, then you need to stick a lot of rhinestones on it, etc. The glue should be used with a special one. You will get a unique accessory that can be used in everyday life. In general, there is where the fantasies take off.





Step 8

If you decide to use decorative braid, then glue it with glue to the lower edges of the fabric.





Step 9

To sew a dress in the Greek style, even a child can do it. Now connect the two strips of fabric - front and back - using brooches. Lay the fabric down nicely.





Step 10

Trim the hem and sleeves so that they look harmonious and beautiful.





Step 12

To complete the costume, it is necessary to place the back and front parts of the fabric crosswise under your arms and secure with a belt / cord. If you use a gold-colored cord, wrap it around the waist so that its two ends intersect, forming an X-shape.





Do it yourself dress in the Greek style!
