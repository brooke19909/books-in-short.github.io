What is brought from Montenegro? Presents for every taste

Holidays in Montenegro - an unforgettable pastime. The country, surrounded by mountains, cliffs and the purest Adriatic Sea, seems simply fabulous! It has a second name - Montenegro. Today, there is not a single production, except for farms. The country is one of the most environmentally friendly in the world. What do tourists bring from Montenegro to their relatives and friends? Naturally, local products, high-quality alcohol, clothing made from natural materials and much more. It all depends on the preferences of the person to whom the gifts are intended.

Lovers have a delicious meal

In the mountains of Montenegro there is a small village called Negushi. There are only no more than 200 inhabitants in it, but they were able to glorify Montenegro throughout the world! Negush cheese is made from sheep or goat milk at home. In taste, it is a bit like cheese, but not so salty.

Pay attention also to Montenegrin olive oil. It is not inferior to Greek in quality, but much cheaper in price. On sale there are both home and factory production.

Alcohol from Montenegro

Alcohol is usually included with delicacies. This is included in the mandatory program of what is brought from Montenegro. Montenegro has excellent wines and strong quality vodka.

The most popular drinks from Montenegro:

  • red wine "Vranats" ;
  • white wine "Krstach";
  • Montenegrin vodka "Rakia".

Clothing from Montenegro

What is brought from Montenegro for the older generation? As soon as you get to the market, the questions will disappear by themselves. Bazaars here are full of handmade warm clothes made of sheep's wool: vests, socks, sweaters.

There is something to please and younger fashionistas. Advantageous neighborhood with Italy has made Montenegrin shops very popular. Pay attention to products made of leather goods. Shoes, jackets, accessories of excellent quality can be purchased here at a very competitive price.

In Montenegro, there are almost no traditional shopping centers, so it is not so easy to find branded clothing. Local fashionistas and guests go shopping in the city of Bar. It is connected with Italy by ferry service. The prices for Italian brands here are just wonderful!

Religious motives

What can be brought from Montenegro to believing people? The famous thing is Montenegrin broyanitsa. This is a bracelet made of sheep's wool. Accessories are created by monks in monasteries. In the process, 33 knots and seven intertwined crosses are obtained. A bracelet costs about 2 euros. They can also be purchased in souvenir shops, and they are cheaper there than in shops at monasteries.

In Montenegro there are a lot of churches, temples, monasteries. The most famous are in Ostrog and Cetinje. From here, believers bring laudanders, icons, and crosses. In church shops, also pay attention to the monastery honey. It is absolutely clean and is found in the most unexpected varieties. You will solve the problem of bringing sweet tooth from Montenegro. The monks are also engaged in the procurement of medicinal herbs, the manufacture of ointments, potions on a natural basis. They can also be bought at the church.

Gifts with national color

Every tourist tries to bring something authentic from a vacation in a foreign country. In Montenegro it is a mouth guard. A cylinder-shaped hat with a double-headed eagle with a shield on top. It symbolizes the difficult historical path of the Montenegrin people.

The first thing that catches your eye in souvenir shops is figurines of old spouses. There are a lot of them. They symbolize marital fidelity and longevity. This is what they bring from Montenegro as a symbol of marital fidelity.

In tourist areas, in small shops you can find ancient coins, rings raised from the bottom of the sea. A great present for those who are interested in antiques and history.

Conclusion

Just going to rest and think what to bring from Montenegro? The reviews and advice of those who have already rested there are different. Men like delicacies and alcohol more. Women are delighted with the local silver jewelry, textiles, scented candles, herbal preparations, cosmetics. Most advise you not to buy many trinkets, but to accumulate impressions and take many pictures! And it‚Äôs better to save money for the next trip to this wonderful country! After all, a good mood, memory and a lot of warmth are what you can bring from Montenegro and keep it all your life.
