Hotel "Azimut". Hotel in Voronezh: reviews

Hotel "Azimut" (hotel in Voronezh) is recognized as one of the best places suitable for temporary residence. It should be noted that guests often leave a lot of positive comments in his address, which we will discuss in more detail later. We also consider the main features of the stay in the mentioned hotel, as well as the features of the service in it.

General information

Located in Voronezh, the Azimut Hotel (pictured above) is a place that is ideal for people who are accustomed to comfort and convenience. Many guests often admit that they choose this place only because everything is at hand: Azimut has a well-developed infrastructure and also offers a lot of opportunities related to organizing various events and trips.

The place in question is assigned a category of 3 *, however, according to vacationers, the service here is at its best. As for ratings, this hotel is rather highly rated on various international portals (most often, Azimut has a rating of 4 out of 5, which is a pretty good result).

Location

The Azimut Hotel is located in Voronezh, in the central part of the city, which, according to many vacationers, is its undoubted advantage. It should be noted that all the main attractions of the city are within walking distance from its building and, moreover, the main objects of urban infrastructure are located at a minimum distance from the institution in question.

From the airport, the hotel can be reached in just 30-40 minutes, and from the train station in no more than 10 minutes. Moreover, any nonresident or foreign tourist who has booked a room here can use the paid transfer service from the place of arrival to the destination.

The address at which the Azimut Hotel is located is Voronezh, Plekhanovskaya, 9.

Attractions nearby

As noted above, within walking distance from the hotel are the main city attractions. Out of their total number, non-resident tourists will definitely find it interesting to visit the central stadium "Profsoyuz", as well as the center "Sunny Paradise". Culturally developed tourists will certainly be happy to visit the State Opera and Ballet Theater, as well as museums. As for lovers of interesting architectural objects, they should turn their attention to the building of Voronezh University.

All tourists who plan to go to Voronezh and check into the Azimut Hotel are advised by the townspeople to visit local parks - Koltsovsky Square and Patriotov.

Rooms

During the previous restoration of the hotel, which was carried out in 2013, its entire number of rooms was completely restored. The interior of all types of apartments has been updated and their technical equipment has been improved. Today, every room has a modern finish and is distinguished by its ergonomics. Each of them has amazing technical equipment, which helps to ensure the maximum level of comfort for all residents. It consists of an individual air conditioner, electronic safe and refrigerator.

As for the furniture equipment of the rooms, it is represented by the availability of beds in the form of transforming beds, and also in any room there is an excellent working area consisting of a table and an armchair. To fulfill their work needs, guests can use free Wi-Fi access.

Each type of apartment is equipped with a private bathroom, in which everything is equipped with the latest technology. In particular, not only a bathtub is installed here, but also a shower cabin with the Rainshower effect. Moreover, the bathroom has bath accessories, the number of which is strictly designed for the number of guests, as well as furniture for their storage.

The room fund of the Azimut hotel (Voronezh) is quite extensive - it consists of 125 apartments, which are divided into three categories: standard, junior suite and suite. Let us consider the main features of each type.

Standard

Standard apartments are designed to accommodate a couple of guests. According to statistics, these numbers are the most popular, since it is in them that a rather comfortable placement is offered, for which the lowest fee is expected: from 2268 rubles per day.

Non standard are presented in a one-room version. Apartment guests often note in their reviews that they like the spaciousness of the rooms, as well as their interior decoration, made in a combination of white and brown colors with a bright purple accent.

As for the furniture equipment of such rooms, it consists of a pair of single beds (some apartments have one king-size bed), a workplace, a wardrobe for storing clothes, as well as a set of standard equipment.

Nutrition

Guests of the Azimut Hotel (Voronezh) often prefer to eat in the AVENUE restaurant, located in the building of the building for the vacation spot in question. As they note in their reviews, in this catering establishment they are attracted by high-class service, as well as the elegant interior of the main hall of the institution.

The menu of the AVENUE restaurant consists of international dishes, which are prepared according to the best recipes, verified in practice by world chefs. Of their total numbers, items such as Caesar salad with salmon, Viennese waffles with berries and ice cream, mushroom soup from mushrooms and chanterelles, as well as pork steak with grilled vegetables and BBQ sauce are especially popular.

The restaurant is located on the ground floor of the hotel in question. The institution works every day. In the morning, a buffet is served in the main hall of the restaurant (from 7 a.m. to 11 a.m.), to which all guests are invited whose price includes breakfast.

Gym

The reviews about the Azimut hotel often say that even if you are here you can keep fit in the gym at the building of the place for recreation. Only modern simulators designed for pumping all muscle groups are installed on the site of the hall.

Within the walls of this institution, the hotel has a large pool in which the process of water purification and its heating is systematized. The sports complex also has showers, changing rooms, and when attending classes, clients are given a towel and a bottle of water.

Reviews of the Azimut Hotel (Voronezh) very often consider the existence of such a hall on the positive side. Guests note that due to this they do not have to relax, keeping themselves in good shape, even staying in another city.

Conference rooms

Often guests of the Azimut Hotel (Voronezh) are business people who have arrived in the city to resolve issues arising from their business. Very often, guests of this type need to provide them with venues suitable for holding meetings with business partners, presentations, as well as negotiations. For this purpose, three conference rooms are equipped in the building of the hotel in question, the equipment of which fully complies with the latest technology. Here, guests have modern furniture designed to accommodate 190 people. Moreover, at the disposal of customers is the organizational equipment, as well as office supplies.

For conference participants, business lunches, as well as coffee breaks, can be organized at the halls.

Special offers

Hotel in Voronezh "Azimut" (the address was indicated above) is a place for relaxation, which regularly offers a variety of special offers. These include various discounts on accommodation for certain periods, as well as events at the hotel, in which its guests can take part.

So among the permanent shares there is the possibility of free accommodation for one day, subject to a one-time reservation of a period of stay of 15 days or more. When entering the hotel with children under the age of 12 years, the provision of space for them is free of charge.
