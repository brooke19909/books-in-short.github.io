Beautiful places to relax: description, names, ways to get there, photos and reviews of tourists

A vacation or a series of days off is a long-awaited time when there is an opportunity to break out to relax and gain strength. This article contains only the best and most beautiful places to relax in the world. Countries and cities are selected in such a way that a person with any budget can choose something for himself and enjoy the weekend.

Beautiful places to relax

You can see photos of such territories in the article.

For many people, true relaxation is associated with the beach and palm trees. You don‚Äôt have to go anywhere and rush. Know yourself lie in the sand and enjoy the sea. Especially for those who like to bask in the hot sun and swim in the azure waters, a list of amazingly beautiful islands has been formed, which are considered the most beautiful places to relax in the world. Their list and description is presented below.

Maldives

This is a real paradise that exists in reality. This place is not without reason called the most beautiful vacation spot on the sea. Pure turquoise water, soft white sand, palm trees and the burning sun - what else is needed for happiness? The island is located near India and is washed by the Indian Ocean. The cost of services and flights to the Maldives is not cheap. For rest in a paradise you have to pay a round sum. But tourists can be sure that their expenses will not be in vain - the days spent in the Maldives will pass unforgettably beautifully. In addition to lying on the sand and swimming, you can go diving and admire the local underwater fauna.

Caribbean islands

Spreading palm trees that grow right on the shore, azure, warm water, pure golden sand and eternal, hot summer await travelers in these places. On request, you can make exciting boat trips to explore the expanses and colorful landscapes of the island.

Bahamas

These islands have long been considered a symbol of luxury and chic relaxation. Many stars buy houses and apartments there to live closer to a paradise tale. Soft, pinkish shades of sand, warm, azure sea, beautiful nature - all this make a vacation in the Bahamas desired. Fans of a calm and comfortable weekend should pay attention to this place. True, the cost of the trip and all the rest will be very expensive. Flight and accommodation on the island will cost one person at least 80 thousand rubles.

Goa

Those who prefer a noisy and fun vacation should visit this Indian island. This place is very popular among travelers. They are actively engaged in diving, ride pleasure boats, fish and swim with dolphins. It is rarely quiet in these places. As a rule, life is in full swing here day and night. The beaches are packed to capacity. In the daytime, everyone bathes in the warm sea, and closer to night people have fun and arrange discos with modern music.

"White Harbor"

This place is located in distant Australia, which is famous for its excellent beaches. One of them is the White Harbor. He stretched along the coast for a long six kilometers. The place is popular with tourists due to the soft, white sand, azure water, diverse fauna and flora. Often in the ocean you can see frolic dolphins.

Halong Bay

It is located in Vietnam, on the island of Hanoi. This is a picturesque place that is washed by the South China Sea. On the island there are a great many caves and cliffs. There are very beautiful views and natural landscapes that you can enjoy forever.

Holidays in Vietnam are quite budgetary, for this reason tourism is rapidly developing in the country.

Where to go in Russia for the weekend?

It is not necessary to go abroad to have a good rest. The following are the most beautiful places to stay in Russia. Mother Motherland is generous with an abundance of nature, seas and all kinds of attractions, which will be interesting to see for every inhabitant of a vast country.

St. Petersburg

Those who have not visited the Northern capital, you must definitely see this city, the great creation of Emperor Peter I. It is a city of bridges, palaces, picturesque gardens and white nights. St. Petersburg is located on the Neva River, along which ships often sail. The climate in St. Petersburg is quite cold, for a comfortable pastime it is best to come here in summer or late spring.

Altai

This place can truly be called the heart and soul of Russia. Crystal lakes with clear water, high mountains, clean air that literally makes you dizzy, caves and forests await travelers here. In these places, mountaineering and fishing are well developed.

Baikal

This is the most famous and deepest lake (about 1 km) in the world. With its beauty, it attracts tourists from all over the country. Pure fresh water, rich wildlife, sandy beaches and pristine nature makes this place unusually attractive. You can get to Lake Baikal through Irkutsk or Ulan-Ude.

Elton Lake

To swim in the salty waters, it is not necessary to go to Israel to the Dead Sea. Elton is located in the Volgograd region. Due to the large accumulation of salts, the lake has a pinkish tint and looks very nice against the backdrop of a sandy beach and blue sky.

Sochi

Those who dream of a good sea vacation without leaving Mother Russia have a direct road to Sochi. The hot sun, warm Black Sea, water parks, dolphinariums, entertainment clubs - and this is only part of what a tourist will expect in these parts.

Where to go with the children?

During a family trip, several mandatory factors should be taken into account: safety, comfort, the availability of gaming and entertainment venues. In this list, the most suitable and beautiful places to relax with a child are further selected.

Orlando, Florida

This is a real American paradise that will surely appeal to children of all ages. The entertainment industry is booming in this city. Here the kids are waiting for the famous Disney Land. It is a whole city with many shops, cafes, attractions, water parks and clubs.

You can not forget about the Universal Studios park, which belongs to the famous film company. He has no less popularity than Disney. On its territory there are shopping centers, water parks, souvenir shops and cafes. Studios Universal has a lot of attractions and places that are entirely devoted to films made in their studio. One of them is the famous Jurassic Park.

Having visited this place, not only children, but also their parents will receive a dose of excellent mood.

Turkey

This country has long been a favorite vacation among Russians. There are several reasons for this - the low cost of services, a beautiful sea and plenty of entertainment to choose from. Children definitely will not have to be bored: there are many places with attractions and water parks. Many modern hotels have games rooms and pools.

Bulgaria

This is one of those countries where you can have a great rest without spending fabulous money. In addition, the level of service here is no worse than in the famous European resorts. Bulgarians are wonderful towards Russians, no barriers in communication arise.

The country will delight travelers with the warm Black Sea, clean sand, equipped beaches and delicious food. Especially for children, many hotels have playgrounds.

In addition to the sea and beaches, Bulgaria is rich in historical sights. These are ancient fortresses, which are not one hundred years old, and monasteries. Here is a very beautiful picturesque nature, which will appeal to both children and adults.

Greece

Here, in the beautiful homeland of Zeus, you can have a great time with the whole family. In Greece, anyone can plunge into an ancient Greek fairy tale, see the ancient Olympic stadium, monasteries and temples with figures of goddesses. Children will certainly be interested in viewing beautiful sights, and then having fun splashing around in the warm Aegean Sea. Greek cuisine is especially good - fresh Mediterranean food will appeal to even finicky eaters.

Vietnam

It's no secret that vacation in Asian countries is quite expensive. But Vietnam is not on this list. A flight, of course, is not cheap, but it is an order of magnitude less than the amount needed for a ticket, for example, to Japan.

It is better to go here in winter or spring, when Vietnam is not too hot. The country has very well developed beach tourism. Those who want to enjoy plenty of sea holidays, it is better to go to the island of Hanoi. And those who want to see the sights of the East or visit the amusement parks with their children should pay a visit to such cities as Ho Chi Minh City and Nha Trang. A huge white Buddha statue, picturesque temples, amusement parks, a zoo and other interesting places will be waiting for travelers there.

You should definitely try the local cuisine, especially the famous pho soup. In addition to Asian cuisine, here you can safely buy a typical European meal - a croissant with coffee. The echoes of the past, when Vietnam was under the French colony, make themselves felt.

Beautiful places to relax in the summer

Not all countries are comfortable visiting during the hot season for a European. Some of the exotic places are located in sunny latitudes, and only locals are adapted to life there. Therefore, before planning a trip, you need to ask your tour operator about when it is better to visit a country. Below is a list of countries that can be visited during the European summer.

Brazil

Who has ever dreamed of visiting the homeland of samba and a fun carnival? Eternally sunny Brazil is always glad to guests. This country is located in South America. Unfortunately, there are no direct flights from Russia to Brazil, you will have to fly with transfers in Europe or Asia.

Most tourists fly there for a sea holiday. The most famous and popular beaches are Ipanema and Capacabana. There are pure sands, a calm ocean, not far from the coast there are shops with soft drinks and cafes. Near the beaches there are hotels and clubs where local youth loves to have fun.

In addition to the beaches, Brazil has something to see. In Rio de Janeiro, the country's largest city, you can look at the legendary and huge statue of Christ, which stands on a mountain, its size exceeds 800 meters. Tourists will see the attraction called Sugarloaf near Guanabara Bay. This mountain is almost 400 meters in size. At its top there is an observation deck with a wonderful view of Rio de Janeiro.

Australia

This is a distant country that attracts tourists from all over the world. Australia is truly an amazing and wonderful place. On its territory live dangerous crocodiles, poisonous spiders and funny kangaroos. The country is rich not only in diverse fauna, but also in flora. Across the continent of Australia are wide forests, lakes, mountains and rivers. One week is not enough to see all these natural wonders.

Be sure to pay a visit to such beaches as White Harbor and Bondi. People there are constantly engaged in surfing, which is a favorite sport among locals.

Spain, Barcelona

Hundreds of travelers visit sunny, vibrant and vibrant Barcelona every year. It is worth taking a walk in Guell Park, admiring the views of the cities from the roof of Mila‚Äôs house, and getting into the Middle Ages through the Gothic Quarter. This list goes on and on. Anyone who came to Barcelona for a sea holiday can visit the local beach of Barceloneta. It is quite old, sandy, is very popular among young people.
