Hotel Amorgos Boutique Hotel 3 * (Larnaca, Cyprus): description, photos, reviews of tourists

Amorgos Boutique Hotel is a small but very cozy hotel that boasts stylish apartments, a decent level of service and a good location. However, this hotel has more advantages. And about all it is worth briefly telling.





Location

Amorgos Boutique Hotel was built in 1990. Despite the fact that the hotel has been over 25 years old, everything inside looks stylish and new. Since the last major overhaul was completed in 2013, not so long ago.

The hotel is located in Larnaca. This is a fairly large city in Cyprus, which is the largest airport on the entire island. Larnaca itself, in terms of tourism, is known for its long beaches that stretch for 25 kilometers.

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is a palm beach, known as Finikoudes. One of the most popular in Cyprus, by the way. There is also the central promenade of Larnaca. From here, you can drive to the airport in 15 minutes. And in the vicinity you can find a lot of different cafes, clubs, shops, stalls and supermarkets. In general, an ideal place for people who love to walk and have fun.

What is in the hotel?

Amorgos Boutique Hotel, like any other presentable hotel with an exclusive service, offers its guests everything that may be needed for comfort. Wi-Fi in its territory is free, in the rooms it is the same. Guests arriving by car (or renting it) are provided with a private parking space.

The hotel has an ATM and a storage room for guests. Ticket services and a tour desk are available. The reception is open 24/7, so that accelerated or individual check-in is available (and departure too).

There is a laundry and dry cleaning service, as well as a business center at which there is an office with fax and photocopying services.





Service

Amorgos Boutique Hotel staff clean the rooms every day. With the same regularity, linen and towels are changed, which also pleases. Everything is fresh, snow-white. And the linen even creaks from cleanliness. By the way, if something is necessary, you just need to contact either the reception or directly to the maids. Many of them speak Russian, and some even come from Russia themselves. Hygiene products are regularly updated as necessary. This soap, shampoo, toothbrushes and toothpaste, shower gel, body lotions and shaving kit.

Guests can use the wake-up service, rent a car (you need to contact the reception), ask for a soundproofed room or non-smoking apartment. There is also the opportunity to order the delivery of drinks and food in the room. By the way, the hotel has a lift for convenience. The hotel has five floors, so it really helps out.

It is also worth paying special attention to the level of staff training. At Amorgos Boutique Hotel 3 * there are real experts who speak six different languages. These are German, Greek, French, Romanian, English and Russian.

Nutrition

Amorgos Boutique Hotel 3 * (Larnaca) has a cozy bar where guests are served a buffet breakfast. Since the hotel has only 46 rooms, there is enough space for everyone. Breakfast includes hot items. As a rule, these are scrambled eggs, fried eggs, mushrooms, sausages and grilled sausages, delicious baked vegetables and cheese slices. There are famous Greek olives and olives. Fresh vegetables are also served - cucumbers, tomatoes. Everything is chopped, you can make the salad yourself by seasoning with olive oil. For breakfast, you can eat pastries with coffee or tea, taste fruit. Even watermelons are served here in the morning.

By the way, if you ask for a juice in the bar during the day, they will give it for free. At dinner, drinks are also served without payment (unless, of course, the person ordered half board). In the evening, they also offer good dishes - salads, meat with side dishes, desserts. However, you can only pay for breakfast, and for dinner try something new in different cafes and restaurants, which are very numerous near the hotel. And in many - reasonable prices.





2-seater "standards"

Everything is designed in harmonious colors, and the interior is very well complemented by various decorative elements. Immediately felt the level of the hotel. However, this can be seen by looking at the above photo.

In the middle is a large double bed, opposite which there is a plasma TV with satellite channels. By the way, there is even one Russian-speaking.

Inside there is a minibar, access to a private balcony with a view of the city, a telephone for communication with the reception, safes (regular and for a laptop). A powerful air conditioning and a work desk are installed. By the way, a guest can be provided with a laptop if necessary. There is a dressing room and a separate dining area with an electric kettle and coffee / tea making facilities.

There is also a private bathroom with a shower and previously listed toiletries. Hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers, towels - all this is also provided to guests. By the way, upon arrival, guests are greeted with wine, fruit, and they also put bottles of purified water. In general, everything is on the level, as befits a hotel of this class.





Cost

Now a few words about the price of a week in such a luxurious apartment. Amorgos Boutique Hotel reviews are extremely positive. Due to the fact that the hotel is not only good for its service, but also does not set excessively high prices for accommodation.

For two people, 7 days with breakfast will cost 40,000 rubles. It happens and cheaper. If, for example, reserve in advance, out of season, or receive a discount. Sometimes you can save 5-7 thousand.

If you want to book half board, you will have to pay more - about 50,000 rubles for two. One apartment can be accommodated in such apartments. About 35,000 rubles will have to be paid for a room with breakfast.

Superior Apartment

Also, these apartments are distinguished by increased soundproofing. And there is furniture on the balcony. Otherwise, all conditions and amenities are the same as those of the above rooms.

These apartments will cost 45,000 rubles for two with breakfast (per week). If you take half board, you will have to pay 7,000 more.

About booking and accommodation

When applying for a reservation, credit card details are required. This property is hosted by American Express, Diners Club, Visa and Master Card. A certain amount may be blocked in the account until the guests arrive as a guarantee.

Check-in starts from 14:00 until 23:00. Departure starts at 7:00 and lasts until noon. If you need to check in earlier or check out later, you should notify the administration about this. Individual registration is possible.

You can stay at the hotel with children, although rarely anyone comes here with kids. Indeed, for them there are no conditions, and indeed, the hotel is focused on business people or a quiet relaxing holiday. Nevertheless, special chairs and cribs are provided for babies. And if the child is not 12 years old, he can live for free. And he will also be provided with an extra bed.

It is worth noting that it is most profitable to come here out of season - in late autumn or winter. Apartment prices are falling very much. Air and water temperatures too. But not really. The sea rarely cools below 17-19 degrees, so people who are not particularly picky about temperature can swim. And even necessary, because the sea here at this time is even cleaner than usual. And the beaches are almost empty.





What do the guests say?

People staying at Amorgos Boutique Hotel 3 * leave good reviews, encouraging others to visit this hotel. All note the level of service and quality of service. They say that according to these criteria a hotel can even be called a 4-star hotel. Everything is clean, neat, stylish and modern. And the attitude towards the guests pleases.

So, for example, if guests have arrived earlier and the room is not ready yet, they will be offered to have lunch - mineral water with ice, hot appetizers, salad, main dishes, desserts. Items can be left in the luggage storage room and relax until check-in.

The waiters at the bar serve quickly, you do not have to wait. And everything is tasty, varied - dishes do not repeat in the next 7-10 days. This can not but rejoice.

In the immediate vicinity are local attractions - this is the Armenian church of St. Stephen, Dimarkiou Square, the city hall, Larnaca harbor, the Byzantine Museum of St. Lazarus and much more. Guests say that even trips can not be booked - just leave the hotel and go for a walk, explore the surroundings. Beautiful and interesting places here at almost every step.

To summarize all that has been said, we can confidently say that this is indeed one of the best hotels in Larnaca, and for many reasons. All of them have been described above in detail. It is definitely worth going here to people who are accustomed to good service and maintenance, or who want to experience it on themselves. Quiet, tranquil, secluded relaxation in a great location, close to the sea, provided for visitors to tourists. Many claim that you want to come back here again. And in fact, it's hard to argue with that.
