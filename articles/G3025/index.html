Negative reviews

Do not overlook the negative aspects that characterize the Rus Hotel (Moscow). Negative feedback is as follows:

  • the hotel looks inconspicuous from the street, and the sign is practically not visible, and therefore it is not so easy to find it;
  • the registration procedure is long, too many extra papers and other bureaucratic formalities (the situation is aggravated by the sluggishness of the reception staff);
  • the key card is constantly demagnetized, and therefore, almost every day you have to contact the administrator with this problem;
  • the actual condition and appearance of the rooms does not correspond to what is presented on the official website (in the photo on the Internet, the rooms at least look more spacious);
  • there is a dummy on the USB-connector in the TV, and therefore you will not be able to watch movies from a flash drive (a strange move for a three-star hotel);
  • the area is quite noisy, and therefore rest with open windows will be problematic;
  • breakfasts are the same every day (fried eggs or porridge);
  • standard rooms have a pair of shifted beds instead of a large bed;
  • ugly appearance and windows;
  • during breakfast, tasteless and not very high-quality instant coffee is served;
  • the rooms are very smoky (the smell of tobacco smoke is especially distinct in the bathroom);
  • The TV works intermittently, catches few channels;
  • no elevators;
  • dental supplies are not included in the hygiene kit, but are available at an additional cost;
  • bath towels are clean, but for some reason they have some kind of musty smell;
  • poor water pressure in the bathroom;
  • there are no mosquito nets on the windows , so if you want to ventilate the room, you run the risk of running mosquitoes and other insects into the room;
  • ventilation in the bathroom does not work well, therefore, it is always damp, and towels practically do not dry;
  • the carpet in the rooms is very old and stained.
