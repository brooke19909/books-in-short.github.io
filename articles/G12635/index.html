Ozonizer "Tiens". Description and feedback

Ozonator "Tiens" is a third-generation environmental device, which was created by the eponymous corporation. In this development, unity of knowledge was found in various fields of science: mechanics, electronics, and microbiology.

Ozonator "Tiens" is designed to perform the following functions:

- disinfect drinking water;

- disinfect dishes;

- disinfect fish, meat and sea products, as well as clean vegetables and fruits;

- Deodorize washing machines and refrigerators;

- to process aquariums and treat fish diseases.





Ozonator "Tiens" is a truly unique device. Its properties are due to the special characteristics of ozone, which bring a person good.





Ozonator "Tiens", reviews of which speak of its positive impact on improving the quality of life, is a necessary device for offices, houses and apartments.

The device purifies water. In this case, it becomes mineral. Twenty minutes after the cessation of treatment, water is sterilized, excess chlorine is eliminated in it, manganese, iron and inorganic compounds are broken down. In addition, there is an increase in oxygen content.

Ozonator "Tiens" helps to clean the air in the room. As a result of his work, the smells of new furniture and recently made repairs are eliminated. In order to achieve the desired result, the device turns on for thirty minutes. At the same time, it is necessary to leave the room by closing the door tightly. It is allowed to enter the room fifteen minutes after the completion of the procedure.





The use of an ozonizer helps to eliminate unpleasant odors in restaurants and cafes, medical institutions and hairdressers, spas and ambulances, pet stores and dry cleaners. The device helps to clean the air of cigarette smoke. It neutralizes the smells of cleaning chemicals, alcohol and drugs. Using this device allows you to clean the air, enriching it with oxygen, eliminate negative ions and accelerate the recovery of patients in clinics. Ozonizer eliminates unpleasant odors from pets and trash. The device is used to enrich the kitchen space with oxygen. This eliminates odors from the refrigerator, as well as fried foods and carbon monoxide.

With regular use of the device, harmful insects (ticks and fleas) disappear. The ozonizer produces antibacterial cleaning of containers and utensils, shoes and clothes. This prevents the onset and spread of infections.

The ozone generator of the Tiens company is able to clean bacteria from the aquarium by saturating it with oxygen. Turning the unit on daily will prevent bacteria, parasites, and fish diseases.

To remove an unpleasant odor in a cabinet, the device is placed among the clothes located there. This prevents mold.

You can make an ozonizer with your own hands. Such a device will consist of a compressor purchased at a pet store and a generator. However, such a device is unlikely to replace the ozonizer produced by professionals.
