Hotel "Mediteran" (Montenegro, Becici): traveler reviews

The Adriatic coast of Montenegro has attracted the attention of Russian tourists for many years. Here you can relax inexpensively, while receiving quality service. When choosing a suitable ticket, you must first pay attention to the hotel in which you have to live. It should be clean, comfortable, have enough entertainment, tasty food, reasonable prices. Consider whether the hotel "Mediteran" (Becici, Montenegro) meets these requirements.

About Hotel Mediteran

The hotel is located in the central part of Montenegro, the infrastructure of which is fully focused on tourists. Mediteran was built in 1972 during the mass development of the Adriatic coast. The complex consists of one six-story building, equipped with two modern elevators. The hotel has direct access to the sea and a private stretch of beach.





"Mediteran" (Montenegro) positions itself as a family hotel, where you can relax with children. At the same time, there are many amenities for youth companies. The hotel has 230 rooms. Among them there are single, and double, and triple apartments. The hotel staff speaks Russian. Check-in with pets is not allowed. The last reconstruction of the complex was carried out in 2004. Thanks to the repair, Mediteran has a modern interior, refined and elegant design. Check-in is at 14.00, and check-out must be no later than 12.00.

Location of the complex

Hotel "Mediteran" (Montenegro) is located in the very center of the country, in the resort village of Becici. The town is fully tourist oriented. The streets are surrounded by greenery and flowers, and along the walking roads there are many shops and restaurants. The complex is located on the coast of the sea just 2 kilometers from Budva - the oldest city on the Adriatic coast. Her story spans nearly 2.5 thousand years. The city forms around itself the Budva Riviera - the famous resort area, which occupies about 30 kilometers of the Adriatic coast. It is known for its pristine beaches, beautiful Mediterranean culture, as well as a rich nightlife.

Mediteran (Montenegro) is located 23 kilometers from Tivat International Airport, as well as 75 kilometers from Dubrovnik. You can get to the hotel from the capital of Montenegro - Podgorica. Adriatic highway passes by the village of Becici, through which you can easily reach the hotel. These places are perfect for independent car travel. Also, paying 1.5 euros, you can get to the hotel on a special tourist mini-train that runs along the coast, making a stop at each hotel. Mediteran is located 100 meters from the beach, so the path to it will not take you more than 3-5 minutes.

Number of rooms

The Mediteran complex (Becici, Montenegro) consists of one building, in which 230 different rooms are located. Most of them overlook either the Adriatic coast or the water park located on site. Tourists can stay in a room designed for two or three people. At the same time, there are double rooms with a common bed and separate beds. The hotel also provides special non-smoking rooms.





The rooms have a quality and elegant repair. The walls are painted in calm pastel colors. The interior itself is also designed in a neutral style. The room has its own furnished balcony. In addition to the bed, the room also has cabinets, a desk, a new TV, as well as armchairs and mirrors. The rooms are decorated with exquisite table lamps and potted flowers. In addition, it will require additional expenses for maintenance, tips, food, drinks and entertainment, as well as visiting the sights that Montenegro (Becici) is famous for. Hotel "Mediteran", reviews of which are rather controversial, is considered one of the best on the coast of Budva, so the cost of the rest corresponds to the quality of the services provided.
