Crochet a summer shirt rather quickly. If there is not much experience, it does not matter. Unlike knitting, when the uniform density of the entire fabric and the same size of the loops are crucial, crocheting allows you to hide minor flaws of an inexperienced craftswoman. The problems of inexperienced knitters

The problem for all beginner knitters is that they knit loops of different sizes. The pattern connected at the beginning differs markedly from the pattern of the last rows. A monotonous lace from different-sized loops does not look quite aesthetically pleasing. When crocheting, this trouble is easy to veil, if you combine different techniques in one thing - floral round motifs, openwork polygons, elements of Brugge and Irish lace, as well as linear patterns.

In order for a crocheted openwork shirt to work out well the first time for a knitter who first grasped the hook, the following recommendations should be followed.

You should not start with a shawl either, because it is usually knitted in a large size with the same pattern. Consequently, uneven ranks or motives will immediately catch the eye.



A crocheted skirt is very beautiful, but you can‚Äôt wear it with every blouse, moreover, making a lining is not an easy task, and the size of the skirt is usually quite large.

Second recommendation

The first thing, knit from acrylic, polyester or other synthetic threads. This recommendation is due to the fact that natural yarn shrinks after washing. If you correctly calculate the number of loops according to your size, then after the first wash you will be surprised how much your shirt has changed and how short and narrow it has become.

Irish knitting pattern

In our article you see photographs that depict a crocheted openwork T-shirt. The diagram describing the individual elements is clear and easy to implement. However, it requires great accuracy and accuracy when connecting individual elements. For this reason, this model can be recommended only to experienced craftswomen.



For a T-shirt you need to knit fourteen flowers and the same number of branches, and then combine them into a single fabric.

For a flower, tie a chain of ten air loops and connect into a ring using a connecting post. Tie this ring with twenty-five single crochet, then around this center tie eight petals.



For a branch, draw a chain of air loops about ten centimeters long, knit one single crochet into each loop. In the process of tying, make branches for the petals, and knit them in accordance with the scheme given in the article.

Before attaching to a T-shirt, all motives should be ironed to give them an even shape.



Bandeau is a good basis for a model of different types of lace

You can very quickly tie in a circle a kind of bando, consisting of only air loops and single crochets. To do this, a chain of air loops is knitted, equal to the length of the circumference of the chest and a multiple of five plus three loops. Locked into the ring using a connecting post. Next, five air loops, a single crochet in the fifth loop, again five air loops, a single crochet in the fifth loop, etc., to the end of the first circular row. Attach the last chain to the third loop of the first arch. Next, knit five loops and one single crochet in the third loop of the arch, again five loops, etc. Knit until the bando becomes the height you want from the waist to the armpits. In the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe chest on the front, several arches should be made of six loops, then again knit five loops. This achieves the required volume in the right places. In the lower part from the waist to the hips, an increase in loops in the arches is also made.



Made in this way, you will get an adjacent silhouette of a crochet shirt for women with a well-defined chest shape and accented waist. However, for very full women, this option is not suitable. Crochet shirt for girls can be crocheted without increases. In any case, it will turn out quite elastic. On this mesh basis it is very convenient to sew Irish lace motifs. The resulting bando must be worn on a mannequin, placed motifs and sewn. The top of the shirt should be tied with one row of columns with pico. In addition, you need to tie two straps in columns with a crochet and fasten on the front and back. On the bottom edge you can snap a beautiful border.

Feature of choosing a model for a child

A crochet shirt for a preschool girl should be crocheted of cotton threads. It will be especially beautiful from Iris. These threads have a very beautiful sheen, do not sit and do not fade. You can safely take multi-colored skeins and knit from them. If the Irish technique seems complicated, then try to make an outfit of square motifs. Crochet tops and t-shirts made in this technique are also very good. The same round or polygonal motifs connected in series with each other create very interesting patterns.
