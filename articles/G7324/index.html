Which is better: installment plan or loan

Who can be provided with installments

If we talk about the conditions and registration of installments in the Euroset, then they are standard. The borrower must have a civil passport in his hands, which will act as the main identity document. You will also need a driver‚Äôs license, pension certificate, passport or SNILS. However, if cooperation is planned with Post Bank, then a second document is not needed, just a passport.

Another condition is that the client of a banking institution must be registered in the Russian Federation. If we are talking about Home Credit Bank, then the borrower must live in the region where the bank branch is located.

Installments are granted only to those who have reached the age of majority. Get a loan does not work for people over 70 years. Also, most banks require confirmation of their solvency. To do this, you must provide a work book with the appropriate entry.

At the conclusion of the contract, you must specify the correct data. If necessary, a bank employee should be able to contact the borrower. In some financial institutions, a prerequisite is the provision of a telephone number for relatives or other persons who can be called by representatives of a financial institution if the client does not pick up the phone.

Experience and income

Not all banking institutions are interested in this. Basically, loans are small, so the conditions are not as stringent as when obtaining large loans. Nevertheless, in order to draw up an agreement with Home Credit Bank, you must confirm that the client has been working in one place for more than 3 months. At the same time, the borrower's income must be at least 6,000 rubles (in some cases, this indicator can be increased to 10,000 rubles).

Other organizations are not so demanding. This is another plus installment plan.

It's important to know

There are several recommendations that can be called general for any transactions with financial institutions. For example, you need to understand that an employee of a mobile phone salon only deals with filling out an application to the bank. If the manager of a financial organization changes the terms of the transaction and raises the annual rate, he must notify the borrower. Only after all the conditions are agreed, you can sign the contract.

If the client decides to pay the full amount ahead of time or, conversely, delays in payments, the store discount is also recounted. This is important to take into account so as not to be surprised at large overpayments, etc.

Reviews about installments of "Euroset" on phones and conditions

Basically, customers are satisfied with the possibility that the goods can be purchased on favorable terms and not save the entire amount for the purchase of an expensive phone. This is convenient, and the interest is not so high. Also, many are advised to immediately include in installments additional accessories. This will not greatly increase the purchase price and save you from future costs.

If we talk about the minuses, then, as a rule, the discontent is caused only by the fact that many banks offer similar services only to certain models of phones or other equipment. Also, when drawing up a contract, many advise to clarify whether insurance products are included in it. It is better to refuse them so that the final amount of the overpayment does not increase. Since the contracts are standard, similar services are included in them by default. However, this does not mean at all that they cannot be excluded.

In all other respects, mobile phone shop customers are satisfied with such acquisitions. Monthly payments allow you to make a purchase not so significant for the budget. Therefore, the financial condition of customers is not much affected.

Conclusion

When signing any loan agreement, you need to carefully study all its conditions and each item separately. Sometimes additional options are indicated in the documents, which, if desired, can be disabled. There is no point in overpaying for unnecessary services. For example, when buying a phone there is absolutely no need for additional insurance. It is beneficial only with large loans.
