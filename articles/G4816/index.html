Phu Hai Resort 4 * (Phan Thiet, Vietnam): hotel infrastructure, room description, service, reviews

Phan Thiet is a fairly young resort, but it has already managed to gain fame as the capital of surfers in Vietnam. However, not only athletes come here, but for the most part lovers of a relaxing beach holiday. Space at the resort is enough for everyone. Phan Thiet stretched for more than forty kilometers along the coast of the South China Sea. So, the city has taken over the surrounding villages, among which the most famous is Mui Ne.

It annually hosts international surfing competitions. Every tour operator will say that daredevils who ride on the crest of a wave on the boards, and lovers to soak up the sand and swim in the calm sea, in principle, do not meet in one resort. But Phan Thiet is unique in that it provides all categories of tourists with a quality (in their understanding) rest. In this article we will consider the hotel Phu Hai Resort 4 *. Where it is located, what are its rooms, infrastructure and service - read below. Our description was built on the reviews of tourists recently vacationing in a hotel.

When to go to Phan Thiet

This resort is located in South Vietnam, and therefore its climate is tropical. But do not indiscriminately argue that the high tourist season in Phan Thiet falls in the winter months, and showers take place in the summer. Rather, all this is so. But a clear sky does not always coincide with the concept of "a good time for a beach holiday." For example, in February the weather in Phan Thiet is clear and dry. For the whole month, not a single rain passes, and days with variable cloud cover a maximum of seven. What is not a good season for beach people? But there is a nuance.

If you plan to swim exclusively in the pool, then the winter in Phan Thiet is completely suitable for you. For it is then that strong winds blow here. On the one hand, they disperse the terrible heat and reduce air humidity. But on the other hand, serious waves surge the winds. These are the same regular shafts that made the capital of surfing the resort. The weather in Phan Thiet in February is the windiest in the year. But in the rainy season the sea is completely calm.

Of course, going to the tropics in the summer is like playing Russian roulette: it can be a completely clear week with one night rain, or it can pour all the time without stopping. Experienced travelers are advised to come to Phan Thiet in the off-season: the end of March, April, October and November are the best periods for a beach holiday.

Territory

Phu Hai Resort 4 * (Phan Thiet, Vietnam) was built in 2001, the last renovation took place in 2017. By the way, repairs at the hotel take place regularly, and a new one is planned for the rainy season (summer of 2019). The territory of the hotel is huge - 30 thousand square meters. Closer to the road is a reception, restaurant and other similar establishments. At the end, away from the sea, there are four 2-4-story buildings. They are mainly rooms of a lower price category, but there are also suites.

Closer to the sea, among the coconut palms and tropical trees, 38 one-storey villas are scattered. The buildings are built in the style of European Art Nouveau. But the bungalows on the exterior resemble traditional Vietnamese huts. But only outwardly! Inside they impress with modern comfort. If the hotel is not 100 percent full, it is possible to upgrade the rooms in the building to the villa. The territory is very beautiful, it is carefully cleaned. At certain times, the bushes are treated for insects, so there are no mosquitoes in the hotel.

Phu Hai Resort 4 *: description of rooms in the buildings

The cheapest room category at Phu High Resort is called deluxe rooms. These rooms are located in two old buildings, and the balcony (or terrace, if this is the first floor) opens onto the garden. The deluxe suites have everything you need for a quality stay: air conditioning, TV, minibar, hairdryer, electric kettle with daily replenished bags of drinks. After a day, the maids bring a fruit basket, and they clean there every day.

Premium rooms have a large area and a safe. Studio is a luxurious and spacious apartment with a living area in the bedroom. Grand deluxe rooms are located in the new building. Depending on the landscape that opens from the balconies, they are divided into garden view and rooms with ocean views.

Villa Overview

One- and two-story bungalows stand apart from each other, creating the illusion of privacy. They are completely surrounded by greenery. Those houses that are further away from the sea have balconies and terraces overlooking the garden. They consist of a bedroom and a separate living room, and the bathroom is partially open-air. The larger bungalow has a room with two bedrooms, a living room and 2 bathrooms. A huge terrace with sun loungers adjoins them.

The hotel Phu Hai Resort 4 * (Phan Thiet, Vietnam) has bungalows standing right on the edge of the beach. Valued such rooms are more expensive. They are also designed to accommodate two, three (with one bedroom) or four guests (with two). The situation in these villas is more chic than in the rooms.
