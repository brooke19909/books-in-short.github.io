What are two-recorders



When choosing a car, most people in the last turn pay attention to the presence in it of a particular audio system. This is due primarily to the fact that this parameter is far from the most important when choosing a modern and high-quality vehicle. However, after some time, for each car owner, it becomes relevant to choose the right radio for your car. And in this case, an excellent solution, according to most experts, are two-recorders, or just 2din.

The main advantages of dual-tape recorders

If we talk about the main advantages of this particular type of electronic device, then for a start it should be said that it does not violate the design and appearance of the front panel and can fit perfectly into the overall style of any car.





In addition, it is worth noting the high functionality that such radio receivers possess. Dual-model, for example, can play a wide variety of file types from a variety of media. They also support Bluetooth technology and, as a rule, are equipped with an LCD touch screen and a built-in TV tuner. Among other things, there are always two-radios with navigation on sale. Such a device will add extra comfort while operating the machine and save time. In addition, a two-way radio with navigator makes it easy to avoid traffic jams.

Types of two-twin radio tape recorders

To date, on sale you can find two main types of this electronic device - these are universal and standard models. The latter are made for specific cars and are installed in them at the stage of assembly of the car. The installation of a two-type universal type radio, as a rule, occurs after purchase, in the process of direct operation. At the same time, this equipment can be built into absolutely any car in which two adjacent slots will be free on the dashboard.





Choosing a two-track radio

When deciding to independently purchase and install this type of electronic device, you must responsibly approach the selection process and pay special attention to several important characteristics. Firstly, it should be remembered that two-twin radios are with a permanent or removable panel. To protect against theft, of course, the latter are more suitable. Secondly, before buying a radio, you must first check with the seller for a list of the audio and video formats that it supports. Thirdly, you need to pay special attention to the type of screen. It is desirable that this was an active TFT-screen, which at any angle can provide the driver with the best possible visibility. Fourth, you should definitely check for the presence of various input and output connectors, as they may differ for different device models. And finally, fifthly, when choosing a car radio, much attention needs to be paid to the manufacturer and the place of production.
