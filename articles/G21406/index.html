Aegean Park Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Marmaris): description of rooms, service, reviews

Turkey is a favorite place for a mass visa-free tourist pilgrimage. The holiday season in 2017 was marked by the replenishment of the Turkish coast with a new portion of bored Russian travelers. The largest concentration of Slavic guests in Turkish resorts is observed precisely on the Antalya coast. But not everyone knows that there is another Turkey - interesting, sober, friendly and even relatively honest. Those who want to relax inexpensively, not bothering with ‚Äúvisa issues‚Äù, avoiding uncultured tourists and Turkish pathos, should pay attention to the western resorts of Turkey.

Aegean coast - another Turkey

This resort area is radically different from the Mediterranean coast. Holidaymakers in Bodrum, Marmaris or Fethiye may seem to be somewhere in Corfu, Crete or the south of Greece. In the western resorts of Turkey you can perfectly relax in a European, cultural, friendship with sports, with a rich excursion program, while not neglecting all the advantages of this country as a budget and visa-free destination.

The climate in the west is softer, drier and windier. It is nice to relax here even in July, when the heat on the Antalya coast becomes unbearable. However, in September-October, the Aegean Sea becomes cold, not too comfortable for swimming.

Cognitive tourism in western Turkey is developing, perhaps, even more intensively than classic tourism. Few novice travelers are aware that approximately 80% of all ancient monuments are not in Greece, but in the Turkish West. But Europeans have long appreciated the local excursion.

Marmaris is the most European and youth among Turkish resorts. Only a few of our compatriots prefer it to the Mediterranean resorts of the same Turkey. But these guys know a lot about good rest. Like the hundreds of thousands of British, Germans or Dutch who occupy Marmaris in the summer.

The city is popular for cycling, trips to the lakes, sightseeing trips, there is something to diversify the standard vacation program, consisting of "all inclusive", shopping and the sea.

What interesting things can you see in the vicinity of Marmaris?

In Marmaris itself there are a lot of attractions with great cultural value, but the resort is rich not only with historical monuments, there are many natural beauties: lakes, mineral springs, coniferous forests, mountains.

The city fortress and adjacent architectural complex is one of the most visited places in the resort. The complex was built by order of Suleiman the Magnificent in 1522.

16 km from Marmaris is the island of Sedir, or the island of Cleopatra, where, they say, the queen herself took baths. In addition to the baths, the island is also notable for the preserved ruins of the ancient theater and city walls.

From natural popular places, guidebooks advise visiting Gyokova Bay, Lake Kecheiz or the town of Datca. All are within 30 km of Marmaris.

Shopping in Marmaris can also be very interesting. The city also has shopping centers with mass-market stores, but the prices for goods there are too high, and the choice is inferior to the assortment in our shopping centers. But in the colorful oriental markets or in small workshops you can buy good, unique things: bags, wallets, leather jackets. You just need to turn on the regime of mistrust and double-checking, because everyone knows about the inventiveness of Turkish traders in matters of deception, body kit or divorce.

Hotel beach

The beach at Aegean Park Hotel 3 * is municipal, it is 30 meters from the hotel. The shore is clean, sandy, the entrance to the sea is gentle. The beach is perfect for the debut acquaintance of kids with the sea elements. Of all the Turkish resorts, Marmaris is considered the most quiet, as it is located in the bay. The absence of high waves and strong currents are well suited for swimming inexperienced swimmers.



Sun beds, umbrellas on the beach are paid. Even so, they are conditionally paid. There is a trick: if you buy drinks in any of the bars on the beach, then the loungers near this bar automatically become free for a valuable customer of the institution.

There are no beach towels at the hotel, neither paid, nor deposit, nor free. You need to bring your own or buy in the city.

Hotel service: paid and free services

For entertainment, the hotel has a lounge for watching TV, table football. There is no animation, but it, as a rule, is not much practiced in the resorts of western Turkey.

Free Wi-Fi is available at the reception. Safe at the reception is provided for a fee.

The hotel also has a free shuttle service to Bar Street - this wonderful street is parallel to the promenade. Many restaurants and night clubs are open here. On foot from the hotel you can walk to it in 20 minutes.

Services for children

Children's service in Aegean Park Hotel 3 * is not as elaborate as in Belek or Kemer hotels. The very orientation of the hotel - budget, youth, noisy, in the center of the resort city, does not oblige to draw up an impeccable list of children's services. But, since the budget cost of living in a hotel also attracts families with children, the hotel has created the minimum conditions for recreation for small tourists.

The restaurant has children's positions in the general menu, there are highchairs for feeding. The complex has a large children's pool and 2 water slides, a children's playground. A cot is available on request.

Marmaris beach is also ideal for children, it is sandy, flat, there is no pebble, as in Kemer or Alanya. The entrance to the sea is shallow, the waves are calm. The beach has a variety of infrastructure, including for children.

However, the proximity of Barov Street, the concentration of nightclubs full of European youth, the atmosphere of noise and courage sometimes complicate vacations with young children.
