Club Ege Antique 4 * - quite a good hotel

Nature has very generously endowed this Turkish resort, located on the southwestern coast of the Aegean Sea. The mild climate of Marmaris, its clear sea and picturesque bays - all this made the city quite popular. Today it is a prestigious resort with numerous hotels, the price range for accommodation in which can suit tourists with any size of wallets.

Holidays in Marmaris

Many people choose this particular Turkish region for their holidays, as club life is constantly in full swing there. Resting in these places, it is difficult to realize that you are in the territory of an eastern country. The resort is open to all, and the locals are so friendly that sometimes you feel like at home. In addition, there is no need to think about observing special rules of conduct; you don‚Äôt have to worry about wearing too open clothes.

From Marmaris, considered a pearl on the Aegean coast, it is very convenient to make sea excursions along the entire Turkish coast, swim to Rhodes or further to Turtle Islands. The resort has now become the center for sailing in the region.

Marmaris hotels, located mainly in the city, have a small adjacent territory. Five and four star apartments with a wide infrastructure are usually located on the first line. In addition to them, in the city recently built many small apart-hotels designed for budget holidays. Club Ege Antique 4 * (Marmaris) is no exception.

general information

It is located in Armutalar, two kilometers from the resort center and one from the beach. Club Ege Antique 4 *, the photo of the building of which indicates that the pink-brick colors of the main building contrast perfectly with the blue pool and greenery on the territory, was built relatively recently - in 2011. Two years later, it was partially renovated, and today the hotel receives its guests in well-equipped rooms.

Less than one hundred and fifty meters from it is a public transport stop. The airport is ninety kilometers away.

Infrastructure

Club Ege Antique 4 * offers a relatively wide range of services. On its territory there are two ATMs; there is a point for currency exchange. It offers residents - laundry, ironing, parking (no preliminary reservation is required), dry cleaning. Here, without going outside the hotel, you can buy souvenirs.

At the registration desk, which operates around the clock, check-in is carried out very quickly. You can immediately receive or send a fax, make photocopies of documents. Guests can use the luggage storage room or rent a safe deposit box. There is also the opportunity to get expedited registration.

Housing stock



There are two elevators in the hotel building. In total, Club Ege Antique 4 * (Marmaris) offers for living one hundred and sixty standard rooms, family rooms and apartments with one or two bedrooms. Modern furniture, a flat-screen TV, a kitchen area with everything you need, a paid minibar with two bottles of water on the day of arrival - all this is provided so that tourists choosing this hotel to stay are comfortable. In front of the rooms there are small balconies with a plastic table and chairs overlooking the patio or pool. Sitting here, you can drink coffee and enjoy the surrounding nature, which in Marmaris is simply amazing.


On the floor - ceramic tiles. All rooms are air-conditioned with split systems. Club Ege Antique 4 * provides rooms for the disabled. All public areas, including the territory, are equipped with ramps for wheelchair access.



In combined bathrooms - new plumbing, there are showers, a large makeup mirror, hairdryer. All hygiene products are updated daily. Room cleaning is done with the same regularity. Bed and towels are changed every other day. Pets are not allowed in this hotel. There are smoking rooms located in one wing of the building.

Nutrition

Hotel guests can choose the half-board concept. There is also the possibility of receiving the service "breakfast in the room." Buffet is offered in the main restaurant, which is open √† la carte. It is possible to order diet meals for children. In addition to the restaurant, on the territory of Club Ege Antique 4 * there are barbecue cafes, snack and lobby bars, a pool bar .

Entertainment

The services of those who for some reason do not want to go to the beach include indoor and outdoor pools (the latter with slides), a park area. There is also the opportunity to play billiards or darts, visit the fitness center, Turkish hammam or sauna. On the territory of Club Ege Antique 4 * there is animation, karaoke. Those who prefer outdoor walks will enjoy the park area with benches. The beach is one kilometer from the hotel. You can get to it by shuttle, departing three times a day. Sun beds, umbrellas and trestle beds on the beach are paid.



For children

An office in the outdoor pool, slides, a games room, a chair in the restaurant, a menu - this is an incomplete list of all the services that the hotel offers for younger guests. There is also a convenient small playground and the possibility of inviting babysitting children.

Hotel Policies

Check-in at the hotel is from fourteen to twenty-four hours, check-out is at noon. In addition, according to the rules, for placing a child over twelve years old or an adult in an extra bed, each person must pay fifteen euros per night. The maximum number of beds in a room is two, in addition to the existing ones. Air conditioning and safety deposit boxes are available at an additional cost.

Impressions

Club Ege Antique 4 * (Marmaris), reviews of which for the most part are only positive, are not yet very popular among Russians. However, those who were already lucky enough to stay in it are quite happy with the holidays spent in it. Our compatriots were satisfied with everything: the rooms, which are quite spacious, and the staff work - unobtrusive and neat. A lot of good impressions were left of the food, which is quite diverse and, most importantly, tasty.



The location of the Club Ege Antique 4 * hotel was chosen quite well. It can be an excellent starting point for independent study of local attractions, which are many in Marmaris. In a word, it presents a good combination of price and quality of services, so many people express a desire to return here again.
