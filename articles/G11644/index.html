Water Park "Fantasy" in Maryino. Waterpark in Maryino: prices, opening hours

The Fantasy water park in Moscow is one of the best water entertainment complexes with fun games, entertaining animators and educators. For several years now, Muscovites and visitors have been enjoying an unforgettable pastime at the Fantasy Park Family Recreation Center. It is located in the Maryino area.



The CSO building covers an area of ​​six thousand square meters, here you can find entertainment for every taste for both children and adults. In the center there are: a water park, children's entertainment areas, bars, restaurants, bowling and other diverse entertainment.

Fiction of the water world in the water park

Fantasy Waterpark in Maryino will give its guests an unforgettable vacation, dip in the hot, sunny summer. Modern infrastructure provides multifaceted activities and games. Water rides, slides, pools will bring extreme sensations, make you forget about time. Having enjoyed all the entertainment, you will leave with positive emotions that will not leave you for a long time.

The Fantasy water park in Moscow is a place not only for family leisure, but also a popular institution for holding children's parties and corporate parties. It is also suitable for organizing a student party. Fans of family vacations will be amazed by the variety and original combination of children's and adult entertainments - there will be no time to be bored here. If you have a party with friends, and there are children at it, then the animators will definitely figure out what to do with the children while their parents relax.

The Fantasy water park in Maryino is the personification of our world with its continents. In one building fit: classical Europe, breathtaking Africa, unusual America and the refreshing, dazzling Antarctica.



Continental Travel

The first place you will visit when visiting the complex is the restaurant "Europe", which has occupied the lower floor of the building. The hall of the institution is designed for a holiday of any format, whether it is a banquet, corporate party, birthday or a buffet table.

Then unbridled Africa will replace classical Europe - this is the mainland for children. Here you will be surrounded by jungle, colored walls, palm trees, parrots and creepers. This is a fabulous world of children's attractions and entertainment.

The atmosphere of the West will sweep you on the third floor. Here you will be met by the entertainment of modern cities. In the "America" ​​hall you can play billiards, bowling or pool.

Cold Antarctica is represented by a concert hall, which, if necessary, can serve as a conference hall or a night club. This continent is intended for organizing holidays, concerts, New Year's festivities, master classes or graduation parties.



Rides

People who have already visited the Fantasy water park leave only positive reviews. Five the coolest and most interesting slides are located in the Central Military District, the length of which ranges from twenty two to eighty five meters, each descent has its own exotic name. On the Tabogan you will be offered to ride on cylinders on a green open slide, on the orange Barracuda you will have to go down in a closed figure eight.

You can recall your childhood and take a ride on a straight slide with the usual slope at Fantasy, the most indescribable extreme sensations you will get at Kamikaze - the name speaks for itself. The faint-hearted have nothing to do here - this is the shortest sheer descent. Fans of non-standard solutions and complex tasks will like the “Sea slope” slide.

If you decide to take a break after active entertainment, you can do it in the Bul-Bul or Jacuzzi pool, enjoy the marine energy and mentally find yourself on the seashore with the help of the Volnovaya pool. At this time, your child will have a great time in the “Frog” - this pool is designed for young visitors and will give them unforgettable emotions.





You can gain strength and refresh yourself on the deck of the Calypso vessel, right away you will be offered various drinks.

Holidays for every taste

Fantasy water park in Maryino is beautifully and modernly furnished. All attractions are located so as not to obstruct the view, there is a lot of greenery around, and under your feet there is a nice cover. The water temperature is comfortable for swimming, the water in the pools is clean.

If you are tired of outdoor activities, crazy descents, you can safely swim or, for example, visit a special pool with fish that will make a pleasant peeling. Beginners can seek help from friendly and attractive instructors who are always happy to help. In addition, you can use the services of a photographer and capture the most interesting and funny shots of your leisure.

Cost of services and promotions

How much money do you need to go with you when going to the Fantasy water park? Prices here are very diverse, it all depends on the day of visit. Adults for their vacation will give eight hundred and fifty rubles, and children's entertainment will cost from five hundred and fifty rubles to one thousand one hundred. Holders of a Muscovite social card have a discount on their time at the water park.





The complex is open daily from eleven to twenty-three hours. You can have fun on the water for two hours or a whole day (depending on the cost of the ticket). Toddlers up to four years old are free of charge, children from five to thirteen years old pay a children's fare and enter only accompanied by adults. With teenagers the situation is ambiguous. They are held either with adults, or with a note from mom and dad. But, of course, they can write it themselves, so recently this option is not suitable. Parents confirm their agreement at the entrance.

There is a special offer for students on weekdays : groups of ten people participate in three programs: a stay at the water park, a game of bowling or billiards and a lunch consisting of pizza and Coca-Cola.

Discount coupon

In "Fantasy" (water park) coupon can be ordered on the discount website. This will allow you to purchase tickets at lower prices. In Fantasy (water park), the coupon gives you the opportunity not only to save money, but also to leisurely enjoy your vacation.

A pleasant gift for visitors is a certificate for a birthday person to visit ten attractions. Such an action is valid on weekdays and makes it possible for an adult to feel like a child and return to the fabulous world of childhood.



How to get there

Water Park "Fantasy" address has this: st. Lublin, 100. By metro you can get to the station "Maryino" Lublin-Dmitrov line. By bus or minibus - to the stops "st. Pererva, 50 ”or“ Protection Center ”. The water park is open daily from 11-00 to 23-00.
