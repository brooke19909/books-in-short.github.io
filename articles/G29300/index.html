4 star Cyprus hotels: reviews

Cyprus hotels use in their classification a standard five-point system for determining the level of service by "stars". Since the island is considered exclusively as a holiday destination, it is difficult to find hostels and hotels with one star. All hotels in seaside resorts start from a "two stars". And what is above five? There are also boutique and apart hotels. Of course, the "stardom" of a hotel depends not only and not so much on the level of service. Here, factors such as location, distance from the sea, the presence and quality of the beach play a role. In this article we will look at the best 4-star hotels in Cyprus. We made a selection using reviews from Russian tourists. They rated hotels by such parameters as territory and location, distance from the beach, service, food and animation.

It may seem strange to those tourists who are used to vacationing in Egypt or Turkey that almost all 4-star hotels in Cyprus do not practice the all-inclusive system. Resort Selection

Going to go to Cyprus, does not interfere with determining the place of rest. Many resorts of the island have their own "specialization." Golden youths usually choose Ayia Napa and Limassol. Local 4-star Cyprus hotels invariably collect the most enthusiastic reviews of animation, entertainment and excellent service. But along with this, some tourists complain of noise and din in the evenings. Lovers of languid bliss settle in the vicinity of Limassol and in Paphos. Reviews mention a high level of comfort. The most budget hotels of the island are concentrated in Larnaca. And families with young children praise hotels in Protaras. The infrastructure for children is most developed there: there are special menus, animation, water parks and more.

Coast selection

A characteristic feature of Cyprus is that the beaches here are municipal. This means that access to them is absolutely free for everyone. This circumstance can be regarded as a minus (for sunbeds and umbrellas you may be required to pay), and a plus (the ability to choose a beach to your liking). But some 4-star Cyprus hotels conclude agreements with the municipality on the provision of beach ammunition to their customers. At the same time, sun loungers and umbrellas by the pools at the hotels are always free for guests. The best beaches in Cyprus are in Protoras and Ayia Napa. Here is a little white and soft sand, with insignificant interspersed pebbles. This fact makes resorts very popular. Hotels are advised to book rooms in advance. In Larnaca, the sand is dark in color. Pebbles are increasingly found in Limassol. But in Paphos, bulk sand, and in the sea at sunset there are stones.

Protaras, whose 4-star hotels are not very numerous, presents a rich animation program for children and their parents. Some hotels, such as Capo Bay, have a diving school and spa.
