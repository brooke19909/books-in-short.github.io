Hotel Splendid Conference Spa Resort 5 * (Montenegro / Budvanska Riviera): photos and reviews

Many people choose Montenegro for their holidays. This is a small state with a lot of attractions, located on the Adriatic coast.

There are a large number of resort towns and, accordingly, hotels in which you can stay during your stay. Hotel Splendid Conference Spa Resort is a 5-star complex, which is rightfully one of the best. And not without reason, there are many reasons for this.





Location

People, deciding which hotel they will live in during their vacation, first of all pay attention to where the hotel complex is located. After all, it is very important that it is located in a convenient place. Hotel Splendid Conference Spa Resort 5 * is in a resort place called Becici. It is located right on the shores of the Adriatic Sea. Literally 20 meters separates the hotel residents from the pebble beach.

Interestingly, the hotel, which was opened in 2006, became the first 5-star European-class complex on the entire east coast of the Adriatic Sea.

Becici is a major tourist center in Montenegro, despite the fact that only 800 local people live here. The main attraction of this place is a long sandy beach. It reaches 1950 meters! And this place is one of the most picturesque in the entire southern Adriatic. He in 1935 in Paris received the award as the most beautiful beach in all of Europe.

By the way, from the hotel you can literally drive to Budva in 5 minutes - there is a taxi and public transport from the complex.

For relax

The Hotel Splendid Conference Spa Resort 5 * has everything that guests may need for their relaxation. This is an outdoor seasonal pool, two terraces (one of which is for sunbathing), a picturesque garden. Towels are available for a beach holiday. By the way, there is another pool - with heating.

Also in the Hotel Splendid Conference Spa Resort 5 * there is a billiard room, table tennis, Turkish bath, library, jacuzzi and massage room. There is an indoor swimming pool and darts. If people come to rest with their children, then they may not worry that they will be bored - for the youngest guests, animation staff speaks day and night. In addition, the hotel has a separate kids club.

In addition to all of the above, the Splendid Conference Spa Resort Hotel 5 * has a SPA and a wellness center, as well as a casino, a solarium, a sauna, a tennis court and a games room. In general, everyone staying here on vacation will find entertainment to their taste.





Bars and Restaurants

In the Splendid Conference Spa Resort Hotel 5 * there are several presentable establishments in which guests can enjoy breakfast, lunch, dinner, or just a snack. The main restaurant is known as La BUSSOLA. It is designed for 550-600 people. In addition to the main room, there is also a terrace. The restaurant serves visitors buffet style. And there is often a demonstration of cooking.

There is also a gourmet restaurant located on the tenth floor of the hotel. It offers stunning panoramic views of the surroundings. The room is designed for 80 visitors.

Gourmets who love to sample local cuisine will definitely like PROMENADA Beach Restaurant. Not only national and Mediterranean treats are prepared here. In this institution you can taste the Oriental and Japanese cuisine. And here is a huge selection of spirits and wines.

The hotel has numerous bars. BRACERA Pool Bar is designed for 80-100 seats. Inside, the cozy atmosphere of the winter garden reigns, and still live music constantly sounds there. There is also a lobby bar, working around the clock. Many will like the elite wine salon, designed for 50-70 visitors. The conference room also has a bar. It is designed for 170 seats.

Even the Wellness Center has a fitness bar with healthy drinks. And finally, the last establishment is a bar located on the terrace, which offers a picturesque view of the sea.





Services

The Splendid Conference Spa Resort Hotel 5 * hotel has all the amenities that should be provided to guests for a comfortable stay. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the entire complex, and a private parking spot is also available for visitors.

The reception is open around the clock - it is possible to check in and check out individually. Concierge and tour desk services are also available. If you need to leave your luggage somewhere, then for this there is a special left-luggage office. By the way, there is also a cash machine on site, which is very convenient.

Family services include the presence of a playground, indoor play area, music, films, books and other entertainment for the smallest. Even a personal nanny can be hired here.

Service

Splendid Conference Spa Resort Hotel 5 * gets extremely positive reviews. What should not be surprising, because this hotel has everything that is needed for comfort. It includes daily housekeeping, laundry service, dry cleaning, ironing. Even shoe shine is offered.

What else is there in the hotel?

Splendid Conference Spa Resort Hotel 5 * (Becici) has many other amenities. If guests wish, then a shuttle service can be arranged. Both from and to the airport. True, for a fee. The distance from Becici to the airport in Tivat is about 35 kilometers.

The entire hotel is non-smoking. But there are separate places where this is allowed. Also, if guests wish, they can offer non-smoking rooms.

The hotel has various shops, which is very convenient - no need to leave the hotel to make purchases. A souvenir stall is also available. Another guests of Splendid Conference Spa Resort Hotel 5 * (Montenegro) are offered car rental.

In addition to all the above, guests can enjoy family rooms, honeymoon apartments, packed lunches with you and room service, wake-up service, the ability to connect heating and even amenities for people with disabilities. There is even a beauty salon with a hairdresser at the hotel. And for access to the upper floors there is an elevator. In general, as you can see, the Hotel Splendid Conference Spa Resort (Montenegro), the photos of which are presented above, really has on its territory everything that can come in handy.

SPA

The name of the hotel clearly hints that the complex has a spa area. And indeed it is. Splendid SPA pleases visitors with air filled with incense, warm water, exotic treatments, excellent service and a unique atmosphere. By the way, the salon is open not only for hotel guests, but also for ordinary visitors.

Interestingly, many SPA procedures use local plants. In general, here you can devote time to anything. There is a cosmetic store with Elemis products, an epilation salon, a steam bath, spa pedicures and manicures, a private sauna, Chromaroma (a holistic procedure based on color and aromatherapy combined with manual massage), body wraps and much more.

The SPA zone also has a medium temperature, Finnish and coniferous biosauna, indoor pools, a traditional stone bath. You can also visit the snow and emotional showers, an ice fountain or hammam.

In general, while staying in this place, you should definitely allocate time for SPA. An unforgettable experience will remain.
