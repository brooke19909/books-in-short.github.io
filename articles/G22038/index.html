"Joule Park" in Omsk: description, entertainment program, reviews

All parents are interested in having their child's vacation organized properly. It is important that the entertainment is not only exciting, but also developing. A center was opened several years ago in the city of Omsk, where not only children, but also parents, grandparents, can relax and have a good rest. We will talk about the park in the sections of the article.

What is the "Joule Park" in Omsk?

Joule Park is an entertainment center where children can learn a lot of new and interesting things about science. They take part in experiments and even create animated films themselves. Many parents and teachers choose this entertainment park for celebrations, as it is here that children can spend time in an exciting, interesting and informative way.

Wonder Museum "Joule Park" in Omsk has a wide variety of exhibits.



There are several halls, walking along which you can see many interesting and exciting things.

Museum premises

Visitors to the institution will need about two hours to see all the rooms of the entertainment center.





The museum "Joule Park" in Omsk consists of several rooms, for example:

  1. Interactive exhibition room. The exhibits presented here help to know in practice some patterns of physical phenomena.
  2. Room of optical illusions. In this room you can see how curved mirrors modify the human body.
  3. A room devoid of gravity. Here you can feel yourself in zero gravity.
  4. Labyrinth of mirrors. Allows children and adults to plunge into the atmosphere of a fairy tale.

In addition, the entertainment center conducts exciting activities, such as chemical experiments. Few people know what dry ice is and what can be done with this substance. Employees of the "Joule Park" are ready to demonstrate to visitors the various wonders of science associated with this phenomenon.

Experiments and show programs

The entertainment center "Joule Park" in Omsk offers children and adults fascinating performances. One of them is a show program, the main characters of which are Fire and Ice. Other experiments will allow visitors to see with their own eyes the change of day and night, to try themselves in the role of a chemical scientist mixing different substances, to observe a volcanic eruption, thunder and lightning. Young guests of the center can learn a lot of interesting things about the human body, for example, about its main enemies, dangerous microbes, and the fight against them. Children are given the opportunity to try delicious ice cream, cocktails and other sweets.

The show program of the entertainment center is suitable not only for the child, but also for the adult. It can become part of the celebration - weddings, corporate events, anniversaries. The entertainment program must be discussed in advance with the employee of the institution. The park is located at K. Marx Street, house number 18. It works every day, from 10:00 to 20:00.

The cost of visiting the center is approximately 390 rubles, show programs cost guests 150-200 rubles.

Customer opinions about the institution

About the organization "Joule Park" in Omsk leave positive reviews. Visitors are satisfied with the work of the staff of the institution. Employees of the organization can provide a good mood and a fun holiday for both children and parents. A variety of entertainment programs, interesting and exciting experiments, cognitive experiments that anyone can put into practice, and which are similar to the real wonders of science and technology - all this makes visiting the center bright and unforgettable. Due to the fact that there are almost no such organizations as the "Joule Park" in Omsk, there are always a lot of people in the center, often there is a long queue. Another minus of the institution is a lot of noisy entertainment that can scare preschool children, so for the smallest it is better to choose other types of recreation.

conclusions

Joule Park in Omsk is one of the few entertainment centers for children and parents to relax together, where you can not only have fun, but also learn a lot of interesting things. Here the child is given a unique opportunity to participate in scientific experiments and see interesting experiments and phenomena with his own eyes. The facility is perfect for holding children's parties. Not only small visitors are happy with his work, but also many mothers and fathers.
