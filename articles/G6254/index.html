And what are the beaches on the island of Cyprus?

Cyprus is a country where there are not many attractions. Therefore, the main area of ‚Äã‚Äãrelaxation here is beach tourism. People from all over the world come here to swim and sunbathe on amazingly clean beaches in warm, crystal water. Cyprus constantly attracts tourists, and the streets of the cities of the island of Aphrodite are always crowded. It turns out that for travelers arriving in Cyprus, beaches are the most important component of relaxation.



There are approximately 90 beaches on the island, in addition, 52 of the total recently received evidence of good environmental conditions and water quality - the European Blue Flag.

By the way, it should be noted a very important fundamental feature of the Cypriot beaches - they are all municipal, which means that everyone can go to any of them and settle down for a vacation. Even if this is the territory of the hotel and it is fenced. The only thing is that the residents of the hotel most often do not pay for sunbeds and umbrellas, and outside visitors will have to pay two to three euros for the item.

On the island there are many shores, fenced off highway, where locals and many tourists also relax and sunbathe. But we will consider and discuss the best such places.



Most vacationers, buying a tour to the island, first get acquainted with the possible location options, because there is a map of the beaches of Cyprus, which shows even the most suitable places, as well as places that are dangerous for swimming. We will begin our journey: from the west of the island, moving slowly to the east. Let's look at the Akamas peninsula, which is not far from Paphos. Here, in a national park, there are sandy beaches untouched by tourists. 12 km from the city is Coral Bay - an excellent sandy beach. To the depths - far away, so this is a great place to relax with children.

Moving on. Petra tou Romiu - pebble beach, a small secluded place. Here, often, especially in the offseason or at night, couples in love are located.

Pissouri Beach - very warm and clear water, gentle beach, sand. There is a diving center, many bars and cafes.

Nissi - during the day, real Pelicans walk around the shore here, and in the evening local musicians sing and play. Macronius - the coast is constantly occupied by the hanging "golden youth".





Taking a tour along the entire coast, you get plenty of fun, swim and sunbathe, it's Cyprus! The beaches of Ayia Napa are considered the best on the whole island of Aphrodite. Nissi Beach, for example, is characterized by an unprecedented blue sea. What is more remarkable about Cyprus? White sand beaches - Sand Bay and Golden Sands located nearby are the best in all of Cyprus. They are his calling card.

The beaches in Protaras also boast of white sand: Flamingo Beach and Fig Tree Bay . Well, the largest, located in the west of Limassol, is called the Ladies' Mile. It is very popular among local residents - Cypriots.

In Larnaca, Finikoudes, Mackenzie and the recently opened beach of the Cyprus Tourism Organization are popular.

If you choose Cyprus for holidays, you will love the beaches. After all, the requirements for awarding the Blue Flag are being tightened every year, and the island withstands this test. By the number of awards, only Spain can compete with the island in Europe. The last beaches on the island of Cyprus that received such a flag are Finikoudes in Larnaca, Coral Bay in Paphos, Governors Beach at Limassol, Perner in Ayia Napa and others.
