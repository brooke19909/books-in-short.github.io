Today, St. Petersburg is being built up in accordance with the concept of integrated construction of houses, so developers are not offering single residential buildings on the market, but entire neighborhoods. One of such projects is the Novoorlovsky residential complex. It is located next to the forest park of the same name, not far from Suzdal Lakes, and it will occupy a rather impressive territory of 46 hectares.



About the complex

LCD "Novoorlovsky" (St. Petersburg) is characterized by a number of interesting features. First of all, the project is interesting in that its creators are the "daughter" of the Finnish construction company YIT - the Russian branch of Yit Dom. As for the architectural concept, the creators chose the neoclassical style, and the very appearance of the complex itself was developed in the Spanish bureau Taller de Arquitectura.

On the outer perimeter of the complex, multi-storey tower houses will be erected , the interior space will be occupied by a wide street, closed to traffic, with infrastructure facilities, and 7-storey buildings will be located in the middle zone. Those who are interested in the Novorlovskiy LCD address should go to the Suzdal highway and look for sections 1 and 12 there. Subsequently, upon completion of construction, numbers will be assigned to all buildings.

The complex is located almost at the exit from the city, and residents of Nizhniy Suzdal Lake can walk from the doorstep of the house in ten minutes. Life almost in nature, combined with the presence of all the benefits of civilization in the form of a developed social and commercial infrastructure of the Primorsky region, make the offer of the developer more than tempting. Do not lose sight of the Finnish quality of construction, which is also a definite plus. In total, about ten thousand apartments will be put up for sale.



LCD "Novoorlovsky": the progress of construction

As mentioned above, work began in 2013. A phased construction is expected. In parallel with the houses, objects of social and trade infrastructure will be built. First of all, the construction of six towers in 25 floors and four 7-storey houses, as well as schools and kindergartens (at this stage it is planned to put into operation three such institutions). In addition, the first floors will be given for commercial real estate, so the residents of the new building will be immediately provided with all the necessary services.

Houses will surrender in stages. The first phase of construction involves the delivery of two high-rise towers, in which there are 444 apartments. To date, no lag has been registered in terms of time, work has been mastered by 88%. Objects will be commissioned at the end of 2016.



As for the end of the construction term for the entire complex, this is 2019.

Ecology

Primorsky district is considered one of the most favorable regions of the Northern capital. There are no industrial production, but there are many forest zones. Large transportation lines are also located at a considerable distance from the complex, so that residents of the Novoorlovsky residential complex will not interfere with enjoying the peace and purity of the air. The proximity of the Lower Suzdal Lake, which has long been considered the favorite vacation spot of many citizens, is also an integral advantage in favor of the project.
