Kryvyi Rih - Moscow. How to get there

Despite the fact that Krivoy Rog and Moscow have been in different countries for more than twenty years, the flow of passengers between the two cities is not reduced.

Workers and tourists from Ukraine travel to the capital of the Russian Federation for further relocation to other regions.

Specialists come from Russia to carry out various tasks at the industrial facilities of the city.

In a straight line "Krivoy Rog - Moscow" the distance is 1100 km.

Before traveling, do not forget to bring your passport with you. The Ukrainian side has long banned the entry into its territory to citizens of the Russian Federation according to internal documents. The Russian side may at any time introduce response measures.

Before traveling, be sure to familiarize yourself with the existing standards for the transport of cash and baggage. Otherwise, there may be problems with customs authorities when crossing the border.

There are many ways to get from Krivoy Rog to the capital of the Russian Federation.

Railway transport

The most popular and affordable way to travel between cities is the railway.





The direct train No. 74 "Kryvyi Rih - Moscow" runs daily, which leaves daily at 10:52 from the station. "Kryvyi Rih" - "Main". Arrival at Kursky Station - at 11:43. Schedule should be clarified on the website of the Ukrainian railway. The train "Kryvyi Rih - Moscow" route passes through Nikopol, Zaporozhye, Kharkov, Kursk, Oryol, Tula.

Travel time is about twenty-four hours. The longest version of the presented.

Classes of cars - reserved seats and coupes. The minimum fare is about 5,000 rubles (1,500 hryvnias). If desired, you can pay for the bed and the required amount of tea at the railway ticket office.

Automobile transport

Distance Krivoy Rog - Moscow can be overcome by car.

Transport services are provided by many private companies that carry out transportation on an ongoing basis. Rolling stock - Mercedes Sprinter, Mercedes Vito. Most of the way in Ukraine passes along the highway of excellent quality Dnepropetrovsk - Kharkov. Through the territory of the Russian Federation, the route passes through Belgorod, Oryol, Tula.





Estimated travel time is about fifteen hours (depends on the speed of crossing the border between states).

There is no direct Kryvyi Rih - Moscow flight from the central bus station. You can get to the Russian capital with transfers in Kharkov, Dnepropetrovsk, Kiev, Zaporozhye. If you choose this route, the trip starts from twenty hours.

Using information from websites about travel companions on the Krivoy Rog - Moscow route, you can go as a passenger with a driver who needs to go to the capital of the Russian Federation, and at the same time he wants to save on fuel. The fare depends on your ability to negotiate. The rapidly developing service has already enabled many people to save significantly on travel.

The most expensive form of road transport is a taxi. The price also depends on the arrangement. In addition to fuel and depreciation, you will have to pay insurance for Russian territory, which Ukrainian drivers usually do not have. Count on less than 7500 rubles (2500 hryvnia) is not worth it.

Air traffic

A year ago, there was a direct flight "Kryvyi Rih - Moscow", which was carried out by the Russian company UTair. But due to unprofitability, it was closed. Until recently, it was possible to get to the Russian capital by air from Dnepropetrovsk, from where direct communication was opened. The flight time was less than one and a half hours.





At the moment, due to the ban on air traffic between the countries, it is possible to get to Moscow from all the nearest airports - Dnepropetrovsk, Zaporizhzhya, Kherson - using a transfer in a neutral state.

Conclusion

Getting from Krivoy Rog to Moscow is easy. It is enough to choose the right date, transport and do not forget to bring the necessary documents with you.
