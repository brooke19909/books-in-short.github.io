We knit a beautiful crochet for autumn and spring

Original hats, caps and hats crocheted not only warm their owners, but also make their appearance more attractive and elegant.

Openwork hats fit into any look, perfectly in harmony with any outerwear, whether it be a coat, jacket or cloak. In this article we will tell you how to crochet with knits for autumn and spring. Such a hat will not only protect your head from the weather, but also emphasize the beauty and attractiveness of its owner. Made of linen, cotton or half-woolen threads, this demi-season takes will change any image, make it more sensual, feminine and romantic.





Crochet: we will prepare a fashionable hat for autumn and spring

The manufacture of any beret must begin with taking measurements. The main one necessary for work is the circumference of the head. You can measure it by fixing a centimeter tape 2 cm above the eyebrow line, slightly above the ears and at the protruding point of the nape. After you take the measurement, you should prepare materials and tools. In order to crochet a knit for fall / spring, decide which type of headgear you want to make - lighter, openwork, voluminous, or, conversely, durable and warm, made with single crochet or crochet columns. As a rule, not too "lush" headgear will require about 200 - 250 g of yarn. You should also prepare a hook, scissors and a measuring tape.

Crochet beret technology

As a rule, all hats are made as follows. First knit "bottom". Then they begin to make increases until the beret has the required volume, and finally, they begin to make decreases, to the necessary head circumference. In addition, perform a single or double band. In the event that the pattern chosen for knitting a beret is particularly complex, the product is made in parts, and subsequently these fragments are combined. The magazines feature a wide variety of crochet beret patterns for fall. Here is one of them.

And we propose to knit a very feminine beret from white yarn. We advise novice needlewomen to relate to reading the scheme with special attention. It is convenient to draw it on paper and mark the already knitted rows with a pencil.

Simple crochet beret pattern for fall



So, to create this charming demi-season headdress, you will need white yarn 240 m / 50 g (100% cotton) and hook number 2. Knitting begins with six air loops and locking them into a ring. Then make three VP for lifting and perform 15 double crochet. Close the knitting with a connecting half-column. The second row begins with three lifting loops, perform 5 VP and one SN. The pattern is repeated seven more times and complete the row with a connecting loop. The third row begins to knit with three VP and two CH. Then perform the element: "two VP + 6 CH". repeat it 7 more times. Complete the series with two air loops, three double crochets and a connecting loop.

We continue to carry out a beautiful demi-season headdress

The following rows are knitted by analogy with the third, but increments of two CH in each "petal" are made. After the width of the double crochet element reaches 30 pieces, two rows are performed without increments. And then they begin to make decreases, gradually removing one CH in the "petals". Knitting is carried out until 12 CH is left in them. At the end, one row of double crochets is made. Knitting a beret ends with the production of a band (8 rows with single crochet). That's all, your gorgeous crochet picks for fall or spring is ready. Decorate it with a beautiful brooch, knitted flower, buttons or beads. The finish of the product may be what you like. Here you can show your imagination and decorate the product as you wish.
