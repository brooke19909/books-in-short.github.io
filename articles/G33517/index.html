The fastest way to get a credit card

Credit systems are in great demand today, as they allow you to quickly receive certain funds in debt. This is especially true when it comes to small amounts. As a rule, when obtaining credit cards, it is not necessary to provide a large list of documents, and the procedure itself takes a minimum of time. However, some banks still have higher requirements or issue approved QCs for too long. Consider the fastest ways to get a credit card loan at different banks.

Sberbank

Since this organization is the largest bank in Russia, the number of people who can count on receiving instant cards is limited. According to the current program, only people who already own accounts to which salaries or pensions are transferred can receive Credit Momentum. Therefore, obtaining a Sberbank credit card is very beneficial for its regular customers.





To do this, you just need to present your passport and wait 15 minutes until the plastic is ready. In this case, there is no need to confirm your solvency, because in the bank the movement of funds in the accounts is already perfectly visible.

After receiving a Sberbank credit card, the conditions for using plastic are very favorable. The client has 50 days to pay off the debt without any interest. The maximum amount for which you can get a loan is 150 thousand rubles. At the end of the grace period, the interest rate will be 18.9%. However, there is one condition: the monthly payment must be at least 10% of the amount taken.

"Europe Bank"

At this financial institution, customers can also count on getting a quick credit card. It takes no more than half an hour to issue it. To do this, the client only needs to present a passport and meet some requirements of the bank. Those who are over 21 years old can count on a credit card. In addition, the potential borrower must be registered in the same place where the bank branch is located. You will also need to provide a certificate confirming the fact that the borrower has a permanent place of work in which he has been working for at least 4 months.





In this case, you can also get a loan with the possibility of interest-free repayment for 50 days. After this period, the interest rate will be 29%. You can get up to 200 thousand rubles from the bank. However, it should be borne in mind that QC service is not free. According to the conditions for obtaining a credit card, its annual service will cost 750 rubles.

"Opening"

This credit organization can issue an instant card within an hour. Moreover, the client can get a loan of up to 500 thousand rubles with a grace period of interest-free repayment of 55 days. After it, the overpayment will be 25%. Moreover, the monthly payment should be at least 5%.





In addition, the bank has an additional Disco program. It can be activated with a quick credit card. If you connect the program, then in this case the QC will work in the same way as the discount one. This is very beneficial if the customer will make purchases in stores that are partners of this credit organization.

Home loan

This organization provides borrowers with the ability to quickly obtain a credit card. At the same time, for instant QC, it is enough to leave an application online. You can also arrange plastic in any branch of the bank.

If we talk about credit conditions, the grace period will be 51 days. After that, the overpayment will be 29.9%. At the same time, customers can get a loan of up to 200 thousand rubles. The card is issued within 30 minutes. Moreover, the monthly fee should be 5% of the total amount.

If the client has already opened an account with Otkritie Bank, then it is enough to present only a passport. New borrowers will need to submit additional documents to quickly obtain a credit card.

Finally

Credit cards are very profitable products from financial institutions. However, you need to understand that it is not profitable for a bank to provide loans for free. This is not necessary, since most citizens do not know how to calculate their financial capabilities correctly. Therefore, before issuing a QC, it is worth considering several times. If the borrower does not fit into the grace period, then in the end he will have to overpay the bank a lot more. Therefore, it makes sense to think about getting a cash loan.





If you arrange a target loan, then the rates will be much lower. You also need to take into account the fact that the less the bank requires documents for obtaining a loan, the less favorable conditions are offered to borrowers. Nevertheless, credit cards will be useful for those who are a client of a particular financial institution, since there are many preferential programs for this. The main thing is to properly assess your capabilities, select the most favorable loan conditions and carefully study the contract before signing it.
