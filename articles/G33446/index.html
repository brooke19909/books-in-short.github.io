Krasnodar hotels: list, reviews and photos

People come to the wonderful southern city of Krasnodar both on business trips and on the way to Crimea. If tourists travel from far away, then in order to relax, many remain here to spend the night. Of course, I want to spend my time in comfort. And good conditions will be offered to you by the hotels of Krasnodar.

Grand Hotel Uyut

This is one of the best options for those who are planning to stay in Krasnodar. The hotel has a category of four stars. The hotel is located in the city center. The restaurant serves seafood and Mediterranean cuisine. For guests with their own car, free parking is available. Each room has a satellite TV, refrigerator, air conditioning. Consider several options for accommodation in a grand hotel in Krasnodar:

  • standard number - 2815 rubles per day if two people live, and 2203 rubles if one person lives;
  • double superior room - 4544 rubles per day for two people and 3744 rubles per day for one person;
  • double deluxe room - 5972 rubles per day if two people live, and 4992 rubles for one person;
  • double superior deluxe room - 6152 rubles per day for two people, and 6052 rubles for one person;
  • Studio - 3917 rubles for two and 3305 rubles for one person;
  • Superior Studio - 5504 rubles and 4704 rubles, respectively;
  • triple standard room - for three people the cost will be 4800 rubles, and 5600 rubles for the accommodation of four people.

The price includes breakfast. If you wish, you can include dinner, but then you have to pay a little more. Each room has carved wooden furniture and free Wi-Fi access. The address of the Grand Hotel Uyut: st. Vorovsky, house 118.

Hotel Rimar

Hotel "Rimar" in Krasnodar is a hotel complex located on the banks of the Kuban River. The hotel restaurants represent the culinary traditions inherent in different countries. Seminars can be held in the spacious conference room, and the necessary equipment is also available for rent. There is secure parking for cars. Hotel guests can swim in the pool, work out in the fitness room. These services are included in the room rate. The number of rooms is 69 rooms, consider some of them:

  • standard number - 7500 rubles per day for two people, and 6600 rubles for one person;
  • Studio - 8800 rubles and 7900 rubles, respectively;
  • Deluxe Studio - 9800 rubles for two people and 8900 for one person;
  • Studio - 10,900 rubles and 10,000 rubles, respectively;
  • Executive Studio - 12,900 rubles if two people live, and 12,000 rubles if one is settled.

Buffet breakfast is included. If you wish, you can refuse this service, then the number will be cheaper. The rooms are bright and large, each with a TV, air conditioning and a minibar, and free Wi-Fi is also available. Among the hotels in Krasnodar, "Rimar" is probably one of the most luxurious. It is located at: st. Kuban Embankment, Building 44.

Aparthotel

The apart-hotel in Krasnodar has a very convenient location. There is a large shopping center just one minute walk away, and the Kuban Stadium is 2.7 km from the hotel. The well-equipped apartments have a microwave, washing machine, stove, fridge and flat-screen TV. Ironing facilities and kitchenware are also provided. All rooms are air-conditioned. The apartments are a private apartment in an apartment building. The elevator is working. The cost of living for one day is 2100 rubles. It can accommodate four people. The hotel is located at: Krasnaya street, 176.

Visitors are offered the following accommodation options:

  • standard double room - 1980 rubles per day for two people, and 1790 rubles per day for one person;
  • Superior double room - 2180 and 1990 rubles, respectively;
  • comfort double room - 2680 rubles if two people live, and 2490 rubles if one person settles;
  • Junior Suite - 2980 and 2790 rubles, respectively.

Breakfast is included. Each room has a TV and air conditioning, free Wi-Fi is available. Pets allowed. Hotel "Marton" in Krasnodar is located at: st. Severnaya, house 91.

The hotel is only five minutes walk to the shore of the Kuban. You will need twenty minutes to get to the Theater Square. Guests can park their car in the free car park. The hotel has its own restaurant. Hotel Visit offers tourists the following accommodation options:
