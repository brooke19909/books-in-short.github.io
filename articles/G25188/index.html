Christmas tree made of sweets: how to make an original, sweet present

On New Year's Eve and Christmas, original hand-made crafts are becoming relevant. It is these products that are a wonderful gift to family members and friends and can become an interesting decoration of any living space. In this article we will share with you a simple technology for making a beautiful and tasty souvenir.





So, a tree of sweets. This product will appeal not only to adults, but, of course, will also please children. A Christmas tree made of sweets will not take up much space and will certainly become an adornment of any holiday table. So, we proceed to the manufacture of this colorful and vibrant craft.

Sweet New Year's present - a tree made of sweets: a master class

To make a sweet souvenir, you will need the following supplies:

  • thick paper (thin cardboard) white or green;
  • pencil, scissors;
  • glue;
  • stapler;
  • Scotch;
  • foil (bow);
  • candy in a wrapper.

The basis for the Christmas tree is a cone made of thick paper or cardboard. For its manufacture, it is necessary to cut a semicircle from the existing material and roll it into a cone, fixing the edges with adhesive tape or glue. Trim the edges of the workpiece so that the craft is more stable. It is advisable to choose green paper, so your candy tree will look more natural. Although you can take white cardboard, paint it with paints or decorate the finished craft with decorative elements, hiding the white areas. If you wish, you can make a Christmas tree of golden or silver material, then your product will look very elegant and rich. It is advisable to choose a color for the "trunk" of the tree so that it harmonizes with the candy wrapper.
