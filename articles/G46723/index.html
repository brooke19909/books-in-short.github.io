Basic Rules

So what should I do if the Lenovo P780 does not turn on after falling into water?

  1. As we already found out, turn off the phone. If the device was connected to a charger, you should very carefully disconnect it from the network. Remember that water conducts electricity perfectly, so you can be shocked. In this case, it is better to disconnect the apartment or house from electricity and then get and turn off the phone.
  2. After you turn off the phone, you need to get the battery. Then we take paper towels and wipe the apparatus dry. You must also make sure that all additional devices are turned off (headphones, SIM card, memory card, etc.).
  3. In order to completely get rid of water, you can try to disassemble the phone a little and wipe it from the inside. Remember that if even one drop remains inside, this will lead to corrosion or short circuit.
  4. You can dry the phone with a vacuum cleaner, but you do not need to expose the device very close to the hose. After 25 minutes, the moisture will not dry. This method is perfect if the smartphone is quickly taken out of the water. Do not use a hair dryer for this, as it can melt some elements with warm air, this will only do harm.
  5. Residual moisture can be removed with rice. If you leave your smartphone for the night in a bowl of rice, then in the morning you can already turn it on. Rice absorbs moisture well, but the gadget must be double-checked before turning it on.
  6. Before turning on, check all ports in the phone. If it looks completely dry and clean, you can turn on the device. Perhaps you have strange sounds when you turn on your smartphone, which means that it is damaged. Also, if the phone does not start to turn on, but looks completely dry, then the Lenovo P780 battery may have been damaged or discharged.
  7. If complete drying did not help, then you should take the phone to a service center, where it will be checked for serviceability and, possibly, brought back to life. Of course, it will be quite expensive, but it is cheaper than buying a new smartphone.
