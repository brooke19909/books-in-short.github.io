Hotel Blue Bay Muine Resort 4 * (Vietnam): description and photos

If you decide to spend your vacation in Mui Ne (Vietnam) and are looking for a comfortable hotel with a large green area on the seafront and away from city noise, then perhaps the Blue Bay Muine Resort 4 * is what you need.



Location

The four-star Blue Bay Mui Ne Resort is located in the Phan Thiet resort of Vietnam . A few tens of meters from the hotel is the beach. The distance to the center of the nearest city of Mui Ne is about 20 kilometers. You can get here by the free bus provided by the hotel. He leaves three times a day. Most of the guests preferred to travel to Muine in the evening. So, the bus leaves the hotel at 17:00. On the contrary, he leaves at 21:30. So tourists will have enough time to buy something, have dinner, take a walk, visit entertainment venues, etc. You can also get to Mui Ne by city bus or taxi. In addition, many vacationers rented motorbikes, which greatly facilitated their movement around the neighborhood. As for the distance from the airport, Ho Chi Minh City's air harbor is located from the Blue Bay Muine Resort 4 * (Phan Thiet) at a distance of 230 kilometers. Thus, the path to the hotel upon arrival will take you several hours.



General information, photo

The hotel in question was built in 2006. For several years, restoration work was carried out here, and in 2015 it again opened its doors to tourists. It has a large green, beautiful and well-groomed territory with an area of ​​just over 20 thousand square meters. meters. The Blue Bay Mui Ne Resort Resort has 85 comfortable and spacious rooms located in two three-story buildings and several bungalows.

The hotel has a restaurant offering dishes of both European and Asian cuisine, a bar with a wide selection of drinks for every taste, two outdoor pools, sun terraces with comfortable sun loungers and parasols, a gym, karaoke, parking, and a conference room. Reception at the hotel is open around the clock.

Our compatriots who have left feedback about the Blue Bay Muine Resort say that this hotel offers the best combination of price and quality. Its rating, based on guest ratings, is 3.9 out of five points. This figure would probably be higher if the hotel was a little closer to the center of Mui Ne. However, not all travelers at the time of booking clearly understood where the hotel is actually located, which ultimately influenced their ratings.



Housing stock

As already mentioned, the hotel in question has 85 rooms. They are presented in the following categories: double deluxe (with one double or two single beds and garden or ocean views), superior double and triple (with garden view), triple deluxe (garden view) and bungalows with sea or garden view. Regardless of the type, all apartments are spacious, stylishly decorated and equipped with everything necessary. So, in each room there is comfortable furniture, a TV with cable channels, air conditioning, a bathroom with shower and hairdryer, coffee maker, kettle, tea set, iron and ironing board, terrace or balcony with a beautiful view. A big plus, according to many guests, is the fact that throughout the hotel, including in the rooms, there is access to free wireless Internet. Moreover, the Wi-Fi speed is quite high. So you can freely communicate and share photos with family and friends staying at home.



Cleaning service

There were no complaints from the guests about the work of the maids of the Blue Bay Muine Resort (Vietnam). So, tourists say that their rooms have always been cleaned. Also, the maids did not forget to change towels and bed linen, as well as replenish supplies of personal hygiene products.

A positive impression was left among vacationers and the rest of the hotel staff. Our compatriots note that the staff of the Blue Bay Mui Ne Resort are very friendly, welcoming and happy to help. True, no one speaks Russian here. Not all employees are fluent in English. However, according to them, no difficulties with communication with tourists arose.



Nutrition

The cost of living in the Blue Bay Muyne Resort includes breakfast only. They pass in the hotel restaurant. In general, according to the guests, breakfast here is standard for hotels in Vietnam. So, in the morning you can eat fried eggs, omelette, boiled eggs, sausage, ham, soup, side dish, pastries, fresh vegetables and fruits, juice, tea and coffee. When the hotel has few guests, you are offered to make an order from the menu. The rest of the time, breakfast is served in buffet style.

Tourists can also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant, but at an additional cost. However, many travelers chose to eat in a cafe in Mui Ne. The fact is that, according to them, the prices in the hotel restaurant are quite high, although they cook there very tasty.

Beach vacation

Almost all guests of the Blue Bay Muine Resort were satisfied with this item. An indisputable advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the sea. In addition, there are practically no other hotels in the neighborhood, so the whole beach will be at your disposal. As for its cleanliness, then, according to our compatriots, hotel workers periodically clean it. However, garbage is still found here. But this did not become a serious problem for vacationers. The tourists were satisfied with the sea. They note that it is almost never calm, but everyone really enjoyed frolicing in the waves. As for beach towels, they are provided to hotel guests free of charge.
