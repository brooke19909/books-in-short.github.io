Planning a trip to Crete? Elounda is waiting for guests!

Greece is a wonderful place to relax: chic service, clean, warm sea, picturesque landscapes - a paradise on Earth! And this statement is true both for the mainland - the amazing trident Halkidiki - and for the placer islands -

Crete, Rhodes and Kos.

Choose a resort

Crete is a real small world, separated from the continent by the waters of the Mediterranean Sea; here, every vacationer will find something special for himself, something that he lacks: solitude or outdoor activities, attractions or a luxurious beach strip, the underwater world and walks by land. The island can be divided into several resorts, each of which has its own zest. If you are interested in history and attractions, you should pay attention to Heraklion. Do you want to get luxurious service and relaxation in the best hotels? Easy! Another resort on the island of Crete - Elounda opens its doors for you.

Location Elounda

This wonderful and cozy town is located on the shores of a small quiet lagoon, but if you wish, you can swim in the bays of Corfo or Mirabello, which are nearby. Thanks to its favorable location on the island of Crete, Elounda is buried in the greenery of trees and shrubs, the sea is surprisingly warm, and the whiteness and purity of the beaches are amazing. This is where the premium hotels are located, with VIP service, excellent service,

wonderful cuisine. Not far from Elounda there is another town in which life is in full swing - Agios Nikolaos. Visit him if you suddenly get bored of Elounda

Holidays in Elounda

When planning to spend a week or two on Crete, do not forget that, in addition to an excellent beach holiday, there are many activities that you can diversify your vacation. For example, if on the whole island of Crete Elounda seemed to you the most attractive resort, do not forget to visit the ruins of an ancient Byzantine-style settlement. You can get there by renting a car or bike; if you are not afraid to overcome distances on foot - do not deny yourself the pleasure of walking through the picturesque expanses of the Greek resort.

The best hotels of the island

Well, have you made some time for relaxation and intend to enjoy luxury in all its manifestations? Then Greece, Crete, Elounda are waiting for you. Here are located hotels such as Grecotel Elounda 5 *, one of the world chain of hotels, Porto Elounda 5 *, Elounda Beach 5 * deluxe, Elounda Mare 5 * and other hotels of a similar level. Especially popular is the hotel on the island of Crete - Elounda Beach, located on a vast territory next to another hotel, Elounda Bay Palace, and with

him a common infrastructure. A whole complex of lounge and lobby bars, restaurants, swimming pools, recreation areas create a wonderful atmosphere for vacationers, and the walking distance of the sea makes the hotel truly luxurious. All rooms of the hotel are characterized by a high level of comfort and are equipped with air conditioning. However, you are unlikely to want to pass the days in the room when there are so many interesting things around! In addition to the animation program for adults and children, you can actively spend free time doing one of the sports offered to guests, or relax in the spa, visit the museum or enjoy one of the daily show programs. A significant advantage of the hotel is the provision of free sun loungers not only by the pool, but also on the beach.

So, if you still came to this country of dreams (in Crete), Elounda will welcome you with its luxury and splendor! Do not forget to visit the theme evening - Greek night at a hotel or one of the local taverns. This is an unforgettable event: tables burst with exquisite dishes of local cuisine, and wonderful music pours through the restaurant, to which artists dance incendiary sirtaki!
