MTS: roaming in Belarus. Tariffs, connection

It is very convenient for Russians traveling to Belarus to use roaming from MTS. In some cases, using this service in Belarus is even beneficial, given that roaming is always much more expensive than communication in your home country. MTS, on the other hand, offers service packages with which you can call home and more, use the Internet and send regular SMS.

What to do before leaving for Belarus?

If you do not want to go to the MTS office, you can connect roaming yourself. There are 3 ways to do this: connecting via a special command that must be entered on a smartphone or another phone, ordering through the MTS official website in your personal account, and a contact center will also help you connect to this service.

To connect to roaming using the USSD command, dial * 111 * 2192 #.

Using the Internet, connection is made after a simple registration on the official MTS website - www.mts.ru. To do this, select your region and use the "switch".

MTS Contact Center has ample opportunities in addition to roaming. So, to connect roaming or solve any other problem, you can use several proposed methods. When to connect roaming independently will not work?

It will be possible to carry out independent connection of MTS roaming for Belarus and trips to other countries in two cases. The first one is if the SIM card that you want to connect to roaming is valid for less than 6 months and the balance on it is less than 600 rubles. The second is a SIM card under 12 months of age, and if during this period at least 1 month payment was missed, that is, it amounted to 0 rubles.

For this, you will need to personally come to the MTS office or communication salon with a passport and have at least some positive balance on your phone account. Cost of services in Belarus

MTS tariffs for roaming in Belarus are quite affordable, especially when it comes to Belarus itself, and Russia too.

Thus, incoming calls from Russia and outgoing to Russia will cost 70 rubles per minute when roaming in Belarus. The same cost of calls in Belarus. If the call is made to one of the CIS countries, Georgia, South Ossetia or Abkhazia, then its cost increases to 109 rubles. To other countries on MTS roaming from Belarus, a call will cost 135 rubles per minute of conversation.

All incoming SMS messages in roaming in Belarus are free, and outgoing ones cost 10 rubles.

The Internet in the presence of the BIT abroad service will cost unlimited exactly 450 rubles per day, and without special services - 10 rubles for 40 Kb of incoming or outgoing information.

In Belarus, the problems that have arisen can also be resolved in the Belarusian MTS stores. In addition, even roaming itself, if it was not connected on the territory of Russia, can be connected in the Belarusian communication salon and make international calls with MTS.
