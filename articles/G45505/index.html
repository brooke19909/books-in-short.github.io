If you are looking for a budget option for accommodation in Phuket, in the Kata Beach area, but want to stay in a comfortable hotel near the sea, then we recommend considering the Easy Resort Kata Beach as a suitable option.

Rooms

Our compatriots were generally satisfied with the housing stock of the Eazy Resort Kata Beach 3 * hotel (Phuket, Kata). According to them, they liked the spacious, comfortable, nicely decorated rooms. Guests note that the apartment has everything you need: a plasma TV with two Russian-language channels, a spacious fridge, a set for making tea and coffee (tea bags, coffee, as well as sugar and cream bring every day), a free electronic safe, powerful air conditioning, bathroom with new fixtures. If you need a hairdryer, you can take it to the reception for free. Tourists pay attention that since all rooms are on the ground floor, there are no balconies. However, there are furnished terraces. From neighboring rooms and the street they are separated by partitions and bushes. By the way, all apartments here have separate exits to the street. Also, experienced travelers warn that if you like to sleep longer, it is advisable to check in when checking in to place you in a room located away from the hotel kitchen. The fact is that they start working here from five in the morning. The noise created by kitchen workers can wake you up.

In general, everything works without interruption in the rooms. However, if some kind of breakdown happens, the hotel staff will very quickly fix the problem. Our compatriots also warn that as soon as you leave the apartment, the electricity in them turns off. So be careful when leaving perishable food in the refrigerator.



the Internet

According to travelers, this hotel everywhere has access to free wireless Internet. However, Wi-Fi speed is very low, and using it is sometimes very problematic. If you want to be able to always be online, then, on the advice of experienced tourists, purchase a SIM card from one of the local mobile operators by connecting an Internet package to it. It will cost a lot.

Cleaning

As for this point, in general, the guests of the Eazy Resort Kata Beach 3 * (Phuket, Kata Beach) had no particular complaints about the maids. So, according to vacationers, their rooms were cleaned every day. Towels and bed linen are also regularly changed. By the way, every day not only baths are updated, but also beach towels that are brought directly to your apartment. In addition, as you spend, replenished supplies of drinking water, tea bags and coffee and personal hygiene products in the bathroom. Please note that some guests in their comments point to the fact that maids often arrive for cleaning quite early (sometimes around eight in the morning). Therefore, if you want to sleep longer, then do not forget to put a Do Not Disturb sign on the door so that nothing will disturb your rest.



Nutrition

The cost of living in this hotel includes breakfast only. They pass in a small dining room from six to ten in the morning. There are several tables for visitors, as well as tables with food and dishes. Breakfasts, according to vacationers, are the same every day. So, in the morning you can taste eggs in different variations, sausage, cheese, sausages, soup, vegetables, fruits, pastries, juice, tea, coffee. Some of our compatriots complained about a not very diverse selection of dishes and a repeating menu in the morning. However, according to them, it was always possible to have breakfast from time to time in one of the cafes in the vicinity of the hotel.

As for lunch and dinner, next to the Easy Resort Kata Beach there are several restaurants where everyone can find food to their taste. So, here you can taste both Thai national food and the dishes of Russian or Italian cuisine familiar to us. In addition, in the vicinity of the hotel there are several shops and a market where you can buy fruits, vegetables, seafood, etc.

Near the beach there are many cafes and bars where you can eat or enjoy a drink.
