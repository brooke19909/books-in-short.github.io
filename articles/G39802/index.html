How to check the insurance policy for authenticity

Fake OSAGO policy can cause serious problems to the car owner. Starting from fines for the actual absence of compulsory insurance and ending with non-payment of compensation in the event of an accident. But how to check who the CTP policy is issued for and is it not fake?





Why bother?

The reason is the fact that the document is not always purchased at the office of the insurance company. Therefore, it is likely that they are trying to sell you a fake.

The offer to issue the policy retroactively should also raise suspicion. Most likely, the license was revoked from the insurer, but the company is trying to earn at least something in the end. Her forms, of course, are no longer valid.

Visual inspection

How to check if the insurance policy is valid if there is no Internet access at hand? Carefully inspect the document. Goznak prints forms of policies, so they have signs of protection. If you wish, you can "expose" even a very high-quality fake.

  1. The form should be 9-10 mm longer than a regular sheet of A4 paper.
  2. Feel, stroke the front surface of the policy. The original has a special fabric texture (like a banknote) and differs to ordinary paper by touch.
  3. Bring the form to the light source to find out if there are watermarks on it. You should see a large PCA logo.
  4. Examine the back of the document and find a metallized thread 2 mm wide. It should be diving and not feel to the touch. If the thread is simply glued or it is easy to feel, you have a fake in your hands.
  5. Upon careful inspection, you will see small red villi on the surface of the form. They glow in the ultraviolet. It is clear that you most likely do not have a special lamp, but note that it is almost impossible to recreate these fibers.
  6. The small guilloche pattern, which forms the green background of the policy, is too complicated for a conventional printer; moreover, it is carried out by the intaglio method - the lines are as if pressed into the paper. If you draw along the pattern on the original with your finger, you can feel the relief.
  7. The document must be green and have an EEE series. Starting May 1, 2015, the green BBB series letterhead is no longer in use, and the blue AAA series letterhead is an outdated version.
  8. The ten-digit number must have a relief and be felt.

Compliance Procedure

It is useful not only to know how to check the policy for compulsory motor third-party liability insurance, but also how the registration procedure looks like in general.

If you are cooperating with an agent, be sure to ask to show the contract with the insurer that he represents and the power of attorney to sign the contracts. Make sure that after completing the transaction you have received all the necessary documents:

  • policy;
  • policy rules;
  • sticker on the windshield;
  • copy of payment receipt;
  • accident notification forms ;
  • list of branches of the insurer.

Before that, it would be nice to check if the license of the insurance company is valid. This can be done on the website of the Central Bank.





If the fake is noticed too late?

What to do if the question of how to check the CTP policy is taken care of you too late - after you have been sold the fake insurance.

If the worst has not happened, get a real policy as soon as possible, and take the linden to the police and write a statement. Then you will not be attracted for falsifying documents.

If an accident occurred, and it is your fault, be prepared for the fact that you have to pay the damage yourself. Theoretically, you can also contact the police. If those who sold you the fake are found, then you can try to recover the amount of damage caused in the accident from these persons.
