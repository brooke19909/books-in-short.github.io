Recreation facilities on the Egorlyksky reservoir

Summer vacations at various camp sites have long been extremely popular among Russians. In recent years, he made real competition overseas trips. And this is quite natural, because it is much cheaper than tours to foreign shores, it makes it possible to relax even in the absence of the opportunity to take a full vacation at work and does not require acclimatization. Demand for various recreation facilities located in picturesque places in the vastness of our country is growing year by year. This season, recreation centers on the Yegorlyk reservoir in the Stavropol Territory are especially popular. Tourists travel to this place with large families in winter and summer. Several recreation centers have been built here, offering a different level of service and a choice of entertainment. If you are interested in recreation centers of the Yegorlyksky reservoir, then our today's article will be useful to you.





Reservoir description

The Yegorlyk reservoir is located only twenty-seven kilometers from Stavropol, which makes it a favorite vacation spot for local residents. Fishermen or ordinary tourists with tents and guitars often come here. Fans of comfort can settle in beautiful and cozy houses equipped with everything necessary for a good pastime. Whatever option you choose, in any case, rest on the Yegorlyksky reservoir will be remembered for a long time.

Fishing: a favorite tourist attraction

They go to the Yegorlyk reservoir not only for beautiful views and tranquility, but also for fascinating fishing. Inveterate fishermen know the most suitable places for this occupation and are ready to spend almost the whole weekend on the water.

Since the reservoir is considered flowing, it is often caught ram, bream, pikeperch and grass carp. Tourists say that fish in a pond are plentiful and it is almost impossible to leave without a catch. Therefore, they come to the Yegorlyk reservoir from all over the Stavropol Territory to later brag to their friends about incredible fishing.





The recreation center "The Little Mermaid"

It is never empty here. Those who plan to actively relax for one day, and those who are ready to spend the night on the shore of the reservoir and spend this weekend in this hospitable place, come to the Little Mermaid recreation center. On the territory of the base there are all conditions for a comfortable and fun holiday.

If you came as a company in order to fry kebabs and swim, then you will have at your disposal barbecue facilities located near the arbors, comfortable sun loungers at the very edge of the water and playgrounds with all the structures.

Sports enthusiasts can take advantage of the volleyball court. It often hosts real competitions. Those who prefer a rest on the water are provided with catamarans. They are often taken by families with children in order to arrange competitions among themselves.

Since most often guests come to the base in their own car, a secure parking lot is equipped for them. You can safely leave your transport on it and not worry about its safety.

On the territory of the Little Mermaid, soft drinks and ice cream are sold, so when going on vacation you can not stock up on them in advance.

Accommodation and cost of living

For tourists comfortable rooms of a sanatorium type are thought out. They can accommodate two and four, if necessary, guests can choose the level of comfort of the rooms.

Accommodation for two people is possible in double rooms and junior suites. The cost of living starts from one thousand rubles. But four guests can only be accommodated in a standard quadruple room. Four thousand rubles will have to be paid for it.





Holiday Village "Fisherman's House"

This place is different from the likes of it with its unique flavor. The recreation center was built on the very shore of the reservoir and is built up with cozy bungalows. This creates an indescribable atmosphere and causes a lot of enthusiasm among children.

Guests who come to the "Fisherman's House" get access to small gazebos, barbecue facilities and various entertainments: boats, catamarans, sports and children's playgrounds. Another feature of the recreation center is a magnificent sandy beach. It has sun loungers with bright umbrellas.

Cost of living

Guests at Fisherman's House are accommodated in a bungalow. One house accommodates up to nine people at a time, which is very convenient for large companies. That is why people often come to the recreation center to hold a corporate event, wedding or anniversary. The cost of living does not exceed three hundred rubles. Holiday Village "Blue Wave"

This place can strike your mind literally from the very first glance. The entire territory of the recreation center is planted with flowers, shrubs and trees. In the summer season, an incredible atmosphere of beauty reigns here, which is difficult to convey in words.

You can relax in the "Blue Wave" in the summer and winter. Cozy houses are equipped with air conditioning, and in the cold season they are heated. Each house has a veranda where it is pleasant to spend evening time.

The recreation center is located one hundred and fifty meters from the shore of the reservoir, so guests are offered a lot of water activities. They can rent catamarans, boats and jet skis for speed races with friends. If the water temperature does not allow swimming, then guests have the opportunity to spend time by the pool.

Tourists often play on the volleyball court or enjoy board games. An excellent playground with a lot of designs is equipped for kids. Often people come to the recreation center in order to hold a large event with an abundance of guests.

If you just want to fry kebabs and relax in nature, then you will also like it in the Blue Wave. On the base are equipped mangal zones with cozy gazebos and awnings.

The cost of living varies from three hundred to six hundred rubles. Tourists can accommodate in a house for two, three and four people. Also on the base are prepared and family rooms, which are usually very popular.





Recreation center "Mayak", Yegorlyk reservoir

This modern recreation center is located a hundred meters from the reservoir itself. On a vast territory spread guest houses, gazebos and play areas. The Mayak offers a wide selection of water activities. Tourists enthusiastically talk about yachting, wakeboarding, banana and catamaran. You can also rent a jet ski. The beach is equipped with comfortable places for relaxation, and at the base you can always rent the necessary equipment and inventory.

Fans of barbecue also did not go unnoticed - special zones with many braziers and canopies were prepared for them. They are located at some distance from the guest houses, so tourists will not interfere with each other.

Guests are accommodated in five cottages, each with four rooms of different capacities. The rooms can accommodate from two to four people. It is worth noting that the living conditions in the cottages are simply magnificent. Guests can use their own bathroom, shower, fridge, kettle and other items. The average daily cost of living is six thousand rubles. This amount includes three meals a day.
