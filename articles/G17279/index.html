The main operating mode of St. Petersburg metro

For residents of a big city, the issue of transport accessibility plays an important role. Every day, millions of people travel huge distances in order to get to work, study or on personal matters. All this would have been impossible without a well-planned urban public transport system.

Operating hours of metro stations (St. Petersburg)

In St. Petersburg, there is a clearly planned schedule of vehicles. The operating mode of the St. Petersburg metro forms the basis of this schedule. It is the metro schedule that adjusts the work of trams, trolleybuses and buses. For most residents of the city, the metro is the main form of transport. It is this morning and evening that avoids traffic jams.

The Metro Directorate in St. Petersburg agreed on a specific mode of operation for the stations. St. Petersburg metro starts at about 6 a.m. and ends after midnight. Each station has its own schedule - it depends on the distance from the city center. The metro in St. Petersburg is one of the deepest in the world, due to soil characteristics.

However, there are events that make adjustments to the mode of operation. St. Petersburg metro works at night for the New Year. This is done for the maximum convenience of citizens who take part in mass celebrations in the city center. During the break in the work of the subway, weekend buses run in the city on weekends and holidays. They completely duplicate the main metro lines and allow you to freely move between different areas of the city.

Metro St. Petersburg at night

The operating mode of St. Petersburg metro is also adjusted in the summer. This is due to the fact that the Neva - a large waterway of the city - passes through a huge number of ships. With the opening of navigation in the city begin to build bridges. One of the main business cards of the city brings some inconvenience to residents. Some areas, such as Vasilievsky Island, are completely isolated at the time when the bridges are drawn.



That is why the city government decided to organize night trains between the Admiralteyskaya and Sportivnaya stations. This enables residents of the city to make movements regardless of the time of day.

The operating mode of St. Petersburg metro must be known to every resident or guest of the city. You can purchase various calendars and booklets at the underground ticket offices and in print shops that contain information on the work schedule of various stations.
