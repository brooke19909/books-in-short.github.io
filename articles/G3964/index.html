Hotel "Jasmine Side", Turkey. Description. Reviews

In the village of Cholakli, 500 meters from the sea, the budget hotel "Jasmine Side" (Turkey) is located. Decent living conditions, quality service and affordable prices are especially appreciated by families with children and youth.

Nutrition

From 10 a.m. to 11 p.m., the Jasmine Side Hotel (Turkey) serves its guests using the all inclusive system. Lunch, dinner and breakfast are served free of charge, as well as locally-produced liquors and soft drinks. In addition, a la carte restaurants are available for guests . There are also two bars on site.

Services and entertainment

With quality service and affordable holiday , Turkey is attracted by travelers from year to year. Hotel "Jasmine Side", the photo of which is presented on this page, created all the conditions for a carefree pastime of tourists. There is an indoor pool, billiards, water slides, bathhouse, car rental, luggage room, elevator, currency exchange, concierge, lobby, parking, games room, laundry, disco, massage room, first-aid post, taxi, sauna, gym and beauty salon . Vacationers can play beach volleyball or table tennis. All water activities are available for a fee.

For children

For kids there is a separate pool. Entertainment events and discos are regularly held. There is a play area. Upon request, a cot can be delivered to the room. Babysitting is available for babies (extra charge).





Beach

The sea is 450 meters from the hotel. There is a regular bus service to the beach. Umbrellas and sun loungers are provided free of charge. There is a bar on the beach.
