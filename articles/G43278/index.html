Knitting slippers

We very often hear such a call from doctors: "Keep your feet warm!". Therefore, each family member should have warm slippers in the house . Knitted on two knitting home slippers will warm not only the legs, but also the soul, because they are made with great love. If you are thinking about gifts for family and friends, then pay attention to this very simple model - it will not require much time and effort from you.

Warm home knitted slippers (with job description)



Such cozy slippers can be made from the remains of yarn by combining threads of different colors into one for the fortress. Melange can be strengthened by weaving another additional thread of durable synthetic material. This model has only two small drawbacks: they slide on the floor and wear out quickly. Some craftswomen reinforce the sole with artificial leather, but we will only describe the process of how to make slippers themselves, knitted on two knitting needles. In preparation for work, the selection of knitting needles is included - these should be straight knitting needles No. 8 or a similar size. Do not forget to prepare a thick needle, which will sew the details of slippers. Measure the foot with a centimeter tape - this figure will be useful to you. Also remember that knitwear tends to stretch, so the length of knitting can be reduced by about 2-3 cm.

Knitted slippers pattern:

  • Type 29 stitches on the knitting needles.
  • Knit the following rows as follows: first row - remove the loop, 8 purl stitches, 1 crochet, 9 purl stitches, 1 crochet, 9 purl stitches. The second row to knit, as the knitting looks (all wrong loops). So continue to alternate these two rows until the canvas is 7 cm shorter than the length of the foot.
  • We turn to knitting a sock. Row 1: remove the first loop, continue alternating: 1 wrong loop, remove one with a crochet - to the end of the row. Row 2: 1 seam loop, remove one loop with a yarn. It turns out bulk gum, which must be continued approximately 7 cm.
  • Open sock loops should be assembled with a gypsy needle and tightened. Securely attach the toe knot.

Now join the heel seam by folding the back half in half. Stitch tightly so that slippers knitted on two needles are worn for a long time without repair. In the photo they look a little ridiculous, but when you put them on, they will take on shape and you will feel a sense of satisfaction from your work.

Show your imagination and come up with your own version of the decor of such slippers. For the elderly, an edge is usually made of thread or fur; for women, pompons or bows are suitable. Children will be happy if their slippers will resemble small animals, or will be decorated with flowers and ribbons. Men will like button or buckle decor.
