Phone "Ark": reviews, descriptions and specifications. Phone "Benefit Benefit" M5: reviews

Buying a smartphone in one of the mobile phone stores or digital technology stores is a matter of no surprise to the average Russian. There are a large number of hypermarkets, shops, salons, Internet services that are happy to offer you a huge amount of modern mobile devices for every taste, color and, most importantly, a wallet: from simple and cheap push-button phones to expensive ultrathin and ultra-powerful smartphones.

There is perfect competition in the gadget market: a huge number of manufacturers, various operating systems, creative design solutions, a myriad of accessories and components. Of course, this cannot but please the buyer, because the more options, the better the chance to choose exactly what you need.





Manufacturer

Before buying a smartphone, buyers usually look at two indicators: price and quality. These criteria, as a rule, are combined in a single indicator - the manufacturer. Yes, it is precisely focusing on the company to which this or that gadget belongs that many correlate the cost of the product offered to it and its technical characteristics.

As mentioned above, there are a substantial number of smartphone manufacturers. These are Russian, and Chinese, and Korean, and American, and even British companies. Most, of course, trust Korea and the United States of America, which are represented by the two largest companies at the moment - Samsung and Apple, respectively. The products of these giants in the field of mobile devices are most popular around the world. Their quality is undeniable, but for most Russians to buy a more or less decent smartphone of these brands is a serious waste of money. And as you know, many of them are not as many as we would like.

Chinese smartphones

Now the Russian market gravitates to Chinese companies. They offer phones much cheaper, and many believe that this has a very negative effect on quality. This is partly true, but this can not be said about all Chinese manufacturers. For example, not so long ago, smartphones from the ARK company appeared in Russian stores and salons (English - ARK). The company entered our market in 2013. One can safely say that anyone can afford to buy an Ark phone. Customer reviews allow us to safely say that this Chinese manufacturer differs from its competitors for the better both in price and quality. Let's get acquainted with the products of this company closer. What is an Ark phone? Reviews, price, features, availability - everything will be considered by us further.

Brand Features

The ARK company is engaged in the production of not only smartphones, but also other gadgets, as well as accessories for them. However, the most popular products are still phones. The line of smartphones from "ARC" is a few devices that differ from each other in appearance, technical characteristics and price. The most popular representatives of the ARC company are the Ark Benefit 12, Ark Benefit M5, and Ark Benefit M7 smartphones.

Reviews of the overwhelming number of device owners come down to the fact that these are excellent options for the ratio of price and quality, in which the quality clearly exceeds the price. The last parameter, by the way, is very, very attractive: from 2 to 10 thousand rubles, depending on the model being purchased and the capabilities that it has. You can buy such a smartphone in almost any digital equipment store or mobile phone shop.

Phone "Benefit M5 Arc": customer reviews and specifications

The first model we will look at will be this particular phone. We will not describe for a long time, let us get down to business immediately.

The smartphone has an operating system Android 4.4, two slots for SIM cards and a 5-inch display like IPS. There is a camera of 8 megapixels, a front camera is also available - there are already 1.3 megapixels. The memory of the phone itself is 4 GB, but there is a slot for SD-cards, and RAM is 512 MB. The battery has a capacity of 2000 mA / h, which will be enough for 7 hours of conversation or 4-5 hours of active work with the device when using the 3G Internet (unfortunately, there is no 4G support).

Looking at these parameters, you might think that the phone will cost about 10-12 thousand rubles, since the characteristics are very decent. But it was not there. Its price is in the region from 5 to 6 thousand rubles, depending on the place of sale. Of course, these parameters are worth it to buy the phone "Benefit M5 Arc." Reviews about this device confirm these words: most users were satisfied and are not going to change the device. Unless on a newer model from the "ARC".
