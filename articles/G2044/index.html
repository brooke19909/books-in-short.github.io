Kirillovka, treasure island water park: what reviews say about it

For many lovers of relaxation on the Sea of ​​Azov, Kirillovka, in the Zaporizhzhya region of Ukraine, has become an annual place of visit. Indeed, in this urban-type settlement, harmony was achieved between developed infrastructure and wild untouched nature. Kirillovka is located on the cape, and from it two braids diverge into the Sea of ​​Azov - Fedotova and Peresyp. There is also the island of Biryuchiy, which is a nature reserve. It is good to lie on a soft sand under a hot sun. But such a pastime quickly becomes boring. It diversifies the rest with events and energizes the guests of the resort called Kirillovka Water Park "Treasure Island" with positive emotions. It opened recently, but has already become a byword. Besides the fact that this is the largest water park in Ukraine, what else can this town of water entertainments amaze visitors with? Read about it later.



Where is it located and how to get there

The Treasure Island is located in the very center of the resort village of Kirillovka. The water park is located at the entrance to the Fedotova Spit. Since it is a favorite beach holiday destination, minibuses from the bus station come here. In addition, in the immediate vicinity of the water park there are other tourist attractions of the resort - a theater giving horse shows, the Kirillov Sich and the Oscar dolphinarium. Not to find “Treasure Island” in the village is unthinkable. Despite the fact that it opened just a little over a year ago, everyone at the resort, old and young, knows the way to it. After all, the country's largest water park has become the pride of little Kirillovka.



Rides on Treasure Island

Sixty thousand square meters - this is the total area of ​​this water fun town. Everything is provided here for every taste. Lovers of passive relaxation can soak up sun loungers on the banks of a huge swimming pool or on rafts slowly sailing along the “Lazy River”. And for those who value adrenaline, there are thirty-four attractions. For the sake of this, many tourists vacationing on the Sea of ​​Azov, at least for a day, come to the village we are considering (Kirillovka). Water park does not let anyone get bored. Children are admitted to eighteen attractions. In general, for kids there is a specially allocated area. It consists of a shallow pool, to the shore of which a life-size pirate ship is moored. Parents can fearlessly leave their child in the children's area under the supervision of professional animators. From adult rides, sometimes breathtaking. Animators work in the central pool. There you can do water aerobics.



Security, infrastructure

The rides on Treasure Island are impressive (especially for adults). But with all its tortuosity and height, they are absolutely safe. You don’t like pools, because it smells like bleach? But this does not apply to the tanks that are in the resort, known as Kirillovka. The water park has its own well. Water is supplied from a depth of one hundred and fifty meters. Before being fed to the pools, it undergoes double cleaning: through various fractions of hydrous anthracite and quartz sand . Therefore, water in its composition meets all sanitary requirements and is equated to drinking. All paths on the sides of the pools are made of special tiles that do not slip when wet. The water park has a video surveillance system. There are dressing rooms, lockers, safes, a pharmacy, a first-aid post. At the entrance to the water park there is parking. At the pools and at the rides, qualified lifeguards are constantly on duty.



Nutrition

In all water parks it is forbidden to bring food with you. No exception is made for the “Treasure Island” in the village. Kirillovka. The water park has many catering establishments. They are designed for any wallet. Soft drinks, ice cream and snacks can be bought at the Welcome kiosk, and burgers can have a bite to eat at Africa Café. Want to have a good dinner? Nothing is easier! Which country cuisine do you prefer? Borsch, cabbage rolls and other dishes of Ukrainian cuisine will be served at the Steering Wheel Bistro. Pizzeria Sicily serves Italian cuisine, while Asia Cafe serves oriental dishes. Fans of fish and seafood should look into the institution "Billy Bons".



Treasure Island (water park, Kirillovka): prices

With turnstiles at the entrance there is a ruler - to measure the growth of children. If your child is below a meter, he can walk for free. If his height is below one hundred and forty centimeters, he needs to buy a children's ticket. But he is only twenty hryvnias cheaper than an adult. The ticket price depends on the time of entry to the territory of the water park. Since “Treasure Island” is open from ten in the morning until seven in the evening, the time spent in it is calculated on a “before closing” basis. If you arrive in the morning, you will have to pay three hundred and ninety hryvnias. In the afternoon, the price drops to 370. After two - 350. But even if you arrive in the evening, planning to spend only two and a half hours in the water sports town, you still have to pay three hundred and twenty hryvnias. These prices were relevant in the summer of 2015.



“Treasure Island” (water park, Kirillovka): reviews

Most visitors were delighted to visit the huge water entertainment town. For safety reasons, children whose height has not reached 140 centimeters are not allowed to ride an adult roller coaster. And on attractions such as “Space Hall”, “Tsunami” and “Wave”, only adolescents with a full fourteen years are allowed. But, despite this distinction by height and age, visitors to the water park can relax with the whole family. Many tourists post on social networks just group photos. Kirillovka, whose water park has earned its best reputation in a year, has become even more famous among beach lovers on the Sea of ​​Azov.
