Home Acoustic: Rating

Shelf acoustics for the home in our time is necessary for every person, because with its help you can watch movies or listen to music at high volume and with clear sound. The range of systems is quite wide - from public models for not too demanding listeners to the Hi-End class.

The article presents the rating of shelf acoustics for the home. It includes exclusively high-quality models that have many positive reviews and a number of attractive features.



Shelf Models

Shelf acoustics for the home are pleasant to listeners that it is rather tiny. Such models easily fit into a limited space, but for normal bottoms in a small volume, owners will have to resort to separate tricks.

A photo of shelf home acoustics will help you understand how it looks and where it is attached. Such models are especially popular. Most often, consumers are attracted by the fact that they have two-way sound, so midbass gets both low and mid-frequency band.



Top manufacturers

Today, many brands are engaged in the production of shelf speakers for the home (Hi-Fi and other models). The most popular of them are:

  1. Yamaha The Japanese manufacturer attracts the attention of buyers due to modern design, excellent sound and versatility. In the assortment there are many models, including shelf options. They have good enough power and functionality, even despite their compactness.
  2. Monitor Audio Bronze. The trademark puts on sale various systems of a professional level, which are distinguished by a large frequency range and tonal accuracy. The highlight of the brand is the fact that under it comes a real sample of the classic Hi-Fi. Its products combine ergonomics, functionality, as well as traditional sound quality.
  3. JBL. Speakers from this brand allow the owner to feel like in a movie theater. They are equipped with a waveguide supporting maximum resolution imaging technology. Excellent sound clarity and sensational power are provided by the simultaneous operation of several speakers. In general, the products are distinguished by an elegant style and an affordable price range.
  4. Heco. Shelf active home acoustics are especially popular with this brand. Systems are created according to strict world technical standards. The speakers have an impeccable design, reproduce all frequencies and make the sound of each octave as realistic as possible. The company is always on the list of leaders in terms of quality and value.
  5. Sony The list is completed by a popular manufacturer that provides customers with the opportunity to choose an accessory at a low cost. Even the most economical models combine power, purity of sound and fidelity of reproducing the highest frequencies.

Monitor Audio Silver 2

This shelf home acoustics became available to customers in 2014. It combines an 8-inch midbass as well as an inch tweeter. This ensures excellent linearity of the frequency response, even when connected to the common output of the amplifier. The tweeter has an aluminum diffuser, which is distinguished by a ceramic coating, guaranteeing excellent dynamics and, of course, detailing at the highest frequencies.

The main advantages of the speakers are:

  • crossover lane separation;
  • beautiful bottoms without losing the quality of the midrange-treble ranges;
  • the ability to connect speakers separately.

Among the shortcomings of the speaker system, one can note only the fact that the rear output of the bass reflex does not allow the speakers to be placed close to the wall. Because of this, you have to think up ways to conveniently place the system on a shelf so that it does not get damaged.

Polk Audio RTi A1

Durable and high-quality material for the manufacture of columns ensures their integrity. In addition, the system features support for separate connection of channels to midbass and tweeter, which helps to customize the sound to taste.

Reviews about shelf acoustics for the home are only positive and they indicate the following advantages:

  • availability of biamping support;
  • quite impressive power with such a miniature size;
  • Convenient wall mount.

The only drawback of the model is considered not the best setting for the internal crossover. This nuance is most often paid attention to those people who are well versed in such systems and want to buy only the best model for themselves.

Monitor loudspeaker fully pays for all the money invested in itself. It has a fairly high sound power and an interesting combination of colors in the design of the front panel.

The speakers have a lot of advantages, but you need to highlight only the main ones:

  • reasonable cost;
  • deep bass;
  • high power

Behringer Truth B2030A
