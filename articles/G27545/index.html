Modern sound technology is developing rapidly and its own cool subwoofer for the home does not surprise anyone. The main purpose of this component of the audio system is to reproduce a low frequency range (not higher than 100 Hz), which are not reproduced by ordinary speakers. Which model to prefer? What options to consider when choosing? Let's try to figure it out.

Main purpose





A subwoofer is equipment that provides a high - quality presence effect, for example, while listening to music or watching a movie at home. Such devices quickly gained popularity and became an important component of a powerful speaker system. When choosing a subwoofer for your home, you need to proceed from indicators such as power and purpose. Note that there are two main varieties of these systems:

  • Active. They are ideal for use in a home theater, as they have the flexibility and ease of installation. Such devices are cheaper.
  • Passive . Subwoofers of this type were originally thought out in such a way that they can only be used in combination with an amplifier. In this case, the amplifier must be of sufficient power.

Active subwoofer for home - a more convenient and correct solution due to the built-in filter and low-frequency amplifier. Thanks to this, the sound gains higher quality at any frequencies. At the same time, using an active subwoofer, you can improve the distorted sound in the entire range.

How to do it from scratch?

A subwoofer is a powerful addition to the speaker system, which could very well become part of a home theater system. But what if there is no money to buy finished equipment, but you want to enjoy high-quality sound? In this case, you can try to make a subwoofer for your home with your own hands. To do this, you need to perform a number of actions:





  1. Buy speakers and a case (preferably wooden).
  2. Calculate the size of the boxes. To do this, you can use computer programs in which you need to specify a number of parameters - the size of the speaker and bass reflex.
  3. Make parts. After calculations, you can begin to create the details of the future subwoofer. It is best to use high quality wood, plywood: in addition to naturalness, they are ideal for a variety of processing.
  4. Assemble the housing. After all the details of the future device are sawn, they need to be interconnected. For greater reliability, the walls are most conveniently fastened with screws.
  5. Connect the speaker to the amplifier and power supply. To do this, you may need drawings of a subwoofer for the home, on which all the nuances of connecting the main elements are noted. The structural diagram of the entire sound system plays an important role, which means that close attention needs to be paid to this side of the assembly.
  6. Insert the ready-made and already reinforced speaker into the housing. Sheathe it. Lead all wires out from the back of the box through special connectors.




Everything, the subwoofer is ready for operation, you can check it. If suddenly during operation unpleasant sounds, rustling appear, it makes sense to check whether all openings in the box are closed. They must be sealed - for this you can use glue or sealant. In any case, you can make a subwoofer for the house yourself, if you understand the diagrams and drawings.
