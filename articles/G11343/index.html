Selen Hotel Icmeler 3 * (Turkey / Marmaris / Icmeler): description, photos and reviews of tourists

The popular resort of Icmeler is only about 25 km from the large Turkish coastal city of Marmaris. This village is located in a picturesque bay, surrounded by high mountains, forested. You can spend time in Icmeler not only thanks to the azure sea and magnificent nature. The tourist infrastructure at this resort is also developed simply superbly.

Many domestic operators sell tours to Icmeler. And most of them work including with the budget hotel Selen Hotel Icmeler 3 *. Despite the very low, even in comparison with other Turkish three-star hotels, the cost, the rooms in this complex, according to tourists, are for rent very good.





Location

The Selen Hotel Icmeler 3 * hotel is located directly in Icmeler itself. The center of this village is only a couple of kilometers from the hotel. To reach the main streets of Icmeler with their large shopping centers, bars, clubs and other guests of the complex can literally 15-20 minutes on foot.

Very conveniently located and in relation to the sea. This hotel is built on the first coastline. To get to the beach, Selen Hotel guests just need to cross the promenade.

Unfortunately, the hotel is located quite far from the nearest Dalaman airport - about 90 km. So, tourists who rented rooms here will have to get here for a relatively long time.
