Summer holidays on Kapchagai. Recreation areas, projected prices for 2016

Kazakhstan is gradually becoming a popular tourist destination. Amazingly beautiful nature and legalized gambling business encouraged local businessmen to develop beach infrastructure. In this article we will talk about the Kapchagai reservoir. When it was created, and it was in 1970, no one thought that there would be a recreational area. They simply evicted the inhabitants of the village of Ili with Soviet directness, flooded it and built turbines of the Kapchagai hydroelectric power station. On the shore of the reservoir formed a new town. Its population was evacuated residents of Ili. The city was named Kapchagay (modern name Kapshagay). For a long time it was a small and unremarkable settlement in the Almaty region. But nine years ago, with the adoption of the law on gambling, the town turned into a resort in a couple of years. Now on the banks of the reservoir there is not only a casino, but also numerous pensions and hotels offering an unforgettable vacation on Kapchagai. Recreation areas on this man-made lake will be considered in our article.



What is the climate in Kapchagai

The reservoir is located in the middle course of the Ili River. It stretches for a hundred kilometers! The width of the reservoir is 25-30 km. The average depths are about forty meters. For its size and proximity to the former capital of Kazakhstan, they called the man-made lake the Almaty Sea. For the weekend, many residents of the region come here. For them, the main attractions are a casino and a wonderful water park. Well, for those who have come from afar, the main thing is a beach holiday. On Kapchagai recreation areas (and there are more than one hundred and fifty) occupied all the banks. These are mainly private guest houses. Only two boarding houses are in state ownership. Of course, it is worth coming here in the summer months. The air warms up to forty degrees, but a deep lake softens the heat. "Fiesta"

This resort hotel is located on the Almaty-Taldykorgan highway, on the southwestern shore of the reservoir, within the city of Kapshagai. Therefore, all the necessary infrastructure at your fingertips. The Fiesta Recreation Area on Kapchagai is a scattering of convenient modern cottages designed for a comfortable stay of six people. The whole house is for rent, regardless of the number of residents in it. Prices in summer vary depending on the days of the week. On weekdays, the house costs 36,000 tenge, and on Saturday and Sunday - 42,000 tenge per day. In the recreation area there is a swimming pool, barbecue, sauna. The last service is at an additional cost. Sandy beach - a hundred meters. It is well-maintained, there are sun umbrellas, changing cabins.





Rio

The recreation area is located on the 96th kilometer of the highway, fifty meters from the equipped beach. On the territory there are swimming pools for adults and children, a Finnish sauna, a billiard room, a banquet hall, a bar with a hookah. You can rent a gazebo with a trestle bed right on the shore, as well as everything you need for cooking barbecue and grill. The Rio Recreation Area on Kapchagai provides economy class houses, rooms in a wooden log house and VIP cottages. Prices - from three to twenty thousand tenge.



"Cabbage"
