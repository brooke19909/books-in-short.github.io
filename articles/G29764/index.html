The recreation center "Volyn". Rest in the Pskov region: country hotel "Volyn"

The recreation center "Volyn" is worthy of the most enthusiastic and kind words. It is not so far from the noisy cities filled with smog and vanity, but here, as if in some unusual magical land, silence and peace reign. The cleanest air, the sound of wind in the treetops, birdsong, the beauty and serenity of the water surface are waiting for everyone who came here.



Location

The recreation center "Volyn" is located in the Pskov region, on a very beautiful peninsula, crashed into Lake Zizzycka, one of the "fish" and largest in the region. Nearby is the village of Kameno (Kuninsky district). On three sides, the base is surrounded by water. The shores of the lake are unusually picturesque, there are convenient paths for walking, beaches, as well as huge boulders covered with mosses, against which many people like to be photographed. In addition, the recreation center (hotel) Volyn has its own forest plot for hunting. You can meet here wild boars, moose, deer, capercaillie, black grouse, even bears. Paradise is here for mushroom pickers. In their bast baskets, boletus, butterflies, mushrooms will surely be.

How to get there

You can get to the Volyn base by rail and road. A train departs from Moscow from Riga Station every evening to the city of Velikiye Luki. He arrives in the morning, at five to seven. There is also a train from St. Petersburg (from the Vitebsk railway station). He arrives at Velikiye Luki station at five to eight. Further vacationers can get to the base transfer.



By car from Moscow you need to follow the Volokolamsk highway, then follow the M9 highway to the Kunya urban settlement, then turn around at the traffic police post and, following the signs with the inscription "Volyn", follow to the final destination. From St. Petersburg, go along the Moscow highway to Pskov. Next, go to the Novorizhskoe highway and follow to the village of Kunya, then go along the same route as from Moscow. The last 20 km of the site is a dirt road.

Description

The recreation center "Volyn" is located on a well-groomed territory surrounded by pines, spruces and birches. Designers created it stylized as an old Russian village. All buildings here are made of wooden logs, even a children's town is fully consistent with the general background. Along the houses laid boardwalks with railings. Of course, the gazebos available on site are also wooden. Everything is very stylish and beautiful.

In addition to residential buildings, the base has real Russian baths (2 pieces), saunas, a jacuzzi, a restaurant, a large tent, a bar, a gym, a pier on the lake with boats and boats, a rental point for sports, fishing and hunting equipment, playgrounds in volleyball, tennis, billiard room.

Rooms

The recreation center "Volyn" offers guests to accommodate 7 houses, called "Hunter", and 7 cottages, called "Volyn". The price of living in them is different, as they differ in comfort and arrangement.





The houses correspond to the class "Standard" and have 2 floors. On the first floor there is a bedroom (for 2 persons), a kitchen (all necessary utensils, a refrigerator, sugar, tea are provided), a bathroom with a shower, a toilet and a washbasin, a TV, a telephone, a safe. The bathroom includes a hairdryer, towels, hygiene products). On the second (attic) floor there is a bedroom (for 3 persons) and a set of necessary furniture. There is a balcony with a view of the forest.

Cottages correspond to the category "Comfort" or "Junior Suite". They are also two-story. On the ground floor there is a living room with comfortable soft chairs, a fireplace, a sofa, a telephone, a safe, a TV, a kitchen with a set of wooden furniture and lots of dishes, a toilet. On the second floor there are 2 bedrooms, each for 2 persons, there are necessary furniture, a bathroom, a balcony. Towels, a hairdryer and a set of hygiene products are provided in the bathroom.

There are no air conditioners in the rooms, but the Pskov region, like the whole region, does not have a very hot climate, so the guests are quite happy with the fans.

The rooms are cleaned only on the day of departure, the linen does not change. Near each house and cottage a parking lot is provided.



Nutrition

The recreation center "Volyn" (Pskov region) has its own restaurant, located in a two-story building stylized as a Russian log hut. All dishes are prepared with fresh products (fruits, vegetables, meat, fish from the base farm or from local farmers, sometimes game is served). Guests are offered excellent breakfasts (the price is included in the room rate), lunches and dinners. Meals can be ordered to your liking. The rooms have kitchens, but no stoves. They need to be taken separately.

There are two banquet rooms at the restaurant (one for 35, the second for 50 guests). This makes it possible to conduct various events and holidays on the base.

Guests can also relax in the bar, which serves a variety of drinks.

For celebration by small companies (up to 15 people), the Volyn country hotel provides a glazed gazebo with tables and a stage, as well as a tent that can accommodate up to 110 guests. There are tables, carpet, stage, dance floor.



Services and entertainment

The Pskov region is rich in unique natural resources. It is also famous for its history. Vacationers at the Volyn base can fully enjoy the beauty of these places, on foot or on a rented bicycle, take a walk in the surroundings, hunt or go fishing. The fish in Lake Zizycki are very diverse. There are carp, burbot, roach, bleak, bream, ruff, crucian carp and others. The huntsmen of the base help in hunting, and also feed fish. Therefore, it is perfectly caught both from the water and from the shore, and in winter - in the holes.

For an interesting leisure, the Volyn country hotel provides:

  • boats and boats;
  • winter snowmobiles, skis, skates, sledges;
  • fishing gear;
  • hunting equipment;
  • bicycles
  • sport equipment;
  • organizes excursions;
  • provides the opportunity to fly on a hang glider;
  • gives skewers, charcoal, firewood for barbecues.

The base works constantly, so here you can celebrate New Year and Christmas, celebrate Shrovetide, Epiphany, Valentine's Day and other holidays.



Reviews

Volyn is a recreation center, reviews of which are always filled with the most vivid and most pleasant memories. Those who have been here note the following advantages:

  • full compliance of price and quality;
  • the uniqueness of the place is beauty, silence, peace;
  • excellent fishing (even a child can easily catch a fish);
  • delicious dishes in the restaurant;
  • wonderful design of rooms and the entire base as a whole;
  • beautiful baths and saunas;
  • the friendly attitude of many employees of the base, always ready to help disinterestedly.

The disadvantages of vacationers here include:

  • lack of stoves and microwave ovens in the rooms;
  • poor access to the base;
  • lack of cleaning in the rooms;
  • incorrect behavior of individual workers;
  • lack of wifi in all rooms.
