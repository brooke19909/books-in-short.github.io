Portugal. Cape of Doom - the western edge of Eurasia

The westernmost point of not only Europe, but also the entire Eurasian continent is Portugal, Cape Roca. It is located near the city of Lisbon, and once it was even called Cape Lisbon. This amazing area has been repeatedly described by poets from different countries who managed to visit here. Even from their lines you can understand that, like the whole of Portugal, Cape Roca has special magic. This is exactly the place where the earth ends and the endless waters of the Atlantic begin.



This Portuguese plain is a major tourist destination. Of course, on local lands there are beaches where you can lie down and relax, and also swim in the ocean in the warmer months. However, it is worth remembering that the westernmost point of the mainland is at the same time one of the most windy zones on Earth. At the foot of small cliffs, the impulses are not as strong as at their peaks, where many photographers and adventurers climb to get a good shot. However, it is categorically not recommended to do this on winter days, as the wind can easily disrupt a person and carry to the bottom of the abyss.



Of course, Portugal also boasts architectural landmarks. Cape Roca is famous for its high and very beautiful lighthouse, which is located near the coast. He gains special magic and beauty at night, when there is no great illumination around and it is he who is the main source of light. At such moments it seems that all of Portugal depends on this bright and flickering light. Cape of Doom is also indicated by a beautiful stella, over which a cross rises. This is a kind of sign indicating that this area is the westernmost point of the mainland.

A stay at a hotel will cost any tourist at a very humane price. The influx of travelers here is observed only at the end of summer, and in the remaining months many rooms are empty, so they are rented out at affordable prices. It is worth noting that not all of Portugal can boast of such cheapness. Cape Roca is also equipped with a beach leading to a long and twisted path. It is worth noting that it is not safe to go down this path in rainy or windy weather.

Humane prices are set here for food. There are not so many restaurants and bars in the region, but those that are are quite cozy and welcoming, and having visited such establishments, you are definitely having a bad mood. Cape Roca is also famous for souvenir shops, antique shops and local galleries. Portugal is a country that is known primarily for its natural beauties. Therefore, going here, it is worth pre-tuning to silence, peace and tranquility, and not to shopping and active entertainment.





As mentioned earlier, the place where the Earth ends and the water surface begins is Cape Roca, Portugal. Photos of this amazing area are attached to the article, and right now you can plunge into this world for a short while. Well, you can taste the Portuguese air, the ocean breeze and feel the strongest local wind only on the most western lands of our continent - on Cape of Rock.
