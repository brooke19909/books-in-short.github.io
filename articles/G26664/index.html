Bright and beautiful craft "The Sun." With our own hands we will learn how to perform it from different materials

From a very young age, kids love to be creative. Drawing, modeling, application are those activities in which kids take part with great pleasure. Moms and dads try to diversify these activities each time, coming up with new and new ideas for needlework. In this article, master classes are presented for children and their parents, which describe how to complete the "Sunny" crafts from different materials. Such a product can become an attribute for outdoor games, and an element of a puppet theater, and a symbol of Shrovetide, and just a decorative decoration in a child‚Äôs room.

We make paper crafts. "Sun" in every home!

This version of the product is very easy to manufacture. It will take no more than half an hour to create it. And as a result, the sun will turn out bright and beautiful. For the creative process we prepare the following materials:

  • yellow cardboard;
  • double-sided colored paper;
  • office glue or PVA;
  • pencil;
  • round object (cup, saucer, etc.);
  • hole puncher;
  • scissors;
  • ruler.


Craft "Sun" with your own hands is performed according to the following instructions. Circle a round object on cardboard and cut out the part. This will be the basis of the product. Punch holes along the edge of the circle with a hole punch. The gaps between them should be no more than 0.5 centimeters. The more often you make holes, the more magnificent and beautiful the crafts will turn out. On colored paper, draw long rays (10-15 cm) in the form of sharp triangles and cut them out. The number of these blanks should be equal to the number of holes. With a sharp edge, pass each ray into the hole, bend the tip and glue it from the wrong side to the round base of the craft. Put the figure under a heavy flat object. If desired, the product can be painted with colored pencils or felt-tip pens: eyes, mouth, freckles. Craft "Sun" done by hand. Such a figure can be a wall panel or a Christmas-tree decoration, if you make a loop on it. Also, such a product is an excellent toy that develops the motor skills of the baby's hands and allows him to study colors with interest.

The sun from paper and threads: an original children's craft

The principle of manufacturing this version of the figure is the same as described in the previous workshop. Only here is yarn used to make beams. It is cut into pieces of 8-10 centimeters. Next, the strings are folded into bundles of 5-6 pieces. These blanks are threaded into the holes and tied into knots. If the yarn is uncomfortable to stick in the holes, then do this with a knitting hook. To the base of the product, to the cardboard circle, you can use a scotch tape or hot glue to attach a wooden skewer or a cocktail tube. Disposable tableware as a material for creativity

In this part of the article, you will learn how to make the ‚ÄúSun‚Äù craft out of a paper plate. In addition to her, such materials will be needed in the work: paper of yellow and orange colors, glue, accessories, ‚Äúeyes‚Äù or two buttons.

We carry out the product according to the following description. From colored paper we cut out rays of any shape. They can be in the form of stripes, long triangles, waves and even palms. Place the plate with the wrong side up. On the base we fasten the beams close to each other. From paper cut out a circle whose size corresponds to the diameter of the bottom. Glue this part on top of the rays. We turn the product over and paint its front side with paints. We draw the details: bangs, nose, smile. Glue the eyes-buttons. Leave the craft to dry. You can use this figure as an attribute of a carnival costume, pendant, wall decoration.
