"Flowers": crochet patterns and decorative elements

Floral motifs in needlework have always been popular and the love of craftswomen. They are reflected in the crochet technique. Simple crochet patterns, patterns for which you will find in this article, can be used for knitting sundresses, lace tops, children's summer dresses. They are very simple in execution and therefore suitable even for beginner needlewomen.

"Floral" motifs

Crochet patterns, the main element of which are flowers, are created according to the principle of continuous knitting. That is, the first half knits the lower halves of the flowers, and the upper - the second. This method is very convenient in that the result is a solid canvas, consisting of as if separate flowers. ‚ÄúFloral‚Äù patterns are distinguished by originality and tenderness, they will become an adornment of any product.

Pattern 1. "Flower field"

This openwork pattern looks like a net of small flowers connected to each other by chains of air loops. It is best to knit it from fine yarn, so the pattern will be clearly visible.

Crocheted flowers pattern: spring hat

Another pattern that differs slightly from the previous one by the density of knitting, so it is perfect for a spring hat.

Description of knitting: we collect a chain of air loops equal in length to the circumference of the head. The number of loops should be divided by eight.

In the first row we make fans of 7 columns with one crochet. Between them we miss 7 air loops of our chain. In every fourth of these seven we knit a single crochet to form a fan.

In the second row, you need to finish our flowers. To do this, between each adjacent fan, you need to knit seven columns with a common vertex. Do not forget that each new row should begin with three lifting loops.

The crochet pattern made of flowers consists of two main rows: in the first we knit the lower petals, and in the second the upper ones. The alternation of these rows gives a solid picture of the product.

The crocheted "flower" pattern looks very elegant and original. It can be performed not only with plain yarn, you can take sectional yarn. So it will look even more unusual.

We crochet a dress for a girl: pattern "flower field"

This pattern, which we reviewed earlier, is perfect for both a light summer dress and an openwork top. It is better to tie the yoke tightly, but on the hem our pattern will look good. The yoke and hem are knitted separately and then sewn to each other.

Flower brooch

Does your product lack zest? Do you think how to decorate your favorite beret or dress? Then be sure to pay attention to the wonderful decorative element in the form of a flower. Such a flower can be sewn monofilament directly to the product, or you can attach it to a pin and pin it as needed. The lower flower can be knitted from green yarn and used as leaves, planted under a relief bud.
