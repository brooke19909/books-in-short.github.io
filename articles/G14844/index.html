Az Hotel Patong 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): description, service, reviews

Anyone looking for the perfect place to relax will certainly find it in Phuket. This island is located in the waters of the Indian Ocean and is a real paradise for adventurers.

Just a couple of years ago, this tourist destination was rightly considered to be elite - the prices for tickets were really not affordable. Everything changed only a couple of years ago. Now rest on the island of Phuket is one of the most affordable. Moreover, the service here is pretty good, and the entertainment is varied. Perhaps that is why Phuket is so fond of Russian-speaking tourists.

For those who want to relax in a quiet place for a small fee, knowledgeable travelers can safely recommend the small hotel Az Hotel Patong 3 *.

Hotel location

The hotel has a prime location. From Phuket Airport, the road takes only 40 km, so tourists can easily reach the hotel in 30-40 minutes.





The hotel is in close proximity to the most popular attractions: Patong Beach (the road takes 10-15 minutes), Bangla Road, Night Entertainment Center (15 minutes walk), a large shopping center with the name "Jungceylon" (5 minutes).

General Hotel Information

For vacationers who appreciate quiet secluded hotels for relaxation, this option is ideal, because Az Hotel Patong (3 stars) is located at some distance from the noisy streets. On the other hand, a 10-15 minute walk is the most popular beach in Phuket - Patong. Here you will find activities for young people and active travelers, so the hotel is a great option anyway.

The only thing worth remembering is that the hotel has a 3-star rank, which means it is more suitable for not too demanding guests.

The hotel building is much younger than many other hotels. It was put into operation only in 2009. The hotel is a 9-story building with an elevator, but there are not so many rooms - only 70. This gives the right to call the Phuket hotel Az Hotel Patong 3 * relatively small, it works on the basis of food, including breakfast only.

Hotel service

Despite 3 stars, rest here will be quite comfortable and enjoyable. Since the building was built recently, the premises are well thought out, a design approach is felt. In addition, the plumbing did not have time to become worthless and looks quite presentable.





The administration and staff of the hotel always try to make the stay of tourists as convenient and comfortable as possible. Service Az Hotel Patong 3 * includes the following services:

  • The information and accommodation service is open around the clock.
  • Concierge service is available.
  • Air conditioning is available in all public areas of the hotel.
  • The Internet. On the territory of the hotel, guests can use high-speed Internet (WI-FI) for free.
  • Laundry. The hotel also offers dry cleaning and laundry services. This service is paid.
  • Room cleaning. The maid comes in for cleaning daily.
  • Change of bed linen and towels. Change is provided 3 times a week (every other day).
  • Breakfast in the room. At the request of guests, Az Hotel Patong 3 * staff will deliver breakfast directly to your room.
  • Morning call. Such a service will not allow you to oversleep.
  • Luggage storage. At the front desk you can use the storage service.
  • Currency exchange.
  • Car rent. This is a very useful service for those who want to explore the surroundings.
  • Parking. On-site parking spaces are available. The service is free.

Food in Az Hotel Patong 3 *

The hotel offers its guests accommodation with a mandatory breakfast. On the territory there are:

  • a restaurant;
  • bar;
  • snack bar.




The intimate restaurant serves Thai and international dishes. There is a large selection of seafood and fresh fruits. Every day, only fresh food from quality products is served.

Near the pool there is a bar where you can always drink juice, soft drinks or alcoholic drinks. It also offers a variety of snacks, so at any time you can have a light bite to eat without even leaving the hotel. True, meals in the bar need to be paid separately.

Entertainment at the hotel

According to the description, Az Hotel Patong 3 * is hardly suitable for daily spending, however, in between excursions and relaxing on the beach, tourists will certainly find something to do.

  • Pool. An outdoor pool is located on the roof of the building. There is also a recreation area: sunbeds and umbrellas. In addition, the site offers stunning views of the surroundings.




  • Darts. This entertainment helps pass the time.
  • Billiards.
  • Gym.

The fitness center is well equipped with sports equipment and exercise equipment, so vacationers will be able to monitor their figure and health, even during the holidays.

More diverse entertainment can be found in the city - the road will take 10-15 minutes.

Beauty and Health Services

The hotel has a massage room. Sessions are conducted by an experienced masseur - you can choose to massage individual zones or the whole body.





  • SPA-center. Those who decide to relax well and tidy up their appearance should definitely not refuse a good spa relaxation. Masks, peeling, body wraps and many other procedures for the face and body - the list of services is quite large. Many tourists vacationing here leave laudatory comments about Az Hotel Patong 3 *, largely due to the quality massage and spa treatments.
  • Steam room. The hotel has its own well-equipped bathhouse.

All these services are provided to guests for a fee.

Beach

Those who stay at the Az Hotel Patong 3 * hotel (Thailand) , first of all go to the beach. Still, because nearby is the most famous beach in all of Phuket - Patong Beach. For several kilometers there is a strip of white fine sand, and entertainment in the area - for every taste.

Usually there are a lot of vacationers here, so bathing will appeal more to young people and active people. If the task is to find a quiet corner, then it is better to go to neighboring beaches. The disadvantage of staying here may not be clear water - the waves bring a lot of algae. However, in everything else, rest here will be bright and comfortable. The beach has a large number of sun loungers and umbrellas (although they are paid). There is a beach bar nearby . The waiter will bring ordered cocktails directly to the sunbed.





Among the entertainment:

  • yachting (on the coast it is one of the most popular entertainments);
  • water skiing;
  • surfing;
  • diving;
  • riding a banana.

Right here it is proposed to rent equipment for scuba diving and surfing, and experienced instructors will help even beginners to get comfortable.

Attractions in the area

Tired of lying in the sun? It's time to go on excursions around the island, especially since there are a lot of places worthy of attention.

  • Wat Suwan Khiri Wong. In the afternoon, you should definitely visit this quiet sacred place. This Buddhist temple is simply amazing in its power and grandeur.
  • Pun Tuao Kong. The local shrine, which is located directly between the slopes of Patong. Local residents and tourists often come here to pray and ask for the fulfillment of their desires.
  • National Park "Khao Lak".
  • Excursion to the island of Phi Phi. Tourists are delivered to the islands by speed boat.
  • Elephant ride. Such a vivid adventure is simply impossible to forget.




As soon as night falls, the area seems to wake up. The Bangla Road highway turns into a pedestrian street at nightfall along the sidelines of which dozens of cafes, pubs, cabarets, and nightclubs open. It is here that you can dance and drink cocktails until the morning.

Az Hotel Patong 3 *: reviews

Let's try to systematize the opinions of vacationers. Among the tourists leaving reviews about the Az Hotel Patong 3 *, there were both satisfied with the stay at this hotel, and those who were disappointed. In the list of negative points:

  • lack of territory at the hotel;
  • great distance from the beach;
  • a small selection of breakfast dishes (boiled eggs, bacon, sausages, fruits, bread, tea, coffee).

Of the commonly cited benefits:

  • cleanliness of the rooms;
  • Helpful, friendly hotel staff
  • high quality paid services (steam room, massage and spa);
  • convenient location regarding attractions;
  • low prices.

In other words , the Az Hotel Patong 3 * hotel justifies its stars and is perfect for all tourists who are not going to spend time at the hotel, but intend to travel around the island or bask on the beach. By the way, on the outskirts of the city, small cafes open their doors. The situation there is not rich, but the kitchen is excellent and is quite cheap.
