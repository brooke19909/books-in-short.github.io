Do-it-yourself tutu skirt in one evening

Choose material



A do-it-yourself tutu skirt is done very quickly, you will choose the right material longer, because buying it is not so simple. It should be a tulle or a soft mesh made of polyester. The material should be resilient and soft at the same time. The best option is nylon tulle. You also need a strong and wide elastic band - 2-3 cm wide. Good scissors and, perhaps, that's all.
