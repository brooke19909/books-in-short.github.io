"Sarzhevskikh Manor", Gorny Altai: description of the complex, reviews

Sarzhevskikh Manor is a suburban hotel complex located on the territory of Turquoise Katun camp site in Altai Mountains. She is known for beautiful landscaping and comfortable living conditions. This guest house opened in 2007. It can accommodate at the same time more than a hundred people. The base operates year-round, hosting guests in winter and summer.



"Sarzhevsky Manor" ("Turquoise Katun")

This recreation center is located in a special tourist, recreational and economic zone of the Altai Mountains. It is called "Turquoise Katun." This zone was created ten years ago. It surrounds Red Stone Mountain and has borders with the Altai Republic. Its main attraction is the Katun River. Therefore, those who like rafting and rafting often come here. Manzherok rapids are especially popular with tourists. The Sarzhevskys Estate (Gorny Altai) is a very convenient starting point for traveling around the area. Cavers also come here - they have at their disposal a complex of Tavdinsky caves and grottoes. On the territory of this zone there are two ski resorts with lifts and pistes. Therefore, visit it all year round. "Turquoise Katun", according to statistics, is one of the most profitable tourist destinations in Russia.



Location, how to get there

The closest to this tourist complex is Gorno-Altaysk Airport. From there, only thirty minutes by car. It is also convenient to get here from Barnaul. The base is four hundred and seventy kilometers from Novosibirsk. This guest house was built on the left bank of the Katun River. The views from any room are very beautiful. Near the hotel there is a paid and very picturesque bridge across Katun called "Clouds". Next to this guest house are camp sites that operate only in summer, such as Tavda, as well as a mini-hotel Malina. Nearby is the ski complex "Turquoise Katun." But in winter, he serves tourists not alone. Forty kilometers from the base "Manor Sarzhevskih" is the famous ski resort with chair lift "Manzherok". Tourists are brought there by order.



Infrastructure

On the territory of the base "Sarzhevsky Manor" there are five two-story cottages that are completely made of wood, namely, from Altai cedar. In their style, they resemble old Russian architecture. Wooden cottages made of timber have everything you need for relaxation. They really resemble estates - carved porches and other colorful details. The territory is guarded. She is big, well-groomed. There trees grow, especially conifers, a lot of green grass. You can walk, just sit in the air. Everything is beautifully decorated with taste. Very sunny. Paved with many paths with paving slabs. There are benches and several gazebos with barbecue facilities for the preparation of barbecue.



Rooms

Each two-story cottage has eight guest rooms. Thirty-six rooms are junior suites. They are designed for one or two tourists. It has furniture (bedside tables, a wardrobe, beds), a kettle, a large TV, a spacious refrigerator and a bathroom with a shower and hydromassage. Five people can stay in the suite - two will sleep on the bed, the same amount on the sofa, and one more on the cot. The rooms of the Sarzhevsky Manor base are very clean, however, as well as throughout. Even in junior suites, three towels are provided for each guest. They are large and comfortable. You can rent a whole house.



Nutrition

The price includes breakfast. Morning meals are served in the restaurant building. It is a two-story cottage, made in the same style as the living quarters. On the first level there is a kitchen, a cafe and a summer terrace. On the second - the restaurant itself. In the summer, all the halls, including the open air, can accommodate one hundred and thirty people, and in the winter - about eighty. Actually, breakfasts, as well as lunches, snacks and dishes that do not require long preparation, are served in the cafe. Complex meals can also be ordered there. Guests praise the cocktails and desserts they have tasted there. Children especially like pancakes for breakfast. The portions in the restaurant are large, the service is good. You can order a banquet, celebrate a wedding or celebrate a birthday.

Service

Manor Sarzhevskikh offers guests various types of services. Tourists with cars can leave them in the parking lot. If you arrived at the base in winter, you can rent skis, skates and snowboards right on the spot. And in the summer firewood and coal are sold here. All conditions for excellent fishing are created. The complex has a small shop where you can buy the most necessary. The administration can arrange a meeting of tourists in Barnaul and deliver them back upon departure. The cafe has discos in the evenings. The staff is well-trained, dressed in uniform.



Recreation and entertainment

On the base you can play billiards on the second floor of the restaurant, take a steam bath in a Russian bath or sauna. Literally a hundred meters from the place where the wooden cottages are located, across the road, there is an artificial lake. It is heated, there is a beach and you can swim. Equipped beach, with sun loungers and a small water park. True, his visit is paid. The lake is fenced and is located directly between the Katun River and the base itself. And on the territory of the complex there is a small pool with a water slide. In winter, a good skating rink was organized. The Turquoise Katun tourist complex has a good playground, so if you come with children, you can go there. They can swim in the plunge pool. Often on the territory of the complex holidays unfold or competitions are held.



Excursions

Mountain Altai is a great place for summer travel. There is a tour desk on the basis of Sarzhevskikh Manor, and the administration will always help with the choice of trips and tell you what you can visit in one day. In summer, tourists rent bicycles, and in winter you can just go skiing in the surrounding area. In warm weather, visitors often have picnics on the banks of the Katun. And lovers of speleology can visit all the local caves, grottoes and waterfalls, each of which is legendary. You will also find riding horses, ATVs and jeeps. Extreme sports are offered rafting on rafts or kayaks. Alloys are carried out three times a day. Not far from the base is Mount Devil's Finger, which is not only a natural monument, but also has historical value. Among fans of healthy eating, an excursion to the apiary is popular, where you can try honey and tea on herbs, as well as purchase various Altai souvenirs. The nearest cave complex to the guest house is Tavdinsky. It is two kilometers from the Manor. There is a rope town for children, as well as an archaeological park.

Base "Manor Sarzhevskih": reviews

Guests like that in this tourist complex there are no problems with hot water, and the rooms smell like cedar. In winter, the houses are warm and cozy. The atmosphere at the Sarzhevskikh Manor is pleasant, and the staff are friendly. The territory is well cleaned. Tourists considered the proximity of the lake an added bonus. This base was called one of the best among other offers of housing in the village "Turquoise Katun". Many guests who have been here over the past two years believe that the service to tourists has improved significantly, including the manager of this mini-hotel.
