All seasons Naiharn Phuket (Thailand, Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Every year, more and more tourists prefer a relaxing holiday on one of the coasts of Thailand. This country can offer not only a rich beach holiday. Here lovers of excursions will find something for themselves. Naturally, in the country there are many hotels ready to receive foreign tourists, providing them with comfort. One of them is the All Seasons Naiharn Hotel. So where exactly is it located and what should guests expect?

Photo and description of rooms in the hotel





All Seasons Naiharn Hotel has 154 rooms at its disposal:

  • 88 Standard rooms;
  • 53 Superior rooms;
  • 5 Junior suites;
  • 2 Thai Sala;
  • 3 Superior rooms (consist of a bedroom, a bathroom and a spacious living room);
  • 3 Family Suites (spacious rooms that consist of two bedrooms, a bathroom and a spacious living room).

The rooms are cozy and clean, the atmosphere is pleasant - guests can count on home comfort. There is a set of equipment, without which it is difficult to imagine the life of a modern tourist. An air conditioner with a remote control system will help combat summer heat. There is a TV with cable channels. At your service - a safe with an electronic lock, as well as a minibar (only drinking water is free). Also, guests are provided with everything necessary for making coffee and tea.

Naturally, there is a private bathroom equipped with a spacious shower. Clean towels are delivered every day during room cleaning. Linen is changed once a week.

Catering for tourists on site





Paying for hotel accommodation, guests can choose the most convenient meal plan for them. As a rule, guests only pay for breakfast at the All Seasons Naiharn. Reviews indicate that the choice of dishes is quite diverse, especially the presence of fresh fruits. In any case, if you wish, you can have lunch or dinner in the hotel restaurant for a fee. By the way, the prices here are quite affordable. There is also a bar on site where you can get drinks, aromatic tea, light snacks and desserts.

In addition, there are several cafes near the hotel, so problems with food should not arise.

Where is the beach located? Water fun for travelers





All Seasons Naiharn Hotel Complex is located very close to the public beach and can be reached in a few minutes. The seashore is quite secluded, a large influx of tourists is rare, so you can have a great time. The beach is covered with soft sand, the entry into the sea is cleared of algae, which is very convenient. There is an opportunity to both swim in the depths and splash with the child near the shore.

Since the beach is urban, the use of sun loungers and beach towels needs to be paid. On the other hand, the prices here are quite affordable. You will have the opportunity to engage in more active types of recreation, for example, boating and jet skiing, boating, renting canoes, etc. Naturally, water activities are not included in the price.

If you wish, you can rent a transport or take a taxi and get to Patong Beach - this is a lively tourist area with a lot of different attractions and entertainments.

Does the hotel offer additional services?

The hotel has something to offer the tourist in order to make living on its territory as convenient as possible. For example, there is a laundry service that can be used. Almost throughout the territory there is access to the Internet, which is very important for most tourists. You can visit the local beauty salon, where experienced hairdressers and stylists work.

The hotel also has a good spacious conference room. By the way, for a small fee, employees will gladly take up the organization of various events, ranging from seminars and business meetings to weddings.

For parents traveling with children, experienced nannies offer their services, who are not afraid to leave their child for several hours.

Hotel All Seasons Naiharn (Phuket): leisure and entertainment





Despite the fact that many travelers use this hotel only for an overnight stay, there is also something to do on its territory. For example, in the courtyard there is a large spacious pool with sunbeds, hammocks and umbrellas around. Here, guests sunbathe, swim, relax, chat and make new friends. There is also a separate pool, designed for children's entertainment.

Tourists also have free access to the gym. It is small, but equipped with all necessary equipment. Leisure activities can be brightened up with a game of table tennis. For a small fee, you can visit the sauna or steam room. Of course, professional massage therapists offer their services at the hotel, ready to offer real Thai massage.

You can also attend cooking classes, where cooks will teach you how to cook the most popular Thai dishes. There are paid drawing lessons on batik. And do not forget about excursions, because Phuket is a place with a huge number of attractions. There really is something to do here.
