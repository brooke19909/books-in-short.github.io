Hotel "Ali Baba" (Hurghada, Egypt): description of rooms, service, reviews. Ali Baba Palace 4 *

When considering routes for travel, first of all, tourists are determined with its character. Will it be a beach holiday or vacation, full of excursions, it should be decided in advance. However, a resort town on the Red Sea coast - Hurghada has been recognized as a popular place combining various directions, from sunbathing to a party nightlife, for many decades in a row. It is located 500 km from the capital of Egypt.

The tourist season is year-round here, and the prices for tickets, as noted in the reviews by vacationers, are very affordable. A wide variety of housing options: from modest corners in mini-hotels to fashionable apartments in world-famous hotels, will allow you to find what everyone can afford. The mild climate, the gentle rays of the sun, clean air will help to heal the body and recharge your batteries for next year.

The four-star hotel "Ali Baba" service offers its guests upscale. Here, tourists will find a wide range of entertainment, comfortable rooms with a unique design and a warm welcome. Polite and helpful staff, according to vacationers, is one of the main advantages of this institution over other similar ones.

general characteristics

The doors of Ali Baba Palace 4 * hotel were thrown open to accommodate the first vacationers at the turn of the millennium, in 2000. During its existence, it was subjected to global modernization in 2009 and partial renovation in 2016. On the territory, whose total area is 182,000 square meters, there is a one-story main building and many two- and three-story buildings. In the main building there is only a reception. Bulk floors, numerous plants in flowerpots, watches of different time zones create the first impression of the establishment.

Park expanses are dotted with green lawns and leafy palm trees. Walking along the well-groomed alleys, where there are numerous amphoras and other original sculptures, bring a lot of pleasure and aesthetic pleasure to each guest. In the evening, everything is illuminated by ground lights. According to tourists, the feeling of being in a fairy tale is created, which is complemented by a pleasant fragrance of universally divorced flower beds.

Location

The convenient location relative to the airport of international importance has determined the popularity of the Ali Baba Palace 4 * hotel. This distance is 8 km and does not require a tedious transfer after a long flight. The remoteness of 15 km from the center of the village allows you to stay away from the bustle of the city and enjoy a wonderful pastime.

Inner order rules

The list of internal rules includes a ban on pets. Ali Baba Hotel (Hurghada) has rooms for people with reduced mobility. They are equipped with all necessary devices for their comfortable stay. The language barrier with staff is eliminated through employee knowledge of Polish, English, Russian and German. It is possible to pay for services with cards of international banking systems. Available extra beds for a fee.

Accommodation

The hotel complex includes 756 apartments. Hotel Ali Baba offers the following room types: Standard, Family Suite and Family Connected. The smallest is 33 square meters, the largest is 70 squares. All are equipped with access to a balcony or terrace with plastic furniture. French glazing fills the rooms with natural light.

The interior is simple. The walls are decorated with paintings and hung with sconces. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles. According to the guests, thanks to the combination of color schemes and warm design, coziness and comfort are the main characteristics of the rooms.

Equipment

A furniture set is standard. They are equipped with air conditioning, TV, free minibar, telephone and hairdryer. Room Service is available at an additional cost. Cleaning is done daily.

The design of the bathroom is simple. There is a large mirror, shower, sink and bathroom.

Hotel "Ali Baba": food

The current all-inclusive system includes three meals a day: breakfast, lunch and dinner. The form of serving dishes is a buffet, according to which everyone has the right to choose what he liked, and to put exactly as much as he wants in his own plate. The variety of menus, according to tourists, is incredibly large. The interior of the main Shahbour restaurant is decorated in a national style. A bright palette of colors and comfortable furnishings make for a casual conversation during meals.

Two restaurants are also open to visitors: Morgana, which specializes in Italian cuisine, and Zomorodah, which serves Oriental masterpieces. Their visit requires compliance with the dress code and prior reservation of the table.

Healthy and nutritious drinks can be enjoyed in the vitamin bar. In addition to it, another 5 such institutions are open: on the beach, by the pool, in the lobby and lounge bar.

Beach

By purchasing a tour to Hurghada, travelers have the opportunity to plunge into the healing waters of the Red Sea. The hotel complex is located on the first coastline, directly off the coast. The private beach of the hotel "Ali Baba" with a length of 280 meters will allow everyone to take both sun and air baths.

A gentle entry into the water allows the kids to dabble in the "shallow" for hours. The sandy-pebbled coast is dotted with chaise lounges with soft mattresses. For those who prefer to stay in the shade, umbrellas are installed. You can retire from the wind and others in a cozy bungalow.

Infrastructure includes locker rooms, showers and a rescue service that constantly watches over the safety of swimmers. In the vicinity there is a bar, a visit to which you can diversify beach activities. There is also a volleyball court, which daily gathers crowds of tourists. Among water activities, according to hotel guests, the most popular are parasailing and jet skiing. Ride over the sea surface, soaring with a parachute, prefer extreme lovers.

Aquazone

It is possible to realize swimming not only in the clear waters of the Red Sea, but also in one of the hotel pools. There are only three of them: one indoor and two open. The main aquazone is a large-scale basin, which, like a reservoir with many tributaries, spreads throughout the territory, is not equipped with a heating system and is filled with fresh water. Its unusual design with many bridges and passages allows you to enjoy and be filled with new emotions. Different depth, flow effect and widespread greenery create, according to travelers, the effect of a paradise.

The presence of its own water park makes the hotel complex "Ali Baba", according to tourists, a favorite among other options for living. Visiting him once during his stay is free. health and beauty

Tours to Hurghada with accommodation in the hotel complex "Ali Baba" - this is not only a wonderful vacation, but also an opportunity to improve your health. For this, there is everything you need, namely a gym with professional instructors, a spa with first-class masseurs and an ice skating rink. The presence of a variety of simulators, treadmills and other equipment allows you to maintain the body in good shape, and if desired, lose a few extra pounds. To play tennis, you must pay for lighting and rental equipment. The ability to cut ice in hot Egypt, according to hotel guests, makes this place unique. Sauna and Jacuzzi will help cleanse the body of toxins, rejuvenate the skin, energize and revitalize visitors.

Entertainment

Diving is one of the priority activities for most travelers. Holidays in Hurghada is a unique opportunity to get acquainted with one of the beautiful parts of the underwater world of the Red Sea. The depths of these surroundings conceal many interesting representatives of the fauna, no less impressive flora and many amazing coral gardens. Diving is available for both beginners and experienced divers.

In your free time you can visit the bowling alley or play table tennis. Darts and Bocce do not require additional fees. But lovers of billiards should be prepared for additional spending. Equipped with a field for playing mini-golf and futsal.

The evening, as a rule, ends with an animation program and a disco. An entertaining show with many bright dances, humorous scenes, tricks, tricks and funny contests gives a feeling of joy and happiness to everyone present.

Children's leisure

Hotel "Ali Baba" (Hurghada) is positioning itself as a great place for families. According to travelers, this is justified, because there are countless options for entertaining kids. The mini club is the place where the kids forget the count of time. Numerous game complexes, tables for drawing and creative tasks organized by a professional teacher will allow kids to show their creative abilities. Holidays in Hurghada, as noted by travelers, will be remembered by absolutely everyone thanks to a caring guide who has created the maximum possible list of additional services.
