Mercure Bali Nusa Dua, Bali: hotel infrastructure, room description, photos and reviews

Bali is the most popular resort in all of Indonesia, respectively, and it is more developed in terms of tourism. In our article we want to talk about one of the island's hotels - Mercure Bali Nusa Dua (Bali).

A bit about the hotel

The Republic of Indonesia is a storehouse of completely alien and incomprehensible to us culture, which is why our compatriots fell in love with the country's resorts. Bali is a wonderful place not only for a beach holiday in the bounty style, but also a great opportunity to admire the local beauties and immerse yourself in a foreign culture.





Mercure Bali Nusa Dua (Bali) is a great 4-star hotel in Nusa Dua. To get to it, you can buy a ticket to Ngurah Rai Airport, which is 14 kilometers from the hotel. The hotel has a good location, located 2 kilometers from Nusa Dua Beach and shopping centers, 14 kilometers from the water park.

The complex is located 10 minutes walk from the coast. It is a modern four-story building surrounded by a garden.

Number of rooms

Mercure Bali Nusa Dua (Bali) has 201 rooms at its disposal, 177 of them are non-smoking apartments. All rooms are divided into the following categories:

  1. Superior Double Pool View - superior double rooms with pool view and one bed.
  2. Junior Suites with double bed - junior suite with one bed.
  3. Superior Double Garden View - double room (superior) with garden view and one bed.
  4. Deluxe Twin Room with Pool View - Deluxe Double Room with Pool View and Two Single Beds.
  5. Deluxe Double Pool View - Deluxe Double Pool View.

All apartments are equipped with TV, tea and coffee sets, desk, air conditioning, minibar, telephone, safe, hairdryer, satellite channels, Wi-Fi.





The hotel respects all safety measures: there are smoke sensors, electronic locks on the doors.

Dining at the hotel

Mercure Bali Nusa Dua (Bali) has a restaurant that serves international and local cuisine. Also in the hotel there is a lobby bar where vacationers can order non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks. Guests of the complex can choose for themselves the most convenient food option: half board, continental breakfast or full board.

Mercure Bali Nusa Dua: hotel infrastructure

The hotel has several excellent conference rooms that are suitable for hosting and organizing any business event. The staff of the complex helps to arrange everything up to the mark. The complex has swimming pools for children and adults. The hotel has a 24-hour front desk that can assist with luggage storage, laundry, dry cleaning and car rental services.





The hotel has its own spa center, which tourists can visit. For a fee, guests can use the shuttle service. The beach is a ten minute walk from the hotel.

Entertainment

Mercure Bali Nusa Dua (Bali) has an outdoor pool with slides. On the beach of the complex, vacationers can engage in water sports, diving, fishing, canoeing, a jet ski or a scooter. To diversify their stay, tourists can visit the fitness room and spa. The hotel has a tour desk inviting guests to visit local attractions.

Children's services

Mercure Bali Nusa Dua Hotel offers children a separate pool, a playground for games. A babysitting service is available for a fee. Children under one year stay free of charge without a separate bed. On request, a cot can be ordered for an extra charge.

A bit about the resort

The Republic of Indonesia attracts tourists with its pristine landscapes, the impressive volcanoes of Gunung Batur, Kintamini, Gunung Agung, tropical forests, endless beaches and ancient temples. Our fellow citizens have not so long ago appreciated the benefits of a holiday in Bali, and therefore gradually the number of Russian tourists on the island's coast is growing every year.





Nusa Dua is an elite resort that stretches along the coast with mangroves. The city has the most luxurious hotels and the best beaches on the island. In addition, the resort has a walking area with cafes, boutiques, restaurants, water centers called Galleria.

To the north of the town stretches the sandy strip of Tanjung Benoa - a former fishing village on whose territory a chain of five- and four-star hotels has been built.

In Nusa Dua, significant ebbs and flows are observed. They swim in the sea until 9 am or after 15:00. In the daytime, the ocean leaves, exposing the beaches, at such a time the depth is about half a meter. Deeper places remain only in the lagoons.

In Nusa Dua, public transport is provided only by minibuses. But they do not go into the main gates of the city, so the beaches can be reached only on foot or by taxi, there are also minibuses running along the coast every half hour.

Resort Attractions

Dusa Nua is an elite tourist guarded city with limited access. This oasis in Bali was built for fastidious and demanding tourists. The best five-star hotels in the city boast vast territories, beautiful pools, tennis courts and private beaches. But at the same time, there are no attractions in Dusa Nua: neither markets, nor traditional Balinese villages, nor shopping stalls with delicacies. The real life of the local population can only be seen outside the artificial luxury of the city. Almost all complexes here offer equipment for sea sports, as this is the main local entertainment.

To see something interesting, you should go travel around the island. There are enough interesting places in Bali worthy of attention: monkey forest, Tanah Lot temple, Bali Barat park, Batubulan village, island botanical garden, rice terraces, marine park and safari park, Besakiikh temple, Taman Ayun temple, etc. .

In Bali, treatment programs are very developed to improve blood circulation, reduce the manifestations of cellulite, destroy fat deposits, and moisturize the skin. Most of the procedures are based on the use of algae, sea mud and salts. Also, for medicinal purposes, use a variety of bathtubs (with sea water, algae, hydromassage, flower, etc.).





Mercure Bali Nusa Dua Reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to turn to the reviews of tourists who managed to visit it. How good is Mercure Bali Nusa Dua (Indonesia)? Nusa Dua is a great luxury resort with good hotels, one of which is our complex. The hotel is located on the second line and is surrounded by its own garden. The distance to the coast is about 1000 meters. According to the guests, the hotel has a perfect shuttle service to its own beach, so there are no problems getting to the coast. Walk to the coast for about 20 minutes. The beach of the hotel is very clean and well-groomed, it is surrounded by bushes. Hotel staff provides towels. On the coast there are sun loungers, and instead of umbrellas there are trees in the shade of which it is very good to relax. Low tides on the coast do not spoil the whole picture too much , you can swim . A lot of pesky merchants wandering around the beach wandering around.

The hotel does not have a bike or car rental point, but on the way to the shore there is an institution where you can rent a bike. But the car can be ordered online. She will be driven to the hotel and taken from there. The cost of renting a car is about 70,000 rupees per day.

The territory of the complex is rather big and very green and well-groomed. The building is located not on the road, but a little off to the side, which is why street noise does not reach the guests.

In Mercure Bali Nusa Dua 4 * (Bali) there are two pools (for adults and children) with overflow. They turn off the water at night, so the noise from it does not interfere with sleep. The pools are beautiful and clean, a recreation area with sun loungers and umbrellas is located nearby.





The hotel has a good spa center, where you can visit massage sessions, and a large gym with excellent fitness equipment.

Hotel Mercure Bali Nusa Dua itself (description of rooms is given in the article) is far from new, but inside it is maintained in good condition. If you do not like the proposed number, then at the reception you will try to help in this matter, replacing it with another. The hotel staff is very friendly and tries to please their guests. Room service and towels are changed daily. In case of detection of equipment breakdowns in the apartments, it is necessary to inform the reception staff on duty, as a rule, such issues are resolved quickly enough. Guest rooms include slippers and bathrobes that are periodically forgotten to be changed during cleaning. Every day, a bottle (300 ml) of free water for each guest is left in the room. She, of course, is not enough, but still such attention is pleasant.
