Krasnousolsk. Rest savages: reviews (2014)

Bashkiria (now Bashkortostan) is one of the most remarkable places on Earth. The greatness of the mountain cliffs, the purity of rivers and lakes, the beauty of the endless forests, the sincere hospitality of the inhabitants - everything here is woven together. But in Bashkiria there is another treasure - healing springs. The most famous of them are located in the Krasnousolsk resort area. Holidays with the savages are no less popular here than on holiday packages. What is the value of the Krasnousol sources, and how does it rest on them?

Location

An amazing river Usolka (the right tributary of the very famous Belaya River) flows through the territory of the South Urals.

On its shores, not far from Voskresenskaya Mountain, is the village of Krasnousolsky, a rural type. It is quite large, at one time it was considered a village, and now it is the administrative center of the Gafuri region. So there are many infrastructure facilities useful for tourists - shops, cafes, and a bus station. From Ufa to it 135 km, from Orenburg - 300, from Sterlitamak - 57. Just 5 km from the village there is a wonderful resort "Krasnousolsk", savages on which people practice summer and winter. A few more landmarks: 12 km from Krasnousolsky lies the famous village of Tabynsky, and 32 km from the railway. station "White Lake".

How to get there

Regardless of how to go to "Krasnousolsk" - a savage or on a trip, getting there is most convenient by car. The advantages are that relaxation and treatment can be combined with visiting holy and simply interesting places at any time convenient for you. And for savages, important advantages are, firstly, the ability to purchase products not only at retail outlets near the sanatorium, but also in neighboring villages, and secondly, a wider (in terms of price) choice of housing options. So, you can get to the village of Krasnousolsky by car from any major city. The journey time from Sterlitamak is about an hour, from Ufa - about 2 hours and a quarter, from Orenburg - about 5 hours. They go on vacation to Krasnousolsk and from Naberezhnye Chelny, Zlatoust, Magnitogorsk, Almetyevsk. The journey from these cities takes approximately 7 hours. In some places, the road is unpaved, especially at the entrance to the village and the resort, into a mud road and in bad weather, impassable. Without a car, Krasnousolsk can be reached by bus or by train from Ufa to the Beloye Ozero station, and then by bus or taxi. Travel time is approximately half an hour.





Curious historical facts

In the middle of the XVI century, the Russians began the active development of the Ufa river basin. A jail (defensive fortress), now the capital of Bashkiria, was erected at its mouth . The archer commander sent his soldiers to scout the surroundings. Cossacks stumbled upon a river with bitter-salty, unpleasantly smelling water. Climbing up a little along it, they found 3 springs with water even more concentrated. Above them, the river was ordinary, fresh. As it turned out, the locals had long been driving cattle to these sources so that it was healthy and obese. The herders themselves drank water to get rid of ailments. In those places, another prison was erected, and later a salt factory. Gradually, the prison turned into the city of Solovarny, and after 1924 it became the village of Krasnousolsky. In the same year, a doctor, two nurses, builders and a cook were sent here. A little distance from the village put a few wooden houses. So the sanatorium "Krasnousolsk" was formed. Rest by savages in these places has been popular for a very long time, only before everything was free, and now the entrance and use of the pools is worth the money. But, despite the thriving business, there are hundreds of people at the resort. And it helps!



Living and dead water

Holidays in Krasnousolsk are popular mainly due to healing sources. They are divided into three groups. In the 1st, keys are born from the banks of the Usolka River. Their main component is carbonic limestones. In the 2nd group, the springs hit on the left side of the river, the rocks of which formed in the Quaternary. Water in these two groups, never mixing with each other, rises to the surface from a depth of up to 600 meters. That is why it is so icy. The 3rd group is represented by the keys of the right bank, consisting of gypsum from the Kungur stratum. It is not very highly mineralized, especially the Gorky Klyuch spring (only 2.2 g / l). But the water of the first two groups has a mineralization of about 77-78 g / l. Vacationers call them springs with living water, dead and rejuvenating. According to an unwritten law, one must bathe in all three in order. Small springs with locker rooms were built near the springs. It is allowed to swim both organized and savages.

Miracle icon

For the sake of this miracle, crowds of people go to "Krasnousolsk" savage. Bashkiria is an amazing country full of unsolved mysteries. One of them is the Tabyn Icon of the Mother of God. According to legend, she appeared to people four times, and each time this was accompanied by miracles. So, at its first appearance, it was discovered by Bashkir shepherds and chopped into pieces. After that, they all went blind. Ripened only after sincere repentance and supplication for forgiveness. There are several dozens of officially documented cases where the icon healed adults and children, saved the city from an epidemic of cholera, from fires. In Krasnousolsk, in the temple, a list (copy) of this icon is kept. Below is a holy cave where people pray, light candles, leave notes with wishes. Some cons

Most people warmly recall Krasnousolsk, the rest as a savage. Photos taken as a souvenir better than any words convey the unique beauty of these places, the stages of self-treatment, the popularity of sources and the holy temple. They come here all year round, especially at Epiphany, swimming in holy water. However, such popularity has its negative sides. So, in recent years, the resort has become too crowded. Those who want to relax calmer come on weekdays. On weekends and holidays, crowds of people and beer sold on every corner can spoil the calmer vacation. But if you do not pay attention to such trifles, treat the body, and take the walks through the woods, inhale the healing aroma of needles, admire landscapes of unearthly beauty, then Krasnousolsk will leave only good memories.
