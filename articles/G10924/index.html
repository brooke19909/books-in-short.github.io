Borrower Requirements

For all loan products, the general requirements apply:

  1. The age limit is from 20 to 85 years. The potential borrower must be less than 85 at the time of payment of all debt.
  2. Official work with an experience of 4 months.
  3. The presence of a permanent residence in one of the constituent entities of the Russian Federation.
  4. Accommodation in the village where there is a branch of Sovcombank. At the same time, registration and accommodation do not have to coincide.
  5. The borrower must provide a home or work landline telephone.
