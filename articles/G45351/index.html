A bit about the hotel





Hotel "Papa" (Abkhazia) is a new large hotel complex. It opened only in 2015. The hotel provides a comfortable stay for single people and large families. The hotel is ideal for those who are tired of the hustle and bustle of the city. Days spent at the Papa Hotel will delight you with peace and comfort. But if you want to have fun, then the city of Gudauta is located just a few minutes drive. There you can find a large number of cafes and restaurants, night clubs, shops, souvenir shops and other places for entertainment.

The territory of the hotel "Papa" is relatively large and fenced. There was a place on it both for lovers of a quiet holiday, and for those who like to play sports.

Hotel Rooms





Papa is a three-floor hotel with forty-four rooms. Among them:

  • double with one or two single beds;
  • triple.

A standard set of furniture in the rooms: single or double beds with orthopedic mattresses and hypoallergenic pillows, bedside tables, wardrobe. From electronics there is a plasma TV and a small refrigerator.

Each room has a balcony to enjoy the views of the picturesque village of Bambora (Abkhazia, Gudauta). Hotel Papa also guarantees air conditioning. Thanks to them, you can set a comfortable air temperature.

Rest in Abkhazia: prices for accommodation at the Papa Hotel





The cost of living at the Papa Hotel depends on the date of arrival and the number of days. Basically, prices for an entire room per person start at 2,850 rubles per day. Accommodation in a hotel for two will cost at least 3300 rubles. This is until the end of 2016. Since 2017, the hotel plans to raise the cost of living. Therefore, the minimum price per room will start at 3,500 rubles.

At the Papa Hotel, children under five years old without a separate bed are accommodated for free.

Unpleasant moments that confused vacationers





Despite the many positive reviews and a high level of service, the Papa Hotel, according to tourists, still has work to do. In particular, people note the following points:

  • Wi-Fi is not available in the entire hotel and quite often disappears.
  • There are no separate smoking areas on the territory, and smoking is allowed everywhere. Therefore, you can easily breathe in unpleasant smoke.
  • The menu lacks fish dishes, pastries and fruits.
  • Payment for services occurs only in cash.
  • Sun beds on the beach are paid.
  • Not enough urns on the beach. Therefore, garbage has to be thrown away at the hotel.

A separate point, which is not entirely related to the hotel, is that there are no nearby places where you can relax. That is, you need to either go to the city or spend time at the hotel. Although some such a vacation fits perfectly.

Despite such seemingly significant comments, many tourists come to the Papa Hotel (Abkhazia) more than once. And this suggests that resting here is very good.
