Intourist Hotel (Brest, Belarus): description, rooms, features and reviews

Today, the hero city of Brest provides its guests with a warm and warm welcome, providing them with a wide selection of hotels of various categories for temporary residence. In accordance with their needs, wishes and financial capabilities, tourists can book a room or apartment.

An attractive accommodation option for travelers who are interested in the historical past of the hero city and for businessmen who come here to solve production issues will be the Intourist Hotel (Brest).

general information

The Intourist Hotel (Brest) was built in the distant 1975 and was primarily focused on receiving guests from the foreign countries of the regional center. Over the long time of its functioning, it underwent changes several times, was completely and partially reconstructed. And today it is a modern three-star hotel with a convenient location in the city center and reasonable prices for services fully prepared for productive business meetings and recreation for tourists.





Location

The cozy Intourist Hotel (Brest) is located at Masherov Avenue, Building 15. The hotel is favorably located relative to the main transport interchanges of the city, only 16 kilometers from the airport and 2 kilometers from the railway and bus stations of the city.

A short distance separates the hotel from the central department store, a children's leisure park and a gambling house. The majestic memorial "Brest Fortress Hero" and the historical and archaeological museum "Berestye" can be reached either by public transport or on foot by yourself.

Number of rooms

The Intourist Hotel (Brest) offers tourists 137 rooms of various categories: from a modest single economy to a two-room suite. This variety allows vacationers to choose the appropriate option, based on personal needs and the budget of the trip. Regardless of the type of room, you can book accommodation without meals or with a continental breakfast in the restaurant of the same name.

Also, the hotel administration is ready to provide discounts for group arrival, on weekends, for stays of more than 10 days, as well as for certain categories of citizens: schoolchildren, athletes and veterans of the Great Patriotic War.

Accommodation in single rooms

It offers three categories of single rooms (single A, B and C), different in comfort. Meanwhile, in each of them for convenient accommodation of the guest there is:

  • private bathroom with bath amenities;
  • bed;
  • Desktop;
  • TV with satellite channels;
  • soundproof windows;
  • telephone.




The single A room has new furniture and plumbing, a refrigerator.

The Intourist Hotel (Brest) will be an excellent option for inexpensive single accommodation. Prices range from 1,200 to 2,000 Russian rubles per day for accommodation.





Accommodation in double rooms

The Intourist Hotel is ready to offer a choice of five categories of double rooms with a private bathroom:

  • one-room twin C;
  • one-room twin B;
  • two-room twin;
  • two-room double;
  • two-room suite.

Regardless of the number of rooms, each room has:

  • one or two beds;
  • table;
  • satellite television;
  • telephone;
  • soundproofing.

The cost of living per day in a one-room double room varies from 1600 to 2500 Russian rubles. Somewhat more expensive will cost guests to stay in rooms with a refrigerator and consisting of two rooms: a bedroom and a living room with upholstered furniture. For a daily stay in the twin and double categories you will have to pay from 2300 to 3700 Russian rubles.





Double two-room suite is the most comfortable room that the hotel is ready to offer. Intourist Hotel (Brest, Belarus) has only six rooms in this category. The bedroom has a large bed with an orthopedic mattress, in the living room there is modern upholstered furniture (sofa and armchairs). The spacious bathroom has an autonomous water heater, shower, bidet, toilet and bath accessories.

The cost of daily accommodation in the luxury category is from 3300 to 3800 Russian rubles.





Accommodation in quadruple rooms

The Intourist Hotel has four Connecticid rooms, which is ideal for families with children. Each room has two beds and a bathroom. A day in this category will cost from 2100 to 3200 Russian rubles.
