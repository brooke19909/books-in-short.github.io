Features of the choice of the type of indoor unit





Before making a purchase of a multi-split system, you must also pay attention to the type of indoor unit, it can be:

  • wall mounted;
  • channel;
  • cassette;
  • cantilever.

The most popular and familiar to the consumer is the wall block, which is selected in 90% of cases. However, you can also find the channel device, which is located under the ceiling, which is why it should be installed at the repair stage. At the same time, hidden installation is achieved.

If you need air conditioning, a 2-room multi-split system can be considered by you. It sometimes assumes the presence of a cassette indoor unit, which also provides the possibility of internal installation. Air is supplied in two or four directions. The cantilever unit can have one of two versions - subceiling and floor. In the latter case, we are talking about a design that is installed vertically on the floor and looks like a battery.
