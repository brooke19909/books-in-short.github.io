Is there a zoo in Sochi? Sochi: all animal shows and tourist reviews

Sochi is one of the largest and most popular holiday resorts in the Russian south. It attracts tourists with its pleasant climate, developed infrastructure and picturesque nature. During the holidays I want to get as many new impressions and positive emotions as possible. Therefore, many are concerned about the question of where you can go with children, is there a zoo in the city? Sochi is a large tourist center, and you can see animals here in several places at once.





Zoo at the sanatorium "October"

Rest houses and hotels often organize on their territory additional entertainment for tourists. The Oktyabrsky sanatorium, where you can visit the zoo, is no exception. Sochi is a very large city, but among all animal exhibitions this is one of the most interesting. The zoo occupies a relatively small area. Moreover, large and rather exotic animals live here in good conditions. If you decide to visit the Oktyabrsky Zoo (Sochi), get ready to meet with kangaroos, leopards, camels, llamas, tigers and peacocks. You can see some other animals here. The landscaped territory of the zoo is suitable for photography, just remember to turn off the flash so as not to scare the animals.

Actual information for visitors

Not only vacationers living in a sanatorium can visit the zoo. Entrance tickets are sold separately to the animal exhibition. The cost of visiting for adults is 400 rubles, for children - 200 rubles. And this is a low price for a resort town like Sochi. The zoo has the following address: Plekhanov street, house 42-44. It is most convenient to get here from the Mamaika railway station on foot. Or on any buses and fixed-route taxis to the stop "Boarding School No. 2" or "Sanatorium" Dawns of Russia "."





Other animal shows in Sochi

Fans of the animal world will be interested in relaxing in the Sochi region. In addition to the zoo at the Oktyabrsky sanatorium, there are other places where you can watch our smaller brothers. In Lazarevsky, the Tropical Amazon Oceanarium operates, in Adler a large terrarium, in Cossack Brod, the ostrich farm Three Sophia. If you want to visit the contact zoo, Sochi will delight you with many small private exhibitions in this category. Animals in such places may not strike your imagination, but you can always touch and feed them. In the contact zoos of Sochi, you can see domestic farm animals, decorative rodents and birds. The main feature of such exhibitions is that it is easy to touch all the pets here, and in some places even hand-feeding is allowed.





Tourists reviews

Many people who have already had time to relax in the Sochi region, advise everyone to go to the animal park at the sanatorium called Oktyabrsky. And this is really good advice to those who ask where to go to Sochi. Zoo reviews are mostly positive. The animals look well-groomed, the area is clean, the children really like it. The cost of his visit is equivalent to the received impressions. Negative reviews can be found about some private contact zoos. After visiting some of them, tourists are dissatisfied with the high cost and low number of animals or poor living conditions.

The oceanarium in Lazarevsky is also liked by most vacationers. According to many tourists, this is a great tour for the whole family. If you choose where to look at animals in Sochi, start by visiting the zoo at the Oktyabrsky sanatorium.
