Hotel Sunrise Grand Select Montemare Resort 5 *: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

The Sunrise Grand Select Montemare Resort 5 * hotel is a unique resort complex which is intended only for adult travelers.

  • in restaurants, as well as in bars you can feel all the charm of local cuisine and have a good time with a glass of your favorite drink;
  • to receive guests, as well as to resolve issues related to departure, the reception desk staff will help;
  • a special service center operates to solve issues regarding accommodation in rooms, as well as to eliminate technical or organizational problems;
  • in all areas of the hotel, except the lobby area, as well as on the street, guests can use free Wi-Fi (the network also extends to guest rooms);
  • It offers luxury apartments of different price categories;
  • You can enjoy all the delights of a sea holiday using the hotel‚Äôs private beach, which is equipped with all the necessary amenities (sunbeds, umbrellas, changing room, toilet, shower, bars);

  • round-the-clock guests are available services of a qualified doctor;
  • if you do not want your suitcases to occupy the free space of the room, then they can be deposited in the luggage room;
  • in the laundry you can hand over clothes that need to be washed or ironed;
  • for personal and rented vehicles car parking is provided;

It is worth noting that the Sunrise Grand Select Montemare Resort 5 * hotel is incredibly conveniently located, because there are many entertainment venues nearby, as well as a short walk from the city center.





Rooms for tourists

The Sunrise Grand Select Montemare Resort 5 * hotel offers its guests accommodation in the following apartments:

  • Superior rooms are a large bedroom adjacent to the bathroom. A balcony is also provided, from which the garden is clearly visible, as well as partially the sea.
  • The deluxe room is a spacious bedroom with a small living area, which is separated by a decorative partition, and also differs in color and interior design. In addition to the hotel pool, the sea will also be visible from your windows.
  • Executive Suite is very similar to the rooms of the previous category, but they have one important feature. Namely - a terrace with its own pool, swimming in which is available to you at any time of the day.

Since all rooms are small enough, you can be offered adjoining apartments to relax in the company. There is also the opportunity to settle in a room that is decorated in a romantic style especially for honeymooners.

The most appreciated are the rooms in the Sunrise Grand Select Montemare Resort 5 *. Prices for accommodation in them range from 5,000 rubles per night in standard apartments to 20,000 rubles or more for an executive suite. It is also worth noting that the room rate may vary depending on the additional services that your package provides.

Room Amenities

In the rooms of the hotel Hotel Sunrise Grand Select Montemare Resort 5 * (Sharm) you can use the following amenities:

  • LCD TV connected to a satellite dish (mainly music channels are broadcast, there are no Russian programs);
  • every 4 days a minibar is filled with refreshing carbonated drinks (drinking water is provided daily);
  • the bathroom has a shower, bath accessories, hairdryer, as well as a cosmetic set;
  • for communication with the service center and foreign calls there is a telephone;
  • the rooms can use free internet;
  • a safe is provided for documents and valuables;
  • there is an individual air conditioner with which you can warm or cool the air temperature in the apartment;
  • There is a small balcony, and rooms with a terrace also have a private pool and garden.

Catering establishments

Service and maintenance

A lot of services and entertainment awaits you at the Sunrise Grand Select Montemare Resort 5 *. Tours include the free use of the following services:

  • good nutrition, as well as unlimited drinks in bars;
  • wireless network (except lobby);
  • entertainment program from full-time animators and guest artists;

Among the paid services, the following are most in demand:

Additional Information

The following rules apply at the hotel:

  • The estimated hours regarding registration and eviction from the hotel are 14:00 and 12:00, respectively;
  • Children who are under 16 years old are not allowed in the hotel;
  • extra beds in the rooms are not provided;
  • at the corporate order it is possible to discuss the provision of discounts;
  • it is possible to pay by bank cards of popular payment systems.
