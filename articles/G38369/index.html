Hotel Anda Beachside Hotel 4 *, Thailand: description, services, reviews of tourists

Phuket is one of the most popular resorts (and island compatibility) throughout Thailand. It is located in the western part of the state. It is here that tens of thousands of tourists come every year to enjoy a beach holiday and amazing nature. This is where the wonderful Anda Beachside Hotel 4 * hotel is located. However, its location and other nuances can be described in more detail.





Briefly about the hotel

Anda Beachside Hotel 4 * is a relatively new hotel. It was built 10 years ago. And it was reconstructed in 2012. So it looks modern and new.

Special attention should be paid to the place where the hotel building is located. This is Karon Beach. It is the longest on the whole island of Phuket. Local people and visiting tourists love Karon Beach for its clean golden sand and clear sea. It is an ideal place to relax.

And in the immediate vicinity are all sorts of attractions. Such as the Karon night market, Suwan Khiri temple, Plaza Kata shopping center and many other interesting places.

At Anda Beachside Hotel 4 *, guests can use everything that is available here. And there are a lot of things. Free Wi-Fi, such as a 24-hour reception, a luggage storage room, as well as an ATM and concierge services. A chargeable shuttle service and food delivery service are available to the apartment. By the way, if guests wish, they can allocate a non-smoking room. An elevator is also available for the convenience of guests. The building is multi-storey, so it helps out, especially if guests took a lot of things with them.

The staff is polite and pleasant, but speaks only English and Thai. Therefore, it is better to take a phrasebook with you on vacation.

Helpful information

What else is worth knowing about this hotel? Perhaps an important nuance is that you can not live here with children. Only adult travelers can be registered as guests. Knowing this condition, those people who dream of a quiet and secluded vacation come here mainly. By the way, there is no animation here, so peace is ensured.

You should also know that upon arrival guests will be charged a security deposit. This is done in case there are suddenly unplanned costs. Usually the deposit is 500-2000 Thai baht. And another thing: when making a remote reservation, you will need to provide your credit card details. The hotel accepts American Express, Visa and Master Card.
