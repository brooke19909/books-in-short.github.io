Hotel "Moscow-Podolsk"

Judging by the reviews of tourists and travelers, one of the most worthy hotels of Podolsk (Moscow region) is considered to be "Moscow-Podolsk", located on the street. Clement Gottwald, d. 1/42. No wonder the hotel has 3 stars - it offers guests comfortable rooms and good service.

Single room is 12 square meters. meters will cost a tourist 2600 rubles, and this price includes a hearty, tasty breakfast. Travelers positively note this service, they say that all the food is varied and fresh. In addition, you can order something from the special children's menu in the on-site restaurant.

Also, tourists consider its convenient location, almost the very center of the city, an advantage of the hotel. From here you can quickly get to anywhere in Podolsk itself, and to Moscow.

This Podolsk hotel offers free check-in / check-out on an accelerated system, parking for private vehicles, Internet access. The room has everything you need for a comfortable stay: a modern soft bed, telephone, refrigerator, bathroom with shower and hairdryer.

Guest reviews speak of cleanliness in the hotel itself and in the rooms, every day they clean the room. I also like the fact that smoking is prohibited throughout the hotel, which is very important for families with young children and people with respiratory diseases. Based on their opinions, it can be concluded that the hotel fully justifies its "star" status, and those who settle in it will not be disappointed.
