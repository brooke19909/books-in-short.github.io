How much are Russian coins?

Collectibles

According to some criteria, coin collecting is divided into three groups: weather jacket, commemorative coins, collecting by variety.

Pogodovka - collecting coins with a distribution by year of issue. Now there are special magazines to which a list of coins is applied to simplify the search.

Commemorative coins - this is the most massive collection direction available to any citizen of the Russian Federation. It is not necessary to have reference books on numismatics and a magnifying glass to distinguish a jubilee coin from a regular one. Their release is timed to some anniversary date or a significant event. Another plus of this direction is that it is not necessary to know how much the commemorative coins of Russia cost. In the year of manufacture and in the next couple of years they can be purchased at face value.

Collecting by variety is a rather narrow direction in which knowledge in numismatics is necessary. Some coins have factory defects, or they are issued in a very small batch. This raises their value hundreds of times.

How to determine the value of Russian coins?

If you do not want to use the services of a specialist in numismatics, you will have to arm yourself with a magnifying glass, a directory of factory defects and visit several exchanges or auctions. Each of these resources has its own price, but they differ, as a rule, not too much. To determine how much Russian coins cost from your collection, you will have to devote a lot of time and patience. Leave yourself room for bargaining. If you are going to sell, then slightly increase the price, 10 percent. When buying, respectively, understate. Also do not forget about shipping costs.
