Introducing Marta multicookers, reviews and criticism

The British company for the production of household appliances "Marta Trade Inc. LTD" offers its customers a whole collection of new-fangled devices - multicookers. At the moment, twenty-eight copies are presented on the official website of the corporation. Agree, the lineup is quite impressive. So what are Marta multicookers?





Reviews collected from various sources are very unambiguous. The bulk of consumers were satisfied with the unit. There are, of course, claims against the manufacturer, but there are not very many of them, in fact. Let's start in order and consider the pros and cons, discuss the disadvantages and advantages.

Perfect combination

The bulk of the goods of this brand sold in our country is collected at a factory in St. Petersburg. Many skeptics are sure that there is nothing good in this, but, based on an analysis of reviews, we can conclude that, in addition to the fact that the devices themselves are assembled quite high quality, the price is quite affordable.

What makes Marta multicookers stand out? Customer reviews are unambiguous, this is the perfect combination of price / quality / design. For relatively little money, you get a cooking appliance that has a stylish look, original color schemes and convenient controls. Moreover, the most expensive units equipped with 3D heating cost less than five thousand rubles. In fairness, I would like to say that analogues of other brands reach 30,000 rubles. Is it worth it to overpay for a big name, of course, you decide.

Baby Casserole

The minimum volume of the inner bowls is three liters. The company has only two such representatives; these are the MT-1965 and MT-1970 models. With such dimensions, the manufacturer equipped multicookers with a weak heating device with a capacity of only 500 watts, which, of course, greatly affects the speed of cooking.

Sterilization

The manufacturer equipped a good half of its units with such capabilities. These include MT-1970, 1939, 1981, 1965 and many others. A great addition for families with small children. Separately, I would like to stipulate the MT-1964 model, which has this function, with a bowl volume of 5 liters, but at the same time equipped with only three cooking modes (cooking, heating and a double boiler). The power of the unit is about 700 watts, and the average price is 1500 rubles.
