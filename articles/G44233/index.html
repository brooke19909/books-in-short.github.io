The Golden Lake hostel, Altai Krai - overview, description and reviews

Two kilometers from the Altai village of Artybash, on the right bank of the most beautiful Lake Teletskoye, one of the oldest and most famous camp sites, Golden Lake, is waiting for its guests. The local population reverently calls it Altyn-K√∂l.

The complex is located on the coastal terrace of the northern part of the lake. High, rocky steep shores and velvety tender greenery of this taiga region conquer at first sight.





Historical reference

Legends claim that in 1927 Vladimir Shemelev, a passionate tourism lover, discovered the place of the future Golden Lake tourist complex when he was looking for a parking lot and made a route map for guests from the capital and Novosibirsk. He prepared the glades, and found future personnel from local residents. And after a couple of days the first tourists came here.

A year later, the base is already known as the official campground for groups sent to the Altai Mountains by the Russian Tourism Society.

By the beginning of construction, the complex shifted slightly east, closer to Artybash.

In 1937, the tourist complex was called "Artybashevsky Tourist House".

After the war, the base is empty for a long time. But someone suddenly remembers the beautiful town of wooden buildings. And by 1952, it was being thoroughly updated and expanded. There is a new hotel complex, administration building, perkarni, kitchens and a large dining room.

By 1956, vouchers were issued to the base. They include a comfortable lodging for the night, a dining room with a terrific menu, a bathhouse, a station and boat rental, as well as qualified guides.





By the beginning of the 60s of the last century, thanks to a large reconstruction, the base has already received more than three thousand tourists. The boat fleet and equipment were significantly replenished, and a river boat was also purchased.

At the same time, the children's complex "Bear Bear" appears at the tourist complex. Young trackers spend all summer in these places, studying, having fun and relaxing.

By the end of the 80s of the last century, not only lovers of active leisure activities, but families with children who prefer a quiet and comfortable vacation, already arrived at the base.

Description

Today, the tourist complex "Golden Lake" can simultaneously accommodate 315 people. The main places at the base are 235, the rest are organized at the request of vacationers. Guests are welcomed in a two-story comfortable building, a well-maintained cottage and numerous summer houses. In the cold season, heated rooms with extra beds accommodate 216 people.

Families with children, newlyweds, various companies, whether friends or employees of the same office, feel themselves here with pleasure and comfort.

The territory of the recreation center is covered by Beeline, Megafon and MTS.

Rules of residence

Nine o'clock in the morning - checkout time, check-in starts at 11 in the morning.

No pets allowed.

Children under five years of age can stay at the base for free (without providing a berth). Guests under 12 years old are offered discounts on excursions and a bed.

You can rent a room at the recreation center "Golden Lake" for at least two days and no more than 14.





Number of rooms

The hotel buildings and houses of the complex are located in the shadow of centuries-old spreading pine trees, cedars, slender firs and weeping birches. Often beautiful landscapes with admiration are described in their reviews by people resting here.

Among this beauty, the recreation center "Golden Lake" offers guests several types of apartments. The price range is great: from one and a half thousand for a suite to 350 rubles for a double summer house per day.

These rooms are located in the cottage. They are designed for two guests, but if you wish, you can add two more (folding sofa). The suite has two rooms on two levels: the living room below, the bedroom above. The apartments have comfortable furniture, a bed for two, a modern TV, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a dining table with soft chairs, a kettle and a microwave. The toilet room is equipped with a washbasin, toilet, shower. Breakfast is included.





Junior Suite

Standard +

Infrastructure

On the territory of the tourist complex "Golden Lake" a large swimming pool with a cafe is available for guests. Near it there is a large summer, central recreation area (with drinks and pancakes). A boat station and a pier, two saunas (each for six people), a sports ground with a rental point for all kinds of sports equipment, kiosks with souvenirs and necessary food are waiting for guests. A medical unit is open around the clock.

Also equipped with outdoor showers, street toilets, barbecue facilities, bonfires, left-luggage offices and a parking lot.
