Foot size

So, how to knit booties-sneakers on the knitting needles? A step-by-step description will be a little lower, but for now you need to decide what kind of stop the little one has.

If the baby is between the time of birth and six months, the length of its sole is approximately 8-10 centimeters. From six months to a year - 11-13 cm. From a year to a year and a half - about 14-15 cm. Therefore, we take for example the average figures - eleven centimeters.

We knit starting from the sole





  1. To begin with, knit 34 loops of white or flesh-colored knitting needles and knit twelve rows. All the time - only facial loops. Only knit the last edge loop from the wrong side.
  2. Add one loop at the beginning and at the end of the row.
  3. Before and after the two middle loops, add one at a time.

If reconciled, knitting knitting booties with needles, with a step-by-step description, then from the wrong side should do this:

  • Remove one loop, then knit one wrong side.
  • After that, you need to knit with facial loops. The exception is the last two, which should be tied as purl.
  • The increase of loops should be done only in the front row, using the front ones, which must be crossed.

What to do next?

  • After this, you need to knit two rows of faces, using blue threads.
  • Then two loops - facial, using the peroxide method. Just do this not all the way, but only before the beginning of its middle.
  • Then again two front loops and two together.
  • Next, we knit two loops wrong side, without reaching the middle of the row a few loops.
  • Now there are two more purl loops and at the end are two purl loops connected together. This should be continued until half of all the loops are on the knitting needles.

You still have to make a hole to pull in the lace. On the front side, knit a row in this way: two front loops connected together, then one crochet. And so on until the row ends.





Now you need to tie the cuffs in the form of elastic. To do this, you need white threads. Knit according to the pattern: one loop is front, one is purl. And so on until the end of the row. Cuffs are made, approximately, to a width of 10-12 cm. It is no longer worth it. When everything is ready, carefully cut the thread, leaving a small piece for the seam.

The seam should be sewn using a hook. They also knit a neat lace, about 42 cm long. Then they thread it into the hole that was left earlier. In the same way, tie another kedik.

Another way to knit

How else to knit booties-sneakers on the knitting needles? A step-by-step description gives novice moms the opportunity to do their job much easier:

  • Start from the sole. It is made in a circle even, with recesses, or curly (here, at the beginning, 4 loops are typed, and 8 knits finish knitting).
  • On each edge, 60 loops are scored for four knitting needles and 6 rows are knitted with front stitch.
  • Then they make a bend of the sole: loops are gradually selected from the bottom row.
  • A new color of the thread is introduced into the knitting in the front and the loops are divided to work on the heel and toe.
  • Knit with a thread of a new shade 31 loops to form a nose, and then add one edge along the edges of the knitting needles.

Now knitting will be the threads of a new color:

  • It is necessary to knit 11 rows with a 1x1 elastic band.
  • In the next row, knit 8 loops, then the same number of reductions and finish the row by connecting the front loop with the wrong loop.
  • Turn the knit upside down and continue to work with the garter stitch, the side loops must be knitted together all the time.

When the sock is ready, you need to put in the thread of the main color:

  • Now you should knit 10 rows of drawstring with purl stitches.
  • Close all loops, wrap a half-strap in half and process the side seams.
  • From the edge of the sole dial 11 loops on each side with a white thread and 7 loops on the heel. You can tie the base for the foot, making the sides garterly viscous, and the back with an elastic band.
  • Having reached the height of the spout, go to the thread of the main color and knit 10 rows of purl for drawstring.
  • The drawer must be carefully unscrewed, the side seams should be processed and the heel sewn to the toe.

Now you can circle the lace.
