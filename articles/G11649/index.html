Replenishment of the Arrow card: popular ways

The Strelka card was issued to pay for travel by transport in the capital. It is very convenient because it allows you to quickly make a calculation. With its help, the flow of funds for travel is controlled. Cardholders can save up to 20%. But not everyone knows how to replenish the Strelka card. However, there are several proven methods to achieve the result.

Online

It will turn out to transfer money to the card through the official website of the company. On the first page there is a special field where by number you can find out about the balance, as well as top up your account.





For card users, there is an application with which it will be possible to control the balance anywhere. And you can replenish it with a MasterCard or Visa. The minimum deposit is 50 rubles, the maximum is 3000.

Use of payment systems

You can top up your Strelka card using Yandex.Money or Webmoney wallets. If you are interested in the first option, you must use the following instruction:

  • open a personal account;
  • visit the section "Products and Services";
  • the service is located in the category "Transport and Travel";
  • after finding the "Arrow" you need to enter the number and amount;
  • At the end, a payment method is selected and credited.

At Webmoney, replenishment occurs in a similar way. There is no commission for such services.
