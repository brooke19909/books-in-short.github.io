Crafts from the ball, "sausages" do it yourself

One of the most interesting materials for children's creativity is long balls. And given the fact that they are associated with the holiday and fun, then working with them will not leave indifferent any child. And moreover, even adults in this work will find many interesting things for themselves.



Where and how to use

Crafts from balloons will be the perfect decoration for any holiday. You can only imagine a sea party among the huge palm trees hung under the ceiling of fish and bushes of algae, placed on the floor.

Huge flowers will also not leave anyone indifferent. And with the help of them it is easy to create a fabulous atmosphere even at home. You can create a colorful blooming garden right in your apartment, which, of course, is suitable for decorations for any children's celebration.

In addition to decorations, crafts made from a sausage ball can be a wonderful entertainment for a children's birthday, after which they will remain with the child as a memorable gift. All kinds of giraffes, dogs, monkeys, swans, planes and swords will not leave indifferent any child. Such creativity will captivate the children for a long time, and as a reward they will receive original crafts from balls.



Manufacturing instruction

The simplest figure of this material is a sword. For its manufacture, a loop is made from one edge of an inflated long ball and secured, then a free edge is inserted into it, thereby forming the handle of the product. The result will be a real pirate weapon.

All crafts from the ball, "sausages" are made by bending and twisting. So, for example, in order to build a dog, an inflated product is twisted, leaving a nose, then small ears and a neck are formed. Then the remainder of the ball is divided into three parts. The first and third parts should be longer - these are the future paws, they are connected in a circle and fixed, and the middle one is left as the body.

They do the giraffe in the same way, only for it it is necessary to form a smaller nose, paws and a long neck.

In order to make a flower, for example a camomile, you need two long balls. One of them is divided into 5 parts, and from each form a circle that will act as a petal. And the second is the stem and leaf for the flower. The petals are also attached to the base by twisting, while a small core of chamomile should be left from the ball-stalk.



Combining business with pleasure

Making crafts from a sausage ball is a very exciting experience, especially if it's modular designs. Craftsmen manage to create objects of almost real sizes from small elements, such as a chair, a bike or a tree. And although such masterpieces will take a lot of material and time, the result will be amazing.

Given the cost of such balls, you can easily experiment and not be afraid to fantasize. Indeed, in fact, it was in such creative torment that a lot of ways were born of creating interesting figures.
