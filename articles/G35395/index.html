Review of the mobile phone Nokia 225 Dual Sim: reviews, specifications, photos

Need a simple phone to make calls? It is recommended to pay attention to the Nokia 225 Dual Sim. Customer reviews that already use this unit are mostly positive. There are also complaints, however, they are aimed at poor equipment. But there are no complaints about the quality. The Finnish manufacturer protects its reputation, so if you need a reliable device to always be in touch, get this model, you will not regret it! And in order to make it easier to make a decision, the article will provide a full description of the phone.



Equipment

Since the assortment has many different colors of the case, a special encoding may be present, for example, the device is white - Nokia 225 (White) or black - Nokia 225 (Black). Dual Sim - these two words are also in the title. They mean that the phone works with two SIM cards.

The first thing I would like to draw attention to is the color scheme. There are five colors for sale:

  • classic - white and black;
  • bright youth - red, yellow and green.

The buyer will not find any special frills in the external design. However, in general it looks organically Nokia 225 Dual Sim. Reviews of many people indicate a convenient form. It is easy to hold during a call, since the device weighs only 100 g with dimensions of 10.4 √ó 55.5 √ó 124 mm. The screen, as for a push-button telephone, is rather big. Screen Reviews

The screen in Nokia 225 Dual Sim will surprise buyers. Reviews about it on the network owners leave only laudatory. In push-button telephones, a 2.8 ∫ diagonal size is rare. Display resolution - 320 √ó 240 pix. Do not worry about the image being fuzzy or grainy. This is completely ruled out. The color reproduction is excellent, the display displays more than 262 thousand colors. But the viewing angles are not very. When the phone is tilted, the picture darkens noticeably. Brightness and contrast settings are provided. The user can set them to individual preferences. A large number of owners rated the font size. People with low vision can use the phone, as the text on the screen is readable.

Battery Reviews

In any mobile device, an important criterion is the battery life. The manufacturer provided information about the battery that is installed in the Nokia 225 Dual Sim. Responses of indignant owners indicate a mismatch of parameters. The battery capacity is 1200 mAh. The manufacturer claims that in standby mode it will work for about 20 days, when listening to music - 49 hours, when watching a video - 13 hours, with an active conversation - about 20 hours. However, users give other numbers. With an average load, the battery discharges in 2-3 days. And such a battery life is considered low, especially when it comes to devices of the Finnish manufacturer.



To summarize

The Nokia 225 Dual Sim is a device designed for those people who value reliability and quality. In terms of functionality, it, of course, is much inferior to modern smartphones, but the price completely compensates for this. This model is ideal for children or the elderly. The menu is simple, the font is large. There is a camera, but its resolution is only 2 Mr. An excellent MP3 player is installed to listen to music. Internet access is provided through the Opera browser. Children will be able to play games on a colorful screen. The volume of the speakers and the microphone is excellent. And all this can be purchased for 4000-5000 rubles.
