Sudak, hotels with a pool, with meals by the sea

One of the main questions for vacationers who arrive in Sudak is finding a hotel with comfortable living conditions. Some tourists have an impressive budget for their trip. "Villa Fellini"

Where to stay in a city called Sudak? You can find a hotel with a pool near the sea by going to the Villa Fellini resort. The latter is one of the most chic and expensive places to stay in the city. The hotel is located just a few minutes walk from the sea coast.





The hotel offers its guests comfortable rooms that meet modern standards, as well as a large swimming pool. The vast majority of the hotel rooms have a balcony with a panoramic view. Among other amenities, it is worth noting televisions with plasma screens in each room, small but quite roomy refrigerators, and electric kettles. Bathrooms have a separate bathtub and shower.

As for food, the hotel has the same name restaurant "Fellini", where guests are offered dishes of Russian and European cuisine. Buffets are regularly served here. The local bar serves refreshments and small snacks. In the immediate vicinity of the complex are numerous cafes and shops, with which you can also organize good meals.

The Hotel Villa Fellini is located at 92 Surozhskaya Street. To get here from the central bus station of the city, you need to overcome some 3 km. The nearest train station is 40 km from the hotel. If you wish, you can arrange a transfer by car or minibus, having previously contacted the staff of the institution for help by phone.

"Imperial"

Overlooking Sudak, hotels with a pool, it is worth paying attention to the resort complex called "Imperial". It is located only one kilometer from the famous Genoese fortress. You can find it at the address: Pogranichnikov street, building 1.





Why is it worth going here, coming to Sudak (Crimea)? Hotels with a swimming pool, saunas, private and free parking, restaurants - these are just a small part of what Imperial offers its guests. Amenities include air conditioning, a large plasma TV, separate showers and bathrooms in each room. Among other services, it is worth noting free Wi-Fi throughout the complex, as well as the possibility of ordering a car transfer from Simferopol Airport.

"Grandee"

Continuing to survey Sudak, hotels with a pool, it is worth noting the Grand resort complex, which is located 400 meters from the city water park and the central promenade. You can find it at the following address: 15 Fireynaya street.

The hotel consists of several separate buildings. The choice of guests presents 44 modern deluxe and junior suites. Each room of the hotel has:

  • spacious balcony with panoramic views;
  • TV with cable channels;
  • free Internet;
  • air conditioning;
  • refrigerator;
  • bath and shower.




In the immediate vicinity of the large outdoor pool, which is located in the courtyard of the institution, there is a bar and restaurant. The complex offers massage rooms.

Astarta

Where else is it recommended to stay when coming to Sudak? Hotels with a pool by the sea always attract vacationers who do not limit their means. One of the best places of the presented plan is the Astarta resort complex, which is located near the Genoese fortress. You can find the institution at the following address: 1a Morskaya street.

It offers guests - luxury rooms, as well as VIP-apartments with a unique design. All rooms are air-conditioned. Each room has a TV with satellite TV, a bathroom, and other amenities.

The hotel provides customers with buffet meals, which is included in the room rate. There is a restaurant on the territory of the complex, the assortment of which is represented by dishes of European cuisine.





On the ground floor of the resort complex there is an indoor pool with free admission for guests. The first floor of the hotel is fully occupied by a sports bar and billiards club. On the roof of the establishment there is a spacious terrace with sun loungers where you can sunbathe and enjoy the picturesque views.

"Bastion"

Where else is it worth settling when arriving in Sudak? Hotels with a pool, which are located directly on the coast, are also a resort complex "Bastion". It is located 150 meters from the sea, not far from the Genoese fortress. You can find the hotel at 3 Ushakova Street.

The complex consists of 5 buildings, which feature modern, comfortable rooms. In the holiday season, it can accommodate up to 250 guests.

The institution offers customers meals to order. Local chefs prepare meals for guests, taking into account individual wishes.





In the courtyard of the resort complex there are several large outdoor pools with sun loungers. Nearby are gazebos for relaxing. On site there are restaurants, saunas, bars and cafes.

"Teremok"

Finally, I would like to consider mini-hotels of Sudak with a pool. One of the most popular and relatively inexpensive establishments of this kind is located on Spendiarova Street, 2 km from the central part of the city.

Teremok Guest House offers rooms for up to 4 people. Each room has air conditioning, refrigerator, TV. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

Meals can be arranged independently, using the kitchen with all the necessary dishes. The inn has a grocery store. If desired, you can eat in cafes and restaurants located near the mini-hotel.





In the courtyard of the establishment is an outdoor pool. At the exit from each room there are tables at which you can relax in between bathing. There is also a grill area.
