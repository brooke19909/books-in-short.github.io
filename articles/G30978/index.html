The recreation center "Boyarsky estate", Ulyanovsk: advantages and disadvantages

Ulyanovsk is a multifaceted and very interesting city. He glorified Russia all over the world with such names as I. Goncharov and N. Karamzin, A. Kerensky and V. Lenin. Many attractions of these places are associated with great names. These are monuments and libraries, memorial complexes. But getting to know history is not the only entertainment in these places. If, after sightseeing, you want to have a good time and have some fun, then welcome to the complex called Boyarskaya Manor. Ulyanovsk is a city of incredible beauty. This also applies to its surroundings.



Location

It is best to relax in a place remote from the hustle and bustle. This is exactly what Boyarskaya Manor is like. Ulyanovsk is a relatively small city, so you will have time to explore all the interesting places, even living in some distance from it (40 km separates this tourist complex from the city center). For motorists, this is not a distance at all.

The exact address: Staromaniysky district, p. Sadovka, where the "Boyarskaya Manor" is located. Ulyanovsk is a very hospitable city that you will feel very well when you first cross the threshold of this hotel. Every tourist is welcome here, no matter what time of day or night he settles.





origin of name

Many tourists are interested in the question, why is it the Boyarska Manor? Ulyanovsk carefully preserves its history and traditions, which is reflected in its modern appearance. In fact, these places have been favorite boyars since ancient times. They sailed on ships to get to the fair. While there was a brisk trade, the boyars were having fun and relaxing, drinking wine and fishing. Times have passed, but the attractiveness of the recreation center has remained. Now you have the opportunity to visit this marvelous northern corner.

Main advantages

What is the difference between the Boyarskaya Usadba camp site (Ulyanovsk). First of all, the advantages of these places are nature itself. Amazingly beautiful places, the cleanest air, the complete absence of the destructive effects of civilization are combined with comfortable living conditions and numerous options for recreation. Every tourist who leaves the tourist base says he will definitely return.

The camp site is replete with reserved corners and rare plant species. Regardless of your preferences, you will definitely find here an option of relaxation to your liking. Hunting grounds and fishing camps will be an ideal leisure for men. The fair sex can go to the forest for mushrooms, berries or nuts. Accommodation for tourists

A special charm is added by the fact that the base is located on the shore of the Staromainsky Gulf. There are all conditions for a relaxing holiday. For tourists there is a three-story hotel with cozy and comfortable rooms. On the first floor there are five two-room apartments, and on the second ten one-room apartments, which are designed for two people. And if you want to enjoy the view from the window, then the third floor will be an ideal option. Large two-room rooms are located here.



Separate houses

For those who love comfort and coziness, separate houses will be a very good option for the whole family to relax. There are 14 of them at the recreation center. These are real chopped huts that organically fit into the surrounding reality. The houses belong to the VIP class accommodation option. They are located in a stunning birch grove and stylized as a Russian village. Here you can relax your body and soul.
