A trip to Kazan in September: tips for tourists

General information about the city

This is the capital of Tatarstan, which has many reasons for pride. This city has the status of the place where the World Summer Universiade was held. In addition, this is the venue for the World Aquatics Championship, as well as the Kazan athletes are also quite famous. Important historical and cultural monuments of the city can be found in the list from UNESCO. Kazan does not need to be advertised, since it is clear to everyone that there is something to see here. Almost after the formation of the region (the Republic of Tatarstan), Kazan gained significant success in the field of tourism. Today, more than one million people visit the capital of the republic every year.

Weather in September

If you want to buy tours to Kazan in September, be prepared for cold weather already in the first days. In addition, you can expect even a slight frost.
