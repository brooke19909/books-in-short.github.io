MTS call detailing: how to order a service

Modern technology has entered our lives thoroughly and deeply. Any student today has a smartphone or a regular mobile phone. Such devices, of course, are extremely convenient and provide opportunities that people could not even dream of about ten years ago. However, using them is always a certain monetary cost, often rather big. In this article, we will examine what MTS calls detailing is and why it is needed.

How to control your expenses?





It often happens that users of mobile operators receive unreasonably high bills. There are known cases when amounts are written off in excess of the subscriber's annual income. Sometimes this happens even with people who haven’t called anywhere and haven’t talked to anyone. Of course, such a situation can hardly be considered normal. You can avoid all kinds of ridiculous or erroneous claims of mobile operators by ordering a service such as call detailing. Nowadays, it is provided for free. It can be used by every cell owner.

What is drillthrough?

This one-time service allows a specific user to receive the most detailed information about their connections, text or voice, with other subscribers. MTS call detailing is a great way to find out where and at what time calls were made. In addition, the table will show what kind of SMS the owner of the SIM card sent. This kind of information can not only help avoid overpayments for using the connection, but also plan your personal budget. After all, finding out how much of it goes into conversations and the Internet will be very useful in any case.

How to get information in the salon?





MTS subscribers have two ways to obtain this kind of information. You can go to any company’s salon of this operator and make a request by indicating the required period. However, you need to know that such a service is provided only after the applicant has presented a passport. The operator girl will provide you with detailed information. The service itself is absolutely free. But in the event that you want to receive information on paper, you will have to pay a printout. At the moment, this service costs about 70 rubles. in one month. If the information is needed for a certain period, payment is made at the rate of 3 rubles. for one day (prices for 2013). However, this method of obtaining information is used mainly only in the most extreme cases. There is another way that most MTS subscribers prefer.

Retrieving information over the Internet





So, how to make MTS call detailing still? There is a much simpler and more convenient method. Information about your own conversations and SMS can be obtained even without leaving home. To do this, just go to the operator’s official website. On this site, you should find the button "Internet Assistant". In order to follow this link, you just need to fill in your phone number and password in the form. In the event that you do not have a password, you can get it easily and without extra time. To do this, dial on your mobile a certain combination of numbers and symbols (* 111 * 25 #). You will receive an SMS asking you to create your password. It is sent as a response to this message.

After you go to the “Internet Helper”, you will need to click on the “Details of conversations” link and indicate the length of time for which you want to receive information (no later than six months). As a way to send an extract, it is best to choose an email address which is indicated in a special line. You don’t have to pay anything. In any case, as stated by the operator on the site. The information will be received by you in the form of a letter with a file attached to it.

Additional cost information

MTS call detailing is not the only service that can be obtained in the Assistant. The operator provides users of its site with a number of useful additions. For example, by clicking on the “Cost Control” link, you can get information about the money spent on calls, SMS, MMS and the Internet, as well as all sorts of receipts to the account with an indication of the time. Such a report may be ordered no more than once a day. This service will allow you to control your spending and more rationally distribute your personal or family budget.

Useful advice





The site mentioned above is quite understandable, and users usually don’t have any difficulties finding one item or another. Therefore, any owner of a laptop or computer will be able to fulfill such a request as detailing calls to MTS. However, it may be useful for older people or not too confident users of modern electronics to know in advance that the “Internet Assistant” button is in the upper right corner. It will be easy to notice her. Before requesting a password, you need to connect the “Assistant” itself. Of course, only if you have not connected it yet. To do this, dial * 111 * 23 #.

Modern operators provide their users with a huge number of useful and necessary services. Detailing MTS calls is one of them. Using it at least occasionally is certainly worth it. Moreover, it is completely free.
