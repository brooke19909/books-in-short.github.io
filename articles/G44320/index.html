Turkish Kemer, Salim Club. Hotel reviews

If you ask the Russian people where they had a good rest, or what place they would advise their friends to visit in the summer, 50% would immediately answer: "Of course, Turkey, Kemer." Club Salima is a real gem of this resort, because in such a hotel it will be good for everyone: couples with young children, and young people, and those hard workers who just want to relax from office busy everyday life (sleep late, eat from a belly, lie down on the beach or pool). Hotel Club Salima is located on the Mediterranean coast, in the village of Beldibi, which is ten kilometers from the famous resort.



By the way, now the full name "Salima Club" (Kemer) sounds like Nurol Club Salima HV-1. What do these last two letters with a number mean? That this is a club hotel with a large territory, equipped as a park garden, and that vacationers live in cottages. The number 1 implies a category - thus, according to the usual scale of hoteliers, the Nurol Club Salima can be compared with the Quartet. Indeed, the territory of the hotel is large - as much as 84,000 sq.m. But this is not just a huge area: it is a huge botanical garden! What plants you will not see there: palm trees, oleanders, various coniferous and citrus fruits ... And among this green and fragrant splendor there are small two-story cottages. But what do vacationers themselves say about this hotel?

Kemer, Club Salima: room reviews



The guests called the room fund quite worthy. The standard rooms are small but very clean. There is a TV with Russian-language channels, a minibar with paid content, a safe, air conditioning, a hairdryer in the bathroom and heated floors. The guests said that the majority of the rooms have separate beds, the only minus is that they should book a French bed in advance. Towels are cleaned and changed in the rooms every day, and bed linen - twice a week. In addition to standard facilities, there are family suites (with a balcony or terrace) and separate two-story villas.

Kemer, Salima Club: nutrition reviews

The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. You can eat from 6 in the morning until 1 in the morning both in the main restaurant and in the bars on the beach, by the pool and in the lobby. Large selection of meat, fish, vegetable dishes. Breakfast includes fresh orange. Free alcohol - only locally produced, but very worthy of red wine and rakia. Truly a huge selection of alcohol in the disco bar. Drinks in it - for a fee. Also, a visit to the Cavaliere Sea-Food Restaurant, where a la carte is served, is not included in the price of the ticket.

Kemer, Club Salima: animation reviews

At this point, the hotel does not have a single negative response. A team of 18 people tries from morning to late evening to make everyone have fun: both children and adults. And they do it absolutely unobtrusively and tactfully. There is a mini-club for young guests (4-12 years old), sections for teens, shows in the evenings. For those who want to play sports, all conditions are created: tennis courts, darts, volleyball court. And those who want to relax are offered a hammam and a sauna with a jacuzzi.
