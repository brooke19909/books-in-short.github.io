Holidays in Sochi. Beaches: general information





This city is actually one of the favorite holiday resorts. This fact can easily be justified in just a few words: wide beaches, good service and lots of entertainment.

The total length of the coastal zone of the resort reaches 146 kilometers, while there are a lot of sanatoriums and boarding houses along its entire length.

Perhaps, it is not surprising that the beaches in Sochi (both ennobled and wild) are quite numerous. In total, there are 131. Here, 101 rest areas are private. They are carefully guarded and maintained.

30 places, including the central beach of Sochi, are classified as urban, admission to them is free, and that is why they are very popular both among the local population and among numerous guests.

Fans of secluded relaxation, who believe that the best beaches in Sochi are, as they say, sections untouched by civilization, will also not be offended. There are also wild recreation areas where you can light bonfires, set up tents and park cars.

What services do beaches provide in Sochi?





The resort areas of this city gained their popularity thanks not only to clean and wide beaches, but also to a fairly well-developed hotel and entertainment infrastructure.

In modern pensions and hotels, everything is done for the comfort of guests and their safety. So almost everywhere you can rent umbrellas and deck chairs. In fact, if you should go somewhere to rest in Russia, it is in Sochi. The beach "Riviera", as well as "Lighthouse", "Little Mermaid", "Swallow" and many others are ready to take vacationers the whole season, from the end of May to October.

Along the coast there are eateries, restaurants and bars for every taste and budget. Here are concentrated establishments that are at any time ready to provide their guests with cuisines from around the world that are not inferior in quality and taste to the best restaurants on the planet. Prices, however, will be much lower here.

For those who prefer an active pastime, there is a wide range of water activities, from riding on an inflatable banana to diving and family vacations in a modern water park. Paid beaches in Sochi sometimes offer massages or refreshments, which are included in the price.

Beach of the village 73rd kilometer





If we talk about resorts, the beaches of Sochi, wild and semi-desert, it is simply impossible not to remember. Calmness, solitude and vast expanses - all this combines the coast of the village called "73rd kilometer". This territory is located in the center of Greater Sochi and stretches for more than 1.5 km with a width of up to 50 m.

A gentle entry into the water, without any differences, and a large number of breakwaters contribute to a safe vacation with children in an ecologically clean place with stunning views of the young mountains of the Caucasus.

There are no bars and restaurants, there are never crowds of vacationers, and that is why the beach is considered a favorite vacation spot for local fishermen.

Beach "Swallow" (microdistrict Mamaika)





This recreation area is open to visitors throughout the daylight hours. In places, its width reaches 50 meters, while its length is 300 meters.

Everyone who visits this territory will agree that such a small size makes it easy to keep the coast clean, while a small pebble beach and a gentle slope into the water will delight swimmers who can splash around in the azure waters of the sea. Those who wish can have a meal in the cafes, bars and dining rooms located on the promenade, as well as rent the necessary beach equipment.

Beach of the sanatorium "Fazatron" (microdistrict Mamaika)





Holidays in Sochi can not even be imagined without this territory. Vacationers know that every day, without holidays and weekends, from 8:00 to 20:00 you can visit the beach belonging to the Phasatron sanatorium.

Its length is 400 m, and the width reaches 10 meters. On this beach you can find everything you need to relax: a variety of cafes, bars and canteens, rental points for beach accessories.

In general, it should be noted that the beaches of Sochi, wild, public and private, are cleaned regularly. The sea is also being cleaned, therefore, ideal conditions for a comfortable pastime are maintained on this shore.

The beach is small pebble, and this automatically means that young mothers with children and elderly people spend summer days there with pleasure. Beach resort "October"





This beach, unlike many others in Sochi, is paid and belongs to the Oktyabrsky sanatorium. You can get there from 8:00 to 20:00.

There are advantages here. Firstly, it is the presence of a service comparable to the leading European resorts. Secondly, the beach area is completely fenced and well-guarded, so you can not be afraid for the abandoned property, boldly enjoying the sea and relaxation.

Also on the beach there are many shops with souvenirs, bars, restaurants, and points that rent sports equipment.

The beach itself is small, only 200 meters long and up to 20 wide. The beach is strewn with small pebbles mixed with sand.

The descent into the water is smooth, there are no sudden changes, and in some places, wooden flooring has been specially installed for convenience. Such devices and a calm sea are wonderfully suitable for families with children; in addition, lifeguards are constantly on duty at the beach, watching visitors bathing and relaxing. Especially for children, slides and a playground are equipped, which will take little vacationers for the whole day.

Beach Hotel "Dream by the Sea"





Holidays for a completely different taste and budget can provide Sochi: hotels with a beach, resorts with their own coastal territory, a place for campsites. There is absolutely everything here.

For example, the beach at the Hotel "Dream by the Sea" fully corresponds to its name: a quiet and calm zone with a hundred-meter length along the Black Sea coastline and a ten-meter wide.

Attendants are ready to provide vacationers with everything they need for an ideal holiday: a small pebble beach with a gentle entrance to the water is suitable for both young children and their parents.

At the same time, the depth increases gradually, so that you can not worry that you will suddenly find yourself in the depths of the water element.

Entrance to the beach, however, is limited, it is available from 8:00 to 19:00.

The administration is actually glad to every visitor and tries to make the rest the most comfortable. All conditions are created for this: numerous souvenir shops, bars, cafes, a wide equipped promenade and the ability to rent all the necessary beach equipment - all this is now available to all vacationers.

City beach "Little Mermaid"





This territory is considered one of the municipal with a length of 100 meters and a width of 25-30 meters.

The recreation area successfully combines everything necessary for recreation: cafes, bars, points for renting beach equipment. Also, due to the gentle entry into the water with slowly increasing depth, people with disabilities can relax here. Specially for these purposes aerarias were established. You can book excursions, rent sun loungers, umbrellas, scooters and catamarans.

Also given the chance to ride on small boats, go diving and fulfill an old dream, jumping with a parachute.

Beach "Lighthouse"

Probably everyone who is going to relax in Sochi, not to mention the locals, at least once in his life heard of a beach called "Lighthouse".

In the summer months, it is on this piece with a length of 500 meters and a thirty-meter width that the active beach life of the city focuses.

Here you can feel a good rest in Sochi. The coast is strewn with small pebbles, which gently tickles the heels, and a smooth descent into the water contributes to the gradual immersion and getting used to the water. No sharp changes, no strong waves. And all this is surrounded by gorgeous nature.

Along the beach there are many entertainment centers, cafes, bars and even relatively cheap dining rooms. There are also numerous rental points for sun loungers and umbrellas. You can ride on catamarans, "bananas", scooters, "pills". Parachutes, diving, boat trips - these delights of a summer vacation are also available at affordable prices.

Those who like fun slides and clean pools can visit the central water park in Sochi. It offers a lot of entertainment - both for adults and for children.

In addition, there is a mini version of the water park created specifically for children, as well as the Festivalniy summer cinema, where concerts are constantly organized in the summer season and the latest film industry shows are shown.
