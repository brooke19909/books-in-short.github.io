Paradise for lovers



This wonderful place is called accordingly: Tunnel of Love. It is located in the ancient historical site of Klevan, the first mention of which in the annals dates back to the 12th century. For a long time, the lands on which this marvelous natural monument gradually grew up belonged to Polish nobles. Already in the 19th century they moved to the Russian crown. At that time, the Tunnel of Love was not as luxurious as it is today. But even then romantic couples walked with pleasure under the arches of the green tent, and old trees heard passionate confessions, languid sighs, vows of eternal love and fidelity. Natural phenomenon



What is the famous Tunnel of Love? This is a fairly large section of the forest in which trees and shrubs grow so close to each other that they form two continuous walls of branches and leaves. Moreover, you will not see the sky above your head - it is also replaced by green or yellow-red, depending on the season, the ceiling. Moreover, the Tunnel of Love is "built up" by nature in accordance with all the rules of architectural art. The elegant emerald arch in a semicircle rises above 4 kilometers of the railway line connecting Klevan itself with the village of Orzhev.

Good traditions



Local youth, and even older people, in whose hearts a tender love symphony sounds, are happy to come here to dream, chat, enjoy the company of a dear person and beautiful landscapes. Myths and Reality





Naturally, the question arises of how this amazing tunnel was formed. In Orzhevsk, a woodworking plant has been operating for a long time. From Klevani and the surrounding area, workers reached him by rail. They and the clerk-servants cut the branches of trees, cleared the rails from grass thickets. When the activities of the plant fell into decay, the working people became much less, and, as a result, the care for the road stopped. Here the vegetation took its toll, creating a natural "cave". It is worth noting that in winter, after a good snowfall, it is no less magnificent than in the midst of spring or summer. You find yourself in the real kingdom of the Snow Queen or Santa Claus. Great impressions remain for a very long time.

Yes, we have not yet talked about the legends surrounding these places. Of course, they are about love - sublime, beautiful and, as usual, tragic. One of the princes of the Czartoryski dynasty fell in love with a peasant girl of indescribable beauty, with a soul as beautiful as her face. The prince wanted not only to meet his beloved, but also to marry her, to introduce into his castle a respected lady, a full-fledged mistress. But class prejudices did not allow mesalliance. The relatives of the young Czartoryski opposed, and the unfortunate virgin was soon found dead. Then the desperate prince planted in the forest an entire alley of precisely those trees that the lady of his heart loved most of all. The landing began, expanded, and then became a symbolic tunnel.
