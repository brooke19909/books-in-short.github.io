Park "Kolomenskoye": how to get to it

Museum-reserve "Kolomenskoye" is located on the southern outskirts of the capital on the banks of the Moscow River. It has the official status of historical, architectural and natural landscape park. For several generations of Muscovites, this is a favorite vacation spot, and they do not need to explain what the Kolomenskoye park is . How to get to it, they know from childhood and remain attached to this place for many years. This is a favorite place for Sunday rest.

In order to break out of the vast metropolis, it is not at all necessary to make a long journey, it is enough to come to Kolomenskoye. The park, the map-scheme of which is very rich in historical and architectural monuments, in addition to familiarizing with the relics of Russian history, also allows you to simply spend time in nature, in seclusion from city noise and crush. Many city dwellers like to come here on weekends with children.

Park "Kolomenskoye": how to get

The easiest way to come here is by metro. Not far from the museum-reserve are the metro stations Kolomenskaya and Kashirskaya. But they are located at some distance, and to get to the "Kolomenskoye", you have to take a short walk. It usually takes no more than 10-12 minutes.
