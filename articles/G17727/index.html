Sony KDL-40W705C TV: reviews

As many potential buyers note in their reviews, the market for 40-inch LCD TVs is crowded, but choosing a decent home appliance is not easy. And this applies both to the budget and the middle class. Manufacturers attract customers to their products with external beauty and great functionality, but they simply forget about the playback quality and performance.





In this article, the reader is invited to familiarize themselves with an interesting novelty in the domestic market - the Sony KDL-40W705C TV. Owner reviews did not go unnoticed in the media. After all, an expensive brand is not every day able to compete with budget devices in the same price category (up to 30,000 rubles).

First meeting

Many buyers are still convinced that digital technology must be met "on clothes." Therefore, it is better to start the review with the packaging in which the Sony KDL-40W705C TV is supplied. User reviews claim that even a child will pick up a large-sized box with a device, because the display itself is made of plastic and has a low weight. You can argue on this topic for a long time, so it is better to get acquainted immediately with the advantages and disadvantages of a lightweight body.





The lighter the TV, the less requirements there are for wall mounting, and there will be no problems installing on your own. On the other hand, a huge screen will have less stability, and it is easier to drop it on its back. Many users are sure that cheap components are installed in the lightweight TV case, for some reason everyone forgets about modern technologies. Just look at the market for mobile technology, where lightweight laptops are several times more expensive than heavyweights.

It is not for nothing that advertising compares healthy human sleep with a Sony KDL-40W705C Black LED TV. There are no questions regarding the appearance of the product. Even among the reviews, the reader will not be able to find a negative for the TV design.

As for the build quality, if we omit the claims of users regarding the low weight, then the buyers also have no questions. Although the assembly of the TV was done in China, it was done in a high-quality way - in Japanese. Unless the remote control somehow does not correspond to a high-quality product. Users often complain about dangling buttons and constantly moving contacts in the battery compartment.

Sound subsystem

The owners have no questions about the acoustics that are built into the Sony KDL-40W705C TV. As for the audio output for connecting headphones and speakers, here the manufacturer tried to surprise even the demanding music lovers who decided to purchase the Sony KDL-40W705C. Multimedia support

For buyers, the fact that the Sony product supports video playback with MKV and Xvid codecs was a big surprise. For a decade, the Japanese have resisted free software and finally surrendered by introducing the Sony KDL-40W705C LCD TV to the world. User reviews for multimedia differ. Work on the Internet

The presence of a Wi-Fi module and the ability to use the device in the access point mode is good, but this is not the functionality that any TV should have, including the Sony KDL-40W705C Smart LED. Owner reviews in the media negatively reacted to the operation of the wireless module. When playing FullHD video over Wi-Fi, braking often occurs that can ruin the entire viewing experience. As for the Wi-Fi Direct function, here the owner is waiting for a catch. The fact is that wireless Internet is distributed only according to the 802.11n standard, and the data transfer speed leaves much to be desired.

There are no questions about the work of the TV on the wired connection. All owners are pleased with the ease of connection and data transfer speed. In their reviews, users detail all the advantages of setting up and running SmartTV, a browser and a program for viewing Internet video (Youtube).

Users are also talking about useless applications that the manufacturer equipped Sony KDL-40W705C. Additional TV features

Scheduled TV recording is one of the few features that is still in demand by many customers. Video can be saved to any removable drive connected to the Sony USB port of the Sony BRAVIA KDL-40W705C TV. The positive reviews are quite understandable, however, there are recommendations from experts regarding the operation of the TimeShift function - it is better to record the broadcast channel to an external hard drive or a fast memory card. The fact is that the TV tuner perceives the slow flash drive as inoperative and pauses the recording without notifying the user about it.





The manufacturer advertises the work of the built-in Skype program, but its implementation is incomprehensible to many buyers - one-way video broadcasting looks very strange. A sleep timer, child protection, DLNA support and many other less useful features are also present on the TV, but few owners use them, so they did not fall into the focus of this article.
