How does the Visa payment system work. Payment systems Visa and Mastercard

A payment card is present in the wallet of any modern person today. When it is issued in many banking institutions, they will offer you a payment system of your choice: Visa or MasterCard. For the correct solution, we find out what are their main differences. If you ask a question to an employee of a financial institution, basically the answer follows: "In general, there are no significant differences." To understand the nuances, you need to consider how the Visa and MasterCard payment systems work . Knowing their functional features will give a person the opportunity to make purchases without leaving home and without wasting his personal time.





If you use the cards of these systems without going abroad, then they do not have big differences. However, if the card is used in different countries of the world, then some features are detected.

What is the difference between a Visa plastic card and a MasterCard?

Payment systems are very similar to each other, but have some differences. If you look at the geographical possibilities of using bank cards, you can see: Visa stands out because it is popular and in demand in almost any corner of the world. Its share of activity in the world is about 57%, which is a very high indicator.





However, the second in terms of system indicators - MasterCard, are distributed by approximately 27%, and a large share falls on the countries of Europe. It is for this reason that it was believed that the first system is American, and the second is European, although this is not so. Both companies have main offices in the United States. The main currency of Visa plastic cards is the US dollar, and in MasterCard both the dollar and the euro can be used.





If the card is used when traveling abroad or for the purchase of foreign goods, then it is worth exploring the main characteristics and choosing the best option for yourself. But in your country it does not really matter which system you choose to use. However, it should be noted that if you withdraw money from the card in a foreign currency, then a mandatory conversion takes place. This must be remembered so as not to lose money. Well-known payment systems can be used almost anywhere in the world, which makes them reliable and universal.

Features of working with payment systems

If you need to cash in foreign currency, a process such as conversion takes place. This is a transfer of funds from foreign currency to state, in other words, to the currency of the country where you are at a given time. You can find out how the Visa payment system works by reading this article.





If your card was opened on the territory of Russia, then payments on it are made in rubles. Visa payment system is quite simple and convenient. For example, if you need to make a purchase in China and pay in RMB, then the conversion will take place according to the following rules.

Visa (payment system): currency conversion for the payment of goods and services

  • Initially, the required amount of money in RMB will be converted into dollars by converting currencies at the official exchange rate in the Visa payment system.
  • After that, the financial institution receives the required amount in dollars, and then converts it into rubles at its own rate.
  • As a result, the resulting amount in Russian rubles will be debited from your account.

Knowing these rules will help you understand how the Visa payment system works.

Currency conversion in MasterCard system

The currency conversion process as a whole follows the same scheme as described above. But there is one point: in this system, the conversion is not in US dollars, but in euros. The rest of the procedure is identical. That is, we see that there is no fundamental difference when working with these payment systems. When choosing a card for yourself, be sure to consider how often you plan to leave the country, as well as make purchases in foreign currency. It is necessary to proceed from these life circumstances in order to make the right choice of a payment card.

Are Visa and MasterCard popular and why?

Visa international payment system allows you to conduct financial transactions anywhere in the world.
