Choosing the perfect phone for a first grader

When a child goes to school, caring parents think about how to buy him the first mobile. On the one hand, the child will always be supervised, and on the other, will this hinder his studies? Choose a phone for a first grader with caution. The basic principles are quite simple: the device should be extremely simple, on the other hand, it should not have too many functions so that they do not distract from learning. Below we will analyze what to look for when choosing.



Price

First rule: the price of the device should not be too high. You should not take an advanced smartphone, the first year of school does not have the presence of accuracy or a sense of responsibility. A broken or lost mobile phone is a common thing. Moreover, a beautiful and expensive phone can attract unnecessary attention from the upper classes. A mobile phone for a first grader should ensure the safety of the latter, and not expose it to unnecessary risk.

Button or touch?

Now itâ€™s hard to surprise anyone with a smartphone with a huge screen with almost no buttons, and their prices have been falling recently. But does your child need this? Returning to the question of accuracy, we can say that the larger the display, the easier it is to damage it. In winter, again, in gloves, neither dial SMS nor call. Based on this, it can be assumed that a cell phone for a first grader is a device of the classic layout with a small display and a convenient button keyboard.

A bit about applications

What is certainly contraindicated for a student is a gadget stuffed full of modern games, programs and other things that are useless in school. When buying such a device, be prepared that at least a month the child's performance will fall, or even not rise at all. We should also mention the possibility of an Internet connection - take care of disabling this function on the SIM card, as Settings of the phone itself, the baby can easily get around with the help of senior comrades. A telephone for a first-grader should, first and foremost, fulfill its main function - to provide constant communication with parents, and not serve as an entertainment center.

Other characteristics

When buying, pay attention to the battery capacity, it should be at least 1000-1200 mAh. You should not hope that the student will regularly charge the device, and therefore, the longer it can remain active, the better. The camera does not matter (at least for parents), but the presence of such trifles as the keyboard backlight, a non-slip case in the hand, etc. only welcome. It is desirable that the phone for a first-grader was not too thin, as it is now fashionable, but, on the contrary, weighed about 100 grams and slightly pulled a pocket - such a mobile will not be lost, and it will be easy to get it.



What is the result? An ideal choice would be a device with a button keyboard, with a minimum of additional functions in combination with an energy-consuming battery. The mobile itself should be comfortable in the hand, not have sharp corners and withstand small loads on the case. Such a phone for a first grader will allow you to worry less about your child and send him to school with a calm soul. What else do parents need?
