How to make cat ears with your own hands: step-by-step instructions and recommendations

Cat ears are an indispensable part of some costumes for a carnival, a children's party or an adult theme party. Modern shops provide a wide selection of costumes. However, in some cases, it becomes necessary to make cat ears with your own hands. In this article we will consider in detail the process of their manufacture.





Why do you need homemade ears?

Before we go directly to practice, we’ll figure out why a homemade accessory may be needed.

Self-made cat ears are a great option for any children's holiday. They can be made of colored paper or cardboard in order to save. Indeed, because of their age, children quickly lose interest in things, can lose them or get them dirty. The paper version will not be a pity to throw or spoil. Still such ears are good for a family photo shoot.

In addition, their manufacture will be a real pleasure for those who want to express themselves through creativity. A do-it-yourself playful accessory is a unique thing that will give a lot of joy to its owner.

For some costumes, especially designer or themed ones, it’s difficult to find ready-made cat ears. With a little effort and turning on fantasy, you can create an original accessory that blends in with the whole look. Ears made of the same material as the entire costume will look especially beneficial.

What materials need to be prepared?





Before proceeding with the manufacture of ears, it is necessary to prepare the materials that will be required in the process. As a basis, the most common hair band is most often taken. It should ideally fit in size - not to press and not to press. The rim can be left “in its original form”, covered with paint, cloth or fur. To make classic ears, you will also need the following materials and tools:

  • artificial or genuine leather;
  • durable threads to match the skin;
  • plain or corrugated scissors;
  • sewing needle.

How to make cat ears yourself?

First of all, you need to draw the contour of cat ears on the skin. You can start with a regular paper pattern. Then you need to cut the ears themselves with simple or special scissors. In this case, the edges should be very neat.





Further, each of the ears individually is stitched with thread at the base. This allows you to give them a more natural shape and make the product voluminous.





To complete the work, you need to sew the finished ears to the hair band. They should be mounted symmetrically.

What other materials can I use?

In addition to leather, you can use a variety of improvised materials. Most popular options:

  • fur;
  • dense fabric;
  • cardboard;
  • paper;
  • plastic.

The standard bezel that acts as the base can be replaced

  • cloth tape;
  • paper tape;
  • a foam-wrapped wire;
  • strong thread;
  • linen rubber.

How to create a spectacular look with cat ears?

Cat ears alone are unlikely to help create a spectacular look. They, in fact, are its foundation. You can complement the image with

  • suitable clothes;
  • gloves imitating cat's paws;
  • any footwear "in the subject", for example, finished slippers in the shape of paws;
  • masks with a cat's face;
  • cat tail.
