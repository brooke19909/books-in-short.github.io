Spa hotels in Narva, near the border

The small Estonian town of Narva stands on the banks of the river of the same name, along which the border of Estonia and the Russian Federation passes. This town is very quiet and calm, as if it was created for tourists who want to relax on the picturesque shore of the Gulf of Finland and breathe in the purest air of pine forests.





Especially resorts and spa hotels in and around Narva will be interesting to Russian tourists on the eve of the New Year or as a weekend break. Due to the fact that native Russia is very close and there is practically no language barrier, almost the entire population of the town is Russian-speaking. The advantages also include high service and fairly fast Schengen design.

Holidays in Narva

Narva is not the smallest city in Estonia, it occupies an honorable third place after the capital of Tallinn and the city of Tartu. It is the easternmost settlement in Estonia. Visiting the city, you can plunge into ancient architecture and medieval history.

On the banks of the Narva River there is a city museum, once a 17th-century castle, as well as an ancient fortress - a structure built by Danes in the eighth century, and now open to visitors as a museum. It is worth getting acquainted with the building of the town hall, the Alexander Cathedral and the Cathedral of the Resurrection of Christ - the only surviving structure in the Second World War.





But not only ancient sights are famous for the city. In the picturesque surroundings of ecologically clean areas, there are spa hotels in Narva where you can relax, heal and regain beauty.

Ust-Narva, spa hotel "Meresuu"

Meresuu SPA & Hotel, which opened its doors in 2008, is a four-star eleven-story complex of one hundred and nine rooms, various in comfort, including rooms for people with disabilities. Almost all of them have wonderful sea views.





The spa has a private beach and seven different pools:

  • swimming, thirteen meters long;
  • large massage,
  • foot contrast;
  • open pool;
  • jacuzzi;
  • outdoor jacuzzi;
  • Children's swimming pool.

To your taste, you can visit any of the five baths: aromatic, salt, Russian and sauna, as well as a Japanese bath. A gym is always open for active work.

The level of spa treatments here is quite high compared to other spa hotels in Narva, Estonia or even Europe. They include massage, various baths and wellness courses with therapeutic mud, laser treatment, therapeutic inhalations, oxygen therapy, beauty treatments, visiting the salt room, cosmetic wraps, children's massage and much more.

In addition to pleasant, relaxing treatments, excursion tours to the city of Narva are organized from the Meresuu Spa Hotel.

Spa Hotel Noorus

Among the spa hotels in Narva, not far from the border with Russia, can be called Noorus, which opened in 2014.

Located on the seafront, this complex has one hundred and fourteen rooms, among which there are non-smoking rooms, for people with disabilities and allergies. Accommodation for children under twelve years old is absolutely free.





The Noorus Spa Hotel also offers unlimited use of the aquacenter, which includes three complexes:

  • main;
  • VIP oasis;
  • children's sector.

The main sector consists of eight baths, numerous jacuzzis, several pools: a twenty-five-meter swimming pool, a pool with aqua-attractions, as well as a children's and outdoor pool. There is also an outdoor terrace and a bar. You can visit the water center every day from seven in the morning until ten in the evening.

The VIP Oasis is designed for those who are over eighteen years old. It has four saunas, a Japanese bath, a separate bar, a pool with various types of massage and a terrace with views of the Baltic Sea.

For vacationers, there is always a gym, a playground and a restaurant with sea views.
