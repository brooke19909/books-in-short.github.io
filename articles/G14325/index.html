The recreation center "Zarubino", Primorsky Krai: overview, description, rooms and reviews

Zarubino village is located in one of the most picturesque corners of the Khasansky district. The distance by land from Vladivostok to the village is 220 km, by sea - 105 km. The village has a functioning port, its own fish factory, and a railway station.

On the isthmus of Kitovy Bay, there are recreation centers Zarubino, Primorsky Territory, Khasansky District, surrounded by amazingly beautiful landscapes. The untouched nature of these places captivates with its picturesque beauties. Thanks to the influence of the monsoon climate, each recreation center Zarubino (Primorsky Territory) provides comfortable conditions for a full recreation stay.

How to get there

It is easy to get to Zarubino from Vladivostok by public transport. Auth. No. 521 will bring vacationers to the center of the village, then they will independently get to their base.

From Vladivostok to the village of Slavyanka can be reached by ferry, then take the bus to Zarubino.

Those who plan to drive a private car from Vladivostok can, having reached the M60 highway, turn to the village. Kiparisovo, following the signs, turn into Razdolnoye. From Razdolny should follow the A189 highway in the direction of the village. Kraskino. After driving 150 km, you need to turn towards the village. Andreevka and Zarubino. At the fork, turn right. Zarubino will be in 6 km.
