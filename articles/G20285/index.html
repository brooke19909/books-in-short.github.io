Rope park on Krestovsky - a place of rest for both children and adults



Probably, if the guests of the city on the Neva, and the St. Petersburg residents themselves, are asked what associations Krestovsky Island, an amusement park evokes for them, will be the first thing that comes to mind. Indeed, the rides there are simply breathtaking. And just a couple of years ago a rope park opened in the same area. Now thrill-seekers can test their strength and courage. This place is called "High-rise city." Indeed, one gets the feeling that between the trees there is a real city with streets, squares, stairs. But only very courageous citizens can take a walk on it. They overcome various obstacles, moving along wooden platforms and ropes between the trees. Although in reality the rope park on Krestovsky is completely safe. After all, you will be met by experienced instructors who will pre-conduct training, give out valuable instructions and will accompany you along the entire route. Therefore, even courageous children of five years old may well embark on this path of adventure. For beginners, both small and adult, the lower "track" is laid. It is located at a height of 1.5 meters above the ground. But those who are not the first time to overcome such routes, can climb to a height of 7 meters and tickle their nerves. And at the end of all the most exciting adventure awaits you - flying on an air crossing on a roller (troll).


Rope park on Krestovsky - a great place for children's parties, birthdays, graduations. It offers a variety of scripts and programs for the celebration. For children from 7 to 12 years old, an event called "In Search of Treasures of the High-Rise City" is held. And from the age of 12 - "Quest". However, the latter may well be adapted for adults. Therefore, if the head of the enterprise planned an unusual corporate event, then Krestovsky Island, the rope park is a great place for team building. After all, some tasks can be successfully completed only as a couple or as a team - a great opportunity for team building. A holiday in the High-Rise City can last 2 hours, or you can organize a full-day event. And on holidays there are very interesting promotions.

As for the price of visiting, it is the same for both children and adults. A high-rise park on Krestovsky is only 20% cheaper when visiting large families upon presentation of a certificate. For everyone else, the cost depends on the route. The lower "track" will cost less: 300 rubles on weekdays and 400 on weekends. In time, its passage takes about half an hour.

The second option is to go through two routes at once: the upper and the lower. In time it is about 40-60 minutes, and in cost - 800 rubles on weekends and 600 - on weekdays. In addition to passing the route, the price also includes equipment and the work of an instructor. The rope park on Krestovsky also makes it possible to purchase a weekday or weekend subscription. In addition, an additional flight on the troll is possible for 100 rubles, or descent without completing the entire route (200 rubles).

A professional photographer also works on the site, who takes pictures both from the ground and from above. Therefore, the rope park on Krestovsky will remain not only in memory, but also in photographs.
