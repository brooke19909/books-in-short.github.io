LED lamps with battery: shapes, features and reviews

LED lamps were introduced not so long ago and gained their popularity. They attract buyers with their strikingly small volume, if you buy them in tape format, ease of placement (the consumer can put them wherever he wants) and relatively inexpensive cost. In this article we will consider specifically LED lamps with a battery, reviews about them and summarize the corresponding results.

LED lamp shapes

Even the most fastidious buyer will be able to find exactly the format of an LED lamp with a battery that he will like. On the market they are presented in various forms: circle, square, rectangles. Some of them are presented as an "independent car", acting as a night light or an ordinary light source.

Various variations of the switches are also presented: buttons, lamps, responding to the application of force to them, that is, to pressing, and others. Since the LED lamp has a battery, you do not need a cord and a socket. Some LED lamps are presented in the form of ordinary bulbs equipped with a battery. They are usually used for emergency or emergency lighting.
