Halkidiki, Sani Beach - luxury and comfort!

"Sani Beach" hotel (Halkidiki) is a luxurious hotel complex with its own sandy beach. Its part is also Sani Resort. The last time the reconstruction was carried out in the winter of 2013. The hotel is 80 km from Thessaloniki International Airport (45 minutes by car and 8 minutes by helicopter). An ideal vacation here is sure to be spent with families with children, youth and businessmen.

"Sani Beach", Halkidiki. General description of the complex



The hotel territory unites 4 hotels, 17 bars, three SPA centers, 17 restaurants, 11 equipped conference rooms (with a maximum capacity of 700 people), three gyms, a kids club, a nursery, playgrounds, a club for teenagers. Heliport available , harbor. The hotel is open from April to October.

Halkidiki, Sani Beach. Accommodation

Each room is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, wireless Internet access, minibar, safe, telephone. For extra comfort, the room features a Nespresso coffee machine and coffee and tea making facilities. Bathrooms include a hairdryer and bathrobes. Total number of rooms - 394. The following room types are available:

  1. Double room with beautiful garden view. There is a balcony, terrace, modern bathroom with shower (tropical rain function). It offers garden views.
  2. Double room with sea view. This type is similar to the previous one, but has a bewitching view of the sea.
  3. Standard room with garden view (35 sq. M.). The rooms have a balcony, bathroom, bedroom with living room. Additionally slippers can be provided. The balcony offers garden views.
  4. Standard room with sea view. Like the previous one, it differs from the view from the window.
  5. Room with panoramic sea views (40 sq. M.). Vacationers are provided with a balcony or terrace, a bathroom with a shower, a bedroom with a living room. Additionally slippers are issued. From the balcony you can see the sea.
  6. This family room has a garden or sea view.
  7. Family Suite with Sea View There is a balcony, a living room, a well-equipped bathroom, a bedroom. Great sea view.
  8. Two-room family suite with wonderful sea views. Living room with a spacious dining area, two bathrooms, two bedrooms. Sea view.
  9. One-room family suite with direct coastal access. At guests' disposal there is a private garden, a nice living room with a dining area, a beautiful terrace, a bathroom, a bedroom. There is direct access to the beach.
  10. Two-room family suite with direct access to the coast (105 sq. M.). Magnificent terrace, private garden, two bedrooms and bathrooms, beautiful living room. Exit to the beach.

Halkidiki, Sani Beach. Nutrition



A brief description of some restaurants:

  • Grill Bythe Pool Restaurant serves Greek, international and Mediterranean cuisine. Located by the pool.
  • Poseidon Restaurant - International and Mediterranean cuisine.
  • Ammos Bar - Restaurant-bar on the coast.

Halkidiki, Sani Beach. Sports and Entertainment

Vacationers can usefully and happily spend time on one of the six tennis courts, a basketball court, a five-a-side football field, and a beach volleyball court. Everyone has the opportunity to attend courses at the football academy, to attend classes in aerobics, yoga, Pilates and water aerobics. The hotel has 2 water sports centers (canoeing, catamarans, windsurfing, surfing, motor boats, parasailing, pedal boats ). Every year, the hotel organizes a festival on its own territory with the participation of famous chefs, and in July and August, an art festival is held here, during which art exhibitions and concerts by stars are organized. The hotel area is so vast, and the services are so diverse that you can be sure: the upcoming vacation in Halkidiki, "Sani Beach", will be the best in your life!
