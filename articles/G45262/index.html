Do-it-yourself electronic load: circuit. Home-made electronic load on a field effect transistor

In order to check the power supplies there is an electronic load. This device works on the principle of signal generation. The main parameters of the modifications should include the threshold voltage, permissible overload, as well as the dispersion coefficient. There are several types of devices. In order to understand the loads, it is first recommended that you familiarize yourself with the circuit diagram of the device.

Modification scheme

A standard load circuit includes resistors, a rectifier, and modulator ports. If we consider devices of low frequency, then they use transceivers. These elements work on open contacts. Comparators are used to transmit the signal. Recently, stabilizer loads are considered popular. First of all, they are allowed to be used in a direct current network. They have a quick conversion process. It is also worth noting that an amplifier and regulator are considered an integral element of any load. These devices are closed on the lining. They have a fairly high conductivity. It is the modulator that is responsible for the generation process in the models.





Types of modifications

Distinguish between pulse and programmable devices. Laboratory ones are suitable for a separate category, which are suitable for powerful power supplies. Also, the modifications differ in the frequency with which they work. Low-frequency loads are equipped with transistors with a channel adapter. They are used on AC power. High-frequency models are made on the basis of an open thyristor.

Impulse devices

How is pulsed electronic loading done? First of all, experts recommend choosing a good thyristor for assembly. In this case, the modulator is suitable only for two phases. Specialists say that the expander should work alternately. Its operating frequency is required to be approximately 4000 kHz. The transceiver in the load is installed through the modulator. After soldering the capacitors, it is worth doing an amplifier.

For stable operation of the load, three channel-oriented filters will be required. A tester is used to test the device. Resistance should be approximately 55 ohms. With an average load, a home-made electronic load produces a rated voltage in the region of 200 watts. Comparators are used to increase sensitivity. With system short circuits, it is worth checking the circuit from the capacitor. If the resistance at the contacts is underestimated, then the transceiver must be changed to a capacitive analog. Many experts point to the possibility of using wave filters, which have good conductivity. Regulators for these purposes are used on the triode.





Programmable models

Electronic programmable load is fairly simple to assemble. For this purpose, an expansion transceiver of 230 V is used. Three contactors that depart from the transistor are used to transmit the signal. Regulators are used to control the conversion process. The most commonly used are linear analogues. The triode is used with an insulator. In this case, a blowtorch is required. Directly, the resistor is fixed on the transceiver.

For the model, conventional comparators with a low dispersion coefficient are not suitable. It is also worth noting that many make the mistake of installing one filter. For normal operation of the Prior only capacitive analogues are used. The rated output voltage should be approximately 200 V with a resistance of 40 ohms. If you assemble devices on a single-junction expander, then linear models are not suitable.

First of all, the device will not work due to the large overload of the thyristor. It is also worth noting that the model will require a line modulator with low sensitivity. Some specialists use stabilizers when assembling. If we consider a simple modification, then an adjustable type is suitable. However, inverting elements are most often used.

Laboratory Modifications

Do-it-yourself laboratory electronic load is assembled with a powerful thyristor. Resistors are used with capacities from 40 pF. Experts say that capacitors can only be used expansion type. Particular attention during assembly should be paid to the modulator. If you use a wired analog, then for the load you will need three filters. A simple electronic load has a phase-type modulator with conductivity from 30 microns. The resistance is approximately 55 ohms. It is also worth noting that the loads often add up to a switched transceiver. The main feature of such devices is high ripple. In this case, the conductivity is provided at around 30 microns.
