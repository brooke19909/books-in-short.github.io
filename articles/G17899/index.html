Pension "Rainbow" (Anapa, Dzhemete): description, reviews

Rest in Anapa is an ideal option for most Russians. Fairly inexpensive, when compared with a vacation in Sochi, but at the same time quite comfortable compared to most small resort towns. That is, it is ideal for most Russian citizens. The Rainbow boarding house (Anapa) is especially distinguished, which we will discuss today.



Location

Pension "Rainbow" (Anapa) is located in one of the most beautiful corners of this resort town. This area is called Dzhemete, and it is a real gem of the Black Sea coast. Fame among tourists and a large influx of guests stimulated the development of this resort area, turning it from a small seaside village into a center of entertainment and recreation.

Just five kilometers from the resort center - and you find yourself surrounded by golden dunes. Nature has created all the conditions for a wonderful holiday with the whole family. For those who love the hot sun, beaches and dunes, where there is nothing but golden sands and sunshine, will be an ideal option. If you have children, then they will probably be more interested in a shallow and very warm sea. And if older people come with you, they will be very pleased with the location of the guest houses, which allows you to get to the beach in a matter of minutes.



Short description

Pension "Rainbow" (Anapa) is located in the center of Pioneer Avenue, near Dzhemete, just 400 meters from the sea, in the area where Anapa 's best sandy beaches are located . Judging by the reviews of tourists, this is the perfect place to relax with children. There is a flat sandy bottom with a gradual increase in depth. However, the territory of a small but very cozy guesthouse deserves attention. Your attention is invited to a green landscaped guesthouse with gazebos and benches, as well as a children's playground.

For family holidays

We have already mentioned who most often visits the boarding house "Rainbow" (Anapa). Reviews say this is the perfect place for parents and children to relax. A fenced green area provides an opportunity for safe games in the fresh air, a well-equipped playground will allow you to throw out all the energy that has accumulated over a long year. At the same time, attentive staff, comfortable rooms and a full range of services will allow you to devote time to relaxation, without being distracted by everyday life.



Guest Accommodation

And again at the height of the boarding house "Rainbow" (Anapa). The reviews tell us that tourists are accommodated in one- and two-story buildings, as well as in separate cottages that accommodate six rooms. Since this is a family-type boarding house , the foundation of the fund is double and triple rooms. Let's dwell in more detail on the features of each of them. The double economy room includes a bathroom and shower, TV and fridge, as well as air conditioning. The cost of this number is from 600 to 1200 rubles per person per day, depending on the season. The price includes accommodation and three meals a day with elements of a buffet.

Be sure to come here to see how hospitable Anapa can be! Pension "Rainbow" offers prices quite affordable for this region, so you can not even try to look for cheaper. If you have a large family, then choose a triple standard. In the room - the same set, it is a refrigerator and air conditioning, a bathroom and shower, as well as TV. The cost starts from 850 at the beginning of the season and reaches 1,500 rubles in July. The price also includes meals.

Finally, there are triple rooms in cottages. Here, on the ground floor, single and double rooms are offered, and on the second floor are family apartments. The cost of living starts from 760 rubles per person (May, October) and then the price rises until July, reaching 1,500 rubles. Considering that the beach season lasts from May to October, you will not lose anything by buying a ticket not in the middle of summer. Total pension is designed to accommodate 150 guests.



For the most demanding guests

Any tourist with open arms awaits the boarding house "Rainbow" (Anapa). Room comfort can satisfy the most demanding guests. Some rooms of this category have a balcony from which you can admire the beautiful nature. You can choose from a variety of options - double, triple or quadruple, depending on the number of travelers who have gathered at sea with you. However, the most popular is the comfort double room. Pension Rainbow (Anapa) has very good reviews. People with children are happy to come back here, and the option of renting a room with an extra bed is perfect for a young family. In addition, all rooms of this category are equipped with the following amenities: a bathroom with a shower and a fridge, a TV and a split system, as well as a safe and hairdryer. Cozy and comfortable, they will be a great place to spend your vacation.

Additional places

This is great news for those looking for a budget vacation in Anapa. Pension "Rainbow" offers your children a vacation with their parents, in one room, with extra bed. Children under two years of age without a separate seat and without food can stay for free. If your child is from 2 to 10 years old, then a ticket to him will be provided with a 50% discount. Finally, if you want a child older than 10 years (or another adult) to live in your double room, in addition to parents, in this case an extra bed is paid in the amount of 70% of the ticket price. Extra bed price includes buffet breakfast.

Today, tourists have to somewhat limit spending and choose options for a budget holiday. Therefore, the boarding house puts forward special offers that will allow you to relax with great comfort and for reasonable money, while taking a lot of walk around Dzhemete. "Rainbow" is a boarding house that takes care of its guests. The offer ‚ÄúMother and Child‚Äù is an inexpensive family vacation for one adult and two children. It offers guests

We will not even touch upon the wonderful excursions that await you in Dzhemete. "Rainbow" is a boarding house that pleases its guests with cordiality and warmth. Upon arrival, you will find a personal guide who will show you the territory and introduce you to the schedule. Here guests can enjoy their own dining room with integrated three meals a day, a sauna with a pool, billiards and table tennis, a tour desk and a guarded parking lot.

Infrastructure

Judging by the guest reviews, it is never boring and there is always something to do. If you want to relax, then welcome to the bath complex. An oriental-style sauna will become a real fairy tale for you after a busy day. There is a large pool for children and adults. Its length is 15 meters and its width is 8 meters. The water is fresh, there is one slide and a small children's section. However, it does not heat up and works seasonally. For a perfect end to the evening you are waiting in the cosmetology and massage rooms. In addition, a Japanese restaurant and pancake house, a coffee shop and an entertainment complex for children and their parents are paid.



Beach vacation

First of all, they come here just to get plenty of fun and sunbathe. The beaches here are equipped, as if in European resorts. Throughout the territory covered with golden sand, there are umbrellas and deck chairs, various awnings. There are locker rooms and toilets on the beach, and if you would like to have a meal, then welcome to the cafe or bar, where you will be treated to refreshments. The distance from the hotel to the beach is approximately 150 meters. And you can choose whichever you prefer - the city's small pebble beach or purely sandy.

How to get to the boarding house

If you decide to visit the boarding house "Rainbow" (Anapa), the tour operator will happily take on all the organizational work. You just have to enjoy your vacation and not worry about anything. One of the most famous operators working in this area is RCC Travel. Leave a request on the site and indicate your requirements, and then the manager will contact you and make some suggestions. It remains only to choose options and book a room for a certain number. The questions of delivery to the boarding house in this case are also decided by the tour operator himself, you do not have to think about how to get to the place.

Traveling alone

However, you can completely do it yourself, the benefit of information today is more than enough. Moreover, it is impossible not to find such a famous resort as Anapa. Pension "Rainbow" (Pionersky Prospekt, 74) - this is the heart of the resort town. You can get to it from Anapa Train Station. This is convenient if you travel by train. So, by minibus number 100, you follow to Anapa bus station, and then transfer to minibus number 128, 114 and 134 and go to the stop Dynamo.

Let's look at other options. If you arrive at the airport by plane, then by minibus number 100 you follow to the bus station, and then - according to the specified scheme. Finally, from the Tunnelnaya station, you can transfer to the Tunnelnaya-Anapa bus, and then by the listed routes. Transfer is possible by prior arrangement. Judging by the reviews of tourists, to get even without the additional services of a tour operator is not difficult. In the resort area, finding someone who will tell you the way is very easy, because most of the local residents are somehow related to the tourism industry. You will surely enjoy your stay in Anapa. Pension "Rainbow" will be remembered by all members of your family and will leave the most pleasant experience.
