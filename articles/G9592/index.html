Hotel Centara Grand Beach Resort & Villas, Krabi - overview, features

Thailand has always been a popular tourist destination. Thousands of travelers come to this picturesque region every year who want to enjoy beach activities and get new experiences.

Of course, many good hotels are concentrated here, but now only one will be told. And this is the Centara Grand Beach Resort & Villas (Krabi), located right on the seafront.

Location

The hotel is located in the picturesque Pai Plong Bay, which is located in an amazing Thai region called Krabi. The hotel complex has its own 500-meter beach, from the expanses of which there is a magnificent view of the limestone cliffs overgrown with tropical vegetation.

This is a secluded place, but airports are relatively close. The nearest (Krabi Airport) is about 20 kilometers away. Phuket International Airport is 60 km away.

The luxurious hotel like Centara Grand Beach Resort & Villas (Krabi, Ao Nang Beach) offers the highest level of service. The infrastructure of the hotel complex is thought out to the smallest detail - not one of the guests will need anything. Here is what is available on the territory:

  • Free high speed wifi.
  • Secured private parking.
  • Transfer from and to the airport and the surrounding area. A service is available for booking tickets for public transport and taxis.
  • Car rental.
  • 24/7 front desk.
  • Individual lockers.
  • Luggage storage.
  • Currency exchange point.
  • Excursion bureau.
  • Providing the services of a private baby sitter.
  • Laundry, dry cleaning, ironing. Trouser pressing and shoe shine services are available.
  • Rooms equipped with modern technology for various events.
  • Business center.
  • Shops on site.

The hotel also provides VIP services, a special service for the bride and groom and for people with disabilities. Everyone can also use the room service (delivery of food, drinks and newspapers to the apartments).

Staff

If you study the reviews left about Centara Grand Beach Resort & Villas 5 * (Thailand, Krabi), you will notice that everyone responds with special warmth to the work of people working in the hotel. Their level of training is especially impressive - the staff speaks 13 languages! And among them, of course, there is Russian.

And they are very attentive to the guests here: after arrival they give out cold water and chilled towels, clean apartments and change linen daily, supply them with high-quality hygiene products (shampoos, gels, lotions), even give out sunscreen, beach bags and mosquito repellents. In general, they do everything so that the guests do not need anything.
