The phone does not turn on beyond the screen saver. How to deal with it?

In our world, people can no longer imagine life without a smartphone. He became an extension of our hand. For most, breaking a favorite gadget will cause panic and tantrum. This, of course, is not very good, but you have to put up with it. Modern mobile devices are very moody things. Sometimes they behave unpredictably. Possible causes of failure

There are several reasons for this smartphone behavior. And depending on them, a decision on repair will be made. The first (and most common) reason that the phone does not turn on beyond the Android screensaver is a failure during the installation of an operating system update. This happens because the Android OS is not very stable. This is not so bad. However, the same result can be obtained after the device falls from a fair height. This is much worse.





Also common causes may be a malfunction of the battery, charger, or the connector itself for charging the gadget. Now let's figure out what to do if the phone does not turn on further than the screen saver. In most cases, you can fix it yourself. Fatal cases of breakdowns are extremely rare. But at the initial stage, the main thing is to correctly diagnose the problem in order not to make mistakes in the future.

System crash

This is the safest of all that can be. If the operating system crashes, you need to go into recovery mode and reset the factory settings with clearing the cache. Be careful! After this procedure, there will not be a single third-party application installed by the user. You can enter the recovery by simultaneously holding down the volume button and the power button. True, for different models of devices there may be different combinations. Then you need to go to the Wipe data and factory reset item and confirm your choice. Then you need to check Reboot system now. After that, the phone will reboot. If everything is fine, then the reason that the phone does not turn on beyond the Lenovo splash screen was because of a system failure.





Sometimes resetting to factory settings does not help. Then there is another option. You just need to reflash the phone. This is not so difficult to do. The web is full of instructions for each specific smartphone. And in any adequate forum you will be happy to help you implement your plan. Do not be discouraged.

If the cause is battery and charging

This can be verified very simply. Just find somewhere like a battery and a similar charger. If, having replaced all the components, you did not see the changes, then the reason that the HTC phone does not turn on further than the screensaver is much deeper than you thought.
