Cherepovets hotels: addresses, reviews of tourists and photos

Cherepovets is the largest city in the Vologda Oblast. The village is located at the place where Yagorba flows into Sheksna. The "City of Metallurgists" annually attracts thousands of businessmen and tourists. Cherepovets hotels will provide a warm welcome to all city guests. You can find accommodation options for every taste and budget.
