Making a simple angel

Let's try to create a simple flat bead angel souvenir. The weaving scheme of this product is based on standard bead knitting techniques, so there is nothing complicated in the manufacturing process.

Having carefully examined the step by step photos, you can understand how this little angel is created.

To begin with, 5 beads or beads are collected on the wire (for a larger product). Further, another 4 is drawn through the method of cross-weaving through both ends. The following rows are weaved similarly, decreasing the amount of beads by one with each step.





After the body is created, proceed to weaving the wings. The required amount of beads of a different color is strung on one end of the wire (in our case 15). Passing the wire through the penultimate row, we form a loop. On the second side, repeat the procedure. So we got angel wings.





To the antennae formed at the top of the product, we attach a round large bead that will serve as the head. On one end of the wire we string beads of the same color as the wings, forming a halo. Carefully fasten and cut the ends of the wire. That's all, a simple angel is ready.





I would like to note that this is a very easy weaving with beads. Schemes for beginners are simple, although the result in most cases is very impressive. Various techniques are used. So, some products are woven, starting with a halo, others - from the torso. Naturally, the figures differ in size, because the amount of beads used is regulated by the master.
