Bar "Cuba Libre", Rostov-on-Don: description, photo, address

One of the most visited places in Rostov-on-Don is the Cuba Libre bar. The conditions presented in it will certainly be interesting to representatives of different generations. You can visit it for different purposes: to spend a sincere evening with your family, meeting with friends or work colleagues. Here, according to vacationers, there is always a laid-back atmosphere of fun, which harmoniously combines with deliciously prepared food, kindly served by the waiters. Some citizens visit this place from the very moment of its opening - since 2015.





Location

There is a bar "Cuba Libre" in Rostov-on-Don on Krasnoarmeyskaya street. Not far from it are objects such as the Astor Gallery, as well as the state circus. From here you can very quickly get to the Gorky park, where families with children and couples spend their time.

Many visitors prefer to get to it using public transport. Near the entrance to the bar there is a bus stop ("Prospect Budennovsky"), as well as a tram ("Circus").

Interior

The owners of the Cuba Libre bar in Rostov-on-Don note that the main goal of their establishment is to give the guests who are within these walls the opportunity to feel the spirit of complete freedom. The arranged interior allows you to completely relax, easily chat with your friends, spend time calmly with your family or even make new friends.





The interior of the hall is decorated with bright paintings depicting the inhabitants of Liberty Island, as well as its natural landscapes. Here everything is arranged in a masculine style, many details are quite massive in appearance, they are made of stone, leather and dark wood. The interior is represented by a harmonious combination of black and brown colors, which are diluted with bright details of Cuban paraphernalia. Here on the walls decorated with brickwork, you can see posters with the image of the great Cuban leader, as well as flags of this country. In one part of the bar there is a counter, behind which young bartenders work. As visitors note, in the Cuba Libre bar (Rostov-on-Don) they can surprise you with the most original signature cocktails, which have no analogues in the whole city. Near the counter there are several tall chairs with soft seats made of red leather, which can accommodate visitors.

As for the places for guests, they are represented by wooden tables and brown leather sofas. For small companies, the hall has tables with wooden chairs.





Kitchen

The menu of the restaurant is small in size and rather concise. It presents several options for each type of dish, all of which are loved by guests. In the proposed list there are several types of inexpensive breakfasts that are in great demand. They offer classic scrambled eggs, as well as scrambled eggs with different types of additives (tomatoes, onions, sweet peppers, mushrooms, etc.). If desired, guests can take the opportunity to order a comprehensive breakfast called "Oriental" - it includes shakshuka, toast with cheese and a hot drink.

The menu has several options for snacks, including both cold (herring with potatoes, a plate of cheese with honey and nuts, assorted fresh vegetables and herbs), and hot (cheese balls, Texas roll, chicken wings BBQ, " Chicken Fingers "). Among the large groups of guests, the assortment of hot Cuba Libre appetizers is widely popular. Among the salads there are four types: Salad Nicoise with tuna, Greek, Caesar with chicken, Cuba Libre.

Hot soups (champignon cream soup, borscht, chicken noodle soup) and burgers (Chicken Burger, Beef Burger, Club Sandwich) are also served at the Rostov-on-Don Club. Among the main dishes, several options are presented, both fish and meat (baked pork ribs with potato wedges, chicken schnitzel with french fries, beef steak with pickled eggplant salad, chicken skewers and baked tortilla, mackerel with tomato and lemon )

For fans of sweet dishes there are several options for desserts (chocolate fondant with raspberry sorbet, cheesecake, Tiramisu). A number of desserts also feature signature ice cream and fruit platter.

Bar

The bar's map is quite large, it offers a wide variety of coffee and tea. It has a selection of energy drinks, juices, fruit drinks and soft drinks. Among the alcoholic beverages there are several varieties of vodka, tequila, cognac, whiskey, rum, gin, vermouth, as well as champagne and wine.





Separately, guests are invited to familiarize themselves with the cocktail list. All the positions presented in it are divided into categories, depending on the country where the cocktail was invented. Guests can be surprised by the creations of Russian (Antonio, Gregory, Theater, Barbarisk, Peter the Great), American (Whiskey Soir, New York Suar, May Tai, Bronx) , "Zombies"), Asian ("Singapore Sling", "Tom Yam", "June Bag"), French ("Fornication Marie", "Experiment No. 1", "Red Dress", "Cyrus Royal", "Eldelflower Spitz "), Italian (Aperol Spitz, Lemon Pie, Negroni, Martina Tonic), as well as Mexican authors (Green Mexican, Chi-wa-va, Margarita, Flirt Margarita "). In addition, it has two or three drinks created by bartenders from countries such as Ukraine, Spain, Austria, Germany, Belgium, Romania, Portugal, Finland and the United Kingdom.

Entertainment

Every day in the halls of the Cuba Libre bar (Rostov-on-Don), lively music plays, which, combined with excellent service, creates an atmosphere of complete comfort. Many visitors to the institution in their reviews call it laid-back. Here, events are often held related to the tasting of new types of cocktails or snacks. On weekends, everyone can watch show programs for which talented dance, music and vocal groups are invited.

Often, guests of an institution watch broadcasts of important world events right in the bar room. To do this, on its walls there are several plasma TVs and one large projector.
