Rios Beach Hotel Certified Municipal (Turkey / Kemer): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Kemer is a center for outdoor activities and entertainment in Turkey. In this area there are a considerable number of resort cities that offer travelers from around the world, including from Russia, a wonderful vacation in an exotic atmosphere.

Very often, everyone who wants to relax is cheaper, tour operators recommend going to Rios Beach 3 *. In the ranking of Kemer (Turkey) hotels that offer budget holidays, it often takes leading positions. As for the ratings of tourists posted on different sites with reviews, very often this place receives 8-8.5 points out of a possible 10, which, in principle, is a very worthy indicator.



general information

Rios Beach Hotel Certified Municipal was built in 1994. Almost from the very moment of construction, order is maintained on its territory. The entire room fund of the hotel is located in a large building of four floors, and the main infrastructure is concentrated in it.

As for the total area, it is almost 5.5 thousand square meters. m. There are several places for recreation, including for children.

The administration of Rios Beach Hotel Certified Municipal recommends visiting this place for families who want to relax with their children - for this there are all the necessary conditions.

Location

The hotel is located in Kemer, in the Beldibi resort area, where night and day life is actively boiling. Not far from it is the Mediterranean coast and the beach, which tourists often visit.

Arriving in Turkey, future guests of the Rios Beach Hotel Certified Municipal Hotel begin their journey across the land from the airport, which is 38 km from the vacation spot. In order to facilitate the movement of tourists from the point of arrival to the hotel, operators must include a transfer in a standard package.

Rooms

The total number of rooms at Rios Beach Hotel Certified Municipal is 113. All of them are located in one building, from one part of which you can see the courtyard, which has a beautiful garden and a large pool, and the other has picturesque surrounding spaces. All of them are presented in one category of comfort - "standard", but tourists claim that the living conditions are excellent.

Each guest who comes here can use individual bath accessories, a set of which consists of toilet soap, shower gel and shampoo. The bathrooms have a shower, a toilet and a washbasin. A hairdryer is also provided for use.

In order to improve the air temperature in their room, guests can use the air conditioning in the apartment.

Interior

All apartments are presented in a small area - 23 square meters. m. They can accommodate two adult guests and one child, for whom, if necessary, a separate bed is installed, and absolutely free of charge.

The central place in the furniture is a large double bed, which in some apartments is replaced by two single beds. For personal belongings of vacationers, there is a wardrobe, the surface of which is used to install a TV. The rooms have satellite TV, which allows you to watch Russian-language channels, this is especially pleasing to tourists from Russia, which they often mention in their reviews of Rios Beach Hotel Certified Municipal. They also note the successful decision to provide a free minibar, the range of which is updated every day with soft drinks.

For an additional fee, tourists have the opportunity to use the safe and telephone with the ability to connect to the international line.



Conditions for children

The Rios Beach Hotel Certified Municipal in Kemer is a great place for a family vacation - this is stated by both hotel administrators and guests who relax in it. Such comments are due to the presence of a number of reasons and circumstances due to which the life of this category of tourists is very comfortable in this place for relaxation.

First of all, in all rooms, if there is a child in the family, a baby cot is installed for free. Also for children in the aquazone there is a small pool, which is built according to all safety standards. Most tourists are convinced that such conditions are quite enough so that children can feel quite comfortable here.

Nutrition

Guests of the Rios Beach Hotel 4 * (Rios Beach Hotel 4 *) in Kemer can eat in a large restaurant, which is organized in its main building on the ground floor. According to the all-inclusive system, vacationers can visit the buffet, which is served here every day at certain times. The institution has its own work schedule: from 7 a.m. to 9 p.m.

Also, for lovers of drinks and cocktails, the hotel has two large bars, one of which is located on the beach, and the second - in the aquazone area. Here they can offer not only a drink, but also a good snack. Tourists from Russia prefer local desserts, which after massively respond in the comments, and only positively.



Aquazone

Many reviews of vacationers say that there is a beautiful aquazone, consisting of a rather large pool. whose area is 250 square meters. m. It has a fully automatic water purification system, so it is always crystal. The guests think that the niche is not heated in a niche, leading such conclusions to the fact that in summer the water here is heated by the sun, and in winter the temperature is not enough.

Around the pool there is a beautiful outdoor area where there are several dozens of sun loungers. As a rule, in the morning they are occupied by hotel guests, comfortably sitting under beach umbrellas. Many of them recommend reading their favorite books while in this place.



SPA

The attention of a large number of tourists is attracted by the small spa area, which consists of an excellent massage area. About it a huge number of positive reviews can be found on sites. In them, vacationers talk about the true masters of massage art who are able to immerse any client in a state of complete relaxation. If desired, travelers can visit the huge thermal zone, consisting of two different parts - a hammam and a sauna.

All services that are provided in the SPA center are paid.



Sport

A favorite activity of many tourists who allow themselves to relax in the Rios Beach Hotel (Beldibi / Kemer) is diving. Extreme sports fans often choose it. Its main feature is that classes are paid and must be held under the supervision of an instructor. Other water sports are also offered on the coastline.

If desired, each hotel guest can look into the table tennis room, in which sessions are completely free. It also has an excellent volleyball court.



Infrastructure

In the description of Rios Beach (Turkey / Kemer) several infrastructure objects are presented that can make the life of guests more diverse. Among them, a beauty salon is in great demand among vacationers, where you can not only renew your hair style, but also do pedicures, manicures and some other cosmetic procedures. which are carried out only by professionals with the appropriate certificates.

Often a huge number of guests gather in the walls of a computer club, which has free Wi-Fi. Here, vacationers can not only play games, but also chat with their family and friends via the Internet.

On the first floor of the Rios Beach 3 * (Turkey / Kemer, Beldibi) building there is one large store. It sells souvenirs and other no less interesting goods that tourists from Russia are actively interested in. Next to the outlet there is a currency exchange and ATM, where you can withdraw cash from international bank cards.

Beach

Beach lovers often gather on the coastline, where the Rios Beach Hotel Certified Municipal has its own plot, which, as vacationers note, is perfectly arranged for a pleasant pastime. It is completely covered with small pebbles and has a length of 50 meters.

On its relatively small area there are several dozens of decent and quite comfortable deck chairs made of wood. They all stand under large folding umbrellas that protect guests from the harmful effects of sunlight. There is a special point on the beach where large towels are handed out for temporary use - a small fee must be paid for them.

Several locker rooms, showers and public toilets are evenly distributed across the area, which can be used by hotel guests absolutely free of charge if necessary.



Rules of residence

Tourists staying at this hotel are required to comply with a small list of rules that are available in other places of rest of this type.

First of all, smoking is prohibited in rooms. For violation of this requirement, a fine is prescribed in the amount established by Turkish laws.

The administration does not have the right to register guests at the hotel who arrive with animals - the rules stipulate that staying with them in rooms is strictly prohibited.

The hotel does not offer the appropriate conditions for people with disabilities in the apartments - they are not available. If desired, representatives of this category can settle in any of the rooms, but on ordinary rights.



Prices

The cost of a tourist package in Rios Beach is very low, if you compare it with prices for accommodation in similar places - it is about 35,000 rubles, subject to a week stay here together. It is this circumstance that often becomes decisive in the choice of travelers for a hotel for relaxation. This amount necessarily includes not only a round-trip flight by plane, but also a transfer of tourists from the point of arrival to the place of rest. This price also includes the all-inclusive concept and three meals a day as part of the buffet in the main restaurant.

A huge number of tourists who have been to this place leave a lot of positive comments about the time spent here, sharing their impressions and vivid photographs taken during the holidays. Often they recommend traveling here to their relatives and friends, motivating it not only with low prices set for trips, but also with decent living conditions provided by staff.
