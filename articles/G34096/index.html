How to Unleash iPhone from Apple ID: Practical Tips

All electronic devices Apple in the process of their operation are tied to a special identifier called Apple ID. In this article, we will tell you how to untie your iPhone or iPad from Apple ID, which is a prerequisite before selling it or transferring it to another person.

Apple ID Features

By creating an Apple ID after acquiring a brand new iPhone, you simultaneously get 5 GB of disk space in iCloud cloud storage. In it, all the time while you are using your device, copies of your phone contacts, notes, photos, settings of installed applications and more will be stored.

From the daily copy of your device created in this way in the cloud, you can restore it in case of unsuccessful firmware or software failure. After purchasing a new smartphone, you can also restore it from a saved copy of the old one, transferring all previously installed programs with settings, phone book and other data.



To delete confidential information stored in iCloud, you need to know how to untie your iPhone from Apple ID.

You can disconnect the device from the identifier in different ways, for this it is not necessary to hold it in your hands. Even if you lose your device, you can remotely erase all personal information from it.

Disabling Apple ID

How to untie the iPhone from Apple ID smartphone? Probably the easiest and most convenient option. The device is in your hands, and you can perform all the necessary manipulations directly on it.

Go to "Settings" on your iPhone and immediately find the Apple ID control section. In the latest version of iOS, Apple prudently placed it in a separate menu item, saving the user from unnecessary searches. In this section you will find all the devices that are assigned to the identifier, and you can disconnect them.



How to unbind iPhone from Apple ID using computer? The option is also very convenient. To do this, you do not even need to have a smartphone on hand. You go to the official Apple website and go to the account management section there.

You will need to enter the ID and password from Apple ID, you may need to repeat the procedure several times by clicking on the links. As a result, you will be taken to a web page with your account. Various information about its parameters will be indicated here, and at the very end there will be a list of connected devices. Choose what you need and in a few clicks, turn off.

How to unbind iPhone from Apple ID using iTunes? If this application is installed on the computer, then you can enter the identifier settings directly in it. How to unbind an iPhone from Apple ID using a support call? Apple has a well-developed and responsive support service. Therefore, in an extreme case, when none of the previous options helped, you can turn to it. The only inconvenience that may arise when using this option is the need to correspond in English. But here any online translation service can come to the rescue.

What to do when buying

When buying with your hands, you should definitely make sure that the goods you purchase are untied by the former owner from his Apple ID. In this case, it is better to lose some time to check, but be sure that the device will work fine in the future. Otherwise, you may be left with a very expensive aluminum box in your hands after the first software update.
