Waterpark in Kharkov - real fun for the whole family!

Residents, as well as guests of the city of Kharkov, should definitely visit the water park called "The Jungle", which is located at the address Kyrgyz Street, 19-B. Here, visitors can plunge into the atmosphere of positive emotions and, most importantly, enjoy an active holiday in the conditions of year-round summer. The water park in Kharkov is the largest indoor water entertainment complex in Ukraine. There is no fear of rain or frost.

Jungle Description

Guests will be especially impressed by the stunning facade architecture. The water park looks like an exotic island among ocean spaces. Here everyone will find their little paradise. From entertainment, the Jungle offers visitors a variety of slides and attractions: high, medium, small, extreme or more gentle slopes, where you can ride alone or at the same time with friends. You can also relax and refresh yourself by swimming in the pool with a comfortable water temperature. The benefit of the species presented here abound. For relaxation, use the hot tub or jacuzzi.





Resting in it, you can completely forget about all the problems. To relieve stress and tension, it is recommended to visit the wave pool. Children can have plenty of fun in the play area in shallow water. For fun companies, there is a swimming pool where you can play team types of games. And those who intend to stretch themselves and practice different swimming styles are provided with 6 lanes 25 m long. Here you can buy a separate subscription and visit the pool at a convenient time.
