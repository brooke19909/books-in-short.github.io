Moscow "Metropol" (hotel): description, address

Metropol is one of the most famous five-star hotels located in Moscow. She is one of the symbols of the Russian capital.

Hotel Metropol, Moscow)

Comfortable beautiful rooms of various categories and excellent service are offered by the Metropol Hotel, located in the most central part of the Russian capital near the Kremlin.



The hotel offers magnificent luxury apartments for guests. Moreover, the hotel does not have two absolutely identical rooms. They have completely different decoration and layout.

Each of the rooms has its own personality due to the interior, preserved from the beginning of the XX century, and a variety of antiques. Moreover, the ancient luxury here is perfectly combined with the most modern achievements, providing hotel guests with a comfortable stay.

In the most historical part of Moscow, the Metropol Hotel is located. His address: Teatralny proezd, house number 2.

Everyone who visited the Metropol Hotel directly touched the history of Russia, its culture. The highly qualified staff of the Metropol Hotel provides a wide variety of services to make guests feel at home.



Hotel Metropol is one of the structures that is part of history. It can be called a kind of hotel-museum.

Metropol (hotel): facts from history

After the reconstruction was carried out (1991), Metropol was recognized as the hotel of the highest category. This hotel complex is the first in Moscow working according to international service standards.

In recent years, the hotel has been visited by many prominent political figures of different countries, members of royal families and other famous people, among which the following stars can be noted: Montserrat Caballe, Patricia Kaas, Catherine Deneuve, Michael Jackson, Julia Ormond, Elton John, Stephen Seagal, Arnold Schwarzenegger , Sylvester Stallone, Mila Jovovich, Pierre Cardin, Gerard Depardieu , etc.

All hotel facilities were built about 100 years ago. The individuality of the historical building gives not only a unique layout, but also exquisite decoration. Ancient luxury in a complex with modern comfort is a kind of visiting card of the Metropol Hotel.

Metropol is a hotel with a rich history. Representatives of the elite of society rested and spent their leisure time in pre-revolutionary times, Bolshevik congresses were held here. Art lovers have always been attracted by both the architecture of the building and magnificent ancient paintings.



Hotel facilities

This beautiful historic hotel offers bars, restaurants, a pool, a sauna, a spa, wellness and fitness centers. The hotel offers 24-hour front desk service, luggage storage, a business center, parking, a hairdresser and dry cleaning.

In the restaurant of the Metropol Hotel you can taste wonderful dishes of Russian national cuisine: the famous Russian hodgepodge and cabbage soup, pancakes with caviar, herring and much more. It also features excellent European cuisine and excellent wines.

A wide variety of drinks, desserts and light snacks await visitors at the Chaliapin Bar. In the hotel cafe you can taste delicious cakes for every taste.



Metropol is a hotel representing modern banquet and conference rooms: 10 magnificent halls for business meetings, banquets, weddings, conferences and other events. Of particular note is the hall with a total capacity of 300 people, where the most modern equipment is installed with simultaneous translation into 5 languages.

Rooms

All hotel rooms have the most modern amenities, and guests are provided with a full range of services. Most rooms have: safe, bathrobe, slippers, toiletries, air conditioning, workplace, bath, sauna, minibar, telephone, dining area (in the kitchen), TV, telephone, satellite TV, Wi-Fi.
