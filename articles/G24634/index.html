Ocelot Revolver - who is it?

Metal Gear Solid is a legendary game series. It is quite difficult to find a work that would have such depth as the brainchild of Hideo Kojima. The game is full of tricky plot weaves, symbols, omissions, etc. In this article, we will discuss one of the most controversial characters in Metal Gear Solid. We will talk about the Ocelot Revolver. Who is Ocelot Revolver?





There are many dangerous soldiers in Metal Gear Solid. Revolver (Shalashaska) Ocelot - the codename of a soldier who specializes in torture, interrogation. Another characteristic that distinguishes him from other fighters is the skillful handling of pistols. It is thanks to this skill that he will receive his nickname - Ocelot Revolver. It is also worth noting that this character has telepathic abilities, which more than once saved his life. Adamsk (real name of the character) is one of the main characters in the series. He was one of the few who personally knew Big Boss and his clones. As a rule, Ocelot Revolver acted as a double or triple agent, acting for the benefit of organizations such as the Philosophers, the GRU, the CIA, and the Patriots.

Revolver Ocelot: biography

Revolver Ocelot was born on June 6, 1944 in northern France, Normandy. Adamsky's mother was a girl soldier Joy (later she became better known under the nickname Boss). The birth took place during the operation "Overlord." Joy was injured, so she had to have a cesarean section. Fortunately, the child was not injured, however, after the procedure, Ocelot's mother left a huge scar that stretched across the entire body. As it turned out in MGS3, Ocelot's father was a soldier named Sorrow. It was from him that Adamsk inherited his telepathic abilities. Soon the child was stolen by agents of the Philosophers.

When Adamsk matured, he was placed in the organization of the NSA (National Security Agency). He worked there as a decoder. But soon Ocelot sided with the USSR and began to work in the special forces, led by Evgeny Borisovich Volgin. A little later, Ocelot, thanks to a relationship with the legendary Boss, received his personal squad. In the 60s, Adamsk became a secret agent. He worked not only for the GRU, but also for the KGB with the CIA.
