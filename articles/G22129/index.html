How to sew a baby dress? Or maybe tie?

In the life of every little princess, many events take place, for the celebration of which a beautiful elegant dress is needed. Luxurious and lush outfits today are not cheap. But it can be much cheaper for you to sew such an outfit with your own hands. This article will discuss how to sew a baby dress.





You can sew this outfit as follows:

1) Take off the size of the baby‚Äôs chest.

2) Take the T-shirt and mark on it the bottom of the bodice line.

3) Fold in half in front of the shirt so that the side and shoulder seams are aligned with each other.

4) Attach the fold of the shirt to the fabric. Circle along the desired cut lines.

5) Add approximately 1.5 cm to the seam allowances in the shoulder area and 1 cm to the armholes and sides.

6) After that, fold the product in half, and then circle the back half of the shirt.

7) Add 2 centimeters for the zipper along the line that runs along the middle of the back. But if you want to make a fastener with buttons, you need to add 4.5 centimeters. Pattern is ready. Now it becomes clear how to sew a baby dress. It remains to start sewing on the finished pattern.

You learned how to sew a baby dress. But did you know that you can surprise your child even more by knitting a dress for him?

Knitting dress for children





For this you will need:

1. Knitting needles number 4 circular.

2. 1 skein of thread Anna Twist.

3. 50 centimeters of a thin satin ribbon.

4. Hook number 4 for strapping.

5. One button.

To knit a children's dress, we will use the peacock feather pattern. Decoding of the pattern: rapport is 16 loops + 1 loop for the full pattern.

The pattern must begin to be knitted from two rows of front surface.

Children's knitted dress. Description of knitting blocks

Block 1. First you need to knit 4 loops two in a slope to the left. For this, the knitting needle must be inserted into the right loop of the pair, which we knit. Thus, two knitted loops are obtained on the spoke. Nakida - front - nakida. According to this scheme, we knit until we get six facial loops and six crochets. After that, we cut the loops (4 front loops with an inclination to the right side) and knit 3 loops (2 of them are removed, and one front must be knitted and stretched through 2 removed loops). We continue to repeat this pattern until the end of the row. The next three rows must be tied with stitch.

Block 2. We knit one front and double crochet four times. That is, you should get 4 facial loops and four crochets. Next, we knit the same four front two by tilt to the right side, three loops with a broach, four front two tilt to the left side, one front and one yarn. Repeat two times. Again, repeat the pattern until the end of the row. Then we knit two rows of stitch. In this pattern you must remember to count the loops. The rapport should be 12 loops. In fact, this is an alternation of 6 yarn with 6 loops. You can come up with a variety of decor for this dress - from knitting patterns or flowers to a ribbon with a bow on the back of the dress.
