Hotel Sunmarinn Resort Hotel (Russia / Krasnodar Krai / Anapa): photos and reviews of tourists

Our article will be interesting to those who are interested in domestic resorts on the Black Sea. Anapa has long become a popular resort center offering year-round recreation. In our article we want to talk about one of the modern new complexes of Anapa - Sunmarinn Resort Hotel.

A bit about the hotel ...

Sunmarinn Resort Hotel is located in the city center and offers its guests a holiday at any time of the year. The hotel complex is built in a picturesque location on the Black Sea coast. The hotel offers its guests the opportunity to have a comfortable stay in modern, well-equipped rooms, all-inclusive meals, use of a private beach and animation. The complex has the largest pool of the resort.



During the off-season, the Sunmarinn Resort Hotel is open for organizing high-level events, trainings, business meetings, seminars and banquets. The hotel has a very favorable location, so literally within walking distance are the main attractions of the resort, shopping and entertainment complexes, the promenade, the central market, the bus station.

Number of rooms

Sunmarinn Resort Hotel cares about the comfort of its guests. The hotel consists of 423 rooms, among which there are different apartments:

  1. Double standard for two people. This room can accommodate another guest on an extra bed. The cost of apartments starts from 3360 rubles per day.
  2. Double suite with one extra bed and sea view. Room rate - 5040 rubles.
  3. Triple standard, the cost of apartments - from 5880 rubles.
  4. Family rooms is a two-room apartment with double and single beds and one sofa as an extra bed. The cost of apartments is 7560 rubles.
  5. Double "studio" is an improved room for two people with a kitchenette. These apartments are very convenient for couples with a baby. Their cost is 4032 rubles.
  6. Double standard in the building at number three. Room rate is 3240 rubles.

Apartments of all categories are equipped with telephones, televisions, mini-refrigerators, air conditioning and other amenities. Bathrooms include toiletries and towels.

Hotel infrastructure

Sunmarinn Resort Hotel (All Inclusive) in Anapa has a well-developed infrastructure with all the necessary facilities on the territory, among which there are two swimming pools. One of them is designed for adult tourists, and the second - for kids. The first pond is considered the largest in Anapa, its size is 25 meters by 9. The children's pool in shape resembles a bowl whose diameter is twelve meters and the depth does not exceed 150 centimeters. The area near the ponds is equipped with a recreation area with shady canopies and sun loungers. The proximity of both pools is convenient from the point of view that children are always under the supervision of their parents.

Hotel beach

Sunmarinn Resort Hotel All Inclusive is 650 meters from its own private beach, which is about one hundred meters wide. For a comfortable stay from the hotel to the coast runs a small bus with air conditioning. The beach area is fenced and equipped with beach paths, awnings, umbrellas, showers, a toilet, dressing cabins, a play area for children and a volleyball court.



A lifeguard and a nurse are on duty at the coast. In the snack bar, tourists can always eat and enjoy refreshing drinks. Every day on the beach there are entertainment not only for children, but also for adults. Tourists have the opportunity to take beach towels at the hotel.

Children's service

Sunmarinn Resort Hotel All Inclusive is a good place to relax with children. For young guests, a group of animators works throughout the day, which offers a variety of entertainment. The hotel also has a children's room, playground, cinema.

For children, a variety of entertainment programs are focused on sports and creativity. Young tourists can not only play board games, but also take part in a talent contest, sports games, adventure tests, and do gymnastics. For children, shows and mini-discos are held.

Parents can leave their children for several hours in the game room, as an experienced animator is involved with them.

Reviews on Sunmarinn Resort Hotel (All Inclusive)

Continuing the review of the hotel, I would like to pay attention to the guest reviews about it. In fairness, it is worth saying that the opinions of tourists are quite contradictory. However, almost all vacationers agree that the Sunmarinn Resort Hotel (All Inclusive) is conceived as a great place, but in many respects the level of service spoils the impression. Tourists have a lot of complaints about many points. But let's talk about everything in more detail.

Basically, the Sunmarinn Resort Hotel is located in a fairly convenient location. Nearby are all the interesting places of the city, restaurants, cafes, shops, and not far from the sea. However, the road passes nearby, if the number opens onto it, then it is noisy at night and day, since a lot of transport passes.



The complex itself consists of several buildings. In one of them, repairs are being made, which greatly interferes with people living in the same building. According to vacationers, the hotel buildings are in different conditions, there is a newer and older one.

In general, the number of rooms of the institution is quite good, if you do not take into account the state of plumbing, which does not cope with the work in many rooms. The apartments are quite suitable for relaxation and are well equipped.

But regarding cleaning there are many complaints. Only some tourists note its normal level, despite the fact that most leave extremely negative reviews. According to the vacationers themselves, it all depends on which maid gets caught, if they are responsible, then you can count on taking out the garbage and changing towels, and if not, you will have to remind her of her duties. Bed linen should be replaced three times a week, but in reality this is extremely rare. The situation is the same with towels, they can be exchanged for clean ones, but you need to remind staff if you run into them at the hotel. Independent exchange from the maids can not be expected.

Nutrition Reviews

Sunmarinn Resort Hotel (All Inclusive) provides its guests with the opportunity to take advantage of the all-inclusive concept. Of course, many vacationers are attracted by the possibility of such a vacation, because, firstly, it is very convenient, and secondly, everyone has long been accustomed to the amenities of such a system.

According to many tourists, the domestic concept, of course, differs from the foreign counterpart, so you should not expect something stunning. In general, the idea is very good, but the performance fails. Food reviews at Sunmarinn Resort Hotel All Inclusive are controversial. This is due to the fact that the "buffet" of the restaurant has a small selection of dishes that are sure to be repeated day after day. There is no talk of any variety. There are very few meat dishes. In principle, you will not stay hungry, but you should not count on special dishes.

A few words must be said about the children's menu. The administration is positioning the availability of separate nutrition for babies. However, according to parents, there is no specialized food for children. In the special area, although tables are set for young guests, they contain the same dishes as on the table for adults: fried potatoes, chicken wings and other unsuitable things. Unfortunately, the local chef does not seek to diversify the range of dishes.

During the day, guests can grab a bite to eat at the local bars and enjoy a glass of local wine. This is very convenient and saves the situation if you are hungry, so no one will have to go hungry.

General impression of the hotel ...

Turkish and Egyptian resorts are famous not only for good food, but also for excellent animation, for which our tourists love them. In fact, once on vacation, you know that you should not care about food, and your leisure will be organized at the highest level. Unfortunately, domestic resorts are still far from overseas. And while we can observe only a few attempts to apply a similar tactic with us, and not always successfully.

In this regard, the Sunmarinn Resort Hotel (testimonials to this confirmation) did a good job, making an attempt to organize animation in the complex. According to vacationers, activites organize good events for adults on the beach and pool, as well as in the evenings. As for children, there are absolutely no toys in the club, and no one is especially busy with kids. Of course, the level of animation in the complex does not reach that of Turkey or Egypt, but the animators are trying hard.

In fact, the Sunmarinn Resort Hotel in Anapa (confirmation of this) confirms itself as a four-star complex with a good level of service. And in fact, the institution does not provide some of the declared services. So, for example, the hotel has beautiful swimming pools (for children and adults), but the water in them is very cool even in warm times, because the heating does not work or it simply does not include it. There are a lot of similar nuances in the hotel.



The hotel complex has its own plot on the coast, the road to which takes about ten minutes at a slow pace. For those who do not want to walk on their own, the hotel has a shuttle service. A small bus runs to the coast every half hour, it is very convenient. Of course, it is not difficult to walk, but with a child or for people of age it is not always easy to do, so it is very important for such vacationers to be able to comfortably get to the shore.

The hotel beach itself is not noticeable. It has sunshades and deck chairs, a snack bar, a dressing room. But there is no toilet, a free institution of such a plan can be found only outside the territory. The sand on the beach is dirty with cigarette butts, so you need to be more careful with children.

The hotel staff deserves special attention. Initially, the complex was conceived as a fairly promising institution, but at the moment the hotel is not the best level of service. And this applies only to maids and some representatives of the reception. The rest of the staff is smiling and welcoming. And the waiters in the restaurant generally always smile sweetly and work rather nimbly. Registration and settlement are fast enough. According to tourists, management needs to work with staff to raise the level of service.

There are interruptions in the hotel with water. In addition, outdated rooms are currently undergoing renovations. Of course, the fact that the work takes place during the rest of tourists is not very pleasant, because noises cause a lot of inconvenience to people.

If you plan to visit Anapa by car, then you should know in advance that the hotel does not have its own parking. At the reception you will be asked to park a car somewhere nearby and write a receipt, the cost of parking per day will cost you at least 150 rubles. It is very strange that the four-star complex does not have its own parking lot, which should be free for all guests of the institution.

In general, the hotel does not have a part of the infrastructure elements that it positions. So, for example, while there is no children's cafe. Perhaps part of the fund is being repaired and will delight guests in the future. However, at the moment in the hotel you can find both refurbished and old rooms.

The convenient location of the complex allows holiday-makers at any time to go not only to the beach, but also for a walk along the sea along the promenade. Nearby are also shops, cafes, shopping centers, where you can always buy everything you need for your vacation: from toothpaste to clothing. There is also a magnificent park where you can walk with children.

Instead of an afterword

If you plan to spend your vacation in Anapa, then you have probably already looked for suitable options for yourself. Surely among the four-star complexes you managed to notice the hotel "Sanmarin", which gives the impression of a beautiful and new establishment. The complex has a number of advantages, which include the number of rooms and animation. But at the same time, the hotel needs serious staff correction, as negligent employees just scare away vacationers from the hotel.



Is it possible to recommend this institution as a place to spend a vacation, the question is complex, since it all depends on your preferences. If you are not too spoiled in terms of food and other household issues, then you can quite head to this institution. If you consider yourself to be people who love impeccable service and attentive attitude, then you should consider other options. In addition, families with young children should not visit the hotel at present, as the hotel is undergoing renovations. The rest of the hotel is quite good and suitable for a beach holiday for not very moody tourists.
