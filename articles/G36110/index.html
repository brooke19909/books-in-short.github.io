Canon 7D Mark II Body camera: technical specifications and customer reviews

Canon is one of the world's leading suppliers of photography equipment, both amateur and professional. The Canon EOS 7D Mark II camera is positioned as universal - designed for both the beginner and the experienced photographer, combining both the most popular traditional and innovative functions. What are its main characteristics? What are the features of the operation of the device?





Device Overview

We will learn the basic facts about the device.

Many experts in the field of photography technology pay attention, first of all, to the equipment of the device with two high-performance Digic 6 processors. Another significant advantage of the device is the availability of powerful autofocus, which is capable of supporting a large number of cross-points. The camera implements a combined exposure metering mechanism - RGB indices and infrared light are taken into account.

The device has the highest automatic shooting speed, which is 10 frames per second. Experts note that the device in question has a technologically advanced, relatively quiet shutter, with a resource of about 200 thousand cycles. The minimum shutter speed is 1/8000 seconds. Thus, the device can rightly be attributed to one of the most advanced developments from Canon.

Modifications to the device: Body and Kit

It can be noted that there are two main modifications of the Canon EOS 7D Mark II camera on the market - Body and Kit. The first comes without a lens - and this is their main difference. The Canon EOS 7D Mark II Body can be equipped with a wide variety of optical elements of the appropriate type. The main thing is that they fit the bayonet mount.

- the camera is classified as SLR;

- the camera is equipped with an LCD display with a diagonal of 3 inches and a resolution of 1 MP;

- camera exposure is 30 - 1/8000 seconds, if necessary, the shutter speed can be manually adjusted;

- the device is able to use 65 cross points when focusing;

- the device can be controlled using the remote control;

- the camera can record video in MOV and MP4 formats in a maximum resolution of 1920 by 1080 pixels and a recording speed of 60 frames per second, in HD mode - the rate can be reduced to 50;

The effectiveness of the device in practice

Now let's look at how the Canon 7d Mark II device works in practice. A review of the capabilities of the camera, compiled by us, will be incomplete without studying this aspect.

But even with this camera engagement mode, few competing solutions are capable of providing a similar indicator. To ensure the stability of recording frames that are shot automatically, it is desirable to use fast memory cards - best of all, those that work in the SDHC standard.
