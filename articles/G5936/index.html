Cardboard carriage for Barbie

Building a drawing for the landau hull

The cardboard carriage described here is made DIY for Barbie dolls. But you can make in a similar way a landau or a trick for other residents of the toy town. When constructing doll carriages with their own hands, drawings should be developed in accordance with the dimensions of the ‚Äúusers‚Äù.





  1. First of all, a contour pattern of the side wall of the carriage is drawn. In our case, it has the shape of a bowl with a rounded bottom or a tulip with a straight top.
  2. At the top of the wall are two round windows.
  3. A door will be located between them. We have a ladder function assigned to it, so the side that will not be cut is at the bottom.
  4. You can make a hole in the upper part of the door, such as a window, or on the side - like a handle.
  5. A sharp blade cuts the contour of the wall, window, hole in the door and its three sides.
  6. The second wall is cut out the same way, just do not make the door. Although the carriage, made of cardboard with its own hands, may well have two entrances.
  7. Preparing the bottom for the carriage. It has the appearance of a long strip of cardboard. This part should be as wide as the width of the crew itself will subsequently be. The length is calculated in this way: the distance from the upper corner points of the side wall is measured. A centimeter tape should be skipped along the lower contour of the sidewall. To this size should be added an allowance for circular bends - decorations on top of the carriage.

Carriage Housing Assembly for Barbie

  1. So that the carriage with your own hands does not turn out to be curved, first calculate the middle of the bottom part and the center point of the lower wall curve. They are connected.
  2. Using adhesive tape, the edges of the bottom part and the curved line of the sidewall are glued at right angles.
  3. Attach the second side wall similarly.
  4. The remaining free ends of the bottom spin.
  5. Fix the "curl" with double tape or a piece of cardboard.

Providing carriage motor function

Since you want to make a carriage with your own hands with wheels, thanks to which it will move, you should take care of this. In principle, preparing such a device is not difficult at all.





  1. For the wheels themselves, they take something round: a coil of thin tape, wire, a cover of the right size. You can even cut a circle out of cardboard. In future wheels, make a hole in the center, if it is not foreseen in the used objects.
  2. A cocktail tube is attached to the bottom of the carriage with tape.
  3. A wooden skewer is inserted into it.
  4. The edges of the skewers strengthen in the center of the wheels.

Work on the appearance of the crew

  1. Everyone decides for himself how to decorate the carriage from the outside. You can cover it with paint and varnish, and glue on top an ornament that is cut out of foil or paper of the desired color.
  2. You can draw an ornament using a template with silver or golden paint.
  3. And if the drawing is done with varnish or glue, on which beads or millet is immediately poured, then it will become voluminous. By the way, grain can also be painted silver or gold before use .
