Vibration on iPhone: how to disable it on your device?

A vibrating iPhone can distract from serious matters or simply annoy. Therefore, owners of "apple" devices are sometimes puzzled by how to turn off vibration on an iPhone. After all, the procedure is quite specific and it is not always possible to do this the first time.

We will talk about all the actions and nuances of how to turn off vibration on an iPhone. For clarity, pick up your "apple" device.

Where and how does vibration turn on and off?

Owners of both old iPhones (2, 3, 4) and newer (from 6) devices feel the vibration of their devices as soon as someone calls them or a message arrives. As a rule, melody and vibro are reproduced simultaneously.

So, so that the vibration on the iPhone 6, 7, 8 or older versions of the device does not sound, it can be turned off in the settings. To do this, open the "Settings" menu on your smartphone, scroll to the "Sounds" category and click on it. In the window that opens, in the "Vibration" column, turn off the switch next to "During a call."

If there is a need to figure out how to turn off vibration on an iPhone for SMS messages, then first put the device in silent mode, and then repeat the above operation, only deactivate "In silent mode".

This is the only way to shutdown.

How to disable vibration on an iPhone?

On Apple smartphones, you can completely turn off vibration. For this:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Find the "General" category.
  3. In the window that opens, select "Universal access."
  4. Select "Vibrate" and deactivate this function by moving the bar to the left.

If you need to return the vibration, then do the same steps.

How to remove vibro from messages in social networks and instant messengers?

When there is a correspondence in social networks and instant messengers, the notification of the arrival of a message is accompanied by sound and vibration. In silent mode - only vibration. Since the vibro on iPhones is loud, when using social networks you can wake up already sleeping ones. Therefore, the option can be disabled. There are several ways to use it:

  1. The first method will turn off vibration while on social networks. When it is minimized, the option is activated again. Consider the example of "VKontakte". To do this, go to the application itself on the smartphone, find the "Settings" function, then select the "Notifications" item, rewind it down by finding "Vibration". Disable this feature.
  2. The second method will turn off vibration on the iPhone even when the social network or messenger is minimized or closed. To do this, open the "Settings" in the menu of your smartphone, scroll down to "Sounds", open and put the switch in inactive mode opposite the line "In silent mode".

To disable vibro in the Viber application, use the same action for Vkontakte.

Conclusion

As you can see, the solution to the question of how to turn off vibration on an iPhone is simple and clear. The only negative is that the action is carried out in several steps, which will require small, but still time-consuming.

This sequence of actions is the same for the first iPhone models with an outdated version of iOS, and for modern "apples" with the updated 11 iOS.
