Hotel Larissa Inn Camyuva Hotel 4 * (Camyuva, Turkey): tourist reviews, features, rooms and description

For several decades, Turkey has attracted the attention of tourists from Russia and neighboring countries as a luxury resort. Its cities, located on the coast, are visited annually by thousands of travelers who prefer an exotic vacation.

Kemer is a small town that becomes incredibly popular in the midst of the spa season. This is due to the fact that on its territory there is a huge number of comfortable hotels of a high category. Also, tourists are attracted by the wonderful tropical climate that prevails here. In hot times, guests of the city do not suffer from high temperatures, as the air softens under the influence of Mediterranean waves.

One of the most popular hotels in Kemer is Larissa Inn Camyuva Hotel 4 * (Turkey). Its conditions offer a decent vacation, which will definitely be remembered for a long time. Tourists, having been here, wish to return again and again, and also advise their friends and relatives to buy tickets to this hotel.



Location

The Larissa Inn Camyuva Hotel 4 * hotel is located in Turkey, on the outskirts of the resort town of Kemer, in the Camyuva district. It was built on the very coast of the Mediterranean Sea, so that every guest living in it has unhindered access to a clean and picturesque coast.

Kemer is a town famous for its developed entertainment infrastructure. Hotel guests often go to the very center of the city, passing a distance of five kilometers, in order to take part in night parties. The nearest airport is 60 kilometers from the hotel - in the large resort city of Antalya.

Rooms

Conditions for children

The hotel has the opportunity to relax with young children, as they have a small number of conditions for a comfortable and safe stay. First of all, a crib can be installed in any room on a free basis, the need for which is advisable to be warned in advance - by telephone when booking rooms or when buying a ticket from a tour operator.

In addition, for babies on the territory of the Larissa Inn Camyuva Hotel 4 * (Turkey) there is a large playground where they can actively spend free time, as well as a pool that is equipped with all safety standards. Parents have the opportunity to send their kids to a special nursery, where they will be looked after by the nanny and a team of cheerful Russian-speaking animators. Nutrition

Tourists vacationing at the Larissa Inn Camyuva Hotel 4 *, living on an all-inclusive basis, eat at the local restaurant, where a daily buffet is organized during breakfast, lunch and dinner. Here, tourists can enjoy dishes of European and traditional Turkish cuisine.

The Larissa Inn Camyuva Hotel 4 * has a magnificent spa area where vacationers can enjoy a massage performed by professionals in this business. Beach

What a vacation by the sea without visiting the beach? The Larissa Inn Camyuva Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey / Kemer) has its own sandy relaxation area, which is stretched along the coastline for 100 meters. It is clean and picturesque, and its territory is perfectly landscaped. Guests resting here have the opportunity to use toilets, showers and changing rooms.
