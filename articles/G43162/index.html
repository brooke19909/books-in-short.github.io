Sun Maris City 3 *: description, reviews. Turkey, Marmaris: hotels

Marmaris is considered one of the best Turkish resorts. He is called the most "European" in this country. It is located in a closed bay, so there is practically no unrest at sea. In general, Marmaris is positioned as a youth resort. There are many bars and cafes right on the promenade, there are excellent places for diving. The city is famous for its water park. Life here is in full swing around the clock. That is why the youth hotels of Marmaris are always crowded with vacationers.



Nature has generously awarded this resort in southwestern Turkey, giving it a mild climate and clear sea, picturesque bays and excellent beaches.

In the Middle Ages, Marmaris was called Fiskos. Sultan Suleiman lived here for some time, preparing for a trip to the island of Rhodes. They say that it was then that the city was renamed. Today this place is gently washed by the waves of two seas - the Aegean and the Mediterranean. The resort, surrounded by coniferous forests and mountainous landscapes, has sandy and pebble beaches and a winding coast with bays and bays.



general information

The city is especially popular with tourists who prefer a fun holiday in Turkey. Its unforgettable eastern streets are always full, especially at night. Here, mainly rest with noisy companies. Marmaris 3 *, 4 * and 5 * hotels are constantly crowded. From here, many take boat trips along the entire Turkish coast, go to Rhodes or the Turtle Islands.

And for some time now the city has been considered the main center where sailing competitions are held in the eastern Mediterranean. During the regatta, all youth hotels in Marmaris are populated by athletes and fans.

However, the resort is chosen not only by young people, but also many well-known and wealthy people. Here come those who combine relaxation and sightseeing of local attractions, which Marmaris (Turkey) is so rich in.

Sun Maris City Hotel 3 *

The hotel is located in the center of Marmaris. It is part of the Sun Maris City hotel chain. Just a hundred meters away is the beach. Nearby is one of the most popular streets in the area, where there are many cafes and bars, life is boiling around the clock and a lot of tourists. This beautiful hotel was built in 1990. The last major renovation in it was carried out in 2007. And now he offers his guests to plunge into the atmosphere of comfort, enjoy the excellent service, which adds the surf, the warm rays of the sun and the picturesque landscapes of Marmaris.

Territory



The territory of Sun Maris City 3 *, the photo of which speaks of its thoughtfulness, although small, but clean and well-groomed. Infrastructure

In the only building Sun Maris City 3 * there is a 24-hour reception desk. When checking in, you need to approach it. There is a small cozy lobby. On the ground floor there is a dry cleaning service and a paid medical office. A currency exchange office is open until six in the evening. Several ATMs are installed.

The reception also has a left-luggage office and a paid depository safe. There is also the possibility of arranging a transfer, which includes meeting at the airport and seeing off to the airport terminal. You can also rent a car or bicycle, order a taxi, use fax or photocopying services.



Inside the building there is also a tour desk organizing tours to many of Marmaris' sights. For those who do not speak English, it is more convenient to use the services of a Russian-speaking guide.

At Sun Maris City 3 *, a small but very convenient parking is equipped. Booking it in advance is not necessary.

Number of rooms

Considered by the number of residents to be very small, Sun Maris City 3 * offers a total of eighty-eight standard rooms. Up to three thousand people can live in them at the same time. The rooms are located in a single six-story building, located on the first line among palm trees. All have balconies overlooking the sea.

Rooms are eighteen square meters in size. Flooring - ceramic tiles. The rooms are decorated in soothing colors with no frills. On the windows there are pleasantly colored curtains, there is also a work desk, individually adjustable split air conditioning, a TV with one Russian channel, an electronic safe, for which it is absolutely necessary to pay extra for the specialist to adjust and demonstrate how it works. On the wall is a large mirror used for applying makeup.

The combined bathroom has a shower, washbasin and toilet. There is also a hair dryer and a variety of cosmetic accessories that are constantly updated. One room is for disabled guests. It is located on the ground floor and has a specially equipped shower and a balcony. The hotel is positioned as smoke-free.

Room service

Every day, all rooms are carefully cleaned, and cleaning is done at a time convenient for those living in them. Cosmetic accessories are replaced every other day. Upon request, hotel guests can get extra bedding. Upon request, residents at Sun Maris City Hotel 3 * are provided with an iron with ironing board. There is also a breakfast in the room and an alarm clock.





Since a significant part of the residents of this hotel are family people, the administration allows children with all ages to be accommodated. Those planning to put extra beds in their rooms for babies need to discuss this with the property in advance. In some cases, this service is provided for a fee. As for those people who can not imagine traveling without their four-legged pets, they should keep in mind: in accordance with the rules of the hotel, accommodation of such guests is not allowed. Therefore, if someone still wants to come to Marmaris accompanied by his pet, he will have to look for another hotel.

Nutrition

The Sun Maris City 3 * hotel operates on an all-inclusive concept, but in some cases, you can get a continental breakfast. Breakfast with lunch and dinner is served in the main restaurant.
