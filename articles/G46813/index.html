"French Quarter" in Vologda - an original solution embodied in an exquisite architectural style

At a distance of 450 kilometers north-west of the capital, the city of Vologda is located, rightfully considered the most environmentally friendly in the state. Thanks to such indicators, for more than seven years the population of Vologda has been growing steadily. This, in turn, requires the construction of more and more new houses and residential complexes.

A bit about the city

There are no particularly large industrial enterprises in Vologda, which allows its environmental situation to remain favorable for many years. That is why this region and city attract immigrants from other cities. Most of the regional economy is trade. The construction of new residential buildings and complexes is very active, one of which is the “French Quarter” in Vologda.

Currently, Vologda is considered the most harmonious city and the most attractive for business. This is a city with a deep ancient culture, fresh clean air, filled with calm and harmony.

Location of the residential complex and transport accessibility

The developer for the construction of the residential complex “French Quarter” in Vologda chose one of the most attractive and promising land plots - on the 6th Army embankment on the very bank of the Vologda river. This is the most environmentally friendly area of ​​the city, its oldest quarter. Get to the place of residence - the "French Quarter" in Vologda - its residents can without any problems. On any form of public transport existing in the city. Or in personal cars.

Description of the residential complex

What is the LCD “French Quarter” in Vologda, bearing such a beautiful name? This is a modern complex development, the project of which provides for the construction of six multi-sectional monolithic-brick buildings of different floors. This residential complex will offer apartments of various sizes and layouts: one-room, two-room and three-room. The minimum area of ​​apartments is 39 square meters. Maximum - over 83 square meters.

Implementation of interior decoration works by the developer is not provided, which allows future residents to significantly save on dismantling old repairs and implement their own design decisions. Houses are built of bricks - the best material used for sound insulation.

All loggias in the apartments are glazed with wooden windows with double-glazed windows. Walls and ceilings are leveled with cement-sand mortar, the floor is poured with screed. In addition, the apartments are equipped with individual heating and water supply systems, controlled by a double-circuit boiler. Entrance metal doors, intercom, sockets, switches, water, gas and electricity meters are installed.

External and internal infrastructure

The “French Quarter” in Vologda is a project designed to fully realize the dreams of its residents about a quiet and peaceful life in new homes in fresh air. For the comfort and development of children, a playground is provided. Adults can relax in the specially equipped relaxation area. The entire territory of the residential complex will be landscaped. Owners of personal vehicles and guests of the complex will be able to park their cars in open parking lots.

The location of the “French Quarter” in Vologda is very good. At the services of its residents, almost the entire infrastructure of the city. In the immediate vicinity and within walking distance are comprehensive schools and kindergartens, hypermarkets, cinemas, sports centers and beauty salons, hospitals and pharmacies. There is also a public transport stop nearby, from where you can reach any point in the city.

House number 4

The “French Quarter” in Vologda, namely house 4, according to the general development plan, is located at the address: embankment of the 6th Army, house 43. Entrance to the house is carried out from the indicated street. Behind it are the remaining residential buildings according to the project of the developer.

There will be no one-room apartments in this house. The area starts with two-room apartments. But then buyers are even offered four-room apartments suitable for large families. The interior decoration of the apartments is the same as in all other houses. In total, the building has 28 apartments. The house has already been commissioned and is inhabited by its residents.
