Horse Country Resort Congress Congress Spa 4 * (Italy, Sardinia, Arborea): traveler reviews

In the magical Sardinia is the resort Horse Country Resort. This is a unique place where untouched nature is organically combined with modern standards and quality service. In addition, here you will have a unique opportunity to spend unforgettable time with horses. These amazing animals deliver a lot of positive emotions, as well as treat the body and soul.





Accommodation

Horse Country Resort has 315 guestrooms. For a comfortable stay and good rest, the following options are provided:

  • Standard rooms are compact in size and modest in design. The room has two single beds. Additional clamshell is possible. The windows overlook the garden.
  • Superior room in size, interior and equipment resembles a standard. The main difference is the presence of a balcony. It is also possible to install two additional cots.
  • The suite consists of two rooms. The bedroom is equipped with a French orthopedic bed, and the living room is with upholstered furniture. The balcony overlooks the park area.
  • Detached cottages are located in the forest zone. 58 detached houses will appeal to lovers of relaxation in the lap of nature. They consist of two rooms - a bedroom and a living room, decorated in English style.

Restaurants

In the resort complex Horse Country Resort Arborea 4 *, close attention is paid to the delicious and quality nutrition of guests. This function is assigned to such restaurants:

  • Fazolare is the main buffet restaurant. It serves traditional Sardinian dishes, as well as international cuisine. The calm and festive atmosphere of the dining room is emphasized by an elegant classic interior. There is a separate buffet for children.
  • Paguro is a beach restaurant that specializes in traditional Sardinian cuisine. Enjoying breathtaking views of the sea and the Sinis Peninsula, you will try spoiled fish or fresh grilled fish, as well as many other dishes. Themed evenings are regularly held here to familiarize guests of Horse Country Resort Arborea 4 * with local cultural traditions.
  • Don Kuster is a real Italian pizzeria. Here you can enjoy dishes cooked in a wood-burning stove. The facility has an outdoor terrace overlooking the pool and park area.
  • "Cowboy Salon" will allow you to immerse yourself in the atmosphere of a western. The institution specializes in meat dishes. Country parties are held regularly.

Bars

High-quality drinks, light snacks and a relaxing atmosphere - all this will give you the bars of the Horse Country Resort Congress Spa . You can have a good time in such institutions:

  • Chedro is the central lounge bar specializing in cocktails. The calm atmosphere and relaxing music make this place the most suitable for friendly conversations.
  • Tropicana is an open pool bar. It serves refreshing drinks and fast food. The bar also hosts all-day entertainment.
  • Mirto is a cozy outdoor bar on the beach. Here you can drink your favorite drink or a hearty snack.

additional information

If you have chosen Horse Country Resort 4 * for relaxation, check out some additional information about the specifics of this institution. Here is what information you should pay close attention to:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests is made from 15:00 to midnight;
  • all issues related to eviction must be resolved in the period from 07:30 to 10:00;
  • cots for babies under 2 years old are available at a price of 13 euros for each night of use;
  • there is the possibility of staying with pets (the provision of the service is agreed in advance and may require an additional fee);
  • to pay the resort fee, guests are invited to purchase a club card, which gives the right to visit the hotel beach and a number of recreational activities;
  • if guests decide to leave the hotel earlier than planned, the cost of living for the remaining period of time will not be returned to them.




Positive reviews

Perhaps the most objective and unbiased opinion about the quality of your stay at Horse Country Resort Arborea and other hotels can be found in the traveler reviews. Here are some positive points from the comments of tourists:

  • Horse Country Resort Arborea 4 * (Sardinia) has a huge sandy beach, which is almost perfectly clean and well-maintained.
  • The establishment is surrounded by coniferous forest, and therefore the air in the territory is very clean, healing and fragrant.
  • The rooms are quite spacious and bright.
  • The appliances and furniture in the rooms are in good condition.
  • Great food in the main restaurant.
  • The hotel territory is almost completely planted with lawns, flowers, shrubs and other greens. All plants are carefully looked after. There are also many trees that create a cool shade.
  • Great quality food in an Italian restaurant. Climbing deserves special praise.
  • There are a lot of sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach. All have enough space, no need to occupy in the morning.
  • Evening performances deserve all praise. The concert program is attended not only by animators, but also by invited artists.
  • Interaction with horses gives a lot of positive emotions. Children remain wildly delighted with the ponies.
  • Great local wine can be sampled at the hotel's catering facilities.
  • Affordable car rental rates.
  • Russian-speaking staff work at the reception. This greatly simplifies the solution of many organizational issues.
  • The rooms are well soundproofed. Neither the voices of neighbors, nor the noise of the corridors, nor the music from the street will interfere with the rest.
  • If some kind of breakdown occurs in the room, the master arrives a few minutes after the call to the reception.




Negative reviews

Unfortunately, the Horse Country Resort 4 * hotel (Sardinia) has some disadvantages. Based on the feedback from experienced travelers, we can talk about the following flaws inherent in the institution in question:

  • The interior and furnishings of the rooms are morally and physically outdated. Furniture and plumbing has long been in need of replacement with a more beautiful and modern one.
  • Small algae is constantly making waves on the beach. Unfortunately, the staff is in no hurry to clean them, they can lie on the shore for several days.
  • In the evening, the smell from the stable spreads across the territory. Apparently, this is due to the cleaning time.
  • In the rooms you can often find ants and other insects. The problem is especially relevant for rooms located on the ground floor, as well as for bungalows.
  • Frequent interruptions in water supply.
  • Animation only in Italian. Some hold events in English. Thus, not all entertainments turn out to take part.
  • Tasteful instant coffee is served during breakfast. Baking is also not the best quality.
  • There is practically nothing interesting around the hotel. Nowhere to walk.
  • If you do not like the room, be prepared for the fact that at the reception you will be denied relocation. You can get a positive solution only through the tour operator from whom you bought a ticket.
