Cassells Al Barsha Hotel 4 * (Dubai, United Arab Emirates): description of rooms, service, photos and reviews

Dubai is the largest city in the United Arab Emirates, which has received the glory of the fastest growing metropolis in the world. Its development practically does not stop. New skyscrapers are constantly being erected, new shops and night clubs are opening. Tourists flock to the city to see with its own eyes its modern architecture. The cost of rest here can not be called cheap, but it fully justifies itself. Often, tourists stop at Cassells Al Barsha Hotel 4 *. Let's consider it in more detail in this article.

Hotel location

The huge tourist complex is located on the busy street of Dubai in the Al Barsha area . Near it you can find numerous shops, souvenir shops, restaurants and cafes. The district is located in the west of the city and is now actively developing. Its infrastructure is fully focused on tourists. Not far from here is the largest ski resort of the United Arab Emirates, as well as the famous artificially built island of Palm Jumeirah . The area is considered a great place for shopping. Several large shopping centers have been built here, for example, Moll of Emirates. Al Barsha borders on the fashionable Emirates Hills area, where wealthy western tourists stay. Particularly luxurious buildings create a beautiful landscape for travelers in a budget area. The beach, unfortunately, is 20 km from the hotel, so if you wish, you can get to it only by bus or car.

The nearest international airports are located 30-35 km from the hotel. The cheapest way to get to the Cassells Al Barsha Hotel is by public transport, although most often tourists choose a taxi. When buying a ticket, you can also arrange a transfer to the complex for an additional fee. You can move around the city by metro, bus, water boat or taxi. In addition, in Dubai there is a special ladies' taxi where only women work as drivers. They often travel single travelers .

Cassells Al Barsha Hotel 4 *: general information about the complex

The large city hotel was opened in 2010. It consists of one nine-story building, which is located on a busy street and almost does not have its own territory. The complex has over 310 rooms, the last renovation of which was held here in 2014. Check-in with small children is allowed. In addition, if a child under 6 years old will sleep in bed with his parents, then he can stay at the hotel for free. When buying an extra bed, a discount is provided. To come here with animals, on the contrary, is prohibited. The hotel has Russian-speaking staff, although most staff speak only Arabic or English.





Check-in starts at Cassells Al Barsha Hotel strictly from 15:00 local time. When registering, tourists must provide a photo ID, Visa or MasterCard bank card and make a deposit, the amount of which is $ 100. If during your vacation you did not spoil any property of the hotel, then it will be returned to you after eviction. The check-out time at the hotel is the period from 10:00 to noon. During this time, you must either leave your room or extend your stay.

Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the hotel has prepared 310 comfortable rooms for tourists. One of them is designed for guests with disabilities. It has wide doorways so that a wheelchair can easily pass into the room, as well as a comfortable shower and sink. The 48 rooms at Cassells Al barsha Hotel are adjoining, therefore connected by a special interior door. These apartments are suitable for large families or friendly companies. 156 rooms are non-smoking. When making a voucher, it is worthwhile to take an interest in the location of the rooms in advance, because the windows of some of the apartments overlook the construction site. Hotel staff daily clean and change towels. It is worth knowing that room service and round-the-clock food delivery are not included in the price.

Room features

In addition to the standard set of furniture, which consists of several beds, a working corner with a desk and chair, wardrobes for clothes and cabinets for things, each room has modern equipment. It allows you to make your stay at Cassells Al Barsha Hotel as comfortable as possible. So, guests can use a small refrigerator in which you can leave not only food, but also drinks. Each room has a central air conditioning that cools the room in hot weather. The apartments also have an electronic safe, cable TV and wireless internet. For a fee, you can use the telephone and minibar .





The bathroom has a shower, toilet and sink with a large mirror. A set of cosmetics is provided free of charge, which includes liquid soap, shower gel and shampoo. All guests are given individual bathrobes and slippers. Some rooms include a hairdryer and tea and coffee making facilities.

Service and Facilities

Kassels Al Barsha is a hotel with a developed network of infrastructure facilities. For guests' convenience, six modern elevators work in the building, which quickly bring tourists to their floors. At the reception, working around the clock, you can exchange your currency for local, arrange a car rental or call a paid doctor in your room. Room service is also available 24 hours a day. Near the complex is a covered free guarded parking for guests. On the ground floor you can find a hairdresser with a men's, women's and children's room, a beauty salon providing wellness treatments for face and body, as well as several shops. In them, tourists can buy clothes and shoes, souvenirs, food, tobacco, jewelry. Laundry facilities and an internet corner are available at an additional cost .





A private bus leaves the hotel free of charge daily, bringing tourists to the beach. In the evening he takes them back. The complex also has its own business center, which consists of two conference rooms equipped with modern computer technology. Their area is 45 and 65 square meters. m

Entertainment

A huge selection of entertainment offers tourists Dubai. Cassells Al Barsha Hotel is generally used by travelers only for overnight stays, although there are many leisure facilities. We list the main ones:

  • 25 sqm outdoor pool m. with fresh water and heating. It is located on the roof of the hotel and is open daily until 22:00. Near the pool there is a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols.
  • Wellness center where tourists can book a massage course, visit the jacuzzi or sauna.
  • Gym and table tennis.
  • Indoor playground for children.
  • Tour agency.




Catering

When buying a ticket, tourists can also pay for meals during their stay at the Cassells Al Barsha Hotel. The restaurant is open every day. Cassells Al Barsha Hotel: positive reviews

It is worth noting that tourists who visited this hotel leave different reviews . And although most of the travelers were satisfied with the rest in this place, some do not recommend coming here. First, consider the advantages of the hotel, which tourists noted:

  • Convenient location. The hotel is located near the metro station, so the road to it takes only 1 minute.
  • Large rooms with a spacious bathroom and separate toilet. Ironing board and iron are available free of charge upon request.
  • The hotel is very quiet at night. Since they do not sell alcohol here, drunk tourists almost never meet here.
  • Hearty breakfasts that are suitable for young children. Sometimes served watermelons and melons.

Negative hotel reviews

As mentioned above, a small portion of tourists were unsatisfied with the quality of the services provided. They list the following shortcomings that they will never come to Cassells Al Barsha Hotel again:

  • The pool is not open every day. In addition, the roof offers an unpleasant view of the construction site.
  • Small windows in the rooms, so even in the afternoon the rooms are quite dark and you have to turn on the light.
  • Poor cleaning of the room. The carpet in the room is not cleaned. Some guests also noted the presence of an unpleasant smell in the apartment.
  • In the morning the restaurant is always crowded, so there are not enough tables at all. The buffet in the hotel is not very diverse.




Instead of an afterword

Thus, the Kassels Al Barsha hotel is a good option for those who want a cheap vacation in Dubai. It is located near the significant sights of the city and the metro, with which you can easily get to any area. Of course, the hotel has its drawbacks, however, the rooms here are not expensive. Most often, adults, young couples and families with children stay at the hotel. Often come here and youth companies.
