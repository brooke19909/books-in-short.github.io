Borisov hotels: description, reviews

Tourists traveling in Belarus often visit the city of Borisov. This is a large industrial center, so people often come here on business trips. Country holidays are popular here. Hotels in Borisov offer all guests of the city modern comfortable conditions for living.

Hotel "Dudinka City"

"Dudinka City" is a tourist complex. It belongs to those Borisov hotels where you can experience the national Slavic flavor. Meals are organized in the hotel's own restaurant. Free parking is available. Equipped playgrounds for picnics, a playground for children. You are waiting for interesting entertainment and sightseeing programs, classes in various sports. For accommodation, several categories of rooms are offered:

  • Double budget - 2 343 rubles per day.
  • Junior Suite - 3 013 rubles per day.
  • Deluxe double - 3 348 rubles per day.
  • Deluxe double with a balcony - 3 682 rubles per day.
  • Junior Suite Triple - 3 682 rubles per day.
  • Quadruple family with a balcony - 4 017 rubles per day.

The price includes a good breakfast. The hotel "Dudinka City" is located at: Dudinka-1, Borisov.

Country house "Manor Gliwinskaya"

The hotel is located in the village of Gliwin. It is one of those Borisov hotels where tourists can relax by the water, pick mushrooms and berries in the forest, relax in the bathhouse, and cook barbecue in the fresh air. Free private parking is available for owners. "Gliwinskaya Manor" is a large brick structure, similar inside to an old estate. Here you will find comfortable conditions, equipped in accordance with modern technology. In a house with three bedrooms and a living room, the cost of living depends on the number of guests:
