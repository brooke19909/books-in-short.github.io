Hotel Harmony Hotel & Resort Makadi Bay 5 *, Egypt, Makadi: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Everyone wants to relax in royal conditions on the shores of the warm sea. Hotel Harmony Hotel & Resort Makadi Bay 5 * gives its guests such an opportunity, surrounding them with oriental luxury, hospitality and care.





basic information

Hotel Harmony Hotel & Resort Makadi Bay 5 * fully and fully justifies its high status. Having a rest at this resort, you can really find harmony with yourself and the world around you, because the wellness and relaxation treatments offered by the local spa deserve only the highest praise.

An important point is that Harmony Hotel & Resort Makadi Bay 5 * is located directly on the beach, so you do not have to get to the sea for a long time and you can breathe in the healing, iodine-rich air all day. And for those who find the waves too cold, there is the opportunity to splash around in the pool with hydromassage, enjoying the sound of the surf.

A true indicator of luxury can be called the apartments of the Harmony Hotel & Resort Makadi Bay 5 * hotel. Marble bathrooms and spacious bright bedrooms allow you to plunge into the atmosphere of Khan's palaces.





Where is the hotel situated

We can say that the hotel is located quite apart from the main attractions and infrastructure that Egypt is rich in. Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * is located 35 km from the airport terminal, 43 km from the center of Hurghada, and 255 km from the popular sightseeing route of Luxor. Nevertheless, this is unlikely to spoil the mood of tourists, because a cozy quiet bay does not want to be left for the sake of noisy and crowded excursions.

Number of rooms

Only two categories of rooms the Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * hotel offers its guests a choice of. Standard offers its guests a beautiful view of the garden and allows you to enjoy a quiet, relaxing holiday. If you wish, you can check into a room with a double bed or single. If you are staying in a room with a child, you have the right to an extra bed.

The most popular among tourists are rooms with sea views, because there is nothing better than enjoying your captivating scenery and listening to the surf from your own cozy balcony or spacious terrace. In all other respects, these apartments are practically no different from standard ones.





Room features

In addition to the elegant interior and a beautiful view from the window, the availability in the rooms of all the necessary amenities, as well as technical devices that would make the stay of guests in the apartments as comfortable as possible, is of great importance. So, the rooms of this hotel complex boast the following equipment:

  • the possibility of checking into adjacent apartments;
  • some rooms have all the necessary equipment for people with disabilities;
  • each room has a balcony;
  • modern TV with satellite connection;
  • a mini-bar in which water supplies are replenished daily;
  • tea and coffee set;
  • personal safe;
  • hair dryer;
  • several outlets;
  • telephone with connection to local and international lines;
  • individual air conditioning;
  • if necessary, children's furniture is provided.

Entertainment

The resort and hotel complex Harmony Makadi Bay Hotel Resort 5 * (Hurghada) is famous for its entertainment, thanks to which not even the most shy guest will be bored during their vacation. So, to fill your leisure, you can use the following services:

  • for fans of tennis at the hotel there is a large modern court (for an extra fee you can rent rackets, as well as rent spotlights that allow you to play at night);
  • in one of the pools there is a built-in jacuzzi and heated hydromassage;
  • a huge number of ping-pong tables and the ability to rent rackets;
  • there is the opportunity to take part in such popular entertainment as boccia, archery and darts;
  • a small football field with artificial turf;
  • riding on thoroughbred horses or riding in the desert on trained camels;
  • every day, animators prepare a rich and interesting entertainment program in which everyone can take part;
  • water attractions;
  • there is a large volleyball field on the beach;
  • sports lovers will certainly enjoy morning exercises, as well as aerobics classes.
