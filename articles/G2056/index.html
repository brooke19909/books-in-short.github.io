Almetyevsk, hotel "Voskhod": address, room selection, ease of booking, quality of service, additional services and visitor reviews

Almetyevsk, which is often called the oil capital of Tatarstan, of course, is not a popular tourist center, but it is very often visited by athletes and people who come here on a business trip or for business purposes. The environmental situation in Almetyevsk leaves much to be desired, so it is important to choose a hotel in a place surrounded by a forest zone, remote from the center. Let it not bother you that you have to overcome some distance to the place of work or training. The town is small, for example, from the Voskhod Hotel (Almetyevsk) the road to the business center takes only 5 minutes by car.

Features

What can help minimize the negative impact of bad ecology on our body? Of course, being in places where there is a sufficient amount of green space, away from the gassed motorways, in a quiet and calm park area. It is in such an ecologically clean corner that the Voskhod hotel is located in Almetyevsk.

Distinctive features of the hotel is a comfortable number of rooms, high-quality service and good value for money. Guests appreciated the hotel‚Äôs interior, beautiful staircases with graceful railings, interesting art reproductions on the walls, lamps with soft lighting.

Location

Despite the fact that the Voskhod Hotel (Almetyevsk) is located very close to the city park, vacationers will not have problems with transport, the infrastructure here is also well developed. So, for example, you can get to Tatnefteprom or to any office building in the center in 5-7 minutes by car. The best option would be to walk directly through the beautiful park, which will take no more than 15-20 minutes.

The exact address of the hotel "Sunrise": Almetyevsk, st. Shevchenko, 48.

Which number to choose?

The interior of the hotel is very modern, the rooms are made in a classic style, light colors prevail. Guests can opt for standards (single, double and quadruple), junior suites and suites. Absolutely all rooms are quite spacious, equipped with appliances (flat-screen televisions with cable channels, air conditioning, telephones), well-furnished (cabinets, comfortable beds, desks, chairs, bedside tables), bathrooms with toiletries and towel sets.

Suites and junior suites are usually chosen for important business partners, company directors and top managers. They are more spacious, with loggias, divided into working and sleeping areas, in the bathrooms for the guests are prepared white terry bathrobes, slippers. After a busy day, you can relax in the fragrant bathtub (standard rooms only have showers). The interior here is more expensive and sophisticated, which can be seen in the photo of the hotel "Sunrise" in Almetyevsk (below).

Throughout the hotel "Vostok" in Almetyevsk there is free access to the Internet (Wi-Fi). A hairdryer and ironing facilities are provided upon request. In the corridors there are always full coolers with clean water.

Prices and booking conditions

The minimum cost of accommodation starts with a quadruple standard room, one guest will cost 1,500 rubles per day. The price of one place in such a room, designed for two guests, will be 1800 rubles per day. For a day in a junior suite and suite you need to pay 3,700 rubles. and 4400 rubles, respectively (prices per room).

On affiliate booking sites, room rates will be slightly higher. If you are not ready to overpay, you can make a reservation by calling the free phone number of the Voskhod Hotel in Almetyevsk or by sending a request to the email address. Payment for the room is possible both in cash and non-cash. Usually about 20% is paid to the hotel account in advance, and the rest of the amount is paid at the time of check-in (accept cards).

What are the guests fed

Guests can enjoy breakfast, lunch or dinner in the hotel's dining room restaurant or use the room service. The quality and variety of dishes at the guests, judging by the reviews, was not satisfactory. If necessary, diet or vegetarian dishes can be selected from the menu. A professional team of chefs prepares the best dishes of European and national Tatar cuisine for vacationers.

The hotel‚Äôs morning meal is included in the price. Vacationers appreciated a wide range of dishes: cereals, cereals and granola, pastries, sandwiches, eggs, sausages.

The bar offers a good assortment of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, snacks. Near the hotel "Sunrise" there are several good cafes and restaurants where you can eat at affordable prices. Most of all, tourists liked the "Batyr" (40 meters from the hotel) and "Kirlay" (a 15-20 minute walk).

Service maintenance

The hotel provides a room that (depending on the purpose of use) is transformed into a banquet or conference room. Residents of Almetyevsk often celebrate celebrations here, organize wedding ceremonies with on-site registration.

For business events (seminars, briefings, trainings, conferences, etc.), everything is provided: chairs and tables, multimedia projectors, boards, microphones, audio and video equipment, LCD TVs. A small cozy meeting room with upholstered furniture and a table is also equipped.

Guests arriving in the city by car can leave it in a private secure parking free of charge for the duration of their stay at the hotel.

The small spa complex offers basic beauty and wellness treatments. In the beauty salon you can make a wonderful hairstyle or fashionable manicure before an important event. Guests can also use the laundry, ATM, without leaving the hotel.

What they write in reviews

There are very few negative impressions of hotel accommodation. Of the main ones, one can note low-quality water for drinking in the room (it was noted above that special coolers were installed in the corridors for these purposes), and average quality of wireless Internet connection. Some felt that the staff was not welcoming enough, the pathos blew from the interior, and the placement itself was mediocre. Others complained about the lack of kettles in standard rooms, old televisions.

The guests really liked the clean and comfortable rooms that did not require repair, high-quality and satisfying food (the pastries were especially tasty). Grateful visitors also appreciated the convenient location of the "Sunrise" near a beautiful park where you could ride the rides. Many people say that they are not stopping at this place for the first time, are going to come again. Positive-minded guests liked the lounge on the second floor, where you can calmly chat, sitting on soft armchairs, or read an interesting book, which can be borrowed in the library corner of the hotel.

Summarize

Based on the information and reviews about the Voskhod Hotel in Almetyevsk, it can be noted that this place will be a good choice for living on a business trip in the city. For accommodation in a room with increased comfort, it is worth booking a junior suite or suite, if you plan to only stay at the hotel, a standard room is also suitable. An important advantage is its convenient location in an ecologically clean environment, but close to the city center.
