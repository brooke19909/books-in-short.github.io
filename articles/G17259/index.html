Hotel Adora Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Belek, Belek Center): reviews of tourists, photos

Belek is a pretty young resort. Until 1984, it was a wild coast. Today, here you can see a huge number of respectable hotels. Today, Belek is considered one of the most expensive resorts in Turkey. He earned great popularity among tourists due to the beautiful golf courses located here, the design of which was done by the best Turkish designers. Another distinctive feature of the resort is a series of pine and eucalyptus plantations that create an amazing aromatic atmosphere. There are many turtles in these forests.

Despite the fact that the resort is young, only a few kilometers away are ancient ruins, architectural monuments and other signs of ancient antiquity.

There are many hotel complexes on the coast, one of which will be described in this article.

general description





Adora Golf Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Belek) was built in 1992 in the resort town, located near Antalya, on the Mediterranean coast. In 2009, the hotel was renovated. Its territory covers 116,000 square meters. There is a private beach 300 meters long. Visitors to the complex can be representatives of completely different ages, everyone can choose for themselves the most suitable type of recreation: active, medical, business, corporate, family, youth.

Everyone will be able to appreciate the high level of service, use the all-inclusive system, enjoy real Turkish cuisine, sunbathe on a sandy beach under the bright rays of the sun, swim in the sea or in the pool, breathe clean air in the shade under palm trees. The hotel hosts various recreational activities and sports programs for both adults and children.

Accommodation

Adora Resort Hotel 5 * (Belek) includes: two 4-storey buildings ("Panorama" and "Fortune"); one 3-storey building (Family House) and ten 2-storey cottages (Club House). A wide variety of rooms overlooking the garden or the Mediterranean Sea will make your stay unforgettable. The hotel has 500 apartments for every consumer taste.

Room Types





The hotel offers its guests several categories of rooms that differ in their level of comfort. But they are all incredibly comfortable and cozy. All types of apartments will be described below.

  1. Land view - rooms with garden views, with or without a balcony.
  2. Duplex - 2-floor rooms designed for families with children.
  3. Bungalows - cool apartments with ceramic flooring, located in 2-storey cottages in the garden area.
  4. Family - a room in the form of a studio apartment, comfortable and convenient, reminiscent of a home environment.
  5. Suite - the rooms are located on the top floors of the hotel, have sea views, a pleasant interior, a bedroom, a living room and 2 balconies.

The rooms also have: minibar, safe with electronic lock, satellite TV, Internet, direct dial telephone, air conditioning, hair dryer, children's attributes are available upon request.
