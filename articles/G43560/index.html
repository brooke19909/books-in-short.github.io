Guest houses in Loo with pool: overview and photo

For many Russians, the small resort village of Loo has become the personification of a serene and relaxing holiday on the Black Sea coast. The picturesque village is located in the Lazarevsky district of the magnificent Sochi at a distance of eighteen kilometers from the city center.



The subtropical climate, a magnificent clean pebble beach 60 meters wide attract thousands of tourists to relax in Loo. Guest houses with a pool, many hotels and resorts offer excellent service. Most tourists believe that Loo is the cleanest village in the suburbs of the famous resort city. In this article, we will present you only a few guest houses in Loo with a pool. We will indicate their addresses here.

"At Christina" (Yeniseiskaya St., 247)

This popular guest house is located in the very center, a 15-minute walk from the sea. Small but very comfortable double and triple rooms with functional, modern furniture. Each room has a fridge and TV.

On the territory there is a spacious equipped recreation area with a gazebo and an outdoor pool. There is also a barbecue area. Guest houses in Loo (with pool) are usually well located. In the vicinity of this house there are numerous shops where you can buy everything you need for your vacation. Very close is the famous water park "AquaLoo".



Guest House U Christina is 5 km from the railway station and 52 km from Sochi Airport.

Guest house "Irina" (st. Luchesarnaya, d. 48)

Today it’s not difficult to find lodging with a pool in Loo Guest houses are a serious competitor to many boarding houses and resorts. An example of this is “At Irina”, which is surrounded by the guest houses “Radiant”, “Mosenergo”, “Don Dawns”, “Ivushka”, “AquaLoo”, “White Nights”. Nearby is the resort infrastructure of these pensions and resorts: bowling, pools, saunas, billiards, discos. Here you can take a course of wellness treatments.



Not all guest houses in Loo (with a pool) are separated from the busy highways by dense forests. But “Irina” is surrounded by a luxurious massif with cork oak and relic pines. For walks in the fresh air, a huge park area is at the guests' disposal. The territory of this house is literally immersed in greenery, it is decorated with exotic southern plants.

Vacationers can relax by the outdoor pool, located in the shade of trees. Guests arriving in their own car can leave it in a large guarded parking lot, which is adjacent to the house.

"Anaida" (Altayskaya St., 22)

Nowadays, thousands of tourists choose to stay in Sochi (Loo). Guest houses with pools, which, of course, belongs to Anaida, are invited to have a good rest by cheerful youth companies and families with children. It features:

  • numbers "economy", "standard" and "luxury";
  • tables under a canopy;
  • clean and spacious yard;
  • barbecue area;
  • equipped kitchen;
  • parking.

A shuttle service is available from Sochi Train Station. Each person arriving at the meeting is given a bottle of homemade wine. It offers an outdoor pool of 6 x 4 meters for adults, as well as a pool for babies, a sauna.

"9 Avenue" (Yeniseyskaya St., 9)

Many guest houses in Loo (with pool) are located almost on the coast. For example, 9 Avenue is located 400 meters from a pebble beach, 40 meters wide, which is considered the cleanest on the coast. The rooms of this guest house are particularly cozy and comfortable, they are decorated in warm colors. In addition, when creating the interior, exclusively environmentally friendly finishing materials were used. It has internet and an outdoor pool. There is a picnic area in the courtyard.



Villa Va-Lensia (Yeniseyskaya St., 9)

Unfortunately, many tourists are frightened off by high prices for accommodation in Sochi. Loo, guest houses with a pool, in particular, are quite affordable. Here, the price of a room per day starts at 300 rubles, which, you see, is affordable for people with low incomes.



Va-Lensia Guesthouse has twenty-two rooms - Economy, Standard, and Suite. All of them are air-conditioned, which allows guests to enjoy a pleasant stay even in the most intense heat. All rooms have a shower room. The villa is very brightly decorated, which immediately raises the mood of guests. It is located a ten minute walk from the beach and fifteen minutes from the popular AquaLoo complex. There is a small pool on site where guests can cool off in the heat. There is a bar with refreshments.

"Mountain Gorge" (Yeniseyskaya St., 47/1)

For the most part, guest houses in Loo (with a pool) are an ideal choice for a family vacation. This fully applies to the guest house "Mountain Gorge", located in an ecologically clean and quiet place.

On the magnificent well-groomed territory there is a gazebo entwined with spitting and equipped with a barbecue area. Nearby are grocery stores, cafes, canteens, bars, pharmacies and the AquaLoo complex. Here, discos are held every night.



Upon request, guests can choose a comprehensive meal - 700 rubles per person. You can choose one, two or three meals a day. At the service of vacationers:

  • large and clean beach;
  • the Internet;
  • parking;
  • Children's swimming pool;
  • outdoor pool for adults;
  • gym;
  • TV;
  • picnic area.

Columbus (Tallinn, 46/6)

This quiet and comfortable place is located in an ecologically clean area, away from the hustle and bustle of large resorts and the noise of night discos, trains and cars. Ideal for family summer vacations. This is a three-story building with small cozy rooms, equipped with modern and comfortable furniture.



The rooms offer magnificent views of the mountains and the sea. On the territory there is a swimming pool with a relaxation area, which has sun loungers and tables. In addition, all vacationers can visit the sauna with a relaxation room, use the parking lot with 24-hour video surveillance. There is a cafe at the hotel where guests are served home-cooked meals. The bar always has a large selection of soft drinks and light homemade wines. A 24-hour shuttle service is available from the train station and airport.
