Weaving newspaper tubes for beginners: the basics and secrets of craftsmanship

Beautiful and stylish interior decoration items have always attracted the attention of both new guests and regular visitors to the house. Photo frames and candy baskets, caskets and posters - all this is an integral part of a cozy home. You can purchase such items in specialized stores, or you can create them yourself with minimal financial costs.

Weaving newspaper tubes for beginners is not only easy, but also very exciting! We will talk about what materials you need, as well as the most light and popular weaving technique available even to children. By the way, if you decide to involve your child in the manufacture of crafts, then an excellent and exciting pastime is guaranteed!

Materials and accessories



In order for weaving from newspaper tubes for beginners to become as comfortable as possible and succeed at the first attempt to get a decent result, you need to take care of the preparation of materials. What is required? Of course, newspapers. Many needlewomen advise taking thin and non-glossy specimens. Because they are harder to stain. Also required for work are scissors, a ruler, clerical glue and a long thin knitting needle. A large number of ordinary office paper clips or clothespins will be useful. Cardboard, an unnecessary shoe box, a brush and a suitable shade of acrylic or water based paint. These are the basic tools that you will need to create a spectacular box or box. Now consider the main methods of weaving newspaper tubes.

How to make tubules

In order to get a beautiful product, you should devote enough time to the preliminary preparation of the material. It is recommended that newspaper sheets be cut into strips 5-6 cm wide. After you prepare the strips, we will master the weaving of newspaper tubes for beginners. Using a knitting needle, gently wind the strip of paper diagonally, turning it into a tube. Try to make a 38-cm tube from a 39 cm long strip. At first, you will have to experiment a little, but in the future the process will take a few minutes. Using glue, it is necessary to fix the last turn. Now, with light rotational movements, remove the tube from the knitting needle and leave it to dry. In the same way we prepare the rest of the consumable. The more you make "artificial vines", the easier the weaving process will become, because you do not have to interrupt during the work in order to form a few more pieces. Next, you should decide: will you paint the product or leave it in its original form?

If you decide to perform it in a certain color scheme, then it's time to color the material. Separate the color and apply with a brush to the tubes. Then roll them across the film or across the table to achieve a uniform shade. Leave to dry. And the most crucial and pleasant moment comes: weaving from newspaper tubes. For beginners, it is usually not particularly difficult.

How to assemble a product

Many recommend starting the first weaving lessons with the simplest thing - the baskets. Select a few tight tubes and form a bottom. To do this, fasten several rods in the shape of the sun, fix them with glue, and then begin work. Layered weaving from newspaper tubes means laying each next row on top of the previous one. Thus, you will get a dense and beautiful braid. Having formed the bottom, gradually move to the side walls. The size of the basket depends on your imagination and desire. Leave a few long tubes from which to form the base of the handle. Then braid it, carefully tuck in and fix the edges with glue. Having varnished the product, you will get your first job!
