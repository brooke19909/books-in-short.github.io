Hotel Royal Holiday Palace 5 * (Antalya): overview, features and reviews of tourists

Among the endless list of resorts in Turkey, Antalya occupies a special place. This is the oldest settlement, which contains a number of historical attractions, well-developed infrastructure and plenty of entertainment that can give unforgettable memories to vacationers of all generations.





green parks and singing fountains are just a few that are accessible to travelers.

Another feature is comfortable accommodation, a high level of service and the friendly staff of local hotels. The five-star Royal Holiday Palace Hotel is a bright and affordable opportunity to spend a carefree vacation away from the hustle and bustle of city life, enjoying sunbathing and swimming in the waters of the Mediterranean Sea.

general characteristics

The hotel is young, was commissioned in April 2011. The building is in the style of a historic ten-story castle, equipped with six elevators. The total area is 53,000 square meters. The distance to the center of Antalya is 17 km, while the airport is 15 km. Such a favorable location led to popularity among lovers of family vacations - there is no need to tire children with long journeys. Due to the mild Turkish climate in these parts, the holiday season is year-round, which allows you to come and spend vacations at any time of the year.





Check-in is carried out at 14:00, payment is accepted by credit cards of international payment systems. Pets are strictly forbidden. The friendly and friendly staff speaks English, Russian and Turkish. The hotel operates on the Ultra All Inclusive system, which allows you to relax and enjoy the full stay here.

The territory of the hotel complex is characterized by unprecedented saturation, rationality of the placement of infrastructure facilities, spacious aquazone and numerous tropical greenery. From the main building to the beach itself, there is a palm promenade that allows you to breathe plenty of fresh air, getting to the coveted coast.

Number of rooms

The interior of the apartments at the Royal Holiday Palace 5 * hotel (Antalya) is made in an oriental style, there are bright colors and high-quality furniture, which includes a bed, an armchair, a table, a wardrobe and a cupboard. The floors are carpeted. The chambers are equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone and hairdryer. The bathroom has disposable slippers, bathrobes, personal care products and toiletries. The minibar is replenished daily with juices, water and carbonated drinks. A tea set consisting of tea, coffee and sugar is updated daily. Towels are cleaned and changed every day; bed linen is changed at least three times a week.

Supply system

You can quench your hunger in the central restaurant Palace. Meals are served in the form of a buffet according to a predetermined schedule three times a day. Lunch and dinner - an extended menu that includes dietary dishes. Local drinks and some imported drinks are personally brought by waiters to tables with guests, with the exception of breakfast. Alcohol for breakfast is not served.

Additional facilities for satisfying hunger and thirst

You can taste the masterpieces of Italian cuisine at Vapiano. Whores of the Far East are presented in the Hashi restaurant. Lal treats visitors with delicacies of national cuisine. Ice cream, a favorite treat for both children and adults, is available for lunch and dinner.

Aquazone

The pool complex stretches throughout the hotel Royal Holiday Palace 5 * (Antalya). There are four pools, among which one is with heated water, and its own water park. It includes five slides of various modifications for adults and three for children. Soft sunbeds, umbrellas and towels are available to everyone. In the winter season, there is only an indoor pool that is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable and safe swimming.

Beach

Royal Holiday Palace 5 * hotel is located on the first coastline. The entrance to the water is gentle, which leads to safe swimming of the children. Beach sand and pebble zone is equipped with numerous sun loungers with umbrellas and mattresses. Towels are provided free of charge.





You can entertain yourself on the volleyball court and numerous water attractions that require additional payment. Among them: scooters, riding a "banana", with a parachute above the water, diving and others. The built pontoon, on which there are sun loungers and descents into the water, makes it possible to sunbathe among the azure waters of the Mediterranean and dive to the depths.

Toddler Leisure

About Royal Holiday Palace 5 * (Antalya), people who come to rest with children leave positive feedback. The organization of leisure for kids is thought out by the administration to the smallest detail. A mini club is available for children from 4 to 12 years old. Those younger are required to be accompanied by adults. Here children can play board games, watch cartoons with the company. Crafts and creative tasks are carried out under the guidance of a qualified educator. Younger guests can play bowling, mini-basketball or have fun with many toys.

Paid services include coloring t-shirts and work done using sand. One of the favorite activities of young princesses is face painting, when under the guidance of an experienced and creative master, the baby's face is painted with various colors. In the evening, the younger generation expects a mini-disco, where they can feel like adults.





Numerous electric rides and swings are located in the park area and require additional payment. There is also an outdoor play area with many walkways, slides, carousels and swings. It will be equally interesting to spend time in the outdoor pool, equipped with safe slides with open gutters. Enthusiasm, drive, positive and good mood are provided to each baby.
