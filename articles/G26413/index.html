How to pay online via Sberbank card via the Internet, via phone?

The modern world can not be imagined without the Internet for a long time, this is no longer a luxury, but an indispensable helper of a person in both labor and personal life. But the services of an Internet provider are not free, you have to pay for them every month, and this procedure takes time, which a modern person has very little. Sberbank always thinks about the convenience of its customers and makes it possible to pay for the Internet through a Sberbank card.

Payment methods for Internet services with a card

Sberbank offers four ways of how to pay for the Internet through a Sberbank card:

  • using the Sberbank-Online option;
  • through the Mobile Bank;
  • through an ATM or self-service terminals;
  • at the cash desk of the bank.

Using Sberbank-Online when paying for Internet services

The easiest way to pay for the Internet through a Sberbank card is through the Sberbank Online service. In addition, it allows you to conduct cash transactions on the card directly from home.

But this option is available only if you are registered in the system. Registration is very simple: you can do this at an ATM or contact a bank specialist. After all the necessary actions, you will receive a password and login with which you will enter the Sberbank-Online system and be able to carry out various operations with money.





To pay for the Internet you need to go to the section "Operations and payments", where you need to select the item "Internet". The system will display a list of providers from which you select the one you need. If your provider is not in the list, then you will have to enter the details manually. Then the fields related to the payment amount, personal account and the month for which the payment is made are filled. If you pay the same amount every month to the same provider, you can save the entered data as a template, then the next time the payment will go even faster.

Internet payment using "Mobile Bank"

The option "Mobile Bank" is another way of how to pay for the Internet through a Sberbank card. Through the phone, Sberbank card holders can also replenish their mobile balance, repay a loan, pay for utility bills and so on. To do this, you need to know the basic SMS commands.

So, how to pay online via Sberbank card from your mobile? The following SMS message is sent to the short number 900: MTS XXXXXXXX 700 N..N, where:

  • MTS is a letter designation of the Internet provider in favor of which payment is made;
  • XXXXXXXX - payment details, this may be the personal account of the subscriber, the contract number, and so on;
  • 500 - payment amount in rubles;
  • N..N - the last numbers of the Sberbank card number.

Internet payment via ATM and service terminals

The third easy way to pay for the Internet through a Sberbank card is to pay at ATMs and self-service terminals. You must have the card with you. It will be better if you choose a Sberbank ATM for payment.

Insert the Sberbank card into an ATM or terminal, enter the PIN code and select the option you need, make a payment. We recommend that you keep the check, it may be needed as a confirmation of payment.





Internet payment by Sberbank card through cash desk

Another option for how to pay for the Internet through a Sberbank card is to contact the cashier. This remains for most bank customers in the most familiar way. To do this, you should take a receipt, but if you lose it, you should not be upset. Details of the Internet service provider serving you can be found in the contract.

How to get a Sberbank card?

In order to be able to use the methods described above (with the exception of payment via cash desk) of Internet payment, a prerequisite is the presence of a Sberbank plastic card.





The procedure for issuing a card is simple, you only need a passport and you will need to choose a suitable card product from Sberbank for you. Not all cards have the same capabilities, not all of them are designed to pay for online purchases, especially in foreign online stores. Before applying for a card, consult with a bank employee.

Those customers who have a savings book from Sberbank can exchange it for a bank card, which will have all the advantages and capabilities of plastic cards. Sberbank did not disregard its young customers. If you are 14 years old and you have a passport, then Sberbank offers to issue a Youth card. All bank cards that are serviced by Sberbank can be connected to the Sberbank-Online and Mobile Bank systems.

The Mobile Bank system is especially appreciated by young people who are used to always being in touch. Using it, you can not only pay for communication services, but it is also a very convenient way to resolve the issue of how to pay for the Internet through a Sberbank card from your phone. In addition, it allows you to pay utility bills at any time, pay off a loan debt at Sberbank or transfer money to another person, knowing only his phone number.
