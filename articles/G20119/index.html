Tariff "Super MTS" (St. Petersburg): description. Free calls to MTS in St. Petersburg and the Leningrad Region

The Mobile TeleSystems company offers the Super MTS tariff to those subscribers who mainly make calls who do not use the Internet very actively. In general conditions, it was noted that all calls to MTS numbers in St. Petersburg and the Leningrad Region are free. Communication is not limited to any packages of minutes. There is a full unlimited. An additional bonus condition of the tariff is 100 Mb of traffic per day.

Connection of a tariff plan

Persons who are not yet subscribers of Mobile TeleSystems can purchase a tariff in any communication salon. The starter kit, which includes a SIM card with a new number and the Super MTS tariff plan, costs 200 rubles.

Those people who are already among the company's subscribers can simply change the tariff on their SIM card. This is done in several ways:
