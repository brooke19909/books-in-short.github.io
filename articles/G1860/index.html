Rest at the recreation centers "Romantic" and "Romance": a brief overview

With the onset of summer, many think about where to go on vacation. Of course, most dream of relaxing in overseas resorts with golden beaches, a turquoise sea-ocean and dizzying entertainment.

But in the end, a vacation spot is chosen depending on financial capabilities. One of the most affordable in terms of cost are recreation facilities.

Depending on your preferences, you can choose a vacation on the Black Sea - then welcome to Zatoka or Kacha, a comfortable rest in the Moscow region - in this case, there is a recreation center in Istra, and if your hobby is fishing, then take tackle and go to Lake Sugoyak in Chelyabinsk region.

But before you go to one of the listed recreation centers, read a brief overview of each of them.

Holiday Village "Romantic", Kacha (Sevastopol)

This recreation area is located in the village of Kacha, which is a suburb of Sevastopol. The village is located 35 km north of the hero city, on the Crimean coast, at the very blue Black Sea. In this area you can sunbathe on the pebble-sand beaches with convenient access to the sea.

The recreation center "Romantic" offers vacationers to stay in comfortable cottages, equipped with standard household appliances and indispensable air conditioning. If desired, you can rent a separate house in which 4 rooms, or a cottage for 2 rooms, each of which is designed for 2 places and has a separate entrance.





The recreation center has its own dining room, where guests are provided with breakfast, lunch and dinner. The menu contains dishes of Russian, Ukrainian, Caucasian, Tatar and European cuisine. Here you can always order seafood, meat, chicken, a large assortment of local vegetables and fruits.

A wonderful vacation awaits you at the recreation center "Romantic". On its territory there are sports and playgrounds, on the beach there are retail outlets selling soft drinks and snacks, a rental point for sun loungers.





Base rest "Romantic" in Istra

Residents of the Moscow region and not only will surely enjoy the rest at the recreation center, which is located in the village of Lopotovo, on the shore of the Istra reservoir. Here you can escape from the everyday bustle of the metropolis and breathe into the lungs the healing and clean air of the pine forest, as well as hold corporate events. The recreation center awaits guests not only in summer but also in winter.

Holidays at the recreation center "Romantic" will be remembered for walking through the territory surrounded by firs, pines and birches, skiing on beautiful slopes. Much attention is paid to the organization of sporting events: there is a football field, volleyball and basketball courts, tables for playing table tennis.





On the territory of the base there is a cinema hall, a restaurant with excellent service and an excellent menu of home cooking, a sports bar with a large TV, a sauna, 5 Russian baths. There is a private beach equipped with sun loungers with a gentle slope to the water and a wonderful view of the sunset. What is noteworthy, there are almost no mosquitoes.

The rooms where the guests of the base are accommodated are cozy, according to their reviews, and in the winter they are very warm. The hotel housing fund is designed to receive 47 guests. The price includes breakfast and lunch.

If you want to feel like a real tourist, you can stay in tents.

As a minus, there is a lack of Wi-Fi throughout the base, but people come here to relax, and not to "hang" in the virtual space. And of course, what a vacation without barbecue and barbecue - for this there are barbecue facilities and gazebos.

Rest at the recreation center near the lake Sugoyak

The recreation center "Romantic" on Sugoyak is located near the village of Lazurny in the Chelyabinsk region, 35 km northeast of the regional center. The area is surrounded on all sides by forest-steppes.

Lake Sugoyak is known for the purity of its waters, which are rich in trace elements and salts useful for the human body. According to their content, water belongs to the sodium-carbonate type, and such a composition is usually used in spa treatment.

Holidays at the "Romantic" recreation center near Lake Sugoyak are chosen by lovers of native nature, as well as those who want to inexpensively mark a significant event away from the dusty city. Here you can celebrate a birthday, have a meeting with friends, relax with your family. The recreation center "Romantic" on Lake Sugoyak awaits guests at any time of the year. It is designed for the simultaneous placement of 70 people who can be accommodated in wooden houses.





Fishermen love to come here, as there are a lot of different fish in the lake: pike, zander, carp, crucian carp, perch, mackerel, bream, whitefish. You can fish here not only in summer but also in winter.

Those who are indifferent to fishing will be able to sunbathe on the sandy beaches and swim in the clear water. Fans of thrills in winter will enjoy swimming in the ice hole, after which you can warm up in a Russian bath.

Odessa "Romance"

The Ukrainian recreation center "Romance" (Zatoka) is located at Solnechnaya station in the Odessa region. It has a very convenient location: only 100 m is a sandy beach with a gentle entrance to the sea, and the territory of the base is located in the very center of the village. Nearby you can see local attractions, as well as go to the market and shops.

The recreation center "Romance" is a small private property, which includes a residential building, as well as its own cafe, providing home meals for guests. Each room has air conditioning, TV, fridge, toilet, shower, as well as a balcony with outdoor furniture.

The territory of the base is well landscaped, and thanks to the green spaces it is easy to breathe.





Zatoka is a popular Ukrainian resort that offers guests numerous entertainment. The beach area is equipped with sun loungers, shaded canopies, water slides are built here, and you can also engage in water sports. In the evening, tourists are attracted to neon lights by discos, night clubs, cafes and bars.

In addition, you can go from Zatoka to an excursion to Odessa, which was sung in many songs and works of popular writers and poets. It is located at a distance of only 60 km from Zatoka.

Good mood and romance

A wonderful mood awaits tourists at any of these recreation centers. The only difference is that for some, a good rest is a chic hotel in a fashionable island resort, and for others, songs by the fire in a wonderful company and accommodation in tents or wooden log cabins.

We used to admire all overseas, forgetting about our origins. But many foreigners strive to get into those countries in which a distinctive culture has been preserved and where you can feel a living human soul. Come to domestic recreation centers and plunge headlong into the romance of your native expanses!
