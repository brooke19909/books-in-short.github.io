Samsung Duos: All 2008-2015 Models

Phones that support the work of two SIM cards is one of the most convenient inventions of our time, which was appreciated by many users: often moving from region to region, having a personal and work number, finding the most profitable offers from different operators, etc. Among all A variety of smartphones with two SIM cards is worth highlighting the Samsung Duos line, all of which we will try to reflect in this article.

Samsung Duos: Features

Phones that support two SIM cards are divided into three large groups:

  1. With two active SIM cards - in talk mode with one SIM activated, the second one can also receive an incoming call, and the subscriber, in turn, can switch between them.
  2. SIM cards are equally active only in standby mode - during a conversation an idle SIM card is not available for those trying to reach the user.
  3. Only one SIM is involved in the work - to use the second, you must disable the work of the first. The mode is typical for the most budget models.

Such principles distinguish all Samsung Duos models, touch and button. Let us further consider their diversity.

2008: the beginning

In 2008, the first three models were released:

  • The pioneer is the push-button D780 DuoS, had two simultaneously working SIM cards. In addition, the phone boasted a two megapixel rear camera, a WAP browser, a USB port, Bluetooch, and a 1200 mAh battery.
  • The D780 DuoS Gold Edition was an exact copy of the first model, but only with a noble golden solution of the case.
  • The D980 DuoS is the first sensor model in the Duos line. It had a screen with a diagonal of 2.6 inches, as well as a very good camera at that time at 5 Mpx. A stylus was also included with the phone.

2013: continuation of the story
