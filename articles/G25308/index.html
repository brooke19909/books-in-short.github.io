How to restart Apple Watch: detailed instructions

Apple watches are incredibly popular all over the world. A smart gadget is able to perform almost any function, just establish pairing with the phone. Few know that a watch can work even without a smartphone. Regardless of whether a connection is established with an iPhone or not, the clock will always display time, weather, sunset and sunrise, and much more. In addition, two gigabytes of memory are allocated for music files in the device. You can also view photos, monitor your physical activity. The device has rich functionality. But occasionally there are times when the watch freezes due to the enormous load. How to restart Apple Watch? More on this later.

What watches are made of

There are two buttons on the watch. By clicking on them, you can completely control the operation of the gadget. The keys perform the same functions as the keys on the iPhone: the side button is the power button, and the Digital Crown is an analogue of the Home button on the smartphone. Beginner users often wonder how to restart the Apple Watch. To do this is easy, you just need to perform all the actions correctly.

Clock reset

Usually, to restore the working state of a device, just put it on standby. After the screen is unlocked, the watch will come back to life. Management is carried out using keys, gestures, sensors. Here are the ways you can unlock your gadget:

  • Press the power button.
  • Make a gesture "view time."
  • Touch the display.

Sometimes the device freezes completely and does not respond to any touch. In this case, you can go in three ways:

  • Turn off the clock in the usual way.
  • Force restart them.
  • Reset the device to factory settings.

It is very important to remember that forced rebooting can ruin the watch or it will not work correctly. It is recommended to resort to this restart method only when all other methods have failed.

The usual way to reboot

If the Apple Watch freezes, how to restart them? To get started, try disconnecting the device as usual. You must hold down the power button for several seconds until the ‚ÄúShutdown‚Äù command and the apple logo appear. The device turns on in the same way, you should hold down the power key.

Forced reboot

When the gadget freezes and does not respond to any actions, only a forced reboot can help. How to force restart Apple Watch? You must simultaneously hold the Digital Crown and the power button. Without releasing your fingers, wait a while until the work is completed (until the apple icon appears). After that, the buttons can be released.

Conclusion

You should now understand how to restart the Apple Watch. You must act according to the above instructions. Only then will the reboot succeed and the watch will return to its normal operating mode. Here are three basic rules that you should familiarize yourself with before resetting your watch.

You cannot restart the watch while recharging it. Therefore, before you restart the Apple Watch, you must disconnect the gadget from the power source. When the operating system of the watch is updated, restarting is prohibited, this can cause the watch to malfunction. Wait until the system finishes the update process, and only after that proceed with the reboot. Forced reboot is intended only for force majeure situations. If it is possible to restart the clock in the standard way, it is better to forget about the forced one.

Errors can occur in the system of any modern gadget, this is normal. It is important to be able to eliminate them correctly.
