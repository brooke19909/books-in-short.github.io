Choose a beach: Ayia Napa

Ayia Napa is waiting for those who want to combine entertainment and relaxation . Cyprus beaches with developed infrastructure located along the entire coast. They are visited by a huge number of vacationers. At this resort, both young people and families can have a great time, you can visit the popular Ayia Napa beach, or you can choose privacy.





From the history

Previously, there was a small simple village on this place, not attractive at all. But over time, a favorable position: the gentle sea, warm sun all year round, and most importantly, clean sand on the shore - made it a rather popular resort town.

Beaches

A long entry into the sea will allow kids to wallow in the water without risk to health, and this is what attracts couples with children. No need to be afraid of northeast winds. The position of the resort is such that they do not blow out there. Blue flag - every beach is awarded with this award. Ayia Napa monitors the cleanliness of the coast and coastal waters. But there is one feature: you will have to pay for using sun loungers and umbrellas, and prices do not stand still. Nissi Beach is the largest beach. Ayia Napa located it in the western part of the coast. He will offer not only passive relaxation on the beach, but also rent sports equipment and participate in games.

After them there is an opportunity to eat in one of the many restaurants or bars. Here you can visit the scuba diving school and conduct the first classes in the water.

Makronissos is a beach that connects the three bays. A very beautiful place, and the sand on the shore is pure white. Fans of outdoor activities will be offered to go water skiing, catamarans, boats, plunge into the depths of the sea (diving and scuba diving).

Landa is a small beach. Ayia Napa hid it between the largest recreation areas. But here you can not just swim in the sea, but also have fun. The infrastructure on the coast is very well developed. Ayia Thekla is a small church that gave its name to another beach. It is famous for its white sand, calm sea and incredible nature.

Pantahou is a beach in the eastern part of the resort. It combines not only a place of rest, but also good fishing. The east coast closes Konnos beach. The children will be delighted with him. Golden sand, various water attractions and sports. This place is also popular among the local population. In the west as well as in the east, secluded spots can be found along the entire coast. But they are often deprived of comfortable rest conditions. But there is another reason that attracts tourists to the beaches of Ayia Napa. A photo! The gorgeous photo shoots that can be ordered here will delight and envy others. Very often, such services are used by newlyweds, as well as young and married couples.





Leisure

Recently, this resort has become very popular among young people, and therefore entertainment is tailored to this generation. Here are hotels for every taste: from three stars to luxury apartments, which are often rented by entire groups. The resort has a lot of various cafes, bars, discos, nightclubs. Foamy parties are held periodically, which gradually turn into dancing on the street.
