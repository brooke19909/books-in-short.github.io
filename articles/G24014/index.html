Hotel Sanya Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 * (China / Hainan Island / Sanya): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Many, speaking of China, immediately represent its huge and densely populated cities on the mainland, where there are many factories and where you can inexpensively buy a wide variety of goods. But this is an erroneous opinion. There are a lot of good resorts in China, most of which are very far from our usual ideas about this country.



Overview

One of the great places to relax in the Middle Kingdom is a tropical island and at the same time the Hainan province. This is a favorite place for those who prefer to swim, sunbathe and be treated in the healing springs, which are quite a lot. I must say that for Russians, especially residents of Siberia and the Far East, Hainan has long been a beaten track.

The island is located in southern China. It is often called paradise. Once it was part of Eurasia. Today, the Hainan is separated from China by the strait. There are many extinct volcanoes on it, after the eruption of which formed thermal springs with healing properties. This makes vacation on the island not only enjoyable, but also beneficial to health. Hainan, according to many, is "eastern Hawaii." There is everything that is characteristic of this tropical resort - coconut and banana palms, a turquoise sea, warm water, white sand on the beaches, a huge number of beautiful coral reefs, etc. The Russians chose the southern part of the island for relaxation. There are several small resorts, for example, Dadonghai, Yalongwan and, of course, Sanya. The number of hotels and large hotel complexes in this part of the island is constantly increasing. They are able to satisfy any, even the most demanding tourist, choosing for himself the direction of Sanya. Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 * (China) is one of them. It is about him that will be discussed in this article.



Description

This 4-star hotel is located on the shores of the South China Sea. Initially, only locals from the northern regions of the Middle Kingdom came here. But for several years, Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel (Sanya 4 * Hainan) began to receive foreign tourists, including Russians. The hotel is within walking distance of the picturesque Dadonghai Bay. In just half an hour you can drive to the airport, which is located on the island. This is one of the reasons why Russians choose Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 *. Hainan is famous not only for its excellent tourist infrastructure, but also for very beautiful places that must be seen. Therefore, many of those who combine beach and cognitive types of relaxation, choose this direction.

The hotel was last renovated in 2015. Judging by the reviews, the first impression of him is that a person was in the palace of a rich nobleman. A huge "floor" chandelier, chic interiors, a spacious lounge - all this impresses tourists.

The city center is four kilometers away. There is a public transport stop at the entrance to the hotel.

Infrastructure

The territory of Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel (4 * Hainan) is a tropical garden and palm thickets. Literally a few minutes walk from it there is a supermarket, cafe, duty-free shop. The island has excellent transport. Therefore, hotel guests can easily reach local attractions. There is an ATM on site. Those who wish can use the free luggage room. This autumn hotel often hosts conferences and seminars, it has all the amenities for corporate and business meetings. Many large companies rent a 4 * business center and a well-equipped conference room at Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel. Fax and photocopy costs must be paid separately.



The reception is open around the clock. Its employees with the courtesy characteristic of the inhabitants of the Celestial Empire will help in solving any issues. A car and bicycle rental service is available on site.

Housing stock

Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 * is one five-story building and a complex of three-story villas. All rooms are compliant with four-star hotel standards. There are a hundred and six of them in the hotel. The highlight of this hotel is its floor-to-ceiling windows, so the rooms are always bright and comfortable. If desired, they can be closed with blackout curtains. Nutrition

Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 * (China, Sanya) offers its guests only the concept of "continental breakfast." On a beautifully decorated buffet-type trays there is always an abundance of dairy products, Chinese noodles, freshly baked buns, sausages, rice. There are many places near the hotel where tourists can have lunch and dinner. In addition, you can eat in the main restaurant Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 * by reservation. There is also a paid service "breakfast in the room."

Services for children

This hotel does not have a playground or a mini club for younger guests. From children's services here you can get only a crib in the room.

Beach

Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 * from the sea is two hundred meters away. Covering your own bathing area is sandy. Those who wish can take a free bus to the city beach. A shuttle is available every hour from nine in the morning until six in the evening.

The beach has a fairly developed infrastructure with showers, toilets, a bar. Sun loungers and umbrellas are available for a fee.

Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 *: reviews

Most Russians were impressed with their trip. Hainan, according to many, is not in vain called "eastern Hawaii." Everywhere is clean, tidy, especially when compared, for example, with Thai resorts.



As for the hotel itself, here the opinions of our countrymen are somewhat different. Some really liked its location, while others thought it was uncomfortable. Yuhuayuan Seaview Hotel 4 * is located on a hill, so there are many steps on its territory. On the other hand, this location provides a quiet and relaxing holiday away from noisy establishments.
