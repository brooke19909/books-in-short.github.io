Multicooker Redmond RMC-PM190: model review, reviews

A crock-pot is a device that allows you to facilitate housework, save time on cooking, and it can also cook breakfast for the owners without their control. A technique of such a plan is suitable for those who do not like to get up in the morning too early.

The article will focus on the Redmond RMC-PM190 model. Some believe that this device is too expensive, and for a lower cost there are great options with more diverse functionality. The approximate price is 9500 rubles. Those who ventured to purchase this technique did not regret their choice at all. The article discusses the pros, cons, reviews and some characteristics of the multicooker.



Technical specifications

The Redmond RMC-PM190 model is a multicooker combined with a pressure cooker. This is what explains the number of available programs. Its power is 900 watts. Heating is due to the tubular electric heater. The bowl has a volume of 5 liters. It is a ceramic container. Automatic modes - 23. The delayed start timer works in the range up to 24 hours. The device weighs a little more than 7.5 kg.

Modes

The Multicooker Redmond RMC-PM190 has many modes. Three programs are involved in stewing fish, meat and vegetables. There are as many options for soups. Cooking modes - three. You can cook food for a couple; There is the option of cooking legumes, as well as pilaf. Three cooking modes are integrated. You can cook bread, yogurt, milk porridge, pasta, pastries and popcorn. The latter mode is different in that the time remaining until the end of the programmed process is not displayed.



"Popcorn"

In order to properly prepare popcorn in a Redmond multicooker RMC-PM190, you need to remove the grains from the package and pour them into the bowl. It should be noted that at a time it is recommended to cook no more than 200 grams. The valve remains open, the cover should be closed and blocked. Then you need to turn on the appropriate mode and hold the "Start" button. After the signal is played, the cooking process will begin. The time is not displayed. Only open the lid after the end signal. It is important to note that by this time popcorn should no longer burst.
