What is an electronic alcohol meter used for?

The manufacture of alcoholic beverages, the main characteristic of which is their strength, began from time immemorial. In essence, alcohol is a semi-finished product, however its properties are directly reflected in the final result - be it brandy, liquor or homemade vodka. The number of degrees or, in other words, the strength can be measured using a special device called an electronic alcohol meter.

Device device

An electronic alcoholometer is a type of hydrometer designed to measure the density of a liquid. To a greater extent, it is used to determine the performance of alcohol-water solutions, and the accuracy of the data obtained will depend on the amount of impurities. It should be noted that alcoholometers are not used to study the strength of wines.

It should be noted that this device is very convenient to use and as accurate as possible in its readings, therefore it is widely used by both professionals and amateurs of the production of alcoholic beverages.

Laboratory alcoholometer

Such hydrometers are designed to measure density in two-component solutions that are manufactured for export and household needs. The device is often used in home brewing and winemaking, since it determines the strength of the finished product with maximum accuracy. A laboratory alcohol meter can be of several types, each of which has its own range on the measuring scale, which allows the study of liquids of various strengths.

It should be noted that such devices are made from environmentally friendly materials and do not contain mercury. The product is quite compact and lightweight, so it does not take up much space during storage. A laboratory alcohol meter is simple enough to use: to determine the density of a solution, the device is immersed in a liquid so that it can float freely without touching the container. In addition, this measuring device is certified and has been in great demand among the population for many years.

An electronic alcoholometer is a necessary and useful thing in the household, as well as a great gift for any winemaker.
