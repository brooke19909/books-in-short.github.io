Nokia Lumia 720 Smartphone: Specifications

Screen



Next, we will discuss what the Nokia Lumia 720 has in terms of display performance. The screen of this phone has a diagonal of 4.3 inches and is created using IPS technology. He really got very large viewing angles. Also during use, high contrast and brightness can be noted, so the Lumia 720 can be used even on an incredibly sunny day. This is a merit of the ClearBlack system. The resolution of the matrix itself is 800 by 480 pixels. In fact, this is a typical option for this diagonal, however, due to contrasting patterns, a lack of resolution is sometimes striking in the eye. In the work of games and applications, we also did not notice any significant delays. The only case where the latency has been extended to a few seconds is the process of restoring or running applications. It is difficult to say exactly what this may be connected with - a small amount of RAM, the OS device itself, or a low flash memory speed.

What is in the box

As for the Nokia Lumia 720, the characteristics of the device are as important as its equipment. The phone comes in a cardboard box, which is familiar to us from other latest devices. The main color of the device is recognizable by all blue. Almost no technical specifications and descriptions are indicated, there are only logos of the Windows Phone OS and Qualcomm platform, as well as a list of the delivery set. The color of the smartphone can be recognized by the sticker, the information on which includes the serial number and model name. On the first level of packaging are the phone itself and a key for inserting a SIM card, on the second tier there is a very compact charger, a headset with a microphone, a Micro-USB cable, as well as a brief user guide and certification information. It is unfortunate that the developers provided a large selection of bright colors of the case itself, but did not design other elements in this way.
