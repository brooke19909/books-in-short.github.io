Marmaris Resort Deluxe Hotel 5 *: description, photos and reviews of tourists

If you plan a calm and measured vacation on the beach and are looking for a comfortable, but inexpensive hotel, then as an option for accommodation we suggest considering Marmaris Resort Deluxe Hotel 5 * (Marmaris, Turkey).



Description

This hotel is located 20 kilometers from the resort town of Marmaris, in the center of Hisaronu Bay. The nearest airport is Dalaman, 100 kilometers away. A number of travelers also arrive at Bodrum Airport. The distance from it to the hotel is 150 kilometers.

The housing stock includes 128 rooms of the categories "standard" and "deluxe". All apartments have everything you need for a comfortable stay: comfortable furniture, plasma TV with Russian channels, safe, air conditioning, telephone, minibar, bathroom.



Marmaris Resort Deluxe Hotel 5 *: reviews of Russian travelers

Since modern tourists are very responsible in choosing a hotel both at home and abroad, they not only study the official description of establishments and consult with the managers of agencies offering tours, but also try to get acquainted with as many comments of people who have already happened in recently visited a particular place. This allows you to make a more complete and close to reality picture of the hotel. We decided to make it easier for you and shift to get acquainted with the generalized reviews of our compatriots regarding their stay in the five-star Marmaris Resort Deluxe. Just note that most tourists were not disappointed in their choice. But we will examine in more detail the details.

The territory and the hotel itself

In general, our compatriots were satisfied with the 5 * rooms offered to them in the Marmaris Resort Deluxe Hotel. However, many recommend that you take apartments in a bungalow whenever possible. The fact is that each of the three-story bungalows has its own small pool and sun loungers, which is very convenient. In addition, many do not advise settling in cottages near the amphitheater, since loud music plays here until late. In general, regardless of the category, all rooms, according to tourists, are quite spacious, with fresh repairs, good furniture, modern appliances. As a rule, no problems occurred, but if something didn‚Äôt work, then the hotel employees very quickly solved the problem after the guest contacted the reception. Guests were satisfied with the quality of cleaning. According to them, the maids not only cleaned the rooms every day, but also updated towels and bed linen.

As for the territory of Marmaris Resort Deluxe Hotel 5 *, then, according to tourists, it is quite large, beautiful and well-groomed. There are many places where you can take a pleasant walk and take wonderful photos.



Nutrition

In this matter, the opinions of tourists are somewhat divided. So, a number of guests claim that the food for a five-star hotel is quite modest. In addition, some travelers who came to rest with children complained that porridge and soups are rarely on the menu, so their children did not always eat the usual food. However, most guests were completely satisfied with the work of the restaurants. This is especially true for those who stayed in deluxe rooms. After all, they have their own restaurant "Lotus". According to them, the dishes there are very tasty, but their choice is not as large as in the main restaurant. However, there are no queues, which is an indisputable advantage.

Beach vacation

Our compatriots mostly speak positively about their own beach Marmaris Resort Deluxe Hotel, the reviews we are reviewing. So, according to them, although it is narrow, it is quite long. There are enough places for everyone. On the beach there are both sandy and pebble areas. At the bottom in some places there are stones, so you need to be careful. The beach is well cleaned, so you will not find garbage here.



Entertainment at the hotel

During the day, guests of the Marmaris Resort Deluxe Hotel 5 * can relax by one of several pools. It has sun terraces and bars. Lezhakov, according to our compatriots, is enough for everyone. In addition, in one of the pools equipped with water slides, which can ride both children and adults. From morning to late evening, the guests are entertained by a team of animators. In the afternoon, they offer to do water aerobics, yoga, play volleyball, darts, shoot with an air rifle, and in the evening shows and performances are held in the amphitheater. After that, the disco begins.

Tourists, who arrived on vacation with their entire families, were satisfied with the fact that during the day their children could attend the mini-club. In the evenings, a mini-disco was held for young guests of the hotel.
