The Volga city of Cheboksary is the center of the Chuvash Republic, its capital. From Moscow it is separated by 660 kilometers, from Nizhny Novgorod - 250 kilometers. In a beautiful place are located Cheboksary. Which region of the Russian Federation is closest to it? It is Nizhny Novgorod.

History of the city



Archaeological excavations suggest that in the 13th century there was a settlement of people on the site of the present Cheboksary. The first annalistic mention of the Chuvash-Bulgarian settlement dates back to 1469. And since 1555, in its place there was already a supporting fortress of Muscovite Russia. Its creation is attributed to Ivan the Terrible. This fortress was a reliable defense on the southern borders of Russia.

Only two centuries later, Cheboksary turn into a city. This happened thanks to the development of trade, which was facilitated by the Volga River. Bread, salt, furs, honey, leather raw materials - all these were sold by brisk Russian merchants. At first, the city was assigned to the Kazan province. However, already in 1781 it became an independent county town.

City climate

The temperate continental climate is typical of Cheboksary. Which region is characterized by the same climate? A significant part of the European part of Russia.



Snowy and frosty winters with an average temperature of minus 12-14 degrees can last four to five months. Snow cover can hold for a long time. The summer months are mainly characterized by a temperature of plus 19-21 degrees, although sometimes it is much hotter. In the off-season, unstable weather is observed. Mostly it rains in the summer, from June to September.

The climate is characterized by relatively high humidity, this is due to the proximity of the Volga. However, often evaporation can exceed the amount of precipitation, and then heat sets in.

The most beautiful place Cheboksary

In 1987, a hydroelectric power station was built in Cheboksary, and with it an artificial bay appeared on the site of the old district of the city. It has become a place of cultural recreation for residents. A pedestrian bridge is thrown across the bay, on which many citizens always walk.

Of the old buildings near the bay, few have survived. The Vvedensky Cathedral is of the greatest interest - it is the oldest temple in Chuvashia. There was a letter of Ivan the Terrible from 1555, according to which a cathedral should be built. And it was built in 1660. It is a white stone church. Inside, frescoes from the time of construction (17-18 century) and the iconostasis are preserved. The cathedral houses the first icon of the Chuvash people - the Virgin of Our Lady, dating from the 15th century.

On the shore of the bay, such ancient monuments as the Church of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary (1763) and the Trinity Monastery (17th century) look very beautiful. The latter is a whole architectural ensemble.



More than 25 temples and religious symbols are concentrated in the city of Cheboksary. Which region or region of Russia can be proud of the fact that in their capital there is such an abundance of religious monuments. Moreover, most of them were built in the 16-18 centuries.

In 1996, another dam was built across the Cheboksary Bay , which ran from Kazan to the Historic Embankment. They began to call her "The Road to the Temple." Soon new modern buildings appeared here, and the surroundings of the bay were improved. Multilevel stairs, bridges, fountains ...

The symbol of the city

In 2003, on a hill near the Historical Embankment of the Cheboksary Bay, a 46-meter sculpture of the Mother Patroness was erected. On the granite pedestal stands the figure of a woman in Chuvash national clothes. The woman spread her arms; she is ready to hospitably meet everyone here. And this gesture symbolizes the patronage of people and the city. At the bottom of the monument is an inscription in Russian and Chuvash languages, it calls people to peace and love.

The monument to the Mother Patroness became a symbol of the city of Cheboksary. What an area of ‚Äã‚Äãindescribable beauty is covered by the look, if you rise to the monument! After all, he stands on a hill. Bay, Volga, bathed in churches, fountains, green parks ...

Museums in Cheboksary

In addition to the museum, Chapaev has many other interesting institutions in Cheboksary. For example, the National Museum, which tells about the history, life and culture of not only the ancient Chuvash, but also modernity.

In the Museum of the history of the tractor you can see the collection of tractors. Why tractors? The Cheboksary Tractor Plant in Soviet times produced a large amount of this equipment and supplied it to a huge country. Now Cheboksary Industrial Tractors Plant OJSC has significantly reduced both its production area and output. And yet this is still a fairly large enterprise, where residents work.



An interesting beer museum with its diverse collection. It can rightfully compete with museums in the Czech Republic and Germany. Among the exhibits of interest are all kinds of beer vessels, barrels, bottles, mugs, implements of brewers and much more. After visiting the exposition, you should definitely head to the tasting room. After all, it is impossible, having visited the beer museum, not to taste this wonderful drink.
