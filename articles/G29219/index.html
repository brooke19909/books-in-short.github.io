"Glory Park" Anapa: photos and reviews of tourists

The best time of the year is a vacation. Everyone dreams of holding it in their own way. Some people like the aroma and coolness of coniferous forests, others prefer fresh ponds with fishing, but still the most popular and beloved is a rest by the sea.

Anapa on the Black Sea - one of the favorite vacation spots of Russians

For several decades, for many families from the Russian Federation, the southern city of Anapa remains a favorite resort. After all, this is one of the best places to relax with children. The gentle sea, sandy beaches and a gentle beach - this is exactly what the kids need. Thanks to the shallow water, it warms up well, kids can splash in the sea on their own for at least a day.

In addition to the warm coast, in Anapa there are many attractions - water parks, attractions, a dolphinarium, numerous excursions. No one will be bored on vacation.





The infrastructure of the city is very developed. Here is a modern promenade, a huge number of restaurants and cafes, souvenir shops, cinemas, clubs and other attractions that tourists like so much.

Hospitable Anapa annually meets vacationers from different parts of Russia, offering accommodation for every taste and pocket. Numerous fashionable hotels, guest houses, motels, pensions, camps for children, inexpensive private mini-hotels and even economical campsites await their guests.

Accommodation for vacationers

The entire room stock of the boarding house is located in three buildings with different levels of comfort.

The first building and the second (the seventh in the hotel) are improved. They are considered such, since the amenities are in the room (shower and toilet). All rooms are of the "standard" category.

There are thirteen rooms in the first building and all of them are quadruple, however, seven of them are one-room and six are two-room. One room can be delivered one extra bed in the form of a clamshell, and two-room - two.





In the second (seventh) building there are two-, three- and four-bed one-room rooms, as well as four-bed two-room.

The furniture in the rooms is simple and necessary (beds, bedside tables). Each room has a small TV, fan and mini fridge.

In the third building (at the hotel he is at number eight), the conditions are even more modest. Facilities are not in the rooms, but on the floor. The rooms are small and very modest. There are three-, four- and five-bed rooms, while you can add up to two additional beds. The room has beds and a fan. TV, refrigerator are on the floor, and they are shared.

The package includes three meals a day (breakfast, lunch, dinner). Between the main meals there is an intermediate nutrition. Juices and water are available all day, but locally produced wines - at lunchtime and for dinner.

Distance to the sea, beach

Warm clear sea - this is why people generally come to the resort of Anapa. "Glory Park" - a boarding house, which is located a seven-minute walk from the sea. Only three hundred and four hundred meters - and you are on the city beach.





The beach at the Slava Park boarding house, as in the whole of Anapa, is wide and sandy, the sea is shallow, the bottom is shallow. If desired, you can rent the necessary beach equipment for a fee.

Hotel infrastructure

The territory of the boarding house "Glory Park" (Anapa), as mentioned above, is very large and guarded. Entrance to the hotel - strictly by passes that are issued to vacationers upon arrival. There are several video cameras that are placed in order to ensure security at the hotel.

The boarding house provides for children and adults animation, various events and holidays, evening family discos are held.





In the pension there are two indoor pools, which are available to vacationers in May and in September, if the sea is cold.

The guesthouse is surrounded by greenery of the park. The coniferous smell is in the air, as lush pines and spruce trees grow everywhere.

For lovers of outdoor activities there are sports fields. For those who come to rest by car, there is a paid parking.

The location of the pension is very convenient. The city center is only one and a half kilometers. In just fifteen minutes you can reach the central promenade and the main attractions of Anapa with a calm step .

Pension "Glory Park" (Anapa), reviews of tourists

Reviews about the boarding house "Glory Park" is not enough. As they say, they talk about him - it means the hotel is popular, but they are positive or negative, this remains to be understood.

It is worth saying that if the boarding house declares that it works on an all-inclusive system, then they compare it with popular resorts with the same system. Hence the negative reviews about the Glory Park. People who go on vacation even at a relatively low price, but using this system, imagine one thing, but in the end they observe a completely different picture.

There are a lot of negative reviews, and mainly they relate to accommodation in a boarding house, conditions of rest, condition of rooms, etc. Progress did not affect this boarding house. The spirit of the Soviet Union remained in it: all the same modest furniture, the absolute minimalism of the interior. Those vacationers who were ready for such an environment abstracted and pay attention to other things - nature, weather, a large green area and, in principle, are satisfied with the rest.





You need to understand: boarding house ‚ÄúSlava Park‚Äù (Anapa), reviews of which are ambiguous, is still a budget hotel, the prices for rest are small, and you should not expect a European level from it.

As for nutrition, things are more fun here. Positive reviews about the kitchen are enough. Vacationers say that the food is quite simple, but varied and tasty. Many are outraged by the behavior of compatriots in the kitchen. As they say, who did not have time, he was late, and you can not catch the most delicious dishes, as some tourists are too zealous. But here the mentality of people is to blame, and not a boarding house, there's nothing to be done. In general, with regard to nutrition, we can conclude: in general terms, it is not bad, even more - good. Not Egypt or Turkey, but tasty and satisfying enough.

What is there about a place like the Slava Park Hotel (Anapa), staff reviews? Mostly they are positive. Vacationers note the hospitality and friendliness of the staff, but at the same time, lack of qualifications and relaxation. It can be concluded that people here are working well, but the authorities do not put forward special requirements for their work. Hence some negligence.

Almost all positive reviews are about the boarding house. There are no complaints about cleanliness, everything is neat and tidy, although quite simple.

Pros and Cons of the Hotel

Based on the reviews of tourists, you can highlight the main pros and cons of relaxation at the Glory Park Hotel.

Pros:

  • large territory;
  • convenient location;
  • good kitchen;
  • low prices;
  • proximity to the sea.

Minuses:

  • no repair in the rooms;
  • old furniture;
  • unsanitary conditions (insects, fungus, dampness);
  • neglect of the territory.

It is hoped that in the near future the administration of the boarding house will nevertheless reconstruct, and the boarding house will comply with world resort standards.
