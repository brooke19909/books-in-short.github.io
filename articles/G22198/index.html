Paper plastic for beginners

Paper plastic is widely used as one of the types of decorative art. Using this technique, you can create original and exclusive postcards for the holidays, and figures, and entire finished paintings. To create such serious works of art, it is necessary to accumulate many years of experience. But the simplest products for which standard blanks are used - patterns, are quite accessible to beginners.



Crafts in the technique of paper plastic

Paper plastic is one of the modern forms of art. It is a synthesis of various areas, such as modeling, applique, drawing, collage, paper design. She is a voluminous art form. The source material, which is flat in nature, is given a more complex, three-dimensional shape. With application, paper plastic is related by the fact that parts of the picture are cut out of paper, and then they are given a three-dimensional shape. Final assembly of the product is done using double-sided tape. With molding, this type of art is related by the need to manually change the shape of the parts of the product, and with the drawing - the ability to finish and color some elements of the product. At the initial stage, ready-made patterns are used to cut out the component parts, and as experience is gained, it will be possible to create your own, interesting and unique. Usual stationery scissors are used for this, but it is easier to use a special paper knife. And in order to give the components the necessary three-dimensional shape, use special tools. Often, to obtain a more complex product, a combination of techniques such as paper plastics and paper torsion is used. Novice masters can make, for example, a holiday card from multi-colored elements with the image of flowers, birds, butterflies and so on.



How to make a simple postcard

First of all, you need to choose a base card. You can purchase a blank or cut it from a sheet of cardboard of the desired color. Then, details of the future pattern are cut out of double-sided colored paper.

Decorative paper plastic in the design of the premises

For interior design, material is often used, which is called "paper-laminated plastic." It is created by sintering at temperature and high pressure of numerous layers of paper impregnated with a special solution. The result is a material that is different in color and pattern, durable, hygienic. Such decorative paper-laminated plastic allows you to create a variety of three-dimensional three-dimensional shapes. It is also widely used for exterior decoration of buildings.

Paper plastic, both as an art form and as a building material, allows the use of spatial volume to create a stylish and interesting result.
