Mauritius island. Travel Reviews

Mauritius Island ... Reviews of tourists convince that it is impossible not to visit there at least once in a lifetime. Why so? Today, trips to hot countries, the natural conditions of which allow you to get vivid unforgettable impressions, have become more affordable. And this land plot can safely be called one of such amazing places, and today we will talk about it in more detail.

Section 1. Mauritius Island. Reviews of nature and location



To imagine the conditions that await a tourist who is on this island, you first need to find out its geographical location. So, the features of the climate of Mauritius become largely understandable, given that it is located in the Indian Ocean, in the tropical zone. The following periods are considered most preferable for visiting the island: from September to November and from April to June. Coral reefs reliably protect the coastal zone, so swimming in warm ocean waters is safe. A beautiful plateau in the center of the island, evergreen vegetation, a powerful ocean - the natural conditions of Mauritius are really amazing!

Section 2. The island of Mauritius. Guest reviews



The weather on the island is usually sunny, so tourists are advised to bring sunglasses and cosmetics to protect themselves from excessive exposure to the celestial bodies. Durable shoes will not allow coral debris to damage the feet. It rains heavily, although not for long, so a light raincoat will be very helpful.

Mauritius island. Relaxation

Reviews of tourists say that vaccinations before the trip do not need to be done. Products sold in the markets can be safely eaten. However, dishes prepared by local chefs are distinguished by an abundance of spices, therefore, for safety reasons, when taking unfamiliar treats, it is better to have medicines with you that can help with stomach upsets.

Of course, everyone wants to buy some souvenir as a souvenir, so that later, at home, they remember the trip. Such a desire is understandable, but it should be noted that breaking off, collecting corals and even buying them is strictly prohibited. Of great interest to tourists are embroidered tablecloths, quilts, macram√©, carved wood products and earthenware. A model of an ancient Moorish sailing ship - such a souvenir is considered exclusively Mauritian.

Regular buses can deliver to any part of the island, tickets for them are inexpensive. Using a taxi involves a mandatory bargain about the cost of the trip, so you should be prepared for this. Boat trips on boats will allow you to get a vivid experience and relax. You can rent a car on the island.

Foreign currency is simply exchanged at any bank. Hotels accept international credit cards. It is very convenient that ATMs are available, they are installed everywhere on the island.

Section 3. The island of Mauritius. Reviews on what to visit first



The conditions for diving in Mauritius are wonderful. Night diving is popular when you can enjoy the lobsters. The behavior of tropical fish is beautifully observed on coral reefs. The east coast attracts the attention of lovers of sunken ships.

The island of Mauritius, reviews report this, provides opportunities for sea work. Fishing boats have all the necessary equipment.
