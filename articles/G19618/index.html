Rest in Hosta: reviews. Hosta (private sector) - reviews. Hosta - photo

Hosta is an incredibly picturesque resort, occupying a small stretch of territory between the center of Sochi and Adler. It has a very harmonious and calm atmosphere. In this town, everyone who avoids noisy entertainment and crowded places will enjoy spending time. Hosta began accepting visitors from other countries and cities about 100 years ago, after which she turned from a modest village into a stunning resort with a well-developed infrastructure.



This place has everything you need for an interesting and comfortable stay: attractive beaches, a large selection of restaurants and hospitality facilities, a huge number of attractions. The village received the status of a city in 1899, while the first settlements appeared here much earlier. In 1912, active improvement of the resort began - this time is considered the heyday of the tourism industry.

Resort beaches

The main attractive feature of the Hosts is the beaches, the length of which is more than ten kilometers. So, the coastal zone originates in the Adler region, while stretching continuously to the center of Sochi. Local beaches are fully equipped. They have the opportunity to comfortably sunbathe or devote their leisure time to various activities, as evidenced by numerous reviews. Hosta also offers its beaches for relaxing with young children, as the sea in this place is always clean and calm.

Resort Flora

Unique climatic conditions are another important distinguishing feature of this resort. They contributed to the development of a unique flora. Walking in squares and parks will give a huge amount of impressions, because here you can see a large number of exotic flowers and plants, as evidenced by reviews. The host also offers for viewing a plantation of cork oak, which has taken root well in the city.



The main part of the excursion objects are natural attractions. At the same time, fans of historical monuments will find something to diversify their vacation in Khost. The reviews of 2014 from the regular guests of the city are positive: each of the attractions is in order and available for viewing. The Khostinsky district is famous for its sanatoriums and health resorts, therefore, its guests will be able to combine health care with a pleasant stay without any problems. The charming and calm resort will not leave indifferent lovers of beautiful nature and beaches; couples in love will also like it.

Yew-boxwood grove

The main attraction of the Hosts is the yew-boxwood grove. This is a nature reserve, a walk along which will be very informative and interesting. It is part of the Caucasus Nature Reserve. Here you can find species of flora that on our planet grew thirty million years ago. Among the unique plants there is berry yew, evergreen boxwood. In his honor, the reserve was named. On the territory of the grove there is an opportunity to see about two hundred species of plants, there is also one historical object.

Khostinsky fortress

You can go into it, heading to the northern part of this grove. This fortification is one of the most amazing sites in the area. The time of building the fortress, as well as its purpose, is still unknown. At the moment, travelers are offered to see the dilapidated towers, as well as the surviving parts of the wall.

The entertainment industry is well developed in the village, a wide beach area is not the only vacation spot.
