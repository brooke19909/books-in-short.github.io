Tilda Lamb. Pattern and tailoring

Tilda-style toys are not just children's toys, they are often used as decorative elements, not only in the house, but also in cafes, pastry shops or shops. In the year of the sheep, you can give your friends a toy made by yourself. It is great to receive such a thing as a gift.





What to take care of before starting work

Sewing any thing has its own algorithm, sometimes it can be violated, but the correct sequence allows you to perform work in a way that is convenient. Also, using special technology, you can sew a wonderful tilde of lamb. The pattern for the toy is available on the Internet, it can be copied from the monitor without problems and increased or reduced in size at your discretion. You can do this manually or use the resize image function on the printer.

For the tilde lamb, there are several options for patterns, all of them are cute and wonderful. Today we will hold a master class on one of the options for this toy.





When you have decided on the pattern, you can choose the fabric and elements for the decor of the lamb.

The right fabric is half the success of the job.

The fabric for the lamb needs to be selected taking into account the fact that in reality the animal has curls. It would be nice to imitate such a texture with the help of a well-chosen fabric. The fleece performs this function well, it has a slight waviness and soft to the touch, but it will not give the effect of curls. It is worth saying that with a large selection of fabrics there is the opportunity to choose a more successful option, then you will get a very natural tilda lamb.

Beige cotton fabric is used for the muzzle and hoof. For work, a dense cotton is selected so that the stuffing is not visible. The lamb tilde pattern is already there, so you can measure how much fabric will go into the craft.

To make a face you will need eyes. They are sold in different sizes, and therefore you can choose the lamb for your tilde. The pattern presented in this article gives several options for the design of the muzzle. Eyes can be purchased, you can use beads or draw eyes with acrylics. To make the cheeks rosy, you can use ordinary blush or lipstick. The mouth and nose can be embroidered. When all the materials are prepared, you can begin to work.

Work algorithm

The tilde lamb pattern needs to be transferred to the fabric, leaving 0.5 cm allowances. We get only two details. The body and head are one-piece. We cut out the patterns of 2 parts of the body and 4 parts of the ears from the same fabric, although the ears can be made from the same material as the hooves. On all sides we lay in allowances of 0.5 cm. Then we cut out the muzzle and hooves. First of all, you need to sew the half of the muzzle to the half of the body. So two things need to be done. Now the two finished parts must be folded with the front sides to each other and stitched with one seam. You can make a lamb tilde pattern in full size. Such a large toy can become a decor in any interior.





It is necessary to lay a line on the details from the tummy to the back, while the legs do not need to be built up. While the legs are not sewn up, you can sew a heart on the tummy, in this position it is convenient to crawl under the fabric with the claw of the machine. Otherwise, you can also sew a heart on the finished product, but you will have to do it manually. Now the lamb needs to be turned towards itself, the seam will be in the middle of the toy. We perform an internal stitch between the legs, the part can be turned out. This is a very convenient tilde lamb pattern. The master class is not complicated, a toy can be made in one day.

Work with small parts

We still have 4 parts - pens and 8 parts - hooves. They also need to be sewn by folding the parts with the front sides and stitching on a typewriter. All small elements must be filled with padding polyester or other selected filler. Details of the ears also need to be stitched together and turned out, they do not need to be filled.

Now we fill the lamb head with sintepon and sew the eyes and mouth. When the toy is completely filled, it will be inconvenient to flash elements on the face. If everything is drawn, then you can fill the toy completely.

The lamb ears are formed and fixed on the sides of the head with a hidden seam. Hooves are sewn to the handles with the same line, the handle fabric in this place needs to be tucked up a little. The hooves on the legs are attached in the same way. The fabric on the legs must be picked up and the hooves sewed with a blind stitch. The finished tilde lamb pattern made it possible to stitch the product very quickly. Handles are sewn along the sides of the body with a blind stitch.





If some details of the face were not embroidered, then they can be finished, browned the lamb and tie a decorative ribbon on the neck. That's all, the master class is over, and you should have a burning desire to sew such a miracle with your own hands.
