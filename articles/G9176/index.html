Recreation centers in the Penza region: description, reviews

Many picturesque places to relax can be found in the Russian outback. Clean forest air, cool river water, mineral springs - each recreation center in the Penza region will give you the opportunity to stay in solitude among the pristine nature, away from city noise.

The recreation center "Maple Grove"

On the fenced and round-the-clock guarded territory of the camp site guests will find many services and entertainment. There is a Russian bath and a sauna for you, there is an indoor pool. You can rent sports equipment. In summer, you can ride a jet ski on a picturesque pond, and snowmobiling is popular here in winter. A playroom is equipped for children, there is a water slide. Meals are organized in your own restaurant, where Russian-European cuisine is presented. If you are interested in a recreation center in the Penza region with all amenities, Maple Grove offers you comfortable accommodation.

  • In double and quadruple cottages, if desired, you can place 1-2 extra beds.
  • The hotel has single, double and triple ordinary rooms, as well as deluxe rooms.

All accommodations are equipped with a TV, fridge, bathroom with shower. The cost of living in a room starts from 2900 rubles per day. Vacationers who have purchased a ticket are provided with three meals a day. The rest is offered a menu. The recreation center "Maple Grove" is located in the Penza region in the Spassky district.

Holiday Village "White Mountain"

At the camp site expanse for fishermen. In the pond you can catch carp, crucian carp, grass carp. In summer, boats and catamarans are at your service, and in winter - snowmobiles, ATVs, skis and sledges. The car can be left in a guarded parking lot. The recreation center "White Mountain" in the Penza region offers to hold a corporate party or a family celebration in the cafe "Brigantine". In the same cafe, guests are provided with three meals a day. Children can participate in festivals where various contests and gifts await them. For accommodation tourists are offered the following options:

  • triple yurts;
  • four-seater motorhomes;
  • VIP houses;
  • summer houses.

The Belaya Gora camp site is located at the village of Zalesnoye, Kamensky District, Penza Region.

"Valyaevskie Lakes"

Wellness complex "Valyaevskie ozera" offers its guests a Russian bath with a large selection of different brooms. From the veranda you can go straight to the pond. By agreement, an additional table and chairs will be installed on the street. Tents and gazebos on the lake have individual beaches and barbecue facilities. The cozy cafe offers banqueting services. It also organizes food holidaymakers. The main entertainment at the recreation center is fishing on the lake. Horse riding is also available. You can rent a boat. To accommodate guests, there are two options:

  • one-story houses on the water, mounted on stilts;
  • one-story houses on land.

The cost of living starts from 2000 rubles per day. The recreation center "Valyaevskie ozera" is located in the village of Malaya Valyaevka, Penza region.

The recreation center "Sosnovy Bor"

Choosing recreation centers in Penza and the Penza region? Pay attention to the camp site "Pine Forest". There are playgrounds for children. The cafe provides comprehensive meals. Sports enthusiasts can work out on the sports ground or in the gym. In summer arbors you will be helped to carry out festive events. In summer, you can go boating on the pond, and in winter you can use skis, sledges and snowmobiles. Fans of quiet hunting gather mushrooms and berries in the forest. It offers accommodation in wooden houses surrounded by pine trees. There are summer and winter houses. The cost of living starts at 600 rubles per day for the summer option. The Sosnovy Bor camp site is located in the village of Pervoe Tarlakovo, Penza Region.

Recreation centers of Penza and the Penza region. Tourists reviews

Tourists who have chosen picturesque Penza camp sites to relax share their impressions in their reviews.

  • At the recreation center "Maple Grove" guests praised the winter holiday. The magnificent nature, crystal clear air, especially pleased riding from the hill on cheesecakes. I liked the Russian bath with brooms. Around the picturesque nature, beautiful ponds, a good sauna with a pool. Only here, many did not like the food in the restaurant, tasteless and expensive. Tourists who came here to ride horses already found out on the spot that there were no horses.
  • "White Mountain", a recreation center in the Penza region, tourists like the fact that here you can rent a house or set up your own tent. Each house has its own barbecue, which is also very convenient. Around the beautiful nature, well-equipped beach. The guests did not like the conditions of animals in the camp zoo. And yet, entry is paid only for those who do not rent a house, but actually take money from everyone.
  • On the recreation center "Valyaevskie ozera" tourists like beautiful nature, clear water in the lake, good beaches. Especially satisfied are those who rented a bathhouse, everything is thought out, there is access directly to the water. Also liked the cafe. But those who rented a house on the water, they write that they have not been repaired for a long time, they give out bad linen, it is damp in the house, they did not stay for the night. Given the fact that the house is very crowded, the guests considered the fee for accommodation to be too high.
  • At the recreation center Sosnovy Bor, guests appreciated clean pine air, a good beach, cozy houses, peace and quiet on weekdays. Among the shortcomings are the poor roads at the entrance to the camp site, as well as the fact that on weekends it is very noisy.
