Hotel Aqua 4 * (Bulgaria, Varna): reviews and photos

Bulgaria is a popular country among Russian tourists. They appreciate not only the high level of local service, but also the low prices for accommodation, food and entertainment. On the coast of Varna, there are many first-class and budget complexes. Especially popular among travelers is the Hotel Aqua 4 * (Bulgaria, Varna), a description of which can be read in this article.

general information

The modern hotel was opened in 2002. It was built in the very center of Varna. The train station is 100 meters away. Very close is the pedestrian zone, created specifically for tourists. For them, there are restaurants, cafes, bars, shops and souvenir shops. Along the road, you can meet magicians, artists, as well as people offering to book an excursion from them. Varna Airport is 8 km from the city. By taxi to the hotel Aqua 4 * (Bulgaria, Varna) can be reached in just half an hour. True, the city beach is quite far away. The coast is more than 1 km away, so you can walk to it in about 15 minutes.





The complex operates throughout the year. It consists of one building, which has 7 floors. For its guests, the hotel has prepared 78 comfortable rooms. It is worth knowing that he does not have his own territory. It is more suitable for businessmen or tourists who prefer to travel around the country on their own. Check-in starts no earlier than noon here. Registration ends at 18:00. No pets are allowed. You must also leave the hotel no later than 12:00. The complex is suitable for tourists who do not know English. Part of the staff here speaks good Russian. Do not confuse the hotel with the Aqua Azur 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Varna), which has a very similar name.

Number of rooms

The future guests of the hotel are waiting for spacious and comfortable apartments. Most of them are designed for two people and a child. There are non-smoking rooms for people and tourists who are forced to move in a wheelchair. Upon check-in, guests can choose between single or double beds. It is worth knowing that the apartments do not have balconies. The hotel windows overlook the nearby urban buildings. The floor is covered with soft carpet. Employees daily clean all the living quarters. Towels and linen are changed 3 times a week. Room service and bed and breakfast are also available at surcharge.





In hot weather, the Aqua 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) has central air conditioning. It is regulated by staff. In winter, the building includes heating. In addition to furniture, the room has a TV. At the same time, some of the rooms are equipped with modern flat models. Cable TV with the ability to view channels in Russian is connected to it. Hotel guests can also use a direct dial telephone and free internet. For a small amount, a minibar and drinks are delivered to the room. All apartments have a private bathroom. Some of them are equipped with not only a toilet, shower and washbasin, but also a bidet. Tourists are also offered a set of cosmetics and towels.

Hotel infrastructure

Local infrastructure is primarily designed for business people. For them, the hotel has prepared 3 spacious conference rooms . The largest of them is designed to receive 140 delegates. All rooms are equipped with modern computer equipment necessary for conferences, seminars and business meetings. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel for free. Tourists can leave their cars in a private parking lot. However, before arrival you need to reserve a place for yourself. Paid public parking is also nearby. In the building of the hotel Aqua 4 * (Bulgaria, Varna) a small store is open.





The ground floor has a 24-hour reception. Here, tourists can not only check in, but also exchange currency, call a taxi, book a tour or transfer to the airport. Large bags can be left in the storage room. For a fee at the reception you can use the safe. Laundry facilities are also available.

Catering

Hotel Aqua 4 * (Bulgaria, Varna) has prepared 2 catering systems for its guests: half board and full board. All meals are served in the main restaurant, which specializes in dishes of Bulgarian and European cuisine. Continental breakfast is served here. It suggests light meals. For example, sandwiches, cereals, fresh pastries, fruits, cereals, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãcheese and ham. For guests offer refreshments, tea, instant coffee and hot milk. Upon request, restaurant waiters can pack food with them or deliver it to their room.





For lunch and dinner, Bulgarian cuisine is served. At the buffet there are always several types of meat, side dishes, fresh vegetables, fruits and feta cheese. Restaurant chefs try to diversify their meals, so every day they cook something new. At the bar you can buy alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as cocktails, snacks , ice cream and desserts. The menu features both local and imported alcohol.

Entertainment

Hotel Aqua 4 * (Bulgaria, Varna), the photo of which can be seen in this article, does not offer a large selection of entertainment. The fact is that here most often business people and tourists live, who spend most of their time outside the complex. The pride of the hotel is its own spa, which offers guests wellness services for face and body. Tourists can visit the massage room, solarium, jacuzzi, sauna, steam room and beauty salon. All services are provided at an additional cost.





The building also houses a small indoor pool, the water in which is constantly heated. Sports enthusiasts can head to the fitness center , equipped with new, modern fitness equipment. Tourists can also relax in the hotel lobby, which has soft sofas and several TVs.

Vacation with children

When checking into the Aqua 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Varna), guests with children can expect a good discount. So, for the stay of a child under 6 years old, who will sleep in an extra bed, you do not need to pay at all. Children from 7 to 12 years old will receive a 50% discount on vacations. Teenagers are settled in the complex, paying all the declared cost. A baby cradle is available upon request. There is no entertainment for kids at the hotel, so parents will have to organize their leisure activities on their own.

Positive opinions

Before buying a ticket, you should definitely read the reviews of previous hotel guests. They will help assess the current state of affairs, talk about the advantages and disadvantages. Opinions of guests about the hotel Aqua 4 * (Bulgaria, Varna) are mostly positive. Tourists believe that the cost of living is consistent with the quality of the services offered. They highlight the following hotel benefits:

  • The plumbing in the room works well. Water leaves quickly, but taps and showers do not leak.
  • Comfortable beds with soft mattresses. High-quality bedding is provided , which the maids always change to clean in a timely manner.
  • Friendly administrators who are always trying to help guests. They speak good Russian.
  • Great location. Nearby is the main street of the city. Nearby there is a park where you can take a walk in the evening.
  • Wi-Fi works well in any part of the hotel. The speed is small, but it is enough to send mail and view social networks.
  • Delicious food in the restaurant. The buffet is constantly replenished, so it is difficult to stay hungry here.
  • The windows offer a beautiful panorama of the old part of the city.




Negative opinions

It is unlikely that you can find a hotel that has no shortcomings. According to tourists, the Aqua 4 * complex (Bulgaria), which there are also negative reviews, has a number of significant shortcomings. Comments of the guests describe the following serious disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Poor soundproofing in the rooms. Not only the noise from the street is heard, but also the conversations and music from the neighboring apartments.
  • Monotonous breakfasts. Sandwiches and cereals quickly get bored.
  • Employees do not always replenish toiletries in a timely manner. We have to constantly remind them of this.
  • The air conditioner is very buzzing, so it is unlikely to sleep in the daytime.
  • In winter, the rooms are very hot.
  • No double beds. Instead, they stand two shifted single, between which there is a large gap. Sleeping together is very uncomfortable because of this.
  • Bad housekeeping. On the carpet you can find old undetected stains, and dust is found on the cabinets.
  • For dinner, sometimes not fresh food is served, but the food left over from breakfast.




Instead of an afterword

Arriving at this hotel, it is worth knowing that it is hardly suitable for a classic beach holiday. It is remote from the sea coast, does not have its own territory and a rich entertainment program. Often, travelers confuse it with the Aqua Azur 4 * complex (Bulgaria). Reviews say that the hotel is aimed at businessmen and tourists stopping by. Youth companies that value the hotel for its proximity to the center of Varna are often vacationing here. Families with children, by contrast, try to settle in other hotels. They do not like the proximity to the station and the port, as well as the rather noisy environment.
