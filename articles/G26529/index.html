Chemical equation of the reaction - conditional notation of a chemical reaction

To simplify the recording of chemical processes and their better perception reaction equation is used. It is a conditional record of the interaction of substances among themselves and, as a result, the formation of new products. With such a schematic "image", in order to comply with the law of conservation of mass of a substance, numerical coefficients are used. Such a description of chemical reactions using numbers and symbols was proposed in 1615 by Jean Begun. Also a separate type is the ionic reaction equation, which, when compiled, has features, because the substances recorded in it are in the form of ions. Also, redox reactions, characterized by a change in the oxidation states of the atoms, will have features in the preparation of a schematic notation.
