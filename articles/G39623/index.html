Ministry of Emergencies Voronezh Institute: history, basic information

Among applicants, the direction of preparation for service in the Ministry of Emergencies has become very popular. The Voronezh Institute, which prepares people for this specialty, is known not only in its city, but also far beyond its borders. Many applicants come here from all over Russia and even neighboring countries, because it provides a real fundamental education. The Institute educates the best citizens of its country. The article will discuss the history of the institute, information for applicants and students. And also student life in the institution will be clearly shown.

History

The Voronezh Fire Institute of the Ministry of Emergencies opened its doors in 1967. But then this institution was called the training detachment of fire fighting activities. The following year, 2 groups of students were recruited. In one of them, fire inspectors and trainers were trained, and in the other, commanders.
A few years later, not far from the unit, two new buildings were built, the groups were transferred to them. Now the institution has received the name "School for the training of junior and middle managers." After 10 years, the School was renamed the Training Center. In 1993, a school was created on the basis of the center, and in 2008 the institution acquired the status of a university of the Ministry of Emergencies. Voronezh Institute immediately gained popularity.

Information for applicants

In order to be trained in this institution, you need to successfully pass the exam in 3 subjects: specialized mathematics, physics and the Russian language. Also, when submitting documents, an entrance exam in mathematics is additionally written and physical culture tests are conducted.

You must also first contact the local department of the Ministry of Emergencies in order to file a personal file that the department will have to send to the university of the Ministry of Emergencies (Voronezh Institute in this case) before a certain date.

The hostel is provided only for students studying on a budgetary basis, for other categories it is not provided. An exception is correspondence students studying on a budgetary basis at the time of the session.





Institute life

  • The institute has a permanent KVN team participating and winning in various competitions and leagues. The most fun and resourceful will be able to join the ranks of the old-timers.
  • Here, special attention is paid to sports, the institute has its own teams, which occupy places in federal competitions. Students who take part in sections and lead an active college life enjoy authority among teachers.
  • The Institute constantly hosts a variety of cultural events.
  • Active work is carried out by the Trade Union.

Thus, the Voronezh Institute of the State Fire Service of the Ministry of Emergencies of Russia, the cost of which does not exceed 10,000 rubles per year, is not only an educational, but also a leisure center for students.
