Sunstar Beach Resort Hotel 5 *: reviews, description, photo

If you are looking for a comfortable, but inexpensive hotel with its own beach in Turkey, then pay attention to the Sunstar Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Alanya).



general information

This hotel is located in the resort of Alanya, on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. Antalya Airport is 130 kilometers away.

Sunstar Beach Resort offers tourists to stay in one of the 159 rooms of the standard and suite categories. They are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.

Meals at the hotel are organized on an all-inclusive basis. The hotel has a restaurant (buffet) and several bars.

Own beach "Sunstar Beach" is a hundred meters away and is equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas.

Housing stock, territory and location

Most of the tourists offered the rooms at this hotel were satisfied. According to them, the apartments here, although not large, are very comfortable, pleasantly decorated, with new furniture and modern appliances. You will not find any creaking beds and cabinets with falling doors in the rooms of Sunstar Beach Resort. If, nevertheless, something fails, you must immediately report the problem to the reception. After that, the fault will be fixed very quickly. Our compatriots also liked the quality of cleaning. So, the maids here, they say, are very responsible. They carry out cleaning every day, and towels and bed linen are renewed every other day. In addition, often, returning to the room, guests found on the beds funny figures in the form of swans, hearts, etc., built by maids.

Nutrition

As tourists note in their comments, dining at Sunstar Beach can be called standard for a four-star hotel in Turkey. So, the choice of dishes here can not be called very diverse, but there is always something to choose from. In addition, the quality of food is very high, the hotel chefs are well aware of their business. So, visitors to the restaurant for lunch and dinner can always taste various dishes of chicken, turkey, meat, fish, snacks, salads, soups, delicious pastries and desserts. Guests can enjoy free local and alcoholic drinks on site.



Sea

Own beach Sunstar Beach Resort Hotel 5 * (Kestel) travelers found quite large and well equipped. So, here at any time of the day you can find free sunbeds and umbrellas. There is also a bar on the beach. However, only non-alcoholic drinks are free for guests here. Beer and wine can be borrowed at an additional cost. The beach itself is pebble. The sea near the coast is very clear, the water is clear. However, some tourists complained about the fact that when entering the sea at the bottom there are sharp stones. Therefore, it is advisable to buy special shoes.

Entertainment

Judging by the reviews, the guests enjoyed spending time by the pool on the hotel grounds. It has a sun terrace with sun loungers and a bar. In addition, the pool has water slides. Animators entertain the whole day with travelers, offering to participate in water aerobics, gymnastics, play water polo, volleyball or darts. In the evening, a show and a mini-disco for children are held.
