If you also decided to spend your vacation in Taba and are looking for a comfortable beach hotel, then pay attention to Sol Taba Red Sea 5 *. Sol Taba Red Sea Resort 5 * (Taba): reviews of tourists from the Russian Federation

Since today, many travelers when choosing a hotel are guided not so much by its description and cost as by the impressions of people who have already stayed in it, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the opinion of our compatriots who spent their holidays this year at Sol Taba Red Sea Resort " in Egypt. Looking ahead a bit, we note that most tourists were quite satisfied with their choice of hotel. They were completely satisfied with the quality of accommodation and the level of services provided, as well as the price of accommodation. But we will understand everything in detail.





Impressions of travelers about the hotel and its housing stock

First of all, many guests pay attention to the rooms they offer. So, the apartments in this hotel were mainly liked by our compatriots. According to them, they are large, clean, pleasantly decorated. Each room also has a spacious balcony, a hairdryer, a convenient clothes dryer and a very spacious wardrobe. The furniture is not new, but everything is in good condition. The apartments offer very beautiful views of the pool and the sea or the mountains. Guests of Sol Taba Red Sea (Ex. Sonesta Beach Resort Taba) also have positive feedback on the quality of cleaning in their apartments. So, professional cleaning was carried out every day. In addition, workers were not lazy to create various shapes from sheets and towels over and over again. By the way, many tourists say that here they were always offered clean linens and crisp towels.

Comments of guests about the territory and location of the hotel complex

According to our compatriots, the hotel has a large green and well-groomed territory. There is where to take a walk, there are many picturesque places for a photo shoot. But outside the hotel there is nothing to do, since there is no infrastructure here. Therefore, "Sol Taba Red Sea" is ideal for a magnificent relaxing beach holiday, combined with trips on excursions. Tourists also note the presence of good pools at the hotel. Many liked the local spa. According to the guests, here you will find quite reasonable prices for a good quality of service.





Tourist Reviews of Nutrition at Sol Taba Red Sea and Staff

In general, judging by the comments of travelers, most of them were satisfied with the food at the hotel. According to them, the food here is delicious, high-quality and quite diverse. True, in the restaurant you will not find pork dishes, but this is not a problem, since you will always be offered an alternative as other types of meat. Also, many celebrate delicious pastries and great ice cream, which can be enjoyed in the afternoon at the beach bar. In addition, guests were always pleased with clean dishes in the restaurant Sol Taba Red Sea.

As for the hotel staff, they made a pleasant impression on the tourists. So, according to the guests, the staff here is polite, friendly and attentive. In addition, almost everyone (with the exception of some cleaners) knows English, and some know Russian. So you should not have any communication problems here.

Guest opinions on entertainment and animation

At Hotel Sol Taba Red Sea you can have a great time sitting on the sun terrace by one of the pools. In addition, the hotel has a good team of animators. During the day, they entertain tourists by the pool in every possible way (fun contests, water aerobics, water polo, gymnastics, etc.), and in the evenings there are various shows (belly dance, "Lord of the Fire"), karaoke (including with Russian-language songs ) and discos. There is also a volleyball court with lighting that works even at night. Wi-Fi is free in the hotel lobby. In addition, many tourists recommend not limited to snorkeling and flippers, but try scuba diving.

As for excursions, in addition to the standard pyramid and Egypt visits to Egypt, many guests of this hotel also climbed Mount Moses (cost about 50 US dollars). If you are interested in such a pastime, then experienced travelers are strongly advised to first adequately assess their physical abilities, since you will have to climb about seven kilometers. In addition, be sure to take warm clothes with you on the road. In addition, many tourists staying at Sol Taba Red Sea Resort 5 * (Taba) often go on a sightseeing trip to Israel (the border can be reached within an hour). Such a trip will cost you between 150 and 200 US dollars. Here you will get the opportunity to see Jerusalem, as well as swim in the famous Dead Sea.

Summing up, we can confidently say that the majority of Russian tourists liked the Sol Taba Red Sea Hotel (Taba, Egypt). In their opinion, this hotel is fully consistent with its five-star category and price. Tourists recommend this hotel to anyone who wants to spend a great vacation in Egypt, because here you can combine a beach holiday with a rich excursion program.
