Hotel Sheraton Sharm Resort 5 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): photos and reviews of tourists

If you are accustomed to a comfortable accommodation and excellent service, and also plan to have a great rest at such a popular resort as Sharm El Sheikh (Egypt), Sheraton Sharm Resort 5 * can be a very suitable hotel for your holidays.





Location

This hotel is located on the coast of the beloved tourists from different countries of the Red Sea, in the resort town of Sharm El Sheikh. Naama Bay is 6 kilometers away. The nearest airport is only 7 kilometers away. So on arrival on the road to the hotel you will take very little time.

Sharm El Sheikh, Sheraton Sharm Resort 5 *: photos and description

This hotel complex was built in 1999. Since then, renovation has been carried out several times here. Today, guests are invited to stay in one of 423 comfortable rooms. They have everything that you may need for your stay - comfortable furniture, flat-screen TV (satellite TV with Russian-language channels), direct dial telephone, air conditioning, minibar, safe, hairdryer, bathroom. Most apartments have a terrace or furnished balcony. As for the Internet, Wi-Fi access is available at an additional cost. Rooms are cleaned and towels daily, and bed linen is updated every three days.

Meals at Sheraton Sharm Resort are all-inclusive. There is both a buffet restaurant and several restaurants where visitors can order a la carte dishes. Also on the territory of the hotel complex there are several bars where you can either treat yourself to a soft or alcoholic drink, or, if you wish, refresh yourself with french fries, hamburger or pizza.

As for the infrastructure, the hotel has several shops, a souvenir shop, a doctor‚Äôs office, a currency exchange office, a rental car, parking, a tour desk, four conference rooms, an ATM, dry cleaning, laundry and much more.

Cleaning

Judging by their reviews, there were no complaints about the work of the maids among the guests of this hotel. So, tourists were satisfied with the quality of cleaning. It was carried out daily. Check in, departure

As our compatriots note, many of them were very pleased with the fact that upon their early arrival at the Sheraton Sharm Resort, Villas & SPA 5 * they did not need to wait until two in the afternoon to get the room keys. Indeed, in most cases, hotel employees try to populate new guests as early as possible. If you arrive at the hotel during breakfast or lunch, then you will be asked to first go to the restaurant and eat, and then hand over the room keys. Your luggage for this time will already be delivered to the apartment.

As for the last day of your vacation, you should release your room before noon. If you plan to leave the hotel only after lunch or in the evening, you can extend your stay for an additional fee. At the same time, you will be able to enjoy all the benefits of ol inclusive until your departure: eat in restaurants and snack bars, order drinks, relax on the beach, swim in the pools and use other hotel infrastructure.

Beverages

The complex has several bars, including by the pool, on the beach and in the lobby. Here you can order alcoholic and soft drinks, drink fresh or taste delicious ice cream.

Beach

Basically, travelers just for the rest on the shores of the Red Sea go to Egypt. Hotel Sheraton Sharm Resort 5 * (Sharm-El-Sheikh), according to our compatriots who have been here, offers its guests an excellent beach holiday. So, despite its location on the second coastline, it has its own beach, which is also used by tourists living in the neighboring hotel complex of the Sheraton chain.

The beach here, according to vacationers, is very large, sandy, well-equipped, clean. There are many comfortable sun loungers and parasols. As our compatriots note, there are always enough sun loungers on the beach, so there is no need to run ashore early in the morning to take a seat, as is the case in other, even five-star, hotels. There is also an outdoor cafe on the beach where you can not only have a snack during the day, but also have lunch, there is also a bar with a wide selection of drinks.

Sea

Most of our compatriots did not forget to mention in their reviews about the Sheraton Sharm Resort 5 * hotel, a photo of which can be viewed in this article, and about the magnificent coral reef located near the coast. According to tourists, here you can see a variety of amazing, vibrant and unique marine life, because the local underwater world is very colorful. Therefore, when going on vacation, be sure to arm yourself with a snorkeling mask and fins. After all, you can swim here and watch the life of the inhabitants of the Red Sea for hours. But, by the way, the necessary equipment can be taken at the local diving center. Here, everyone can take a scuba dive and take a few lessons from a professional instructor.

As for entering the water, most vacationers preferred to use the pontoon. From it you can go down the stairs directly to the depths and find yourself next to the reef. Those who swim poorly can go into the sea directly from the shore. However, in this case, it is recommended to use special shoes so as not to injure your legs on the coral. Also, family tourists note that the beach has a small area, completely cleared of stones and corals. Here you can swim with young children.





Entertainment

As already mentioned, in the territory of Sheraton Sharm Hotel Resort 5 * there are several pools. Next to them are sunbeds with sun loungers and parasols. An animation is held all day around the central pool. So, everyone is invited to do yoga, aqua aerobics, step, gymnastics, zumba, play volleyball, darts, water polo, etc. If you want to relax in silence, you can go to another pool where no one will bother you.

In the evening, the hotel begins an entertainment program with the participation of both animators and guest artists. After that, tourists can head to the disco bar.

Fans of outdoor activities can use tennis courts (illuminated in the dark), sports fields, visit the gym, and take a few lessons at the diving center or windsurfing school.

Guests wishing to relax and take care of their appearance and health can visit the spa and beauty salon, where you will be offered a lot of health and beauty programs and services. According to our compatriots, they were quite satisfied with the quality of work of local specialists.
