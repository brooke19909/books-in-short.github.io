Housing stock

As already mentioned, Lyra Resort Hotel, a photo of which can be viewed in this article, has at its disposal 400 rooms. They are represented by the following categories:

  • Standard apartments in the main building (area up to 31 sq. M, maximum 3 + 1 guest, one room, view of the surroundings or the sea). Three rooms of this type are specially equipped for disabled people (wide doorway, adapted bathtub, etc.).
  • Family apartments in the main building (area 30-36 sq. M, sea view, two bedrooms separated by a sliding door, one of the rooms is a walk-through, it does not have windows, a double bed, two single or bunk beds and a chair in a separate sleeping place , can accommodate up to 4 + 1 people.).
  • Club standard rooms in a bungalow (area 23 sq. M, maximum occupancy 2 + 1 guest, garden view).
  • Club family rooms in a bungalow (30-36 sq. Meters, two rooms, garden view).
  • Villas (three rooms, 80 sq. Meters, for a maximum of six guests, two separate bedrooms and a living room with kitchen in the American manner).

Regardless of the category, all rooms are equipped with necessary furniture, as well as a TV with Russian-language channels, air conditioning, a refrigerator with a minibar, an electronic safe, a telephone, a hairdryer, and a bathroom. On the floor in the part of the apartment there is a laminate, and in the part - ceramic tile. Rooms are cleaned daily. Guests can also order a room service.

Infrastructure, entertainment

The hotel "Lira Resort" has all the conditions for a comfortable stay and a varied pastime of guests. So, for example, in the hotel complex, in addition to the main restaurant, there are five more a la carte restaurants. In them you can taste fish dishes, as well as dishes of Turkish, Asian, Mexican and Italian cuisine. In addition, the hotel has several bars offering a wide selection of drinks for every taste.

Fans of outdoor activities can play beach volleyball, tennis, basketball, billiards, water polo, futsal, as well as shoot archery, go diving, go canoeing. From morning until night, tourists are entertained by a team of animators. Also, vacationers can visit the gym and cinema.

Cost of living

When choosing a hotel to stay, one of the main factors, of course, is the price. Today, more and more people are trying to choose a comfortable hotel, but with the lowest possible cost of living. As for the Lira Resort hotel in Side (Turkey), which we are considering, then, according to our compatriots, the price of living here is quite consistent with the quality of accommodation and services. Despite the fact that this hotel is a five-star hotel, it is not part of a group of elite places. Therefore, the prices here, as they say, do not bite. So, for example, a seven-day stay in August for two in a club room will cost from 82 thousand rubles. For a standard room with sea view for the same period you will have to pay from 90 thousand for two and from 119 thousand for three. A club family room for 7 days will cost in the amount of 150 thousand rubles, a standard one - from 160 thousand. Please note that the indicated prices include all-inclusive meals.

Sea

Since almost all tourists traveling to Turkey in the summer are counting on an excellent beach holiday, and this moment is one of the fundamental ones when choosing a hotel to stay.
