Blue Sea Hotel Lagos de Cesar 4 *: photos and reviews of tourists

Blue Sea Hotel Lagos de Cesar is a great option for a seaside vacation. Here you will live near the famous public beach, surrounded by comfort and incredibly high-quality service.





Brief description of the resort complex

Blue Sea Hotel Lagos de Cesar is a unique place where incredibly beautiful nature is combined with modern amenities for an unforgettable vacation for travelers. On the one hand, you can enjoy beautiful views of the sea, as well as its melodic noise. On the other hand, the territory of the hotel rests on picturesque cliffs that demonstrate natural strength and grandeur. You can watch all this beauty from the windows of your apartments or from the large terrace equipped with sunbeds and sunshades.

The Blue Sea Hotel Lagos de Cesar hotel complex is just perfect for those tourists who are used to spend their holidays actively. In the open air there is a huge swimming pool, which is equipped with heating devices in case the weather goes bad. You should also pay attention to the modern sports hall. Here you can practice both independently and under the supervision of an instructor. There is a gym, as well as a separate room for fitness, fully hung with full-length mirrors. In the meantime, you will be busy with yourself, your children will be able to have fun in the game club or to remain under the supervision of a nanny or animation staff.

You can not ignore the incredibly beautiful and comfortable rooms provided by the Hotel Blue Sea Lagos de Cesar 4 * hotel. Each of them has its own balcony, and therefore you will always have the opportunity to admire the stunning nature, sitting in a cozy wicker rattan chair. It has modern amenities such as air conditioning and satellite TV, and much more that the traveler may need. In addition, guests are allowed here due to the presence of folding upholstered furniture.





Resort Location

The Blue Sea Hotel Lagos de Cesar resort has an incredibly good location in the central part of Puerta Santiago - it is the west coast of the famous island of Tenerife. Here, within walking distance, there is a huge number of entertainment venues, restaurants and retail outlets, as well as cultural and natural attractions. A trip to Tenerife International Airport will not take you more than forty minutes. Arriving on the island, be sure to call the Blue Sea Lagos de Cesar. How to get to the hotel, administrators will tell you. The easiest way is to take a taxi or book a transfer.

Hotel Blue Sea Lagos de Cesar: service rating

To make a general impression of the resort, it is important to evaluate each of its features individually. This also applies to the Hotel Blue Sea Lagos de Cesar. Traveler reviews allowed us to make the following rating on a five-point system:

  • The hotel infrastructure has earned a rating of 4, which is mainly due to the fact that insufficient attention has been paid to the improvement of the territory. Bars and restaurants also close too early, forcing travelers to travel to establishments outside the hotel.
  • The quality of service was also rated not very high (3.5 points). The best impression was made by the quality work of the staff, but the animation is rather sluggish. Also, guests are not satisfied with the quality and speed of the reception.
  • Guest apartments earned a rating of 3.7. Air conditioners work perfectly, and therefore the room is cool even on the hottest day. But the furniture and plumbing are very old and require replacement. In addition, the rooms have terrible sound insulation, which is why it is simply impossible to relax in the room.
  • Children's infrastructure is estimated at 2.6, as it is not thought out. In addition to the playground, the hotel can no longer offer kids.
  • The location of the hotel is rated 4. Very close to the beach, as well as cafes and retail outlets. But the sights and entertainment clubs are located at a considerable distance.

General impression

The island of Tenerife is a rather expensive place to stay, and therefore the Blue Sea Lagos de Cesar 4 * hotel will be a real find for budget travelers. Traveler reviews suggest that this is one of the most budget accommodation options. At the same time, there is quite a decent resort infrastructure and a good level of service. The low price for accommodation is due to the fact that the hotel has not been renovated for a long time. If you do not pay attention to old furniture and scuffed interior, then this option is ideal for you.

It is worth noting the location of the Blue Sea Lagos de Cesar hotel. Reviews of tourists say that it is incredibly beneficial for those who do not want to spend all the time in the hotel. This is the first coastline, and therefore literally a few steps from the beach. By the way, it is not clean and comfortable, and therefore, if you want to fully enjoy a sea holiday, it is better to choose more distant options. It will not be difficult, because there is a bus stop very close by, with which you can easily get to anywhere in the resort. It is also a good starting point for those who want to explore as many attractions as possible.

We can conclude that this hotel is good for those who do not like to sit still. Here you can get a comfortable overnight stay and meals, but no more. It would be better to go on an excursion or at sea for the whole day. Otherwise, you will start to be struck by such hotel flaws as the outdated interior and the almost complete absence of animation. In addition, if you want to have a romantic dinner or just have a cocktail in the bar, it is better to visit establishments outside the hotel. Everything is much cheaper in them, it is much tastier, and they also have a more convenient work schedule.
