A little bit about the resort ...

The small town of Evrenseki is located just 10 kilometers from Side. The resort is washed by the waters of the Mediterranean Sea and has a flat landscape. The region is located in the subequatorial climate zone. Warm mild weather is observed even in winter. The resort has become famous for its endless and wide beach with a very gentle sunset. That is why it is popular among couples with children. Here in hotels all conditions are created for a comfortable stay even with the youngest tourists. For children, clubs, amusement parks, playgrounds and pools work. And adults will not be bored here.

Evrenseki is significantly different from Side. There is no such congestion of tourists. The resort is less noisy and less crowded. There are no loud entertainment venues in the city that can break the silence. Therefore, lovers of solitude choose it.

In the village there is a promenade that stretches along the entire coast, where you can take a walk in the evening. The most active tourists have the opportunity to visit the attractions of the region, which include the Museum of Ancient Art in Side.
