The well-known Dorogomilovsky market

Perhaps this is one of the oldest grocery markets that operate smoothly in the capital. Older people remember that shopping malls appeared in the area around the mid-thirties of the last century. Dorogomilovsky market, located near the Kiev railway station, has traditionally been a very convenient place for trade. Since the time of the historical Dorogomilovskaya outpost located here. It is no secret to anyone that precisely the point of location is one of the determining factors for the success of any commercial enterprise. And the place here is very lively, this is a kind of intersection of many roads.

Dorogomilovsky market. Moscow. Territory of success

Currently, it is one of the most popular trading floors. Many people prefer to buy food here. Some demanding Muscovites are not even stopped by the very significant distance that they have to cover in order to get to the Dorogomilovsky market. This is despite the fact that prices here are by no means pleasing with their availability.

What, then, makes buyers come here from the other end of Moscow? The answer is obvious: the quality level of the products presented here. Almost everything that is sold here corresponds in its parameters to premium products and is located in the upper part of the price range. For better or worse, the Dorogomilovsky market is not for the poor. And in Moscow there are enough people for whom a similar price level is quite acceptable. This is evidenced by the simple fact that the Dorogomilovsky market never complained about the lack of buyers.

The marketplace of the Dorogomilovsky market is designed for a successful middle class, for people who know how to make money, count them and spend them properly. This is the reputation of this place. It even manifests itself in the fact that sometimes various commercials and elements of television shows are sometimes shot here. And politicians like Zhirinovsky like to speak here, posing against the backdrop of food stalls. And one should pay tribute to the management of the trading company, for which status is a significant concept. Therefore, funds in the development and reconstruction of the trading floor are invested in a sufficient amount. And there is reason to believe that this market has good prospects.
