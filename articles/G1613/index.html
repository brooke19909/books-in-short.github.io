Negative reviews

So that unpleasant moments do not spoil your impression of a vacation, find out in advance about the shortcomings that you may encounter at Daima Biz Resort. Reviews note the following disadvantages of this institution:

  • during the main meals in the restaurant some mournful music plays, which terribly hurts the head;
  • use of the safe and wireless Internet in the room is available only for an additional fee (1 euro per day for each service);
  • unclean around pools;
  • the rooms smelled of tobacco smoke;
  • on the beach there are a lot of cigarette butts and plastic cups that hotel staff do not clean, but simply buried in the sand;
  • not enough sunbeds around the pools (it is almost impossible to find free space);
  • Lunch boxes are not provided for those who go on an excursion or check out before breakfast;
  • at every step guests are obsessively offered paid services;
  • for the use of beach towels, a deposit of $ 20 is required.
