Dionysos Central Hotel 3 * (Cyprus / Paphos): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Cyprus is a country that has managed to win the favor of tourists from all over the world. After all, here they are waiting not only for the clear waters of the sea and sandy beaches, but also for interesting sightseeing tours and, of course, luxurious resort complexes. One of the travelers' favorite hotels is the Dionysos Central Hotel.

Naturally, during vacation planning, every tourist tries to get as much information as possible about the hotel. So what does this hotel look like? What conditions of accommodation and food can offer? Is it possible to have fun with the kids? Is the beach close to it? Answers to these questions will be useful to many travelers.

Location Description

Convenient location is always a plus. The hotel has this advantage, as it is located almost in the center of the resort area, in a popular, vibrant area called Kato Paphos. Dionysos Central Hotel is perfect for a beach holiday, because it is located just a few minutes walk from the seashore.





Near the hotel complex there are many shops, colorful taverns, bars. You will have the opportunity to have fun in the heart of Paphos. By the way, in a few minutes you can reach the seaport. The airport is about 16 km away. By the way, hotel guests are provided with a shuttle service: in 30 minutes you can reach the hotel building. The settlement policy here is loyal: if possible, tourists settle earlier, so as not to make them wait.

What does the hotel area look like?

The Dionysos Central Hotel is relatively small. Nevertheless, there is a territory where you can walk, relax, enjoy the beauty of nature. Lush greenery and bright colors of flowers are always pleasing to the eye.

By the way, in the neighborhood are the ruins of ancient Roman villas. Such a neighborhood could not but leave an imprint on the architecture and design of the hotel. The hotel itself consists of two large buildings. A few years ago, reconstruction was carried out here, all rooms are renovated, and the territory is improved.

Hotel Dionysos Central Hotel (Paphos): description and background of rooms

Living conditions are an important point, because after an eventful day it is always pleasant to return to a comfortable, cozy room. The Dionysos Central Hotel is not too big: there are 97 rooms on its territory. Most rooms are standard double rooms, although there are several Deluxe categories, with an increased level of comfort.





As you can see in the photo, the rooms here are not too big, but clean and comfortable, furnished with new furniture and modern appliances. At your service - a powerful air conditioning system, without which it is difficult to do in the summer heat. There is a TV, however, you can watch on it mainly local channels. There is also a small safe and mini fridge. The minibar is empty and is filled only at will.

The room is complemented by a bathroom, which is equipped with modern fixtures. Most rooms have comfortable showers. By the way, water is supplied constantly, there are no problems with this. Residents receive a set of clean towels, which are replenished every day during cleaning. According to reviews, they are also cleaned well. In a word, it is always a pleasure to spend time in a hotel room.

What kind of food do guests offer?

The choice of food scheme at the Dionysos Central Hotel (Paphos) is left to the guests. For example, you can pay a full boarding house, attend breakfast only or opt out of meals at the hotel. Nevertheless, the all-inclusive scheme is quite popular.





The hotel has several establishments where tourists can eat. Buffets are served at the central restaurant Dionysos. Here you can relax in a pleasant setting while enjoying delicious local and international dishes. Traveler reviews suggest that the menu is quite varied, and there is always plenty of fresh fruit, fish and seafood.

From May to October, the doors of the Italian restaurant Amore Trattoria Italiana are open. Near the pool, in the open air, La Place Royale cafe is organized. Here guests can order hot snacks, desserts and drinks, relax, chat and enjoy interesting performances, live music. By the way, the cafe is open until 2 am, which will delight tourists who like to have fun after sunset.

How close is the beach? Water leisure tourists

Of course, most tourists choose Cyprus as a vacation destination precisely because of the golden beaches. What can the hotel complex Dionysos Central Hotel 3 * offer in this regard? Paphos is a city famous for its seaside resorts. The nearest beach is located about 300 meters from the hotel, which is very convenient, because you can get to it in a few minutes.





It is worth saying right away that the beach here is small, as it is located on the hills. However, for a small fee, you can rent a sunbed with a mattress and a parasol. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient, but special rubber shoes are still necessary, especially if you are relaxing with a child.

There are opportunities for more active recreation. On the coast it is always easy to rent a boat or canoe, for a small fee go water skiing and motorbikes, boats, banana. Fans of diving can contact the local scuba diving center, where they offer excursions to the most interesting places, as well as equipment rental and practical training courses for beginners who have never dived before.

Does the hotel offer an additional service?

For its customers, the Dionysos Central Hotel tried to create all conditions for maximum comfort. Dry cleaning and laundry services are also available, and guests are also offered ironing service. If necessary, staff will call you in the room in the morning to wake you up. There is no medical center here, but you can call a good doctor at any time. Here you can exchange currency.





Guests enjoy free parking, next to which there is a rental point where you can rent a car or bike. A minimarket is also constantly working, where it is easy to purchase various little things, products and interesting souvenirs from the hotel. The prices are quite reasonable, and there are many other shops near the hotel. There is also a flower shop - you can please yourself or your friends with a beautiful bouquet.

The hotel is often used for various business events. There is a spacious conference room with projectors and other necessary equipment.

How do guests spend their time on site?

In the courtyard of the Dionysos Central Hotel 3 * (Paphos) there is a small pool around which there are sun loungers (in sufficient quantity). Animators are constantly working on the territory, who organize various contests, quizzes, team games during the day. And guests can sign up for sports (gymnastics, aerobics, yoga, water aerobics), as well as various lessons (dancing, painting on silk).





Do not forget that Paphos is a city with an interesting history. The hotel will help you choose an interesting excursion if you want to explore the surroundings and travel a bit around the island. By the way, there is a winery nearby, which can be visited for a small fee.

Is it possible to relax here with a child?

It's no secret that many parents prefer to spend holidays with their children. The family has slightly different requirements for the hotel. So what can the hotel complex Dionysos Central Hotel 3 * offer?

Despite the fact that this hotel is not a fashionable complex, it provides some services. In particular, parents can count on a rollaway bed in the room, as well as chairs for feeding in local restaurants. The hotel does not have a special children's menu, but you should not worry too much about this, as the choice of dishes is large, and therefore it is easy to choose something for a child of any age.

There is a small section in the pool where children can splash around. There is also an experienced nanny on site who will be happy to look after your child. Naturally, its services must be paid separately.

Paphos, Dionysos Central Hotel 3 *: how do tourists respond?

The opinion of people who have already managed to visit a particular place is a source of valuable information. Today, many travelers spend their holidays in a city like Paphos. Dionysos Central Hotel 3 * for the most part leaves a positive impression.





The hotel is not too big, but has its own cozy territory. There is a pool and a range of other entertainments, which is why guests rarely have to get bored. An undoubted advantage is the proximity to the beach, shops, restaurants and other facilities of the resort area.

According to reviews, the food here is also at a decent level - there is always a choice of dishes, and the food is fresh and tasty. We should also say that most employees understand the Russian language, which greatly facilitates communication, especially if you do not speak English too well. The staff here are polite and helpful.

Hotel Dionysos Central Hotel 3 * is perfect for a relaxing holiday. Accommodation prices here are also quite democratic, especially considering the proximity to the beach and the quality of service in the hotel itself. Travelers definitely recommend visiting this place.
