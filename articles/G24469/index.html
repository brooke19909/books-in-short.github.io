How to tie a bow from a ribbon effectively and in a couple of minutes?

So, you decided to decorate a gift box or a hair clip with your own hands , but you have no idea where to start? Or is the main part of the work done, but still something is missing? Then it's time to figure out how to tie a bow from a ribbon so that it becomes a real work of art, and use it to decorate any objects! What is needed for this, and where to start? We will talk about everything in order.

How to choose a tape

In order to get a beautiful and stylish bow, you need to choose the perfect ribbon. Satin or silk is great, but you can also use organza ribbon or a thin strip of brocade. It should be the most suitable width. If you plan to make a bow for a gift box of a small size, then the optimal size will be a strip of 2-3 cm. If the gift is large, or you are going to decorate a frame for a mirror, bag or other, large enough object, you will need a tape at least 5 cm wide So, how to tie a bow from a ribbon? Try to work out on unnecessary tapes, and then you will definitely get a stunningly beautiful luxurious copy that even the most experienced craftswomen will envy!

Manufacturing technology

Any girl knows how to tie bows from ribbons. The very first lessons are bows on the hair of your favorite doll. But several years pass, and there is a need to create a beautiful and stylish, fluffy or strict bow. So how to tie a bow from ribbons in such a way as to surprise relatives and friends? Consider one of the simplest, but at the same time effective options. Bow ball. To create it, you need to stock up on a ribbon whose length is 10 times greater than the estimated bow size. Form a few (8-10) perfectly aligned rings. It is advisable to use a special blank for these purposes. Her role is perfectly played by your own palm. Then you need to find the center and slightly flatten the ring on the sides. Hold the center firmly. Now cut along the edges of the tape in the center of the small triangles, so that there is only a jumper between them. Make sure it is strong enough. Drag it very tightly with a thin ribbon that fits perfectly with the main bow. And then start pulling one loop from the middle. At the same time, try to rotate the finished loops 90 degrees. Thus, you will be able to form a luxurious ball. Well, you can attach it to the product using threads or double-sided tape. This is one of the simplest, but at the same time effective ways of how to tie a bow from a ribbon.

How to decorate finished bows

For those who already know how to tie a bow from a ribbon, but have no idea how to decorate it, we offer several simple and original solutions. One of them will be the use of buckles for belts or shoes. The bow should be suitable in size. An ordinary classic bow looks great with such an adornment. The most basic thing is that the tape that closes the mid-mount is passed through this buckle. As a result, we get a stylish and unique bow. You can also successfully use the remains of beads, individual beads, rhinestones, sequins and other, no less funny and unnecessary gizmos. Now you can create your own exclusive bows from satin ribbons, which will decorate many old things.
