Divnomorskoe: boarding house "Seaside". Where you can stay for a vacation

Resorts of the Krasnodar Territory are famous for their well-groomed beaches and friendly sea, bright sunshine and clean mountain air, numerous health resorts and Caucasian hospitality. Coming to rest with children, many of us are looking for quiet and calm places, but with a variety of daytime activities. One of the best resorts for family vacations is Divnomorskoe.

Divnomorskoe





The settlement received its modern name relatively recently, in 1964. Prior to this, the village, located at the mouth of the Inogua River, was called Fake Gelendzhik because of a similar coastal outline. Another, Adyghe name Mezyb, is translated into Russian as "woodland". Indeed, the village, located far from sea and transport highways, is surrounded on all sides by mountain slopes. It is from here that the famous pine forest of Pitsunda begins. Unlike the Big Gelendzhik (which includes the Divnomorskoe Sea), there is an open coast and a cleaner sea, fewer noisy bars and night discos. When choosing a vacation (in a boarding house) with young children, it is advisable to look for a place that will be located slightly away from the waterfront. After all, it is here that the majority of institutions are located in which night festivities are held with singing in karaoke and incendiary dances.

Family pension





Located in the center of the village with the name Divnomorskoe, boarding house "Primorsky" is an infrastructure-developed complex and ideal for families with children. Six modern buildings are designed for 830 seats. The territory of the base is simply buried in beautiful flowers and greenery of various flowering shrubs and trees. Along the well-groomed alleys are playgrounds, benches and gazebos. The guarded area also has a parking lot, swimming pools for children and adults, a sports complex. Vacationers will be offered a varied and tasty three meals a day according to the buffet system.

Number of rooms





In the village with the name Divnomorskoe boarding house "Primorye" offers tourists rooms of three types of comfort: single, double and triple rooms with all amenities. Each is equipped with air conditioning, fridge and TV. The presence of constantly cold and hot water in the guesthouse will delight guests. Each room has a balcony, on which, comfortably sitting in the evening and breathing in the fresh sea air, you can admire the picturesque surroundings. The maids of the complex are busy with restoring order in the bedrooms.

Fun for kids





Arriving on vacation in Divnomorskoye (boarding house "Primorsky") with children, you can be sure that your children will surely like it here. Indeed, for this in a complex all conditions are created. In the playroom, you can leave the child under the supervision of an experienced teacher who will find something for him to his liking. There are mini-mazes, toys and board games, books and a TV, and the children will be happy to watch their favorite cartoons. Also in the pension there is a playground with various ladders, horizontal bars, slides and swings. Nearby is a swimming pool with fountains and a slide, in which the water is heated, and therefore is comfortable for kids. A stone's throw from it is a swimming bowl for adults measuring 25 x 11 meters. And all this splendor is surrounded by cozy sunbeds and parasols.

Beach for swimming

Divnomorskoe, boarding house "Primorsky" in particular, has its own beach, which is located 350 meters from the main building. Here, just a four-minute walk, there is a beach pebbled with small pebbles with a gentle slope into the sea. On the beach there are sun loungers, awnings and benches, as well as a pier from which you can dive into the water. Attractions and entertainment

Divnomorskoe, boarding house "Primorsky" will give a lot of new positive emotions and impressions. A memory photo can be taken by yourself or ordered from a professional working at a recreation center. In addition to the already described entertainment, holidaymakers have the opportunity to go on excursions and enjoy the local attractions. I must say that in the village itself and its environs there are a lot of them. This is a children's water park located on the waterfront, and an amusement park for kids in the boarding house "Energetik". Fans of extreme relaxation can enjoy a trip to the mountains on ATVs, jeeps and even an armored personnel carrier. At the same time, you can admire the ancient dolmens, beautiful waterfalls and swim in the mountain river. For those who prefer outdoor activities, there is the opportunity to take part in a bicycle tour of the picturesque places of the village of Divnomorskoye.
