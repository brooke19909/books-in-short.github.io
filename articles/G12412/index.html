Road to the beach

However, of course, guests do not come here because of the pool. They are attracted by the beautiful colorful and rich fauna and flora of the Red Sea and, of course, a beach holiday in Egypt. As we mentioned above, the Tropicana Azure Club Hotel (Egypt) does not have its own beach. However, its guests use the beach of the five-star Tropicana Grand Azure Hotel for free. This is not altruism. The reason is that both hotels are owned by the same person. Until 17 00, guests covered by this article of the hotel can walk to the beach through the territory of the aforementioned five-star.



Before heading to the beach, hotel guests should take a coupon at the reception indicating the number of the Azure Club Resort Ex Tropicana occupied by them and the number of residents. It will be useful to them for presenting to the bus driver, regularly, every 15 minutes, making flights to the beach. According to the recommendations of regular hotel guests, it is better to go to the beach after 11 00 , when the wind over the sea is less cool. Note that it is not necessary to go to the beach: if the bus takes the vacationers to it in five minutes, then the walk will take about fifteen minutes.

Moreover, on foot following, as we already said, the path will pass through the territory of the aforementioned five-star Tropicana Grand Azure.
