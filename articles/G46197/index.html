The recreation center "Primorskaya" is a way to relax economically and well

As soon as warm days come, people are drawn, like a magnet, into the hospitable village of Novomikhailovsky, which is located on the Black Sea coast. There are many places where you can comfortably accommodate. One of them is the recreation center "Seaside" (Tuapse district).

At the camp site you can relax at any time from May to October.

Beach



Located in a picturesque bay recreation center "Primorskaya" has its own beach. Sand and pebble surface is always clean and well-groomed. Every morning, the base staff cleans the resting place of algae or other debris that has been clogged up during the night. On the territory there are cabins in which you can change clothes, also canopies are installed to save from the sun. There is a free shower and toilet. For children, an inflatable slide is installed. There is also a water entertainment rental service. Here you can ride a catamaran, boat or scooter. Water bananas and tablets are provided.

Here you can go on an excursion to the White Cliffs by boat. This is where the Diamond Hand movie was shot.

The recreation center "Primorskaya" organized an outdoor fresh pool near the shore. By the way, thanks to him, the swimming season can be made a little longer. And it is in it that many people like to swim when the sea is restless. Also, when it is storming, there is the opportunity to swim in the sea, if you leave a little over the cape. Going a little further, you can see the wild rocky beach, shrouded in silence. Here calm, warm water and picturesque landscapes.



Food at the camp site

On the territory of the base there is a dining room, which offers three meals a day. If you prefer to eat in cafes or restaurants, you can take a walk to the village and in 15 minutes find yourself in a cozy place. Relaxing here, you can easily find the cuisine to your taste: there are Italian, Caucasian, Japanese, Russian and other dishes.

Infrastructure and Services

The recreation center "Seaside" offers to settle in rooms of any level of comfort. No matter what you choose, you will not have to be bored, because this place is filled with resort entertainment. Here are equipped sports grounds, a video salon, rental offices, table tennis, boat trips. In the evening you can go to a noisy disco or sit in a quiet restaurant. There are shops, barbecue, stalls, a market. If you are a travel enthusiast, head to the tour desk and you will be offered many options. You can choose a bus route or a walking tour. There are 33 waterfalls, canyons, caves, cities, wine tastings, fishing and other offers of interesting leisure.
