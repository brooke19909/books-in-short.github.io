Waterpark "H2O". Description of the place and the original slides

The H2O water park was opened in Rostov-on-Don in 2013. This complex has eight different slides:

  • "Space hole";
  • Bodyslide
  • "Black hole";
  • "Free fall";
  • "Flying boats";
  • "Tsunami";
  • "Magic hill";
  • Multislide.

The most extreme of the rides is Free Fall. On this hill you can reach speeds of more than thirty km / h. Flying from eighteen meters will give a lot of emotions. The magic hole is the longest slide, more than 156 meters.
