The best hotels of Zelenogorsk: photos and reviews of tourists

Zelenogorsk is one of the most beautiful resort suburbs of St. Petersburg. It is only an hour's drive from the center of the Northern capital, and at the same time it is a place with very clean and fresh air. The name "Zelenogorsk" speaks for itself, this city is replete with green spaces.

In these places you can feel the spirit of the real Baltic, because truly world events were happening here. This is really a fertile land for tourists. Rest in these places can be the most varied. That is why every year more and more tourists come to the city of Zelenogorsk, and many come back more than once. Zelenogorsk is a city with a great historical past. The city and its nearest territory became part of the Russian Empire in 1721, after the victory in the Northern War. At that time it was not yet a city, but was a small village called Therioki. Renamed Zelenograd in 1946, in the same year it ceased to be a village and received city status.

Rest in Zelenogorsk

Zelenogorsk is an excellent resort in the Leningrad region, you can say the pearl - the Gulf of Finland. This area is rightfully considered the most environmentally friendly on the coast. Dense coniferous forests, refreshing sea air, many sandy beaches, as well as a huge number of historical sites and monuments - all this combines this wonderful resort. By the way, about the sights, they can be attributed to unique fresh lakes, healing mud, as well as coniferous parks. Zelenogorsk has a special healing climate, and therefore there is a huge number of various sanatoriums, rest houses, as well as many cafes and restaurants. And therefore this resort town was so fond of tourists, vacationers and just residents of St. Petersburg, who often come here for the weekend.
