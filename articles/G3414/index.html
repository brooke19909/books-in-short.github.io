A fabulous country called Crete. Holidays in Crete

The largest island of Greece is Crete - a border separating the Aegean and the Mediterranean. The coast of Crete is rocky, they are indented with many bays and bays. The landscape of this island is diverse - high gorges and mountains, beaches and plains, sandy and pebble coasts. Crete is a beautiful historical island along which many conquerors passed, leaving behind a variety of monuments of different eras. Its history goes back thousands of years. The locals of this island with their characteristic hospitality and cordiality will tell anyone who wants a lot of legends and myths. Every corner of this unpredictable island will help you discover a lot of interesting things.

Holidays in Crete can rightfully be called enlightening, its frequent guests are not only ordinary tourists, but also archaeologists who still find interesting antiquities. Greek museums very carefully preserve the wealth of our ancestors. The island of Crete will reveal its secrets to anyone interested.

In addition to the rich history, vacation in Crete combines modern entertainment and, of course, indescribable beauty of nature. The climate there is very favorable, Mediterranean. Winters are short-lived, and summers are very warm and relatively dry. Precipitation falls mainly in the winter in the form of showers. Holidays in Crete are considered the most favorable from May to August, as well as by the end of October. Winter in Crete is warm from 12 0. So the sun shines over this island almost always and holidays in Crete are beautiful at any time of the year.

One of the main natural attractions are the mountains and fertile valleys, where orange and olive gardens and endless vineyards sprout. This area is indescribably beautiful.

Sand and pebble beaches stretch for thousands of kilometers. Many people from all over the world come to the island in order to soak up a little on these amazing, clean sands. Also, this island is famous for its beautiful caves, there are more than 3 thousand of them in Crete. Flora of the island, namely the flowering of orange trees , will help you to get in a romantic mood . The inhabitants of the island themselves and of course tourists do not cease to admire the beauty of blooming orange trees.

Holidays in Crete are unforgettable, and visiting this island at least once, you will want to return there again and again. Having purchased last-minute tours to Crete, you should tune in to a joyful and positive vacation - bewitching nature, interesting excursions and magnificent beaches, all this and much more can be seen when visiting this island. And everyone will be able to choose the most comfortable and suitable vacation option for themselves.

If you want to relax, it is best to visit the resort of Elounda. It is here that romance lovers spend their time. There are many cozy restaurants, luxury hotels.

Active travelers are invited to visit the excellent Hersonissos hotels. It offers guests bars, restaurants, fun cafes, many shops, as well as luxury hotels.

For couples with children, tour operators recommend choosing a service system where all-inclusive. And if you take last-minute trips to Crete, then this option will be not only comfortable, but also cheap. Holidays in Crete will be remembered for a long time with joy and enthusiasm.

Especially for women, it is possible to take a shopping tour. One of the most popular products in Crete are fur coats that are specially sewn for export. This kind of gift for yourself beloved for many years in harsh winters will warm, and remind you of a wonderful time in a fabulous country called - Crete.
