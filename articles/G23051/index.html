How to get around

Of course, this is an expensive pleasure - a vacation in Bang Tao Beach (Phuket). The reviews, however, assure that there is some compensation for the fact that you will pay a round sum for accommodation, meals and purchases in local stores. This is completely free transport within the resort. Shuttles go between all the beach hotels, and ferries that carry tourists and do not charge for it go along the bay. But if you want to leave the resort, you will have to travel exclusively by taxi. Transport in Phuket is generally expensive, but in Bang Tao this is especially noticeable. You will have to pay at least eight hundred butts for a one-way trip to Patong or to the island's capital. But in the village of Surin and the beach of the same name, you can walk along the coast. The road will take no more than a quarter of an hour. To leave for the airport, you need to order transport at least one day in advance. This can be done either at a taxi stop, or at various travel agencies, of which there are a great many.
