Dimitrion Central Hotel 3 * (Greece / Crete): photos, rating and reviews

Dimitrion Central Hotel 3 * is a small modern hotel that offers travelers comfortable conditions for living and recreation. Here your vacation will be truly unforgettable.

Resort Summary

Dimitrion Central Hotel 3 * is a modern resort hotel located on the very seashore. The hotel offers its guests 93 comfortable rooms decorated in a modern minimalist style. The rooms are furnished with the latest technology, and therefore guests will feel no less comfortable than at home.

The Dimitrion Central Hotel 3 * hotel has a fun and relaxed atmosphere. There is everything for guests to relax and forget about all their worries. You can sunbathe by the pool for days or enjoy your favorite drink at the bar. And lovers of active entertainment will also not have to be bored, because here you can do water sports or take part in a real jeep safari.





Resort Location

In the heart of the famous resort of Hersonissos, the luxurious Dimitrion Central Hotel 3 * hotel is located. The address of this establishment is 92 Eletheriou Venizelou. It is approximately 25 kilometers from the capital of Crete, Heraklion and its international airport. Just 2 km from the hotel there is a huge water park, as well as a luxurious mountain range. Travel guides also strongly recommend visiting the Lichnostasis Open Air Museum, which is about 3 km away.

Hotel Guest Apartments

An important aspect of the rest are comfortable living conditions. Guest apartments of the Dimitrion Central Hotel 3 * hotel can satisfy the needs of even the most demanding guests. The following accommodation options are provided for you:

  • Standard rooms offer double and triple occupancy. This is one spacious room in which the bedroom and a small sitting area are harmoniously combined. The room has access to a private balcony with comfortable plastic furniture.
  • Junior suites consist of two spacious rooms. The bedroom has a large French bed, a dressing table, as well as a place for a working corner. As for the living room, it is equipped with comfortable upholstered furniture, which, when unfolded, can serve to accommodate additional guests.

One can not say a few words about the interior of the guest rooms of the resort hotel Dimitrion Central Hotel 3 *. Photos taken by tourists and hotel agents demonstrate a modern rational design. Against the background of light and calm tones, bright impregnations suddenly appear that give a good mood and a charge of vivacity.





Room Amenities

Each traveler can feel the atmosphere of home comfort if he chooses Central Dimitrion Hotel 3 * for his accommodation. Crete is a very hospitable island, which is why every hotel considers it its duty to provide its guests with maximum comfort. For this purpose, the apartment provides the following list of amenities:

  • individual air conditioning, which will allow you to escape from the hot Greek climate;
  • a bathroom, which is equipped with a bath or shower, depending on the category of the room (an electric hairdryer, a set of cosmetic and bath accessories are also provided);
  • own balcony or terrace (if we are talking about the ground floor), furnished with plastic chairs and a small coffee table ;
  • portable clothes dryer (can be installed both in the bathroom and on the balcony);
  • landline telephone with access to various hotel services, as well as for calls abroad;
  • a modern widescreen TV connected to a satellite dish (mainly music channels are broadcast);
  • a small refrigerator for your products;
  • a safe with a combination lock, the use of which is paid extra.

An additional pleasant moment for you will be that upon arrival at the Central Dimitrion Hotel 3 * guests are provided with a free daily supply of drinking water, as well as bags with coffee, tea and sugar.





Animation program

You will not have to be bored if you choose an institution such as Central Dimitrion Hotel 3 * for your vacation. Crete is famous for its colorful entertainment, and therefore this hotel is trying to keep up with the general trend. The animators prepared the following entertainment program for the guests:

  • every evening in the lobby area you can listen to live performances by singers and musicians (quite often you can hear the performance of folklorists);
  • periodically, the restaurant hosts theme nights where you can get acquainted with traditional Cretan cuisine and culture;
  • barbecue parties are regularly held outdoors;
  • at night you can compete with animators and other hotel guests in the skill of singing in karaoke;
  • fiery pool parties;
  • belly dance shows, as well as training workshops in this area.

It is worth noting that the main emphasis in the animation program of the Central Dimitrion Hotel Hersonissos 3 * hotel is made precisely on local traditions. Thus, you will have a unique opportunity to get acquainted with the color and culture of Greece.

Restaurants and bars of the hotel

Hotel Central Dimitrion Hotel 3 * takes care of the proper nutrition of its guests. The following establishments work on the territory:

  • EL GRECO is the main restaurant, where breakfast, lunch and dinner are served for you if you are going to relax on an all-inclusive basis. For an additional fee, you can also order those dishes that are not available in the standard menu. During the day, you can also order traditional Greek cuisine. There is an outdoor terrace located in the garden.
  • The lobby bar is located in the lobby of the hotel and is characterized by an elegant setting. Here a pleasant and calm atmosphere prevails, conducive to friendly communication. Here you can enjoy your favorite drinks and light snacks, as well as use the wireless Internet. It is worth noting that there is a pool table.
  • Tropical Bar is located by the pool. He specializes in exotic cocktails, fresh fruit and cold drinks. Also here you will be offered fresh beer. Children and adults will certainly enjoy ice cream.
  • Fans of nightlife are simply obligated to visit the disco club Sugar. Here you can enjoy your favorite drinks around the clock, and incendiary discos are held in the evenings. You can also enjoy the performances of guest DJs.

Useful information for hotel guests

In any resort hotel there are certain rules and regulations that govern the accommodation and recreation of guests. The Central Dimitrion Hotel 3 * (Heraklion) was no exception. It will be useful for tourists to familiarize themselves with the following information:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests in the apartment begins strictly after 14:00 (however, if the hotel has prepared rooms available, then early registration may be carried out for an additional fee);
  • you need to leave your room on the day of eviction before noon (if you want to stay in the apartment for longer, you will have to pay extra for each additional hour of stay);
  • if children under 12 years old can be accommodated in existing bedding, then there is no charge for their stay;
  • payment of an extra bed is half the cost of one person per day in a room;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • You can pay for the services received both in cash and with plastic cards or through electronic payment systems.

General impression of the hotel

Many guests visit the Central Dimitrion Hotel 3 * (Crete) annually . Guest reviews suggest that this institution is fully consistent with its three stars. This applies to both prices and accommodation, food, service and other aspects. Counting on a budget holiday, you should not expect a stunning interior, luxurious food and high-quality service. Of course, you may be outraged by poor-quality cleaning (or rather, its almost complete absence), but you will certainly receive the necessary amount of attention and care from the employees.

Particular attention should be paid to nutrition. If you are counting on a five-star menu, then you are unlikely to get it. Get ready for the fact that you will be fed the same dishes every day. Nevertheless, you will not stay hungry. You will always find a delicious meat or fish dish, as well as a vegetable salad. In addition, the ice cream served at the hotel is one of the best at the resort. Tourists who have already been to this hotel do not recommend full board. It is better to order breakfast only, and the necessary variety of dishes can be found in neighboring cafes and restaurants.

It is worth noting that this hotel is absolutely not suitable for families. Arriving with children, you will be surprised to find that in addition to the slide and sandbox for the little ones, practically nothing is provided for here. Another disappointment for you may be that loungers on the beach are provided only at an additional cost. If you decide to take a seat on the sand, the guards will hint to you that you are interfering with the rest of the guests. But there is a way out of the situation. Having walked for about 20 minutes, you will find yourself on a beautiful wild beach where you can enjoy the clear sea and relax under the shadow of picturesque cliffs. Thus, the hotel fully justifies belonging to the budget category.
