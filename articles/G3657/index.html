Cheap hotels of Pyatigorsk: photos and reviews

In the heart of the Stavropol Territory is Pyatigorsk. This is the second largest city, a mud and balneological resort of federal significance. Tourists from all over the world come here and people involved in business trips.

Regardless of the purpose of visiting the resort, it is very important to choose a comfortable place to stay at affordable prices. All hotels in Pyatigorsk are happy to welcome visitors, but you should consider which of them suggest decent accommodation at a budget cost.





Hotel Intourist

The building is located in the city center, within walking distance from the famous mineral springs. Most of the rooms offer magnificent views of the surrounding landscapes, the Caucasus mountain ranges. Guests can choose accommodation in rooms with a balcony, a private bathroom (with a full set of toiletries), cable TV.

On the territory of this Pyatigorsk hotel there are restaurants where you can enjoy dishes of Italian, European and Caucasian cuisine.

The distance from the hotel to major transport hubs - the railway station and the airport - does not exceed 3 km. A shuttle service is available at an extra cost. The status of the complex is three stars. The staff speaks two languages.

Hotel Beshtau

The building is located in the city center, opposite a large shopping complex. From the windows of the rooms there is a great perspective on the Caucasus Range, the Beshtau and Elbrus mountains . The train station is located three kilometers from the complex, the airport is a 30-minute drive away.

Hotel "Beshtau" (Pyatigorsk) successfully fits into the concept of a business trip, as it has a modern conference room and a business center. In this place you can hold a business meeting at any level.

There is a 24-hour restaurant on site, serving Russian, Caucasian and European dishes. Tourists can enjoy delicious desserts, classic salads and hot dishes. There is a lobby bar in the lobby. The front desk is open 24 hours a day.

The hotel complex has a sports club with a pool and a gym. After training, visitors can relax in the Turkish or Finnish baths. The phyto bar offers mineral water and fruit juices.

Bristol (Pyatigorsk) is a premium class hotel with the most affordable prices. It is located in the city center. Walking distance to the Tsvetnik park and the Lermontov Gallery, mineral springs. The hotel staff is available to assist with tour arrangements, and after an informative walk, guests can enjoy spa treatments. There is a free Wi-Fi zone throughout. Travelers can use the parking lot. At the request of arriving and departing persons, a shuttle is organized to the transport hubs of the city.
