Hotel Hestia Resort Spa 5 *, Turkey, Side: overview, description, features and reviews

Many people who want to relax on the picturesque seashore choose Turkey as their vacation destination, because this country is famous not only for the beauty of nature and rich history, but also for quite affordable prices. One of the most popular resort areas is the city of Side. It is here that the hotel complex Hestia Resort Spa 5 * is located.





Naturally, before the trip, tourists try to get all the possible information about the place where they are going to stay. So where exactly is the hotel located? What room conditions can I expect? Are there any conditions for a fun holiday with children? How close is the beach? Answers to these questions will be useful to many travelers.

Location Description

Where exactly is the hotel complex Hestia Resort Spa 5 * located? Territory and infrastructure of the hotel Hestia Resort Spa Side 5 * (Turkey, Side)

This hotel complex is located almost on the seashore. The area is about 26 thousand square meters. Hotel Hestia Resort Spa 5 * consists of eight small four-story buildings built in a modern style. This complex stands out for its developed and convenient infrastructure. Almost the entire courtyard is reserved for swimming pools. There are several terraces for relaxation and sports grounds for more active pastime. Nearby there is a spacious park area with trimmed lawns, tall palm trees and figured shrubs - guests love to stroll here, enjoying the beauty of nature and fresh air. By the way, the hotel grounds are also decorated with greenery and bright exotic plants.

The hotel complex Hestia Resort Spa 5 * was opened in 1996. The last thorough reconstruction was carried out in 2011, and cosmetic repairs - in 2015. Therefore, tourists can count on high quality service and the availability of modern amenities.

How many rooms are on site?

Luxury hotels with all amenities are just what Turkey is famous for. Hestia Resort Spa 5 * is no exception to the rule, as guests are expected to have spacious comfortable rooms of different categories. The hotel offers its guests 506 rooms:

  • Standard rooms for 2 adults (+ child). Depending on the location of the room, the window offers views of the sea, courtyard or park area.
  • Superior rooms (suites).
  • Spacious one-room family rooms for 3-4 people; consist of a sleeping and living area with folding furniture.
  • Family rooms, consisting of two large separate rooms.

The hotel also has several rooms with special equipment for the comfortable stay of people with disabilities. There are also several romantic rooms for honeymooners.

Photo and description of rooms





What conditions should be expected by guests of the Hestia Resort Spa Side 5 * hotel (Turkey)? In fact, there is everything you need for a truly comfortable stay. Regardless of the category, all rooms are clean and spacious, furnished with new furniture. The rooms are decorated in a simple yet elegant style. They tried to create a truly homely atmosphere.

The floors are either covered with carpets or laminate, depending on the residential building. From the room, the door leads to a spacious balcony, where for more convenience guests have arranged comfortable furniture - it is nice to enjoy views of the sea or the garden from here.

Naturally, there is a set of much-needed household appliances. There is a central air conditioning with a convenient control panel. In the room you will find a TV on which you can watch satellite channels (there are, by the way, several Russians). There is also a telephone. Each room has a small convenient safe, but you must pay extra for its use. As for access to the Internet, this service is also paid. The minibar is replenished daily with drinking water and soda - the remaining drinks are delivered to the room only on request, at a separate price.

There is a spacious bathroom - depending on the category of the room, it has a bath or shower. There is also a toilet, sink, large wall mirror and hairdryer. Each guest receives a set of towels, which are changed every day during cleaning. You will also find a set of cosmetic and hygiene products, the stock of which is replenished as you use it.

What kind of food is offered to tourists?





For many travelers, the issue of nutrition is important. So what can the hotel complex Hestia Resort Spa 5 * offer? Reviews of tourists who have already managed to visit this place indicate that the food here is really at a decent level.

Like most hotels in Turkey, Hestia Resort Spa offers its guests an all-inclusive scheme. What does it mean? Hotel guests are entitled to a full three meals a day in the main restaurant. In addition, you can get free drinks and light snacks in the hotel bars.

According to reviews, the choice of dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner is really large. In the morning you will find various slices, several types of scrambled eggs, delicious pancakes, a large selection of fresh fruits and pastries. Lunches and dinners are more diverse - you will find numerous vegetable dishes, cold dishes, cheese slices, several types of side dishes, fish and meat dishes, and, of course, an abundance of sweets.

The hotel complex Hestia Resort Spa 5 * also offers its guests to visit a la carte restaurants. For example, in a cozy, decorated in a national style restaurant Osmanli you will find a huge selection of Turkish dishes. You can enjoy the sophistication of an Italian dinner in a place called Il Lievoto. Have a great time at Bon Apetit Restaurant, which serves French and Mediterranean cuisine. If you are a lover of spicy food, then you should visit the Mexican cafe Grand Kanyon. Pescatore Fish Restaurant serves delicious, healthy fish and seafood dishes.

The hotel has several bars. One of them is located by the pool, which is very useful, since in hot weather guests simply can not do without soft drinks. You should definitely visit the stylish Sunset Bar, where you can enjoy strong drinks, exotic cocktails and a relaxed, cozy atmosphere.

If you wish, you can always eat outside the hotel Hestia Resort Spa 5 *. Side is a large tourist city where there are many cafes, restaurants, bars and other catering establishments for every taste.

Beach holidays for guests

What beach vacation can a tourist offer the hotel Hestia Resort Spa 5 *? Turkey, Side in particular, is a place where they are primarily sent in order to make the body healthier and have fun on the seashore.





The hotel has its own beach, which is located about 300 meters from its territory. It can be reached on foot in a few minutes. If you don‚Äôt like walking, then for greater convenience, a shuttle service constantly runs from the hotel territory, bringing tourists to the beach and back.

The seashore is clean and comfortable, covered with soft white sand. The entrance to the sea is convenient, there are shallow areas that will please parents with children - swimming is safe here, and the water is always warm. A sufficient number of comfortable sun loungers with sun umbrellas are located on the shore. There is also a bar where guests can get cocktails, chilled drinks, beer and light snacks.

Naturally, fans of more active recreation will also not be bored. For example, you can ride a canoe, boats, catamarans. For a small fee, tourists can get a lot of positive emotions by trying parasailing, water skiing, a banana, or by taking a short boat trip along the coast.

The local coast is popular among fans of windsurfing, however, you can deal with it only with the appropriate license. In the sports center of the hotel you can pick up a board, rent a suit. Beginners are offered to take a few lessons from professionals.

Conditions for relaxing with children





For parents who are going on a trip with their children, an important issue is the availability of appropriate conditions. What can the Hestia Resort Spa Hotel in Side offer? Turkey is a great place to relax and improve your child, and this hotel can offer the necessary amenities.

Naturally, the hotel administrator will help you choose the appropriate room, and if necessary, you will be provided with a cot for the child. Unfortunately, there is no special children's menu in the local restaurant, but since the menu is quite diverse, you can easily find something tasty and healthy for children.

As for entertainment, here the Hestia Resort Spa 5 * hotel has something to offer. As already mentioned, the sea here is warm, with a convenient entrance, so children of different ages can swim. Also in the courtyard there is a specially equipped children's pool. There are several water slides for tourists of different ages - they will surely appeal to older children.

In the open air, surrounded by greenery, there is a spacious playground with swings, slides, sandboxes and even a real fairy-tale house. Nearby is a mini club where children from 4 to 12 years old are accepted every day. By the way, many animators and educators speak Russian, so your child will not have communication problems. Here, children can play, paint, dance, needlework, take part in fun games and competitions.

The hotel also has several professional nannies. If necessary, you can leave your child for several hours under the watchful supervision of a teacher, although this service is paid separately.

The hotel has created conditions for a comfortable stay. To begin with, it is worth saying that a currency exchange office with a fairly favorable rate is constantly working here.
