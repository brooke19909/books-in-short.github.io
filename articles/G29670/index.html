What is interesting in Sousse (Tunisia)?

The hotels of this ancient city are located in the so-called tourist zone. Sousse as a resort began to develop since the sixties of the last century. Medina is the old part of the city. It was founded by the Phoenicians in the XI century BC. e. In ancient times, it was an important port. The Romans captured the city in 202 e. and they called him Gadrumet. In the Byzantine Empire, he bore the name Justinianpolis. When the Arabs recaptured the Barbary coast in the seventh century, they reduced this long name to Sousse. Many historical events left traces in the city. It was captured either by the Spaniards, then by the Venetians and the French, and during World War II, Sousse was badly damaged by the bombing.
