Madame Cuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 *, Phan Thiet, Vietnam: hotel description, reviews

The resort and hotel complex Madame Cuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * offers its guests an unforgettable and comfortable stay on the sea coast, as well as a high level of service and a huge list of quality services.





Hotel Feature

Resort Madame Cuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * offers its guests to settle in comfortable rooms, decorated in a modern and quite elegant style. In addition to the interesting design, the apartments will also delight travelers with a wide range of amenities that create a warm homely atmosphere. A nice addition to this situation will be a beautiful view from the window and quality room service.

Great attention is paid to providing leisure to travelers who prefer to stay at Madame Cuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 *. For this purpose, an excellent resort infrastructure has been developed, which includes many serving and entertainment facilities. The specialization of this institution can be considered spa treatments that will help you to completely relax and have a good rest. You can also tighten your figure by visiting the well-equipped gym. There are many entertainment facilities that will not let you get bored.

At the Madame Cuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * hotel, you will be provided with a fine breakfast buffet each day. All dishes are very satisfying and varied, so you can recharge your batteries for new discoveries and get acquainted with culture. You can also taste the local cuisine at the restaurant. Chefs can also prepare traditional European dishes for you.





Where is the resort located

Madame Cuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * hotel is located on a beautiful private beach, which is located near the resort village of Mui Ne. This beautiful and very picturesque complex is located about 8 km from the large local market, where, in addition to products, there are many souvenirs, items of clothing and other goods necessary for tourists. To look at the famous sand dunes, you have to overcome a path of 7 km. If you drive 3 km further, you will find yourself at incredibly beautiful waterfalls called Fairy Springs. The nearest airport is 200 km from the hotel.

What facilities are provided in the rooms?

In each of the rooms of the resort complex you will find the following amenities:

  • since the weather in Vietnam is very changeable, you will undoubtedly have to have an individual air conditioner, which can not only cool, but also warm the air if necessary;
  • a TV connected to a cable transmission package will help fill your leisure time while in the room;
  • You can call the reception or take advantage of the international line thanks to the availability of a telephone;
  • the bathroom has a hairdryer, bath accessories, as well as all the necessary cosmetics for the shower;
  • a minibar will provide you with your favorite drinks at any time of the day or night;
  • the apartments have special kits so you can always make tea or coffee yourself;
  • there is a refrigerator for storing the products you bought.




Positive feedback and thanks

Many tourists prefer the quiet hotel Madame Cuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * for a quiet and relaxing holiday. Reviews about it demonstrate the high quality of services, as well as many vivid impressions of the rest:

  • breakfast includes a variety of European and local dishes, and fresh exotic fruits are served daily;
  • due to the secluded and remote location of the hotel, it is completely fenced from the noise of the road and city bustle;
  • the territory of the resort complex, although small, but very picturesque, with lots of vegetation and exotic flowers;
  • the hotel has a very nice clean pool, which has a children's section (and since the weather in Vietnam is windy and the sea is restless, it is very important that there is a place where you can swim);
  • the guest rooms are very bright and spacious, and they are cleaned every day (even though there is no tip, the room will always be clean).




Negative feedback and comments

Despite the fact that in general the reviews on this hotel are quite positive and approving, there were some negative points and negative comments:

  • in the hotel restaurant the prices are several times higher than in similar institutions in the city center (apparently, the hotel owners are using the fact that getting to other cafes is long and inconvenient);
  • on the beach, as well as by the pool there are not enough sun loungers, because not everyone has enough places (the problem is especially acute in the morning);
  • a lot of insects live in the hotel (both in the rooms and on the territory), which sting painfully (then irritation forms at the site of the bites);
  • When paying in a restaurant, always check the bill, as guests are often cheated;
  • the staff does not speak Russian.

This hotel is just perfect for those who are looking for tranquility and privacy. It is worth noting the pleasant atmosphere of the institution, as well as the high level of service. Of course, it was not without minor flaws.
