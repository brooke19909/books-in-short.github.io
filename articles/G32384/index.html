Kleopatra Hermes Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): hotel description, reviews

One of the most unusual resorts in the East is the city of Alanya, which is located on one of the shores of Turkey. Nature rewarded this coast with an unusual shape - in the form of a horseshoe. Alanya attracts with clean, narrow beaches with unusual yellow sand. This place is characterized by a special arrangement of rocky capes and lovely sandy bays.

Ideal place for family holidays



The resort is ideal for families with children, the sea is not deep, and the family budget is not particularly affected. In this city, especially to attract a large number of tourists, the most affordable prices are set. A luxurious water area offers its services to adults and children. In the amusement park, you can recharge with adrenaline and get a lot of emotions, taking a ride on a couple of extreme attractions. Alanya breathes an atmosphere of oriental sophistication, comfort and warmth. Excellent cuisine, entertainment for every taste and budget, hospitable people, the warm sea and the gentle sun - all this is available here, in this amazing resort. But most noteworthy are the hotels in Alanya (Turkey). Accommodation prices here are among the lowest, but in terms of service they are not inferior to more expensive offers.

If you want to economically relax in Turkey, the city of Alanya is what you need. Here you can easily find inexpensive accommodation near the sea coast. All kinds of exotic fruits are mere pennies. The largest banana plantation is located in this city.

In Alanya, there are several areas, one of the most popular is called Alanya-Inchekum. It is here that you can find accommodation for every taste. Some historical sources mention that Cleopatra loved to relax in this place. Thanks to this legend, a huge beach was named after her.

Why choose Alanya?





Romantic lovers can indulge in a nightly boat trip. Young people here also have something to do. They are attracted by the abundance of clubs, restaurants, dances of oriental beauties and a host of other entertainments. The nightlife in Alanya does not stand still. Because of this, the city is often called Turkish Rio de Janeiro.

Every year, Alanya is visited by a huge number of tourists. It has a very mild maritime climate, and the beaches are crystal clear and comfortable. This is the most versatile city for recreation, there is a lesson for everyone. For a noisy young company there are many clubs and discos, the newlyweds can enjoy the sea and exotic nature. And even for people of advanced age, too, there will be their own entertainment. But the main property of Alanya is luxury hotels. One of them will be discussed in this article.

Location of the hotel Kleopatra Hermes Hotel 3 *



The hotel is a 2.5 hour drive from the airport. The center is only 10 minutes walk (500 m), not far from the complex stretches the eponymous beach Kleopatra Beach. All the necessary souvenirs, fruits can be purchased in stores or in the market near the hotel. In the evening you can stroll through the park area, at the end of the street there is an excellent BIM grocery supermarket. For long journeys there is a bus stop, you need to walk 15 minutes on foot.

Short description

Hotel Kleopatra Hermes Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya) consists of a 5-storey beautiful building with an elevator and stairs. This is a relatively young building, it is a little over 20 years old, its area is 1500 square meters. The hotel management is planning an early restoration, as the latter took place in 2007. The rooms are always clean and spacious, each vacationing staff tries to choose the most comfortable conditions for their stay. Each apartment has its own style, the walls are decorated in a special way. The main guests of the hotel are Russian-speaking tourists, here you can always meet interesting people and find yourself an excellent company for the rest period. The hotel staff is very attentive and friendly. Although most of them practically do not know Russian, everyone does the work diligently and always solves your requests. The main advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the sandy beach, various park areas and an abundance of souvenir shops.

Nutrition




The Kleopatra Hermes Hotel 3 * hotel offers four meals a day: half board or full board.

For breakfast, cooks prepare something light and dietary. It can be salads, chicken eggs, bread, for dessert - jam or honey. An abundance of fruits and vegetables can also be enjoyed for breakfast. At lunchtime, fish, chicken or soy meat is offered, along with vegetables, rice or pasta. The first is tomato soup. For an afternoon snack, flour products are served, most often cupcakes. Dinner is practically no different from lunch. As for food, vacationers are mostly satisfied with the service in the restaurants of the Kleopatra Hermes Hotel 3 *. How to get to the beach

The famous Kleopatra Beach is located 50 meters from the Kleopatra Hermes Hotel 3 (Alanya). Reaching it will not be difficult, you will spend a maximum of 5 minutes on the road. This time can be used to advantage and purchase the necessary products or things along the way in mini-shops. This sandy beach is the best in the city. The clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea, warm yellow sand and clean surroundings will delight every visitor. On the beach you can rent sun loungers, umbrellas and other beach accessories. If you forgot a towel, then it can be purchased on the beach for a small cost. Kleopatra Beach is so popular that all guests of Alanya come to sunbathe at least once on this beach. Due to its enormous size, there are always empty seats.
