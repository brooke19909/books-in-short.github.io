Hotels of Feodosia by the sea: addresses, reviews

The city of Feodosia, located on the Crimean peninsula, is a very popular place among tourists. This settlement attracts travelers with its proximity to the sea, developed infrastructure, and the availability of comfortable hotels. About where you can stay in Feodosia, read the article.

"Robinson's Cabin"

This hotel in Feodosia by the sea is one of the most popular in the city. It is located only seven kilometers from the central region, on the Kerch highway, 72v. Nearby is a dolphinarium, on the territory of the hotel there is a well-kept beach with soft sand. Guests can rent a sun lounger or umbrella to enjoy spending time by the sea.





"Robinson's Cabin (Theodosius) - comfortable hotel. Each room has a private balcony with spectacular sea views. The rooms located in the buildings belong to five categories, such as luxury, semi-luxury, standard and its variations. It is possible to settle in a separate hut built of wood and equipped with its own kitchen. This house is designed for the simultaneous residence of five people in it.

Rooms

Depending on the chosen building, the number of inhabitants in the room varies. So, in rooms of buildings 1 and 3, 2-3 people can simultaneously accommodate, and in the second building only 2 guests. There is a difference in the types of beds. For example, the rooms in the first building are equipped with double beds and sofas, the second with two single beds, and the third with transforming chairs. At the request of the guest, the staff of the Robinson's Hut Hotel (Feodosia) provides guests with an eurofold or crib with high sides.

The hotel rooms are equipped with air conditioning, with which you can maintain a comfortable temperature. In addition, each room is equipped with a safe, refrigerator, TV. Wireless internet is available for a fee. The rooms have a bathroom with washbasins, showers or bathtubs, which always have fresh towels and basic cosmetics.





Suite, junior suite, as well as standard improved layout - rooms in which there is a hairdryer and telephone. Hot and cold water is supplied around the clock; in the winter, autonomous heating is available. The staff provides daily housekeeping, and once every few days provides guests with fresh linens. In their reviews, guests note that the hotel staff are polite and always perform their duties efficiently.

Services

Hotel in Feodosia on the beach consists of several buildings. In one of them there is a dining room called "Cutlet", as well as a cafe "Sail" and the restaurant of the same name. Guests can enjoy dishes of European, Black Sea, Ukrainian cuisine.

The range of services provided by the hotel is very wide. So, those who wish can visit the Finnish sauna or massage room, play billiards. For guests with children, a playground is built on site.

Complimentary hotel services include room service, free parking with 24-hour security and a permanent reception. For a fee, you can use the laundry service, rent a yacht or take a guided tour.

"Golden Sands"

Not far from the city is the beach of the same name, but there is also a hotel. Hotel Golden Sands (Feodosia) was built in 2005. Since then, vacationers from different countries come here.

The hotel has 20 rooms belonging to different categories. Each of the rooms has everything you need for a comfortable stay, most of them have access to a balcony or terrace. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the Golden Sands Hotel (Feodosia).





The mini-hotel is located on the seafront, on Kurortnaya street, 42, just two kilometers from the center. Nearby is a golden sandy beach. The hotel has parking, a covered courtyard, a playground for children. Those who wish can visit the sauna. Guests leave positive feedback about the service at this mini-hotel. Guests note that the rest was held with maximum comfort.

Miletus

There is another hotel in Feodosia by the sea. It is called "Miletus." This guest house is located on the Black Sea coast. Just half a kilometer is the concert hall "Star", a gallery named. Ivan Aivazovsky and the Museum. Alexander Green. You can get to these places in ten minutes. The distance from the hotel to the local bus station is less than a kilometer, and the train station is only 500 meters away.





Hotel Miletus (Feodosia) is located on 22a Black Sea Embankment. It is very comfortable and cozy. The rooms here are decorated in pastel colors. Each room has air conditioning, a fridge, a TV and a safe. A bathroom is also in the room.

There is a restaurant where you can enjoy homemade dishes. If you want to take a walk before dinner, you can walk to the cafe on the waterfront. It takes no more than five minutes. The location of the hotel, according to the reviews of couples resting here, is very convenient, because the basic infrastructure is within walking distance.

"Scarlet Sails"

This hotel in Feodosia on the seafront is very popular. A comfortable 4-star hotel is located by the Black Sea, on Aivazovsky Street, 47-B. At a distance of 50 meters is the promenade, as well as a clean beach.

The hotel grounds are ennobled by a green park, which combines the natural beauty and skill of gardeners. Balconies and terraces offer scenic views of the greenery around the hotel.





Here you can not only have a great vacation, but also organize a banquet, buffet, conference. This hotel, like the other best hotels of Feodosia on the seashore, is equipped with a swimming pool, gym, playground, restaurant, laundry and conference room. In their reviews, guests note that the hotel provides a transfer service from the airport and back, and in case of health problems you can contact the medical center for help.
