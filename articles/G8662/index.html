When is it better to go on vacation?

It should be noted that the beach season in Sanya lasts almost the whole year. The climate here is mild and pleasant. In high season temperatures reign up to +30.2 ¬∞ C. The sea is warmed up to +25.9 ¬∞ C.

It is cooler here during low season. Temperature indicators of air and water do not fall below +22.6 ¬∞ C. But it rains often.

It is best to go here in November, March or April. And by the way, at this time it is often possible to purchase a ticket at a bargain price, since vacations for all, as a rule, fall in the summer months.

Who better to go here? Definitely for those people who crave an impression and dream of a fruitful acquaintance with a new culture for them. This is not a place for relaxation and unity with nature. Sanya is a city for emotion seekers.
