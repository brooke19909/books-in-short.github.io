Convenient ways to recharge a Sberbank card

Most residents of cities in the Russian Federation already use various means of electronic payments, but few people know how to recharge a card. Sberbank - one of the largest financial organizations in Russia - offers several convenient ways to conduct this operation. Even retirees can withdraw money from their plastic or credit card. Indeed, for this it is enough to contact the nearest bank branch or search for one of the many ATMs. If you need to replenish the card of Sberbank yourself or some other person, you can resort to one of the methods described



Online Transfer - Money Online

For modern people who spend a lot of time at a computer at home or in the office, there is a special opportunity to carry out this operation from their personal account. If money needs to be transferred to an existing client of the bank, simply use the Sberbank-Online service. True, for this operation it is necessary to connect this service, but then you can not worry about how to replenish the card. Sberbank provides the opportunity to manage their funds in the current accounts and deposits of this organization, as well as transfer money to any cards of individuals or legal entities.





We use an ATM

Almost everyone knows how to recharge a Sberbank card through a terminal. To do this, you do not need to go to the branch of this financial institution, it is enough to find a regular ATM. Similar devices can now be found at every step: in stores, various institutions and just on the street. The actions are simple:

  • you need to insert the card into a special hole in the ATM;
  • enter PIN code;
  • then select the item "Cash Reception";
  • deposit the required amount and pick up the check.

It is noteworthy that the transfer of funds to the balance occurs in 1-2 minutes, and sometimes instantly. And if you need to transfer money to another person's account, how to recharge a card through an ATM? Sberbank offers the following instructions:

  • after entering the PIN-code, select the "Transfer" section;
  • in this box enter the recipient number;

The nearest bank branch

For conservatives and those who are not friends with technology and various electronic equipment, there is a logical way out. So that such people do not suffer from the question of how to replenish the card, Sberbank offers professional assistance to its managers. To carry out such an operation, you will need to come to the nearest branch, taking with you a passport, the necessary amount of money and details where the funds will be transferred. Usually the full details of the recipient are not needed, an experienced employee will have enough of the full name of the owner and the serial number of the card.

Transfer from abroad

To replenish the card account of Sberbank, it is not necessary to carry this piece of plastic with you. However, you will need to know the SWIFT code of the branch that issued this card, as well as its bank details, the owner's full name and address. The entire package of information you just need to give in the window and indicate the amount of transfer, the rest will be done by the operators of the representative office of Sberbank, from which the payment is made.
