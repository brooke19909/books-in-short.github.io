Ramada Resort Akbuk 4 * (Turkey / Didim) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If you plan to spend your summer vacation in the resort town of Didim (Turkey), Ramada Resort Akbuk 4 * will be an excellent option for living. Since this hotel is a four-star, it has a fairly high quality service at an affordable price. We suggest today to get to know this hotel better and find out what is offered to tourists and at what cost. We will also find out what our compatriots think about living at the Ramada Resort Akbuk hotel 4 *.

Where is the hotel complex "Ramada Resort Akbuk" 4 *

Ramada Resort Akbuk 4 * hotel is located just 900 meters from its own sandy beach. The center of Didim resort is 22 kilometers away. The nearest airport is Bodrum City Airport, 50 km from the property. If your plane lands in this air harbor, you can reach the hotel within an hour. Relatively nearby there is another airport. It is located in Izmir and is removed from the Ramada Resort Akbuk 4 * hotel by 150 kilometers.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the rules adopted by the Ramada Resort Akbuk 4 * hotel, accommodation for newly arriving guests in their booked rooms is from 14:00. But if you arrived at the hotel earlier, you can count on an immediate check-in if your room has already been vacated by previous guests and prepared by the hotel staff. On the day of departure, you must leave the room before noon. Then you should pay for the reception for accommodation and use of additional services. We accept cash and plastic cards of Visa, Mastercard and American Express systems. Also, when booking a room via the Internet, be prepared for the hotel to withhold prepayment from you. In this case, when you check out of the hotel, you just have to pay the rest of the amount for accommodation and the cost of additional services. If you have already paid for the hotel accommodation to a travel agency, you will only have to pay for a separate service that is not included in the price.

Since the Ramada Resort Akbuk 4 * is mostly oriented towards family accommodation, children of all ages are allowed here. Moreover, if you place a child under twelve years old in an existing bed or a baby up to two years old in a crib, then you do not need to pay extra for their accommodation.

As for pets, their accommodation in the Ramada Akbuk Resort (Didim) 4 * is not allowed. So if you plan to go on vacation in the company of your four-legged pet, you should look for another hotel.

The hotel has several swimming pools: a large outdoor with slides, a relaxing one and a children's pool with a funny octopus-shaped slide. Next to them is a sun terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as a bar with drinks, ice cream and snacks.

Ramada Resort Akbuk 4 *: traveler reviews

Since many tourists choose hotels based on the comments of people who have already visited them, we decided to summarize the reviews of our compatriots about the Ramada Resort Akbuk 4 * hotel.

So, with regard to the appearance of the hotel complex, the interior and furnishings of the rooms, no one complained here. After all, the hotel began its work only this year, and everything here is new and modern. Air conditioning and plumbing also work properly. Many travelers really liked that the rooms are equipped with literally everything you need: from slippers and daily replenished tea and coffee sets to a clothes dryer located on a spacious balcony. There were no particular complaints about cleaning and replacing bed linen.

As for the hotel, it did not cause much enthusiasm, since for the most part it is a concrete site with a minimum of vegetation.

Some travelers were not very happy with the beach. Moreover, complaints were caused not at all by the fact of the need to travel there by bus (they went regularly), but by the state of the coast itself. So, the sand here is mixed in places with clay, as a result of which the water off the coast is very muddy, which caused discomfort to some vacationers.

Almost no complaints were noted by our compatriots about nutrition. So, the dishes here were very diverse and tasty. Everyone could find food to their taste. Also, many were pleased that during the whole day it was possible to have a snack in snack bars, to taste ice cream.

As for the staff, mainly the guests were satisfied with it. The only minus tourists say is that most of the hotel staff speaks only Turkish or English. Finding a person who understands the Russian language is almost impossible here. And this despite the fact that the vast majority of guests were tourists from Russia and other CIS countries. However, this fact could not adversely affect the rest. It is also quite possible that after the first season of work at the hotel some changes will occur. And next year the situation will change for the better.

In general, almost all tourists were satisfied with their stay at the Ramada Resort Akbuk hotel. They admit the possibility that they will come here again and are ready to recommend the hotel to their friends and relatives.
