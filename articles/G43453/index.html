Technology

Now let's take a step-by-step look at how a crocheted little dog is performed.

Amigurumi is a special art of knitting small, and sometimes tiny, products. It originated in Japan and was recognized by needlewomen around the world.

You can knit amigurumi with either knitting needles or crochet. It all depends on personal preference. But the size of the knitting needles and hook should be deliberately chosen smaller than for ordinary knitting. And all because the product connected in this technique should turn out to be quite dense, without gaps or holes.



The article uses the generally accepted notation:

  • sbn - single crochet;

  • VP - air loop;

  • x () - repeat the action as many times as indicated in brackets;

  • * - repeat the previous action.

Each detail (tail, legs, body, head, ears) will be considered separately. This is necessary so that even beginner needlewomen can understand how to get a crocheted dog. Description of the knitting technology will be detailed and understandable. And you will surely succeed!

How do legs fit?

The knitting technology of all amigurumi toys is very similar. Having dealt with her on the example of a dog, you can easily tie any other animal.

Start knitting a dog with legs. They, unlike the head, are not very visible, and if at first something does not work out, it will not be noticeable.

So, the legs of a dog crocheted do not have a circuit as such. But there is a small step-by-step instruction. Following the prompts, you will learn how to crochet a dog.

Knitting Technique:

  1. Collect 6 loops and connect the last to the first. Now until the end of the whole product just knit in a circle.

  2. In the second row of each bottom loop, knit 2 sc, pulling out two new loops in turn. When the row is finished, you get 12 loops.

  3. In the third row from each loop of the previous row, knit 1 sbn and add 1 VP. Such manipulations are repeated until the end of the row. At the end you will have 18 loops.

  4. 18 rows of 1 sb in each loop of the lower row.

  5. When finished, fasten the thread and cut it.

The second leg is knitted according to the same principle. Only at the end, do not cut the thread, but tie 5 VP from it and connect it to the second leg.

Dog assembly

That‚Äôs practically all. Parts of the toy are ready, now they need to be connected. To do this, use a hook or, if you prefer, a needle.
