Pillow-cat: pattern and manufacturing technology

Handmade home decorations are in fashion now. All kinds of textile accessories very well help create an atmosphere of harmony and comfort. A cat pillow will look fun and unusual . The pattern of this product is very simple. Even a novice needlewoman will cope with the manufacture of a soft sofa guest.



What do you need for sewing?

Use the list below to make a beautiful cat pillow:

  • pattern;
  • fabric of several shades, natural for an animal, or bright motley decorative flowers;
  • pins, chalk, scissors;
  • thread with a needle;
  • filler (synthetic winterizer, holofiber);
  • a sewing machine, although it is possible to make a pillow manually, for example, from felt, which does not require edge treatment and is connected on the front side, and details such as eyes and nose can be easily glued to the base.

So, nothing special is required to make a cute souvenir. You probably have small patches at your place. Any workpiece can be made even from several parts, just need more seams.





Which sample to choose?

In order for you to get an original cat pillow, the pattern must be appropriate. First of all, you need to choose how your product will look and find the appropriate template. If you want to take your favorite blank, simply print it on the printer in the right scale. If the item does not fit completely on the sheet, divide it into two or more, and then glue it with tape.

Consider your experience, if you are a beginner, try simple schemes. Below are the templates designed for beginners, so you can safely choose any option.

How to sew a pillow-cat (pattern and sequence of actions)?

To make a simple accessory, you need to do the following:

  1. Make paper parts.
  2. Lay out the elements on the fabric, circle with allowance for seams and cut.
  3. Usually the parts are sewn down on the wrong side, followed by inversion and filling with sintepon or holofiber. Do not forget to leave a small hole for this, which is manually sewn at the very end.
  4. If the ears and tail were cut out as separate parts, they should be sewn to the finished product or to the seam.
  5. Eyes, nose, mouth and mustache are embroidered (don‚Äôt forget to use the embroidery frame) or part blanks are attached with a needle and thread. This must be done before sewing the base seams. You can stick the details in the last place.

Work always goes according to such a scheme, regardless of the complexity of the product. The technology differs in the number of seams that need to be made. The simplest thing is to connect two identical parts in the form of a cat's silhouette.

In this case, you will actually only make one seam if the tail is not made by a separate part. In the figure below, the pattern suggests the presence of a side part in the pillow. For this, two additional bands are made. So, the product can be given a larger volume.
