The recreation center "Lumivaara" (Karelia): reviews, prices

The Republic of Karelia is a unique corner located in the North-West of Russia. The originality of this region lies in its picturesque surroundings, unique rhythm and lifestyle. Virgin forests, winding rivers with clear lakes harmoniously intertwined with massive stone archipelagos and flat landscapes. The surrounding beauty attracts people from different countries, it fascinates and leaves a feeling of inner peace. Every season in Karelia is unique.

Natural resources seduce with mystery and mysterious color. Anyone who has ever been here once will certainly return again. This is a true paradise in the bosom of wildlife, alluring with a cultural heritage and rich history. Hundreds of suburban complexes open the doors all year round to travelers. More recently, another recreation center, Lumivaara, has opened on the shore of Lake Ladoga , offering guests unique entertainment.

People come here for the weekend to fully spend leisure time, gain strength and recharge with positive energy. There is everything that fans of outdoor recreation can only dream of. Constantly tourists express admiring reviews about the Karelian complex. This is an opportunity to abandon a dull civilization, to relax spiritually and to be alone with the environment.

Exact location and short description





All suburban recreation centers in Karelia are the best solutions for active leisure away from the busy metropolis and gassed air. Especially if your activity involves a sedentary lifestyle, then you just need a tour to this region to cheer up and stretch your muscles. Forest base "Lumivaara" is located two hundred meters from Lake Ladoga, in a quiet village under the same speaking name.

Nearby is the border zone with Finland. And 200 km - the Northern capital of the Russian Federation St. Petersburg. Only 18 km from the railway station. The area itself is buried in coniferous vegetation, which is a huge advantage and a highlight of the complex. It becomes clear why the choice of most guests stops at this base. Just a couple of years ago, this territory was closed, due to which the surrounding nature retained its primacy.

There are a lot of berries and mushrooms in the forests, a variety of fish are found in the lake. Places are surrounded by clear reservoirs and small waterfalls, everywhere you can see sheer cliffs and rock formations. The recreation center "Lumivaara" will give you the opportunity to fully experience the charm of a suburban weekend. You will find exciting routes, campfire evenings, great hunting and fishing, and much more.

You will not be left without a roof over your head

Karelian camp site "Lumivaara" with open arms and joy welcomes distinguished guests. In a cozy and beautiful area for people to live, wooden cottages and small houses are built that can accommodate up to six people. It is recommended to reserve your favorite accommodation option in advance - approximately 5-7 days before arrival.

If you plan to stay for a few days, you can rent a room upon arrival. But it must be borne in mind that there may not be free cottages, then you have to live in a tent. In such a wonderful place, many organize solemn corporate parties, anniversaries, mark significant dates.

There are no interruptions in hot water. For your personal use there will be a bathroom, a spacious lounge with a large TV (satellite channels). Directly in the house is equipped a kitchen with household utensils, there are sleeping rooms. Near the cottage there is a terrace and barbecue. Internal infrastructure





Attentive and caring staff working in the forest complex will help to ensure that your stay is positive and fun. The base "Lumivaara" (Karelia) offers a rich assortment of interesting outdoor activities. Be sure to take a horse ride to the local area.

Communication with these good-natured animals will delight the youngest guests and will positively affect the nervous system. Sightseeing tours in Karelia





Coming from a faraway region or country, get a tour of the republic. Together with the guide or yourself, get acquainted with the architectural and historical structures and natural objects that are the property of the region. Recommended routes: Valaam Island, Lake Syamozero, Kivach Waterfall, Marble Quarry, the mysterious Arctic Circle and much more. Any trip will leave a pleasant impression.

Services provided





Have you ever been in a floating bath? If not, do not miss the chance to try. The recreation center "Lumivaara" offers everyone to rent a steam room with direct entrance to the lake. There is also a compact terrace. After the sauna, you can plunge into a cool reservoir, cleanse your body and soul of all the negative.

Connoisseurs of active leisure can take a kayak trip along winding rivers. If you want to retire - rent a boat, ride along rocky cliffs, admire the indescribable beauty of small islands (skerries) and waterfalls. For a thorough study of the area the whole day is not enough. In summer, at the full disposal of the water of Lake Ladoga. You can swim, dive and enjoy for days.

Winter holidays in Karelia are no less charming. The ski season opens. Sleds, snowmobiles and all necessary sports equipment are hired. Equipped slopes for descents. A storm of adrenaline and fireworks of positive emotions will constantly haunt you. For beginners, instruction is provided.

Fishing at the camp site "Lumivaara" (Karelia)





For lovers of a more relaxing pastime, fishing is organized at any time of the year. You can engage in fishing not only on Lake Ladoga, but also on small rivers and dams - these cannot be counted in the territory of the region. All gear is rented in the Lumivaara complex (a map of the region is presented in the article), it is also allowed to bring your own gear.

You can fish from the shore and boats, or sail on your own boat. About 50 species of fish are found in reservoirs : ruff, pike perch, grayling, salmon, pike, trout, etc. If you have been fishing for many years with this occupation, it is quite possible to catch a large individual that you can immediately fry on a fire.

The catch, of course, largely depends on a number of factors: weather, temperature, wind and banal luck. But what, in general, is the difference how many fish you catch, the main thing is the feeling of carelessness, the beauty of the landscape, the purity of the air and the peaceful silence.
