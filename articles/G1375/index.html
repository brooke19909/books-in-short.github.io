"North Slope", Toksovo: ski resort within the Leningrad Region

In the Leningrad region, not far from the village of Toksovo and only 20 kilometers from St. Petersburg and 20 meters from the lake Hepoyarvi, the resort complex "Northern Slope" is located. This is a picturesque place where you can relax with your family at any time of the year. But most often they come here in order to ski.

The "North Slope" in Toksovo became popular back in the 80s of the last century, but then athletes were involved here, today the ski slope is open to all comers. It got its name due to the fact that the slope faces north.

Infrastructure and where to settle

On the "North Slope" in Toksovo, parking spaces for vehicles are equipped for visitors. It is possible to rent a barbecue and order corporate services. Ski and other winter equipment can be rented. For children, the doors of the playroom are open and there is a playground. On the territory of the complex there is even a zoo.

And those who first arrived at the ski resort can use the services of an instructor.

There are three ski lifts designed for adults, and one for children. Minimum prices for lift services on weekdays. On holidays and weekends it can reach 100 rubles, but it decreases depending on the number of climbs.
