Hotel Kahlua Boutique Hotel 4 * (Crete, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Kahlua Boutique Hotel is a small but cozy boutique hotel with a small number of rooms. The hotel has a total of 39 apartments. But they all boast a stylish interior, as well as the presence of all the amenities necessary for a comfortable stay. However, everything should be told in order.





Location

Kahlua Boutique Hotel is located in Hersonissos. This is one of the most famous cities and, concurrently, resorts in Crete. It is located 25 kilometers from the capital of the island, namely Heraklion. The airport is also located there.

The main advantage of this presentable hotel is its close proximity to the sea. A private beach can be reached in just a minute.

It is also worth noting that the hotel was located in a place known as Limenas Hersonisu. This is the city center. It is here that many bars, restaurants, cafes, shops, as well as everything else, which is interesting for tourists, are concentrated. The AquaWorld Aquarium is 500 meters from the hotel, and the Lychnostatis Folk Museum is two kilometers away. No one will be bored here.

What is available in the hotel?

Kahlua Boutique Hotel has a variety of facilities and services. Despite the fact that every boutique hotel is focused on comfort and high quality service, you can still have a good rest. On the territory of the evening program with music, discos. But the main entertainment is a visit to a private beach.

But the number of services designed for comfort is greater. The hotel has free Wi-Fi, a shared lounge, luggage storage, an office with a photocopy and fax machine, and a car rental service. Here you can order a transfer, food delivery with drinks to your room, express and individual check-out / check-in. Since the hotel is multi-storey, for the convenience of guests, an elevator was created.

Still pleased with the level of training of workers working at Kahlua Boutique Hotel. People working at the hotel speak not only Greek, but also English, German and Russian.





Nutrition

The Kahlua Boutique Hotel 4 * (Crete) has a good restaurant that serves its guests a buffet and a menu. For breakfast, eggs are served in different types (boiled, omelettes, fried eggs), all kinds of salads, chopped vegetables, cereals, milk, watermelons. You can drink tea or coffee from the machine. Delicious fresh bread is served, from which you can make toasts and hot croissants.

For dinner, you can choose one hot dish from several (usually fish, meat, pasta and something vegetable, vegetarian). There is also a Greek salad, delicious fresh desserts and ice cream. The hotel also has a bar. Many guests like to relax there in the evening to the live music of the saxophone, smoking a fragrant hookah and drinking some exotic cocktail.

By the way, if you want delights, then you can look into one of the nearby taverns and order some national Greek dish. Many tourists do so.





Economy class

Now you can talk in more detail about those rooms that are available to potential guests of the Kahlua Boutique Hotel. The photo provided above shows economy class apartments. Their area is 16 square meters. m, inside there is one large bed for two. There is a safe, excellent air conditioning, dressing room, satellite TV, an electric kettle and a refrigerator.

There is also a bathroom with shower, hairdryer, cleaning products, hygiene items. Towels, linen and slippers are also provided.

And there are such little things as a telephone, sockets, spotlights and various decorative elements. But there is no balcony - you need to pay attention to this.

Apartment prices are very cheap for Greece. A week of accommodation for two people will be a little more than 19,000 rubles (with breakfast included in the price and free cancellation). If you need half board, you will have to pay 4-5 thousand more. However, even in this case, about 1,700 rubles will be charged per person per day.

Other options

Rooms feature city views. They have a smaller area (15 sq. M) and a different price - about 30 thousand rubles per week.





But guests can also rent 3-bed apartments. Their area is 22 square meters. m. Inside, usually one double bed and an additional, standard. But there are options with three separate beds. The conditions are the same. There is a view of the city, a bathroom and everything previously listed. Only the interior of the rooms is different, it can be seen in the above photo.

Three guests for 7 nights in this apartment (with breakfast) will have to pay 45,000 rubles. This is only 15 tons. per person per week. Booking and accommodation

Check-in at 14:00 and lasts until midnight. Departure starts at 8 am and ends at 12:00. But if the guests are interested in another time, they just need to talk about it with the administration. Usually guests go to a meeting.

The hotel allows children of any age, however extra beds and cribs are not available for them, just like many other conditions. However, this is a boutique hotel, and it is not oriented to rest with kids.

Nevertheless, the hotel is popular among business people and those who like a relaxing holiday by the sea in a place that boasts high-class service. That is Kahlua Boutique. Staff

Many people leaving reviews about Kahlua Boutique Hotel note the experts who work here. Everyone has a responsible approach to business, they treat guests well. Many speak Russian. High-quality cleaning, daily, timely replacement of linen and towels (beach ones are issued separately). Cosmetic kits are also replenished as needed. They include soap, shampoo, shower gel, hair cap, shaving kit, toothpaste with brush and body lotion. In general, everything you need. Therefore, you do not need to take anything from cosmetics with you. Here she is good, quality. There is no doubt whatsoever, because Greek cosmetics is known everywhere.

And also, if you plan to leave the hotel early, you should go to the reception and order a continental breakfast. The fact is that a standard meal starts at 7:30. Before that, guests can be offered tea, coffee, yoghurts, bread, cheese and honey. The staff is responsive, and guests will not be denied such a service.





Guest Comments

Kahlua Boutique Hotel 4 * reviews receive mostly positive. Which, in principle, is not surprising. After all, this is a great hotel with stylish rooms and more than reasonable prices.

The main contingent of vacationers are young people, couples in love, single travelers. The ideal choice for such tourists is comfortable, budget, close to the center and the sea. And besides, Hersonissos is the youth capital of Crete! So lovers to walk and have fun in nightlife should go here.

The sea is nearby, the beach is clean because it is that the bottom of the sea is not sandy. Pebbles and pebbles. The entrance to the water is through stone slabs. Throughout the day, guests of the beach are offered free cool water in glasses.

People who have been here claim that this is a great place for those who want to have fun. Very close to the hotel is a club called Palm Beach. The best parties are held on Fridays and Saturdays. Different music sounds, even Russian. The club is large, modern, they serve not only alcohol, but also hookahs. A little further there is Star Beach, where at 16:00 foamy parties are held daily . The entrance is free.

In Hersonissos it is noisy, and in the evening life is just beginning, but nothing will stop a good sleep if you close the door to the balcony or windows and turn on the air conditioning.

In general, if you want to have fun and spend time in a good hotel with a decent level of service, paying a small amount of money, definitely worth going here.
