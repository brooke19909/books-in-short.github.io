Features of the recreation center "Golden Fish" in Ulyanovsk

The base is located in the heart of natural beauties. The "Golden Fish" is located in close proximity to the city, and it is very easy to get there either by car or by public transport.

The administration of the place for rest is able to find an individual approach to each client and will help to organize a variety of events:

  • corporate party;

  • noisy youth parties;

The open spaces of the Zolotaya Rybka recreation center in Ulyanovsk can accommodate up to 40 people at a time, and shelter up to 20 people at night.

Accommodations

For vacationers who want to stay in the recreation center "Golden Fish" in Ulyanovsk, there are two wooden towers with all the amenities inside. Inside the structures there are:

  1. Lounge areas where you can gather in a large company and spend your time sincerely if the weather is bad.
  2. Bedrooms with large beds.
  3. A bathhouse heated with firewood, which can be used at any time.
  4. TV.
  5. For lovers of singing, there is karaoke.
  6. Bathroom with shower and toilet.
  7. A game room for the youngest vacationers with everything necessary for a fun and interesting pastime.

The interior of the room is thought out to the smallest detail, so inside the towers it will be comfortable and cozy for everyone.
