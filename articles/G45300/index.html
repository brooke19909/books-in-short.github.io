Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists

Alanya, located in the southern part of the coast, is one of the warmest and most beautiful resorts in the country. This city is famous for its centuries-old history, unique sights and well-developed tourism business. Many hotels of all categories offer their services here. Gunes Suntime Hotel among the three-star occupies a leading position. Most of the tourists who rested here dream of returning without fail.

Location

Hotel Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 * is located a few steps from the center of Alanya. The capital of the Antalya region is 138 km from here, and Side Museum is 45 km away. The Mediterranean coast is a 3-4-minute quiet walk from the hotel. In the immediate vicinity of the Gunes Suntime Hotel there are numerous clothing stores, shoes, souvenirs, grocery stores, vegetables, ATMs, markets. In addition, quite close to the hotel you can find dozens of bars, cafes, restaurants, discos, nightclubs. Many sights of Alanya, such as Damlatas Cave, Red Tower, ancient fortress, port, guests of Gunes Suntime can take walks, so they are conveniently located in relation to the hotel.



How to get there

The nearest international airport, accepting flights from many cities of Russia, is located in Antalya. It is only 126 km or 2 hours from the Gunes Suntime Hotel (Alanya) along an excellent asphalt highway. Basically, all tours to this hotel provide a comfortable shuttle service. The problem may be that sometimes tourists travel to different hotels with one bus, so travel time is stretched. If a transfer is not provided for the tour, you can order a taxi at the airport yourself or take the shuttle bus. There are a lot of them from Antalya to Alanya, both direct and passing. Travel time is a little over 2 hours. Ticket price - from 10 dollars. Near Alanya, just 40 km or a 30-minute drive, lies the town of Gazipasa. There is also an airport there, but direct flights from Russia there are not operated there, only with a transfer in Istanbul.



Description

Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey) received its first tourists in 1986, but in 2004 it was completely reconstructed. As for a city hotel, Gunes Suntime has a rather large territory (1500 sq. Meters). Here, surrounded by evergreen trees and flowers, stands one six-story building, in which tourists are accommodated. There is an elevator to go up to the desired floor. The comfortable Gunes Suntime lobby has lounge chairs. It works great wireless internet. The hotel reception is open around the clock. Employees help to solve all problems, if any, take things to the left-luggage office, allocate a safe deposit box (paid). Also at the reception you can exchange currency, make a photocopy of documents, book an excursion. There is a free car park in front of the hotel, which is convenient for tourists who rent a car.



Rooms

Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 * has 80 standard rooms. They are small in area: from 16 to 23 square meters. meters. Also vary in size and balconies in the rooms. And on the 6th floor there are none at all. The design of all rooms is similar. The only difference is one double bed or two single beds. The situation in the rooms is modest, no frills, but everything you need to stay in stock and in working condition. Tourists are provided with:

- TV with a couple of channels in Russian;

- air conditioning;

- hair dryer;

- telephone;

- a set of furniture (bedside tables, beds, wardrobe with hangers).

The balconies have plastic tables and chairs. The view from the windows depends on the location of the room. There are those from which the sea and the city are visible, but there are those whose windows overlook the outbuildings.

Outlets in the rooms are ordinary, suitable for chargers and electrical appliances taken from home. The linens at the Gunes Suntime Hotel, like the hotel itself, are not new, but always clean. Change it every 3-4 days. Towels - once every 2-3 days. There is no refrigerator and safe in the rooms.

The bathroom is equipped with a toilet, washbasin and shower. The cabin as such is not provided. It is replaced by a curtain. Shampoo and soap are issued only on the day of arrival, toilet paper is replenished regularly. Clean the rooms every day.

There are no insects such as bugs and cockroaches in the hotel. Mosquitoes are sometimes found, so the funds from them will not hurt.





Gunes Suntime Hotel (ex Gunes House Suntime Hotel) 3 *

Across the street from Gunes Suntime is a subsidiary of Gunes House. In some brochures, it is declared as a four-star, but this is the usual three, only new. They have one owner who does not prevent guests from using the bars, pools and other services of both hotels. Many tourists, arriving at Gunes Suntime, are asked to relocate them to Gunes House, which is done without hindrance. The rooms here are more spacious, the furniture and appliances are newer, the bathroom has shower cabins, but there are no refrigerators either. The rooms are cleaned daily. The quality of cleaning depends on the integrity of the maids. Some make up beds, wash floors, wipe dust, others only take out the trash and change towels. Such a problem exists in the Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 *. Reviews of tourists who came to different maids are different. In some, there are complaints about cleaning.



Nutrition

In brochures, it is sometimes indicated that Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 * has two restaurants and two bars. However, its guests eat at the Gunes House. Breakfast here is not as varied as in the "five", but everyone has enough. The menu includes several types of cereal, eggs (boiled or scrambled eggs), sausages, ham, vegetable salads, milk and milk soups, fruits, pastries, cereals, cheese, tea, coffee, cottage cheese. For lunch and dinner there are always several types of dishes from potatoes, chicken or turkey, rice, pasta, many salads, snacks, there are fish and seafood, hot dishes, sweets, cakes, pastries, seasonal fruits, juices. The bars of both hotels offer soft drinks (fanta, cola, water) and alcoholic drinks (beer, vodka, gin, white and red wine). It is in bottles and barrels, from which you can pour as much as you like. Drinking water is in coolers, you can pour it in containers and take with you to your room. If you believe the reviews of people resting here, in the hotel restaurant no one watches how many people ate what, no one measures portions and does not prohibit taking an additive of both food and drinks. On the one hand, this is wonderful, but on the other hand, if you arrive late in a restaurant, the dishes that most vacationers like may not be enough.



Leisure at the hotel

For relaxation, there is a pool in the Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 *. Photos of tourists demonstrate how spacious and comfortable it is. Gunes House also has a pool. Next to each there is a bar serving alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Sunbeds and umbrellas are free, no towels are provided.

Among the paid services offered by the hotel are Turkish bath (hammam), sauna, massage. There are no sports fields and a gym here, no animations either. Basically, this does not bother anyone, as there are many leisure points near the hotel - bars, restaurants with live music, discos and clubs.

Leisure outside

A huge plus of the Gunes Suntime Hotel in its location. The proximity of dozens of outlets will appeal to shoppers. In the reviews, people write that here you can profitably buy leather products (jackets, coats), shoes, bags, summer clothes, but you need to be able to bargain, since initially sellers inflate prices by about 3 times. Once a week, a large market operates on the street adjacent to the hotel. Closer to the center there is another bazaar where you can also make profitable purchases. The most popular among Russian tourists are the shops "Waikiki", "Rodi Mood", "De Facto", YKM, and the supermarket "Migros".

In addition to shopping, tourists are waiting for exciting excursions. You can buy tours from tour operators or on the street from private traders. Jamal is especially popular. He is a professional in his field, and his tours are 2-3 times cheaper than in a hotel. You can take rafting, jeep safari, night and day boat trips, trips to Israel, Antalya, Side, to waterfalls, an aquapark, etc.



Beach

The free beach is located literally a few tens of meters from the Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 *. Reviews of vacationers in this hotel always note this fact. Sun beds and umbrellas here cost from 2 to 4 lire per day (depending on the season), but you can buy a beach pass at the hotel. Then everything will be cheaper. By itself, this beach is not bad, sandy, with a gentle entry into the water. However, the depth where you can comfortably swim begins far from the coast. Sunset at sea, although gentle, but along the way there is a strip of stones about a meter wide. The water in the sea here is not quite transparent, and after stormy weather, algae float in it.

Many tourists prefer to relax on the beach "Cleopatra". There you can walk in about 30 minutes, or you can drive in 2 lira by dolmushka. On this beach, sand and sea are cleaner, the depth is closer to the shore, the views are more beautiful. Admission to the Cleopatra is free, but for sun loungers and umbrellas you need to pay 4 lira per day.



When is the best time to relax in Gunes Suntime?

Good weather in Alanya is from May to September. Even in April and October, the air temperature during the day holds + 20-25 degrees, and sometimes +30. Only in these months there is more precipitation than in May and September, and after rain it is always somewhat cooler. In autumn, windy days are also often observed. In June, July and August, the air warms up to +35, and the sea near the coast to +27 degrees. Precipitation is rare in these months.

Reviews indicate that relaxing in the Gunes Suntime Hotel is wonderful at the beginning of the season, and in the middle, and at the end. April and May are good because there is no influx of holidaymakers, so you can always choose the best room. The hotel‚Äôs outdoor pool is unheated, but in May you can already swim in it. In the sea, the water is still cool, but for tanning and for excursions, the time is most suitable. In September, and especially in October, there are also enough free rooms and the atmosphere in the hotel is calmer. The sea has not yet had time to cool down, so a beach holiday is quite feasible, only after the rain and on cloudy days it becomes cool.

Summer is a great time for a vacation at the Gunes Suntime. True, there are many people at this time, but the service is no worse. The sea is gentle in the summer, the water is warm, there is almost no wind, there is an abundance of fruit on the market, absolutely all excursion bureaus, car rental offices, bars, restaurants and discos are open in the city. But during peak season, hotel rooms are more expensive, some services and tours in general.

Guest opinions

Many tourists believe that it is fully consistent with the declared category Gunes Suntime Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey). Photos of guests demonstrate the condition of the rooms, the hotel building, its territory. Of the advantages of the hotel in the reviews noted:

- location in the city center;

- proximity to the beach;

- reasonable prices;

- excellent food;

- attentive, caring, friendly staff.

The disadvantages of the hotel, according to people resting here, are as follows:

- for relocation to the best room, you almost always need to pay extra;

- no beach towels ;

- there is practically nothing for children;

- lack of refrigerators in the rooms.

This hotel is ideal for young people, for couples, for relaxing with friends. The elderly will also like it, as the sea, shopping, and sights are all nearby.
