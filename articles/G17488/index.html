Hotel Kata Beach SP House 3 (Phuket): description, photos and reviews of tourists

A good budget hotel Kata Beach SP House 3 * is located on the island of Phuket, on the shores of the Andaman Sea, between the beaches of Kata and Karon. The distance to the airport is about fifty kilometers. By car to the island's capital - Phuket Town - drive about half an hour. For those who love these places, this hotel is a great base for exploring the beautiful surroundings. As for the price for it, it significantly covers some of the inconveniences inherent in Thai "treshki" - in particular, the fact that this hotel is on a rock, and you have to go uphill from the beach. If you want to relax with great comfort, then you can consider a hotel of a similar category - Beach Boutique House.
