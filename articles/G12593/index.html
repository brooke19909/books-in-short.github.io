How to make felt doll houses with your own hands?

Doll houses made of felt have recently become very popular. Many mothers are wondering how to make such a toy for their daughter. You can come up with a lot of options for houses, they will functionally differ from each other, depending on the purpose.

The principles of manufacturing a toy felt house

Volumetric house must keep its shape. Therefore, it must be layered.

  • The outer side is made of felt. It is decorated with decorative elements - windows, shutters, a door, flowers, a fence - all that you would like for the craft to look like a real house or a castle of a princess.
  • The inner part can be made of a thin sheet of foam rubber and a dense plastic film (covers are made from it for the sale of bedspreads, blankets and pillows). The film will protect the house from moisture, foam will be a material that helps to keep in shape.
  • The interior of the toy house can be very different! It is most convenient for a home-made toy to have one room with access to the courtyard. Although, if desired, such an craft can be made two or three-tier. Then there can be several rooms in the house.




It is impossible not to mention how to make a floor. The house must be reliable, even if it is a toy, and even felt. Therefore, when sewing the frame of the house in the cavity of the floor, above the film and foam rubber, you should lay a thick sheet of cardboard - for example, from a packing box.

How to furnish a house and how to close it?

Since felt toy houses are a three-dimensional construction, this allows the use of three-dimensional furnishings as well. They can also be made from fabric or improvised materials, or you can use purchased toy furniture of a suitable size.

There are several ways to assemble a dollhouse out of felt:

  • sew two detachable zippers on the side seams on the front side;
  • use valves with Velcro (with Velcro tape);
  • connect the parts of the house with decorative large buttons and loops.

In any case, the house must be securely closed so as not to lose things during the transfer.
