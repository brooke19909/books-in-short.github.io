Entertainment in Sevastopol: overview, features and reviews

Sevastopol is the largest city on the Crimean peninsula. This is a military, commercial and fishing port, a cultural and scientific center. Hero City, withstood the brutal blows of several wars.

Sevastopol invariably attracts tourists coming to the Crimea who want to get acquainted with the sights of the city. What entertainment in Sevastopol may interest tourists? For many, this is an important issue.

Currently, for tourists seeking entertainment in Sevastopol, both cultural and beach events are offered. For children, these are attractions, for youth, clubs, for pensioners, centers of culture and recreation. It also offers relaxation in karaoke, riding bicycles, horses, ATVs, jeeps, diving in the sea.

Aquarium

A wonderful entertainment in Sevastopol for children and their parents will be a visit to the Sevastopol Aquarium. A lot of fish from small bright clowns to large black and white sturgeons invariably captivate visitors, as they say in their reviews. In addition to the living inhabitants of the aquarium, there are frightening stuffed animals of various sharks, a crocodile (which can be mistaken for a sleeping one).

The Sevastopol Aquarium was opened at the end of the XIX century. Numerous stands in the halls tell about its history.

Count's Quay and Monument to the Sunken Ships

On the main square of the city - Nakhimov Square - is the famous Count's Quay, often depicted on calendars and postcards, which is an architectural and historical monument.

Not far from the Count's wharf there is a monument to the sunken ships - the emblem of the city of Sevastopol.

All visiting tourists are eager to visit these attractions in order to admire them and take beautiful photographs.

Entertainment for adults in Sevastopol

Any adult tourist is interested in visiting the Inkerman vintage wine factory . The length of the wine galleries is 55,000 square meters. Specialists tell visitors about the history of the plant and share the secrets of winemaking. At the end of the tour, a wine tasting is provided. Those who are going on an excursion to the factory should be aware that the temperature there is not more than 14 degrees Celsius.

Windsurfing is available at Omega Bay. It is absolutely safe! Vacationers should not give up this entertainment, even if they have never done this before. Beginners are trained by experienced instructors.

The goal of this game is to defeat the opponent‚Äôs goal, of course, at a time allotted for this purpose. The victory goes to the team that manages to score more goals.

A distinctive feature of bumperball is that in this game you need to foul. You can bring your opponent to the ground, push, do not let return to the field.
