Where to go in Gelendzhik in summer or winter?

Nice, sweet, cozy, hospitable - such epithets are awarded to Gelendzhik by those who have visited this small seaside town at least once. How is it different from other resorts? Despite the small size of the city, there is no need to think about where to go. In Gelendzhik at any time of the year there is something to have fun.

Summer Gelendzhik

Gelendzhik all year round

This small town is one of the few resorts where even in winter you do not have to think about where to go. In Gelendzhik at any time of the year work:

  • The Wax Museum.
  • Oceanarium. Still very young, he leaves a strong impression. Especially children like traveling along the ocean waves.
  • Dolphinarium. The program in it is quite standard, but there is one undeniable advantage: it is closed, so in extreme heat (and it is not uncommon here) and in bad weather, visitors feel very comfortable.
