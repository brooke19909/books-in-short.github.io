What are devices for?

VTB24 ATM with cash-in function is a device through which funds will be deposited to the card account. This method is used because of the following advantages:

ATM Terms of Use

VTB24 ATM with cash-in function is used according to the following instructions:

  • You need to insert a card.
  • Enter the pin code.
  • In the menu, select "Cash deposit".
  • Insert bills.

Bill acceptors can accept one bill or a bundle. After making the bills are checked. If they do not meet the desired quality, they are returned. The screen will display information about the amount. It is necessary to check everything, and then confirm the operation. At the end, a receipt is issued.

The reviews of many people show that ATMs are convenient to use. You can use them at any time of the day. You can transfer funds to cards, bank accounts and for services. The advantages include small fees for performing financial transactions.

ATM Recommendations

Using around-the-clock devices is very risky. In the dark, you need to use video surveillance devices indoors. In crowded areas, ATMs should also not be used. After all, this leads to the risk of the disclosure of personal information.





It is necessary to prepare money in advance, straighten it, because only such money is accepted by the device. The receipt of the operation must be kept until the money is transferred to the account. If there are difficulties with the operation of the ATM or failures, then you need to contact the bank. Any problems can be solved at the request of the client.
