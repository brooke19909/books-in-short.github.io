Hotel Patong Holiday 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): overview, description and reviews

All tourists planning a vacation in the cold season assume that there are not many destinations at their disposal. If you prefer a beach holiday, and all you need is the sea, a sandy beach and stunning scenery, you should not even think. Buy trips to Thailand. This resort has long been chosen by tourists from different parts of the globe. Rest here is relatively inexpensive, if you purchase a ticket in advance, you can save some money. Yes, Thailand is difficult to compare with fashionable resorts in terms of the level of service offered and the quality of relaxation, but for a winter holiday this is a great option. All lovers of nature who want to spend the days of their vacation in a paradise, to fully enjoy the color and exotic of the resort, will probably prefer not the mainland, but Phuket. Yes, in this case the flight duration will increase, the cost of rest will increase slightly, but in return each tourist will receive snow-white sandy beaches, crystal clear sea water and a rich excursion program to the most famous islands and provinces.





Do not forget that Thailand is an exotic country. When planning your trip, you need to more carefully approach the choice of hotel. Phuket is no exception. We strongly recommend that you get acquainted with the reviews of tourists, because it is they who help to objectively evaluate each potential option. Hotel Patong Holiday 3 * (Phuket) is a fairly popular option. It remains only to understand what he can offer potential vacationers. This information is invaluable for modern tourists who are aimed at a quality and full of positive rest.

About hotel

To begin with, Patong Holiday 3 * (Phuket) is a fairly large and well-known city hotel in Phuket, which has a good rating. It occupies the second coastline, respectively, the beach and the sea are from it at a distance of up to 1000 meters. The airport is only 38 km away, so travel time will be short.





Location

The hotel is located in the center of the island's resort life, within walking distance from the most popular and famous beach - Patong Beach. This is the favorite region of modern tourists. Everyone who rested here is looking for hotels in this region for their second trip. Tourists are primarily attracted by the proximity to all infrastructure. Only 5-7 minutes walk from Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * (Thailand, Phuket, Patong) is the largest shopping and entertainment center of the resort Jungceylon.

All lovers of classic Thai boxing just 300 meters from the hotel will find a good sports stadium, where regular fights are held. Moreover, all interested tourists can receive several lessons from recognized masters. Bangla's famous nightlife street is just a 10-minute walk away. If you choose Tuana Patong Holiday 3 * hotel (Phuket) for your stay, you will definitely be in the know about all the events of tourist and resort life, get access to all infrastructure facilities.

What comfort in the rooms can the hotel Patong Holiday 3 * (Phuket) offer? Reviews of tourists are very mixed. Room service

Now for the room service. Even the most beautiful, modern and stylish interior can spoil the dirt in the room, a constant puddle on the bathroom floor, dirty linens and insects - all this is Patong Holiday 3 * hotel (Phuket). Quite a lot of tourists complain about the disgusting cleaning, even after the staff visit the room has a lot of sand and dust. Towels and bed linen are changed several times per week stay. That is why at least bath towels are better to take with you. If you can clean yourself, armed with a broom to remove sand, wipe the plumbing and furniture, and in principle are not ready for the highest quality room service - these shortcomings will not spoil your vacation.

Beach

Only 800 meters from the Patong Holiday 3 * hotel (Phuket) is the most famous beach resort - Patong. There are so many vacationers here in the season that finding a free sunbed becomes problematic. This is the center of spa life. During the day, the beach is not much different from the rest, perhaps only in the presence of a pile of garbage left by tourists, but in the evening it turns into a platform for bright and enchanting show programs. At the reception of the Patong Holiday 3 * hotel (Phuket), you can take beach towels - they are required to be returned. On the beach you can rent a sunbed and an umbrella, there are points for renting sports and water equipment. The sand is small, fairly clean, the sea is not as clean as on the paradise islands, but definitely more pleasant than in Pattaya. All vacationers who want to find a more secluded and quiet place to stay can take a walk along the coast - there are many beautiful places with gorgeous views.
