Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya / Obakoy): description, reviews of tourists

Turkey pleases Russians with its hot climate and the opportunity to relax inexpensively. And if you add to this, for example, a developed hotel infrastructure, exciting excursions, recreational activities, tolerance of local residents to our tourists, you can understand why the number of our compatriots in the resorts of this country is increasing every year.



general information

Elderly, young, and even parents with infants come to Turkey. Everyone wants to relax as comfortably as possible, choosing for this hotels that match their capabilities. Almost every tourist coming from here tells about their bright and unforgettable impressions, about the gentle sun and warm sea, about interesting excursions and, of course, about oriental hospitality, honed by millennia. In Turkey, washed by the seas from four sides, you can really have a great vacation. Its coastal resorts offer a wealth of entertainment. Moreover, Turkey is the leader in terms of attendance by tourists, because here you can get quality service, delicious food, the opportunity to see interesting architectural monuments at a low cost. It is difficult to unequivocally name the most popular resort in this country. Among the most popular resorts - Antalya, Kemer, Marmaris, Bodrum, Dalaman, Side, Alanya, etc., many of them have a measured life in the afternoon, and in the evening a real riot of entertainment begins.

Not only in our country, but throughout the world, trips to Turkey are quite in demand. The variety of prices in the huge list of modern hotels allows travelers to choose the most convenient option. In the list of quite popular hotels to date, you can call Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 *. Alanya - the resort in which this hotel complex is located has long been chosen by the Russians. It is positioned by tour operators as a hotel with a high level of service and a highly developed infrastructure. This can be confirmed by those who have already managed to visit the Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 * (Alanya). Reviews of tourists say that this hotel is suitable primarily for families.



Description

This hotel complex, consisting of several separate storey buildings, was built in the year 2000. The last major renovation in it was carried out in 2004. The territory on which Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 * stands is five thousand eight hundred square meters of parkland. The hotel is located one and a half kilometers from the center of the Turkish resort of Alanya in the small village of Obakey. Nearby there are inexpensive cafes where you can taste real black coffee and excellent baklava, many shops, there is a market where you can inexpensively buy fresh fruits and fish. A daily bus leaves for Alanya every morning in the morning. Alanya Incekuma resort area is a five-minute walk away.

The territory of Krizantem Beach Hotel (4 *, Turkey), although relatively small, however, it is always clean. Everything is thought out to the smallest detail, everywhere lush vegetation, well-groomed lawns, benches, signs with basic infrastructures. The hotel has its own compact parking. Free places on it must be reserved. The nearest airport is one hundred and thirty-two kilometers. Those who wish are provided with a transfer service carried out on large comfortable buses.

Infrastructure

Being a four-star, Krizantem Beach Hotel (4 *, Turkey, Incekum, Alanya) offers a very extensive list of services. In its administrative building for vacationers there is a hairdresser and a medical office, a lobby, and there is a porter. Laundry and dry cleaning services are located in a separate unit. Between floors in the buildings, high-speed elevators work. There is a shop and a souvenir shop on site. In the lobby of the main building there are automatic machines for water and juice, a coffee machine, as well as an ATM. For those who, if necessary, combine vacation with work, the hotel provides a business center with direct dial telephone and fax.



The registration desk of Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 * is open around the clock. Among the workers there is a Russian-speaking girl. According to tourists, registrars very quickly check in at Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews of vacationers indicate that they had to wait a maximum of twenty minutes. In the same place, at the reception desk, people entering the house are given a printout of all paid and free services that are provided at this hotel. There is also a depository safe, as well as a left-luggage office. Those who wish can use the photo service, as well as book tickets in any direction.

Beach

The distance from Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) to the sea is about fifty meters. Tourists reach the beach by crossing the motorway separating the hotel from the bathing area through a specially equipped underpass. The territory is own. Judging by the reviews, the beach is clean. It has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay, including a bar. Trestle beds and umbrellas are issued free of charge. Sunset at sea is a little difficult, there are stones, so it is important to have special slippers for swimming.

Services for children

Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 * offers its little vacationers a playground and a kids club for kids from five to twelve years old. Parents in the restaurant for the smallest visitors are provided with high chairs upon request. There is a children's tray with dishes from a special menu. For convenience, parents can also order a paid babysitting service. The clean and shallow pool provided for small ones is always under the supervision of a special worker. There is also a low slide.



Pools

On a fully thought out and well-equipped territory, Krizantem Beach Hotel 4 * has three outdoor pools for adults and two shallow ones for children. Around them are many sun loungers with umbrellas, for which you do not need to pay. The largest pool has a water slide. There is also a bar. Alcohol is given only on tables. For those who wish, morning aerobics classes are held.
