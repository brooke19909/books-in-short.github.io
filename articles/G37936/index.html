Canon EF 135 mm f / 2L USM lens for realizing unique photography ideas

Any professional and semi-professional, as well as often amateur photography with a modern SLR camera is carried out using good lenses. For Canon cameras, it is recommended that you purchase lenses from the same company. The quality of shooting depends on many factors. This article is dedicated to the Canon EF 135mm f / 2L USM lens, which has established itself among amateur photographers.

General lens specifications

A good photographer will pick up not so much high-quality equipment as exactly the one that suits him. The search will help the characteristics. Of course, the numbers indicated in the parameters do not mean anything to the novice and amateur. Especially for such people all points will be described as accessible as possible. The Canon EF 135 lens has the following features:

  • The type of lens is a telephoto lens. It is suitable for shooting any objects that are at a great distance from the photographer. For example, an air show, a stadium, a concert. It should be noted that this lens is fixed, that is, you cannot change the focal length. Therefore, in order for the object to get into the frame as needed, you will have to run in search of a good place.
  • Focal distance - 135 mm. It is these figures that indicate how far you need to stand and what objects it is advisable to photograph. The larger the focal length, the stronger the magnification of the subject in the camera. Televisions with a focal length of about 400 mm can even photograph craters on the moon. Everything below 100-120 mm does not apply to telephoto lenses (except for zoom lenses).
  • Aperture - F2. This option is very important for shooting, especially indoors and in bad weather. What is this parameter? Aperture. It will seem to beginners that the more F, the better. This is actually a mistake. If the working aperture of the lens is more than 2, then the frames will be of poor quality most often, you will have to use an additional flash. For taking pictures in fine weather, lenses with F 3.5-4 are also suitable. The Canon EF 135mm f / 2L USM lens makes it possible to shoot anywhere, because its aperture allows it.
  • Mount - Canon EF. If you buy only Canon lenses, then mounting is not important. But when studying and choosing a lens of other brands (Sigma, Tamron, Zeiss and others), you will have to look carefully at which camera is designed. There are exceptions for Canon, when in the "carcass" is a mount type EF-M.
  • Autofocus - available. This is a very important parameter. Without it, even professionals will not be able to photograph normally. Lack of autofocus can be torture. The techie will immediately recall the theory of probability and understand that trying to adjust the focus is the same as looking for a needle in a stack of dried grass.
