Water park "Dreamland" in Minsk: prices and reviews

When the summer is outside, the best option for outdoor activities is to visit water entertainment complexes where you can have fun with your whole family, celebrate a special occasion or just meet with friends.

One of such places is the Dreamland water park in Minsk. The opening, which was planned two years ago, back in 2013, took place on July 17, 2015. The delay was due to complex hydro- and geological conditions, the scale of the project, as well as complex technical processes. However, despite all the difficulties, the entertainment complex has launched its work and is ready to simultaneously accept up to 3 thousand visitors.





It is the largest open amusement park in Europe and covers an area equal to 9 hectares of land. It is a worthy analogue of the latest water complexes located abroad.

Location

Waterpark "Dreamland" is located on the picturesque bank of the Svisloch River, near Lake Komsomolskoye. The exact address of the entertainment complex is 80 Orlovskaya Street. There is a convenient transport interchange, so getting to the water park is easy.

Entertainment

For those who like outdoor activities, the Dreamland water park offers a lot of entertainment, including a dozen water slides and pools, including wave and children, extreme stationary attractions - a trampoline and a slide (it is descent on a special rug), as well as a centifuge and a moon walker . For young visitors there is a real expanse. On the territory of the water park there is a children's town with many mini-labyrinths and familiar slides, an inflatable trampoline where you can jump into plenty, an obstacle course, a train and a race track beloved by many. And adults can take their souls on special machines equipped with an electric drive and a laser sight. Here you can deploy a real battle. These and many other entertainments are offered by Dreamland.





Buy an entrance ticket and you will be allowed to use all the rides. For an additional fee, you can play table tennis, visit the horror castle, ride a water ball, a bicycle or an electric car, as well as an ATV. Cafe and parking





After outdoor activities, of course, a good appetite appears. You can quench your thirst and hunger in the recreation area, where six cafes with a varied menu and a wide range of products are open for visitors, as well as a restaurant where you can order a set lunch. For visitors arriving by car, there is a spacious parking lot for up to two hundred cars.

Safety in the water park

Having chosen the Dreamland water park for outdoor activities, you can not worry about the safety of yourself and your children. At least thirty instructors are constantly on the shift in the territory, who, if necessary, can immediately provide first aid. The complex also operates under the control of a separate security service, which monitors the order and comfortable relaxation of all visitors. All areas of the water park are under 24-hour video surveillance. And near each of the cash desks there are telephones by which you can turn to a health worker for help. If necessary, pre-medical procedures are performed here.





For these purposes, a medical center has been organized on the territory where first aid is provided. In order to avoid serious injuries and damage, you should adhere to the rules of behavior when descending on attractions, being in the pool and in the rest of the entertainment complex. It is forbidden to stay in the water park while intoxicated. Young children should be under the personal supervision of their parents. Compliance with safety precautions will help you to get extremely positive emotions and impressions from relaxing in an amazing place called Dreamland.

Water park in Minsk: ticket prices and timetable

The schedule of the entertainment complex is 11: 00-20: 00, with the exception of Monday and Tuesday. Separately operates from 10:00 to 22:00 without days off, the water park "Dreamland" in Minsk. Prices are also divided depending on whether you choose a comprehensive visit or only one direction. For example, an adult admission ticket will cost around 200 thousand Belarusian rubles (about 770 Russian rubles). Children whose age does not exceed three years pass free of charge. For those older than 4 and younger than 13, the cost of the ticket is from 100 to 130 thousand Belarusian rubles (390-500 Russian rubles). Certain categories of people can use the entrance at a reduced price. These include retirees, students, and large families. This opportunity is provided only if there are documents confirming the special status.





It is also available to rent a left-luggage office, the cost of which is 50 thousand Belarusian rubles (approximately 170 Russian rubles). The complex accepts applications for group visits, which should be submitted three days before the expected date. In this case, discounts apply for entry tickets. On weekends, payment for entrance to the water park is different. In "Dreamland" prices then are as follows: an adult ticket will cost from 200 to 300 thousand Belarusian rubles (770-1300 Russian rubles), for preferential categories - 150-270 thousand (420-1200 Russian rubles), children up to 13 years - 150-220 (420-860 Russian rubles), up to 17 - 180-250 (690-960 Russian rubles). In case of loss of the bracelet, it will be necessary to pay an amount equal to 50 thousand rubles. (190 Russian rubles). All details can be found by calling 8 (029) 115-01-76.
