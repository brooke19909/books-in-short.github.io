California Garden 3 * (Spain, Costa Daurada, Salou): hotel infrastructure, room description, service, reviews

In a restrained Europe, not every place is to the liking of a wide Russian nature. Most of our compatriots prefer to relax in hot resorts, where not only a great sea and clean beaches, but life is in full swing all day. One such place is Spain. It is located in the south-west of Europe and is considered by its climatic conditions one of the warmest and most favorable countries. Among Russians, the popularity of Spanish resorts is incredibly high. And this is not surprising, because there is a high-class European service, sandy beaches, many of which are marked with the Blue Flag for cleanliness and comfort, extensive excursion programs - not every tourist country has such an impressive list of advantages.

general information

Holidays in Spain are suitable for everyone: families with children come here, as well as large companies, and romantic couples. Almost every resort in this country boasts dozens or even hundreds of excellent beach areas. One of these tourist centers is the Costa Dorada. Spain annually receives nearly one hundred million tourists. Almost a quarter of them prefer to relax in the south of Catalonia.

This is where this world-famous resort is located - Costa Dorada (Spain). The southern part of Catalonia is known for a rather long tourist season. Mountains surrounding the Costa Dorada protect the resort from winds blowing from the mainland. In summer, the temperature here does not rise above +34 ‚Å∞. Costa Dorada has many excellent beaches with clean fine sand and a convenient gentle entry into the water. Translated from Spanish, the name of the resort means "Gold Coast".

Salou

Costa Dorada stretches one hundred and forty kilometers west of Barcelona. One of the reasons why whole families come here with pleasure is the largest amusement park in Southern Europe called Port Aventura. Young people are attracted to numerous clubs and discos, and history buffs are given the opportunity to get acquainted with the monuments of the Middle Ages and the Roman era. The tourist capital of the Costa Daurada is considered the province of Salou, located two hours from Barcelona. The wide and clean beaches of this resort stretch for eight kilometers from north to south, from Cape La Pineda to the city of Cambrils. Along the coast, beautiful promenade walkways are equipped. There are also numerous shops and bars, restaurants, cafes. The light and music fountain in Salou is considered the most beautiful on the entire coast.

Most hotels in this Spanish resort town are located on the first line of the sea. They suit any category of vacationers. One of the relatively inexpensive hotels in Salou is considered California Garden 3 *.
