Hotel Aloha Resort 3 * (Thailand / Koh Samui): description, reviews

The coast of the Thai island of Koh Samui is very popular among tourists. Travelers from all over the world come here in search of a bright beach holiday and an unforgettable experience. Of course, there are a huge number of hotels. One of the popular holiday destinations is the Aloha Resort 3 * hotel.

Of course, before the trip, every tourist tries to find the maximum amount of additional information. Is the hotel located far from the sea? What service can you count on? Is there a good spa nearby? Is this place suitable for families?

Where to look for a hotel? Location and General Information





Of course, the location of the hotel can be both an advantage and a disadvantage. Aloha Resort is located in the east of Koh Samui, about 20 km from Chaweng. The hotel was built almost on the seashore, namely in the southern part of the world famous Lamai beach.

The hotel complex occupies a fairly large territory. It consists of a three-story main building, small cozy bungalows and several one-story buildings. Naturally, there is a spacious courtyard, a lush garden that pleases with greenery all year round, pools, terraces, in short, everything for a pleasant pastime.

Hotel Aloha Resort 3 * (Samui): description and photos of rooms





Regardless of which category category you get, you can be sure of its comfort. All rooms are spacious with large windows (some rooms also have balconies). They are decorated not brightly, but with taste.

Of course, the room has a set of necessary furniture and household appliances. Air conditioning allows you to create suitable temperature conditions in the room, which is a must in hot periods. There is wireless Internet access, and on TV you can watch satellite channels - you will not find yourself divorced from civilization.

Guests can use the small safe for free, which is very convenient. The minibar is replenished for free only with water, but for all other drinks you need to pay extra. By the way, room service is available around the clock. There is also an electric kettle. Tea and coffee making facilities are included.

Of course, rest is impossible without hygiene procedures. The room adjoins a spacious bathroom, where there is either a shower or bathtub (depending on the category). Also, guests can rely on a constant supply of water, daily change of towels and replenishment of toiletries. By the way, the bathroom has a hairdryer. They clean here often and efficiently - the rooms are neat, fresh and clean.

Dining at the resort: restaurants and bars





Of course, the issue of nutrition is important for almost every traveler. It‚Äôs worth saying right away that the hotel has two pretty decent restaurants where you can eat tasty and inexpensive. For example, the colorful Mai Tai Cafe is considered to be popular among guests - here you can order delicacies of real Thai cuisine, although the menu, of course, also contains more familiar continental dishes. It also serves seafood barbecue - it is definitely worth a try.

Approximately the same menu (including grilled seafood) is offered at the Captains' bar-restaurant. This is a wonderful place located almost by the sea - during lunch or dinner you can enjoy not only aromatic food, but also breathtaking views of the sea.

Water activities and beach vacations





Of course, they come to Samui in search of entertainment on the seashore. As already mentioned, the hotel was built almost on the Lamai Beach, you can get to it in five minutes, which is very convenient.

This is a fairly popular coast of the island, and therefore you can not count on silence and loneliness. On the other hand, on the shore you can easily find a comfortable sun lounger with an umbrella, rent a large clean towel or even have a snack at a local cafe, enjoying sea views.

Lamai beach is public, and therefore there are always many tourists. Fun competitions, water rides, foam parties and discos in the evenings - everyone has fun here, regardless of age and nationality. Naturally, tourists can canoe and catamarans, take boat trips, windsurf and parasailing.

Do tourists offer an additional service?





For a truly comfortable stay, modern tourists sometimes need some additional services. What does the hotel Aloha Resort 3 * offer? In fact, this place is perfectly adapted to give the guest everything necessary for an unclouded vacation. For example, laundry, dry cleaning (for an additional fee) is constantly working here, there is even an atelier where the master quickly fixes clothes or adjusts to the figure.

Guests can use the administrator safe. By the way, on the territory of the town, as well as in the hotel itself, you can easily and quickly rent a car, bike or motorcycle. In fact, most tourists use rented transport, as it is not too expensive, but it greatly simplifies travel around the area, saving time and getting to know the most striking sights of the island. The hotel also offers free parking spaces.

How to have fun on site?





Samui is an island of sun, beaches and fun. Of course, almost all tourists go on excursions during their vacations, have a good time on the beach, shop and enjoy the delights of local cuisine. But is there anything to do on the territory of the hotel itself?

Of course, here you can find something to do. It features a large outdoor pool with a terrace, with sun loungers in even rows. You can have a good time playing billiards. There is also a volleyball court. The hotel also offers a spa center where travelers can relax, improve their body and improve their well-being. It offers a real Thai massage, which is worth trying at least once in a lifetime.

Are there any conditions for hanging out?

Many couples travel exclusively with children. Of course, for a comfortable stay on site with a child, some additional conditions are required. What does Aloha Resort 3 * offer?

In fact, families with children of different ages often stay here. As for amenities, parents can count on an extra bed, which will be immediately delivered to the room. In local restaurants you can always find dishes that will appeal to the child. And in the courtyard there is a special children's pool - it is spacious, but shallow, and the water is always well warmed up. If you wish or need, you can leave your child with a professional nanny who will take care of your child with pleasure for a relatively small fee.

Hotel complex Aloha Resort 3 *: reviews





It's no secret that a lot of relevant and useful facts can be obtained by talking a little with people who have already managed to visit this or that hotel. The hotel complex Aloha Resort 3 * is popular with travelers - mostly positive reviews are left about it.

The hotel grounds are beautiful and the rooms are comfortable, clean and spacious. The hotel restaurant serves excellent food, and the staff is always friendly towards the guests (by the way, almost all the staff speak good English). An important advantage is the presence in the immediate vicinity of the most popular beach, where you can find entertainment for every taste. The hotel is ideal for almost any type of vacation.
