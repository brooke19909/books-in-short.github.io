Hotel Courtyard Marriott (Kazan)

The city of Kazan, the pearl of the Republic of Tatarstan, is considered one of the most beautiful cities in the Russian Federation. Thousands of tourists come here from different countries and corners of Russia every year. Kazan attracts tourists not only for its architecture and untouched nature: the capital of Tatarstan has a rich history, passed down from generation to generation. The city is famous for the Kazan Kremlin and the Millennium Square. But the residents consider its main advantage the peaceful neighborhood of two religions: Christianity and Islam.

Tourists who are going to visit this magnificent city should think about accommodation. One of the most chic and sought-after hotels in Kazan is the Courtyard Marriott Kazan Kremlin Hotel. It is about her that will be discussed in this article.

Hotel Description

Courtyard Marriott is a business-class hotel that is part of the world-famous Marriott International hotel chain. That is why the hotel provides service in accordance with established international standards. Despite the fact that the hotel was opened in 2010, today Courtyard by Marriott Kazan is on the list of the best hotels in Kazan.

Hotel location





Hotel "Marriott Kazan" has a very good location in close proximity to the main attractions of the city. Despite the fact that the hotel is located in the center, the noise and polluted air of the guests are not disturbing. The hotel project was created in such a way that there were as few roads as possible and, consequently, noise. Five minutes from the hotel are the Kazan Kremlin, Millennium Square and the legendary Bauman Street.

The Courtyard by Marriott is located very close to the Orthodox church and the Muslim mosque. Famous places such as the Tatar Academic State Opera and Ballet Theater, the Museum of Fine Arts and much more are just 250 meters from the hotel.

Hotel address

Hotel "Marriott Kazan" is located at Karl Marx Street, 6. The nearest metro station is the Kremlin. The station is located just 350 meters from the hotel. The hotel itself is located a 25-minute drive from the Kazan airport of the same name.

Accommodation Service Contacts

Phone of the accommodation service: 567-4000.

The Courtyard Marriott Kazan Kremlin Hotel has its own website.

Number of rooms





The hotel has 150 beautiful spacious rooms of various categories:

  • "Deluxe" - one hundred thirty rooms.
  • Deluxe (overlooking the Kazan Kremlin) - twelve rooms.
  • "Suite" - eight rooms.

The feature of these rooms is the presence of ergonomic work areas and modern LCD panels, large comfortable beds, comfortable working areas and an air conditioning system installed in each room.

Restaurants





On the roof of the building there is a cafe with an outdoor terrace, from where guests can enjoy views of the capital of Tatarstan (including the Kazan Kremlin). The cafe offers traditional Italian cuisine: a variety of pastas, pizzas, lasagna and much more. Exotic drinks are also available here. And on the 1st floor of the hotel there is a grill restaurant called "Main Hall", decorated in a Mediterranean style. It serves mainly international diet dishes. In the hotel lobby there is a large space where you can hold business meetings or other events. In addition, the lobby bar serves breakfast according to the generally accepted global buffet system. The Courtyard Marriott Kazan Hotel also has its own conference room, which contains soft armchairs, sofas and chairs. The capacity of the conference room called "Musa Jalil" is from fifteen to one hundred people.

Entertainment for guests

Hotel "Marriott Kazan", reviews of which are mostly positive, offers its guests such additional services as a gym with a treadmill and other cardio machines, a sauna, a solarium, table tennis, volleyball and even skiing!

Not far from the hotel is located the famous all over Russia Kazan water park "Riviera", a large number of excellent restaurants and a circus!





Important information: what you need to know at check-in?

1. Each guest must have an identity document (passport).

2. If there are children, parents must provide their birth certificates.

3. Check-in takes place at 15:00 in the afternoon.

4. Check-out takes place no later than 12:00 pm.

5. For children under the age of eight, the hotel provides free accommodation.

6. For children under the age of two, the hotel provides a free cot.

7. Extra bed (extra bed) is provided for adults or children over the age of eight for an additional fee, which is 1000 rubles per night.

8. The room cannot accommodate more than one extra bed.
