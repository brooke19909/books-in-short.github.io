Khimki hotels, Moscow: review, description, rating and reviews

Khimki are located in the Moscow region. It is an original and quiet city. Increasingly, there is information that they want to join it in the capital. But time will tell. One can say for sure that in terms of industrial enterprises and way of life, it is not inferior to Moscow. There have always been many different plantings: squares, parks. Not so long ago, flower beds appeared on the sidewalk in the city. In recent years, new fountains have been erected here one after another. There are also cozy and comfortable places for relaxation, designed for visitors to the city of Khimki.



Hotels, hotels in their development also do not lag behind - in this place new establishments often open, as well as old ones are reconstructed. Therefore, there is enough space for everyone.

1. Midland Hotel Sheremetyevo

Arriving in Khimki, economy class hotels are easy to find. Among them, Midland Sheremetyevo is worth highlighting. It is a modern hotel located near the Sheremetyevo airport. Judging by the reviews, the hotel has 85 comfortable rooms.



In addition to accommodation, the hotel will be happy to offer guests themed nights. Here for businessmen there are conference rooms equipped with everything necessary for fruitful work.

Cost: from 1000 rubles / person.

2. Hotel "Olympian"

This hotel consists of 8 eight-story buildings. Moscow (Khimki, to be more precise) offers this complex to accommodate guests for all kinds of celebrations and business events, as well as for living. The hotel is conveniently located - remoteness from major highways ensures peace and quiet on the banks of the Klyazma River, while the establishment is located near the airport.

The complex has a children's playground for games, conference rooms, restaurants. Judging by the reviews, the territory has its own paintball club. All rooms are equipped with everything you need from amenities, each has a balcony.

Cost: from 2000 rubles / person.

3. Hotel "Vintage"

Considering the hotels of Khimki, it is necessary to highlight the hotel "Vintage". It is located in a mansion of the XIX century. The hotel offers a free shuttle service to or from Sheremetyevo Airport and the Internet. Just a mile and a half from here is a large shopping and entertainment complex. Transport stops are located nearby, therefore, it will not be difficult to get to Moscow. The hotel offers accommodation in rooms of various categories, equipped with a TV, a private bathroom and other necessary amenities. All rooms are also serviced.



It offers guests a tour desk, which will prompt the most interesting sights of Moscow. There is also a 24-hour front desk. Reviews about the restaurant suggest that pets are allowed in the rooms at no extra charge. The hotel is 5 kilometers from Sheremetyevo.

Cost: from 2700 rubles / person.

4. Hotel "Mon Plaisir"

Mon Plaisir is located near Sheremetyevo Airport. At this hotel in Khimki , accommodation prices are not the lowest - from 2900 rubles per day. It offers guests 19 rooms (single and double). All of them are exquisitely decorated and equipped with everything that is required for a good rest. Free internet is available on site, therefore guests will always be in touch with loved ones.

Shuttle service and laundry are available here. Guests can leave their own car in the parking lot. Judging by the reviews, the attentive and hospitable staff is happy with each of its guests, helps with pleasure in resolving any issues that arise, in addition, tries to organize their stay at the highest level.

Cost: from 2900 rubles / person.

5. Hotel Concept Hotel

Concept Hotel is located in Khimki. This will allow you to quickly come here from the Belorussky Train Station, Sheremetyevo Airport. Nearby is the Crocus Expo Exhibition Center, as well as the center of the capital. It should be noted that a healthy sleep of guests is the concern of this Khimki hotel! The rooms have custom-made mattresses, blankets and pillows made of natural materials, as well as blackout curtains.



The rooms have all the necessary amenities. Each of them is decorated in rich, vibrant colors. Reviews about the institution suggest that for guests who come here on a business trip, the following services are provided: translation, printing of documents, banking services, consulting and legal services. The territory of the institution is under video surveillance, there is a free parking lot.

Cost: from 2800 rubles / person.

6. Business Hotel Eden

Eden is a hotel consisting of eleven rooms equipped to the highest standards. Cabinet furniture, modern comfortable beds, calm colors pleasant for perception in each room have a rest and business mood. Here 1-, 2- and 4-seater accommodation is possible - it is very convenient for business people, as well as for tourists, as evidenced by numerous reviews.

All rooms at this Khimki hotel have a bathroom equipped with a shower. In addition, there is its own restaurant, where you can have lunch and rent it for various celebrations. The price includes breakfast, which will be very convenient for all guests of this hotel.

Cost: from 3500 rubles / person.

7. Hotel North Star

This hotel is located on Leningradskoye Shosse in a convenient location and always welcomes every guest. The hotel has 50 rooms of different categories, which in their reviews are noted with pleasure by people who love a large selection.



Sheremetyevo Airport is located 12 kilometers from here. Guests of this Khimki hotel will get here without difficulty.

Cost: from 3600 rubles / person.

8. Hotel "Novokurkino"

Quite often, guests of the city require an hourly hotel. Khimki also has such an institution - this is the Novokurkino Hotel. It offers rooms with a huge bathtub. Each one has a large TV, a spacious wardrobe and a large bed. Free internet is available on site.

For people traveling by car, parking is available. Many guests in their reviews note that in the immediate vicinity there are all kinds of cafes and shops. It is also worth noting that this is the nearest hotel to the Sheremetyevo Airport (it is located at a distance of 4 kilometers). A shuttle service is also available upon request.

Cost: from 1800 rubles / person.

9. Hotel "Club Vodnik"

Vodnik Club is a hotel located in a great location, not far from the Ring Road and on the coast of the Klyazma Reservoir. The hotel operates year-round: in summer it offers its guests boat trips, a catamaran and sail, jet skis and motor ships, in winter there is the opportunity to rent a snowmobile and ride a skating rink, as evidenced by numerous reviews.



For leisure, guests of this Khimki hotel are also offered bowling, billiards, a beach, a heated pool, and mini-golf. Here is a wonderful restaurant where you can try dishes of European and Mediterranean cuisine.

Cost: from 1500 rubles / person.

10. Hotel Planernoe

This hotel is open to guests in one of the most beautiful natural corners of the Moscow region. He will delight his guests with picturesque views of the vibrant green zone, as well as high-quality service of the level of European standards. The rooms, decorated with the requirements for high designer taste and technical equipment, will satisfy even the most demanding guest, which is confirmed by the reviews of guests of this institution.

If you plan to hold a banquet, a wedding celebration or a business meeting here, the event will certainly help you make it unforgettable. For guests are offered billiards, sports fields, various types of saunas, a fitness center, in addition, a large natural complex located on the coast of the river. A gangway that spans 20 hectares.

Cost: from 5000 rubles / person.
