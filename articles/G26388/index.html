Beach vacation

Makadi Spa 5 * is a relax hotel located on a personal chic beach. This especially attracts those people who come here in order to enjoy the sea. It is worth noting that the hotel has a very favorable location. Very close are a variety of shops, restaurants and cafes, where you can go if the hotel options are boring. Every day a bus runs from the hotel to the largest shopping center called Grand Mall (Hurghada). The complex itself consists of a five-story main building and 88 chalets in two and one floors, which are located throughout the territory. The extensive sandy beach has a shower and bar, as well as its own pontoon for entering the water. This is an ideal place for diving, which is especially suitable for guests who want to try water sports. And, of course, is it worth talking about the presence of such trifles as mattresses, sunbeds and beach towels.

The rooms at this hotel are top notch and furnished with everything you might need. Standard apartments are made in an elegant style, they are spacious, each junior suite has access to the loggia.
