Tours from Moscow to Samui are very popular among domestic vacationers. And of course, many Russian tour operators offer trips to this warm Thai island. How to get to Samui from Moscow

Unfortunately, there are no direct flights to this Thai island from the capital of Russia or any other cities of our country. Therefore, tourists who rent rooms at Chaweng Noi Pool Villa 4 * often have to fly to Bangkok or Phuket first, and already make a transfer to the island. Thus, tourists who rented rooms here will most likely have to go on their own to this hotel. Such travelers from Phuket or Bangkok airport to Samui and the hotel can be reached both by plane and by bus.





Travelers who bought organized tours to this hotel from Moscow to Samui are, of course, delivered by tour operators. In this case, a trip to the hotel for vacationers will likely be less tiring. But be that as it may, tourists who travel to Samui by bus, usually do not regret too much about the time spent on the road. Thailand is a very beautiful country. And on the way, travelers can admire truly paradise landscapes, the blue sea, white luxurious beaches, overgrown with picturesque green mountains, etc.

Location Chaweng Noi Pool Villa 4 *

There are several resort towns and villages on Koh Samui. Chaweng Noi Pool Villa 4 * hotel is located in the north of the island - in the former fishing village of Bophut. Tourists who arrive on Samui with a plane transfer can reach this complex in just a few minutes. The island's airport is located in close proximity to Bophut. Drive to the hotel from here will take no more than 20-30 minutes.





Very convenient, this hotel, according to many tourists, is located in relation to the sea. Hotel Chaweng Noi is built very near the coastline . To the nearest beach , to sunbathe or swim, its guests can be reached in just 5 minutes. Chalong Beach, the most popular among tourists on Koh Samui, is a seven-minute drive from the hotel.

Pretty good location in terms of the location of the hotel Chaweng Noi Pool Villa 4 * tourists consider Bophut itself. The center of this resort is only about 5 km from the hotel. Due to this location of the hotel, its guests are, firstly, free from city noise, and secondly, they can fully use all the amenities and infrastructure of Bophut itself. Less than a kilometer away are, for example, such popular tourist destinations as the Solo Bar and Jungle Club.

What is a complex

The Chaweng Noi Pool Villa 4 * hotel was built in Bophut not so long ago - in 2013. That is, in fact, it is completely new. This hotel accepts guests both in the main multi-storey building, and in several detached villas. The territory of the hotel has its own, well planned and equipped. When is the best time to come to the hotel?

As elsewhere in Thailand, the high season in Koh Samui is from January to March. At this time, the country rarely rains, and the air warms up to a comfortable 30 0 C. For example, a very good decision would be to come to Chaweng Noi Pool in Thailand for the New Year.

The most unfortunate times of visiting Samui is considered to be the period from the second half of October to mid-December. Tourists on the island will not be able to relax well in these months anyway. At this time, Thailand simply has a huge amount of rainfall, and sometimes floods even occur.

If you fail to come to Thailand on New Year's Eve, you can book rooms at Chaweng Noi Pool in the summer. At this time, the so-called second season begins on Koh Samui. It rains in Thailand in summer, of course, more often than in winter. However, usually they are very short-lived and tourists do not interfere too much with rest.
