Sights of Kirov: monuments, temples, museums, gardens and parks. Where to go to relax in Kirov

For some reason, it is believed that you can see something interesting only abroad. By the way, in Russia there are many cities that can amaze you with their sights. One of them is Kirov. This is the administrative center of the region, it is located on a river called Vyatka. Almost half a million people live in the city. The sights of Kirov will surprise you. And although there are not many, there is still something to see and where to visit.

Monuments of the city



The sights of Kirov are represented by numerous monuments. If you are familiar with the work of A. Green, then you definitely should see the sculpture “Running on the Waves”, installed in honor of the heroine of the writer’s story of the same name. It is located at a small pond. The stone for the work was brought from the Urals. And the monument was paid with money from the governor's fund. It was performed efficiently, so weather disasters are not fearful to him. In addition, security cameras are installed next to the sculpture to protect against vandalism.

There are even more original monuments of Kirov. For example, all visitors are interested in the sculpture "Family". The city was presented by the Megafon company. They opened it festively and cheerfully. And already on the first day, the couple celebrating the wedding, engendered a good tradition - make a wish, sitting down on an impromptu bench. Now all tourists coming to Kirov do this.

Monuments of architecture

Kirov's monuments are represented not only by original contemporary compositions. Attention deserves, for example, the house of A.Yu. Levitsky, provincial doctor. The architecture of the cottage is similar to that which can be seen in England, Holland and the Baltic countries.



The architectural monuments of Kirov are both the building of the former Order Chamber, and numerous buildings created in the style of classicism and empire. And next to the Drama Theater is the Wish Tree. This composition is unique. Several centuries ago, the famous Silk Road passed through the city. And the merchants of the Lyangusovs received permission from Peter I to transport silk, leather, furs, and herbs from China on this route to the country for the manufacture of medicines. It was on this site, where caravans allegedly once passed, and the Wish Tree was established. The foundation for this was laid for seven days. And under the tree is a box with the desires of local residents, which they sent to a local radio station. That is, the monument literally stands on the aspirations of the townspeople. It is made of concrete. The master who created the monument carved a large ear on it. It is in it that everyone can come and whisper their dreams and desires.

Kirov Park



If you want to find out more about Kirov, the sights of the city are worth seeing and those that are represented by parks. The main one is named after Kirov. It is located in the center. The park began to be created back in the 70s. This wonderful place with alleys, ponds with cascades, lawns, deciduous and coniferous trees quickly pleased the residents. A little later, rides appeared here. And now it is the main leisure center of the city. Thousands of people come here. There are playgrounds, a boat station, and a beautiful fountain.

Numerous city parks

There are other parks in Kirov. One of them is located near the Palace of Pioneers. Popular among residents are the Victory Parks named after Gagarin. These green areas of the city are great for walking. Kochurovsky Park and the Alexander Garden are also worth a visit. These are quiet places where you can escape from all the problems. Parks of Kirov must be visited upon arrival in the city.

Botanical Garden



The gardens of Kirov deserve attention. There are two of them in the city. The first is the Botanical Garden. Its history began in 1912, and Istomin Alexey founded it. This man was a retired colonel; he loved nature very much. Having bought a plot of land, he founded a garden, which recently celebrated its centenary. As it developed, this place was improved, new plants were planted. The garden quickly became one of the most interesting places in the city. However, ordinary people could not see all this beauty. Alexey Istomin opened the garden only for members of his family. General access was allowed only after 1917. Be sure to come here by visiting the city. The sights of Kirov will not be fully revealed to you if you do not see the Botanical Garden.

Alexander Garden

There is another interesting place in the city. The aforementioned Alexander Garden is the oldest park in the city. After all, they founded it back in 1825. Mountain ash, bird cherry, birch, and linden were planted at the selected location. And along the perimeter of the object then there were various architectural structures, buildings. The opening took place ten years after the foundation. Currently, the Alexander Garden is a vivid example of a work of park landscape design belonging to the era of classicism.

Joy for theatergoers

Many, leaving the capital, consider themselves divorced from cultural life. But it's not right. Theaters of Kirov, or rather their only representative, fully satisfy the needs of citizens in the pursuit of art. The Kirov Drama Theater was founded in 1877. The structure was wooden. The main activity of the theater at that time was the holding of dance evenings, balls, masquerades, concerts of visiting artists. His story is full of ups and downs, but he showed a lot of real masterpieces. Chaliapin, Padarin, Plevitskaya and other wonderful artists performed on the stage of the theater. In 1935, a decision arose to erect a new building. The project was prepared in a short time. And in 1939, the renovated theater showed its citizens a new performance.

Trifonov Monastery: the beginning



The monasteries of Kirov are represented by an amazing ensemble. This city has something to see. You simply must visit and see the ensemble of the Trifonov Monastery. It is an architectural monument of 18-19 centuries. The ensemble was supplemented for several centuries. So, in 1968 the Cathedral of the Assumption of the Virgin was built. Three years earlier, the St. Nicholas Gate Church arose. Over the course of the 18th century, the Annunciation churches, the Three Saints, and the rector’s chambers were built. A bell tower, fraternal cell buildings, a brewery and a corner tower were also created.

The place for the construction of the monastery was reserved by order of Tsar Ivan the Terrible. And construction began where there used to be an old city cemetery. Rev. Tryphon became the first abbot, and he was engaged in erection. The first buildings became possible thanks to the donations of the inhabitants of the city. At first the monastery was a small monastery, but over time it turned into a beautiful wooden ensemble. It included 4 temples, a belfry, a chapel. After a while, the buildings began to become stone. And new ones were built on the site of the old.

The development of the monastery in our time

At the beginning of the 20th century, the monastery looked amazing. He was surrounded by an old garden with raspberries, linden, currants, flower beds. Color was added by arbors, a cast-iron pavement, bridges, asphalt paths, a pool, a chapel, ponds, a well-kept cemetery. Tryphon has collected more than 150 books in his entire life. They became the basis for the rich library at the monastery. A little later, a museum of church values ​​was founded here. By the way, the relics of the first abbot are buried in the Assumption Cathedral. Due to hostilities in the mid-20th century, part of the buildings was lost. And their restoration began in 1980.

Museums for history buffs



The sights of Kirov will be interesting to history buffs. In particular, these include museums. There are many such institutions in the city. One of the oldest not only in Kirov, but throughout Russia is the regional museum of local lore. Thanks to the initiative of P.V. Alabina (statesman and public figure), he was created in 1866. The museum’s collection attracts thousands of tourists, because it has more than 250 thousand exhibits. This museum is a real paradise for numismatists. Here they can see about 45 thousand original coins of different eras. The collection of written sources has nearly 40 thousand exhibits. There is also a collection of gypsum sculpture, and natural science, which includes the rarest exhibits. Museums of Kirov are almost all part of the regional study of local lore. There are eight exposition departments that offer visitors to see different collections.

Exposition departments of the Kirov Local History Museum

  1. The main building was opened recently, this event marked the 140th anniversary of the museum. It is recognized as a monument of regional importance. There is an exposition telling about the features of national history, you can see a collection of folk house painting. A variety of interchangeable exhibitions are constantly held in the main building.
  2. MVU "Diorama" is a rather old museum object. It was opened back in 1977. The basis of this object is an art canvas called "Vyatka 1917." Exhibitions are held here all the time, various forms of work with visitors are actively used. Everyone who visits this museum will get acquainted with the culture and traditions of the Vyatka region.
  3. MVK "Nature" opened almost 15 years ago. First, an exhibition was exhibited there about the living past of the earth, then - telling about the Ice Age. And a year after the opening of the complex, one could see an exhibition called “Vyatka Forest”. This museum is located in a green area, it is popular among local residents, especially among children.
  4. Museum "Vyatka Kunstkamera". Now it is a historical monument of regional importance. Visitors can see here collections of household lighting fixtures, earthenware and china, watches and so on.
  5. “Vyatka folk arts and crafts” is a museum that was opened during the Soviet Union in a building that was considered at that time one of the first stone buildings related to civil architecture of the 18th century. The exhibits exhibited here tell about how folk arts and crafts developed in the Vyatka region. Visitors can see products made of burl, wooden and clay toys, lace and so on.
  6. The Museum of Military Glory in the city was recently opened. But its construction began in 2009. His exposition is dedicated to everyone who fought in Chechnya, Afghanistan, fought with the enemy on the fields of World War II.
  7. There are literary museums in the city. In them you can get acquainted with the life story and work of A.S. Green, M.E. Saltykov-Shchedrin.

Other sights of Kirov

If you are concerned about the question of how to organize leisure, having arrived in the city of Kirov, then the answer will always be found. You can visit one of the above attractions. There are other options: go to the Puppet Theater, Kirov Planetarium, circus, cinema, library.

Local cinemas



About Kirov cinemas is worth talking separately. Their visit is now very important for young people. In local cinemas, you can watch both Hollywood films and domestic films. Moreover, these institutions in the city are quite qualitatively and modernly equipped. This applies to the cinema "Globus", and "Kinomax", and "Change", and "Coliseum".

Entertainment Fans

In addition, young people often prefer going to museums or theaters to go to clubs and restaurants. Such pastime on vacation is not uncommon. Well, all kinds of leisure activities can be easily combined while in Kirov. There are several nightclubs that delight visitors with excellent show programs, stylish interiors, and performances by artists. The entertainment centers under the names “Victory”, “Planet” and “Neon” are especially popular in the city.

Restaurant Offers

There are several billiard clubs in Kirov, bowling, numerous cafes and restaurants. The latter, by the way, are famous for a large assortment of dishes from different cuisines of the world. In the restaurant "Castle Camelot" you will plunge into the atmosphere of antiquity. The Shinok establishment will delight Russians with Ukrainian cuisine. There are both oriental restaurants and pubs in the city.

For athletes

If you are closer to a healthy lifestyle, the sights of Kirov for you are gyms, fitness centers, swimming pools, baths, solariums and massage rooms. There are plenty of them in the city.
