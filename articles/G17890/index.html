Cosiness and comfort of the hotel Caliente Bodrum Resort (Bodrum)

The purpose of our virtual trip will be to get acquainted with the possibilities of relaxation in the comfortable hotel Caliente Bodrum Resort. Bodrum (Turkey) is a city of secrets of many civilizations. Here was born the "father of history" Herodotus.

Here are the remains of a monumental burial structure - the tombs of Tsar Mavsol. Over the course of the long 19 centuries, the locals looked up at the level equivalent to the 12th floor of a modern high-rise building, where the king and queen Artemissia ruled the marble four horses. Bodrum today is famous for the largest in Turkey disco "Halikarnassus", which resembles an ancient amphitheater.

Holidays in this very western Turkish resort are always in demand: there is no heat on the shores of the Aegean Sea, a pleasant light breeze always blows from the sea. The air is clean, fresh, I want to breathe deeply. Here reigns the spirit of Mediterranean hospitality: 172 classic rooms, 97 economy rooms, 9 two-room suites with a living room are at the disposal of the Caliente Bodrum Resort. Bodrum is famous for its bright Mediterranean greenery, and this hotel complex, immersed in the splendor of pines, palm trees, olive trees, rosemary, garriga, juniper, stone linden, frigans, was no exception.
The sea coast is indented with small sand and pebble coves, comfortably equipped with numerous hotels. Crystal clear sea waters are typical of the surroundings of Caliente Bodrum Resort. Bodrum invariably attracts the best divers, not only from Turkey. Convenient rocky niches make it possible for both beginners (3 m) and experienced athletes (up to 30 m) to dive. Almost every dive pleases acquaintance with textured octopus, unhurried lobster, larkfish (pike), shrimp, dangerous moray eel, dainty orthosis.

Leisure guests are well organized by the animators. Volleyball court is not empty , water polo, table tennis, water aerobics are popular. You can shoot from a sports bow. Caliente Bodrum Resort Hotel has a professional spa. Positive feedback is left by guests about the Turkish bath located at it - a hammam.

The hotel's favorite leisure destination is 2 outdoor pools, one of which is equipped with three slides.

Animators will entertain children (4-12 years old) by organizing their leisure time in a mini-club. Always lively at the playground at the Caliente Bodrum Resort. Bodrum is a typical Mediterranean city, where children are traditionally warmly treated.

The food is professionally organized by two restaurants: the main one (classical, Turkish cuisine), accommodating 650 visitors in its open part and 350 under the roof, and also the additional one on the beach (delicious Italian dishes). Five bars of the hotel complex are actively working.

In the bars you can take juices, cocktails, local drinks according to the all inclusive system , but expensive - for a fee. For breakfast, the main restaurant offers guests oranges, grapefruits, watermelons, melons, apricots, apples. In the evening, meat and fish dishes are prepared for them on the grill.

Summing up, it remains only to recognize the merits of the management of a four-star hotel and the professionalism of its staff.
