Hotel The Journey Patong Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

If you are young, active and plan to spend your vacation in Phuket (Thailand), but do not want to spend too much on accommodation, then The Journey Patong Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket, Patong) is exactly what you need. We suggest talking further about what awaits guests here.





Location

This hotel is located on the outskirts of Patong, which is located on the island of Phuket (Thailand). It was located on a busy street with a lot of cafes, shops, supermarkets, rental cars, massage parlors and other tourist infrastructure. Moreover, according to travelers, the prices here are almost all slightly lower than in the city center. According to our compatriots having a rest at this hotel, the nearest Patong Beach can be reached on foot in about 20 minutes. Tourists warn that along the way you will have to move along a busy roadway for some time without sidewalks, which is somewhat unpleasant. Therefore, most travelers preferred to rent a motorbike for the entire period of stay in Phuket.

This greatly facilitates the process of moving around the city. In addition, you will have the opportunity to visit various sights on your own and to visit other beaches of the island. It will cost much cheaper than taxi services and tour agencies. As for the proximity to the airport, the hotel is 25 kilometers from Phuket's air harbor. So upon arrival on the road to the hotel, it will take you about forty minutes.





General information, photo

The Journey Patong Resort 3 * (Phuket, Patong) was opened in 2014. It is a small city hotel without its own territory. Many tourists liked its modern and original design.

The housing stock is represented by 24 comfortable rooms located in a five-story building. Please note that the hotel does not have a lift. The hotel is not particularly designed for families with children. According to tourists, it is great for young and active people who are not embarrassed by either stairs or remoteness from the beach.

In general, the travelers who left reviews about The Journey Patong Resort 3 *, were satisfied with their choice. They argue that the cost of living here is consistent with the quality of accommodation and the level of service. The main thing is simply to study all the information about the hotel in advance in order to be ready for some of its features (remoteness from the center and the sea, lack of an elevator, the impossibility of installing extra beds in rooms). The rating of the hotel, based on the ratings of travelers who have been here, also suggests that most of the guests were satisfied with it. So, he makes 4 points out of the maximum possible five.





Housing stock

As already mentioned, The Journey Patong Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket, Thailand) has at its disposal 24 rooms with large double or single bunk beds. The apartments have a stylish design. They are small in area, but they have everything you need for a comfortable stay. So, in the room you will find furniture, a spacious refrigerator, a powerful air conditioning that is easily adjustable, a TV with cable channels, a free electronic safe, a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, a small balcony. Wi-Fi access is also available throughout the hotel. Using the Internet is completely free.





Service, staff

As for cleaning, they carry out it at the request of guests. So, if you want the maid to clean your room, then when leaving, put a sign on the door with the appropriate inscription. Towels and bed linen are changed several times a week.

As for the hotel staff, according to the guests, all the staff here are smiling, welcoming, friendly and helpful. Despite the fact that no one speaks Russian here, and workers also do not speak English at a high level, the staff always tries to do everything possible to help tourists.

Nutrition

When booking accommodation, tourists can choose either breakfast included in the price or not eat at the hotel at all. Many of our compatriots who visited this hotel are advised to stay on the second option. The fact is that there is no own restaurant here, so breakfasts are very scarce and monotonous. According to travelers, they consist of omelet, a piece of bacon, toast, tea or coffee. In the vicinity of the hotel there are many cafes and restaurants where you can eat tasty and inexpensive. There are also shops and supermarkets where you can buy a variety of products.
