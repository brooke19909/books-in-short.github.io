Hostels of Tallinn: addresses, rating of the best, choice of room, booking, service, reviews of visitors and customers

In Tallinn, there are many options for budget accommodation with a European level of service and maintenance. In inexpensive hostels there are various types of rooms that can accommodate both large companies and single tourists. If you stay in the city center, then all the attractions will be within walking distance and you will not have to waste time and money on public transport.

Hostel in the Old Town

The Monk's Bunk Party Hostel is the best hostel in Estonia. It was recognized as such in 2015 and 2016. The hostel is located right in the center of Tallinn, close to the Old Town and main attractions. Address: Tatari, 1. The cost of living - from 12 euros per person.

  • Fresh linens.

  • Equipped kitchen with utensils (can be used around the clock).
  • Luggage storage room.
  • A bar with snacks and cheap drinks.
  • Shared room with sofas, pool, games and a large screen.
  • No curfew: you can come and go to the hostel at any time.
  • Free Wi-Fi in every room and the ability to use guest computers.
  • Laundry service for a small fee.
  • Free tourist maps of the city with recommendations on what to see and what to do.
  • Separate smoking area.
  • Organization of airport transfers for large groups.

Check-in starts at 15-00, check-out is until 12-00.

For large companies

Hostel Old Town Alur enjoys a quiet location in the heart of the Old Town on Lai Street 20. Just one minute walk from Town Hall Square, it offers easy access to the bus station and airport. This is the largest hostel in Tallinn, which is suitable for groups of tourists as well as for single travelers.

The building, built in the 15th century, was completely renovated in 2015. Spacious bright rooms, parquet floors, soundproofed windows, modern bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen - all comply with European standards. All rooms are equipped with wooden furniture.

Old Town Alur Hostel offers a range of services, tours and events, as well as an airport shuttle. The cost of living starts from 26 euros per person.
