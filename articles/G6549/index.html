Hotel Globus 3 * (Prague, Czech Republic): photos and reviews of tourists

The Czech Republic is one of the most popular countries to visit with Russian tourists. People come here to take a look at the stunning ancient architecture of the country, try local beer, visit Czech restaurants and enjoy the many shops located in the narrow streets of Prague. Going on a trip, tourists first of all choose a hotel in which they will live. A good and inexpensive option is the hotel Globus 3 * (Prague).

Holidays in Prague

In central Europe, there is hardly a city that is more beautiful than Prague. The majestic and ancient capital of the Kingdom of Bohemia is located on both banks of the Vlatva River. Everywhere here you can see ancient bridges, the most famous of which is Charles Bridge. Amazingly beautiful statues stand along its entire length, which will not leave any tourist indifferent. Prague has retained the appearance of a medieval city. On small cobbled streets, each building is an architectural monument. The Czechs themselves say that you can walk around the city every day and discover something new every time. Coming here, it is worth paying attention to souvenir shops located near the Globus 3 * hotel (Prague).





Of course, not only architecture is famous for the capital of the Czech Republic. Tourists are also attracted by delicious Czech beer. There are so many of its names that any gourmet will find a drink to his liking. For a beer, you should definitely try the famous Czech cuisine. A variety of meat, smoked sausages, pork ribs baked in honey, boar knee - the most popular dishes in all restaurants of the city. The meat is necessarily served with special sauce and dumplings - bread slices made according to a medieval recipe.

Brief information about the Globus Hotel

Small hotel Globus 3 * (Czech Republic, Prague), located on the outskirts of the park, opened its doors to tourists in 1991. Consists of two five-story buildings. It offers tourists 157 rooms, which are designed for single travelers and for families with children. In addition, the hotel can be accommodated with your pets. To do this, you must notify the administration in advance of their arrival. This service is paid and depends on the number and size of animals. For small children, the hotel provides a cot in the room. Discounts on accommodation for babies are not provided.
