Vita Komaruga

Hotel Vita Comarruga 4 opened its doors to tourists in 2007. Three years later, a complete restoration was carried out. It is located on the exquisite coast of the Mediterranean Sea, in the serene and unflappable corner of Spain - the village of Coma Ruga. Despite this, here you can find a large number of entertainment and infrastructure facilities, and the main attractions can be easily reached by train or taxi. Just a few minutes walk from the hotel is the beautiful center of the resort, where there are many shops and restaurants, 30 kilometers from Reus Airport, and half an hour to Barcelona by car. Also near the hotel there is a bus stop and a train station, from where trips to all directions of the country are open.





On a small, but rationally designed interior of the hotel complex, there is one snow-white five-tier building, decorated in a calm and measured classical style. This hotel is one of the most sophisticated and fashionable resorts in the Costa Dorada.

Number of rooms

Hotel Vita Comarruga 4 (Spain) provides guests with comfortable apartments. The decoration is dominated by neutral calm tones in harmony with warm coffee shades, colorful accessories and interior details are created using upholstery and fabrics of the color of ripe cherry, and curtains with patterns of gorgeous colors. Unusual decor of the apartments are wall paintings with contrasting black and white sketches from the life of the resort. The hotel offers 236 comfortable spacious rooms for tourists:

Standart room. A larger number of rooms in the hotel is represented by standard apartments, which are equipped with two spacious double beds, as well as a small armchair that transforms into a cot for the baby. The total area is 26 square meters, with convenience there can accommodate five people.

Junior Suite. Lovely family room, equipped with two wide beds, a sofa bed and a bathroom with modern fixtures. The total area is 35 square meters, the maximum number of guests who can comfortably accommodate is six people.

Suite Luxurious roomy apartments located on the fifth level of the main building. They have a spacious veranda and a jacuzzi, as well as a magnificent panoramic view of the sea. The total room size is 35 square meters, they include a bedroom, a bathroom with a hydromassage and a shower. The maximum number of guests in a room is six.





The hotel also has four rooms for disabled people (located on the first level and with a wide doorway). Unfortunately, pets and smoking are not allowed in Vita Komaruga.

Room features

The hotel complex Vita Comarruga 4 has recently been completely renovated, so the room decoration and interior design are modern and functional. The spacious apartments of the hotel are equipped with everything necessary for a complete and high-quality holiday in Europe: individual air conditioning, satellite TV with three Russian channels, telephone, wireless access to the global network, digital safe and minibar. You will have to pay extra for using the last two services.

The bathroom is equipped with modern plumbing equipment, some apartments have a bathtub, others have a shower. All bathrooms have a hairdryer, bathrobes and towels, which are changed every day.

Nutrition

Hotel Vita Comarruga 4 (Costa Dorada) provides four types of ration: breakfast, half board, full board and all inclusive. The all inclusive program operates in popular seasons and involves full-fledged meals in the main restaurant of the complex, one meal in two a la carte establishments, as well as in bars - free snacks, light meals, fruits and salads, ice cream, drinks, alcoholic cocktails, coffee and tea , beer and wine "Sangria". Also, this system includes the use of a safe, parking at the hotel, free access to the spa and gym, one session of water cosmetic procedures, a beautiful view of the room from the sea.





In the main restaurant, guests will be able to taste the buffet breakfast, which has a wide and varied assortment of dishes. For lunch and dinner, culinary specialists prepare exquisite masterpieces of local and international cuisine. In the spacious dining room, as well as on the summer terrace with stunning views of the sea surface, tourists can taste various dishes: salads, fish and cold cuts, a large number of vegetables and fruits, sweet dishes and pastries.

Guests can also enjoy the culinary delights of talented chefs in two restaurants a la carte format . One of them - Arrocera - offers delicious dishes of Mediterranean cuisine, and in the second - Di Mare - they prepare delicious Italian pizza. There are bars on the territory of the hotel and by the pools, where you can also have a snack, drink refreshing drinks or taste sparkling wines.

Beach

Hotel Vita Comarruga 4 is located on the first coastline, fifty meters from the sea. From the magnificent and clean public beach with golden enveloping dunes, it is separated by a promenade. The entrance to the sea is shallow and shallow, swimming here is safe even for the smallest children. Going further, you can enjoy a beautiful view of corals and amazing small exotic fish. Sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas are provided for a fee.

Infrastructure and Services

The Vita Comarruga 4 has a great infrastructure that will allow you to spend a comfortable and memorable stay. On a small, but tastefully decorated area, there are four pools of different depths, one of which is indoor. For a pleasant sunbathing and enjoy the warm breeze, a large number of comfortable sunbeds and umbrellas are installed near them. For guests at the hotel - laundry, paid parking, Internet cafe, several shops that operate in the summer and sell souvenirs, as well as a gym. For business guests who are accustomed to combine work and leisure, there is a meeting room with a capacity of 220 people.





For connoisseurs of the fair and the fairer sex, the spa-center hospitably opens its doors, where you can relax and enjoy massage sessions, body wraps and hot tubs. All tourists will be able to swim in the warm pool, restore their strength in the Turkish bath and sauna, as well as recharge their batteries after a cold shower and an ice fountain.

Sports and Entertainment

The hotel complex Vita Comarruga (Costa Dorada) 4 has a wide range of interesting and exciting entertainment. Table tennis, a cinema, beach volleyball and billiards can brighten up the rest of the tourists. Paid sports activities include water skiing, archery, horse riding, minigolf, water sports (diving, canoeing, surfing). In the daytime, a team of professional animators works for kids and adults, and in the evening a variety of shows and shows are held.





For children

Hotel Vita Comarruga 4 is recommended primarily for couples with kids of different ages, as it is part of the Sun Family club resort complexes. A wide infrastructure has been created here for an interesting, exciting and safe vacation with children. The hotel complex has a colorful indoor children's club with a television room, where all young tourists from 4 to 12 years old can enjoy watching animated films, as well as a room for teenagers. A colorful and bright playground with modules will allow you to have fun and joy, a large number of toys and creative material will not allow your kids to get bored at the resort. Harmonious development includes the following components: gaming, creative, sports activities and fun animation.

For a safe and enjoyable swim, a shallow children's analogue is equipped next to the adult pool. In the daytime, a team of Russian-speaking professional animators from the Sun Family club organization works with the guys, which will delight them with interesting games, recreational activities in the fresh air and original programs. Every day in the evening there is a mini disco for kids. Meals for children are offered in a specialized restaurant with highchairs. The hotel also has a specialized room for mom, which is equipped with a microwave oven, a blender and a sterilizer for children's dishes.

Cost

High-quality service, a wonderful location on the cleanest beaches near the mountains, as well as a wide infrastructure will be able to offer Costa Dorada for its guests. Prices for a tour to the Vita Komaruga hotel start from 16 900 rubles per person for a seven-day stay, which includes accommodation in beautiful apartments, breakfast, transfer to the hotel.

Reviews

Excellent service of a high level, wide infrastructure and a comfortable stay can give the southern resort of Spain Costa Dorada. Photos of the surrounding area and the interior of the hotel confirm this. Reviews about the hotel are mostly positive, but there are also negative ones.

Among the positive opinions tourists highlight: polite and qualified staff, convenient and comfortable family vacations, wide infrastructure for children, a clean beautiful beach, a beautiful interior, a wide range of tasty dishes on the menu, spacious apartments. Among the negative reviews, guests of the resort note: inconvenient location, remoteness from the main attractions and sightseeing buses, a small town with a small infrastructure, access to the sea through the highway, poor soundproofing in the apartments.
