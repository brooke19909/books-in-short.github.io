Shopping center "Rook" in Mitino

Mitino is a relatively young, but already well-populated area in the north-west of Moscow. The developed infrastructure and the well-thought-out location of all its facilities allows residents to solve all pressing problems without leaving the district.

In Mitino there are about a dozen retail chains, and among them the shopping center "Ladya", which is located in the very center of the district, right at the exit of the metro.

The shopping center was opened in 2005 at the intersection of Mitinskaya and Dubravnaya streets. He owes his name to the boat depicted on the symbols of the district - the coat of arms and flag.

Shopping center "Rook" in Mitino is considered one of the best places for shopping and recreation. Here are concentrated shops, restaurants, establishments of consumer services, beauty salons, a cinema.





The shopping center is an architectural structure on four floors with an atrium inside and with a facade in the form of a plastic screen. The total area is 26,000 square meters. For visitors, all conditions are created here: modern convenient elevators, a ramp, video surveillance, wireless Internet access throughout the complex and much more.

For car owners there are parking guards, ground and underground, designed for more than 300 cars, so visitors who arrive by personal transport can safely go shopping.

The owners of the leading chain companies rent the space in the shopping center Ladya in Mitino: Detsky Mir and Eurodom stores, Perekrestok supermarket, IL Patio restaurant, L'Occitane Ile de Beaute cosmetics stores, Luxor multiplex and others .

The popularity of the shopping center is evidenced by its attendance: 20,000 people on weekdays and 30,000 on weekends on average.

The shops

On the territory of the shopping center "Ladia" (Mitino), there are about a hundred stores, including those offering products of famous world and Russian brands. Products of various directions are widely represented: clothes, underwear and shoes for adults and children, accessories and bags, cosmetics and perfumes, jewelry, souvenirs, gifts, goods for home and sports, children's toys and much more.

Here you can buy goods of famous brands, including: Reebok, ECCO, Donatto, Giovanni Botticelli, OGGI, Adidas, SELA, InCity, ZARINA, Oodji, Bustier, Ochkarik, Trial-Sport, ION, Tvoe and many others.





There is a system of discounts for buyers, regular promotions and sales are held, which makes it possible to purchase quality goods at very attractive prices. Shops are open from ten in the morning until ten in the evening.

Entertainment

Not only profitable shopping attracts residents of Mitino "Rook". Luxor Cinema , equipped with unique acoustics and modern video equipment, is a popular place of entertainment for citizens. The hotel has six lounges, a Japanese restaurant and a small bar. Here you can watch films of various genres - from thrillers to romantic comedies. Multiplex "Luxor" in the "Rook" in Mitino belongs to the cinema network, which covers many large Russian cities. The first session begins at nine in the morning, and work ends at midnight. The cost of tickets is 60-310 rubles.

You can safely go shopping with kids at the Rook shopping center in Mitino. After all, there is a complex "Little Genius", where children will find many games, including developing ones, while parents go about their business.

Where is it and how to get there

The address of the shopping center "Rook": Moscow, Dubravnaya, 34/29. The nearest metro stations are Mitino and Tushinskaya.

The easiest way to get there is by using the subway. If you get to the Mitino station, the shopping center will be right at one of the exits from the metro.

In addition, you can use the land transport, which follows the streets Dubravnaya and Mitinskaya.
