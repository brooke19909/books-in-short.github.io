Hotel Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * (Cuba / Varadero): photos and reviews of tourists

Despite the difficult political and economic situation, Cuba today remains a popular tourist destination. After all, this place is generously awarded by nature: almost anywhere in the island you can admire incredibly beautiful landscapes, not to mention the miles of snow-white beaches. Add to this an interesting culture, a rich history - and here is a real paradise for travelers eager for something new.

Naturally, on the coast of Cuba there is a huge number of large hotel complexes and cozy colorful boarding houses. One of the popular tourist stopping places is the Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * hotel. Of course, during the travel planning period, each person tries to get additional information about the chosen vacation destination.

How close is the hotel to the beach? What category of tourists is the hotel suitable for? What conditions of food and accommodation do guests offer? What kind of reviews do travelers leave? The answers to these questions are relevant to many readers.

Location Information





Location is one of the advantages of the Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * hotel complex. Varadero is one of the most colorful and popular resorts in Cuba. It is here, almost in the very center of the city, that the hotel is located. By the way, the picturesque beach is only a few meters, which is also convenient.

Varadero International Airport is about 25 km away. The capital of Cuba, Havana, with all its attractions and entertainment, is 180 km away. There is a tourist transport stop nearby: travelers can move around the city and the surrounding area (there are even regular buses to Havana).

Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 hotel: complex description





The hotel owns a rather large territory, on which there are two main two-story buildings. Around is a picturesque garden with palm trees, walking paths, flower beds. On the territory of the garden there are several small bungalows that are suitable for guests who prefer silence and solitude.

Naturally, there are bars, restaurants, pools, areas for recreation and sports - in short, everything that is necessary for a comfortable and interesting pastime. Hotel Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * was opened in 2000, and not so long ago, repairs were carried out here. This place is brand new, but already has a good reputation.

How many hotel rooms?

This hotel is considered a medium-sized complex. On its territory there are 282 spacious rooms. Of these, 160 are located in the main buildings. Another 122 rooms are located in separate cozy bungalows.

It is worth saying that these are standard double rooms with modern design and spacious balconies (or terraces, if we are talking about bungalows and rooms on the ground floor). There are several connected rooms that are perfect for travelers traveling with a large family or company.

Hotel complex Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4: photo and additional information about the rooms





Whatever number you are provided with, you can be absolutely sure that you will spend time in a calm, even homely atmosphere. The rooms here are quite large and bright, and the windows offer views of the seashore or the beautiful garden. Naturally, the room has the necessary furniture, including large beds, wardrobes, tables, sofas and chairs.

You can count on the equipment so necessary for comfort. An air conditioning system with a manual control panel will help to create a pleasant coolness in the room. There is a TV on which you can watch local channels (although there are several satellite ones). In addition, the room has a telephone (direct dial feature is connected), as well as a small safe with an electronic lock.

The room is adjacent to a small but fully equipped bathroom, where you can take a shower at any time of the day. Guests are provided with a set of clean towels (change daily), a hair dryer, shampoos, soap and other toiletries. Reviews say that the rooms here are really clean, because the maids work to their conscience every day.

What kind of power supply scheme should I expect?





What in terms of food does the Starfish Cuatro Palmas e. 4*? After all, it is difficult to imagine a vivid, memorable vacation without hearty and tasty food.

Paying for accommodation, you can count on a full three meals a day. The all-inclusive system also consists of locally produced alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. The main meals are buffet style at the main restaurant. Reviews say that the food at the hotel is at a high level: the choice of dishes is always large, and the food is fresh, hearty. There are salads, side dishes, cheese slices, snacks, seafood, different types of meat and fish. And at the end of the evening it is always pleasant to treat yourself to homemade ice cream, fresh crispy pastries and fragrant tea.

On the territory of the Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * hotel there are three a la carte restaurants where you can eat and relax in a relaxed atmosphere. It is worth considering that a place here must be reserved in advance. Dishes are prepared to order, they need to be paid separately.

There are several bars at the hotel where you can have a good time (one round the clock). Here, by the way, they serve not only aromatic coffee and alcoholic cocktails, but also real Cuban rum, which is worth a try at least once.

Distance to the beach and beach services





For many travelers, Cuba is associated with endless beaches and warm waters of the Caribbean Sea. So what can the Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * hotel offer the guest? Varadero is a beach resort that is considered almost the most popular destination in Cuba. The hotel territory faces directly to the sea, depending on the location of your room, the distance to the beach is about 200-500 meters.

Reviews say that the coast here is really magnificent. The coast is covered with fine white sand, and the entrance to the sea is convenient, the depth increases gradually. The beach is cleaned every day, so it is always a pleasure to spend time here. Of course, tourists can count on sun loungers and sun umbrellas.

Water recreation and entertainment

Healing sea air, swimming in warm waters and sunbathing are, of course, good. But what should travelers do who prefer to relax more actively? You should not worry about this, as a sports recreation center is equipped on the beach.

Tourists have the opportunity to do kayaking and parasailing. It is the right place for sailing and windsurfing. By the way, on the shore you can not only rent a board and other important devices, but also take a few lessons from real professional instructors. You can also rent a boat or pedal boat at any time . There is a chance to get a lot of fun when skiing. Tourists love to make small boat trips.

Description of additional services

What quality of accommodation can the guests of the Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * hotel expect? The services provided by travelers help make your stay here as comfortable as possible.

On the territory of the hotel you can easily rent a comfortable transport, which can then be left in the local parking lot. The laundry is constantly working. Here you can exchange currency at a relatively favorable rate. Guests can also get a safe at the reception free of charge.

At the local store you can buy small household items. And at the hotel there is always a medical office where vacationers are always ready to provide qualified assistance.

How tourists spend their free time: leisure and entertainment on site





The hotel tried to create all conditions so that the leisure of guests was as interesting and intense as possible. In the courtyard there is a large two-level pool where you can swim. Guests love to relax on comfortable sun loungers, enjoying exotic cocktails and a pleasant company of tourists.

You can also visit the gym with all the necessary equipment. A fitness center is constantly working, where you can work out individually with an instructor or sign up for group courses, including water aerobics, gymnastics and even Latin American dances.

The hotel also has a jacuzzi and a sauna. For a small fee, you can enjoy a wellness massage. Guests have a good time playing table tennis and billiards, and there is a golf course near the hotel where fans of this sport can have fun. There is Internet at the hotel, but you need to pay extra for it.

Throughout the day, a group of animators works on the territory, which create a light and festive mood for guests, arrange various contests and group games. In the evenings, you can enjoy live Cuban music and passionate dance performances.

How do travelers respond about the hotel?

Sometimes a few minutes spent in the company of people who have already managed to visit a particular hotel may be more informative than searching for information on the Internet. So what do travelers say about the Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * hotel?

Reviews are by and large positive. Vacationers take home only pleasant memories. To begin with, tourists note that the territory here is spacious and beautiful: palm trees and beautiful flowers grow everywhere, there are walking paths, benches and terraces for relaxation. The buildings themselves, although not new, are well maintained and renovated. The rooms are bright, the maids are cleaned every day. All equipment is in working condition.





Travelers leave special thanks to local chefs. The cuisine here is really very tasty, freshly prepared food, and the choice of dishes is simply huge. The staff are incredibly polite, speak English and always help in the event of a problem.

One of the main advantages of the hotel is its favorable location: it is close to the beach from here, and city entertainment centers are within walking distance. That is why many travelers choose the Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 * hotel as a temporary residence. Cuba, Varadero is one of the most interesting places to spend a truly unforgettable vacation.

What categories of tourists is the hotel suitable for?

What type of vacation hotel is suitable Starfish Cuatro Palmas 4 *? Varadero is one of the most popular Cuban resorts, which is visited annually by thousands of tourists. Here you can find almost everything: the picturesque Caribbean Sea, and upscale hotels, and a lot of entertainment.

The hotel is perfect for families, as there are necessary conditions for children. It often stops youth companies, craving for high-profile parties and bright performances. Often a hotel is used for weddings and a honeymoon. And do not forget about sightseeing holidays, because you come to a country with a rich culture and fascinating history.
