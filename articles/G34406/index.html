About consumer loans

You can take a consumer loan without collateral or guarantee for absolutely any amount up to one and a half million rubles from fifteen thousand. The term can also be any - up to five years from three months, and this is determined by the Sberbank depositor. Customer reviews of loans indicate rates starting at 14.9 percent per annum. The simplest documents will be needed: a questionnaire, a statement of income, a passport and a copy of the work book. If the salary is transferred to the Sberbank account, then you will not need anything other than an identity card. The amount received can be used for absolutely any purpose: household appliances, car, travel, apartment repair.

If you need an amount from one and a half million rubles to three million, you can not do without a guarantee, and such an application can also be issued online. In this case, documents are needed both for the person applying for a consumer loan and for the co-borrower (just as accurately: a questionnaire, passport, a copy of the work book and a statement of income). If the salary is credited to a card or Sberbank account, you only need a passport. Citizens under the age of eighteen and who have crossed the line of seventy-five years by the time of repayment of the debt cannot issue such a loan. The loan term can be adjusted from three months to 5 years, but the interest rate is higher - from 19.5 percent per annum. Within two days, the application will be considered, and if the applicant for a loan is a client of Sberbank, then this period is also reduced.
