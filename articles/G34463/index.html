How to pay the state fee at Sberbank: payment procedure, tips and tricks

How to pay the state fee at Sberbank? A similar question arises sooner or later in every resident of the Russian Federation. After all, it is Sberbank that is a financial organization that allows you to quickly and without problems and commission make state fees and pay fines / taxes. The main thing is to know how to act. Below you will find the best tips that reveal the features of paying state fees through Sberbank. After that, each person will be able to choose the appropriate algorithm of actions.

Ways to solve the problem

How to pay the state fee at Sberbank? There are different methods for solving the problem. And they all have their own characteristics.

Citizens can pay state fees:

  • in the office of Sberbank;
  • through payment terminals;
  • through ATMs;
  • using online banking.

In fact, all these methods will not bring any real difficulties. The main thing is to prepare for the procedures in advance. We will talk about this further.

What may be required

How to pay the state fee for a passport through Sberbank? And to pay for the registration of vehicles in the traffic police? To begin with, a citizen will have to choose a method for solving the problem and carry out preliminary preparation.

In general, for the introduction of duties without difficulty, the following components may be required:

  • cash money;
  • bank card
  • profile on Sberbank Online;
  • details of the recipient of the money (can be clarified in a particular government agency);
  • identification;
  • payment order.

That should be enough. Ideally, you should have a civil passport and a mobile phone with you. All this can come in handy for fast payment of fees for certain services.

Bank payment

How to pay the state fee at Sberbank? You can personally contact any branch of this financial institution and cope with the task. This approach is often used by people of the past generation. It has several advantages. For example, there is no need for ATM training. But there are also disadvantages to payment through the bank's cash desk. Let's say this is a pretty long process. And it can be carried out only during the opening hours of the selected branch of Sberbank.

In any case, if you want to contact the Sberbank office to pay the fee, you must:

  1. Take with you documents - passport, money, a completed receipt with the details of the recipient.
  2. Contact the cashier of any branch of Sberbank.
  3. Transfer the payment and money to an employee of a financial company.
  4. Present at the request of an employee of the organization an identity card.
  5. Pick up the receipt, check and change.

It is done. As we have said, this technique is effective, but it takes a lot of time. Sometimes you have to sit in line for several hours. Therefore, we will further study faster and simpler methods for solving the problem.

ATM and payments

How to pay the state fee at Sberbank? You can use an ATM to make a payment at any convenient time. Some ATMs work around the clock. They allow you to use cashless payments. It is this alignment that is found in real life more and more often.

Instructions for the implementation of the task will have approximately the following form:

  1. Find an ATM of Sberbank.
  2. Insert bank plastic into the machine and start working with it. To do this, you must specify the PIN using the numeric keypad of the device.
  3. In the menu that appears, select the "Payments in my region" section.
  4. Go to the tab ‚ÄúDuties, fines, taxes‚Äù.
  5. Select the option "State duties and fees."
  6. Indicate the recipient of the money.
  7. Enter data about the payer. To do this, you have to use the on-screen keyboard.
  8. Register and indicate the features of the payment - the reason for its implementation and the amount.
  9. Check upcoming transaction details.
  10. Confirm procedure.

After a couple of seconds, a message will appear on the screen confirming the successful completion of the payment. A citizen can pick up a bank card and a check for payment. It will have to be saved to confirm the transaction, if necessary.

Sberbank Terminals

And how to pay the state fee through the terminal of Sberbank? To act in this case is necessary according to the previously proposed instructions. The difference is that after checking the details, the citizen will have to deposit cash into the terminal. Change by such machines is not issued. And this must be taken into account.

Consider another way to make a payment using ATMs or payment terminals. The guidance will include the following steps:

  1. Find the terminal of Sberbank and click on any place on the screen. The main menu appears on the display.
  2. Select the "Search payee" section.
  3. Click on the "By TIN" or "By order number" button.
  4. Indicate data from the payment system.
  5. Select the desired organization in the search results.
  6. Enter the reason for the transaction and payment information.
  7. Print payer details.
  8. Indicate the amount of state duty. Sometimes it is installed automatically.
  9. Deposit money in the prescribed amount and confirm the process.

Can I pay the state fee through Sberbank? Yes. And now we met with another method of solving the problem. The proposed instruction is suitable not only for terminals, but also for ATMs.

Important: some Sberbank payment terminals accept bank cards. Then there is no need to deposit cash for the transaction.

By barcode

How to pay the state fee at Sberbank? The following technique helps to cope with the task with the help of terminals or ATMs. For him, a citizen must necessarily have a receipt of the established form.

The guidance provided below most often allows you to quickly pay fines and taxes. And only occasionally duties. It looks like this:

  1. Get started with ATM.
  2. Select "Search payee".
  3. Click on the "By barcode" button.
  4. Bring the receipt of payment to the reading beam with a barcode.
  5. Check the data is correct.
  6. Confirm the operation by pre-registering the payment amount, if this was not done automatically.

Fast, easy and very convenient. As already mentioned, this option is used when paying fines and taxes.

Internet banking to help

Need to pay a state fee? Sberbank Online will help solve the problem. This technique is used most often. It allows you to work with bank transfer and make payments at any time of the day. The main thing is to pre-register on the service.

Once this is done, you can begin to take action. How to pay state fees through Sberbank Online?

The step-by-step procedure looks like this:

  1. Open the Sberbank Online website in a browser.
  2. Log in to the portal using your username and password.
  3. Confirm the procedure by entering the secret code. It will come in the form of SMS to a mobile phone tied to an Internet banking profile.
  4. Open the "Payments and Transfers" section.
  5. In the search bar write the TIN of the payee. This information is available in the payment details.
  6. Choose the right company.
  7. Specify payment parameters following the on-screen prompts.
  8. Choose a bank card with which money will be debited.
  9. Click on the "Pay" button.

Now the screen will display information about the successful completion of the procedure. You can save a receipt for payment or print it immediately on a printer.

Important: state fees can be paid to Sberbank Online through the menu ‚ÄúDuties, taxes, fees‚Äù. In this case, the procedure will resemble working with an ATM / payment terminal.

Conclusion

How to act? We have already said that each citizen himself chooses the appropriate option for the development of events. And therefore, there is no difference in how to pay the state fee for a passport - through Sberbank Online or in the office of this company. The main thing is that now people will be able to familiarize themselves with possible methods of conducting transactions and determine for themselves the best approach.
