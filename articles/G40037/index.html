Hotel Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): photos, description, reviews

If you plan to relax in Thailand, namely on the island of Phuket, Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort 3 * can be a very suitable option for a comfortable and at the same time budget accommodation. We will find out more about what this hotel offers to its guests, as well as what impressions it left for tourists.



Location, general information, photo

This 3-star hotel is located in Karon Bay, eight kilometers from Patong on Phuket Island in Thailand. The nearest airport is 59 kilometers away. So the journey from the air harbor to the hotel will take you about an hour and a half. The popular Karon Beach is 200 meters from the Best Western.

This hotel was built in 1989. Recently, a renovation has been carried out here, so that guests expect comfortable, pleasantly decorated rooms with a fresh renovation. In total, the hotel complex has 180 apartments of the categories Superior, Deluxe, Deluxe Family and Suite. All of them are spacious and equipped with everything you need - large comfortable beds, wardrobes, bedside tables and other furniture, a bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer, a safe, a balcony or a terrace, a telephone, air conditioning, a TV with cable channels, a set for making coffee and tea, mini -bar. The free-of-charge mini-bar in the room is replenished daily with several bottles of drinking water. The rest of its contents is paid.

Rooms are cleaned and towels and bed linen are changed daily. Also, maids, as they are spent, replenish supplies of bath and toilet accessories.

The price of the hotel includes breakfast. But if desired, guests can have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant. It serves deliciously prepared local cuisine. Also on site is a bar with a wide selection of drinks for every taste.



Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort 3 *: traveler reviews from Russia

Each experienced tourist knows perfectly well how important it is not to make a mistake in choosing a hotel where you plan to stay during your vacation abroad. After all, even an unassuming traveler wants to return to a cozy, pleasantly decorated room with a comfortable bed and working equipment. Therefore, tourists do not spare time and effort to study the reviews of those who have already had a chance to stop in a particular place recently. After all, former guests, as a rule, do not hide the truth about the hotel where they spent their holidays and describe not only its advantages, but also its shortcomings. This helps to form a more complete and real picture of what will await you on vacation, so that you can make the right choice. In order to save your energy, we offer you the feedback from travelers from Russia and other CIS countries who recently stayed at the Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort Hotel during a trip to Thailand. Looking ahead a bit, we note that almost all of our compatriots absolutely did not regret their choice. In their opinion, this hotel is well worth the money spent and is suitable for living on vacation. But we learn more about everything.



Housing stock

Judging by the comments of our compatriots, they were quite pleased with the apartments offered to them at the Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort (Karon Beach). Especially guests liked the rooms, which overlook the pool. According to tourists, all the apartments here are newly renovated, very spacious, bright. The furniture and appliances here, however, are not very new and modern, but all of high quality, in working condition. If some kind of breakdown happens, then after reporting it at the reception, the malfunction will be fixed as soon as possible. Travelers also liked the fact that the rooms have free and fairly roomy safes. So you can leave documents and valuables in them, without fear of theft. The guests also included the fact that the apartments have everything you need. So, here you will find, in addition to standard TVs, air conditioners, hair dryers, minibars, a convenient wooden clothes dryer, a full set of personal hygiene products (cotton buds, razors, shower gel, soap, shampoo, shower caps, toilet paper, etc.), a sewing kit, an electric kettle, a set of dishes and tea and coffee bags, as well as an umbrella in case it rains outside. Therefore, as our compatriots note, despite the fact that this hotel has only three stars, it is thought out to the smallest detail, which leaves an extremely pleasant impression.



Cleaning

About the work of the maids at the Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort hotel, tourists left reviews exclusively positive. So, according to them, housekeeping is carried out daily and very high quality. As necessary, maids replenish bottled drinking water in the bar (it's free) and personal hygiene products in the bathroom. Towels and bed linen are changed daily. According to travelers, the quality of cleaning here does not depend on whether you leave a tip to the maid or not.

Territory

Hotel Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand), according to our compatriots, has a small, but very well-groomed, beautiful and green area. According to the guests, the hotel workers are constantly watering something, cutting, transplanting. So being in the hotel is always a pleasure.

Nutrition

The cost of living in the Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort includes breakfast only. According to tourists, every day in the mornings the same set of products and dishes is offered here, however there is always something to choose from. So, for breakfast you can eat a fried omelette, fragrant pastries, porridge, soup, fresh fruits and vegetables. As for food during the day, you can have lunch and dinner here in the hotel restaurant. However, according to our compatriots, eating here will be somewhat more expensive than in one of the cafes located in the vicinity of the hotel. But they admit that they cook at Best Western just fine, and the service is always on top. In addition, guests can buy something at the mobile kiosks, which, in the afternoon, line up near the hotel. Here you can eat while you cooked seafood, delicious pancakes with a variety of fillings and other fast food. In addition, there are several supermarkets near the hotel where you can always buy products (for example, fruits or alcoholic beverages).



Beach vacation

According to many tourists, a great advantage of the Best Western Phuket Ocean Resort is its proximity to one of the best beaches in Thailand. This is Karon Beach. The distance to it is only a few hundred meters. The beach here is very large, with a fine clean sand. Travelers also note the amazing azure color of the water in this place. The beach here is in municipal ownership, so if you want to comfortably sit on the sun loungers, then you will need to pay extra for this. But many tourists prefer to be placed directly on the sand, laying a mat. There are no stones here, so it will be convenient for you to lie. On the beach there are several cafes and bars where you can order a drink and eat.

Some travelers also went to Kata Beach for a change. According to them, the cheapest way to get to it is by tuk-tuk. But you can also take a taxi if you wish.
