About Greece

Greece is a country with an ancient history, great potential for historical values, a large European state with its traditions, mentality and characteristics of national culture. Hellas has long attracted the attention of tourists from all over the world. This is a country where a beach holiday is combined with visiting local attractions, spending active time and contemplating the natural beauties.

Greece is akin to Russia: Russian speech is often heard there, Orthodoxy prevails, and some national dishes of local cuisine are similar to Russian dishes. That is why many vacationers from Russia prefer holidays in Greece to any other.

Greece is diverse, its territory is the archipelagos, individual islands and part of the mainland. One of the popular tourist destinations is Crete. This is a large island located in the Mediterranean basin, washed from different sides by three seas: the Mediterranean, Aegean and Ionian.

The resort and its attractions

Hersonissos has ancient roots, so a large number of attractions are associated with its history. In walking distance there are ancient theaters, an ancient Roman port, an old fish tank, a Saracen fountain, a basilica, the defensive walls of ruined fortresses, the ruins of the ancient city of Festos. If you wish, you can book an excursion to explore the Knossos Palace or the Lychnostatis open-air museum.

There are modern attractions in Hersonissos, specially created for the entertainment of tourists:

  • Cretan aquarium;
  • water park "Water town";
  • El Greco Museum;
  • Labyrinth Park;
  • boardwalks and national parks.

Nature lovers will love the Samarya Gorge and the Skotino Cave. The cave got its name for bizarre calcium growths inside, resembling the heads of various animals. Horseback riding along the sea coast and sea riding on catamarans or a sailing boat will bring a lot of pleasure.

Unique will be a train ride around the city. A mini-locomotive rides vacationers along the entire nearby coast, passing through all interesting places.
