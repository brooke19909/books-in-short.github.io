Hotel Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah 2 *: overview, description, features and reviews

Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah was opened relatively recently (in 2009), but has already managed to win the hearts of tourists due to its favorable location and high standards of service.

Hotel Summary

Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah offers its guests a comfortable and carefree vacation in the popular resort of the United Arab Emirates. You will certainly be pleased with spacious beautiful rooms decorated in the best traditions of oriental luxury, which, thanks to professional designers, skillfully combines with European laconicism.

At the Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah, you should definitely visit the restaurants to experience the originality and originality of the spicy aromas of oriental cuisine. In addition, you will certainly appreciate the advantageous location of this hotel complex.





Hotel location

Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah Resort is located in the heart of Sharjah. Having walked from the hotel for some 5 minutes, you can be close to the famous Golden Market, a large shopping center or the historical part of the village where the main attractions are concentrated. The journey from the airport to the hotel will take you no more than 20 minutes.

Hotel guest rooms

The hotel Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah 2 * for guests provides 168 rooms that provide different accommodation conditions. So, tourists are offered single and double occupancy in standard apartments. They are compact enough, but if necessary can accommodate another guest in an extra bed.

The suites of the Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah 2 * hotel provide their guests with more living space, because they consist of two full rooms: a bedroom and a living room. Due to the presence of a set of upholstered furniture in the room, it can accommodate even more guests without extra beds.

The best option for a hotel stay is the deluxe category. The room has a chic design, as well as a spacious area. There is a bedroom, a living room, and even a small kitchen, which at the same time can also serve as a dining room.





Guest Room Features

In each room of the Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah resort (3 stars, not two - this is the minimum, by the way, which the hotel deserves for some indicators), a wide range of amenities is provided for guests:

  • individual air conditioning, which allows you to independently control the temperature in the room, regardless of the general ventilation system in the hotel;
  • a large flat-screen TV with a widescreen screen, through which you can watch not only satellite channels (including Russian), but also movies from disks and other media;
  • the number is covered by the wireless Internet;
  • Each apartment has an international telephone, which also works to communicate with the internal services of the hotel;
  • there is a minibar with refreshing drinks and alcohol.




Free and paid services

For those who have long dreamed of visiting the United Arab Emirates, Sharjah is a great option. Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah provides its guests with many services, some of which do not require additional payment, while the rest are available only for a certain amount of money. So, the opportunities that are included in the price of accommodation include the following:

  • Drinking bottled water and fresh tropical fruits are regularly delivered to the hotel rooms;
  • Arriving at the hotel, you can count on a welcome surprise in the form of a non-alcoholic refreshing drink;
  • the entire hotel operates a wireless global network;
  • twice a week, hotel transport carries guests to a shopping center for shopping;
  • every day the bus takes excursion groups to the historic city center;
  • You can contact the reception desk staff at any time with a question that interests you;
  • expressing such a desire, you can check into a room located in a special wing for non-smoking guests;
  • use of a left-luggage office, where you can check in excess bulky luggage;
  • car parking with security and video surveillance.




Subject to additional charges, you can use the following services:

  • washing and ironing in the hotel laundry;
  • direct room service, which includes the delivery of food, newspapers and more;
  • the services of a professional nanny who will look after your children while you are on excursions or just want to relax from worries in a relaxed atmosphere;
  • for a certain commission percentage, you can make a currency exchange;
  • use of the conference room facilities and the use of office equipment are available in the business center.




Hotel infrastructure

In order for guests of the Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah 2 * (Sharjah) to relax fully, and also spend their time fun and interesting, the following infrastructure facilities are provided for them:

  • a modern sports complex, which makes it possible to use fitness equipment, as well as do fitness with a qualified instructor;
  • cafes, restaurants and bars, which serve both those dishes and drinks that are characteristic of local cuisine, and those that are more familiar to European tourists;
  • own hotel transport, which can deliver you to the airport or any other place you need;
  • a modern conference room, which has all the necessary furniture and equipment for comfortable meetings and business negotiations;
  • room for banquets and celebrations;
  • some of the rooms, as well as a number of other hotel rooms, are specially equipped in accordance with the needs of people with disabilities;
  • there are apartments for people prone to allergic reactions (all furniture and equipment in them are completely made from environmentally friendly materials);
  • Large families are given the opportunity to move into combined rooms.




Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah 2 *: reviews

This hotel is very popular among tourists. This is largely due to the fact that low star rating implies a low price for hotel accommodation. Nevertheless, in terms of service, it is practically not inferior to status hotels. In this regard, on specialized resources you can find many positive reviews about this hotel complex:

  • the hotel rooms are spacious enough, they have everything you need to relax in comfort;
  • the furniture is quite new and is in very good condition;
  • the room is cleaned daily and includes mopping and dusting;
  • friendly and smiling staff, which is reasonably well explained in English;
  • regularly replenished cosmetic set;
  • every day a free shuttle bus runs from the hotel to the beach and back;
  • reasonable prices for accommodation.




Comments and complaints about the hotel

Crystal Plaza Hotel Sharjah received not only positive comments, but also a number of serious comments:

  • despite the fact that the furniture is quite new, its upholstery needs serious cleaning;
  • air conditioner filter is very dirty;
  • food in the hotel restaurants is quite monotonous, fresh and absolutely tasteless;
  • chefs are unclean, this is manifested in the fact that guests often find hair in their dishes;
  • the area on the outskirts of the hotel is very dirty and groomed;
  • in the bathrooms of many rooms there is a disease-causing mold;
  • construction is constantly underway near the hotel, which is why the rooms from the very early morning become unbearably noisy;
  • too cold water in the taps;
  • Hotel staff do not speak Russian;
  • despite the fact that smoking at the hotel is strictly prohibited, there is a persistent smell of tobacco smoke in the halls;
  • to get to the nearest public beach, you will have to overcome about 6 km (the road is especially difficult if there are traffic jams).

This hotel is a fairly budget option, if we talk about the Emirates, but for people accustomed to Turkey and Egypt, it may seem quite expensive for such a low level of accreditation.
