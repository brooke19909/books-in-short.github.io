City of Lucerne: reviews, attractions

Quite an interesting point for travel lovers is Lucerne. Reviews of tourists indicate that this is one of the best cities in Switzerland, as well as a major center of culture of the population that speaks German. Here you can relax without moving too far from the benefits of civilization.

Description

This locality became the first member of the Swiss Confederation. Lucerne is a city credited with the status of a wonderful resort. The transport network is very well developed. From here you can easily get to the center of the state.



There are many fascinating stories and legends about this ancient city. Even in the folk tale of W. Tell, this place is mentioned. Tourist activity in the city unfolded in the second half of the XIX century. The writer Mark Twain has been here. In one of his essays, he left a call to stimulate the development of the souvenir business in Lucerne, which happened later.

Occurrence

The city has a rather long history, since researchers claim that the foundation of the settlements took place under the rule of Ancient Rome, and possibly earlier. A monastery called St. Leodegar named after Gough opened in the first half of the VIII century. The first mention in the annals of this church dates back to 840.

A wall was also erected around the village and towers with fortifications, which increased the level of protection and security.





Position reinforcement

Another important date is the year 1332, when equality was established between villages and cities. For the development of the cantons, this was a rather important step. The power of the city only grew stronger, and after some time it gained the status of a city-state.

In 1386 there was a liberation from Austrian oppression, due to the victory at Zempach. In the XVIII century, the power of the patriciate was established here, which included 29 genera. In the Swiss Confederation, Lucerne, as its first member, was also given a privileged position.

In addition, its favorable location was taken into account. He had every chance to become the capital of the state, however, this role went to Bern. In the 19th century, the influence of the village decreased, but later on it became an important tourist destination and cultural center of Switzerland.





Transport

Many get here by flight from Zurich. You can also use the Moscow-Lucerne route provided by Aeroflot, other companies provide the opportunity to fly, making transfers. Then the tourists change trains and spend an hour on the trip. Departure interval - every 60 minutes. There is a railway connection with Interlaken, Bern, Lugano, Geneva.

Weather conditions

Climate plays a big role for everyone who is going to another city. What temperature conditions can characterize Lucerne? The weather here is relatively warm even in winter, since on average it is + 2 degrees during the day and slight frost at night.

There are practically no severe cold weather here, as well as excessively oppressive heat. On a summer day, most often around +22, and at night - +15, which can be called almost ideal conditions. In the cold season, local time is calculated in accordance with the time zone - UTC + 1. In the spring, a change to summer time is carried out. In the warm period, calculating local time, it is worth being guided by the data corresponding to UTC + 2.
