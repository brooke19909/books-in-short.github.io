"Blue Bay" - a water park in Yalta that tourists like

Resorts of Crimea are incredibly popular among our compatriots as an option of inexpensive and high-quality summer vacations. What is especially pleasing, in recent years this region can offer us not only the sea, the sun and the incredible beauty of nature, but also an abundance of modern entertainment for vacationers. Do not know what to do during your vacation in Crimea? Visit the water park in Yalta "Blue Bay".

What is unique about this water entertainment complex?

The name of the entertainment center was not accidental; it is located in the village of Simeiz, on the coast of the Blue Bay of the Black Sea at the foot of Mount Cat. The water park is unique in that today it is the only one in the whole of Crimea that uses sea water. The water entertainment complex even has its own water treatment station. Water is taken from the sea at a depth of 12 meters, 400 meters from the coast. The next step is filtering. The water park in Yalta is equipped with a modern purification system, water passes through mesh mechanical filters, and then is exposed to ultraviolet radiation. The safety of visitors to the amusement complex is also ensured by the special coating of the slopes and pools - Alkorplan, which minimizes the likelihood of injuries during the slopes.





"Blue Bay" - water park in Yalta: photos and descriptions of slides

They come to the center of water entertainments first of all in order to take a ride on various attractions. On the territory of the water park there are even slides the height of a seven-story building. The most popular slopes are: Boa, Kamikaze, Multipista, Tobotan and Virage. There are 11 slides in the complex. The water park in Yalta is an entertainment complex focused on family vacations. There are no age restrictions, but each parent must understand that he personally bears responsibility for his kids in such a place. For young visitors to the complex there is a separate shallow pool, which has four attractions.





Reviews of those who have already visited the water entertainment center

Should I visit the water park in Yalta? "Blue Bay" is a fairly large modern center of water entertainment. If you believe the opinion of tourists who have already managed to visit this complex, there are enough attractions on its territory and it is not boring to spend the whole day here. In addition to slides and pools, the water park has recreational areas on land, as well as a cafe and even a water bar. The complex offers scenic views. The water park in Yalta, "Blue Bay" boasts the use of purified sea water. And this means that while swimming here and descending from the attraction, each visitor will get the maximum benefit, and certainly will not smell the bleach. As for the cost of visiting, the prices here are reasonable.





Water Park in Yalta "Blue Bay" reviews of tourists receives only positive. People enthusiastically recall the wonderful time spent on its territory. It is a must visit if you are staying somewhere near the village of Simeiz during your vacation or plan to visit several cities in Crimea to get acquainted with local attractions.
