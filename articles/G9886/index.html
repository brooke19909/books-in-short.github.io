Guest House "Villa Yuzhnaya", Anchor Slit: description, services, reviews

The territory of the Russian resort called Greater Sochi is very extensive. There are many villages where a vacation in which will give true pleasure to all tourists. You can choose some well-known place, for example, Lazarevskoye, Adler or the Central District of Sochi itself.





And you can stay in some not very popular, but quiet and comfortable place. One of such areas of a large resort is Anchor Slit. Guest houses and hotels here are always happy for tourists and are ready to offer decent service for quite reasonable money.

A quiet vacation near Sochi

The anchor slot is a microdistrict that is part of the Lazarevsky district of Greater Sochi. It is located 32 kilometers southeast of the village of Lazarevskoye, and you will have to drive about 40 kilometers from Central Sochi from here.

The mild and very warm subtropical climate of this place allows vacationers to enjoy the wonderful weather throughout the summer and even the beginning of autumn. In general, the weather conditions in the Anchor Slit are not much different from the Big Sochi, but the number of vacationers here is clearly inferior to the neighboring resort.

Thanks to this, the Anchor Slot resort will be a great place for all those who need a quiet and relaxing holiday by the sea. This is a great opportunity to be alone with loved ones and loved ones, away from numerous eyes.





Here, all the infrastructure necessary for a good rest is well developed, so tourists will definitely not need anything if they choose the Anchor Slot microdistrict for their vacation. Guest houses and the private sector will offer housing for every taste, and finding a store or restaurant here will also be easy. All sorts of water activities are available at local beaches. However, for other vivid impressions and entertainment facilities, you will have to go to neighboring larger villages.

Hotel accommodation options

Since the hotel building is not very large, the number of rooms offered for guests is also small. It offers guests rooms of the category "standard", each of which is decorated in light pastel colors.

Tourists who come to the Villa Yuzhnaya Hotel of the Anchor Slot can live in double, triple and quadruple rooms, which have everything necessary for their vacation to go well.
