Ski complex "Listvennaya" - a full-fledged entertainment complex

For active recreation ski complex "Mountain Deciduous" provides a variety of services throughout the year. In winter, you can ski on the slopes. For professional athletes on the "Deciduous" there are their favorite dangerous slopes. In summer, you can ride a buggy, and bike lovers are offered two bike trails. Rest for every taste.

About the resort and how to get there

Ski complex "Listvennaya" is located in close proximity to the city of Berezovsky (6 km) and from the city of Yekaterinburg (15 km). You can arrive at the resort from Yekaterinburg along the Berezovsky tract, focusing on the Lukoil gas station. After it, turn right and go to the "Deciduous Mountain" sign. From the pointer 6 kilometers. You can come to the resort the second way, having left Yekaterinburg through the Uralmash microdistrict. Move along Cosmonauts Street, after the bridge - to the right, and you are on the bypass. Further move towards the village of "Sadovy", from it 8 kilometers are separated from the ski complex.

As already mentioned, the ski resort "Deciduous" is a year-round resort. For summer holidays, there are paintball, volleyball, basketball courts, there is a football field. Skateboards and mountain bikes can be rented. You can swim in the outdoor pool with a beautiful waterfall from the wall.



Those who wish can take a horse ride. There is where to make barbecue. For this, special places for barbecues are reserved. The complex has five Russian baths. There are numerous gazebos - open and closed. They accommodate up to 15 people. The administration of the ski complex provides the opportunity to conduct various events with the full organization of their conduct.

Ski area

The winter skiing season starts in November and ends in April. Ski complex "Deciduous" has the focus of the training and training center. No competitions are held here. There are a school and sports sections in the ski complex, where a team of instructors conducts classes on skills in skiing.

The complex has 3 tracks of varying complexity and designed for amateur skiing and beginners. The tracks have their own names. Track maintenance and rental points

Deciduous tracks are serviced by four ski lifts. Regardless of the weather conditions and the snow cover, the Deciduous system has an artificial snow formation system. Snow compaction equipment, retracks, regularly works on the slopes. There is night lighting on the tracks.



In winter, you can rent ski and children's sets for ski and ski hire at the Listvennaya ski resort, which includes skis, boots and poles. There is all this separately. For snowboarding at the box office - snowboards and boots for them. Helmets, goggles, skates and cross-country skis can also be rented.

Food and accommodation

The complex has two cafes. Dynamite is located on the top of the mountain. It is designed for 45 seats. The second - "Villa", has two floors, accommodates 70 people.

There is no own housing at the base. But there is a recreation center "Topaz" and "Svetlaya" next to the ski resort, where you can stay for a few days. Not far from "Listvennaya" there is also a club "Forest Glade", where there are residential houses and comfortable rooms in the photo below.

For nonresidents who wish to ride this ski resort, it is possible to stay at one of the hotels in Yekaterinburg or Verkhnyaya Pyzhma and come daily to the complex. This in time can take no more than an hour.
