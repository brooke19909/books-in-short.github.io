Biography

Mamut Alexander Leonidovich was born on January 29, 1960 in the Russian capital. His parents were experienced lawyers: mother Tsitsiliya Ludwigovna took an active part in the famous Uzbek cotton business, and his father, Leonid Solomonovich, successfully defended his doctoral dissertation.
