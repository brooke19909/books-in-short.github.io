The idea for Instagram: create and implement

"Instagram" is not just a fashion trend. This is a whole art that allows you to share important points, mood or even make money. Therefore, it must be treated accordingly. First of all, it is necessary to form an idea for Instagram. Unfortunately, many believe that it is enough to get a smartphone with an acceptable camera and a selfie stick to boldly upload your photos to the Web. This approach is largely doomed to failure. But what make your page popular?





The idea for Instagram is the key to success

Let's take a closer look at the basic principles of creating a photo for the named social network. Unfortunately, many novice users do not consider it necessary to trouble themselves and come up with photo ideas for Instagram. At best, they take them from the Internet. But how can you do something without understanding what exactly you want to achieve with this? After all, any professional activity or even a daily shopping trip follows a specific action plan. Therefore, to create a masterpiece, you must:

  1. Write what exactly you want to get as a result. What important elements should the photo contain.
  2. Decide on the place of embodiment of the idea for "Instagram".
  3. Take into account all the technical aspects of the matter: clarity, lighting, the presence of passers-by, etc.
  4. Choose the most optimal time for the implementation of your venture.




No one account!

One person - one account. So people are used to thinking, believing that scammers start a second account on the same social network. Naturally, such a mess discourages potential subscribers. After all, people want to find something that inspires them, and the need to constantly look for a thematic photo among images that are not interesting to them makes them quit viewing such a page.





Therefore, first of all, you should decide on the theme of the account in the "Instagram", to understand what attracts subscribers to it. And, if you have several leading topics (for example, you post photos of your culinary creations and cats on overexposure), then you should have a separate page for each direction. That is, for example, on the culinary account, you will upload everything related to the recipes you have implemented, and in the account with cats, those kids whom you want to attach.

Please note that if your professional activity also finds a weighty display on your page, and even career growth directly depends on advertising on Instagram, you should create a separate profile for work and a separate one for your hobby.

New original solutions

When creating an idea for Instagram, think about how you can look at familiar things in a new way. There are many solutions for creating a composition, but 4 of them are the most popular:

  • macro photography;
  • additional details;
  • unusual angle;
  • interesting places.




Macro photography allows your subscribers to take a closer look at miniature things. If you want to surprise the world with your unusual manicure - then take an enlarged photo so that people can examine in detail the smallest patterns on the nails.

Often, in order to give the composition originality and create a special mood, it is necessary to add additional details to it. For example, a music notebook will look very organically next to a pen decorated with antique.

An unusual angle will undoubtedly make your photo a hit with many likes. Indeed, the simplicity and originality of the new presentation of the old idea will appeal to many subscribers.

Well, the interesting places captured by you are ready-made hits. The brightest ideas for Instagram photos are old and proven methods:

  • capture the beauty of distant lands;
  • their color;
  • architecture
  • Landscapes
  • national dishes, etc.

What could be better ?! At the same time, images of places from rare exotic tourist destinations enjoy success.

Constantly evolve

Can't come up with an idea for a Instagram photo at home? Then subscribe to the pages of those people who inspire you. Looking through their creations, you will immediately see their strengths and weaknesses and understand how to best organize your composition. In addition, in this way you can make new friends who are interested in the same as you.

Do not forget about editing

Even if you are an ardent fighter for naturalness, do not neglect image editors. A small correction of the photo in terms of brightness and contrast will definitely not hurt him. Pay attention to the signature. They should be memorable and evoke desired emotions. Various curious expressions and aphorisms are especially popular.

Do not forget about humor. After all, people always lack vibrant positive emotions.
