Location, Territory

The recreation center "Factory of Rest" is located from the city of Barnaul, 30 km along the M52 highway towards Biysk. Upon reaching the sign (in front of the gas station), you need to turn onto the primer, let's say, not of the best quality. It is difficult to drive through it in bad weather. The office of the base, where prepayment for accommodation is made, in 2015 was located in Barnaul itself, in the Arlekino park on the part of Victory Insurance Company. The territory of the base is large and beautiful, many trees, green lawns, flower beds. For the convenience of holidaymakers, there is a parking lot, a pool with sun loungers set up around, a children's and sports grounds, cozy gazebos with barbecue facilities, a billiard room, a Russian bath with an equipped dressing room, a banquet hall, a cafe, playgrounds for playing table tennis, paintball and darts.

Accommodation and meals

1. Two-storey house with a furnished fireplace (10 + 1 extra beds) with all amenities, a set of upholstered furniture, a TV and a hairdryer.

2. Two-story house with a stove (5 + 2 extra beds). With this accommodation option, amenities are on the street.

The cost depends on the category of housing and the month and ranges from 1300 to 9000 rubles. per day per house.

Food on the basis of the "Factory of rest" (Barnaul) is carried out in a cafe. Meals can be ordered in the room. It is also proposed to take vouchers without meals or rent a banquet room without accommodation.

Leisure

Employees of the "Factory of rest" (Barnaul) are trying to provide their guests with a decent pastime. When checking into any type of house, a complimentary breakfast is offered. Its menu on Friday, Saturday and Sunday is various and includes milk soups, sandwiches, toasts, tea, coffee, pancakes. For entertainment at the base there is the opportunity to engage in cycling, play billiards, tennis, darts, relax in the pool, attend a foam party, take a steam bath with brooms in a Russian bathhouse, and then spend time in the waiting room for a cup of tea and watching TV. Also, everyone can cook their own barbecue in the barbecue area and just walk around the area, relaxing from the bustle of the city.
