Are you planning to go to Crimea to have a rest? Resort towns of the peninsula

Many Russians still like to relax in the Crimea. Firstly, there is no need to apply for a foreign passport, and secondly, there is no need to make a visa, I got on a train or plane at any time - and go! Thirdly, everyone there speaks Russian, although this is another country. And most importantly - the peninsula is directly intended for recreation. A rich history, excellent climate, unique nature - everything calls us to the Crimea. Resort towns are located along the coast, and it remains only to make the right choice.



There is a flat steppe, and mountains, and many kilometers of beaches, and forests, and a lot of sun almost all year round. That is, you can choose a place for a vacation for every taste. In contrast, for example, from the same Turkey or Egypt. In principle, every Russian citizen knows the resort towns of Crimea. Reviews about them can be heard from friends who have already rested on this Ukrainian peninsula.

Well, it's time to name the most famous resorts. These are Yalta, Feodosiya, Saki, Koktebel, Sudak, Sevastopol, Yevpatoriya and Alushta. Less famous, but also popular - Nikolaevka, Black Sea and Kerch. All these famous resort towns not only throughout Crimea are washed by the gentle warm sea and are ready to meet and receive vacationers and guests from all over the world year-round.

Nevertheless, all these settlements are fundamentally different from each other. If in Sevastopol you can count more than two thousand archaeological, cultural and historical monuments, that is, places where there is only resort infrastructure and nothing will distract you from a beach holiday. But no matter what you choose, you will breathe the cleanest air everywhere, you can take a boat trip, wander along the coast or enjoy a delicious dinner in one of the Ukrainian cafes.



Also, you should know when you arrive in Crimea: resort towns provide an opportunity not only to have a great rest, but also to improve your health. If you stay for a couple of weeks in one of the sanatoriums of the peninsula, you will get real treatment. It will be not only useful, but also enjoyable. After all, water from local mineral springs, mud baths and water procedures can work real miracles.

There is another way by which you can pre-evaluate the resort towns of Crimea. Photos of these places can tell you a lot. At a minimum, see their nature. Maybe you will like the area so much that you decide to stay in a tent near the beach.

Since Ukraine has become a separate state, many upscale hotels have been built on the peninsula, the level of service has grown significantly, and resort infrastructure has grown. Yes, in summer, at the peak of the season, vacation prices here increase significantly, in some places, for example, in Yalta, doubled. But all the same, the cost of the Crimean holiday is not more than in foreign resorts. And we already wrote about the pros.



On this we will end up exploring the Crimea. The resort cities of the peninsula, it should be noted in conclusion, are intended for tourists with different "wallet thickness". Keep in mind that at the peak season in Yalta you will have to spend a lot of money to get the most out of your vacation. Meanwhile, in Feodosia, a city located on the east coast, the water is much warmer than in other places, and rest is much cheaper. Relatively inexpensive, you can have fun in Kerch. If you come on vacation with your children, then Evpatoria will be the best city for you. All local resort infrastructure is focused specifically on family vacations. Everything is done here for children - and children's beaches, and attractions, and much more. We hope that you have helped a little with the choice of a place of rest.
