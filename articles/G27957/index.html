General Description of Cyprus Resorts

We will not paint Cyprus and say that everyone already knows: that this is one of the best places in the Mediterranean, that there is a safe and comfortable stay, a gentle sea, excellent service, effective thalassotherapy, a pleasant climate and excellent food. It is much more interesting to describe Cyprus resorts. Everyone will be able to choose a place to their liking, because this island is suitable both for family vacations and for the youth club “breakaway”.

Description of Cyprus Resorts: Ayia Napa

The youngest and booming Cyprus resort. It is located in the southeast of the island in the bay, where once there was a modest fishing village. Today, Ayia Napa is wide beaches with golden fine sand, a calm and predictable sea (thanks to the bay), hotels from two to five stars and unceasing music that sounds in countless clubs, karaoke bars, restaurants. Young people from all over the world come here to enjoy the vibrant club life, which does not stop even during the day. Family vacations are also possible here: a welcoming sea will not scare away even the smallest children. Every tourist will like diving, various water attractions, festivals and holidays. “Chip” resort - the largest in Europe, themed water park Waterworld Waterpark with scenes from ancient Greek mythology.





Description of Cyprus Resorts: Larnaca

Located in the south-eastern part of Cyprus, it boasts sandy beaches and pebble beaches. The sea near the coast is shallow, therefore it is always warm and calm. It offers vacationers - water rides, sporting events. Larnaca is great for a secluded or children's holiday. The resort is equipped with hotels mainly of 3 * category (the most popular - Beau Rivage) or villas on the coastline. There are no more hotels from the 5 * segment except Golden Bay. The resort's “trick” is the Funikoudes embankment with taverns, cafes, restaurants, pubs, which offers tourists a stunning view of the concentration of snow-white yachts in the Larnaca waters. Like all resorts in Cyprus, Larnaca reviews are mostly positive. If you want a quiet measured rest, this resort is suitable for you.






Description of Cyprus Resorts: Limassol

Limassol is located in the south of the island. The beaches here are with dark gray sand of volcanic origin, considered healthy. As in all resorts in Cyprus, the sea is calm, crystal clear. There are many water activities and attractions. The resort is mainly equipped with 3 * hotels in the coastline area. There is also 4-5 * on Dasoudi beach with spa areas and pools. The resort's place has a rich history, which is reflected in the most ancient monuments - they are both in the city center and in its environs. Limassol - it's carnivals, festivals, shows and active nightlife in stylish clubs. Kids will love the zoo, three water parks, amusement park.

Resorts of Cyprus: description of Protaras

Protaras is dotted with gorgeous beaches with delicate sand, framed by rocky bays. The resort is chosen by lovers of a peaceful and leisurely vacation. This is favored by pastoral landscapes, the gentle sea and cozy hotel complexes. It offers guests - water activities and attractions, taverns, restaurants, souvenir shops, discos. The obligatory program is a trip to Cape Greco, famous for its sea caves and stunning picturesque landscapes around.



Pathos

An ancient port city where everything breathes centuries-old history. The resort is literally dotted with exclusive architectural monuments, many of which are listed as UNESCO World Heritage Sites. The beaches of Paphos are mostly rocky, which makes it difficult to enter the sea. However, the resort is considered to be elite and aims at a respectable, relaxing holiday. The city has a lot of ancient picturesque streets, souvenir shops, a huge number of taverns, restaurants, cafes that serve "meze" - authentic snacks. 4-5 * hotels are located by the sea, but are often equipped with rocky beaches. Hotels of category 2-3 * are located further, but many have sandy beaches.
