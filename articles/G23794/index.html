More recently, vacation on the island of Mallorca was very expensive and could not afford most of our compatriots. However, recently, prices for various services for tourists in Spain have been significantly reduced. Today, virtually any average Russian can afford a vacation in Mallorca. There are, of course, a huge number of comfortable hotels in this resort. For example, some domestic tour operators work with a comfortable hotel Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 *.

How to get to the hotel

This large complex is located on the territory of the second most important resort city of Mallorca - Manacor. The nearest airport is 67 km from the property. A taxi ride to this hotel, therefore, can be quite expensive. However, in most cases, Russian vacationers book rooms in the Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 * complex not independently, but through a tour operator. Such companies usually provide travelers with a shuttle bus with an escort. That is, tourists who have bought vouchers do not need to think about how to get to the hotel.





Hotel Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 * was built on a small rocky hill not far from the Mediterranean coast. At the same distance from it are two of the most popular beach in Manacor - Cala Domingos and Cala Antena. The distance from the hotel to the sea is approximately 700 meters.

Complejo De Calas De Mallorca: a description of the beaches

Many tourists find the way from the sea to the hotel buildings quite tiring. After all, the hotel is located , as already mentioned, on the mountain. A long staircase connects the hotel grounds and the coast.

Just next to the hotel there are three fairly comfortable beaches. The nearest of them needs to be reached on foot in about 7-10 minutes. Near the first are two more larger beaches. They are further down the coast - behind the rocks.

Most of the guests like the first small beach, first of all, for their lack of population. The coast and the sea are very clean. The only thing is that a lot of algae sometimes appears in the water. This happens mainly when the wind blows from the sea. The small beach also has a small bar. Here you can order all kinds of drinks.

All the beaches located next to the Complejo Calas De Mallorca 3 * hotel are covered in fine sand. Sun beds and umbrellas on them are paid. But, according to most tourists, you can sunbathe in the sand by simply laying a rug or towel.
