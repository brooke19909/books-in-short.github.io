ATMs of Sberbank, Vladivostok: addresses and opening hours

In Vladivostok, all conditions are created for a comfortable life for people. This also applies to banking services. One of the most popular financial institutions here is Sberbank. Residents of the city who actively use credit cards and other cards should be well aware of the addresses of Sberbank ATMs in Vladivostok. How many are installed? What mode do they work in? We will try to figure out which terminals you can withdraw cash from.

In most cases, the operating mode of Sberbank ATMs (Vladivostok) is not round-the-clock, but we also found those terminals that allow you to withdraw or deposit cash even late at night. It is worth noting that some of these devices can be found in self-service areas in Sberbank branches. During office hours, all financial institution employees are happy to help. For night operations, you need to have a bank card with you.

The operating time of devices operating around the clock most often does not change even on holidays. However, sometimes it is better to clarify this issue on the bank's website or in a mobile application.

Sberbank ATMs in the Leninsky district (Vladivostok) can be found at the following addresses:

  • st. Neibut, house 53, building A. The device has a number No. 502792. Works around the clock. ATM No. 10447432 was installed at the same address, which also works around the clock.
  • st. Neibut, 51.
  • 47 Neibut Street, Building A.

All ATMs of Sberbank (Vladivostok) located in the Leninsky district, provide customers with the opportunity to make banknotes, as well as make cash and cashless payments.

The device at ul. Neibut, d. 51 operates in the operating mode of the installation site (store). This house is very long, there are four ATMs of various financial organizations on the ground floor. Sberbank's apparatus is among them. Available services: receiving and issuing cash, making cash and cashless payments.

An ATM on the same street in house 47A is installed near the Domovoi shopping center. This is very convenient for residents of the city, as they can withdraw the necessary amount of money when they come for shopping or for entertainment.

Centrally located

There are several ATMs of Sberbank in the center of Vladivostok. Payment device No. 44318 is located at ul. Mordovtseva, d. 3. It works around the clock. Here you can make cash and cashless payments. Payment device No. 48991 is located at ul. Fontannaya, d. 18. Available cash and payments.

Sberbank ATM in Vladivostok No. 60004769 is located at 45 Svetlanskaya Street. Financial transactions are available without presenting a card.

Please note that the terminals located on the territory of shopping centers often operate according to the mode of operation of the institution. If the ATM is located next to the round-the-clock supermarket, then it operates 24/7.

Located in the "Second River"

A complete list of ATMs in the area is available on the official website of the financial institution. It also provides detailed information about the services available.

With currency exchange function

Residents of the city can make financial transactions using a Sberbank ATM in Vladivostok at the address: 150 Century Anniversary Vladivostok Avenue, 150 V. This machine allows you to independently conduct currency exchange operations. Operating mode: round the clock. In addition to the currency exchange service already indicated, clients have the opportunity to deposit and withdraw money (rubles), to make cash and non-cash payments.

When exchanging currency, pay attention to the rate offered by the financial institution. Information can be found directly in the terminal itself.

ATMs with self-collection function

The apparatus No. 255162 at the address st. Strelochnaya, d. 19 operates in the operating mode of the installation site. This is a dairy plant. The following services are available: issue of banknotes (rubles), cashless payments. ATM No. 570560 at ul. Lugovaya, 91 also operates in the operating mode of the installation site. This is a Three Whale supermarket. Clients get the opportunity to withdraw cash from the card on their own, make payments of various kinds.

ATM No. 570570 is located at Naberezhnaya Street, building 10. It also operates in the operating mode of the installation site (TC "Parus"). Available services: issuing and receiving cash, making payments. The device No. 505088 at the address Verkhneportovaya street, 76 A operates in the operating mode of the installation site (MFC). Available services: cash deposits, payments of various kinds.

Carrying out operations without presenting a card

ATM No. 60006367 at Tramvaynaya street, house 16 allows Sberbank customers to carry out operations without presenting a card, withdraw cash, deposit money into the account.

The apparatus No. 60004770 at 42 Shilkinskaya Street, operates for both individuals and legal entities. The following services are available to customers: conducting operations without presenting a card, issuing and accepting cash, and making payments of various kinds.

It is worth noting that customers in some Sberbank terminals have the ability to withdraw and deposit cash to the card not only in Russian, but also in the most popular foreign currencies: dollars, euros. The cardless transaction service expands the range of clients' capabilities. The currency exchange function also allows you to exchange dollars for euros or any other currency without contacting the bank directly. The operation can be performed at any time convenient for the client, according to the operating mode of the terminal.

In total, the city of Vladivostok has more than 150 devices installed by Sberbank. A complete list can be found on the official website of the financial institution. It is worth noting that the operating mode of a particular ATM can change during state and local holidays. Also, the terminals may not be available due to insufficient cash in the device.
