Incekum Beach Resort Hotel 5 *: hotel description

Connoisseurs of natural beauty and the clean sea, as well as peace and tranquility often choose hotels located at the foot of the Toros Mountain for their holidays. Incekum Beach Resort Hotel 5 * boasts a spacious territory of 90,000 square meters. m with its own bay and luxurious sandy beaches. It is also worth noting the original architectural solution, which fits perfectly into local landscapes. The hotel was opened in 2004, and in 2011 a complete reconstruction was carried out.

Location

On the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea is the resort complex Incekum Beach Resort Hotel 5 *. This is the resort village of Okurcalar, which is 100 kilometers from Antalya International Airport. Nearby (6 km) is the Alarakhan fortress, and having overcome 30 kilometers, you can visit the Damlatash cave and fortifications.

Number of rooms

In 12 residential blocks of the Incekum Beach Resort Hotel 5 * hotel, 358 comfortable rooms are provided for guests. You can stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • The economy is designed to accommodate two guests in single beds. There are also bedside tables and a desk.
  • Standard rooms are designed for double and triple occupancy. Guests can be offered one and a half or double beds. There is access to a private balcony.
  • For large companies or families, two-room rooms are provided. One of the bedrooms has a balcony and is equipped with a double bed. The second is equipped with a pair of one and a half, as well as a folding sofa. Both rooms have their own bathroom.
