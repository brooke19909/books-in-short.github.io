Elisir 4. Hotel Elisir 4 (Rimini, Rivabella): reviews

The prestigious Hotel Elisir 4 * is located in an amazing place in Italy, replete with entertainment and shopping centers. The territory of the hotel complex merges with sand spits, which allows you to enjoy beach bliss plenty. The hotel has a Spa-salon - a place of great relaxation and restoration of vitality.





Hotel Location

Comfortable Hotel Elisir 4 * well located. It is located in the quiet quarter of Rivabella in the spa town of Rimini. From the doors of the complex to the beach framing the Adriatic coast, no more than thirty meters. The street where the hotel rises is two kilometers from the city center.

The central quarter is lined with alleys along which branded shops, gourmet restaurants and trendy clubs stretch endlessly. Entertainment venues are interspersed with original sights, which are unusual museum displays.





Tourists here stroll along the paths of the picturesque parks "Italy in Miniature" and "Fiabilandia", on the bridge of Tiberius and Cavu Square. Vacationers have fun with children on amazing attractions in the world of fabulous fun. Travelers have a great time at Arenas Water Waterpark, admire the splendor of Sigismondo Palace and shop at the Antique Fish Market.

Infrastructure

The hotel is equipped with a luxurious lounge, a beautiful lounge, a cozy TV lounge, a spacious veranda, a solarium, a beauty salon and a fitness center. The hotel complex is surrounded by a magnificent garden. Hotel Elisir 4 * has laundry and parking. Laundry and parking services are charged separately. Free internet is available in public areas. Rent a car and garage at the reception.

Apartments

The hotel Elisir 4 * (Rimini) has 44 beautifully furnished rooms. The elegant interiors of the hotel rooms shine with a delicate range of colors; coral, yellow and orange tones harmoniously merged in them. The designers refreshed the pastel palette of caramel shades with greenery of ornamental plants. Room cleanliness is maintained by daily cleaning.

Guests can make use of the telephone, high-speed wireless internet and satellite TV in their rooms. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with refrigerators, minibars, flat-panel TVs, desks and safes. They have access to the balcony. Bathrooms come with free toiletries, a bidet, shower and hairdryer. In addition, guests are given branded sets.





Rehabilitation in the spa

In the Elixir Spa-center of the Elisir 4 * hotel, a wonderful environment has been created for rehabilitation and relaxation. SPA treatments restore health, fill with vital energy and promote rejuvenation. Accumulated fatigue is removed by effective cosmetic and water procedures, as well as aromatherapy sessions.

Body and soul are filled with light sweet languor in the Finnish sauna or Turkish bath, whirlpool, from relaxing, water, aesthetic and lymphatic drainage massages. There is no private pool in the hotel. Therefore, guests are invited to go to the territory of the neighboring hotel complex Gaston, equipped with a pool. It is twenty meters from Elisir (Rivabella) 4 *. Pool services for vacationers there are free.





Nutrition

Culinary delights are offered in the restaurant and lounge bar. The Elisir (Rivabella) 4 * hotel has tariffs that imply only payment for apartments, and an all-inclusive system. At full board, guests pay only for specially ordered food and drinks that are not included in the standard menu.

For cooking, the Elisir 4 * hotel buys organic products. The buffet is richly served with dishes prepared according to recipes of international, Italian and Romagnol cuisines. The American Lounge Bar and Restaurant has a rich wine list offering locally produced alcoholic drinks.

Guests can enjoy light snacks and original refreshing cocktails at the bar. Coffee breaks can be enjoyed on the outdoor terrace with scenic panoramic views. Tourists, sipping a cocktail or enjoying aromatic coffee, make friends and talk slowly, gathered in a pleasant company.

Reviews about Elisir 4 *

The apartments in the hotel, according to tourists, are spacious, bright, well-groomed with small balconies or open areas. The bed and towel in them are changed three times a week. The bathtub is miniature, equipped only with a shower. A huge minus of the bathroom is the lack of hooks for clothes and towels.

The food is excellent in the Elisir 4 * hotel. The breakfast, lunch and dinner reviews are excellent. Breakfast includes yogurts, granola, cheeses, ham, boiled eggs, delicious pastries, fruits, vegetable salads and various drinks.

For lunch, according to travelers, they offer salads, 2 first courses to choose from (this is usually pasta with various additives), second courses with meat or fish and fruit desserts. Slices of fruit are beautifully served, poured with chocolate and toppings.

Dinners are richer than dinners. You can choose from three main dishes and a dessert (ice cream or pastries). Many guests refuse abundant dinners, preferring no less hearty dinners. The only thing that vacationers lack is slices of fresh vegetables (cucumbers, tomatoes and other fruits).

Vacationers note that on the beach "Mickey" for sun loungers and umbrellas charge a fee. It is advantageous to buy a subscription for a week. It allows you to save money and get a specific place on the beach. Entering the water here is gentle. Shoal stretches far into the sea. Water in the lagoons hardly reaches the knee.





All entertainment venues and large retail outlets are located in the center of Rimini. A walk to the lively central district takes 50 minutes, according to hotel guests. However, it can be quickly reached by bus. Near the hotel there are only fruit shops.

The hotel is ideal for families, travelers aged and those who prefer privacy, peace and quiet. Funky youth companies have more bustling hotels in Marino Centro.
