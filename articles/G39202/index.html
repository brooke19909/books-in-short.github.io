How to weave a keychain from elastic bands on the machine and without the machine?

Crafts made of rubber bands for weaving recently won their place among the already familiar items of everyday life. Often you can see on girls dresses, swimwear made of colored rubber bands or all kinds of accessories: from an umbrella to an evening bag. And how nice it is to receive a small souvenir hand-woven as a gift: a bracelet decorated with different figures, a ring with a woven bead, but for a young man you can give a keychain for keys to the house or car.

How to weave a keychain from elastic bands? Start

First you need to decide on the color of the rubber bands for weaving. Bright and rich colors are more suitable for girls, but a young man should choose a calm color - blue, white, black, dark green and so on.





First we‚Äôll look at how to weave rubber bands on the machine. We take 20 red and seven green elastic bands - the keychain will be made in the form of strawberries. We throw two green gum on the first columns of the center and the far row. The second pair - the same, but in the middle and middle row. We put two red elastic bands on the first two columns of the far row, then also on the second and third. We do the same with the central and first, second columns of the near row. We throw two more elastic bands from the second column of the near row to the third central one. Two red - on the third and fourth columns of the center, two more - on the fourth column of the center and the third column of the far row. Again, we throw two red ones on the second columns of all rows. We twist one more on the hook with an eight, put on the third columns of the center and the far row. We twist the last red one three times and put on the last column with red elastic bands.

Stage Two

We look further on how to weave a keychain in the form of strawberries out of elastic bands. Twist the green gum twice and throw it on the first column of the near row. We do the same with the distant first column. From the fourth column of the center we remove the elastic bands that are stretched from the third column of the far row, and we throw it on it. We do the same with elastic bands from the third column of the center, and after that we remove the elastic bands from the second column of the near row (we put them on it). We also act with elastic bands of the second column of the center. Next, remove the gum from the second column from the third column of the far row and throw on it. We do the same with elastic bands from the first columns of all rows. From the first column of the near row, remove the green rubber band and throw on the first column of the center. From the far we also throw to the center. We lower the hook into the central first column and from below we put on the last green elastic, reducing its ends from above and tightening the loop. Carefully remove all the gum from the machine without releasing the green. The strawberry is ready!

If there is no machine

If you don‚Äôt have a machine, don‚Äôt be upset. Further we will learn how to weave a keychain from elastic bands without a machine tool. This will require a hook, fork, iron ring and elastic bands of several colors (eight pieces each).





The first gum (for example, gray) is worn by an eight on a slingshot, four white - on the right and left horn. Above - the next eight-shaped gray elastic band, we hook the lower one through the white ones and fold it to the middle. We do the same with the second side. Add four elastic bands of a different color to the slings, then again the eight-shaped gray and then do everything the same as in the first case. When all the colors are on the horn, we put on another gray gum on top, throw off the previous gray on it, and then remove it from the right horn and throw it on the left. We put the two ends of the gray gum from the left horn on an iron ring and remove the finished keychain from the horn. So, we learned how to weave a rubber key chain on forks.

Meet the slingshot

In fact, there is nothing complicated in working with this tool. How to weave a keychain from elastic bands on a slingshot? Yes, just like on a fork. According to the previous technology, the elastic bands are thrown alternately into the center on each side of the sternum, fixed with one or two elastic bands, worn immediately on both ends of the tool. At the discretion, already woven paired rubber bands can also be thrown off not only to the center, but periodically removing them from one end of the slingshot and throwing them to the other end. Then weaving will be a little more difficult, and the keychain will turn out to be more interesting. By the way, the previous sample of the keyring can also be woven not with a fork, but with a slingshot. The only difference between how to weave a keychain out of rubber bands on a slingshot and how to weave a fork on a fork is that in the second case the tool is larger in size, which means that you can use more rubber bands in the weaving.

How to make an unusual keychain?

You can make a multi-function keychain. We already know how to weave a keychain out of gum without a machine tool, and you can replace the slingshot or fork with your fingers.





And this means that the keychain can be made anywhere and anytime. For example, using the above technology of weaving a simple keyring on a fork, you can weave an ordinary bracelet, using absolutely different number of colors in it. To connect such a bracelet is best with two plastic clips, for reliability, tightening the final rubber bands at their ends. Or a small ring is better, at least from plastic, even from iron. In this case, one end of the bracelet can be thrown off the clip or ring, eventually turning the bracelet into a simple keychain.





If you need several trinkets

How to weave trinkets from elastic bands so that they are exactly the same? And most importantly, how to do it quickly? Suppose you need to make several key rings for friends, but so that they are no different.





We take a hook and put fifty rubber bands of one color on his handle, and then the same amount, but of a different color. It seems that now the most difficult moment is ahead, because we already know how to weave a keychain out of elastic bands (we wrap it, throw it off, carry it over, twist it). But not in this case. Here we are preparing the simplest souvenir - a flower keychain. After a hundred rubbers put on the handle, we simply drag one that we firmly grip on both sides. You can diversify the keychain in the third color (taking 33 gum each) or wearing them through one. Such a sun-flower will certainly please anyone.

What other ideas could there be?

If you add a little imagination to an already familiar work, you get a completely different result. For example, how to weave a keychain from elastic bands so that it differs from others in its originality? Yes, very simple.





For example, take and braid the top of the key. Such a keychain will protect the key from scratches, and your hands - from touching cold metal. How to weave trinkets of rubber bands so that the recipient was satisfied, you can tell for a very long time. Starting with braiding the top of the key and ending with a cover for the alarm panel - there would be a desire to fantasize and translate fantasies into reality. It is worth adding a pair of eye-beads, a nose and a smile to a simple braid - and such a keychain will certainly cheer up its owner.

In the end

How to weave a keychain from elastic bands, we learned. In principle, nothing complicated. The main thing is to be patient and in no case to rush. But there are some more important criteria. Elastic bands should be selected high-quality, because if you accidentally pull it somewhere, the defective ones will simply break, ruining all the previous work as a result. Do not quit your job halfway: then it will be really difficult to remember from which column or edge you finished weaving, and remembering the whole process step by step will not be easy.
