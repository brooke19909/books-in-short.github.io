Hotel Esperos Village Hotel 5 * (Rhodes, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Recently, Greece has become a very popular tourist destination. Which, of course, can be understood. It has picturesque nature, clean beaches, the azure sea and excellent hotels. But in which hotel complex is it worth staying? Well, a good option would be Esperos Village Hotel 5 *. It has a lot of nice features and benefits.





Location

Esperos Village Hotel 5 * is located on the island of Rhodes. And to be more precise, in a resort called Faliraki. This town is located on the coast, in close proximity to the golden sandy beaches. From the hotel to the beach - within easy reach. As, in fact, to numerous clubs, restaurants, taverns and other entertainment establishments.

The airport is also pretty close. It can be reached in 15-20 minutes. Ten kilometers from Faliraki is the capital of the island, where tourists like to go for entertainment and impressions.

There is a bus stop near the hotel from where you can get anywhere - including the cities of Lindos and Rhodes. By the way, a local water park is located 500 meters from the hotel. All other attractions must be visited, but not for long - they are at a distance of 7-10 kilometers.

Service

On the territory of Esperos Village Hotel 5 * there is absolutely everything that you may need. In the lobby there is a chamber designed to store things, an exchange office, a 24-hour reception. Free public parking is available near the hotel.

There is a dry cleaning service, laundry service, a conference room, a business center, a mini-market on site, shops and even a church. Car rental is also available.

It offers non-smoking apartments, the ability to connect heating, a wake-up service, as well as the provision of individual safes. The staff speaks German, Greek and English. But, as the guests here said, some also know Russian. Many in Greece began to master it - this is due to the increased influx of tourists from Russia.





Entertainment

Of course, most tourists go to the Esperos Village Hotel 5 * to enjoy a vacation on the sandy beach, which can be reached in just a couple of minutes. By the way, those people who do not want to walk are taken to the sea in a free minibus. But the beach is not the only way to have fun here.

The hotel has a swimming pool, where evening entertainment programs are held. Lovers of physical activity will like the presence of a fitness center with fitness equipment. There is also a billiard room and tennis court. Guests can also soak in the jacuzzi or sauna. And the hotel also has a massage room - it is also worth a visit.

In Esperos Village Hotel 5 * (Rhodes) there is one restaurant and two bars. There you can taste delicious dishes and a variety of drinks.

The people who stayed here assure us that staying hungry will not work. Breakfasts are good, tasty, for an additional fee they offer freshly squeezed orange juice (one glass costs two euros). Served as a buffet.

For dinner, you can order dishes from the chef. For two more guests give a bottle of good wine. Guests can choose - pink, white or red. Cola, beer and many other drinks are also served here. On the tables, there is a bottle of water for everyone. Anyway, the choice is great. There are a variety of fruits - cherries, strawberries, kiwis, melons, watermelons. Accommodation options

In Esperos Village Hotel 5 * (Faliraki) there are apartments of different categories. The most popular rooms are double rooms, with one large 2 double bed, powerful air conditioning, a plasma TV with satellite channels and a CD player. There is also a safe, hairdryer and fridge inside. Naturally, the apartments are equipped with a bathroom with toilet and bath. Inside there are small hygiene kits for each guest, and they have everything you need for personal care. This soap, shower gel, shampoo and hair conditioner, cotton pads, hair caps. And also a nail file, a sponge for shoes and a set of threads. In general, everything you need. By the way, the toilet is equipped with a bidet, which pleases many.

How much will a week stay for two in such a room? Quite a little - about 30 thousand rubles, with breakfast. If you need half board, you will have to pay an additional 10,000 rubles on top.

What is worth knowing?

Attention should be noted several important nuances. For example, check-in starts at 14:00. But, if the hotel is not crowded and there are free apartments of the category that guests ordered, they will be settled.

It is important to know that the hotel is focused on recreation for adults. Only guests over 16 years old can be registered as guests. But then you can come to the hotel with pets. However, that the guest intends to take a pet with him, you need to warn the administration in advance, specifying the breed of the animal.

And when registering, you need to specify credit card information. The property is hosted by Diners Club, Visa, Master Card and American Express. Part of the funds may be blocked on the account until the arrival of guests - as a guarantee.
