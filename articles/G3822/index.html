The iPhone does not turn on: what is it connected with and how to fix the situation

It happens that on one far from perfect day, the owner is faced with the fact that his iPhone does not turn on. Usually this happens as follows: the device was turned off, and it cannot be turned on, since there is no reaction to pressing the power button. The first thing to do is connect the device to the charger and leave it for half an hour alone. When the time has passed, right in the charging mode, simultaneously press the Power and Home buttons and hold them for at least 15 seconds. Maybe the problem will be resolved at this stage, but if, despite the actions taken, the iPhone does not turn on, you will have to carry it to the service center for diagnosis and repair.

What is the cause of the trouble? And how much will her decision cost? There are several options. Let's try to figure out what could cause such a nuisance, and what to do if the iPhone does not turn on. By the way, this can happen with a brand new device, and with a phone released in 2008.

  1. The battery comes first under suspicion. It is she who fails most often. Moreover, in older devices, the battery usually just runs out, and the iPhone 3g does not turn on mainly due to factory defects made during the assembly of the battery. Despite the cost and good quality of most of the batteries, production is never complete without marriage, especially since these parts for phones are manufactured in China. If it is determined that it is the marriage that causes the problems, the device must be replaced. If the battery simply has outlived its life and can not cope with the load, you can replace it with a battery with increased capacity. It will cost about 2000 rubles.   2. The next most common problem is mechanical damage. Remember, maybe the iPhone does not turn on due to the fact that it was recently dropped? And not necessarily on the eve - some breakdowns occur after a certain time, and not immediately. Even if there is no damage on the case, this does not mean that everything inside the device is also good. So, what could have happened as a result of the blow:
    • there was a breakdown in the power circuit, repairs will cost from 1,500 rubles;
    • the charging controller has burned out, the replacement cost can vary from 1,500 to 4,500 rubles;
    • the motherboard has deteriorated, you can change it for 7500 rubles or more.
