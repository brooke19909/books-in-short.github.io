"Big stones" (recreation center): description, features and reviews of tourists

The north of the Leningrad region is famous for its natural beauties. There are many picturesque places, one of which is the coast of Lake Vuoksa. Near the reservoir is a tourist complex "Big stones". The recreation center has earned special recognition from the avid fishermen and hunters.

Description of the complex





The spacious tourist base consists of small cozy cottages made of wood, which are harmoniously distributed throughout the picturesque area. Each house has comfortable apartments, which consist of a living room and a bathroom with a toilet. In total, the base can accommodate 52 people. All rooms have all the necessary furniture made from environmentally friendly materials. Spacious beds, new furniture, necessary household appliances and appliances, clean air, amazing nature - the Big Stones tourist complex boasts all this.

The recreation center offers its guests a range of additional services, such as a Russian steam room, volleyball court, billiard room. Free parking is available. On the base, you definitely won‚Äôt be bored. Even when the weather is bad, you can have a great time in a closed area.

Fishing and hunting





Why do all lovers of good fishing strive to get into the "Big stones"? The recreation center is located near Lake Vuoksa, which is rich in fish. The depth of the reservoir reaches 25 meters. There is practically no vegetation at the bottom. Pike, burbot, perch, roach, bream and zander are well caught in the lake. You can get a good catch if you sail away from the coast by boat.

Big stones are also good for hunting. The recreation center is surrounded by a pine forest. It is better to bring equipment for hunting with you. By agreement with local rangers, you can find out the places most suitable for hunting.

To get to the base is not difficult. You need to get to the village of Krotovo, behind which is the recreation center "Big stones". Reviews about the complex can be found very different. But mostly travelers note a special cozy atmosphere and friendly local service. Well, fishing in these places is generally a pleasure.

In the Far Eastern part of Russia is another region that is quite promising in terms of tourism. The natural beauty of Primorye, the proximity of the Pacific coast make this region popular among tourists who want to relax away from civilization.

In Primorye there are many recreation centers that provide the opportunity to relax in comfortable conditions. Below will be described complexes located near the city of Big Stone.

Holiday Village "Pearl"





The tourist complex is located on the shore of Ilmovaya Bay. The base is surrounded by a picturesque natural landscape. Tourists live in a three-story building and small wooden cottages. The rooms located in the building are equipped with the necessary furniture and modern household appliances. There are rooms both with and without a kitchen. The bathroom is located separately from the building on the street. Wooden houses are divided by a partition into two rooms with a common entrance. The house is designed for three people.

Conditions for children and adults

The recreation center "Pearl" (Big Stone) offers its guests an active wellness stay. Guests have the opportunity to play sports on the equipped sports ground. For children there is a playground. For motorists free parking is equipped. Guest reviews of Pearls suggest that, in comparison with other places to stay (hotels, the private sector), a recreation center is best suited for a good rest in the fusion with nature. Big Stone is a city where there are many such bases.

"Aquarius" - a cozy recreation center





This complex works only in the summer. Vacationers are accommodated in double, triple and quadruple rooms located in the capital buildings. All apartments are equipped with necessary appliances. Only pleasant impressions remain with visitors to the city of Big Stone. The recreation center "Aquarius" is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. For self-catering, the double rooms also have a kitchen.

Base "Neptune" - the best offer for recreation





The base is located on the coast of Ilmovaya Bay. Nearby is a sandy equipped beach. For vacationers, there are three accommodation options. You can settle in two-, three- or four-seater houses. It is also possible accommodation in cottages. They have modern furniture and a kitchen. The windows of the 2nd and 3rd floors overlook the bay. In front of the cottage there is a lawn and a low fence. Vacationers can enjoy spending time on the open veranda. This accommodation option suits people accustomed to comfort.

At the recreation center, guests are offered various entertainment. You can go in for sports (football, volleyball and basketball). There are sports grounds for this. Also presented is the whole range of water activities (rides, diving, catamarans and gliding over the water surface on a paraglider). Fans of a quiet holiday can play checkers or chess. There is also a modern cinema.

The whole family should go on vacation to the Big Stone (Primorsky Territory). Recreation facilities here allow you to feel comfortable even the most recent children.

Boring holidays with children





The recreation center "Neptune" offers excellent conditions for families with children. For children, classes are provided at the playground. There is also a creative workshop in which children, under the guidance of qualified teachers, can master the skills of drawing and design. Children have the opportunity to ride on a swing or ride on an electric car. Why is the recreation center "Neptune" so popular?

The reviews of numerous visitors testify to the developed infrastructure of the base and the wonderful leisure that it provides to its guests.

A trip to the recreation center will be a great option for corporate relaxation, as well as staying in a large company. Therefore, if you need a good recreation center, Big Stone is a place that is definitely worth a visit. And this confirms a large number of people.
