Hotel Star Beach Resort 2 * (India / Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Star Beach Resort 2 * hotel is an excellent budget vacation option for those who want to get to know India more closely.





Resort Summary

Hotel Star Beach Resort 2 * (South Goa, Colva) is simply an ideal option for those travelers who dream to relax in one of the most famous world resorts without spending too much money on accommodation. In this hotel you will receive all the necessary services and amenities at a very reasonable price.

Despite its low status, the Star Beach Resort 2 * resort will delight you with a variety of services. Not only standard services are provided here, but also quite exotic procedures according to traditional Ayurveda techniques. It is also worth a visit to a local restaurant in order to feel for yourself the originality of local culinary traditions.





Resort Location

Star Beach Resort 2 * hotel is located in the southern part of Goa, literally on the shores of the famous Colva Beach, which is known for its white sands. Having overcome 6 km by any means of transport, you will find yourself in the city of Margao, which is considered to be the business and commercial center of Goa. Here you can visit many establishments, get acquainted with a number of attractions and make a variety of purchases. There is a railway station eight kilometers from the hotel, and Dabolim International Airport is 20 km away.

Varieties of numbers

The most important reflection of the quality of service in any hotel is its number of rooms. Hotel Star Beach Resort 2 * has the following types of apartments for guests:

  • Most of the hotel has standard rooms. They suggest double occupancy on a king size bed or a pair of single beds. These apartments have two price categories. The cost may vary due to the presence or absence of air conditioning.
  • Deluxe rooms are much larger than standard apartments. They also feature richer decoration and exquisite interiors. There is not only a bedroom, but also a small living room area with comfortable furniture and a kitchenette with a dining table. You can also choose a room with or without air conditioning.




Apartment Amenities

Speaking about the comfort of guests, it should be noted that in each of the rooms of the Star Beach Resort 2 * hotel there are the following amenities:

  • a large double bed or a pair of small ones (an extra bed is also possible);
  • a bedside table or a small dressing table;
  • in some of the rooms there is air conditioning, with which you can make the air temperature in the apartments cooler, while the rest has a ceiling fan ;
  • The bathroom has a shower and bath amenities.
  • a prerequisite is the presence of a refrigerator;
  • being in the room, you can enjoy watching cable channels, thanks to the availability of a fairly modern TV;
  • You can sit in the fresh air while admiring the surroundings, or you can dry bathing suits and towels thanks to the private balcony in the apartment.




Infrastructure facilities

In the resort Star Beach Resort 2 * (Goa), guests can take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • in a small business center, guests can access computer equipment, use the Internet, make copies of documents and send faxes;
  • for business events, a conference room is provided, where there is all the necessary furniture, as well as technical support;
  • round the clock reception staff are ready to answer your questions;
  • on the ground floor, in the lobby of the hotel, there is a currency exchange, which works for a commission ;
  • laundry staff will help you with stains on your clothes (ironing services that are wrinkled in suitcases or after washing are also provided here);
  • there is a medical office on the territory where a doctor visits several times a week (in case of emergency it is possible to call a doctor in an unscheduled room), however, this service is paid;
  • hotel guests have the opportunity to swim in a clean, albeit small pool with fresh chlorinated water;
  • there is a massage room in the hotel building, where you can get the services of specialists who know the basics of Ayurvedic techniques;
  • In the restaurant you can enjoy a buffet breakfast, and the rest of the time you will be offered Asian and international dishes.




additional information

Going on vacation to this hotel, you should familiarize yourself with some information regarding the rules of residence here:

  • no matter what time you arrive at the hotel, they will check into your room no earlier than two in the afternoon;
  • You must leave the room on the day of departure and hand over the keys to the administrator no later than 11:00, otherwise a fine will be collected from you;
  • since the hotel does not have children's infrastructure, as well as special furniture for babies, special benefits and discounts on their accommodation are also not provided;
  • in each room, only one extra bed can be provided;
  • pets are strictly prohibited;
  • This hotel is exclusively cash;
  • at the time of registration, you are required to present an identity card that contains your photo.




Positive reviews

Many tourists choose for themselves Star Beach Resort 2 *. Reviews about it may contain the following positive points:

  • the linen is very clean and fresh (although, from constant washing, it turned a little gray);
  • the beach is fairly close;
  • paying 10 dollars, you can not wait for the checkout time, but immediately check into your room;
  • the territory is quite clean and pleasant, with a garden and even an improvised tennis court;
  • There are two swimming pools with clean water - it's nice to swim in them.

Negative reviews

A number of negative impressions are caused by guests at the Star Beach Resort 2 * hotel. Reviews note the following points:
