Holidays in Italy will never be forgotten

Italy attracts many tourists with its rich history and culture, this is a real treasury of world art. Each city in Italy has its own image, its own unique features. Holidays in Italy will allow tourists to get acquainted with the cultural heritage of this country, broaden their horizons and leave an unforgettable experience. The main feature of this country is the cordiality and hospitality of local residents who know how to be carefree and have fun from the heart, which is manifested in street festivities and carnival processions.

Almost all travel companies offer tours to Italy. The price includes round-trip airfare, accommodation in a three-star hotel, comfortable bus transfers, excursions and a Russian-speaking guide. Last-minute tours to Italy will save money. At your request, you can choose the type of food: HB or BB, and for an additional fee you can apply for an Italian visa. True, due to the tourist attractiveness of last-minute tours to this country, there are almost no.

If the main tourist is a beach holiday in Italy, then you need to visit Rimini, which is the most famous resort on the Adriatic coast. Sandy beaches stretch along the coast for fifteen kilometers. Here you will find first-class hotels, restaurants and bars, discos and nightclubs, surfing and diving schools, yacht clubs and various attractions.

All this attracts a lot of young people here on vacation. But not only young people come to Rimini, but also many couples, some bring children with them. For children there are water parks, theme parks, dolphinariums and other entertainments. You can simply leave your child on the playground under supervision and take a quiet trip on a yacht or go diving.

Holidays in Italy will be incomplete without visiting the famous cities of Venice, Milan, Pisa, Florence.
