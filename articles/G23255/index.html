Laguna Mare 4 * (Bulgaria, Albena): hotel description, services, reviews

Modern Bulgaria is focused on a quiet and family vacation. Often tourists come here with children who are looking for an inexpensive place to stay. Albena is considered the youngest resort in the country. It is located on the territory of a nature reserve, so the air here has healing properties. High prices for accommodation - this is the only drawback of the resort. Tourists coming here with children often choose the Laguna Mare 4 * complex. What can he offer his guests?

general information

The main building of the hotel is located in a huge green park. The complex is located on the first beach line. The distance to the sea is only 130 meters. A leisurely step can be reached in just 2-3 minutes. The hotel has its own stretch of beach, so parasols and sun loungers are provided free of charge. Near Laguna Mare 4 * there is a shopping alley and a medical center. The nearest airport is near Varna, which is 45 km from Albena. Transfer to the hotel takes about an hour.





The hotel was built in 1971. Its seven-story building surprises travelers with its stepped architecture. It houses approximately 150 rooms that are designed for small families. You can come here with pets, but their accommodation will require additional payment. When you make a tour, you must always notify the administration of your intention. Check-in starts here at exactly 14:00. Checkout time here is noon. Tourists must leave their rooms before 12:00 or extend their stay for another day.

Housing stock

The hotel offers 155 standard rooms. All of them are designed for two or three adults, as well as two children. The living room has a double bed and two single beds. Some apartments have bunk beds or a fold-out sofa. Each room has its own furnished balcony and a combined bathroom. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe apartment is 18 square meters. Maids daily clean their rooms, and linen and bath towels are subject to change twice a week.





Each room has individual air conditioning. With its help, guests can independently adjust the temperature in the room. The bathroom has a hairdryer and toiletries (soap, shampoo, shower gel). Opposite the bed is a small TV with satellite channels in different languages. For an additional fee, tourists can make international calls by direct telephone. A minibar is delivered to your room upon request. It is empty, and filling it will also require a certain amount. Free of charge guests can use the wireless Internet.

Entertainment offered

Hotel Laguna Mare 4 * (Albena) offers its guests a varied entertainment program. It is designed for both calm and active recreation. The complex has a large freshwater pool. Tourists can take sunbeds and umbrellas for free. Towels and mattresses are issued only after making a small deposit. Wellness treatments for face and body are offered to guests by a local beauty salon. Tourists can book a relaxing massage, manicure, pedicure or visit the hairdresser. A gym equipped with modern equipment is open daily.





The complex has a team of experienced animators who conduct sports and entertainment events both day and evening. Rest with a child will be incomplete without active games. Tourists can take table tennis and beach volleyball equipment for free. You can also play golf and billiards. It is worth visiting the water entertainment center on the beach, where guests can go diving, windsurfing. Horse riding lessons and bike rental are available at an additional cost.

Facilities and service

The hotel pays a lot of attention to the development of local infrastructure. For his guests, he prepared a large number of services to make the rest even more comfortable. So, on the territory of Laguna Mare 4 * (Bulgaria), a small souvenir shop and a mini market are opened, where you can buy alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, light snacks, newspapers, beachwear and shoes. Next to the complex is a public parking lot, where for a small fee, tourists can park their car.





Reception is open around the clock. Here tourists can use the safe for storing valuables. The administrator will make a currency exchange at any time. Guests can take dirty clothes and shoes to dry cleaning and laundry services. The hotel building is equipped with three spacious elevators, as well as all the amenities that tourists with disabilities may need.

Supply system

The Laguna Mare complex offers guests an all-inclusive system. The restaurant serves buffet meals. It features a spacious common room, a separate non-smoking room and an outdoor terrace. Free baby and vegetarian meals are available upon request. All meals consist of Bulgarian and international dishes. The rate also includes local soft drinks, including alcoholic drinks. Freshly squeezed juices and imported spirits are available at an additional cost.





Wine, draft beer, concentrated juices, sparkling and mineral water can be ordered in the lobby bar, which is located in the lobby of the Albena Laguna Mare 4 * hotel. Near the restaurant there is also a small buffet serving salads, pizza, light snacks, desserts, fruits and ice cream. The all-inclusive system is open until 23:00. If you want to purchase drinks later than this time, you will have to pay for them separately.

Children's holiday

Tourists coming to rest with a child can count on a discount. So, children under two years old, placed in a cradle, can stay at the hotel for free. A cot is available upon request from parents. The restaurant is also equipped with high chairs for feeding.





The children's pool has a shallow compartment where kids can swim next to adults. Additionally, it is equipped with small water slides. For children over four years of age, there is a mini club that organizes sports events, contests, quizzes and useful lessons for them. In the evenings, children's discos are held. Toddlers can also visit the local amusement park. It is located directly on site, but attractions are only available at an additional cost.

Positive reviews

Reviews of tourists are considered an important indicator of the quality of the provided vacation. Opinions of guests about the Laguna Mare 4 * hotel are usually positive. They note the following advantages of the complex:

  • Smooth entry into the water on the beach. The sea here is very shallow, so it is perfect for children.
  • Beautiful territory of the hotel, which is surrounded by greenery. Everywhere is growing a huge amount of color, including roses.
  • Varied and tasty food. Several types of meat were served daily, as well as vegetables, fruits, fish and seafood.
  • A huge amount of entertainment for children. The guests appreciate the work of the mini-club and local animators.
  • On the beach there is a free library, where there are books in Russian.
  • The hotel has a common territory with two more tourist complexes, so vacationers can freely visit their territory, pool and even a restaurant.
  • The pool is constantly cleaned of debris and fallen leaves. The water in it does not smell like bleach.




Negative reviews

It is unlikely that there is a hotel, a vacation in which absolutely all tourists would like. The Laguna Mare 4 * complex was no exception. Responses to living here describe many shortcomings. Guests indicate its following disadvantages:

  • The room has a pungent smell of dampness. She dried clothes very slowly.
  • Cleaning ladies only make the bed and take out the trash during cleaning. The floors do not wash. Bed linen is not changed on time. For cosmetics you have to go to the reception yourself.
  • The furniture in the rooms requires updating. The beds creak heavily, and the door to the balcony opens with great difficulty.
  • The staff is not friendly. They do their job well, but they do it without much enthusiasm.
  • Poor soundproofing. Well heard guests from neighboring rooms
  • There are always a lot of people on the beach and pool, so not everyone has enough sun loungers. You have to get up early in the morning and occupy them in advance.

Draw conclusions

The tourist complex Laguna Mare 4 * is perfect for a quiet and comfortable stay. Thanks to a lot of entertainment, it will be a good option for families with children. Also, elderly people and friendly companies often come here. The cost of accommodation here is quite high, but it is fully consistent with the amenities provided.
