How much does an engineer earn in different regions of the country?

The profession of an engineer is considered to be very popular in almost all sectors of the national economy. This type of activity has many directions. It can be mechanics, oil workers, designers. Depending on the specialization, the salary of an engineer may be different.

Profiles





The profession has many directions. How much an engineer earns depends on his specialization. In Russia, professionals have approximately the following income:

  • drilling specialists - 119,000 rubles;
  • oil industry workers - 100,000;
  • designers - 100,000;
  • directional drilling specialists - 96,000;
  • drilling technologists - 95,000;
  • geophysicists - 65,000;
  • food technologists - 60,000;
  • builders - 57,000;
  • chemical chemists - 34,000.

Each sphere is in demand, since all specialties are needed in the national economy. Salaries for all are different depending on work experience, type of enterprise, list of responsibilities.

So, the highest income among employees of resource-extracting industries. Low salaries for chemists, builders and food processors. Therefore, how much an engineer earns depends on the scope of the enterprise. The average income of specialists is 35 - 37 thousand rubles.

After graduation, each specialist has a job opportunity, since this profession is in demand in many industries. Moreover, with her there is an opportunity for career growth, which guarantees an increase in income.

Salary level in Moscow

The salary of an engineer differs in the regions of Russia. This is determined by the level of the living wage, the demand for the sphere and other factors. But, despite the difference, the profession remains popular everywhere.





How much do engineers earn in Moscow? In the capital, the income of these specialists is the highest. Its average size is 48,000 rubles. In Tyumen and other oil and gas regions of the country, the average salary is 40,000 rubles.

To identify the average income, not only mineralization specializations are taken into account, but also other areas. It is precisely because the salary of the technologist, builders and food industry employees in Moscow is higher that the overall income level in the capital is higher. Although drilling professionals in Tyumen, designers and oil workers are considered the most paid.

Salary in other regions

How much does an engineer earn in St. Petersburg? The income of employees is in the range of 40,000 rubles. In the Northern capital, salaries are higher than the national average. How much does an engineer earn in other regions? The income of specialists is as follows:

  • 34 000 rubles - Khabarovsk;
  • 33,000 - Irkutsk;
  • 32,000 - Samara;
  • 30,000 - Nizhny Novgorod;
  • 29,000 - Novosibirsk.




As can be seen from the statistics, the Volga Federal District follows Moscow, St. Petersburg and the oil and gas regions . This is due to the fact that it has developed infrastructure, and therefore it is considered promising.

Specialty Benefits

If we compare the profession with others, we can conclude that the engineer is a fairly well- paid job, in addition, the profession is considered prestigious.

Nevertheless, before making a choice in favor of the specialty, one must take into account that this work is very laborious. Its implementation requires a lot of knowledge and great responsibility, since the income of the enterprise and employees depends on it.

Salary of chief engineers

How much does the chief engineer earn? The average income of such a specialist in Moscow is 110 000 rubles, in St. Petersburg - 100 000, in Volgograd - 65 000, in Yekaterinburg - 85 000, in Krasnoyarsk - 87 000. It should be borne in mind that the position requires workers with an experience of 5 years . Mandatory requirements include the availability of higher education.





The employee should be well aware of SNiPs, GOSTs, instructions, thanks to which all activities are ensured. The responsibility includes the possession of modern software, engineering systems and management techniques. In the absence of work experience, applicants can apply for a salary in the range of 60 - 80 thousand rubles.

A slightly higher income for employees with experience of 1 year. Employers more often choose job seekers with completed projects. The experience of approving the created documentation in operational institutions will be useful. Applicants for the position need to be prepared for business trips. Often requires a personal car that will be used for business purposes.

The highest income is received by employees of large enterprises. Valued specialists who previously ran a staff of 500 people. The maximum income of such workers is 180,000 rubles.

Software Engineer Salary Level

The active development of the Internet has led to a shortage of various specialists in the labor market. The salary of software engineers is affected by the availability of skills and work experience. The specialist must be fluent in English, programming languages. Higher education is also important.

How much does a software engineer earn? The income of such an experienced specialist is 170,000 rubles. The more requirements for a candidate, the higher his income. It also depends on the tasks performed by the software engineer.





There are many other areas of engineering training. This may be a builder, technologist, cadastral specialist, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, oilman. Professionals are required in the railway and biomedical fields. All directions always remain in demand, so finding a person in any field will not be difficult. Moreover, in each area there is a career growth prospect.
