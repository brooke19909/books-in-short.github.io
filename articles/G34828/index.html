The resort offers to enjoy a relaxing holiday away from the bustling central streets. The institution is a 10-minute walk from the city center and the market, the distance to the beach, without rushing, can be overcome in 8 minutes.

Sanatorium "Miskhor "


The resort complex is located in the city of the same name: Alupkinskoye Shosse, 9. In the summer season of 2015, prices for one-day accommodation for one person are from 1,500 to 1,900 rubles in March and up to 2,600 rubles in July and August. The price, in addition to accommodation, includes four meals a day, the use of the beach and treatment of your choice. For children from 2.5 to 4 years up to 65% discount.





Health complex "Horizon"

The boarding house is located at: Sudak city, Tourist Highway, 8.

The territory has an outdoor pool with warm water from the Black Sea. It will appeal to all lovers of an active lifestyle, as the resort has all kinds of popular sports equipment.

Prices for accommodation are from 1100 rubles per day.

Pension "Swallow's Nest"

The location of this resort complex is the city of Sudak, Gagarina street, 55.

Guests are offered accommodation in double and triple rooms, as well as in separate triple houses. The maximum price for accommodation in a triple "suite" is from 3500 rubles per day.

"Sun Stone"

This wonderful sanatorium, located in a luxurious forested area, is located at: Sudak city, Morskoe village, 1 Cote d'Azur street.

The cost of accommodation per person per day is from 1220 rubles for a double room to 1567 rubles for a double "suite" in May. In September, prices are slightly higher - from 1830 to 2091 rubles, respectively.

"New World"

The resort complex is located at the address: Crimea, Novyi Svet village, Lev Golitsyna street, 7. The sanatorium specializes in improving the health of people suffering from diseases of the respiratory system.

Prices per person per day, excluding meals, range from 700 rubles (in May and October) to 4,000 rubles (from June to September), depending on the category of room. If you add food to the cost of the permit, the surcharge will be 600 rubles per day.

Excursions

Do not forget everyone who comes to the city of Sudak: Holidays in the Crimea are not only beaches, but also an extensive excursion program offered by tour guides working in all sanatoriums and boarding houses.
