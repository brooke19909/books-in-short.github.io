Hotel description and room stock

The chic hotel complex includes more than three hundred rooms designed for a family vacation in comfortable conditions. This is the Standard room (24 m 2 ) and Family room (31 m 2 ).

The rooms are decorated in oriental style, taking into account all the needs of potential tourists. Each room has air conditioning, a TV and household appliances necessary for living. In addition to the standard set of services and the necessary items for each room, there is a list of bonuses provided for a fee. These include the use of safes and enhanced comfort conditions, which are at the request of customers.

Each room includes a mandatory twin bed or one king size. The staff can establish an extra bed for which no additional charge is required. In addition, all guests of the establishment can use the minibar, which you will have to fill out on your own at your own discretion or order drinks according to a separate price list. Televisions are in every room, broadcasting programs is via satellite.

The institution has three special rooms for people with disabilities. But they need to be booked in advance so as not to be in an awkward situation during the holidays.

Excursions from the hotel on the island of Djerba

If some tourists dream of quickly stretching out on sunbeds, others love to attend excursions. At the Velcom Meridian Djerba Hotel you can buy excursions from the guide or from local reliable sellers. This will help make your vacation more diverse and memorable.

Gelal Museum

This is a large and interesting museum that tells the story of the life of local residents. In each separate room there are figures of people who are engaged in everyday activities.

Not far from the museum is a pottery workshop. The locals of Gelala are the forefathers of pottery on the island. Here they will tell what and how to make dishes and other items. And for a fee they will give an opportunity to try to create figures themselves.

Sahara Desert

One of the most exciting and interesting excursions on Djerba is a trip through the Sahara desert. A trip to the desert will not seem easy, and a two-day walk is possible. But it's worth it, along the way you can find places where episodes were filmed from Star Wars. Horseback riding and camel riding, the opportunity to see the Berber houses and capture the sunrise in the Sahara will fill life with new sensations.

Crocodile farm

This is a whole complex that accommodates about 4,000 thousand crocodiles of various species. Here you can see both huge individuals and very small ones with whom there will be an opportunity to take a picture.

Special attention deserves the moment when the crocodiles are fed. You can see a huge number of individuals, and feel the full power of reptiles.

The village is quite small, and accommodates several Berber houses. These are mainly farm workers.

Cities Hum Souk and Medun

Mostly these towns visit to buy souvenirs and various spices. Here you can taste the real food of the inhabitants of the island. Prices will also pleasantly surprise, for a relatively low cost, you can buy souvenirs and food.

ATV rides

Here lovers of drive are waiting for a whole uncharted road. During the entire route you can go around the whole island. Visit villages, ponds, and steep closed roads. During the trip, you can take pictures in the most unusual places.

Sidi Mehrez Beach

The most beautiful and clean beach on Djerba. It is a good place to relax. Here you can try a variety of fruit shakes, enjoy the warm sea water. This is one of the oldest beaches on the island. There are places along it where you can quench your thirst and have a bite to eat.

The beach itself stretches for almost 13 km. It has a developed infrastructure, so hotel guests can sunbathe in different places while relaxing.

El Ghriba Synagogue

According to legend, a sacred stone fell into this place, this marked the importance of the place. The building will not cause strong emotions, but the inside is amazing. The walls inside are made of decorated panels. The temple also holds sacred records.

Water entertainment

Riding on various water pillows, tablets and jet skis is worth the money spent. During the walk you can meet dolphins, visit a desert island and try yourself as a fisherman.

Camel and horse riding

This is perhaps the most common tour. Available in almost every town or village. If you are tired of the hustle and bustle, it is worth a ride on calm animals in the quiet corners of the island.

The island is really impressive for its attractions. And every year the number of tourists increases several times.
