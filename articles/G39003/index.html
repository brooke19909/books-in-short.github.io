Knitting needles in one turn: dimensions, pattern and width

For more than one season, fashionistas and fashionistas all over the world prefer to insulate themselves with such a stylish accessory as snood. The difference from the usual scarf is that it does not need to be tied, the ends of the snood are connected in one warm and cozy ring. This does not mean that there is no way to experiment, such a scarf has a lot of options on how to wear it. It can replace a hood, a wrap on shoulders, it can be tightly wrapped around the neck or created with its help a fashionable sloppy image.

Snood can be purchased at any store both mass-produced and the luxury segment, but it is much more pleasant to create accessories with your own hands. Such things are always unique, ideal for you in color and size. With the onset of cold weather, it's time to learn how to knit a snood in one revolution with knitting needles, and not only.





Snoud models

Scarves are divided not only into light for off-season and warm for winter, but also into long and short, narrow and wide. Single loop (Single Loop), or snood in one revolution, knitting or crocheting knits very quickly, it is convenient to wear, as you do not need to wrap around the neck. Such a scarf looks like a pipe, it covers the whole neck. Some models can even be worn on the head instead of a scarf or hood; they are wider.

There are also longer snoods that can be wrapped around the neck two or more times. Scarves that are too long are not very comfortable to wear; they look bulky. The size of the snook in two turns is usually 90-100 cm.

A variety of patterns of patterns for scarves allows you to create a unique accessory. It can be delicate lace or rough ‚Äúgrunge‚Äù knitting, it all depends on your preferences and personal style.





Which yarn to choose?

If you knit a scarf in spring or early autumn, then cotton yarn is ideal , in combination with acrylic. It is not as warm as wool, and you will not be hot in the room. In addition, it is natural, does not irritate delicate skin and does not cause itching.

In winter, you can not do without a warm woolen accessory. True, natural wool can be prickly, so choose a mixed yarn, for example, wool + acrylic or mohair + acrylic. The most pleasant types of yarn to wear: the most gentle kid mohair, alpaca, merino, angora, cashmere.

Knitting needles

Having decided on the type of yarn, we choose knitting needles for work. What tool to choose to knit a snood in one revolution with knitting needles? For work, you can use both simple straight knitting needles and circular. In the first case, the ends of the snood will need to be sewn or connected using a hook, in the second case, knitting goes in a circle and the product is obtained without seams.

Choose the thickness of the needles based on the recommendations for the selected yarn. But if you want the scarf to be softer and loose, take the knitting needles one or two numbers thicker than recommended. The same advice can be given if the accessory is made of mohair.

Choose the material of the knitting needles from your preferences: plastic, metal or bamboo. The main thing is that they are straight and have no burrs. At circular knitting needles, make sure that the cable is firmly attached to the base of the knitting needles, and the joint is strong without a gap, otherwise puffs on the yarn cannot be avoided.





How to choose a scarf size?

The width of the snooze in two turns with knitting needles is usually from 30 to 50 cm. The larger size is suitable for a scarf-pipe in one revolution. Such a snood can be worn on the head; it will replace a hat or a hood. This accessory looks very feminine and does not spoil the hair. Choose high-quality natural yarn so as not to injure or electrify your hair.

It is better not to make the long and short scarves too narrow, as they will not properly cover and warm the neck.

Knitting pattern for a long scarf

The most ideal pattern for a long snud is double-sided, which looks beautiful on both sides. If the wrong side of the picture is unsightly, then the accessory will have to be constantly adjusted so that the inside is not visible. The "pearl" pattern looks beautiful, as well as almost all types of elastic bands - English, French, Polish. The latter looks most original.

How to knit a snood in two turns with knitting needles with a Polish gum? To get a scarf with a length of 100 cm, put on circular knitting needles from 60 to 80 loops (depending on the thickness of the yarn) and connect the knitting into a ring. The rapport of the picture is 4 loops, so the number of loops drawn must be a multiple of 4.





  • In the first row we alternate three facial loops and one wrong side.
  • In the second, we knit two facial, then one wrong side and one facial loop.
  • The third row repeats the first.
  • The fourth is identical to the second.

Knit according to this scheme until the size of the knitting needles in two turns reaches the desired size according to your idea, ideally 40-50 cm.

Knitting pattern for a short scarf

Knitting needles in one turn can be knitted with a beautiful openwork pattern. The wrong side of the product will not be visible, so the two-sided pattern is not fundamental. The ideal length of such a scarf is 50-60 cm.

Openwork patterns are best knitted from yarn of medium thickness, about 300 m per 100 g. Also for work you will need circular knitting needles suitable for yarn (No. 4-4.5) and a knitting pattern for knitting. The scarf will have two turns or one, it doesn‚Äôt matter for lace, such patterns look beautiful on any length of the scarf.





The rapport of this pattern is 15 loops in 16 rows. Since knitting goes in a circle, we read each row of the pattern from right to left.

  • Type on the knitting needles the number of loops that are multiples of 15, for example, 165.
  • In the first row you need to knit two wrong stitches, eleven front stitches, three stitches with a left bend, double crochet, front stitch, double crochet. We continue this way to the end of the row.
  • We knit the second and all subsequent even rows according to the figure.
  • We start the third row with two wrong loops, then seven front loops, three with an inclination to the right, PL, yarn, PL, N, LP.
  • In the fifth row we knit two PIs, five LPs, three loops with an inclination to the right, two LPs, N, LP, N, 2 LPs, 2 PIs.
  • The seventh row also starts with 2 PIs, then 3 LPs, 3 loops with a slope to the right, 3 LPs, N, 1 LP, N, 3 LPs, 2 PIs.
  • We start the ninth with 2 IR, N, 1 LP, N, 3 P with an inclination to the left, 9 LP.
  • The eleventh row we knit 2 PI, 1 LP, N, LP, N, LP, 3 P with incl. left, 7 LP.
  • We start the thirteenth with 2 PI, then 2 LP, N, LP, N, 2 LP, 3 P with incl. to the left, 5 LP.
  • The fifteenth we knit 2 PI, 3 LP, N, LP, N, 3 P with incl. left, 3 LP.

Continue knitting according to this pattern to the width you need.
