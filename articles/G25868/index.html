Ostashkov hotels: how to get? Reviews

Numerous islands with picturesque shores, places for pilgrimage, beach vacations and excellent fishing conditions - every year tens of thousands of travelers come to Lake Seliger. Wildlife lovers spend the night in camps, and for those who value comfort and coziness, Ostashkov hotels are open. This small town captivates with its provincialism and measured way of life.

Far from the metropolis

Ostashkov is often called the capital of Seliger - from here you can get to the main attractions of the lake on a boat, enjoy the architecture of the 18-19th century, enjoy plenty of blueberries, smoked fish and mushrooms, or just relax from the frantic rhythm of life.



It is interesting that the city is at the same distance from two megacities. The bus journey from St. Petersburg takes 7 hours, and the journey on the Moscow-Ostashkov train will last 12 hours.

An ideal option for a trip would be a personal car, because the most interesting places are scattered over tens of kilometers. Public transport here runs on schedule - sometimes you can only get from one village to another by morning bus.
