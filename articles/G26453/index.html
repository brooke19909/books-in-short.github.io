Summer T-shirts for girls: hook, yarn and fantasy

Summer, sun, light breeze. Nice to dress so that it is spacious. Knitting can help with this, namely the ability to independently make all kinds of T-shirts for girls. The hook here will be a great helper than knitting needles. With it, the products are more airy.





Option in which there are no seams

This knitted T-shirt for girls (crochet) is created from top to bottom with a single canvas. She will need acrylic or cotton yarn. If the density of the thread is 250 meters per 50 grams, then knitting a product for a girl of five years will require two skeins. Perhaps a little more if you want to make it a little longer.

Another handy hook number 1.5. They need to dial a chain, which consists of 172 loops. These include those that go to the straps and yoke front and back. For the first, 49 are allocated, the second - 37 loops.

Odd rows consist of double crochet in each loop. In the corner elements will be located two columns of two adjacent loops, separated by two air.

In even corners, you need to connect the same way, and the main figure consists of alternating an air loop and a double crochet.

It is recommended to tie 9 rows in total. Then the product should be folded in half so that the back coincides with the front and the straps remain on the sides.

Further knitting goes in a circle. Now you need to make chains of 14 air loops to close the back and in front of the T-shirts for girls (crochet). Knitting patterns of the pattern can be chosen at the discretion of the needlewoman. Finish the product at the desired length. The bottom can be tied with a step of the dog to give it a neat appearance.





With circle straps

To knit this T-shirt for girls (hook No. 1.75), cotton yarn is required. If the weight of the skein is 100 grams with a thread length of 230 meters, then they will need two.

The front and back are made in a single cloth in a circle. The beginning of knitting in a set of chains sufficient to encompass a girl's figure.

Then knit to the armhole with the selected pattern. It may be the simplest, because all the charm of this summer T-shirt for girls (hook) in straps. By the way, it is possible to use sectional dyeing yarn. In this case, the entire product should be made with double crochet alternating with air loops.

From the armholes, finish knitting the back and continue to knit the T-shirt in front so as to reduce in each row alternately one or two loops. Until there is enough length to fasten the straps.

For them, you need to dial 8 loops and lock them into a ring. Knit a circle of two rows. Run it with double crochet. In the first there will be 16, and in the second 32 (each vertex has two columns). Fasten the thread. Such circles may require from 10 to 12, depending on the age of the girl. Then sew them together, fasten along the top of the front of the product and to the back.





Simple T-shirt decorated with applique

It can be connected in a circle or in two parts. In any case, you get a beautiful summer T-shirt (crochet) for the girl. The main thing is that the needlewoman should be comfortable and comfortable knitting.

Getting started in chaining. Only in the first case, they need so much to grasp the hips, in the second - half as much. For the indicated figure, the number of loops should be divided by four.

Knit the first two rows with single crochet. All subsequent to the armhole will be the same. Third row: three air loops for lifting. Then two double crochets from one, between them two air loops. The following are the same columns with air loops in the fourth loop. Fourth and subsequent: 3 lifting loops, in the arch of air loops the same element (double crochet, two air, double crochet).

Tie straps with the same pattern. They need to start from the front and fasten to the back.

From the remains of bright yarn, knit flowers and petals. You can also ladybug and sew them on the chest.





With vertical pattern

The beauty of this T-shirt for girls (hook) is that it expands downward. And knitting goes from top to bottom. To make the product look neat, it is better to knit it with one cloth in a circle.

First you need to dial the chain and close it into a ring. It is required to take into account that the pattern will be repeated every 12 loops.

The first circle: three lifting stitches, 4 double crochets in 4-7 loops, in the 8th double crochet, three air stitches and another single crochet, in 8-12 again with a double crochet.

The second circle repeats the first, only two columns, separated by three air loops, need to be knitted in the resulting arch.

Third to fourth: repeat the second round.

Fifth circle: in the arch, knit not one column on each side, but two. So will increase the bands between the arches.

Sixth-eighth: repeat according to the drawing.

Etc. In each first round of four add one additional column in the arch. And three knit according to the drawing.

The straps are formed from double crochet knitted in the right number of rows. And their number is such that there is a comfortable width. For a little girl, you can stop at five.
