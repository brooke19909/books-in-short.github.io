How to make beautiful flower rims with your own hands

The more unique a thing is, the more interesting and attractive it is. That is why handmade headbands with flowers look one level above their store counterparts. Such accessories will always be in fashion, so you should learn how to make them yourself.

This decoration has become an integral part of the female image. It can be found on the heads of young girls and respectable ladies, even the stars of show business were no exception.

Materials

So, how do beautiful headbands with flowers do with their own hands? In fact, creating an interesting accessory is much easier than it might seem at first glance. As a basis, you can use the usual plastic hoop, elastic band or elastic lace, and absolutely any material is suitable for flowers: satin ribbons, beads, felt, knitted fabric or leather - in any case, an interesting rim with flowers will come out.

Workshop on creating headbands with felt flowers

For the first two models you will need elastic lace, elastic, felt and a hot glue gun. From these materials, original rims with flowers will be obtained. With your own hands, making them will be very simple. We cut the lace and elastic according to the size of the head volume, close them in a circle and fix with hot glue. Then we make decorations. For one rim, you can make a flower from circles, and for another - from petals. So, for the petals, we draw 3 templates of different sizes on cardboard or paper, cut 5 pieces from the material of each and connect them into the blanks of the blank. After gluing them together by laying on top of each other, we attach a bead or button to the center of the flower. For the second rim, we cut out 7 identical circles from felt, one of them is smeared with glue and we spread 4 circles on it, folded four times. We also fold one more detail and glue it in the center. The final step for one and the second rim is to glue the flower to the elastic. From the inside, everything is closed in a circle from the same felt.

Satin Flower Headband

Satin ribbon flowers look very elegant in hair . How to make such rims with flowers with your own hands? For such an accessory you will need 6 circles of satin of different sizes, a lighter, a bead or a stone and an elastic band in the volume of the head. We close the elastic again in a circle, and fasten the flower to the junction. To make it, circles of fabric are melted with a lighter and glued blanks in order from larger to smaller, in the middle place the decoration. They fix such a flower in the same way as in the previous description: by gluing a flower, an elastic band and a circle of felt.





It is interesting that several colors with leaves or with the addition of ribbons, nylon mesh, fur or fluff will look on the rim. Flowers made of polymer clay that is so popular right now just look great, although some skills are needed to work with it.

Having basic knowledge of how to make flower headbands with your own hands, you can get to work. There are simply a huge number of ideas for creativity and the creation of beautiful jewelry for hair, if you wish, only your own imagination can become a limitation here.
