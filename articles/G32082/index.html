Bulgaria. Ravda - a unique resort on the Black Sea

Ravda is a beautiful resort in Bulgaria, which is located on the picturesque shores of the Black Sea. This village is famous as a children's and youth resort with a well-developed sports base. Sandy beaches, mild climate, gentle sea and beautiful natural landscapes - all this is rich in Bulgaria. Ravda attracts with comfortable hotels, children's camps and good conditions for water sports.

Location

Many resort cities have such a wonderful country like Bulgaria. Ravda is one of them. This village is located near the town of Nessebar. The nearest airport in Burgas is only thirty kilometers away. You can get to the resort of Ravda by bus or minibus.



The climate of the resort of Ravda

The village is famous for its mild climate, which is ideal for healing, both adults and children. In this region, protected by mountains from the cold northern winds, it is always warm and sunny in the summer. Air temperature within 27-29 degrees. The waters of the Black Sea warm up to 23 degrees, the swimming season begins already in the month of May and lasts until the end of October. Good climatic conditions for well-being are famous for Bulgaria. Ravda is one of the most remarkable resorts where rest and recreation are ideally combined.



Natural landscapes and the history of the village

Bulgaria is famous for its many natural attractions. Ravda attracts with its golden beaches, clean air and beautiful landscapes. In its vicinity are numerous melons and a huge number of vineyards. The story tells about the first settlements in this coastal region back in the early 18th century. Mostly Greek people inhabited it, later Bulgarians moved here from Aegean Macedonia. Ravda used to be a small fishing village, now it is a developed resort with modern infrastructure. The mild climate, healing air, friendly sea and natural beauty contributed to the development of the village. One of the wonderful gifts of nature is Cape Ravda, which is surrounded by two bays. The south beach is an ideal place to relax with young children. There is a sandy beach and a gentle entrance to the sea. Towards Nessebar lies the second bay, it is located between Cape Akrotiriya and Ravda. The local beaches begin with steep rocky shores, but then go into flat beach areas favorable for relaxation.





Entertainment and extreme sports

Holidays in Ravda (Bulgaria) annually attract thousands of tourists from Germany, Macedonia, the Czech Republic and other European countries. Comfortable hotels, small boarding houses, children's and youth camps are spread along the coast. In addition, this seaside resort has a good sports base. Ravda is ready to offer swimming, rowing, surfing, water skiing and many other sports. In the holiday season, vacationers can attend diving, surfing, sailing courses, as well as try their hand at these sports under the guidance of qualified instructors, but lovers of extreme sports can do water parachuting. For children in Ravda there is a beautiful water park with various water slides and attractions. Walking on water bicycles or a boat along the coast will be a wonderful pastime for the whole family. All the conditions for a good rest at sea are in Bulgaria (Ravda). Reviews about this resort are only positive. They have to choose this particular place.
