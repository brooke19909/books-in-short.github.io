Nizhny Novgorod Hotels in the City Center: Overview, Features and Reviews

Nizhny Novgorod is a huge city in Russia, on the territory of which there are many objects that carry historical memory. In addition, there are a large number of nature reserves and simply beautiful places that many tourists tend to visit.

Among tourists, hotels in Nizhny Novgorod in the city center always remain popular, since it is from this point that easy to get to the main attractions. Which of them are the most popular and where are the best living conditions provided? About it below.

"Monarch"

This hotel in the center of Nizhny Novgorod offers truly royal relaxation in luxurious apartments. It offers two types of rooms: standard and deluxe. They differ from each other in the degree of comfort. Each room has modern furniture, which is designed for a certain number of guests (1 or 2). Also, everything is carpeted in them, there are lamps in gilded lampshades all around, as well as air conditioning systems in all apartments.

Near the territory of this hotel you can find the main city attractions that you can visit. This is very pleasing to his guests, which they constantly mention in their comments.

The cost of living in a room of such a hotel costs at least 2000 rubles per day. It is located at the address: ul.Ulyanova, 6c.

Minin

Next to the aforementioned "Monarch", also along Ulyanov Street, 7/2, is another wonderful hotel-hotel. In Nizhny Novgorod, in the city center, its location is very advantageous, especially for tourists who do not know the terrain at all.

Her room fund offers guests apartments of such classes as standard and studio. Paying for accommodation in them, the minimum cost of which is about 3,000 rubles per day, guests can immediately include food in it, paying a certain amount, as agreed in a separate price list.

Next to this hotel is a huge number of entertainment venues, cafes, restaurants and important urban infrastructure. Not far from here is the famous Transfiguration Cathedral, Park them. Sverdlov, he is also near the Kremlin. Guests positively appreciate such proximity to the main city attractions, often they show photo reports about their visit, sharing their emotions.

The hotel in the center of Nizhny Novgorod Minin is located near major transport hubs, such as the railway station and airport.

"Slavia"

A hotel in Nizhny Novgorod in the city center is a great budget option for both foreign tourists and Russians. It has several rooms of the standard, comfort, studio and luxury classes, each of which is comfortable in its own way. Guests staying here have the opportunity to park their car for free in a secure parking lot, as well as free access to the Internet. Staying here, you can visit the nearby Philharmonic Concert Hall and several beautiful temples.

The cost of living here starts from 1800 rubles, if you need to install an extra bed, it will cost 700 rubles. The hotel is located at: Varvarskaya street, 10b.

"Nicola House"

On Pozharsky Street, 18, in Nizhny Novgorod, in the city center, the Nikola House Hotel invites tourists to its walls, where there are several comfortable rooms furnished with modern furniture and air conditioning. Each room has an individual shower room with a bathroom.

Hotel guests often respond very positively to the staff working here, saying that it consists exclusively of professionals in their field.

The cost of hotel accommodation is 3000 rubles.

"Ostrog shaft"

Ostrozhsky Val is a hotel in Nizhny Novgorod, in the city center, which offers quite acceptable living conditions for its guests. In her room fund there are economy, standard and luxury classes. There is also one room in which beds are rented, it is designed for 10 people.

The cost of hotel accommodation depends on the conditions provided. Its minimum value is 800 rubles, which, according to tourists, is a very inexpensive option. Luxury apartments will cost from 2500 to 5000 rubles per night. Breakfast is already included.

Hotel guests note that for a fee you can order a transfer service from the airport or train station to the hotel and vice versa - it is paid separately from the stay. In general, according to them, the Ostrog Val is very comfortable, responsible staff work here.

The hotel is located at: Minin street, 12.

"At Peter"

Another great hotel option in Nizhny Novgorod, in the center (near the Kremlin). It is located next to a historical object that is attractive for tourists from different regions - the walls of the Kremlin. It is located at: Pochainskaya street, 14.

"At Peter" is, perhaps, one of the best hotels in the center of Nizhny Novgorod, since a wide range of conditions are offered on its territory, favorable for a comfortable stay of guests. Its rooms are equipped with only modern furniture, beds are covered with orthopedic mattresses. Each apartment has a private bathroom and shower room.

This hotel is chosen by representatives of different fields and age categories. It is great for people who came to the city on a business trip, couples in love, families with children, as well as lonely travelers who come to get acquainted with local attractions, which, by the way, are nearby.

Near the guest house "At Peter" is the airport and train station. Hotel guests claim that it is here that provides excellent service and excellent living conditions. Some of them share their photo reports from the vacation spot on social networks and on thematic sites, saying that the hotel owner is a very hospitable person who is attentive to each visitor.
