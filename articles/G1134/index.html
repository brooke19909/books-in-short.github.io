Crochet square napkins photos, descriptions and patterns

Given that crocheted napkins are a classic of needlework, it is not surprising that almost all beginner knitters try to make them. The usual round beautiful napkins (crochet) are unusually in demand, however, products of a square, oval and hexagonal shape are no less popular.

Types of square napkins

Like any others, napkins having a square shape can consist of:

  • From one major motive with harness.
  • Of several motifs with a common border.
  • From a grid with a binding.


Border, that is, a narrow openwork strip running along the edge of the product, is an indispensable element of napkins and tablecloths. It distinguishes an independent product from a motive designed to connect with other elements. Almost all schemes of square napkins (crocheted ) are designed so that the edge ends with a strapping that gives the product a neat and complete look.

Web reduction

In a similar way, the opposite effect is performed: they remove some elements of the napkin and reduce its area. You can remove the same components that in the previous paragraph recommend adding:

  • Stripe pattern.
  • Strip of mesh.

There are schemes where the elements are not arranged in circular stripes, but go from the center to the outer edge.

Unfortunately, changing the size of these napkins is a thankless task. As a result of painful efforts, you can get a shapeless product with disturbed proportions of the ornament.

A few words about yarn

Traditionally, thin cotton thread of light shades is used to make napkins. The thinner the yarn, the more accurate and elegant the product will look. The thickness of 500-600 m / 100 grams can be considered optimal. Do not be afraid that this thread will be very difficult to work with. A napkin is a small product, so its production will not take too much time.
