Ski resort Sheregesh. How to get from Moscow?

Sheregesh is a small ski resort in Russia, which is located near the city of Novokuznetsk. In fact, Sheregesh is a small village in the south-east of the country that no one would ever visit if not for the most wonderful ski slopes, the length of which is more than 2500 meters.





Hard road

There is no direct connection with Sheregesh from Moscow. In order to solve the problem of how to get from Moscow to Sheregesh, you need to buy a ticket to the nearest major city with an airport, railway or bus station. The nearest major city is Novokuznetsk. From there, you can drive to Sheregesh in 3 hours.

How to get from Moscow by plane

If you decide to fly to the resort, then you should take tickets to Novokuznetsk (Spichenkovo ‚Äã‚ÄãAirport). Unfortunately, there is no direct plane from Moscow to Sheregesh. Next, it will take about three hours to get to the destination by taxi. Airplanes depart daily from two Moscow airports at 9 and 10 pm. Arrival in Novokuznetsk is from 5 to 6 in the morning. The cost of tickets is about 10 thousand rubles.





Other large cities are located 500 km from the resort: Barnaul, Gorno-Altaysk, Novosibirsk. Each of them has an airport. If suddenly there are seasonal sales, and the cost of a ticket to these cities will be significantly lower, then you should take a chance and come this way. After 5-6 hours, using a bus, taxi or rented car, you can get to the right place. An air ticket from Moscow to Barnaul will cost 12-13 thousand rubles (at the usual price).

Taxis, of course, will cost a lot during the holidays and in the season. For a budget holiday, you can get by bus from the airport to the city of Tashtagol, and from there daily trains run every 15 minutes. Thus, for 200 rubles you can get to Sheregesh.

How to come from Moscow by train

You can get to Sheregesh from Moscow both by plane and by train. This is a more budget option, suitable for lovers of money.





There is no train station in the village itself. The nearest station is 20 km from Sheregesh. You can get there from Novokuznetsk, trains depart every other day, on odd numbers on the calendar. Already in Novokuznetsk can be reached from different parts of Russia: Moscow, St. Petersburg, Anapa, Vladivostok and so on. The cost of tickets depends on the point of departure, and when buying in advance, the price is reduced.

The cost of a ticket from Moscow for one to Novokuznetsk will be 5-6 thousand rubles. On the way, the passenger spends about 40 hours. Train No. 118E leaves every other day, at odd numbers on the calendar, at 13 hours from Moscow. The Moscow-Barnaul train runs every other day, leaves at 19:20 from the Kazan station. The cost of a ticket for one will be from 10 to 14 thousand rubles.

How to get to Sheregesh by car

The distance between Moscow and the village is about 4 thousand kilometers. Travel time will be more than 60 hours. When traveling by car, the cost of fuel will cost 12 thousand rubles.





Leaving Moscow, you need to head to the southeast of the capital. After the Moscow Ring Road, go onto the E5 highway. On the way there will be an opportunity to visit large Russian cities: Tyumen, Novosibirsk, Omsk, Novokuznetsk.

Tours

Tours to Sheregesh from Moscow are most popular in the winter: on New Year's holidays, Christmas and students' vacations. Every year the resort attracts more and more tourists, because there is everything for entertainment and relaxation. Modern convenient tracks that meet all European standards, a huge number of good guest houses and hotels, a healing climate and amazing nature.

The cost of a tour to Sheregesh from Moscow per person is about 50 thousand rubles. The price includes a flight to Novokuznetsk, transfer to the hotel, accommodation, meals and visits to the bath complex. The tour desk offers a trip "Following the Trail of Snow People".

The average duration of the tour is 5 days. The price may change during the holidays, promotions. Also depends on the placement: standard room or luxury.





For example, a tour with accommodation in the Shorotel hotel in a double room will cost about 80 thousand rubles. This is a classic modern hotel, with comfortable rooms and a convenient location - a minute's walk from the ski lifts. Hotel reviews are excellent, many people like the location. Guests are invited to eat in the hotel's restaurant, which serves Russian cuisine.

The hotel "Chalet on Taiga" offers a more budget type of accommodation, so the cost of the tour is reduced to 50 thousand rubles. The hotel is located 5 km from the nearest ski slope. Each room is equipped with its own shower room, kitchenette, TV, fridge and wireless internet. Guests can use their own car parking facilities.
