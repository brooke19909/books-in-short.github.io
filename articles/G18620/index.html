General impression

This hotel can be called a budget "Quartet" with living conditions and services that are fully consistent with the price. The low cost of tours is mainly due to the fact that the hotel is located far from the sea. Walk to him for about 20 minutes, and by bus - 5 minutes. As for nutrition, you can look at it in two ways. The range of products is very limited. Nevertheless, it is worth paying tribute to the chefs who manage to create such a variety of delicious and nutritious dishes from a small number of ingredients. The only comment is the amount of spices that Europeans cannot afford.

Animation at the hotel is practically absent. This factor makes the hotel an ideal place for retirees, as well as families with small children. Evening events are held a couple of times a week. They are so vivid that there are enough memories and impressions for a long time. And for those who can not live without entertainment and a day, it will be proposed to go to the center of Kemer, where many nightlife venues operate. Walking to the main promenade will not take you even five minutes. Another advantage of the location is the incredibly beautiful mountain scenery that this region is famous for.

Despite minor flaws, the overall impression of the hotel is positive. The secret of success lies in the fact that the staff is a large family that strives to surround each guest with the warmth and attention. Neither nationality, nor the category of the room, nor the presence or absence of a tip play a role. You will be given immediate assistance on any matter related to your stay. Also, employees will kindly tell about their city and its environs, advise places for study tours.
