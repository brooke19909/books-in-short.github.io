Turkey, Telatiye Resort 5 * hotel: description and reviews of tourists

A Turkish small resort called Konakli is located on the Mediterranean coast. It is surrounded by magnificent mountains, covered with dense thickets of pine forests and plantations of orange trees. In itself, it is a calm and cozy town with quiet streets, which are small shops, bars and restaurants. On Wednesdays, an oriental bazaar unfolds in Konakli, where you can buy everything from small souvenirs to beautiful Turkish rugs.



Relaxation

Here the tourist will find everything he wants. Fans of club life in the city can visit night discos, although for active pastime it is better to go to nearby Alanya, which can be easily reached by taxi or bus.

Konakli has many beaches. Most of them have a pebble coating, so rubber shoes are required. For lovers of purely sandy beaches on the outskirts of Konakli there is a so-called "turtle" bathing area. Description

Telatiye Resort 5 * (Alanya, Konakli) until the middle of May 2011 was called Seal of Star. After the change of ownership, it was completely renovated. This hotel has a very good location - the Mediterranean coast is literally one hundred and fifty meters. The large resort of Alanya, popular among Russians, is twelve kilometers distant. Antalya International Airport is a two-hour drive away. The center of picturesque Konakli can be reached on foot in just ten minutes.

Near the Telatiye Resort 5 * there is a bus stop, from where the flight routes depart in various directions.

Infrastructure

The hotel in all respects corresponds to the declared level, including the proposed list of services. It has two conference rooms for two hundred and fifty and forty people. In addition, the Telatiye Resort 5 * offers a full range of opportunities for celebrations and business events.

The infrastructure of a modern hotel includes: mini market, souvenir shop, small shop, lobby, concierge services. Residents can, for an additional fee, return things to the laundry or laundry, and visit the medical room.



Hotel Telatiye Resort 5 * has its own free parking. Protected places do not need to be reserved in advance. The registry is always open. At any time of the day, having arrived at the hotel, you can go through registration and get the keys to the reserved room. At the counter, you can send a fax or Xerox documents. Here you can get a printout of all the services that are offered in Telatiye Resort 5 *. The hotel has an office where you can rent a transport - a car that is very relevant for independent excursions or for shopping.

For children

Beach

Telatiye Resort 5 * (Turkey) is only one hundred and fifty meters distant from the sea. The hotel has its own, albeit small, bathing area.
