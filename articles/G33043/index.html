Where to go in Krasnoyarsk for tourists: a list of interesting sights of the city

Krasnoyarsk is one of the largest cities in Siberia. It is located on both sides of the Yenisei River, which divides the city into several areas: left-bank and right-bank. Each has its own unique places of recreation and entertainment. The historical center of the city (over 300 years) is well preserved. Streets and avenues are now cultural and architectural heritage. Here you can find old log houses with beautiful carved shutters, as well as massive stone buildings in which rich merchants previously lived.

Description of Krasnoyarsk





The central part is small, it can be bypassed in just a couple of hours. The specific geometry of this magnificent city will not allow tourists to get lost. The streets are flat, very beautiful, with interesting buildings. Newly arrived guests are wondering "where to go in Krasnoyarsk." We must say right away that there are many places and attractions in the city for different tastes and abundance.

The right bank is less saturated with cultural and entertainment facilities, unlike the left bank of the Yenisei. Rather, it is the industrial part of the city, on which there is a large Circus, founded in 1964 and regularly hosting prominent artists in its arena. A special attention was paid to the railway bridge, which received in 1900 at a competition in Paris a gold medal for perfect architectural performance and the Grand Prix.





On the left bank there are many ancient museums, theaters and monuments. The symbol of Krasnoyarsk is the Praskeva Pyatnitsa Chapel (the building dates from the 19th century) 15 meters high, which is located at the very top of the Karaulnaya Mountain. It offers stunning panoramic views of the entire city. Next to this unique object is a cannon, from which a shot is fired daily.

Also in the city there are about 12 park zones, more than forty night clubs and 22 museums. In particular, tourists have sculptural compositions and magnificent fountains. If you are still tormented by the question "where to go in Krasnoyarsk," then read on.
