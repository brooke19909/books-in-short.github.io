Sea Breeze Beach Karon hotel - rest in Thailand at a reasonable price

Thailand's Sea Breeze Beach Karon Hotel, located in Phuket, is renowned for its quality service and incredibly beautiful scenery around. It is located in the central part of the beach called Karon, 10 km from Patong Beach, famous for its fascinating nightly entertainment. The city airport is a 40-minute drive from the hotel.

About Phuket Hotel

The 3-star Sea Breeze Beach Karon was refurbished in 2014. The building consists of two four-story buildings, includes 87 guest rooms. There is no swimming pool on site, a cozy restaurant and a bar with refreshments are at guests' disposal. The beach is a sandy public type.





The rooms of Karon Sea Breeze Beach by on 3 have a TV, air conditioning, free wireless internet, a shower, a safe for valuables and a telephone. A mini fridge is replenished every day with free water. For children, the hotel provides a cot in the room, and the restaurant has special chairs. Guests at Sea Breeze Beach Karon can benefit from currency exchange and laundry services. Check in at the hotel at 2 pm, check out at 11 in the morning.

Available rooms and room arrangements

The room structure of the Karon Sea Breeze Beach 3 hotel (Phuket, Thailand) is as follows:

  • 12 "Deluxe" - 28 square meters. m., for 2 or 3 people, you can choose with a balcony.
  • 61 "Superior" - 25 square meters. m., for 2 or 3 people.
  • 14 “Superior Partly” with sea view - 25 sq. M. m., for 2 or 3 people.

Bed linen and towels are changed every day. Cleaning is carried out regularly. You can choose a room with a bath or shower, depending on individual preferences.





What do tourists like

Tourists from different countries celebrate the quality of service in Karon Sea Breeze Beach Phuket, which is fully consistent with the stardom of the hotel. At the entrance, you can pay the administrator so that the view from the room is on the territory of the hotel. Also at the reception you can ask for an electric kettle if necessary. The rooms have an ashtray and glasses for soft drinks (the number corresponds to the number of guests checking in). Television is satellite, there is a DVD player, water bottles are added to the refrigerator every day, drinks in the minibar are paid. Visitors note the quality of breakfast, the original menu in the hotel restaurant. Wi-Fi is free in every room.

For the safety of visitors, a doctor is working at Sea Breeze Beach Karon around the clock. The "minus" of the hotel is the lack of its own beach and pool on the territory, but the public - clean and well-groomed - is quite close. For any question, the administrator can be contacted around the clock.

Visitors Comments

When leaving the hotel, you should pay attention to the integrity of the equipment in the room, since in case of breakdown you will have to pay about one and a half thousand baht. Many people prefer to use not Wi-Fi at the hotel, but a local SIM card with mobile Internet, which can be purchased at any retail outlet. In addition, the cost of calls in roaming on it is minimal.





All guests of Karon Sea Breeze Beach by on 3 are happy with daily housekeeping, in which the staff changes all bathroom amenities, bathrobes, towels. Money is best changed not in the hotel itself, but in exchangers marked in blue and green. Near the main building of the hotel there are many massage parlors where visitors can relax and receive a small souvenir. Nearby is a market with a large assortment of clothes and shoes, there are practically no tropical fruits and vegetables.

Everything about food in the hotel and nearby cafes

Breakfast at the three-star hotel - buffet. The menu includes the following dishes and products:

  • Scrambled eggs or scrambled eggs.
  • Fruit and berry juices.
  • Flakes of various kinds.
  • Pineapples, watermelons.
  • Coffee Tea.

In order to visit the buffet, a special card is set up at the reception. There are various cafes near the main building, the average cost of a full meal in which is about 170 baht. The restaurant is happy daily for visitors to discounted hotels.





Tourists recommend visiting the VANNA cafe, where they prepare delicious dishes at an affordable cost. A small gift in the form of a tropical fruit or a small dessert is provided for each visitor. The chef cooks poultry meat, sea fish, pork, as well as seafood. Fans of Italian cuisine will also love this cafe. Near the hotel you can buy ripe exotic fruits from private traders, such as mangoes or papaya.

Guided tours and hotel beach

Having decided to relax in Thailand, you must definitely sign up for all kinds of excursions. An excellent opportunity to spend time actively is provided by Karon Sea Breeze Beach. The hotel works with many companies specializing in organizing interesting tourist programs. Interesting options for day trips to the Pi Don Don, Pi Ley, as well as Krabi. The cost is quite high - about 1,000 baht per person - but the tour will meet all expectations. It is best to order photo sessions with private individuals: the pictures are original and very exotic. Sightseeing tours are also available - an option for those who want to get the most information about the place in which they are resting.





The sandy beach is within walking distance of a three-star hotel - across the road. Despite being public, cleanliness is perfect everywhere. The sand is white, the water is clear. You should be careful with plankton near the shore, as their bites cause certain inconvenience. On the beach you can buy a ride on a jet ski, boat or parachute. Children will love the variety of unusual animals with which you can take pictures for memory. Nearby there are many outlets with clothes where you can buy profitably and buy a souvenir from Phuket for friends.
