Chelyabinsk "Chadograd" - a city of professions!

In the beautiful and not at all severe city of Chelyabinsk there is a unique city - "Chadograd". It is located in the shopping center "Focus". Anyone aged 4 to 15 years can get into this city, but always with their parents. Chadograd will give children the opportunity to recognize themselves in different professions, get their first experience, new interesting knowledge, skills, and even their first salary.

"Dream! Create! Incarnate!"

This is the motto of the city of professions. In Chelyabinsk "Chadograd" there are streets, a square and houses (workshops). In these well-equipped with everything necessary for work, houses with children mentors are engaged. You can imagine yourself as a florist, seller, banker, miner, tourist, farmer, rescuer, fireman, actor, stylist, mayor, photographer, cook, car service worker, police officer, etc. More than 40 professions! In one workshop can be no more than 6 people.

Documents are issued to young employees, they are marked that the child has acquired the skills of a particular profession. The guys earn improvised money (currency - chervonets), later paying them, you can continue to study in city workshops.

A visit to Chadograd will not only be entertainment. The city of professions arouses interest in work and shows the diversity of professions.

Birthday is a fun holiday!

In Chelyabinsk "Chadograd" there is a wonderful venue for spending a birthday, graduation, celebration and other children's celebrations. For the event, you can order a workshop, street and even the whole city.

There is a special offer for those who have a birthday in the city of professions "Chadograd". You can take a friend with you and get two for the price of one ticket. But this requires a birth certificate. This offer can be used three days before and the same after the birthday indicated in the document.

Reviews

And, of course, you need to find out what the reviews about Chadograd in Chelyabinsk look like.

Young visitors to Chadograd like a variety of professions, they claim that they want to come back here again and again to learn a new profession, in which they did not have time to try on a previous visit. Children and parents are generally enthusiastic about the animation team. Children ask their parents to return to Chadograd, because it is very interesting for them to work with the workshop mentors.

According to the parents, the guys have off-scale emotions, their eyes burn! They wait and wait for the next visit, making plans for a future career in a particular area, and, of course, aiming at the mayors of the city.
