Pension "Bylina" (Novosibirsk) on the shore of the Berdsky Gulf

Berdsky Bay is one of the largest in the territory of the Novosibirsk reservoir. It is located in the lower reaches of the picturesque river Berd. He even has a story. The bay with a small current formed in the fifties. Swimming here is a pleasure. The shape of the bay is elongated, from a height it looks very beautiful and picturesque. Therefore, if you have the opportunity to admire such a unique panorama of its kind, you should definitely use it.

The bay gained great popularity among fishermen. The reservoir is rich in animals. Most of the locals go fishing, then sell fresh or dried fish to tourists. The last one here is very tasty. Therefore, connoisseurs of fish delicacies go to rest here.

The amazing nature of the Bay of Berds

It pleases not only the water world, but also the surrounding nature. The area around the bay is very picturesque. There are birds that sing lively in the morning and wake up at dawn. In ancient times, this territory was not absorbed by water. Various buildings were built here, people lived safely. If you look closely, under the water you can see the destroyed houses. Better yet, go diving and study everything in more detail. This place especially attracts those who dream of finding artifacts or a real treasure in old flooded buildings.

There are a lot of tourists with scuba diving. Diving under water is not only those who are trying to become owners of easy money, but also various creative people, making shots of incredible beauty here. Tourism is very developed in this area, a wide range of services, various procedures and recreational activities are offered. Numerous recreation centers receive tourists year-round. One of them is the Bylina boarding house. Novosibirsk is the city near which it is located.





This is an ideal option for a good rest. The place is unique, because high-class staff and all the necessary services for quality rest and relaxation are competently combined here. Pension "Bylina" (Novosibirsk) is really an atmospheric place. The person who is here does not leave the feeling of a fairy tale, because the whole situation contributes to this. The staff is simply amazing, throughout the rest the guest feels respect and attention.

Despite the fact that the boarding house is almost always filled with guests, the administration finds time for everyone, fulfills all the desires and whims of the guests. Entire families come here to really relax and heal the children. When you return to this place again, you get the feeling of coming to a small homeland. It seems that old friends have been waiting for you for a long time, and here you are again with them. The guesthouse is located in the city of Novy. You can get here by bus, taxi or own car.

Detailed description

Many tourists like the Bylina boarding house. Novosibirsk invites guests to visit this hotel complex with a rich infrastructure. It is located in a magnificent pine forest, which allows guests to breathe clean air and put their health in order. Moreover, the complex stands on the shore of the bay, so tourists can swim and relax on the beach.

On the territory there is a huge restaurant that seats 150 guests. Very often weddings are held here, as well as various kinds of photo shoots. The highlight of the boarding house is a very cozy lounge with a fireplace. It is small, designed for 25 seats. But here an incredible atmosphere of warmth has been created, so all hotel guests enjoy spending evenings by the fireplace, enjoying fine wine.





For business negotiations or meetings, several conference rooms are equipped. One of them is small - for 40 people, the other large, which seats 90 people. A superbly equipped sauna with a swimming pool will delight every guest. Also on site are several cozy bars and restaurants. The administration arranges leisure for tourists, the complex itself is open all season. For every season of the year, various entertainments are thought out here.

Description of rooms

The recreation center "Bylina" does not provide for the availability of low-class rooms. There are apartments of the category "Suite" and "Junior Suite". All rooms are equipped with new and very comfortable furniture. The rooms are located in several complexes. In each of them the administrator is on duty. His responsibilities include a number of specific services that each guest can use. These include:

  • Wake up the guest at the appointed time.
  • Provide all necessary kitchen utensils.
  • Deliver ordered drinks to the room. It can be cocktails or tea.
  • Also, the on-duty administrator can call a taxi or order food in the room.

Pets are allowed

In this institution, their guests are very loyal. The issue of accommodation with animals should be decided in advance. Please contact the property prior to arrival to resolve this issue. Accommodation with children

Pension "Bylina" (Novosibirsk) is very kind to the placement of children. Younger guests under 15 years old are accommodated for free and provide an extra bed. But free service is provided only if the family came with one child. If there are two children, then you still have to pay for one.

The hotel has several playgrounds, a special pool, a separate menu in the restaurant, as well as professional babysitting and animators. Each child will be comfortable living in the complex.
