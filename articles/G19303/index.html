Residential complex “Otradnoe. New Quarter ”- a new direction of suburban construction in Voronezh

A fundamentally new project for the capital of the Black Earth Region was a very popular suburban construction project in European countries. Located in the suburbs of Voronezh, the residential complex "Otradnoe. New Quarter ”includes nine panel-brick houses with different number of storeys (from 5 to 9 floors). In total there are 839 apartments of various planning decisions (from one to three rooms) and sizes (from 38 to 88 m 2 ). All houses are combined into a single infrastructure, which has its own shopping center, pharmacy, bank, cafe and even a swimming pool. The first houses in the residential complex have been in operation since November 2012, and the entire project is planned to be completed before the end of 2014.



Developing a residential complex “Otradnoe. New Quarter ”, reviews and wishes of its future residents by the project developers were considered as priority areas for future construction. And, it seems, they managed to realize them. Each square meter of area is used as rationally as possible. On the landing there are from two to five apartments. All windows are equipped with double-chamber double - glazed windows of increased noise and heat insulation. There are high-speed freight and passenger and passenger elevators, garbage chutes in the entrances, there are rooms for concierges and security. The buildings are also equipped with a fire alarm and fire extinguishing system. Everything is provided for a comfortable stay for people with disabilities.

Optimum air temperature in the living quarters of the Otradnoye. The New Quarter ”is supported by adjustable central heating radiators, as well as a supply and exhaust ventilation system. All apartments are connected to high speed internet.



In the complex, everything is thought out to the smallest detail. For a quiet and relaxing holiday there is a walking area with a fountain, gazebos. Sports enthusiasts can use the multifunctional sports ground or ride a bicycle on a specially equipped cycle path. Car owners are offered 320 outdoor parking spaces, as well as covered parking for 150 cars.

For residents of a metropolis who, due to family circumstances, still doubt whether to leave or not leave Voronezh, “Otradnoe. New Quarter ”offers a new kindergarten, which in the very near future is planned to significantly expand and modernize. A school and a hospital are nearby.

An ecologically clean area, affordable price and European quality standards - this is what is most important for those who are going to buy an apartment in the residential complex “Otradnoe. New quarter. ”

On its territory a real botanical garden is laid: wild grapes, maple, ash and oak alleys. Landscaping events were attended by those who already reside here, and those who are only planning to purchase housing.

The builder of the new project is Instep LLC, by visiting the official site of which you can not only familiarize yourself with the detailed layout and availability of apartments, but even watch the construction progress online. The sale of apartments is carried out by Eltaro LLC.



Despite the fact that the residential complex “Otradnoe. New Quarter ”is located in the suburbs, sometimes getting to the center of Voronezh from here is even faster than from any other district of the city. The whole road takes no more than half an hour.
