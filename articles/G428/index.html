Hurghada Hotel Golden Fife Huge leisure complex

The resort of Hargada in Egypt stretches its sandy beaches for forty kilometers along the Red Sea. More than two hundred hotels are already located on this coast, and this number is constantly increasing. Truly, here you can make a choice even to the most fastidious tourist. But we will now turn our attention to the Hurghada Hotel Golden Five. This large hotel complex was built in 1997. On its vast territory there are seven buildings and more than 40 detached villas.

The location of the Golden Five Hotel in Hurghada shows that this is a huge complex consisting of several hotels located on the same territory. All buildings of the complex have their own star rating and their own name: Paradise Golden Five 5 *, Al Mas Palace 5 *, Diamond 5 *, Emerald 5 *, Club Golden Five 4 *, Topaz Club Hotel 4 *, Sapphire Suites Hotel 4 *. You can choose a body for every taste. On the first line of the sea is the Diamond Building, it operates on the widespread all-inclusive system. A little further from the coast, behind the pool, but also on the first line is the Main Building. In this building, guests are offered services on the half-board system. On the territory of Hurghada Hotel Golden Five are the most famous in Egypt, "Singing Fountains." The show is held daily and gathers numerous spectators from all over Hurghada. For hotel guests, the show is free.

The main restaurant has the name "Red Sea", there is an Italian restaurant for lunch and dinner, a coffee shop, three more cafes and bars, including a pool and a beach. There is a large artificial lake with sea water. Hurghada Hotel Golden Five complex is equipped with a huge outdoor pool, which has five branches and a children's pool. For vacationers there is a cable car, a currency exchange, a shopping center. There is a business center with 4 meeting rooms. The water park is equipped with a jacuzzi, it has five slides for adults and two children's slides. There is also a water park equipped in the lagoon on the water, there are icebergs, trampolines and a water swing.

The entire hotel complex has a common infrastructure located on a common territory. Vacationers living in any of the buildings can use the services of the entire infrastructure of the complex. Naturally, with such popularity, there are numerous reviews of the Golden Five Hotel in Hurghada. Basically, all the reviews are positive and enthusiastic. Visitors to the complex like a large assortment and quality of food. The choice of food is simply huge, before desserts and pastries it is usually already difficult to maintain an appetite, since you can eat meat, fish and vegetables. The restaurant staff are helpful and do their job well. Many people like how they clean the rooms and monitor the cleanliness in them. They try to decorate the room by making flowers, swans and crocodiles out of clean towels.

Hurghada Golden Five Hotel is also famous for its beach and clear sea, which has many beautiful exotic fish. The staff on the beach are friendly and helpful, everyone smiles, helps with sunbeds and tries to create the most comfortable conditions for relaxation. There are also negative reviews from tourists vacationing in this complex, but most of them are associated not with the hotel itself, but with the shortcomings of the tour operators. The conditions themselves in all buildings of the complex are pretty good and do not cause negative emotions. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a balcony or terrace, satellite TV with Russian channel. The bathtub has all toiletries, a hairdryer and a bidet. Rooms are cleaned daily and all towels are changed. The concept of the whole complex is to make it completely self-sufficient, so that tourists living there do not even have the need and desire to go beyond its territory. It has all the entertainment and the entire leisure industry.
