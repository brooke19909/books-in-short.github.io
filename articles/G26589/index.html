Where to swim in St. Petersburg? Choosing a good place!

However, not everyone knows where in St. Petersburg you can swim. This is especially true of foreigners who have just visited this amazing city with a long history. It is for such guests of the northern capital that we will conduct a brief excursion and tell you where to swim in St. Petersburg, as well as what rules should be followed.

To start with the rules of conduct on the beaches. Even if you install stands and posters with safety rules on the water through every meter, swimmers still do not pay any attention to them. But in vain! Remember that the question: "Where to swim in St. Petersburg?" is not a priority, because you can always find a place. But without knowing (or not adhering) to the rules of behavior in water, you can get into a very unpleasant situation. So, one of the main requirements when bathing is not to go far. In any case, try to refrain from picnics with alcohol near the river.



Now we turn to the question of where to swim in St. Petersburg. At the moment, 25 recreation areas are registered in the city. However, on some of them you can only get a tan, as it is forbidden to enter the water. Others, on the contrary, are complex, allowing you to swim and sunbathe on the beach.

If you want to get a great tan, then your attention is given to the beaches in Komarovo. It was about them that they sang in the famous song "For a Week ...". The level of service here can be called one of the best in the city. There is also a cafe with soft drinks and fruit cocktails, and rental sun loungers, and even dry closets. As they say, everything you need for a good vacation. In the Vyborg district of St. Petersburg, you can also visit several beaches, tasting delicious cakes, french fries and fried chicken. When wondering where to swim in St. Petersburg, remember the Kronstadt beach. It combines both active and passive rest. Here you can not only get a great tan, but also participate in "races" on catamarans, as well as rent an inflatable boat. Since the lake near the beach is simply huge, some people choose it for fishing, going by boat straight to its center.



For a good rest with swimming, you can go to Sestroretsk and visit the famous Dubrovsky beach area. This is a great place for picnics and family vacations. If you are not inclined to carry huge packages of food, you can dine in a good restaurant near the complex.

As you can see, there are a lot of places where to swim in St. Petersburg. Each of them is unique in its own way. Enjoy and have fun!
