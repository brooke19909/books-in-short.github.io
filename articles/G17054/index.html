Where is it better to insure life and health?

Intense life rhythm forces to take measures to compensate for the consequences of accidents, accidents and other incidents. After all, they cause damage to life and health. Although many situations cannot be prevented, it is possible to provide damages. Can life be insured? This service is common today.

In many companies, you can insure life. Making a policy will create a stable financial reserve, which at any time will provide an opportunity to improve health. It is advisable to contact a trusted company that offers favorable conditions for its customers.

The Importance of Life and Health Insurance

Life and health insurance is necessary for:

  • guarantees to support a decent life in different situations;
  • giving children a chance to study at prestigious educational institutions;
  • confidence in the future.




Insurance is issued for a long period. Typically, policies have a validity period of 3 to 5 years, but a maximum of 20 years. The document is valid around the clock, so help is always provided. When an insured event occurs, you must contact the company to receive compensation.

How to get insurance?

Companies offer different insurance methods: for financial support in difficult situations, during treatment or for accumulation. To insure life and health, you need to choose the right company, contact the office, sign the contract. Naturally, this requires investment. But after all, upon the occurrence of an insured event, great help will be provided.

An example would be a funded program from a large firm Ingosstrakh, drawn up for life. The advantage of such a policy is the transfer of a sum of money in inheritance. It turns out that you can bequeath to your relatives much more than you have to give the company. Money will be provided after the occurrence of the insured event.

What is written in the policy?

The document is drawn up according to generally accepted rules. It contains information about the parties to the transaction. The rights and obligations of participants, as well as the terms of cooperation, are registered there. Risks for which compensation is due must be indicated.





The insurance contract indicates the period during which compensation is valid . No less important are the information on the amount that is due for payments.

Features

If life is insured, when will the whole amount be received? The main risk is death during the period specified in the contract. You can pay for the inclusion in the document of a clause that involves exemption from regular payments for disabilities of 1 or 2 groups. Then you need to pay contributions, and the amount is still charged in full.

If life is insured, it becomes possible to compensate for the costs of restoring the health of oneself and loved ones. Also, the service allows you to accumulate a certain amount, which will allow you to maintain a decent life. You just need to know what you need to design this service.

Types of insurance

To insure life, several service options can be offered:

  1. A risky option for which accumulation is not allowed during the designated period. The company must pay the amount marked in the contract if an insured event occurs. If it does not exist, there will be an expiration of the service, and funds will not be given to the client. The advantages of the risky option include a low cost, which allows applicants with a low income to choose it.
  2. Savings insurance. According to this method, payments are made in the prescribed amount, even if there was no insured event. The contract is drawn up for a specified period.
  3. Cumulative view. The policy will operate indefinitely. The rest of the service is similar to the previous option. But there are differences, for example, investment income - an increase to the insurance amount. This involves the payment of funds, the yield of which is 3-5%.




If a perpetual contract is drawn up , then it has the name of a life contract . Compensation is due to relatives who are beneficiaries. But there is a right to terminate the document before the deadline set in the contract, after which an amount with accrued income is issued. Such an order is carried out at the request of the client.

Accidents

An ordinary contract of this type is executed for a shorter period - for 1 year, if we compare it with the above-mentioned cases involving financial protection for 5 years or more.

You can insure life from an accident using the following protection options:

  • full - carried out around the clock 7 days a week;
  • partial - for a certain time, for example, at the time of vacation.

The insurance provided for a certain time happens:

  • renewable - the contract can be re-signed;
  • convertible - there is the possibility of independent changes in conditions;
  • with decreasing coverage - the program is designed for older people who cannot regularly pay dues.

Types of Compensation

If you insure the life of a child or an adult, then compensation will be put. Their size depends on the size of the damage:

  • Payments in full - death or loss of legal capacity.
  • 10-20% of the payments fixed in the contract - in case of injury and temporary disability.

The amount of the insurance amount is set by the client. But it should be remembered that the higher the requests, the more you have to pay. The minimum contribution is determined at a rate of at least $ 10 each month.





Some companies offer preferential conditions in case of difficulties at the first stage - a delay, which can last from several days to 2 months. If it is not possible to regularly deposit amounts, then you can stop the contract with its subsequent renewal if the financial condition improves.

Loss of policy - what to do?

In case of loss of policy, you need to contact the company that issued this document. You must have a passport and a medical card with you. A statement should be written stating the circumstances of the loss of the policy. Even if there is no card, this is not considered a reason for refusing to receive a new document.





If a corporate type document is issued, you need to find out your insurance number in the territorial clinic. You can also contact a health insurance fund. Receiving involves filing an application with a description of the situation and the contract number. Then the lost policy will be canceled, after which a new one will be issued.
