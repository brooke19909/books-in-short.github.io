How to make a do-it-yourself birthday card for dad: ideas and tips

Beautiful birthday card for dad

In order to make such a card, you will need craft paper, white paper, glue and scissors. For decoration, you can take the usual wide satin ribbon.

Instead of roses, you can use anything you want: buttons, beads, even coffee beans.

  • Cut out the heart from white paper. Glue it in the middle on kraft paper.
  • Next, using the roses or what you replaced them with, glue the white heart around the edges so that a three-dimensional heart is obtained, as shown in the photo above.
  • After that, cut a small rectangle from another sheet of the craft and glue it just above the heart. Cut a rectangle of white paper even smaller than the previous one and paste it on top of the craft rectangle.
  • We take a pre-prepared satin ribbon, assembled by a bow, and either attach it to the card with glue, or hold it behind and tie it.
  • At the very end, when the handmade birthday card for Dad is ready for birthday, we write a wish on a white rectangle. Anything, the main thing - with all my heart, so that dad would be pleased.

The second version of the postcard

At first glance, it might seem that making such a voluminous birthday card for dad is very difficult. In fact, this is not so. So what do we need?

Colored and white cardboard, felt-tip pens / paints, glue, scissors.

  • We take colored cardboard (any color), bend it in half and unbend it.
  • We glue white cardboard on the inside so that it is a centimeter smaller than the main (color) on each side.
  • Three colored rectangles of different sizes are cut out of colored cardboard of a different color. We also fold them in half and use glue to connect them as shown in the photo above.
  • On the rectangles we write with felt-tip pens or paints congratulation for the father. The usual "Happy Birthday," "I Love You," and so on. If you want a handmade birthday card for your dad to be original, you can write something that only two of you will understand. Go for it!

Postcard "Ties"

Such a creative postcard is rarely seen. It is unusual, beautiful and easy to manufacture. Surely you are wondering about how to make such a card to dad for his birthday. Answer: very, very simple!

You will need, as in all previous cases, colored cardboard, white cardboard, colored paper, a pen, glue and scissors. And also a wire.

  • We glue the white cardboard on top of the colored one, paying attention to the fact that the white one is shorter by a centimeter on each side.
  • Next, you need to build something similar to a hanger from the wire. It will not be so difficult to do this.
  • Next, cut out ties from colored paper and "hang" them on a hanger. The hanger is glued to white cardboard.
  • Using a pen, in a beautiful handwriting, we write a congratulation to my father. And also, if desired, you can draw a frame around the edges of the card for greater color.

Everything, a creative DIY postcard for your dad for your birthday is done!

Postcard with the hands of a child

The above postcards are more suitable for adults - children are unlikely to cope with them, at least without the help of an adult. Children are better off trying to draw a birthday card for dad, as shown in the photo below.

Any child can handle this pattern. All he needs is cardboard, paints and his own palm.

  • The kid dips his hand in the paint, leaves its imprint on the sheet and goes to wash the pens.
  • Further, the child already draws a brush on the candles with a brush (although it can also be done using the fingertips). For greater beauty, you can draw wavy lines on the "short", as shown in the photo.
  • If the child does not already know how to write, then you can help him finish the card: ask what he would like to pass on to dad, and write it on the card.

Plasticine postcard

How to draw a birthday card for dad, we discussed, let's see how it can be blinded.

To make such a wonderful postcard, you will need cardboard, felt-tip pens, plasticine, a modeling board and a special knife - a stack.

  • The head and torso of the "dad" can be sculpted with any colors - as his imagination tells the child. For the head you need to roll the ball, then flatten it on the board with your fingers. In general, it will be necessary to flatten all parts of the body, since on the card they should be more or less flat.
  • The ball-body rolled into a sausage will become an oval. Handles and legs are also sausages.
  • After the little man is stuck together, we fix it on cardboard. We draw him buttons and a bow, as shown in the photo. We make a face: we draw eyes, a mouth, a nose and hairs. In the end, we write on the remaining plasticine-free part of the card a happy birthday for Dad.
