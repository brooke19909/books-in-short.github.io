Mysterious Egypt: cities and resorts

Egypt - a country with an ancient culture and rich history - invariably attracts tourists from all over the world. Many people come here for the sake of the crystal clear and transparent Red Sea, where, thanks to the hot climate, thousands of species of fish and amazingly beautiful corals live. Egypt, the cities and resorts of which amaze travelers with its architecture, attractions and tourist offers, is a must visit. At least once in a lifetime.





Hurghada

It is considered the main resort center of this sultry country. It is warm all year round, and right in the middle of the desert dozens of hotels are built that can satisfy the needs of any client. Every tourist who visits the city has a unique opportunity to go diving. From Hurghada, you can take a fascinating boat trip to the coral island or visit the Mysterious Castle, where once the beautiful Sheherazade a thousand and one nights told her tales to the harsh Sultan ...

El Gouna

If you plan to visit the most important cities and resorts in Egypt, you should definitely replenish the list with this picturesque paradise. Here, in the midst of the crimson mountains of the Eastern Desert, lies the real "Venice". Cozy houses are built on picturesque islands connected by neat bridges. The neighboring hotel can be reached by boat, just like in Italian Venice. The magnificent beaches and the beauty of traditional Egyptian architecture, the bright emerald lagoons with clear sea water are as if created for those who want to relax their soul and body.





Safaga

Egypt, whose cities and resorts boast ideal snorkeling sites , attracts divers and surfers from all over the world. The list of places to stay can be replenished with another unique settlement - Safaga. People who suffer from diseases of the musculoskeletal system, as well as skin ailments, including psoriasis, come here to relax and to be treated.





Sharm El Sheikh

Many tourists associate this name with Egypt. No wonder the cities and resorts of this country are considered the most environmentally friendly places on the planet. Travelers love the transparent Red Sea, filled with different types of flora and fauna. Sharm El Sheikh is a true paradise for all fans of underwater exotic. Fishing and the mooring of boats are prohibited here, so you can swim in a mask right from the beach, enjoying the beauty of the "Gardens of Eden" - that's what corals are called.





Aswan

There is another reason to visit Egypt. Cities and resorts of this country are attractive for tourists in that people get the opportunity to see one of the longest rivers in the world. To do this, travelers travel to Aswan, located on the Nile, south of Cairo. In ancient times, this city was considered a major shopping center. Aswan reached its peak during the reign of the Pharaohs. The world famous pink granite was mined here. Nowadays, people come here to relax in the winter to admire the beauty of the majestic, picturesque Nile, hear the ancient Nubian music and buy bright Nubian embroidery. In general, the resorts of Egypt, the prices of accommodation and food in which are very attractive for tourists, are filled all year round with guests dreaming of a wonderful holiday.
