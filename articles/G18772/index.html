Generous "filling"

The developers tried, providing powerful features for a smartphone such as Highway Honor 5C. The company received enthusiastic reviews about the performance. It is worth considering that for its price the device came out quite powerful and efficient.

The main thing in this model is the availability of the Kirin 650 system from HiSilicon. It allows you to harmoniously assemble two quad-core processors. In addition, the i5 coprocessor was placed on the phone, which works only with sensors and a sensor. The graphics processor here is the Mali-T830. The RAM here is 2 GB, which is normal for this price category. The internal memory is 16 GB, of which only 11 GB will be free.
