Negative reviews

Any resort has its own flaws and shortcomings. The Alba Royal Hotel 5 * (Side) was no exception. Reviews will tell you about such negative moments of relaxation:

  • instead of large double beds, the rooms have shifted single beds, which guests are not warned about in advance;
  • uncomfortable narrow blankets that do not match the width of the beds;
  • uncomfortable hard and flat pillows (and if you want to change the pillow, employees will not be able to help you in this matter);
  • the staff speaks mainly German and English, which is why it is sometimes difficult to communicate with employees;
  • despite the fact that smoking is prohibited on the territory, many guests smoke both indoors and on balconies, which causes non-smoking guests (the staff does not take any measures to combat this phenomenon);
  • Internet use in rooms is paid, and in the lobby area the signal is very unstable and slow due to the large number of people who want to use it;
  • in a neighboring hotel, with which this resort complex has a common territory, there is a mini-zoo, which is in terrible condition;
  • in the gym, many simulators are disabled, and the room is terribly stuffy;
  • there is practically no sound insulation between neighboring rooms (audible even when the neighbors just talk to each other);
  • weak animation (if some concerts are still being held in the evening, then in the afternoon you have to entertain yourself).
