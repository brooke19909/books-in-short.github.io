A bit about the hotel ...

If you plan to go on vacation to sunny Egypt, you should pay attention to Jaz Mirabel Beach 5 *. Here you can feel the cheerful and laid-back atmosphere that is characteristic of the whole country as a whole.

The hotel is located on the coast of the most famous resort of Egypt - Sharm El Sheikh. It was built in 2006 in the Nabq Bay area. In 2013, the restoration of the complex was carried out. In total, its area is 30 thousand square meters.

On site you can relax while admiring the scenic Nabq Bay. Jaz Mirabel Beach 5 * is just a ten minute drive from the international airport in Sharm El Sheikh. The hotel is focused on the reception of family tourists. On its territory you will find a lot of interesting things for yourself and plunge into a special atmosphere. The hotel building is made in the Tuscan style, and the restaurants serve the best dishes of Mediterranean and Oriental cuisine. Real oriental sweets will not leave anyone indifferent. The hotel has open doors for shops, bars and restaurants.

Number of rooms

Jaz Mirabel Beach 5 * is a beautiful hotel located on the Red Sea coast. The institution offers accommodation in comfortable rooms. Among 645 rooms you can choose apartments of the following categories:

  1. 102 standards (area 25 sq. M.). The windows overlook the garden. They are designed to accommodate two guests.
  2. 480 family apartments with a high level of comfort (area 37 sq. M.). These rooms can accommodate up to four people (2 adults and 2 children or 3 adults and 1 child). The rooms overlook the sea coast, pool or garden.
  3. 60 superior rooms (area 31 sq. M.). They can accommodate parents with one child. The rooms overlook the garden, pool or sea coast.
  4. 3 business suites with private pool (120 m¬≤).

All rooms of Jaz Mirabel Beach Resort 5 * are equipped with individually controlled air conditioning. Therefore, you have the opportunity to adjust the room temperature that is comfortable for you. All apartments have spacious balconies or terraces equipped with tables and chairs. Here you can have a good time with your family, contemplating the unique landscapes.

All rooms are equipped as standard, they include: luxurious bathrooms with toilet and shower, minibar, satellite channels, LCD TVs, safes, international telephone line, hairdryer, tea and coffee making facilities.
