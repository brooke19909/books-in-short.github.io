Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 * (Thailand, Pattaya): description, service, reviews

Many travelers consider Thailand the best resort country in the world. Thanks to a large number of interesting excursions, stunning beaches, the cleanest sea on the Thai islands, a favorable climate and the peculiarities of the Thais mentality, more and more tourists come here every year.

In the Kingdom you can relax throughout the year, the most important thing is to choose the right place. But in general terms, in terms of weather, the most unstable month in Thailand is September. At this time, rains practically do not stop here, and the sky is always overcast, especially if we talk about Bangkok and its environs. The best time to relax here is considered to be the period from December to March. By this time, the rainy season is already over, and the period of sultry heat has not yet begun.





Travel to the land of smiles

Thailand is a beautiful country where you can go both independently and through a travel company. Despite the fact that an independent trip will be more interesting and exciting, still many citizens prefer not to complicate their holidays and stay in hotels that experienced tour operators offer them.

There are a lot of hotels in the country, and no matter where you go, everywhere there is one that suits both the requirements and the possibilities. Below we will specifically focus on one of the hotels in Pattaya - Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 *.

Hotel Description

The Beston hotel complex will be an excellent option for a relaxing family vacation on the Gulf of Thailand. It is perfect for both young people and tourists who come for a family vacation. The hotel is new, it is one eight-story building, built in 2016, has four stars according to European standards, excellent service, comfortable spacious rooms, decorated in modern style.





Location

The complex is located in South Pattaya, in a quiet and peaceful area. The distance from the Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 * to the beach is one and a half kilometers, to the airport - 122 kilometers. South Pattaya Road is a short walk from the hotel. The famous Walking Street is 1.5 km away. from the hotel. By public transport, anyone can easily get to the nearest attractions and shopping centers. Guests wishing to appreciate the local cuisine will find many cafes and restaurants in the immediate vicinity of the complex.

For guests stay

For the comfort of guests at the Beston Pattaya 4 * hotel in each room all the amenities for living are provided:

  • air conditioning;
  • refrigerator;
  • TV;
  • the Internet;
  • kettle;
  • safe;
  • telephone;
  • shower;
  • hair dryer.




In total, the hotel has 231 rooms. Some rooms have access to the pool. There are family rooms. The hotel allows children of all ages. On the day of settlement, a deposit of 1800 rubles is taken.

Service and Facilities

The Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 * service includes such services as a left-luggage office, free internet, children's and adult pools, a 24-hour reception, a gym, and parking. It features a restaurant, a shared lounge area with a TV.

Beach

Judging by the description, Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 * is located 1.5 km from the municipal sandy beach (3rd coastline). Sun loungers and parasols on the beach are provided for a fee, beach towels are provided by the hotel.





Accommodation with children

The hotel allows children, but special services are not provided for them. Parents need to be prepared for the fact that there is no playground, chairs in the restaurant, rental strollers or cribs. Also Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 * does not provide extra bed in the room. Therefore, before booking this hotel, parents need to think carefully about whether they will be comfortable with their children here. Also, when choosing this hotel, it is worthwhile to understand that the road to the sea with the baby can be difficult and exhausting.

Cost

Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 * offers its guests the best value for money. If you compare the hotel with other accommodation options in Pattaya, tourists get more for the same amount. Approximately the cost of eight nights in the "Beston" with breakfast for two adults will cost about 55 thousand rubles. If a child is added to adults, the cost of the tour increases by approximately 20 thousand.

What do tourists say?

Judging by the reviews of Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 *, we can conclude that the hotel is a good place to stay in Pattaya. On the one hand, it is located in a quiet place, and tourists can spend their holidays here calmly and comfortably. On the other hand, it is very easy to get from the hotel to the party places that are on foot (20-30 minutes), and that on tuk-tuk (10 minutes).

Another important plus of the complex is its excellent rooms with huge panoramic windows. In the rooms, according to vacationers, it is clean and comfortable, the furniture is new, the equipment works flawlessly. The cleaning is good, towels are changed daily, glasses are washed, and drinking water is brought.

The hotel has a very friendly and friendly staff. The guys are trying to quickly resolve all issues, however, there are no Russian-speaking employees.





As for the food, the guests of the complex respond well. Breakfast is standard, everything is tasty and fresh, the trays are constantly replenished, you do not have to wait.

Among the shortcomings, guests note the lack of territory and balconies in the rooms, a small pool (about 80 "squares"), lack of entertainment for children, a long way to the sea, a large number of noisy Chinese.

As for the sea, in Pattaya, as many people know, it is not the cleanest, the beaches are dirty. But you can get from the hotel to the nearest island with azure water and white sand in just 30 baht.

Where to go in Pattaya?

Pattaya is beautiful because absolutely every traveler will find something to do here. The city has a huge number of attractions and various places that will be interesting to visit for any category of tourists.

It is interesting at all times of the year and in any weather. Even if you arrived in Thailand in September or October, during the rainy season, you still will not be bored, on the contrary, visiting many excursions in cloudy weather will become more comfortable.

Below is a list of places that are most popular among tourists vacationing in Pattaya:

1. The River Kwai.

A very rich excursion, which includes visiting amazing temple complexes, places where Thai boxing is taught, elephant and rafting, swimming in thermal springs. In addition, the tour included an impressive walk through the most beautiful place in the Kingdom - ‚ÄúErawana‚Äù (National Park) and swimming in a seven-step waterfall.

2. Bangkok.

A trip to Bangkok includes visiting the important attractions of the country's capital - the Royal Palace and the most famous Buddha Temples. It also includes rafting on long-tailed boats and climbing to the observation platform of the highest Asian hotel.





3. Cambodia.

A fascinating tour where you will see the world's largest temple complex known to everyone as Angkor Wat.

4. Discovery.

The Discovery excursion program provides for a visit to the main attractions of Pattaya in one day. It includes visits to the Khao Kheo Zoo, an oyster farm, a tiger zoo, a pineapple farm, the Temple of Hell and Paradise.

5. Madagascar.

Sailing on a yacht. As part of the program of this excursion tour, you will visit uninhabited islands with crystal clear water and snow-white beaches, a monkey island, have fun at the foamy disco and go fishing.

6. Khao Kheo.

This is an open zoo of enormous size, where you will have the opportunity to see many different animals that are completely not afraid of people.

7. Nong Nooch (tropical garden).

An amazing garden with many picturesque corners: Italian, Japanese, French corners - each is attractive in its own way. Plus, you will see the Orchid Garden and a folk show of trained elephants.

8. The park of stones.

The park has a unique collection of huge boulders, there is also a crocodile farm and a show is shown with the participation of reptiles. The program also includes a visit to the spice garden and snake farm.

9. The flight of Tarzan.

Fun for thrill-seekers. You are waiting for a flight over the jungle, carabiner crossings, bungee, "flying" bicycles, rope bridges and a lot of positive emotions!

10. Transvestite show.

"Tiffany", "Colosseum", "Alcazar" - great show programs. Beautiful outfits, interesting scenery, delightful dances of slender women who were recently men!





In the end, we can say that Thailand is a country that is perfect for both beach and cultural recreation. And if you consider yourself an undemanding tourist who wants to save on housing, take a closer look at the Beston Hotel Pattaya 4 * hotel, in which you will always be welcome.
