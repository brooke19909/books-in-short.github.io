Features of toponymy

Nowadays, from the point of view of history, it is absolutely necessary to follow the Volokolamsk-Moscow route. The distance between these cities is very small. However, during the trip you can gain a lot of knowledge about the history of the Moscow region.

Volokolamsk - Hero City

This locality played an important role in the Great Patriotic War. As in other difficult times, during the war years Volokolamsk assumed the role of a shield that reliably protected the capital from German invaders. At the time when the battle for Moscow was taking place, the western direction was the most important. The defense line stretched for hundreds of kilometers, this direction was commanded by K. K. Rokossovsky. The Germans attached great importance to the capture of the important Volokolamsk-Moscow road, a huge number of fascist divisions were sent here, among which there were many tank divisions.

For almost a month, large-scale battles in the area lasted. However, thanks to the courage of Soviet fighters, the invaders failed to break through the defensive line on the way to Moscow.
