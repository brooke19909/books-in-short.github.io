Hospitable Spain. Calpe - a cozy and beautiful resort

The warm, mild climate, beautiful landscapes, the gentle sea, clean beaches and picturesque mountains are met by colorful Spain. Calpe is one of the best resorts in the country, because there is everything for a comfortable and interesting vacation. The town itself is cozy and quiet, it is located in the resort area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Costa Blanca. Like many other Spanish resorts, Calpe was founded a very long time ago, although the tourist area in it began to develop only in the last century. At different times, the city was inhabited by Arabs, Phoenicians, Iberians, Romans, Visigoths. Due to its good location, the coast was often attacked by pirates from Africa, and for added protection the townspeople erected walls and built a city tower.



Spain offers all kinds of benefits to foreign tourists. Calpe is striking in its favorable climate, the sun shines here 10 months a year. This resort is mainly intended for a beach holiday, although there will be something to do for active travelers. The coastal zone is 11 km long, so absolutely everyone has enough space in the sun. Tourists note the cleanliness of the local beaches, two of them (Arenal-Boil and Cantal-Roig) have repeatedly been awarded the Blue Flag. Fans of diving and secluded relaxation will benefit from isolated bays; you need to get to them only by sea.

It's no secret that for many centuries Spain was engaged in fishing. Calpe was also originally a fishing village, today the world-famous fish exchange is located here, it works every day from 15:00 when fishing schooners call at the port. Near it is the largest number of restaurants and cafes, where you can taste amazingly delicious seafood dishes. In general, the resort has about 170 restaurants with different cuisines, so no one will remain hungry.



Spain has prepared respectable hotel complexes, inexpensive rented accommodation, different star hotels . Calpe will be interesting for travelers of different ages and with different incomes. Las Salinas del Calpe Salt Lake is a unique natural area that Calpe is proud of. Spain accepts a huge number of migratory birds. On the lake in the summer live storks, flamingos, royal herons.



Visiting interesting ethnographic and archaeological museums, swimming in the sea, yacht cruises, scuba diving will make an unforgettable vacation in Calpe. Spain has a lot of archaeological sites, those who are interested in ancient Roman times will be interested in visiting the Royal Baths (at one time the structure was used for salting fish). You can also get training in diving and sailing, rent a yacht or boat in a luxury yacht club. Mountain lovers should visit the climbing school and take a stroll along the local mountain trails. At an altitude of more than 300 m above sea level, you can play golf, city volleyball courts and tennis courts are also at your service.
