Spain, Canary Islands ... An amazing place for those who want a vacation

We know quite a lot about Spain itself, but if we are talking specifically about its island part, then many of us will be in a difficult position. But this country has a whole necklace of pieces of land, and each of them is like a precious pearl. And as for the most important value that Spain has, the Canary Islands is their name. The origin of them, as the scientists managed to find out, is volcanic, and as proof of this, one can already observe the cap of the volcano, which rises above Tenerife.



By the way, this island is a key point for all tourists. Why? Now let's try to figure it out. Spain, the Canary Islands (whose map is on a variety of specialized resources), store in themselves such natural diversity that it is simply unbelievable. People who can appreciate the riot of nature, and young naturalists will also be interested in visiting this corner of the world. After all, Spain, relaxation, the Canary Islands, nature and bliss - this is what awaits you after buying a tourist package. The archipelago, as a small unique civilization, lost in the vast expanses of the ocean, attracts travelers. The territory of Tenerife already known to us is small, but, to the general surprise of scientists, climatic zonality has been preserved here, as evidenced by plant diversity. People who want to combine beach vacations and educational walks do not lose out if they recover here. Spain, the Canary Islands are waiting for you and are guaranteed to be rewarded for the efforts spent on the road and the time spent on the trip.



Needless to say, this paradise has been a key destination for many years in the travels of many stars and powerful people of this world? But not only millionaires can touch the beautiful, these islands will offer you relaxation for every taste and budget. And with respect to the density of hotels, the northern and southern parts of Tenerife are very different. Fans of noisy parties will not interfere with those who choose a secluded vacation. Experienced tourists are advised to go for impressions in Tenerife. It will be interesting to everyone, both adults and children. After all, the island of eternal spring will not leave anyone indifferent. See for yourself!



The Spaniards themselves love to come here, especially older couples, here they relive the period of love. And it's hard not to feel this bright feeling, if the view outside the hotel window is amazing, the air is filled with the aroma of exotic flowers, and the sand on the beach is like silk. Speaking of plants. Scientists estimate that there are more than one thousand two hundred species.

The water temperature in the ocean will exceed all expectations of tourists. When visitors splash in the clear, warm water, they will surely recall the dank autumn weather in their native metropolis. Spain, Canary Islands - the key to quality rest; for ten days of their stay, they will heal the tourist from all the worries and experiences with which he lived in everyday life. This is truly an eternal spring, in which you want to return again and again, each time discovering something new for yourself.
