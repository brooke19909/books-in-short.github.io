Roman Hotel 3 * (Paphos / Cyprus): photos and reviews

Our article will be of interest to those tourists who are planning to recover in Cyprus and are looking for a budget hotel to stay. Roman Hotel 3 * (Paphos, Cyprus) is a three-star complex in the heart of Paphos. The hotel has an interesting exterior design, designed in the same style with the interior of the building.

Roman Hotel 3 * (Paphos, Cyprus): description

The hotel has a convenient location. It is built in the central part of Paphos, 400 meters from the beautiful Lighthouse Beach. The seaport is a kilometer from the hotel. In general, close to the hotel there are shops, taverns, restaurants, bars, archaeological sites. Getting to it is not difficult, the distance to the airport is only 16 kilometers.



A feature of the Roman Hotel 3 * (Paphos, Cyprus) is its appearance. Decorated in an old style, it is filled with the spirit of antiquity. The themes of ancient Greek myths slip through its decor, the rooms have a thematic design and are decorated with unusual frescoes. Antique furniture, statues, panels, lamps are designed in the same style and create a unique charm of the complex. The hotel is surrounded by a beautiful garden with lush vegetation. The hotel has a rooftop swimming pool with a sun terrace.

The hotel was built in 1990 and since then has been hosting many tourists every year. The last renovation of the complex was in 2013.

A little bit about the resort ...

Paphos is not just a resort, but an ancient city with many attractions. It was taken under the protection of UNESCO back in 1980. Currently, it is an elite resort for wealthy tourists. But at the same time, the city is not focused on children's leisure. In local hotels there is no children's animation. But the resort has a great variety of all kinds of restaurants, hotels, cafes, bars.



Paphos attracts guests from all over the world not with a developed infrastructure, but with a rich cultural heritage, its history has been at least 5 thousand years old. The city was founded by Agaperon. Here a temple was built in honor of the goddess of love, which now attracts an incredible number of tourists. One of the rulers of Paphos decided to move the city a little closer to the shore, which is why there was a division into a new and an old part.

Currently, Paphos is divided into two parts. Upper is located on a hill and is a commercial center. And at the bottom are hotels, cafes and restaurants. Local authorities are constantly working to make the city even more interesting for tourists. Now in the city a completely new promenade has been built along the sea, walking along which you can admire beautiful landscapes.

How to get to the resort?

We have already said that there is no difficulty in getting to the Roman Boutique Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos). The reviews of tourists confirm this. For example, there are direct flights from Moscow (flight duration is about 4 hours), the cost of which starts from 200 euros. The price of tickets to the airport in Paphos is always somehow more expensive. This point is worth considering. And from the airport to the hotel can be easily reached by bus. Cyprus has excellent transport links.

To get from Larnaca to Roman Boutique Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos), you can use three different options. The first is to arrange a transfer (it is convenient and not so expensive). Taxi services will cost about 110 euros. The third option is the cheapest, but more tedious and time consuming, since you have to make transplants.

Resort beaches

The beaches of Paphos have a completely different coating: stone and sand. Sea calls are far from always convenient, especially near rocky shores. The quality of the coast does not depend on the level of your chosen hotel. A three-star hotel can be located on a better beach than a five-star hotel. The fact is that the resort was originally aimed at the British and Germans, who prefer to relax by the pool than go to the sea. When choosing a hotel, you should definitely pay attention to this nuance.



It is believed that the best beach in Paphos is located in Coral Bay, and in Lara Bay you can admire the most beautiful coast on the island, there is also a reserve of green sea turtles.

Paphos Attractions

Paphos is rich in cultural and historical heritage, there is an incredible number of attractions. During the excavations in the old part of the city, a large number of mosaics were discovered, created long before our era. In those days, they decorated not only temples, but also the houses of the nobility. A museum was created in Paphos where you can see the best mosaic paintings. In addition, in the city you can see the ancient theater "Odeon", the ruins of an ancient castle, a complex of "royal graves". It is also worth visiting the fishing village of Lachi, the Akamas Nature Reserve, the Panaya Hrizoroyatissa and Agios Neophytos Monastery.

Aphrodite's baths are located near Paphos, as the myth says, it was here that the goddess came to the coast from sea foam. However, in real life this place is completely unattractive.

Most of the monuments of Paphos (catacombs of St. Solomon, the column of the Apostle Paul, mosaics of the House of Dionysus and others) are included in the UNESCO list of values.



Paphos is famous not only for its beaches and excursions, but also for its unique nature. A pine forest grows in the middle of the city; its healing air has been cured of diseases for many centuries. And on the outskirts of Paphos there is a beautiful park, which is home to hundreds of species of birds and even exotic animals. Alleys of the park are buried in verdure, walking along them you can admire its inhabitants.

In Paphos there is a water park, which is worth a visit with children. No less interesting is the aquarium, in which Mediterranean and tropical fish live in 72 aquariums, and a bird park with pink flamingos, toucans, parrots, cranes and other birds.

Hotel Roman Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos): reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to discuss the reviews of tourists who managed to visit it. Hotel Roman Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos) has a very favorable location.
