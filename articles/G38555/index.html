Size matters

They could not think of screens of similar sizes 5 years ago. Now smartphones with a diagonal of 5.5 inches have become familiar. Six-inch models are increasingly attracting attention. But options with a 6.44 display may still surprise someone.



The first audience was pleased with Sony, releasing its "shovel" Xperia Z Ultra a couple of years ago. After that, some manufacturers made attempts to create models with similar dimensions, but then the buyer was not ready for this.
