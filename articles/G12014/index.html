Turkey, Side, hotel "Side Beach" ("Star") - description and reviews

I think everyone has heard of one of the most beautiful and famous for its attractions in the region of such a wonderful country as Turkey, Side. Hotel "Side Beach" (full name - "Side Star Beach") is a good five star hotel on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. It is quite far from Alanya and Antalya. But the village of Side with the ruins of ancient temples and other monuments of archeology is located only a ten to fifteen minute walk. The complex is surrounded by several white three-story buildings, a beautiful park with palm trees and exotic plants.



Turkey, Side. Side Beach Hotel - accommodation and services

The hotel has more than two hundred rooms for every taste. There are standard rooms, rooms for family people and specially designed apartments for the disabled. They are equipped with everything necessary for the resort, the floors in the rooms are wooden, the minibar is free and replenished with drinks. From the balcony, which is in every room, you can see the sea, a beautiful tropical garden or the surroundings of Side (Turkey). Hotels, prices starting at 7,000 rubles per person per week, as a rule, have a certain range of services. This hotel is no exception.

It can offer guests Internet (wired for money and Wi-Fi for free), billiards, tennis courts, the ability to wash clothes, as well as free parking. The hotel is very good for kids. There is a playground, a club, and a disco. If the child is not eleven years old, he can live for free with his parents, without taking up a separate place. Animation and the opportunity to dance are provided, of course, to adult guests. The hotel has a beautiful fitness room and spa with massage and aromatherapy sessions, not to mention the traditional sauna and hammam.
