Chalet "Primorskaya", the village of Novomikhailovsky: photos and reviews of tourists

Everyone who is looking for a high-quality, comfortable and interesting vacation for relatively little money will find a wonderful Primorskaya camp site. There are quite a lot of camp sites with this name. This article will focus on the camp site located near Tuapse in the Novomikhailovsky urban settlement. Many tourists like it from year to year for its service and prices. Perhaps that is why the reviews about this resort are completely positive. The minus is called only the remoteness of the camp site from the central resorts, which is also a plus for a large number of vacationers.



Location

The Primorskaya camp site is located near the modern resort town of Novomikhailovsky (Tuapse district). It is only 500 meters away or 4-5 minutes of calm walking. Novomikhailovsky has everything that a beach resort should have - shops, restaurants, bars, discos, a hospital, post office, beaches, a bus station, where buses from Tuapse and Krasnodar arrive, a diving club, a yacht club, a lot of fun for children and adults. From Tuapse, the Primorskaya camp site is approximately 42 km away. Despite such a short distance, the road takes about an hour, since most of it passes along the mountain serpentine. From Krasnodar on the highway to the camp site about 140 km or 2 hours of travel. The bus station PGT Novomikhailovsky is located about 3 km from the camp site. Getting to the place of rest by public transport is very simple. It is necessary to arrive by train or by plane to Krasnodar or by train to Tuapse, and then by bus to Novomikhailovka. You can take a taxi to the camp site.

Infrastructure

The Primorskaya camp site (Novomikhailovsky village) has everything you need to provide a quality carefree and pleasant holiday in all respects. On its territory there is a free guarded parking lot, a large swimming pool, a children's playground with a large selection of swings, slides, carousels. Those who are not interested in attractions may not go away from the base at all, as there are several shops, cafes, their own small market, 6 covered stalls with goods of various kinds, kiosks, a tour desk, a library, a massage room (for money) , has its own concert complex and a very spacious dance floor. For sports enthusiasts, sports grounds for playing volleyball, football, and basketball are equipped at the base. There are also table tennis tables.



Accommodation options

Chalet "Seaside" offers several accommodation options that differ in the availability of amenities, price and remoteness from the beach. In the areas "Grove", "Mountaineer" and "Lunar" guests are offered houses built in Soviet times. They are not equipped with amenities.
Shower, toilet and washbasins are located in the base. In the houses the rooms are two, three and four local. In the areas of "Sputnik" and "Sunny" are also summer houses without amenities, only new buildings. A toilet, shower and washbasin are within 50 meters. The equipment of the house is very simple - a set of necessary furniture. There is no refrigerator and air conditioning. For tourists from the "Lunar" and "Mountaineer" to the beach will have to go down the high stairs.

The Primorskaya camp site (Novomikhailovsky Village) offers an inexpensive option for accommodation in rooms where amenities are located on the floor. Such a service is in the Elbrus area, as well as in the Standard rooms located in the buildings of the Solnechny, Novy, and Sputnik districts.

Rooms with all conveniences are offered in the buildings of the Central and Flagman districts, as well as rooms of the Lux category in the building of the Elbrus district. These rooms have refrigerators, televisions, air conditioning and soft floors.



There is a Novaya Roscha building at the camp site, which should not be confused with the Grove district. The building offers comfortable rooms (only double), equipped with all amenities and the necessary electrical equipment (refrigerator, TV, air conditioning).

Soft coverage and balconies are not available in all rooms, which must be specified when booking.

"Volgograd cottages" are two-story, designed for 4 vacationers. The rooms are a living room, two bedrooms, a kitchen equipped with furniture and electrical appliances, a garage, a balcony.

In addition, having arrived at the camp site, you can stay in rooms with all the amenities of a building on Mount Orlan. There is also a private dining room.

Nutrition

Two types of trips - either with three meals a day, or only accommodation in the chosen house offers the Primorskaya camp site. Feedback on such a system is very positive, since anyone who does not want to pay extra money or eat on time can choose any cafe located on the territory of the base or outside it. If vacationers decided to eat at the base, a dining room works for them, where there are two spacious halls. The service in them is organized so that vacationers come to the dining room by the appointed time, when dishes are already waiting for them on the set tables. No flea market, no queues. Everyone knows the number of his table, sits down and starts a meal. Complex meals, including breakfast, lunch and dinner. For breakfast, the porridge menu (with and without milk), coffee, tea, cheese, bread, butter. At lunch, the tables are always first and second courses, salads from fresh vegetables, fruits. Dinner is complete without baking. The menu also has ryazhenka, kefir, sweets.

The cost of food is 750 rubles per day.



Sea

Wonderful beaches are famous for the village of Novomikhailovka. The Primorskaya camp site is located almost on the coast. The distance from the buildings to the beach is different, but not more than 250 meters. The beach at the camp site is own, very clean, well maintained and well equipped. Coverage - small pebbles, on which it is not painful to walk barefoot. Entry into the water is very convenient for all categories of citizens. Depth begins seven meters from the coast. The peculiarities of the geographical location of the base create wonderful conditions for a beach holiday, since the water here is much warmer than on other beaches of this section of the Black Sea. Behind the prominent cape you can find the greatest solitude and peace, since there is no beach tourist service, only the coast and the sea. The people converge there only when the sea is turbulent, since there are almost never waves behind the cape. The main beach is equipped with a toilet, canopy, shower, cabins where you can change clothes. There are also a lot of attractions, such as riding a "banana", "tablet", water skiing, catamaran. There is an inflatable slide for children.

Entertainment

Calm and pleasant vacation offers its guests the camp "Primorskaya" (Novomikhailovsky). The photo shows a spacious and clean pool, located in a convenient location on its territory against the backdrop of the virgin forest of the reserve.


It is especially popular with vacationers when it storms the sea. Fresh water in the pool, free sunbeds and sun loungers with umbrellas around are always available. Using the pool costs 150 rubles for the whole day. In the evening, guests of the Primorskaya camp site can relax in any of the 6 cafes located near the beach. There, until 12 at night, music plays, a billiard room. Fans of a more relaxing holiday will enjoy evening walks along the promenade, admiring the sunset, and during the daytime active tourists can walk along the territory of the reserve adjacent to the base. At the very base of "Seaside" animation, as such, no. But on the concert stage, evenings of music with dancing are often arranged, competitions are held, holidays are organized. In addition, in just 3-5 minutes a quiet walk is the Mikhailovsky Plovdiv Territory, where a beautiful promenade stretches along the sea with bars, restaurants, discos in the evening and at night, and in the daytime with water attractions for children and adults.

Additional Information

An excellent resort, inexpensive and comfortable is Novomikhailovka urban settlement. The Primorskaya camp site is located on its outskirts, which makes it possible to rest unusually calm for everyone who is not looking for noisy parties and fun until the morning. This location is one of the main features of the camp site. The whole local atmosphere with coniferous and floral aromas, the twittering of birds, the murmur of a stream (flows through the territory in an equipped channel) creates complete harmony with nature and offers absolute relaxation.

You can come to the base with children of any age and for any number of days. For children under 3 years of age, payment is not made.

Extra bed for an adult or child costs 400 rubles per day.

On the base you can spend the night not only in the room, but also in a tent or car. The cost is up to 150 rubles per day. Unorganized tourists for an additional fee can take meals, use the shower and luggage storage base.

Reviews

A very benign stay and good service is provided to its guests by the Primorskaya camp site (Novomikhailovsky). Reviews about it are mostly positive. Pros:

  • unique nature;
  • wonderful beach, where there are always few people;
  • good rooms;
  • good food;
  • the ability to take a room without meals;
  • low prices;
  • many inexpensive excursions.

Of the minuses are noted:

  • remoteness from Tuapse;
  • tiring road to the base.
