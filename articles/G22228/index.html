Equipment

Smartphone hardware

A 4-core production platform is at the heart of this gadget. We are talking about the MCM8228 chip from the company, the developer of Qualkom, which belongs to the Shapdragon 400 family. Its peak frequency can reach 1.6 GHz. A similar decision by Chinese development engineers provides an excellent level of performance for Xiaomi Red Rice 1S. A review of the complete documentation indicates the presence of a graphics adapter - Adreno 305. Based on all the above, we can confidently say that its hardware platform is enough to solve any problem. All programs, including the most demanding games, will go without problems on this device.

Memory subsystem

The ideal memory subsystem for Xiaomi Red Rice 1S. Smartphone equipped with 1 GB of RAM standard DDR3. This is quite enough to run any programs. The built-in drive has a capacity of 8 GB, of which about 2 GB is occupied by pre-installed software. Everything else can be used by the user for his needs. The MicroSD format is supported, and the corresponding slot is located next to the SIM card installation locations.

Appearance and usability

Despite the rather impressive dimensions (137 x 69 mm), the device in the hands does not look like a "shovel". All buttons are correctly grouped. Some of them are displayed on the right edge of the gadget (switching on and the sound control swing), and part - harmoniously fit under the screen (three classic touch buttons). The front panel is made of tempered glass "GorillaGlaz" 2nd generation. This material is resistant to scratches and problems with maintaining its appearance should not be. The sides are made of matte plastic. To ensure that your smartphone does not quickly lose its original appearance, you need to buy an additional accessory such as a case. Xiaomi Red Rice 1S is a smartphone model so popular at the moment that there should not be a problem with this.





But what about autonomy?

The weak point is the battery of Xiaomi Red Rice 1S. A review of its technical characteristics shows a battery rating of 2000 mA / h. Let's be objective: for such a diagonal and similar hardware resources, such a capacity will not be enough. If you use the smartphone only for calls, then one charge is enough for 2 days. In reality, with more intensive use of the device, it is only 8-10 hours. The problem can be solved either with an external reinforced battery, or with a second internal similar battery. In any case, you need to buy an additional accessory to increase the autonomy of the device. Otherwise, it may discharge at the most inopportune moment for this. Moreover, the most practical solution is an external battery, which can be connected to the MicroUSB connector at any time.
