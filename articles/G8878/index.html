New Year in Prague: reviews of tourists

The Czech Republic is one of the most attractive holiday destinations for Russians during the New Year holidays. During this period, she is especially beautiful. Since the beginning of December, the spirit of a winter fairy tale has reigned in the air, smart streets greet guests with fairs, sales, concerts, fireworks and festivities.





Why is it worth celebrating the New Year in Prague?

In the Czech Republic, they treat the New Year holidays with great love. According to tradition, when the famous Prague chimes break through , champagne and hot mulled wine will pour in the river, carols will come out. On Christmas Eve, smart, sparkling Prague turns into a real fairy tale. The atmosphere of magic and romance reigns in the Czech capital. Those who visit here will remember the New Year in Prague for a long time. Reviews of tourists note the beauty of cobblestone pavements illuminated by neon signs, the charm of ancient castles and the enchanting look of the city, where you can have a great winter vacation.



Here you will find entertainment for tourists with any needs. Fans of a relaxing holiday can meet the holidays in one of the cozy Czech restaurants. Fans of noisy companies prefer to spend the main night of the year in clubs or right on the town square - a place of universal fun. No one will miss New Year in Prague! Reviews from tourists are full of admiration from Czech ski resorts. Here you can find both slopes for beginners and difficult slopes for professionals, where international competitions are held. After such rest you will dream of exciting Prague for a long time.

To Prague with the whole family



The New Year in the Czech Republic is very beautiful: fluffy carpets sparkling in the sun lie on the roofs of houses, the windows of restaurants are covered with ice patterns, the tiled roofs of ancient castles put on white hats. You will receive such a snow gift if you spend the New Year in Prague. Reviews of tourists convince you to visit a real winter miracle, where on every corner you can enjoy delicious sweets, amazing ice cream and exquisite culinary delights from meat, fish and cheese. In such a cozy atmosphere it is especially pleasant to anticipate the onset of the main night of the year. And if the whole family is nearby, then such a New Year will truly be the happiest in your life. And you will understand that magic is very close.
