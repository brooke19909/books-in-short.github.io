Waterpark in Tbilisi "Gino Paradise": Georgian Paradise

One of the largest water leisure centers in Eastern Europe is the water park in Tbilisi. For visitors, a number of water attractions, entertainment areas and a health spa are equipped here.

Location Gino Paradise

Waterpark Tbilisi Gino Paradise is located in a picturesque corner of Georgia. On the one hand it is surrounded by a magnificent pine forest, and on the other - Lake Tbilisi. Near the entertainment center is a public transport stop. Bus number 60, circling the northwestern coast of the reservoir, brings vacationers directly to the gates of the water park in Tbilisi. For those who use taxi services, it is necessary to remember the address: Vakhtang Ninua Street, 3.

Water procedures in Tbilisi water park "Gino"

In the park there are both open areas working in the warm season, and closed, functioning in cooler weather. But if you plan a vacation in the water park of Tbilisi for the whole family, then the summer season is best for this, since it is at this time that the largest choice of entertainment.

On the territory of the center for children and their parents there are a large number of fascinating attractions:

  • 25 meter long open-air main pool;
  • children's water park with comfortable chairs, triggers and a waterfall;
  • pool with real waves and the attraction "Wild River";
  • breathtaking 31-meter toboggan, considered the highest and fastest in all of Eastern Europe;
  • salt and fresh water jacuzzi, which can be used by those who monitor their skin

The theme of pirates is quite naturally embodied in the Gino Ship zone, which is an indoor jacuzzi pool in the form of a schooner. There is also a bar on the deck of the ship, a delicious cocktail from which you can enjoy during a pleasant stay.

On the territory of the Gino Paradise water entertainment center, themed parties and holidays are regularly held to coincide with the birthday of one of the guests.

Wellness and SPA - relaxation in the style of Gino Paradise

In addition to the entertainment part, the water park in Tbilisi boasts a chic relaxation center, where its guests can choose for themselves a program that meets their needs. To do this, the center has two zones - VIP and Excellent. Welness part includes an original room, made in the ancient Roman style with the golden throne of A. the Great and the relaxing baths of Queen Cleopatra with natural components: wine extracts, milk or champagne. For fun companies, a jacuzzi with an interesting name "Beer Mug" was also located here, where you can not only drink beer, but also swim in it.

The most popular types of baths and saunas are located in the SPA-zone:

  • Finnish
  • Roman
  • herbal;
  • infrared;
  • "Foxy burrow";
  • tepidarium.

Next to the relaxation area is a fitness area equipped with cutting-edge exercise equipment. The staff of the relaxation center will help relieve stress and fatigue with the help of therapeutic massage.

Every year, reviews of the water park in Tbilisi are replenished with new positive comments from satisfied guests and residents of the city who visited Gino Paradise. Words of gratitude are expressed not only by local residents, but also tourists coming from neighboring and more distant countries.
