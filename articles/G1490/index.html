"Valley of the Volga." Country hotel "Valley IVolga" in the suburbs - rest, reviews

Russian tourism is developing quite successfully. New modern complexes that grow in the vast expanses of the country are no worse, and in many respects much better than foreign ones. A vivid example of this is the "Valley of the Volga" (Konakovo), hospitably opening the doors for guests in any weather, at any time of the year. The organizers tried to create comfortable conditions for people of any age and social status, youth, retirees, fun companies, children. It is good to relax here for absolutely everyone.

Description

The tourist complex "Valley IVolga" was built on the border of the Moscow and Tver regions in the village of Konakovo. This is from the Moscow Ring Road (if you go along the Leningradskoye Shosse) about 120 km. The complex occupies an unusually large area of ‚Äã‚Äãabout 11 hectares, located in a pine forest, on the banks of the Volga and on a separate island. Each district is a separate valley: "Forest", "River" and "Fishing". "Forest" is the largest in size. Cottages and houses are located here at some distance from each other, which provides guests with peace and some privacy. In this valley there is a large restaurant, a mini-zoo, gyms, baths, saunas, sports grounds, an equipment rental, an equestrian club, many convenient gazebos with barbecue facilities, playgrounds with swings and carousels.



The river valley is territorially somewhat smaller, but it is located on the very bank of the river. There is a wonderful beach, a pier, a boat station, a rental of water equipment.

Valley "Fishing" is the most isolated. Here fishermen of all skill levels get an excellent rest and a lot of pleasure. To their joy, there are conditions in Rybatskaya for the preparation of fish soup from fresh catch with their own hands.

Rooms

The country hotel "Valley IVolga" has a sufficient number of rooms to accommodate a large number of vacationers. At their service are one- and two-story cottages with all modern conveniences, a parking area, an excellent staircase, guarded parking lots, gazebos near the houses, attractive recreational areas. You can rent a room or a full cottage for any period - from one day to several months.

The IVOLGA Valley complex has rooms of all categories - from VIP to a simple tent. VIP rooms have an entrance hall, three spacious rooms, a kitchen, a bathroom, a sauna, a living room with upholstered furniture and a fireplace, a TV, internet, air conditioning, carpets on the floor and paintings on the walls. These numbers are calculated for 4 people plus two additional places.



Junior Suites also include three spacious rooms, chicly furnished and furnished, but they do not have a sauna and a fireplace.

Two-story detached wooden cottages equipped according to the latest requirements of modern tourism are very popular among vacationers. They are also designed for 4 people and two extra beds for an adult and a child.

For the middle class of the population, the country hotel "Valley of the Volga" is able to offer one-room "Junior Suites", which also have a set of all the necessary equipment for relaxing, a large double bed, a set of room furniture, a TV and a spacious bathroom.



Guests can also stay in rooms of the Standard and Budget categories. It has all the amenities, two single or double beds, a TV and a set of necessary furniture.

Students and supporters of leisure "savage" waiting for camping with tents. It is located on the peninsula, but for guests staying here, all services are available in the Lesnaya and River valleys.

Services for vacationers

The recreation center "IVolga Valley" does not let its guests get bored. In summer, anyone can rent boats, yachts, ATVs, bicycles, roller skates, equipment for tennis and table tennis, for air hockey and windsurfing, fans of quiet games are offered drafts and chess. For vacationers, there is a SPA salon, a sauna, a wood sauna, a well-equipped gym, numerous outdoor sports grounds, a billiard room, a bowling alley, and a children's playroom. Those who want can also go horse riding.

In winter, skis, skates, sledges, snowmobiles are issued.

Banquet halls

Hotel "Valley IVolga" is perfect not only for many days of relaxation, but also for various events such as anniversaries, weddings, holidays. For this purpose, several banquet rooms are equipped here.

The most capacious is called "Universal". It has a very convenient area and ceiling height, allowing you to arrange stands, podiums and accommodate up to 200 people.



A banquet hall with the unusual name "Aquarium" is popular. Its area is 120 square meters. meters allows events for groups of up to 60 people (banquets and conferences) and up to 150 (receptions).

There are also three halls, designed for groups of 25 to 30 guests, and a dance hall.

Pergolas and tents

For events in the warm season, not only indoor facilities are offered by the IVolga Valley complex. Konakovo is located in a wonderful area surrounded by spruce forest. The unique Volga also decorates it. Being far from city hustle and bustle, many dream of organizing celebrations closer to nature. For the services of such vacationers, the complex offers comfortable gazebos equipped with barbecue facilities. By agreement, skewers, charcoal, and flame-raising fluid are provided. The arbors are designed for groups of up to 20 people.

And in rainy but warm weather you can rent tents. There are two of them in the complex. One is located in front of the central restaurant. Its capacity is up to 60 people. The second tent has the name "Pagoda", which fully justifies its unusual appearance. It is also located near the restaurant, but is designed for only 20-30 people.

There is another structure reminiscent of a yaranga of northern peoples. It is called "Yurt" and accommodates a company of up to 20 people.



Additional Information

Wonderful country hotel "Valley IVOLGA" is able to accommodate an additional number of vacationers, in connection with which in the VIP rooms, "Junior Suite" and in the cottages it is possible to establish one extra bed for an adult and one for a child. Payment for this service is made at a separate rate, which varies depending on the time of year. On weekdays, payment is lower than on holidays and weekends. The complex may also contain animals. Payment for their accommodation is from 500 to 700 rubles.

All prices are calculated per day. Check-in is from 17:00 Moscow time, check-out is until 15:00.

When booking a room, payment must be made at least one day prior to arrival. In this case, the recreation center guarantees the provision of all declared services. In case of cancellation of the reservation more than a day, the amount of losses incurred by the hotel will be charged. In case of cancellation in less than a day, the customer must pay a cost equal to a one-day stay at the hotel.

Reviews

  • beautiful territory;
  • the presence of guarded parking lots;
  • spacious rooms;
  • own equipped beach;
  • the possibility of active diverse recreation;
  • playground, and if necessary the opportunity to order a nanny.

Among the minuses are such comments:

  • not quite convenient beach location for cottage guests in the Lesnaya Valley.
