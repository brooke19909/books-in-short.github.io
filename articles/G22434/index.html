History

In 1925, it was decided to build an aerostation in the city of Orenburg. The airport was already launched by 1930. In the fall of this year, a six-seater airplane on a Moscow-Tashkent flight landed on its territory. This event was the birthday of the airfield.

In 1931, it was decided to improve the air traffic of such a city as Orenburg. The airport "Nezhinka" lasted from 1931 to 1987. After a series of reorganizations, the airport was able to serve up to 400 passengers per hour, and in 1978 a special commission issued a certificate giving the right to operate the airport at the ICAO meteorological minimum of the first category.

In the near future, new changes awaited Orenburg. The airport received international status in 1992 and the first flight abroad was sent. Until 2000, there was an active accumulation of the base of aircraft and helicopters. Since 2009, a complete reconstruction of the facility began, and a year later we had a modern, competitive airport with excellent equipment.

In order to possess complete information about the flight status and possible changes at any time: delay, delay of departure, you can always refer to the electronic scoreboard. Passenger Information

In the frenzied rhythm of modern life, sometimes you need to arrange a flight with minimal temporary losses. Orenburg Airport offers full information on booking a ticket. Through the company‚Äôs website you select the necessary flight and go through the step-by-step procedure for filling out the questionnaire. Then, within the specified time, it is necessary to pay its cost through any cash desk in the city or make a transfer from a bank card.

If, due to unforeseen circumstances, the airline is forced to change the class of your ticket, for example, economy, instead of the originally ordered business, then you will be returned the difference in fares. If they provide a seat in a more expensive class, then the passenger can perceive this as a pleasant bonus.

Orenburg Airport warns passengers about the most common mistakes when booking tickets. It is necessary to fill in the fields in the correct order: first last name, then first name. In addition, the data should be fully consistent with those indicated in the document. Errors and typos are not allowed. In addition, check the pre-entered passport number and age of the passenger.

Under various force majeure circumstances, a ticket can be returned and a part of the spent amount can be returned back. If the flight is delayed or canceled due to the fault of the airline, then the passenger can get 100% of the ticket price. If you voluntarily surrender at least 24 hours before departure, you can get back up to 50%.

If you needed to make changes to the conditions of transportation, then you can contact the information. In most cases, the company meets passengers, but only the flight number and flight date can be changed. You will no longer be able to change the chosen direction and length of stay at the place of arrival. If there are no free seats on the new flight at the fare you have chosen, you will have to look for a compromise solution together with the manager.

Buying a ticket over the Internet has an additional degree of protection 3-D Secure. Most plastic cards support this technology. You will need to find out if this service is connected to your bank card and receive one-time passwords for issuing a specific ticket.





Additional service

Need a corporate flight? Book a charter flight to any region of Russia, Europe and other countries. You can order an aircraft for the flight by filling out a special form on the website. For any questions you may have, a manager will advise you.

Another convenient service is group ticket issuance. If you are a manager and send employees on a business trip, it will be enough to fill out the form, indicate the number of people, features of baggage and transfer payment for the flight. Minimum time costs with high quality service.

A separate line are the services of cargo transportation and the transfer of valuable correspondence. This is a very popular service, because within a very short time the recipient can accept safe and sound what you are afraid to send by mail. The exception is the transportation of pets in the cargo compartment, it can be dangerous to their life and health.

And finally, we must mention the possibility of choosing a place in the cabin of your class. Using the indicative map, you can determine the most comfortable chair for yourself. This service is paid, but the price is not too high.

How to get to Orenburg Airport

Interestingly, but such a question arises quite often. Theoretically, a bus number 101 runs to him from the bus station. In practice, it is very difficult to see it. At the bus station information desk, the dispatchers reply that there is no such flight, but airport employees suggest that it exists, but the movement takes place only from 8:30 to 16:30 with an hourly interval. If you do not want to risk that the plane will fly away without you, then it is better to use taxi services. They are not cheap, about 500 rubles, but you have to go for it. The second option is to ask for help from friends and relatives with a private car, if any.

If you decide to come by personal transport, then the airport has paid parking. For 200 rubles a day, employees guarantee the safety of your transport.
