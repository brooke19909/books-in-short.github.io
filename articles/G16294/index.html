Cheap hotels in Ryazan

Ryazan can rightfully be considered the historical and cultural center of Russia. This city is famous throughout the country for its many museums and reserves. In addition, Ryazan is also a large industrial, scientific and military center. All this contributes to the fact that a huge number of people visit it daily. Some come here for work, while others in order to get to know the history of our country more closely or simply admire the beauties of this land. For numerous visitors, the hotel infrastructure is well developed. But not everyone can afford to stay in first-class hotels. For those who are for some reason limited in finances, relatively inexpensive hotels are provided. There are a lot of them in Ryazan. For example, you can consider some of the most interesting options.

Hotel "Breeze"

Usually, cheap hotels in Ryazan are searched for business trips and those who find themselves in the city passing through. They are interested in minimal comfort and reasonable prices. There are about twenty establishments meeting such requirements in the city. What to advise to people who are looking for cheap hotels in Ryazan? Indeed, there are really many options.

First of all, pay attention to the Breeze Hotel. This is a small three-star hotel located in the southeastern part of the city.





Visitors can contact here around the clock. For everyone there is a cozy room for any period. And guests who have arrived in their car can park it for free. The hotel rooms are clean, bright and fairly spacious. Each of them has a TV and air conditioning. The bathroom has an additional separate shower. A bathrobe, slippers and necessary toiletries are provided for each tenant. It is very convenient and always helpful. Accommodation in such a room costs from 1335 rubles per day. According to many visitors, the Breeze Hotel is the best place to stay when choosing cheap hotels in Ryazan. For people on a budget, this is ideal.

Hotel Lovech Sport

There are situations when a person needs to spend time somewhere until the morning. For such clients there are also special hotels in Ryazan. Inexpensive for the night you can stay alone or in company with friends. For such purposes, the Lovech Sport Hotel is suitable. It is located on Kolkhoznaya Street, 1.8 kilometers from the city center.





The hotel does not have a restaurant, pool or fitness center. But for the guests there are 23 separate rooms of economy class for one, two and even four people. Each of them has a bathroom, toilet, shower, telephone and TV with satellite channels. The rooms are quite spacious and equipped with high-quality soft furniture. Separately, there are even non-smoking rooms. Visitors can eat in the evening at the local cafe-bar, and in the morning everyone is provided with breakfast. For a night in such a hotel you have to pay from 1105 rubles. This cost is relatively low, given the pretty decent conditions and a fairly wide range of additional services provided at the request of the tenant.

Hotel Priokskaya

Those who need a hotel in Ryazan inexpensively in the city center can take advantage of several establishments. Everything will depend on the location of the hotel and the financial capabilities of the future tenant. A good option in this case is the Priokskaya Hotel.





This 3-star hotel is located in a quiet side street, just a five-minute walk from the famous Kremlin. The institution itself is located in a building built in the 19th century. Outwardly, it is an unremarkable building. But inside everything reminds of the past. The hotel has 27 rooms with one or two bedrooms. In addition to a refrigerator and a minibar, each of them has a TV with cable channels, a bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. The rooms themselves are decorated quite simply, but very cozy. All visitors enjoy free Wi-Fi. In addition, room service is provided with the possibility of ordering special diet dishes. The rest can eat at the bar or restaurant. Staying at such a hotel costs from 1400 rubles.

Hostel "Like"

For tourists who usually come in groups, there are other hotels in Ryazan. For a day you can inexpensively stay at Like Hostel. Here for collective applications there are numbers for 6, 8 and 12 people. Beds in these rooms are located in two tiers. Near each of them there is a special box that is locked with a key. A fairly spacious sliding wardrobe is provided for storing personal items. In addition, visitors can use the refrigerator, microwave, washing machine and, if necessary, take an iron. All this is absolutely free. The cost of living for one person per day in this room is only 350 rubles. The hostel is often populated by couples with children. For this, deluxe rooms with a large bed are provided.





True, in this case, the visitor will have to pay 1300 rubles for the entire number. But this is quite a bit, considering that the institution is located in the very center of the city near the main historical sights.
