Resort Cheval Blanc Randheli 5 *, Maldives: description, service, reviews

The Maldives is an indescribable riot of colors, a composition of crystal clear azure water and white beach sand, an unforgettable vacation in the shade of palm trees under the gentle whisper of sea waves. Tourists from all over the world, including our compatriots, flock to the Maldives to enjoy the surrounding beauty and relax on the most popular beaches in the world. The best resort hotels are ready to accept vacationers all year round, one of them is Cheval Blanc Randheli 5 *. What is this hotel, what services are provided to guests in it - all this can be found in this article.





general description

A resort called Cheval Blanc Randheli is a five-star luxury hotel located on Nona Atoll north of Male. The flight to it from the mainland takes about 40 minutes. The atoll was opened in 2013 and is considered one of the best in the Maldives. The hotel belongs to the LVMEich group, which is a leader in the luxury goods market and the owner of several dozen elite brands such as Dior, Hennessy, Fendi and many others.

Hotel Cheval Blanc Randheli is one of those times when high value pays for its superior quality of service. The design and decoration of the hotel was led by the famous Belgian designer Jean-Michel Gati, it was thanks to his work that a unique atmosphere of comfort and luxury was created here.

The hotel offers 45 individual villas of various categories. The price of a trip to the Maldives for two is high, but an unforgettable vacation on the ocean is worth it. Each villa is decorated in the style of "loft", from their windows a stunning panorama of the lagoon or the Indian Ocean. It also has a private beach, a fitness club, hammam, spa and much more.

The hotel is located in a picturesque place, on all sides it is surrounded by a tropical garden, this is what gives an indescribable sense of peace and tranquility.

Weather in the Maldives

November in the Maldives is considered the best month to relax, as the season filled with wet steam is left behind, the time for sunny and calm days comes. There is much less rain, some tourists simply do not notice them, although, according to forecasts, the rainfall is quite impressive. This is explained by the fact that it rains mainly at night, and by morning, under the influence of very hot air, moisture evaporates quickly.

Holidays in the Maldives in November provides an opportunity to hide at least briefly from the upcoming winter.

Entertainment for guests

Cheval Blanc Randheli Hotel in the Maldives offers its guests an extensive entertainment program. Tourists can go canoeing, scuba diving and jet skiing, windsurfing and night diving. An interesting attraction for adults and children will be water adventures. These include boat trips on boats and catamarans, during which guests can observe the behavior of stingrays, sea turtles and dolphins. Fishing enthusiasts will be given fishing equipment.

You can also cruise on a snow-white yacht, at night in the open air to watch an interesting movie or conduct a seaplane safari.

For children

At the Cheval Blanc Randheli Hotel for children, services are also well thought out. A variety of game programs are prepared for little tourists. An on-site children's club is open, in which a team of animators works. They conduct various events, quests, outdoor games and much more.

There is a large playground for children in the open air, where kids can play and play in the evening. When it is too hot outside, they spend time in the games room, which is equipped with a wonderful library, lots of entertaining and intellectual games.

A club is open specifically for teenagers in the territory where children can socialize, play table tennis, table football, billiards, chess, and also take a walk in the adjoining garden.



Sports leisure

Cheval Blanc Randheli has a fitness club and tennis court. Vacationers can enjoy cycling, water sports, darts or table tennis. If you wish, under the guidance of an instructor, you can take a yoga class. Also, many spend their leisure time at the pool table, organizing entire tournaments.

Tourists go to the Maldives not only bask in the sun, but also improve their health. Free services include an outdoor swimming pool, as well as an indoor pool where you can spend time when it is too hot outside.

Holidays on a paradise island

New Year in the Maldives brings some newness to the familiar holiday feel. Instead of broadcasting the Twinkle, Olivier salad and snowdrifts, Russian tourists feel hot sand under their feet and listen to the gentle rustle of the ocean. The inhabitants of the Maldives are Muslims, but playing the role of hospitable hosts, they try to create an appropriate festive atmosphere for the guests of the island.

Several Christmas trees are decorated on the territory of the hotel, and Santa Claus in a red coat, in a red cap and fins solemnly takes pride of place. Dressed animators come to the rooms where there are children and congratulate them. In the evening, everyone is invited to take part in the celebration at the hotel restaurant, the uniform is usually free. The gala dinner ends with traditional fireworks and the symbolic burning of the scarecrow of the Old Year. Then the fun and dances begin, everyone is completely captivated by the joyful and light atmosphere of the holiday, so the New Year in the Maldives always passes unforgettably.
