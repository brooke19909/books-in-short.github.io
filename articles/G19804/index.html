DIY monkey. Schemes, patterns. Christmas toy

To make a dolly with your own hands, you do not need to make a lot of effort. We suggest that you familiarize yourself with simple workshops and learn how to make a toy monkey with your own hands from improvised materials.

Craft Socks

You will need two terry socks, a thread and a needle, scissors, any filler (cotton wool, synthetic winterizer), a white cloth and two black beads.





The monkey herself with her hands from socks is done as follows:

  1. Take two identical socks (picture 1).
  2. Turn them inside out. Now the monkey‚Äôs pattern is made : on one toe a straight line is drawn from the bottom to the heel, on the second the same place is divided into three parts, the sock is separated and a small piece is drawn on the fold (picture 2).
  3. The sock is cut off, the filler is placed inside and the part is stitched (picture 3).
  4. The second toe is cut in the indicated place, filled tightly with a filler and sewn in the place of the cut and on top (pictures 4 and 5). You got a body with legs.
  5. Three strips are cut from the second sock, which should be sewn in pairs and place the filler inside. Now the pens and ponytail are ready.
  6. The handles are sewn on the sides and the tail on the back (pictures 6 and 7).
  7. Now you need to make a face. To do this, sew the part made in step 3. Two white small circles are cut out of the white fabric, which are sewn over the muzzle, and beads on top. Ears are made from the side of the sock (picture 8). In the face should be walked smooth - you get a mouth.

Terry toy is ready!

Finger Toys for Toddlers





Master class on creating finger monkeys:

  1. Take fleece or felt in brown, beige and white. You will also need a fabric of a bright shade (for example, pink).
  2. Put your finger on the paper and circle it. Step back from the edge a few millimeters, and also draw the ears on the sides. Cut out the part.
  3. Cut the other same parts out of fabric.
  4. Sew together the two parts, leaving a hole at the bottom. Make a seam outside.
  5. Cut two white circles.
  6. Cut out a beige face.
  7. Sew the items by making a face.
  8. Embroider the eyes with black thread and the mouth with red thread.
  9. Cut a bow from a bright piece and sew in the middle of the figure.

Let the monkey with his own hands for each finger have its own bow color.

Knitted monkey

You can knit a wonderful toy on your own. First you need to make a head and ears.





Instructions on how to make crocheted monkey :

  1. From a thread of color A, knit nine stitches in a chain, lock it.
  2. Knit three single crochet columns (hereinafter - sbn) in the second chain, and in the other - 1 sbn, in the last - four sbn.
  3. Continue knitting in a circle without turning the work.
  4. Make two increases, six sbn, four increases, seven sbn, two increases.
  5. 3 sbn, nine times, two half-columns with a crochet (hereinafter - PSN) and fifteen sbn.
  6. Tie two loops in sbn, repeat the pattern nine times: 2 increases from ssn and 1 ssn, fifteen sbn.
  7. Insert thread B and knit a row behind the back wall of 44 sc.
  8. Knit four more rows from 44 sb.
  9. Twice repeat 20 sbn with one decrease.
  10. Six times knit five sbn with one decrease.
  11. Start stuffing the craft.
  12. Make a series of four sbn and one decrease, repeating the scheme six times.
  13. Six times repeat 3 single crochet and 1 decrease.
  14. Knit six times for 2 sbn and 1 decrease.
  15. Make 1 sbn and 1 decrease six times.
  16. Make six decreases and tighten all the loops using a thread of a different color.
  17. Knit the ears. Take a thread of color A and make six sbn in an amigurumi ring.
  18. The next row consists of 12 single crochet.
  19. Six times make one increase and 1 sb.
  20. Make one increase and two single crochet six times.
  21. Repeat six times one increment and three single crochet.
  22. Take a thread of color B and knit 30 columns connecting in a circle.

Making arms and legs





Workshop on how to tie monkey handles:

  1. From a color A thread, tie six single crochet into an amigurumi ring.
  2. Make six increases.
  3. Knit 13 single crochet.
  4. From a thread of color B, make six decreases three times and one single crochet.
  5. Seven times repeat 1 single crochet, 1 increase, 1 sb.
  6. Make six decreases.
  7. Fill the part with filler and tighten all the loops.

Tie another handle in the same way.

Master class on how to make legs:

  1. Twist the air loops out of color A.
  2. Knit four single crochet in the second stitch, in the next four - one sbn, three sbn in the last.
  3. Turn the product over and knit 1 single crochet in four loops in a circle.
  4. Make three increases and 4 sb, repeat the combination again.
  5. Knit a set of twenty single crochet stitches.
  6. Knit another row of twenty single crochet stitches using color B.
  7. Knit together 3 sbn, six times repeat 1 sbn, one decrease and again 6 sbn.
  8. 1 increase, six sbn, again an increase and six sbn.
  9. Five times make a decrease and sbn, knit 1 single crochet.
  10. Make five decreases and knit 1 sb.
  11. Push the filler into the part and tighten all the loops.

Tie another leg.

Make the torso





To knit a monkey body, take a thread of color B and knit the following rows:

  1. Pull six single crochet into the amigurumi ring.
  2. Make as many increases.
  3. Repeat six times one increase and one sb.
  4. Make a row of twenty single crochet stitches.
  5. Knit one crochet and two single crochet six times.
  6. Make a row of twenty-four single crochet.
  7. Six times 1 increase and 3 single crochet.
  8. Thirty sbn.
  9. Six times, knit 1 increase and 4 single crochet.
  10. Knit five rows of 36 single crochet stitches.
  11. Six times make a reduction and 4 sc.
  12. A row of 30 single crochet.
  13. Six decreases and 3 SC.
  14. Six decreases and 2 single crochet.
  15. Six decreases and 1 single crochet.
  16. Make six decreases.
  17. Fill the part and tighten all the loops.

Sew all the details together - the crochet monkey toy is ready!
