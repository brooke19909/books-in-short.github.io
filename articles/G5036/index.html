Entertainment Street - Volkin Street in Pattaya

Volkin Street is the center of nightlife in Pattaya, the main entertainment venue of one of the most popular places in Thailand. Toward evening, for transport, it completely overlaps, after which you can walk on it exclusively on foot.



This is a place in which life flows from 5 p.m. to 2 a.m. (all establishments must be closed by law by this time). There is everything for fun entertainment: all kinds of restaurants with cuisines of many countries of the world (there are also Russians), souvenir stalls and shops, discos and night clubs, Thai boxing, mini-markets, a cinema, etc. Although this is not the main thing here - everyone may surprise Pattaya. Volkin Street offers entertainment for adults: Go-Go bars, various establishments with easy-going ladies, erotic shows, frank massage, etc.

Here there are guesthouses and hotels, while quite inexpensive. Of course, they are not suitable for families with children, but fans of the noisy nightlife who like to be in the very center of the party will love it.

Go-Go at Pattaya Volkin Street

You got where you need to, if you are now a single man. Various exotic pleasures await you here at very low prices. Young pretty girls will hang in clusters around your neck, persuading them to come in to watch an erotic show or striptease in a bar. Here you can also find simple "night butterflies" pestering every single man.

In this place all the bars are closed, shooting is prohibited, therefore, you can not see, without having been inside, a photo of Volkin Street. Pattaya retains the intrigue, due to which more and more tourists would like to walk along this street. And, of course, visit her bars.



Pattaya Volkin Street Restaurants

There is also a huge number of simple drinking bars that do not have doors. The main articles of their income are high prices for bar fines, drinks, as well as lady drinks (a drink as a gift for a girl you like).

Disco & Nightclubs on Volkin Street in Pattaya

There are also several discos and nightclubs in this place. The best of these are Maxx and Insomnia. The first is located closer to the beginning of the street, the other - at its end, not far from the pier.

Discos and nightclubs here provide an opportunity to relax and dance, as well as get acquainted with young Thai people. Independents (self-employed) come to these institutions specifically to find a client. It happens that drinks are used as a payment, not money.

Security at Pattaya Volkin Street

At this point, the security issue is important. Among tourists resting and sipping strong drinks, those who professionally make money from pickpocketing can easily get lost.



At the very beginning of the street, the police can be found, therefore, there are no major fights or showdowns. But the theft of wallets and other minor troubles have a place to be, so watch out for property carefully. Also, do not go far from Volkin Street, especially go on foot through the pier to go-karting, on Tappraya or Protomnak streets. This area is criminal and quite deserted, and your pockets can easily be relieved by simply putting a knife to your throat. Better not take the risk.
