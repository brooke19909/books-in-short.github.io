The Element 120 card from Post Bank receives favorable reviews from the owners. There is not much time, but many people have already managed to use it. So what is this map?

On the Element 120 card from Pochta Bank, the terms of provision are very loyal. To become its owner, it is enough to fulfill some requirements of the lender:

How to apply?

Consider how the process of obtaining a credit card begins. To apply for an Element 120 card from Post Bank, you must visit any office of a credit institution or use the Internet. It saves time, because the application can be made from any device that has Internet access, even from a smartphone.





To submit an online application, a potential borrower needs to go to the Bank Post personal website. There, customers will see a menu item called "online application." Next, click the "credit card" section. It sets the desired limit on the card. Next, fill in all the necessary fields in the loan application. Tariffs and cost of service

I would like to immediately draw the attention of potential borrowers to the fact that the issuance of a card is not free. She is nine hundred rubles.
