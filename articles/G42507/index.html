general information

This hotel complex was opened almost 25 years ago. But, despite this age, the hotel is still quite new and modern. After all, renovations and repairs are regularly carried out here. Recent works of this nature were in 2014.

Malama Beach Holiday Village 4 * is located in the southeastern part of Cyprus, in the city of Protaras. In ancient times, several centuries BC, there was the ancient city of Lefkola. Now modern Protaras is a famous resort, which is famous for its beautiful sandy beaches. It is also interesting that it is only a 5-7 minute drive from Ayia Napa.
