Hotel "Mercury" (Hurghada, Egypt): description, overview of services, photos, guest reviews

Fans of hot sea vacations should pay attention to the four-star Hotel Mercury in Hurghada, which was opened in 1995. Last renovation was carried out in 2011. Here you can enjoy the developed resort infrastructure, the purity of the Red Sea and the beauty of coral reefs. The description and reviews of travelers will help you make an impression of the institution.

Location

Hotel "Mercury" in Hurghada is located at: Safaga road, km-120, on the very shore of the Red Sea, 12 km from the resort center. The nearest international airport is 6 km away. Resting here, you can visit the following tourist sites:

  • aquarium - 1 km;
  • Senzo Shopping Center - 4 km;
  • new marina - 10 km;
  • Star Fish Restaurant - 10 km;
  • Sakkala Square - 11 km;
  • Giftun Island - 14 km;
  • Egypt Miniature Park - 16 km.

Number of rooms

Guests of the Mercury Hotel in Hurghada are provided with the most comfortable living conditions. The accommodation options are as follows:

  • Standard room (22 m 2 ) is equipped with twin or large orthopedic bed. There is a cozy niche with a soft sofa for relaxing. From the windows and from the furnished balcony you can admire the hotel garden, swimming pool. In addition to two adults, one child can be accommodated in addition.
  • Superior (48 m 2 ) - a spacious room equipped with large or twin beds, as well as a sofa bed. You can enjoy garden or pool views from the windows and from the furnished balcony. In addition to two adults, two children can be accommodated in addition.
  • Family Superior (48 m 2 ) - two-room suite. The master bedroom has a large bed and a pull-out sofa. The small children's room is equipped with a bunk bed. The balcony offers garden or pool views. This room can accommodate three adults and two children.
  • Superior Suite (96 m 2 ) - a luxurious two-room suite. The spacious bedroom has a king size bed. There is also a living room with a large pull-out sofa and a dining table. There are two bathrooms. The private furnished terrace offers pool views.
