5 Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa, Turkey, Side: review, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

The 5 * Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa invites its guests to plunge into the inviting atmosphere of marine entertainment and relaxing spa treatments.





Hotel Description

5 * Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa is a kingdom of rest, relaxation and fiery entertainment. It has everything you need to spend an unforgettable vacation on the Mediterranean coast. The largest spa, a huge outdoor pool, children's entertainment and many other features are offered to guests of this hotel.

At the 5 * Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa, special attention is paid to the comfortable stay of guests in the rooms. Apartments will certainly please tourists with an original interior and the availability of all necessary amenities and technical capabilities. And if you have any problems or questions, then at any time the reception staff will come to your aid.

You will be amazed at the size and cleanliness of the private sandy beach of the 5 * Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa. And if you suddenly get tired of lazily sunbathing in the sun, then you can rent a bicycle or take advantage of many other opportunities for active and fun holidays.





Where is the hotel situated

The hotel complex Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa 5 * (Side) is located on the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea, 55 km from Antalya Airport and only 17 km from the center of Manavgat. Hotel guests can also visit such popular resort cities as Belek and Lara, which are 24 km and 42 km away, respectively.

Hotel Rooms

Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel has a number of rooms in the amount of 288 apartments, which differ in size and capacity.

Double and triple room rooms can accommodate 2 and 3 guests, respectively. Accommodation can be made in double or single beds.

Also available to guests are similar rooms of the category "economy". They differ from the previous version in the reduced area, as well as the lack of a balcony.

All hotel rooms are equipped with necessary amenities, the list of which is as follows:

  • modern flat widescreen TV with a wide selection of satellite channels (including Russian);
  • individual air conditioning system;
  • a minibar that is filled daily with refreshing drinks;
  • bathroom with shower and hairdryer, as well as cosmetic set.




Hotel infrastructure

The Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel (Side, Colakli) has the following resort infrastructure:

  • free parking with security and 24-hour video surveillance;
  • Reception desk - works round the clock to receive and evict guests, as well as to resolve issues arising during the stay;
  • the hotel has several conference rooms, as well as facilities for celebrations and banquets;
  • You can buy gifts for loved ones, as well as buy something in memory of the rest in a souvenir shop;
  • during the warm season, guests can swim in the outdoor pool;
  • the hotel has its own well-kept garden;
  • a large terrace from which you can enjoy the sea or mountain scenery;
  • a mini market where you can purchase food and personal hygiene items;
  • for the convenience of guests living on the upper floors, an elevator is provided;
  • some of the rooms, as well as a number of other hotel rooms, are specially adapted to the needs of people with disabilities;
  • in the cold season, you can swim in the indoor pool;
  • several restaurants and bars where you can get acquainted with the peculiarities of local cuisine;
  • free wifi;
  • a bank branch with the ability to exchange currencies and withdraw money from plastic cards;
  • laundry and dry cleaning;
  • rental cars and mopeds;
  • a left-luggage office where you can hand over suitcases and excess luggage;
  • office services (copying, scanning, sending documents by fax);
  • Private sandy beach with free sunbeds, parasols and mattresses.




Entertainment, recreation and sports

The hotel offers the following opportunities for entertainment and outdoor activities:

  • the pool has water slides for both adults and children;
  • in the fitness center, you can bring a figure into shape with the help of modern simulators or doing under the supervision of a professional instructor;
  • on the beach you can do sea sports and entertainment;
  • In the spa you can get a wide range of relaxing and beauty treatments, as well as use the services of the wellness center;
  • Guests enjoy such popular games as billiards, darts, table tennis;
  • daily and evening entertainment program provides a team of animators;
  • a massage parlor works;
  • it is possible to rent a bicycle to explore the surrounding attractions;
  • every night guests are invited to take part in an entertainment show;
  • For the youngest tourists there is a kids club and a games room.




Additional Information

The hotel Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey) has the following rules regarding accommodation and leisure for tourists:

  • check-in and check-out at 12:00 and 14:00, respectively (it is possible to use the paid services of early check-in and late check-out, subject to the availability of a sufficient number of free rooms);
  • for accommodation in rooms of children under 12 years of age there is no charge;
  • the size and layout of the rooms of the hotel complex do not provide for the creation of additional beds;
  • pets must not be brought to the hotel;
  • for the services received, you can pay by credit card;
  • every hour, a small bus awaits guests at the main building of the hotel, which runs to the beach.




Service Ratings

Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Side) is very popular among tourists. Rating (rating) of the services provided is as follows:

  • The infrastructure of this hotel complex is rated at 4.5 points. The convenience of the parking zone, as well as a high level of security, is noted.
  • Services and service level were rated at 4.1 points. Particularly noted is the work of the reception, and complaints and comments are mainly related to the animation program.
  • Hotel rooms are worth 4.6. Guests are pleased with the new furniture and good plumbing, as well as a good level of sound insulation. The quality and regularity of cleaning raises some questions.
  • The beach is rated 4.2. The score was lowered due to the fact that the infrastructure is not complete and well thought out.
  • Children's rest is rated at 3.9 points due to poor infrastructure, as well as low-quality animation. At the same time, the children's menu deserves all praise.
  • The hotel is rated 4.2 for its location, as there are many attractions nearby, as well as shops and cafes. At the same time, there are practically no nightclubs and other entertainment facilities in the vicinity.

Review of Diamond Elite Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel in Turkey

Since this hotel is already loved by many tourists, there are a lot of positive reviews about it:

  • guests are immediately fed upon arrival, even if it is not yet time for check-in;
  • since the hotel is quite new, all rooms, furniture and technical equipment are in good condition;
  • both by the pool and on the beach a large number of sunbeds are provided, and therefore there are no problems with the lack of free places;
  • the road to the beach goes through picturesque green alleys;
  • the staff is very friendly and helpful (despite the fact that no one speaks Russian, they always try to help and solve all the problems that arise as soon as possible);
  • Two pools are provided (outdoor and indoor), designed for different weather conditions;
  • excellent varied nutrition (especially a huge selection of fruits).




Remarks and complaints

  • half of the hotel employees speak neither Russian nor English;
  • there are no clothesline for drying clothes on the balcony (you have to take it with you);
  • due to the fact that the animators occupy almost half of the pool for their games, there is practically nowhere to swim and swim;
  • the hotel does not have its own territory as such;
  • bad work of animators ;
  • Despite the fact that a tennis court was declared in the brochure, in fact it was not there.
