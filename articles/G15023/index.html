Hotel Don Juan Tossa 4 * (Spain / Costa Brava): photos and reviews

Spain is a wonderful country. Its resorts have long gained popularity among our tourists. Moreover, not only one coast is of interest here, but also famous attractions. That is why we want to talk about one of the Costa Brava hotels.

Location

Hotel Don Juan Tossa 4 * is located in the town of Tossa de Mar, near the center. The distance from Barcelona Airport to the hotel is 105 kilometers. But the Girona airport is much closer. It is only 42 kilometers away. From Hotel Don Juan Tossa 4 * to Playa Gran Beach, no more than two hundred meters.



The hotel was built in 1988. After many years of work, it was partially renovated in 2012. Hotel Don Juan Tossa 4 * - an acceptable place for a quiet budget holiday. It is suitable for those tourists who plan to relax on the beach and see the country, and at the same time are not too demanding on living conditions.

Don Juan Tossa Hotel 4 *: description

The hotel offers its guests more than three hundred rooms in a three-story and four-story building. The hotel, of course, cannot boast of a variety of categories of apartments, as it relates to the budget option. All rooms are Standard. They are designed to accommodate two adult guests and two children. Among the apartments there are several rooms for people with special needs.

The rooms have a TV, telephone, bathroom, air conditioning, cable TV and a balcony.

Hotel infrastructure

Hotel Don Juan Tossa 4 * has a good infrastructure. On its territory there is a bar, restaurant, parking, conference room, outdoor pool, Internet cafe, safe, park.

Sports and Entertainment

Don Juan Tossa Hotel 4 * offers its guests to do water aerobics, play tennis, darts, billiards. There is also a tour desk.

Services for children

The hotel administration is trying to take care of its young guests. Therefore, the hotel has a kids club (from 2 to 12 years old). In the evenings, for the kids, the hosts arrange discos. Animation programs entertain children from June to September.

Dining at the hotel

Don Juan Tossa Hotel 4 * offers its visitors a buffet. The hotel restaurant is very good. It serves a delicious continental breakfast and serves a variety of dishes for dinner and lunch. If you are going to go on an excursion and understand that you miss the time for lunch or dinner, then at the reception you may be given dry rations in return.



As a rule, vacationers choose the all-inclusive option, but you can take half board or a continental breakfast. All Inclusive, unfortunately, does not include any drinks, so they must be purchased in a restaurant at a decent price.

Hotel beach

Don Juan Tossa Hotel 4 * (hotel photos are given in the article) is located near the beach (200-300 meters). On the shore there are sun loungers and umbrellas, but they are paid. But this does not stop being placed directly on the sand.

A bit about the resort

Don Juan Tossa Hotel 4 * (Spain, Tossa), as we have already mentioned, is located in Tossa de Marr. It is a small town in the east of Spain. It belongs to Catalonia and is one of the most beautiful places on the Costa Brava. The city is surrounded by greenery and amazes tourists with warmth and tranquility. For a long time it was known as a favorite vacation spot of wealthy Europeans. As a resort, Tossa de Marr began to develop in the mid-twentieth century.

The city is in a cozy bay, and the coast is indented with cliffs. High cliffs form very beautiful deep grottoes and narrow straits.

Attractions and entertainment

Resting at Don Juan Tossa Hotel 4 * (photos are given in the article), you should definitely take the opportunity to see all the most interesting places, but there are a lot of them.

Tourists love to visit the medieval fortified city called Villa Vella, built in the twelfth century to protect against pirates, on the rocky coast of Tossa de Mar. To this day, towers and ramparts are very well preserved. The castle houses a museum of modern art.



Fans of museum exhibits will be interested in visiting the lighthouse, the ruins of the ancient Roman Villa, the historical museum on the shore, and ancient churches. Vacationers can walk along the well-known walking route along a stone staircase that leads to Villa Vella, and then up along the winding walking paths you can climb to the site and look at the surroundings and the sea. There is a place for viewing on the tower of the Moors in the fortress. And along the way there are many cafes offering food and relaxation, enjoying Spanish motives.

In addition, tourists can stroll along the Costa Blanca, wander through the streets of the Old Town, visit the botanical park "Sa Riaera". The town is still famous for its national festivals. Such holidays demonstrate the historical traditions of the region.

Resting in Tossa de Mar, tourists try to definitely visit Barcelona. It is a terrific city worth seeing. It is world famous for its fountains built on Mount Montjuic. They have long been the most visited place in Barcelona. Once fountains were built by Carles Bugyas, and then reconstructed in 1992 for the Olympics. They impress with their beauty all the guests of the Catalan capital. An amazing combination of music, water and light is an unforgettable sight.



A beautiful show begins after sunset, to the sounds of classical music, the jets take a variety of shapes and colors, they soar upward, then scatter millions of drops. To create such a colorful effect, 50 various shades are used. The harmonious combination of light, music and water is the key to the success of this show. Each year, an amazing musical pyrotechnic show is also held here in honor of the end of the famous Catalan festival.

Don Juan Tossa Hotel 4 *: reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to discuss the reviews of tourists who managed to visit here. Hotel Don Juan Tossa 4 * (Costa Brava) is one of the complexes with a bit of controversial reviews. I would like to note that this is an economy class hotel. Vacationers come here to not only relax on the sea and enjoy the local scenery, but also to see Catalonia, which is famous for its beautiful and interesting places. Tourists note that in the hotel, as in the city itself, a lot of Spanish pensioners are resting. Periodically, teenage groups lodge in the hotel, who have something like our camps here. They are settled in the building behind the main building.

The hotel always has a quiet and calm environment, as in the whole city. There is a night disco next to the main building - everyone can go to it. In general, the hotel is not aimed at youth. People come here far from night parties. Active young people will probably be bored here, and therefore it is better to look for a city with a richer nightlife.



I would like to pay special attention to hotel rooms. They are not small, but with shabby furniture. Particularly picky holidaymakers note that they need to be repaired for a long time, and the furniture should be replaced with newer. The apartments are cleaned daily and pretty good. Tipping the maid can not leave. Towels are changed daily, and bed linen a couple of times a week. Soap, gel, shampoos staff refills as needed. But bathing caps, sticks and cotton pads are not here.

Food is the most interesting topic, causing heated debate among tourists. Moreover, as a rule, reviews about the same place can be very controversial. This also applies to Don Juan Tossa Hotel 4 * (Tossa de Mar). However, I want to note that most of the vacationers are very pleased with the food.

For breakfast, for example, eggs, omelet, cheese, sliced, sausage, breakfast cereals, numerous fruits, milk are served. From drinks offer juices, coffee, tea.

For lunch and dinner, it offers a larger assortment: fish, meat, soups, sweets, fruits (peaches, watermelon, pears, apples, grapes, oranges, grapefruits, kiwi, bananas, pineapples) and vegetables, ice cream.
