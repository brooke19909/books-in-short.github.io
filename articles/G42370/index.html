Hotel Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 * Greece, Crete: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Gouves is a resort area located eighteen kilometers from Heraklion, formed by two villages: Pano Gouves and Kato Gouves, the last of which is located on the Mediterranean coast, has developed infrastructure, excellent beaches (marked with the Blue European Flag), a magnificent promenade, many restaurants, taverns, shops and modern hotels. One of such hotels, namely, Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 *, will be discussed in the following review.





Gouves is ideal for relaxing and relaxing with young children. Here the clear sea, well-groomed sandy beaches, mild climate, this is what makes holidays with children in Gouves as comfortable as possible. Young people here also have something to occupy themselves with: nightlife is not too stormy, but rather lively, there are several clubs and many bars and restaurants for every taste and budget. It should also be noted that in the vicinity of the resort there are a large number of various attractions and architectural monuments that are most conveniently inspected by a rented car.

Hotel description Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 * in Crete

Blue Palace is a renovated hotel located in the popular tourist destination Kato Gouves. By European standards, the hotel has recently been assigned a four star category. The hotel complex has about a hundred comfortable, spacious rooms located in two buildings. There is an outdoor pool with a separate section for children, a clean beach within walking distance and many interesting places near the hotel.





The hotel was built in 1998, renovation of rooms and buildings took place in 2013.

The location of the tourist complex

Aparthotel Diogenis Blue Palace 4 * (Crete, Greece, Kato Gouves) is located eight kilometers from Hersonissos, and fifteen kilometers (no more than 20 minutes drive) from Heraklion airport. Not far from the complex, five kilometers away, is the famous Cretan Aquarium. The village center is 500 meters away. At a distance of no more than 20 kilometers are many attractions and interesting places.

Number of rooms

The complex offers its guests 96 comfortable and sunny rooms of various categories (Standard, Double Sea View, Apartment ). Each is equipped with individual air conditioning, satellite TV, refrigerator, telephone. The rooms also have a balcony or terrace, shower, bathroom amenities, hairdryer. Cleaning is done every day.

Features

  1. The hotel offers family rooms and non-smoking rooms.
  2. Children under 12 years old without extra bed are accommodated in the room free of charge.
  3. Upon prior request, pets are allowed with a weight of no more than five kilograms (a separate fee may be charged for this service).

Catering

Dining at Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 * is organized according to the "All inclusive" system in a buffet format. It features a main restaurant and pool bar, as well as a Crazy bar (not included in the All inclusive system). The hotel's main restaurant serves a variety of Mediterranean, Cretan and international dishes daily.





Restaurant Schedule:

  • Breakfast starts at 7:30 and ends at 9:30.
  • Lunch from 12:30 to 14:30.
  • Snack from 16:00 to 17:00 (toasts, pastries, pizza).
  • Dinner from 19:00 to 21:00.

During the day, the hotel guests are offered an unlimited selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks: water, juices, tea, coffee, hot chocolate, beer, wine, cognac, ouzo, crayfish (local production).

Beach area

Diogenis Blue Palace 4 * (Greece / Greece) has a private beach, which is fifty meters from the hotel (on the first coastline). Passage to the sea is via a small road. The beach is sandy, you can reach it in just one minute, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided for a fee, there are also free sunbeds and tents, but in limited quantities.





Entertainment and sports

For entertainment, the Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 * in Greece offers a game room, a TV zone, karaoke, Russian-language animation, BBQ and Greek evenings. For a fee, guests can play billiards.

Hotel Services

Diogenis provides its guests with both a paid and a free range of services.

Among the services for a fee are:

  • room service;
  • safe at the reception;
  • Internet corner
  • massage;
  • spa treatment center;
  • laundry;
  • dry cleaning;
  • doctor's services (if necessary).

Free hotel services:

  • housekeeping;
  • sun terrace;
  • pool with umbrellas and sun loungers;
  • pool bar;
  • Wi-Fi in the lobby
  • luggage storage room;
  • iron at the reception;
  • 24-hour reception with Russian-speaking staff;
  • parking.

Hotel for children

Judging by numerous reviews, Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 * is perfect for families with children of different ages. Here your children will be very cozy and comfortable. For young children, you can order cots and high chairs in the restaurant, and also for entertainment there is a games room, a playground (on the street) and a pool. The restaurant has a special diet menu. Older children will also be very interested here, they will have a great rest and gain strength before the new school year.





Many guests are strongly advised to visit the oceanarium and water park with children, which can easily be reached by bus. Every child will be delighted with these places.

Gouves attractions and what you can visit while staying at the Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 *

Hotel guests are very convenient to get to the various sights of the village and the island. A bus stop is a 20-minute walk from the hotel, there are car rental facilities in the immediate vicinity, and there is free parking on site at Blue Palace. Also for those who like to go on guided tours, the hotel has a tour desk.

Attractions nearby:

  • Cretan Golf Club is 5.9 km away.
  • Water park 6.8 km.
  • Oceanarium 7 km.
  • Knossos Palace is 14 km away.
  • City garden 15 km.

Also at a distance of less than 15 km from Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 * r there are museums of folk art, the history of Crete, Nikos Kazantzakis.

A little further, at a distance of 15-20 km, there are such popular attractions as the Art Gallery, the Archaeological Museum, the sea fortress, the Pankritio stadium, the Venetian walls.





Fans of natural attractions should be interested in excursions to the Palace of Knossos and the cave of Zeus, as well as a trip to Lake Kournas and Balos Bay. According to tourists, a tour to the Samarya Gorge is very interesting. If desired, you can go to the islands (boat trip).

And of course, all vacationers in Gouves should visit the most popular excursion in Santorini. Santorini is considered the hallmark of Greece and one of the most interesting islands in the whole world. Here you will find colorful beaches, stunning sunsets, incredible surroundings and special architecture (numerous white houses with bright blue roofs).

Fans of sightseeing holidays will find many interesting things in the vicinity of Gouves. You can visit some historical sites on your own and even walk to them. In addition, not far from the village are the most popular cities of the island (Heraklion, Hersonissos), which also have something to see. For trips to Crete, many are advised to rent a car: the traffic here is calm, the roads are good, and using the map you can easily find the main attractions.

To summarize

If you are planning your vacation along the route Greece - Crete - Heraklion, Diogenis Blue Palace Hotel 4 * is a very good option for accommodation. Following a recent leadership change, the hotel has been upgraded and upgraded to four stars.
