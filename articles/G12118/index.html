"Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge": description, specifications and reviews

In 2015, Samsung made a breakthrough and radically changed the concept of creating smartphones. Then new unusual flagships entered the market - S6 and S6 Edge. The version with curved edges of the display became the most popular, so in the first months, the developers did not have time to make up for the shortage of this model in store windows.

Comparing with last year's flagship S6 edge, it is noticeable that the brand new models immediately increased the diagonal of the display, so the Galaxy S7 Edge is no longer a smaller copy of its older brother, but a smartphone with the same dimensions.

At first glance, there are no external changes. But still, the developers finalized the sharp edge of the case, made it more curved. Now smart has become more convenient to hold in your hand. Its dimensions also increased slightly due to the new volumetric battery and durable finishing materials.

The main changes that touched the flagship body are the return of the protective coating against water and dust. Here is the familiar IP68 standard, which works stably, without any errors. Basically, the design remained the same as before. Of course, no frames can be found on the screen. Above the display is a speaker, on the sides of it are sensors and a front camera. The company logo flaunts right there. Under the screen is a classic button.

Energy costs

A special option, Always On, was presented at the press release, which looks really good. It works as follows: when the user locks the device, the calendar, clock and some notifications are still displayed on the screen. That is, the phone is still in partial activity. The developers claimed that Always On spend only 1% of the charge per hour. In practice, it turned out that the function is quite expensive for the energy of the smartphone. If you turn it off, the phone will live 20-30% longer.

‚ÄúMemorable‚Äù changes

Last year, the company decided to remove support for memory cards from all its flagships. Most likely, such changes have occurred in the pursuit of fashion. Then followed a series of mistakes. It was decided to release smartphones on 32, 64 and 128 GB. In practice, it turned out that it is almost impossible to find the latest version of the device. In 2016, microSD support was returned. The situation with internal memory remained the same with the Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge. The price depends on the version of the device. The most expensive is the 64 GB model.





Considering that you can now put microSD in your smartphone, the 64 GB version has become less popular. By the way, the tray for the memory card and SIM card is combined. The user can choose what he needs: two SIM cards or one, but paired with a memory card.

"RAM" in the new flagship also became larger - 4 GB. Naturally, such an improvement affected the operation of the phone.

Performance

The company changed its preferences and instead of the famous Qualcomm, the Exynos 8890 is located inside the smartphone. Fans were at first skeptical about replacing the processor, but, as various tests show, the 8-core device not only bypassed the latest Qualcomm Snapdragon 820, but also noted moderate power consumption.

Testing the new Mali-T880 graphics chipset also showed stunning results. Compared with the previous version, this one has become "faster" by as much as 80%.

Activity

The new Galaxy S7 Edge has a powerful 3600 mAh battery. Its autonomy shows excellent results, given the size of the display, performance indicators and multitasking. The developer took care of excellent optimization, which allows you to save the device‚Äôs charge for a long time.

Despite the new-fangled Type-C connectors, Samsung did not move away from the proven microUSB. The kit includes a charger that allows you to power the phone from 0 to 100% in just 100 minutes. There is also the possibility of wireless charging.

Pleasant trifles

At the time of the release of Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge, it was not found in Svyaznoy. Which seriously disappointed fans, especially those who had been waiting for a new product with an amazing camera since the press release.





There is nothing special to say about the front. Its resolution is 5 megapixels, it copes with its ‚Äúselfie‚Äù function perfectly. Face correction, color change and correction of its geometry were added to the menu.

Thus, the developers did not increase the number of pixels, but their size, which qualitatively affected the photo.
