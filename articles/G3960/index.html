Crete Island: best beaches and resorts

Greece is a country of olive trees, rich culture and golden sea coasts. One of the most popular tourist destinations is Crete: the best beaches, comfortable hotels, interesting excursions - everything for tourists! The abundance of beaches is easy to explain: Crete is located in the middle of the Mediterranean Sea, all its shores are gentle sandy or sandy-pebble.

Panormo is a small town where tourists can easily accommodate. There are a couple of excellent restaurants, souvenir shops, small supermarkets, as well as the ruins of ancient buildings. On the outskirts of Panormo is the large Marina Palace Hotel.

On its territory there is a sandy beach, which is a small bay or bay. It is well equipped and has sun loungers throughout. Nearby is a pebble entrance to the water, designed for outdoor enthusiasts. In general, if you prefer a quiet relaxing holiday in a cozy corner of the earth, then you should choose Crete, the best beaches of which are difficult to list, since each of them is good in its own way.

You can find great beaches on the east coast, for example, the resort of Elounda, whose sandy beaches are popular among the political elite of Greece and Russian oligarchs. Part of the territory is built up with cottages and villas with pools. The best sandy beaches in Crete are located mainly along its northern coast. True, few choose them, because here it is more windy.

There are many amazing places in Greece, but one of the most amazing is Crete. The best beaches of this island are literally created by nature itself, for example, a beach with the unusual name "Deer Island". This is a small peninsula, which can be reached by water, passing through a narrow spit.





But the beach is worth it: it is covered with pale pink sand, here you can swim with children, as nature itself has formed a shallow backwater with azure blue water.

It is no coincidence that many tourists choose Crete for their holidays with children - the best beaches on the island are rightfully recognized as one of the cleanest and most environmentally friendly on earth. Local air has a beneficial effect on health, especially on the beach "Kedrodasos", which means "cedar grove" in Greek. A riot of colors gives this place a truly stunning look. Dark green cedars, growing along the beach, create an amazing contrast with white sand, which smoothly turns into bright blue sea water.
