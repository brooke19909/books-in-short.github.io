Sinatra Hotel (Turkey / Kemer): reviews and ratings

Hotel "Sinatra" in Turkey is one of the most frequently visited places by tourists. Every year, a huge stream of guests from all over the world come here who wish to enjoy the exotic atmosphere and a pleasant stay. It is often recommended to those tourists who want their vacation to be inexpensive, but very enjoyable. What are the features of this place? What attracts guests to it?

general information

Hotel "Sinatra" was built in 1998 and from that moment firmly captured the leading position in the ratings of the best hotels, ideal for youth recreation. In order to maintain the original fresh look of the hotel territory and its buildings, small restoration work is carried out here almost every year, the time for which is usually chosen during the winter period when travelers are not resting here.

At the moment, the hotel occupies a leading position in various ratings compiled among vacation spots of this type. As a rule, the Sinatra hotel receives a rating of 4.5-4.7 from its possible five points from its guests - this result speaks for itself.

As for the total territory of this place, its area is just over 2000 square meters. m. - this is not much, but such a place is enough to accommodate a large building and an aquazone, as well as several places for guests to relax.



Location

Hotel "Sinatra" is located in the small resort town of Camyuva, which belongs to the Kemer region. In the center of this city there is a huge amount of entertainment, which is often sought by hotel guests - here they can choose a vacation to their liking. There is a short distance to the central part of the district - only 8 kilometers.

Literally 60 kilometers from the hotel there is an international airport - in the city of Antalya. In order for foreign tourists not to be forced to stay on a long, exhausting journey at the time of arrival, a transfer service is included on every trip that is purchased at a travel agency. As many guests note in her comments, she finds herself in a very comfortable passenger car that is equipped with air conditioning.

Rooms

The hotel offers guests a relatively small number of rooms, consisting of 84 apartments. Between themselves, they are not divided into categories; they are presented in only one format - the standard.





The apartments have some amenities, in particular, they include an individual bathroom, where only the most modern plumbing is installed, which was replaced during one of the latest renovations (in 2015). There are also several wardrobes, where guests will find bathrobes and bath towels. Each tourist at the time of entry to the Sinatra Hotel receives a set of bath accessories.

All rooms have modern air conditioning, with which every guest, staying in the room, can surround themselves with the desired temperature conditions, regardless of the weather that is currently standing on the street.

A considerable number of tourists in their reviews of the hotel mention that in each room it has a balcony from which you can admire the surroundings.



Room Interior

According to tourists, all the apartments that are available in the hotel are decorated the same and at the same time quite comfortable. Here in each room there is a rather light and stylish interior, decorated using a large number of details from light wood.

Each room has a small area, which is 14 square meters. m., everything is arranged on them so that two guests can accommodate. Here, as a sleeping place, a large double bed is provided, next to it there is a pair of nightstands on which guests can store small things. For personal items, a stand is installed here, the surface of which is functional - a telephone and a TV are installed on it, which is connected to a cable broadcasting system. By the way, most tourists say that Russian-language channels are also broadcast on it - this is a positive moment for guests from the CIS countries.

On a paid basis, guests can request a safe, a minibar and a telephone with the ability to connect to an international communication line to their apartments - the use of such facilities is paid.



Nutrition

To ensure that tourists staying at the Sinatra Hotel (Kemer) do not remain hungry, several catering establishments are provided for them, which serve excellent tastes. Hotel guests often talk about the fact that the buffet, which is organized every day in a local restaurant, serves Turkish and European dishes, cooked with notes of oriental flavor. The all-inclusive system, according to which tourists eat, also includes a wide range of drinks. Among them, a huge number of those that are produced in Turkey are offered.



In addition to the large restaurant, there is a large bar that welcomes guests from 11 a.m. to 11 p.m. It is located near the pool, and its regular visitors, as a rule, are guests of the aquazone. Its menu offers the lightest dishes - appetizers and salads, as well as desserts. In their comments, guests often recommend tasting branded ice cream, which is prepared here according to a secret recipe. Fans of drinks and cocktails will be pleasantly surprised by their huge assortment.

Services

All guests who are in the hotel "Sinatra" (Turkey / Kemer) can use a fairly wide range of services, many of which are not included in the price of the permit and are provided for a fee.

Car rental is the most popular of them. Vehicles are provided for daily rent, the rental price also includes the provision of a parking space in a guarded parking lot located within the hotel. A huge number of tourists recommends that they go to the center of Kemer in a rented vehicle in order to have fun, and some tell in their reviews that you can go by car to see the sights, which are many in the district.

Also, Sinatra offers laundry and dry cleaning services, it has an excellent Internet corner where tourists gather in order to connect to the network and connect with relatives and friends. If desired, guests can use the paid services of a doctor, whose office is located on the ground floor of the main building.



Aquazone

A considerable number of guests in their reviews of the Sinatra hotel say that they very often visit the aquazone, which is equipped on its territory, next to the main building. Sport

Connoisseurs of a sports lifestyle often in their reviews of the Sinatra Hotel (Kemer) talk about the fact that on its territory there are some conditions for maintaining a healthy mind in a healthy body. Their favorite place is a large gym, where there are several options for simulators designed to work on different parts of the body. In the open area there is a small volleyball court, where guests often arrange team competitions in this game.

SPA

For lovers of relaxation, the hotel has a small massage room. According to the guests, it employs real experts who know how to immerse the client in a state of complete relaxation. His guests also pay attention to the interior of the entire office - its light shades immediately set the visitor in the right way.

If desired, tourists can visit the local thermal zone, which is also equipped in the redistribution of the building, for free. It consists of a large sauna and hammam. Here you can spend free time for quite some time, enjoying the high temperatures and professional work of the parmesisters.



Beach

About the beach strip is often said in reviews of the hotel "Sinatra" (Turkey). This place to relax has its own plot on the city beach, which, according to vacationers, is perfectly arranged for a pleasant pastime. According to them, staying here is quite comfortable, as here you can sit well on comfortable sun loungers, which are provided completely free of charge. If desired, guests can open an umbrella over themselves in order to protect themselves from the harmful effects of sunlight.

Guests can rent large bath towels, the cost of which will be approximately 30 rubles per day. On a free basis, you can take in use an air mattress, the issuance of which is carried out in a specially arranged paragraph.

Along the coastline, as vacationers note, there is a large amount of entertainment, among which there are many extreme ones. Not far from here is a rescue team who will assist if necessary.
