Hotel Mehtap Family Hotel 3 * (Marmaris): traveler reviews, photo

Marmaris is a favorite vacation spot of Europeans. He heads the region of the same name, stretching along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. Resort hotels are focused on serving visitors from the UK, Holland, and Germany. Today in Marmaris, Russians are resting.

Weather





The climate in these places is mild. Even at the height of summer, there is no sweltering heat in the vicinity of the city. The swimming season at the resort opens in late May. During this period, the water warms up to 20 ° C. In June, the sea becomes very warm. Its temperature is 22 ° C. In July - 24 ° C, in August - 25 ° C. In autumn, the water area gradually cools. The last vacationers leave these parts in October.

In mid-autumn, the thermometer shows 25 ° C. The nights are cool. In November, the air temperature after sunset does not exceed 11 ° C. The climate in the region is dry. Therefore, local resorts are chosen by patients suffering from pulmonary diseases. In February, sometimes it snows.

Description





Mehtap Family Hotel specializes in families with children. The hotel has a compact territory. Tropical plants grow on it. Dining tables and sun loungers are set in their shade. There is a pool. There is a restaurant.

The hotel complex consists of several buildings. The height of the main is three floors. It contains premium rooms. There are no elevators at Mehtap Family Hotel. The hotel was built in 1989. The last renovation took place in 2012. The area occupied by the hotel exceeds 3,000 m². Number of living rooms - 100.

The procedure for registering new customers begins at 14:00 and lasts until midnight. Check out time is 12:00. Mehtap Family Hotel does not accept pets. There are no rooms for disabled guests.

Hulls rise two hundred meters from the city beach. Nearby are the hotels Begonville Hotel, Aegean Park, Sunprime Beach. The hotel has a prime location. Silence is provided by remoteness from the main transport arteries of the resort. To get to the sea, you need to cross the highway. The movement on it is very busy. There is no underpass.

Service





Mehtap Family Hotel has a 24-hour front desk. Accommodation staff exchanges currency. Call a taxi. Contact representatives of travel agencies and insurance companies. The hotel accepts Visa, MasterCard plastic cards.

Services included in the travel package:

  • All-inclusive meals
  • swimming in the pool;
  • housekeeping;
  • bed linen change;
  • Internet connection.

Options to pay for:

  • rental of sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach;
  • imported alcohol;
  • Turkish coffee, cappuccino, juice;
  • barbershop;
  • laundry;
  • water sports equipment rental .

There is no gym in Mehtap Family Hotel 3 *. The all-inclusive service system begins to operate at 10:00 and stops at 22:30. The animation team in the hotel is not provided. The beach is in municipal, not private ownership. Towels on it do not give out.

Housing stock





Tourists are accommodated in family apartments and in standard suites. Superior rooms have a balcony. It offers views of the city street or the courtyard of Mehtap Family Hotel 3 *. Air conditioning is installed. Remote control must be obtained from reception staff. The guests complained that they had to manually adjust and adjust the blowing power and the degree of cooling.

The apartments have a miniature TV. The phone is used to contact the administration. In the bathroom - shower, mirror, hair dryer. In Mehtap Family Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) in Standard and Economy Rooms, access to the safe was opened after making a deposit. Standard rate - 150 rubles per day. The area of ​​the family suite is 35 m². Laminate flooring. The refrigerator does not fill up. Linen is changed twice a week.

The remaining rooms occupy 20 m². They include beds, a wardrobe, nightstands, a table and chairs. A clamshell is provided for children. Upon request, an arena is issued. Standard rooms in Mehtap Family Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) are located in additional four-story buildings. Travelers have many complaints about the quality of service.

Nutrition

All meals within the framework of the All Inclusive concept are held in the hall of the hotel’s main restaurant. It opens at 08:30. Service ends at 21:30. Breakfast lasts an hour and a half. For dinner, they invite at 12:30. Tables begin to clean at 14:00. Mehtap Family Hotel (Marmaris) has a tea ceremony. An afternoon snack falls on time from 16:00 to 17:00. The evening meal starts at 19:30. Visitors are escorted at 21:30.

The bar is located in the aquazone. It is open from 10:00 to 22:30. Free of charge they give out soft drinks, fruit drinks, snacks, Turkish-made alcohol. For ice cream, cocktails, strong coffee you need to pay. Visitors were unsatisfied with the quality of the products used. Salads were not fresh. They were served with tough meat. Water activities





Since the hotel does not have its own beach, the residents of the hotel visited the municipal recreation area. Admission to it is free. The cost of renting a sunbed is 150 rubles. The coast is covered with pebbles. There are islands of coarse sand. The entrance to the water is smooth, but there are a large number of sharp stones in the sea. During a period of excitement, swimming in this part of the resort can be dangerous.

There is music on the coast. Photographers and travel agents offer their services. Here you can have lunch or a meal in one of the many bars and bistros. The kids ride on the "bananas". From morning until evening, a rental point for fishing equipment, scuba diving and water sports is open.

Mehtap Family Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) has an outdoor pool. Its functioning is seasonal. The area of ​​the bowl is 250 m². The aquazone is open from 08:00 to 19:00. Installed one children's slide. Access to the attraction is carried out in two shifts. In the morning, ride from 10:00 to 12:00. After lunch, the descent into the water opens at 14:30. The fun ends at 17:00.

Neighborhood





Local taverns “Khasis Cafe”, “Samdan”, The Great Corner, No Name invite you to eat cheap and tasty food. The best places for romantic meetings are Saranda, Steak, Mia Marmaris. They try seafood and meat, delicious sweets and unusual salads. The cost of dishes is higher than in other resort establishments. High-quality service more than compensates for this drawback. Tourists were satisfied with visiting these places.

For a bite to eat on the go, Ricky's Chippy, Cousins ​​Steakhouse, White Lion, Oasis cookery. They specialize in cooking English, Turkish, Chinese, Italian and Indian cuisines. You can combine meals with entertainment in the bars of Talk of the Town, Andy's, Jacob's, Sarahs, Greasy Spoon.

The resort's pubs serve a variety of beers. The assortment includes products of local manufacturers and imported drinks. Bono is famous for its lamb menu. In "Vamos" you can simultaneously taste the original cocktails and sunbathe on the sun loungers. All of these facilities are located a five-minute drive from Mehtap Family Hotel. Reviews confirm this.

Positive





Hotel guests noted a lot of positive points. In their comments, they thanked the hotel staff. Administrators, bartenders, waiters, reception staff were polite and courteous. They always readily came to the rescue. Standard rooms in Mehtap Family Hotel 3 * were regularly cleaned. The maids carefully monitored the cleanliness. They washed floors and vacuumed. They put things in order every day.

The convenient location of the facility has become one of the main criteria when choosing a hotel in Marmaris. It is located in a quiet street. From the hotel a stone's throw to entertainment and shopping centers.

Negative opinion

Tourists were disappointed with the quality of service in the rooms located in the Anex category buildings. These rooms were not cleaned. Towels were issued the day after check-in. The Russians complained about the noisy neighbors who came to the hotel for the weekend. These guests did not have club bracelets. They listened to loud music. Interfered with sleep at night.

The staff with understanding listened to the complaints of the other guests, but did not intervene in the conflict. They said that the relatives of the owner of Mehtap Family rest in this way. No one dared to argue with them.
