Nautilus Beach in Novosibirsk: Features, Events, Reviews

In the summer, everyone wants to go to the sea in order to relax from the bustle of the city, working everyday life and family worries. Unfortunately, not everyone has such a happy chance. What to do in this situation? You can go to the Nautilus beach in Novosibirsk. The inhabitants of the northern region have a unique opportunity to arrange a short vacation at any convenient time.





Features

The opening of the Nautilus beach in Novosibirsk in 2014 took place. This was a major event for every resident of the city. It exists today, and has already turned into the largest summer entertainment complex in the region. In total, several features can be distinguished:





  • The beach has a convenient location, occupies the left side of the coast. It can be reached by private or public transport.
  • Despite the fact that it is located in the city, the air here is always fresh.
  • The entertainment complex has a well-developed infrastructure: there are paid toilets, changing rooms, umbrellas, sun loungers and baths for washing feet.
  • At the Nautilus beach in Novosibirsk is always fun. Organizers include modern music for all visitors.
  • There is also entertainment for children. If you wish, you can go with them to a cafe or a children's town. It is worth noting that the operating hours of these institutions are limited - from 9.00 to 19.00.
  • Also for the youngest visitors there is a swimming pool, water slides and attractions.
  • As for infrastructure, there is ample parking, a medical center and security.

The beach has become not just an entertainment complex, but a real attraction of the city, to which all locals relate with great trepidation.

Key events

For the third year in a row, the opening of the Nautilius Beach in Novosibirsk has been carried out, but without a swimming season. This suggests that the water has not yet been tested by epidemiologists, and it can become dangerous. Despite this, the entire coast by this time is already cleared of garbage, it has all the necessary infrastructure and a cafe. What to do before the start of the swimming season? Various events are organized here every week.





Young people can participate in competitions (rugby, beach soccer or volleyball). Winners receive a symbolic cup for a long memory. Periodically, concerts of local amateur performances or popular open-air stars are held.

On weekends, beach parties and themed dating contests are held here that help you find your soul mate.

Positive reviews

Reviews about Nautilus Beach in Novosibirsk were divided into two categories - positive and negative. Most of all people like the convenient location. From the stop where public transport arrives, you can walk on foot in just 10-15 minutes. Like the attractiveness of the place. It looks like a tropical island in the city center, sometimes you can drive past from work and watch how people relax on the shore carefree. It cannot but attract the availability of this institution. Entrance to the territory is free (except parking), ice cream and cold drinks are inexpensive. Another advantage is cleanliness; daily cleaning is carried out in the territory. The presence of left-luggage offices allows visitors to leave their belongings in a safe place and enjoy their vacation in peace.

Negative reviews

How can you not like a place in which there is warm sand, clear water, always good and fun? That is why negative feedback from visitors is extremely rare. However, many people have a negative attitude to the fact that there are a lot of people here in the midst of the season, there are not enough sun loungers, places to take sunbaths, there are always long lines in the changing cabins, toilets and cafes. Such a congestion causes fatigue. Local residents would like the flow of people to be regulated in some way, but so far this has not been possible. The only way out in this situation is to arrive early and take the best places in the sun.

Despite this small flaw, the Nautilus beach in Novosibirsk has long been a favorite entertainment complex for the entire region.
