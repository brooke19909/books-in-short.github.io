What is the Children's World bonus card? Activating it is not difficult. But more on that later.

Tariffs

To begin with, we will deal with the features of accumulating bonuses. After all, the activation of plastic takes only a few minutes.



So, when making purchases in the "Children's World", points will be awarded to the client on the bonus card. How much exactly? It is enough to focus on the following indicators:

  • 5% - of the cost of products from the sections "clothing" and "shoes";
  • 2% - for all other goods.

It is important to remember that 1 point = 1 ruble. Thus, the client can actually pay some purchases with bonuses sooner or later.

Validity of points is 1 year. Bonus accrual day is not taken into account. Therefore, you should not rush into spending. But everyone should think about how to activate the "Children's World" card. Otherwise, using plastic will fail.

Cash desk help

Everyone can activate the Detsky Mir bonus card by contacting any company outlet. This technique is suitable for those who do not want to work with an Internet request.

What to do? To activate, you will need:

  1. Take a card and passport with you. The second document is optional.
  2. Contact a cashier or an employee of Detsky Mir with a request for plastic activation.
  3. Give the card to the store employee.
  4. Wait a few minutes.
  5. Pick up a Yo-Yo card that is fully prepared for future use.

Detsky Mir employees themselves will register and activate the card in the database, after which the plastic can be used for its intended purpose. It is allowed to accumulate bonuses even before completing the studied procedure.



Internet and getting started

Activating the Detsky Mir map online is easier than it sounds. But not everyone understands how to do this. Usually the process comes down to filling out a special questionnaire. You can find it on the page with a description of the bonus program.

How to activate the Children's World map via the Internet? The instruction will look as follows:

  1. Open the browser page detmir.ru/bonus-card-new/anketa-step1.
  2. Enter the bonus card number in the designated fields.
  3. Click on the "Fill in the form" button.
  4. Enter information about the card holder, about his children.
  5. Indicate passport data.
  6. Check the box next to "I agree with the terms of the program."
  7. Click on "Submit".

After the actions done, it remains only to wait. As soon as the request passes the processing stage, you will no longer have to think about activating the Detsky Mir card. It will happen automatically.
