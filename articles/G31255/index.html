What were the exploits of Dobrynia Nikitich? What is the main occupation of Dobrynia Nikitich?

The main occupation of Dobrynia Nikitich is the defense of Russia from external enemies. This Russian hero in importance takes the second position after Ilya Muromets. Together with Alyosha Popovich, they form the famous three heroes. In addition, the epic about Dobryn Nikitich mentions him as a relative of the prince. In other legends, the hero is called the nephew of Vladimir. Of all the warriors, he most often fulfills the prince's instructions: to marry Vladimir to the bride, to negotiate with passers-by, to check Duke's praise. The hero Dobrynya Nikitich goes as a companion to Vasily Kazimirovich on an important task - to collect tribute in the horde.

In some epics, the merchant origin of Dobrynia Nikitich is mentioned. Bogatyr from Ryazan, is the son of Nikita Romanovich. The father dies early, so the mother brings up the boy.
