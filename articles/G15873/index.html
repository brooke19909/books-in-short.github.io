Arkhipo-Osipovka is a resort village. From an administrative point of view, it is part of Gelendzhik. Arkhipo-Osipovka is located 56 kilometers from the city. The village is located right on the seashore.



General information

The village is located in a picturesque valley. Two rivers flow here - Teshebs and Vulan. This place is one of the largest in terms of population and area among the resort villages of Greater Gelendzhik. Arkhipo-Osipovka is very popular across the region and country.

Benefits

The main advantage of the village is its location. It is located on the Federal highway M4, namely in the place where the road goes to the Black Sea. From here to Krasnodar - about 130 kilometers. The long road running through the mountain serpentines attracts not many. This grueling journey will have to be overcome on the way to other resorts. Such prospects do not please most tourists. For this reason, it is Arkhipo-Osipovka that becomes the place to spend a vacation.



Relaxation

Pensions located in the village, for the most part, operate year-round. The settlement itself is actually a small city. It has everything you need for a decent pastime. The village Arkhipo-Osipovka, boarding houses and resorts which offer high-level service, has recently been gaining more and more popularity among tourists. One of the undoubted advantages is a favorable combination of quality and cost of service. This mainly attracts tourists to Arkhipo-Osipovka. Pensions, the prices at which are quite affordable, offer comfortable accommodation. Rooms in residential buildings are equipped with everything you need. A wide range of services is another advantage possessed by Arkhipo-Osipovka. Pensions with meals are very popular among family tourists.

Entertainment Information

Many tourists note that leisure is very well organized for guests. They can visit water parks, a dolphinarium and ride on all kinds of attractions. Guests should definitely look into the museum, which is called the Mikhailovsky Management. Here you can find out a lot of interesting things about the history of the village. The embankment of Arkhipo-Osipovka will delight visitors with its picturesque views. There are many cafes and souvenir shops. In the evenings, nightclubs open where guests can go to the disco. The surrounding area is full of interesting historical and natural attractions. Guests can book various excursions. During these events, vacationers will enjoy the views of dolmens and waterfalls. The price level here is about the same as in other well-known Black Sea resorts. Arkhipo-Osipovka is very popular and very rarely lacks guests.



Location Features

There are many pebble beaches in the village. They are equipped with umbrellas and awnings. Jet ski and sun lounger rental is available. Here guests can enjoy all kinds of water activities. They are designed for both adults and children. The beneficial features of the sea include its cleanliness and amenities. Children can also swim here, as the set of depth is very smooth. Dry and warm weather prevails in Arkhipo-Osipovka all summer and early autumn. In the village, vacationers can enjoy many sunny days. The territory of Arkhipo-Osipovka is surrounded by a mixed forest and mountains. This place has an excellent ecological environment. Rest in the village is ideal for those who want to improve their health and gain strength. Arkhipo-Osipovka in the offseason is no less popular. At this time, the local atmosphere becomes especially charming.



Features of the hotel sector

Those who are going to spend their holidays in Arkhipo-Osipovka should know some nuances. The length of the village is quite large. Accordingly, the hotel sector in the resort is extensive. Almost all hotels are located at a distance from the sea. Guests should be aware that you will need to walk a certain path to the coast. It can take anywhere from ten minutes to half an hour. The distance is affected by the remoteness of the holiday home, where guests prefer to stay. In many ways, this arrangement of the hotel sector was influenced by the features of the building. There are very few hotels in the immediate vicinity of the beach. In the midst of the season, you should take care of pre-booking rooms. During this period, finding a suitable hotel near the sea is a rather difficult task. This is especially true for family tourists who are attracted by Arkhipo-Osipovka. Pensions with meals are an ideal place to stay. However, it should be remembered that every year the number of tourists only increases. Therefore, rooms must be booked in advance.
