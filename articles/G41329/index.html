Beautiful Gelendzhik: tours of the city and the surrounding area

Many people come to rest in Gelendzhik. However, relaxing exclusively on the beach can quickly get bored. Therefore, numerous travel agencies offer a variety of excursions: educational, extreme, entertaining. You can look at various attractions and get a lot of unforgettable experiences in the city of Gelendzhik.

Excursions to the Museum of Local History

Here you can study ornithological and herbarium collections, admire the

various minerals, look at insects and the various inhabitants of the Black Sea, coast and surrounding forests. Visitors will be told about what tribes lived here a long time ago, about the most interesting archaeological finds, about the ancient colony of Torik.

"Safari Park"

The first Black Sea animal rehabilitation center is located in the city of Gelendzhik. Excursions to this park allow you to plunge into the natural habitat of animals brought from all over the world. If you climb the cable car, you can enjoy a panoramic view of the city from three different viewing platforms. Walking along the picturesque trails, you can stumble upon the oldest dolmen, which is a heritage of the Bronze Age.



Dolmens

They are the greatest mystery and represent the oldest mysterious structures. They were found in South Korea, France, Great Britain and the Krasnodar Territory. There is no consensus on their mission in the city of Gelendzhik. Excursions to dolmens are one of the most popular, especially among lovers of esotericism, archeology, history. According to one version, the most ancient people built these structures to bury the best representatives of the community. According to another version, dolmens, like the pyramids of Egypt, are the "receivers" of the Information Field of the Universe.

Waterfalls

A unique place is Gelendzhik. Excursions are recommended to start with exploring the local waterfalls. Just imagine, there are more than 70 of them here! Despite the fact that they are small in size, the places in which they are located are very picturesque.
