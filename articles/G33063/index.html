Palmiye Garden Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

One of the oldest and most interesting regions of the Republic of Turkey is Side. Every year many tourists come here from different parts of the world. They are attracted by both the magnificent local climate, the presence of very interesting historical and architectural attractions, as well as a large selection of hotels and entertainment for every taste and budget. If you also decided to spend your holidays in Side, Palmiye Garden Hotel will be a good economical option for your stay. We offer you to get to know this hotel more closely today, finding out what it offers to travelers and at what price. We will also try to figure out what impressions our compatriots left here.





Hotel location

The three-star hotel "Palmie Garden" is located just one and a half kilometers from the center of the resort region - the city of Side. Another famous coastal settlement - Manavgat - is eight kilometers away from the hotel. As for the nearest airport, it is 65 kilometers away in Antalya. After landing in the air harbor, the path to the hotel will take you a little more than an hour. A very important factor when choosing a place of residence in Turkey is the proximity of the sea. Hotel Palmie Garden is located half a kilometer from the beach. Thus, the path to the coast will take you no more than a quarter of an hour.

Hotel "Palmie Garden" (Turkey, Side): photos, description

This hotel complex was opened recently - in 2013. It consists of a main four-story building and three additional blocks. In total there are 102 rooms, among which: standard and family rooms, as well as a room specially equipped for the comfortable stay of people with disabilities. The hotel's own territory is small, and its area is only 1700 square meters. However, it fit a large outdoor pool, water slides, restaurant, bars, fitness center, hammam and sauna. You can find more entertainment on the beach, equipped with everything necessary for practicing water sports. In general, Palmiye Garden Hotel 3 * is an excellent option for a comfortable, economical vacation for family people with children of all ages, as well as for youth groups or couples in love.





Posting Rules

As in most other hotel complexes around the world, the Palmie Garden has a set time for check-in and check-out. So, settling of the staying tourists here is carried out starting from two in the afternoon, and to leave the room on the day of departure should be before noon. However, if you arrived at the hotel much earlier, the administration will do everything possible to make you check in as quickly as possible. But during the "high season" sometimes situations arise when previous guests have not yet had time to vacate the room, and accordingly, the maids could not prepare it properly for arrival. In this case, you will be offered to leave bulky things for storage in the luggage room and stroll around the Palmiye Garden Hotel, take a refreshing dip in the pool, have a bite to eat in the restaurant, or go straight to the nearest beach.

On the day of departure, upon delivery of the keys to the reception you will also need to pay for the stay. If you booked a tour through a travel agency and already paid the full cost of the accommodation, then in the hotel you only have to pay for the additional services provided, if you used them. As for accommodation with children, the hotel only welcomes guests with children of different ages. If you are used to going on a trip in the company of a four-legged friend, then you should contact the administration in advance about the possibility of placing it in the hotel.





Number of rooms

As mentioned above, Palmiye Garden Hotel (Turkey) has 102 rooms located in the main four-story building and three additional, also four-story, blocks. All buildings have elevators for the convenience of guests. The hotel is represented by the following categories of rooms: 92 standard (22 square meters), nine family rooms (36 square meters) and one room specially equipped to accommodate people with disabilities.

Room Options

Regardless of the category, all rooms in the Palmiye Garden Hotel 3 * are equipped with air conditioning, a minibar, a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, a flat-screen TV and a telephone, and there is wireless internet access. For an extra charge of two euros per day, you can use the in-room safe. Rooms are cleaned every day. Towels and linen maids are changed twice a week. Also on a paid basis at any time of the day you can order room service.





Meals: restaurants, bars

Dining at the three-star Palmiye Garden Hotel is organized according to the "ol inclusive" ("all inclusive") system. The restaurant of the hotel complex from eight in the morning until ten in the evening serves a buffet with first and second courses, appetizers and desserts of Turkish and international cuisine. There are also bars on site where you can enjoy free local drinks and foreign drinks at an additional cost.

Beach and sea

The Palmiye Garden Hotel (Side, Turkey) is located on the second coastline and does not have its own beach. The nearest beach is 500 meters away from it and is municipal. It is sand and pebble. There are sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas, as well as everything you need for water activities and sports. The use of all beach equipment is paid.





Entertainment

The Palmie Garden Hotel has a spacious open-air pool equipped with water slides. Near it there is a sun terrace, where hotel guests can take sun baths, conveniently located on the sun loungers. There is a bar near the pool where you can enjoy your favorite drinks.

If you want to stretch a little, then there is the opportunity to play table tennis and visit the gym. In addition, the Palmiye Garden Hotel 3 * has a spa center where you can visit the sauna, Turkish bath (hammam) or massage. For the convenience of the smallest guests of the hotel complex on its territory there is a playground and a shallow pool in which kids can safely frolic in clean water.

"Palmie Garden" (Turkey, Side): accommodation prices

Since this hotel is a three-star hotel, the cost of accommodation in it is quite affordable. So, living in a standard room will cost you from 1287 rubles per night, and in a family room - from 1660 rubles.





Reviews of tourists about the hotel "Palmie Garden"

Since the vast majority of travelers choose a hotel for rest, based not only on its description, but also on the comments of people who have already visited it, we have summarized the reviews of our compatriots staying this summer at Palmiye Garden Hotel 3 * (Side). In general, we can say that most tourists liked the hotel. In their opinion, it is consistent with both its stated description and cost. Now we offer to understand the details in more detail.

As for the rooms, most travelers did not show any dissatisfaction with the accommodation. However, some tourists were unhappy with the poor quality of cleaning the bathroom, poor lighting, and noise. The hotel area seemed to tourists a bit small. However, those who came to rest with the children did not complain, since their babies were always in sight.

Now let's focus on the issue of nutrition. In general, most tourists had no complaints about the quality of the food. Of course, the assortment of dishes was not very diverse, but this situation is quite normal for a three-star hotel. The main thing is that everything here was quite tasty and edible, so that no one was left hungry. The only thing that confused some of our compatriots a bit was that the dishes in the restaurant were not always clean. However, guests could always replace plates or glasses with clean ones. Another drawback on which some vacationers drew attention in their comments was the very meager lunch boxes that could be taken at the hotel if you went on an excursion early in the morning. So, according to travelers, it is simply impossible for an adult to eat such a breakfast.

As for the beach, then, as stated in the description, it is several hundred meters distant from Palmiye Garden Hotel 3 * (Turkey). At the same time, it is necessary to cross a rather busy roadway along the way. In general, the road to the beach will take you no more than a quarter of an hour. But here you will have to go back longer, as you will need to climb a rather steep hill, which may not always be pleasant and comfortable after a day on the beach. The beach itself is quite clean and well equipped.

Some vacationers were a little disappointed with the entertainment offered. So, some of them complained about the lack of animation and discos. As for the animation for children, every night there was a mini-disco, which both the young guests of the hotel and their parents were quite satisfied with.

The rather contradictory comments of our compatriots concern the administrators of the Palmie Garden Hotel (Side, Turkey). So, some travelers note a respectful and attentive attitude on their part, while some tourists claim that the workers allowed themselves an unacceptable and even boorish treatment towards them. In particular, such incidents were related to complaints about noise in the room and requests to relocate guests to a quieter room.

To summarize, it can be noted that many tourists were pleased with their choice of hotel. So, in their opinion, the three-star hotel complex ‚ÄúPalmie Garden‚Äù in Side (Turkey) has the best combination of price and quality. Our compatriots do not exclude their return to this hotel and are ready to recommend it to their friends and relatives as a good option for a budget holiday on the coast of the Republic of Turkey.
