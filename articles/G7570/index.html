MTS periodic services - what is it? List of MTS services

Periodically, owners of mobile phones have to deal with the problem of reducing cash in the account. Often during a call to specialists of a company that provides communication services, or viewing details, it turns out that a number of options have been activated on the number, which subscribers did not even have to hear about. Most of the additional newsletters and packages offered by mobile operators, for example, MTS, are paid. The subscription fee for one can be deducted every day, for others - once in a certain period.

Noticing a slight balance change that occurs daily is not so simple. Payment for the period (some may exceed 100 rubles) attracts more attention of the subscriber. MTS periodic services - what it is, how they are connected, what actions should be performed to deactivate, how to check what is activated on the number - we will consider all these and other issues in this article.





What is meant by ‚Äúperiodic services‚Äù?

The mobile operator MTS in its arsenal has a wide variety of options and additional services. Some of them are included in the basic category and are provided without a monthly fee (possibly even without an activation fee), while others are used for a fee. These may include:

  • various newsletters (news, entertainment, categories for adults, etc.);
  • service packages (minutes, megabytes, SMS and MMS);
  • additional services that allow you to get discounts on communication services, for example, while in roaming, the Internet without restrictions, etc.

Periodic services are those options, newsletters or packages that are valid for a specific period of time (for example, for a month). As a rule, these are some promotional services or offers. Money can be debited from them either daily (the payment amount is an average value calculated from the total cost in proportion to the days in a month or in a week) or one-time, for example, when connecting.

The full list of MTS services can be viewed on the official portal of the mobile operator in the corresponding section.





How can I disable periodic services?

The subscriber has the ability to independently disable the periodic MTS services. What does this mean? Most of the services are managed via a mobile phone: SIM card menu, USSD-requests, Internet self-service system. If you cannot get rid of unnecessary services using these methods, you should contact the employees of the mobile operator company (by phone or in person, at the office). To deactivate the options, you will have to go through identification procedures: by phone - specify the data of the owner of the number, as well as his passport, in the office - present an identity document.

If the subscriber has doubts about where the money goes from the account, and there are suspicions that any additional services are connected, you should find out if they are reasonable. To do this, you can use any convenient method: through a mobile phone, the Internet, with the help of an employee of a company that provides communication services. The most convenient option for viewing the status of the room and managing the connected options and services is the "Personal Account" of the user. Here you can get full information and get a list of MTS services that are activated by this subscriber.
