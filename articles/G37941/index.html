Guam gorge: recreation facilities. "Aquarius" (Guam Gorge): photos and reviews of tourists

Perhaps the most beautiful thing on Earth was created not by human hands, but by nature itself. In every corner of our vast country there are interesting and fascinating places. One of them is the Guam Gorge. Recreation facilities ("Aquarius", "Doe", "Teremok" and others) are waiting for tourists all year round and offer a comfortable stay in a picturesque place.



Where is the gorge located?

Guam Gorge is located in the Krasnodar Territory. The distance to it from Krasnodar itself is about 150 kilometers, from Gelendzhik - 250 kilometers. The closest more or less large settlement is the city of Apsheronsk (50 kilometers). The Caucasus Mountains are generally very majestic and beautiful. But the Guam Gorge, which is a kind of vestibule to the Lago-Naki plateau, is especially stunning with its grandeur.



general information

The gorge can be called an open-air botanical garden. Many thermophilic plants of the most diverse species grow on its slopes. It stretches for almost three kilometers: from the village of Mezmay to the village of Guamka. The depth of the gorge is about 500 meters. Outwardly, it looks like a deep crack in the ground. Scientists believe that there was once a sea on this site, and after it there were limestone deposits that prevented the flow of mountain rivers from the Lago-Naki plateau. Centuries passed, and the water gradually washed the gorge. The Kurdzhips river rushes along its bottom swiftly, with a roar. The gorge is also beautiful because the water washed all kinds of layers of rocks that have different colors. In some places, waterfalls formed. In winter, they freeze, forming an ice sheet on the walls of the gorge. Rich vegetation is what the Guam Gorge can boast of. Recreation facilities (Aquarius is one of the most popular) located near the gorge, also have landscaped courtyards with flower beds, trees and shrubs. Therefore, urban residents who are not used to such lush greenery feel especially good here.

Guam gorge. "Aquarius" and other recreation centers

In the immediate vicinity of the gorge there are many recreation centers. Each of them has a number of features. One of the popular bases is the Doe, located in the immediate vicinity of such a stunning natural monument as the Guam Gorge.



Holiday Village "Doe"

From the windows of this recreation center offers a beautiful view of the mountain village of Kamennomostsky, which is considered one of the tourist centers of the Krasnodar Territory. It offers tourists rooms of choice (double, triple and quadruple), equipped with a bathroom. There is hot and cold water around the clock (which is important when you are located in small villages). Comfortable buses provide daily excursions to various attractions in the area, including the Guam Gorge. Photos and stories of tourists who have already been here act as advertisements, and, of course, there is never a stop from those who want to go for a walk along the gorge. In the gorge itself, you can walk, or you can ride on horseback.

You can relax after the tour in a cafe serving Russian and Caucasian cuisine.

"Walnut Grove"

This is another cozy recreation center located in the village of Guamka in the immediate vicinity of the gorge. It offers vacationers entertainment for every taste: Russian bath, pool, billiards, fishing. On the territory of the base there is a playground, parking. In winter, you can ride on a snowy hill. The cafe offers dishes of Kuban, Caucasian and Russian cuisine. The rooms are very comfortable, there is no wallpaper or paint inside - everything is made of wood. And, of course, it is from this village that the Guam Gorge originates. The recreation center took a very favorable location.

"Aquarius"

This recreation center is a fairly vast territory, on which there are chopped houses for two, four or six guests. Of course, the Guam Gorge (Aquarius contributes to this) is a great vacation option for couples or honeymooners. In the double houses there is a huge room with a double bed, an equipped kitchen and a bathroom. The rooms have everything you need: a bedside table, wardrobes, hangers, a TV. The kitchen has dishes, stove, dining table, chairs. In the houses for 4 guests there are 2 rooms, each of which has two single beds. A house for 6 guests is a great opportunity to relax in a big company. They have three bedrooms and a kitchen. Near each of the guest houses there are benches with tables under a canopy. Here you can have lunch, relax and talk, even if it rains outside. In addition, there is everything for the preparation of barbecue: barbecue, firewood and skewers.



Entertainment at the recreation center "Aquarius"

Guam Gorge offers tourists all kinds of entertainment. Recreation centers (Aquarius is no exception) usually provide their guests with everything necessary for fishing. Nearby flows a beautiful small river Kurdzhips. Fishing is usually successful. Caught fish can be cooked on the grill yourself or ask the cook about it. In addition to fishing, you can take part in hunting.

For those who like a more peaceful holiday, there is a tennis court in the recreation center. And you can have lunch and relax in the cozy cafe "Aquarius".

The hot springs

Of course, the most popular entertainment at the recreation center "Aquarius" are thermal springs. A large open-air hot water pool is never empty. Many come here without settling, buying a daily bathing subscription at the source. The pool is equipped with changing rooms with hangers, so there will be no inconvenience.



A similar source is a rarity. The temperature of the water leaving the well reaches 85 degrees. In the pool, it is much colder - 37 degrees. It is advisable not to be in it for more than 10 minutes without a break. Those who are not swimming for the first time, the duration of stay in the water can be increased. The pool water is rich in various minerals: calcium, magnesium, bicarbonates, fluoride ion. Due to this composition, water has a therapeutic effect. Sources are indicated for people with diseases of the nervous system and musculoskeletal system. Water restrictions apply to people with heart and kidney disease.

Why is it worth going to the Guam Gorge?

Firstly, the Guam Gorge (photos prove this) - the place is very picturesque. There will be no shortage of entertainment here. It is especially good to relax for those who like mountain landscapes and who like to walk along the slopes and breathe fresh air. Secondly, according to tourists, in this place there is almost always excellent weather. Guam Gorge is characterized by a favorable climate. Mountains form a rather enclosed space with its own microclimate. It's pretty wet here. In summer it is not too hot, especially good in the gorge. However, experienced travelers warn that you should avoid walking along the gorge in the rain. Of course, nothing bad will happen, and you can always take refuge under the stone of the protruding rock. But the fact is that even the slightest rain turns into a downpour: streams of rain flow down rocks and stones, so there is a lot of water here.

Numerous positive reviews from tourists are attracting more and more people to the Guam Gorge. Recreation facilities (Aquarius and others) are welcoming guests, the staff is very welcoming and friendly. Among the shortcomings, tourists note quite high prices. Also, not everything in the recreation center "Aquarius" is in proper form (the reviews mention broken bolts). But in general, the impressions of vacationers are very good.
