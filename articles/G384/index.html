Hotel "Kaissa", Adler: reviews, photos

If you dream of an unforgettable vacation not on the Black Sea, then you should definitely go to the Kaissa Resort Hotel (Adler). Since this institution was built relatively recently, you will not find shabby furniture and frayed walls here. Here you will be provided with a high level of service and home comfort.

Brief description of the resort hotel

Bright and unforgettable rest can be provided by the Kaissa Hotel. Adler is famous for its beautiful beaches and distinctive nature, and this hotel will only complement the pleasant impression of the surrounding beauties. Since, due to the unique landscape, this area is completely protected from the wind, you will be warm and comfortable swimming in the outdoor pool, which is located in the courtyard of the hotel. There are also barbecue grills for barbecue, a huge terrace for sunbathing and exploring the surroundings, and much more.

The comfortable and beautifully decorated apartments boast the Kaissa Hotel (Adler). They provide everything you need so that in the middle of the summer heat, the room becomes a real oasis for guests. Air conditioning can help you get a comfortable temperature, and you can pass the time in the evening (if you don‚Äôt want to go anywhere) or in bad weather in front of a wide-screen TV broadcasting many satellite channels. There is also such necessary equipment as a refrigerator, telephone, upholstered furniture and much more.

One cannot but say about the restaurant, in which the Kaissa Hotel (Adler) provides food for its guests. This institution specializes in traditional Russian cuisine. The restaurant is combined with a bar, which always has a large selection of high-quality alcoholic beverages. If you want to try something else, you can visit a lot of neighboring establishments, the road to which will take you no more than 5 minutes.





Resort Location

The resort hotel "Kaissa 3 *" (Russia, Sochi, Adler) is characterized by a rather advantageous location - just a few minutes from the railway station. This can be considered a huge advantage, because after a long move you don‚Äôt want to go a long way, but there is a desire to quickly relax in your comfortable room. Hotel Guest Apartments

"Kaissa" - 3 * hotel (Sochi, Adler, Hosta), the administration of which invites travelers to stay in the following categories of comfortable apartments:

  • Small standard rooms - can accommodate two guests. This is a compact bedroom with two single beds and a full list of amenities for a comfortable stay. Despite the small size of the room, it is possible to install a clamshell for an additional guest.
  • Studio apartments - almost double the size of standard rooms. Here, in addition to two small beds, there is also a sofa, which, when unfolded, can be used as a sleeping place for two guests.
  • Superior Studio - in an impressive area there is a bedroom area, a living room, as well as a small work area. In addition to a folding sofa, there is also the opportunity to install folding beds. Thus, the room can accommodate up to 6 adults.
  • Family room - consists of two bedrooms and is designed to receive 4 guests, excluding extra beds. One of the rooms has a double bed. It adjoins a bathroom with a bathtub, as well as access to a balcony. The second bedroom has two single beds and a shower. The rooms are interconnected through a door.
  • Luxury apartments consist of two rooms. The bedroom is quite small and has a large bed. The living room has upholstered furniture for guests.

Hotel Kaissa (Adler): traveler reviews, comments

Any hotel, even the best, has some shortcomings and disadvantages. Certain disadvantages are also characteristic of the Kaissa Hotel (Adler). Reviews indicate the following negative points:

  • there is a cooler with drinking water only on the ground floor, and therefore you will have to go down each time (it‚Äôs good that at least an elevator is provided);
  • the rooms really lack an electric kettle;
  • terrible insulation in the apartment (or rather, its absence);
  • in the fall for some reason they do not turn on the heating, although it becomes very cold in the rooms (the maximum you can count on is a fan heater, which helps a little);
  • the hotel has some problems with sewage, and therefore the bathrooms in the rooms located on the ground floor have a very unpleasant smell;
  • in spite of the fact that heating is provided in the pool, it is not included, and therefore in cool weather it will not work to swim in the sea or in the pool;
  • as a family-friendly hotel, it would be nice to consider the possibility of pets;
  • there is no curtain in the bathroom, and therefore, after bathing, puddles constantly form on the floor;
  • if you arrive at the hotel during the cold season, then all the smoke from the stove heating of private houses will be in your room;
  • a huge and very unpleasant surprise was to see cockroaches in the bathroom;
  • in the fence around the hotel it would not hurt to make another entrance from the side of the station, since you have to make a very large hook;
  • For those who are in the city for the first time, the unpleasant moment is the lack of an Adler card at the reception staff.

General impression of the hotel

For lovers of the sea and beautiful nature, the Kaissa Hotel is perfect. It is surrounded by green spaces, as well as in close proximity to the sea. Very close to the train station, from which you can reach the hotel on foot. Sochi Airport is also relatively close - no more than 20 minutes by taxi.

Judging by the reviews, the main advantage of the hotel is the ratio of the quality of services and the cost of living. Most hotels located in mountainous areas are expensive. Here the price is quite affordable. And due to the fact that the station is within walking distance, you can quickly get to any popular resort. This hotel also has excellent food and a pleasant atmosphere. The hotel was built relatively recently, but because the repair in it is quite fresh. As for the staff, all employees are very polite, smiling and friendly. Even though there are minor flaws, this hotel can be considered one of the best for a holiday in Adler.
