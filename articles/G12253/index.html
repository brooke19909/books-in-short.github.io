Vladivostok - Khabarovsk: how to get

Many residents of Vladivostok and city guests are wondering how to overcome the distance "Vladivostok - Khabarovsk". These cities are connected by the federal highway M60, which runs through picturesque Far Eastern lands. In addition, there is a railway on which branded fast trains run. If you wish and in a hurry, you can take advantage of air connections between these cities.

Distance

Distance "Vladivostok - Khabarovsk" is 763 kilometers. The road between the cities is quite good. The situation is complicated by the fact that some sections of the track are often subjected to repair, which significantly reduces the speed of movement along the highway.

Airplane

Of course, the fastest way to get from point A to point B is to fly by plane. In our case, the plane will cover the Vladivostok - Khabarovsk distance in an hour or a half, depending on the type. Currently, the ticket price is from 3.9 to 6 thousand rubles. The price depends on the airline and the class of the place. Airports in both cities are modern, comfortable, equipped with everything necessary for convenient waiting for a flight departure.





Several airlines operate the necessary route, so flights depart daily. There may be several flights on some days of the week.

Car

By speed, this is the second way to get to the right place. The Vladivostok-Khabarovsk highway passes by many Far Eastern villages and villages where you can dine with more or less comfort. Also along the route there are many small hotels where you can stay. On average, a car trip along this route takes from about 13 hours (at a speed of 60 km / h) to 7 hours (at a speed of 110 km / h). However, do not be too optimistic, as you won‚Äôt be able to go all the way at high speed. On the way there are quite a few areas where you need to follow at a low speed. Therefore, you should count on 10 hours on the road.





The advantage of this type of travel can be considered complete freedom of action. If you are not in a hurry, then you will certainly turn to mineral springs, stop at the lotus lake, have a picnic on the shore of one of the many rivers found on the way. One of the largest rivers will be the Khor - a beautiful mountain waterway, on the banks of which it is pleasant to sit on a hot day. Locals enjoy swimming in the cold waters of the Choir.

The fare depends on the price of gasoline and the fuel consumption of a particular car. At the moment, you can keep within about 2000 rubles.

Bus

The Vladivostok-Khabarovsk road, the distance between the end points of which is slightly more than 750 kilometers, can be covered by bus. The cost of a ticket for an adult is 1200 rubles. The bus will be at the Khabarovsk bus station 13 hours after departure from Vladivostok. On the way, various buses stop 3 to 5 times. During the stay, travelers can buy a snack on the road or visit cafes located at small stations.

Buses move strictly along the highway, not allowing passengers to visit interesting places located along the road or slightly removed from it.

A train





Train tickets "Khabarovsk - Vladivostok", as well as in the opposite direction, can be purchased not only directly at stations, but also without leaving home. This is an obvious advantage of train travel. The movement time depends on the type of composition. So, the fast train "Moscow - Vladivostok" overcomes this distance in 12 hours. However, the usual train will travel more than 15 hours. The cost of a reserved seat is from 1700 rubles. A place in a compartment will cost from 2.5 thousand, luxury seats - from 7 thousand. In addition, you need to consider the class of the train - in the near future the price for the same seats will be higher than in ordinary ones. Some trains also have common wagons, the price of a seat in which is a little more than a thousand rubles.

The advantage of overcoming the Vladivostok-Khabarovsk route by train is the absence of the need to sit for many hours in a row, the ability to lie down or even sleep.

When choosing a transportation method, it is necessary to focus on specific parameters: time, money, comfort. Cheaper and more interesting to travel, of course, by car. If there are several people in the cabin - the price for each is significantly reduced. Even if you are traveling with children, it will be easier for them to move the road in the car, as they can go out to stretch their legs at any convenient time. In addition, a large number of things can be loaded into the trunk of a car.





If there is no car, it is better to opt for a train, as long-term sitting in one place on the bus is extremely tiring. If money is not a decisive moment, and you are afraid of heights, then in a luxury compartment the trip will be very comfortable.

Well, for those for whom the most important time is to choose a plane. For relatively little money and in the shortest possible time you will find yourself in the right place.
