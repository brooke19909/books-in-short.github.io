Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * (Tunisia, Monastir): reviews and photos of tourists

Our article may be useful to those people who are planning a vacation in Tunisia and are busy looking for a good hotel. We want to talk about one of the Magic Life network complexes located near the city of Monastir. Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * is a four-star hotel, built on the coast.

A bit about the hotel ...

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * has a long history. It was opened in 1962, the last major overhaul in it was made in 2013. During the work the hotel changed several names.



Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * has a good location. It is quite easy for vacationers to get to it, since the distance from the airport (in the city of Monastir) is only one kilometer. Sousse is also nearby. The indisputable advantage of the complex is the vast park with which it is surrounded, bordering a wide sandy beach. The hotel is good not only for family vacations, but also will appeal to lovers of active time spending.

Number of rooms

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * offers its guests 320 rooms of different categories:

  1. The standard can accommodate no more than three guests, it is equipped with one double or several single beds and one extra bed.
  2. Family rooms can accommodate two adult tourists with two children. For younger guests there is a children's area with two single beds.
  3. The four-bed apartments are also designed to accommodate four guests, but these rooms have two separate bedrooms.
  4. Apartment with direct pool access. These rooms can accommodate three guests, they have their own access to a shared pool.
  5. Duplex - two-story apartment with a staircase, which can comfortably accommodate no more than six people.

All rooms in the hotel are very cozy and bright, they are decorated in pastel colors. The apartments have tiled floors, air conditioning, and there is a telephone, a bathroom, a telephone, a hairdryer, a safe and a mini fridge. Almost all rooms have a terrace or balcony. Connecting rooms are provided if necessary.

Dining at the hotel

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * (Monastir) operates on the All Inclusive system. The main restaurant offers its guests a buffet. The local chef prepares excellent dishes of European and national cuisine. The hotel bars offer guests to taste all-inclusive wine, beer, soft and alcoholic drinks, coffee, tea (all drinks are only locally produced).

The main restaurant "Flamingo" organizes culinary shows for tourists, themed culinary evenings. Special dishes are prepared for children. There is also a corner with a microwave, bottle warmer and sterilizer.



On the territory of Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * (Monastir) there are three a la carte establishments: Bahia (Italian food), Sofra (Tunisian national dishes), Steak House (grill dishes). The seven-day tour includes one free visit to one of these restaurants by appointment.

In the afternoons, tourists can grab a bite to eat in the hotel bars: this is a lobby bar, a bar in the water park, a beach bar, a Moorish cafe, a lounge bar, and a sports bar.

Complex infrastructure

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * (Tunisia, Monastir) has two pools, one of which has slides. Near the water area there are umbrellas with sunbeds. One of the pools is a mini-water park with attractions.



In addition, the hotel has a laundry, hairdresser, spa with beauty treatments, bathhouse, hammam, shops, ATM, luggage storage, car rental, fitness center.

Sports and Entertainment

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * (Tunisia, Monastir) offers its guests evening and afternoon entertainment. Animators organize theme nights, shows, discos, music evenings. In the daytime, vacationers can visit water attractions: polo, skiing, parasailing, scooter. In addition, tourists can enjoy horse riding lessons, darts, a games room, several tennis courts, table tennis, a fitness room, and a mini-golf course.

Beach and sea

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * (Tunisia) is located just a hundred meters from the coast. The hotel has its own equipped beach with umbrellas, sunbeds and a bar. A guard is constantly on the shore.

Children's holiday

Magic Life Skanes Family Resort 4 * takes care of the leisure of its young guests. The hotel has a children's pool, playground, mini club (for children from 4 to 12 years old). In the evenings, animators organize mini-disco.

A bit about the resort ...

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * hotel is located near Monastir, which is a beautiful resort and is no less popular than Sousse or Hammamet. A huge advantage of this place is the presence of its own international airport, making it very convenient to get to Monastir.



In the era of ancient Rome, the town was just a small village. And now Monastir attracts many tourists with inexpensive holidays and endless white beaches, historical sights, modern thalassotherapy centers. The city is also popular because of its proximity to the youth resort - the city of Sousse. Local Attractions

Monastir is filled with historical places that may be of interest to tourists. The ruins of Rabat Hartem are considered a symbol of the city. This defensive structure was built in the eighth century, later it became part of the entire defensive fortress. In Monastir you should see the old part of the city (medina), the Great Mosque with marble columns, you can visit the museum of national costumes.



Shopping lovers can buy all kinds of trinkets, ceramics, cosmetics based on natural oils, key rings, souvenirs in the large supermarket Yasmina Center, which is located on the medina.

The best restaurants of the city are located in the port, they are worth a visit to taste delicious and fresh seafood dishes, as well as national food.

Monastir is popular for diving. The underwater world is not as rich as in other places, but still there is something to see. In small bays you can admire the underwater fauna, reefs, see starfish, hedgehogs, sponges and algae.

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 *: reviews

Summing up the conversation, I would like to refer to the reviews of vacationers who visited the hotel. Almost all tourists are satisfied with the service and living conditions in the complex. Frequent guests of the complex note that in different periods there are changes in nutrition and animation. In high season, the variety of dishes at the buffet is simply amazing, as is the intensity of the entertainment. In September and October, the resort gradually calms down, and the hotel staff becomes noticeably smaller, as well as animators. This certainly affects service and entertainment. However, such nuances are not so critical.



The hotel complex is quite spacious and has good rooms, which have everything that tourists need. Some of them go to the amphitheater, where evening performances take place. These apartments are not entirely suitable for those who crave silence in the evenings, as entertainment programs end late. If you wish, you can contact the reception and replace the room with another one located in a more peaceful place. As a rule, guests are welcomed, sometimes you can even do without surcharges.

The rooms are cleaned daily, tips can be left at will. Maids change towels, but sometimes they have to remind them of this.

As for food at Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * (Tunisia), reviews about it speak for themselves. Most guests note the variety of buffets. So, for example, for lunch and dinner they offer at least five meat dishes, not including seafood. Salads and snacks are also varied. From fruits you can try: grapes, oranges, melons, plums, peaches. The waiters immediately replenish the ending dishes. There are no lines in the restaurant. The menu even has vegetarian food.

A huge plus of the complex is the presence of its own beach, which is not allowed on by representatives of the local population and strangers. There is constantly security on the shore; a police patrol car periodically arrives. The hotel is located on the coast, the road to the beach does not take more than a few minutes. The beach is sandy, with a gentle descent into the water. The sea is warm and clean. During the storms that occur in September and October, algae appear, but hotel workers try to clean them in time.



Tourists especially praise the incendiary team of hotel animators. Every morning they invite vacationers to exercise and water aerobics, after which everyone receives a boost of energy and energy for the whole day. Animators do not forget about children, occupying them with interesting events. Kids do not even notice how quickly time flies. Well, an evening disco for young guests is the highlight of the program.

Adults are also satisfied with the show programs. It is simply impossible to be bored here. You can also watch local attractions as an entertainment. At the hotel there are always representatives of tour operators offering trips. To use their services or to travel on their own - you decide. Knowledgeable tourists are advised to visit Monastir on their own, but on a tour of the Sahara you need to go only with a guide.

Instead of an afterword

Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * is a good option for a budget holiday combining excellent conditions and varied food. Tunisia has long been popular with tourists from all over the world due to its beautiful beaches and affordable prices. Therefore, if you plan to visit it, then you should pay attention to the Magic Skanes Family Resort 4 * hotel. Of course, as in any other place, there are some disadvantages, but in general, tourists are very happy with their stay at the hotel.
