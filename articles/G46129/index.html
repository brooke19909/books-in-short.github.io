Hotel Le Meridien Phuket Beach Resort 5 * (Thailand, Phuket): description, photos, reviews of tourists

Holidays in Thailand are becoming more popular year by year. Tourists from all over the world come here. Phuket is located off the west coast of Thailand and leads the list of its largest islands. For a long time, Phuket was not a tourist center. Today he has changed a lot. The number of hotels here is growing every year, for every taste and budget you can find a place that will remain in your memory for a long time.

A few words about the island

Phuket is an amazing place. This is the largest island of Thailand. It is located in the west of the country and is a separate province. Phuket is not only a tourist center, it is a large economic zone of the country. Hevea is grown here, from which rubber is made. Profitability rubber is comparable only with the tourism component. Pineapples are also grown here and jewelry is made.

The climate of the island is hot, tropical. It is warm all year round and there is no sharp difference between summer and winter. The air temperature averages 28 ¬∞ C. From the end of October to the April month, the dry season is set here. At this time, the flow of tourists increases significantly. During this period, all sea excursions are open, including long-distance ones that attract citizens of other countries. In the summer, these excursions become inaccessible due to frequent precipitation and monsoon winds. The number of tourists is somewhat reduced, but they still come here to rest.

According to statistics, several thousand citizens of foreign countries live in Phuket permanently.





On the island there are over a thousand hotels. No one keeps such statistics in detail, so there is no exact data. Here you can find a hotel of any level at any cost. However, there are hotels that deserve special attention. One of these is Le Meridien Phuket Beach Resort 5 *. It is located between Karon and Patong beaches.

Phuket beaches and their importance for hotels

Phuket has many beaches, some of which have earned special popularity among tourists. Many hotels indicate the name of the beach in their name. This is done so that the tourist can understand where the institution is located.

All the beaches of Phuket are qualitatively different from each other. There are no similar ones.

  • The most famous beach of the island is Patong. It is very large and located on the west side. This is a tourist attraction center. There are enough discos and shops, so tourists from other areas of the island come here in the evening. At the same time, Patong is the most polluted beach, the water here is not as clean as in other areas. The reason for this is the large concentration of guests.
  • Karon is another major beach in Phuket. It is located south of Patong. Karon is famous for its yellow quartz sand. It is very beautiful, but walking on it in sunny weather without shoes is not recommended. Karon has a large number of hotels and cafes.
  • The third most popular beach in Phuket is Kata, which flows smoothly into Kata Noi. These are cozy little beaches in the south of the island. A sailing regatta is held here annually.
  • The beach, where a large number of large hotels are located, is Bang Tao. It is located in the northwest of the island, away from noisy discos.
  • The small but cozy beach of Surin is famous for its boulders and emerald water.

In addition to these beaches, Rawai, Nai Harn, Kamala and Nai Yang deserve attention.





Phuket Le Meridien Phuket Beach Resort is located on its own private beach, but is often referred to as Karon Beach.

How to get to the hotel?

To get to Le Meridien Phuket Beach Resort (Karon Beach), you need to go from Phuket Airport, which is located in the north of the island, towards Karon Beach. After passing Patong, you must follow the rise further and at the highest point there will be a turn to the right and a pointer. The hotel has its own separate territory, so there will be a barrier along the way.

Exact hotel address: 29 Soi Karon Nui, Tambon Karon, Amphur Muang, Phuket, 83100, Thailand. If you have to travel on your own, with a rental car at the airport, you should understand in advance that in Thailand the traffic is left-hand. In Patong, as a rule, there will be a large number of mopeds, but in general the road is quite free. Distance from the airport - more than 40 kilometers. Travel time is at least 50 minutes.
