Cottage "Blue Lagoon" (Koblevo, Nikolaev region): reviews, photos

Looking for a budget double room or other options for an economical vacation, but at the same time want to get a high level of service? We offer to consider the hotel "Blue Lagoon", which is located in the resort town of Koblevo.

A bit about the hotel





Blue Lagoon (see photo above) is a cottage-hotel, which was built in 2014 and is located directly in the park area. The houses are made of timber.

A distinctive feature of the Blue Lagoon Hotel (Koblevo) is that the hotel has a small lake where you can sit at tables or lie on sunbeds. There are also many gazebos and barbecue facilities. The territory itself is very large, various trees are planted on it, flowerbeds are broken. And most of the land is covered with lawn grass. Only alleys and paths are left.

Number of rooms





The hotel "Blue Lagoon" is represented by cottages. Each house is equipped with a set of furniture that is necessary for a comfortable stay and relaxation: single and double beds, bedside tables, a dining table and chairs, a wardrobe. Also in the rooms there are such electric household appliances as a plasma TV, a small refrigerator, an electric kettle, a hairdryer. For greater convenience, each cottage has a minimum set of dishes: cups, glasses, plates and cutlery.

The climate is regulated by air conditioning. Each house has its own bathroom with shower and a set of cosmetics (shampoos and shower gel, toilet soap and so on).

Each room has its own exit and individual balconies-terraces.

The cost of living as of 2016 starts from 170 hryvnias per day (1 hryvnia is two and a half rubles). The maximum price for the cottage was 1200 hryvnia. For children without a seat, accommodation is free.

List of services provided by the Blue Lagoon





Blue Lagoon Hotel (Koblevo, Ukraine) offers its guests the following services, the cost of which is included in the payment:

  • wireless internet access (Wi-Fi);
  • satellite television in the rooms;
  • guarded parking lot, including for marker cars;
  • beach umbrellas and sunbeds by the lake;
  • a separate kitchen area with a gas stove, a large refrigerator and the necessary utensils where you can cook your own food;
  • first aid kit and doctor's call;
  • the provision of needles and threads.

Also, for an additional fee, the Blue Lagoon Hotel (Koblevo) offers a transfer from Odessa or Nikolaev and vice versa.

You can eat on site in the cafe "Brazil", which serves homemade cuisine. All guests staying at the Blue Lagoon Cottage Hotel receive a fifteen percent discount on menu items. It is also possible to book a stay with breakfast included or a comprehensive three meals a day (breakfast, lunch and dinner).

Leisure activities





Around the cottage-hotel "Blue Lagoon" there are many cute cafes and restaurants, bars and discos, nightclubs. On the sandy beach you can have fun with a variety of water attractions.

On the territory of the Blue Lagoon Hotel there is the bed of the Tiligul Estuary, which is very picturesque. For walks in the surroundings there is a pine alley and a park area with alpine plants, waterfalls and ponds. There are numerous benches and swings for gatherings. Not far from the lake with mirror water there are comfortable playgrounds for barbecue, rattan gazebos, a children's playground.

You can also visit the rodeo park, do kite surfing on tiligul, plan a boat trip and organize fishing.

Negative moments





Despite the fact that almost all visitors stay in the cottage-hotel "Blue Lagoon" (see photos in the review) for good, there are some minor unpleasant moments:

  • You can cancel a full refund reservation at least fifteen days before the check-in date.
  • If you have a premature departure, the hotel will not refund the paid stay.
  • The hotel's own beach is paid.

If you do not pay attention to such nuances, then relaxing at the Blue Lagoon cottage hotel will not spoil anything, and you will remember it for a long time.

Hotel Location





Cottage-hotel "Blue Lagoon" is located at: Nikolaev region, Berezansky district, Koblevo, st. Chisinau, 12/1. You can get from Odessa in the following ways:

  • By taxi.
  • From the intercity bus station by any scheduled bus that follows in the direction of Nikolaev.
  • From the suburban bus station "Privoz", which is located 500 meters from the railway station, by shuttle bus Odessa - Koblevo.

From Nikolaev you can get to the Blue Lagoon Hotel like this:

  • By taxi.
  • From Nikolaev intercity bus station by shuttle bus that runs to Odessa or by direct flight Nikolaev - Koblevo, directly to the resort area. You can get to the bus station from the railway station by minibuses number 8, 21, 88, 51 or trolleybus number 2.

From Koblevo center, driving your own car takes about fifteen minutes. By public transport you can get from the bus station located on the highway Odessa - Nikolaev. Shuttle buses run every thirty minutes.
