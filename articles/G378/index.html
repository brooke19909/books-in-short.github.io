How to make toys from socks and tights. Description and recommendations

Quite often, housewives find in their house unnecessary pairs of socks, from which the children have already grown. What to do with this good? Are they now a direct road to the landfill? No, do not throw away small socks and tights. With some effort, you can make fun little things out of them.

Interesting pastime

Surely in every house there are socks or tights that children no longer wear. It also happens that a thing is practically new, and the child has already grown out of it. This is exactly what you need!

To begin with, it is worth studying the instructions, which describe how to make toys from socks and tights. This lesson is very exciting and developing. If you do not know what to do with your child at home, invite him to make new plush friends. Surely he will like to work with unusual material.





Socks

So, we make toys from socks. What do we need? First of all, of course, socks. It will be better if you find children's colorful products. It is also necessary to take a few buttons, a needle, threads and a synthetic winterizer or other material with which you can stuff a little thing. Since you can make a variety of toys from socks, choose what your children like. In this case, we make a cat.

  • Take one sock and cut off its edge with scissors.
  • Fill the cut piece with a synthetic winterizer and tie the end with thread. It will be the head.
  • Next, using the buttons, sew the eyes and nose.
  • With the help of thread you need to embroider your mouth. This is simple and requires only a few stitches.
  • Take the second sock and fill it with a padding polyester. Subsequently, it will be the torso. Sew the base to the head with thread.
  • Next, you need to make ears, paws and tail.
  • Cut out the details of the desired shape from the remaining sock.
  • Fill them with filler and sew to the body.
  • If you want the tail and legs to be flexible, insert a small wire into them. In this case, you can change their position yourself during the game.

These handmade toys will be a wonderful gift for the New Year or another holiday. By right, they can be called priceless, because they are made with their own hands and bring warmth and love.

Tights

How to make toys from socks, you already know, but what can you make from tights? Perhaps the easiest option will be a snake. This is especially true for striped and bright items of clothing. You will need small tights, a synthetic winterizer, threads with a needle and various decorative elements.

  • Take the tights and cut them into two parts so that you get 2 long pieces.
  • Fill the product with synthetic winterizer or other filler.
  • Sew open base.
  • Using buttons or a regular applique, make eyes.
  • Embroider your mouth in thread.
  • What a snake without a tongue? To make it, use cropped flaps of tights.
  • Sew your tongue carefully to your mouth.

Your snake is ready! It can be placed on the windowsill or under the slit of the front door as a decorative insulation and protection against drafts. And you can simply leave it in a conspicuous place, because such a toy is unique and presented in a single copy.





Conclusion

How to make toys from socks and tights is up to you. It can be funny bunnies and teddy bears or beautiful dolls and funny gnomes. Do not be discouraged if you do not have enough imagination. Nowadays, you can find many workshops on how to make a toy from a sock. Photos and detailed descriptions are always attached.

Imagine and do it yourself! Try not to throw away old things, but give them a new life. Such a creation will always be appreciated.
