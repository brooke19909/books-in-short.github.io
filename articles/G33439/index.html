How to use

This device is recommended to be used if alcohol or food was taken half an hour before the test. Prior to the first test, the device performs the setup within 30 seconds. The device is quite simple to use, since its use does not require special knowledge and skills. It is enough for the user to turn on the device and wait for the preparation to finish. Then it will take several seconds to blow into the mouthpiece. A successful check is accompanied by a sound signal. After 10 seconds, the result appears on the device and the corresponding color is displayed. Depending on the amount of alcohol in the blood, the display will turn green, yellow or red.

Green color means that the amount of alcohol does not exceed the norm. Yellow color means that a person drank alcohol and the ppm indicator slightly exceeds the norm. A red color indicates a high concentration of alcohol in the blood. The testing process does not take more than 1 minute, and after 45 seconds the device will automatically turn off. Many users have noted the convenience of using this device. If the owner is not sure of the results of the testimony, you can repeat the procedure again.
