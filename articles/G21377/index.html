LCD "Comfort Park", Kaluga. Description, features of the layout of apartments, photos, reviews

about the project

"Comfort Park" (Kaluga) is a new generation real estate project, the most modern quarter of the city today, in which many dream to live. Concept

The photos presented in our material will help to get to know the concept of the Comfort Park LCD project (Kaluga) closer. The general plan involves the construction of 11 high-rise buildings in 5 phases, including residential buildings, parking, social and economic infrastructure. The complex, designed for 2,754 apartments, will become the largest and most populous in the city. The first phase of the project has already been built and put into operation, the first tenants managed to settle down and form their own opinion on the complex and technical conditions that the developer provided.

Technology

Unique modern technologies and high-quality building materials formed the basis of the Comfort Park project (Kaluga). The reviews of the first residents became the main confirmation of this, because they did not see anything like it in the city and the nearest regions. The white and green gamut chosen for the exterior decoration of residential buildings looks modern, stylish, harmonious. Moreover, the materials used provide excellent heat and sound insulation properties. In combination with the latest model of plastic windows, it is possible to increase the energy efficiency of each structure by 30 percent, reducing the amount of future heating costs.

Location

For the construction of the Comfort Park residential complex, an ecological clean district of the city, the right bank of the Ob River, was chosen. By local standards, this is a prestigious area surrounded by a pine forest, which has a chic promenade. At the same time, only 15 minutes to the central part of the city, which allows you not to break away from public life, take part in all city events. The developer put into the project the construction of a park that is closed to outsiders, which will become a favorite place for walking and recreation for all residents of the complex. And the windows of the apartments offer stunning views of the pine forest and the Ob River.

Responses LCD "Comfort Park" (Kaluga) cover the topic of ecology. So this complex is a real gift for those who have long looked after an apartment that combines the charm of urban and suburban life. No noise, fuss, on the contrary, clean air saturated with resins of conifers, plenty of places to relax in the fresh air, and all this in close proximity to the central part of the city.

Infrastructure

The developer put a lot of effort in order to create a truly unique project, which has no analogues in Kaluga and the region. The infrastructure of the Comfort Park residential complex (Kaluga) is oriented towards complete satisfaction. A kindergarten, all necessary shops, pharmacies, laundries, bank branches and other organizations in the service sector will operate on the territory. Fans of active leisure appreciated the presence in the project of fitness centers, gaming and sports grounds of various orientations, as well as tracks for rollerblading, biking and hiking.

Underground parking and its own indoor park are the highlight of the complex, which all those who already managed to personally get acquainted with the project managed to appreciate. In addition, residential buildings are being built simultaneously with infrastructure facilities, so that even the first residents have everything they need right at hand.

Apartments, layouts

Apartments in the complex are represented by a variety of planning solutions. A choice of small, one-room, comfortable two-room and spacious three-room options ranging from 43 to 92 square meters are presented to customers. Here you will not find free layouts that are so popular today, as well as European ones, which imply a combination of the kitchen and living areas, which did not prevent the creation of truly functional living spaces. Large kitchens, several balconies, isolated rooms, the presence of an additional bathroom are what the first tenants managed to note. They also liked the competent organization of space, which allows you to use every square meter of space - nothing remains idle.

Opinion of residents

The first phase of the project was completed and put into operation, so the opinion of those who managed to get the keys to their long-awaited apartment has already appeared.
