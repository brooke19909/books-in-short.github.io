Hotel Admiral 5 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands): traveler reviews

If you want to relax in a warm family atmosphere, pay attention to the Admiral 5 * hotel. Bulgaria, Golden Sands is a European resort with a Slavic character. This feature attracts travelers from all over the world.





a brief description of

The Admiral 5 * hotel has a rather long and interesting history. Bulgaria, Golden Sands - a popular tourist destination, which has always been in demand. Until 2004, the hotel was called "Homeland", and after reconstruction and rebranding, it received its current name. The hotel is a beautiful 7-story building surrounded by a garden and numerous tourist facilities.

They go to the Admiral 5 * hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) not only for sea vacations, but also then to improve their well-being. The hotel has a wellness center where you can get medical advice and undergo many procedures. In addition, swimming in the pool, exercises in the gym, as well as healing sea air and the warm sun contribute to strengthening the body.





Hotel location

On the very shore of the sea is a resort hotel Admiral 5 * (Bulgaria). Golden Sands, 9007 - the address of this resort. To get to the beach, you have to walk no more than 30 meters. It is worth noting that the hotel is located in the very center of the resort, where most of the shops, entertainment centers and catering facilities are concentrated. In addition, the famous artists alley runs right through the hotel. The distance to the city of Varna is about 20 km.

Hotel rooms

If you want to relax in a cozy atmosphere, go to the Admiral Hotel. Bulgaria is a popular budget resort where you can find a decent accommodation option for little money. The following room categories are available to guests in this hotel:

  • Standard double rooms are the most economical accommodation option. There are two single beds, a desk, chairs and bedside tables. From your own balcony you can enjoy the park or the sea.
  • Studio apartments can accommodate up to 4 people. The main berth is a double bed with an orthopedic mattress. Extra guests can sit on a pull-out sofa. The bedroom and living room areas are separated by a decorative partition.
  • For guests accustomed to luxury, luxury apartments are provided. The bedroom with the original design has a king size bed, and the living room has designer soft furniture. The specifics of this category of rooms can be considered a panoramic sea view.

Comfortable living conditions are offered to travelers by the Admiral Resort Hotel. Bulgaria is a very welcoming and hospitable country. This is evidenced by the wide range of amenities in the hotel apartments:

  • Radio, thanks to which you can listen to songs of local artists all day long;

  • in your apartment you can use wi-fi;

Additional Information

A number of internal rules govern the work of the Admiral Hotel (Golden Sands). It will be useful for travelers to familiarize themselves with such information:

  • You can check into the room you booked after 14:00 (except in cases where, due to the availability of rooms, early registration service is provided to guests);
  • before noon, you should have time to resolve all organizational issues related to eviction (there is the possibility of late check-out subject to payment of the hourly fee);
  • Children under two years old are provided with free special cots and playpens;
  • in this hotel you cannot stay with pets;
  • to pay for services in cashless form, you can use plastic cards;
  • if any holiday falls during your stay at the hotel, a gala dinner is included in the price;
  • birthday people are given a birthday cake (can be delivered to the room or served during dinner);
  • newlyweds are provided with themed rooms, fruits and a bottle of champagne;
  • regular guests (those who have stayed here more than two times) get the right to upgrade the room category if there is a sufficient number of free places.




Positive reviews

Based on the comments of experienced travelers, we can conclude that Admiral 5 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is a pretty good place to relax. Reviews indicate a number of positive points:

  • excellent location - the very center of a busy resort, but in close proximity to the sea;
  • beautiful views from the windows of rooms located on the upper floors (the picture becomes especially beautiful at night, when the whole city is illuminated with colorful lights);
  • very large rooms;
  • if you have any problem in the room, then by contacting the reception, you can count on its speedy resolution;
  • there are no cases of theft in the hotel, and therefore it is not necessary to spend money on a safe (even maids will not take a tip if you leave them in any place other than the edge of the bed);
  • excellent quality of wireless Internet both in the territory and in the room;
  • the hotel has a very tasty, satisfying and varied food;
  • good animation for both children and adults;
  • swimming in the pool, you can enjoy the sea;
  • the restaurant serves real fruit juices, not powder;
  • some workers speak Russian well.

Negative reviews

The Admiral 5 * hotel (Bulgaria) is characterized by a number of shortcomings and shortcomings. Golden Sands is a fairly budget resort, and therefore you should not count on an ideal vacation. According to the reviews of tourists, the following negative points can be distinguished:

  • a huge minus can be considered the lack of an iron in the room (but it can be brought on request);
  • fairly high prices for drinks in the hotel bars (as an option, they can be bought in neighboring stores);
  • employees often forget about the birthday, and therefore, in order to get the proper compliment, you have to remind yourself several times;
  • the bathrooms in some rooms have a very unpleasant smell, which can be caused by a clogged sewer;
  • despite the fact that there is a lot of greenery in the territory, it is not high, and therefore there are no areas with a dense shadow;
  • due to the fact that the hotel has a lot of families with children, the area is very noisy;
  • paid sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach (if you do not want to spend money, you can sit right on the sand).

General impression

If you want to spend your vacation in a warm family atmosphere, then pay attention to the Admiral 5 * hotel. Bulgaria is a fairly popular destination, and therefore it is constantly developing. If we talk about this hotel, then recently it has been completely updated, but because here you will not find relics of the past. Buildings, rooms, territory - everything looks very beautiful and modern. The staff works very diligently, however, leisurely (but this can be considered a national feature of the Bulgarians).

Resting in this resort hotel, you should not order a comprehensive three meals a day. It is enough to stay at breakfasts, which will please you with an abundance of fruits, vegetables, cheeses and other goodies. As for lunch and dinner, they may seem too modest to you. The best way to get to know traditional local cuisine is to eat in the many cafes and restaurants located in the neighborhood.
