How to sew a Peppy Long Stocking costume with your own hands?

The bright red-haired girl became popular after a series of book stories by a Swedish writer were filmed. And it is not surprising that many children choose this image for a carnival costume.

Brightness and simplicity

The main feature and advantage of this image is that even a child can make a Peppy Long Stocking costume with his own hands. After all, you don’t have to invent anything new and complicated, and all the necessary details for creating an image can be found in almost every house where there are small children.

Peppy has no strict canons, and you can experiment and combine different styles and forms of clothing. And now let's take a look at every detail that you should know about if you decide to make a Peppy Long Stocking costume with your own hands. The photo below can serve as an example for creating a costume.





Dress

The main outerwear for this costume is a dress or sundress. A denim sundress or a blue dress is perfect, which will bring the image closer to the original.

If you could pick up a blue dress, then in addition to it you need to sew an apron. You can see what a Peppy Long Stocking costume looks like below.





Under a sundress or dress, you must wear a sweater with a long sleeve, be sure to strip. Color does not matter, the main thing is the presence of horizontal stripes. If active movements are supposed, it is possible to use a t-shirt, but also striped.

The finished dress for the image is decorated with patches, which can vary significantly in color. Sew them in a chaotic manner using bright threads. Use the shapes of hearts, stars, circles and other geometric shapes.

If you could not pick up a dress or sundress, put on a blue skirt or denim. Just fix it with the help of suspenders - and get the same Peppy Long Stocking costume. The photo, which can be seen further, just depicts such an option.





Hair

Peppy is a red-haired girl who most often braids two pigtails. Therefore, try to get as close to the literary character as possible. This can be done using a hair wig or temporarily dye your hair with crayons. But do not despair if this is not possible. Now let's explain why.

The main feature of Peppy's hairstyle is her braids, which stick out in an unnatural way. Therefore, you can braid two braids from your hair, insert a wire in the middle of each of them. This is necessary in order to be able to change the position of the braids. This move will greatly improve the costume of Peppy Long Stocking. The photos that you see in this article confirm the amazing and unusual effect of this technique.

And if you decide to use a wig, then when choosing it, give preference to the thread option. These wigs are made of dense yarn, which is easily assembled into braids and fixed with wire.

Footwear

The image of Peppy implies a cheerful and agile girl who never sits in place. Therefore, shoes are suitable for her at low speed and athletic; sneakers are the best option.

But Peppy's Long Stocking costume is considered incomplete unless stockings are worn. These socks are most often chosen in striped colors, knee-length. But the most amazing thing is that you can use two different golf courses. It can be completely different colors. Solid or striped - you choose.





Such stockings do not have to be worn neatly spread over the entire length of the legs. It is allowed to lower one of the golfs to emphasize the explosive character of the heroine of our image.

Peppy Long Stocking Costume can be created using tights. When choosing them, they prefer bright, with patterns.

If any of the above material is not available in your home, do not despair. Try to create an image from the available clothes and shoes and do not be afraid of experiments.

Optional accessories

In the story about Peppy, there are two best friends - a monkey and a horse. Therefore, if you can get one of these soft toys, you can complement the image.

We also recommend that you pay some attention to the appearance of the future Peppy. From the stories we know that this girl has many cannabis on her face. So we advise you to paint with yellow or orange dots on the girl’s face.

It would be useful to portray a light blush on Peppy's face. After all, this active girl never sits in place and is constantly busy with something.

Beads, bracelets and rings are suitable as jewelry. Such jewelry will give our Peppy femininity and remind us that no matter how mischievous she is, she has something economic in it, as evidenced by the presence of an apron on her dress.

In general, as you understood from the article, Peppy Long Stocking costume is a complete expanse for imagination and a combination of unexpected clothes and paraphernalia.
