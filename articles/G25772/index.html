Italy, Milano Marittima. All about the resort

Fans of an exotic Italian holiday probably rested in Milano Marittima (Italy). Reviews about the resort confirm that you can have a great time there as a large company, as well as a family with children. This article is dedicated to this extraordinary resort. Briefly about it can be read below.





Where is

Thanks to its unusual geographical position, even a schoolboy knows where Italy is located. Milano Marittima is a small district of the city of Cervia, which is located on the northernmost coast of the Adriatic Sea. The city of Cervia can be called the first cultural and historical center of the Adriatic. Tourism began to develop there in the early 20th century. Thanks to the construction of the unique pine park Marine Milano, the tourist industry began to develop much more actively.

How to get there

The resort is located 30 km from the airport of Rimini. Nearby is the city of Ravenna. In addition, the nearest neighboring airports of Forl√¨ and Bologna are 30 km and 90 km away. Thanks to regular charter flights, getting to the resort is easy even from Moscow. Direct non-stop flights will be a little over 3 hours. From any airport, the resort can be reached by bus or taxi.

Climate

Italy (Milano Marittima) is known for its warm Mediterranean climate. There are very few rainy days, mostly always sunny and warm. The best time to visit this exotic place will be the period from May to October.





Hotels

Most resort hotels are located on the seashore. There is no separation of the beach and the city, so the main restaurants, bars and discos are located right on the sand of Milano Marittima (Italy). Hotels, as a rule, do not have their own beach, tourists use the public city, especially since it is located a stone's throw from any hotel. Here you can find both luxurious 5-star villas, and inexpensive "treshki". Pricing is about the same as in Italy as a whole. As for food, here restaurant food is more expensive than in the neighboring city of Rimini. Hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis, but the range of services, drinks and products is strictly individual.





Beaches

The main feature of local beaches is cleanliness and well-groomed. Not every day you will find a sandy beach surrounded by a green pine forest. A gentle entry into the water and white sand without pebbles and stones is another feature of the local coastline. Milano Marittima is a favorite vacation spot for families with children, they often spend time with kids there, without worrying about their safety.

Each beach is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay - there are changing cabins, sun loungers, umbrellas, etc. In addition, there is a water transport rental point and playgrounds on the shore. Mandatory vacation attributes are not only on private beaches, the municipal Castagnoli Beach and Bagno Holiday can boast the same. There you can go water skiing, a surfboard or a boat.





What to see

Italy (Milano Marittima) is rich in various interesting architectural monuments and structures. In Cervia, you can see a lot of interesting things. Most history buffs head to the city center to visit St. Michael's Tower or the Salt Museum. There is also a unique cathedral of Santa Maria del Assunta and an unusual museum "Butterfly House".

Having visited this resort, tourists with children must visit the local water park "Aquabel", in addition, there are many attractions for adults. Tired of the beach, the sea and the sun, you can unwind in the Italian Disneyland or take a walk in the Myoabilandia theme park.

What else is Italy (Milano Marittima) famous for? Of course, with its forested area, where you can enjoy the silence, breathe fresh and healthy air. In addition, there are several mud and water treatment centers near the park. The most famous health and beauty complex is Cervia de Terme. There you can restore strength by taking thermal baths. Sauna, massage and other medical procedures help to cope with the disease and improve health. Qualified medical staff and modern equipment guarantee visitors quality care and treatment.

Another popular activity in Milano Marittima (Italy) is shopping. Many shops and shops will give maximum pleasure to squanders. There you can buy not only souvenirs and gifts for loved ones, but also Italian branded clothes and shoes, and the prices here are pretty reasonable.

The number of bars and restaurants in the resort is amazing. The Chili Pepper Bar, the Idroscalo Restaurant with live music, the Cognac Bisquit Matisse Patisserie, and even the Irish Pub, Irish Pub , are just a few of the entertainment venues.

Sports enthusiasts can visit the yacht club, work out in the special room, play golf and visit the riding school.





In Milano Marittima (Italy), reviews of which, full of enthusiasm, as a rule, leaves young people, you can have fun at night. The famous Adriatic discos work here - Stork and Pinetta, and you can dance on Papeete Beach until dawn.

A varied vacation awaits tourists in Milano Marittima (Italy). The map will tell you how to get there, any tour operator will show the most popular hotels, and reviews of vacationers will help you decide on a hotel. The main thing is to choose the right time for the trip. The period from May to August is the vacation season for students and the influx of young people. With a family in Milano Marritima it is better to relax from September to October.
