Lenovo S820T: reviews. Lenovo phones overview

Relatively recently appeared on sale smartphone mid-range Lenovo S820T. Reviews about him come in both positive and negative. We will deal with its strengths and weaknesses and determine the appropriateness of its purchase based on their ratio.





CPU

Everything was simple with its predecessor: the MTK 6589 4-core processor with a frequency of 1.2 GHz was the basis of the Lenovo S820. S820T (MTK6592, an 8-core CPU, indicated in the specifications of one of the modifications of this smartphone, and MTK6589, integrated in a simplified version of the device) can be equipped with one of these processors, depending on the modification of the gadget: "flagship" or "stripped down".

But according to the test results, it turns out that instead of the first of them, MTK6572 is installed with two real cores and six stopped and a clock frequency of 1.3 GHz instead of 1.7 GHz. To verify this, just download the utility "CPU - Z" from third-party sources and install it. Yes, it really will show after the launch that MTK6592 is installed. But this is bad luck - only two out of eight cores work. The rest are in stop mode and it is impossible to start them. As a result, it turns out that the MTK6572 processor is really integrated into the device. He has the same architecture - ‚ÄúCortex A7‚Äù, clock frequency - 1.3 GHz and only 2 cores on board. In general, the Chinese software trick: you buy one thing, it‚Äôs like what you ordered (at least the software convinces you of this), but behind all this is hidden completely different at the hardware level.

The only difference is the presence of an external communication module for working in third-generation networks. This is the basic configuration of the Lenovo S820T. Reviews of frustrated owners about this smartphone confirm the previously stated trick. But with the 4-core version, everything is fine. It uses the classic MTK65859T with a frequency of 1.5 GHz. The more cores, the more productive the smartphone, the longer it will have enough hardware resources. Two cores are not enough for comfortable gameplay, but four will do just fine with this task. A similar situation is with resource-intensive applications. Therefore, when buying, you need to clarify the real processor model that is installed in this device.





Graphics adapter and screen

The screen size of this smartphone is 5 inches. The claimed resolution for the 8-core model is 1920 by 1080 pixels. But given that he has only 2 of them in work, most of the applications will slow down. Do not forget that in such a situation a maximum of two touches will be processed, not five. In turn, the model with the MTK 6589 CPU has more modest characteristics - 1280 by 720 pixels and is capable of processing up to five touches inclusive. Type of installed matrix AMOLED. The picture quality is excellent. But with graphics adapters the situation is interesting. In the 8-core version, 450 MP from the Mali company is programmatically registered. But in reality, according to the test results, 400 of the same developer for Lenovo S820T will be indicated there. Specifications this graphics accelerator is an order of magnitude worse. Again, this adapter usually comes with the MTK 6572 central processor and 512 MB of random access memory. Moreover, this is an outdated model today. But the 4-core modification is equipped with the SGX544 graphics card from PowerVR. This is a more productive solution that will perfectly cope with most tasks today.

Memory and its quantity

An interesting memory situation is obtained with the Lenovo S820T. The feedback from the perplexed owners is another confirmation of this. Let's start with the basic version of this smartphone. According to the documentation, 2 GB of DDR3 RAM is integrated. But after turning on and checking the parameters, an interesting picture is revealed. Yes, its really 2 GB. But the system is constantly in a strange way takes 1.6 GB or more. In the best case, 200 MB is allocated for the user's needs, and maybe even less. In general, again, Chinese programmers cheated. They designed 2 GB of software, but in fact, 512 MB are installed, which are standard for the MTK6572 processor. But in the second modification with four cores, everything is fine, and 1 GB is integrated there. With the internal memory, everything is fine. In both the first and second cases, its 4 GB, of which 2 GB is occupied by the system, and the rest is provided to the user. There is also a slot for installing flash cards in MicroSD format with a maximum capacity of 32 GB.

Camera and its features

As noted earlier, Lenovo's cell phone of this model is equipped with two cameras at once. One of them on the front of the device is designed to make video calls. The battery and everything connected with it

In general, there are no obvious problems, as with the processor, memory and graphics adapter. Another plus is the ability to replace a faulty battery. Data Capabilities

Finally, we consider the communication capabilities of this smartphone. For wireless connection to the Global Web, you can use Wi-Fi (maximum speed up to 100 Mbit / s) and mobile networks of the third or second generation (in the first case we get a maximum of 3 Mbit / s, in the second - hundreds of kilobytes). If you need to download large content (for example, a movie), then it is better to stop the choice on wi-fi. But you can even browse sites and communicate on social networks even on JMS networks. For navigation, the ZhSM module is installed. Alas, it does not work with the GLONASS system. From this side, this Lenovo model looks flawlessly. Phone S820T, a review of wireless connections indicates this, is not equipped with an infrared port. But this technology, both morally and physically outdated. So there is nothing to worry about. Among the wired data transfer, the following types of connectors can be distinguished: MicroUSB (for connecting to a PC and charging the battery) and 3.5 mm audio jack for connecting external speakers or headphones. It should be noted right away that the stereo headset that comes with the smart phone is of very poor quality. Therefore, lovers of quality sound should buy other headphones.

Summary

To summarize. Oddities with the processor, memory and graphics adapter cause a lot of complaints on the Lenovo S820T. Reviews only confirm this. In any case, two cores today are not enough. A similar situation exists with RAM (only 512 MB in the basic configuration) and with a graphics adapter ("Mali-400" is clearly outdated). At the same time, the Lenovo S820T has practically no positive points. The price of $ 140 is clearly overpriced. If it was based on a full-fledged 8-core chip, then this was an excellent acquisition. And so the purchase of such a device is not entirely justified today - the cost is too high, and the filling with it is not very.
