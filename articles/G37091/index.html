Residential complex "Meshchersky Forest": reviews, address, developer

Reviews about the "Meshchersky Forest" is important to find out to everyone who is considering this residential complex, choosing a new apartment. The developer, who is engaged in its construction, promises some advantages, painting the LCD in admirable colors. But is this really so? This is what we have to figure out.

about the project

Reviews about the "Meshchersky Forest" are very diverse. Those who pay attention to this residential complex are primarily attracted by the slogan "Return to nature." The company, which is engaged in the construction of these new buildings, emphasizes that the quarter is being built in a picturesque area in the west of the Russian capital. To be more precise, between Meshchersky park and Borovsky highway. Govorovo metro station nearby (about five minutes walk).

This complex consists of six blocks of variable number of floors - from 14 to 25 floors with green landscaped courtyards. The facades of all houses are lined with bright ceramic tiles. Yards-parks are completely freed from cars, given for walks, games and sports grounds. Between themselves, all the houses are connected by a wide promenade, which allows local residents to spend maximum time in the fresh air, enjoying nature. During the design, all details and nuances were carefully thought out, which together with modern architecture ensures a pleasant and comfortable life.

It is important that the Meshchersky Forest residential complex in the future will have its own developed infrastructure. Two kindergartens, two schools, several modern ground parking lots will appear here. The first floors are intended for commercial premises. There will be convenience stores, hairdressers, cafes.

Information about the developer

The developer of the residential complex "Meshchersky Forest" today is riveted increased attention from equity holders. This is explainable. Nowadays, one of the determining factors when choosing a new building is the reliability of the company that is engaged in its construction. People are tired of the fact that they are regularly deceived, therefore they are ready to trust their money only to trusted companies that have long been working in this market, have proved themselves to be the best.

LCD "Meshchersky Forest" is being built by the PIK group of companies in the Solntsevsky District of the Western Administrative District of Moscow. This is an economy class residential complex , which is being built in several stages. On sale there are apartments from 22 to 94 and a half square meters. Work is underway on panel technology.

The PIK group of companies is building apartments in Moscow and the region. Currently, in addition to the Meshchersky Forest, projects are being implemented for the residential complexes Dmitrovsky Park, Salaryevo Park, West Port, Mikhailovsky Park, Orange Park, Belaya Dacha.

PIK Group of Companies is one of the largest domestic development companies that implements projects in Moscow, Moscow Region, and also in other regions of Russia. She has been working in the construction market since 1994, specializing in the implementation of projects in the affordable housing segment. During this time, it has already built about 19 million square meters of real estate, more than 300 thousand families are provided with apartments.

The company operates in ten regions of Russia, the land bank of the developer is about twelve and a half million square meters.

Location

Houses in the residential complex "Meshchersky Forest" in Moscow are located next to the forest park and recreation areas, in an area with minimal traffic load. About thirty various floor plans for one-, two-, three-room apartments and studio apartments are presented for customers to choose from. If necessary, it is possible to design a separate exit to the street from the first floor, this is done for the development of commercial real estate in the residential complex "Meshchersky Forest".

The ceilings on the ground floor reach three meters, and on the rest - 2.8 meters. The entrance to the entrance in all buildings is equipped at ground level, without ramps and steps, which is most convenient for people with disabilities. In addition, all buildings have wide and spacious elevators.

The yards are completely exempted from cars, they provide exclusively for children and walking areas. All yards have internet coverage.

Infrastructure

Currently, the infrastructure in this residential complex is poorly developed, but the developer intends to work on this issue. The construction of two kindergartens and a school in the residential complex "Meshchersky Forest" is planned.

Currently, service centers and shops are already operating in the microdistrict. In the nearby territory of the Bakovsky Forest Park there are bike paths, entertainment areas, everything you need for sports and fitness.

Large shopping centers OBI Borovskoye and Nash are already operating next to the new building, and a ten-minute ride by public transport has a clinic, a fitness center with a swimming pool, kindergartens, schools and a gymnasium.

Despite the proximity of the residential complex to the Moscow Ring Road, there is a favorable environmental situation.

Address

Address LCD "Meshchersky Forest" - Borovskoe highway, 2a. You can get here by private car or public transport.

If you are traveling by car, then you need to move along the Borovsky highway, which is located in the junction area at the 48th kilometer of the Moscow Ring Road. The LCD itself is located just 500 meters from the Moscow Ring Road in the direction of Moscow Region.

Knowing the address of the residential complex "Meshchersky Forest", you can come here by public transport. From Kiev station by train, you can get to the Skolkovo railway station. It is located about one kilometer from these new buildings.

In the immediate vicinity there is a metro from the residential complex "Meshchersky Forest". Shuttle buses No. 91 and No. 92 go to the Yugo-Zapadnaya station. And in August 2018, the Govorovo station was put into operation, which is five minutes walk from the complex.

This area is characterized by attractive transport accessibility.

Progress

Construction of residential complex "Meshchersky Forest" is carried out in several stages. It is worth noting that the developer initially delayed the deadlines, but then began to hand over the hull earlier than planned.

For example, the first phase was to be commissioned in the second quarter of 2017, however, this only happened in the fourth quarter. But before they passed the second turn: not in the first quarter of 2018, but also in the fourth quarter of 2017.

At the end of 2018, the third phase was commissioned, which they promised to build only in the first quarter of 2019. Currently, work is underway on the fourth, fifth and sixth stages. Recent work should be completed at the end of 2020.

Advantages and disadvantages

To summarize, we can formulate the main advantages and disadvantages of this residential complex. Unconditional advantages include the availability of a metro station within walking distance, as well as a large and well-equipped park. Nearby is also a railway station, by train, you can quickly get to the Kiev station.

Apartments can be purchased with or without decoration, in installments and mortgages, work is carried out by a reliable company that has been working in this market for more than twenty years. In the houses themselves, high-speed elevators are equipped, for a metropolitan apartment near the metro, the price is more than affordable.

True, there are enough shortcomings. One of the main ones is the nearby radioactive dump of cesium, which is currently bombarded and reclaimed, but still worries many locals. In addition, noise is heard from the railway and Borovsky highway, which can interfere with a good rest. The residential complex is located next to the Moscow Ring Road, next to it is a large cemetery. Panel houses, which affects their quality, there is no underground parking, which means that problems with where to park your car will appear sooner or later.

Having delayed the first place, the subsequent developer began to hand over, ahead of schedule. But speed negatively affects quality. Panels fall from some houses, tiles fall off. In addition, the location itself is extremely unsuccessful. The residential complex is actually sandwiched between Borovskoye Shosse, Moscow Ring Road and railways. Even in the courtyards there is a high gas contamination, construction is being carried out on the site of a cement plant, the demolition of which has not yet been completed.

There is a possibility that there will be an unfavorable criminal situation in the microdistrict, since near the Skolkovo metro station, about one and a half kilometers from the residential complex, there is an existing social hostel for former criminals and people without a fixed residence.
