Pattern "royal braid" knitting. Scheme, algorithm of actions

A hobby for a person is an indispensable source of positive emotions, a pleasant pastime and relaxation from the hustle and bustle of everyday problems. Among the abundance of varieties of such a holiday, knitting takes an important place. Many managed to turn their favorite business into an excellent type of stable income. After all, knitwear is always in demand, regardless of the variability of fashion. Knitting has a lot of advantages, the main of which is the creation of unsurpassed handmade things.

There is a huge variety of patterns that help create creative products from threads. Beautiful patterns with knitting needles can transform any knitted thing and make its owner an object of admiration. The pattern "royal braid" is very popular.





When creating a "royal braid" pattern with knitting needles, the pattern is an indispensable tool. It is she who will help beginners learn the necessary actions in knitting. In the future, having the skills, you can refuse to use it.

The algorithm for creating a pattern

If you knit a royal braid pattern with knitting needles, the canvas layout should consist of front or back loops. The number of loops for knitting braids should be a multiple of thirty, which will make it possible to make a clear picture. Next, the necessary conditions are met:

  • The first row is tied with facial loops.
  • Starting the second row, it is necessary to remember that after it all even rows are purl.
  • When creating the third row, you need to take five loops and, without knitting them, transfer to an additional knitting needle.
  • Then the subsequent loops (5 pcs.) - with facial loops, and only after them you need another 5 loops (for the "braid") to process with facial knitting. Having done this, we undertake to knit another 10 with a viscous face (5 - throw it again on an additional knitting needle). Having processed the loops with the help of facial knitting, we proceed to work on the loops with an additional knitting needle, also knitting them with the front.
  • The fifth and seventh rows will need to be knitted with the help of facial loops.
  • In the ninth row, strictly adhere to the sequence: the first 5 stitches are facial, and the next 5 are thrown to the third knitting needle. Next, you need to knit the next five stitches in the facial way, then proceed to the removed loops, knitting them also with facial loops. Having tied the next 5 loops, fold 5 again to the third knitting needle. The next five loops will be connected by facial ones (5 filmed also). The 5 remaining loops on the main needle will also be identical.
  • We completely knit the entire eleventh row using facial knitting.
  • The beginning of knitting, as in the first row, falls on the 13th row.
