How to make plasticine at home?

Kids are very fond of crafting various funny crafts from plasticine. Today, this material is easy to buy at any stationery store. However, such plasticine for children does not always meet all quality requirements and can cause an allergy in a child. For the most caring parents, there is a good way out of this situation. You can make the material for sculpting yourself. The main thing is to know how to make plasticine at home.





What is needed to prepare the material?

For the preparation of plastic material you will need ordinary food products. They will guarantee that if the baby licks his fingers, making another rabbit, he will not receive a dose of allergen. To prepare clay, you need to take a glass of water, two glasses of flour, a glass of salt, a spoonful of vegetable oil, food coloring and PVA glue.

How to make plasticine?

To understand how to make plasticine at home, you do not need serious skills and special equipment. To begin with, pour salt and flour into the dish. Then, stirring gently, pour in water. Next, add vegetable oil and PVA glue, mix well. In order for plasticine to turn out different colors, divide the resulting mass into parts and add multi-colored food colors to each. From a beautiful adhesive material, it is easy to fashion any figure you like or a whole landscape. You can store your clay at room temperature. If it starts to dry out, just add a little water to it and start working again.





How to make smart plasticine?

Another option for preparing the foundation for creativity is smart plasticine. In composition, it resembles ordinary chewing gum. Its peculiarity is that it sticks to hands, has no smell, and can also change its properties under the influence of temperature. Clever plasticine gives peace, relieves fatigue and develops imagination in children.



To understand how to make clay at home, you need to follow the simple instructions outlined below. First of all, prepare everything you need. Purchase sodium tetraborate in advance at the pharmacy , and also take PVA glue and food coloring. Squeeze glue and dye into the dish, then pour a spoonful of sodium tetraborate and slowly mix the mixture until it thickens well. Transfer the finished mass into a regular plastic bag and mash, achieving elasticity. Thus, the basis for creativity is ready. Now, together with your baby, you can create amazing masterpieces. You can store smart plasticine at room temperature, but in no case in the refrigerator, otherwise it will deteriorate.

So, knowing how to make plasticine at home, you can sculpt various original figures or crafts with your children, as well as original souvenirs or fridge magnets that will surely please your family and friends. Together with your household, you can arrange a competition of crafts, and then decorate beautiful figures or present to friends.
