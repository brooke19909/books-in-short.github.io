The issue of renting small premises in new buildings



Every year there are more and more new residential complexes. Construction companies tirelessly erect high-rise buildings in densely populated neighborhoods of large cities. The number of small enterprises and private organizations that require specialized office space is also growing and developing. A growing number of car owners need a good parking spot and garage.

Basement in new buildings: making the right choice

As you know, demand creates supply. Unpopulated, the so-called basement premises are becoming more and more popular. This space is a semi-basement, which is unsuitable for living. They, as well as non-residential premises located on the first floors of residential buildings, can be rented and used for various needs. But with one caveat: you need to make a quality repair, ensure the proper level of ventilation, take care of heating, etc.

Such premises may belong to both residents of the new building and other legal entities and individuals. Given that the house has just been built, the uninhabited floor is much easier to equip for your business. To learn more about the benefits of renting commercial premises in new buildings, please visit http://www.praktis.ru/buildings/search/ .

Who is interested in renting commercial premises?

Among the tenants of such tiers are:

  • Beauty Salons;
  • fitness clubs;
  • mini-cafe;
  • bank branches;
  • medical centers;
  • pharmacies;
  • company offices.

Rentals for multi-level parking are also very popular.

Construction companies offer various layout options, high-quality finishing, favorable location. Directly at the construction stage, anyone can purchase a commercial building. On sale there are various areas, from several tens to several hundreds and even thousands of square meters. Prices for such premises are attractive. The buying process is simple, the main thing is to deal with a trusted company developer.

Great demand and a choice of the described areas make it possible for people interested in renting or buying to choose a basement for themselves under favorable conditions. As high competitiveness in this area has increased, prices are becoming more flexible and enjoyable for customers.

When choosing a room, one should take into account what exactly the area is needed for. Organization residents should not disturb the peace.
