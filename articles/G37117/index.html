What to do Beeline subscribers with a zero balance

Even the most attentive subscribers are not always able to control the balance on their mobile number. Sometimes situations arise when you urgently need to call, send a message or use the Internet, and there is not enough money in the account to complete the operation. In this case, of course, you can get to the nearest office of the operator or ATM and replenish the balance. However, sometimes this is not possible. In such cases, Beeline subscribers with zero balance can use free services that allow you to always stay in touch. In this article we will talk about them in more detail.





Service "Trust payment"

By connecting this service, Beeline customers with zero balance can not think about the lack of funds in the account for three days. Trust payment means transferring a certain amount to the subscriber's account, which can be used for any services provided by the mobile operator. The size of such a payment is determined individually, based on how much the client spends each month on communication. By the way, only those subscribers who use a number from three months old can activate this service. To check the amount available, enter * 141 * 7 # on the phone. Service activation is performed by the command * 141 #.

There is also a variant of this service, when a trust payment is activated automatically when a certain balance value is reached on the subscriber‚Äôs number (for example, as soon as the amount is less than 50 rubles on the account, accrual occurs). This can be convenient for forgetful customers: they will not have to think at a critical moment on the question of how to call with a zero balance. Beeline enables customers to take care of their account in advance. You can activate the service through the Internet assistant, as well as through the operator of the client center.





Service "Live Zero"

A similar option is provided free of charge and is available to all Beeline users. Services with a zero balance, implying the possibility of the client receiving incoming calls and text messages, remain active. And this means that, despite the lack of funds, the subscriber will always stay in touch.
