The electronic "reader" PocketBook 626 Touch Lux2 is called by experts one of the most technologically advanced in the Touch line. The model is distinguished by a stylish design, the highest screen quality and functionality. The most common market modification of the device is PocketBook 626 Gray. Feedback from users and experts about the new product is diverse, but in general comes down to a positive assessment of the gadget.





Despite the fact that some functions present in previous models were excluded from the device, the brand's inherent style and performance remained, as experts admit, at the level. The same opinion is also shared by users who are willing to leave feedback on the operating experience of the device.

What are the most notable features of the PocketBook 626? Reviews of owners who have studied the capabilities of the device - what is their degree? Is the device praised or criticized more?

Specifications

The Reader is equipped with a 6-inch display made by E-Ink Pearl technology, a processor with a frequency of 1 GHz, 4 GB of flash memory (expandable to 32 due to additional microSD modules). The device is able to recognize almost all common formats of electronic documents and pictures. There is support for WiFi. The device is running Linux.

The above characteristics of hardware, experts say, are more than enough to perform basic user tasks. "Readers", unlike their "brothers" in the form of tablets, laptops and smartphones, are devices that are not designed to run games and do not require high-performance applications.





Users who left reviews on the capabilities of PocketBook 626 reviews also find the totality of its technical characteristics as completely consistent with the tasks assigned to the reader. At a fairly affordable price (about 6-7 thousand rubles) of the device, its hardware characteristics, as the gadget owners, as well as experts believe, are in no way inferior to those of competitive solutions.

Appearance

The e-book has a stylish design with a minimum of frills and external controls. The body material in the main modification is gray, matte plastic, quite resistant to fingerprints. There is also a white version - PocketBook 626 White. Reviews about the design concept of this modification of the gadget, left by experts and owners, are also very positive.

The buttons on the case are plastic. This appeals to so many users who thought that the rubber counterparts of these controls that were used in previous models were not a very good idea. Users who have left reviews regarding the operation of PocketBook Touch 626 believe that plastic is a material that provides incomparably greater comfort in controlling the device, as well as a noticeably faster reaction of the device to external commands. In addition, according to many experts, this material blends better with the elegant "reading room" style.





Key functions are standard - "Return", "Forward" and "Home". The buttons are pressed very easily. The build quality of the case is evaluated by both experts and users as very high. There are no gaps, creaks and backlashes. At the bottom there is a slot for a USB cable.

Many owners of PocketBook 626, whose reviews are quite common, note the minimalist design of the device. There are no extra buttons, access to the basic functions is opened through a few simple steps. The same opinion is also shared by many experts who tested the capabilities of the reader. The case of the device is very thin (8 mm), and therefore any case for PocketBook 626 will look great without violating the harmony of proportions.

File manager

Other key software components include a file manager. Owners of PocketBook 626 Touch Lux2, whose reviews allow us to judge the quality of the device, talk about the interface of this application in an extremely positive way. Concerning the capabilities that the PocketBook 626 e-book has, the reviews practically do not affect the aspect related to the lack of the device's ability to play audio files.

There is an application with which the device user can buy "virtual" literature. This is the BookLand online portal. By the way, many literary works can be downloaded from there for free. The PocketBook 626 e-book has a software module for taking notes.
