How and why to use the mass registration address?

One of the most significant criteria for a good legal address is its mass character. What is it and what threatens the address of mass registration of the enterprise? Why are such addresses created and is there a chance to protect oneself from such a misfortune.



Definition

The very concept of ‚Äúmass registration address‚Äù applies to legal addresses at which 10 or more legal entities are officially registered. There is also a term and the opposite of this - "non-mass address" - one that was used for registration by less than ten companies. Since modern business life is developing quite rapidly, there are also addresses with a large number of organizations "a dime a dozen" registered there. For example, it can be a variety of office complexes and business centers.

In fact, it might seem that this is not a big deal. However, in practice, everything does not look so harmless at all. Today, on the Internet, you can find a huge number of various offers regarding the sale of yuradres. The options are offered a wide variety - from the actual rental of one square meter of a real existing office with the obligation to promptly transmit to you all the correspondence in your name, to the frank offer of fake documents. This is where the problems begin. The latter method is most often used by one-day firms, cooperation with which is extremely dangerous.



Appearance story

Before the Unified State Register of Legal Entities was created in Russia, or rather, until July 2002, the registration of legal entities was carried out by a variety of bodies. At the same time, in some regions of the country they required to provide information on the so-called ‚Äúlegal address‚Äù for registration, although such a requirement was not legally confirmed, and such a term was not enshrined at all by any normative acts. Despite the fact that such requirements were not substantiated in any way, such a practice was firmly established and has been preserved to this day.

Over time, it has become a vicious practice when, during state registration of a company, applicants who do not want to show the actual location of their company or simply do not need to maintain an office simply enter in the documents the address provided by the intermediary registrar.

As a result of such actions, a large list of enterprises having the same registration address appeared in the field of view of the tax inspectorate. Check

Today, anyone can check if the location of the company is registered as a mass registration address. The Federal Tax Service offers to do this openly, right on its website. You can immediately say that this list does not include addresses at which business centers, large office complexes or large administrative buildings are officially located. It is clear that few enterprises simply cannot be registered at such addresses, because the very purpose of their existence is to lease premises for the offices of various companies.

Where to check the address for "mass"

So, you want to track a company that, in your opinion, has a mass registration address. Checking this is very simple: go to the site https://service.nalog.ru/addrfind.do and in the window that opens, select the parameters we need:

Signs of Inaccuracy





Is it so scary to find a mass registration address? By itself - no, it is much more important when choosing a business partner to detect signs of unreliability of his legal address. This can be done based on the following criteria:

  • the location of the company is listed not only as the address of mass registration of individuals (IP), as well as enterprises of other forms of ownership, but it is also impossible to communicate with most of them; for example, correspondence sent to such an address is returned with the words ‚Äúthe addressee has left‚Äù or ‚Äúbeyond the expiration of the storage period‚Äù;
  • such an address does not exist in reality or the building previously located there is destroyed;
  • in fact, the address is conditional, for example, it is assigned to an unfinished building project;
  • at the specified address are state authorities, military units, consulates, diplomatic missions, etc .;
  • There is an official statement of the owner, where he prohibits the registration of legal entities at the address of the specified property.

If at least one of the above circumstances is clarified, and the applicant has not submitted to the registration authorities any other documents confirming the possibility of contacting him at such an address, then he will be deemed unreliable. In this case, the court may decide to liquidate such an enterprise.

Risk of using purchased address

What could threaten the company that bought the address of mass registration. The consequences can be diverse, it all depends on the honesty of the intentions of the parties to the transaction.

The negative consequences include the following:

  • a package of documents for renting an office or other real estate property may turn out to be fake, and the room itself does not exist at all;
  • you can be refused registration if it is proved that the location of the company is the address of mass registration and is on the "black list" of the tax service; True, the legitimacy of such a refusal is highly doubtful, but in such cases there is no particular argument;
  • sometimes it may be difficult to open a current account, although there are no legal grounds for this, but who wants to have a conflict with the bank where it is going to be served;
  • difficulties with receiving correspondence; if the letter from the Federal Tax Service does not reach the recipient registered at such an address, but returns back - this may become an unofficial basis for a personal visit by the tax office of the company with a sudden check; and if this is not possible, the company may be liquidated by a court at the initiative of the Federal Tax Service;
  • if, at the address indicated during registration, the employees of the tax or internal affairs bodies (on the instructions of the Federal Tax Service) do not actually find the enterprise, this may serve as a basis for enrolling the company in the rank of one-day firms; after this, the refusal of VAT deduction and other unpleasant ‚Äúsurprises‚Äù for its counterparts will come in time.
