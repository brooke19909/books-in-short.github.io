What is the median salary: calculation, statistics. Median income

We often wonder why such huge numbers are shown in statistics. A part of the population believes in an unequal distribution of incomes, which means that somewhere at the top officials illegally fulfill their powers, others believe that against the background of the total number of able-bodied people, a small group with an income above the average is leading, which causes such statistics. In general, the way it is.





Distinctive features of the average salary

Before you start talking about the median salary, you need to understand what is the average salary and what is common between these two types of accruals. So, let's begin.

According to the Federal State Statistics Service, the average income in Russia is about 40,000 rubles, 50,000 in St. Petersburg, and about 65-70 thousand in Moscow. But such data seems a bit exaggerated to most network users, and that's why. In terms of average salary, one cannot trace the real dynamics of the standard of living of a group of people. Consider a classic example from economics. A tycoon with an income of 5,000,000 rubles a month came to an ordinary Russian village with an average income of 4,700 rubles and a population of 100 people. Since then, the average monthly income of residents has amounted to more than 50,000 rubles. Do you think the standard of living of the local population has changed? Naturally, no, except that the tycoon opened his own company in the village and raised the salaries of its residents.





In order to calculate the standard of living of an average large group of people, statisticians use two tricks: they drop 10% of the poorest and richest citizens and average their salaries or find out the median income indicator. The second method is more common and is used much more often.

Median salary and its criteria

So that in this case there are no questions, we came up with a special calculation criterion called the median. This indicator is used in statistical reports, replacing the average. Median salary is a chart that most correctly reflects the real state.

Median salary in the Russian Federation

According to some statistics, if the median salary is 23-29% lower than average, then payments for 55% of Russians in a particular region of residence will be below the average by about 25%.

Based on the foregoing, the median salary in Moscow, according to the statistics of 2016, amounted to 51,000 rubles a year, in St. Petersburg - 34,500 rubles, in other regions of Russia - within 27,000 rubles.





But do not you think such indicators are slightly understated and untrue figures? Of course.

Calculation Examples

We will deal with an example of calculating the median salary for 10 able-bodied people. For example, the dynamics of earnings of 9 people looks like this: 100, 101, 102, 103, 104, 105, 106, 107, 108, another one earns 10,000 rubles. So, the average salary will exceed 1000 rubles. But the median will be only 104 rubles. What is the median salary in Russia? Based on an example, we can say that the median is the salary of a person who is average in a row / list. Simply put, half of the people get less than his salary, the other half - more.





This practice is more common in EU member states. In our region, median wages are calculated a little differently. That is why many people have the question: "What is the median salary?"

One more example

The median salary is different from the average, and its indicators are almost always lower. Let's try to parse it using an example.

The organization employs 3 people: courier, accountant, manager. The daily earnings of an accountant - 330 rubles, a courier - three times lower, a leader - three times higher.

We calculate the average value of wages by the formula: (110 + 330 + 990) / 3 = 476.6 rubles.

Let's try to calculate the median. This concept means the salary of an employee in the middle of the salary list. That is, in our case, the salary of an accountant is 330 rubles, which, according to the considered example, is in the middle of the list. As you can see, the amount of median wages was an order of magnitude lower than the average.
