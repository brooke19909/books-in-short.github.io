Subsidies

This payment method involves the use of a special certificate issued for a fixed amount. This document is intended solely for the purchase of an apartment. This certificate can be used to repay a previously issued loan or to make a down payment on a mortgage.

There is a special opportunity for families in which a second or even third child was born. They can receive maternity capital available for use as a down payment on a mortgage, as payment for installments, as a missing amount for 100% payment.

Installment plan

It is also possible to arrange an installment plan for no more than 48 months. At the same time, if the client is able to immediately provide the full price of the apartment, he will receive a twenty percent discount.

When applying for installments, interest is not charged. The minimum allowable down payment should be thirty percent of the price of the apartment, however, under certain conditions, you can get an additional discount on this contribution (twenty percent if the installment plan is issued only for the period of construction work; fifteen percent if the installment plan is valid for thirty-six months). The discount is not valid when applying for installments for the entire possible period (forty-eight months).
