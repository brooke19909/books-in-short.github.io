CPU

The computing foundation is really good at Lenovo Vibe Shot. A review of the technical parameters of the gadget indicates that it uses the "Snapdragon 615". Of course, this is not a flagship solution, but its capabilities are quite enough to solve any problem. This chip consists of two computing clusters. The first of them provides high energy efficiency processor solutions. It includes 4 computing cores based on the A53 architecture, which are capable of overclocking to 1 GHz. The second module provides high-performance chip. It also consists of 4 computing modules of the same architecture. But here they can increase their frequency, unlike the previous 4 cores, they can already up to 1.7 GHz in the highest load mode. If necessary, all eight cores can work in parallel, but the battery of the gadget will be discharged just before our eyes.





Some of the most demanding toys will not be able to go with maximum settings on this gadget. But, on the other hand, this device is aimed at fans to take high-quality photos or record videos. And with this, the problem of this central processing unit certainly will not arise.

At the same time, the screen resolution is already very good by today's standards, 1080p, or 1920x1080. The density of pixels on its surface is 441ppi. It goes without saying that with such a density, a single pixel certainly cannot be distinguished by the ordinary eye. The matrix of the screen is made according to the most advanced technology at the moment - ‚ÄúIPS‚Äù. Because of this, viewing angles and energy efficiency are at a decent level. Another plus of the device is the availability of OZHS technology. That is, there is no air gap between the touch panel and the surface of the screen matrix. As a result, image quality is getting an order of magnitude better.

Cameras

Of course, the main plus compared to competitors is the main camera in Lenovo Vibe Shot. Photos and videos taken with her help are really high quality. She has a 16 megapixel sensor. Well, with so much free random access memory you can not install additional special software that will clean the RAM. The capacity of the integrated storage device is 32 GB. About 6 GB of them are filled with system software. The remaining free space the user can use at his discretion. This is quite enough to install a substantial amount of applications, to store many musical compositions or several films in good quality. With an average load on the device, one charge will definitely last 1.5 days. If you transfer this device to "Emergency mode" (the phone can only make calls and receive and send short test messages), then it will be possible to stretch for 3 days on a single charge. Well, if you launch some rather demanding three-dimensional toy, then the smartphone will be discharged in 4-5 hours. Therefore, the autonomy indicators in this gadget are very mediocre. Well, you can‚Äôt expect more from a 2900 mAh battery, a processor with 8 cores on board, a large screen of 5 inches (although it is manufactured using energy-efficient technology, but its resolution is 1920 √ó 1080).
