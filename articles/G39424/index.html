Sewing a stuffed pillow is not easy

You can use the most standard trick - to make a pillow stuffed. But this is only so simple at first glance - I sewed a cover and filled it with a padding polyester, for example. The details of which the pillow-knot consists of are long and narrow, such that they can be beautifully tied. And the whole complexity of the work will consist precisely in this - to fill narrow fabric pipes with filler. The length of the tow on the pillow should be 3-4 meters. You can, of course, and more. From pieces of suitable material with a width of 20-30 centimeters, and in length - along the cut of the fabric, you need to sew such a long four-meter workpiece. Then fold it in half, lay a long, long seam and stitch another seam - a short one. Unscrew the part to the front side through the unstressed short. Now it is necessary to fill the long pipe with filler. It will be more convenient to stuff the future pillow if you make the beginning of the part stiff by inserting a sleeve from paper towels there. Through this tip using a long stick (you can use the handle from the squeegee, of course, to start washing it and wiping it dry), fill the pipe with a synthetic winterizer or other filler. When it is full, remove the sleeve and sew the edge.





Twisted ropes

How to make a pillow-knot in such a way as not to suffer with stuffing long parts? What does that require? It is much easier to make a knot-shaped pillow using harnesses trimmed with fabric. They can be twisted from any suitable material - synthetic winterizer, batting. You can use old kapron or children's tights, cutting off from them tight traces and the upper part with an elastic band. You can not throw out the old drape coat, but rip it apart and wash the fabric, in the same way give it a second life in the form of an unusual pillow. Everything that can be rolled up into a long roll and then sheathed with fabric should be put into business for the manufacture of an interesting decorative element.

So, the length of the roll should be at least 3 meters, and the thickness of 5-7 centimeters in diameter. Twisting the individual parts, they can be joined, grabbing the thread with a needle so that they do not creep out during the operation of the pillow. When the roll is ready, it is wrapped in cloth and the cover is sewn up manually with a blind seam. In this case, it is convenient to use knitwear - an old sweater, sweater, children's plaid. You can wrap finished rolls of fleece - this material is well suited for creating all kinds of warm and cozy things.
