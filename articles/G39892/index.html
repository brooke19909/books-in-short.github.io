Hotel Hillside Beach Club Fethiye, Turkey: description and reviews

In the tourist brochures located in Fethiye, the Hillside Beach Club is positioning itself as a luxury resort complex that will take vacationers to paradise with stunning sea views in Kalem Bay. So is it really or not, you judge. And we will tell you what interesting the hotel offers its guests, what categories of rooms are provided in it and how many accessible beaches are nearby.





The resort complex is the most famous of the "luxury" category on the Aegean coast. He is the owner of his own picturesque bay and several beaches.

Location and general description of the hotel

One of the main strengths of the Fethiye Hillside Beach Club Hotel is its location. The complex was built in the picturesque bay of Kalemia on the first coastline. The distance to the beach, which is 315 meters long, is only 100 meters. The nearest airport is Dalaman 55 km, the city of Fethiye - 4 km.

The hotel was built in 1992, the last renovation was held in 2016. The complex covers an area equal to 100 thousand square meters. m. All vacationers can fully enjoy the beauty of the sea coast. A huge green area offers hiking. The hotel consists of seven buildings, the total number of rooms is 350.

Types of accommodation





The hotel offers its guests four categories of rooms. Guests will be delighted not only by the surrounding nature, the view of which opens from the windows and balconies, but also by the stylish interior of the rooms. Every detail of the room is designed and created for a comfortable stay. Vacationers are alone with nature and the sea, away from the bustle of the city.

  • Superior Family Room is a luxurious family room with the largest area in the Hillside Beach Club (Fethiye), 50 m 2 . It has a private terrace with views of the bay. It can accommodate up to four people.
  • Superior Double Room - a spacious room (40 m 2 ) with a terrace and views of the Aegean Sea. Maximum accommodates three people.
  • Double Club Room - an ordinary double room of 30 m2, without a balcony.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, LCD TV, minibar, safe, hairdryer and telephone. Free Wi-Fi is available on site. The rather modest rooms for a five-star hotel are compensated by a wide range of additional services.

Hotel Restaurants





Accommodation at the hotel does not include meals according to the usual all-inclusive system, but half board (breakfast, lunch and dinner, plus some privileges). The Hillside Beach Club (Fethiye) has three restaurants. The main one is located in the main building, near the reception and pool. It is there that they serve a rich and varied buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The menu consists of the most popular dishes of world and Turkish cuisines, there is light vegetarian food. Families with children will be pleasantly surprised by the presence of the so-called ‚Äúbaby chef‚Äù. He will be happy to prepare delicious, nutritious and healthy dishes for young guests.

The second restaurant, Pasha on the Bay, is located on the coast. It can be visited by adults and children over the age of twelve years daily (except Sunday). However, a table for dinner must be booked in advance. The menu includes Mediterranean dishes, fish and seafood. Drinks are paid extra.

On the beach, everyone can visit the Beach Bar & Restaurant, specializing in Italian cuisine. The restaurant is open daily. However, lunch and dinner are served outdoors only in suitable weather. In order to visit it in the evening, you must first reserve a table. Drinks are paid extra.

Bars in the complex





The resort of Hillside Beach Club in Fethiye has as many as five bars, evenly scattered across a vast territory.

Blue is located in the central part, near the main beach. In it you can enjoy a variety of cocktails to the sound of the surf and the sounds of jazz music. The beach bar will appeal to young people. Dynamic modern music does not subside in it until late at night. The best DJs perform at the Pasha Bar, while Abacco is more suitable for those who want to watch the sunset in peace and quiet, with a glass of good wine. In addition, there are two bars on the main beach and on Serenity.

Entertainment at the hotel





Entertainment at the Hillside Beach Club Hotel (Fethiye), the photo of which is presented in the article, is enough, and for every taste. Fans of measured beach holidays can visit any of the beaches or stay near the huge pool. Its surface area is 680 m 2 , the water is constantly heated. Amateur water polo competitions are held throughout the day.

Lovers of outdoor activities will love water sports. It was in this hotel for the first time in Turkey that the official section of wakeboarding and water skiing was created. Guests can do ni-boarding, ringo, sky-skiing, windsurfing, take a catamaran tour, and also take a diving course (including with children), get the appropriate certificates and dive into the waters of the Aegean Sea.

In addition, everyone who has been trained in sailing by experienced instructors can rent one of the yachts and take an independent trip to Fethiye bays with friends or family.

SPA salon





This complex belongs to the hotels of the club system of the first category (HV1). Hillside Beach Club Hotel (Fethiye, Turkey) places its guests in small cottages or bungalows. As a rule, the level of such complexes corresponds to 3-4 stars. However, Hillside Beach Club offers a wide range of additional services, has a vast territory and claims to be a five-star. In particular, he has his own SPA-salon. Within its walls, guests can relax not only with their bodies, but also with their souls. The catalog of services includes various types of massage, a traditional Turkish bath, a sauna, a jacuzzi and other wellness and anti-aging treatments.
