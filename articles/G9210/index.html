Woraburi Resort Spa Karon Beach 4: review, description, features and reviews of tourists

Woraburi Resort Spa Karon Beach 4 * is a resort spa hotel, which is located right on Karon Beach. The building of this hotel complex is visible from afar - it fits very well into the surrounding landscape. In Thailand there are many popular hotels, this one refers to them.





Briefly about the hotel complex

One of the main advantages inherent in the Woraburi Resort Spa Karon Beach 4 * hotel is its extremely favorable location for tourists. Firstly, this place is only 45 minutes from the international airport. Accordingly, transport links here are very well developed.

In the immediate vicinity are various interesting sights. For example, the legendary Chalong Temple - it is only five kilometers away. Anyway, it’s very picturesque here - there are lots of different interesting places that you should definitely visit. No wonder this part of the city was recognized as the most beloved among tourists who have ever come here on vacation.

Hotel Room Categories

The complex Woraburi Resort Spa Karon Beach 4 * in total there are six categories of apartments. All of them differ in cost, design, the availability of certain amenities and, in principle, conditions.

So, the first and second categories include superior double rooms with one double bed or two separate (offers a view of the pool and the surrounding area). There is also a deluxe apartment, designed for two guests. There are rooms of the same category, but only an additional bonus is access to the pool.

Still guests have the opportunity to reserve a junior suite or executive suite. All of these numbers are popular, so I would like to talk about them in more detail and discuss issues of cost and proposed conditions.





Superior 2-bed Apartment

It’s worth starting with this category. Such numbers are considered in the Woraburi Resort Spa Karon Beach 4 * the most inexpensive. And in fact, it is. The cost of booking for 2 full weeks of rest is 55-80 thousand rubles for two. The price may vary, but it depends on several factors, which are the season, the availability of discounts and special offers. If you are lucky to get into one of the most profitable periods, it will turn out to be a good save - about 30% of the standard cost.

Now about the proposed conditions. It’s worth saying right away that this room is decorated just fine. Inside there are two wide beds (which, by the way, are declared as 1-sleeping) or 1 huge, for two - it all depends on which option the potential guest will choose during the booking. There is a balcony and a spacious bathroom with a shower and a bathtub. In addition to the above, there is a TV with cable and satellite TV, a safe, air conditioning, as well as such nice “bonuses” as a hairdryer with a bathrobe, slippers, a minibar and toiletries. By the way, the room even has its own seating area and a kettle with coffee maker.

By the way, apartments of the same category, but with access to the pool, will cost approximately 65-83 tons. for two for the same stay.





Deluxe Apartment

These rooms are also popular at Woraburi Resort Spa Karon Beach 4 *. In any case, they receive the most positive reviews. Guests assure that here and comfort at a decent level: the design of the room, and amenities. The cost, by the way, is not much higher than in the previous case. 65-85 t. for two. If with breakfast - then 77-100 t.

The room itself is a spacious apartment with a living area of ​​39 square meters. m, where there is a huge 2-double bed “king” size. They are decorated in a modern national style and have a balcony with sea views. In addition, the room has a wide plasma TV (of course, with satellite and cable channels) and other amenities necessary for a comfortable stay and good rest. Everything that is available in the previous categories of rooms is also in these apartments.

If a person wants to book the same deluxe room, but with access to the pool, you will have to pay 86-115 thousand rubles for two (with breakfast, for 15 days and 14 nights). Only the room will be less spacious - not 32, but 39 square meters. m





Junior Suite

This category is in special demand at Woraburi Phuket Resort Spa 4 * (Thailand, Phuket). So, the junior suites are spacious, modern rooms, with a living area of 60 square meters. m! And the cost of booking for 14 weeks is 146-178 thousand rubles for 2 guests (with breakfast). The apartment has a large bed, has its own dressing room and a separate seating area. A TV with a DVD player and all kinds of channels, a safe, air conditioning, a comfortable soft sofa, a telephone ... Inside there is everything that you might need for a full stay. Even a refrigerator in some apartments is worth it. People who stayed in the junior suites of Woraburi Phuket Resort (Karon Beach, Thailand) assure: if you want to have a great rest in a modern and comfortable room, then definitely you need to make a choice in favor of this category.





Suite

This is an executive room, which is the most spacious, comfortable and, of course, expensive of all existing in this hotel complex. The suite is designed for two people, and you can be sure that it will be as cozy, convenient and comfortable as possible. 76 square meters - this is the area of ​​these apartments. And they will cost about 185-238 thousand rubles with breakfast for 15 days. Despite the high price, rooms are often booked. So if there is a desire to stay here, then the reservation should be puzzled in advance.

This suite, one might say, is a full-fledged separate apartment with magnificent sea views. It has a separate living room, bedroom and bathroom. Sofas, bedside tables, TV, mirrors - inside there is absolutely everything that is needed for comfort. And, of course, the other amenities listed earlier in the description of the other categories are also available in the deluxe room.





For rest and leisure

The reviews about Woraburi Phuket Resort Spa are mostly positive, and this is not surprising, since the hotel complex really deserves them. The hotel offers its guests not only comfortable and modern rooms at budget prices, but also various kinds of entertainment. There is an outdoor swimming pool and outdoor barbecue. Fans of actively spending time will like the presence of table tennis and a fitness room, which has everything for holding full-fledged workouts.

Still guests of Woraburi Resort Spa Phuket 4 * (Phuket, Karon, Thailand) can go in for water sports or just snorkel and snorkel. And lovers of creative entertainment will definitely enjoy karaoke.

And yes, of course, there is a bar on the territory of this popular hotel complex. There is also a cafe, known under the pleasant name Smile. It serves original Asian and European dishes. Buffet breakfasts are also served in the same cafe. Guests can enjoy a variety of cocktails and drinks at the bar called Slik or in the lobby lounge. There is, by the way, a bar near the pool. They also serve alcohol and juices.





Service and maintenance

The Woraburi Phuket Resort Spa in Karon (Thailand) has an excellent level of service and an appropriate attitude towards guests. This has been repeatedly confirmed by people who have been here. Everything is organized perfectly - from the delivery of drinks, food and packed lunches to the room to the expedited check-in or check-out. There is also parking on site, and if a person arrives on vacation without a car, he may be offered a car rental for the duration of his stay. All necessary conditions are provided to guests of the Woraburi Phuket Resort Spa 4 * hotel (Phuket, Thailand). Transfer, currency exchange, left-luggage office, even a nanny and laundry - that's all there is to it. Even a business center and conference and banquet facilities. No wonder the people who were already here decide to return next time.

The hotel is really good. Numerous positive reviews are a direct confirmation of this. Reasonable prices, comfortable conditions, a decent level of service, nice staff - what else does a person need for a good vacation?
