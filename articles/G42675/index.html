Hotel Sonnen Hotel 3 *, Marmaris: description and reviews of tourists

The port city of Marmaris, located on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey, is famous not only for its convenient beaches and green tropical forests, but also for its extensive tourist infrastructure. Local hotels offer tourists usually very comfortable rooms at a low cost. Domestic vacationers can stay in Marmaris, for example, in the Sonnen Hotel 3 * complex .

What is a complex?

This comfortable three-star hotel has been hosting guests of the city since 1995. That is, experience in serving tourists here is already very large. The rooms in this hotel are equipped in one multi-storey building with elevators.





Check-out time at Sonnen is 2:00 p.m. Previously, tourists are not allowed into the rooms here. In some hotels in Turkey, you can check into rooms immediately upon arrival, simply by paying staff. In Sonnen Hotel 3 * this, unfortunately, is not possible. However, lodgers here still offer lunch to arrivals before checkout time.

Of course, while staying at this hotel, its guests must abide by certain rules of residence. For example, pets are not allowed in this hotel. Smoking in the hotel rooms, of course, is also impossible. However, for cigarette lovers this hotel is still equipped with special places.

Most of the rest in this complex are Russians, Ukrainians and Poles. Also quite often Turks settle here too. The courtyard of this hotel, despite the fact that it is urban, has its own. It is quite large in size, well landscaped and looks, according to vacationers, beautiful.

Location Features

This hotel is located directly in the town of Marmaris. Her vacationers consider her location to be very successful. The fact is that this hotel was once built in the immediate vicinity of the city market. So, its guests still have the opportunity to purchase everything they need for themselves, as well as all kinds of souvenirs at a low cost with absolutely no problems.

The distance from Sonnen Hotel 3 * to the center of Marmaris is only about 1 km. That is, hotel guests can get here on foot in literally 30 minutes. In the immediate vicinity of the complex there are many cafes and restaurants.





In relation to the airport, this hotel, according to vacationers, is also located quite well. Tourists arriving by plane on a tour operator's bus usually have to get no more than an hour.

This hotel is located on the first coastline. Its guests need to go to the beach no longer than a couple of minutes. In this regard, the hotel Sonnen Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) reviews from tourists deserved especially good.

Hotel rooms

In total, the Sonnen Hotel has over 140 rooms for Marmaris guests. The complex also has single apartments. In all rooms of the hotel are provided:

  • air conditioners and televisions;
  • wardrobes and minibars.

Most rooms also have comfortable balconies. The views from many of the rooms rented at the hotel open up simply magnificent - to the mountains and the sea.

Of course, equipped in all apartments of the hotel and individual showers. Here, tourists are offered a standard set of bathing accessories and a hairdryer. To ensure the safety of guests, smoke detectors are installed in Sonnen rooms . This hotel also offers a service such as a crib in the room. Televisions are equipped with LCD televisions.





Sonnen Hotel 3 * (Marmaris): guest reviews on rooms

The rooms in this hotel, according to vacationers, are offered to tourists quite comfortable and cozy. The sizes handed over to S onnen numbers in most cases are large. The exception is only single rooms. But here too too guests in a hotel are never too crowded. The presence of balconies, most vacationers, of course, refers to the big pluses of the hotel. Air conditioning and plumbing in this hotel are in most cases working properly. But even if something breaks down here, the employees of the complex instantly put everything in order.

Cons of rooms

Vacationers consider only a rather weak WIFi as some drawback of hotel rooms. Also, in the rooms rented out at the hotel, according to vacationers, there could be better soundproofing. This hotel is located on a rather busy street. And loud music coming from the bars can interfere with the sleep of the guests of some rooms.

Hotel infrastructure

Parking directly in the complex Sonnen Hotel 3 * (Marmaris ) is not. But guests of this hotel can leave a car rented in the city not far from its buildings. Near the hotel there is quite a lot of free parking.





There is also an outdoor swimming pool at this hotel. The guests of the complex in the morning, afternoon or evening can swim in fresh water. The hotel has comfortable sun loungers next to the pool. Also here, like almost any other three-star Turkish hotel, there is a bar.

In addition to the pool, in the complex there are:

  • safe at the reception;
  • meeting room;
  • fireplace.

Sonnen guests can pay for all kinds of services with credit cards. In the evening, hotel guests have the opportunity to sit on the terrace or take a walk in a small beautiful garden.

In addition to an adult, this hotel also has a children's pool. The hotel also has a currency exchange service. On the territory of the complex you can make a photocopy of documents or send a fax from here. Of course, equipped in Sonnen and laundry.

Infrastructure Reviews

Many tourists believe that this hotel is suitable mainly for relaxing unassuming young people. Families with children and senior citizens, according to vacationers, would be better off looking for themselves in Marmaris some other, more expensive and comfortable hotel. But, of course, such guests can check into this hotel. Judging by the reviews, the service at Sonnen is offered to tourists, albeit slightly intrusive, but still quite acceptable. In addition, children under 7 years old can stay at this hotel without extra beds for free.

Tourists find the swimming pool in this hotel very convenient. It is not too large in size, but the water in it is always warm and clear. Hotel staff periodically clean the pool with a special vacuum cleaner. Therefore, he is never dirty here.





The staff of this hotel in general deserved very good reviews from tourists. Sonnen employees treat their guests with warmth and affection. Many tourists who come to Turkey later and stay in other, more expensive hotels, even sometimes come to Sonnen, just to chat with those of his employees with whom they have already made friends.

How to feed at the hotel?

The all-inclusive system is, according to tourists, one of the undoubted advantages of the Sonnen Hotel 3 * (Marmaris, Turkey). In Marmaris, such a catering scheme for three-star hotels is a rarity. Guests in this complex are offered not only breakfasts, dinners and lunches, but also afternoon tea. Drinks at the bar are available for free.

Regarding the dining room of this complex itself, one can read mostly good reviews on the Web. According to vacationers, the menu in this hotel is almost the same as in any other Turkish three. That is, the dishes at the hotel offer residents quite tasty, warm and fresh. But their special variety in the hotel restaurant, unfortunately, is not provided.

Of the meat guests in this hotel, they can take mainly chicken. But there are plenty of fish dishes in the hotel restaurant. Also, tourists can take salads or vegetables, various drinks in the dining room. Sometimes the hotel chef prepares for the guests even a rather tasty likeness of Russian food.





In the hotel bar in the evenings there are a lot of people. The selection of drinks here is quite large. The work of local bartenders, as well as cooks, many hotel guests praise.
