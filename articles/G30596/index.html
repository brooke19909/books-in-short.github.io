The harm and benefits of friction

Not going - just going

Because it is icy

But it falls perfectly!

Why is no one happy?

Such a naive nursery rhyme at first glance - and how much it contains if you look at it from a physical point of view! Indeed, it is in him that the system of a contradictory attitude to the notorious friction force is enclosed. This constant battle, where two concepts compete with each other - harm and the benefits of friction, will never have a winner. After all, what is convenient and profitable for one person, often quite the other way around, is just the opposite - bad, as in this poem.

Remember the story of Nikolai Nosov about the ice slide that the guys built in the yard? And when they all went to dinner, the one who did not participate in the construction came out. He tried to climb onto it, but only hurt himself, but he could not climb. And the kid guessed to sprinkle ice on the sand - it became very convenient to climb to the very top, even on ice!
