The recreation center "Amber". Uvildy Lake in the Southern Urals

It can hardly be argued that the region of the Southern Urals suffers from an excessive influx of tourists. Mass demand is in completely different directions. But the Ural Mountains have the originality and attractiveness of the nature of the middle zone. And the tourism potential of the region is quite large, but at present it is far from fully realized. In the Chelyabinsk region, there are many places of traditional recreation for local residents, such as recreation center "Yantar", Lake Uvildy. But the tourism infrastructure of the region requires investment for further development. Such investments are very promising. Let us take a closer look at what already exists.

The recreation center "Amber", Lake Uvildy

The recreation center is located on the southeastern shore of one of the largest lakes in the entire Urals. The total length of its coastline exceeds one hundred kilometers. And this coastline is very picturesque. It is quite winding, with many bays and quiet backwaters, the hilly coast is covered with dense coniferous and mixed coniferous-deciduous forests.

Similar relic coniferous forests are still preserved in many places of the Urals, despite their predatory destruction over the past two centuries. With the whole complex of its natural components, this lake is favorable for relaxation. Among the many tourist infrastructure facilities existing on its coast, the Yantar recreation center is favorably distinguished. Lake Uvildy in this place has a slightly medieval appearance. Seven buildings of this recreation center give it such a look. They are stylized by architects as ancient castles, decorated with towers and other attributes of Gothic architecture. But this antiquity has a purely decorative character. Here everything is equipped in accordance with modern standards and there is everything necessary for a good rest and treatment. This opportunity provides its visitors recreation center "Amber". Lake Uvildy, among other things, also has sapropelic mud. They traditionally treat a whole group of rheumatic diseases. And not so long ago, several radon sources were discovered near the coast.
When some try to translate into Russian the meaning of the toponym "Uvilda" as "cup of health and longevity", then it is difficult to verify. However, such a translation does not contradict the truth, the lake completely corresponds to such a definition in terms of its recreational and healing properties. And many residents of Chelyabinsk are well aware of this address: recreation center "Yantar", Uvildy. It is 80 kilometers from the city, and they come here to rest and improve their health.
