Greece, Chania: recreation, attractions, hotels

Where to go on vacation this summer? A fairly affordable option for today is Greece. Chania, located on the island of Crete, is considered the greenest region of the country.

Chania - Greek Paradise

The entire coast of Chania is surrounded by a huge number of stunning resorts such as Paleochora and Kournas, Georgioupolis, Agia and Marina, Gerani and Sfakia, Elafonisi, Platanias, as well as Kolymbari. The most popular of them are Platanias and Georgioupolis. These resorts are mysterious and unique, full of romance and adventure.





Convenient geographical location, very developed infrastructure is famous for resort Greece. Chania will shock anyone with the beauty of its landscapes and unusualness, as well as the abundance of architectural monuments of the Turkish and Venetian era. The Mediterranean and Aegean seas are washed by Chania, playing a strong contrast of bright blue waves and green dense vegetation. When you look at the White Mountains with forests of cypress trees, you get the feeling that they have not changed their appearance since the Cretan Minoan civilization, and the sun is still illuminating an even strip of warm sandy beaches, adding a special touch to this place.

sights

Greece is famous for its picturesque places. The island of Crete, Chania is especially popular with tourists. It is worth noting that most of the attractions are located in the so-called Old Town. It represents the historical part of the city with narrow quarters and old houses, small parks, mansions and palaces that previously belonged to wealthy Venetians. A walk through the Old City in terms of beauty and delight from what you see is not inferior to a trip to the museum.

In the bay, the Venetian port was successfully located, in which you can meet an extraordinary number of fishing boats. The port offers stunning sunset views, as well as many taverns and small cozy restaurants. Also in the port is the Turkish Janissary Mosque, which is the oldest Muslim building in Chania and a cultural historical monument.

Frikas Fortress is located near the port, it was there that the flag of Greece was first raised . The Maritime Museum, located in the fortress, can be visited by tourists who are interested in considering various models of ships, ancient maps, marine instruments. The museum has a fairly large collection.

The Church of St. Francis is the largest, its construction dates back to the times of Venetian rule. At the moment, there is an archaeological museum.

The city market, which was founded in 1911, cannot be deprived of attention. Its originality lies in its arrangement in the shape of a cross. Everything can be found on the market - from souvenirs to food products that Greece is rich in.

Chania is also a shopping center. Fans of original and exclusive products just need to visit the "Leather Street". Whatever you meet there. A huge assortment of leather items, from skirts to raincoats, as well as leather bags, wallets, belts and, of course, shoes. Prices in the market are low and affordable for everyone.

A peculiar symbol of the city is a lighthouse located in the harbor of Chania. It is one of the oldest buildings.

Not only beautiful places attract tourists to Greece (Crete, Chania). The hotels here are simply gorgeous. Everyone will be able to choose a suitable option, from budget to luxury. Many old buildings here have been restored and converted into hotels. In most of them there is no registration and booking via the Internet, all this is possible only on the spot.

Fereniki Holiday Resort & SPA - great offer at good prices

This hotel is located in the resort village of Georgioupolis. He has everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Daily entertainment shows for guests, a recreation area for children, a sauna, a very well-groomed area and an indoor heated pool ... Hotel Fereniki 3 * (Crete, Chania, Greece) is rightfully proud of all this.





In total, the hotel can offer its guests 4 pools, all of them are filled with fresh water. Separately for children, 3 sections in the pools are allocated for a safe stay on the water.

Bars and shops are located on the seafront, which shares a beautiful promenade with the hotel. The public transport stop is a five-minute walk from the hotel, this will be relevant for vacationers who wish to visit Chania separately from the excursion program.

Services

The hotel operates on an all-inclusive system, there is one bar located near the pool and a restaurant. But the food at the hotel is not limited to this. There are 3 paid bars and a La carte tavern. High chairs are provided for children at all food outlets on site.

Of the paid services, the hotel is pleased to offer its guests internet access and the use of a safe. For a fee, spa treatments and the organization of various beach sports are also available. Upon request, guests can use an iron at the reception. For those who like to relax by the sea, and not by the pool, the hotel offers a sandy beach, sunbeds and umbrellas are provided, but you will have to pay about 5 euros for one set.

Hydramis palace

The first guests of Hydramis Palace 4 * (Greece, Crete, Chania) were gladly received in 2003. The hotel is located in the resort village of Georgioupolis, on the shores of a pristine beach. In the immediate vicinity are all the famous attractions and monuments. Rooms are 155 rooms, which are located in 10 buildings. The hotel has a lift for guests' convenience.





Hotel rooms are divided according to the size of the rooms and the availability of certain additional services provided.

Housekeeping is daily and bed linen is changed 3 times a week. The hotel has 2 bars and 1 restaurant, which has a conference room for up to 250 people.

The best conditions for children and adults

A separate plus is the availability of various services for children, such as a children's play pool, a playground for games and a mini club for children aged 4-12 years. Children can also visit the theater and have fun with electronic games. Parents can take advantage of babysitting services, but at an additional cost. For people who are keen on sports, aerobics classes are offered, because the hotel has its own fitness center. Bike rental is available. It offers vacationers tennis courts, volleyball and basketball courts, as well as a football field.

The hotel has everything you need. A hairdresser and laundry services are offered, as well as a currency exchange. It is possible to rent a car, there is a private parking.

The hotel has the opportunity to offer its own beach for relaxation, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided absolutely free. On the beach, lovers of outdoor games can engage in beach volleyball. Horseback riding and scuba diving are available upon prior request and at an additional cost.
