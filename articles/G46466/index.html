Positioning

The manufacturer was in no hurry to reduce the cost, so over time, the Samsung GT-E2232 Duos phone gradually gave way to other, more functional devices. The situation was not even saved by the support of two SIM cards, a standard headphone jack and other set of options.

The model is positioned as a phone for calls. There is no point in demanding more from him. Design

The Samsung GT-E2232 Duos mobile phone is a typical device with a keypad. Design maker chose standard. There are no special remarkable elements on the case. Half of the front panel is occupied by the keyboard unit. It consists of soft keys and numeric buttons. At the top there is a glass protecting the display. The speakerphone Samsung GT-E2232 Duos (see photo in the article) is closed with a silver trapezoidal lattice. The microphone is brought to the lower end, and to the upper - a flashlight and an audio jack. The side faces are not informative, only on the right side is the slot for connecting the charger cable. Features of SIM cards

The main "chip" of this phone is the support of two SIM cards. That is what allows him to compete with other devices of the budget segment. The developers used a single radio module of the Duos Lite type. It provides alternate operation of SIM cards. What does this mean? When one card is active, the second is blocked.

The benefits can also be attributed to the existing communication settings in the Samsung GT-E2232 Duos. Reviews about the phone confirm the information, which says that the user independently appoints the main SIM card, which will be activated by default. Slots for them are located under the battery. Each of them has a serial number. It is used when working with the SIM manager. The advantage of this application is the ability to assign any name to the SIM card. According to many users, this solution helps to avoid confusion that occurs with standard names using ordinals - SIM1 and SIM2.

When storing a number in the address book, it is suggested to select the phone or SIM memory. According to the choice, the subscriber will be marked with a special designation.
