Sharm Cliff Resort 4 *, (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): hotel description, reviews

If you plan to spend your vacation in Egypt on the Red Sea and are looking for a comfortable, but inexpensive hotel, then Sharm Cliff Resort can be a very suitable option.





Location

This four-star hotel is located on a hill, a few hundred meters from Naama Bay. The Red Sea is 700 meters away. The nearest airport, located in Sharm El Sheikh, is only 20 kilometers away.

Sharm Cliff Resort 4 *: description of the hotel, photo

The four-star Sharm Cliff Resort was built in 2004. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãits own territory is 14 thousand square meters. meters.

The hotel has 223 rooms. They are located in two- and three-story snow-white and decorated in the national style buildings. There are apartments of the following categories: "standard", "family" (two bedrooms) and "general suite" (living room and bedroom). All rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay: comfortable furniture, a TV with satellite channels (including those with Russian broadcasting), air conditioning, a minibar, a telephone, a safe, a hairdryer, a bathroom and a furnished terrace or balcony with a beautiful view.

The food system at the Sharm Cliff Resort 4 * hotel is all-inclusive. During the day, guests can eat in the main restaurant, working in the buffet format, as well as in the snack bar by the pool.

The hotel offers a 24-hour front desk, parking, free internet in the lobby, laundry service, currency exchange, several shops and much more.

The hotel has two swimming pools (one of them with water heating), a hookah room, a sun terrace, a tennis court, a volleyball court, and a children's playground. If necessary, guests can use the services of a doctor (for an additional fee).

Housing stock

Our compatriots, according to them, were quite satisfied with the rooms offered to them at Sharm Cliff Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt). Check in, departure

In accordance with the internal rules of Sharm Cliff Resort 4 * (Naama Bay), the settlement of new arrivals is carried out after two in the afternoon. However, if you arrive at the hotel in the morning, you can ask to be accommodated as soon as possible by offering the administrator at the reception a couple of tens of dollars. In most cases, your request will be granted. As for the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. If your flight is planned for the evening, then for an additional fee you can extend your stay at the hotel. In addition, you can hand over the keys to the apartments, leave your things in the luggage storage room and continue to relax, using the entire infrastructure of Sharm Cliff Resort.

Sharm Cliff Resort 4 *: beach vacation

This item, as a rule, is one of the most important for tourists planning to spend their holidays on the seashore. After all, most people come to Egypt, counting on a magnificent beach holiday. Hotel "Sharm Cliff Resort", according to travelers, in this regard will satisfy all guests. Despite being not on the first coastline, tourists living in it have the opportunity to use two beaches at once. One of them is located in the Naami Bay area and is called Wind Beach. You can reach it literally in 10 minutes with an unhurried step. This beach is small, sandy, with a gentle entry into the water. The local bars play nice music. However, there are very few fish and corals near the coast. If you want to snorkel and watch the life of the amazing inhabitants of the Red Sea, then you should go to another beach at the disposal of the Sharm Cliff Resort Hotel. It is called Hadaba Beach and is located in the bay of Ras Um Sid. Experienced travelers who have repeatedly visited Egypt, Sharm Cliff Resort 4 * is also appreciated for the fact that the Hadaba Beach at its disposal for the wealth of underwater life is one of the best not only in Sharm El Sheikh, but also throughout the country. The beach here is very large, clean, sandy. Entering shallow water. It is best to swim in special shoes, since the sand is at the bottom at the bottom, but then corals begin, which can hurt your legs. Tourists resting here are sure to advise, going on vacation, to bring a mask for snorkeling and flippers. After all, here you can spend hours swimming over a coral reef, exploring its most interesting inhabitants - colorful fish of different sizes and shapes, octopuses, stingrays, moray eels, crabs, etc.

You can get to both Wind Beach and Hadaba Beach using the shuttle service provided by the hotel. So, a bus runs all day between the hotel and two beaches with an interval of half an hour.
