The best time to relax, weather

Sri Lanka has two main seasons - rainy and dry. So, almost 95% of the total annual rainfall falls in the months from May to October. However, if you decide to go to a place like Sri Lanka in July, you will not be disappointed either. So, the air temperature during the day at this time is about 28 degrees Celsius. Precipitation falls mainly at night. Therefore, those who come to this country in the summer season, as a rule, do not complain at all. After all, during the holidays they, as a rule, do not have to adjust their plans very often because of the rain. In addition, during the day they are mostly strong, but short-lived. So Sri Lanka in July will definitely not disappoint you. Therefore, if your vacation falls on the summer, do not hesitate to go to this wonderful country. The weather in Sri Lanka is also warm in other months. But in winter there is practically no rainfall. The air temperature is about 28-30 degrees, as well as in the summer season. The only exceptions are mountainous areas. Here, the air temperature can drop even up to +10 degrees.
