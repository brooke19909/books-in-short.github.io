Popular Four Star And Five Star Hotels Sardinia

Hotel Abi D'Oru 5 * (Abi De Oru)

Abi De Oru is a five-star hotel considered one of the best in Sardinia. It is located right on the ocean in a picturesque bay. The location of Abi De Oru directly on the coast makes it, like many other hotels in Sardinia, very attractive for relaxation. Porto Cervo, a major social life center, is 5 km from the hotel. The nearest airport to the hotel is 15 km. The exterior and interior of the hotel are made in a Mediterranean style, combining with individual elements of tipico sardo style. The hotel has 175 rooms. Each room has a telephone, a safe, a minibar, and a hairdryer. Depending on the type of room and its location, they have balconies, terraces or patios. The services provided in Abi D Oru include 24-hour room service, buffet breakfast, water sports, tennis, golf, etc. The hotel has three restaurants, two bars, a buffet, an adult and a children's pool, a mini club , tennis courts, boutique, beauty salon and parking. Near the hotel is a well-equipped golf course. The only drawback is the expensive cost of living, from $ 300 per day.

Cala di Volpe 5 * (Cala di Volpe)

Hotel Cala di Volpe is located in a picturesque bay on the Costa Smeralda. Not far from it is a small but quite famous town - Porto Cervo. The nearest airport to the hotel is 30 km in the city of Olbia. The architectural complex of Cala Di Volpe consists of individual mansions. The project was designed by renowned architect Jacques Quell. Due to its magnificent design, the hotel is a masterpiece of modern architecture. In 2005, Cala Di Volpe entered the list of the best hotels in the world Conde Nast Traveler. The hotel has 123 rooms. Of these, 8 are single, 99 double rooms, 13 category rooms, 1 room of the Presidential Suite category, 13 rooms of the junior suite category. The rooms are decorated to a high standard and feature light and dark wood furniture made in the Italian classical style. Considering all the reviews about hotels in Sardinia, Cala Di Volpe is rightfully one of the best. If you do not take into account the expensive price of the hotel, the service here is at its best.

CHIA LAGUNA Resort 5 *

The hotel complex is located in the southwestern part of Sardinia in the small village of Kia. 50 km from the hotel there is the capital of the island of Cagliari - Elmas, there is also the nearest airport. The complex consists of four hotels and several luxury turn-key mansions. Chia Laguna Resort is located in a natural park on a hill. This hill offers stunning panoramic views of the bay and beaches. Like many other hotels in Sardinia, Chia Laguna is located near the coast, which is its significant advantage. In all hotels of the complex there are, in total, more than 500 rooms. The furniture and other items in the rooms are made in the traditional Italian style. The complex has several restaurants and bars, swimming pools. The only negative is quite noisy during the day.

Hotel Villa Del Parco 5 * (Villa Del Parko)

The hotel, which is part of a major tourist complex Forte Village Resor. Located near the sea beach. The main advantage of this tourist complex is the huge number of services provided. Among them are evening shows, night bars, restaurants, concerts with famous artists, boutiques, catamarans, canoes, a pool, a jacuzzi, mud baths, massage, hydromassage, a hairdresser, horse and underwater sports. Spent at this hotel, a vacation in Sardinia will allow you to master many interesting and exciting activities. A wide selection of services for children - tennis, football, surfing, a children's restaurant, etc. In total, the hotel itself has 25 rooms, in the complex along the hotel itself there are 18 magnificent turnkey villas. The suites have balconies overlooking the hotel‚Äôs beautiful tropical garden, separate bedroom, living room and spacious terrace. All rooms have a private bathroom with shower, air conditioning, telephone, minibar and safe.

Flamingo 4 * (hotel Flamingo)

Located 34 km from the city of Cagliari on the beach. Four kilometers from the hotel is the small town of Pula. The hotel has 134 rooms, with balconies, air conditioning, bathrooms, minibars and televisions. The rooms are serviced 24 hours a day. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, shower, balconies with sea or park views. Among the services provided at this hotel include water sports (windsurfing, water skiing, pedal boats), tennis (regular and table), golf, minigolf, horse riding, a free pool and others. A mini club is provided for children. There is a golf course 4 kilometers from the hotel, and an arena 1 km away. The only negative is far from the airport and the city center.

Abamar 4 * (hotel Abamar)

Located 40 km from the city of Cagliari. The main advantage of this hotel is that it is located on one of the most beautiful beaches of Sardinia. The hotel is a 5-storey building and accommodates 80 rooms. The hotel rooms are equipped with air conditioning, telephone, minibar, TV, bathroom or shower, balcony with sea or beautiful pine grove view. Like other hotels in Sardinia, Hotel Abamar provides various additional services. The hotel infrastructure includes a large swimming pool with a 3-meter-high springboard, a children's pool, a beach bar and restaurant, a TV room, a tennis court and boat rental. A special playroom is provided for children. The only minus of the hotel is far from the airport and city center.

Baia Delle Ginestre 4 *

The hotel is a residence in the south-west of Sardinia in the city of Teulada. The appearance and the surrounding nature makes a very pleasant impression. Surrounded by greenery, the hotel descends directly to the seashore. Of the four-star hotels, Baia Delle Ginestre is one of the best. The hotel has 100 standard rooms, with bathrooms and showers, TV, air conditioning, good furniture, telephone and minibar. The hotel infrastructure includes two restaurants, two bars, a pizzeria boutique, a beauty salon, a congress hall and convenient parking. The hotel has a large freshwater pool as well as a well-equipped pebble beach. Another advantage of the hotel is the scuba diving center and diving school at this center. Equipment rental and classes with scuba diving instructors are provided. It is a real diving paradise.

Hotel Carlos V 4 *

The hotel is located on the seafront, 15 km from the nearest airport, located in the city of Alghero. The hotel has 110 rooms with telephones, minibars, air conditioning and satellite TV. The hotel also has a restaurant, two bars, a large beautiful garden, two swimming pools and two tennis courts. You can call at the hotel, both full board and half board. The downside is the relatively small number of services provided. However, such hotels in Sardinia are great for congresses or important business meetings.
