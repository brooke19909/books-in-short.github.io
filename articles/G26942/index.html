Rooms

At the same time can accommodate and accommodate 845 people sanatorium hotel "Coral" (Adler). Reviews about rooms and service, as often happens, are ambiguous. There are people who liked absolutely everything. But there are those tourists who expressed a number of complaints. For the most part, this concerns the work of staff (maids) who did not clean the rooms as expected. This disadvantage can be attributed to the human factor. On the floors where conscientious people worked, tourists had no comments on the service.

The Coral building is high-rise, so four elevators work here. Rooms for accommodation are provided in different categories: standard (I category), luxury, studios and apartments. All of them are equipped with amenities including a toilet, washbasin, shower or bath. The apartment has a bathtub and shower with a tray. Each room has a balcony with a selection of plastic furniture.

Standard rooms are designed for 2 people and are furnished with furniture, including beds (two single or one double), wardrobe, table, chairs, bedside tables, a large mirror. From electrical appliances there is a TV, a small refrigerator, air conditioning, hair dryer, flashlight, telephone.

The modern design and cozy appearance distinguishes the deluxe rooms available at the Coral Sanatorium (Adler). Photos perfectly demonstrate how these rooms look.

They have two rooms - a bedroom and a living room with upholstered furniture, carpeted floors, beautiful curtains with lambrequins, new plumbing in the hygiene room.

Studio rooms are not inferior. Here, among other amenities, has its own small kitchenette.

And of course, the most luxurious rooms are apartments. They have 3 rooms, 2 bathrooms, a kitchen. The bedrooms and the living room are furnished with Italian furniture, the design is done in soft pastel colors, from the balcony and the windows offer beautiful views.

All rooms should be cleaned daily. Bed sheets and towels are changing according to the schedule. Toiletries are provided only on the day of arrival. Upon departure, the room must be handed over to the maid for signature.

Beach, rest outside the sanatorium

Everyone who comes to the sea is primarily interested in the beach. ‚ÄúCoral‚Äù (Adler) is just a stone's throw from the coast, which you have to go through the tunnels laid under the railway tracks. Some vacationers also cross the railway, noting this as a lack of a sanatorium.

The "Coral" has two beaches - with sand and pebbles. It is always crowded here. The second beach is freer, but the pebbles there are large, and entering the sea is more difficult. Adler's climate is somewhat peculiar, due to geographical features: on the one hand the sea, and on the other - mountains. In winter, it is a couple of degrees warmer than in other areas of the coast, and in the summer it is somewhat cooler.
