How to knit a men's scarf

A long man‚Äôs scarf tied with a woman‚Äôs hands will warm the body and soul of a loved one. To give a product created by your own hands, you need to master simple tricks for working with knitting needles or crochet.

The first example will describe how to knit a scarf. For a classic scarf with a length of 155 cm and a width of 23 cm, 150 grams of wool yarn will be needed. If desired, the edges can be decorated with brushes.





English gum is the most suitable technique for doing this job. To prevent the edge of the product from being pulled when a set of loops, the thread is taken twice.

It is necessary to dial an odd number of loops, for a given width - 95. One spoke is released. The edge loop from the dialed is removed on it.

Then one loop of the front is knitted. We put the thread before work, the next loop is removed with a crochet.

Further movements are repeated - one facial, the next removed in the same way. The pattern does not change until the end of the row. Edge is tied to the wrong side.





Work turns upside down. Edge, both in the first row, and in all subsequent ones, they are removed without knitting. Behind her will go the wrong loop, which is removed with a crochet.

We knit the next one behind the front wall.

The series is being worked through to the end. The last loop - as in the front row.

All rows are performed equally. You need to finish work freely without pulling. You can close the loop with a needle.

There are many other techniques to knit a men's scarf. But this one is the simplest one available to beginners. Knitting looks great and does not take much time.

Simply crocheting a scarf is simpler. Firstly, for those who have never knitted, it is difficult to hold long knitting needles in their hands, it seems that the loops are about to slip off and the work will crumble. Experienced craftswomen know that this sometimes happens, but it is not difficult to assemble everything into place, having opened one row. There are no such problems when crocheting.

The choice remains with the needlewoman, which technique to use and how to knit. Crocheted scarf looks different. Using a hook, it is better to knit women's lace scarves, as well as products from Dantela threads. Men's scarf requires a more strictly style.

But for those who have opted for a crochet hook, brief recommendations are given below on how to crochet a men's scarf. Depending on the length, 150-200 air loops are typed. The chain length is equal to the selected scarf length. The size of the hook should match the thickness of the thread.





There are many crochet techniques, but for a beginner, in order to complete the work, it is enough to master one of the simplest ones, for example, a single crochet.

After the chain of the desired length is dialed, the working thread is hooked into the second loop. Thus, two loops are obtained. The working thread is picked up and passed through them. The same movements are repeated throughout the row.

To knit the next row, it is necessary, before turning the knitting, to make one additional air loop. All actions are repeated until the required width is connected. At the end, the thread is tightened, the main thing is that the work is not too tight
