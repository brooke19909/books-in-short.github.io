Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa 5 * (Greece, Crete): hotel description, services, reviews

Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa is a unique 5-star hotel complex. He is rightfully considered one of the best in all of Crete. And there are many reasons for this. It should be told about what this hotel is. This will help to understand why it is so incredibly popular with travelers.





Briefly about the hotel

Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa consists of numerous individual houses built of white stone. There are apartments in which guests are accommodated. But, in addition to the houses, there is also a large main building, next to which there is a huge outdoor saltwater pool and restaurants.

It is worth noting that the roofs of all buildings are made of tiles, which is a durable, environmentally friendly and fireproof material. Interestingly, it was made by special order of the Mitsis Group. It is also interesting that during the construction of the complex used a large amount of wood.

Thanks to the combination of gold and white in the design, it feels like it's not a hotel, but some kind of architectural landmark or even a palace. But even more interesting is the fact that the frames of mirrors, lamps and many other decorative elements are really covered with gold. Not jewelry, true, but leaf.

It should be noted that Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa was nominated as the best five-star hotel with an all-inclusive system for families.

By the way, a few words should be said about the place where the complex is located. This is the village of Anissaras, located next to Hersonissos in a hilly area. Here, tourists can enjoy the cleanest sandy beaches and warm, gentle sea. An ideal place for a quiet, secluded vacation. And if you want to immerse yourself in the vibrant nightlife of Greece, you can go to Heraklion, the capital of Crete. This city is 28 kilometers from the hotel. And the airport, respectively, in the same place.

The 5-star Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa is not without reason considered one of the best in all of Crete. It has everything. One of the main highlights of the hotel is its huge conference room with a capacity of 500 people. restaurants and bars

In Mitsis Laguna Resort and Spa 5 * there are several luxurious and presentable establishments where you can taste delicious dishes. To be more precise, there are eight restaurants, one of which is the main one. In the rest you can try dishes of Italian, Asian, Cretan, Mexican, Japanese, Greek cuisine. And also on the territory of Mitsis Laguna Resort and Spa 5 * there are several bars. One of them - the lobby - is open from 10am to 11pm. There is also a beach and pool bar. For lunch, guests are invited from 12:30 to 15:00, and for dinner from 19:00 to 21:30. But that is not all. There is a late dinner. It starts at 22:30, and ends at half past twelve.

SPA services

As mentioned earlier, the hotel has a spa. This is also indicated by the explicit prefix "spa" in the name of the complex. So, they really offer unique services here. For example, Elemis therapy. She is known all over the world. ELEMIS is a world leader in cosmetics and spa. In addition, he is the only British company that clinically tests the effectiveness of their therapies. Efficiency is noticeable even after the first procedure.

In addition, Thalgo-thalassotherapy is also offered. She should also take her time. Everyone knows that the Thalgo research center collaborates with renowned microbiologists and algologists (specialists studying the healing properties of algae), and is also a leader in the field of thalassotherapy.

Even in the SPA-center of the hotel you can swim in indoor pools with sea water (one of them is heated), devote time to face and body care, go for shiatsu (Japanese acupressure). You can also visit the hammam, pedicure, manicure, fitness room. Or enjoy a regular massage.

Sport

Hotel Mitsis Laguna Resort & Spa offers its guests a variety of services. Here, everyone will find something to their liking. Including lovers of an active lifestyle.

As already mentioned, the hotel has a fitness center. The hall is equipped with everything necessary for full-fledged training - bicycle and weight training equipment, treadmills, dumbbells, barbells. Aerobics and tennis are also available. True, there is only one court in the complex.

There is a field with artificial grass for football and futsal. You can also practice beach volleyball, play darts or billiards. There are also pedal boats, slides and canoes.

By the way, even a lounge for the bridge in the complex is available. As many knowledgeable people know, this card game was recognized as a sport by the International Olympic Committee.

You can also do water polo or rent a bike. For an additional fee, guests will have the opportunity to ride ponies and horses. Motor water sports, by the way, are also offered.
