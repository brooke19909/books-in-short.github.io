What does thermal water consist of?

The thermal waters of the resort include iodine, salt, bromine, titanium, silver, tin, barium and many other elements that, in reaction with others, help people suffering from various diseases. Being in the water, the body becomes lighter, thereby reducing the load on the joints, tissue metabolism improves, and with it the blood circulation.

Mineral water is also used in the treatment of asthma by regular inhalation. There is also a drinking course, which is prescribed for diseases associated with the gastrointestinal tract.

Indications for treatment

Like any world resort, Hajduszoboszlo waters have their indications for use and contraindications.

Consider the positive effect for the body. Thermal springs will be useful if a person suffers:

  • chronic inflammation of the joints;
  • muscle aches;
  • nervous disorders and chronic inflammation of the nerves;
  • vasoconstriction
  • chronic diseases of the spinal column;
  • skin and gynecological diseases.

Thermal water treatment is indicated for people undergoing rehabilitation after sports injuries and suffering from paralysis of the limbs. The direction here is also written out for the healing of severe fractures.

Contraindications

There are actually not so many contraindications. It is not worth using the services of the resort for people with acute inflammation, the presence of a malignant tumor of any stage, tuberculosis, high blood pressure, diseases of the central nervous system, as well as pregnant women.
