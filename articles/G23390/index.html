Five-star Sunset Beach Hotel (Turkey, Alanya): description of services provided

It's great to relax on the shores of the hot Mediterranean Sea, where you don’t need to think about busy weekdays and work, enjoying the picturesque nature, incredibly beautiful beaches and warm water like fresh milk ! Give yourself a fabulous trip - go to the Sunset Beach Hotel (Turkey), it is here that you can spend unforgettable vacations and enjoy the high level of comfort. Near the resting place are a mosque, a Byzantine fortress, Damlatas cave and many other interesting objects.





This complex is very popular, it is visited annually by thousands of tourists from around the world. It has a good location - in the resort village with the interesting name Kestel. This cozy place is located 5 km from the center of Alanya and 125 km from the airport. The hotel has a fairly spacious area of ​​25,000 m2, on which tropical plants are planted.

The Sunset Beach Hotel (Turkey) has been renovated more than once and now it again appears before its guests in a chic guise. The complex surprises with a homely atmosphere, hospitality and cleanliness. Attentive staff are very friendly, try to please in everything. A shuttle service to the station or airport can be arranged upon request. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive concept and offers a wide selection of entertainment services, including for babies.





The hotel can be recommended to people who prefer a secluded, calm and measured rest. Couples with children will be delighted with the rich infrastructure and proximity of the beach line. For an acceptable price, you will gain vivid impressions, get a beautiful tan, put in order the nervous system and recharge with positive emotions.

Number of rooms

The Sunset Beach Hotel (Turkey) consists of a 4-storey building, which contains more than two hundred rooms of different classrooms. There are rooms with sea or garden views. All rooms are well-maintained, with air conditioning and a spacious bathroom.





On the upper floors there are comfortable furnished balconies. To services of tourists - a telephone line and a bar with drinks (filling is paid). There is a TV with Russian channels and a safe (surcharge). On certain days (at least three times a week) cleaning is carried out.

Nutrition

Sunset Beach Hotel (Turkey) receives only positive reviews about the food. Tourists are satisfied with the system and quality of service, a varied menu and the freshness of the dishes. On the tables there are always meat, fish delicacies, seafood, fish and drinks. Every day tourists are offered a rich assortment of fresh salads. Professional chefs make wonderful compositions from berries and fruits that attract children so much.





The dessert menu is distinguished by an exquisite selection of oriental sweets, pastries and ice cream. For kids, local milk is served and some dishes are less fatty. From drinks freshly squeezed juices, tea, coffee and alcoholic beverages are presented. Restaurants and bars are available for guests.

Beach

The Sunset Beach Hotel (Turkey) is located just 50 meters from the beach line. The coast is well-groomed, sand and gravel, with a pier. The beach is equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas, a children's trampoline and a sandbox with a canopy. Mattresses, catamarans and motorbikes are provided. Towels are issued once per stay. Additional kits can only be taken on bail.

Additional Information





Active tourists will find tennis courts, futsal, sports fields and a fitness center with new modern equipment. For passive relaxation, 5 outdoor pools with water slides were built. Near the pools there are bars with soft drinks and cocktails.

For children, play areas are equipped right in the middle of the green garden, with swings and carousels. There is a club for children of different ages. For very young guests strollers are offered (for a fee). All objects are evenly dispersed throughout the territory.





In the evening, animated performances are organized with magicians, contests, dancers and bingo. Live music is constantly playing, and after midnight a disco opens. If you wish, you can visit the night club "Auditorium", located near the hotel. You don’t have to be bored during the day either, billiards, aerobics, bocca, and darts are at your service. There is a wellness center with various procedures, a Turkish bath, a beauty salon and a sauna.

Quality services, comfortable accommodation and a lot of entertainment are offered to tourists by Turkey (Alanya). Sunset Beach Hotel is the place to get everything at a reasonable price.
