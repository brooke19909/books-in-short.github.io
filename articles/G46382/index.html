From Rostov to Mineralnye Vody: how to relax with health benefits

Mineralnye Vody is a small, cozy city in the Stavropol Territory that serves as a gateway for hundreds of thousands of tourists to enter the most popular medical resorts. According to the name of the city, we can conclude that it is here that healing sources are concentrated. But this is not so - all sources of mineral waters are located in nearby cities: Lermontov, Pyatigorsk, Zheleznovodsk, Kislovodsk and Essentuki.

Mineralnye Vody is one of the best places for relaxation and rest. It has everything: mountains, forest, clean air, warm climate, as well as many places where you can recover using healing water from mineral springs.

Treatment in Mineralnye Vody

For a long time, Caucasian Mineral Waters have been considered a health resort in southern Russia. Since the days of the USSR, thousands of people from different parts of the country gathered here to improve their health. Thanks to the healing climate, healing mud, mineral baths, the body is cleansed of toxins and toxins, it is quickly restored after injuries and diseases.

In the vicinity of Mineralnye Vody, there are several health resorts and sanatoriums that treat diseases such as gastritis, ulcers, obesity, diabetes, kidney and liver diseases. Different methods can be used in the treatment - this is drinking water from healing sources, and mud therapy, and therapeutic baths with mineral water.

sights

Of course, Mineralnye Vody is a great place for healing. But besides visiting sanatoriums, there are many other options for hanging out.

Walking around the city and its environs, you can see many natural and architectural attractions. One of the most interesting natural attractions is Mount Zmeyka. Here you can observe incredibly beautiful mountain landscapes, explore the unique flora and fauna, drink water from a holy spring.

Those wishing to study the culture, history and architecture of the city will be interested in walking through the local cathedrals and temples. The main religious attraction of Mineralnye Vody is the Cathedral of the Protection of the Holy Virgin.

To Mineralnye Vody from Rostov

Mineralnye Vody is a city with the largest transport hub in the region. Here is an international airport and a major interchange of railways. Also, a federal highway passes through Mineralnye Vody. All arrival stations in the city are in relative proximity to each other, so there is no problem with transport accessibility - Mineralnye Vody is easily accessible from anywhere in Russia.

Most often, people from neighboring regions come to Mineralnye Vody. For example, from Rostov-on-Don. Direction Rostov - Mineralnye Vody runs many routes. You can get there by bus or by train, but the fastest way to overcome the path is by plane.
