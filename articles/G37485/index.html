Children's camp "Dawn" in Anapa: photos, reviews

The tourist infrastructure of the city of Anapa (Krasnodar Territory) is getting better and better every year, thereby attracting many tourists. The main reason for choosing this resort for the rest of Russians is, of course, the coast of their native Black Sea. Everything here has a pleasant and carefree holiday. For decades, both adults and children have been resting, tempering and treating almost all diseases in Anapa.





Anapa is considered the sunniest resort in the entire Russian Federation. There are several springs with mineral waters and healing mud, which allows you to successfully develop spa treatment. Dozens of children's health camps and sanatoriums in Anapa annually host more than 600 thousand people.

In this article we will dwell in more detail on the children's health camp "Zorka". Photos, descriptions, prices and services of which you will see below.

Camp information

The main task of the Zorka camp is to effectively heal children, make their immunity stronger, and the body hardened and strong.

Since the camp carries out its work on the territory of the sanatorium, there is a base thanks to which the treatment of children is provided. In the summer months, special attention is paid to climate treatment. Ecology and nature of the region contribute to relaxation, peace, stress and stress relief. Resting and having fun, children become stronger and healthier.





Coordinates

Camp "Zorka" is located in the Krasnodar Territory, in the city of Anapa, on Pionersky Prospekt, in building number 40.

How to get there:

  • From the train station by minibus (number 100).
  • From the bus station to the stop "Black Sea Dawn" (route number 114 and 128).
  • From the airport take taxi number 113.

The organization has its own website on the Internet, where you can see all the information you are interested in: prices, a detailed description, phone numbers for communication.

Accommodation and rooms

Camp "Dawn" (Anapa) annually takes a huge number of children. The entire territory of the sanatorium is guarded throughout the day. The guys live in two-, three-story buildings with major repairs, in rooms designed for three to four people. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe rooms is up to twenty square meters. All rooms decorated in blue tones have new comfortable furniture (beds, tables, chairs, wardrobe, nightstands), balconies overlooking the picturesque park. Facilities (shower, toilet) are located on the floors. On each floor there are coolers with boiling water and cold water.





In the halls there are relaxation areas with a TV and upholstered furniture. Throughout the day with experienced children there are teachers and educators who are at any moment ready to come to their aid if necessary.

The camp has its own boiler room, which provides buildings with heating and uninterrupted water supply. Electricity is supplied from its own diesel substation.

Dining room dining

Camp "Zorka" provides five meals a day, which, in addition to breakfast, lunch and dinner, includes an afternoon snack and a dream book. Children eat in bright spacious halls, there are only two of them, each is designed for two hundred people. Service is provided by waitresses. In addition to vitamin and healthy dishes, the menu always has fermented milk products, natural juices, fruits, and delicious desserts.





Meals are provided in accordance with a 10-day menu approved by Rospotrebnadzor.

The territory of the children's camp

Throughout the day, children take part in various events, sports competitions, engage in sections and circles. On the territory of the pioneer camp "Zorka" there is a stationary stadium that can seat up to four hundred spectators. There is a volleyball court, tables for playing tennis, treadmills, a library, a dance floor, gazebos, a modern octopus playground. Every child with any temperament will find something to his liking in the camp.





Boarding house beach

What can the Zorka Camp offer? Black Sea, sandy beach, fresh and clean air. From dormitory buildings to the coast can be reached in five minutes. Throughout the picturesque beach, famous for its healing fine-grained quartz sand, there are shadow canopies and comfortable sunbeds, there are several changing rooms, a rescue post, and a medical center. Hollow shore goes into the sea and creates favorable conditions for children's water procedures. The average sea temperature from May to September (swimming season) is kept at + 20-25 degrees Celsius.





In the immediate vicinity of Zorka, there is a pier, a new water park, a penguinarium, a dolphinarium and an aquarium.

Creative development

Each shift in the camp "Dawn" provides for the creative development of children. It hosts sports competitions, contests and matches, there is everything necessary for professional sports activities.

Creative, environmental, business, outdoor, role-playing and intellectual games are held daily with children. All events are attended by absolutely all children.

There are several circles in the camp:

  • Partisan school.
  • Circle "Young fashionistas."
  • "Crazy hands".
  • Vocal lessons.
  • Acting Studio.
  • Dance studio (DnB-step, hip-hop, Jumpstyle).




Exciting excursion routes are offered. Several are included in the price. The first route is a sightseeing tour of the sights of Anapa. The second tour is a trip to the zoo called "Animal World". In addition, you can organize additional excursions (for a fee).

Wellness treatments

The medical building of the camp offers the prevention and treatment of the following diseases:

  • Allergic diseases of the skin and upper respiratory tract.
  • Digestive system diseases.
  • Diseases of the cardiovascular system.
  • Chronic respiratory diseases.
  • Problems with the musculoskeletal system.
  • Diseases of the nervous system.

The treatment is with mineral waters, healing mud and herbs.

Camp shifts

  1. From June 3 to 23, the Black Sea Dawn (children's camp) welcomes children who are in a difficult life situation, as well as the winners of the main competitions.
  2. From June 23 to July 13 a creative shift (artistic orientation) is working.
  3. From July 13 to August 2 (orphans and pupils of state institutions of Komi).
  4. The next shift is from August 2 to 22 (different categories of children).
  5. The last shift is from August 22 to September 11.

Each shift has a duration of 21 calendar days.

Children are accepted from the age of six (until adulthood).

Tour Prices

- For children who are left without parents and are brought up in institutions of Komi, financing is provided from the budget of the republic. The full cost of both travel and vouchers is paid.

- For orphans raised by guardians, the ticket is paid in full, travel - 90 percent.

- For gifted children (prize-winners and winners of olympiads and competitions) the permit is free, travel is at their own expense.

- For other categories of citizens, the average cost of expenses (937 rubles / day) is reimbursed from the budget of the republic.

- The price for a regular trip to the camp (without refund) is 26,628 rubles per shift.

The price includes:

- accommodation in rooms;

- five meals a day;

- use of sports equipment and playgrounds;

- use of the beach;

- treatment;

- cultural program of the camp;

- Two fascinating excursions.

Reviews of children and their parents

A trip to a children's camp is a real gift for a teenager. Every child is a fan of adventure, and the Zorka camp is not just a wellness vacation, but also a rich entertainment program full of games, creativity and, of course, the sea. Here every day is a holiday with interesting contests, prizes and a disco. And the warm sea and the golden beach are the pride of the camp.

Reviews about the "Black Sea Dawn" are mostly positive, as professional educators, teachers and doctors take care of the children here. The whole camp is located in a huge pine forest, there is a large and beautiful avenue strewn with roses, incredibly clean air around. According to many children, camp counselors are very good, funny, kind. In the evenings, they hold cool discos. Many guys leave the camp with tears, so they do not want to part with new friends and favorite mentors.





Cons of the camp, according to his guests, are not very clean toilets and monotonous food in the dining room. Vacationers at the end of the season usually do not like the water in the sea, as it begins to bloom and becomes cloudy.

"Black Sea Dawn" - a camp for a perfect holiday. The proximity of the sea, healing air, silence and solitude with nature will restore and strengthen the children's body. The entertainment program will provide an opportunity to comprehensively and harmoniously develop, gain new knowledge and skills.

Every day in the Zorka health camp is a holiday and a real adventure!
