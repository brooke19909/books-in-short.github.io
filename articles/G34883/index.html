Bin Majid Beach Hotel (Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates): photos and reviews of tourists

The United Arab Emirates is an amazing country. A few decades ago, there was nothing remarkable in its place, and today in the middle of the desert, in the literal sense of the word, the magnificent beauty of the city - the emirates, rise. Tourists come here to see the wonders of technology and modern architecture. In this country, where citizens themselves wear traditional Arabic clothing, a tourist can feel free. There is a lot of entertainment for children, and for youth, and for adults. There are such emirates in which life is very expensive, and there are cities where you can meet quite affordable prices. In some of the Arab Emirates, morals are more than strict, and in others - although there is no debauchery, it is quite possible to feel free and relaxed. In a word, a vacation in the UAE will give you a lot of positive emotions and as many vivid impressions that you will remember for many long and many years to come.



Features of rest in the UAE

Surely everyone knows what an oasis is. It is a green and blooming paradise in the middle of the endless sands of the sultry desert. Naturally, this place was chosen for the construction of cities. In addition, most emirates are washed by the waters of either the Persian Gulf, or the Red Sea, or the Atlantic Ocean. Naturally, tourists come here not only to look at the beauty and originality of these young giant cities, but also to relax in the gentle sun, swim in warm water, when there, far away in their homeland, a blizzard rages in full, and lowers the thermometer column almost to the limit. In a word, many of our tourists choose holidays in the UAE for winter holidays. Tours during this period, of course, are relatively inexpensive. Of course, we are not talking about the period between Catholic and Orthodox Christmas. At this time, there is nowhere for the apple to fall, and prices are inflated to incredible heights. But the rest of the time you can visit this amazing country without ruin for your own bank account. And if you choose a hotel not of a luxury class, but an economy, for example, the Bin Majid Beach Hotel, then you can save a lot of money.

Emirate of Ras Al Khaimah

As you probably know, the UAE is a union of seven emirates: Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Ajman, Umm al-Quwain, Ras al-Khaimah, Sharjah, Fujairah. Each of them is a tourist center. Some more than others, some - a little less. The aforementioned Beach Hotel By Bin Majid, which we will discuss in more detail in this article, is located in Ras Al Khaimah. It is the northernmost of the emirates, located about one hour from magnificent Dubai. It is very picturesque here: in a bizarre form of mountains in places they almost approach the azure sea with snow-white beaches. The capital of this emirate, which is part of the UAE, is Ras al-Khaimah (the city of the same name). It is divided by a bay into two parts, which are connected to each other by a bridge. The eastern part, where the international airport is located, is called Al-Hamranya. The western part - the so-called Old Ras al-Khaimah - is rich in historical monuments. In this emirate, the dry law is completely absent, therefore it should attract our tourists more than the rest - lovers of relaxation with a glass of drinks of various strengths. However, it, unlike other resorts, is less appreciated by tour operators. Nevertheless, there is everything conducive to a wonderful holiday. Wonderful nature, mild climate, magnificent beaches. Some perceive it as a gate to enter Oman. However, there is a category of tourists who come here specifically to relax. On the coast of the Persian Gulf there are pretty decent hotels. Ras al-Khaimah is also famous for the relatively low prices for all services and accommodation. The only minus, perhaps, is that there is no bus service from this emirate to others, however, taxi services can be used. The way to Dubai, for example, will cost 25 dirhams, and inside the city - very inexpensively, about 10. To the delight of holidaymakers, at the Beach Hotel By Bin Majid, directly opposite the entrance, there is a taxi rank. Another advantage of Ras al-Khaimah is the abundance of shopping centers in which you can find all kinds of goods at fairly low prices.

General Description of Bin Majid Beach Hotel and Location

The hotel is located in close proximity to the center of the emirate of Ras al-Khaimah, that is, in the southeastern part of the Arabian Peninsula, washed by the waters of the Persian Gulf. In order to get to this hotel, tourists have to fly to Dubai airport, and then from there go by taxi to the hotel. As you remember, there is simply no other transport connection with this emirate. There are two more options: car rental in advance or transfer included in the price of the ticket.

This hotel was built in 1987. That is why many of the tourists staying in it, later write in reviews of decay and shabby. However, this, of course, is said too loudly, just a lot has not changed here for several decades. And the hotel was renovated in 2005. ‚ÄúBin Majit Beach‚Äù is part of the Bin Majid network. The hotel building is a five-story building, which is surrounded on all sides by a lush garden. There are also bungalows on site. The biggest advantage of this hotel is that it is located in close proximity to the seashore. He has his own beach, two kilometers long. By the way, very close to the Bin Majid Beach Hotel there is a wonderful water park.



Number of rooms

This hotel has a total of 136 comfortable rooms, mostly standard, but with a different type of accommodation. There are single, double and family rooms. In addition, as already noted above, there are 33 bungalows that are scattered throughout the hotel. The windows of the main building overlook the Persian Gulf. The picture is simply breathtaking. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, minibar, telephone with international dialing, safe for an additional charge. Among the rooms there are some that, although not pulled by a suite, are superior rooms. They have a seating area and a table with chairs. Bathrooms are the same everywhere. They are well equipped and provide tourists with all toiletries and bath accessories (bathrobes, towels and slippers), as well as a hairdryer. Service is quite acceptable. Linen is changed every other day, and cleaning is daily. Each room has a balcony overlooking either the bay or the park area.
