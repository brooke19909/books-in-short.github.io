Copac Hotel, Vietnam, Nha Trang: overview, description and reviews

If you want to have a good rest in Vietnam and are looking for a budget, but comfortable hotel near the coast, then the three-star Copac (Nha Trang) can be a suitable option.





General description, photo, location

Copac Hotel is located in the Vietnamese city of Nha Trang on Hong Bang Street. Kamranh Airport is within a 45-minute drive. The nearest tran Phu Beach is 500 meters away.

The hotel's housing stock is represented by 59 rooms of the following categories: standard, deluxe, suite and superior. All apartments have air conditioning, safe, telephone, TV, bathroom with hairdryer.

Hotel rates include breakfast only. However, during the day, guests can eat at the hotel's restaurant for an additional fee. There is also a bar on the 15th floor with panoramic views.

At the hotel you can book an excursion you are interested in, rent a scooter or bicycle, exchange currency, use the laundry and dry cleaning services.

Number of rooms

According to the guests, they really liked the apartments at the Copac Hotel. So, the rooms here are medium in size, very comfortable, decorated in a traditional style. They have everything you need for a comfortable stay - comfortable large beds, air conditioning, fan, TV, telephone, bathroom with personal hygiene items and a hairdryer. Also, many tourists as a plus note the presence of a free safe. According to our compatriots, not all rooms have balconies. As for the view from the window, tourists are advised to settle on higher floors, since in this case you can enjoy all the surroundings, and if you're lucky, then the sea. Judging by the comments of a number of guests, the hotel is well equipped with ventilation. Therefore, according to some tourists, you can even smoke in the rooms - there is no smell left here. Many travelers claim that this fact is very rare for budget hotels not only in Vietnam, but also in other countries.

Nutrition

As travelers say in their reviews, they were very pleased with the restaurant at the Copac Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang). So, the price includes only breakfast. According to our compatriots, they are very diverse. So, in the mornings, guests can eat soup, porridge, bacon, sausage and cheese, delicious pastries, fresh fruits and vegetables, etc. The restaurant itself is located on the ninth floor, and its windows offer a very beautiful view of the surroundings.

As for lunch and dinner, many tourists ate both outside the hotel and in its restaurant. According to them, the Copac Hotel cooks very tasty, and the prices are pleasantly pleasing. However, for a change, they, of course, advise you to try different dishes of national cuisine in other cafes and restaurants of Nha Trang.





Sea

Despite the fact that the Copac Hotel is not at all on the first coastline, this did not become a problem for the vast majority of guests. So, according to tourists, the beach from the hotel can be reached in just a quarter of an hour by leisurely step. At the same time along the way you will meet a variety of shops, souvenir shops. Also, on the way to the beach or the hotel, you can go to the massage parlor, buy freshly squeezed juice, etc. If for some reason you do not want to walk to the sea on foot, you can rent a scooter or use a taxi.

As for the local beach, it is municipal. So for the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas have to pay. The beach is quite clean, sandy, with a gentle entry into the water. There are bars and cafes along the coastline where you can enjoy a drink or a snack if you wish. By the way, beach towels are issued at the hotel reception.

Infrastructure

Since the Copac Hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is a city hotel, it practically does not have its own territory. Therefore, there is no pool. However, on the 15th floor of the hotel building, a bar is equipped with an excellent view of the city. There is also a sauna, bowling, there is a terrace with sun loungers.
