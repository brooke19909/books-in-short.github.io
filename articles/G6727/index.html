The best hotels in Kerch

Kerch is a city that is visited by a large number of tourists every year. Some come to enjoy the local attractions, while others come to relax on the seashore. All guests will find useful information about hotels in Kerch. The institutions described in the article received the maximum number of positive reviews. They not only offer comfortable living conditions, but also organize entertainment programs. Some of them are located directly on the Black Sea coast, which is ideal for people who want to have a great rest. According to statistics, in recent years, the largest number of tourists have in the summer season. It was at this time that the Crimea came to life. Kerch (hotels here are equipped in accordance with European standards) - a city in which everyone will find entertainment to their liking.

Guest house Travel House

Welcomes the guest house Travel House. In his arsenal there are comfortable rooms of different capacities. Each of them has a modern interior, new furniture, LCD TV and kettle. For your convenience, the rooms have en suite bathrooms with hot water. Cleaning at the request of visitors. The reception is open 24 hours a day. On site there is free parking, Wi-Fi, equipment for table tennis. Breakfast is included in the price. Many guests confirm the fact that the offered dishes are very tasty. On a 10-point scale, guests rated this establishment at 9.5. Not all hotels in Kerch can boast of such a result. Visitors in their reviews noted the advantages of this guest house, which are cleanliness, comfort, location and convenience. Also, importantly, the staff here are very polite and welcoming.



Hotel "Pearl of the Sea"

On the Black Sea coast there is a beautiful Pearl of the Sea hotel. Here, standard, junior suites and deluxe rooms are offered for living. The last windows open directly to the sea, there is a balcony or terrace. Barbecue areas are on site , and there is a bar and restaurant. For guests arriving by car, free parking is available. All air-conditioned rooms have free Wi-Fi. The hotel received the highest ratings in the following areas: cleanliness, staff work, comfortable conditions.

Hotel "Monica" (Kerch)

On the street Frunze in the house number 34 is the hotel "Monica". Tourists are invited to settle in class rooms: standard, comfort, deluxe. They include toiletries, a hairdryer and slippers. Each is equipped with a satellite LCD TV and air conditioning. For the convenience of guests, the room has an electric kettle and a refrigerator. Like many other hotels in Kerch, Monica is equipped with free parking and Wi-Fi. Visitors liked the availability of family rooms. It is also worth noting that this is one of the few hotels that allows guests with pets. Many guests rated the work of staff high scores. All hotel staff are highly qualified, welcoming and helpful. The cleanliness of the institution and living conditions also deserve special attention.



Hotel Midas (Kerch)

Positive feedback from city guests was noted by the Midas Hotel. His fund has rooms from economy class to luxury. All are equipped with new furniture, air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. The design of the rooms is modern. Each guest will find a bathrobe, towel, slippers, hairdryer and cosmetics in their room. Cleaning is done at the request of guests. It is worth noting that all hotels in Kerch were highly rated for the cleanliness of the premises and territories. This indicates the appropriate qualifications of staff. For couples, special rooms with one large bed are available.

On the territory there is free secured parking, Wi-Fi. You can find this institution at the address: Sovetskaya street, house number 4.



Guest house "Svetlana"

Many hotels in Kerch are located near the beach. This is the guest house "Svetlana". The road to the sea will take no more than 5 minutes. Next to it is a historical museum. Guests can stay in rooms of the class: standard, deluxe and junior suite, there is also a double economy with one bed. For the convenience of visitors, each of them is equipped with a bathroom with all amenities. The suites include spa baths. The interior of the guest house is made in soft, warm colors. Each room has an LCD TV, air conditioning and DVD. Breakfast is included in the price. It is served on the terrace with sea views. There are restaurants and cafes next to the guest house, which are happy to welcome guests. Svetlana has a travel agency that organizes interesting excursions. Living here, you can enjoy the view not only of the sea, but also of the mountains. Visitors praised the staff for their work, convenient location and cleanliness.





Mini-Hotel Edelweiss

A large number of positive reviews was noted mini-hotel "Edelweiss". His fund has economy rooms, standard and deluxe. The hotel is located near the promenade. Visitors are offered excursions to the obelisk of Glory, to the historical museum and other interesting places. It features an indoor pool, spa services, and free Wi-Fi. And, importantly, there is the opportunity to live with pets. The rooms are decorated in a classic style. Each has a kettle and fridge, while the bathroom comes with a hairdryer. This spa hotel (Kerch) offers a range of services including shuttle service, dry cleaning, non-smoking rooms, ironing service and laundry. Many tourists noted the presence of a barbecue area and terrace with sea views. If desired, guests can order drinks and food in the rooms. For those traveling with a lot of luggage, a luggage room is offered. For the convenience of guests, the reception is open around the clock, there is a service of expedited processing of documents for departure / arrival.



Gostiny Dvor Hotel

Very popular hotels in Kerch on the beach. One of these is the Gostiny Dvor. It is located just 2 minutes from the beach. It features a sauna, hammam and free Wi-Fi. Importantly, there is free parking. The hotel fund has apartments for 7 people. They have 3 bedrooms. Also large in capacity is the executive suite. Its windows overlook the sea. Each room has a bathroom and a living room. The latter is equipped with air conditioning and LED TV. Some rooms have washing machines.



Visitors in their reviews noted the good work of the staff, comfortable living conditions and cleanliness. This hotel is located at Bolshaya ulitsa 12. You will have to overcome a distance of 5.2 km from the train station.
