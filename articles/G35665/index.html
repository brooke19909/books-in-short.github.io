Horse embroidery: patterns and descriptions

Many people cannot remain indifferent when they see for the first time such a strong and stately animal as a horse. And in this article we will provide you with some simple and not very schemes that you can then use in your work.

Beads or threads?

First decide what you want to embroider the horse. You can use beads or floss. And depending on the option you choose, buy either a canvas for cross-stitch and a thick needle, or a special fabric for embroidery with beads and a thin needle.

Keep in mind that Chinese beads for embroidery will not work, as it is usually poorly calibrated. You can buy either Czech beads or Japanese. But for cross stitch Chinese thread floss is quite suitable. However, before you buy a floss from China, read the reviews and make sure that these threads do not fade with time.

First define the middle on the canvas. To do this, divide the sides in half and connect the opposite lines. The intersection of these lines will be the middle of the embroidery.

If you embroider a cross, then focus on the cells. If you embroider with beads, it is best to first draw a picture on the fabric with a special pencil or marker. When embroidering with beads, the exact pattern is optional.

More complicated embroidery pattern for the year of the Horse

The pattern below is best embroidered with a cross. Due to the presence of many shades in the drawing, we recommend buying more expensive mouline thread from a French or Spanish manufacturer. So you can choose the most correct colors and shades. The size of the embroidery is approximately 30 by 40 centimeters.





Before embroidering, identify the center on the canvas and on the pattern. Use a rectangular hoop to make it more convenient to work.

Horse embroidery according to the sketch

And how to create a unique and inimitable thing? You can buy expensive and large cross-stitch horse embroidery. Be sure to see the size of the stitch: the smaller it is, the more detailed the picture will be and the more difficult the work.

Such embroideries look realistic and can decorate any interior. Pay attention to the background: if you do not want to waste your energy on it, then buy horse embroidery on a simple plain background.

Beadwork according to the drawing on the fabric

For such embroidery you will need beads, needles with a small and thin eye, threads, fabric, pencil and hoop. First draw a pattern on the fabric. Differentiate the shadow areas, mark the same shades with the icons, so as not to get confused in the future. Then hoop the fabric. It‚Äôs best if the hoop is the same size as the pattern itself so you don‚Äôt have to rearrange them.





Buy a cloth. Then pick up the shades of beads. Beads can be transparent, translucent, transparent with a dyed middle, matte, glossy and opaque, as well as with the effect of mother of pearl or metallic. Before buying beads, attach it to the fabric and see how it will look. It is better to embroider the horse itself with simple opaque beads, but it is better to spend beads with a metallic effect on glare from the sun.

Start embroidering the picture. The more shades you use in your work, the more realistic it will turn out. After finishing work, pick up a frame in the framing workshop and insert your work there yourself or using the wizard. For safety, embroidery is best set for glass. Bead embroidery is ready! Now you can hang it on the wall or give it to someone.
