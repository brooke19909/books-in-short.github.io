How to change the tariff on MTS conveniently and without unnecessary actions



If you follow the changes and updates that mobile operators constantly offer, you will notice that new offers appear on the mobile services market every month. Therefore, the issue of how to change the tariff for MTS becomes relevant. Fortunately, the ways to implement this in some places are trivial, but in some places not very, now there are a lot of tasks. But all these methods are different, and each of them has its pitfalls that have yet to be learned.



The most common method of changing the tariff for MTS was sending a command from a mobile phone for a long time. In principle, it was extremely convenient - just a few taps, and after a few minutes a completely new way of calculating with the operator was often much more convenient than before. But selecting an offer to use was a real torment, since it was very difficult to find out the MTS tariff that would be most beneficial, and not only for this operator. It is necessary to collect booklets, which were far from everywhere everywhere in the required volume, and the most "tidy" tariffs were often hidden so deep on the counter that they could not always be found not only on the first, but also on the second attempt. Plus, the printing method made it possible to hide the disadvantageous sides of the proposal - the quality of glossy paper in combination with small text gave blurry obscure notes in which the most uncomfortable clauses of the contract were hidden.

The next way to change the tariff for MTS was to use the services of company salons. There, the operator‚Äôs employees, according to their job descriptions, should provide maximum assistance to leave the customer satisfied. But after all, it is not profitable for the communication provider to miss the possible profit, therefore, in such places MTS tariff plans were offered with the expectation of withdrawing a little more money from the user than he would like. Usually for this, offers were issued with a large number of additional services that could not be disabled in the first month. Typically, customers forgot about such trifles and lost at least one hundred rubles.



The most convenient method at the moment on how to change the tariff for MTS is the choice of services using your personal account. Having received the password via SMS, you can enter the convenient control panel with connected offers. There you can not only choose among the existing tariff plans by looking at all the notes that cannot be hidden on the network, but also turn off unnecessary connected functions that remove money or simply interfere with the use of mobile communication services. Another indisputable advantage of this method was that in case of any problems with the transition between tariffs, you could immediately write an appeal to the operator. The response to such letters is much faster than to other forms of complaints, which means that the problem can be solved in a short time without various delays and delays in reaching a verdict on appeal.
