Manousos Hotel 3 * (Greece / Rhodes island) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If you decide to spend an unforgettable vacation on the Greek island of Rhodes and at the same time choose a budget, but comfortable option for accommodation, then pay attention to the Manousos Hotel 3 *. We offer a closer look at this hotel.





Description and location

This hotel was built in 1969. The last reconstruction was carried out in 2004. Today it is a completely modern city-style hotel, offering tourists accommodation in one of 140 comfortable standard rooms. It is located just a quarter hour drive from the airport and a couple of minutes walk from the nearest beach. Also in the vicinity of the hotel there are many taverns, bars, cafes, nightclubs, shops and other tourist infrastructure facilities.

Rhodes Manousos Hotel 3 *: prices

The cost of living in this hotel, many tourists consider acceptable. In their opinion, it is fully consistent with the conditions of accommodation and quality of service. So, a weekly stay in August will cost you from 476 euros for a single room, from 665 euros for a double room and from 903 euros for an apartment for three. However, prices include breakfast only. You can have lunch and dinner at the hotel for an extra charge.





Manousos Hotel 3 * (Rhodes): reviews of Russian travelers

In order to save a little time for those tourists who, when choosing a hotel, are guided not only by their official description, but also by the impressions of people who have already stayed here, we give generalized comments from our compatriots who spent their holidays at Manousos Hotel (Rhodes, Greece ) Running a little ahead, we note that most travelers were quite satisfied with their choice. Moreover, according to them, despite a very reasonable price, this hotel has a very high level. But we learn about everything in detail.





Comments of tourists about the location and own territory of the hotel

According to most of our compatriots, Manousos Hotel 3 * has a very good location. So, next to it there are a lot of taverns, travel agencies, rental offices of cars and other vehicles, shops, supermarkets, etc. The nearest beach can be reached in just a couple of minutes. The path to the Old Town will take no more than a quarter of an hour. Just a couple of minutes walk from the hotel is the Street of Bars, where you will find a lot of nightclubs, bars and discos.

Since Manousos is a city-style hotel, it does not have its own territory as such. However, despite this, there is a pool. According to tourists, it is clean and spacious enough. Next to it there is a small sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols.





Hotel Housing Impressions

As for the rooms of Manousos Hotel 3 * (Rhodes), then, according to travelers, they are clean, pleasantly decorated and equipped with everything necessary. It is noticeable that recently a repair was made. Furniture and plumbing are not new, but everything is in good condition. The rooms are small, but there is enough space in them for a comfortable stay. The newlyweds were pleasantly surprised: the hotel gave them a compliment in the form of fruit and wine in the room. There is also a safe in the rooms, which can be used at an additional cost. Air conditioning can be used for free. Also, tourists in their reviews note the presence of a TV with two channels in Russian.

A small minus was some vacationers considered good audibility in the rooms. So, according to them, if the noise from the street does not bother you when the windows are closed, then in case you get too noisy neighbors, some discomfort is provided. Therefore, if you are used to getting enough sleep, it is recommended to take ear plugs from the house. However, in fairness, it should be recognized that a similar problem is characteristic not only for Manousos Hotel 3 *, but also for many other hotels both in Greece and other countries.

As for cleaning, according to our compatriots, it is of high quality and daily. If you want towels to be changed, throw the dirty ones on the floor. Bed linen was updated every three days. Also, travelers are advised to pay attention to the fact that there are no beach towels at the hotel. Therefore, they must be taken with you from home or bought on the spot.





Reviews of food and hotel staff

As for food, then, according to tourists, breakfast at the hotel is standard European. So, here you will be offered granola, prepared in different ways, eggs, sausages, cheeses, dried fruits, fresh vegetables and fruits, jams, pastries, tea, coffee and juices. This is not to say that the choice is huge, but you will not stay hungry. As for lunch and dinner, they at Manousos Hotel 3 * are offered at a price of 10 euros. However, most guests preferred to eat in the many taverns located in the vicinity of the hotel. Those who had lunch and dinner at the hotel claim that there were always delicious seafood and meat dishes on the menu.

Vacationers have a very positive opinion about the hotel staff. According to them, the staff here are polite and friendly. Try to populate as quickly as possible. At the same time, upon arrival, they will not ask you to fill out any forms (as in a number of other hotels). So, here you only need to show your passport, and the administrators at the reception will give you the key to the number. Our compatriots also pay attention to the fact that the hotel staff speaks only English.

As for the contingent of vacationers, for the most part it is represented by young people (about 17-20 years old) from various countries. However, those tourists who stayed at the Manousos Hotel 3 * in September-October, argue that at this time there are almost no young people here, and the guests are mainly people of retirement age.

Opinion about beach vacations and entertainment

Many travelers who spent their holidays here were delighted with the opportunity to go swimming in the beaches of the Mediterranean and the Aegean. Go to the first for about 10 minutes, and to the second - no more than a couple of minutes. According to tourists, the Aegean Sea is quite turbulent and waves are frequent here. Therefore, many vacationers spent time on the beaches of the Mediterranean Sea. In addition, the water here is warmer than in the Aegean. However, when entering it, note that after a few meters from the shore, the bottom goes down and becomes deep enough. Therefore, people who do not know how to swim, and travelers with children should be alert.
