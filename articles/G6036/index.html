How to call Moscow from abroad? How to make cheap calls?

Today, many people visit different countries every day. This is due to work, that is, business trips, or regular travel and the desire to see something new and unusual. But all of us in our homeland remain close and friends with whom I really want to share information or impressions about the rest. To do this, you need to know at least how to call Moscow - the capital of Russia. Consider this process as an example of the famous city, as it is the most populated, and, accordingly, the relatives of tourists there the most.

Sometimes a phone call decides the fate of a person. No one would like to be in a situation where you urgently need to make a call to a subscriber who is in Russia from abroad, but you do not know how to make it better.

Which, it seemed, could be easier than calling Moscow from a landline or mobile phone. Probably, for this, you just need to dial the subscriber number, pick up the phone and wait for an answer. But it was not there.

Let's figure out how to call to Moscow.





First of all, you need to know certain rules for dialing a phone number, which depend on your location. If you are located in Russia, you need to use the usual scheme of calling the subscriber: you dial the country code, then add the city code after the dial tone and at the end dial the subscriber number, after which the connection is established. There is nothing complicated here. But the situation is completely different when you are abroad. You can find out how to call Moscow from a landline telephone at any information desk in each country. But in order not to waste time, you only need to remember a certain code.





So, how to call to Moscow from a landline phone?

8-tone beep- (499/498/497/496/495) - YYY- YY -YY

The numbers 495/496/498/499 are Moscow city codes. It is not necessary to use all, but only one of them.

YYY- YY- YY - digits of the mobile or landline phone number of the subscriber you want to call

Which of the city codes you need to use, you need to find out from your operator or the subscriber to whom you are making a call.

So we figured out how to call to Moscow from a landline phone. As you can see, you just need to remember a small combination of numbers, when using which you will not have difficulties with calling a subscriber.

Now let's figure out how to call Moscow from a mobile phone. The scheme of such a call is somewhat different from the above scheme of a call from a landline phone. Changes are completely miserable, or rather - they relate to just one digit.





So, remember how to call to Moscow from a mobile phone:

+7 (499/498/497/496/495) - YYY- YY-YY

+7 is the international code of the Russian Federation, which is used when calling a subscriber from Russia

(495/496/498/499) - Moscow city codes

YYY- YY- YY - mobile or landline number of the subscriber

What is the cost of calls to Russia?

First of all, it depends on the tariffication of your operator. Today, many companies offer advantageous tariff plans for calls to Russia from abroad. When choosing a special tariff, you can make calls to this country almost for free - for a few cents per minute. But this way of cheap communication with subscribers from Russia is relevant only for mobile phone users. But how to make cheap calls to your homeland from a landline telephone?

You can use special codes that significantly reduce the cost of a call. Before the number, you dial a specific set of numbers and call at a different tariff. You can also find these codes from mobile operators.

In today's article, you learned how to call Moscow from a mobile and landline phone at a low cost. Good luck!
