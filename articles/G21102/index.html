How to top up your Troika card online

Troika is a universal card used to pay for public transport: ground and underground, electric trains and aeroexpress trains. Why did most Muscovites choose her? Firstly, it is convenient. Secondly, it is beneficial.

What tickets can be recorded on the "Troika"



Since Troika operates on all types of transport, you can record on it any fare for paper tickets that is currently in force. In addition, there is a replenishment of the Troika card for any amount not exceeding 3,000 rubles. When passing through the turnstile, the fare will be deducted in accordance with the tariffs.

Three card: how to replenish the balance?

The Moscow City Department recently reported that the event, which had been waiting for so long, finally happened. From now on, it became possible to replenish the Troika card via the Internet.

Earlier it was possible to replenish the card through the ticket offices of the Metropolitan and Aeroexpress, through the terminals "Eleksnet", Europlat, "Megaphone" and velobike.ru.

Top-up via mobile phone



Now, subscribers of any operator can replenish the balance of any Troika card remotely from their mobile phone account. How to top up the Troika card? The algorithm is quite simple. You only need to have Internet access. On the official website of the Troika card, enter the number of the replenished card, the mobile phone number from which the payment will be made, and the desired amount. The size of the payment varies from 10 to 2500 rubles. A few seconds after the transaction is completed, an SMS message with a code should be sent to the mobile phone, which will need to be sent in a reply message in order to confirm the payment. With this type of replenishment of the Troika card, no fees are charged.

But that's not all. With remote replenishment, money does not immediately reach the card. They are stored in the transport system of the Metro. To activate them, you must attach the card to the yellow information terminal. In addition, the operation can be completed in special terminals installed in the Megafon communication salons.

Top-up using Webmoney, Qiwi Wallet and Yandex.Money e-wallets

Now, members of the Webmoney system have the opportunity to replenish their balance if they have a Troika card (metro). How to replenish? You can use the service on the project website. Payment is instant. Replenishment is possible only with R-wallets. In addition, the site has the option of replenishing with a Qiwi wallet and Yandex.Money. The replenishment algorithm is the same: enter the number of the "Three" card, indicate the amount by which you want to replenish, and in the payment method, select one of your electronic wallets.



How to top up the Troika card? You can also put funds on her balance using the Yandex.Money personal wallet. Crediting occurs instantly and without any fees.

Card replenishment agents

How to top up the Troika card? What methods still exist? In addition to all of the above, you can transfer money to the card through the terminals "National Payment Service", through the Comepay wallet and the payment system plat.ru.

What can I do to ensure that purchased tickets get on the Troika card?

The funds transferred to the Troika card electronic wallet via the Internet, that is, through remote replenishment, cannot be used right away. They are in the status of deferred payment, and they must be activated - write to the card. This can be done using the yellow information terminals installed in the lobby of the subway stations. On the screen, you need to click the "Remote recharge" button and attach the card to the reader. Just a few seconds - and the payment is activated, and the amount transferred will be displayed on the screen.
