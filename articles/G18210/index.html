10W LED floodlight - how does it shine? LED lightening

To illuminate the territory, pools, tunnels, as well as objects that need protection, various lighting devices are used. Among the most popular are LED streetlights. Why do they use them? After all, they have not only positive, but also negative sides.

General information

Street LED floodlights can function in any climatic conditions, that is, withstand large temperature differences and any precipitation. In addition, they are not subject to mechanical damage.

Another significant indicator is low power consumption. Thanks to this quality, the LED spotlight has gained popularity. It began to be used not only for domestic purposes, but also for lighting industrial and security facilities. It is increasingly used to illuminate small objects such as a store, garage, advertising signs.

The advantages of flashlights

Among the positive qualities of LED spotlights, the following are distinguished:

  • Low power consumption. Compared to conventional incandescent lamps, LED lights consume 10 times less. This indicator was compared with energy-saving lamps. The measurement result showed that power consumption is 3 times less.
  • Long service life.
    The layout of the LED spotlight is quite simple, so it can function for 10 years.
  • Made of environmentally friendly materials, that is, do not harm the environment.

disadvantages

Like any device, such lights have negative sides:

  • high price;
  • many manufacturers assign a life of 10 years. However, this figure is not true. The lamp works no more than 5 years. It all depends on the conditions of use. During continuous operation, the LEDs deteriorate. First, they lose brightness, and then burn out completely.
  • To increase the service life it is necessary to use an additional source of cooling and power. Such devices serve to protect the flashlight from power surges. However, the cost of such devices is quite high.

Design

LED lights consist of such structural elements:

  • reflector;
  • voltage transformer;
  • in some cases, motion sensors are used.

Classification of lighting equipment

LED spotlights are divided by the following characteristics:

  1. By appointment: general-purpose and special devices (used to protect various objects).
  2. By color.
  3. Type of housing: can be rectangular, square, round.
  4. Voltage.
  5. Protection level: can be used both for ground use and underwater.

Flashlight with sensor

They are for short range use only. The principle of operation is that the spotlight starts to shine when people, animals, cars appear in a given zone, and fades away after a while. Thanks to this mode, energy is significantly saved and the service life of the device is increased.
