Lipetsk Zoo: animals, address, mode of operation, ticket price

A small but very diverse zoological park will give joy to everyone. Visitors instantly enter the world in which animals, birds, and fish live. What else can surprise the zoo, is described further in the article.

History of creation

It began still forty years ago. The then chairman of the city executive committee, Nikolai Georgievich Yakhontov, took the initiative in creating the zoo, and also contributed financially to this.



However, in the 90s a critical moment came - animals became smaller, and they began to starve. Many leaders changed, and it didn‚Äôt lead to anything good.

But when Alexander Ivanovich Osipov became the director, the construction of the hay hangar, the strengthening of cages, the reconstruction of the roof over the Winter Garden, and the construction of the grain warehouse began immediately. Further, a summer arbor for birds, a veterinary corner, and new aviaries and housing for animals were built on the territory already.

Huge efforts were made to form the collection, and as a result, the number of species increased three times, and there were nine more copies. Already not only the house, but also the birthplace for animals has become a zoo. Lipetsk became one of the few cities in which the aquaterrarium first appeared. At the moment, boas and anacondas, skinks, bearded agamas and many other types of exotic animals live there.

Twelve years ago, Lipetsk Park became a member of the Euro-Asian Regional Association of Zoos and Aquariums (EARAZA). Also takes part in projects that are focused on breeding and conservation of rare species of animals.

In 2005, the opening of the branch of the Lipetsk Zoo "Bird Paradise". It is located in the cultural park of the city of Michurinsk. More than 35 different species of animals were presented to visitors of Bird Paradise.

Live here

Today in the zoo's aquaterrarium you can get acquainted with a boa constrictor, anaconda, skinks, trionixes, snake-necked turtles and others. More than ten aquariums have fish of extraordinary sizes and different shades. Also, guests of the zoo admire the catfish, which swim belly up, admire the restless barbs and devoted cichlids.

About 30 species of animals that live in the menagerie are on the list of the Red Book of Russia.

Ligers also live there - these are the largest cats in the world, which are among the rarest species of predators. The largest territory in the zoo is occupied by ungulates.



After entering the territory, the lake is located on the right side. It is a start when examining all the beauty.

The lake is large enough, and in the very center you can see bird houses. Nearby is an interesting fountain in the form of a heron, from whose beak water pours. Various representatives of the duck family swim in the lake.

The royal beauty of the menagerie in Lipetsk is the cranes.

Thanks to connections with zoological parks of other cities, animals and various kinds of information are exchanged, which accordingly replenishes a number of inhabitants of the paradise.

Little visitors

For the most mobile fidgets, in the very center of the park is located the Children's Entertainment Complex, which runs until October. He attracts kids with his variegated color scheme, as well as the fact that the playground, attractions and a contact zoo are located next to each other. Everything was created so that young visitors could approach, feed and pet animals safely and without fear.

The Contact Zoo for Children is a home for a variety of specially tamed animals that allows children to communicate. Careful selection of inhabitants excludes aggression on the person. At the same time, security measures are provided in the contact zoo: an employee on duty who has everything necessary for first aid is on duty.





"Flower Garden"

This is a paradise, which is absolutely suitable for such a name. Forged patterned fences in the form of a frame are in perfect harmony with the arches. Inside it is a stunning exposition: flowers, shrubs, trees and other elements of natural decor intertwined into an amazing picture. To relax visitors to the zoo provided benches.

The visiting card of the Garden of Flowers is an open shell with a pearl from which water beats. You can also meet figures of characters from fairy tales that adorn the zoo. Due to such beauty, Lipetsk blooms culturally. Residents and guests of the city sincerely rest.

For all visitors

The zoo in Lipetsk is located, its address: Petrovsky proezd, 2, in the Lower Park. At the entrance you can visit the pet shop, which sells various souvenirs, animals, special food and cages for home use.

The procedure consists of the fact that the contract is drawn up, and the "adoptive parents" take part in all the festive events of the zoo. Children personally contact and take pictures with the inhabitants of nature, which remains for a long time in their hearts.

Ticket price

Each guest of the city is advised to visit a paradise when he arrives in Lipetsk. The zoo, the price of a ticket to which is not at all high, is 180 rubles for an adult; it welcomes everyone very hospitably. For a child, the entrance will cost 70 rubles.



There is also a single ticket to the zoo and carterrarium. For adults it costs about 300 rubles, for children - 100. On holidays and weekends you can ride a horse for a fee. The cost of a ticket for an on-site demonstration, where special lectures are held, is 60 rubles. Having visited the zoo in Lipetsk, you will definitely return there again!
