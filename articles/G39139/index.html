Wardrobe for the little princess - with the hands of mom. Knitted hat for girls (knitting)

How lucky for the children whose mothers are knitters. Such kids are always noticeable. They are dressed in original handmade clothes, made with knitting needles or crochet. As a rule, most young mothers, having gone on maternity leave, try to master the basics of this craft. This is very good, because learning is never too late. Where to start

new to knitting? What little wardrobe thing can be done quickly and easily? It can be a scarf, scarf or hat. It is about the latter that we will discuss in this article. The readers are presented with information on how to knit a hat for a girl with knitting needles. Having studied the descriptions, you will learn how to do this piece of clothing with your own hands in the form of a bonnet for a baby and a headdress with a lapel for an older princess.

Knit a hat for a girl (with ties)

This cap model is suitable for newborn babies. You will need knitting needles No. 3 and 100 grams of yarn for work. To complete the ties, prepare a narrow ribbon or braid (30-40 cm).

How does a similar hat fit for a girl with knitting needles? Dial 69 loops and knit 2 centimeters with an elastic band: alternating the front and back loops. This will be the headgear cuffs. Next, carry out the main part of the product. Divide the total number of loops by 3 (23 each). Next, knit only the middle part, in each row knitting the last loop of this section from the first side (according to the principle of knitting the heel of the toe). Thus, do the work until 23 loops of the central and 3 loops of the left remain on the knitting needles. Now make reductions throughout the row, knitting all the loops in two together. After a series, repeat the same steps. Tuck the cuffs connected with an elastic band and sew them. Through it, pull the tie from a braid or tape. Everything, the bonnet is ready.



Hat for girls with knitting needles (bonnet): tips for choosing patterns and yarn

Novice craftswomen can perform it with the most ordinary garter or hosiery. Experienced knitters, as a rule, use various patterns of front, back loops and yarn. For the winter version of the bonnet, the patterns are dense, and for the demi-season, you can also use openwork knitting.

When buying strings, pay attention to the label. There should be a note "children's". Such yarn does not prick, does not cause an allergy in the child. For a winter hat, take wool or half-wool. Autumn-spring version of the bonnet can be made of cotton, viscose, acrylic.

Hat for girls (knitting): "Bouquet"



Simple in execution, but very beautiful and original headdress for a daughter-paw, you can knit in just a couple of evenings. Why "Bouquet"? But because the decor in this model will be decorative flowers.

Prepare knitting needles No. 4, half-woolen yarn (100 g). Dial 90 loops and knit the fabric with a 2x2 rubber band (alternating front and back loops). When the product reaches a length of 23 centimeters, knit 1 row with reductions. This is done as follows: * 4 facial, one of two stitches, double crochet *. Repeat this way from * - * to the end of this row. In the next row (wrong side), follow the pattern. In places where there was a yarn, holes form. Next, continue working with the same rubber for another 5 centimeters. After this, close the loops. Pull the tie through the holes. It can be made of decorative lace, satin ribbon or crocheted with a chain of air loops. Lapel around the bottom edge. Knitted hat for a girl, ready to warm a small head. But for the product to fit its name, you need to decorate it. For these purposes, you can use artificial flowers made of fabric or yarn.
