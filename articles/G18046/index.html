Teberda: Hotel resorts

In the Karachay-Cherkess Republic on the mountain slopes of the Caucasus is a unique resort - Teberda. Travelers come here from many corners of Russia and from abroad. It offers beautiful scenery, mountain air and mild climate. There are many sights and natural monuments in and around Teberda. Rest here is comfortable at any time of the year. Each vacationer will find in Teberda interesting and affordable entertainment. In the city and its environs there are numerous sanatoriums and recreation centers where balneological treatment is carried out. Here, in the city of Teberda, hotels will offer their guests good conditions at a reasonable price.

Nature reserve

The Teberda Nature Reserve has long been a popular route for vacationers. Here you can get acquainted with the unique fauna and flora by visiting the ecological trail. The natural landscapes of the reserve are very diverse: beautiful mountains, ancient glaciers, mountain lakes are real pearls . There are more than one hundred and fifty of them. According to legend, their waters have healing properties and can save people from many diseases.



Klukhor Lake is one of the main attractions of the Teberda Nature Reserve. This mountain lake, covered with ice for almost the whole year, is unusually beautiful.

Dombai

Like Teberda, Dombay attracts many guests. It is located in the center of the Teberda Nature Reserve, it is rightly called the heart of the mountains. They are surrounded by them from all sides. The village of Dombay is located at the confluence of very beautiful mountain rivers - Alibek, Amanauz and Dombay-Ulgen. The local climate is healing, there are many sanatoriums for the treatment of asthma and other respiratory diseases.

Dombay is a popular ski resort. Tourists admire a lot: the amazing beauty of the mountains, amazing nature, air. Fans of alpine skiing with pleasure visit sports and fitness centers, master mountaineering and ski slopes. There are twelve descents of varying difficulty with the possibility of fast skiing. Ski slopes are designed not only for lovers, but also satisfy the needs of skiing professionals.

Many tourists are attracted by interesting excursion routes. Vacationers will be offered to see amazing mountain landscapes with snow-capped peaks, waterfalls and lakes.



Hotel "At Alla"

The indisputable advantage of a holiday in Karachay-Cherkessia is a wide selection of hotels. The hotel business is constantly evolving. Hotels in Teberda, hotels and guest houses in Dombay are available to guests with different material incomes and offer modern services. Tourists can choose them based on their convenient location. The U Alla hotel also has such an advantage. It is located twenty-three kilometers from the Dombai resort and less than one kilometer from the Teberda Nature Reserve. The hotel is within walking distance of Kara Kel Lake. The hotel offers beautiful views of the mountains and glaciers.

Infrastructure

Paid parking is available on site. Nearby is the bus station. The hotel has more than thirty rooms with accommodation from two to five people. The rooms have private bathrooms, a hairdryer is provided for guests, free toiletries and cleaning products are provided. The rooms are equipped with both double and single beds. The hotel rooms are soundproofed, have a TV with cable channels, carpeted floors and mosquito nets on the windows. It offers guests dressing rooms and ironing facilities.





The staff of the hotel is staffed by responsible and caring staff. For the convenience of guests, the hotel has shops, including souvenir, ski equipment for rent. Guests will be fed tasty and inexpensive, they will offer dishes of national cuisine.

Dombay ski resort, the city of Teberda, hotels and resorts are always ready to welcome guests and place them in the most comfortable way. Rest in these protected areas is unforgettable.
