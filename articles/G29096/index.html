Cinar Family Suite Hotel. Cheap hotels of Turkey. Side, Turkey, 4 Star Hotels

Every season, the flow of tourists traveling to Turkey is growing. There are several reasons for this. Firstly, this Asian country is one of those where you can spend your long-awaited vacation relatively safely and inexpensively, and secondly, both the hotel service and the quality of services offered in Turkish resorts improve from year to year.



An important advantage is the local climate with natural landscape. Turkey has everything: mountains and waterfalls, historical monuments of ancient times and nature reserves. But the most important thing is the stunning coast and beaches.

Cheap vacation

This country has one more definite plus: here you can have a good rest for fairly little money. Cheap hotels in Turkey offer a very wide range of services. True, their level is somewhat different from the European one, but for many, including Russians, this circumstance is not a problem.

The tourist service here is well developed, so you can find a cheaper and calmer hotel even in the famous noisy resorts where young people relax. However, many prefer to go to such Turkish cities, where you can relax while enjoying peace. Judging by the reviews of vacationers, the most inexpensive can be called Fethiye, Alanya and Side. Turkey, whose 4-star hotels are not very different from those that have a higher category, today accepts tourists of all kinds of incomes. Her hotels can be a great place to relax, if, of course, correctly determine their choice.



Side

The resort is located seventy kilometers from Antalya. This is not only an ancient seaport, but also a place where the greatness of nature, ancient monuments and the rhythm of the modern city are best combined. Recently, more tourists have come here. Everything suits them here: the sea is crystal clear, and sandy beaches, and, of course, a fairly developed infrastructure.

Side is famous in Turkey for its landscapes. The city is surrounded by majestic mountains, the green slopes of which are very harmoniously transformed into golden beaches. There are a lot of hotels in the city, including four-star ones. Prices range from two thousand nine hundred rubles per day, as, for example, in Venus Hotel, to eight and a half - in Barut Cennet & Acanthus.

Cinar Family Suite Hotel

Reviews about this hotel are mostly positive. It was built in 2001. Then it was called Cinar Garden Apart. It is located in a small area, which is fully planted with a garden. It has a fairly rich infrastructure, including two pools, one of which has slides. Cinar Family Suite Hotel 4 * is perfect for those Russians who go on vacation on a limited budget. The cost of a weekly stay in a double room of this hotel for two people is about five hundred and twenty dollars excluding the road. The price may vary slightly depending on the season and occupancy of the hotel.



Near the hotel there are many shops, restaurants, cafes and discos. There is a bus stop right at the gate. The distance to the airport is sixty-five, to the bus station - five and a half kilometers.

Infrastructure

The hotel has ATMs, a laundry service, a currency exchange office, and a depository safe where you can leave the most expensive things. The reception is open around the clock. Here, at the counter, you can use the fax or photocopy machine. There is a service such as express check-in at Cinar Family Suite Hotel. The hotel has a tour desk, where you can book tours to Side's historic sites. If you wish, you can rent a car here and independently study the ancient monuments, which are abundant in the region.

The hotel grounds are guarded around the clock. There is a small parking lot that does not need to be reserved. According to the rules of the hotel, pets are not allowed. There are no rooms for wheelchair users.

Housing stock

Cinar Family Suite Hotel is positioned as a place where you can relax with the whole family, including with young children. The hotel has seventy rooms, some of which can accommodate up to five people at a time, not counting one small child. Apart from the standard beds in the bedroom, the luxury apartments also have a living room with a door, upholstered furniture, including a sofa bed.

The rooms have all the necessary furniture: makeup mirror, wardrobe, bedside tables, chairs and a table. The apartments also have a kitchen area with everything you need. Bathrooms are combined, there is a hairdryer.

There are small balconies, on the floor - tiles. The service is standard: bed linen is replaced after two, towels - every day.
