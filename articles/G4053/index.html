Hotel "Dolphinia" (Corfu, Moraitika): description, reviews. Delfinia Hotel 4 *

Greece is a favorite country of those tourists who want to relax inexpensively, but with high quality. Here you can enjoy the magnificent beaches, and the highest level of service, and the famous sights of Greek culture. Corfu Island combines all these qualities, complementing them with beautiful nature and quiet, secluded atmosphere. There are many tourist complexes for every taste. One of them is the hotel "Dolphinia" (Moraitika). Should I choose it for a vacation?

Location "Dolphin"

Complex "Dolphinia" is located in the small tourist village of Moraitika, located on the coast of the island of Corfu. This region is famous for its magnificent nature, fine cuisine and ancient monuments. You can get to the village from the nearest airport in Corfu. The distance to it is only 21 kilometers. Travel companies organize transfers to and from the airport. From Moscow and St. Petersburg during the holiday season, cheap charter flights often fly here.





The Delfinia Hotel (Moraitika) 4 * hotel itself is located near the center of the village, on the first beach line. Go to him from here no more than 5 minutes. The complex is surrounded by a lush garden that flows smoothly into a pebble beach. Also, the hotel is located in close proximity to the famous attractions that you must visit when you come here. Resting here, it is worth looking at the following objects:

  • Achilleion Palace (8 km);
  • Vlacherna Monastery and Mouse Island (11 km);
  • the summer residence of King George III - the Mon Repos Palace (13 km);
  • Church of St. Spyridon (14 km);
  • Museum of Asian Art (15 km).

Brief summary

Hotel "Dolphin" (Corfu) began to receive tourists since 1967. On its territory there are several residential buildings: one four-story and two two-story buildings. Together they can offer tourists nearly 200 rooms. The last major redecoration of buildings was carried out in 2001. Currently, the rooms are partially renovated, but not all of them are fully updated. The hotel is suitable for active youth recreation, and for families with children. Pets are also allowed for tourists.





The hotel has staff who speak Russian. This is quite convenient, because not all vacationers know English or Greek at the proper level. Check-in takes place strictly from 14:00 to 00:00 local time. Eviction should be carried out from 7:00 to noon.

Number of rooms

In all three buildings of the Delfinia Hotel 4 * hotel, there are 185 rooms. Most of the apartments are designed to accommodate 2-3 people. But some of them can accommodate up to 4 adults. All rooms are 23 square meters. Each room has a private balcony, while the ground floor apartments have a terrace. From the windows you can see the sea, pool or garden, from all sides surrounding the hotel. There are also private non-smoking rooms.

All rooms are tidied every day. Bed sheets are changed at least 3 times a week, and new towels in the bathroom appear daily. In addition to the standard set of furniture, which includes a double bed, a desk, a chair, nightstands for clothes, each room is equipped with the following amenities:

  • A modern flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some rooms are complemented by a radio that broadcasts in Greek.
  • Individual air conditioning, which tourists can adjust as they wish.
  • Safe, which can be used only at an additional cost. You can leave money and valuables in it without fear that they will be stolen by someone from the staff.
  • A small refrigerator for storing food.
  • Paid internet.
  • Telephone for communication with the reception.
  • Heating, which includes at the beginning and end of the tourist season or just on cold days.
  • A bathroom complete with personal hygiene products, towels and a hairdryer.




All about nutrition at Dolphin

For its guests, the Hotel Dolphinia (Corfu) offers a half-board system, that is, the price includes only breakfast and dinner. In the morning, standard international cuisine is served: scrambled eggs, omelettes, toasts, pancakes, cereals. In the evening, tourists can enjoy European and Greek dishes in the restaurant. There are always a variety of fruits on the tables. Meat, fish and seafood are also served daily. The price includes drinks that are served with meals. In addition, you can try national food in restaurants located on the island of Corfu.

Hotel "Dolphinia" (4 stars) offers to enjoy food in one of the restaurants and bars located on its territory:

  • Feaks Restaurant, where guests can have breakfast and dinner free of charge;
  • a tavern on the beach offers tourists to try grilled meat and vegetables, as well as light snacks, desserts and delicious ice cream;
  • The cocktail bar, also located on the beach, specializes in soft drinks, so here you can try alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails, juices, soda and mineral water.




What kind of entertainment is prepared for tourists?

Guests of Delfinia Hotel 4 * can enjoy all sorts of entertainment for every taste. The island of Corfu offers mainly a quiet and relaxing holiday, but lovers of active life will find something to do here. The following activities are offered to hotel guests:

  • Outdoor fresh water pool. You can also sunbathe near it, using sun loungers and umbrellas for a small fee.
  • A beach that is within walking distance of the hotel. Sun loungers and towels are also paid. Tourists can rent surfing equipment, ride a boat or a catamaran, play beach volleyball and go diving.
  • A spacious room for video games, where not only children, but also adults can play on the console.
  • Playgrounds for sports. Tourists can play volleyball, table and classic tennis, darts.
  • For fans of intellectual entertainment, the hotel has gigantic chess and tables for playing backgammon.
  • Paid billiards.
  • Gym for sports.
  • A show program that invites tourists to enjoy live piano playing. Greek and theme nights are also regularly held.




Services provided by the hotel

Dolphinia offers its guests quality service. The hotel cares about the level of customer service and strives to provide them with all the amenities that may be required during a holiday. For this, the administration of the complex offers the following services:

  • paid Wi-Fi at the hotel;
  • conference room with a capacity of about 100 people;
  • free guarded parking lot, where you can park or rent a car, rent bicycles;
  • dry cleaning, where guests can wash clothes, clean shoes;
  • paid transfer to the airport of Corfu;
  • round-the-clock information desk with a Russian-speaking administrator, ready to solve the problems;
  • exchange of rubles to euros.

Holidays in the "Dolphin" with children

Hotel "Dolphinia" (Corfu) offers a standard set of services for families with children. All children are welcome. For them, one crib per room is provided for free, in restaurants and bars parents can feed their children free of charge in highchairs. If adults need to leave and leave the child at the hotel, then they can hire a nanny who can look after him day and night.





From entertainment for children, the hotel has a shallow pool with warm fresh water. Tourists can also buy water toys at an additional cost. For outdoor recreation, there is a children's playground with modern swings and carousels.

Advantages of the hotel, according to tourists

Tourists visiting the hotel "Dolphinia" (Corfu) are satisfied with the rest. They note the following advantages of the complex:

  • well-groomed and very beautiful territory, which is often tidied up, throwing garbage from the sea, cigarette butts, packages;
  • the hotel is remote from the noisy city, so it is quiet at night, no one is making noise;
  • clear sea without garbage floating on the water and the smell of gasoline from ships;
  • varied and tasty food in the main restaurant, and in the evenings there is live music;
  • Friendly and helpful staff who try to solve the problems as soon as possible;
  • It is believed that the whole hotel has paid Wi-Fi, but the bar has a free access point that the staff knows about, but still allows it to be used;
  • the beach is not crowded with people, there are always a lot of free sun loungers and deck chairs.




Disadvantages of "Dolphin", according to tourists

But tourists leave not only positive opinions about holidays on the island of Corfu. "Dolphin" is a hotel, reviews of which are often negative. We list the main shortcomings noted by guests who rested here:

  • the rooms are in need of repair, in general, the situation looks rather shabby, although all the equipment works well;
  • to enter the sea without special shoes is impossible, because the bottom is strewn with small sharp stones;
  • a lot of people from other hotels come to the beach, occupying the loungers of the guests of the "Dolphin", and no one is watching this;
  • dirty dishes in the restaurant, they give only one set of devices for the whole meal (sometimes you have to eat watermelon with a fork stained with fish), they don‚Äôt tidy up the tables, they can serve drinks in broken glasses;
  • not enough outlets in the rooms;
  • maids clean rooms when they want, so they can come early in the morning or late in the evening;
  • the hotel has a lot of mosquitoes and other insects, but the administration does not take any measures to eliminate them.

Should I go here to rest?

It is safe to say that all tourists should visit Corfu. "Dolphin" - a hotel whose prices do not quite match the quality of service. Despite what he considers a four-star complex, a rare tourist will give him more than three stars. It is worth paying attention to the shortcomings that can seriously ruin your vacation. Those to whom they seemed insignificant will enjoy the hotel. This quiet place will be a good option for families with children, as well as for business people, elderly couples and even friendly companies.
