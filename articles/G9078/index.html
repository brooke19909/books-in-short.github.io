Venezia Hotel 3 * (Rhodes, Greece): hotel description and reviews of tourists

The Greek island of Rhodes is very popular with tourists from all over the world due to its gorgeous beaches, a wide selection of hotels for every taste and budget (from economy class hotels to fashionable establishments), a lot of entertainment, as well as the presence of secluded places where you can enjoy the majesty of the local nature . If you plan to relax in Rhodes in a comfortable hotel in a cozy home environment, then pay attention to the Venezia Hotel 3 *, which will be discussed in our article.





Location

The three-star hotel with the beautiful name ‚ÄúVenice‚Äù is located in the large city of Falirnaki on the island of Rhodes, owned by Greece. It is located almost in the city center, so guests can reach all the tourist infrastructure facilities in a few minutes. Other major cities of the island - Rhodes and Lindos - are 14 and 30 kilometers away from the hotel, respectively. You can get to them by bus (the stop is located opposite the hotel), by taxi or by rented a car. The airport closest to Venezia Hotel 3 * is located in Rhodes, 12 kilometers from the hotel. So, upon arrival at the air harbor, you can literally get to Venice in a quarter of an hour. The peculiarity of this hotel is that it is removed from the beach by a sufficiently large, according to some tourists, distance of one and a half kilometers. However, for most guests this is not a problem, since for them the ability to take short walks is only an advantage.





Description and photo of the hotel

This hotel is located on a fairly large green and well-groomed own territory. It features a restaurant, bars, an outdoor pool with a sun terrace, a children's playground and parking. The housing stock of the Venice Hotel (Rhodes, Greece) is represented by 125 comfortable rooms and apartments. The owners and employees of the hotel were able to create a truly cozy and almost homely atmosphere here. In the restaurant you will be offered a variety of delicious dishes of both national and international cuisine. Experienced bartenders will happily prepare your favorite cocktail for you. On Sundays, theme nights and barbecues with music, songs and dances are held on the restaurant‚Äôs outdoor terrace. Holidays here are perfect for people who come to Greece not only for beach holidays and entertainment, but also for sincere communication with tourists of different nationalities and local residents.

Rooms

The housing stock of Venezia Hotel 3 * is represented by 125 rooms and apartments located in a three-story main building. There are rooms of the following category: standard double, superior double with sea view, double rooms of the category "premium" and "deluxe", junior suites, as well as apartments for four. All rooms have a private bathroom with a hairdryer, fridge, air conditioning, TV, balcony or terrace. Some apartments have a kitchenette.





Prices

The cost of accommodation in this hotel is consistent with the conditions of residence offered here, the level of service and the availability of infrastructure. So, a week-long stay at this hotel in August will cost you from 518 euros (standard double room), from 602 euros (superior double room with sea view), from 861 euros (four-bed apartment with sea view), from 903 euros (double room of the premium category), from 994 (double room of the deluxe category) and from 1064 euros (junior suite for two). Prices shown include breakfast only.

Rhodes Venezia Hotel 3 *: impressions of tourists from Russia

Since today, when choosing a hotel, a lot of people are guided not so much by its description on the websites of tour operators as by the reviews of travelers who stayed in it, we decided to make it easier for you and provide generalized reviews of our compatriots who spent their holidays in Venice on the Greek island of Rhodes. Just note that most tourists, judging by their comments, were quite satisfied with their decision in favor of this hotel. In their opinion, this hotel is consistent with both its category and value. But we will understand everything in detail.





Reviews of Russians about the hotel itself, its territory and location

The territory of Venezia Hotel 3 * our compatriots found quite large and well-groomed. There is an outdoor pool with a sun terrace. In the lobby there is a small shop with everything you need. The hotel itself is very clean and tidy. Since it is located on a hill, from it (especially from the terrace of the restaurant) a gorgeous view of the sea and the surrounding landscape opens. The rooms are small, but it is quite possible to comfortably accommodate. Experienced travelers are advised to take apartments with sea views. Many tourists were pleased with the presence in some rooms of the kitchenette with a set of dishes. There is also a hairdryer in the bathroom. The TV accepts several Russian channels, and also has a DVD player. Air conditioning is available at an extra cost. But the need for this is not always the case. Also, guests note a good cleaning of the rooms and always a smiling, pleasant maid. Sheets and towels are always fresh and clean. The only negative was some guests considered small showers in the bathroom. However, according to them, it is quite possible to adapt to them.

As for the location, the Venezia Hotel 3 * hotel is removed from the sea at a sufficient distance (about one and a half kilometers). So on foot to the nearest beach you can walk slowly for 20-25 minutes. Since many tourists come here with their own cars or rent a car directly in Rhodes, this does not create problems. In addition, you can always, if desired, get to the sea and by taxi.





Travel Comments on Catering and Staff

As for the food at the Venice Hotel (Rhodes), according to the majority of our compatriots, it is of high quality and quite diverse for the three-star level of the hotel. The restaurant always has meat and fish dishes, salads, various snacks and pastries. True, the choice of fruits is not as great as some would like, but you can always pick something up to your taste. On Sundays, a barbecue with music and dancing is organized.

The staff of this hotel deserve special attention. So, all the employees here are members of one large Greek family, to which Venice belongs. So the hotel staff makes every effort to create a family friendly atmosphere. All the staff here are very hospitable, polite, smiling and attentive. However, some of our compatriots were a little embarrassed by the fact that no one at the hotel spoke Russian. However, remembering the school curriculum of the English language, you can quite explain yourself.





Impressions of a beach holiday

According to some tourists, from the Venezia Hotel to the beach to go far (about 25 minutes). However, most travelers knew in advance about this location of the hotel and specifically chose it in order to at least do a little fitness during the holidays. Indeed, both in this hotel and in numerous restaurants in Greece, the food is very tasty, so you run the risk of gaining a few extra pounds in a week or two. Walking to the beach will allow you to burn at least part of the calories acquired during the rest.

The beach strip in Faliraki stretches for four whole kilometers. You can accommodate in any place you like. Sun loungers and parasols are available everywhere at an additional cost. Also on most beaches there is the opportunity to use equipment for water sports.





Conclusion

Summing up, we note that, according to most tourists from the Russian Federation and other countries of the former Soviet Union, the Venezia Hotel 3 * hotel (Rhodes) is a good option for those who want to spend a vacation in a quiet place with a home like atmosphere on the Greek coast. Our compatriots believe that this hotel is fully consistent with its category and price of accommodation.
