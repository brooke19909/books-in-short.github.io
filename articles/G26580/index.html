Dubna (river): rafting, fishing, beaches. City of Dubna

Summer is the favorite season of the year for fishermen, when they can not only take the time of their passion, but also take a break from the bustle of the city, live in a tent, enjoy the surrounding beauty and return home full of strength and energy. If at the same time there will be good fishing, then we can assume that the rest was a success. Fishing on the Dubna River allows you to spend quality time doing what you love.

River Dubna

This river is one of the five largest reservoirs in the Moscow Region along with Klyazma, Moscow, Oka and Pakhra. Being the right tributary of the Volga, it forms an arrow with it of stunning beauty, in which Ratmino is located - the former estate of the Vyazemsky princes. Located in the immediate vicinity of the confluence of the two rivers, Kalitnikovsky Bor, in which old centuries-old pines grow, beautiful beaches with clean sand, excellent fishing places - all this makes travelers want to stay here longer.





Dubna - although the river is not wide (maximum distance of 60 m), is nevertheless known not only to fishermen and lovers of relaxation near the water, but also to kayakers. Throughout the course of the course (167 km), its banks change, then narrow to 10 m, then move away when tributaries flow into it. It winds and spills immediately into two areas - Vladimir and Moscow. Depth also fluctuates: from 2 m at the junction of regions and up to 4 m after entering the Sister tributary.

On this river are located: the city of Dubna, the villages of Berezino, Brykovy Gory, Ratkovo, the village of Verbilki and other small towns.

Dubna (city)

Located on the Volga with natural borders in the form of the Dubna and Sestra rivers, this old fortress in the 12th century was of great strategic importance. The city of Dubna, in which it was built, was once part of the Rostov-Suzdal principality, which at that time belonged to the son of Prince of Kiev Yaroslav the Wise - Vsevolod Pereyaslavsky. From the middle of the 13th century, the fortress passed from Tver to Moscow and back, until it acquired the status of oprichnina under Ivan the Terrible.

Dubna acquired its title of city in the new history at the end of the 50s of the 20th century. An urban settlement of Ivankovo ‚Äã‚Äãwas attached to it. A strategic center for nuclear research appeared here, based on the basis of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Physics.





Since the time of Yaroslav the Wise, there are no antiquities left here, therefore the most recent sights were the Church of the Praise of the Mother of God, built in the Vyazemsky estate in 1827, and the church of the Smolensk Icon of the Mother of God, erected in 1891 in honor of the rescue of the inhabitants from cholera with the help of the saint icons.

Today it is a small provincial town in which the leading scientists of nuclear physicists live and work.

Ice fishing is no less popular here, as ice grows from 50 cm to 80 cm by the end of winter.

Rafting on the Sestra River

Some kayakers prefer Dubna to its largest tributary - the Sestra River. It is quite full-flowing, has several complex rifts, is winding and has a fast course. It is especially suitable for practicing skills for beginners.

If you go kayaking from the place where the river flows into Lake Senezhskoye, then in the area where it flows from it, there is a whole network of fishing ponds and Lake Endless, located in its floodplain. It is small, but the depths are unprecedented, like beauty.

All this distance the Sisters' water remains calm until the first riffles appear behind the Slobodsky bridge.





At the confluence of the Yakhroma River, the current increases significantly, and dangerous areas appear that do not allow kayaks to approach the opposite bank.

Finishing the rafting is best after the confluence of the Sisters with the Dubna River in the Ratminskaya Spit, which is famous for its beauty, clean beaches and fishing.

Dubna Rafting

Rafting along the Dubna River is more suitable for beginners, as it is leisurely for most of its course, although it is very winding and often changes direction. It does not have a level of complexity, so it is better to consider it as a way to relax on the water surrounded by beautiful nature.





The most picturesque places on Dubna are located at the confluence of the Veli River and to the village of Verbilki. These lands are 80% covered with forests that are rich in raspberries, black currants, mushrooms and bird cherry. There are convenient beaches for parking.

A small disturbance of the water begins at the confluence of the Sisters and Dubna, after which it becomes truly full-flowing until it flows into the Volga.

Holiday house on Dubna

Tourists who prefer a comfortable rest on the water, are invited to the recreation center "Ratmino". It is located 4 km from the city of Dubna in a very beautiful place surrounded by relict forest. It offers 2 buildings with 160 beds with modern comfortable rooms.





For active people, basketball and volleyball courts, a football field, a gym and a tennis court are equipped here. Younger guests will find a playground, and their parents will have a dance hall and a billiard room.

Rest in this place can be combined with the treatment and prevention of diseases. In the medical building, clients can undergo a course of electro- and phototherapy, heat and hydrotherapy, manual and mechanical massage.
