Airsoft (Perm)

This is entertainment for real men. It is understood as a military-sports game in which several teams take part. For her, pneumatic copies of military weapons and plastic bullets are used. They beat sensitively, but leave no traces, so the game is conducted on honesty. After the first hit, the fighter puts on a red bandage and goes to the "cemetery", the place of deployment of the killed players.

Airsoft in Perm is only gaining its popularity. Despite the difficulties, it has several advantages:

  • High realism of what is happening. The exact models of military weapons are used, from which you still need to learn how to shoot.
  • Reduced risk of injury. If the firing range is more than 50 meters, then the impact of the ball is not felt as painful.
