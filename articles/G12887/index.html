LCD "Aurora", Krasnodar: location, description of the complex, layout of apartments, photos and reviews of residents

Modern Krasnodar is completely different from the city of Soviet times. Today it is a metropolis and one of the most dynamically developing cities in the country. Its residents have also changed, who are increasingly choosing between a private house and an apartment, preferring the latter option. And all because modern residential complexes have turned into separate micro-districts with their own infrastructure, which allows each resident to be provided with maximum comfort on the territory of the microsocium.

LCD "Aurora" (Krasnodar) - a unique residential complex that has attracted a lot of attention. We will talk about how the project turned out, what advantages it has incorporated, within the framework of this material.

about the project

So, LCD ARS "Aurora" (Krasnodar) is a unique project of an apartment building with author's architecture, being built in the very center of the city. In the region, you certainly will not find a single similar house. The Moscow investment and construction holding, formed by the merger of several major construction companies, led the construction of the Aurora residential complex (Krasnodar), which led to such significant success. Architecture

LCD "Aurora" (Krasnodar) consists of six variable-height block sections from 17 to 24 floors, forming a semi-enclosed circle with a courtyard. A choice of potential residents presented 532 apartments from one to five-room apartments.

The architecture is extremely unusual for the region. The developer opted for the Empire style, which is characterized by the presence of a rich, even luxurious decor: arches, columns, bas-reliefs.

The complex is being built at the intersection of the Festival and Central microdistricts. Location is the main advantage of the project and the Aurora Park residential complex (Krasnodar), which is quite similar to it. The reviews of those who managed to visit the construction site emphasize that this is the best place to live in the southern city. Nearby is the central Red Street, all the most famous attractions. Address "Aurora": Krasnodar, Garage street, house 67.
