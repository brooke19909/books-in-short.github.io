Baubles made of floss - bracelets as a sign of love and friendship

Weaving from threads of a mouline thread as it was, still remains an elegant and beautiful view of needlework. But, unfortunately, today baubles made of mouline thread as friendship bracelets have lost their original meaning. Now in our country they are used rather as a stylish and original accessory. It can be worn by everyone, regardless of age and gender. Although, according to customs, a handmade bauble is presented as a sign of love or friendship. The one who made the bauble must put it on and tie it on the arm of his friend, and only in this case it will bring true success to the wearer.





Baubles made of floss. Direct weaving of baubles

Without a doubt, the most memorable and spectacular have always been baubles made of floss with names. Making this bracelet is not very tricky. The process of such weaving is available to any beginner. Direct weaving of baubles is the most popular and easiest way to make these beautiful jewelry among needlewomen. It is only required to remember a few very simple rules. They consist in the fact that there are only two ways to weave baubles. The first is oblique weaving, the second is direct weaving. Both of these types are made on a lace or frame. Between themselves, they differ in the way they knot and the pattern that will result.

Methods of tying knots of direct and oblique weaving

Very often, it is with the help of direct weaving that baubles with names or words are made.

It differs from the oblique not only in the pattern, but also in the method of tying knots. In oblique weaving, it is necessary to make a normal knot, and in direct - a double knot. Mouline baubles are usually tied as follows. A braid is thrown onto the thread that is braided, which should go into the created loop. After this, you must immediately tie another knot identical to the first. Both must be tightened. After tying a whole series, it is necessary to weave knots with the same thread, but already in the opposite direction. They should be done as if in mirror image.

Mouline baubles by direct weaving

To make baubles of floss using the direct weaving method, you will need to purchase some tools and materials. These are special threads that can be replaced without any problems with threads of iris, pins, since it will be very difficult to do without them if the knot is incorrectly or unsuccessfully tied, and scissors. A simple adhesive tape is also used for mounting purposes. Making these amazing baubles is easy. To get started, take two floss threads of three colors each, tie them into a knot and try to attach to the work surface.



Braid 2-3 centimeters in a pigtail. Holding the second thread, tie it the first 2 times. You should get 2 knots. Next, start tying similar knots on the following threads. Weave a few rows, for example, in red and a couple of rows in white, a few more in black. After that, start again with the first, for example, red. This weaving is simple enough, but to make it very beautiful, you need to make an effort. Try to make the nodules equal and even. If necessary, tighten them. At the end, tighten the threads with a knot. If you followed all the above recommendations, then you should get an amazingly beautiful bauble that will show all the sincerity of your friendship.
