Pensions in the Yaroslavl region: rating of the best, room selection, quality of service, additional services and customer reviews

Pensions in the Yaroslavl region are quite popular among residents of the region. As a rule, they are located outside cities, surrounded by forests, on the banks of water bodies. Tourists with pleasure come here on the weekend or on vacation to escape from everyday worries, city bustle and noise. Consider some motels and pensions in Yaroslavl and the Yaroslavl region.

"Clear Dawns"

This resort is 3 km from Tunoshna. It is only 30 kilometers from Yaroslavl. People with diseases of the endocrine, nervous, musculoskeletal, cardiovascular systems, as well as respiratory and gastrointestinal diseases are taken here. The sanatorium has a well-developed infrastructure, there is an excellent medical base, and comfortable living conditions have been created.

For accommodation, guests are offered rooms of different levels of comfort:

  • "Standard".
  • "Improved".
  • Junior Suite
  • "Suite".
  • "Apartments".

All rooms have modern furniture, bathrooms and showers are equipped. Each room has a TV and additional appliances.

It is important that in this sanatorium there is a diagnostic base, doctors of different profiles work. Vacationers can visit massage rooms, therapeutic baths and mud, various showers, undergo speleotherapy, drink mineral water according to indications.

The complex has a modern spa, a bathhouse and a sauna. This boarding house in the Yaroslavl Region with a pool and buffet serves meals in two spacious dining rooms of the restaurant.

The cost of accommodation, food and treatment is an average of 2300 rubles. per day.

"Forest Tale"

This guesthouse in the Yaroslavl region is located on the outskirts of the city of Myshkin. Therefore, the noise and traffic does not interfere with vacationers. There are rooms for one or two people.

Each room of this guesthouse in the Yaroslavl region has single or double beds, all the necessary equipment and furniture. Hygiene rooms are equipped with toilet and shower.

Guests can arrange meals on their own. The complex has a spacious shared kitchen with all necessary appliances, sets of dishes and a dining area. In addition, there is an inexpensive cafe on site offering a wide selection of dishes.

Active rest in the boarding house of the Yaroslavl region "Forest Fairy Tale" is provided to each guest. It features a gym, fitness center and rooftop swimming pool. Indoor gazebos with barbecue facilities are installed on the territory, there are playgrounds.

The average room rate per day is 1500-2000 rubles.

"Small salts"

This is one of the best resorts in the Yaroslavl region. People come here with diabetes, disorders of the musculoskeletal system, pathologies in the circulatory system. Good infrastructure has been created here. Standard rooms for 2 people and Suites are available for guests.

The territory has a spacious dining room, where high-quality and wholesome meals are organized. The sanatorium occupies a rather large territory.

She is very well-groomed. There are many benches, decorative figures and small fountains. In addition, you can have a picnic in designated areas.

The sanatorium has a lot of treatment rooms with different procedures:

  • Massage.
  • Speleocamera.
  • UHF
  • Exercise therapy.
  • Gym.
  • Aroma room.
  • Mud therapeutic baths, etc.

The resort has an indoor pool, a capsule for skin rejuvenation. Here you can also visit the bathhouse and sauna.

The complex has a small church. Younger visitors can play in the playground in the summer, and spend active time in the games room in the winter.

This pension in the Yaroslavl region for the elderly holds various promotions. The cost of a stay with accommodation, meals and treatment for a pensioner will cost only 2000 rubles per day.

Uglich

The sanatorium is located in the village of Altynovo. It is surrounded by a beautiful forest, the Volga flows nearby. Vacationers here can enjoy the silence and breathe in full fresh air.

Pension guests can stay in rooms for 1-2 people. All the necessary furniture is installed in the rooms, there is a TV, a toilet and showers.

A special pride of the complex is the updated medical building. Here you can visit the hydropathic center and undergo the following procedures:

  • Exercise therapy.
  • Massage.
  • Infrared camera.
  • Pool.
  • To the sauna.
  • Cedar barrel.

In the dining room of the sanatorium, every vacationer is struck by a winter garden. Huge plants grow here, which throughout the year delight visitors with their beauty.

In Uglich, a three-time balanced diet is organized. Here, fresh vegetables, fruits and juices are served throughout the year.

The average cost per day is 2400 rubles.

Blue Stone

This wonderful recreation center is located in the village of Kriushkino near Pereyaslavl-Zalessky. Here, almost all buildings are made of natural wood.

In the pension you can have a picnic with barbecue. There is also a bathhouse and a sauna. Guests can stay in one of four cottages.

The rooms are equipped with modern furniture, TV, a bathroom. The lounges feature soft corners and a small kitchen with a microwave, electric kettle and fridge.

The price includes breakfast. The remaining meals can be ordered at a cafe in the complex. There is also a bathhouse on site. Guests of the recreation center can arrange fishing for themselves, in the warmer months, swim in the pond.

In summer, the beach is equipped here, camping is available. Also at the recreation center there is a cozy small pizzeria.

The average cost of living per day per person is 3,500 rubles.

Saharej

The sanatorium is located in the Nekrasovsky district near the railway station of the same name. The buildings were built in a pine forest on the banks of the picturesque Nerekhta River. The complex consists of five three-story brick residential buildings, a dining room and a house with entertainment facilities.

The resort has indoor and outdoor sports grounds. There is also a video room and games rooms for children, and a billiards room can be used for an additional fee.

Meals are organized in the spacious dining room. The price includes three meals a day. Throughout the year, there is a children's recreation camp for 500 people.

The territory of the complex is ennobled. There are benches and walkways. The average cost per day is 2200 rubles.

"Boars"

The recreation center is located in the village of Goloperovo. This area is very popular not only in the summer. In winter, many tourists come here for skiing.

As accommodation, guests are provided with cozy small houses for 2 and 4 people. They have comfortable beds, other necessary furniture, TV, fridge, microwave, electric kettle and fan.

Each house has a small cozy veranda. There are picnic places with barbecue facilities on the territory of the recreation center. There is also a billiard room.

Meals can be arranged in their cottages or in a small local restaurant. Here you can ride horses and go to the forest for berries and mushrooms.

The average cost of living per day is 3000 rubles. for the cottage.

Positive reviews

Basically, guests leave good comments about the work of sanatoriums and boarding houses in the Yaroslavl region. Vacationers like that most of them are located in a forest zone, so the silence and landscapes around the boarding houses are impressive. Many visitors indicate the friendly attitude of the staff towards customers.

Tourists note that in almost all sanatoriums they are well fed. The food is varied and satisfying. In addition, vacationers write in reviews that the entertainment base in some pensions is at a high level.

For example, in Red Dawns, parents praise the level of equipment in the game room. It has a soft coating and small slides. There is also a small dry pool with colorful balls.

For elderly boarding house of the Yaroslavl region "Forest Fairy Tale" is most suitable for infrastructure and living conditions. Vacationers can stroll along the sidewalks and relax on the benches near the fountains.

In the "Small Salt" clients on the positive side note the treatment base. They like the variety of procedures and the team of doctors. In Uglich, vacationers celebrate an interesting entertainment program, which takes place daily, as well as the opportunity to visit various excursions.

Pensions and rest homes in the Yaroslavl region have many positive qualities, but there are a number of shortcomings.

Negative reviews

Negative comments are very similar in all boarding houses and sanatoriums of the Yaroslavl region. Guests at the complexes complain of poorly functioning plumbing in the Economy or Standard rooms. There are also comments about the insufficient serving sizes in the canteens.

Some customers note that the rooms are damp and cool in the winter, and large companies gather in boarding houses during the holidays, which make noise and disturb other guests. Unfortunately, the administration reacts poorly to such comments.

Concerning the sanatorium "Saharezh" there are double opinions. Visitors note that the buildings have long been in need of repair, but the entertainment base in it is not bad.

Pensions and holiday homes in the Yaroslavl region on average deserve a rating of "4".
