Unknown Crimea - Kacha, reviews about the rest

On the western coast of the peninsula (23 kilometers from Sevastopol) there is a small urban-type settlement that Crimea is so rich in - Kacha. Reviews about the rest at this resort are the most controversial. Many tourists are delighted with the wild nature of this area, the proximity of a large city with military fleets of two powers and affordable prices. Others call this resort a vivid example of the "immortality of Sovdepi" and promise to come here at least once. Which of them is right, let's try to understand this article.



So - the western Crimea, Kacha. Reviews of vacationers here with a minus sign mainly relate to a clean, but, in the literal sense of the word, wild beach. And not because they sunbathe naked here, but because it is completely not equipped for tourists. However, you can go down to the beach by a metal staircase, and the cleanest sand and warm sea can compensate for the absence of sunbeds and a shower. However, another minus of the local beach holiday is that sunbathers need to constantly be wary of landslides and landslides, which, unfortunately, are not uncommon in Kache. Kacha, the reviews of tourists in this regard are unambiguous, it can also surprise with its historical monuments. Not far from the village there is a cave monastery, which rises above the highway (you can go along it to Bakhchisaray). At the entrance to the valley, tourists are met by the Kachinsky Gate, where it is customary to make wishes, which, they say, tend to come true. Behind the gate opens the magnificent Kachi Kalon, in the rocks of which a small church has been created, accommodating only ten to twelve people.

Holidays in Crimea (Kacha is no exception) can be spent in hotels or pensions, as well as in the private sector. However, this particular resort does not have a large selection of hotels. They are, but there are very few of them, and the service there, of course, is not at the highest level. But tourists are always welcome in the private sector. It is located on the territory of four to five streets that are located along the sea and have no more than a hundred houses each. Accommodation prices here are very affordable.

It is possible that the cheapest and easiest vacation offers Kacha (Crimea). The beach, even with landslides, is clean, the sea is warm, the weather is sunny, which means you can get a full beach holiday in the village.
