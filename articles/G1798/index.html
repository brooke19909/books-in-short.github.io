Refinancing of loans of other banks - conditions

The conditions for re-lending in this financial institution are as follows:

  • the initial amount of money should be at least 300 thousand rubles;
  • You can send money to a bank account either online or through numerous payment terminals;
  • the grace period is 55 days;
  • within 30 days, the customer must make a purchase at least once (using a card);
  • to repay the loan, an interest rate of 6-8% is established;
  • the interest rate per year is approximately 19.9-34.9%.

At Tinkoff Bank, an individual interest rate on loans can be set for each client. Its size is considered taking into account previous lending conditions, credit history and other important factors.

Refinancing Details

After the client draws up a card from Tinkoff Bank, he needs to call the support service of this institution and indicate that he wants to refinance the loan received at another bank. In this case, the consultant will give all the necessary recommendations regarding further actions.

Also, an application for refinancing a mortgage at Tinkoff Bank can be filed on the Internet site. However, at the same time, it still makes sense to first speak with representatives of a banking institution to find out how much money can be transferred.

In addition to all of the above, the client is required to provide personal data: F. I. O., card account number. As soon as this information is verified, the process of repayment by Tinkoff Bank of a loan in another bank will start. You will not have to pay any fees for such a service. After that, the client receives approval for re-lending - the opportunity to get a new loan at Tinkoff Bank.

Refinancing Limitations

Re-lending is approved only in the following cases:

  • age from 20 to 65 years;
  • issue of money for a minimum of 90 days, and for 5 years - a maximum;
  • the absence of any delinquencies in previous loans;
  • availability of income certificate, as well as the necessary information about the salary for 6 months. from the last place of work;
  • in general, over the past 5 years, the minimum work experience should be at least 1 year.

A certificate that a person has a permanent job may not be required at all. Moreover, if a person at the time of re-lending is already a client of this financial institution, he is entitled not to provide some documents.
