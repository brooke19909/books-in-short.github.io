The recreation center "Skiff" in Nikolaevka - an ideal beach vacation

The western coast of Crimea is a popular holiday destination, where thousands of tourists from Russia and not only come together annually. A variety of resorts for every taste and budget invite you to spend time on the beach. Budget holiday offers recreation center "Skiff" (Nikolaevka) , located in the southern part of the resort village.



Resort Nikolaevka - an ideal beach vacation

The small but methodically developing resort village of Nikolaevka is famous for its clean beaches and nature. For many kilometers around there are no factories or highways, from large cities in the neighborhood are Simferopol (39 km) and Saki (34 km). Often on weekends and holidays, Simferopol residents choose to rest in Nikolaevka, as this is the closest seaside village from the capital of the republic. In addition, there are many different locations. The sea line of Nikolaevka is one of the longest in the whole Crimea.

The population of Nikolaevka totals about 2800 people. Most of them are agricultural and tourism workers. For guests of the city are open hotels, hotels, recreation centers, boarding houses on the first coastline. The distance to the water does not exceed 50 meters. The private sector and urban infrastructure are well developed. Walking distance to restaurants, cafes, clubs, grocery markets, alcohol markets with local wines, convenience stores, pharmacies. Guests are waiting for a comfortable stay with everything you need.

The main attractions of Nikolaevka are the sea and the promenade. The beaches of Nikolaevka are small pebble, with a gentle entrance. There are no big differences. For relaxation, you can rent umbrellas and deck chairs, air mattresses, children's circles, diving equipment. Guests are provided with a full range of water activities: sea fishing, riding on bananas, scooters, boat and catamaran rental, water slides, etc.

Base "Skiff": location

Tourists sailing along the West coast of Crimea can notice a white rotunda standing on a hill, not far from the central promenade. This is a business card and the main landmark of the recreation center "Skif" (Nikolaevka, Crimea ). The complex consists of several cottages, standing along the sea. Each of them has a wonderful modern number of rooms.





How to get to the base?

You can easily reach your destination by taxi or your own car. The exact address is Chudesnaya Street, house 2. On the base's website you can find a detailed map.

It is also possible to arrive in the village by public transport (for example, bus or minibus from Simferopol). In this case, the guests of the recreation center should go to the Nikolaevka bus stop, the central bus station. It is the terminal on the Simferopol - Nikolaevka highway . The recreation center "Skiff", whose hotels are located at different distances from the sea and from each other, is 1-1.5 km from the station.
