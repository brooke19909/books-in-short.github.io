Huawei (tablet) MediaPad 10 FHD - a great device at an affordable price

Huawei (tablet) MediaPad 10 FHD from the company of the same name is a device that was designed by the manufacturer to compete seriously with models such as the iPad 4 and Acer Iconia Tab A701. Whether he got it or not, we will try to understand this article.

Screen

For the successful sale of many popular tablets, you need to thank high-quality screens, which are suitable not only for Internet surfing, but also for comfortable watching videos, games and reading books. Fortunately, MediaPad 10 FHD was not deprived by the manufacturer of a good display either. Here it has a serious resolution of 1920x1200, standard for such tablets with a 10-inch diagonal and Full HD quality. The image on the screen remains excellent even with significant deviations of the gaze thanks to the IPS-matrix. There is also an automatic brightness adjustment in the device, but it itself, unfortunately, leaves much to be desired. Many will not pay any attention to this, because it is rare to work with a tablet under the bright daylight.





Design and housing

For some reason, many manufacturers have the opinion that it is not necessary to come up with an original design for their devices, because there is already an image brought to the ideal created by Apple. Huawei this time, too, decided not to fantasize much and took a lot from the iPad tablets. However, many potential buyers of MediaPad 10 FHD will only be happy with this fact. Huawei (tablet) MediaPad 10 FHD turned out to be light and thin. Coupled with a catchy, albeit unoriginal, design, this gives him some advantage over competitors. There are not many connectors on the beautiful case of the tablet, since for some reason even the usual Micro-USB and HDMI were not added by the manufacturer. On the top of the device there is a slot for a SIM card (in the tablet Huawei Mediapad 3G) and a microSD slot (available in any version). The left side is empty except for the standard headset jack. Charging port and accessories on the bottom of the case. The volume rocker for a couple with the Power button are located on the right side. On both sides of the case there are cameras - front and rear. When looking at the tablet created by Huawei, none of the above is striking, since all this is very well located.





System and performance

The tablet is controlled, of course, by the Android system, which has been optimized and configured specifically for the Huawei (tablet) MediaPad 10 FHD and therefore works without delay. The performance of the device is at the level, or rather, at the level of the Acer Iconia Tab A701. Everything is fine with playing the video, so people are usually only interested in gaming performance. RAM - standard 1 GB, which is still enough to complete all tasks. The Vivante GS4000 video accelerator does not fail and allows you to get a decent amount of FPS in most games. The processor here is quite powerful - 4-core with a frequency of 1.2 GHz. Its only minus is lack of fame, because of which some games sometimes can not start just because they were not optimized for this architecture. However, most applications still come, and the abundance of entertainment for Android allows you to forget about those that for some reason did not go. Flash memory, depending on the version of the tablet, can be from 8 to 32 GB.

Price

The expectations of many that the tablet will cost inexpensively, unfortunately, have not been confirmed. You will have to pay not much less for it than for the more attractive iPad 4. At the moment, its cost is about 14,000 rubles.





Conclusion

The Huawei Mediapad 10 tablet does not stand out among its competitors in terms of performance, or original appearance, or any unusual functions, but it performs its tasks very well. He launches games, loses video, knows how to read books, with music is also in full order (two powerful speakers), looks very attractive. What else does the buyer need?
