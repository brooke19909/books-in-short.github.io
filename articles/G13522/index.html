Microphone SVEN MK-150: review, connection and reviews

The need to purchase a microphone arises in a person under different circumstances. Someone wants to become, for example, a video blogger, or maybe a microphone is needed for a good and high-quality Skype connection. Also, people who teach online courses via the Internet or simply record training videos often buy a microphone. Naturally, nobody wants to spend too much money on the purchase of a microphone, and everyone is trying to find the optimal and high-quality option for modest money.





It is precisely such a model that today's review will be devoted to - the SVEN MK-150 microphone. As part of the review, we will consider the configuration of the model, its characteristics, reviews, cost, and, of course, we will conduct several tests. Let's get started!

Appearance

The appearance of the microphone clip SVEN MK-150 is quite simple. The case is made entirely of plastic, and the most ordinary. On the front of the microphone there is a small hole through which, in fact, the sound is captured. A little lower is the company logo. It was applied with paint, and most likely, after a few months nothing will remain of it.





At the back of the microphone there is a patch with Velcro, with which the device can be fixed on any hard surface, for example, on the side of a monitor or somewhere on a computer table. You can, of course, attach the Velcro to clothes, but after that it will no longer cling to anything, and you will have to make a replacement from double-sided tape.





If you remove the cover, then under it there will be a small plastic clip that will allow you to attach the microphone to the clothes. It is made right on the case, without any springs and other mechanisms. The principle of fastening is simple - to bend the edge of the clip with one finger and cling it to a T-shirt, shirt, vest, etc. with the other hand. It is not very convenient in fact, since it will not be possible to bend the clip very strongly, otherwise it will simply break and hook the microphone to a thick one the fabric is extremely problematic, as it strives to slip.





And the last thing that remains here is a wire with a pink 3.5 mm plug (almost all microphone plugs are designated this way), which comes out of the case. What can be said about him? The wire is long enough, and for most tasks it will be enough, although among the reviews you can find those whom the length did not suit. The thickness of the wires, of course, does not inspire confidence, but if handled carefully, they will last a long time.

In general, the characteristics are pretty good for a microphone clip, although there are more powerful models, but now is not about that. The type of microphone is electret, which is not surprising. Electret - these are microphones that do not require additional phantom power sources. They can also be safely called the younger versions of condenser microphones.

Connection and setup

So, it's time to tell how to connect a SVEN MK-150 microphone. In general, there is nothing complicated. You need to take the microphone plug and plug it into either the appropriate connector on the motherboard, or into any recording device, such as a voice recorder. In addition to the connector on the system board, the microphone can also be connected through the connector on the front of the computer. It is usually located next to the headphone jack and USB ports.





Now that everything is connected, you can tell how to set up the SVEN MK-150 microphone. Actually, it all depends on the type of device, but as an example we will consider a PC.

So, to get into the microphone settings menu, you need to move the mouse pointer over the speaker icon. It is located in the lower right corner, next to the clock. Then you need to right-click on this icon and select "Recording Devices" from the menu that appears. After that, a small window will open where the connected microphone will be indicated. It is marked with a green tick.





Now, to configure it, you need to right-click on this microphone again and select "Properties". A small window will open with several tabs, where just you can adjust the sensitivity, volume and more. Nothing complicated.





Testing

The test results were pleasantly surprised. The SVEN MK-150 microphone was incredibly sensitive and literally caught all the sounds within a radius of 2-3 meters on the recording. The quality of the final recording is a bit upsetting. There is not a lot of noise, the voice is heard well, but with distortions. A better result can be achieved if you place the microphone directly next to your mouth or as close to it as possible. Then the sound becomes much better, and the voice becomes cleaner, but still does not reach the ideal.

Cost and reviews

Today you can buy such a SVEN MK-150 microphone for absolutely ridiculous money - 90-150 rubles. For this money, this model is perhaps the best solution on the market.





Reviews about the SVEN MK-150 microphone, which, by the way, are quite numerous, show that the device works very well, but not without drawbacks. So, there are 3 main disadvantages that users do not like. The first is a thin cord. The second is build quality. Here, most likely, everyone has different ways, because backlash and creaking are not observed at all. And the third minus is an extremely uncomfortable clip for mounting.

Conclusion





At the end of the review, I would like to summarize. The SVEN MK-150 microphone is a pretty good option for its very modest money. It is unlikely to be suitable for recording sound on a video blog video or some training video, but it is just right for video calling or telephony.
