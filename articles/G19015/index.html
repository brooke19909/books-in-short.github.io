Beeline Internet Settings on Various Devices: Walkthrough

During initial initialization of a phone or smartphone, Beeline Internet settings should be received automatically. But this does not always happen: either the phone is not certified, or the smartphone is new. The reasons can be completely different. In such cases, you have to call the operator. The second solution to this issue is manual configuration of the mobile device. In any case, there is nothing complicated in configuring a smartphone or phone, and each subscriber can handle this task.





Nuances

Before you configure any device to connect to the Internet, you need to make sure of some points. Firstly, does your phone support data transfer. Although rare enough, such devices are now found. Next, your account balance should be positive. Otherwise, the data service is automatically disabled. Do not forget to activate the Internet connection by phone 0611.

Auto tuning

The easiest way is the Beeline Internet settings get automatically. When you turn on the gadget for the first time, the mobile operator‚Äôs network receives information about its model. Further, based on the received data, a search is performed in the database of devices. If the necessary parameters are found, then they are sent to the device. It is enough to accept and save them. In some cases, it is necessary to enter the security code of the device in order for the changes to take effect. It can be found in the instruction manual for the device. But there is one problem. If your smartphone is not certified for use in the Russian Federation, then its settings may not appear in the operator‚Äôs database. A similar situation with a completely new device arises, since the necessary configurations appear late. In this case, there are two solutions: call the operator or enter the necessary parameters manually.





We order from the operator

It is easiest for an untrained subscriber to order Beeline Internet settings from a service center operator. Just dial the short number 0611 and then press the call button. Then, following the instructions of the autoinformer, you need to connect with the operator. You order universal settings that work on all devices without exception.

Testing

After setting up Beeline Internet on your phone done, you need to check the correctness of all performed manipulations. It is recommended to restart it after making changes to the parameters of the mobile device. That is, forcibly turn it off and then turn it on. Next, we activate the data transfer by number 0611, as mentioned earlier. Then we turn on the Internet connection on the smartphone. Phone owners skip this step. Next, launch the browser and enter: google.com in its address bar. After that, click the "Go" button, the start page of the search engine should open. If this does not happen, then we repeat everything again and find the error. In extreme cases, we call 0611 and ask for help from the service center operator, but in most cases this will not be necessary, since the settings come automatically. When a positive result is achieved, we start browsing the Internet.
