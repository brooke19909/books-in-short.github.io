How to get from Krasnodar to Rostov?

A huge number of tourists travel daily from Krasnodar to Rostov and vice versa. Why are they doing this? There may be several reasons!





The main reasons for a trip to Krasnodar

Krasnodar Territory is located directly on the Black Sea. Accordingly, it is famous for its beautiful landscapes, which every person should look at least once in a lifetime. It can be called a unique place for young people. In the summer season, a huge number of large-scale festivals take place here, a huge number of bars and clubs are equipped on its territory. In Krasnodar, you can arrange an interesting excursion for yourself, strolling through the squares, parks and squares. From here it is easy to get to the Black Sea coast.

Main reasons for a trip to Rostov

The city of Rostov is primarily famous for its delicious desserts and aromatic fruits, and you can buy them at a low price. It is mandatory to arrange a guided tour of the crossings. Wall paintings will show the true culture of this glorious city. This place has the largest number of interesting monuments that need special attention.

Why do tourists travel from Krasnodar to Rostov? As it turned out, the possible reasons for this can be listed endlessly. Separately, it is worth talking about which vehicle you can take this fascinating journey.

Method 1 - by car

The distance from Krasnodar to Rostov is only 284 kilometers. This suggests that the distance between the two southern cities can be easily overcome with a personal vehicle. You can overcome it in just 3 hours 30 minutes, provided that the driver will move in the right direction without stopping. In summer, people from all cities come to these two points, so the route is crowded. The time period can increase several times.





Unfortunately, it is impossible to approximately calculate the cost of travel on this type of transport, since gas prices are constantly changing.

There are several alternatives to a personal vehicle. The first is a taxi. The approximate cost of the trip is 5800 rubles. In addition, you can find a companion through the Internet.

Method 2 - by train

The favorite way for locals to move from one point to another is the Swallow Train. It allows you to make a move in the forward direction without transfers from Krasnodar to Rostov. Departure is carried out twice a day - at 7:20 and 18:40. It is worth noting that the total travel time is only 3 hours 15 minutes. The cost of a ticket for one person is about 870 rubles. For children on this type of transport there are favorable discounts. In summer, prices can be significantly higher.



Method 3 - by train

In addition to the train, a train follows from Krasnodar to Rostov. It is the most convenient mode of transport, as moving is carried out at night. A person can fully sleep and relax before a fascinating tour. Unfortunately, there is no direct flight between these two settlements. You can buy a ticket for a passing flight from Moscow, Izhevsk or Tambov.

The ticket price is different, it depends on the type of train and carriage, the minimum price per person is 1600 rubles.

A huge number of tourists travel to Krasnodar and Rostov in the summer, so tickets for them may not be available a month before the expected departure date. It is worth taking note of this fact.

Method 4 - by bus

Four times a day, the bus Krasnodar - Rostov leaves from the main station. This route is one of the most sought after. The first flight leaves quite late at 12:45, and the last at 16:45. This may seem uncomfortable for many passengers, as they need to be in the right place early in the morning. Total travel time is 3 hours 30 minutes. This indicator may also vary depending on traffic congestion. This vehicle is the most economical, the cost of one ticket is 670 rubles. In the summer season, the temperature in this region exceeds 35 degrees. Therefore, it is not the most comfortable option.

Method 5 - by plane





Why do many people prefer to travel by plane? It is fast, reliable and safe! Despite the fact that there are wonderful airports on the territory of Krasnodar and Rostov, there are no direct flights between these two points. This is due to the small distance between them. If you wish, you can try to pick up a flight with transfers. But this option is not profitable both financially and in saving time. Moreover, the cost of such a trip will be about 15 thousand rubles.

Russia is an amazing country in which there are a huge number of interesting, fascinating historical places. Fortunately, long-distance communication between all the cities on its territory is excellent. This suggests that each person has the opportunity to move from one point to another on any convenient vehicle.
