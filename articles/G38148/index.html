How to make a home for Monster High: tips and tricks



Today, Monster High dolls have become very popular among girls , they have pushed into the background even the beloved Barbie. The opinions of parents about such original toys, which are representatives of a practically other world, are divided. However, a huge number of mothers support the interest of their children.

These dolls gained popularity thanks to the cartoon "School of Monsters", and toy manufacturers, having picked up this movement, have been producing beautiful and original dolls for several years. A lot of accessories, furniture and even houses have been developed for them. But if parents can allocate finances for an expensive doll, then with the rest of the equipment it is not so simple. However, there is a way out of this situation: making a Monster High home with your own hands and attaching a daughter to the process, you can extract a lot of positive emotions and experiences.

Home Styling for Monster High

First of all, before you make a home for Monster High, you need to study the peculiar style of the dolls. For inspiration, it is advisable to watch a cartoon, where it is clearly visible how cobwebs are neatly hung on the walls and furniture is laid out, designed in a theme that is not quite familiar in ordinary life. These are tables in the form of coffins, lamps in the form of skulls, spiders as wall decor and other interesting elements.



Home building process

So how to make a home for Monster High? As a frame for the house, an ordinary cardboard box is ideal. To get a house with a roof, you need to put it on a wide side and cut off the closing flaps of the top, from which later it is easy to build partitions for rooms. To do this, pieces of cardboard are glued with strips of paper in all places of contact with the frame. Next, outside the box, mark the windows and exit to the balcony or terrace and cut them with an office knife. The next stage is wall decoration, here self-adhesive film is ideal. This is a very practical material for a children's toy. In which case such walls of the house can be wiped with a damp washcloth. In addition, the sheen of the film will add gloss and glamor to the finish. Bright pink walls with a black cobweb, a dark purple ceiling and a floor on which white fur with a long pile will look great as an original will look original. Outside the house also needs to be pasted over and highlight the window and door frames with a contrasting color. Here you can safely fantasize and experiment, directing the children's flow of ideas in the right direction.

It is worth noting that even children can cope with this task, because making a house for Monster High is very easy, the main thing is to turn on your wit.

Design in detail

For the game, you can make not only housing, it will be interesting to look at the school of monsters themselves. How to make a house has been described above, and by the same principle it is easy to make a frame for an educational institution. School desks, a plaque on the wall, a teacher's desk and a couple of rows of lamps on the ceiling will be appropriate here. Lamps are easy to make from large beads or decorative glass, which is enough just to glue with hot glue in the right place.

Knowing the basics of how to make a home for Monster High, even those who have never done anything like this can handle this venture. It is very important to involve the child in the process, it is his opinion regarding the interior that should be the main one. After all, how pleasant it will be to play in a house that was created to your liking!
