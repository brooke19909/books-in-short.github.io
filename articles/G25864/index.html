How to check the balance on Rostelecom for Internet users through a personal account

My account is an Internet service for Rostelecom subscribers. It allows you to view information on payments, find out account status, change tariff plans, and manage additional services.

So, how to check the balance on Rostelecom for Internet users through a personal account? To do this, you need to perform simple steps. Everyone will cope with them.

  1. Register in a special section of the company‚Äôs website. To register, you need an email address. You can go through this procedure using profiles in the social. networks.
  2. Bind a service. Before you check the balance with Rostelecom, it is imperative that Internet users connect their personal account and the desired service. It is enough to indicate the identification number obtained during the connection and follow the prompts of the system.

Balance check via Sberbank-online and self-service devices

Having stopped delivering bills to most subscribers, Rostelecom made sure that there were enough ways to verify it. Users of the online service from Sberbank at the time of payment can receive information about the current account debt.





Consider how to check the balance of the Internet "Rostelecom" through "Sberbank-online":

  1. You must log in to the Internet banking system by entering the username and password, as well as confirming the operation with a one-time code.
  2. Go to the "Payments and Transfers" section and select the desired service.
  3. Indicate the region and data of the service (login, contract or phone number).
  4. Click Continue.

On the next page you can see the current balance of the personal account and, if necessary, replenish it by the desired amount.

You can always find the help desk number for a specific region on the Rostelecom website.
