DIY shell crafts: a workshop for beginners step by step

Shell crafts will appeal not only to children, but also to adults. Beautiful, embossed shells that differ in shape and size, mentally return to the warm bright summer on the sea coast. We offer to make DIY crafts from shells. The master class is presented in this article.

Preparatory stage





It seems that nature created shells precisely in order to make all kinds of decorations out of them. And while on a sea vacation, in the resort souvenir shops, we can observe a huge number of beautiful crafts. But why buy if you can do it yourself? We will tell you how to make crafts from shells with your own hands. A master class is worth studying after preparing the source of raw materials for creativity:

  • Wash.
  • To get rid of the smell of mollusks, boil shells in salt water.
  • Walk through the sharp corners with sandpaper.
  • Add shine to shells with baby oil or varnish.

How to glue shells

Those who already have experience with shells will be much easier. Beginners will have to learn the correct gluing technology. Only after that you can do crafts from shells with your own hands. Everyone will enjoy the master class.

The shell is glued in several ways:

  1. With a glue gun. The glue is good in that it is transparent, but if it is overheated, it may turn yellow. Be extremely careful when sticking small parts, the glue in the gun is very hot and you can get burned.
  2. Using silicone glue. This method is good for beginners. Silicone dries out for a long time and, if you want to replace a part, you can easily do it.
  3. With the help of "Moment" superglue.

If you need to make a hole in a shell, then use a thin drill. Drill over the protective film. Tape the shell with tape and drill. So you prevent a split.

Crafts from shells for children

A fascinating activity for children is the creation of little animals from shells. To simplify the task, use not clay, but plasticine, moreover, it is much safer. Consider several options for crafts that you can do with children.

Snail

You will need a spiral twisted shell. From plasticine, make all the small details of the parts of the body of the cochlea, the shell will serve instead of the shell. Attach the parts to the sink - and the toy snail is ready. Using the same technology, you can make a bug. Choose a shell for the shell, and blind all the other details from plasticine. Seashell flowers

With the baby, you can do other crafts from shells with your own hands. A master class (flowers, crab and glass) is described below. In stages you can create real masterpieces.

Take a thick colored cardboard. From a plasticine, make a ball and stick to cardboard. Shells will serve as petals for the flower, and the plastic mass will become the core. Attach the shells to the plasticine in a circle. If the shells are of different shapes, then the finished application will look much more interesting. Now the flower needs to be made a stalk. Roll up the sausage and glue it to the cardboard. So you can create a whole flower meadow. To make the application brighter, finished flowers can be painted with paints.





In the same way with a child, you can create a picture with the seabed. Draw the underwater world, and use shells and plasticine to create the inhabitants of the oceans.

Craft from shells "sea crab"

We will make simple crafts from shells with our own hands. The master class (animals, crabs and turtles) is painted in great detail. You can use the finished product for a variety of purposes. For example, decorate your interior or present to friends as a souvenir. The size of the crab depends on which shell you have.

You need to take:

  • Bivalve shell.
  • Cone-shaped shells.
  • Paint.
  • Glue.
  • Red knitting threads.

Cover the shells with paint, preferably in two layers, then the color will be more vivid. It is better to use acrylic. Let the shells dry. Use a marker to draw eyes.





The paws are made of knitting threads. Cut 12 strings of equal length and stick them on the inside of the shell.

Glue cone-shaped shells to the front sections. They will become claws.

The crab is ready! By creating more of them, you can decorate the whole house with them. This animal will become a wonderful talisman or amulet.
