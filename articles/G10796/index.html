Valentines from beads: a master class and a weaving pattern

What are valentines made of? Usually "love letters" are made of chocolate, plain paper and cardboard. Let's try to learn how to make valentines from beads: beautiful and original.

Consider in detail several options for beadwork, highlight the complex and problematic places of work. We hope that using the described techniques, you will be able to make a unique gift message to your loved one.

Valentines by square tourniquet technique

Before you make a Valentine, you will need to prepare the following materials:

  • large-sized beads - for the fulfillment of the heart;
  • beads (any of your choice with which you want to decorate the composition);
  • fishing line (0.20 mm thick);
  • a needle;
  • chain and satin ribbon.

Delicate beaded valentines: master class

To create a romantic and delicate composition, prepare the following materials:

  • medium size beads;
  • glass transparent beads with a diameter of two millimeters;
  • thread or fishing line;
  • connecting rings (to create a keyring or pendant).

Consider how to make a valentine from beads in a slightly different way. The presented master class for a beginner is a bit complicated. However, to cope with it is quite possible on condition of phased implementation, without violating the valentines scheme described in the article.


In the future, after mastering the basic principles and acquiring the skill, it becomes clear where and how you can experiment and add your own elements.

Valentine heart. First half

So let's get started:

  1. The whole composition is braided with one working thread. Start using the diagram from the first link. First, collect three faceted beads and three simple beads, then close the ring.
  2. Next, weave four more links, each of which should contain the same number of beads and beads. Their alternation and sequence should be as follows: 1 bead - 1 bead. Keep alternating. Weave each new link with the previous one.
  3. When the final link is finished, you need to collect one bead and pass it through the bead of the first link.
  4. Compare your progress with the full product outline.
  5. Dial one bead, after one bead and again one bead. With a set of such beads, go through the beads of the previous link. After that, again through the newly woven links.
  6. Weave each new link of the product exactly according to the pattern.

The second half of tender beaded valentines

Weave the second half of the heart in a similar pattern as the first. You will already have the contours of the first half and new links. For convenience, it is better to use a visual diagram.



Weave wings

Wings will add special romanticism to your product. They will need to be made from beads. In work you should have only one thread.

  1. For wings, take large beads. At the base, the wings should have a finer shape than along the edge.
  2. The main part of the wing is woven according to the general pattern.
  3. After the wings are woven into designated areas. To complete the work on the composition you need a chain and rings. Use these decorative details to give the product a complete look.

If you wish, you can make a unique pendant or a unique and romantic keychain from a heart with wings.
