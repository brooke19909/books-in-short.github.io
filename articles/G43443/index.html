Disability

One of the most common insurance cases that customers have to deal with is temporary disability. At the same time, there are times when insurance, as is usually the case, was imposed when applying for a car loan, but the company refuses to pay. In fact, it turns out that they remain within the framework of the law, without violating anything. Therefore, you should know in advance and be prepared for the fact that not in every case you can receive the payment you are counting on, even if, from your point of view, everything necessary has been met.

We will explain this situation in more detail using a specific example. When applying for a car loan in a bank, a traditional insurance policy is imposed on the client in case of illness or death. The contract states that you can rely on an insurance premium in case of illness and temporary disability. When a heart attack occurs with a client, followed by an operation, it is likely that he will be denied payment on completely legal grounds.

The thing is that under the terms of the standard insurance contract, which is concluded in such a case, illness is understood as a health disorder not caused by an accident and diagnosed for the first time after the entry into force of this contract. If the disease due to which the heart attack occurred was first diagnosed before the conclusion of the contract, then this event, unfortunately, is not considered an insured event. Such tricks are used today by most companies operating in this market. Thanks to them, it is possible to minimize the number of payments, so getting a real insurance payment is not so simple.
