Branches of Sberbank in Orel: addresses, opening hours

To visit the office of Sberbank, customers will find out not only the addresses of the branches, but also their operating modes. In each city, branches are located depending on the number of customers, stopping points and convenience for visitors. The eagle is no exception. Branches of Sberbank in Orel are represented by more than 21 branches within the city.

How many branches?

The population of Orel is 350 thousand inhabitants. In this small city for customers of Sberbank there are more than 20 branches in Orel and its regions.

Most of Sberbank's branches are in Orel, the remaining offices are located in territorial centers, for example, the towns of Zmievka and Glazunovka, the cities of Livny and Mtsensk.

The operating hours of Sberbank branches in Orel depends on the size of the office and its location in the city center. More than 89% of branches are open 6 days a week, including Saturday, but there are additional offices in the city that work seven days a week.

Features of the separation of offices in Orel

Most of the offices in the city are located in the central part of Orel, in the Soviet district. These are departments where patency of customers is at least 600 people a day. Smaller offices are located in other areas: North, Railway and Factory.

The largest are considered branches:

  • 8595/018, on the street Komsomolskaya, d.66.
  • 8595/001, st. Brest, d. 12.
  • 8595/010. The office is located on the street. Oktyabrskaya, d. 134.
  • 8595/020. Branch on the street Metallurgists, d. 32.
  • 8595/011 at the address Komsomolskaya St., 356.

These offices are open Monday through Saturday. Full information about the Oryol branch of PJSC Sberbank 8595 Oryol, with a list of all branches and the mode of their operation, is indicated on the bank's website. There is a special section with an interactive map to simplify the search for the right office.

Sberbank in Orel: additional offices with weekend work

In the city of Oryol, Sberbank branches are represented by both branches operating according to the standard schedule and large offices that provide customers with services even on weekends. There are two offices in Oryol that work on weekends.

Addresses of Sberbank branches in Orel that work seven days a week:

  2. St. Metallurgov, 32. The additional office 8595/020 is open seven days a week, from Monday to Friday on a schedule from 08:30 to 19:30, on Saturdays 09: 00-15: 30, on Sunday 09: 30-13: 30.

Mortgage Offices

Sberbank in Orel is represented not only by branches for individuals. The city has special offices that deal with the design and maintenance of mortgages. These are mortgage lending centers (CECs).

Previously, the CEC was located only on Sq. Shchepnoy, but since June 2018 it was divided into 2 blocks:

  1. The first is located at the old address - Schepnaya pl., D.1. In the same building there is a branch for individuals 8595/0102, on the left side of the office. The mortgage support branch is located on the right side of the Sberbank building. To get to the Central Election Commission, you can get off at the Chaika or TsSTI stops.
  2. The new branch is now located at office 8595/003. This is an office on pl. Polikarpova, d. 10. Now you can get a consultation on a mortgage agreement, write a statement to reduce interest rates or conclude an agreement on the transfer of rights at the new address. Stop - cinema "Rodina".

In other branches of Sberbank, clients can also send an application for a mortgage loan or arrange early repayment, but they deal with all issues, including transaction management, only at the indicated CECs. Offices are open Monday through Saturday, Sunday is a day off.

24 hour offices

A feature of Sberbank is the presence of the widest network of branches and terminals in Russia. Almost every office has an ATM or terminal where a client can receive up to 90% of services around the clock.

Near each branch of Sberbank in Orel there is a self-service zone. It is open around the clock for all users. At the entrance to some offices after work (18: 00-19: 00) you will need to use a credit card as a "key".

The zones are equipped with at least one terminal and one ATM for payment of services and cash withdrawals. In addition to belonging to the bank's offices, the terminals are located at stopping points, in medical institutions and shopping centers.

Where to get the service to legal entities?

In Orel, legal entities, including individual entrepreneurs, can become a client of Sberbank. A company representative can open an account or transfer cash at ul. Oktyabrskaya, d. 24. Stop "Sports Palace".

The office for legal entities 8595/017 is open from Monday to Friday, from 09:00 to 18:00. In other branches of Sberbank, legal entities are not serviced. Clients can apply for help to other branches only as an individual, including by transferring funds to the accounts of suppliers and contractors.

Special accounts for candidates for deputies can be opened in several branches, the list of which is constantly changing. It becomes available 30-60 days before the start of the election and is placed on the door of Sberbank offices in Orel.

Premier Service

VIP-level customers can get advice on Sberbank products in an office on ul. Komsomolskaya, d. 189. The Premier zone is located at the base of the branch for individuals 8595/014 (Transmash stop, former Thank you store).

The office is open from Monday to Saturday. Opening hours on Monday: from 09:00 to 19:00. On Saturday, the office receives customers from 08:30 to 15:00.

For special customers, a branch of "Sberbank First", which is located on the street. Saltykova-Shchedrina, d. 23. Here customers can get not just a high-level service, but also a personal offer for themselves and their family members on banking, insurance and investment products.
