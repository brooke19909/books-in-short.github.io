Lush carnation from a napkin: a flower that will not fade

A handmade carnation from a napkin is a rather simple decorative element that can act as a decoration for a festive table, interior of a room or a children's fancy dress.

Such simple crafts are also well suited for conducting classes on children's creativity. Colored carnations (a masterclass from napkins includes literally several stages) are made by teachers of kindergartens and elementary grades, as well as by mothers at home, in order to develop fine motor skills of children's fingers.





Below, we will consider the basic methods for the manufacture of paper flowers imitating carnations.

Necessary tools and materials

First of all, you should prepare the main material from which the clove will be made: thin paper. Layered wipes are best suited. In this case, one-color material of different colors should be chosen. It is not at all necessary to monitor the observance of certainty in the selection of colors. However, a color close to natural will only add to the flower of the glasses.

If it was not possible to find suitable napkins, you can colorize white. This delicate work is best done with watercolors, they will allow you to get smooth color transitions.

A beautiful carnation from a napkin is obtained when the contours of white petals are painted with purple or pink felt-tip pen. To make stems, you need to prepare green napkins.

What else will you need:

  1. Sharp scissors.
  2. Stapler.
  3. Durable thread.
  4. PVA glue.
  5. Wire or wooden skewers.

Depending on where the finished flowers will be placed, it is worth taking care of a basket, a vase or wrapping paper.

How to make cloves from napkins: the easiest way

For such a clove, several napkins are needed: from two to six. Each of them needs to be folded "accordion" and wind in the middle with thread, tape or tape.



It is important not to damage the material (do not cut it with thread). To get the spectacular uneven edges of the cloves, you should cut off both ends of the resulting rectangle with scissors so that sharp teeth come out (as in the photo).



Then it remains only to separate all layers of folded napkins.



Carnation with torn petals

If you tear off a narrow strip of fingers from the edge of all the napkins, and then fold them with an "accordion", bandage and straighten, then the clove from the napkin will have a completely different look. Her petals will be smoother, that is, not so sharp. In addition, this method allows you to complete all the work much faster.

Such a clove from a napkin is very voluminous and magnificent. It can be glued to the gift box with tape or glue, and also attached to a fishing line, thread or stalk.

How to arrange a composition

Carnations can be used as a standalone decor and in combination with other colors. Usually they are planted on the stems and collected in bouquets. The more colors there are in the composition, the more attractive and fuller it is.

As a package, a classic set of wrapping paper and ribbons is suitable. Can be combined with paper flowers containing candy in the core.

Flowers collected in a basket of twigs or paper tubes look very interesting and extravagant. In this case, foam or a floral sponge, which is decorated with green paper, is laid at the bottom. The sharp edges of the stems are fixed in this filler, and empty space is filled with paper and fabric ruffles.
