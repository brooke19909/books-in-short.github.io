Hotel "Bristol", Yalta: description, photos, reviews of tourists

Yalta is beautiful at any time of the year. This small coastal city is famous for its natural beauties as well as historical architectural monuments. In summer it is so pleasant to sunbathe on one of the many beaches and splash around in the gentle waves of the Black Sea. But the cooler season is the perfect time to visit all kinds of excursions and admire the natural and historical beauties. Hotel "Bristol" (Yalta) is just the perfect place that will add many new colors to the treasury of your impressions of the city.

Hotel Description

Hotel "Bristol" (Yalta) is a unique combination of ancient architecture and modern standards of quality service. This is perhaps one of the first hotels based in the city. It has a rich and interesting history, in which such famous names as Rybitskaya, Vorontsova, Ashliman and others sound.

The hotel is located at ul. Roosevelt, 10, is located in the old district of the city. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are attractions such as the Church of St. John Chrysostom, as well as the first Yalta customs. Hotel "Bristol" (Crimea) is 40 and 10 kilometers from the airport and railway station, respectively.
