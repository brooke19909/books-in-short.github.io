Five-star hotel "Movenpic" (Tunisia): luxury and nobility

The five-star Resort Hotel Movenpick, Tunisia (Movenpickt & Marine Sousse) is conveniently located in the heart of Sousse, forty kilometers from the nearest airport. The hotel makes the most favorable impression thanks to the refined and stylish design, luxury and fashion. The rooms and facilities of the hotel are well maintained and shine with cleanliness.





Hotel rooms

In total, the hotel has 618 rooms of various levels: from a standard double to an elite Deluxe room, for every taste and budget.

Each room has a flat-screen satellite TV, air conditioning, a bathroom and a minibar. Superior rooms include bath amenities, bathrobes and tea / coffee makers.

In the Hotel Movenpic (Tunisia) the prices are as follows: a standard double room will cost you about 58 thousand rubles, for one person - about 45 thousand a week with breakfast. Prices are quite affordable, given the "stardom" of the hotel and its relatively recent date of construction - the end of the 2000s. Most hotels in Tunisia have a rather old foundation.





Nutrition

According to the food system of the hotel "Movenpic" (Tunisia) "all inclusive", guests can use three restaurants to choose from:

  1. Sendai. Japanese Restaurant You can treat yourself to freshly prepared sushi. The menu is available in English and French.
  2. La Villa. Dishes prepared on the grill are offered: meat, fish. All the freshest and very tasty. You can order stewed vegetables or a light salad mix.
  3. Mosaique. Mediterranean cuisine and luxury wines. Especially popular is the Spanish paella in deep pans.

Tourists can always have a bite to eat and drink soft drinks in several bars of the hotel, for breakfast buffet dishes are offered.





SPA-center

"Movenpic" (Tunisia) is famous for its luxurious spa, which provides the following services:

  • spa facial and body care;
  • thalassotherapy;
  • sauna;
  • massage rooms;
  • hammam bath;
  • body wraps and beauty treatments;
  • special areas for relaxation;
  • heated seawater pools.

Qualified specialists will select for you an individual program based on a combination of modern technology and ancient traditions of health restoration in the spa of one of the best hotels in the state of Tunisia - "Movenpick". Reviews of vacationers about this Spa-universe are full of enthusiasm and a constant desire to experience amazing procedures again.





Children's holiday

The hotel positions itself as a family, where all conditions for relaxing with children are created. There is a kids club on the territory of the hotel, open from 10 a.m. until late evening - until 10 p.m. You can put the child in the caring hands of caregivers for almost the whole day. The attitude towards children is very gentle and careful: the staff is attentive, tries to occupy the child with games, takes him to a restaurant for lunch and dinner. There is a small playground with a pool.

Hotel Movenpic (Tunisia) offers adult visitors four pools, where you can always find free sunbeds. Very close is a clear, clear sea. Sandy beach with sunbeds.

The five-star Hotel Movenpic undoubtedly deserves your attention and deserves to be called one of the best resort hotels in Tunisia.
