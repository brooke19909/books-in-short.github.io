Coconut Village 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) - overview, features and reviews

Coconut Village is a cozy three-star hotel within walking distance of the famous Patong Beach. It is very popular among tourists - many vacationers who have already visited this hotel once come back again.

Why is this hotel so good? What are its benefits? How high is the level of service? These and many other questions should now be answered.

Location

Patong is a huge beach that stretches 4 kilometers in length. White sand, azure water, exotic vegetation - these are its three main features. And Coconut Village is within walking distance of this paradise.

In general, the location at the hotel is really good. You can quickly get to Patong Pier, a market called ‚ÄúOTOR‚Äù, a local cabaret, Banana Walk and Jungceylon Shopping Center.

The airports of Phuket and Krabi go 25 and 80 kilometers respectively.

When is it better to come?

The holiday season in Phuket reigns year round. But the best time to spend a vacation here is winter.

In December, January and February, the air temperature varies from +30.6 ¬∞ C to +31.4 ¬∞ C. And also at this time there is very little rain, unlike summer. The sea, of course, is warm. In winter, its temperature indicators are in the range of 27-29 degrees.

And in December, January and February you can get on any excursion. In summer, during the rainy season, many routes are closed. In winter, this problem is irrelevant. And excursions here must be ordered, as there are a lot of picturesque places in Phuket.
