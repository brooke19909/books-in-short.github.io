Kurgan: Baden-Baden Hot Springs

The city of Kurgan, located in the Kurgan region is a small province, a peripheral region. There are not so many places of pleasant pastime, but the existing ones function 100 percent. One of the most popular places for relaxation with taste is "Baden-Baden" (hot springs, Kurgan). The world-famous resort has become closer to the inhabitants of the Trans-Urals. A wide variety of entertainment and excellent service: what else is needed for an ideal weekend?

Discovery of the first hot spring in the area

December 2012 was marked by an important event for the entertaining life of the population. On the 8th, a grand opening took place in the city of Kurgan: Baden-Baden (hot spring) hospitably opened its doors for visitors. The large and beautiful pool was the first point for relaxation, gradually additional services were added to the services. Exactly one year later (in December 2013), a complex with a chic steam room was completed, a sauna began to work, and a cafe appeared. The dynamic development and the quality of reception are kept at the highest level from the first day of work.





How to get there

The most convenient way to arrive in Kurgan (hot springs) by car: from the city center along the Tunin highway to the village of Evropeisky. The distance is 17 kilometers. Many travel agencies organize centralized vacation tours.

Thermal pool

The life-giving moisture of mineral water in the thermal pool located in the city of Kurgan, hot springs will sweeten the body and soul with an unforgettable pastime. You can swim at any time of the year. The most impressive vacation is in the winter, when there are snowdrifts and frost around, and vacationers enjoy the hot warmth of healthy water. The size of the large artificial pond is impressive: its area is 225 square meters. It is divided into several zones:

  • children's (depth - half a meter);
  • adult No. 1 (depth - one meter);
  • adult number 2 (depth - one and a half meters).

Additional services:

  • hydromassage installations (in the amount of 6 pieces);
  • air massage installations (in the amount of 2 pieces);
  • waterfall (two);
  • sun loungers and umbrellas;
  • rides (slides for adults and children).

The water temperature is comfortable for staying and in winter is 39-40 degrees, in summer it is maintained at 35-36 degrees.





Bath complex

In the city of Kurgan, whose hot springs attract residents of all nearby villages, it is worthwhile to come only because of the amazing bath complex. The steam room since ancient times is considered the best way to improve the body, relieve stress and improve mood. Be sure to come to the mound. "Baden-Baden" (hot spring) beckons with its variety of opportunities for a pleasant pastime:

  • classic Russian steam room for lovers of a traditional bathhouse;
  • cedar steam bath - especially useful for health and maintaining tone;
  • pool with water of a pleasant temperature;
  • two types of shower: circular and tropical; similar water procedures improve blood circulation and have a massage effect;
  • salt room: is a cozy room with healing salt boulders, decorated with lamps;
  • font with cold water for hardening after the steam room;
  • a cozy sitting area with a TV and audio system;
  • comfortable dressing room with spacious lockers and hooks for bathrobes and towels;
  • a comfortable veranda functions in the summer, serves to relax, there are swings in the territory.

On sale at the recreation center there are always basic necessities: shales, shampoos, birch brooms. Sheets and towels are provided upon request upon request.





Hotel complex and cafe

Hotel "Baden-Baden" has at its disposal 14 comfortable rooms. The atmosphere is relaxing, the raw wood interior conveys the warmth of home hospitality. For newlyweds a special room design is offered. The hotel complex includes:

  • Cafe;
  • billiard area;
  • sitting area with sofas;
  • inventory rental (skates, snow tubing, skis);
  • children's play area.

Also on the territory of the base are two two-story cottages that can be rented. The capacity of each is up to ten people. It offers guests a barbecue area for self-cooking barbecue, grill or barbecue.





Price policy

In the city of Kurgan, Baden-Baden hot springs provide a European level of service. Despite the impeccable service, prices remain at an affordable level for the middle class. An hourly stay is offered (from 200 to 400 rubles per hour). During the holidays, many people want to visit the hot springs in the city of Kurgan; therefore, prices for vacations are significantly increased.

The organizers strive to do everything for the comfort of guests. From the visitors of the complex you can get only rave reviews. There is an established system of promotions and discounts for senior citizens, students and children. On New Year's Eve, a special program with a banquet menu is held.
