Angel Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

Incekum is a small resort on the Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is famous for fine sand, wide shallows and clean mountain air. The length of its recreation area is eight kilometers. The hotels of the village are oriented towards families with children and offer all-inclusive service.

Premium vacation





Angel Beach Hotel 4 * is located in a picturesque valley surrounded by a dense ring of pine forests. Since the municipality is actively developing, new souvenir shops, cafes and eateries appear annually next to the hotel. A few minutes drive from the hotel stretches of ownership of the promenade. It serves as a tourist attraction. From morning till night, incendiary music sounds here. Performers are street performers. Bars and discos open their doors.

For children, playgrounds and pools are provided. Farm stalls are bursting with fresh fruits and vegetables grown in the fields of Turkey. Prices are low and affordable.

How to get there





Angel Beach Hotel 4 * is 100 km from Antalya International Airport. It is on the path leading from Side to Alanya. The journey from the administrative center to Incekum takes two hours. In the season of influx of holidaymakers - 3. Shuttle taxis run from the villages to the resort. Drivers charge 150 rubles. The tariff depends on the distance to the destination.

The federal highway connecting all coastal settlements passes beyond the territory of Angel Beach Hotel 4 *. Vacationers do not interfere with car horns and the buzz of cars passing by. There are practically no attractions in Inzekum, so tourists often visit neighboring Antalya. The city is famous for its rich nightlife, an abundance of shopping and entertainment centers, nightclubs and branded boutiques. They come for leather goods, jewelry and jewelry. Antique salons work.

In Side go for an unforgettable experience. The municipality is fraught with the heritage of past eras. Prices at local eateries are much lower than in Antalya. Even in the afternoon, there are free places on the open terraces.

Key points





In their reviews of Angel Beach Hotel 4 *, customers note the magnificent view that opens from most of the hotel rooms. Since the hotel occupies the first line, from the bedrooms you can see the sea caressing the sandy coast. In the distance loom the outlines of the ancient castle of Alanya. At the disposal of guests are about 80 standard rooms, 25 family apartments and 105 standard suites.

The residential complex is designed to accommodate four hundred travelers. The hotel infrastructure includes an aquazone. It has hot tubs and pools. Angel Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey) has bars and a restaurant. There is a terrace for sunbathing. There are grocery stores, a market and tents selling beach equipment on site.

For children, playgrounds and an animation room are provided. At the reception, which is open 24 hours a day, travelers can use the services of currency exchange, car rental, rental equipment for scuba diving, booking boats and yachts, booking excursions and boat trips.

Angel Beach Hotel 4 * (Alanya) is located three kilometers from the district center. 6 km to the bus station. The local airport in Gazipasa is 30 km from the resort. The standard rooms in the hotel are 25 m¬≤. Family Apartment is 60 m¬≤. Each suite has carpet flooring. Parking spaces for cars are paid. The price of the vouchers includes buffet meals.

Accommodations





The complex was built in 2000. Renovation and renovation of residential premises took place in 2017. The area occupied by the hotel exceeds 4,000 square meters. The institution specializes in organizing family beach vacations.

Registration of guests at the Angel Beach Hotel 4 * (Standard room, Family apartments, Suits) starts at 14:00. If available, visitors are settled as they arrive at the hotel. Usually there is no additional charge for this service, but tips are always welcome. You must leave the room on the day of departure no later than 12:00.

If necessary, luggage can be left in the storage room. The tenants who vacate the room can freely be in the complex and use the service in full. According to the description of Angel Beach Hotel 4 *, the hotel accepts Visa and MasterCard bank cards at the cash desk. The exchange rate is not the most profitable.

The beach is a five minute walk. The length of the coastline, which belongs to the hotel, is 250 meters. Between the buildings of the hotel and the sea lies a highway. Behind it stretched a promenade, after which begins a recreation area. It is equipped with sunbeds, umbrellas and awnings. Towels and mattresses are provided for vacationers.

Positive reviews





Vacationers praised the landscaped hotel area. She is buried in greenery. Well-groomed paths abound in tropical flowers and vegetation. The rooms are small but comfortable and clean. Textiles are snow-white. From the windows of the rooms you can see the sea. It is worth noting that there are few positive opinions about the hotel. All of them date back to 2011.

At that time, the complex was just beginning to receive tourists from Russia. Prior to that, he specialized in serving the European public. At the moment, the responses of the Russians are negative.

Negative reviews

According to Russian tourists, hotel staff refuse to feed guests who arrive after 22:00. They are not even offered sandwiches or fruits. The family rooms have air conditioning, the power of which does not match the size of the apartment. Therefore, the rooms are stuffy and hot. In some suites, the windows were broken. They did not close. Travelers complained about broken mirrors.

There were stains on the carpet. Flakes of paint and plaster streamed from the ceiling. The furniture was old and dilapidated. Mattresses are squeezed and hard. The food in the restaurant was terrible. Potatoes and pasta were served. To them are soy cutlets. The vegetables had a sour taste. There were no desserts. For breakfast, fed eggs and bread.

The beach was enjoyed not only by hotel guests, but also by guests of neighboring complexes. Therefore, in the season, sunbeds and mattresses were not enough for everyone. On this basis, there were quarrels, which sometimes turned into fights.
