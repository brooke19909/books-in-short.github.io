Hotel Grand Yazici Mares Hotel 5 (Turkey / Marmaris)

Description

Grand Yazici Mares Hotel is a luxurious guesthouse located in a picturesque spot in Turkey, just 3 km from the resort town of Marmaris and 90 km from the airport. The hotel was built in 1988 on the first coastline, reconstructed in 2012. Number of rooms

The Grand Yazici Mares Hotel 5 has 440 rooms of different class and category. The smallest room is not less than 28 square meters. m. There are also comfortable rooms for non-smokers and for people with disabilities. Each room has a balcony or a spacious terrace with panoramic views of the outdoor pool or sea.





It offers a free safe, direct dial telephone, central air conditioning, satellite TV with Russian television, a small bar with free still water. A hairdryer, cot and stroller are included.

Nutrition

According to the all-inclusive system in the main restaurant in the form of a buffet. It has an outdoor terrace and offers free wines for lunch and dinner daily. Late breakfast, dinner and diet menus are available.

For free you can order any cocktail or dessert in one of six bars. A'la Carte restaurant invites you to visit, where you can taste Turkish and Italian cuisine.

Beach

A gorgeous sandy beach, 1 km long and with two piers, is a few steps from the Grand Yazici Mares Hotel. All amenities except towels are free to use (bar).





Additional Information

Ten technically equipped conference rooms are located at the Grand Yazici Mares Hotel. Reviews from business travelers regarding business services are only positive. Professional staff will always help in organizing and conducting various celebrations.

Of the free services: two outdoor pools with water slides, sauna, fitness room, Turkish bath, entertaining animation, two tennis courts with a sand cover, aerobics, basketball / volleyball.

For a fee at your service: massage room, spa with procedures, solarium, diving, bike rental.





For children - a separate pool, a development club and a dance floor. For the care and supervision of babies, you can use the help of a nanny.

Digest

The excellent guesthouse Grand Yazici Mares Hotel is recommended by almost all the tourists who rested in this place. According to the guests, the hotel is the best option for a complete and fun holiday in Turkey. Delicious food, clean and tidy rooms, unobtrusive service and polite staff - all this awaits you at the hotel.

The only negative is the presence of a huge number of people, especially during the peak season. Even at this time, the quality of service does not suffer at all. Interesting entertainment, fun animation, an abundance of sports activities and excursion programs add zest to the guesthouse.
