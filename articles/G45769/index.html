Plush yarn is a great choice for knitting amazing things

Recently, soft plush yarn products have become very popular. After all, from it you can knit unusually beautiful blouses, stoles, cardigans, sweaters, baby clothes. Features of the use of plush yarn in knitting

Plush yarn is often used to knit various beautiful accessories for the interior of a house or apartment: covers, blankets, pillows, rugs, rugs and so on.

Plush yarn from popular manufacturers

Plush yarn from companies such as Adelia Sofia, Alise softy, and Nazar are especially popular. The first is an Australian manufacturer, the other two are Turkish firms. Plush products from the threads of these manufacturers are soft, elastic and airy.

The pile length of the Australian "Adelia" is 0.5 centimeters. Therefore, plush products look disheveled and thick. The disadvantage is that products from Adelia fade when washed in hot water. And the pile of the Turkish manufacturer "Alice" is shorter, so things from it are lighter, neater and smoother.

Unusual products are obtained if both of these yarns are combined. This allows you to create unique, original things.
