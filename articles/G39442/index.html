Hotel Batont Garden Resort (Turkey): description, photos and reviews of tourists

For those who want to relax by the sea, in a fabulously beautiful place with amazing nature, Kemer is perfect. This magnificent Turkish resort, located forty kilometers south-west of Antalya, is located at the foot of the Taurus Mountains on the shores of the sea bay. The city is also famous for the fact that it was described by Homer in his Iliad.

Rest in Kemer



To say that it will be extraordinary and intense is to say nothing. Although Kemer is considered a relatively new resort - it is a little over ten years old, but it has already become a fully-formed modern leisure center with chic hotels and nightclubs, excellent restaurants and colorful oriental cafes. The dynamics of nightlife here are so swift that visitors do not have time to visit all the available establishments.

However, tourists in this enchanting beauty of the area are primarily attracted by its natural wealth - secluded coves closed by lush vegetation, almost transparent, turquoise water, pine thickets.

The sea breeze, picking up the aroma of fir trees, creates healing air, and the surrounding rocks with orange groves form a truly unique microclimate. But that's not all: Kemer has great beaches. Many of them have been awarded the Blue Flag for environmental cleanliness. Experienced travelers really like to come here to swim and sunbathe on the beach with the romantic name ‚ÄúMoonlight‚Äù.



The unique climatic conditions provided by the sea and the surrounding mountains make it possible for tourists to almost not feel the high temperatures inherent in this region in high season. Here the sun shines year-round. In summer, the water temperature is twenty-seven, and in winter does not drop below ten degrees.

Kemer Hotels

As you know, the criteria for choosing a hotel for accommodation are different for everyone. For some, an excellent hotel is one that offers impeccable service; for others, the quality and quantity of food is important; for others, the presence of entertainment and a fun atmosphere. At the same time, who, if not the tourists who have visited this resort more than once, will be able to evaluate this or that hotel more real than the others, talk about its advantages and disadvantages.

Kemer hotels have a variety of price categories. There are many comfortable apartments, for example, Asdem Beach 5 *, Catamaran Resort Hotel and others. Four-star hotels such as Festival Tekirova, Ambassador Plaza (Turkey, 4 *), Batont Garden Resort, Hotels Park Claro, etc. are very popular.

In hotels of this category, tourists are offered roomy and well-kept rooms. And although the situation in them may not be as luxurious as in the more elite ones, nevertheless they have a very developed infrastructure and have absolutely everything to be able to have a decent rest. Batont garden resort

Previously, travelers knew this hotel as Visage Luxe, and before that it was called Club Siesta Garden. It is located nine kilometers from Kemer, in the former fishing village of Goynuk. The nearest Antalya Airport is forty-five kilometers from the hotel. Many Russians opt for Batont Garden Resort 4 *, reviews of which are mostly positive, precisely because of the short transfer time. Indeed, for tourists coming to rest with young children, this is especially true.

The hotel is built on a territory of twelve thousand square meters. It consists of three three-story buildings, which are buried in a green garden. Balconies arranged in front of the rooms provide an opportunity to admire not only the sea, but also the majestic mountains, from the slopes of which you can hear a pleasant coniferous aroma.

The hotel area is fully landscaped. Lawns, palm trees, spruce trees, exotic flowers, benches arranged under the crowns of tree branches, litter bins are everywhere, there are signs indicating all the main infrastructure facilities - all this is done to make living as comfortable as possible. The green spaces are monitored by a special staff of gardeners, there is a staff responsible for cleanliness.



In accordance with the norms, the hotel territory is thought out for the quiet movement of tourists in wheelchairs, for which on the ground floor there are several specially equipped rooms with comfortable bathrooms. Elevators are also provided for them. The Batont Garden Resort 4 * hotel has its own free parking with the possibility of parking for large buses. Booking in advance is not required.

Housing stock

Batont Garden Resort Hotel is positioned as average in the number of rooms. It consists of three three-story buildings, perfectly renovated. The housing stock is one hundred and seventy-seven rooms.

171 Standard Rooms measuring 26 square meters for a maximum of four people; six Family Rooms in forty square meters. m, designed for six and consisting of two bedrooms, separated by an interior door. There are also two rooms for guests with disabilities. Each of the latter can accommodate a maximum of two people.

Although recently built, the Batont Garden Resort has nevertheless been overhauled once. The rooms are equipped with a minibar, in which upon check-in there is one and a half liters of bottled water, a TV with six Russian and one music channels, a safe, and a direct dial telephone.



The furniture is brand new. On the floor - carpet. For a comfortable climate in the rooms are installed split air conditioning systems. The Batont Garden Resort Hotel (Kemer) has an electronic card system for opening doors, a fire alarm and a fire extinguishing system. Rooms are cleaned every day. The maids change towels and bed linen three times a week.

Lighting, floor lamps, curtains, bedspreads - all thought out by professional designers. All this creates a special atmosphere of comfort in the rooms. There are also balconies overlooking the pool or the sea. Plastic chairs are installed on them , there is also a dryer for linen. The bathrooms have high-quality ceramic tiles that do not slip, there is a hair dryer, shower and washbasin. All accessories are updated daily.

Nutrition



Hotel Batont Garden Resort offers its guests meals on the concept of "all inclusive", which is valid from ten thirty to ten in the evening. A buffet is served for breakfast (from 07:30 to 09:30), lunch (from 12:30 to 14:00) and dinner (19:00 to 21:00) . In high season, diet meals and a special children's menu are also available.

Sweets and snacks are served from two to five days in the main restaurant, coffee and tea, as well as soft and alcoholic drinks from local producers in one of the three bars - by the pool, in the lobby and on the beach. Freshly squeezed juices are offered for a fee in all places. Some foreign drinks, including champagne and wine, can be bought in the lobby bar. Children under the age of eighteen are not selling alcohol.

For safety reasons, all drinks are served in bars on the beach and pool only in plastic glasses. To get food on the all-inclusive system, residents must wear their wristbands. Depending on the weather in the main restaurant, tables are served either on the terrace or indoors. The menu is European cuisine. The restaurant can simultaneously accommodate about three hundred and fifty people.

Beach

Batont Garden Resort 4 (Kemer) has a small private area in the public bathing area. Its length is one hundred and fifty meters. The coating is sand and gravel, so for a comfortable entry into the water it is better to have special rubber shoes with you. The beach is eight hundred meters from the hotel. For the convenience of its guests, the administration of the Batont Garden Resort hotel at certain times according to the schedule organizes a free shuttle to the sea and back. On the beach on an all-inclusive basis, mattresses, umbrellas and sun loungers are issued free of charge. To get beach towels at the registration desk you need to leave a deposit.

Batont Garden Resort Hotel 4 travel agencies is positioned as a family hotel. And indeed, it provides everything so that the little guests feel good, that they have something to do.

A children's pool is equipped for them, from ten in the morning until six in the evening there is a mini club. Paid entertainment

Batont Garden Resort Hotel 4 offers its customers to visit the spa center, which offers Turkish hammam and sauna. You can also visit the massage room, which is also a paid service. Free entertainment

The Batont Garden Resort Hotel has a fairly large outdoor pool of three hundred and fifty square meters, next to which there is a children's section. Umbrellas, mattresses and chaise lounges arranged around it are very convenient both for pleasant pastime and for sunbathing.

The amphitheater periodically hosts performances and show programs, there is also a fitness group that conducts training on a special outdoor area. Anyone who loves table tennis, bocce or darts can do this for free. In the mornings, a group on water aerobics is engaged by the pool.
