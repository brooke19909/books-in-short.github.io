Hot Springs Pearls in Buryatia: therapeutic effect of waters, tourist tips on how to get there

There is no way to do without official medicine in the treatment of diseases. But in the prevention of the "chronicle", many use unconventional methods. A vivid example is the Hot Springs Pearls in Buryatia. Not only residents of the republic and the Irkutsk region come here, but also people from other regions. The earth is full of rumors - many say that after preventive procedures the pain in the joints decreased, an active life began to bring joy again, sleep and well-being in general improved.

The healing effect of water

It is believed that the thermal water of the hot springs Pearls in Buryatia has no analogues in the world. Its uniqueness is in the combination of carbon dioxide and methane with radon and hydrogen sulfide. Best of course, of course, is not a single procedure, but a ten-day or longer course in a sanatorium.

This prophylaxis is extremely useful for:

  • diseases of the musculoskeletal system;
  • diseases of the musculoskeletal tissue;
  • consequences of injuries;
  • peripheral nervous system diseases;
  • skin and subcutaneous diseases;
  • diseases of the female and male reproductive system.




In general, thermal waters help strengthen immunity, improve well-being. Their high temperature helps to improve blood circulation, the removal of toxins from the body. Water pressure has a massage effect, helps to relax muscles.

Pearl Pools

Some vacationers at the Pearl resorts in Buryatia prefer a shower. This is not only beneficial mineralization, but also massage.

There are two pools on the territory of thermal springs:

  • The first is methane. The average water temperature is 36 degrees above zero. The pool is equipped with two showers.
  • The second is siliceous, carbonic, magnesium-sodium, chloride-hydrocarbonate water. The average temperature is 52-55 degrees.




The Irkut River also flows nearby, where you can not only sunbathe and swim, but also take mud baths.

Features of rest and recreation

It should be noted that there are always a lot of visitors at the Pearl hot springs in Buryatia, especially in the season - these are adults who come to be healthy, and children who are more important here to frolic in the healing waters of the pool. For souls, it is usually a big turn, so unfortunately it will not work for a long time to stand under the massage jets. There will also be difficulties with parking - in the season it is not easy to find a free place for a car.

For visitors, a water park opens in summer. You can also ride camels or horses. There is a shooting range, cafes and shops.

The best weather in Zhemchug (Buryatia) in July and August. Someone prefers to go here in the winter - for the sake of exoticism. People bathe in a hot spring, and the snow around is wonderful!





We also recommend that you do not forget to bring the following with you:

  • Swimsuit.
  • Beach sneakers.
  • Rubber cap for those who are afraid to wet their hair.
  • Bath towel.

Visitor Information

Hot springs Pearls in Buryatia operate around the clock:

  • In winter - 10.00-21.00.
  • In the summer - 8.00-23.00.

The prices are as follows:

  • Shower:
    • A visit for an adult, a child - 150 rubles.
    • A visit for a senior citizen - 100 rubles.
  • Thermal bath:
    • A visit for an adult, a child - 200 rubles.
    • A visit for a senior citizen - 150 rubles.

How to get there

The distance from Irkutsk to the Pearl sanatoriums in Buryatia is 235 km. You can get to the sources in the following ways:

  • On a personal vehicle. Follow the highway Irkutsk-Chita (M55). You will need to go to Kultuk - a place very popular with tourists. Here you can enjoy Lake Baikal and enjoy the freshest local fish. Another interesting attraction is the Circum-Baikal Railway. After passing Kultuk, at the fork, turn right - towards the mountains. You will find yourself on the Kultuk-Mondy highway (A164). You need to advance 94 km to the village of Zhemchug. After passing the sign, follow, without turning, right up to the Kharagun River. After crossing the bridge after the "Tower" sign, turn right. After 3 km you are there!
  • By minibus or bus. You will need the Irkutsk-Nilova Desert route. You will spend about 4.5 hours on the way, the fare is about 500 rubles. Departures at 8.45 on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays. You need to stop at the HSE, and then overcome 3 km on foot.
  • By train or train. You need to find an electric train or a long-distance train to Slyudyanka at the Irkutsk railway station (ticket - 300-500 rubles). In this village, to get to the Pearls, transfer to the Slyudyanka-Kyren bus.




Hot springs Pearls in Buryatia are very popular for their preventive and therapeutic effect. We hope that our tips will help you better plan your vacation.
