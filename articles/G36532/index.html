Microdistrict "Bright" - the best solution when choosing housing

Ufa is the capital of the Republic of Bashkortostan - a city with a population of more than one million people. The Demsky district of this largest economic, scientific and cultural center has accepted the Bright and microdistrict into its bosom. The city is washed by the Belaya River and immersed in the greenery of trees, which creates a favorable ecological environment. Not devoid of picturesque landscapes and the neighborhood "Bright".

Ufa is an industrial city with many enterprises, including the largest oil refining complex. Therefore, its residents appreciate the opportunity to live in the fresh air.





Location of the residential complex

Ufa consists of seven administrative districts, including Demsky. It was on its territory that a building place was allocated. This is a picturesque place surrounded by forest and lakes. You can get to the neighborhood by your own or public transport along the Demskoye Highway, leading directly from the city center.





LCD Description

The Bright neighborhood was designed by Dmitry Vinkelman as the ‚Äúcity of the future", taking into account all the interests and needs of residents. Each quarter, according to the project, should become one of the world capitals - Rome, Tokyo, Amsterdam. The number of storeys of houses will depend on the proximity to the center of development: the closer - the higher and more high-rise buildings. Then the houses will be built in five floors. On the outskirts there will be three-story houses.

From the windows of all apartments will be visible park, lake, forest. The developer designed the Yarkiy microdistrict with closed yards, where it is impossible to drive in cars. Thus, children can safely play in the yard, and parents will not be afraid for the safety of their babies. But car owners will not have to suffer and look for parking places. Each apartment will have a specially equipped parking space. These are the so-called landslide parking. In them, the ground part is given to sports and playgrounds, recreation areas.





Infrastructure and social facilities

Microdistrict "Bright" (Ufa) was conceived as an example of housing construction of the twenty-first century. In addition to residential buildings, his project provides absolutely all the infrastructure necessary for a comfortable life. These are four schools, seven kindergartens, two sports complexes, medical facilities, pharmacies, shopping and entertainment centers, catering establishments, a children's theater, an amphitheater, and a bicycle track.

With such a large-scale construction, the developer did not forget about the main, vital detail - the environmental situation. Therefore, all available green spaces on the territory will be preserved. Since the Yarkiy microdistrict is actually being built on the shore of Lake Kustarevsky, a well-equipped embankment is being equipped. And in the vicinity of the microdistrict, a park is ennobled, where it is good to take a walk in the evening or relax with family on weekends. Moreover, the promenade and the park are connected by a pedestrian zone.

The developer also provided the opportunity to combine leisure and work at the same time, setting up a free Wi-Fi zone in the park, gazebos with sunbeds. In them, residents will be able to retire with a book.

Ecology

It is located in the most favorable ecological region of the city, the "Bright" microdistrict. Reviews of potential buyers still contain doubts. Not far from the residential complex is a railway. However, this does not frighten many citizens. Much more important is the ecological condition of the area. On the left side of the neighborhood is in contact with the lake. From the northern part - with a forest. All this greatly saturates the air with oxygen.

The lakes are cleaned of dirt and debris and are prepared for rest and bathing of the future residents of the complex. They will build piers, equip the beach area, equip it with sun loungers, umbrellas, changing cabins. Yards will be equipped with alpine slides, children's and sports grounds, lawns, bicycle paths. Already, most of what the Bright neighborhood is promising is being confirmed. A photo of the constructed objects is the best proof of this.





There are no analogues of such a residential complex throughout the city. This is a whole mini-city with an autonomous infrastructure, with which it will not be required to travel in order to receive any social services. Houses are built in whole neighborhoods so that the noise of the construction does not interfere with the already settled residents. For true connoisseurs of a calm and comfortable life, there is no better option than the Bright neighborhood.
