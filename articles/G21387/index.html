Arseniev hotels: where to stay for tourists?

Arseniev is a city in the Primorsky Territory, conveniently located near the Sikhote-Alin mountain range. An unusually attractive sight for tourists is the local landscape created by the Arsenyevka River flowing near. Every year during the holidays people come here who want to spend their leisure time among the yew groves, which Primorye is famous for. The city has a wide range of amenities for vacationers. Arseniev hotels are presented in the richest variety. They can find suitable conditions for themselves, both lovers of luxury, and those of visitors who are currently limited in means.

What is attractive vacation in Arseniev

Tourists in this relatively young (founded in 1952) city will undoubtedly be interested in its sights. For lovers of history there is a real expanse: about 40 archaeological sites will allow them to feel the spirit of the ancient sites and settlements of the natives, mysterious hillforts and caves. On Orekhovoy and Kazennoye lakes you can meet an amazing lotus flower, breathe in the aroma of juniper, and marvel at the originality of rare relic plants. You can admire the beauty of the Primorsky Territory from a bird's eye, turning to the services of a local flying club. Urbanists will be pleased to get acquainted with the charming city, with its monuments, picturesque parks and green squares.

Lovers of summer vacations in these places usually enjoy swimming in the river and sunbathing, playing paintball, parachuting. In winter, the surroundings of Arsenyev become a place of pilgrimage for fans of ski slopes, laid just 300 meters from the city. You can stay both in one of the many hotels of Arseniev, and in any of the suburban recreation centers, providing vacationers with a decent level of comfort and service.
