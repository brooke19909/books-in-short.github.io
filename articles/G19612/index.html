Review ASUS K551LN Notebook: description, specifications and reviews

Notebook ASUS K551LN is a representative representative of the universal line from a well-known brand. It is perfect for both work and for extensive entertainment, perfectly coping with the tasks, whether it is office or gaming applications.





In order to cope with multimedia and modern games, the device was equipped with a discrete graphics card from Nvidia represented by the GeForce 840M, fans of typing texas will surely like an ergonomic keyboard, but all those who often travel and travel will appreciate the high-quality metal case.

So, the hero of today's review is the ASUS K551LN-XX522H laptop. Let's try to identify all its advantages, coupled with disadvantages, taking into account the opinions of experts and reviews of ordinary users.

Design

The device looks very attractive, stylish and monolithic. The cover of the ASUS K551LN is made of brushed aluminum in black with an unchanged company logo, while the work surface has a silver tint.

The appearance of the gadget does not claim to be original, and there are no all kinds of inserts, coupled with decorative elements in the design, but it blows notes of grace and elegance due to well-made panels.





If we talk about the practicality of the ASUS K551LN, then everything is thought out - the materials that were used in the design have a low mark, so leaving fingerprints on the device is quite difficult, and even if you succeed, you can erase them without any problems.

The bottom itself and the display frame are made of high-quality plastic. The bottom is equipped with one ventilation grill and two hidden holes, under which there are stereo speakers. The upper part of the display frame is reserved for the webcam and microphone. Dimensions ASUS K551LN-XX522H meet the standards of 15.6-inch models and are 380 x 258 x 23 mm with a weight of 2.2 kg, which is very good for such models.

Display

The base model is equipped with a 15.6-inch display in a glossy style and with a resolution of 1366 by 768 pixels, which, judging by user reviews, is already considered too low. But the designers provided for a more expensive and acceptable to modern standards modification in the face of ASUS K551LN-XX522H with Full-HD-scan and a resolution of 1920 by 1080 pixels, which is good news. The picture in this case turns out to be very worthy and attractive.





Movies are viewed with comfort without delay and twitching, especially since the gadget has a convenient aspect ratio of 16: 9, typical for the ASUS K551LN line. Owner reviews about the matrix are very disappointing: TN-scan significantly narrows the viewing angles of the screen, and why engineers did not install modern IPS technology, one can only guess.

As for the stock of brightness of the display, it is enough for working indoors, but when you go outside, the screen is filled with glare and reflections of sunlight.

The color rendition of the ASUS K551LN turned out to be saturated thanks to Splendid technology, which adds brightness and fullness to the image perception on the screen. Depending on what you are going to do with the laptop, there are several basic modes like standard, game or video.

Sound

The acoustic performance of the device is supported by the proprietary SonicMaster technology and through the pre-installed MaxxAudio application, which can significantly optimize the delivered sound in terms of frequency and depth due to the wide range spread.

The bass speakers do not shine, but still complement the mid and high frequencies, making the sound richer and fuller. The owners in their reviews note that even at maximum volume, the sound is not distorted into hoarseness and cacophony.

Camera

The built-in webcam is not at all pleased with its performance. A small resolution of 0.3 megapixels is enough only for ordinary communication on Skype, although many owners do not need more. For pictures, it is better to use third-party gadgets, the local camera is suitable exclusively for making avatars and more.

Input Devices

Notebook ASUS K551LN-XX522H is equipped with a full-size island-type keyboard AccuType, which complements the digital unit. In principle, there is nothing to complain about: standard size keys, slots are minimal, they are pressed easily and quietly with good feedback.





The layout is standard, but the only thing users complain about in their reviews is the arrow block. Too small buttons, which are difficult to get into, sometimes cause a storm of emotions, especially when the work is on or something else. Nevertheless, after several weeks of work, you begin to get used to such a feature and almost do not notice discomfort.

Touchpad

The manipulator is centered on the main keyboard, and not the entire body. The touchpad is relatively large and made in a modern buttonless style. The response is acceptable, and there are no addiction problems.

Multi-touch gestures are supported in full: horizontal and vertical scrolling, rotation, scaling. The touchpad is designed quite intelligently, because it can be advised as a great alternative to a mouse.

Performance

ASUS K551LN comes with a preinstalled "Windows" eighth version of 64 bits. Depending on the modifications, the models are equipped with different low-voltage processors from Intel: Core i7, i5 or i3.





The device has 6 GB of RAM on board type DDR3-1600, which can be increased to 16 GB if desired. The integrated graphics chip HD Graphics 4400 is responsible for the graphics, and the external discrete GeForce card from Nvidia with 2 GB of memory will help to cope with modern games.

Offline work

The gadget is equipped with a three-section lithium-polymer battery with a capacity of 4,500 mAh (50 Wh). With an average load (watching videos, web surfing) a laptop is enough for about four and a few hours. If you actively play games, the device will not last more than two hours. In general, judging by user reviews, battery life is quite comfortable with many gadget owners.

Summarizing

If we talk about the ratio of price and quality, then the model should appeal to many fans of the brand. Distinctive features of the device are its strong case, good design, productive filling and quite good autonomy.

It has everything you need to work and relax: an ergonomic keyboard, a responsive touchpad, all modern ports and connectors, as well as a lot of space on the hard drive. Maybe the basic screen resolution is not the best option for viewing FullHD-video and games, but thanks to Splendid support, the picture quality is not far from the more expensive options.

The price of ASUS K551LN on large Internet sites ranges from 45,000 rubles.
