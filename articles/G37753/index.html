Description

The Renaissance Hotel (Minsk) consists of one building with 11 floors. Its external design combines airiness and solidity, elegance of forms and harmonious tones. For the convenience of guests traveling by car, the hotel has a two-level parking lot (underground parking for 26 cars, and ground parking, which can accommodate even buses, for 123 cars).

The spacious and stylishly decorated lobby has a 24-hour reception desk. Here you can quickly resolve any issue related to accommodation, food or service. Also in the lobby and on the first floor there is a lobby bar, souvenir shop, currency exchange, SPA salon, wellness club. For organizing business events, the Renaissance Hotel (Minsk) has ten conference rooms of various sizes.
