Description of the recreation center "Allure" (Perm)

Almost every modern person seeks to travel out of town if possible to take a break from the hustle and bustle. Many guest houses offer their guests excellent facilities and services, thanks to which you can relax and forget about everyday problems. The recreation center "Allure" in Perm is in great demand among the townspeople. This is due to its convenient location. Most people choose a weekend tour from Perm for their vacation. After all, the base allows guests to rent houses for the whole weekend, as well as for a longer period. There are all necessary conditions for guests to stay. In addition, the place is often rented for various kinds of celebrations.

General information

The recreation center "Allure" in Perm has a number of rooms in the amount of 35 beds. It is also available to resettle a certain number of people if necessary. On the territory there are several houses with different capacities. The largest building is suitable for the resettlement of 21 people. It has all the necessary conditions and furniture for living. Another house is designed for 8 people. It has a lounge, a small kitchen and karaoke. Guests can rent a separate cottage with two floors for six people. It also has a kitchen, karaoke and all the necessary set of furniture. For the comfort of guests, they are prepared in advance: dishes, various equipment, cleaning and hygiene products, soap, towels and much more.

There is a separate area for barbecue and picnics. The price of accommodation (from 2000 rubles per day) already includes breakfast. For cooking, you can use the kitchen, fridge, microwave oven. Guests have access to a restaurant. All rooms have a TV, shower and toilet. Guest houses are suitable for families, companies or couples. There are several pools at the base, one of which has a beautiful fountain. For children, there is a velodrome and other entertainment. There are several rooms available for celebrations and events.

Where is the base

Base address: Krasny Voskhod village, Novoselskaya street, building - 59.

The base is only 50 kilometers from Perm. The Ust-Kachka River is located three kilometers from the establishment. More details about the route are available from the administrator by phone number of the Allure recreation center in Perm. It can be found directly on the official website.

Holiday Prices

Weekend tour from Perm to the Allure base allows citizens to relax after a working week in the country. One day at the base will cost 2000 rubles per person. Breakfast is already included. You can also come for half a day or three hours. Then the cost of rest varies from 500 to 1500 rubles. Also for guests, many travel agencies offer profitable options for recreation at the base. There are programs for picnics, various celebrations, corporate events, business tourism and many others. You can stop at the base not only on weekends, but also for a longer period.

Why do guests like the recreation center

The recreation center "Allure" (Perm) has become a favorite place for many citizens. They note that there is excellent service and service. In addition to good conditions, guests also praise the staff, they write that the staff is trying very hard and attentive to each guest. Also, reviews about the recreation center "Allure" (Perm) are often associated with the services that are provided at the base. Visitors really like the opportunity to swim in the pools, which are heated. Thanks to this, at any time of the year, you can start the day with swimming in the morning.

Additional features

The recreation center "Allure" (Perm), many newlyweds choose for their most important day. The institution has a banquet hall, which base employees can help decorate for any occasion. A banquet for one person usually costs 2500 rubles. The hall seats about 150 people. Outside check-in is also available for guests. You can use the projector and the necessary musical equipment. The territory of the base is very picturesque, so most often the bride and groom arrange a photo shoot in the open air. The pictures are very effective and original.

Guests can also relax in the sauna complex. It is available to use a shower, a jacuzzi, a steam room (Russian bath), sylvinite stones. The complex accommodates about 35 people. Upholstered furniture, karaoke, light music, a table for Russian billiards are prepared for their relaxation. Visitors can visit neighboring complexes where you can ride horses and take air walks. Also nearby is an ostrich farm where you can see rare birds and animals.

If you wish, you can rent a conference room at the base and conduct seminars. Lovers of an active lifestyle can rent a wide variety of equipment. In summer, fishing enthusiasts can visit the trout ponds located on site. And in winter, skiing and snowmobiling are very relevant. The base also has a pneumatic shooting gallery, laser tag, paintball. For the youngest guests, holidays and animation programs are held.
