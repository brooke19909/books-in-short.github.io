Tower Bay Resort Spa 4 * (Egypt): photos and reviews of tourists

It's no secret that Egypt is especially popular with Russian tourists. Exotic nature, an ancient haze of history, a mysterious culture, a warm sea and high-class service - this is what awaits the traveler in this country. In turn, Sharm el-Sheikh is one of the most popular cities equipped with developed tourist infrastructure. Among the many hotels on the territory of the city, the Bay Bay Resort Spa 4 stands out. Where is this hotel located and what conditions does it offer? What do travelers say about her? Many readers are looking for answers to these questions.

Location of the hotel complex





Where is Tower Bay Resort Spa 4 located? Sharm El Sheikh - this is the place where this large hotel is located. The distance to the beach is only some 100-200 meters. This area is called Tower Bay (which, in fact, the name indicates). The Old Town is 5 km away. Approximately the same distance must be overcome to look at the picturesque Naama Bay. The international airport is a little further - 18 km from the hotel complex.

Description of infrastructure and territory





This hotel complex has a fairly large territory. By the way, it consists of two buildings: the old - Tower Club (located closer to the sea) and the new - New Tower (located on some elevation). To date, the old building does not work. The new part of the hotel is a large long two-story building, built in a classic style.

It is also worth mentioning that the hotel territory itself is well-groomed - here you will find tall palm trees, in the shade of which you can hide from the heat, as well as comfortable walking paths paved with tiles, bright exotic plants and colorful flower beds that delight the eye.

Hotel Tower Bay Resort Spa 4: photo and description of rooms





There are 175 rooms of various categories, namely:

  • 157 standard rooms;
  • 14 suites with a high level of comfort;
  • 4 family rooms.

It is worth saying that all the rooms are bright, spacious and kept clean. Naturally, there is access to a balcony or terrace - by the way, almost every room offers beautiful views of the beach and the sea.





The room is furnished with quality wooden furniture. There is a set of necessary household appliances. For example, air conditioning will help to escape from the heat. You can watch satellite channels on TV, including some Russians. There is also a telephone and a small safe for valuables. There is a minibar in the rooms, but its filling is not included in the price of a standard package of services.

The bathroom is spacious enough. Here you can take a shower. There is also a wall mirror, a sink, a set of towels, bathrobes and hygiene items, and a hairdryer for drying hair. Room cleaning is carried out every day, linen is changed several times a week.

What type of food does the hotel offer?





The luxurious resort complex Tower Bay Resort Spa 4 offers its guests an all-inclusive system. Three times a day buffet is organized on site, serving international and local cuisine. It is worth noting that the menu is quite diverse - snacks, slices, cheese platter, meat and fish dishes, as well as desserts and drinks. The hotel has an Italian restaurant. In addition, you can visit the pizzeria, which serves not only delicious pizza, but also sandwiches and other instant dishes. There are small but cozy bars in the lobby and poolside. You can also visit the hookah - the cost of this pleasure is not so high.

Beach and water recreation

What beach vacation can the Tower Bay Resort Spa 4 hotel complex offer its customers? Sharm el Sheikh, Naama Bay in particular, is famous throughout the world for its huge golden beaches and warm waters of the Red Sea. And the hotel is located on the first coastline - the beach is just a stone's throw from here. True, the resort is located on a certain elevation, but a convenient staircase leads to the sea.





There is a lively coral reef near the shore, so comfortable wooden pontoons are equipped for descent into the sea. The beach here is clean, well-groomed, cleaned every day. It also has comfortable sun loungers and parasols. From morning until night, a bar is open, serving refreshing drinks and light snacks. By the way, this site belongs to the hotel, so outsiders will not bother you here.

It is worth noting that the coral reef attracts scuba diving enthusiasts from all over the world. Here you can rent the necessary equipment. In addition, there is a diving school, where you can get training if you wish.

Naturally, this is not all the opportunities for recreation. The beach is equipped with a volleyball court. You can also ride a boat, canoe, water skiing, catamarans. By the way, a boat trip with a transparent bottom is especially popular. It is possible to rent a yacht. In a word, it is unlikely that anyone will be bored here.

Are there any conditions for living with a child?

Today, many travelers go on vacation with their families. And in such cases, the question of the conditions for children's recreation become extremely important. So what can the Tower Bay Resort Spa 4 have to offer?

To begin with, it is worth noting that there is a small pool for children of the appropriate depth. Children can have fun on the water slide, which works every day. There is also a playroom where you can leave your baby for a while. And in the mini-club experienced educators and animators are happy to do art with children, arrange fun games, competitions and performances.

As for comfort issues, a crib will be brought to your room upon request. There is no special children's menu here, but among the abundance of food you will surely find something that will suit your child.

Additional service on site

Are there any additional services that the Tower Bay Resort Spa 4 can offer? Sharm El Sheikh is a city famous for its hospitality. Naturally, they tried to create all the conditions for a really good and comfortable pastime.

The hotel has its own small parking lot, which is constantly guarded. By the way, here you can rent a car or other type of transport at a relatively affordable price - this will greatly facilitate the movement around the city and its environs.

Naturally, there is a laundry room, for the services of which you have to pay separately. If necessary, you can use the safe at the reception. There is also internet access and a small business center. The hotel offers guests the services of experienced photographers who will help you capture the interesting moments of your vacation forever.

Leisure and entertainment for travelers





What to do for guests of Tower Bay Resort Spa? In fact, the choice of entertainment here is really big. Many tourists prefer relaxing by the pool - there are, by the way, two of them. Water aerobics classes are often held in the mornings, and water polo competitions are organized in the daytime. Around the pools in sufficient numbers are loungers with comfortable mattresses, as well as umbrellas. Here you can take a clean towel.

The hotel has sports grounds for volleyball, basketball, as well as futsal and mini-golf, which will surely please fans of outdoor activities. If you are one of those travelers who want to keep fit, the hotel invites you to visit the local fitness club and gym, full of modern equipment. You should definitely go for a massage at least once, as the hotel has exceptionally good specialists. There is also a games room where you can play darts, table tennis, billiards or just spend the evening in good company. In the evenings, guests are invited to dances and discos, so you won‚Äôt be bored.

As in any other good hotel, animators work here - local and Italian troupes perfectly entertain guests. Throughout the day, they organize various competitions and sporting events, and in the evenings you will find bright shows, oriental dances and interesting performances. By the way, performances are often held at the local amphitheater, which gives the event even more charm.

What else can the Tower Bay Resort Spa 4 have to offer? Egypt is an ideal place for a rich excursion vacation. Therefore, if you like not just relaxing, but also traveling around the cities and getting to know the culture of the country, you can use the services of a tour desk - they will help you choose and purchase a suitable tour.

Tower Bay Resort Spa 4 Hotel: traveler reviews

Of course, travelers are often interested in the opinions of those people who have already managed to use the services of various hotels. So what do tourists say about the Tower Bay Resort Spa?

Reviews are for the most part positive. The rooms are really spacious, cleaned every day. Hotel workers are polite and welcoming, always ready to help, and any problems that arise are quickly resolved at the reception.

The undoubted advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the sea and its private beach. Fans of excursions and night clubs will not have to be bored, as they are also located in the reach zone. The food is pretty good - the dishes are varied, fresh and hearty. This hotel complex is perfect for a relaxing holiday by the sea.
