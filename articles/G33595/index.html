Hotel "Podmoskovye", Podolsk: address, description of rooms

The city of Podolsk was founded almost 4 centuries ago and was the patrimony of the Danilov Monastery, located in Moscow. It all started as a small settlement, which grew rapidly. This was confirmed by the decree of Catherine II, in which Podolsk was awarded the title of city. The railway laid in 1866 served as the basis for economic development: two of the largest factories were built here in a short time: the cement factory and the Singer, where famous sewing machines were produced.

Location

Hotel Podmoskovye (Podolsk) is located 1.5 km from the city center. It is a 25-minute drive to Domodedovo and Vnukovo Airports, the same number to Bitsa Forest Park, Krylatskoye Sports Complex, St. Basil's Cathedral and other important sights. From the train station to the hotel a very short distance - only 3 km.

The nearest station is Paveletsky (35 km), to Kievsky and Kursky 36 and 38 km respectively.

In walking distance - Ice Palace, DC "October", the stadium "Trud", many cafes, shops, including the shopping center "Gallery".

You can get to the hotel by any transport. Those traveling from the capital can take minibuses: the road from the Anino or Yuzhnaya metro stations to Podolsk takes only 40 minutes. And it costs quite inexpensively - 65 rubles.
