Prepayment is due:

  • Cash at the cash desk of the wellness center. In this case, the client receives a cashier's check and a memo indicating the date, time and number of the sauna.
  • By bank transfer through the terminal from the card. The client receives a check of the terminal and a memo indicating the date, time and number of the sauna.

With the loss of a memo or a check, the institution does not refuse to provide the client with services if confirmation of his data is possible.
