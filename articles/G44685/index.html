Interesting ideas for an autumn photo shoot in nature

Sometimes it seems that autumn is a time to feel sad and shut up in an apartment, wrapped in a warm woolen blanket with a book or tea in hand. But do not look at this sometimes rainy and gloomy season so one-sided. After all, the classic called this weather "eyes charm." Golden autumn captivates, calls to freeze, stop, look around and capture this feeling of fading ("magnificent nature wilting") and at the same time waiting for a revival. Therefore, a hand reaches for the camera (or the phone of a friend of the photographer), and legs on a sunny (or not so) day off just run to the forest, to the park, to the river, to nature.





Everything is in nature!

Perhaps the best time to go to the forest with the whole family and create wonderful photos in the family archive is one of the days off in September or early October, when the trees are already yellow, but the foliage has not fallen. An excellent occasion to jog in the park, ride a family on bicycles along the new route, go fishing or pick mushrooms in the forest. The main thing - do not forget to bring a charged camera, a good mood and a variety of ideas for the autumn photo shoot in nature.





Do not forget to take the props

You can think in advance what will come in handy for the autumn photo shoot.

  • You can take a bright umbrella with you - perhaps the most autumnal attribute. Then even the rain does not hurt to make you a couple of cool photos.
  • Great shots can be made using the picture frame. It is good if it is aged or, conversely, in the modern style.
  • Homemade swing on a tree will give a lot of joy and wonderful pictures. They can be decorated with foliage or berries.
  • An excellent option would be to arrange a canopy of tulle over a colorful blanket of fallen leaves. Or a plaid captured from home.
  • Perhaps the main ‚Äúattribute‚Äù of the golden autumn is fallen foliage. You can throw it, weave wreaths from it, wallow on it, make bouquets from it.   • Even the image of a forest nymph can be created with the help of professional makeup and paraphernalia, on which the autumn forest is so generous.
  • Grab a basket of autumn fruits. Pumpkins look very harmonious in the frame.




How to choose a place

When choosing a beautiful view for a photo, you can use the following ideas. For a family autumn photo shoot in nature, a direct path in a forest or park, framed by trees with golden foliage, is well suited. If you went for mushrooms, you should brag to the camera about your "catch", because it will be eaten all soon. On a bike ride, you can take a dynamic series of fun shots. A beautiful fallen tree can become a traveler not only a temporary refuge, but also a great decoration.

An interestingly located shop in a neighboring park will help to realize your ideas.

For an autumn photo shoot in nature, you can use a place near a reservoir. Well, if trees grow close to the water's edge. The fog that forms in the morning will add mystery to the photographs and give the photographer ample opportunity to play with the morning light.

If you really have no time to get out of the city, then choose a place where in the background there will be red-yellow foliage. This photo can also be safely called autumn.





Good results are obtained in the field, among tall grass or against the background of distant mountains / river / bridge / castle / forest.

If the frame is the expectant mother

Photos of mothers with tummies are perhaps one of the most touching pictures. As a rule, expectant mothers prepare carefully for such photo sessions.





So, what ideas for an autumn photo shoot on the nature of pregnant women can be used:

  • You can take a photo of the tummy itself, decorated with various details, with a drawing printed on it, an inscription on the clothes.
  • Photos with a husband and / or an older child are good. At the same time, the attributes and ideas described above will be useful for the autumn photo shoot in nature.

Eye charm

Do not miss the fall, capture it not only in your memory, but also in photographs. Enrich your photo collection with golden photographs and wonderful memories of a good day.
