General concepts

By law, every owner of a car must have insurance. Without a policy it is impossible to register a vehicle (vehicle) in the traffic police and use it. The absence of compulsory motor third-party liability insurance entails penalties and even a ban on the operation of cars. With the policy, the owner receives the right to compensation for material damage to other participants in the accident. The number of insured events is not limited.

DPS officers today have the opportunity to verify the validity of the policy through the IMTS of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Russian Federation or through the database of Russian car insurers. And since the data on the insured is entered almost simultaneously with payment, then patrol traffic police are not allowed to prosecute the driver, according to part 2 of art. 12.3 Administrative Code.

Electronic insurance policy at Alfa Insurance (EOSAGO), as in any other company, it is calculated by multiplying the base rate by various individual coefficients. These may include the region of registration, engine power, the period of use of the vehicle, the age and length of service of the driver, etc.





What is CTP for?

Firstly, it is saving your own money in the event of an accident. For the policy enables the insurance holder to compensate without loss the damage caused to the driver, property or vehicle injured in the accident.

Secondly, the OSAGO system is a civilized way of calculating between the parties to a road conflict.

If there is no policy, you will have to cover all expenses associated with an accident from your wallet (and the amounts are usually rather large).

There are three scenarios in the event of an accident.

  1. If, due to the fault of the insured in Alfa Insurance, harm is caused to the health and / or car of another driver, then the company pays the amount indicated by the experts after evaluating the expert commission. The culprit repairs the personal vehicle at its own expense.

  3. If both drivers are to blame, then each court determines the measure of each, and AlfaStrakhovanie calculates the ratio of the damage suffered in monetary terms and makes payments.
