Which hotel to choose: Greece, Crete

In Europe, it is easy to choose the right hotel, in accordance with the needs and financial possibilities. Greece (Crete in particular) there is no exception to this issue. On the contrary, there are much more hotels on the resort island. You can book a chic room in a deluxe hotel, settle in a comfortable “four” or “three rubles”, rent a house, apartment or even a room. If you rented a car, then you can find a very convenient and cozy shelter in a family hotel in some mountain village.



About nutrition

What can I say, based on reviews of hotels in Greece? Crete is not the place to look for an all-inclusive hotel. The fact is that such a menu will, of course, not be scarce, but very monotonous and designed for ... the average American. That is, french fries, steaks, one kind of fish. Meanwhile, going outside the hotel, you will certainly want to try the appetizing-smelling dishes of Greek cuisine, which are served in numerous taverns and fish restaurants.

Greece, Crete Green Hotels

Buying a ticket, you are likely to find yourself on the north or east coast of the island. The majority of “untwisted” hotels are concentrated there, often unreasonably expensive. If you yourself are looking for shelter through the system of "Bucking" or "Hotels com", then pay attention to the south and west. It is impossible to understand a country without visiting its outback. Live at least a couple of days in a quiet fishing village or in a town on a mountain balcony. Ierapetra and Makriyalos, Tsutsuros and Kutsunari, Matala, Agia Galini, Kalamaki, Plakias, Paleochora, Sugiya and Loutro are waiting for fans of eco-tourism.



Youth Hotel - Greece, Crete

The city hotels of Hersonissos are at the epicenter of nightlife. Fun here subsides only in the winter months. But behind the noisy beach parties you should still go to the town of Rethymnon, as well as to such popular resorts as Georgioupolis, Panormo, Stalida, Platanias, Malia and Amoudara. There is no shortage of entertainment in Heraklion, Chania and Lasithi. For "poor students" recommend very budget hotels class C (correspond to the continental “2 stars”): “Adele Beach” in the village of the same name, “Aliantos” in Plakya, “Trefon Hotel-Apartments” in the vicinity of Rethymno.

Chic hotel

Greece (Crete in particular) is interesting for its luxurious tourist complexes. Most deluxe hotels (corresponding to 5 stars) are located in the north and east of the island. For glamorous girls and oil moneybags, we recommend Elounda - the place of the secular party of celebrities around the world. Coastal city Agios Nikolaos is also not in vain called the Cretan St. Tropez. A luxury resort for the elite is considered a small, lying in a secluded bay, Agia Pelagia. Fans of chain hotels can offer the Grecotel chain. It is widely represented on the coasts of the island. This and the “Creta Palace Deluxe” and “Grekotel Ritimna Beach”, many others.



Holidays with the whole family

You will find a quiet and relaxing holiday on a clean beach by the shallow sea in Milatos, Anissaras, Sissi, Skale, Almirida, Analipsis, Kisamos and other resorts. Here you can find a typical family hotel for these resorts . Greece (Crete and other islands) has good sandy beaches. And if your child is no longer satisfied with building castles by the water, but wants active games and pleasures, there is nothing better than settling near Chania, in the village of Kokkini-Khani. The local Water City water park will delight not only small guests, but also their parents.
