How to make holes and scrapes on jeans. The answer is here!

But the problem is that there is no way to take them down! Give your jeans a second chance!

What can be done with jeans that are out of fashion? Yes, anything! Neat holes, scrapes, various applications and, finally, they can simply be altered into a wonderful bag, skirt or shorts.
