Amazing Greece. Rhodes. Unrivaled attractions

A country like Greece will not leave anyone indifferent. Rhodes, whose attractions fascinate every person, is the true pearl of this Mediterranean paradise. One of the most beautiful islands, it is completely saturated with the sun and history, filled with luxury and magnificence. According to legend, Zeus ordered to pick up a piece of land from the depths of the sea, which he presented to the sun god. And Rhodes, dedicated to Helios, and the truth is sunny - 310 days a year the daylight generously sends its rays to the earth.



The whole of Greece is rightfully proud of the island . Rhodes, whose attractions are multifaceted and diverse, attracts tourists from all over the planet. The statue of Colossus of Rhodes, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, brought world fame to this place. Neither the exact dimensions nor the exact appearance of the monument are known today. Different authors give different descriptions of the monument, but it is known that its dimensions were really huge. The statue of God, which towered around the main port of the island, died as a result of an earthquake and rests somewhere at the bottom of the sea.

But not only the lost miracle is proud of Greece, the island of Rhodes. Its sights are also nature, which is truly divine. She is so beautiful, as was the nymph Rhodes, lover and wife of Helios. There are many bays, gorgeous beaches, the azure sea, a unique valley of butterflies and an abundance of flora and fauna. It is not for nothing that UNESCO declared the island an object of world significance and carefully protects it.



Greece invites everyone to visit the fashionable resort. Rhodes, whose sights are amazing, is located in the Aegean Sea. Ten miles separate the island from the Turkish coast, and this previously allowed it to be a kind of bridge between the East and the West. At different times, it was owned by the Greeks, Phoenicians, Saracens, Persians, Genoese, Turks and Crusaders. They left their traces here.

Combining a fertile climate, ancient history and conditions for an excellent holiday, Greece is a real tourist mecca. Rhodes attractions (its map is literally filled with interesting places) offers a variety of attractions for viewing, and therefore attracts celebrities and ordinary travelers. Here the most expensive real estate and the highest prices for services. However, rest on the island can not fail!

How beautiful Greece is! Rhodes, whose sights have long been popular, is visited by hundreds of tourists, and everyone is eager to photograph everything as a keepsake. You can not refuse to tour the fabulous medieval city - the capital of the island. Mighty walls, narrow streets that have retained their original appearance, the amazing palace of the Grand Masters of the Order of the John of the sixteenth century, mysterious dungeons - this is where you can shoot any historical picture!



But at the same time, Rhodes is a modern resort where life is in full swing, there are first-class food and entertainment facilities, shopping centers, hotels. You can get here by air (the airport is located sixteen kilometers from the island capital), as well as by water, while enjoying a magnificent picture of the wine-dark, as Homer said, of the Mediterranean Sea.
