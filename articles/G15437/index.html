Hai Au Seagull Resort 3 * (Vietnam): tourist reviews, photos

The beach hotel Hai Au Seagull Resort 3 * is located in the Phan Thiet resort in Vietnam, but closer to Mui Ne Village. No wonder the hotel is popular with honeymooners choosing a cozy nest for their honeymoon. Recently, the hotel has become fashionable not only among Vietnamese, but also among foreign tourists, including our compatriots. Hai Au Seagull Resort 3 *, the photo of which you see as an illustration of the article, has a good reputation among Russian travelers. Excursions

One of the local attractions - Fey Creek - can be explored very nearby. To do this, you need to leave the hotel, go towards Mui Ne to the source. This is the place where the stream flows into the sea. It is noticeable by the way the water turns yellow. And then you need to follow right along the stream. A beautiful waterfall awaits you there. You can rent a bike for five dollars a day and ride around. Organized trips are best bought on the street. Many recommend a trip to Dalat, especially for those who like to walk in the mountains. One of the most popular long-distance excursions is a two-day trip called the Emerald Triangle. There you can explore the architectural complex of the ancient Vietnamese civilization "Cham", visit the thermal baths, as well as visit the islands, swim in the ocean and even go diving. You are waiting for a dinner on the ship, prepared from freshly caught fish, amazing Buddhist temples and other adventures.
