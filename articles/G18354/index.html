Holiday Park Hotel (Bulgaria): traveler reviews

A large number of modern tourists choose holidays on the Bulgarian coast. Bulgaria welcomes guests from all over the world with luxurious resorts and hotels. But where is the best place to stay? Such a question torments every traveler who decided to purchase a ticket to the Bulgarian Black Sea coast. Despite the variety of hotels, many choose the Holiday Park hotel. Bulgaria is a favorite resort for many Russians. Many of our compatriots stay in this hotel.





A bit about the resort

Holiday Park (Bulgaria) is located in one of the largest resorts in the country - Golden Sands. It is located near the city of Varna, in the territory of the famous reserve. The resort got its name several hundred years ago. According to local residents, the pirates buried their treasures in the sands of the coast, since then the resort is called the "Golden Sands". In addition, the sand here is considered the finest and cleanest on the entire coast. The beautiful beach stretches for several kilometers, on the territory you can find entertainment for every taste - water skiing and motorbikes, boat trips, beach volleyball, diving and much more.

Holiday Park (Bulgaria). Location

The hotel in Golden Sands was built relatively recently. This modern hotel is located in the heart of the park with lush vegetation and a flower garden. The proximity of the sea pleases the guests of the resort, the sea breeze refreshes, and the picturesque landscape of sandy beaches appeal to lovers of natural beauty.





Hotel Description

Holiday Park (Bulgaria) was built in 2003. Its area is about 6000 square meters. m, which, of course, is not enough for the scope of rest of the Russian tourist. Many vacationers complain about the small territory of the hotel. By the way, the hotel building was recently renovated: in 2010 all work was completed, so Holiday Park can be considered quite new compared to others.

The hotel building consists of 5 floors, which are located 145 rooms. Unfortunately, the hotel does not have a single room with a sea view, but from the balconies of the rooms of this cozy hotel you can enjoy a luxurious park with green spaces and a swimming pool.

Most often, Holiday Park Hotel (Bulgaria) is vacationing with entire families. The hotel is equipped with everything necessary for families with children.





Description of rooms

All hotel rooms are divided into two types: Standart Room and Studio. Most often, tourists stay in rooms of the first category, this is due to their number - 109 pcs. The standard room has two double beds and a pull-out armchair. As a rule, two tourists occupy this area. Maximum room size is 20 square meters. m. In studios, which are slightly smaller - only 36 rooms, tourists with children more often stay. It can accommodate four, the room has two single beds, two extendable chairs, as well as a kitchenette with sink, stove, coffee maker and all the necessary appliances. Hotel Attractions

Each hotel tries to offer the most comfortable pastime within its walls. Most often, hotels have their own gym, sauna and cafe. Such a standard set of services is also available in Holiday Park 4 * (Bulgaria). Reviews of tourists about the hotel‚Äôs entertainment program are mostly positive. The only thing that confuses many is that almost all services are paid. Free of charge in Holiday Park you can play table tennis and visit the fitness center, which is located in the main building of the hotel. As for the massage, sauna, gym, jacuzzi, SPA center and car rental, you have to pay extra for this.

In addition, at the reception you can use the safe. If you urgently need medical attention, the doctor will certainly provide it, but for an additional fee. Laundry, shuttle bass, wireless Internet, currency exchange - also for money. Tourists who had a rest in the "Holiday Park" note that because of the paid Wi-Fi, I had to use the Internet in the lobby bar, where this service is free for vacationers.

As for the hotel, it is small, there are umbrellas and sun loungers near the pool. The only thing vacationers need to go a little. In their impressions of the rest, tourists recall with displeasure the path to the beach, which ran just past landfills with unpleasant odors.





Holiday Park - Family Hotel

Holiday Park is most often chosen by tourists with children. For the rest of the kids at the hotel everything is provided - there is a children's pool and a playground for kids. The pool in the "Holiday Park" is unheated, so little tourists need to go swimming with caution. A baby cot is available upon request for free.

As for the hotel restaurant, even there you can comfortably relax with the children. High chairs and a children's menu will allow kids to feel like full travelers, who can have breakfast and lunch along with adults.

In addition, for children there is additional entertainment in the water park, which is nearby. Tourists are satisfied, noting the optimal combination of price and quality of rest.

Tourists note the quality of food positively, the only one, the assortment of breakfast is often poor. But you can close your eyes to all these unpleasant moments, because at lunch and dinner any non-alcoholic drinks can be taken in unlimited quantities.

Most Golden Sands guests choose Holiday Park as a temporary stop. Thanks to the positive feedback from tourists, more and more travelers from all over the world come here and are satisfied.
