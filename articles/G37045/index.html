What are apartments abroad

Our compatriots prefer a holiday by the sea, not in their own country, but in the resorts of European countries. This is due to low cost, the ability to open new horizons, change the climate and get acquainted with the features of another culture. Recreation is gaining popularity in countries such as Bulgaria and Montenegro. This is explained simply: the magnificent climate, the sea, clean beaches, the richness of nature, the similarity of cultures and linguistic community contribute to relaxation and allow you to enjoy your vacation.





What are apartments abroad?

The concept of "apartment" has not been frightening for a long time with the assumption of an overestimated cost of living or buying. On the contrary, it implies convenience and comfort. Private accommodation has become a very convenient vacation option.

The analogue of apartments in Bulgaria and Montenegro are apartments with their own kitchen, furniture, necessary equipment. They are different:

  1. Studio apartments. As a rule, they consist of one bedroom and a living room, with a kitchen unit, an area of ​​up to 40 square meters. m. Designed for 2-4 people. Ideal for a small family.
  2. Two-room apartments have an area of ​​up to 90 square meters. m, consist of 2 bedrooms, living room and kitchen. It can accommodate up to 6 people.
  3. 3-4-room apartments. Their area exceeds 100 square meters. m, there are three or four bedrooms, a separate living room, dining room and kitchen. They can accommodate up to 12 people, ideal for a group of friends or two families.




What is an apartment in terms of amenities?

A distinctive feature of foreign real estate is considered infrastructure. Apartments in Montenegro on the coast, as well as in the resorts of Bulgaria, are in houses with additional service. Usually it is assumed that there is a concierge, security system, video surveillance, swimming pool, playgrounds for children, SPA centers for adults, saunas, tennis courts. For the maintenance of such residential complexes an additional fee is charged from the owner, as a rule, it is low, especially when compared with similar tariffs in Spain, France and Italy.

What are apartments to buy?

In recent years, buying property in resort countries has become a profitable venture. This is explained by the availability of prices, ease of registration of property, low cost of maintenance. Apartments in Bulgaria and Montenegro can be rented out, while there is no need to do this on your own, this will be taken care of by the management company with whom a contract for the provision of services is signed . It is possible to obtain a loan for the purchase of an apartment in Bulgaria, while in Montenegro this possibility is not yet foreseen, since the banking system is imperfect in the matter of collecting debt from foreign borrowers.

Buying real estate provides for a good investment in terms of its resale. As the price growth index shows , every year there is a significant increase in housing prices in Bulgaria and Montenegro (both new and secondary).





What is a holiday apartment?

A great opportunity to rent apartments in a foreign country is provided to families with children, large companies who do not want to adapt to the hotel schedule. This will allow you to choose your own menu, prepare the children’s dishes that are necessary for you, calmly buy a bottle of delicious wine in the nearest supermarket and, while sitting on the terrace with sea views, enjoy the evening sunset. Renting an apartment will cost less than a vacation in a hotel, since the fee is not charged per person, but for the accommodation itself.

Of course, in holidays abroad in your own apartments there are many advantages. The only condition: it is worth considering the traditions and features of the lifestyle of the local population. Do not forget that each country has its own rules, and it is necessary to honor them.
