Holidays in Cyprus: hotel Paramount Hotel Apts 4

Description: Paramount Hotel Apts 4 is located two and a half kilometers from the resort of Protaras, on the beach. Nearby are many small shops, small taverns, there are cafes and restaurants, hotels, including the Pernera Beach Hotel, Chrystalla, etc.



Many interesting sites are within walking distance, including the National Park.

The well-developed infrastructure of the hotel includes an elevator, a 24-hour medical office, a business center, laundry, luggage storage, hairdresser, currency exchange, free outdoor parking that does not need to be booked in advance, a newsstand, and concierge services.

The hotel area, although small, but completely landscaped.

The registration desk is always open. Here you can arrange a transfer, order a taxi, rent a bicycle or a vehicle, use the safe. The reception has a tour desk.

A bus stop is located next to the hotel. In 5 minutes you can get to Protaras, in 10 - to Cavo Greco.



The distance to the nearest airport is 30 kilometers.

Rooms: Paramount Hotel Apts 4 offers studios and one-room apartments. In front of each of them there is a small balcony, sitting on which you can admire the surrounding nature and the sea.

In each room, in addition to the most necessary furniture for living, there are kitchen utensils, including a kettle, microwave, cutlery, dishes. You can cook in the kitchenette if you wish.



The TV shows satellite channels, there is a direct dial telephone, a paid safe, which holds a laptop, a refrigerator.

For convenience, blackout drapes are provided on the windows that open.
All rooms are air-conditioned and have ceramic tiled floors.

The combined bathrooms have a bathtub with shower, a large mirror, a hairdryer, and everything necessary for personal hygiene.

Upon request, free wake up service is available.

There are several wheelchair accessible rooms on the ground floor.

Meals: Dining at Paramount Hotel Apts 4 is based on the breakfast buffet concept, which is served as a buffet in the main restaurant. You can have lunch and dinner there, by reservation. Those who do not want to eat on site can visit the nearby small and inexpensive restaurants and cafes.

There are bars by the pool and beach.

Beach: The beach of Paramount Hotel Apts 4 is sandy. It is located a hundred meters. The entrance to the water is gentle, which is convenient for children swimming. Sun loungers, trestle beds, umbrellas are issued free of charge.

Additional Information: For its young clients, Paramount Hotel Apts offers an equipped playground, a swimming pool, as well as the possibility of installing cots on request.



Adults can play billiards, beach volleyball, darts in their free time, ride ATVs, engage in water sports on the beach.
The hotel has a clean freshwater pool, next to which there is a sun terrace.

Vacationers reviews: The hotel always has a homely calm atmosphere that is perfect for families. The staff are friendly and attentive. The territory is beautifully furnished, the rooms are cleaned perfectly.
