Residential complex “House on Profsoyuznaya 69”: customer reviews, layout and features

Buying your own housing in Moscow is a dream for many people. Its cost is such that you have to earn for your own nest for many years. And when the dream is ready to be fulfilled, you need to think carefully before making the final choice. We will help you with this and tell you about “House on Profsoyuznaya 69”. Customer reviews say that it deserves your attention. It is located in an area with a developed infrastructure, and you do not have to worry about the lack of schools or hospitals. There is everything you need for life.



Where is located

Having met such information, one may be surprised, because the area is completely built up. Where did the new house on Trade Union come from. 69? Customer reviews suggest that this complex arose on the basis of buildings of an earlier construction. Indeed, this is a comfort class project near the Belyaevo and Kaluzhskaya metro stations. The developer went on a simpler path, namely, he bought a panel building and carried out a major overhaul. As a result, a new residential complex appeared between the two metro stations, the name of which came from its address. All work in the building has already been completed, the apartments are fully ready for occupancy.

general information

Today, the residential complex is only preparing for the meeting of its residents, so it’s still difficult to find customer reviews on the network. “House on Profsoyuznaya 69” is an interesting object, therefore, people track information on the official website and leave comments. And first of all, they emphasize the relatively low prices for this area. In a two-section building, 238 apartments were designed for delivery. These are mainly small studios and one-room apartments. The total area is from 17 to 38 m 2 . It is possible to combine several neighboring apartments, so that you can have a three- or four-room apartment, which can be redesigned as you want.



Home decoration

This moment is very significant, as it greatly affects the final cost of apartments. It turns out to be more profitable to buy an option without finishing (this allows you to reduce the initial investment), and then gradually carry out repairs. In any case, so say customer reviews. “House on Profsoyuznaya 69” is an attractive object for people with a wide range of incomes. Options without finishing can be considered a budget option.

Some of the apartments are offered to be bought in finished form with a fine finish. A laminate is laid on the floor, the walls are plastered and ready for painting, new plumbing and electrical switches, PVC windows and entrance doors are installed. It remains only to bring furniture - and you can live.

Clearance transaction

Apartments in the residential complex “House on Profsoyuznaya 69” are already for sale. All information can be found on the official website. Sales are conducted with the obligatory execution of contracts, so you can track the transparency of the transaction and get acquainted with each item. Mortgages are issued without down payment. Installment payments are provided, in some cases - interest-free.

Future buyers smartly discuss the new building, its pros and cons on the official website. In their opinion, low prices are formed due to the small areas of the apartments offered. New buildings today often offer housing of a much larger area, according to the European standard. Of course, his price is also much higher.



A few words about the developer

Residential complex “House on Profsoyuznaya 69” is a newcomer to the real estate market. The company is named in the same way as the object it implements, which indicates the high significance of the result of the work. This object will forever remain a business card. LLC Profsoyuznaya 69 is engaged in the construction of a new facility in the Moscow region of Konkovo ​​(South-Western Administrative District). The organization carries out repairs in the building. In addition, the company is the owner of apartments in a future new building and their seller. The responses of the developer of the residential complex “House on Profsoyuznaya 69” are characterized on the positive side, focusing on meeting deadlines and a high pace of work. Further characteristics can be given about a year after the tenants move into the apartments.

Facility infrastructure

Residential complex “House on 69 Profsoyuznaya Street” is an excellent budget option for most young families. There is no private parking near it, but you can leave your car in the territory adjacent to the house. On the ground floor there is a travel agency, printing, car service, a bank and a hotel.

Customer reviews of the residential complex “House on Profsoyuznaya 69” are also evaluated in terms of transport accessibility. In walking distance there is a metro station, nearby are the main roads of the area. Public transport stop 100 meters from the house. There are minibuses and buses in the area. Just 1.5 km is the vast green zone - Bitsevsky forest. Two rivers flow here and the Sevastopol stream.



Main advantages

Since a number of apartments are already sold out today, people are starting to look closely at their housing. And therefore, reviews of residents begin to appear. “House on Profsoyuznaya 69” is located in a very attractive area, and many want to live here. The low cost of housing is the main advantage, which for most is decisive. What else can be noted:

  • The proximity of the subway. In walking distance at once two branches.
  • The house is located in an area with existing infrastructure. Lack of such is the main minus of all new residential complexes.
  • Quick commissioning. The frame of the building was initially ready, so only redevelopment and major repairs are underway. The object will be surrendered by the end of 2017.

Minuses

Reviews about LCD "House on Profsoyuznaya 69" are not limited to pluses. There are moments that customers are not too happy with, and they openly write about it on the official website:

  • In the arsenal are only small-sized apartments. It is precisely because of this that prices are low for this area of ​​Moscow.
  • The developer is inexperienced. This is the first project in the company's portfolio. On the other hand, this is not the most difficult project, because the frame of the building already exists.
  • A big minus is the lack of own parking. That is, residents are invited to leave cars right next to the house, which is not too convenient.

Cost

Now let's look at the prices. LCD "House on Profsoyuznaya 69" is aimed at young couples, as well as single people. The standard studio has a total area of ​​17 m2. Its cost is 3 million rubles. Today on the fifth floor a cozy studio is for sale. The living area is 8 m 2 . The commissioning deadline is December 2017. One-room apartments have a larger area. On average, it is 37 or 38 m 2 .

Customer opinion

Reviews about LCD about “House on Profsoyuznaya 69” are affordable housing in the center of the capital. There are finished apartments with finishes upon request. It is proposed to buy apartments of a small area. At the same time, here you can find an option for any family, since having bought two apartments, it is possible to combine them. Due to this, create different layout options. If your family grows, it will be possible to increase living space.

Those who have already made a purchase emphasize that a considerable part of the work is yet to come. Yes, people already live somewhere. However, most apartments are still without decoration. All this is carried out under the contract, after the purchase. Immediately pay attention to the thickness of the walls and the quality of decoration, so as not to overhear the conversations of neighbors. It is very difficult to verify this today, because you are shown not the apartment selected on the plan, but the standard one, which is prepared for this. If the sound insulation is bad, then you have to do it yourself, and this is an additional cost.

On the floor, the developer places the laminate. It is difficult to judge whether there is a substrate, whether the laying technology was followed. Therefore, here, too, after checking in, one may encounter surprises. Another point that buyers note is heating. In the office room on the second floor, a battery is the width of the window. And in the rooms for sale, it barely makes 1/3 of its width. It turns out that after acquiring an apartment it will most likely have to be changed, otherwise it will be cold in winter.



Office service

In fact, the reviews are not unambiguous. Some note the warm welcome and polite attitude of managers, others note some inconsistencies. The site does not have open data on which apartments have already been sold and which are free and ready for sale. In this case, the cost is indicated only minimal. That is, you come to the office at the facility and find out all the necessary information on the spot. What awaits you here:

  • There is no finished price. It is calculated each time with the client. This creates certain difficulties in comparing prices among themselves.
  • Managers here do not show a complete list of available apartments. To the request to show all available options in printed form, they answer that the information is closed.
  • Documents that will have to be signed are not given into the hands. They can be read only at the time of signing.
  • If possible, come to the office with a competent lawyer.

As you can see, when choosing an apartment in this complex you can encounter certain difficulties and pitfalls. Therefore, you need to be extremely careful. Your future life and calm depend on the choice.
