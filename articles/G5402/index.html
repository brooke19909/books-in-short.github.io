Location

To understand where White Lake is in Belarus, it is important to realize that it is not the only one. This name was assigned to several reservoirs of the country, which are located in different areas. But now the conversation will be about White Lake (Belarus), which is one of the reservoirs of the Narochansky group. It is located in the Myadel district of the Minsk region. Fishing

How I want to show everyone Lake Beloe (Belarus)! Photo, unfortunately, will not be able to convey all the enthusiasm from the surrounding beauty, but it will allow you to imagine yourself with a fishing rod in this place.

The reservoirs of the Narochansky park, which include Lake Blednoye (White), is a fishing paradise! There are a huge number of species of fish, and specifically - rudd, bream, tench, perch, roach, pike and others. Fishing is paid, only amateur fishing is permissible. You can buy a ticket in several places. The main registration takes place in Naroch and in Naroch, Novo-Myadel, Myadel, Konstantinovsky and Syrmezh forestries. You can also buy a ticket in Minsk, Smorgon, Ostrovets, Molodechny and Vileyka. Its cost can range from 800 to 1700 rubles.

You can fish from the shore, but you will have to go a little in shallow water to the depth, and from small boats. The motor installed in the vehicle should not exceed 15 l / s in power.
