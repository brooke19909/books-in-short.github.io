Sun Hall Hotel 4 * (Larnaca, Cyprus): hotel description, services, reviews

Recently, the flow of tourists from Russia to Greece has increased significantly. This wonderful country is loved by many. And you can understand why. It is warm, beautiful, interesting, there is a sea. What else do tourists need? Perhaps a good modern hotel, located in a good location. There are many of them. And Sun Hall Hotel 4 * is very popular. That's about it and it is worth telling in more detail.





About hotel

Sun Hall Hotel 4 * is a fairly "adult" hotel complex. It was opened in 1972. The last renovation was carried out in 2012. The hotel building looks neat and new, inside - everything is the same. The apartments are furnished with good quality furniture and modern amenities.

And there is a hotel in Larnaca. This is the third largest metropolis in all of Cyprus. Located in the southeastern part of the island. By the way, it is in Larnaca that is the largest airport in all of Cyprus.

The hotel building blends harmoniously with the surrounding landscape. The hotel is impossible not to notice, because it is located on the famous palm promenade, just 50 meters from the spacious beach. Within 1-2 minutes you can walk to attractions such as Europe Square, Larnaca Harbor, Armenian Church of St. Stephen, the Byzantine Museum of St. Lazarus and many other interesting places. In general, the hotel is located in a very successful and convenient place in every sense of the word.





Service

Sun Hall Hotel 4 * is loved by many tourists, not only because it is located in a good location. This hotel is good in that it has absolutely everything for a comfortable and carefree vacation - from private parking to free Wi-Fi.

The lobby has a 24-hour reception, as well as a currency exchange, tour desk, ticket service and storage room.

There is also a conference room, which seats 250 people. There is a business center with him.

The list of services also includes such basic conditions as dry cleaning, laundry, drinks and food delivery, car rental and babysitting. Incidentally, non-smoking rooms or adjoining apartments can be provided.

All descriptions say that at Sun Hall Hotel 4 * the staff speaks English and Greek. But if you pay attention to some of the reviews left by our compatriots, it becomes clear that people who know Russian still work here. And it pleases.
