The history of the reservoir and its appearance

Now it is worth paying attention to the history of the spill. According to many sources, this reservoir 100 years ago did not possess such dimensions as it is now. The depths of the Lakhtinsky spill at the beginning of the 20th century averaged no more than 2.5 meters. A lot of time has passed since then, and the topography of the bottom of the reservoir has changed dramatically. Despite the fact that the depth at that time was less, the surface area itself was much larger. It is known that then the size of the spill was about 13 square meters. km Such changes occurred due to the large development of the northern part of the Neva Bay. For these purposes, it was actively washed. Thus, we see that over the past 100 years, the body of water has noticeably changed. This applies not only to its depth and area, but also to many other, less noticeable components, such as the animal and plant world.





Recreation and fishing in this pond

Of course, many have already become interested in the Lakhtinsky spill. This is not surprising since it is great for a variety of recreational purposes. The existence of the lake is known to many people who often visit the Primorsky district. St. Petersburg boasts such a wonderful place to relax, you can often meet fishing lovers here.

More recently, this pond was not very suitable for fishing. This was mainly due to the fact that a large number of networks were installed here. In this regard, it was impossible to fish on a spinning rod. Due to poaching in the Lakhta spill, the fish population has noticeably decreased. Recently, however, close monitoring of the reservoir has been carried out, special raids have been periodically carried out to combat illegal fishing methods. Thanks to such measures, the number of fish in the pond is increasing. Sometimes quite large individuals are found here.





What fish is found in the lake?

Probably, many fishing enthusiasts are interested in what can be caught in the Lakhta spill? It is worth considering this issue in detail. At different times of the year, the likelihood of meeting different species of fish here increases. For example, in spring, crucian carp is most often caught in reeds. You can also meet a houster. In rare cases, fish such as ide and tench are found.

Most often, pike, perch, bream, and crucian carp become prey for lovers of sitting on the shore with a fishing rod. There are many fish such as bleak in the pond.

So, we see that this place is great for purposes such as recreation and fishing. Often people come here as a family to spend a weekend in nature and enjoy the fresh air in the city.
