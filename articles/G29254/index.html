Resort Town Goryachy Klyuch

50 km. from Krasnodar, between the slopes of the two ridges of the Caucasus Mountains is a quiet resort town Goryachiy Klyuch. Tuapse, one of the most famous resorts in the south of Russia, is 117 km away. From him.

The city has a railway station, there are trains. He is considered one of the cleanest cities in the world, most of the territory is forest. The town is inhabited mainly by Russians.

The main attraction of the resort and source of income is a network of centers for spa holidays and treatment. It employs more than 10 resorts.

Tourists are attracted by the uniqueness of lakes and ponds, the sight is simply mesmerizing. For example, Adov waterfall is 20 meters high.

Healing springs are also popular with tourists. They contain in mineral waters, which are very beneficial for health.

Mountains and gorges are breathtaking, attract creative people. Visit the Krasnodar Territory and not visit the Caucasus Mountains? No, this cannot be! The Abadzekh Mountain gave rise to the birth of the resort town of Goryachy Klyuch, which has now turned into a modern, popular resort.

There is a very interesting and unique Dantovo Gorge - a cultural heritage. It is a natural monument. By the way, it appeared artificially, and nature made it very beautiful and wide. The gorge is higher than 10 m, its length is over 100 m.

A variety of ranches and museums attract animal and nature lovers, telling and demonstrating the life and history of the resort, the city, and the development of the region. There are many exhibits and museum compositions dedicated to the outfits of residents, very peculiar and beautiful. Worth a look!

A large number of monuments have been established. For example, a bust of the famous academician Pavlov, Nobel laureate. He rested and was treated here, spent a lot of time. The monument is installed opposite the Aquarium building.

As you can see, a fairly extensive program awaits tourists in the Hot Key. Tuapse, unlike him, offers beach vacation. It should be borne in mind that from the balneological resort to the sea you have to go by train or car.

How to get to the Hot Key

The easiest way to get there is by train. Electric train Goryachiy Klyuch - Tuapse runs very often, all the time on a schedule, so there is an opportunity to get to sanatoriums and recreation centers without problems.

Distance Goryachiy Klyuch - Tuapse is 117 km, it takes about 1.5 hours to go by train, the car will turn out the same.

You can leave from here in several directions:

  • Goryachiy Klyuch - Saratov;
