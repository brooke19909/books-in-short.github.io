Turkey in October: traveler reviews

Usually people try to take a summer vacation in order to have time to enjoy sunny days, swim in the sea and sunbathe on the beach. But what if we managed to free ourselves only by mid-autumn? If there is no money for overseas resorts, then the choice remains small: Egypt, the Greek islands, Turkey. I would like to dwell in more detail on the latter option, since this country offers high-class service and a wide selection of places to stay. But you need to understand that not all of Turkey is suitable for a beach holiday in October. Reviews of experienced tourists recommend buying tours to the resorts of the Antalya coast in the fall, because the Aegean Sea is already pretty cool at this time, and the weather in Bodrum, Marmaris and Fethiye is rainy.



No worse than in summer, Turkey welcomes its guests in October. Reviews of travelers about the velvet season in this country are only positive. Until mid-November, in Antalya and nearby villages, the air temperature remains around +30 ¬∞ , the sea is also warm - +25 ¬∞ . The sun pleases with its presence very often, but you need to be prepared for rainy days, since it is no longer summer in the yard. In cloudy weather there is also something to do. You can visit one of the excursions or rent a car and travel all the interesting sights on your own.

Holidays in Turkey in October cannot be boring and monotonous. In addition to visiting architectural and historical sites, you can go rafting on local rivers, diving, exploring underwater reefs and wrecks. Many will be interested in lake or river fishing, visiting a water park, and dolphin performances. In rainy weather, you can go shopping to buy the necessary things and buy souvenirs for friends and relatives.



Tranquility, silence, tranquility will meet holidaymakers in Turkey in October. Reviews of tourists convince that in the fall there is not a big crowd of people, so you can freely use water activities, and even require a discount on their cost. There are free beaches for tourists, but you still have to pay for the umbrella and deck chair. Turkey in October is also attractive for lower hotel prices. Traveler reviews allow you to choose the most profitable vacation option.



If you buy a ticket, then the gain in money will be small, because in the velvet season discounts in travel agencies are insignificant. At the same time, hotel prices fall almost twice. To save money, you can book a room on your own, and only get a transfer to the hotel and airline ticket from a tour operator. Do not be upset if you could not relax in the summer, because Turkey has a lot of advantages in October. Prices for a weekly stay at a 3-star hotel are around 100 euros per person. If you go to relax with the whole family, it is advantageous to rent an apartment, a weekly stay in which will cost no more than 200 euros. The velvet season has its advantages and you need to use them to the maximum.
