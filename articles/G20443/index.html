Hotels in Crimea: rating and description

Every year, there are more people wishing to enjoy a vacation in Crimea. Tourists from all over the world come to the peninsula. To host so many guests, Crimea has a large number of hotels. You can understand which one is the best from this article.

Hotel Rating

In Crimea, a huge selection of housing, but not all hotels meet modern standards. To make it easier for guests of the peninsula to determine the best place to stay, you should carefully review the list of hotels on the Crimean peninsula.

  1. One of the best hotels on the peninsula is located in Yalta, under the modest name of Seaside Park.
  2. "Yalta-Intourist" takes an honorable second place.
  3. Three-star hotel in the small town of Alushta - Golden resort.
  4. Hotel Lexx in Koktebel also deserves special attention.
  5. In the city of Sudak is the Kodak Sudak Hotel, which is known throughout Russia.

Of course, this is not the whole list of famous hotels.

"Seaside Park" in Yalta

It extends for more than 2 km along the Black Sea and is one of the elite hotels on the Crimean peninsula. Seaside Park owns a spa center, which uses all modern technologies in the field of beauty and health.

The small hotel building accommodates about 30 rooms of various categories, the cost of which starts from 10 thousand rubles per night. All vacationers at the hotel can visit the largest wine cellar in the Crimea. There are more than 5 thousand bottles of wine from Italy, France, Zeeland, Chile, South Africa, Spain and so on. A tasting is held daily for hotel guests, and those who wish can purchase a bottle of good wine.

Hotel "Yalta-Intourist"

This is one of the largest hotels in the Crimea on the seashore. Yalta-Intourist, one of the new hotels on the peninsula, was opened in 2011. He takes second place in the rating of hotels in Crimea. The huge hotel building is suitable for family vacations, romantic trips, as well as all-Russian and international congresses are held here.

The hotel has more than 500 rooms of various categories: standard room, suite and junior suite. The cost of living varies depending on the time of year. So, for example, in the summer, the price of a standard room is 10 thousand rubles, and a suite will cost 20 thousand per night. From November to March, prices are reduced, and you can spend the night at the hotel for 5 thousand rubles.

On the territory of the hotel complex there are more than 20 restaurants serving dishes of all cuisines of the world. Own beach meets all modern standards, you can get to the beach without going outside, using the elevator directly from the hotel.

For recreation with children, the hotel provides a playground, theme parties with animators, a children's pool and a petting zoo.

Golden Resort

Since Alushta is a small city on the Crimean coast, which is not widespread among holidaymakers, thanks to this there are excellent clean beaches that are almost deserted even in the season.

The Golden Resort resort in Alushta is located 40 km from Simferopol Airport, which allows you to get to the hotel in 30 minutes by taxi. Ideal location - 500 meters from the beach, mild warm climate and a large number of sunny days allowed the hotel to take first place in the rating of Crimea hotels.

The hotel has more than 700 rooms. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, minibar, safe, TV, bathroom. Housekeeping and linen change are provided daily. The price includes meals, visits to sports fields, swimming pools, theme parties. Near the administration there is a tour desk where you can arrange trips for various excursions: Swallow's Nest, Bakhchisaray, in the mountains of the peninsula and so on.

The cost of living at the hotel will be about 7 thousand per night.
