Ski resort Tahko, Finland: slopes, accommodation, equipment, reviews

Tahko in Finland is a popular ski resort operating for tourists all year round. It is located near the city of Nilsia, and from the regional center of Kuopio, approximately 70 kilometers. The resort itself is located directly on Lake Suvari in the Tahkovuori Mountain region.

For the first time, tourists began to receive tourists here in 1968, when the first ski slope was launched. Since then, the resort has constantly changed. Now he has acquired the distinctive features of a modern international tourist center.

About the resort

Tahko in Finland is one of the best resorts in the central and southern parts of the country. Here there are as many as 23 ski slopes, half of which has a length of more than one kilometer, and elevations - up to two hundred meters.

It is worth noting that this place is famous not only for highways, but also for the presence of special areas for beginners and children. A separate lift is available for snowboarders.

Ski trips are regularly arranged at the Tahko ski resort in Finland, where vacationers have the opportunity to get acquainted with the winter landscapes of this place. Tourists especially enjoy these events. Cross-country skiing trails run across the ice of a frozen lake and its diverse landscape. Moreover, part of the route is also lit.

It is believed that the Tahko ski resort in Finland is an ideal place for entertainment and a comfortable stay. On the slopes there are several cozy restaurants where you can take a break, drink coffee, taste fresh pastries, order a drink for every taste. In Tahko (Finland), there are two rental points where you can rent snowboards, the necessary ski equipment, sets of cross-country skis, and if necessary, lubricate and prepare them before going to the track.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure of the Tahko ski resort is able to impress even demanding athletes. The choice for tourists here is represented by more than 800 accommodation options of various levels. These are fashionable hotels, and cottage villages, and apart-hotels.

Currently, this resort has 23 slopes and 14 lifts. It offers a variety of leisure opportunities. This is cross - country skiing, skiing, ATV and snowmobile safaris , mountain biking, golf, canoeing and kayaking, hiking, horse riding, fishing, rowing. Since 2005, there has been a swimming spa pool, bowling, and other types of recreation based on a multifunctional sports complex.

activity

Ski conditions

Almost ideal conditions for practicing this sport are created here. The ski slopes in Tahko are located within walking distance from civilization, which is considered an obvious plus. On the slopes there is a wide range of opportunities, a well-groomed network of tracks attracts athletes of different ages and training.

There is enough space for snowboarders who will be able to demonstrate their skills. The pedestrian bridge allows you to cross the bay, in addition, parking directly on ice opens immediately, as soon as weather conditions allow.

This resort has a popular ski pass system.

Equipment

If you did not bring your ski equipment to Tahko with you, that's okay. There are three stores offering its rental services right on the slopes. They will give you everything, including sets of cross-country skiing and snowboards.

In addition, there is one point of training equipment. If necessary, you can sign up for ski school lessons, where you will be taught the secrets of sliding - from telemarking to snowboarding.

Rent price

On average, the cost of renting a set of skiing for one day will be 32 euros, for children cheaper - 24 euros.

A professional kit will cost more - at 42 euros. A set of snowboarding equipment will cost 38 euros.

If necessary, you can rent a snowboard separately (31 euros), boots (23 euros), a helmet (10 euros), skis (20 euros), poles (5 euros).

Slope reservation

The resort is very popular. Here regularly there is a need to book slopes. To do this, you need to check the availability of free time in a special table, and then send the appropriate application to the email address. It should indicate the time, date, name of the club, contact person with an international phone number, discipline and group size.

Applications are accepted in the order they are received. The only condition is that they should be sent at least 48 hours before the start of the reservation.

The head coach is responsible for creating the track. It is important to ensure that the reserved area is equipped with the signs provided for this. In this case, all team members and equipment must remain inside the reserved part of the slope.

Travel Impressions

In reviews of Tahko, tourists often note a large number of accommodation options, a markedly developed infrastructure.

Many are satisfied with almost everything, from living. It is especially convenient to stay in hotels or cottages within walking distance of the ski lifts. The rooms are carefully thought out to make tourists as comfortable as possible. There is a clothes dryer, own kitchen, sauna.

There is a separate locker in the basement for storing inventory. The variety and number of slopes can impress even the most demanding skier.

Negative

Of the negative aspects, travelers say that the organizers do not prepare the tracks well. Some had to deal with a situation when only four out of 23 were open, and even those badly broken.

In addition, on most slopes, too steep and short zones for braking, which is extremely difficult for beginners. The only outlet for cross-country skiing is well-prepared and varied. Compared to other Finnish resorts, this one is notable for its high prices and crowding.

Some leave here completely disappointed. Just because of the unjustifiably high cost, a small number of open routes and their unsatisfactory level of training. Because of this, the whole impression of a holiday spoils. Long lines are almost constantly formed on the lifts, and by the middle of the day, the tracks are simply smashing to smithereens. And all this at a high cost, clearly not in line with the level of service provided here, on which customers rely.
