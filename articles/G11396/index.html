What should be a washing machine for a summer residence

The modern world provides us with a huge number of opportunities for managing our free time much more correctly and rationally. Almost every family has been using washing machines for quite some time, and someone is surely going to acquire such a benefit of civilization. However, in the country, where there is no water supply and sewage, it is quite problematic to install such equipment. And if you have communications, you don‚Äôt want to spend significant financial resources on its acquisition, since you do not live there the bulk of the year. And here the question arises of what should be a washing machine for a summer residence.



Each time, making a new purchase, each of us expects that the thing will serve for a long time. If this is a washing machine for a summer residence, then it is quite difficult to choose the appropriate option. Therefore, it is worth considering what the modern market is ready to offer us.



A washing machine for a summer residence can be of a drum and activator type. In the first case, we are talking about loading laundry into a drum that rotates, periodically filling with water. The second involves the use of a disk with ribs to mix laundry, detergents and water. The best option to ease the work as much as possible is the first type automatic machine. But this is the most expensive way out. If you are interested in just such a washing machine for a summer residence, then you should not spare money. The presence of such a device requires the observance of certain conditions: connecting to a source of water and electricity, the machine should not be transported often, so for its installation you need to choose a permanent place.



So that a washing machine for a summer residence (automatic) does not cost you too much, you need to decide on the necessary functionality. Usually quite a few basic modes of operation are sufficient. Since in the country there is an opportunity to dry clothes on the street, you can choose a lower spin class, as well as exclude the drying function. Since you will not use the machine very often, you can opt for a lower class of energy consumption. You should not choose a model that has a large number of electronics, since due to voltage drops in the network it can fail. The machine can be with vertical or horizontal loading. The first type is more reliable and convenient, and some models of this type allow you to load laundry during the washing process. At the moment, front-loading models are much larger, due to their popularity. Very convenient options are "mini" - washing machines for summer cottages, which have compact dimensions and provide the necessary functionality.

It is worth saying that if you do not have a water supply, this is not a reason to abandon the "automatic" model. Now there are cars with a water tank that holds up to one hundred liters. Activator type devices for summer cottages are a very popular solution. Such units operate without running water, but require constant monitoring by the owner. Such a washing machine for a summer residence has several advantages: during the washing process, linen can be reported, loading is vertical, there is no strong vibration during spinning, more linen is placed in it.
