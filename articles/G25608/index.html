Learning Competency



In modern pedagogy, a new and resonant term has recently appeared - a competency-based approach to teaching . What is meant by it?

First you need to consider what does the word "approach" mean? This term is often used equivalently to the word "method". But, nevertheless, he has a broader understanding. The approach implies the existence of a whole complex of interconnected ideas, principles and methods that contribute to the solution of a particular problem. Here, an important role is played by the understanding of socio-economic, philosophical and psychological-pedagogical prerequisites. The stages and mechanisms ensuring the achievement of the set goals should be clearly worked out.

In pedagogical practice, many time-tested approaches and methods have gained wide popularity. These include traditional approaches that focus on obtaining strong knowledge, their systematic nature, personal orientation of students and the complex relationship of these approaches. The competency-based approach refers to modern developments that went into teaching practice at the end of the twentieth century. Such are situational, informational and some others. They have not yet reached their completion and have not received a full scientific justification. Although they are quite widely recognized.

The competency-based approach first appeared in the 80s of the last century. In the beginning, it was about the competence of the individual and the competencies that were to become the goal and result of education. It was understood as "deepening knowledge", "mastering skills" and "the ability to use skills." But later this concept expanded more and more, and now in pedagogy they are already talking about a holistic approach of this type in education.

Let's take a closer look at the competency-based approach in modern professional education, its goals and content. After all, this is not a tribute to the latest fashion, but a reality caused by the socio-economic and educational prerequisites of recent years, when the market economy has fully declared itself. Nowadays, completely different requirements are presented to a modern specialist, which were not taken into account in old programs for training specialists.
