Police salary in Moscow: salary level, comparison by region, real numbers

The work of the police is dangerous and difficult. It is them we call for help when our lives are in danger. The salary of police officers in Russia depends on many factors that take into account the specifics of the job, the rank of the employee and his professional achievements. Consider what kind of monetary reward Russian police receive for their hard work.

Law Enforcement Advocates

The police are part of the Russian Ministry of Internal Affairs system. In the country, it acts to ensure public order. The Russian police protect citizens from offenders of the law, ensuring their safe stay in the state. She also fights crime and prevents crime.

The police is a body of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, managed by the president of the country. The structure of the Ministry of the Interior provides for the position of Minister, who runs the entire service.

Police officer - an employee of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, carrying out his activities on the basis of an employment contract or contract. Police salaries in Moscow are considered the highest. In fact, its size may vary significantly among employees of the Ministry of Internal Affairs. Monthly monetary compensation depends on the functions performed, work experience and position held.

How to become a policeman?

Citizens of the Russian Federation (boys and girls) aged 18 to 35 years can apply for this position. They should not have health problems. With regard to qualification requirements, for some posts it is sufficient to have a full secondary education. But in order to become part of the senior or senior management team, you must have a diploma of higher profile education.

Many try to get into service in the Russian capital. And this is understandable, because the salary of police officers in Moscow is the highest in comparison with other regions of the country. But it will not be able to earn candidates who have convicted relatives in the family, or have recorded a personal criminal record in the past or repeatedly brought to administrative responsibility. Such people simply will not be hired by the Ministry of Internal Affairs. A person who has his own business and who is not ready to keep state secrets will not be able to serve in the police.

Future law enforcement advocates are testing. Check not only their physical fitness, but also emotional stability. Upon successful completion of all stages of testing, the candidate for the police is accepted for an internship that lasts from three to six months. And only after this the employee is certified and enlisted in the staff of the Ministry of Internal Affairs with the assignment of a certain rank.

What does the salary depend on?

The salary of a policeman has several components. Total monthly income includes:

  1. Tariff salary.
  2. A bonus for an existing title.
  3. Extra charge for length of service.
  4. A coefficient that depends on the region of service.
  5. A bonus for complexity, work stress.
  6. Surcharge for irregular schedule.

The salary of a policeman in Moscow is formed in the same way as that of a military man. But the latter - the monthly income is often higher than that of an employee of law enforcement agencies.
