How to choose a home theater with karaoke: an overview of the best models and reviews

Home cinemas tightly occupied a niche in the market of multimedia equipment and, in modern design, offer a huge range of functions and entertainment opportunities. One of the most sought after options has long been support for karaoke. Although the consumer pays more attention to image quality when choosing equipment for watching movies, the musical reproduction capabilities are also expanding. Technological means of acoustic design and ergonomics - these are the main qualities that manufacturers are striving to give a modern home theater with karaoke function. The price of such systems, unfortunately, is also growing inexorably - models from well-known brands cost on average from 15 to 40 thousand rubles. But, as the owners of such cinemas note, the pleasure of using them fully pays for the investment. The main thing is to choose the right model.

Main characteristics





Home cinema is a complex complex, therefore, the set of parameters for assessing its performance is quite wide. Actually, all the characteristics can be divided into two groups - those that relate to the parameters of the screen, and the acoustics, which are provided with a home theater with karaoke function. Characteristic of the screen relative to its size is key - the average value is 42-55 inches. By the way, the highest class is represented by models at 61-80 inches. It will also be useful to determine the resolution of the TV, which is most often presented in 720p and 1080i formats. The most advanced screens support 3D technology and UltraHD format.

No less important are the characteristics of acoustics. The fundamental selection criterion in this case is the system configuration and input power. In standard versions, a home theater with karaoke function involves the use of front, rear and one center speaker with subwoofers, which can have the formats 5.1 or 7.2. As for power, the optimal indicators are in the range from 500 to 720 watts.

Karaoke function - what to consider?





The minimum acoustics requirements for karaoke require five-channel sound reproduction with a total speaker power of at least 300 watts. Actually, musical equipment will be the basis for the implementation of this function, but without additional accessories its use is impossible. It is advisable to immediately choose a home theater with karaoke function with a disc on which melodies will be recorded for performance. Today you can find offers with the number of songs in excess of 4000. For sophisticated performers, manufacturers also provide systems for scoring the quality of the sung song. If you plan to use karaoke in a duet, then you need to provide a second microphone, as in the basic configurations it is usually found in one copy.
