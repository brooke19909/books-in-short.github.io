How to make your own Halloween costumes?

In Russia, Halloween is not an official holiday, but despite this, more and more people annually on October 31 prefer to attend a theme party in one of the clubs or organize their own indoor masked ball. Outfits that help anyone who wants to turn into a fairy-tale character or hero of legends are an integral attribute of the holiday. If you do not have time to prepare in advance, do not be discouraged. Many do-it-yourself Halloween costumes can be made from improvised materials.

Do-it-yourself Halloween costumes



Before you start working on creating your own magical image, you should choose a role model. The most popular heroes on Halloween are vampires, zombies, devils and devils, and other evil spirits. On the eve of All Saints Day, in any souvenir shop you can find wigs and all kinds of accessories for the costume of such a character. If you can not find professional makeup, you can whiten the skin of the face and neck with the lightest powder or foundation. Witch or lady vamp makeup examples are often seen on the Internet. It is recommended to supplement your image with a pattern on the cheek or temple in the form of a web. Plug-in plastic fangs are great for a vampire costume .



The main accessories of the witch are a pointed hat, which can also be made from cardboard with your own hands and covered with fabric. A broom and a decorative pot for sweets will come in handy. As for clothes - for girls, dresses in dark colors, modern or antique, will be the best option. Men's Halloween costumes depicting vampires or witchers can be made from classic costumes, complemented by thin cloaks with deep hoods. Surely a vampire can afford to appear at a party and in a tailcoat, or a medieval shirt with a frill. Do not forget about silk gloves, lace handkerchiefs and other little things that complement the look. DIY zombie costumes are also popular. You can turn into a living dead body by whitening the skin, dressing in rags and drawing scars and wounds. If you have time to visit the fun store, buy stickers depicting all kinds of skin lesions.

Other Original Halloween Costumes



The mummy is one of the simplest and most spectacular costumes for the festival of evil spirits. To create it, you only need bandages - regular or elastic. You can turn into a tomb dweller with the help of white toilet paper. Do-it-yourself Halloween costumes are very simple to use, depicting ghosts. To do this, you need a sheet in which it is not a pity to make slits for the eyes. Among the ladies, the image of a dead bride is popular. You can take a real wedding dress, or assemble it from individual items of clothing in white. It is advisable to pat a little and stain a skirt, as well as complement the suit with appropriate makeup.
