We learn to knit a female cardigan with knitting needles. How to knit a women's cardigan?

Women's sweater with knitting needles is knitted from wool, mohair, cotton. Choosing the right pattern, you can create a summer and winter version of clothing for home, work, business meetings and romantic dates. Let's consider in the article several knitting patterns of stylish women's pullovers for different weather, in each of which any beauty will look elegant and feminine. But first, let's pay attention to fashion trends.

What sweaters are in fashion today?

Hand-knit products are still relevant today. The jumper can be decorated with embroidery, pockets, cuffs, buttons, peplum and other decorative elements. The style distinguish free, fitted, elongated models with sleeves of different lengths.

An elongated jumper, like a dress or tunic, is perfect for leggings with elegant long-heeled shoes or knitted boots with flat soles. The free model is suitable for jeans, and trousers, and for a warm skirt.

Knit a female sweater with thin and thick yarn. In the first case, the model is more delicate and elegant, it fits trousers and a classic shirt and complements the business style. Elegant airy fishnet sweaters are worn with a skirt over the tops and a beautiful bra.

The shape of the cutout models also have their own characteristics. For example, the classic V-neckline more clearly emphasizes the office style, with a round neck sweaters can be worn over dresses, and unusual multi-faceted pullovers open the neckline.

Sleeves, yoke and connection

We continue to create openwork sweater for women with knitting needles. For sleeves on circular knitting needles we dial 62 loops and knit two rows with a garter pattern. In the 3rd row, close the first 18 loops that define the middle of the seam, and at the same time add 10 loops along the circular row. Thus, there must be 54 loops on the needles of each sleeve.

The coquette is knitted from 288 loops of the back, front and sleeves of the first and second openwork pattern of 46 rows. Just alternate rapports of 14 and 18 loops to the end of the row. Decreases are made only in the second openwork pattern: in 4-5, 12-13, 20-21, 28-29, 36-37, 44-45, 46 rows - along the loop on one side; in the 6-11, 14-19, 22-27, 30-35, 38-43 rows - in a loop on both sides. Thus, in the last, 46th row, 6 loops remain in the rapport, and 180 loops on the knitting needles.

Then you knit a row with front stitch, decreasing along the row of 68 loops. Then 112 loops will remain on the knitting needles. We have almost ready a women's jumper. Knitting remains to raise the back. To do this, stretch the thread in the center of the part and knit according to the scheme:

  • 7LP on the face, turned the work and pulled the thread.
  • 14 IP on the wrong side, turned the product and pulled the thread.
  • 21LP on the face, turn the work and pull the thread, etc.

Continue knitting in this pattern for up to 70 loops. Next, 4 rows need to be knitted on all loops with a garter pattern and close the loops at a height of 62 centimeters.

Basic rules for knitting

When you knit women's sweaters according to the schemes with knitting needles with a description, then adhere to some rules:

  • pick up several models for your appearance;
  • purchase all the material;
  • tie samples of all patterns and compare with the recommended density;
  • wash and dry the sample and measure again;
  • draw patterns according to your sizes;
  • tie all the details;
  • make a product and try on;
  • Stitch the details on the figure.

Any sweater can be knitted in a couple of weeks, if you work on the hour every day. In order not to forget your calculations, draw a diagram and description in a notebook, marking each time which row we stopped at.
