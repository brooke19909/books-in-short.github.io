Hotel Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas 5 *, Vietnam, Nha Trang: photos and reviews of tourists

Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas is one of the best accommodation options for those who want to spend their holidays in sunny Vietnam. Here, the local flavor is organically combined with the European level of service.





Location

If you want to have a good time and get to know the new culture, head to the Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas. Vietnam, Nha Trang is a popular resort rich in attractions. Here travelers can get acquainted with such tourist sites:

  • bamboo island (3 km);
  • the imperial palace of Bao Dai (5 km);
  • Institute of Oceanography (5 km);
  • Winperl Cable Car (5 km);
  • sailing club (7 km);
  • Nha Trang Beach (7 km).

Hotel rooms

Comfortable accommodation for travelers is one of the main advantages of such an institution as Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas . Here tourists can choose one of the following accommodation options:

  • 42 sqm suite m - a luxurious bedroom, made in accordance with the classic interior traditions. A large bed is provided for guests. For relaxation - 2 soft poufs. Due to the fact that one of the walls is almost completely glass, a lot of sunlight penetrates into the room, which makes it visually more spacious. From the furnished balcony you can enjoy the garden, the sea or the golf course.
  • The family suite consists of two spacious bedrooms, each with a private bathroom. There are French and twin beds. Upholstered furniture is provided for relaxation. One of the bedrooms has access to its own terrace with comfortable sunbeds.
  • Executive Suite is a three-room luxury apartment. The bedroom with a huge French bed has access to a private balcony and adjoins a large bathroom with a jacuzzi. The living room is equipped with upholstered furniture, a dining table, and also has an additional bathroom. A full-fledged study with natural wood furniture is also equipped.




Hotel Villas

Fans of secluded relaxation will certainly appreciate the luxurious villas Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas offers them. Travelers will have the following choice:

  • Villa with private pool can be designed for 2 or 3 bedrooms, each of which is equipped with its own bathroom. There is also a shared lounge for spending time together. It features a small kitchen and dining room. Outdoors there is a spacious terrace surrounded by a decorative garden.
  • Some villas are located directly opposite the sea. They are neat two-story buildings that are also designed for 2 or 3 bedrooms. On the lower level there is a common living room with kitchen. There is a private terrace with sun loungers. Each of the bedrooms has access to a furnished balcony from which you can enjoy the sea.

Entertainment

Do not have to miss the guests of Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas 5 *. Vietnam is a great place for outdoor enthusiasts. Here you will have the following entertainment options available:

  • heated outdoor pool;

Catering establishments

Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas 5 * will delight travelers with excellent food and quality drinks. Guests can visit the following facilities on site:

  • The Greens & Sands restaurant is located on the ground floor of the residential building. Guests will be provided with tasty and quality food. Three times a day, guests are invited to a buffet table, where you can try both local and European dishes. Guests can sit in the spacious dining room or enjoy a delicious meal in the fresh air.
  • Aqua is a bar with panoramic sea views. The institution is open from 09:00 to 23:00. In addition to refreshing drinks, there is also a wide range of snacks and fast food.
  • The lobby bar will delight guests with its luxurious design and relaxed atmosphere. In the daytime, you can enjoy pastries with coffee. In the evenings, concerts and other recreational activities are held here.




Spa package

Of course, for active travelers, the hotel is just a place to sleep. Nevertheless, the Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas 5 * provides a so-called spa package, the acquisition of which is the key to a complete and carefree vacation. It includes the following items:

  • double accommodation in a suite overlooking the tropical garden;
  • daily access to the spa with the right to undergo 4 procedures;
  • meeting at the airport and transfer in the opposite direction on the day of departure;
  • unlimited voucher for visiting the water park;
  • three meals a day in the main restaurant;
  • welcome compliment in the form of a fruit basket;
  • the right to late eviction after 15:00 subject to the availability of rooms for new guests.

Additional Information

Before traveling to Vinpearl Golf Land Resort Villas 5 * it is important to familiarize yourself with some rules of accommodation in this institution. It is worth paying attention to the following data:

  • at 2:00 p.m. and 12:00 p.m. there are scheduled check-out hours during which check-in and check-out are carried out;
  • Children under 12 years old stay on existing beds for $ 37 per day, and for extra - for $ 55;
  • for placing adult guests on extra beds will be charged 91 dollars per day;
  • pets are not allowed;
  • upon check-in, guests are required to pay a security deposit in case of damage to hotel property (upon eviction, these funds will be returned if you did not cause material damage to the hotel);
  • a prerequisite for settlement is the provision of a passport and birth certificate for each of the children;
  • on New Year's holidays, the cost of a gala dinner is automatically included in the total amount for the stay.
