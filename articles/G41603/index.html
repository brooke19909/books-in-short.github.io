How to get started?

Investors will need to open an investment account and transfer funds to it for trading. To do this, just visit the nearest branch of a financial institution with a plastic card, TIN and passport. Then you need to fill out a statement in which you need to specify contact information. Reviews say that paperwork does not take more than 30 minutes. The Bank does not make specific requirements for the minimum deposit amount. Therefore, the size of the deposit directly depends on the solvency of the client.

The employee will provide the investor with rates, a questionnaire, a risk declaration, a copy of the documents and a code table card. After that, you should install the appropriate software, which is licensed by a financial institution. At the final stage, it will remain to use the brokerage services of Sberbank to start making money. If a client opens an account for the first time and does not understand the nuances of work, the credit institution assumes the responsibility of developing an investment strategy.

Tariff packages

Turning to the brokerage services of Sberbank, you should be aware of current tariffs. A credit institution attracts customers with affordable prices for financial products. The second provides the right to conduct independent activities in the QUIK system. For its correct operation, it is necessary to install the appropriate program on the PC. When connecting one of the listed packages, investors can work with at least two trading floors.
