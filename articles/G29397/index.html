Mysterious Switzerland. Lugano and his secrets

Mysterious and very interesting for any traveler is the country of Switzerland. Lugano - a city located on the coast of the mountain lake of the same name, is a real magnet that attracts tourists from different parts of the world from year to year. And this is not surprising, because, being located in the Italian part of Switzerland, this settlement combined both the features of southern cultures and the severity of alpine traditions.





If you are looking for variety, fun, the beauty of nature and architecture or just new experiences, the place you just need to go to is Switzerland, Lugano. In this seemingly small town you can relax, sunbathing on the beach and climbing the mountain peaks. It is located on the shores of Lake Lugano - one of the most beautiful not only in Europe, but throughout the world. Due to its high water and cleanliness, it resembles a sea bay, so windsurfing, sky surfing, jet skiing and other entertainment that are often found at sea resorts are very popular here. Tourists who prefer a more original vacation can go to the mountains, explore the incredible beauty of the flora and admire the landscapes that open from the largest peaks, which Switzerland is famous for.



Lugano is also one of the world centers of Shopping. In this city you can easily purchase branded items, paying at the same time half as much as you could do, say, in Milan. And for lovers of revelry, the Kursal entertainment complex is located in the center of the city, in which a record number of casinos, bars, restaurants, nightclubs and other institutions are uplifting.

As a rule, among countries that are considered warm and suitable for summer beach holidays and entertainment, strict and cool Switzerland is rarely mentioned. Lugano is an exception city, which has a very mild and warm climate. It is snowy and frosty only in the winter months, and starting in March, bushes and trees begin to bloom in this region, the air warms up, and Lake Lugano becomes warm. Switzerland appears before the tourist in a new guise, and having once been in this region, it is impossible not to want to return here again.



Since this city is one of the historical centers that go back to the far Middle Ages, there are also ancient buildings, which can be examined for a very long time. The Palazzo Civico Palace, located in the eponymous park in the city center, enjoys popularity. In the same place, concerts are often held in the open air, at which tourists and locals gather. Not far from the palace are the church of Santa Maria del Angola, Villa Malpensata and the incredible beauty of the Cathedral of San Rocco, made in the late Gothic style.

You can talk endlessly about the merits of this city, and when you learn about them, you involuntarily understand that the place you really want to visit is Lugano, Switzerland. Hotels in this city are famous for very reasonable prices - you can book a room in a 4-5 star complex for 5-6 thousand rubles a day. The prices for food and entertainment here, although higher than in the more popular resorts, are nevertheless fully justified.
