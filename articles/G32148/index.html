SEC "Rio" (Vologda). A brief overview of shops, entertainment, cafes

A large selection of the best shops, places for recreation and entertainment, a good movie theater - all this is in the Rio shopping center (Vologda). The most inveterate fashionistas of the city love to visit this place, because only here you can find clothing stores of the most famous world brands, and after a long and tiring shopping there is always the opportunity to relax and have a bite to eat at the Restaurant Courtyard cafe.



Cinema

One of the main entertainments in the shopping center "Rio" (Vologda) is a cinema. After all, this is where you can go with your soulmate or in the company of good friends to watch any movie novelty.

Cinema "Rio" is part of the cinema "Cinema Star". In the hall there are very comfortable soft chairs, the backs of which recline. Here, the best equipment for watching movies is installed and there is a huge screen with excellent image quality. Very pleased with the low prices for watching movies, as well as the timely appearance of new products.

Entertainment

In the shopping center "Rio" (Vologda) there are many places where you can spend your leisure time, having a rest from working days.

Together with friends, you can go bowling, which works every day. You only need to book a track in advance for a specific time. There are no age restrictions when visiting a bowling alley, so you can go there not only with friends, but also with your family. Various broadcasts are often held here and games with prizes are organized.

Together with the child you can go to the children's playground "Anthill", where you certainly have fun. It is worth noting that only children under 9 years old are allowed there. You always have the opportunity to send your baby to the "Anthill" and leave for pleasant purchases. You can be completely sure that your child is safe, since all the playgrounds of this complex are designed in full compliance with all standards. There are many attractions for children and games are held for the development of kids.

Before the New Year, the Selfibox photo booth appears on the first floor of the Rio shopping center (Vologda), where everyone has the opportunity to take a selfie or an instant photo on a passport.

And finally, entertainment that is perfect for both adults and children is the Mizar planetarium. It is equipped with four projectors that can project an image onto the dome, where, in fact, the screen itself is located. The hall is designed for 25 visitors. It is equipped with comfortable chairs that recline for a more comfortable viewing of the program.



Cafes and restaurants

After outdoor activities or tedious shopping, there is always a need to restore the energy spent. To do this, you can go to one of the cafes or a restaurant located in the Rio (Vologda) shopping center. Here you can be invited to visit the cozy atmosphere of the Chikibamboni cafe (next to the Cinema Star cinema), 33 penguins (on the second floor), Restaurant courtyard, and Tashir pizza (second floor).

Visitor Conditions

For the comfort of the guests of the Rio shopping and entertainment center (Vologda), on the ground floor of the center there is a parking for up to one thousand cars. The building has the best air conditioning, good ventilation and fire extinguishing system, for safety. For access to social networks there is free Wi-Fi with high speed. All these and many other conditions will make your stay in the Rio shopping center (Vologda) very comfortable.



Address: District Highway 12, Vologda city.

There is a shopping and entertainment complex every day, from 10 am to 9 pm.
