Enki Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Tourists who want to relax inexpensively in Turkey have long discovered a small hotel on the coast - Enki Hotel 3 *. Accommodation prices are affordable. However, the quality of service does not suffer. Savings are created due to certain services that not every vacationer needs. In other words, those who do not have special requirements for the service and just want to spend their holidays on the beach can safely choose this particular hotel. However, before booking a room here it is not superfluous to familiarize yourself with the services that this hotel offers. This will save you from possible unexpected situations.

general information

Enki Hotel 3 * is a small cozy hotel located in one of the most beautiful and most beloved tourist areas of Turkey - the city of Alanya. The building was built in 1997 and completely renovated in 2015. Now it is a mini-hotel, represented by a 5-storey building, which consists of 86 rooms. For added convenience, the building has an elevator.

This hotel offers all-inclusive accommodation. The cleanliness and comfort that prevail in the rooms have a good rest. Even couples who come with their children will feel comfortable here.





The territory adjacent to the hotel is carefully thought out. There are 2 outdoor pools, places for relaxation, green spaces, trimmed lawns.

Hotel Location

In Turkey, Enki Hotel 3 * is located in the city of Alanya, just 2 km from its center. Tourists booking rooms here get to the country by plane to the airport in the city of Antalya. From here to the hotel Enki Hotel 3 * can be reached by taxi. The distance is 127 km, so the journey will take about 1.5 hours.

Near the hotel there are shops and attractions, so vacationers will be here as comfortable as possible. The beach is 300 meters away - this is the 3rd coastline. You can walk on foot in 5-7 minutes.

Hotel facilities

The hotel provides everything for a comfortable stay for guests of the spa town:

  • Registration desk. It is available to guests 24 hours a day.
  • Room cleaning. The maids clean the rooms daily, so the rooms are always clean, fresh and comfortable.
  • Regular change of bedding in the rooms and towels in the bathroom. It is carried out 1 time in 3 days.
  • Luggage storage. Luggage storage is also available at the front desk.
  • Wi-fi Throughout the hotel guests can use the Internet for free.
  • Air conditioners. All common areas have air conditioning (lobby, restaurants).
  • Dry cleaning and laundry. Hotel guests can use this service for a fee.
  • Guarded car parking. You can park your car at the hotel.
  • Tour agency. Hotel staff can help with tickets for excursions and local activities.




Room Amenities

The hotel has 86 rooms. Among them:

  • standard rooms - 72 (room area 22 m 2 );
  • family rooms - 14 (each area is 45 m 2 ).

In total, the hotel has a capacity of 210 beds.

The number of beds depends on the type of room:

  • Enki Hotel 3 * standard (standard) - guests can choose 2 single or 1 double bed;
  • triple rooms - there are 2 beds (single and double);
  • family rooms - there are 3 single beds and 1 double.

Families traveling with children can request an extra baby crib in their room.

Each room is carefully equipped for the maximum comfort of guests. Here you will find everything you need:

  • telephone;
  • air conditioning;
  • TV with satellite television;
  • dressing table and wardrobe for clothes;
  • hair dryer.

The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. There is a shower and toilet. Tourists are offered a set of free toiletries. Therefore, vacationers do not have to bring a lot of hygiene items with them. Soap, a roll of toilet paper, cotton pads and, of course, shampoo are issued.

Towels are issued per person for free:

  • very small for the hands;
  • large bath;
  • special for the legs.




Each room has a balcony with a wonderful view of the surroundings.

Once upon arrival, the minibar is filled with mineral water.

Entertainment at the hotel Enki Hotel 3 *

You can have a good rest here without leaving the hotel at all. Pools will help to have fun by the water. There are 3 of them at once, they are all open.

  • The pool is large. Its depth is 1.5 meters, and the parameters are 15 by 7 m. This pool is equipped with 2 water slides.
  • Small in size pool. Its depth does not exceed 1.4 meters, the dimensions are also more modest - 7.5 by 7 m.
  • The pool is specially for children. Dimensions - 3 * 5 m. Bathing here will be safe even for the smallest, however, leaving babies unattended in the water is still not recommended.

The water in the pools changes regularly, disinfection is carried out, so it is always clean and pleasant to swim here.

From free entertainment the hotel offers:

  • equipment for table tennis;
  • billiards;
  • animation program (conducted in several languages);
  • darts;
  • use of sun loungers and umbrellas by the pools.




health and beauty

In addition to beach activities, the hotel has the opportunity to take care of their health and appearance.

  • Sport. A fitness center will help you keep fit. The hall is well equipped and is perfect for daily visits.
  • The hotel has a massage room where experienced masters conduct massage sessions. You need to pay for this pleasure separately.
  • SPA-center. Peeling procedures, masks, wraps. The cost must be clarified on the spot.
  • Sauna. Customers can choose at their own request a Finnish or Turkish bath (hammam). These services are paid separately.

Nutrition

The Enki Hotel 3 * hotel operates on an all-inclusive system, and that is why meals in the restaurant are already included in the price. The following establishments work here:

  • a restaurant;
  • pool bar;
  • a store where you can buy everything you need.

Restaurant Enki Hotel 3 * specializes in local cuisine, so most dishes will be Turkish. The food here is organized like a buffet. Three meals a day are offered to guests:

  • breakfast is served from 07:00 to 10:00 in the morning;
  • for lunch, guests are expected from 12:30 to 14:00;
  • dinner in the restaurant runs from 19:00 to 21:00.

All food is exceptionally fresh and varied. Always available dishes of meat, fish, poultry. There is an abundance of fresh fruit.





The poolside bar is open from 10:00 to 23:00. Here you can always drink freshly squeezed juices, as well as delicious Turkish coffee.

  • From 14:00 to 16:00, snacks are served.
  • From 16:00 to 17:00 you can come for afternoon tea.

In the period after 23:30, the order of any drink (regardless of whether it will be alcoholic or not) will be paid.

The restaurant has dishes for children, so vacationers with kids will not have nutrition problems.

Attractions in the area

Those who come to Turkey not only for a beach holiday, but also in search of sights and interesting excursions, are advised to visit several places located near the Enki Hotel 3 * Alanya.

Ataturk House Museum in Alanya. This house has become an architectural monument of the 19th century. It was here that in 1935 the founder of the Republic of Turkey, Atat√ºrk, was resting. The museum is 3.5 km away.

Archaeological Museum in Alanya. The history of this city has nearly 2,000 years. It is in the museum that archaeological finds from different periods are collected. The museum is 4.9 km from the hotel.

Red tower. The fortress, which has become a classic example of Seljuk architectural art. It is 4.3 km from the hotel Enki Hotel 3 *.

Waterpark in Alanya. A visit to the water park will appeal to both children and adults.

Natural beauty lovers can head to the harbor (4.7 km from the hotel), Dalmatash Caves (4.9 km), and Alanya Gardens (4 km).





Beach

Tourists are invited to relax on the municipal sand and pebble beach. It is clean enough, as the coastal area is regularly cleaned. You can use sun loungers and umbrellas, however, such services are provided for a fee.

The entrance to the sea is gentle, convenient. This allows you to relax in this resort, even with children. Nearby there is a bar where tourists are offered refreshments, snacks, fast food.

There is enough entertainment on the beach.

  • Diving. This coast is famous for its picturesque underwater world. Such amusement can be chosen even by beginners in this business. There is rental equipment for diving, experienced instructors work with divers.
  • Riding on yachts, motor boats, water skiing.
  • Parasailing. Parachuting, which is tied to a boat, has gained wide popularity among thrill-seekers.




In addition, you can always take part in beach volleyball and other entertainment.

Additional Information

Enki Hotel 3 * Alanya Turkey operates on an all-inclusive basis, so drinks and meals are included in the room rate at the pool bar and restaurant. However, there are some limitations. Taxes are already included in the invoice. Possible costs for tips to staff.

Room rates do not include:

  • some alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks (imported and elite);
  • mini bar;
  • bottled alcoholic drinks.

Check-in to the rooms of Enki Hotel 3 * starts at 14:00.

Departure - noon.

Children under 6 years old can stay at the hotel for free if they are not provided with a separate bed.

The hotel strictly prohibits pets.

Smoking is allowed on site only in specially equipped areas.

Enki Hotel 3 *: reviews

In the hotel you can often meet Russian-speaking tourists, which is not at all surprising. Despite the fact that the hotel has the status of a budget complex, it has gained good fame among travelers.

Among the advantages of a vacation at Enki Hotel 3 * in Alanya, reviews most often note the following points:

  • Russian-speaking staff who are distinguished by courtesy;
  • cleanliness of the rooms (thorough daily cleaning);
  • comfortable apartments, pretty good furniture;
  • delicious fresh food - many Russian tourists greatly praise the cuisine of the Enki Hotel 3 *;
  • constant availability of fresh fruit;
  • many attractions (there is much to go, what to do);
  • nice sandy beach (there is pebble at the entrance to the water).

In general, the popularity of Enki Hotel 3 * in Alanya is fully justified. At a relatively low cost (from 4,000 rubles per day), vacationers are offered comfortable living conditions. Three meals a day, animation program, water rides on the hotel territory deserve attention. An undoubted advantage is the long swimming season in Turkey (from May to October), picturesque surroundings and well-developed tourist infrastructure (vacationers will definitely not be bored).
