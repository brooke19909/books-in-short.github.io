How to reset an Apple ID account: setup, reset, account deletion, instructions and recommendations

An Apple ID account is an important component, without it the owner of the "apple" device will not be able to work with the existing functionality of his device. For example, purchases through the App Store will be closed. In addition, you will not be able to activate the Find iPhone option, as well as use the iCloud cloud service. Therefore, for normal operation with iOS, you will have to create Apple ID. Unfortunately, registration data is forgotten over time. And in some cases, you have to think about how to reset your Apple ID account. Can this be done at all? Below you will find information about the mentioned "apple" account, as well as how to work with it. For example, how to disable a profile or restore access to it. Such information is useful to every owner of Apple products.

Profile Description

What is an Apple ID? This is what they call a certain "identity card" of a person in the iOS system. An account is a profile with which the user can use all the existing functions of the "apple" devices. If you want to back up data or restore your iPhone, Apple ID will help. The main thing is to know how to work with this profile.

Managing your Apple ID account is not as difficult as it sounds. First you will have to register a new profile and come up with a password to enter the system. After that, it remains to go through authorization on the mobile device and study the options offered by Apple.

Unfortunately, sometimes you may need to know how to reset your Apple ID account. There are many ways to cope with this task. Usually, the algorithm of actions is chosen taking into account the life situation. Below we will consider all possible circumstances and means of recovery / reset "Apple ID".

Logging in

But first, a few words about how to properly manage the "apple" account. Consider two main functions: login and logout.

Let's start with the first operation. For her it is necessary:

  1. Open the "Settings" of your "apple" smartphone or tablet.
  2. Go to the Apple ID block.
  3. Tap on the corresponding inscription and indicate in the "Identifier" field the email address to which the account is linked.
  4. In the "Required" section, write a password to log into the system.
  5. Click on "Login" and wait.

After a few seconds, data will be downloaded from the Apple ID account. The user will be able to work with his account without much difficulty.

Important: to log in to Apple ID, you must have an Internet connection on your mobile device.

Sign out

How do I log out of my Apple ID account? Such an operation can also be useful to modern owners of "apple" devices. For example, if you need to reset your Apple account before selling.

It is recommended to act in this way:

  3. Switch to Apple ID section.
  4. Click on username. Information on the owner of the questionnaire will appear on the device‚Äôs display and a small functional menu will light up.

Reset and restore methods

Recover password: mail help

The first way to recover your password: use e-mail from your account. To use this technique, the user must:

  1. Go to the Apple account page.
  2. Click on the words "Forgot your password?". It is located under the authorization log in the system.
  3. Provide your Apple ID.
  4. Choose the team you want to execute. In our case, this is "Reset Password".
  5. Decide on how to restore access to your Apple account. For example, Email.

  7. Click on the "Reset Password" hyperlink.
  8. Go to the tab with the password recovery form and specify the new data for authorization (two times).
  9. Submit a processing request.

What's next? In fact, the password has been reset. You can log in to your Apple ID and unlink your account from the apple device or re-link it to another email.

Change mail

How to delete an Apple ID account? Once access to it is restored, you can choose from several available and fairly quick solutions. The first is manual logoff; it has already been described. The second trick is working with the settings of the "apple" account. For example, you can reassign your Apple ID to a different email address.

What is needed for this? The owner of the "apple" device is recommended to act as follows:

  1. Open Apple ID page.
  2. Log in to your profile using login and password.
  3. Click on the item "Manage ...".
  4. Scroll through the menu that appears. You need to stop near the e-mail section.
  5. Click on the "Edit" hyperlink.
  6. Enter the address of the new mail to which you want to bind the "apple" account.
  7. Save corrections.

As a rule, now Apple ID will be re-associated. The old email address is exempted from the "apple" profile. Mail can be used to register the new Apple ID.

Important: Similarly, you can reassign your Apple ID via iTunes.

Complete removal
