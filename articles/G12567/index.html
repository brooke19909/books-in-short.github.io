MSC: the essence of the concept

This concept arose only recently - 3 years ago. Then the state wanted to minimize the costs of motorists, namely, to reduce the cost of compulsory motor third-party liability insurance for so-called accident-free drivers. In other words, if the car owner did not become the culprit of the accident during the insurance period, then he can count on a certain amount of discounts. The amount of benefits for the driver and determines the very KBM.





Varieties of KBM

MSC can take on different meanings. Its exact size depends on the accident-free experience of the driver. The more years a car owner has not been involved in an accident, the more profitable for him will be the purchase of an insurance policy.
