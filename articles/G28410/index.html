LCD "Dominant" in Volgograd: description, infrastructure

In the southeast of the European part of Russia there is a rather large city - Volgograd. He is often chosen for permanent residence when moving from the Far North and the Far East due to the temperate continental climate. Developers are offering more and more new projects for implementation, including the Dominant residential complex in Volgograd.

A bit about the city

Volgograd is a city with a population of more than a million people. Mild winters, quite hot and long summers attract residents from more unfavorable regions in terms of climate. A well-developed construction, chemical, metallurgical industry, the presence of numerous manufacturing enterprises - all this only contributes to the fact that the influx of population does not stop. Many people prefer not to purchase real estate outside the city, but to use all the benefits of urban infrastructure. For those who wish and built LCD "Dominant" in Volgograd.

Location of the complex

On the border of the Sovetsky District, on Turkmenskaya Street, three 24-storey residential buildings were built and commissioned - this is Dominant residential complex in Volgograd. The apartments here are different: from one-room to three-room. Thanks to such a good choice of location, from the complex to the city center, you can quickly reach major transport routes, both with your own car and public transport. Travel time does not take more than 10 minutes. Also within walking distance is the metro station "Elshanka".
