The concept of "geolocation"

What is geolocation? Geolocation is data that reports in real time the exact location of a computer, tablet or phone and, accordingly, its owner. Through this service, data such as the country in which the subscriber is located, the city, street and house are established.





How it works

An indispensable condition for the service to work is to connect the device to the Internet. Each mobile device has special software that allows you to track its current geolocation.





Thanks to the Internet connection, the service determines the location of the device using the current IP address of the subscriber. What is a geolocation service, we reviewed above. Now you need to understand how it works.

Why do you need

Now many programs developed for smartphones, tablets and personal computers, upon registration, and some during use, request data of the current geo-location.

Some programs need this in order to place this data in the client profile; other users might see their real location.

Applications designed for orientation on the ground, which request geolocation, in order to tell the subscriber where exactly he is at a given time, help to get the shortest route to the right place.

Work with search queries

Geolocation is of great importance when processing user searches. What is geolocation in a search engine and how does it help?

Depending on the location of the subscriber, search engines provide relevant answers to his requests. This data filtering is very convenient and saves time on finding the right information.

So, for example, when asking about how much it costs and where to buy a new car, the system will first show sites that advertise cars for sale in nearby cities.

In mobile devices

What is geolocation in the phone? The included service helps determine where the nearest cafes, restaurants, theaters, fitness centers, etc. are located.





In addition, the included geolocation system in the phone will help you find the device in case of loss or theft. This service will be valid even if the SIM card was replaced when the phone was stolen. The main thing is that the Internet continues to work. It can be both the Internet, working with a SIM card, and Wi-Fi.

Service cost

This service is completely free. The only thing that costs money or megabytes is the traffic used to download maps. If the phone uses exclusively the Internet of a cellular operator, then payment will be charged according to the tariff to which the subscriber is connected.

If the mobile device uses exclusively Wi-Fi or is connected to unlimited Internet from a mobile operator, then only Internet traffic will be consumed.

For business

What is geolocation for business? How can she help in its development? By tracking the demand for certain products in specific regions, the company can change the pricing policy in its branches. So, for example, you can set lower prices for goods that are not in demand.





In addition, for the convenience of customers, it is possible for each region to indicate prices in the currency used for payment.

What is geolocation for advertising? Thanks to the location of customers, it is possible to place advertising banners aimed at specific consumers interested in these products.

How to connect

What is geolocation in a smartphone? How to connect it and how to use it? On the phones of the fourth series, to enable the function of determining your current location, you need to go to "Settings". In this menu you need to find an item called "Geolocation" and activate this function by moving the key to the side.

After enabling this function, you will be asked to choose which programs you allow to use data about your location.

Next, you will be prompted to select a time zone. You can activate a function that will show on the main screen that the geolocation service is activated on your device.
