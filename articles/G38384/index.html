Night club "Platinum" in Chita - a favorite place for youth

During the working week, we all look forward to the weekend. After all, this is an opportunity to take a break from the routine work of bosses and colleagues. Each has its own ideal vacation option: someone likes to sit at home with his family, while others prefer more active entertainment. Among the young generation, nightclubs, karaoke cafes and bars are very popular. It is not surprising that nightlife exists in almost every city. One of the best in Chita is the Platinum night club. In the article we will talk more about him.

Night club "Platinum" in Chita: useful information

Do you like dancing until the morning to incendiary music from the best DJs? Then the night club "Platinum" is what you need for a perfect vacation! He opened his doors to youth in March 2013 and immediately began to be very popular among residents. Address of the Platinum night club in Chita: 40 Lazo street.

An undoubted advantage of this institution is the availability of convenient parking with a large number of spaces. Outside, the clubhouse is decorated with a bright, neon sign that shines with many lights in the evening, attracting visitors. At the entrance is a rather severe face control. The imposing guards monitor the calm in the club all night. They do not allow drunken fights and, if necessary, will come to the rescue. Also, do not forget that the club is an entertainment facility, there is a dress code in it. Too elegantly (long dresses on the floor, suits) should not be worn, but visitors are not allowed in tattered, dirty clothes. Entrance for girls until 01-00 is free, then the cost is 100 rubles. For young people - 200 rubles throughout the night. Opening hours: from Thursday to Saturday from 22-00 to 06-00.

Interior

Inside the club is very spacious, which allows a large number of visitors to gather in it. The interior of the club is dominated by exotic, slightly mystical motifs from the countries of Mexico and South America. There is a bar in the room where experienced bartenders make amazing cocktails, and you can also order delicious hot dishes and cold snacks.

You can book a table, there are a lot of them in the Platinum night club (Chita). They are designed for both two people and a large company. Table reservations are made upon advance payment. Privacy lovers are advised to sit in the VIP zone. There are much fewer outsiders and a little quieter. Another plus of the VIP zone is the ability to smoke a hookah (it is forbidden to do this in other places in the club).

A huge dance floor is intended for dancing, where there is enough space for everyone to have fun. Charming dancers with incendiary numbers perform on stage. The host creates the desired mood. He holds funny pranks and contests, as well as constantly sprinkles jokes and jokes. Do not know where to relax in Chita? We recommend visiting this entertainment venue.

Delicious food and drinks

Now let's talk about those dishes in the menu of the Platinum nightclub in Chita that are most often ordered by visitors. We start with food, and then move on to drinks.

The cuisine is represented by the best dishes of European cuisine. Be sure to try Caesar salad (a delicious combination of chicken and cheese) and Greek (spicy olives, fresh lettuce and cherry tomatoes). They also make delicious pizza. The most popular: Carbonara (ham, bacon and a lot of cheese) and Chicken (the most tender combination of chicken and mushrooms). For lovers of Japanese cuisine there is the opportunity to order rolls and sushi. They are made only from quality, fresh products.

The alcoholic card will be enjoyed by the most sophisticated connoisseur of alcoholic beverages. Champagne, wine, cognac, tequila and beer are available. The cocktails made by the bartenders of the Platinum Club are considered the most delicious in the whole city. Prices are quite reasonable. The average check is from 500 rubles. There is both cash and bank transfer.

Visitors reviews

Young people relaxing in the Platinum night club (Chita) leave mostly positive comments about this place. Among the main advantages are the presence of parking, the absence of drunken fights and rowdy, a nutritious and varied bar menu, good alcohol. They also respond well to club employees, noting their professionalism and friendliness.

If we talk about the cons, then some visitors complain about the lack of free tables. Therefore, if you are planning to spend the evening time in Chita in Platinum, reserve a place in advance. Have a great and memorable stay!
