Crocheted knitted stole. Patterns and colors

On cool winter evenings you really want to wrap yourself in a warm fluffy blanket. This thought haunts us relentlessly not only at home, but also at work. And if you can afford weakness in the bedroom or in front of the TV, then in the office you will look at least strange, wrapping yourself in a plaid! However, there is always an alternative. Knitted stoles. Crocheted or knitted, collected from various motifs ...



How to crochet stoles

Schemes can be used very different! If you want, you can make the product in the form of a wide scarf, across which thick braids or rhombs go. Depending on the chosen color of yarn, you can get both a delicate female model and a stylized stole in unisex style. As a rule, they try to make them out of woolen or half-woolen yarn, since the main task of this wardrobe item is to warm its owner in the cool season! It is good to knit several palatines at once, from yarn of different quality and color. It is safe to say that deciding to crochet the palatines, schemes are not needed. The rectangular shape is a distinctive feature of the product. In order to make a product, you need to determine how wide the stoles you plan to knit. Then dial the desired number of air loops and start crocheting the stoles. The scheme is very simple - observe the number of loops in each row. Only in this way the finished thing will be the same width and will look very neat. If you are going to make a warm model - knit in a column with one, maximum, with two crochets. It makes no sense to make a thick and warm stole patterned, as this will significantly reduce the amount of heat it retains.

What color to choose, deciding to crochet stoles

Patterns and color are the main points that need to be thought out in advance. What are you going to wear it with? If together with office clothes, and the company has a clear dress code stipulating the colors of the suit or dress, it is recommended to take this into account when choosing yarn. Corporate colors - red, white, blue and greenish - then you can choose only from them. Otherwise, the company management has the right to make a comment to you. You should also carefully consider the drawing. When crocheting palatines, drawing patterns, its size - all this must be taken into account. Only in this case, such a thing will become an organic addition to a work suit or dress.



Looks great models made of yarn called "grass". They create an imitation of a fur cape. Of course, only if the shades are selected elegant. Otherwise, you risk becoming a peacock amid gray office staff.

Crochet Poncho



Schemes are absolutely necessary for such a product. In addition, you still need the correct pattern, which allows you to make a model that is fully consistent with your standards. In the process of knitting, you can combine different shades of yarn, use embroidery elements on an already finished fabric. In any case, you will get a warm and beautiful cape that will warm in cool and damp weather!
