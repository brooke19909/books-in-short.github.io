Park-hotel "Salyn" (Bryansk region): description, service, reviews

Tourists are more inclined to travel and leisure within the country. Thus, you can save on expensive flights and enjoy the beauties of Russia. Park Hotel Salyn has created all the conditions for a comfortable and useful stay for its guests. They seek to increase the level of service to the maximum.

Where is it located and how does it work

The complex is located in the Dubrovsky district of the Bryansk region. Directly, it is located near the village of Salyn, in whose honor the hotel is named. You can get here from the Dubrovka district center by regular buses or taxis.

Also, from the regional center (Bryansk), fixed-route taxis move from bus stations in this direction. By personal transport you need to go along the highway Orel-Smolensk towards Dubrovka.





Park Hotel Salyn operates year-round. The buildings have modern heating, so the guests of the complex feel comfortable in the cold season.

Park-hotel "Salyn": description

The hotel complex is surrounded by forest, there is a lake nearby. Here you can see landscapes that great artists would love to paint. At any time of the year, a picture of wildlife is pleasing to the eye at the Salyn Park Hotel. Here you only want to breathe deeply and forget about all the city problems and fuss.

The complex consists of a hotel, cottages and townhouses. In addition, other buildings are located on the territory, which houses restaurants, treatment rooms and sports facilities.

The hotel is built in the style of old Prague. Cottages are made of natural wooden beams. Walking sidewalks are laid throughout. In the evening, the complex is lit by decorative lanterns.

Service in the park-hotel "Salyn"

In the complex you can not only relax and have fun, but also undergo a course of treatment and prevention of various diseases:

  • cardiovascular;
  • neurological;
  • respiratory system;
  • metabolic disorders;
  • spine and joints.

To do this, in the park-hotel "Salyn" in the Bryansk region a special medical center is equipped and various procedures and technologies are used:

  • phototherapy;
  • inhalation;
  • salt cave;
  • hydrotherapy;
  • physiotherapy;
  • magnetotherapy;
  • massage room;
  • laser therapy.




The complex has a special program "Mother and Child". Vacationers with children from 3 years old can use it for health improvement with the permission of pediatricians. In the medical center, KUF-therapy is carried out for children often suffering from acute respiratory viral infections.

The resort also has a modern center for SPA-procedures. In it, vacationers can take care of the skin of the face and body:

  • wraps;
  • cosmetic procedures;
  • massages using various components;
  • sauna.

There is a special program for cleansing the body. It includes phyto-enemas, specially designed diets, exercises in the gym, massage of the abdomen.

Food and additional services

Park Hotel "Salyn" refers to institutions operating on an all-inclusive basis. This means that meals are included in the price. There is a spacious dining room for hotel residents. They eat on the buffet system.

The menu necessarily includes fresh vegetables and fruits by season, greens, cereals, soups, a variety of snacks, meat and fish dishes, side dishes. Be sure to put on the shelves desserts and pastries. Drinks are also served in a good assortment.

Guests can request a cot, high chair in the dining room, ironing board and iron. On a day, tourists can rent a bicycle, scooter, boat, table tennis and billiards for free for 1 hour.

The complex has a bathhouse and a sauna. You can also ride horses at the equestrian club. There are two bars and one large restaurant on site. You can also shop at the grocery and gift shops.





For an extra charge, tourists use the laundry facilities. The administration arranges excursion programs. There is a modern entertainment center on site. Celebration of any celebrations, including weddings, is organized.

Entertainment

The complex has a children's center. The room is spacious and it is always warm. There is a soft trampoline, a pool with colorful balls, a large soft designer, there are blasters with foam balls. There are tables for creative activities, board games.

The territory is equipped with a jungle liana park. It has rope mazes, a net, slides, a Swedish wall. Here children of different ages spend time with animators. They arrange contests, quests and competitions.





In the warmer months, the beach at the Salyn Park Hotel operates on the lake. In winter, a natural ice rink forms here. A tube slide is also formed.

The complex has an entertainment center "Gloss". In it you can sing karaoke and participate in a show program organized by animators. Fans of outdoor activities can also play table football and dance at a disco.
