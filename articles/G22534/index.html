Hotel Baron Resort 5 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): overview, features and reviews

Sharm El Sheikh is the most popular resort in Egypt, providing great opportunities for both outdoor enthusiasts and those who like to spend their holidays calmly and leisurely. Choosing a Baron Resort 5 * hotel for yourself, you can be sure that complete with the hot sun, well-groomed beaches and azure waves, you will also receive high-quality service, dizzying entertainment and a cozy modern interior.

Description

The hotel complex Baron Resort 5 * is a luxurious modern hotel in which each guest can feel like a real titled person. We can say that this complex fully justifies the availability of five-star accreditation.

Well-trained, friendly staff will be happy to help and make your stay as comfortable as possible. Developed infrastructure and a huge number of services will make your leisure time full and interesting. Special attention should be paid to the interior of the hotel, which helps to create a pleasant romantic atmosphere.

Respect for guests is the philosophy of the Baron Resort 5 * hotel. It all starts with such pleasant things as natural cotton linen in the rooms. In addition, the hotel staff took care of a varied and satisfying diet of tourists and a rich entertainment program.





Location

Hotel Baron Resort 5 * is located quite favorably with respect to the main infrastructure facilities:

  • the distance from the Sharm El Sheikh International Airport to the hotel complex is only 8 km, and to the city center 15 km (this is no more than 20 minutes if you take a taxi);
  • the hotel is 3 km from the famous Soho Square, and 15 km from the picturesque Naama Bay;
  • if you want to visit the Ras Mohammed National Park, the hotel staff will gladly organize a tour for you, and after an hour and a half boat trip you will be able to fully enjoy all the beauties of this beautiful place.

Rooms

The hotel Baron Palace Resort Sahl Hasheesh 5 * has 360 rooms, which are divided into several categories:

  • Double Rooms - spacious rooms with a huge double bed. In addition to the bedroom, the apartments also have a living room and a dressing room. Each room in this category has a balcony or a private exit to the terrace.
  • Twin Rooms - like the previous category, these rooms are designed for double occupancy. The only difference is that two single beds act as sleeping places.
  • Executive &. Junior Suites - this category of rooms belongs to the class of "Junior Suite". The apartments have a bedroom with a large double bed, a living room, a dressing room and a terrace. A distinctive feature of this category of rooms can be considered a picturesque view overlooking the island of Tiran.
  • Royal Suites are royal suites that can compete in size with a full-fledged city apartment. There is a bedroom, living room, study, bar and dining room. In addition, on your private terrace you will have the opportunity to splash around in your own pool.

Each room, regardless of category, has the following technical equipment:

  • TV with a large selection of cable channels;
  • telephone for communication with the reception;
  • mini bar;
  • built-in hair dryer.




Infrastructure

Since the territory of the Baron Palace Resort Sahl Hasheesh 5 * hotel is closed, and it is located at a certain distance from the city infrastructure facilities, it is very important that all necessary services are available to vacationers. So, the infrastructure of this hotel complex includes a number of objects. It:

  • Reception that is open around the clock. For any questions you may have, you can contact the reception staff in person or contact them by phone.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning staff will help you tidy up your clothes.
  • At any time, you can exchange your current currency at one of the specialized points at a favorable rate.
  • Souvenir and grocery stores are at your disposal.
  • There is a well-maintained parking with 24-hour security.
  • For various kinds of business or special occasions, a conference room is open for you, which can accommodate a maximum of 235 people. There are also 4 similar rooms, but with a smaller capacity (up to 40 people).
  • You can check in your luggage in a special left-luggage office.
  • If necessary, you will be provided with a consultation with a doctor.
  • A tour desk is constantly working, providing a huge selection of tourist routes.
  • A beauty salon where professional masters will provide you with a wide range of hairdressing and other types of services.
  • For guests with disabilities, all the necessary amenities are provided.
  • There is a well-kept private beach, where each guest is provided with free sunbeds, towels and sun umbrellas.

Entertainment

To make your vacation rich and vibrant, the staff of the Baron Resort 5 * hotel (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh) have prepared an entertainment program that includes the following leisure options:

  • Two large well-groomed tennis courts with all the necessary equipment. At night, the field is illuminated, so you can enjoy the game at any time convenient for you.
  • For sports enthusiasts, there are such opportunities as parachuting, athletics, as well as bicycle rental and horse riding.
  • A huge selection of marine activities, among which canoes, jet skis and skis are especially popular.
  • The diving center, located on the hotel‚Äôs private beach, offers not only underwater excursions, but also a full cycle of training for all the intricacies of immersion.
  • There are several well-maintained golf courses.
  • Especially popular is the entertainment called Bedouin Dinner. It involves going to a nearby village where locals introduce you to traditional cuisine, customs and folklore.
  • In the modern SPA-center you can get massage and acupuncture services, take a steam bath in the Russian bath or Turkish hammam, as well as visit the modern gym equipped with various gym equipment.
  • For lovers of swimming, there is a large freshwater pool, equipped in accordance with Olympic standards.
  • Separately equipped swimming pool with Jacuzzi and hydromassage, filled with real sea water.




Taking care of children

Hotel Baron Resort 5 * with special awe refers to providing high-quality children's leisure. So, for the smallest clients of the hotel complex the following set of services is provided:

  • All children from 4 to 12 years old have the right to visit the club, where they have prepared fun games and other entertainments. The opening hours are from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
  • There is a spacious games room with a large arsenal of toys, as well as professional animators.
  • There is a special playground with attractions in the courtyard of the hotel.
  • There is a special pool, developed according to special standards, providing a comfortable and safe children's holiday.
  • A special menu has been developed for the youngest visitors to the hotel.
  • A babysitting service is available for a fee.

restaurants and bars

One of the main features of the famous oriental hospitality is the abundance of delicious and satisfying treats. The network of elite restaurants and bars working on the territory of the Baron Resort 5 * hotel (Sharm El Sheikh) does an excellent job of this function:

  • Khan El Khalili Cafe is a thematic establishment decorated in the style of ancient Egyptian palaces. This cafe specializes in hookahs, as well as all kinds of traditional oriental drinks, which include both alcoholic and non-alcoholic.
  • Internet Cafe & Billiard Bar - here tourists will have a good time playing a game of billiards or having a glass of beer with fresh popcorn. In addition, customers of the institution have the right to use wireless Internet.
  • AI Sakia Sea Food Restaurant & Grill - on a cozy open area on the sea coast you can enjoy a variety of fish dishes.
  • Sinai is a restaurant specializing in cuisines from around the world. A feature of the institution is an open kitchen, so you can personally observe the whole process of creating culinary masterpieces.
  • Egyptian Restaurant - specializes in traditional Egyptian cuisine. The concept of the institution is emphasized by the interior, designed in accordance with national characteristics.
  • Bella Vista - in this restaurant you can fully experience all the features and subtleties of traditional Italian cuisine.
  • Palm Beach Restaurant & Bar - Offers a wide range of tropical alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails. The facility is located in such a way that visitors can enjoy a picturesque view of the island of Tiran.
  • Taj Mahal - a restaurant serving national Indian cuisine. the eastern atmosphere is emphasized by the traditional interior painted with columns and stained-glass windows.
  • Oasis Pool Restaurant & Bar - round the clock offers a wide range of drinks and snacks to visitors to the Olympic pool.
  • Panorama Lobby Bar & Lounge - open to guests in the hotel lobby.

You can verify the quality of services provided by the Baron Resort Sahl Hasheesh 5 * hotel by reading the many reviews of tourists who have previously visited this resort and hotel complex. The following features were especially praised by vacationers:

  • Convenient location relative to the sea coast and natural attractions;
  • the presence on the beach loungers of clean soft mattresses;
  • daily housekeeping, as well as the replacement of bed linen and towels;
  • fast registration and resettlement;
  • spacious area with a huge amount of greenery;

Helpful information

Any hotel or large resort and hotel complex has certain internal rules. Baron Palace Resort Sahl 5 * is no exception. Going on vacation to this hotel, check out some of the features:

  • Use of the wireless Internet is free only in public areas of the hotel. Those who wish to use this service in their own room will be charged extra.
  • Check-in with children of any age is possible. Moreover, if the child does not require additional space, then his stay is provided absolutely free.
  • It is not allowed to place more than one extra bed in any room.
  • It is strictly forbidden to check into a hotel with pets, regardless of type and size.
  • When checking into a hotel with one child, his nutrition program will not differ from an adult.
  • In restaurants operating on the territory of the complex, there is a dress code system. Visiting these establishments is available only in evening or closed everyday clothing. An exception is made only at lunchtime for those restaurants that operate outdoors.
  • The honeymoon room category is not suitable for children.




bonus program

The Baron Palace Resort 5 * hotel, by analogy with other hotel complexes, maintains the tradition of providing compliments to certain categories of customers.

With special trepidation, the staff and management of the hotel are among the newlyweds who have chosen this hotel complex for their honeymoon. Upon arrival, they are provided with fruit, as well as a pleasant surprise in the form of creative design of the room. When booking a tour, be sure to inform about your desire to participate in the bonus program, and also do not forget to bring a marriage certificate with you.

Those guests who celebrate their birthday during their stay at the Baron Resort Sharm-El-Sheikh 5 * hotel will not be left without attention. A pleasant surprise for them will be a birthday cake. To take advantage of this privilege, you must be sure to inform about your date of birth at the stage of booking, as well as during direct registration at the hotel.

Hotel Baron Resort 5 * Sharm especially appreciates its regular guests and provides them with a basket of fruits during check-in. To get into this category, you need to be a visitor to a hotel complex two or more times. In addition, a hotel employee must be informed of their desire to receive a compliment at the time of check-in.





The hotel complex Baron Resort Sharm-El-Sheikh 5 * Deluxe is a truly paradise place that will make your stay in Egypt rich and unforgettable. Arriving at this resort once, you will not want to look for another place to spend your vacation. You will come back here again and again for vivid impressions and dizzying entertainments.
