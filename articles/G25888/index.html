Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * (Turkey / Belek / Kadriye): photos and reviews of tourists

For many Russians who like to spend their holidays in Turkey, Belek resort has become an almost native home . Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * is a country hotel located near this village. Until September 1 of last year, it was called the Verde Resort, and still on the sites of some tour operators it can be found under this name. Although its guests mostly come here on sea vacations, it is difficult to call it beach. The fact is that the hotel is located on the third line from the sea. What are the charms and bonuses of this hotel? What do tourists write about it, because usually vacationers love hotels located right on the beach? Let's find out about this not only from the description of the hotel, but also from the reviews of the travelers themselves.



How to get there

To get to the hotel Innvista Hotels Belek 5 *, you need to fly to Antalya Airport. And then it all depends on whether you are an independent traveler or have acquired a package tour. In the second case, a transfer awaits you. But depending on how many hotels will deliver guests, your trip time will be more or less. But the minimum to get to this hotel will take about an hour, although Antalya Airport is only 25 kilometers away, and to the city itself - 35. The famous Belek resort is no more than 5 km from here. And in order to get from Antalya Airport on your own, you need to catch a bus that will take you to the bus station. From there, you will transfer to a minibus or other type of public transport that goes towards Alanya or Belek. Usually drivers stop near large country hotels.

Kadriye

Tourists who travel to this hotel often initially think that they are going to Belek. Innvista Hotels Belek 5 *, however, is located in the village of Kadriye. And this is five kilometers from this famous resort. However, Kadriye is considered part of Belek, and the landscapes there are very similar - eucalyptus groves, white sand and the clear Mediterranean Sea. The coast here is mainly built up with good hotels, although there is a public beach. Kadriye is famous for its beautiful views and clean air. The infrastructure here is very good and in no way inferior to more hyped resorts. There are no undercurrents or strong waves, so families with children really appreciate the rest in the area. Kids can be safely left to play on the beach. There is also a beautiful shady park in Kadriye. It is well suited for picnics and hanging out with children. There are many sports and playgrounds. Summer in Kadriye is hot, dry and cloudless. This is probably why tours to Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * are most popular in June-August. And in the village there are many eucalyptus and pine forests, where paths for hiking are laid. But in order to enjoy them, you need to come in the spring or autumn.



What is nearby?

The hotel complex Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * was built two kilometers from the village. It borders on a beautiful pine forest, whose aromas make the air in the hotel extremely clean and healthy. True, it is located almost a kilometer from the sea coast. This hotel is surrounded by other five-star palaces, such as The Land of Legend, as well as hotels belonging to the Kaya line - Belek and Palazzo. Opposite the entrance is a bus stop. If you go a little further along the sea, you can find cozy authentic restaurants, both youth and with a more romantic atmosphere.

Territory

Hotel Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * covers an area of ​​more than 22 square kilometers. There are a number of beautiful buildings built in a modern style: the administrative building and three residential five-story houses with elevators. On the territory there are various kinds of shops, a spa center, many pools, a beautiful park. The area of ​​the hotel is simply huge, with different pools, paths, stairs. However, the territory is carefully cleaned, it is very clean. Everything is compact and comfortable for guests. In addition, the hotel looks simply gorgeous and many guests appreciated it. The concept of the hotel is that guests do not need anything and can find everything they need either in their hotel or in neighboring ones.



Numbers and their contents

The Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * hotel is quite large. He offers tourists to stay in one of the 315 rooms. Most of them (193) belong to the “standard” category with an area of ​​24 square meters, which can accommodate a maximum of three tourists. 83 rooms - “Family”, measuring 35 square meters, up to 4 guests. 38 guest rooms are two-room suites with an area of ​​almost 50 meters. They are also designed for four people. 4 rooms are designed for people with disabilities and limited mobility. From the windows you can see the pool and beautiful gardens and park. Bathrobes and slippers are provided for adults and children. The minibar is filled once a day with drinks - juices, beer, Fanta, Coca-Cola, Sprite, as well as mineral water. You can use it for free. Children especially like this.



Dining at the hotel

Tourists at the Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * (Turkey) are fed in the main Innova restaurant. It serves Ultra All Inclusive. In addition, the hotel has five themed à la carte restaurants. Their visit is also free, but for this you need to pre-register at the reception. These are restaurants of Mediterranean cuisine, Far Eastern, National Anatolian, fish New Waves, as well as Kamelia Garden Garden, where you can try a traditional Turkish breakfast. Each of these establishments can be visited only once for free. Eight bars are open in the Innvista Hotel Belek 5 * hotel (Turkey, Kadriye) - in the lobby (open 24 hours a day), on the beach, by the pool, on the terrace, in the disco ("Amethyst"), and also in the territory - "Aqua "," Amphi "and" Nova. " For a typical breakfast, the hotel serves freshly squeezed juices - orange or pomegranate, depending on the season.

A special buffet is open for children. A huge variety of desserts, fruits, cheeses and sausages. Good milkshakes. A lot of meat (chicken, beef, duck, lamb, turkey, kufte cutlets and kebab), fish (red and sea bass), seafood (shrimp, mussels). From 11 am to six in the evening they give ice cream, so there are practically no queues for it. On the street they bake delicious gozleme cakes and grill meat and fish.



Service

The Innvista Hotels Belek (ex Verde Resort 5 *) has all the conditions for playing sports. You can play tennis, volleyball, basketball, take archery lessons. Animators work day and night; after dinner, guests are invited to various shows and performances. All this is played out in a special amphitheater with a stage. There are three mini-clubs for children: for kids up to 6 years old, for older guests (juniors) up to 13, and for teenagers up to 17. The rooms are covered, divided into different game zones by age. There is a corner for daytime sleep and outdoor areas. All this is included in the price. You will have to pay extra for room service, in particular, for ordering food in the room, as well as for alcoholic drinks that are not included in the “ultra all inclusive” concept. For money, various types of water activities on the beach are also offered. The hotel can use the services of a beauty salon - massage, peeling, hairdresser, beauty treatments. You can call a doctor or babysitter. There is a games room.

If you are organizing a conference or a seminar at this hotel, then you can rent special equipment. Dirty clothes can be taken to the laundry or dry cleaning service. For babies strollers are offered on bail. The newlyweds are provided with a fruit basket and cake, while breakfast is served in the room. The "Land of Legends" hotel hosts concerts free of charge for the Innvista guests, you only need to make an appointment in advance.



Staff

According to tourists' reviews, the staff of the Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * hotel (Turkey) are attentive, welcoming and responsive, especially in relation to families with children. Receptionists speak Russian, help solve many problems, if any. Since the hotel is mainly family-run, and there are a lot of children here, all the attention of the staff is focused on small guests. Cleaning, change of towels, service in bars and restaurants is excellent and practically does not cause complaints. Tourists express special thanks to the animators. They are funny, kind, helpful. Thanks to them, the hotel has a lot of different circles and sections, where not only children but also adults go. Every evening, something new was arranged, the program was never repeated - this is evidenced by tourists who were at the hotel for two weeks. Although no one asks for tips, or even hints, the guests assure that they themselves wanted to leave money, so people are diligent.



Sea holiday

Since the hotel is located almost a kilometer from the sea, a transfer takes tourists there. The bus delivers vacationers to the sea all day. It is cool, air-conditioned, goes to the sea for only five to seven minutes. The beach complex is private, indoor, it has a restaurant and bar, and entertainers entertain guests. Yoga, bocha, volleyball - one fun replaces another. Towels for sunbathing can be taken right on the spot. Sun loungers, parasols and other accessories are free of charge. Sunset at sea is good, including for children. Because a lot of kids are swimming near the shore. Well, so that the guests of the Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * hotel (Turkey, Belek) do not suffer from the fact that their hotel is far from the beach, they have as many as 9 pools on the site. This includes several children's paddles, an artificial sea with waves, a special department for water activities, a water park with slides, an indoor pond, and so on. Near each pool there are sun loungers, umbrellas, sun loungers, which all hotel guests can freely use.

Excursions

Hotel guests most often go to the neighboring hotel "Land of Legends". There you can visit a very large water park. In addition, an excellent fountain show is organized in the evenings. Travelers claim that such ideas are worth going there - you feel just like in a fairy tale. Moreover, all this is only ten minutes away. Well, if you want to explore the surroundings, then tourists are advised to buy organized trips not at the Innvista Hotels Belek Kadriye 5 *, as it is very expensive there. It is easier and cheaper to do this at the tour desk, which is a two-minute walk from the hotel. Want to see something near Kadriye? First of all, it is a national park, where nature lovers often come. There you can see many rare animals, birds and plants. Also nearby is Kursunlu Falls, which is not only beautiful in itself, but also hides a very interesting cave behind its jets.

Shopping

Every 20 minutes from the stop opposite the Innvista Hotels Belek 5 * hotel there is a bus to the bazaar. True, the real eastern market is there on Tuesdays and Fridays, but this area itself is a commercial one. There are all kinds of shops. They trade from morning until late in the evening, and you can agree on big discounts - half the price or even two-thirds. When the bazaar is open, you can buy almost anything there - fruits, Turkish sweets and jewelry, as well as good-quality items, including sheepskin coats and leather jackets, and if you're lucky, exclusive Indian fabrics. The bazaar is located in the center of Kadriye and the surrounding streets. This is a good enough place to shop and not go far from the hotel.

Innvista Hotels Belek 5 *: reviews

Most of the reviews about the hotel are enthusiastic. The rooms are nice, the linen is white, the cleaning is excellent. The food is excellent, and the food in à la carte restaurants is generally beyond praise. No one is bored. Especially fascinating is children's animation. Riding on the beach is absolutely not a burden, on the contrary, you do not need to go in the heat. The bus never had to wait long, the driver in a white shirt, always friendly. Very good reviews about the SPA center - after visiting it, the skin becomes velvety, and the tan lays on it evenly. Alcoholic drinks of very high quality; the ultra all inclusive system includes not only Turkish, but also imported ones. According to the rating of many sites where tourists leave their reviews, Innvista Hotels deserves an excellent rating.
