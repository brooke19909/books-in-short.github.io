Marmaris Sea Center 3 * (ex.Sun Maris Central Hotel) (Turkey, Marmaris): description, reviews

Marmaris is a city in Turkey, loved by thousands of tourists from all over the world. They come here in order to have a good rest in wonderful climatic conditions, soak up the sun in the hot sun and admire the exotic nature. This resort town has a considerable number of hotels that are ready at any time of the year to welcome travelers with different preferences within their walls. One of them is Sun Maris Central Hotel 3 *, which is located in the coastal part, near the public beach.



general information

The hotel was built in 1998 and since then on its territory the proper conditions for living have been constantly maintained. Various reconstructions are often carried out here. During one of them, which was completed in 2011, the climate system was updated and the apartments were renovated. Thanks to this, guests staying in his rooms can enjoy their beautiful and rather bright decoration.

Its area is very small - it is 1400 square meters. m. They are a modern building, which extends up to five floors. Tour operators recommend to go to this place to all those who wish to arrange a youth vacation - according to them, this is excellent here.

Location

Sun Maris Central Hotel is located in one of the most famous and largest resort areas of Turkey - Marmaris, in the center of this village. Thanks to this, travelers staying here can enjoy a huge variety of entertainment places located in the area.

In order to get to the nearest international airport, tourists need to overcome a rather impressive distance - 90 km (Dalaman). Because of this, payment for the transfer service is necessarily included in each ticket. Many travelers in their reviews write that it is provided quite qualitatively, a passenger with an air-conditioner picks up guests from the point of arrival and delivers them to the place of rest very quickly.

Rooms

In total, the Sun Maris Central Hotel has 55 rooms, which are presented in one category - standard. At the moment, thanks to the recent repair work, they look quite modern, visitors are attracted by a beautiful view of the sea and vibrant nature. Here, according to vacationers, it is always cool on hot days, which is ensured by the operation of the central air conditioner.

Each room has a private bathroom, which has not only plumbing, but also bath accessories designed for each guest. In addition, the hotel provides slippers, bathrobes and large bath towels.

Room furniture

All apartments have the same furniture set, which is needed for a comfortable stay for a couple of guests. There is a large double bed, which in some of them is replaced by two single beds. For a small number of personal belongings of guests, a pair of cabinets is provided here, and for large luggage, it is best to stay here to rent a cell in the storage room - this is done at the reception desk.

Guest rooms at Sun Maris Central Hotel have a large plasma TV. As tourists from Russia note, the positive point is that the screen broadcasts Russian-language satellite channels, thanks to the satellite broadcasting system. On a paid basis, you can use the phone with access to the international communication line.

The hotel does not have the possibility of renting a safe and a minibar - this does not please many guests.



Conditions for children

Despite the fact that Sun Maris 3 * (Marmaris sea / Center) is designed more for youth recreation, it is quite possible to stay with children on its territory. For them, there is a small number of conditions that make living for themselves and their parents as comfortable as possible. In particular, this concerns the possibility of installing a playpen type crib in any apartment, which is intended for guests aged 4 to 12 years. This is done completely free of charge, but by prior request, as the number of beds of this type is limited. Individual placement of children is also provided in the main catering facility of the Sun Maris Central Hotel - there are small highchairs here.

If desired, parents can send their child to practice water procedures in a separate section, which is available in the main pool.

If you need to leave your child for a while under supervision, you can use the paid babysitting services. They are charged hourly, the cost is about $ 10 per hour.

Nutrition

In order for the hotel guests not to remain hungry, several catering establishments are provided on its territory, among which there is one main - a restaurant. Travelers love to visit it, this is especially reflected in their reviews. They talk about food with unique taste, which is served as part of the buffet three times a day. All guests who live on an all-inclusive system can visit it. As for the type of cuisine, not only traditional Turkish dishes are served here, but also European ones.

If you want to taste refreshing drinks or exotic fruits, vacationers have the opportunity to go to any of the two bars, which are also on the territory of Sun Maris 3 * (Marmaris sea / Center). In addition, the bar menu offers several types of snacks and oriental sweets.



Services

If desired, any hotel guest can take advantage of a number of services that are offered to everyone on a common basis. One of them is the possibility of currency exchange on the ground floor of the main building. In addition, all guests staying in this place for relaxation can take advantage of the opportunity to access the Internet.

If necessary, everyone can request the services of a doctor, but they are paid.

A big minus, according to tourists, is that in Sun Maris Central Hotel 3 * there is no possibility of renting vehicles, as can be done in other hotels. Guests can drive to interesting places by car.



Aquazone

The aquazone, which is available on the territory of the Sun Maris Central Hotel, draws much attention of vacationers. The description of the hotel says that it is small in size and consists of an outdoor terrace with several sun loungers with umbrellas, as well as a bar that offers refreshments. In its very center there is a small niche with water, its total area is 35 square meters, there is also a section for bathing children. Water in the pool is updated every day, thanks to which it is kept clean. There is no water heating in the niche - this process is provided under the influence of sunlight.



Beach

Most tourists note that, staying at the Sun Maris Central Hotel (Marmaris), the best vacation can be arranged on the beach, which is located 50 meters from the building of its building. In order to get to its territory, tourists need to pass the road, for which the underpass is intended.

The entire coastline here is covered with medium-sized pebbles and fine, rather soft sand. It has several dozen sun loungers and umbrellas that can protect vacationers from the adverse effects of direct sunlight. Also for the convenience of guests there is a public toilet and a dressing room. Staying here, you can have fun on the water, riding on air cushions, as well as on a rented boat.



Features of accommodation

Guests of the "San Maris Central", while in the hotel, must follow the rules of accommodation, which are established for absolutely everyone. In particular, pets are strictly prohibited here. Also, in accordance with Turkish laws, it is forbidden to smoke in the apartments of the hotel - for this, separate places are allocated on its territory.

There are no rooms for people with disabilities in the room fund. If necessary, they can be placed on a common basis in standard apartments.



Prices

The cost of a ticket to this hotel in Marmaris (Turkey), according to many tourists, is somewhat overpriced. According to average statistics, it is about 60-65 thousand rubles. For comparison: in similar places for recreation in this country, the price fluctuates around 40,000 rubles.

However, many guests after a holiday note the excellent service at the Sun Maris Central Hotel, as well as excellent conditions for staying in rooms. According to the guests, they created a rather bright and comfortable interior in which you can relax and enjoy pleasant moments. The balconies available in the apartments offer great views of the countryside.

The cost of the voucher purchased at the agency, without fail, includes all transportation costs, including the provision of a transfer service. In addition, it provides three meals a day, provided as part of the all-inclusive concept.

Having visited this hotel, many recommend it to their friends for relaxation, and also share their experiences with their subscribers on social networks. Vivid photos are often attached to such reviews, which reflect the atmosphere prevailing in the district, attracting a huge number of travelers from different parts of the world.
