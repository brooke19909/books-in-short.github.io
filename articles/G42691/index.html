Hotel Napa Prince Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): photos and reviews of tourists

Ayia Napa is one of the most sought after resorts on the island of Cyprus. The main advantage of this Greek village, attracting thousands of visitors from around the world every year, are its wide sandy beaches. Since the popularity of Ayia Napa among vacationers is really very high, the tourist infrastructure here is developed just fine. Find inexpensive and comfortable housing in this village will not be absolutely no trouble.

Hotel Napa Prince Hotel Apts 3 *: Location Features

This small Greek hotel offers its guests not only comfortable rooms, but also pretty good service. Napa Prince Hotel Apts is located about 700 meters from the coastline and within walking distance of Ayia Napa's restaurants and entertainment venues. Heraklion International Airport is located just 6 km from the hotel. Tourists who come to the island on a package tour are usually provided by the operator company that sold it. Those who have booked rooms at the hotel themselves can easily reach the hotel by taxi.





The train station is just a short walk away. Ayia Napa Harbor (3 km) is also very close to the complex.

What is a hotel

The Napa Prince Hotel Apts 3 * hotel was built, reviews about which on the Web are mostly only excellent, was in 1995. This, of course, by hotel standards, a long time ago. However, in 2009 the hotel underwent a large-scale reconstruction. Therefore, its interiors and exterior look quite attractive and very neat. The hotel is owned by a wealthy Cypriot family. Therefore, the hotel is a true example of classical Greek hospitality.

According to most guests, the best hotel is Napa Prince Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus) is suitable for an economical, relaxing family vacation. Registration of new arrivals at this hotel starts at 14:00. Guests must leave at the end of the payment term before 11:00. In summer, the hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. In winter, hotel staff can be found at the reception only from half past eight in the morning until half past five in the evening. Tourists arriving sooner or later in order to call the administrator must call the emergency number. It is located directly at the reception. Children in this hotel can stay from any age.

Rules for placing children

Many tourists come to this hotel as a family. Of course, for such guests the hotel provides a number of additional amenities. Children under two years old can stay at the hotel with their parents for free. For a child under 6 years old, subject to being placed in a separate bed, guests are charged 7.5 euros per day. For the accommodation of children from 6 years old and adults with extra bed, you need to pay 15 euros per day. The hotel's restaurant has special chairs for young guests.

Napa Prince Hotel Apts 3 *: hotel food reviews

Rooms at this hotel can be rented with or without breakfast. According to most former hotel guests, the food here is very good. Cooks at this hotel are almost home-made. Breakfast is served from 9:30 to 12:30. In addition, the hotel has a Russian menu in the dining room. The choice of dishes in this hotel is not too large, but all of them, without exception, are fresh, heated and very tasty. Hotel guests especially praise her signature toast with cheese and bacon.

There is, of course, a bar in the hotel. It is located next to the pool. However, most tourists do not recommend buying anything here. The trade markup in the hotel bar is more than 100%. In cafes and shops located next to the hotel, drinks can be bought much cheaper.

In the winter time period (from November 1 to April 30) the hotel bar does not work. Breakfast is not provided for tourists in the cold season.

Beach Hotels

The sea from the Napa Prince Hotel Apts 3 * hotel is located quite far. In addition, to go from the coast to the hotel you need, unfortunately, uphill. Therefore, going to the beach, the guests of the complex should immediately take with them everything they need. In most cases, the residents of the hotel go to the beach for the whole day, since it would be too difficult to overcome the slope, at the top of which it is located.





Hotel guests sunbathe and bathe usually on the famous Nissi Beach. Most tourists attribute its advantages to beautiful views and a very gentle entrance to the sea. The main drawback of this beach is tourists consider a large crowd. The sea in this place is pretty clean. However, in it, unfortunately, just a huge amount of algae always floats. Moreover, no one ever removes them.

If desired, guests of the Napa Prince Hotel Apts 3 * hotel have the opportunity to visit other, no less beautiful and comfortable beaches in Ayia Napa. A 7-minute walk from Nissi Beach, for example, is Landa Beach. Its tourists consider the best in all Ayia Napa. Relaxing on the coastline here is no less convenient than on Nissa Beach. At the same time, there are much fewer people on Landa Beach, and besides, there are no algae in the sea.

Experienced tourists advise to sunbathe on the beaches of Ayia Napa carefully. The sun at this resort is very hot (much stronger than even in Sochi), and therefore burns can be obtained almost instantly.
