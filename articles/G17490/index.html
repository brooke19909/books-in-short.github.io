Where is it better to relax in Turkey with children? Tips & Tricks



Still wondering where it is better to relax with children in Turkey , so that the vacation goes as positive as possible and leaves only pleasant impressions?

Turkey has become almost dear to many of our compatriots. That is why every year more and more Russian tourists go to this "resort paradise".

Where is it better to relax in Turkey with children? The choice is huge and varied. Let's consider in more detail.

Turkey. Black Sea coast

This paradise of the planet is famous for its nature, extraordinary and unique beauty. The main "trick" of the Black Sea coast is that here you can see not only magnificent lakes and picturesque green landscapes, but even glaciers! The Turks themselves choose this place for relaxation.

The Black Sea coast is conditionally divided into eastern and western parts. The beach resorts that are most famous in these places: Amasara, Chakraz, Giresun, Gerze, Hamsilos, Abana, Sinop and Fastu.

If you want to spend time in a relaxed atmosphere exclusively with your family, then the beaches listed above are created especially for you, since here it is quite crowded and very cozy. By the way, beaches can be both sandy and pebble.





Climatic features of the Black Sea coast

The climate here is subtropical, Mediterranean and moderately warm. It is also worth noting that the humidity in different parts of the coast is different. In the east, it is higher, due to the presence of mountains it is much warmer here. The average daily temperature is + 22-24 0 C. The rainy season is observed from mid-October to the end of March. In May, the swimming season begins (water temperature is +17 0 and 24 0 , respectively). In late August, the water temperature also begins to gradually decline.

You are still tormented by the question: “Where is it better to relax with children in Turkey?” Then let's continue.

Turkey. Aegean coast

The main resorts: Bodrum, Fethiye, Dalaman, Didim and Marmaris.

Climatic features. Humidity is not as high as on the Black Sea coast. Despite the heat, there is always a light refreshing breeze. And the air ... It is truly healing and full of aromas of natural essential oils.

If you are going on a trip in the spring, you will catch the air temperature + 15-25 0 C. The water temperature in May is already +20 0 C. In summer it is quite hot here, about 40 0 C. However, such heat is not felt due to the light breeze and low humidity (35%). September-October - the air temperature during this period is +30 0 , and water + 25-28 0 .



Where is it better to relax in Turkey with children? Kusadasi is one of the resorts of the Aegean coast and a wonderful place to relax with the whole family. Kusadasi has a developed modern infrastructure and sandy beaches that will appeal not only to children, but also to their parents. Turkey hotels with children are found here at every step. By the way, it is worth noting that this country pays great attention to ensuring a comfortable family vacation. The resort town is located in a picturesque and convenient bay. At 7 kilometers from Kusadasi is the city ​​of Ephesus, or rather its ruins. From the resort to the second largest city ​​in Turkey - Izmir - can be reached in about 2 hours.

Young people, as a rule, choose Marmaris and Bodrum (the true party areas of Turkey) for recreation. Of course, when you go abroad, you first of all think about making the children comfortable, but you don’t need to forget about yourself. So, consider these places is also worth it.

Hotels in Turkey for children are equipped with everything necessary, and you do not have to type a whole bunch of "important" little things. You will find everything there.
