Hotel Dead Sea Spa Hotel (Jordan) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

One liter of water from the Dead Sea contains about 300 grams of salt and almost the entire periodic table. Every year, this reservoir is besieged by thousands of tourists who want to improve their health. Jordanian holiday resorts are no less popular than Egyptian or Turkish. And, of course, many hotels have been built here, offering guests of the country comfortable rooms. Tourists from Russia, for example, can book good rooms at the Dead Sea Spa Hotel.

Where is

This four-star hotel is located directly on the Dead Sea in the small village of Sowayma. There are no big cities nearby. Amman with its airport is approximately 55 km from the hotel. That is, to get to this complex on a tour operator's transfer bus, its guests have to spend about an hour and a half.





From the sea, Dead Sea Spa Hotel is approximately 200-300 meters away. That is, on foot to the shore, its residents can literally reach 15 minutes. A feature of this spa hotel is that it is located at the lowest point on Earth. The terrain in which it is built lies about 400 meters below the ocean.

Hotel Features

The advantage of the Dead Sea Spa Hotel, in comparison with many other hotels of the Dead Sea, is that it has its own access to the beach, to a small bay. This hotel accepts tourists in one multi-storey building and several bungalows. Of course, as in all other hotels located in the area, here you can relax and heal at the same time. After all, tourists go to the Dead Sea not only to sunbathe and swim. The hotel, of course, offers guests including mud therapy. Therefore, in fact, in his name the word Spa is present.

It is believed that this four-star hotel is best suited for family travelers with children and the elderly. There are no special entertainments for youth in this complex. This hotel is focused primarily on a relaxing holiday.

In addition to cash, cards are also accepted for various additional services at this hotel. They can be paid, including in shops located on the territory of the hotel. Too much currency at the airport experienced tourists are not advised to beginners who have rented rooms in this complex. At the hotel, this can be done at a better rate.





The residents of Grand East Hotel Resort Spa Dead Sea are mostly Germans. There are also British and French. There are usually not too many Russian tourists in this hotel. In any case, local residents do not behave rudely with tourists. They check in at the hotel on weekends, usually with children, and all the time they are in its territory they simply bathe in the pool.

Check-in time at this hotel is 15:00 h. Previously, tourists are usually not allowed in the rooms here.

What accommodation does the hotel offer?

If you wish, tourists who come to the Dead Sea for wellness can rent one of 277 spacious rooms at the hotel. SPA complex Dead Sea Spa Hotel 4 * (Jordan) offers guests of Sovaym standard class rooms and suites ranging from 40 to 80 m 2 . All apartments at the hotel have balconies. The suites, among other things, are visually divided into two zones - a living room and a bedroom. The view from the rooms of the rooms rented at the hotel can open to the sea or pool. In the first case, the apartment numbers end in even numbers.

The rooms of the standard class for guests include:

  • TV;

  • air conditioning;

  • telephone;

  • kettle;

  • mini bar.

Tourists can store valuables in this complex in a safe. In the shower rooms for the convenience of guests, in addition to the standard bathing set, a hairdryer is provided. This, of course, many tourists find very convenient. The climate on the coast of the Dead Sea, although it is not very large, is rather humid. Therefore, after taking a shower, tourists' hair here dries for a long time. If there is a hair dryer, for example, hotel guests do not have to go to bed with a wet head.
