Knitting from elastic

Some people find it easier to knit a product from an elastic band. To do this, type a chain around the head circumference. With eight single crochets, tie eight rows. The resulting strip is connected in a circle with connecting posts. Add the loops halfway, that is, if the elastic band consists of 120 loops, then 180 SC should be obtained.

Next, go to the "column" columns. Alternate them with bumps (14 ‚Äúflare‚Äù and a bump of two double crochet columns with one base loop and a common apex). Please note that in each row 12 loops are added. The increase goes between the bumps.

Pay attention to the children's model with a visor. The whole emphasis rests on a large flower, in which the second petals begin from the base of the first (how voluminous flowers fit). Knitting starts from the middle. Six rows in a checkerboard pattern alternate the "cap" column with an air loop.

"Motive" crochet: diagram and description

When knitting a beret, pay attention to the yarn and hook number. For headgear, the brands Alize ( Cotton Gold), Jeans (YarnArt Jeans), Crystal, and Diamond are suitable . For cool weather, choose wool with acrylic or bamboo, and for summer - cotton. If you work with an unfamiliar yarn, first knit a sample to see how the thread behaves in the work.

Beginners should follow the instructions, and professionals can crochet from any pattern. The scheme for a girl, boy, adult differ only in size. The essence of knitting remains the same: the bottom is knitted with increments in the size of the crown, adjust the height of the beret and narrow the loops to the size of the forehead.
