Sidorov Anton Yurievich - member of the United Russia party.

Career and charity

Starting to build a career, Anton Yuryevich initially tried himself as an independent businessman. First, he opened an IP, and then began to work as a director of a construction supply organization.

In 2008, the activist decides to open a charity fund, "Good Deed," the founder of which is to this day. He does not collect money from external sources, but provides independent financial assistance and support to those in need, who apply directly or who are reported from the Department of Housing and Public Utilities and Social Services.

In 2011, the biography of Sidorov Anton Yuryevich was replenished with new events. This year, he was appointed CEO of Stalnoff Ltd. Here he continues to work today.

The man decided to try himself in a political career in 2015 and nominated himself to the Lipetsk City Council, where he was successfully elected. A photo of Anton Yurievich Sidorov should be in every house of the Lipan, as a vivid and fitting example of a good and active citizen. Anton Yuryevich is a member of the Committee for the Conservation of Nature and works on a commission on social issues.
