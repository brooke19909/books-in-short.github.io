TT Hotels Pegasos World 4 * (Turkey, Side): description, prices, reviews

Holidays in Side, one of the most popular resorts in the Country of Stars and Crescent, is bright and multivariate. Indeed, in addition to the gentle waters of the Mediterranean Sea, which Homer poetically called viniculture, the land here is simply breathing history. In this romantic place, amidst a powerful resort infrastructure, two TT Hotels Pegasos hotels are built - five and four-star. In their joint construction, a commercial plan is seen - to provide vacationers of various costs with quality resort services of various costs.

Meet the five stars of TT Hotels Pegasos World

TT Hotels Pegasos World 5 * is located in the village of Titreengel, 6 km away from the now Turkish, and earlier - the ancient Greek City of Artemis, whose age is more than eight thousand years. We are talking about Side. Its name in local dialect denotes a garnet garnet - one of the symbols of the mentioned goddess. The ancients loved to give cities such intricate symbolic names.





The hotel complex was built on a unique project. The paths to it and its entire territory are landscaped with diligence and taste. Car parking, an impressive water park and swimming pools, a hairdresser, a spa complex with a sauna and massage, a summer theater, a sports complex are equipped. There is a night club, currency exchange, first-aid post. Here you can purchase various excursions and rent a car. The basic complex of hotel services is already included in the price of the tour.

Families usually come here to take full advantage of a variety of resort services. The contingent of vacationers: the British, Scandinavians, Russians, Ukrainians, Germans, Belarusians. The climate here is purely Antalyan: dry and hot summers (the air warms up to +45 degrees) is replaced by a rainy and relatively warm winter.

Tips for hotel guests

We conclude the story of the five-star with a little advice. Before traveling to Turkey, be sure to check out its real market prices for consumer goods. Resting at TT Hotels Pegasos World 5 *, you will surely come across a standard shopping excursion for it. Those who wish to shop are usually taken to the Tiger Fur fur coat shop. Our recommendation is simple and concise: bargain, because the price originally announced to you is doubled! The real price of a decent sheepskin coat or a good women's leather jacket is $ 500.
