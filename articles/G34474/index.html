Travel to Berdyansk. Recreation facilities: short description, services, prices

Many people prefer to spend their holidays on the shores of lakes, seas and oceans. A special place in the soul of most tourists is a visit to Ukrainian reservoirs. So, one of the most popular cities of this state is Berdyansk. Recreation facilities, which are simply not counted here, and the number of beautiful beaches pleases everyone. It is about them that will be discussed in this article. Let's get acquainted with the most popular and affordable for all people institutions.





"Golden shore"

On the Berdyansk spit there is a wonderful recreation center, which was called the “Golden Coast”. This place is famous for its healing mud, amazing flora and simply stunning beaches. There is everything in order not to feel alone: ​​a tennis court, a billiard room, a swimming pool, a gym, etc. In almost all establishments of this type, one can find similar conditions for spending leisure time. This is what distinguishes Berdyansk. Recreation centers are not without Internet. Wi-Fi is available on the Golden Coast. Where would a modern person be without him?

For lovers of parties, discos are constantly held here. There is also the opportunity to go on an excursion. And for those who prefer a more relaxing holiday, saunas and baths are suitable. If guests have health problems, they can take advantage of special services. We are talking about herbal medicine, inhalations, massages, therapeutic baths. In addition, other activities are being carried out to maintain health.





The food is comprehensive. The dining room is designed for 500 people. Food intake occurs 3 times a day. The most expensive dinner will come out - for it the guest will have to pay a little more than 250 rubles. For breakfast and dinner, the tenant will give only 190 rubles. However, you need to remember that the recreation center is located on the territory of Ukraine, so you will have to pay with local currency. Believe me, you will really like Berdyansk. Recreation facilities with a swimming pool are easy to find here: "Golden Coast" - an institution of this type. It is a leader among the like.

So how much does it cost to pay for a room? The cheapest option is a double “standard”. It will cost almost 2 thousand rubles. per day.
