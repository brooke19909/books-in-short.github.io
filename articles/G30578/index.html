Oberhof city in Germany

Biathlon lovers do not need to explain anything about the city of Oberhof in Germany. It is a world center for winter sports. Here is the famous biathlon stadium, where, since 1984, the stages of the World Cup have been held. Bobsleigh, ski jumping, cross-country skiing - these winter disciplines are also associated with Oberhof.

Where is the city located

But the city is not only the center of biathlon - it is a balneological resort. Oberhof in Germany is located in the very center of the country, the forests of Thuringia, at an altitude of 900 meters above sea level. Low mountain ranges, covered with forests, with hiking trails laid along them, are a resting place for residents of Germany. Fresh air, beautiful landscapes attract thousands of people here. Not only fans come here, but also those who wish to improve their health. In Oberhof there are hydropathic centers, swimming pools, climate pavilions and rest houses. How to get to Oberhof (Germany)

It‚Äôs not difficult for the Germans to get to Oberhof, since it is connected by roads to all German cities. You can come here by bus or by rail. But there is no airport. To get to the city from Russia, you must first come (fly) to one of the cities of Germany, the city of Erfurt is closest to Oberhof. The distance to it is 40 km. Features of a trip by rail

Given that Germany is a small country, all trains here are without berths. They are divided into three classes:

  • ICE - high-speed trains. Usually they ply between large cities, with a considerable distance.
  • RE - regional trains that do not go to remote cities, something like our trains.
  • STB - local small trains, often consisting of one or two wagons. They are more like small trams.

Tickets can be bought online or at ticket offices at train stations. There is a significant price difference. Given that train tickets are very expensive, it is best to purchase them online in advance, in this case there are big discounts. Getting from the remote cities of Germany to Oberhof, you will have to make transfers and ride on trains of all classes.

Where to stay

If you decide to visit Oberhof, then you need to remember that this is a small city, and there are a lot of tourists, especially during the competition, so you should not rely on luck, but you need to take care of the trip in advance and book accommodation. The Germans book accommodation for the year. Prices in hotels, of course, are not cheap. If it was not possible to find accommodation, then you should look for a place to sleep in the neighboring cities of Thuringia, getting to Oberhof by train.

Trips can fly a pretty penny. Therefore, it is necessary to consider as an option the purchase of a regional Thuringian ticket, which is valid throughout the day. The cost of a trip for one person is 20 euros, the cost of a ticket for a company of 5 people will be 28 euros. It is beneficial when the settlement where you are staying is far removed. If you live near Oberhof, it will be more profitable to buy a one-time ticket.

To use a Thuringian ticket, you need to look at the Internet for train schedules and transfer stations. You can use the machine at the station. Enter the starting and ending points, and it will give an accurate description of the route with all the transfers.
