CTP

Since 2003, the insurance policy has become mandatory in our country for all motorists, without exception. This is compulsory motor third party liability insurance for harm to life, health or property of third parties when operating a car.

Every motorist is obliged to have this policy for the past decade and a half, for example, without it, he will not be able to perform any registration actions with a car or other vehicle. Currently, the possibility of registration CTP significantly simplified. This can be done not only when visiting the office, but even in electronic form. To do this, you only need to calculate the cost of the policy and place it through your personal account on the website of the insurance company.

In the reviews on compulsory motor third-party liability insurance in Ingosstrakh, customers note that it has become much easier and more convenient to cooperate with the company. The policy guarantees payments in case of harm to the life and health of pedestrians, passengers and other road users, as well as indemnifies the damage caused by the guilty party in a traffic accident.

CASCO

If you want to protect yourself not only from payments to third parties, if you find yourself guilty of an accident, but also to cover your own expenses in the event of an accident, then you will need to apply for a CASCO. This is protection against any damage to your car, as well as theft and theft. The programs even include such cases as risks in case of damage to a vehicle in a parking lot, when hitting an obstacle, bouncing off an object, as a result of illegal actions of third parties, as well as due to natural disasters.

The CASCO insurance program allows the client to protect themselves from a wide range of risks that will be most relevant in your particular case.

If your car is stolen, a premium will be paid, which is enough to buy a new car. In case of damage incurred in a traffic accident when hitting an obstacle due to glass broken in the parking lot, the amount of payment should cover the repair or restoration of your "iron horse".

Also, the CASCO premium is paid in the event of a complete loss of the car as a result of an accident or other damage.
