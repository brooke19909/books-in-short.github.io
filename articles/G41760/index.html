Location

The Royal Hotel (Anapa, Vityazevo) was erected a 5-minute walk from the Central Beach and a 15-minute walk from the paid beach Golden Cove . Its construction was completed in 2013. The building offers scenic views of the sea, the water park and the city stadium.

The hotel provides a relatively inexpensive shuttle service to the hotel complex. The distance from Anapa Train Station to the hotel is 8 km, and from the airport 18 km.

The hotel's location is convenient for exploring local city life. The city center is only 300 meters away from the hotel.
