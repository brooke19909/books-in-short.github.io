Hotel Koh Chang Cliff Beach Resort: photos, description and traveler reviews

Koh Chang Cliff Beach Resort is a popular destination for travelers. After all, thousands of tourists coming to this country wish to relax on the seashore, enjoying modern comfort. Moreover, even buying tickets to Vietnam, many people try to spend at least a few days off in Koh Chang, one of the most beautiful Thai resorts. So what awaits the tourist at this hotel?

Description of the hotel and its location





As the name implies, the hotel complex is located on the island of Koh Chang, namely in its western part, very close to the seashore. By the way, many trips to Vietnam from large travel agencies include a visit to this island - a paradise in the middle of a limitless sea. Klong Prao Bay is a five-minute walk from the hotel.

The hotel was built in 2001, but not so long ago there was a thorough renovation. The hotel covers a large area, 20 acres of which is reserved for the evergreen, tropical garden. The complex consists of the main building, several two-story buildings, as well as small secluded bungalows and villas surrounded by lush thickets of exotic plants. By the way, in some sources the hotel appears as Koh Chang Cliff Beach Resort 3 *, although it has already been upgraded. This is a great place for a relaxing holiday.

Hotel Koh Chang Cliff Beach Resort 4 *: which rooms to count on?





The hotel is considered to be small, as it consists of 33 rooms. These are standard rooms and superior rooms. For families and large companies, more spacious rooms in villas and bungalows are suitable. By the way, almost every room comes with a balcony or its own secluded terrace, from which you can admire the garden or the sea landscape.

All rooms feature colorful Thai design and modern facilities. You can watch Russian channels on TV, use the phone and access to the Internet. There is a safe and a convenient air conditioning system. You can count on modern plumbing in the bathroom, as well as a constant supply of water, clean towels every day and a hair dryer.

Conditions for relaxation by the sea





As already mentioned, the hotel was built almost on the seashore. Kai Bay Beach is just 200 meters away. The coast is covered with sand and small pebbles. According to reviews, the entrance to the sea is convenient, cleared of stones and algae. The waters are calm almost all year round, although sometimes the wind rises.

Guests are offered sun loungers and parasols, and beach towels can be requested at the hotel. Naturally, there is a sports center where you can rent some types of water transport (boats, kayaks, catamarans), do more active activities (parasailing, water skiing) or even book an excursion along the coast.

Leisure, entertainment and extras

Koh Chang Cliff Beach Resort offers some additional facilities. For starters, perhaps it's worth talking about nutrition. When paying for a ticket, you can choose either breakfast only or breakfast and dinner. In the mornings, a buffet is opened for guests. Reviews indicate that the menu is standard, but the food is quite decent. But the dinners here are more varied.





Throughout the territory there is access to the Internet. Tourists can also use the laundry service, exchange currency on site, and also buy some products and small items in local stores.

Also, travelers like to relax by the outdoor pool, around which sun loungers and umbrellas are placed for greater convenience. There is also an outdoor jacuzzi. You can visit the gym, have a good time playing a game of billiards, use the services of a tour desk, as well as try out all the delights of a real Thai massage.

Reviews about Koh Chang Cliff Beach Resort

Travelers who are fortunate enough to visit the island leave good reviews about the hotel. The main advantage, of course, is the proximity and privacy of the beach - you can relax away from the noise. The territory of the hotel is incredibly beautiful - everywhere there is greenery, bright flowers, breathtaking landscapes. The rooms are clean and comfortable. Separately, tourists praise the local dinners, which are truly luxurious - the dishes are original and incredibly tasty, the portions are large, and the atmosphere is calm. Employees practically do not understand Russian, but they are fluent in English. By the way, all employees are very polite, ready to help with any problem - conflicts rarely arise here. Travelers say that the Koh Chang Cliff Beach Resort is perfect for people looking for a relaxing, relaxing holiday in the midst of nature.
