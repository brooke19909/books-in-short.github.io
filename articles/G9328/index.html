We create summer blouses with our own hands

Human hands can do almost anything. Therefore, nowadays, things and handmade gifts are very much appreciated. In addition, in the process of work, you can show your imagination, sew any clothes and just save money. Moreover, a thing can be done "for oneself", namely: to choose a color, material, decoration and other elements independently. Last summer, self-made summer blouses were very popular. On the streets, literally every third girl fascinated everyone with her skill and art. But this is really convenient. No need to run around the shops, with tears in your eyes, refuse the unsuitable thing and just spend so much time choosing clothes.



Simplicity available to everyone

Every girl can make summer blouses with her own hands, we assure you. Even if a person has never sewed anything in his life, this is not a problem. So, let's begin. For work, we need viscose knitwear (any color you like) and sewing threads of the same shade. At the beginning of the work, you should determine the type of blouse and measure the required length of fabric. In addition, you need to find a suitable pattern or make it yourself (if this item leads you to a dead end, and none of the options are suitable, you can use the service of a professional). Depending on your wishes, you can add buttons, a belt, insert small zippers, decorate with rhinestones and more. Summer blouses with their own hands are sewn strictly according to the pattern. Throughout the work, it is necessary to iron stitched seams.



We study the pros and cons of handmade

The main advantage of sewing clothes on your own is the individuality and exclusivity of the thing. You can choose absolutely any model you like. If you are a beginner, it is very difficult for you to sew summer blouses with your own hands, then you need to carefully approach the choice of fabric and pattern. Materials, as a rule, choose knitted or are guided by a simple style. However, the variant of elastic dress fabric is not ruled out (it provides a good fit and fit of the product). The ideal pattern can be taken from any sewing magazine. It is very easy to transfer the pattern to the fabric yourself, and then it remains only to sew all the details. It is recommended not to start very complex models right away, but to start with something easy.

Let's consider everything in practice

Sewing a blouse with your own hands begins with the sewing of shoulder seams and tucks. Then it is necessary to process the sides, neck, collar, sleeves and pockets. The final step should be the processing of the armholes, bottom and side seams. It should be noted that the appearance of the product will largely depend on the processing, so everything must be done very carefully.
