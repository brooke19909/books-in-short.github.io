Apart-hotel "Velvet Seasons. Alexander Garden" - reviews, features and services

A huge number of Russian tourists set themselves the goal in the summertime to relax in the " Velvet Seasons " in Sochi. This is due to the fact that this quarter is not only modern and on its territory everything is arranged in the best way for a proper rest. Tourists are attracted by the local climate, which is famous for its high, but very mild air temperature, as well as a huge number of sunny days a year.





"Velvet Seasons"

This is a large resort area, which residents and tourists call the hotel city. And indeed, besides the fact that its territory covers a huge area (420,000 sq. M. ), It has all the necessary infrastructure , from small cafes and restaurants to luxurious night clubs, free-standing parks and interesting places for entertainment.

The territory of the " Velvet Seasons " is divided into several quarters, each of which has its own characteristics. One of them is called the Alexander Garden. It will be discussed further.



general information

This quarter is located near the picturesque promenade, within walking distance from the sea shore, where tourists can relax. It consists of 20 modern and very beautiful buildings that were built in 2013.

At the moment , the Alexander Garden quarter is able to accommodate more than 3,000 people in its rooms. On its territory there is a well-developed tourist infrastructure, which allows vacationers to feel in a state of complete comfort.

Rooms

In 20 buildings of the complex, there are a total of 1641 rooms of the categories " standard " and " luxury " . Almost every of them provides for the settlement of two guests. They strictly prohibit smoking and pets. There are also 94 apartments for disabled people - they differ from the classic " standard " in that they have custom-made furniture, taking into account all the characteristics of people who have limited physical abilities.



All rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, a toilet, and a sink. It also has all the necessary bath accessories and a hairdryer. Towels, bathrobes and slippers are provided.

Each room at the Alexander Garden Hotel is equipped with an individual air conditioning, with which you can adjust the temperature conditions to optimal, regardless of the weather outside.

"Standard"

In reviews of the " Alexander Garden " in " Velvet Seasons ", tourists talk about the fact that it is very comfortable to be in his rooms. Everything is furnished exclusively with modern and high-quality furniture, which is pleasant to use. You can enjoy beautiful mountain views from the windows of your apartment. Some rooms have their own private balcony or loggia, which can also be conveniently located for relaxing.

With regard to furniture, each room has comfortable beds in the form of double beds with orthopedic mattresses. If desired, guests can rent apartments with two single beds. In addition, the furniture has several cabinets, including for storing personal belongings, as well as lighting fixtures, a telephone and a TV with the ability to view satellite channels. Guests have the opportunity to use the minibar for free, the range of which is replenished daily with soft drinks.

The guests are also presented rooms of the category " standard " , consisting of two rooms. They have a living area divided into a living room and a bedroom.



"Suite"

Tourists who stayed in luxury apartments, in reviews of the " Alexander Garden " in the " Velvet Seasons " Group of Companies , say that they have increased comfort conditions. Here, an area of ​​45 square meters. m is divided into two spacious rooms: a bedroom and a living room, each of which is equipped with good modern furniture. Guests note that slippers and bathrobes are not given out to residents, which is a big minus. Negative moments, in their opinion, also include the emptiness of the minibar.

The sleeping area in such apartments is arranged in the same way as in the " standard " . In the living room, everything is different: there is a large soft sofa, two armchairs and a coffee table. A large plasma TV is installed on the wall. According to tourists, when choosing between the rooms of the comfort level " standard " and " luxury " , it is better to give preference to the first option.

Conditions for children

Often parents and their young children relax in the complex. In their reviews of the 3 * Alexandrovsky Garden quarter in Velvet Seasons , they note that this hotel is not very suitable for families with children . According to them, the conditions provided by the hotel for children are simply not enough.

Of all the possibilities and amenities, only highchairs in the restaurant are presented here, as well as a special bed, which, by prior arrangement , can be provided free of charge. In the open area there is a small playground.



Nutrition

Guests of the " Alexander Garden " have the opportunity to eat in a local restaurant, whose name is " Admiralteysky " . A daily buffet is served here, serving tasty and nutritious dishes of European and Russian cuisines. Vacationers who live on an all-inclusive basis can visit this establishment during breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Also , if desired , guests have the opportunity to eat in neighboring cafes, bars and restaurants that are located on the territory of the " Velvet Seasons " . In reviews of the Aleksandrovsky Sad Hotel (Sochi), many tourists share the idea that in the evening you can stroll around the parks with your loved one or family in the local parks and dine in small cafes.

Sport

There are several places in the quarter where you can go in for sports and maintain your health in shape. There is a large and good gym, where a considerable number of simulators designed for different muscle groups are installed. Among the female half of guests, fitness rooms and aerobics classes are very popular. Fans of team games can go to specially equipped playgrounds for volleyball, football and basketball. There is also a well-equipped table tennis room.

In reviews of the " Alexander Garden " ( " Velvet Seasons " , Adler), it is often said that all classes in the above places are paid, which is a big minus.



Services

Tourists who relax in the buildings of this quarter can use a number of services that are provided here. On a free basis, vacationers can get advice from administrators at the front desk , use the services of an ATM or call a taxi from the complex. Throughout the " Alexander Garden " there is free access to the Internet (Wi-Fi) - this allows vacationers to always stay in touch with their family and friends.

For a separately set fee, guests can use the services of a doctor who is on duty around the clock in the building. There is also the opportunity to hand over things to the laundry or dry cleaning - this is also done for money. If desired, you can visit the beauty salon, where they can do a new hairstyle , makeup and various cosmetic procedures. In reviews of the " Alexander Garden " ( " Velvet Seasons " ), one can often find comments on the good work of beauty salon masters. In them, many tourists are indignant at the lack of a spa and pool in the quarter.



Beach

The clean and wide coastline, covered with large pebbles, is located literally 50 meters from the buildings of the Alexander Garden. The beach area is considered common to all areas of the " Velvet Seasons " , its total length is 5 km and a width of 50 meters.

In their reviews of the " Alexander Garden " ( " Velvet Seasons " , Sochi), tourists share their impressions from staying in the territory of this recreation area. They say that the beach is always clean, it offers stunning views of the surrounding surroundings. Everything is arranged here for a comfortable stay: there are public toilets, changing rooms and cheap cabins. However, tourists note that the use of sun loungers and umbrellas will also have to pay a rental fee.

Along the coastline you can find a considerable amount of entertainment. In particular, fans of extreme types of relaxation can ride on airbags, water skiing or parachuting behind. Here you can also rent a yacht, a small boat or a catamaran and go on a small cruise. In their reviews of the " Alexander Garden " 3 * ( " Velvet Seasons " ), tourists often recommend taking this opportunity to couples in love - the atmosphere in the open sea will be very romantic.



Prices

As for the cost of living in this complex, it directly depends on what services will be included in the travel package. So, according to reviews of the Aleksandrovsky Sad quarter in Velvet Seasons , the prices for renting rooms are quite reasonable, but since you have to pay for all the additional conveniences and opportunities, they strongly recommend that unknowing guests use the services of travel agencies and purchase from them permits . As a rule, a package of such a package includes accommodation for two in a standard room, three meals a day, the ability to use the gym, transfers from the nearest transport hub and use of a parking space. The average cost is about 1,680 rubles per day - this price is considered by tourists to be quite acceptable for the level of conditions provided.
