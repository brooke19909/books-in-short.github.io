Hotel Alassio Hotel & Thalasso 4 *, Tunisia - overview, description and reviews of tourists

The popular Tunisian resort of Monastir was once a Roman settlement and was called Ruspina. Today, this picturesque southern town offers tourists, including Russian, inexpensive and at the same time quite high-quality rest by the sea. There are, of course, plenty of comfortable hotels here. For example, guests of Monastir can rent rooms in the hotel Alassio Hotel & Thalasso 4 *.

Where is?

This comfortable hotel is located not in the city itself, but not far from it - about 10 km - on the very shore of the sea. To get to the beach, guests of Alassio Hotel & Thalasso 4 * can literally in 2-3 minutes.





Quite a convenient location of this complex, vacationers believe in relation to the airport. Monastir's air transport hub is just 6 km from this hotel. That is, tourists can reach the hotel upon arrival in just 15 minutes. Most often, vacationers on special buses to the hotel are taken, of course, by the operators who sold them permits. But the road to this complex will not cause any difficulties for those tourists who rented rooms here on their own. A taxi ride to the hotel will not cost too much.

Holidaymakers consider that the thermal power plants are in close proximity to it. Also, next to the hotel there are, unfortunately, several salt marshes. Therefore, on its territory there are usually a lot of mosquitoes. Tourists who decide to live in the south at this hotel should definitely take some kind of insect repellent with them on the road.
