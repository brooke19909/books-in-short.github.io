Harry's Hotel Protaras 3 *: tourist reviews

The history of Harry's Hotel dates back to 1996. After the reconstruction of 2006, it became even more comfortable and attractive for tourists from all over the world.





Brief description of the hotel

If you are a supporter of a quiet and relaxing holiday alone with nature, then Harry's Hotel 3 * Resort is exactly what you need. But this does not mean that it will be boring here. You have the opportunity to play tennis, go diving, as well as visit the steam sauna and take advantage of a huge range of services. After an active day, be sure to eat in the restaurant, which serves the best of Mediterranean cuisine.

Harry's Hotel 3 * cares about the comfort of its guests, and therefore provides beautifully decorated rooms for living. There is a complete set of necessary furniture and household appliances. But the central place is occupied by the balcony, especially when it comes to the upper floors. It offers incredibly beautiful views of the sea and the surrounding nature.

Not far from the Harry's Hotel (Cyprus) is a public sandy beach. But if you do not want to leave the hotel, an outdoor pool is at your service. It is very well maintained and clean, and around it there are a large number of sunbeds. If the weather turns out to be cold, then the hotel staff will include water heating.





Where is the resort located

Resort Harry's Hotel 3 * (Protaras) is located in a quiet area on the outskirts of the city. This place is characterized by beautiful beaches and nature. Since Pratoras is a rather small town, literally in 5 minutes you can reach its center, where there are many shops, cafes and entertainment centers. You will also have the opportunity to visit the famous resort of Ayia Napa, which is only 10 kilometers away from this village. As for the Larnaca International Airport, you will have to overcome almost 90 km to it.

Guest apartments

All rooms at Harry's Hotel 3 * Resort (Cyprus, Protaras) are in the standard category. They represent a spacious room, which is divided into zones of the bedroom and living room. Accommodation can be made both on large and one and a half beds. The apartment also has two armchairs and a soft sofa, which serves as an extra bed. All rooms have access to a small private balcony. You can choose the option of accommodation with sea or city views, which in some way affect the cost of living.

Perhaps the only drawback is the Harry's Hotel room interior. Reviews, photos - all this indicates that the rooms are decorated very simply and nondescript. In addition, all furniture, plumbing and household appliances are quite old, and some require not only repair, but also a complete replacement.





The amenities provided in the apartment

Guest rooms at Harry's Hotel Resort include the following amenities:

  • depending on which category of room you are staying in, the combined bathroom will be equipped with a modern shower or a large bathtub with hydromassage;
  • there is a minibar, the filling of which is subject to an additional payment, but you can use it just like a refrigerator;
  • if you want your valuables and money to be protected, then just pay for the corresponding service at the reception and you will be able to use the electronic code safe;
  • individual air conditioning will always maintain a comfortable microclimate in your apartment;
  • it has a large flat-screen TV that broadcasts both satellite and cable channels (most of them entertainment and music);
  • thanks to the presence of a balcony (or terrace, if we are talking about the first floors), furnished with plastic chairs, you can always breathe in the fresh sea air, admiring the scenery.

Entertainment opportunities

If you enjoy outdoor activities and vibrant entertainment, then Harry's Hotel Resort will delight you with the following features:

  • You can use your soul and health to spend time in a steam sauna, a Russian bath or a traditional Turkish hammam;
  • lovers of billiards will certainly appreciate the cozy club with a large number of tables, as well as a beer bar and televisions broadcasting sports channels;
  • You can rent a tennis court, as well as rent all the necessary equipment for the game;
  • by renting a bicycle, you can quickly move around, sightseeing, while at the same time benefiting your health and figure;
  • You can take diving lessons, as well as dive with an instructor to study the deep sea.




Useful information for tourists

It will be useful for tourists to familiarize themselves with the following list of information regarding accommodation in this resort hotel:

  • check-in and check-in are done after two in the afternoon, but with an additional fee, this procedure can be expedited if the hotel has free rooms;
  • You need to leave your room and hand in the keys for reception on the day of departure no later than noon, otherwise you will be charged for every hour spent in an overtime room;
  • for babies up to two years old special cots are provided absolutely free of charge;
  • if an extra bed is not required to accommodate a child under the age of 12 years, then for his stay in the room there is no charge;
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring or bring any animals to the hotel grounds;
  • credit cards accepted;
  • A prerequisite for checking into a hotel is the provision of an identity card with a photograph.

General impression

In order to characterize this resort hotel, one word will be enough - budget. The low cost is due not only to the remote location, but also to the general dilapidation of the hotel. The room decor is quite old, and the equipment works with varying degrees of success. Needless to say, you should not even dream about quality food in the dining room. The sea is located, although not far, but the beach is very small and quite dirty. The only consolation here is the pool, which is kept clean.

We can say that this hotel will be a good economical option for tourists who do not like to sit still. If you just need an inexpensive place to sleep (also with a beautiful view from the window), then here you are. In the afternoon, head to the center of Protaras. Here you will find inexpensive restaurants with very tasty food, shops and a lot of entertainment. You can also find a more spacious and well-groomed beach. Be sure to go on an excursion to neighboring settlements. The ideal option is to rent a car to familiarize yourself with all the tourist attractions. Thus, settling in this hotel, you can save a lot of money to ensure interesting leisure.
