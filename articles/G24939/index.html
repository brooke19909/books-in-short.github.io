The stadium "Youth". Perm invites to the rink

In the very center of Perm, not far from Gorky Park, the Yunost Stadium is located, the ice rink of which has long remained popular among ice sports enthusiasts. Both adults and children, and skating enthusiasts, and professionals in this business appreciated it. That is why he has long acquired the status of a family.





Youth stadium, Perm. General information

This stadium is part of the large sports complex "Eaglet". Young and adult athletes in the stadium, depending on the season, engage in various sports: in the summer - athletics, basketball and football, and in winter the stadium is covered with ice, it turns into a huge ice rink for mass skating. In addition to all this, fans of field hockey and speed skating will find something to do at the stadium - they also have corresponding sections. On the territory very often sporting events of various formats are held: from small sporting events to large-scale city concerts.





Youth stadium, Perm. Rink

The skating rink at the Yunost stadium provides for the possibility of both single and mass skating. You can visit the rink either by subscription or simply by renting skates for one session, paying a fixed price. Both regular skates and professional skates are available for rent.

For regular visitors the administration of the skating rink offers the opportunity to purchase subscriptions for 4, 8 and 12 visits. Yunost Stadium (Perm) is also always happy to host guests with their entire families, for which there is a family subscription, whereby parents can go to the rink with their children. Everyone can participate in mass skating, which takes place every Wednesday and Sunday, as well as in competitions that are sometimes held by event sponsors.

Each session lasts 45 minutes, after which there is a break for 15 minutes, which is necessary in order to correct the ice before the next call. During each session, background music plays, which sets the rhythm of skating. As for the cost of the ticket, it varies depending on whether the visitor arrives on a weekend or weekday. Also, for a pensioner or a child under the age of 10, a ticket will be cheaper than for an adult.

Ice conditions and quality

The ice on the rink is good enough, smooth, on which cracks are rarely observed. Visitors are pleased with the large size of the rink - even with a massive influx of visitors, people do not interfere with each other and have the opportunity to ride safely, avoiding collisions. It is these conditions that attract more and more visitors to the Yunost stadium (Perm).

Rink. Mode of operation

Unlike earlier times, the rink now operates not only on weekends, but also on weekdays (except Mondays). So, from Tuesday to Friday you can visit it from 19-30 to 22-00, and on weekends - from 17-00 to 22-00 on Saturday and from 15-00 to 22-00 on Sunday.





Additional services

In addition to ice skating, people who come to the Yunost stadium (Perm) can use other services at the same time. Sharpening of skates is especially popular among them . To do this, you need to purchase an additional ticket for a fee. By the way, the cost of sharpening new and old skates will be different.

After active skating, people can go to the nearest cafe, which is located on the territory of the sports complex - where visitors are offered to taste sweets, pastries and hot drinks.

The stadium "Youth" (Perm) attracts a fairly large number of visitors, as Permians, it turns out, are very fond of winter fun, in particular a trip to the ice rink. By the way, visiting tourists is also recommended to visit it - pleasant impressions will remain in the memory of everyone who has visited there at least once.
