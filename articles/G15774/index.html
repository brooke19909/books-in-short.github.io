The recreation center "Korobitsino-Cascade", Leningrad region: review, description and reviews

If you want to spend a relaxing weekend outside the city, pay attention to the recreation center "Korobitsyno-Cascade". This place is suitable for both relaxing and active pastime.





Location

Apartment Korobitsyno-Kaskad is located in the Leningrad Region, 75 km from Saint Petersburg. This is a picturesque park area, remote 4 km from the Red Lake. You can get here by your own car or by public transport. If you don‚Äôt have a car, get to the Parnas metro station and transfer to shuttle bus No. 898. Starting your journey at 9:30, already at 15:00 you will reach the village of Korobitsyno.

Driving your own car will take you about an hour and a half. Having left on the Vyborg highway, you need to turn towards Pervomaisky. Having passed along the village, move towards the Foothills. Now turn towards Michurinsky, and after 10 km, turn right at the fork. After driving 5 km, you will find yourself in Korobitsyno, where you will see a sign to the recreation center.





Infrastructure

Korobitsyno-Cascade is not just a recreation center. This is a whole complex of entertainment and relaxation, located in the middle of a green park area. The infrastructure of this institution is as follows:

  • the main building, where rooms and administrative rooms are located;
  • a cafe with a pool table and a small children's corner;
  • luxurious restaurant "Cascade", where guests are served Russian and international dishes;
  • complex of cottages;
  • Russian sauna;
  • relaxation complex with sauna, pool and billiard room;
  • banquet room for special occasions;
  • fireplace room with upholstered leather furniture;
  • arbors of different sizes with barbecue and dining tables;
  • open buffet;
  • specially equipped picnic area;
  • sports stadium;
  • refreshing fountains, next to which there are benches, tables and gazebos.

Hotel Restaurant

Restaurant "Cascade-Korobitsyno" is an excellent suburban establishment that will quench the appetite of the most demanding guests. Its specificity is that cooks use natural home-made products and preparations. Especially visitors like traditional Russian pickles and berry jam.

The restaurant specializes in dishes of Russian and European cuisines. Guests can sit in a cozy room decorated with natural wood, as well as on the outdoor terrace, where a pleasant aroma of coniferous forest and dishes prepared on the grill is heard. The restaurant also has a spacious banquet room for 120 people.

The restaurant opens at 11:00 and serves guests until 23:00. The average bill at this restaurant (excluding alcohol) is 700 rubles. In addition to food and special occasions, watching sports matches is also organized here. Separately, a menu is provided for those who observe the post.

The Korobitsino-Cascade Country Hotel is a great place for those who like to spend a weekend in nature. Here guests will enjoy clean pine air and a beautiful lake.
