Entertainment in Novosibirsk: recreation parks, attractions, entertainment centers

Novosibirsk is a big millionaire city. It is so beautiful and rich that the night metropolis looks like a real Las Vegas. The whole city glows with thousands of colorful lights. It is not surprising that in such a beautiful metropolis there are many interesting places.

Entertainment

Of course, like any modern cultural city, Novosibirsk has various entertainment clubs, cinemas, restaurants, cultural centers, cafes, philharmonic societies, and recreation centers. But still, local residents prefer cultural rest in the fresh air.



Therefore, almost all of the entertainment in Novosibirsk is in the parks. There are an incredibly large number of them in the city. Parks are located in almost every district of Novosibirsk. They offer their guests a completely different entertainment, from the shooting gallery and ending with arcade games, you can always find the Ferris wheel there. There are even fortunetellers who can turn on the cards.

Holiday Homes

So where to relax in Novosibirsk? The city has 16 holiday homes. The most popular of them are the Barvikha hotel, the guest tourist house Pristan, the guest complex Na Kropotkina and many others. All these establishments offer customers round-the-clock relaxation in comfortable rooms, first-class cuisine in a chic restaurant, a private beach with a pool, a water park, a sauna, billiards, a gym and a karaoke bar. Excellent service and attentive staff will provide guests with an unforgettable vacation.

Clubs

There are entertainment in Novosibirsk and for lovers of nightlife. Where else to entertain the youth, no matter how in nightclubs. Moreover, there are so many of them in the city that you can pick up an option for every taste. These are the nightclubs "Dancing", "Anti-Glamor", "Pike", "Vatel", "Alpin Grotto", "Maestro", "Zephyr" and others. There every evening new theme parties, bowling, karaoke, cozy bars, dance floors, clockwork music, striptease and even beauty contests are waiting for guests. In addition, admission is often free for girls, but tight control is in effect - people under 21 are not allowed!

And where can I eat?

If residents or guests of the city want to have dinner with the whole family, invite their loved one for dinner, or meet with business partners, you can always go to a restaurant. Such establishments in Novosibirsk offer their visitors absolutely any cuisine. In the Restaurant "Europe" and "Liverpool" you can taste European food, in the bar "Bonfire" - Austrian dishes, in "Hokkaido" prepare dishes of cuisines of different nations. No matter which restaurant the choice falls into, they all monitor their reputation and have the highest customer ratings.

Recreation Park Zaeltsovsky

The most common entertainments in Novosibirsk are walks in the parks of culture and rest. These places are most suitable for family leisure, and especially for children.





A very interesting Zaeltsovsky culture park. You can have a great time here. Especially interesting in the Zaeltsovsky winter. Here you can bask in the ethnic settlement, and children can ride on the railway or play with squirrels.

Weak



For lovers of "tickle your nerves" there is a rope park "Weak?" The park has rental bicycles, skis, a trampoline, an autocinema, a football pitch, paintball, excursions, a summer cafe and a restaurant.



In summer you can always go to the central beach. It is located on the banks of the Ob reservoir. Everything on the beach is equipped for outdoor activities: gazebos, tables, barbecue facilities, as well as electricity. If you do not want to cook yourself, you can always visit a local cafe. Vacationers are supervised by the administrator and rescuers.

Yard

But the most interesting thing in the city is entertainment for small residents. Novosibirsk for children offers special cafes, entertainment and research centers, theaters, musical plays and much more.

Toddlers can be brought to the children's zoo "Yard". It is open all summer. Here it is allowed to play with animals, pet them and feed them. And here you can wander in the maze.

Amusement park

There is also an amusement park in Novosibirsk. Visitors of all ages come here with pleasure. An incredible entertainment program awaits guests here: a trampoline, a shooting gallery, a Ferris wheel, arcades, laser shows, big dolls, water rides, popcorn and cotton candy, cafes, and other entertainments!

"Madagascar"

The real island of entertainment for kids is the Madagascar game center. Here, children can play with toys, jump on a trampoline, eat in a children's cafe, and on weekends participate in a circus show program with real circus artists. And at the end of the day, kids will have a children's disco and a cartoon on the big screen. This is a great vacation not only for kids, but also for adults.
