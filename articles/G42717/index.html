Kiwi wallet: create in five minutes

All modern people have long had an idea how to use electronic money. There are a lot of payment systems , and one of them is QIWI. Surely you have often seen her logo when paying for a mobile phone through the terminal. However, if you have a Qiwi wallet, you can save money on payment. Just when you pay for your mobile phone through the terminal,

a decent commission is taken from you - from three to five percent, and sometimes more. In other words, you put one hundred rubles, and the account was replenished only by 95. Now calculate how much money you spend on mobile communications per month, multiply this amount by 12 (a year) and calculate from it 5%. Agree, the money is decent. And if you have a Qiwi wallet, which is not difficult to create, you can save these funds. If this prospect inspires you, then head to the nearest QIWI terminal. You will need no more than five minutes to register a Qiwi wallet.

In other words, the mobile number will be the number of your personal wallet. Agree, this is very convenient. Next, you will receive an SMS on your phone, where the password will be indicated. With it, you can enter the payment system and view your Qiwi wallet, which you still decided to create. You can use this secret code in any QIWI terminal from those located in the country.



After you deposit funds to your wallet, you will have the opportunity to manage them from your computer. To do this, you need to enter the QIWI website and register, indicating your mobile number. You will again receive an SMS message with a password. You will use it in cases of working with the wallet online. Thus, your Kiwi wallet, which is very easy to create, will have two passwords: one for terminals and one for the Internet. His number will be the same - your cell phone number. With the help of this payment system you can easily and economically pay any expenses, be it housing and communal services, cable television, mobile communications or the Internet. In addition, with its help you can make purchases in various online stores, deposit money into the balance of your accounts on various sites that are of interest to you. Also, if you have a Kiwi wallet, which can be created in just five minutes, you can register another one and transfer money there from the first.

In this system, the electronic wallet RUB (abbreviated MWRUB), which is the equivalent of the Russian ruble, is used for settlements. The Qiwi system is also good because you do not need to specify anything more than a phone number. In other payment systems, almost all data is required, even the passport number. To protect your savings in case you lose your phone, you can set a special PIN code to enter the Java application, except you, no one could.
