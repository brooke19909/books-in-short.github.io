PocketBook 613 Basic New: reviews, features, instructions

If you think about it, you can do without Internet access. Moreover, this greatly discharges the battery. The speed of the PocketBook 613 device is quite high, it supports all major formats, allows you to make bookmarks that are easy to manage, and the purpose of the buttons can be customized as you wish.

The device comes in a relatively compact box, made in white and light green. What comes with the PocketBook 613? Instruction manual, USB cable, warranty card and ... everything. But this is not surprising, given that the model is budgetary.

Using keyboard

To search for books and the right words in the text, the developers have provided an on-screen keyboard. By the way, it is well thought out, which is why it is convenient to use. If you adapt, the text will be typed faster than messages on the phone.



For ease of typing, all characters and letters are divided into five groups. During typing, the group is first selected, and after that the desired letter is searched for in it, which is assigned by pressing the central button on the joystick.

Work with formats

The PocketBook 613 device supports a huge number of formats. Their complete list is indicated in the instructions. They were present in previous models, with the exception of the new PRC format, which is designed to read books on mobile devices.

Pages are flipped using the buttons below the screen. If you hold them, the transition will be made immediately ten pages forward or backward. If there are footnotes or links in the work, a long press will also open a menu for navigating through them.



Holding the button in the center of the joystick brings up a menu through which you can select the desired bookmark, find a page, open settings or a dictionary, as well as change the orientation of the book.

The reader can boast of great features when working with the FB2 and EPUB formats. As for PDF, despite the small screen size, reading in this format is quite convenient. After all, there are several zoom modes. Also added is the column mode, which is a useful innovation. The fact is that there are files when reading which two pages are displayed on the screen at once. So, this mode is able to divide the text into two columns, so that you can read them in turn.

Offline work

The device has a capacious 1000 mAh battery. It charges about two hours. Whether it is long or not, everyone will decide for himself. The developers claim that the charge should be enough to turn 7500 pages. And this is quite plausible data.

In sleep mode, the device is able to live a very long time. No hidden processes consume energy, as was seen in many competitive models. Yes, and why there is a charge, because even the WI-FI sensor is missing.

Conclusion

Summing up, it can be noted that the manufacturer with each new model is trying to improve its device and correct past mistakes. Therefore, PocketBook 613 Basic New turned out to be somewhat better than its predecessor. It has high speed, high-quality, comfortable-to-read screen, interesting design and the ability to recognize a large number of formats. Of the minuses, we can distinguish the lack of WI-FI and mp3 support. But these are doubtful shortcomings, which are offset by a low price.
