Other beaches

Outside the city there are many beaches. The most popular are San Rafael and Praia do Evaristo. For connoisseurs of beauty, San Rafael, located between the crevices of the rocks, is ideal. Immerse yourself in the underwater world, see the marine life on the Praia do Evaristo, because this is a great place for diving.
