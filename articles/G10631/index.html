Sanatorium "Russian Forest", Vladimir Region: description, features and reviews

Sometimes the body needs a break from the cycle of work, personal problems, eternal haste and fuss. To do this, a person must periodically change the situation. A good place for this is the sanatorium "Russian Forest".

Description

This complex is located at a distance of 150 kilometers from the capital of the Russian Federation. The territory is ecologically clean. The forest is mixed. Nearby flows a wonderful river. Klyazma, on the banks of which it is extremely pleasant to spend time.



Sanatorium "Russian Forest" (Vladimir Region) is quite far from densely populated cities. Here you can get in touch with the wonderful miracle of nature, breathe in the fresh air that is generated by a wide variety of trees. Throughout the year, you can get high-quality recovery, undergo various rehabilitation programs, get healed of a wide variety of pathologies. Sanatorium "Russian Forest" is built on an excellent landscape base. Due to this, excellent conditions were created here for insolation or for receiving aerotherapy. You can get here during any season. Both childless couples and visitors who take their kids with them are accepted.

Features

Very beautiful sanatorium "Russian Forest". Vladimir region can be proud of it. It consists of cases that are built with maximum convenience and compactness. There are two buildings in which there are eight floors. There are rooms where only one room is located. There is also a two-room semi-suite type accommodation. In addition, visitors are offered wooden cottages. They can be located with maximum comfort.

A similar opportunity is provided to people who decide to settle in a new attic-type building, which refers to a complex of rehabilitation and treatment. The Russian Forest sanatorium acquired this part in the winter of 2007. A year later, the building began to function, where there are rooms with increased comfort. Thanks to this, it became possible to receive even more visitors. To ensure heating, its own boiler house was commissioned in 2004. Therefore, you can always use hot water.





Population

In the sanatorium "Russian Forest" (Vladimir region - the place of its deployment) you can get from the capital. People come here directly on buses of a high level of comfort at the disposal of the administration of the complex. When visitors arrive, they are accommodated in comfortable rooms. They have the latest interior elements, televisions, refrigeration equipment for storing food, sanitary facilities with cool and warm water. In December 2007, a new complex for treatment and rehabilitation began to function. The cabinets in it are quite bright and large, endowed with the best types of equipment. Here you can undergo an excellent complex of treatment that will help get rid of old ailments and significantly increase the overall tone of the body.





Wellness

Quite an extensive medical base has a sanatorium "Russian Forest" (Vladimir region). Reviews about local services, as a rule, leave positive. The same goes for visitors from other regions. Physiotherapy procedures, treatment with a magnet, a laser, various equipment that copes well with varicose veins and other diseases are especially praised. There are sessions using current. Effective treatment is called electrosleep, bath complexes of dry carbon dioxide type. A wonderful apparatus is functioning, with the help of which electrophoresis or galvanization procedures are carried out. Special attention deserves ultrasound therapy, as well as inhalation.

Vladimir region, Sobinsky district, sanatorium "Russian Forest" is the place where you can get treatment with a nebulizer, become a user of aromatherapy or heal yourself with fresh air. Do not write off the recovery with the help of the purest water here. You can visit the sauna, take a bath of the balneological type or shower, get a hydromassage. In addition, the impact on the muscles can be provided by the local masters. Specialists provide classic, acupressure, reflexology massage services, provide mechanotherapy.

Improvement in many areas is carried out by the Russian Forest sanatorium. Reviews indicate that another no less interesting procedure is the treatment with herbal teas. They can affect the most diverse spectra of the human body. Also quite effective direction can be called physical therapy. The complex has the opportunity to visit the sports hall. There, each visitor will find modern simulators, all kinds of equipment used in the needs of customers.





Improving mental state

Sanatorium "Russian Forest" - a place where you can cure not only the body, but also the soul. To do this, you need to attend a special lecture, where classes on psychotherapy are held. Particular attention should be paid to the halochamber. Staying in it is combined with music therapy, which provides a good psychological relief. Those who are sick with pathology of the bronchi and lungs can get a spiroanalysis. Regular monitoring of electrographic indicators.

As for the personnel working as part of the team of CJSC “Sanatorium Russian Forest” (Sobinsky District), these are true masters of their craft with high qualifications. In their arsenal - the latest methods of medical and diagnostic activities, as well as methods of psychotherapy.





Nutrition

You can get food in the dining complex. It is located not too far from the bedroom building. Type of menu - custom. Patients are served by waiters. If there is such a need, the doctor may prescribe a special diet. Dishes are offered in a wide assortment. All of them are rich in useful substances and vitamins. The emphasis is on fresh fruits and vegetables, various juices.

Entertainment

It’s not at all like a Russian Forest sanatorium. Feedback from visitors suggests that it is extremely interesting to spend leisure time here, go to a club, find fascinating information in the library, watch a film, take part in a dance evening, go to nature to contemplate its pristine beauty, watch a concert.



Here come quite well-known groups of the Vladimir Drama Theater, Philharmonic of the region, folklore ensembles, wind instrument orchestras. In addition, performances at which one can get acquainted with works related to amateur performances of an artistic type are quite popular. Of considerable interest are sightseeing trips to Vladimir, to the territory of Gus-Khrustalny and Suzdal.

Leisure

To play tennis, basketball and volleyball, you can occupy a special area, rent the right equipment of high quality. There is an opportunity to try your hand at billiards or ping-pong. There is a playground for children, where there is a swing, various houses made of wood and slides. So young visitors here are no less interesting than adults.

You can go to the sauna with a Russian and Finnish bath, a small pool where there is contrast water. Many people like to visit the wonderful river here, fry kebabs and have fun outdoors. There is a covered area, a pavilion that is heated, and several barbecue facilities. If there is a need to purchase something, there is the opportunity to go to a store on the territory of the sanatorium to shop. Evening relaxation can also be enjoyed at the bar.

To communicate with family and friends, they use a local payphone that works in intercity and international destinations. If you plan to come here by car, you can leave the vehicle in the parking lot. She is guarded.

Looking forward to see you

Over the years, this complex has successfully met the needs of visitors for a healthy, comprehensive, effective vacation. The health resort serves, in particular, vulnerable groups of the population, that is, participants in the Second World War and veterans, people with disabilities, and patients of retirement age. Many guests return to these walls next year, noting the high level of service.



A trip here is a great way to relieve stress, improve your health and get an unforgettable experience. Influence on a person’s condition is especially effective here. Many clients normalize the state of the psyche and organs. They come home happy, rested and full of energy.
