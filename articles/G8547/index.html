Guests in the hotel room can be from 8 am to 11 pm. If you decide to stay with your friend at the hotel, you will need a passport, as well as 800 rubles.





Wet cleaning is done at the hotel every day from 9 am to 5 pm. Bed linen in the rooms is changed once every 3 days, and towels - daily.

During the stay at the hotel, customers are required to:

  • pay bills on time;
  • comply with fire safety regulations;
  • register your visitors with the administrator at the reception;
  • monitor the silence in the room;
  • Do not disturb others (neighbors, etc.);
  • register a personal vehicle, if any;
  • leaving the room, close the windows, turn off the working electrical appliances and lights, as well as water;
  • take care of the hotel property, and in case of damage or loss, compensate Victoria for the damage.

In turn, hotel guests are prohibited from:

  • disturb the peace of other residents;
  • smoking in the room (a fine of 3 thousand rubles);
  • carry weapons, radioactive and chemical substances, as well as flammable and unsafe materials;
  • transfer the key card from the room to unauthorized persons;
  • keep any animals in the room.

Reviews

This hotel complex is one of the best in the city, because for more than 18 years it has been stubbornly earning a good reputation. By the way, recently a very large-scale event was held here in honor of such an event as the birthday of the Victoria Hotel (Chelyabinsk), which is celebrated on September 19 every year. A huge number of comments were left about this celebration in Runet, testifying to an unforgettable holiday.

As for the reviews about the hotel itself, they are also quite positive. The guests of the complex claim that Victoria is an excellent hotel with friendly staff, whose representatives always respond quickly to any customer requests. The rooms here are large and clean, as well as comfortable, but sometimes there is a smell of dampness in the bathrooms.





Regular visitors of the hotel write that in different rooms of the same category there are slightly different conditions: somewhere there are excellent bathrobes and high-quality slippers, while somewhere the bathrobes resemble something disposable, but there are no slippers at all. Other inconsistencies are also noted: sometimes there are 3 or 4 towels in the room for one person, and there are situations when there are only 2 of them. Of the advantages of the complex, people distinguish a pool, sauna and gym, as well as a high level of service.

In conclusion, we note that often there are reviews indicating monotonous breakfasts and very high prices for drinks in the minibar, but this can also be attributed to the little things.
