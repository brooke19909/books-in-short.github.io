Bus

The bus runs from Tver to St. Petersburg infrequently, only twice a week, so if you intend to go on a trip, take care of buying tickets in advance.

The trip will take longer than on a similar route by bus. In total, you will spend about 13 hours on the road. On average, a ticket will cost you 1000 rubles. If you use the services of a popular carrier Busfor, then you will pay about 900 rubles.





Regular buses run through Tver to St. Petersburg. Their cost is approximately the same - about 1000 rubles. At the same time, you will spend less on the way: from 7 to 9 hours. Some buses run daily, so call the dispatcher at the bus station more often and check for bus tickets.

By air

Oddly enough, you can get from St. Petersburg to Tver by air. For example, you can fly to Moscow, and from there take a train to Tver. A plane ticket will cost from 1,800 to 14,000 rubles. You will spend 1 hour 15 minutes on the road.

After that, you go to Belorussky Train Station, from where the Sapsan train leaves every three hours. Travel time is only one hour. In this case, the ticket will cost from 650 to 1,200 rubles. As a result, you will spend 5.5 hours on the road and the amount in the range from 2 600 to 15 500 Russian rubles. Of the total time, you will spend about two hours on a transfer.

At the same time, the cost of moving depends on which Moscow airport you fly to. For example, the cheapest option is Sheremetyevo. The total cost of the trip may be 2,600 rubles. The next cost option is to land at Vnukovo. Be prepared to spend at least 3,000 rubles and 6 hours on a trip. If you land at Domodedovo, the cost of the trip will also start at 3,000 rubles.

How to get from the airport to Moscow?

From Sheremetyevo Airport to Belorussky Train Station you can be reached by the Aero Shuttle line train. Pros - no traffic jams and a comfortable trip. You will spend only half an hour on the road, and spend about 60 rubles on a ticket. The train leaves every half hour.

If you land at Domodedovo Airport, take the Aeroexpress. The road to the city center will take about 46 minutes, trains depart every half hour. However, be prepared to pay more here: a one-way ticket will cost about 500 rubles.

From Vnukovo to the center there is also an aeroexpress train. The trip will cost 500 rubles, the duration of the trip will be 38 minutes.

As you can see, there are many ways to get from St. Petersburg to Tver. All of them vary in cost and duration. When planning a trip, choose the option that will be most optimal for you, then the trip will be as comfortable as possible and will not bring inconvenience.
