What hotels in Olonets attract tourists

Before considering the hotels of Olonets, let us dwell on a small characteristic of this Karelian city. Why do thousands of tourists from different parts of our country seek here every year?

Quick reference

The city has a mild, humid climate, as Lake Onega is located near it. The population is about 9 thousand people. There are many private houses, and a few five-story buildings are located mainly along the Olonka River. This quiet, clean town impresses with its natural beauty; it is famous for museums and numerous temples. It has its own railway and bus stations, a dairy plant, a bakery.

Tourists book Olonets hotels in order to enjoy the splendor of the natural landscape, explore local attractions, and breathe in fresh air. Many seek to be in these places in order to take an active part in the international festival of Santa Clauses.

How to choose a hotel

How to choose the right place of residence if you decide to visit Olonets? Hotel "Olonia" is located in the very center of the city in a building of unique architecture, which was built by the project of the leading architect of Karelia Elena Itsekson. Guests note the excellent location of the hotel, the high level of service staff, as well as quality food. If you analyze the reviews about Olonets hotels, this particular hotel is in maximum demand among foreign guests who come to this quiet Karelian town to go fishing on Lake Onega and enjoy the pristine natural landscape.

When choosing a hotel, you need to be guided not only by your material wealth, but also by the guest reviews that can be found on the tourist portals.

Among the services offered by the hotel "Olonia", we note:

  • convenient parking;
  • a sauna;
  • a restaurant;
  • beauty salon (hairdresser);
  • bar;
  • banqueting hall;
  • taxi call;
  • safe for valuables.

This Olonets hotel is located near the railway station, which increases its attractiveness among tourists.
