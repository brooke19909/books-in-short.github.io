Although sometimes more expensive specimens are found. In each city there is at least 1 such flea market, and in Krasnodar there are as many as 4 flea markets.

Flea markets of Krasnodar

The indigenous inhabitants of Krasnodar are very lucky, there are a lot of such bazaars and they simply amaze with their abundance. But if local residents are well aware of these wonderful places, then tourists are probably wondering where is the flea market in Krasnodar. So on the territory of the market "Optimus" is one of these flea markets. Her range is simply mesmerizing. Here you can find not only old things in the second-hand department, but also vintage jewelry, antique dishes and much more. The market is located on the street. Moscow, 100.

The second flea market of Krasnodar is located on the territory of the East Kruglikovsky market on the street. Vostochno-Kruglikovskaya 8. This place is notable for the fact that here the entire range of goods is located strictly in accordance with their purpose, namely in the form of trading platforms:

  1. A platform for the sale of animals. Here you can buy not only your favorite animal, but also cages, food, veterinary drugs and accessories.
  2. Plant selling site. This is perhaps one of the most colorful venues on the market. After all, here you can find not only flowers and plants, but also seeds, pots, soil and mineral fertilizers for them.
  3. Second-hand playground. And this place is the most favorite place for collectors. After all, only here you can buy old coins, badges, interesting books, as well as interesting, original clothes and shoes.

The third market is located on the territory of the shopping center "Turgenevsky" at the address: st. Turgenev, 189. Here you can also buy jewelry, dishes, clothes and interior items, among which quite valuable exhibits are sometimes found.

At the fourth flea market, which is located on the street. Traveling, you can meet anything you want, namely:

  • Musical instruments.
  • Vintage turntables, radios and tape recorders.
  • Dishes from the post-Soviet space.
  • Soviet Christmas toys and other New Year's accessories.
  • Joiner's and locksmith tools, as well as all kinds of cogs, bolts, carnations and much more.
