Hotel Panorama, Vityazevo: overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

For lovers of relaxation on the Black Sea coast, the Panorama Hotel can be an ideal option . Vityazevo is a village, and the hotel is a tall tower with huge, full-height rooms with panoramic windows, from which vacationers can admire both the sea and the beautiful resorts of Vityazevo and Anapa. Due to its unique architecture, the hotel was called Panorama.





Location

The Panorama Hotel is located in the village of Vityazevo at the address: 101 Gorky Street. It is a three-story building. Just a ten minute walk away is a clean sandy beach. Near the hotel there are old manors, a pump room with mineral water, Byzantium park, Paralia embankment. A ten-minute walk from the hotel, guests can visit the water park. Vityazevo is located 11 kilometers from the center of Anapa resort. The village stands on the banks of the Vityazevsky estuary, and the sea is shallow. It warms up quickly. The beaches in the village are large and comfortable. Vityazevo has become a favorite vacation spot for many tourists, so in the holiday season the population of the village increases several times.
