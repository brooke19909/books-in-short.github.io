White Sand Beach hotels: description, reviews

In the harsh rhythm of city bustle, sooner or later, everyone thinks about relaxation on the shores of the azure sea, about a sun lounger and warm sun, about everything that cannot be found in a megalopolis or on simple tourist trips. And now, with the arrival of thoughts about an ideal vacation, a feverish search for the very paradise town, booking a hotel and ordering tickets begins. The most popular white beaches, White Sand Beach with hotels on them, are located in Bali and Thailand.

Holidays at White Sand Beach Hotel in Pattaya will not disappoint

Sea, waves, sun, beach and a high level of service - these are the main components of a delightful holiday that White Sand Beach Residences 5 * can offer - a magnificent hotel in Thailand, located in the suburbs of Pattaya Na-Jomtien, next to a snow-white beach. The distance to the main U-Tapao airport is about 20 km, and the central Pattaya area is only 11 km from the hotel, Jomtien Beach is 6 km, so the problem of getting there or back from anywhere is very easy.

Climate in Pattaya

The climate of Thailand is almost ideal for a beach holiday, walks and a carefree pastime. Tropical and humid, it improves not only the general condition, but also gives a feeling of relaxation, and the high temperature contributes to a pleasant swim in the waves of the Andaman Sea. It is considered one of the most beautiful, the water in it has an azure blue color, on its shore is the hospitable White Sand Beach Residence Pattaya. The rainy season begins in May and ends in October.

White Sand Beach Residence 5 * is a hotel for the whole family, you can come here with your spouse or with relatives in their entirety, and children under 6 years old who sleep on their beds with parents can relax here for free, provided for them playgrounds.

White Sand Dreams Beach in Bali

White Sand Beach in Bali is the only white-sand beach in the east of Bali. It is located 8 km northeast of Chandidasa and 8 km north of Amlapura in the Karangasem region. This is the closest beach to the mainland berths of ferries, it is conditionally divided into southern and northern parts. The south side is much more visited, but you can get here only after going through one of the hotels. The northern part is distinguished by its calmness: there are few people and not many buildings are located nearby.

Also in this area are areas with volcanic sand and boulders.

The white sand beach stretches for about 3 kilometers, the entrance to the sea is quite shallow, which is very convenient when vacationing with children.

A distinctive feature of the beach is the perfect combination of relatively calm from the waves of sites and sites for surfing, where the wave is perfectly caught. On the White Sand Beach there are both generously sunlit places, especially for lovers of sunbathing, as well as many trees under which you can relax in the shade.

In the tourist seasons, White Sand Beach, of course, is not always so spacious and quiet, although most of the time it is in serene tranquility.

Inside the beach, umbrellas, sunbeds and beach towels are rented. In some cases, all this is provided free of charge, for example, if you are a guest of the nearest hotels on White Sand Beach.

Toward night, especially in the tourist season, the beach changes its appearance: the nearest cafes and eateries take inventory to the street and sell tasty and fragrant food, trees are decorated with various lanterns and flowers, which is very similar to the passage of a festival.
