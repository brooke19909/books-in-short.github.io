Novosibirsk hotels in the city center: photos and descriptions, service, addresses and reviews

Novosibirsk is one of the largest cities in Russia (in particular, in terms of population, it takes an honorable third place). This is the scientific, business, industrial and cultural center of Siberia. It is not surprising that a continuous stream of tourists and business people is directed here. Novosibirsk city center hotels are a great option for accommodation.

Hotel Marins Park

The largest hotel in the center of Novosibirsk is the Marins Park. It is located at Vokzalnaya highway, 1, in the immediate vicinity of the railway station. The following features are provided for guests:

  • Accommodation in rooms from standard to luxury apartments. The cost of accommodation is from 2300 rubles per day.
  • 4 conference rooms ranging from 66 to 163 square meters. m. Rental price - from 1500 rubles per hour.
  • The Cinema restaurant with a classic European menu, the Berman Grill restaurant with an emphasis on the grill menu and Asian cuisine, the Cup of Coffee coffee shop with hot drinks and desserts, a cozy round-the-clock lobby bar.
  • Wellness center with beauty and spa services.
  • ATMs of Gazprombank, Credit Europe Bank, Sberbank, Tinkoff Bank.

Reviews

Tourists who visited this hotel in the center of the city of Novosibirsk, call such advantages of the institution:

  • delicious and hearty breakfasts;
  • excellent beer restaurant;
  • large televisions in the rooms;
  • successful location in the city center in close proximity to the railway station;
  • beautiful decoration of the hall.

And such cons:

  • uncomfortable creaky bed;
  • air conditioning is very weak;
  • stuffiness in the rooms (you have to sleep with the windows open);
  • terrible quality of cleaning;
  • strange thin towels in the bathroom;
  • unstable wireless internet signal.
