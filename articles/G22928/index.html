Water park in Barnaul in the shopping center "Europe": photos, description and reviews of visitors

Barnaul has many modern shopping centers and all kinds of entertainment. However, until recently, the city did not have a large indoor pool with attractions. Today the situation has changed, because more recently a water park was opened in Barnaul in the shopping center "Europe". It is in this place that we will go on a virtual tour today.

Attractions and extreme entertainment

Only a significant percentage of lovers of water parks go to water entertainment centers only to calmly swim in the pool. Many visitors are primarily interested in slides and other entertainment. The water park in Barnaul offers its guests traditional family slopes and extreme attractions. In the largest pool is a complex of five slides. These are two Tobogans, Kamikaze, a closed hydrotube and a classic wide direct family descent. Small slides are also available in the children's pool. The large pool has refreshing spray fountains; they are very popular with children and adults.
