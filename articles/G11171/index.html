Qatar: general information

Qatar is located on the Arabian Peninsula (southwest) and is washed by the pristine waters of the Persian Gulf on 3 sides. With the UAE and Saudi Arabia, it borders on the south. The area is 11 thousand km¬≤, the population is about 2,258 thousand people, while the Arabs mostly inhabit the country.

About beach holidays in Qatar

According to tourists, a beach holiday in Qatar is beautiful not only in the summer. Excellent quality of service, modern comfortable hotels, fabulous surrounding nature (especially from March to May and from September to January) - this is Qatar.

Of course, next to the most popular and popular neighbors (Abu Dhabi and Dubai), Doha, the capital of Qatar, looks more modest. Here you will not see huge crowds of tourists enthusiastically looking at local attractions (and there are fantastic skyscrapers here), you will not find numerous shopaholics scurrying along the malls and buying everything up.

Doha is not so popular yet, and the rates for local hotel rooms are lower, but the locals have a great desire to make the rest for guests the most comfortable and unforgettable.

Each tourist who chooses for himself a holiday in Qatar (reviews of tourists are presented below) receives an excellent quality of service, while not overpaying for the name of any resort, as for a well-known fashion brand.

About nature

In hotels with a beautifully landscaped oasis and excellent service, you spend your holidays in Qatar. Reviews of tourists most often talk about living conditions. However, the landscapes of Qatar are not particularly attractive and bright. The relief of the northern part of the country is represented by a low sandy plain, covered with moving dunes. The central part of the territory is a rocky desert covered with pebble, and in some places with salt marshes. The southern part of the state is sandy high hills.

The peninsula is very poor in water, there are oases, but they are small and rare. With all this, the nature of Qatar is unique.
