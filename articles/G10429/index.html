Dolce Gabbana-style bezel: how to make a stylish accessory with your own hands

Fashion is changing rapidly, but women always want to stay in trend. In recent seasons, hair bands have become a real hit. Black or color, wide or narrow, with various decorations - a huge number of options. This stylish accessory is not necessary to buy, you can make it yourself.

Dolce Gabbana style bezel - luxury and style in one look

In 2012, the Dolce Gabbana headbands were especially popular. At that time, many celebrities wore precious jewelry at various ceremonies and social parties.



This accessory is able to emphasize tenderness and femininity, with its help the image will be completed. The headband in the style of "Dolce Gabbana" is made from materials of pastel colors, it is decorated with sequins, large beads or shiny stones. These accessories go well with feminine dresses and classic hairstyles. Hair can be loosened or beautifully styled.

How to wear the Dolce Gabbana headband?

Girls put on them not only under a dress. Headbands will look great under classic trousers and airy shirts. Accessories are harmoniously combined with short or long dresses and shoes with high or low heels. It is best to choose clothes that will also contain lace or beads.

The Dolce-Gabbana-style bezel is like a luxurious diadem. However, it can be worn in everyday life, experimenting and trying on the most daring images. For everyday wear, you can pick up a bezel with a small amount of stones and beads. For special occasions, it is best to choose a more luxurious option.



Do-it-yourself Dolce-Gabbana-style bezel: essential materials and tools

Such an accessory is very expensive, not everyone can buy the same. There are also inexpensive options that are similar in appearance, but they use cheaper materials and non-precious stones. Each girl will be able to make a bead in the style of Dolce Gabbana with her own hands, you just need to make a little effort and acquire the necessary materials:

  • First of all, you should choose the appropriate jewelry - multi-colored stones, rhinestones, beads, pearls, metal or ceramic flowers, etc.
  • the rim is black, white or golden (the accessory made of suede will be the most successful option);
  • if the rim is plastic, then it is additionally necessary to prepare a synthetic winterizer and velvet fabric;
  • glue (glue gun), threads with a needle, which are needed for fastening decorative elements;
  • toothpicks and wand.

Manufacturing instruction

First of all, you need to prepare the basis for the rim. It‚Äôs good if the store has a ready-made bezel trimmed in velvet. If not, then the plastic or metal base is lined with velvet fabric and stuffed with padding polyester.

Next, you need to prepare decorative flowers of various shapes and sizes, white pearls and rhinestones. You need not make a mistake with the size and quantity so that the details are enough for the decor of the entire rim. For more convenient work with glue, you need to use a stick and a toothpick; with their help, glue is much more convenient to apply to small parts.

When everything is ready, you can begin to lay out the pattern on the hoop. Details can be placed in a chaotic manner, but the bezel in the style of "Dolce Gabbana" will take on a more accurate appearance if any symmetry is visible. Flowers and stones should alternate among themselves. Smaller parts must be attached around the edges. The tips of the product are left without decor (5 cm from the beginning should be left free).



Thus, each girl has the opportunity to create a wide variety of headbands. You can do without flowers, using only beads of the same color scheme in different sizes. They can also be glued to super-glue, or they can also be sewn to a base lined with velvet or satin. Stones and beads are placed randomly, however, symmetry should also be traced here.

The bezel in the style of "Dolce Gabbana" thanks to the manner of its execution and the used decorative elements resembles a product in the luxurious Baroque style. Even if large beautiful beads are used in the manufacture, the accessory can be worn not only under a chic evening dress. It is perfect for everyday life. Of course, in the evening look the rim will look like an exquisite tiara, but it can be a great addition to jeans - you get a stylish and unusual combination. You can have several different jewelry in your arsenal that will suit any style of clothing, because you already know how to make a headband in the style of Dolce Gabbana. You do not have to spend a lot of money on buying expensive accessories.
