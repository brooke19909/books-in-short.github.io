Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 * (United Arab Emirates / Ajman): reviews and ratings

You can sunbathe under the hot sun and swim in the warm sea in winter at different resorts of the planet. Many tourists choose for this purpose, for example, the UAE. This southern country is perfect for both individual and family holidays. The tourist infrastructure of the UAE is well developed, and you can spend time here with great comfort. Of course, there are many convenient hotels in this state. One of these hotels, offering tourists comfortable rooms and pleasant service, is the Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 *. Reviews of vacationers this hotel deserved just magnificent.

Location Features

The Crown Palace Hotel is located in the quiet resort town of Ajman. This small town is the capital of the emirate of the same name. Life here, in contrast to the same Dubai, flows slowly and calmly. In this case, according to most tourists, there is everything you need for a good vacation.





Actually the Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 * hotel itself (Ajman) is located on one of the busiest streets of the city. A public transport stop is within walking distance from it. Thus, from the hotel, you can quickly and easily leave not only in any part of the city, but also in Dubai.

Hotel reviews

This hotel is a huge 17-storey building, forming in terms of the letter C and designed in a modern style. The hotel was built in 2009 and renovated in 2016. The combination of a white and cherry shade of the facade and a somewhat unusual shape make it bright and noticeable against the background of ordinary city houses.

Inside, the hotel, according to most of its guests, looks simply luxurious. The ceiling in a huge hall is supported by massive columns trimmed with artificial stone. Tourists who have booked rooms at this hotel can wait for registration if necessary on soft upholstered sofas. Golden, white and beige tones are chosen for interior decoration. Thanks to this and rather expensive decoration, the corridors, rooms and other rooms of this hotel look solid and presentable.

Most tourists consider the landscaped area in front of the hotel to be very beautiful. The hotel's courtyard is tiled in beautiful gray and white shades. In order to get into the hall, you need to climb a small staircase of several steps. Directly next to the facade of the hotel are neat lawns and palm trees. In most ratings, compiled on the basis of reviews of tourists by specialized sites, this hotel is among the ten best hotels in Ajman. Usually Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 * takes 7-13th places.





Hotel rooms

The hotel offers its guests 210 comfortable, spacious, well-equipped rooms. If desired, guests have the opportunity to remove:

  • Studio (35 m 2 ).

  • Bedroom Suite (60 m 2 ).

What amenities does Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 * offer its guests?

Studio - one-room rooms designed for two adults. If desired, you can order one more additional place. The rooms of the Bedroom Suite, in fact, are small apartments, so tourists like madly. They consist of a bedroom, a kitchenette, a living room and a corridor. In both types of rooms there are bathrooms with a shower, sink and toilet. The view from the windows of the apartments at the Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 *, according to all guests, is truly magnificent. The lagoon of the Persian Gulf looks really very impressive. Unfortunately, the sea is not visible from all the hotel rooms. From some rooms you can admire the beauty of the city itself. Modern Ajman looks no less impressive than the sea. And especially at night. With the onset of darkness, just a huge number of multi-colored lights light up here.





Room Reviews

Of course, the ratings of the best hotels are not compiled randomly. The basis is always taken reviews of hotel guests. This hotel was highly praised by tourists for a reason. The rooms here are not only very spacious (large area and with high ceilings), but also well equipped. If desired, hotel residents have the opportunity to use:

  • telephone

  • a refrigerator;

  • TV.

A bathrobe, slippers and a hairdryer can be provided upon request. The television in the hotel is cable. Our compatriots have the opportunity to watch as many as three Russian-language channels. Of course, there are in the Bedroom Suite and modern air conditioning. A well-equipped minibar is available for a fee. For coffee lovers, an electric kettle is provided in the room. It is not necessary for hotel residents to purchase bags on their own. Maids bring them to their room daily in the morning. This service at the hotel is included in the price.

Hotel infrastructure reviews

Tourists have a good opinion of this hotel, including because it is possible to use here for free:

  • Wi-Fi (on site and in the lobby);

  • currency exchange office;

  • by the pool;

  • TV room.

If desired, this hotel can celebrate an anniversary, hold a wedding or corporate event. For this, the hotel has a banquet hall for 300 people. It should be rent, according to most tourists, is quite acceptable. Also completely free in this hotel you can visit the cozy Internet cafe. For a fee, the hotel offers the following services:

  • call a doctor;

  • rent a business center.

The hotel also has laundry facilities. Here guests have the opportunity to wash their own dirty laundry. Many hotels in the UAE provide this service for free. But, unfortunately, at Crown Palace you will have to pay a certain amount (not too much) for it.

Entertainment at the hotel

In addition, tourists at the hotel have the opportunity to visit the gym. Get in shape at the Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 * is completely free. The hotel also has a sauna. A fee for her visit is also not charged. There is also a small billiard room in this hotel. But her visit is possible only after payment.



Hotel Bus

In addition, the Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 *, reviews of which are good also for this reason, offers its guests another very interesting service. The hotel has its own comfortable bus, where tourists can drive to the major shopping centers Ajman City Center for free. Of course, this is very convenient. Many guests use the hotel bus. Due to its presence, many tourists respond very well about the hotel. Although the distance to the beach from the hotel is not too great, the bus is also used to transport guests to the seashore. This hotel service is also free.

Hotel pool

The Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 * belongs to the three-star group. Therefore, here, as in most of these hotels, there is a swimming pool. It is located on the roof of the hotel, and swimming in it, according to many tourists, is very convenient. The views from the site are simply magnificent. The pool water is always clean and warm (temperature control is provided). Umbrellas and sun loungers are set nearby. You can use them for free. The opportunity to swim in the warm clean fresh water of the pool is enjoyed by many hotel guests.





Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 *: hotel beaches

Relaxing in this hotel is thus very convenient. But tourists are considered equally no less comfortable and the beach located 2 km from it. Towels for swimming in the sea are provided to hotel guests for free. Actually the coastline near the hotel is covered with soft fine sand. Therefore, sunbathing here is a pleasure. The entrance to the sea in this place is also good - gentle and without holes. The hotel bus goes to the beach twice a day. If you wish, you can leave for the hotel on a city minibus. Umbrellas and sun loungers on the beach are free of charge (subject to availability). But many tourists prefer to sunbathe on rugs. There are no stones on the beach, neither large nor small.

Of course, there are other equally convenient beaches in Ajman. But tourists resting in this village should know that for some of them women from 10 years old are allowed only on special days. The UAE is a Muslim country, and of course, it is imperative to respect its traditions.

Nutrition Reviews

The food at the Crown Palace hotel, according to most of its guests, is very good. The hotel provides the following types of food:

  • VV - "bed and breakfast."

  • HB - half board.

In the latter case, breakfast and dinner are included in the price. If desired, in this hotel you can rent a room without meals. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the hotel restaurant according to the very convenient buffet system.





Family holiday

Of course, if you wish, the Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 * (the photos on the page clearly demonstrate how beautiful and comfortable the hotel is) can come with children. For small guests here are provided:

  • special menu in the restaurant;

  • playground.

Of course, the hotel restaurant has small highchairs. If necessary, parents have the opportunity to rent in this hotel a crib of the right size (for babies from 3 to 8 years).





Instead of a conclusion

As you can see, quite rightly deserved the good hotel Crown Palace Hotel Ajman 3 * reviews from tourists, and its rating is actually very high. Both single tourists and business people or families with children can spend time here comfortably. Having booked a room in this wonderful hotel, you can make your stay in the UAE as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.
