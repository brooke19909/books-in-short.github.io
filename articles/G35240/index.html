"Mandarin," Adler. Rest in Adler: reviews of tourists and photos

The entertainment complex "Mandarin" (Adler) is one of the most popular among all tourist institutions in the south of Russia. The main reason for this is the highly developed infrastructure, quality service, as well as the ability to visit the coast daily, swim and get positive emotions.

Where is?





The Mandarin Hotel (Adler) and the shopping center to which it belongs belongs territorially to a part of the resort city of Sochi, which until 1961 was a typical urban-type settlement. From here, it is only 5 kilometers to the railway station and airport, which daily take flights from various parts of Russia. Several thousand tourists arrive daily in the summer in Adler to enjoy a wonderful vacation.

In addition, in Adler there are a large number of health resorts and sanatoriums, the services of which are used by patients suffering from diseases of the musculoskeletal system, nervous system, digestion, as well as the cardiovascular system. Doctors from all over Russia constantly send their wards to the south of Russia to improve their health. Many of them stay at the Mandarin, which is located at ul. Bestuzhev, house 1/1.

"Mandarin" - what is it?





Shopping and entertainment complex "Mandarin" (Adler) is one of the innovations in the entertainment industry. It is the only shopping center in Russia, which is located in the open. In European countries, such shopping and entertainment complexes have long been commonplace; in Russia, such objects are not yet common.

The mall includes not only countless shops, but also a nightclub, hotel and even its own beach. The favorable location of the complex makes it even more convenient for vacationers. Shopping Malls

SEC "Mandarin" (Adler) consists of nine buildings, interconnected with the help of bridges and terraces. This design of the complex allows its visitors to freely visit all the stores available here. Owners of local outlets never sit idle, thanks to low prices it is here that 40% of the population of Adler goes shopping.

It is here that you can find stores of well-known prestigious brands: L'Etoile, Basic Instinct, Moncasa, which have long established themselves in the world market as suppliers of high-quality goods. In addition to shops, there is also a food court, where shoppers tired of shopping will be able to dine in comfortable conditions. For fast food lovers, the McDonald's fast food restaurant is open around the clock.

Where can I stay?





The Mandarin Hotel (Adler) has only three stars, but it is in demand at any time of the year, since it is located close to all the most important infrastructure facilities of the city. In total, 58 rooms are offered to vacationers, among them there are single, double and triple rooms, there are also family and low-budget rooms, so renting a house here is not difficult.

All rooms have televisions, refrigerators, air conditioning and all necessary for living. Vacationers can use Wi-Fi, parking, banquet and conference room services, if necessary, hide jewelry, you can use the safe, just notify the duty administrator. For an additional fee, guests can even live with their pets, this service is very popular here, since not all hotels allow guests with cats and dogs to come to them.

If you want to dance ...





For fans of nightlife, the Mandarin Club operates here, Adler is generally famous for a large number of such institutions, since tourists come here primarily to relax and unwind. The club is located on two floors and is divided into a large number of recreation areas. Fans of delicious food can choose the Provence restaurant, where you can taste several dishes at once.

Those who want to relax in a calm environment, and who are comfortable with a quiet atmosphere, will like the Chateau - a green veranda that blooms and pleases the eye all year round. Those who can sing or just love to do it are best to go to the Versailles karaoke bar, where a professional team of backing vocalists and sound engineers will help you feel like a star of the stage.

If you are heading to Mandarin (Adler), be sure to visit the Vogue, the party terrace with a magnificent view of the mountains. The Vogue management often holds celebrations on order, so if you are organizing them, you can safely contact them. For gentlemen, the Bordeaux men's club operates here, where professional dancers will let you forget about everyday routine and get real pleasure watching the art of erotic dance.

Guest House





Guest house "Mandarin" (Adler) is another institution where tourists can stay and enjoy their vacation. The facility is located next to the sea on Medova Street, it is very convenient. Its visitors can purchase here vouchers for a period of not less than nine days, such is the living condition, which does not include meals. The house has its own dining room, which feeds guests for an additional fee.

If we talk about the cost of living, "Mandarin" (Adler) in this case offers a vacation at very affordable prices. The price of a 9-day stay here ranges from 11 to 20 thousand rubles for an adult and from 10 to 19 thousand for a child under 14 years old. These prices are valid if you occupy the main places. You can also purchase additional space, its cost ranges from 7 to 14 thousand rubles.

Reviews





If you come to the south and are looking for the most environmentally friendly beach, "Mandarin" (Adler), whose reviews are purely positive, is the best option. And in fact, the owners of the beach try to keep it in good order, cleaning it several times a day. The beach area is constantly updated with new attractions and infrastructure facilities, and the equipment provided for rent is high quality.

If we talk about the entire complex "Mandarin" (Adler), reviews about it are mixed. First of all, guests are dissatisfied with the services provided at the local hotel, it is about the service, which, in their opinion, leaves much to be desired. Negative impressions of the hotel are smoothed out by the opportunity to soak up the sun, swim in the warm sea and pass the time in local entertainment facilities.
