Nokia Lumia 800 - specs and model overview

The Nokia Lumia line of models , as soon as it entered the market, was immediately liked by smartphone users for their stylish image, practical material, and productive filling. In this article, we will consider the flagship model Lumia 800 from Nokia.

The history of the appearance of the smartphone

As you know, Nokia signed a cooperation agreement with Microsoft, after which in October 2011 the famous Nokia Lumia 800 was introduced, a characteristic that fans of the brand liked. The gadget runs on the Windows phone operating system. The smartphone has inherited the best technology from its predecessor N9. A beautiful operating system, presented on the desktop with shortcuts, tiles, a polycarbonate case, heavy-duty glass, a bright display - all this inspired confidence at first sight. In Russia, the smartphone went on sale in December 2011. On the shelves of shop windows, this device immediately stood out among other smartphone models that were so alike.

The gadget "Nokia Lumiya 800", the characteristic of which differs from the phones of other famous manufacturers, has become a real symbol of the revolution among modern personal technology.





operating system

For the first time, Nokia introduced a smartphone based on the Windows Phone 7 operating system, which could later be updated to version 7.8. The latest improved version of the OS has aligned the tiles across the entire width of the desktop, added animation, and fixed minor flaws in the crude version of the first mobile seven. Compared with Android, the speed is much higher and, according to experts, there is no need to install an antivirus. All software is protected and isolated from external interference, since applications are installed from the Windows Phone Marketplace and all programs that want to "live" in your phone must meet several requirements - have a digital signature and a certificate of compliance. In general, the system is convenient and reliable, but has a number of limitations. So, for example, you will not be able to see system files, use Explorer, task manager, there is no equalizer in the music player and so on. It seems that the "axis" was created for an undemanding user, with a minimum of manual settings. The developers claim that they had to make such sacrifices for the sake of security and stability of the system.

But for our Russian mentality, this, of course, is a significant drawback, since we are not used to going to service centers to replace the battery, and in order to replace it, we will have to take apart a half-phone.
