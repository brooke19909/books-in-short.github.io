How to pay by mobile via Mobile Bank: popular ways

The convenience of the Mobile Bank service from Sberbank has been appreciated by many customers. Rich functionality allows you to make various payments without leaving your home. With it, it will turn out to transfer money, pay for the Internet, phone, housing and communal services, make a loan payment. How to pay mobile via Mobile Bank? Balance replenishment is carried out in several ways, from which you can choose any convenient. This is described in the article.

Mobile Bank Connection

Before using the service, you need to connect it. This can be done through a branch of Sberbank or a terminal, an ATM. The service is free, but a fee is charged for using SMS notifications.





There are 2 tariffs:

  1. "Complete . " At this rate, notifications and requests for transfers and expenses of funds will be received. 60 rubles are charged per service per month, while a full range of services is provided. But if the card is student or social, then the fee is 30 rubles. Gold card users are served free of charge.
  2. "Economical . " There is no charge for this tariff, with the client receiving a minimum set of notifications. If you use additional services, then this will be done for a fee. According to the tariff, there are no messages about income and expenses. You can make a request yourself, but it will be paid.

Shutdown

To disable the service, you can use 2 ways:

  1. Contact the bank with a statement. To do this, you need a passport and a contract for a card. Within 3 days, the service is turned off. Typically, the client receives a message.
  2. Call the contact center. The operator needs to provide personal data, and the service will be disabled. This procedure is free.

Through personal account

With its help, you can make a payment to any mobile. How to pay for a phone through Sberbank Mobile Banking? To do this, go to your personal account using your username and password. If you wish, you can receive one-time passwords or get printed data at an ATM. This method is considered convenient, because you can transfer money wherever there is Internet. Moreover, all payments are made quickly and safely.





You must select the payment section, and then find a mobile connection. It is important to select an operator and enter a phone number. Then you need to check and confirm the operation. Money is credited quickly and without commission for any operator.

For Sberbank customers there is a mobile application that can be installed on smartphones and tablets. This is the so-called mini-bank, which has all the functionality. The system is installed quickly and free of charge. How to pay for a mobile using the Mobile Bank with its help? It is enough to perform the procedure described above.

Via telephone

If you have a mobile phone, you can replenish his account from the card at any time. The main thing is that the Mobile Bank service is activated. And this procedure is performed in the office and through a specialist contact center. How to pay for a phone through Sberbank Mobile Banking? To do this, you need to poison SMS with the amount to number 900. Funds are debited from the payment card opened by the user. If there are several of them, then the one where the money is present is selected.

If you want to transfer from a specific card, then in SMS you need to write the amount and the last 4 digits of the card. A message is sent to 900. When using these methods, the minimum amount is set to 10 rubles, and the maximum is 1000 rubles per day.

How to pay for a mobile phone through the Mobile Bank if the number of another person? To do this, you need to write in SMS ‚Äù: TEL (number to which you need to top up your account) 100. This is the phone number and amount. A message is sent to 900. How to pay for a mobile via Mobile Bank from a specific card? In this case, the last 4 digits of the card must be added to the message text.





If you deposit funds to a phone that is not connected to the Mobile Bank, the bank will send an SMS through which the transfer must be confirmed. When replenishing money to a number not registered with Sberbank, the minimum is 30 rubles, and the maximum is 3,000 rubles. These are all answers to questions on how to pay for your phone through the Mobile Bank.

USSD team

You can pay for mobile communications not only by the above methods, but also using the USSD command. For example, to send funds in the amount of 100 rubles, you need to dial * 900 * 100 # from your phone. Then the money will be transferred to a personal number. If you need to transfer funds to another phone, you need to dial * 900 * 9123456789 * 100 # (where 9123456789 is the number of the number to be recharged). After this, the funds are credited.

ATMs and terminals

You can pay for a mobile phone with a card through an ATM or without a card through a terminal. Such devices are available not only in bank branches, but also in various trading establishments, so there should not be any problems with payment.





ATMs and terminals have a mobile payment section. You must enter a phone number or choose from those that are. Transfer will be any amount, but not less than 10 rubles. After the operation, you need to pick up the check.

Auto payment

You can pay for mobile communications in a more convenient way, for example, to activate the Auto Payment service. You can do this at an ATM or terminal. The service is connected in your account.





The advantage of the service is that money is transferred automatically. You only need to specify the necessary data once, after which the account will be replenished itself if the phone balance is reduced to the specified amount. Funds are transferred instantly, and a message is received about the operation.

Thus, customers are provided with convenient ways to replenish the balance of the phone. You can use any of them. Funds are credited quickly and without commission, and this is not in every bank.
