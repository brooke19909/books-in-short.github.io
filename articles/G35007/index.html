Quadrocopters DJI Phantom 2 Vision +: review, specifications and reviews

Quadrocopter DJI Phantom 2 Vision + is, without a doubt, one of the best drones that do not require assembly. According to user reviews, thanks to the built-in stabilized high-definition camera, transmitter and GPS positioning system, this radio-controlled quadrocopter has everything you need for flying and capturing breathtaking shots. In addition, to expand the operating range, it is equipped with a Wi-Fi amplifier, so it can fly higher and further than many other radio-controlled drones. All these qualities brought the quadrocopter to the category of the best in the world.

Main characteristics

Phantom 2 models are very similar in many ways, but also have significant differences. Their main characteristics are as follows:

  • The flight time during which the drone can operate on a single battery charge is 25 minutes for all models of the 2nd generation.
  • Operating range - the maximum distance that the aircraft will be removed from the transmitter has been increased from 305 m for DJI Phantom 2 to 610 m for Vision Plus.
  • The maximum speed of the quadrocopter is 15 m / s. At takeoff, it is 6 m / s, and at a decrease - 2 m / s.
  • The weight of the cargo that the drone can carry has been increased from 300 g of the DJI Phantom 2 model of Vision Plus to 300 g.
  • The weight of the model is 1284 g.




Functionality

The quadcopter DJI Phantom 2 has a speed of 54 km / h and remains stable with moderate wind and rain. However, in difficult weather conditions and during sunset, less optimal photos and videos should be expected. On one charge, the drone is able to remain in the air for 25 minutes. - even today it is one of the longest flying quadrocopters.

Vision + is powered by a 5200 mAh lithium-polymer battery and has a working range of 610 m. When the drone leaves the field of view, the screen of iOS and Android devices allows you to see what the onboard camera takes and is easy to control the quadrocopter. When a critical battery level is reached or beyond the operating range, the GPS system will direct the drone back to the launch site, ensuring a safe landing.





Dimensions and weight

The personal quadcopter DJI Phantom 2 is ready to fly right out of the box and does not require assembly: it is enough to charge the battery for 3 hours. The device is quite compact, it can be carried in a backpack by removing the rotor blades. The dimensions of the model are only 35 x 29 x 21 cm.

Piloting

Managing a quadrocopter is a bit more complicated than a toy helicopter. It can definitely be attributed to the highest class of drones. Phantom 2 is aimed at amateurs, professionals and filmmakers. Control over the device requires some practice, and before you go on a flight, you must definitely get acquainted with the operation manual and training videos.

The built-in positioning system allows the quadrocopter to determine its position during the flight, preventing deviations from the course. The drone also makes stabilized images, resulting in more clear frames. The built-in camera captures 14-megapixel pictures and video resolution of 1080 p.





The Phantom 2 Vision + is equipped with a triaxial brushless gimbal that provides shooting stability. When you rotate the drone clockwise, the camera will rotate the same number of degrees in the same direction. This synchronizes it with the maneuvers of the quadrocopter to get an even and clear video. If it is necessary to modify an aircraft for the transport of goods, then it will be able to carry a payload weighing about 300 g.

The quadcopter is paired with an iOS device or Android smartphone or tablet through the application. You can plan a route in advance, and the drone will fly to a given fixed point, linger over it and then go to the next stop. The drone even has a function of returning home, which allows the device to return to the starting point if it loses contact with the control panel or the battery reaches a critical level. Probably, the user will want to avoid using this setting if the launch took place from a boat, because the GPS is a bit wrong, increasing the likelihood of an automatic landing on the water.





Accessories and supplies

DJI Phantom 2 Vision Plus is characterized by a fast preparation time for work and, according to user reviews, after some practice it becomes quite convenient to use. This drone will cost less than self-assembly of a quadrocopter with almost the same characteristics and the purchase of basic accessories such as a transmitter, memory card, GPS-receiver, etc. All this is already included in the device‚Äôs kit. In addition, the user receives a transmitter, a battery for it, additional propellers and a mount for the smartphone. Not to mention that the drone has a built-in camera that provides first-person viewing, incredibly expensive and difficult to install when using a kit from a third-party manufacturer.

Help and support

DJI provides a guarantee on all of its Phantom aircraft, but its duration depends on the specific part of the device and varies from 3 months to a year. But if the drone crashes, then the guarantee will not cover this case. If something goes wrong, and some part of the quadrocopter for some reason turns out to be faulty, the manufacturer will take care of this. It offers users a telephone, email and online chat. In addition, the manual, application, updates and software are available for download from the DJI website.

Conclusion

According to user reviews, the DJI Phantom 2 Vision + is one of the best consumer quadrocopters. It comes with everything you need for a flight. In addition, the model is equipped with a built-in GPS-receiver and a camera that can produce incredibly high-quality aerial photography in the form of individual photos and videos.
