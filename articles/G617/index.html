The best place to collect the beau monde is Soho Rooms (Moscow)

One of the most luxurious establishments in Moscow, Soho Rooms, is located in the building of the old Moscow Silk factory on Savvinskaya Embankment. It is here that stars, politicians, successful businessmen and simply the richest people in the capital of Russia have fun every night.

general information

Soho Rooms is a real complex of five expensive establishments. Its structure includes a bar, restaurant, night club, terrace with pool and summer garden. Inside each Soho Rooms block, sophistication and luxury reign. Only at the entrance here is truly breathtaking.

Entrance to this institution occurs exclusively after passing face control.



DJ bar

DJ bar or, as it is also called, "Bar Room", is located on the very first floor of the entire complex. It is here that it is very convenient to make appointments with friends or colleagues. In general, the bar area is designed for 50 seats, but up to two hundred people can accommodate this establishment.

The view from the huge stained glass windows simply delights with its beauty - the guests of the establishment enjoy views of the Savvinskaya embankment.

A row of chairs stretches across the bar, designed by designers specifically for the Soho Rooms project. The hall also has a large fireplace, which performs not only a decorative function, but also functions well. The ceiling of the bar is decorated with a huge designer chandelier inlaid with real Swarovski crystals.

The bar is equipped with the best sound and lighting equipment and a DJ remote control, because it is in this area that the best Moscow DJ parties with the participation of famous DJs are held.



Restaurant "Soho Rooms"

The restaurant is a two-tiered establishment located on the 2nd and 3rd floors of Soho Rooms. It is within its walls that the most luxurious dinners and receptions are held, stars, politicians and businessmen celebrate their holidays here.



The restaurant consists of two luxurious halls, which in total can accommodate up to 150 people (just as many seats are provided here). Guests can sit on large leather sofas, at tables made of expensive wood. One of the restaurant's halls is furnished with expensive antique furniture and also has a fireplace. Another highlight of the restaurant is a huge winery with a collection of the most expensive wines collected from around the world.

As for the second room, it is an old library, since in addition to places for the comfortable location of guests there is a huge old chest of drawers with books. The hall has a large plasma screen through which sports and music programs are broadcast.



As for the cuisine, the menu available in this institution can offer guests dishes of Russian, Mediterranean, Eastern, European cuisine. Here you can choose from snack sets for large companies, light salads, gourmet soups and nutritious main dishes.



Terrace with pool

This Soho Rooms area is located on the very roof of the establishment and operates at any time of the year. It is here, in the open air, that the pool is located, which, with the onset of cold weather, becomes indoor. While on this roof you may feel as if you were in some luxurious villa.

On the terrace there is a chic large bar constructed of black onyx. Armchairs and sofas are set on the terrace. There are lively exotic flowers everywhere .



On such a terrace, Muscovites and guests of the capital have the opportunity to bask in the sun during the day and have fun at the disco at night.

Night Club Soho Rooms

This area is often called the "Disco Room". It should be noted that the "Disco Room" at night is one of the busiest places of Soho Rooms. It is here that Moscow collects the entire elite for a noisy and good holiday.

The "Disco Ruma" square allows you to place on its territory up to five hundred people, there are also 80 seats. This zone works only on Friday and Saturday (from midnight to the last guest).

At the nightclub, everything is equipped with the latest sound and lighting technology. It is here that a unique transformer scene is set up, designed specifically for the Soho Rooms project - photos and reviews about this scene can well convey all its capabilities. Vacationing guests and performing stars often share the convenience of its remarkable ability to lower and raise the curtain, rotate, change the scenery.



Summer garden

The summer garden is perhaps the most vibrant and green area of Soho Rooms. It is here, in the very center of Moscow, spreading a vibrant tropical garden with unprecedented plants and flowers. Being in this part of the club, you feel yourself somewhere in India or Sri Lanka. It also has a large bar and seating. Reviews and pricing

There are a lot of enthusiastic reviews about such an institution in social networks and on the Internet. Guests are simply delighted with the successful organization of each zone and the thoughtfulness of the idea in the complex. Naturally, in such an institution, the service is top-notch - guests also often talk about this. The spoiled visitors are captivated by the taste of cooked dishes and the atmosphere prevailing in any of the zones of the complex.





As for the prices, they are very high here. On average, the bill for one vacationer is about 6-7 thousand rubles, but for this cost a lot of incredible emotions are provided that will remain in your memory for a long time.

Address Soho Rooms: Moscow, 12 Savvinskaya Embankment, Building 8 (the nearest metro stations are Frunzenskaya, Kievskaya and Sportivnaya).
