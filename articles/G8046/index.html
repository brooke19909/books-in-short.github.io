Strandja 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): hotel description and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria today is traditionally one of the most popular countries among tourists from Russia. The best seaside resort of this state is Sunny Beach. There is a wide hotel base, excellent beaches and a great climate. If you, as well as thousands of other travelers, plan to spend your vacation in this place, then as an option for accommodation, be sure to consider the Strandja hotel complex (Strandja) 4 *. Today we will introduce you more closely to this hotel, as well as summarize the comments of our compatriots who have already stayed here.





Location

The hotel in question is located in the northern part of Sunny Beach. It is only one and a half kilometers from the center. The place here is quite calm and great for families. The nearest international airport is in the city of Burgas, from which the Strandja 4 * hotel is only thirty kilometers away. Thus, after landing in the air harbor, your journey to the hotel will take about half an hour. Especially this fact will appeal to those tourists who plan to take young children with them on a trip to Bulgaria.

Another important factor for travelers who want to relax on the seashore is the proximity of a hotel in question to the beach. As for the four-star Strandzha hotel complex (Sunny Beach), the distance from it to the nearest beach does not exceed one hundred and fifty meters. Along the way, you will need to cross the road and the promenade that runs along the coast. The beach here is municipal and equipped with everything necessary for an interesting and varied vacation.





Hotel Description

The hotel complex under consideration offers everyone to stay in one of 270 comfortable rooms. They are located in multi-storey buildings equipped with a high-speed elevator. For the first time, the four-star Strandzha Hotel (Bulgaria) opened its doors to travelers back in 1968. The last restoration work was carried out here in 2008. Today it is a modern hotel complex, which has comfortable rooms and all the necessary infrastructure for an excellent stay and relaxation.

In addition to residential buildings, there are restaurants, bars, swimming pools, sports grounds, a SPA center, a beauty salon and much more. This hotel complex is ideal for families on the coast. However, young people will not be bored here.

Hotel rules Strandja 4 * (Sunny Beach)

Along with most other hotels, Strandzha has a set checkout time, which determines the time of registration of arrival and departure of guests. So, in accordance with these rules, the arrival of tourists arriving after 14:30. If you arrive at the hotel before the specified time, then if you have free rooms, they will try to check in as soon as possible. At the height of the season, you probably have to wait until the previous guests have vacated the room, since at this time the hotel complex is almost 100% full. On the day of departure, leave your room before noon. In this case, you need to hand over the room keys to the administrator, as well as pay for the entire stay and use additional services. You can pay here either with cash or using a Visa, Diners Club or MasterCard credit card. By the way, if you pre-paid your stay in Strandja 4 * (Sunny Beach) for a tour operator, then at check out you will only have to pay for the use of additional services, if any.





Guests with children

The hotel in question is mainly aimed at family vacationers, therefore, travelers who are on holidays with children of various age groups are always welcome here. If you come with a baby up to two years old, then for a fee of 2.5 euros per night, he can be provided with a playpen, which will be installed in the room. Please note that the hotel in question does not have the possibility of extra beds for older children, as well as for adults. When booking a room, this fact must be taken into account.

Pets welcome

As you know, some travelers prefer to take their four-legged pets with them on all trips. Fortunately for them, the Strandja 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) does not prohibit guests with pets. However, you must inform the property in advance that you plan to arrive on vacation with your pet. Also keep in mind that this service may be charged.

Nutrition

The gastronomic infrastructure of the hotel in question is represented by the main restaurant and a la carte restaurant. In the first of them , chefs serve a buffet during the day , and in the second, everyone can order a varied menu. As for the catering system in the Strandzha hotel complex, it is organized according to the all-inclusive scheme. In addition to restaurants, the hotel has bars with a wide selection of drinks.





Beach and sea

Club Hotel Strandja 4 * complex is located on the second coastline. The hotel does not have its own beach. The nearest beach is municipal and is 150 meters away. On the way to it you need to cross the roadway and promenade. In general, it should be noted that all the beaches of Sunny Beach are very clean (this confirms that they have a quality mark in the form of a blue flag) and are equipped with everything necessary. So, here you will find sun loungers, umbrellas, as well as a variety of equipment for sports and recreation on the water. Entrance to the beach is free, but a certain amount of money will have to be paid for using the equipment.

Entertainment

The four-star Strandzha Hotel (Bulgaria) has on its territory a large open-air pool with a sun terrace and a bar. In addition, it features an indoor pool, as well as a "paddling pool" for the youngest tourists.

The hotel complex has a mini-golf course, tennis courts, as well as equipment for ping pong, billiards and darts. On the beach, you can use water equipment for an extra charge.

Those wishing to relax can visit the spa and beauty center. During the day and in the evenings, the hotel has animators, and in the evenings there are fun discos.





Infrastructure

For the convenience of guests, Strandja 4 * reception is open 24 hours a day. Therefore, at any time, you can contact the administrator for an urgent matter. They also change currency, leave things for storage in a safe, receive a password for wireless Internet access, book tours and rent a car. There is ample parking on site. So if you plan to rent a car upon arrival in Bulgaria, there will be no problems with parking.

The Strandzha hotel complex has a roomy conference room. It is equipped with everything necessary for a variety of business meetings and other events.

Accommodation cost

According to many tourists, the price of rooms in Strandja 4 * (Bulgaria) is more than acceptable for accommodation in a four-star hotel. So, a seven-day accommodation in the midst of the tourist season (July-August) in a double room will cost you in the amount of thirty thousand rubles. Living at another time will cost slightly less.
