Need a car loan? Gazprombank will be happy to help

Today Gazprombank is one of the three largest banking organizations of the Russian Federation for the provision of investment and financial services for private and corporate clients. At the moment, its client base includes more than 3 million individuals and about 45 thousand legal entities. If you need to take a car loan when buying a new car, Gazprombank can provide such a service on very favorable terms. This loan can be issued both in Russian currency and in foreign. Another advantage is the absence of restrictions on the maximum possible loan amount, since when calculating it, the organization takes into account the total earnings of all family members.



In order to get a car loan, Gazprombank requires a certain set of documents, namely:

  1. Application form for receiving funds.
  2. The borrower's passport with compulsory registration not far from one of the bank branches.
  3. Driver's license.
  4. Certificate of income for all family members for the last 6 months.

In order to obtain a car loan, Gazprombank may request additional papers confirming the formation of the borrower and the existence of property that can be used as a collateral. In addition, one of the prerequisites for obtaining is compulsory insurance of the purchased car at its full cost in the company indicated by the banking organization.

Gazprombank. Car loan: terms of registration



A financial institution does not charge any additional fees for opening and maintaining a credit account. The borrower is required to pay a one-time fee in the amount of 3,100 rubles and repay the loan in equal amounts each calendar month. The special loan calculator of Gazprombank can help calculate the required amount of payments. Car loans are part of standard loans. Therefore, its calculation will not be much of a problem.

Standard program

This loan is issued for the purchase of a new car for a period of 3.5 to 5 years, depending on the country of manufacture of the car. Gazprombank provides an opportunity to get such a car loan without a down payment. The loan is provided for a period of seven years at interest rates of the long-term program.
