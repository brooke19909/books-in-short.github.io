The best clubs of Tomsk

Tomsk is a small town located in the same region. Here is not such a high standard of living as in the capital, St. Petersburg or Yekaterinburg. But as far as entertainment complexes are concerned, here they are of the highest level! Especially in the city there is a nightlife for active youth. There are especially popular clubs in Tomsk, which are always crowded with visitors!

"Metro"





Looking at various clubs in Tomsk, it is impossible not to pay attention to an entertainment institution called the Metro. This is the most popular place in the city, where visitors of various age categories gather. What attracts them so much to this place? First of all, the optimal prices for alcoholic beverages, banquets and table rental. Also here there are minimum prices for a pass ticket. Sometimes you can visit the institution for free, it is important to carefully monitor the advertisement!

The club has been successfully operating since the end of 2001. During this time there was not a single day when there were few people in it. It is always fun here, the most popular tunes are played and the most fiery presenters perform. Recently, the reputation of the entertainment complex has declined markedly. The reason for this was that the main visitors were young teens (which is prohibited), alcoholics and rowdy people. Currently, the fate of the club is a big question.
