Location

Smartline White City Beach Hotel 4 * is located near the village of Konakli, which also accepts tourists on its territory, as well as seven kilometers from the central part of Alanya, where nightlife is raging. Rooms

The fund of the hotel is represented by comfortable apartments of the "standard" category, which are conditionally divided into several subgroups, depending on additional conditions. In particular, tourists can choose for themselves a room with a large area or one that has a personal terrace for relaxation. In total, the Smartline White City Beach Hotel 4 * (Alanya) has 113 rooms ready to move in at any time.

In all apartments, regardless of their level of comfort, there is a private bathroom, which is fully equipped with everything necessary for living: there is a bathtub, which in some rooms is replaced by a shower, washbasin, and a bathroom. All bath accessories that the hotel offers for use by guests are in a separate cupboard, here you can find bathrobes, slippers and towels.

The rooms also have individual air conditioners - with their help, guests can adjust the optimal temperature regime in their room.

Room Interior

As tourists note in their reviews about the Smartline White City Beach Hotel 4 *, the interior of its rooms is very beautiful, it is able to attract the attention of guests. They often say that in this setting you can easily relax and truly enjoy your vacation. The hotel guests pay great attention to the beautiful view that opens from the large windows of the rooms - from here you can observe the endless expanses of the sea, as well as tropical plantings.

The general color scheme of the interior is represented by a successful combination of dark blue, white and black colors, there are a lot of geometric details. As a place to sleep, there are large double beds, the rooms also have an extra bed, represented by a single bed. There is an excellent working area in the form of a large white desk and a black soft chair. Guests can relax sitting at a small round white table on a soft armchair - in this position you can read your favorite book.

The room has a large plasma TV that broadcasts satellite channels, many of which are Russian-speaking. Guests also recommend, if there are valuables in the suitcase, to transfer them to an electronic safe, which can be rented for several days. The room has a landline telephone that can be used inside the hotel - for international calls you have to pay separately. If desired, a minibar can be installed here, the content of which will be updated regularly.



Conditions for children

In the rooms of Standard Smartline White City Beach Hotel 4 *, if necessary, a cot can be installed on which the baby will sleep comfortably. Parents note with positive comments the possibility of renting strollers here, on which it is more convenient to transport the child.
