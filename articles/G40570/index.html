Review of popular Shivaki TVs

Every day, companies, models, and modifications appear in the world of electronics. Everyone is trying to gain the trust of customers and their location. Accordingly, the success of a product is measured by its popularity. Shivaki TVs are no exception. For the first time such a technique appeared on the shelves of Russian stores in 1999. Its origins go far beyond the borders of the Federation - to Japan. This is understandable in name. At the moment, the factories that produce this equipment are located in Hong Kong, South Korea, France and other countries.





What are the distinguishing characteristics of these televisions? They consist in the fact that this company produces high-quality equipment that can be attributed to the premium class. Quality and low cost speak for themselves. The manufacturer often claims that excellent TVs should not be unattainable for ordinary buyers, so the price is low.

The production strategy was so successful that people around the world started talking about this company and its technology. In this article, we will consider the rating of the best models that were at the peak of popularity in 2016-2017. You can buy them at any specialized store. On the forums they write great reviews about them.

"Shivaki LED 14" (32 inches)

This model has long been in demand and is considered one of the best. This 32 "Shivaki" TV will delight any customer. The image is as sharp and sharp as possible. The sound is beautiful. The display has a resolution of HD. The refresh rate is 50 Hz. Color reproduction is as natural as possible, so any buyer will like it. Thanks to the backlight, this model will be convenient for installation in any stylistically designed rooms. Moreover, the device is small in size. The TV is budget. However, the quality of its image is quite acceptable.





The main advantage of the model is that it has excellent multimedia options. The panel plays content from the internal tuner, as well as from external drives and media players.

"Shivaki LED 14" (24 inches)

This TV was distinguished not only by its cheapness, but also by the quality, which is usually inherent in products of this category. The screen has a resolution of HD.
