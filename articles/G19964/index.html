Crochet a plaid from motifs. General recommendations

Any plaid knitted in a technique involving a combination of motives is performed in compliance with general rules. Here is some of them.

The simplest option, suitable for crocheting plaids from motifs, is the manufacture of square-shaped elements.



Assembled into a single whole fabric is usually tied around the entire perimeter. Types of tying can be different - from openwork, made with a wide strip, to dense and narrow, for example, using the "step step" technique. Crocheted blankets of motifs knitted from the remains of yarn, pleasing with their beauty and brightness, although they resemble a "rustic" style. But if you choose other shades and in a small amount, a knitted blanket will look very stylish.

The connection of motives

Crocheted blanket of square motifs will be of high quality, provided that all elements are skillfully fastened together. To combine motifs into a single canvas, there are several ways. Consider a few of them. One of the most difficult is the option of joining during knitting motifs. Knocking out the last row of one motive, you need to perform its fastening to another. For beginner needlewomen, this is a rather complicated way. In addition, it requires special calculation and use of the connection diagram. And it‚Äôs not suitable for any form of motive. Therefore, we turn to the consideration of simpler methods.

One of them is needle connection. For this, the motifs are folded with their faces to each other and sewn with the same thread that was used to knit them. Please note that the seam should be very neat and as invisible as possible. Since, unlike clothes made of motifs, a plaid is a two-way product.

Crochet Method

Another way is to use a hook. As in the previous method, the motives are added face in relation to each other. Next, using a hook and yarn, they are secured with connecting posts. These columns resemble single crochet, but differ in that, having dragged the thread through the edges of the motifs, it is immediately pulled through the existing one on the hook, without capturing the working thread. Such a seam will be neat and will not be evident.

And finally, the last way. You will also need a hook to use it. The connection of motives occurs by knitting the net between them. It is done like this. A thread is attached to the edge of one of the motifs, and two air loops are knitted, then they are attached to another motif using a single crochet. Then again two air loops and joining the first motive. As a result of such bonding, an openwork mesh is obtained. The size of the cells is controlled by the distance between the mounts. Such a connection, made with a contrasting thread, looks very beautiful.

Baby blankets. Stylish ideas



Knitting a children's plaid is practically no different from those listed above. But what is the difference between a blanket for a baby and an adult option? Of course, the way of decoration. Any baby will like a plaid, decorated, for example, with the faces of animals. In the photo above, these are cubs. This children's plaid is made in the same technique as adults. And the applique is made of crocheted elements. It is not difficult to do this, since it is quite simple to connect the circles with columns with a crochet, this will be the muzzle. And the nose, eyes and mouth can simply be embroidered with a needle.

Original children's motif plaid

Not many people decide to crochet a baby blanket , since they believe that it will be tougher than that made with knitting needles. But some options are striking in their beauty. And you can get rid of stiffness using thinner threads and a hook, the size of which is slightly larger than necessary for the selected yarn. Here is an example of such a plaid designed for little princesses. Even novice craftswomen will be able to cope with its implementation. The main thing when knitting this blanket is the selection of yarn. With a harmonious combination of colors, your plaid will look in a special way, like a real veil in the royal chambers.



Knitting of this masterpiece begins with motifs made with crochets. What form they will be up to you. You can make them square or rectangular, large or small. The highlight of this model in color combination and exquisite shuttlecocks along the perimeter of each motive.

So, having connected the necessary number of motifs in two shades, you can start assembling the plaid. To do this, use one of the connection methods above. This is either a connection with a needle or using a hook.

After the plaid has acquired the shape you need, we proceed to decorate it. To do this, we knit the single crochet around the perimeter of each element in the same color as the motive. In order to get a kind of flounce, you need to knit several columns in one loop. The more they will be located in one place, the more magnificent the shuttle will be.
