Hotel Hilton Al Hamra Beach & Golf Resort 5 * (United Arab Emirates / Ras Al Khaimah): photos and reviews of tourists

Over the past few years, the United Arab Emirates has become one of the most popular countries for tourists. Naturally, travelers are offered a huge number of hotels of various categories. One of them, and quite popular, is Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 *, which is located in the cozy resort town of Ras al-Khaimah. Of course, before the trip, people try to find out as much information as possible about a particular place. So what is this hotel like?

Location Description





Of course, first of all, tourists are interested in the question of exactly where the hotel complex Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 * is located. Ras al Khaimah is a picturesque resort village located right on the shores of the Persian Gulf. It offers a breathtaking view not only of the sea, but also of the Hajar Mountains. The nearest international airport is 25 km away. But the distance to Dubai Airport is about 78 km. In about an hour's drive, you can reach the city center of Dubai with all its attractions and famous entertainment complexes.

What does the territory of the complex look like?

Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort occupies a fairly large area, which faces directly to the seashore. Here is a two-story main building, as well as several villas and bungalows with living rooms. All buildings are built in an interesting oriental style that creates a fabulous atmosphere. The entire territory of the hotel is well-groomed - there is a garden that pleases the eye with greenery almost at any time of the year, as well as many flower beds. There are flat, neat lawns, playgrounds and terraces for relaxation, a large golf course. By the way, the last major overhaul was carried out in 2014.

Hotel Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 *: photo and description of rooms





The hotel complex is considered relatively large - on its territory there are 266 rooms. These are mostly standard rooms, but there are also deluxe rooms. By the way, tourists vacationing in a large company can rent a whole villa right away. There are rooms for allergy sufferers, as well as rooms with special equipment for the disabled. Depending on the location, the balcony or terrace offers views of the lagoon, garden or mountains.

Regardless of the category, the rooms here are bright, comfortable and kept in perfect cleanliness. Of course, there is a set of necessary furniture and household appliances. In particular, in the room you will find a TV connected to a satellite dish, a DVD player, air conditioning with an individual remote control, a small safe and a direct dial telephone. Internet access is available, but this service is charged separately. The minibar is filled with water for free, all other drinks are not included in the price.

The spacious bathroom is equipped with a shower, spacious bathtub, washbasin. There is a mirror and a bidet. Guests receive a set of towels, a set of hygiene products, as well as slippers and a bathrobe. A hair dryer is also present.

What kind of food does the hotel offer?





When paying for a room at the Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 * hotel , customers can choose a meal plan, whether it is only breakfast, half board or all-inclusive. By the way, tourists recommend paying for the full three meals a day, as there are no other good catering establishments near the hotel - you will have to travel to the city.

It offers international, Italian and Lebanese cuisine. The choice is huge. The menu includes dishes from different varieties of meat and fish, vegetable salads, side dishes, fresh fruit and, of course, sweet pastries, delicious desserts and homemade ice cream. The hotel has several bars, as well as a cafe where you can order coffee and light snacks.

Beach and water activities





As already mentioned, the hotel complex Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 * is located near the seashore. The hotel‚Äôs own private beach is located 150 meters from the main building. There are plenty of sun loungers with soft mattresses, as well as sun umbrellas. There is also a bar, there are several changing rooms. The beach, by the way, is covered with soft sand.

In the lagoon, the water is almost always calm, there is a backwater where children can swim. The underwater world is quite interesting, so tourists love to swim with masks. In addition, you will have the opportunity to ride a boat, water ski, boat, canoe, go windsurfing, try sailing or even take a trip along the coast.

Can I take a child with me?





It's no secret that more and more modern tourists prefer to travel with their children. In such cases, comfort and relevant conditions come first. What can the hotel offer Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 *? Reviews suggest that you can have a great time with the children on site.

For starters, perhaps it's worth talking about amenities. Some rooms have folding sofas, while others have a pull-out bed at the request of their parents. In the main restaurant, you will definitely be offered a chair for feeding, and the choice of food is quite large, so it will be easy for a child to make a menu.

With entertainment on site, everything is also in order. A special pool is equipped for children, the water in which is constantly heated. There is also an outdoor playground with swings and other attractions - here children can have fun with their parents. And a mini club is constantly working, where children are entertained by animators. The hotel has several professional nannies who are not afraid to leave a child for a while - their services must be paid separately.

Are additional services included?





Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 * hotel offers its guests a number of additional services. In particular, you can use the laundry, dry cleaning, ironing, as well as rent shoes for professional cleaning. There is a luggage room for unnecessary luggage, you can rent an administrator safe. Here, guests can exchange currency, rent a car, book tickets for a train or plane, etc.

The hotel has a superbly designed business center where guests can use computers, printers, fax, video and audio equipment for meetings. There is also a large conference room. Hotel staff, incidentally, are involved in organizing various events. Also on the territory there is a newsstand, souvenir shop and a shop where you can purchase various necessary trifles.

How do guests spend their free time? Leisure and entertainment





Of course, the hotel has the opportunity to have fun. In the courtyard there is a swimming pool with sun loungers and umbrellas around - here you can swim, sunbathe and just chat calmly. There is a volleyball and mini-golf course, as well as several tennis courts. Guests are also invited to play golf on an 18 hole golf course. By the way, here you can rent clubs and even take a few lessons from professionals.

You should definitely visit the spa center, where you can steam up in the bath, relax in the jacuzzi, sign up for a massage. Here, guests are offered a huge selection of cosmetic procedures for face and body. Women will certainly enjoy the presence of a hairdresser and beauty salon, where extremely talented stylists work.

Guests can use the gym at any time or sign up for group sports. There are even special jogging tracks.

In the evenings, live music is played on site, sometimes dances, fun contests and performances are held.

And do not forget about excursions, as there are many interesting places in the vicinity, including the Haat district, which is famous for its healing mineral waters, as well as the ancient mosque of Jazirat al-Hamra. Of course, you can go to Dubai, take a walk along the beach, do some shopping in one of the shopping centers and have fun at any of the night clubs. On the territory of the city, as well as the hotel itself, you will be offered a huge number of tours for every taste.

Hotel Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 *: guest reviews

What do travelers say about this place? Reviews about the hotel Hilton al Hamra Beach Golf Resort 5 * are positive, since everything here is up to standard - living conditions, food, service. The territory is very beautiful, as well as the seashore and the view and rooms. The rooms are clean and tidy, household appliances work. For tourists who stay here for a long time, the kitchen may seem somewhat monotonous, but the dishes are always fresh and served in sufficient quantities (some of them even cook with you). Disco parties from noisy parties at the hotel are not held, so fans of nightlife here may seem bored. But for a relaxing, family holiday by the sea, this hotel is the best suited.
