How to call Belarus as a neighbor

There have always been close relations between Russia and Belarus. And not only because we are neighbors, although this is also very important. Our countries are inhabited by Slavic peoples-brothers. Russians and Belarusians have common historical roots. In addition, for a long time we were one country. And as a result, the number of Russians living in Belarus makes up more than 8% of the total population of the republic.

It so happened that in a neighboring country, many live, if not relatives, then friends. And for many, knowing how to call Belarus is a must. Moreover, the close neighborhood promotes close ties between citizens of fraternal countries.



How to call Belarus

A phone call to a neighboring country can be made from both mobile and landline phones. But, not knowing how to call Belarus correctly, you can dial the number for hours and not reach the desired subscriber.

First you need to dial the number 8 and wait for the end of the first beep. Then we dial 10 - this is a well-known number in order to get the opportunity to access long-distance calls. The following is a set of code for Belarus, this is a three-digit number 375. It should be found in advance before calling to Belarus.



And before the last step, it remains for you to enter the area code where you want to call. If you need to call Minsk, the capital of Belarus , dial code 17. Other settlements in this country have their own codes, which can be found on many Internet sites.

After you have marked the area code, you should dial the subscriber number. It must be remembered that in all major cities of Belarus, telephone numbers are seven-digit, and in small towns and villages they are usually six- or five-digit.

We studied how to call Belarus from a landline phone, now we will consider how to do this from a mobile device. At the beginning, instead of the number 8, you must type the + sign. This symbol is used by all mobile operators to replace the international access code. The next step is to enter the called number.

Determining how much a call from a mobile phone to a mobile phone will cost you is quite difficult, since the rates are constantly changing and each operator has their own. The average price is about $ 3 per 1 minute of conversation. If someone calls from Belarus to your phone, then such a call will be free for you.

Before calling Belarus from a mobile phone, Beeline subscribers should make sure that they have at least $ 50 in their account, otherwise roaming will simply not be activated by the mobile operator. But the MTS company includes roaming automatically.



If you want to know how to make cheap calls to Belarus, we are reporting that you need to use the Internet services for this. Some social networks, such as Odnoklassniki, allow you to make calls around the world. And the conversation from computer to computer is carried out without charge.

Those who often visit the Republic of Belarus are advised to call Russia using special cards from pay phones. For calls from a mobile phone in Belarus it is advantageous to purchase SIM cards of MTS and Velcom operators.
