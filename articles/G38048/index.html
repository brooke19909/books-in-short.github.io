Where to go at the resort: "Tropic Park" in Yevpatoriya

Tropic Park in Yevpatoriya is one of the favorite places in the city among vacationers and city dwellers. This is an unusual and original zoo, albeit not the largest, but allowing adults and children to contact and communicate with all kinds of animals and birds. The impression of the resort city will be incomplete if you do not visit the Tropic Park - the pride of Yevpatoriya.

Nice features

Named place of rest interestingly designed, so it is easy to find. From far away in the Children's amusement park you can see the dragon head - this is the entrance to the zoo in Yevpatoriya. To step inside, some visitors need some courage.





But inside, bird voices sound, a small waterfall rustles, and the grotto leads deep into the mysterious cave. Tropic Park occupies 3 floors, but you need to carefully look under your feet: on the 1st floor terrariums are built into the floor, insect dwellings are built into the walls, and on the 3rd floor a rope bridge leads to bird aviaries above the open crocodile pool. All enclosures are designed so that you can imagine the natural living conditions of animals.

In the "Tropic Park" in Yevpatoriya you can take pictures and shoot for free. And in order to establish friendly communication with animals, you should buy a glass of delicious and healthy goodies. It costs only 50 p.

For an additional fee you can buy treats for snakes and crocodiles, as well as marmoset monkeys.

Inhabitants of the Tropic Park

About 150 species of animals, birds and reptiles live here. Most of the inhabitants are either in the zoo from the day it was founded, or were born and raised right there. Regular visitors already well know long-livers in person and bring them gifts - toys.





In total, the zoo has about 10 species of primates, over 30 species of birds, more than 150 species of amphibians and reptiles, as well as numerous representatives of insects.

Mammals in the Tropic Park

Many different mammals inhabit the contact zoo. The favorites of the public are a pair of raccoons. Such neat animals still look: they carefully wash all the goodies that they offer.

Charming noses stretch their paws trustingly in the hope of a treat.

And in the Tropic Park of Yevpatoriya there are a lot of monkeys who have stairs, swings and beds in open-air cages. But you have to be careful: many of them have the habit of quickly snatching not only treats from the hands of visitors, but also hats, glasses, bows and hiding them.

Near the cell where a couple of lemurs live, there are always a lot of people who want to be photographed.

Among the inhabitants of the Tropic Park of Yevpatoriya there is a large collection of rodents (various mice, Degu squirrels, chinchillas, hamsters, rats) and rabbits. Cute dwarf horses prefer to treat themselves to carrots.

On the 2nd floor there are rare species of primates: oedipal and red-armed tamarines, white-eared marmosets, squirrel monkeys. These tiny creatures are only about 10 cm tall and at the same time are very curious and sociable. In addition, this is a curiosity, and not in any metropolitan zoo you can see the smallest monkeys in the world.

Evpatoria Zoo: snakes and fish

Terrariums are the pride of the Tropic Park of Yevpatoriya. Visitors' reviews about them are always laudatory, because in each aviary there is a piece of real jungle, desert or tropical forest with a waterfall.





In the terrariums are not only snakes, but also crocodiles, turtles, lizards, frogs. Here old-timers will be happy to show you:

  • reticulated python, more than 7 m long;
  • brindle python, whose age is over 30 years old;
  • a huge anaconda, for which a spacious enclosure was built, and before she lived in the bath;
  • Crocodile Vasya - one of the favorites of the zoo workers;
  • land and sea turtles.

Among the lizards there are iguanas, basilisks, skinks, monitor lizards, scales, and others.

The rich world of insects and fish

The zoo in Yevpatoriya fully provides an opportunity to imagine how diverse and rich the nature of our planet is! Brightly colored spiders of the tropical jungle, Madagascar cockroaches and other insects are of genuine interest not only in children but also in adults.

And among the fish you can find an aquarium with a rainbow crab.

Birds in the Evpatoria Zoo

On the 1st floor in "Tropic Park" Evpatoria are colorful macaw parrots. Their cries of "ara-ara" drown out even the conversations. The parrot and the cockatoos delicately take treats in the clutches, talking with visitors in their own language.





On the 3rd floor there are many birds. Among them are owls, doves, Amadinas, various parrots, as well as a curiosity - a rhinoceros bird and a Turko - African cuckoo.

How to find Tropic Park?

Finding a Tropic Park contact zoo is easy. It is located in the city center in the territory of the Children's Park, 200 meters from the main resort promenade Frunze Street.

Exact address: st. Tokareva, 43. We must go through the Children's Amusement Park and go out to the street enclosures, where there are foxes, birds of prey, poultry, rabbits and nutria. To the left of the enclosures is Tropic Park.

Getting on public transport, you need to get off at the Polyclinic or Nekrasova Street stops.

The zoo is open seven days a week, all year round, from 10:00 to 21:00.
