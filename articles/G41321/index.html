The forests and mountains of Halkidiki make it an ideal place for walking and hiking, and its beaches have a good selection of water activities, sailing and diving. Many operators offer car rentals and explore the surroundings of the resorts to visit traditional villages and authentic taverns.

Mount Athos, on the easternmost peninsula, has the largest collection of monasteries in Greece, some of which date back to Byzantine times. Although tourists cannot visit the monasteries, they can be seen in the distance from sightseeing boats that run daily from Ouranoupolis.

In Siviri there is an amphitheater where summer concerts are held, and many archaeological sites, such as Stagira, the birthplace of Aristotle. For those who want a taste of the islands, some resorts offer boat trips to nearby options such as Skiathos.

No. 2. Sithonia is a peninsula on the peninsula. Many consider Sithonia the most picturesque place. And it's hard to argue with that. Numerous beaches are dotted along its coastline.

No. 3. Mount Athos. Tourist information about Halkidiki has a lot of information about Mount Athos. Not only does this refer to the actual mountain, which is the highest point of the peninsula, but it is also the name of the northern peninsula of Halkidiki. It is also next to Uranupoli, where the Eagles Palace resort is located.

This wonderful peninsula, in fact, is a large living religious site, including 20 vast monasteries. These buildings offer a place of contemplation and solitude for the numerous Christian Orthodox monks who live and pray within their walls. The area is considered a monastic republic, and the peninsula can control the movement of people and goods within its jurisdiction.

Best travel time to Halkidiki

It all depends on what kind of vacation is preferred. If tourists do not want to see large crowds on the beaches and in restaurants, then September, closer to the end of the tourist season, will be the best time to visit. Those who want more excitement, party atmosphere and higher temperatures, judging by the reviews of tourists about holidays in Halkidiki in Greece, July and August is the best time to travel. It should be borne in mind that many hotels and restaurants close at the end of the tourist season. Exact dates vary by location. You must definitely check this in advance.
