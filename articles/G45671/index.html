Sony Xperia E1 D2005: review, specifications, reviews

Not so long ago, Sony was presented to us by several smartphones from the initial and middle price groups. Including the inexpensive Sony Xperia E1 D2005, which has become an updated version of the budget device and is fully focused on music playback. Such a gadget will appeal to those who are a true music lover, although you have to expect maximum performance from it. At the top of the case there is a headphone jack, as well as a quick start button for the media player.

General information and appearance

The smartphone of the Japanese company Sony Xperia E1 D2005 continues the tradition of Sony and has a shape that is easily recognizable by users. The appearance is endowed with some severity, but it is softened by rounded corners, which gives the appearance of the device some dynamism. The display is a four-inch one with a resolution of 800x480 pixels, installed in this stylish case.

The image on this screen is characterized by wide viewing angles and phenomenal colorfulness. The size of the device is compact, which is very important for lovers of mobility, as it easily fits in your trouser pocket. Presented Sony Xperia E1 D2005 in three colors: purple, white and black. Its cost is from 5500 rubles. By the way, this design is called OmniBalance, and it is made at the highest level.

Smartphone Overview

First of all, of course, it should be noted a dual-core processor with a frequency of 1.2 GHz, which pretty well provides the speed of the music gadget and maximum performance, while the battery is not depleted. So, here is the Sony Xperia E1 D2005. We continue the review with information that the processor used is asynchronous, its kernels are switched off and started independently of each other.

That is, only the power that is currently required is used, and the battery is not consumed unnecessarily. This is due to the STAMINA mode, which recognizes which functions you are not using and turns them off. All important notifications are saved at the same time. As soon as the smartphone enters the active mode, the functions are restored.

Continuation of the review

RAM Sony Xperia E1 D2005 is not enough for the current time, only 512 megabytes. This is a serious flaw, despite the small price of the device. But what is - that is, there is nothing to be done. The internal memory is also "funny" - 4 GB, but at least there is an exit - the gadget supports memory cards in standard microSD format, so there is a place to store your favorite songs. By the way, a fully charged battery will allow you to listen to music for at least 81 and a half hours. The operating system is installed by default - Android 4.3.

Already bought a sufficient number of Japanese gadgets Sony Xperia E1 D2005. Reviews about them are very diverse, mostly, of course, positive, but there are also negative ones. They are associated with the already indicated small amount of RAM. Also, users are not very happy with just the three-megapixel camera and not very good, respectively, the quality of the photos.
