Kapetanios Odyssia 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol): hotel description, services, reviews

The small - sized hotel Limassol Kapetanios Odyssia 3 * is located on the coast of Limassol and is surrounded on almost all sides by exotic plants and fancy flowers. At a distance of just 100 meters from the hotel there is an ennobled beach, and several bars, clubs, restaurants and other infrastructure are built a few steps away.

What is near the hotel

In addition, there is a cluster of various architectural monuments and attractions nearby, which have long been loved by many visitors to tourists and even local residents of the island. The oldest temples of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker and St. Thekla enjoy special authority. These magnificent prayer places still keep within their walls the centuries-old history and energy of people who have been inside the building for all this time.

The monastery of the Black Holy Cross invites you to visit excursions and see the numerous exhibits. There is a museum of ancient photography, and a gallery from the time of Byzantium, and other museums that store many cultural values.

Advantageous location of the hotel

Hotel Kapetanios Odyssia 3 * is located very close to the picturesque park. At least every day guests can enjoy walks and local entertainment. The city park of Limassol is ready to offer everyone, especially young guests, a lot of active entertainment, cafes, restaurants, attractions and more. The audience here is predominantly family-friendly, which allows children to spend time engaging in joint games in the fresh air.

Among other things, this park is completely planted with amazing exotic plants, flowers and shrubs, and beautiful peacocks with chic feathers on their tails walk freely in the vast territory.

High fountains in the park are constantly illuminated, which causes a feeling of delight for any passerby. In addition to peacocks, there is another living creature in the park, for example, beautiful fishes of different breeds live in the reservoirs, which can be watched for hours on end.

For very young visitors to the park there is a playground in the fresh air, which can be used for an unlimited amount of time and, even more joyful, completely free of charge. A miniature zoo invites young holidaymakers to look at zebras, mouflons and cheetahs.

Adult travelers who come to rest at this place regularly have an excellent opportunity to visit the famous September wine festival with tasting of plant products and a fun party after.





Accommodation

The room fund of Kapetanios Odyssia 3 * hotel (Limassol) is presented in several categories, such as: standard rooms, business suites, superior rooms and exclusive rooms. Each room, regardless of class, has a standard set of options (bathroom, wardrobe, desk, set of hygiene items, TV, telephone, refrigerator, all necessary furniture and Internet access).

Nutrition





In terms of food, the Kapetanios Odyssia 3 * hotel (Cyprus) provides an all-inclusive menu. There are all kinds of side dishes, meat and fish dishes, coffee, a variety of salads, juices, coffee, tea, cappuccino, any choice of snacks and much more. For breakfast, you can choose cereal, omelettes, scrambled eggs or light refreshing freshly made juice. Local spirits and real foamy beers are always available in the lobby. The pool also has a bar where you can always order your favorite dish or cocktail.

Additional services

Movement between floors is possible both with the help of stairs and a comfortable high-speed elevator. For business negotiations at any level, a modern conference room is available, equipped with a fax and all the necessary office equipment. If desired, it is possible to order the delivery of selected dishes directly to your room. Smoking and non-smoking rooms are always available.

Apartments





All hotel rooms are located in the main and auxiliary buildings. The last floor is occupied by parking. All rooms are located from 1st to 4th floor. Each room has 4 rooms. The category of apartments is the same. The rooms are very spacious. Each has a large double bed and one single bed. For those who do not like to walk, they can watch TV (shows several Russian channels). Of household appliances there is a hairdryer, a refrigerator, an iron with an ironing board, an electric kettle and a stove. From the dishes, a set of spoons, forks, cups, glasses, pots and a pan is provided. Meals can be prepared in your own kitchen. There is a work surface, sink and stove. From the bedroom furniture there are chairs, bedside tables, a wardrobe, armchairs, small drawers. Each room has a balcony with plastic furniture. The floor is tiled with artificial marble.

The bathroom is big enough. Toiletries in the form of soap, shampoo, paper and shower gel are regularly replenished.

Room Service

The rooms are cleaned very well, the maids even move the beds and dust in all secret places. Towels are changed daily. The room is given 3 bath, 3 facial towels and 1 for the feet. Tipping does not affect the quality of cleaning.

Beach vacation





The hotel has a swimming pool, but almost no one is near it. Everyone prefers to go to the beach. Moreover, it is very close. The water in the sea is cool. Sun loungers and umbrellas are available at an additional cost. Especially exotic looks dark sand, which is strewn with the entire coastal zone. Volcanic rocks give it such an unusual color. The water in the sea is clear, sometimes algae are nailed to the shore. A significant minus is that the beaches almost never clean, they just dig in the sand with a rake. The entrance to the sea is gentle. With children, relax comfortably.
