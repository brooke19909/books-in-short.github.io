Hotel Pambos Napa Rocks 2 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

A fabulous place in Cyprus called Ayia Napa is considered one of the most popular resort areas for many vacationers. Tourists from all over the world come to rest in Cyprus in huge numbers.

A trip to any resort involves some planning of their capabilities. First of all, you need to understand what exactly you expect from the upcoming vacation. The choice of hotel will depend on this. If you want to spend all your time in the hotel complex, then you need to choose more respectable complexes of 4-5 star category. But if you are limited in finances or do not like to sit in the hotel on vacation, you can safely even choose a two-star hotel. In this case, the Pambos Napa Rocks 2 * hotel will be a good option.

Description of the complex





This economy-class hotel is located in the central part of Ayia Napa and has about 194 apartments of various levels of comfort as part of the number of rooms. In addition, there is a summer pool and an outdoor area for sunbathing, equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas, air mattresses and sunbeds, which can be used completely free of charge.





Beach

Nearby there is a sandy beach, the distance to which is just over 250 meters. The coastal zone is ideal for bathing young children, since the entrance to the sea is gentle, there are practically no waves and storms, and a high embankment protects from the wind. Each beach in Cyprus is approved and marked with a special blue flag.

It was clean beaches with azure waters and golden sand that served as the basis for the love of Cyprus for representatives of all age groups, without exception.





All city monuments and attractions, including Ayia Napa Monastery, the water world and the center of Talas are within walking distance. The central areas of the city are removed from the hotel by an average of 1 kilometer. Larnaca Airport is a 40-minute drive away.

The rooms are divided into studios, standard and studios of improved layout. Each of them has a balcony, a plasma TV, a safe, a compartment for storing wardrobe items and a wardrobe.

Food at the hotel

The restaurant at the complex on the beach can offer its guests a variety of dietary dishes of any cuisine, and in the bar you can enjoy delicious cocktails. From the services the hotel is ready to kindly provide a photocopy machine, a copier and a roomy room for business negotiations.





For entertainment, guests are invited to play a game of tennis, volleyball or darts. As for the Internet, this service is provided for a fee, albeit a small fee, which is about 4 euros. The price is calculated per hour of use.

If we talk about the general access to the Internet, then it is provided in specially designated areas. You can park your car for free. There are vending machines on the territory of the complex, registration of guests is carried out around the clock. The hotel staff speaks several languages.

Additional services

The hotel has rooms for both smoking guests and those who do not have this addiction. It is also possible to serve people with disabilities. There is a fireplace in the lobby, a currency exchange and souvenir shops with a wide selection of goods. There is no elevator in the hotel, pets are not allowed in the hotel A luggage storage room for bicycle and personal items is available.

If you wish, you can play billiards in a special room.
