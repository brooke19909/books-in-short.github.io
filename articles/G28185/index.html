Corrugated Sample

To create such a branchy tree, you will need a piece of corrugated cardboard, a simple pencil and large scissors or a sharp knife. You need to divide the sheet into two equal parts. On one side they draw a trunk, a wide stand from below and several branches. On the contour all this is cut with scissors. Then the cut out template should be attached to the second half and circled with a pencil. The barrel and the stand must exactly match. Branches may be different.





In order for the bulk tree made of cardboard to stand steadily , with your own hands you need to draw a line in the middle in one part. On it the trunk is cut from top to center. On the other side, on the contrary, along the drawn line, the section is made from the bottom to the center. In order for the two parts to join together freely, the cut lines should be equal to the thickness of the cardboard, approximately 0.5 cm.

It remains only to assemble the parts together, putting one part on top of the other. From below, all supports should be at the same level so that the product stands firmly and does not stagger. When the main part of the work is prepared, you can begin to decorate the branches. The guys will do it.

Detailing

To decorate a volumetric tree from cardboard with your own hands, you need to cut a lot of leaves and stick them to the branches. If the tree will be used in kindergarten or other institutions, and for different occasions, it is better if the decorative elements are removable. Pay attention to the photo below. All leaflets hang on threads, and the green lush crown is generally represented by satin wide ribbons that hang in turns from the ceiling tiles. You can also string all the leaves on a long rope and hang them on branches like a garland.





Large items, such as a birdhouse or an owl sitting on a branch, can be attached to brackets or paper clips. Then the elements are easy to remove and put in a box for the next holidays.

Twisted tree

Now, among hand-made masters, many are fond of quilling. This is a curl of paper strips. We also used this method to create such an unusual volumetric tree. The master class of the craft is described below.

  1. Compressed brown paper is sold in rolls. It is long and suitable for making such an unusual barrel. The roll needs to be unrolled and the paper curled in a roll in length. Get a twisted "rope". Several of these twisted parts are placed along the trunk line, expanding the part to the upper edge.
  2. From below, several layers of grass are glued. This is a colored double-sided paper with a fringed edge.
  3. Now the main work begins on the design of the crown. It is performed in the quilling technique, only using wide thick paper. The parts are not twisted tightly. The edge is glued to the last turn. Then, the end part of the roll is smeared with PVA glue and glued to the location in the picture with a light pressure of the palm on top.
