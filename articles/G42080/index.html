Thermoregulation of the human body allows you to keep the body temperature constant

When changing the parameters of the environment surrounding the person, in this case the microclimate, his thermal state also changes. If any conditions violate the heat balance of the body, then reactions that restore it immediately occur.

Thermoregulation of the human body is the process of regulating heat emissions, contributing to the maintenance of a constant body temperature, which is close to 36.5 degrees. Conditions that violate the normal thermal regime of a person are called uncomfortable. Conditions under which the metabolism in the body is normal, there is no tense situation with heat transfer, are called comfortable. They are also optimal. The zone that completely removes heat generated by the body, in which the voltage of the thermoregulation system does not occur, is a comfort zone.

There are three ways in which thermoregulation of the body is carried out:

  1. Biochemical method.
  2. Change in blood circulation intensity.
  3. The intensity of perspiration.

In the first method, biochemical, the intensity of the processes occurring in the body changes. For example, when the ambient temperature decreases, muscle tremors occur, which increases the release of heat. Such thermoregulation of the human body is called chemical.

In the second method, the body independently regulates the flow of blood, which, in this case, is considered as a carrier of heat. It transfers heat from internal organs to the surface of the body. In this case, the necessary narrowing or vasodilation occurs. At high temperature around - the vessels expand, blood flow from internal organs increases, at low temperature the reverse process occurs. The vessels narrow, blood flow decreases, less heat enters the outside.

With a decrease in air temperature, there is a decrease in heat transfer, perspiration, and skin surface moisture, therefore, due to a decrease in evaporation, the heat transfer of the body decreases. Large moisture losses can be dangerous to humans.

In the second and third cases, physical thermoregulation of the human body occurs.

The microclimate significantly affects the condition of a person, his performance. The comfort of living and working conditions is influenced by the gas composition of the air, optimal weather conditions. Microclimate parameters provide heat exchange between the body and the environment. This is the thermoregulation of man.
