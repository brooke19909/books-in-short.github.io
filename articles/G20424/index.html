Hotel Breezes Bella Costa 4 * (Varadero, Cuba): description and reviews of tourists

For many travelers, Cuba is associated with a true paradise. Here, amazing nature and white beaches are ideally combined with a unique culture and history. By the way, one of the most famous resorts of this island-state is the city of Varadero. Of course, there is more than enough beach on its territory. And the hotel complex Breezes Bella Costa 4 is popular.

Of course, choosing a hotel for a vacation is a serious occupation, so it is worth it to be taken with all responsibility. Is the hotel location convenient? How far do you have to go to the beach and shops? Can one count on cleanliness, comfort and delicious food? How to diversify leisure? What do tourists who have already visited her say about the hotel? The answers to these questions are worth a look.

Location Information

Without a doubt, location is an extremely important factor when choosing a stopping place during a trip. Where exactly is the Breezes Bella Costa 4 hotel complex located? Varadero is one of the most spectacular and popular Cuban resorts, it is here that many tourists prefer to spend time.





By the way, the location can rightfully be considered convenient. In just a few minutes you can walk to the luxurious soft sand beach. The city center with its boiling life and bright colors is only 5 km away - this distance can easily be overcome by taxi or tourist bus. And the huge Plaza Americas Shopping Center is easily accessible even on foot.

Varadero Airport is approximately 30 km from the Bella Costa Hotel Complex (ex Breezes Bella Costa 4). If you have landed at the Havana airport, then you should prepare for the three-hour drive.

What does a resort complex look like?

Breezes Bella Costa 4 is a large hotel complex located almost on the seashore. A luxurious hotel courtyard enters the beach.





By the way, the territory here is simply huge and incredibly beautiful - there is a garden, sports grounds, and terraces for relaxation. Walking alleys are lined with bright exotic flowers on both sides, and trimmed lawns are pleasing to the eye.

The hotel itself consists of a main six-story building and six three-story bungalows, which are ideal for tourists who want to relax in silence. Despite the fact that the buildings are not new, not so long ago they were completely renovated, so today travelers can enjoy all the modern amenities. By the way, in 2002 the Breezes Bella Costa 4 hotel entered the well-known international hotel chain Iberostar.

Living Room Categories

As already mentioned, the Super Clubs Breezes Bella Costa Resort 4 is quite large. It consists of 396 rooms of different categories. Tourists have plenty to choose from:

  • 293 Standard Rooms - spacious single or double rooms located in the main six-story building.
  • 62 Junior Suites - These rooms are located in small bungalows in the middle of the garden.
  • 28 Junior Suites Duplex - spacious rooms, which consist of a bedroom and a living room with folding furniture. These rooms are ideal for a large family with children.
  • 13 Suites - rooms with additional furniture, appliances, with a high level of comfort.

What does the number look like? Photo and additional information about the arrangement

It offers spacious rooms with private balconies and large windows, from which you can enjoy breathtaking ocean views. Of course, the rooms are furnished with all necessary furniture, including a work desk, a large bed, a wardrobe, bedside tables for storing things.





Tourists can use the TV, air conditioning, telephone. There is a small but roomy refrigerator, as well as a coffee maker and an electric kettle. For an extra fee, guests have access to a safe and minibar. Includes a bathroom with shower and other amenities. Maids regularly change towels, replenish stocks of soap, shampoo and other hygiene products.

Meals: restaurants and cafes in the hotel area

When planning a vacation, you should definitely think about food, especially if you are going to Cuba. What should the guests of Breezes Bella Costa 4 count on? Like many hotels on the island, this one provides its guests with full, three meals a day.





Three times a day, guests are invited to the main Varadero buffet restaurant. Here tourists are offered a wide selection of dishes, including meat, soups, side dishes, vegetable salads, cheese slices and snacks, as well as exotic vegetables and pastries.

In addition, the hotel has other interesting places. For example, in the Criollo restaurant, guests are always ready to offer delicious Mediterranean cuisine, slightly redone in a Cuban fashion. But at the La Parilla steakhouse, guests are delighted with delicious grilled food. By the way, you need to book a place in advance - otherwise you may not find a free table.

Bars are open throughout the day. One of them is located by the pool, and the second - right on the picturesque beach. There is also a bar in the spacious, air-conditioned lobby. Here, tourists are offered cocktails, ice cream, strong drinks, as well as light snacks.

Since the hotel complex is located near the center of Varadero, you should not worry about food - there are a huge number of restaurants and cozy cafes in the city where you can have a wonderful time and enjoy Cuban dishes.

Beach: location, amenities, entertainment

As already mentioned, the hotel complex is located almost on the ocean - from the main building to the azure waters you can walk in just a few minutes.





The beach is private, so it is rarely too crowded. Hotel guests use sunbeds and umbrellas for free and, as the reviews indicate, there are enough of them for everyone.

For a small fee, you can take a boat trip, ride a boat or catamaran, do parasailing. Of course, there are diving and surfing centers near the hotel - here you can take an express training course, get the necessary certificates and improve your skills.

Conditions for children's entertainment

Many tourists are interested in information about the hotel Breezes Bella Costa 4 (Cuba, Varadero). Rating, reviews, living and dining conditions are important issues. But for parents traveling with a child, the presence of an additional service is also important.





As already mentioned, the hotel has spacious two-room rooms, although if necessary, an extra bed will be delivered to the standard room. The restaurant does not have a special children's menu, but parents always have the opportunity to choose some tasty and healthy dish for their child.

As for additional services, for a small fee you can leave a child with an experienced nanny. Kids have a great time on the territory of the playground (there is a swing and a sandbox). Also, kids love to splash around in the pool and play on the picturesque ocean.

Hotel Breezes Bella Costa 4 (Cuba, Varadero): service

Choosing this hotel complex as a stopping place, tourists can, with a clear conscience, count on a certain level of service. For guests there is a parking area, next to which there is a rental car and other vehicles.

In the lobby you can always exchange currency at a favorable rate. By the way, for an additional fee, guests can connect to the Internet, use a computer and some other office equipment.

Entertainment: how to brighten up leisure on site?

Travelers in most cases prefer to spend time on the beach, rejoicing in the warm sunshine and the azure waters of the ocean. Nevertheless, at the hotel you can diversify your leisure.





In the middle of the tropical hotel garden, there are two swimming pools, around which there are wide sun loungers. Here, guests relax, relax, arrange competitions on the water floor, do aerobics and just talk to each other. For an additional fee, you can enjoy all the amenities of a jacuzzi.

A small but fully equipped gym is available for guests. Guests have fun playing volleyball, practicing archery. Of course, the hotel has a dance class in which tourists are taught the basics of temperamental Cuban and Latin American dances. Instructors are happy to deal with experienced dancers as well as beginners. And in the evenings, they organize bright parties to live music. There are also tables for ping pong, billiards.

A prestigious golf club with a large 18-hole golf course is just 1 km away - not only experienced players are waiting here, but also beginners, for whom special lessons are happy to be organized. And, of course, do not give up excursions, because Cuba is a country that is largely unique. Here, and natural beauty, and unique culture, and vibrant attractions.

Hotel Bella Costa 4 (Varadero, Cuba): prices, reviews, recommendations

When booking, be sure to carefully study all official information. But it will never be superfluous to get acquainted with the opinion of former guests. What do the Breezes Bella Costa 4 Hotel say? Reviews are for the most part positive.

A huge advantage is the presence of your own, well-groomed beach. The coast and ocean waves look simply amazing, and the weather is good almost all year round. By the way, Cuban travelers prefer to spend time by the pool, so the beach is rarely crowded.

The guests still have some comments, for example, not too new furniture in the rooms. On the other hand, the rooms are quite comfortable, and the plumbing works. The service is not bad - tourists are always polite and courteous, smiling and trying to help solve a particular problem.

Sometimes tourists complain about a certain uniformity of the menu, although no one leaves the restaurant hungry. And in bars, polite employees often even prepare cocktails that are not on the menu. The location of the hotel is convenient - you can easily reach the city center and local attractions. As for prices, a ten-day stay at the hotel will cost about 150-190 thousand rubles (for a double room).
