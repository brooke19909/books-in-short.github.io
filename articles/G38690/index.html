Mountain tourist village Esto-Sadok in Krasnaya Polyana (Sochi): hotels, entertainment and attractions

The small village of Esto-Sadok, located 4 km from Krasnaya Polyana, is part of Greater Sochi. The local population does not exceed 1000 people. The resort area is located near three ski resorts: OAO Gazprom, Gornaya Karusel and Alpika-Service. The village is very popular, as are Krasnaya Polyana and Rosa Khutor.



On the territory there are many recreation centers, medical boarding houses, sanatoriums, hotels and hotels of different classes from budget options to luxury apartments. In some villas, the price per day reaches 100 thousand rubles. Each hotel has entertainment centers, night clubs, bowling and restaurants of the highest category.

Everyone can go to Sochi on the embankment - the road will not take more than an hour. Nearby is the train station and the freeway. People come here for new emotions and thrills to go skiing, sledding, ice skating, take a fascinating walk along the high peaks and picturesque ranges.

You will be offered horseback and walking routes, rafting and, of course, familiarity with local attractions.

How to get to the resort?

The village can be reached from both Adler and Sochi. From the railway station the electric train "Swallow" with soft seats regularly runs. The road takes about 1-1.5 hours. The commuter train is very comfortable, equipped with air conditioning and a bathroom. Buses No. 105, 135 and minibuses go to the village. You can order a taxi if you are ready to pay 1000 rubles for a trip (more expensive for private traders).





Activities at the ski resort

The village of Esto-Sadok (the photos presented in the article allow us to form a definite opinion on the subject of our conversation) is the dream of a skier. Snow-covered slopes and winding tracks will please even the most experienced athlete. In certain areas, the elevation changes are really impressive, they are breathtaking. Here even walks are fascinating. Pure mountain air intoxicates, fills with life-giving energy. Nature itself took care of people.



Professional skiers will be able to test the extraordinary track on Mount Psekhako and master the four cable cars in the Alpika-Service complex. Exciting extreme descents await you at the Mountain Carousel. Beginners will not be left behind: a large training slope with a ski lift has been built for them .

All ski centers are characterized by a developed, rich infrastructure. Those who are not interested in skiing can visit the 3D cinema, wellness centers, salons with luxury cars, bowling or a sauna. Many vacationers consider Esto-Sadok district to be the real Russian Switzerland. The weather at the resort is favorable. In winter, the temperature is naturally minus. In summer it is warm and comfortable here (around +26 ¬∞ C).





What to do, what to visit?

In addition to the picturesque hills, in the township district there is something to see and where to go. To enhance self-education and for the sake of interest, visit the Achipsse Fortress, the old Anton Hansen Tammsaare House Museum. Go to the local history museum or to the Hunter's Lodge.

In the mountain village of Esto-Sadok there is a small copy of the Cheops pyramid, the most prominent part of this place. The village is famous for its flowering gardens and delicious honey, which is collected in numerous apiaries. Almost every second resident is engaged in beekeeping. The choice of honey is truly huge. Tourists will be able to taste various varieties for free and choose the most suitable for themselves.

Excursion travel agencies are operating in full force, offering interesting and memorable routes through the famous places of the highlands and Greater Sochi. Caving trips, rafting and jeep tours are organized. If you wish, you can travel and explore the sights on your own, with your own car or bicycle.

Where to stay?

A wide selection of hotels in the resort of Esto-Sadok. Hotels are designed for a completely different audience of customers. There are more than 20 guest houses in the village, so it will not be difficult for you to choose the best option. And best of all, the internal infrastructure of each hotel is at a high level. We offer you to get acquainted with the most popular hotels.

Modern hotel "Gala Plaza"

It is conveniently located right at the foot of the Achishkho and Aibga mountain ranges , literally a stone's throw from the cable car and ski lifts. The following room categories are available for accommodation: standard, studios and apartments. Each room has a compact balcony attached with a wonderful panorama of the forest, mountains and the Mzymta River.





Tatiana

The hotel is located in the picturesque village of Esto-Sadok, close to ski resorts. The administration offers its customers comfortable service, high service and versatile leisure. Every morning you can enjoy a deliciously delicious hot breakfast, followed by skiing and sledding. In the evening, visit the Finnish or Russian sauna. Finish the day in the pleasant atmosphere of the restaurant with a gourmet dinner.





Hotel Aibga

Stylish tourist complex in the very center of the village, near the motorway leading to the ski slopes. It is a four-story building in the form of a mountain chalet. The hotel is equipped with burglar alarms, a security service and a fire system. The price includes: Internet, breakfast, free delivery (round-trip) to the ski resorts, cinema and parking.

Here are just a small part of tourist sites. The village of Esto-Sadok is slowly turning into the most popular resort center. Spending vacations in this place is a pleasure. In the bosom of picturesque nature, a person forgets about pressing problems, relaxes and gathers strength.
