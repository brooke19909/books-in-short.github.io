Hotel Kipriotis Panorama Suites 5 * (Kos island, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

What could be better than relaxing in a 5-star hotel? Only the realization that it will be necessary to pay for it is not a very large amount. In Greece, there is one luxury hotel that boasts fairly low (for the five-star level) prices. And it is called Kipriotis Panorama Suites 5 *. Here I would like to talk about it in more detail.





About hotel

The opening of Kipriotis Panorama Suites 5 * took place in 2001. This complex consists of a main seven-story building located on a hilltop, as well as small cottages, 1 or 2 floors high. The hotel is notable for the fact that it is part of the well-known network of Iberostar Greece SA, and also participates in The Consular Corps Discount Scheme.

The complex is located in the eastern part of the island of Kos, a few kilometers from its capital of the same name, in the town of Psalidi. The airport can be reached from here in about half an hour by car.

In the immediate vicinity there are no special attractions, except for landscapes. But a 3-5-minute drive away is the Loggia Mosque, the Hippocrates tree, the ancient Agora, the monastery of Agia Paraskevi, the amphitheater, Asklepion Kosa, as well as many other interesting places.

But the most important thing is that from the hotel to its own private beach can be reached in 2 minutes on foot. To the sea here - within easy reach, and this is the main highlight of the hotel.





What is in the hotel?

Kipriotis Panorama Suites 5 * is a deluxe hotel, so it has all the services that can create comfort for guests. There is free Wi-Fi, parking nearby, 24-hour reception, tour desk, luggage storage, currency exchange - in general, every service that should be in any self-respecting hotel.

Guests can also take advantage of babysitting services for children, transfers, food and drink delivery, dry cleaning or laundry services, a beauty salon or a hairdresser, and you can also rent a passenger car if necessary. On the territory there is a church, several shops, a souvenir stall. It even has facilities for guests with disabilities. To be more precise, separate rooms for them, in which a shower, a bathroom and in general everything available in the apartment is adapted to their specific features.

Leisure

In addition to its own private zone on the beach, Kipriotis Panorama Suites 5 * has many others that can help guests spend time interestingly and cheerfully.
