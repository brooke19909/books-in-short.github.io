We select and process credit cards of the Agricultural Bank

Making and issuing a loan is one of the main directions of development of this banking structure. Credit cards of the Agricultural Bank allow you to take money on an interest-free loan for up to 55 days. The amount that can be taken on one such card can reach 1 million rubles. After a two-month period, the borrower will have to start servicing the loan at an interest rate of 21% per annum. For normal servicing of a loan, contributions of not less than 10% of the total debt are required. In case of violation of the regime, the client will be required to pay at least 0.5% daily of the total loan amount.



How to apply for a credit card of the Agricultural Bank

In order to receive a plastic card, a bank client must meet the following requirements:

  • be not younger than 21 and not older than 60 years;
  • have a valid passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation;
  • have a residence permit not far from one of the branches of the Agricultural Bank;
  • have a clean history of relationships with financial institutions.




Any credit cards of the Agricultural Bank can be issued by spending from 15 minutes to an hour in any of the branches. To do this, just fill out the appropriate application form and conduct a short interview with the manager of the loan department. If, after checking the history of relations with banks, everything is in order, then the organization will issue a permit to receive plastic. Credit on the card is revolving, which means the possibility of re-obtaining a loan immediately after repayment of the debt. In fact, you can return the funds and immediately put them back into circulation.
