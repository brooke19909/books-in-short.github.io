Terms of Accommodation

Check-in starts at 14:00 Moscow time. During the high season, an earlier check-in is guaranteed, subject to payment of a full day of stay. At other times, an earlier check-in is possible for paying half the cost of living.

Departure of guests is carried out until 12:00 of the settlement day. Late check-out is possible for an additional fee (until 23:00 - 12 hours, after 23:00 in the amount of payment for a full day).

All rooms can arrange an extra bed. Children under 7 years old can be accommodated with parents or accompanying people at no extra charge and without a separate bed. It is possible to place on this principle no more than 2 children in the room. The cost of an additional place to relax depends on the season and ranges from 1,500 to 2,500 rubles.

After 23:00, hotel guests must be registered at the hotel (at the reception). Smoking is strictly prohibited on site.
