Hotel Asialoop G-house 3 *: tourist reviews, specifications and features

Thailand is a country that annually attracts a huge number of tourists to its open spaces. Its territory is rich in picturesque surroundings, pleasing to the eye of nature lovers, as well as attractions that travelers want to visit.

Phuket Island is one of the most visited resorts, which is loved by many vacationers for the luxurious beach located on it - Patong. Here is a huge number of hotels offering different levels of accommodation. One of them is Asialoop G-House 3 *. Reviews about it can be found on the Internet.

Location

The hotel is located on the island of Phuket in Thailand, in its southern part. Its main advantage is that it offers relaxation on the territory of the most famous beach in the whole country - Patong. Not far from it are all the city infrastructure, including the large Jungceylon shopping center, which tourists like to visit not only for entertainment, but also in order to purchase interesting goods, traditional for Thailand, as well as souvenirs for relatives and friends. At 200 meters from the place of rest there is an excellent nightclub "Simon Cabaret", which is often visited by guests. The district also has a huge number of various bars, restaurants, karaoke, clubs and other entertainment venues.

The coastline is located a little less than a kilometer from the hotel. In order to get on it, guests need to pass not only the road, but also the promenade. However, many do it with great pleasure, admiring the beautiful nature along the way.

As for transport routes, Asialoop G-House 3 * (Phuket / Patong) is located at a short distance from the central bus station. The international airport, where all foreign tourists arrive, is a 45-minute drive away.

Rooms

Asialoop G-House 3 * (Phuket Island) is a small place to relax, as there are only 30 apartments in its room stock. All of them are designed so that each guest can feel as comfortable as possible in them. All of them are presented in several categories: "Economy", "Standard", "Superior", "Family" and "Deluxe".

Each type of apartment has a private bathroom combined with a bathroom. Here guests are provided with a standard set of plumbing. Upon entering the room, guests receive one individual set of bath accessories, as well as bathrobes and towels in addition.

Deluxe

Deluxe apartments offer guests superior comfort. Staying here, guests can enjoy the interior, made in Asian traditions. Tourists in their reviews of Asialoop G-House "3 stars" (Patong Beach) often share their positive impressions received from what they saw. According to them, every detail is filled with oriental flavor.

The deluxe rooms are 28 square meters. only modern furniture is located, and from the window and from the balcony you can admire the breathtaking sea view. Services

In reviews of Asialoop G-House 3 * it is often said that for the guests of this hotel a wide range of services is offered, which are offered by the staff and the administration. One of the most popular among them is washing and drying clothes in the laundry room, often travelers resting here go to dry cleaning.

The hotel provides a transfer service from the point of arrival of tourists. The reviews very often say that the hotel to provide such a service has a fairly comfortable passenger transport, equipped with air conditioning.
