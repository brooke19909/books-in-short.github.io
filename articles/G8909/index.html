Turkey, Mahbury Beach. Accommodation

The hotel has 200 cozy and comfortable rooms, which are divided into categories:

  • standard (182);
  • for people with disabilities (3);

All Family rooms have amazing sea views. Absolutely all rooms are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, private bathrooms, minibar, telephone and hairdryer. The rooms are decorated in a modern and classic style. A cot will be provided in the room upon request.





Turkey, Mahbury Beach. Nutrition

The restaurant is open buffet style. Three meals a day - breakfast, lunch and dinner. Four bars are open at different times. There is a bar in the lobby, in the garden, on the beach and a snack bar. Here you can order not only soft alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, but also snacks.

Turkey, Mahbury Beach. Pools and beach

For those who like to spend time in the water and near it, the hotel has 2 swimming pools, 1 indoor with warm water, a children's pool and three water slides for adults, as well as a rich assortment of water fun on the beach. A full relaxation of body and soul will be ensured by a trip to the SPA center, where you can have an excellent time in the sauna, bathhouse or for cosmetic procedures. The list of free services includes: Turkish bath, jacuzzi, sauna, steam room, sports center. The hotel has its own sandy beach, which is 30 meters from the building. Guests can use the shower, umbrellas and changing cabins for free. Mattresses for sun loungers are available at an additional cost.
