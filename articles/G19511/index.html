How to make a rabbit out of paper (for beginners)

Origami paper figures can not only decorate the room and give the interior a themed mood, but also become a great way to have fun and usefully. How to make a rabbit out of paper? There are a lot of options for instructions. Some models, the simplest ones, can be made even by beginners.

Cute bunny

Cute easter decoration can be cute handmade bunnies. It is advisable to use special paper for origami, but this is not necessary. How to make a rabbit out of paper? If you train well and carefully follow the instructions, it can take no more than one minute. Necessary materials

For work you will need:

  • medium density paper;
  • coloring materials (markers, paints, colored pencils).

Instructions for beginners

First you need to decide on the color. It can be an ordinary white bunny, but you can consider more interesting and vibrant options. You can draw a muzzle and ears both before and after folding. But to apply tinting and background image is best in advance. If paints are used for staining, then the main work should only begin after the base has completely dried. Detailed instructions will answer the question of how to make a rabbit out of paper. So let's get started:



1. Fold a square sheet of paper in half diagonally to make a triangle.

2. Gently fold the side of the triangle that used to be the diagonal of the square.

3. Then we fold the figure in half so that the fold lines from the previous step are not visible, and then turn it back.

4. Take one of the sides of the triangle (ear) and fold it to the top without crossing the fold line from the previous step.





5. Do the same on the other side.

6. Turn the rabbit face up and bend the bottom corner about 2 cm inward to make the chin less sharp.

7. Do the same with the top of the muzzle.





Done! So you have learned how to make a rabbit out of paper, even if this is one of the simplest possible options. You can do everything according to the pattern, but you can go even further and use torn pieces of paper, natural materials, fabric, stickers, rhinestones and so on. Unleash your imagination!

How to make an origami paper rabbit?

Prancing paper rabbit is a variation on the theme of the traditional origami jumping frog. This funny project is also quite simple in terms of execution. Thanks to a special folding method, a spring effect is achieved. If you press the back of the rabbit, it will bounce up. Fast and fun, and how much joy for the children!





What do you need? A sheet of paper, felt-tip pens, scissors, glue or tape. Detailed instructions will show how to make a paper hare (origami for beginners):

  • Take paper or cardboard with dimensions of 6 by 10 cm. For best results, blanks should have straight sides and square corners. Bunny dimensions may vary. The main thing is to maintain proportions (3: 5). You also need to prepare small pieces of paper or cardboard of the same color to make the ears, otherwise the bunny will be more like a frog.
  • Fold the sheet in dashed lines as shown. This will help to make a triangle, in which there will be two more triangles.
  • Then fold the lower ends of the large triangle to its top.
  • The sides of the rectangle also fold to the center.
  • After that, the lower part of the rectangle is superimposed on the upper part of the figure. A reference point for laying the fold is the lower corner of the rhombus.
  • We cut out two rabbit ears and use glue or scotch tape to fasten them to the head of the animal. Also on the head you can draw eyes and nose.
  • In order for the rabbit to be able to jump, you need to fold the Z-shape from the back of the figure, which will act like a spring.
  • All is ready. Now you can arrange competitions with friends to see whose rabbit is jumping above all.
