Upon arrival, tourists expect a welcome surprise and a quick check-in. The hotel is really quite popular with Russian citizens. It can be safely recommended for a health, business, romantic, family or beach holiday.





Number of rooms

All 353 rooms have balconies with panoramic views of the adjacent area or the sea. The rooms are well-furnished and equipped with modern furniture. Each room has a bathroom with shower, sink, toilet and bath accessories.

For a more comfortable stay, guests have air conditioning, a telephone line, satellite TV. In addition, there are radio, hairdryer, safe, bar with paid drinks. A round-the-clock service is offered for everyone.





Nutrition

Amir Palace Hotel (Tunisia) operates on an all-inclusive basis. At their discretion, tourists can choose the most suitable meal option: half board or full board. Two restaurants are open on the territory, offering exclusive dishes of national and international cuisine.

Connoisseurs of elite alcohol will enjoy an extensive range of wine products. Light snacks, beer, freshly squeezed juices can be enjoyed at the snack bar or the Moorish cafe. A variety of pastries are offered by the pizzeria.





Beach

A few steps from the complex "Amir Palace" (Tunisia) extends a snow-white sandy coast. Daily sand on the shore is sifted through special equipment and cleaned of debris. The beach is very well maintained, equipped with sunbeds and sunshades.





Description of services

The compact adjoining territory of the resort center Amir Palace (Tunisia) accommodates an outdoor pool with a children's area, near which there are comfortable sun loungers. Directly in the building is a second indoor pool with heated water. For guests there is a beauty salon, massage rooms, a gym with a relaxation area, a sauna and a solarium.

The spa center provides wellness and beauty treatments, thalassotherapy. Throughout the day, entertainment shows featuring professional animators await customers. Scenes, contests and competitions are held in which guests are involved. A fun, unobtrusive and interesting program tunes you into positive emotions and perfectly cheers you up.

For a fee, you can play mini-golf, tennis, billiards, soccer or shoot archery. For business travelers - a conference room for 1000 people. There are trading pavilions, car rental, laundry department, a games room and a disco club. Children will be busy in the development center or on-site games.
