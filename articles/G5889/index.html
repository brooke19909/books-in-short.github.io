Hotel Best Delta 4 * (Mallorca): room description, service, reviews

Planning an unforgettable vacation in Spain? Have you chosen the beautiful island of Mallorca for a vacation? Looking for a comfortable hotel in a quiet location away from the hustle and bustle of the city? In this case, Best Delta 4 * can be an excellent option for you.



Location

The hotel is located in a quiet location on the second coastline. Palma de Mallorca Airport is just 15 kilometers away. In the vicinity of the hotel there are shops, car rental, souvenir shops, as well as several restaurants and cafes. There is a bus stop near the Best Delta, from where you can get to neighboring towns.



Best Delta 4 *: room description

This hotel is part of the Best Hotels group. It consists of two seven-story buildings located on a fairly spacious private green area. Despite the fact that the "Best Delta" was built back in 1979, today it can be safely called a completely modern hotel thanks to regular renovations. The hotel's housing stock is represented by 288 rooms completely updated in 2016. All of them are standard and are designed for two or three guests. The apartment is 24 square meters. meter. There are rooms with separate single or matrimonial beds. Each of them has all the necessary furniture, a bathroom, a safe (for an extra charge), air conditioning, telephone, hairdryer, balcony, refrigerator, cable TV and free wireless internet. Rooms are cleaned every day, and towels are changed upon request.



Best Delta 4 * (Mallorca): traveler reviews

Every experienced traveler knows firsthand how important it is to plan your trip. Especially when it comes to the long-awaited vacation. After all, I want everything to go perfectly, and the memories of a great vacation have pleased us for a long time. When planning a trip, special attention should be paid to choosing a hotel. Especially if you plan to stay in a comfortable hotel, rather than a cheap hostel. In addition to the official description and advice of tour operators, it makes sense to pay close attention to the reviews of tourists who have already visited the place of interest to you. This will help to create a more realistic picture of what to expect from living in a particular hotel. And based on this, you can decide whether to book this hotel or is it better to continue the search. Today we offer to find out what impression our compatriots had, who stayed in Best Delta 4 * (Mallorca) during their holidays in Spain. Reviews of tourists, it should be recognized, for the most part are positive. But we will understand everything in more detail.



Rating

Travelers who chose tours to Mallorca and chose to stay at the Best Delta Hotel were mostly satisfied with their decision. So, the rating of the hotel, compiled on the basis of guest ratings, is 4.3 of the maximum possible five points. Tourists rated the infrastructure, staff, room equipment and value for money service the highest. The location of the hotel seemed not very convenient for some guests. We suggest considering each item in more detail.

Territory and infrastructure

Best Delta 4 * (Mallorca) is located in a charming secluded place in a quiet village. The hotel is surrounded by pine trees, filling the air with its aroma. So if you are tired of the hustle and bustle of a big city and want to relax in a quiet place by the sea, then Best Delta 4 * is perfect for you. Entertainment for guests here is quite diverse. So, on the territory of the hotel there are several tennis courts, mini-golf and football fields, basketball courts, three swimming pools with comfortable sun loungers and parasols. Vacationers can visit the sauna, gym, play billiards, darts, board games. Also, tourists in their comments note an unobtrusive animation program. The hotel grounds are well-kept, green and clean. Those who arrived on vacation with the whole family were pleased with the availability of playgrounds. Also, guests are happy to mention the availability of free parking. By the way, please note that you can rent a car directly at the hotel. At the same time, tourists note that very often promotions act on this service. Therefore, if you take a car for several days, then most likely you will get a very significant discount.



Housing stock

Judging by the reviews left, almost all the guests of the Hotel Best Delta were very pleased with the rooms offered to them. So, according to them, they are all quite spacious, stylishly decorated, with freshly renovated and modern new high-quality furniture and fixtures. Tourists were pleased with the presence of balconies with a table and chairs, where you can comfortably stay with a glass of wine or a cup of coffee and enjoy the sea and the surrounding area. Among the advantages, guests note comfortable hangers in the closet, high-quality linens, and the availability of free Wi-Fi. True, some guests felt that the Internet is not fast enough. However, most claim that they did not have problems surfing the Web and when communicating with family and friends on Skype. As for the minuses, some of the travelers were unhappy that the safe in the rooms can be used for an additional fee.



Location

Based on the comments of our compatriots, we can conclude that Best Delta 4 * (Mallorca) is best for tourists who plan to rent a car for the rest. In this case, you can visit a variety of beaches and visit the local attractions that interest you. There is also a bus stop near the hotel, from where you can reach the nearby resort towns of Arenal and Palma de Mallorca. Experienced travelers are advised to visit the second village. In it you will find a lot of interesting places. The city is also famous for its excellent beaches.

Accommodation

In accordance with the rules of the hotel, the arrival of new arrivals is carried out in the afternoon. On the day of departure, you will be asked to leave the room until 10 a.m. or to extend your stay for an additional fee. By the way, as some travelers note in their reviews, it was a surprise for them to pay an environmental tax upon arrival at the hotel. For two guests it is 30 euros.



Service

Tourists staying in Best Delta 4 *, the service was not particularly highly rated. No particular criticisms were noted, however, some guests complained, for example, of the fact that, as a rule, only one employee is present at the reception. As a result of this, queues are often formed from guests who want to complain about a problem or get an answer to a question. Also, some complaints about the work of the maids. According to our compatriots, they carry out cleaning rather superficially, often forget to replenish supplies of personal hygiene products or replace towels.



Nutrition

On this point, vacationers had practically no complaints. Moreover, according to our compatriots, the food at Best Delta is organized at a very decent level. When booking a stay, most tourists chose the half board option, which includes breakfast and dinner. All meals are served in buffet style. As the guests note, in the morning it was possible to eat a variety of foods and dishes. A surprise for many was the availability of several varieties of champagne. As for dinners, the guests were also very pleased with them. So, in the evening you can taste various fish and meat dishes (veal, pork, lamb, rabbit, chicken), a variety of side dishes, fruits and vegetables, desserts and pastries. In addition, depending on the theme of the evening, additional tables were set with seafood, dishes of Chinese, Mexican, Asian or Italian cuisine. Free ice cream is also available. But if you do not eat on an all-inclusive basis, then you will have to pay extra for drinks at dinner.

In addition, tourists note that next to the hotel there are several shops and supermarkets where you can buy groceries. Also in the vicinity of the "Best Delta" you will find several restaurants and pizzerias with affordable prices.

Beach

People who are interested in tours to Mallorca, as a rule, in addition to the cultural and sightseeing programs plan to have a great time, basking on the beach and swimming in the warm sea water. Therefore, proximity to the beach plays an important role in choosing a hotel. What does the Best Delta 4 * considered by us offer in this regard? The beach closest to the hotel, judging by the reviews, somewhat disappointed many tourists. The fact is that it is rocky, without a convenient entry into the water. There are no sunbeds here, and it‚Äôs rather tough to settle directly on the plates. Therefore, some vacationers bought air mattresses. The distance from the hotel to the beach is about one and a half kilometers. Moreover, part of the path is a serpentine, which can be quite difficult to climb. In this regard, many tourists preferred to rent a car and visit other beaches.
