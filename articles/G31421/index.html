Destination Overview





If we talk about the famous resorts of Slovenia, Portoroz is simply impossible to ignore. This settlement is located 130 km from the capital - Ljubljana, and 100 km from the rather large city of Pula.

Having gone deep into history, you can find out what its name means "Port, abundantly planted with roses." Incidentally, it starts from the small village of Lucia and stretches to the medieval city of Piran - the pearl of the Adriatic.

This delightful corner of the Slovenian Riviera combines amazing relaxation and wellness programs in the sauna center and thermal pools. The city of Portoroz (Slovenia) is also a modern center of thalassotherapy and Thai medicine. In addition, there is no shortage of Russian-speaking guides, since in the hottest months not only Austrians and Italians come to the resort, but also many Russians or citizens from the countries of the former USSR.

How to get to your destination





Not everyone knows that Portoroz has its own small airport, accepting flights from Ljubljana and Trieste. Why not? Prices for flights are quite acceptable, and stocks arranged by various carrier companies are quite common.

The resort itself is easily accessible by regular buses. Sometimes the hotel staff comes to the rescue. For example, the Grand Hotel (Portoroz, Slovenia), as a rule, organizes meetings of its tourists in the capital.

It should be noted that in Portoroz, shuttle buses run between morning and evening with an interval of about 15 minutes connecting the resort town with Piran, the village of Lucia and a complex of sanatoriums and hotels.

Natural healing properties





In the 90s. XX century In Portoroz, rare, rather deep and very healing thermal springs were found that contain not only sulfur and sodium chloride, but also other useful marine components. The healing properties of this marine mineral water for human health are simply invaluable. That is why Portoroz (Slovenia), the photo of which is presented in the article, is a place that every traveler must visit.

The resort also has a thalassotherapy center that combines 5 beneficial factors in medical procedures, such as thermal mineral, sea and salt water, mud and clay. The amazing climate of Portoroz with a rich composition of iodine and bromine in the air also contributes to the treatment of various diseases.

Salt water is produced in a saltworks. It does not contain allergens, enriched with magnesium, iodine and bromine. Such salt water helps in the treatment of degenerative and rheumatic pains, muscles and joints, after operations and with nervous diseases. Inhalations with this healing water provide excellent cleansing of the respiratory tract in chronic diseases.

Also in Portoroz, the treatment uses high-quality homogeneous black clay, the so-called fango. It consists of a large number of healthy minerals and salt water. In the treatment process, the chemical and thermal properties of fango are especially used.

The main specialization of the resort

The resort specializes in the treatment of respiratory diseases, neurological disorders of the musculoskeletal system, skin diseases, overweight and neuro-physical exhaustion.

The wonders of the local climate





Portoroz (Slovenia) perfectly combines sandy beaches and beaches, mild marine climate, amazing nature and healing factors.

Of course, many would argue that, they say, marine recreation has always been and remains the most useful, even in Sochi, even in Alushta, even in Thessaloniki. But, unlike other settlements, in Portoroz, in addition to the sea, there are thermal springs and a wellness cosmetology center.

Among other things, the resort's surroundings are also rich in lush southern vegetation, fruit trees, flowers and incredible air filled with aromas of pine needles.

Portoroz (Slovenia) is waiting for its guests both in summer and in winter. In the warm season, it is humid and warm. Not very exhausting, comfortable for relaxing temperatures and a light refreshing breeze from the sea will make the trip unforgettable.

The healing air, saturated with minerals and useful natural substances, warm water and clean beaches is very beneficial for the human body.

No less nice here in the winter. In Portoroz, there are never severe frosts and sharp drops in temperature. Considering the wishes of travelers, a special park with pools is equipped in the resort area.

What to do on vacation





The resort has a very developed infrastructure. Here, not only a modern thermal center has been built, but also there are decent-quality hotels, miraculous saunas, pools (both with fresh and sea water) and the possibility of holding press conferences.

Fans of outdoor activities will be pleasantly surprised by the superbly equipped tennis courts, yacht clubs, sports gyms, horseback riding and the opportunity to learn different kinds of water sports.

In general, Slovenia (Portoroz in particular) is perhaps a real haven for entertainment. The resort in question constantly hosts social parties and excursions. You can visit the famous karst cave in Europe - Postojna Pit, a modern stud farm, Predyamsky castle with an underground system of grottoes and galleries.

By the way, not everyone knows that Venice, filled with romance, is located some 200 km from Portoroz. You can go there by bus or catamaran. Excursions are held on a regular basis.

2 km from the resort lurks the ancient town of Piran. It was founded long ago by the Venetians. To this day, in Piran, the labyrinths of narrow streets, fortress walls and medieval churches have survived.

In addition, this settlement is famous far beyond its borders for the best fish restaurants in Europe.

Interesting excursions





Relaxing at the resort, you can go on an interesting excursion and visit the alpine city of Bled. There is a medieval lake with a small island in the center. Everyone is recommended to visit it.

You can look at the beauty of old houses, the amazing design of facades and arched courtyards in Ljubljana.

No less impressive impact on both adults and children will be excursion to the famous stud farm in Lipica. Here, exclusively elite breed of Venetian horses was originally grown, now professionals are simply breeding thoroughbred horses.

Excursion to the local caves





An excursion to the karst region with underground caves, pine forests and limestone cliffs will not leave anyone indifferent.

That is it, Slovenia. Portoroz is just the most characteristic example of this area.

Of particular interest is the Postojna Cave. It consists of corridors 20 km long. The excursion begins with the fact that the first two kilometers deep into the cave, tourists travel by train. And only then does the walking route begin.

It will also add adrenaline to the blood and a tour along the Shkotsyak cave, protected by UNESCO. In it, the natural uncouthness has been preserved to this day. Here, courageous travelers must pass along a narrow bridge over a dark abyss with a depth of almost 80 meters. An underground river is seething at the very bottom , and whole flocks of bats hang overhead, as in a horror movie.
