How to transfer music from iPhone to iPhone without iTunes

How to transfer music from iPhone to iPhone? Often the need for this arises when you have purchased a new smartphone and want to transfer all the data from the old one to it. For a simple and quick solution to this issue, PhoneTrans Pro is most suitable.



On the one hand, iTunes makes it possible to transfer content from your gadget to a new mobile. But in this case, you will have to spend several hours on tedious and stupid data backup / restore. On the other hand, for a quicker solution, you can simply follow the step-by-step guide below.

How to transfer music from iPhone to iPhone without iTunes

Download the PhoneTrans Pro service from the network (free). Connect your iPhone with the right content to your computer. If you have two USB cables, then you can connect two iPhones to the PC at the same time. Open PhoneTrans Pro> Music. Select the songs you want to transfer and click on the selected device.

The transfer process, for example, 6,000 songs can take about twenty minutes. At this time, you can use the computer to perform any tasks, because PhoneTrans Pro is a program that does not affect the multitasking of the device.

Bottom line

When you purchase a new iPad, iPhone or Itouch and want to import all the songs and movies from your old device, you can do this with PhoneTrans Pro. This is a convenient assistant that can simply solve the problem of how to transfer music from an iPhone to an iPhone, as well as to any other iOS device. Thanks to the convenience and speed, this service allows you to instantly share music with family members and friends.





How to transfer audio files from iPhone 4 to iPhone 5 in another way?

If you need to copy music from iPhone 4 to the fifth generation gadget, you can use another transfer option. The free iOS Transfer app lets you do this with ease. This service is another way to add music to an iPhone without iTunes. Such an application can completely control the music in your device without iTunes. It helps the iPhone user to import, export, delete tracks directly, without additional connections. In addition, this software makes it easy to transfer video files, playlists, applications, photos and other contents of your iPhone, iPod and iPad to your computer.

Below is a step-by-step instruction on how to transfer music from iPhone to iPhone using IOS Transfer.

Step 1. Connect the device to the PC. To do this, you need two USB cables, since you need to synchronize both gadgets with your computer (at the same time).

Step 2 Download IOS Transfer and run on the computer. This program is available on many resources for free.

Step 3. Open the data preview on the iPhone. As soon as the application determines the device, on the left side of the service interface you can see it in the list. All data stored on your iPhone 4 and 5 will be listed in organized categories below the Library item in the left pane. Just click on types or specific files to make a preview.



Step 4. After that, select the files that you need to upload to Iphone 5, select them and click "Transfer" - the button located in the right corner. You will be able to see a pop-up window with various options. Select from them "Transfer to the list." Continuing to follow the instructions on how to transfer music to iPhone 5, just click the Transfer NOW button in the lower right corner and everything will be copied automatically.
