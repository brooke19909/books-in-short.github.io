Geisha costume. Image of a mistress for a photo shoot or event

One of the most popular images, a symbol of a real woman-temptress, many representatives of the fair sex wish to repeat the geisha costume. In the literal sense of the word, geishas are people of art. They are the ones who hold the famous tea ceremonies in Japan and maintain a conversation with the guests of the establishment. And they act as a model of a versatile, charming and dignified woman. Today it will be told how to make a geisha costume with your own hands.

The main features of the image

Since geisha are mostly popular in Japan, their clothing style also suggests a national motive. For the appearance of a geisha are characteristic:

  • kimono;
  • a fan or an umbrella;
  • okobo sandals with wooden soles;
  • high hairstyle with flowers and accessories;
  • whitened face and bright makeup.

Perhaps this is the basis of what is included in the geisha costume.

By repeating all or part of these points, you will get a recognizable image.

Kimono

Today, stores offer many options for ready-made kimonos for every taste and budget. If buying a ready-made kimono is not possible, there is an option to rent it in one of the salons offering such a service. The price for one day is significantly different from the purchase of the finished one.

You can also search for kimonos on Chinese sites. Prices there are much lower than domestic stores offer. If you could not find a kimono, you can replace it with a long Japanese-style silk robe. The only thing that will have to be replaced in this case is the belt. Because the kimono belt is distinguished by its width. Below is a pattern of an obi belt, along which you can repeat this element of the image of a geisha.

Geisha shoes

Since buying real okobo sandals one or more times can be too expensive, you need to come up with an alternative. For example, well complement the image of wedges on wedges. It is desirable that the sole of the shoe was made under a tree or simply in brown tones. If you create a geisha costume for a girl, then, of course, you will have to refuse a wedge heel. Replace it with ordinary slates or leather sandals, suitable in style.

Hairstyle and makeup

Also, do not forget to stock up on hairspray, because the hairstyle must hold tight to the head, be collected.

Try to repeat this feminine and mysterious image yourself. And also bring something of your own into it, making the image truly unique. Such a suit obviously will not go unnoticed by others and will be remembered for a long time.
