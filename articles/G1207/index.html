Hotel Emerald Beach Resort Spa 5 * (Ravda, Bulgaria): description and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria at the present time as a resort does not give up its positions and remains as popular as before among our compatriots. That is why in our article we want to talk about one of her five-star hotels, located in the city of Ravda.

A bit about the hotel ...

Emerald Beach Resort Spa 5 * is located in a wonderful natural park, from the windows of the rooms of the complex offers beautiful views of the Black Sea coast. The hotel was built in 2007, its area is 120,000 square meters. m. A huge plus of the complex is its convenient location. It is located in the very center of Ravda, and therefore everything you need is nearby (within walking distance). In the same case, if you need to go somewhere, you can use public transport, a bus stop is located 500 meters from the hotel. Such pleasant trifles make your stay in the hotel incredibly comfortable and convenient. From the airport to the complex is also not difficult to reach, the distance is only fifteen kilometers. On the one hand, the hotel is located at some distance from the bustle of large cities, located in a clean natural area, but at the same time it will not be difficult to get to key places.

Local Attractions

As we already mentioned, in order to enjoy the beauty of local attractions, you need to go to Nessebar - an ancient city-museum. He is now the spiritual and cultural center of Bulgaria. First of all, the unusual architecture of the city is striking; it was built during the national Renaissance, which was reflected in the style of houses. In fact, the architecture of the city is the history of the whole country. In Nessebar there are a great many monasteries and churches, as well as the ancient Metropolis. From the past, the gates, the remains of the fortress walls, part of the residential buildings and the town square have been preserved.

Old Nessebar is just a real miracle. A couple of centuries ago, he completely went under water, on land there was only a small piece of land (300 meters by 850), on which all city churches remained. ‚ÄúUnderwater Nessebar‚Äù can be seen from a boat in calm weather.



The modern city is conditionally divided into two parts. Its new part is hotels, comfortable cottages, restaurants, shops and bars. And the old one remained on a very small peninsula, connected to the land by a narrow isthmus. The old city has a unique atmosphere, which is achieved thanks to the presence of medieval churches, wooden houses, the ancient port and the remains of the fortress walls. It is here that you can walk along the cobbled streets among the buildings of the 19th century, not noticing the time.

An old windmill and a Turkish bath are popular with tourists. In addition, the city has museums: ethnographic and archaeological. In them you can see coins (4-1 century BC), gold jewelry (4-3 century BC), black figured ceramics of Antiquity and the Middle Ages, Thracian stone anchors, traditional costumes and jewelry of Bulgarians.

But from natural attractions you can see unusual white cliffs and natural sand dunes, the height of which is 7-8 meters. They create an almost fabulous landscape.

In addition to historical sights, in Nessebar you can visit water parks and Lunapark, not to mention the many night clubs, restaurants and taverns. Shopping lovers will also find something to do here. Wonderful shops and shops are scattered throughout the city, where you can buy not only souvenirs, but also clothes.
