MPM Boomerang 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): hotel description, services, reviews of tourists

Tourists can find everything that is needed for a good rest in MPM Boomerang 3 *. Here you can enjoy not only beautiful nature and magnificent beaches, but also a high level of service.





Location

A 10-minute walk from the luxurious public beach is the resort MPM Boomerang 3 *. Sunny Beach, 8230 - the address of this institution. It is about 25 km from Burgas International Airport. Also, relaxing here, you can visit the following tourist sites:

  • Grand club (1 km);
  • go-kart track (1 km);
  • Kuban (1 km);
  • action water park (2 km);
  • Cocoa Beach (2 km);
  • Nessebar (4 km).




Hotel rooms

If you value comfort and even want to feel the atmosphere of warmth and coziness even far from home, opt for the MPM Boomerang 3 *. Here, travelers are available with the following options for accommodation:

  • Double standard means accommodation for one or two tourists. The room can be a large or a pair of twin beds. There is also a work area and a lounge area. From your private furnished balcony you can enjoy the courtyard with pool.
  • For lovers of home comfort, such an accommodation option as apartments is suitable. They are a large room, which has a large bed and a seating area. Separately equipped kitchen with necessary equipment and a dining table. Private veranda is equipped with comfortable sunbeds.




Room Amenities

All that is needed for a comfortable stay is provided in the rooms of the MPM Boomerang 3 * resort complex. Bulgaria (Sunny Beach) will show you its hospitality thanks to the following set of amenities:

  • TV with a large package of satellite channels;
  • mini fridge for storing food and drinks;
  • individually controlled air conditioning system;
  • own furnished balcony;
  • The bathroom includes a hairdryer.

Catering establishments

At the MPM Boomerang 3 * hotel, much attention is paid to the nutrition of guests. This function is assigned to the following institutions:

  • The main restaurant provides guests with the possibility of three meals a day. A huge number of dishes are served in buffet style, including local traditional food. In addition to the main dining room, there is also an outdoor area surrounded by a dense garden.
  • The pool bar provides guests with quality imported and local drinks. It also serves cocktails prepared according to original recipes (your own combinations are possible). In addition, guests are treated to confectionery, ice cream and hearty snacks.

Sunny Beach, Sunny Beach MPM Boomerang 3 *: negative reviews

Unfortunately, any hotel has its drawbacks. MPM Boomerang 3 * (Bulgaria) was no exception. It is worth noting such negative moments of relaxation in this institution:

  • the pool on the territory is too small, and therefore swimming in it is pleasant only in the off-season, when the hotel is almost empty;
  • in the hot season, there is almost never the possibility of early settlement due to the absolute occupancy of the hotel;
  • instead of a double bed, the rooms provide a pair of shifted ones (but, given their large size, sleeping on them is quite convenient);
  • there is no heating in the pool, and therefore swimming in it before lunch is almost impossible;
  • there are few sun loungers near the pool (no more than 50 are provided for more than 200 vacationers), and therefore you have to take up space before breakfast;
  • the hotel has practically no territory, and when you exit the residential building, you immediately get on the roadway;
  • the road to the beach can take up to 20 minutes;
  • expensive sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach (rent will cost you about 20 leva per person);
  • too small tv in the room;
  • there is no curtain in the bathroom, which is why during showering water spills on the floor;
  • very close bathroom, which will be uncomfortable for people with a large physique;
  • a hygiene kit is issued only upon check-in, after which its supplies are not replenished;
  • in spite of the fact that as a whole the sound insulation in the hotel is quite good, there are thin walls between some rooms, which makes it very audible;
  • Wireless Internet in the rooms practically does not catch (except for those rooms that are closest to the lobby area);
  • in the cereals that are served for breakfast, too much sugar (it would be better if it was served separately and everyone would add to their taste);
  • there is no clothesline and clothespins on the balcony, and therefore you have to buy it yourself.

General impression

Despite the fact that the MPM Boomerang 3 * Sunny Beach has only three stars, the quality of service here is at the highest level. Guests will appreciate the friendly and courteous attitude of the staff, as well as perfect cleanliness in the rooms and throughout. Given that the hotel is located almost in the very center of the resort, you will have access to numerous attractions, which are not enough in the territory.

Special attention deserves food at the hotel. Of course, there are no gourmet delicacies here, but all the dishes are very tasty and of high quality. The atmosphere in the dining room is very comfortable. Everything is thought out so that there are no queues for distribution. In this regard, remoteness from the sea does not seem such a serious drawback, because a walk helps to burn extra calories.
