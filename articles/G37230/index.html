What is a good radar detector? Reviews and prices

Every motorist knows that anything can happen on the road. But to insure yourself in such difficult situations, you need to equip your car with high-quality systems. One of the most popular devices today is a radar detector. Which is better? Reviews, prices are very different about each manufacturer, so it is not always possible to make the right choice. We offer you an overview of not only popular radar models, but also the main parameters, based on which you should make your choice.

The main thing is police radar recognition





The Russian police armed themselves to the teeth - everywhere on the road there are various radars: "Golden Eagle", "Vizir", "Falcon", and the most treacherous - "Strelka". Its peculiarity is that it analyzes the traffic situation at once on all the lanes and transfers all data about the speed drivers to the traffic police database through a special program. That is why, when choosing, you need to focus on the fact that your radar detector effectively copes with signal recognition. Which is better? Prices in this case do not always play a decisive role, since the main thing nevertheless is the functionality.

4 selection rules





Just note that most radars differ only in hardware and software. And these differences ultimately affect the number of false positives and the possibility of receiving a signal at a certain distance. And additional functions, of course, greatly facilitate the life of the driver behind the wheel. So, to know which good radar detector to choose, consider the following rules.

  1. Frequency check. It is important for Russian drivers to capture the signal at frequencies of X and K, this, as well as equipping a laser receiver, is quite enough. In our country there are only three radio bands, so we do not need additional frequencies.
  2. What is the signal reception range? This indicator is able to talk about how far from the police radar your device will be able to recognize it. Experts advise choosing models equipped with a GPS module, which confidently takes a signal, including Strelka. According to reviews, among advanced models Neoline radars are attractive in their functions and capabilities, capable of accurately recognizing Strelka.
  3. Immunity to interference. This parameter should be taken into account when choosing radar detectors. Which is better? User reviews come down to the fact that cheap models respond to any interference, including phones or radiators. It is best to take those devices that can distribute the power of the received signal, and therefore are resistant to interference.
  4. Functionality Many strive to buy a multifunctional device that can do a lot for little money. But experts, and numerous customer reviews, say the opposite: the simpler the device, the more reliable it will work. So when deciding for yourself which good radar detector, and which is not very good, pay attention not to unnecessary functions, but to important elements: the ability to switch the city / highway modes, the convenience of fastening, an intuitive display, and a compact body.
