Moto Corporation

The company was founded in 1928 by the Galvin brothers in the United States. She got the name thanks to one of her first products - the Motorola autoradio receiver (moto - motor, ola - radiOLA). In the 50s of the last century, an emblem in the form of bat wings was developed, which made Motorola phones (old models) so recognizable. But that is all later.

Success and sunset

In 1956, the company released the world's first pager, and in 1983 - the first commercial mobile phone in the history of mankind.

But already in the 2000s, the production of mobile phones became unprofitable for the corporation. In 2011, Google bought Motorola Mobility for $ 12.5 billion.

What about now?

We remember the Motorola phones, the old models of the company. But the electronic company in 2016 released a new line of models already Moto smartphones. True, under the protectorate of Lenovo. You could see them on the shelves of Moscow stores since March 2016.
