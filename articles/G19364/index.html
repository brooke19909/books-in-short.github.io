Tilda doll: clothing patterns, interesting ideas with photos and sewing tips

Tilda doll became popular very quickly. Everyone liked the gentle stylish doll, which dresses in beautiful outfits made of natural fabrics in pastel colors. All tildes are alike. Despite the different growth, completeness or color, they are easy to distinguish from many others. Since the tilde is a handmade doll, there are no two identical ones. What distinguishes them from each other?

The individuality of the doll gives the costume. Therefore, patterns of clothes for tild dolls are very diverse.

When is a tilde doll born

A feature of the Norwegian doll is the ability to sew to her part of the outfit during manufacture. This can be a fixed underwear (T-shirt or T-shirt and panties), a corset or the entire upper part of the dress. In this case, the pattern of the torso and arms will be from elegant fabric. It remains only to sew a skirt.

For a skirt, you can choose a nylon ribbon and cut segments from it for three tiers. The lowest tier looks better if it is made of a wide strip, with large folds. When tilde changes dress

Over time, a non-removable outfit will fade, get dirty and require replacement. If the trousers under the skirt are not striking, then the blouse that has lost its appearance spoils the whole impression. You can gently tear open the handles and sew new ones. Such dresses can be sewn by measuring the fabric directly on the doll. The principle here is simple: the volume of the arm determines the volume of the sleeve, the volume of the hips - the width of the skirt. For a very wide one, a 2: 1 coefficient is chosen, that is, two hips, for an average - 1.5, for a very small expansion - 1.2. Building a base pattern: back

The procedure for constructing a back pattern:

  1. Draw a rectangle with a length equal to Cr and a height equal to Dst.
  2. Measure the center line and mark the intersection at the top and bottom.

  4. Two millimeters are laid down from the top intersection point. Draw an arc through this and the two previous points. This is the collar sewing line.
  5. Five millimeters are laid down from the top intersection point. Draw a horizontal line. The highest point of the sleeve is laid on it, departing 1.8 cm from each point of intersection of the sewing line of the collar with the upper horizontal line of the rectangle.
  6. From the lower intersection of the rectangle, the center line is laid 2.2 cm in each direction.
  7. From the bottom point of intersection of the rectangle, the center line is laid out the measure of Br and draw a horizontal line. Connect the points where it intersects with the vertical lines of the rectangle with those obtained in step 6.
  8. Connect the points obtained in paragraph 7 and paragraph 5 by an arc. This is the sleeve threading line.

Simple pattern of trousers

A huge advantage of sewing clothes for rag dolls is tilds - patterns can be stabbed directly on the doll, since it includes pins. You can sew a dress according to the pattern of the jacket. There is no point in building a fluffy skirt according to the pattern. Measuring the waist and hips, it is easy to cut from the fabric. For creases, leave three centimeters for stock.

Tilde trousers are better not to be sewn to the waist, they will not suit her. Small models sitting on hips - this is her option. Sewing them is easy, you do not need to build a drawing for this. Putting the chrysalis on the fabric folded in half, measure the length of the trousers. They should reach at least to the wrists. If a little higher - that's okay. The width of the trousers is measured along the bottom of the torso, where the legs are sewn. This is the widest place. One centimeter allowance is given for freedom - 0.5 cm on each side. The place where the legs are sewn is measured - this is the beginning of the legs. Cut to this place.

A two centimeter allowance is left for the hem and elastic at the waist. The bottom edge of the legs can simply be hemmed with a stitch, or you can insert a lace. For simple trousers, skirts, vestments, do not build drawings. Just put the chrysalis on the tissue and measure the control points, then connect them. This is how life-size tilde patterns are obtained.

Boho style clothes

Boho style allows you to combine lace and leather, ethno and classic, bulky shoes and frivolous chintz floral. Such elements, assembled together, create an image of comfort. For an interior doll, this is what you need. There are several stylistic trends of boho: classic, eco, hippie, vintage.

Skirts must be fluffy, with a lot of frills. The basic measurement for them is from the waist to the toes of the tilde's legs. Patterns of boho clothes are required only to create sleeves and a bodice, while skirts are laid directly on the fabric. The pupa is placed on a piece of fabric, the waist and the widest place of the torso (where the legs are sewn) are measured out, connecting lines are drawn. Give an allowance of one centimeter from both ends. There will be two such details. Sew side seams, sew frills from guipure, lace, chiffon.

At the back of the skirt, you can make a hook clasp or button so that you can remove it. The jacket is made according to the principle of undershirts with one-piece sleeves. They can be decorated with long lace cuffs or just tuck. Remember to give the doll a bulk bag.

Conclusion

Sewing for a doll is not just a game. This is an opportunity to get a hand to work with various tissues, and gain experience in modeling. Patterns of clothes for tilda can be built in three ways: classic, with the construction of drawings, measuring the dimensions in full size and combining from rectangular cuts and ribbons.

All three methods broaden your horizons and increase your skill. If you are not using any of them yet, there is a reason to try to sew a new little thing for your tilde.
