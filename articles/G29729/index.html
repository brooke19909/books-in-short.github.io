Do you know who created the first globe? When was it invented and who is the author

One of the greatest discoveries in geography is the invention of the globe, with the help of which it is easier to remember the location of oceans, seas, continents, islands, tropical forests, ice deserts, etc. Subsequently, this amazing subject has been improved by numerous scientists all over the world. He has his own ancient and rather fascinating history.

Who created the first globe? Around this invention, passions still boil.

What is a globe?

The globe from the Latin word globus means ball.

This image of the map on the surface of the ball, preserving the similarity of the contours and the ratio of sizes (areas). There are geographical globes that display the surface of the Earth, the lunar surface, celestial globes, etc.

Globe History

The first globe appeared even before our era (II-century), and it was created by one inventor who was very fond of poetry. It was a learned philologist and philosopher named Krates Malosky. For days he could listen to the poem "Odyssey", and often after listening to it, he plotted on the map all the routes along which the protagonist walked. And at that time it was already known about the spherical shape of the earth, so he painted the ball.

Although this subject corresponded to the level of knowledge of those times, it was a real globe. He was perfectly appreciated by his contemporaries, but for several centuries, who was the author of the first globe was forgotten.

In 1492, another globe was created in the city of Nuremberg (Germany) with the goal of visualizing the geographical discoveries of Portuguese sailors. Thus, the scientist Martin Beheim received the title of the first inventor of the globe.

That globe was called the "Earth apple." It represented a ball made of metal, no larger than 50 cm in diameter. It should be noted that the continent of America was still absent on it, in connection with its discovery by Columbus at a later time. Also on the globe there were no latitudes and longitudes, however, there were tropics and meridians, and there was a short description of countries. Now the first globe (1492) is stored in the Nuremberg Museum.



Since that time to the present day, a large number of the most unique, even unexpected, globes have been created, which have amazing sizes, designs and materials. But two of these specimens cannot be omitted here: the largest and most unusual and oldest.

Who created the first globe - the largest in the world

The American company DeLorme has created a giant globe Eartha. This organization is developing maps and GPS navigation systems.

The diameter of the globe is 12.6 meters, which is the height of a 4-storey building. Now this unique creation is located in the city of Yarmouth in America.



The giant globe is a 792 fragment of a map fixed between itself by hidden bolts on a large frame. The last element was built from 6 thousand aluminum pipes. The peculiarity of this magnificent building is also that it is placed in a glass building and is illuminated from the inside, which gives it an unusual look.

This masterpiece is listed in the Guinness Book of Records.

Scientists have the assumption that this globe was created in the workshop of Leonardo da Vinci, as there are certain sketches inherent in the works of the great artist. Depicted on this subject are continents signed in Latin, various animals, and even a man-sailor who crashed.
