Hotel Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Side, Colakli): photo, description of rooms, service, reviews of tourists

In recent years, many luxury hotels have been built in Turkey, offering their guests high-quality service. Of course, a ticket to such a complex will be expensive, but there will be a lot of positive impressions. One such respectable hotel is the Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa in Side, which will be discussed in this article. Let us consider in more detail the infrastructure of the complex, its number of rooms, as well as the reviews of guests who have been here before.

Where is this hotel located?

The large resort Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa 5 * is located on the outskirts of Side. To be more precise, it was built in the miniature village of Cholakly, fully focused on the needs of tourists. It is here that along the coast there is a whole chain of expensive five-star hotels. The village is also famous for its clean beaches. This resort is perfect for tourists who do not like to leave the hotel without the need. The local infrastructure is fully focused on a family relaxing holiday. The center of olaklƒ± is about 2 km from the hotel. If desired, tourists can easily get to Side, where there are Turkish bazaars, night clubs, restaurants and bars. You can get to it by bus or taxi in about 30-60 minutes. It is 11 km away.

In their reviews, many tourists note that the beach is far removed from the hotel, which, of course, is considered a lack of location. To get to it, tourists will have to walk about 550 meters. The coast is separated from the hotel by road, which also complicates the journey. At the same time, the complex does not provide a shuttle service to the beach.

Antalya International Airport is also significantly removed from the hotel - it is 55 km from Cholakli. Therefore, tourists need to take care of the transfer in advance. As a rule, it is paid even when buying a ticket. Then guests will be picked up from the airport by a comfortable bus that will quickly deliver them to the desired hotel.

General information you need to know for future hotel guests

The Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa 5 * complex is considered a new hotel, because it was built recently - in 2013. It occupies a very small territory, on which the main infrastructure facilities are compactly located. Its area is 7400 square meters. m. Tourists can stay in one of three five-story buildings, built in a modern style. In total, the hotel offers 270 comfortable and spacious rooms. The complex is considered a crowded place to relax, because more than 500 guests can live here at the same time.

The hotel has standard check-in and accommodation rules for many hotels. For example, you cannot come here with any pets, even guide dogs. But the complex welcomes guests with small children: they are given substantial discounts on accommodation. The hotel is often visited by tourists from Russia, so you can always find a Russian-speaking administrator here who will help tourists who do not speak English with filling out documents or solving any problems. The time of eviction from the hotel rooms is also standard - after the end of the rest you need to leave them before noon.

Room Description Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa

And although this hotel is considered a five-star, it does not offer a large selection of room categories. Most of them are considered standard. They are designed for 3-4 adults and one child under 12 years old. In their reviews, guests note that all rooms at Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa are spacious and comfortable. They consist, as expected, of a bedroom and a bath. Ceramic tiles or laminate are laid on the floor. Everywhere there is also an open balcony, which overlooks the garden, pool or nearby buildings. The standard room is 35 square meters. m

For guests are also offered superior family apartments, which can simultaneously accommodate 6 adults at once. In terms of their equipment, they practically do not differ, but occupy a large area - 55 sq. M. m. The hotel also has three rooms equipped with facilities for physically disabled guests. It is worth noting that all living rooms, as expected, are cleaned daily by hotel staff. But bed linen and towels are changed only twice a week.

Room Amenities

A significant advantage of the Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa tourists consider modern room repair. Since the complex was recently built, furniture, plumbing, and equipment have not yet become outdated. However, the rooms have pretty standard equipment. The furniture has beds, including single beds, a desk, chairs and armchairs, wardrobes and bedside tables for clothes, mirrors. On the wall you can see a plasma TV with a large diagonal, to which satellite TV is connected. Among the channels you can find several Russian speakers. Each room is necessarily equipped with an individual split-system with a control panel.

A minibar is also provided free of charge, and staff replenishes it with daily drinking water. The rooms have wireless internet access. To access it, you need to contact the administrator, who will give you a password. In the bathroom, guests can find a mirror, a built-in hairdryer, towels, as well as a standard set of bath accessories. However, not all equipment is provided to guests free of charge. For example, to use a safe for storing valuables, you will need to pay an additional amount, which you can find out from the administrator. The same goes for using a wired telephone.

Infrastructure and service at Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa

Many tourists who come here on vacation do not like to leave the hotel without good reason. Therefore, for them everything necessary for a comfortable stay is equipped here. However, some services are provided for a fee.

All hotel guests can use the following infrastructure facilities:

  • a business center with its own conference room for up to 100 delegates;
  • mini market and souvenir shop;
  • free car parking for all guests without prior reservation, you can also rent a car at the reception or ask the administrator to order a taxi;
  • hairdresser - provides its services for a fee;
  • laundry;
  • doctor‚Äôs office - for a fee, you can also call a doctor directly to your room.

In addition, free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby.

Food concept

Meals at Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa, like many other hotels in Turkey, are provided on an all-inclusive basis. It includes not only required meals, ice cream and light snacks, but an unlimited number of local soft drinks and spirits. All of them are poured into glass glasses. The menu of the main restaurant consists of European, Turkish cuisine. All food is served at the buffet. Vegetarian, diet and children's menus are also available. For a fee, you can purchase imported alcohol, as well as freshly squeezed juices.

In addition to the main restaurant, the hotel has four bars. They are located on the beach, by the pool and in the lobby of the complex. If you wish, you can also rent a banquet hall.

Beach vacation

Often, just for an unhurried vacation at sea, tourists come to the Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa. It has its own beach, so you do not need to pay for entering it. Sun loungers and parasols are also provided free of charge. If necessary, at the reception you can even get towels. The beach here is completely sandy, so you can walk along it without special shoes. The entrance to the sea is gentle, without boulders, which is also an advantage for parents with small children. As expected, the beach has a water entertainment center, a volleyball court and a bar.

It was already noted above that the beach is half a kilometer away from the hotel, so some tourists do not rest on the coast, but by the pools. There are two of them in the hotel. Both of them are open and filled with fresh water, which is not additionally heated. Next to them there is a set of water slides on which even children can ride. There is also a sun terrace by the pools. Here you can take a sunbed or umbrella for free.

What else can you do at the hotel?

Different tourists come to this hotel, so entertainment at the Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa is here for every taste. Outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy volleyball, table tennis or darts. Free access to the modern gym. If you want to improve your health, then pay attention to the local spa. At the same time, part of its services is included in the cost of rest. For example, a sauna or a Turkish bath. For a fee, you can also visit the steam room or jacuzzi. In addition, the hotel has a daily team of animators who entertain tourists day and night by conducting a variety of thematic events.

What conditions does the hotel offer tourists with young children?

Often, for their vacation, tourists with young children choose the Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa. They like that the complex provides such guests with discounts on accommodation. In addition, all the conditions for their comfortable accommodation are created here. For example, guests with babies may request an extra cot in the room. It is provided free of charge, but it is better to check with the administration in advance. In the restaurant you can always find high chairs for convenient feeding of babies. And if necessary, a nanny can be called into the room, including one who speaks Russian.

A set of entertainment for small guests is quite standard. The hotel has a small shallow pool with fresh water, as well as a playground with a sandbox and swings. For children over 4 years old, a mini club is open daily. Animators are ready for several hours to take care of all the babies, entertaining them with contests and conducting recreational activities.

Positive reviews

Reviews about Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa are mostly positive. Tourists liked the rest in this place, they believe that the level of service corresponds to the cost of living. In addition, guests would love to come back here again. In their opinion, this complex has the following advantages:

  • clean rooms with fresh repair, which are carefully cleaned daily;
  • usually European tourists rest at the hotel, therefore, they do not have noisy parties with alcohol, it is always calm in the territory;
  • the water in the sea is very clean, the territory of the beach is also constantly monitored, removing garbage;
  • the hotel staff is very friendly and tries to quickly solve the problems of guests;
  • the animation team arranges interesting events, but it is not imposed on tourists who do not want to attend them.

Negative reviews

Of course, it was not without criticism, although tourists consider most of the shortcomings to be insignificant. Nevertheless, the hotel owners, according to the guests, should pay attention to the following shortcomings:

  • a small selection of meat dishes, fruits and desserts in the restaurant;
  • free internet has low speed, so it is difficult to use it even for browsing social networks;
  • the territory of the hotel is very small, so there is no place to walk here;
  • low quality local alcohol;
  • Sun beds on the beach are not enough for all tourists.

Thus, we can say that the resort complex Bone Club Sunset Hotel & Spa is suitable primarily for a relaxing holiday with your family. It can be recommended to those tourists who like to spend most of their time in the hotel.
