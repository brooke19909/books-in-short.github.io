Vasilievsky island - the pearl of the city



Vasilievsky island is the main pearl of the city. By the number of attractions concentrated here, the island is far ahead of the rest of the city. On its territory the whole history of the Russian state was reflected. At one time, admired by the beauty of the island, Peter I wanted to make it look like Venice and declare it the center of the city. And although this plan has not been implemented, the island is one of the most interesting and unusual places in the city. That is why hotels on Vasilievsky Island are never empty. St. Petersburg offers here for visitors a lot of hotels and hostels of different levels of stardom and comfort. Thanks to this, each guest of the city has the opportunity to choose the option of accommodation that suits him specifically.
