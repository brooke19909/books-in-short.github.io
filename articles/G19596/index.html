Description and photos of the hotel "Park-Hotel" (Izhevsk). Hotel-Park: reviews of tourists

An ideal accommodation option for those who are traveling for business or leisure purposes to Izhevsk - Hotel Park. Here you will be offered luxury apartments with all necessary amenities.





Brief description of the hotel

Not only picky tourists, but also quite demanding guests arrive in Izhevsk. Hotel-Park offers luxurious apartments decorated in a classic style with Baroque decorative elements. It is worth noting that all rooms are equipped with a large number of modern amenities that will ensure a comfortable stay even for the most fastidious guest. Separately, it is worth noting the beautiful view from the window.

If you arrived on vacation in Izhevsk (Hotel-Park), then you do not have to worry about your leisure time. Here you can take advantage of wireless internet, swim in the indoor heated pool, play billiards, sing karaoke and take a steam bath in the sauna. Special attention should be paid to the spa and wellness center. If you arrived in the city by your own car, you can use the private secure parking.

Naturally, you can find many restaurants in such a large city as Izhevsk. Park-Hotel has one of the best establishments in the city. Here, not only traditional Russian dishes are served, but also foreign dishes, which you can order directly to your room. If you want to drink your favorite drinks at the bar, then this opportunity is provided to you around the clock.





Izhevsk, "Hotel-Park": address

If you want to relax in silence, in the lap of nature, then this hotel is just perfect for you. To the address: st. 5th km of the Yakshur-Bodinsky tract, d. 2 - located "Park Hotel" (Izhevsk). Hotel staff will tell you how to get there if you contact them upon arrival in the city. The easiest way is to take a taxi or get to your destination by public transport. You can also order a transfer. The road from the hotel to the airport will take about 40 minutes. And after 10 minutes you can get to the state circus or to St. Michael's Cathedral.

Negative reviews

Rest in this institution is characterized by the following negative aspects:

  • reception staff are not very welcoming guests;
  • the rooms are not very carefully cleaned, and therefore the smell of dust is felt;
  • there are problems with the mixer in the bathroom (cold and hot water change dramatically);
  • there are no special shelves for cosmetics in the bathroom;
  • in the hallway there is a lack of hangers for outerwear (it is very inconvenient to constantly hide it in a closet);
  • inconvenient location - it is possible to get only by taxi (or if you ordered a transfer);
  • piles of building materials spoil the beautiful area around the hotel;
  • there are no cafes or shops nearby;
  • poor soundproofing in the rooms.

General impression

Park Hotel in Izhevsk is a luxurious establishment where every guest will feel like an important person. Here, even standard rooms are characterized by a beautiful interior decorated in an intelligent classic style. True, the equipment is not entirely thought out, since there are not enough shelves and hangers. Breakfast is included in the price, as well as a half-hour visit to the pool (however, as guests say, this opportunity is not always possible). It is impossible not to say about the most beautiful territory with a unique landscape design.

As for the location of the hotel, this is both a virtue and a disadvantage. The hotel is located in a forested area on the outskirts of the city. There are no public transport stops, and therefore you can get here only by taxi or hotel car (for a fee). Nevertheless, such a location gives you the opportunity to relax in silence and breathe in the fresh forest air. And since outdoor recreation is always appetizing, you will certainly appreciate the fact that guests are provided with a rich breakfast.

Thus, the Park-Hotel hotel (Izhevsk) is suitable for business people who want to relax in the lap of nature from the city bustle, as well as for tourists who wish to spend time in the fresh air.
