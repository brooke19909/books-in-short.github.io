Hotels with a view of the Eiffel Tower in Paris: hotel overview, room selection, quality of service

Paris is a city visited by millions of tourists. The most popular attraction in the capital of France is the Eiffel Tower. There are a large number of hotels overlooking the symbol of Paris. They will be discussed in this article.

Accommodation options

Paris hotels overlooking the Eiffel Tower vary in quality of service and equipment. The most budget options are hotels with a 2 star rating. Here you can stay in small rooms with a shower and toilet. According to reviews, the food in such hotels is rather modest: for breakfast you will be offered a croissant with butter or jam, as well as a cup of coffee or tea.

There are 3 star hotels offering continental or American breakfast in the morning. The second option is more nutritious. The rooms have a toilet and bathrooms, and in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are parking lots. Tourists advise to pay attention to the fact that the cost of living in a hotel directly depends on its distance from the metro station.

The most common 4- and 5-star hotels in Paris near the Eiffel Tower. In hotels with a star rating of 4, wealthy travelers stay. The spacious rooms are air-conditioned and have a minibar. According to reviews, the quality of service is very high. Breakfast is buffet style. Most often, hotels have saunas and pools with heated water, but this information needs to be clarified on the official websites of hotels.

The 5-star hotels are considered the best. Guests stay in enormous French-style rooms. The apartments are equipped with not only air conditioning and TVs, but also computers. You can control the equipment using tablets on the bedside tables. According to reviews, this class of hotels has fitness rooms, bars and restaurants. The service is top notch. Experienced travelers are advised to reserve a room in advance.
