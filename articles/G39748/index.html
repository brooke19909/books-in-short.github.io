What is a vacation in Kazakhstan? First of all, it is picturesque landscapes, unity with nature, peace and tranquility. All this can be found in the village of Bayanaul (Kazakhstan). It is a district center of Pavlodar region. Its population is approximately six thousand people. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe local park is about seventy hectares. The village is located near the Bayanaul mountains.



General information

The climate in the region of the Bayanaul Mountains is mostly continental. This is due to the fact that this area is located in the very center of the Asian continent. The average temperature in the winter period, as a rule, does not exceed the mark of -14 o C. In the summer, this territory stably holds +14 degrees. Sometimes the temperature can rise above +30 . Sandstorms are typical for Pavlodar region. However, they do not reach the territory of the Bayanaul district. In this area, strong winds are unusual.

Local nature

The highest point of Banaul is Mount Akbet. Its height is more than a thousand meters. Majestic mountain ranges can be seen already at the entrance to the village. Mostly coniferous forests grow there. The local nature can impress any traveler. The tourist‚Äôs gaze opens with an amazing contrast: all the way he saw around only the steppe expanses, and then suddenly the most beautiful landscapes that Kazakhstan is so famous for appear before him.

Bayanaul, rest: path to the mountains

The road passes through the village and further leads upward. It continues to the ecological post. It is a small booth with a barrier. In order to drive further, you need to pay. About 350 tenge will be taken from each person. Stopping at the post does not take much time from travelers. Directions will be open immediately upon payment.



Further road

After the ecological post is left behind, you need to go a little more upstairs. The climb is not very steep. During the journey you can admire the massive mountain boulders, which now and then flicker along the sides of the road. There are also pine trees, which have rather unusual forms. There are suggestions that this is due to the influence of the sun. It, in turn, is much closer here than on the plain expanses. A steep helical descent replaces the climb after about two hundred meters of the path. He is famous for the whole Bayanaul serpentine. From here, the traveler offers stunning views. You can see the holiday homes, which are located on the shores of Lake Dzhasyby. Small ripples above the water can be seen even from this site. The lake itself is very clean and transparent. On the sides of all this beauty are majestic mountains. Many roads in Bayanaul are well paved. In general, their quality is up to standard. There are no dangerous sections and potholes, therefore, any car with even low ground clearance will easily pass here.



Bayanaul: recreation areas

"Birch" is one of the most popular camp sites in the village. The complex is located on the left bank of Jasybai Lake. Visitors can stay in a five-story building. They have the opportunity to admire from the balconies the picturesque views that surround this recreation area. Bayanaul is considered a village with a developed tourist service, offered at relatively affordable prices.

Accommodation at the camp site

Tourists are provided with double rooms with the main range of amenities. The room has a wardrobe, table, chairs, beds. Visitors can also stay in a two-story building. In order to enter the hotel, you need to climb the iron stairs. The complex has double and triple rooms. They have a basic set of furniture - bedside tables, beds, a table and chairs. Five meals a day are provided for guests of the complex.

Services

The complex itself is located on the lake. Its territory is fenced around the perimeter. The complex is guarded around the clock. There is a private beach nearby. The holiday home also has its own boat station, dance floor and children's area. A boat station, a dance floor, a kids club - all this is located in the territory occupied by the recreation area. Bayanaul is famous not only for its picturesque nature. Here, every tourist will find something to their liking. According to reviews, this complex is the best recreation area. Bayanaul annually receives a huge number of tourists in the summer. Jasybai Lake

It is clearly visible from the top of the serpentine. There are many legends about Lake Dzhasyby. It is believed that it was named after the Kazakh batyr, whose name was Zhasybai. Legend has it that this valiant warrior died on the lake during a battle. The water here is very cold. The lake fills the pit between the mountain ranges. You can get used to the temperature of the water. However, in some places swimming is not recommended. The water in the lake is clear. Here you can often find ducks. It is believed that many snakes live in the local territories, so it is better not to lose vigilance. The beaches of Bayanaul are especially popular. Here you can always rent a rowing boat or catamaran.



Finally

In Bayanaul everyone can find something to their liking. First of all, here is expanse for outdoor enthusiasts. Extreme sportsmen can try climbing. Fans of a more relaxing holiday can take walks along mountain paths, ride bicycles and swim in the lake. This place is also suitable for families with children. The weather does not upset with surprises, and the local landscapes do not leave anyone indifferent. Positive impressions will remain with visitors for a long time.
