Accommodation

For those who came to rest in the resort village of Kabardinka, the Primorsky boarding house offers accommodation in a 5-storey building with a capacity of 180 beds. There are 82 double and triple rooms in the building, 20 of which have a view of the sea coast. Each has a bathroom, shower, air conditioning and TV. There is a refrigerator, but it is designed for two rooms. Each room can accommodate one extra bed upon prior reservation. There is a loggia with a beautiful view of the sea or the mountains. Cold water in the rooms is constantly. Hot water is supplied on schedule. For families with children, accommodation is permitted if the latter have reached the age of four.
