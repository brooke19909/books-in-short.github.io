Realistic game world: how to make a book for dolls

Girls at a young age are very fond of playing with dolls. They seem to create a new world there, and to make it more like the real one, it needs to be supplemented with details from ordinary life. Learn how to make a book for dolls, then the puppet world of the child will become at least slightly, but more realistic.





Glue book

Before you learn how to make a book for dolls, you need to prepare materials:

  • Sheets of white paper or lined (for a puppet notebook), sheets with text (for a book).
  • Paper napkin.
  • PVA glue.
  • Cardboard.
  • Cover material (leather, fabric, paper, foil, etc.).
  • Paints.
  • Ruler.
  • Pencil.
  • Scissors.

What do we have to do:

  1. From the paper, cut the pages of the required size (about 1.5 by 2 centimeters).
  2. Put them together to form a book. Coat the future crust with glue and apply a piece of cloth.
  3. Make cardboard cover details out of cardboard. Two large rectangles the size of the book itself and one small the size of a crust.
  4. From the material prepared for the cover, cut the pieces 3-5 mm more than the prepared cardboard. Leave a small gap between the cover and the crust so that the book can open and close. Glue the protruding parts inwards.
  5. Make an flyleaf, for this you need to cut out two rectangles the size of a book from paper, bend them both in half and stick one side of each rectangle on the inside of the cover so that the second side can then be glued to the main sheets.
  6. Fold the cover. The fold lines should have turned out. If the cover is made of leather, then they are probably not very noticeable. Therefore, the relief can be created by extruding it with a spoke.
  7. Paste the sheets into the book.
  8. To make the appearance of the finishing braid, glue the thread from the bottom and top in the place where the sheets come into contact with the spine.

Having learned how to make a book for dolls, you can begin to experiment.

The insides of the book

If you do not have the opportunity to print pictures for the book, then use the magazines. In many places there is advertising. Just these little advertising records will help you. Use booklets from different stores. They are small, and the manufacturer is trying to fit as many examples of their products as possible, from which the size of the images is small. How to make a book for dolls "Monster High"? To do this, paste pictures of your favorite characters on the cover.

Book on a thread





In the first version of how to make a book for dolls, the craft rests on glue. It is not as durable as if you were stapling sheets. Another way to create:

  1. Roll sheets for the book in half and put into each other. Create approximately 4 blocks of sheets.
  2. Now sew together the sheets of each block, and then the blocks themselves together.
  3. Stick a napkin on the spine.
  4. Then proceed as in the first embodiment.

House book

How to make a house book for dolls? You will need:

  • Thick cardboard.
  • Colored paper.
  • Pencils, paints, felt-tip pens.
  • Glue.
  • Wallpaper (optional)
  • Scissors.
  • Fabric (optional).
  • PVA glue.

Working process:

  1. Fold the sheets of cardboard in half and glue the roots. It will turn out 8 small rooms or 4 large.
  2. Decorate the walls. To do this, glue the wallpaper or paper.
  3. Draw a window. From the fabric, cut out the right-sized rectangles. Glue to the window, it turns out the curtains. Can be replaced with paper.
  4. Draw the door.
  5. To make paintings on the wall, draw them or cut out suitable images from the magazine.
  6. For the floor, cardboard or old pieces of linoleum are suitable. Cut them to fit the room. You can stick a painted carpet on top.
  7. Now put the doll furniture you have when the game is over, remove it and close the book. The floor will also be cleaned.
  8. You can draw more details: a floor lamp, a flower, a mirror, a wardrobe and so on.




With this knowledge you can make the puppet world of a child more realistic!
