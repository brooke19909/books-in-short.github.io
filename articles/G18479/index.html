Dubai Hotels with Private Beach: Reviews

Going on vacation in the UAE, tourists fall into a real oriental tale. Every resort place in this country is an impeccable standard of comfort. There, even in the little things, luxury reigns. At a high level, hotels in Dubai with a private beach and European service offer a vacation. Dubai's beachfront hotel complexes are recognized as the best in the world. They are incredibly popular with travelers.

Jumeirah Hotels

The coast of Jumeirah is famous for its sandy beaches, rich in shopping and sports and entertainment centers. Luxury hotels in Dubai with their own beach are concentrated here. Jumeirah is the prestigious quarter of Dubai, dotted with luxury hotels equipped with heated pools, wellness rooms, spas and fitness rooms.





They have yacht clubs, golf courses, and tennis courts. Guests are invited to visit the races, go on excursions with sightseeing, take part in safari and falconry, make shopping. The doors of restaurants, night clubs, bars and discos are hospitably open for them.

Jumeirah Beach Hotel in Jumeirah

Jumeirah Beach Hotel is Dubai's most famous hotel complex with original architecture. The shape of the structure resembles a giant wave. The hotel, which has its own spotless beach, is famous for its perfect service and excellent equipment for outdoor activities. The 20 restaurants serve excellent cuisine.

The hotel has about 300 luxury apartments. The entire room fund of the prestigious hotel is equipped with panoramic windows, which offer stunning views of the picturesque Persian Gulf. The hotel has five swimming pools and a climbing wall. At the pools and on the beach, the staff delivers ice cream and offers to refresh the face with napkins soaked in fruit extracts.





Moevenpick Hotel Jumeirah Beach in Jumeirah

The Movenpic and other Dubai hotels with their own beach are located in the exclusive Jumeirah Beach Residence. This presentable hotel is surrounded by famous shopping complexes. Complimentary amenities include spa treatments, beach kits, fruit in the apartment and WiFi.

Children under 10 years of age eat for free. The menu in the restaurants is updated every day. The apartments have balconies with panoramic views of the coast. On the beach, staff provides tourists with unlimited towels, umbrellas and water.

Burj al arab

The outlines of the Burj Al Arab hotel complex are like a sail. To the pearl of the UAE - the 56-story Sail Hotel - adjoins three beaches with landscaped areas. For sunbeds, umbrellas and towels from the hotel guests are not charged. Each floor of the hotel has its own reception desk. This hotel complex offers a free shuttle service to central Dubai, where large shopping malls are crowded.





5 * Beach Hotels in Dubai

Prestigious Dubai hotels with a private 5-star beach surround guests with comfort, providing a cloudless vacation. They have Russian-speaking staff. Service in 5 * hotels is beyond praise. Television broadcast Russian channels. In their environment, every little thing has been thought out, right down to the needle and thread. Moreover, each hotel complex has individual features.

For example, there are hotels in the halls of which there are corners with tropical gardens. On a snow-white beach adjacent to the Habtoor Grand Resort & Spa, guests use the services of a steam room, sauna, spa rooms. They are pampered with delicious dishes and drinks in a dozen bars and restaurants.

The Sheraton Hotel is bordered by a beach strewn with white sand. The hotel complex's restaurants serve international dishes. Guests relax by the pool, in the sauna and hammam, do fitness in the gym with exercise equipment. Elite Dubai hotels with their own beach, including the Sheraton Hotel, provide guests with the opportunity to use the shuttle service to large shopping complexes.

At the Hilton Hotel, guests enjoy stunning panoramic views of the picturesque bay, and use the sauna and steam room for free. On the fenced beach there are umbrellas and sunbeds, a swimming pool is equipped. The bars serve refreshing drinks and light snacks. There are often staged promotions that give guests the right to enjoy free meals during lunches and dinners.

5 * Hotels with Family Beach in Dubai





Luxury hotels in Dubai with their own 5-star beach and for the rest with children took care of the provision of a nanny. Children under 12 years old stay free of charge (unless they have a separate bed). A bed for a child can be ordered for a fee. Children are entertained by animators and specialists in children's clubs, playrooms and nannies on the playgrounds.

On the beaches for children, special areas have been allocated, often protected, with gentle entrances to the water. Pools for children are equipped with special areas. The restaurants have a special menu for younger guests.

3 * Beach Hotels in Dubai

Cozy hotels in Dubai with a private beach (3 stars) are among the prestigious hotel complexes. The hotels scattered along the sea coast are distinguished by high-level service and excellent apartments. In the beach triples, the traditional list of sports and recreational activities is complemented by water activities. In 3 * hotels, alcohol is usually not sold.

The 3-star beachfront 3-star hotel Montreal has settled almost in the central part of Dubai, in the territory of the Deira shopping district. Nearby are restaurants and supermarkets. The hotel's restaurants have live music and international dishes. The flat roof space is reserved for an outdoor pool. Guests relaxing by the pool overlook the beauties of Dubai. Wellness treatments are organized in the fitness center, sauna and massage parlor.

In the hotel "Regent Beach Resort 3 *", adjacent to the beach, rest is relatively cheap. Guests are accommodated in two-story ten-bedroom villas. Meals are available at the Indian restaurant. Guests relax by the heated pool and in the gym with exercise equipment.





Private parking and a wellness center, as well as beach towels, are provided free of charge. This hotel and other hotels in Dubai with its own beach is adjacent to a large supermarket and a series of numerous restaurants. Adherents of nightlife have fun at a disco. Opposite the hotel complex Regent Beach Resort 3 * is a zoo.

Hotel Ramada Deira 3 * is located next to the shopping center Reef. Its territory is equipped with a sauna and a fitness center. Wi-Fi is free in public areas. The tour desk is open 24 hours a day and can arrange shuttle and car rental services. On the beach you have to pay for sunbeds and umbrellas.

Dubai Beach Hotel Reviews

Luxury accommodation provides Dubai hotels with their own beach. The reviews of tourists are a reliable confirmation that the quality of service at unattainable heights in prestigious hotels in the Emirates. Public areas and apartments are distinguished by their unusual design, unique atmosphere of coziness and comfort. Walking from comfortable buildings to well-maintained beaches takes a matter of minutes.





Vacationers note the high cost of living in beach hotels in Dubai (it can reach $ 2000 per day). However, for the money they provide a vacation worthy of the eastern sheikhs. Relaxing in luxury hotels in the UAE with fantastic infrastructure and marvelous nature, tourists realize that paradise places really are on earth.
