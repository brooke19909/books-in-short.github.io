Butterfly Bead - Glass Grace

Beadwork is familiar to every girl, because since childhood everyone wove unpretentious bracelets and cute beads from multi-colored glass beads, trying to add a beautiful and bright accessory to their image. But it was so long ago that today for girls and women weaving from beads seems to be an empty and worthless occupation, a children's hobby. But in vain!

Weaving from beads is a very profitable business, if you approach it from a commercial point of view. Today, society has almost departed from stereotypes in its desire to create uniqueness in everything: clothing, hairstyle, interior, accessories. All this is excellent, let us return to beadwork. And remember the beautiful insects with which you can also associate carefree childhood memories.

Butterflies from beads - it is beautiful, it is bright, it is unique. Why not play around with these elements in everyday life. Today there are too many dark shades in everything, but bright colors please the eyes . So, the pattern of weaving butterfly beads is very simple. There are hundreds of bright butterflies on the Internet that have so gently opened their beautiful wings.

Beaded butterflies are best woven using wire. For those familiar with techniques such as beadwork, wire butterflies will immediately seem preferable. This is due to the fact that the structure of the butterfly will be more rigid, they will take exactly the form that its creator will ask. If stringing beads on a fishing line, you can create a three-dimensional figure, but from this the wings of the butterfly will become thick, saggy and unpretentious. Definitely not these epithets appropriated by such beautiful insects. Therefore, if you want to create a butterfly from beads, stock up on wire. When weaving this fragile insect, the simplest scheme is used. No need to master any technology, every girl since childhood weaved such figures. Therefore, difficulties in creating butterflies should not arise.

It is worth considering what color the creation will be. Will it fly from the rainforests or will it be a subject of fantasy and imagination? All these are trifles that you should not pay attention to. Much more important are those feelings when a person creates the beautiful. After all, aesthetic education is in the highest place, and weaving from beads is the development of craving for beauty.

It is worth paying attention to where then the created insect will be used. There is no need to argue for a long time. There are many options:

1. Create a beautiful brooch that can be pinned to a dress or summer T-shirt. Butterfly beads that wave their wings reverently on their shoulders or chest on a sunny day will be a wonderful highlight in casual wear.

2. Add small beaded butterflies to the hairstyle. Let them settle on the spit and sparkle when exposed to sunlight, creating a beautiful crown from their creator.

3. Place several winged creatures on the computer monitor, and let everyone know that a charming girl who appreciates comfort is sitting behind him. In addition, such a glass insect will cheer up anyone who catches your eye.

4. Decorate the mirrors with beaded butterflies that can be entered into any interior and create with their help not only a unique design, but also a great mood.

5. Decorate the lampshade on the bedside table with a dozen butterflies with different wings. Not only the intimate atmosphere will surround the room, but also the tropical atmosphere, which will always be there.

Thousands of ideas can be chosen, the main thing is to have inspiration and imagination, and then the bead butterflies will settle in your house on every corner, look at your neighbors and friends and give an excellent, sunny mood all year round, which will lighten the smiles on the faces of others. Do not hesitate for a long time to hesitate, grab the child by the hand and go straight to the needlework store.
