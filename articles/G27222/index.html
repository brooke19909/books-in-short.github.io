Friendship of Peoples Monument (Izhevsk, Republic of Udmurtia)

The monument "Friendship of Peoples" (Izhevsk) is also known under the name "Forever with Russia." It was erected in honor of the 400th anniversary of the entry of Udmurtia into the Russian Federation in 1972. It is a symbol of the republic and the city of Izhevsk in particular. We learn many interesting facts about him later in the article.

Prerequisites for the creation of the monument

The monument ‚ÄúFriendship of Peoples‚Äù (Izhevsk) arose in the minds of designers as a plan in 1958, when they wanted to make a special holiday surprise for citizens on the 400th anniversary of the unification of Udmurtia and Russia. We decided to build this grandiose monument, which later became a symbol of unity of two groups of citizens. For a long time without delay, they laid a stone-symbol, which was at the point where the Hall of Fame now stands.

However, they turned from words to deeds already in the late 60s. The project organizers wanted the statue to be 52 meters high. It was supposed to be attended by four sculptures symbolizing universal peace, the equality of people, fruitful work and steps to the present. However, the aviation of the Soviet Union had its own requirements, according to which on a structure reaching a height of 50 m, it would be necessary to install lights that would warn flying planes about the presence of such a tall building. The monument "Friendship of Peoples" (Izhevsk) then would not look as impressive, beautiful and impressive as the developers intended.





Decision

I had to sacrifice a high relief that depicted modernity. Thus, the height was reduced to 46 meters, which allowed not to impose unnecessary responsibility on themselves. In addition, from the very beginning two low steles were to participate in the construction, they were an integral part of the monument. They wanted to decorate them with mosaics of different colors made of smalt.

Drawings on them were supposed to tell about the historical events taking place in Udmurtia. Drawings approved in Moscow. However, the absence of steles can be explained by a very banal circumstance that arose before the developers: there was not enough money.

Interesting promotion

May 9 Izhevsk celebrates Victory Day, like many cities of the Russian Federation. Near the monument last year a time capsule was laid . Letters are stored inside, which in the future will be read by the one to whom the lines are addressed. When the 100th anniversary of the victory will be celebrated, the papers will be seized and reach their recipients.



Many people took part in this interesting action. The paper was wrapped in triangles, marked the addressees and indicated the open or closed form of the message. In the first case, the content will be read and published, and in the second - delivered to the destination.

Messages are packed in plastic film and buried in a weapon box, which has a length of 1.3 meters. It is processed by means that do not allow paper and wood to rot. The next protective layer is the sarcophagus and its granite lid. On them you can find the date of closing and opening, as well as a logo symbolizing the 70th anniversary of the Great Victory.
