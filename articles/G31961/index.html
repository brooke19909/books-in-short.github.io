Green LightHouse Hotel 4 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): description, service, reviews

Green LightHouse Hotel 4 * is a luxury hotel, which is located just minutes from the beach. You can also quickly get to the airport (only 25 km). And literally at arm's length is the Ksom Moi market, 2/4 square, a railway station and a bus stop. However, a convenient location is far from the only pleasant feature of this hotel.





What is in the territory?

Green LightHouse Hotel 4 * has everything that can only be useful to tourists on vacation. There is free Wi-Fi, parking nearby (price - $ 5 per day), an excellent pool with panoramic views. In the lobby, guests are greeted by a 24-hour reception. There is also a currency exchange, tour desk, ticket service and luggage storage.

In addition to the above, the hotel has dry cleaning, laundry, fitness center and, of course, SPA-salon. There, guests are offered a variety of services - from cosmetic procedures (peeling, masks, wraps) to massage (body, arms, head, legs, neck, back).

By the way, the staff working in the hotel speaks not only Vietnamese, but also English.

Nutrition

In Green LightHouse Hotel 4 *, like in any other hotel of a similar level, there is a bar and restaurant. The last guests are offered breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Guests resting here say that the food is excellent. The restaurant itself is cozy and clean, and the menu is varied and changing. So the assortment does not bother even in two weeks.

Fresh vegetables and fruits are offered here, a lot of different pastries, excellent freshly ground coffee, natural juices. A considerable selection of soups and salads, all kinds of dishes from fish, meat and poultry, eggs (boiled, fried, omelettes), granola and cereal with milk. There are also Vietnamese delicacies that will definitely appeal to lovers of spicy and spicy.

In general, as the tourists who were here assure, one breakfast can be eaten so that you do not even want to have a bite to eat at lunch.





Accommodation options

Green LightHouse Hotel 4 * has a total of 100 rooms. And everyone belongs to the category of "Deluxe". They have the same area, in terms of conditions and amenities, they also do not differ at all. But this does not mean that the apartments are bad. On the contrary, each room is decorated in a pleasant, not tasteless style, and the furniture pleases with comfort and novelty.

What in terms of amenities? All rooms have air conditioning, a safe, a wardrobe, a plasma TV with cable channels, a minibar, an electric kettle and a bathroom with a toilet and a bidet. Essential little things like slippers, towels, linen and hygiene items are also provided to each guest without fail.

Cost of living

Now about the price. The hotel offers guests extremely competitive rates. And now, what price does a weekly stay include breakfast, 10% VAT and 5% service charge:

  • 20-25 thousand rubles for two people in the usual "Deluxe".
  • 35 thousand rubles per room with a balcony.
  • 40 thousand rubles if three people live. This apartment has a large double bed and one separate.

It is worth noting that due to such low prices, Green LightHouse Hotel 4 * in Nha Trang is popular, so if you want to stay here, it is worth booking apartments long before the trip.

Recommendations

Many tourists vacationing in Nha Trang, in Green LightHouse Hotel 4 *, like to give advice in their reviews. And some of them really prove to be useful. Here are some things to consider for people who are just about to travel here:

  • If you want to go on a tour of local attractions with a guide, then you should contact the Russian Information Center - in Nha Trang, the office is not far from the hotel. The prices are reasonable, the guides are Russian, everything is interesting and understandable.
  • It is definitely worth a visit to Gorky Park, located on the waterfront. This is a complex of bars, restaurants and pools - entertainment lovers will definitely like it there.
  • Need an exchange office with a favorable rate? Then go to the Angora Treasures jewelry store.
  • Do you want to go somewhere? Better take a taxi. Rates are low, payment by meter. And you need to take a small car, since the larger the car, the more expensive the trip will be.
  • Have a desire to go shopping? Then you need to make your way to LOTTE - a huge supermarket with a lot of boutiques and an impressive selection.

Such simple tips will help tourists who come to Nha Trang for the first time to spend their holidays with benefit, without loss of finance and time. So not only the description of Green LightHouse Hotel 4 * is worth studying - it will not be superfluous to periodically pay attention to reviews.





Good to know

If a person is going to Vietnam and wants to stay at this hotel, then he should take into account some of the nuances. So, firstly, check-in here starts at 14:00. Check out lasts until 12:00. It is important to consider this when selecting a flight.

Secondly, if you need a transfer from the airport, you need to send a corresponding request to the accommodation facility. Better yet, contact the property directly by calling. You need to do this after buying tickets, as you will need to specify the flight data.

Thirdly, the hotel can accommodate children, but extra beds are not available for them, and they are counted as adult guests.

Fourth, when making a remote reservation, you must indicate your credit card number in the application. Subsequently, it is presented upon arrival with a passport. The accommodation facility accepts Visa and MasterCard.
