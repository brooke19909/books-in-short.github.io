Pattern long dress. Sewing a long dress with your own hands

Want to upgrade your wardrobe by summer? Are you going to a party with friends or a gala event? Sew your outfit yourself. You will be irresistible and absolutely emphasize your personality. Similarly, no one will come in such clothes. You will be the queen of the event. Ready-made pattern of a long dress will help you in the realization of your dreams.

Types of Long Dresses

Before choosing a pattern and buying fabric, it is worth considering which option is more suitable and pleasant for you. A long straight dress visually slim, but too tight model will not work if you have a few curvaceous forms. In this case, it is better to choose such styles that go from the chest or have a loose cut and do not fit the figure.

Types of long dresses are as follows:

  • A-line;
  • ball gown;
  • evening;
  • imperial style;
  • caftan;
  • case;
  • high waist;
  • sundress;
  • rural dress;
  • tramp.

For example, dresses of the first kind may diverge downward in the form of the letter A, both from a tight-fitting waist and from above. The ballroom is usually done with an open back and shoulders, as well as a massive bottom. The evening version is designed for special occasions, so it is made of expensive fabrics using finishes. As a rule, it has a floor length, a deep neckline and is characterized by the absence of sleeves. An imperial-style dress is sewn with a high waist and a V-neck. The caftan has a simple shape, so it is easy to sew. The case clearly sits on the figure, emphasizing its shape. Sundress - a summer dress on the straps. The rural version is the embodiment of simplicity. The shape of the tramp in shape resembles a musical instrument - a pipe, since it diverges sharply downward.



Where to begin

To determine the style that you will sew, analyze your skills, and also go to the store to try on. They don‚Äôt take money for this. So you can understand that the most advantageous is sitting on your figure. Remember, color can also greatly influence the impression you make in this outfit.





When choosing a fabric, you should attach a flap to yourself and look in the mirror. If you like a color or pattern on a roll, this does not mean that you will look advantageous in this version.

What is required

It is worth noting that at the preparation stage it is better to perform steps in parallel. After all, you will need not only a model (image), but also a pattern of a long dress. Already on the basis of it (the size of the dress, the number of parts, the method of cutting elements), you need to choose the fabric, given its width, the ability to stretch on the figure, the visual impression of color and pattern.

So, to sew an outfit yourself, you need the following:

  • pattern of a long dress;
  • safety pins (with a head);
  • chalk or remnant with a sharp edge;
  • scissors;
  • thread with a needle;
  • sewing machine (preferably with the function of processing edges with a zigzag seam);
  • iron;
  • decor elements (optional).

Nothing special really needed. If you decide to sew the dress yourself, you probably already have all of the above.

How to sew a dress with your own hands: patterns

The most important thing is to find ready-made templates or build all the details of the product pattern yourself. The figure below shows the construction scheme, using which you can make a pattern for a model with a sleeve in size.





The disadvantage of this method is the need to take measurements, that is, you can‚Äôt just take and print a blank for your, for example, 44th size. However, any template for specific proportions is performed according to average values. If you have too narrow hips or small breasts, you may need to adjust the finished pattern. In this case, it makes sense to build it yourself.

So, in the figure above, the lines indicated by numbers correspond to the following:

  1. Neck (neck).
  2. Chest.
  3. Waist.
  4. Hips.
  5. Bottom edge.

For any symmetrical product, the pattern is built only for half of the front and back. The complete part is obtained by folding the fabric on which the template is fixed. Accordingly, the dimensions from the vertical (axis of symmetry) you will have to put off the calculated ones, that is, determine the size of the hips and mark along the 1/2 half-girth line.

As a basis, you can take the pattern shown in the following image. All dimensions necessary for building are indicated. Relate them to your own and, if necessary, adjust. Do not forget to round the corners after finishing drawing work.





Thus, a pattern of a straight dress, A-shaped, with a high waist and another, can be made.

If you do not like to build on paper, but still want to sew a dress with your own hands, you can take ready-made patterns. If this is a normal screen image, it needs to be scaled to fit its size. Do it by your own standards. For example, the size of the breasts on the pattern matches yours, but the hips are bigger. Print it this way - trimming excess is always easier than building, adjusting, adding.





Please note that the direction of the shared thread is indicated by an arrow on the parts. That is how you need to lay out patterns on fabric. If there is no designation on the template, remember that the details of the front, back, and sleeves are laid out along the shared thread. This should be taken into account when buying fabric, be sure to look at how wide it is, and take the length in accordance with the height of the dress with a margin for allowances and additional details.
