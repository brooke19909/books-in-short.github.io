Plane crash over Sinai: description, causes, number of victims

October 31, 2015 - the date of departure of the Airbus A321-231 airliner from the airport of the resort city of Sharm el-Sheikh. People who rested in Egypt were returning home to Russia on this plane. Landing was to take place in St. Petersburg. However, this was not destined to happen. The plane crashed. All crew members and passengers of the ill-fated flight are victims of the crash over Sinai.

Events Prior to the Tragedy

Shortly before the tragedy, the Airbus A321-231 airliner made two passenger flights. The first and second flights were carried out along the route Egypt (Sharm el-Sheikh) - Russia (Samara) - Egypt (Sharm el-Sheikh). Abroad, the plane landed on the afternoon of October 30. The crew did not have any comments on the airliner.

The next morning, the Airbus A321-231 went through maintenance before the scheduled flights. During it, no problems were detected. The next landing on the flight began. Boarded 192 adult passengers and 25 children. The crew consisted of 7 people.





Plane crash

At 06:50 Moscow time, the airliner flew from the airport of the Egyptian resort city to St. Petersburg. 23 minutes after takeoff, communication with crew members was lost. The plane disappeared from the radar. Search teams advanced to the alleged crash site.

The crashed airliner was found in the central part of the Sinai Peninsula, among the mountains. On a vast territory of more than 20 square meters. km debris and belongings of passengers on board were scattered. People who arrived at the scene of the tragedy heard the sounds of mobile phones. Relatives and friends of the victims called their loved ones, hoping that they were late for the flight.





Versions of the causes of the crash

The international media indicated that the plane could crash due to a technical malfunction. However, the airline Metrojet, which acquired this airliner on lease, has denied this version. Spokesman Alexei Smirnov noted that the plane crash over Sinai could not have happened due to a malfunction, because the plane was checked several days before the tragedy. No problems were found.

The error of the crew is another version that was put forward during the investigation. Metrojet Airlines said that the plane was operated by quite experienced people. The commander of the aircraft was Valery Yuryevich Nemov. He flew over 12 thousand hours. The co-pilot was Sergey Stanislavovich Trukhachev. Its total flying time was 5641 hours.

Explosion on board a makeshift bomb - version confirmed during the investigation. The President of Egypt made a statement that the Russian airliner crashed over the Sinai Peninsula as a result of a terrorist attack. Involvement in the perfect act was announced by ISIS militants.

Victims of the tragedy

The crash over Sinai claimed the lives of 224 people. There were several families on board the crashed plane. On the day of the tragedy, a memorial appeared in Pulkovo Airport in St. Petersburg. People brought flowers, toys, laid candles in memory of those who could not return from the Egyptian resort city to Russia.

The bodies of the dead were delivered to their homeland. The first plane with the victims flew to St. Petersburg on November 2 at about 6 in the morning. There were 144 people aboard this airliner whose lives were claimed by the plane crash over Sinai. The bodies of the dead, found later, personal belongings of people, fragments of bodies were brought to Russia on the following flights.





Identification procedure

After the arrival of aircraft from Egypt with the bodies of the dead, experts began to prepare for the identification procedure. The bodies were gradually given out to relatives and close people. The first funeral took place on November 5th. On this day, in St. Petersburg, they said goodbye to a 31-year-old resident who had a 2-year-old son and wife. In the Novgorod region, a 60-year-old woman who worked at a local school was buried.

The issue of the remains was completed on December 7, 2015 in St. Petersburg. During the events, it was not possible to establish the identities of 7 victims. Those who died in a plane crash over Sinai with the consent of relatives and relatives were buried by unidentified people.
