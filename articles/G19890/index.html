What does computer science study as a science?

There are many sciences in the world that focus on computer technology. One of the most important places belongs to computer science. What it is? What does computer science study as a science and where can one see the fruits of its research? What aspects are she interested in?

What is called computer science?



This is the name of the branch of science that studies the structure of electronic computers, their common properties. This word appeared from a combination of the other two: information and automation, which pretty well makes it clear the interests of this science. The term itself is of French origin. Computer science deals with issues related to the search, collection, transformation, processing, storage, dissemination and use of information in various fields of activity. If you look at all very broadly, then she is interested in everything related to any data, including in wildlife (but these aspects are transferred to biological sciences). If we want to know what computer science is studying, we need to identify the areas where it operates.

What is meant by fields of activity?



This includes almost all types of activity of modern man. So, practical informatics is interested in the work of the secretary-machinist, design engineer, librarian, the use of atomic energy, space exploration and many others. As you can see, the range is very wide. The principles that are used in computer science have found application in both the scientific and everyday spheres of life. How? Remember: any science must collect information, analyze and research it in order to establish connections and patterns. And based on the data obtained, it was possible to create or receive something new. Thanks to the correct organization of information and its effective use, good results can be achieved.

In everyday life of a person, computer science has also found its application. After all, we are looking for data on what timetable the transport is moving to, where it passes, what book we should read at our leisure - and we can continue truly endlessly. Note that we have not even started talking about what computer science is studying! We just go through the fields of activity that interest it. But the application of the principles and provisions of this science can significantly facilitate the work with data and use it as efficiently as possible in order to make the right decisions and get the desired result.

Questions under development

So what does computer science study in practice? She is primarily interested in such questions:

  1. The accumulation, processing and storage of various volumes of information, as well as a quick search for the necessary data.
  2. Performing scientific, design and economic calculations.
  3. Paperwork (aspects of the preparation and execution of documents).
  4. Acquisition of professional skills through training.
  5. Publishing.
  6. Construction of diagrams, drawings, product with information component.
  7. Imitation of the work of an expert (person) in a specific area.
  8. Communication of people at considerable distances.
  9. Features of computer technology.
  10. Fun and games.

But the most interesting for us are the features of electronic computers. After all, they can be used to solve a wide range of various information processing tasks. Computer science can be used as a similar term, but its use is most often observed in the USA and Great Britain.
