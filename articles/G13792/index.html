Peterland Waterpark in the Northern Capital

There have never been any particular problems regarding how to have fun in St. Petersburg. Nevertheless, residents of the northern capital eagerly awaited the opening of a water attraction park. And the recently opened Peterland water park did not deceive their expectations.



Welcome!

This is the largest such entertainment complex in Russia. But Peterland water park attracts general attention not only for its size. Here architects are cleverly conceived and a lot of new conceptual solutions are embodied by builders at a high professional level. Take for example only one unique wooden dome structure crowning the entire structure. The dome is fascinating not only with its impressive size, but they are grandiose: 90 meters in diameter and 45 meters in height. Its peculiarity is also that it allows visitors to sunbathe almost all year round, its upper part is covered with a special film that transmits ultraviolet rays. But in summer you can sunbathe on the terrace open towards the Gulf of Finland. In general, the wooden here is not only a dome. Waterland "Peterland" has all the achievements of design ideas in the field of water attractions. There is everything: water slides of any height and complexity, wave pool, massage rooms and baths. It is worth mentioning especially about them - there are as many as 14. The developers of the water park made sure that visitors could choose any bathhouse: from Finnish to Turkish inclusive.

Waterland "Peterland" an abundance and variety of its slides will appeal to children. These structures of different heights and configurations, their total length reaches half a kilometer, and the longest descent exceeds 200 meters. Particular attention is drawn to the unique slide, which allows you to climb up along with the flow of water.





Waterpark "Peterland", visitor reviews

In a relatively short period of time, the complex has become popular. The inhabitants of the northern capital have another favorite vacation spot - the water park "Peterland". Photos and videos from the new water amusement park are in abundance on many news and information resources. The inhabitants of St. Petersburg, for the most part, are very demanding people. They have something to compare the comfort level of the new water park: a lot of people have been in complexes of this kind throughout the world. And for the city on the Neva, this is the third large water park in a row. But a simple analysis of the feedback on the forums allows us to verify the prevalence of a positive public reaction to the new water attraction park. Peterland is significantly superior to everything that St. Petersburg saw in this area before its opening.


In addition, the water park is very well located in the old park on the open shore of the Gulf of Finland. The recreation complex fits well with the urban infrastructure. It is quite easy to get here even by public transport. There are convenient parking facilities.
