Hotel Sunshine Park 3 * (Costa Brava, Spain): photos and reviews of tourists

The Costa Brava is a striking region, surprising not only with the extraordinary beauty of nature, but also with how people managed to settle down on an uncomfortable landscape, rugged by mountain slopes, formed by stony hills overgrown with coniferous trees and impenetrable shrubs. On the Costa Brava, residents have mastered all places protected from the northern winds by bays and cliffs. Here you can observe an amazing combination of harsh nature with emerald water of the Mediterranean Sea, framed by coastal cliffs. The beaches here are beautiful - clean and secluded, they attract vacationers of a wide variety of categories.

The most developed is the territory of Lloret de Mar, which is considered the tourist heart of the Costa Brava. The resort is the center of all entertainment on the coast. In a relatively limited area there are many nightclubs, bars and discos with laser shows and dance programs, as well as hotels. One of the most popular guest accommodation options in Lloret de Mar is the Sunshine Park 3 * hotel.





On the narrow streets of the city, hotels are presented in abundance, similar and at the same time completely different in design. Hotel Sunshine Park 3 * differs from its analogues in a convenient location in the center of the spa town, close to Lloret Beach.

About the resort

Spain is known as a country with an interesting flavor, extremely rich history and magnificent resorts. Those who are looking for the opportunity to relax in Spain at the best price are recommended by travel agencies to take a closer look at the mainland resorts, in particular the town of Lloret de Mar. The best beaches are located here, in addition, the famous Barcelona is not far from here. Sunshine Park 3 * (Spain, Lloret de Mar): Acquaintance

Guests can swim and sunbathe on the rooftop of the hotel, which has a pool. Sunshine Park 3 * does not have a patio, but inventive designers have built terraces on the second floor, planted with flowering plants. Relaxing on the green terraces, guests are as if in a real garden.





Reservedly decorated and simply furnished, but comfortable rooms represent no less comfort. 50 rooms are equipped with satellite TV, air conditioning, safe. Spacious bathrooms have hot and cold water regularly. All rooms have balconies where you can slowly enjoy a cup of coffee in the morning and a cocktail in the evening. Although the view from here opens mainly on the windows and roofs of neighboring buildings, but tourists do not have to spend so much time at the hotel, the contemplation of the roofs does not have time to tire them. It is much more interesting to spend days on the local beaches, in places of entertainment or on excursions. Meals at Sunshine Park 3 * (Lloret) are provided in buffet style.
