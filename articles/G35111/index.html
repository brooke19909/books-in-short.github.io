Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria 4 * in Tunisia ("Borj Sedria") - photos, prices, description and reviews of tourists

On the southwestern coast of the Mediterranean Sea is located sunny Tunisia, whose beaches are a great vacation destination for all lovers of beach activities and thalassotherapy. Some tourists who choose this country as the place to spend their vacation, think that they will meet unbearable heat here, because it is located on the African continent. Nevertheless, in the resort areas of Tunisia, the climate is quite mild and comfortable. Of course, if you keep your way inland, you can find yourself in a hot desert.





Features of rest in Tunisia

There is a category of tourists who, having arrived on the coast and staying at one of the seaside hotels, for example, at Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria (Sun Beach Resort 4 *), go from there on a journey through the desert. Others are more interested in historical sights: excursions to the ruins of ancient cities, visits to museums, etc. In short, Tunisia is perfect for mixed tours: educational (sightseeing), beach, extreme, health, etc.

Tunisian Resorts

In this exotic country there are many wonderful resort areas for every taste and budget. However, for Russians, the most popular are Hammamet, Monastir and Mahdia. This is where hundreds of thousands of tourists from various cities of Russia annually keep their way. They dream at least once in their life to plunge into the exotic atmosphere of the African continent.





Each of the resorts has its own characteristics. Hammamet, which houses the aforementioned Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria, is considered the most calm, conservative and at the same time respectable. Tourists of retirement age, as well as couples, love to come here .

Hammamet

Although this Mediterranean resort is located on the African continent, it fully complies with European standards of service. It is here that thalassotherapy centers are concentrated - the direction that came here from France. Despite the fact that the resort is considered the most tranquil of all Tunisian, the nightlife here is also in full swing. There are many bars, restaurants, nightclubs and discos. As for hotels, they serve tourists in the highest class. But about the hotel complex Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria (Tunisia) reviews of former guests are not entirely positive. Moreover, many of the guests believe that he does not pull on his 4 stars. However, this is not a bad hotel. You will be convinced of this by carefully looking at his photos, as well as by reading information about the Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria. Photos, of course, will help you to present both the external and internal structure of the hotel.





How to get there?

You can get to Hammamet by direct flight from both Russian capitals, and during the season - even from other major cities of the Russian Federation. The resort does not have its own airport, so you will have to fly to either Monastir or Enfid (by the way, it is the closest, located 110 km from the resort). And from there already by car: minibus, bus, taxi or rented car; or by rail. Buying a tour at the Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria, do not skimp and pay a transfer from the airport to the hotel and back. Upon arrival, you will be met at the airport and comfortably delivered to the hotel.

general description

This hotel is located in a quiet location on the beachfront in Borj Sedria, which is part of the Hammamet resort area. The hotel is designed to relax all members of the family. As already noted, from the nearest airport to the territory of the hotel Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria 110 kilometers, but to the city center - 25 km.

The hotel has its own private beach. By the way, this hotel is part of (or rather, one of the buildings) of the giant resort complex "Caribbean World Boyed Sedia", located near the famous international golf club. Once in the complex, you feel as if not on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, but somewhere in the Caribbean.

According to the brochures, the last reconstruction at the hotel was carried out in 2013. However, this is not true, since tourists vacationing at the Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria 4 *, leave the most negative reviews about it. This hotel operates on an all inclusive system , which concerns not only food, but also animation and other entertainment directly on the hotel territory.





Number of rooms

The Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria has a total of 370 rooms of various categories, including non-smoking. They are located in the central building of the hotel and in small comfortable bungalows scattered throughout the hotel amongst lush tropical plants. There are two, and three, and four-seater. All are equipped with modern appliances and trendy furniture. Each of them has a balcony or terrace, a comfortable bathroom with toiletries and bath accessories, as well as a fairly powerful hairdryer. Since the hotel operates year-round, in addition to air conditioning, there is also a heating system. A minibar and a safety deposit box are provided for guests for a fee. The living rooms also have satellite TV and a direct dial telephone. As for the room service, cleaning is done daily, and towels and linen are changed once a week.

To do this, there is a well-equipped conference room, meeting room, as well as a business center.





Nutrition

As already noted, this hotel is served according to the All Inclusive system, which means that from 10 am to 11 pm tourists can use the services of restaurants, cafes and bars in the hotel (including the beach) for free.

In the main restaurant 4 times a day (from 7-10; 10-11; 12.30-2.30; 7-19-21.30) a buffet is served with a wide variety of local dishes - Tunisian, and international cuisine. Among the drinks you can find water, soda, beer and local wines. By the way, in Tunisia, tap water should never be drunk.

Entertainment and sports

In front of the central building of Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria 4 * (Hammamet) there is an outdoor pool with slides and a children's section. Around him in the sunbathing area are deck chairs with umbrellas. For lovers of outdoor activities there is a tennis court, darts, a fitness center with fitness equipment, mini-golf, a children's playground and room, as well as a varied animation program and a disco.

Beach

This hotel is located on the edge of a magnificent sandy beach and has its own plot with sun loungers and bamboo umbrellas, which are provided to tourists free of charge (or rather included in the all-inclusive). There is also a bar. Fans of water activities for a fee can ride a banana, water skiing, scooter and catamaran, go diving and surfing, as well as parasailing.





Tourist reviews of Sun Beach Resort Borj Sedria 4 *

Unfortunately, tourists do not always leave positive reviews in their comments about this hotel. Although, if you look from the other side, it becomes clear that not every review can be considered true, because the information contained in it is almost always subjective. What one does not like may be liked by another.

Of course, some negative assessments are supported by photographs taken by tourists during their vacation, which are proof of what is described in the comments. For example, among the reviews you can find complaints of dirt and untidiness prevailing at the Sun Beach Hotel, that is, staff not observing hygiene rules. However, this also needs to be treated with understanding and not to blacklist this hotel. After all, unscrupulous personnel can always be replaced, and then everything can fall into place.
