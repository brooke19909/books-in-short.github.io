The best MP3 player. Review and rating of the best models

Listening to music is a favorite hobby of almost any person. However, it is far from always possible to enjoy your favorite songs outside the home. Of course, it is impossible to take a computer or laptop with you. However, for this there are inexpensive MP3 players that play audio tracks without problems.

Choosing products of this type, of course, you need to pay attention to the manufacturer. However, it is much more important to consider the characteristics that are inherent in a good portable player.

Varieties

Today there are several types of MP3 players:

  • Clips. Such products are fixed on clothes with a small clothespin or clip. Thanks to this, the device does not need to be put into a pocket, carried in one‚Äôs hand or afraid that it will be lost.
  • Classic candy bars. This type of portable player is considered the most popular today. The case can be of different sizes, depending on what characteristics the player has. At the same time, a fairly wide selection of various forms of products is presented. For example, you can purchase high-quality MP3 players in the form of a circle, square, drop or rectangle. However, all such products have one common characteristic - they are a single unit that is not equipped with any kind of mounts. Thanks to such a configuration, in such monoblocks the widest selection of characteristics is presented. Most often, such compact players can be used not only to listen to music, but also to view video files and photos. Some monoblocks also have additional applications, for example, for fitness, watches and much more.
  • Headphones. In this case, the portable product is mounted directly on the head. This type of MP3 player is used mainly by athletes or those who do not like wires. All control buttons are located directly on the product itself, which is located on the user's head. However, you need to understand that in this case the possibility of using other external headphones of better quality is completely excluded. In this case, if 1 of 2 ‚Äúears‚Äù ceases to work (a common problem), then such a player can be safely sent to a landfill.

Display

To date, you can find on sale as a player without a display at all, and products equipped with a rather large touch monitor, so outwardly they strongly resemble a smartphone. Thanks to this, a person can easily see the names of tracks, album covers of artists, view photos and even videos. However, it is worth paying attention to some display parameters.

If we talk about the type of matrix, it is better to give preference to models in the characteristics of which IPS is indicated. However, keep in mind that their cost will be an order of magnitude higher. These are the best MP3 players, however, not everyone wants to spend a lot of money on a product that is used only on the way home from work or while jogging. In this case, you should buy a product with an LCD matrix. However, in this case, the picture quality will be significantly lower.

If we talk about the diagonal of the display, then in this case everything again depends on the wishes of the user. If he wants to use the player much longer without recharging and at the same time not watch the video, but only read the names of the tracks, then a 3-inch screen will be enough for this. In this case, a person can easily use the equalizers and other settings, and the device will work longer without recharging.

If the user wants to watch movies, then you should choose a larger monitor from 3 to 4.3 inches in size. The same goes for permission. If we are talking about players with a fairly large diagonal, then in this case the optimal resolution would be 480 x 800 pixels. If this indicator is less, then on the big screen the picture will be blurry.

Built-in memory

Considering the ratings of MP3 players, it is worth paying attention to this indicator. As a rule, modern players have a built-in hard drive or flash drive. A person can store about 320 GB of music tracks and other files on portable products. However, in this case we are talking about products with the type of drive HDD. Unfortunately, such models are considered unreliable. Therefore, it is worth giving preference to other products.

Best if the final memory capacity is between 64 GB. It is also worth paying attention to the fact that in many modern products there is an opportunity to further expand the memory. If you install a microSD card in the player, then in this case you can use up to 256 GB. However, if a person plans only to listen to his favorite collection of music, then in this case there will be enough internal memory up to 32 Gigabytes. The best option is to purchase a player with 4 or 8 Gigabytes and expand this indicator with an additional drive.

Playback Formats

In this case, everything also depends on the wishes of the buyer and the cost of the product. As a rule, even a budget MP3 player is capable of producing MP3, WAV and WMA formats. This is usually enough to watch videos, listen to music and enjoy photos.
