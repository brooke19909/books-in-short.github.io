Hotel "Aristocrat", Kostroma: address, description of rooms, reviews

If you like comfort and luxury, then an accommodation option like Aristocrat Hotel is just perfect for you. Kostroma will seem even more beautiful and welcoming if you are surrounded by the care of the staff of this hotel. It is worth noting that this is a network of three hotels of different levels, each of which meets the highest standards.





"Grand Hotel and SPA Aristocrat", Kostroma

This hotel can be classified as a Premium. It is located in an incredibly beautiful place, literally, a 10-minute drive from Kostroma. Here you can enjoy the beautiful natural landscapes, as well as the magnificent interior decoration, which is designed in a unique Italian style. Presented to your attention are 40 comfortable apartments, as well as the possibility of living on an all-inclusive system.

A wide range of services is provided to its guests by the Grand Hotel & SPA Aristocrat (Kostroma). Coupons for sauna, pool, massage and jacuzzi are provided upon check-in. There are also modern rooms for celebrations and business events. The hotel staff provide all possible assistance in their organization.

Hotel "Aristocrat"

Comfortable living conditions are provided by the Aristocrat Hotel. Kostroma is famous for its beautiful nature, and this institution is located in an ecologically clean park surrounded by greenery. The interior of the hotel is made in a classic style with elements of luxurious stucco molding and forging. This is an excellent accommodation option for both tourists and business people.

Hotel "Aristocrat" (Kostroma) provides its guests with the following list of services:

  • wireless internet throughout and in the apartments;
  • washing, ironing, and clothing repair;
  • private parking with 24-hour security;
  • organization of excursion programs;
  • food in the restaurant, as well as room service;
  • use of meeting and conference rooms;
  • transfer or taxi call.




"Park Hotel Aristocrat"

If you like silence, then "Park Hotel Aristocrat" is just perfect for you. Kostroma is famous for its beautiful nature, and in this secluded corner you can hide from the city noise among green trees and birdsong. The hotel is located in a huge park area with three picturesque ponds. All this picturesque picture is complemented by comfortable rooms, as well as a full list of services that a modern tourist needs for a good rest.

Hotel Policies

"Aristocrat Grand Hotel" (Kostroma), as well as other institutions of the network are guided by the following rules:

  • The check-out time at this hotel is set for noon (early check-in or late check-out may be provided, provided that there are a sufficient number of rooms available);
  • There is no charge for children under 6 years old;
  • the cost of an extra bed is 1000 rubles per night;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • Guests can use bank plastic cards to pay for services received.




Positive reviews

Very popular among guests is such an accommodation option as the Aristocrat Hotel (Kostroma). Reviews about this place contain the following positive comments:

  • very beautiful atmosphere in the hotel itself, as well as in the territory;
  • bath towels are replaced daily;
  • Friendly and helpful staff who quickly respond to requests and comments;
  • just perfect cleanliness of the rooms;
  • new comfortable furniture;
  • it is just an ideal place for a quiet family vacation that will not overshadow any city noise;
  • the work of the restaurant chef deserves the highest praise;
  • for each guest in this hotel an individual approach;
  • the presence of a large parking zone with round the clock security;
  • in winter, the rooms are very well heated (even you have to open windows);
  • upon arrival, guests can drink tea or coffee if the room is not yet ready;
  • the bathroom has warm floors, and therefore you will not freeze even without slippers;
  • large orthopedic bed in the room.




Negative reviews

Speaking about the negative characteristics of such an institution as the hotel "Aristocrat", it is worth noting the following:

  • a rather meager selection of dishes offered for breakfast;
  • it catches wireless very poorly;
  • the hotel is located in a very remote place, where there are no restaurants, cafes or shops nearby;
  • it is strange that in a hotel of such a high level guests are not provided with slippers, as well as sets for cleaning teeth;
  • high prices for accommodation and meals;
  • there is no kettle in the room, and therefore you always have to go down to the restaurant for boiling water;
  • the towels in the bathroom are old and rather shabby (but clean);
  • there is no curtain in the bathroom, and therefore, after taking a shower, all floors are flooded;
  • when using the jacuzzi, the bathtub is filled with dirty water;
  • if you are not traveling by car, it is better not to check in at this hotel, because you will have to walk about 40 minutes to the city;
  • there are no special child seats in the restaurant;
  • Despite the high cost of living, for many services you have to pay extra - coffee, ironing board, food delivery and more;
  • there is not enough meta in the room to place luggage;
  • even if you booked a double room, bath and toiletries are still provided for one (add on request).

General impression

The Aristocrat hotel chain is very popular among guests who love a luxurious, secluded vacation surrounded by natural beauties. Nevertheless, this option should be chosen only for those guests who travel by their own car or are not afraid to splurge on a taxi or walk a lot. This is due to the fact that the hotel is located in a place remote from the central part of the city, where there is practically no infrastructure (shops, cafes, restaurants and so on).
