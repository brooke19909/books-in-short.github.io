How and where to get a diagnostic card for OSAGO? Tips for Car Owners

Diagnostic card - an official document that is provided to the driver after a technical inspection of the car. Previously, all motorists received a technical ticket. According to the current law, registration of a compulsory insurance contract is impossible without a diagnostic card. In this case, many drivers have a question: where to get a diagnostic card for compulsory motor liability insurance?

CTP and a diagnostic card for the car

Today, it is not possible to get a compulsory insurance contract without a diagnostic card. Moreover, representatives of insurance companies will control the presence and passage. Without a diagnostic card, only new cars (up to 3 years), transit cars or cars registered in a foreign country can be accepted for insurance. All other vehicles are required to provide the insurer with an inspection card to complete the contract.



Many are interested in the question of what should be the term of a diagnostic card for compulsory motor third-party liability insurance for a representative of an insurance company to draw up a contract. According to the current law, the diagnostic card must be valid at the time of insurance.

How to pass an examination and where to get a diagnostic card for compulsory motor liability insurance

Technical inspection takes place at specialized points that operate on the basis of a license. For inspection, you must provide the car in its purest form. If the machine is in a malfunctioning state, then an act is issued that indicates all the shortcomings that need to be addressed. Within a month, the driver must correct the defects and give the car for a second inspection.



If no defects are found during the re-examination, a diagnostic card form is issued, in which the expert‚Äôs signature and validity period of the card are present. In addition to paper media, an electronic card is registered in a single EAISTO database, where it is stored for 5 years.

How to issue a CTP without a diagnostic card

Currently, many insurance companies for a fixed fee offer to make a diagnostic card when registering CTP at their station. In this case, you just need to pay the state fee in the amount of 446 rubles, take the contract, according to which you need to go to the insurance company station after compulsory motor third-party liability insurance, and undergo a technical inspection. After the inspection, a completed diagnostic card with the results of the check will be provided.



Some companies are willing to immediately issue a completed inspection form for a higher fee. In this case, the representative of the insurance company registers the card in a single EAISTO database, fills out the form and gives it to the client. A diagnostic card for compulsory motor third-party liability insurance without inspection is issued not only very quickly, but also simply. This form is no different from the one that is issued to drivers at the station with real passage. Which option to choose, each motorist decides independently.

What you need to check before undergoing inspection

Before deciding where to get a diagnostic card for OSAGO, you need to pay attention to the following important points:

  • serviceable fire extinguisher;

  • warning triangle;

  • first-aid kit with all the necessary preparations;

  • machine in its purest form.

Diagnostic Card Timing

According to the current law, vehicles up to three years are exempted from technical inspection. After the specified time, you must provide the car for inspection and get a card for 2 years. After 2 years, the technical inspection procedure is repeated and a form is received for the next 2 years.



In subsequent years, the driver must provide the car and receive a card for a period of 1 year. If you do not know where to get a diagnostic card for OSAGO, you can contact the insurance company, which will give you a referral to the station and help in solving your problem.

It is also necessary to take into account that some motorists provide a valid diagnostic card at the time of insurance, but it ends during the insurance year. In this case, it is necessary to renew the card, otherwise, when an insurance event occurs, the insurer may refuse to pay insurance compensation.
