Vitebsk - Moscow: features of the trip along the route

The distance from Vitebsk to Moscow is 560 kilometers. Between the cities there is one train and several buses. In addition, you can travel from Vitebsk to Moscow with a change in Orsha, from where there are more trains. Below we consider all the options.

A trip by rail from Vitebsk itself

Between Vitebsk and Moscow there is only one train of the Belarusian formation.

He leaves at 22:18 and arrives in Moscow at 06:42. He walks daily. There are only two long stops - Smolensk and Vyazma. All the rest are short - 2 minutes each (Mozhaysk, Yartsevo, Liozno).

The ticket price depends on the seasonal fare, tentatively reserved seats will cost from 2100 rubles, and the coupe - 3600 rubles.

In terms of kilometer, the tariff is quite high. This is due to the fact that railway tariffs between Russia and Belarus still have international tariffs.

In the opposite direction, from Moscow to Vitebsk, the train leaves at 23:37, and arrives at its destination at 07:55.

Bus ride

From Vitebsk to Moscow, buses leave in the evenings from the bus station, which is located near the railway station. There are flights at 21:00, 21:30 and 22:00. The trip takes 8.5 hours. A ticket costs from 900 rubles. The place of arrival in the capital may be the bus station South Gate and Tushinskaya.

If evening flights are uncomfortable, you can first go to Smolensk, and there already transfer to Moscow. Buses depart from Vitebsk to Smolensk according to the following schedule:

  • 08:00.

From Smolensk to Moscow, take a bus for 6-7 hours. A ticket costs from 900 rubles. You can leave at 13:00. This option fits comfortably with two morning flights from Vitebsk. You can also leave at one in the morning,

4 hours to go, a ticket costs 400 rubles. Much more convenient than a bus.

Car trip

By car from Vitebsk to Moscow it is really possible to get in 6-7 hours. It is necessary to drive 520-580 kilometers. First, on the R-21 highway through Liozno, you need to enter Russia and then move to Smolensk. Then follow the E-30 highway to Moscow. The density of settlements along the road is decent, a gas station is enough.

On the way, you can call in Smolensk and see this ancient city. It is somewhat more interesting than Vitebsk, for example, there is a large Kremlin with many towers.
