Which is better to choose a smartphone - Redmi 4 or Redmi Note 4? Model Comparison

Today, the market is full of smartphones of various brands and models. Demand is growing for smartphones from Chinese manufacturers. They are not only cheaper than leading brands, but also not inferior to them in quality.

In 2016, Xiaomi (Xiaomi) became the most popular brand . Manufacturers release new models of gadgets quite often, improving specifications and correcting flaws.

In this article, the following gadgets will be considered: Xiaomi Redmi 4 and Xiaomi Redmi Note 4. You will learn about their main characteristics and price and you will be able to choose the model that will suit you.

Kit

In the box with the phone of both models, be it Redmi 4 or Redmi 4 Note, the manufacturer puts a charger (2 amp charging unit), a USB cable, a clip for opening hybrid trays.





A hybrid tray is a tray for SIM cards and flash cards. It usually has two compartments.

You can insert, for example, two SIM cards into the hybrid tray: one with favorable Internet, the other with a favorable tariff for calls and SMS messages. Or one SIM card and one flash card to expand the memory, if you do not have enough built-in.

Design

The appearance of smartphones is not much different. These devices are very similar in appearance, but there are some differences.

Perhaps only one design will help determine what you choose - Redmi 4 or Redmi Note 4 . Especially if performance is not important to you. Look at the comparisons:

  1. Body color . Xiaomi Redmi 4 can be purchased in three colors: gold, silver and gray.
    But Xiaomi Redmi Note 4 is richer in color. Initially, only gold, silver and gray models were produced. A little later, in the current 2017 year, the company added two more colors - black and dark blue. An important nuance: these colors are available only for smartphones of new versions: model 3/32 GB (for black) and 3/64 GB (for dark blue).
  2. The color of the front panel. The front panel of the Xiaomi Redmi 4 was originally black or gold (for gray and gold cases, respectively). In the new version, 3/32 GB added a version of the smartphone with a white front panel. For Xiaomi Redmi Note 4, the front panel can be obtained in white (for light cases) and in black colors (for dark cases).
  3. Frames . It is not known for what reason, but the black frames on the screen annoy everyone. They are found on both Xiaomi models. The version of the "Note", however, has less scope. This is noticeable to the naked eye. If you can not stand the framework, but want to buy Redmi 4 or Redmi Note 4 , then the second option will suit you more. In order not to notice the frame at all, it is worth taking a version with a dark body and a black front panel.
  4. Panels . Both Redmi 4 and Redmi Note 4 have panels on the top and bottom of the case. The first device almost matches their color with the case. The second panel is clearly outlined with light stripes.

In general, Redmi 4 and Redmi Note 4 are similar. There are no significant differences in them, so the design should not become a determining factor.

Memory

If you still think what to buy - Redmi 4 or Redmi Note 4, pay attention to the specifications. This is the most important aspect in choosing a device. Separately, it must be said about memory.

The amount of memory is operational. Redmi 4 exists with only 2 GB or 3 GB of RAM. Users of modern devices 3 GB will suffice, as they say, with a head. 2 GB is not enough, but if you buy a smartphone for simple tasks, then this will be enough for you.

Note-version is more modern: you can get as much as 4 GB of RAM. But you can also take 2 and 3.





The amount of internal memory. The younger model can give us 16 or 32 GB of memory. The oldest is from 16 to 64 GB.

  1. Xiaomi Redmi Note 4 with 2 GB of RAM and 16 GB of internal memory. Such a smartphone will not shine, but it is quite suitable for simple operations.
  2. Xiaomi Redmi Note 4 with 32gb internal memory and 3gb RAM. Already a good option to perform several tasks, for games and other joys.
    Many video browsers call this version optimal for ordinary users.
  3. Xiaomi Redmi Note 4 on 64gb internal and 4gb RAM. This is already a smartphone at the level: it will be able to cope with new games, applications, launch many tasks. Such a device is suitable for those who need high performance.

Battery and firmware

Both smartphones have a removable 4100 mAh battery. The phone is charged for 2.5 hours. At the same time, the battery lasts for a long time - 2-3 days without recharging.
