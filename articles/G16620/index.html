Hotel Dong Hung Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam): description and photos

Planning a vacation in Vietnam? Want to stay in Nha Trang? Looking for an affordable but comfortable hotel in the city center? In this case, Dong Hung Hotel 3 * can be a very suitable option for you to stay.



Location

This hotel is located almost in the center of the popular resort city of Nha Trang (Vietnam). So, to its European quarter, its guests can literally walk in 10 minutes. In the vicinity of the hotel there are lots of cafes, restaurants, bars, massage parlors, shops, supermarkets, currency exchange points, tour agencies, car rental companies, a market, etc. Some shops and cafes are open around the clock. Three kilometers from the hotel is the train station. Dong Hung Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) is 30 kilometers away from Kamran International Airport. On the way from the air harbor to the hotel, tourists, according to their reviews, take about 40 minutes.

With regard to proximity to the sea, Dong Hung is located on the seventh coastline. According to travelers, they reached the beach on foot in about a quarter of an hour. In general, many knew about this feature in advance, so no difficulties arose.



General information, photo

Dong Hung Hotel 3 * is a typical city hotel. It consists of one eight-story building. The hotel does not have its own territory, with the exception of parking. In total there are 50 comfortable rooms. For the convenience of guests, the building is equipped with a high-speed elevator. In addition to housing, the hotel has a restaurant, lounge and reception.

According to our compatriots who stayed here during their stay in Nha Trang (Vietnam), this hotel will be an excellent option for active tourists who prefer to spend a lot of time on the beach, walking around the area and going on excursions. Travelers with small children did not complain about the hotel. True, some family guests noted that it took them a couple of days to get in with a stroller between scooters everywhere parked on sidewalks, but this did not cause any particular complaints. If you are embarrassed by the need to go to the sea for about 15 minutes, then it makes sense to consider more expensive hotels closer to the coast.



Housing stock

As already mentioned, the Dong Hung Hotel 3 * hotel has 50 comfortable rooms. They are represented by the categories of economy, deluxe, superior and VIP and are designed for two and three guests. Experienced travelers advise those planning to visit the "Dong Hung" not to opt for economy rooms. The fact is that, in addition to a small area, the windows in these rooms are very small, and some generally do not look outside, but into the elevator shaft. However, a number of tourists managed, upon arrival at the hotel, to change their apartments to better ones for a small surcharge.

On the whole, our compatriots were completely satisfied with the numbers he offered here. So, they note that all rooms are pleasantly decorated, fresh repairs are noticeable. All apartments are equipped with comfortable furniture, a TV with cable channels, powerful air conditioning, a small refrigerator, a bathroom with shower, hairdryer, tea set. Also a big plus, according to many hotel guests, is the fact that all rooms have Wi-Fi access. It is provided free of charge. The connection speed, according to tourists, is not always high, but it is quite enough to contact relatives and friends through instant messengers and send them some photos.

As for cleaning, there were no complaints about the work of the maids among the guests. According to them, the rooms were always clean. Maids also changed linen and towels regularly.



Nutrition

The price of accommodation in Dong Hung Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) includes breakfast only. They pass in a restaurant on the eighth floor, which offers panoramic views of the city. Breakfast, according to many tourists, is quite monotonous. However, no one was left hungry here. So, in the mornings, hotel guests have the opportunity to eat omelet, scrambled eggs, soup, meat dishes, salad, vegetables and fruits, pastries, tea, coffee, juice.

Our compatriots preferred to have lunch and dinner in a cafe in the vicinity of Dong Hung, in the center of Nha Trang or on the beach. Moreover, there is no shortage of institutions of this kind.
