Working conditions and salary at McDonald's

McDonald's is one of the world's most famous fast food chains. Many scolded restaurants for the quality and harmfulness of food offered to visitors, but the network continues to grow dynamically. There are always plenty of open vacancies in any city. Is it worth going to work in such a restaurant and what is the salary at McDonald's?

Fundamental differences of the network from other cafes



According to the principles of its work, McDonald's is significantly different from any other cafes and restaurants. There is no clear separation of duties. Beginners begin by cleaning the premises and washing the toilets, and very soon, for their hard work, they can go into the category of ‚Äúexperienced‚Äù and find themselves in the kitchen. The most important thing for fast food restaurants is to serve each client at the proper level for the minimum amount of time. Surely, any visitor to the establishment of the network noticed that I began to collect his order even before calculating by check. And when buying food with you, you rarely have to wait longer than 2-3 minutes, and this is in a lively queue. If you observe the employees, it is immediately evident that no one is sitting idle, even when there are not so many visitors. And these are also corporate style features.

Salary at McDonald's and employee responsibilities



For employees in the restaurant there is a hard dress code: clean form, lack of jewelry and a minimum of makeup for girls. Do not wear high-heeled shoes. It is forbidden to take a telephone with you to the workplace. It is unacceptable to communicate with other employees on personal topics or to talk about something with visitors. It would seem that under such conditions the salary at McDonald's should be very high, but this is not always the case. Payment is hourly, individual scheduling is possible. In addition, the personnel manager always observes the employees and evaluates their work. It is on the basis of this control that a decision is made at the end of the month on the issue of an employee bonus. As in any other catering establishment , employees are offered a free lunch (with a full shift) and a break for rest.





Features of work at McDonald's

In fairness, it should be noted that the salary at McDonald's in Moscow, even for a full five-day period, can approach 25-30 thousand rubles. The regions are much smaller: a newcomer is rarely offered more than 15 thousand rubles in the provinces, even with full employment. Salary increases after about 2-3 months of work. This is a kind of trial period and encouragement for those who take work seriously. A significant drawback is the lack of tips. The restaurant policy is such that accepting gratitude from visitors to employees is strictly prohibited. Indeed, why - after all, the salary at McDonalds is good. There is a noticeable plus: everyone can get here. Only a sanitary book is needed, no one will ask about work experience and educational level. Such a vacancy is suitable for those who love monotonous activities and are afraid of responsibility. If this is about you, the work of McDonald's (Moscow) really suits you. Let the salary leave much to be desired, but you will have to answer only for overcooked potatoes and not enough clean floors.
