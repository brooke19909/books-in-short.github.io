Sirocco Beach 3 * (Tunisia / Mahdia) - photos, prices and reviews

Hotel Sirocco Beach 3 - a good Tunisian "treshka" very close to the sea. It is inexpensive, suitable for tourists who come to have a good time on the beach, eat well and even improve their health. There are not so many services here, but it is enough for relaxation. Tourists are primarily attracted by the price, location, good attitude of the staff and, of course, the sea. What is located next to the hotel

Sirocco Beach 3 is almost on the sea, and this is one of the decisive points that are taken into account during the trip. In addition, it is located near the city of Mahdia - about two kilometers to its center. Closest to the hotel is Monastir Airport. The distance to it is about fifty kilometers. And from the airport of the city of Tunisia - as much as two hundred and thirty. Three hundred meters from the hotel there are Oasis grocery stores with good prices, quality goods and an assortment better than in the market.

We arrive at the hotel - how and when?

The hotel is located quite far from the airport where charter flights arrive. Therefore, a transfer to Sirocco Beach 3 (Mahdia) takes an hour and a half or two. And if you are an independent tourist, then most likely you will fly through Monastir. From there it is easy to get to the hotel by train or bus. Go to Mahdia and minibuses (piles). Like all local hotels, this hotel is located on the first line, near the shore. Therefore, public transport inevitably passes by, and the minibus can stop right near the entrance. You need to come here exactly when the hotel is open - that is, between May and September (inclusive). July and August are very hot and good for passive vacationers. A refreshing breeze blows at sea at this time, and you don‚Äôt want to go anywhere. But for those who want to explore the surroundings, it is better to choose the beginning and end of the season.



Tunisia, Mahdia

This city is located near the cape, which bears the same name with the continent - Africa. At one time, he was even the capital of Tunisia. Those who come here usually know that Mahdia is not a party place. He is loved by those who value secluded relaxation, huge beaches and the cleanest sea. These places are good for traveling to the sea with children. All you need to know before buying a ticket to Sirocco Beach. Infrastructure

Hotel Sirocco Beach 3 (Standard, Mahdia) is a large snow-white four-story building with an elevator. It is located in the middle of a small green park. The territory is quite compact. The hotel has a special lounge for watching TV shows. Valuables can be left in the safe at the administrator. A store is located on site. The hotel can also be used for seminars - there is a special room for this. Open tennis court. Internet is available on site, but the connection is rather slow.

Tunisia, Sirocco Beach 3 Hotel - rooms

The hotel has seventy-two rooms for guests. The rooms are quite basic, air-conditioned. Room cooling is centralized and works seasonally - from the last days of June to mid-September, when it is very hot in Tunisia. A refrigerator can be ordered at the reception, but for a fee. The rooms of Sirocco Beach 3 Standard are clean, large. There is everything you need for relaxation. They are decorated with national color. Hangers in the closet are present. Each room has huge balconies. There is a table, chairs and dryer. They clean the rooms daily and it is very clean. The TV may not be, but at your request it will be put in the room. The room has few outlets, so a tee and extension cord are very useful. There are a lot of rooms with sea views, so it is likely that you will come across just such a house.



Services

It‚Äôs nice to come to the Sirocco Beach 3 * hotel with children - the hotel management is trying to attract families. For example, if you have a child under the age of fourteen, you will be entitled to a significant discount for his accommodation. In season, the hotel has a team of animators. There are many entertainments for vacationers. Before lunch - gymnastics and water aerobics. Darts competitions are held: the winner receives fresh juice as a prize. In the evenings, discos for children, shows and dances for adults are organized. Animators have been working since ten in the morning and until late.

If you have any kind of breakdown, then after a while an employee comes and fixes the problem. Hotel workers are very friendly and attentive. Here is a very good host, and in general, the hotel is positioning itself as a family. There is a mini club for children.



Eat and drink at the hotel - what is the quality?

As in many Tunisian "treshki", the "all inclusive" system is practiced here. The cuisine at Sirocco Beach 3 (Mahdia) is excellent. Breakfast is better than in Turkish hotels of the same category - there are cereals and yoghurts. As usual in the East, baking is beyond praise! From the dinner and side dishes there are rice, potatoes, vegetables, baked or grilled. Lots of salads. There are meat, poultry and a variety of fish - more than five species. Buffet, so you impose your own meals. Fine fruits - especially watermelons, melons and grapes. For a bite to eat at the bar and pizzeria, guests can enjoy a meal for free. They allow you to drink alcohol in a restaurant that you bought somewhere in duty-free - and not every hotel will find such an indulgence.

If you want to enjoy local seafood, tourists recommend not going to restaurants, where all this at sky-high prices, but to fish shops. There, in the evenings, the catch of the day is grilled on coals, and the pleasure is completely inexpensive. But if you have already decided to visit a cafe like Sidi Salem (this is in Medina), then be sure to try couscous with fish. Here it is prepared just amazingly.

Swim and sunbathe

The beach is next to the hotel. Sunbeds and umbrellas, according to vacationers, are free, although the opposite is found on tourist sites. The sea is very clean, the beach itself is large, wide, with white sand. There are no jellyfish at all, although in the neighboring areas there are quite a lot of them. A center has been opened where you can engage in various water activities. On the beach there is a playground where they play volleyball. Sirocco Beach 3 Hotel (Mahdia) has a large outdoor pool. It is very spacious, and it‚Äôs nice to relax near it. A path from it leads directly to the sea. You can swim here from morning to evening. Beach towels are available for hire.



How much is the trip?

Many vacationers choose this hotel because they are very happy with the price. It starts from three to four thousand rubles per room per day. Although many services are paid. Using the refrigerator - 25 dinars per week. The safe in the room is also for a fee - two dinars per day. The same cost and rental of beach towels. Otherwise, you need to take your own. Riding on the beach on a "banana" costs seven dinars, on a parachute - 20. Bottle beer at the hotel - $ 2.5. Tourists highly recommend buying Tunisian wines and date liquor (the latter costs about eight dollars). If you want to go to the medieval quarter - Medina - from the Sirocco Beach 3 Mahdia hotel by taxi, then pay no more than two or three dinars. Entrance to the fortress costs 3 TLD. You have to buy excursions at the hotel, as there are no street agencies in Mahdia. A full-day sightseeing tour of the main attractions will cost about forty-five dollars per person. You can buy olive oil for six dinars per liter. A diving course will cost three hundred and twenty dollars.



Where can I go and go on an excursion?

There are no special attractions within walking distance of the hotel, so if you want to wander around the shops, the market and the cafe, then go to Medina. Although interesting shopping, as some tourists claim, can be done very close to the hotel - along the promenade there are shops with clothes, a supermarket with good prices. Merchants near the hotel are quite polite and do not impose anything. And in the city do not forget to bargain - the more skillfully you do it, the more you are respected. In Medina, beautiful old buildings - forty-meter gates, the remains of the walls of the Fatimid era, when this city was the capital of Tunisia, the fortress of Bordj al-Kebir, which is located directly on Cape Africa. Of the excursions, vacationers like a walk on the "pirate ship", which includes lunch and drinks, as well as a trip to Tunisia and Carthage. True, tourists advise history buffs to go to Carthage on their own, since during the excursions they visit only Antonin's baths.
