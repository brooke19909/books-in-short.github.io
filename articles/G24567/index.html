Hotel Rooms

Tourists have long been fond of Adler. Atlant Hotel, the photos of which demonstrate simplicity and comfort, can offer its guests the following accommodation options:

Useful information about the hotel

If you are planning a vacation in Sochi, Adler, be sure to read the rules of accommodation at the Atlant Hotel:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests begins strictly after 14:00 (unless there are a sufficient number of rooms available);
  • on the day of departure, you must leave the room before noon (for each hour spent in the room in excess of the norm, you will have to pay separately);
  • children under 3 years old stay in the room free of charge, if their placement is possible on existing beds;
  • an extra bed will cost 1000 rubles for each day;
  • the hotel is not allowed to check in with pets;
  • it is possible to pay for the services received through bank plastic cards.
