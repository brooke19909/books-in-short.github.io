Location

Cretan Kalypso Cretan Village is located in a town called Plakias. A little less than 50 years ago there were about a dozen houses. But now Plakias has become a popular resort, famous for its pristine beaches, amazingly beautiful nature and proximity to all sights.

If you look at the hotel from above, it may seem that it seemed to have grown out of a cliff. Indeed, the complex is located on a hill, surrounded by lush flowering vegetation, in close proximity to the quiet beach of Caravos. From here to Rethymnon, which is one of the most beautiful Cretan cities, can be reached in 30-40 minutes. The airport, however, is far away - the nearest is 115 kilometers away (located in Heraklion). But in 15 minutes you can drive to the Frangokastello fortress, which was built in the XIV century.

Facilities and service

Cretan Kalypso Cretan Village has everything you need for comfort. On the territory there is a minimarket, a hall for conferences and banquets, a souvenir shop. It offers free Wi-Fi and a shuttle service (both in the city and to / from the airport). The registration desk works 24/7, therefore it is possible to carry out individual or accelerated registration of guests.

There is also a room where visitors can leave their luggage, public parking near the hotel, as well as non-smoking rooms and family apartments.

But the most interesting is the multilingual staff. The hotel employs people who speak six different languages. In addition to Greek and English, they speak French, German, Polish and Russian.





Entertainment

Located in Cretan Kalypso Cretan Village, you can brighten up your leisure activities in different ways. In addition to the swimming pool and sun terrace, where entertainment shows are held in the evenings, you can still enjoy water sports. The hotel provides all the necessary facilities for this. After all, he even has his own beach.

In general, Plakias is widely known as a place popular among divers. Here, in southern Crete, an indescribably beautiful underwater world. And you should definitely try yourself in diving, staying here on vacation. Underwater gorges, caves, coral reefs, barracudas, moray eels, stingrays, lobsters, lobsters, crabs, bright tropical fish - all this can be seen here with your own eyes.

The hotel also rents bicycles. You can also play billiards, darts, table tennis, basketball, volleyball, horse riding or fishing. The hotel has everything you need for these outdoor activities.
