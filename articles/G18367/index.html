Rest in Kazan. Attractions, entertainment, excursions

The heart of the whole of Tatarstan is the colorful city of Kazan, in which not only the architecture of different centuries is snugly located, but also Muslims and Orthodox Christians coexist. The origin of the name of the city is shrouded in many of the most mystical stories, one of which tells of the Bulgars' search for a place suitable for life. The greatest sorcerer advised them to find the territory on which the boiler could boil without fire, and luck was waiting for the travelers there. Kazan was warmed up only in the lands of Lake Kaban, which is now located in the very center of the glorious city.

When planning a vacation in Kazan, tourists should work out the entire travel route well so as not to miss the most interesting places in the city, as well as to imbue the spirit of grandeur of the multifaceted third capital of Russia.





Bauman Street

The first step is to decide where to take a walk in Kazan. Hiking not only fills the body with vital oxygen, but also allows you to fully admire the architecture, landscapes, people and sky. In the city, the oldest Bauman street has faithfully stretched. It existed during the time of the Kazan Khanate. At that time they called it the Nogai and, if possible, built up it. Therefore, at the present time, walking along the famous street, one can consider both modern stylish buildings and solid houses of the last century.

Monument to Chaliapin

Where to go for a walk in Kazan? Thinking about further places, it is mandatory to appear at the monument to the great Fedor Chaliapin, which is installed all on the same Bauman street, in the territory of the chic hotel "Chaliapin Palace". The bronze figure reveals to the audience the whole gamut of the singer's feelings at the moment of farewell to Russia. It was installed in August 1998 and until now has been gathering fans and tourists around the marvelous city.

Palace for Farmers

In order for the rest in Kazan to be complete, visitors to the city should definitely visit the Palace for Farmers. The large-scale building in the Baroque and ambiguous Renaissance style is located on Palace Square, in the heart of the Tatar city. It was built in 2010, but disputes and scandals have not subsided to this day. The headquarters of the numerous Ministry of Agriculture of the Republic received the monster nickname because of its oversized dimensions: luxurious domes, symmetry of the entire structure, bronze tree with green backlight in the central portal. Architects, journalists, and ordinary people speak about the style and significance of a building the size of a rather big residential quarter, but its integrity and harmony put the palace on a par with its centuries-old neighbor, the Kazan Kremlin.





Kul Sharif Mosque

Many Muslims proudly go to majestic Kazan. Holidays on the Volga can be combined with a trip to the reconstructed Kul-Sharif mosque. It perfectly complements the Kremlin ensemble in the western part. The most famous mosque was destroyed almost to the foundation by the forces of Ivan the Terrible during the assault on Kazan, the winners of the republican design competition took part in the restoration work. The finest handmade domes, the Roman exquisite mosaic, the unique stained glass windows - all this attracts tourists and parishioners during the day, and at night the illumination of the entire complex, ideally incorporated into the project, attracts the eyes of all passing people.

Boar Lake

Those who wish to plunge into water recreation can also safely go to Kazan. Lakes, recreation on the territory of which can give a beautiful tan, training for the whole body, as well as give aesthetic pleasure, are pleased to receive tourists and visitors to the city. The main trump card of Tatarstan is the amazing Lake Kaban, consisting of three picturesque lakes with a total length of 10 kilometers.

The history of the Tatar shrine is shrouded in many versions, but the most colorful of them speaks of an old man whose prayers cleared the swamp lands and opened three large lakes to the world. But the very name of the amazing reservoir is most often associated with the legendary Prince Kaban, whose wounds healed the local waters. But the main secret of the lake is the disappearance of the Khan's treasure. The legend tells of hidden gold at the bottom, which daredevils and scientists still cannot find.

"Riviera"

On the picturesque shore of a magnificent lake there is a water park in Kazan under the sensual name "Riviera". Many guests of the third capital knew the inimitable color, beauty and convenience of a huge entertainment center. Thanks to the meticulously designed design, the water park can receive guests during the winter cold and on hot days. For visitors, the Riviera has prepared about 50 modern technically designed rides, downhill, wave pool, as well as diving workshops.

Also, the water park in Kazan offers those who wish to plunge into the exotic of the beautiful Amazon River, whose firmament is covered by either a mysterious starry sky or a beautiful morning light. Those who want to pamper their body with spa treatments can safely go to the local salon, where the country's leading experts are working hard.

Museum of Tatarstan

When planning a vacation in Kazan in the company of a family, it is worth visiting the National Museum of Tatarstan, which tirelessly protects the archaeological, scientific and research works of the whole republic. The beautiful building is located on Kremlyovskaya Street, on the site of the former Gostiny Dvor. At the moment, the fund has 800 thousand valuable products, as well as several scientific and methodological tips. The luxurious museum building has its own restoration workshop, a convenient technically equipped repository, a hall for literary evenings and exhibitions.

Kazan Arena Stadium

Athletes can also enjoy their holidays in Kazan, because here they have their own sports heart - the Kazan Arena stadium. The modern, technically thought-out structure has a capacity of about 45 thousand people, which pleases football fans of Tatarstan. Convenient parking, cozy restaurants and a hall for large-scale conferences expand the stadium's capabilities in holding cultural events. The territory of 32 hectares is filled with dynamics, thirst for life and accomplishments. Especially pleasant will be a walk around the stadium for young athletes.

Conclusion

Many people consider rest in Kazan in September the most colorful and inspiring for both small and large companies. The city is permeated with the spirit of the Kazan Khanate, immersed in the brightest shades of foliage, the air is filled with warmth, freshness and kindness.
