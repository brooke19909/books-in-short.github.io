Testing

The purpose of the test: to understand how well the razor copes with such tasks, whether it is convenient to use. The device is quite inexpensive, its cost is 3 thousand rubles. Advantages: reviews

A brief look at the benefits that users provide. The device is convenient, accurate, and also small. They can easily make simple drawings on the beard, if necessary. Disadvantages: reviews

Of the minuses, it should be noted that shaving should be done only against the hair. The accuracy of trimming is imperfect, there are problems with the tips. The latter may fall out. The lock button sometimes does not work, as mentioned above, often nozzles just fly off.

During operation, such deficiencies could not be identified. That is, when shaving, the nozzles are well fixed and hold well.

How to shave with this device?

As with a conventional machine, the head of this device must be carried along the skin. Without problems, this device will shorten long hairs. This sets him apart from a wet razor. What is the trick? With OneBlade, the blades are along the longest edge, due to this the razor cuts the beard from the side. Therefore, hair can be of absolutely any length. The main thing is to adapt to work.

It should be noted that the results of shaving are good, but imperfect. In order to get an acceptable result, you have to walk around the place several times. Especially when it comes to problem areas. Crucial is the direction of movement and hair growth. As mentioned above, this device can only be shaved against the cover. Whether irritation appears after this depends entirely on the type of skin.

If you press the razor hard and put it into the skin, then there will be no damage. In general, most buyers are satisfied after purchasing the device, given its cost. The device is completely waterproof, so you can not be afraid and complete all the processes in the shower.



Good or bad?

The reviews say that the Philips OneBlade QP2530 is ideal as a trimmer. Due to the small blade, light weight and compact handle, the process of editing the edges of the beard is quite convenient.

For greater accuracy, a man can use a nozzle that directs hair at a certain angle. In this case, the edges will be even better shaved.

However, in the reviews, many write that a flat nozzle works better than a corner nozzle. In the latter case, individual hairs are skipped, which must be re-shaved.

In the configuration, the choice of nozzles is completely small. There are only four plastic tips. They will allow you to change the length of the beard from 1 to 5 mm. With overgrown hair, the trimmer will not be able to cope. To do this, you will have to purchase a new razor, which has an integrated version of the nozzles. It's called the Philips One Blade PRO. Reviews of her confirm that with her help you can adjust the length of your beard up to one centimeter.
