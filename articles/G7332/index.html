Golden Time Hotel (Nha Trang, Vietnam): description, photos, reviews

Tourists call this hotel, located in a quiet side street, not far from the beach, a fairly cozy establishment in which guests are offered clean and fresh rooms. According to reviews, Golden Time Hotel is one of the most respectable "triples" in Nha Trang.

Golden Time Hotel was opened in 2014. The establishment is located in Nha Trang (Vietnam), 700 meters from the center, near Tran Phu beach (across the road), 35.5 km from the airport.

About living experiences

According to reviews, the hotel is located in a cozy nook, which is a big plus, since it is not as noisy as in establishments built along the road. The beach is located across the road (2-lane), in the immediate vicinity of the hotel, on the first line. It can be reached in 3 minutes, crossing the road, quite busy. Traffic in all of Vietnam, according to tourists, is extremely busy, but after a few days you can get used to it. For those who fear, travelers recommend joining any of the Chinese groups (from 20-30 people), usually stopping the flow of cars.

All rooms in this hotel are quite small, equipped with a wardrobe, a nightstand, beds, air conditioning (quite old, but working), a desk, a mirror, a flat-screen TV (Sharp with one Russian channel), a hairdryer, a safe, a refrigerator, and chairs. Residents call the rooms livable, although the walls in them are dirty in some places. According to reviews, the bathrooms in the rooms are quite ordinary, with windows, a shower and a floor drain.

Clean the rooms daily. According to guests, cleaning is done carefully: they clean the table cleanly, sweep and wash the floor, and clean the bathroom. Cups in the rooms do not change or wash, which is the norm for Asia. Towels can be provided with stained towels that do not wash. They can be changed to clean by contacting the maid.

The restaurant provides good breakfast buffet: eggs (scrambled eggs, fried eggs, boiled), sausage, toast, porridge, bacon, butter, stewed fish or meat, fruits, vegetables (fresh), sweets - everything is cooked quite tasty and served in in sufficient numbers, none of the guests remain hungry. Many people like the coffee here. The staff at Golden Time guests are called responsive, polite.

Residents recommend choosing this hotel not for a permanent stay, but exclusively for an overnight stay.
