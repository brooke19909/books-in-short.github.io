Audio quality

ZTE has really worked on the Axon 7 audio system, and it makes itself felt. Most of the phone manufacturers hardly pay due attention to the sound, but the manufacturer spent a lot of time and effort to make sure that the sound quality of the model is amazing.

The phone is equipped with two dedicated AKM AK4961 and AK4490 audio processors, providing Hi-Fi playback through speakers or headphones, front stereo speakers and Dolby Atmos sound enhancement system.





The stereo is implemented using a pair of 2.15-B speakers, which are among the most high-profile ones ever used on mobile phones. Axon 7 outperforms the competition, even outshining the NTS 10, which has decent audio performance. According to user reviews, the speakers are able to reproduce a higher volume than the one that is limited to the maximum level. The sound is crisp and clear, so it is free from the distortion that smartphone speakers typically suffer from. Clear bass, as well as high notes of wind and string sections.

Headphones ZTE Axon 7, according to reviews, work no worse. The phone supports 32-bit Hi-Fi audio with a sampling frequency of 192 kHz, enhanced by Dolby Atmos technology. The software can play videos, music and movies through professionally configured equalizer profiles and simulate 7.1 surround sound system. The illusion is pretty convincing.

Axon 7 can record audio during playback. The smartphone is equipped with 2 noise-canceling internal microphones that pick up sound from a distance of up to 7 m and in any direction. The phone ‚Äúlistens‚Äù for the source while minimizing ambient noise, enhancing the recording if necessary. According to reviews, everything happens in full accordance with the advertisement - the microphone sound is crystal clear.

Camera

On the rear panel is a 20-megapixel camera with a relative aperture of f / 1.8, which uses phase-detection AF to quickly sharpen the subject. Optical stabilization mitigates the effects of trembling hands and bumpy roads when driving a car. Finally, the wide dynamic range increases the brightness and contrast of images.

In the afternoon, the Axon 7 image sensor is performing well. City landscapes are excellent. Colors gravitate toward the colder end of the color spectrum, and sky tones look especially intense. When HDR mode is on, the brightness of photos borders on oversaturation. Not everyone will like such courage, but the effect created is beautiful.





Unfortunately, excellent daytime work does not apply to shooting in low light conditions. The Axon 7 image sensor struggled to display objects in dim light, showing a significant amount of grain. Autofocus is becoming sluggish, and the colors are disappointingly faded and soft compared to photos taken on the Galaxy S7 and iPhone 7. The 16-megapixel camera of the ZTE Axon 7 Mini also does not cope with the lack of light.

An 8MP front-facing image sensor delivers great shots and videos. Unfortunately, he does not have enough flash, but photographs taken in well-lit rooms and in daylight come out bright, clear and without noticeable grain or softness.

Fortunately, the main camera of the ZTE Axon 7 phone, according to reviews, shoots video much better. The recording is smooth and impressively stable. Users also appreciate the options available, including 4K (4096 x 2160) at 30 fps, FullHD (1920 x 1080) at 30 and 60 fps and slow motion in 720p with a frame rate of 240 fps. The capabilities of the smartphone camera ZTE Axon 7 Mini 32GB, according to owners, are much more modest and limited to 1080p video at 30 fps.

For comparison: ZTE Axon 7 Max reviews are praised for having a 13 MP dual camera, one of which serves to provide depth of field, and a laser autofocus system. The model is also equipped with a high-end audio chip and a display layer that allows you to view 3D content without special glasses.

Compared to the Mini model, the specifications of the ZTE Axon 7 are called impressive. Short sessions of fairly easy use (mainly checking email, installing applications and sending messages) consume a minimum of energy. The ZTE Axon 7 smartphone, according to reviews, easily withstands a whole day of work, and those who use it less experienced users are likely to last a day and a half.





User interface

The smartphone runs on the Android platform with several changes and additions. The smartphone has a proprietary user interface manufacturer MiFavor. Recent updates have supplemented it with a number of features, including Night Mode, which adjusts the color profile of the screen to reduce eye strain at night, and support for 256-GB microSD cards.

The lock screen has a nice feature. It changes the background every time you unlock the Axon 7.

MiFlavor leaves the other interface elements pretty much untouched. Warranty

The manufacturer provides a standard one-year warranty that provides repair and replacement of devices with "manufacturing or material defects." There is also a free advanced exchange option. It is enough to provide a credit card number, and ZTE will send a replacement for the defective phone. The company even pays for shipping.

In addition, the manufacturer replaces or refunds for any defective devices within the first 30 days of ownership. If the smartphone is stolen or lost, the company will reimburse the owner up to 6,000 rubles.
