Porto Azzurro Club Mare 4 *, 5 * (ex. Club Bella Mare, Turkey / Alanya) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

When planning to relax in Turkey, many of our compatriots opt for the prestigious Porto Azzurro Club Mare 4 * hotel . This four-star hotel complex, consisting of five six-story modern buildings, is located in one of the most beautiful areas of Alanya - a resort that is preferred by tourists of various categories. Come here and those who imagine the holiday as a measured sunbathing on the beach on the shores of the wonderful sea, and those who like to combine a vacation with familiarization with local attractions. After all, Alanya was once considered a haven for bloodthirsty pirates. They brought looted goods to the coastal grottoes; here, in the caves, they set up prisons for abducted concubines.



Maybe this is precisely the mysterious history of the resort and is the second, after the wonderful sea and beaches, the reason that its hotels and hotel complexes are almost always crowded. Porto Azzurro Club Mare is no exception.

Almost all the reviews of vacationers are full of laudatory words about the menu. Residents at Porto Azzurro Club Mare eat on an all-inclusive basis. Meals - as a buffet. For those who want to try more exotic dishes of Asian or European cuisine, there is a restaurant on the second floor of the main building, tables in which you need to reserve in advance at the reception desk. The all-inclusive concept of food involves free local alcoholic drinks. Porto Azzurro Club Mare also has three bars, including on the beach and around the pool.

Sea

The hotel has its own sandy beach. It can be reached in three minutes, bypassing the freeway, under which a convenient underground passage is arranged. For children, the entrance to the sea is very convenient. Sun loungers, couches and umbrellas are available for free at Porto Azzurro Club Mare.

Entertainment

Especially a lot of positive feedback tourists leave about the animation. From twelve o'clock in the afternoon, when the sun is at its zenith, vacationers gather in the shade near the pool, they are offered an interesting entertainment program. For children there are slides, and for parents there is a bar serving excellent pies. In the evenings, discos are held on site and competitions are held. Those who express a desire to watch the fire show are provided with a free shuttle bus that takes them to the neighboring five-star hotel.



Gymnastics, darts, backgammon, volleyball, basketball and table tennis are free entertainment. A visit to the sauna or hammam, cabaret, Turkish night and revue, as well as a visit to the Wellness center are also included in the price of the ticket. However, water sports that are on the beach, including banana, jet ski, parachute or canoe, must be paid separately.

For children

Porto Azzurro Club Mare offers a small pool with slides, a club and a games room for its young guests. Those guests who did not bring a stroller with them on holiday can rent it. For a comfortable meal, children are offered a highchair in the restaurant, there is also a special menu. If parents have urgent matters, they can invite a paid nanny. In general, many believe that Porto Azzurro Club Mare is ideal for relaxing with your family in Alanya.
