Bavarian Versailles

Locals call the former summer residence of the Bavarian rulers Versailles, and tourists who get acquainted with it recognize the validity of this statement. Cozy alleys, neat paths, bewitching canals and bridges through them attract the adult audience and children.





Parents will be interested in looking at the luxurious interior decoration of several halls of Nymphenburg, open to the public, and the kids will appreciate the numerous museums whose exhibits are quite unusual. Hofgarten Park

Hofgarten is located in the city center, where everything is done with good taste. Guests speak with enthusiasm about the amazing corner, which helps to realize the divine beauty of our world. Where to go with the kids?

Tourists who decide to relax in Munich in November with their children may not worry that their offspring will be bored. The Hellabrunn Zoo is the largest in Europe, and the kids are happy to touch the animals and even feed them.





The aquarium with a gigantic collection of inhabitants delights all the children who have fallen into the underwater kingdom. Sea Life is appropriately decorated: sunken ships, jewels scattered at the bottom and other decorations are immersed in a fairy-tale world. But the biggest impression is the walk through the tunnel covered with thick transparent glass. Sharks and fish swim freely behind it, and some predators even closely monitor visitors.

Where to visit Munich in November and what else to see?

It is in the last month of autumn that grandiose Christmas sales begin, when everyone can purchase fashionable and high-quality items from popular brands at a great discount. Therefore, those who want to update the wardrobe, it is advisable to stock up on time and, of course, means.

Enthusiastic tourists who fell into the city decorated for the holidays, regale themselves with nuts and the famous Bavarian sausages fried on fire, warm themselves with mulled wine.
