Hotel Ariadne Beach Hotel 4 * (Crete, Greece): reviews and photos

Ariadne Beach Hotel 4 * is a luxurious bungalow complex, decorated in a traditional Cretan style. You can enjoy an unrivaled marine vacation and an unforgettable authentic atmosphere.





Location

Just 50 meters from the luxurious beach, the Ariadne Beach Hotel 4 * is located. Crete, Agios Nikolaos, Gargadoros, 72100 - address of the resort. The city center is only 800 meters away. Heraklion International Airport is 60 km away. Only 3 km from the hotel is a large museum of local lore, and 6 km from the aquarium.

Negative reviews

However, there are a number of negative characteristics and shortcomings in such a resort hotel as Ariadne Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews in 2016 indicate the following disadvantages of the institution:

  • monotonous breakfasts that bother after a few days;
  • sunbeds paid not only on the beach, but also near the pool;
  • breakfast does not serve cereals;
  • even if you booked an improved room, you will still be accommodated in the standard, so that later you will require a surcharge for the relocation;
  • paid safe;
  • the presence or absence of a tip greatly affects the quality of cleaning;
  • if the hotel has few guests, employees flush the pool, citing the fact that it is faulty;
  • sometimes there is a strong smell of sewage on the street, which forces the windows to close tightly;
  • old washed towels (some even have holes);
  • use of beach towels is paid (in addition to 2 euros for use, you must still leave a deposit of 10 euros);
  • guests often find hair in food;
  • Considering that the main contingent of vacationers is European pensioners, it is rather boring in the territory;
  • inflated prices for excursions (there are tourist offices near the hotel, where the rates are much lower);
  • there is practically no entertainment on the territory (mainly they are concentrated in the neighboring hotel, which guests can freely visit).

General impression

Ariadne Beach Hotel 4 * is very popular among tourists who want to relax in a relaxed atmosphere and enjoy the sea in plenty. The hotel is located in one of the quietest areas of the resort. And the absence of entertainment on the territory is the key to silence, which may seem to someone an unattractive characteristic. Nevertheless, the proximity to the sea and the friendly attitude of the staff covers many of the shortcomings and shortcomings that are characteristic of this hotel.
