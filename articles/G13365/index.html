Hotel Riagor 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): overview, beach, rooms and reviews of tourists

Budget holidays in Bulgaria have attracted tourists from Russia for many years. Some of them come here, trying to find an alternative to the bored resorts of Turkey and Egypt. And travelers choose this country for therapeutic rest. The combination of sea and mountain air will be useful to residents of megacities. For inexpensive accommodation, the 3-star Riagor Hotel is perfect. "Riagor" (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) - one of the most popular tourist complexes located in the resort.

general information

The construction of the tourist complex was completed in 2011. Unlike most hotels built in the Soviet era, it is significantly distinguished by its modern architectural style. It can not be called a large complex - it provides a total of 62 rooms. Therefore, this place is well suited for a quiet holiday with your family. Hotel Riagor 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) offers significant discounts on accommodation for small children. It employs Russian-speaking staff who also speaks English, Polish and Bulgarian. Check-in with animals is not permitted even at an additional cost.





The hotel boasts a prime location. It is located in the southern part of Sunny Beach, which is considered the largest resort in Bulgaria. The city beach is 250 meters away from the hotel. You can walk to it in 5 minutes. Guests with children should visit the local water park, 300 meters away. Burgas International Airport is 35 km from the resort. 5 km from Sunny Beach there are other large cities: Nessebar and St. Vlas.

What rooms does the complex offer?

All rooms are located in the main six-story building. Hotel Riagor 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) offers guests comfortable, but small apartments. All have a private bathroom with shower. For the comfort of tourists, a soft carpet is laid on the floor, which makes it pleasant to walk barefoot. Each room has its own small balcony with a view of the garden surrounding the hotel or the sea. Almost all rooms are designed for two adults and a child. But there are several spacious apartments in the complex, which can accommodate four people at once.





Proper equipment of all rooms allows guests to feel the cozy atmosphere. We list the main equipment that is offered to guests of the Riagor 3 * complex (Sunny Beach):

  • Direct telephone allowing to make paid calls to other cities and countries. In addition, it is convenient to contact the hotel staff with its help.
  • Individual air conditioning that maintains cool temperatures on hot days. Guests can independently turn it on and off, as well as adjust the level of cooling.
  • An electronic safe for storing valuable items. With him, you can not be afraid to leave things in the room, fearing that employees of the complex can steal them.
  • Modern flat-screen TV with satellite channels. There are both children's and Russian-language channels.
  • Wireless internet, which guests can use for free.
  • Minibar for cooling drinks. Its filling is not included in the cost of rest.
  • Bathroom amenities include an iron and hairdryer.
  • Daily tidy of all rooms. Every two days, employees will also change towels and bedding.

Food: concept, restaurants and bars

On the territory of Hotel Riagor 3 stars (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) is the main restaurant, which hosts all breakfasts, lunches and dinners. Food served at the buffet. The menu includes local and European dishes. Usually it includes several types of meat (chicken, pork, beef, lamb and duck), sometimes fish (catfish, mackerel), as well as various side dishes, salads, pastries, fresh vegetables and fruits. Food is prepared differently every day. The dishes are not sharp, so they are suitable for people suffering from diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, and for young children. The restaurant has a spacious hall accommodating more than 100 people, as well as an outdoor terrace for 30 visitors.





Making a ticket, tourists can pay for the all-inclusive system. It includes all meals, as well as drinks. Free local alcohol, hot drinks (tea, coffee, milk), concentrated juices and mineral water are provided. For guests there is a lobby bar where you can have a bite to eat fresh pastries, ice cream or dessert. The pool bar offers light snacks and refreshing cocktails.

Tourist service: services

The modern Riagor 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach) can offer its guests a developed network of infrastructure facilities. It provides a comfortable stay at the hotel throughout the vacation. Tourists can use the following facilities and services at any time:

  • A shop where guests can buy newspapers, souvenirs, tobacco, spirits, beachwear and shoes.
  • Private parking. You can leave your car here without prior reservation. The approximate cost of services is 5 euros per day. Car rental is available.
  • Luggage storage located at the reception. Hotel guests can leave large bags in it so that they do not take up space in the room.
  • Facilities for the disabled and people with disabilities. These include ramps, wide doorways and an elevator.
  • Paid safe. Own laundry room where guests can take laundry.
  • 24 hour currency exchange.
  • Shared living room with sofas, armchairs, coffee tables and a TV.




Entertainment Details: Pool and Beach

The entertainment program of the Riagor 3 * complex (Sunny Beach) is aimed at a calm and relaxed vacation. An outdoor freshwater swimming pool is open daily for guests of the hotel. Next to it is a terrace on which you can sunbathe on free sunbeds. The complex has a small but well-kept garden. Vacationers love to stroll here in the evenings. You can play sports in the gym, equipped with modern equipment. His visit is not included in the price.





At 300 meters from the complex is a city beach. It has a convenient and even entrance to the sea, allowing you to swim without shale. There are almost no pebbles and small stones in the sand. Here umbrellas and sunbeds are provided for a fee (10 lev for one item). There is also a water entertainment center on the beach. For a fee, tourists can rent a motor and non-motor boat, windsurf, play beach volleyball. The sea is shallow, therefore suitable for swimming with children.

Vacation with children: conditions and entertainment

Orientation of the Riagor 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach) is a family vacation with young children. Children under 12 years old are offered a discount on shared room with parents. Babies under 2 years old are provided with a paid baby cradle. In the restaurant, parents can always ask the waiters for a high stool for feeding.

There is practically no entertainment for children at the hotel. In the adult pool there is a separate shallow section for babies. In the neighboring tourist complex there is a children's playground. The hotel does not have a mini club and animation, so parents should think about leisure for their child before arriving at the hotel.





Guest opinions: positive

About this tourist complex, guests leave a variety of reviews. "Oh, Hotel Riagor 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is a great place to relax!" - say the most positive of them. People living here describe the following advantages:

  • Spacious and clean rooms with new furniture. The design is also modern. The hotel does not resemble a Soviet sanatorium, like many other complexes in Bulgaria.
  • Friendly and understanding staff. Check-in starts at 14:00, but the administrator will necessarily make concessions and give the keys earlier if there are free numbers.
  • A variety of dishes in the restaurant. There are a lot of fruits, and they are replenished regularly. Delicious ground coffee is served.
  • The hotel has families with children of preschool age, so already at 22:00 there is complete silence.
  • The sea in summer is very warm and clean. The entrance is convenient, but it is very shallow. Suitable for tourists with small children, but not for diving enthusiasts.
  • The complex is not fenced, so you can walk around the neighboring hotels. Parents love to take their children to their playground.




Guest opinions: negative

Travelers who rested in the Riagor 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach) leave negative reviews. In them they talk about the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • There is no shower on the beach, toilets are paid, and changing cabins are very cramped and uncomfortable. Umbrellas and sunbeds are more expensive than in Europe.
  • Go to the sea much longer than stated in the description. We have to go around other hotels, cafes and restaurants. On average, a one-way trip takes 15 minutes.
  • Terrible soy sausages, which are simply impossible to eat, are served for breakfast. The restaurant constantly smells something unpleasant.
  • Hot drinks (tea, coffee) are served only for breakfast.
  • The hotel is very far from the central promenade, which houses all the tourist attractions.

Should I stop at this place?

The new tourist complex Riagor ("Riagor") 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) can be advised for family vacations. It differs significantly from other three-star resort hotels with new and clean rooms, as well as high standards of service. It is suitable for the elderly, who will be easy every day to go to the beach.
