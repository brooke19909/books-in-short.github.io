What is Volte?

Surely one of the readers has already seen or heard the phrase VoLTE. Let's see what it hides under itself and how relevant today in Russia.

VoLTE - what is it?

VoLTE has a simple definition - it is a technology for voice transmission in LTE networks (the second name is 4G). Accordingly, it is available only on smartphones with 4G support. Based on VoLTE's IP Multimedia Subsystem (IMS). Its innovation is that the technology allows telecom operators to provide voice services, delivering them from the addressee to the addressee in the form of a data stream in LTE networks, which becomes the reason for its greater capacity and higher quality than when making a call using 2G or 3G.





VoLTE translation is fully consistent with the definition. The combination of letters means Voice over LTE, which translates from English as "voice over LTE."

The technology is not super new - it was presented by Singapore-based SingTel in May 2014. Initially, VoLTE supported only one gadget - Samsung Galaxy Note 3. Since 2015, telecom operators around the world, including Russian ones, have become actively interested in technology.

Disadvantages of VoLTE

Le VoLTE has a couple of disadvantages, which are also important to note:

  1. The new technology implies a slightly greater load on the smartphone, as a result of which it will discharge a little faster during a conversation.
  2. LTE towers are mainly installed in cities and large settlements. Therefore, on the highway, on nature and recreation facilities, in villages, etc., communication may be lost. To reach a specific subscriber, the user will have to manually switch his smartphone to 3G or even EDGE, GPRS - to a mode that is supported in the area. It is possible that the developers of gadgets will soon "teach" their devices to perform this action automatically if necessary.

VoLTE on iPhone

Today, the technology mentioned is available to owners of iPhone 6 and newer. We will tell you how to make detailed VoLTE settings on these phones:

  1. Go to the "General" section in the phone settings, find the "Software Update" item. Once in it, make sure your iOS version is higher than 10.3. Otherwise, update the system.
  2. After these manipulations in the same basic settings, find the "About device" tab, then - information about the operator. Supports VoLTE version of the operator no lower than 28.3 (for "Megaphone", "Beeline"). If possible, upgrade it to this level.
  3. The next item will be a reboot of the gadget.
  4. After the reboot, you should re-enter "Settings" - "Cellular". In the "Data Options" in the "Voice and Data" tab, check the box for LTE.
  5. Restart your iPhone again.
  6. When the mobile Internet is on (make sure that you are in the 4G - LTE coverage area), make a call to another subscriber. If the LTE icon does not disappear while the Internet is working properly, then at the moment you have tried the new Voice over LTE technology.
