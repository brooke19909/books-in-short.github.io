Yevpatoriya Hotels: reviews and photos. Hotels of Evpatoria near the sea

Evpatoria is a seaside city located in the northwestern, steppe part of Crimea. It stretches along the Kalamitsky shallow bay 14-kilometer strip.



Evpatoria is considered one of the most ancient cities of Crimea. Its history dates back to the VI century. BC e., when settlers from Greece founded a trading post here called Karkinitida. From this moment, Evpatoria began to accumulate archaeological sites of different peoples. Karkinitida owned by the Romans, Scythians, then it was robbed by the Huns, Goths, Khazars, Pechenegs.

At the end of the 15th century, where Evpatoria later appeared, the Gezlev fortress was erected. Nowadays, there are preserved Teki dervishes (monastery), Khan's mosque (Juma-Jami), Karaite houses, a Turkish bath. The city began to be called Evpatoria in 1784, after entering the Russian Empire.
