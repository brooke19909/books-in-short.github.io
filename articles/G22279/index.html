Is there a water park in Petropavlovsk?

Water parks and indoor swimming pools are very popular among people living in the cold regions of our country. What could be better than in the middle of a harsh Russian winter to break out on a weekend in the warm summer, swim with all your heart, ride a water slide and bask in the sauna. Is there a water park in Petropavlovsk? A detailed answer to this question is specifically for our readers in this article.

"Neptune" - the center of rest and health



In the fall of 2012, the first center of water entertainment and related recreational and recreational services was opened in the city of Petropavlovsk. On its territory, guests enjoy year-round swimming pools (including a jacuzzi), attractions for children and adults, as well as baths and saunas. As conceived by the creators, this complex will help to popularize a healthy lifestyle and help diversify the leisure of the city residents and its guests. The water park in Petropavlovsk is designed to comfortably accommodate thousands of visitors per hour.

Fun at the water park and related services



Most people come to the water entertainment complex in order to ride the rides. Neptune Waterpark in Petropavlovsk will delight guests with water slides made by White Water. Among them there are 4 attractions for adults and adolescents and 3 for children. Also in the water park there are 10 hydromassage units and 4 Jacuzzi bathtubs. Guests are invited to warm up, at choice: a hamam, a Russian bath or a Finnish sauna. Waterpark in Petropavlovsk is a real center of a healthy lifestyle. In the complex "Neptune" you can also visit the gym, sports center, undergo spa-procedures and use the services of a medical office. In addition, the aquaclub offers group aerobics classes, swimming lessons and other wellness programs for children and adults. There is also a cafe in the complex where you can quickly have a bite to eat or have a full lunch.

Waterpark in Petropavlovsk: photos of the complex and prices



Complex "Neptune" is located at the address: Petropavlovsk city, Medvedev street, building 41. The water park is open daily from 8.00 to 23.00. A standard ticket allows you to spend 2 hours in the complex. Its cost on weekends is: 2500 tenge for adults and 2000 tenge for children. On weekdays, the price of visiting the water park is much lower. Discounts are provided for customers on their birthday, and if desired, a holiday can be celebrated in the complex. Waterpark "Neptune" in Petropavlovsk implements charity programs. Periodically, visits to the pool and attractions are organized for children from orphanages and boarding schools, as well as children from dysfunctional families. Much attention is paid to hygiene and safety conditions. Once every three hours, complete filtration and purification of water in the pools is performed. Rescuers are constantly working at the water park. Neptune is a great place for families. The water park in Petropavlovsk is very popular among residents and visitors of the city. Come here to appreciate all the advantages of this complex personally.
