Address of the Avanta Hotel (Novosibirsk)

This hotel has an ideal location. The bustling city center is three kilometers away. But within walking distance there are three metro stations at once. Thanks to the subway, you will be in the business center of Novosibirsk in ten minutes.

However, it is a very quiet, calm and respectable place. Guests do not feel at all on the outskirts, because some sights of the city, such as the opera and ballet theater, the church of Alexander Nevsky, the cathedral mosque, the banks of the Ob, go no more than fifteen minutes.

The hotel address is as follows: Novosibirsk, Dzerzhinsky district, Gogol street, 189/1. If you look at the map of the city, the hotel is located in its northern part. Hotel guests put "Avante" 7 points out of ten for the location. This is a quiet residential area where the city hospital is located, many kindergartens. Well developed infrastructure. Near the hotel there are grocery stores, pharmacies, ATMs. 600 meters from the Avanta Hotel is located the Siberian Mall shopping center.

How to get there

The Dzerzhinsky district of Novosibirsk is connected to other parts of the city by a well-established public transport network. A seven-minute leisurely walk from the Avanta Hotel is the Birch Grove recreation park.

This is a wonderful place to walk at any time of the year. On its territory is the Assumption Church and a small pond. There is a park on Dzerzhinsky Avenue.

Near the PKiO "Birch Grove" (Novosibirsk) there is a public transport stop. You can drive here:

  • on buses No. 6, 18, 35, 30, 41, 39 and 44;
  • trolleybus number 23 (convenient for travelers from the railway station), 10 and 22;
  • minibuses No. 48 and 44a.

The Avanta Hotel is located almost at the intersection of Gogol and Krasin Streets. Therefore, it is most convenient to access it by buses 19 and 64. The stop is only 130 meters from the hotel entrance - two minutes walk.

If you prefer underground transport to avoid traffic jams, the nearest station is a quarter-hour walk away. It is called "Birch Grove." Novosibirsk has a not too branched metro. There are only two lines. Station "Birch Grove" belongs to the green branch.

By the way, the Avanta hotel offers its guests a private transfer to the airport and railway station and back for little money.

Territory

This hotel is urban. Therefore, it has no territory except a small parking lot in front of the entrance. But the building of the Avanta Hotel (Novosibirsk) impresses passers-by.

It was built in 2011 on an individual project. The building is an eight-story building, beautifully lined with blue glass. The Russian Architectural Festival awarded the hotel the highest award - the Golden Capital.

The administration did not put four stars on the facade on its own whim. This classification is confirmed by the State Certificate, which can be seen at the reception.

The hotel is open around the clock. Guests are always welcome here. There are a lot of hotels in Novosibirsk, but it is in Avanta that the touring stars who come to the city stop.
