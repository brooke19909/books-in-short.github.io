Wireless earphones "Sinheiser": description, specifications, models and reviews

In recent years, manufacturers of acoustics and portable player devices have experimented with one way or another with designs and materials, which, however, has allowed us to fill the market with quite interesting offers. The revolutionary breakthrough was the introduction of wireless systems that saved users from inconvenient wires. The first models of this type were based on infrared signal transmission modules, which averted a considerable part of users from the new concept. Key Features

The fundamental drawback of models operating without a direct connection is the presence of interference in signal transmission. In order to compensate for this shortcoming, the developers had to improve the characteristics that Sennheiser headphones provide: type - wireless, frequency range - on average from 22 to 22 000 Hz, impedance - 106 Ohms. In terms of signal reception for different models, the indicators diverge greatly. Moreover, you can talk about high-quality and stable reception only at home, that is, at a distance of 5-10 m.

Reviews on RS 110 Headphones

From the very first model of this type, the creators failed to complete the task of high-quality sound. This is a representative of the entry-level family, which embodied all the common shortcomings of wireless models. In particular, users note a strong intrusion of background noise, an inconvenient design, as well as sound permeability. That is, Sennheiser RS 110 wireless headphones not only pass third-party noise through isolation, but the sound from them is heard in close surroundings. However, even in an unsuccessful model, the manufacturer retains the inherent advantages of the company. This is, first of all, high-quality assembly and ergonomics, although there are different opinions on this. In addition, a big plus of this version is a modest price for models of this type, amounting to 3.5-4 thousand rubles.



Reviews on RS 120 Headphones

In this version, a better study of the structural basis is noted. According to the owners, physically, the headphones are almost not felt thanks to the soft and comfortable ear pads. Hours of listening does not contribute to neck fatigue, which is a significant advantage for music lovers. But the inconvenient placement of controls is emphasized - the right cup is equipped with two wheels, which are responsible for the sound settings and volume. The problem is that they are placed under each other, which causes discomfort when performing manipulations. As for the sound, the Sinheiser wireless headphones in the RS 120 modification show an average result. Comparing with wired models, users do not highly appreciate the German development, but adjusted for the general capabilities of the representatives of the radio segment, the device still copes with signal processing with dignity.





Reviews on RS 195 Headphones

Again, the model does not cause claims in terms of embodiment of the design. A comfortable headband relieves your ears of pressure. But the main advantage of this version is precisely in the sound quality, which has been improved thanks to Kleer technology. The owners of this model compare the quality of its work with CD players, which in itself is a noticeable step towards clean playback. In any case, expert reviews speak of the superiority of the model over devices working with the Bluetooth channel. Sennheiser wireless headphones broadcast a signal with a bitrate of 320 kbps, almost without losing complex frequencies in processing. Of course, a new level of quality could not but affect the price tag. In this case, it is 15-16 thousand rubles.

Conclusion

Sennheiser is considered almost the reference manufacturer of audio equipment. Until recently, the company was criticized except for the boring design, which, of course, did not belittle the main advantages of the product - acoustic. The segment in which the Sinheiser wireless headphones are presented is not easy for German developers. If there are no special complaints about the design, then there are problems in the sound, which users point to. This is confirmed by the RS 110 model and, in some ways, the older version with the index 120. But the appearance of RS 195 headphones changed the situation radically. This development has demonstrated that wireless technologies can provide good sound without interference and noise blockages during the broadcast.
