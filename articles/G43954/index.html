Hotel Fun & Sun Jacaranda resort in Belek

The magnificent resort hotel under the name Fun & Sun Jacaranda Resort 5 * is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea in a majestic setting surrounded by citrus trees and evergreen pines.





This complex offers a magnificent comfortable and interesting vacation for families with children (both young and old), newlyweds and married couples with experience, as well as older people. Originally designed hotel zones will make it easy to navigate and not waste time searching for the place you are interested in. Designers of Fun & Sun Jacaranda Resort made sure that every guest feels cozy and comfortable here. A huge green area will allow you to spend time peacefully and take a break from the noisy fun, but as soon as you gain strength, at any time you can return to the thick of events: entertainment for every taste and sports activities.

As for the location of the complex, Fun & Sun Jacaranda Resort is located in the Turkish resort village of Belek on the seashore. The hotel was built in 2004, and in 2012 it was renovated.





In total, the hotel complex includes a six-story main building and 49 two-story bungalows. Thus, Fun & Sun Jacaranda Resort has 650 rooms. The rooms of the main building are represented by 117 standard rooms and 184 family rooms. The bungalows accommodate 331 standard rooms and 18 family rooms. Each room is equipped with a bathroom, air conditioning, telephone, television with 12 Russian-language channels, a minibar, a hairdryer, a safe and a balcony with a wonderful view. Room service, daily housekeeping and linen change are also provided. The hotel's food category is Ultra All Inclusive.

The complex offers four outdoor pools of various sizes with clean water and one indoor. There are also four water slides, two of which are for children. The hotel has an amphitheater, an open-air cinema and a TV room. If you want to buy something from clothes or beach accessories or souvenirs for friends and relatives, then by all means look at the shopping center located on the territory of the complex.





There is also a gym, tennis courts, a minigolf course, darts, beach volleyball, archery, interesting animation, live music and game rooms for the youngest guests.

In addition, Fun & Sun Jacaranda Resort provides its guests with paid services of a doctor, beauty salon and laundry.

As for beach holidays, the hotel has its own 300-meter-long beach, equipped with showers, changing cabins, towels, sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses.

If you have not yet decided on a summer vacation, then feel free to go to Fun & Sun Jacaranda Resort, reviews of enthusiastic guests of which can be found in abundance on the Internet. In this magnificent hotel complex, you will spend unforgettable time and relax with both your soul and body, indulging in a wide variety of entertainments or simply basking on a wonderful beach.
