Sunlight Garden Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Side, Manavgat): description, service, reviews

Manavgat (Side) is an incredibly cozy town, which is located on the banks of a picturesque full-flowing river with the same name. This pond is part of the resort area, which attracts tourists more and more every year. This place was created for those people who want to visit new and unexplored corners of Turkey. It is this city that welcomes each guest with incredibly beautiful nature, which will give harmony to soul and body. Sights of Manavgat (Turkey) are fully accessible for tourists at any time of the year.

Manavgat Waterfall





The mountain river, which is called Manavgat, is famous not only among local residents, but also enjoys great popularity among tourists. The waterfall is incredibly beautiful. Against its background, you can make a huge number of impressive photos. The waterfall itself is located near the city of Side. To get to it, you need to overcome only 3 kilometers. This route can be reached even on foot, enjoying the local beauties. The waterfall reaches a height of 2 meters, a width of 40 meters, it can not be called big, but its beauty has already won many hearts and continues to amaze its fans. If there is a bright sunny day outside, then an incredibly beautiful rainbow can be observed at the waterfall, which is reflected in the spray of water. In spring, snow melts in the mountains, the river becomes full-flowing, and walks in these surroundings are quite dangerous. Not far from the river there are gardens where you can relax, enjoy the fresh air and just enjoy nature. In addition, on the banks of the waterfall there are a huge number of cozy and wonderful cafes and restaurants where you can try a variety of delicious dishes. There are also souvenir shops where you can buy wonderful gifts for relatives and friends.
You can visit not only the natural attractions of Manavgat (Turkey). There are unusual architectural structures and unique temple buildings.

Big mosque

This mosque is one of the most pompous in Antalya. It is located near the Alanya-Antalya road, in one of the most elite and expensive areas of the city. It is this mosque that is listed as a place to visit. It has 29 domes that amaze with their beauty. The entire minaret completes this composition, which complements the overall ensemble. It is amazing that at the same time 4,000 people can comfortably accommodate in these walls. The mosque is incredibly large, the sounds from it are carried far down the street, which adds greatness to it. To get to the temple, you must go through certain procedures. During the service tourists are not allowed. But in order to get there, it is necessary to wait for the divine service, after which it is necessary to undergo the ablution ceremony, and then you can enter the mosque without fear. The interior of the mosque is incredibly rich, the painting is really amazing, you can also observe a large number of diverse and beautiful carpets.

These and other attractions can be seen from one of the cozy hotels of Manavgat. A great budget choice for a tourist can be the hotel Sunlight Garden Hotel 3.
