The best headphones up to 1000 rubles: review, rating and selection tips

Music includes a huge number of meanings, concepts and has been with us since time immemorial. The 20th century brought its own, and considerable adjustments to this area. Not only a host of new musical directions saw the light, but also equipment for their reproduction.

Along with players, smartphones, radio players and computers, headphones occupy a special place. The latter helps to completely immerse yourself in what is happening on the screen or surrender to the music without looking back at the people rustling nearby and the noise of the city.

On the shelves you can see a lot of such devices. But not everyone is willing to pay for models from the premium segment. Of course, they offer exceptional sound quality and extensive functionality. But many consumers are looking for something simpler and cheaper. As a price threshold for such requests, you can take a sum of 1000 rubles that is quite acceptable for most users.

Our article will help you choose the right headphone option and not miscalculate your purchase. In addition, to facilitate the flour of choice, we will provide a list of the most interesting models that can be found on sale.

Selection tips

If you are thinking about buying headphones worth up to 1000 rubles, you should know that any of your choice, whatever it may be, will be a compromise. You can find a more or less optimal device only by properly prioritizing. First of all, we will talk about portability and sound quality.

That is, if one decreases, then the other increases. A small speaker will never give out the volume and amazing sound experience, but the modest dimensions of such devices allow you to carry them with you anywhere and anytime.

At the same time, large headphones have a better sound and offer a more complete immersion in a song or game. But at the same time they have a decent mass and an impressive size design. So you will need to decide which is more important - portability or sound quality.

Application area

Just like any other device, headphones are selected to solve specific tasks and fulfill specific goals. Here you need to clarify for yourself how and when you will use them. The selected scenario largely determines the future form factor of the model.

Home and office

For a home or office, as a rule, they buy full-size closed- type headphones with the most comfortable fit on their heads. This type is great for long work or listening to songs. In addition, good soundproofing qualities will not distract you from classes.

You can also try and overhead type devices, which are noticeably more compact than full-size models. Ears do not sweat from them and they are practically not felt on the head, but noise isolation leaves much to be desired. So they are suitable only for office workers and those who got a quiet room.

When choosing a full-size headphone, be sure to pay attention to the quality of the material from which the ear pads are made. Synthetics or velor will not make your ears sweat so much, unlike natural or artificial leather.

Town

Vacuum earplugs are perfect for city walks and trips in public transport. They are located inside the ear canal and deliver sound immediately to the destination. In addition, such models weigh a little and are compact, which creates a minimum of problems when moving in a crowd of people.

The ear pads of such headphones are made mainly of silicone. The latter allows manufacturers to not limit themselves in colors and sizes. The ear pads made of porous foam are quite popular. They are also called anatomical. When heated by body temperature, they take the form of an ear canal. Products made of porous foam are less durable than their silicone counterparts, but they transmit sound with almost no distortion.

Be sure to pay attention to the braid. Ordinary budget wires very poorly tolerate low temperatures and break. But a rubberized or fabric-protected cord will feel much better in extreme cold.

Games

The choice of headphones for gamers is striking in variety, but in most cases there is only one form factor - full-sized closed models. A mandatory attribute of such devices is a chat microphone.

Another important criterion is surround sound. Imitation of solid systems of 5.1 or even 7.1 format will be an excellent advantage in 3D battles, where you will not only hear every rustle, but also feel its direction.

Also, you must pay attention to the length of the wire and its braid. If an ordinary music lover can sit comfortably in an armchair, having adopted a favorite pose, and enjoy the composition, then the gamer actively works with the keyboard and moves his head. The cord of the gaming headphones should be at least two meters long and with a reliable braid made of fabric or supple rubber.

It will also be useful to clarify the presence of special thickenings at the plugs. They not only eliminate kinks and kinks, but also help prevent accidental breakdown of the connector when in a rush a player jumps up and forgets that he has headphones on his head.

Noise reduction is also an important criterion. And this applies not only to the headphones themselves, but also to the microphone. You cannot fully immerse yourself in the world of competitive games if you are distracted by someone or something from the outside. The same goes for the microphone. During communication with friends, extra sounds only interfere and do not allow you to concentrate on what was said.

Plug

A lot of consumers forget about it and with annoyance take the purchase back to the store just because the wrong plug was selected. Well, if you bought the device somewhere around the corner, and if in an online store, then the return turns into a real headache.

Almost all models of headphones, which are presented in computer stores and mobile phone stores, come with a standard mini-jack plug - 3.5 mm (photo above).
