Curl Secret Babyliss: specifications and reviews

Curl Secret Babyliss curling iron revolutionized the fashion world when it first appeared on the shelves. Women literally chased this novelty, despite the rather high cost of forceps, and shops did not have time to order goods from the warehouse. After some time, passions subsided, however, the demand for a styler does not fall to this day. Moreover, there are many analogues of this model (high-quality and not very), which are sold for more modest money.

What is the reason for the popularity of Curl Secret?

How can I curl my hair? It would seem that the answer is obvious: you clamp the tip of the lock with a curling iron, wind it around the heating element. But what about hair on the back of the head that is not visible or is out of reach? Well, somehow. Your best bet is to ask someone else to help. Cases when the hair at the temples are twisted perfectly, and barely perceptible waves are visible from the back, are self-curling constantly.

The Babyliss Curl Secret electric tongs solved this problem radically. They automatically tighten the lock and wind it around the heating element. It is enough just to clamp the tip of the hair - the rest will complete the tongs themselves. It is equally convenient for a styler to curl hair both at the temples, and on the back of the head, and on the top of the head.

The range of stylers from "Babybiliss"

Only one model was originally released - the Babyliss C 901 Curl Secret. However, progress does not stand still. The Babyliss C 1100E Curl Secret followed by the latest Babyliss M iraCurl model followed . By their action, all these stylers are of the same type, they differ only in small options.

Description and specifications of Babyliss C901 Curl Secret

Externally, the model resembles tongs with a roller heater in a plastic case. There are practically no open elements, so the probability of getting a burn is minimal. The temperature controller is set to two operating modes plus shutdown. There is a button for quick heating to maximum temperature (in this model it is 205 degrees). You can also note a long cord rotating on a hinge. When working, he turns in the right direction and does not bend, allowing you to move around the room, and not sit at the outlet for the entire period of curling.





There are two temperature settings for styling. The first is 185 degrees, designed for brittle, split and weakened hair. The second is 205 degrees. It is suitable for normal, healthy hair. To determine when the device is ready for use, the light indicator located on the housing will help. When the tongs are turned on, it starts to blink, and when the set temperature is reached, it glows.

Stacking procedure

Before starting work, it is necessary to prepare the hair - it must be clean, dry and well combed. Hair styling starts from the lower back of the head, highlighting locks with a width of 3 cm. Position Curl Secret Babyliss so that the cylinder is turned to the head, the hair is placed strictly between the two handles, clamped from the end with your hand and close the styler. The closer the lock is captured to the head, the longer the curl will turn out. The tongs are equipped with a warning system. If the owner forgets to release the tip of the hair and the device cannot wind it, a signal will sound. The same thing happens if the lock is too big. This system eliminates hair tangling. When the estimated time for warm-up comes out, an audible signal will sound again - the forceps handles can be opened and the lock released. To make the hairstyle look natural, the styler changes the direction of rotation and the curls turn curled in different directions.

Safety system

To save energy and prevent fire, the curler is equipped with an automatic shutdown system. If the device is not used for an hour, it ceases to heat up. Thus, the owner does not have to worry that she left the included curling iron, gathering in a hurry for the holiday. In addition , if Babyliss C 901PE Curl Secret is not used for more than 20 minutes, it will go into standby mode and reduce the surface heating temperature to 150 degrees. To activate the styler, just squeeze and unclench the handles.

Babyliss C901 Curl Secret

According to customer reviews, the main advantage of the Babyliss C901PE Curl Secret is that this styler minimizes the number of manual operations and automates the curling process.





Curls are obtained in the same size, the styling process is reduced. Girls who happen to get into such an unpleasant situation argue that the locks can get stuck quite badly. The forceps must be pulled out of the socket so as not to burn the hair. Another feature of the styler is its high cost compared to conventional curling irons. Babyliss Fashion Curl Secret is an order of magnitude higher than manual models in which curls need to be wound on their own, but at the same time almost all owners claim that this is a reasonable payment for convenience. Complaints sometimes cause the dimensions and weight of the device. Compared to conventional curling irons, Babyliss Curl Secret is rather massive and bulky. Owner reviews indicate: when using additional styling products (foam or air conditioning), the effect of the styler is much more noticeable.

Features and specifications of Babyliss C1100E Curl Secret

Babyliss c1100e Curl Secret IONIC is a modification of the previously released Babyliss C 901 PE curling iron . In many ways, these stylers are similar. The principle of action is identical, identical to the shutdown system. The main modes of operation differ. The maximum heating of these forceps is 230 degrees, while in the previous model it was only 205. Accordingly, it is easier to curl heavy, stiff, poorly styled hair with such a curling iron. In addition, the owner has the opportunity to set the heating time itself - 8, 10 or 12 seconds. The end of each interval is accompanied by a notification.

Warming the curl for 8 seconds will provide smooth waves (the tongs will emit 3 beeps). If you need to get soft curls, the locks can withstand 10 seconds. At the end of the work, the curling iron will emit 4 signals. And finally, if you need cool, tight curls, choose a heating time of 12 seconds. Another feature of the model is a built-in ionizer, which neutralizes static electricity and gives hair shine.

Reviews on Babyliss 1100E Curl Secret IONIC

Compared with the previous version of the automatic curling, Babyliss Curl Secret IONIC compares favorably with the ability to select the duration of heating, and not just the mode. Analogs

On sale there are many analogues of the Babyliss C01PE Curl Secret model and further modifications of this curling iron. They cost significantly less and at first glance are no different from the original. However, as the experience of buyers shows, the difference in assembly quality is quite significant, despite the fact that China is the manufacturer in all cases.

Cheap counterparts are much more likely to fail after several uses, while the original model for many works for several years. In addition, budget tongs from little-known manufacturers are much more likely to chew hair during styling, often several times in a row. Given this feature, the buyer needs to weigh all the risks, deciding to purchase a particular model.

Care and Cleaning

Like any other technique, the forceps require careful care and cleaning. After several curling sessions, the remnants of detergents and styling foams, particles of hair may stick to them. If the styler is not cleaned on time, when it is turned on, it will emit an unpleasant smell, the curls will begin to ‚Äústick‚Äù to the dirty heater, which, in turn, will interfere with the normal operation of the device. Therefore, the kit includes a U-shaped felt sleeve. Before washing it is immersed in a soapy solution. Then with a sponge wipe the roller and the inside of the tongs. After washing, the styler is wiped dry, and the sleeve is rinsed. Of course, during cleaning, the appliance must be disconnected from the mains.
