Tbilisi is a paradise for a thinking person, as the city is rich in intellectual and cultural heritage, which includes rare museums and galleries, enchanting churches, divine cuisine, craft heritage.

Rooms Hotel Tbilisi belongs to the Room Hotel group of hotels, which is also part of the heritage due to the merger of the Georgian tradition with modernism in the field of hotel services - a local modern movement. Wooden facades, severe windows, together with glass panels reflect the identity of the city with the hotel.

About hotel

Rooms Hotel Tbilisi is a new, modern hotel in the Vera district of Tbilisi. The hotel received 4 stars, however, according to many critics, it deserves a much higher rating than many five-star Tbilisi hotels. This hotel is the twin brother of Rooms Hotels Kazbegi, which is one of the best in Georgia in terms of scale and level of service.

The hotel is three hundred meters from the center to the beginning of Rustaveli Avenue and the Georgian metro station of the same name, as well as 2 kilometers to the border of the Old Town.

Rooms & Suites

125 rooms of the hotel are a symbiosis of the New York style of the 30s of the 20th century and the charm of Tbilisi thanks to expensive leather trim, local works of art, custom-made furniture. The client should expect a chic bohemian atmosphere of dark wooden floors, upholstered beds, with sharp corners and iron elements of post-industrial aesthetics. Each room at Hotel Rooms Tbilisi is elegantly decorated with a range of unique, contemporary handmade silk-screened wallpapers.





The rooms offer views of the lively Hovelidze Street or the garden, and the terrace is located on the fifth floor of the hotel.

The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, minibar, LCD flat-screen TV. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and slippers for each guest.

Infrastructure and services Rooms Hotel Tbilisi

For the hotel visitor is invited to visit the restaurant, bar. There is also a separation of rooms for smokers and non-smokers. Absolutely for all customers throughout the hotel there is free Wi-Fi.

The reception is open around the clock, including an accelerated check-in and check-out process, and there is also a free left-luggage office if you need to leave things before departure from the hotel or before check-in.





Business customers can make use of the conference room. The hotel offers laundry, dry cleaning, ironing by staff on request, shoe shine.

The only food option is breakfast only, but for an additional fee you can buy food from the restaurant's menu, as well as request a special diet menu.

There is a possibility of transfer from and to the airport, transfer to somewhere is available at an additional cost.

Based on the concept of the hotel, as well as on the feedback of visitors, it is worth to conclude that in reality the hotel is worthy. The kind staff and level service at Room Hotels Tbilisi will make you return to Georgia again.
