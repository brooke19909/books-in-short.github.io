Knitting needles: simple patterns

An important quality of knitted snacks is the ability to drape well. Knitted from a very thick thread, it loses the ability to soft drape, but looks great due to the texture of the yarn. Knitting with a garter stitch, as already mentioned, is very simple. All rows of fabric are knitted with facial loops. To obtain a plastic edge, edge loops are performed: the first loop of the row is always removed, and the last is sewn on the wrong side.

Double-sided knitting patterns for a snout



The classic garter stitch, like almost all types of elastic bands, is a two-sided pattern. For such a wardrobe detail as snood, this is very important. It twists and shows the same pattern on both sides, which looks very profitable, despite the fact that the products use the simplest patterns for a knitting needles. Schemes for them are not needed, drawings are easily performed according to the description. Such a pattern looks identical on both sides: a large braid of 16 loops connected by alternating front and back stripes (like a 2x2 elastic band) on a garter weave. The braid is knitted 16 rows, in the 17th weaves: 8 loops are dropped on an additional knitting needle in front of the canvas, 8 loops of the second part of the braid are knitted, and then loops from an additional knitting needle. The braid pattern is necessarily maintained during interweaving, which creates a two-sided canvas effect.
