Hotel Morjim Hermitage 2 * (India, Goa): description, rooms, reviews

Morjim Resort is located in the very popular Indian state of North Goa. The main distinguishing feature of this settlement is the presence of the Russian community. In Morjim, at the moment there is even a street on which cafes, yoga schools, guesthouses and other institutions owned by our former compatriots work. And actually the infrastructure of this village itself is aimed primarily at Russian-speaking tourists. Among other things, there are, of course, in the village of Morjim and comfortable inexpensive hotels. For example, many of our compatriots choose the hotel Morjim Hermitage 2 * for accommodation in this resort.

Hotel Location Features

This hotel in Morjim is located on the first coastline - just 100 meters from a convenient beach. Hotel guests need no more than 3-5 minutes to get to the sea. The nearest bus station to the hotel is only 15 km from it - in Mapus. Dabolim Airport, where in most cases tourists come from Russia who rented rooms at this hotel, is located 56 km from the hotel. That is, domestic vacationers travel to the place for a very long time - about 1.5 hours.





Of course, the easiest way to get to the hotel is by taxi. Those tourists who find this method too costly can take a bus to Mapusa. Getting to Morjim from the airport by public transport is thus a snap. However, tourists who have booked rooms at the Morjim Hermitage 2 * hotel , most likely, will not have to take care of such things at all. The fact is that most domestic tour operators selling permits to Morjim with accommodation at this hotel provide their customers with a shuttle service as well. That is, they bring vacationers from the airport directly to the hotel gates on a comfortable bus.

general description

The hotel is hosted by the Morjim Hermitage 2 * hotel (India, North Goa, Morjim) in one rather large main building and several small bungalows of rather pleasant architecture and convenient layout . The facades of the main building are painted blue, and the parapets of the balconies are white. The view and the main building of the hotel are thus quite harmonious. The hotel bungalows are built of wood and also have a pretty attractive appearance.

The reception at this hotel is not in the building, but simply in the gazebo, which at the same time serves as the dining room. Actually, the hotel territory itself is a rectangle 12 m wide and about 300 m long. The hotel courtyard is well landscaped and, according to many tourists, looks quite attractive.





Number of rooms

If desired, guests of the village of Morjim have the opportunity to rent a hotel room for every taste and budget in the Morjim Hermitage Hotel. The hotel provides tourists with class rooms:

  • Suite Rooms with balcony.

  • Standard Room with bathtub.

  • Family cottage.

  • Wooden Beach Cottage.

All types of rooms in the hotel, according to many vacationers, are equipped very well. In their rooms, hotel guests have the opportunity to use:

  • a refrigerator;

  • safe.

All rooms have small balconies where it is convenient to dry beach items. Also in the rooms of the hotel there are also quite comfortable individual showers. Some have a TV. The family apartments also include kitchenettes. Of course, all rooms are air-conditioned.





Guest reviews of hotel rooms

The opinion of the guests about the rooms in this hotel was quite good. Most tourists believe that living in Morjim Hermitage is quite convenient. Good reviews handed over at this hotel were given primarily for the large area and uninterrupted operation of household appliances. Many vacationers also really like the presence in the rooms of a large number of outlets. According to many tourists , the design is quite sophisticated, but they are very good for charging phones. Ventilation in the hotel rooms is adjustable.

Hot water is always available in the showers of this hotel. Which, of course, can also be attributed to its advantages. The pressure of water in the shower in Morjim Hermitage 2 *, judging by the reviews of tourists, is almost always very good. Power outages in the hotel are quite rare and for a short time. In addition, especially for such cases, the rooms have large battery lights.

There are mixed reviews about the cleaning of the rooms in this hotel. Some guests believe that the hotel maids treat their duties well. According to others, hotel employees clean rooms in rooms not as thoroughly as we would like. But bed linen and towels, according to most guests, are changed here quite often.

In addition to not too thorough cleaning, some of the shortcomings of the hotel rooms include the absence of mosquito nets on the windows. Unfortunately, flies and mosquitoes often fly into rooms in a hotel. Ants can also crawl in the rooms. Therefore, crumbs from food on tables, floors and beds in this hotel is better not to leave.

In order for the maids to clean the hotel room, the tenants should leave the key at the reception. This is the majority of tourists attributed to the advantages of the Morjim Hermitage 2 * hotel . The population in India is very poor, and in local hotels, unfortunately, cases of theft are not uncommon. Specifically, there are no negative reviews about Morjim Hermitage Hotel in this regard. However, risk should still not, of course. Leaving the key at the reception to guests is worth putting all the securities and things in a safe.





Hotel Infrastructure

Although the Morjim Hermitage 2 * hotel (India, North Goa) is only a two-star class, a lot of amenities are provided for tourists on its territory. If desired, guests can use services such as:

  • wireless Internet;

  • library;

  • rental point for mopeds, cars and bikes;

  • exchange office;

  • baggage storage room.





The hotel also has a laundry room with special equipment installed in it (cars, ironing boards, irons). Parking on site has its own. Unfortunately, there is no pool in the courtyard of this hotel. For improvement, residents of the complex can also visit the massage room.

What tourists think about hotel infrastructure

As with the rooms, the service provided at this hotel is mostly just good reviews. Many tourists like it, for example, because on the territory of the Morjim Hermitage 2 * hotel (India, North Goa) it very well catches Wi-Fi. Of course, many attribute the presence of a rental point for bikes and cars to the unconditional advantages of the hotel. In addition, the hotel provides several small free parking lots, which are located between the bungalows.





A feature of this hotel is that smoking on its territory is completely free (everywhere except at the reception). Some tourists like this, others, of course, not too much. The exchange rate at the hotel, according to many tourists, is quite profitable. But in the village itself, if desired, you can find points in which local money can be purchased cheaper.

Mostly Russian tourists and Indians live in the hotel. No noisy Chinese or Koreans in the rooms of this hotel are not populated.

Hotel Morjim Hermitage 2 *: traveler reviews of food

Rooms for rent in this complex, of course, with breakfast. To the dining room in the hotel, its former guests also in most cases have no special complaints. Dishes are served quality, fresh and warmed up. The only, as in many other two and even three-star Goa hotels, the menu at Morjim Hermitage is quite monotonous. Served for breakfast here are mainly omelets, scrambled eggs, toast, jam and drinks. In addition to the dining room, there is a small bar on site.

Hotel guests can have dinner and lunch in the village of Morjim. There are a lot of shakes and cafes here. The food in some of these establishments, as in almost any other resort, is good, in some it is not very good. But in any case, finding a decent inexpensive cafe for the guests of the Morjim Hermitage 2 * hotel will most likely not work.

Beach Hotels

Too long to get to the sea, the residents of the Morjim Hermitage Hotel , as already mentioned, do not have to. Own beach at the hotel, since it is only a two-star, of course, no. Its guests are usually visited by the public, belonging to the village of Morjim itself.

Wide and long beaches - this is what distinguishes North Goa, Morjim. Morjim Hermitage 2 * was built almost on the ocean) in this regard is no exception. The beach located next to the hotel is quite long - 3 km. His width is also significant. Because of these sizes, a special flea market is never observed here.

Opinion of the hotel guests about the beach

Good reviews of tourists passing near the Morjim Hermitage 2 * hotel, the ocean line has earned including the fact that they clean it almost every day. In terms of cleanliness on this beach, tourists have absolutely no complaints. Water in the ocean itself in this place, as elsewhere in North Goa, can be completely transparent in good weather and unclear after a storm. In any case, she is always very warm.





Actually from the hotel on the beach, sun loungers and umbrellas are not offered. However, hotel guests can at any time rent these convenient accessories in necks and cafes located on the coastline. By the way, you can take them completely free. But only after ordering in these institutions any food and drink.

The infrastructure of the village near the hotel

Actually in the village of Morjim , the Morjim Hermitage 2 * hotel is located somewhat on the outskirts. The hotel guests have to go about 15-20 minutes to the nearest market and shops with vegetables and fruits. To save time, many residents of the hotel go by taxi for groceries and other necessary things. If you wish, you can call him directly at the reception.
