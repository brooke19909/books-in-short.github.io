Kata Silver Sand by Eazy 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): hotel description, services, reviews

Phuket is located in the south of Thailand. To many, the island, equal in area to the territory of Singapore, resembles a mountain that grows directly from sea waters. Translated from Malay, its name really means "mountain". Phuket has been known to sailors for a long time. Merchants and sailors, from the Chinese to the English, have long noted the convenient location of the island on the way from China to India.

Modern Phuket, with its white sandy beaches washed by the gentle waters of the Andaman Sea, is considered by many to be a tropical paradise. The island is the place where the most wonderful beaches in the world and the best resort hotels are located. One of the recognized most successful vacation accommodation options is the Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * hotel.



About the resort

The tourism business in Phuket is based on the principle of absolute conservation of the natural environment. A wonderful variety of numerous local hotels is developing and improving on the island: from the most expensive and exclusive hotels to cheap and very popular beach bungalows. The extraordinary beauty of the local nature, the hills covered with lush tropical vegetation, stunning views, snow-white beaches and rocky bays, the clear azure of the sea - all this attracts a huge number of tourists.

In addition to the pristine purity of pristine nature, guests are attracted to the island by excellent transport links to the mainland, interesting sights, a wealth of opportunities for entertainment and outdoor activities, as well as a high level of service and conditions for a real comfortable stay that distinguish local hotels.

Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * (Phuket): Acquaintance

The hotel was built in 2009. It has 60 rooms with garden or city views. Maximum occupancy is 3 guests. Area - 25 square meters. m. Cards for payment: Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa, American Express. Located in a floor building. Hotel size is average.



Description

Hotel Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * is located on the quiet, calm outskirts of the charming resort of Kata Beach. On site, guests have access to a swimming pool (outdoor), a restaurant (the menu consists of local dishes) and a tour desk. It offers accommodation in modern air-conditioned rooms with a balcony (personal) and TV (flat screen, cable channels).

Furnished balconies Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * delight residents with magnificent views of the tropical landscapes. The rooms are decorated with classic-style wooden furniture. The design uses home textiles from a local factory. Amenities include a private shower room with hot and cold water.

From Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * Hotel you can walk to shops located on Kata Beach in 10 minutes. It takes about 20 minutes to lively Patong Beach, and about 45 minutes to Phuket International Airport. Those who wish to independently explore the sights of Phuket in Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * are given the opportunity to rent a moped or bicycle. Guests also have parking and Wi-Fi in public areas (free).

The hotel's al fresco restaurant serves American breakfast and local seafood.

Location

Hotel Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * (Phuket) is located on the 2nd line. Many vacationers find its location very convenient.

The hotel is 550 meters from Kata Beach (7 minutes walk). The airport is located 65 km from the hotel (40-50 minutes). Patong Beach is 16 km away. Phuket's Old Town is a 30-minute drive away.

Address: Thailand, 83100, Phuket, Muang, Karon, Kata Road, 70. Phone: 076-333-280. The cost of a taxi from the airport to the hotel: from 1870 rubles.

Infrastructure

On the territory of Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * (Superior), guests get the opportunity to use the Wi-Fi connection, a restaurant (Thai and international dishes on the menu), parking, reception, pool (open), safes for storing valuables, towels and sun loungers at pool (can be used for free). Laundry at the hotel provides services for a fee.



Service

  • Doctor on call.
  • Security at the hotel (24 hours).
  • Motorcycle and car rental.
  • Organization of transfer.
  • Room cleaning.

For entertainment and sports

For full leisure time, the hotel has a small outdoor pool, which provides for the presence of a children's section. Sun beds, mattresses, umbrellas and towels by the pool can be used for free. The hotel lacks animation and other entertainment. It offers guests a Thai massage and spa treatments. On the beach you can enjoy beach and water sports.



For children

Children are offered a special section in the swimming pool. Babies from 8 months to 3 years old are provided with cots (on request). There is no animation.

Beach

City beach (2 line), located 550 m from the hotel, however, you need to cross the road. Sand cover, gentle entry into the water. Beach towels are provided free of charge. Water activities available.

Nutrition

Catering in the hotel provides: AB (American breakfasts), BB (breakfast buffet). The hotel's restaurant serves interesting Thai and international dishes. At the bar next to the swimming pool, guests can enjoy cocktails (alcoholic and non-alcoholic, fruit), as well as various fruit slices.

Guest Impressions

Guests of Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * hotel reviews leave mostly positive.

According to the guests, it takes about an hour to get from the airport to the hotel. The first impressions of Phuket, as tourists say, are unusual for a resident of the middle zone of stuffiness and humidity, which, however, do not interfere with comfort on the road.

Guests called the hotel very neat and clean. They constantly scratch and wash something.

Settlement does not always occur immediately upon arrival, sometimes you have to wait two hours. At check-in, you must leave a deposit of $ 50, which is refunded upon departure. The contingent of the hotel, judging by the reviews, consists mainly of Russian tourists.

Vacationers believe that the hotel ideally combines price and quality.



Guests say that the hotel is very similar to the runway: all the buildings in it are arranged in a row, and those who are accommodated at the very end will have to walk a lot. The higher the first digit, the further the number will be located.

Many guests call the rooms excellent: spacious, with a large bathroom equipped with a shower. They have everything you need: from soap, shampoo, shower gel and cotton buds to a kettle, replenished daily stocks of dried cream, coffee with sugar and bottles (0.6 l) of water per person. But there is no tea. Therefore, tourists warn: those who love this drink should take bags with them. Beach and bath towels are provided, they are changed daily, pillowcases are also worn fresh, the entire set of linen is changed 1 time for 11 nights. The furniture is in very good condition.

The rooms have balconies with a table and two chairs. Here you can sit and have a coffee. The panoramic window opens onto a spacious terrace, but the view from there, contrary to advertising promises, opens onto one of the buildings of the neighboring hotel. The rooms themselves, guests say, are clean, smells nothing, the air conditioners work stably. Cleaned daily.

The employees are very friendly, always smiling. From this, according to reviews, vacationers have a "joy in the soul." The hotel staff look very happy and constantly thank vacationers for everything. Cleaning, as a rule, is carried out in the afternoon, as vacationers believe, sometimes this is not timely, since guests often want to take a nap after dinner.

Wi-Fi in the rooms works well, you can write and call home on WhatsApp. The safe, as guests write, does not open upon check-in. To use it, you must definitely contact the reception.

The hotel, according to guests, is located very convenient: to the beach "Kata" (to the left of the hotel) to go up to 10 minutes. To "Kata Noi" - 25 minutes. In some places it is necessary to climb the mountain, but the beach here, according to the guests, is more comfortable, less crowded, there are fewer waves, but after a storm it can be dirty. Nearby are chain stores, cafes, street vendors selling fruit, crepes and ice cream. At the hotel, at lunch time, there is a daily merchant from whom you can buy food. Delicious dishes (about 10 items) are prepared directly in the presence of the buyer.

Guests respond negatively to catering at the hotel. Many refuse breakfast, considering them very meager. Breakfast usually consists of scrambled eggs, sausages, bread, toast, jam, vegetable slices, steamed rice or pasta. Instead of oil, a vegetable spread is served. Tourists prefer to buy food at markets and merchants, as well as eat at local cafes and bars. Many advise families who come with children to eat at the Spice House Russian cafe: here, the menu shows dishes that are recommended for children. Everything that is cooked in this restaurant is very tasty.

The pools delight those, vacationers recall that there are no restrictions on working hours and you can swim around the clock.

Guests are happy to recall being at the resort. As one of the peculiarities of the local weather, they note a lot of humidity and are advised to take with them more swimsuits, since in the local conditions nothing dries.

According to the guests, the excursions in Phuket are very interesting, and vacationers especially liked trips to 11 islands.

Staying at Kata Silver Sand By Eazy 3 * filled the life of vacationers, according to their own reviews, with a lot of positive emotions. As an advantage of the hotel, they note its excellent location, cleanliness and comfort in the rooms and on the territory, the presence of a pleasant, friendly staff. The downside of the guests consider meager and tasteless breakfasts, poor Internet performance in some rooms.
