Zelenograd hotels: overview, photos and prices

Many tourists come to Zelenograd. It is located 37 km from Moscow. This locality is considered one of the main centers of electronics and microelectronics. People come here more often for business purposes than for leisure.

This article discusses the hotels of the city where you can stay. In addition, the article talks about several institutions located in localities nearby.

Hotel "Record"

This hotel has been operating in Zelenograd since 1964. There is a car park, sauna, gym, park. If guests like to spend their leisure time actively, then they can play tennis, volleyball, and basketball.

The hotel has several categories of rooms: "luxury", "standard", "economy". There are cheap apartments. The rooms have a TV, fridge, bathroom.

On the territory of the complex there is a cafe where you can eat deliciously. If you need to hold a conference, an important meeting, a seminar, then you can use the conference room. It has all the necessary equipment. The train station is 15 minutes from the hotel.

This Zelenograd hotel (Moscow region) has paid parking, and internet access is available for free. There is also a bathhouse and a sauna.

Judging by the reviews, the administration treats the guests well. The rooms are clean and comfortable. The breakfast is varied and delicious. Guarded parking. The hotel is conveniently located within the city, nearby is the Leningradskoye Shosse. The cost of living is optimal. Often guests stay at this Zelenograd hotel for an hour or two.

Cost per day: approximately 2500 rubles.

Motel Sak

The motel features non-smoking, honeymoon and family rooms. The rooms have a flat-screen TV. Some units include a seating area and a bathroom with electric massage function. The cheap rooms do not have a private bathroom, so guests are offered a shared bathroom. In individual - there are slippers and toiletries.

You can eat in the shared kitchen. If desired, you can order breakfast in your room. You can rent barbecue equipment and cook outdoors. The airport is 20 km away.

This Zelenograd hotel offers a terrace, karaoke. On its territory there is a restaurant. There are special places for smoking. The reception is open 24 hours a day. There is a playground for children. There are shops on site. Parking is free, private.

In the reviews they write that the institution is located near the highway. Therefore, this option is very convenient for those who want to pause and relax while traveling.

Cost per day: about 800 rubles

Hotel "House of Scientists"

Each room at this Zelenograd hotel has a TV, kettle and private bathroom. The latter has a hairdryer and hygiene items.

The rooms are maintained in a simple classic style. The furniture is made of quality wood. Moreover, the rooms are spacious. The shared kitchen has a fridge and kettle.

A kilometer from the complex are supermarkets and shops. A restaurant is located nearby. To the airport - 10 km, to the railway station - 5 km. Khimki is 17 km away.

Internet is free of charge at this Zelenograd hotel. There are luggage storage facilities. The reception is open 24 hours a day. You can speed up check-in and check-out. You can come with pets.

The reviews write that the hotel is quite good. You can stay in it for several days.

Judging by the reviews, the furniture in the rooms is excellent, always clean. The local restaurants serve delicious food. If desired, you can order breakfast / lunch / dinner in the room.

Cost per day: about 4200 rubles.

Hotel "Andreevka"

This hotel is located near Zelenograd: just 5 km from it in the village of Andreevka. It is cozy and comfortable. The rooms are spacious, you can choose the option that you like: there are various categories and price offers.

The apartments have a TV, fridge, bathroom, internet access. The bathroom must have individual hygiene items. There is a shared kitchen, in an expensive apartment - own.

A grocery store is 2 minutes from the hotel. The airport is 18 km from the hotel.

Parking is free, but it is not guarded. There is a banquet room. For businessmen a conference room is provided. The maid cleans the rooms every day. You can ask the administrator to wake up at a specific time. There is an ironing room.

From entertainment there are billiards, futsal. You can rent audio and video equipment. A coffee machine is installed.

Many stay at this hotel for one night. They write that it is clean, comfortable, the administration is friendly.

Cost per day: about 2200 rubles.

Family Hotel Greenwood

This hotel is located in a picturesque area. It is only 6 km from Zelenograd. A bath and a patio are provided. Vacationers are offered a cozy and stylish room.

The interior is decorated in modern colors. There is a restaurant on site. It is possible to order meals in the room. The train station is just 4 minutes by car.

There is a fridge in the shared kitchen. Air conditioning is installed, there is heating. You can come with pets. Parking is available for free. You can play tennis and use the sauna. There is a shared lounge with a TV.

Cost per day: about 25 thousand rubles.

Conclusion

The article describes the hotels in Zelenograd. Given that most travelers stop no more than a day, all of the options described are ideal. They are cheap and comfortable. No one complains about cleanliness.
