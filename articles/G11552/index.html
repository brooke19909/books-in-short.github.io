Hotel Capsis Astoria 4 * (Greece, Crete, Heraklion): description of rooms, services, reviews

Capsis Astoria 4 * is one of the most attractive hotels in Crete. And he is such for many reasons. Before listing all of them in as much detail as possible, it is worth briefly telling about the complex itself.





general information

So, the hotel belongs to the Capsis hotel chain. This is the first thing you should know about her. The second interesting nuance is that it is one of the oldest in Crete. After all, the building was built back in 1966. But it was regularly updated and restored. The last such work was carried out in 2007. And cosmetic repairs are organized here regularly. So the hotel looks and is new, modern, like its rooms, as well as everything that is inside.

And it is located in the very center of Heraklion, which is the administrative center of Crete and a popular holiday destination. The airport can be reached in two minutes - it is only 5 kilometers away. And indeed, in the immediate vicinity of the complex there are many interesting places. For example, the Archaeological Museum is a minute's walk away, the Battle of Crete Museum must be walked another hundred meters, and the municipal art gallery with Venetian walls is located a little further. Also nearby are the Kules Sea Fortress and the Historical Museum of Crete.

But to go to a good beach for about 20 minutes. However, if you take bicycles and go there on them, you can turn a walk to the sea into a small cognitive journey, including exploring the surroundings. In general, Capsis Astoria 4 * is more suitable for people who went here on a business trip, or who like to walk and get acquainted with the local culture.





What can I use?

The hotel has everything that can make your stay more comfortable and carefree. There is free Wi-Fi, a room where guests can leave their belongings and luggage, a currency exchange office, and a 24-hour reception.

If people come with children, then for an additional fee they can order a nanny - she will sit with the child.

The hotel also has laundry and dry cleaning services, which also provide ironing services. Also in Capsis Astoria 4 *, like in any other hotel of the appropriate level, there is a small business center and four conference rooms, the total capacity of which is 300 people. They are often rented for banquets, trainings, meetings and other events.

What else is available at the hotel?

Guests staying at Capsis Astoria Hotel 4 * can either rent a car or book a transfer (both in the city and from / to the airport). Both possibilities are available here.

There are several vending machines (with snacks and drinks) and safes for guests in the hotel lobby. In addition to the previously listed services, drinks and food are also delivered to your room. If necessary, guests may be asked to check in their non-smoking room. By the way, there are still apartments that are fully adapted for guests with disabilities to stay in them. Keep this in mind.

Capsis Astoria Hotel 4 * is not a resort type hotel, so there is practically no entertainment here. With the exception of the pool overlooking the Aegean Sea, of course, and a beauty salon. It is available here.





Nutrition

Capsis Astoria 4 * (Crete, Heraklion) has an excellent restaurant called Erofilli, which serves traditional Cretan cuisine. There is also a cafe where you can taste sweets, snacks, drinks and coffee. And the pool bar serves original cocktails (both refreshing and alcoholic).

Most of the guests staying here book breakfasts only, preferring to try delights for lunch and dinner in various local restaurants and bars, which are here at every step. But the morning meal here is quite diverse. There are fried sausages, fried eggs, omelettes, cereals and granola, yogurts, bread, cheese and meat slices, vegetables with fruits, tea, coffee - in general, there is a choice.

Helpful information

In Capsis Astoria 4 * (Greece, Crete, Heraklion), the check-in according to the rules starts at 14:00. However, if guests arrived earlier, they may be settled earlier. If there is a free and prepared room, of course. If guests know the time of arrival, then it is better to inform the administration in advance, then the apartments will be prepared directly for their arrival. And departure is traditionally until 12:00.

The hotel can accommodate with children, although few go here with a child (after all, the hotel is not a resort). If the baby is not three years old, then for him they will install a small crib in the room. And for his accommodation will not need to pay extra. The same applies to more adult children (up to 7 years old), only for them they will install an extra bed, not a small one. But if guests come with a child aged 7 to 12 years, then you will need to pay extra. The price is fixed - 18 euros per night.

If you need any of the services, it is worth informing the administration in advance so that they can prepare everything for arrival. By the way, you can come to the hotel with pets. But this should also be reported, indicating the breed of the animal.

It is also worth knowing that the hotel's rooftop pool does not always work, but only in the holiday season - from mid-May to early October.





What do tourists say?

Many people relaxing in this hotel are happy to share their impressions and emotions. They assure: this is the best and most correct hotel complex in Heraklion. Favorable location, very modest prices, good food - what else does a traveler need?

Of course, the hotel is not suitable for families with small children or people who are on beach holidays. There is no entertainment, animation, and the sea is far away. But people know about this nuance, so you can always find another option.

The staff here is good, good-natured. The only negative is that they do not speak Russian. But then the cleaning is daily and high-quality, regular change of linen and towels. And administrators are always happy to help with advice. In general, the attitude towards the guests here is excellent.

In general, if we conclude, then we can say: this is a great option for people who are accustomed to comfortable conditions, stylish rooms and silence. This is exactly what you can get from the Capsis Astoria. Lovers of walks and exploring local attractions can not find the best option at this price in Heraklion.
