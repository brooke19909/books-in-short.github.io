The small North African state of Morocco as a tourist region compares favorably with its neighbors in Magrib and Egypt. The Pearl of North Africa

Over the past decade, with the help of investors from Western Europe, the number of rooms in the country has increased significantly. Several hundred hotels in all regions of the country offer their services to customers with any budget and requests. The quality of service is constantly improving. Morocco 5-star hotels are located in all major tourist centers: in the capital, Rabat, as well as in Marrakesh, Casablanca, Agadir, El Jadida, Essaouira, Fes, Saidyi, Varzazate, Tangier.
