How to create a costume of Ivan Tsarevich with your own hands

Thinking of an original carnival costume? Choose something from Russian fairy tales. For example, the image of Ivan Tsarevich. It will be difficult to make a costume with your own hands, but if you ask for help from friends or relatives, then by joint efforts you can achieve a very good result.

Costume for baby



Ivan Tsarevich is a Russian folk favorite. He is a little lazy, likes to laugh, but does not forget to work. Therefore, the children's costume of Ivan Tsarevich is in incredible demand at all matinees. Carers love when parents dress their children not with monsters, but with characters from folk tales. How to make a costume of Ivan Tsarevich with your own hands?


A pattern of its base is attached above. It will be a bright caftan. You can sew it on the pattern of a children's dress. The fabric should be chosen dense with a clear gold or silver pattern. The back of the caftan should be decorated with a cloak. This item of clothing can be either separated from the top, or be an integral part. The front of the outfit is decorated with decorative overlays and large transparent stones. The caftan is belted with a wide belt or scarf.

We made the costume of Ivan Tsarevich with our own hands, it remains to figure out what to sew a hat from. It can be made from the same material that went on sewing a caftan. As a decorative element, a hem will act here. It can be made both fabric and fur.

Ivan Tsarevich with a bow



This costume is similar to the previous one. Only caftan here is made of red fabric. Therefore, outerwear looks more decorative. We will do the costume of Ivan Tsarevich with our own hands according to the following principle. We take a pattern of a children's raincoat and sew a camisole on it. We use gold braid as decoration. She needs to frame the entire product around the perimeter.

In the center, in place of the buttons, we also sew the gold lining. They also need to be used to decorate the hem and sleeves. The collar can be trimmed with fur. The caftan is belted with a wide scarf. The headdress of Ivan Tsarevich is a hat. We make it from red fabric, wrap it with fur, and in the center we sew gold embroidery. Do not forget about the boots. They should be red. As a decor, you can sew gold braid on them.

Costume for a student



Such an outfit can be done even by a beginning seamstress. It is necessary to redraw with your own hands the pattern of the costume of Ivan Tsarevich given above. The length and width of the camisole gown should be adjusted to the size of the child. You can sew an outfit from one fabric, or combine two or three.

The camisole, which consists of white and blue matter, looks beautiful. As a decor, a gold or silver braid should be sewn to the costume. Pants need to choose or match the camisole. They should be of some neutral color: white, black or brown.

Feet should be worn in soft cloth boots, and crown your head with a hat that you can do with your own hands. It is sewn on this principle. A circle is cut out and is divided into two parts. They are stitched along the long side. The hat has already turned out, but in order to make it look prettier, it is worth processing the edge with a zigzag, and in order to keep it well on your head, you should insert an elastic band at the edge of the product. The costume of Ivan Tsarevich is ready. You can come up with a lot of variations based on it.

Ivan Tsarevich in a folk costume



But not only schoolchildren need fancy dress. Adults also often go to parties where a dress code requires a costume. What to dress a young guy? The adult costume of Ivan Tsarevich looks no worse than a child. After all, folk heroes are loved not only by small, but also by large girls. Which of the women does not dream of meeting the prince at a party?

How to make a costume of a national hero? Very simple. Its base is a long white shirt. You can find one in the chests of your grandmother in the village, or you can make it yourself. The costume of Ivan Tsarevich is sewn on a pattern of elementary women's dress.

To make a simple white robe look like a folk shirt, it is worth decorating. Sew a burgundy ribbon to the hem. Decorate the sleeves with black braid. Collect the gate to the eyeballs. And, of course, gird the shirt with a wide belt or ribbon. A great addition to the suit will be harem pants. If these are not available, you need to wear black pants. Only in no case jeans, they will ruin the whole surroundings. You need to put on black boots, but bast shoes will be just perfect.
