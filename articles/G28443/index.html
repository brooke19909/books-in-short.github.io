Three-star hotel "Azimut" (Moscow / "Tulskaya"): description, infrastructure and reviews of visitors

The three-star Azimut Hotel (Moscow) looks very much like the old Moscow factories, as it is located in the building where the largest textile enterprise used to be - Danilovskaya Manufactory (1867). The entire surrounding area has been used for warehouse trade for a long time. And only in 2008 all the buildings were closed for reconstruction. The former abandoned factory was supposed to be turned into a business quarter.





As a result, the idea became a reality. Today, on the site of the Danilov manufactory, there is a modern business complex, where the museum atmosphere of the 19th century, complemented by office color, is floating. The Azimut Hotel (Moscow, Tula) is also part of the business center. The quarter's infrastructure also includes bank offices, a parking lot, retail premises, restaurants and cafeterias. The hotel, as, in general, the whole territory, is admirable.

Location How to get there





Hotel "Azimut" (Moscow) is conveniently located in the Donskoy district, on the Warsaw highway. Nearest stations: Serpukhov-Timiryazevskaya and Tula. For a guide to tourists: nearby are the Don and Danilov Monasteries, the Shukhov Tower, the Neskuchny Garden, the Theater Theater.

Within walking distance are the Olympic Plaza and the Olympic Sports Complex. A huge plus is the proximity to historical sites such as the Novodevichy Cemetery and the Novodevichy Convent. Nearby is Domodedovo Airport, only half an hour away by car.

By the way, in the capital there is another business hotel "Azimut" - four stars. It is more focused on business travelers. Where is the Azimut Hotel (Moscow) located? Olimpiysky Prospekt, 18/1, next to the Tsvetnoy Boulevard and Prospekt Mira stations. The hotel building consists of 12 floors. It offers accommodation on the club floor with a wider range of services.

It offers a comfortable environment, respectful and attentive service, a network of modern restaurants, a variety of entertainment services, plenty of opportunities for business meetings (16 conference rooms) and gourmet cuisine. There were even shots of the famous film "Police Academy"! The level of the complex and pricing policy are quite high.

Three-star hotel "Azimut" (Moscow, "Tula"): number of rooms





The 5-story building has 144 comfortable, individually decorated rooms with wooden furniture and air conditioning. For your relaxation, the rooms have a flat-screen TV with cable channels, free high-speed Internet and a radio. All rooms are equipped with hygiene facilities. Created conditions for people with disabilities.

Nutrition

For a fee, a morning meal is offered in the hotel restaurant. Serving is carried out according to the European buffet system. Upon request, guests can request for packed lunches (at an additional cost). The building has two restaurants with European and national cuisines. On the menu you will find a variety of branded pastries, salads, sandwiches, meat and fish delicacies, pizza, pasta, freshly squeezed juices and a rich collection of spirits.

Internal infrastructure

Azimut Hotel (photo is provided) registers newly arrived tourists at lunchtime (at 14.00). The front desk is open around the clock. Multilingual staff will always help with booking and purchasing tickets. Transfer services are provided (paid).





Young couples can use the wedding service. In turn, the administration of the complex will give the newlyweds a symbolic gift. For business clients, the necessary conditions for negotiations and presentations are created. There is a conference room equipped with video and audio equipment.

Placement of children

According to the rules of the hotel, children under the age of two stay in existing beds for free. Children under the age of six are provided with extra beds and free accommodation. For older preschool children will have to pay 800 rubles per day.

Additional Information

Hotel "Azimut" (Moscow) does not allow visitors with pets. If you made a reservation and were unable to check in, then you must cancel or change the arrival date in two days. In this case, penalties are not levied. If the order was not timely withdrawn, the hotel will deduct the full price of the stay.

Reviews and opinions of visitors

The three-star Azimut Hotel regularly receives rave reviews from customers. Many consider this place one of the best in Moscow in terms of price and quality. Pleasantly pleased with the rooms with LED lights and a large soft bed. The modern design of all rooms and high technology allow you to get an unforgettable pleasure and a high level of comfort.





The friendly and professional staff of the hotel will always offer help, tell you how to get to the object of interest and give a map of the city. Summing up, I want to say that the hotel is great for people with low incomes.
