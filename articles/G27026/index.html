What is better to choose for relaxation: Nha Trang or Phan Thiet?

It would seem, what's the difference - Nha Trang or Phan Thiet? These Vietnamese resorts are located not so far from each other and are equally loved by Russian tourists (that is, there will be no salvation from compatriots either there or there). But still, reading the reviews, we can conclude that between these cities are not two hundred twenty-five kilometers, but about a thousand. And even in terms of climate, these resorts are not alike. Nha Trang is often attributed to Central Vietnam. The phantasy is guaranteed to belong to the southern part of the country. We can say that settlements lie in different climatic zones. Therefore, the notorious "rainy season", which is so scared by holidaymakers, comes to these cities at different times. In our article, we will try to compare two popular resorts in Vietnam - and not only in terms of weather conditions. We will analyze the tourist infrastructure, beaches, hotel offers.



Best season in Central Vietnam

What month to go to the resorts of Nha Trang or Phan Thiet - July, September or even in winter? And is there a difference between these cities in weather conditions? It turns out that a distance of two hundred and thirty kilometers is very important and plays a big role - especially in some months. In principle, Central Vietnam, to which Nha Trang belongs, is comfortable for a beach holiday all year round, except for November and December. But it is worth moving a hundred or two kilometers to the south, as there is no trace of the monsoon - a clear sky, calm sea ... The resort of Phan Thiet lies in the north of the subequatorial zone. Therefore, its climate can be compared with weather conditions on the famous island of Phu Quoc. The rainy season here falls in the summer months. More precisely, showers begin in May and continue until September, slowly subsiding in October. If you are wondering what to choose - Nha Trang or Phan Thiet - in November, stop your eyes on the latter - the rest here will be more comfortable. But in the summer months, on the contrary, you should prefer the northern direction.

Entertainment: Nha Trang or Phan Thiet?

Water sports enthusiasts see the huge difference between the two resorts. In Mui Ne, a special wind rose. This makes the resort a Mecca for kiters, windsurfers and those wishing to ride the crest of the wave, standing on the board. But this circumstance makes snorkeling and diving impossible. In Nha Trang, despite the fact that you have to be content with city beaches, the sea is more transparent, with better visibility. And there are a lot of other entertainments in the big city. One of the main attractions of Nha Trang reviews is called Hon Che Island with the incomparable water park "Vinpearl Land". But Phan Thiet cannot boast of such infrastructure. If you are not an athlete, then the trip to the banana will be the only entertainment on the water. And in the evening you are just waiting for a voyage to the restaurant. Discos and night clubs are only available at hotels.

Summary
