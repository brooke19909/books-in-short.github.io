Popular hotels in Aksay, Rostov Region

Aksai is a quiet industrial city located in the Rostov region. The main guests of the city are not tourists, but businessmen and entrepreneurs. Most tourists come here from stalkers from Rostov. At the same time, the most popular place among them is Mhana Balka, where they dug a refuge in the 50s, fearing a nuclear war. For them, this is precisely what Aksai is interested in (Rostov Region).





Hotels (prices in them depend on various factors, for example, on the level of service, the number of services offered, etc.) can offer visitors of the city high-quality service and comfortable rooms. The polite staff will meet you with a smile, in addition, make efforts to make your stay here as comfortable as possible. But on the spot it is quite difficult to choose a hotel; you should find a room in advance when planning a trip to Aksay (Rostov Region). Hotels, prices in them we will consider in this article.

Hotel "Old Castle"

Hotel "Old Castle" (Aksay, Rostov Region) is always ready to receive its old and new guests. Here you will always be served with attention and care. Experienced staff will be able to answer all questions. All rooms are equipped with modern furniture and appliances. Thanks to all of the above, you can feel the comfort of home here.

Cost: from 1020 rubles per day.

Sanatorium "Chateau Spas"

It is a world-class recreation and health center. For guests are offered comfortable rooms of several categories, created in the style of a castle in France. Each has everything you need: air conditioning, bathroom, comfortable furniture, refrigerator.





Restaurant "Chateau" pleases visitors with a dance floor with the possibility of parties or concert programs and light music. The choice of guests offers a varied menu, which was developed taking into account the requirements of medical and dietary nutrition. In the sanatorium you have the opportunity to undergo various treatment programs, as well as visit a hamam or SPA complex.

Cost: from 6374 rubles per day.

Motel Eurasia-Don

We continue to consider hotels. Aksay of the Rostov region, as mentioned above, mainly attracts entrepreneurs. For those who do not want to overpay for the night, there is an excellent Eurasia-Don motel. The polite staff of the institution is always happy to new guests. Guests can not worry about food - a cozy cafe is located directly on the territory of the motel.





If you come by your own car, use the parking lot. In addition, various celebrations are possible here.

Cost: from 2300 rubles per day.

Hotel Marseille

Here you are offered a comfortable accommodation option. The facility is located 9 kilometers from the airport. It is ideal for travel. Guests are offered 10 rooms of various classes. All rooms of this hotel in Aksay, Rostov Region are equipped with a bathroom, air conditioning. Internet is also available everywhere. The staff will be happy to see you at any time of the day. In addition, guests who come here by their own transport can use the parking lot located on site.





Cost: from 2500 rubles per day.

Hotel "Victory"

Hotel "Victory" is located in a place where guests can get there as quickly as possible. The rooms of this Aksay hotel in the Rostov Region are made up of rooms of various categories, while the amenities here are in the room and on the floor. Ironing boards, irons and hairdryers are provided free of charge. There is no cafe on the territory, while there is a kitchen for cooking, and already it has all the necessary utensils and household appliances. There is also a small dining area with chairs and tables.

Near this hotel in Aksay, Rostov Region are located: The old customs Cossack outpost, as well as the Village Starocherkasskaya, which often hosts festivals of the Cossacks.

Hotel complex "Aksu"

This is one of the best hotel complexes located in the city. From the Turkic name is translated as "living, white water." Any guest will be greeted by the friendly staff of this hotel in Aksay, Rostov Region, and will enjoy a light, relaxed atmosphere. For guests of the complex prepared rooms of several categories. All of them are equipped for absolutely comfortable accommodation. Guests of the hotel complex can eat in the local restaurant. Here you can enjoy live music and home cooking.





The standard room has 2 single or double bed, wardrobe, bedside tables, air conditioning, TV. The bathroom has a sink, shower, toilet.

Cost: from 2000 rubles per day.
