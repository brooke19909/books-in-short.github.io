User's manual





For maintenance, you must turn off the equipment, and for cleaning - wipe with a soft, dry cloth. It is not recommended to use abrasive and chlorine-containing substances. If there is a need to clean the indoor unit, turn off the power supply. If you notice that a large amount of dirt has accumulated on the indoor unit, you can use a cloth that has been moistened with cold water to clean it. After this, the surface must be wiped with a dry cloth. Do not use fabric that has been treated with chemicals. It is also necessary to exclude the use of a dusty rag to clean the unit.

Neoclima must not be cleaned using hot water whose temperature exceeds 40 ¬∞ C. This also applies to the front panel, since when exposed to this temperature, the surface may become deformed or lose color.

Air filter cleaning





Cleaning of air conditioners can be carried out by the user independently, these operations should include care for the air filter and the refreshment filter. If these elements are clogged, then the cooling efficiency of the unit may noticeably decrease. Cleaning must be done once every two weeks. To do this, the panel of the indoor unit rises up to the corner until you hear a click. For some models, suspended traverses are used, which allow the support of elements. The air filter handle must be held up by slowly lifting it so that it becomes possible to remove the filter holders. The next step will be pulling up the element. The air filter can be removed from the indoor unit, and then cleaned. This can be done using rags dipped in water or a vacuum cleaner. Removable parts should be dried in a cool place after cleaning.

Cleaning the refresh filter





Cleaning air conditioners improves their efficiency. If there is a need to clean the refreshment filter, then it should be removed from the support frame. Additionally, this component of the design includes a plasma filter, a silver ion filter, a biofilter, as well as an air purifying filter. It is necessary to clean this part of the air conditioner once a month, and replace it once every five months. Cleaning can be carried out using a vacuum cleaner, after which the filter is left in a cool together until completely dried. Do not touch the plasma filter for 10 minutes after the front panel is open, otherwise you may encounter a problem of electric shock. The light filter must be installed after cleaning in a place that is located on the rear surface.
