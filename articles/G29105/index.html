Hotel "Zarya" (Moscow): description, service, prices, reviews

Are you going to the capital for work and looking for a decent, but inexpensive hotel? Or maybe you want to relax and change the atmosphere and plan to see the city? An excellent option for accommodation will be the Zarya Hotel (Moscow). Why is it so remarkable, you can learn from this article.

general description





Cheap hotels do not always have poor living conditions. So, "Dawn" boasts an excellent ratio of "price - quality". This is a four-story beautiful hotel with a good location in one of the most peaceful and clean areas of Moscow, a restaurant with delicious dishes of Russian and European cuisine, a lobby bar, spacious comfortable rooms with the necessary equipment and furniture, free high-speed Internet and wide facilities for holding various conferences, seminars and other business events.

For those who do not forget about their health even on vacation or on business trips, the hotel has a spa center with a gym and various types of saunas. Travelers may be interested in the tour desk and ticket service for various events at the hotel reception. Families with children will be pleased that babies are welcome from any age and cots are provided free of charge. In a word, accommodation at this hotel will appeal to tourists with various trip goals.

Location

The first plus of this hotel is its location. It is located in one of the quietest and most ecologically clean areas of Moscow - a few minutes walk from the Vladykino and Petrovsko-Razumovskaya metro stations. Thanks to this, you can quickly get to anywhere in the city. From the Sheremetyevo International Airport to the hotel - about fifty minutes by car, from Savelovsky railway station - about twenty.

Hotel "Maxima Dawn" will please its location and tourists who love hiking. Within walking distance are the Moscow Botanical Garden of the Russian Academy of Sciences, the Ostankino TV Tower, the All-Russian Exhibition Center (VVC), the Ostankino estate and park and the Sheremetyevo Palace.

Number of rooms





Hotel "Dawn" (Moscow) offers its guests one hundred and ninety-two rooms of different levels of comfort. They belong to the following categories: one-room single and double and two-room triple standard, two-room double studios, business and luxury.

Each room is equipped with an equipped bathroom with a bath or shower, spacious rooms are decorated in European style and have comfortable furniture and modern appliances. All rooms have air conditioning.

Standard

The rooms of this category can accommodate from one to three people, the area is from fourteen to thirty-eight square meters. The rooms have a bed, desk, satellite TV, minibar, wardrobe, telephone, upholstered furniture, tea accessories.

In a sufficiently large bathroom, guests will find a hairdryer, cosmetic and hygiene items, and towels. The bedrooms have fresh linens. Guest reviews indicate that it is changed every day. Triple rooms have a balcony.

Studio





These are spacious superior rooms with a living room and a bedroom. There are also the above amenities. The difference from the standard category is in the larger size and decoration of the rooms, as well as in the stunning royal bed, which is also available in all more expensive rooms.

Business

For those traveling on a business trip, the Maxima Zarya Hotel offers particularly comfortable conditions. These rooms are up to 39 square meters. m and with all the necessary amenities. In the spacious bathroom, guests will find not only hygiene and beauty products, towels and a hairdryer, but also a bathrobe and slippers to make you feel at home here. And in order to be in good shape during the trip, there is a hot tub.

Suite

The most chic hotel room measuring 55 square meters. m, decorated in a marine style, deserves special attention. It will satisfy the needs of even the most demanding tourists, despite the fact that the hotel belongs to the 3 star category: there are portholes, a barometer, a market, and in the very middle of the room a hammock is comfortably located - a unique embodiment of a design idea. In the living room you will find a rocking chair and expensive comfortable leather furniture so that you can truly relax and unwind.





In addition, the room has a music center so you can enjoy the high-quality sound of your favorite music, and a large TV for watching programs and movies. This room is the most popular in the hotel, so it is advisable to book it in advance.

Nutrition

Hotel "Dawn" (Moscow) offers its guests a wide selection of delicious dishes of Russian and European cuisine in the restaurant "Europe". The restaurant's interior is made in a classic restrained style with modern elements. Thanks to the gourmet cuisine and beautiful interior, this place is often used for banquets and weddings.

The price of the hotel passes includes breakfast in this buffet restaurant. If you want to have lunch or dinner here, this can be done at a small additional cost. Also in the restaurant you can order a business lunch, which is served from 12 to 16 hours.





You can enjoy light snacks or soft drinks, as well as meet friends or business partners over a cup of coffee in the lobby bar, which is located on the ground floor of the hotel. The prices here are quite acceptable for the average tourist, and the quality of the food is excellent.

The hotel street on which the hotel is located has many cafes and shops nearby, where you can also tasty and inexpensive refreshments. In addition, drinks or meals can be delivered directly to your room at an additional cost. In short, you are definitely not able to stay hungry here.

  • Late check in and check out.

  • Tour desk so you can fully immerse yourself in the history of the capital.
  • Sale of plane or train tickets.
  • Ironing, laundry, dry cleaning and shoe shine services are especially important for business travelers.
  • Daily room service is scheduled.
  • Car and bicycle rental.
  • A souvenir shop so you can bring along a memorable little thing about this wonderful vacation.
  • Order movie or theater tickets to add a touch of culture to your stay.
  • Transfer to the station, airport or any other point of the capital.
  • Order a taxi if you need to get to a certain place quickly and comfortably.
  • Newspapers delivered directly to your room.

For children

You can come to a place like the Zarya Hotel (Moscow) with children - they are accepted here from any age. Children under four years stay free of charge; a cot is provided. When booking, it is advisable to inform the hotel staff that you plan to come with a child so that all the amenities are prepared for you.

For business travel

The hotel offers ample opportunities for conferences and seminars. Here are two conference rooms under the names "Picasso" and "Renoir", as well as two conference rooms - "Gauguin" and "Matisse". The halls are equipped with the latest technology to make your event a bang.





The hotel provides fax services, photocopying, as well as rental computers and office equipment. After successful negotiations or conferences, you can hold a celebration in the banquet hall.

The hotel will be ready to help you organize coffee breaks or lunches. If you need a financial report to receive travel, the hotel is also ready to provide the necessary document.

Terms of accommodation

Hotel "Zarya", the price of accommodation in which is one of the lowest in Moscow, follows the pan-European rules of time of entry and exit: check-in is from 14:00, check-out is to 12:00. Pets are not allowed. The cost of living in an extra bed for children over four years of age and adults is 900 rubles. The maximum number of extra beds in a room is one.





Please note that cancellation of the reservation at least two days prior to arrival is free of charge. If cancellation occurs later or the tourist does not enter the hotel, the full price of the stay will be charged. In each case, the conditions of the reservation may change, be sure to check this information with the hotel.
