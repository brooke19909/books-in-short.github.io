What determines the size of the bank commission?

The presence or absence of a commission, as well as its size directly depends on the destination of your payment. For example, the commission for the payment of utilities, fines, penalties, legal costs, charitable donations and taxes is absent completely or is minimal. Moreover, the size of the commission does not in any way depend on the transfer method chosen by the client. This is due to the fact that VTB Bank has a practically uniform tariff rate.

Is it possible to do without a commission?

You can completely dispense with the commission, but only on condition that your opponent complies with the established VTB Bank standard. For example, he is the owner of a charity fund with which a financial organization cooperates, taxes must be paid, etc. Or he works in a government department, for example, in the tax service. In most cases, the execution of an operation on VTB cards with the subsequent transfer of funds to Sberbank plastic is subject to a fee.

Thus, we can conclude that, by transferring from one card to another, with the exception of individual cases, commissions cannot be avoided. But its size will directly depend on the purpose of the payment and its amount.
