The best Nice hotels on the coast: photos and reviews of tourists

Nice is one of the most beautiful cities on the Cote d'Azur in France, which is rich in historical monuments, picturesque, incredibly beautiful landscape and well-developed tourist infrastructure. Since the 19th century, this Mediterranean resort has become a favorite vacation spot for the European aristocracy, and in particular for the Russian nobility.

Today, Nice continues to be considered an elite resort, but every day it becomes more accessible for tourists of average income from different countries. Two-, three- and even one-star hotels in Nice offer city guests quite normal conditions for living.

This is truly a paradise. Nice is called the unofficial capital of the Cote d'Azur. It is located in Angel Bay. There is never a real winter here: it is always warm and sunny, there is also no excess of humidity. Of course, in the winter months the resort is not suitable for a beach holiday, but just then you have the opportunity to enjoy the beautiful sea views, the air becomes incredibly clear, and the sky takes on a special bright shade that merges with the slightly darkened sea. In addition, it was during this period that Nice hotels by the sea became more affordable.

Despite the fact that Nice is a glamorous resort where people come mostly to hang out, here you can also meet many family tourists who want to spend their holidays with children. Which Nice hotels are suitable for kids?

The most famous hotel of the Cote d'Azur and its distinguished guests

In the history of Nice, the Negresco Hotel plays an important role. This palace is one of the oldest in the world. Its building borders the Promenade des Anglais. The snow-white walls and the turquoise-pink dome of the tower attract the eyes of all visitors to the city. Negresco is considered a treasure of Belle √âpoque.

Well-known artists, politicians and simply very wealthy citizens who value perfect service and a high degree of comfort stayed here. This architectural masterpiece requires that it be treated with great attention. The hotel was built in 1913, and its first owner was Henri Negresco - a cool businessman, businessman (as they say today), originally from Romania. Eduard-Jean Nieermann, famous in France, and not only, was invited as an architect-designer. The interior was also handled by the most upscale professionals. The owner of the soul himself did not cherish in this hotel and did everything possible and impossible for him to take a unique look.

At the opening of the Negresco Hotel, seven royal blood persons were present. This was the right marketing move. Learning about who will be present at the opening, the building was literally surrounded by journalists and paparazzi. The pictures taken by them quickly hit the press, and the hotel gained fame and megapopularity. However, with the outbreak of World War I, difficult times began for Negresco. He was turned into a military hospital. Here, mainly Russian aristocratic officers were treated. The Romanian owner went broke very quickly and sold his beloved brainchild. They say that he could not cope with the loss and died a few years later.

Rebirth

At one point, Jeanne Augier became the owner of the famous hotel in Nice. This happened in 1957. At first, the hotel liked her as a place to stay for her disabled mother. The elevators in this building were so wide that they contained her wheelchair. Finding herself here, Jeanne immediately fell in love with her property and decided to make it into a museum, which she filled with many beautiful and inspired works of art.

Thanks to the new owner, the hotel today stores more than 6 thousand exclusive masterpieces of art. She divided the hotel into several zones corresponding to a particular historical era. For example, an entire floor of Napoleon appeared in Negresco. At one time, the hotel loved to visit Salvador Dali. It was here that he walked his cheetah, so Jeanne decided that one of the zones should have been dedicated to the great artist. Picasso, Hemingway, Michael Jackson, Grace Kelly and her crowned husband also liked to come here. The famous Sophia Loren still loves to stay at Negresco. Other famous hotel guests to this day are Charles Aznavour, Elizabeth Taylor, Catherine Deneuve and others.

Many of them leave their reviews about the Nice Negresco hotel in the most commendable terms. But to the representatives of the young generation, it seems somehow non-modern, even after in 2009 it was reconstructed and adapted to modern realities. After that, on its facade one more star was added to four stars.

Book or not book accommodation?

Some tourists like to relax "savages", relying on chance. However, booking hotels in Nice is the right move, because in the season it is very difficult to find free housing at reasonable prices. During the holidays, as well as during the festivals, the cost of living increases sharply, but for those tourists who have taken care of the place of stay in advance, this does not apply. By the way, if you do not want to use the services of travel agencies, you can book the hotel yourself through any online resources or by contacting the hotel administration by e-mail or phone.

This pearl of the Mediterranean Sea offers a huge selection of accommodation options, from affordable hostels and mini-hotels to chic five-star hotels. So if you visit Nice - your dream, then do not put it off into the far corner, and welcome to one of the most beautiful resorts in the French Mediterranean.
