How to get to the Big Reach?

In order to get to the place, you can use the three available options. The first option is to take the bus route from the cities of Abakan or Krasnoyarsk to the village of Shira. Then take a taxi there and get to your destination.

The second option is no less affordable than the first. Residents of Abakan, Krasnoyarsk, Moscow and Tomsk can take the train and get to the Shira station, from where they can then start taking a taxi in the direction of the Bolshoi Ples recreation center.

The third option will be possible for owners of personal vehicles. In this case, arm yourself with the navigator and hit the road. Your task is to move along the Abakan highway M-54 from Krasnoyarsk to Novoselovo. Accommodation and prices

On the territory of the recreation center "Bolshoi Ples" there are 32 separate houses and a hotel complex with seven rooms. The houses are divided among themselves into: 4-, 6-, 7- and 8-seater. A quadruple lodge will be an ideal accommodation for a small family with children. The rest of the houses will be suitable for larger companies. Of the furniture in the houses there are double and bunk beds, shelves, refrigerators and mirrors. In addition, there is a well-equipped bathroom with toilet and shower, as well as a beautiful terrace overlooking the lake. You can spend quiet evenings just on it, for this guests are provided with natural wood furniture.





The buildings at the recreation center "White Reach" inside are divided into rooms, which is comfortable for several families in the same house.

In the hotel complex you can choose a double, quadruple and six-bed room. In the standard rooms you can use the amenities on site. The deluxe rooms have a private bathroom with a toilet and a hot shower.
