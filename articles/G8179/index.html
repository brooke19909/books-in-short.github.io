Where is Tolmachevo - the largest airport in Siberia

Not everyone knows where Tolmachevo is and what it is. However, travelers are probably aware that this is an international airport in Novosibirsk. It is the largest in Siberia. It is a key hub on air lines from North America to India, Asia, and also from Southeast Asia to European countries. All of Russia is accessible from this airport. Tolmachevo is also the base of military aircraft of the Russian Air Force.

History

This international airport is the only one in Novosibirsk. The exact position where Tolmachevo is located is 21 km from the capital of Siberia, in the satellite city of Ob. Not only residents of Novosibirsk use it, but also people from nearby cities: Barnaul, Tomsk, Kemerovo, Novokuznetsk. The fact is that a large number of flights to other countries fly from here. That is why many tourists, preferring direct non-stop flights, go to Novosibirsk.



The airport was founded in 1957. On July 12, the first passenger flight to Moscow flew up from here to the sky. Until that moment, the place where Tolmachevo was located was considered exclusively a military airfield.

After 5 years, a large terminal building was opened, which is capable of sending up to 1000 people every hour. Passenger traffic has increased over the years. It's time to build not only new air strips, but also a new airport terminal.

Until 1992, only regular domestic flights across Russia flew from Novosibirsk. But in the 92nd year, the airport received international status. The first to launch destinations in Indonesia, Germany, the UAE and China.
