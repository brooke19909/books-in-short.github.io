The most expensive hotels in the world.

There is such a category of people in our society who can buy all the best for themselves. They have huge fortunes and rest in places that are not affordable for mere mortals. The most expensive resorts open their doors to them. Their owners do everything for the convenience and comfort of guests. Year after year, experts determine which expensive hotels in the world attract rich people under their roof. For this, the cost of living, the availability of additional services, the richness of the interior, the level of service and many other factors are taken into account. The most expensive hotels in the world are at the top of this rating.

The leader is undoubtedly the President Wilson Hotel, which is located in Geneva and boasts an elegant interior, a host of modern amenities and magnificent views of Mont Blanc and Lake Geneva. It is located in such a part of the city, from where it is close to parks and shops. Of course, the price per day is appropriate here. Those who like to stay in the royal penthouse are ready to shell out 54 thousand dollars a night. This hotel has a professional security service and a large number of various services: bars, restaurants, a sauna, a gym, a hairdresser, a pool, as well as parking and car rental.

The most expensive hotels in the world are proudly represented by Hugh Hefner Sky Villa, Palms Casino, Resort in Las Vegas. Its owner is Hugh Hefner, known as the founder of Playboy. This hotel is in second place in the rating thanks to apartments, the cost of living in which is 40 thousand dollars. Everything here is in the style of Playboy. This price includes the services of a professional butler, sauna, poker table and gym.

The next position is the Japanese Ritz-Carlton Hotel, which is located in Tokyo. Prices are already much lower, although not everyone can afford 17.2 thousand dollars. The interior is designed in a classic modern style. This hotel has a chic spa and stunning views of Mount Fuji and the city itself add exotic. In this hotel you can taste not only Japanese dishes, but also European ones.

The most expensive hotels in the world can be proud of the Martinez hotel palace. It is located in Cannes very close to the boutiques street and, most importantly, the sea. Prices for accommodation at this hotel reach 43 thousand dollars. The most popular apartments are Presidential Suite. Of course, the atmosphere in it is gorgeous and everything is done at the highest level. This is a favorite place for many stars. A gastronomic restaurant will delight guests with Mediterranean cuisine and the highest level of service.

In a country like the UAE, expensive hotels can be said at every step. For example, Emirates Palace is considered the most-most in the Middle East. Here, marble, gold, Swarovski crystals and simply traditional oriental luxury are everywhere. And Burj Al Arab, located in Dubai, has an original sail shape. Its main suite is also decorated with gold and marble. This hotel even offers helicopter services, of course, for a fee.

France also boasts a chic Royal Suite hotel, Four Seasons George V. Here are magnificent apartments that are exclusively furnished with antique furniture and will cost you 16 thousand cu per day.

Hotel Mandarin Oriental is also attractive for wealthy people. Its Presidential Suite impresses with its interior. Here is an office where you can see oriental artifacts and carpets exclusively handmade. You will pay about 15 thousand dollars a day.

Thus, the most expensive hotels in the world offer their guests the highest level of service, magnificent interiors and very decent prices for accommodation. After all, an ordinary traveler is looking for a hotel in which he expects to see cleanliness, convenience and comfort. But people who allow themselves not to save, also want to see originality, luxury and wealth.
