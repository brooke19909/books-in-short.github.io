Picturesque landscapes

Amazingly beautiful nature meets vacationers from the first moments of being on these lands. The picturesque landscapes represented by hills are covered with a tropical carpet of vegetation, rocky quiet bays with snow-white beaches break the shores, the purity and transparency of the waters with lots of amusement and attractions - all this attracts an endless stream of holiday-makers every year.

The island of Phuket, whose dimensions are 48 by 21 km, is the largest in Thailand. It is connected to the mainland by a dam with a highway. There is an international airport where regular flights from major cities and capitals are carried out.

In addition to the possibility of sunbathing on the territory of velvet beaches, tourists have a chance to get acquainted with the rich history of this place, due to the presence of a number of attractions. Ancient architecture incorporated the echoes of Portuguese discoverers, English industrialists and Chinese entrepreneurs.

Beach

Bangtao Tropical Residence 4 * hotel, the description of which is presented to your attention in the article, is located 800 meters from the public beach. A promenade runs through it, intersected by the roadway. For the convenience of tourists, a bus runs in both directions throughout the day.
