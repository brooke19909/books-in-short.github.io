Where is the resort "Volginsky" and how to get to it

The health resort is located in the Vladimir region, near the border with Moscow. Therefore, the journey from the capital does not take much time. The resort is located in an ecologically clean area. Nearby there is a pine forest, the clean river Volga flows (it is also called Volgina), which gave its name to the health resort. She is the left tributary of the Klyazma. One hundred and ten kilometers from the capital of Russia, 80 km from the MKAD, is where the Volginsky sanatorium (Vladimir region) is located. How to get to the health resort from Moscow? From the Kursk station, quite often trains leave for Nizhny Novgorod, Vladimir, Cheboksary. Take the train to Pokrov station by train. From the local station, take a taxi or city minibus to the place where the regular bus leaves for the village of Volginsky. You need to get off at the "Department Store" stop. You can get to Pokrov from the Moscow bus station Schelkovsky. Then you will not need to travel around the city to take a flight to Volginsky. Own vehicles from Moscow are easy to reach. It is only necessary to taxi to the Moscow Ring Road and take the exit onto the highway to Nizhny Novgorod. Having passed Pokrov, you should look for the road sign of the Federal State Institution "Center for Rehabilitation of the FSS of the Russian Federation" Volginsky "." This is a left turn on the highway. On this road to the resting place is only three kilometers. If you move from the city of Vladimir, then along the highway Nizhny Novgorod - Moscow to the turn on "Volginsky" about 80 km.

Enclosures and territory

Have you ever rested in a real estate manor? Not? Then come to the vicinity of the village of Mashinostroitel (Vladimir region). Sanatorium "Volginsky" was located in the former family estate of the Przorowski Princes, who owned these lands since 1625. The estate, and now the territory of the sanatorium, occupies thirty-six hectares! It is beautifully landscaped, everywhere there are therapeutic paths (paths), alleys, flower beds. Some rooms have also been preserved. But, of course, the sanatorium does not look a bit like it was during the time of the last master, Prozorovsky Semen Vasilyevich. In the health resort, which is already more than eighty years old, they built a pool and built new buildings. This is a wonderful combination of modernity and historical antiquity.

Volginsky (sanatorium, Vladimir region): an overview of medical services

The list of indications with which patients are referred here is quite extensive. These are diseases of the respiratory organs, circulatory disorders, ailments of the gastrointestinal tract, nervous disorders, problems with the musculoskeletal system. What is Vladimir Region so famous for in terms of treatment and rehabilitation? Sanatorium "Volginsky" stands on the site of access to the surface of mineral water. Therefore, vacationers have the opportunity to swim in the healing pool, take radon baths. The health resort has a whole hydrotherapy system. The sanatorium has not only a mineral well, but also the latest technical facilities. Highly qualified medical staff carries out the diagnosis and treatment of diseases. There are offices of narrow specialists: cardiologist, gastroenterologist, gynecologist and others. It is used electro-, light-, water-, thermo-, mechanotherapy, massages.



How to feed in a health resort

Sanatorium "Volginsky" (Vladimir region) offers vacationers healthy five meals a day. When you check in, you are examined by a doctor, who prescribes one of the fifteen diet menus. Fresh fruits and vegetables are served on tables throughout the year. The sanatorium has its own bakery. For healthy people who have arrived on vacation, there is the opportunity to order ordinary (non-dietary) dishes. The methods of fractional, separate meals, fasting days, etc. are applied. They feed the guests in the large bright hall of the dining room.



"Volginsky" (sanatorium, Vladimir region): reviews

Many people get to this health resort on preferential vouchers. But they are not traveling alone, but with family members who pay for their stay at full cost. Children are placed here at any age, but treatment requires that the child is four years old. Those who came just to relax say that it is not necessary to be bored here. There is a tennis court, and karaoke, and billiards, and sports grounds. The administration conducts recreational activities, arranges excursions. In the resort "Volginsky" (Vladimir region) come for several days (weekends, holidays, New Year's Eve).
