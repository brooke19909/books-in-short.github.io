LED lamps for indoor lighting: features, types, characteristics

If there is a need to create the right and comfortable lighting in the house, you should use light sources that would have a long service life, be able to significantly save electricity and emit uniform safe light. All these requests are fully consistent with LED lights for indoor lighting.



They are especially popular among professional designers, because with their help you can make zoning of rooms, highlight certain places in the interior. They are compact and impress with a variety of shapes, sizes and stylistic solutions.

Features and Benefits

  • Economic efficiency. Conventional incandescent lamps will consume forty to fifty percent more electricity than LED lamps for indoor lighting.
  • Long operation. The lifespan of such devices is about one hundred thousand hours. If you translate this into years, then a high-quality LED lamp can last from eleven to fifteen years.
  • Security. Such lighting devices are made from environmentally friendly materials that are not harmful to human health.
  • Strength. Since there are no glass cases and incandescent filaments in LED structures, they are resistant to various external influences (shock, vibration, etc.).
  • Soundless operation and correct color presentation. The white LED lamp will not, like a fluorescent lamp, wink or crack during operation. Such devices give even lighting without flickering. Luminous flux stability has a positive effect on eye health. They do not get tired, even if you worked all night with the LED lamp on.
  • Easy installation. Standard connectors, which are equipped with all LED lamps for indoor lighting, allow you to mount them, even without skills in working with electricity.




Manufacturers pay great attention to the quality development of lighting products. Power supply of LED lamps is an important point that determines the lighting and functional capabilities of lighting devices. At the factory, various factors are taken into account, ranging from efficiency and ending with the quality of the light flux and the temperature of the light.

Varieties

Currently, manufacturers of LED lights offer a huge variety of models. Devices differ in their functionality, technical characteristics and external design.

  • Point.
  • Outboard.
  • Linear
  • Overhead.
  • Sconce.
  • Portable (floor lamps and table lamps).
  • LED strip.

Each room has its own lamp.

If you want to focus on some interior details or furniture elements, it is recommended to use LED lamps for indoor lighting. For example, to equip a living room, round or square overhead lamps are best suited. They are usually equipped with elegant shades and can decorate any interior. For the hallway, a suspended light source is better, which will not take up much space, will not make the ceiling of a small room heavier, and will give the maximum amount of light.



For children's rooms, manufacturers of LED lamps offer a variety of thematic models. It can be a month or a bunny, a ball, a balloon, a butterfly, etc. Strict geometric forms have models designed for libraries, workrooms and similar rooms requiring a concise design. As for the utility and technical rooms, you can choose a lamp of any shape.

Furniture items

In recent years, recessed LED lights for lighting furniture have become especially demanded. They are mounted both from the inside and on the outer surface. Such devices are very convenient if you need to open a drawer in the dark or open the cabinet door in the dressing room. They are also often installed on furniture in pantries, basements, laundries or technical rooms.



When choosing furniture LED fixtures, it is necessary to take into account the specific place of their installation. If the installation is planned in rooms with high humidity (bath, pool, home sauna, kitchen), then the lighting device must have a high degree of protection.
