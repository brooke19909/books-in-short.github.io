Hotel Bella Vista Resort 4 * (Egypt / Hurghada): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Hurghada is one of the most famous tourist cities in Egypt, where travelers can not only find a comfortable overnight stay and have a lot of entertainment, but also plunge into the mysterious atmosphere of the East. It is in this resort area that the Bella Vista Resort hotel complex is located. Of course, travelers who choose it as a temporary refuge are interested in any additional information about this place. So what can a hotel offer?

Where is the hotel complex located?





Hotel Bella Vista Resort has a huge advantage, namely, a favorable location. To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel complex is located on the territory of the New City with all its entertainment and attractions, and the sea is just a stone's throw from here. Very close to the hotel territory there is a large market where you can buy almost everything, from food to souvenirs, clothes and jewelry. Hurghada International Airport is located 5 km from the hotel - you can get to it in 15-20 minutes, which is also convenient.

What do hotel rooms look like?





The Bella Vista Resort has 270 spacious rooms. These are standard rooms of different capacities. The rooms are in good condition - there is a set of necessary comfortable furniture, and the design here is pleasant, giving the rooms a homely feel. It has a private terrace or balcony with outdoor furniture and offers views of the garden, sea or city.

Of course, in all rooms there is a set of household appliances, without which it is difficult to imagine a really comfortable stay for a modern tourist. For example, you can spend an evening watching your favorite satellite channels. All rooms are equipped with powerful air conditioning. There is a direct dial telephone, as well as a minibar, which, however, is filled at the request of residents. Some rooms have a small safe.

The spacious bathroom has a shower. There is also a toilet, sink, large mirror, hairdryer, as well as clean towels, sets of shampoos, soaps and other hygiene items.

The hotel complex Bella Vista Resort provides its customers with full meals. The main meals are organized in the premises of the main restaurant, where all guests are invited. It serves traditional international and more exotic oriental dishes. According to reviews, the choice of dishes is quite large - delicious breakfasts, a variety of cold snacks, slices and side dishes, as well as fish and meat dishes are served on the table. Where is the beach located? Water activities for vacationers





Beautiful coral reefs and long sandy beaches are what Hurghada is famous for. Bella Vista Resort owns a section of its own beach, which, in fact, goes to the hotel area. The coast here is covered with soft sand, there is a cleared backwater, which is suitable for bathing children. There is also a pier, which is used by lovers of swimming in the depths. By the way, the marina is located very close by - you can rent a sea ‚Äã‚Äãtransport here or purchase an excursion along the coast.

On the shore in sufficient quantities are sun loungers and deck chairs. Here, guests can receive clean beach towels. Fans of more active recreation will not be bored. For example, here you can swim with a mask, because there is a small coral reef nearby. You will have the opportunity to rent a boat, catamaran, go boating, water skiing, sailing. Many tourists turn to the local diving school, where you can take training courses, rent the necessary equipment, and also purchase a sea tour to the most interesting places on the coast.

Are there any conditions for relaxing with a child?

Since many tourists prefer to travel with their children, the issue of the availability of appropriate amenities is also important. What can the hotel offer? To begin with, it is worth saying that an extra bed will be delivered to the room upon request, and in the restaurant you can always ask for a high chair for the baby.

In the adult pool there is a separate section for bathing children - here the water is always warm, and therefore the guys love to splash and play. There is also a small playground with swings where you can relax. If necessary, a professional nanny will remain with the child, whose services, however, are not included in the price.

A currency exchange office is constantly working here. There are also several small shops where you can buy souvenirs and household items. Entertainment and leisure of tourists





Despite the relatively small territory, hotel guests still have something to do. As already mentioned, in the courtyard there is a large freshwater pool, and around it on the terrace are sun loungers with mattresses and sun umbrellas. There is also a bar where you can get chilled drinks and delicious cocktails.

In addition, you can visit the fitness center, which built a good gym. Here you can sign up for group classes in water aerobics, gymnastics, and oriental dances. There are tennis tables and a billiard room where you can also have a good time. The hotel has a small team of animators who organize various contests, competitions and shows throughout the day.

Not all guests spend time at the Bella Vista Resort. Egypt is a real paradise for tourists. Here you can get an excursion to interesting places in the vicinity or even the country. And in the city center you will find many entertainment, including nightclubs, discos and various attractions.
