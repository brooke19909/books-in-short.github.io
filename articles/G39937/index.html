Appearance

The first thing that everyone pays attention to when choosing a smartphone is its appearance. And here it is classic enough for ZTE products. The rear camera is located in the middle of the upper part of the smartphone, next to it is a flash, and on the other hand is a stereo speaker. Pleasantly surprises customers the fact that the phone has a matte finish. It is it that contributes to the fact that the device does not slip in the hands, and it is not so necessary to purchase a cover. The back cover adjoins the smartphone very tightly, therefore, it does not play or creak when used. On the bottom touch panel in the middle there is a blue round control ring and two more buttons, the focus of which can be changed independently. For example, to call up the menu or instantly start the camera. On the bottom end panel there is a microphone and a micro-usb connector for connecting a charger or cable. Communication

Pleasantly pleased with the presence of two active SIM cards. In modern times, a rare person manages for calls only one. And each has its own reasons for acquiring a dual SIM card. The format of SIM cards is supported by mini-size. With this, problems no longer arise. Cellular operators by default provide SIM cards of different sizes. They are inserted into the Blade L3 under the back cover, for which you have to pull out the battery. This is done without much effort, but at the same time the battery holds tight, without flying out by itself. The antenna of the smartphone is quite strong, the connection catches confidently even in problematic places where the coverage area is not too high quality. It is noteworthy that the first SIM card slot has a moisture sensor. The device notifies that a wet SIM card is inserted. A notification appears in the quick access toolbar. Wi-Fi also works very high quality, keeps the access point to the last, while this allows the coverage area.
