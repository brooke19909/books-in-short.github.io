Coral Beach Rotana Resort Tiran 4: a luxury vacation in Egypt

Description: Coral Beach Rotana Resort Tiran 4 is a luxurious hotel located in Sharm El Sheikh, which is considered to be one of the most popular resort areas, especially among foreign tourists. This wonderful hotel complex was built very close to the sea coast, not far from the picturesque Naama Bay. The nearest international airport can be reached in just fifteen minutes.





To begin with, it is worth noting that the Coral Beach Rotana Resort Tiran hotel complex includes several buildings. There is also a large, luxuriously decorated private territory. Here, large swimming pools, playgrounds for relaxation and games, as well as winding paths for walks and a garden, where you can relax, enjoying the shade of palm trees and the beauty of exotic plants, are waiting for vacationers.

Coral Beach Rotana Resort Tiran 4 is renowned for its hospitality, high level of comfort and excellent service.

Description of rooms: The hotel has 365 rooms. Here, each guest can choose what suits him best - there are standard rooms, suites, family rooms, as well as spacious luxury apartments.





All rooms are very comfortable, decorated in a classic European style. Here guests will find comfortable furniture, necessary household appliances, as well as a balcony or terrace, from where you can enjoy breathtaking views of the sea or the tranquil beauty of an exotic garden.

Of course, all rooms at Coral Beach Rotana Resort Tiran 4 are air-conditioned. There is also a telephone, TV and a generous minibar. The bathroom is attached to the room.

Meals : Coral Beach Rotana Resort Tiran 4 operates on an all-inclusive basis. Guests are offered full meals in bars and eateries. On the territory of the complex there are two excellent restaurants with a wide selection of dishes of international and Mediterranean cuisine. There are also wonderful stylish bars and cozy cafes. In addition, the resort town has a lot of interesting places where you can have a tasty lunch or just have fun.

Beach : The hotel has its own private beach, which is located about 50 meters from the main building. Umbrellas and deck chairs are placed here. There are all conditions for a wonderful pastime. You can practice volleyball, scuba diving or take a boat ride.





Additional information : As already mentioned, the hotel offers first-class service. There is a large parking lot, laundry and dry cleaning, room for ironing. In addition, massage rooms, a sauna, a traditional Turkish bath are working. The hotel has several shops, as well as a currency exchange. Almost throughout the territory there is free internet access. If necessary, you can call a doctor who will offer truly high-quality medical care.

The smallest guests of the hotel will also be pleased, as there is a large and safe pool for children, a playground for games with bright swings, as well as a games room where experienced babysitters look after the children.

And, of course, it is worth signing up for at least one excursion, because in this part of Egypt there is something to see. In addition, the guides know their job, while traveling you are unlikely to be bored. Here, each guest can find a suitable way to have fun.

Reviews: Many tourists choose the Coral Beach Rotana Resort Tiran 4 hotel for relaxation. Reviews about it are mostly positive. Almost every client first of all notes luxuriously decorated territory and comfortable rooms, where it is always a pleasure to spend time. The food is also on the level - the dishes are varied and very tasty. And, of course, it is worth noting a clean beach with soft sand, where it is so nice to relax. Families who come here with children are also satisfied with the living conditions, because even the smallest tourists have fun.
