Crochet maple leaf photo, application and diagram

Maple leaves with carved edges have long become a kind of autumn symbol. A few weeks after the end of the summer, they can be seen almost everywhere: in stores, in social networks, on the pages of magazines and, of course, on the sidewalks and streets of your city.

Modern knitters, like real artists, reflect in their work the surrounding reality and their view of reality. Every day they develop a host of new schemes for creating two-dimensional copies of the most diverse gifts of nature. Maple leaves are no exception.



Knitting asymmetric motifs

There are many schemes that depict the crochet maple leaf creation algorithm. This is not surprising, because every master strives to achieve uniqueness and originality in his own way. Often the crocheted maple leaf pattern is based on a circular motif, and the carved edges are depicted as asymmetric branches. By adjusting the number of rows, the craftswoman can influence the size of the motive. A crocheted maple leaf scheme can be not only a strict instruction, but also an impetus to the development of a new design. Often the most successful are precisely those products that were created on the basis of borrowed, but modified and improved developments.



Crochet: Maple Leaf - Hot Stand

The resulting canvas can be used as an independent product, for example, as a stand for hot cups. Also, maple leaves of different shades and sizes are often used to compose Irish lace.

Three-element maple leaf

To get a decorative motif resembling a maple leaf, you can use the following scheme.



In its center there is also a circle of SSN (rows 1 and 2), however, the carved elements are made in a completely different way. In the second row (in parallel with the implementation of the CCH), the designer proposes to knit chains of VP in three places. The third and fourth row are built on the strapping of these chains.



The relief is achieved thanks to several tricks:

  1. At the top of the chains, columns are added.
  2. Between the chains (in the hollows), the columns are reduced.
  3. In the process of performing the last (fourth) row, the craftswoman must knit a picot from three VPs, as well as a leaf stalk.

Ready-made motif can be used as an interior decor or as part of an applique. Improving the description of maple leaf, you can make delicate motifs.
