Azimut: recreation center in the Leningrad region

In the Leningrad Region, in Zelenogorsk, near the coast of the Gulf of Finland, Azimut was built - a recreation center for the whole family. Two kilometers from the hotel is located Lake Beauty, which received the name for a reason. The Karelian strict nature of this area is incredibly aesthetic, especially on summer evenings, when the sun goes down, never leaving the horizon - they enjoy white nights for almost two months at this latitude.





Azimut: advantageous location

A convenient venue for a weekend for a resident of St. Petersburg is the Azimut recreation center. Zelenogorsk is located just 60 minutes by public transport from the cultural capital of Russia. They get here by train, bus, minibus, private taxi, in a personal car. The route is going over a distance of 60 km. From the railway station to Azimut 2.5 km.





"Azimut" is a recreation center built near the Pukhtolovaya Mountain, where guests will find a modern ski resort. While the snow lies, the classic snow track is open, there is an inexpensive equipment rental point.
