How to put a ringtone on Lumiya? Smartphone Nokia Lumia: instructions

A new mobile phone is always the reason for a long study of the interface and functions of the gadget. By trial and error, sooner or later, of course, you can achieve the desired result. But you can save time and learn in advance about how the phone works. In this article, we will take a closer look at the Nokia Lumia 630 mobile phone and see how to put a ringtone on Lumiya.

Nokia Lumia 630: specs and features

Phone "Nokia Lumiya" is a smartphone or, as it is sometimes called, a handheld computer. A micro SIM card is suitable for him. A smartphone only supports one SIM card. This gadget does not work on the Android platform, but on the Microsoft Windows 8 and Windows 8.1 operating system. In addition, there is 0.5 GB RAM and a built-in 8 GB memory reserve. The Nokia Lumiya phone can support an additional microSD flash card with memory up to 128 GB.





As for the operating time, the smartphone operates 16 hours in 2G mode, 13 in 3G, and 648 hours in standby mode.

Display Nokia Lumia 630 has a diagonal of 4.5 inches, the screen resolution is 854 x 480. There is a sensor to adjust the brightness. The display is equipped with touch navigation. The color scheme is 16 million different colors.

The processor of the Lumia smartphone is 4 cores. Camera - 5 megapixels. However, there is no front camera and no flash. The camera is able to zoom 4 times. The smallest focus distance is 0.1 m.

In addition, the smartphone can connect to Wi-Fi points and distribute it to other devices (you can connect up to 8 extraneous gadgets). "Nokia Lumiya 630" is capable of transmitting and receiving files via Bluetooth, supports GPS. There is also a headphone jack, radio functions, MP3 player. You will not find any buttons on the smartphone (except the power button and volume), all data is entered using the touch screen.

The first start of the device: instruction

In order to set any ringtone, you must first turn on a new phone. This is not done as usual, so the instruction will be presented later. "Lumiya 630" first requires charging. New phones are always completely discharged, so it is recommended to charge them repeatedly. However, once is quite enough.

  • Press the start button. By the way, instead, you can turn on the phone with a double tap on the screen, but only if such a feature is enabled. You can find out what functions of Lumiya were already launched at the time of purchase of the gadget from the sales assistant in the store.




  • The first time you open the offer to create a Microsoft account. If you already have it on another device, then you can use it here or create a new one.
  • Further we will familiarize with the menu. The smartphone has a desktop and a list of applications. Frequently used applications for quick access are usually placed on the desktop. To see all of them, swipe left on the display.
  • To select an operation for a particular application, press and hold the tile icon. Then you can select "To the desktop" or another function.

After an easy acquaintance with the interface, you can proceed to the question of how to put a melody on a call to Lumiya.

Nokia Lumia 630 ringer volume

To adjust the ringer volume, you must use the "Ringer + notifications" function at the top of the screen. You can set the level you need not only for a call, but also for audio and video files.



  • Click on the small arrow next to "Call + notifications."
  • To adjust the volume, drag the slider to the right or left.
  • To completely switch the phone to silent mode, click on the bell icon. The vibration alert will turn on. To turn it off, click the Vibrate on icon.

How to set a standard ringtone

If you are tormented by the question of how to put a melody on a bell on Lumiya, do not rack your brains. Everything is quite simple, you just need to open the phone settings.

You will see the "All Settings" button if you swipe down from the black bar at the top of the screen. Click the button and go to settings.

Like most phones and smartphones, Nokia Lumia has a certain number of standard factory ringtones. In the list of settings that opened in front of you, select "Ringtones + sounds." How to put your melody on a call

Many people think how to put a ringtone on Lumiya that will differ from standard melodies. It is pretty simple:

  • Touch the Select Song button. You will see a list of all available audio files in the built-in memory and on microSD.

Separate ringtones for contacts

In addition to all the mentioned characteristics of the smartphone, there is another very useful feature ("Nokia Lumiya 630"). You can put music on a call by defining separate ringtones for each number in your contact list. This will allow you to find out who is calling without taking the phone out of the bag, which is very convenient.





To set different ringtones, you need to follow the following instructions:

  • Open your contact list.
  • Select one of the numbers by touching it. Various options for actions that can be carried out with a contact will appear on the screen.
  • Click the pencil icon at the bottom of the screen. This button means Change.
  • Find "Ringtone" and tap it.
  • A list of ringtones will open. Now you can choose one of the melodies specifically for this contact.
