Hotel Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 * (Limassol, Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

If you want to get acquainted with the culture and sights of Cyprus, opt for a resort such as Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 *. It combines comfort, quality service and budget accommodation rates.





a brief description of

Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 * is a cozy apart-hotel, which is located in the heart of Limassol. Thanks to the overhaul, which was carried out in 2008, the institution is characterized by a modern interior and comfortable living conditions. Also, you will certainly be conquered by the friendly and friendly staff, which makes the atmosphere of the establishment warm and cozy.





Location

In the heart of the resort of Limassol, the Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 * Resort is located. It is 70 km from Larnaca International Airport, and 65 km from Paphos Air Harbor. As for the beach, then it is no more than 200 meters. Resting in this institution, you can visit the following tourist sites:

  • City Zoo (3 km);
  • archaeological museum (3 km);
  • Museum of Local Lore (3 km);
  • Dasoudi Beach (4 km);
  • Limassol Fortress (4 km);
  • marina (5 km);
  • Amathus (6 km).

Hotel Rooms

Hotel Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 * is quite small and includes only 46 apartments. Guests will have access to the following options for accommodation:

  • Standard one-room rooms are compact in size. Twin beds can accommodate 2 guests (they can be moved if necessary). It is also worth noting the possibility of providing additional seats. The window has a small seating area, which is represented by a coffee table and a pair of armchairs.
  • The studio is suitable for families. In addition to the bedroom area, there is also a living room with two soft sofas. In total, it can accommodate up to 4 guests. It is also worth noting the presence of a small kitchen area with electric appliances and utensils.




Room Amenities

The restrained and at the same time elegant interior characterizes the rooms of the Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 * resort. Photos of the apartments demonstrate conciseness and practicality, which organically combines with local motifs. In terms of amenities, guests can take advantage of the following options:

  • a bathroom with a shower cabin, a washbasin and a set of bath accessories;
  • a safe for storing valuables, documents and cash;
  • air conditioning system with the ability to individually control the air temperature in the room;
  • TV with numerous cable and satellite channels;
  • landline telephone through which you can contact the reception, as well as make a call abroad;
  • an electric kettle, dishes, as well as bags with hot drinks;
  • refrigerator for storing food and drinks;
  • private balcony with plastic chairs and a table.




Hotel infrastructure

The resort hotel should have a developed infrastructure that will allow guests to receive all the necessary services without leaving the territory. As for Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 *, this institution can offer the following:

  • reception desk, the staff of which at any time will come to your aid;
  • 2 bars where guests can enjoy quality local and imported drinks;
  • restaurants with local and international cuisine;
  • a souvenir shop where you can buy products of local craftsmen, as well as products with the hotel logo;

  • specially equipped smoking rooms and outdoor areas;
  • an elevator that will help you quickly get to the upper floors;
  • car parking, which all guests can use for free;
  • vehicle rental service;
  • a safe at the reception, which each guest can use for an additional fee;
  • wireless internet in public areas;
  • the necessary infrastructure for the comfort of people with disabilities;
  • own tropical garden with walking paths and decorative elements.




Services for guests

You will be surrounded by attention and care in such an institution as Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 *. Limassol is a welcoming resort, which is manifested in high standards of service. The following service is available to tourists:

  • food delivery from the restaurant to the apartments;
  • the formation of an individual diet menu;
  • secretarial services for business people;
  • organization of negotiations and celebrations;
  • use of laundry and dry cleaning services for an additional fee;
  • round-the-clock currency exchange.

Entertainment

A lot of entertainment awaits you in such a resort complex as Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 *. Cyprus is a great place for fans of outdoor activities. In this hotel you will have the following options:

  • outdoor swimming pool with the possibility of heating;
  • municipal landscaped beach;
  • Turkish hammam, as well as a steam sauna, where guests can relax, feel better and just cheer up;
  • a billiard room with a bar and TVs for watching music channels and sports matches;
  • tables for playing ping pong;
  • Guests can enjoy water sports at the beach.

For the smallest

An ideal place for a family vacation is Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol). Limassol is a great place to relax with children. This hotel has the following features for young tourists:

  • children's pool with a lot of inflatable toys;
  • a playground with swings, slides, horizontal bars and a sandbox (a safe cover is provided);
  • the restaurant includes a children's buffet and a corner with special comfortable furniture;
  • if necessary, cots, playpens and high chairs are provided in the rooms;
  • A qualified nanny can look after your children.

Additional Information

You should familiarize yourself with the rules of the hotel before the trip. As for this institution, it will be useful to know the following information:

  • Check-in starts after 14:00, and you need to leave the room on the day of departure before noon (early check-in and late check-out are possible for an additional fee);
  • children under 2 years stay free in the room with a baby cot;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • guests have the opportunity to pay for services received in cashless form.

Negative reviews

The Jasmine Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus) is characterized by some negative aspects. Reviews allow us to draw conclusions about such shortcomings of this institution:

  • monotonous breakfasts with a fairly modest assortment of dishes;
  • the staff does not speak Russian, and therefore you need to learn at least a few basic phrases in English;
  • the maids are not thorough enough to clean the room (of course, there is no dust and debris, but practically do not pay attention to mirrors and plumbing);
  • bed linen is changed very rarely;
  • in the bathroom, the drain works terribly, and there is also an unpleasant smell of sewage and mold;
  • the rooms, lounges and other rooms smelled of tobacco smoke;
  • they do not clean or change filters in air conditioners, and therefore guests are forced to breathe dust;
  • old furniture in the rooms;
  • the hood does not work in the kitchen area, and therefore, if you want to cook something, the whole room will be filled with the smell of food;
  • to get to the beach, you need to cross a busy highway;
  • even if you book a room in advance, upon arrival it may turn out that your room is occupied, and therefore you will have to spend one or several nights in apartments of another category;
  • the TV does not show Russian channels, and therefore it is boring to be in the room;
  • the hotel has almost no entertainment (especially boring in the evenings);
  • in order to use the TV, you need to make a deposit of 10 euros per remote control;
  • at night, with a strong roar, garbage is removed in the hotel courtyard, which is especially audible if you like to sleep with the windows open;
  • if the windows of your room overlook the outbuildings, many flies will fly into the room;
  • maids do not wash the floor on the balcony;
  • in order to get a cosmetic kit, you need to independently guard the maid and ask her;
  • sheets on beds with holes;
  • old plumbing in the bathroom;
  • old repair;
  • the room has poor lighting;
  • in the rooms located on the ground floor, you can find insects.

General impression

This hotel is quite old, and therefore the atmosphere in it is appropriate. Repair has not been carried out for a long time, but because the walls, furniture and the whole interior are pretty worn. As for the rooms, their equipment has also not been updated for a long time. Nevertheless, the atmosphere is very comfortable due to the successful layout, large windows and concise design. If we talk about nutrition, then it is not replete with diversity.

The main advantage of the hotel is its convenient location. It is located in the very center of Limassol near the beach and city infrastructure. That is why this option of accommodation is chosen by lovers of outdoor activities. If you prefer to spend most of the time on site, then after a few days the situation will seem depressing to you.
