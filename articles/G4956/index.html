Hotel Byblos 4 *, Tunisia. Tunis Hotel Reviews

Which country for vacation is best advised to a tourist who wants to combine a unique sightseeing trip with a visit to the beach, walks in the picturesque surroundings and colorful shopping? Of course, Tunisia. Here, architectural monuments and ancient cities are still preserved, there are comfortable resorts and famous oriental bazaars.



Features of rest in Tunisia

Along with Turkey and Egypt, Tunisia is considered one of the most popular destinations for tourists from Russia. The state is located in the north of Africa, Islam is practiced here. You do not need a visa for the trip, it is advised to take euros or dollars from the currency with you, which can be exchanged at the airport or in hotels.

If the purpose of your trip to Tunisia is relaxation, Hammamet is the ideal place to choose a hotel. Naturally, all free time is better spent outside the hotel, because the country is unusual and fabulously beautiful. Pay special attention to your appearance, try to avoid too frank and open outfits - in Tunisia holy laws are respected . Avoid drinking alcohol while walking around the city, it is also forbidden to smoke. It is impossible to take photographs of local residents without permission; it is forbidden to take pictures of the Presidential Palace.

The best time to travel to these places is from April to June, as well as from September to mid-November. The weather is comfortable and not too hot. The best accommodation option if you choose Tunisia (Hammamet) for your trip - 4 star hotels where there is everything you need for a comfortable stay at affordable prices. In this article we will pay special attention to the Hotel Byblos 4 * hotel.





Hotel surroundings

Nearby is the Zizou Paintball Club Hammamet - a popular center where in the lap of nature they offer to play paintball. In the resort of Hammamet, where the hotel is located, several golf clubs are organized, equipment is available for rent.

Medina of Hammamet - an ancient fortress. Nearby there is a colorful market where you can buy souvenirs and handmade products. As in many eastern bazaars, prices here are overstated at times. You should go shopping, check prices and only then bargain.

Traveling with children, go to the local zoo "Frigiya". Here live giraffes and ostriches, tigers and elephants. There is a swimming pool with dolphins, which arrange interesting performances. The zoo has restaurants and cafes where you can relax and eat. An amusement park with slides and playgrounds is nearby.

Sea vacation

Own sandy beach of the hotel Hotel Byblos 4 * (Tunisia) is an excellent option for a comfortable holiday for tourists. The only drawback, judging by the reviews, is the range (the distance is about 550 meters). It is easy to add color to the rest if you use the trolley that the Omar donkey drags ("transport" delivers tourists to the sea and back at will). Coastal water is clean and clear.

Tourists are not provided with beach towels , there are umbrellas and sunbeds, you need to pay separately for mattresses. There is a bar where you can buy water, juice and other drinks.

Sightseeing vacation

"Tunisian Sahara". The tour lasts 2 days and begins with a visit to the city of El Jem, whose main attraction is the Colosseum. Holidays in Hammamet will be inferior if you refuse this trip. The Tunisian Colosseum has survived better than all those that exist today. It was here that the most impressive shots of the Gladiator were shot, and today, rock singers and opera performers become its frequent guests.

Further along the course is the city of Matmata, in which the tribes of troglodytes live. The uniqueness is that it consists entirely of caves dug in grottoes from chalk. The next stop is planned in the city of Douz, where the Sahara desert symbolically begins. Camels are prepared for a trip through the desert. The journey through the sands lasts no more than an hour, after the tourists are returned and placed at the hotel for the night. The last place to visit will be Kairouan - the sacred Muslim city in which there are about 85 temple complexes.

Carthage is one of the oldest settlements on the planet, built in the 9th century BC. Today, there is little left of the old buildings, the Roman part with the aqueducts, therms and facades of the former office buildings is best preserved. As a rule, a trip to Carthage is combined with visits to other attractions.

Dugga is a city built by the ancient Romans. Today, there is an open-air museum where you can see the temple of the goddess Concord, the sanctuary of Mercury, the capitoline temple and the mausoleum.

There is an excursion with a visit to Sidi Bou Ali - the largest reserve in the territory of which there is a center for breeding ostriches.
