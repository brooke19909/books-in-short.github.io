Smartphone HTC One E8 Dual Sim

HTC is a notorious Taiwanese-British company manufacturing smartphones and tablets. The products of this company have always been famous for their high quality, reliability. In this article, we will take a closer look at the recently released offspring of HTC - One E8 Dual Sim. Want to know more about this smartphone? Welcome to this article.

HTC One E8 Dual Sim: review

In fact, the new One E8 is a slightly simplified model of the flagship One M8. The HTC One E8 Dual Sim phone borrowed design and some technologies from previous lines. But this does not mean that the new smartphone is just a pale copy of the flagship. The One E8 has tons of interesting and unique features that have not previously been seen on HTC phones. You can verify this by reading this review.

Design

Smartphone HTC One E8 Dual Sim has a rather interesting design. In fact, this is a mixture of two other flagships of the company. Thanks to rounded corners, a curved back panel and flat edges, the smartphone looks very stylish. As for the material, the case is completely made of high quality plastic. Glossy and matte versions are also available.





The phone is ideally in your hand, no matter how you take it. The body of the device is not sticky, quite pleasant to the touch. Another advantage of plastic is that it does not show small scratches or damage. In addition, a variety of models are pleased. Smartphones are available in various colors (dark red, gray, blue, white, etc.). Therefore, everyone will be able to choose a gadget in accordance with their taste preferences.

Specifications

On board the HTC One E8 Dual Sim, there is a quad-core processor called Qualcomm Snapdragon 801. Its clock frequency is approximately 2.5 gigahertz. Adreno 330 acts as a powerful video adapter. The phone itself has 2 gigabytes of RAM and 16 gigabytes for storing various data and applications. If desired, you can purchase a microSD-card and increase the memory capacity up to 128 gigabytes. It is also worth saying a few words about the design. Thanks to the competent arrangement of components, the smartphone does not overheat even with high continuous loads.

Performance

The brand new One E8 is a direct confirmation that good optimization is much more important than the "stuffing". Although the brainchild of HTC cannot boast of super-powerful hardware, in terms of performance, it can compete even with 64-bit phones. This was achieved thanks to the amazing optimization. HTC One E8 Dual Sim is capable of launching the most demanding games without any sagging or delays (for example, NOVA 3, NFS, Asphalt, etc.).





Display

E8 inherited from its predecessors not only design, but also the screen. The main features of the 5-inch display are high brightness, contrast and maximum viewing angles. This protects against annoying glare and allows you to read even in the sun. In addition, high pixel density pleases. Thanks to this, the smartphone gives a very clear, detailed picture. Among modern smartphones, it is unlikely to be able to find a model with a better screen. The sensor layer is capable of supporting up to 10 simultaneous touches.

Sound

A high-quality sound system is a feature of high-budget smartphones from HTC. And the brand new One E8 is no exception. The phone boasts front speakers and unique BoomSound technology. All this gives a very rich, clean, lively and loud sound. And thanks to the screen of high and medium frequencies, deep bass is very well felt. Listening to music with HTC One E8 Dual Sim is one thing.

Camera

This is another advantage of the One E8. The 13 megapixel main camera delivers amazingly amazing high-resolution images. Their quality is comparable even with professional SLRs. In addition, the camera has a fairly wide viewing angle. Therefore, no important detail will be lost. As for the front-end, everything here is also up to par. An additional 5 megapixel camera allows you to take clear and detailed pictures. In addition, the smartphone has a unique technology called Touch Up, through which you can make minor cosmetic adjustments. Thanks to all this, the HTC One E8 Dual Sim can be safely called a selfie phone.

Also in the smartphone there is a proprietary function from HTC called Zoe. It allows you to combine pictures and short videos with each other, gives you new editing options, etc.

Battery

One E8 from its older brother M8 received a lithium-polymer battery with a capacity of 2600 mAh. Thanks to this, the smartphone boasts amazing autonomy. A full battery lasts for 2 or even 3 days of operation. In addition, the phone has two power-saving modes. The first simply optimizes the processor, lowers the brightness of the display, removes background data and vibration. The second (emergency) puts the phone in a special mode with a new interface, in which only basic functions are saved. The smartphone, in emergency mode and having about 10% charge, can last more than 30 hours.





Features

We should also talk about the new features that were implemented in One E8. Perhaps one of the most high-profile and significant innovations is the Motion Launch system. Thanks to it, you can control your smartphone using gestures. It is very comfortable. For example, in order to answer a call, you can simply bring the phone to your ear. And to turn on the camera, you should hold the smartphone in a horizontal position and press one of the volume buttons.
