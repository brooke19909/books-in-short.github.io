What is a binary option?

‚ÄúBinary‚Äù means two outcomes: either all or nothing. When exercising the option, the player receives a reward in case of a win. And no matter how much the price of the asset has changed. The main thing is to guess the direction of change.

European and American rules differ when determining the actual and set price. In Europe, a check is made at the time the option is closed. In America, the comparison goes over the entire range of the option from the moment of opening to the moment of closing.
