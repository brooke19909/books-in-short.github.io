Voronezh hotels: list, reviews

A reason to visit the capital of the Black Earth region - Voronezh - may be a business trip, study or just relax. This is a developed city, recovered and revived after the devastation during the Great Patriotic War. The doors of many hotels are open for guests of the city, which warmly welcome tourists and businessmen, as well as provide high-level service and a warm welcome to each guest. In the article we will consider the most popular hotels in Voronezh. Photos of each of them are also presented to your attention in the article. The list of the most popular hotels is as follows:

  • Marriott Voronezh Hotel.
  • Park Hotel "AMAKS".
  • Country Hotel Taiga.
  • Hotel Voronezh.
  • Hotel Azimut.
  • "Hotel Yar".

Marriott Hotel in Voronezh and its privileges

Many hotels are located in the central part of the city, close to historical attractions. One of the newest hotels, which has been welcoming guests since 2017, is the Marriott Voronezh Hotel. Glass facades, modern design of rooms, interesting color schemes in the design of apartments, according to guests, are fully consistent with modern trends and a high level of comfort. The fund includes 221 rooms, which belong to the categories: deluxe, executive room, junior suite and premium suite. All are equipped with air conditioning, tea and coffee set, safe and telephone.

Buffet breakfast is included in the price. A wide selection of diverse delicious and nutritious dishes, as experienced tourists note, is impressive. There is a children's menu. Grill and Grain is a restaurant specializing in first-class steaks and seafood. Here you can taste long-aging wines and relax in a calm and relaxed atmosphere. Restaurants Olea and Avenue 38 will delight visitors with masterpieces of Mediterranean cuisine and delicious pastries.

Check In Rules

Accommodation at the Marriott Hotel (Voronezh) with pets is not allowed. Smoking is prohibited throughout. The staff speaks Russian and English. Room service and newspapers are available. Cleaning and cleaning is carried out daily. There are 5 conference rooms equipped with innovative technology for negotiations and meetings. High-speed Wi-Fi is free everywhere. The modern fitness center is equipped with cardiovascular equipment that will help maintain beautiful body shapes and diversify leisure activities.

Park Hotel "AMAKS" and its features

The AMAKS Park Hotel is recognized as an excellent business-class establishment, located 11 km from the airport and 9 km from the railway station. According to guests staying in it, you can not only spend the night here, but also have a great time in the company of friends or business partners, visiting a bowling alley, billiards or a sauna. Within walking distance are the leading exhibition centers of the city, the aquarium and shopping centers. Check-in starts at 14:00, leaving the apartment on the day of departure should be before noon. The reception is open 24 hours a day. High speed internet in open access. A gift shop is located on site.

Living conditions and additional services

Within the walls of the park-hotel AMAKS (Voronezh) it is possible to live with animals. However, this service requires an additional charge. Smoking in rooms is strictly prohibited. Transfer and Room Service are paid upon.

For settlement offered 138 rooms of various categories. The interior is modest, but overnight incredibly comfortable. Here everyone can choose housing that meets individual requirements. The floors are carpeted. The walls are decorated with paintings. The furniture is made of wood. There is air conditioning, hairdryer, telephone and minibar, which is empty upon check-in. There is a set of utensils for tea drinking.

This park hotel in Voronezh is quite popular. Own spa center allows guests to forget about everyday problems, the commotion of city life and plunge into the world of true relaxation and peace. A Finnish sauna, a Russian bathhouse and a lounge are what will give visitors a good mood and good spirits. The business area will combine a pleasant pastime with work.

Country Hotel Taiga

Stay away from the noisy city life and retire in a calm atmosphere full of tranquility and tranquility is easily realized in the Taiga hotel complex. It is surrounded by numerous pines and oaks, giving each guest a surprisingly clean air and unique beauty of landscapes.

According to holidaymakers, strength is quickly restored in these parts and a good mood appears. The room fund of the Taiga Park Hotel (Voronezh) includes 14 log cabins and the main building with 15 rooms. All of them vary in size and categories: standard, deluxe and deluxe comfort. Breakfast is served as a gift for all guests. You can satisfy your hunger in a two-story restaurant or in one of the local cafes. A meal by the water, as guests note, is the most romantic and inspiring.

Infrastructure

This place is considered ideal for celebrations and business seminars or corporate events. For those who wish to cook kebabs, barbecue facilities are provided. The high temperatures of the Russian bath will allow you to relax and restore strength. The services of a hairdresser and makeup artist will make you look beautiful 24 hours a day. There are playgrounds for sports games on the territory. You can play billiards or table tennis. The beach is neat and tidy. There is a children's play area. Bicycles and scooters, catamarans and boats are provided. Wireless high-speed Internet access is available anywhere in the complex.

Walking in the fresh air along the paved alleys among the fragrant aromas of greenery will increase immunity and improve the body. A visit to this tourist base is possible year-round.

Staying here will not only relax, but also enjoy a long-awaited vacation.

Pastime at the Hotel Yar

The history of the hotel complex Hotel Yar in Voronezh began at the end of the twentieth century. In 1997, the first tourists and guests of the city crossed the threshold of this institution. A global reconstruction was carried out in 2015, after which he was awarded 4 stars. Location in the forest zone, according to vacationers, is one of the main advantages. Clean atmosphere, fresh air, silence and remoteness from the bustle of the city - an environment that will give an excellent vacation and a great pastime.

The designers worked on the interior of the apartment in full. Each of the 44 rooms has a unique design. The guests say that here it blows with comfort and homely atmosphere. Wall paintings, cornices, unusual chandeliers and extraordinary design solutions allow you to radically change your usual environment and enjoy a country vacation. The chambers are equipped with satellite TV, a safe, a minibar and free Wi-Fi.

Infrastructure and entertainment

The complex has an Orthodox church and a souvenir shop. You can get enough in one of two restaurants, the menu of which will satisfy the needs of even the most demanding visitor.
