Vilnius hotels: addresses, services, reviews

Vilnius, the capital of Lithuania, is an ancient city, its history is rich in events and the fate of many people. It has beautiful nature and numerous attractions. According to the published data of the Statistics Department of this country, the number of travelers visiting the capital of Lithuania is growing year by year. Especially a lot of guests come here from abroad. In the first half of 2016 alone, Vilnius hotels accommodated about half a million people in their apartments. In this regard, hotel complexes are being built and developed in the capital of Lithuania, and the quality of the services they provide is growing.

Hotel Air Inn Vilnius

Tourists arriving by plane to Vilnius or leaving here can get a comfortable overnight stay. The hotel at Vilnius Airport Air Inn Vilnius is located very close to its terminal.



Due to such a close location in this institution, you can often meet guests from various countries. Air Inn Vilnius often hosts airline flight crews and passengers. Guests with children will be comfortable. Here is a loyal attitude towards guests with animals.

"Panorama"

In the old town, near historical sites, on Sodi Street 14, the Panorama Hotel is located. There are many well-known brands, souvenir shops, restaurants and cafes in Vilnius, and many of them are within walking distance of this hotel.



224 rooms correspond to a three-star level of service. Convenient transport location of the hotel - near the bus and train station. For travelers who prefer car trips, there is a parking lot next to the building. Her services are paid extra. For settlement, standard double and single rooms, apartments and suites are offered. The set of furniture and the services provided correspond to three-star hotels. The restaurant hall is cozy, seats up to 160 visitors. The cuisine is exquisite, many dishes on the menu - with marine and Italian themes. There is a bar with a large selection of drinks.

The main feature of the hotel is its conference room, one of the most spacious in the capital of Lithuania. It is divided into eight halls. Hotel Panorama has become popular with travelers precisely because of the opportunity to hold conferences and seminars here. For the convenience of their participants, rooms are willingly booked here. The average cost of living is about 70-85 euros, you can book a room with a substantial discount.

Guests note the excellent location of the hotel and the high level of service. Good reviews about the restaurant, amazing views. Many clients express special gratitude to the service staff, who welcomes all visitors with cordiality and friendliness. Some travelers were not impressed by the size of the rooms and the level of lighting in them.

Hotel Carolina in Vilnius

Not far from the center of Vilnius on 13 Sausio Street, there is the Carolina Hotel, which has a 3 * plus category. There are suites and standard rooms. From their windows, guests can see the park and pine forest, TV tower. Here is a quiet and peaceful place. Tourists can park their car for free at the hotel for the entire stay.



Like many Vilnius hotels, Carolina has conference rooms - one large, for 550 people, and ten smaller. Their total capacity is 1800 people. There is a business center that offers the services of personal assistants, computer and copy machines. There is internet connection.

The hotel restaurant is cozy, has a summer terrace. Upscale restaurant chefs prepare food of national and other cuisines, striking the variety and sophistication of the dishes created.

The rooms have satellite TV, a telephone and free internet. A hairdryer and iron are available upon request. Two-room suites are equipped with a double bed, in the living room there are additional places in the form of a sofa bed.

For an active and enjoyable holiday there is a bowling and billiards, a sauna with a pool.

Guests note that the hotel has a cozy and clean, friendly staff that helps to quickly resolve issues of the guests.

The cost of living in a hotel with breakfast starts from 50 euros.
