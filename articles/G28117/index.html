The best hotels of Kislovodsk: photos and reviews of tourists

Kislovodsk is a famous resort city, which was flooded by the citizens of our vast country back in Soviet times. Today it is one of the most famous balneological and climatic resorts in Russia. Here is about a third of all health resorts in the region, that is, this city is the second in Russia in the number of resorts. Kislovodsk hotels are rarely empty, and a wide choice awaits tourists with a variety of incomes and requests. In this city, everyone will find a hotel for themselves, to their taste, you just need to get acquainted with the offers that exist on the market.



Kislovodsk Hotels in the Center

Many tourists prefer to stay in the city center so that you can easily choose any tourist routes. If we talk about such a clean city, then there is no big difference, living on a central street or on the outskirts, you will still breathe fresh air. However, the price range in the central region may be slightly higher than on the periphery, which must be taken into account. Let's look at the central hotels of Kislovodsk.

Hotel "Park Hotel"

This is a four-star complex, which amazes guests with its scope. It is located in the central part of the city, which means it provides step-by-step access to such attractions as the Narzan Gallery, the Colonnade, and the spa park. Not all Kislovodsk hotels can boast of so many opportunities for a great holiday. Here each room is furnished with love and care for its guests. Each vacationer can make his own choice, whether he will just have a good time or will be engaged in treatment. The hotel has a modern medical center that will allow you not only to undergo an examination, but also recommended procedures. Tourists respond very warmly to this hotel, especially emphasizing the possibility of taking children under 12 years old with them for free.



Entertainment center "Atlantis"

However, not all Kislovodsk hotels are designed for a relaxing family vacation. If you want to have fun in the company of friends, then welcome to this entertainment center. It is located in the city center, next to the Central Narzan Gallery. It offers modern rooms, as well as a range of entertainment. This is a hookah room and bar, a cozy cafe and bowling alley. What could be better, especially if it rains outside! Judging by the reviews, it is better not to find a place if you decide to celebrate a birthday at the resort.

Mini-hotel in the city center

Another option would be private hotels in Kislovodsk. Usually these are small hotels that differ in a warm welcome and a homely atmosphere. This is exactly what Beshpagir boarding house is like. It is located next to mineral springs, in the city center and is primarily aimed at those who like to have a good and comfortable rest. Judging by the reviews of tourists, being here, it is difficult to get rid of the feeling that you are at home, so cozy and calm in these walls. Like many hotels in Kislovodsk, Beshpagir primarily relies on the client, that is, his desire and comfort above all. At any time of the year you will find warmth and comfort.

Fashionable Algrado

If you do not like small and homely hotels in Kislovodsk, then pay attention to this modern and absolutely stunning hotel. This nine-story building, which was built in 2009, will provide you with all the necessary conditions for a comfortable and cozy stay. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a minibar, modern furniture and household appliances. The hotel is surrounded by local attractions, and the tour desk offers a range of options for interesting leisure activities. These are excursions to the most famous places of the Caucasian Mineral Waters. You are waiting for the resort park and Zhelyabovsky spring. Judging by the reviews, tourists of all ages will be interesting here, in addition, children under 4 years old live with their parents for free.



Hotel complex "Venice"

This is a modern complex that invites you to Kislovodsk. Hotels, reviews of which are full of words of gratitude, are not uncommon, but over the seven years of operation, Venice has gained a record number of regular customers who visit it every year. The advantage of the hotel complex is its convenient location. Tourists can choose where they will undergo treatment, within walking distance of not only the sanatorium, but also the Medical Park, as well as drinking pump rooms. The hotel building itself organically fits into the shopping and business center, being its integral part. Each room has soft carpet and comfortable furniture, climate control system and household appliances. The comfortable bathroom is equipped with everything you need: cosmetics, hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers.



Hotel near the popular sanatorium

Many come here to receive medical treatment, because Kislovodsk is famous for its sanatoriums. Hotels, hotels, pensions - all of them are designed to comfortably accommodate guests of the city. The Krugozor sanatorium is especially famous among visitors. The best doctors work here, and modern equipment allows you to diagnose any disease and develop the most effective treatment regimen. Very close is the hotel "Friendship-Rostov." Living in her comfortable apartments, you can visit treatment rooms and visit the main attractions of the Caucasian Mineral Waters. The hotel has 30 comfortable rooms, that is, it is designed for only 60 people. It can accommodate a small company and have a great corporate event. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for relaxation. Judging by the reviews of tourists, Krugozor is a place for an excellent family vacation, clean air and mineral springs will strengthen immunity and prepare for upcoming achievements. Very warmly, hotel guests speak about the staff, and especially about the skill of the cooks. At the same time, prices are not too high for an ordinary Russian family, which is extremely important. Room rates start at 750 rubles.



Guest houses

In any resort city, they grow like mushrooms after rain. Kislovodsk was no exception. Mini-hotels with a cozy interior, homeliness and affordable prices are the choice of most tourists. So, a small private house "Cascade" is located almost surrounded by the sanatoriums "Plaza" and "Kislovodsk", "Central Military". It is this location that provides an influx of tourists, because living here, judging by the reviews, is much cheaper than in a sanatorium. There are seven double rooms in the guest house, each of which consists of two rooms, a bedroom and a living room. Many city guests stay here from year to year, as they are completely satisfied with the level of service.

Hotel and restaurant complex "Corona"

This is a modern, very beautiful and even luxurious hotel, which was built in 2002 and consists of three floors. However, only 18 guests can be accommodated here at a time. The fact is that the most famous restaurants of the city are located here. Especially famous restaurant "Bosphorus". All guests of the city are eager to visit it, as the choice of dishes here is truly unimaginable. True professionals work in the kitchen, which means you can count on the highest service. Judging by the reviews of tourists, this hotel is perfect for those who are used to luxury, five-star hotels. And this is despite the fact that the prices here are quite affordable.

Judging by the reviews, the rooms here are great for maximum relaxation. In addition, you can always attend any city events, as the hotel is located very conveniently. The choice is yours: slowly strolling through the park hotels or heading towards the lights of the modern city.
