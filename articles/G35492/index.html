Calm Seas Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): room description, service, reviews

Calm Seas Hotel 3 * is located in Nha Trang, which is 500 m from Cape Hongchong. Travelers call it a great hotel with good helpful staff, quality food and a good location. From Calm Seas Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) to the center 15 minutes by bus. Within walking distance from the hotel there are cafes with good cuisine, as well as the Ponagar Towers, the Northern Market, the Old Thermal Springs and the Rock Garden.





According to tourists, Calm Seas Hotel 3 * is designed for those who like a measured quiet family vacation. Wi-Fi and private parking are available free of charge. Rooms at Calm Seas Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) are equipped with TV (flat screen), some have a seating area. From the windows you can enjoy the panorama of the city or a magnificent view of the sea.

Acquaintance

Calm Seas Hotel 3 * hotel is located in the beautiful picturesque tourist city of Nha Trang (Central Vietnam). Departing to a new country, travelers are always interested in the climatic and economic conditions that they will have to meet there. Resorts in Vietnam in this sense are distinguished by certain specifics.

Resort

Resort Nha Trang (central part of Vietnam) is one of the most popular holiday destinations located on the shores of the South China Sea. There is a bus station, a railway station, an airport (international), a small port (fishing). The population is mainly engaged in fishing and tourism. Holidays in Nha Trang is popular not only among foreign tourists, but also among the indigenous population. The city has Vin Pearl, one of the largest amusement parks in the country.

Beaches

The beaches in Nha Trang stretch for 6 km. The presence of numerous cafes, restaurants, night clubs and health centers contributes to the popularity of the resort among Russian tourists.





According to reviews, the city beaches in Nha Trang are quite clean. They are equipped with changing cabins, sun loungers and umbrellas. Although most of the beaches are located within the city limits, they are mostly calm and deserted. The sea in Nha Trang, as tourists who have been there, says is very beautiful, clean and transparent. In sunny weather, it acquires a bright turquoise color and becomes so alluring that many have a desire to take a run, without undressing, to jump into the beautiful shining water.

Excursions

In Nha Trang, tourists can be offered a large list of excursions, including surfing, diving, snorkeling or organizing sightseeing tours in the nearby islands. In addition to entertainment, travelers can visit the legendary Thap Ba mud baths and many famous attractions.

Nha Trang is a small city, you can take a taxi to visit all the beautiful places in one day and explore the whole city.

Climate

The weather at the seaside resorts in Vietnam is determined by high humidity combined with intense heat.

The tourism season here lasts all year, as the climate in the country is mostly sunny and hot. Sometimes a natural decline can be observed, but the air temperature drops below + 22 ¬∞ C very rarely. The climate in Nha Trang is very humid, pleasant, comfortable. Even on the hottest days, due to their proximity to the sea, vacationers do not feel the drying up Vietnamese heat.

The highest air temperature is + 37 ¬∞ C, and the lowest -10 ¬∞ C. It should be noted that such a temperature is very rare in the country.

The cool weather in Nha Trang is in January and February. These months are not recommended to go to the resort.

In the spring and summer months, the weather at the resort is the hottest and driest.

The wettest weather in Nha Trang is in October. In the country at this time the greatest amount of rain falls. The monsoon winds that prevail in October off the coast of the South China Sea drive clouds onto the mainland, carrying a huge amount of moisture. Weather in Nha Trang in October, as in Da Nang and other resorts, is the peak of the rainy season. The temperature indicators of autumn are not much different from the summer ones - the temperature almost never drops below 28 degrees during the day. If the rainfall in October in Da Nang is simply extreme (from 600 mm), then in Nha Trang it is more comfortable - in a month it rains to 320-330 mm. Those who travel to Vietnam during this period should not forget to grab an umbrella on the road.
