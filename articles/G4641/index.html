Andakira Hotel 4 * (Phuket, Thailand): description, features and reviews

Andakira Hotel 4 * is a wonderful hotel, which is located in one of the most picturesque places in all of Thailand. Namely on Patong Beach. Here, tourists who come to visit can really have a great rest and get a lot of pleasant experiences. And about how this hotel complex is so special is worth telling in more detail.





Briefly about the hotel

Perhaps one of the key features of the Andakira Hotel 4 * complex is the fact that it is in a very convenient (from all points of view) location. Firstly, near the sea, and this is one of the main criteria by which potential tourists choose a hotel. Secondly, not far from a variety of night clubs, restaurants, shops. And finally, not so far from the airport. 45 minutes is enough to get to it. A very convenient location is close enough, so you don‚Äôt have to worry about such a thing as a transfer, but it is also relatively far away (in any case, airplanes will not interfere with a relaxing holiday).

Andakira Hotel 4 * opened in 2009. And for all the time of his work, he has already become a favorite for many guests. And this is understandable, because there is impeccable service, polite staff and comfortable rooms. Yes, and in the vicinity of tropical landscapes and the beach.





About nutrition

Many potential tourists, before booking a room in Andakira Hotel 4 *, try to find out as much as possible details about this place. And one of the most exciting topics is nutrition. Well, heading to this hotel, you don‚Äôt have to worry about food. There are several establishments in the complex where you can have a delicious and original lunch with fresh dishes. All guests are usually sent to the eponymous Andakira restaurant. He works from seven in the morning until 11 in the evening. Guests leave good comments regarding the restaurant of Andakira Hotel 4 *. Reviews say that here you can try any dishes of European and local cuisine. All guests are offered a varied menu, and the treats are always fresh and unusual, which will definitely appeal to those people who like to have an original dinner.

There is also a lobby bar, open from eight in the morning until 20:00, as well as a regular pool bar. It is open at the same time period. Guests claim that in these establishments you can try refreshing soft drinks and quality alcoholic cocktails.





Most Popular Accommodation

Andakira Hotel 4 * (Phuket) offers several options for booking. Well, it‚Äôs worth starting with a story about the most popular. And this is an improved double room. It is usually offered either with one very wide bed, or with two ordinary ones. The price of a reservation for two weeks with breakfast included is approximately 50,000 rubles for two! Which is a very low cost for Thailand. True, if a person will reserve a room with the ability to cancel the reservation, it will be much more expensive - about 85,000 rubles. with the same conditions. So it‚Äôs better to think about saving in advance.

Guests booking such rooms assure you that the conditions are excellent. It does not compare with some luxury rooms in 5-star hotels in other resort countries. The furniture is modern, upholstered, comfortable. In the form of a greeting - towels laid out in the form of swans on the bed and rose petals. Private balcony with scenic views of the tropical garden. Also a telephone, cable TV with a DVD player, air conditioning, a work desk - there really are all amenities inside! And a wardrobe with a wardrobe, and a bathroom, and an electric kettle. Even nice touches like slippers, a hairdryer, and a bathrobe are also provided. In general, a chic room at an affordable price.

Deluxe and Grand Deluxe

Another frequently booked type of apartment. A much larger area (not more than 30 square meters, but almost 50) and a more elegant room design. People staying in such apartments say that this is an option for true aesthetes. It will cost about 70 thousand rubles for two (14 nights) with breakfast included in the price. Decent conditions: a spacious bed, a bathroom, a toilet, a seating area, a minibar, a good view of the city. And of course, everything is the same as in the number described above.

Grand deluxe apartments in Andakira Hotel 4 * (Phuket) are more expensive. About 80 thousand rubles for 14 nights (without breakfast) for two. The conditions are the same as in deluxe. Only the room is more spacious (53 sq. M), and the beds are wider. And the bathroom has a shower besides the bathtub.





Special conditions

Andakira Hotel 4 * (Phuket, Patong) also offers rooms with special conditions for booking. There are, for example, separate deluxe apartments, booked by which guests get access to the pool. The cost of such a room is about 87,000 rubles (with breakfast, for two, for 14 days). These apartments are located on the fourth floor of the complex, have their own terrace, from where there is direct access to the swimming-sports pool. But in such apartments only adults can stay, without children.

And finally, the last category is a family suite. Designed for four adults. Such a room will cost about 130-150 thousand rubles for all (again, 14 days of reservation and with breakfast included in the price). Apartments are similar, rather, to apartments, since they have two rooms. Each has one or two wide beds. And by the way, the rooms in this category have two bathrooms, not one.

Fun and relaxation

In Andakira Hotel (Patong) 4 * you can still have some fun. Outdoors, there is an outdoor swimming pool, a beautiful tropical garden and a sun terrace. There is also a SPA and a jacuzzi - for lovers to relax in body and soul. There is also a billiard room and massage room. And of course, your own beach. In addition to being a few dozen meters from the hotel, free sun loungers with umbrellas are also offered there.





Service and maintenance

All the people who stayed at this hotel were satisfied with the service. They claim that this is the place where guests are taken care of. There is everything you need. Delivery of food or drinks to the apartments, the possibility of renting a car, arranging a transfer (free of charge, by the way), a tour desk, a currency exchange office, a luggage storage room, even dry cleaning with laundry and babysitting services (which especially pleased people who flew to rest with children ) is also there. The hotel even has two rooms - for banquets and conferences. There is also a business center. In general, any services that may be needed are provided here.

By the way, if a person wishes, he can get a non-smoking room. There are also apartments for people with disabilities.





Booking Nuances

And finally, a few words about the booking process. Check-in is traditionally made from 14:00, check-out is before noon. But if a person needs to arrive early or leave later, then the administration can provide this (perhaps for a small surcharge). The hotel is allowed to come with children, but then not all rooms are suitable for reservations. If the child is not 12 years old, then he is allowed to stay in the apartment for free. If the baby is two years old or less, then a cot will be put in the room, and the stay will also be free of charge. But only about this the administration needs to be notified in advance so that the crib can be prepared.

Upon arrival, you must provide your passport and credit card (the one for which the reservation was made). And yes, one more thing. The reviews recommend taking care of the issue of room reservation at least one and a half to two months before the planned arrival, because the hotel is popular, and there may simply not be any free apartments. The season is all year round, so you can easily be late with your reservation.
