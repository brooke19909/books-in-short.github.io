Green gradient in room design

There is a strong belief that the green color calms the human nervous system, allows you to relax and correct your mood. This fact can be confirmed by any person spending time in nature. Being in a park, outside the city, we feel emotional discharge, relax, our sleep after an evening walk becomes stronger and calmer. This is partly due to the fact that we perceive the abundance of greenery as a signal that we can relax. This article discusses the features of the green gradient.

Color of calm

The design of the office or children's room in green tones will allow you not only to remain calm and peaceful, but also to perform daily tasks fruitfully. However, so that the green color of the walls or ceiling does not become an irritant (as you know, any saturated color quickly starts to bother, and that is why you want to start repairing again) it is worth using a green gradient. The shade of greenery, flowers and grass raises the mood and contributes to the formation of a positive attitude. Also, it‚Äôs not only pleasant, but also useful to add living vegetation to the air to clean the air we breathe. A couple of indoor plants - and you will not need air humidifiers!

Using transitions

The green gradient background is a great design solution for your room. Use several tones of green paint, smoothly transitioning from more saturated to light, as if the color fades in the sun. Such a transition will create an atmosphere of dynamics, add to your room more air and free space.

You can dilute the gradient background with the help of small drawings, applications or even stucco moldings. All this is sold in designer and ordinary construction stores, there may be a variety of colors, but they mainly choose those tones that will be in harmony with the color of the furniture and floor.
