Where to go in May: options and directions

Rest is a must. Working moments are exhausting, domestic everyday life accumulates fatigue in us, and if you add any other life problems here, it generally turns out to be hard. And no matter what month you have a vacation, you can always find a place for good relaxation at any time of the year.

Many traditionally try to take a vacation in May. In the summer, not all employers can give their employee a break, and in May it is already warm, you can go to the cottage, or you can fly back to rest. Let's deal with the question of where to go in May.

Our compatriots, living not in the hottest conditions, are most often looking for warm countries for recreation. Therefore, the question of where to go to the sea in May is especially important.

Israel

In May, the weather is optimal. The main resorts of the country are Tel Aviv and Eilat. These resorts may be the right answer to the question of where to go with your child in May. Israel is an ideal country for families with children.

In May, the air temperature reaches + 30 ¬∞ C, the water temperature is slightly lower: +26 ¬∞ C. The weather is sunny all May. The beaches of Israel are clean, well equipped and completely safe for bathing children, the water is clear.

On the territory of the beaches there are cafes, various restaurants, as well as all the necessary equipment for relaxation (umbrellas, deck chairs), here you can rent a yacht, water skiing and more. In general, in Israel, rest is very good and comfortable.

At the disposal of tourists are modern hotels and apartments in the private sector. In Israel, you can relax in a very interesting and varied way, the country has an interesting rich history and gorgeous natural landscapes.

Jordan

Another correct answer to the question of where to go to the sea in May. The country is a popular destination in May. This month the air temperature warms up to +31 ¬∞ C, and the water temperature reaches +27 ¬∞ C. Jordan's most popular resort is Aqaba. If the purpose of your trip is exclusively a beach holiday, then Aqaba is waiting for you.

The beaches of the resort are numerous and diverse, there are sandy and pebble beaches. The coastal area is clean and fully equipped. Most often, beaches are assigned to hotels or are their property.

In Aqaba, scuba diving is well developed, in the coastal waters of these places are very beautiful coral reefs, as well as underwater flora and fauna. Jordanians are very hospitable people, they are especially warm towards Russians.

UAE

Are you still thinking where to go in May? Drop doubts and fly to the UAE. Here you can relax with the child. Shopping, excursions, attractions - all here are world-class! In local Abu Dhabi, the air temperature in May reaches an incredible +38 ¬∞ C, and the water warms up to + 29 ¬∞ C, in Dubai the situation is almost the same.

The country is famous for the purest white sand, azure calm sea and sultry sun. If you are just bored lying on the beach, you can go scuba diving or go out into the open water on a modern yacht. Extreme relaxation is also presented here.

Is it necessary to clarify that vacation in the UAE is a pleasure for wealthy people? But rest corresponds to the money spent on it. It will be awesome.

Maldives

This is a romantic answer to the question of where you can go in May. I would like to hear it from my soulmate when you are planning your vacation. A trip to these, without exaggeration, paradise islands will allow you to spend one of the best beach vacations in your life.

In the Maldives, an incredible and rich underwater world that you can know if you like diving. The islands also have rich exotic nature, fresh sea air and fascinating natural landscapes. The Maldives has public beaches and private ones. On the private beach you can enjoy all the pleasures of the resort without strangers.

The air temperature in May in the Maldives rises to +31 ¬∞ C, water about +28 ¬∞ C. For fans of outdoor activities, in addition to the aforementioned diving, there is a rental of ATVs, walks in the tropics and more. Local cuisine is very interesting, it belongs to the category of exotic.

Thailand

Where to go on a beach vacation in May? Of course, to Thailand! It's hot in May (air temperature about +33 ¬∞ C, water temperature +28 ¬∞ C). The country has many beaches, good infrastructure, many options for excursions. Spicy lovers will enjoy the local cuisine. Although you can find something classic.

On the beach you can rent water skis, a yacht and ride a banana. If the trip is planned with children, then consider the islands of Phuket or Pattaya. The most affordable in terms of finance is Phuket, while the service can be safely called upscale.

If you are traveling without children and want to take the maximum out of your trip, then you are in Samui - this is a resort that is full of variety, as well as a noisy nightlife. Here you will find many excursions, get acquainted with the sights of the country and beautiful natural landscapes.

Be that as it may, Thailand has everything for families with children, but in reality few people take very young children there.

Greece

The least exotic option for a Russian when thinking about where to go on vacation in May. The flight is relatively short-lived. Here you can relax with children. The prices are reasonable. The trip itself will be informative and with a minimum of unusual exoticism, which sometimes goes too much.

The warm weather of Greece allows you to enjoy your vacation here from the very beginning of May. The brightest and hottest resorts in the country are Athens and Rhodes. The weather in May is optimal. Air warmed up to +25 ¬∞ C, water to +22 ¬∞ C. The beaches are clean, equipped, with developed service. There are paid and free beaches.

The country has an interesting history, the child will be cognitively acquainted with it. For relaxation without children there are great bars and nightclubs. Rhodes is famous for its first-class beaches, stunning natural landscapes and incredibly vibrant nightlife. The island is popular for youth recreation.

Traditionally, you need to remember the very saying that says that Greece is a country where there is absolutely everything. For vacationers for sure!

Cuba

Looking for where to go in May without a visa? Cuba is waiting for you! To visit Liberty Island you only need a passport and a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation. In May, in Cuba, the air warms up to +31 ¬∞ C, water + 28 ¬∞ C. On the island you will find endless sandy beaches with full service.

For lovers of water fun, Cuba offers windsurfing, diving, snorkeling, as well as various yacht trips, water skiing, exotic sea fishing (your catch will be prepared right before your eyes). The most popular destinations are Havana, Santiaga de Cuba, Cayo Coco.

Havana is always full of entertainment, carnivals, clubs and more. This is one hundred percent the most fun resort of the island. But, in addition to all the fun, there are plenty of excellent beaches, colorful local cuisine and interesting sights.

It is interesting for those who found the USSR to visit Cuba, some areas in the cities of Cuba are like pictures from those old times.

In May, the beaches of Tunisia are not too full, this is the best time to relax here with the children. Resorts of the country are developed, service is at a sufficient level. Tunis is a popular destination among Russian tourists. This means that service in Russian is a very common case.
