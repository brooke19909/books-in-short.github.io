Hotel Azia Resort & Spa 5 * (Cyprus, Paphos): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Five-star hotels have always been of particular interest to tourists. After all, 5 * is an upscale service, stylish and comfortable rooms, excellent cuisine. In fact, the view is correct. And vivid proof of this is the Azia Resort & Spa 5 * hotel (Paphos).





Location

The hotel complex is located in Cyprus. In the city of Paphos, to be precise. It is located in the southwestern part of the island. It is this settlement that is considered the place in which Aphrodite was born. But, of course, the fact that Paphos is the very first city that was the first in Cyprus to be protected by UNESCO (this was in 1980) is much more interesting.

It was here, 50 meters from the sandy beach, the Azia Resort & Spa 5 * hotel was located. Paphos is an interesting city, there are many attractions. At 4 kilometers from the hotel are the tombs of kings, a little further away is the metropolis of the city. The municipality of Paphos was also located there. You can also get to St. Paul‚Äôs column, water park, bird and animal park from here. There is a bus stop near the hotel, and the city fare costs 5 euros.

Leisure

In Azia Resort & Spa 5 * (Paphos) there is a lot of entertainment. There is a seasonal outdoor pool, sun terrace, beautiful garden and barbecue. Of course, the main entertainment here is the beach. But, in addition to this, the hotel has many other ways to brighten up leisure.

For example, the hotel has a SPA-salon, which can be understood by looking at the name of the complex. There you can devote time to hydrotherapy, jacuzzi, massage, stone therapy and body care. Beauty and wellness treatments are also available. You can also take a steam bath in the sauna or Turkish bath. And then - visit the beauty salon.

There is also a bicycle rental, a library, darts, a billiard room, a golf course, a tennis court and a fitness center. By the way, you can also play volleyball, go diving, shooting, tennis, sailing, surfing or rent a boat / canoe. In general, there is a lesson for every taste. There is even a mini club for children, a playground and a shallow separate pool.





What is in the hotel

In Azia Resort & Spa 5 * (Cyprus, Paphos) there are services of a very different nature. From free Wi-Fi to private parking.

Guests can take advantage of babysitting, concierge, shuttle service, tour desk and ticket service. There is a currency exchange, 24-hour reception, a luggage storage room. There is also dry cleaning, laundry and ironing, where guests can clean their shoes. There is also a large conference room with a business center operating under it (which still has an office with a photocopy and fax machine).

There is a shared lounge, shops on site, a souvenir stall, a car rental service, and even your own small church. If necessary, guests will be provided with a non-smoking room or facilities for people with disabilities. And also, as in any other 5-star hotel, they provide VIP services.

By the way, the hotel has multilingual staff. The hotel staff speaks Greek, German, English, Spanish, French, Romanian and, of course, Russian. This level of training cannot but rejoice.

Restaurants

Hotel Azia Resort & Spa 5 * (Paphos, Cyprus) has several excellent restaurants. The main one is known as Katoi. It serves vegetarian and international dishes, and serves a buffet. Open facility for breakfast and lunch.

Dinner can be enjoyed at Akamas Restaurant. If you want Asian, modern or gluten-free cuisine, you can head to Oku Asian. Local, Greek and Cypriot dishes are served at Cipro Restaurant.

Very popular institution Assari Shibuya. If you want to go there for dinner, it is better to reserve a table in advance. The same goes for Flambe Night and Cypriot Meze restaurants. And the last institution of all that the hotel has is the Amphitheater, where guests are served not by the menu, but by the buffet system.
