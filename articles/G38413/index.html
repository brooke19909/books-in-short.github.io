What is amigurumi





Do not be afraid of such a name when you hear it for the first time, since the complexity of pronunciation and memorization of a word is completely not justified in practice. So, amigurumi is the Japanese art of crocheting different toys. This can be almost imperceptible animals, as well as large-scale pillows, caps and other elements containing many details.

Since this is an eastern current, it is accompanied by very cute faces, colors and shapes of toys. In addition, the silhouette of amigurumi is its main feature, thanks to which it is so recognizable around the world. Round shapes, slightly bulky heads, a huge number of tiny details: all this emphasizes the individuality of this style of knitting. These will be our crocheted amigurumi dogs , the schemes and description of which we will consider in detail right now.

Crochet dogs: schemes and description of the process





It's time to start making toys. In order to make everything clear and no questions arise, we will write down all the steps as detailed as possible. This will not only simplify the process of perceiving information, but also speed up the process of work.

  • To begin with, we will tie the upper paws: for this, we will make an amigurumi ring (KA) and knit 6 single crochet columns (RLS) into it with the color of the future toy dog. Tighten the ring a bit to give it a neat shape. Now, in a new row, in each single crochet (RLS), we make two of the same loops: total in a row will be 12 finished loops.
  • Now we make an increase every second single crochet (RLS) to get 18 loops in a row. Thus, we gradually increase the handle, then knit two columns (RLS) in every third element. If you count, then at the end you should get 24 loops.
  • Now we change the color of the thread, as the blouse sleeve begins. You choose its color yourself, and we will take lime. We knit the first row with a thread of a new color, and then make a decrease (UB). To do this, you need to knit each 3 and 4 loops together. As a result, you will have 18 single crochets (RLS).
  • Now, from the seventh to the twenty-fourth rows, we simply knit all the single crochet (RLS) columns. As a result, we get a long "pipe" of green color. When all the rows are ready, we fill the foot, leaving a little empty space, fold the pipe hole in half and carefully knit the loops together, getting the finished part. We also make the second foot.
  • We turn to the manufacture of hind legs: we knit a heel of dark yarn, gaining a chain of 10 air loops (VP). Now, starting from the second loop, we knit 8 single crochet (RLS), in the 9th loop we make an increase (PR) of five columns and again 8 single crochet (RLS). We make the 1st column (RLS) in a new row, in the 2nd we make an increase (PR), we knit another 6 loops, in the next two columns we make the 1st increase (PR), and then we create 2 more columns without a crochet ( SBZ).
  • We knit the next half in the mirror order, as we knitted the first part, getting 28 loops at the end of the row. In the new row we make 10 single crochet (SBZ), in the next 9 loops we do the 1st increase, and then again 10 loops. Total 38 columns that complete the heel.
  • Now we take a crochet thread of the color of the hair of our dog, we gradually get acquainted with the schemes and description of which, and we knit all 38 columns. In a new row, we knit 10 loops, and the next 12 we make decreases (UB), completing the row again with 10 columns. Then we knit 12 loops, 5 columns of decrease and again 12 loops. We start the row with 8 single crochet, make 6 columns of decrease, turning them into 3 columns, and end the row, 9 loops. In total, 23 columns are obtained.




We change the thread to a dark color depicting pants, we knit the first 23 single crochet (RLS). We knit in this way from the 10th to the 23rd rows. At the first leg we cut the thread, carefully tucking it into the canvas, and at the second - leave it untouched. Do not forget to put the filler.

Soft tummy

Now that the legs and handles are ready, we proceed to the manufacture of the body. It was not for nothing that we left the thread on one of the lower paws, since it is from it that we will continue to knit the body of the toy.

  1. In the 23rd row, we knit all the loops on the leg, then we collect 10 air loops and again 23 columns near the second leg. The resulting 56 loops we knit the next 3 rows. And in the 4th row we decrease, getting 50 SC Thus we knit 4 rows.
  2. In the 5th row, we reduce the total number of loops by 7 parts, change the thread to the green color of the sweater and knit 43 loops of 3 rows. Again we decrease by 6 loops, but we already do 11 rows of 36 columns, again we decrease by 6 loops and we knit 2 rows. In the next row, we decrease another 6 loops, make one row and leave the thread for further fastening.
  3. We knit the head according to the scheme below, having typed 60 loops, make 9 rows, and then we go down, filling it with a synthetic winterizer in advance.

Mustache, Bows and Tail

We knit the ears and muzzle according to the same pattern, but of different colors, making an amigurumi ring, picking up columns in it and knitting rows. In the end, you should get a "cup" from which we will make the ears, folded in half, and knit the muzzle, fill the blank with synthetic winterizer.
