Crochet summer blouse - stylish and comfortable

The anthem of femininity, charm and beauty is an exquisite knitted summer blouse. Crocheted such products fit very quickly and do not require, as it might seem at first glance, vast experience and the highest level of skill. But this is only if some crochet techniques are used that even beginners can afford.



First, get acquainted with the loin technique. It is based on a mesh that is crocheted. Use double crochet stitches. Grid cells are empty or with loops loosened. This alternation of empty and filled cells creates an original, refined and feminine accentuating lace pattern. A crocheted summer blouse made in this technique looks very impressive and original.

Previously, filet knitting was widely used in the manufacture of napkins and tablecloths, but it should be noted that women's blouses with its use look simply amazing. This technique is not very complicated, but requires attention and perseverance.

For best results, use thin threads and the thinnest pointed head hook.



Fashionable crochet blouses made using the Irish lace technique are always original. The basis of this technique is the manufacture of individual elements, which are called motifs, connected into a single product. Usually these are plant ornaments consisting of leaves and flowers. They can be knitted from yarn of the same color, or use different shades.

Which option you will choose is not so important. In any case, you get an amazing result. This technique attracts beginners to knitting. And, probably, this is not surprising - to link several separate parts is much easier than the whole composition. But the fact is that often the connection of parts causes certain difficulties. Traditionally, motifs are connected using a grid of any structure you choose.

But if this is too complicated for you, then first tie the grid, and then attach the finished elements to it.



A summer blouse crocheted on a fork can be made even by a beginner knitter, although this technique has not yet been widely used. An important advantage of this technique is the speed of manufacture of the product. The basis of this method lies in the manufacture of original ribbons that can be combined into elements or into finished products. Even if you are not yet an experienced crochet master, and you come across interesting patterns of products connected on a fork, take it easy, you will succeed.

The original and fashionable summer blouse, crocheted or knitted, is always modern and relevant. It will be appropriate at work, and on a romantic date, and on vacation by the sea.

Crochet - a very exciting activity. Once you have tied some small thing, you can no longer release the hook from your hands. And do not do this! Your original, fashionable products will delight not only your relatives and friends.

The main thing is that you yourself will receive incredible aesthetic pleasure.
