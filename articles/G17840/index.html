This hotel has 391 comfortable rooms for guests. Guests can stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • If you do not want to spend a lot of money on a hotel, you can stay in economy rooms. The compact room has two twin beds, bedside tables, dressing table. There is a clothes hanger in the hallway. This room category does not have a balcony.
  • Standard rooms can accommodate one, two and three guests. This room can accommodate large or twin beds. There is also a sitting area with upholstered furniture. The balcony overlooks the pool and palm garden.
