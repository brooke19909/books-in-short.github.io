El Mouradi Palace 5 * (Tunisia, Sousse): description of the hotel, services, reviews

Tunisia is not the most popular tourist country, but it may well become an alternative to bored Egypt. The state is also located on the Mediterranean coast, has a warm climate, good beaches and interesting excursion programs. In addition, in Tunisia, tourists can try the famous thalassotherapy (healing mud wraps). If you decide to relax in this country, then it is worth choosing a hotel with quality service. One of them is the tourist complex El Mouradi Palace 5 * (Sousse). But is it worth it to stay in it?

Hotel location

The tourist complex is located near Sousse - the most popular youth resort in Tunisia. In addition to the five-star El Muradi Palace, there are many cheap hotels at the lowest prices. The city is filled with nightclubs, bars, restaurants, shops, golf courses and pleasure boats that provide guests with an active holiday. History buffs will be able to visit a huge number of excursions to the ancient monuments of the city.

You can get to the El Mouradi Palace 5 * hotel (Tunisia) from Monastir Airport, which is 20 km from Sousse. Other airports are located a little further : Enfid (30 km) and Tunisia (140 km). You can get by taxi, intercity bus or tourist train that runs to the resort. The hotel itself is located about 10 km from the city center. Unlike hotels located in the city, here you can not hear the noise from discos and nightclubs, and the sea and coast are much cleaner. The complex's private beach is 300 meters away. On average, the road to it takes 5-10 minutes on foot. Not far from the hotel is the most famous golf club in Tunisia.

general information

The construction of the El Muradi Palace Hotel was completed in 1995, and it began to receive its first tourists in 1996. It is located on a vast territory of 135 thousand square meters. m. Tourists are offered to settle in a huge four-story building, built in the Arabic style. Over 500 rooms of various capacities have been prepared for them. The hotel itself is designed to accommodate various types of tourists. Families with children, elderly people, youth companies, and businessmen can have a great rest here. It will be a good option for travelers with disabilities, because the hotel is considered a major health center.





You can come to rest here throughout the year, but the best time for a vacation will be the summer and autumn months. In winter, the temperature is also moderately warm, but the water in the sea is quite cold. September and October are considered the velvet season, because the strongest heat is declining, and the number of vacationers is significantly reduced. The hotel El Mouradi Palace 5 * (Sousse) stay mainly French and other Europeans. Russians are also represented in large numbers here. Staff can communicate in Russian, but knowledge of English is also welcome. Last update of numbers occurred in 2013.

Number of rooms

For its guests, the complex offers 515 rooms equipped with modern furniture and first-class amenities. Each of them has its own small balcony, and the rooms on the lower floors have a terrace. Soft carpeted floors are on the floor of the apartment, so that tourists can move around the room without shoes. The repair is designed in a classic minimalist style. Guests of the El Mouradi Palace 5 * hotel (Tunisia) receive a standard set of furniture, which includes a bed, a desk, a chair, a mirror, bedside tables, several lamps. There is a dining set on the balcony. Rooms are cleaned daily while there are no tourists. Every few days, linen and towels are changed.





For guests of the complex there are several types of rooms:

  • Standard room is 24 square meters. m overlooking the park, pool or sea. Guests can choose from apartments with either two single beds or one double.
  • Junior Suite measuring 29 square meters. m. Spacious room with a large double bed. It offers a view of the sea.
  • Superior room with a single bed available on request. Its area is 24 square meters. m, and the balcony and windows partially overlook the sea or pool.

Room Amenities

For a good rest, good repair and modern furniture is not enough. Therefore, the El Mouradi Palace 5 * hotel offers superior rooms. Hotel guests can take advantage of many amenities, such as:

  • A modern TV with the ability to view satellite as well as Russian (First) TV channels.
  • Central air conditioning controlled by staff. But hotel guests can only use it between June and mid-September.
  • Free minibar filled with mineral water. To order other drinks, including alcoholic drinks, you will have to pay an additional amount.
  • A small safe in which you can store money, documents, phones and other valuables for free.
  • Telephone. Tourists can call the hotel administrator for free at the reception, and international and other calls are not included in the price.
  • Bathroom equipped with bath or shower. There is also a washbasin with a large mirror, a hairdryer, toiletries and cosmetics. And the complex does not provide a bathrobe and slippers.

Dining at the hotel

The complex serves national and European cuisine. Several options of food are organized for tourists, so that all guests can choose the most comfortable type of service. We list the main ones:

  • Half board. The price includes breakfast and dinner, free tea, drinks with meals.
  • Full board. Includes a full breakfast, lunch and dinner with a choice of dishes on the buffet of the restaurant of the hotel El Mouradi Palace 5 *.
  • All Inclusive (Ultra). This includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as afternoon tea and an early breakfast. A variety of drinks, including alcoholic ones, are offered free of charge for tourists. Guests can try for free a huge selection of desserts, ice cream, fruits, various snacks. Offers 2 free admission to an international restaurant.




Meals are held according to a strict schedule, according to which tourists can eat around the clock. So, an early breakfast starts at 3 a.m. Accordingly, a late dinner ends at the same time.

Tourism complex infrastructure

A prerequisite for a quality holiday is to provide tourists with all the necessary amenities. Hotel El Mouradi Palace 5 * has a developed network of infrastructure facilities that can be visited by all tourists living here. The main ones are:

  • free parking; car and bicycle rental;
  • a business center equipped with a conference room that seats 750 people, and a meeting room;
  • round-the-clock services of a paid doctor;
  • currency exchange;
  • facilities for people with disabilities;
  • 4 modern elevators;
  • shop, souvenir shop;
  • Internet room
  • laundry;
  • beauty salon and hairdresser.




Entertainment offered by the hotel

Tunis (Sousse) offers a huge selection of diverse entertainment for travelers. Hotel "El Muradi Palace" is no exception. For guests living here, there are many classes for a fun pastime for all categories of tourists. The most popular of them are:

  • Several pools, including indoor, which works in bad weather, as well as in winter. El Mouradi Palace 5 * complex (Tunisia) has a seawater pool. Sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas are provided free of charge for guests. But towels can only be obtained after making a small deposit (10 dinars).
  • Paid water activities on the beach. Guests can enjoy windsurfing, waterskiing, catamaran, sailing boat or canoeing. You can also rent a jet ski. There are free surfing lessons at sea.
  • Beauty and wellness services are offered by the spa. Tourists can order a tonic massage, visit the Turkish bath, sauna, use the jacuzzi for free. You should definitely try healing mud wraps or thalassotherapy.
  • Free gym.
  • Day and evening animation, discos.
  • Own casino, billiards and darts halls.
  • Sports games: beach volleyball, golf, classic and table tennis, archery.

Children's holiday

Children at the hotel are considered to be guests aged 0 to 12 years. Free accommodation at the El Mouradi Palace 5 * hotel (Port El Kantaoui) is provided for infants up to two years old. In restaurants there is the opportunity to use high chairs. The territory has its own outdoor children's pool with a depth of 0.5 meters and an indoor heated pool. There is an entertainment center for children under 12 years old. It is possible to hire a babysitter.





Positive feedback from tourists

Having described all the listed amenities that the complex has, it is worth mentioning the opinions of guests of the El Mouradi Palace 5 * hotel. Reviews about him are left different, but first we list the positive:

  • a large and comfortable territory of the complex with a huge number of flowering shrubs and trees, a beautiful road to the beach;
  • clean, sparsely populated white sand beach;
  • spacious bright room, equipped with a separate bathroom, clean and high-quality linens;
  • good service in the building of the El Mouradi Palace 5 * hotel (Tunisia) and in restaurants and bars;
  • a large selection of drinks in bars, delicious lunches and dinners;
  • many inexpensive and affordable entertainment.




Negative reviews of tourists

The tourist complex El Mouradi Palace 5 * reviews are not only positive. Some guests were not satisfied with their stay here. They note the following disadvantages:

  • in some rooms, the carpet needs serious cleaning, it has large dirty spots;
  • the rooms are poorly cleaned, food may be left unfinished by the former guests in the refrigerator, and good service can be obtained only with a tip;
  • garbage and algae are constantly floating in the sea, which are not always cleaned immediately;
  • the staff does not understand well in Russian and in English, sometimes they do not provide an iron, hairdryer and other necessary things;
  • monotonous animation and loud music every night.

Draw conclusions

The hotel El Mouradi Palace 5 * leaves a double impression. The description of the hotel offers tourists a modern quality vacation, but when they come here, travelers discover that the price does not match the quality. The complex is significantly short of the title of a five-star hotel, having rather significant shortcomings.

If you are not confused by the above disadvantages of the complex, then you can safely go on vacation to El Mouradi Palace 5 *. Tours are offered by many travel companies. The cost of rest will primarily depend on the length of stay and food options. It is safe to say that the hotel is suitable for couples and youth companies. And families with small children and older people here may be too noisy.
