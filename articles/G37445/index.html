Avrora Spa Hotel (Moscow region): hotel description, services, reviews

In an amazing location, surrounded by beautiful nature, Avrora Spa Hotel is located . This is ideal for a relaxing countryside holiday in a quiet and cozy atmosphere.





A bit about the hotel

The main advantage of the Avrora Spa Hotel is the absolute silence and atmosphere of tranquility, donated by the surrounding nature. The place for the construction of the hotel was not chosen by chance. In the books of the healers dated 1750, the incredible energy power inherent in this area was described. Not only ordinary people came here, but also the clergy in order to purify thoughts and strengthen the spirit. It is no accident that a huge number of churches were built in the vicinity.

It is worth noting that the work of Avrora Spa Hotel is based on the concept of environmental responsibility. In the construction were used only natural materials that are safe for human health. As for the interior decoration, it corresponds to the classical style. The interior shade was given a shade of luxury thanks to the elegant interior elements.





Aurora SPA Hotel: Address

The hotel is located at: Stepankovo ​​village, st. Christmas, 11. To get to the suburbs (north), you need to go to Dmitrov highway. You will have to drive 10 km to Eremino. At the intersection, you need to turn, according to the sign, to Stepankovo ​​and drive for about 10 minutes.

If you get by public transport, first you need to get to the Altufevo metro station. From here to Novoseltsevo can be reached by bus number 401, and then by taxi. From the railway station Lobnya to Novoseltsevo, bus number 36 runs, and then also by taxi.





Accommodation

Comfortable surroundings and luxurious interiors will delight guests at the Avrora Spa Hotel. The following options are available for placement:

  • Standard rooms are located on the ground floor of the cottages. It can accommodate 2 guests. Extra beds are not available (cot is an exception).
  • On the ground floor of each cottage are junior suites. Accommodation is made on a double bed. Additional accommodation for 1 guest or 2 children is also allowed. The room has a seating area with soft armchairs and a coffee table.
  • The superior junior suite is characterized by a spacious area, as well as an elegant design, which is dominated by natural materials. Guests can stay in large or twin beds. The apartments are equipped with a spacious bathroom with a hydromassage bath.
  • The suite covers an area of ​​30 square meters. m. In this space can accommodate 2 main guests, as well as an additional guest and 1 child. The room is equipped with a king size bed, as well as upholstered furniture. The room has its own spacious terrace, where there is a place not only for gatherings over a cup of coffee, but also for morning exercises. Also in the room provides a large selection of literature.
  • The bungalow has a private street entrance and a large furnished terrace. At the entrance, guests enter the spacious entrance hall. The living room is a combination of bedroom, living room and kitchen.
  • Superior Suite 70 sqm m is a huge studio, decorated using natural materials. The interior is decorated in light colors and features wooden beams. The room includes a bedroom, living room, and dining room. There is a spacious terrace furnished with a rocking chair.
  • The apartment covers an area of ​​70 square meters. m. They consist of two rooms. The first is a studio that combines a living room and a kitchen. Behind the sliding door is a bedroom with a large bed and a built-in wardrobe.

Positive reviews

Residents of a noisy metropolis seek a quiet vacation at the Aurora SPA Hotel. Reviews note the following advantages of this institution:

  • beautiful architecture of residential buildings;
  • large car parking with convenient access;
  • the hotel area is well guarded;
  • polite reception staff;
  • both rooms and grounds are kept in perfect cleanliness;
  • convenient location, which makes it possible to get not only by car, but also by public transport;
  • a good spa with a wide range of services;
  • The hotel is designed mainly using natural materials;
  • the hotel is surrounded by beautiful nature;
  • modern playground for children;
  • good wifi signal;
  • quiet and peaceful atmosphere contributes to a good rest;
  • new orthopedic furniture in the rooms;
  • quite tasty and high-quality food that will appeal not only to adults, but also to children;
  • a lot of greenery and flowers in the territory;
  • many opportunities for lovers of an active lifestyle;
  • for guests staying in standard rooms and junior suites, a well-equipped shared kitchen is provided;
  • A wide range of services is included in the price.




Negative reviews

There are a number of disadvantages in this hotel. Reviews note such negative points:

  • in the hallway of the room there are no hangers for outerwear;
  • the hotel has almost no entertainment;
  • inflated prices for accommodation and other services;
  • the walls in the rooms are quite thin, and therefore there is a strong audibility between the rooms;
  • use of the pool is available only until 13:00;
  • low-quality instant coffee is served for breakfast;
  • new cottages are under construction on the territory, which not only spoils the appearance, but also causes some inconvenience;
  • small pool.

If you want to take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city, it is best to go to the suburbs, which is famous for its beautiful nature. This hotel will provide comfortable living conditions and a host of services for a good rest.
