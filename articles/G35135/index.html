Grandeur Hotel 4 * (Al-Barsha, United Arab Emirates): photos and descriptions, hotel infrastructure, service, traveler reviews

Want to spend an unforgettable vacation in the UAE? Then you should go to Dubai. And, more precisely, in its new western region called Al Barsha. It was built recently, but there are already many hotel complexes and hotels. Now we will talk about one of them. Namely, about the Grandeur Hotel 4 *.

Location

Al Barsha is a very profitable area. From it, the airport can be reached in 25-30 minutes by car. In addition, the famous Mall of Emirates, which is the hallmark of Dubai, is located here. It is the largest shopping center in the world, including a ski indoor resort called Ski Dubai.

The Grandeur Hotel is just 1 kilometer from such a popular destination. Accordingly, there are many other attractions in these surroundings.

Also, from here in half an hour you can get to the free economic zone of Media City. It is also important to note that the hotel provides a free shuttle service to the sandy Kite Beach and Outlet Mall.

Facilities and service

The Grandeur Hotel has everything that a hotel of this level should have. Here is a list of services and amenities offered:

  • Free high speed wifi. Available both on site and in rooms.
  • Free private secured parking.
  • Transfer from the airport and back.
  • 24-hour front desk.
  • Luggage storage.
  • Currency exchange point.
  • Excursion bureau.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning. Shoe shine services are available.
  • Business center, office with photocopy and fax.
  • Individual safes for guests.
  • Beauty saloon.
  • Delivery of drinks, food and newspapers in the room.

It is important to make a reservation that Grandeur Hotel has non-smoking apartments, as well as for people with disabilities.

If guests are interested in something, they can simply ask the staff for help. The administrators working here are fluent in four languages ‚Äã‚Äã- English, Arabic, Hindi and Russian.

Leisure

The vast majority of people come to Grandeur Hotel in order to enjoy unforgettable shopping in the largest shopping malls in Dubai, and also for the sake of kitesurfing, actively practiced on the nearest beach.

However, many people, choosing a hotel for their future vacations, pay attention to its leisure infrastructure. It is important for them to have something to occupy themselves with, even in a hotel. So, here's what you have for entertainment at the Grandeur Hotel Dubai:

  • Rooftop swimming pool.
  • Sun terrace
  • A salon in which they make an amazing massage of everything: hands, head, body, legs, neck, back, as well as for couples who decide to have a joint relaxation.
  • Health Center.
  • Sauna.
  • Jacuzzi.
  • Billiard room.
  • Gym.

Although this is a city hotel, it has a well-developed leisure infrastructure, even a swimming pool. So everyone will find a way with which he will be able to brighten up a couple of free hours.

Nutrition

Of course, the Grandeur Hotel (Al Barsha, Dubai) has its own restaurant. This establishment is called D'Fusion, and, in addition to European treats, guests are offered unique Indian dishes developed by a local chef.

This restaurant also offers a wide range of soft and soft drinks. In the evening, entertainment shows are held here, and live music is constantly heard.

Other dining options include Cafe La Rez and Spira Sports Bar.

What about the assortment? Guests relaxing at this hotel assure that no one will remain hungry here.

Here, for example, what is served for breakfast: juices (orange and apple), hot milk, tea and coffee. Several types of cheese, ham, fresh and baked vegetables, beans and chickpeas in tomato, potato dishes (slices or pancakes), baked tomatoes with spices and cheese, eggs (boiled, scrambled eggs, fried eggs). Toasts, hot meat dishes, muffins, croissants, as well as butter, jam, honey and juicy fruits (both fresh and canned) are also required.

In Grandeur Hotel 5 * only 125 apartments. All of them are divided into the following categories:

  • Executive 2-seater. Area - 37 square meters There are rooms with both a king-size bed and two twin beds.
  • Deluxe Room Area - 41 square meters m. Inside there is one very large 2 double bed.
  • Executive Suite. Area - 48 square meters. This room is divided into a living room, dining area and bedroom. There is also a separate workspace.
  • Deluxe Suite. Area - 44 square meters. m. These rooms are decorated in a luxurious style. They consist of a dining area, a bedroom and a separate lounge. The photo below, by the way, presents a deluxe suite.

All apartments have a minibar, telephone, safe, air conditioning, hairdryer, radio, widescreen TV with satellite and cable channels, as well as a bathroom with toilet. Bathrobes and hygiene items are provided for all guests.

What do tourists say?

It is worth paying attention to the reviews left by Grandeur Hotel. This is what most people who have already been here talk about:

  • When checking in, a deposit of $ 100 is required. He will be returned in full upon eviction.
  • If guests arrived earlier than check-in (from 14:00), they can be accommodated immediately. But only if there are free and ready to move in numbers.
  • The apartments are gorgeous, as in the photo. Many worry that there are no balconies, but this is not a problem - the windows open, so that no one will be stuffy.
  • When placing 1 child under 12 years of age, an additional fee for him is not charged. For children under the age of 3, a separate cot is provided for free.
  • The rooftop pool is striking in its cleanliness. Towels can not be taken - they are issued at the entrance. In the jacuzzi, the water is very warm, and the massage option can be turned on independently. However, the rooftop does not catch Wi-Fi.
  • Guests are given 2 bottles of water upon arrival. Every day in the refrigerator report more.
  • Cleaning is carried out daily and efficiently. They change towels and bedding, replenish supplies of sugar, cream, tea, coffee. Maids also report soap, shower caps, creams, gels, shampoos. You can not take anything with you on vacation.
  • The safe and refrigerator in the room can be used for free, and not for money, as in many other hotels.
  • In the lobby there is a small Internet corner with computers, one of them with a printer. You can use it for free.
  • If you need to call the local number - you can do this from the reception. Managers will politely help you book a tour, tour, call a taxi, etc.

After reviewing the reviews, we can conclude that the Grandeur Hotel is a city hotel with modern comfortable rooms, chic service and excellent attitude towards guests. And with low prices for accommodation besides! About them will be discussed later.

Recommendations

People who have already visited Dubai often leave tips in their reviews that may turn out to be useful for travelers who are only going to come here. Here are the 5 most common recommendations:

  • The area is very busy. Nearby there is everything - shops, restaurants, Internet cafes, hypermarkets, exchangers, as well as countless branded boutiques. We definitely need to go here with a considerable amount of money in order to spend it with pleasure on profitable shopping.
  • If you leave the hotel, then go right and go around the building, you will see a small grocery store across the road. There is a good choice, low prices, and you can still pay in dollars.
  • Change currency is best in Mall of the Emirates. This shopping center is located next to Carrefour hypermarket. You can get to it quickly - you just need to turn right from the hotel and go straight ahead.
  • If tourists went on a trip with a company of 4 people, it is most profitable to take a taxi around the city. In the UAE, it is very cheap. In addition, it will bring where it is necessary - there will be no need to knock down legs in search of this or that place.
  • It is highly recommended that you look at the singing fountains. People resting here went to them every day. This free sight gives unforgettable emotions.

By the way, if you want impressions, you can order several excursions. It is better to contact the hotel administrator for help and in no case take them to the beach. There tours are offered at, to put it mildly, inflated prices.

Voucher cost

  • Flights from Moscow to Dubai and back.
  • Accommodation in a 2-bed superior room (7 days / 6 nights).
  • Breakfast
  • Medical insurance.
  • Transfer from the airport to the hotel and back.

This is just one example. Today there are many trips - you can go on a trip at least 3 days, at least 3 weeks. The price will differ from the term, room category, airline and, of course, the time of year. In low season, tour operators offer the lowest rates.
