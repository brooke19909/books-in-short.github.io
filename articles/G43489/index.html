Holiday Village "Brigantine" (Lipetsk): recreation, prices and reviews of tourists

Rest is something that every person needs from time to time. And the recreation center "Brigantine" offers its guests not only decent living conditions, but also a lot of entertainment for every taste. Naturally, many travelers are interested in questions about exactly where this wonderful place is located and what conditions for living and recreation it offers.

Where is the Brigantine base located?





The recreation center "Brigantine" is located in one of the most beautiful and cozy places in the Lipetsk region. Very close to the complex is the famous Matyr reservoir, which makes it possible for an excellent beach holiday. The distance to the city of Lipetsk is only 15 kilometers.

Short description of the complex for vacationers

Immediately it is worth noting that the Brigantina recreation center (Lipetsk) is open all year round - there are a lot of entertainment awaiting guests in both summer and winter. The hotel complex consists of a large office building, several residential buildings and individual cozy cottages, "lost" in the forest.

For whom is a vacation in the base suitable? In fact, people of any age can have a great time here - families with children come, and youth companies, and elderly people. The resort complex is surrounded by forest and is away from large roads, so peace and quiet are guaranteed.

Accommodation conditions, room description





The recreation center "Brigantine" (Lipetsk) offers its customers decent living conditions. On the territory of the complex there are several separate cottages, as well as an administrative building with 20 rooms of different capacities. Guests are offered check-in in both single and double rooms. It is worth noting that regardless of the category, all rooms are in perfect order - here you will find new furniture, as well as the necessary set of household appliances, including air conditioning, TV and refrigerator. Each room is equipped with a separate bathroom with toilet, shower and washbasin.

The recreation center "Brigantine" also has at its disposal several cottages of different capacities - these are houses for four, six, eight or ten people at a time. It is very convenient for large companies. These cottages have a sufficient number of bedrooms, as well as a bathroom. On the ground floor there is a fully equipped kitchen - there is a fridge, an electric kettle, a sink, a microwave oven. Beautiful views from the balcony. Some cottages also have a separate spacious veranda, where it is always a pleasure to spend time enjoying the beauty of nature and fresh air. If necessary, 1-3 people can be lodged in the house (depending on its initial capacity), as there are folding comfortable sofas.

Food scheme for vacationers





Of course, the recreation center "Brigantine" (Lipetsk) offers its customers all the amenities, including food. There is a cafe-bar "Brigantine" on the territory of the base. There is also a spacious tavern "Boatswain." It is here that guests are served a buffet three times a day. You can sit both inside the tavern and choose one of the many convenient wooden arbors located around the establishment.

What is the menu for vacationers? Here everyone will find something to their taste, as guests are served mainly international and Russian cuisine. In addition, every day there are fresh fruits on the table, as well as meat, fish and vegetables cooked on the fire. And children will enjoy delicious branded desserts.

Entertainment and leisure of tourists





It is immediately worth noting that the Brigantine recreation center (Lipetsk) is rich in surprises - here everyone can find something to their liking. Very close to the cottage town there is a superbly equipped beach where you can sunbathe and swim. In addition, several areas on the territory of the base are reserved for various sports, including futsal, volleyball, basketball, etc. Balls and other equipment can be rented at the rental point.

In addition, there is a sauna and a small pool to help you relax. And you will always have the opportunity to have fun and chat with friends while playing darts, paintball, billiards and other interesting games.

On the other hand, the recreation center "Brigantine" (Lipetsk) also organizes various holidays. Here you can really hold almost any event, including anniversary, birthdays and even weddings. It offers spacious gazebos, a spacious banquet hall (for 150 people), and even tents, if you want to relax and have fun in the fresh air.





In addition, corporate parties are regularly held here, as well as group vacations. The base workers even created a special wellness and intellectual program aimed at uniting a team of employees.

Holiday Village "Brigantine" (Lipetsk): prices

Immediately it is worth noting that the prices here depend primarily on the time of rest. For example, on weekends or holidays, the cost of base services is higher than on a weekday. First, talk about nutrition. Here you can separately order food in a cafe, cook it yourself (if you live in a cottage with a kitchen) or pay three meals a day for full meals, the cost of which is 550 rubles per day.

As for the rooms, the rooms in residential buildings cost from 1150 to 2000 per day. Cottages naturally cost a bit more. For example, a day in a four-seater house will cost 4500-6200 rubles. Accommodation in an eight-bed cottage costs 4,000 to 6,500 thousand per day. In any case, the price should be asked at the time of pre-booking.
