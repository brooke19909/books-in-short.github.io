China, Sanya. Sanya Island, China. Holidays in China, Sanya

Perhaps it is difficult to find a more popular tourist destination than China. Sanya is one of the most beautiful islands, which are often called peculiar Hawaii. There is absolutely everything you need for a wonderful holiday: long beaches, sun-drenched and washed by azure waves, as well as unsurpassed opportunities for outdoor activities.

Features of pastime

Holidays on the popular island of Sanya are popular at any time of the year. This is primarily due to favorable weather conditions: even in January, the air temperature warms up to twenty-five degrees Celsius. That is why Europeans like to celebrate Christmas holidays here.

Sanya Island (China) is not only a kilometers-long sandy coast and blissful relaxation throughout the entire vacation. These are also unsurpassed conditions for diving and surfing: the beaches of Sanya Bay, Dadong and Yalong are amazing places with a rich underwater world. In addition, this island is one of the largest centers for pearl mining. After bargaining with the seller, you can purchase amazing jewelry at a very attractive price.

sights

Are you interested in holidays in China? Sanya is an island that is rich primarily for its natural attractions. Lush greenery, azure waves and many kilometers of sandy beaches - could there be anything better than a vacation in such a place?

Going on a trip to about. Sanya, be sure to visit Nanwan. This is a small peninsula on which about two thousand macaques live. The animals are very friendly and playful, so tourists should be extremely attentive to their belongings.

Special attention should be paid to the Sanya Nanshan temple, built recently - in 1998. The majestic building, crowned with a statue of Guanin, is located in a picturesque park. It is worth noting that, despite the rather high cost of an admission ticket (150 RMB), tourists are happy to visit this attraction of the island.

Holidays and festivals, active life

Which country has a special culture and mentality? Of course, this is China. Sanya is no exception in this case.

The active life of the island is represented mainly by unsurpassed conditions for diving, surfing, water skiing, as well as for sightseeing in Sanya. In addition, the ethnic minority park, which is located just thirty kilometers from the city, deserves special attention. Here you can get acquainted with the colorful representatives of ancient cultures.





Locals in traditional costumes, the unique customs of the Li and Miao dynasties, folklore performances, traditional crafts and, of course, Chinese cuisine - all this and much more awaits tourists near the island of Sanya. Weather allows you to travel to these places at any time of the year.

However, there are some months during which a more intense cultural program awaits tourists: extraordinary wedding festivals, a traditional festival of Li and Miao dynasties and an annual beauty contest in which the most beautiful girls from all over the island participate.

Infrastructure

The island has its own airport, which regularly receives flights from major cities of the country, as well as from Moscow and St. Petersburg. In addition, some tourists prefer to get to Haikou (the capital of Hainan province) first, and then use the services of one of the local airlines.



There are other options that exclude travel by air. Have you already come to China? Sanya is a 35-hour train ride from Beijing, and just 15 hours from Guangzhou. The journey is long, but it is great for those who are afraid of flying on airplanes.

On the island of Sanya (China), hotels are mainly three- or five-star. All of them offer excellent quality of service.

In order to enjoy their vacation to the fullest, tourists are invited to rent a car, thanks to which you can visit the most picturesque places of Sanya.





Holidays in Sanya with children

Currently, avid travelers are increasingly trying to choose directions and means of accommodation, taking into account the opportunities for recreation of their children. Many factors are taken into account here: the ecological situation, the organization of children's entertainment, the convenience of accommodation, food, special programs for the smallest.

Have you been attracted by China for a long time? Sanya offers many options for traveling with children. Young travelers are offered a variety of children's programs and entertainments, game complexes located directly on the hotel territory, as well as a separate kitchen. Parents have the opportunity to leave their child for some time with qualified employees who will surely find a way to make the pastime of the child fun.





In any case, the child will definitely not be bored in Sanya: excellent opportunities and conditions for swimming, a trip to the famous monkey island, as well as animation programs and a lot of opportunities for outdoor activities will give the child many unforgettable impressions and emotions.

To summarize

Of course, the island of Sanya is the best suited for a beach holiday with a rich cultural program. Magnificent beaches and a comfortable climate create a special atmosphere that allows you to escape from worldly worries and get real pleasure from a vacation.





Adding to this the beauty of local landscapes and excellent service, you can get ideal opportunities for a beach holiday. Moreover, due to the relative remoteness of the island, Sanya has not yet suffered so much from the influence of impressive flows of tourists - is this not a great opportunity for the long-awaited seclusion?

Holidays on the island of Sanya are a chance to enjoy the amazingly beautiful nature of China, put in order your thoughts, take a break from the daily rush, and also get a lot of pleasant experiences and stock up on energy until the next vacation.
