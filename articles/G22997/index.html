How to mold spider-man from plasticine? We create with children

Of course, all small and large children love to sculpt and draw. In this article, we will talk in detail about how to mold spider-man from plasticine.

Spiderman mask

First, we blind the simplest craft, namely the mask of the protagonist. Before you mold a spider-man from plasticine, wash your hands and prepare the workplace. For sculpting, it is better to take a small plastic board. Take red, black and white plasticine, golden sparkles and a stack.





Take the red plasticine and make a cake of about five to seven millimeters thick. Then, with a knife or stack, carefully cut the head of the protagonist. We cut out a heart-shaped head, but you can make it oval. It all depends on your desire. Then smooth the edges so that they are sloping. Take the black plasticine and roll a lot of thin black sausages out of it, which then set aside.

Then, from the same black plasticine, form two small cakes in the form of ovals and stick them to the red layer. Then make two of the same cakes, but smaller ones from white plasticine. Stick them to black cakes. The eyes are ready. Then lay out a net pattern from black sausages. Your product is ready.

How to mold a spider - man from plasticine in stages?

Now let's start creating a more complex model of Spider-Man. By the way, if you want to make yourself such a keychain, then you can take baked polymer clay. How to mold spider-man from plasticine from one piece? You will find out now. Take a piece of red mass and knead it well in your hands. Then carefully begin to stretch your head, arms and legs from this piece. Remember that the head should be slightly larger than the legs and arms. If you sculpt from plastic, then bake the product and then paint.





If you sculpt from plasticine, now you will need to make blue clothes, a black spider and eyes. Roll the blue plasticine into a thin-thin layer and cut out a jacket and pants that are suitable in size and shape. Stick it all to Spider-Man. Roll a small ball from the black mass and stick the figures to the chest. From thin black sausages, make the legs of a spider. Then take the white plasticine and roll it into the same thin layer. Cut out triangles from it and stick to the head. Cover your eyes with a thin black sausage. Spider-man is ready.

How to mold a full-length spider-man from plasticine?

But not everyone can sculpt figures in full growth! This is primarily due to the fact that such figures require a wire base. Therefore, if you are thinking of how to mold a spider-man to the full height from plasticine, then you will first have to work on creating a solid frame.

First make a wire frame. If you do not want to spend a lot of material, then you can wrap small pieces of foil to the frame. Then, carefully glue the frame with red and blue plasticine. Carefully work out the spider-man figure. It will be great if you find an anatomical atlas and study the location of the muscles.





A shield is also best done with a frame, for example, from the same wire or plastic. In the same way, cover the shield with red, white and black plasticine. From white and black masses blind eyes. Now you know how to mold a full-length spider-man from plasticine. It is difficult, but quite doable.

Connect the fantasy!

If you are thinking of how to mold a black Spider-Man from plasticine, then it is created in a similar way. It is only necessary to change the color. In addition, keep in mind that black plasticine usually gets dirty better than others, and you will have to wash your hands more often.
