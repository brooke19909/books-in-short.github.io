Amsterdam hotels: review, reviews. The strangest hotel in Amsterdam

Tourists often wonder about which hotel is better to choose in order to have time to explore the maximum number of famous places. Amsterdam hotels on the outskirts are much cheaper in cost, but keep in mind that getting to the center will not be easy. Public transport in the city is expensive. A ticket for a tram, bus and metro costs from 2.90 euros / 205 rubles. for one hour trip and 7.50 euros / 531 rubles. for a day.

Hotels in the center of Amsterdam will save you time and visit many city attractions.

LloydHotel & CulturalEmbassy - a unique concept

Lloyd Hotel & Cultural Embassy is a design hotel designed for travelers with different tastes. It is believed that this is the strangest hotel in Amsterdam. Over fifty designers and artists worked on the design of this unusual hotel. Lloyd is constantly hosting cultural events and exhibitions. And its unusualness is that this hotel in Amsterdam is a former prison.

Rooms

In "Lloyd" rooms have different stars. There are 117 of them, and each is unique in its own way.

  • Deluxe - five-star rooms can surprise even the most sophisticated guest. Swing hanging from the ceiling, a bathroom with a view of the stars set up for romance. All rooms are equipped with quality furniture, each room is original in its own way.

Features of "Deluxe": area - 50 square meters, round-the-clock service, free Wi-Fi, TV, hairdryer.

  • Superior - four-star rooms with an original design. Some of them are two-story, in others the bed is hidden in a closet, others are decorated in the art deco style.

Features of the Superior rooms: "area - 30 square meters, round-the-clock service, Internet access, hairdryer, TV, the ability to drink tea or coffee on the floor.

  • "Standard" - spacious rooms with separate bathtubs are perfect for families or businessmen.

Features: area - 30 square meters, tea or coffee on the floor, TV, hairdryer.

  • "Economy" - bright rooms with a free layout, some have bathrooms.

Features: area - 25 square meters, Internet access, drinks on the floor, TV.

  • "Budget" - one-star rooms in which every centimeter of space is thought out. The windows overlook the canal. Since the shower is on the floor and is shared, bathrobes are provided free of charge.

The strangest hotel in Amsterdam presents various spaces for cultural and business events:
