Ai-Petri cable car: trail between heaven and earth

The cable car on Ai-Petri was built only 25 years ago, but has already managed to gain immense popularity. This "airway" connected the highest mountain of Crimea with the picturesque resort village of Miskhor. By cable car, tourists have the opportunity to climb a fairly considerable height and enjoy the amazingly beautiful panorama of the southeastern coast of the peninsula - from Sudak to Foros.

Immediately after the completion of the construction of the cableway, it was recognized as unique. In addition, she now has no analogues. At first glance, what could be special about it? Ai-Petri cable car: price and schedule

The "air tram", running between the village of Miskhor and Mount Ai-Petri, carries out its work at this time:

- The rise is from 10 to 17 hours;

- The descent takes place from 10 to 18 hours.

In 2013, the price of an adult one-way ticket amounted to 60 UAH (about 240 rubles), for a child - 30 UAH (about 120 rubles).

Ai-Petri cable car : is it worth the risk?

The "air" road consists of two stations. At the first stage, the cabin slowly hovers over a beautiful coniferous forest. After the Sosnovy Bor station, the passenger cabin is rapidly gaining height and is rapidly heading for the snow-white Ai-Petri prongs.

It is difficult to describe the feelings a person experiences when he rises to Ai-Petri. Picturesque views, sparkling sea and dense forests - all this makes such an extreme trip one of the most memorable moments in life. Exciting flight experience and the most vivid impressions - this is what the cable car on Ai-Petri gives. It is very convenient that on the top of the mountain you can eat in numerous cafes and restaurants, buy original souvenirs.



Daredevils who were not afraid to get here are waiting for truly incredible adventures. Here, on the viewing platform, you can contemplate the pleasant azure of the Black Sea and admire the steep cliffs of the mountain ranges growing in front. And what could be nicer than enjoying the crystal clear air and the hospitable southern sun ?! Tiny cars float below, the roofs of houses are barely visible, and the life of Crimea is in full swing. You too can take an incredible chance and watch all of this from the highest point of Crimea - the amazing Ai-Petri Mountain.
