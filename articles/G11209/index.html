Winter washing machine care

In the event that the device is located where there is a possibility of water freezing, it is necessary to perform some actions in order to avoid various damage to the mechanism. To begin with, close the faucet that delivers water. If there are foreign objects in the drum, then they must be removed. In order for the water in the pipe to not freeze, it is necessary to start the spin mode for the entire cycle. This will get rid of excess fluid. Also remember to remove the hoses and completely drain the water. You must unplug the power cord, open the drum and dry it completely. Then close the door. The box in which the powder is placed should also be washed and dried. Then install it in place. At the end of the winter period, before carrying out the first wash, it is necessary to start the process with powder, but without linen.
