Pitsunda, Abkhazia, "Paradise": overview, description and reviews of tourists

During the Soviet Union, the resorts of Abkhazia were considered the most elite, picturesque and most developed. Rest in the famous Gagra, Sukhumi or Pitsunda - this was the cherished dream of every Soviet person. After the collapse of the USSR, a difficult share befell this proud and beautiful country. Armed conflict has caused enormous damage to the infrastructure of resort towns. However, thanks to the magnificent nature, unique subtropical climate, clean warm sea and hospitable locals, tourists from Russia and the CIS countries still go to Abkhazia.

Holidays in Pitsunda

Pitsunda is a resort town in Abkhazia, located on a wide cape. Pebble and sand beaches of Pitsunda are one of the most chic on the entire Black Sea coast.

The highlight of the resort is a strip of coniferous forest of relict pines, which stretches along the banks of Pitsunda. A mixture of coniferous and sea air creates a unique healing climate.





Subtropical vegetation captivates all vacationers. Huge palm trees, fragrant eucalyptus trees, blooming magnolias, evergreen mimosa, oranges, tangerines, persimmons, kiwi, figs, pomegranates and many other southern trees will not leave indifferent any tourist. Abkhazia is a paradise. A person who once visited this paradise will undoubtedly want to return here again.

Surrounding Pitsunda are majestic mountain ranges with fabulous caves, silver waterfalls, fast mountain streams and crystal lakes. It‚Äôs impossible not to fall in love with all this splendor!

Suburb of Pitsunda - Alahadzy village

In the suburb of Pitsunda there are many cozy resort villages, ideal for a quiet family vacation. In such places, tourists are guaranteed peace, tranquility and unity with nature.

One of these villages is Alahadza, located on the outskirts of the famous Pitsunda. This small and well-maintained village is well-equipped enough for a good rest. The village is rich in southern vegetation - palm trees, pines, cypresses, citrus trees, evergreen shrubs and exotic flowers grow everywhere.

Alahadzi beach is the pride of the village. A wide sand and pebble strip, where the crystal-clear turquoise sea splashes, is what vacationers come here for.





Entertainment in the village is minimized. Just a few cafes, a tour desk and water beach fun. The local market is full of ripe fruits, which are plentiful among the local population.

Private hotels, holiday homes and hotels in Alahadzi offer vacationers to spend an unforgettable vacation in comfortable conditions at affordable prices.

Hotel "Paradise" in the village of Alahadzy, Pitsunda

From May to October, hospitable Abkhazia awaits tourists from Russia and neighboring countries on vacation. Paradise is a new modern hotel located in the suburb of Pitsunda in the small resort village of Alahadzi.

The hotel is a complex of two three-story buildings and a cozy summer cafe. The Hotel "Paradise" (Abkhazia) was built in 2015 and has its own guarded territory.





The hotel is ideal for a quiet family vacation. It is located on the main street of the village, at a distance of about five hundred meters from a wide sandy-pebbled town beach.

The degree of comfort at the level of three stars offers its tourists the hotel "Paradise 3 *". Abkhazia today has a sufficient number of such places of rest, where the level of service fully meets all the basic requirements of this category. Hotel "Paradise" is no exception. All the norms and requirements set by the rules of the standard are met in the hotel - the availability of our own cafe, a wide range of services, room amenities, comfortable furniture, TVs, refrigerators and other benefits of civilization.

Accommodation for vacationers

The hotel has 69 standard and junior suites. The main ones are standard one-room rooms - double and triple. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary, in accordance with the requirements of 3 stars: comfortable modern furniture, plasma televisions with cable television, air conditioning and mini-refrigerators. Each room has its own bathroom - shower, toilet, sink, toiletries. Judging by the photo of the rooms, everything is very civil. Each room has access to a shared balcony with views of the beautiful landscapes that are so rich in picturesque Abkhazia.





Paradise Corner took care of tourists who value increased comfort and prepared junior suites. This, of course, is not an apartment or even a suite, but a little higher in class than usual standards. The rooms have a large area, expensive furniture and plumbing.

Accommodation prices

Room prices at the Hotel "Paradise 3 *" (Pitsunda, Abkhazia) are dynamic and vary depending on the season.

So, for example, the placement of one person for a day in a standard room in May and October 2016 was estimated at 1750 rubles, in June and September 2016 - at 1850 rubles, in the high season (July, August) - at 2000 rubles.

Superior rooms are more expensive by 250 rubles, that is, the price ranges from 2000 to 2250 rubles per person per day.

Accommodation in an extra bed is a bit cheaper, namely 125 rubles per day. Children under 2 years old stay free of charge. Children from 2 to 12 years old on an extra bed receive a discount of -250 rubles per day.

An undoubted advantage is the fact that these prices include not only hotel accommodation, but also three meals a day.

Hotel infrastructure

The hotel area is small, green and comfortable. There is a summer cafe and a bar on site. For children, a small playground and an inflatable pool are equipped.

In the evening, lights are lit all over the area and live music is played in the cafe. Wi-Fi is available on site.





In the village of entertainment a bit. There are several restaurants, shops, souvenir shops, a market. Pitsunda center is a twenty-minute drive away. There is a tour desk in the village, and everyone can order any tour of the famous places rich in fabulous Abkhazia.

"Paradise": reviews of tourists

Reviews for the hotel "Paradise" are quite contradictory. Many tourists leave positive reviews in which they write about the friendliness of the staff, about new clean rooms, about delicious cuisine and quality service. However, there are also negative reviews, which are mainly addressed to the administration and staff. Upon arrival, there were no previously booked rooms, the administration did not respond to the requests of some customers, the staff rude the customers, and the rooms were cleaned at the wrong time and poorly.





In almost all reviews, guests of the Paradise Hotel (Abkhazia, Pitsunda) tell about quality cuisine. Vacationers say that the portions are large, the food is varied, the cooks cook deliciously at home.

Pros and Cons of the Hotel

From the reviews and reviews of hotel guests, we can conclude about the main positive and negative sides of this place of rest.

Pros:

  • fresh repairs in the rooms;
  • quality furniture;
  • tasty and quality food;
  • good work of cafe staff and musicians;
  • wonderful nature, silence, free beach, clear sea.

Minuses:

  • rural village, on the beach you can find cows and sheep;
  • there are a lot of dogs in the village;
  • poor administration work.

We will not draw conclusions about the hostility of the staff and hotel owners, since the question remains open. Opinions of the hotel guests on this score are divided and are completely opposite.

If you want to relax your soul and body, away from noisy cities, in complete unity with nature, then you should definitely visit this wonderful and fabulous place - Abkhazia, Pitsunda, Paradise Corner Hotel.
