Rating of Tinkoff Bank. Deposits, loans, reliability assessment

New technologies have penetrated almost all areas of our lives. Buying clothes, ordering food from a restaurant, paying utility bills and much more today can be done using a smartphone or tablet.

Credit organizations also did not stand aside. Tinkoff Bank (network address - tinkoff.ru) offers its customers exclusively remote service. This is the only commercial bank that does not have its own ATMs and branches.

Reliability Assessment

According to the latest information, Tinkoff is located on the 44th line of the rating for equity and on the 49th line in terms of assets among Russian banks.

The rating of Tinkoff Bank directly depends on the assessment of its reliability. This indicator is a combination of factors that affect the fulfillment of obligations. The data of the largest agencies Moody's and Fitch Ratings say that in the long term the bank was rated B2 (significant higher vulnerability) and B + (significant highly speculative rating), respectively.

To conduct a reliability assessment, the following indicators must be taken into account:

- liquid assets;

- the level of security of loans issued;

- structure of own funds;

- credit risks.

Significant is information about falsification of reports, change of ownership and various rumors. Given all of the above, over the past year, the rating of Tinkoff Bank cannot be called optimistic. Various data indicate the emergence of negative trends, which in the future may affect its financial stability.

Traditionally, Forbes portal has its own rating. This time, the bank with remote servicing was in 51st place.

New Generation Bank

A stationary computer, tablet, laptop or smartphone - regardless of the type of device, customers always have access to Tinkoff Bank. A personal account is the main way to manage your own accounts.

Payment of utility bills and fines, transfers between accounts and other individuals, opening deposits, connecting additional services - just a few clicks, and a new generation of online banking will present a schedule of expenses and help you plan your budget.



Subscribing to taxes and penalties will allow you to always be aware of important information, and setting limits not only saves you money, but also protects you from fraud.

Deposits

Two years after the opening, Tinkoff Bank offered its customers a new service. Deposits to individuals are also processed remotely. You will find detailed information about savings accounts on the official website.

To fill out an application, you only need a passport, and the whole procedure will take no more than ten minutes. A debit card and a set of documents will be delivered at a convenient time by the courier.

You can replenish the deposit in cash at points of partners, by bank transfer from an account or another card.

If a client transfers wages to a Tinkoff Bank card, a personal account allows you to control not only current expenses, but also plan savings and partially replenish your open deposit.

Top apps

The high rating of Tinkoff Bank is largely due to IT specialists, developers and analysts. The creation and implementation of software systems deals with the department, which employs 400 people.

Thanks to joint efforts, Tinkoff Bank applications for smartphones based on iOS, Windows Phone and Android took first place in 2015 according to a study by the well-known analytical agency Markswebb Rank & Report.



In addition, three years ago, bank experts began developing specialized applications that allow you to make quick transfers from card to card or pay fines to the traffic police.

About the disadvantages

At first glance, Tinkoff Bank customers (address: 1st Volokolamskiy proezd, building 10, p. 1) should be satisfied with the availability of banking services. The support service works around the clock without a break for weekends and holidays.

Remotely, you can carry out all the necessary actions, which in the office of another commercial bank will take time to wait in line or collect documents.

Experts note that the technical structure of the bank is built perfectly. However, there are weaknesses too. For example, due to the lack of a network of Tinkoff Bank offices, cash loans cannot be obtained - borrowed funds are available only on credit cards, the issuance and delivery of which also take some time.

Reviews of regular customers note a repeated change in the terms of service unilaterally (a reduction in deposit rates, a decrease in the limit on credit cards and a lack of information on new fees).

When registering a new product, the consumer in advance does not have the opportunity to get acquainted even with the contract.

In general, remote service is designed for a certain age category. The lack of additional offices for clients of the old school is unlikely to inspire confidence, and the qualifications of some support staff do not always meet expectations.
