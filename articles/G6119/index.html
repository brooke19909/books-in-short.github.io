Hotels in Odintsovo: overview, reviews

One of the most comfortable and expensive cities of the Moscow Region is Odintsovo. Recently, he has been actively developing not only residential buildings, but also business-class buildings. In summer, the weather in Odintsovo is excellent, so many people come here to relax. Consider what kind of hotels are in demand among tourists.

In the reviews, guests write that the rooms are clean and comfortable.
