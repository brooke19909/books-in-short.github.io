LCD "Leonovsky Park": description, reviews

Zheleznodorozhny is a small town near Moscow with a developed transport network. If you decide to purchase an apartment in the capital, but are limited in funds, we suggest paying attention to the Leonovskiy Park residential complex. This is a bright and modern complex, where all the necessary conditions for the most comfortable life are created. In the framework of this material, with the help of the feedback of the first inhabitants, we will produce an objective review.





about the project

Currently, residential complexes in ecologically clean areas of large Moscow are very popular. Tired of the hustle and bustle of noise and gas pollution in the capital, we are looking for a paradise, preferably in the middle of a forest belt, on the banks of a clean lake. It turns out that in order to feel all the charms of suburban life, one does not need to say goodbye to the developed metropolitan infrastructure. Pay attention to the LCD "Leonovsky Park". Reviews of the first customers covered the location chosen for the construction of the new complex. Moreover, the developer was able to implement all the wishes of modern residents of the metropolis, to provide them with comfort and safety.





6 buildings of varying number of storeys, erected using monolithic technology, surrounded by a forest belt - this is what the developer offers. The territory of the complex is fenced, is under round-the-clock protection. The use of advanced technology and innovation has allowed us to achieve impeccable quality and practicality.

About the developer

Shared construction is a huge risk. Investing our money, getting into huge debts, each of us must be sure of the reliability of the developer. The LCD "Leonovsky Park" was no exception. The builder was Mosstroymekhanizatsiya-5, on the account of which, over the course of almost half a century, several dozen successfully implemented projects were accumulated in Moscow, the Moscow Region and St. Petersburg. Attracting qualified specialists to work of any complexity, using advanced technologies and our own production capacity allows us to refuse the services of subcontractors and take the construction of houses into our own hands. The company is reliable, all new buildings are delivered on time without the slightest violation.

Location

LCD "Leonovsky Park", a photo of which you will find below, boasts an excellent location. All buildings are being built just 8 km from MKAD, at an equal distance from Zheleznodorozhny, Reutov and Balashikha. Just 1.5 km south is the Nosovikhinskoye Shosse - a wide road with perfect pavement, free from traffic jams. Kuchino Train Station is just 2 km away, and the nearest Novokosino Metro Station is a 10-minute walk. Near the house there is a public transport stop: minibuses and buses connect the microdistrict with the capital.





Infrastructure

LCD "Leonovsky Park" is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The project laid the construction of its own kindergarten, comprehensive school, sports complex and shopping and entertainment center. The basement floors of the buildings are reserved for commercial infrastructure: there will be shops, bank branches, restaurants and cafes. Remoteness from civilization in this case is compensated by smart recreation areas. The huge Kuchinsky forest park, the bank of the Pekhorka River have already become a favorite place for recreation and walks of local residents.

Not without flaws. In particular, the first tenants complain about the insufficient number of parking spaces. In the context of dense housing development and the presence of a car in almost every family, I would like to see a multi-level parking.

Apartments, layouts

A variety of planning decisions is what all residents of the Leonovskiy Park LCD focus on. Spacious kitchens, isolated rooms, a separate bathroom, wardrobe - the developer tried to create comfortable conditions even in the smallest apartment.





The property is transferred to the property in draft finish. All major work has been done: cold and hot water were supplied, electrical wiring was completed, and heating radiators were installed. All surfaces need leveling and subsequent decorative finishing.

At the moment, the cost of apartments in a new residential quarter starts from 4 million rubles. Real estate can be purchased for cash or on a mortgage basis.

To summarize

LCD "Leonovsky Park" - a kind of "city in the city." This is a new neighborhood isolated from a noisy metropolis. A great opportunity to escape from the hustle and bustle of the city, to be in a paradise, surrounded by a forest belt on all sides. Some buildings have already been commissioned and settled, infrastructure facilities are being gradually leased. But very soon, residents will have access to the developed infrastructure of the district and will be able to enjoy the comfort of life. Do not miss the chance to buy an apartment in the LCD "Leonovsky Park" on favorable terms.
