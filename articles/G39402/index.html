Silver Sand Beach Resort 3 * Hotel: tourist reviews

Clean beaches, warm sea waters, mild climate and breathtaking beauty of nature - this is what Goa offers to tourists. Naturally, there are many hotels of different sizes and categories. One of them is Silver Sand Beach Resort 3 *. What is this place really about and is it worth it to stay here? This question interests every traveler.

What does the hotel look like? Description of location and infrastructure





The small picturesque Silver Sands Beach Resort 3 * hotel is located in the southern part of Goa, namely, in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe resort town called Colva. An indisputable advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the beach, which can be reached in a matter of minutes. Margao is 3 km from the property. But the nearest airport is located further, about 22 km, in the city of Dabolim.

This is a small but incredibly comfortable hotel complex. Its area is approximately 4.4 thousand square meters. In the center is the main building of the hotel, which, incidentally, was built in 1990. But in 2009, the last complete restoration was carried out and today this place is striking in its beautiful design and elegance. The whole territory is decorated with greenery. There is a small garden where tourists like to spend time. In the courtyard there is a swimming pool, relaxation terraces, sports grounds, evenly cut lawns.

Hotel Silver Sands Beach Resort 3 * (Colva): description and photos of rooms





This is a medium-sized hotel, which offers customers check-in in one of the 70 rooms. There are 52 standard rooms (their area is 22 square meters), as well as 18 suites. Regardless of which room you choose, you can count on coziness and comfort.

Immediately it is worth saying that there are no balconies in the rooms. Nevertheless, there are huge windows that fill the room with light and heat. They also offer views of the garden, surroundings and the ocean. The furniture is comfortable and the beds are large, so it will be nice to relax.

A set of household appliances is also available. Using the air conditioner and fan, you can maintain a comfortable temperature in the room. There is also a TV, watching which guests spend their free evenings. Guests are provided with a set for making coffee and tea. In the room you will find a minibar with a refrigerator, but fill it with drinks only upon request, and this service is paid.

The room has an attached bathroom. The situation here is simple - there is a shower cubicle, a toilet, a washbasin, as well as a dressing mirror, a convenient hair dryer, and a basic set of toiletries. Of course, guests are offered clean towels.

The maids work well, the rooms are always clean. As for cleaning, it is carried out on request. Guests must leave the room key at the reception, asking for cleaning. But, according to reviews, many residents rarely resort to this service.

Does the hotel offer food?





Since food is important for many travelers, this issue is worth discussing. Silver Sand Beach Resort 3 * hotel offers daily breakfasts, although this service can be refused. In the morning, a buffet is served for guests. It is worth noting that the food here is mainly dietary, vegetarian. You will be offered cereals, toasts, fresh buns, omelets, etc. At lunch and in the evening you can also eat in this restaurant - the menu will be more varied, and the prices are affordable. Guests can enjoy a cup of tea or a drink in the local bar. In addition, there are many cafes, bars and cozy colorful restaurants near the hotel where you can enjoy traditional Indian and Asian dishes.

Where is the beach located? Water activities for vacationers





The hotel is located just 200 meters from a large public beach - a convenient path leads to it. It is nice to swim and sunbathe. By the way, renting sunbeds and mattresses is paid. There is an opportunity to windsurf, ride a boat, try parasailing. In addition, taxis can be used to reach other beaches.

In the courtyard there is a small but deep pool. The water here is always clean, so guests often relax in this part of the hotel. Naturally, umbrellas and sun loungers are nearby. There is also a section for bathing children. In the evenings, by the way, guests themselves arrange dances and other entertainment by the pool.

It features a steam room and a sauna. There is an Ayurvedic center where they offer quality massage and other services. And do not forget that Goa is a wonderful place for excursions.

Silver Sands Beach Resort 3 * Hotel (India): what do tourists say?

Surely everyone knows that much more useful information can be obtained by talking with tourists who have already managed to visit a certain place. What do they say about Silver Sands Beach Resort 3 *? Reviews are for the most part positive. The hotel is truly modern with stylish decor. The rooms are large enough, the bathroom is equipped with everything necessary. They are cleaned well, but you can refuse this service.

According to reviews, the Silver Sand Beach Resort 3 * hotel is perfect for tourists who like to spend time on the beach, explore the city, and go on excursions.
