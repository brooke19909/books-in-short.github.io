How to make bead grapes with beads

Having learned how to make grapes from beads beads, you will stop puzzling over how to complement a gift for friends. Such a cute thing can serve as a key ring or become earrings. But you can learn to create even a whole sculpture that will decorate a room.

Earrings





Bead grapes with beads is an elegant accessory suitable even for an evening out. Complement them with a little black dress and you will shine at any event!

Materials:

  • 42 round glass beads of red or purple color.
  • Two acrylic leaves.
  • 42 gold pins with a head.
  • 12 gold rings 22 caliber.
  • 1 pair of earring hooks.
  • Pliers.

You can change the colors and shapes of the blanks.

Working process:

  1. Place the beads on the heads of the pins.
  2. Using pliers, bend the tip of the pin sticking out of the bead at right angles. Rotate the tool to twist the pin, then bend it a little back (a hook has formed). Finally bend the tip to the point where the pin exits the bead. The loop turned out.
  3. Make a loop on each bead.
  4. Assembly of parts. There are 5 rings on each earring, on which 21 beads should fit. On the first ring there are 4 beads, on the second 8, on the third and fourth 4 and on the last 1.
  5. Insert leaflets into the rings.
  6. Attach all the details to the base.

This method can also create a suspension, then you get a whole set.

Earrings. Second way





Grapes earrings from beads are created in another way, which requires weaving skills. Have to take:

  • Large purple beads.
  • Little green and white beads.
  • The wire.
  • Earring hooks.
  • Pliers.

What to do:

  1. Cut 60 centimeters of wire, string 29 green beads on it.
  2. Add a purple bead and four more green beads.
  3. Pass the wire through the fifth and sixth beads from the large bead.
  4. String two green, one purple and another green bead.
  5. Pass the wire through all the new beads, grabbing two more from the base.
  6. Repeat this seven times.
  7. And at the end, do the same thing twice, but replace the purple beads with small white ones.
  8. Add two beads and pass the wire through the very first bead. It should be a loop.
  9. Attach an earring hook.

Now you have a beautiful accessory.

Grape bush. Leaves





Having made grapes from beads, you can decorate a thick branch. You get a miniature berry bush, which is not ashamed to put on the shelf. And from afar, the craft looks like a real vine. What you need to create:

  • The beads are green and purple.
  • A wire with a diameter of 0.3 and 1.0 millimeters.
  • Green threads or fabric.
  • Thick branch.
  • Scissors.

What to do:

  1. Take a wire with a diameter of 0.3 mm and put three green beads on it. On both sides of them should be the same amount of wire.
  2. Pass the ends of the wire through the opposite beads, tighten.
  3. Put on the left end two beads, on the right one. Pass both ends through them.
  4. Using the same technique, continue to weave to the fifth row, gradually increasing the number of beads by one.
  5. After the fifth row, begin to gradually narrow. In total, nine rows should be obtained.
  6. Start weaving the second part of the leaf, but starting from the point of narrowing, braid the first part. Do the same with the third part.
  7. Twist the remaining ends of the wire.
  8. About 40 such leaves are needed.

Grape bush. Grapes

Now begin to learn how to weave grapes from beads to beads. What to do:

  1. Place four beads on a 0.3 mm diameter wire. Pass both ends of the wire through the end bead. The result is a cross.
  2. On the left end, place one bead, on the right two. Pass the left end through the extreme bead of the right. Ready one more cross.
  3. There should be four crosses in total.
  4. When done, pass the left end of the wire through the very first bead. Do the same with the right end. All weaving bent in half. Twist the wire.
  5. Make 14 such details.
  6. Collect a bunch. To do this, take one part and screw four more above it, slightly above six, and then again four.
  7. You need to make 35 clusters.

Grape bush. Assembly

To collect grapes from beads with beads, you must:

  1. Create branches. To do this, take a wire with a diameter of 1 millimeter and cut 9 parts from it. They can be of different sizes.
  2. Wrap each with green thread or cloth.
  3. Attach the finished parts to the branch. On one branch there should be four clusters and five leaves.
  4. When you finish assembling, tie everything to a thick branch.
  5. Glue to a piece of wood.

The result was a sculpture "Grapes from beads." The master class spoke in detail about all the stages of creation.

Trinket





There are no difficulties in the process of weaving from beads. Grapes can be obtained even by beginners, especially if you get acquainted with this method. Materials:

  • 18 lilac and 24 green beads.
  • Wire.
  • Adapter ring.

Working process:

  1. Place three beads on the wire. Pass both ends of the wire through the end beads.
  2. Put four beads on the left end, pass the right end through them.
  3. In the next row, do the same with five beads, and then with six.
  4. Twist both ends.
  5. String 12 beads on the left end, form a loop. Do the same with the right end.
  6. Create the required key fob length by twisting the wire.
  7. Attach the adapter ring.
