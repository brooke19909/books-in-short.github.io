How to make an autumn wreath with your own hands?

Autumn is a special time of the year, colorful leaves rustle underfoot, and on the table are many fruits of various kinds. It's time to go for a walk in the forest and get natural materials to create a unique home decor. For example, try to make an autumn wreath with your own hands for the home.

We collect materials



To produce thematic decor, you will need special materials. On a fine autumn day, go for a walk with the whole family and collect the gifts of the forest. Colorful leaves, ripe berries and small vegetables and fruits that can be stored for a long time will do. Do not forget about the auxiliary elements - seeds of herbs and trees, pieces of bark and moss, mushrooms. You can add decor - made of clay and plastic bugs and butterflies, ribbons and other decorations. Do not forget that for the manufacture of interior decoration you will need a base, it is most convenient to use a wire frame, but you can also make a wreath from twigs.

How to make an autumn wreath: some original ideas



The easiest way to create a composition dedicated to the outgoing summer is to use a circle of thin twigs or dried grass as the basis. One side of the finished wreath can be decorated with bright leaves or berries. Decorations from dried flowers look no worse. You can decorate them not only with foliage and forest fruits, but also with fruits or vegetables. Inshell nuts and ornamental plants like physalis also look good. Do not be afraid to combine natural and artificial materials. Decorative inflorescences made of plastic or fabric will only decorate your wreath. Fruits can also be used artificial. From natural, only small berries and artificial pumpkins retain their shape well. Wreaths collected from one species of plants or fruits look interesting. As a basis, you can also use a wire or a floral sponge. Fix the decorative elements on the wreath close to each other or at some distance, leaving colorful leaves or rods in sight. If you intend to fasten juicy fruits or vegetables, use pieces of wire or sharp toothpicks. You can also decorate an autumn wreath with your own hands, using mushroom dummies.
