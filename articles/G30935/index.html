Hotel "Palazzo Trezzini": description

Hotel "Palazzo Trezzini", the photo of which is presented in the review, is one of the best places for tourists who visit St. Petersburg. Its guests note that within the walls of this place to stay there are all conditions for a comfortable pastime for guests of different categories. Often, travelers stop here who have arrived in the city in order to explore local sights and visit interesting places, but also there are often business people who are in the cultural capital of Russia in order to solve work issues. Some young couples also choose the Trezzini Palace boutique hotel for a romantic getaway - there are also a number of conditions for this.

Let us further examine in more detail what are the features of the stay of tourists in the considered place for rest, as well as what conditions the guests can offer here.

General information

Hotel "Palazzo Trezzini" as residents of the city and tourists are often attributed to the number of local attractions. Everyone is captivated by its beautiful design, made in the old style. The date of construction of the historical building in which it is located is considered the distant 1723. Since 2013, it began to function as a hotel, offering guests and residents of the city accommodation in luxury apartments.

The hotel administration notes: here tourists can enjoy a high-class vacation, as well as have a great time in the luxurious atmosphere created by the expensive details of the classic interior of the Trezzini Palace Hotel (St. Petersburg).

The hotel in question receives a considerable number of reviews addressed to it by tourists who had a rest in the "Trezzini Palace". Based on them, this place has rather high ratings.

Based on international standards of comfort and level of service, the hotel in question has been given the status of "five stars", which indicates the best living conditions.

Location

A huge number of tourists in their reviews of the Hotel Trezzini Palace notice that it is located in a very convenient place - in the historical district of St. Petersburg. Not far from here you can find excellent catering establishments, as well as decent places for relaxation, supermarkets and even a small market where you can buy national products or interesting souvenirs for loved ones.

The nearest airport is 15 km from Palazzo Trezzini - in Pulkovo. Due to the fact that the distance from the place of arrival of many tourists to the hotel is quite considerable, most travelers prefer to use the paid transfer service, which is carried out on a passenger car equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable trip.

The address of the Trezini Palace Hotel is 21B Universitetskaya Embankment, St. Petersburg . Not far from here is the Vasileostrovskaya metro station, which provides transport links to hotel guests with almost all corners of the huge city.

sights

As mentioned above, the hotel in question is located in the historical district of the cultural capital. This place is surrounded by a huge number of attractions that attract the attention of guests of the city.

Just a 15-minute walk is the world famous St. Isaac's Cathedral, as well as the famous Palace Square. Fans of historical sights, while staying here, can also visit the Nikolaev and Menshikov Palace. For all those who wish to get acquainted with the best objects of architecture, many tourists who have been here are recommended to visit St. Andrew's Cathedral.

As for the most frequently visited sights, among them it is necessary to highlight the Hermitage Museum, the Church of the Savior on Spilled Blood, the Russian Museum, the Mariinsky Theater, as well as the Admiralty. Often, tourists vacationing at the Hotel "Trezzini Palace" purchase excursion tours to the Konstantinovsky Palace, as well as the park in which it is located.

Rooms

In total, the hotel in question has 21 rooms, each of which is arranged taking into account the best conditions of comfort. All of them are divided into three categories: luxury, premier and palazzo. Many tourists who rested here note that, getting into the apartment, you involuntarily begin to feel in the atmosphere of the 18th century, when balls, dinner parties and the like were arranged all around, in general, everything was saturated with the spirit of imperial Petersburg.

All apartments have rich furniture, beautifully complemented by modern technology. As for the interior, it is executed in a classic style, using a huge number of fabrics, expensive wood, as well as painting. By their appearance, the rooms presented are more like palace chambers.

All apartments have a luxurious bathroom, which is equipped with modern plumbing, as well as furniture, which stores bath accessories provided to guests free of charge. Staying here, guests can use hair styling devices, as well as personal hygiene products - they are also provided individually.

Rooms of any category are equipped with air conditioning and also have access to the Internet (Wi-Fi).

Suite

The suites offer stunning views of the Neva River, as well as the hotel courtyard and the vibrant streets of St. Petersburg. Each of them boasts a bright interior, represented by an original combination of expensive fabrics and wood elements made by talented craftsmen by hand.

Luxury rooms are equipped on a large area - from 50 to 60 square meters. m. All of them are divided into two rooms, one of which is a bedroom, and the second - a living room. The total number of beds in apartments of this group is designed for a couple of guests. The walls here are decorated with expensive paintings and murals made by talented artists.

As regards technical equipment, the luxury rooms at the Palazzo Trezzini Hotel have a large plasma TV with satellite channels, scales, a minibar and a safe.

Palazzo

Palazzo category apartments offer vacationers a stay in the richest conditions. The rooms are also designed for a couple of guests. In total, the hotel has three rooms of this category: Reale, Imperiale and Del Presidente. All of them are located on the second floor of a historic building reserved for the hotel, with stunning views of the Neva and the lively promenade.

As for the equipment of the apartments, here a huge bed with expensive Mascioni linen, as well as with down pillows and blankets is presented to the attention of the guests. The interior also contains classic armchairs and sofas, upholstered in expensive fabrics decorated with gold paintings. There is a TV corner in the relaxation area, as well as a safe and a minibar, where water is updated daily.

Staying in rooms of the type in question, tourists can use the photocopier, which is also installed here - guests note that this feature is very convenient for those people who are staying in the hotel on a business trip.

Accommodation with children

The hotel "Palazzo Trezzini" pays special attention to those guests who call with children. In order for certain conditions to be provided for a child in advance, you must first indicate such a need even at the time of booking.

It should be noted that in this hotel, children under the age of 7 can stay for free, but without extra bed. If the child has reached the age of 8, then for him the hotel staff can install an extra bed.

In the local restaurant, visitors with children are offered an excellent menu, which presents dishes prepared exclusively from healthy products.

Nutrition

At the Hotel Trezzini Palace, guests have the opportunity to visit the beautiful restaurant, which is located on the ground floor of its building. He is very fond of visiting not only the guests of the hotel in question, but also ordinary residents of the city. Here, according to the guests, a list of dishes is offered that can surprise even the most sophisticated gourmets. In the restaurant you can try dishes prepared in the best traditions of European and Russian cuisine. There are unique author's dishes from the chef of the institution.

The restaurant "Maria Carlotta" provides hotel guests with room service, which is made upon special requests. Tourists who relax here talk about the fact that this service is carried out at a high level, like everything else that is available here. Moreover, guests are pleased that the service is provided around the clock.
