Nerton 4 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If among all the resorts of the Republic of Turkey for the summer holidays you have chosen Side, but at the same time you want to stay in a comfortable hotel at an affordable price, then we recommend considering Nerton 4 * as an option. What tourists expect here, we learn from this article.





Location and description of the hotel

This hotel is located just one kilometer from Side and four kilometers from the resort village of Kumkoy. The hotel has its own sandy beach. In the vicinity there are many restaurants, cafes, shops, supermarkets, travel agencies, etc. The nearest airport is 62 kilometers away. So upon arrival, you can reach the hotel within an hour.

The housing stock of Nerton Beach Hotel 4 * (Side) is represented by 86 rooms. 83 of them are standard-type apartments, and three are luxury. Each room has a private bathroom, air conditioning, refrigerator, heating system, safe, cable TV, balcony. Cleaning is done on a daily basis.

The hotel has two swimming pools, two restaurants (Turkish and international cuisine), bars, sun terraces, sports grounds, parking, laundry. Nerton Beach Hotel 4 *: reviews of Russian travelers

Impressions of location, own territory and number of rooms

Many travelers found the location of Nerton Beach Hotel 4 * very successful. So, the hotel is located near all major tourist sites. In addition, the hotel‚Äôs own beach is just a couple of tens of meters - you just have to cross the promenade. The hotel territory cannot be called large. Rooms are cleaned every day. Daily, maids add a few bottles of water to the refrigerator. Bed and towels, according to the guests, were changed every other day.





Hotel Nerton 4 *: guest reviews on food

As for the food at Nerton, most of the guests liked it. According to them, there are always meat, chicken, turkey, snacks, salads, fresh vegetables and fruits.
