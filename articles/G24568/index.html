Washer Hotpoint-Ariston ARMXXD 1297: manual, specifications and reviews

How long has mankind ceased to attribute washing machines to luxury goods? Today, this phrase does not surprise even our grandmothers. However, at the initial stage, washing machines still required close attention and inseparable proximity to themselves. But today, the functionality of this household appliance is simply amazing in its diversity, and the housewives can only choose the model they like most.

Recently, the choice of consumers has been increasingly falling on devices that not only wash, but also dry clothes. The range of such devices is gradually expanding. A striking representative of such devices is the Hotpoint Ariston ARMXXD 1297 washer-dryer . This is an Italian-made device.

As for the Hotpoint Ariston ARMXXD 1297 model, it largely meets the needs of even the most picky housewives, since it belongs to washing machines that have a high economic class B in terms of energy consumption. Laundry class - A.

  • setting for the drying function;
  • setting the required temperature;
  • lock and spin mode.

On the display screen, you can view all information about the entered program with a phased reflection of the current functions, including the time remaining until the end of the wash.

Dry Mode

The Hotpoint Ariston ARMXXD 1297 washing machine has two main drying modes, the time in them is set from 40 to 180 minutes:

  1. Getting slightly damp laundry for easy subsequent ironing.
  2. Getting dry laundry for laying in the closet. The longest drying cycle. After it, the clothes are perfectly dry, the mode is suitable for terry towels.

According to user reviews, do not neglect natural drying in the fresh air and use the function of the same name constantly. Firstly, the fibers of the fabric deteriorate, and secondly, it is more difficult to iron over-dried underwear.
