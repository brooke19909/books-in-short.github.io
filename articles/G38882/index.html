How to make a family emblem?



The coat of arms of the family is best done with thick cardboard or multi-colored paper. The base of such an emblem is cut out in the shape of a shield and can be divided by lines into several fields, where you will capture things that are important to you. Choose the base color and other shades with which you will draw the lines. The emblem should be bright, but harmonious. A helmet should rise above the shield: its color will indicate the status of your family (for example, the golden helmet was placed on the coat of arms by the nobles, the silver helmet - less noble families).

On the sides of the emblem, depict the figures that hold it: these can be lions, unicorns, eagles, angels, etc.

A self-made coat of arms of the family should reflect the main desires and goals of each person who lives with you. Here you can draw a large number of miniatures, for example, put the faces of all relatives in one square: this way you will show that the most important thing for you is to be together. In another part, draw sports equipment: this will mean that you love sports. In the third corner, you can draw musical instruments, animals, books, travel, etc., and a certain square will reflect your habits and desires.
