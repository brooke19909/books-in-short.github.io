Hon Rom Sunlight Resort 2 *: hotel reviews

Fans of a relaxing beach holiday should definitely visit the Hon Rom Sunlight Resort 2 * hotel, which is located at: Binh Thuan Province, Vietnam.

The hotel was built recently - in 2014.

Lien Huong Airport is just 88 km away. Nearby, 9 km from the hotel, is the Storm - the center of kiteboarding.

Territory

Hon Rom Sunlight Resort Hotel (Vietnam) is surrounded by a magnificent garden with plants exotic for a Russian person.



The sea is 10 meters from the hotel. It is very convenient that sun loungers with umbrellas are located right on the hotel premises, as the whole territory is paved with tiles, which guarantees protection of things from sand. The staircase that goes down to the sea has only 4 steps.

The seashore is sandy, with many shells, but without stones. The water is cloudy, clear only on the coast, very warm. You can swim safely, because the depth is small and there are no strong waves, since the hotel is hiding behind a ledge in the bay.

Hon Rom Sunlight Resort has a beachfront bar with a variety of refreshments and ice creams.

Living conditions

The hotel is located in one two-story building and has several categories of rooms:

- double rooms with one bed (superior, de luxe) with different views: garden or sea;

- Superior quadruple rooms;

- family apartments.

The total number of rooms in Hon Rom Sunlight Resort 2 * is 69. Each visitor will choose a suitable option. The price for a room is from 1198 rubles.

The cozy rooms have everything for a comfortable stay. All are air-conditioned. Each room has a private bathroom with shower. Free shampoo, shower gel, slippers, hair dryer. You can watch cable TV, which is installed in all rooms.



For the convenience of guests, rooms are equipped with electric kettles. The floors in the rooms are tiled.

Hon Rom Sunlight Resort has a variety of reviews, however, according to many visitors, the rooms are very clean, towels and bed linen are regularly changed.

Nutrition

For breakfast, cooks organize a buffet: omelets, fried eggs, pancakes, juices, fruits, tofu, salads, tea, coffee, cookies and soups.

The reviews are mixed - some speak of a variety of dishes, others, on the contrary, of scarcity.

On request, breakfast can be delivered directly to your room.

Hon Rom Sunlight Resort has its own restaurant where you can taste delicious dishes. The restaurant serves Asian dishes and European national food. Prices are relatively low for a hotel.

You can buy fresh seafood (squid, shrimp), and, at your request, the purchased seafood will be prepared at the hotel, very inexpensively.

What else is noteworthy in the vicinity of Mui Ne

1. The statue of a reclining Buddha on Mount Taku, on the way to the mountain you can visit the Pitahaya plantation (dragon fruit) and Ke Ga lighthouse.



There is a long way from Mui Ne to the mountain, but it is worth it to cover a long distance. You can climb the mountain by cable car or on foot through the wild jungle, but this will take about three hours. Having reached the top, travelers see not only a statue of a reclining Buddha, whose length is 49 meters, but also a pagoda. It offers tourists and pilgrims right on the mountain a restaurant.

Below, near the mountain, a whole complex is built, which includes a garden, parking, souvenir shops.

Ke Ga Lighthouse is famous for being built of French granite, and its height is 64 meters above sea level.

2. White dunes. A trip to them can be combined with a visit to the red canyon, red dunes, as well as the lotus lake.

The most famous place in Mui Ne is the White Dunes, the landscape of which is similar to the Sahara desert.

3. The city of Phan Thiet. This is a modern Vietnamese city in which tourists mainly visit the supermarket. But you can just walk around the city or visit the sights, which include the water tower, the temple of the whale, the Ho Chi Minh Museum, etc.

Do not expect grandiose impressions from Mui Ne and Fantiet. Since you chose Hon Rom Sunlight Resort (Phan Thiet), it means that the main purpose of the trip is a beach holiday, and not sightseeing tours.
