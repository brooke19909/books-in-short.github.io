Program for creating patterns for cross stitch

Embroidery is an art that can depict anything. The same picture, photograph, drawing, but much more appreciated due to the fact that it requires much more strength for the embodiment. It happens that you want to embroider something, but there are no suitable schemes on the Internet. Especially when I decided to embroider a photo. But this is such a great gift. Soul and heart are enclosed in it, if you are given this miracle from a floss, it means that you are very appreciated and respected. A self-made present was always valued more than what was bought. A program for creating patterns for embroidery comes to the rescue! It is not in vain that they say that it is not so difficult to embroider a picture, how to find a good pattern.

What is the essence of these programs and how do they work?

The bottom line is simple, you just upload the necessary photo and get a scheme by which you can create. Choose how it will look. It can be symbols, signs, each denotes a certain color. In general, the same as in the usual schematic drawing. If the finished result does not have many colors of different shades, then you can make the scheme more visual. Each box will indicate what color the future cross should be.

The program for creating cross-stitch patterns indicates which article has a certain color of some popular mouline thread firms. Used such as Anchor, DMC, Coats and others. This is very convenient in that you do not have to run through all the hardware stores in search of suitable shades. You immediately know what you need. It is advisable to use the recommended companies, because they create high-quality material. This saves you from problems such as tangling and tearing.

You yourself choose the number of colors that should be used, their smoothing and image size.

The program for creating embroidery patterns can be paid, free, work online on the Internet or require downloading to a computer.

When choosing an image to create embroidery, consider several factors:

  1. There should be high quality and image resolution.
  2. Skip faded or too dark areas, as usually floss threads are bright colors.
  3. You should not choose a too bright original, which is already ‚Äúpainful‚Äù to look at, hurts your eyes.
  4. Keep a balance of colors. Everything should be even.
  5. Too many-colored images require many different shades and colors to get a decent result.
  6. If you are not sure that you have the patience to embroider the big picture, then do not choose a photograph with a lot of small details as the basis.
