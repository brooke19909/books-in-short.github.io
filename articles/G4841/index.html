Connection scheme

The set-top box "Rostelecom" is connected using an HDMI cable to the TV. An Ethernet cable is connected to the LAN connector , connecting the set-top box with the router. The set-top box "Rostelecom" comes with a power cable that connects to the network.

In the absence of an HDMI connector on the TV, the connection to the IPTV set-top box is carried out using the AV adapter. A special adapter for the SCART connector is also supplied.

Connecting a second set-top box

If there are two televisions in the house, it is advisable to buy two sets of equipment, since connecting the equipment to one set-top box will allow you to broadcast the same channel simultaneously on both TVs. Independent broadcasting is possible only with different consoles.

Both consoles can be connected to one router, since it has 4 LAN outputs. For example, the first TV can be connected to the first set-top box, which is connected by cable to the LAN1 port, the second TV, respectively, to the second set-top box and LAN2 port. The Ethernet cable supplied with Rostelecom equipment may not be long enough, so it is advisable to purchase a wire of suitable length in advance.

Two simultaneously working TV set-top boxes require a good Internet connection, since the traffic they use increases at times, which can provoke both interference in broadcasting and Internet malfunctions.
