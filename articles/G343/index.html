Nox Inn Beach Resort & Spa, Turkey: hotel description, reviews

Nox Inn Beach Resort & Spa is a fairly young resort hotel whose history dates back to 2006. Far from the bustle of the city, surrounded by natural beauties, next to the azure coast, you can enjoy a Mediterranean holiday. And this picture is complemented by quality service and an elegant interior.

Basic hotel information

Resort Nox Inn Beach Resort & Spa will allow its guests to immerse themselves in the unforgettable atmosphere of azure beaches and quality service. Here you can fully feel the meaning of the word "comfort". At your service are swimming pools, water rides, as well as many other pleasures that can be considered integral attributes of relaxation.

The rooms at Nox Inn Beach Resort & Spa deserve all praise. No matter which apartment you live in, from everywhere you will have a gorgeous view of the sea or the green garden. In addition, you will be pleased with the interior of the room and the number of amenities presented in it.

Everyone who has come to Turkey to rest is advised to get acquainted with its cuisine. The restaurants of Nox Inn Beach Resort & Spa will kindly provide you with this opportunity. And so that hearty dinners do not have a detrimental effect on the figure, be sure to visit the gym and take part in sporting events.

Hotel complex rooms

Hotel Nox Inn Beach Resort & Spa 5 * has 158 rooms, which can accommodate a maximum of 480 people. Most of the hotel has standard apartments, which are one spacious room, namely a bedroom.

Family category rooms are suitable for large companies or those tourists who are used to traveling with their loved ones. There are two rooms here - for adults and for children. An additional convenience is the fact that each of the bedrooms has a private bathroom.

Two rooms are specially equipped for guests with disabilities. For convenience, they are located on the ground floor. It is important that all furniture and other equipment fully meet the needs of the disabled.

Hotel Policies

Tourists coming to rest at the Nox Inn Beach Resort & Spa (Turkey) must follow a number of established rules regarding accommodation:

  • the estimated hours on the day of arrival and departure are assigned, respectively, at 2 pm and at noon;
  • children under 11 years old stay in the room free of charge without extra bed;
  • there is no charge for a crib for babies under 2 years old;
  • in the room it is possible to install no more than 1 crib, and for adult tourists extra beds are not provided;
  • You cannot bring any pets to the hotel;
  • for the services provided, you can pay with a plastic card according to the international payment system;
  • it is strictly forbidden to visit restaurants and the dining room in swimsuits and other beachwear;
  • all hotel pools are open until 19:00, after which swimming in them is not allowed;
  • the all-inclusive system for beverages is valid from 10 a.m. to 11 a.m.
  • for using the safe, which is installed in the room, you have to pay 1 euro per day.
