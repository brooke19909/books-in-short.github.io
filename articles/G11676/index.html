Krasnoufimsk hotels: addresses, services, reviews

Krasnoufimsk is one of the cities of the Sverdlovsk region. About forty thousand people live here. This place has ancient buildings, interesting monuments, a park, a cathedral and other attractions. If you decide to come here, you can stay in hotels. We invite you to get acquainted with the best of them and make your choice in advance.

Krasnoufimsk hotels: advantages

Despite the fact that the city is small, tourists can easily find comfortable housing. Of course, each place has its own differences, but there are also enough features that unite them. We list them:

  • reasonable prices;
  • friendly and quality service;
  • daily housekeeping;
  • providing guests with additional services;
  • booking rooms in advance;
  • cozy rooms, which are provided with everything necessary for living;
  • the presence of a parking zone.

Now let's get acquainted with the individual features of Krasnoufimsk hotels.

"The ark"

It is located near the city center. But this is not the only advantage of the Ark Hotel. About twenty dozens of cozy and comfortable rooms are provided for customers. Prices are quite affordable. Laundry facilities are also available. The rooms have refrigerators, televisions, comfortable beds and much more.

Address: Spartak street, 10.

"On the Volga"

A few years ago a modern hotel was opened in Krasnoufimsk, which is called "On the Volga". There are rooms of different classes for a large number of people. All of them are equipped with a bathroom, refrigeration equipment, a berth, as well as a working area. Among the additional services, customers are provided with:

  • Guarded parking;
  • wireless Internet;
  • the presence of a dining room;
  • satellite television

Location: Volzhskaya, 8.

"Homeland"

The review of Krasnoufimsk hotels continues another very worthy place. Hotel Rodina is located next to the train station. The rooms meet the highest requirements of the guests. There are rooms here:

  • single;
  • double rooms;
  • triple;
  • luxury.

Address: Rogozinnikov street, 14.

"On the hill"

Not far from the city of Krasnoufimsk there is a hotel complex. At the service of clients:

  • seventeen fully equipped rooms;
  • Russian sauna;
  • billiards;
  • sauna;
  • parking area;
  • round-the-clock cafe where you can always buy hot food.
