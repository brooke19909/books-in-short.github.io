Hotel Blue Sea Interpalace 4 * (Spain / Tenerife): photos, reviews

An unforgettable vacation will give travelers Blue Sea Interpalace 4 *. Here you can enjoy the warm sea, mild climate, a lot of attractions and quality service.





Location

The luxurious resort Blue Sea Interpalace 4 * is located in Puerto de la Cruz, on the island of Tenerife, which is especially popular among travelers. It is only 20 minutes from the international airport. Also, having traveled about 40 minutes, you can visit the Teide National Park. The hotel is only 2 km from the central part of the resort.

What to visit

If you like excursions, a accommodation option like Blue Sea Interpalace 4 * is suitable for you. Spain, Tenerife is a resort rich in attractions. So, the closest to the hotel are the following tourist sites:

  • Botanical Garden (0.4 km);

  • Martianez shopping center (0.7 km);

  • complex of artificial lakes (0.9 km);

  • Puerto de la Cruz Casino (0.9 km);

  • the central square of the Palace-Charco (1.5 km);

  • Taoro Park (1.5 km);

  • Hardin Beach (2.5 km);

  • Loro Park (3 km).

Guest rooms

The Blue Sea Interpalace 4 * will provide its guests with comfortable rooms. Spain will seem even more attractive to you if you admire it from the windows of rooms in the following categories:

  • Standard rooms are available in double and triple versions. In addition to beds, there are cozy soft chairs for relaxing. A workplace is also provided. From the balcony you can enjoy views of the city and the park area.
  • Superior rooms consist of two rooms. The bedroom has orthopedic beds and dressing tables. The living room is presented with upholstered furniture and a dining table. Also feature of the room can be considered a sea view from the windows and from the balconies.

It is worth noting the sophisticated design of the Blue Sea Interpalace 4 * apartment. Photos demonstrate a laconic design, done in warm, soothing colors.

Additional data

A number of additional information should be presented before a trip to the resort complex Hotel Interpalace by Blue Sea 4 *. Reviews focus on the following points:

  • Check-in of newly arrived guests in the rooms is from 14:00 to midnight;
  • evictions are held from 07:00 until noon;
  • kids up to 2 years old stay in rooms for free with the possibility of providing cots;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • it is possible to pay with plastic cards for the services received;
  • for corporate bookings of 6 rooms or more, discounts are possible;
  • To confirm the reservation of the room, guests are required to make a prepayment in non-cash form within 5 business days;
  • a prerequisite for registration is the provision of an identity card with a photograph;

  • it is important to confirm the need for a transfer before arrival by calling the administrator;

Negative reviews

Tourists should be prepared for a number of negative moments that they may encounter at the Blue Sea Interpalace 4 * hotel (Tenerife). Reviews pay attention to the following facts:

  • old and shabby furniture in the rooms;
  • Wireless Internet is intermittent (this also applies to a paid signal);
  • every day the breakfast menu is repeated;
  • if you are resting on an all-inclusive system, then an unpleasant surprise for you will be that drinks are not served during dinner (they can be obtained only for an additional fee);
  • the adjacent territory is very small (almost absent);
  • the staff does not speak Russian, which makes it difficult to resolve organizational issues;
  • the hotel is far from the beach;
  • paid use of the safe;
  • windows of rooms located on the lower floors overlook the fence or wall of a neighboring hotel;
  • old carpet, which is dotted with stains;
  • poor insulation, due to which you will hear all the noise from the neighboring rooms, as well as from the corridor;
  • gray, washed towels with holes;
  • old small TVs that work with interference;
  • on the beach - dark sand that sticks to a swimsuit and stains it;
  • most plates in a restaurant with chips;
  • there is no private beach, and on the municipal sunbeds and umbrellas are paid;
  • hotel employees are forbidden to jump into the pool;
  • through the ventilation, smells of food from the restaurant penetrate into the room (and this problem is present in all rooms, regardless of where they are located);
  • uncomfortable road to the sea (you have to overcome many steps);
  • elevators do not work well (it is faster and safer to climb stairs on foot);
  • the hotel does not have its own gym (guests can visit the fitness centers of neighboring institutions, but this service will be paid for them).

The hotel described above provides travelers with all the opportunities for a good rest. And the location on a hill provides beautiful views. Nevertheless, such a dislocation has its drawbacks.
