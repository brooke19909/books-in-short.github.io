Hotel Fiore Resort Healthy 4 * (Vietnam, Phan Thiet): photos and reviews of tourists

Phan Thiet Province of Vietnam has established itself as a quiet, tranquil resort with sandy beaches, an abundance of palm trees and pine trees that are so dear to Russian tourists. Thanks to the gentle entrance to the sea, it is great for families with children, and the presence of ‚Äúgood‚Äù waves has made it a favorite place for surfers.



Rest in Phan Thiet

This resort is rightfully considered one of the best in Vietnam. On its coast, drowning in vegetation, problems and city bustle are easily forgotten. The coastline stretches from Phan Thiet to the Mui Ne Peninsula and is more than twenty kilometers long. And along the sea, a chain of hotels are located, which are mainly of a three- or four-star level.

It is not only quiet, but also safe, especially for children. Fine golden sand with a gentle entrance create excellent conditions for relaxation. And thanks to the sand dunes and peninsulas overlooking the sea, Phan Thiet has its own special microclimate. Here the weather is good all year round, and rains are extremely rare. The exceptions are July and August.

Phan Thiet Hotels

Almost all the hotels located on the first line have not only territory fenced off from the outside world, but also private beaches equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds, which are issued to residents free of charge. Each hotel is distinguished by its architectural features and landscape design. Most of them are built on the principle of a villa or a bungalow, although sometimes there are hull buildings, however, no higher than three floors. All hotels are buried in thickets of coconut palms, pines and tropical vegetation, allowing tourists to forget about the outside world and enjoy their vacation in isolation.



Phan Thiet tour operators are positioned as one of the best options for spending holidays with children. This resort is a cross between busy Nha Trang and more than calm Fukuoka. Most of its hotels are located in a common, fairly long bay.

The resort is suitable for families with young children for several reasons. Firstly, almost all hotels have a large territory and all the infrastructure necessary for children. In addition, within walking distance there are many shops and cafes.

Many hotels offer the concept of All Inclusive, so beloved by the Russians, but in some of them tourists receive only breakfast. The four-star Fiore Resort Healthy also works on this system.

Description

This resort is ten kilometers from the famous Ke Ga Lighthouse. Phan Thiet resort center is a ten-minute drive away. The hotel is built on the first line, a hundred meters from the sea. Nearby, almost within walking distance, there are many shops, cafes and entertainment venues. Three kilometers from the Fiore Healthy Resort are excellent golf courses. The hotel is located about four hours drive from Ho Chi Minh City, where the nearest airport is. Guests can relax and unwind while enjoying both first-class service and a wide range of services at Fiore Healthy Resort.

The hotel has its own garden, a large landscaped area, fully planted with exotic plants. There are two small artificial lakes with fish that children will love. There is a bus stop near the entrance , from which every half an hour a shuttle departs.

Infrastructure

Hotel Fiore Healthy Resort 4 * (Vietnam) was built in 2010. In 2014, it was undergoing major repairs, furniture and plumbing were replaced, new services were added. The infrastructure includes a business center, a banquet and conference room, in which it is convenient to hold corporate events, there is also a large secure parking.



The 24-hour reception is located in the administrative building. Here you can rent a car or motobike, buy a sightseeing tour. From Fiore Resort Healthy 4 * three times a day a free shuttle to the city is organized. The nearest hotels are two kilometers away. Fiore Resort Healthy provides laundry facilities and luggage storage. Those who wish can purchase tickets in any direction, as well as use an ATM. There is also a hairdresser, laundry, currency exchange, concierge services, ironing and shoe shine. A daily press is available in the lobby daily.

Housing stock

The hotel is considered small in size. It has forty rooms of the following categories: standard, deluxe and deluxe with views of the ocean or the internal garden. All are equipped in villas and bungalows. The hotel has a maximum of three floors, no elevators.

The rooms have all the amenities: new furniture - beds, luggage rack, wardrobe, plasma TV, dressing table, upholstered furniture. Blackout curtains on the windows so that children can sleep peacefully during the day. The rooms have balconies or terraces. There is straw furniture on the lawns in front of the bungalow. Here you can drink coffee or tea, enjoying the silence and the surrounding nature, especially the singing of birds.

In addition, there is a paid minibar and a safe. Air conditioning is manually controlled, and Wi-Fi is free.

The design of the rooms is a cross between European and national styles. In the combined bathrooms, a new, well-functioning plumbing, non-slip floors. Superior rooms have a jacuzzi, while others have bathtubs and showers. The ceilings of the bathrooms are glass.



Fiore Resort Healthy offers orthopedic mattresses and great bedding. On the floor - ceramic or marble tile flooring. Many of the floor-to-ceiling rooms offer amazing ocean views. The interior design of the rooms meets the international standards of hotels of this category.

For children

And although the Fiore Healthy Resort (4 *) is somewhat distant from the airport, due to which the transfer is quite long - four hours, however, there are plenty of children at the hotel. Many attribute this to the fact that Phan Thiet has excellent roads, and a long drive to the hotel is easy for young tourists.

For them, the Fiore Healthy Resort, reviews of which vary somewhat on some issues, provides a playground, there is also a shallow compartment in the pool. Parents can get a cot in the room upon request, as well as a high chair in the restaurant.



Beach

The hotel is built on the first line. It has a private sandy beach, which has all the necessary amenities. Umbrellas and sunbeds for guests staying at the Fiore Resort Healthy are free. Here, on the shore, vacationers are offered a wide range of water activities.

The entrance to the sea is gentle, so there is no need to bring special rubber shoes to Vietnam. Tour operators warn: if a red flag is set on the beach, it is better not to enter the water, because the waves are very high.

Entertainment

The hotel has a fairly large freshwater pool. Around are loungers and mattresses. It is here that vacationers prefer to spend time swimming and sunbathing when the ocean is turbulent. There is also a bar.

Fans of a healthy lifestyle will love massage and spa treatments, for which you need to pay extra. Those who wish can play billiards by renting a table; Do yoga with an instructor. You can rent a bike or motobike and drive around the resort, for example, to the dunes and the lotus lake, to Mount Taka.



Many Russians leave 4 * reviews after arriving from Fiore Healthy Resort. In them, they strongly advise visiting a crocodile farm, alpine Dalat and the island of Vinpearl, which can be reached by cable car over the sea.
