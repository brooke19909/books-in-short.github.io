Novotel Phuket Vintage Park (Phuket): description, service, reviews

Thailand is a country that tourists of different age categories who adore beautiful nature and wonderful weather conditions will prefer to visit. Travelers from Russia often come here in the winter months to bask in the sun, while in their native lands frost and snow sweep.

As for the monthly weather in Phuket - one of the most popular tourist destinations in Thailand - the most successful months for a vacation here are from November to the end of April. It was at this time that the air warms up perfectly, the water near the coast becomes warm, its temperature keeps in the region of +23 ... + 25 degrees, which is quite comfortable for swimming. From May to September, the real rainy season in Phuket begins. It is accompanied not only by heavy rainfall, but also by the presence of cooler air masses on the island. However, there are some tourists who prefer to relax here just in such a period. Experienced tourists nevertheless recommend, before visiting Phuket, the weather for months to be studied in sufficient detail for months - this is necessary in order to be in the desired temperature conditions.

One of the most popular hotels in this island resort region of Thailand is Novotel Phuket Vintage Park 4 *. His description, presented by various travel agencies, impresses many and makes him want to be in the luxurious conditions that he offers to all vacationers.
