About paid medicine

The salary of a doctor in Russia ranges around fifteen thousand. This applies to state clinics and hospitals. This figure is given for cities with a population of over one million. That is, a sufficiently large city, a high standard of living and income, far from rural areas, and at the same time, the salary of such a valuable shot is less than that of a loader at the station. Amazing country. Work in a paid medical center is much more profitable. In such centers, it ranges from 30,000 to 50,000. But the requirements for such vacancies are higher than in municipal structures. Certifications, decent work experience, residency documents and much more are needed. In addition, the doctor of the commercial clinic must fulfill the plan for prescribed tests, treatments, and the average size of the check. Private clinics do not work to improve public health, but to increase the profits of their organization.
