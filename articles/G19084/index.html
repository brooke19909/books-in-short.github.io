Hotel Blue Sky City Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece / Rhodes): reviews, photos

The luxurious resort complex Blue Sky City Beach Hotel 4 * will provide travelers with an unforgettable Greek vacation. This is a unique symbiosis of local traditions and modern standards of service. Built in 1969, the hotel still does not lose popularity. Renovation, dated 2006, brought the interior and architecture of the institution in line with modern trends.

Special offers

For guests of the Blue Sky City Beach Hotel 4 * resort complex, a number of special offers have been developed that make the vacation not only enjoyable, but also more profitable. It is about the following:

  • During the New Year and Christmas holidays, guests receive a 25% discount on standard room rates. Also, one guest is entitled to a one-time massage session.
  • Between April and May, a 20% discount on accommodation and all related services is provided.
  • When booking a room for at least 3 nights, guests are entitled to free use of wireless Internet and a gym. A 15-minute massage is also included.
  • In the autumn, when booking a room for at least 4 nights, guests can use Wi-Fi for free. When checking in, a basket of fruits is provided as a compliment, and there is also the possibility of a late check out.

Helpful information

It is worth familiarizing yourself with a number of useful information before going to the Blue Sky City Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews especially note such data:

  • check-in at the hotel is from 14:00 to 04:00, and you need to leave the room on the day of departure before noon;
  • in order to prevent material damage to the hotel on your part, you must leave a cash deposit during registration;
  • check-in is impossible without providing an identity card with a photograph;
  • self-registration is possible only from the age of 18;
  • children can stay in the room free of charge (provided that they are sharing the same beds with parents);
  • pets are not allowed;
  • cash payments may not exceed 1,500 euros (local law requirement);
  • children under 13 years old cannot use the pool on their own;
  • under 18 years old, it is forbidden to visit the spa area;
  • visiting the massage parlor is possible only by appointment;
  • late check out costs 30 euros;
  • the fee for using the safe in the room is 20 euros (regardless of the length of stay);
  • the cost of breakfast is 6 euros;
  • an hour of using wireless Internet in public areas will cost guests 2 euros.

Negative reviews

Not only positive moments, but also a number of disappointments are accompanied by a vacation in the Blue Sky City Beach Hotel 4 * (Rhodes). Reviews indicate its following shortcomings:

  • Wi-Fi is paid throughout the entire territory (even in the lobby area, where the use of this service is usually free);
  • the pool is small and there are few sun loungers around it (moreover, this territory is in the shade for most of the day);
  • the price does not include drinks;
  • paid sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach (8 euros per day);
  • for the entire period of rest, breakfasts are not updated, but because they quickly get bored;
  • the impression of a panoramic view of the sea is spoiled due to the roadway passing nearby, which makes a lot of noise;
  • the hotel has a lot of noisy youth, whose behavior the administration does not react in any way;
  • maids do not clean in good faith;
  • the hotel does not have its own parking, and the nearest public parking is paid;

  • a tiny shower, which is why during bathing guests constantly touch the walls and the plastic curtain, which is not only unpleasant, but also unhygienic;
  • old miniature TV with terrible picture and sound quality;
  • the external decoration of the hotel and the quality of service does not reach 4 stars;
