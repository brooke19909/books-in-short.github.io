When you need to go to Anapa on vacation

In resorts, prices are largely dependent on the season. But if all over the world the highest limit of the cost of rental housing coincides with the tourist peak, then in Russia this excitement falls on the summer months. More precisely, in July and August. But everyone says that in the Black Sea resorts of the Caucasus it is best in September. But guest houses in Anapa - near the sea or at a distance from it - are aimed at summer tourists. They begin to receive visitors, as a rule, from the first of June. Prices are the lowest this month. And after an unprecedented leap (sometimes twice), they return to their minimum in September. According to many vacationers, the first month of autumn is the best time to travel to the resort. There is no stuffy heat, a warm sea, a lot of fruit, few holidaymakers and the most optimal prices.
