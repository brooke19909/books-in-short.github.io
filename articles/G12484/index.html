About the resort

Tourists who were fortunate enough to visit the Argo boarding house (Abkhazia), reviews about the resort leave extremely positive.

Gudauta is a climatic resort in Abkhazia located on the shore of the Gudauta Bay. 77 km is separated from it by Adler. At 44 km from the resort is Sukhumi. The history of the region tells that Gudauta has always been in the shadow of other famous Abkhaz resorts. Visitors to Gudauta have private guest houses at their disposal, as well as actively providing housing in the private sector. The cost of rest in Gudauta is much lower than in other resorts of Abkhazia.
