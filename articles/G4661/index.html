You can carry small photo frames with you: photos of your favorite people will always be there.

This is where progress has come: photo frames have been created in which you can store and view a huge number of photos.

Digital photo frames: overview

To view photos on the display, you do not need either a computer or a laptop. Today, a digital photo frame will handle this. At the very frame, the amount of internal memory is small - and this is not required. They provide the opportunity to enjoy photos from any other medium on which they are stored. It can be a memory card, USB. Therefore, internal memory up to 512 MB is enough.

When choosing a product, you need to pay attention to the size of the screen: the frames are available in conventional and widescreen. This is important to ensure that the parameter matches the apparatus with which pictures are taken. If the format does not match, then the photos may be cropped - they simply will not fit. But with a small image that will not be enough for the whole screen, black bars will appear on the sides.





Diagonal frames

Digital photo frame is available with different display sizes. It can be very small and be only one inch, or maybe 19 inches diagonally. The most popular are photo frames whose display size reaches 8 inches. On them, the picture quality is optimal, and the size is familiar to the perception.

You should pay attention to such an indicator as the brightness and contrast of the image. The average brightness is 200-300 cd / m2 (light intensity for the display). Image quality is important during viewing. At low brightness, it will be dark photos, poorly viewed plots. This is simply unacceptable with digital devices - poor quality remains with analog gadgets.





Contrast and Resolution

There is also such an indicator as contrast, indicating the ratio of dark and light areas of the image. Attention should be paid to this indicator: it should not be lower than 200: 1. The display resolution indicator plays an important role when choosing a photo frame. The higher this indicator, the better the image.

As statistics show, there is another indicator that is important to consider when choosing digital photo frames; reviews speak about this quite definitely. This is the viewing angle. After all, it is worth a little deviate from it - the image is changing, getting worse. A viewing angle of 170 degrees vertically, and horizontally up to 180 is a good indicator.

All of the above indicators must be taken into account when buying this gadget: then viewing photos will be a real pleasure.





Case and its parameters

The case is usually made of plastic. It also surrounds the display, which can be replaced if necessary. On the body are located and control buttons, slots for connectors. Some manufacturers have switched to touch screens to reduce the number of buttons. But digital photo frames, reviews, about which enthusiastic people write - these are frames that do not have a touch screen. And the point is not only that the cost of this increases: such screens have worse image quality, especially in the sun.

All photo frames have slots for USB ports, and they can be connected to computers, headphones, and a USB flash drive. It is desirable that the power be mixed: both from the battery and from the mains.





Additional options

In addition to the usual functions, digital photo frames are equipped with additional ones. They have the option of an alarm clock, hours, different timer modes. There are frames that can only be used with the remote control. Texet Digital Photo Frame is one of the popular models for this product.

Thanks to additional functions, they can be configured as desired: upon returning home, the frame itself will turn on at the specified time. By previewing, you can select the desired photo or start the slide mode. A zoom function is necessarily provided. It serves to enlarge the image on the screen. The frame can be rotated - the image rotates with it - the rotation function is responsible for this. This is a very convenient feature that many cameras, phones, iPhones and tablets are equipped with.





In addition to photos, it is possible to watch videos: when choosing, you need to pay attention to this indicator. Of course, photo frames with such functions will cost much more. But if the budget allows and you want to have such a device, you can spend a large amount. Their cost today ranges from 1750 to 5500 rubles.
