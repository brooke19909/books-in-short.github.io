Precautionary measures

It is important to understand that any technical device should be used exclusively in accordance with the recommendations and instructions that are attached to each product. If all the conditions are met, then any quality product will not lose its power and will delight the owners with quality work. Therefore, it is extremely important to familiarize yourself with the basic precautions that were indicated by the manufacturer, including the instructions for the Liebher refrigerator.

First of all, it is important to note the location of the device. It must not be near the hob or other electrical appliances that may cause automatic defrosting.



It is not recommended to have a refrigerator by the window if it is the sunny side. The exposure to ultraviolet rays increases the load on a single compressor and makes it work for wear, which can also adversely affect the condition of the technical device. It is preliminary recommended that you carefully think over the place where it will be located, since inaccurate movement can damage the mechanism.

Modern models do not differ in one aspect from ancient refrigerators - it is forbidden to put hot food on the shelves, as this will also lead to defrosting and spoilage of products. Also, the instructions for the Liebher refrigerator indicate that in order to prevent premature failure of the device due to an unstable power supply, the consumer must provide protection. For these purposes, it is best to connect the appliance through a voltage regulator.
