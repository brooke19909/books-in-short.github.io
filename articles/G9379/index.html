History of the term





The flagship phone is an already well-established phrase. Interestingly, the first part of it was taken, oddly enough, from the maritime industry. It would seem, what does this have to do with electronic technology, and even more so with mobile equipment? But there are reasons for this, which are quite easily explained using elementary logic. So, in the Navy it is customary to say the word ‚Äúflagship‚Äù when they mean a ship on board of which there is a commander in chief. Thus, it is there that the headquarters and command post are located. In fact, this unit is responsible for global communication and synchronization of actions between all other units. Separate combat units were also controlled directly from the flagship. Let's try to apply this semantic meaning to our technique. It is easy to guess that in this case we will call the flagship the device on which, in a sense, all the other devices are equal. At the same time, such smartphones at some point are the most beautiful and powerful in terms of filling creations of the manufacturer. Thus, we can draw a direct and easy conclusion that any large model of a mobile company can be called flagship.

How do flagship cell phones differ?

So, what did we all find out earlier? It turned out that the flagship phone is a device that can be called advanced in terms of the technologies used in it. This is indirectly confirmed by official sales data. They differ from ordinary models in advanced hardware and a set of several chips that automatically turn a smartphone into a very interesting device. But different companies put their creations as a flagship on the mobile phone market. And how to understand which of them will win in this unspoken battle? In this case, the title should go to that model, which, in comparison with competitors, will have the most powerful hardware, a more optimized software part and, of course, a better screen.
