Connection with esotericism

The very concept of "Shambhala" is inseparable from esotericism. Initially, this ancient country existed in Hinduism, and many ideas were taken from there. Classical Hinduism linked Shambhala with the new avatar of Vishnu - Kalka, which is due to be born soon. Over time, the concept and awareness of Shambhala has changed, but today only Blavatsky has managed to at least partially reflect the concept of a wonderful place .



According to Mahabharata, the location of Shambhala cannot be where caste division exists. Thus, it should be a territory where Vedic traditions are respected, and the society is controlled by the priests of the brahmanas. Following this description, it can be assumed that Shambhala should be sought in the Vedic Semirechye - the place where Brahminism originated and spread to all of India.
