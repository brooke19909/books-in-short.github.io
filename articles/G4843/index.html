Handheld vacuum cleaner: model overview, specifications, rating, reviews

Stylish hand-held vacuum cleaners of all shapes, capacities and sizes have flooded the markets, but the consumer needs just the one who does the job in a quality manner. The right choice of vacuum cleaner is crucial in creating the comfort of a modern home.

A vacuum cleaner is a device that is now taken for granted, but this has not always been the case.

History says that the first attempts to create a vacuum cleaner were made in England in 1599.

In June 1869, Chicago inventor Ives McGaffey developed and patented a sweeper. McGaffey was not driven, cleaned carpets with a hand pump and was the first analogue of vacuum cleaners in the United States. The vacuum cleaner was made of wood and canvas and was called the "Whirlwind".

John Thurman invented the first gasoline-powered vacuum cleaner in 1899. He is considered the first invention for a motorized vacuum cleaner. In the UK, Hubert Cecil Booth worked on his own version of a motorized vacuum cleaner and patented it on August 30, 1901. This car was so big that it rode on a horse-drawn carriage, worked on gasoline and consisted of long hoses that ran into the room through the windows.

A manual vacuum cleaner for the home was very bulky, noisy and inefficient. The first disposable vacuum cleaner bag was made in Toledo, Ohio in 1920. A bag with a filter fiber was thrown away after removal from the vacuum cleaner.

Vacuum cleaners continue to improve and adapt using new technologies. The inventor of the first vacuum cleaner probably did not think that a modern sweeper would look like the photo.

Types of Dust Collectors

Currently, vacuum cleaners are divided into two types: a manual vacuum cleaner from the network and a cordless one on batteries. The principle of operation of network vacuum cleaners is very simple and affordable even for a child. The plug is inserted into the outlet, the ‚ÄúStart‚Äù is turned on, and the cleaning process begins.

The main advantage of wired vacuum cleaners is unlimited working time and power from 1200 to 2200 watts. The price depends on the brand of the vacuum cleaner, its configuration and functionality. On average, it ranges from 12,000 to 30,000 rubles.

Such a vacuum cleaner is not always convenient. It is not safe to pull the cord around the room, especially when minor children live in the house, they can catch the cord and get injured. It also cannot be used outdoors, for example, when it is necessary to clean garden furniture.

In the case of such cleaning, cordless vacuum cleaners have an indisputable advantage . They are not tied to electricity, they allow you to freely move around the room and clean things that are far away, these can be seats or mats in the car. They are not as efficient in power as wired, they work for no more than 60 minutes, nevertheless they are in demand among their customers. They are used in small areas and for cleaning surfaces outside residential premises.

With a device not tied to an outlet, cleaning takes less time, and most of them are great for use in cars, which makes them even more versatile.

Hand vacuum cleaner rating

Half the effort when vacuuming is spent constantly connecting and disconnecting the vacuum cleaner to get to the right place. Cordless vacuums do not have these drawbacks.

Instead of doing a large cleaning every week, it is easier to quickly and immediately clean dirty stains and remove spilled crumbs after dinner so that they do not spread throughout the house. A good cordless vacuum cleaner is expensive, and their battery life is different. The most powerful ones last about 60 minutes, and the less powerful ones last 20 minutes, and the buyer will have to make this choice between cost, cleaning efficiency and time.
