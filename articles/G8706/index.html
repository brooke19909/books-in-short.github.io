History of the formation of the resort

In this regard, Sheregesh has nothing to surprise: an ordinary village, which was previously built for workers who worked in iron mines. Its history began in the 50s of the 20th century. Shortly after the construction of the village, a ski complex was built next to it, which was intended for the sports day, and this was in 1981. And only at the dawn of the new millennium did the development of the resort begin. And now modern tourists have at their disposal a high-class ski resort, not inferior to European counterparts. Skiers liked the Sheregesh resort, where there is not only a sports complex, but also numerous slopes.

Variety of slopes

Ski slopes of various difficulty levels will amaze both beginners and professionals. In total, the resort has about 15 different slopes for ski slopes. If you add together the length of each of them, then as a result, visitors to Sheregesh will be met by more than 20 kilometers of high-class slopes.
