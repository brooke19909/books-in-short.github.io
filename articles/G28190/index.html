Belaya Rus (Tuapse) - reviews of vacationers



At the end of the last century, or rather, in 1993, the Belaya Rus sanatorium was opened. The sanatorium is located in one of the most ecologically clean areas of our country, which has a wonderful microclimate, near the city of Tuapse, and is a wonderful place for treatment and relaxation of visiting people all year round.

The popular wellness center, known as the Belaya Rus sanatorium (Tuapse), whose reviews are called there, has well-appointed rooms, from whose windows a lovely view of the sea and mountains opens. Each private room has air conditioning, a telephone, a fridge and a safety deposit box. The rooms are single and double, in total there are more than two hundred and fifty of them in the sanatorium.

The food here is very good and great even for those on a diet. A huge selection of different dishes, both ordinary and diet, is represented by the buffet system, however, there is the opportunity to eat to order. Sanatorium "Belaya Rus" (Tuapse) pays great attention to baby food. The children's menu includes only healthy products necessary for the growth of the child, contributing to his healthy development.



Many vacationers like the dishes offered by the Belaya Rus sanatorium (Tuapse). Guest reviews are especially noted by two restaurants located on the territory of the complex. In one of them they prepare dishes of European cuisine, in the other - oriental. In the evenings, the restaurant organizes leisure activities for vacationers.

The medical building of the sanatorium leaves only positive impressions. Everything is thought out in it to the smallest detail, therefore, the convenience and comfort of tourists is provided here. The polyclinic of the diagnostic and treatment center has all the medical departments in which exclusively highly qualified specialists work, and only imported equipment is used.

Almost all existing diseases are treated here, all kinds of diagnostics are carried out. Those who at least once rested or were treated here again want to return to here - to the resort "Belaya Rus" (Tuapse).

The reviews of vacationers about the mud baths of the sanatorium are inspiring, they are quite pleasant to listen to. They say that they use mud from the Anapa and Pyatigorsk deposits, which are useful for diseases of the nervous system, skin diseases and many others. The staff of the hospital is famous for its attentiveness, courtesy, respectful attitude to tourists.



The territory on which Belaya Rus is located is also noteworthy - the Tuapse sanatorium. The reviews of all who have been here impress with their enthusiasm. This area is a forest park with lawns and lawns, beautifully decorated with taste. A lot of ornamental shrubs, beautiful trees and flowers with wonderful aromas turn entertainment and walks into a pleasant pastime. For fans of outdoor activities, a football field, tennis courts and other sports fields are always open .

In the sanatorium there is a library that provides readers with a huge selection of literature for every taste, various literary evenings are held here. In the concert hall almost every night they show films, conduct various seminars and conferences.

About the sanatorium "Belaya Rus" (Tuapse) the reviews of vacationers leave nothing to be desired. The magnificent beach complex includes equipped locker rooms, offers the possibility of renting various equipment that makes the rest even more comfortable, and swimming - fun and interesting. Cafes, snack bars, and everything else that vacationers may require are at their complete disposal. What more could you wish for?



Sanatorium "Belaya Rus" has a children's department, which accepts up to 150 people from seven to fifteen years in one run. Rooms for children, which can accommodate three to four people, are equipped with air conditioning, lamps, necessary furniture. The children are treated at the sanatorium based on the conclusions of the attending physicians, and also take sea and air baths.
