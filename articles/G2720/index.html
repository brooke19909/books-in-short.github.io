"Lenovo R780" - reviews. Characteristics "Lenovo R780"

The Chinese company Lenovo, which produces computers, has long begun to develop the smartphone market. Her first products were perceived without much enthusiasm, but those who managed to evaluate the work of the models of this manufacturer were satisfied. First of all, success was promoted by a serious approach of specialists to the implementation of these products. Smartphones also use high-quality materials for the case. But the most attractive feature of this brand was the low price of products.



Today there is no definite answer to the question of whether to buy smartphones from Lenovo or not? It all depends on what the user is used to working with and what features he needs. And the latest smartphone "Lenovo" R780 has plenty.

Who is who

For a clear classification of their products, specialists have a very simple and understandable labeling. Everyone is already used to having a letter in the first place. It means a device class. The letter K (for some reason, the last letter of the word Geek) means the most advanced achievements and is assigned to flagship models. The letter S (from the word Stylish) is assigned to stylish models that have distinctive features in appearance. If at the beginning of the name is the letter A (from the word Affordable), then these are budget models. And finally, the letter P means that the model is made for the business class, although it is taken from the word Proffesional. To this group, as you might have guessed, the Lenovo P780 phone belongs. In this line, devices with characteristics that are slightly smaller than the flagship ones are collected. But there is something different from ultra-functional advanced devices.

The back cover of the device is completely made of metal, which gives a pleasant chill in the palm of your hand. There is also a metal edging along the contours. It is worth noting that not many companies are busy with metal parts and prefer plastic. In this regard, the characteristics of the Lenovo R780 compare favorably with competitors.

Power and memory

After impressing with the physical data, the user will be no less surprised by the hardware. At the heart of the device is a crystal with four cores. Each of them works with a frequency of 1200 MHz. High-speed memory for some reason, there is 1 GB. But still, this bunch is more than enough for the Lenovo P780 firmware to work reliably and quickly.



There is 4 GB of internal storage space for files, and it can reach up to 32 GB via memory cards. However, additional memory will have to be definitely purchased, because of the 4 built-in memory only 2.8 GB is available.

For processing video data, a processor is used, which is called Power VR SGX 544. It copes fairly well with its duties and does not disappoint the inhibitions of fans to watch movies in high quality.

If you look closely, you can see that the surface of the protective glass has an anti-reflective coating. It completely copes with its duties. Photo and video features

For photography enthusiasts, the smartphone has an 8-megapixel camera. She shoots with an average quality that will not disappoint, but will not surprise. For video calls, there is a small front camera of 0.3 megapixels. Its quality is enough only purely for this purpose. But you can take a picture for contacts.

Two SIM cards and their mode of operation

To distinguish between business and home calls, the Lenovo P780 phone has the ability to install two SIM cards. Since the device has one radio module, they work alternately. When talking on one operator, the second will be unavailable. The location of the cards is very convenient, since you do not need to remove the battery to remove them.



To configure the work of operators, you should immediately assign a card to use the Internet, send SMS and other functions. To make calls, the phone will each time ask for the choice of the operator.

Wireless and useful programs

You can use the built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules to exchange data with other devices. They will allow you to send and receive files, as well as access the Internet.

To work on the network there is a pre-installed browser. The smartphone‚Äôs menu has already installed icons for quick access to the accounts of all popular social networks.

An office software package is installed for working with documents. If you need to record something, and there is no pen at hand, you can use the built-in voice recorder. All this is perfect for those who often work with text files and who need to capture various information.

Device battery

The undeniable advantage of the smartphone is its powerful battery. According to the manufacturer, it is able to maintain up to 43 hours of talk time. Most likely, this is a very real picture. With intensive use, the device lasts about three days, which by the standards of such devices is a very good result.



The battery itself occupies a large part under the back cover. The manufacturer does not recommend removing it. That is why it is screwed. To prevent this, a special strip in the form of a seal is even glued. By the way, the warranty will cease to be valid if damaged.

Since the smartphone does not want to part with the battery, a reset button is provided. It is located near the memory card slot and is colored red.

Usability

Controlling the functions of a smartphone is familiar to those who have been using this technique for a long time. Holding the device in one hand, you can safely use it. The only remark on the account of Lenovo P780, reviews of which were found on the Internet, is the location of the lock and power button on the upper end of the case. This performance makes you reach your finger with it every time you use it.



Near the button is a slot for connecting a USB cable. It is sealed with a special plastic cover, which can be opened only if there is a threat to break the nail. It is really very tightly closed. You will have to fight it every time you need to charge the phone.

In terms of managing functions, everything is convenient and thoughtful. On top of the Android 4.2 system, a small proprietary shell is installed. Everything works fast. For example, when you start the navigator, satellites are detected surprisingly quickly. Therefore, in this regard, the characteristics of the Lenovo P780 are well suited for a car that needs an interactive map of the area.
