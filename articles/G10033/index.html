Renting an apartment: what, why and how

Renting an apartment is a normal everyday alternative to its full purchase or, for example, staying at a hotel. Renting an apartment is of two main types: long-term and short-term (that is, renting an apartment for rent, for several days or for a night). In life, there is everything and not always enough money to buy a house or pay for a hotel. A considerable percentage of people live like that, renting an apartment for a long time and moving from one to another.
