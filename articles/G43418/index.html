Double in the knowledge of ancient people

The Egyptians have always believed that every person at birth is necessarily endowed not only with his soul, but also with his second instance. The double is a kind of patron. It is spiritual in nature, but is invisible to human eyes.

The ancient Egyptians were convinced that after the death of a person, his soul and twin are separated from him. In this case, the double can rise again. To do this, he needs support in the form of a body or his image in the form of a statue, a bas-relief or painting.

This is precisely how the theory of immortality originated, which resulted in the erection of a huge number of tombs. The Egyptians believed that one who outlived himself in his double has the right to continue earthly life even in the next world.

A little later, the same view of the world was expressed by the Neo-Pythagorean Filolaus. This sage said that the center of the universe does not exist on Earth, but in the so-called Hestna, which is a central fire. Filolaus was the founder of the theory of cosmogony. According to the ideas of this science, all planets revolve around a central fire. They include even the Sun, which does not shine, but plays the role of a mirror, reflecting the brilliance of Hestna. At the same time, Filolaus claimed that there is a twin planet of the Earth. This celestial body moves in the same orbit, but it is located behind Hesna. Philolaus called this planet the Anti-Earth. Apparently, according to his ideas, the world of human doubles existed there.
