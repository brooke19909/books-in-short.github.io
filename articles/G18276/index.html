Razer - the secret to success

Razer is a leading global manufacturer of gaming equipment. The company makes headphones, keyboards, etc. But why do gamers prefer Razer products? The answer is simple. As the company's tagline says: "For gamers. By gamers". In Russian, this means: "For gamers. From gamers." Indeed, these words fully characterize Razer products. The company understands what the players need and gives it to them. Razer equipment provides maximum comfort and ergonomics. Therefore, the periphery of this company is used by both ordinary players and professional e-sportsmen.

In this article we will discuss the new brainchild of Razor. Talk about the Razer Kraken Pro headphones. Want to learn about the technical characteristics of this gadget, its features? Then this article is for you.

Razer Kraken Pro: device overview

Perhaps the main feature of this model is the name. Kraken (Kraken) is a sea monster that could be found in myths and legends. The creature had an impressive size and deadly tentacles that could smash the fleet to smithereens. If you draw parallels, it is understood that using the Razer Kraken Pro headphones, the gamer will get a huge advantage over his enemies. Guided by the sounds, the player can easily determine the location of opponents and easily kill them in the manner of the bloodthirsty Kraken.

Design





Razer has always been known for the superior design of its devices. The new headphone model was no exception. The Razer Kraken Pro has a superb look. Matte poisonous green color attracts attention and stands out from the competition. Young gamers will surely like such a bright coloring. On top of the surface of the headband you can see the company logo.

The ear pads are equipped with a very soft, adaptable upholstery. Thanks to this, the headphones do not crush during the game, and you can safely spend a couple of hours in them. In addition, the ear cushions fit snugly enough, which results in excellent sound insulation. The weight of the device does not exceed 300 grams, so the neck will not get tired even after a long gaming session.

Headphones, breaking in two places, are able to fold for easy carrying. Another important detail worth noting in the Razer Kraken Pro is the microphone. It is located on a flexible rack, which is hidden on the left side. This design provides a comfortable and convenient operation for the player.

Specifications

The headphone frequency range is from 20 to 20,000 Hz. The resistance is about 32 ohms, the sensitivity is 110 dB. Input power (maximum) - 50 mW.





The Razer Kraken Pro can be described as oversaturated with bass. Headphones just drown out the rest of the bands. For this reason, using Kraken Pro to listen to music is not a good idea. The gadget is able to change the musical composition beyond recognition. Therefore, if you are a music lover who needs a device to listen to music, the Razer Kraken Pro is not for you.

As for the games, then everything is diametrically opposite. The rumble of artillery, the sound of shots and even the quiet steps of enemies - all this is perfectly audible. Kraken Pro creates an amazing soundscape that complements the game world. Headphones also perform well in competitive games. For example, when playing CS, be sure that not a single step or shot will fly past your ears.

Software





It is also worth noting that Kraken Pro has native software. In order to get it, you need to connect the headphones to the computer and register on the official Razor website. Then you will get access to a special utility. Why is it needed? When using special software for headphones, you will be able to perform various manipulations with the gadget. For example, calibrate the device, change the sound level, bass, activate additional options.

Conclusion





Razer Kraken Pro is a delightful earphone for real gamers and eSports players. The gadget helps to immerse yourself in the world of the game. In addition, the headphones perform well in various competitive games and shooters. You will hear every step and shot of the enemy, thereby receiving information about his location. As for the price, the headphones are quite expensive. But the cost is fully justified.

If you are a professional e-sportsman or gamer who spends most of his time playing games, then Razer Kraken Pro is the best choice for you.
