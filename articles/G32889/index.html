What is Takhtala mountain and why does everyone like it so much?



Geographic data

"Tahtals" in Turkish means "from the boards," or "boardwalk." The fact is that the rocks of which the body of Mount Takhtaly is composed look like layers or folds pushed one on top of the other. These are Paleozoic and Tertiary deposits, remotely resembling rows of bars. Many mountains in the Balkans and in Asia have the same structure, and not just the Taurus mountain system, to which Takhtaly belongs. They began to rise above sea level in the era of alpine folding, which continues in our time. Perhaps in the future, Tahtali Mountain, whose height is currently considered 2365 meters, will become even grander. But even now it dominates the surrounding area so much that it is clearly visible from the boundless distance, for example, from Belek, to which more than 50 km from Kemer.



Tahtal nature

Those who choose Kemer to relax, Mount Tahtali can give wonderful weather conditions during their holidays. The Beidaglari mountain range, of which it is the peak, protects the area from the north winds. As a result, it creates its own microclimate, pleasing with a greater presence of sunny days than in neighboring resorts. At the same time, on the top of the mountain you can see snow even in June, although in summer it is not so much, only separate small layers. Anyway, bring a souvenir in the form of a snowman in the form of a snowman in the heat +35 and above from the original trip. In the spring, in the region of Kemer, African winds often blow, bringing reddish dust with them. She paints the snow-white peak of Takhtala in a red color, which looks somewhat unusual. The vegetation on the mountain is represented mainly by conifers and grassy-floral undergrowth interspersed with evergreen shrubs. All this is beautiful and colorful closer to the foot, but the higher, the vegetation becomes poorer and completely disappears at the borders of 1800-1900 meters. Only the bare rocks remain.

Animals and birds

The fauna of Tahtali with an influx of tourists has become very impoverished. Therefore, the Turkish government creates reserves in order to at least save something for posterity. Now, on the slopes of Mount Takhtaly, if you are lucky, you can find hedgehogs, land turtles, badgers, mouflons, lizards, and if you are not lucky, you can see vipers and mice. It is very rare for fallow deer and wild boars to live here, living in places of the Taurus Mountains that are still inaccessible to humans. There are also meetings with a fox or a jackal (usually in winter). Uphill on foot

The top of Tahtali mountain has always attracted tourists and all desperate romantics. The lift appeared here only in 2007. Until now, climbing was carried out on its own. And now for everyone, this route has been saved. It is part of the famous Lycian trail and is suitable only for physically hardy people. Approximately up to a height of 1811 meters, the road is very interesting. You can not only breathe the cleanest air, not only admire nature, but also touch it with your hands. To be honest, near Tahtali Mountain opens quite differently. The photo is a confirmation of this. Further, the landscape becomes monotonous and, if not a fan of geology, boring. All that catches your eye is gray-brown bare rocks, along which you need to stomp up another half a kilometer. And at the top there is a well-deserved wonderful vacation with a visit to the toilet, shop and cafe (whoever wishes). Getting on the trail is easy. You need to get to the village of Beichika (BASIC). In that place there is a pointer with an arrow. It is impossible to get lost further, since they trod the path well. It is better to make such an excursion in the autumn months or in the spring, since in the summer, until you climb up a kilometer and a half, the heat is immense.



And on the lift

For all those who cannot or do not want to climb the mountain on their own, in 2007 a lift was launched. The cable car, called Olympos Teleferik, was built by the Swiss company Doppelmayr Seilbahnen GmbH, which has proven itself in creating such structures in Austria, Singapore, and America. Everywhere their lifts work perfectly, they do not break off the cables. These lines seem inappropriate until you sit in a booth and start climbing to the coveted peak of Takhtaly Mountain. About a third of the way, when the earth is already 500-600 meters away, the cabin at the next junction can significantly jerk or even freeze. Tahtali Mountain: how to get to the ski lift

Takhtaly from Kemer is located 7 km, not far from the villages of Camyuva and Tekirova. The cable car starts not from the foot, but from the lower station, located on the slope of the mountain at around 726 meters. Here you can also take a walk, take beautiful photos, admire the pond with fish and ducks. All excursion buses are brought here along the serpentine. You can get to this place on your own in a personal car or taxi and already here you can pay for a place in the booth. So it turns out 15-20 dollars cheaper. In addition, you can buy only one lift. Down then either on foot or on a paraglider. The flight takes about 40 minutes and ends on the Tekirova beach. Those who are afraid to fly in the clouds themselves can go down with an instructor. The lift cabins are more than roomy. They can simultaneously contain 80 people, but in reality this happens infrequently. The walls of the cabin are transparent, so you can start taking pictures on the way.

Legends of Tahtali

Residents in the vicinity of Kemer have a sincere legend about the visiting card of their city - Mount Takhtaly. By the way, the Greeks who arrived here called her Olympos. They also christened the city built at its foot, from which picturesque ruins have been preserved. The legend about the appearance of Takhtala Mountain is this: once upon a time there lived the mortal beauty of Takhtala, in whom the immortal god Taurus fell in love. It so happened that the girl died from a snake bite. The unfortunate lover tried in every possible way to kill himself in order to also end his life, but nothing came of it. You can‚Äôt argue with immortality. Then he asked for the service of Zeus. He took pity on the feelings of poor Taurus, forever connecting him with his beloved. He turned one into a mountain range, the other into a beautiful peak.
