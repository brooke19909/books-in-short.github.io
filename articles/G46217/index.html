Where to go for the New Year holidays with children? The choice is yours!

If your child has reached school age, then do not forget that educational institutions are closed for the holidays before the New Year. So, you have to spend as much with your beloved child as much as two whole weeks. Where to go and where to spend the New Year holidays so that both you and the student will be interested? Below you will find some ideas on how to make the holiday more memorable and joyful.



Primary school children still believe in the existence of Santa Claus. Why not go visit him? A trip to this winter fairy tale will appeal not only to the baby, but also to you. In the Finnish town of Rovaniemi, which is located in Lapland near the Arctic Circle, the good bearded man Joulupukki (aka Santa Claus and Santa Claus) lives in a hut. He is helped by elves and gnomes who accept letters and pack gifts for especially obedient children. Handing your wish directly into the hands of a good wizard is the dream of any child. Adults enjoy riding reindeer sledding and contemplating the aurora. New Year holidays in Finland in 2014 - the most popular destination. The budget option for a trip to visit Grandfather Frost will be a trip to the Russian city ​​of Veliky Ustyug, where the residence of the "domestic analogue" of Santa Claus is located.





The modern generation is brought up on Walt Disney cartoons. Girls dream of Bambi, Sleeping Beauty Castle and Snow White, and the boys would be happy to meet the real Mickey Mouse. And all of them, together with joy, would ride on a variety of slides and other attractions in a grand amusement park near Paris. Do you still think where to go for the New Year holidays with children? Choose Disneyland, where you will find a kaleidoscope of impressions and a powerful explosion of adrenaline in the blood!



Christmas in Western Europe falls on December 25th. But the holidays associated with the coming of Jesus Christ into this world continue! When deciding where to go for the New Year holidays with children, consider the option of Spain. In this country, with the pomp they celebrate the arrival of the Magi - the feast of the Three Kings. In all cities there are colorful processions of “Sages with a retinue”, which give children sweets and other sweets. And adults can make a very successful shopping, bring home many interesting souvenirs, learn about the traditions of the celebration of Christmas and New Year of other nations.

If all members of your family miss the sea and the sun, then the question of where to go on New Year's holidays with children is not at all worth it. Choose Emirates, Egypt, South Vietnam, Cuba, the Indian state of Goa, Dominican Republic. In all these places, excellent conditions for family vacations. True, the flight to distant exotic countries is quite long (and expensive), but the tropical paradise, into which you will get from snowy Moscow, is worth it. Just imagine: festive fireworks and explosions of crackers against the background of a turquoise lagoon and swaying palm trees beneath the light breeze ...

If your child is confidently skiing and loves to conquer the mountain slopes, then the choice of where to go for the New Year holidays with children is simply huge. Here you will find both domestic resorts (Karelia, the Urals, the Caucasus), and interesting places in the near and far abroad. If the whole family goes to the Alpine Courchevel or Andorra in the Pyrenees for you a little, choose Slovakia or Polish Zakopane. There are excellent ski resorts that are in no way inferior to the “western” ones in the Ukrainian Carpathians, Crimea, Armenia and Kazakhstan.
