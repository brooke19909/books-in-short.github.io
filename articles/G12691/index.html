Hotel Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 * (Crete, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

If we talk about beach holidays in Europe, then Greece is considered one of the most popular countries among tourists, namely its island part. After all, here they can offer not only high quality service, but also relatively low prices.

Crete is one of the most popular Greek islands. After all, here modern comfort organically merges with the mysterious atmosphere of ancient legends and genuine Greek hospitality. Of course, there are many hotels on the coast. And one of the good places to relax is the Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 * hotel complex.

Of course, when planning a trip, it is important to consider some details. After all, first you need to find out whether the hotel‚Äôs location is convenient and whether guests provide decent living conditions. So how do tourists respond to this place?

Is the hotel location convenient?





Of course, the location is an important factor, because you want to have everything at hand. So where is the Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 * hotel complex located? Crete, a picturesque resort area called Malia - it is here, not far from the beach, that the hotel is located.

It is worth saying right away that in walking distance not only the coast, but also numerous cafes, taverns and shops. This area is calm, mainly tourists live here. The center of the resort town is only 450 meters away. The city of Heraklion (the capital of the island), along with its large port, entertainment venues and historical sites, is about 30 km. Heraklion Airport is about the same distance from Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 *. Tours, by the way, already include a comfortable transfer from the airport, and the road is unlikely to take much time.

What does the territory look like? Description of infrastructure





Golden Bay Hotel 3 * is a small hotel complex, which consists of the main building, as well as several separate one- and two-story bungalows. The hotel area is large and, according to reviews, very beautiful.

Here you can relax by the pool, stroll through the secluded Mediterranean garden, enjoy the views of the surroundings, spend time on neatly trimmed lawns, play sports, in short, actively and have fun on vacation. Most vacationers are young people from Great Britain and Germany, although compatriots also stop here from time to time.

Hotel Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 *: photo and description of living rooms





This is a very small hotel complex - on its territory there are only 26 rooms. These are either apartments in the main building or spacious studios in separate bungalows. Each room has a private balcony or a secluded furnished terrace - here you can relax while enjoying views of the meadow or garden.

Each room consists of a sleeping area with a large bed, a cozy living room and a kitchen corner with everything necessary for cooking. The furniture here is new and comfortable. Home appliances, without which it is simply impossible to imagine a comfortable stay, is present in the room. You can watch TV and use the phone. For a fee, a small safe is brought into the room. In summer, guests use air conditioning, and in cold weather a central heating system works. For an additional fee, you can access the Internet, which is important for many travelers.

The kitchen has everything the hostess needs, including a small stove, mini fridge, sink, microwave, electric kettle, kitchen and tableware. Drinking water and some other drinks are brought to the room daily.

The room has an attached bathroom. The situation here is simple - there is a shower, toilet, washbasin, wall mirror. Of course, you can count on branded cosmetics, as well as clean towels (they are changed every day) and a hair dryer. Reviews say that the rooms are really very clean - it's nice to relax here.

Are tourists provided with food?





Of course, when planning a vacation, it is important to consider such a factor as nutrition. What can the Golden Bay Cyprus 3 * Hotel offer to its guests? In fact, guests can independently choose a convenient meal plan, whether it is only breakfast or a full three meals a day.

Guests are invited to a local restaurant with an outdoor terrace - here, in a relaxed atmosphere, you can eat while enjoying views of the courtyard. The menu is varied, which is confirmed by reviews of guests. Breakfast includes cereals, granola, hot sausages, light snacks, salads, fruits and, of course, pastries. Lunches and dinners are more diverse - restaurant guests are pleased with dishes of fish, meat and seafood. By the way, even if you have not paid in advance, you can still have dinner at the hotel, however, paying the bill separately.

Most rooms have their own kitchenette, so tourists can cook some snacks on their own. And in the city there are many taverns, bars and cafes where you can relax and eat.

Distance to the beach and amenities for vacationers





The hotel complex Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 * is located almost on the seashore. The distance to it is about 150 m. The beach here is very beautiful and clean, as they clean it every day. The coast, as well as the entrance to the sea, is covered with soft sand.

This is a public resort area, so there are always a lot of tourists and entertainments. Rental of sun loungers and umbrellas is paid separately, but the hotel can ask for a large beach towel. There are several bars nearby serving chilled drinks, delicious cocktails and even hearty dinners. The sea is calm, although from time to time light beautiful waves rise. There is even a shallow area - an ideal place for children's bathing.

Fun by the sea

Immediately it is worth saying that this section of the coast is designed for youth recreation, so there is enough entertainment at any time of the day. Sports grounds are equipped on the shore - tourists often play volleyball and other beach games.

For a reasonable fee, you can rent a catamaran or boat. Also, travelers like to water ski, boat, banana. You will have the opportunity to do parasailing and sailing. Often, windsurfing and diving enthusiasts relax here. By the way, in the sports center you can not only rent sports equipment, but also take a short effective training course.

The list of additional services





Guests of the Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 * hotel can count on a number of additional services. To begin with, it is worth saying that in public areas there is wireless Internet access. Also in the main building is equipped with a spacious lounge, and the reception is open around the clock. By the way, you can rent a safe from the administrator - here your things will be completely safe.

The hotel has a currency exchange office, and the rate here is advantageous. The property offers luggage storage. Ample parking is available on site, which tourists use for free. By the way, in the city it is easy to rent transport, which greatly facilitates movement around the island. It offers guests a small business center where you can use a computer and some office equipment, as well as send a fax. At your service is a souvenir shop selling hotel branded products and original handmade souvenirs. There is also a small grocery store.

Hotel Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 * (Crete, Greece): leisure of guests





Despite the fact that most tourists come here to relax on the beach, you can have a good time at the hotel. For guests there is a swimming pool and a terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas, where you can relax and sunbathe.

A mini golf course is located on site. Guests also enjoy playing table tennis or billiards. Near the hotel there is a large golf course, where fans of this sport will be able to work out and just have a good time. Activities such as fishing, horse riding and cycling in the surrounding area are offered to tourists.

There are several nightclubs in the city where young people dance, have fun and have a great time. And on local beaches, parties are regularly held. And do not forget about excursions, because Crete is an island shrouded in ancient legends. An interesting tour can be purchased both in the city and in the hotel itself.

Are there any conditions for relaxing with a child?

Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 * quite often becomes a place of family vacation. There are no special conditions for children here. Nevertheless, the guys feel quite comfortable. The rooms are spacious, there are folding sofas on which children can sleep. Free of charge in the room bring the cradle-playpen for the smallest guests.

As for nutrition, in the restaurant you can always choose simple and healthy food for the child. In addition, you can cook yourself in the room. As for entertainment, the kids feel great on the beach, and there are several entertainment centers in the city where not only little travelers, but also their parents have fun.

How do tourists respond?

What do former guests say about Golden Bay Studios Apartments 3 *? Reviews are for the most part positive. The hotel is really clean and comfortable. The rooms are comfortable - there is comfortable furniture, a private secluded balcony, and the equipment is working properly. Maids visit the rooms every day.

The hotel area is large and well maintained. An important advantage is the proximity to the beach and entertainment venues. Relax and have fun at the hotel. By the way, it is worth considering the relatively low cost - this place is suitable for people who want to relax on a budget, without losing quality of service. This is a really good place to relax on one of the most mysterious and beautiful islands of Greece.
