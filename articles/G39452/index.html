Kemer, Beldibi - some of the best resorts in Turkey

For many tourists from Russia and Europe, Turkey has become almost a native country. The resorts of this beautiful state cannot but admire. The infrastructure is almost everywhere developed, amazing landscapes are pleasing to the eye, and interesting sights and entertainment programs will not let you get bored. Among all the Turkish resorts in popularity and beauty I would like to highlight Kemer. Beldibi is a village located near its popular neighbor. If Kemer for some reason is not to your taste, for example, due to excessive noise and active life, you can always stay in a calmer and quieter place, which is a small resort 17 km from the city.



Beldibi is adjacent to another popular resort city - Antalya. If there is not enough entertainment in the village, then the more famous tourist centers are always ready to receive foreign guests. Kemer is famous for its beautiful nature, clear, azure sea, beautiful coast. Beldibi is also famous for its spectacular scenery, besides being located right by the water. Most hotels are built near the seashore, so you just have to cross the road, and you can swim and sunbathe as much as you like.

The village is conditionally divided into three parts. The first of them is dominated by 3 and 4 star hotels, for an economical vacation this sector is ideal. When choosing a place of residence, you should pay attention to the beaches, because after all it is not Kemer. Beldibi cannot boast of sand, but if you go on vacation without children, then this fact is not so important. Pebble beaches prevail in the first and second sectors; bulk sand can be found only near large hotels. In the third part of the village there is a concrete platform, the descent into the water is not very convenient, so it is better not to stop with children here.



Fans of shopping for large purchases should go to Kemer. Beldibi also has several markets and small shops. Necessary things and products for relaxation can be purchased at these outlets. If you are interested in boutiques with branded clothing or large shopping centers, then you should go to nearby Antalya or Kemer. Beldibi is suitable for tourists of all ages, young people will like nightclubs, incendiary open-air discos, older adults can sit in cozy bars and cafes. To appreciate the beauty of the panorama of the night coast and admire the laser show, it is worth visiting a disco on the mountain.



Beldibi is located near many interesting sights. So, in the nearby village of Kirish is Mount Takhtaly, which attracts with natural phenomena in the form of flashes of fire that arise from underground. And from the top offers stunning views of the surrounding places. You can also visit the ancient city of Phaselis, which preserved the ruins of a theater, aqueduct, city cemetery and square, Roman baths. Adrenaline lovers will enjoy river rafting. If you really want to relax your soul and body, escape from everyday worries, look at interesting sights, swim in the sea, then you should go to the address: Turkey, Kemer, Beldibi. The map will help you not to get lost and visit all worthy places.
