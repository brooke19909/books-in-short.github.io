Pumpkin from felt: a great idea for the fall

Want to create a unique autumn decoration for your interior? Then we will tell you how to make a pumpkin out of felt with your own hands, especially since it is so simple. It will not only be a bright detail in your room, but also a wonderful source of autumn inspiration in these gray and cloudy weekdays.

Symbol of autumn

Undoubtedly, pumpkin is one of the main symbols of autumn and stands on a par with maple leaves and frequent rains. She is also loved by needlewomen who just don‚Äôt do with this farm product: candle holders, baskets, caskets and coasters, and much more.

Even the cooks worship the pumpkin. After all, it is from it that one of the most famous dishes of the autumn is obtained - pumpkin soup with cinnamon and coriander.

But it is frustrating that these vegetables are not stored for a very long time, they quickly lose the sunny colors of autumn, they begin to dry out, grow moldy and smell unpleasant. In this case, our idea will help us - a pumpkin made of felt, which is created in two counts and does not deteriorate for a long time.





What will we cut from?

Before pumpkin from felt with our own hands will please our eyes, we need to prepare all the necessary materials. They can easily be found in any sewing or creative store.



  • Felt. The main component of our pumpkin, and we chose it for good reason. This material largely wins over expensive suede fabrics not only for its price and quality, but also for the brightness of its palette and unusual texture. After all, the truth is, with the word ‚Äúfelt‚Äù there are only bright and juicy associations of colors. In addition, prices do not really bite, but rather, lure the buyer.
  • Filler. In this case, you can choose both synthetic winterizer and synthetic winterizer and holofiber. In addition, if you are a supporter of natural materials, then you can use hay or sawdust to add a unique touch of freshness to your toy.
  • Sewing supplies. It is very cool that a felt pumpkin is easily created without the participation of large equipment, that is, a sewing machine. Thanks to the thin and pliable material, manufacturing can be handled manually with a needle and thread in the color of the fabric.
  • Hot glue. They can safely notice all the sewing work, if you, for example, are in a hurry or do not sew very carefully. But, frankly, the work sewn with a needle will look much more decent and professional.

Pumpkin from felt: pattern

Now that the necessary materials have already been prepared, it's time to cut the felt. This is not difficult to do, having at hand a pattern, fabric, sewing pins and a bar of soap or a pencil.



  • We print the pattern in the size we need or transfer it to paper from the computer screen, and then cut the blanks along the contour.
  • Straighten the fabric, removing all the bumps, and pin the cut blanks with pins. We circle the parts with soap or pencil, and then, moving the workpiece, make the required number of copies.
  • We cut out all along the contours.

The long-awaited moment

Now we can proceed to the most interesting: the process of stitching parts together. We will describe this moment step by step. Therefore, even a beginner in sewing will cope with the work.



  • Take 2 cut "petals" of felt and chop them together, face inwards, to get a kind of pocket. Sew the common side, and then attach the next petal.
  • So, step by step, we sew all the details, leaving a small hole so that the pumpkin from felt easily turns out. Do not forget to fasten the dots at the base and stuff the toy tightly so that it holds its shape and is stable.
  • Now we form a handle from green felt, we sew everything around the perimeter, also filling it tightly.
  • To make the pumpkin from felt look more impressive, in the intervals between the "slices" we will stretch the yarn of orange or red color. Thus, the edges will be sharper.
  • In addition, if desired, you can embroider the muzzle or eyes of our toy to add cartoonishness to it.
  • Now you can attach the stalk: we will use a drop of hot glue and, carefully applying it to the pumpkin, we will press two parts together. It is worth holding the entire structure for several minutes to wait for it to dry completely.

So our felt pumpkin is ready - a wonderful detail for any autumn room design. In addition, it can be used as a full-fledged sofa cushion or an original gift to your relatives and friends, for example, for a housewarming party.
