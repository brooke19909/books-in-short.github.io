How to transfer money from MTS to the card of Sberbank and other banks?

Money may be needed at any time, no one is safe from this. But what if the only source of funds now is the balance of the mobile phone? Or did you accidentally make a mistake and put more than you planned? How to transfer money from MTS to a bank card, and is it real? Let's get it right.

Mobile Features

The services of mobile operators are now far from being limited only to the provision of cellular communications. Money transfers are included in the list of services, and a large number of people use them.

What are they needed for? This makes it possible to make transfers without opening a bank account. It is also an opportunity to transfer money to people who are in other cities and even countries.

This method is often used to raise funds for charity.

How to transfer money from MTS to a card?

The algorithm is as follows. You need to go to pay.mts.ru. There you will see a large number of different icons. We are interested in "Transfer to a bank card." Let's try to transfer money from MTS to a Sberbank card.





A special form will open before you. In it you need to carefully fill in the fields. Enter the cell number from which you are going to make the transfer. Please note that it starts with +7. Then you need to enter the amount. The next line automatically calculates the amount, taking into account the cost of services. It will be written off from your account. By default, there is a dot in the column ‚Äúfrom the MTS account‚Äù, you can change it to transfer from the card.





Click the "Next" button. A window opens in which you need to specify the recipient's data. This is a payment card number. Click "Next."





A receipt for transfer opens. The source is indicated in it - this is the MTS personal account, the mobile number from which the transfer will be debited, the card number of the person to whom the payment is intended, the amount of the transfer and the amount with the commission to be debited. This data must be carefully checked. If all is well, you need to click the "Get Password" link at the bottom of the receipt.

A password will come to the indicated number, which you must enter in a special field and click on the "Confirm Transfer" button. Almost all the steps in the algorithm of how to transfer money from MTS to the card have been completed.

Next, you will receive an SMS message on your cell phone. It will duplicate the data from the receipt. You will need to confirm the operation or refuse it. To confirm, you need to send any text in response SMS, to refuse, you need to send 0. This completes the operation. So it is possible to transfer money from MTS to Visa card of any bank.

The cost of transferring from MTS to a card

The operation, of course, is paid. The size of the commission is 4% of the amount, but not less than 60 rubles.

If you will transfer from card to card, and not from the balance of the phone to the card, then the commission is less - 1%, the minimum fee is 40 rubles.

There are also restrictions on the amount and number of transfers. The minimum transfer size is 50 rubles, the maximum is 15,000. You cannot transfer money from MTS to a bank card more than five times a day.

Which card can I transfer to?

The money transfer system works with cards of any banks. It can be Visa or MasterCard.

If you want to transfer money from MTS to a Sberbank card, then the algorithm of actions will be similar to that described above. The amount of the commission does not change; restrictions on the amount and number of transactions are identical. You will also need to act in the same way if you want to transfer money from MTS to a VTB card.

What other translations are possible?

You can transfer money from MTS not only to bank cards. You can replenish the Yandex electronic wallet. There is no minimum amount, the maximum is 15,000 rubles. The cost of services is 10 rubles per operation.

Consider the WebMoney e-wallet. There is no minimum amount, the maximum is 5,000 rubles. The cost of the service is also 10 rubles. And a whole list of different electronic wallets. You need to follow the same algorithm as in the case of a bank card.

You can make contributions to various charitable organizations, there are about 30 of them on the site. Including such popular ones as Greenpeace, World Wide Fund for Nature, Khabensky Fund , AiF Kind Heart and many others. In the transfer form, you only need to indicate the amount, all other details are already indicated. If desired, you can set up auto payment.

Card transfers from other operators

Two other major operators - Beeline and Megafon - also offer money transfer services. How is this service implemented with them?

Beeline provides the ability to transfer from Visa, MasterCard and Maestro cards. You can do this using the site or using SMS. If you make a transfer from the site, then in the "Payment" section you need to find the "Pay from phone account" subsection, and there is already the sub-item "Money transfers". Among the drawn pictures you need to find "On a bank card."





Then select the option ‚ÄúTransfer from site‚Äù. Next, we find the icon "Transfer to Visa Card". Fill out the receipt. You will need the card number of the person to whom the payment is intended, the mobile number, the amount and code from the picture. Next, a check mark in the line of agreement with the terms of service and the button "Pay".

If you want to make a transfer using SMS, then you need to send SMS to 7878 with text that contains the word Visa, then the card number of the person to whom the payment is addressed, and the amount, all without spaces. For sending SMS payment is not taken.

If the transfer amount is from 50 to 1000 rubles, then the amount of the commission is fixed - 50 rubles. When transferring from 1,001 to 14,000 rubles, the commission fee is 5.95% + 10 rubles.

We pass to the "Megaphone". We need the website money.megafon.ru. Here in the left column we select the item "To a bank card".





There is also a choice: you can do this using SMS or through the form on the site. You will need to get a password on your mobile phone, for this you need to enter a number. Then enter the card details and the transfer amount. Commission for services depends on the amount of transfer. If the amount is up to 5000 rubles, then the fee is 7.35% of the amount, and 95 rubles need to be added to this. If the amount is more than 5000 rubles, then to the same percentage of the commission you need to add another 259 rubles.

Well, sms. You need to send a message to the number 3116 according to the template: card type, its number, month, year, amount. All this data must be entered in the message with a space. It is necessary. The system works for VISA and MasterCard cards. Enrollment takes place over a period of several minutes to five days.

Now you have an algorithm of how to transfer money from MTS to the card of any bank, electronic wallets, also there are instructions on money transfers from other operators. Use - it is very convenient.
