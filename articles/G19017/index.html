Interesting Facts





Mount Belaya is located near the small village of Uralets - it is 37 km southwest of Nizhny Tagil. According to official data, the peak is considered one of the highest in the Urals. Being at the very top of the mountain, you can see fogs, clouds and even the cities of Yekaterinburg, Nevyansk, Kirovgrad and Nizhny Tagil. Spectacular panorama - as if in the palm of your hand.

The hillsides are almost year-round covered with a snow blanket, for this reason it got its name. The history of this place begins in 1770, when the scientist Pallas traveled along the Ural ridges. There he noticed limestone layers, which subsequently attracted researchers and famous travelers.

More than once in its vicinity came scientific expeditions. But Belaya Mountain (Nizhny Tagil) was famous not only for its valuable breed, but also for its magnificent landscapes, which caused delight and awe among artists, writers and scientists. Not far from the place, the obelisk of Europe-Asia was established.

Description of the complex





"Mount Belaya" is a high-class ski resort, known for about 50 years. Its doors are open all year. There are many visitors here both in summer and in winter. A variety of entertainment, interesting leisure and comfortable conditions attract completely different categories of tourists. But, of course, the resort is more focused on winter activities. Mother Nature herself endowed the place with uniqueness. All 9 months of the year on the slopes lies snow. The variety of mountain trails is impressive.

For extreme people and professionals equipped with winding slopes with drops. Beginners will enjoy the quiet wide trails. Riding time is not limited. Trails work both in the morning and in the evening. The slopes are well lit, and for the safety of people, a medical center and a rescue service are provided. With hospitality meets on its territory "White Mountain".

How to get to the resort?





From Nizhny Tagil regularly shuttle bus number 113. Travel time - no more than half an hour.

On your own car from Yekaterinburg you should go along the Serovskoye highway, and after Lenevka, turn left onto the bypass road. Following the signs, turn towards Uralets. Soon you will notice the obelisk of Europe-Asia, and after about two kilometers you will find yourself at the foot of the peak.

Entertainment Services

"Belaya Mountain" (ski resort) offers its guests a wide range of first-class services. On the territory there are many sports facilities, a service center, a rental point and modern lifts. For winter recreation, 5 ski slopes were built, which are equipped with the latest technology and fully meet the safety requirements.





There are also two tracks for flat running. Tourists will be interested in walking routes surrounded by rocky reliefs and picturesque nature. Absolutely all mountain trails pass along forest paths. The complex has a rental point for the issuance of sports equipment and equipment for citizens of different ages (skates, skis, snowboards).

Certified instructors will help you choose the equipment. They will conduct individual and group training for adults and children. Tourists do not need to take bulky equipment with them on a trip. In addition to the ski slopes, the administration of the Ural region complex has opened a large ice rink.
