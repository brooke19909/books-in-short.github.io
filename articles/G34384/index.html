Paperless visor



The most convenient material from which you should start your profession "couturier" is paper. There is a lot of it in every house where there are children. And there are other consumables: pencils and paints, glue, scissors. And now more about how to make a cap. First, measure the head circumference of your sailor. Use a tailor's centimeter. If it is not - a piece of thread, lace, ribbon, etc. The main thing is the correct measurement, so that then the hat does not slip onto the boy's forehead. The next step is how to make a cap out of cardboard (this is the type of material we will use) - cut out a strip of the appropriate length plus 1 centimeter for gluing from it. Its width is 5-7 centimeters. It turns out a blank for a small box. Fasten the ends with an overlap using a stapler or glue. By the diameter of the finished ring, only increasing it by 3 centimeters, cut out the bottom of the visor. Make cuts on it at the same distance from each other. Gently bend them inward. You coped with the basic workpieces!

Finishing work



And then there are details - how to make a sailor cap comfortable and attractive. Pellet, aka Tula, should not rub the child's forehead. To do this, proceed as follows. Using glue or a stapler, connect the notches on the bottom of the cap to the base. Check durability. Now, with a strip of soft fabric from the inside, turn over the cap. You can do the same operation with the bottom. Outside, paint it the way you want. Draw a logo, come up with an inscription. And do not forget about ribbons with anchors! Having finished work, hand solemnly to the little son a new thing, having dedicated it to a rank of a young man. And happy sailing on the seas of games and relaxation!
