How to sew a bear on a pattern with your own hands

Everybody loves good-natured, well-fed bears, and almost everyone in childhood had their favorite teddy bear.

You can again plunge into those happy days, make a bear for yourself from a pattern of mohair fabric, faux fur, fleece, flannel, bright fun rags.





It will take quite a bit of time and patience, but as a result you will get a great unique toy.

If you sew a bear according to a pattern for your child, it will be his favorite bear - a talisman, which he will enjoy even when he grows up.

Get ready for sewing

First you need to prepare everything you need for work.

For the first stage of manufacturing the pattern, you will need paper, pen, tracing paper or transparent polyethylene, scissors.

Take also thread with a needle, synthetic winterizer. Eyes can be bought ready-made (with moving pupils) and glued, you can pick up buttons or embroider. You will need black threads for the nose, glue.

The color of the material will suit any: in brown tones, blue, pink, white, green, floral, checkered. Do not limit your imagination.

For such a model, you can use both smooth and fleecy flaps.

We sew a bear

The success of the event largely depends on the accuracy of the bear pattern from the fabric .

Print it, copy it onto tracing paper, carefully cut it out. Lay out the details on fabric or fur, make sure that you have enough.

On the front side, mark the location of the eyes, nose.

According to the same pattern, you can easily tailor clothes for teddy bears, vests, a sarafan or shorts, given the size and characteristics of the figure.

By reducing the details, you can make bear children, and, after trying, gather a whole family.

Pin the body parts on the fabric from the wrong side so that they do not move, circle with a small or thin bar of soap. Pay attention to the amount of detail. Cut, leaving small allowances for seams, connect the parts on a typewriter or manually from the wrong side.



It is important to make notches correctly (green lines in the figure) so that the body takes the desired shape when inverted. In places of bends, cut the fabric perpendicular to the seam, literally 1-2 mm short of it. Do not overdo it.

Combine the received details, leaving a small gap in an inconspicuous place to stuff a toy with a padding polyester. With its help, it is necessary to shape the figure so that it is packed evenly, looks smooth, without dips, the legs have the same thickness. The toy should be like the one shown in the picture. Sew the hole with a blind stitch.

The final touch is embroidering the nose with a stitch of black thread. The main thing in this model is that everything is symmetrical.

Embroider a triangular or round nose exactly in the middle, and sew button eyes over it. On the legs, four pulling stitches, indicating the claws, must be made.

Tie a beautiful bow to a bear, think of a name for him!

Children also participate!

If the child is small, it is enough that he watches how you sew a bear on a pattern. Observation for children is also training, they get acquainted with the magical process of creativity, remember what you are doing.

Bear is the best gift

Having mastered the art of sewing bears on a pattern, you can always solve the issue of gifts for the New Year, birthday and Valentine's Day. You can leave the toy yourself as a mascot.





For such a simple pattern of a bear, anyone can do it yourself.

Children like this model, as the toy turns out to be light, it is convenient to wear it, take it for walks and to the kindergarten. Bears of different sizes can decorate your interior, add notes of warmth and comfort to it.
