Knitted shoes

For needlewomen who are friends with knitting or crocheting, the easiest way to choose this method is how to sew shoes for the doll. Experienced craftswomen will need, perhaps, no more than half an hour, so that new clothes adorn the legs of the little girlfriend of her daughter or granddaughter. But, unfortunately, not in all cases it is possible to make shoes this way. For beauties like Barbie or Monster High, you can try to make shoes according to the type of sandals, attaching knitted particles to an already prepared sole with heels. The simplest situation is with simple dolls resembling babies who have chubby legs with a flat foot. As a basis for work, you can take one of the options for manufacturing ordinary booties. The difference will only be in reduced proportions.





Phased work

First measure the foot length of the doll. Then knit in rows:

1 row - dial a chain of loops equal to 4/5 of the specified measure;

2 row - tie the chain on both sides with half columns, making increases from two ends for rounding;

3rd row - repeat the previous row, increasing the number of loops in the places of the toe and heel to obtain the desired shape;

4 row - we make a hem to go from the sole to the main part. To do this, we knit a row with double crochet in a loop on the inside (and not through two, as usual);

Parts manufacturing

Consider how to make a paper pattern of shoes for a doll. One of the options for making shoes can be tailoring. By the way, there will probably be no problems finding material. Indeed, in any home there will always be many beautiful, seemingly unnecessary pieces. Moreover, the dimensions should be, one might say, miniature. But first, it will be necessary to make a pattern. To do this, use the following method.

- Take a piece of paper and place a doll on it.

- Circle the handle, 1-1.5 cm from the foot (future allowances for the seams). The sole of the shoes is ready.

- Then lay a piece of paper on top of the front. Holding it a little, bring it on both sides back, trying to connect behind the heel (you can chop it).

- Squeeze the paper in a circle and outline the cut line.

And how to make shoes for dolls so that they fit snugly against the leg? For greater accuracy, take measurements and transfer them to the pattern before cutting.



We sew shoes from fabric

So, the measurements are taken, the pattern is done, and pieces of beautiful fabric are waiting in the wings. Before you begin, you need to consider how to facilitate the whole process. After all, dealing with tiny sizes, it is possible to simplify manufacturing technology somewhat. First of all, in order not to make a lining, duplicate the fabric using an iron with proclamine. And only after that carry out cutting. Rigid cardboard or thick colored paper to match the fabric is suitable as a sole. Make a double layer to remove the fabric in the gap. Free open sections do not have to be tucked; you can manually process them with a finishing seam or a beautiful narrow trim.

There are many options for making shoes for dolls! Choose the most suitable and master for the joy of children!
