Work in the bank: employee reviews, pros and cons

Today, the issue of employment is acute for millions of people. Even employees who have vast experience have to face the need to re-search for a job. There are a lot of reasons for this. For example, a reduction could occur in production, some have to quit due to serious disagreements in the team or amid too low wages. It is becoming increasingly difficult for young and ambitious people to find a good job, even if they graduated from a higher educational institution. However, despite all these difficulties, today there remains a huge number of unreached vacancies, which many may not have thought of before. Today, bank employees are widely required in the labor market.

Some are confused by the fact that there are too many such vacancies, and employers promise too good financial support, as well as an interesting work schedule. In order to understand whether it is worth applying for such a vacancy, you need to consider reviews about working in banks. It will also not be superfluous to get acquainted with the positive and negative sides of such activities.

Pros of working in a bank

Back in the early 2000s, it was believed that getting a job at a financial institution was a huge success. Work in a prestigious organization was an indicator that a person really has a special mentality and is able to conduct business. Gradually, a huge number of different vacancies formed in this area, and there were much more offers than demand. At the same time, banks began to dictate their terms, and job seekers gradually lost interest in such employment. However, this does not mean that there are no positive aspects in such work today. First of all, it is worth highlighting comfortable working conditions.

This is definitely one of the positive arguments in favor of getting a job at a bank. In this case, there are 2 options: you can get an operator in a bank branch or work at home and do the work in the bank's call center. Feedback on both types of employment is mostly positive. Regardless of the type of work chosen, a person can work in the most comfortable conditions.

If we talk about offices, they are usually equipped with very good offices. They have air conditioners, heaters, modern office equipment and everything that is necessary for normal operation. This is not surprising, because banks strive to make a good impression on their customers.

If you choose the second option, then in this case it will be possible to work at all without leaving your home. You don‚Äôt have to rack your brains on how to pick up a child from a kindergarten, or worry that you need to stand in traffic jams daily. Also, according to reviews of working in banks, this activity has other advantages.

Official employment and salary level

First of all, you need to understand that the so-called gray salary is not practiced in banks. This means that you will not have to receive money in an envelope without official employment. In the course of the financial institution, deductions to the Pension Fund are made for each employee. In addition, you can get sick leave and not lose money.

Financial organizations adhere very clearly to the Labor Code of the Russian Federation, so you can be absolutely calm for the legality of such work. The same goes for salaries. Any processing and other additional time costs on the part of the employee are necessarily encouraged with bonuses. Advances are always charged clearly and on time, without any delay.

The ability to use profitable bank products

Any employee of a financial institution automatically enters the loyalty program. This means that, if necessary, he can get a loan at a lower rate or a credit card on favorable terms. Of course, each operator of the bank is much better than any client understands the varieties and features of tariff rates. He understands which contract is more profitable to conclude, and which conditions are better to refuse. In their reviews of working at the bank, many say that they have gained invaluable experience that will be useful throughout life.

In addition, the employee always has growth prospects. If he will competently cope with the tasks, then the leadership will definitely pay attention to him. Banks value those people who do not want to be just ordinary operators. Financial institutions are spreading at a tremendous speed today. Hundreds of offices open in cities. Of course, when the question arises of hiring a manager there, first of all, the candidates are considered not for those people who came ‚Äúfrom the street‚Äù, but for their own employees.

However, if you read the reviews of employees about working in a bank, you can find information about the minuses of such employment. Therefore, it is worthwhile to examine in more detail the negative aspects.

Disadvantages of working in a bank

First of all, many note that several decades ago it was really much easier to work in the banking sector, with significantly fewer requirements for employees. They felt comfortable. However, today there are often problems with the bosses, the team, or just unfavorable conditions for employment are provided. Fortunately, of course, this does not happen in large banks, but there are some nuances that may be important in making the right decision.

Considering reviews about working in a bank, one can stumble upon information that, first of all, an employee must sign a liability agreement. In principle, there is nothing terrible in this, since a person is really constantly in contact with large amounts of money. However, it is worth considering that even if the applicant gets a job where contact with the banknotes is not implied, then in this case he will still have to sign the relevant documents. This is what causes many fear and apprehension.

Another drawback noted by some former employees is that they are obliged to obey the senior manager and other people in higher positions without a doubt. Of course, there is nothing reprehensible in this, but problems in the banking sector very often arise against the background of such a hierarchy. Some unscrupulous managers begin to literally morally mock employees and criticize them. But in this case, it all depends not on the bank, but on specific people. Such a leadership can be found in absolutely any field.

Another unpleasant minus is noted mainly by the fair sex. The fact is that at such work it is necessary to adhere to a certain dress code. Moreover, most often in financial organizations it is stipulated that all employees should wear black-and-white clothing. Some companies even issue special vests or uniforms.

According to feedback from employees about working at the bank, some had to deal with the fact that the volume of work was too large. Others complain that the work schedule in such institutions is not always standardized. Sometimes you have to stay up late. But all overtime hours are paid according to the law.

Separately, it is worth noting the disadvantages in reviews about working at home. The bank offers fairly convenient conditions, but the problem lies elsewhere. The fact is that you have to ring up a large number of people. At the same time, you need to talk about new promotions and other products of a financial organization. Potential customers are far from always ready to listen to such advertising. They can easily cheat and call the operator the latest words. If the nervous system is stable, then this should not confuse when applying for a job.

Having considered the pros and cons of such work, it is worthwhile to study in more detail the specific financial organizations and the opinions of the employees who worked in them.
