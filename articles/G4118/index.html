The Sands Khao Lak by Katathani 5 * (Thailand): room description, service, reviews

Thailand is one of the countries of Southeast Asia, in which there are friendly locals, unique attractions, pristine beaches and chic hotels.

It is in the city of Khaulak (Thailand), which is located in the southern region, the hotel The Sands Khao by Katathani is located.

Address and Location

The Sands Khao by Katathani Resort is located at 10/18 Petchkasem rd. Moo 7, Kukkak, Nantong Beach, 82190, Khaulak. From the nearest airport of Phuket go to the hotel about 60 km.

Near the hotel there are: a large Nangthong supermarket, McDonald's, a large market, Tsunami Memorial - Rue Tor 813, a tourist center, Tublamu pier, Sairung waterfall and many others. other

Terms of Accommodation

Rooms must be booked in advance. Guests can check into the hotel after 14:00, and leave strictly in the morning - until 12:00. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.

Guests who book a room in bulk (from three) are offered a special discount system. But this condition is negotiated individually.

Three types of payment cards are accepted for payment:

  1. MasterCard.
  2. VISA.
  3. AMERICAN EXPRESS.

Important! The name and surname of the guest must coincide with the surname and name of the card holder, which must be presented at the time of check-in. In addition, upon registration, vacationers must show identification. For the special wishes of guests, an additional charge may be required.

Allowed to stay with children from one year. A child under 3 years old is provided with a bed upon request, while it is completely free. Children from 4 to 11 years old will be charged 905 rubles for a sleeping place in the room. overnight. If guests ask for an extra bed for a child, then you need to pay a cost of 3,700 rubles. overnight.

In addition, an extra bed is provided for children from 12 years old and adults, but a fee of 3,700 rubles is charged.

The Sands Khao lak by Katathani 5 *: room description

It offers spacious, clean and comfortable rooms that have everything you need for your stay.

There are several types of apartments:

  1. Double Rooms (Twin Sands) with garden or lagoon views. You can choose apartments with one large double or two ordinary single beds.
  2. Family rooms for two adults and two children. They have one sofa bed and one double bed.
  3. Family rooms with access to the pool, where there are two single beds and one large double.
  4. Junior suites with sea views, which have one extra large double bed.
  5. Junior suites with access to the pool. It has only one large single bed.
  6. Two-bedroom suites designed for teens. The first bedroom has one bunk bed and one sofa bed. In the second bedroom there is only one, but a very large bed.

All rooms of The Sands Khao by Katathani 5 * hotel have: air conditioning, bath with shower, TV, satellite channels, DVD player, free Wi-Fi, a seating area with a sofa or comfortable chairs, a wardrobe, a hairdryer, toiletries, a kettle, refrigerator. Junior suites and suites have a telephone, an iPod dock and a balcony.

Prices

Consider the cost of living at The Sands Khao by Katathani 5 * hotel per day. So, the price of a double room with two single beds is only 5383 rubles, and with one large double bed - 5982 rubles.

The cost of family rooms and junior suites with sea views varies from 7558 to 11 662 rubles. The price of junior suites with a separate access to the pool is from 8489 to 13 100 rubles. The cost of family rooms with access to the pool is from 9111 to 14 059 rubles. The price of deluxe rooms is 13 804 and 19 172 rubles.

The price depends on the living conditions, view, booking conditions, etc. However, the price of each ticket includes a good breakfast.

Nutrition

The hotel has two good restaurants serving Western and Thai cuisines. In addition, seafood is cooked without fail. The first restaurant, Floating Marketing, invites hotel guests for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. There is a buffet and a separate menu.

The second restaurant, Talay Restarant, is open only for lunch and dinner. Dishes are offered exclusively on the menu. There is no buffet in this restaurant. In addition, there is a bar near the pool where guests can order any soft drinks.

If you do not want to eat on site, you can go to any beach. There are many restaurants and cafes for every taste. The nearest beach is Nang Tong, where there are also restaurants and cafes where even children can order certain dishes.

Territory

The hotel has two parking lots. One is free, and the second is guarded (paid separately). Sports enthusiasts are offered: indoor and outdoor pools with a personal trainer, tennis court, table tennis, fitness, yoga classes, spa, wellness center, golf course.

In addition, the territory of The Sands Khao lak by Katathani 5 * hotel includes: luggage safes, ticket services, tour desk, round-the-clock registration, several mini-shops, souvenir market, exchange office, ATM, business center, conference room, airport shuttle, car rental, elevator, non-smoking rooms.

It is worth paying attention: not all of the listed services are free. Therefore, before ordering any request, specify the cost.

The Sands Khao lak by Katathani 5 *: service

The hotel service is top notch. The staff is polite, attentive and helpful. Each room is cleaned daily. Bed linen and towels are changed during cleaning.

You can always take your personal belongings to the laundry room. However, this service is paid separately, it is not included in the price of the permit. At any time, guests can call the reception and order room service.

The staff speaks three languages: German, English and Thai. Unfortunately, hotel staff do not speak Russian.

Vacation with children

Of course, many vacationers come to Thailand with children of different ages. As mentioned earlier, at The Sands Khao lak by Katathani 5 * hotel for a child under 3 years of age sleeping places are provided for free. Older children will be charged between 50 and 100%, depending on the room and extra beds.

The hotel has a playroom and a kids club where every child will be interested in spending time. If parents need to leave, you can order a nanny. She will look after the child for as long as necessary. However, this service is paid separately. There is also a small water park, it is designed for older children.

In addition, a playground is provided for children, which is located on the street near the terrace. Therefore, parents can rest peacefully, and the child will be in front of his eyes all the time. Yes, and outside the hotel there are many children's playgrounds. The ideal time to travel with a child to Thailand is May. There is still no heat, but the rainy season has already ended. May is considered the warmest and most pleasant time to relax.
