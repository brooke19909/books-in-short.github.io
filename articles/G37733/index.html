Pension "Sheksna" (Sochi, Krasnodar Territory): photos and reviews of tourists

Sochi is the most popular resort on the Black Sea coast of the Caucasus. Warm sea, comfortable beaches, subtropical vegetation - all this attracts thousands of tourists every year. One of the most comfortable pensions in this city is Sheksna. There is literally everything for a rich, interesting vacation.

Location

Sochi (Krasnodar Territory) is a city divided into four zones. The most prestigious neighborhood - Lazarevsky - is known for its convenient beaches and clean air. The Sheksna complex was built 32 kilometers from the center of Sochi in the immediate vicinity of the village of Vardane in 1995. It is designed to accommodate 795 people.





The boarding house is located on a small hill, away from noisy highways. Its rooms offer magnificent views of the sea and the surrounding picturesque surroundings. Boarding house infrastructure

Resorts in Sochi have recently become increasingly popular with Russian tourists. Those who have recently preferred Turkey or Egypt, today with pleasure go to the Black Sea boarding houses. In Sochi resort area, a lot of new modern complexes have been built recently and old ones have been reconstructed. Holidays in the Black Sea villages have recently become quite prestigious. The Sheksna complex is no exception. There is everything for a comfortable pastime.

Sheksna boarding house hosts guests in eight buildings connected to each other by covered galleries. This is a very quiet complex, the territory of which is not crossed by motorways. Usually families come here. Everything is provided for a comfortable rest for children - several indoor and outdoor pools with water slides, a playground for games, a town, a room with a nanny. Animation takes place day and night.

Sheksna boarding house (Sochi) will be liked not only by children, but also by their parents. The price includes:

Additional services

Laundry and car rental services are also available. If desired, you can hire a driver. Also in the pension there are manicure and beauty parlors. Money can be withdrawn from an ATM. Some additional services in the boarding house are allowed to be paid by bank transfer.

Business tourism

Of course, the best place to spend a business vacation is Sochi. Summer is sunny and hot, and winter is warm. As in all pensions in the Black Sea zone, in Sheksna halls are rented for seminars or symposia. Their participants can not only usefully spend time, but also have a good rest. There are two conference rooms in this boarding house - for 30 and 20 people. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and other modern equipment. In the halls you can use:

  • whiteboard
  • laser pointer

  • equipment for video conferencing or digital shooting,

For a business holiday, the resorts of Sochi are just perfect. And one of the most convenient places for organizing a seminar or conference is the Sheksna complex.

Number of rooms

The rooms in the Sheksna boarding house are modern and comfortable. The complex offers its vacationers single, double and triple rooms. There are also two-room apartments designed for four vacationers. Families with children usually come here. In addition, Sheksna has six two-room junior suites and three suites. There are also studio maxi and studio rooms.

All rooms are equipped with refrigerators, televisions and telephones. Air conditioning is not only in triple rooms. In family rooms, as well as deluxe and junior suites, the bathroom is separate. In double and triple apartments - combined.

Here tourists will find everything for a pleasant pastime. If desired, you can use a deck chair and an umbrella. There are locker rooms, a shower and a massage room.

Medical base

Sochi in the summer is usually not only a vacation, but also a recovery. The Sheksna boarding house has a licensed medical center. Among other things, here you can visit the cabinets of ultrasound therapy, aromatherapy, naphthalanotherapy, rheovasography, electrocardiography, electric glow and some others.

Exclusive treatment

Sheksna boarding house is the only resort institution on the Black Sea coast where health-improving measures are carried out using the technologies developed by the medical professor Fomin. They are aimed at strengthening blood vessels, increasing efficiency, strengthening immunity, etc.

Boarding house address

The Sheksna complex is located at the address: Sochi (Krasnodar Territory), Lazarevsky microdistrict, Vardane settlement, ul. Lviv 8/5. The nearest airport is Adler. Also, by plane you can fly to Krasnodar or Gelendzhik. First, you need to get from the Adler airport to the Avtovokzal stop (Sochi) by bus or minibus number 124. Next, transfer to number 41 or 155. Go to the Sheksna stop.

You can get to the boarding house by train. To do this, you should take a minibus 141 or 155 from the Sochi railway station to the Sheksna stop.
