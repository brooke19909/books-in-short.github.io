Four-star hotel "Jasmine" (Side / Turkey): description of the services of the complex

Connoisseurs of ancient history and beachgoers are increasingly choosing the resort town of Side, where the sun shines year-round. It is here that numerous hotels of various categories are concentrated, offering comfortable conditions for their guests. I would like to highlight the hotel "Jasmine" (Side) - this is one of the best guest houses in its class. A great option for people with low incomes.





Here you will find fascinating and informative sightseeing tours, interesting entertainments, delicious food and a storm of positive emotions. Its opening took place in 2007, in 2010 the 5-storey building, to which three more buildings adjoin, was updated. The Jasmine Hotel (Side) is located 500 meters from the Mediterranean coast, in the picturesque village of Cholakli, an hour's drive from Antalya Airport. A few meters from the building of the complex is a market row with all kinds of food and clothing.

The administration provides a free shuttle bus to its own private beach for its dear guests. All beach amenities are included in the price. For active people, water activities are offered, such as: catamarans, diving, windsurfing, boat trips. There is a bar on the coast where you can have a tasty meal and enjoy oriental sweets. Everyone will be able to purchase interesting tours right in the complex and see the outstanding objects of this magnificent country, get acquainted with its traditions and cultural heritage.

You will spend your holidays calmly and measuredly in this paradise. The staff is very respectful and courteous with all clients, fluent in Russian and always responsive - these are the staff of the Jasmine Hotel (Side). The 4 * hotel will appeal to businessmen, couples with young children, newlyweds and senior citizens. The national composition of holidaymakers is mixed and consists of 50% Germans, 40% Poles and 10% Russian-speaking tourists.





For settlement, the hotel offers excellent rooms of different layouts and areas. Due to the convenient location of the building, the windows of the rooms offer a picturesque view of the blue sea. All rooms have furnished balconies with table and chairs. It has an individual air conditioning system, a comfortable bathroom, a TV, a telephone, a safe and a bar with free water. Employees clean the premises daily and change bedding on certain days.





In the morning, a hot breakfast buffet awaits you. For lunch and dinner, stews, fresh vegetables and fruits are served. From 10 a.m. to 11 p.m., everyone is given alcohol, light snacks and sweets. For those who want to lose weight, the Jasmine Hotel (Side) offers diet food. On the territory of tourists are served restaurants and bars with a rich assortment of alcoholic and non-alcoholic products.

After a busy day, an entertainment program, numerous sports games and activities await customers. All day animators work for you, arranging funny performances for adults and children. For a relaxing pastime, outdoor pools with slides and sun terraces are built on site.





Active leisure supporters can play tennis, volleyball, darts or billiards. The four-star Jasmine Hotel (Side) is equipped with massage rooms, a sauna, its own shops and functional meeting rooms. There are conditions for children, there is a swimming pool and a development club.

You will receive the most comfortable rest from a trip to the Jasmine Hotel (Side). Reviews of most tourists indicate high service, good nutrition and hospitality of the staff.
