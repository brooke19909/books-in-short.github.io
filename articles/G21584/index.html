Jacaranda Hotel Apartments 3 * (Protaras): reviews

Russian tourists are so fond of Cyprus that many of them in their reviews call it the second home, but even for them the small town of Protaras was a discovery. Some 40-50 years ago, fig trees grew in its place, and the Cypriots harvested a good crop there.

Today it is a young resort, which is becoming fashionable due to its location, a beautiful bay with cozy beaches and comfortable hotels. Jacaranda Hotel Apartments 3 * in Protaras is one of them.

Young resort

The sun and sea are much more expensive than fig groves, Cypriots understood this with the advent of the first tourists on their beautiful island. If people are willing to pay to lie on the beach and sunbathe, why not make a profitable business out of it? Since then, Cyprus has become a tourist Mecca, and the young resort of Protaras - one of its pearls.





It just so happened that conditionally the island is divided into separate tourist zones:

  • The beaches and hotels of Larnaca will suit those who are used to getting the best. Here the prices are much higher, but the quality of service corresponds to them.
  • Ayia Napa resort was chosen by party people, so there are so many nightclubs and discos, foam parties and various attractions.
  • Protaras is a zone of silence, tranquility and a graceful family holiday.

Hotel Jacaranda Hotel Apartments 3 (Cyprus, Protaras) is most suitable for parents with young children looking forward to a budget holiday. It provides its guests with an excellent choice: those who do not like the scorching sun, relax near one of the pools, and those who are ready to expose their bodies to ultraviolet radiation go to the beach, which is only 400 m away.

You should not expect a large number of attractions from the town, which 50 years ago was a small village, standing on the site of the ancient Greek policy that had sunk into oblivion. It is unlikely that the remaining ruins will impress those who saw the real ruins, but not for their sake tourists come to Protaras. Jacaranda Hotel Apartments 3 *, according to vacationers, corresponds to the "troika" in its status, and you should not expect more from so many stars in a young developing resort, although the beaches and surrounding nature are beyond praise.

Beaches in Protaras (Cyprus), Jacaranda Hotel Apartments 3 *, reviews from lovers of the sun and the sea are praised, and there are the following reasons for this:

  • Fourthly, the hotel in Protaras Jacaranda Hotel Apartments 3 is located so that from it to other beaches of the resort in less than 15 minutes by bus.
