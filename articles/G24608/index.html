Is Barvikha a Moscow or a Region?

Many have heard of Barvikha, but not everyone knows what she is. Is Barvikha Moscow or the Moscow Region? Let's get it right.

Barvikha - what is it? Many have heard the name, but do not know what kind of settlement it is. Barvikha is an elite village in the Moscow region (Odintsovo district). It is located near the Rublevo-Uspensky highway, 2 km from the Saminka River.





Village History

Not so long ago, back in the 19th century, a pine forest was located on the site of a fashionable cottage village. The local places were called Oborikha (later - Borikh). Today's name was assigned to the settlement in 1920.

The village began to be built by General Alexander Kazakov, the owner of the nearby village of Podushkino. It was such a resort for the extra income of his estates. Already in 1872, the first barrier and gatehouse were erected, which became the analogue of a modern checkpoint in the village.

Barvikha are three elite villages merged together: Barvikha Club, Barvikha Village and Barvikha - 2.

On both sides of the village are hills and ditches. Nearby is the Castle of Baroness Mayendorf, the country residence of the presidents of the state.

Oligarchs in Barvikha

Who lives in an elite village? Of course, there are no poor people to meet. The bulk of the population is politicians and oligarchs. Although one implies the other. All politicians in Barvikha are also oligarchs. Even in the village live celebrities, stars of show business, relatives of wealthy people and so on.

If you are going to buy or rent a cottage in Barvikha, you should know that the presidents and owners of large holdings and corporations will be your closest neighbors: Roman Abramovich, Mikhail Fridman, Mikhail Prokhorov, Vagit Alekperov, Alisher Usmanov, Iskander Makhmudov.





Land value

And this is not a complete list of well-known businessmen who are not at all confused by the local prices for land and houses. Very often, land in Barvikha is compared with the price of gold, with a slight correction for the fact that gold is cheaper.

So, a hundred square meters of land will cost the buyer 150-400 thousand dollars. The streets of the village are packed with exquisite shops. Secular life of local residents takes place either in Barvikha or in the neighboring village of Zhukovka. All of them are found at parties with themselves. Barvikha is a venue for concerts, constant festivals and various fashionable parties.
