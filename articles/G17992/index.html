Amphibian Man: actors and roles

How many of us have not seen a cult Soviet film called Amphibian Man? Actors after the release of the picture on the screen became real stars, and the beautiful cinema story still pleases the hearts of millions of viewers. Recall today those who starred in this film.

The main character

This young man drove him crazy with his beauty; he was beautiful as the Greek god Apollo. His name is Ichthyander. From the first shots, the actor became the idol of the girls of the USSR. Who played the role of a young dolphin man?

Vladimir Korenev - actor (Amphibian Man is his most famous role) - was very young when he was invited to work in the cinema. He did not know much, but his natural charm and beauty helped him. On the set of the film, the actor tried to do without stuntmen, starred in difficult scenes on land and in water.

Glory came immediately after the premiere of the film in 1962. More than in any picture, the young actor failed to play like that. His filmography in the 70-80 years. extensive, but there were mostly episodic roles. Kornev at that time began to pay more attention to theatrical works.

Amphibian Man: Supporting Actors

In addition to the images of the three main characters (a couple in love and one villain), the film has many bold creative works.

Nikolay Simonov brilliantly coped with the role of an old professor, the father of a wonderful youth dolphin. The professor serves science with all his heart and cannot conspire with people who have neither honor nor conscience.

Anatoly Smiranin also brilliantly played the role of the father of the main character - the old sailor, who, because of poverty, is forced to give his beloved daughter to marry a rich man with a dubious reputation.

Vladlen Davydov played the role of a young journalist, unrequitedly in love with the beautiful main character. And although the girl does not share his feelings, most likely she will share his life and her fate with him, because this young journalist is unusually honest, noble and brave.
