How much do tablets of different brands cost?

Tablet computers at the dawn of their appearance were positioned as devices that could "kill" laptops. But that did not happen. Still, there is something in these touchscreen gadgets that does not allow them to fully compete with familiar laptops. And their percentage in the modern market of mobile devices is not so high. However, there are people who suit them. And a completely logical question appears. How much does a tablet from a specific manufacturer cost? Try to answer this question. But first, some general information.

What is a tablet?

A tablet is a mobile device with a touch screen, created on the principle of a smartphone, but different in size. The tablet computer can be with screen sizes of 7, 9, 10 and 12 inches. It all depends on the specific manufacturer, model and status of the device. The first tablets were created by Apple after the triumph of their iPhone smartphone. Then the tablets were positioned as devices for entertainment. No one intended to use them for productive work. But everything has changed. So the question arose sharply about how much the tablet costs? But the answer is not as simple as it seems.

In fact, almost all tablets are devices with an increased screen size and good performance. Only apple products of the Cupertino company of the same name can boast of relatively high prices. All other gadgets of this kind are quite affordable for the average user. Of course, it all depends on the model, hardware platform and device status. But especially the prices do not differ. Horse racing is negligible. So how much does a tablet cost from different brands? To answer this question, you will need to consider the devices of the most popular manufacturers and look at their prices.

Apple iPad

These are probably the most expensive tablets in our ranking. And it does not matter what the hardware platform of these devices is. The price will still be sky-high. How much does an Apple tablet cost? It all depends on what year the device is manufactured. The price of the iPad 32 GB in 2017 starts at 23,000 Russian rubles. But there are also various modifications. For example, a ten-inch iPad Pro will cost 46,000 Russian rubles. And it is in the basic configuration. The most affordable tablets from this manufacturer are iPad Air and iPad Mini devices. They cost 20 and 22 thousand rubles, respectively. The buyer receives a high-quality device with wide functionality and proprietary iOS on board. The hardware platform of these tablets is such that they will be relevant for another 2-3 years for sure. And in the secondary market, you can purchase these devices even cheaper. There, the price tag for the iPad Mini starts at 8,000 rubles.

Samsung Galaxy Tab

These tablets are cheaper than "apple", but also very good. It should be noted that Koreans have long been fighting with the Cupertians in this segment. So how much does the Samsung tablet cost? The answer to this question depends on the model of the device, its year of manufacture and positioning. So, for example, the Galaxy Tab 2017 release with 8 gigabytes of internal memory, 7 inches of screen and a dual-core processor on board will cost almost 9,000 Russian rubles. And the lite version of the same tablet is only 5,000. But this is the most budget option. There are higher class models in the arsenal of Koreans. So, a ten-inch Galaxy Tab 3 with 16 gigabytes of internal memory will cost 14,000 Russian rubles. A modification of the same version - at 15,000. And what about the premium segment? There are such models in the Samsung catalog. For example, Galaxy Tab S3 costs as much as 46,000 Russian rubles. But it has a quad-core processor, 4 gigabytes of RAM and 32 built-in and ten-inch screens with 2K resolution. A stylus is included with the device. So, how much does the Galaxy tablet cost, we already know. Let's move on to other manufacturers.

Conclusion

So, we figured out the question of how much the Samsung Galaxy tablet (and not only it) costs. The price tag for the device depends on the year of manufacture, the hardware platform, performance, quality of the materials used, the diagonal and screen resolution and the brand itself. Each manufacturer (except, probably, Apple) has a budget segment model. And mid-range devices that can satisfy any user are also available. The main thing is to carefully study the market and understand what exactly you need. And there is no shortage in the assortment.
