Rest on Baikal. Comfortable guest house Goryachinsk

The cleanest and deepest lake in the world attracts tourists from all over the globe. The local beauties give an incomparable, vivid impression.

Lake Baikal is an amazing place. Here you can relax not only physically, but also psychologically. Unusual nature, clean air, friendly and surprisingly hospitable locals who are glad to everyone who wants to get acquainted with their small homeland - all this contributes to a good mood and restoration of strength. In addition, you can undergo treatment in sanatoriums.



Lake Baikal has long been attractive to tourists from Europe. They want to see with their own eyes a wonderful natural object with crystal clear water, the depth of which at some points reaches 1698 meters.

Goryachinsk, Kultushnaya, Gremyachinsk, Maksimikha, Enkhaluk, Baikal surf Sukhaya - this is not a complete list of amazing places where you can perfectly relax on Lake Baikal. But today we will go to Goryachinsk. You will learn how remarkable it is, how you can spend your vacation here.

Goryachinsk

The village of Goryachinsk is located in Buryatia on the shores of the famous lake, 188 kilometers from the city of Ulan-Ude. It got its name in honor of hot healing springs.

The magnificent nature of these places with picturesque bays, well-groomed sandy beaches, coniferous forests and hot springs is as if created for relaxation and treatment.



Goryachinsk has a special microclimate - it is noticeably warmer in winter and not too hot in summer. But the main advantage of this small village is thermal nitrogen-silicon springs and therapeutic mud, which have antispasmodic, anti-inflammatory, analgesic properties. In the sanatorium "Goryachinsk", which is located in the taiga, adults and children can relax and improve their health.

Here you can improve the condition of the musculoskeletal system, get rid of many skin diseases, strengthen the respiratory system.

Summer rest

In Goryachinsk you can have a great rest both in summer and in winter. In summer, there is expanse for lovers of beach holidays - you can swim in the lake and sunbathe on the sandy beach.

Fans of excursions will not have to be bored either. You can visit the famous Barguzinsky National Park, go to the Goddess Yanzhim, on Ushkany Islands, the Ivolginsky Datsan and visit other attractions - the Turtle Rock, Cape Bezymyanny, Svetly Polyana.

Winter in Goryachinsk

Do you like snow, skiing, sledging? Come to Goryachinsk! Winter is warm and snowy. You can ride a snowmobile. But there are especially many ice fishing enthusiasts in these places.

Gorhon

This is a very popular mini-hotel. Gorkhon Guest House in Goryachinsk is located on the eastern shore of the lake, a few minutes' walk from the sanatorium mentioned above.
