Beltur Hotel 4 * (Turkey): description, prices, reviews

Turkey, many people call the country of universal vacation. This opinion has developed about her for several reasons:

  • The country has a warm climate.
  • There are many natural and historical attractions.
  • The coast of Turkey is washed immediately by four seas.
  • Everyone of average income can afford to rest here.




The country has a unique and very healthy microclimate, there is a huge coastline, a diverse flora, insanely beautiful views, high mountain peaks. Deciding on the best holiday destination in Turkey is very difficult. Each region of the country is special and deserves the attention of visiting guests. But nevertheless, a significant part of travelers prefers to rest on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. Here is the legendary city of Kemer, beautiful views, emerald and crystal clear sea water. In Kemer itself and in the surrounding villages there is a huge variety of hotels - from luxury to economical, one of which we will discuss in this review.

Beltur Hotel 4 * (Kemer)

One of the many Turkish hotels located in the Beldibi area. It was opened thirty years ago and until this year it was considered a three-star hotel of economy class. Note that in the beginning of the summer of this year Beltur was renovated, and now it received 4 stars according to the European classification of standards. The hotel consists of five three-story buildings. Located on the territory of six thousand square meters, on the second coastline, 182 standard rooms are provided for guests to stay here.

Hotel location

Beltur Hotel 4 * is located in the center of a quiet and peaceful village called Beldibi, surrounded by beautiful mountain peaks and dense deciduous forest. The journey from Antalya Airport to the hotel complex takes about an hour. The center of Kemer can be reached by public transport in 15-20 minutes, the fare is $ 2 one way.

The village is ideal for a quiet family vacation and for people seeking privacy and tranquility.

Infrastructure

Beltur Hotel 4 * (Kemer) is a standard hotel, not different from ordinary economical Turkish complexes. The hotel has a restaurant, two swimming pools (one with a water slide), three bars, a sauna, a hammam, a SPA center, a TV room, a souvenir shop, a currency exchange, shops, parking, a conference room.

At 1 00 meters, across the road from Beltur Hotel 4 *, there is a pebble beach Beldibi Beach with a length of fifty meters. Mattresses, sun loungers, towels and umbrellas on the beach are provided free of charge. The entrance to the sea is pebbles, the bottom is sand.

Hotel reviews

Judging by the reviews, Beltur Hotel 4 * is suitable for picky tourists and for those who do not like to spend a lot of time in the hotel. Usually people who bought a tour at a low price were satisfied with their vacation, but tourists who paid decent money expected more from their vacation.

Among the advantages of "Beltura" the guests distinguish:

  • Cleanliness both in the territory and in the hotel rooms.
  • Low cost of living.
  • Proximity to the sea.
  • Large landscaped area.
  • Nice pool.
  • Beautiful nature in the area.
  • The polite staff.




If we talk about the shortcomings, many say:

  • Old furniture.
  • Simple rooms with a damp smell.
  • Monotonous food.
  • The lines in the dining room.
  • Weak animation.
  • Lack of sunbeds on the beach.

Cost of the tour to Beltur

Taking into account all the pros and cons of this hotel, the cost of a week's stay in it with the All-Inclusive food system is very tempting. For two adults and a child, the price of a ticket starts from 55 thousand rubles. The price may vary depending on the city of departure and the date of purchase of the tour. When buying a voucher for the "early booking" or "last-minute offer" promotion, you can relax in the above hotel for 40 thousand rubles. Estimated price for a trip for two adults 30-40 thousand rubles (7 days).

Conclusion

Before you go to rest in Beltur Hotel 4 *, you need to understand that this hotel is a fairly economical and budget option for a vacation. In general, the hotel is not bad, but with its minuses. If you are not confused by the old number of rooms, a weak animation program and a simple kitchen, then you should like it here. But if you consider yourself a rather demanding tourist, then it is better to look for a different accommodation option for your vacation, as, most likely, this hotel will disappoint you.
