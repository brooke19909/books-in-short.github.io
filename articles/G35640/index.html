The best beaches of Goa

The small state of exotic India attracts many tourists to its coast from year to year. The best beaches of Goa are liked by couples, and fans of extreme sports, and youth. Everyone can choose a vacation to their liking.

The main directions are north or south of the state. What beaches in these places deserve attention?

The best beaches of Goa in the north

Lists of special places for a seaside vacation necessarily include Anjuna Beach. This is the heart of the state, once popular with rastamans and hippies. The spirit of freedom and music is alive here and now. The local market is full of exoticism and colors, and along the coast you can relax in a variety of bars for every taste. But only in the sea you need to swim carefully - there are coral reefs.

Those who want a more relaxed and measured vacation, it is better to go to Arambol beach. This place also has the right to be indicated in any list that lists the best beaches of Goa. Reviews dedicated to Arambol mark a creative, inspiring atmosphere. Here you can do yoga on the purest white sand in the morning, and in the evening arrange open-air dances. Living in these places is best in guesthouses - they are located on the first line of the beach. Nearby is a freshwater lake

Sweet Lake, which has a banyan tree, known for meditating under its crown throughout the famous Four Beatles.

Fans of youth vacations can go to Vagator beach: there are rather low prices and a varied nightlife. In addition, the place is very picturesque, surrounded by cliffs and famous for a beautiful sunset.

Looking for the best beach in northern Goa for relaxing with young children, pay attention to Mandrem. Fine sand, silence and clear water are ideal for families with any budget. The colorful surroundings will not let adults get bored, and children can engage in gathering starfish on the shore.

The best beaches of Goa in the south

In the southern part of the state stands out Palolem Beach, famous for the lack of waves.

A beautiful bay surrounded by coconut palms is perfect for families with children. If you wish, you can hire a kayak or a boat and go to visit the dolphins. In the evening, cafes and bars are located off the coast.

For a romantic retreat, go to Varca Beach. There is pure white sand, warm water and silence. For living, you can choose both a democratic bungalow and a five-star hotel. Of course, the prices here are quite high, but this is typical for the entire southern coast. Listing the best beaches of Goa, it is also worth mentioning such as Cavelossim, Colva, Utorda and Majorda. These places are similar to Varca beach with their privacy and a wide selection of places to stay.

Finally, the pearl of the south is Cola. A small bay surrounded by hills looks extremely picturesque. A river flowing into the ocean creates a blue lagoon, a quiet and secluded place full of magical landscapes. There is only one restaurant and several hotels. But there is a drawback - big waves. Therefore, swimming with children will not be very convenient, which means that for a family vacation it is better to choose another beach.
