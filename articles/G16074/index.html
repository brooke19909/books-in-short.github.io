Hotel "Plaza" (Volgograd): description of rooms, service, reviews

The hotel "Plaza" in Volgograd is always ready to open its doors to its guests. The city will give you a lot of opportunities for work and tourism, and this hotel will provide a good rest.





A bit about the hotel

Hotel Plaza opened its doors to the first tourists back in 2012. This is a modern hotel complex located in the distance from the busy city roads. Qualified staff will provide guests with absolute comfort. You can contact the administrator for help at any time of the day or ask a question that interests you.

For a nutritious meal guests are served by a luxurious restaurant, where a rich and delicious breakfast is served every morning. There is also a cookery on site and a bar where you can eat during the day. The hotel provides all the necessary conditions for productive work and sports.

Hotel address

The hotel has a convenient location. 2/1 Novouzenskaya Street, Volgograd, Russia - the address of this institution. This is the historical and business center of the city, where a lot of attractions, as well as infrastructure facilities, are concentrated. To get here, you can order a transfer or just call a taxi, knowing the address of the hotel. I must say that a vacation in Volgograd is simply unthinkable without visiting its attractions and theaters, and therefore it is worth paying attention to the proximity of the institution to such urban sites:

  • New experimental theater (3 km);
  • river station (3 km);
  • Memorial and History Museum (3 km);
  • Alley of Heroes (3 km);
  • Youth Theater (3 km);
  • Pavlov's house (4 km);

Hotel "Plaza" (Volgograd): room description

Place of residence plays an important role in the overall impression of the holiday. Comfortable rooms are offered to its guests by the Plaza Hotel. You will enjoy Volgograd even more if you stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • Suite is a two-room suite, which has an exquisite interior and modern facilities. The living room has a set of upholstered furniture and a TV for you, and the bedroom has a comfortable double bed.
  • Comfort is a spacious cozy room that has a stylish and calm design. A large bed and a folding armchair are provided for guests. It is also worth noting the availability of a workplace.
  • Economy - a budget accommodation option for two guests. A pair of one and a half beds is provided for guests. The interior is made in pleasant light colors.
  • Economy twin differs from the previous category by the presence of a large double bed. The interior is dominated by classic elements, wrought iron decor and textiles.
  • VIP is a chic two-room suite for the most demanding tourists. It is also a great place for newlyweds. Given the availability of a full-fledged working area, these apartments are also suitable for business people. the central place in the room is a four-poster bed.

Hotel Policies

A trip to Volgograd is a fascinating journey for tourists and unlimited opportunities for business people. If you decide to stay at the Plaza Hotel, you should familiarize yourself with these rules of accommodation in this institution in advance:

  • If you intend to extend the period of stay at the hotel, the guest must inform the administrator about this no later than one day;
  • Checkout time - noon;
  • registration and check-in is carried out exclusively in the presence of identity documents;
  • when staying in a hotel for less than 24 hours, payment for the room is still made in full;
  • strangers are allowed in the room only between 08:00 and midnight;
  • if an unauthorized visitor intends to stay in the room after midnight, registration of the resettlement is carried out with the corresponding payment;
  • bed linen is changed every three days, towels are changed daily, a cosmetic set is provided only upon check-in;
  • when causing damage to the hotel, the guest fully pays for the cost of the damaged property;
  • Pets are not allowed.

Negative reviews

Even in the most expensive and luxurious hotel, as a rule, one cannot do without negativity. The Plaza Hotel (Volgograd) was no exception. Reviews of it contain negative comments as follows:

  • the rooms are very hot, despite the presence of air conditioning (no matter how you try to regulate the temperature, nothing changes);
  • the attitude of the staff towards the guests can be called dismissive (with the exception of individual employees);
  • there is no hood in the bathroom, which is the reason for the increased humidity;
  • in the information folder, which is provided in the room, there is no information about the menu and prices of the restaurant;
  • when paying for services in cash, the guests are not given a check;
  • the breakfast menu is quite scarce, in addition, if you linger by the beginning you will have to be content with leftovers (products at the distribution do not replenish);
