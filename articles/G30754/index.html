Hotel Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * (United Arab Emirates): overview, features and reviews

Dubai is a modern city, the popularity of which is growing among tourists every year. It attracts people not only the warm sea, but also the unique architecture of the city. Dubai is hardly a place for a budget holiday. If desired, tourists can find an inexpensive hotel in an area remote from the sea, however, most resort complexes have a five-star category, so accommodation is expensive. But the service here is fully consistent with the prices.

For example, the Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * hotel is considered respectable. Where is she located? What level of service does it offer? Did you like the local service for tourists? We will try to answer all these questions below.

Hotel Location Information

The modern complex is located in the central part of tourist Dubai, where other luxury hotels are built. It is located on the man-made sand island of Palm Jumeirah, which is connected to the city by a bridge. Most of its territory is occupied by hotels, tourist infrastructure, villas of wealthy residents of the city. You can get to the center of Dubai by bus, metro or car. The Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah Hotel itself is built right on the beach, so you can walk to it in a few minutes. In addition, there is a large water park nearby, which can be reached by car or bus in 10 minutes. You can get to the central part of Dubai, where there are iconic attractions, large shopping centers and the tallest skyscraper in the world, the Burj Khalifa in half an hour.

The city's international airport is 42 km away. Therefore, future guests of the complex will have to spend about 1 hour on the road. A paid shuttle service is available if you wish. A comfortable bus equipped with air conditioning will pick them up from the airport and deliver them to the complex as soon as possible. Also, tourists can independently get to it by taxi, rented car, bus and metro.

General information about the complex

The opening of this luxury hotel took place in 2013, so it is considered a new and modern tourist complex. Its convenient location is ideal for relaxing pastime in the company of family or friends. Tourists with small children often come here. At the same time, iconic sights and shopping centers are close enough so that guests do not spend much time on the road to them. This hotel is also famous for the modern interiors of its rooms, decorated in a luxurious classic style. There are 319 of them in the complex. Among them there are huge apartments and standard double rooms.

Tourists are accommodated in the main eight-story hotel building. It also has its own sandy beach, several swimming pools with sun terraces, restaurants and bars, as well as a spa and sports grounds. For a fee, the hotel staff is happy to organize a wedding or any other festive ceremony for guests.

Check-in starts at the hotel from 15:00 local time. However, in the presence of free rooms, tourists try to accommodate earlier than this time. When registering, you need to present to the administrator not only the necessary set of documents, but also a cash deposit. He returns to tourists after the rest. Guests must leave their rooms no later than noon, so that the staff have time to prepare rooms for new guests.

This complex cannot be called an inexpensive place to stay. Prices at Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * are quite high even in low season. As a rule, a double standard room will cost about 13,000 rubles per night. At the same time, fees for respectable and spacious apartments can reach 60,000 rubles per day.

The number of rooms of the hotel Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * in the UAE

It has already been said above that there are 319 rooms in the hotel. Each of them boasts luxurious decoration and a large area. They are decorated in an elegant classic style. The living rooms offer views of the Persian Gulf or the surrounding Dubai. Some of the rooms are equipped with additional amenities for guests with reduced mobility. Some apartments are adjoining, so for convenience they are interconnected by an internal door. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms.

The housing stock of the Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * complex consists of the following apartment categories:

  • King Superior Room. It consists of one bedroom with a separate living area, a separate bathroom and a balcony with views of the sea and Dubai. Room size varies from 55 to 64 square meters. m. Here 3 adults can live right away.
  • Queen Deluxe Skyline. Also a room with the same area. However, it has 2 double beds, so 4 people can live here at the same time.
  • Deluxe Suite. Spacious apartment consisting of a bedroom with a large double bed, a living room, two separate bathrooms and a balcony. Can accommodate up to 4 adult guests. Room size can vary from 100 to 126 square meters. m
  • Waldorf Astoria Suite. Room for wealthy guests, consisting of a living room with a separate dining area, a bar, a bathroom, a separate kitchen and a bedroom. The balcony offers sea views. The apartment can accommodate 4 guests. Room size varies from 170 to 190 square meters. m
  • 2 Bedroom Deluxe Suite. This apartment accommodates up to 7 guests. Their area is 155-190 square meters. m

Room Amenities

Checking into the rooms of the Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * hotel, tourists can appreciate their first-class equipment. They have not only furniture, but also a host of amenities that make living in this place truly comfortable. We list the main ones:

  • Plasma TV.
  • Free wireless internet.
  • Marble bathtub and bidet.
  • Electronic safe for valuables.
  • Ironing board and iron.
  • Central air conditioning.
  • A set for making hot drinks, as well as your own coffee machine.
  • A bathrobe, slippers and several towels for each guest.
  • Cosmetic accessories (shampoo, liquid and bath soap, shower gel, toothpaste and others).
  • Hair dryer.
  • Minibar, which is replenished for a fee.

The maids at the hotel clean the rooms every day, take out the garbage, ventilate the room and change the bedding for a new one. Guests can use the apartment service: order food, hot and cold drinks and cocktails, newspapers, call a doctor, but at an additional cost. In addition, some rooms have additional privileges. This is a separate living room for their guests, and individual accelerated registration, and free hot drinks and cocktails throughout the day.

The territory of the complex and its infrastructure

Hotel Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * has a very large territory, which hosts many diverse facilities. These are restaurants, shops, conference rooms, spa. Therefore, if desired, tourists can have a good rest without leaving the hotel. We will tell you about the most popular objects that guests can visit while living in the hotel:

  • 24 hour free parking. You can arrange car rental at reception.
  • Internet cafe in WA Lounge. Wi-Fi access is available throughout.
  • ATM on the ground floor and currency exchange.
  • A 24-hour laundry service, however, its employees provide their services only for a fee.
  • Beauty salon, hairdresser.
  • Several shops, including souvenir and jewelry.

In addition, the hotel has a huge business center equipped with modern computer technology. Here, tourists can rent 6 conference rooms accommodating from 40 to 600 guests. The hotel also has a ballroom and banquet facilities for weddings and other celebrations.

Catering

When paying for accommodation, future guests can choose one of the following food concepts offered at the hotel:

  • BB - guests pay only for breakfast.
  • HB - this system includes not only a morning meal, but also lunch or dinner to choose from.
  • FB is a full board consisting of a full three meals a day, but you will have to pay separately for drinks, desserts and other snacks.

All meals are served in the Mezzerie's main restaurant. A shared buffet is served daily here. At the same time, they serve not only international dishes, but also exotic delicacies. Every day of the week they are new. For example, on Monday at the Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * hotel (Jumeirah), you can try Asian dishes, and on Thursday - Arabic.

Beach and Pools

The resort complex Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * (UAE, Dubai) has its own sandy beach with a length of about 200 meters. The coast is fully equipped for a comfortable and safe swim in the sea. Sun beds, umbrellas and towels are provided free of charge. The beach also has a volleyball court.

In addition, the hotel has 2 swimming pools, equipped with an additional temperature control mode. Their area is 320 and 760 square meters. m. Tourists can relax near the pools on the sunbathing area. Like the beach, there are also sun loungers and umbrellas that save from the hot sun.

Sports and recreation

For its guests, the Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * hotel in Dubai has prepared a lot of options for outdoor activities. Sports enthusiasts can visit the gym, as well as go to tennis or aerobics classes. On the beach there is a water entertainment center where you can ride a "banana" or water skiing. Guests can play tennis or windsurf, paying extra for equipment rental.

Fans of a relaxing holiday will also find something to their liking. The hotel has a huge spa, where for a fee you can take a course of beauty and wellness treatments. Guests can also enjoy massage services, a jacuzzi, a Turkish bath, a steam room or a sauna.

Conditions for staying with small children

Quite often, the hotel Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * in the UAE is chosen by tourists with children, as it is wonderful for a relaxing pastime. For the little guests there are prepared all the necessary amenities. Upon request, guests can receive a cradle for babies, which can be delivered free of charge to the room. The restaurants have high chairs for feeding. If desired, tourists are provided with a stroller. They can also book a babysitter in the room. For entertainment for children, the hotel has its own mini-club, a games room and a private pool, located in the shade.

Positive feedback about Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 stars

Tourists often appreciate their stay at this hotel. Many people like the quality of local service, so they believe that the high cost of living here is justified. The advantages of this hotel tourists call:

  • Great location and great views of the sea, Dubai and other nearby hotels.
  • A varied diet - every day a huge selection of dishes is presented at the buffet.
  • Daily maids bring guests a basket of fresh fruits.
  • Friendly staff who speaks excellent English and Russian.
  • Good soundproofing in the rooms, so they are always quiet.
  • On the beach and by the pool serves not only drinks, but also make a hookah.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, the perfect resort complexes do not exist. The Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah hotel (Dubai, UAE) was not an exception, which was not liked by all tourists. Some of them in their reviews describe not only its advantages, but also disadvantages. Often they can not be called significant, but some of them can spoil the impression of rest. Tourists most often point out the following disadvantages:

  • Slow waiters in restaurants.
  • The price does not include drinks, only food.
  • The hotel has few trees and shrubs.
  • Air conditioners operate at full capacity, so the rooms are sometimes too cold.
  • On the beach in the sand you can injure yourself on small shells and stones, so it is better to wear beach shoes.
  • Jellyfish can come into the sea.

To summarize

Thus, we can say that the beach hotel Waldorf Astoria Dubai Palm Jumeirah 5 * in Dubai is ideal for a first-class vacation in the UAE. Tourists positively appreciated the local level of service, although they do not deny that the complex has its own minor flaws. The prices for accommodation at the hotel can hardly be called budget, but they are fully justified by the quality of service, luxurious rooms, a clean beach and varied food.
