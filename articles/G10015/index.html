Acoustic system Morel Tempo 6: description, specifications, reviews

The Morel brand is a very well-known brand among manufacturers of car speaker systems. This article will discuss the component system Morel Tempo 6. What is this technique? These devices are an exploded system of car speakers, that is, basses and tweeters are made by separate nodes.



Description of Morel Tempo 6

The Tempo component system is a successful combination of high-quality acoustics and affordable prices. Using the truly legendary Morel technology, the new Morel Tempo 6 speakers offer lovers of high-quality sound the best performance of this class of systems. The diameter of the tweeter is 28 mm, it has a soft dome made using EVC technology, which allows us to talk about a new quality standard. Such a dome is able to provide an expanded frequency-amplitude characteristic, picturesque sound and expressive scene. There are four different ways to install a new tweeter. The midbass of the Morel Tempo 6 component system is equipped with fairly powerful ferrite magnets, thanks to which the speakers are highly efficient. However, the size of such a magnet is 35 percent smaller compared to similar ferrite magnets. This allows you to significantly reduce the installation depth of the products. The speaker diffuser is designed to provide even and clear reproduction of the lower mid-range, as well as dynamic bass. A lot of attention was paid to the design of the crossover of the Morel Tempo comp 6 system: it is made of high-quality components, as a result of which any obstacles to the signal passage are eliminated. To provide just awesome sound, the crossover can adjust the tweeter with a slope of the frequency response of 0 dB and +3 dB. The manufacturer claims the rated power of the system is 120 watts.



Specifications:

  • sensitivity - 90 dB;
  • rated power - 120 W;
  • maximum power (peak) - 280 W;
  • landing depth - 62 mm;
  • resistance - 4O m;
  • crossover section frequency - 3800 Hz;
  • frequency response - 40 Hz - 10000 kHz;
  • crossover slope - 6 dB / oct;
  • the size of the low-frequency speaker is 162.5 mm;
  • voice coil speaker low frequency - 25 mm;
  • the material of the high-frequency diffuser is silk;
  • the size of the high-frequency speaker is 45 mm;
  • mounting diameter - 50 mm;
  • installation depth of the high-frequency speaker - 20 mm;
  • diffuser type - dome;
  • high frequency speaker voice coil - 27.5 mm.

Morel Tempo 6: reviews

According to audio enthusiasts, the Morel Tempo 6 car speaker system is characterized by perfect sound. The high frequencies of such speakers, however, like all Morel models, are beautiful and musical. The bass is crisp, powerful and resilient. Due to its high sensitivity, this speaker system can be placed not only under the car radio, but also under the sound amplifier. Although Morel-Tempo is the foreground car speakers, it can also be installed back (for example, as an extra) into the competition class system. Tweeters, when positioned correctly, will not drag the scene to the background.
