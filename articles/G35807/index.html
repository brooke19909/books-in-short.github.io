Where is the hotel built?

This cozy small hotel is not located directly in Side, but in its suburbs. The center of this resort is about 1 km from Selge Hotel 3 * d . That is, if you want to walk to the lively areas of Side, tourists can even just walk - in 20-30 minutes. This many vacationers, of course, are attributed to the advantages of the hotel. At 7 kilometers from the complex is another quite popular small tourist village Manavgat, quite popular with tourists from all over the world.





Unfortunately, this hotel is located quite far from the airport. The nearest international air transport hub is located in Antalya, 65 km away.
