The recreation center "Forest Lake" in Stavropol: description and reviews

A great place to spend a vacation and weekend with family or friends can be the recreation center "Forest Lake" in Stavropol. A picturesque pond surrounded by forest can be an ideal place for a wedding or corporate party.

Description

Forest Lake is located in an ecologically clean area in the village of Staroruskoye. Stavropol from this wonderful place is at a distance of 15 kilometers. At the camp site there is the opportunity to relax without living.

The purchase of an entrance ticket to the recreation center "Forest Lake" gives the right to rent a gazebo with barbecue, where you can spend time outdoors. Car owners enjoy free parking at the base, and a playground for sports is also provided for adults. The necessary products can be bought at a store located on the premises. Vacationers can eat both independently and take advantage of the restaurant.

Also at the camp site a small company can comfortably sit in the Military Headquarters cafe, furnished in the style of a military. Here you can spend your family celebration or a corporate party in the banquet hall of the restaurant, designed for 120 people. There is a playground for the kids.

The camp site is open all year.

Located "Forest Lake" at the address: Stavropol, Shpakovsky district, the village of Verhnerusskoye, Podgornaya street, 326/1.

Accommodation

Vacationers are accommodated in guest houses of two types - ordinary or with a bath.

A simple guest house at the recreation center "Forest Lake" can simultaneously accommodate six people. The apartment consists of two rooms - a bedroom and a living room. The latter has upholstered furniture, a TV, and the bedroom has several single beds. A small refrigerator is provided for storing food in the house, and lunch can be warmed up in the microwave. For cooking there is the necessary utensils. The room also has a shower and toilet. On the territory adjacent to the house is equipped with a parking space, as well as a gazebo and barbecue.

A house with a bathhouse is ready to receive four people. The living room is equipped with a TV, armchairs, necessary furniture. The bedroom has four single beds. For cooking, there is a microwave and utensils, a refrigerator. This cabin has a private bathroom and a private entrance to the bathhouse. In the surrounding area there is the opportunity to cook barbecue on the grill and relax in a cozy gazebo.

The cost of living at the camp site "Forest Lake" in Stavropol starts from 5,000 rubles per day.

Services

Of course, in nature, vacationers should not be bored. Therefore, the administration of the Forest Lake base in Stavropol offers:

  • take a steam bath in a real Russian bath;
  • plunge after the bathhouse into the pool with clean spring water;
  • swim and sunbathe on the lake on an ennobled beach;
  • avid fishermen catch trout in a separate, specially equipped pond. If desired, the cook will prepare a catch;
  • celebrate the wedding. The wedding ceremony is held on the shore of the lake, and in the restaurant experienced specialists organize a gorgeous banquet;
  • hold a photo shoot. On the playground there are many figures of famous cartoon characters and forest animals;
  • use the parking lot and sports field, and for free;

You can replenish food supplies in the store, which is located in the recreation center.

The recreation center "Forest Lake" (Stavropol). Tourists reviews

Tourists leave interesting and informative reviews about their vacation in Forest Lake. From them, for example, you can find out that the "Forest Lake" is a clean and well-groomed place for cultural rest and springs flow into it, which is why the water in the pond is always cool, and it is nice in hot weather.

Vacationers by large companies note that there are a lot of greenery and very fresh air in the base. And if you want to cook kebab, then coal can be purchased from the administration. There is lighting in the arbors, so you can stay up late.

Many people write that it is very convenient when, in addition to the table and benches in the arbor itself, there are additional tables and benches near the arbor and near the barbecue.

In addition, there is a football field and a volleyball court on site. Couples with children notice that on the playground there are usual slides and swings, as well as a trampoline (true, paid).
