Where to look for the beach: beachfront amenities





When deciding to spend a vacation in Vietnam, many tourists expect a pleasant pastime on a sunny beach. What can the Sophia Sky Hotel 2 * offer? Nha Trang is famous for its beautiful coast. The golden beach is located just 300 meters from the hotel building, a convenient road leads to it. For most travelers, daily walks are enjoyable.

According to reviews, the coast is clean, and the entrance to the sea is gradual. By the way, a rescue team is constantly working on the shore, so tourists with their children can spend time under watchful eye. How to have fun on the beach?

It is always pleasant to bask in the sun on the beach and swim in the warm sea. But many travelers prefer to spend their holidays more actively. Are there appropriate conditions on the seashore?

Of course, entertainment on a public beach is enough. Tourists love water sports, snorkelling, boating, catamarans and water skiing. You will have the opportunity to try sailing and windsurfing. On the shore, you can rent the necessary equipment and even take the help of an experienced instructor.

Additional service





Having settled in a hotel, tourists can count on some additional conditions. Despite the small territory, parking is available near the building, which guests use for free. In the hotel you can rent a moped, car or even a bicycle, and at a very reasonable price.
