We plan a vacation: how to get to Anapa by car

Anapa is a city that has been loved by tourists since the days of the Soviet Union. Here were located the best motels and health centers. The modern city of the Krasnodar Territory has a developed infrastructure, sea, sun and healing air. Today's tourists are happy to come here from year to year, not only to relax by the sea, but also to fully enjoy the beautiful scenery. Many people have a question about how to get to Anapa by car. In this article we will answer it.





History of the city

The development of the city began from the moment when Anapa became part of Russia. The previously unknown city developed so rapidly that it soon had the proud title of the largest port city. The development of the port entailed a flow of tourists, the development of Anapa in the resort and health direction began.

By fate, the city got an amazing place on the map. It is at this point that the Taman Peninsula and the Greater Caucasus intersect. A gorgeous combination of the plain Kuban region with the Black Sea and strong mountains. Anapa is able to give a tourist an unimaginable range of feelings. And the purest mountain-sea air will positively affect the health of the body. So feel free to go by car to Anapa.

Anapa beaches





The incredible beaches of Anapa have been awarded a separate theme . After all, where else in Russia can you find such a place in which the former river serves as the current beaches. Earlier, the Kuban River flowed into the Black Sea, but over time its channel changed, and the reservoir itself dried up. From now on, magnificent sandy beaches with a gentle entrance to the sea flaunt in its place. This is an ideal place to relax with children, here there is sand and relatively shallow. An impressive coastline allows you to feel comfortable even during the high tourist season. It remains to figure out how to get to Anapa by car.

How to get there

The convenient location of the city will allow you to quickly, comfortably and cheaply get here from anywhere in Russia. This resort can be a great alternative to overseas travel. You do not have to pay for an expensive flight or visa, and this will help to save a significant part of your budget. There are several options to get to Anapa. The choice of the fastest and most comfortable way directly depends only on where in the country you are. You can use the following modes of transport:

  • Airplane.
  • Intercity bus.
  • Car.
  • A train.




From the capital to the resort - 1475 kilometers, we will now consider the exact route by car to Anapa.

Of course, the aircraft will be the fastest, but also a little more expensive. Using the services of railway transport, you can save about 30% of the cost of tickets. If we talk about comfort, then a train will be preferable, but it will take more time than any other type of transport.

We go by car to Anapa

And now more about how to get to Anapa by car. It is better to leave in the evening, so that at night you can drive without traffic jams. First you need to go to the M-4 Moscow-Don highway. From Rostov it is necessary to turn towards the Krasnodar Territory.

A shorter path passes through the settlements of Kanevskaya and Leningradskaya. You need to go to the R-268 highway and head towards Timashevsk. Turn onto the road that will lead to Slavyansk-on-Kuban. Further you will meet such settlements: Anastasievsk, Korzhevskoye, Varenikovskaya and Anapa. How to get there by car and avoid traffic jams, now you know.

Where to stay

A wide selection of hotel base in Anapa will easily satisfy the wishes of tourists both business class and those who have a limited budget. At your service are luxury VIP hotels with a full range of services, including SPA salons.





For a more modest, one can say home holiday, local residents offer to rent daily rooms in guest houses or cottages.

Any resident of the Krasnodar Territory will tell you how to get to Anapa by car. Before this city from Moscow will have to travel 1,475 kilometers.

How to have fun while on vacation

Like any sea resort, Anapa offers its tourists a variety of water activities. However, recently there has been a trend that more and more people are interested in the cultural as well as historical monuments of cities.

You should definitely take the time and go on an exciting excursion to the Abrau-Durso Valley. In addition to the fact that this in itself is quite picturesque, with beautiful landscapes , there is another curiosity here. The well-known champagne wine factory has been operating here since the beginning of the 19th century and delights tourists with its products. A great addition can be a horse ride or safari.





The choice of this resort as a family vacation will be very correct. Here, not only the shallow sea, which is very popular among families with children, but also many other attractions. Your child will definitely enjoy visiting the city's oceanarium, because here you can get acquainted with the inhabitants of the deep sea in the immediate vicinity. And, of course, a visit to the dolphinarium will cause indescribable sensations. Dolphins can not only be admired, but you can also swim with them.

Do not forget the city and youth. Nightlife here is developed at the highest level. If your plans include a fun holiday by the sea, Anapa will find something to surprise you with.

The magnificent sea vacation that this city is capable of giving will remain in your memory for many years as a bright and fascinating journey. Now you know about another beautiful resort in the Krasnodar Territory, as well as how to get to Anapa from Moscow by car.
