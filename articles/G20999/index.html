Apart-hotel "Vertical", St. Petersburg: hotel reviews, photos, contacts

One of the most attractive options in the cultural capital is the hotel "Vertical". St. Petersburg is an incredibly beautiful city, and a correctly selected hotel will help to make the experience even more vivid.





Brief description of the hotel

Relatively recently, the hotel "Vertical" (St. Petersburg) was opened. The date of its foundation is 2014. This is a modern institution that meets all standards of quality service. A feature of this hotel can be considered a modern original design, which can be traced both in the design of the hall and apartments.

As for the guest rooms, there are 126 in this hotel. It is also worth noting that the Vertical Hotel occupies as many as 12 floors. St. Petersburg, thanks to this, will be at your fingertips. The rooms attract not only modern design, but also a high level of comfort and functionality. Here, every tourist will feel at home.

It is worth noting that, taking into account all the advantages, the hotel "Vertical" is characterized by fairly affordable prices. St. Petersburg is a rather expensive city, and here the prices are almost twice lower than in other similar institutions. In addition, you will not be forcibly included in the price of certain services, and you can create this package yourself.

Hotel Location

Apart-hotel "Vertical" (St. Petersburg) is located near the central part of the city. You will have to spend no more than 7 minutes of your time on the road to the famous Nevsky Prospekt (if you take the metro). If we talk about the railway station, the distance to it is about 3 kilometers, and the nearest international airport is only 15 km away.

Apart-hotel "Vertical" (St. Petersburg) - rooms

If you are interested in living in comfortable and beautiful rooms, then pay attention to the hotel "Vertical", which offers you the following accommodation options:

  • The apartments of the Standard category offer comfortable conditions for accommodating one or two guests. The room is quite small, however, it consists not only of the bedroom area, but also has a small living room with a soft sofa (since it can be folded out, it can act as an extra bed).
  • The studio has two variations - single and double. All the essential amenities are here to make you feel at home. Since there is no kitchen area provided, this option is usually chosen for short-term stays.
  • Comfort apartments are a two-room apartment with a rather impressive area. The stylish bedroom is decorated in calm warm colors, which will ensure you a good rest. The living room is combined with the kitchen area, where, thanks to all the necessary equipment, you can cook your favorite dishes on your own.

What is provided in the guest apartments

Apart-hotel "Vertical" (St. Petersburg) offers its guests the following amenities in their rooms:

  • a wardrobe with hangers where you can easily place all your things;
  • individual air conditioning, which will allow you to create a comfortable temperature in the room, even if the weather outside;
  • a full set of orthopedic furniture to create comfortable living conditions;
  • a modern TV with a huge flat screen, on which you can watch several dozen satellite channels, as well as video from external media;
  • a landline phone will allow you to contact the reception at any time, make an order in a restaurant, and also make a call to another city or abroad;
  • thanks to the presence of an electronic code safe, your valuables will be fully preserved;
  • a full kitchen is provided with an electric stove, microwave, electric kettle, as well as a set of dishes and cutlery;
  • the bathroom has a hairdryer, bath accessories and all the necessary cosmetics.




Hotel Infrastructure

Of great importance is the infrastructure available to the hotel. Apart-hotel "Vertical" (St. Petersburg) offers you the following facilities:

  • a modern fitness club where you can conduct a full-fledged workout using all the necessary equipment (it is also possible to get advice from a professional trainer)
  • the restaurant offers you a huge selection of dishes, among which you can meet traditional food from around the world, as well as original inventions by local chefs;
  • in a night club you can relax after a busy day, enjoy alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, and also make new acquaintances; in the lobby area there is a library with a huge selection of books and comfortable chairs for reading (mainly, literature topics relate to business and self-improvement);
  • if you stained your things, then this problem can be solved using the self-service laundry (you can set the mode yourself and choose a detergent);
  • the lobby bar invites you to enjoy hot drinks in a relaxed atmosphere, as well as a huge assortment of desserts;
  • if your luggage is too bulky or you still have a significant period of time between eviction and departure from the city, then you have the opportunity to use a left-luggage office.




Useful information for guests

The hotel has a number of rules regarding accommodation, which guests undertake to strictly observe:

  • check-in to the room is carried out only after filling out the registration card, as well as paying for the service in full;
  • it is possible to provide room keys in an amount corresponding to the number of residents;
  • on the day of departure, you must resolve all organizational issues before two in the afternoon;
  • payment for services is possible both in cash and in non-cash forms;
  • during the registration procedure, the client must provide all the information that the administrator will request;
  • strangers staying at the hotel is possible only until 23:00;
  • gives the opportunity to stay in rooms with pets at the Hotel Vertical, St. Petersburg (contacts for clarification - 8 (812) 490-70-50).




Positive reviews

Many tourists choose the hotel "Vertical" (St. Petersburg) for their stay . Reviews about this place are characterized by the following positive comments:

  • friendly friendly staff who are always ready to help guests;
  • cleanliness and pleasant interior in the room;
  • expensive high-quality hair dryer in the bathroom, which is not typical for hotels;
  • if for some reason you are late for breakfast, this does not mean that you will be left hungry (if you warn about this in advance, then the food will be postponed for you);
  • very convenient location near the metro station;
  • pleased with the presence in the room of a full kitchen, which eliminates the additional cost of food;
  • good noise isolation (neither street sounds nor neighbors' conversations through the wall will disturb your vacation);
  • spacious bathroom.





Negative reviews

Of the negative aspects of living in this hotel, it is worth noting the following:

  • the price is a bit overpriced regarding living conditions and quality of services;
  • if you want to air the room, then this is almost impossible, because the window opens at a very small angle;
  • overpriced breakfast in a cafe (in any nearby institution you can eat half the price);
  • settling in the room, in buckets you can find garbage from previous guests;
  • paid use of wireless internet.

General impression

Apart-hotel "Vertical" is a comfortable accommodation option in the Northern capital of Russia. A feature of this institution can be considered relatively low prices. At the same time, the living conditions are quite comfortable. Due to the cozy design, as well as the presence of a full kitchen, this hotel is often chosen for extended stays. However, there are also some drawbacks. for example, too expensive breakfasts, as well as insufficiently thorough cleaning after previous guests.
