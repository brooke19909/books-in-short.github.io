Circle bird

In the photo at the beginning of the article, consider a craft assembled from three circles. And how to make a bird out of paper, read on:

  1. Two identical large circles are cut out according to the drawn pattern. It will be wings.
  2. A small circle corresponds to the head. The paper is double-sided, as the craft is voluminous and will be viewed from all sides.
  3. Large circles fold in the same way, the fold is slightly higher than the middle.
  4. Then, in large parts, they stick together, but with an inset head mug in front.
  5. Free "wings" are cut with scissors, imitating feathers.
  6. Small parts are cut in duplicate. They pasted both sides of the craft.

Swallow

For the next job you will need:

  • white, black, and orange double-sided paper;
  • scissors;
  • purchased toy eyes;
  • adhesive pencil.

How to make a bird out of paper in stages, it can be clearly seen in the photo below.

  1. The body of a swallow is cut out from a white strip and glued together by the edges. The result is a droplet shape.
  2. The head is assembled from a strip of black color of a smaller size. The edges of this part are attached inside the circle.
  3. The tail and two wings are drawn independently and glued to the upper body with a glue stick.
  4. It remains to attach the beak of orange paper and stick the eyes.
