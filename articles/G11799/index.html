If we judge this household appliance only in appearance, then we can say that the designers did not stint on the design of the case. The crock-pot represents a kind of stylish "egg-pot". Safety and control system

The security system that provides protection for both cooking dishes and the Redmond RMC-P350 multicooker itself includes four elements. This is a pressure sensor, a fuse that responds to excessive temperature and two valves: a shut-off valve and a steam outlet. This combination allows you to avoid the most common dangers of any kitchen equipment used in cooking - this, of course, is overheating and high pressure inside the unit. And the main enemies of any such technology: swings and unexpected blackouts in the power supply - are not afraid of it either.

The multicooker has its own non-volatile memory, and all its settings are saved and valid for 15 minutes after turning off the power supply. This is enough to take action and change the scenario of their culinary work without major changes to the dish.
