Where to relax in the summer with children in Belarus?

Summer is the time for vacations. What do you want during this period? Of course, to fully relax, get a lot of new impressions and gain strength before the new labor year. Fortunately, in the world there are a large number of beautiful places that can give it all. Many people are interested in the question of where to relax in the summer in Belarus. This place is rich in the most beautiful natural springs!

This amazing nature

Where to relax in Belarus in the summer on the lakes? Undoubtedly, the choice should fall on the Braslav lakes. This is the largest and cleanest reservoir in the country, which has always attracted local residents and tourists. The first thing that impresses is the fresh air. I want to inhale it again and again. The nature in this place is picturesque, its view simply cannot but bewitch! Near the lake there is an opportunity to rent houses. Rest here will be not only pleasant, but also very useful.





The most favorite place for fishermen is the Naroch pond. The water in it is so clear that you can carefully see the bottom. There is always enough fish for everyone here, nobody has remained without a catch! Nearby are several comfortable hotels. You can enjoy the beauty of this amazing place as much as you like.





For those people who have been to this country at least once in their life, the question does not arise of where to relax in the summer in Belarus. Chalk quarries have become a favorite place for many people. The unique landscape and turquoise water will give a good mood.





The best recreation centers

Many families plan to spend their holidays with the smallest family members. They face a logical problem: where to relax in Belarus in the summer with children? Experienced tourists recommend going to the recreation center. In such institutions there is picturesque nature, healing air, a place for sunbathing and swimming. Many of them have organized the most eventful leisure for visitors of a very young age. In total, three of the best tourist camps can be distinguished:

  • In the Vitebsk region there is a unique estate "Malinki". Adults can go fishing here, pick mushrooms or berries, and open the hunting season. For children, a playground made of natural wood is organized. If you wish, you can rent sports equipment: scooter, rollers, bicycle. On the territory of the base there is a volleyball court. You can arrange real family competitions!
  • The recreation center "Alesya" is famous in the Minsk region. All visitors will have a wonderful time on the shore of a picturesque lake. For children, several sports and playgrounds are organized here. Several times a season large-scale holidays are held here, to which cheerful animators are invited.




  • In the Brest region there is a unique place for rest "No problem." There is some special atmosphere of harmony, tranquility and comfort. Arriving here, everyone enjoys the maximum relaxation, forgetting about all the household chores. A small children's room is organized for children here. In the cafe, incredibly delicious desserts are prepared for young visitors.

In such entertainment facilities there is leisure for a person of any age. Polite staff, beautiful atmosphere, fresh air - all this will make your stay as intense as possible.

Wonderful reserve

Where can a child and an adult have a rest in Belarus in the summer? Everyone will be delighted with the Berezinsky Biosphere Reserve. On its territory you can find the most exotic animals. A separate area has been highlighted in which cute bears live. Looking at them is a pleasure! An adult visitor must visit the Museum of Nature. Needless to say, how charming are his landscapes? For children, the guide conducts scientific classes about each type of animal. They will be useful to them at school.





Among the attractions here are bicycle excursions, horseback riding and the most extreme rope park. A photo shoot is allowed on the territory of the reserve. You can bring some memorable pictures with you!

National park

Most children like to have fun and have fun in the fresh air. This type of activity brings them maximum pleasure. Where can such active travelers relax in Belarus in the summer? It is better to give preference to the Pripyatsky national park.





What is this unique place famous for? First of all, a rich wildlife. On its territory you can find about 300 species of various animals. Many of them are tame - they can be stroked, fed and picked up.

There are many outdoor games organized for children. After a long walk in the open air, appetite is noticeably played out. You can have a bite to eat in a cozy cafe located on the territory of the reserve, where you can buy a delicious set lunch for the whole family at the best price.

Health resort

The sanatorium "Belorusochka" in the Minsk region is the best place to relax in the summer in Belarus. Photos of this recreation center speak for themselves! There is a beautiful landscape, fabulous nature and a magnificent water source.





The main purpose of the arrival of many tourists is treatment. This is the best center in the country where qualified specialists work. They can quickly cope with diseases (including childhood), such as diabetes mellitus, hypertension, impaired lung function and much more.

In addition to medical services, the most saturated leisure was created here: a playground, a miniature pool, a cinema hall, a room with a teacher and trampolines. Several times a month, children's parties are organized on the sandy beach with animators from their favorite cartoons. Rest will be unforgettable.

Journey to a fairy tale

Many tourists know where to relax in Belarus in the summer. But few people know that in winter there is also rich leisure! Every child dreams of going to Belovezhskaya Pushcha. With the onset of cold weather, a real fairy tale is created here!





Experienced presenters organize thematic contests. Each participant can stay in the role of a favorite hero. There is an opportunity to get acquainted with the real Santa Claus and his granddaughter, as well as ride horses with them!

The logical conclusion of the festivities will be a joint dinner, where you can taste real national Belarusian dishes.

Agritourism

Mostly think about where to relax in the summer in Belarus, the most active tourists. For parents and children, agritourism will be an ideal entertainment. All comers are offered fascinating trips to the most unexplored places, overnight in tents on the banks of a picturesque lake, evening by the fire.

Urban residents will have the opportunity to feel like a villager. They will need to cope with everyday life at hand. The real test will be the lack of telephones, the Internet, computers and other modern gadgets.

The program also includes training lessons in fishing, horse riding and hunting. Those who are interested in the question "where to relax in the summer in Belarus by car", therefore, should come to the recreation center "Krasnoe", "Priluchki" or "Prilukomorye". It is worth noting that such a vacation is very close.

Rest in Minsk

Not all people love nature in all its manifestations. There are those who prefer entertainment centers, amusement parks, circus and other similar institutions. For those who are looking for a place to relax in Belarus in this way, there are several attractive options:

  • Every tourist must visit Minsk! This is an amazing city in which there is a huge amount of entertainment. On its territory there is a spacious water park "Swan". It is impossible to count on the fingers how many slides, pools and exciting attractions for children and adults are in it.




  • It is worth a look at the dolphinarium. Several times a month they organize an unusual show with these animals. There is a special program for the youngest visitors, it will give a good mood.
  • On the streets you can see playgrounds, and in shopping centers there are good game rooms.

Right in the central part of the capital of Belarus there is a sandy beach. On hot days, it will be an excellent substitute for a vacation at sea. You can stay in any comfortable hotel. In this place, you can distinguish more than ten good institutions for living.
