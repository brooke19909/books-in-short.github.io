Concorde Sport Area - Recreation and Sports

Lie on a white sand and soak up the rays of the gentle sun ... Isn‚Äôt that what we all dream of on long winter evenings ?! But what if you add the opportunity to everyone to practice their favorite sport or to learn a new one to relax on the beach? The creators of the Concorde Sport Area Hotel (Sharm El Sheikh) found this idea inspiring. You will like it too!



The Concorde Sport Area's rooms offer stunning views of the Red Sea. The hotel was opened in September 2001. In 2003, the Concorde Sport Area 5 was completed, and in 2005, work to expand it was completed.

It is located in the southern part of the Sinai Peninsula - 500 meters from a magnificent private beach, in the second line of the Concorde El Salam Hotel Sharm El Sheikh. You can get to the first line and the beach by special transport. The trip will take you only 15 minutes, as much as to the tourist center. A trip to the airport is 10 minutes. If you do not want to wait for transport, you can rent a car or even a whole limousine.

Concorde El Salam Sport Area offers visitors the widest selection of sports activities. For this, the latest sports center was fully equipped, which includes a standard football field, 3 tennis courts, 2 squash courts, 2 horse tracks, a multi-function court and an ice rink, which became the first in Sharm El Sheikh . In addition to this, there is a huge pool for water sports. Recently, sailing is gaining popularity , golf and desert safaris are no less popular.





In addition to sporting events, the hotel also has a variety of entertainment shows. Performances and entertainment events are organized for you.

You can visit various places, for example, Ras Mohammed - the most beautiful marine National Park. The monastery of St. Catherine on Mount Sinai is one of the oldest functioning Christian monasteries. It is recognized by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site. In the middle of a desert mountain, near Nuweiba, is located the Color Canyon. This is a magnificent rock formation created on the seashore over the past millennia. It takes its name from the many colored layers of the breed.

For young visitors, the hotel has a kids club. There is a children's playground and pools.

Concorde Sport Area hosts international sporting events. For example, in 2005 he hosted the International Equestrian Championship.

Sharm El Sheikh is a favorite place for scuba divers who come to see stunning coral reefs and exotic fish, enjoy the warm waters of the Red Sea. This area is famous for its picturesque landscapes, long sandy beaches and a dry temperate climate throughout the year.

In addition to everything in the Concorde Sport Area, visitors are offered the services of an indoor pool, sauna, fitness center, business center, night club, beauty salon, shops, massage rooms and SPA center.



There is a restaurant on site. Here is a varied menu of Eastern and European cuisine. For the convenience of visitors there is a bar with soft drinks and spirits.

A complimentary breakfast is a great addition to excellent service. The rooms are equipped with a minibar, air conditioning, satellite TV, a private bathroom. Most of them have sea views from the balcony or terrace.
