For example, everyone can go to the city of Chekhov, Moscow Region, located only 70 kilometers from the capital. Of course, it is not very big, but to walk along its streets, look at the river called Lopasnya, visit its attractions is worth at least for general development.

Nice hotel in Chekhov

The building of the hotel "Olympic" was built in the city in 2005 by order of the administration of the Chekhov district. The work was carried out by the construction company LLC RemMontazhServis, which managed to create a modern building that meets all the requirements.





Hotel "Olympic" (Chekhov), flaunting its glass-covered facade, stands out from other urban buildings. Within its walls, friendly staff is ready to receive guests throughout the year. Here you can find a cozy room for a day or longer. A special hourly payment service is also available to guests, thanks to which you can check into your room for several hours.

This hotel often stops both ordinary tourists and corporate workers. For the services of the latter there is a comfortable meeting room.

How to find a hotel in the city

Any tourist who comes to an unfamiliar or even unfamiliar city wants to quickly find his hotel. This also applies to all those people who find themselves in a city called Chekhov. The Olimpiyskaya Hotel, whose address is Sovetskaya Square, Building 2, is quite easy to search, because thanks to its appearance it is noticeable even from afar.

However, you also need to know information about how to get to it better. By car, you can get here both from Moscow and from the nearest international airports Vnukovo and Domodedovo. It will be necessary to move along the Simferopol highway to the city of Chekhov, Moscow Region, and then follow to its center.





You can also come here from Moscow by public transport. A bus number 365 runs from Yuzhnaya metro station to Chekhov. Guests will need to leave at the Hotel or Tank stop. You will have to spend about an hour on travel.

Suburban trains also go to Chekhov. You can catch them at the Kursk station or at the railway stations near the metro Tsaritsyno and Tekstilshchiki. You should go to the Chekhov station, and then transfer to bus number 1. It will take travelers to the Hotel or Tank stops, near which the Olympic Hotel (Chekhov) is located.

Offered rooms in the Olympic

The room fund of the central Chekhov hotel consists of 49 comfortable rooms. Among them there are 32 standard rooms, 10 superior standard rooms, 6 comfort rooms, as well as one suite.





Each room offered by the Olimpiyskaya Hotel (Chekhov) to its guests is equipped with a refrigerator, air conditioning, telephone, personal safe, TV and a combined bathroom. Also in the rooms there are all necessary furniture and access to wireless Internet.

Standard rooms are equipped with two single beds, the rest of the categories offer guests wide double beds. The suite consists of a bedroom and a living room, so that guests of this room can not only have a good rest, but also have a good time with friends.
