Hotel category 3 stars in foreign resorts

Of course, a tour operator in paints will tell any customer only about the benefits of a 3-star hotel stay, if he has a limited budget. But how different is reality from stories?

All-inclusive hotels in Kemer (3 stars), as well as in other cities in Turkey, offer accommodation, meals, use of sunbeds, animation, swimming in the pool, visiting a restaurant with a particular cuisine, safe and air conditioning.

What is it worth to be prepared if, nevertheless, your choice fell on a 3 * hotel

Firstly, the repair is likely to be old or new, but without any frills: modest wallpaper, paint or plaster.

Secondly, furniture and plumbing are also likely to be not the first freshness. Sometimes they can be broken or have defects.

Thirdly, the arrangement of the room itself is not a luxury apartment, but rather a modest corner, where there is a bed for rest, chairs or an armchair, a table or a bedside table, a small wardrobe (often built-in) for 4-5 hangers. The bathroom is also a small room with a toilet, a washstand and a shower cabin (often imitated and made according to the principle of an ordinary "washbasin").

Meals here include a buffet. Although Kemer hotels offer 3 stars all-inclusive, sometimes chefs will put dishes in portions on a plate (while the additive may or may not be).
