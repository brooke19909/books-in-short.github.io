Nakhodka hotels. The best deals at affordable prices.

The city of Nakhodka, located on the shores of the Sea of ​​Japan, is attractive for tourists who want to get acquainted with the beauties of nature and the sights of the Russian Far East.





Every year, a lot of people come to the city both from Russia and from foreign countries for business purposes. There are twelve modern hotels in the city . Nakhodka hotels are comfortable apartments for people with different income levels.

Hotel Nadezhda





The hotel is located in the city center near the central square and the business center, one hundred and fifty kilometers from the Knevichi airport and just three kilometers from the train station. The rooms overlook the bay.

The number of rooms consists of thirty-five apartments of various categories (“standard”, “studio”, “improved standard”, “luxury”). All of them are equipped with necessary appliances. Comfort of living is ensured by the presence of an air conditioning system. The rooms are non-smoking.

Hotel Nadezhda (Nakhodka) accepts guests with pets only if there is a veterinary certificate for an additional fee. While in a comfortable room, guests have the opportunity to watch satellite TV or surf the Internet via Wi-Fi. Free toiletries and tea are provided.

How to spend your free time?

Guests can relax in the cafe-restaurant serving Russian, European and Japanese dishes. The restaurant’s summer terrace offers gorgeous views of the bay. Breakfast is included in the price. You can also visit the Finnish sauna and swim in the pool. For those who want to play sports there is a well-equipped gym. Guests are provided with services for storing valuables in safe deposit boxes.

For business meetings, conferences, seminars, there are several conference rooms. Office equipment is also provided free of charge. For motorists there is parking, which is under 24-hour security.

Comfortable Rent Hotel





Hotel Rent (Nakhodka) is considered one of the most prestigious in the city. It is part of the PRISCO RENT complex and includes thirty-six rooms belonging to various categories (“VIP Suite”, “Suite”, “Junior Suite”, “Room of the 1st Category”, “Room of the 2nd Category”, “Double room”, “single studio”), ranging from twenty-eight to forty square meters. The best Nakhodka hotels have a modern interior in their apartments. The rooms at Rent Hotel are decorated in a European style.

Accommodations





Guests can choose a room for smokers or non-smokers. All apartments are equipped with comfortable modern furniture, TVs. The bathroom is in the room. Guests can watch cable and satellite channels in several languages ​​(Russian, Japanese, Korean and English). Tea in the room. Children under ten years of age (without an application for an extra bed) stay free. The most popular Nakhodka hotels are located in the central part of the city. Rent is one of them.

The hotel has a convenient location, close to the railway station "Ocean" and the bus station. An additional convenience is the ability to reach the beach within ten minutes. Guests can purchase various souvenirs in the hotel lobby.

The hotel has a convenient online booking system. Guests can leave valuables in the safe deposit box. The hotel offers guests a variety of entertainment, including access to the gym, saunas.

Extra beds are available in rooms and extra breakfasts are available at extra charge. Nakhodka hotels provide their customers not only with overnight accommodation, but also with a host of other services. For a fee, laundry and ironing services can be arranged. Massage services are also provided.

The hotel constantly organizes all kinds of promotions and special offers (Packages - "Wedding", "Weekend", "Golden Autumn"). For guests who have arrived in groups of ten to twenty five or more people, discounts of five to ten percent apply.

The meals are organized in the restaurant and includes (at the option of the client) full board, half board. Breakfast is included. The restaurant also offers a business lunch. Guests can enjoy the bar.

Hotel complex "Horizon"

Hotel "Horizon" (Nakhodka) is a modern complex with European standards for the provision of services. It has one hundred rooms of different categories ("standard", "luxury"). The most expensive is the "presidential" number. Its area is eighty nine square meters.

The best option for a business trip.





Guests have free internet access. Entertainment includes visits to the Saloon Texas nightclub, the F-city fitness club. For guests there is a Japanese restaurant. Various events, conferences are held in a modern conference room for up to fifty people. The staff of the hotel complex is responsible for serving guests and has significant experience in receiving various delegations, including foreign ones. The principle of the hotel staff is an individual approach to each guest.

Nakhodka hotels provide guests with an extensive range of services. All of them have a developed infrastructure that provides high standards of customer service. The variety of room categories presented gives guests the opportunity to choose the most suitable accommodation option, based on aesthetic tastes and financial possibilities.
