How to get from Nizhny Novgorod to Bor? Bor city of the Nizhny Novgorod region: description, interesting facts, reviews

On the banks of the great Russian Volga River there are many interesting cities. We list only a few of them: Saratov, Yaroslavl, Volgograd, Samara, Nizhny Novgorod and many others. They are familiar to a large number of people. Today we will talk about the city, which is also located on the banks of the Volga River. It is called Bor. Many people are interested in the question of how to get to it. The article discusses all the possible options for routes from Nizhny Novgorod to Bor, as well as sets out interesting facts about this place. Let's start our story.

Interesting Facts

When you pronounce the name of this city, you imagine dense coniferous or pine forests. And this is actually so. Bor is located among a wide variety of trees and shrubs. The first mention of this place dates back to the XIV century. What else is known about the city of Bor in Nizhny Novgorod?





  • In ancient times, Finno-Ugric tribes lived here.
  • The first settlements in the vicinity of the city of Bora appeared 2-3 thousand years BC.
  • After the 18th century, stone houses and churches began to be built here.
  • Such famous people as Maxim Gorky and Fedor Chaliapin came here.
  • The main activities of the inhabitants of the village of Bora were carpentry, carpentry and hunting.
  • One of the important enterprises in the city is the Glass Factory.

How to get from Nizhny Novgorod to the city of Bor

More than 70,000 people live here. Many residents work in urban enterprises, but some go to work in Nizhny Novgorod. Bor is considered one of the most beautiful and environmentally friendly places in the region. There are beautiful forests, lakes and meadows. Many tourists come here to go fishing or horseback riding, as well as get acquainted with the history of the city, which was once a large trading village. Residents are very hospitable and with great pleasure communicate with visitors, and also willingly answer all questions.



Many tourists are interested in the route from Nizhny Novgorod to Bor. How can I get here? What is the distance from Nizhny Novgorod to Bor? How long can it be overcome? There are several options.

Car

Since Nizhny Novgorod is located on one side of the Volga, and Bor is on the other, you can get from one point to another using the railway bridge. The road will take very little time. On the way you will pass through two settlements: Neklyudovo and Botalovo. The distance is approximately twenty nine kilometers. By car, they can be overcome in 25-30 minutes. This is one of the fastest and most convenient ways to get to Bor. You can go by your own transport or use the Bla Bla Car service. So you can save money and meet new people.

Bird's eye view

One of the most unusual and beautiful ways to get from Nizhny Novgorod to Bor is by cable car. It was built several years ago and is very popular among tourists. Travel time takes about twelve minutes. In Nizhny Novgorod, to take the cable car, you need to come to Kazan embankment, 8. And in the city of Bor, the station is located at: st. Lunacharsky 12 A. The ticket price is 100 rubles.



Bus routes

If you are in Nizhny Novgorod, then you need to come to the stop "Sergei Yesenin Street" or to the bus station. You will need to take buses No. 229, 237, 245. At the bus stop there are always people who are sure to tell you the route you need. Buses depart every ten minutes. Travel time is 35 minutes. Tickets are bought at the box office.

Travel by train

Many people like to ride on electric trains. To get from Nizhny Novgorod to Bor, you need to come to the Moscow station. Here you buy a ticket to the Mokhovye Gory railway station . The train to the city of Bora takes forty minutes. Ticket price - 55 rubles. Electric trains run daily. Despite the fact that the path will take a little longer than by car, this option allows you to quickly be in the right place. The fact is that by car you can get stuck in traffic.

Water transport

On a warm day, you can go to the city of Bor (Nizhny Novgorod region) by water. The coolness of the river will make your trip enjoyable and quick. You can buy a ticket for any passenger ship or ferry.



Residents and tourists reviews

The city of Bor in Nizhny Novgorod is a place where you can have a great time. Here you can not only ride a cable car and experience dizzying sensations, but also get acquainted with a large number of attractions. Many tourists like the Museum of military equipment, which is located in the open. Children with great pleasure vividly study Russian tanks, planes, artillery mounts.

Finally

Clean air and amazing beauty of the surrounding nature await you in Bor (Nizhny Novgorod region) - one of the most beautiful and ancient Russian cities. Now you know how to get to the city from Nizhny Novgorod. Choose any route and enjoy the journey.
