How to clean the speaker on iPhone 6? Easy way

iPhone 6 is a problematic smartphone in terms of sound. Often users complain about a bad sound. These posts are usually teeming with different forums. Sometimes it‚Äôs enough to simply blow through the speaker, and sometimes a weak sound is the result of a software flaw and is eliminated by software. But more often than not, the speaker needs to be either cleaned or replaced. Therefore, it is important to know how to clean the speaker on the iPhone 6. This may well return your smartphone to its previous high-quality sound.





Causes of poor sound from the speaker

There may be several reasons. If water spilled onto the phone, then a bad sound from the speaker is a logical consequence. Also, the speaker could become clogged with dust, and even worse - with sand. Therefore, remember if the device fell on the sand on any beach.

It is worth noting that iPhones have a special metal mesh over the speaker. It serves as a protection, but during operation it becomes clogged naturally.

Software is rarely the cause. There are reports on the forums that the issue of quiet sound was resolved after a software update. So for starters, just try installing the latest software or, if it isn't already, roll back to your previous iOS. If this does not help, then read the article below, which will tell you how to clean the speaker on iPhone 6. Try to adhere to the tips.

What is needed for cleaning?





We need only 2 ingredients or tools:

  1. Brush with a hard pile. If you find a brush with a soft pile, then that's okay. Just trim the pile; it will be hard at the base of the bristles. This one is perfect for us. You can use a cotton swab, but it is worse in terms of effectiveness.
  2. Some kind of liquid containing alcohol. It can be cologne or alcohol itself. It is available to use gasoline for lighters (high purification).

All this is in almost any home. Prepare a brush and liquid. Let's say it will be gasoline.

How to clean the speaker on iPhone 6? Instruction manual

Pour gasoline into some small container. You need to pour a little bit. The main thing is that the brush can be dipped in gasoline and moistened, no more. Do not allow gasoline to drip from the brush.





Moisturize the brush and very carefully process the speaker grid. Disassemble the phone and get the grid is not necessary. Simply moisten the assembled phone grid with a brush. Repeat this procedure several times and very carefully. After the second or third brushing, visually you will see that the speaker has become much cleaner.

After that, simply wipe the remaining gasoline from the speaker grid and from the phone with a cloth. Now the smartphone should work better. Check if the interlocutor is better heard. If not, then you have to clean it from the inside. This will require special tools. At the very least, you need a screwdriver with an asterisk, which only people who repair phones and small electronics have. You also need the skills of disassembling and assembling smartphones, because when you remove the cover of the case, you can accidentally break the cable. Therefore, if you do not know how to clean the speaker on the iPhone 6 from the inside, then it is better not to go inside, but give the phone for repair.





Although usually the simplest external cleaning helps with a hard brush and an alcohol-containing solution.

How not to clean?

Some users do not know how to clean the speaker on the iPhone 6, and try to do it with a toothbrush, but do not need to. The toothbrush is very stiff and with long hairs, so it can damage the mesh, which can later clog the speaker itself.

It is also advisable to use alcohol solutions, rather than alcohol itself. Although the latter is also suitable. But in no case do not use water. Water is oxidizable and not a degreasing agent. Alcohol and solutions based on it degrease the surface and are not susceptible to oxidation, which is why they are ideal for cleaning.

Alternatively, you can safely use disposable alcohol wipes, which are usually intended for hands. But make sure that a piece of the napkin does not stay on the speaker grid. Also, do not use alcohol-free wipes, because most wet wipes do not contain it now, although they also slightly degrease the surface. Some users try to do this with a toothpick. Do not use it to clean smartphones, no matter how. To clean the speaker on the iPhone 6 at home with a toothpick will not work. You just break the net, which is already not durable.

Now you know how to clean the speaker on iPhone 6 S and other models.

How to prevent clogging of the speaker?

It is impossible to advise anything new in this regard. Standard smartphone care tips will do. Just do not spill water on it, do not throw it in the sand and try not to drop it at all. In any case, sooner or later the speaker will clog, and the interlocutor will again be hard to hear, no matter how well you monitor the state of your smartphone. But now you know how to clean the speaker on the iPhone 6 plus at home, and you won‚Äôt be afraid of any blockages. Use this method and recommend to friends.
