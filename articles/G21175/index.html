Leading Sanatoriums of Belarus. Gomel region

Everyone sooner or later needs rest and medical treatment. Sanatoriums of Belarus gained great fame not only among local residents, but also citizens of the former USSR. Gomel region is one of the most popular destinations for excellent relaxation and treatment. Several dozens of centers of different medical profiles are all the sanatoriums of the Gomel region. Prices for services are available to almost anyone, and a mild climate has a beneficial effect on the restoration of the body. It is known that 30 percent of the region is occupied by coniferous forests.

Natural features

Gomel region is located in Polesie, the natural heritage of Belarus. This region is rich in peatlands and marshy areas, and the main therapeutic methods are: a wide selection of mineral waters with different variations in chemical composition, as well as mineralization. Calcium-sodium, bromine with a high content of hydrogen sulfide and others. Peat and sapropelic mud with healing properties. Mild climate with a large amount of tarry substances in the air. There are at least 190 sunny days, which favors the metabolism in the body and strengthens its immunity.

sights

Health resorts of Belarus (Gomel region) provide not only first-class health-improving services, but also decorate your holiday with cultural leisure. Gomel region is a storehouse of historical and cultural attractions. No wonder Gomel is considered the cultural capital of Belarus. There are more than thirty various museums in the region. The most famous of them is the Rumyantsev Palace, which is part of the Gomel Palace and Park Ensemble. The building dates from the 18-19th century. In addition to this architectural monument, the complex includes a huge park, a chapel, a winter garden and a watch tower.

It is worth visiting one of the most ancient cities of the Republic of Belarus - Turov. It was built in the eleventh century and is ready to tell vacationers its story. He is known primarily for the foundation dated to 1170, as well as the church of All Saints.

It is also recommended that you go to the Pripyatsky National Park as part of your vacation . It is better to inspect it, accompanied by an experienced guide. Auto safari, a museum in Lyaskovichi, a boat ride and just a walk in the park. It is worth allotting for this event all day.



Summary

Rest in the sanatoriums of the Gomel region is not only treatment and rehabilitation, which became possible due to the peculiarities of the climate and well-developed medical infrastructure, but also a diverse cultural program. Adding to this low prices and a high level of medical services, it is safe to say that many resorts in Belarus (Gomel region) will like it.
