How to make a DIY Tsokotuhi costume with your own hands?

Next, you will have to buy a little tulle and make a tu-tu skirt from it (see below). A satin bolero vest will complement the suit.

Tu-Tu skirt

If you do not like to sew, then this option will be the best for you.

You will need a satin ribbon and tulle. Further:

  • strips of equal width and two times longer than the desired length of the skirt are cut from tulle;
  • take one of the strips and tie it around the ribbon, slightly departing from the edge;
  • tighten the loop until it stops;
  • They do the same with other stripes of tulle until all the space equal to the circumference of a person‚Äôs waist is filled.
  • lay out the skirt on the table and cut off the uneven ends of the ribbons.

The outfit will turn out even more beautiful if you take a tulle of two colors and alternate stripes.





Wings are the most important thing

To make a costume of a Fly-Tsokotuha with your own hands, first of all, you need to figure out what and how to sew or craft this element.

Several options are possible:

  • A frame in the form of two wings of the correct form is made of a durable wire. Tighten with a transparent cloth. The edge is manually trimmed with an oblique trim. Draw on fabric with a special contour with a sparkle of veins. If you wish, stick or sew on the wings along the edge of the sequin. Two colored elastic bands need to be sewn onto the wings so that the child can wear them like a backpack.
  • From a transparent black tulle or organza sew a cloak with a hood, gathered at the neck. Behind it, they cut it to make wings. If the fabric is not loose, then the edges can not be processed or "walk" on them with a lighter flame.
  • The wings can also be sewn from the satin of a suitable color in the manner described above. In this case, they will need a contrast lining, preferably also shiny.

Accessories

Many mothers, making the costume of the Fly-Tsokotuha with their own hands (see photo above), come up with various accessories for it. For example, high leggings with ties in the form of satin bows under the knees are well suited to a tulle skirt. They can be made, for example, from old leggings, decorating with sequins. In addition, you can sew over the black organza or kapron patch sleeves-gloves longer than the elbow and with a loop for the middle finger. They can also be decorated with sparkles. Cape wings will be attached to them, the manufacture of which is described above.

In some cases, the headgear can be replaced with a small top hat with a black veil, descending just below the eyes. It can be decorated with a beautiful flower made of fabric, which went to make the lining of the wings.





Costume Spider from "Fly-Tsokotuhi"
