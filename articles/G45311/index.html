SEC "Premier" (Tyumen): cinema, shops, address

SEC "Premier" in Tyumen is the first shopping and entertainment center that was opened in the city. Here is an excellent selection of women's and men's clothing, sporting goods, there are children's shops for the whole family, shoes, cosmetics, jewelry, books, leather goods, repair and dry cleaning services. There is also a large Megamart supermarket, many cozy cafes and a modern multiplex cinema.

From the very moment of opening, the shopping center has become a favorite place for recreation, shopping and meetings for residents of the central part of Tyumen, as well as suburban areas and tourists.

About city

Tyumen is a city in the Russian Federation located in Western Siberia, 2,200 kilometers from Moscow, on the Tura River.





At one time, the city was founded on an ancient trade route from Central Asia leading to the Volga region. Through the water borders, Tyumen is connected with the Far East and the Far North.

The city began to develop especially rapidly during the construction period of the Trans-Siberian Railway on its territory.

Currently, Tyumen is a large industrial, transport, scientific, educational, cultural, historical, tourist center of the region and the country. The population is 744.5 thousand people.

This is the first Russian city in Siberia. His story is interesting and unique in its own way.

When the boom in the construction of shopping centers began in the large cities of the country in the early 2000s, Tyumen was no exception. In November 2005, in the heart of the city, the Premier shopping center was built. In the city, this is currently not the only institution of its kind, but due to its good location, good internal atmosphere, the presence of many shops, a cinema and a cafe, it remains the best.

Description

SEC “Premier” (Tyumen) is a large-scale building consisting of 3 above ground floors and a basement level. The total area of ​​the entire center is 32 thousand square meters. There is also ground parking for 1000 cars.





The Premier shopping and entertainment center includes about 120 stores, most of which are boutiques of international branded women’s and men’s clothes and shoes, as well as clothing for the whole family, children’s clothes, household electrical goods for the home, a grocery supermarket, a pet market, pharmacies, and various services (dry cleaning, repairs, and so on). For relaxation and leisure there are cafes and a cinema.

Here you can go shopping, dine in a cozy restaurant, visit a movie theater with your loved one or friends.

Cinema "Premier"

At the time of opening, it was the first modern multi-screen cinema in the city. There are currently 6 rooms. Located on the 3rd floor of the Premier shopping center (Tyumen). Sessions are held daily from 10.00 to 02.00. Comfortable rooms, 7 sound channels, soft chairs, food court nearby.





There is a large hall, decorated in red, with a capacity of up to 250 people. Here on the big screen world premieres and the best Russian films are shown.

Other halls for up to 140 visitors each. It shows action films, romantic comedies and melodramas, thrillers and so on.

Each room is equipped with a good ventilation and air conditioning system. As well as wide enough aisles between rows, amphitheater locations and coffee cup holders on each chair.

The Premier Cinema in Tyumen is an excellent vacation with friends, family, a loved one to escape from the daily bustle, plunging into the fantastic world of cinema and gastronomy.
