How to choose a tablet inexpensive but good? Tips for choosing a quality and affordable tablet

How to choose a tablet inexpensive but good? This question is of interest to many users. The choice depends on many factors, including the parameters of the gadget, its technical characteristics, operating system, etc.

What is a tablet?

Before choosing a good gadget, you first need to figure out what a tablet is? It is a multifunctional device that combines the functions of a personal computer and a telephone. Almost all modern tablet models have the ability to access the Internet. This is achieved in several ways: through the built-in Wi-Fi adapter or using 3G and 4G connections.

The tablet may have different sizes, specifications and capabilities. Defining usage goals will help you choose a tablet. Tips can be obtained from sellers, acquaintances or on the Internet.





Tablet features and applications

Most often, a tablet is bought for entertainment, study or work. Before choosing a tablet (inexpensive, but good), you need to decide which functions of the tablet will be used:

  • Work with various applications, including office programs, databases, document scanning, etc.
  • Work on the Internet: browsing the web, using search engines, social networks, etc.
  • Reading books in different formats: epub, pdf, txt, doc, etc.
  • View and edit photos.
  • Listening to music and watching videos.
  • Work with email.
  • Implementation of online conferences, including using a camera.
  • Various games, etc.




Depending on this, the tablet model is also selected. If we are talking about, for example, how to choose a tablet for the Internet, then you need to focus on the speed of the gadget on the network. If the device will be used to work with different types of files, then preference can be given to models with weaker technical characteristics, but with a large and convenient screen, etc. When choosing, you also need to pay attention to other factors that directly affect the work with the gadget.

Operating system selection

The operating system is one of the main factors affecting how to choose a good tablet. Customer reviews indicate that each option has its own distinctive characteristics, as well as positive and negative sides.





In today's market, tablets with three operating systems are provided. This is Android from Google, iOS from Apple, and Windows from Microsoft. The most popular, of course, is the first operating system. Android products make up two-thirds of the modern market, followed by iOS, which occupies almost a third of the segment, and a product from Windows, which is less common, but has recently become increasingly popular. Each system has its pros and cons.

Android

This is the most common operating system that is installed not only on tablets, but also on smartphones, netbooks, various multimedia players, electronic reading aids, etc.

As for the positive aspects of this operating system, they are as follows:

  • A huge number of applications that are available for download through the Google Play service.
  • Convenient synchronization with other services from Google.
  • The presence of a built-in voice assistant.
  • Easy to connect tablet to PC.
  • Fast and easy data transfer to other devices.

As for the negative sides, Android has the following:

  • High rates of energy consumption. Android device owners know that you need to charge the battery often enough.
  • Unlike other operating systems, Android is often updated, which sometimes leads to incompatibility of some programs.




iOS

When it comes to choosing the right tablet, many users prefer the iOS operating system, which is installed only on Apple products. As for the advantages of the OS, they are as follows:

  • High rates of speed and stability of the system.
  • Huge selection of applications through the AppStore.
  • Convenient and intuitive interface.
  • The presence of a special voice assistant Siri.
  • Low power consumption, which allows you to work with the device for a long time without additional recharging.
  • Convenience of synchronization of OS with other devices.

As for the cons of the operating system, they are as follows:

  • To work with a computer, you must have a special iTunes program.
  • More complex data exchange process.
  • The operating system does not support memory cards.

Windows

If we are talking about which tablet to choose (inexpensive and high-quality), more and more users prefer devices with Microsoft OS. The company offers both Windows 8, available on various devices, and Windows RT, designed specifically for tablets. Windows has its advantages:

  • Convenient and fast synchronization with other services from Microsoft.
  • The presence of a pre-installed package of office programs Microsoft Office.
  • High speed operating system.
  • Easy to connect the device to the computer.
  • Convenience of working with files on different devices.

As for the cons, they are as follows:

  • A relatively small number of applications for the user.
  • High power consumption.




Which screen size should I prefer?

Screen size is an important factor in understanding how to choose a tablet. User reviews indicate that the usability of the device directly depends on this. The following screen sizes are available:

  • Screen size from 5 to 7.9 inches. If we are talking about how to choose a tablet inexpensive, but good, then this category should be preferred. Such tablets are convenient to use due to their compactness, but can not boast of high technical characteristics.
  • Screen size from 8 to 9 inches. The most common types of tablets that are convenient to work with, as well as play games and spend time on the Internet.
  • Screen size from 9 or more inches. Large and fairly heavy options. It is very convenient to work with such gadgets, however, it is difficult to hold them in your hands all the time.

Each type of screen has its advantages and disadvantages. The goals of using the gadget will allow you to choose a quality tablet for any situation.

Specifications

When it comes to how to choose a good tablet, you should definitely pay attention to the following technical characteristics of the gadget:

  1. RAM. The speed of the device directly depends on this indicator. It should be ensured that the minimum indicator of RAM is not less than 1 GB. Otherwise, the device will slow down, and applications will run for a long time.
  2. CPU. The processor will determine which applications the user can use. As for Android, it is better to give preference to a dual-core processor. If we are talking about Windows, then the choice here is not very large, but thanks to a huge selection of manufacturers, the user can choose the option for their needs.
  3. Physical memory. The amount of data that can be stored on the tablet directly depends on this indicator. You can increase this indicator using a memory card, if the OS supports it.

In addition to these indicators, it is also important to pay attention to other factors, in particular the variety of wireless technologies.

Wireless technology on a tablet

If we are talking about how to choose the right tablet, then you must also pay attention to the wireless technologies that come with modern gadgets:

  • Wi-fi Almost all tablets have a Wi-Fi antenna that allows you to connect to a wireless network at home or use the Internet in places equipped with wireless access points.
  • 3G The presence of a built-in 3G module in the tablet allows you to use the World Wide Web in places where there is no Wi-Fi.
  • Bluetooth Almost all modern gadgets are equipped with Bluetooth 3.0 technology, which is capable of transmitting large amounts of data at high speed. The latest tablet models also support Bluetooth 4.0 technology, which additionally has low power consumption. It should be remembered that for the exchange of data another device must be compatible with Bluetooth 4.0.
  • HDMI Tablets that have an HDMI output can be connected to a high-definition TV.
  • GPS Technology that allows you to work in navigator mode. For this, in addition to the module itself, a navigator program is also required.
  • NFC This technology allows you to share photos and music with other users without the need to establish an additional connection with devices.
  • DLNA DLNA technology allows you to connect your tablet to a wireless home network, including home cinemas and other audio and video equipment. The tablet in this case will act as a remote control.
  • USB USB technology allows you to connect a variety of external devices to the gadget, including disk drives, flash drives, card readers and much more.
  • G-sensor. Most modern tablets have an accelerator that determines the position of the device in space. Due to this, when turning the screen of the device switches from horizontal to vertical and vice versa. This feature is also used in games.

In addition to these technologies, modern tablets are also equipped with other additional features that allow you to use gadgets in a variety of situations.





Using multimedia

When it comes to which tablet to choose (inexpensive and high-quality), then you need to think about multimedia. Modern tablets have the following features:

  • Audio player. Gadgets are equipped with full-fledged players with an equalizer, which allows them not to be inferior in quality to music players.
  • Video player Almost all tablets have a built-in video player that supports a variety of video file formats.
  • Radio. Many users prefer a radio player. For these purposes, a special application on the tablet is used, as well as headphones as an antenna.

Multimedia is one of the key factors when choosing a tablet. The price of the device depends on the quality of embedded applications.

Advanced tablet selection options

When choosing a tablet, you should also pay attention to the following points:

  • Camera. It can be either frontal or rear. The quality of photographs and video calls depends on the quality of the camera. A quality camera directly affects the cost of the tablet. If it is important, then the choice should be stopped on the option with an indicator of at least 5-8 megapixels. Great if the camera will support Full HD format.
  • Battery. The battery will directly depend on how much it will be possible to use the gadget without the need for recharging it. The choice depends on the screen size and operating system.

How to choose a tablet inexpensive but good? This question is of interest to many users. The choice of device depends on a number of factors, including the screen size, OS, technical characteristics of the gadget and the availability of additional features.
