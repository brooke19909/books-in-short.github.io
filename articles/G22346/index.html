Hotel Gumuldur Resort Hotel 4 *, Turkey: description and reviews of tourists

Kusadasi, or Bird Island, as this Turkish resort is also called, is located on the shores of a cozy sea bay. It is known for its clean water, numerous marinas and a developed tourist infrastructure. This former pirate village today has become a fairly popular beach resort.



general information

Almost all the hotels here are located on the seashore. Judging by the reviews, here even in modest establishments with three stars, decent service and good food are offered. At the same time, the cost of living in Kusadasi hotels is much lower than in Antalya counterparts.

The resort is located in the western region of Turkey, on the coast of the wonderful Aegean Sea, almost ninety kilometers from Izmir. Around the city there are many monuments of antiquity. Tourists can visit such ancient cities as Ephesus and Priene, mineral hot springs, known throughout the world.

The nature of this resort is somewhat reminiscent of the Greek landscape: there are a lot of olive groves and date palms. Kusadasi is famous not only for its picturesque landscapes, but also for its amazing fauna and flora. The underwater world on this part of the coast of Turkey is quite interesting, so the region is very attractive for divers. The water in the sea here is clear and amazingly clean.

The resort offers a large selection of hotels of different categories. Tour operators say that the most popular among them are tours to four-star hotels. In recent years, Russians have begun to increasingly choose this category, for example, the Gumuldur Resort Hotel 4 *. The beaches in Kusadasi are not very crowded even in high season, since most of them are large. That is why people can rest comfortably without interfering with each other. This article provides general information about the hotel Gumuldur Resort Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi), reviews about it, infrastructure and the proposed service.





Description

The peak season for tourists lasts from May to September and falls on the summer months. It is during this period that the hotels of this resort are crowded. Gumuldur Resort Hotel 4 * is no exception. It is located a kilometer from the village of Gumuldur - one of the municipalities of Kusadasi. The airport is only thirty kilometers away, so the hotel is attractive also for tourists with small children, for whom a long shuttle service is a big problem. Gumuldur Resort Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi) was built on the territory of about twenty-two thousand square meters. meters. It is fully equipped and landscaped. The hotel is considered relatively young: it was opened in 2006. The last time his room fund and public areas were renovated in 2016. The hotel area is a neat park area, which contains all the available infrastructure. To the sea only a hundred meters.

Infrastructure

Gumuldur Resort Hotel is a four star hotel. Judging by the reviews, the service offered in it is fully consistent with the declared category. Convenient free parking is arranged on its territory, there are shops, including a grocery, a souvenir kiosk, a conference hall for 150 people. The administrative building has a post office, exchange office, laundry, shoe shine service, vending machines and ATMs.

The registration desk works round the clock, without a break. Here you can use the fax and depository safe, luggage storage, order a taxi, arrange express check-in and transfer.

For children

Gumuldur Resort Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi) is a great option for a vacation with the whole family. Its infrastructure includes a children's playground, a mini-club, a shallow compartment in the pool. Upon request, parents can invite a nanny to care for the child, as well as receive a cot in the room. In the restaurant in the public domain you can get chairs for the smallest.

Beach

The sea - that's why every year the army of tourists flock to Turkey. In Kusadasi, it is surprisingly transparent and clean. The beach is only a hundred meters away from the hotel. This private bathing area is sand covered. True, judging by the reviews, at the entrance to the water there is a narrow strip of pebbles, so you need to have rubber shoes on hand.





Entertainment

The hotel has a swimming pool with sun loungers, which organizes water aerobics classes in the morning. Those who wish to Gumuldur Resort Hotel 4 * can visit the Turkish bath and sauna, fitness center, paid massage room. There are some good entertainment programs. A lot of entertainment is offered on the beach. Here you can go diving and snorkeling, ride a boat, canoe or catamaran, play beach volleyball. There are many attractions in this region that can be arranged for paid excursions. Judging by the reviews, there are many places to see.
