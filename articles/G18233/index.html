How to choose the basis for the costume

To sew a beautiful goat costume with your own hands, you need to choose the right fabric. For this purpose, velor, velvet or thin fur with curled small pile is ideal. It is best to give preference to well-stretching materials. Even novice craftswomen will cope with them, since when landing on a figure, all the flaws of the cut are a little hidden due to stretching the fabric. It should also be noted that a special needle for knitted fabrics will be necessary for the sewing machine. Without it, it is unlikely to be able to lay even lines without gaps and holes on the fabric. Another plus of knitwear is that it does not need to be swept, it is enough just to shake off the cut well so that the pile damaged by the cut will fall off. It is easy to sew such fabric not only on the machine, but also manually.

The basis of the costume may consist of a dress, skirt or panties with a blouse. Fitting leggings with a wide elastic band at the waist and a short skirt-bundle will look great. For the top, it is not necessary to sew a blouse on your own, you can pick up a white T-shirt or turtleneck over which a cape will be worn.



How to sew a base

To sew a goat costume with your own hands, you will need patterns. How to make them? The easiest way is to loop around the child‚Äôs clothes and get patterns of leggings, trousers or dresses.

In order to make a skirt, it is necessary to cut a strip of fabric, connect the edges to the top, sew on an elastic band or make a drawstring for the elastic band. If a tutu skirt is planned, then you will need 6-7 meters of tulle, which is cut into strips of 10-15 cm and tied to a dense elastic band as long as the child‚Äôs waist.

The cloak is also cut out in the form of a rectangular cloth. Its edges can be tucked up or treated with an oblique inlay, on top the cloak cloth is turned inside out by about 1.5 cm and a seam is laid, a satin ribbon 3-5 cm wide is inserted into the hole so that the child does not accidentally pull the tie from the drawstring, at the edges of the main fabric you can attach soft buboes. To do this, circles with a diameter of about 10 cm are cut out of the fabric, along the edge the part is stitched and pulled together, filling the bubo with synthetic winterizer. Making a goat costume with your own hands is not difficult - a little imagination and perseverance will give its result.

How to make horns

Horns in the shape of a goat are one of the main details. They can be made using the papier-m√¢ch√© technique, and then painted with ordinary watercolors or sewn from brown fabric and filled with synthetic winterizer. It is necessary to cut out four parts from matter (two from the front, two from the wrong side) in the form of a curved horn and cut out taking into account the increase for the seam (1 cm). Next, you need to sew the blanks in pairs, turn them out and fill with filler. At the base, the horn is carefully sutured with a hidden seam. The size of the horns can be very different. For a glamorous goat, small horns that are barely noticeable in the hair are suitable, but for a formidable goat, large and swirling ones, catching up with fear.

As a base, you can use a bezel, crocodile hair clips, textile dressing or a hat. Next to the horns, small ears of the same fabric as the outfit itself can be sewn onto the base.

How to make a goat mom‚Äôs costume with your own hands so that no one doubts the chosen character? In this case, large horns should be attached to the scarf and complement the outfit with an apron and a long skirt.



Costume Accessories

To make the goat‚Äôs carnival costume sewn with your own hands and look chic, and the look is finished, you must definitely add accessories: beads with a bell, bracelets, flowers or a crown in the hair, a ponytail, white locks of hair in the hairstyle, and even hooves and fur legs. These interesting elements will enliven and add zest along.

Hair strands can be bought on the market or made independently. It is necessary to take a satin ribbon 5 cm wide and cut it into pieces of the required length. For each hairpin, you will need two segments. Tapes are glued with hot glue to the hairpin and dissolve them from the bottom to the junction. As a result, excellent curly curls come out, which can only be fixed in the hairstyle under natural hair.

Such hair clips can also be made from curls from an ordinary wig, which is easy to purchase on the market.

How to make a hoof

It is clear that the child will wear shoes or Czech shoes for the matinee, however, if the legs are properly arranged, the outfit will be simply stunning. So, how to make a goat costume with your own hands with hooves? If the base of the outfit is with leggings or panties, then they should be made flared from the ankle by about 10 cm. A strip of dark fabric will need to be sewn down. For greater effect, you can sew two semi-oval details filled with padding polyester and sewn to the bottom of the trousers. Such a detail will look very impressive.

Hooves can be made not only on the legs, but also on the sleeves.
