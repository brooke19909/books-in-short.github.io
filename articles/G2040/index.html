Soundbar - what is it?



We can say about the soundbar that this is such a music column. That is, not one speaker, but the body, literally stuffed with many speakers with the most diverse characteristics and often installed at different angles in order to fill the room with surround sound as efficiently as possible.

The soundbar's heart is its processor. Namely, it should have sufficient power to cope with the task of controlling multi-channel sound and reproduce surround sound of sufficient power and quality using special audio algorithms for the interaction of controlled speakers.

How it works?





So, soundbar - what is it and how does it work? Probably, one can say about the appearance of such a device as a soundbar, that it is an elongated column under the TV - this device is usually fixed under the image source. It is difficult to imagine for an ordinary person what possibilities the latest audio speakers possess, the diffusers of which are made of carbon fiber and which work in an ensemble with coaxial speakers under the control of the conductor - processor. We learned what a soundbar is. An overview of popular models will be presented later.





The sound, as a rule, comes from the TV to the soundbar and is already laid out in the processor in frequencies, the process of algorithmization passes. Further, in a converted form it is fed to the speakers in that sequence, duplication, duration and frequency, which is able to correspond to the concept of powerful and surround sound with sound quality much higher than even the CD-ROM format.
