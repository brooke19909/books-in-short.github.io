LCD "North", Ryazan, st. Zubkova: description, layout and reviews

At a distance of a little more than one hundred and eighty kilometers from the capital of the state, a large city - Ryazan - spread freely. Its population today is more than half a million people and each of them needs to live somewhere. Therefore, construction in Ryazan does not stop and more and more new buildings and entire residential areas appear. One of these is the LCD "North". Ryazan, or rather its authorities, kindly provided a land plot for the development of this complex almost in the center of the city.





A bit about the city

Ryazan is one of the thirty largest cities in Russia in terms of population. It is located on the right bank of the Oka River, is the largest military, scientific, industrial center. The city consists of four administrative districts, one of which is Oktyabrsky, which sheltered the Severny residential complex (Ryazan). St. Zubkova is the address of the new complex.

Location of the complex

Fifty hectares of land have been allocated for the development of the complex, including the coast of two lakes located on the territory. A reference point can serve as the Ice Palace and the Olympic town, next to which the complex is being built. Description of buildings

In the period from 2014 to 2018, the developer plans to build eleven twenty-five-story houses. But the project is not limited to this. Two kindergartens will also be built so that the children of future residents do not have to go far or ride. Those older children will be able to go to school, also planned by the development company. A shopping center is being built to make purchases and receive other social services. Opportunities for maintaining a healthy lifestyle are provided by the nearby Ice Palace and the Tennis Center.





The highlight of the complex will be a park with an artificial lake. And for small residents, the zoo, which they plan to place in the newly created park, will be most to their liking. This is in addition to the previously existing water bodies with sandy beaches.

Park design solutions

The park created in the residential complex "North" (Ryazan) will be truly unique. Ten hectares of land are allocated for it, on which trees and shrubs will be planted, and green lawns will be created. You can walk along the alleys, and if you are tired, sit down to rest on the benches.

Those who prefer outdoor activities will be able to play basketball, volleyball or hockey in specially equipped areas, ride bicycles or go jogging on the tracks. An entire street gym is created in the park. The younger children also did not ignore the builder and equips them with gaming complexes, a skateboard.

The park is created so that future residents of the complex can relax after a working day, spend a weekend in it with their families, breathe fresh air or play sports.





Naturally, the center of the park will be a cozy artificial lake, which was already mentioned. Near it is a zoo. Animals can not only rejoice, but also feed them.

Territory Security

LCD "North" (Ryazan) will provide complete safety to its residents, not only adults, but also kids. Professional security officers and integrated security systems: video surveillance, providing round-the-clock monitoring of the entire complex, will do this. All types of ventilation: fire, smoke, pumps - fall under the control of the dispatch service.

The appearance of buildings and decoration by the developer

For a more comfortable view, the developer chose panoramic and stained glass with profile translucent structures. External and internal decoration of buildings is carried out using only the highest quality and durable materials: porcelain stoneware, clinker tiles, natural stone.





The decoration of the apartments consists of plastering the walls, cement screed, plastic windows. A metal front door, radiators, metering devices are installed. Fully electrical wiring.

Infrastructure

Reviews of the residential complex "North" (Ryazan) suggest that this residential complex embodies everything necessary for the life and upbringing of children. Until the buildings of their own infrastructure have been erected, already settled residents can take advantage of nearby social services. Children can attend schools located across the street just a few meters from the house. At the services of residents are hypermarkets "Magnet", "Pyaterochka". Specialists of the City Clinical Hospital No. 11 will help the sick.

You can also get to the complex without any problems. For example, you can get to Severny residential complex (Ryazan) by your own transport. St. Zubkova, reviews of residents about transport accessibility say that public transport also provides delivery of residents to the house.
