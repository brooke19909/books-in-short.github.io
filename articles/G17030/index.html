Honey Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): description, photos and reviews of tourists

If you have chosen the island of Phuket as a place of relaxation and are looking for an inexpensive, but comfortable hotel with an excellent location, then Honey Resort 3 * may suit you perfectly.



Location, surroundings

According to many tourists staying at this hotel, "Honey Resort" boasts a very good location. So, you can walk to the popular Kata beach from it in a leisurely step in just a few minutes. Also within walking distance is Karon Beach. It can be reached in a quarter of an hour. If you wish, you can take a walk to Kata Noi. This road will take about half an hour. In addition, you can easily reach any point by tuk-tuk.

In general, judging by the reviews of travelers, Honey Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) is located in a place with well-developed infrastructure. So, in the vicinity of the hotel there are many cafes, bars, shops, supermarkets, shops, massage parlors, etc. There is also a market nearby. Here you can buy fresh seafood. Prepare them right there. Twice a week there is a night market where you can buy a lot of things - from clothes to fruits.

As for the distance from the airport, it takes about 45 minutes to get to the air harbor. According to tourists, the location of the hotel will appeal to those who plan to often go on excursions. So, due to its location, they will be the last to pick you up, and they will be one of the first to bring you. So you will have to spend less time on the road than travelers staying in other hotels.
