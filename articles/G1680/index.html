Village Olenevka, Crimea: reviews about the rest

Do you want to relax in a quiet and beautiful place that fascinates with its unbridled beauty: clean beaches, steep beaches and rich fauna? A kite or windsurfing and scuba diving? And so that all this is inexpensive and close. If so, then Olenevka is perfect for you! Do not know such a settlement? Today we will find out what Olenevka (Crimea) is. Photos, reviews and other information will help us get to know this paradise.

general characteristics

The village of Olenevka is located at the extreme western point of the Crimea, on the Tarkhankut Peninsula, off the coast of the Karanndzhinsky Bay. It belongs to the Black Sea region of Crimea.





Many incorrectly pronounce the name of this settlement, giving out the second letter "e" for "e" and stressing it. The emphasis should fall on the first e. In historical documents you can find other names for this settlement - Karadzha and Kara-Adzhi.

Olenevka is the nearest settlement from the western point of Crimea - Cape Tarkhankut. The village is supplied with electricity, water, internet and mobile connection. It has a bus station, a market, several restaurants, cafes and shops. The central street is Lenin Street.

Resort Specialization

The village of Olenevka (Crimea), reviews of which became the basis of this article, is a very picturesque and calm place. This is a true steppe Crimea with unique air and natural gifts. On three sides, the village is surrounded by warm Black Sea waters.

More recently, Olenevka was a small village, but today it has evolved to the status of a real resort and continues to win more and more tourist hearts every year. The fact is that divers, kite and windsurfers like to meet on the local coast. All pensions are oriented specifically for these sports. Here you can easily find rental wetsuits, scuba gear, masks and fins, as well as refueling cylinders. There are special diving centers in Olenevka (Pelican, Bottlenose Dolphin), which not only rent equipment, but also teach the beginners of diving. And experienced divers are transported here for immersion in the most picturesque places of the district.





Rest and treatment

There are no enterprises and large settlements on Cape Tarkhankut , therefore nothing merges into the sea. Thus, if you are looking for one of the most environmentally friendly areas of the peninsula, then Olenevka (Crimea) is suitable for you. Reviews confirm that nature is really notable here. Therefore, every year more and more tourists come here, not only to dive and go surfing, but also for the purpose of healing and a relaxing family vacation.

Thanks to the sea breezes - soft winds that change direction twice a day (at night they blow from land to sea, and during the day - from sea to land), Olenevka air is a natural inhaler. It is saturated with ozone, calcium, sodium chloride, potassium, magnesium, fluorine, bromine and iodine. A variety of odorous herbs growing in the local steppe give the wind an even more sophisticated aroma. All this has a very positive effect on the health of tourists.

In July-August, sea water at local beaches has a temperature of about 24-26 ¬∞ C. Near the village there is a salt lake of the Sary-Gol estuary. Its medicinal brine and mud contains active ingredients that have a preventive effect on the body. Due to the special climatic conditions of the cape, the summer season here begins in mid-May and ends in October. Here it is Olenevka (Crimea). Reviews show that this place is very fond of people who promote a healthy lifestyle, as well as who prefer family vacations.

Sea and beach

There is only one beach in the village, but it is quite large: 2 km long and 20-40 m wide. It is famous for its clear water and beautiful golden sand. Due to its size, even in high season, the beach is not completely filled. This is another advantage of Olenevka. Gradually increasing depth in the sea allows you to comfortably and safely relax with your children. Many vacationers come here with tents or vans and live right on the beach. On both sides of the beach, the coast is stone cliffs with a height of 20 to 40 meters. Below there are caves in which nudists like to relax. And some extreme people even put up tents there.

The sea elements cut the peninsula, making it look very picturesque: cliffs, grottoes, tunnels, coves, arches - all this is here. Sometimes dolphins come here, which is considered a very auspicious sign.

Estuary

Olenevka (Crimea), the reviews of which we are discussing today, is also famous for its estuary with healing mud and warm water. The diameter of the estuary is 1300 meters, and the depth is 160-190 centimeters. This place is loved by extreme people. The length of the sandy shore, where you can conveniently start kaiter, is 1,500 meters. And windsurfers come to the water from anywhere in the coast of the estuary.

Entertainment and Attractions

In the south of the village there is a lighthouse, which became a guide not only for sailors, but also for lovers to walk along the steppe. The 42-meter tower was built back in 1816. During this period, she needed only a space repair. By the way, now in the tower there is a museum of ancient anchors.

In the north of the village is the Dzhangul Nature Reserve, which is especially attractive in May-June. Almost its entire area is covered with rare Crimean plants and shrubs. The reserve is also good because there are many places on its territory where tourists can pitch a tent camp.
