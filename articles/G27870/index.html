Rest in the rest house "Betta"

Many people start planning their vacation in advance. This allows you to choose the best option for yourself with good conditions. Many Russians go on vacation to the Betta holiday home for many years. This place perfectly combines a good location, picturesque nature and excellent service.





For accommodation, guests are offered various options that allow you to choose housing for different tastes and affluence. The presence of the Black Sea nearby makes Betta village even more popular. Good weather and mild climate have a positive effect on the health of vacationers.

basic information

Recreation center MO "Betta" is comfortably located in the famous resort called Gelendzhik. Nearby is the Black Sea, so the flow of tourists every year becomes more and more. On the territory for guests' accommodation are provided buildings with rooms and summer houses. Rooms with all amenities require a toilet, a refrigerator and everything you need for a comfortable stay. In the three-story building, a toilet and a bathroom are provided for the unit. Summer houses have amenities right on site. For meals, the holiday home has its own dining room, accommodating about four hundred people. Meals are made three times a day.





Both Russians and guests of the country want to spend their holidays in the Betta holiday home. In the boarding house you can make an appointment for treatment of a general therapeutic profile. There is a pebble beach next to the holiday home. The road to it is about 300 meters. The sea temperature is suitable for swimming from May to October. Near the pension there is a wild beach for walks in the fresh air. Tourists often organize excursions to beautiful and significant places. Sports clubs and playgrounds, places for children's games and outdoor activities have been created on the territory. Near the village there are various shops, a hairdresser, a market and other necessary institutions.

How to get there

Getting to the Betta military holiday home is pretty easy. It is located in the Krasnodar Territory (Gelendzhik District). The holiday home is located in the village of Betta, which can be reached by several means of transport:

  1. By train to the city of Novorossiysk, and from the station, get to the destination by bus. Departures are every day.
  2. By bus to the city of Gelendzhik. From there, there is transport to the village of Betta.

Visitors reviews

Every year, many people come to rest in the Betta holiday home. Some of them are regular customers, while others come for the first time. Many visitors note the excellent service and good places to stay. Almost all guests talk about the unique nature and the sea resort. After all, this place just involves a vacation of this type. The green area provides clean air, which is very good for health. Among the trees there are pine, blue spruce, laurel cherries and many others.





Also, reviews about the Betta holiday home are often associated with the sea and the beach, which guests rate quite high. Weather at the resort allows you to spend your vacation with benefit at any time of the year. If you wish, you can swim in the sea even in October, as the temperature of the water allows. As for the rooms, most of the guests are satisfied with the living conditions. Some guests would like better food. There are those regular customers who very often come to rest at the Betta holiday home. Their reviews can be found on the Internet, where they leave thanks from the whole family to individual employees or the staff of the pension as a whole.





Additional services

The Betta military holiday home offers its guests quality service. Parents will be able to leave the baby in the kindergarten, where the teacher will look after him while they are busy with their own affairs. Also for children there is a good playground with attractions. The holiday home has two cinemas for the winter and summer seasons. Literature lovers will find a library with many books. Fans of outdoor activities will also not be bored, as a lot of options are offered for them. In the cold season there is a special winter hall for dancing. And in the summer you can rent a jet ski, boat or catamaran. For a healthy lifestyle, there is a gym, as well as sports grounds for tennis and volleyball. Those who wish can also look into the shooting gallery or billiard room. On the territory of the holiday home there is a free medical office.
