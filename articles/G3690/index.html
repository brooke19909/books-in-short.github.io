A natural miracle of Transbaikalia - Heetei cave

The mysterious underworld has always attracted people who dream of walking through the narrow galleries that were created by nature. Mysterious caves are the most explored objects of our time, keeping many secrets. The silent magic kingdom is a real delight with special beauty. Unique natural monuments are located in different parts of our planet, and today the story will be about the largest miraculous sights of Transbaikalia, where primitive people lived millions of years ago.

Unexplored natural monument

Beautiful at any time of the year, Heatei Cave was discovered in the 17th century. A unique karst object was created by water, which eroded the rocks for many years. He gained fame after visiting Transbaikalia by academician I. Gmelin, who described a natural monument in 1737. However, even for three centuries, the ice kingdom, which does not store ancient rock paintings, has not been fully studied by experts. And only from the middle of the last century, cavers seriously engaged in a natural object, of which there is no mention in the local epic.



Two names of the sacred cave

The name of the karst monument from Buryat translates as "patterned", and it is given for openwork lace of ice crystals decorating a local attraction. Earlier miraculous miracle was called Mikhachin ("cannibalistic"). Allegedly, residents used it as a refrigerator and stored meat supplies here, and it was for this reason that it received such a strange name. However, the old people, recalling the old days, when single travelers went to underground grottoes and never returned, do not agree with this statement and consider this place extremely dangerous.
