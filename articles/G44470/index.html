3 * Hotels: Alidia Beach Resort, India. Photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel 3 * Alidia Beach Resort is a quiet secluded place. It is just perfect for those travelers who are fans of a relaxing holiday away from city noise.





Resort Summary

Hotel 3 * Alidia Beach Resort is a rather old institution with a rich history, which dates back to 1989. Since that time, the hotel has undergone several renovations, during which something new was constantly added for the comfort of guests. We can say that this hotel has become an ideal place for lovers of a colorful and at the same time calm and quiet holiday.

A distinctive feature of the 3 * Alidia Beach Resort can be considered its territory, which is almost completely covered with greenery. The dense plantations create the effect of a real tropical jungle, in the shade of which even in the most intense heat there is a pleasant coolness.

There will be something to do for guests at the hotel Alidia Beach Resort 3 * (Goa). Travelers who have been here speak of a beautiful, clean and well-kept pool, a wonderful restaurant with delicious Indian cuisine. Also, it is impossible not to note the proximity of the hotel to a clean and beautiful public beach, where you can spend days enjoying the warm sun and the gentle sea.





Hotel Location

Hotel 3 * Alidia Beach Resort is located in the heart of the popular tourist village of Baga. In view of this, you can be sure that there will be many retail outlets and catering facilities near your hotel. It is important that the hotel is in close proximity to the sandy public beach. The nearest large settlement is the city of Panaji, which is 15 km from the hotel, and the airport will have to cover a distance of 45 km.





Types of Guest Apartments

Resort Alidia Beach Resort 3 * (Goa) has 40 rooms. Tourists can choose for themselves one of the following accommodation options:

  • Standard room is a small room without air conditioning. Since its dimensions are quite miniature, only an mattress can be offered as an extra bed.
  • Suite - spacious apartments, which consist of a bedroom and a small living room. The rooms are separated by a door.
  • The deluxe room is identical in size to the standard. Nevertheless, there is a larger set of furniture and a rather elegant interior (there is also air conditioning).
  • Superior is a fairly compact apartment. Since they were recently renovated, these rooms are considered the best in the hotel.




Amenities at Guest Apartments

Hotel Alidia Beach Resort 3 * (India, Goa) offers tourists to use the following amenities provided in the apartments:

  • a small terrace with a table and chairs, as well as a rope for drying clothes;

  • Deluxe and Superior rooms have cable TV;
  • Some apartments have individual air conditioners, and the rest (lower category) are equipped with ceiling fans;
  • the bathroom is a toilet combined with a shower, and a cosmetic set and towels are also provided;
  • if necessary, use a hairdryer, you can take it for temporary use at the reception.




Hotel infrastructure

In the resort hotel Alidia Beach Resort 3 * (North Goa, Baga) a variety of infrastructure facilities are available for travelers:

  • a huge tropical garden, which occupies almost the entire territory of the hotel complex;
  • a small but well-kept swimming pool with clean fresh water, around which deck chairs are placed (there is no need for umbrellas, as trees create a thick shadow);
  • the bar offers a huge variety of local drinks, as well as imported alcohol;
  • the hotel restaurant offers tourists breakfasts, as well as an extensive menu, which, in addition to national, presents international dishes;
  • since most rooms do not have televisions, the hotel has a special room where you can watch live shows and movies;
  • if you need to wash things, then you can use the laundry;
  • upon request, you can use the services of a professional doctor;
  • for the use of car parking an additional fee is charged;
  • At the reception you can use the safe.




Positive reviews

Many tourists dream of visiting Goa. Moreover, quite often their choice falls on Alidia Beach Resort 3 *. Reviews of this hotel contain a lot of positive information, which can be expressed in the following comments:

  • since the hotel is located in the heart of the resort village of Baga, there are many shops, shops, outlets and cafes nearby;
  • the appearance of the hotel shows that he is being carefully taken care of;
  • due to the fact that there is a lot of greenery on the territory (both trees, flowers, and other plants), a feeling of real tropical jungle and unity with nature is created;
  • the vegetation of the garden attracts a huge number of birds, as well as animals (here you can find mongooses);
  • the hotel staff is polite, friendly and helpful;
  • in spite of the fact that there is a carriageway near the hotel, there is practically no noise in the territory and in the rooms;
  • the apartments are quite spacious, and they also have everything you need for a comfortable stay;
  • the rooms are very carefully cleaned every day;
  • the nearby beach is clean enough, and you can use the sunbeds on it provided that you order at local cafes (the prices are very affordable);
  • the hotel is very conveniently located relative to the sea (there is a private exit, from which you need to walk no more than 5 minutes to the beach on clean sand).




Negative reviews

A very mixed assessment is given by some tourists to the resort hotel Alidia Beach Resort 3 * (Goa). Reviews about this institution, in addition to positive ones, also contain negative comments:

  • the water in the pool is very cold due to the fact that there is no heating in it, and the shade from the trees does not allow it to heat up from sunlight;
  • Internet is paid and rather slow;
  • because of the increased dampness in the bathrooms of some rooms frogs settled;
  • since the shower is not separated from the toilet in any way (there is not even a curtain), during bathing everything is always flooded with water;
  • the rooms are very uncomfortable old beds, and I also want to note the washed clothes;
  • a lot of mosquitoes both on the street and in the rooms;
  • very poor soundproofing - all conversations and movements of neighbors are heard in the room;
  • paid breakfast is very modest - juice diluted with water, as well as toasts with butter and jam (apparently this was done so that tourists could order paid dishes from the menu in the morning);
  • the owner of the hotel is not too caring with the guests and constantly makes some meaningless remarks to the guests;
  • the safe at the reception is paid, in addition, the cost of this service is 2-3 times higher than in neighboring hotels;
  • despite the fact that the hotel does not provide meals and you have to buy food yourself, there are no refrigerators in the rooms - everything spoils very quickly (apparently, again, this was done so that tourists would leave money in the hotel cafe);
  • poor voltage in the rooms (it is very problematic to use a hairdryer and other electrical appliances, as well as charge the phone);
  • the apartments have very little furniture and the walls are uneven.

General impression

Resort Alidia Beach Resort 3 * (North Goa) makes a fairly positive impression, because its territory is like a real jungle. In addition, the advantageous location offers many opportunities for relaxation. Nevertheless, it can not do without negative points. In almost everything, savings can be seen, which can not always be called reasonable. This applies not only to the furnishings in the rooms, but also to a number of services. So, the cost of some services is several times higher than similar services in neighboring hotels. This is especially true of tariffs for using the safe, car parking, as well as prices in the restaurant.
