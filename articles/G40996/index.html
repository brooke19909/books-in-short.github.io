Kazan, Regina Hotel (Petrovsky, Kazan): description with photos, guest reviews

If you want to spend a pleasant weekend surrounded by beautiful nature, pay attention to the Regina Hotel in Petrovsky in Kazan. Kazan is a beautiful city with a long history. It attracts travelers with a mass of historical and cultural sites, some of which are under the protection of UNESCO. But if you go outside the city, you will fall into the arms of a distinctive nature. If you want to spend time enjoying the silence and clean air, the hotel in Petrovsky is perfect for you.

Additional Information

If you want to relax in the Regina Hotel in Petrovsky in Kazan, do not forget to read some additional information about this institution. Namely:

  • Check-in of newly arrived guests is after 13:00, and check-out is before noon.
  • If you check in early after midnight, you will be charged no more than half the full cost of the accommodation.
  • The cost of an extra bed is 400 rubles per day.
  • This room cannot accommodate more than two extra beds.
  • Pets are allowed by prior arrangement.
  • Only MTS mobile communication operates on the territory. There is no signal from other mobile operators.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, the Regina Hotel is not without drawbacks and shortcomings. Based on the reviews of travelers, it can be concluded about these negative points:

  • poor soundproofing of rooms (even quiet conversations of neighbors are heard in details);
  • the TV shows with interference (this applies to both image and sound);
  • in the off-season, heating does not work well, which is why the rooms are very cold (you have to sleep in warm sweaters or tracksuits);
  • there is no heated towel rail in the bathroom (considering the high humidity, towels are constantly wet);
  • interruptions in hot water supply;
  • Wireless Internet is only in the main building at the reception, which is especially inconvenient for those who are going to work on vacation;
  • in the rooms there are no information sheets about the infrastructure and services of the hotel, and the employees themselves do not tell anything (you have to ask them or get information yourself);
  • old and in some cases defective plumbing in the bathroom;
  • the restaurant is open only until 22:00 (and orders for dinner are generally accepted only until 21:00);
  • on a vast territory very small parking;
  • insufficient number of staff;
  • uncomfortable old mattresses on the beds;
  • old washed bedding;
  • unpleasant stale smell in the bathroom (apparently, this is due to poor ventilation);
  • cold water in the pool;
  • the hotel is difficult to find even by the navigator;
  • the breakfast menu is repeated every day, which is especially annoying for a long stay;
  • the hotel is quite old and has long been in need of repair and equipment upgrades;
  • very cramped and uncomfortably equipped bathroom;
  • if there should be a banquet in the evening, the cafe next to the lake will be closed throughout the day;
  • old small televisions in the room;
  • problems with mobile communications.
