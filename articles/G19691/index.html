Location

LCD "Horizon" (Tyumen) is being built in the river part of the city, across the Tura River. You can get to the center from here in ten minutes, a maximum of a quarter of an hour. If you cross the river and the next railway line, you can get into a dense urban area with many noisy and gassed streets with dozens of shops, pharmacies, offices and other infrastructure. But in the district, the picture is different. Here everything is more calm, there are few buildings, which is why the air is cleaner and less fuss. Address at LCD "Horizon" - st. Melnikayte, 2. Some indicate the address Druzhby Street, because the complex is being built just at its intersection with Melnikayte. To the left of the fork is the Vatutinsky Grove, to the right of the forest park named after Gagarin. In the immediate vicinity of the complex are located the houses of Zarechensky microdistrict No. 5, Vatutino, as well as the popular lake of Alebashevo and the Church of the Three Great Saints.

Ecology: Cons

As conceived by the designers, living in the LCD "Horizon" (Tyumen) should be wonderful. But potential buyers complain of unpleasant odors that are felt at the intersection of Druzhba and Melnikayte streets, just where the complex is being built. The reason is the KNS-2, infamous in Tyumen, and, in a simple way, the sewage discharge from the whole city is located nearby. That is why a beautiful residential complex is not in demand. Regarding KNS-2, a question was raised at the level of the city administration. He was engaged in by deputy A. Chepik, deputy. Mayor A. Shpirenko, deputy prosecutor M. Smirnov. As a result, the construction of a sewer station in the village of Kopylovo was begun in order to close the KNS-2. So, the future residents of the Gorizont residential complex will already be from 2018, although it is unclear from which quarter they will be able to breathe fully.

The same can be said about Lake Alebashevo, which is now overgrown with grass and is not of great value as a resting place. The mayor's office allocated a large sum for the restoration of this reservoir, so there is a hope that here everything will be normal. Putting the lake in order should also be completed in 2018.

The third point, which alarms some Tyumen, is the cemetery, which is only 800 meters from the Horizon. It will not expand, because there is nowhere, so here you can be calm. There is no point in worrying about the sad landscape either, since a number of houses and household facilities were built between the cemetery and the complex.

Construction technology

Of course, it is very important for equity holders how warm the apartments in the Horizon residential complex will be. Tyumen is located in the zone of sharply continental climate, where frosts can reach more than 30 degrees. Therefore, all residential buildings are being built here using special technologies. In this complex, the supports are made monolithic of reinforced concrete, and the external walls are built like sandwiches. The first layer (inner) is ceramic brick, the second is mineral wool, the third (outer) is facing brick. The width of the outer walls is 550 mm. They have increased strength, and most importantly, high thermal insulation qualities.

Internal walls and partitions are made of expanded clay concrete, considered the most environmentally friendly among building materials. The supporting structures in the houses are located around the perimeter, which makes it possible to redevelop the apartments at the discretion of the residents.

For greater heat saving, all buildings will be rented with glazed loggias, and it is planned to install euro windows with triple glazed windows in the rooms. Before renting the house, tenants can agree with the builders and also install similar double-glazed windows on the flats of their apartments.

Entrance to any building will be only from the yard. From the side of the road, no entry is planned. Each building will have a spacious hall and two elevators of the Belarusian brand ‚ÄúLiftmash‚Äù.

Construction progress

The company "Aqua", erecting LCD "Horizon" (Tyumen), the deadlines have already changed several times. The first phase, including 7 buildings, was planned to be commissioned in the fourth quarter of 2015, then in the second quarter of 2016, but the houses were not ready. Therefore, the change was postponed to the IV quarter. In October, the wiring to the buildings of all communications was completed, in two of them the glazing was finished.

In November, finishing work was actively carried out in the porches, plastered walls, glazed loggias, put sockets, switches, heated towel rails in the apartments.

In early December, they launched a transformer substation, connected the sewage system, turned on the boiler room, that is, the heat went into the apartments.

Therefore, interest holders have great chances in the I quarter of 2017 to get the keys.

Phase II is scheduled to be commissioned in 2018, and the entire complex - in 2019.
