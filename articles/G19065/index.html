Snowman from threads: how to make an original craft?

The annual decoration of the house for the New Year holidays is a creative process. You can decorate your home thematically using the decor purchased in the store, but nothing will give you so much pleasure as making decorative crafts with your own hands. An interesting and simple idea is a snowman made of threads. How to make such a decoration? A detailed master class is specially for you in our article.

Materials and Tools



The hand-made article that looks to you is quite impressive, but it is incredibly easy to manufacture. If you still do not know how to make a snowman out of threads and balls, it does not matter - we will teach you! And you are sure to try. The power to make such a craft even for a small child, the most important thing is to do everything exactly according to the instructions. So, to create openwork balls you will need PVA glue, threads (for a snowman - white or blue) floss or iris, balloons, saucer, large needle. Do not forget to prepare decorative elements - beads, colored cardboard and shreds. Use everything that inspires you, and then you will get a very beautiful snowman made of threads. How to make such an interesting decoration? First you need to master the principle of creating blanks.

The technology of manufacturing openwork balls





Start by creating a foundation. To do this, inflate the balloon to the desired size of the openwork form of threads. After this, the workpiece should be arbitrarily wrapped with threads dipped in glue, changing their direction. There are two ways. In the first case, the thread should be threaded into a large needle and pierced through it with a bottle of PVA. Next, you need to stretch a floss or iris through a bottle and wrap it. The second option is to pour glue in the saucer and immerse the end of the thread there. Next, slowly pull and wrap. When there are enough layers of thread, cut off its end and hide it carefully. Then the finished workpiece must be put to dry for several hours in a warm place. We figured out how to make openwork balls, but how to make a snowman out of thread and glue? To create a decorative figure, you will need 2-3 blanks that differ in size from each other. Make them right away and put them to dry, and only after they have dried, start the most interesting part of the creative process.

How to make a snowman out of threads: step-by-step instructions for assembling crafts





Checking the readiness of the balls is very simple - just touch the threads. They must be dry and hard to the touch. If the result is achieved, you can gently pierce the balls with a needle and pull them out. You should get openwork and hollow inside the workpiece of thread. Arrange the balls in the correct order relative to each other. After which it is necessary to glue the workpieces together. It is advisable at this stage to choose the option of placing souvenirs in space and make a suitable mount. The snowman from the balls can be hung by attaching a rope on top. No less spectacular your craft will look and installed on a stand. After choosing the mounting option, stick the resulting figure from the balls to the base or fix the ribbon at the top.

Placement in the interior



Craft from openwork balls will be an interesting addition to the New Year's home decor . The finished snowman can be hung on a Christmas tree or put anywhere. Try to make very tiny figures or, conversely, large ones. You can assemble a composition from several such crafts, it will certainly look more interesting than a lone snowman made of threads. How to make an even more original home decoration? Try to collect several medium-sized snowmen in one garland and hang them under the ceiling. It is possible that you will have other ideas for decorating the premises with these wonderful crafts. Remember only that such souvenirs must not be wet and placed in the immediate vicinity of an open flame.

What else can be done from openwork balls?





Making balls of thread and glue is an exciting and very simple process. Surely after the first crafts you will want to create more in this technique. You already know how to make a snowman out of threads and balls, and why else can you use such openwork blanks? The easiest option for their application is to make simple Christmas toys. Balls can be made from threads of various colors or dyed after drying. In the end, you just have to arrange them (sparkles, sequins, rhinestones, bows, etc. decor will do), attach the ropes - and you can hang the finished decorations on the Christmas tree.

From openwork balls you can collect a variety of shapes, and not necessarily it will be a snowman of thread. How to make new characters? Everything is quite simple: try to depict animals by adding the necessary eyes, ears and mouth to one ball. Or decorate the blank for a snowman under Santa Claus or the Snow Maiden. The options can be infinitely many, and if you put your soul into your work and try, the finished crafts will surely please the eye and cause a smile.
