Where is Qatar located? Brief description of the state

There is such a category of people who are madly in love with traveling. They are constantly striving to visit new corners of our planet.

There is one truly heavenly place. This is a small but amazing country called Qatar. The standard of living is very high. Tourists can visit beautiful and unique places. Impressions, which will be very much after visiting the sights of the country, will be enough for many years.



Where is the state of Qatar located?

On the northeastern outskirts of Arabia, on the small peninsula of the same name, is the small country of Qatar. The state is the monarchy of the Islamic world - the emirate. On the mainland border, it is adjacent to Saudi Arabia, and the rest of the state has access to the Persian Gulf. The capital of Qatar is the city of Doha. Where is the city located? Qatar, like its capital, is located in the Middle East. The terrain here is mostly deserted. Naturally, such a geographical location directly affects many factors, such as climate, flora, fauna, etc.

The history of the emirate of Qatar begins in the period BC. Records of people living in these territories were left by Herodotus and Pliny. The peninsula becomes the state in the 7th century AD, when Islam comes to this territory. Of course, this was influenced by where Qatar is located, and more precisely, neighboring states.

The formation of Islam takes place under the influence of the neighboring Arab caliphate. For a long time, Qatar was in the Ottoman Empire. After its collapse, the state became a protectorate of Great Britain. In 1971, the country gained independence and became officially called the state of Qatar.



Relief

In order to properly characterize the relief of a given area, it is necessary to pay attention to where Qatar is located. According to the physical and geographical characteristics, its entire territory is a desert. Only in the north occasionally appears flat terrain and a few oases. The southern region is slightly elevated and is represented by high sandy hills.

Climate

The climate of these places also directly depends on where Qatar is located. It is tropical, continental type and very dry. Inland waters

Of course, if you understand where Qatar is located, it immediately becomes clear that there are practically no rivers on the peninsula. There are also waterways that dry up in the summer. However, the inhabitants of the country learned how to obtain drinking water by desalination of sea water. There are underground sources in the oases, but they are not enough throughout the country. Thanks to them, only the northern regions are provided.

Governing body

According to the state system, Qatar is an absolute monarchy. However, in the Islamic world, this word means something else. The fact is that the emirate is a monarchy of the non-hereditary type. That is, all power (executive, legislative, judicial and military) may not necessarily be inherited. Due to the state system in Qatar, it is forbidden to create political parties or trade unions, as well as to conduct various kinds of political demonstrations. There is an Advisory Council for consultation on diplomatic and public affairs in the country. It includes 35 people.



Administrative divisions and population

Qatar consists of 7 regions - municipalities. The population is 1,900,000. More than 90% of the population lives in the capital Doha and the suburbs of the capital. According to the national composition, about 40% of the population are Arabs (this is understandable, given the location of Qatar), 18% are Pakistanis and Indians, 10% are Iranians and 14% are representatives of other nationalities. About 80% of the population are Muslims, 9% profess Christianity. Other religions are also common.

Economy

The state economy is mainly focused on oil and gas production. 85% of the extracted gas and oil products are exported, replenishing the country's budget by 70%.
The country has developed many industries. 25% falls on the service sector, but agriculture in the country does not develop at all. Only some northern regions, due to their close proximity to oases, have the opportunity to garden and grow date palms. From livestock, nomadic tribes are engaged in the breeding of goats, sheep and camels.

Qatar is also developing in the military sphere. Officially, he has been cooperating with the United States in this regard since 1992. One of the major foreign centers of the United States Army is deployed here.

The number of armed forces of Qatar is more than 12 thousand people. Of these, 8.5 thousand - land forces, 2.1 thousand, marines - 1.8 thousand.

Terrorism

The Republic of Qatar (where the state is located, described above) has been repeatedly accused of interacting with various terrorist organizations of the planet. The reason for this was the presence of the popular television channel Al Jazeera in Doha. There were speeches and messages from famous terrorists, for example, Osama bin Laden.
