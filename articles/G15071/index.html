Motel "Eurasia-Aksai" in Rostov-on-Don: tourist reviews and photos

Aksai is a small industrial city in the Rostov region. Mostly its guests are not tourists, but entrepreneurs and businessmen. Often in hotels in the city stop those who find themselves passing through here. Aksay hotels offer comfortable rooms and decent service. Well-trained staff welcomes visitors in a friendly manner and makes every effort to make their stay in the institution as comfortable as possible. But choosing an option to stay on site is quite difficult, so potential guests should take care of the room in advance.

Many people think that the most convenient place to spend the night on the Moscow-Rostov route (M-4 Don highway) is the Eurasia-Aksai Motel. There is also a gas station and several cafes.

Reviews

Visitors give the hotel very conflicting characteristics. Of the advantages, many call a convenient location, large clean rooms, closed parking. The biggest minus the reviewers consider the cost of living. They even recommend driving past the hotel without stopping, as the local prices do not correspond to the level of service at all. Mostly the Eurasia-Aksai motel reviews of tourists characterize negatively.

According to them, everything is shabby in the hotel, the rooms are saturated with an unpleasant odor. Reviewers say this is the worst place they have ever had to stay.

Many people call the service at the hotel a complete swindle, believing that it is completely not provided for a comfortable overnight stay and you can‚Äôt stay here even for one night. Tired travelers at the hotel can neither swim nor sleep peacefully. The hotel lacks basic living conditions, according to the guests. Some had to face a broken sewer, a broken toilet, and a lack of bedding. In the shower, the drain can be clogged, water completely fills the bathtub and pours onto the floor, there are no towels, no soap, no hairdryer, or other necessary trifles, the hotel guests say.

Rooms

The rooms are very smoky, many say. According to the residents, the tulle in the rooms was worn 10 years ago and, maybe, it was once white, but at the moment it is saturated gray-yellow. The beds are narrow and short. Residents with height from 180 cm have to sleep all night with their legs bent. The mattress is completely squeezed and terribly creaky. Each turn on the back or on the side is accompanied by a loud creak, and the springs painfully dig into the body. Instead of duvet covers, guests can be provided with sheets. The bedding is gray and disgusting to the touch. The appearance of the blankets for guests is often not so inspiring that they have to give up the desire to use them and freeze all night.

Microscopic TV in the room, the authors of reviews say, ruffles, and the number of channels is limited.





Hotel guests also talk about the fact that when the windows of the rooms (huge, full wall) go to the side of the highway, this is very inconvenient, since the windows in the room are too noisy. Right above the bed, some rooms have small windows overlooking the parking lot. If you open them, you can hear everything that happens there: the arrival and departure of cars, people talking, dog barking, etc. In addition, the noise of passing cars from the highway is clearly audible. If you close the window, it will be very stuffy to sleep. The air conditioning in the room is too weak. There is no Wi-Fi in the room.

Nutrition

Breakfast, according to the guests, consists of 4 dishes (complex), which you have to choose in advance from the evening, while not getting to know the menu. The next morning, the food chosen is completely tasteless.





The dining room is three steps away. The food ordering system, as guests say, is "interesting." Many had never seen anything like it before. At the entrance to the dining room there is an attendant with a menu in which already collected meals are registered. Guests choose their lunch numbers, they calculate the total amount, give a ticket with a list of ordered dishes, after which the tourists go to the distribution point. And here it turns out that the selected dishes have already ended, something is not ready yet, and something is not at all foreseen for cooking in this dining room.

Many reviewers report that they have never seen a more untidy hotel. It would be better to spend the night in a car - some tourists share.

Conclusion

Impressions of tourists about staying at the Eurasia Aksai Motel are very mixed. The institution has both its cons and pros. But the fact is that many people passing by on the road fully, according to their reviews, did not succeed.
