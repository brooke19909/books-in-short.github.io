Latanya Park Resort 4 * (Turkey, Bodrum): room description, service, reviews

Turkish resorts are incredibly popular among ordinary Russian inhabitants. Often they choose the town of Bodrum for their vacation, which attracts many with its picturesque and excellent climate. In this city and in the settlements, which are located near it, there are many hotels that offer a chic vacation with the best conditions. One of them is Latanya Park Resort 4 *.

Location

The hotel is located in the village of Yalichiftlik, which is almost on the border with Bodrum. In the half-hour availability there is a huge amount of entertainment, which can be reached by taxi or car rented at the hotel.





The weather in Bodrum is quite warm and pleasant, the climate is temperate. This allows hotel guests to feel completely comfortable at any time of the year, since the summer here is not famous for the hot heat and the winter is not cold. The water temperature in the sea during the holiday period reaches 25 degrees.

general information

Hotel Latanya Park Resort 4 * was built in 1996. Since that period, both small and significant changes, as well as restoration, have been made on its territory. In 2007, an overhaul was made, during which the entire territory was modernized, and some innovations were introduced into the entertainment complexes. The territory of Latanya Park Resort 4 * is 17 thousand square meters. m. It housed housing for guests, as well as places for their relaxation and entertainment.

Rooms

All rooms are located in its buildings, which are located at a short distance from the sea coast. Most are located in the main building, which is a three-story building, the rest are located in four three-story blocks. All apartments are divided into several categories: economy, standard and family.





In total, the entire hotel has 204 rooms, each of which is designed to create the most comfortable living conditions for guests of all age categories. Modern furniture is installed here, as well as a climate system that allows residents to create optimal conditions regardless of the weather in Bodrum.

Economy

Economy rooms are apartments that occupy a small area - about 25 square meters. m each. The furniture that they have is designed for a maximum of a couple of guests. The furniture here has a large double bed (you can choose a room with two single beds), two small cupboards, a wardrobe for personal items, as well as a telephone and a safe. Guests can use shower cabins and a bathroom, which are individually located in each apartment. The description of the rooms of Latanya Park Resort 4 * must also indicate that almost all of them have access to a personal balcony or an open terrace, where it is possible not only to explore the surrounding expanses, but also to take sun baths. For relaxation, there is a small table and a couple of chairs.

Standard

The rooms, which offer standard conditions for guests, also have the necessary furniture set, consisting of a bed and storage space. In addition, there is a plasma TV with the ability to view satellite channels.





This room can accommodate from two to four guests. Each of the apartments has a private balcony with beautiful views of the beach and surrounding gardens. For free, guests can use the minibar and electronic safe.

Family

This type of apartment in Latanya Park Resort 4 * is designed to accommodate groups of guests up to five people. This option is ideal for travelers who relax in the company of friends or the whole family. The territory of the room is divided into two sleeping areas, as well as a living room, which is equipped with upholstered furniture, coffee tables and plasma TVs. Here for the whole room there is one bathroom, in which there is a sink, a bathroom, as well as a cheap cabin. This room has access to its own outdoor terrace.

Conditions for children

Hotel Latanya Park Resort 4 * is perfect for family holidays. It has large rooms, where there are conditions for small guests. A large playground is equipped here, where kids can actively relax, play and get to know peers. They also have the opportunity to attend a personal club, but there are some age restrictions in it - they only let children from 4 to 12 years old. Among other things, there is a small pool with high slides where kids can frolic. Everything is equipped here in compliance with safety standards.

In every catering establishment that is available in Latanya Park Resort 4 * (Bodrum), there are special highchairs, as well as a personal menu designed taking into account the individual characteristics of the kids. It contains only the most striking and mouth-watering dishes that, with their appearance, may be of interest to these fastidious gourmets.

Entertainment for adults

The well-developed infrastructure of the Latanya Park Resort 4 * hotel does not allow vacationers to get bored. Every evening for the guests arranged funny discos with contests and a show program. A team of professional animators works here, which entertains guests throughout the day.





In the mornings and until lunchtime in the large pool there are sports competitions and relay races, in which vacationers take part, and the rest of the time guests can gather in a group and go on an excursion, but such a pleasure is not free. The hotel has a relaxation area with tables for playing billiards, darts and table tennis.

Sport

Fans of a healthy lifestyle will certainly visit the local gym, where a large number of exercise machines are installed. Guests also attend aerobics and fitness classes. The hotel has well-developed facilities for water sports. Here, experienced instructors can help with canoeing, water skiing, sailing, and windsurfing. All those who love team games can go to the soccer field, volleyball or basketball court.

Pool

The hotel has two pools that are open. One of them is for adults, and the second is for children. They have a small water park consisting of several slides. The large pool is regularly cleaned, it also has a backlight and automatic water heating, which is triggered by a sharp drop in temperature. Around it is a large sun terrace, equipped with comfortable sun loungers and beach umbrellas. Here, for a certain time, air mattresses and towels are issued.

Nutrition

Restaurants in Latanya Park Resort 4 * are famous for serving amazingly delicious food prepared by professional chefs. In them you can taste dishes of European cuisine and those that are traditional Turkish. Complex breakfasts, lunches are served here, as well as dinners for guests. Guests can also look at the buffet here. For guests who are watching their figure, there is a diet menu specially developed by unique methods.





In addition to restaurants, the hotel has four bars where vacationers can enjoy cold drinks, alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails, as well as light snacks and salads. The menu of the local bars has a large selection of desserts, ice cream, tea and coffee.

For business

Often in Latanya Park Resort 4 * (Turkey) people who are connected in one way or another with a business stay. Often, they need to arrange a meeting with business partners or conduct a presentation right during the holidays. In this case, there is the opportunity to use the services of one of the two conference rooms that are in the hotel. They are small in size but very well equipped. Each hall has a certain number of comfortable seats (15 and 70), a small tribune, and a presentation board. In addition, visitors are presented with projectors, laptops, good sound and lighting equipment, as well as a large number of office supplies.

Hotel Services

Service Latanya Park Resort 4 * is good enough. It offers a wide range of services, some of which are paid, and some can be used free of charge. So, guests can use the free Wi-Fi Internet access service throughout the hotel, as well as in rooms. A currency exchange office operates here, and several ATMs are installed in which foreign tourists can receive cash.





For a fee, guests have the opportunity to use the laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as call a doctor who is on duty at the hotel around the clock. Here it seems possible to rent a car or bicycle, by the way, their parking in this case will be free.

Prices

The cost of living in this high-level Turkish hotel cannot but please lovers of a good and inexpensive vacation. On average, a weekly stay for two in a standard room will cost about 75-80 thousand rubles. Tour operators include flights, insurance premiums, room rental, transfers from the airport to the hotel and back, and in some cases a number of other services as standard accommodation packages. The price of the package may also depend on the place of departure and, in fact, the company where it was purchased.

Who would like a vacation at Latanya Park Resort

This Turkish hotel is a great place to relax for all ages. Often, newlyweds stay in his rooms, who come to Turkey in order to spend their honeymoon. The hotel is also incredibly popular among couples who want to stay at the resort together. A large number of families have a rest here with small children. Kids here really like it, because there is plenty of entertainment for them on site. The complex has a lot of noisy and quiet places, so every guest will find a suitable atmosphere for him.

Guest reviews

Tourists who spent their holidays in Latanya Park Resort 4 * in their reviews very often share their impressions. They can be found on social networks and on open spaces of sites on a tourist subject.

In their comments, tourists almost always share their impressions of local service. According to them, the hotel has a polite staff who are professional in their work. In particular, the reception desk is supported by administrators who are attentive to each guest. They can help with the solution of different situations, as well as answer your questions. By the way, many of them speak several foreign languages, including Russian. This greatly facilitates the stay at the hotel of domestic tourists. The animation team very often receives a lot of pleasant reviews for their attention to children and adults, and the cooks in the restaurant are able to win the heart of any gourmet.

As for entertainment, tourists themselves note that





there are a lot of them in the hotel. As many say, if you wish, you can get far beyond it - on an exciting tour, which has a certain cost. Fans of noisy nightlife can go to a night disco, and sports fans have the opportunity to visit everything from the gym to the tennis court. Most tourists express a lot of enthusiasm about the fact that on the beach of Latanya Park Resort you can engage in extreme water sports, and under the guidance of experienced instructors.

Young parents who come to rest with their children share their comments on living conditions for their babies. As they say, everything is arranged here as favorable as possible for their fun pastime, and besides, the parents themselves can take care of themselves by hiring a nanny for their children.

Guests are also pleased with the local climate, clear sea and the magnificent nature that is around. Here, according to vacationers, it is always sunny, warm and fun. That is why many people wish to return to the hotel again and recommend that their friends, relatives and, of course, subscribers on social networks go to rest here.
