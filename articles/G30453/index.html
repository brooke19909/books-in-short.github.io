How to make a flower for mom?

Roses, daisies, tulips, peonies, dahlias, lilies and many other flowers we used to collect in bouquets and give to loved ones. Any woman with pleasure will accept fragrant flowers as a gift. You should be especially careful when choosing a bouquet of flowers for your mother. But what to do when there is no way to spend money on buying fresh flowers, but you want to make a pleasant one for your loved one? There is a way out - to make a bouquet with your own hand. This will be a very original and fresh solution that will not go unnoticed. So, in order to decide what to make a flower for mom, options with a detailed description will be given below.

Open tulip

For the manufacture we need colored square leaves (8x8 cm), A4 green paper , scissors and glue.

1. Let's get started, let's make the flowers. To do this, fold a square piece of paper in half, then again and for the last time vertically.

2. We unfold, there are bending lines on the sheet, they will serve as a guide for incisions. The bend lines form four small triangles, each of them must be cut in the middle. The notch should be larger than the middle of the line.

3. Next, you need to glue the future flower for mom. The corner that we cut off must be glued to the next corner. In total, four triangles need to be glued together.

4. The tulip bud is ready, now let's move on to making the legs. Take a sheet of green paper, cut it in half. After this, you need to twist the sheet into a tube, gluing its end.

5. We proceed to fix the stem to the bud. To do this, cuts are made at the end of the tube 1-1.5 cm long. Having spread the incised shreds, you need to grease them with glue, and then glue them to the bottom of the bud. That‚Äôs all done.





In order for our flowers to be more natural for our mother (with our own hands), you can cut and paste leaves on the stem. The more flowers you make, the more magnificent the bouquet will turn out.

Voluminous tulip

This is a more complicated version of origami, but the effort is worth it. To make a flower, you need a square sheet of colored paper and patience. In just 13 steps, the original flower for mom will be ready. So let's get started:

1. We take a prepared sheet of paper.

2. Fold it diagonally for layout.

3. The resulting triangle is folded in half again.

4. Return the sheet to its original position, and then fold it horizontally.

5. We have a rectangle with marked triangles. Two small triangles on the left and on the right are folded inward, like an accordion.

6. The result is a double triangle. Bend the top layer to the left and right.

7. We do the same on the reverse side.

8. Turn the top layer on both sides from right to left.

9. The top layer of paper is folded toward the middle. To do the same operation from the reverse side.

10. It is necessary to fill the left corner in the middle of the right.

11. Turning the workpiece over, perform a similar operation.

12. A small hole forms on top of our tulip. Through it you need to inflate a tulip.

13. The last step remains, bend four petals from the top. And our tulip is ready.

Paper flowers as a gift to mom can be arranged in the form of a bouquet. An interesting decision to make tulips of different colors, pieces 5-7, tied with a beautiful ribbon.





Colored hyacinths

Gorgeous flowers that are sold exclusively in pots. But in a paper version, hyacinths with stems can be made. In order to make a spring flower for mom, you need colored paper, scissors and glue. The following is step-by-step instructions.

1. First we make the stem, for this we roll up a green sheet of A4 paper. We fix the end with glue for strength.

2. Now let's start making flowers. To do this, cut a color sheet of A4 format into four parts.

3. Take one resulting square, fold in half and cut along the entire length of the bend line. Leaving approximately 1 cm untouched.

4. Now proceed to the assembly of the flower. We apply glue to the untouched part of the incised square and glue it round to the top of our stem.

5. Now we bend the previously made incisions for the appearance of volume.

6. Do the same with the rest of the squares. The number of squares may vary in each case.





So we got wonderful flowers for mom. With your own hands you can make the necessary amount for the bouquet. Ready-made hyacinths can be put in a jar and decorated with a ribbon.

Spiral rosettes

Roses from a spiral look very original, which you can make according to the instructions below. To get started, we will prepare the necessary materials, namely: colored paper, pencil, scissors, toothpicks or bamboo skewers.

1. Determine the size of future roses, based on this we cut out the necessary circle from colored paper.

2. Draw a spiral on the resulting circle.

3. And now we cut along the lines with scissors.

4. After, armed with a toothpick, we begin to twist the cut spiral. A toothpick is needed only at the beginning, then you can twist it with your fingers.

5. To fix the bud with a drop on the center circle glue. Before that, fluff our curls. Rose is ready.





You can experiment with the stem. It can be made of wire or wood, wrapped in green paper. You can even use live tree branches. Remember that the flower for mom was more exquisite, you can stick leaves and thorns on the stalk.

Simple clove

If you do not like to bother with cutting and gluing, then you will like this option. Carnations are based on paper molds for muffins and wire. So let's get started:

1. Take a wire and string on it cupcake molds.

2. The number of forms depends on how much clove is planned.

3. Now squeeze the molds and fix the bottom of the bud with wire.

The flower is ready, it remains to wrap the wire with green corrugated paper and glue the petals.





Fantasy flight

The above are just some of the options that will help you choose which flowers to make for your mom. You can experiment and try your options. After all, the main thing is that they carry a piece of your warmth and attention with your own hand-made flowers.
