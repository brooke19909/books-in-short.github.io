What about interest charges?

An anonymized metal account with Sberbank is not accompanied by interest accrual. In Russia, it is not customary to charge additional funds to accounts of this category. If a financial institution offers a rate of 1 to 2%, which will be calculated in grams of metal, then this is an incredibly advantageous offer, and you should immediately agree to it. In Western countries, the practice of accruing interest on a contribution is quite widespread. The investor receives profit on this category of deposits due to a change in quotations.
