If you want to do well, do it yourself

Most of us are used to giving children soft toys for various holidays. However, many modern copies, placed on store shelves, are very expensive, and look somewhat strange, and sometimes even scary. Therefore, many creative people are thinking about how to independently make a funny, cute and kind character. The charming doggie shown in the main photo will surely please the child. Perhaps he will even become the most beloved and dear friend. Largely because the dog was tied to a soft toy by a close person - mother, grandmother, aunt, sister.

Preparatory stage

Before you start knitting a cute doggie, you should prepare the stuff for stuffing. Experts believe that ordinary cotton wool is better not to use. After all, such a product will turn out to be too heavy, if necessary it will be quite difficult to wash and dry it. Therefore, it is wiser to take a synthetic winterizer or holofiber. For example, you can gut an unnecessary pillow and stuff it with your product contents.

It is also worth noting that the toy shown in the photo has circles on its paws - appliqu√©s. The dog is crocheted according to the technology that we will study further, and the stripes are cut from felt. However, this condition is optional, if you want, the needlewoman can relate these details.

It is still necessary to prepare a sewing needle, threads of suitable colors and scissors. It is more reasonable to choose a hook during or after the purchase of yarn. After all, the tool should be equal to the thickness of the thread.

Yarn selection

Needlewomen, who are professionally engaged in knitted toys, note that special children's yarn is best suited for such products. It is very soft and does not cause allergies. In addition, it is absolutely safe for babies who are used to chewing everything that comes to their hand.

To knit the dog shown in the photo, knitting threads of four colors are required: the main one is beige and three additional ones are white, black and blue. And it is better to use yarn from one company, but in different shades. Then the finished product will be the same density.

It is impossible not to note one more important nuance, in the main photo the toy being studied is a soft dog, made in beige and blue tones. But if you wish, you can use your own combination or what the child likes.

Knitting features

For people who are familiar with the technique of amigurumi, the implementation of the studied product will not cause difficulties. However, it is important for beginners to clarify several important nuances that determine the quality and beauty of the craft. To begin with, any toys are knit in a spiral with columns without crochets. Moreover, a new loop must be tied in such a way as to grab both threads of the previous row. The technology can be explored in the following picture. The image on the left illustrates the wrong actions, on the right - the right ones.

Another important feature of knitting toys "Soft dog" is the initial stage. Having formed an amigurumi ring, the next 2-3 rows must be knitted, moving counterclockwise. That is, like any other thing. Then the circle needs to be turned inside out. As a result, the initial tail will be inside the craft. Next, knit in the opposite direction - respectively, clockwise.

How to knit amigurumi ring

In the previous paragraph, a term incomprehensible to many beginning needlewomen was mentioned. Therefore, now it is necessary to clarify it, because otherwise, studying the scheme and description of the dog with a crochet will be meaningless. The product is still not very neat.

So what does the amigurumi ring mean? In fact, this is a special way to start knitting any toy. The technology is pretty simple:

  1. We wind the thread two times on the index finger of the left hand.
  2. Carefully remove the loop.
  3. And crochet, making six single crochet.
  4. We connect the last loop of the row with the first.
  5. Then we gently pull on the initial tip so that the center of the circle tightly closes.

If the necessary actions have not been clearly explained, you can consider the graphical instructions.

We knit the head

Having dealt with the preparatory stage, the terms and features of the technology, we proceed to the implementation of ideas. To do this, carefully study the MK Crochet Dog presented below. We start by completing the head of a soft toy:

  1. We take the main yarn.
  2. We form an amigurumi ring.
  3. In the 2nd row, we knit two out of the loops of the previous row - we make an increase. Thus adding 6 new loops.
  4. In the 3rd, we increase through one single crochet. Also add 6 loops.
  5. Turn the circle and knit in the opposite direction.
  6. From the 4th to the 11th row, we also add loops. But with each row we increase the interval between increases - the number of columns without a crochet. In the fourth row there should be 2, in the eleventh - 9.
  7. In total, 66 loops should be obtained.
  8. The next part of MK "Crochet Dog for Beginners" involves knitting 4 rows in a spiral.
  9. Next, we begin to reduce the loop. From the 16th to the 24th row in even rows of 2 through - 31, 30, 29, 28, 27 single crochet. Odd just knit.
  10. In the 25th row, the interval is 12 bars, in 26 - 11, in 27 - 6, in 28 - 5.
  11. We leave 36 loops and knit 7 rows in a spiral.
  12. We fill the head and knit another 7 rows.
  13. We reduce 6 loops in 5 rows.
  14. Do not forget to add filler.

We knit paws

  10. From the 1st to the 10th row we knit, based on a pattern for the head.
  11. We knit the 11th row, applying only one loop of the lower row.
