The main attractions of the archipelago

If you are on Liberty Island, then you must see these sights:

  • Old Havana, which is an architectural monument of the country;
  • Castle of El Morro on the shores of the azure ocean;
  • The Bolshoi Theater of Havana and the Finca Vihia Museum, where you can get acquainted with national features;
  • the mausoleum of Che Guevara and the Museum of the Revolution will open the mysterious curtain over the turbulent past of the state.




In addition, Cuba in November is famous for endless noisy celebrations and concerts. St. Cristobal's Day (Christopher Columbus) is celebrated on November 16. He is considered the heavenly protector of the islands. Here they believe that he takes away all troubles and misfortunes. In honor of the event, fun festivals are held - music pours through the territory of entire islands. The festive atmosphere frees the mind of travelers from all the troubles and unrest that could have been accidentally captured from their homeland.





You must try it.

Cuba in November is famous not only for weather and attractions, but also for national cuisine. Its principles have evolved over hundreds of years under the influence of other states. The culinary tradition of Liberty Island has become the quintessence of Spanish, African and Creole tastes.





Every year, Cubans hold numerous festivals of local culinary talents. Professional chefs show their skills in order to surprise guests. If you find such an event, then you must visit the holiday. Traveler reviews report that this is not to be tried anywhere else.





In addition, Cuban sauces are known everywhere. They have an exquisite piquant taste and will appeal to even gourmets. Tomatoes, garlic, peppers, onions, various oils and various fruits are just a short list of those products that can be used in the preparation of Mojo and Salsa criolla sauces.

  • Rum is a magical pirate liquid that can cheer you up even on a worse day. Here you will find a huge number of branded drinks, the aging time of which can reach a quarter of a century.
  • It is believed that black coral is able to exert a magical effect on its master. Unfortunately, only about 10 percent of the products sold are original. Black corals are cast in red with shades of light. This differentiated sign will reveal falsification.
  • Guayabera is a men's shirt that will appeal to the stronger sex. Often they are colored, but you can also find a monophonic outfit.
  • Image of Che Guevara - the national hero of the country. It is found almost everywhere. His photo will be the perfect complement to your souvenir set.




As you can see, there are many souvenirs on Liberty Island. Surely everyone will find something for themselves and their friends. Reviews of tourists are advised to pay attention to the quality of the purchased products, as often it is quite low. You will need to spend some effort and time to find a good product. Do not be lazy and conduct a thorough search. The result will certainly comfort you. And later it will delight for many years and decades, recalling a vacation in a paradise of the globe.
