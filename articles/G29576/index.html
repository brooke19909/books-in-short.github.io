Recreation facilities in Altai in winter. Leisure

Not one place on Earth can be compared with the Altai Mountains in beauty. No wonder he is considered the pearl of Siberia. The fabulous splendor of this region, created by nature, attracts a huge number of tourists to these places. There are snow-capped mountain peaks, and spreading valleys, and full-flowing rivers, picturesque waterfalls, crystal clear waters of the lake, lush vegetation and amazing wildlife. However, Altai Territory is famous not only for its natural wealth. There are also monuments of ancient architecture.





Mountain Altai is striking in its diversity and diversity. All year round you can enjoy a wonderful holiday.

Recreation facilities in the Altai Mountains in winter provide the best conditions for an impressive pastime.

Tourist base "Gold mining"

Not all tourist complexes of the Altai Mountains can boast of such a variety of services that Zolotorudnaya provides its guests. The base is located at the very foot of the mountains. There is no noise of highways, only the tranquility of beautiful nature. Therefore, the camp site is especially popular with those who prefer solitude or a relaxing holiday with children, who value every minute spent in harmony with nature, and whose heart is filled with happiness among mountain ranges, forest glades and birch groves.

Features of rest

The tourist complex "Zolotorudnaya" occupies 2.5 ha of land. There are no special buildings in the base. Everything is created so that a person can be alone with nature. Nevertheless, there is a well-equipped area for recreation, and even playgrounds for children.

The pearl of the Altai Mountains is the Katun River. Zolotorudnaya provides its guests with all the conditions for exploring this majestic river. You can have a great time on its shores, especially since the base has its own access to its coast. It has everything you need: a picnic area and the opportunity for fishing.

Recreation centers of the Altai Mountains





In winter, you can relax no worse than in summer. Altai Mountains is famous for its tourist resorts, which welcome guests all year round. Some of them deserve special attention. The hotel complex Park - Hotel Aya, located near the lake of the same name, combines a calm comfortable rest with various entertainments. In winter, you can ride on a skating rink or on a roller coaster. It is an ideal place for conferences, seminars. The restaurant can hold banquets or receptions. A guarded parking lot is open all year.

The unique tourist complex "Turquoise Katun" is also designed for year-round visits. On the vast territory of the base there are three modern slopes with a total length of 3,500 m. Beginners can try their hand at the training slope. All tracks are equipped with lighting and lifts. The attraction of the camp site is its own artificial lake.

One of the newest tourist bases in the Altai Mountains is Atlant base. The complex is located on the banks of the Katun River. It provides its visitors with accommodation in a comfortable hotel, meals, use of a sauna and two saunas.

The tourist complex Zaimka Kamza is located in a picturesque place surrounded by forests and mountain slopes. The base is located on the banks of the Cox River. The complex offers its visitors comfortable rooms, the opportunity to choose their own menu, as well as wellness treatments.
