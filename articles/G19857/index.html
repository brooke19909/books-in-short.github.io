Why is this place so popular?

Uvildy is not only the beauty of mountains and bays, it is also incredibly transparent, clean and healthy water. A huge number of people visit these places in order to improve their health, because on the shores of the lake there is healing mud and many sources with weak mineral radon water. People who suffer from ailments of the nervous, cardiovascular, endocrine systems and many others seek to visit the lake. A mild climate, a high degree of ionization of air, dirt, radon waters - this is not even the whole list of those factors that attract travelers to these beautiful places.

What about fishing?

While women and children admire the local scenery, men can take their souls out fishing, not only in summer but also in winter! Forest Lake Uvildy is known as a reservoir, which has not only, so to speak, ordinary fish species, but also rare ones listed in the Red Book. Pike, ripus, bream, crayfish, nelma, omul, muksun, carp, etc. are found here. For lovers of spearfishing and diving, it is simply better not to find a place! You can go fishing for the soul, or you can take part in competitions that are constantly held here. However, be aware that only pensioners and children under 12 years old can enjoy this type of leisure for free. Even in winter, fishing enthusiasts go to these places with full equipment.
