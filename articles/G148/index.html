Among the huge number of possible variants of motion, of particular interest is the oscillatory one, which is distinguished by the fact that the system repeats a change in its coordinates (or physical quantities) at certain intervals of time - cycles. It cannot be otherwise - after all, the phase of oscillations is an argument of the function that describes these oscillations.
