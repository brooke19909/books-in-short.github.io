Weave a bead pendant

The most wonderful and unique product in the form of a pendant can be created with your own hands. You only need to know how to properly perform the product and what materials to use for this.



What bead pendant can weave

Models of pendants are diverse and unique for each performance. It is impossible to create two absolutely identical products, even observing all the rules and nuances of weaving. Each craftswoman does all the work with her originality and makes it a step-by-step method convenient only for her. Therefore, all products have distinctive features. This guarantees you authorship even when performing products already known to all. Also, when weaving, you can easily add something of your own to the scheme or replace the elements with completely opposite ones. It all depends on your individual taste and on an extraordinary approach to working on the product.

What materials can be used



The basis of the pendant consists of a strong thread or fishing line, the diameter of which depends on the thickness of the beads or beads used. They can be completely different in size and color, as well as in shape. Beadwork looks good in the form of drops or a flower. Also, the core of decorative stones or copper plates with inscriptions can add originality to the product. They are braided with beads and firmly fixed in the product.

Things to consider when performing a product

1. Weaving a pendant from beads and making a lace for it takes a little time, so every fashionista must make it for herself. But it is not suitable for every wardrobe and not for everyday use.

2. If you are the first time to weave a pendant from beads, then choose an easy-to-make and understandable diagram for you. Buy all the necessary materials with a margin, just in case. Select beads by size and use only the same. For convenience, pour everything you need into a tray or in a low box. This will speed up the weaving process. Be sure to follow all the steps in the scheme and do not be afraid.

3. If your pendant from beads turns out to be uneven and does not straighten, then this means that you pulled the beads too tight. In this case, you have to start all over again or change the basis of the product. If you were weaving fishing line, then try replacing it with a strong thread, a little larger.

4. Only after completing several products you can weave pendants to order. After all, the acquired experience and knowledge will be able to help you in the manufacture of more complex and expensive models.



Finish weaving the product

Even before the first steps of the product, decide on the clasps and "ears" for hanging the pendant. After all, these elements simply need to be fixed at the beginning and end of weaving. There are various types of carabiners, clasps and other methods of connecting the ends of the product. Also prepare the elements on which you hang your pendant from beads. The types of such methods are also different and depend only on the chosen accessory execution scheme. Try, experiment and don't be afraid - you will surely succeed.
