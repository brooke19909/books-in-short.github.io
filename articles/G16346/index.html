The United Arab Emirates is a terrific country, in which all tourists strive to get. In our article we want to talk about one of the beautiful hotels of the famous island of Palm Jumeirah - Amwaj Rotana Jumeirah Beach Residence.

A bit about the hotel

Amwaj Rotana Jumeirah Beach Residence was built on an island of man-made origin on a busy promenade called Jumeirah Beach. The hotel is made in an original style, in its architecture, the influence of Balinese and Thai culture is noticeable. Palm Jumeirah is considered the heart of the new Dubai.

The complex is located just a few minutes walk from the coast on the famous promenade Walk, along which there are restaurants and boutiques. Amwaj Rotana Jumeirah Beach Residence was built in 2010 and is part of the Rotana chain of establishments. It provides its guests with a high level of service and offers accommodation in beautiful rooms. The hotel complex, located on the coastline, across the road from the famous Persian Gulf, is very popular among tourists who prefer to relax in the best hotels.





The hotel is just thirty kilometers from Dubai International Airport, and a five-minute drive from Media City as well as Internet City.
