What is speedcubing?

Fans of the game organize real competitions in cube assembly speed. Special speed puzzles are on sale. The rotation mechanism of such Rubik's cubes is very high-quality, and the turns of faces and rows can be made with the movement of one finger.

The World Cube Association (WCA) is a non-profit organization supporting the speedcubing movement. The WCA regularly hosts competitions around the world. In almost all countries there are representatives of the organization. Anyone can become a participant in a speedcubing event, you just need to register on the site and meet the assembly standards. The most popular discipline in such competitions is the high-speed assembly of the Rubik's Cube 3x3. Designations of schemes and methods of rotation of the cube

Before you begin to disassemble the formulas of the Rubik's Cube 3x3, it is worth learning the notation used in speedcubing. All puzzle movements are indicated by capital letters. The absence of an apostrophe above the symbol means that the rotation is carried out clockwise, if there is a sign, then it should be rotated in the opposite direction.

The first letters of English (or Russian) words denoting movements are considered generally accepted:

  • front - F or - rotation of the front side;
  • back - B or T - rotation of the back side;
  • left - L or L - rotation of the left row;
  • right - R or P - rotation of the right row;
  • up - U or B - rotation of the upper row;
  • down -D or H - rotation of the bottom row.

Pointers can also be used to change the position of the cube in space - the interception movement. Everything is simple here, too, from the school course of geometry, the coordinate axes X, Y and Z are known to everyone. The X movement means that the cube must be rotated by the F face to the place of the U face, when Y - F is shifted, it should be replaced by L, and when Z is rotated - F moves to R.

The following group of notations is rarely used; it is used in drawing up patterns of patterns:

  • M - the rotation of the middle row, between the right (R / P) and left (L / L);
  • S - rotation of the middle row, between the front (F / F) and the rear (B / T);
  • E - rotation of the middle row, between the upper (U / B) and lower (D / H).

3x3 Rubik's Cube Build Algorithm for Beginners

Although there are many ways to solve the puzzle, simple and understandable schemes for beginners are not so easy to find. With each passing step of the assembly, the formulas of a 3x3 Rubik's Cube become more complicated. It is necessary not only to change the pattern correctly, but also to preserve what has been done before. Below will be one of the options for how easy it is to assemble a 3x3 Rubik's Cube.

Conventionally, the whole process can be divided into the following stages:

  1. The assembly of the cross in the upper face of the cube.
  2. The correct preparation of the entire upper face.
  3. Work on the middle layers.
  4. Proper assembly of the ribs of the last row.
  5. Assembling the cross of the lower bound.
  6. Correct orientation of the corners of the last face of the cube.
