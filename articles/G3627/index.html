Luxury Hotel Kaya Palazzo Resort

Turkey is a welcome country for the rest of many Russians. Due to the rich history of the country and the presence of delightful ancient monuments, beaches and bays, more and more people go there every year. To enjoy the stunning beauties of nature, to see the sights and relax in this charming country with your family to the fullest, you should choose the right hotel.

Turkey offers a variety of hotels for all categories of tourists. The main indicator of the hotel is the number of stars on which the level of service depends. So, the five-star Kaya Palazzo Resort hotel will be an ideal option for tourists who want to luxuriously relax in Turkey.



Description

The luxurious hotel complex Kaya Palazzo Resort, built this year, is located on the beach, 2.5 km from the center of the small tourist village of Kadriye near Belek. The hotel for tourists accustomed to luxury is located 27 kilometers from Antalya International Airport. Moreover, the distance to Antalya from the hotel is 35 km. A pleasant atmosphere, luxury rooms, a high level of service and a picturesque beach will leave a lot of pleasant impressions and provide a great vacation.

Service: rooms, food, services



In total, the hotel has 633 rooms, each with a hot shower, split air conditioning, tea and coffee, a terrace, a hairdryer, a balcony with great views of the beach, a telephone (extra charge), an LCD TV, and a bathtub and change of bed linen.

Kaya Palazzo Resort features an all-inclusive meal plan. Vacationers are offered breakfast, continental breakfast, after vacationers will provide lunch and dinner - buffet. At the request of tourists, you can purchase bottled drinks for a fee.

Since the Kaya Palazzo Golf Resort is located directly on the sea, it has its own private beach, which is 200 meters long. On the beach and at the pools, tourists are provided with a couple of towels free of charge, as well as umbrellas and sun loungers.

Entertainment

The hotel complex Kaya Palazzo Resort for vacationers hosts discos, parties and night shows. Sports enthusiasts can visit the mini-golf, indoor soccer, tennis court, diving and kite surfing.
