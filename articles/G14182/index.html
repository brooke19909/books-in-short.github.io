Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey): description and traveler reviews

Turkey, in particular Side, is a place that receives thousands of tourists from all over the world every year. After all, here travelers are waiting for miles of luxurious sandy beaches, comfortable hotels and, of course, a lot of entertainment. One of the popular vacation spots is considered a large hotel complex Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 *. This place is ideal for almost any type of vacation.

Features of location and infrastructure





Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 * is a large complex that can accommodate more than a thousand travelers. It occupies a huge area and consists of several multi-storey buildings. The territory here is beautiful and well-groomed - there are terraces for relaxation, sports grounds, swimming pools, secluded arbors, in short, everything for a pleasant pastime.

So where is the Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 * located? Evrenseki - a small resort area near the city of Side - it was here, a few meters from the sea, that the hotel was built. By the way, the distance to the center of Side is only 8 km. The nearest airport is in the equally famous city called Antalya - the distance is about 60 km.

Hotel complex Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Evrenseki): what do the rooms look like?





This is a large hotel complex, which consists of 540 rooms. These are both standard rooms and rooms with a high level of comfort. There are family rooms, which consist of two rooms. Almost all have their own spacious balcony or secluded terrace.

The rooms are decorated in a modern style, there is the necessary set of furniture and household appliances. It includes a plasma-screen TV, telephone, safe, radio, and a mini fridge. Guests can use the air conditioning. The bathrooms are equipped with modern fixtures - in addition to the shower, toilet and wash basin, there is a hair dryer, a set of soft clean towels, as well as cosmetics, including soap, shampoos. Rooms in Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 * are cleaned daily. Maids change bedding several times a week.

Travel Scheme





Primasol Hane Family Resort hotel, as well as almost all Turkish hotels, operates on a convenient all-inclusive system. Here, the price includes three meals a day, which are held in two main hotel restaurants. According to reviews, the choice of dishes is huge, and the masterpieces of Turkish and international cuisine are presented on the menu. You can also have a bite to eat in one of the many bars. By the way, local drinks are free for tourists.

The hotel has a Turkish and Italian a la carte restaurant where you can dine in a relaxed atmosphere. True, tables need to be booked in advance. It is also worth a glimpse into the hotel confectionery, where guests are offered fresh pastries, homemade ice cream and delicious desserts.

Where is the beach located? Entertainment and facilities





Of course, most tourists come to Turkey in search of a beach holiday. What does the Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 * offer its customers? The hotel has its own private stretch of coast, to which the territory directly opens. The beach is clean, cleaned here every day, and the entrance to the sea is quite convenient and cleared. By the way, this site is intended for recreation only for hotel guests. You can count on sunbeds with soft mattresses, umbrellas and large beach towels - these services are free.

Of course, tourists can count on all possible entertainment, including beach volleyball, water skiing, scuba diving, windsurfing and sailing.

Hotel Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 * (Evrenseki): entertainment and additional services





This hotel complex provides its guests with the maximum number of amenities and entertainment. Currency exchange works here. Clothes can be taken to the laundry or dry cleaning service. Ample parking is available on site. Almost every corner of the hotel has internet access. A good business center and three conference rooms are also equipped for travelers. Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 * hotel offers services for the organization of business and holiday events.

Of course, guests are not bored. Throughout the day, they are entertained by a group of animators, and in the evenings everyone is invited to bright shows and a disco. By the way, sports are regularly held here (morning gymnastics, aerobics, water aerobics), as well as dance lessons for everyone.

There are 10 pools on the territory (two of them are for children), as well as water slides. You can play tennis, try archery, and have fun on the mini-golf course. At your service is a shopping center (prices are quite reasonable), as well as a medical office and a spacious gym. There are trampolines (both for adults and for children), as well as tables for ping-pong and billiards. It is worth visiting the TV center, where guests are offered a large selection of masterpieces of world cinema. A large playground is equipped for children, and a mini club is open daily. The hotel is actually designed for family vacations.

How do guests respond?

What do tourists themselves say about the Primasol Hane Family Resort Hotel 5 *? The reviews are positive - travelers really have a good time here. Naturally, a pleasant fact is the presence of a spacious territory, comfort inside the buildings, comfortable rooms and the presence of the necessary equipment. The food is at a very high level - the dishes are always fresh and there is plenty to choose from. The hotel is perfect for family vacations, as children here are offered the most eventful leisure and high-quality, professional care of teachers. Do not forget about the sea, because the presence of a private beach, and even so close is an undoubted advantage.
