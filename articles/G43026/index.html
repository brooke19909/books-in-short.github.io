Hotel Memory Boutique 4 * (Greece / Crete): photos and reviews of tourists

Many tourists come to Greece in search of outdoor activities. Travelers explore cities on their own, visit excursions and explore historical sites. They usually need an inexpensive hotel for an overnight stay with comfortable rooms and a minimum of services offered. Arriving in Hersonissos , they often stay at the Memory Boutique 4 * hotel. What can she offer her guests?

Hotel location

The hotel is located in the center of Hersonissos , which is considered the most lively resort in Crete. Next to it is a busy street, built specifically for the needs of tourists. Shops, souvenir shops, restaurants, cafes, Greek taverns and bars are open everywhere. This city is suitable for young people who love active nightlife. Families with children, by contrast, are unlikely to appreciate this bustling resort. The city beach is separated from the hotel Memory Boutique 4 * (Crete) by a small road. It is located 50 meters from the building, so you can walk to the sea in just 5-10 minutes.

The international airport is located near Heraklion and is 25 km from the hotel. You can get to it by taxi. Tourists who want to save money can first take a bus to Heraklion , and then transfer to the route going to Hersonissos . Public transport is 40 meters from the hotel. It can be used by guests traveling independently on the island. The main attraction of Crete, Knossos Palace of King Minos , will have to be overcome 27 km. The oceanarium is 400 meters from the hotel. For golf lovers, a special club is 5 km away.

Important information

Hotel Memory Boutique Hotel 4 * was opened in 1986. It consists of two small buildings, which are separated by a road. The main building, in which the reception is located, has four floors, and an additional - three. In total, the complex offers guests 38 modern rooms that are designed for families or large friendly companies. The hotel accepts children of any age, offering guests a discount on their stay. The local staff speaks many languages, including Russian and English. Pets are not allowed with pets of any size. In early 2016, the hotel renovated public spaces.





Arriving at the hotel, it is worth knowing that check-in here begins at 14:00. Tourists who have arrived earlier can leave their things at the reception and go to the beach. Registration runs until 21:00. You need to leave your rooms after a vacation before noon. Additional services can be paid by credit card, Maestro, MasterCard or Visa. In the evenings, the hotel has a dress code . Guests are advised not to wear beachwear at this time.

Housing stock

Both buildings of the hotel offer tourists 38 rooms. Their complete renovation was carried out here in 2010. The design of the apartment is classic. The standard rooms are 16 square meters. From their windows a partial view of the Aegean Sea, as well as the nearby buildings. The rooms have a double bed. On request, the hotel Memory Boutique 4 * (Hersonissos) can provide an extra bed in the room. Cots are available for babies, and rollaway beds for adults. There are family apartments in the hotel, which can accommodate four adults.





All rooms have a private balcony and a bathroom. All apartments have laminate flooring. Hotel staff tidy up all accommodation daily. Towels and bed linen they change 3 times a week. All rooms have good soundproofing, so tourists will not hear their neighbors or loud music from the street.

Room features

Many guests stay in a hotel just to spend the night. Therefore, they make high demands on the equipment of rooms. Being a four-star hotel, Memory Boutique 4 * has prepared for its guests a lot of amenities to make living here more comfortable. So, tourists can leave their things in a mechanical safe, which is available for a fee. It locks with a key. For his loss, a substantial fine is imposed. Upon arrival, all guests receive a set of bath accessories, which is then replenished in a timely manner. Clothes can be stored in the closet and bedside tables. An iron and an ironing board will help to put things in order. They are issued by the hotel administrator upon request.





All rooms are equipped with an individual split system . With its help, tourists can control the temperature in the living room. A wired telephone will help you contact the administrator. For a fee, you can call from him to other cities and countries. There is a small TV in front of the bed. Satellite TV channels are connected to it, among which there are several Russians. Free Wi-Fi and radio are provided. The room has a mini fridge for storing food and drinks .

Dining at the hotel

When making a tour, travelers should choose one of the food concepts. In the hotel Memory Boutique 4 * ( Heraklion , Crete) there are two of them: half board and all inclusive. The first concept offers vacationers breakfast and dinner. It will be an ideal option for guests spending all their time in the city. All-inclusive, on the contrary, is suitable for tourists who are constantly in the hotel. By paying for this system, you will receive breakfast, lunch and dinner. They are accompanied by master classes and cooking shows from the hotel chefs. Themed evenings are dedicated to Cretan cuisine, vegetarian and organic nutrition.





All meals are served in the hotel‚Äôs main restaurant. The dishes are usually served red or white wine, mineral water, juices, draft beer. From 20:00, the main bar offers strong alcoholic drinks (rum, vodka, brandy, liquor, whiskey and cocktails). The lobby bar serves pizza, sandwiches, desserts, fruits and ice creams throughout the day. Instant coffee, hot chocolate and tea are also served here. Refreshing drinks and cocktails are available at the poolside bar. It is worth knowing that imported alcohol and freshly squeezed juices are offered to guests only at an additional cost.

Service and Facilities

Arriving at the Memory Boutique 4 * hotel, you should not count on luxurious service. At the same time, the hotel provides tourists with all the services necessary for a quality vacation. Around the clock, guests can contact the administrator behind the reception. He will help you connect Wi-Fi , exchange currency, deliver things to the laundry. Here you can leave large bags and luggage in a spacious left-luggage office. For a fee, tourists are offered fax and photocopying services.





Near the hotel there is a small private parking designed for guests. Seats are provided free of charge. At 500 meters from the hotel there is an ATM, where tourists can withdraw money from a credit card around the clock. The main building has ramps and an elevator, so people with disabilities can stay here.

Leisure activities

In fine weather, a small freshwater pool is open for guests. Next to it there is a wooden sun terrace with free sunbeds. On the ground floor there is a cozy lounge equipped with a TV and air conditioning. Other entertainment Memory Boutique 4 * does not provide, because most tourists usually relax outside the hotel. The beach is a 5-minute walk away. For a small fee, you can buy a deck chair and rent a sun umbrella. The entertainment center offers tourists equipment for water sports. There is a playground for children near the hotel. There are several types of swings, slides and a sandbox on it. In addition, tourists can visit the large water park, which is 3 km from the hotel.





Positive reviews

Modern tourists, before issuing a voucher, always read the opinions of previous guests who stayed at the Memory Boutique 4 * hotel. Reviews about it leave mostly good. Guests rated the following facilities at this hotel:

  • Cozy rooms, the interior of which creates a homely atmosphere. The furniture in the rooms is in good condition.
  • Good location. Within walking distance is the beach, a shopping street with shops, nightclubs, bars and cafes.
  • Nice staff. The hostess of the hotel speaks good Russian. Administrators will always help to solve problems or even replace the room at the request of tourists.
  • Beach towels can be taken free of charge at the reception.
  • Internet works well on the ground floor.
  • Maids will not steal valuables, even if they are not left in a safe.
  • Tourists rarely swim in the pool, so in hot weather you can cool off and swim freely.




Negative reviews

Often the hotel Memory Boutique 4 * (Crete) receives negative reviews. Tourists in their comments describe serious shortcomings that can ruin the impression of a vacation. We list the main ones:

  • Poor soundproofing in the rooms. At night, even with the windows closed, loud music is heard that plays in a nearby night club.
  • It is impossible to drink drinking water from coolers. It smells like chemical impurities and bleach.
  • A small shower in the bathroom, in which it will be very uncomfortable to wash full and tall people. In addition, water constantly spills onto the floor.
  • Monotonous food in the restaurant. They do not serve fish, and the assortment of fruits is small. Hot dishes are heavily salted.
  • An unpleasant man works in the bar, who treats the guests scornfully.
  • Wi-Fi does not catch in the room, and in public places it works intermittently.
  • Bad housekeeping. Maids do not make beds or replenish toiletries.

Who is this hotel suitable for?

Hotel Memory Boutique 4 * (Crete), reviews and photos of which can be seen above, is perfect for youth and student holidays. Often travelers are attracted by the low cost of living and the high quality of the services offered. On the contrary, it is not recommended to settle here with young children. Soundproof windows do not always save from noise on the street, so kids often experience sleep problems here.
