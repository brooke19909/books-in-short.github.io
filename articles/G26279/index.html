Location

Hotel Half Moon 3 * (Rimini) is located almost in the center of the resort in the Rivazzura area on the second line. Since the hotel is located just 150 meters from beach number 108, it can be reached in just 3-5 minutes on foot. This is a pretty modern hotel, although it was built in the 90s of the last century. Wireless internet is available throughout the hotel, which is important for tourists from different countries. The hotel building is a five-story building with an elevator, with balconies and a summer terrace. Here you will not see any excesses, no scenery. The modern design of the hotel is modest and even, at first glance, a little strict for the resort hotel. But the service is up to standard here. You will be greeted immediately at the entrance with a friendly smile, register immediately and check into the desired room, in which you will find everything you need for temporary residence. This hotel is aimed at all categories of vacationers, both adults and children. However, young people come here most often. The biggest plus for boys and girls is the proximity of the hotel to the famous disco throughout Rimini.

Hotel Interior

According to the rules established inside the hotel, check-in is from 14 hours and check-out is until 10 hours. Children under 3 years stay free of charge. Pets are not allowed in the hotel, international credit cards are accepted when paying. The hotel does not have a lobby, but there is a lounge with a seating area next to the reception. Another lounge area is in the hotel courtyard.



Number of rooms of the hotel Half Moon (Rivazzurra) 3 *

The five-story building of the hotel accommodates only 36 standard rooms for up to 4 people. Each room has air conditioning, heating, a bathroom (toilet, bidet, shower, mirror, hair dryer, hygiene products and towels), wardrobe, beds, a large mirror, a chair and a table, a mini-safe. As you can see, there is not much furniture here, but the modest size of the room does not imply anything superfluous. But cleaning, towel change and the provision of hygiene products are daily, and bed linen is changed twice a week. In addition, the rooms have a TV and satellite channels, as well as a telephone. Balconies, although small, but they are in each of the rooms.

Nutrition

Each of the guests can choose for themselves an individual type of food. This can be type BB, that is, accommodation + breakfast, VN (accommodation + breakfast + dinner) or OI (all inclusive). However, most tourists choose the first option, since the evenings at the resort can be diversified by visiting various restaurants and trying different dishes of the famous Italian cuisine. By the way, in the reviews of Russian tourists you can see complaints about a meager breakfast and a monotonous dinner at the Half Moon 3 * hotel. Spoiled by Turkish hospitality, Russian tourists, of course, notice a lot of minuses in Western European hotels of this level. However, here it is considered the norm, and if this state of affairs does not suit you, then you need to choose a 4 * hotel, which, incidentally, will cost an order of magnitude higher. Therefore, you either have to put up with it, or not spare money on your vacation.



Beach

For guests of resort hotels, of course, the most important is the condition of the nearest beach, because tourists spend most of their time outside the walls of the hotel, on the beach or on excursions. As noted above, the Half Moon Hotel is 150 meters from the shoreline. This is a wonderful, well-equipped beach with golden sand. Beach equipment must be rented for a fee set by the administration. For guests of the hotel who are resting on an all-inclusive basis, umbrellas and sun loungers are provided free of charge, and for a single room 1 umbrella + 1 sun lounger, for a double room - 1 umbrella and 2 sun loungers. On the beach, tourists will find a wide range of water activities, from beach volleyball to water skiing.



Excursions

The city of Rimini is an open-air museum. There are a lot of historical attractions, for example: ancient palaces in Cavour Square, which are real architectural masterpieces; the triumphal arch of Emperor Augustus (1st century BC); Gothic temple of Malatesta (15th century); Municipal Museum of Rimini; the ancient Roman amphitheater (2nd century BC) and others. Not far from Rimini is the smallest state in the world of San Marino. If desired, tourists can go there by bus and explore this wonderful principality.

Conclusion

Rimini is a wonderful resort on the Adriatic. Whichever of the local hotels tourists choose to relax, they will certainly be satisfied. The sea, beaches, attractions - everything here has a wonderful pastime and relaxation.
