5 Star hotels - Top Level Hurghada Holidays

Hurghada. This oriental tale is located on the shores of the purest and most beautiful Red Sea. Mild climatic conditions allow you to enjoy a holiday by the sea at any time of the year. Hurghada is an ideal place for diving and surfing. Amazing underwater landscapes delight with its beauty. Known as an entertainment center, Hurghada offers vacationers numerous 5 star hotels, famous all over the world. A good stay is more dependent on the quality of the hotel. The best hotels are located on the coastline. They have their own beaches where tourists enjoy the sea without going outside the hotel. If you are going to visit Egypt, Hurghada 5 * hotels will hospitably welcome you, offering a full range of entertainment. Some of them can be noted as the best hotels.  



Beach Albatros Hurg.

Located on the seashore, 7 km from the airport. The complex of one four-story building and several three-story, contains on its territory three restaurants, two outdoor pools, a beauty salon, a photo salon, an exchange office and other places of recreation and entertainment.

Tourists will be offered free of charge: evening shows - programs, volleyball and aerobics classes on the beach.

For a fee: two tennis courts with racket rental, horseback riding, minigolf, diving courses, babysitting services, discos, basketball, soccer, windsurfing, sea fishing boats and much more.

For lovers of beach holidays, Beach Albatros Hurg will offer an excellent beach with free provision of umbrellas, sun loungers, towels.

The rooms of this hotel are equipped with air conditioning, minibar, telephone, TV. Each room has a toilet and a bathroom with all toiletries. Cleaning is done daily.

Meals - buffet.

The disadvantages of the hotel include rather weak animation and outdated rooms.


Ali Baba Palace.

The hotel is located on the coast, 13 km from the airport. On the territory: two-story buildings for accommodation, two restaurants, four bars, three pools. In addition, visitors are provided with shops, a conference room, discos, a fitness center, a kids club, a hairdresser, car rental, a children's playground and much more.

Free guests will be offered: a sauna, a Turkish bath, table tennis, a jacuzzi, minigolf, a tennis court, entertainment programs and more.

Like other 5 star hotels, Ali Baba Palace will offer tourists paid services: massage, scuba diving courses, boats for sea fishing, rental fins, masks and equipment necessary for scuba diving.

On the beach of Ali Baba Palace, sun loungers, towels and mattresses are provided free of charge.

The rooms have a toilet, bathroom with toiletries, telephone, satellite TV, refrigerator and air conditioning.

Power is all inclusive.

The hotel has its drawbacks. The furniture in half of the rooms is old, the staff is slow and not very clean. The kitchen does not always have variety.


Golden Five.

Five-story building, located 16 km from the airport. The hotel has two restaurants, three bars, a coffee shop, two swimming pools, a shopping center, a conference room, a cable car, a musical fountain, laundry and more.

For free, like other 5 star hotels, Golden Five will offer entertainment programs, a kids club, soccer, volleyball, basketball, aerobics, live music.

For a fee: Jacuzzi, sauna, bathhouse, "Octopus" disco, rental equipment needed for scuba diving, fins and masks, windsurfing school, gym, horse riding, tennis courts, mini-golf table tennis ,.

Towels, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided free of charge on the beach at Golden Five Hotel.

The rooms have a terrace, telephone, TV, toilet, bathroom with toiletries, air conditioning. Room cleaning is done daily.

The problems of the hotel are standard: not always kind staff, outdated rooms, difficulties with checking into a good room. The bottom on the beach is poorly cleared, you can get hurt.


During a trip to Egypt, Hurghada, the 5 star hotels listed above are very popular among tourists from Russia and other countries. In them you can relax with children, in the company of friends, couples or alone. Good service, lots of entertainment and excursions will make your stay unforgettable.
