How to make a fish out of paper: a master class

One of the most exciting activities is the creation of small masterpieces from the simplest materials, such as paper. In particular, children like it when something unusual and interesting comes out of something ordinary. How to make a fish out of paper? Detailed instructions will help you deal with this easily.





The easiest fish

To begin with, we bend a square piece of paper diagonally on both sides and in half horizontally. Then you need to iron the folds well and deploy them back. After that, we connect the points along the edges of the horizontal bend so that we get a triangle.





Now hold the bottom left corner of the triangle and fold it towards the center along the diagonal fold. Do the same with a different angle - we got a fish tail. Make and glue the eyes, draw a mouth - and that‚Äôs it, the inhabitant of the deep sea is ready.





This is probably the simplest instruction that will teach you how to make a fish out of paper with your own hands even the smallest. An incredibly cute little project is ideal for spending leisure time together with your children, and by making several fish of different color and size, you can even create a good applique to decorate the walls in the children's room.

Making bulk fish out of paper

For work you will need: one piece of thick plain paper, one piece of thick paper in a different color, white paper (for eyes), scissors, glue or adhesive tape and a marker.





Steps:

1. Take a piece of paper.

2. Bend the bottom right corner up to make a triangle.

3. Trim the excess.

4. Fold the sheet diagonally so that the crease extends from the upper left to the lower right.

5. Fold the bottom left corner to the center.

6. Bend the upper right corner to the center.

7. Iron the folds well and unfold the sheet.





8. Fold the square along the central diagonal crease.

9. Take the scissors and make a few cuts (about 20) without crossing the two side folds.

10. Expand the square.

11. Take the glue stick and spread one of the side triangles to the fold.

12. Glue the two sides together by laying two triangles on top of each other.





To get the head of the fish, you need to cut off the lower left corner. Use contrasting colors to cut the fins and tail. Decorate them with stripes using a marker. Take the white paper and cut out the eyes. Glue them on the head of the fish, after drawing the pupils with a marker.





Glue the tail and fins.

It was a master class on how to make a goldfish out of paper in a voluminous version. It can be used for children's games, and you can also decorate the room with it.

Sophisticated Origami Fish

To try yourself in more complex things, it is better to first learn how to make a fish, airplane, boat in simpler variations from paper. Initial success will stimulate further training in paper art.





Having trained well, you can learn how to make a fish out of paper with more complex elements - fins and tail. Numerous manipulations to get enough wrinkles take time and patience.





And only industriousness, perseverance and readiness for the fact that not everything will immediately turn out smoothly and accurately will lead to a brilliant result. Only then will a real miracle happen: a simple sheet of paper turns into something fabulous and incredible.





Origami is a real art.
