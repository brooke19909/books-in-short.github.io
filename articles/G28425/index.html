Details on how to sew zippers in trousers

Today we will tell you how to sew a zipper in trousers. Sometimes this item needs to be replaced. It is also possible that you sew trousers yourself, and the next step in the work involves sewing on the zipper. We will tell you how the easiest way to cope with the task. The following is an algorithm that should facilitate your work.

New model

Now we will tell you how to sew zippers in pants correctly if you create this garment yourself. Before you begin, you need to prepare. Let's start with a careful selection of lightning. It can match the color of the trousers or, conversely, contrast with them. The final choice depends on the purpose of the trousers and their style.





Preparation is completed, we pass to the practical part. To solve the question of how to sew zippers in trousers, we make an allowance along their midline. It should be equal to 1 cm.

  • Next, we pin the zipper with pins. Her teeth should come close to the fabric and not reach the top by about 4 mm.
  • We fit the trim part of the codpiece under the zipper.
  • Then it must be noticed.
  • Sew the zipper along with the bottom part.
  • Remove the pins.
  • At the next stage, you need to notice the lightning on the back of the codpiece and stitch it on a typewriter.
  • We mark out the element of interest to us.
  • We build off the codpiece on the created line. The work is completed.

Second-hand

Now we will consider how to sew a zipper in trousers if they have been used for quite some time. In this case, the order of work is slightly different.





  • Unfasten the old snake and remove the threads.
  • Try on a new zipper. It should be similar to the previous one.
  • We sweep along the old seam our lightning from the side of the gap.
  • We sew the product on a typewriter. We strictly adhere to the old seam and observe a distance of 2 millimeters from the edge of the fabric. We use threads close in shade to the original ones. Otherwise, the seams will be very noticeable.
  • Next, you need to unzip and grind from the codpiece.
  • Sew on. We sew from the front side all the seams that were previously on this element.
  • Remove all pins.
  • Sew on the belt at the stripping places.
  • We iron all seams with an iron.

Hidden element

Now we will tell you how to sew a zipper in trousers, if it is secret. In this case, the element of interest to us, as a rule, is located at the back or side. This design fastener can improve the appearance of trousers. Before stitching a zipper, it is necessary to stitch it.





  • Next, you should overcast the edges and iron. Moreover, all the steps described must be performed so that the cut for the zipper becomes a continuation of the seam.
  • Turn the trousers inside out. Open the zipper. We apply the tape on one side, in accordance with the allowance for the snake. It is important that the edges of the clasps of the fastener are located at the level of the ironed edge of the cut. This solution will allow you to completely hide the zipper in the buttoned form.
  • Then you need to catch the fastener.
  • At the next stage, we fix the beginning, as well as the end of the line. In this case, the seam should not shift.
  • We repeat similar actions from the opposite side. Care must be taken not to stretch the fabric under the zipper. Otherwise, the finished seam will be with tubercles and uneven.
  • We turn out the trousers. We fasten the zipper. We evaluate its appearance. If there are defects or irregularities, it is necessary to correct them. After stitching, they will not disappear anywhere, and it will be more difficult to fix them.
  • We attach both edges of the zipper with a special foot for the machine. In the absence of this tool, work can also be done. However, in the latter case, it is necessary to ensure that the seam does not leave the previously planned line.

As you can see, everything is quite simple, but the main thing in such work is patience and accuracy.

Pants for the fair sex

Next, we will tell you how to sew a zipper in women's pants. Actually, this action is not much different from the above algorithm, but still pay special attention to it.





Procedure

We translate the line of the center of the codpiece to the part from the pattern. Go to the next step.

  • On the right side of the trousers we glue the codpiece with non-woven or doublerin.
  • Mark the center line. This must be done on the left and right sides of the trousers.
  • Center lines must be flashed with contrasting thread.
  • Sew the middle of the trousers. The reference point in this case is the beginning of the codpiece.
  • Iron the seam.
  • The glued codpiece on the right side of the trousers is folded to the wrong side along the swept line. We invite you.
  • We cut the codpiece on the left side of the trousers. Leave an allowance of 1.5 centimeters from the center line.
  • On the left side of the trousers we add the cropped codpiece.
  • We retreat from the center line of 4 mm.
  • On the wrong side we wrap everything else. We invite you.
  • Next, take the lightning. We pin it on the left side of the codpiece, close to the edge of the bend.

Now you know how to sew zippers in trousers.
