Five reasons to visit Orenburg





Orenburg is an amazing city that has always been famous for its breathtaking architecture. On its territory you can see a huge number of amazing monuments. There are five main reasons why you need to visit this place.

  1. Walk along the old bridge. The main attraction of Orenburg is the pedestrian bridge that passes through the Ural River. According to historical data, it was built back in 1800, there is a legend that the famous Russian writer Vladimir Dal also participated in its creation.
  2. Buy a souvenir in a trading shop located on the territory of the Gostiny Dvor. This is a real value for the city, which every tourist wants to visit. The vast shopping complex still retains the design of the pre-war era, despite the fact that it is periodically restored.
  3. Enjoy the sound of chimes on Sovetskaya Street. This area has its own remote, unlike other places atmosphere. It is always calm here, I want to forget about all the fuss and stop for a while in time. It is believed that the bells were made in the second half of the twentieth century. The tower itself was built quite recently, its height reaches 35 meters.
  4. Take a ride on the children's railway. This attraction should be visited by every tourist who comes to this city with a child. Here, every detail is similar to real.
  5. The most beautiful place in this area is the Lenin Square. On its territory you can see many ancient attractions. This place got its name due to the presence in it of a monument built in honor of Vladimir Ilyich.

In addition, it is worth visiting a wonderful city in order to walk along the beautiful streets, parks and the promenade. Each building will surprise with its unique appearance.
