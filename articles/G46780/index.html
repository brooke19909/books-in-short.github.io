Neptune and Ilmen are famous Miass hotels. Infrastructure, service and visitor reviews

In the south of the Urals, in the center of the Chelyabinsk region, is the city of Miass. Picturesque mountains, protected lakes and forests, a unique Ilmensky mineralogical reserve, a ski resort attract numerous admirers of nature or sports recreation, tourists to these places.





Miass city: how to get there?

Miass has a bus station and a train station. They provide transport links with cities - Moscow, Novosibirsk, Tyumen, Samara, Belgorod, Ufa, Anapa, Chelyabinsk, Yekaterinburg and others. A few kilometers from the regional center of Chelyabinsk there is an international airport, which organizes the reception of flights from major cities of Russia and from abroad.

Local hotels

Miass is a small city. Before visiting, you must take care of booking a hotel room. Many hotels offer their services for accommodation of tourists. Miass has become a sought after destination for travelers. Therefore, it is necessary to choose comfortable living conditions for visitors in advance. In the city and beyond, guests are waiting for their guests, hostels, holiday homes, there are different levels of hotels. Miass offers its guests various services and living conditions. The price for them is set in the range from 600 to 9000 rubles per day, depending on the stardom of the institution. Learn about the advantages or disadvantages of accommodation will allow reviews of guests who visited local hotels. Miass often becomes the object of descriptions on popular tourist sites.

Hotel Neptune

In the Mashgorodok area is the Neptune Hotel. Miass landscapes, the most beautiful lake Turgoyak, the Ural Mountains - these beautiful views from the windows of the hotel often attract guests here.





The hotel has 115 rooms - from economy class to luxury rooms. Guests are offered single, double or triple occupancy. In luxury and standard rooms it is possible to buy extra beds. All apartments have their own bathroom, necessary furniture, TV and refrigerator, free Wi-Fi. Laundry facilities are also available.

The choice of guests is offered two rates of payment for accommodation - standard and half board. The standard includes a continental breakfast in the hotel cafe, half board involves paid breakfasts and dinners in the restaurant.

The restaurant of the Neptune Hotel is decorated in a marine style. The menu contains recipes of the Ural cuisine and dishes recognized around the world. Hotel guests can order food from the restaurant to the room. It features a cozy cafe with a fireplace and a bar.

A visit to the sauna or Turkish hammam will allow you to relax and relax. Guests leave positive reviews for the Neptune Hotel, admiring the cleanliness of the rooms and the helpful staff.

Reconstruction was made to ensure a pleasant stay; today, 44 rooms of the hotel meet business class standards. Also for accommodation are standard and deluxe rooms. They are pleasantly decorated, have comfortable furniture. Household appliances (refrigerator and TV), a bathroom with shower cabins are available in each room.
