Menorca. Reviews of sights and places of interest on the island

There are many interesting countries in the world, each of which has its own uniqueness and originality. Spain is ready to offer a huge number of attractive places to tourists . Menorca Island is exactly the part of the country that is recommended to be visited. Despite the fact that Menorca is not as popular as the neighboring islands of Ibiza and Mallorca, there is something to see. In addition, this island is an ideal place for those who prefer a calm and measured rest. The tourist service here is limited to four recreation areas. However, lovers of exotic and archaeological sites should come here.



Menorca is a beautiful island located in the northeast of the Balearic Islands. It is considered the territory of Spain. Its population does not exceed 80,000 people. The most significant sights are beaches. Thanks to them, tourists learned about such an island as Menorca. Responses of vacationers show that there are more beaches here than on all the Balearic Islands. Their total length is 217 kilometers. In addition, there are many places that are of great interest to lovers of history and culture. The beauty of the entire territory of Menorca can be observed from Mount Monte Toro, which is the highest point of the island.



In the northern part of the island there is a steep coast and small beaches with pebbled reddish-sandy coating. As for the southern coast, there are numerous ravines on it. It should also be noted that diving enthusiasts go specifically to the island of Menorca. Reviews show that all the interesting places here will be able to see during the first vacation. The architecture of the island can be divided into two types. The first includes white houses. They are typical for the Mediterranean buildings. The second type is buildings built in the English style. They indicate the various stages of the presence of the British in these places. In addition, there are many archaeological sites in Menorca that make the island an open-air museum.



Particular attention should be paid to the remains of civilization, which lived here in the second millennium BC. The covets, tauls, and talayotes are megalithic stone buildings. If you single out the largest cluster of prehistoric monuments that Menorca is famous for, reviews of tourists show that it will be Naveta des Tudons. It is here that you can see the large funeral structure of the island, the so-called "libel", the shape of which resembles a ship turned upside down, its height corresponds to the level of a two-story house.

In the southern part of the island there is an equally famous ancient attraction - the prehistoric village of Torre d'en Gaumes. Three stone towers rise here - the Talayots, surrounded on all sides by defensive walls. Of great interest is the construction of Taula - a kind of temple, the building of which is built in the shape of the letter "t". And this is not all the ancient places attracting tourists, which Menorca is famous for. Reviews show that the dolmen funeral room is of interest to guests of the island.



With regard to buildings related to newer history, cities such as Mahon and Ciudadella should be visited. The first is the capital of the island and the most important commercial center. The main attraction of the city is the natural harbor. Moreover, it is the best natural port of the Mediterranean. The list of interesting places should be supplemented with such structures as a house overlooking the harbor, the Church of St. Mary, whose main value is a huge organ consisting of 3,000 pipes. It is recommended to visit the distillery, where a fine gin is produced using old technology. But this is not the only city that the island of Menorca offers for visiting guests. Reviews show that the city of Ciudadella is no less interesting. It is completely different from the capital. Gothic and Baroque buildings, mansions and palaces built in the 18th century prevail here.

And on the east coast of Menorca you can visit nature reserves, a walk along which will leave a lot of unforgettable impressions.
