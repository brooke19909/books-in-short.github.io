The recreation center "Berezhok" (Kazan): reviews, photos

The recreation center "Berezhok" (Kazan) is one of the few places that have preserved the beauty and virginity of nature. Pine forest, so beloved by Russian people, gives clean air, and the aroma of heated resin - a feeling of indescribable happiness.

Location

The complex is located far in the forest, from the final bus stop for about an hour and a half on foot. The recreation center "Berezhok" (Kazan) is located in Laishevsky district, only 35 km from the city. She really goes to the golden shore of the Volga, where it is so wonderful to sunbathe and swim in the summer heat. Immediately be prepared for the fact that with the onset of dusk, the air temperature drops sharply, so you need to stock up on warm clothes.

Nearby are several lakes where you can sit with a fishing rod, "hunting" for perch and crucian carp.

Accommodation for tourists

The recreation center "Berezhok" (Kazan), basically, has simple wooden houses of various sizes, designed for one, two, three or more residents. Each visitor will be provided with bed linen and kitchen utensils. The houses have beds and bedside tables for things, a table, chairs and a refrigerator. There is a shower with warm water and a toilet. Judging by the reviews, most vacationers are satisfied with the service, especially since almost all the time they spend outdoors.

For more discerning guests there are luxury houses. They include a TV, upholstered furniture, shower and toilet. However, if the amenities are familiar and necessary for urban residents, then the meaning of coming here and sitting with the remote control in hand is difficult to understand, but to each his own. You can rent a house for rent at any convenient time, whether it be a full vacation or a weekend.





Nutrition

Most tourists, renting a house, prefers to cook on their own. After a day spent in the forest, the fastest and easiest soup will go with a bang, gourmet dishes are not required. On the territory there is a large, well-equipped kitchen where everyone can cook whatever he wants, or just reheat food in the microwave. But if there is no desire to devote even a minimum of time to cooking, then you can order a full board. At the base there is a dining room that will provide you with three meals a day.

Prices

Every summer you will find the recreation center "Berezhok" (Kazan). 2014 is no different from the previous ones, prices remained the same. Renting a triple house for a day will cost 1,500 rubles, a four-seater - 2,000 rubles, a five-seater - 2,500 rubles. When you book a room immediately for several days, the price in terms of a day goes below. For example, for 5 days a triple house will cost 5150 rubles. Depending on the season, prices may vary slightly.

Luxury houses will cost more. Their cost is calculated not for places, but for amenities. On average, it is from 2500 to 3000 rubles per day.





Useful with pleasant: we combine rest and treatment

The recreation center operates the sanatorium "Berezhok" (Kazan). The main profile is the rehabilitation of patients after diseases. The treatment of patients suffering from diseases of the heart, blood vessels, respiratory, digestive, central and peripheral nervous systems, disorders of the musculoskeletal system. Separately, patients are restored after severe injuries and operations.

All medical staff has tremendous experience in providing rehabilitation care to patients. The developed diagnostic base enables doctors to realize themselves in this area. Many patients come here for examination, diagnosis and recommendations. There is an excellent clinical hospital, which allows you to quickly get rid of diseases, restore vigor and excellent ability to work. The work of the sanatorium is highly appreciated by patients. In reviews, words of gratitude are often heard to doctors who managed to restore a person to life after a serious illness.

Tourist season

Guests are welcome here from June to October. Earlier arrivals are possible, but for this you need to individually negotiate with the administrators.

How to get there

You can use personal or public transport. By bus number 103 you can drive from the stop "Victory Avenue" to the "Gardens". Traffic on this route is from 06:00 to 19:00 with an interval of 20 minutes. Bus route number 331 runs from the street. Pavlyukhin to the stop "Gardens". Movement from 05:40 to 18:00, interval 2 hours. From the stop "Gardens" to the recreation center "Berezhok" to get another 4 km, about an hour on foot.

By car, the trip will be more comfortable. Drive onto the highway towards the airport, turn right near the police post. Then you will need to turn left at the Stolbishche and move towards Atabaev. Further, the highway will turn right, towards Nikolsky, then left, and the dirt road begins . It will smoothly turn into concrete, on which you need to go down to the side of the bay and turn left.
