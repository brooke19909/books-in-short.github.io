Hotels in Koktebel: addresses, description, reviews

The Crimean village of Koktebel is widely known for its wonderful beaches. Thanks to clean and dry air, relaxation becomes unusually comfortable. The impressions of tourists who prefer to rest in Koktebel are greatly enhanced by the contemplation of the beautiful landscape and sea landscape.





Recently, the tourist infrastructure in the village has been significantly improved. Many new hotels appeared, and existing ones were repaired. Koktebel hotels will be discussed in this article.

The guest house is located at Sadovaya street, 5, a fifteen minute walk from the bus station. Near the hotel is the main attraction of the village - the extinct volcano Kara-Dag. Description

The territory is a flowering garden with many gazebos and a playground. In the courtyard there is an alpine slide, benches and even a barbecue. For self-catering, there is a shared kitchen with modern equipment. Throughout the territory and in rooms, vacationers can access the Internet for free. For tourists arriving by car, there is a secure parking. Room cleaning is available upon request. Holidays in Koktebel can be both calm and full of sightseeing trips and walks in the surrounding places.

Since Koktebel is a small village, you can easily get from any hotel to any of its points (shops, market, embankment). Guests can travel by minibus from the guest house to the coast.
Tourists, in order to get to the hotel, for an additional fee, can use the transfer from Feodosia or Simferopol.

This hotel deserves only positive reviews. Guests note the courtesy and competence of the staff, the excellent location of the hotel. Also, the condition of the rooms is at the highest level.

Guest House Villa Blues





The hotel is located in the center of the village at the address: School School Lane, 8a, a ten-minute walk from the water park. Nearby there is a promenade, a market and a bus station. The room stock consists of standard double rooms with one or two beds. All rooms are air-conditioned and have kitchenettes for self-catering.

Almost all hotels in Koktebel offer their guests comfortable rooms with modern renovation. Vila Blues is no exception. Each room has a TV with numerous satellite channels, a bathroom and a balcony with gorgeous views of the sea and Mount Kara-Dag. Each room has a safe for storing valuables. Especially a lot of reviews are about the beautiful view that opens from the windows of the hotel. Tourists also note good service and cleanliness.

Food and entertainment

On the territory there is a barbecue and barbecue grill. It offers guests outdoor relaxation on the outdoor terrace. Throughout the territory, the possibility of wireless access to the Internet is provided. The library is working.
Guests are served in the cafe, which menu includes seasonal dishes. Vacationers can eat outdoors. The guest house has a tour desk, using the services of which you can book tours of the Crimea.

Vacation with children of any age is also possible. For children up to six years old, accommodation is free. Extra beds are available in the room for a fee.

For motorists there is a paid private parking. The staff of the guest house speaks Russian, Ukrainian and English. Check-in is from 12 hours 30 minutes. Departure - until 12 noon. Settlements in the guest house are made in cash.

Hotel Orchid





Koktebel is a small town, but very welcoming and cozy. Almost all local residents are happy to host travelers, but local hotels are not inferior to them in hospitality. Hotel "Orchid" (Lenin St., 10) is located in the famous House of Writers and specializes mainly in family vacations. It is a quiet place away from sources of noise. The hotel consists of one-room and two-room rooms of the standard, junior and deluxe categories. All apartments are air-conditioned and have a bathroom with shower. Junior suites and suites are located in separate houses with balconies, which offer wonderful views of the sea and mountains. On the territory for a comfortable stay there are gazebos and a beautiful park. Private hotels in Koktebel provide many additional services for the convenience of tourists.

The list of services includes washing and ironing. Room service is available for a fee. There is a sports field equipped with modern equipment. Internet access is available in all rooms and on the premises for guests. A shuttle service to and from the hotel is available at an extra charge.

The hotel has its own parking, but you will have to pay for use. For families with children there is a playground. The hotel staff is friendly and clearly performs its duties.

For those who prefer to inhale the sea breeze around the clock, the best place to relax is the village of Koktebel. Hotels by the sea - the best option for a comfortable stay.

Hotel Victoria





The mini-hotel is located next to the extinct volcano Kara-Dag, on the street. Victory, 22. The entire territory of the hotel is simply buried in numerous green spaces and flowers. Just a hundred and fifty meters is the gentle warm sea. Is this not a reason to come to rest in Koktebel? Hotels by the sea, a jungle of blooming greenery, clean air and good-natured locals. The infrastructure here is also excellent. The promenade, market and the nearest store are within walking distance.





The hotel, thanks to its own autonomous water supply system, always has hot and cold water. In reviews, this point is often mentioned. Indeed, on vacation, everything should only please, and such a trifle as interruptions in water can only upset. Guests also enjoy the cuisine and service at the local cafe.

Accommodation, food and leisure

The number of rooms is represented by double, triple and quadruple rooms of economy class, as well as junior suites and suites in separate houses. All rooms are equipped with modern appliances (TV, air conditioning, electric kettle, iron) and kitchenware. They also have new comfortable furniture. Shower and toilet are located opposite the rooms.
Vacationers are provided with barbecue facilities, free internet access, and tours on the Crimean peninsula are also organized upon special request. At the request of hotel guests, a shuttle service to the hotel and back from Feodosia and Simferopol is provided for an additional fee. Almost all hotels in Koktebel offer their guests full board.

Meals are provided in the on-site cafe. The menu includes dishes of European, Russian, Ukrainian cuisine. Due to the presence of a kitchenette in each room, vacationers can independently prepare their own food using local products purchased at a nearby market.

From the hotel it is convenient to make sightseeing trips throughout the Crimea. Vacationers, having booked a tour at the hotel, have a unique opportunity to get acquainted with the sights of the peninsula (to visit the ancient Feodosia, visit the village of Vyazemskoye, at the famous biostation, see the ancient Genoese fortress in Sudak). Fans of naturism can visit the Fox Bay, famous among fans of this direction. Tourists who came to the village of Novyi Svit have the opportunity to taste the famous varieties of Crimean champagne. Hotel Victoria (Koktebel) is considered one of the best places to stay in the village.

The benefits of a vacation in Koktebel





Thus, the village of Koktebel, being a unique holiday destination with a developed tourist infrastructure, is attractive for tourists to visit. Each vacationer, having visited this wonderful, cozy and picturesque place, will find a rest for himself. You can get to the village by taxi or bus from Simferopol airport or by personal car via the crossing at Kerch.

Most hotels in Koktebel have an approximately equal level of comfort, so tourists can safely choose any of them to stay. In search of a hotel, you should focus on the reviews of guests who have previously visited it, and see photos presented on numerous tourist portals. Important is the degree of remoteness from the sea and the availability of infrastructure. In this regard, all hotels in Koktebel fully meet the most demanding requirements. All of them are located a hundred meters from the sea coast and have everything necessary for a comfortable stay (sports and playgrounds, equipment, barbecue facilities and gazebos). An undoubted advantage is their location in the most picturesque corners of the village, surrounded by orchards and cypress groves. Vacationers go to the sea along beautiful alleys. Night walks along the Koktebel embankment in the light of lanterns are especially good.
