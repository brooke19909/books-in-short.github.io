Bravo Garden 4 * (Tunisia). Tunisia 4 Star Hotels

Foreign exotic countries have become more accessible to modern people. Sometimes rest outside our country is cheaper than in domestic resorts. A great example are the hotels of Tunisia, located in North Africa. The tourist infrastructure in Hammamet is especially rich, there are a huge number of hotels here, but Bravo Garden 4 * (Tunisia) deserves special attention.





Location

The hotel is located in the Yasmin district. Enfida Airport is 39 km from the hotel. The location of Bravo Garden 4 * (Tunisia) allows you to quickly reach the large entertainment center Carthage Land, where various categories of citizens have fun. Gambling tourists can try their luck at Casino La Medina. In the evenings, couples in love stroll around Port Yasmine, enjoying a magical, romantic setting.

There is a public beach near the hotel. At a distance of 820 meters is the Pomodoro Restaurant, which is famous for its Italian cuisine and seafood specialties. If you want to fly to Tunisia, 4 star hotels are the most interesting offer from a tour operator, as they are located near the beach and various attractions of the country. Most often, such hotels operate the "All Inclusive" system, which is always in demand by travelers.

Rooms

In total, Bravo Garden 4 * (Tunisia) has 220 comfortable apartments, all of them are equipped with air conditioning, a telephone with the possibility of international access and modern furniture. The stylish interior is decorated in soothing colors to create a pleasant environment for relaxation. Each room has a balcony or terrace (depending on the category of apartments), satellite TV is also required. Among all, there is 1 Russian channel. A minibar can be provided upon request, but this service is paid. Quadruple rooms are most relevant for families. The bathroom can be equipped with a shower cabin or a jacuzzi. Smoking and pets are strictly prohibited. The hotel has a rule - do not provide an extra bed in the room. You can even come to Tunisia as a "savage", Bravo Garden always has available apartments due to their large number.





Nutrition

You can pay a tour, which includes only the cost of living, but in this case you will have to pay for food separately. If you want to save your money, you should take a ticket, which includes the above power system.

The guests are offered a varied food, consisting of national and European dishes. The buffet always has meat, fish, fruits and freshly squeezed juices. If you look at the reviews written by tourists, Bravo Garden (Tunisia) becomes an even more desirable place to relax. Here you will see only balanced and healthy food that will enrich the body with vitamins and minerals. High chairs are available on request for young children.

Territory

Thanks to the rich infrastructure of the Bravo Garden 4 hotel, Tunisia has become one of the most visited destinations. On the territory there is a large outdoor pool for adults, there is also a small section for bathing children. Comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas are free for guests. An indoor heated pool allows you to swim and do aerobics in the water even in bad cold weather.

The restaurant offers guests to taste aromatic and mouth-watering food for an additional fee. In the evening, most of the Bravo Garden residents dine at this place, enjoying pleasant, calm music and soft drinks. Another restaurant is located in the open air, it has an a la carte category and requires an appointment. There you will try different meat dishes. A polite waiter will offer suitable drinks.



In the morning tourists are attracted by the pleasant aroma of a coffee shop. An invigorating drink along with fresh pastries will give a good mood for the whole day. Coffee is brewed exclusively from high-quality beans right in front of the client. The services of this institution are not included in the "All Inclusive" system. There is also a Moorish cafe where a large number of guests always gather.

The hotel has its own sandy beach, located 200 meters from the main building. For the use of sun loungers and umbrellas, cash is not required.

Service

Hammamet Bravo Garden 4 has a large lobby with high-speed internet access via Wi-Fi. This service is absolutely free.

As in any other hotel, the reception is open around the clock, which is very convenient for potential guests arriving at night. At the reception there is a safe where tourists store money and valuables. This service is also paid.

Dirty clothes should be taken to the laundry. The cost of washing depends on the weight of the laundry.





Free parking relieves guests of problems with the location of the car. The hotel staff is responsible for the safety of the vehicle.

If tenants order food in the room, it will be paid, regardless of the food system. This also applies to any other additional service.

Business meetings, birthdays and other events are held in the conference room, which is provided by the hotel for a fee.

Room cleaning is done daily, which creates even more comfortable living conditions. Sheets are changed once a week. If necessary, you can order additional cleaning, but this service must be paid.

Tipping staff are given based on your personal desire.

Paid entertainment

Arriving at the hotel, tourists first of all visit the local SPA-center, where you can relax, forget about current problems and get incomparable pleasure. Here you will have an excellent massage using various attributes, for example, heated miniature stones and aromatic oils.

For lovers of gambling, billiards, darts and a golf club are located nearby. Slot machines test the level of luck of guests. Table tennis and lawn tennis can help you stay fit during your vacation. On the beach, tourists can play basketball or volleyball.

For younger guests there is a mini-club, designed for children from 4 to 12 years. Interactive group games are conducted with them, and the kids also communicate with each other. Upon request, you can use the services of a qualified nanny, who will look after your child.





Shopping enthusiasts will love the many shops and souvenir shops located on site. Here they can find fancy figures, vases, clothes, dishes, leather goods and much more.

Free leisure

Guests always spend a lot of time swimming in the pool or doing aerobics there under the close supervision of an experienced trainer. Access to the gym equipped with modern equipment is free of charge.

Animation will not let the hotel guests get bored: daily competitions are held with the participation of tourists and various skits. The staff leads round dances, involving everyone. For kids, a special program is held with a costume performance.

Hammamet Bravo Garden 4 provides guests with an exciting nightlife. Every evening in a specially equipped room are discos.

Bars

The hotel has 3 bars. One is located by the outdoor pool. It offers free drinks to guests whose tour provides for the "All Inclusive" system, everyone else will have to pay. The second offers refreshing drinks (without alcohol) for a fee. In the third, you can buy cocktails and alcohol. It is located in the main building of Bravo Garden. The on-site disco bar daily attracts guests with its original atmosphere. Here tourists can buy cocktails with or without alcohol.





Guest reviews

In order to have the most complete picture of the hotel, you should familiarize yourself with the opinions of former guests. So, we are studying the reviews. Bravo Garden attracts many tourists thanks to the excellent work of the staff. People praise the hotel infrastructure and service. There is a fun nightlife at the hotel. All guests write that it is possible to have a great time without going beyond Bravo Garden (Hammamet). Former residents like the proposed kitchen and several establishments located on the territory of the hotel, which allow you to change the usual atmosphere and spend free time. Families devote their feedback to animation, which helps diversify leisure activities not only for adults, but also for small tourists. Also, special attention is paid to the clean beach with a shallow approach, which ensures safety for swimming with children.

tour cost

Agents offer several accommodation options for those who are interested in holidays in Tunisia (all inclusive), but most tourists choose Bravo Garden. The price of the tour varies from 1,500 to 5,000 rubles per day. The cost depends on the availability of the "All Inclusive" system, the number of days spent in the hotel, room category and flight distance. The hotel website always has more detailed information. There, if you wish, you can independently book your favorite room. If we talk about travel agencies, it should be borne in mind that last minute trips always cost an order of magnitude cheaper than the initial offer, but sometimes the date of departure does not coincide with the potential guest. This option is relevant for people with a large vacation or part-time.



During its existence, Bravo Garden has become one of the main attractions of Tunisia. It is impossible to assign a limited qualification and call it a family or youth hotel, since it is universal. Here everyone will find something to their liking. When viewed from the point of view of Tunisia's popularity, 4-star hotels are in great demand, making the country one of the leaders in the field of recreation.
