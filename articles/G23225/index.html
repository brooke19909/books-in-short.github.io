Charming flower made of sweets with your own hands. Bright poppies with a secret inside

Any festive event is often accompanied not only by gifts, feasts and good mood, but also by flowers. Living create a special atmosphere of celebration, and made with your own hands from paper, sweets, fabrics and other improvised materials diversify the holiday and will surely please relatives and friends.

On the eve of the New Year holidays, we would like to tell you how to learn how to create an original gift - a flower from sweets with your own hands. Make it easy, the main thing is to devote a little of your time to creative work. Beautiful sweet bouquets will bring joy and positive emotions to any of your guests.

Flower from sweets: we make cheerful poppies with our own hands

In order to create a bright and effective bouquet of poppies, you need corrugated paper in three colors: scarlet for petals, green for the stem and black for the core of the bud. You also need to purchase sweets. It is convenient enough to work with round sweets. In addition, you will need glue, a ruler, a thin wire and adhesive tape. Well, of course, you need to start work in a good mood! So, we begin the creation of crafts "flowers" from sweets. A master class with a description and photos will tell you all the subtleties of its design.



First we take the corrugated black paper and cut off a strip of 23x9 cm. We retreat from one side a segment equal to half the candy. Finely chop the rest of the paper with a fringe. We take the candy and carefully wrap it in black paper in two turns - so the core of the poppy will be more magnificent and voluminous. Do not forget to fix the workpiece with glue or transparent tape. After that, we put a thin elastic band on the lower edge of the workpiece (or wrap it with tape), and wind a thin wire over it, forming a loop around the core. Also make the stem. A flower made of sweets with your own hands is almost made. It remains only to decorate it with bright red petals and decorate the wire stalk.

DIY flower made of sweets. Bright and sweet poppies bouquet



To make the petals, we need red corrugated paper. Cut off a strip of 11x21 cm from it, fold it three times and cut three petals. You can make a blank on plain white paper, attach it to a piece and carefully cut along the contour. It should be remembered that the upper part of the petal should be rounded.

Stretch the upper part of the petals a bit, giving them a realistic waviness. Now we wrap the black core with the candy inside in the petals, not forgetting to fasten the elements with glue. Petals overlap at the base. So we got a magnificent bright scarlet poppy inflorescence. It remains to arrange the stem of the plant. We take green paper and cut off the strip from it. Slightly stretch and wrap it around the wire. We fix the paper with glue in several places, do not forget to put the glue on the base of the bud. Everything, a flower from sweets with your own hands is ready!

Let's make flowers and bouquets. From sweets and corrugated paper you will get a charming sweet present.



In order to create a beautiful bouquet, it is necessary to make several poppies using the technology described above. As a rule, 5 flowers are enough to make the bouquet look presentable and luxurious. You can decorate poppies with decorative floral ribbons and decorate them with organza. You can also pick up a small basket and place poppies in it. So you get an unusual composition of paper flowers with a sweet secret inside that will delight anyone!
