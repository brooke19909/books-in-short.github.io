How to withdraw money from a qiwi wallet?

On the Internet, there are many opinions on this subject, both positive and negative. Consider the following withdrawal methods:

- to the MasterCard;

- to the Visa card (Qiwi Wallet);

- to a bank account.

In the first case, you can withdraw funds to the card of any Russian bank. But keep in mind that at each withdrawal you will be charged 2% + 20 rubles (fee). Money can be cashed at any ATM in the country. MasterCard standard also allows you to do this abroad. The withdrawal of funds to a Visa card is similar. Write off approximately the same commission. And the term of enrollment will be on average 2 days. The best way to withdraw money from a kiwi wallet is to issue a Visa Qiwi Wallet card. Funds are withdrawn almost instantly. The service declares that the card is not personalized. That is, any resident of the country can use this withdrawal method. And no identification is required, as, for example, on the Yandex.Money card. Formally, the card is also suitable for residents of other countries, but problems in the operation of mail services do not allow sending cards to foreign clients. And the last way is to withdraw funds to a bank account. Moreover, it is the most convenient of the above. This is due to the fact that the Kiwi system allows you to choose the account of any Russian bank. That is, within the country you can open an account in any commercial bank and withdraw funds to it from a qiwi wallet. The withdrawal speed is not very high and is 2 days (excluding weekends).
