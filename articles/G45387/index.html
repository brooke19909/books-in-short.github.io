Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 *: reviews, photos, description, height

Bangkok is a beautiful modern city, and you can only appreciate it from a bird's eye view. The Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 * will provide you with such an opportunity - you will not find it higher in all of Thailand! In addition, here you will find friendly staff, comfortable rooms and a huge range of services.





Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 *: description

This hotel is simply unique. The height of Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 * is as much as 309 meters - 88 floors. This is not just a hotel, but also one of the most popular attractions of the city. On the 77th floor, as well as on the roof there are observation platforms (one of them rotates), which thousands of tourists are eager to get to. An additional pleasant moment is the availability of free Internet, which is valid in the entire hotel. Also be sure to visit the SPA center, where you can enjoy traditional Thai treatments for beauty and health.

It is simply impossible to ignore the guest rooms of the Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 *. They differ not only in their luxurious design, but also in an incredibly beautiful view from the window that opens before you all the charms of a huge city. The highlight of the apartments can also be considered marbled in luxurious bathrooms. In addition, there are many more modern amenities that will make your stay at the hotel unforgettable and incredibly comfortable.





If you are interested in national Thai cuisine, then you should definitely visit the themed cafe, which operates on the territory of the Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 *. Its huge advantage is that this institution is around the clock. The restaurant serves mainly seafood dishes and also has an impressive international menu. In the bar you will be pleased not only with a huge assortment of drinks, but also with live music performed by invited singers and musicians.

Hotel Location

Hotel Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 * is located in the Patumwan district, which is considered the very center of Bangkok. Literally a few meters away is a small train station that allows you to quickly get to the international airport, as well as other settlements. It is worth noting that the hotel itself is one of the main attractions of the city. A convenient location will allow you to explore other interesting places and structures.

Hotel Guest Apartments

In Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 * apartments are located on floors 27 to 74. You can choose one of the following options:

  • Superior is a spacious one-room apartment, which is furnished in the form of a studio. It combines a bedroom, a living room, and a small entrance hall. Guests are invited to sit on a huge double bed with a comfortable orthopedic mattress. You can also comfortably sit in soft chairs to read, drink coffee or enjoy the beautiful cityscape.
  • Deluxe apartments consist of two rooms (but no door is provided between them). The bedroom is compact, and it can have either a large bed or a pair of one and a half. But the living room is quite spacious and bright. In addition to upholstered furniture, there is also a large dining table and many interesting decorative elements.
  • Executive apartments are designed to accommodate business people who do not forget about work even on vacation. There are two rooms. One of them is a rather cozy study, which also serves as a living room. Of course, there is a bedroom with two twin beds. Orthopedic mattresses contribute to a comfortable and healthy sleep.
  • Junior is a huge one-room apartment, in which the living room is separated from the bedroom by decorative zoning. Two guests can sleep in one and a half beds. Did you see a friend? No problem. The guest can comfortably sit on a comfortable folding sofa. Due to the huge window, as well as the lack of partitions, the room has a lot of light.
  • Superior is perfect for family vacations. Here the living room and bedroom are separated from each other by an interior door. Accommodation is made both on beds and on extra beds (folding sofas). In total, the apartment can accommodate up to 4 people.
  • Deluxe Suite is distinguished by its impressive size and original design. The living room is combined with the bedroom, but thanks to a successful layout, your peace and privacy during sleep will not be disturbed.
  • Presidential Suite is the most luxurious and spacious hotel apartment. Their area is just over 100 square meters. m. A spacious living room serves both as an office and dining room. There are also two bedrooms, decorated in different colors. The apartment also has two bathrooms.

Allows you to verify the luxurious design of the rooms of Baiyoke Sky Hotel photos taken by guests of this hotel. Notice how classic and modern conciseness are successfully combined here.

Useful information for travelers

Be sure to read the rules of staying in a particular hotel if you intend to visit Bangkok. Baiyoke Sky Hotel has established the following rules for tourists:

  • registration procedures, as well as check-in of the guests in the apartments begin after two in the afternoon, but if the hotel has a sufficient number of available rooms, then for an additional fee this process can be accelerated;
  • You need to leave the room on the day of departure before 11 a.m., but if you want to spend time in your room before the plane, then for every hour you will have to pay at the established rate;
  • for children under two years old, a cot and other necessary equipment is provided absolutely free of charge;
  • if an extra bed is not required to accommodate a child under 12 years old, then there is no charge for his accommodation;
  • an extra bed (a folding bed or a soft sofa) is provided to the guest for 1000 baht per day;
  • guests are not allowed to bring pets and other animals to the hotel, otherwise employees have every reason to refuse registration;
  • for all services provided at the hotel, you can pay by credit card;
  • the hotel provides a free shuttle service to guests to the nearest train station, and additional charges apply for travel to other transport hubs (the service must be pre-ordered);
  • if you want to book a room, you must certainly have a registered credit card (debit payment instruments are not accepted).




Baiyoke Sky Hotel: Travel Reviews Positive points

The opinion of travelers, at times, becomes decisive in choosing a hotel, because it reflects the most objective picture. A huge number of comments can be found regarding the Baiyoke Sky Hotel. Reviews about this institution, in general, are very positive:

  • the windows of the rooms offer a stunning view, and at night, when the street lighting is turned on, it becomes even more impressive;
  • for hotel guests visiting the observation tower is absolutely free and available at any time (but for outsiders, there are time limits, as well as a certain tariff);
  • the rooms are very beautiful, spacious and incredibly comfortable due to the huge number of amenities;
  • near the hotel there is a railway station, from which you can reach the airport in high speed train in just half an hour;
  • they clean the rooms well enough, with the exception of minor nuances;
  • the restaurant, which is located on the top floor, makes an unforgettable impression - there is simply an incredible view from the window, especially during dinner;
  • There are several local markets right next to the hotel, which is why you can enjoy colorful shopping;
  • excellent speed and quality of wireless Internet connection;
  • the hotel staff is well trained and very courteous with guests;
  • the spacious bathroom, which is decorated with natural marble, makes a good impression;
  • A huge selection of dishes is presented at the buffet as a breakfast.




Negative hotel reviews

Despite the abundance of positive comments, there were some gaps in the work of the Baiyoke Sky Hotel 4 * resort. Reviews indicate the following negative points:

  • the air conditioner works like a beast, and therefore you can catch a cold very quickly (but if you turn it off, it will be incredibly stuffy);
  • the carpets in the rooms are very dusty, as if they had never been vacuumed (especially allergic people will feel this);
  • ventilation is arranged somewhat strange, and therefore in rooms located near the restaurant, there is often a smell of products from the kitchen;
  • the pool is small enough for so many rooms;
  • when checking into the hotel you have to leave a deposit ($ 100 or their equivalent in local currency);
  • the rooms lack balconies where one could breathe fresh air or dry things;
  • very high prices in restaurants;
  • quite often there are problems with the return of the deposit (be sure to keep a receipt);
  • the choice of excursions is rather scarce, but the prices are simply sky-high;
  • due to the fact that there is a market around the hotel, access to it by taxi is very difficult;
  • when you arrive at the hotel, no one will meet you at the entrance and will not help with your suitcases;
  • there is no ramp, and therefore you have to literally drag your luggage up the stairs;
  • the whole impression of the room is spoiled by an old, leaky, dusty carpet;
  • in the low category rooms are just miniature televisions;
  • the plumbing is already a little thin, and therefore leaking;
  • only a small part of it works from the entire observation deck, and all the rest are closed for repairs (because of this, constant crowds are created, and it is simply impossible to admire the city);
  • despite the fact that the hotel is positioning itself at a very high level, tables and chairs are wrapped in plastic in the dining room, as in a cheap eatery;
  • when paying for services, carefully monitor the actions of staff and require a check, as often money is often debited from the card twice;
  • this situation often arises that you may not be accommodated in the apartments that you paid for, but in a room with a class lower, so control this process;
  • despite the fact that the hotel's feature is in the panoramic windows, they are rather dirty, and therefore the whole impression of the city landscape spoils.

General impression

A rather interesting accommodation option is the Baiyoke Sky Hotel (Bangkok). Reviews about him, as you can see, are quite contradictory. The hotel is interesting in that it is one of the main attractions of Bangkok due to its height. Accordingly, you can enjoy stunning views both from the windows of the rooms and from the observation deck. Nevertheless, there are some flaws that are simply unacceptable at such a high cost of accommodation. We are talking about dirty carpets, old plumbing, as well as frequent misunderstandings with payment for services and the return of collateral. In general, the hotel is worthy of the attention of travelers.
