Universal charger: how to restore battery performance

A universal frog charger is widely used to power lithium batteries used in cell phones and other small-sized technical devices. This device cannot charge other types of batteries. It is also used to build up completely discharged energy storage devices.



The universal charger for phones has on its body two sliding mustaches, with which it is connected to the contact pads of the batteries. In some cases, these places on the battery can be from two to four.

When connected to the battery, the mustache of the device is moved apart to the required distance and installed on the negative and positive platforms of the battery. At the same time, polarity is not always necessary. The automatic device will independently determine this parameter.

If the universal charger has buttons on the case, then after connecting the battery, make sure that the connection is correct. To do this, press the left button. If the diode located under the words "FUL" and "CON" lights up, it means that the device is connected correctly.

If the indicators do not light up, you can judge the connection is incorrect or the battery is completely discharged. In such a situation, the polarity should be reversed. If this time, after pressing the button, there is no result, then we can conclude that the battery is completely discharged or, possibly, the mustache does not touch the battery compartments.
