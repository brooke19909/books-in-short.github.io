DIY cat toy: master class, cat patterns

DIY toys will surely be a nice gift for friends and children. They are also a decoration for the home. Cute home-made toys carry warmth, complement coziness, create a pleasant atmosphere and charge with a positive mood. Moreover, to sew a toy is very simple, and the materials for this are simple and affordable.

Let's see together how to sew a toy using the example of a cat. We have prepared an excellent master class for you and are ready to offer a simple pattern for this.

Materials

In order to sew a cat, you will need:

  • dense fabric;
  • pencil;
  • needle;
  • thread in fabric color;
  • floss thread for embroidery muzzle;
  • cotton wool or any other filler for toys.

In addition to all of the above, you will need a pattern. Sewing a cat with your own hands is not at all difficult, as it seems at first glance. A suitable pattern can be found on various forums on the Internet. You can also draw it yourself or take advantage of our offer.

DIY toys are often stuffed with various fillers synthetic winterizer, foam rubber, cotton wool. Sawdust, dried herbs, which give an incredible flavor to the toy, are also used.

Sewing cats

Print or draw a cat pattern, estimating the size and appearance of the future toy. Cut the pattern along the contour and lay on a piece of fabric, folded in half. Move the drawing. When cutting a cat, add a couple of centimeters for sewing to the contour.

Sew two identical parts from the wrong side, leaving a little below. Since we have a tail, do not sew up a place for it so that you can stuff the toy.

Twist the tail and body, stuff them with cotton and carefully sew them together. Spread cotton wool over the entire body of the toy.

Decoration

Decorate our cat. First of all, we need to draw his face. On monophonic fabric, you can use resistant paint, but the best thing on a makeshift toy looks embroidery stitch.

Look at this cat pattern, how cute its muzzle is. Carefully embroider eyes and mouth on the cat's face, make a magnificent antennae. Do not forget to emphasize the ears.
