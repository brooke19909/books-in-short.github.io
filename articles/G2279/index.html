Sights of Poltava: photos and descriptions

Poltava is an ancient and beautiful city of Ukraine. Most residents know him thanks to the play by Natalia Kotlyarevsky, Natalka-Poltavka. Meanwhile, in this village, which began the twelfth century from the time of its foundation, a large number of cultural and historical monuments are concentrated. Despite his esteemed age, Poltava still looks young. She did not waste her perfection, and every year she only becomes more beautiful. And today the sights of Poltava are included in the list of the most visited places on the planet.



The architectural ensemble of the Round Square

Perhaps starting a tour of Poltava is, perhaps, with the Round Square and its architectural ensemble. This is one of the most striking sights of this city. The complex is located in the center, so getting here is absolutely not difficult.

The appearance of the Round Square began to form at the very beginning of the XIX century. In general, all the sights of Poltava (photos that can be viewed below) began to be established for a long time. And this is not surprising when you consider the age of the city itself. So, the architectural ensemble of the Round Square is made in the style of classicism. The patron of the building was the then-famous Semyon Kochubey. And construction work lasted from 1805-1840.

The following administrative institutions are included in the ensemble of this attraction: the house of the vice-governor, the house of the governor-general, and the Little Russian Post Office. There is also an old governor's house, a building of the noble assembly, a house of provincial public places, a communication house and a building of district public places. Today, all these buildings are converted to the needs of modern residents. And now they can be called so: the sights of Poltava of the XXI century.



And Kotlyarevsky is still there

To describe the sights of Poltava and the Poltava region and not to mention the house-museum of Ivan Kotlyarevsky means to forget the most intimate of the history of the city. After all, Poltava is impossible without Ivan Petrovich, as day cannot exist without night, and the moon without sun. These are two inseparable concepts.

So, in the Ukrainian village, on Cathedral Square, is the house-museum of the great master of speech. In this room, Kotlyarevsky spent almost all of his life. The writer created most of his literary masterpieces in this house. Among them are the famous Natalka-Poltavka and Aeneid. True, the true estate of Ivan Petrovich could not survive until our days. But at the end of the 60s of the last century, the estate of a classic of Ukrainian literature was recreated from the drawing of the no less famous Taras Shevchenko.

Historical heart of Poltava

You can see all the sights of the city of Poltava on Ivanova Mountain. This place is the best viewpoint of the village. Here is a huge complex of outstanding architectural monuments. Here is the symbol of the city dating back to 1909. And the name is its white rotunda. Famous food - dumpling, also found a haven on Ivanova Mountain. A magnificent panorama of the whole Poltava can be seen from the mountain. On the right side, you can see the Institute Mountain, on the left - the Monastery. Below is the city itself and the Vorskla flows, and on the hill located in the neighborhood, the Holy Cross Monastery flaunts.

Come to Poltava - eat a dumpling

Sights of Poltava are not only monuments of architecture and nature, but also symbols of the city that do not belong to material attributes. Dumplings are considered the gem of local cooking. This is an original Poltava dish that glorified the city both throughout Ukraine and the neighboring countries. Dumplings a bit like dumplings, but still different from this dish.



In this village, cooks cook more than a dozen varieties of dumplings. And the aforementioned dish monument serves as an association with well-being and wealth. The monument is a huge deep bowl, in which there are a dozen ready-made flour koloboks and a spoon.
