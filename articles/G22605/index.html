Hotels and villas in Phuket. Villa rental

Today, many vacationers, having arrived in Phuket, prefer to settle not in a familiar and even a bit boring hotel, but in a luxurious villa. No matter how well vacationers spoke about hotels and hotel complexes in Phuket, nevertheless there sometimes problems arise with neighbors or annoying reception desk staff, which cannot be said about living in separate cottages. Villas in Phuket can either be detached private houses or be located in hotel complexes. Some of them will be described below.

Hotel Centara Grand West Sands Resort & Villas Phuket 5 *





This hotel is located in the Mai Chao Beach Conservation Area. The beach is in close proximity to the hotel, the journey to the airport takes 15 minutes, and it takes about half an hour to reach the Laguna.

Conditions



The number of places for vacationers to stay is 558. From this number you can choose a haven of any level, from Deluxe to luxurious individual buildings such as a penthouse. Each room offers gorgeous views of the ocean, and the rooms themselves are equipped with everything you need for a good time. There is a service, a kettle, sugar, tea and coffee for preparing a full morning awakening drink.

Room sizes vary by class and range from 30 to 500 square meters. The pools near the villas are very large, their size is about 40 square meters.

Villas can have from 1 to 5 bedrooms, designed for VIPs and guests of a premium class. Such houses are able to fit inside about 12 guests, which certainly saves time on the choice of housing in the event that a large company has come to rest. Those who choose villas and penthouses for living have the opportunity to eat 5 times a day on the menu, as well as visit clubs and all entertainment venues with special club cards.

Number of rooms

The Centara Grand West Sands Resort Villa Hotel (Phuket) has the following types of rooms for accommodation: Deluxe (Suite), Deluxe family (Family Suite), Deluxe Suite 1-bedroom (Suite Single), Deluxe Family Residence (Family Villa with multi-bedroom apartments), Deluxe Family Suite 2-bedroom, Luxury Suite 1-bedroom, Luxury Suite 2-bedroom, Penthouse Pool Suite 3-bedroom, Penthouse Pool Suite 4-bedroom, Deluxe Pool Villa 2-bedroom, Deluxe Pool Villa 3-bedroom , Luxury Pool Villa 2-bedroom, Luxury Pool Villa 3-bedroom.

Service



As for meals, the hotel has a guesthouse system with the possibility of replacing lunch with dinner, as well as choosing meals in the buffet or according to the available menu.

From the free service to the services of vacationers there is a room cleaning and linen change service, a personal concierge, services of a water park built into the hotel. For a fee, the owners are ready to kindly provide the following services:

  • Gym with lots of exercise equipment.
  • Badminton court.
  • Volleyball field.
  • SPA center.
  • 2 outdoor pools directly on site.
  • Providing shuttle-bus services (transportation or transfer).

The same service that delivers visitors directly to the shopping center, which costs about 200 baht, is provided free of charge to children under 3 years old.
From the hotel, the bus takes the guests away at 14:00, and brings it back at 20:00.
As for children's leisure, vacationers are offered a kids club (free of charge), and for an additional fee you can order the services of an experienced nanny.
The club leisure industry is represented by two bars and a restaurant, where there is also a menu and everyone can choose dishes to order.

The hotel has at its disposal a private beach with a length of about 800 meters.
Hotel prices are not too high, only 200 dollars per room. Renting a villa in Phuket does not take much time. It is enough to come to the hotel and choose the appropriate option.

Villa Botany Kata Beach



This unusually bright and comfortable complex is located in Kata Beach. The hotel is a series of houses for vacationers, united in a common adjoining territory, which also has cozy gazebos.

Each gazebo has its own view of the pool and garden with an abundance of tropical plants and shrubs. Winning the Villa Botany Hotel (Phuket) is also in a favorable location, since the distance to the center of Thailand is only about a kilometer, and to small shops just a stone's throw away.

Near the pool there is a special recreation area, and from household accessories at the disposal - an ironing board. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available.

All the furniture in the rooms is suitable for living and very comfortable, made in a simple classic style. The bathroom has towels and all bath amenities, a bathroom with toilet.

Villa "Kata". Description





The resort area of Kata Beach is famous for its picturesque landscapes. It is here that the cozy "Kata" (villa, Phuket) is located. There is a garden on site, and a gorgeous view of the mountain peaks opens from the windows. Nearby is an elephant farm where you can go on an excursion. You can park your car for free on site and use the internet.

The house has three bedrooms and two bathrooms with hydromassage. You can cook your own meals in your own kitchen. There is a separate area for eating and relaxing. There is a TV with many channels. Activists are provided with bicycle rental and snorkelling equipment. Within walking distance is the beautiful Kata Noi Beach and the Dino Park Golf Course. Especially popular here are diving, windsurfing and just cycling in picturesque places. The nearest airport is 33 km from the villa.
