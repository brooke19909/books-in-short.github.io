Colva Kinara Colva 3 * (Colva / India): reviews and photos

Colva is a pretty and quite popular resort, in which an abundance of colonial-style villas and fishing huts is noteworthy. Many local residents traditionally come to the local beach to escape from the summer heat. Colva is known as the largest and oldest resort in South Goa. Tourists call it safe swimming, the coast, located some distance from the central beach, is said to be pristine, well-groomed.

The resort has a huge selection of accommodation: here you can live in a luxurious chalet with a pool, or in a budget guesthouse. Most mid-range and high-end hotels are located on and around Main Beach Road. Simpler hotels are located in the Ward No. 4 area (north side of the resort). Vacationers assure that it is easy to get here along the path laid northward from the Johnny Cools restaurant, or along the road going from Main Colva - Vasco Road to the west.
