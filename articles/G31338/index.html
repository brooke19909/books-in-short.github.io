The recreation center "Shavskaya Dolina": infrastructure, package of services and reviews about the club

In recent decades, the hotel business has been actively and quite successfully developing in Russia. A lot of hotels, guest houses and hunting houses, tourist centers, boarding houses are being built. Each of them offers its guests a range of different, sometimes exclusive and exotic services. In addition to comfortable living in different parts of the country, guests are offered horseback riding, spa treatments, aqua holidays, visits to farms, dog sledding, visits to hotel museums, organization of fishing and hunting, and many other interesting entertainments. In recent years, suburban family clubs have become popular.

Country Club "Shavskaya Valley", Nizhny Novgorod Region

In a forest near the village of Shava, half an hour from Nizhny Novgorod, on the territory of about two hectares is a suburban family complex. The room fund of the Shavskaya Dolina club is represented by thirteen rooms located in a two-story building.





Two rooms are in a secluded cottage, one in a log cabin in eco-style with a veranda and hay. A variety of entertainments is offered to guests' choice - a cozy bar, for conferences there is a hall with a capacity of up to fifty participants, a cinema with a disco function, an entertainment room for children. Adult guests will enjoy table tennis and billiards, a massage room.

Food conditions

In a separate building is the dining room of the Shavskaya Dolina country club. It accommodates up to sixty guests. Here you can taste traditional Russian cuisine, three meals a day are organized. For the convenience of holidaymakers with young children, highchairs are available. A good visit to the club's bathhouse will help you to relax and recharge. A fireplace in the relaxation room, a cold pool with a font, a steam room and a shower will allow you to enjoy water procedures.



For a pleasant outdoor recreation, a barbecue is equipped. This is a covered area with barbecue and furniture. On the territory of the Shavskaya Dolina family club there is a gazebo and patio. It is designed for one hundred people and is equipped with comfortable furniture, barbecue, bar counter and toilet.

Rest and accommodation

For active recreation, guests of the Shavskaya Dolina club have sports facilities: volleyball and football fields, a summer outdoor pool. Winter sports enthusiasts will be attracted by ski slopes, an outdoor ice rink, and a large ice slide. Sports equipment is available for rent.

It offers rooms from economy class to luxury apartments. In the halls of residential buildings are equipped with recreation areas for guests with comfortable sofas. Standard rooms are equipped with double and single beds, equipped with extra beds. The guest rooms have a wardrobe, coffee and dining tables and chairs, a mirror. Household appliances are represented by a refrigerator, TV, electric kettle. Guests are offered cutlery. The bathroom is in the room, it has a shower and a washbasin. For economy options, there is one bathroom for two rooms. A refrigerator and microwave are on the floor.



The suite is furnished in a country style. In its decoration used natural material - wood. Large stained glass windows allow you to enjoy beautiful views of the local nature. The room has a double bed and a sofa, a wardrobe and a chest of drawers, a dining and coffee tables, a hanger and chairs, armchairs. A TV, microwave, fridge and electric kettle are also included. Such rooms are equipped with their own sauna, bath and shower, toilet and bidet, washbasin and mirror. Bathrobes and towels are provided. You can take a small house for four people. There is a hayloft and an attic. The house is equipped as a standard room.

Organization of holidays with children, corporate holidays

Interesting and diverse events are offered by the Shavskaya Dolina club. The recreation center positions itself as a family complex. Here they offer developing vacations for children of different ages. Teachers and psychologists, animators deal with them. Interesting educational and game programs for children are offered, graduation parties and other school holidays are held.





Corporate clients are offered outdoor activities. It organizes sporting events, military sports games, there is equipment for paintball and airsoft. Near the recreation center, in the village of Shava, there is a ski complex "Terraski Park".

Cost of living and services

The cost of living is quite low in the club "Shavskaya Valley". Prices for one adult person, depending on the category of room, are from 1400 to 2700 rubles per day. There is a 10% discount on check-in on weekdays. The price includes: accommodation in the room, organization of three meals a day, visiting children's playgrounds, a game room, a cinema hall, and the use of a barbecue area. Near the recreation center there is free parking.

Guest reviews

The services of the Shavskaya Dolina boarding house (Nizhny Novgorod region) are popular and in demand. Guest reviews testify to this. Many of the visitors note that they had a great rest here as a family. Guests like the care of the staff, good conditions for living and recreation.





Tasty Russian cuisine offered in the club's dining room does not leave indifferent. The work of children's animators, who find a special approach to each child, has earned rave reviews. The guests are puzzled by the shabby appearance of some pieces of furniture and bed linen.
