Hotel Resta Grand Resort 5 *, Marsa Alam, Egypt: overview, description, features and reviews

Tourists who choose a suitable place to stay in Egypt should pay attention to the young but rapidly developing resort of Marsa Alam. There can not please guests with many entertainment places, but a quiet and relaxing holiday will be provided to everyone. One of the hotels that is ready to offer tourists excellent living conditions is Resta Grand Resort 5 *. In it, the rest will fully reveal the ability of the Egyptians to surround guests with comfort and provide them with excellent living conditions.

Where is the hotel situated?

Resta Grand Resort 5 * was built in 2009 in the quiet fishing village of Marsa Alam. It is located on the coast of the Red Sea. Hurghada International Airport, where most guests arrive, is 220 km away. Arriving tourists have to get to the hotel for quite a long time. El Quseira Airport is approximately 40 km away. Guests arriving here are delivered to the hotel within 10-15 minutes.



Hotel Description

Grand Resta Resort 5 * (Egypt, Marsa Alam) is a delightful and rather sophisticated hotel that will amaze with its beauty even tourists who have seen a lot. It was built in the Egyptian style and consists of a 2-story main building and many two and three-story buildings that are part of this complex.

The territory owned by the hotel is striking in its well-groomed. Since the hotel complex is quite young, lush gardens have not yet been grown here, but the surroundings simply abound in different colors. There are a lot of palm trees that give the area a special charm, very well-groomed, neatly trimmed lawns.



Luxury is felt everywhere in the Resta Grand Resort 5 * hotel. Reviews of tourists confirm that, once in the hotel lobby, you can appreciate the beauty of the rich decoration. There is a lot of greenery, creating a special atmosphere, filling the room with freshness and comfort. After a long journey, tourists will like comfortable upholstered furniture on which they can sit comfortably while waiting for check-in.

Infrastructure

Guests of the Resta Grand Resort 5 * hotel will be pleased with the variety of services provided for free or for a certain amount of money. Administrators can be contacted at any time, as the reception is open around the clock. Here you can find staff who are fluent in Russian, which greatly simplifies communication. All employees are very well prepared for their work, so each guest will fully feel the care of the hotel.



Hotel Resta Grand Resort 5 * (Marsa Alam) has its own parking, which is provided to guests free of charge. Those who do not have personal transport are offered the opportunity to rent executive cars.

Laundry or dry cleaning services are available at the hotel for an extra fee, where they can quickly clean their clothes. They will also pet her, if necessary.

Business people at the hotel have all the necessary conditions for meetings or negotiations. The conference room here is equipped with all the necessary modern equipment. It can accommodate up to 280 people. There is an additional charge for using the room.

Hotel guests really like the hotel pools. In one of them, the water is heated. The second is a huge pond decorated with various bridges and other decorative elements. In the middle of the pool on the islet there is a bar. Guests can sit here on the chairs, which are located directly in the water and order a drink. The only negative that almost all hotel guests mention is the lack of toilets near the pool. If necessary, tourists have to go either to the reception or return to their rooms.

You can seek medical help at any time. The doctor will consult the patient for a fee, or prescribe the necessary medicines.

Number of rooms

The hotel Grand Resta Resort 5 * (Marsa Alam) offers guests a huge selection of rooms. Of the 748 options, preference can be given to rooms that are best suited to guests in terms of cost and space. In particular, for accommodation you can choose:

  • Standard Room.
  • Superior (Pool View Room).
  • Deluxe (Sea View Room).
  • Family Room (Garden View).
  • Junior Suite.
  • Connected Rooms.

What's in the rooms?

In any room, guests will be provided with everything necessary that may be needed during the stay. Very happy holidaymakers new furniture. There is a TV on which you can even watch Russian channels. The phone can be used for free calls at the reception, or for an additional fee to make calls abroad. The safety of valuables will be provided by a safe, which is also equipped with each room.



The apartments are equipped with individual air conditioning, which cool the rooms well. On the balcony there are chairs and a table, and on the terraces there are also sun loungers. Each room has a kitchenette, where guests are given the opportunity to make hot drinks. Tea or coffee ingredients are provided upon arrival and are not renewed.

In the bathrooms, guests can take a bath or shower, and use a hairdryer. There is a bidet. Toiletries are closely monitored. If necessary, the set is replenished.

Daily room cleaning is carried out at the proper level, and does not cause discontent among vacationers. A change of linen is carried out daily or upon request, which depends on the preferences of the guests.

Beach vacation

The weak point of the hotel, according to tourists, is the beach. It does not have a large area. Resting areas are arranged on two levels. There are a lot of sun loungers and umbrellas that guests of the Resta Grand Resort 5 * hotel (Marsa Alam) can use for free. The beach is sandy, but since corals and small pebbles are often carried out of the sea, you should not walk on it barefoot. When bathing, it is also better to use aquatic swimming pools, since the bottom of the sea is rocky.



Near the coast, the sea is very shallow and more suitable for swimming for children. Lovers of swimming can get to the depths on the pontoon located on the beach of a neighboring hotel. But here it‚Äôs unlikely that you will be able to swim in plenty, because in the area very often winds blow, raising huge waves. At the slightest suspicion of a threat to the lives of vacationers, the pontoon closes and tourists have to return to the beach.

Nutrition

Hotel guests do not have to complain about the quality of food offered here. The food at the hotel is very good. Since the all-inclusive system operates here, guests can enjoy delicious and satisfying breakfasts, lunches and dinners. The menu of the main restaurant is very diverse, so none of the guests will have problems finding the right dish. Fruit is also always enough. They are served whole or cut.



In the restaurant hall, thanks to the diligent work of the waiters, it is always clean and there are no queues. All dishes are washed well, which also pleases many guests.

Bars located on the beach, by the pool and in the hotel lobby offer their services to hotel guests. It offers a variety of snacks, delicious pastries and drinks. Also, guests can visit the three restaurants A la Carte, where you can taste dishes of cuisines from different countries or prefer seafood.

Entertainment at the hotel

So that guests at the hotel do not have to be bored during their holidays, there are various types of entertainment. You can engage in table tennis right by the pool. Aerobics and water aerobics courses are held daily with instructors. Tennis fans have the opportunity to use the court for free. But racquets and a ball are issued only for money.

Guests of the hotel can play table or five-a-side football for free, and practice darts. Fans of an active lifestyle will love the local fitness center, where you can use various exercise machines. On the beach you can play volleyball in a special area.



It is difficult to ignore water sports, which in the Resta Grand Resort 5 * hotel can be practiced only for a fee. Diving is especially popular here, because when immersed, tourists can see the amazing and beautiful underwater world of the Red Sea.

Fans of evening entertainment will also not be bored. They can visit the disco, which is held daily at the neighboring hotel. There are also often organized performances by various local creative groups.

Hotel Resta Grand Resort 5 * (Marsa Alam): traveler reviews

According to tourists who have previously visited this beautiful hotel, the rest here will be great. Responsive staff, comfortable rooms and excellent food will leave a pleasant experience. A beach vacation may be a bit disappointing, but this can be compensated by visiting various bays, which are a huge number in Marsa Alam. So everyone who visited here liked the hotel very much.
