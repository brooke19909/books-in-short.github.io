How to get there

How to get to the hotel "Caves Beach Resort" (Hurghada)? The reviews assure us that for this you should first get to the city airport. Many tourist companies make charter flights to Hurghada, so if you have a ticket, this is not a problem. From there you can get to the place where the hotel is located, by taxi (it takes about fifteen to twenty minutes), transfer (this is longer, depending on how many hotels the bus serves), as well as by minibus to El Gouna.
