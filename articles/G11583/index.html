For almost 20 years, the pond has been regularly stocked, new opportunities for outdoor activities appear. When a new section of the road was opened on the M4 highway in 2010, another roadside hotel appeared.

Today Ryabinushka complex is not only two motels with 40 rooms, but also two roadside complexes, parking lots, tire fitting, showers, laundry, cafe-dining room.
