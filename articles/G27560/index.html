Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Jumeirah (Dubai, UAE): description, rating, photos and reviews

The United Arab Emirates is an actively developing tourist destination that is gaining popularity among travelers from Russia. Dubai is not only clean sandy beaches and huge skyscrapers, but also unforgettable oriental hospitality. If you are impressed by the Arabic culture, be sure to pay attention to the hotel Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Jumeirah. Where is this hotel located?

This luxurious resort complex, built in the Arabic style and reminiscent of its appearance of the eastern palace, rises above the coast of the Persian Gulf. According to tourists, it has an extremely favorable location. The Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Hotel in Dubai is far from the bustling city streets and freeways, so it‚Äôs quiet and peaceful at any time of the day. At the same time, the complex itself is located on the first coastline, so tourists can walk to the beach in just 5 minutes. General Description of the hotel Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Jumeirah

This resort is one of Dubai's most famous hotels. It is worth noting that it is part of the huge Madinat Jumeirah complex, which includes not only other, less well-known hotels, but also numerous restaurants, cafes and other infrastructure facilities. The hotel itself is the hallmark of this complex. The translation of its name from Arabic is "harbor of the world." Indeed, the hotel is perfect for a family and leisurely holiday on the shores of the warm sea. It consists of a huge seven-story building, decorated in oriental style. Next to it is an outdoor pool of impressive size and a picturesque garden, lined not only by winding paths, but also by water channels. The hotel consists of 292 comfortable rooms. Both small and spacious apartments are available for guests.

The hotel itself was built back in 2003, when the tourist destination in Dubai was just beginning to develop. In 2015, it was completely renovated, so now the rooms here are not inferior in quality of repair to other modern complexes.

Hotel Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Jumeirah in the rankings of the best hotels in Dubai is constantly ranked high. So, according to tourists, he is in the top 50 best complexes of the city, occupying 33rd place. It is worth noting that in the city itself more than 1000 various hotels are open. Hotel guests also appreciate their stay here. In their opinion, he deserves a rating of 4.5-4.78 out of 5, which is an extremely high indicator.

More on Catering

There is no all-inclusive system at the Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Jumeirah. Upon request, tourists can pay for accommodation without meals or include only breakfast in the price. Full board is also provided - this is three meals a day.

The complex has a huge number of restaurants specializing in international, Mexican, Arabic and Greek cuisine. They work daily. In fine weather, you can dine on the outdoor terrace overlooking the ocean. On the beach is a Greek tavern.

You can enjoy a cocktail, soft drink or alcohol at one of the hotel‚Äôs many bars. They are located in the lobby, on the roof and by the pool. Some bars are open only in the evening. Visitors under the age of 21 are not allowed to attend. In addition to drinks, a hookah is also offered.

Beach holidays at the hotel

Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Jumeirah Resort has its own stretch of sandy beach. Its length is more than 4 km. Sun loungers and parasols for guests are installed along the coast. For a fee, you can rent a separate tent. In the center of water entertainment you can go diving, as well as ride on water in motorless and motor boats.

The hotel has 2 pools - outdoor and indoor. Both are filled with fresh water, the temperature of which is constantly monitored. Next to them is a comfortable terrace where you can sunbathe.

Also, guests can visit the water park, considered one of the most modern in the world, for free. Tourists are offered 31 one water slide for adults and 7 for small children. The water park is open daily until 20:00.

Sports leisure

Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Jumeirah offers a wide range of active leisure options. So, tourists can visit the gym, which is open around the clock. For guests on site are equipped with platforms for playing table tennis, basketball, beach volleyball and football. Aerobics classes are held several times a week. There is also a tennis court, but equipment rental, lessons and the inclusion of lighting are paid separately. The highlight of the complex is its own climbing wall about 6 meters high.

Other entertainment options

The Jumeirah Mina A Salam Madinat Jumeirah also has relaxing options. In their reviews, tourists recommend visiting the local health center and spa, where you can order wellness treatments for face and body skin. There is a free sauna, steam room and jacuzzi. For a fee, rehabilitation and toning massage courses are available.

Conditions for staying with small children

The hotel is focused on family vacations, so you can come here with children of any age. A set of amenities here corresponds to the status of a five star hotel. So, upon arrival in the room with the baby you will additionally receive a cradle. Its use is included in the price, so you do not need to pay for it. All restaurants are also equipped with high chairs for feeding infants. At your request, the administrator will invite a nanny to your room, but her services are paid separately.

The entertainment program also corresponds to the level of a five-star hotel. A mini club is open daily in the morning and afternoon, where children from 2 to 12 years old are accepted. In the shade is a shallow freshwater pool, equipped with fountains and safe slides. The hotel also has an outdoor playground and children's rooms in a residential building.
