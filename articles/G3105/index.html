Hourly rent: the pros and cons

Renting apartments or rooms with an hourly or daily rate is very popular among guests of the capital. Many visitors consider this accommodation option to be economically advantageous. However, private apartments adapted for the hotel business, as a rule, do not provide guests with proper systemic and qualified services. According to reviews, and the level of sanitation in such places often leaves much to be desired. In addition, mainly apartments for rent for a day are located either in residential areas or at a considerable distance from the metro. Another important factor that visitors should remember: renting rooms from private individuals in Moscow is not always safe.

Alternative

A great alternative to renting a private room or apartment for an hour is to stay in an hourly mini-hotel. Particularly famous among tourists and business travelers was the Amsterdam chain of hourly mini-hotels. This popular chain of mini-hotels "for an hour" offers guests of the capital accommodation in rooms of various capacities and prices. In any of the Amsterdam hotels in Moscow, you can comfortably stay, traveling both alone and with a group of tourists. You can reserve a place in the mini-hotel online or by phone. Visitors are given the opportunity to rent a room hourly, day or night.
