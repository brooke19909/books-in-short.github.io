The resort of Forio (Italy) is located at the foot of the extinct volcano Epomeo. The historical center of the city conveys the originality of Italian city landscapes: narrow streets, churches of the Middle Ages, Baroque style.

Here is the famous Villa Colombaya, the house where the cult director of Italy, Luchino Visconti, was engaged in creativity.

General Hotel Information

Park Hotel Terme Mediterraneo 4 * is located on a hill, a five-minute drive from Chitara Beach, which is considered one of the best on about. Ischia in Italy. The hotel provides the opportunity to book an individual transfer to Naples Airport (Kapodichino), located forty kilometers away, or to meet a guest there and deliver him to his place of residence.

Terme Mediterraneo 4 * (Ischia) is a partner of the Negombo balnearia. Another famous spa is located near the hotel, less than a kilometer from it.

Balnearium is a system of body and face care using sea water. The hotel‚Äôs thermopark is a great opportunity to take a range of spa treatments for relaxation and wellness.

Territory and infrastructure

On the territory of the hotel, which is twenty thousand square meters, there are four buildings of one- and two-story buildings.

A private tropical garden, in which buildings are buried, form rocks of rare, subtropical plantings that cannot be found in other places and hotels in Ischia.

At Terme Mediterraneo 4 * hotel guests are offered private parking, a lounge with seating, an Internet corner, high-speed Internet, and a conference room. The services of the conference room can be used when organizing field corporate events.

The hotel has many thermal pools, which together with the flora of the park creates a therapeutic effect that complements the experience of relaxation.

Rooms

Guests of the Terme Mediterraneo 4 * hotel are offered three categories of rooms, which are either in the main building, or one hundred fifty-two hundred meters away from it, in the hotel park. Namely:

  • Standard.
  • Deluxe level.
  • Chalet.

The rooms have LCD TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, heating. In the bathroom there is a bathroom, shower, bidet, hairdryer and toiletries.

Although the rooms are designed and decorated in a minimalist style, the cosiness is created by modern furniture, a minibar, air conditioning and a heating system. A large number of windows provides a feeling of spaciousness and light. Each room has a balcony or terrace. Thanks to its elevated position, the hotel offers magnificent panoramic views of the beautiful landscapes of the park and the surrounding area.

Not everyone welcomes such a feature as the location on the Terme Mediterraneo 4 * hill. Reviews of tourists contain complaints about the long rise from the sea to the hotel, especially if the vacation is with children.

Service

The concept of the hotel includes customer service in the language of the country where the guest arrived. The 24-hour front desk allows you to instantly solve questions and problems that arise for guests.

Faster registration of arrivals and departures saves vacationers time, which is not often found when serving customers in other hotels.

At the reception at the Terme Mediterraneo 4 * hotel You can buy tickets, order a transfer, get advice on tour services for visitors. You can leave things in the luggage room on the day of arrival and departure, devoting free time to walks in the hotel's park area.

Check-in at the hotel is from 13.00, check-out is until 12 noon. Facilities for disabled guests are provided here. Pets allowed upon prior agreement.

Nutrition

Meals are organized in the Terme Mediterraneo 4 * restaurant, which serves Mediterranean and local cuisine.

Breakfast is included in the price (as well as access to the thermal pools) and is provided after an overnight stay at the hotel.

During the day you can eat at the snack bar, piano bar. A diet menu is available upon prior reservation. Dinner is quite varied.

Thalassotherapy and entertainment

The beach is at a distance of about a kilometer from the hotel, amenities (sunbeds and umbrellas) on it are available at an additional cost.

Park Hotel Terme Mediterraneo 4 * - Thermal the complex, on the territory of which there are four pools with different temperature regimes, one indoor, as well as the Kneipp therapeutic pool, SPA, Therme & Beauty Wellness Center with a large selection of healing programs and treatment procedures. All pools are open from seven in the morning until seven in the evening.

To visit them you need a special hat, which can be purchased on the spot. There is a specially organized terrace for a safe tan.

Additional services:

  • Jacuzzi.
  • Sauna.
  • Massage and spa treatments.
  • Turkish sauna.
  • Solarium.

In the Terme Mediterraneo (Forio) 4 * hotel, you can order excursion programs that are widely represented by various tour operators. You can visit Mount Epomeo on your own, from the viewing platforms of which amazingly beautiful landscapes open up, they should be captured in the photo. It will be interesting to visit the Aragonese Castle (admission price is 10 euros), the Botanical Garden (admission is 12 euros).

Italy is a recognized world cultural center and the cradle of shopping. The choice of leisure options depends only on the desire of the traveler, his opportunities, as well as on free time and preferences.
