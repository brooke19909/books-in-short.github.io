Android touchscreen calibration: how to set up a touchscreen

A huge role for modern phones is played by touchscreen calibration. Android is the operating system that will be discussed. It is with her to work further. Touchscreen settings on Android can be done in several ways. Each person decides how to conduct it. But what options are possible? What you need to know about calibration? All this will be described below. In fact, everything is much simpler than it might seem at first glance.

Calibration is ...

To begin with, you have to understand what Android touchscreen calibration is. What kind of process is this? Touchscreen calibration is called screen adjustment to properly respond to commands sent by the user. Of course, you can do without it. But then over time, the smartphone will respond for a long time to the commands sent, as well as shift the press on one or another touch button.





Most often, calibration is needed:

  • after replacing the touchscreen;
  • when the smartphone falls;
  • after system failures;
  • if all gadget settings have been reset.

It doesn't matter what caused the sensor to adjust. The main thing is that the calibration of the Android touchscreen can be carried out in several ways. Which ones?

Sensor setup methods

They are not very many. But the proposed range of actions is enough so that each smartphone owner can choose the most effective, fast and simple method for setting up the sensor.

Most often, the process is carried out in the following ways:

  • through third-party specialized programs;
  • through the engineering menu;
  • in service centers.




The latter option is recommended to be postponed until better times. Usually, even a novice owner of Android devices can do the calibration. Next will be described in more detail about each method.

Engineering menu

Let's start with independent actions. How to calibrate the Android touchscreen through the engineering menu? To do this, you must adhere to some algorithm of actions. Usually this method is the fastest, but not always effective. Calibration of the Android touchscreen through the engineering menu requires the following actions:

  • Turn on your mobile device. You must wait until the operating system is fully loaded. If the gadget is already turned on, it is better to complete the work with all previously active applications and games.
  • Go to the device settings.
  • Select the menu item "Display" in the list that appears.
  • Examine proposed operations. At the bottom of the list will be the inscription "G-sensor calibration". You must click on this line.
  • Put the smartphone on a flat surface. This is important for proper device setup.
  • Click on the "Calibrate" button. Follow the onscreen instructions. Wait a bit.
  • Reboot your smartphone.




That's all. Such calibration of the Android touchscreen after replacing the sensor or simply if necessary is subject to each owner of a mobile phone. As already mentioned, this method is not always effective. For example, if you do not put the device on a flat surface, then there may be a malfunction when configuring the sensor. Then it is recommended to give preference to another method.

3rd party applications

How is Android touchscreen calibrated? A program for configuring the sensor is what helps to solve the task. As already mentioned, third-party software is also often used for calibration. This solution is not the safest, but extremely effective.

How can I set up the touch screen on Android? To do this, you have to:

  • Find and download a touchscreen calibration program. The following is a list of proven and safe software for translating ideas into reality.
  • Download the appropriate application to your mobile device.
  • Install this or that program in which the Android touchscreen will be calibrated.
  • Close all active applications, programs and games on the smartphone. Run software for calibration.
  • Press the "Start / Calibrate" button. Labels may vary depending on the application used. Before this, it is recommended, as in the previous case, to lay the device on a flat surface.
  • Wait for the process to complete. It is recommended that you restart your smartphone after finishing the sensor setup.




Quick, easy, convenient! This option for customizing the gadget is used by many users.
