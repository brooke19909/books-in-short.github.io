Which batteries are better - Durasel or Energizer: specifications, comparison, reviews

When two or more competing firms are represented on the battery market, the consumer naturally questions about their advantages and disadvantages. From this article it will be possible to get detailed characteristics and indicators of the two companies and find out the answer to the question of which batteries are better - Durasel or Energizer.

Batteries: what is it?

We do not hesitate to turn on the TV from the remote control, find out the time by the working wall clock, use a desktop calculator, and for us this happens naturally. But these devices work with the help of small elements, the existence of which greatly facilitates life and saves our time.

And of course, talk about the battery. It is a power source that produces electricity. And this happens under the influence of the ongoing chemical process. The principle of action is based on the use of a chemical reaction of two metals (possibly their oxides) in an electrolyte with the simultaneous occurrence of an electromotive force. Since such reactions are irreversible, they are called "primary".

Types of batteries

The labeling of chemical power sources occurs in accordance with the composition of the electrolyte and active metal, which is used in their device. According to this parameter, there are five types of round (cylinder-shaped) batteries, but two can be considered as the most common and often used:

1. Saline. Made from inactive coal. The peculiarity lies in the fact that when a break in activity takes place, the batteries are recreated. This helps extend battery life.

2. Alkaline (alkaline). Here, an alkaline electrolyte acts as a chemical element. This type of battery can be stored for a very long time. During use, the voltage at the electrodes changes to a much lesser extent than salt ones.

Energizer Batteries Characteristics

One of the most famous companies in the field of creating batteries is Energizer. The logo of this company is known all over the world. The products are divided into the following groups: Ultimate Lithium, Maximum, Plus and Alkaline.

Alkaline batteries work stably at low temperatures, have high charge capacity. They also have a very small degree of leakage and a long storage time. Features of the Plus class is the innovative PowerSeal technology, characterized in that it retains charge up to 10 years. This long period allows you to have no doubt in the reliability of the source used.

Maximum batteries are created in accordance with PowerBoost technology and are characterized as the most durable in the Energizer alkaline battery line; their battery life is 70% longer. Produced in all five sizes.

Durasel batteries: specifications

In many ways, the American company Durasel received the title of world leader in the production of high-quality batteries and accumulators. The initial batteries produced by it were mercury sources. They were more often used in military affairs. In the future, Durasel regularly created batteries using innovative technologies that were far ahead of competitors in terms of performance.

Durasel occupies approximately one fourth of the market in this area, but their popularity has declined over time due to the increasing distribution of competing rechargeable batteries. This company produces three main classes of alkaline batteries (alkaline): Basic, Professional and Turbo Max.

Battery Comparison by Specifications

After studying all the tests to which the power sources of these companies were subjected, it can be noted that the high price is not necessarily high quality. Which batteries are better, Durasel or Energizer, cannot be answered without considering their technical characteristics.

  • The first indicator to evaluate is how quickly the batteries drain when they are continuously discharged.   • The second indicator to evaluate is how quickly the batteries drain when a powerful, but short-term (pulsed) discharge occurs. This is an imitation of the work of any gadgets that are characterized by such a discharge, such as a digital camera with a flash.
