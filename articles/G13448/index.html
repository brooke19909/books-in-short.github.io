Belek Soho Beach Club Hotel: paradise on Earth





Belek Soho Beach Club is located just two kilometers from the world famous Belek village. Here tourists from all over the world relax. The resort was built in 2006, then it was called Poseidon. Two years ago, Belek Soho Beach Club was completely rebuilt and turned into a real paradise. Here you can find a truly royal refuge and lovers of living in comfortable bungalows, and those who prefer to live in ultramodern rooms. Around Belek Soho Beach Club there is everything that is interesting for tourists: markets, shops, shopping centers. Right at the hotel and in Belek you can buy tickets for a variety of excursions, near and far. Belek Soho Beach Club can be reached in less than an hour from the airport, just 35 kilometers away.

Rooms

Belek Soho Beach Club 5 today is forty comfortable bungalows (2 floors) and one modern multi-dimensional building. By their design, both the rooms and the bungalows are almost the same. Each of them has a balcony or terrace, television with Russian channels, comfortable bathrooms with everything you need.





Beach

The local beach is 500 meters from the well-equipped area with free sun loungers and towels. Animators entertain almost around the clock, sports fields are also constantly open. Belek Soho Beach Club has a bar on the beach.

Nutrition

Owners position the hotel as Ultra All Inclusive (ultra). It provides meals in the main restaurant (European cuisine), nightly snacks, snacks in the beach, lobby and disco bars. Once upon reservation, tourists can enjoy free Turkish cuisine in the on-site à la carte restaurant.

But tourists will have to pay for imported spirits, which does not quite correspond to the Ultra prefix. However, the food is arranged in such a way that in bars and restaurants there is enough local drink. These establishments work so that tourists can get food at any time.

Information for vacationers

The service at Belek Soho Beach Club HV is excellent. Among the free offers, it is worth mentioning two huge swimming pools with slides for adults, SPA, sauna and Turkish bath, several types of aerobics and the ability to use sports halls and playgrounds. For children, a kids club, playgrounds also work for free, a menu and special comfortable chairs are provided. For a small surcharge, you can use the services of massage therapists and hairdressers, makeup artists. You can rent canoes, jet skis, tennis accessories and much more. The territory is constantly and thoroughly cleaned, and the pools are cleaned.





Reviews

Visitors note the main advantages of the hotel: a luxurious green area, excellent staff work and a well-equipped beach. According to tourists, despite the vast territory, everything is located within walking distance. The hotel rests local families, the Dutch, the French. Therefore, it is possible to make interesting acquaintances. Tourists also like the opportunity to spend time outside the beach. In the hotel itself and in neighboring Belek, there are all possibilities for this, from the availability of travel agencies to the possibility of renting a car. A distinctive feature of the hotel: many tourists, having been here once, come back again and again. Many like the friendly staff, while others appreciate its well-trained and obligatory nature. Without exception, everyone praises food: even spoiled gourmets will be able to find a suitable dish.
