Unawatuna, Sri Lanka. Sri Lanka, Unawatuna - hotels. Unawatuna (Sri Lanka) on the map

For many years, the resort of Unawatuna (Sri Lanka) has caused great interest among tourists. This is a very beautiful place and a calm beach resort, which is located 5 km from the city of Galle. The country's authorities are making great efforts to restore the resort to its former reputation, which suffered greatly after the powerful 2004 tsunami. The entire tourist infrastructure - hotels, shops, restaurants - was pretty quickly rebuilt. Now Unawatuna is quite ready to receive guests at the highest level.



General characteristics of the resort

From Halle, this paradise can be reached by the coastal southern road in 10 minutes. If you look at Unawatuna (Sri Lanka) on the map, you can see that it is 76 miles from the capital of Colombo. The main advantages of this village are its magnificent beaches, an abundance of fruit trees and palm groves.

Resort beaches

On both sides of the reef strip, the ocean and the local lagoon are separated, so there are rarely large waves in this place, which makes swimming quite safe. At the same time, a strong current will not allow you to calmly swim far from the coast on a mattress. You can safely come to the resort with children, as there are shallow areas on the beach that are protected by reefs even from small waves. The depth in such places is also small - about 1.5 meters.



Sri Lanka, Unawatuna Beach is a favorite place for surfers, divers and just lovers of swimming. The water here is always crystal clear, which makes this place quite popular. In addition, in the vicinity of the beach, you can contemplate the rich flora and fauna, in particular a huge number of birds: a whistling duck, a white heron, a water chicken, a sandbox, a rock-stove and others. Many of them live exclusively on the hill of Rumassala.

Divers are particularly attracted to coral reefs, a variety of fish and turtles, and traces of shipwrecks. Previously, the surrounding areas where mango forests and swamps were located were often visited by ecotourists. True, now most of the swamps are drained, and hotels have been built in their place. A ridge of restaurants and cafes stretches along the beach line, which in addition to food and drink also offer tourists umbrellas and sunbeds.

Sri Lanka, Unawatuna: hotels

This picturesque village for recreation is better for lovers of measured and relaxing holiday. There are no pompous hotels in Unawatut, and if you wish, you can settle in local hotels on your own. The village has mainly three- and four-star hotels.

Flower Garden 3 *. This is a wonderful and very cozy mini-hotel. It consists of chalet houses and an administrative building, located in a magnificent tropical park. The beaches are only a few minutes from here. Each house has its own garden and terrace, deck chair and wicker furniture. The rooms are decorated in a tropical style with all amenities.

Calamander Unawatuna Beach 4 *. This hotel is located on the 1st line. The hotel is represented by standard rooms and suites with a jacuzzi. On the territory of Calamander there is a swimming pool, tennis court, restaurant.



Dalawella Beach Resort 3 *. This is a small but very comfortable hotel with upscale service. The hotel has only 7 rooms, which have air conditioning, hot water, telephone, balcony and much more. There is also the opportunity to settle in a small house with a veranda. Local beaches are just a short walk away.

Lavendish Beach 2 *. This hotel is located in a quiet and very scenic area overlooking the Indian Ocean. It features internet, a 24-hour front desk, laundry service, a bar and a restaurant. All rooms are equipped with a minibar, air conditioning, fan, TV and necessary furniture. And the luxurious suites feature a jacuzzi with stunning ocean views.

Meanwhile, you can choose other, no less comfortable hotels, the benefit of them in abundance on the island of Sri Lanka. Unawatuna (hotels is not the only type of housing in the village) can offer a guesthouse (housing in a private house with local residents), a hostel (special amenities are not provided, but the price can be quite good) or a motel (suitable for vacationers with a car, however, the level service is unlikely to please).

Unawatuna Restaurants

On the entire beach you can dine in the many eateries and cafes. The bars and restaurants are dominated by European and Sinhalese cuisine. A buffet spread in almost all hotels, but remember that local dishes are very spicy. With alcohol, you need to be extremely careful, many of them have a quick intoxicating effect and a strong hangover effect.



Resort Attractions

Unawatuna (Sri Lanka) is not only a passive beach holiday. You can diversify your trip with walks in the surrounding jungle, where you can see many interesting representatives of exotic flora and fauna. An hour away from the village is a UNESCO reserve - Kottava. It is an ancient fortress with bastions and impressive walls. You can also go diving, surfing, fishing or snorkeling.

Entertainment

Unawatuna (Sri Lanka) is a great place to relax with your family. In the evenings, on the coast, which is bent by a horseshoe, restaurants, jewelry shops, cafes light their many lights. The sounds of pleasant music invite you to a disco where you can dance and enjoy some exotic cocktail.

We assure you that you will definitely enjoy your vacation in this paradise. It is also worth noting that, according to Discovery, the best beach is located here - the island of Sri Lanka, Unawatuna. Reviews of tourists directly confirm this fact. This cozy village is happy to welcome guests all year round.
