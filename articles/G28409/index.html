Hotel Electra Palace Rhodes 5 * (Rhodes, Greece): Tourist reviews

Electra Palace Rhodes is one of the most interesting hotels in Greece. It was built back in 1974, but reconstruction was carried out in 2007, so the hotel is modern and original. It is notable for its art deco style, which also used Italian elements. However, style is not the only feature of the hotel.





Location

Electra Palace Rhodes is located on the island of Rhodes. And to be more precise, in a city called Ialyssos. It is located in the northwest of the island. The international airport is 10 minutes by car from the hotel, which is very convenient. By the way, from the hotel at your fingertips. After all, the building is located on the coast.

Ialyssos, by the way, is an extremely popular city. Rhodes, the capital, is 8 kilometers from it. And the city itself is famous not only for its rich history and appearance in the 1500s BC, but also for its climate and weather conditions. It is an ideal place for sailing and various types of surfing. After all, powerful north-westerly winds always reign here , catching big waves. That is why Ialysos holds international championships in water sports.

Entertainment

At Electra Palace Rhodes there are a huge number of ways to brighten up your leisure time. There is an indoor pool, a gym, a private beach (the main tourist attraction), bike rental, darts, billiards, karaoke and even a library. Also every evening there are animated shows and parties. By the way, there is tennis (both table and full). And for the kids that their parents took with them on vacation, there is a separate playground and a mini club.

Lovers of relaxation with both soul and body will definitely enjoy the presence of a SPA center in the hotel. There you can devote time to health and beauty treatments, visit the massage room, take a steam bath in the Turkish bath and sauna, soak in the jacuzzi. However, the list of services offered there is quite impressive. Arriving at the SPA-salon, guests will be able to familiarize themselves with it and choose a procedure that will appeal to them.





Service and Facilities

In Electra Palace Rhodes 5 * there is absolutely everything that can be required for comfort. Free Wi-Fi, public parking near the hotel, storage room, currency exchange, 24/7 reception are just a small list of services.

Guests can call a nanny to look after their child, use the laundry and dry cleaning service, ironing, contact a separate office to send / receive a fax or photocopy documents.

On the territory there is a mini market, a common lounge, shops (including souvenir) and even a car rental point. Do not want to rent a car? Then you can order a transfer. Here such an opportunity is provided. If guests wish, they can arrange room service for drinks and food or prepare packed lunches for the trip. Non-smoking and soundproofed apartments are also available. And also the service "Wake-up call" is available to guests.

Speaking about the service, one cannot fail to note the level of staff training. The people working here speak not only Greek, but also English, German, French and Russian.





Nutrition

In Electra Palace Rhodes 5 * (Rhodes) there are three magnificent restaurants that treat their guests with delicious and fresh dishes. All the people who have been here assure: the food is really excellent. There are Greek, Chinese and Italian restaurants.

Guests say that everything is very diverse. They serve seafood, mussels, shell snails, meat, fish, poultry, delicious desserts and even a milk pig. There are goat cheeses, natural yogurts, juicy fruits. For breakfast, hot omelettes, fried eggs, pancakes are always served (all this is cooked directly by visitors). If you do not want this, you can taste fried bacon, cottage cheese, potatoes or cereal with milk, washed down with delicious juice. They also serve stewed and fresh vegetables (but already for lunch), delicious rice, salads. You can taste grilled dishes, barbecue, shrimp and even octopus.

There are also soups. Baking, cakes, desserts - all this is offered in large quantities. And a lot of delicious ice cream. The only caveat - coffee with tea is served only for breakfast. But it is in a restaurant. In bars, these drinks are served throughout the day. By the way, even in unlimited quantities, guests can drink red and white wine, as well as beer. In general, it will not work to stay hungry here.





Classic economist

This is the first category of rooms that I would like to touch upon. In Electra Palace Rhodes Resort 5 * they are quite popular. Largely due to its relatively low price.

The room is 25 square meters. There are two single beds next to each other. There is a plasma TV with satellite channels, a balcony, and also such trifles as a radio, telephone, air conditioning, safe. It is possible to connect heating. The spacious, clean and bright bathroom has a bathtub with shower, hairdryer, bathrobes with slippers for each guest, as well as small hygiene kits with French cosmetics: shower gels, body lotions, shampoos, razors, and liquid soap. There is also a fridge and an electric kettle.

A week of living in such apartments for two will cost about 35-38 thousand rubles (with half board). However, prices often differ. Depending on the season. Sometimes you can save 20-30%. It is worth knowing about it.

By the way, apartments with sea views will cost about 47 thousand rubles. These rooms besides the landscape opening from a balcony can brag of even more magnificent interior.
