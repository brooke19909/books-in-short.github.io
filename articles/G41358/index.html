Goldfish costume: materials and stages of work

Today, many children's clothing stores present a wide variety of carnival costumes. Only their purchase can result in a decent amount, and the child flaunts in this outfit only once. Therefore, we propose to make a costume of the Goldfish yourself.



Outfit components

The easiest way to sew a cloth outfit. Even to mothers who are not very versed in sewing, this task will be on the shoulder. It is important that the material is selected correctly. Well and, of course, fantasy is required. Start by choosing the right clothes in your wardrobe. Supplementing it with a few individual details, you will receive a costume of the Goldfish.

So, the main components of the outfit should be a turtleneck and a short skirt. The preferred colors for them are white, yellow, orange, beige, that is, light shades. At the next stage of work, we will manufacture tail for fish, fins, crowns.



Stages of work on a children's costume "Goldfish"

Remember that this fabulous heroine is golden. So, sequins, sequins, fabric with lurex - these are the materials that you do not need to spare. Everything will come in handy for work.

To make a crown, you need cardboard. After cutting the part from it, use for foil pasting with a golden color. You can make a crown of colored cardboard. In this case, yellow will look good. Glitter will be the decoration of this detail.





So, our work on the Goldfish costume continues. Getting to the fins. For their manufacture, light fabric is needed. Perhaps in the home stocks there are scraps of chiffon, pieces of organza. Well, if there is both. The edge of this part does not have to be flat and have the correct shape. As for the length of the fins, it is measured individually for each child.

This will hide it and make wider the place where the fin parts will be sewn. Sew fabric stitches. Work on the fin is over.



We continue to craft the costume of the Goldfish with our own hands. According to the same scheme, you need to make another fin. Find the old belt. He will go on to make a fish tail. Sew pieces of light and transparent fabric to it. We will tie the belt in front. Put fins on the turtleneck, tie the tail, put on the crown, pick up elegant shoes. Admire, do you like the do-it-yourself Goldfish costume?
