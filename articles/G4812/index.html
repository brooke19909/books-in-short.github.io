Interested in how to sew a zipper into a skirt? You will find the answer in this article.

Initially, in the wardrobe of women there were only dresses, tunics, tunics. Skirts first appeared in the 15th century. Before that there was a certain robe that was gathered at the throat, and its length was up to the knees. Both men and women wore such clothes. The first skirts were very heavy, wide, long and made up of several layers of fabric. In addition, the ladies had to tuck a few underwear under them.

Over time, the silhouette of clothes changed, and products appeared more convenient: narrow, pencils, flared, with frills and others. All modern skirts usually have a zipper. Here she often fails her mistress. But if you know how to sew a zipper into a skirt, then you can fix your clothes at home at any time.



Preparing the skirt for repair

Before stitching the zipper, you need to get rid of the old clasp. You will also need to prepare the product for work. First you need to gently flip the old broken zipper with a blade. Usually there are threads on the seams. They need to be removed and the seams themselves steamed. Many are interested in how to sew ordinary zipper. This is very easy to do.

Steps for stitching the zipper

  • Bend the side seams along the fold line. Usually after tearing up, small traces from the machine line remain.
  • From the inside to the curved side seams, it will be necessary to catch a new zipper manually with centimeter stitches. Begin work necessarily from above, and not vice versa. The first stitch must be 0.3-0.4 mm higher than the teeth. First, one side is swept, then the other in the same way. If you know how to sew a zipper, then everything can be done quickly.
  • If the zipper on the skirt is at the back, then one side (usually the left) should be visible near the teeth (the distance should not exceed 2 mm), and the other (right) at a distance from them (about 1 cm). And if you are interested in how to sew a zipper into a skirt, if it is on the side, then it is also easy. The distance from the cloves should be the same - about 1 cm.
  • Then you need to see if everything is swept correctly and if there are creases. It is important to pay attention to the fact that the ends of the zipper are at the same level. If the work done is satisfactory, then you can start grinding by car.
  • The zipper can be sewn with a regular presser foot. Only if you perform work near the cloves, then it can slip, due to which the seam will turn out uneven. It is better to use a special device in such work.

Zipper Sewing Technology

Sometimes it seems to many that they know how to sew a zipper. But when they sit down at work, everything turns out not as we would like. In this case, as well as in any other, there are some tricks.

  • You need to start stitching from above. After the machine needle reaches the turn, you need to raise the foot and turn the seam line 90 degrees.
  • Then lower the foot and make a few stitches to the next seam.
  • Raise the foot again and turn the product upward again by 90 degrees and continue sewing.

The final step in sewing on the zipper

When the fastener is stitched, you need to remove the manual overlock stitches. How to sew a zipper into a skirt is now clear. Next, the side seams must be ironed. If the skirt is made of woolen or other dense fabrics, then it is better to use gauze or a steam iron. If the material is cotton or linen, then you can iron with a conventional, well-heated appliance.

Then it remains only to grind back the belt, if it was, or fix it on the back side of the grind.

Interested in how to sew a zipper into a skirt, if it is secret? Here, too, have their own technological features.

  • Zipper should be swept to the side seams on the front side. It is important to straight into the fold of the fabric. After basting, you should try to fasten the zipper and check whether everything is done at the same level.
  • If everything suits you, then you can proceed to stitching. In this work, use only the special foot. The seam should be close to the teeth, otherwise there will be an impression that it diverges.

If everything is done neatly and correctly, then the zipper will not be visible when it is fastened. It remains only to fix the trim, if the skirt is without a belt. If he was, then we are grinding him to his former place. Now you can put on your favorite skirt again and go on business.
