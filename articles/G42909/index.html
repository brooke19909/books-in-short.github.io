Spanish real estate: profitable purchase of an apartment in Barcelona



Overseas property is an excellent investment for Russians. Many citizens are interested in Spanish, Greek, Italian apartments for subsequent rental. But beautiful Barcelona and other cities conquer tourists with its picturesque places and attractions. Therefore, many people remain abroad, buying houses and apartments for permanent residence.

The state of the Spanish real estate market is encouraging for potential buyers. The lowest price growth rates were observed in local cities about eight years ago. But now there is no longer the past rise in the value of houses and apartments. The Russians are trying to find the most profitable option as soon as possible, but analysts are sure that in the next five years you can take your time. No drastic changes in prices for Spanish property are foreseen. Housing will become increasingly attractive from an investment point of view. According to the law, after five years of living in Spain, a Russian can obtain permanent residence, and after another ten years apply for the citizenship of this country.

Spanish real estate can be divided into urban and resort. The latter is especially popular with wealthy foreigners. Not everyone can afford a country house or villa in Marbella or the Balearic Islands. Property in Barcelona successfully combines developed infrastructure with picturesque places and cultural attractions. The Spanish government is not against foreign investors. On the contrary, Russians are always welcome here with hospitality and offer optimal conditions for the acquisition of real estate.
