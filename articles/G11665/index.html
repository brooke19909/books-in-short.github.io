DIY crafts from a zucchini

All children, and adults too, are always interested in making a toy themselves. A self-made product is original and exclusive. This craft can be presented as a gift.

To do this, it is good to choose material that is affordable and inexpensive. Vegetables as material for crafts are becoming increasingly popular. Zucchini is well suited as a material for creativity. It is moderately hard and not too soft, but such as needed. In addition, it does not turn yellow on the cut and has a diverse skin color.

Zucchini crafts such as various animals and fish are very common. For example, a shark can be quickly made by cutting its mouth and eyes in a vegetable. The parts cut off from a smaller zucchini or cucumber are attached using matches or toothpicks, fins are obtained.
