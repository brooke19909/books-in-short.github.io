Overview of the largest smartphones

Not everyone distinguishes between a tablet and a large smartphone. Often in stores there are options that are difficult to attribute to the latter category. A few years ago, the phablet was a smartphone, whose screen exceeded the figure of 4.6 inches. To date, this parameter has grown a lot, although there is no official data that can be used to determine the largest smartphone.

Many experts take the lower range of 6 inches, the upper - 7. You should consider an important nuance: what gives the owner a large-screen phone ? More expensive models allow you to use the device as a tablet or smartphone at the same time. Every day on the technique of this kind is growing in popularity. In the article, we will consider the most successful models and highlight the best smartphone with a large diagonal.

The initial modification has a working storage of 4 GB, and the oldest - 6 GB. Each of them is equipped with integrated memory. In the first case, it is limited to 128 GB, in the second - 256 GB. The rear camera has an excellent matrix (16 megapixels), the front optics is equipped with a 5 megapixel sensor. It is possible to work with 2 cards of a mobile operator of the "nano" type. The battery received a capacity of 4400 mAh. The average cost is $ 850.

This phone, according to some consumers, was called the smartphone with the largest screen. The device looks interesting. Due to the lack of special frames near the display, it does not seem too overall. For the practicality and reliability of the gadget, the developers used a metal case.
