The best hotels of St. Petersburg: overview, addresses, description

St. Petersburg is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia. Here, the spirit of history organically merges with the trends of modernity. Thousands of foreign and domestic tourists come here every year. The best hotels in St. Petersburg will provide them with a welcoming meeting, comfortable living conditions and a high level of service.

Hotel "Alex"

"Alex" is a network of romantic hotels, which are considered to be one of the best hotels in St. Petersburg for newlyweds. A unique romantic style, a high level of service and a wide range of services are all designed to make the most important day in the life of lovers unforgettable.

At the moment, the hotel chain "Alex" in St. Petersburg has 13 branches in different parts of the city. The single reservation center is located on Ropshinskaya street, 19/40. Guests are offered the following accommodation options:

  • Stylish wedding suite with a jacuzzi - 6000 rubles.
  • Designer wedding comfort with a jacuzzi - 5500 rubles.
  • Laconic standard - 4500 rubles.

In addition to comfortable accommodation, newlyweds can use the following services:

  • meals in a restaurant or room service;
  • romantic decoration of the room;
  • bath and cosmetic accessories in the room;
  • visit to the solarium.

Reviews

Hotel "Alex" in St. Petersburg received such positive comments:

  • there is a jacuzzi in the room;
  • interesting design of rooms;
  • comfortable wide beds;
  • operational service (just call the reception desk and they will immediately bring everything you need to your room);
  • Perfect cleanliness in the rooms.

Hotel Prestige

The three-star Prestige Hotel in St. Petersburg is located in the very center of the city. It is located on Gorokhovaya Street 5. From here, you can walk to the Hermitage, Palace Square, St. Isaac's Cathedral, the Winter Palace and many other attractions. Business people are attracted by the convenient location relative to the Manege exhibition hall and the Lenexpo exhibition halls.

The hotel is equipped in a historic building, which was once the home of Count Orlov-Davydov. It was in this house that composer Mikhail Glinka completed his famous opera Ruslan and Lyudmila.

Here you can hold a business or a festive event, as well as just relax, imbued with the atmosphere of art and your beloved city.

Boutique Hotel "Alexander House"

The rating of St. Petersburg hotels worthy of the attention of travelers quite deservedly included the Alexander House Apart-Hotel. This is a private guesthouse with a rich history. The building was built in 1826, and the new life of the hotel began in 2003. The hotel is located on 27 Kryukova Canal Embankment. This is a historical district of the city in close proximity to the Nikolsky and Trinity Cathedrals, the Mariinsky Theater, the Rimsky-Korsakov Conservatory.

To accommodate guests, the following options are provided:

  • "Beijing" - a cozy bright room equipped with wicker Chinese furniture.
  • Nairobi is a room with a romantic atmosphere. The main feature is a large four-poster bed, as well as African decor.
  • Stockholm is a simple, elegant room located on the attic. The main emphasis in the decor is wooden beams on the ceiling and on the walls.
  • "Copenhagen" - in terms of layout and style reminds of the previous category.
  • "Bali" is a gentle and romantic room, the design of which is dominated by floral motifs.
  • "Venice" - the room is distinguished by an elaborate interior. It is decorated with a four-poster bed and reproductions of paintings.
  • Mallorca is a bright, comfortable room, decorated with natural wood panels, as well as wallpaper with a floral motif.

Reviews

A review of St. Petersburg hotels, and in particular Alexander House, would not be complete without traveler reviews. Here are some positive comments they leave about the institution in question:

  • atmospheric hotel with original cozy rooms;
  • good location with respect to many attractions, as well as business facilities;
  • the friendly attitude of the staff towards the guests;
  • delicious, hearty and varied breakfasts.
