A few words about the resort

Goynuk is located, as mentioned above, next to the popular Turkish resort of Kemer. To be more precise, only seven kilometers separate them from each other. The Toros mountains, which cover the village as if in a half-ring, reliably protect its territory from all kinds of winds, and therefore the climate here throughout the year is very, very conducive to relaxing on the seashore. The mountains themselves are covered with ancient pine forests, while the village is dominated by orange and pomegranate groves. In the late nineties, the village was divided in two by a highway leading directly to Antalya, which, together with the close proximity of Kemer, contributed to the heyday of the village of Goynuk (Turkey). The hotels here began to grow one after another, as a result of which, over the course of ten years, the unremarkable area acquired the status of a successful fashionable resort.

... and about hotels

In the resort of Goynuk (Turkey), hotels were erected on a grand scale, and therefore many of them have a five-star status. Almost all hotels of this category are located on the first coastline, which is extremely convenient, since vacationers do not need to get to the sea for a long time. 5-10 minutes of leisurely walking - and you are already on the beach. There are not so many four-star hotels here, and they are all located already on the second line - that is, somewhat removed from the sea. Nevertheless, 4 * hotels (Goynuk) are also quite in demand, because, by and large, a short walk through the beautiful resort town does not cause any protest. Especially when you consider that there are a lot of various shops and cafes along the way where you can have a wonderful time - do some shopping or just sit at the table, delighting yourself with a glass of tart wine or a cup of delicious coffee.
