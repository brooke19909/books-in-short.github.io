"Petrovsky Dvorik": address, description of rooms, services, infrastructure, photos and reviews

Petrovsky Yard is an amazing hotel designed in the style of the royal estate. This country hotel will give you an unforgettable vacation in an old classic atmosphere. This place is suitable for a family weekend in nature, and for celebrations or corporate events.

Location

Boutique Hotel Petrovsky Dvorik is located 40 km from the Moscow Ring Road and 10 km from Sergiev Posad in the picturesque village of Vozdvizhenskoe 76.

Travelers are advised to visit the following nearby attractions:

  • The Exaltation Church (1 km) is an outstanding work of the Moscow Empire, built in 1898 by order of Mukhanova.
  • Radonezh (2 km) is one of the most beautiful places in Russia.
  • Pokrovsky Khotkov Convent (9 km) is the oldest monastery in the Moscow Region, located on the high bank of the Pazha River.
  • Toy Museum of the Russian Academy of Education (14 km) - founded in 1918 by artist Bartram.
  • Holy Trinity Sergius Lavra (15 km) - a monastery founded by Sergius of Radonezh. It is distinguished by its beauty and rich decoration.
  • Gremyachiy Klyuch Waterfall (37 km) - three springs, beating from a slope at an altitude of 25 m.

Hotel Restaurant

On the second floor of the Petrovsky Dvorik Hotel there is a luxurious restaurant. It consists of the main hall, where you can listen to live music and sing in karaoke, as well as cozy VIP rooms, where you can enjoy your meal in a quiet secluded atmosphere.

In the main dining room there are two aquariums with sea water - for sturgeons and for lobsters. The hall seats up to 70 people, which makes it possible to hold celebrations and corporate parties here. The presence of a large projection screen allows you to demonstrate presentation slides.

A unique furnace deserves special attention. It was brought from Germany and tiled with tiles from the time of Peter I. Thanks to the chamotte interior, the taste of dishes cooked in the oven is as authentic as possible.

The menu presents traditional Russian dishes, as well as European cuisine:

Aquaclub

The Petrovsky Dvorik Hotel has its own aquaclub, which includes the following options:

  • Turkish sauna. This is an oriental-style steam room with a high level of humidity. The floor heating function allows you to maintain a constant air temperature at the level of 45-50 degrees. The aroma of essential oils gives tone, a sense of vitality and uplifting.
  • Sauna with a Mironov stove. The bottom line is the gradual and even distribution of heat in the steam room. Thus, an increase in air temperature does not become stressful for the body, but acts gently.
  • Cedar barrel. This is a mini-steam room in which steam is circulated, enriched with essential oils and plant extracts. A constant 100% humidity and a temperature of 65 degrees are maintained. This procedure is suitable for everyone - from childhood to old age (even those who are contraindicated in visiting regular doubles).
  • Sea water pool. Salt water charges the body with negative ions, which relieve stress and normalize metabolism. Due to the increased iodine content, memory is normalized, and brain activity is activated. Bathing in salt water helps keep your skin fit and supple.
  • Salt room. A halochamber is a room whose walls and ceiling are finished with salt slabs. A unique microclimate is created in the room (temperature, humidity and pressure), thanks to which the salt molecules are activated and begin their therapeutic effect on the body. The procedure helps to strengthen immunity, increase efficiency, get rid of stress, and treat chronic fatigue syndrome.

Negative reviews

Not without negative reviews. At the Petrovsky Dvorik Hotel, guests notice such shortcomings:

  • insufficiently thorough cleaning - there is a lot of dust and small debris under the furniture;
  • the rooms and the hotel as a whole have a lot of minor scuffs, so it's time to make redecorating;
  • limited time free access to the aqua complex for guests;
  • old shabby plumbing in the bathroom;
  • on wedding days, corporate parties and banquets, eating in a restaurant is almost impossible (and such events are held quite often);
  • upon arrival, booked rooms are usually not prepared (you have to expect check-in at least half an hour);
  • the rooms have a lot of minor breakdowns that employees do not eliminate, despite the comments of the guests;
  • It would not hurt to install safes in the rooms;
  • the rooms that border the restaurant and utility rooms are very noisy;
  • in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is no infrastructure or interesting places for walking;
  • the drain in the shower and in the wash basin does not work well;
  • the bathroom is very cold;
  • the bathroom has mold;
  • there is no garbage bin in the room (only in the bathroom, but it is very small);
  • the room has few free sockets for charging gadgets;
  • the rooms clearly smell tobacco smoke;
  • permanent overlays with reservation (when ordering a room of a certain category, you cannot be sure that you will be lodged in it);
  • high prices for accommodation and related services;
  • for two guests, only one pair of slippers is provided;
  • too low air temperature in the bath;
  • no bathrobes in the rooms;
