Hotel Soho Hotel Apartments 3 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus): overview, rooms and reviews

The most diverse vagaries of travelers can satisfy the resort complex Soho Hotel Apartments 3 * . Ayia Napa will reveal to you all the benefits of a Greek holiday.

Location

The convenient location attracts tourists Soho Hotel Apartments. Ayia Napa, Cavo Greco 22, is the resort's address. It is located just 5 minutes from the beach. The distance from the main street of the resort is only 1 km. Speaking about the nearest tourist sites, it is worth noting the following:

  • sculpture park (1 km);
  • bridge of lovers (1 km);
  • seaport (4 km);
  • Seer Elijah Church (4 km);
  • national park (5 km);
  • Sunrise Beach (5 km);
  • Konnos Beach (5 km);
  • water park (7 km);
  • Agia Thekla Beach (9 km).




Double standard room

Of the 30 guest rooms, Soho Hotel Apartments (3 stars) has 24 standard double rooms. Guests can stay in the bedroom, which is equipped with a French or twin beds. In the hallway area there is a hanger for outerwear, as well as an entrance to the bathroom. The room has a fully equipped kitchen with appliances, utensils and a dining area.





Studio

For a family vacation, Soho Hotel Apartments 3 * is an excellent studio. Soho Hotel offers guests to stay in spacious two-room rooms. The bedroom is equipped with a French bed and a desk. There is also a spacious living room with soft sofas, which can also be used to accommodate additional guests. Separately allocated dining area and a full kitchen.





Room Amenities

Equipped with a full range of all necessary amenities, the rooms of the Soho Hotel Apartments 3 * hotel. Ayia Napa always offers only a quality and comfortable stay. The following options are available in the apartments:

  • combined bathroom, which includes a toilet, wash basin, bath or shower, as well as a set of bath accessories;
  • kitchen area with appliances and utensils;
  • furnished balcony (on the ground floor there is a terrace);
  • individual air conditioning;
  • central heating system;
  • TV with satellite channels and a player for laser discs;
  • landline telephone (domestic and international).

General impression

Soho Hotel Apartments 3 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) can be considered quite a worthy economical accommodation option in Cyprus. The relatively low prices for accommodation and other services can be explained by not the most advantageous location, as well as a very outdated number of rooms. Thus, lovers to spend a lot of time on site this option will not work.

The main advantage of this hotel is that the room has a full kitchen. This makes it possible not to spend money on food in restaurants. By the way, in this institution breakfasts are not of the highest quality and delicious, and therefore the opportunity to cook food yourself is especially valuable.

The main drawback of this hotel is the careless attitude of employees to their work according to the guests of Soho Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa). Reviews note that this is manifested not only in the quality of cleaning, but also in the work of catering establishments and other services. It is also worth noting the dismissive attitude towards the peace of guests and connivance with respect to noisy companies.
