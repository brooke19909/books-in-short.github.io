Hotel Taprobana Wadduwa 5 *, Sri Lanka: reviews

According to many tourists, Wadduva is a real tropical paradise, lost among exotic groves, tall palm trees and picturesque mountains. Most vacationers who come to Sri Lanka who prefer a relaxing beach holiday and complete unity with nature come to Sri Lanka. And of course, at sea such a traveler wants to feel as comfortable as possible. And perhaps this, of course, only when choosing a good hotel. Quite good hotels in Wadduwa are offered by many hotels. For example, vacationers can stay in a comfortable small complex Taprobana Wadduwa 5 * . Reviews from tourists, this hotel has earned very good.

Where is the hotel situated

This recreation center is located in the city of Wadduwa within walking distance from the coastline. In the immediate vicinity of it is a very popular and well-known tourist beach Kalatura. If necessary, travelers staying at the Taprobana Wadduwa 5 * hotel can independently visit such attractions of Sri Lanka as Richmond Castle or Kalutara Bodhiya . They are located in the same area as this convenient complex.





Tourists renting rooms in this hotel first fly to Sri Lanka by plane to Colombo airport. The capital of the country is located about 35 km from Wadduwa. Bandaike Airport is located about 70 km from this resort. Therefore, judging by the reviews, the future residents of the Taprobana Wadduwa Deluxe 5 * complex (Sri Lanka) have to get to their buildings, usually, unfortunately, for quite a long time. In most cases, the journey by tour operator's bus to this hotel takes tourists 1.5-2 hours.

Hotel Features

This small relax hotel in lively Wadduwa was built recently - in 2014. It takes guests in two four-story buildings of stylish modern architecture. The small courtyard of the hotel is well landscaped and has, according to many vacationers, a very convenient layout. Hotel Taprobana Wadduwa 5 * belongs to the group of five-star complexes. However, the classiness of hotels in Sri Laki, according to many tourists, usually does not coincide with global standards. This should be borne in mind by tourists who rented rooms in this complex. They should nevertheless rely on the Taprobana hotel to a level of no more than 4 *.

According to most vacationers, this hotel is most suitable for lovers of a relaxing holiday. The territory of this hotel is almost always very quiet. And the sound insulation in the buildings here, judging by the reviews, is not bad. At the hotel, vacationers can also take a walk in a small garden.





With pets, tourists are not accommodated in the rooms of this complex. Among other things, there are good conditions for people with disabilities. This hotel has earned good reviews, including the fact that trista can pay for all kinds of services on its territory , including plastic cards. For the convenience of guests in the hotel buildings are equipped with elevators.

Smoking is not permitted on this property. For this purpose, the hotel is equipped with special places. The reception in this complex is open, of course, around the clock.

Number of rooms

In total, this hotel offers guests 27 rooms equipped with new furniture and high-quality home appliances. The deluxe rooms at Wadduwa Hotel 5 * are for rent the most. You can also rent Superior or Suite rooms at the hotel. Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms of the complex. Also for the convenience of guests of the resort in the rooms of this center are provided:

  • safe with minibar;

  • TV;

  • Kettles

  • hair dryers.

A pleasant microclimate in the hotel rooms is provided by modern air conditioning. This hotel features rooms with balconies. The hotel's showers offer hairdryers.

What travelers think of Taprobana for rent

There are not many reviews of Taprobana Wadduwa Hotel (Sri Lanka) for 2017 on the Web. And no wonder. After all, the hotel is still relatively new. But be that as it may, those vacationers who have already managed to live in this complex, his rooms are considered quite comfortable. The rooms in this hotel, judging by the reviews, are spacious and comfortable. From the windows of all handed over at the hotel, apartments offer simply wonderful views of the ocean. In particular, a delightful picture in front of hotel guests opens here in the morning and during sunset.





In the evenings, hotel guests bring sweet cookies to their rooms. Tourists consider this addition to the service, of course, very pleasant. Another of the undoubted advantages of this hotel, many of its residents include the fact that, if desired, food can be ordered here from the restaurant directly to your room.

Regarding the cleanliness of the apartments in Taprobana, tourists have no complaints about the administration. His employees come to clean the rooms of this hotel not even once, but twice a day. The advantage of the hotel Taprobana Wadduwa 5 * ( Shri Lanka, Wadduwa), many tourists consider very comfortable beds. The mattresses on them are laid new and soft.

The baths in this hotel, according to many tourists, are simply magnificent - spacious and well equipped. Taking a shower at the Taprobana Hotel, according to vacationers, is very pleasant. Many of the rooms at this hotel have bathtubs, including balconies.

Among the pluses of the hotel, its guests include the fact that the rooms here simply catch WiFi. Tourists living in this complex can not only communicate in social networks with friends and relatives, but also, for example, watch movies.

Cons of rooms

According to many guests, there are practically no drawbacks to those being handed over to Taprobana Wadduwa 5 * k omnat. The only thing - in some rooms of this hotel, unfortunately, are not installed fumigators. There are a lot of mosquitoes in Sri Lanka in the evenings in the summer. But at the same time, residents of the hotel can ask for reception at the reception at any time.

Also , tourists consider the disadvantage of T aprobana (W adduwa, Sri Lanka) that the rooms have poor air conditioning. In addition, in some cases this household appliance can make a lot of noise at the hotel.

Some vacationers do not advise vacationers to book rooms on this ground floor in this hotel. Next to such rooms in the hotel is the kitchen. Therefore, various odors can penetrate into these rooms.





Facilities at the complex

The infrastructure in this hotel, according to many vacationers, is developed quite well. This hotel is almost never crowded. Therefore, tourists staying here can feel as free and comfortable as possible. The garden in the hotel T aprobana Wadduwa 5 * is located next to the restaurant. It also has a large 25-meter pool. The area with sun loungers at this hotel is located in the shade of palm trees. This many guests of the Taprobana hotel relate, of course, to its unconditional advantages.

In addition, the following amenities are provided on site at this holiday center:

  • laundry;

  • airport shuttle;

  • SPA and fitness centers.

Equipped in this hotel, among other things, conference and banquet facilities. WiFi hotel guests, of course, can use not only the rooms, but throughout the complex. Of course, this hotel has its own free parking. Residents of the complex can leave cars rented in the country for free.

Reviews of tourists about the infrastructure

The most popular service at this hotel, like almost any other, with most tourists is, of course, the pool. He is in this hotel, according to many of his guests, quite comfortable. You can swim in the pool of Taprobana Wadduwa complex (Sri Lanka) with comfort, as it has a sufficiently large depth, and the water in it does not smell like bleach. There are enough sun loungers in the hotel courtyard even in peak season for all guests. Residents of this hotel do not need to occupy sunbeds by the pool in advance. As soon as the hotel staff sees that the guests have decided to lie on a deck chair, they immediately bring them a mattress and a towel.

Many vacationers consider the fact that there is practically no entertainment here as some drawback of the hotel infrastructure. The guests of the complex cannot, like in other hotels, even three-star hotels, play billiards or, for example, tennis.





The main disadvantage of the hotel

On vacation, travelers, of course, want to relax as much as possible and forget about all sorts of problems surrounding their homes. But, while staying at the Taprobana Wadduwa 5 * hotel , you should still be more attentive. The fact is that on the network from tourists there is also information about cases of theft in this hotel. Money and valuables, resting here, should be stored in a safe. Perhaps the theft in this hotel is nothing more than an accident. But caution, the residents of this complex should still be observed unconditionally.

Taprobana Wadduwa Hotel Staff Reviews

Unfortunately, there are mixed reviews about the staff of this hotel on the Web. In general, hotel workers are courteous to their guests. However, some tourists note the fact that the staff of this recreation center can behave towards the guests somewhat intrusively. It so happens that the hotel workers go after the guests literally on their heels, offering them something to buy. This, of course, can be attributed to the disadvantages of Taprobana.

Unfortunately, the staff of this hotel cannot speak Russian. But they understand English pretty well. The peculiarity of this hotel, among other things, is that the staff here are almost completely male. Even rooms in Taprobana are cleaned mainly by boys under the age of 30.

How to feed at Taprobana

Of course, rooms for rent in this hotel with meals. Dishes can be served in the restaurant of the Taprobana Wadduwa 5 * hotel at different times, either by the "buffet" system or by the menu. The latter scheme is usually used at a time when few people live in the complex. There is a dining room in this hotel on the ground floor, next to the reception.

The menu at the Taprobana hotel, according to many vacationers, is quite diverse. And actually the food itself in this hotel, judging by the reviews, is quite tasty. The work of the cook of the complex as a whole has earned very good reviews from vacationers. Tourists in the restaurant of the complex are served pancakes, omelets, waffles, burgers, etc. There are, of course, various kinds of drinks in the hotel dining room. Queues for dishes in the restaurant of this hotel almost never happens. Even at the peak of the season, hotel guests can take their favorite food completely free.





On Saturdays, this complex exhibits, among other things, a grill. Here guests can take meat, fish, sausages, lobsters. The majority of holidaymakers in the hotel also praise this food very much. There is, of course, alcohol in the hotel restaurant. But for him, during the day, vacationers, unfortunately, have to pay separately.

What you should know about

The disadvantage of T aprobana Wadduwa 5 * hotel, many tourists consider, among other things, that tourists can charge an additional tax of 15% for food here. Moreover, tour operators do not provide this information to their customers who choose this hotel to stay in Sri Lanka, unfortunately.

Hotel Taprobana Wadduwa 5 * (Sri Lanka): beach hotel reviews

Despite some of the shortcomings, this tourist center is considered by most tourists to be quite comfortable for living. Pretty good reviews on the web from vacationers are available about the beach of this hotel. Hotel Taprobana does not have its own waterfront part in the city. This is a feature of most Sri Lankan luxury complexes. Hotel guests must visit the public beach.

The entrance to the sea opposite the hotel is quite convenient - not too gentle, but not particularly steep either. Regarding the cleanliness of the hotel's beach, there are relatively good reviews on the Web. There are, of course, all kinds of rubbish here. But it is usually not too much on the shore.

City infrastructure at the hotel

There are not too many shops, cafes and restaurants near this hotel in Wadduwa. Nevertheless, the infrastructure of the city near this complex is developed quite well. On the beach, for example, residents of this hotel can go to a very good fish restaurant. The food here is inexpensive and tasty. On the street near Taprobana there are two small shops where you can buy all kinds of little things - chips, soft drinks, etc.





About 15 minutes walk from the hotel is the Wadduwa train station . From here, guests can reach both Galle and Colombo. At some distance from the hotel - behind the railway, as some tourists report, you can find supermarkets and small grocery stores. There are even ATMs here. Of course, hotel guests and various cafes and restaurants can visit this place.
