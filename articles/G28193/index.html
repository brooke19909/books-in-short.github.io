We plan a vacation in advance. Mountain Altai. Where to go first?

Mountain Altai ... Where to go in this truly fabulous place? After all, it is not a secret for anyone that this region is able to impress even the most experienced traveler and adventurer.

The variety of natural monuments here is so great that experts consider it the richest place on earth. The local population keeps many legends about heroes, dragons, golden lakes and amazing caves, which can tell a lot of enthusiastic tourists.

Section 1. Mountain Altai. Where to go? General information about the area





Mountains and plains in the south-eastern part of Western Siberia - these wonderful places are called Altai. We offer to pick up the atlas of the world and see. The map of the Altai Mountains cannot but arouse increased interest. Why? The thing is that everything deserves special attention here: majestic mountains covered with snow caps, icy lakes and fast ice rivers.

By the way, few people know that the name of the area itself comes from the original Turkic word "golden". Beautiful and very symbolic, right? And in fact, here you can admire the coniferous taiga and the splendor of the Alpine meadow, see the beautiful forbs on the subalpine level, and semi-desert rocky steppes are very close by.

Section 2. Mountain Altai. Where to go? Glaciers, mountains, waterfalls and lakes





Finding yourself in this area, you really understand how unique the nature of our planet is. There are very beautiful mountains and a huge number of glaciers, so you definitely will not have a shortage of excursions and trips. By the way, in the zone of the North Chuysky Range there are flat valleys where excellent conditions for mountaineering are created. But this type of entertainment will appeal only to the most courageous travelers.

This region has its own zest. The fact is that many glaciers here are quite unusual from the point of view of modern scientists. They differ in special conditions. Waterfalls in the Altai mountains are also very different. There are stormy ones, tearing off mountain cliffs, and there are also those that, breaking out between the rocks, almost immediately disappear into the gorges. Lakes also can not be counted. Altai is even called the country of amazing blue lakes. On the shore of the ponds, it seems as if time has stopped - this feeling does not leave while standing near the water and looking at the surrounding mountains.

Moraine-dammed lakes are characterized by great depth. Located high in the mountains and surrounded by coniferous forests, they greatly adorn the local landscape. In the permafrost zone, you can see wonderful thermokarst lakes. They have a shallow depth, and the water is dark in color. And the largest size is Teletskoye Lake, which has a tectonic origin.
