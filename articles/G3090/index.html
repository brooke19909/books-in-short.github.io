Gulf of Thailand



For many vacationers, the key question is: what is the sea in Thailand? Pattaya is ready to offer you the Gulf of Thailand. It is part of the South China Sea, also washed by the shores of Cambodia, Vietnam and Malaysia.

The bay itself is not famous for its fast flow, because of this there is a large influx of clean fresh water. The salts in sea water, in principle, are few, not more than 3.5 percent. Truly salty water in the Gulf of Thailand is only at great depths, starting from 50 meters.

Another factor that is pleasant for tourists is the consistently high temperature of the water. Because of this, in many places of the bay, near the coast, coral reefs are formed.
