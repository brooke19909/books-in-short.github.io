Hotels in Faliraki, Greece: overview, description and reviews of tourists

The resort of Faliraki, located on the Greek island of Rhodes, is an incredibly picturesque place that has a developed infrastructure. That is why there is a huge number of luxury hotels. This resort is a favorite place of youth. Tourists relax in affordable facilities. Faliraki hotels are located literally on top of each other, you just need to choose the one that you like the most, or the most suitable option for your price. Each of the placements is built so that the road to the beach is the shortest. In a word, everything here is arranged in such a way as to provide tourists with the most comfortable and unforgettable vacation.

Most affordable apartments

The most affordable hotels in Faliraki are, of course, two-star institutions that cannot boast of ultra-modern repairs, rooms and their equipment. But in them for little money you can normally spend the night. What else do you need when you are in Rhodes? If you spend the whole day on the beach and explore the sights, there will be no time for life at the hotel. So, the most famous hotels with two stars are the following establishments:

  • Tivoli Hotel - The hotel is located 10.6 kilometers from the Palace of the Grand Masters and the Acropolis of Lindos. It has facilities such as a safe, a free luggage storage room and car parking. Tivoli Hotel is just 20 minutes from the resort center. And the road to the airport does not take more than a quarter of an hour.
  • Rhodian Rose Hotel - 42 spacious rooms to choose from. It also has a pool and sunbathing terrace. This is a family-run hotel with air conditioning and a kitchenette in every room. The hotel has a bar, Hotel Has A, where guests can eat.
  • Saint Amon Hotel Faliraki is a welcoming hotel with modern rooms. It is located in a beautiful place, in the very center of the resort. Each room has a private balcony, refrigerator, air conditioning, safe and wardrobe. All bathrooms are equipped with a shower and have free toiletries.

Holiday with three stars.

Hotels of Faliraki, on the sign of which three stars show off, boast better conditions and a wide range of services. So, most often tourists at this resort choose three-star hotels such as, for example, Orion Hotel, located near the water park and Rhodes beach. The rooms have balconies, safes and satellite TV. From the hotel windows you can see Faliraki Bay, and the institution itself was built next to the fabulous gardens.

More travelers like Sevastos Studios Hotel, located near Anthony Quinn Bay. This is a budget accommodation with a TV and a kitchenette. In the morning you can buy cheap full and delicious breakfasts here. Sevastos Studios Hotel offers car rental services.



Also popular is the three-star Hercules Hotel, which has 43 rooms. There is a sun deck and a seasonal outdoor hotel. In addition to the standard set of services, here you can order delivery of purchases, there is parking, a currency exchange office and a 24-hour reception.

Four Star Deals

If you want the most comfortable stay, but for a reasonable price, then the hotels of Faliraki with four stars are at the disposal of tourists. The most prestigious institutions of this class include the following.

Esperos Mare Hotel. Rhodes Acropolis and the Kahal Shalom Synagogue are located not far from this stylish hotel. The hotel has 152 stylish rooms, among which are family and double. The rooms offer a picturesque panorama of the sea.

If you want your hotel, among other things, to also have some kind of entertainment, then you should consider the Haven Boutique Hotel. It offers windsurfing, scuba diving and water skiing. Haven Boutique Hotel offers bike or car rental. And you will live in rooms with a kitchen and a balcony.

In this case, Greece (Faliraki) hotels offers the following: Elysium Resort & Spa, which can be found between the villages of Faliraki and Rhodes. Two hotel buildings are located off the seashore. Like many European placements, Elysium Resort & Spa operates according to the Bad & Breakfast system. On the territory of the institution there is a sand and pebble beach. A spa bath is included.



Another hotel of the highest category is La Marquise Luxury Resort Complex. The complex has at its disposal 120 thousand square meters of land. On this huge area there is one indoor and six outdoor pools, as well as 25 separate reservoirs, which you can enter from the terrace of your room. From La Marquise Luxury Resort Complex to the sea is 400 meters, but if you overcome this distance is too difficult for you, you can use an electric car.

One of the best

One of the most sought after hotels in Faliraki is the Mitsis Faliraki Hotel. It was built in 1973, and in 2011 it was completely renovated. They love children here, because they have an amusement park and a water park, as well as a bowling club. 323 rooms of the institution are clean and modern style. They include Wi-Fi, a balcony, multi-channel TV, a minibar, heating and marble bathrooms.

The advantage of the Mitsis Faliraki Beach hotel complex is the presence of a sandy beach with a Blue Flag. For families with children, this is just a great place.

A rich breakfast buffet is served each morning. If you are a vegetarian, then you will be happy to prepare suitable dishes.



Other popular hotel services

Hotel Mitsis Faliraki (Rhodes) offers its guests many other services. For example, sushi lovers are advised to relax in the local Main Bar, which also serves snacks and spirits.

In order to satisfy all the needs of guests, Mitsis Faliraki Beach has a currency exchange, gift shop, copier, photocopy machine, free parking and a conference room. Every day on the territory of the complex entertainment programs, karaoke and animations are organized. The hotel provides leisure activities: mountain biking, basketball, diving, aerobics and a gym with modern fitness equipment.

Another successful hotel complex

The Faliraki Beach Hotel is not the only resort location that has worldwide popularity. There are several more hotels in the city that are known to many travel lovers. One of them is Apollo Beach Hotel - an attractive establishment with luxurious rooms. The complex began to receive tourists in the mid-1970s, and in 2005 it was completely restored.

293 rooms of Apollo Beach Hotel are equipped with telephones with international connection, wireless Internet, multi-channel TV, safe, individual air conditioning and other amenities.



Hotel reviews

Faliraki hotels have earned reviews and continue to earn good ones. Tourists like the facilities and their close proximity to the sea and places of interest. Also, guests praise the equipment of the rooms and their cleanliness. People say that even in two-star accommodations, staff conduct regular cleaning. And in four- and five-star hotels, visitors are most pleased that all the conditions for children's recreation are provided here. Each of the hotels in Faliraki has certain advantages that are appreciated by vacationers.
