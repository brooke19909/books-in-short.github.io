Home cinema - which is better to choose? Review of the best models

Until recently, we were content with one simple TV and did not think about the fact that something could be cooler. But the desire to watch your favorite movies in high quality leads to the fact that we are looking for more modern means, for example, a home theater. Which is better, and what to look for when choosing these innovative tools?





For starters, the main characteristics

Let's say right away - there are a huge number of companies offering modern home cinemas. Someone is too famous and does not need extra words, but someone needs to be introduced. But any home cinema should be functional, and therefore, when choosing, see if the model you like meets the following important characteristics:

  1. 3D support. Without this feature, it‚Äôs hard to imagine a modern home theater. Which is the best of today's popular models? Experts advise choosing those that are equipped with unique 3D-Blu-ray technology. This is not only the ability to watch videos in the highest quality, but also play the latest games.
  2. HDMI inputs The more of them, the better, because you can connect both a TV and a game console to the cinema at home, and this serves as a guarantee of better and purer sound.
  3. Online streaming. iPlayer is one of the popular features that complement advanced home cinema systems. Especially convenient is the movie streaming service.
  4. Auto sound calibration. Home 3D-cinema is chosen by many because it has a high-quality picture and the same perfect sound. Especially good when the sound balance of the speakers can be adjusted using a microphone and save the calibration once.
  5. DLNA is a unique system that allows you to watch movies, listen to music that are launched from a computer or stereo.
  6. 7.1. This sound system involves the use of additional rear speakers, so you can completely immerse yourself in what is happening on the screen. Such a powerful home theater in terms of sound will receive only the best reviews.
