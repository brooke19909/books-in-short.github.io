Description, specifications, rooms and reviews Welcome World Beach Resort & SPA (Thailand / Pattaya)

Thailand is perhaps one of the most favorite and most popular resorts for Russian tourists. Gathered good reviews Welcome World Beach Resort & SPA - a wonderful, very cozy, stylish and comfortable hotel. Here you can relax as much as possible, get pleasant SPA-procedures, relax your soul and body in the literal sense of the phrase. This hotel is the ideal choice for those who value quality.

Where is the hotel?

One of the main advantages of the Welcome World Beach Resort & SPA hotel (reviews confirm this) is its convenient location. Only 2 minutes walk away from Dongtarn Beach and 5 from Jomtien. Nearby there are shops, cafes and other institutions.

Cozy and comfortable rooms

People who write reviews about the Welcome World Beach Resort & SPA 4 * (now it is already five-star) pay special attention to the description of the rooms in which they have stayed. These are comfortable and spacious rooms, their equipment includes everything for a pleasant stay and a wonderful dream. On the sofa, installed right by the window, you can read a book or watch TV, lying on the bed. The working area is equipped with a table and chairs, the living room has a coffee table and armchairs, and the hallway has a wardrobe and a cupboard. Some rooms have panoramic windows, all have balconies. The rooms are bright and comfortable.

Bathrooms are stylish and also quite spacious. The bathrooms of some categories of rooms are equipped with a window with shutters, from which a bewitching landscape of the park zone and the ocean opens. In the bathroom towels and individual hygiene kits.

The dining area is equipped with the necessary utensils and appliances, there is a kettle and a coffee maker. Food and drinks can be stored in the refrigerator with a freezer.

In total, the complex has 228 rooms. The rooms always have air conditioning, a minibar and a TV with Russian channels. There is a telephone for communication with the reception. Internet connection in the rooms is free - a good option to stay in touch with friends and family. Additionally, the bathroom has a hairdryer.

Welcome World Beach Resort & SPA 5 * (Thailand, Pattaya) offers 3 room categories:

  • Deluxe The total area is 47 square meters. By entering the balcony, guests can enjoy the ocean. The rooms are divided into zones: working, living room, bedroom and hallway. There is a refrigerator with a freezer, a bar, air conditioning and a safe. The bathroom has a comfortable large bathtub and shower. The room has 1 quin size bed.
  • Suite A. Rooms in this category are 78 square meters. They are as equipped as the previous category, only, in addition to 1 double bed, there is an additional single bed. In addition, the bathroom also has slippers, bathrobes and bath accessories.
  • Suite B Jacuzzi. The same arrangement as in the previous versions, the room area is 78 square meters. A seating area is allocated, one bed is a double, but there are additional folding beds.




Relax or outdoor activities - the choice of hotel guests

It is immediately worth noting that some reviews (Welcome World Beach Resort & SPA) emphasize such a drawback as the remoteness of the hotel, which is why after the excursion, vacationers from this complex were brought home last. But there are advantages to this. The hotel is calm, secluded, and from the tour there are such vivid impressions that the road to the hotel does not seem too long.

Those who prefer a relaxing holiday are invited to take a walk around the hotel or go down to the beach, or go explore the surroundings. A good idea would be to lie on a mattress or deck chair by the pool.

You can recover and rejuvenate yourself inside and out in the sauna, massage room or SPA center. They offer various relaxation treatments that will give a lot of pleasant sensations. But those who want to spend time actively can visit the gym and fitness center or go to the coast, where beach games and water sports await.





Services at the five-star hotel: paid and free features

The hotel complex is the best suited for relaxing during the holidays, as well as for work. If you refer to the reviews, Welcome World Beach Resort & SPA provides all the necessary facilities for this. First, about the rest - and no work.

The beach has free admission, as it is public, and towels. For sun loungers and umbrellas you will need to pay a rent. Also, if you want to go on an adventure on the water or play on the beach, rental equipment will require additional payment.

The fitness center opens its doors to all comers, but playing billiards, massage and treatments in the SPA center will cost money. It is worth noting that the use of the Internet is free only in rooms, in public areas you need to pay for it.

A cot and baby sitting are available for a fee, and a pool for babies (as well as adults), a games room, as well as menus and chairs in the restaurant are provided free of charge.

As for business services, this hotel provides everything you need for business people, you only need to pay to rent a conference room. There are 4 of them, with a capacity of up to 600 people. Equipped with the necessary office equipment. There are also banquet facilities.

The reception and the elevator are open around the clock, room cleaning is done every day (this is included in the price). Clothing can be taken care of in the laundry and dry cleaning (for a fee).





Will the rest in this hotel be expensive?

Booking a room will cost an average of 6-9 thousand rubles per day per person. However, the living conditions that are offered in the Welcome World Beach Resort & SPA 5 * (reviews confirm this), 100% compensate for the relative high cost. Everything is thought out in the hotel so that the rest becomes memorable and comfortable.

Food system in a hotel complex

For hotel guests only breakfast is provided, that is, only he is included in the price. The rest of the time you can eat in the same restaurant or visit the bar. In addition, there is such a service as ordering food in the room, if there is no desire to go somewhere.

Breakfast is served every morning in the buffet restaurant. Drinks are available, as well as hot or cold dishes. If it gets very hot, there is a lounge bar with refreshing cocktails.
