Centara Seaview Resort Khao Lak 4 *: description of the hotel, apartments and offered services

Thailand is an exotic corner of the planet located in Indochina, which is very popular among tourists from different countries. In a wonderful town called Khao Lak there is a complex known as Centara Seaview Resort Khao Lak 4 *. This hotel is very popular, and travelers often stop here. Well, it is worth talking about all its features in more detail.





Location

So, there is the Centara Seaview Resort Khao Lak 4 * in the resort town of Khao Kak. He, in turn, is located on the western coast of the peninsula called Malacca. It is about 100 kilometers from Phuket. There are many interesting sights here. The most famous are two waterfalls (Chon-Fah and Lampi), national parks Khao Sok and Khao Lak Lam-Ru, Lake Cheo-Lan and much more.

Khao Lak is located far from Phuket Airport - 80 kilometers away. And the railway station is 170 km away. But from this hotel you can just a minute walk to the cleanest beach with the azure sea.

This complex is impossible not to notice. It consists of small cozy bungalows that are decorated in a traditional Thai style, under a tree. Many people like the way this hotel is surrounded by greenery and numerous ponds. An ideal setting for a relaxing break.

About sports and leisure

The Centara Seaview Resort Khao Lak 4 * has everything you need for entertainment. Naturally, there is an outdoor swimming pool and a sun terrace - as in any good hotel.

In addition, guests have the opportunity to go diving, play golf on a nearby golf course for this sport, devote time to tennis, and work out in the fitness center. You can also enjoy a hot tub, massage and wellness treatments in the SPA center. There is also the option of playing darts or billiards.

By the way, if potential guests are planning to take a small child with them on vacation, then they can not worry that he will have nothing to do. There is a children's playroom and a playground with a pool. Incidentally, babysitting services are also provided.





Services

The Centara Seaview Resort Khao Lak 4 * has everything from free Wi-Fi to free parking (near the hotel). Naturally, there is also a restaurant. The dishes are varied and delicious. True, in the restaurant there is a rule called One Eating: when guests go inside through the main entrance, the administrator writes down the room number. That is, breakfast a second time will not work, and even a bite to eat until lunch. But no one forbids entering the institution from the pool. Many bypass this rule. By the way, in addition to the restaurant there is a bar with a variety of drinks.

The Centara Seaview Resort Khao Lak (Thailand) also offers ticketing services. The complex has a dry cleaning service, laundry service, a 24-hour reception desk, a currency exchange, a tour desk and even a large conference room.

It is not surprising that guests leave only positive reviews about their stay at Centara Seaview Resort Khao Lak. Indeed, there is really everything here! Guests note the attention to the delivery of food, drinks and newspapers to the rooms, the availability of family rooms and facilities for people with disabilities, as well as other services. I am glad the opportunity to rent a car, order a packed lunch with you, buy something directly in an on-site shop. In general, top-level service is a fact.
