Mountains are perhaps the best way to escape from the everyday city bustle. Holidays in the mountains are to the heart of true connoisseurs of the majestic beauty of the peaks, delightful landscapes towering above the world, narrow paths, the silence of pristine nature, crystal chime of waterfalls and amazing, enchanting birdsong.
