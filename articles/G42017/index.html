Hotel "Banana". Turkey, Alanya. Description

The comfortable hotel "Banana" (Turkey) is located in a picturesque place near Alanya on the Mediterranean coast. Decorated in a contemporary style, the hotel buildings are surrounded by greenery of huge spreading palm trees and orange trees. This hotel is a great place to relax with your family or in the company of close friends.

Description

Hotel Banana (Turkey) was opened in 1968. And to this day it is very popular among tourists. The last major restoration was carried out in 2008, thanks to which the hotel acquired a new modern look. The hotel consists of two- and three-story buildings located along the coastline and one seven-story building equipped with an elevator. The total area is 8500 square meters. m. 1.5 km away is the famous resort village of Alanya, 120 km away is the nearest airport.

Rooms

The Banana Hotel (Turkey) has 358 comfortable apartments with stunning views of the picturesque garden or the sea. Each standard room is designed for three people. It is equipped with stationary beds, a small refrigerator, central air conditioning, satellite TV with music and Russian-language channels, a telephone, a shower room, a hairdryer, a balcony, a safe.



Suite type apartments are designed for four people. They have two separate rooms (bedroom and living room). The hotel also has special apartments for the disabled. Upon request, a cot is available in the room.

Nutrition

Hotel "Banana" (Turkey) sells meals to its guests according to the "half board" system. There are also two restaurants and four bars where tourists can enjoy exquisite dishes of European and Turkish cuisines.

Sports and Entertainment

For tourists who were attracted by Turkey with its unique flavor, the Banana 4 * hotel is ready to offer a lot of various entertainments. Two freshwater pools and children's sections are constantly operating at the hotel. There are also mattresses, umbrellas and deck chairs. In the afternoon and in the evening, entertaining entertainers entertain entertainers. There is also a TV lounge, you can play table tennis, volleyball, darts, billiards, aerobics, visit the massage room, hammam, sauna, hairdresser, and wellness center. It offers guests dry cleaning, laundry, doctor, shops, car rental, photographer. Near the pools are equipped with water slides. There is an entertainment club for kids working in the daytime, and in the afternoon a children's animation program is held.

Beach





Turkey is famous for its amazingly clean pebble beaches. The Banana Hotel, photos of which are presented on this page, is located just 30 meters from the sea. It has its own comfortable beach.

Reviews

The most pleasant experience left the hotel "Banana" among tourists. The grounds and rooms are very beautifully decorated. Dishes are always tasty and varied, lots of vegetables and drinks. Particularly liked the holiday bungalows located near the sea. It is always quiet, calm, and the beach is within easy reach. The hotel staff is very attentive and welcoming.
