Serious exchanges and stores are responsible for the security of the transaction, so if the goods are not received by the buyer or things of inadequate quality are received, he can count on a refund of the money spent.

Some stores, such as "Ebay", thanks to a rather long history of their existence, managed to gain trust in society. Here, sometimes you can find not just good things, but also unique copies of books, vintage clothes.



Registering on the website of the online store is easy, it will take literally a few minutes. And then you can select the product, looking at the proposed options. At the moment when the things you like will be selected in the basket, the beginner may have a question about how to pay for purchases. On "Ebay" bank cards of the international payment systems Visa and Mastercard are accepted. Money transfer operations are carried out mainly through the PayPal payment system. The scheme is available in almost all countries of the world, including the Russian Federation and other CIS countries. If the Ebey online store itself was too complicated for the consumer, then he can purchase the goods not directly, but using the services of a Russian-speaking intermediary. In this case, the selection and purchase process will be more comfortable, but the intermediary will take an additional commission from the client for their services.



Some users believe that PayPal is the only way to pay in the online store. In fact, there are several ways to pay for purchases on "Ebay". These are transfers through the Western Union system, and the use of BidPay checks. However, there is a single principle that works at this auction - prepayment. Despite the fact that the administration of the store fully guarantees the security of the transaction, the goods will not be sent until money arrives for it and its delivery.



So the question "how to pay for purchases on Ebay?" the correct answer will be - in a timely manner. And each user will choose the simplest and safest method himself, although experts still advise PayPal.

How to buy on "Ebey"

This online store is available for sellers and buyers from all over the world. But, unfortunately, not all products are delivered to any country. It may happen that the thing you like cannot be sent to the right state. But, it turns out, there is a solution to this problem. There are so-called forwarding companies that provide services for the reception in your warehouse of things purchased through the Internet and then sent to the customer. Of course, in this case you will have to pay both for the storage of goods and for the services of the intermediary himself. But the consumer can accumulate a certain number of items in the warehouse and get them in one package. It will be much cheaper with a few separate shipments.

As a rule, using this site several times, the buyer can cope with the choice of goods without any problems. Yes, and how to pay for purchases on "Ebay", it becomes clear literally from 2-3 acquisitions. In this matter, as in any other, the main thing is experience.
