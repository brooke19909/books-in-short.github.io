A Brief History of Pillows

It is no longer possible to imagine a comfortable dream without a comfortable pillow. Many centuries ago, only rich people could take advantage of the privilege of buying pillows, and the poor did not even have a clue about such luxury. The history of the pillow (briefly) will be told to the reader in the article. We will talk about classic products, decorative, sofas and pillows, toys. So, let's begin.





Pillow history

The very first information about the appearance of the pillow refers to the rule of the ancient Egyptian pharaohs. Although the pillows did not resemble modern ones, their function was performed by wooden planks on a stand. To protect the pharaoh from the dark forces, they were depicted of the gods, so that the ruler could rest calmly after day cares. In Japan, pillows made of wood, metal, stone or porcelain were used everywhere until the 19th century.

Pillows and mattresses served to ensure a comfortable pastime for noble citizens. They judged the high status of the patrician, and the richness of the finish emphasized his financial capabilities. Sewed pillows made of fabric or leather, gave a different shape. Feathers or down of birds, as well as animal hair, were used as filler.

In ancient Rome, pillows soon came into use. Gradually, items filled with fluff gained unprecedented popularity among the Romans. The military leaders liked to rest on them so much that they sent subordinates to get goose down for pillows.





The magical properties of pillows

The history of the pillow is also interesting because from ancient times it was believed that this product has magical powers. For a sound sleep, Emperor Nero placed a snake skin bracelet under his pillow. Octavian Augustus could not sleep for a long time at night, so he wanted to get a pillow of one ruined patrician. The emperor believed that, in addition to the pillow, he would provide himself with a calm and sweet sleep, because on it an incredibly person could fall asleep to his ears in debt.

During the Middle Ages, small pillows were sewn under the legs to keep warm in the cold season. Such an invention saved from frost in stone castles, where fireplaces could not heat huge arches, and drafts were a common occurrence. Also then, people were extremely religious, and not everyone could spend several hours on their knees during prayer. Therefore, for convenience, they used pillows to withstand night vigils.

In Russia, divination with a pillow is connected with divination, when a twig from a broom was put under it to dream about the future bridegroom. At Christmas, a spruce branch should be hidden in a pillow to ensure happiness and health for the whole year. Richly embroidered pillows always gave the girl a dowry. The poor stuffed items with horsehair or hay, and the rich, in dowry to their daughters, gave feather and down pillows from expensive cloth.

Pillow Use Studies

The history of the pillow also contains some unpleasant moments. For example, in the 19th century, Otto Steiner added a little negative to the product‚Äôs reputation. Well-known physician conducted research, and highlighted the results in the book "Bed". If the air humidity rises even slightly, the feather pillow starts to smell unpleasant. Steiner suggested that this is due to residues on the feathers of particles of skin, fat and poultry. All this content begins to decompose and release harmful and badly smelling substances.

Researchers have proven that feather pillows have dozens of parasitic microorganisms. Therefore, allergy sufferers and asthmatics should not sleep on such pillows so as not to provoke an attack. To avoid trouble, you must follow the rules: air and dry the pillow in the sun, at least once a year to change for a new one. In Europe, they tried to avoid the problem by filling the pillows with goose down, and the material was also subject to additional processing. Although the necessary sanitary standards have not been achieved.





Modern tendencies

Everyone knows that over time, the pillow becomes heavier. Even with the automatic plucking of bird feathers, it is not possible to completely clean the feather from the tiny pieces of tissue that the ticks feed on. One centimeter of feathers can accommodate more than 200 dust mites. They tried to solve the problem with foam pillows, but here they saw flaws. In the 20th century, scientists synthesized artificial fiber. Such a pillow can be easily washed and dried; it does not lose its original shape. Parasites and dangerous microorganisms do not settle in the product.

Different styles and trends in fashion succeeded each other, the functions assigned to the pillows changed. Invariably one thing - thanks to the pillows, comfort and coziness of home decoration is provided. In the apartments for many years, "settled" sofa cushions, as well as products in the form of funny animals for the smallest.
