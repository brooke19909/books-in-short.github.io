Hotel "Berlin"

The hotel "Berlin" fully complies with all these requirements. Moscow is not only the center of business and political life.

Services

After a business or sightseeing day, you can relax and improve your health without leaving the hotel grounds.

The hotel offers guests a sauna with two rooms, steam room, hydromassage, karaoke. When ordering a trainer, fitness is also possible at the Berlin Hotel. Moscow, as a large business center, requires some tension, and the services of the hotel complex will give you the opportunity to quickly put in order your health and muscle tone.

Room service is available 24 hours a day from the lobby bar. Laundry services (at an additional cost) are also included in the list of services of the Hotel Berlin.

Nutrition

The Berlin Courtyard restaurant, the Frau Marta coffee house, the lobby bar and the summer terrace represent the catering system offered by the Berlin Hotel (Moscow). Photos that demonstrate coziness and respectability convey a chamber atmosphere, but cannot convey the taste of dishes performed by a chef specially trained in Germany.

Therefore, it is worth visiting this cozy place with the aim of tasting Bavarian sausages and draft live German beer.
