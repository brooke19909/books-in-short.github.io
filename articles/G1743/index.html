An unforgettable vacation in Egypt: Hotel "Arabia Azur" (Hurghada)

On the Red Sea coast, in the resort town of Hurghada, the beautiful Egyptian Hotel Arabia Azur is spread. It has a category of 4 stars and is very popular among vacationers. The city airport is located only 7 kilometers from the hotel, and it is 3 kilometers away from the center of the village. The hotel received its first visitors in 1990. On its balance sheet, it has three buildings: the main two-story and two three-story residential buildings. The first is the reception. The territory with numerous green spaces allows vacationers to take refuge in the shade during the hottest hours of the day. It should be noted that this hotel has a common territory and a number of services with the adjacent Bel Air Hotel.



Rooms

The housing stock of the Arabia Azur Hotel (Hurghada) has 526 rooms, including 502 standard 24 junior suites and 9 connected rooms. Pets are not allowed. The room has central air conditioning, bathroom, hairdryer, TV. A satellite TV is supplied, there is a Russian-language channel. There is a telephone, a fridge, a minibar (filled for an additional fee). All are decorated in light colors and tiled floors. The rooms located on the first floor have a terrace, while the second and third rooms have a balcony. There is no safe in the room, there is at the reception (for guests free of charge). Daily, during breakfast, cleaning is done, linen is changed every other day. A baby cot is available upon request.

Beach

Hotel "Arabia Azur" (Hurghada) has its own sandy beach, the length of which is 1500 meters. Nearby is a coral reef. During swimming, it is recommended to use special shoes. Beach equipment is provided free of charge.

Territory

The hotel "Arabia Azur" (Hurghada) has all the conditions for an excellent holiday. There is an outdoor adult pool and two children's. For those who combine leisure with work, 2 conference rooms are equipped: the capacity of a small one is 40 people, the large one - 100 people. In addition, 4 meeting rooms are provided. For various events there is a banquet room that seats up to 1250 people. For those who want to watch a movie on the big screen, there is a TV room. Vacationers are provided with a number of additional services, including laundry services, dry cleaning, currency exchange, a doctor on call, a nanny. Various shops are located on site. For young tourists, a beautiful playground has been built, a mini-club is working.



Guest reviews

Those who have already rested at the Arabia Azur Hotel (Hurghada) leave only positive reviews. Marked by a polite and attentive staff, excellent food and developed infrastructure. The hotel has a huge selection of various entertainment and activities, including sports. For residents open gym, 2 tennis courts. There is a volleyball court on the beach. There are darts, billiards, squash, animation, disco. And for those for whom this will not be enough, there are a number of paid entertainment, including camel riding, diving, windsurfing, canoeing, catamarans, steam room.
