Catamaran Description

A trip on the catamaran Anapa-Yalta promises to be comfortable, as it is equipped with air conditioning and TVs. Convenient and comfortable passenger cabin resembles an air liner, where soft chairs are located. True, those passengers who will sit in the middle of the cabin will not see the sea, but you can admire them from the open deck, breathe the sea air. You can spend six hours without problems, time will fly by quickly.

The catamaran has a small buffet with soft drinks and ice cream. Traveling for six hours, fresh sea air awaken your appetite, so take a light meal with you, especially if you have children with you. Buffet prices are high.

The ship has two decks for passengers. On the upper deck is a passenger compartment and small open areas located in the rear and on the sides. The lower, more spacious, also has a passenger compartment and one small open area.

Route description

Yalta has always been considered the pearl of the Crimea. Many wish to visit here. Picturesque places, the warm sea, clean sandy beaches attract thousands of vacationers. Many tourists will go to the Black Sea this summer, and not everyone will like combining vacations with hours of standing in traffic jams at the Kerch crossing. There is an opportunity to avoid this. Catamaran Anapa - Feodosia - Yalta will help you quickly and comfortably get to the Crimea. In addition, a six-hour sailing on the Black Sea will bring a lot of pleasure. Traveling by sea and along the coast will diversify the rest, make it unforgettable.

The catamaran Anapa - Yalta, the flight schedule of which will be given below, travels in 6.5 hours. Its route consists of three Black Sea cities of Anapa, Feodosia and Yalta. Just go two catamarans, which make one flight per day and sent towards each other. They do not swim all year round, but only during the holiday season.

The catamaran from Anapa to Feodosia moves along the open sea, then it goes along the coast to Yalta. This is a kind of small trip that will allow passengers to get acquainted with the coastal line of the Crimea and at the same time experience the beauty of sea navigation.

The route is new and the greater part of the population that has not visited Crimea before does not mean anything. But the Internet and a large number of press releases attract attention. Most users positively rated the catamaran, reviews are good, but many complain about the high prices.





Catamaran Anapa - Yalta: timetable

From Anapa the catamaran leaves at 11:00. Arrival in Feodosia - at 14:00. Parking in Feodosia - 30 minutes. Arrival in Yalta at 17:30. Total travel time - 6 hours 30 minutes.

Departure from Yalta at 10:00. Arrival in Feodosia - at 13:00, here he makes a stop for 30 minutes. He arrives in Anapa at 16:30.

Where can I buy tickets. Cost

In Yalta and Feodosia tickets can be bought on the catamaran itself. In Anapa - at the sea station, at ticket offices. It must be remembered that the boarding of passengers on the Anapa-Yalta catamaran is carried out one hour and ends half an hour before departure. For boarding you will need a ticket and a passport, for a child - a birth certificate or passport.

The total ticket price from Anapa to Yalta is 2700 rubles per adult and for a child from 6 years old - 1350 rubles. Children under 6 years old - free of charge. From Yalta to Feodosia the cost of a ticket is 1200, for a child - 600 rubles. The cost from Feodosia to Yalta is 1500, for a child - 750 rubles.





Pros and cons

I would like to note that a trip on a catamaran from Anapa to Yalta is, first of all, an unforgettable trip that will be remembered for a long time. The time spent sailing along the Black Sea is much less than what you have to spend on a trip through the Kerch Strait, for a long wait for the line for crossing.

On the catamaran, due to its design, you can sail for those who are afraid of pitching. The pitching on it is not as strong as on other ships. Comfortable conditions and a fairly high speed compared to motor ships, which can also be reached to Yalta.

The disadvantages include the high price of the ticket for the catamaran Anapa - Yalta, reviews of vacationers indicate precisely this problem. This is the main and most significant drawback that prevents many vacationers from acquiring a catamaran ticket. Another minus is the small open decks. But not the fact that this flaw would deter a trip on such a beautiful and comfortable ship.

The disadvantages include the fact that if the sea is stormy, flights are delayed. In such weather, catamarans do not go to sea.
