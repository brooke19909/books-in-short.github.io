Holiday Village "On the Edge", Yekaterinburg. Description. Reviews

The recreation center "At the Edge" in Yekaterinburg is located just a fifteen minute drive from the regional center, surrounded by pine forest. This makes it attractive for lovers to spend weekends or holidays aside from the bustle of the city. Comfortable living conditions and well-thought-out infrastructure will allow you not to worry about anything, enjoying peace and fresh air.

Description

The recreation center "On the Edge" in Yekaterinburg offers guests to stay in cozy cottages. Near each house there is a gazebo and barbecue. Several Russian baths are equipped on the camp site, after which you can plunge into the pool with artesian water.

If you like to move around, there are volleyball and soccer fields, a basketball court at your service. There are billiards and karaoke. Guests can enjoy homemade dishes at the on-site cafe. In a large covered gazebo with air conditioning, the hotel staff will help you organize a corporate party or banquet. Car owners are given a place in a guarded parking lot, which is under surveillance. No internet access.

Young tourists spend their free time on the playground. The territory of the suburban complex is well-groomed and landscaped. Here you can stroll along the paved alleys, admire the neat lawns, colorful fountains. In winter you can rent skis or skates. In the summer, tourists go swimming in a quarry that can be reached in fifteen minutes. The recreation center "On the edge" (Ekb) is located at: Starye Resheti village, Pushkina street, 148/2, 17th km. Art. Moscow tract.
