Hotel Victoria (Arkhipo-Osipovka): hotel description and guest reviews

Arkhipo-Osipovka is a small village that is part of the famous resort town of Gelendzhik in Russia. It is located on the Black Sea coast. This explains the presence in the village of a large number of tourists who come here from different parts of our country and neighboring countries. The Victoria Hotel (Arkhipo-Osipovka), which is considered one of the most popular establishments here, hosts many guests annually. Therefore, to talk about it is more detailed.

Hotel Description

The Victoria Hotel (Arkhipo-Osipovka) is a rather large tourist complex by local standards, which consists of three buildings located on Morskaya Street:

  1. The first contains economy class rooms for 2 or 3 people. In addition to basic furniture (beds, a table, bedside tables and chairs), they have a bathroom, a refrigerator, air conditioning and an electric kettle.
  2. In the second building, the rooms are more comfortable. They additionally have a telephone and a TV with satellite channels, and in the bathroom, in addition to a shower, toilet and washbasin, a hairdryer and personal hygiene products are provided (individual set for each visitor). And in the VIP-rooms there is also a jacuzzi.
  3. The third building is a cross between the first and second. Here, in addition to furniture and a bathroom, there is a TV, a refrigerator and an air conditioning system.




Residents feed on their own. To do this, the institution has its own bar and cafe. Hotel Victoria (Arkhipo-Osipovka) offers visitors a diverse range of additional services. On the territory there is:

  • sauna;
  • pool;
  • billiards;
  • BBQ for those wishing to treat themselves to barbecue;
  • free parking;
  • elevator to the upper floors.

Hotel Victoria (Arkhipo-Osipovka) is located just fifty meters from the beach. Here at any time you can rent sun loungers, catamarans, jet skis and windsurf boards. There are always those who wish to use this service.

Opinions of visitors

Many guests liked the Victoria Hotel (Arkhipo-Osipovka). Reviews about this institution can only be heard good.





Almost everyone who happened to stay at this hotel, noted the following positive points:

  • Friendly and very friendly staff who faithfully carry out their work;
  • timely and high-quality room cleaning (daily);
  • the hotel is located near the sea, so the road to the beach takes no more than 10 minutes;
  • amazing view from the window;
  • the presence of a pool;
  • good work of cafe chefs (many residents even placed an order with them for a comprehensive three meals a day);
  • compliance of the cost of the permit with the quality of the proposed vacation;
  • the hotel has a left-luggage office, which allows you not to load the room with unnecessary luggage at the moment;
  • free internet access for all residents.

Hotel Location

Many Russians prefer to rest in the Krasnodar Territory. For this, the Victoria Hotel (Arkhipo-Osipovka) is simply the ideal place. Photos of this institution and the surrounding area help potential customers better assess the place of their future vacation.





Firstly, the village itself is located in a very picturesque place. On the one hand, it is adjacent to the coastline, and on the other, it stretches deep into the continent along the Teshebs and Vulan rivers that flow into the Black Sea. The village has a well-developed infrastructure, which is mainly focused on numerous tourists. The Victoria Hotel itself is located very well. From its windows opens a picturesque view of the sea, which you can admire while sitting on the balcony. For many people, living conditions are very important. Therefore, they should pay special attention to the photos of the rooms in which tourists are accommodated. Even in small pictures, you can appreciate the spacious and comfortable rooms, furnished with comfortable upholstered furniture. The owners of the hotel made sure that the guests were as comfortable as possible.

Price of pleasure

The address where the Victoria Hotel is located is Arkhipo-Osipovka, Morskaya, 15 and 13. Entrance to the hotel is from the street through the central five-story building. Further through the patio you can get to other rooms.





For visitors, the reception is open around the clock, where a friendly administrator will help with check-in. Accommodation in such a hotel is relatively inexpensive. Owners of rooms in the three-story building number 1 per day will have to pay from 700 to 900 rubles. By resort standards, this is quite a bit. Those who have purchased tickets to the five-story building number 2, rest will cost more (from 900 to 3000 rubles). Everything will depend on the season, specific room and time of stay. Residents of building No. 3 should expect that they will have to pay 800-1100 rubles for 1 day. The price, as a rule, includes accommodation and the ability to use additional services (parking, internet, 2 swimming pools). For the average Russian, such a price is quite acceptable. Perhaps that is why the hotel rarely has free rooms due to the influx of tourists.
