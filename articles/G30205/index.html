The most famous museums for children

  1. Kunstkamera will be interesting to any child, if he is not very small. The exhibits of the museum are so unusual that children will be delighted to see various rarities created by nature and people. For example, the skeleton of a very tall person or a huge globe. For the children in the museum special excursions are held to introduce them to history and ethnography, game programs and competitions.
  2. The Zoological Museum is interesting to children of all ages, however, in one day you can‚Äôt get around it with a small child.   3. A good entertainment for children in St. Petersburg is a visit to the planetarium. Here you can visit the observatory with the largest telescope, observe the stars and the movement of the planets. Children are offered various educational and entertaining programs, an experiment laboratory and space travel.

Modern museums that interest children

In the past 20 years, St. Petersburg has offered much more entertainment for children than before. Many thematic exhibitions and museums were opened, interesting to visitors of any age.



  1. The water universe is one of the new museums located in the water tower. It introduces visitors to the history of water supply. Children will be interested to see a multimedia exposition that tells about the importance of water for humans. And also visit the hall "Underground Petersburg", where they will find out where she goes from home.
  2. The Bread Museum not only tells about the history of the product, but also introduces children to the traditions of baking in Russia. There you can see tools and equipment for baking, product samples.
  3. Children interested in anatomy can visit the Human Body Museum or the Hygiene Museum, which tells about a healthy lifestyle.

Different museums for boys and girls

When parents choose where to take their child, you need to consider whether he will be interested in this place. For example, in St. Petersburg there are museums whose exhibits may interest only boys and not please girls, and vice versa.

  1. Great entertainment for children in St. Petersburg are two slightly similar small institutions: the Puppet Museum and the Toy Museum. In the latter, girls not only get acquainted with the history of the creation of these products in Russia, but also see the national toys of the peoples of other countries. And in the Doll Museum you can visit the hall of fairy-tale characters and workshops on making souvenirs.
  2. The Naval and Military History Museums will be interesting mainly to boys. There are collections of weapons, models of ships, tanks and self-propelled guns. In museums you can learn about the history of weapons, about the great naval battles of Russia and about the discoveries of travelers.
  3. And children interested in warships can also visit the legendary cruiser Aurora.

Museums for kids

Where to go in St. Petersburg with children who love animals

This city offers many places where you can mingle with wildlife. In addition to the magnificent world-famous zoo, you can bring children to the place where they not only see animals, but also touch and feed them.

  1. You can chat with dolphins and watch the performance of sea lions in dolphinariums. Here, children will hear whale songs and see a walrus.
  2. Even more representatives of the sea and river depths are represented in the aquarium. Children will be delighted to see outlandish fish; they will stand near an aquarium with piranhas and sharks.
  3. Fans of cats will be interested in visiting the only museum in Russia dedicated to these furry pets, and its branch "Republic of Cats".
  4. Great entertainment for children in St. Petersburg is to visit this tropical forest. By visiting the Butterfly Garden, you will not only enjoy the views of wildlife, but you can also take a close look at the living winged beauties.
  5. A visit to the Living Key nature museum is simply an unforgettable vacation with children. There are also exhibitions in St. Petersburg where you can touch and pet animals, but this place is unique. The museum is interactive, so children are waiting for real adventures in the desert, cave or in the forest.
