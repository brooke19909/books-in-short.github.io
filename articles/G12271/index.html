A little bit about the resort ...

The most attractive moment for vacationers are the good beaches of these places. They have good gentle descents into the water and a rugged coastline, which makes it possible to hide from the waves in any of the many picturesque bays. For this reason, divers love this place.



There are a lot of completely different hotels in Okurcalar. However, the lack of any cultural attractions should be considered a disadvantage of the resort. Tours and attractions

As we have already mentioned, the Litore Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel is in a great place to relax. However, to see local attractions, you will have to go to the famous Alanya, located only thirty kilometers away. The city is one of the most beautiful and charming places in Turkey. Of course, it can not be compared with the bustling Istanbul, but it is attractive in its own way as a resort.

He deserves to break away from sea recreation and admire its beauties. The main attraction of Alanya is the fortress. It is perfectly preserved and is a historical value, because it stands out by its considerable size among similar ancient monuments in Turkey. Once inside the fortress there was a mint, a church, a mosque and a monastery, a winter palace of the sultan himself.



The Kyzyl-Kule tower located in the port of Alanya is no less interesting in historical terms. Countless tourists visit her. This structure appeared thanks to the famous Seljuk Sultan Aladdin Keykubat. To appreciate the beauty and power of the tower, you need to climb to its very top in 85 steps. She faithfully served for many centuries the inhabitants of the city, and therefore deservedly became its symbol.

Fans of active and educational recreation should definitely visit the famous caves of Alanya. Nature has created along the Mediterranean coast a huge number of grottoes and caves. Three of them are most interesting for tourists: Dim Magarasy, Karain Magarasy, Damlatash Magarasy.

Damlatas Cave is located in the very center of Alanya. It was discovered during the construction of the port in 1948. The cave is stunning with its beauty, it is almost completely covered with stalagmites and stalactites, whose age reaches 15 thousand years. It is very useful to be for those people who suffer from respiratory diseases. Strong humidity, stable air temperature, high carbon dioxide content have a beneficial effect on the human body.

Leisure

As a rule, they go to sea resorts as a whole family to relax, and therefore it is necessary to look for entertainment that will be interesting to absolutely everyone. Such a place is Sealanya Park. It can be spent all day. To fully see it, you should ride on the "Slow River" (the so-called water attraction). There is also a dolphinarium in the park.
