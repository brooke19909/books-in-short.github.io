Who are transformers?

In the wake of this popularity, the birth of the concept of characters from "Lego", which have a humanoid shape, and when the rearrangement of parts take the image of any vehicle. It was this important moment that served to develop interest in how to make a transformer out of Lego.

Transformers are robot characters coming from comics. They gained popularity and popular recognition with the release of films about alien creatures called Autobots and Decepticons. Each of them is able to turn into any technological tool. It could be a car, a plane, or something else.

Tips to get you started

If we discuss the question: ‚ÄúHow to make a transformer out of Lego?‚Äù, It is worth noting that there is a significant difference between assembling a similar model from creating other characters. The fact is that each fragment has dual functions: it is part of the body of a humanoid robot and at the same time a mechanism. This point must be kept in mind when assembling. Another important detail regarding the creation of a transforming robot is the construction of a stand for a figure from Lego. Robot assembly

If you need a simpler option, in which the robot will not fold into a vehicle, but will have an anthropomorphic shape, then it is much easier to implement such an idea:

  1. It will take two small Lego fragments to construct the feet. They must be fixed on a pedestal parallel to each other.
  2. Then begin to build the legs of the robot, superimposing parts one on top of the other. At a certain height, using two identical parts, designate the knees of the robot.
  3. The body of the transformer should be massive (especially the chest and shoulders), so you need to fold a large rectangle, which in the upper part should be weighted with additional details and connect it to the finished legs.
  4. If you want to give the robot a look that indicates a potential transformation into a vehicle, then the body and limbs can be supplemented with fragments of the designer equipped with wheels.
  5. Next, on the body on both sides, you need to attach the hands of the robot created from long narrow blocks.
  6. And finally, in the center you need to install a large cube - the head of the transformer. You can also attach small rectangles on its sides to it. Sometimes there is a special robot head in the Lego set: in this case, use it.
  7. At the base of the rectangles mounted on the head of the toy, blocks with wheels should be strengthened.
