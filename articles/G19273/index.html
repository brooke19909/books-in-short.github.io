Hillside Resort Pattaya 3 *: reviews, room descriptions, service. Holidays in Pattaya

Soon, a wonderful vacation time will come again. And this means that many of our compatriots will face the question: where to go to relax and which direction this time to choose? Today, Russians, for obvious reasons, are increasingly choosing exotic tours. The fact is that today people are interested not only in idle holidays on the seashore - a beautiful tan and bathing. Many try to combine it with sightseeing.



Recently, tours to Pattaya have been very popular. From Moscow to this Thai resort, seven to eight hours of flight. But such a long flight today few people stop. After all, Pattaya is considered one of the most famous resorts in the kingdom of Thailand.

general information

Few people know that once upon a time there was a small fishing village. The situation radically changed during the years of the Vietnam War, when American troops began to be deployed nearby. Everywhere began to build hotels, bars and other entertainment venues, initially intended for the military.

Today, most tourists associate this resort with complete freedom. It is for such sensations that about two million vacationers fly here every year. Our compatriots do not stand aside. Tours to Pattaya from Moscow, Novosibirsk, etc. in the high season are literally like hot cakes. Some Russians prefer to stay in hotels, of which there are several hundred, while others rent villas or apartments. In Thailand, foreigners, including our compatriots, are loved, which is not surprising: they are the main and sometimes the only source of income for local residents.

Popular resort

Pattaya, reviews of travelers about holidays in which the vast majority are delighted, receives tourists from all over the world all year round. There is always beautiful weather. Even from April to November, when the rainy season begins in the rest of Thailand, the flow of tourists to Pattaya is not reduced. Unlike other islands, the prices at this resort are very affordable. Here tourists are offered a huge list of entertainment. Restaurants, bars and nightclubs are open until the morning. Time in Pattaya is very fast.

People come here not only to swim and sunbathe, but also to go scuba diving, to study the local culture and attractions, and simply in search of new adventures and sensations.

Unfortunately, Pattaya is not as developed as, for example, Phuket. There is no international airport. The nearest air harbor is about forty kilometers away. The airport operates mainly charter flights. I must say that this is a fairly profitable way to fly for those tourists who save on everything, including airfare. From our country, charter flights are carried out mainly from Moscow, although there are flights from Omsk, Novosibirsk and Rostov-on-Don.
