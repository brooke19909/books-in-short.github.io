Hotel Detelina Park Hotel 3 * (Bulgaria / Golden Sands): description of the hotel and leisure, reviews

The first thing that comes to mind when mentioning Bulgaria is its wonderful resorts, which have attracted Russians since Soviet times. Especially popular among them have always been and remain Sunny Beach and Golden Sands. It is in the last of them that the Detelina Park Hotel 3 * is located (“Detelina”, a hotel), which we will talk about in this article. But before proceeding with its description, we also bring to your attention a brief overview of Bulgaria as a tourist destination, as well as the Golden Sands resort itself, whose beaches can compete with the most prestigious resorts in the world.



Sunny Bulgaria

What attracts this country to tourists from all over the world? Bulgaria is one of the most ancient and colorful countries, not only in Eastern, but throughout Europe. It is famous for its centuries-old traditions, its beautiful architecture, incredibly tasty and as "sunny" as it is in general, cuisine, in a word, this country has a rich history. It is located on the Balkan Peninsula, but is not washed by the waters of the Mediterranean, but by the Black Sea. But, as the Russians say, visiting the resorts of Bulgaria, they have it is very special. Indeed, you will not find such beaches in either Georgian or Russian, including Crimean resorts. They are strewn here with the smallest, absolutely golden sand, which is the main "wealth" of Bulgaria. Moreover, this country is favorable for mixed tourism: it is attractive both for fans of excursions and for lovers of “lazy” beach holidays. In addition, there are very affordable prices, and comfortable triples, for example, Detelina Park Hotel 3 * (Bulgaria / Golden Sands) provide tourists with the highest services at very low prices. In addition to the most famous resorts mentioned, you can also visit Albena, Bansko, Borovets, Nessebar, Sozopol and others.



The climate of Bulgaria

If you want to buy a tour to the hotel Detelina Park Hotel 3 * (Golden Sands), then you are naturally interested in how long to book it, in which month the weather is most favorable for a beach holiday. So, the climate on the southern coast of Bulgaria is very close to the Mediterranean, which means that the tourist season opens here from the end of May and lasts until the end of September - beginning of October. True, in comparison with many resorts of the Balkans washed by other seas, here the air temperature is several degrees lower. In July, in Varna (in the closest city to the Golden Sands resort), the air warms up to 30 degrees, and the water - up to 23 degrees. In summer, the days are mostly sunny, there is not much rain, but the sea can storm for several days. And then on the beaches a black flag is hung.

How to get to Bulgaria?

Firstly, all Russians and citizens of the CIS countries who want to visit this country need to apply for visas. Well, that’s if they go there on their own, "savages." If you buy a tour to a hotel, for example, in the Detelina Park Hotel 3 *, then all documents, including visas, will be issued by a travel agency. Depending on which resort you are going to, you can purchase tickets to one of the 4 airports in the country. Air gates are available in the capital of Sofia, in Burgas, in Plovdiv and in Varna. The choice, of course, depends on which city you want to go to first or which hotels you choose. Bulgaria, Detelina Park Hotel 3 *, as you already know, is located in the Golden Sands resort, which is located in close proximity to the city of Varna. Tourists are often offered to take the train or bus.



Zlatni Pyasitsi

In this chapter we will tell you about the famous Golden Sands resort, where the aforementioned Detelina Park Hotel 3 * is located. It is by far the most popular among all recreation areas belonging to the Black Sea coast of Bulgaria. The resort stretches for several kilometers between Varna and Kavarna. The first tourist sites appeared here in the middle of the 20th century. Since then, the infrastructure in the area has developed with incredible intensity. Hotel complexes, restaurants and bars, cabarets, clubs, discos and other entertainment facilities opened with incredible speed. Due to the proximity of mineral springs, balneological health resorts were built in the wooded area. After leaving the socialist camp, the development of the resort went in a different direction. For several years, hotels belonging to the global brand were opened, numerous water parks, attractions, etc. appeared. In short, the resort began to live a completely new life. For incredible cleanliness and grooming, for high service, Golden Sands Beach was awarded the EU Blue Flag. This achievement attracted tourists from capitalist Europe, especially the northern parts. From a topographic point of view, Golden Sands looks like a simple, even, straight and wide strip of sand, along which squares of hotels and resorts are located not far from each other. One among them is Detelina Park Hotel 3 *. It is located on the border of yellow and green lines. Behind the hotel-beach zone begins a wide strip of the natural park.



Detelina Park Hotel 3 *: hotel description and location

This hotel is located in a beautiful park. The distance from Varna Airport is 20 km, from the city itself - 16 km. The hotel is part of the resort "Chaika", which is located 3 kilometers from the center of Golden Sands. The distance to the sea is about 300-400 meters, which is not so far. It will appeal to all those who love peace and quiet. Most tourists go down to the sea only in the early morning, after breakfast, or in the evenings, and spend the rest of the time by the beautiful pool with a relaxation area like tents and a beautiful pool bar. Various events are often organized here: weddings, anniversaries, graduations, corporate parties, conferences and symposiums. However, most often families come here who are attracted by the opportunity to spend their vacation calmly away from the hustle and bustle. Although for them there is a small minus, which you can find out if you read the 3 * reviews left about the Detelina Park Hotel. We are talking about its inconvenient location. The thing is that the hotel is built on the slope of a rather steep hill, and if many people enjoy the descent with ease, the climb, especially for kids, is tiring.

Number of rooms

The Detelina Park Hotel 3 * hotel has 126 comfortable rooms of various categories. Among them, 101 are standard doubles, 18 are triple, also standard and 7 rooms belong to the “luxury” category. They, unlike the standard ones, consist of two rooms - a living room, upholstered furniture with a coffee table and a bedroom. One of the suites is designed for honeymooners. All rooms have as many beds as they can accommodate. If necessary, a clamshell is installed in the room. The list of mandatory room equipment includes air conditioners, minibars and safes (for a fee), direct dial telephones, TVs with satellite channels, among which there are 3 Russian ones, bathrooms have a toilet and shower, a set of towels, a hairdryer and toiletries. The deluxe rooms are spacious and consist of 2 rooms - a living room with a seating area and upholstered furniture. The rooms are equipped with balconies or terraces with outdoor furniture. Room service operates around the clock (you can order food and drinks from the restaurant). Linen is changed and rooms are cleaned 1-2 times a week.



Hotel infrastructure

This hotel offers luggage storage and a safe; parking for cars (some tourists come here in their own cars, others rent them at local “car rents.” There is also a currency exchange, fitness facilities, a children's corner, laundry and dry cleaning, etc. In front of the hotel among The park has a fairly large outdoor pool, with sun loungers and umbrellas, which, unlike those on the beaches, are free, and there is also a billiards table and table tennis.



Nutrition

This three-star hotel offers tourists the following types of food: BB - breakfast only and HB - half board. All tourists enjoy a morning meal, but not everyone chooses dinner. Nevertheless, the cuisine in the hotel is very diverse, with a predominance of Bulgarian dishes rich in vegetables and spices. By the way, there is the possibility of ordering packed lunches, which tourists can take with them to the beach. This allows them to stay longer by the sea and not rush to return to dinner at the hotel. The pool has a bar. Tourists who prefer to relax by the pool, as a rule, order here soft and hot drinks, light snacks, etc. There is also a lobby bar at the reception. Various alcoholic and soft drinks are served here. On the territory there is also a barbecue area where you can taste grilled meat, fish and vegetables, as well as a wide variety of salads. The main restaurant is located on the ground floor. It holds about 120 seats. It adjoins a covered terrace with additional places.

Conference hall

The Detelina Park Hotel 3 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) has a well-equipped room for conferences and other large-scale events (up to 100 people). There is also a meeting room, a business center. An interesting program is organized for participants in these events, including excursion and entertainment.



Beach

The hotel has no private beach. The nearest city is 400-600 meters away. Locals come here on weekends, so if you want to have time to take a deck chair and an umbrella, you need to hurry. In addition to a wide range of water activities (parachute flying, scooter, diving, snorkeling, catamaran), you will also be offered horse riding, beach volleyball and tennis.

Reviews about the hotel Detelina Hotel 3 * (Golden Sands / The Seagull)

Guest ratings for the aforementioned hotel are mixed. There are both positive and negative. What is the reason for this? Well, firstly, do not forget that this is just a 3-star budget hotel - do not demand from it the service inherent in luxury hotels. The main complaints are that cleaning and linen change in the rooms is very rare. In the bathrooms it smells bad, the Internet catches badly, and the TV remote does not always work. In addition, some Russians feel the hostility of the hotel staff, attributing this to Russophobia. Although there are tourists who, on the contrary, talk about the hospitality and hospitality of the local population.
