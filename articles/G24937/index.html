Tax return for transport tax. Sample filling and deadlines for filing a declaration

In Russia, cars that are equipped with engines are taxed. The higher the power of the vehicle (TS), the more you will have to pay money. Read more about how to calculate and fill out a declaration, read on.

Who should submit the data

The transport declaration is used to provide reports on the amount of tax for all types of vehicles. The payers of the fee are the legal entities on which the car is registered (Article 357 of the Tax Code of the Russian Federation). If the company does not have a vehicle, then the report is not submitted.





When and where to submit data

The state registration of a car is carried out at the location of the enterprise or its separate division. The tax return for transport tax is submitted to the appropriate inspection. Immediately appropriate checkpoint. If the organization is classified as a large taxpayer, then reporting should be filed at the place of registration. The deadlines for filing a declaration are regulated by the Tax Code. According to Art. 363, reporting should be submitted no later than February 1 of the year following the reporting year. Untimely submission of a declaration is an offense for which a fine of 5% of the tax amount is provided, but at least 1000 rubles. (v. 119). If the organization paid part of the fee, the penalty is calculated from the difference between the amounts. If the report is submitted late, the inspection may charge an official a fine in the amount of 300-5000 rubles. Therefore, it is worthwhile to know in advance the deadlines for submitting a declaration.

More about the odds

Calculation of tax on an expensive car has its own nuances. Since 2015, increasing coefficients have been applied to such vehicles, and advance payments for transport tax are calculated in the usual manner. The list of luxury cars is approved by the Ministry of Industry and Trade and is displayed on the agency‚Äôs website. If there is no specific vehicle in the list, then the tax is calculated in the usual manner. If the car is listed, then you must first determine two indicators: age and average cost of a car. The size of the coefficient depends on them.





The age of the vehicle corresponds to the year of manufacture. According to Art. 361 of the Tax Code, the number of full years is determined as of January 1 of the current year. In this case, the calculation starts from the period following the release of the car. That is, if the tax amount is calculated in 2016 for 2015, the number of years is determined as of 01.01.2016. It is very important to correctly fill out the declaration at this point. Even if the car is old, but is listed, there may not be a coefficient on it. Then the tax will not increase.

Average car price

This indicator is calculated in the order approved by the Ministry of Industry and Trade No. 316. The document has two formulas. In both cases, identical indicators are used: the purchase price of the car and the current value at the time of release. The calculations are strongly influenced by the price of a new TS analogue that is in effect during the billing period.

The first formula is used when the vehicle manufacturer or its authorized person has a representative in the Russian Federation. In this case, the calculation is based on retail prices. Manufacturers provide these data to the Ministry of Industry and Trade, they are available on the Ministry‚Äôs website. The average cost of the vehicle is calculated by adding up the retail prices valid on July 1 and December 1 of the reporting period. The result is divided by 2.





The second formula is used when the manufacturer and his authorized person do not have their representative offices on the territory of the Russian Federation, or when data on the machine is not provided to the Ministry of Industry and Trade. In this case, for the purposes of tax calculation, retail prices for new vehicles with the appropriate characteristics (model name and year of manufacture) indicated on December 31 of the reporting period in Russian or foreign catalogs (FSUE NAMI, Audatex, KelleyBlue Book, DAT, Mitchel, Canadien are used) Black Book, Motor, Schwacke). The same data must be used for vehicles older than 5 years.

To determine the average price for Russian catalogs, it is necessary to sum the maximum and minimum cost of a car for the reporting period at the prices indicated in the catalogs, and then divide the result by 2.

Prices from foreign catalogs are calculated in two stages. First, the cost of the vehicle is determined by the formula used for Russian catalogs. Then, the obtained value is adjusted by two coefficients: conversion of prices into rubles and utilization fee.

Average cost = value obtained by the formula for Russian catalogs * price conversion ratio + amount of utilization fee and import duty

Price conversion ratio = (foreign exchange rate as of 01.01 of a car + foreign currency rate as of 31.12 of a car) / 2

Individuals

Citizens of Russia who have cars must also pay the transport tax. The return of the declaration is carried out online. To do this, you need to log in (enter the TIN and password) on the tax service website . Then go to the section "Objects of taxation" and enter data about the car. If the user cannot get into the personal account on the site, then he needs to gain access: contact the tax office with a passport, code and register in the system.

There are situations when a car is listed on the property list, but a tax notice is not received. In such cases, you need to submit an application in your personal account with a request to calculate the amount of the fee on the vehicle. A scan copy of the registration certificate, Title, sales contract is attached to the letter.

Since 2017, fines are introduced. In case of failure to provide information on the availability of vehicles, an individual will have to additionally pay 20% of the tax amount to the budget.

Some citizens still receive notice by mail. The document is sent at least 30 days before the payment date. Each region of Russia has its own deadlines, but in general, the payment date should not be earlier than 30.11. That is, citizens can pay the tax for 2014 after 11/30/2015.

Information on the availability of debt can be found in the Bank of enforcement proceedings. The FSSP has developed special applications for the social networks VKontakte and Odnoklassniki. There are also applications of the same name for smartphones, through which you can find out the amount of tax calculated and receive the latest news.
