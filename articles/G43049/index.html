Chukovsky Park in Rostov-on-Don: address and reviews

Each city can boast of its own park, as this is the place where a person can relax in body and soul. It has long been known that the green massif and beautiful views soothe much better than any healing broth. Foliage helps to feel the freshness of the air and fill it with new strength. Chukovsky Park in Rostov-on-Don is the pride of the townspeople, and is also often visited by city guests. To find this object is quite simple, since almost all Rostovites know about it.





general information

Many parks bear the names of famous personalities. In particular, the names and surnames of writers and artists are often used for names. So, the Chukovsky park in Rostov-on-Don reminds the townspeople of a writer who made a considerable contribution to literature. In addition to wonderful fairy tales for kids, he is known for many more outstanding deeds. The author also studied linguistics, translations, studied literature, and tried himself as a critic. During his life, he wrote many significant works that gave pleasant minutes and emotions. Citizens call this park in another way. His second name is Ashgabat. The place has a rather curious shape, since on the map it looks like a triangle. In the Soviet era, one could come here to enjoy a movie directly in the open air. Now in the park you can see only a reminder that there was once a cinema here. But he still remains one of the favorite places of Rostovites. Here you can see a considerable number of people not only on weekends, but also on weekdays. People save the park from pollution and clutter, as they spend subbotniks here. Usually, organizations involved in environmental protection are responsible for this.

The perfect place to relax

The park attracts citizens with shady alleys and fresh air. It is also beautiful in the cold, so you can often see family photo shoots. Chukovsky's park in Rostov-on-Don is decorated with another attraction - the church of the Roman Catholic parish of the Last Supper. She collects a lot of people, and in the summer near her you can admire beautiful flowers, among which roses and other gifts of nature. The building began to be built back in 1999, and the church opened a little later. Divine services are held in it, to which many people come. In 2004 the church was consecrated.





In the park you can see mothers with children, couples walking and nature lovers. Many people come here with their four-legged friends, for whom there is expanse. In 2017, a plan for landscaping was approved. The park will be able to boast a brand new football field and a complex for children. This is great news for parents, as their children will be even more interesting to walk there. Model Natalya Vodyanova and her foundation have allocated the necessary funds for this. The park will soon also have new paths, benches and lighting.

Fight for the park

The history of the park, which is one hundred years old, could be supplemented by the construction of another church. The townspeople decided to stand their ground firmly, as they opposed the development of their favorite vacation spot. Not far from the park, in addition to the Catholic Church, there is also the Orthodox Church. Residents of Rostov created a petition and actively participated in ensuring that everything remains as it is. The government of the region after a wave of protests reported that in the near future they do not plan to build anything like this in the Chukovsky park in Rostov-on-Don.





Park reviews

The place of rest attracts people of all ages, as it is the most popular in Rostov. Citizens really appreciate the green areas, since there are not so many of them in the city. In their reviews of the park, most often they report that they hope for its further refinement. In the blogs of users, you can see a lot of photos of Chukovsky's park in Rostov-on-Don, where green alleys and flowers are depicted. Girls really appreciate roses that grow right next to the church. This makes a good impression, especially in sunny weather. However, Rostovites also have concerns that buildings are possible on the territory that will reduce the number of trees. Therefore, there are many activists who are following this issue.





How to get there

Finding a popular park is a snap. Even if you just look at the map, you can see a green triangle, signed by the name of a famous writer. Chukovsky Park in Rostov-on-Don is located in the Oktyabrsky District. On three sides it is surrounded by streets: Caesar Kunikov, Ashgabat and Stavropol lanes. The green oasis got its second name in honor of one of the streets. Therefore, the citizens can also ask Ashgabat Park. In any case, the path will lead exactly to where you need it. Closer to Ashgabat Lane there is a cafe called "Argo", where you can eat after a walk in the fresh air.

The address of Chukovsky Park in Rostov-on-Don: 1 Stavropolsky Lane.
