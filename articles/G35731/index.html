Where to go on vacation in Saki? Weather, sea, hotels, prices

G. Saki is located on the western coast of the Crimean peninsula, five kilometers from the sea. Many people come to this well-known balneological resort to improve their health. And really, mineral springs, healing mud, arboretum and magnificent nature - all this helps thousands of tourists not only from Russia, but also from the former CIS countries and Europe to come to Saki. Crimea, the prices for vacations in which are becoming more and more stable from year to year, over the past two or three years has become a favorite vacation spot for many of our compatriots who previously chose several other destinations, for example, Egypt or Turkey.

A bit of history

There are several versions about the origin of oikonym. According to one, the city was given the name of the Scythians, who once inhabited it. According to the second version, ‚Äúmud‚Äù is translated from Turkic as ‚Äúsaki‚Äù.



The exact date of the appearance of the first settlement on the site of the city has not been established. About twenty-five centuries ago, this part of the Crimean coast began to be actively inhabited by Greek colonists. Some historians believe that it was from this time that the history of Sak began. At the beginning of our millennium, the village was well known to the Romans, as evidenced by the numerous ancient coins found during excavations in the vicinity of the resort.

The mud from Lake Saki for medicinal purposes began to be used by people in the Middle Ages. According to legend, he noticed the healing of local silt Chumak, who came to these places for salt. When his heavily overloaded oxen got stuck in the coastal mud, he had to carry with them until the morning, stamping in slush with his sore feet. However, on arriving home, Chumak was surprised to find that a long-standing pain that tormented him for many years had left him ...

Since 1827, the county doctor S. Auger began to officially practice in Saki, doing exclusively mud therapy. This decision was prompted by the results of those studies conducted by Russian scientists who revealed the chemical composition and unique healing properties of the mixture.

Relaxation

People come to Saki to plunge into a salt lake, which stretches for five kilometers near the village. It appeared on the site of a river flooded about five millennia ago by the Black Sea, at the bottom of which clay, pebbles, silt and salt were gradually deposited. The resulting mixture, as it turned out, has healing properties. It is for the sake of this therapeutic mud that many come here to rest. You can come to Saki in any season. The mud here has a unique composition, in some respects it is even better than that in the Dead Sea.



Salt of local lakes in the Middle Ages began to be consumed: it is here that the famous Chumatsky Way runs along which the Cossacks used to go to Crimea for ‚Äúwhite gold‚Äù.

Another factor due to which many Russians choose a vacation here: tourists come to Saki to visit the slightly alkaline mineral springs. Their water in its properties is not inferior to analogues from Essentuki or Truskavets.

What can be seen

People come to this resort not only for rest: many fly to Saki, judging by the reviews, in order to combine treatment with visiting local attractions. Here you can visit the Church of St. Elijah, the local history museum, the Greco-Scythian pantheon Kara-Tobe.

The city park is one of the most beautiful places in Sak. Here, in a man-made arboretum, under the shadow of eighty species of trees and bushes, thirty interesting sculptural compositions are placed.

The combination of thermal springs, silt mud, sea air and a large number of pine groves makes the Sak climate very beneficial. Being here, you should definitely visit Mikhailovsky Lake.

Sanatoriums in Saki

The resort is known as a place where people come not only to relax, but also to be treated. According to doctors, ideal conditions have been created for the treatment of many gynecological diseases, including infertility. Local climatic conditions combined with balneological resources help doctors successfully cope with diseases of the nervous and cardiovascular systems, as well as digestive ailments.

Poltava

Many Crimean sanatoriums were built in Soviet times. But today they are completely renovated and turned into unique hospitals. Some of them, for example, Poltava (Saki), had at one time all-Union fame. Today, the fame of this Crimean health resort has reached Europe. People come here from Germany and Israel, from Canada and many other countries. Annually, Poltava receives more than two thousand patients from far and near abroad. She was awarded high awards both from domestic resort organizations and from European ones. The cost of living with treatment in a double room (with meals) in the high season starts at one thousand eight hundred rubles per day.



Sea and beaches - a wonderful vacation

You can come to Saki at any time of the year. Rest here is somewhat different from that offered by neighboring Crimean resorts. The fact is that in Saki the sea beach as such does not exist, since the city is not located on the coast. Therefore, those who want to leave the hotel in a couple of minutes to be on the beach are not recommended to go here. You need to get to the sea by public transport, which drives off to the beach stop. From Sak to the sea ten minutes drive.

The closest is the central city beach. It has a sand cover, it is fully equipped, admission is free. In seven kilometers, near the village of Novofedorovka, there is another good beach. But many prefer to go to the wild bathing area, located behind the children's camp. Titova.

Where to stay

Guest houses are very popular with tourists. Saki is a place where you can come, hoping for an economical vacation. Guest houses here are surprisingly high level of service and reasonable prices. In the private sector, as elsewhere in the Crimea, you can relatively inexpensive to rent a house that fully meets the needs. This option is perfect for those tourists who come here on an economical vacation, in guest houses. Saki, judging by the reviews, is a resort where everyone will find a room for their taste and budget. And guest houses offer services similar to those in hotels or hotels, but at prices much more affordable.
