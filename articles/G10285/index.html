"Nikitinsky estate" - modern Peter

Nikitinsky Manor is a district of the northern capital that has been gradually being built up with low-rise buildings for more than 15 years. And a huge number of people have become property owners here. Various reasons have pushed them to make such a decision.

Quality, progress, a decent standard of living



Nikitinsky Manor is a new solution, a progressive version of the development of St. Petersburg. There are no typical "anthills" - high-rise buildings. And therefore there is no gas contamination, noise. The architecture of the area is in perfect harmony with nature. Each house has good consumer characteristics. Price and quality indicators in this case are combined in the best way. Residents of the district are socially homogeneous. They have similar views on how modern housing should be. It is safe and comfortable here thanks to the presence of good neighbors.

Safety and nature



Club houses in the area use control and security systems. On its territory there are surveillance cameras. This is necessary to ensure the safety of residents. There are few random people here - almost everyone knows each other in person. The whole charm of a low-rise area is that the quality of life in it corresponds to high European standards. "Nikitinsky estate" is located on a hill. Century-old trees grow here, there is a park, square, pond. Great ecology is another reason to choose this area to stay. Silence and fresh air allow you not to close the windows at night, enjoying peace.

Location and status of the area



At the beginning of the 19th century, there was the mansion of Alexei Nikitin - the hero of the war of 1812. And the building of this estate is now considered a monument of federal significance. Each street of the district is saturated with a peculiar spirit of antiquity, space, Russian way of life. "Nikitinsky estate" is located in the developed part of the northern capital. Nearby are hypermarkets, numerous shops, medical centers. There are several stadiums, parks, a huge number of playgrounds. It is easy to get to the city center from here - it is really possible to do this in just 20 minutes. Transport accessibility is one of the reasons for choosing this area to stay.
