Hotel Checkin Garbi 3 * (Calella, Spain): photos and reviews of tourists

Since the Spanish coast is popular among our compatriots, in our article we want to talk about one of the resort hotels with the beautiful name Calella. Checkin Garbi 3 * is a family-run hotel that will delight its guests with its proximity to the sea, which is important for sea holidays.

A bit about the hotel

Checkin Garbi 3 * is located in the town of Calella, near Maresme. Barcelona is only fifty kilometers from the hotel. The complex can be reached on its own, without any transfers by train. Near the hotel there are many shops and cafes. Chekin Garbi 3 * can be a great place to relax as a whole family, or a group of friends.



The hotel was built back in 1971 and since then every year has been welcoming many tourists within its walls. Its last reconstruction was made in 2008. Since the complex is located almost in the center of Calella, it is easy to get to local attractions from it, which include the excellent water park Water World, the Badalona Sports Palace, the Iberian town of Turo Dan Bosca.

A bit about the resort

Calella is a resort and municipality in Spain, part of the Maresme region of the Autonomous Community of Catalonia. The city is quite democratic and perfectly suits completely different categories of tourists. A huge number of modern hotels, restaurants, cafes, shops have been built here. The developed infrastructure gives vacationers the opportunity to actively have fun and engage in water sports, ride on yachts and take part in other resort activities.



Calella for a reason has gained popularity among tourists. Its gorgeous beaches with golden sand and clear water regularly receive the Blue Flag for cleanliness and amenities, which is a high indicator.

Calella dates back to the first century BC. Already at that time there was a village that had an agricultural focus. Calella acquired the status of the city only in 1925, and until that time it was simply considered a fishing village. Tourism here began to develop only in the second half of the twentieth century. And now Calella has the status of a famous resort, attracting many tourists every year.

Calella is located in a zone of wonderful climate (Mediterranean tropical) with warm summers and cold winters. In summer, the average temperature is +28 degrees, and in winter it almost never drops below +15 degrees. The season at the resort lasts from April to October.

How to get to the hotel?

Hotel Checkin Garbi 3 * has a good location, making it easy to get to. There are regular flights from Russia to Barcelona. And during the peak season, many charters are added. From Barcelona to Calella is very easy to get, a comfortable train will take you to the resort in just an hour. A ticket for an adult will cost you only 6-8 EUR, and for a child - 4-6 EUR. The train station is located near the hotel. Therefore, tourists may not arrange a transfer at the hotel, but will get to their destination on their own.



Resting in Calella, you will certainly want to see Spain. For independent travel, it is best to rent a car. However, you should know that for this you will need international rights and, of course, a passport (to confirm your age). The cost of renting a car is very different, but on average it is about thirty dollars a day. By the way, you can rent a car right at the airport. Such a service is very common in Europe.

Resort beaches

Calella attracts tourists with its stunning beaches. The sandy coast from the embankment is limited by a beautiful shady walking alley that stretches along the entire city (more than three kilometers), and then goes into a rocky, picturesque stretch of the coast. Local landscapes are secluded coves with sharp cliffs. The coastline is very similar to the scenery of the Costa Brava.



The beaches of Calella are beautiful and clean. Moreover, the coast is far from one beach. There are a sufficient number of them. The largest is the central one, but it is always crowded with people. Therefore, in search of a more peaceful place, you can walk in the direction of Sant Polu, where there are very sparsely populated bays. But with all this, the entire coast is landscaped.

Checkin Garbi 3 *: reviews

Speaking of the complex, I certainly want to discuss the reviews of tourists who rested in it. Why is the hotel Checkin Garbi 3 * good? Reviews of tourists about this hotel are very positive, although, of course, there are some nuances that we want to discuss.

According to guests, the hotel has a great location and is located on the first line. It would seem, what else can you dream of at a seaside resort? Indeed, the complex was built near the sea, and therefore to the beach go no more than two minutes. But such a close location to the embankment has its negative aspects. There is a nightclub near the hotel, where music rattles until the morning, and European youth after the disco behaves quite noisily. All this prevents tourists from resting at night. It is impossible to sleep with an open door to the sounds of the surf, but closed windows and doors do not contain much noise.

The rest of the hotel is quite decent. Rooms overlooking the coast have stunning sea views. Although the hotel is not new, it is in good condition. Maids clean the apartments very carefully daily. There are never any problems with the replacement of towels and bed linen, they are changed according to the schedule. The maids are very friendly and always perform quality work.

Local Attractions

In the city, new beautiful neighborhoods are adjacent to the old streets. There is something to see here. Local excursion programs necessarily include a visit to the ancient Iglesia de Santa Maria church (16-18 centuries), palaces and a park, which is a green array of tropical plants, which is a real oasis during the heat of the day.



In Calella you definitely need to walk, there are many hiking trails that cover the entire historical part of the city. It is no less interesting to walk along the shady alley on the embankment, along which an incredible number of shops and cafes are located. And late in the evening, numerous discos and night clubs begin to work here. An incredible number of German youth come to rest in Calella. However, the city is also known as a cultural center, as it hosts folklore, sports and other events. They are of interest to spectators of all ages, so you can safely go to Calella on vacation with your whole family.
