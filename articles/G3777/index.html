About company





So, you should start, of course, with the submission of the developer and the publication of the information that is posted on its official website, and is also available in other open sources of information on the network. After that, reviews will be described about the developer "Granel", as well as about those complexes that were built and commissioned by this company.

First of all, it is worth noting that the company began its work back in 1992. The initial region in which the company planned to conduct its business was Moscow. Further, in the process of growing the customer base and with the gradual expansion of the development business, the company also began construction of facilities in the suburbs.

The structure of the developer is quite complex: the entire business is divided into several companies; moreover, the parent of all of them is GK Granel. The reviews also note that in addition to it there is also Granelle Development, which is related as a subsidiary and at the same time organizes the planning of entire residential neighborhoods that the group is working on.





As for the lines of activity, the company can be called broad-profile, since its specialization includes not only the construction of residential real estate, but also the arrangement of infrastructure for it, as well as the construction of commercial facilities for business. In addition, a wide profile of work is evidenced by the fact that the company's specialists oversee the entire construction cycle, from the creation of plans to the management of running facilities for a comfortable stay and work of people.

In total, if we take into account all the commissioned objects, the company managed to build more than 3 million square meters of real estate, which is equivalent to more than 75 thousand apartments. Agree, already these statistics make a serious impression!
