Baglioni Hotel Luna

Many hotels in the center of Venice have a five-star status. These are the most luxurious and expensive hotels in which at least for a short time you can feel like a royal person. Such establishments offer good accommodation and decent service.

Baglioni Hotel Luna - a hotel in Venice, 5 * - is located in the heart of the romantic city, just 80 meters from the famous Piazza San Marco. It is worth noting that the main feature of the institution is its unique interiors with beautiful frescoes and stunning views of the lagoon. Hotel guests are guaranteed a wonderful vacation. To visit the walls of the aristocratic palace and have a good time is a real pleasure. Staying at such a hotel, you can fully experience the spirit of antiquity. This is facilitated not only by the old building, but also by the district itself, which is considered the oldest in Venice.

The number of rooms of the institution is very diverse. All apartments are luxury. In them you can feel yourself a special royal blood. Tapestries and frescoes are all that will surround you at the hotel.

The room fund of the institution is represented by deluxe suites, superior. All of them are quite spacious. The smallest area is 30 square meters, and the cost of living starts from 250 euros (17.6 thousand rubles).

All apartments have their own characteristics. They are well equipped with the latest technology. It also has marble bathrooms, open terraces and Venetian balconies overlooking Piazza San Marco and the famous island of San Giorgio. Antique furniture and Murano glass chandeliers complement the already luxurious interiors.

The hotel is small. But a stunningly beautiful restaurant, designed in a general style. The chef of the institution offers visitors the most exquisite dishes of Italian cuisine. A buffet breakfast is served each morning. During the day, guests have the opportunity to order snacks in the room. It is worth noting that the luxurious 5 * hotel of Venice even has its own pier. So you can get here by gondola or boat. Literally 50 meters from the hotel there is a water bus stop where you can reach all the key points of the city. The hotel complex is located in a very convenient location, since near St. Mark's Square there are many attractions and interesting museums that all guests seek to visit.

Hotel Daniel

Hotel "Daniel" in Venice can rightly be attributed to one of the best institutions in the city. It has an interesting story. The hotel building was built in the fourteenth century on Calle del Rasse. It offers views of the promenade. At that time, the palace belonged to the Dandolo family. Experts say that in historical chronicles it is said that the palazzo was one of the richest and most pompous in the city. It often hosted social events. Later, the owners of the building changed, but this did not make it any less luxurious. In 1822, Giuseppe Dal Neal bought the second floor of the palazzo and turned it into a hotel. Subsequently, the entire building became the property of the owner of the hotel. Dal Neal restored the entire complex, as time has already made itself felt. As a result, he converted the palazzo into a chic hotel complex. On the first floor were, as is usually the case, shops and cafes. In the future, the hotel owners changed several times. Repair work was carried out in it in connection with the introduction of electricity, heating and other benefits of civilization. In addition, neighboring buildings were attached to the building using covered bridges. The result was a beautiful architectural complex, which consists of three buildings dating back to the 19th, 14th and 20th centuries. Currently, the hotel's interiors are decorated with valuable carpets, Murano glass chandeliers, antique furniture and marble columns. In 2008, the building was completely renovated. And since 2009, it is on the lists of the 500 best hotel complexes in the world.

One of the best hotels in Venice (photo is given in the article) is a stone's throw from St. Mark's Square. Nearby are museums, cafes, restaurants and attractions. The hotel offers a very large selection of rooms. All of them are in the Venetian style. Tapestries, marble and murals are what awaits you in the luxury apartments. And at the same time, there are all the modern benefits in the form of the Internet, air conditioning, LCD televisions.

On the top floor of the building is a restaurant, which is considered one of the most exclusive in the city. Once upon a time, representatives of the local nobility invariably visited this place, who loved to relax here, watching how merchant ships from the East came in. The restaurant menu is replete with the most exquisite dishes. Here you can taste not only Mediterranean, but also Far Eastern cuisine.

Hotel Ai Reali

The hotel is represented by suites, junior suites, deluxe, classic and comfort. All of them are made in an elegant style and are as comfortable as possible for guests. Rest in such an institution will bring many pleasant minutes. Tourists recommend visiting the spa, which offers a wide and varied range of treatments.

And St. Mark's Square can be reached in just ten minutes. The hotel was opened in 2006 after a major restoration, during which it was possible to reproduce all the pristine splendor.

Currently, the institution pleases with magnificent interiors and freshly renovated. However, there is no antique furniture. The rooms are in Venetian style with elements of modern decor. The apartments have all the necessary amenities.

Aqua Peleis

Aqua Peleis is a classic four-star hotel in Venice, located in the historical center. The complex is located near the bridge and the Biennale Gardens. It was completely renovated not so long ago, in 2010. Since then, his design is designed in antique style. Near the hotel there is a basilica, a theater and several museums.

A diverse number of rooms is made in an elegant style. All apartments are equipped in accordance with modern standards and requirements. In addition, guests will surely enjoy such an element of luxury as a marble bathroom. In the mornings, tourists can enjoy breakfast at a local cafe.

Al sole

Of course, three-star apartments cannot be compared with the chic of institutions with 5 and 4 stars, which is understandable. It is sustained in the Venetian style. It offers its guests comfortable and cozy rooms. All of them are designed in the usual Venetian style.
