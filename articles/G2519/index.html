Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * (Vietnam Duong Dong): hotel description and photos

Vietnam has not yet become one of the most popular tourist destinations, but it is confidently striving for this. Our compatriots became interested in this country not so long ago, but the enthusiastic reviews of tourists left after the trip become a kind of advertising that pushes other people to such a trip. Many of our compatriots from buying a tour to Vietnam are deterred by the fact that it is quite difficult to find reliable information about hotels and its resorts. Travel catalogs present holidays in this country as paradise, but experienced travelers try to find real reviews about holidays in any Vietnamese resort on the Internet.

If you plan, despite everything, to go to this amazing Asian country, then we suggest you stay at Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 *. Reviews about the hotel are few in number, because tourists from Europe and China mainly relax in it, however, our compatriots have already managed to love it. In our article, we tried to collect the most relevant information about this hotel complex, which will help you decide on the choice of a tourist permit.





Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 *: hotel description

The hotel complex, which we are talking about today, is considered one of the newest. However, this distinguishes most of the hotels in Vietnam. The fact is that tourism began to develop in this country not so long ago and provoked numerous construction projects. Until now, tourists have come across resorts at the fact that the noise of construction vehicles is constantly heard around their brand-new hotels, and even at night, the sounds of work reach some rooms.

Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * is two nine-story buildings located opposite each other. It is worth noting that the architects tried to make the hotel as interesting as possible by placing a large swimming pool with a relaxation area between the blocks. It is separated from prying eyes by a wall, so no one will stop tourists from enjoying the gentle sun.

Looking at the photos of Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 *, which are in large numbers posted on the Internet, we can conclude that the territory of the hotel complex is quite small. But every corner of it looks well-groomed and pleases the eye with fresh greenery.

It is noteworthy that the Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * does not position itself as a hotel complex, designed for families with children. There are quite a lot of youth and couples of any age. But still, our compatriots, at their own peril and risk, go to this hotel with the kids. Later, they share their experience with other tourists, considering it more positive. Therefore, we can recommend this complex for a family with a child.

Hotel Location

All tourist catalogs indicate that on the largest island of Vietnam is a four-star Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 *. Phu Quoc is considered the center of the country's tourist life; new hotel complexes and various entertainment venues are being actively built here. In the near future, most tourists from all over the world will come here.

The direct location of Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * is the town of Duong Dong. At the same time, the hotel is in a very convenient place for tourists, from where it is quite easy to get to the main attractions of the island.

For example, Coco Bar, the most visited by guests, is located only five hundred meters from the hotel complex. Those who want to get to know the life and culture of the local population closer can visit the Dean Kau night market. Tourists reach him on foot in about ten minutes.

Another popular tourist destination is the Kau Temple. It is separated from Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * by a distance of seven hundred meters.

Our compatriots, who have already visited the resorts of Vietnam more than once, include the road from the international airport to the resort to the significant disadvantages of such a holiday. Usually it takes at least three hours, in the case when tourists have taken care of an individual transfer, the time can be reduced to one and a half to two hours.

Those flying to Phu Quoc will not have to spend long hours in transport. Indeed, from the international airport to the Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * (Phu Quoc) can be reached in just ten minutes.





Territory and infrastructure

The territory of Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * (Phu Quoc) is not too big. By the standards of city hotels, it looks quite worthy, but those who are used to the vast territories of Turkish hotel complexes may be disappointed with the compactness of everything that surrounds them.

The main place where almost all the guests gather is the pool. It is a rectangular bowl, hidden from prying eyes by a white and blue fence. Around the pool, sun loungers and umbrellas are installed, between them groups of installations made of stones and green spaces are installed. All this turns the hotel into a picturesque garden where you can spend your free time.

There is one restaurant, a bar and a conference room for guests, where you can absolutely free copy the necessary documents or send a fax.

In one of the residential buildings is a spa. It offers hotel guests a fairly wide range of services. In reviews of Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * you can find information about wellness treatments, beauty programs and sauna access. Some of these services are included in the price, while others are paid separately. The price list is available at the entrance to the spa.

Reception is open 24 hours a day. However, the staff practically does not speak Russian. Therefore, to resolve all issues with the hotel staff will have in English. And be prepared for the fact that even English sounds a little peculiar in Vietnam, which can cause certain difficulties in understanding each other.

At the reception there is a luggage storage room and a currency exchange office. Most often, our compatriots on vacation prefer to change money exactly on the territory of hotels. Many avoid communication in an unfamiliar language with bank employees.





Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 *: services

Naturally, before purchasing a ticket to a particular hotel complex, our compatriots want to learn more about the quality and quantity of services provided to vacationers.

The descriptions of Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * quite often provide information about the service, and it is rather highly appreciated by guests who managed to relax here. After all, everyone can fully feel the care of the staff, hourly creating an excellent mood for guests.

For example, it is difficult to imagine a good vacation without access to the Internet. The Wi-Fi signal is excellent at the hotel, so tourists can easily contact their loved ones at any time.

Hotel Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * (Phu Quoc) has a laundry service. It is quite convenient and many tourists enjoy the opportunity to relax from everyday life, despite the fact that this service is paid.

The hotel staff is ready to fulfill your requests around the clock, delivering food, fresh newspapers or other things to the apartment. However, do not forget that at check out you will have to pay for this service.

Most travelers take the opportunity to book a transfer to and from the hotel. If you want to be met at the airport, be sure to indicate this when booking. After checking in, you can always agree at the reception on a transfer to anywhere in the island. The cost of the service depends on the distance. For example, for a transfer from the airport you will pay about two hundred thousand dongs.

Experienced tourists who like to learn new things not as part of a group, but on their own, often rent vehicles to move around the resort. If you consider yourself in this category, then keep in mind that the territory of the Ocean Pearl Hotel 4 * has free parking. Without an appointment, you will be allocated a place on it for the duration of the vacation.

Good to know

At the stage of booking a room, tourists can write about their special preferences, which the staff will surely fulfill. This may relate to food, the location of the windows in the apartment, accommodation and other nuances of relaxation.

Check-in occurs in the hotel complex after two in the afternoon. Almost all guests can check in earlier than the indicated deadline, provided the room is ready. You need to leave it before noon. It is advisable by this time to pay all bills issued for additional services.

Pets are not allowed in the hotel. This is strictly prohibited by the rules of the hotel complex.

If you book more than five rooms at a time, you can get a discount. Its size is usually immediately displayed on a computer monitor.

Rules of settlement and accommodation with children

The hotel complex can be accommodated with children of any age. Children under the age of four years stay in a room with their parents for free, provided that they are accommodated in the same bed.

Children from four to twelve years old can be accommodated in existing beds at an additional cost. Breakfast is also charged - three hundred thousand dong per day.

For children over the age of twelve, parents must pay extra as adults. The cost of one night is equal to twenty-five dollars.

Please note that only one extra bed can be accommodated in this room. In this case, a request for a bed is made out during the booking process. Moreover, the administration of the hotel complex reserves the right to refuse to provide this service due to the fact that no extra bed can be installed in each of the hotel rooms.

A bit about nutrition

Guests eat in the main restaurant. Usually they purchase tours with breakfast included in the price. Almost all guests were satisfied with them. Of the dishes included in the buffet concept, you can always choose something for yourself. In the reviews, tourists write that, despite the small variety of dishes on offer, they are all very tasty and clearly prepared from fresh products.

Cost of living

I would like to say that traveling to Vietnam and staying in a four-star hotel cannot be a cheap pleasure. So get ready to pay a significant amount of money for the tour.

For example, two weeks in this hotel with a flight and breakfast will cost two travelers two hundred and fifty thousand rubles. A similar tour, but for a week will cost a little more than one hundred thousand rubles.

Some tourists prefer to travel on their own. Therefore, separately book air tickets and rooms. The cost of living in superior apartments starts from three thousand rubles per day, the maximum price for a room ranges from fifteen thousand rubles.
