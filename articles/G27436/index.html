Goat do it yourself. Do-it-yourself lambs and goats: patterns, patterns

Want to sew a soft fabric souvenir? Think which idea to choose? Do-it-yourself sheep and goats are easy to do. Take a template. Use it to complete the parts. Make a few stitches and your beautiful home accessory is ready.

DIY goat: patterns

If you decide to sew a soft toy, pillow, souvenir in the form of an animal, you will need a template. You can take it ready or build parts yourself. If you have chosen the sample you like, simply print it on the printer in the required scale. Even if the image is not of very good quality, you can still draw contours. Another way is to attach glass to the monitor and transfer the patterns to a film glued to it.

DIY goat can be made in different ways. There are simple and complex options. If you are not too experienced in this matter, take a pattern with fewer details.

The diagram usually has a legend. Dotted lines indicate the locations of bends or attachments of other elements. In complex multi-part templates, they can be indicated by colored dots at the junction of parts. If you need to make two identical parts, it is better to do them in one step on a fabric folded in half.

DIY interior goat doll:
Master Class

These popular souvenirs are made in the form of animals: cats, dogs, goats, lambs. Make the figure itself according to the pattern shown in the photo below, and sew any outfit. For one character, make panties from the sample, for another - a sarafan or a beautiful dress. A cute couple will decorate your cozy sofa or shelf.



An interior goat doll is sewn with its own hands according to the following pattern:

1. Prepare the necessary tools and materials:

  • fabric for body and clothes (the first patch should be beige, the second - any one at your discretion);
  • internal filling (cotton wool, synthetic winterizer, foam rubber, shreds, special balls);
  • threads
  • pins
  • a needle;
  • scissors;
  • paper for the pattern;
  • plastic eyes, nose;
  • decorative items for clothing (optional): bows, beads, sequins (this is especially true if you are sewing a female doll).

2. Make a pattern in any convenient way that was mentioned above.
3. Cut the received parts.
4. Lay them on the fabric, attach with pins, draw around the contours, cut. Remember to make seam allowances.
5. Sew the items along the wrong side. If necessary, fill the parts with packing.
6. Put it all in one whole object.
7. Clothing is often sewn separately and then worn on the base.
8. Attach the eyes, nose, decor.

Your interior doll is ready.

Goat pillow

Such an accessory is able to update the look of your sofa and diversify the interior. A small pillow can be made in the shape of a toy, enlarging any pattern to the desired size, or stitched in the form of a square, rectangle, circle and supplemented with the corresponding characteristic elements: ears, horns, eyes and nose. Follow the recommendations, and in a few simple steps you will have a cute goat pillow with your own hands. A master class will help with this.





To get such a product, you need to do the following:

  1. Make an oval-sized pillow out of thin paper, a triangular shape for the horns, a pattern of ears and the dark part of the muzzle.
  2. Fold the white fabric in half, pin the pillow pattern with pins, circle the contour with allowance for the seams.
  3. Do the same for the brown part.
  4. Separate the elements from the ears of white and brown fabric and the horns of light.
  5. Sew all the small parts, stitch the brown part on the front side of the pillow, and sew the nose and eyes on it. If they stick, this can be done last.
  6. Sew both parts of the cushion on the wrong side, leaving a hole sufficient to turn the product off. Do this and fill the pillow with prepared soft material.
  7. Gently sew the exposed part of the seam manually.

The pillow is ready!

Application goat

If you are not very confident in your abilities and doubt that you can handle a voluminous product, try decorating an object with a fabric applique. Such a goat with your own hands is done very simply. It is necessary to cut out the necessary parts, for example, muzzles, and stitch them in a zigzag seam to the base. So you can decorate a pillow, bag, cover on furniture, a seat for a stool, a napkin, a bedspread or make a panel on the wall.
