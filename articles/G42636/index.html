Hotel at the Kazan station in Moscow: description, reviews of tourists, address

Kazan Station is the main railway junction. Every day, thousands of people pass through the doors of this station, including visitors to the city. Tourists and business travelers most often need housing in the very place from where it will be convenient for them to get anywhere in the city. The hotel at the Kazan station in Moscow is the convenience and comfort of guests, as well as the opportunity to stay near transport links.

"Retro Hotel"

Address: Komsomolskaya Square, house 2. Room rates from 3000 rubles per day. Nearby is the Olympic Sports Complex, GUM, the Bolshoi Theater and Zaryadye Park. Buffet breakfast is included. Tourists who are looking for cheap hotels near the Kazan station (Moscow) should consider this option.

Interior Description

The interior of the hotel is of European type. Bright rooms with cozy curtains. The rooms have a TV, a set of dishes and a private bathroom. The apartments are located on floors along long, light corridors with water coolers. On the ground floor there is a lounge area with spacious sofas and a TV where you can relax and have coffee.

The restaurant serves European dishes. Hotel guests can order in addition to breakfast, lunch and dinner in the restaurant.

Guests rate this hotel 7.5 points. The biggest disadvantage is that it is very difficult to find the entrance to the hotel. There are no signs or signs. Many say that after reconstruction this problem will be eliminated.

The hotel is located directly at the Kazan station, which is both a plus and a minus. After all, a large number of comers and noise from the station sometimes bring discomfort. Some guests in their reviews note that the audibility in a hotel is worse than in similar hotels.

Guests in reviews are satisfied with both price and quality of service. This is ideal for relaxing off the road or spending the night for several days.

Hilton

If you are looking for hotels near the Kazan Station (Moscow) inexpensively, then you are definitely not in the "Hilton". Here, pricing is above average.

Hotel "Hilton" allows residents and guests of the capital to feel like a celebrity. European service and a trendy interior are completely conducive to this. The impressive cost of rooms (about 10,000 rubles) also confirms the status of the hotel.

A large high-rise building with a lance on the roof is hard to miss. Guests can count on quality service, as well as a wide range of additional services. An indoor pool, gym and hot tub are at guests' disposal.

All rooms are in an interesting design, equipped with a TV, refrigerator, air conditioning and necessary furniture. A combined bathroom with toiletries is also present in all apartments.

Services and meals

Modern stylish and fashionable restaurant "Janus" offers dishes of several world cuisines. Traditional European and Russian snacks, as well as cocktails and other drinks can be enjoyed at the bar, which is located in the lounge area. Ordinary hotels near the Kazan station (Moscow) often do not offer such a number of additional services to their guests.

A large number of reviews from guests suggests that the hotel is popular. For some guests the inconvenience was the rule by which it was necessary to present a card for which the reservation was paid.

Guests are happy with their breakfast and service in their reviews. Comfortable mattresses and adequate layout. The room has everything you need to stay at the hotel. On the ground floor there is a wonderful relaxation area.

Travel Tips

The hotel at the Kazan station in Moscow is the best place to stay overnight by location. It is convenient to get from here not only to anywhere in the capital, but also to the country. A wide transport hub makes this place the most popular for overnight tourists.

Hotels near the Kazan station (Moscow) differ not only in the interior and level of comfort, but also in the price category. Many tourists believe that the cheaper the room, the worse the conditions in it, and vice versa. Unfortunately or fortunately, this is not always the case. Often for a moderate fee you can find a room in a hostel better than in a famous hotel.

Tourists should pay attention to the opening year of the hostel or hotel, and at the time of its last reconstruction. It will be true to assume that in old hotels both furniture and plumbing have worn out during their existence. Therefore, it is best to give preference to newly discovered places.

It is important to understand that in hostels you can meet a large crowd of people of different nationalities. Often they live there for a long time. Therefore, it is worth preparing yourself in advance for such a neighborhood.

Be sure to check which establishments are next to the hotel. Sounds from a club or bar can disrupt your stay.
