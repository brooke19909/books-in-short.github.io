Club Meeting Hotel 4 * (Rimini): reviews and photos of tourists from Russia

Club Meeting Hotel 4 in Rimini is located on the Adriatic coast, just a hundred meters from the picturesque promenade of the city. By the way, Rimini is considered the center of the Roman Riviera. This is the largest Italian resort town, stretching between the Marecchia and Ausa rivers. The ancient city, the birthplace of the famous F. Fellini, will delight tourists with wonderful views, beautiful cathedrals and bridges, ancient monuments and wonderful nature.





When you stay at Club Meeting Hotel 4 in Rimini, you will not only be close to the magnificent beaches of golden sand and turquoise sea water, but you will also plunge into the history of the ancient Roman state. After all, every urban stone can tell the history of Italy from the period of the Roman-Byzantine Empire.

Getting to Club Meeting Hotel 4 in Rimini is easy. From the train station along the promenade runs bus number 11. The stops have no name, but are located under the numbers. The closest stop to this hotel is No. 20. Tourists are advised to buy food in the Conad supermarket, at a stop at number 14. From the railway station, vacationers can optionally make an unforgettable trip to the pearl of all Italy - Venice.

Rimini Beauty

In such an ancient city there is always something to see. Not far from Club Meeting Hotel 4 is the famous Arch of Augustus, about the same distance from the hotel is the beautiful old temple of Tempio Malatestiano. On all postcards of Rimini you will definitely find the stone bridge of Tiberius, which is only 2.9 km from the hotel.



Walking along the old narrow streets of the city, you can go to the Federico Fellini Museum. Families with children will be interested in having fun in the water park "Aquafan" or go to the amusement park "Oltremare", or in another - "Fiabilandia." Sports enthusiasts will also find something to do in Rimini. Indiana Mini Golf Course is just 9 km away. And for lovers of high-speed cars and sharp turns, it is proposed to go to the Misano International Circuit named after Marco Simoncelli. On the Adriatic coast there are many water attractions and entertainments for people of any age, so you will be guaranteed an unforgettable experience.

Hotel Description

You can find the Club Meeting Hotel 4 in Rimini at Viale Regina Elena 171, Rimini City Center, 47900, Italy. This is a small four-story hotel with only 47 rooms. The hotel was built relatively recently, in 2003, the major repairs were last carried out in 2014, all the furniture here is modern, the plumbing is fresh and beautiful.





The hotel is very comfortable for tourists and sightseeing groups, business trips. The location is very good, easy to get, the main attractions and the beach are within walking distance. Around everything is equipped for the convenience of vacationers. The area is replete with restaurants, shops, small cafes.

Nearby is the huge stadium Stadium 105, where you can go to a performance in the evening or listen to a concert program. Yes, and the train station is nearby, so you can travel around Italy to nearby Florence, Venice or its small counterpart, Chioggia. Boat trips on a boat or yacht will leave a vivid impression. Hotel Interior

The interior of the Club Meeting Hotel 4 (Rimini), according to reviews and photos of tourists, is very creative and modern. At the entrance to the hotel you get as if at an exhibition of contemporary art. Huge brown leather sofas occupy almost the entire hall. On each small table or bookcase there is a figurine, a vase or some other decorative element.





But this was not the end of the designers' imagination. The red heart-shaped armchair is a bright spot in the dark interior. On the walls hang panels of mirrors of various shapes. Above the sofas in hanging glass round bowls, like an aquarium, green climbing plants grow. Sitting in the lobby you can get aesthetic pleasure from viewing all these creations of the masters of our time. But all this beauty is located only on the ground floor, in the lobby of the hotel. On the other floors simplicity and purity of white color reigns. However, in the rooms you can also find interesting objects, such as a coffee table in the form of a white drop dripping from glass. Come and get acquainted with the creative ideas of Italian designers.

Additional hotel services

Tourists at the hotel are offered a number of services, both paid and free. On the ground floor of the building there are free left-luggage offices. Other services are paid: shoe shine and laundry, dry cleaning and fax facilities. You can make a photocopy of documents directly at the hotel.
