Holiday Village "Birch" (Uvildy, Chelyabinsk region) - photos and reviews

In order to escape from the bustle of the city, relax with body and soul, gain strength, it is not necessary to go to foreign resorts. You can arrange a wonderful vacation for the whole family or have a great time with friends without leaving your homeland. On the territory of Russia there are wonderful recreation centers located in picturesque places. Here nature will fill you with energy, and modern living conditions will allow you to spend time in comfort. One of such places is the recreation center "Birch" (Uvildy). Reviews about this place vacationers leave mostly positive. Holidays in such a picturesque corner will appeal to both adults and children.



Where is the recreation center "Birch" (Uvildy)? How to get there?

The recreation center is located near Lake Uvildy (on the south-west coast) in the Chelyabinsk region. If you go from Chelyabinsk, the base will be on the 10th kilometer of the Kuznetsk-Karabash highway. On the route from Ekaterinburg - on the 12th km of the Kyshtym – Karabash highway there is a turn towards the city of Kuznetsk. The recreation center "Birch", Uvildy (photos are available in the article), covers an area of ​​2.5 hectares, the length of the coastline exceeds 200 m.

Lake Uvildy

This body of water is the largest fresh, low-mineralized lake in the Chelyabinsk region. Its area is 68.1 square meters. km The lake is located at the foot of the Ural Mountains. Most of its coastline is covered with pine and mixed forests. The water in the lake is crystal clear and transparent, with a blue tint, for which the reservoir was called the Blue Pearl of the Urals.

  • The maximum width of the lake is 9 km, length - 14 km.

  • The average depth of the reservoir is 14 m, the largest - 35 m.

  • The transparency of the water is 8.2 m.

  • The bottom is rocky, sandy.

The lake is rich in fish and vegetation. It also has several islands: small and large, wooded and rocky. Starving is the largest island, its diameter is about one kilometer.



Services

The recreation center "Birch" (Uvildy) has a developed infrastructure. Vacationers are offered a cafe-dining room with a banquet room for 150 people and reasonable prices, as well as a shop where you can buy everything you need, a bar on the outdoor terrace, sports grounds with exercise machines, a billiard room, baths with relaxation areas and a summer pool (two Finnish and one Russian), dance floor, barbecue awnings, playgrounds for sports (badminton, volleyball, football), rental. There is also a children's room and a playground, which, according to many, is very convenient - the children are passionate, parents are resting.

The recreation center "Birch" (Lake Uvildy) in the evenings will delight its guests with live music, open-air discos. In addition, there is the opportunity to celebrate anniversaries, weddings, children's parties, graduation parties, corporate events.



Fishermen will be satisfied

All conditions for fishing enthusiasts are created: storage of outboard motors, rowing and motor boats. A variety of fish species are found in the lake, including of trophy size. There is a good bite all year round, and there are specially equipped places for cooking fish soup. In a word, a pleasant rest is provided to you, and numerous reviews of true anglers are proof of this.

Sand beach

The recreation center "Birch" (Uvildy) has one of the most comfortable and large beaches of the lake. Vacationers can rent catamarans and boats, snorkeling and fishing equipment. From June to September, a water park with a wide variety of water attractions operates on the territory of the base: such as: water slides, water volleyball and polo, slippery football, springboards, trampolines, scooters, water balls, swings, zobras, boat bumpers, jet skis, aquasiper etc. For children there is an inflatable town, a trampoline, mini-boats, a pool with a slide.

Accommodation for vacationers

Guests are accommodated in comfortable two- and three-story dormitories, in rooms with all amenities. Each of them is equipped with a TV, fridge, wardrobe, etc. Two, three, four people can live in one room. A large company of 12 people can be accommodated in comfortable cottages with two or four bedrooms and a living room.

The recreation center "Birch" (Uvildy) also offers its guests apartments (superior rooms).

VIP clients and newlyweds can reserve VIP suites for themselves.

Four summer summer detached houses, equipped with a canopy for cooking barbecue and a refrigerator, are suitable for an economy class vacation.
