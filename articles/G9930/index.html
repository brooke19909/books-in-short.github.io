Hotel Blue Marine Resort Spa 5 * (Crete, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Blue Marine Resort & Spa is a place where everyone would be happy to spend their holidays. In all respects, this is a great option. The hotel is presentable, with a decent level of service, all-inclusive service, which, moreover, often provides excellent discounts. Well, you should pay a little more attention to this complex.





Location

Blue Marine Resort & Spa is located in the resort village of Amoudara. It is located 60 kilometers from Heraklion. And, accordingly, the airport is at the same distance. In about five minutes you can drive to a place called Agios Nikolaos. It is the capital of Nome Lasithi and a city very popular among tourists.

It should be noted that from Heraklion airport to the village of Amoudara there are buses No. 1 and No. 6. So you can save money and not take a taxi.

There are no attractions nearby - you need to go to everyone. The nearest interesting place is the port of Agios Nikolaos, the archaeological museum and the church of Panagia Kera (about 3.5 kilometers). Ho, but the sea is directly close. It is literally within reach, which pleases all tourists without exception.

Service

Blue Marine Resort & Spa, like any other good hotel, provides its guests with everything that is necessary for their comfortable stay on vacation. Naturally, there is free Wi-Fi and private car parking, dry cleaning with laundry, luggage room for guests, currency exchange and car rental.

There are two meeting rooms, a business center, an office with fax and photocopy services, as well as 2 conference rooms (with a capacity of 75 and 120 people).

Also on the territory there are several stores, among which one is jewelry. Blue Marine Resort & Spa has apartments of various categories, but it is important that there are even rooms for people with disabilities. Everything in them, including the bath and shower, is adapted to their specific features.

It is important to note that guests receive a shuttle service from / to the airport and it costs 90 euros (round trip). The staff speaks French, Greek, German and English, and, although this is not mentioned in the descriptions, there are employees who speak Russian. Bo anyway, as people who have been here say.





Leisure

Blue Marine Resort & Spa is a 5-star complex, so you can not only relax comfortably, but also have fun. There are two outdoor pools and one indoor, an excellent sports hall with modern fitness equipment, as well as a football field and volleyball court. But this is not the whole list. Here you can also engage in water sports and diving, play water polo, billiards and table tennis, and try archery. And here, as you can understand from the name of the complex, there is a SPA center. It is called Eyphoria.

There, guests can sign up for any massage that they like according to the description, take time for hydrotherapy, enjoy a jacuzzi, Turkish bath and sauna, go for a peeling, manicure, pedicure and cosmetic facial treatments that use mud. However, guests can check out the full list of services provided at the SPA upon arrival.

By the way, if guests arrived with children, then they may not worry that their children will be bored. For the smallest, there is a mini-club where professional educators look after them.





restaurants and bars

The Blue Marine Resort & Spa Hotel has several excellent facilities where guests can quench their thirst and hunger.

The main restaurant is called Blue Aegean. It offers pool views. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served there. A place called Olive Tree Restaurant is worth a visit for dinner. Amazing Mediterranean dishes are prepared there. And the Panorama restaurant, which also works only in the evening, delights guests with Italian delights.

There are two bars in the complex. One is called Pelagos, and the second is D and D Disco.

Guests who have already been to this hotel are happy to share their impressions of the restaurants. They say that the food here is excellent - there is a choice, and it is a solid one. I want to try everything at once, because many even overeat while being here. Even breakfasts are varied - in addition to hot and familiar eggs in several variations, they also serve fresh croissants, Greek yoghurts, vegetables with fruits, and oatmeal. And dinners are a separate issue. There is everything - starting jn seafood and ending with a milk pig. And the alcohol here is good, not diluted, drinks such as wine and local beer are especially good.





2-bed apartments

The Blue Marine Resort and Spa 5 * hotel has 202 rooms in its fund, each of which belongs to a certain category. Their area is 30 square meters. m. Inside, in a bright and stylishly decorated room, there are two single beds, which easily move into one large one.

There is access to a private furnished balcony with sea views. Or a view of the pool - it depends on which option the guest chooses. There is also a plasma TV with a DVD player and satellite channels, good air conditioning with the ability to connect heating, as well as a safe and refrigerator. There is also a plumbing unit - with a toilet, shower, bathtub and hairdryer. By the way, guests can use a service such as a wake-up call instead of an alarm clock.

The standard cost of a week's rest in such apartments is about 80 tons. for two people. Given that the hotel serves an all-inclusive system, this is a fairly modest price. In addition, there are discounts, as mentioned earlier. If, for example, you take advantage of early booking, you can save about 30%. And still here it is cheap to have a rest in the winter.

More spacious options

Blue Marine Resort & Spa (Crete) also has the so-called family apartments. They are designed for two adults and their children (under the age of 12). Rooms are 45 square meters. m. Inside there are three single beds and a sofa. The apartments are conveniently divided into two sleeping areas with a special partition. In addition to all the previously listed amenities, there is also an electric kettle with tea and coffee making facilities.

The standard price, without discounts, is slightly less than 100 tons. for 7 days.

There are also rooms for three adults. Their area is 30 square meters. m. In principle, these are the same 2-bed apartments, which were described earlier, only with more beds. Inside are three single beds. Although there is another option. It has a large 2 double bed and one standard.

The cost of a weekly stay in these rooms is 105 tons. By the way, even without discounts, this is a pretty good price for an all-inclusive hotel. Indeed, a week for each person will go to 35 tons.

Helpful information

At this hotel, check-in starts at 14:00 and check-out is until noon. But, if it so happened that the guests arrived earlier, they can be issued immediately - if their number will be free and prepared.

The hotel can stay with children of any age. For babies who are not even two years old, they will provide a free cradle. And for older children (but not older than 12) an extra bed will be installed in the apartment. However, all this is available upon request. You should write about your wishes in the reservation request, and then wait for confirmation from the administration.

By the way, not only a child, but also an older guest can come with guests in addition. However, for it will need to pay 30% of the cost per person for accommodation.
