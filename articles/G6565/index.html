When is it better to relax with a child in Egyptian resorts?

Egypt is very hot in summer. The sun burns mercilessly almost all year round. And this means that you can not show your children the most interesting of the sights located in the open. But in the autumn period the heat decreases a little, but the sea still remains warm and gentle. Autumn in Egypt, rainfall is a rare, even exceptional event. And this means that your vacation will not be spoiled by bad weather. Doctors do not recommend traveling with children to Africa, even North, in the summer. A trip to Egypt with a baby is also convenient in the spring. The weather is not hot, but the air feels fresh. In addition, during this period there are few people here, which means that the prices for tours are lower. Moreover, Hurghada and Sharm el-Sheikh are more suitable for recreation.
