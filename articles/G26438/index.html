North India: location, climate, best places to relax

India is the only country in the world in which there is diversity in culinary, geography, and culture. Here, travelers will be able to see the mountain peaks covered with snow, and slowly flowing rivers, and picturesque beaches.

Country features

South and North India are attractive destinations for many travelers. Since the country is rich in climatic features, before you go to India, you need to make a choice: enjoy a few warm days in the tropics or enjoy the snowy peaks of the mountains.

Manali and Shimla

North India is suitable for lovers of relaxation with cold and snow. It is here that the most beautiful places of those that can be found on our planet. What unique species does North India have? It is in this part of the country that there is Mount Jakhu, Rakhla Falls, Rohtang Pass, the Jung River, as well as many other picturesque places that you should definitely see.

Manali is famous for sporting events in skiing, as well as in mountaineering.

Auli

What else does North India offer tourists? A trip for ski lovers in Auli is the perfect solution. It is this place that skiers from different parts of the globe prefer to improve their skills.

In your free time, you can enjoy the amazing scenery of the Himalayas, get acquainted with the largest artificial lake.

Udaipur

What else can North India please? The most visited place in the world is Udaipur (Rajasthan). He impresses the traveler with the magnificence of his palaces, temples, and the number of old buildings. All buildings preserved in this region are the cultural and historical heritage of mankind.

Northern India is a place where tourists strive to get in order to see the Monsoon Palace, Lake Palace with their own eyes. This is only a small part of the places that you need to visit, if there are no restrictions on the timing of travel. The capital of Jaipur - Mount Abu is a famous attraction, occupying a leading position in the number of unique monuments and structures.

Goa

What places is India rightfully proud of? North Goa (Candolim) - a favorite place for fans of beach holidays. The country has a fairly long coastline, so you can choose a place to relax for every taste.

This is what attracts tourists to India. North Goa (Morjim, Karangut) is chosen by supporters of a luxurious holiday.

The best discos, hotels, beaches, seafood - all this is located in Goa. Among the beaches, which are always crowded, we mention Calangute, Colva, Anjuna.

Goa is historically and administratively divided into northern and southern zones, and the Zuari River flows as a natural boundary between them. Such a division also affects the specifics of tourism infrastructure, recreation, and makes the distinctions in terms of atmosphere and energy visible. It is difficult to understand in which part of Goa it is better to relax, everyone chooses the part that seems more convenient to him.

For example, North Goa is preferred by fans of outdoor activities, parties, discos. This is not the destination of tourists who dream of meditation alone with nature.

Local beaches are constantly filled with youth, noisy and fun. In the northern part of Goa there are no ultramodern hotels and elite tourist services.

The cost of trips to the northern part is quite acceptable for the middle-income strata of the population.

Conclusion

For those tourists who first went to India, experienced travelers are advised to go to North Goa. This will provide an opportunity to get acquainted with the peculiarities of life of the inhabitants of the country, to appreciate the spirit of true freedom from civilization, to get real pleasure from ordinary human joys. It should be noted that for Russian travelers, the most favorable season for the first acquaintance with India is winter.

When there is a snowstorm and severe frosts in Russia, the Goa season is the velvet season. The time of the monsoon ends, and in most of the country dry and sunny weather sets in. Given that the territory of India is quite large, in the regions there may be some differences in climate, but the average temperature is +30 degrees Celsius.

Each region of India has its own "highlight", so it is difficult to give advice on choosing a particular place to stay. Amazingly beautiful beaches, friendly locals, affordable prices are factors that attract tourists from all over the world to India.
