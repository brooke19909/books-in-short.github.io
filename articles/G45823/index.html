Journey to the ends of the world


Another strong magnet for travelers is the so-called "end of the world", which is located on the Atlantic coast. Cape of Doom (it is also called the Cape of Fate) - the westernmost border of the land not only of Portugal itself, but of the whole European continent. Standing on a cliff blown by the winds and looking into the distance at the endless ocean, you can truly believe that this is where the land ends - tourists find this place so impressive. The height of the cliff of Cabo da Roca (this is the name of the place in Portuguese) is one hundred and forty meters. On the top of this cape in 1772 a lighthouse was built.
