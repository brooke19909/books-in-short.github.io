What is interesting about abandoned villages?

If you carefully look at the map, you can see many abolished villages and cities. Why is now no one inhabits them? There are a number of reasons for this. Among them are the horrors of revolution, civil war and the ensuing red terror, which

claimed many millions of lives, and, in particular, led to a sharp reduction in the number of men. As a result, the surviving inhabitants left their deserted villages. Part of the settlements in the Civil War were simply burned. In addition, during the famine of the 1930s, which affected some regions of Russia, some villages became extinct. Part of the settlements was destroyed during the Great Patriotic War. In the years 60-70, the process of enlargement of settlements began. Residents moved to larger villages. Nowadays, villages continue to disappear. Their inhabitants are gradually moving to the city, especially young people.
