Hanoi Location

Before talking about Hanoi hotels, you need to find out where the city is located.

Hanoi is the capital of Vietnam and the second largest city in the country. In addition, it is the main political, cultural, economic center. If you literally translate the name, you get a "city between rivers" or "a place surrounded by a river." This settlement was built in 1010 on the banks of the Hongha River. Since then, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city has increased significantly, because some provinces were added to it. It is worth noting that in 2006, Hanoi was included in the list of ten most attractive cities for Asian tourists for foreign tourists.

The climate in the city is subequatorial, monsoon. Winds have a big impact. This type of climate is characterized by a hot rainy period from April to November and a cool dry season from December to March. It is worth noting that the weather here changes much more sharply than in other cities at the same latitude. In the rainy season, it is incredibly hot and stuffy, it almost always rains heavily. The average temperature in the summer ranges from +33 to +35 degrees. In winter, the thermometer columns drop to +16 ... + 17 degrees.
