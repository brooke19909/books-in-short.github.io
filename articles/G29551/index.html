Beautiful bouquets from Raffaello. With our own hands we create unique gifts

An unusually beautiful bouquet of sweets will be a wonderful gift for any holiday. Such a bright, original and memorable present will make the celebration unforgettable and will surely surprise the person presented.

If you want to learn how to make sweet floral arrangements, this article is for you. In it we will share two interesting master classes on creating beautiful bouquets of sweets. Make it possible for anyone who does not even have any knowledge of floristry. In addition, much less money will be spent on the self-production of original presents than on the purchase of similar ones in the store.

Bouquets from Raffaello. With our own hands we create an amazingly beautiful and stylish composition

In order to make a sweet gift, you will need to prepare some materials and tools. First, you need to purchase Raffaello candies in an amount of 25 pcs. It is convenient to work with these round-shaped sweets, in addition, they are very tasty. Secondly, you will need corrugated paper in several shades. We will use gold (metallized) to decorate the buds, and lilac - to decorate the bouquet. Thirdly, floristic wire and double-sided tape are required . To decorate the composition, you can use floral ribbons, lilac organza, beads and any other decorative elements.



The technology of manufacturing a sweet composition

How to make bouquets from Raffaello with your own hands, we will tell further. To start, let's create flowers. We take metallized corrugated paper and cut out 25 small rectangles. Now, gently wrap the sweets in these blanks, forming a tight bud and leaving the bottom where the sepals should be, the tips. Now we prepare the floristic wire - we form hooks from one end of each segment. This is necessary so that the stem of the flower does not pierce the candy. Next, we wrap the wires with double-sided tape. We attach candy buds to the stems. And then we decorate the blanks with golden corrugated paper. Now we take the lilac organza and cut out from it squares 20 * 20 cm. They will need 25 pcs. Fold in half. We wrap each bud in organza, forming beautiful airy petals.

We form a bouquet and decorate the craft

After all the flowers are done, proceed to the assembly of the bouquet. For strength, we fix the stems with adhesive tape. Wrap flowers with lilac corrugated paper. Now we take a large section of organza (about 2 m), bend it by a third and decorate the bouquet. We fix the fabric well. That's all, the bouquet from Raffaello is almost ready. It remains only to decorate it with ribbons, beads or any other decorative details to your taste. Now you know that it is very easy to make bouquets from Raffaello with your own hands! We hope you succeed. Good luck

We bring to your attention another way of making a charming bouquet of sweets. To create it, we need the following materials and tools:

  • wicker basket for flowers;
  • mounting foam;
  • Raffaello sweets - 50 pcs.;
  • floristic crepe paper of yellow, orange and pink colors;
  • wooden skewers;
  • wire;
  • green wrapping paper (50 * 50, 2 pcs.);
  • color foil (red and gold);
  • floral grid (30 * 40, 1 pc.);
  • yellow and green ribbons;
  • braid.

You will also need to prepare tape, knife and film. At will, decorative elements such as flowers, twigs, etc., can be used to decorate the bouquet.



Getting started with the idea: chic candy roses

How to make a bouquet of Raffaello? First, prepare the basket. We will cover any flat surface with a film, squeeze a little polyurethane foam onto it. After drying, cut the workpiece of the desired shape. Then we fix a piece of polyurethane foam in the basket using double-sided tape. Take the green wrapping paper and decorate the blank. Decorate the crafts on top with a floral grid. Tip: for these works it is convenient to use hot glue or adhesive tape. We handle the basket handle with braid or tape, fixing the ends with glue. How to make bouquets from Raffaello with your own hands, we will consider further.

Create gorgeous roses

To make flowers, we take sweets, wooden skewers and floral wire. About 40 pcs We fix "Raffaello" on sticks, and the rest on wire. Next, cut out of red, gold foil and floral crepe paper oval, slightly elongated petals (4 paired for each rose). We give them a realistic shape. Next, we make out all the available buds with petals.

As a result, you should get 10 red roses and 10 gold from foil, and crepe paper - the same number of orange, pink and yellow flowers. If desired, you can make several unopened buds. Cut sepals from green paper. We attach them to inflorescences.

Stems of roses are decorated with green paper or ribbon. When all the flowers are ready, proceed to the assembly of the bouquet. We insert the stems of the flowers into the workpiece of polyurethane foam. Tip: try to place roses in the basket evenly so that they do not get large gaps between them.

If desired, a bottle of sparkling wine can be placed in the center of the composition . Remember to attach it to the handle of the basket by the neck. We decorate the bouquet with twigs, flowers and any other interesting details at our discretion. Everything, a beautiful bouquet of "Raffaello" is ready! You see, making such an original and practical gift is not difficult. Creative success!
