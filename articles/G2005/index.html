Gas or electricity: which is cheaper, the better it is heated, the pros and cons

Residents of apartments are deprived of a choice, and as a rule, they do not have a question of how cheaper it is to heat a house: gas or electricity. However, such a dilemma often occupies the minds of owners of private buildings. Indeed, not only the convenience of operating the heating system, but also the amount of monthly cash costs depends on the choice of one of the options. If you choose at random, without considering the advantages, disadvantages and cost of both heating options, it is likely to make a mistake, the elimination of which will bring great costs in the future.

Electric heating

This method involves the use of boilers, which are divided into two types, namely floor and wall.

The principle of operation is quite simple. In the steel tank are tubular electric heaters that convert electricity to heat. These elements heat the coolant, and then, thanks to the circulation of water, the building is heated.

The advantages of electric heating

Any method of heating a room carries certain advantages:

  • Availability and ease of installation. These are perhaps the most important benefits. In addition, it is not required to coordinate the project, which further reduces the financial costs of implementing the idea.
  • Ease of maintenance. You must admit that during operation it becomes important not only what is cheaper: gas or electricity. Equally important is the time savings associated with the fact that this type of boiler is easy to maintain. In particular, it is not required to carry out preventive maintenance, as well as periodic cleaning of equipment items.
  • Environmental friendliness. Electricity is an environmentally friendly fuel that allows you not to worry about the environment. However, it is worth noting that not all homeowners give sufficient attention to this issue.

Cons of heating with electricity

Do not ignore the shortcomings of this type of heating in search of an answer to the question of which is cheaper: gas or electricity. Let's list them briefly.

  • First of all, the high cost. In Russia, electricity is considered the most expensive form of energy.
  • It is necessary to maintain constant power to ensure the operation of the boiler.
  • Power outages, which entail the inability to heat the living room.
  • Inconvenience in operation. Experts say that the use of electric heating is an irrational idea for home owners whose area exceeds one hundred square meters.

Gas heating

Gas is considered energy-intensive fuel, during the combustion of which a considerable amount of heat is released. Modern heating boilers are characterized by high efficiency, compact size, the presence of battery life and ease of maintenance.

However, you need to understand that the boiler itself will not be able to cope with the function of heating the room. This will require the creation of a heating system that, in addition to the boiler, will include aluminum or cast iron batteries connected by pipes through which water circulates.

The advantages of gas heating

This method of heating a room certainly brings with it certain advantages. Namely:

  • Cheapness. The use of this type of fuel is economically advantageous. Gas heating is cheaper than electricity. That is why the initial cost pays off as the equipment is operated.
  • Environmental friendliness. Experts assure that the combustion of gas does not produce harmful substances that can pollute the environment.

Cons of gas heating

Despite the advantages, gas heating has some disadvantages. It:

  • High cost. Moreover, this does not concern monthly utilities, but initial costs. To install a gas boiler, you must first conduct gas. It is worth noting that this is not cheap.
  • The difficulty of installation. Gas equipment is more difficult to mount than electric equipment. For this reason, most likely, you will have to contact specialists.

Which is cheaper: gas or electricity?

The answer to this question is obvious. Many people know that gas heating is cheaper. This justifies the high popularity of this type of fuel in Russia.

If we make a comparison, it turns out that electricity costs about two times more expensive than the energy that can be obtained by burning liquefied gas.

Now you know how to heat cheaper: gas or electricity.

If your choice is the second option, be prepared for the fact that the amount in the receipt for utilities will be impressive. Especially in the cold periods of the year when you have to heat the room to a more or less comfortable temperature.

With the answer to the question of which is cheaper: gas or electricity, everything is relatively simple. However, no one can say for sure what is better to heat.
