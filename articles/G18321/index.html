Hotel Grecian Fantasia 2 * (Greece, Rhodes, Faliraki): photos and reviews of tourists

One of the largest islands of the Dodecanese group is Rhodes. It occupies the southeastern part of Greece. This place is already surprising in its name, which from Greek translates as "island of roses." The collapse of the Aegean led to the formation of numerous islands, one of which is Rhodes. By its size, the island is in fourth place. But the biggest advantage of Rhodes is its unique climate.

general description

The island is washed by the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas. Its length is 77 km and its width is 37 km. The coastline stretches for 220 kilometers. 130 thousand inhabitants live on the island, half of which inhabit the island's capital of the same name. The city is located in the northern part of the island.

Myths and legends

The origin of Rhodes is unusual. Many legends originating from the ancient world, tell of the amazing events that led to the emergence of the island. One of them says that the ruler of the Universe Zeus defeated all enemies and conquered the Earth. He divided his possessions among other gods. Helios, for example, received a small island in the middle of the sea, on which he immediately shed its magic light. This place has turned into the most attractive corner of the Earth. When Helios saw his brainchild, he himself was in some shock from such magnificence. Then he gave the island the name Rhodes. Helios's beloved wife was called Rod. Legend has it that it was in her honor that this paradise was named.

A bit of history

Most of the island is covered with mountain slopes. On small plains incredibly fertile land. A variety of medicinal herbs, conifers, majestic oaks and plane trees grow here. Man began to explore Rhodes in the deep past. Since that time, stormy events unfolded on the island. Numerous empires sought to dominate such a rich land. Already in 1400 BC e. Achaeans lived on the island. Later, the cities of Ialysos, Lindos and Kamiros formed on this territory.

Features of rest

Despite the fact that today it is widespread to go on holiday abroad, having applied to various travel companies that offer ready-made tours, it will still be wiser to decide before which trip to which hotel to stay. To begin with, Rhodes is washed by two seas: the Mediterranean and the Aegean. The east of the island can be compared with Turkish resorts. Sand on the beaches, almost no vegetation, the sea is warm and calm. For those who prefer a comfortable beach holiday, this is an ideal option. The part of the island washed by the Aegean waters is a mountainous area. Almost always, winds blow here, which is why the sea often storms. The southwestern part of Rhodes is great for windsurfers.

This article will focus on the hotel Grecian Fantasia Resort 2 *, located in one of the most popular resorts - Faliraki.

Holidays in Faliraki





Not so long ago, fishermen lived here peacefully and calmly, but today this place is a real city of fun and entertainment. Tourists here will find a lot of activities to their taste. Most travelers choose Faliraki for their holidays.

Tourist note

  1. Anyone who prefers a beach holiday, it is better to visit Faliraki from May to September. This period is ideal for swimming and water activities. Those who are interested in sightseeing can come to the resort in winter. At this time, the sun is less active.
  2. You can and should bargain on Faliraki, but only in small souvenir shops and in private shops. When making purchases, it is imperative to have small bills with you.
  3. Valuable items on the beach is better not to take. Bags, towels, creams and other accessories should not be left unattended.
  4. Almost all shops and entertainment operate at the resort seven days a week. In summer, they close late at night, while in winter their working hours are reduced.
  5. A good solution for a large company that plans an active vacation is to rent a car. To do this, you must present a driver's license and passport. A security deposit is also required, the amount of which may vary from the cost of the car and the rental period.
  6. In your budget you need to include an item of expenses for tips. In large restaurants, their amount is 10% of the size of the check. In small establishments, you can leave a tip at your discretion.
  7. Taxis are inexpensive, but a double tariff is included at night.
  8. There are telephone booths on the streets. From here it is the cheapest way to call abroad.

Travelers with a small budget who decided to visit Faliraki should pay attention to the Grecian Fantasia 2 * hotel.

general description





The airport is 15 km from the complex. The capital of Rhodes is 12 km away. The hotel is located almost in the center of Faliraki. This is very convenient, as the most popular resort attractions are located nearby.

Hotel Grecian Fantasia 2 * has a total of 57 rooms. In 2010, the complex was restored.

The beach is located 10 minutes. walk.

Infrastructure





An outdoor freshwater pool is on site. Free use of sun loungers and parasols. There is also a pool for children.

Restaurant and bar are in the same room. There is also a children's corner with small chairs. A baby cot is available upon request. At the pool there is another bar, working on the "buffet".

The hotel has an internet cafe. Wi-Fi access is available for a fee.

You can also use the laundry, dry cleaning, play computer games, call a doctor if necessary.

There is a water entertainment point on the beach. But you also have to pay for it. But mini-football on the territory of the Grecian Fantasia 2 * hotel can be played for free. It is also possible to watch TV in the TV room. Parking is available.

Number of rooms





The Grecian Fantasia Resort 2 * hotel (Faliraki) has several types of apartments.

Standard room occupies one room. It is equipped with two single beds and one extra, designed for three people.

Superior rooms are on the top floor. It can accommodate two people. Single beds. Some rooms have balconies with beautiful sea views.

The family apartment consists of two bedrooms, a living room and a dining area. There are two beds and two sofas. Room can accommodate up to five people.

Room service





Apartments are cleaned daily except weekends. Each room has a TV with satellite channels, mini fridge, hairdryer, balcony. Bathroom and air conditioning, a safe and an electric kettle are only in the superior rooms. The rest has a shower. Other amenities are available at an additional cost. There is ceramic tile on the floor.

Towels and bed linen are changed twice a week.

The Grecian Fantasia 2 * hotel makes a rather good impression on tourists. Guest reviews are mostly positive.

Comments vacationers





Superior rooms deserve special attention. Guests argue that even in 4 * hotels it is not always possible to meet such comfort. The rooms are freshly renovated and have everything you need for a good rest and even more. Do not be upset that air conditioning is available for a fee. Here you can easily live without it. Sometimes it is stuffy in the rooms, but bearable.

Ground floor apartment for an amateur. Instead of a bath there is a shower and a small bathroom. If possible, it is better to choose rooms on the upper floors. In addition to the bathroom, they also have a beautiful view of the sea. The staff is very caring, although it does not speak Russian at all. Garbage is cleaned daily. Toiletries are not included in the room rate, so it is best to bring them with you. Soap can not be taken.

Dinner is more varied. Please note that all drinks at the hotel are paid. Fruits are not particularly spoiled. The entire period of rest can be fed only oranges.

Beach vacation





Almost no Rhodes hotel has its own beach. Grecian Fantasia 2 * (Faliraki) is no exception. The nearest public beach is 500 meters away. The area is equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. The cost of a deck chair is 5 euros, an umbrella is free. On the beach there are places shaded by the crown of trees. Therefore, you can save on beach accessories.

Those lucky enough to visit Faliraki claim that this is the perfect place for a beach holiday. You should not go here for shopping or for excursions. Although a couple of them can be ordered for a change. Rest here will be to the taste of young people. A few minutes walk from the hotel Grecian Fantasia Resort 2 * (Rhodes) is a street with many bars and nightclubs. Some even have free cocktails. Entrance to the bars is free. Some clubs charge an entrance fee (about 5 euros), but in return give a drink for free. In all establishments, they try to keep the client by any means.

Family people here should also like it. Faliraki is a quiet, calm area with clean beaches, warm sea, magnificent landscapes and hospitable hotels. You can safely go here with children. In almost all hotels, children's rest is provided, but under the supervision of parents.
