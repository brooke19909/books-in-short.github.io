Hotel Lotus Mui Ne Resort SPA 4 * (Vietnam): reviews and photos, description

Vietnam has recently become an increasingly popular tourist center - thousands of tourists come here every year, including our compatriots. Naturally, in the coastal zone there are many resorts that offer vacationers all the amenities. The hotel complex Lotus Mui Ne Resort SPA 4 *, located almost on the seashore itself, is considered quite good. So what are the conditions awaiting tourists at the hotel?

Location and brief infrastructure description





Lotus Muine Resort SPA is a hotel complex with a fairly large common area. It is located in the province of Binh Thuan, very close to the famous tourist town of Mui Ne. By the way, the city of Phan Thiet, no less popular among travelers, is located at a distance of 15 kilometers.

It is immediately worth noting that the hotel is designed for a quiet but comfortable stay. The large territory of the hotel is an example of landscape art - here you will find exactly trimmed lawns, walking paths, a small flowering garden, as well as bright flower beds, garden sculptures and fountains. The rooms are located in small one-story bungalows, so peace and quiet are guaranteed.

Rooms in the hotel Lotus Mui-Ne Resort SPA 4 *





The facility under consideration refers to medium-sized hotels - on its territory there are only 78 rooms of different capacities, which are located in small cozy bungalows and are "scattered" throughout the complex.

It is worth noting that the Lotus Muine Resort SPA hotel is famous for its spacious, bright rooms with large windows, balconies and terraces. All rooms are furnished with modern furniture - here you will find a large, comfortable bed, sofa, wardrobes, bedside tables and even a wooden desk. There is constant access to the Internet, which is very convenient.

As for household appliances, there is everything you need for a comfortable stay. There is a TV connected to the satellite dish. There is also a telephone, air conditioning with a control panel, a DVD player, a small safe with a combination lock, a radio. Some rooms have a small kitchen area with a dining table and a set of tableware.

The spacious bathroom is equipped with modern fixtures, you can take a bath or shower. Naturally, each guest is entitled to a set of towels, a bathrobe and slippers, as well as hygiene items. The rooms are cleaned every day, mainly in the mornings, so you are unlikely to complain about cleanliness.

Nutrition

When buying a ticket to the Lotus Muine Beach Resort SPA, guests are offered a choice of a meal plan - BB (breakfast), HB (breakfast and dinner) or all-inclusive. In the main restaurant, guests are served tables. Mostly tourists are offered local Vietnamese, as well as more familiar international dishes. The choice of food is quite large - there are also fresh fruits, vegetable and meat salads, as well as snacks, slices, desserts.





In addition, every day a small cozy cafe Marina Seafood Buffet Restaurant opens its doors, where guests are offered fish and seafood dishes. Tourists in their reviews note that this place is definitely worth a visit. The Moon Beach Bar & Restaurant is open almost round-the-clock, where you can order light snacks, delicious desserts, as well as drinks and exotic cocktails. And from the summer platform of the bar offers a breathtaking view of the coast.

Beach and water recreation





The hotel complex Lotus Muine Resort SPA 4 * is located 100 meters from the sea shore. Unfortunately, the hotel‚Äôs own beach is not suitable for swimming, as the bottom has not yet been cleared. Nevertheless, it is always pleasant to walk along the shore. In addition, just a few meters away is the beach of the neighboring hotel, where tourists can also sunbathe in the sun, bask in the shade of palm trees and swim. Naturally, there are all conditions for more active recreation, including boat riding, yachting, water skiing. By the way, the coast is very popular with fans of surfing, as the waves are high every year.

Entertainment and additional services for tourists

Of course, the territory of the hotel Lotus Mui-Ne Resort SPA 4 * has everything you need for a truly carefree vacation. In particular, guests are offered laundry, dry cleaning and parking services. There is also a rental point where you can rent a car, motorcycle or other type of transport. This service is very popular with guests, as it facilitates movement around the area and makes it possible to make a short trip.

Lotus Mui-Ne Resort SPA 4 * also has a modern business center with all necessary equipment, two meeting rooms and a large conference room, which, incidentally, is often used for banquets.





In the courtyard there is a large outdoor pool, where it is quite pleasant to spend time. Around there are a sufficient number of sun loungers and umbrellas, beach towels are provided free of charge. You should definitely visit the local SPA center, which offers a lot of wellness treatments, including massage and hydromassage. There is a sauna and a small fitness center. The hotel has a tour desk to help you organize trips to the most interesting places. By the way, there are several interesting sights nearby, including the Poshanu Cham tower, which is definitely worth a look.

And from time to time on the territory of the hotel arrange exciting performances and show programs.

Hotel complex Lotus Muine Resort SPA: reviews of tourists

Of course, travelers are primarily interested in the opinions of tourists who have already managed to use the services of this resort. It is worth noting that the reviews about the Lotus Muine Resort SPA 4 * hotel are for the most part positive. Customers first of all note spacious rooms and beautiful territory - it is really pleasant to spend time here. In addition, the distance from the city center and an excellent soundproofing system create good conditions for a quiet, measured rest.

According to the comments of former guests, the employees here are polite and do an excellent job. The city center can be easily reached by taxi or rented transport, which, you see, is good. Perhaps the only drawback of the hotel, judging by the reviews of tourists, is the lack of its own beach, but the administration is already working on a solution to this problem. Many vacationers were satisfied with their stay at the hotel and are planning to return there again in the future.
