Great photos. New York: hobbies as art

A person without any hobbies in our time is a boring and gray character. If you have a favorite hobby, then it becomes the best antidepressant and a means to unlock creativity. You can get involved, for example, collecting coins or interesting pictures.

How does photography become a hobby?

Some people skillfully combine business with pleasure - they travel and take pictures of their routes. Someone goes to distant Alaska or to the Mayan pyramids, and someone brings a lot of impressions and vivid photos from America. New York attracts many. Looking at the pictures of the metropolis, you are like returning to your beloved Statue of Liberty, the lights of Broadway and the powerful bronze bull from Wall Street.

Why do tourists take such photos? New York - what's interesting about it?





The thing is that the camera is one of the most important accessories of a modern person, and the Big Apple is one of the oldest cities in the world. Many consider it the unofficial capital of the United States, because it is here that all the important financial events take place, the brightest festivals and the best films are made.

Let's talk about where to take amazing photos. New York played a special role in the life of many. Briefly consider the history of its appearance.

How did the Apple come about?

The Dutch once bought the place of the American dream , with just a few coins and a bunch of shell beads. Now Broadway is considered the longest street, the criminal Brooklyn was remembered as the home district of many NBA players, and Theodore Dreiser became famous for Wall Street. Previously, in their place were the sites of the Indian tribes, and even today, there are spearheads, arrows, coins that seem to bring us back to the past, give impressions and photos. New York is appealing to many connoisseurs of historical and cultural heritage.

What do they like to photograph in New York?





Like every city on earth, New York has places loved by all photographers and tourists. The most important thing is the Brooklyn Bridge, whose length is 2 kilometers. It has long become a symbol of the city. Broadway is no less popular. Many photographers rightly consider it their bread. What are the premieres of musicals about which are broadcasting neon posters, Soho or the magnificent Cathedral of the Holy Trinity!

Well, and where, without reflecting the business life of the city in the photo! You will never know New York unless you find yourself on Wall Street. And how is it possible to miss the place where the toughie saved the world, Nicolas Cage looked for the treasures of the nations, and Leo DiCaprio and Michael Douglas made their fortunes ?!

Do not forget that the photos of New York at night that you bring from your trip will not only help you create your own gallery, but also turn into beautiful photo wallpapers that will transform the interior of your apartment.
