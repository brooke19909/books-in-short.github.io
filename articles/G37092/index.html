Manual settings

How to configure the camera on the phone? Of course, it all depends on individual needs. But most users recommend using manual settings.

Depending on the version of the operating system, certain shooting modes and options will be available to you. Among the modes you can find "Manual".

A line appears at the bottom of the screen where you can adjust a number of parameters. For example, here you can configure the ISO. This parameter is responsible for photosensitivity. He has several indicators, as well as an automatic mode of operation. The parameter is best adjusted in case of poor lighting, in another situation, its automatic adjustment is suitable.

You can immediately choose the shutter speed. This parameter delays the open iris for the specified time. The longer this takes place, the more light will fall on the matrix. It is necessary to monitor the overexposure. Excerpts are usually taken during night or evening shooting.

Display exposure with plus and minus icon. The parameter helps to adjust the brightness or darkness of the frame. White balance allows you to adjust the cool or warm tint of the picture. Here you can adjust the saturation, contrast of the picture.

Some people find it best to use HDR on how to set up the camera on the phone well. This function, on the one hand, can really make a beautiful shot, and on the other, it is not always applicable. HDR creates several frames with different exposures, and then combines everything into one photo, which is devoid of darkened or illuminated areas. The function is best suited for landscape photography.
