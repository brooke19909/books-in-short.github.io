DIY potato costume: materials and stages of work

Preschool children often participate in thematic morning performances. And then they have to try on different roles. For example, seasonal vegetables. Parents take care of the costumes. We will explain how to make a potato costume with your own hands. This will save you from searching stores.



Necessary materials

  • Lightweight fabric (cotton or satin is fine). After all, the child will spend about an hour in the outfit. Velor suits bought in a store look unusual, but in practical terms, their purchase is disadvantageous. The preferred color is brown, resembling a potato peel.
  • Scissors.
  • Felt.
  • Pins




Stages of working on a potato costume for a girl

Expanding a piece of prepared fabric, it is necessary to note the middle in it. Ten centimeters to the right and left should be measured from it. These values ​​are approximate, therefore it is more convenient to work with individual measurements of the child. After that, you can cut the neck for a suit of potatoes with scissors.

The next step is to prepare the parts for the application. They will become the “face” of the outfit. You can cut them out of felt (another suitable fabric will do). Finished parts need to be attached with pins at the place where they are planned to be sewn in the future. On the sewing machine, the appliqué is attached on the front side of the potato suit (photo below). Usually it turns out to be large in size and is located in the center of the outfit.



Master sleeves

The next part, the manufacture of which we will analyze, is the sleeves. As a matter of fact, they are not provided for in this model. Therefore, we conjure over a kind of sleeveless jacket. After preliminary fixation of the fabric by tailor pins, it is necessary to cut a pair of symmetrical armholes in the marked places. There is still quite a bit of work left, and the potato costume for the girl will be ready.

You can use another way: the side parts of the outfit are sewn together in previously doubled fabric. In this case, with the holes for the hands will have to do otherwise: they do not need to be sewn. This method will require overlocking the edges.

In the upper part of the back it will not hurt to make a small incision, then with the dressing of the outfit the child will have no difficulties. The main thing is to provide for fixing a Velcro, button or button (the option listed first, more practical). A do-it-yourself potato costume is almost ready.

Now it remains to complete the application. Its shape can be arbitrary. In this case, the details will differ depending on the gender of the child. Potato “girl” fit long eyelashes. Male vegetables can be supplemented with made mustache. Further decoration of the outfit depends on the master’s own imagination.

How to make a vegetable foam

Do not be surprised: you can make a potato suit with your own hands from this material. It should be thin or medium in thickness. The detail cut out of it should represent something between such figures as an oval and a rectangle. Make sure she gets rounded corners. There will be two such details.



If there is fabric (at least lining, any color), then a couple of details must also be cut from it. They should be more than foam rubber. Cloth foam is required to be sewn together at the shoulders and sides. If you want to give the material extra volume, then you can not do without a few tucks. Having quilted parts in several places, you can get an imitation of eyes. If the fabric cannot be found, then you can resort to coloring the foam in the selected shade. A photo of a potato suit is available in the article. So, if there is nothing left before the matinee, you should not be upset. After all, the process of making a potato costume with your own hands does not take much time.
