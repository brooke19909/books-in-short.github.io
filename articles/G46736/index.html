Comparison

I would like to start the comparison of Meizu and Xiaomi with a list of pluses, because in most cases they are the key factors that influence the acquisition of a particular device.





5 pluses of Meise

The first will be a review of the Meise. Although, probably, this can not be called a full review, but rather a listing of the main advantages of the company's smartphones.

Design and ergonomics

The design of the Meizu phones has always been one of the greatest strengths, even though the appearance of most devices resembles an iPhone. The build quality is excellent, the arrangement of all elements is thought out, so using Meizu devices is very convenient.





Sound

Since Meizu has been developing and manufacturing portable players for a long time, listening to your favorite music with headphones on the company's smartphones is much more pleasant than using Xiaomi. Of course, it is impossible to say that the sound is perfect, but it is clearly better than most Chinese counterparts.

Branded button

M-touch is a proprietary button that has been installed on all Meizu devices for many years. It acts as a back button, and when pressed, returns to the main screen. In addition, a fingerprint scanner is built into the button, which is much more convenient to use due to the location. On Xiaomi smartphones, the scanner is located on the back side, which is not very comfortable. Also, the big advantage of the m-touch button is the ability to set certain functions on it, for example, double-clicking is responsible for starting the camera.

Price

Well, the price is the final plus in the Meise review. 5 Pros of Xiaomi
