Novomikhailovsky, "Tornado" (recreation center): rest and reviews

There are unique places in Russia where you can fully spend your vacation. One of the popular and affordable resorts is the village of Novomikhailovsky. It is located near the picturesque Cape Beskrovnoy, in the place where the deep-water river Nechepsuho flows.

This is a fairly large resort of the Tuapse region with numerous sanatoriums, hotel complexes and developed infrastructure. The village is more focused on economical holidays. The main part of the boarding houses is created for family leisure with young children and diving enthusiasts. If you belong to this category of people, welcome to the Novomikhailovsky resort. "Tornado" - a modern recreation center - invites everyone to visit.





The hotel is located in a quiet area, close to entertainment venues and shopping centers. The hotel has a measured, laid-back, sightseeing and romantic weekend. With a similar name, the Tornado-Mizar (Novomikhailovsky) boarding house was opened, not far from the indicated hotel. Both hotels provide quality services, have pebble beaches and delight customers with first-class amenities.

Description and location





The Tornado complex is located right on the coast of the blue sea, in a quiet bay, which is surrounded by abundant vegetation. Convenient location makes the hotel even more popular and in demand among our compatriots with low incomes.

Tourists can always go to Tuapse and Big Sochi - the train station is 40 km away, and the Adler district airport is 35 km away. Within walking distance of the market square with a pharmacy and a mini market. The hotel is open from early June to late October. The area is surrounded by a fence and a small tropical garden. Its main advantage is the presence of a yacht club and a sailing school for children.

Tornado: services





In the open air there is a fairly large swimming pool filled with crystal clear fresh water. Near it, guests will be able to take sunbathing on cozy sun loungers for free, or just relax and have fun while sipping a delicious cocktail.

A wide selection of sports entertainment for active people. Indoors there is a tennis table, a billiard room and ping pong tables. It offers a range of diverse simulators located near the pool. All the necessary sports equipment is rented at a rental point. Excellent rest in the Novomikhailovsky "Tornado" for younger guests. A large play area with swings, slides and horizontal bars has been built for the children.

Coastline





A few steps from the buildings stretches the sea coast, strewn with small pebbles. The beach is owned by the hotel and is closed to strangers. The shore is regularly cleaned, therefore it is always clean and comfortable. The beach is convenient for children, as it has a gentle and safe descent into the water.

There is a small working bar, changing cabins, a shower, a toilet and a set of beach amenities (awnings, sunbeds). For extremals, a leisure center is open, which offers everyone who wants to get a shot of adrenaline from parachuting or diving deep into the sea with scuba gear.

For passive relaxation - water catamarans and boat trips on a boat. If you have arrived at the New Mikhailovsky Tornado, you can always rent a luxury yacht and go on a trip to the Black Sea. Near the beach there is a calm harbor for boats and boats - a yacht club.
