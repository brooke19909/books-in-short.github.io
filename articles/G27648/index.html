Compound of the village of Ust-Kasp - a trip to the taiga tale

If you want to go somewhere as a team, with friends or family, you can choose the village of Ust-Kasp. It may be good to live in the city, but the frantic noise of the metropolis is exhausting, but here you can see the Siberian taiga, so clean, so magnificent, admire the mountain river, breathe fresh air.

Infrastructure and accommodation

All guests can live in cozy houses, there are 3 of them, each house has stove heating. A Mongolian yurt is also offered for accommodation. The beds in the houses are double, consist of two tiers. There is an electric kettle, a TV and an electric stove.

The toilet is in the yard, water needs to be brought. There are dishes for cooking, but you need to bring food with you. Electricity is supplied around the clock. Cellular communication at a good level in Ust-Kasp. The recreation center accommodates approximately 26 people.

The first house can accommodate two adults and two children. In the second - a maximum of 12 people. In the third - 8.

Mongolian Yurt

This is a new service for guests. This is one of the ancient types of housing, the approximate time of appearance is the XII century BC. The yurt is made of dense natural felt.

In winter, the yurt is very warm, and in summer it will be cool. In winter, stove heating is turned on in the yurt.

Entertainment

In summer, you can play badminton, ride bicycles, play volleyball, soccer. Winter holidays in Ust-Kasp include skating, skiing, downhill, as well as horse-drawn sleigh rides.

There is a playground for children. There is a small souvenir shop where you can buy oil and balm from cedar or fir.

Very cheerful discos are usually held in the evening.

For lovers of extreme sports there is the possibility of rafting on mountain rivers.

An excursion is held for those who wish to the unique beauty of Shinda Falls.

A mountain bike is available for hire.

At any time of the year in good weather you can go boating.

Blooming Sally

Ivan tea is collected in the courtyard, on the basis of which various preparations are made, for example, from raspberries, mint, mountain ash and so on. Earlier, Ivan tea was considered very valuable. In common people, Ivan tea is called fireweed. He was often prescribed for ulcers, stomach diseases, headaches. It contains many vitamins, improves metabolism. If you wish, you can try an unusually aromatic and tasty drink in a tea yurt specially designated for this.

Bath in the tub

If someone remembers the tale of the humpbacked skate, then there was also a cauldron. A cedar font is installed on the courtyard. Everyone knows about the excellent properties of pine nuts. The Slavs knew that if dishes were made from cedar, then milk would stand there for a very long time and would not turn sour. If you make a closet, then there will never be a moth. And in order to soot a fish or cook a kebab of meat, husk from cones is perfect.

Cedar oil can be used externally. Cedar needles treat scurvy. Fir is very healing, essential oil is made from pine needles, and camphor, in turn, is made from it.

Now about the vat. It is installed in the open air, heated at a fire, the temperature is about 40 degrees. The volume of the tub is 1,500 liters. In the bathing water, a decoction of fir lapnik is added. Chan is made of cedar wood. Such bathing has a charity effect on the human body: the nervous system calms down, the work of many organs improves. After bathing, it is recommended to drink tea from herbs.

Cost and Services

The approximate cost of living is 1000 rubles. (for an adult). The price includes using the ice rink.

Separately, you need to pay for the barbecue, bath and cedar font.

Advantages

The places here are pretty beautiful. In Ust-Kasp, for example, Victor Astafyev, a famous writer, together with his friend, artist Vladimir Zelenov, chose a manor.

If you are a fan of outdoor activities, you can go hiking or rafting along the Kizir mountain river.

The water in it is bluish, with an emerald hue. This is especially evident from August to September. There are several settlements near the river. You can get there by car. If you go from the center of Krasnoyarsk, the distance will be approximately 330 km. You can also buy a train ticket and travel by rail.

Near the village and next to it are many cedars. If you go in the season itself, you can harvest a crop of cones.

Fans of fishing need to go to the first threshold, climb a little higher, grayling is found there.

So go to Ust-Kaspu for clean air, for the smells of the Siberian taiga, for a healthy bath in a tub, for a good mood and a charge of positive.
