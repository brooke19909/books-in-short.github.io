Stop Place - Moscow, Vnukovo, Hotel "Crew"

Near all Moscow airports you can find a place to relax between flights. Often near significant transport hubs there are several hotels in different price segments. Tourists should be careful when choosing a hotel near the airport. You can always choose a suitable place for an overnight stay, focusing on the capabilities of your wallet.

The hotel near the Vnukovo International Airport - the Hotel "Crew" - refers to budget accommodation. At the same time, she has all the qualities and services to remain in demand among tourists. The hotel, 1 km from Vnukovo Airport - Hotel "Crew" - is located in a green area at the symbolic address: 1st Reisovaya Street, 13. The location will delight nature lovers: within walking distance of the accommodation facility, they will find a lovely lake with gazebos and playgrounds for children .

And the hotel is only 23 km from the center of Moscow .

Rooms of the hotel "Crew"

The hotel has the status of a 2-star, ready to move around the clock. For guests traveling through Vnukovo Airport, the Crew Hotel offers accommodation in 107 rooms of various categories: budget single and double, as well as superior comfort. The interior is made in a classic style. All rooms are equipped in accordance with the requirements of a modern tourist. In addition to the standard set of furniture, guests will find:

  • Private bathroom or shower
  • TV
  • air conditioning;
  • telephone;
  • free wifi
  • essential toiletries.

Hotel "Crew" (Vnukovo, 1st Reisovaya street, 13): additional services

The hotel offers a regular shuttle service. The bus route "Vnukovo Airport - Hotel" Crew "runs on both sides with an interval of 45 minutes. The service is free for airlines, hotel guests can use the shuttle for 50 rubles.

Parking is available at no extra charge. Also for the convenience of guests in the hotel operates:

  • barbershop;
  • express laundry (24 hours);
  • mobile account recharge terminal;
  • currency exchange point;
  • ATM.

Ironing facilities can be used at any time. For the comfort of guests, round-the-clock access to hot and cold drinking water is organized (2 coolers are installed on each floor). Superior rooms are equipped with safes, there are also cells at the reception and a left-luggage office.

For the organization of leisure in the "Crew" operates:
