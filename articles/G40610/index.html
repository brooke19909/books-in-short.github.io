How to check the battery capacity of the phone? Testing and determining the actual battery capacity

All modern smartphones, unfortunately, are quickly discharged. The mobile device can be charged several times a day, since the battery capacity does not allow you to comfortably use the gadget for a long time. Because of this, many people doubt the real capacity of the battery. And now the most important question arises: "How to check the battery capacity of the phone?" When you first start the application, the Internet on your phone should be turned on. Why is this needed? The program will take all the necessary information about your smartphone from the Internet. She has her own online database, where she draws information about what current consumption is on your device.

Perhaps your device will not find the application in its database. This is happening with new mobile devices. You can find all the necessary information and add your smartphone to the database, for this you will need to fill in the main fields. Of course, you will need to turn on the Internet.

So, your device is present in the database of the selected utility, and there is an opportunity to get the necessary information about it. If you have met all the necessary criteria, then you can proceed to determine the battery capacity.

How to check the battery with a multimeter?

It is quite difficult to measure the battery capacity with a multimeter, it can even be said impossible. This conclusion follows from the fact that a fully charged battery must be connected to the multimeter. Next, with this device you will find out the consumption of the device (current). It is also necessary to detect the time during which the battery is completely discharged.

How to check the battery with a multimeter? After obtaining the necessary indicators, you can calculate the capacity according to the well-known formula: capacity = current * time.





How to measure the necessary indicators with a multimeter? Everything is a bit more complicated here. To obtain accurate readings, direct current is required. This is difficult to achieve, and it must be maintained until it is completely discharged, which is quite difficult. This can only be done in the laboratory.
