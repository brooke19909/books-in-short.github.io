Caesar Hotel. Side. Description

On the seashore in the village of Kumkey there is a comfortable Caesar (hotel). Side, a nearby city, promises its guests a lot of interesting things - world-famous historical sights and popular tourist places. Among them are the Apollo Temple, the ancient village of Selge, the canyon of Kerplulu, Aspendos, etc. The hotel has a small tropical garden where you can relax in the shade of sprawling trees or just take a walk.

Description

"Caesar" - a hotel (Side) - covers a fairly large territory of 45 000 sq. M. It was built in 1989, and in 2010 there was a complete restoration. The hotel complex consists of two buildings (5 and 6 floors) located across the road from each other. The five-star Cesars Resort is located on the first coastline, and the four-star Cesars Palms is on the second. The hotel is located in the town of Kumkey, just five kilometers from Side, 65 km from the airport and 13 km from a popular tourist destination - the village of Manavgat, famous for its picturesque waterfall.

Residence

Hotel Caesar (Side), the photo of which is presented in this article, has 687 apartments overlooking the garden or the coastline.

There are also separate, specially equipped rooms for disabled and non-smokers. All apartments are equipped with air conditioning, bathroom, hairdryer, telephone, safe, television, minibar, balcony. Three times a week, linen is changed, cleaning is carried out daily.
