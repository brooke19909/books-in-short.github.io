Mortgage credit lending

According to customer reviews, Alfa-Bank offers one of the most convenient lending programs secured by real estate in Russia today. The client has the opportunity to arrange a loan for the purchase of finished or under construction housing. The bank has the following mortgage programs:

  • Mortgages for finished housing at a minimum interest rate of 9.29 percent. The maximum loan amount is fifty million rubles. The down payment must be at least fifteen percent of the loan amount. Requirements for the borrower:

-citizenship of our or foreign state;

- at least twenty-one years of age on the day the loan was issued and not more than seventy years at the date of loan repayment;

-the period of work at the last place of work is at least four months, the total period of work is at least one year.

- Obligatory insurance is the loss of an object, the loss of property and life rights, the working ability of a mortgage borrower.

  • Mortgage on housing under construction. The conditions are similar to the first program. The reviews of Alfa-Bank confirm that the rate of 9.29 percent is valid for the bank's clients participating in the salary project, taking into account insurance of all the risks listed above.
  • A loan secured by housing at a minimum interest rate of 13.29%. The maximum loan amount is six hundred thousand rubles. The loan term is not more than thirty years. The ratio of loan amount to property value is up to sixty percent for targeted and up to fifty percent for loans for consumer purposes.

Special conditions for mortgage lending:

  • The base rate is 9.79%. The rate of 9.29% is valid only for salary clients, provided that all risks are taken into account in the insurance contract.
  • For clients not participating in salary projects, the rate is reduced by 0.2 percent provided that they enter the transaction no later than thirty days from the day the bank makes the decision to issue a loan.
  • If you refuse insurance of any risk, the rate increases by two percent.
  • Refinancing a loan for a residential building with land or a town house increases the rate by 0.5 percent or 0.25 percent, respectively.
  • Maximum loan amount: in Moscow and the Moscow Region - fifty million rubles, in St. Petersburg and the Leningrad Region - twenty five million rubles.
  • The maximum loan amount for participants in salary projects is: eighty-five percent of the cost of housing; seventy-five percent of the cost of apartments and fifty percent of the cost of a residential building with a land plot. The maximum loan amount for non-salary customers is eighty, seventy and fifty percent, respectively.

Credit card insurance

In any branch, judging by the reviews of Alfa-Bank credit card, the client can insure himself as the owner of a credit card in case of loss of ability to work or his death. The benefits of an insurance program are as follows:

  • One hundred percent protection in the event of an insured event.
  • The fee is charged depending on the amount owed.
  • Convenience of operational design.

Requirements for the insured person:

  • A person owns an Alfa-Bank card with a credit limit.
  • The age of the client is not less than eighteen and not more than sixty.

The limit of insurance liability is set equal to the amount of actual debt on the card. It cannot exceed half a million rubles.

The amount that the client will receive upon the occurrence of an insured event is called insurance payment:

  • The insurance premium for death risk equals one hundred percent of the sum insured.
  • The insurance payment for the risk of disability is defined as 0.3 percent of the sum insured, starting from the twenty-first day of continuous incapacity for work, but not more than sixty days.

Translations

Features of transfers at Alfa-Bank:

  • Transfers in national currency are possible between bank cards issued by credit institutions of the country.
  • To transfer, you need to enter the card data of the sender and recipient. The validity period of the recipient card is not required.
  • To transfer, you do not need to have an account or a card in Alfa-Bank.
  • Money is credited to the recipient‚Äôs card almost instantly, you don‚Äôt need to go somewhere to receive money.
  • If the sender card supports 3DSecure technology, then the transfer is carried out.
  • Transfers between different payment systems are possible.

Translations prohibited for any translation channels:

  • On cards that do not support 3dSecure technology.
  • Transfers from corporate cards.
  • Sending funds from Visa cards of any banks of other states.
  • The bank of the sender or recipient may refuse to transfer.

Transfer Limits:

  • The maximum size of one transfer is one hundred thousand rubles.
  • The largest amount of all transfers per day on one sender card is one hundred fifty thousand rubles.
  • The largest amount of all transfers for a calendar month on one card is one and a half million rubles.
  • The volume of all transfers per calendar month on one card is not more than fifty transactions maximum.

All except daily limits can be applied to the cards of the sender and the cards of the recipient via any channel for receiving the service.

Tariffs for translations:

  • No commission fee between Alfa-Bank cards, provided that the sender card is not credit.
  • No commission fee from another bank card to Alfa-Bank card.
  • From the payment card to the card of another credit institution of the country or between the cards of different institutions, a commission of 1.95 percent is charged.

Timing of funds transfer: instantly - to Alfa-Bank cards and up to an hour - to cards of other banks.
