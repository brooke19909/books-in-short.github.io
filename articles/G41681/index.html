Holidays in the city of Alanya: "Kemal Bay" 5 * - one of the best options

Have you decided to spend your vacation in Alanya? Kemal Bay is a hotel to watch out for. It is a colorful tourist complex with numerous green spaces, located on the front page of the Mediterranean coast. In the immediate vicinity is the village of Konakla, and from the nearest city of Alanya it is separated by a distance of 8 kilometers. The complex is surrounded by small hills. The forest is just 500 meters away.

The Kemal Bay Hotel (Alanya) received its first guests in 1993. In 2008, it was overhauled. The hotel consists of a residential 6-storey building and several auxiliary concrete structures built in a classical style. Nearby stands the Green Hill 5 * hotel, which has a common territory and a coastline with Kamel Bay. Antalya Airport is located 120 kilometers away.



Rooms

For guests who come to rest in the city of Alanya, Kemal Bay provides 285 standard rooms, half of which have sea views. They have a different area - from 28 to 34 square meters. One room can accommodate up to 4 people. There are 9 family rooms that have two bedrooms, as well as 5 rooms equipped for disabled guests. All apartments are cozy and very clean. The floors are carpeted, parquet or ceramic. Each room has a balcony, allowing vacationers to fully enjoy the beauty of the surrounding nature, as well as breathe the sea air. Phones, TVs are installed. There is a split air conditioning, satellite TV with many channels, among which 1 is Russian-speaking. There is a minibar (daily replenishment with water), a safe (for a fee).

The open section of the restaurant seats up to 500 guests, and the closed section - 400 people, air conditioning is installed. There are two summer restaurants and two winter ones. In addition, there are three bars. Local drinks are provided free of charge. All imported drinks and freshly squeezed juices are at extra cost. Special chairs are provided for small guests.



Beach

Like most hotels in the city of Alanya, Kemal Bay has its own private beach, covered with sand and pebbles, which is 150 meters long. Beach equipment is provided free of charge. Changing cabins are installed, there is a shower. The entrance to the sea is gentle. For an additional fee, you can engage in various water sports, play volleyball.

Tourist info

Those tourists who have already visited Kemal Bay (Alanya) leave diverse reviews. One thing is certain: the hotel has a good infrastructure and a huge amount of entertainment, which allows you to make your vacation active and eventful. On site there is an adult pool and a children's pool. There are two scheduled water slides. In addition, the hotel has a number of different shops. Guests will not be bored by the wonderful animation in Turkish and English, as well as in Russian. You can play billiards at an extra charge. In the evening, a disco awaits guests. For sports enthusiasts, there is a gym, tennis courts are open. For those who support beauty and health during the rest, a hairdresser, sauna, Turkish bath, beauty treatments, services of a qualified massage therapist are provided.
