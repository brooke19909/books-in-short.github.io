Which banks can I take a car loan without hull insurance?

Car loans are the provision of money for the purchase of a car. Moreover, the transport may be new or used. The bank issues funds after approval of the application. At the same time, lenders evaluate their risks. Loans are secured by a car. Many banks issue them only when buying a CASCO policy. Is it obligatory? Is it possible to get a car loan without hull insurance? The answers to these questions are presented in the article.

Is it obligatory?

With car loans, the bank transfers the money to the seller. Prior to reckoning, the car will be in the form of a deposit at the bank. For most, buying a car on credit is the only way to solve a transport problem. An advantage is considered to be a quick acquisition and with a minimum amount of savings.

And overpayment is considered a drawback. You have to pay about 50% more than the car costs. Therefore, many want to save in every way possible. Can I get a car loan without hull insurance? The client has the right to this, in addition, then the cost of transport decreases.

What do banks offer?

Without insurance, the bank takes risks. If the car gets into an accident or is stolen, and the owner cannot pay the loan, then there will be nothing to withdraw. Therefore, a car loan without CASCO is usually provided on the following conditions:

  • the rate rises to 22%;
  • credit history is studied more carefully;
  • loan period changes;
  • the amount is limited to 1 million rubles;
  • down payment up to 50% is required;
  • additional fees are charged;
  • there is a need to purchase a car only in partner salons.

If everything suits you, you can safely arrange a car loan without hull insurance. These loans are provided for the purchase of new and used cars. But it is important to consider that the client is also at risk. If the car is stolen or suffers in an accident, you still need to pay the loan in full, while no compensation is paid. Therefore, at first it is important to weigh the pros and cons of such a purchase.

Conditions

Car loans without hull insurance are provided by banks on more stringent conditions, which helps minimize risks. The main conditions include:

  1. Increased rates. Since the risk of losing collateral increases without restoring it, the bank protects personal assets in another way - it increases the rate. Each bank has different indicators, but usually such a car loan is 3-5% higher.
  2. Short maturity. The longer the loan period, the higher the risk that something will happen to the transport. Therefore, without CASCO, the repayment time is reduced. Instead of 5-7 years, a contract is usually drawn up for 3 years.
  3. Limitations on the amount. A car loan without CASCO for a new car or a used one is provided in the amount of 500 thousand - 1 million rubles.
  4. Increased first payment. In some banks, it can reach up to 50% of the cost of the car.

Banks giving a car loan without CASCO may have different conditions. But the above nuances work almost everywhere. A car loan without CASCO insurance is usually not provided without a certificate of income. It should indicate the place of work in which the client has been employed for more than 6 months or 1 year.

How to get a car loan without hull insurance? In advance, you need to select the make of the machine, check its condition and equipment, and also find out how much to apply for. Some car dealerships only cooperate with specific banks, which also needs to be clarified. The procedure for obtaining a car loan without CASCO for a new car or supported is as follows.

Bank selection and filing

But not all credit institutions agree to provide services without CASCO. You can apply to several banks. But it is important to consider that a preliminary positive answer is not a guarantee of 100%. Documents will be checked when visiting a bank branch before signing the contract.

Standard requirements include:

  • age (21-65 years);
  • citizenship of the Russian Federation;
  • permanent registration in the field of service;
  • lack of criminal record;
  • income - not less than 30-40 thousand rubles.

Collection of documents

When an application for a car loan without CASCO for used cars or new vehicles is approved, you can prepare a package of documents. Availability required:

  • passports
  • loan applications;
  • driver's license;
  • copies of the work book;
  • Help 2-PIT.

Some financial institutions need other documents. This may be a military ID, certificate of education, paper on property. If the solvency, in the opinion of the bank, is insufficient, additional conditions are set for them. For example, 1-2 guarantors are required.

Signing a sales contract

When permission of the bank is obtained, you can conclude a contract of sale. When a car dealership and a bank work in conjunction, the procedure will be simpler. Then in the cabin you can choose a car and immediately sign an agreement with a bank representative.

Conclusion of the contract and making the first payment

Before signing the contract, you must read it. Particular attention should be paid to the following points:

  1. Bid size. Decent banks indicate the rate on the main page of the document, and do not fix it in the notes.
  2. Amounts of commissions for operations. The lower they are, the better.
  3. Methods of paying a loan. It is advisable that there are several options for depositing funds, including an online repayment method.
  4. Early payment terms. Some banks may have fees for the full payment of funds before the expiration of the contract.
  5. Owner Rights.

Then you need to make the first payment, which will be credited to the account of the seller of the car. Banks do not provide a car loan without a down payment without CASCO, as this poses a big risk.

Car registration and debt payment

It is necessary to put a car on the account in the traffic police. This must be done within 10 days after the conclusion of the sales contract. You can contact any department of the traffic police.

It is necessary to pay the fee and provide documents for the car, including the mandatory insurance policy. After registration, the buyer becomes the owner of the vehicle. It remains only to comply with traffic rules and timely pay the loan debt.

Bank selection

It depends on how much you have to pay monthly and on what terms. For the requirements to be acceptable, you need to know how to choose the right bank:

  1. You need to get acquainted with the ratings of banks. It is advisable to consider independent ratings. It also happens that a small regional bank offers the best conditions and is ready to provide a loan for 1 day. But with the bankruptcy of this institution, it will be necessary to go through many unpleasant procedures to find out new details. It is better to choose famous banks.
  2. You must read customer reviews. They can be found on trusted online services. Information of interest is still recognized through friends and relatives.
  3. It is important to compare the terms of cooperation. First you need to know what documents are provided. Each bank has its own conditions for the provision of car loans.

Care must be taken with express car loans. Winning over time often leads to higher bets. Other nuances include additional fees for opening and maintaining an account, unfavorable conditions for early payment.
