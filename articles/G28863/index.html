The best hotel in Sol-Iletsk according to tourists reviews

Just 5 years ago, vacation in Sol-Iletsk was not very common. Hotels here could be counted on the fingers of a hand. The level of service in them was so terrible that it was easier to stay in a rented clean apartment or rent a room near the lakes in a small house. In addition to the fact that there were practically no free rooms, the prices were still the same as in Orenburg.



Currently, hotels are not a problem for visitors here. There are plenty of them in the city. Local service can easily handle the increased flow. I am glad that the price policy in the city has significantly softened: the cost of living is not so much reduced, but the level of service has improved, which provided the tourist with everything necessary for the same money.

If you need a hotel in Sol-Iletsk, you do not need to focus on its proximity to the lakes. This is a fairly small city, and each tourist attraction, historical landmark or body of water, is here within walking distance. Pay your attention to other parameters: the quality of service and the level of the hotel, the correspondence of the price and the declared service.

Hotel "Riviera"

The Riviera Hotel in Sol-Iletsk is a new luxurious establishment located in the very center of the city, 200 meters from the entrance to the famous salt lakes.

It offers guests several rooms (2-seater and 4-seater) of the "luxury" category with individually designed design combining home comfort and elegance.

All rooms have a private bathroom, air conditioning, LCD TV, and everything you need for a normal stay. There is free Wi-Fi access in the lobby and rooms of the hotel.



The hotel has swings, cozy gazebos, a playground and benches. At the same time, outdoor enthusiasts have the opportunity to play badminton, volleyball, and also ride a bike. In addition, various additional services are offered here, including good food. The rooms are cleaned every day.

Hotel "Paradise" in Sol-Iletsk is a cozy manor built of logs. The interior is designed in the theme of a traditional Russian estate. Ecologically clean and pleasant material gives visitors a warm feeling of a cozy home.

The facility is located next to the main attraction of the city - Salt Lake.

At the hotel you can find everything you need - draft beer, a grocery store, a gazebo, a kitchen, a barbecue.



You can also stay in chic deluxe or standard rooms.
