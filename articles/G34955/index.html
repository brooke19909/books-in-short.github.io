Holidays at Beldiana Hotel 4 will be unforgettable

Turkish Beldiana Hotel 4 was erected near the city of Kemer in 1998 in the small village of Beldibi. This four-story pink single-hull building was renovated in 2004.

Location The nearest international airport from Beldiana Hotel is located south of Antalya, at a distance of 39 kilometers. The village is located in the Mediterranean resort area along the Taurus mountain slope, in a narrow and long plain. Beldibi has many different shops and hotels for vacationers. By car or bus you can go to the nearby cities of Kemer and Antalya and visit museums, temples, an aquarium, and admire the local

sights. Fans of outdoor activities will be interested in a walk through the mountainous terrain covered with pine trees. Trails lead to the mountains, one of them is trodden along a dried river bed. Wild boars are found in the forests.

The rooms. Beldiana Hotel 4 has 113 standard apartments (for 2-3 people) and 10 family-type rooms (for 4 people). Each room for vacationers has air conditioning, a telephone with direct connection, a TV with satellite channels, a desk, a large mirror, a minibar and a small safe (extra charge). In the bathroom there is a shower or bath, hairdryer. Carpeted floors. Each room has a balcony where you can hang clothes to dry. The room is cleaned daily, and bed linen is changed twice a week.

Beach. Beldiana Hotel 4 has its own sand and pebble beach with a length of 50 meters and two outdoor pools (adult and children). TO

beach and pool side spa guests enjoy free mattresses, umbrellas, beach towels and sun loungers. Near the beach, a wooden pier with a length of 35 meters is equipped. A bar is only available on site by the pool.



Nutrition. Three meals a day (on an all-inclusive basis) are provided in the hotel restaurant. For vacationers, local chefs prepare a variety of vegetables in any form, poultry, fish dishes.

Additional services. Visiting hotel provides laundry and dry cleaning services, doctor and massage therapist, wireless computer network, car rental. There is an opportunity to do water sports.

Beldiana Hotel 4 has a playground for children and a games room. Adults can use the tennis court, gym, sauna, jacuzzi, play billiards or volleyball on the sports ground. Daily and evening entertainment programs, discos are regularly held.
