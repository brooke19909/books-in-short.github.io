Deposits with a monthly interest payment: banks, rates and terms

Today, perhaps, there is no such bank that would not offer deposit programs for the population with a monthly interest payment. They differ most often in the amount of the minimum contribution, interest rate and term of the deposit.
How to choose the best? About this in our article.

Basic concepts

Deposits with a monthly payment - this is an urgent investment. Interest on these deposits is accrued every month. Depending on the terms of the contract, they can be withdrawn to a separate open account, cashed, or attached to the main deposit.





One type of fixed-term deposits are rental deposits. They differ in a rather large amount of the deposit placed in the bank. The amount of monthly payments allows the owner of the deposit to live off the profits.

Rent deposits with a monthly interest payment have the following features:

  • Profit is directly proportional to the term of the deposit.
  • The standard contract does not provide for replenishment of the deposit and partial withdrawal.
  • The interest rate is lower than conventional term deposits.

Package of documents

Most often, a bank requires a passport and identification code to process a deposit. Instead, you can provide a passport or military ID. An application is filled in place in the presence of a bank specialist.

Interest accrual

Deposits in banks with a monthly payment - this is a regular profit. The interest calculated and paid by the banking organization, the client can receive in the form of:

  • cash;
  • transfer to a specially opened account;
  • additional amount to the main investment.

The amount of interest on the deposit will increase in proportion to the period for which the deposit is open, and the increase in the deposit. That is, for example, funds entrusted to the bank for two to three years will be approximately 3% more than the same funds, but for a period of three to six months.

Early termination of the contract

If the client demands to return the invested funds before the expiration of the agreement, then the interest in this case will be accrued as on demand deposits, unless otherwise specified in the contract.

If the client does not apply for the return of the invested money either ahead of schedule or at the end of the term of the deposit agreement with a monthly payment, the contract is considered prolonged automatically (unless otherwise provided by the agreement).

How to withdraw funds?

For this, 7-10 days before the deposit closes, you must personally come to the office. You need to have a passport, your own copy of the contract and a statement from the current account with you. The action plan is simple. In the presence of a bank specialist, a statement is written about the early termination of the contract. Then a copy of the statement is made.





If a deposit in rubles with a monthly interest payment is closed ahead of schedule, then the banking organization has the right to reduce the interest rate over the past 30 days (sometimes to zero).

If the money is withdrawn on the day the deposit expires, the application is not written, since the entire amount is automatically reserved in the client‚Äôs account.

If you withdraw the amount of 200,000 rubles or more, you must contact the bank in advance and write a statement indicating the amount and denomination of banknotes. The application must be approved and the day the funds are paid out.

Today, all the procedures described can be carried out via the Internet on the official website of a banking organization and receive money into the account without ever appearing in the office.

Deposit closing

Opening a deposit with a monthly payment, the client and the bank agree on its expiration date. Upon its expiration, the contribution is either closed or automatically prolonged. But what if you need to close the deposit ahead of schedule?





Documents are provided to the office where the deposit was opened: a passport or other identification document, a copy of the agreement and, if available, a passbook. An application is made on the spot, on the basis of which the bank specialist closes the deposit.

The foreign currency deposit is closed according to the same scheme. You can use an ATM or terminal, but the Sirius system must be installed on them. It is understandable even to the most inexperienced user, and the scheme of its action is similar to a similar procedure in online banking.

Insurance

Current legislation obliges banks to insure all investments up to 700,000 rubles. The state deposit insurance system guarantees return (in case of bankruptcy of a banking organization).

Are deposits with monthly interest payments beneficial?

Like any banking product, such deposits have their disadvantages and advantages. The latter include:

  • Almost every contract allows you to add funds to the main contribution.
  • At the end of the agreement, it is automatically prolonged (unless otherwise specified in the contract).

By cons, many attribute a ‚Äústrange‚Äù return. If we compare two deposits with the same validity periods and interest rates, but in one they are paid monthly, and in the other at the end of the term, then the latter will be more profitable. Here it is worth considering - when the money is needed: monthly, but in small amounts, or one-time, but large.

Among user reviews of deposits with a monthly payment, the theme of the complexity of the bank‚Äôs actions when transferring these percentages is often encountered. Most customers think this is intentional. Actually, maybe this is about taxation? Often, banking organizations pay deposit taxes for their customers. It should be noted that deposits are taxed only with a fixed rate of over 13%. This figure is floating. It depends on the refinancing rate of the Central Bank and is calculated as the sum of the key rate of the Central Bank on the day of conclusion or extension of the agreement with the bank and 5% of the annual rate.

An undoubted minus is a sharp decrease in the rate with early closure of the deposit.
