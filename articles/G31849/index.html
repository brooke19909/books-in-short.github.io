How to take a screenshot and edit it on an iPhone: tips for users

What is it for and what is its use?



What it is

A screenshot (screenshot) is an image that was captured by a computer, laptop, phone, tablet, iPhone or iPad. It usually shows exactly what the user sees on the screen of his device. Screenshots are used mainly in order to make a manual for beginners. For example, step by step, using screenshots explain how to use a particular program. So this is a very useful feature!

How to take a screenshot on an iPhone?





So, what is it, we have already found out. Now, let's move on to the actions:

  1. Turn on your iPhone.
  2. Open the desired page or tab that you want to photograph.
  3. Press two buttons at the same time - Power (on / off) and Home (round button at the bottom of the screen).
  4. Wait for the click or flash of the screen. This will be a signal that the picture has already been taken.
  5. Go to the "Photos" application and see the resulting image, it will be saved in the "Camera Roll" folder.

Now you know how to take a screenshot on an iPhone. But perhaps this is not enough. After all, sometimes you need to get a picture of not just the entire screen, but some part of it. That is why, after the screenshot is in the folder with photos, it would be desirable to edit and crop the excess. A special program designed to work with images can help us with this.

How to edit the received screenshot in the Photos application?

So, the screenshot is already ready. It remains to edit it a bit, but for this:

  1. Open the application.
  2. Select the picture you want to work with.
  3. Click the "Edit" button. It is located in the upper right corner.
  4. As you can see, you are offered four editing options: Rotate, Improve, Red Eye, Crop. Choose what you need.
