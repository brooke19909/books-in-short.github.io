Shopping center "Babylon" (Tver) - a modern shopping center

"Babylon" - a shopping center in the Moscow region of the city of Tver, designed to provide the population of the region with necessary goods and services.

The shops

On the ground floor in the shopping center "Babylon" (Tver) there are supermarkets "Economy" and "Trolley", which are popular among the population. This is all the more convenient because many people come here to shop for a few days. Supermarkets are open from 9.00 a.m. to 10.00 p.m.





On the 1-4 floors there are chain stores of many famous and popular brands. Among them are BOGGI , Oliver , ESCADA , Telemax, Children, Bizzarro, INCITY, OstZola, Kari, 1000 and 1 Bag, Promenade, Time Shop, Absolute, Alexandrite "- this is only a small part of the outlets of the Babylon shopping center (Tver). A pharmacy kiosk also works here. Department stores are open from 10.00 a.m. to 9.00 p.m.

In the basement there are also shops of local entrepreneurs. Here, working hours are from 11.00 a.m. to 8.00 p.m.

For children

For the kids who came here with their parents, there are also fun. The Ladita center operates here, where animators are engaged with them, the Zhivaya Skazka contact zoo, where many small animals are represented, as well as slides and cars. Very often during the season an amusement park is set up on a large area in front of the Babylon shopping center (Tver), a zoo and a dolphinarium arrive.

Healthy lifestyle

For lovers of health and sports, the ATHLETIC GYM fitness center is located in the mall. You can visit the gym or group classes. It offers yoga and dancing, Pilates and stretching, martial arts. Classes are led by certified instructors. There is also a bath complex with an infrared sauna and a spa cabinet. All classes are conducted by certified instructors.

You can come to the fitness club both for a one-time lesson or purchase a subscription, which is much cheaper. Fitness club is located on the fourth floor of the shopping center "Babylon" (Tver). You can come to the club from 7 a.m. to 11 a.m. on weekdays, and from 8 a.m. to 10 a.m. on weekends.

Salons of communication

On the ground floor of the complex there are points of the main communication salons. This is the "Messenger", MTS, "Megaphone".





other services

Before the shopping center "Babylon" (Tver) there is a free parking for more than 300 cars. On the third floor there are sanitary rooms.

Tver has a network of Japanese restaurants "Niyama". In the shopping center, there was also a place for one of the institutions. In addition, there is also a pizzeria. Perfectly solve the problems with food in the shopping center "Babylon" cafe.
