Hotel The Brother's Residence 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Have you been to Thailand and fell in love with this country? Or do you just want to discover its beauty and enjoy a wonderful vacation? If at the same time you want to find a place for a budget, but at the same time comfortable accommodation, then pay attention to the three-star The Brother's Residence Hotel (Patong, Phuket Island). We will talk further about what is offered in this hotel, as well as about the opinion that has developed among tourists who have been here.



Location

The Brother's Residence 3 * confidently boasts a very advantageous location. So, the distance to the nearest international airport is only 26 kilometers. Even if you get stuck in traffic on the way to the hotel, you still won‚Äôt spend a lot of time on the road. The hotel in question is also just a short walk from Bangla Road and Patong Beach. Nearby is a market where you can buy fresh fruits and seafood. In the vicinity of the hotel you will find a lot of infrastructure facilities - massage parlors, cafes, bars, grocery and gift shops, companies offering excursion services and car rental, and much more.



Description

The hotel in question was built in 2010. It is one five-story building. In total, it contains 52 rooms of the categories "standard" and "family". The building is equipped with an elevator. The hotel has a coffee shop, reception, luggage storage, tour desk, ATM and much more. An airport shuttle service is available at an extra charge. The hotel staff speaks Thai and English.

Rooms

All apartments at The Brother's Residence are quite spacious, stylishly decorated, furnished with quality furniture and modern appliances. Each room has the necessary furniture, individual air conditioning, refrigerator, flat-screen TV with cable channels, hairdryer, bathroom. Many apartments have small balconies with a pair of chairs and a table. Here you can comfortably stay with a cup of coffee or a glass of wine and enjoy the surroundings. It offers free Wi-Fi. Rooms are cleaned every day. Towels maids change as needed. Sheets are updated every week. Beach towels are also provided, which you can take with you outside the hotel. If they are lost or damaged, you will have to pay a fine.



The Brother's Residence 3 * (Phuket): traveler reviews

Everyone, even a not very experienced tourist, probably knows how important it is to take care of many things in advance of a trip on vacation. So, it is worth considering travel time, ordering tickets, learning as much as possible about the country in which you plan to spend several days or even weeks, and, of course, choose a hotel. The last point is one of the most important. Indeed, even if you do not plan to spend a lot of time at the hotel, it will nevertheless become a second home for you on vacation. And you will probably want to be able to come to a comfortable, cleaned room every day, sleep on a comfortable soft bed with fresh linen, use all the benefits of civilization and count on excellent service and attention from the staff. Therefore, many tourists long before the expected date of departure begin to collect information about the hotels of their place of interest. At the same time, experienced travelers are advised to definitely get acquainted with the reviews of former guests of a particular hotel. After all, tourists are trying to truthfully talk about the pros and cons of a particular place that they had to face during their holidays. Thus, this will help you to form a more complete and closer to the real state of affairs idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhat will await you in a particular hotel, and from this make a choice that you will not have to regret later on. Today we offer to learn together about what impressions our compatriots left when they stayed at The Brother's Residence 3 * (Phuket, Patong).



Guest rating

In general, The Brother's Residence 3 * reviews get quite good. This is indicated by the rating of the hotel, which is four points out of the maximum possible five. Most of all, tourists were satisfied with the location of the hotel, the equipment of rooms, the quality of service and the nearby beach. Travelers expressed some complaints about the lack of convenient parking and entertainment for children. But we offer to learn more about everything.

Environment

In their reviews, many tourists note that The Brother's Residence 3 * hotel (Thailand) has a very good location. So, Patong Beach can be reached in leisurely steps in just 10 minutes. Moreover, the path runs past the market, a variety of shops where you can buy something you need or have a bite to eat. The hotel is also located very close to the city center - the entertainment Bangla Road. It can be reached in five minutes. A lot of entertainment venues are concentrated here - night clubs, bars, discos, etc. In addition, in the vicinity of the hotel you can easily find a variety of cafes and restaurants (both Thai, Russian and other cuisines), shops, supermarkets, pharmacies , tour desk, car rental and more.

Travelers also noted the relative proximity of the hotel to the airport. So, even taking into account traffic jams, it will take you no more than an hour to travel from the air harbor.

Housing stock

Judging by the comments of our compatriots, almost all of them were quite satisfied with the rooms provided to them in the hotel in question. So, the apartments here are quite spacious, pleasantly decorated, with not new, but quite high-quality and comfortable furniture, well-functioning appliances. Each room has one or more beds, chairs or a sofa, bedside tables, safe, air conditioning, hairdryer, TV, minibar, a minimum set of dishes, a bathroom. Some guests, however, complained about the absence of an electric kettle in the room, but this did not become a serious problem. Tourists did not complain about the work of household appliances. Guests also noted with pleasure that on television it was possible to watch three Russian-language channels.

Please note that hotel policies do not allow fresh fruit to be brought to the rooms. The fact is that if you do not throw away food waste, then ants or other small insects may appear in your room. In this case, the hotel will fine you. Therefore, if you want to eat fruit in the apartment, do not forget to throw the leftovers into the trash can near the hotel.



Nutrition

As a rule, when paying for accommodation at The Brother's Residence 3 * (Phuket), travelers also take breakfast. In general, according to our compatriots, in the morning here, of course, you can eat without problems. However, a special variety of breakfasts at the hotel does not shine. So, you will be offered fresh vegetables (carrots, cucumbers, tomatoes, lettuce) and fruits, scrambled eggs or omelette of your choice, cereal, granola, toast. Also in the morning you can eat two hot dishes, one of which is spicy. Some guests prefer not to have breakfast at the hotel every day. So, you could always walk in the morning to a supermarket located very close by and buy a fresh sandwich, takeaway coffee and fruits.

As for lunch and dinner, almost all tourists ate outside the hotel. Moreover, both in the vicinity of the hotel, and in general in Phuket, there is no shortage of cafes with reasonable prices. In addition, you can pamper yourself by tasting dishes from different cuisines of the world. So, in addition to Thai cuisine, many cafes here offer Indian dishes. If during the rest you want the usual dumplings, dumplings or borsch, then several Russian restaurants are at your service.

In addition, tourists are happy to buy fresh seafood at the local market. Here, for a small surcharge, they will be immediately prepared with rice or another side dish, if desired. Travelers are advised to definitely try pancakes and other dishes that are prepared right next to you on mobile food carts. So it‚Äôs simply impossible to stay hungry here!



rules

In accordance with the internal regulations of The Brother's Residence 3 * (Phuket), a photo of which can be seen in this article, new arrivals are checked into their rooms after two in the afternoon. However, as our compatriots note, even if you arrive at the hotel earlier than this time, then you will most likely be accommodated almost immediately. Please note that upon check-in you will be asked to make a deposit. Its size is 2000 baht. On the day of departure, if everything is in order in your room, the deposit will be returned to you in full. The apartment should be vacated on the last day of your stay before noon.

Beach

As mentioned, Patong Beach is a ten minute walk from the hotel. Experienced travelers say that this, of course, is not the best beach in Phuket. So, it is quite crowded and not always very clean. However, it can be visited several times. Next to it there are lots of cafes and bars where you can have a bite or have a drink with an alcoholic or soft drink. Many tourists prefer to spend an active vacation, every day discovering new corners of Phuket, which implies not only attractions, but also beaches. Moving around the island is easiest on a rented motorbike. According to travelers, despite the fact that traffic on the roads here is rather chaotic, with caution, you can drive quite safely.
