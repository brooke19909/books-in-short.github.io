Dolphinarium in St. Petersburg: description

In 1998, a dolphinarium was opened in St. Petersburg. This establishment is a branch of Utrish. During the performance, you can see fur seals, beluga whales, sea ​​lions, dolphins and even walruses.

Show

Visiting the dolphinarium in St. Petersburg, you can see how animals jump through the rings, emerge from the water. In addition, dolphins carry on the backs of trainers, sing and even draw. They also perform other tricks, which, of course, delight parents and children.

Where to keep dolphins?

If we talk about water, then in the pool it is artificial. In composition and salinity, it is close to the waters of the Black Sea. Of course, keeping marine animals in their natural habitat in St. Petersburg will not work. Although the dolphinarium workers are doing everything they can to make the artists comfortable there.

Where is the facility located?

The dolphinarium is located in St. Petersburg on Konstantinovsky Prospekt 19, in the Spartak basin. The metro station located nearby is Krestovsky Ostrov.

Opening hours and ticket prices

The duration of the presentation is fifty minutes. The show can be watched at 12, 14, 16 and 18 hours.

If you want to visit the dolphinarium in St. Petersburg, then you should familiarize yourself with the prices. For an adult ticket you need to give from 300 to 1000 rubles (on holidays), depending on the sector. Children's (from three to ten years) costs from two hundred to seven hundred rubles.

For shooting a performance, you need to pay three hundred rubles to your camera, and 300-700 rubles for the dolphinarium technique (depending on the size and type of the photo).

Additional services

Dolphinarium in St. Petersburg offers additional services. These include swimming with dolphins. You can spend time with marine animals from the age of fourteen. Swimming with dolphins implies tactile contact with them, playing together using various details.

The cost of such a service is seven thousand rubles per person.

Dolphinarium (St. Petersburg): visitor reviews

People who have already visited this institution speak about it only positively. Kids and adults love the performance of marine animals. After such an event, everyone's emotions just go wild. Those who swam with dolphins were pleased with swimming. The time spent with these marvelous animals was remembered for a long time. The only negative that visitors noticed is the condition of the building. It needs repairs.
