Eilat: reviews of tourists about the sea



The resort is located on the Red Sea, and therefore you can go here to relax in the winter. Although many claim that the best time to visit Eilat is March-April. There are not very many people, and the weather is great. In addition, here, as in Egypt, a stunning underwater world. You can observe with a mask the colorful fish that inhabit the reefs on the Coral Beach (towards Sinai). Or you can visit the underwater "observatory", where the inhabitants of the depths will surround you from all sides. Diving is another activity for guests of the city of Eilat. Israel, the reviews of tourists which are often told about holy places, appears here as a country for sea holidays. Diver clubs here simply can not be counted. Moreover, in summer the water does not get very warm, and in the heat it is very pleasant to swim. And the entrance to the sea is completely safe for children, because there are practically no waves.

Eilat: reviews of tourists about food and entertainment



At this resort you will not get bored. As in any similar place, there are a lot of restaurants on the promenade, cafes closer to the center, evening discos and other entertainment. Enough of nightclubs. Among the bars among Russian-speaking tourists, the Three Monkeys Pub is especially popular, as well as a cinema with a 3D effect. Inhabitants of expensive hotels in cafes and restaurants do not eat, because for the most part they believe that they are fed to the dump and very diverse in their own hotel. The food in Eilat institutions is naturally kosher, but it introduces you to the peculiarities of Jewish traditional cuisine. Almost all tourists praise seafood (perfectly cooked and in huge portions) and beautiful exotic fruits. Some note the special taste of local pomegranate wine.

Eilat: reviews of tourists about shopping and excursions



As in any Mediterranean country, in Israel there are many local souvenirs that vacationers take from here. To do this, visit the local bazaar. And those who wish to buy some sort of branded thing are advised to walk along the main shopping streets, and in the evening along the promenade. There are boutiques and shops of famous designers, and the prices are far from sky-high. It is interesting to walk around the city, but the public transport system is well developed. In addition, many locals speak Russian and they will show and tell you everything, as they say, "in the best possible way." Of the excursions, the most common are boat trips - by boat, a trip to the so-called Dolphin Reef, where you can swim with these smart creatures. Also, as in neighboring Egypt, various kinds of safaris, visits to jewelry factories, camel farms and more are widespread. Often book excursions to Jerusalem. Even on New Year's Eve people come to Eilat. Reviews of tourists (including 2013) convincingly prove that whenever you decide to visit this resort town, you can always find entertainment to your liking.
