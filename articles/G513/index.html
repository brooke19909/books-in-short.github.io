Hotel AquaSplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * (Tunisia / Sousse): photos and reviews

Tunisia is a fairly well-known and popular country among tourists. After all, here they are ready to take a traveler to a luxury hotel, offering modern comfort with a touch of oriental tale. And of course, tourists just love to relax on local beaches, because the country is warm even in winter.

If it comes to Tunisia, then every traveler knows that Sousse is the most popular resort city in the country. Of course, a huge number of hotels work here all year round, including the Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 *. What conditions should a tourist expect and for what type of vacation is a hotel suitable? Every experienced traveler is used to looking for answers to these questions.

Where is the hotel located?





Can the location of the Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel (Sousse) be considered convenient? Sousse is a wonderful resort city with a developed infrastructure. And you have to live in a quiet area just 4 km from the center. By the way, the beach is very close, as well as international airports. The nearest of them is in the city of Monastir, which is 22 km away. But the distance to Enfida Airport is about 44 km.

Do not worry about a long trip if you intend to stay at the Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel. Tours, as a rule, already include a comfortable transfer, and the journey will not take so much time.

By the way, in walking distance you will have not only the sea, but also cafes, interesting sights, shopping centers and entertainment venues.

Features of the infrastructure and brief information about the territory

The hotel complex is considered to be large. It consists of several multi-storey buildings, as well as many secluded bungalows, where tourists will find peace, solitude and peace.

The hotel area is huge - it resembles a small town with its own infrastructure. There is everything you need for a fun holiday, including swimming pools, walking paths, a picturesque garden, relaxation terraces, a large water park, restaurants, bars and even your own shops. By the way, in 2006 a radical reconstruction was carried out here.

Hotel Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 *: photo and room information





The hotel complex consists of 470 rooms. Of course, tourists are offered rooms of various categories, including standard double and large family rooms. You can also settle in a separate bungalow.

The premises will delight you with fresh repairs, discreet, but beautiful design, as well as modern appliances. For example, you can use a telephone, individual air conditioning and a TV (it is possible to watch even some Russian satellite channels). A minibar is available, but it is filled only upon request. Drinking water, by the way, is not delivered to the room, but there are coolers throughout the territory. For a small fee, you can use the safe - it is in every room.

The bathrooms have recently been completely renovated, and all the plumbing has been replaced with a new one. Depending on the category of room, you will find either a shower or a bathtub. Of course, there is a toilet, a washbasin, and a hair dryer. Shampoos, soap and clean towels are delivered to the room every day during a scheduled cleaning.





What kind of food does the hotel offer a tourist?

What does the hotel Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * offer its guests? Tunisia is a country in which the all-inclusive system is considered common. Most hotel complexes offer tourists a good meal. Having settled here, you can count on daily breakfasts, lunches, lunches and dinners, which take place in the form of a buffet table. Guests can enjoy international and Tunisian cuisine, as well as good service. By the way, the menu is quite good, and the restaurant premises can accommodate all tourists.

The hotel has four a la carte restaurants, where visitors are offered to enjoy the most delicious masterpieces of local, Mediterranean, Asian and British cuisine. Visiting these restaurants is paid separately, but guests have the right to eat once in one of them for free (by appointment).

There is a Moorish cafe until late at night, and the hotel also has several bars (only drinks from local producers are provided free of charge). Until two in the morning there is a disco bar where you can relax and dance.

Beach: beach holiday for guests of the Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Sousse)





Sousse is a resort town famous for its incredibly beautiful beaches. This is precisely the reason why tourists come to this country. As already mentioned, the hotel complex is located almost on the beach itself, since its territory faces the sea. This is a private stretch of the coast, and for the most part only guests of the Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel have a rest here.

On the other hand, during the period of maximum occupancy of the hotel, there are a lot of people on the shore. Nevertheless, there are enough sunbeds and umbrellas for everyone - by the way, they can be used for free. Beach towels are provided at the hotel with a small security deposit.

Reviews say that the beach here is clean, beautiful and covered with soft yellow sand. There is wooden flooring on the shore, which greatly simplifies walks. The entrance to the sea is convenient and completely cleared of stones and debris. It has a bar and a rescue tower.

As for more active entertainment, tourists here are not bored - you can rent almost any type of water transport. There is a large volleyball court on the beach. And tourists love to spend time learning to surf, diving and other water sports.

Service and additional services





When deciding to spend a vacation at the Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel, you can certainly count on additional service. The reception is open around the clock - here you can not only get the necessary information, but also exchange currency. Also, guests enjoy a large parking lot and rent vehicles directly on site. The laundry is constantly working. There is a cozy souvenir shop and a small shop selling various household items.

There is Wi-Fi in the hotel area, but you have to pay for Internet access (prices are quite affordable, especially if you pay immediately for a week). There is also a business center with a full set of office equipment and a fax, plus a conference room where you can organize and hold not only business, but also a festive event.

How are tourists having fun? Leisure in the hotel area





There is enough entertainment at the hotel, which, in fact, is said by all former guests. There are four outdoor pools, as well as a large water park with 14 slides of different sizes and degrees of difficulty (for guests, admission to the park is free). In the evening or in cold weather, you can refresh yourself in the indoor pool - the water in it is constantly heated.

As for sports, there is a gym, group aerobics and dancing classes, basketball, mini-football and mini-golf courses. You can practice archery. And guests are invited to the wellness center, where they conduct a variety of cosmetic and wellness treatments. During the day, tourists are entertained by animators, and in the evenings you can dance to live music or enjoy exciting show programs. The hotel has its own travel agency, where travelers can help pick up an interesting tour of the area. Tunisia is a country with an interesting and ancient history, a lot of attractions and museums.

Vacation with a child: comfort and entertainment





The Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel complex is designed specifically for family vacations - ideal conditions are created here not only for adult tourists, but also for their children. Families are advised to rent bungalows or spacious apartments, and upon request, they always bring a crib.

As for entertainment, the kids have a great time in a special pool with small safe water slides. A lot of interesting attractions for children can be found in the water park. There is a mini-club for children, which deals with children aged 4 to 14 years. And next to it there is a spacious playground where the kids ride on a swing and play in sandboxes, and the older children play sports and participate in team games.

Reviews: what do the former guests say?

What do former guests say about the Aquasplash Thalassa Sousse 4 * hotel? The reviews are mostly positive. Here you can have a great fun holiday enjoying delicious food, a mild climate, a clean sea and, of course, modern comfort. The territory is large, but clean, as well as living rooms. The appliances in the rooms work, and the staff clean every day. By the way, most tourists here are Russian speakers. Communication problems do not arise - this also applies to hotel staff. Travelers recommend this hotel for beach, youth and family vacations.
