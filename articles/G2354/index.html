Pensions of Crimea "all inclusive" with pools. Rest in Crimea

Crimea has always attracted huge crowds of vacationers. The reason for this is the unique natural conditions of this peninsula. There is a sea for those who like to lie on the beach or swim. Those who prefer a vacation in the mountains will also be pleasantly surprised by the opportunities that the Crimean Mountains open for them.



The holiday season in Crimea lasts about 5 months and is characterized by hot, dry weather and extremely rare rainfall. The peninsula provides ample opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts: hiking, diving, mountaineering and many other attractions await in the Crimea. For those who are fond of history or just like to look at the sights, there is also a lot of interesting things: old castles, ruins of ancient cities, monasteries and temples - just a feast for the eager eyes of lovers of antiquity and architecture.

In addition to the natural landscapes and the beautiful heritage of antiquity, in the Crimea you can try local wines. Tasting rooms are located throughout the peninsula, allowing you to appreciate the world-famous varieties of Crimean wines. The boarding houses of Crimea will also be pleased: all-inclusive, with pools and tennis courts, bars and restaurants, and, of course, with unique views.

Luxurious hotels and pensions span the coast. Rest in Crimea on the all-inclusive system is gaining popularity. Most local hotels have their own beach or are located near the sea, they are also equipped with excellent attractions and entertainment facilities for adults and children. Below will be considered the best motels and resorts of Crimea.

Pension "Massandra". Yalta

In the eastern part of the city of Yalta there is the Massandra boarding house. Not so long ago, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out in it, which made it even more attractive for vacationers. The guesthouse is located in the picturesque Massandra Park, from its rooms offer wonderful views of the mountains or the sea. You can get from the boarding house to the beach in just a couple of minutes. If you need to visit the center of Yalta, then the road there will take no more than 15 minutes.

Hotel Nord, Partenit

In the very center of Partenit village, among the majestic mountains, washed by the gentle waves of the Black Sea, the Hotel Nord is located. "Blue Bay" is a good option for those who are interested in the boarding houses of Crimea. All inclusive, with pools and other amenities, clean air and well-kept beaches - what could be better? "Blue Bay" - one of the largest resorts in Koktebel. It is able to comfortably accommodate up to 700 vacationers. If you decide to have a good and inexpensive vacation, then the Crimean peninsula is an excellent choice. Here, beautiful seascapes harmoniously transform into harsh mountain ranges. Everyone who came here to relax will be able to enjoy the incredible natural landscapes without laying out such fabulous sums as in foreign resorts. History fans will also not leave Crimea indifferent - excursions to historical monuments, ancient ruins and medieval fortresses will leave an indelible mark on the soul of every researcher. Children will also be delighted with the rest, as each boarding house has playgrounds and other entertainments for young tourists.
