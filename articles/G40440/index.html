A great budget option to stay abroad at the Derin Hotel 3 on the Mediterranean

The small Derin Hotel 3 is located in the Turkish resort of Beldibi, at a distance of 15 kilometers from the city of Kemer and forty kilometers from the international airport of Antalya. This southern village is located on the Mediterranean coast at the foot of the Taurus Mountains.

In the evening, you can visit discos in the village. Shops are open during the day. Twice a week, a shopping market operates for tourists, where you can buy leather goods, clothes, jewelry, sweets, souvenirs and local fruits.

You can use public transport every ten minutes to explore Turkish attractions in neighboring cities.

Rooms : Derin Hotel 3 consists of 52 double or quadruple rooms. Each room has access to a small balcony and is equipped with a TV, air conditioning and telephone. The rooms have satellite communications and a local telephone line. From the windows of the rooms a picturesque panorama of the mountainous terrain of the resort opens. The room has a safe, a large mirror, a desk, a minibar, and the bathroom has a shower, toiletries and a hairdryer. The flooring in the apartments is carpet or ceramic. Every day, the room is cleaned according to the schedule and a change of terry towels. Twice a week, bed linen is provided.



Beach: Derin Hotel 3 has its own, consisting of small pebbles and gray coarse sand. It is located 500 meters from the hotel. The coastal seabed is rocky. Sun loungers, parasols and showers are available on the beach at an additional cost. From entertainments - water attractions. In addition to the beach, the hotel has two outdoor pools (for adults and children).


Meals: The hotel's restaurant Derin Hotel for vacationers provides three meals a day according to the buffet. The restaurant's menu includes a variety of dishes from vegetables, fish and meat, dairy products, mashed soups, fruits, drinks, salads, desserts, ice cream, juices. Near the pool there is a bar where you can taste alcoholic and hot drinks (wine, gin, beer, juices, coffee, tea).

Additional services: For the convenience and comfort of vacationers, the hotel provides some services for a fee. These include: medical care, the use of a safe, dry cleaning and laundry services, and an Internet cafe. There is a playground for children. In their free time from excursions and the beach, guests can play table tennis in an open area near the pool. In the evening, a disco is held in the open air; admission is free.



Reviews : About Derin Hotel 3 guest reviews are very diverse. This hotel is more suitable for not too demanding tourists. The low cost of a trip, a small territory, modest conditions and fairly uniform food relate the hotel to a budget option. Cleaning of the grounds, beach and rooms is desired. The positive side is the Russian-speaking staff, the clear sea, the surrounding mountain landscape and the Mediterranean natural area.
