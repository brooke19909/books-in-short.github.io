Crochet a bag for summer

What to do if in the spring you managed to buy a stunning blouse, light and delicate, but you can’t find a handbag for it? It seems to be a common shade, but there is nothing suitable for sale. Either the size is not the same, or the fittings do not match the style. The solution suggests itself: we crochet a bag! It is not as difficult as it seems at first glance.



What threads should be used

For the manufacture of bags, the most popular are cotton threads. We crochet a bag only if it was possible to choose the appropriate shade or the appropriate accessories were found. Otherwise, you risk getting a product that will not be worn with anything. Do not forget to choose the right size hook. It should fully match the thickness of the thread. If you plan to make an openwork bag, in this case you need to choose the material so that the inside does not contrast too much with the yarn. Decide if you need a zipper, buckle or other kinds of fasteners. It is possible that the bag will be with a valve, in the lower part of which a magnet is sewn. In this case, you must take care of its availability in advance. If you could not find anything similar to the desired option in the sewing accessories store, then you can cut off the fasteners from the old, old-fashioned bag.

Process description



On the street or in a cafe, in a shopping center or in the office, a crocheted bag always attracts attention. The schemes used in their manufacture are, incidentally, found not only on bags. More often , a blouse is simply knitted with one pattern , and then the elements from it are inserted in the process of knitting the bag. Can be made even simpler. Crochet a bag. A double crochet or a single crochet, depending on what you plan to get in the end. If you need a more rigid form - use a single crochet. And for a more feminine handbag, a column with one or even two crochets is perfect. The simplest one is a crocheted square or rectangular bag. You need to take a sheet of paper and draw its desired size. And then complete the pattern. That is, cut two identical squares (front and back), a side insert, and, if necessary, a valve. Then, using the selected method (with or without a crochet), we crochet a bag: we knit all the details and connect together. From a high-quality, color-appropriate fabric, we cut and sew the lining. We connect the base with the lining, sew the handles.



Jewelry

After you have tied the base of the bag, you need to make several motifs that resonate with the new blouse. You can use the yarn to which the base was knitted, or they can be made in a different color. The combination of shades can be harmonious or extravagant and unexpected. It all depends on what you are going to wear the future product with, and your mood. Not everyone knows in advance what the finished work should look like. Crocheted bags, photos of which you will find in this article, will help to get a general idea of ​​how such a thing can look! In addition, it can be decorated with beads, rhinestones, decorative elements or just silk ribbon. Everything is in our hands if we crochet a bag ourselves.
