Animals from Autumn Leaves

Crafts from natural material for kindergarten can be made voluminous from cones, chestnuts or acorns, etc. And you can create a picture on cardboard from dried autumn leaves. Walking in the park with children, think about what crafts can be done for the autumn exhibition in kindergarten or school. Consider the shape of the leaves with your child, imagine what they look like, what you can make of them. You can use both leaves from one tree, and from different. They differ in shape and color. The photograph shows several different animals and birds collected from leaves of linden, oak, acacia, poplar, and mulberry.

To create the image, maple seeds and thin branches of shrubs are also used. You can combine not only the shape of the leaves, but also the colors, because autumn gives a rich color gamut to the nature around us - from bright yellow to shades of red and brown. Before you start making crafts from natural materials for kindergarten, you need to put the leaves in a thick unnecessary book and put on top the load. You can do this in the evening and leave the blanks under pressure for the whole night. In the morning you can already start work. If there is still moisture on the leaves, you need to iron them through the newspaper. After that, the leaves are well smoothed and glued onto PVA glue to the cardboard sheet.

Crocus from the spoons

Crafts-flowers can be made from disposable plastic spoons. White, yellow or lilac products are used (after all, such flowers are in nature). You will also need fluffy velvet threads on a yellow and green wire. For each flower you will need three identical spoons.

They bind together in a bundle of transparent tape. The photo above shows how to arrange the spoons relative to each other. A yellow thread is pre-inserted into the center. Then the flower crafts is inserted into a plastic cup filled with crumpled colored paper. You can use sisal or foam balls.

Crafts from napkins

From white and colored napkins, you can make voluminous fluffy flowers, strengthen them on the stems and put the bouquet in a vase. The following materials will be needed: a pack of napkins, a marker in contrasting color, a strong thread or thin rope, wooden skewers for the stem, green corrugated paper, PVA glue.

Crafts from napkins are made as follows. For each flower, several pieces are taken with cut bends. A marker outlines all the edges around the perimeter of the workpieces.

Then the napkins are folded ‚Äúaccordion‚Äù and tied exactly in the center to a knot. Long edges are left for attachment to the shaft. You can cut a leaf and wrap the edges of the stick. The ends are attached to the PVA.

Shell Owl

What can be made crafts from shells? The most diverse. Bird houses

If you do not know what crafts can be made from plastic bottles, pay attention to the following option presented. From two-liter bottles, the necks are cut off in semicircles. Then the excess part of the bottle, about 5 cm, is removed. A window for a rounded bird is cut out in the middle of the house.

A hole is made in the cover with an awl and a thin rope tied to a knot is inserted. Thus, the birdhouse can be hung on a hook in a corner of nature or decorate a living corner at school with crafts. It remains to beautifully paint the craft so that it is bright.

Snowflakes for decorating a kindergarten group

What crafts can I make from plastic bottles to decorate a room? We can offer kindergarten teachers to color the bottoms of the bottles to make snowflakes. First, there is preparatory work to cut the bottoms with scissors. Then you need to melt the cut edges with a hot iron so that children do not cut when painting.

Bottles are better to choose a small volume, for example, 0.33 or 0.5 liters. In the lesson, blanks are distributed to the children, and they paint snowflakes in white on the model of the educator. Finished crafts can be attached to clips on curtains, hung on a Christmas tree or create a garland, decorating the room of a group room.

Octopus made of yarn

What thread crafts can be made, read on. You can play fun octopuses from yarn without fear that the toy will break. However, you will need a lot of threads to work. First, a thick layer of thread is wound around a pattern. Usually use a thick cover book. Winding takes place around the cover of a book. Then from above the threads are connected to a strong knot. From below the threads are cut. It turns out a long bunch.

Next, make the head of a marine inhabitant. To do this, crush a couple of napkins and roll a ball out of them. It is inserted inside, under the bundle, and the threads are straightened so that the ball is hidden completely. Under the napkins, the threads are tied to a knot. It remains to distribute the threads into equal bundles and weave from each ordinary braid. Eyes are made from buttons by sewing.
