Configuring satellite dishes without assistance

In our country, the installation and configuration of a satellite dish is often required, since this type of television is gaining momentum every year and is becoming more popular. Typically, specialized companies are involved in this business, but installation can be done with their own hands. The equipment is assembled according to the instructions without causing any special difficulties. Careful attitude requires an antenna mirror made of aluminum. After assembly, make sure that all fasteners are secure. Especially if you are setting up a satellite dish yourself. In no case should you neglect the installation of a satellite convector, because the reception quality will depend on it.



To prevent the tuning of satellite dishes into real torture, installation is recommended in easily accessible places. If you plan to place outside the window, then for the cable you can drill a hole in the corner of the window frame. When located on the roof, low-current risers of the house are more often used. You can also skip the cable along the facade of the building. The diameter of the holes should be slightly larger than the thickness of the wire, otherwise the outer sheath may be damaged. At the last stage, work is being done to seal the holes.



An interesting setting is the offset-focus satellite antennas with an oval shape. Such devices allow you to install multiple converters for receiving a television signal and receive several satellites at once. The fastening nuts should not be tightened to the end, because the antenna will have to be directed in different directions. First, a central satellite is searched and connected to the first input. Then, in the same way, the rest are found. In fact, the connection order of the converters does not really matter. It is only necessary that the actual connection matches the displayed satellites in the menu.



The classic tuning of satellite dishes is based on the position of the sun. Usually at 1 p.m. a "plate" is sent to it, while the time zone and time of the year should be taken into account. Then it turns very smoothly until a reliable signal reception is detected. If there is no signal, it is recommended to lower or raise the antenna. And in the same way, continue to search for a steady impulse. After a successful search, all fasteners are fully tightened. After some time, the receiver will automatically find all the TV channels.

Thus, we can say with confidence that the installation of satellite dishes does not constitute anything supernatural. With the right approach, you can save a significant amount by setting up the equipment yourself. However, a thorough study of the instructions is indispensable. Using the recommendations of specialists, you can watch your favorite TV shows and films in an hour. Installation work can be considered completed when all channels designed for a given antenna diameter appeared in the satellite tuner.
