Hotel "Little Raccoon" (Krasnogorsk): description, reviews

Krasnogorsk is a city in the Moscow Region that hosts hundreds of guests daily. Someone comes here to work, others to friends and relatives - in any case, the issue of residence is relevant. The choice of hotel should be approached quite carefully, taking into account not only the cost of living, but also the location, level of comfort and quality of service.





Hotel "Little Raccoon" (Krasnogorsk) - at least, an institution with an interesting original name. It is really intriguing, I want to know what lies beneath it, what potential guests can count on. This is what we will talk about in the framework of this section.

general description

If you are looking for an inexpensive option with good service, the hotel "Little Raccoon" in Moscow and the region is waiting for you. This is a chain of hotels located not only in Krasnogorsk, but also in Moscow in the vicinity of the Mitino metro station. There are two hotels in Krasnogorsk: on Ilyinsky and Volokolamsk Highways.

Rooms

Hotel "Little Raccoon" (Krasnogorsk) offers guests rooms of various categories:

  • Budget rooms with one double bed or two single beds. All rooms are equipped with modern furniture, a TV, a safe and a bathroom with shower.
  • Standard - rooms with an elegant interior, bright and spacious. Equipped with a double or two single beds, a table with chairs, bedside tables, a TV and a safe. There is a hotel bathroom with a shower and a set of necessary bath accessories.




  • Junior Suite - Spacious rooms with designer finishes and vibrant color accents. The furniture in the room has wide beds, a table, chairs, comfortable chairs. Technical facilities include satellite TV, a mini-safe. There is a jacuzzi in the bathroom.
  • Luxury - chic rooms, the interior of which was worked by first-class specialists. The most spacious rooms of the hotel with exquisite furniture, equipped with everything necessary for a luxurious vacation.




  • Two-room suite - a two-room suite, represented by a living room and a bedroom. All of them are made in a modern style, impressive in scope and luxury. In the room of this category, in addition to the declared furniture and plumbing, a minibar with soft drinks appears.

Prices

Of course, each potential guest is interested in the pricing policy of the institution "Little Raccoon" (hotel, Krasnogorsk). Prices primarily depend on the selected room. So, a day of stay in a room of the category "Budget" will cost 4,000 rubles, "Standard" - 4,500 rubles, "Junior Suite" - 5,000 rubles, "Suite" - 6,000 rubles and, finally, "Two-room suite" - 8500 rubles. It is possible to provide an extra bed in each room. Surcharge will be 50% of the room price. Hotel "Little Raccoon" (Krasnogorsk) allows hourly rental rooms. The first two hours at the hotel start at 2,000 rubles, followed by a surcharge of 300 rubles for each hour.

Service

Reviews of the hotel "Little Raccoon" (Krasnogorsk) confirm the availability of good service. All guests can count on a covered parking, which can be used for free. For an additional fee, the hotel staff will tidy up your things: wash and iron them. If necessary, at the reception you can order a taxi to the airport, railway station and any necessary place.





Attentive and polite staff tries to create an atmosphere of home comfort in the hotel, and is ready to help in solving any problem.

Nutrition

The only drawback of the hotel is the lack of a restaurant. Among the additional services - only breakfast. But if you believe the reviews of vacationers, this is a full meal. In the room at the appointed time they will bring porridge, cheesecakes, pancakes with various fillings, aromatic coffee. You can have lunch and dinner at local cafes located within walking distance of the hotel.

To summarize

Hotel "Little Raccoon" (Krasnogorsk) is a good option for city guests. It offers comfortable, clean and spacious rooms in the selected category.
