Ski resort Malakhovo, Tula region - affordable weekend breaks

Today you can ride on the ski slopes in the central strip of Russia. Active winter sports are becoming more and more popular and affordable, and everyone can try to go skiing or snowboarding without leaving far from home. The ski resort of Malakhovo in the Tula region is distinguished by its favorable geographical position and developed infrastructure. If you wish, you can come here to ride for one day from Moscow or stay for the whole weekend.

Ski slopes





Malakhovo is an ideal place to relax with the whole family. Among the resort's trails there are professional ones for amateurs, beginners and children. You can go skiing, snowboarding and tubing at the ski resort. Four slopes are at guests' disposal, divided into tracks of varying difficulty. All of them have lifts - these are special children's elevators and telescopic yokes. The ski resort of Malakhovo, Tula region, awaits guests in any weather. Even if the winter turned out to be insufficiently snowy, it will be possible to ride here, since the uniform coverage of the slopes is ensured by snow cannons. The resort also boasts modern lighting, thanks to which you can ride daily until 23.00. In addition to all this, Malakhovo has an ice skating rink.

The infrastructure of the ski village

The administration of the Malakhov resort is interested in promoting winter sports. All interested guests can enroll in a skiing or snowboarding school or hire a personal instructor. Training is also offered for children and juniors. Not everyone is ready to immediately buy expensive equipment to conquer the ski slopes. This does not matter, because the ski resort of Malakhovo, Tula region offers to take everything you need for skiing at a rental point.
