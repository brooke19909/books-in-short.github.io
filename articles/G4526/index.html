Performance and hardware platform: part 1

So what are the specifications of the Honor 8 Pro? To do this, look "under the hood" of the device. Under it, we see that the basis for the production of Huawei HiSilicon Kirin is used as the central processor - it is an eight-core processor (64 bits, clock speed 2.4 gigahertz). RAM is two-channel, energy-efficient in the amount of 6 gigabytes. The graphics processor is responsible for the Mali G71 coprocessor, which can handle almost everything, including OpenGL and DirectX. It is clear that with such a filling for a smartphone, nothing is impossible. Even demanding games like World of Tanks Blitz, Asphalt 8, and Modern Combat deliver stably 60 frames per second. Is it worth saying that the device works quickly and smoothly, and applications of any simpler type "fly" at all? Such performance is a branded feature of Honor smartphones. They have always been very powerful.

Smartphone display

Let's move on to the Honor 8 Pro screen. Characteristics and an overview of the main parameters quite clearly hint to us that just a smart panel is installed here. Judge for yourself. Screen Type - LTPS LCD. This is even better than the notorious IPS. The display resolution is 1440 by 2560 pixels. That is, this is not even Full HD, but all 4K. The screen has realistic color reproduction, chic viewing angles and a decent pixel density per inch. By the way, the screen size is 5.7 inches, and this, by the way, is almost a gigantic size. But it is not particularly felt when using a smartphone. It is worth noting that the screen has a decent anti-reflective coating and excellent brightness. This allows you to easily use the device outdoors on a sunny day. Fingers glide well on the touchscreen, there are almost no prints. This is a merit of a high-quality grease-repellent coating. It is also possible to work with gloves. The Honor 8 Pro screen is definitely one of the best in the segment.

Battery life

Technical characteristics of the Huawei Honor 8 Pro 64GB (as, in principle, versions with other amounts of memory) in the field of the battery are truly amazing. Video playback at maximum screen brightness can last more than 10 hours. But there is no need to talk about such trifles as the reading mode or listening to music. The gadget in these modes will easily work for a day. This is the most autonomous flagship of all. This device can easily work in mixed mode.
