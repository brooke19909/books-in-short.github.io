Novospassky bridge. Berth

Historically, Novospassky Bridge is the oldest of Moscow bridges. By age, only Borodinsky can equal him. At the moment, attributing the Novospassky bridge to the sights of the capital can be difficult. For tourists and photographers, it can be attractive in that it can capture beautiful views of the city, which will be inaccessible from other points.

History

Initially, the bridge was wooden and served to connect the banks of the Moscow River. Novospassky Bridge was used to move large flows of people, horse-drawn carts, which were sent to the nearby Novospassky Monastery. It was his name that passed to the bridge. By the way, the monastery is still operating today, and a very beautiful view opens from the bridge.

The bridge was rebuilt many times. The stone version was erected in 1911. In 1937 it underwent reconstruction, it can be said that it was rebuilt. From the former there were only supports. The bridge was raised to increase shipping dimensions. The length of the bridge after the reconstruction became half a kilometer, the width is 21 meters. The wood flooring was replaced with asphalt. The bridge is intended for vehicles, and for pedestrians only the section passing directly through the Moscow River is intended.

Since then, serious changes and changes in the architecture of the Novospassky bridge did not occur until 2000, when its design was improved.

Berth

On the embankment, not far from the Novospassky bridge, there is a pier, from where pleasure boats and river buses depart .

The pier "Novospassky bridge" is a wooden flooring. There are no benches or canopies from the sun and rain for passengers waiting for their river ship. Caring shipowners put some plastic chairs. There is a ticket office of the Capital Shipping Company, where you can purchase tickets and find out the schedule. Tickets can also be bought directly on the ship during boarding.



Mooring vessels do not stay here for long, since the berth is paid. Twenty-minute parking costs shipowners more than eight thousand rubles. So the stop, as a rule, is made only in order to drop off passengers and put in new ones.

On weekdays, ships leave after about 40 minutes, on weekends - after 20.

Often, on the way to the pier, guests are met by representatives of shipping companies who specify the purpose of your arrival at the place, agitate for a river walk and instruct where and when the boat will be boarded.





The cost of travel for an adult starts at 600 rubles. There is a ticket for one day on which you can ride the boat all day. The cost of such a ticket last summer (2016) was 1000 rubles. Tickets can be purchased at a stock - 50% cheaper. Such offers are often posted on travel Internet portals.

sights

Not far from the Novospassky bridge are the most beautiful places. One of them is the Novospassky Monastery - a place that you can admire both from the bridge and from the side of the river, and even better - wander through its territory, go to temples. Here, despite the proximity of the center of the capital, calm and peace reign.

An interesting view opens up on the Krutitsky Patriarchal Compound, on the territory of which the Cathedral of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary is located. Its domes are visible from behind the trees. The house of music, green areas spread along the embankments - all this beauty can be seen from the side of a pleasure boat or from the Novospassky bridge.



By the way, a plate with the words "New Spassky" through a hyphen can be considered an interesting attraction. In other sources, this does not occur.

From here you can see one of the Stalinist skyscrapers.

How to get there

You can get to the Novospassky Bridge pier by metro, reaching the Proletarskaya station. From here, 10-15 minutes walk (700 meters) - and you are at your destination. You need to exit the subway in the direction of 3rd Krutitsky Lane, to Krutitsky Val, Novospassky Monastery. The landmarks can serve as monastic domes, and then the bridge itself will become visible. The pier is located on the right side, opposite the monastery. From the pier to the subway is even easier to reach. You will surely be led out by a stream of people moving along the street. It must be remembered that the monastery should always remain on the right hand when moving to the bridge. Accordingly, in the opposite direction - on the left. You can go one stop on the 35th or 38th tram.



Address

The mooring "Novospassky bridge" is located on the Krasnokholmskaya embankment, at its intersection with Sarinsky passage. If you go by car, you can set the address on the navigator: Sarinsky passage, 9. There is a parking lot.
