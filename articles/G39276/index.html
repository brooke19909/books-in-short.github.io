On inventions cunning



Scrapbooking paper can also be made from improvised means.

1) First you need ordinary paper for drawing, otherwise - watercolor paper, as well as a set of acrylic paints or any other. Try to create a creative background on a blank sheet of paper. Spray paint, leave your fingerprints, make stamps with a sponge, in general, show imagination. A good effect happens when you use masks, stencils, texture paste and even doodling (drawing curls with a pen). Such a paper will have several advantages - it is individual, creative and unique. Although not for all styles and work, this is suitable. For example, not every wedding couple will agree to such a non-standard album.

2) We cook paper for scrapbooking. The tricky name hides a simple undertaking. If you need unusual paper for scrapbooking, and even with a delicious smell, then you can just soak sheets of plain paper in coffee, strong tea, add vanilla and cinnamon to taste. Such sheets will decorate winter cards, handmade notebooks, etc.

A few words about scrapbooking paper



In fact, you can create a masterpiece from absolutely nothing. You can take a regular sheet of cardboard left over from any packaging, cover it with white acrylic primer to hide the inscriptions. Now add some beautiful labels from clothes, a couple of ribbons, a twig with berries, coriander seeds, vanilla pod, a congratulatory inscription. Sprinkle all this with artificial snow, and you get a very beautiful composition. In general, in scrapbooking everything is in use - from a small button to packaging from under the TV. Show your imagination and save money.
