Hotel "Union" in Prague: description, service, reviews

Prague is the capital of the Czech Republic, the majestic city of a hundred towers and beautiful parks. This city, as many tourists say, is amazingly beautiful. It impresses with the luxury of architectural buildings and the coziness of cobbled streets. The capital of the Czech Republic is one of the five most popular tourist cities in Europe. If you decide to spend your vacation in the picturesque city of Prague or come here on a business trip, you should choose a hotel for accommodation.

Tourists, considering possible options, pay attention to the location of the hotel, its pricing policy and conditions in it. We suggest you pay attention to the Hotel Union. This magnificent old building is located near the historical center of the city. Everyone who has been here only shares their positive emotions. They say that the hotel is really worthy of attention. Is it so? Let's discuss this.

General information about the Hotel Union

The wonderful and comfortable Union Hotel, located in Prague, offers its guests to live within the walls of a building built at the beginning of the 19th century (1906). Union Hotel was previously named Acc-Nifos Union Hotel. In 1958 it passed into the possession of the Communists, and in 1991 it began to be controlled by the grandson of the founder and first owner. He still continues the family business and cares about the reputation of his brainchild. That is why it is so comfortable and cozy here. The hotel was renovated in 2006, although some details remained untouched by time. This adds her special charm and greatness. In the article you can see photos that are indisputable proof of that.

Hotel Union (Prague) offers upscale service. This fact is confirmed by guests in their numerous reviews. At their service - 57 elegant rooms, a chic restaurant and a cozy bar. The hotel itself is decorated in modern style, which gives it a special charm and grandeur.

On the well-kept territory of the Hotel Union in Prague, there is an Internet cafe with free Wi-Fi. For guests arriving by car, the hotel offers to leave transport in a paid secure parking.

It is also worth noting that smoking is prohibited throughout the hotel. For everyone who has a pet, the good news is: pets are not prohibited.

Location

The Hotel Union (Prague), the description of which is brought to your attention in the article, has a rather advantageous location - in the historical center of the city - next to Vysehrad Castle, well-known to all tourists, not far from the Congress Center. The historical center of Prague can be reached in 10 minutes by trams Nos. 18 and 24. As many tourists note, when staying at the Union Hotel, everyone will have the opportunity not only to have a pleasant time in its walls, but also to go on an excursion anywhere in the city or go shopping.

Hotel Union rooms

The hotel offers its guests 57 luxury apartments for every taste and budget. Hotel Union types of rooms offers its guests the following:

  • Single
  • Double rooms.
  • Triple rooms.
  • Suite
  • Family penthouse.

Many tourists in their many positive reviews say that, having settled in each of them, you can enjoy modern amenities, a stylish luxurious interior and a romantic atmosphere.

Description of categories of apartments

Consider each of the categories of rooms offered by the Union Hotel (Prague) in more detail.

  • Single Room. A cozy room that offers a standard set of furniture and amenities. There is a bed, a nightstand, a wardrobe for clothes, a coffee table, a TV. The room has a bathroom with all necessary accessories.
  • Double Room. The room is equipped with a double bed or two single beds. At the request of guests, the hotel can provide an extra bed. The room also has a bathroom with essential amenities.
  • Triple room. These apartments have three single beds or one double and one single. An extra bed for a child can be arranged upon request.
  • Suite The luxurious room has a bedroom and a living room with a cozy soft sofa. In such apartments it is possible to accommodate up to 4 adult tourists.
  • Family penthouse. A two-bedroom room is an ideal choice for leisure travelers who come with their families. At the request of tourists, the hotel provides extra beds for babies.

Everything sparkles with cleanliness, bed linen and towels are changed on time. The staff here is multilingual, which eliminates the possibility of a language barrier between the guest and the staff. Everyone will be able to find a way to explain. The administration takes care of the comfortable stay of each guest. Tourists note that at the Hotel Union the staff is ready to come to the rescue at any time of the day and resolve issues arising as soon as possible.

Restaurants, bars

As mentioned above, there is a restaurant and a cozy bar at the Union Hotel. The first serves both local and international cuisine. As many visitors note, everything is amazingly tasty and fresh. Many guests who come to rest with children note that there is no separate children's menu, but from the dishes offered by the restaurant, you can choose what the child will definitely like.

The bar is a great place to meet friends. Here you can socialize and enjoy a local beer. Here, as guests note, the variety of this drink is simply huge. At times, it is difficult to choose one. Moreover, beer offers a wide variety of snacks.

Hotel Services

As noted earlier, guests arriving at the hotel with their car can leave it in a paid parking lot. Tourists are joyfully welcomed by the reception staff. If it is not possible to check in immediately, you can leave things and documents in the storage at the reception, and go for lunch or drink coffee in a local restaurant. As tourists note, there is no need to worry about the safety of things.

The Union Hotel (Prague) has a currency exchange, laundry and dry cleaning services. The hotel organizes excursions to the sights of the city. If you wish, you can rent a car or bicycle on site and go on a tour yourself. For festive events there is the opportunity to rent a limousine.

After fascinating walks, many have a desire to relax, to relax. To this end, it is worth visiting the hotel's spa with modern equipment. It offers massage services, beauty treatments. There is also the opportunity to visit the sauna and solarium.

For those who want to relax actively in the territory of the Hotel Union 4 * there is a golf course. The hotel has billiards tables directly.

Union Hotel (Prague): traveler reviews

Having analyzed all of the above, we can draw some conclusions. The Hotel Union is truly worthy of the attention of tourists visiting Prague. Among the undoubted advantages it is worth highlighting, first of all, its good location. Having settled here, guests will not encounter difficulties such as how to get to one or another attraction of the city. Tourists also highlight the special coziness and comfort prevailing in the hotel. According to guests who visited the Union 4 * hotel, you feel at home here. There is no discomfort or desire to leave here faster. Another significant advantage of the hotel is the fact that it has the opportunity to accommodate guests who have arrived with the whole family. If the need arises, staff will provide an extra bed for the child. Great is the ability to independently control the temperature in the room, since each has air conditioning. For people who want to be always in touch, an undoubted advantage is the ability to connect to the Internet for free.

Some tourists were confused by the fact that the in-room bar and safe were paid. But this is understandable, since the Hotel Union is a four-star hotel, and it does not work on the all-inclusive concept. However, in the mornings, guests have the opportunity to have a delicious breakfast, as the bar serves a buffet. The food in the hotel restaurant also earned numerous praise. Chefs make every effort to prepare dishes that will appeal to even the most sophisticated gourmets. True, as mentioned above, there is no separate baby food, but the menu has many dishes that are allowed for use by kids.

Many guests like the fact that the Hotel Union offers a wake-up service. This is very convenient for those who have scheduled business meetings or bought tickets for an excursion.

In general, many guests say that the hotel is optimal in the ratio of price and quality. Tourists who have been here recommend the Union to their friends and acquaintances.
