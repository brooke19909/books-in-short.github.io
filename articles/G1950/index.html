ATM Crimes in Perm

It is recommended to visit the offices of a financial organization during the day. In Perm, night-time crime related to ATMs became more frequent. Attackers came up with a new method, how to extract money from the device, using gas. The first time unknown persons tested this method on April 18, 2016. Criminals blew up a Sberbank ATM in Perm, in the village of Ferma, on Nekrasov Street. The incident occurred at about 4-00. The first attempt was not too successful for the "experimenters": banknotes burned out. However, the attackers continued their criminal activities. As if deciding to recoup for failure, they blew up an ATM in the village of Komsomolsky in the Kungursky District already on the morning of April 19, 2016. This time, the criminals managed to make about 1.5 million rubles.

On the night of June 28, 2016, three ATMs in the branch were destroyed by explosions at the Cosmonauts Highway 112. In total, more than 4 million rubles were stolen. The compartment roof was badly damaged.

A week later, also at night, an unknown man made an attempt to destroy the Sberbank ATM on the right bank of the Kama at the address: st. Lazvinskaya, 60. A bottle of detonating substance (presumably with ammonium nitrate) exploded ahead of time, the criminal, scared, fled. The ATM was not injured.

The most recent crime was committed on September 5, 2016. Its details have not yet been disclosed in the interests of the investigation.
