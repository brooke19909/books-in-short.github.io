Cape Meganom (Crimea) is not only the mesmerizing magnificence of the natural landscape and the purest sea bays with wild beaches, but also a very mysterious place with quite powerful energy.

Located between the Black Sea resort of Sudak and the small poetry-filled village of Koktebel, the domed peninsula (358 m above sea level) attracts with its mystery and unique charm.

Modern realities

The mysterious stories do not end there, but get a modern continuation that helps attract ufologists, parapsychologists and just lovers of everything unknown on Meganom. Cape quite often becomes a place where you can watch UFOs. In addition, yellowish rings of unknown origin with a width of about 50 cm regularly appear on the surface of Meganom.

Four Cape Meganoma

Meganom - a cape consisting of four separate massifs that separate deep gorges and valleys from each other. Climatic features

Intensively scorching sun, almost complete absence of precipitation and very strong, sometimes almost hurricane winds are the three most significant factors affecting the state of the ecological and natural environment on Meganom.

These places are considered the warmest in the Crimea, and the beneficial mitigating effect is due to the close proximity of the Black Sea. Its life-giving breath makes it easier to stay in a semi-desert climate among weathered cliffs, slopes and steep banks, devoid of lush vegetation.

Sea beauties and rarities

Really exciting boat trips by sea with the possibility of diving into the amazing depths of the Engraving bays have recently attracted an increasing number of diving enthusiasts at Cape Meganom. Reviews of the stunning beauty of the seabed, consisting of rock fragments and huge blocks of stone, are fascinating.

Many fans of historical rarities are interested in the cemetery of anchors. Their diversity can not be compared with any museum collection. One of the anchors is striking in its size, weighing at least two tons by the most rough estimates. It causes a lot of hypotheses about its origin and use. But the mystery of this anchor still remains unsolved for humanity.

Extreme in the elevator tower

A special type of entertainment for fans of underwater extreme sports is the elevator shaft (or tower), which is hidden in Cape Meganom in its marine surroundings. Photos of the underwater world, as well as the most stunning landscapes with cliffs cutting through the skies and natural piles surrounded by clouds and sunlight breaking through them, attract the attention of true artists. An elevator tower is a vertically-vertical hole in a stone rock, the entrance to which is located at a 7-meter depth. In order to get to the water surface, you must swim down about 8-10 meters and get out through one of the three rocky holes.
