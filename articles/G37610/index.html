Rivne (Ukraine): a beautiful ancient city

When it comes to the cities of Ancient Russia, for some reason few people recall Rivne (Ukraine). And it was founded in 1283 according to the official version. According to unofficial information, local historians pushed this date back 250 years, and maybe even further. What city is it, the former capital of the country occupied by the Germans?



A bit of history

The regional center belongs to the historical Volyn region and is located in the northwestern part of the country. Rivne (Ukraine) celebrates the day of the city on the last August Sunday.

1283 - the date of the first mention of the name of the settlement, which was part of the Principality of Galicia-Volyn. But during excavations in the central part of Rivne, archaeologists discovered objects dating from the tenth century. These are the remains of buildings, pottery, weapons. It remains to find only the legendary arch, which flaunts on the arms of the city.

origin of name

So, we found out that the city of Rivne appeared on the map of Ukraine about a thousand years ago. What does its name mean? The first version says that it means the area on which the city is located - a lowland plain. In documents of the early years occasionally, but there are other toponyms - Rivne, Rivne. But there is another hypothesis, which states that in the past the city had powerful defensive ditches filled with water. In support of this version, researchers cite the existence of a suburb called Zarovoye.



The fate of the city

The city of Rivne (Ukraine) is beautiful and ancient. He always had a favorable geographical position. That is why in the course of history he repeatedly passed from hand to hand. After the Mongol invasion and the collapse of the Galicia-Volyn principality, the city moved to the Grand Duchy of Lithuania. In 1793, he became part of the Russian Empire, and in 1941 the Germans chose it as their center in Ukraine. After the independence of Ukraine, Rivne became a regional center. The modern city is beautiful, green, cozy and interesting in terms of attractions that are not only on the surface of the earth, but also under it.

Catacombs

Rivne dungeons (Ukraine) is another attraction of the city, which, however, is very little studied. Recently discovered moves are striking in their grandeur and complex system.
