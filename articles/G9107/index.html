Resorts in Bulgaria: where is it better to go on vacation? Travel Tips

Resorts in Bulgaria are well known to Russians. In recent decades, this sunny country has attracted more and more tourists. Most importantly, at any time of the year you can visit the resorts of Bulgaria. Where is better to go? We will try to answer this question in this article. We offer you a small overview of the most popular and visited places that offer a comfortable stay.



Winter holidays in Bulgaria

In winter, it is better to spend a vacation in the mountains of Bulgaria. The magnificent nature and mild climate will allow you to relax. Consider some of the most popular sites, taking into account the reviews and recommendations of experienced travelers.

Bansko - ski resort

Bansko is a city located in the south-west of the country, at the foot of North Pirin. 145 kilometers separate it from Plovdiv and 150 km from Sofia. Here is the famous ski resort. The ski season begins in December and lasts until April.

Near the town is the Pirin National Park, along which the Glazne River flows. The Pirin Mountains are a great place for skiing, and here both amateurs and professionals feel comfortable. Bansko is a ski resort with the highest point of 2560 m and its lowest point of 1000 m. This popular resort is known for its main piste, which is 75 km long. A third of it is equipped for beginner athletes, it is served by 170 machines that produce artificial snow.



In Bansko there are cable cars, an elevator and other necessary attributes of a good ski resort. On the territory of this complex quite often there are international competitions in alpine sports. Tourists who visited this resort can combine outdoor activities with sightseeing. Near the city there are ancient cultural monuments - the church of St. Elijah and St. George, and the church of the Holy Trinity, located in Bansko, was consecrated back in 1860. Next to it is a permanent exhibition of icons.

It should be noted that today tourists from all over the world come to the resorts of Bulgaria. Hotels here are designed for guests with any income. Take, for example, the Belmont 3 * hotel, located in the center of Bansko. It was built in 2005 at the foot of the Pirin Mountains, 400 meters from the lifts that lead to a height of 2600 meters. This homely hotel guarantees excellent service, excellent conditions for recreation for lovers of skiing.



Borovets

This wonderful place is located at an altitude of 1390 meters above sea level, near Mount Musala, the highest in the Balkans (2925 m). Borovets resort (Bulgaria) got its name because of the dense boron surrounding it from all sides. Thanks to this, the area is famous for its unusually clean air.

The resort is 70 km from the capital. The highest point is 2560 meters. Borovets is the most prestigious and oldest ski resort in the country, having excellent ski slopes of varying difficulty, modern systems for the production of artificial snow. Customers note the well-organized work of the lifts and developed infrastructure.



This resort dates back to 1896. At first, the area was intended for recreation and hunting of the kings of Bulgaria. Now it is a modern ski resort with a wonderful territory. Here you can engage in various winter sports - snowboarding and skiing, skating and sledding, etc. With regard to the location of guests, Borovets offers its visitors apartments, hotels, villas of different levels.

sunny Beach

Popular are many resorts in Bulgaria. Where is it better to go so that the rest becomes pleasant and memorable? Experienced travelers recommend going to Sunny Beach. This is the largest resort in sunny Bulgaria, which is known far beyond its borders. It is located between Varna and Burgas.

Every year, guests from all over the world come here on vacation. The resort is located in a picturesque area, has proven itself to be clean and well-kept beaches and a calm sea. Sunny Beach gained its popularity with our compatriots back in Soviet times. Then the rest here was considered very prestigious.



The beaches of this resort stretch along the coast for sixteen kilometers. The bay is calm, the bottom is shallow, which is especially appreciated by families with children. This is a resort where no one will be bored - neither adults nor children. For kids, there are many magnificent playgrounds with carousels, slides, pools. There are even children's discos here, talented animators work who organize outdoor games and interesting events.

Albena

Experienced tourists know how diverse the resorts in Bulgaria are. Where is better to go with children? We advise you to go to a relatively quiet place, which is located 30 km from Varna. Albena is considered the third largest seaside resort in the country.

It is an ideal place to relax with children. A year ago, they built a magnificent water park with dizzying slides, pools for adults and very young vacationers. Ten kilometers from Albena is the Botanical Garden of Balchik. This is a very beautiful place where you can see unique plants from around the world. In addition, there is also a palace, which at the beginning of the 20th century was the residence of Queen Mary.
