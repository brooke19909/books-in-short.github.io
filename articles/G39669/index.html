Hotel "Aquamarine", Moscow: reviews, address

Hotel "Aquamarine" is a modern institution that has received the status of a design hotel. Here you can enjoy a comfortable stay surrounded by elegant design.



Brief description of the hotel

Hotel "Aquamarine" is an excellent accommodation option for those who value comfort, aesthetics and high standards of service. Here you can take advantage of services such as a sauna (dry or wet), free Wi-Fi, a wellness center, private parking area, as well as a gym and much more. An airport shuttle is also provided.

Beautifully decorated rooms are one of the main advantages of such an institution as the Aquamarine Hotel. They are characterized not only by a beautiful interior, but also by a full range of modern amenities that will ensure a comfortable stay for guests. In addition, a beautiful view from the window will be a nice bonus.





If you are interested in Mediterranean cuisine, then by all means visit the Topaz restaurant. There is also a traditional Russian menu. Another hotel "Aquamarine" has a bar "Rubin", where there is always a large assortment of drinks. The windows overlook the green garden, which creates an atmosphere of peace.

Hotel Aquamarine (Moscow): address

This institution is located in the central part of the capital. Walking to such famous sights as the Kremlin and St. Basil's Cathedral takes no more than 20 minutes. Also very close is the Novokuznetskaya metro station. The nearest airport is 40 kilometers away from a place like the Aquamarine Hotel. Address - Ozerkovskaya embankment, 26.

Hotel "Aquamarine" (Moscow): reviews, advantages

Guests of this hotel note a lot of positive moments that characterize accommodation and rest:

  • a fairly reasonable ratio of price and quality (but prices could be lower);
  • very soft and comfortable beds;
  • very beautiful decoration of rooms and the hotel as a whole - you can consider the whole day;
  • very convenient location in the city center, near the metro station, as well as the main attractions;
  • Responsive and polite staff that promptly responds to all requests of guests;
  • tasty and hearty breakfast;
  • very quiet and relaxing atmosphere in the room;
  • good hygiene kits that contain not only shampoos and shower gels, but also toothbrushes;
  • the gym is open around the clock, which is very convenient if you are on a day trip or are busy with business matters;
  • The room rate includes a 24-hour access to the lounge, where you can have a free meal and coffee.

General impression

Hotel "Aquamarine" is a fairly popular accommodation option in Moscow. Despite the high price, this place is chosen by many tourists who want to relax in an atmosphere of luxury and comfort. This is a design hotel that boasts a beautiful design. In addition, it is worth noting a convenient location in the central part of the city. This is a quiet business district, from which you can easily reach the main cultural attractions.

As for the shortcomings, these are overpriced (both for accommodation and food in the restaurant). In addition, it is worth noting the absence of any signs by which to find a hotel. In general, this is one of the most worthy options in the capital, given the price-quality ratio of the services provided.
