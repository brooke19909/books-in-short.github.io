Hotel "Malachite", Chelyabinsk: reviews, photos, address

Congress Hotel "Malachite" is the largest in Chelyabinsk. He is ready to offer guests of the city convenient accommodation in one of his comfortable rooms, which have a different degree of comfort. Each tourist will find here the most suitable option for him with the best value for money.

general information



The Malakhit Hotel (Chelyabinsk) received its first visitors in 1979. In 2008, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out and new conference rooms with the most modern equipment were built. Therefore, since 2010, the hotel received a new status and became known as the Congress Hotel.

2012 opened a new milestone in the history of Malachite: the unique Emerald Hall was opened here - the first multi-profile banquet venue in the city, which can simultaneously accommodate up to a thousand guests. Here you can hold events of various levels. In addition, in the same year, restaurants, a sauna club and a night club were opened.

"Malachite" (Chelyabinsk) - a hotel that receives the most honored guests of the city. Here, at different times, Princess Olga Romanova, Pugacheva, other artists of show business and other celebrities stayed. To this day, the hotel is a favorite place where politicians, famous cultural and art workers, pop stars and others prefer to stop during business trips or tours.

"Malachite" (Chelyabinsk) is a hotel where participants of the World Judo and Taekwondo Championships resided. It is located in the city center, within walking distance of many attractions, government agencies, exhibition and shopping facilities, such as the Megapolis Center and the Concert Hall. Prokofiev. In terms of its territorial location and scale, no other hotel in Chelyabinsk can compete with this hotel.

Four kilometers from the Malachite is the city railway station, and the Balandino International Airport is a half hour drive away.

rules

As in many other hotels, the Malachite Hotel has a time for check-in and check-out: two in the afternoon and noon, respectively. If customers arrived earlier or stayed at the hotel for several hours more than they should, they do not need to pay the full amount. The administration accepts the cost for both a quarter and half a day.

Bills can be paid in cash or by credit card. If customers have questions about payment, you need to contact the hotel reception.



Smoking is prohibited on the territory of this congress hotel, although there are specially designated places for those who cannot give up this bad habit. On all floors, as well as in corridors and public places of the hotel, fire detectors with fire extinguishers are installed, there is also a fire extinguishing system.
