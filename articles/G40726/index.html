KNOX - what is it? How to remove or disable KNOX on Samsung?

The Samsung brand is popular in the Russian Federation. Almost all household appliances and, of course, mobile phones are in high demand. Owners of smartphones and tablets of this brand are faced with an application such as KNOX. However, only a few know what it is intended for and how it works.

Most often they talk about him in forums dedicated to firmware on Samsung devices. KNOX installs on top of the Android platform, performing protective functions. Many buyers complain that it is not possible to obtain detailed information about this application at points of sale, and official ones. Salespeople are reluctant to talk about this topic.

Why? This question is quite interesting. And in order to answer it, you will need to delve into the intricacies of software.



KNOX - what is it?

Skilled programmers claim that all smartphone owners are not protected from hacker attacks. Their personal data is at risk. This problem becomes especially acute for users of the corporate segment. How to protect yourself? Samsung last year developed a special application, which is essentially an add-on to the operating system. Its main task is to maximize the protection of personal data stored on mobile devices. The KNOX system itself consists of several extensions developed on the Google platform. It is with their help that the user creates a protected environment. An important advantage of the application is the ability to use an isolated zone, secure boot and a special encryption method.

Resetting KNOX, i.e. resetting the counter, is not possible on all Samsung smartphones. Therefore, it is recommended to be extremely careful with the firmware, since the application may simply not recognize it as a license, and this will lead to a complete failure of the device.

Using this application, the owner is confident that the device is completely protected from spyware, network hacking, copying information by third parties.



Principle of operation

KNOX (what kind of application is described above) is an independent component of the Android operating system. Compatible with devices starting with Galaxy S3. It is not officially installed on earlier devices, but theoretically this is possible.

How does this application work? Consider the example of Skype. The user needs to log in to the program in order to access the functions. This is done using a previously registered username and password. However, if KNOX is installed on the mobile device, a second account (additional) is created. It is he who is in the protected zone. To enter it, you will need to enter a password that is different from the previous one, or use a fingerprint scanner. The account at KNOX is independent. If you delete Skype on the device, it will remain in the protected application.



Application launch

When you turn on your tablet or smartphone, Samsung KNOX starts automatically. At this point, the system starts checking the kernel for integrity. After its completion, the application performs a secure download. If there were no unauthorized interventions, the device will start in normal mode.

It is important to understand that KNOX is designed to block any actions that are not authorized by the owner.

Reviews

KNOX, reviews of which are ambiguous, has both disadvantages and advantages. The advantages of consumers include a high level of protection, user-friendly interface, ease of use, locking the device at a distance (for example, in case of loss) and remote search.

However, not everything is so rosy. Many owners of tablets and mobile phones complain about frequent application crashes. Constantly appearing on the screen messages that inform about an unlicensed program or about downloading from an unauthorized source. Disabling alerts is hard enough. In order to help the owners of such devices, the article describes some ways to solve this problem.
