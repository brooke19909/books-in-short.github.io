Knitted backpack

Original crochet handbags are very easy to carry out. Perhaps the simplest and most democratic model is a backpack for a young girl from bright yarn with a large comfortable pocket. It will require approximately 200 grams of thick twisted yarn. For example, 100 grams of saturated brown and 50 grams of light beige and green. For a backpack, you need to tie a rectangular canvas with single crochet columns, 25x60 cm in size, which is then sewn together on the sides into a rectangular bag. Then, the upper edge of the bag is tied in a circle first with a strip of beige yarn, then of green. The work ends with a wide strip of brown yarn, on which, departing from the edge by 5 cm, holes are made for the cord. Before stitching a backpack, a large pocket of beige and green stripes about 15x15 cm in size is knitted on its front side, in the center. The pocket can be tied separately as a square, starting from the center or from the bottom, or tied directly onto the fabric by inserting a hook into the central columns and pulling successively loops that are knit with crochets. After the desired pocket size is connected, it is sewn on the sides to the main part of the backpack.

Then twist two cords with a length of 120 cm. For each take 2 strands of beige and two green 300 cm each. Connect their ends with knots. Tie one end of the threads to the doorknob and begin to twist them in one direction rather tightly until they are twisted along the entire length. Remove the end attached to the handle, the cord is bent in half. He immediately twists into a tight tourniquet. Swipe it up and down to level it. Similarly, you need to make a second lace. Ready twisted harnesses are inserted into the holes at the top edge of the backpack. One on the front side, the other on the back. At the bottom, the ends of the cords are connected together and decorated with a brush.

Crocheted bags complement well with leather details. For this, straps, valves, side walls of old, already fed up models are suitable. They are carefully evaporated and used in a new design. Crocheted handbags in combination with leather should be tight enough and keep their shape well. Therefore, the knitted fabric should be duplicated with a special dense material, and the entire product "put" on the lining.

A beautiful crocheted bag is obtained from individual motifs (circles or squares), which are then collected in a single piece. An elegant bag is obtained from openwork elements, from simpler and smoother ones - everyday.
