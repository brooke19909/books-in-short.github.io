Energy Saving and Battery

The phone has a Li-Ion battery, a capacity of 2800 mAh. As for the energy-saving mode, it allows you to use the phone for almost two days, while actively using calls and Internet services. Therefore, "Samsung Galaxy S5" reviews on this occasion gathered very comforting.

An undoubted advantage in the S5 piggy bank is that in the settings there are two additional energy saving modes, and for them the quick start button is even displayed. One of them is a mode in which the background work of applications is limited and only gray colors of the screen are turned on.





When you enable this feature, you can use the phone to the full, even watch the video, but it will be in gray. Thus, the device will last 10% of the battery for another 2 hours with active use.

The second mode is the maximum limit on energy consumption, it turns on on a 15% battery and also activates a gray color. But this function limits almost everything on your phone: communications, lists of running applications and all background processes. You can only call and send SMS, but with it, the phone will last a whole day.

In view of these energy-saving features, we can confidently say that such a step was an excellent solution to problems with saving battery power of the Samsung Galaxy S5.
