How to transfer money from Megafon to Megafon and other transfer methods

The article will discuss how to transfer money from Megafon to Megafon. The benefit of this opportunity is provided by the mobile operator to its users. However, this is not all: other ways of transferring money will also be agreed.





Method number 1: "Mobile transfer"

So, let's talk about how to transfer money from Megafon to Megafon. The first method will be the Mobile Transfer service. It is provided by the operator and is completely legal, which will save your money from loss or theft.

Before describing how to transfer money from Megafon to Megafon, it is worth making a reservation and telling about the commission and limits.

If you use this service, then from the account of the sender, with the exception of the transfer amount itself, 5 rubles will be charged - this will happen if the transfer is carried out in the territory of one region. If the transfer is between different regions, the commission will be 0 rubles. As for the limit, up to 5 thousand rubles can be transferred per month in the territory of a single region, and between subscribers of different regions this figure will rise to 15 thousand rubles.

After clarifying all the intricacies, you can proceed directly to how to transfer money from Megafon to Megafon.
