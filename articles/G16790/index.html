Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 *, Alanya: reviews and photos

Alanya is one of the most popular regions in Turkey. Every year, a huge number of tourists from around the world come here who wish to relax in an exotic atmosphere on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea.

Tour operators from different countries very often offer travelers trips to one of the hotels in Alanya - Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 *. Reviews of tourists about it arouse interest in this place among many. As agents and vacationers themselves note, this hotel has low prices for accommodation and a rather high level of service. Each guest here is received very warmly, which makes me want to come back here again.

general information

Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 * in Alanya was built relatively long ago - in 1996. However, despite this, at the moment it has a fairly modern and fresh look. Such results were achieved due to regular work to maintain the general condition of the building and the territory adjacent to it. One of the last such renovations was made in 2015, at the same time the interior in the apartments was updated.

To date, the hotel has the highest category of "five stars" and is located on a fairly large area - 100 square meters. m

As noted in the official description of the Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 * hotel (Alanya), it is perfect for family vacations, as well as for spending time with your loved one.



Location

The hotel is located in the resort region of Turkey, Alanya, actively visited by tourists, in the small village of Konakli, the coast of which is washed by the warm waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Tourists are often attracted by the originality of its position - Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 * (Alanya) is located almost on a cliffside, on the very shore of the sea. According to tourists, this place is incredibly picturesque, which is often reflected in the photos taken by vacationers on its territory.

The nearest air traffic hub is in the large city of Antalya - it is 110 kilometers from the hotel, with regard to night and daytime entertainment, the center of their cluster is located 10 km from here - in the central part of the city of Alanya.



Rooms

For guests of Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 * (Alanya) a large selection of rooms is provided, the total number of which is 410. All of them are located in 18 two- and three-story buildings, which have a very modern look. In their reviews of Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 * (Alanya), tourists often on the positive side note that most apartments offer stunning sea views from their windows.

All apartments that are available here, depending on the conditions provided for living, are divided into two categories: standard and family. Each of them has a personal air conditioning, with which you can adjust the temperature regime based on the individual preferences of the guest, regardless of the weather that is on the street.

All rooms also have a private bathroom, which is equipped with modern fixtures and bath accessories, designed for each guest.



Standard

The standard rooms in Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 * (Alanya) are the predominant majority, each of them is located on a relatively small area - 22 square meters. m. The apartments have only one room, where the number of beds is designed for three.

The central place in the furniture is a large double bed, which in some apartments is replaced by two single beds. If necessary, an extra bed in the form of a folding sofa can be used. In addition to the sleeping area, there is also a place for relaxation - it is represented by a sofa and a coffee table. Here guests can read their favorite books and drink hot drinks. For tourists' things, there is a large wardrobe, which is divided into several shelves for clothes.

Guests have the opportunity to request in their room a personal safe for storing small valuables, a telephone, as well as a minibar, the content of which is updated every day.



Family

In reviews of Larissa Green Hill Hotel (Alanya), tourists often focus on the fact that family-class rooms are perfect for tourists who organize their holidays with their children. All this is due to the presence of a larger area here, in comparison with standard apartments, as well as the possibility of free cots in the room, for which you need to make a preliminary request. It is possible to check in four guests, three of which can accommodate in beds, and one in an extra bed in the form of a folding sofa. The furniture here also has a pair of cabinets, two cabinets standing near the berths, as well as a small table. In their free time, tourists have the opportunity to watch TV channels broadcast on a plasma screen. Here, a minibar is installed on a free basis, the range of which is updated once a week.

Conditions for children

In the description of Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 * (Alanya) it is always said that it is very comfortable to live with children on its territory, and therefore the hotel is ideal for families. All this is due to the fact that in this place a wide range of conditions is provided that make the stay of small guests more comfortable. One of them is the availability of free individual beds for babies. In addition, they have a small outdoor pool, which is equipped with everything necessary for their fun and safe relaxation, as well as a playground for games.

Parents in their reviews say that the hotel has a wonderful mini-club, where an animation team works, many of whose members speak Russian - this is very convenient for tourists from the CIS countries. Here kids can play, watch educational programs and favorite cartoons.

Nutrition

A large number of laudatory comments are often addressed in the direction of the food offered at the hotel. Here, every tourist who rests on a package including the all-inclusive concept can visit the buffet organized in the restaurant three times a day. This place, as tourists say, is truly very beautiful, but the first thing that attracts them is a wide range of dishes presented for guests. All of them are prepared in the best traditions of Turkish, Mediterranean and European cuisines and are served very brightly. Guests recommend paying special attention to food prepared with seafood. She, according to them, has a special taste.

Also, a lot of tourists' attention is paid to the bars, of which there are only four in the Larissa Green Hill Hotel 5 * hotel in Alanya. Each of them offers a huge selection of drinks and cocktails, each of which is a real work of art. You can also order desserts and snacks here.

As guests in their reviews say, each member of this group can find an approach to a guest of any age, including children, which often pleases parents.
