Restoration of accounting and reporting by a third-party organization

Unfortunately, there are still many cases when organizations or individual entrepreneurs incorrectly maintain accounting records in the services sector or do not conduct them at all. Often this is due to the low professional level of employees or lack of knowledge of legislative changes. But the issue is very serious, violators may face tax and administrative liability. Fines are measured in tens of thousands of rubles.



How can such troubles be avoided? Hire professionals, not yesterday's students, willing to work for a penny to gain experience. But the level of an accountant who came for an interview can be difficult to assess if the owner or director does not have the appropriate knowledge. If problems have already arisen, you will have to contact the organization that provides such a service as restoration of accounting and reporting.

What is this procedure? Bringing the balance sheet, tax returns and statements in line with existing accounting papers, as well as the restoration of primary documents, which should be, but are absent. In short, this is the harmonization of all records in accordance with the requirements of the law.



What threatens the rejection of such a procedure as the restoration of accounting and reporting? The top of the organization and its owners do not have complete and reliable information about the financial state of affairs. Firm accounts may be arrested. In addition, the leadership will have to be responsible for everything, since it will be impossible to attract people with access to money, as well as inventory items .

For what reasons may the restoration of accounting and reporting be required? There may be several. First of all, this is a change in accounting personnel and their insufficient qualifications. Errors in the application of the new requirements of the law or complete ignorance of them are also possible.

Depending on the situation, the restoration of accounting and reporting can be divided into full and partial. For example, the organization has problems with only one direction, which means that you can not touch the rest. Of course, this will cost less than ordering a comprehensive service. If necessary, restoration of financial statements, registration, personnel, tax accounting, etc. can be selectively applied.



Who else might need to carry out such procedures? Third-party organizations can also resort to organizations that suffer from a lack of accounting personnel or who decide to insure themselves, identify and solve all the problem points before a tax audit.

Previously, professionals evaluate the current state of accounting. They analyze the available documentation, identify areas of upcoming work and work out a program of action. Then the main registers are formed, if the accounting was not previously conducted at all, primary documentation is collected and registered . The last step is the preparation and submission of reports for the restored period. The cost is always individual and depends on the complexity of the case and the amount of work.
