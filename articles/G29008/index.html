Hotel "Dvor Podznoeva", Pskov: photos and reviews of tourists

Located in the heart of the ancient Russian city, “Podznoeva Yard” began its work relatively recently, in 2009. However, he revealed exactly what the townspeople had long been waiting for - a journey into the past, real merchant chambers, trimmed with the corresponding luxury. Fans of antiquity and amazing cuisine are ready to exchange a few days at sea for a trip here. Therefore, Podznoev’s Courtyard almost immediately turned into one of the most sought-after facilities in the city. It attracts the townspeople precisely with its originality and uniqueness, as well as with the highest level of service.



Short description of the complex

Even just being nearby, you will certainly be interested in the grandeur of this complex. These are two completely restored and restored buildings of the merchant houses of the XVII century. They very harmoniously coexist with other architectural monuments of the ancient city. Podznoev’s Yard is located just a five-minute walk from the stunning Pskov Kremlin. Here, even foreigners quickly understand what Russian hospitality means. Helpful staff greets each guest with joy and reverence, all the time while you are staying at the complex, the staff will be happy to fulfill any of your wishes.

What is the Podznoev Yard complex?

A huge territory is presented to your attention, on which there is everything that is necessary for a great vacation. There is a large and very cozy main building of the hotel, a business building, a spa, three restaurants, a cafe and a shop. In addition, you are offered a unique wellness center, “Refectory Chambers” and “Beer”, “Coffee” and “Pie Chambers”. For business people, there are two huge conference rooms and a business room that are equipped with multimedia systems and simultaneous translation and are perfect for a variety of business and holiday events.



How to get to the hotel?

Podznoev’s Yard (Pskov) is located in the historical center of the city. It is easy to get to it from the railway station, so if you are a newcomer and immediately want to start examining it, then take bus number 2 or number 6. You need to get out at the Victory Square stop, but the conductor can also tell you this. It remains to walk on foot to the intersection of Sovetskaya and Nekrasov streets, and you will see the corresponding sign. It should be noted that viewing of historical expositions is free of charge, and not only hotel guests can have a tasty lunch here.



Complex Feature

First of all, the Podznoev Yard complex (Pskov) distinguishes a thematic focus. A lover of antiquity and history will be very interesting here. Along with a fairly standard list of services, a map of the city with the main historical sites, as well as a historical exposition, are offered to guests' attention. It presents objects found in excavations that people used 1000 years ago. There are jewelry and weapons, as well as household items.



Hotel and rooms

To accommodate guests is the hotel "Dvor Podznoeva." It is represented primarily by the main building, which has the class of a four-star hotel. There are 50 comfortable rooms, a 24-hour lobby bar, as already mentioned, 2 conference rooms, a living room and a banquet room. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. It has comfortable beds and an LCD TV, telephone. All guests have free internet access. In addition, refrigerators and safes are provided, as well as carpeted floors. A business building is also presented to guests. These are only 25 rooms of the highest level of comfort with luxurious interiors, modern upholstered furniture and household appliances. It is here that the historical exposition, as well as a business room for negotiations, are presented to the attention of guests.

The cost of living is quite affordable, a standard double room will cost 2800 rubles, superior - 3800. Triple rooms cost 4100 rubles, quadruple - 5500. Therefore, if you want to examine Pskov properly, the Dvor Podznoeva hotel is at your service.

For relaxation and wellness

Among the townspeople, the wellness center, which offers its guests "Podznoev's Court", is also famous. The spa center within the hotel is a unique three-level complex that will appeal to absolutely everyone. Each level is connected with others, but provides separate services. This is a caring, healing and relaxation spa. The price includes access to the gym, pool and bath. Separately, you can order massages and peels, body wraps and other, more serious procedures. It is guaranteed that you will come out of these walls ten years younger, more energetic and more fun. And you are definitely provided with a positive attitude.



“Refectory Chambers”

We smoothly move on to the description of a multi-level restaurant, which is famous throughout Pskov and not only. This is a truly unique place. The first violin here is the described restaurant, expensive and sophisticated. Here, sophisticated gourmets are offered gourmet cuisine, Russian and European. The interiors here are very noteworthy, the designers were inspired by the lost aesthetics of the 17th century, when stone houses began to be erected to replace several wooden buildings with wooden floors. Stained-glass windows began to be used as glazing, and mirrors and precious stones in the interiors were no longer such a rarity. This is exactly what the sophisticated modern visitor “Podznoev's Court” (Pskov) appears to be. The menu is also striking in its luxury: beluga and salmon caviar, seafood, fried pigs, pickles, stewed fruit drinks and fruit drinks from local berries - everything here is simply impossible to try.



The Pirogov Chambers

The most attractive and accessible cafe on the territory of this complex. Such delicious and beautiful Russian pies can not be found anywhere else, in addition, the prices here are quite affordable. The Pirogov Chambers (Podznoev’s Yard) offer you delicious cakes, pies and pastries, a huge selection of various breads. The recipe for all dishes is based solely on natural products, so the cafe has gained great popularity among the townspeople.

Other cafes in the complex

Indeed, the choice of entertainment for tourists does not end there. After all, you have not been to the Beer Chambers, have not tasted an amazing drink brewed according to old recipes, as well as a huge assortment of snacks. These are homemade sausages and shanks, smoked trout and salted salmon, as well as several dozen types of various delicious things that are customary to serve with beer in various countries.

Finally, the sweetest "Coffee Chambers" are waiting for you. Here you seem to be transported to another reality, and it was intended to fully convey the spirit of the era when Russia first learned the taste of coffee and chocolate. This is the era of Peter I. Here you will always be offered a large number of handmade sweets, as well as several dozen types of coffee, each of which is distinguished by its unique taste.



Tourists reviews

Rest in this complex, according to tourists, can not be called budget. Hotel accommodation itself is comparable to prices in hotels of this class, but if in addition to this you want to visit a fabulous restaurant or coffee shop, then get ready for the costs. An average dinner for three without alcohol costs about 3,000 rubles, and if you like coffee and desserts, the amount will increase even more. But to be here and treat yourself is still worth it. Stunning interior, caring staff, gorgeous menu - all this creates a festive atmosphere. But the "Pirogov Chambers" are famous for their availability, despite the fact that the pastries here are simply amazing. It is impossible to pass by and not go again. To summarize, I want to add that, judging by the reviews of tourists, this is a great place to spend a luxurious weekend. Therefore, get together as a family and come to Pskov.
