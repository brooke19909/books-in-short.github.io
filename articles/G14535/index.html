Hotel "Villa Fellini", Sudak: reviews and photos

The long-awaited vacation is approaching, and the search begins for a suitable place to relax. European and exotic resorts remain in fashion, attracting Russian tourists with unusual attractions and original cuisine, but the price of all this diversity is considerable. You can relax with taste, comfort and touch the high service in your native country. The tourist infrastructure in Russia today is well developed, and sometimes not even inferior to overseas resorts. One example of such a professional level is the Hotel Villa Fellini (Sudak). Thanks to the reviews of millions of travelers, he has already twice won the competition for the title of best from TripAdvisor Internet service. We will get acquainted with the features of the service and reviews of tourists.



Location and surroundings

"Villa Fellini" is located in the Sudak Valley, in one of the most incredible in its beauty and freshness of the air places of the Crimean Peninsula. The hotel is surrounded by the azure sea, picturesque landscapes and bright sun. The Alchak rock towering above it is breathtaking, around which there is a hiking trail. The rugged surface of the mountain then rises sharply to 50 m in height, then gradually descends to the cozy sea bays. In addition to aesthetic pleasure, the area also brings health benefits. The Black Sea, coupled with pine forest belts, creates incredibly fresh and invigorating air that will be appreciated by all who are welcomed by Villa Fellini (Sudak).

Description of the hotel by travel agencies is like a fairy tale. But it is difficult to convey in words the contrast of the majesty of Sudak nature and the elite resort of the category of four stars, historical buildings and modern architecture, attractions.

As a rule, when planning a vacation, rooms here are booked in advance. One of the frequently asked questions to the administration of the Villa Fellini Hotel (Sudak) is how to get to the resort. From the local train station of Feodosia there is a comfortable shuttle bus that can deliver passengers to the place in half an hour. The journey from the airport in Simferopol to the hotel by car takes about an hour.



Description

The hotel itself is a four-story building, the architecture of which captivates with sophistication of forms and cozy organicity with a well-groomed green area around. Mirror windows, spiral staircases, small round-shaped balconies, bright flower beds create an atmosphere of an enchanted castle, which is simply impossible to pass without curiosity and enthusiasm.

The interior is not inferior in its sophistication and gives respectability to the hotel. Each element: paintings, elegant sofas, floor tiles, mirrors - is part of an elite style, which, it seems, simply cannot be disturbed.

The weather is far from always favorable to those who visit Crimea. Sudak (Villa Fellini), by contrast, is considered a paradise with a mild climate. Of course, the most suitable season for tourists is summer. The maximum air temperature reaches 38 degrees. The sea in the Sudak Bay warms up early to 22 degrees and more. The swimming season here is one of the longest on the entire coast - from June to mid-October. Despite the cold winters, the hotel is often visited during this period of the year. To do this, the administration provides central heating in the hotel.

Entertainment

Villa Fellini (Sudak) is located about 500 meters from the Black Sea beach. On the way there you can visit the water park, which has earned the title of the best on the coast and offers exciting water attractions. A little further from the hotel is the Nemo Dolphinarium. All these activities can be exciting adventures for children and adults.

To explore the surroundings, you can book a walk and a tour of the historic sites. Of greatest interest are hiking in the mountains and the Genoese fortress of the late 17th century, shrouded in legends.

Prices

Despite the high level of service, convenient location, beauty of the area and an impressive entertainment complex, Villa Fellini (Sudak) offers quite affordable prices. In addition, when planning a long vacation (from a week or more), discounts on accommodation (up to 15%) apply. Reviews

"Villa Fellini" (Sudak), the description of which is given in the article - this is only one side of the perception of the resort. Often the decisive role in the choice is played by reviews of experienced travelers.

Holidays on the Black Sea for Russians are usually associated with Anapa, Gelendzhik and Sochi. Sudak Valley is a relatively new tourist destination. The seaside town was initially known for its wine production. The main advantage of the area is its majestic nature, but now the expansion of tourist infrastructure makes it an attractive resort.

One of the pioneers of local service is considered "Villa Fellini" (Sudak). Reviews of tourists often describe the tedious road among unfinished hotels. After getting into a comfortable hotel, the opinion drastically changes to a positive one. And this is not surprising! The four-star category provides coziness, comfort, carefree and long-awaited relaxation of guests. But tourists are different, so you should get acquainted with diverse opinions to see for sure whether to visit this hotel.
