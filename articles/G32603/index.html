We select hotels: Greece, Kos

Kos Island (Greece), the hotels of which we will now consider, differs from the others in the Dodecanos archipelago. It is located so close to the mainland that the Turkish city of Bodrum is already visible from it. The cool waters of the Aegean Sea soften the summer heat, and the beaches are so numerous and varied that both sand lovers and pebble lovers will be satisfied. The proximity of Turkey led to a slight decline in prices. This affected both hotel services and the catering industry. And the quality remains unchanged at the European level.



Resorts on the island of Kos (Greece) are located on the first or second strip of the beach. The hotel base extends to all price categories, from deluxe complexes to small cozy apartments. A small territory and first-class transport links allow you to get acquainted with all the resorts of this island, and at the same time take a ride for shopping or on an excursion to Turkey.





Five Star Hotels: Greece, Kos, Kardamena

Thanks to the tiniest soft sand on its coast, this fishing village in a few years has become the island's most fashionable resort. Luxury is blinded by the Porto Bello Royal hotel complex with its own pier for private yachts of VIP guests. Spacious rooms, suites and villas are equipped with a Hi-Fi system. In six themed restaurants and four bars, they are fed around the clock using the ultra all-inclusive system. Adherents of chain hotels are pleased to receive the Doubletree by Hilton massage, spa, jewelry store, rooms with private pools and other glamor.

Resorts: Greece, Kos, Kefalos

The luxurious Blue Lagoon complex will also delight supporters of deluxe-style relaxation. There are two distinct areas: for family tourists, where the emphasis is on silence and tranquility, and the "adults only" sector. There is fun around the clock. Eight theme restaurants feed all guests with an Ultra All Inclusive program. Children can not only attend the mini-club, but also study at the Academy of Performing Arts. In this resort there are also hotels for 4 and 3 stars, private apartments for rent.

Luxury Hotels: Greece, Kos, Mastichari

A huge Neptune hotel complex is located right on the sandy beach. There are five swimming pools, four restaurants, a night club, seven bars. A special menu is provided for children. No less impressive is the Marmari Palace. You can stay here both in rooms in the building, and in the villas, and even in suites that are located directly on the beach. At the guests' disposal is everything that relies on the European Five: air conditioning, refrigerators, safes, bathrobes, branded toiletries.



Four and Three Star Hotels: Greece, Kos

The hotels, which have 4 stars in their signboard, are about seven on the whole island. They are not much inferior to the "fives." Is that the territory is smaller and not so many themed restaurants. But the rooms have everything for a comfortable stay: air conditioning, refrigerator, safe, Internet access. The only thing to consider when reserving the Quartet is that a fairly small number of them work on an all-inclusive system. Many practice half boards or even breakfast. There are 45 three-star hotels on the island. Bathrobes are not offered here, but the rooms are clean and comfortable. To lure guests, 3- and 4-star hotels offer so-called specialization.
