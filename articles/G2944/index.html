List of VTB ATMs in Ulyanovsk

In Ulyanovsk, there are more than 50 terminals and ATMs for self-service of VTB Bank customers, most of which allow you to conduct banking operations without a commission at any convenient time, according to the schedule of the terminal. A list of a number of ATMs is published below.

Installed in organizations

ATM "VTB" is installed on the territory of the locomotive depot in Ulyanovsk. The self-service device is assigned serial number 395349. Location address: Zheleznodorozhnaya Street, 48. The device allows you to withdraw cash in rubles.

Installed in bank branches

VTB ATM in Ulyanovsk is installed on the territory of the additional office of Ulyanovsk Bank of Branch No. 6318 at 33 Goncharnaya Street, Building 2. Customers can use the following services:

  • cash deposit in several currencies;
  • making payments and transfers;
  • cash withdrawal in national currency.

The terminal operates according to the following schedule: from Monday to Friday from 9 a.m. to 7 p.m. and on Saturday from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. On Sunday, a self-service banking device is unavailable. It is worth noting that this terminal also has several additional ATMs, which can be used around the clock, they are also available on holidays.
