Where from?

Not all users know that this smartphone is the brainchild of China's largest company, Huawei. The corporation has been operating since 1987 and is located in China.

The company has been manufacturing various equipment for a long time and has already managed to become popular in the CIS region. Among the products of the manufacturer there are not only smartphones, but also other products. Huawei is working on the release of wireless equipment, core network devices, network devices. Engaged in the production of applications and hardware, as well as terminals.

But it was the phones that brought the company to the world market. By 2015, she took third place in the production of smartphones worldwide. The same situation persisted by the beginning of 2017.

Photo / Video

Honor 8 received a lot of reviews about the camera. The manufacturer himself did not think for a long time and decided to introduce the well-known lenses from Huawei P9 into the new product. In the last smartphone, the camera was a "chip", apparently, therefore, it was duplicated in the new model.



To begin with, these were one of the first attempts to introduce dual-chamber devices on the market. This is now everyone is happy with the numerous "apple" and Chinese lenses. Last year, it seemed a gimmick.

The phone has two cameras that do not work on a stereo image or super blur. It was decided to install two modules for more important things.

Each lens works in a resolution of 12 megapixels with a matrix from Sony. The aperture of both cameras is F2.2, the viewing angle is 27 mm. The right eye is responsible for color shooting, the left - for the creation of monochrome pictures.

This couple works according to the following principle. The black-and-white module collects information about the amount of light. The sensor can analyze the dynamic range. Accordingly, sensitivity increases. The second module remembers colors. All this is combined, and good photos are obtained.

"Honor 8" customer reviews in this regard collected the most positive. Of course, there is information that the novelty was equipped with a different version of the modules, but the pictures did not deteriorate much.

Daytime photos are unusually cool. No need to use HDR: the camera does not hide the brightened areas and does not show non-detailed dimming. There are no problems with colors and white balance.

When the lighting diminishes, the photos are slightly worse. Sharpness disappears and noise appears. Although the colors are still bright and saturated.

Many wondered how Honor 8 was making a video. Customer reviews on this subject are different. Someone liked the quality of the shooting and the fact that the videos are obtained in FullHD resolution. Someone did not have the maximum frequency of 60 frames.

In fact, the videos are recorded in medium quality. But they differ in fast focusing and good sound.

Technical component

The manufacturer independently worked on the chipset. HiSilicon Kirin 950 is manufactured according to the 16 nm process technology, works with 8 cores. Half of them operate at a speed of 2.3 GHz, half at a speed of 1.8 GHz. Responsible for the schedule is the Chinese representative of Mali-T880.



There is a lot of RAM inside 4 GB - even a year later. The internal memory can be 32 or 64 GB, depending on version. In this case, you can supplement the storage with a memory card.

The battery inside received a volume of 3000 mAh. On average, moderate use discharges a smartphone in 18 hours. If you use 4G, then the operating time is reduced by three times. Continuous play will last 4 hours, and video viewing - 7 hours.
