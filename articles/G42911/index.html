Cape Town is one of the most famous cities in Africa. What part of the mainland is it located in? What are the coordinates of the city of Cape Town, and what attracts tourists to it?

City on the shores of two oceans

Cape Town is a unique city. 20 kilometers south of it is the Cape of Good Hope. The one that delimits two oceans - Indian and Atlantic. Thus, the city is located on the shores of two bodies of water on a planetary scale!

Cape Town is one of the three capitals of the Republic of South Africa , namely the legislative one. Here is the country's parliament. In addition, it is the most important economic and financial center of South Africa. It houses the offices of large banks and companies.

The city arose in the middle of the XVII century. The history of its appearance is closely related to the search for a sea trade route to India. About 3.7 million people live in modern Cape Town. It is the second most populated city in South Africa. The metropolis is also one of the most beautiful and most visited cities on the African continent.

Cape Town: Geographic Coordinates

The city is located in the southwestern part of South Africa, within the Cape Peninsula. A peculiar and very spectacular background for him is Table Mountain, which rises more than a thousand meters above the sea level.

Cape Town is located on the shores of Table Bay - one of the most picturesque in Africa.

  • It houses the largest aquarium in the Southern Hemisphere.
  • Cape Town's twin cities include French Nice and Russian St. Petersburg. Decent company!
  • Cape Town is the largest (by area) city in South Africa.
