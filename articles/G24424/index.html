Where will the exits of the Kotelniki metro (Moscow region)

Many residents of the capital of the Russian Federation who use the Tugunrog-Krasnopresnenskaya line are interested in where the Kotelniki metro exits will be. It was opened in September last year in the city of the same name in the Experimental Field microdistrict.

Importance to the public

Anyone who was worried about the question of where the Kotelniki metro exits could now use the passage in Vykhino-Zhulebina, as well as in Gorodok B of the Lyubertsy district.

The development of this paragraph was a very important step for the infrastructure of the region as a whole. High-tech transport hub are Kotelniki. The metro, the opening of which enriched the city with a second station, which operates directly outside Moscow (there is also the Mayakinino), greatly facilitated the lives of people. Many from the suburbs need to get to the center. In addition, when we find out where the Kotelniki metro exits will be, we see that there is a connection with the capital and the outskirts. Project

In May 2012, a resolution of the metropolitan government was signed by the head of the city. In accordance with it, they planned the construction and launch of the Kotelniki metro station. How quickly this plan is being implemented was controlled by M. Khusnullin, deputy mayor who is involved in urban planning.

Beginning in October of the same 2012, the issue of the project, according to which the Kotelniki metro station was to be commissioned in the future, was taken up by one of the SK MOST divisions. They began work on the construction of part of the line. Its development was supposed to be a fragment, stretching from Zhulebino to the very points where the exits of the Kotelniki metro would be. It was planned to lay tracks 900 meters long. The station was built in an open type. The complex itself was supposed to occupy 374 m. Where to go

The path that lies to the north-west is located at Zhulebino district number 6. You can also get to City B from here. Passing east, you can find yourself on the highway Novoryazansky. In the future there will be TPU, to which access will also be opened.
