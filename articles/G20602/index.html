The thing is that the main thing here is a pattern, and not how you can quickly sew a dress, a shirt or trousers. If the cutting takes a minimum of time, the fit according to the figure does not require additional grooves and strictly calibrated folds, then the creation of the dress takes a maximum of several hours.





Couture Dress

Even the most famous styles and silhouettes often have a simple cut. One of these is a truly stellar dress depicted in the first photograph. Carrie Underwood and Whitney Port flaunted at social events in it. But how to sew an evening dress for a beginning dressmaker? Flared one-piece sleeves make the pattern of this masterpiece one of the simplest, and tailoring is fast. So here we go.

For work you will need: a piece of knitted black fabric with white peas, one and a half meters wide and 1.8 meters long, your favorite knitted T-shirt with sleeves or a dress. The t-shirt should fit well. If you select a material that does not crumble, then you can not waste time processing the edges of the product and sleeves.

Next, proceed to the pattern. Fold the cut in four layers, with the outside inward. The basis of the pattern will be a previously set aside knitted shirt that sits well on the figure. The shirt also needs to be folded in half and chopped with pins. Now apply a T-shirt to the fold of the fabric and cleave. Draw a chalk line of the side seam at a distance of 2 cm from the edge of the T-shirt. Then we carry out a pattern of sleeves. If at first the seam line runs at a distance of 2 cm from the sleeve located on the fabric of the T-shirt, then at a distance of 25 cm from the edge the sleeve must be flared, gradually increasing its width to 25 cm. The bottom of this part of the dress is slightly rounded.

Now draw the neck. On the shelf, we make a V-shaped cutout 15 cm long and 12 cm wide, and at the back we make a boat, we cut everything together. Sew two side seams. We process the sleeves, neck and bottom of the product. New evening dress is ready. No need to think of what and how to quickly sew a dress. Just take a knitted fabric and make a masterpiece in one evening.





A one-piece flared sleeve in a pattern allows you to create a variety of styles and models. In such models, product length, material and fit differ. You can make a new thing that fits tightly on the figure or, conversely, freer. If, on the pattern of the previous model, when cutting the side seam, draw at a distance of 4 cm from the T-shirt and take a denser fabric, then we get a completely different product in general silhouette and appearance. A small one-piece sleeve will greatly simplify sewing the outfit for the warm season. After all, in the heat, the question of how to sew a summer dress quickly and easily is especially relevant.

Lace dress

Beautiful and stylish lace mini dress will be a real highlight of any party, and the unique maxi is suitable for an exquisite celebration. Many acquaintances will not believe that such a dress was sewn with their own hands. Here is a detailed instruction on how to quickly sew a dress from lace fabric.





The principle of cutting such a dress is similar to the previous one. To work, you need lace one and a half meters wide and a length of 1.40 m for a mini. And for maxi, you need to measure the distance from the shoulder seam to the bottom line. The cut length will be two such lengths.

As a basis for the pattern, we take that very T-shirt and again we fold and chop it with needles in half. Next, fold the fabric for a new outfit four times. Pin a t-shirt. Now we draw a line of the side seam at a distance of 4 cm from the edge of the T-shirt, making a flare to the bottom. We cut a sleeve. To do this, mark the end point of the sleeve of the T-shirt, draw down the perpendicular to a distance of 35 cm. Now we connect it to the armhole. We perform the neck in the form of a boat and cut out the pattern. How to quickly sew a dress of this style? It will take you only 5-10 minutes to sew. After all, it is necessary to make only 2 side seams. Most of the time is spent on cutting and figured design of the bottom and sleeves of the product.
