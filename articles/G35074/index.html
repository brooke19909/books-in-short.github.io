The service "Super Caller ID" company "Megaphone"

With the advent of the service " caller ID" there was a need for subscribers of mobile operators to recognize unknown calls. For this purpose, the service "Super Caller ID" was developed by Megafon. By connecting it, you can see who is trying to hide the number. In addition, if you make a call from a hidden number, information about this will be displayed. Thus, it is easy to determine if a call is made from a hidden or unclassified number. What features does the Super AON service suggest? How can I activate and deactivate it if necessary?





Financial terms of use of the service

Consider the cost for the Moscow region. You can activate Super AON service completely free of charge (we are talking about both the initial connection and the subsequent ones). The monthly fee is one and a half thousand rubles per month. Some subscribers are afraid of this amount of payment. However, it is worth clarifying that it is charged daily, in proportion to the days in the month. The daily payment is about 50 rubles. This is a huge advantage - after all, the service may be required for only a few days, which means that such a pleasure will cost 150 rubles.

Other conditions that the subscriber needs to know about

The service is not limited to the home region, traveling to other cities of the country, you can also determine hidden numbers.

In international roaming, the operator does not guarantee that the Super-identifier will act correctly.

You cannot activate the option on every mobile device (as a rule, this feature is not available for some outdated phone models).
