Hardware equipment

Specifications Samsung Galaxy Ace Plus S7500 even at the time of release were considered rather weak. The smartphone is based on the Qualcomm MSM7227a processor. Such a "filling", although not considered quite powerful, but allows the gadget to work without freezes. Most owners noticed that the phone is stable and copes with all everyday tasks.
