Easter chicken (hook): diagrams. Crochet Easter Chickens

Easter is a bright holiday, the symbol of which is colored eggs and Easter animals. In our country, the Easter chicken won love. A hook in this matter can become an indispensable tool. Knitted birds look nice and easily decorate your holiday table. Show your talent and delight with the bright products of your loved ones and relatives.

White chicken

We offer to decorate your home with a charming knitted chicken. She can take center stage this day. Around it is to lay multi-colored eggs. Crocheting Easter chickens is a very exciting activity. Any needlewoman can create such a poultry .

You will need white yarn and a hook number 2.5 or number 3. You need to start with the body of the chicken. To do this, dial a chain of three air loops. They must be closed in a ring. Next, you need to knit a bird, adding additional loops in each row. We choose the simplest pattern - half columns.





The body fits from the head to the middle of the tail. Give your bird the desired shape. The size is set according to your desire. All guests will enjoy this Easter chicken. A hook is the main tool with which this masterpiece is created. After the base is ready, you can proceed to the legs and wings. The legs are first made of white yarn, and then a yellow thread is taken. The pattern is used the most elementary. To make the legs stable, a thick wire base is placed inside.





Wings can be knitted according to any pattern that you like. It is advisable to choose embossed so that there is an imitation of feathers. All parts are sewn into a single design. The finishing touches will be the scallop, cheeks and beak. Beads are used as eyes.

Cute chickens for table decoration

If the first option seems complicated to you, then you will be pleased with simpler Easter knitted crochets. The schemes of such "birds" are elementary and will not cause unnecessary questions. These creatures will decorate trays with colored eggs. They can be varied in color. In order to create such kids, use the remains of yarn.





To make a homemade miniature bird, you need to tie the blank. It can resemble a boat. To do this, dial thirty air loops. The blank is knitted up with single crochet columns. Please note that at the edges an extra double crochet is added each time.





As you can see, the easter chicken is very easy to create. Crochet (you won’t even need any schemes) type eight rows. Cut the thread and find the middle. Now step back from the center in each direction the same number of loops. It will take four straight rows. No additions are made at the edges. The resulting workpiece is folded in half and stitched along the edge.





It is very easy to crochet an Easter chicken. After the edges are processed, it is worth turning the product to the front side. We stuff the blank with cotton or synthetic winterizer. We use orange wool to create a scallop and a beak. As a tail, you can use the most common pompom. Sew eyes on both sides. So the easter chicken is ready. You can use any hook. Its number depends on the thickness of the thread.

Flat cock: delivery for a mug

Not everyone may like flat hens. Then boldly knit flat birds, which will be ideal coasters for mugs. On them you can place colorful eggs. These crocheted Easter chickens are suitable as a gift. Schemes for their creation will be described below.





First, a circle is knitted, which will be used as the basis. Pick up five air loops and lock them in a circle. Next, knit the crochet. Each row needs to be increased by several loops. You should get a circle. If the additions are done incorrectly, the edges will begin to curl.





We make the head of the chicken on one side of the circle, gaining columns with two crochets. After the main workpiece is ready, knit small ruffles of a different color along the edge. The wing is made in the form of a circle. Use different shades of yarn to make the product rainbow and festive. Thus, you can knit a potholder for a gift.

Funny chickens

Now you know how to make an Easter chicken crochet. Workshop on creating funny chickens - next. Use leftover yarn to knit these decorations on your table. They will be perfectly located on a tray with the main dish of the holiday - colored eggs. Guests will definitely notice your creativity and creativity.





We will knit from the crown. We collect three air loops and close them in a circle. Next, knit with ordinary single crochet stitches. Additions are made in each row to form a cone. Then tie the wings, beak and scallop. The paws are made of yellow yarn. Chicks planted around the holiday table.

Hats for eggs

A very entertaining lesson is crocheting Easter chickens. Especially if they look like these yellow attractive babies. These hats fit on eggs. Your guests will be surprised to see such creatures on a tray.





They are made very simple. First, we knit the base in the form of a hat. After that, we decorate the template with beak, eyes and wings. Scallops can simply be tagged, these are also little chicks. And if you create a white chicken and put on such caps on the eggs lying next to you, you will get a whole Easter composition.

Chicken with shuttlecocks

Many imagine that a chicken is a chubby and fluffy bird. You can create just this yourself using a hook and woolen threads. To begin with, the base fits. You probably already understood how this is done. After this, the plumage should acquire an Easter chicken. The hook for this we take number one. We need to make shuttlecocks all over the body of the bird.





First we knit the upper row, and then the lower ones. In order to make such feathers, you need to type double crochet columns from one loop. Their number will depend on how voluminous you wish to make the plumage.





Colored hens

A crocheted rainbow Easter chicken looks fun. The schemes are very simple, so any needlewoman can create such birds. Knitting occurs on the same principle as the previous chicken. That's just the wool used bright, catchy. You can take the rest of the yarn from knitting other products. Use beads as eyes.





Do not forget about the decorative details: scallop, beak and paws. Such hens will certainly become the main accessories on the festive Easter table. You can use them as a gift or just sit on the plates of your guests. This will amuse everyone who comes to your house.
