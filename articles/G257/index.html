Atelika Oasis: short description, reviews

Summer vacation can and should be spent with children. Children recover, get a lot of impressions and expand their horizons. Adults on vacation with children get the opportunity to devote themselves to children, which is unacceptable luxury in everyday life and employment in the city.

If the decision is made on a joint vacation with children, the best option is to spend it in the Krasnodar Territory, the resort of Anapa. This place is rightfully considered the best children's resort on a national scale. Everything is provided for the relaxation of young guests: beaches with a gentle sandy entrance to the sea, landscaped area, recreation parks. One of the options for a budget holiday is the Atelika Oasis Hotel. Get to know him better.

Hotel Description

The hotel is located on the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory in the city of Anapa. At 10 kilometers from it is the city's airport, 5 kilometers - the city railway station.

You can order a transfer from the hotel, which will meet you at the place of arrival and quickly deliver to the place of accommodation and relaxation in the resort of Anapa.

Hotel facilities

The resort complex provides guests with a number of services that are included in the price. This is a three-day complex meal, which is provided in the Tavern restaurant with indoor and outdoor areas for eating.

There is also daily animation at the Atelika Oasis hotel; guest reviews of the hotel indicate its great diversity. It all starts with morning gymnastics, sports entertainment and competitions throughout the day and ends with evening show programs. The hotel has several swimming pools with equipped recreation areas for adults and children. A free Wi-Fi zone is in the restaurant of the complex.

For an additional payment at the hotel you can get the following services:

  • Car parking.
  • Safes.
  • Transfers
  • Excursion programs.

Special attention is given to children. For their relaxation, the hotel has an equipped playground and room, events for children are held in accordance with age groups: sports games, competitions, performances of the children's theater, quests. Children have their own swimming and relaxation area.

Booking conditions

Atelika Oasis Hotel has certain conditions that should be considered when booking a hotel:

  • Children under the age of three are accepted for free, without the provision of space and food. Children under 6 years old are accommodated in an extra bed, with meals.
  • The free stay service is provided only to one child in the room; for other children, a discount on accommodation is provided: 20% for children under the age of 12 in the main place; 30% - for children and adults (when booking an extra bed).
  • Pets are not allowed.
  • Smoking is allowed only in designated areas.
