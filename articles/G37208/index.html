DIY thread crafts

Any person had to deal with the threads in the household, especially if you need to sew something. Or, for example, in needlework to create beautiful knitwear or elegant embroidery. But not everyone will think that with the help of threads you can create an original product without using needles, hooks or knitting needles.

What can be made of threads?

It is hard to believe, but such a simple material will be an excellent basis for original earrings, hairpins, a scarf or just an object that emphasizes the originality of the home interior.

How to create an original hairpin?

The fair sex can spend a lot of time in the store in search of a beautiful and exclusive hair jewelry. These include elastic bands and hairpins, which are the most popular accessories. But it will be very disappointing then to see any fashionista have exactly the same decoration.

To avoid unpleasant experiences, it is enough to make a little effort and create a truly original craft from threads.

What do you need for work?

Before you begin to create hairpins, you need to acquire the following materials:

  • threads;
  • buttons
  • an elastic band;
  • decorating elements.

Step-by-step instruction

Step number 1. Thread wraps two fingers on the hand. If the working hand is right, then you need to reel on the left, for lefties everything is done the other way around. How many turns to make, each one decides independently.

Step number 2. After the required number of threads is wound on the fingers, it is necessary to fix the future decoration with another thread, which will pass in the middle between the fingers, as a result, a small bow should be obtained.

Step number 3. For decoration, you can use a button, a large bead, pearls of artificial origin and much more. The selected decor element is pinned in the middle. At the last stage, you need to fix the bow on an elastic band for hair. To make the decoration more original, you can take threads of different colors.

The original DIY thread craft is ready.

How to make a bracelet?

In this part of the article, we will focus on the manufacture of another jewelry that will be useful for fashionistas. If you have old bracelets that have already lost their appearance, do not rush to get rid of them. You can always give a new decoration a new life and at the same time be sure of the exclusivity of the resulting little thing.

What do you need to have on hand?

To work, you need the following things:

  • multi-colored threads;
  • glue;
  • decorating elements.

How to make?

To make a craft out of threads and glue, you need to select the threads of the desired color, and then wrap the bracelet in such a way that the base is not visible. All ends of weaving are fixed with glue. For decoration, you can use beads, sequins, decorative flowers, in a word, everything that comes to the mind of a craftswoman.

Christmas tree decoration

Yes, threads can be used for such crafts. As a result, the New Year tree will receive an original decoration, and all guests will certainly pay attention to such a memorable toy.

It is worth noting that this option will be an ideal craft from threads for children.

Before starting work, you must make sure that you have the following materials at hand:

  • cardboard boxes;
  • multi-colored threads;
  • scissors.

Manufacturing instruction

The cardboard is cut into small rings, which will subsequently be wrapped with threads. Before weaving, it is necessary to cut the threads to a length of about 25 cm.

One thread is taken, folded in half and fixed on a cardboard ring so that the tails pass through the loop made. You also need to do with all the other threads. All weaving is made dense, so that cardboard is not visible. At the end of the work, the remaining tails extend through the ringlet to the opposite side, then they are tied with a thread of the same color and cut to the desired length. The result is an original hat that will serve as an excellent decoration for the Christmas tree.

  • cardboard box;
  • threads

Work stages

How to make a voluminous toy?

A voluminous toy will always be an interesting craft, then we will talk about how to make something similar. Here, preference is best given to mouline thread, which have a large number of colors. There is nothing complicated in the work, the main thing is to take care of the following materials in advance:

  • balloons;
  • clerical glue;
  • threads.

Step by Step Actions

1. Inflate the balls to the size that the toy is planned. Due to the fact that in stores you can buy balls of any shape, a large scope of additional ideas for crafts opens up.

2. The surface is coated with glue so that the liquid is evenly distributed throughout the ball. Or you can pierce the container with glue and pull the threads through the hole, where they will be impregnated with an adhesive substance.

3. Threads are wound around the ball in random order. It is recommended to use a colorless grade of glue so that when it dries, it does not change the color of the threads. Before you play the toy further, you need to wait until the threads dry, it takes about 24 hours.

4. At the last stage, you need to pierce the ball and pull it out.

All of these steps are required to make this or that volumetric toy. Everything else depends on the master. The resulting balls can be used as decorative elements.

But to make a charming snowman, just fasten 3 balls together, draw a face, attach a nose. As a headdress, a half from a plastic egg from kinder surprise is quite suitable.
