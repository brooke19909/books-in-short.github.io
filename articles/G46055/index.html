Night Club "Provincial" in Smolensk

Many people look forward to the weekend to have a good rest after a hard week. Someone spends time with a book on the couch, and some like more active forms of relaxation. Young people prefer to attend nightclubs. "Provincial" in Smolensk, the place in which everyone comes to dance and eat deliciously. All conditions have been created here in order to fully relax your body and soul. We will talk more about this place below.

Night Club "Provincial" in Smolensk

The institution opened its doors to visitors in 2004. For 14 years of work, the club has gained the trust of citizens. Many call him a favorite dance and concert venue.

For some time the club was closed, and in January 2010 it again opened the door for its guests, but in a new design. Now an incredible atmosphere reigns here, many say that the "Provincial" has become even more comfortable than before.

At the same time, up to one and a half thousand people can rest in the institution. For lovers of dancing - this is the perfect place. Foreign and domestic DJs often come here to perform. The club regularly hosts theme parties: from retro to electro. But the owners of the "Provincial" do not forget about those who want to enjoy live music. For all the time of work, almost all Russian artists performed here.

In addition to dancing, you can have a good dinner here. The institution prepares European cuisine. In the club you can hold a corporate event or order a banquet. Employees of the "Provincial" will help organize and hold a party at the highest level.

Some rules of the Gubernsky club in Smolensk

In order to make guests of the club feel comfortable, there is a list of rules for all visitors:

  1. Entrance to the club is always paid, the price can be found at the box office. Sometimes special promotions are held during which admission is free.
  2. Only people over 18 years old can be in the institution. Club guests must show a passport or other document confirming age, if necessary.
  3. For security, security guards may inspect things at the entrance to the Gubernsky club in Smolensk. If they refuse to inspect, they may not be allowed to enter the club.

Club guests are prohibited from:

  1. To bring drugs, any weapons and explosives to the club.
  2. Pass to the institution in dirty clothes, or in sportswear.
  3. Be aggressive towards other guests and staff.

These are the main points from the list of rules for visitors, in case of violation the administration of the Gubernsky club in Smolensk reserves the right to ask the visitor to leave.

Location and opening hours

Night dance and entertainment club can be found at: Marshal Zhukov street, 4, the city of Smolensk. Opening hours from 23.00 to 6.00, from Thursday to Saturday.

In the "Provincial" there is a place for everyone. All lovers of nightlife, music and dancing will get a lot of positive emotions here.
