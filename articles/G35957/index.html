Halkidiki island: photos, description





Greece is an amazing country. Here you can relax easily and naturally, taking great pleasure in nature and local flavor. Halkidiki (photo of this island you can see in our article) is the divine, enchanting place of Greece. It is especially good for romantic tours, travel and family pastime. There are sandy or pebble beaches on the 500-kilometer coastline.

You can choose the best option for yourself. Many of these beaches have been awarded the Blue Flag nomination, which indicates high environmental cleanliness of the water, safe swimming and the proper organization of beach areas.

As for nature, it is as beautiful as everything else on the island of Halkidiki. Photos are confirmation of this fact. Guests are surrounded by lush, juicy, emerald greenery. For anyone, even the most demanding tourist, there is everything here to make the trip unforgettable.

Amazing Greece: Halkidiki

The photo of this place fascinates with its beauty. The outlines of the island are drawn in our imagination by the trident of Poseidon, each individual blade of which is a separate peninsula. There are several of them - Athos, Sithonia, Kassandra. The population of the entire island is about 92,000 people, the main city of Halkidiki is Polygyros. The landscape of the island is diverse. There are majestic mountains that occupy the most extensive territory: the highest point, by the way, is almost 1200 meters above the sea line. The mountains themselves are not rocky formations or stones, but a completely vibrant array of greenery. Magnificent plains and, of course, the saturated blue sea, where you can admire the small neat coves, will open to your attention.

Sithonia





The island of Halkidiki ... You should definitely capture a memory photo in the wild and natural environment, having visited the middle blade - the peninsula of Sithonia. It is less known to tourists and less populated by locals. Its beaches are cleaner and more beautiful compared to the beaches of Kassandra.

Athos

Athos is the third part of the whole island of Halkidiki. His photos are magnificent and mysterious. It is on its territory that Agion Oros is located - a monastic state. Only the stronger sex can get here. Women can see this part of the Greek trident from the side, being on a passing ship. The landscape of Athos is pristine, majestic and wild ...

Temperature and Climate

Tourists can be pleased with the fact that resort life begins in May and lasts all summer until September. The air warms up to +30 o C. In July and August, which are considered the hottest months, the temperature can rise to +35 o C. The climate is dry, so the lack of humidity makes it easy for tourists to tolerate heat. The mountains surrounding the peninsula prevent winds from blowing up the tourist area, which helps to stabilize the temperature of the water and calm the water. In summer, the average sea temperature is +26 o C.
