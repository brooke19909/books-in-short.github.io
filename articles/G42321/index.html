Account cash warrant. Form and order of filling.

The procedure for cash transactions in Russia is regulated the main instruction of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation for No. 40 of 1993. All cash transactions conducted are carried out on standard forms approved as primary documentation for all business enterprises.

At all enterprises, regardless of the form of ownership, warrants (incoming and outgoing) are used to account for cash transactions.

Account cash warrant is a strictly regulated form No. KO-2.

All cash documents are registered in the book of special accounting, as they relate to the primary financial documents.

An expense order, the form of which includes the following mandatory details: it contains the name of the business entity and its subdivisions, state codes of the enterprise, date and registration number, correspondent account and subaccount.

How to fill out an account cash warrant? Clarity of filling and faultlessness is very important. Errors and inaccuracies, corrections should not be found in cash documents. First of all, issuing money for cash register the order should not be carried out without two mandatory signatures of the managers (chief accountant, manager).
