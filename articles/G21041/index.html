Hotel "Long Beach", Turkey, Alanya: photos, reviews of tourists

Many tourists, choosing a place for a long-awaited vacation, try to take into account many factors. Some travelers prefer excursions, someone wants to soak up the sunshine on the seashore, and someone likes a rave nightlife. All these different facets of one trip are easily combined in one of the most popular resort cities in Turkey - Alanya.

Alanya and its merits

This small, but surprisingly picturesque town is located on a cozy peninsula, surrounded on one side by the Toros Mountains. Here, every vacationer, regardless of age, will find something to his liking.

This area is famous for its many ancient fortresses, ruins of castles and a number of educational museums. Here you can not only relax, but also expand your knowledge of history, as well as completely immerse yourself in ancient times. However, along with this, a number of fashionable hotel complexes will allow you to enjoy a long-awaited vacation.

Twelve months a year for guests of the city of Incekum, which is located 20 km from Alanya, the doors of the five-star Long Beach Resort hotel complex are open. He offers his guests to remember this journey for life. Elite apartments, high-level service and helpful staff, according to experienced, are the main advantages of this institution.

general characteristics

The doors of the wonderful Long Beach hotel in Turkey (photo in the article) swung open to accommodate the first guests in 2006. Over the years of its existence, he tirelessly improved both in terms of architecture and service. The last renovation was held in 2017. On a spacious territory of 75,000 square meters lies the main nine-story white building and a number of two-story villas. The main building is equipped with elevators for the comfort of guests. The chic garden area is equipped with seating areas. Lush palm trees, vibrant flowers and rich vegetation - this creates a wonderful atmosphere for a good mood.

There is a water park, numerous pools, a fitness center and mini-clubs. The hotel is perfect for a romantic weekend or for a family vacation. Those who have been here once dream of returning again, as noted in the reviews of the regulars.

General rules

As with most hotels in Turkey, pets are not allowed at Long Beach Resort Hotel & Spa. Smoking is not allowed throughout. Of the many rooms, 8 units are designed to accommodate people with disabilities. They are equipped with special devices so that this category of people can enjoy their vacation in full. Check-in time - standard - 14:00. But you should leave the apartment before noon. Payment for services is possible with bank cards. The language barrier with staff is easily removed thanks to its multilingualism.

Beach

Due to its good location, the beach can be reached in less than 5 minutes. To do this, go to the roadway. There is an underpass. The beach strip is covered with sand and pebble. The infrastructure is quite developed. Comfortable deckchairs with soft mattresses are located along the coast. For lovers of shade, you can take a place under an umbrella and enjoy the air baths on the Mediterranean shores.

For the safety of swimmers, a lifeguard service tirelessly watches. Showers and changing cabins are available. A beach towel counter is available to all guests at Long Beach Resort. Pontoon operates seasonally and depending on changes in the weather.

Children's leisure

Hotel "Long Beach Resort" (Turkey, Alanya) is suitable for the implementation of family vacations, because due attention is paid to the organization of children's leisure. The doors of the mini-club are open for kids, where they can perform creative tasks, play board games and compete in sports. You can rent a stroller. In the fresh air there is a game complex with slides, swings and labyrinths. Kids can frolic in the lunapark, riding on the race track and train.
