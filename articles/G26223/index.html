Anapa bus station: location and schedule

Anapa is a famous resort in Russia. Thousands of tourists visit this city every year. You can get to Anapa by plane, train or bus.

The railway station is a transport terminal of a modern type. It is located three kilometers from the city. It has waiting rooms with comfortable seats, air conditioning. There are cafeterias and shops on site. Near the station there are bus stops and taxi rank.





Anapa bus station is owned by the structural unit of Kuban Passazhiravtoservis OJSC. The building was built back in Soviet times, but after the renovation, which was recently completed, acquired a modern look. It has a waiting room and there are automatic left-luggage offices. There is a bus station on the street. Red Army, 11.

Opposite the building is a parking lot for taxis and cars. At night, the bus station and ticket office do not work. At the same time, the waiting room is also closed.

Bus station Anapa located at the bus stop "Central Market. Bus station". You can get to this place from the city, Vityazevo and Pionersky Prospekt by shuttle bus. Bus number 128 stops at a stop on the side of ul. K. Solovyanoy. He carries passengers to Vityazevo. From the street Krasnoarmeyskaya there is a stop of city buses, which are moving in the opposite direction, towards the microdistricts of the city.





Anapa Bus Station is located near the city central grocery market. It is located to the left of the building. There are various grocery stores, malls of the clothing market, catering outlets. Behind the market is Krymskaya Street - the main street of Anapa. From this point, you can take a bus to any area or suburb.

Bus station Anapa It has such transport routes that allow passengers to get to any city in the Krasnodar Territory. Buses transport to other regions of Russia and Ukraine.

The schedule has daily flights, as well as flights that are operated only on odd and even numbers. Buses depart from the Anapa bus station, which is the starting point or transit. Kuban Passazhiravtoservis cooperates with various road carriers.

Ticket offices are located on the street. Red Army.





You can specify the time of bus departure or get other necessary information by calling the Anapa bus station.

The schedule of direct daily flights on the route Anapa - Krasnodar can be found by phone or on the Kuban Passazhiravtoservis website.

The bus leaves in this direction every hour, from 5:30 to 16:30. He is on the way 3 hours 30 minutes and arrives at the Krasnodar Central Bus Station.

Every day, through the Anap bus station (the schedule is quite simple to clarify), buses pass in the direction of Krasnodar, Armavir, Kislovodsk, Kropotkin, Rostov-on-Don, Stavropol and White Clay.

The timetable must be clarified on the eve of the trip, as the trucker reserves the right to make changes to intercity flights.
