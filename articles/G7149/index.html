Hotel Alan Xafira Deluxe Resort & Spa 5 *, Turkey, Alanya: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Every traveler knows that Alanya is one of the most popular Turkish resorts. It is here that the Alan Xafira Deluxe Resort Spa 5 * hotel is located, which is a large, luxurious complex where guests are offered accommodation in comfortable rooms, good food and a lot of ways to have fun. Naturally, many people planning to stay here are looking for any additional information about the hotel.

Where is the hotel located?





Hotel Alan Xafira Deluxe Resort Spa 5 * stands out for its favorable location. It is located in a picturesque resort village called Turkler. About 5 km is another coastal town Konakli. The lively center of Alanya, with its shopping centers, markets and entertainment venues, is only 15 km away. The equally famous resort town of Antalya is 110 km away. It is worth saying that the nearest airport is in the city of Gazipasa, which is 60 km away. But most tourists arrive at Antalya Airport , which is about 100 km away. Naturally, travel agencies provide a transfer from the airport, but you should prepare for a long trip.

Food scheme: what does the hotel offer?





The all-inclusive system is what Turkey in most cases offers to tourists. Alan Xafira Deluxe Resort Spa 5 * is no exception to the rule - guests here are also provided with good food. Three times a day, guests are invited to visit the main restaurant, where a buffet is served for them. The choice of dishes is large, there is even a special diet menu. Late breakfasts and dinners are also available. In local bars (there are six of them) you can get drinks of local production (for imported products you need to pay separately). Where is the beach located?

The hotel complex Alan Xafira Deluxe Resort Spa 5 * (Turkler) owns its own section of the beach, which is located about 650 meters. A road leads to the seashore - the distance can be covered in a few minutes. In addition, shuttle bass regularly runs from the territory.





Guests can use sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas for free. Here you can get clean beach towels. There is a bar on the shore where tourists can have a snack and refresh themselves with a chilled drink. The sea here is clean and warm. Of course, travelers have the opportunity to engage in more active types of recreation, including boating, boats, catamarans, banana, water skiing, windsurfing lessons, sailing , etc. These services cost not too much.

There really are conditions for a pleasant stay. Laundry facilities are available for guests to take on. A doctor is constantly working at the hotel, who can be contacted in case of health problems. There is also a spacious conference room, which hosts seminars, meetings, business and holiday events, the organization of which, incidentally, is handled by the hotel staff themselves.

How are tourists having fun? Leisure of guests





The hotel has a lot of ways to have a great time - no one will be bored here. To begin with, it is worth saying that there is a large swimming pool, and two "paddling pools" are equipped for children. Morning exercises are held here, as well as aqua aerobics classes that tone and help improve health. In the evening and in the cold season, an indoor pool works, the water in which is constantly heated. In the hotel's aqua park you can ride on one of the six water slides, which is also popular with many vacationers.

Fans of more active recreation will also not be disappointed, as the hotel has a gym and several sports fields where you can play volleyball, basketball and futsal. There is a paved tennis court (everything you need for a game can be rented nearby). You can also have a good time playing table tennis, billiards, darts.

The hotel has a good SPA center, where a real Turkish bath, a sauna, a jacuzzi and several massage rooms are constantly working. Customers are offered a lot of wellness and anti-aging treatments.

And children can safely be left in the mini-club - the guys here really have a really good time. By the way, on request you can also get a baby chair in the restaurant, a crib in your room, and if necessary, hire a nanny. There is also a good playground with swings and slides.

The animation in the hotel is at a decent level - various entertaining contests are held throughout the day, and in the evenings all guests are invited to interesting performances and shows. And in the evening, discos are held with fun music. Of course, tourists can use the services of a tour desk, because Turkey is a country where there really is something to see.
