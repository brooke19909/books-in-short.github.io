Orient Hotels Palace Family 5 * - one of the best family hotels in Alanya

Description of the hotel. The Turkish Riviera gave travelers a delightful stay. Every year, hundreds of thousands of tourists flock to this fantastic corner to enjoy the warmth of the gentle sun, the splash of blue waves and all-inclusive hotels.

One of the most popular Turkish resorts is considered Alanya. Here, a traveler can feel himself in a completely different world: bright natural colors surrounding temperamental Turkish buildings, seasoned with national colors and sprinkled with syrup of singing speech - all this creates a fantastic harmony, which is so lacking in the noisy stone jungle.





Here, every traveler will find a hotel according to his needs and possibilities. Starting with two-star hotels, which also provide excellent leisure facilities, Alanya is replete with top-notch hotels. For example, Orient Hotels Palace Family 5 *, located just a few kilometers from the resort center. This elegant all-inclusive establishment consists of one main building and three additional buildings. Built in 2004, this hotel has already gone through one major overhaul, during which not only the internal layout of the building, but also its exterior has changed.

The rooms. In total, the hotel has 240 rooms. Of these, almost 80% are located with sea views. Designed for a number of two to eight people, the apartments are divided into standard one-room, two-room and three-room family-type rooms, and two-story five-room penthouses. The main segment that this institution is focused on is couples with children. After all, no wonder the Orient Hotels Palace Family 5 * is called family. The standard rooms are equipped with a bathtub, while others offer comfortable shower rooms for the clients. Individual air conditioning, televisions with Russian programs, minibar, balcony, comfortable beds and dressing rooms - these are the amenities that the hotel offers travelers.



Nutrition. Like many hotels in this category, Orient Hotels Palace Family 5 * practices all-inclusive meals. At the same time, customers can also choose a diet or vegetarian menu. Breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner, late dinner and all this according to the "buffet" - the tourist can only choose a convenient time to visit.

Beach. Of course, one cannot help but like the fact of having your own beach, located 800 meters from the main building of the hotel. Smooth gray pebbles washed by the warm turquoise waters of the Mediterranean Sea, soft gentle caresses of the Turkish sun - which could be better for a lazy beach holiday. The undoubted attribute of any beach belonging to a hotel of this type, such as Orient Palace Hotels resorts, is the presence of sun loungers, umbrellas, towels, mattresses and a great bar. Starting at 8 a.m., a bus starts taking from the main building of the hotel, taking people to the beach and back. At the same time, the traffic frequency is 2 times per hour.



Additional information. Neither adults nor children will have to be bored: outdoor and indoor pools equipped with water slides, a sauna, a bathhouse, massage and a jacuzzi - there is something to have fun. For active sports guests are offered a tennis court, billiards, table tennis and a gym. Young visitors to Orient Hotels Palace Family 5 * will enjoy the children's pool and playground. While parents enjoy their all-inclusive holiday, the babysitter will look after the kids.

Various animations of Orient Hotels Palace Family 5 * will stir up any traveler, even the most lazy in the heat. Incendiary melodies of a local disco will help clients relax and fully plunge into heady dance rhythms.

Reviews Located on a hilly area that offers travelers a magnificent panorama of the Mediterranean Sea, offering luxurious service coupled with friendly staff and an all-inclusive system - this is Orient Hotels Palace Family 5 *. Reviews of tourists who have visited this place vary, as do tastes. Most travelers are satisfied with the location of the hotel, and its services, and service. Some are not happy with hilly terrain: older people and people with a sedentary lifestyle are not ready for constant descents and ascents. Also, those customers who want to get to this hotel in order to have fun at night will be disappointed - this place is more suitable for couples and a relaxing holiday. On average, a rating on a five-point scale ranges from 4. But, one way or another, a complete picture of the hotel can be made only by visiting it.
