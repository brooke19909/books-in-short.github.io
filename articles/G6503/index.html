How AirPods Work: Description and Feature

In 2016, Apple pleased its fans with a new gadget - the AirPods wireless miniature headphones. They immediately gained popularity with a large part of consumers, since they are unique in some way. Their main advantage is that now they do not need to be connected to a phone or other device, that is, with them the consumer received the desired mobility. However, the new gadget has other advantages. Consider how they work, what devices are connected to, how to properly configure them.

How AirPods Work

It‚Äôs worth starting with the principles of operation of Apple branded headphones, the technologies involved in them, as well as the capabilities of the gadget. From a technical point of view, AirPods are not much different from other wireless headphones. To transfer data and connect using the Bluetooth protocol.

This is a standardized technology that works in all such devices. The difference is that AirPods use not only Bluetooth, but also a Wi-Fi connection on a smartphone and tablet to synchronize data with various players, whether it be a computer or a phone. AirPods also differ from their competitors in instant automatic connection. As soon as you open the case from the headphones, they will immediately try to connect to the nearest iPhone or iPad.

You can use tapping to control the headphones. By default, two taps to AirPods activate Siri, but this can be changed in the settings. You can make double-tap to pause the music or video or to include the next track in the playlist. You can assign different actions to different headphones.

"Filling"

How AirPods work is understandable. The technology is the same as 10 years ago. What makes them special? The most important thing about the gadget is its hardware along with software. AirPods are equipped with a W1 processor, which opens up new possibilities for the headphone owner.

First of all, the processor helps maintain a stable connection. Unlike many other Bluetooth headphone models, AirPods are much more stable and rarely disconnect from your phone or computer.

Also, the processor has a positive effect on their efficiency: operating time from one charge and the connection distance. The distance at which AirPods wireless headphones work can be considered a truly important advantage of the gadget. An Apple device keeps an active connection up to 10 meters away.

Construction and appearance

Earphones AirPods visually almost no different from EarPods. These are the same inserts that were presented at the 2012 conference. Of the differences, in addition to the lack of wires, we can note only additional holes in the body of each earphone. They are necessary so that an increased air flow leaves them. This has a positive effect on sound quality.

At the same time, like 6 years ago, AirPods differ from other similar devices in a bizarre (at first glance) form. According to Apple, its engineers have been studying various forms of the auricle for a long time to create something that is most versatile and convenient. That is how the design of EarPods was born, which AirPods inherited. Therefore, wireless headphones are also lightweight, comfortable and cute.

Battery and recharge

Choosing headphones and considering an option from Apple, you need to think not only about how AirPods work. Wireless headphones should work long enough so as not to cause discomfort.

Nobody wants to charge them constantly, therefore the most ‚Äúlong-playing‚Äù options are needed. Apple Headphones are a real engineering breakthrough. This tiny gadget can work up to 5 hours in music listening mode. How many AirPods actually work is hard to say.

It all depends on the sound reproduction volume and battery wear. Apple tested the device at a volume of 50%. If you love louder, you have to come to terms with the fact that AirPods will work an hour less than the stated time.

Also, autonomy may decrease after prolonged use. A year later, they will be discharged faster than in the first days after purchase.

In talk mode, the headphones work for about 2 hours. If you need to extend communication, you can use one earphone, and then the second. AirPods work separately. Therefore, one of the headphones can be charged while the other is in use.

Complete with inserts there is a special charging case. With it, you can extend the working time up to 24 hours. The case itself can be recharged with a standard Lightning cable, with which you charge your iPhone and iPad.

How to set up AirPods

Apple hates instructions, suggesting that their devices are already fairly easy to use. Therefore, you will not find it bundled with AirPods. Actually, you really will not need it. The setup process is extremely simple. The only thing that can stop you is marriage.

So, to connect the headphones to the "iPhone", you need:

  • Turn on Bluetooth and Wi-Fi on your phone.
  • Open the case from the headphones.
  • Wait until a notification appears on the phone screen that the headphones are configured and ready to use.

In rare cases, the initial setup fails, so you have to do it again. Fortunately, this is pretty easy. You need to hold down the key on the back of the AirPods case and hold it for 10 seconds.

To connect your headphones to a Mac, you need to:

  • Open the Bluetooth settings in the system settings.
  • Find in your AirPods device list.
  • Choose them.
  • Launch iTunes Player.
  • Press the AirPlay button.
  • Select AirPods from the list of devices.

To connect the headphones to the Android-smartphone, you need:

  • Go to your phone settings.
  • Select the Bluetooth submenu.
  • Get AirPods out of the case.
  • Find them in the list on the phone and select to connect.

After that, you can turn on the music and listen. It is important to note that to monitor the charge level and the operation of some other functions of the headphones, you will have to download a special application.

What devices do AirPods work with?

Despite the fact that it is more convenient to use AirPods with the iPhone and iPad, they remain Bluetooth headphones. This means that they can be used with any smartphone and computer that supports Bluetooth.

That is, both Android and Windows are in order. You can connect an Apple gadget to either system. Some functions, for example, automatic connection, will not work, but the sound will not get worse. Therefore, AirPods may well be considered for purchase.

It is important to note that there is a restriction on connecting to some models of Apple smartphones. Therefore, the question arises as to which iPhones AirPods work with. According to the manufacturer, you need to have any phone that supports the iOS 10 operating system. These are all models starting from 5S.
