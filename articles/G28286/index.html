An unforgettable trip to Nizhny Novgorod

The history of a beautiful city called Nizhny Novgorod dates back to the distant 1221, when it was founded by George the Second - the prince from Suzdal, who is the grandson of Yuri Dolgoruky himself.





The active development of the city since then has led to the fact that today it is one of the most important industrial and economic centers of Russia. It is located just 400 kilometers from Moscow, at the very confluence of two magnificent rivers: the Oka and Volga.

Various cruises along these rivers pass through the city, which allows tourists to see Nizhny Novgorod from very favorable sides. But just coming here by car, train or plane is also a great pleasure, because there really is something to see.

Interesting places in Nizhny Novgorod

A huge number of historical monuments, which are magnificent examples of stone and wooden architecture, and beautiful modern buildings are sure to become the highlight of any route among tourists interested in architecture.
