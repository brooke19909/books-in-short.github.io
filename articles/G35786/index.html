Beautiful crochet topics: patterns for women

If you are just starting to master the hook, it is best to hone your skills on tops. They can look like T-shirts with narrow or wide straps, tied around the neck or back, like swimsuits, look like classic T-shirts with a cropped bottom, or look like ‚Äúbat‚Äù blouses. Basic rules for knitting tops

For work, you will need measurements of the length of the neckline and the product from the shoulder to the armpit, from the armpit to the waist, grasping the waist, chest, arm, neck. Depending on the model of the top, certain measures fall out.

For summer models, take cotton yarn, a thin hook (No. 1-2.5). Choose the color scheme taking into account clothes with which you will wear a topic. Before work, knit a sample with different hooks, change the yarn to choose the best option. Next, take measurements, wash and compare the results.

A crocheted female topic can be made without patterns if you knit on yourself. To do this, start work from the bottom of the product. For elastic, choose a dense pattern (from 2 centimeters). Measure it with your waist, chest and only then close it in the ring.

Now knit in a circle, periodically trying on yourself. Having reached the armpit, mark with needles on the product in front and the back of the top. In the same way, determine the neck and length of the straps. The last step is to tie the neck, armpit with ordinary single crochet or pico columns (three air loops with one base). If you knit the model on another person, then draw patterns.

Top of square motifs

Knitters often resort to products from motives: beginners do not get tired of work, and professionals can knit elements even in traffic jams. Consider the steps of creating a top from square motifs.



To begin, draw a full-length model pattern, determining the length of the product, the straps, neckline and waist circumference. Next, connect the motive, conduct preparatory work with it, measure the dimensions and calculate how many elements are needed for the top. Pencil note their location.

If you have a small space, scatter the obtained centimeters for all reasons, that is, knit the pattern in one more row. If, on the contrary, the elements protrude a few centimeters, then you need to replace the yarn, take a hook with a lower number or reduce the number of rows.

In our version, you need fifty-two squares to crochet a summer topic for women. Choose small motive schemes, then the product will be more elegant. The element we have chosen is suitable for women of any complexion.

Openwork crochet topics: schemes for women

The bottom of the canvas can be decorated with ordinary arches from air loops. To make the product not look like a simple grid, alternate arches with filled columns. For example, every two arches with 7 loops, knit nine double crochets.

In the next row above the posts will be three empty arches. The columns are staggered. The penultimate row is decorated with arches from the columns, and in the last row you knit pico.
