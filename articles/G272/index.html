Tropical Princess Beach Resort & SPA 3 * (Dominican Republic / Punta Cana): description

Dominican Republic is a small island state that attracts Russian tourists with its climatic conditions, as well as a host of attractions and hotels that offer ideal conditions for living and recreation. Tropical Princess Beach Resort & SPA is one such hotel. Next, we consider the main features of living in it, the level of service, as well as a list of the main infrastructure facilities located on its territory, which make the pastime of tourists in this corner more pleasant and interesting.

Total information

Tropical Princess Beach Resort is a place that tour operators often recommend for holiday lovers of outdoor activities. According to most guests, there are all conditions for living with children, as well as for those who wish to spend their time with their loved one.

The hotel was founded in 2010. To date, several reconstructions have been carried out on its territory, the implementation of which has allowed to preserve the freshness of the appearance of both the buildings of the five buildings, of which the Tropical Princess Beach Resort consists, and its territory, presented in the form of a garden and recreation areas, as well as for entertainment. The area occupied by the hotel is quite large. That is why, to ensure the comfortable movement of vacationers around the hotel, there is a slow-moving train operating at any time of the day, on which you can get to any place free of charge. Reviews of the hotel in question very often say that riding on this train is a favorite pastime for children.

Currently, the rating of the place in question is quite high. Tropical Princess Beach guests rate their stay here at 8 points out of 10, and also leave a lot of positive reviews about this resort corner, according to which we can draw the appropriate conclusions.

Location

The vacation spot is located in the Dominican Republic, in the world famous resort region of Punta Cana.

The hotel in question is located within walking distance from the coastline, which, of course, is to the taste of most tourists from Russia and other countries of the world. Relatively close to it is the international airport - literally 35 km (Punta Cana).

In the vicinity of the Tropical Princess Beach Resort there are other hotels that are no less popular among Russians: Paradisus Punta Cana and Punta Cana Princess.

Rooms

The hotel has five large buildings, in the buildings of which the entire number of rooms is located. In total, the Tropical Princess Beach Resort (Dominican Republic) has 310 apartments, which, depending on the degree of comfort arranged in them, are divided into two types: Superior and Family.

No matter which group the room belongs to, each of them has a well-equipped bathroom, equipped with furniture, plumbing and bath sets, designed for each guest.

Staying in the apartments of the hotel in question, guests have the opportunity to use paid telephone services, as well as an electronic safe, for the rental of which you must pay by the day. Tropical Princess Beach Resort guests can enjoy free drinks offered in the minibar, the range of which is updated every day.

In the negative reviews about the hotel you can often find the opinions of tourists that their apartments do not provide the possibility of using Wi-Fi.

Superior Apartment

Considering the features of the hotel room stock, you should definitely pay attention to the arrangement of Superior rooms, which are designed to accommodate a couple of tourists.

The apartments of this type have modern furniture necessary for rest and sleep. For guests there is a large king-size bed, covered with high-quality linen, which is changed every day.

Of the furniture in the Superior rooms, there is a large wardrobe designed to store clothes, as well as a pair of bedside tables. In their free time, hotel guests can watch programs on the large plasma screen that hangs on the wall.

In reviews of Tropical Princess Beach Resort you can often find the opinions of tourists that Superior rooms can be very comfortable. Many of the former guests admit that in the mornings you can sit on the balcony, have a cup of coffee and enjoy the views of the surrounding surroundings and the beautiful flowering garden.

Conditions for children

As noted above, the Tropical Princess Beach Resort 4 * hotel in the Dominican Republic is a great place for a family vacation. Such allegations are connected with the fact that on its territory, as well as in the buildings, excellent conditions are provided for living not only for adult tourists, but also for children.

On the territory of the hotel in question there is a mini-club in which kids can frolic and spend their time playing educational games in the company of their peers. Moreover, on the territory of the Tropical Princess Beach Resort there is a playground for active children's recreation, and in the aquazone there are two small pools, equipped with all the requirements for the safety of children.

If necessary, a cot can be installed in any room. As for local catering establishments, they offer not only a separate useful children's menu, but also comfortable places to accommodate the smallest tourists.

In hotel reviews, you can often find recommendations from tourists regarding the use of babysitting services. They say that when you pay 10-15 dollars per hour, you can provide your child with a decent supervision, which is carried out by highly qualified specialists in the field of parenting.

Nutrition

In Tropical Princess Beach Resort & SPA, according to vacationers, the food system is very well developed. Here, on site, there are several restaurants offering deliciously prepared dishes of Dominican, European and Mediterranean cuisine. Moreover, at any time of the day, hotel guests have the opportunity to visit local bars, which function both in the buildings of the recreational area and in the aquazone area.

Dining at Tropical Princess Beach Resort is all-inclusive, which operates around the clock. Breakfast and dinner in the main restaurant are presented in the form of a buffet, where guests can enjoy delicious snacks, desserts and the best meat dishes cooked over an open fire.

Entertainment

Guests of the Tropical Princess Beach Resort & SPA 4 * hotel (Dominican Republic), while on vacation, pay special attention to the entertainment that is offered at the hotel, and, as you know, there are many of them.

The reviews of the hotel in question say that, being here, you should definitely take the opportunity to visit the disco, which can be done exclusively on a fee basis. Also, on fans, a lot of recreational activities are held outdoors by the pool and on the beach.

Fans of gambling have the opportunity to visit the Avalon Casino, which is equipped in one of the buildings of the Tropical Princess Beach Resort & SPA 4 *.

Fans of outdoor activities, relaxing in the Dominican hotel, can attend several paid surfing or diving classes, during which the most inquisitive travelers manage to gain new knowledge and get a lot of unforgettable experiences.

Aquazone & Spa

In reviews of the hotel in question, one can often find a lot of positive comments about the local spa. Here, according to hotel guests, you can truly relax and enjoy your stay in an exotic country. Tropical Princess Beach Resort 4 * Spa offers paid massage services, as well as a wide variety of beauty treatments. Moreover, it has an excellent thermal zone, represented by a combination of a hammam, a jacuzzi and a pool, where you can cool off after hot procedures.

In the open area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel there is a stunning aquazone, which is to the taste of all guests. It is represented by the perfect combination of two fairly deep pools and an open terrace with a bar and a huge number of places for sunbathing. Hotel guests note that the water in the niches is constantly cleaned, and its temperature is maintained at the proper level.

Infrastructure

According to most vacationers, the hotel in question is very well developed infrastructure. Without leaving its territory, vacationers can rent a car, book an excursion to local attractions, and also use the services of a doctor - all of them, starting from a standard examination and ending with any manipulations, are paid.

A laundry service, an internet corner and a small beauty salon are constantly on site. Moreover, ATMs and a currency exchange office are installed on the ground floor of one of the buildings of the resting place. There is also an agency for organizing wedding ceremonies, the holding of which in the Dominican Republic has recently become quite a fashionable trend among Russians.

The hotel building has a large conference room equipped with everything necessary for meetings with business partners, as well as conferences and presentations. Its total area is 317 square meters. m

The hotel building has an excellent gym, which can be visited at any time of the day or night, and absolutely free of charge.

Beach

Among the guests of the Tropical Princess Beach Resort & SPA in the Dominican Republic there are many who love beach holidays. In reviews of the beach of the place in question, quite often you can find a lot of enthusiastic comments about spending time on the coastline washed by the waters of the Atlantic Ocean. They say that it is here that you can do various extreme sports, moreover, under the guidance of experienced instructors. For a fee, you can go diving here.

Tropical Princess Beach is within walking distance of the hotel. Its area is completely covered with fine and clean sand, staying on which is completely safe for young children. Tourists who rested here with their kids often note in their comments that the entrance to the ocean is rather shallow, due to which the water temperature is always maintained at a high level - about +26 degrees.

Arriving at the beach, vacationers can take advantage of free sun loungers arranged under huge umbrellas. Anyone here has the opportunity to use a towel, which is also issued free of charge.

A beach bar is constantly operating on the beach, and there is also a large volleyball and indoor soccer field.
