Hotel Semiramis Village 4 * (Hersonissos, Crete): description, services, reviews

Semiramis Village 4 * (Hersonissos) is located in a very beautiful place. But this is not the only advantage of this hotel. After all, every second hotel in Greece is located on the beach, by the sea or in some other picturesque place. But this complex has its advantages.





Briefly about the hotel

Semiramis Village 4 * (Hersonissos) was built in 1993. The last restoration was carried out in 2007, because the hotel looks quite modern. The hotel's location is advantageous - very close to the sea, you can walk to the beach in a few minutes. True, the hotel rises on a hill. This is both a plus and a minus. The good thing is that due to this particular arrangement of the building, the rooms offer stunning panoramic sea views. And the minus is that for many, the descent to the beach seems tedious and long. In particular, the elderly, visitors with children and just those who do not like to walk a lot. However, you can find out about this in advance and not go to the hotel.

But the airport is located nearby - only 30 kilometers away. He is in Heraklion. And literally 5 minutes from here you can drive to the center of Hersonissos, which, incidentally, is very loved by young people. After all, there are many different night clubs, discos and restaurants. So in the evening life in Hersonissos is just beginning.





Service

The Semiramis Village 4 * (Hersonissos) has everything you need for a comfortable and carefree vacation. Free Wi-Fi is available on site, there is a private car parking. By the way, if guests wish, they can rent a car right on the spot - the administration provides such an opportunity. They also have bike rental available. Many people like to drive to local attractions on this vehicle.

The hotel also provides safes for storing things. And to make it more comfortable for guests to get to the upper floors, an elevator has been created for them.

By the way, the hotel can carry out individual check-in. The reception is open around the clock, because this service, if available and prepared for living, can be provided. The staff speaks English and Greek - you also need to know about this. Although, according to the guests, some employees understand Russian and speak a little of it. Most likely due to the fact that recently the influx of tourists from Russia has increased.

Leisure

Being in Semiramis Village 4 * (Hersonissos), you can entertain yourself in many ways. The main one, of course, is a trip to the sea. But many more enjoy spending time by the pool or sunbathing on the terrace.

There is also a hot tub (i.e. jacuzzi), billiards room, table tennis and massage room. And people who like an active lifestyle will like the gym, which has all the necessary equipment for training. By the way, there is also a full-fledged tennis court. You can also play darts or play bridge. The hotel also has an internet corner and a TV room.

By the way, people who came to the Semiramis Village Hotel 4 * with children can be calm for them - the kids will not be bored. For them, a separate pool and playground are organized here. And they are engaged in professional nannies.





About nutrition

The Semiramis Village Hotel 4 * has an excellent main restaurant, serving its visitors on a buffet basis. Breakfast starts at 7:30 and ends at 9:30. In the morning, guests can enjoy pancakes, try cottage cheese, sausages, eggs, vegetables, pancakes and much more. There are various delicious dishes, but no cereals.

Lunch starts at 12:30, in the same place, and ends at 14:00. Dinner is available from 19:00, but the restaurant closes at 21:30. The kitchen is very diverse. There is meat, barbecue, fish, grill. You can try beef, pork, chicken, turkey, lamb. And in any case - fried, stewed, steamed. Drinks are also presented in a good variety. In addition to water and tea with coffee, there is also white and red wine (homemade), rakia, ouzo and local brandy. Soft draft beer, juices and cold drinks are also served.

From 17:00 to 18:00, guests can enjoy tea or coffee and treat themselves to pastries. In addition to the restaurant, the hotel still has several bars, it is also worth a look there. By the way, according to several options they can serve guests of Semiramis Village 4 *. Breakfast and dinner is half board. There is also an all-inclusive. And some just order breakfast. However, whoever wants what. In any case, nearby, on the Embankment there are a lot of all kinds of taverns and cafes - they also serve good dishes.





Rooms

People planning to stay at Semiramis Village 4 * (Greece) can book any of the unoccupied. Standard 2-bed apartments are very popular. Their living area is 25 square meters. m, and they have one double bed. Inside there is air conditioning and access to a private furnished balcony. It offers amazing views of the Aegean Sea.

The room is beautifully decorated, everything is done in warm pleasant colors. Inside there is a bathroom with a toilet and a hairdryer, and there is also air conditioning. A mirror, a TV, a tall and wide nightstand for clothes, nightlights and lamps - everything you need for comfort is inside.

About 40,000 rubles will have to pay two guests for a weekly stay in such a room. By the way, this price includes the all-inclusive service. If you only need breakfast, then the price will drop to 20-22 tons. The exact cost depends on the tour operator, season, availability / absence of discounts and other nuances.

Option for three

The apartments for 3 guests differ from the above only in furnishings. Inside, in addition to a 2-sleeping bed, there is also one standard. Although guests can choose. If they want, they will be provided with a room with three twin beds. Other facilities and conditions are the same. Room with all-inclusive service for three guests will cost 50 tons. If you order only breakfast, you will have to pay less - about 27 tons.

Guests also have the opportunity to reserve apartments with a personal safe, fridge, plasma TV and satellite channels. But this is already available for a fee, since in Semiramis Village 4 * (Greece), such numbers are considered an improved option.





About accommodation

Check-in at the hotel starts at 14:00, and guests must check out before noon. However, individual registration is possible, as discussed earlier.

In Semiramis Village 4 * (Crete) you can live with children of any age. If people came to rest with a baby (up to two years), then they will provide him with a cot. And for the stay of the child does not have to pay extra. Free and stay for those children whose age is less than 12 years. But they cannot provide an extra bed. The child will have to sleep in the places available in the room.

By the way, even in the hotel Semiramis Village 4 * (Crete) is allowed to come with animals. Only a person planning to take a pet with him should notify the administration in advance. The animal accommodation service is free, but you must contact the property.

When making a remote reservation, you will need to provide your credit card details. The hotel accepts Master Card and Visa. Some of the funds may be blocked as a guarantee of the arrival of travelers. When you check in, by the way, you will need to provide a card.

Guests staying at the hotel

Semiramis Village 4 * reviews get mostly good. People who rested in this hotel, first of all, advise it to those who want a budget vacation with the all-inclusive service and in a picturesque place. But, since the complex is located on a mountain, it is not suitable for everyone.

There is a good, clean sea. There are paid sunbeds on the beaches, but tourists claim that you can do without them - a spread towel will be enough. The beach is clean, they clean it every day, there is no garbage, the sand is pleasant. Sunset at sea is also successful. The cleaning of the rooms themselves is also not bad. The apartments are cleaned daily and towels are changed to clean.

Still pleased with the location. The hotel is located in a fairly secluded place, but there is a bus stop nearby where buses run regularly in different directions. So getting to the sights or entertainment is not difficult. By the way, you can drive to the water park in five minutes. In general, car rental is quite modest, you can afford a car.

It is also worth noting that smoking is allowed on the territory of this hotel and in the rooms. But cigarette prices are very high, you need to take this fact into account.
