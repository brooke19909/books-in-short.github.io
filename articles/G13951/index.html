Restaurant "Rest" in Podolsk: menu, restaurant features and atmosphere

Rest Restaurant in Podolsk is a great place for holding business meetings, New Year's holidays, gatherings with friends and even romantic dinners. The name of the institution justifies the atmosphere prevailing in the institution. Restaurant "Rest" in Podolsk, reviews of which are extremely positive, is located in a picturesque place of the city, so it is possible to relax and enjoy communicating with loved ones.





Services

The Rest Restaurant in Podolsk invites guests to celebrate family celebrations in a cozy and friendly atmosphere. For weddings, corporate parties, receptions, graduations, birthdays and just family events, a specially composed menu is available. Quite often, bright and dance show programs are organized for guests, as well as talented music performers.





Kitchen

The Rest Restaurant in Podolsk serves European, Italian and Caucasian dishes. Institutional guests are served:

  • side dishes (french fries and boiled, rice, grilled vegetables, mashed potatoes);
  • hot dishes (medallions, pike cutlets, trout with vegetables, steak and tartar from marbled beef, baked salmon with peaches, steak, etc.);
  • BBQ (chicken wings, pork skewers and ribs, potatoes, dorado, sea bass, salmon, lamb, chicken, kebab kebabs served with pita bread and sauces);
  • salads (with shrimp, "Caesar", with smoked eel, meat, "Greek", with squid and other seafood);
  • hot snacks (shrimp and squid, julienne, baked eggplant, suluguni with lingonberry sauce);
  • cold appetizers (fish and meat assortment, cheese plateau, assortment of smoked meats, herring with mustard sauce, vegetable bouquet);
  • soups (hodgepodge, mushroom cream soup, soup with homemade noodles and chicken, tom yam, borsch);
  • desserts (Millefeuille, strudel with cherry or apple, truffle, chocolate cake, chocolate fondue, ice cream and fruit).




Restaurant Features

  • If desired, visitors can try their singing talents in karaoke.
  • In the warm season, guests of the Rest Restaurant in Podolsk can visit the terrace, furnished with comfortable upholstered furniture in bright colors.
