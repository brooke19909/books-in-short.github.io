Hotel My Sea Hotels Incekum 4 * Turkey, Alanya: reviews

Okurcalar is the sunniest and hottest resort region of Turkey. Its coast is covered with large yellow sand, which is interspersed with pebbles.

In its open spaces reigns subtropical climate. Holidays in these places are a great choice for families with children. The lack of a developed entertainment industry guarantees peace and quiet.





Provincial chic

The resort has an abundance of hotels at various levels. There are multi-story premium hotels and budget facilities. The cost of living in this part of Turkey is considered the lowest on the Mediterranean coast. Okurcalar is a young but rapidly growing resort.

On the territory of the village is the construction of shopping and entertainment centers, shops, water parks and sports grounds. Twice a week, the city has a real oriental bazaar. Its counters are choked with local fruits. Turkish-made clothing and textiles are sold on the market square.

The municipality is 90 kilometers from Antalya International Airport. The nearest large settlement is Alanya. It is only 30 km away.
