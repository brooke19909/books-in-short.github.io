Park "Island of Dreams": description, features and interesting facts

According to the deputy mayor of Moscow for urban planning policy M. Khusnullin, the construction of an indoor amusement park, the largest not only in Russia but also in the world, will be completed by the beginning of 2018. Despite the current difficult situation, the investor does not stop funding, and the construction of the Children's Park "Dream Island" is proceeding at a good pace, the official said. According to preliminary forecasts, the launch of the facility is scheduled for the 1st half of the next (2018) year.

About the unique roof design

According to the authors of the project, the roof over the central square of the park, made in the form of a glass dome, is not afraid of snowfall, heat or rust.

Its area will be 9 thousand square meters. m, that is, about one and a half football stadiums. The dome will be the largest in the country. It will be built in the form of the most lightweight glazed mesh shell made of metal. The roof is assembled from many cast elements. To each of them are attached structurally supporting rods made in the form of beams diverging in different directions. In its form, the system is similar to a honeycomb. An aluminum profile and 10 cm thick tempered glass are attached to the mesh shell.

Such a roof is not afraid of frost, heat and corrosion. Under it, it will be warm throughout the year. Climatic installations are provided, which will prevent the accumulation of snow. To drain melt and rain water, the creation of special drainage gutters is provided. Purification of air from fire and smoke is possible through a special ventilation system and additional opening flaps arranged in the dome. Transport connection

It is known that near the project the opening of a new metro station Technopark has already taken place. It includes the construction of an elevated pedestrian crossing equipped with travelators, from Art. metro to the Dream Island theme park through Ave. Andropova. In the future, they are going to connect the Nagatinskaya floodplain with the 2nd South Port Passage. An automobile bridge up to 600 m long will be erected here.

In the future, they plan to build the Avtozavodskaya station on the Moscow Ring Railway (MKZHD) next to the park. The plans also include the construction of a road that can connect Ave. Andropov with the Moskva River, and a bridge for connecting to the Warsaw highway. Underground parking and flat parking are under construction for cars.

Investors

The first indoor park "Island of Dreams" is estimated by experts at $ 1.5 billion. The facility is fully funded by the REGIONS Group of Companies. The implementation of the thematic part is ensured by the conclusion of licensing agreements between REGIONS-Entertainment JSC, a member of REGIONS Group of Companies, and the largest animation and media companies that contain agreements on the use of popular animated brands in the implementation of the project.
