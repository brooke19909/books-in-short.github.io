Pension "Solar Valley" (Crimea, Olenevka): overview, features and reviews

In the westernmost part of the Crimean peninsula is the boarding house "Sunny Valley" in Olenevka. Tarkhankut - the cape on which the village is located, is an ecologically clean area. The boarding house is open on the territory of the estate, which in the XVIII century belonged to Count V.S. Popov.

The manor park and garden, preserved almost in its original form, delight with its beauty, and the snow-white manor house - with luxury. A magnificent old park, a well-kept sandy beach, clear sea water - are the pride of this guesthouse. "Sunny Valley" is a calm and very cozy place, which is suitable for people of different ages.



Why visit a boarding house

Having visited these marvelous places, you will appreciate a certain “detachment” of this place. You will get a unique opportunity to visit the protected areas Atlesh and Dzhangul - amazingly beautiful places that you have all seen. Yes, yes, it was here that the shooting of the films Amphibian Man and Pirates of the 20th Century took place.

Fans of outdoor activities like cyclists, divers, surfers, yoga lovers in the "Sunny Valley" in Olenevka. Tarkhankut in Crimea is an amazing land, with an unusually beautiful sea, which is surrounded by picturesque cliffs and spacious beaches.



Crimea, Olenevka, boarding house "Sunny Valley" invites

The boarding house occupies a huge steppe territory, next to the village of Olenevka. These picturesque places have original vegetation: in the spring the steppe changes its colors - at first it turns red due to flowering poppies, and later white and blue. This is a land with a long history, and the main building of the health resort is an architectural monument of the XVIII century.

Pension "Sunny Valley" has its own beach, located on Cape Tarhankut. The entrance to the water here is gentle and long, for which vacationers with children are especially appreciated. The beach, covered with fine clean sand, stretched for six hundred meters. It is equipped with awnings, changing cabins, in the summer cafe on its territory you can eat light snacks.

Vacationers have the opportunity to visit guided tours in historical places. This place is ideal for people who prefer a calm and quiet holiday on the sea coast. Here you can walk around the boarding house park for a long time or go on horseback riding, during which you will discover magnificent local landscapes.



The boarding house has a constantly operating medical room in which experienced specialists will provide the necessary assistance, and next to the beach there is an estuary with healing mud.

Conditions and cost of living

Pension "Solar Valley" in the Crimea at the same time can take about five hundred vacationers. For a comfortable stay, guests are offered rooms in the hospital buildings, in log cabins, in cottage villages, and even in the tent city.

"Castle"

This is the main building of the boarding house. An old mansion, previously owned by Count Popov (XVIII century). Today it houses 17 double, triple and quadruple rooms. The cost of living ranges from 1770 to 6500 rubles, depending on the season and the selected room.



"Nautical"

The five-story building is located on the seashore. It offers stunning views of the sea and the park. In its open area there is a heated pool. Vacationers are offered to settle in one of one hundred and three double and quadruple rooms with balconies. The cost of living in this building ranges from 1650 to 6500 rubles, depending on the time you visit the boarding house.



Cottage town

In the boarding house “Sunny Valley” in Olenevka you can settle right on the seashore, in the shady part of the park. A cottage town of the same name was built here. He can take fifty-eight vacationers in double and triple cottages. The rooms in them are designed for two persons, but if desired, they can be understaffed with another additional place. The cost of living is from 2700 to 5450 rubles.





"Sunny corner"

Another cottage town, surrounded by an alley of bright and fragrant flowers, along which a stream flows, filling the air with coolness. In this town there are 52 places located in double and quadruple rooms, with the possibility of installing additional space. The price of rooms is from 3250 to 5350 rubles per day.

Tarkhankut

The town is located in the depths of the park at a distance of 250 meters from the main building. On its green and well-groomed territory there are many flowers and a heated outdoor pool. The beach is very close (450 meters from the town). It can accommodate seventy-two guests in double and quadruple rooms. The cost of living is from 2 thousand to 5650 rubles.





Log Fellings

Twelve log cabins of superior comfort are located among cypresses and pines. One and two-story houses with all amenities are within walking distance from the beach. The cost of living is from 3,500 to 10,800 rubles.

All listed prices include breakfast for each guest. Rooms are cleaned daily. For younger guests up to four years, the cost of living is not charged: only meals are paid. Children over seven years of age receive a forty percent discount. Payment can be made in cash or by bank transfer.



Nutrition

Guests of the “Sunny Valley” boarding house can choose the option of three meals a day:

  • "Buffet";
  • custom menu (vegetarian or kosher);
  • integrated.

Mostly traditional Russian dishes are offered, but experienced chefs can diversify your diet with dishes from around the world. The assortment always contains fermented milk products, fresh vegetables and fruits, and pastry. For children there is a special menu. The boarding house has two cafes on open terraces - “South” and “Nautilus”, as well as a pizzeria.

Additional services

As you know, relaxing on the beach quickly gets bored, so the administration of the boarding house took care of the variety of leisure activities of vacationers, providing a number of additional services:

  • two heated outdoor pools;
  • bathhouse;
  • sauna;
  • playground;
  • gym;
  • dance hall;
  • billiard room;
  • cinema hall;
  • disco;
  • playground;
  • fleet;
  • bike rides;
  • horseback riding;
  • excursions.

How to get there

The boarding house is located at: Olenevka village, st. Eliseeva, 25. From Simferopol you should get to the village of Chernomorskoye by shuttle bus or regular bus. Then you should transfer to the bus next to Olenevka. In addition, you can order a transfer, which will cost you one and a half thousand rubles.

Guest reviews

Many visitors to the boarding house are confused by the rather long road to the resting place, but later they positively assess the privacy of this place. Most vacationers like a clean and well-groomed beach, the ability to choose a place to settle from several options, however, there are opinions that in season the cost of living is somewhat overpriced. Guests appreciate the varied food and excellent service of the friendly and helpful staff.
