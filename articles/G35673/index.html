Affortel Magnum Resorts 3 * Hotel Goa, India: overview, description and reviews of tourists

India attracts domestic tourists, including because there are both elite and budget places to stay. One of the most popular of the not too expensive resorts in this country is the city of Candolim. The village is small, but the tourist infrastructure here is developed just fine. Of course, there are hotels in this town that are convenient for tourists. One of the best, according to many guests of Candolim, is Affortel Magnum Resorts 3 *.

Location Features

The city of Candolim is located 15 km from Panaji, the capital of Goa. The most suitable time for a beach holiday here, as elsewhere in India, is the period from October to February. Buses run regularly from Panaji to Candolim. They leave from the station of the capital Goa every 20 minutes. You can get to Candolim from Panaji in just 15-20 minutes.

Some tourists who book rooms at the Affortel Magnum Resorts 3 * hotel get to the place and by taxi. It costs usually not too expensive. The cost of the trip from the airport is 810 rupees.





The location of the Affortel Magnum Resorts 3 * hotel itself is considered by most tourists to be very successful. It is located in close proximity to one of the busiest streets of the city - Candolim Beach Road. According to many guests, the hotel is a convenient starting point both to the south and to the north. General description of the hotel

This hotel is a modern four-story small building with balconies. The exterior, in fact, of the house itself, is rated by most tourists as very good. The walls of the building are painted in a pleasant green color, and the balconies are in blue and white. Such an organic combination of shades makes the facade of the hotel fresh and elegant. Tall palm trees grow in the courtyard next to the hotel. A solid large pool with clear blue water also gives a solid look to the area. The hotel courtyard is paved with beautiful neat paving tiles.

On some specialized sites, this hotel is classified as a two-star hotel. The range of services here is really not too big. But, since the service at this hotel is not bad, the furniture is new, and the equipment is working properly, it can nevertheless be considered a three-star with some stretch. Yes, in principle, this is not particularly important to which group the hotel belongs to. The main thing is that living in it, judging by the reviews of tourists, is quite convenient.

Each room consists of a small bedroom, a hall, a corridor, a kitchen and a bathroom. The rooms of this hotel have earned good reviews, including those that look like small apartments. Dining at the hotel

The hotel also has a restaurant on site. Here you can enjoy traditional Indian dishes at any time. Also, tourists at the hotel can visit the bar and cafeteria. Beaches

This hotel is located about 500 meters from the coastline. Many of its guests prefer to relax near Monica's, as the beach is clean and there are always a few people. Yes, and you can eat here if you want for little money.

One and a half kilometers from the hotel is one of the most famous beaches of North Goa - Calangute. It is popular with both locals and tourists. Its total length is 7 km. Here vacationers will find plenty of water activities and all the necessary infrastructure. An umbrella and sunbed on this beach can be borrowed for free. There are many bars and cafes along the coastline. In these establishments, tourists have the opportunity to order local and European dishes, as well as any drinks. The view from the coastal cafes and bars to the sea offers truly magnificent.

Hotel reviews

In most cases, tourists praise the hotel Affortel Magnum Resorts 3 * (Goa, India). The staff here are polite and helpful. The maids treat their duties very well. The rooms are cleaned with high quality. Towels and bed linen are changed regularly. Although it is, according to many guests, and slightly worn, but not torn and always clean.

The views from the windows of this hotel, according to most tourists, are not bad. The presence of balconies is also attributed to the advantages of the hotel. From some windows of the hotel you can see the sea, from others - a neat courtyard with a pool. Water in the latter, by the way, is most often clean.





Tourists also praise the Affortel Magnum Resorts for the fact that there, in contrast to many other similar establishments in India, there is no living creature. Rats and lizards do not appear in the hotel rooms. Sometimes, however, small ants make life difficult for guests.

The air conditioners in all rooms are brand new. In any case, they work, judging by the reviews, regularly and perform their functions well. Hotel guests do not suffer from stuffiness. Moreover, all rooms are equipped with ventilation.

The rest in this hotel is mainly residents of India itself, coming from deep provinces. Therefore, guests with a European appearance are always in the center of attention of curious neighbors. Such a slightly annoying interest can perhaps be considered to some extent a minus of this hotel. Also, many tourists attribute to the shortcomings of the hotel frequent interruptions with hot water. But this is also a disaster for most Goa hotels. Without interruptions, hot water in India can only be used by guests of those hotels in the bathrooms of which flow heaters are installed.
