Ways to get to Ikea (Teply Stan): metro or car

Ikea is a Dutch trademark with Swedish roots, which sells a wide range of products: from household kitchen items, furniture, decor items, to food.

What is an Ikea store like?

First opened in Russia in 2000, in Khimki. The store was loved by customers with its concept of inexpensive goods, and over the past ten years it has become very popular throughout the country. Ikea was opened in many large cities throughout Russia: in Moscow, St. Petersburg, Rostov-on-Don, Omsk, Yekaterinburg, Nizhny Novgorod, Kazan and others. Until now, this brand is one of the most stylish and high-quality firms producing furniture and other decor items in their price segment.

Why is the Ikea store so popular in Russia?

Its popularity in our country can be explained by the fact that this brand is famous for affordable prices and convenient location near shopping centers. The price tag for goods remains low, because the assortment presented by Ikea is made from recycled materials, which, to one extent or another, indicates the environmental friendliness of the products. In addition, the store has a wide range of colors for various household furniture, utensils, etc., which undoubtedly attracts customers. As well as free pencils, which at one time magically increased the store‚Äôs revenue by about 7%. Let us turn our attention to Ikea stores, which are located in Moscow.

Where are Ikea stores in Moscow?

In the capital, Ikea stores are located at three different points in the city:

  • Teply Stan in the south-west of the capital;
  • Khimki in the northwest;
  • Belaya Dacha in the southeast.

How to get to "Ikea" ("Teply Stan"), we will consider a little lower.

All "Ikea" are located outside the Moscow Ring Road, so they can not be reached simply by metro without additional transport. If you are going to visit this store for the first time, then plan your trip carefully in advance. Usually, there are free buses (which alas, but have long been paid due to the large number of visitors) to the Mega shopping and entertainment centers, near which Ikea is located, from the metro or minibuses. Of course, if you have your own car, then you can easily reach your destination on your own.

In "Ikea" by personal transport





If you do not know how to get to Ikea by Teply Stan by car, then first of all you need to find out its location. This store is located near the shopping and entertainment complex "Mega" ("Teply Stan") on 21 km of the Kaluga highway (in the village of Sosenskoye), or 41 km of the Moscow Ring Road. It is best to plan your trip in advance so as not to overshadow shopping with lost time in traffic during rush hour.

If you do not have personal transport

As mentioned above, there are usually direct buses and shuttles from the metro to the Mega shopping center - Teply Stan. Transport runs every 15 minutes, so you don‚Äôt have to wait too long.

How to get to Ikea on Teply Stan from the metro? If you live on the orange branch of the Moscow metro, then it will be most convenient for you to get from Yasenevo (the first route at 9.45 bus No. 961), or from Teply Stan (minibuses No. 504 and No. 952 from 8:00, bus No. 964 from 9.45, and also bus number 804 (Mega-Ob stop), the first flight at 6.00)



But what if you live in Moscow on a gray branch? How to get to "Ikea" - "Teply Stan"? From the metro station "Dmitry Donskoy Boulevard" by bus number 962, which begins its first flight at 14.25. Therefore, if you need to get to Ikea earlier on your own, it is best to take the metro to Yasenevo or Teply Stan.

For residents of the nearest Moscow region, in particular, the Moskovsky village, from 8.00 in the morning, a fixed-route taxi No. 46 starts from the Moscow Grad stop right to the entrance to the Mega shopping center (Teply Stan), and, accordingly, Ikea. Residents of South Butovo will also not have a problem getting to the store on the 1068 minibus, but those from Novo-Peredelkino and Solntsevo will need a minibus No. 5 to get there (the start of the routes starts at 7.30).

If you are wondering how to get to Ikea on Teply Stan from the Yugo-Zapadnaya metro station, then minibus No. 1011, which also passes by the Troparevo metro station, and minibus No. 953 will help you.

Opening hours of the store "Ikea" ("Teply Stan")

The Ikea trademark specializes not only in the sale of goods for home and office, but also has a Swedish food store and two places to eat. Opening hours of different units of the Ikea complex vary somewhat. The shop itself, a shop with Swedish food and a bistro are open from ten in the morning until twelve at night, and the restaurant from 9.30 to eleven in the evening.



Since all buses stop in front of the main entrance to Mega (Teply Stan), you need to take into account the opening hours and the shopping center. Despite the fact that the mall itself works in the same way as Ikea, i.e. from 10.00 - 24.00 hours, so if you take your own bus, keep in mind that on Fridays and Saturdays they finish their work at 00.30, and on other days at 23.30.
