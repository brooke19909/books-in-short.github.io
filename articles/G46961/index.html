Hotel Albatros Aqua Vista Resort Spa 4 *, Hurghada: hotel description and reviews

You will not be mistaken if you choose the Albatros Aqua Vista Resort Spa 4 * hotel for your vacation . Hurghada will be remembered for a long time not only due to the gentle sea and warm sun, but also because of the high-quality service and friendly service.

Hotel Description

Hotel Albatros Aqua Vista Resort Spa 4 * is a huge hotel complex, designed in the style of ancient Egyptian palaces. Its territory is combined with two neighboring institutions, so that tourists have more opportunities for recreation, entertainment and walking.

The main specialization of the hotel Albatros Aqua Vista Resort Spa 4 * is a family vacation. This focus is emphasized by a well-developed children's infrastructure, which includes numerous pools, playgrounds and attractions for young tourists.

A huge well-kept garden located in the hotel complex Albatros Aqua Vista Resort Spa 4 * deserves special attention. There is a huge variety of trees and plants that are regularly cared for. And thanks to the numerous decorative elements, tourists have ample opportunities to create original photos for the family album.





Hotel location

The Albatros Aqua Vista Resort Spa 4 * hotel is located quite favorably regarding a number of important facilities. So, the distance of the hotel from the airport is not more than 7 km and 15 km from the center of the resort city of Hurghada. Since the hotel is located on the second coastline, it does not have its own beach, but guests can use the access to the sea belonging to the neighboring hotel (it is at a distance of about 1000 meters).

Infrastructure

The Albatros Aqua Vista Hotel presents the following infrastructure facilities for its guests:

  • a small conference room that can accommodate up to 50 people;
  • large guarded parking lot;
  • currency exchange office and several ATMs;
  • a kiosk with a huge selection of souvenirs;

Service included in the tour price

The staff of the Albatros Aqua Vista Hotel makes sure that the rest of its guests is as comfortable as possible. To this end, tourists are provided with a wide range of services. So, the hotel guests are available within the cost of a tour package:

  • all-inclusive meals in buffet style with a huge selection of dishes from various cuisines of the world;
  • round-the-clock service by the hotel complex staff;
  • free wireless Internet is available at the reception and terrace of the hotel;
  • animation program both in the afternoon and in the evening;
  • car parking;
  • sun loungers and umbrellas for a comfortable stay by the pool or on the beach;
  • transfer to the sea coast (every half hour from the main entrance to the hotel a specialized bus leaves).

Service for an additional fee

Hotel "Albatross Aqua Vista" will provide you with the following services for an additional fee:

  • at a predetermined time, the hotel staff will call a taxi for you;
  • additional room service;
  • a certain commission is charged for currency exchange;
  • dry cleaning and laundry services;
  • use of a shuttle bus to the city center and back;
  • calling a doctor if the disease or injury is not included in the coverage.

The cost of services is indicated in the price list, which is located in the closet of each room. You can also clarify the price by contacting the reception.

Leisure activities

Hotel Albatros Aqua Vista Resort Spa 4 * is a great place for those who are a fan of an active lifestyle, because in this hotel complex there is a lot of entertainment for guests:

  • in the spa you can visit the sauna, bathhouse, Turkish hammam, jacuzzi, as well as undergo massage and aromatherapy;
  • the hotel has 5 large freshwater pools, 3 of which are equipped with heating devices;
  • fully equipped gym;
  • aerobics classes with professional instructors or sports animators;

  • modern well-groomed playgrounds and equipment for such popular sports as tennis and table tennis, football, beach volleyball and billiards.

restaurants and bars

Guest Compliments

Eastern countries are famous for their hospitality and hospitality. Egypt is no exception. Albatros Aqua Vista Resort Spa 4 *, like many other hotels located in Hurghada, for many years in a row has supported the tradition of providing its guests with pleasant bonuses and compliments.

Each tourist who buys a tour or book a room in this hotel complex, automatically becomes a member of the loyalty program. It implies the provision of discounts on a number of popular services, and also gives the right to receive some privileges and pleasant surprises.

The hotel provides compliments for the following categories of guests:

  • All newlyweds who wish to spend their honeymoon in this resort are provided with a comfortable room with a huge double bed. In addition, a pleasant surprise awaits guests in the form of a large basket with tropical fruits. To receive this bonus, indicate the purpose of your visit to the hotel at the stage of booking, as well as during the registration process and do not forget to bring a marriage certificate with you.
  • If your birthday falls on the period that you plan to spend at the hotel, then you can count on a pleasant surprise in the form of a birthday cake. In addition, a day before the holiday date, you can reserve a table in any restaurant. Do not forget to indicate your date of birth during the acquisition of the tour, as well as during registration.
  • Regular guests of the hotel receive a compliment in the form of a fruit basket, as well as replenishment of the minibar. Upon arrival at the hotel, do not forget to remind the administrator that this is not the first time you have visited this resort so that it can check the base of registered tourists.




Important information

If you are going to rest at the Albatros Vista Hotel (Hurghada), you need to familiarize yourself with some of the rules governing accommodation in this hotel complex:

  • Check-in is possible after 14.00, but check-out should be done no later than noon;
  • You can come to rest with children of any age;
  • if a child under the age of 12 does not need an extra bed, then accommodation is free of charge;
  • Payment for a crib for babies up to 2 years old is not charged;
  • the cost of an extra bed for a child is calculated as half the price of an adult in a room;
  • It is strictly forbidden to check into a hotel with any pets (including dwarf ones);
  • It is not allowed to visit restaurants in beach or home clothes.
