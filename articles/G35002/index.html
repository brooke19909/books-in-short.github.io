Setting alarms with auto start: choosing a security system model

One of the important concerns of motorists is to protect the car from theft. A foreign car or a domestic model is equally worthy of attention. Therefore, it is worth considering options for how to set alarms with auto start, what kind of systems and their features are.

Car alarms: main functions and types



In order not to become a victim of the modern Yuri Detochkin, it is enough to equip your car with a protective system. As a rule, their functions include not only theft prevention. They allow you to quickly start the car (even in cold weather and after prolonged downtime), warm up the interior. This is especially important for cars that run on diesel. Moreover, the presence of special programs allows you to set a specific time to start the engine. They turn on, responding to a drop in temperature in the engine. And, of course, the car alarm will protect the car around the clock from other people's attacks. If the hijacker appears, the system will work, shut off the engine and give a signal to the owner's key fob. New models of systems are controlled remotely by means of a mobile phone, SMS-messages, through Internet sites.

The systems are divided by price categories and capabilities. Lower in cost may not have a dialog code, they are equipped with higher-class alarms, they also have a CAN bus.
