Hotel Marti La Perla 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey): reviews

Resorts - a kind of "chip" in Turkey. You do not have to go outside the hotel territory for the whole vacation. Unless, of course, your hotel is on the first line from the sea. So, looking for a suitable place to stay in Turkey, you should pay attention to the hotels, in the name of which there is the word "resort".

But there are many hotels that are excellent resorts in themselves and do not need additional advertising. In this article we will consider one of them. It is called Marti La Perla 4 *.

Turkey, or rather its coast, is divided into regions: the Mediterranean, Aegean and Black Seas. Marmaris, in the vicinity of which the hotel described by us is located, is an amazing city. It is unique in that it is located near the confluence of the Aegean and Mediterranean Seas. To look at how the azure and ultramarine waters of the two water areas combine, excursions come. But those who live in the village of Icmeler can observe this bewitching phenomenon even from the window of their room. We get a little ahead. Let us, in order, describe all the qualities of the Marty La Perla Hotel.





Where is the hotel located

Address Marti La Perla 4 *: Turkey, Icmeler (Marmaris). The place itself is a resort village (formerly fishing), which has recently entered the city limits. It is located on the Mediterranean coast. This is a bustling youth resort, famous for its Bar Street. If you want your vacation to be incendiary, you need a hotel near the city center. A little further to the west stretched the resort area. It stretches for many kilometers along the coast.

The areas of Marmaris Turunch and Icmeler are favorite places for lovers of a quiet family or romantic vacation. But it cannot be said that it is impossible to have plenty of fun. True, all entertainment is concentrated on the territory of resort hotels. Icmeler is famous not only for its beautiful sandy beach, but also for its thermal springs.

Love this area of ​​Marmaris and divers. There are underwater caves in the Mediterranean. You can also watch turtles, octopuses and other animals in its habitat. The only negative location is the distance from Marmaris.

Reviews recommend ordering an individual transfer. If you ride a big bus with other tourists, then the road from Dalaman airport will drag on for four hours, until they are all taken to hotels.





Hotel Territory

Marti La Perla 4 * (Marmaris) is part of the hotel complex. It also includes a hotel with the traditional name for Turkey "Resort". More precisely, Marti Resort de Luxe 5 *. Guests of the "Pearls" (that is how the word "La Perla" is translated) can use the entire infrastructure of the Resort Hotel, and vice versa. But, in truth, the Quartet's winners benefit from this service.

Both the one and the other hotel are located on the front page. In order to get to the beach, you need to cross a small footpath. The territory of “La Perla 4 *” is small, but if you take into account the fact that you can safely go to the “Resort de Luxe 5 *”, then there is already where to take a walk.

The whole complex was built in 1988. And of course, after this was repeatedly renovated. The Marty La Perla Hotel consists of two five-story buildings, perpendicular to each other. “Resort de Luxe” is simply stunning with a huge, beautiful and well-groomed territory with many pools.





Room Categories

Many tourists dream that the windows of their bedrooms overlook the turquoise expanse of the sea. And in the Marti chain hotels, they understand this very well. La Perla 4 * buildings are constructed in such a way that the lion's share of all rooms in them have at least a side view of the sea.

Compared to the neighboring Hotel Resort de Luxe 5 *, this one is aimed at more frugal tourists. Therefore, the main category of rooms in the Marti La Perla 4 * hotel (Marmaris) is Standard. Their area is 21 square meters.

There is also a Family Rooms. If the “Standards” can accommodate two, maximum three people, then family rooms are designed for 4-5 guests. The area of ​​such premises is 35 square meters. The room consists of a living room, two bedrooms without an adjacent door. On the upper floor of the buildings there are attics, which also have rooms for vacationers - “Roof Rooms”. The hotel also has superior rooms. This category is called Deluxe. The area of ​​these rooms is 43 square meters, although they are designed for two or three people. The deluxe bedroom has a separate living area.

What's in the rooms

The Marti La Perla 4 * hotel (Marmaris) has all the conditions for a comfortable stay by the sea. The furnishings and decor are thought out to the smallest detail. Two rooms are reserved for persons with disabilities. All rooms for guests, even those located in the attic, have balconies.

The filling of the Standard room is very decent. There is: a quiet and powerful air conditioning with manual control, electronic safe, satellite TV, telephone and minibar. Its contents are paid.

Deluxe rooms are complemented with upholstered furniture. On the spacious balcony there is an elegant table and two chairs. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer. Cleaned in rooms daily and very thorough. Bed linen is changed every other day, towels - on request. The floors in the rooms are smooth and cool tiled. The only minus of the rooms in the reviews was the lack of Wi-Fi. In the modern world, being in touch (including via the Internet) is very important.





Restaurants, bars

Marti La Perla 4 * in Marmaris practices the all-inclusive power system. Guests are fed from seven in the morning until two at night. However, unlike Ultra All Inclusive, the restaurant opens its doors for meals at certain hours. The service there is buffet style.

In addition to the main one, there are two more à la carte restaurants. They specialize in Italian and Turkish cuisine. But their visit is paid, and preliminary reservation of tables is required.

But in the "Snack Bar" you can freely dine on fast food. This is if you do not want to go change into your room to visit the main restaurant. You can drink in various places on the territory of Marty La Perla. There are several bars: the already mentioned Snack, where they not only feed, but also pour it, in the lobby, near the restaurant and Phoenix. Ice cream, fresh juices and imported alcohol - for a fee. In the public domain there are only refreshing drinks and cocktails from them. As well as Turkish alcohol - wines, beer and distillates.

What nutrition reviews say

About how they feed at the Marti La Perla 4 * hotel (Marmaris), reviews widely sing laudatory odes. Tourists say that the food here is like in a five-star resort hotel. Three types of soups for lunch and many grilled dishes for dinner. And boiled crayfish, mussels, fried fish, beef, turkey, lamb. Not to mention the famous Turkish desserts. There was always fresh seasonal fruit at the meal.

The restaurant is bright and airy, well-conditioned. There is a terrace under the canopy. The waiters are very quick and clean the tables quickly. There is a coffee machine in the lobby. The bars serve excellent drinks. Especially appreciated the reviews of local wine.



Beach and Pools

Two Marti hotels - La Perla 4 * and Resort - have their own beaches. They are awarded the Blue Flag every year for cleanliness. These beaches are covered with small pebbles, in some places there is a soft sand. There are plenty of sun loungers with umbrellas on the shore. Beach towels are provided free of charge and without deposit. And the beds are covered with soft mattresses.

La Perla Hotel has one outdoor swimming pool with a shallow area nearby. Its edges are also lined with sun loungers and umbrellas. There are also Bali-bad tents on the beach, but using them is for a fee. The neighboring hotel "Resort de Luxe 5 *" has several outdoor pools and one indoor, indoors.



Hotel Infrastructure

Modern tourists are very interested in the availability of free Internet access on vacation abroad. After all, calls in roaming are so expensive! Hotel Marti La Perla 4 * (Marmaris) has free Wi-Fi in the lobby.

Happy tourists are entertained by a team of funny animators. In the evening, they conduct shows on their own or with the involvement of professional artists. Live music is played in one of the bars. Reviews recommend taking advantage of the neighborhood of the five-star Marty Resort de Luxe hotel and going to have fun there. There is an amphitheater for 250 people, where movies are held once a week.

The Resort de Luxe also has a small spa area with a sauna and hammam. For sports enthusiasts, the La Perla Hotel has a fitness room with exercise machines. In season, two water slides of medium size work for vacationers.

Paid resort services

Not all service is included in the price of a ticket to the Marti La Perla 4 * hotel. Reviews mention that you will have to pay for using tennis racquets, balls, billiards. All water sports, even non-powered catamarans, require additional costs. Free for vacationers are only slides in a mini water park, a fitness room, darts, football and beach volleyball. You will have to pay for a massage session, beauty salon services, for diving lessons. And, recall the reviews, in two restaurants of Turkish and Italian cuisine, where the menu is served in a very solemn atmosphere, they also require payment.



The concept of "16+". What does it mean?

In Turkey, all residents are very child-loving. And in this country there are many hotels where the most excellent conditions are created for children and even for babies. But the administration of the Marty complex rightly reasoned that sometimes people want to take a break from their children - theirs and especially those of others. Thus, the hotel area was divided.

“Resort de Luxe” positions itself as a family hotel. There are many children's pools, playgrounds, etc. But in Marti La Perla Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) only those who have reached the age of sixteen are lodged. You will not hear any crying at night, noise and din at the pool, whims in the restaurant.

The concept of “16+” also influenced the national composition of hotel guests. Here the British, Germans, Danes, Dutch have a rest. And recently, the number of Ukrainians has increased. There are few Russians here. Whether this is good or bad is up to you. You will find a large number of compatriots in the neighboring Marty Resort de Luxe 5 *.

Marti La Perla 4 * (Marmaris): reviews 2017

In recent years, the hotel rating has grown. Tour operators even designate her star status as “4+”. The staff is very helpful and friendly. After your first visit, bartenders will remember your name and what drinks you prefer. At the entrance to the restaurant you are helpfully led to a table.

Maids, without any tips, clean the room for conscience. And for a small reward, they spin figures from towels and decorate the room with fresh flowers.

Tourists really like the concept of the hotel "adults only". No mothers will infringe on your freedom under the pretext that there are children. In short, tourists believe that they are lucky that they got to Icmeler. Here the service is like in Antalya, and the “European” Marmaris is also within easy reach.
