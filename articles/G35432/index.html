Bosch laser level PCL 10 Set: specifications, photos and reviews

The classes of household and professional building tools in many aspects of operation converge and suggest common areas of development. For example, this refers to the reliability of devices, ease of use and durability. At the same time, not every measuring device designed for domestic use should provide the ability to work over long distances and at the same time demonstrate increased accuracy with wide functionality. The Bosch PCL 10 Set level model is part of the line of home levels designed to perform basic tasks to control the position of work surfaces and objects. Although the device does not have the capabilities of a professional laser level, it is quite suitable for obtaining accurate results in the process of performing general repair and installation operations.





General information about the level

The model comes in different trim levels, which may include one or another set of additional accessories. In this case, we consider the full version of the device with the inclusion of the necessary additions that expand the capabilities of the Bosch PCL 10 Set. A tripod made of aluminum, for example, allows you to conveniently control the position of objects without outside help. Also included is a case and two batteries.

The device is part of a family of automatic laser levels, so the user can count on ensuring high measurement accuracy. Unlike most household levels, this model gives cross marking vertically and horizontally, which also improves the quality of work. At the same time, the Bosch PCL 10 Set marking device eliminates the need for painstaking settings. It is enough to adjust the basic mechanical position of the tool with orientation to the target object, and then the electronic filling by means of the generated rays will independently project the lines on the working surface, and if necessary, perform the necessary calculations.





Specifications

According to many characteristics, the model gives its origin from the line of home levels. This also applies to the modest supply radius of an LED beam, modest by the standards of professional models, and the minimum number of additional options. Nevertheless, the Bosch PCL 10 Set level has a rather strong technical base, the parameters of which are presented below:

  • Dimensions of the device - 108 x 92 x 66 mm.
  • Weight - 1.11 kg.
  • The fixing thread in the tripod is 1/4 inch.
  • The number of LED rays is 2.
  • The laser wavelength of the level is 635 Nm.
  • Range of measurements in range - up to 10 m.
  • The error is about 0.5 mm per 1 m.
  • Measurement time - an average of 4 seconds.
  • The duration of the charge is 15 hours
  • The voltage of the power element is 1.5 V.

The "filling" of the device is quite balanced in terms of operational capabilities, and in terms of structural design. Thanks to this, the tool is suitable for performing measurements in the confined conditions of a small apartment, as well as when working on a large construction site.





Features of the model Bosch PCL 10 Set

In the household tool segment, manufacturers rarely provide technological sophistication. But the German company still implemented several special solutions in this model of the level. These include the presence of a switch with integrated protection. This device allows you to securely fix the device in order to perform further marking with high accuracy. It often happens that even with proper positioning, laser level operators note blurry lines. To prevent such distortions, the Bosch PCL 10 Set level is provided with a glass cover, due to which the device directs bright and at the same time clear rays. And this is not to mention the mechanical protection provided by the same cover.

Positive tool reviews

The main share of the owners of this device is ordinary home craftsmen, most of whom do not have professional skills in construction. It is for this audience that the model is designed. It is praised for its well-implemented ergonomics with convenient controls, reliability and versatility. Experienced users note that the device should be purchased just complete with a tripod. If necessary, the Bosch laser level can be combined not only with branded accessories, but also with devices from other major manufacturers of such tools. By the way, thread with a size of 1/4 inch allows you to use the device in combination with most popular tripods. As for the working properties themselves, the owners emphasize the generation of clearly visible rays that are projected with a minimum error of up to 10 m.





Negative reviews

Based on the fact that this model of the level was initially oriented toward domestic use, some critical reviews can be left aside. Such, for example, include claims in terms of a short feed distance, mention of limited functionality, as well as modest possibilities for performing geometric calculations. These options are usually available to more reputable representatives of the segment - usually from the category of professional models. Nevertheless, there is also a justifiable criticism of the Bosch PCL 10 Set, which concerns power elements. Today, even the lowest class of such levels is equipped with small, but battery packs. In this case, the developers have provided power from the batteries, which is not always convenient during long working sessions.





Conclusion

The device is endowed with a basic set of functions that modern building levels perform. Moreover, in practice, the model adequately solves its problems without gross errors. Also, the Bosch laser level in the PCL 10 modification has a robust body and comfortable handles. Those who want to become the owner of this model should pay about 4.5-5 thousand rubles for these and other advantages. Obviously, the price tag for a model of this level is slightly overpriced, but, according to the users themselves, the high accuracy of the markup, the durability of the device and the ease of use are worth the investment.
