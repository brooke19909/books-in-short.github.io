Washing machine "Mile": reviews, instructions. German-made Miele washing machine

Miele is the world famous and largest brand of German household appliances. Washing machines of the German assembly "Mile" are not the most affordable in terms of cost, but they are of high quality and reliability.

Miele brand technology: features

Household appliances produced by this company are practical, environmentally friendly and reliable and have the following features:

  1. The Soft-Tronic system facilitates the washing process and launches many functions in automatic mode. For example, the Fuzzi Logic option evaluates the amount and composition of the laundry loaded into the drum, calculates the required amount of water and the amount of detergent, selects the appropriate washing mode and time. Users in their reviews of Mile washing machines with a similar function note that automated load control saves resources and time. On the front panel of the machine are indicators with which it can be connected to a computer. Thanks to the Update option, you can update the programs of the Miele washing machine by downloading the necessary software from the official website of the manufacturer.
  2. Miele machines have a delayed start option: you can schedule a wash with a time delay of up to 24 hours, so that the user can adjust the wash to suit their daily routine.

German washing machines "Mile" are equipped with honeycomb drums that protect the fabric during washing from deformation and damage.

Specialists of the company claim that such drums were developed on the basis of bee honeycombs with convex cells on the inside, which protects the laundry during washing, forming a layer of water between it and the metal walls. Similarly, the fabric is protected during the spin process, even when setting the maximum number of revolutions.





Technical parameters of washing machines

Many users in their reviews of Mile washing machines note the technical parameters of household appliances:

  1. Waterproof and Watercontrol systems control external and internal hoses, connections and gaskets, preventing and protecting against leaks.
  2. The rotation speed of the drum in the spin mode is 1800 rpm. Smooth start of the drum and multi-stage control of its operation protect the fabric from deformation. The humidity of the pressed laundry is only 45%.
  3. Miele washing machine can wash things with an insignificant degree of pollution in half an hour thanks to the "mini" mode: at the same time, the water temperature is 40 ¬∞ C, the maximum drum load is two and a half kilograms.
  4. Woolen, silk and other delicate fabrics are cleaned of stains of any complexity by a hand wash program.
  5. The minimum noise level, according to reviews on Mile washing machines, allows you to start washing at any time of the day, even at night.
  6. The control of the machine is greatly facilitated thanks to the Russian-language panel.

Miele WDA 101 W

The automatic washing machine Miele WDA 101 W is a relatively inexpensive compared to other models appliances, which has good functionality. In spin mode, the drum rotation speed is 1400 rpm, the energy consumption of the highest class is A +++. You can load up to 7 kg of laundry in one cycle. A variety of washing programs expands the functionality of the machine: super rinse, washing delicate fabrics and black things, pre-washing, preventing wrinkling of things and washing in large quantities of water are available. The inner side of the drum of the Mile washing-drying machine is made in the form of a honeycomb, which preserves delicate fabrics and does not damage them during washing.
