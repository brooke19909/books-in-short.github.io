Which Egypt Resort to Choose

The most famous are Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh. These former fishing villages have grown enormously, occupying hundreds of kilometers of space along the coast. They have got satellite villages, such as El-Gouna or Makadi Bay in Hurghada and Nabq in Sharm el-Sheikh. But in recent years, completely new resorts have appeared and began to actively develop: Safaga, Taba, Dahab. All of them have their own specifics that you need to know in order to get what you want from a vacation in Egypt. If you want to see the historical sights of this country, then you should choose the resorts of Big Hurghada or Safagu. They are located near Luxor - the ancient Thebes. By the way, Hurghada is much cheaper than Sharm el-Sheikh. But at the last resort the public is more pretentious, and the quality of leisure and services is higher. After all, Sharm el-Sheikh translated from Arabic means "royal." And the rest is waiting for you here. Last-minute tours to Sharm El Sheikh will save you some.



When to go to Egypt

The climate of this African country is such that you can sunbathe and swim here all year round. Water in the Red Sea never cools below + 20 degrees, because only in this temperature can corals live - another attraction of Egypt. Due to climatic features, the country is going through several tourist seasons. They fall mainly on holidays. Christmas and New Year are replaced by a very low season. Indeed, in the winter in the desert strong winds blow. In late January and early February, last minute packages to Egypt can be bought for $ 350. This price includes flights, transfer to the hotel and accommodation for a week in a three-star hotel according to the all-inclusive concept. In the summer, this country is incredibly hot. Rest at such a time is attracted only to extremes, because +45 in the shade is difficult to withstand, and you certainly will not go on excursions. Therefore, the high season in Egypt can be called spring and autumn. Our hotel is located in Sharm El Sheikh, on the Sinai Peninsula. This is the northeast of the country. Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh: April

At the beginning of the month, great tourist activity has not yet been observed, and prices are encouraging: tours cost a little more than in February. But from the second decade of April in the Sharm el-Sheikh protected from dust storms the beginning of the tourist season is observed.
