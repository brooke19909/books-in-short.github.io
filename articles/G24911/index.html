Knitted shoes (crocheted) with a description and scheme. From slippers to boots

Boots, ballet shoes, sandals and slippers are all crocheted shoes . You can work with a description and diagram of each of them, modifying to your taste and needs. Then you get a unique thing. And if you pick up elegant yarn, you will get really beautiful knitted shoes.





Ballet shoes for dancing

Knitted shoes (crocheted) are presented here with a description and diagram for the top of the ballet shoes and for the sole (see picture below). Experienced craftswomen recommend knitting the bottom of the ballet shoes with a thread in two additions. If you want a denser knit, but without adding up the threads, you can replace the double crochet with double crochet. In this case, the number of rows is supposed to increase.





In order to tie the top of the product, it is necessary to continue knitting in a circle with the same elements. So knit three rows. Then start the reduction of the toe loops.

To do this, in the first row you need to connect two double crochets with one common vertex. If single crochet columns are selected, then they are supposed to be simply skipped. There should be 4 loops on each side of the toe.

The next two circles are recommended to knit with a decrease of 3 loops on both sides. If the work went with yarn in two additions, then in the next row you need to break one of the threads and replace the element with a smaller one. That is, if there was a double crochet, then knit it without a crochet. The ballet shoes, which were originally crocheted with crochets, need to continue unchanged, but in one thread. At the same time, make one decrease on the sides of the socks.

Perform another one or two rows without changing the number of loops. Then associate the series with "pico." That is, alternate columns with chains of three air loops.





Festive boots

Why should crocheted shoes (with a description and a pattern) necessarily have the appearance of slippers? You can make high or low boots and show off in them on holidays. It should be borne in mind that their sole will also be tied, so it is better to wear such boots already in the room. By the way, some pull them directly on shoes or sandals. And the heel pokes through the hole left in advance.

Since the pattern of the proposed model is simple, for knitting you need to choose a beautiful yarn. Melange thread with lurex looks original and festive. It should be thin, and the hook will be required under the number 1-1.75.

Product manufacturing algorithm:

  1. Knitting starts with a sock. On a ring from a sliding loop, knit 20 columns with a crochet. This is the first row.
  2. All subsequent circles create a pattern of the top of the sock. To do this, 9 columns are left on the sole and fit without change. Between the two extreme columns on both sides, you will need to add one column at a time. A fillet net is tied between these applications, that is, after each double crochet, you need to make an air loop.
  3. The second paragraph is supposed to be completed two more times. That is, the addition should be done in three circles.
  4. Then knit without changing the pattern to the place where the heel will be. Leave a hole with a chain of air loops. Moreover, it should be longer than the loops left untied.
  5. Then continue to knit a filet net around the circle. Finish knitting at the right height.

In order for the boot to fit tightly around the ankle, it is recommended to knit this part with different height columns: with two or three crochets. Fix the top of the boot to an elastic band of a suitable color.





Sandals

They do not need a lot of yarn, but you need to stock up on an espadrilles sole. Tie a toe and a heel. Moreover, you can choose any pattern: from a completely closed toe to narrow stripes. In order to get knitted shoes that are comfortable to wear, the master class prescribes to take cotton yarn.

The details of the toe must be knitted so that they fit the leg tightly. They are recommended to be even slightly pulled. Then sew them with an overcast stitch to the prepared sole. The heel with stripes of ties should also be tight. To sandals held firmly on your leg, you can knit a small circle in the upper part of the toe and pass a strip from the heel into it.
