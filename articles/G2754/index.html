Room Types

The rooms are located in a seven-story building, from the first to the fifth floor. You can choose from economy-class housing, standard categories and superior comfort. But even those who stayed in economy-class rooms at the Crimean Dawns Hotel (Alushta) left reviews about it as a place where you would definitely like to return.

Economy class

There are four types of rooms: two single and two double categories A and B. The first are in the attic, the difference between the categories is that in class B rooms there is a direct ceiling instead of beveled, a slightly larger area and a balcony with sea views. The rooms are equipped with all necessary amenities.





Double rooms also vary in size, balcony and ceiling shape. The amenities in each type of room are the same.

Standard

There are five types in Standard rooms: two single categories A and B and three double classes A, B and C, respectively. Single rooms vary slightly in size, as well as the presence of a balcony and view: for type B - on the sea, for A - on the summer terrace. The facilities in the rooms are identical.

Double rooms of categories B and C do not differ from each other in area, but they are larger than A. All rooms have a balcony, but with a different view: for A this is a patio, for B a view of the medical building, summer terrace or the sea, for C - only at sea. All rooms have the necessary amenities.

How to get from the station?

If you are going to arrive by train, and your destination is Alushta, Crimean Dawns, how to get from Simferopol Station to the hotel is a very important question. It's simple: take the trolley at number 51 and get to the final stop. From this place you can walk to the boarding house, which takes no more than ten minutes, or call a taxi if you have too many things with you.

If you plan to get from the bus station in Simferopol to the hotel "Crimean Dawns" (Alushta), traveler reviews say that you should take the bus Simferopol - Yalta, or Simferopol - Alushta. Then you can catch any taxi going to the promenade - these are routes numbered 5 and 6, as well as trolley buses 1 and 2.

There is an option to also order a transfer from the hotel in advance, its cost will depend on the type of car selected.
