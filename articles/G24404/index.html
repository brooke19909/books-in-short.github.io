Where to go with children in Tolyatti: an overview of entertainment centers, children's cafes, reviews

Where to go with children in Tolyatti on the weekend? This question interests many parents who want to have an exciting time with their child. But not everyone knows what places to visit. We will help in this and tell you where to go with a child in Togliatti today.

Kids cafe

Where to go on vacation with children in Togliatti? You can visit large shopping centers, where parents and children can enjoy not only gaming halls, but also catering places.

There are many places in the city where you can sit with your children, but not have fun, but have a tasty meal. For example, children will be interested in going to a children's cafe. Parents will not be bored either. After all, time spent with children is priceless.

Jumanji

This children's entertainment venue is located at Tolyatti, Avtozavodskoye Shosse 6. The hours of a possible visit to the site are from 10:00 to 22:00. She works without breaks and days off. This is a very bright and beautiful playroom that will not leave any child indifferent. There are slides, a pool full of balls, trampolines.

Visitors' feedback on visiting this place is often positive, because the creators have provided a semi-closed zone in order to monitor their child during the games. At this entertainment venue for children in Togliatti, funny animators work, as well as a disco with soap bubbles.

If you order a holiday in this center, then you will be provided with a festive cake, face painting and chic decoration of the entire room in the chosen style.

"At the hippo"

The amusement park "At a hippopotamus" is another place where to go with children in Togliatti is not only possible, but also necessary. It is located on the street 70 years of October, 31A. The park operates seven days a week from 10:00 to 21:00. In this amazing place, every child will find their favorite swing or entertainment. All attractions are absolutely safe, people with pedagogical education work with children.

In addition to attractions, children are offered ball games. The park has earned good reviews for the opportunity to feel like an artist by painting various paintings. Experienced staff also like visitors. They will accurately determine which attraction will be interesting and at the same time safe for a child of a particular age group.

Madagascar Park

This entertainment center is located at 14 Lev Yashin Street, on the 3rd floor of the Madagascar shopping center. The cost of visiting starts from 300 rubles. In the play area, entertainment is provided for children of different ages. This center will be interesting for both kids and adults.

This park has a sandbox, dry slides, a sand pool. Admitting a child to such attractions is recommended from the age of 4. For entertainment, large areas are involved. A child can do whatever he wants, even stand on his head. In the center of this zone is a tower. The kid can have fun, jump and run as much as your heart desires. The labyrinth zone, carousels and slides are intended for children up to 7 years.

In addition, a pirate labyrinth and trampolines are available for children over 6 years old.

Fanny Park

This huge park is located on eight hectares on Frunze Street. There are a lot of trees, trampolines, slides, regular and extreme attractions.

A swimming pool is open in summer, and an ice rink opens on November 1. Ice skating enthusiasts can take advantage of skate rental, wardrobe and cafe services.

Park of Culture

Do you know where you can go with a child in Togliatti if you do not have extra money, but it is simply necessary to entertain the baby? In the park of culture and rest. There are two of them in Togliatti: one is located on Victory Street, the second - on Liza Chaykina Street.

If you are planning a vacation with your child in the winter, then you just need to go to the Russian Amusement Park winter amusement park. It has three different descent heights and lengths, various attractions that train accuracy in children and adults, as well as a city made of ice. Address: Central Park, Gorsad stop.

Viking village

Extreme Park "Viking Village" is located at: Obvodnoye Shosse, 92. Suitable for children over 6 years old. The entertainment program includes ATV rides, children's paintball, a laser tag, an aqua park.

Aqualand

The park is located at: st. Banykina, 19A, building 1.

This water park will not leave anyone indifferent. There are water and land slides, as well as playgrounds for playing volleyball and basketball, table tennis. A children's pool is available. And so that parents are not bored, there is a fast food area, an elongated bar, a dance floor.

Contact zoo

Also in Togliatti, an unusual contact zoo has been built, in which you can pet and feed all animals. The park has an iguana, python, boa constrictor, chameleon, terrarium. There are domestic and decorative animals: rabbits, guinea pigs, kids and rabbits.

From exotic countries came to Russia meerkat, caiman, ostrich, African hedgehog, raccoons, squirrels and ferrets. You can find out about the work schedule or order tickets by calling on the official website. Address: st. Revolutionary 52A, TRC "Russia on the Volga".
