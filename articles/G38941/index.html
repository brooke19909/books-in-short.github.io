Blue Green Hotel 4 *: description, service, reviews

Blue Green Hotel 4 * hotel is located on the coast in Turkey near the resort town of Bodrum in the village of Gumbet. Due to the proximity of the beach, comfortable rooms and many attractions in the area, this hotel has gained wide popularity both among the residents of Turkey and among foreigners.

General information

The hotel was built in 2005, in 2014 was overhauled. The total area is 4,000 square meters. m. Here 11 buildings of the hotel are compactly located, which are small 3-storey cottages. This hotel was also known under a different name - Poseidon Suites.



The private hotel also has compact green spaces, pools and parking. Distance to important places:

  • Bodrum city center is 3 km from the hotel.
  • The nearest airport is 38 km away.
  • The beach extends a 5-minute walk from the hotel (200 meters).

Service quality

The hotel Blue Green Hotel 4 * provides everything you need for a comfortable stay.

  • 24 hour front desk service. If necessary, you can use the express check-in or check-out.
  • Luggage storage at the reception.
  • Safe box for storing valuable items - such a service is provided for a fee.
  • Transfer (meeting at the airport and transfer to the hotel) is available on request and is paid separately.
  • Car rental.
  • Bicycle rental for walking (price should be clarified by the administration).
  • There is street parking. This service is free of charge, reservation in advance is also not required.
  • Internet (Wi-Fi) is free, it is in all public areas.
  • Climate control system is present in all public areas.
  • Cleaning and laundry services.

Room service

The hotel has 149 rooms, including:

  • 40 promo standard rooms without a balcony of 16 square meters. m (suitable for 3 people);
  • 40 standard apartments (20 sq. M, maximum occupancy for 3 people);

  • 3 grand rooms (3-room apartments, suitable for 6 people).




The rooms are equipped with everything you need:

  • separate air conditioners;
  • a bathroom (there is a washbasin, mirror, shower, toilet);
  • TV with satellite channels;
  • the telephone in the room is free;
  • mini-bar for a fee (mineral water, drinks, alcohol).

Maids change linen 2 times a week, cleaning is carried out daily.

Nutrition

Hotel Bodrum Blue Green Hotel 4 * accommodates guests on an all-inclusive system. On the territory of work:

  • main restaurant (buffet service);
  • pool bar;
  • snack bar;
  • There is a children's menu, but it is considered a paid service.

Entertainment

The entertainment infrastructure of the hotel is well developed. There are:

  • pool with equipped area (sunbeds and umbrellas);
  • tables for table tennis;
  • billiard tables (use of equipment for a fee);
  • massage room;
  • animators (entertainment programs).




Blue Green Hotel 4 * (Bodrum): reviews

According to the reviews left by Russian-speaking tourists, several shortcomings of the hotel should be noted:

  • poor-quality room cleaning (many guests had to go to the administration and demand cleaners);
  • monotonous food (most tourists in the local restaurant only had breakfast, lunch and dinner were spent in urban small cafes).

Among the advantages can be called perhaps the low prices for trips and proximity to Bodrum and its attractions.

Who should take a closer look at the hotel? To all those who are undemanding to the service and ready to give up some amenities in favor of a budget holiday. This hotel is suitable for those who are not going to spend all the time in the hotel, but prefer to relax on the beach, go on excursions or go on walks.
