Amigurumi: crochet monkey. Schemes, description, photo

A self-made small toy with soft stuffing is an amigurumi monkey (crochet and knitting). Schemes, description of such toys will be given a little below. Amigurumi as a way of making soft-stuffed toys of miniature size came from the Land of the Rising Sun - Japan. Products can be knitted from various types of yarn. From thin acrylic threads you will get a graceful monkey-flower amigurumi crocheted. The scheme and description of the product are presented in this article. You can replace the banana flower. Such a cute toy will be a wonderful gift for loved ones.

Amigurumi Monkey

A very simple crochet amigurumi monkey is performed. Schemes, photos are presented below. To make a toy, you need to prepare the following materials:

  • thin and light thread of the type "Iris" or acrylic in three colors: light brown, light beige and yellow;
  • knitting hook number 2;
  • a needle with a large eye (it is better if the end of the needle is bent - it will be more comfortable to fix amigurumi details)




  • soft filler;
  • eyes;
  • coffee-colored floss thread;
  • thread of contrasting color or markers for knitting;
  • wire - for the frame, but if the toy is made for a small child, it is advisable to abandon the frame in favor of soft stuffing.
