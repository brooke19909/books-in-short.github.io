Capsule hotel, Sheremetyevo: photos and reviews about the hotel

Modern technologies are developing rapidly. This is noticeable not only in the technical direction, but also in the service sector, hotel business and other industries. More and more accustomed to comfort, modern travelers are trying to find for themselves the most comfortable places to spend time between flights. Everyone will agree that it is better to pass the hours of connecting the aircraft not in a noisy waiting room, but in a specially equipped place where you can not only sit with a book in your hands, but also have a good night's sleep. Thanks to the excellent project that was developed in Japan, some particularly large hub stations are equipped with capsule-type hotels. Now in Russia there is a capsule hotel. Sheremetyevo provides its passengers with all the amenities in a minimal space.





History of occurrence

Capsule hotels were born in Japan at the end of the 70s. They are built on the principle of honeycombs. Small rooms, equipped with everything necessary, are intended only so that people can get a good rest between flights. However, if in Japan they gained great popularity due to the large tourist flow, then in Russia such a miracle is almost impossible to meet.

Japan is a country with its principles and rules. Capsule hotels, which can be found both in small cities and in megacities, differ from those in Europe, one small feature - almost all of them are male. Of course, in some of them one floor is allocated to accommodate women, but in this case, the beautiful half of humanity is strictly prohibited from entering male territory.

At the most modern level, Sheremetyevo Airport is equipped. The capsule hotel here has gained immense popularity.





Hotel location and structure

One of the first Moscow hotels, created by capsule type, is the "Air Express". It is designed for passengers traveling by plane to spend a relatively short amount of time in it. You can find this hotel in the Aeroexpress terminal, it is located at Sheremetyevo Airport.





It is located on the fifth floor of the terminal. There are more than 65 rooms of very compact sizes. The smallest area does not exceed 8 square meters. The most spacious room has a size of 22 square meters. For a moderate fee (about 2700 rubles for single occupancy), you can spend four hours here. If you intend to spend a longer period of time in the room, then payment will be made hourly. For the most demanding customers, the hotel has VIP rooms. For passengers of any level, a capsule hotel (Sheremetyevo) is suitable .

Hotel Policies

Everyone has long been accustomed to the fact that in the global hotel chains, regardless of the country of location, the same rules of behavior and service apply. The same thing is observed in such hotels. It is very convenient for those people who, through work or simply because of their lifestyle, travel the world. Arrived in a new country, but already know how to behave in a hotel.





At the entrance, you need to remove your shoes and put them in a specially designed locker for this purpose. It locks with a key. All things can be taken to the left-luggage office, the key to which will also be kindly provided to you. As a result, we have three keys that open different doors. The third unlocks your number. After passing the first two stages, you can safely settle in your small but comfortable room, curtain the windows and immerse yourself in a measured rest.

European capsule hotels

What is characteristic of Japanese culture is practically not suitable for European countries, and even more so for tourists, which is why pragmatic Europeans have transformed minimalistic capsule hotels. If in Japan the numbers are made very modestly, in calm and discreet tones that can pacify, then everything is exactly the opposite. Modern European capsules are very beautiful, with a thoughtful interior and a design line. You can find those that literally repeat the cabins of luxury cruise liners. The guests of these rooms completely disappear the feeling that they are waiting for a transfer. You can even feel like a captain of a ship. Now, the inhabitants of Russia have a chance to visit the capsule hotel. Sheremetyevo gives all travelers the opportunity to conveniently take a break between flights.
