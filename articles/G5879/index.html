Hilton Nubian Resort Marsa Alam 5 *, Marsa Alam, Egypt: reviews, photos

If you prefer to stay in comfortable hotels with excellent cuisine and high-class service and choose Marsa Alam (Egypt) as your place of relaxation, Hilton Marsa Alam Nubian Resort 5 * can be a very suitable option for you.



Location

This hotel is located on the shores of Abu Dabab Bay, 30 kilometers from Marsa Alam and Port Ghalib. The nearest airport is 35 kilometers away. However, many of our compatriots arrive in Hurghada's air harbor, which is 220 kilometers from Mars Alam. In this case, the road to the hotel will take about three hours.

Egypt, Marsa Alam, Hilton Marsa Alam Nubian Resort 5 *: general information, photo

This hotel dates back to 2011. It consists of a reception building and a complex of 1- and 2-storey buildings. The Hilton Marsa Alam housing fund is represented by 395 rooms of the categories Standard, Deluxe and Suite. All apartments are spacious, stylishly decorated. Regardless of the category, they have a TV with satellite channels, a telephone, a minibar (drinking water is free, everything else is at an additional cost), a bathroom, air conditioning, a furnished terrace or balcony, a hairdryer, tea and coffee making facilities. On the floor - ceramic tiles. Internet is available at an additional cost. Housekeeping is provided daily.

Meals at the hotel are organized on an all-inclusive basis. It features a main restaurant (buffet) and several themed restaurants (√† la carte), as well as snack bars.

The hotel complex has four outdoor pools, one of which is equipped with a water heating system. There are sun terraces with sunbeds and umbrellas, day and evening animations are held. The hotel also has a gym and sports facilities.



Hilton Marsa Alam Nubian Resort 5 * (Marsa Alam, Egypt): reviews of Russian tourists

Every experienced traveler knows how important it is to approach the issue of planning a trip abroad with all responsibility. After all, it is necessary to provide for a lot of different parts. Perhaps the most important point in planning is the choice of a hotel in which you will stay for several days, or even weeks, during your vacation. Therefore, the hotel should maximally correspond not only to your financial capabilities, but also to preferences in terms of comfort and service. An invaluable tool in finding a hotel, according to many tourists, is the collection and analysis of reviews of those who have already stayed in a particular place. After all, former guests, as a rule, narrate not only about the advantages of hotels, but also about the shortcomings that opened during the stay. Therefore, you can make the right choice based on truthful and reliable information. So that you do not spend time and energy searching for reviews, we bring to your attention the generalized comments of Russian tourists about their stay in the five-star hotel of Egypt Hilton Nubian Resort Marsa Alam. Looking ahead a bit, we note that the vast majority of our compatriots were very pleased with their choice and are ready to recommend this hotel to everyone who wants to have a great rest. But we learn more about everything.



Housing stock

According to them, the tourists were very pleased with the accommodation in this hotel. So, the rooms here are spacious, freshly renovated, clean and comfortable. Guests note good soundproofing, which is not often found in Egyptian hotels. The rooms here have everything you need, including a full range of bath accessories, an iron and an ironing board. If you are arriving on vacation with a child, an extra rollaway bed will be installed in your apartment. It is advisable to inform the hotel in advance.

Cleaning

Our compatriots had no complaints about the work of the maids of the Hilton Marsa Alam Nubian Resort Hotel. So, they note that cleaning, change of towels and linen were carried out daily. Also, as necessary, replenished supplies of bath accessories, bottled drinking water in the minibar and tea bags, coffee and sugar. The maids, according to the tourists, could tell at what time it was advisable for them to come. You can be sure that your number will be removed by your return. In addition, maids often make funny figures in the form of hearts, swans, snakes, etc. from towels and petals on the beds. This especially delighted the youngest guests. As for tips, many travelers claim that for such attention it is not a pity to leave a maid for a dollar from time to time.



Check in, departure

In accordance with the hotel‚Äôs internal rules, room keys should be handed over to new arrivals after two in the afternoon. However, according to our compatriots, if you arrive at the hotel earlier than this time, they will try to accommodate you as soon as possible. In any case, you can use the hotel infrastructure immediately after arrival. As for the day of departure, the occupied apartments must be vacated before 12 noon. However, after this, the bracelets are not cut off to the guests, so if you plan to leave the hotel in the late afternoon, you can leave things in the luggage compartment and continue to enjoy your vacation.



Territory

As guests of the Hilton Nubian Resort Marsa Alam note in their comments, the hotel has a fairly large territory. She is very green, clean, well-groomed. Our compatriot liked the mass of flower beds, shady trees, huge palm trees, neatly trimmed lawns, etc. Here you can enjoy a walk and find several places suitable for a photo shoot.



Nutrition

This item, judging by the reviews analyzed, satisfied almost all the guests of the Hilton Nubian Resort Marsa Alam. So, as for the main restaurant of the hotel, it works in a buffet format. As our compatriots note, most of the dishes here relate to European cuisine, therefore they are familiar to tourists. However, if you wish, you can also try something from the Egyptian cuisine. So, guests can always enjoy a wide variety of dishes from meat, fish, seafood, poultry, soups, salads, snacks, vegetables, fruits, pastries, desserts. Children are offered free ice cream. An indisputable plus, many travelers call the fact that all the juices here are poured into the devices from the packages. In most hotels, including five-star hotels, these drinks are prepared on the basis of powder. Hilton Bars also offer freshly squeezed fruit juices.

In addition to the main restaurant, vacationers can also visit √† la carte restaurants. Here you will be offered dishes of Italian and Mexican cuisines. Guests recommend not to miss this opportunity and be sure to enjoy great dishes.

Alternatively, during the day, travelers can grab a bite to eat at the beach snack bar. Here you will be offered sandwiches, pizza, hamburgers, french fries, nuggets, etc. So some vacationers do not even return to the hotel for lunch, eating right on the beach.

The tourists were satisfied with the work of the bars of the Hilton Marsa Alam Nubian Resort 5 *, a photo of which can be seen in this article. So, according to them, the choice of both non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks is quite wide. Their quality also quite satisfied vacationers.



Sea

According to our compatriots, the five-star Hilton Nubian Resort Marsa Alam offers its guests a magnificent beach holiday. So, the hotel complex has its own sandy beach with a large number of sunbeds and umbrellas. However, entering the sea here is quite inconvenient - at the bottom there are many stones and dead corals. Therefore, if you want to swim and watch the inhabitants of a living coral reef, then you should go to the neighboring municipal beach. It can be reached on foot. On the road, according to tourists, it takes about five to ten minutes. Also, if you wish, you can always get to the beach or return from the shore to the hotel in an open car with wagons. Shuttle service is free.

The beach is sandy. True, the coast is full of small shells. For adult vacationers, this is not a problem, but for kids it is recommended to purchase special shoes. The entry into the water is sandy. Over several tens of meters, the depth is minimal. Parents with young children prefer to spend time here. Going further, you will find yourself in the depths and will be able to swim to the coral reef. According to tourists, it is inhabited by a mass of beautiful mosquito inhabitants. So, here you can see sea turtles, and colorful fish of different sizes and shapes, and stingrays, and crabs. Therefore, when you go on vacation, be sure to bring a snorkeling mask with you. By the way, those who wish to go diving can take the necessary equipment in the diving club of the hotel. There are also instructors who, if necessary, will give you several lessons.
