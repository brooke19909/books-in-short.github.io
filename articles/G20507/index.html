Nokia 5210 Mobile Phone Review

Nokia 5210 is a mobile phone developed by Nokia specifically for fans of active activities. This is evidenced not only by the external performance of the gadget, but also by its technical characteristics.

The model was introduced in 2002 and at that time was an innovative solution in terms of reliability.





Appearance

Nokia phones are distinguished by their reliability and durability of the case. And the Nokia 5210 was no exception.

Naturally, the first thing that catches your eye is the unusual appearance of the phone. The Nokia 5210 case is not made as a single design, but it is divided into two halves, which are easily disconnected from each other by the type of nested dolls. Having removed the case, you find the phone itself with a SIM card slot and a battery.

Housing Nokia 5210 is made of thick rubber and plastic, which increase the reliability of the phone. Thanks to this design, you can safely take your phone with you to any sports, even extreme ones. In this case, do not be afraid that the gadget will suffer from falling to the ground or from getting wet. The rugged case is equipped with a robust function of protection against dust, shock and moisture. As for the latter, Nokia 5210 should not be completely immersed in water, but the case will be able to save the housing from drops of water and damp.

Another feature of the phone is that the housing can be changed. To do this, you need to remove the old one: by clicking on the side points of the lower part of the phone, its upper part is removed; the lower one is removed in a similar way, but when you click on the points of the upper part.

The whole appearance of the phone gives out a representative of Nokia in it: the shape, location and controllability of the keys. The number buttons are made of high quality rubber, and the control keys are made of plastic. Pressing is light, effortless. On the side there are volume buttons, and on the top there is an on / off button.

The screen is monochrome, in the on mode it is highlighted with an orange backlight.





Interior fittings

The internal content is identical to previous Nokia models. The menu contains all the functions necessary in everyday life:

  1. A phone book whose memory is designed for 250 numbers. It is possible to use voice dialing, but it is limited to 10 numbers.
  2. WAP-browser that allows you to access the Internet.
  3. A calendar that is capable of carrying out reminders.

Specifications

The Nokia 5210 is a lightweight device weighing just 92 grams. Its parameters are 105.5 x 47.5 x 22.5 mm. The gadget is conveniently located in the hand, it is very convenient to press the keys.
