Crimea, Nikolaevka, boarding house "Sunny": description, features and reviews

So that during your vacation you can properly relax and fully enjoy the warm days, now you need to start planning your vacation in Crimea. Nikolaevka (boarding house "Lazurny", "Solnechny", "Energetik" and so on) is a great place to relax well away from the city bustle. It is especially good here with young children: the sea warms up quickly, the bottom is shallow.

General characteristics of the "Solar" (Crimea, Nikolaevka)





Pension "Solnechny" for many decades has been hosting various guests. It consists of three buildings and two buildings: one with a dining room, the other with a water sports center. Around is a large landscaped area, which is nice to walk not only in the evening, but also on a sunny day. Due to the large number of trees, shade and coolness is preserved.

There is a boarding house on the first coastline and has its own equipped beach.

Number of rooms





You can sit in one of three buildings. In 2010, a major reconstruction was carried out in each. The first and second buildings are two four-story buildings that are connected to each other by a common hall. The third has three floors and has two entrances. It is built as an apartment building, and there are four deluxe rooms on each floor. There is no elevator in any of the buildings.

Vacationers are offered to stay in single, double, triple, quadruple rooms and suites. Also on the territory there are two one-story log cabin houses. Everywhere there is a necessary set of furniture (single or double comfortable beds, bedside tables, wardrobe, table and chairs), as well as a refrigerator, satellite TV, internal telephone, safe, kettle and a set of dishes (plates, cups, tableware) .

The luxury apartments are additionally equipped with air conditioning, sterile machines and a full bathroom. Other rooms have shower stalls only.

The apartments are further divided into those with access to the balcony, and without it.

Services and services provided in the boarding house





The price includes the following services:

  • Three meals a day;
  • use of the pool, sports ground, tennis court;
  • visit to the cinema;
  • entrance to the beach and rental of beach equipment;
  • participation in cultural events organized by the boarding house administration;
  • use of a safe in the room and at the administrator;
  • use of ironing room;
  • visiting a salt cave;
  • parking a personal vehicle.

For an additional fee, you can also use the services of a masseur, sauna, bar, visit the attractions.

Pets are allowed in the boarding house, but only by prior arrangement.

Meals in a boarding house





Pension "Solnechny" (the village of Nikolaevka, Crimea) has its own dining room for up to 350 people. It serves buffet breakfast. There is also the opportunity to organize three meals a day from the custom menu.

Additionally, in the pension there is a bar.

Leisure activities





Pension "Solnechny" (Crimea, Nikolaevka) has a large territory, on which there are a lot of different entertainments. There are indoor and outdoor pools, a small cinema, a sports ground, a tennis court, a billiard room, bike rental, a sauna, beauty and massage rooms, and a hairdresser. Disco works with a certain frequency.

Private beach is a few meters from the residential buildings. The sandy and pebble shore. There is a point for issuing beach equipment (sunbeds, tents), as well as a lot of attractions (for example, water slides, scooters, catamarans and so on).

On the territory of the pension there is a salt cave, in which you can spend time with health benefits in comfortable seats.

For those who like to visit interesting places on vacation and learn something new, it is possible to book a tour directly from the administrator of the boarding house.

Unpleasant moments





Among the many positive comments, there are several points that vacationers do not really like, they would like the administration to correct them:

  • The rooms here are not the cheapest. Especially with this ratio of price and quality of service.
  • Pension ‚ÄúSolnechny‚Äù (Crimea, Nikolaevka) has its own dining room, where a table is assigned to each vacationer, so you cannot choose a place on your own. As it does not come out for breakfast, dinner alone or only with your family.

  • Pension "Solnechny" has existed for a long time, and even despite the fact that major repairs were carried out in the buildings, the influence of the years lived is felt in some places.
  • The site stated that the hot water in the guesthouse is constant. But in fact, this was not so. It is given on schedule - in the morning and in the evening. If you want to take a shower at another time, you will have to be content with cold water.
  • Wi-Fi in the pension does not work well. The rooms have virtually no internet.
  • Parking is not designed for a large number of vacationers. Those who do not have enough space are offered to leave the car on the roadway next to the boarding house.
