"Pine Paradise" (camping) - reviews. Camping "Pine Paradise" in Arkhipo-Osipovka (Krasnodar Territory)

If you want unity with nature, cheap holidays on the Black Sea coast, if you are comfortable sleeping in a tent and you are romantic, then Pine Paradise will be the ideal place for spending your summer vacation. This campsite is located in the Krasnodar Territory, near the resort town of Arkhipo-Osipovka.

What is this recreation center





The campsite is located in a pine-juniper forest, so the air there is not only clean, but also healing. Usually people who want to relax in cars come here, taking a tent with them. Next, you need to register with the administrator. Then, the camping staff will set aside for the new arrivals a place where you can set up your mini-camp.

Having put the car in the parking lot, you can start arranging your life and look around to find out what Pine Paradise offers. Camping includes the following amenities:

  • toilets;
  • showers;
  • wooden tables with benches;
  • drinking water;
  • barbecues.

The administration of the recreation center takes care of the preservation of the surrounding nature, safety, therefore, vacationers are allowed to light a fire only in special iron braziers. It is forbidden to chop, spoil trees, harm the flora and fauna, in the circle of which is "Pine Paradise."

Camping is suitable for those who are tired of the noise of cities and want privacy. Here lovers of southern nature, lovers of gatherings around the campfire with a guitar will also like it. By the way, from 23-00 to 7-00 it is forbidden to make noise here, turn on loud music, so a wonderful night's sleep and rest are guaranteed.





How to get to the campsite

If everything suits you, then it is worthwhile to find out about the route to easily find the way to this recreation area.

First go along the M4 highway. When the Vulan River, a bridge over it, is on your way, you will see a pointer. It will show the direction in the direction where such campsites are located. "Arkhipo-Osipovka" will be written on it. Take the following signs and continue along the main road. On Rabochaya Street, turn right.

Follow the main road, then on Vishnevaya street turn right. When you reach the T-junction, turn right again and keep the path to the sea.
