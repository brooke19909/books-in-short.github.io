LCD "Bright Valley": description, reviews

Kazan is one of the most beautiful cities in the country, the capital of the Republic of Tatarstan. The city is actively being built up and developing, new residential complexes and entire neighborhoods appear here, where all the necessary conditions for the most comfortable life of modern residents of the metropolis are created. Residential complex "Bright Valley" is one of the large-scale projects of modern urban development, which has absorbed all the charm of life in a megalopolis, starting from its advantageous geographical location and ending with thoughtful plans for every taste. Want to get to know the complex better? We will help you with this. The objectivity of the review will provide feedback from real residents.

Location

For the construction of the residential complex "Bright Valley" was chosen Sovetsky district, the eastern part of Kazan. The excellent location of the complex, convenient and thoughtful transport interchange on the Mamadyshsky tract, many public transport routes make living in the complex convenient for various categories of citizens. It is an ecologically clean area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city, ideal for families with small children.

Even those who have never been to this part of Kazan will be able to get to the complex. Moving from the center in the direction of the Mamadyshsky tract, after about 1.5 km you need to turn left, onto Nathan Rakhlin Street. Here you will see chic new buildings representing the complex "Bright Valley".

About the complex

33 hectares of land - just such a plot is reserved for the construction of a new microdistrict for life. 21 houses, built using monolithic-brick technology, make up the Svetlaya Dolina residential complex. About 16,000 people will find here a comfortable home of their dreams.

The complex is represented by several modules, separated by streets and driveways. Traditionally, the first floors of new buildings will be reserved for commercial infrastructure. Car owners do not have to worry about the place for their vehicle, because the project provides for the construction of underground and ground parking lots throughout the complex.

Architecture

In the construction of the complex "Bright Valley" the best traditions of modern buildings were used. Elegant French balconies, huge panoramic windows with excellent views of the city and its beauty are what many buyers focus on. The feedback of those who have already visited the site confirms how reverently the developer approaches the implementation of the project, how high-quality materials are used in the work process.

Infrastructure

Residential complex "Bright Valley", reviews of which already now testify to the high quality of the work performed, first of all offers apartments for families with small children, because all the conditions for the most comfortable life are created here. The developer provided for the construction of three kindergartens in the complex. You can always make the necessary purchases in shopping centers, the first of which was already opened five years ago. Within walking distance are several educational institutions, including those with in-depth study of foreign languages, children's clinics and medical centers.

Each courtyard has modern playgrounds and sports grounds, designed not only for children, but also for adults. Moreover, walking alleys and full-fledged recreation areas are provided where you can have a great time with friends and relatives.

Reviews

Reviews about the project "Bright Valley" are different. No modern residential complex after delivery can not avoid negative reviews. Many apartment owners are satisfied with the location of the complex and the favorable environmental situation in the area. The improvement of the local area, the quality of playgrounds and sports grounds are truly delighted. The residents are quite satisfied with the layout, the quality of construction: the houses turned out to be warm, with excellent sound insulation.

The disadvantages relate only to minor malfunctions in the operation of a number of engineering networks, primarily electricity supply. But according to the assurances of the developer and the management company, these failures are only temporary. Some residents are unhappy with the balconies, considering them inconvenient to operate. In general, the Svetlaya Dolina residential complex met the expectations of potential buyers. Apartments here at the moment can be purchased on favorable terms.
