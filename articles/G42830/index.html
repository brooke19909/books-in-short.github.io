Do you know how to pay for a phone with a Sberbank card?

Modern technology every day makes the life of ordinary people easier and easier. Many things can already be done with just one click. And of course, large companies keep up with this trend. Sberbank and the big three operators were no exception. They offer several options for how to pay for a phone with a Sberbank card. The client only has to choose a suitable method for himself. Moreover, payment is made without additional fees.



The most obvious and easiest is to pay at the nearest branch through an ATM. It is enough for the client to select the appropriate menu at the ATM and indicate the recipient's phone number. Moreover, for the convenience of its customers, the bank stores information about previously entered numbers on its server. If payment is made to another phone, you must also add a number to it. Through Sberbank-Online, you can pay for your phone with a Sberbank card remotely, via the Internet.
