Where is fake money used?

Fake bills may be needed not only for children who want to play in a cafe or a store, but also for adults. For example, for playing poker, Monopoly or another economic strategy.





Unfortunately, the game currency is often lost, torn and becomes worthless. Therefore, it is good to know how to make money out of paper. Of course, you can replace them with the purchase of a new set of banknotes or the whole game, but it is much easier and cheaper to make them with your own hands. Also, an additional kit may be needed if you want to play with a larger number of players than what the game was originally designed for.

Paper money can be used to teach children how to count. With their help, it is easy to explain to the baby how to pay at the store and correctly calculate the change. The main thing is not to forget to mention that this currency is not real and that it can only be used for games and training.

Three easy ways to make money yourself

Let's consider some options. The fastest way to make money out of paper is to find the right photo on the Internet and print it on a printer at home or go to a copy center.





For people with artistic skills, it will be easy to draw bills. It is not necessary that they be a copy of real banknotes. You can invent your own currency or redraw an existing game currency. For example, from the game "Monopoly".

If you have a scanner and a printer on hand, you can simply scan real bills and then print them. In the absence of a scanner, you can photograph a banknote on a phone or camera. For printing, a color MFP or a laser printer is better.

How to make money more believable?

To make money more credible, you should color the paper on which they will be printed or painted in tea or coffee. The process of making such paper is simple:

  1. Pour loose tea or instant coffee into a bowl of very hot water.
  2. Carefully place and strain.
  3. Allow fluid to cool slightly.
  4. Take an ordinary sheet of office paper and dip it in water for a couple of seconds.
  5. Remove the leaf from the water and leave to dry.
  6. When the leaf is almost dry, iron it. This will make the paper more even and comfortable for further work.
  7. Allow to dry to the end.
  8. On the resulting sheet of paper you need to print or draw "fake money."




The resulting color of the sheet will more closely resemble the material on which real money is printed. If you take office paper with a density of 180-210 grams, then such banknotes will resemble real ones even by touch.
