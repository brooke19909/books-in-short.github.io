If you have long dreamed of relaxing in exotic countries, then a trip to Bali will be just unforgettable for you. This island is considered to be the most popular Indonesian resort, where tourists from all over the world come to see the picturesque landscapes, as well as a large number of cultural and historical attractions, religious buildings and visit various entertainments. A trip to Bali for the New Year can be your perfect vacation, no matter how you imagine it. Are you dreaming of lying on sunny beaches or playing sports, sightseeing trips or relaxing in a spa? Seasons in Bali

A trip to Bali is possible both in winter and in summer - the impressions that the island leaves behind will not depend on the weather. But if you do not want to hide during the holidays from the rain or the scorching sun and the real heat, you need to calculate what season you want to relax.

In the spring, the off-season begins on the island, rainy weather gives way to the gentle sun. If showers are still pouring in March, then in April it is already quite possible to come to Bali without fear of not getting to the beach. The driest and sunniest month among the spring months is May. At this time, the air temperature reaches more than 30 degrees.





In summer, you can also relax in Bali, as this season is sunny and the humidity level is minimal. However, you will not feel the heat, as fresh air here will provide a light breeze from the coast. The water temperature reaches more than 25-27 degrees Celsius, so you can enjoy plenty of fun in the ocean or go diving.

At the beginning of the autumn season, the climax of the beach season begins, but already in the last September decade you will feel how the north wind blows. October and November are times of heavy rains and high levels of humidity in the air. During this period, the island becomes stuffy, therefore, due to the heat, the tourist flow is significantly reduced.

In winter, in December, a period of local summer begins in Bali. You will be able to observe the growth of tropical plants. However, sometimes it may rain, and not quite short. But you should not worry about the weather in Bali for the New Year. At this time, tourists still come to the island. In a secluded atmosphere you can meet the holidays in Bali. Having decided to go on holiday to Bali for the New Year, you should first learn about what features Indonesia has.
