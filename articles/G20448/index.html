"IPhone-10": photos, descriptions, specifications, owner reviews

The photos of the iPhone-10 phone are impressive, the company released this device as a gift to customers on their anniversary. The phone is completely different from previous smartphones, so it immediately interested buyers. Particularly noteworthy are the cameras and the screen. More detailed information about the advantages and disadvantages of the phone will be presented in the article.

Specifications

It should be noted that not only the iPhone-10 photo is impressive, but also its characteristics. They shocked fans of the company, and even haters.

  • Display type OLED.
  • Resolution 2436 x 1125 (5.8 inches).
  • The maximum brightness level is 625 nits.
  • The phone runs on an A11 processor.
  • It is 64-bit, has 6 cores.
  • Co-processor - M11.
  • It functions on the iOS 11 operating system.
  • Memory: operational 3 GB and internal 64/256 GB.

The main camera of the double type, 12 megapixels, has dual optical stabilization, LED flash, double zoom. Shoots videos in 4K resolution (60 frames per second). Front camera - 7 megapixels. The battery is non-removable, supports fast charging.

Wireless charging is also supported. The kit includes headphones, charging, cable, adapter from the installed headphone jack to the standard one.

Dimensions: 14.36 x 7.06 x 0.7 cm, weight: 174 g. Went on sale November 3, 2017. Cost - 80 thousand rubles.

Display

Why are buyers so impressed with the iPhone 10? This is due to the use of a new type of display, previously in Apple smartphones a completely different screen was used. The new one added support for HDR10, True Tone, the glass is covered with an oleophobic coating, thanks to it, the phone is not affected by moisture and there are no fingerprints left.

The display perfectly displays HDR quality content. Density - 458 dpi. Due to the wide range of color shades, anyone will be delighted with the use of this phone.

Iron and operating system

Photos of iPhone-10 do not convey how well the phone works and why. The device runs on an A11 processor, six cores are installed, the nominal clock frequency is 2.5 GHz. Graphic acceleration is at a high level, the coprocessor is M11. There is a neural connection, thanks to it, machine learning is implemented and artificial intelligence works perfectly. Due to the installed chipset, smartphone performance is at the highest level. The smartphone is faster than some MacBooks. As mentioned above, the RAM in the phone is 3 GB, built-in - 64 and 256 GB. The release of the operating system that is installed on the phone took place on September 19 of the same year.

Camera phone

If you copy a photo from iPhone-10 to a computer, you will notice the highest image quality. In the new smart, a pair of 12-megapixel modules received double stabilization. The first has aperture f / 1.8, the second - f / 2.4. Such indicators in modern conditions are the best. The front-facing camera with a resolution of 7 megapixels has support for the Retina Flash on-screen flash, as well as HDR.

The main camera has augmented reality support. The ARKit function is already available to a large number of users, but in the "top ten" it is implemented using the TrueDepth rear camera.

In the reviews they write that any objects are obtained perfectly on images taken on the iPhone-10. Photos of flowers or a small butterfly - all this will be beautifully detailed.

Autonomy

Inside the case is a lithium-polymer battery. The manufacturer claims that the phone works two hours longer than the iPhone-7. In music playback mode, the device operates for about 60 hours and 21 hours - with constant conversations. The company installed fast charging, but the adapter that comes with the kit is not able to provide the required power.

Sales in Russia and cost

The phone went on sale on November 3, not only in Russia, but also in other countries of the world. On October 27, a pre-order smartphone was opened. By that time, a large number of iPhone-10 photos appeared on the Web. The device is sold in shades: silver and gray space.

The cost of the phone is about 80 thousand rubles for a configuration with 64 GB of memory, and the top-end configuration with 256 GB will cost 90 thousand.If you want the phone to be able to quickly charge, you will have to pay 4 thousand rubles, the same will cost wireless.

Features

The phone operates at a transfer rate of 1.2 Gb / s. In addition, the smartphone was able to work with fast Wi-Fi. Installed "bluetooth" version 5, NFS and Glonass. The smartphone also works with a mobile payment system, stereo speakers, a voice assistant are installed.
