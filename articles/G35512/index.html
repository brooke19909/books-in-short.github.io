Zakynthos. Reviews of vacationers about a unique and fabulous vacation

Among the turquoise waters of the warm Ionian Sea is a paradise called Zakynthos. This is a small Greek island, covered with green vegetation. It is located near the island of Peloponnese and the island of California. Tourism is developing at a rapid pace, but at the same time, the interests of residents are taken into account in order not to disturb the natural environment of Zakynthos. Feedback from numerous tourists suggests that in the last three years this territory has become more accessible for Russian citizens. This is due to the opening of direct flights.



If you have chosen a country such as Greece for your holidays, the islands (Zakynthos and others) will be the perfect place for an unforgettable experience. Zakynthos is a pretty beautiful island with excellent landscapes. Most of it is small mountains covered with pine forests. In the eastern and southern parts there are magnificent beaches, and the western coast is covered with cliffs with mysterious caves. Among these rocks there are cozy coves with almond and olive groves.



Those who have already rested on the island of Zakynthos, leave only positive reviews . Most tourists who come here prefer a beach holiday. The most popular resort is Agrassi, whose advantage is the vibrant nightlife. Here are the cleanest sandy beaches with all kinds of entertainment. Guests of this resort are offered to stay in hotels of various categories depending on their needs and financial condition. In the evening and at night there are restaurants and night clubs.



But this is not the only popular resort on the island of Zakynthos. Guest reviews confirm that Tsiliva is no less popular, where sandy beaches are marked with the Blue Flag. And for those who can not imagine their vacation without water sports, it is recommended to choose the resort of Alikanas. It is here that almost all young people strive. In addition, the forums quite often mention the resorts of the Laganas Bay, which are highly rated in the reviews. Its beaches stretch for 9 kilometers. It is here that you can see rare sea turtles. It should be noted that at night the beaches are closed to tourists.

What other places are popular on the island of Zakynthos? The reviews left by holidaymakers contain references to the Vasilikoe peninsula with its beautiful sand and pebble beaches and convenient access to the sea. Those who prefer a moderate and relaxing holiday come here.



You need to know that beautiful beaches are not the only attractive places in this paradise called Zakynthos. Attractions include on their list such an amazing place as an orchid garden located in the village of Agios Nikolaos. It will be interesting to visit the agricultural museum, which is located in the village of Pigadakya. And visiting the National Park, you will learn a lot about the turtles and other inhabitants of these places. In the capital of Zakynthos, you can see old mansions, the temple of St. Dionysius, which is considered the patron saint of this island, and the Byzantine Museum, which contains a collection of icons, ancient statues and sculptures.
