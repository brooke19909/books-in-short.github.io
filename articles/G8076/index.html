Tips for hostesses. Remaking old jeans

It makes no sense to ask a modern person if he has jeans in his wardrobe. Of course there is. And, most likely, not alone. These clothes have won worldwide love for their convenience, practicality and durability. And it often happens that your favorite jeans become outdated morally, but not physically, because durable fabric can be worn for years. In this case, alteration of old jeans can help: it will not only refresh the product, but also allow you to create your own unique style.





When will remaking help?

You need to give a new life to old jeans if they are well-fitting and not worn out but out of fashion. After lying a year or two in the closet, these jeans are sent as a gift to a friend or mother or change their purpose and are worn in the country. No need to do this, because adding a few new elements and refreshing the design, you can get a new, relevant thing. And it will cost quite inexpensively, and new, well-fitting jeans will appear in the wardrobe.

A second life can be given to a spoiled thing. Often, a beloved couple is no longer worn due to a hole, spot or puff. Such annoying troubles can be masked and turned into virtues. And then your jeans will sparkle in a new way.

Sometimes you just want variety and fresh colors that are different from what ‚Äúeveryone wears‚Äù. With your own hands you can do an absolutely exclusive thing that will emphasize the dignity of the figure and the taste of its owner.





Old jeans with new holes

Ripped jeans return to fashion with enviable regularity. Depending on the trends, slightly worn trousers or continuous holes with threads become relevant, for which many do not want to give large amounts. Remaking old jeans will help to get an trendy thing without significant costs.

It‚Äôs easy to do this:

  • on clean jeans you need to mark the places where you want to see large or small cuts with a piece of soap or chalk;
  • try to do the initial marking on wearing jeans, so you can avoid cuts in uncomfortable or not very beautiful places;
  • carefully using a pair of scissors, cut along the intended line;
  • pull the thread along the edges of the cut with a needle to give a look of casual shabbiness;
  • wash updated jeans in a spin washing machine for a more natural look.

Remember that any cuts and holes on jeans increase in size over time, so do not overdo it. For greater originality, you can sew lace or colored fabric from the inside of the hole.





Jeans are turning ...

A very popular alteration of old jeans is trimming the legs for shorts. Indeed, it is not difficult to do this, it is enough to choose the desired length and carefully cut off the legs. And denim shorts are always a thing you need in your wardrobe.

They can be made just above the knee or short. Denim shorts with frayed edges look stylish. To obtain the fringe effect, it is necessary to cut the edges with a needle after cutting the legs, pulling the transverse threads to obtain the desired appearance.

You can also hem or overcast the edges of shorts, sew lace or trim around the edge. Having shown imagination, you can get a convenient and original thing.





Decor and decoration

Sometimes jeans repair does not cope with the task and quietly sew up a hole or wipe the stain does not work. In this case, decorative elements that will not only decorate old jeans, but also mask minor defects, can help.

You can use ready-made applications, patch decorative pockets, rhinestones, beads, bugles, fringe. You can decorate only pockets and seams, the finish of jeans turns also looks very nice. Decor in random order over the entire surface of the legs always looks fresh and original.

Do not be afraid to experiment: remaking old jeans that have been lying on shelves for years will help you to show your creativity, an individual sense of style and give you a new stylish thing!
