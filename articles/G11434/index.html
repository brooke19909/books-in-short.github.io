Residential complex Nevskaya Zvezda, developer of IPS: address, construction progress

LCD "Nevskaya Zvezda", which is being built in the Nevsky district of St. Petersburg, is a complex development. The apartments in it belong to the economy class, which in no way detracts from their quality. True, unfortunately, the start of construction work coincided with the onset of the economic crisis, which affected many, including the real estate market. Many objects were completely frozen. But not LCD Nevskaya Zvezda. The developer, the IPS company, well-known to many, has adequately emerged from the current situation. The deadlines for the completion of the facilities, however, had to be reviewed, however, work on the sites did not stop for a minute.



A few words about the company

Construction work is carried out by the IPS company. Neva Star is far from its first project of such a plan. The developer enjoys well-deserved respect in the market and is famous for his excellent reputation. In addition, it works in a very interesting format. All procedures are completely carried out by the developer independently - starting from the project creation process and ending directly with the construction and installation works. The company does not involve third-party organizations in its activities, and therefore, it always hand over the objects on time and without any imperfections.

The concern takes a very responsible approach to its activities, trying to offer St. Petersburg residents the most favorable prices for apartments, while making every effort to ensure that the quality of housing does not suffer. All work is carried out using the latest technologies in the field of construction and using only the best materials.



About the complex

LCD "Nevskaya Zvezda" (St. Petersburg), as already mentioned, is a complex development. It will be a whole microdistrict equipped with its own facilities of all necessary infrastructure - both social and shopping and entertainment. It is planned to commission five houses of different storeys, consisting of several sections. The construction is carried out according to a mixed technology - frame-monolithic with partial use of gas silicate blocks. In parallel with the houses, infrastructure facilities as well as two parking lots are being built. At the end of construction, it will be a full-fledged town, the inhabitants of which will be able to enjoy all the benefits of civilization without leaving the microdistrict.

Already today you can buy apartments in the residential complex "Nevskaya Zvezda". Stage 1 was commissioned at the beginning of 2016, at this stage work is underway on the construction of buildings of the second and third stage.



Construction progress

The project is considered to be complex, because the construction is planned to be carried out in several stages. As already mentioned, work began in the midst of a severe crisis. In this regard (and not at all due to the fault of the builder), the deadlines had to be postponed from the end of 2014 to the beginning of 2016. What provoked a lot of different rumors about the residential complex "Nevskaya Zvezda". Reviews and interest holders, and those who considered the complex as an option for buying a home, were very negative at that moment. Nevertheless, the company survived, and today you can buy in the complex any apartment of 404 put up for sale in the first stage.

At this stage, the construction of houses of the second and third stage is underway. All work is carried out in strict accordance with the schedule, no delays are observed, so, according to experts, the deadlines will be met. A three-sectional house with 20 floors will be commissioned in early 2017. Already today you can buy any of the 342 apartments offered on the basis of equity participation in the house of the second stage of the Nevskaya Zvezda residential complex. Stage 3, the construction of which is also at an active pace, will be commissioned in 2019.

The IPS developer provides everyone with complete information on the construction progress and allows equity holders and potential buyers to visit their construction sites. So everyone can find the residential complex "Nevskaya Zvezda" at the address: st. Grandmother 61.



Ecology of the region

Nevsky district is considered quite prosperous in terms of environmental conditions. There are no industrial enterprises here, in addition, it borders on a suburban area, and therefore there are forests and a river nearby. In addition, in order to improve the environmental situation, the developer plans to carry out mass work on landscaping the territories of the microdistrict.

Transport connection

The multi-apartment complex is located in close proximity to the Proletarskaya metro station, which is a definite plus in living conditions in the metropolis. In addition to the metro, residents of the LCD can use the services of buses and minibuses, the stop of which is located next to the houses. On Obukhovskaya Defense Avenue , you can get to the city center (assuming there is no traffic jam, the journey will take no more than ten minutes by car), in addition, there are two exits to the roundabout two minutes from the complex, which makes it convenient to go to the Leningrad Region.
