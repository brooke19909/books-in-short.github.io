How to choose a flat-screen TV? Which brand to prefer?

For many buyers, a lot of different parameters play a role when choosing a TV. However, not everyone will be able to explain for themselves the first time what exactly they want to see before their eyes every night. On the Internet, there are a lot of tips on how to choose this technique correctly. The range is so wide that anyone's eyes will run wide. Most buyers focus on those devices that impress with their functionality and useful life. Flat panel TVs have long been popular. What should I look for when choosing such a technique?



Types of Flat TVs

The most popular are plasma, LED and LCD TVs. How do they differ among themselves?

Plasma TV displays the picture due to the fact that it sends an electric discharge to the plasma, which fills the cells.

Liquid crystal devices are distinguished by the fact that they consist of liquid-type crystals (hence the name). They are located between two plates of glass. The display is due to the fact that a discharge is supplied to the crystals and a fluorescent lamp illuminates them.

LED flat-panel TVs are similar in design to the previous ones, however, instead of the light source described above, they use LEDs.



Contrast comparison

Before buying, the consumer should see how well the TV is capable of showing bright and darkened pictures at the same time. The higher the contrast level, the better the image quality. If it is low, then in fairly bright / dark pictures a person will not be able to examine certain details. For some buyers, this aspect is very important. Often all flat-panel TVs have excellent contrast. Plasma comes first, LED comes second, and liquid crystal comes third, respectively. It should be noted that LED devices have a backlight. If they have its lateral type, then together with LCD devices will be noticeably inferior in parameters.

In most liquid crystal devices, the contrast starts at 600: 1, while in plasmas this figure is four times greater.

You need to choose the flat-screen TV that shows black images best. This question seems not serious, but many budget models give such a picture faded.



Brand selection

To choose the right flat device, you need to pay attention to many parameters. An important role is played by the brand. Any specialist will say that you need to purchase such a device, starting from your own preferences. In most cases, all large and small flat TVs are created using the same technology, regardless of manufacturer. But many consumers think differently - therefore, the question of choosing a brand becomes quite relevant.

In order to understand which brand is guaranteed to give only a pleasant experience, you need to pay attention only to already popular brands. Next, you should consider all the models you like and make the final choice. Flat TVs from Samsung and Sony are considered the best in this category of technology. Therefore, it will be incredibly difficult to choose a truly worthy among them - you will have to compare all the criteria.
