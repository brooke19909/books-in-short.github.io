How many cantons, united, created Switzerland? Briefly about each

Switzerland is a state that is located in the western part of Europe and borders with countries such as Germany, France, Austria, Liechtenstein and France. The system of administrative division of Switzerland involves the division into cantons, which, in turn, are divided into districts, and districts - into communities and cities. From this article you will find out how many cantons, having united, created Switzerland and briefly information about each of them.

Berne

The capital of the state - Bern - is both the center of the canton of the same name and the administrative center of the Bern-Mittelland district. This city is located in the central region of Switzerland, in a northerly direction with respect to the Alps near the river Aara. Bern is just the fifth largest city in the country with a population of 133.9 thousand people. The capital is also the political center of the country, here is the government, central bank and parliament. The territory of the canton covers an area of ​​5959 km 2 , and a population of almost 980 thousand people.

Few people know how many cantons created Switzerland, but it turns out there are only 26, of which 6 are semi-cantons.





Appenzell-ausserroden

This half-canton is located in the north-east of the country, a German-speaking population lives on its territory. The administrative center of this region is Herisau, and the vowels of the judiciary are located in Trogen. The population of this half-canton is about 53 thousand people, and the area is 243 km 2 .

Appenzell-Innerroden

This is another half-canton located in the north-east of the country. Its capital is the city of Appenzel, and the population is about 15 thousand people with an area of ​​173 km 2 .

Argau

This canton is located in the north of the country. Its area is 1.4 thousand km 2 , and the population is about 573 thousand. The administrative center of this territory is the same name as the region. This is a fairly flat area, the Are River flows here, which divides the region into two parts. 11 districts converge here, which, in turn, consist of more than two hundred municipalities.

Basel Land

The cantons of Switzerland, the list of which is presented in the article, mainly German-speaking, Basel Land is no exception. The capital of the region is the city of Liestal, covering an area of ​​518 km 2 .



Basel Stadt

This is one of the smallest half-cantons of Switzerland. Its capital is the city of Basel, and the territory is only 37 km 2 .

Valais

It is a southwestern canton whose capital is Sion. The area of ​​the region is 5224 km 2 , and the population is almost 300 thousand people. The capital of the canton is located on the banks of the Rhone, and to the north-east of it is the resort of Crans-Montana.

The French-speaking canton, which is very small in size, is located in the south-west of Switzerland. The capital is the city of Geneva. The population is 448 thousand people. The capital of the canton - the city of Solothurn - is considered the largest city in the country, built in the Baroque style. Located on the Are River, at the foot of the Jurassic Mountains.

Lucerne

This is a German-speaking territory, the area of ​​which is almost one and a half thousand km 2 , with a population of 351 thousand people.

St gallen
