Photos Tokio Hotel - admire

What does the phrase Tokio Hotel tell you? Yes, this is translated from German as "Tokyo Hotel". But still, it will be about a youth musical group from the German city of Magdeburg.



Composition

If you look at the photo of Tokio Hotel, you can see that the group consists of four young men. The foundation for its creation was laid by two twin brothers, born in September 1989. Already at the age of nine, they began a singing career, and in 2001, meeting with two more future members, they created the Devilish group.

And only in 2003, when fate brought these young musicians with the famous producer Peter Hoffman, after signing a contract with Universal Music, the band became known as Tokio Hotel.

The exceptional authenticity of their music permeates with a mysterious sound, drawing a large audience into an enchanting cycle. Everything that was heard before the amazing songs of these guys began to seem gray and boring. Numerous photos of Tokio Hotel clearly represent this. And most of them have a bunch of fans who are ready to give everything in order to just touch these young musicians or get their coveted autograph from them.

Numerous fans of this group have already grown up, some have started families. What happened to these gifted guys now, because nowadays not so much is heard about them? To do this, just look at the photos of Tokio Hotel on the World Wide Web sites, to evaluate how they have changed.
