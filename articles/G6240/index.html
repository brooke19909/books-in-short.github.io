Are Kemer hotels suitable for families with children?



Going on vacation with the whole family is not only a troublesome business, but also a responsible one. How to plan your trip so that it is comfortable and interesting for everyone: children (fastidious toddlers and moody teenagers), and his wife, and even mother-in-law? Many tourists choose Turkey for such trips. A short flight, the absence of the need to ask for a visa from the embassy for a long time, the mild climate of the Mediterranean make this country a key vacation for the whole family. And how not to notice Kemer among the various offers? This is a wonderful resort near Antalya, protected from the northern winds by green mountains. But are Kemer hotels suitable for families with children? Let's try to figure it out.

In Turkey, children are treated with reverence, and you can safely come to this country even with a baby. In this regard, Kemer hotels are no exception. For families with children, there are cots with high sides, high chairs in restaurants and a lot of specific entertainments for kids: from playgrounds to mini-clubs and water parks. To lure couples, the hotel administration offers big discounts for small guests, and often takes kids for free.

Of course, in order for the tourist tourists to be comfortable in every sense: accommodation, food, leisure, one should choose four- and even five-star Kemer hotels. For families with children, some of them, such as Marti Myra Holiday Village, even have children's rooms in two-story cottages. Most of these hotels are located on the front page of the sea, and small legs will not get tired of walking from the shore for lunch and back. Covering the beaches of Kemer - pebbles, but so small that you should not be afraid of injuries. On the contrary, unlike sand, it does not stick to the body.
