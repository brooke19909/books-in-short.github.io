Global Location Systems

All modern navigation devices work closely with the orbiting satellite constellations of systems such as GPS and GLONASS.

Between these systems, the difference is very small, and often people do not even notice it. However, this does not mean that it is not. GLONASS - Russian satellite orbital constellation. She works great on the territory of Russia. Its main drawback is that the quality of work deteriorates as it moves away from the Russian borders, and it also has fewer satellites.

GPS is the second navigation system that Starline M15 can work with. Let's be honest, he interacts with her much better. Firstly, the orbiting satellite constellation is larger, and the devices themselves are more reliable and modern. The system operates flawlessly throughout the planet, and the beacon signal will not disappear even in the most remote regions of Russia. Secondly, this system works with less errors and is generally easier to configure.

Special purpose

Search lighthouse "Starline M15" is installed in various vehicles. These are optional cars. It is used on agricultural machinery, on water and is even used in tactical games related to geographical marks.

The beacon allows the vehicle owner to track the exact location. So with it you can accurately find out the trucker's route or see if the company car is used for personal or work purposes. It is also an important element of the anti-theft security system. However, unlike other protective devices, it should not prevent a crime, but rather facilitate operational-search measures.

The principle of the lighthouse

The principle of operation of any such beacon is quite simple. A special location terminal is hidden in the device‚Äôs body, which is connected with satellites. They receive a request to the device, and the beacon answers them. Thus, the fixation of the geolocation of the object. Then all the information is uploaded to a special secure server, and there the user can access it. The principle of operation sounds complicated, but in fact, this chain runs almost instantly and without delays, and Starline M15 pleases the operator with a quick response.

There is only one problem, and it is also a common one. Thunderstorm front, heavy cloud cover or normal weather - all of them lead to an error in the coordinates. Also, the signal does not pass well through structures. Reinforced concrete completely shields the lighthouse, interrupting its connection with the satellite. If during a bad weather the vehicle was led into a reinforced concrete garage or underground parking, it will be difficult to find it, because the coordinates will be approximate.

Base model M15

The Starline product line does not end with a single beacon. It is represented by several good products. They vary both in price and in functionality. However, all of them, according to motorists, perform the same function.

As a rule, attackers capture lighthouses and retrieve them from the vehicle. It will be very difficult to do so with the M15. In addition, this mode of operation protects the battery.
