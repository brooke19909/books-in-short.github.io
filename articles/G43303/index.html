Bread machine "Philips": instructions and reviews

In a modern kitchen you can not do without many special appliances. Including the current housewives are already accustomed to such a popular technique as a bread machine. One of the most common brands of such equipment is Philips. Bread machines of this company have gained great popularity due to its functionality and ease of use. Philips bread machine is a convenient device that will allow you to bake fresh bread without leaving your home. What are the advantages and disadvantages of such models? We will talk about this later.





Bread machines Philips 9020

Such devices allow you to bake a wide variety of products. The Philips HD9020 bread machine includes twelve cooking options, and also has a volume of 1000 g, so it is often chosen on the technology market. Devices are presented in two colors - white and lilac.

Features of using Philips HD9020

Management of the bread machine does not cause difficulties for the majority of users. Convenient buttons make it easy to configure the necessary options.

First you need to choose the type of baking, its weight according to the recipe, as well as how much it should be rosy. Next, you can run the program you need. Using the icons on the device, you can easily understand the meaning of each button. Also, any selected setting is always displayed on the device display.

Philips bread machine allows you to add ingredients to bake bread with different additives.





Inside the device capacity is equipped with a coating with non-stick properties. In addition, the bread machine has a removable lid, so it‚Äôs easy to clean (just detach the lid and clean it, which will take very little time).

In the set, along with the technique, is a recipe book, as well as a measuring spoon and a glass. Using the viewing window, the user can observe the baking of products.

Set of programs of the Philips bread maker 9020

This equipment has 12 options for preparing various products. You can bake whole grain or gluten-free bread, French rolls or pastries. In addition, Philips 9020 allows you to cook pastry for pasta or pizza, sweets, or even make jam and jams.

In the case when you have very little time, you can use the program for accelerated baking, with the help of which it becomes possible to get the finished product in just an hour!

There is a timer that will delay the start of cooking. In this case, you can schedule a start after a certain period of time up to as many as thirteen hours!

The Philips 9020 bread machine, like other similar devices, allows you to bake bread of different sizes. Using special marks on the device, you can cook products that will weigh 0.5 kg, 0.75 kg and 1 kg.

In addition, you can choose how much the pastry will be rosy. The crust can be light, medium or dark (it depends on the recipe or on personal preference).





Philips bread makers HD9045

This model has a more advanced temperature control system. The Philips bread machine makes it possible to bake a variety of breads that have a light, golden or completely dark crust. To do this, just click on the appropriate button on the device.

Using a special addition indicator, bread can be cooked with various additional ingredients. To do this, when kneading the test, the device will emit a certain sound, which will indicate that the user can add the necessary products.

Philips 9045 bread machines have an original design. Their work is not heard, because the noise level is minimal - only 55 dBA.

A recipe book is also sold in a set along with a bread machine.

With the help of a special window, you can watch how the product is prepared and how a golden crust appears on it.

The device is functional, however, like many other Philips bread makers. Reviews suggest that such a technique can be safely easily transported, since the handles on it do not slip at all. Due to the small size of the appliance, in any kitchen you can find a place for it.





Philips Software Suite HD9045

The programs that are available here guarantee the required temperature and baking time for different types of bread.

Products can be of various sizes (medium or large). Indicate the necessary parameters when preparing the product by pressing the appropriate button on the control panel of the device (from 750 g to 1 kg).

This model already has fourteen programs with which you can bake whole-grain, gluten-free or French pastries, sweet muffin. The user can prepare products in accordance with the original recipe. So, a bread machine allows you to bake such types of bread as Borodino, Easter pastries, and just make dough, jam or yogurt.





In the absence of a large amount of time, you can use the accelerated cooking mode.

There is also a delayed start mode for baking bread, so you can set a timer for the night and get the finished product right in the morning.

Model 9016

The material for making the case of the Philips 9016 bread machine is plastic. The container in which the products are placed is equipped with a non-stick coating. In front, like on other models manufactured by Philips, there is a control panel with a screen and buttons that allow you to choose the level of browning of the crust of the product, start and finish cooking, and set the baking time.

At the top and in HD9016 there is a special window for observing bread. On the screen during cooking, the time remaining until the end of baking is displayed.

The set of Philips bread maker contains instructions and a collection with recipes. There are not very many of them, and most of them relate only to bread, but the book describes the preparation of a huge number of its types. With the help of this collection, it will be much easier for a novice housewife to learn how to bake various products. The recipe book also lists the main ingredients, describes how to measure the volume and weight of products. Each type of bread is accompanied by a description of its variations. Any recipe can be improved independently, for example, with the help of various additives.





The bread maker makes any noise only at the stage of kneading the dough, but otherwise it works absolutely silently.

Be careful with this technique! When the bread maker is working, a certain amount of hot air may come out of the ventilation on the lid.

The equipment is easily washed from excess dough, etc. It is enough to simply remove the container and clean it (you can even not use detergent).

This model of bread machine is easy to find at points of sale.

HD-9016 Software Suite

The bread machine can work in twelve different programs. There is a mode of baking white bread (or accelerated cooking of white bread, when the product is small in size and has a high density of the product), as well as bread from rye and baking flour together, French rolls and sweet pastries. You can, for example, use gluten-free ingredients. A bread machine also prepares such pastries, as well as whole grains, Easter cakes, makes dough or can even make yogurt and jam.





How to cook yogurt in a Philips bread maker?

For many housewives, this possibility seems rather unusual. However, preparing yogurt in this way is very simple. Just pour milk into the right container from the set that comes with the bread maker (the ultra-pasteurized version is recommended). Then add 50 g of yogurt to it. After the bread machine finishes cooking the product, you can also add different berries or jam. The process lasts 3 hours.

But according to customer reviews, the result is really impressive. At the exit, after the end of cooking in the bread machine, we get a whole 1 liter of yogurt. And despite the fact that initially only 50 grams of the product is added to milk.
