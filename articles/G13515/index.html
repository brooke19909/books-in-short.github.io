The Pearl of Asia - Lake Borovoe, Kazakhstan

The real pearl of Asia is Lake Borovoe, Kazakhstan. This unique corner is located in the north of the country between the capital Astana and the Kokshetau mountains. Many, probably, cannot even imagine that there can be such places in the Kazakh steppes. What is unique about Borovoy? Today we will reveal all the beauties of Northern Kazakhstan.

Nature and climate



The mountains of Kokshetau with their highest point, Kokshe, popularly called Sinegorye, rise 947 m. Rare crystalline rocks for this area lie in massifs among the steppes. The slopes of the mountains are covered with pine and birch. The natural landscape under the influence of time looks unusual, mysterious. Ponds with pure water fill Northern Kazakhstan. Lake Borovoe is a resort area, which includes 14 large lakes, and the most important of them is Shchuchye. This is probably why this place is considered the main natural attraction of the country. The local climate allows you to relax here all year. In summer, there is warm water and a sandy beach, in winter - skiing and always clean air.

Rest on Lake Borovoe (Kazakhstan)



To date, the government has made tourism development one of the priority areas. The resort area was organized in the city of Schuchinsk, on the territory of which there is Lake Borovoe. Kazakhstan has become famous on the world stage not only for its achievements in economics and politics, but also for this natural zone. Thousands of tourists from the CIS countries rest here annually . Therefore, Borovoy's infrastructure is at the proper level, and nature itself has been taken under state protection. What rest does Borovoe offer? Here you can not only relax in good quality, but also improve and improve your health by living in hotels, resorts, while at recreation centers. We will tell you more about some of them.

Hotel "Gloria"

The hotel is a building of three floors with suites, junior suites and economy rooms. 200 meters from it is Lake Borovoe itself (Kazakhstan). The hotel is equipped with a swimming pool and sauna, and massage services are also available there. Guided tours on horseback or horseback are conducted by experienced guides. You can sunbathe on the sandy beaches, and swim in the lake. In the restaurants of Eastern and European cuisine you can eat deliciously, and all this for a moderate fee.

Sanatorium "Zhumbaktas"

It is represented by a three-story building and several summer houses. On the territory of the sanatorium, located among the pines, there is a medical base. The beach is 1 km from the sanatorium. Here they carry out mud treatment, laser treatment, various kinds of body cleansing, etc. Guided tours are also provided, while sports facilities, discos, bars and restaurants are available for entertainment.

Recreation center "Aigerim"



This recreation center has its own summer houses and a hotel complex, located 1 km from Lake Borovoe. Accommodation here is inexpensive, but meals and additional services are available for a fee.

Of course, much work still needs to be done in terms of developing such a wonderful place as Lake Borovoe. Kazakhstan is developing rapidly, which means that in the near future they will know about this pearl of Central Asia far beyond the borders of the country. And now, holidays in Borovoye are an ideal solution for those who want to relax for a little money away from city noise and enjoy the beauty of unique nature.
