general characteristics

The hotel complex Palm Garden Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Bodrum) opened its doors to guests in the past millennium in 1997. During its functioning, travelers from every corner of the globe visited it. The available number of rooms for settlement is 240 units, divided into categories - standard and family. The smaller ones are 24 squares, the larger ones are 50. Beach

A public sand and pebble beach is located two kilometers from the hotel. During the day, a free bus runs in both directions. The use of sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses is free of charge.
