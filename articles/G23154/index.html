Hotel itself

In general, the hotel complex The Desert Rose Resort 5 * (Hurghada, Egypt), our compatriots were satisfied. So, last year a number of tourists pointed to the fact that some rooms need minor repairs. In the same year, travelers in their reviews note that the hotel began renovation. Therefore, vacationers planning to soon stay in this hotel will probably have to live in already renovated apartments. In general, the rooms here are spacious, bright, clean and pleasantly decorated. Tourists note modern technology, comfortable beds, a spacious shower room, nice balconies with a pleasant view. The only drawback that some guests indicate in their comments is the fact that there is no way to watch programs in Russian on TV. This was especially not liked by family tourists, whose children are used to watching cartoons. Therefore, experienced travelers recommend going here on vacation, just in case, upload a few cartoons for your children on a tablet or laptop. As for the Internet, guests at the hotel can use Wi-Fi for free. True, its speed is not very good, but for checking mail and social networks this is quite enough.

Our compatriots expressed some complaints about cleaning. When the hotel was not filled to capacity, there were no problems with this. However, in periods when there were a lot of guests, the maids, apparently, simply could not cope with the amount of work. But in general, for the vast majority of tourists this was not a problem.



Comments about the territory and location of the hotel

Our compatriots in their reviews regarding accommodation at The Desert Rose Resort (Hurghada, Egypt), note that the hotel complex has a huge, very well-groomed, beautiful and clean area. Tourists especially liked that everything was very well planned here. So, on the territory there are several pools and bars. All of them are located at some distance from each other, so people, as they say, are not crowded. And even at a time when the hotel is almost completely full, you do not have to stand in a long line for a cocktail at the bar or take a sunbed by the pool early in the morning. There is enough space for everyone. However, some guests note that a number of residential buildings are located at a considerable distance from the beach. If you come on vacation with young children, this can be a slight inconvenience. Therefore, family tourists are advised to ask the administrator when placing them in buildings located closer to the sea.

I liked the guests of Desert Rose and the location of the hotel. So, according to our compatriots, he is in the city, and literally a stone's throw from it is a shopping street. Bars

Sea

Since the vast majority of tourists from various countries arrive in Egypt, counting on an excellent beach holiday, this issue is very important when choosing a hotel to stay. As for The Desert Rose Resort 5 * (Hurghada), this hotel will not disappoint you. The hotel has its own large sandy beach. It has a sufficient number of sun loungers and parasols for everyone. The beach is regularly cleaned, so being here is very nice. Sunset in the sea, according to our compatriots, is sandy and gently sloping. This fact was especially liked by guests who came to rest with children. Many tourists did not miss the opportunity to admire the underwater inhabitants of the Red Sea, armed with a mask for snorkeling. Of course, as some travelers note in their comments, the underwater world here is not so rich, bright and diverse, as, for example, in Sharm el-Sheikh, but there is something to see. There is a diving center on the beach. So all comers have the opportunity to dive into the sea with scuba diving.



Entertainment Comments

A beach holiday is not the only entertainment that the Desert Rose Resort 5 * hotel offers its guests. So, on the territory of the hotel complex there are several large pools. There are sunbeds with sun loungers and parasols nearby, and there are bars with a wide selection of drinks for every taste.

In addition, on the territory of Desert Rose there is a small water park with water slides. According to our compatriots, both children and adults have a great time here.

The hotel guests are entertained all day by a team of animators. They offer everyone who wants to do yoga, water aerobics, step, play volleyball or darts, take part in funny contests, and also learn belly dancing. In the evenings, shows and discos are held. The youngest tourists were not deprived of attention here either. For them, a mini club works during the day, and in the evenings there is a mini disco. According to our compatriots, vacationing at the hotel with their whole family, their children were happy to attend these events, and upon returning home they remembered these pleasant moments for a long time.



Guest opinions about staff

As for the employees of the five-star Desert Rose Hotel (Hurghada, Egypt), the vast majority of our compatriots were satisfied with them. Judging by their reviews, the staff here are very courteous, attentive, efficient and well trained. In addition, many employees speak Russian. So our compatriots felt at home here, without experiencing any difficulties in communication.
