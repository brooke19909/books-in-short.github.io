Headphones Yamaha: a description of the best models

It is difficult to imagine modern life without headphones. They help pass the time on a long trip, allowing you to enjoy listening to your favorite music. In them, in order not to disturb others, we watch films and play. Manufacturers flood the market with various models that differ in shape, capabilities and functionality. Today, Yamaha headphones boast superior quality.

The company is based in Japan and is famous worldwide for its musical equipment, as well as motorcycles. Yamaha headphones can satisfy any music lover, but for high quality you have to pay a decent amount of money. What will the company surprise users this year?

Yamaha HPH-200





Headphones Yamaha HPH-200 are made in full size, that is, they are the usual "burdock". Sound is provided by electrodynamic emitters, which are used in many models. However, Yamaha headphones, the reviews of which we will provide in our article, according to users, cannot boast of good noise insulation.

But they are able to reproduce frequencies in the range of 20-20 000 Hz. We got a big power - in 1000 mW. Connect via wire. The weight of the headphones is 180 grams, the membrane has a diameter of 40 millimeters. They are fixed on the head in a standard way, that is, by means of a rim.

For convenient transportation, the developer made the design folding, which avoids damage. Headphones are connected using the usual 3.5 mm Jack. For better conductivity, the contacts are gold-plated.

Yamaha HPH-M82





Headphones Yamaha HPH-M82 are made with an emphasis on style. At the first glance, an irresistible desire arises to buy this unusual model.

The bezel is made with an interesting bend, the ear pads received a square shape, instead of the usual round. They are connected using a wire, the length of which is 120 centimeters; the 3.5 mm jack (gold-plated) is used.

Headphones Yamaha HPH-M82 received dynamic emitters. Reproduce sound in the range of 20-20 000 Hz. The maximum power of the model is 300 mW. They are attached to the head with the usual rim, sit comfortably and do not put pressure on the ears. There is a built-in microphone. Distinctive features include neodymium magnets.

Yamaha EPH-M100





Headphones Yamaha EPH-M100 presented in the form of earbuds or, as they say in common people, "plugs". However, the build quality and sound, judging by the reviews, they are seriously different from cheap models.

They are connected via a wire 120 cm long. Work due to electrodynamic emitters. The headphones are distinguished by excellent sound insulation, which was made possible thanks to the correct form and high-quality details. They have their own volume control. Able to play tunes in the range of 20 - 20,000 Hz, the power is 30 mW.

The developer did not provide any devices for attaching the "plugs" on the ear. Jack 3.5 mm jack is gold-plated, special magnets are used in the design. The kit comes with 5 additional ear pads and a case for carrying headphones.

Yamaha HPH-MT120





Full-size headphones with excellent sound quality and good noise isolation. The model will appeal to music lovers and fans of computer games who spend a lot of time in headphones.

This model can distinguish high-quality materials and solid assembly. Headphones sit well on the head and do not crush. They are connected to devices using a wire that has a 3.5mm Jack jack. High sound quality is provided by electrodynamic emitters. Headphones play music in the range of 20 - 22000 Hz, have a high power of 1600 mW.

The disadvantage is the weight, which is 320 grams.

Yamaha HPH-MT220





Expensive and high-quality headphones, from which, judging by the reviews, every fan of high-quality sound will be crazy. The first thing that catches your eye is a stylish design. The manufacturer used high-quality, durable plastic for manufacturing, which does not collect fingerprints and is not scratched. In the hands they feel reliable and expensive.

The basis here was the electrodynamic emitter, which provides a deep and clear sound. In addition, they boast good noise reduction. Sound is reproduced in the range of 15-28,000 Hz. These headphones are one of the most powerful in the line, giving out 1,600 mW. However, the power had to pay a mass of more than 400 grams.

Connect using a twisted wire with a 3.5 mm jack. The cable length is 120 centimeters. In addition to high-quality sound, these products boast a convenient design that is perfect for any head.
