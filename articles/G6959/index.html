Hotel Eri Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece / Crete): description and reviews of tourists

The resorts of Crete, the southernmost Greek territory, are always very popular. Thousands of tourists from Europe and Asia come here on vacation annually. Why is this legendary island so attractive?

Lovers of antiquity can find many cultural and historical objects, including monuments of the oldest in the world of the Minoan civilization that originated on this island. But even without attractions, Crete is famous for its unique healing climate and a large number of clean sea beaches.

For a relatively inexpensive family vacation, travel agents often offer to use the services of the hotel complex Eri Beach Village Hotel 4 * (Crete). Reviews of tourists who visited this hotel complex are very different: some are enthusiastic, extremely positive, with thanks to the tour operator and staff, others are sharply negative. However, they both agree that the four-star Erie Beach Hotel is located in a place that is the most successful combination of picturesque Mediterranean landscapes and modern infrastructure. The hotel is an ideal refuge for anyone who plans not only to relax by the sea, but also to get acquainted with the historical sites and attractions of the legendary island of Crete. What is most often told by our compatriots who chose the Eri Beach Hotel 4 * (Crete) as their vacation spot?





Hotel complex, infrastructure

Eri Beach Village Hotel 4 * (Crete) - a hotel complex for budget holidays, includes two cozy four-star hotels, Eri Beach and Eri Hotel, consisting of nine buildings. The hotel buildings were built in the early eighties, but the hotel complex is in good condition, regularly renovated.

The hotel complex is located within walking distance from the center of the village of Hersonissos - a small resort village in the northern part of the island state.

Eri Beach Village Hotel 4 * is located on the first coastline of the sea coast. The fenced area includes a small pebble beach of the hotel complex. Entrance to the beach for hotel guests is free; there is a rental of sun loungers and beach umbrellas.

The infrastructure of the hotel complex includes everything necessary for leisure and recreation for hotel guests. The hotel has two swimming pools with sea water, a children's pool with water slides, a freshwater pool - all pools are equipped with sun loungers and parasols. Sports facilities and a gym provide outdoor activities.

On the territory there is a restaurant, several bars, a sauna, souvenir and grocery outlets, there is a parking lot for private vehicles.

Opinion of hotel guests about the rooms in the "Erie Beach"

From the description of the hotel complex rooms on the official websites of travel agencies, we can conclude that the situation in the rather small rooms of the hotel is done without frills, in the spirit of minimalism. Sanitary condition

It is especially worth noting that, despite the relatively good reviews about the cleanliness of the Eri Beach Hotel 4 *, some tourists had to face extremely unpleasant phenomena. Entertainment, animation

In discussions devoted to leisure at Eri Beach Hotel 4 *, reviews about the entertainment and work of hotel animators are not always positive and enthusiastic. In the brochures of tour operators and on tourist sites, such events are announced for hotel guests as dance lessons, board games, afternoon and evening entertaining interactive show programs. But a lot of guests vacationing in the hotel complex, note that the hotel's animators work quite sluggishly, inactive. Most often, all activities at the Erie Beach Hotel are held in German.

The main entertainment venue is the water park and Star Beach, located next to the hotel. This is one of three entertainment complexes offering water activities for every taste: from aquatic hills to extreme types of entertainment. For adult visitors, there are five swimming pools, slides of various heights and attractions, including the ‚ÄúLazy River‚Äù, beloved by tourists, where you can enjoy a cocktail or soft drink without leaving the water, just by swimming past the bar. For the most active visitors, there is a water sports center at Star Beach. Here you can ride a jet ski or a ‚Äúbanana‚Äù, try your hand at surfing, water skiing, experience an unforgettable experience, soaring above the beach with a parachute or making a dizzying jump down on an elastic cable. Also on the territory of Star Beach you can visit the spa, there are several bars, an Internet cafe, a mini-market, a restaurant serving local cuisine and much more. For young visitors there is a playground, a safe children's pool with water slides and fountains. Unlike adult entertainment, all children's attractions in the water park are free.

Star Beach hosts visitors throughout the swimming season, from April to October. In the hottest months for tourists daily discos and parties are organized in the water park. The beach "Star Beach" is considered the most comfortable and marked with a "blue flag".

But we have to admit that it is with the close proximity of the water park that one of the most common complaints from guests of the Eri Beach Village Hotel 4 * is associated. Reviews of loud music playing late at night in a disco in Star Beach, because of which vacationers cannot sleep peacefully, are on almost every tourist forum.

You can have fun not only in the Erie Beach Hotel or water park: a large number of restaurants, places for shopping and nightclubs work in the resort village of Hersonissos. For the most part, entertainment venues are open until the morning, local clubs are quite popular not only with the residents of the village, but also with tourists. Vacationers, talking about the nightlife in Hersonissos, note that it is quite possible to find an institution with reasonable prices for food and drinks, the atmosphere of many night clubs is quite comfortable and modern. The only complaint, perhaps, relates to periods when there is a large influx of holidaymakers in the village. The tourist forums often complain about the fullness of the clubs at this time. The situation is aggravated by the fact that in most Cretan clubs there is no ban on smoking.

Excursions, sights

In the small village of Hersonissos there are practically no historical sites or memorial sites. This settlement cannot be called an interesting place for excursions; there are reviews that the village is too modern, it does not have "Greek flavor" and "spirit of antiquity" - all that for which tourists come to Crete. Therefore, all excursions include trips to Heraklion and other cities of the Greek island. What excursions can tourists who choose to relax Eri Beach Hotel 4 * (Crete) visit? Heraklion is the largest city and the "capital" of this historic area. It is not for nothing called the "gate" of the island of Crete: all roads converge here, there is an airport and the main seaport. The hotel complex is located approximately 30 km from the main island city, trips are organized mostly day trips.

What might interest tourists in the island capital? The cultural program of almost any excursion in this city includes a visit to the Historical and Archaeological Museums. The Heraklion Historical Museum has an invaluable collection of originals of ancient icons by El Greco, Mikhail Damaskinos and other legendary painters. The exposition of the Archaeological Museum includes priceless archaeological artifacts and ancient documents - evidence of the Minoan culture, discovered at different times during excavations on the island of Crete.

Many interesting objects are located in the vicinity of the island capital. Most of them are monuments of the richest ancient culture discovered during archaeological research: the Agia-Triad palace complex, the Malian and Phaistos palaces, the remains of the ancient Greek cities Amninossos and Gortin, and much more. Lovers of ancient Greek myths and legends will surely enjoy an excursion to the legendary Knossos Palace, where the labyrinth of the Minotaur is located .

A visit to Heraklion will be interesting for those who are not interested in Greek history. The capital of Crete is the main shopping center on the island, known far beyond the borders of the country for its incredible number of retail outlets and lower prices for all goods compared to other European cities. In addition to the island capital, excursion trips to Rethymnon and Chania, small towns with a large number of ancient buildings, are very popular among the guests of Crete.

For guests of the "Eri Beach Hotel" all excursions are organized in the representations of travel agencies located directly on the territory of the hotel complex. And for those who want to explore the island on their own and enjoy the Cretan landscapes, ‚Äúexperienced‚Äù tourists are advised to purchase a guide and rent a car. According to vacationers, such a trip can be much more fun than an organized tour. In addition, any movement on the roads of Crete is quite safe: the island is known for relatively good roads, and the inhabitants of Crete - for their friendly attitude towards tourists and cordiality.
