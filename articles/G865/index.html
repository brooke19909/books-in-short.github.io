Simply put, a couple of bright details are enough to make it clear who the child is dressed, and the authenticity of the image is not so important. So, we sew a fox costume!

Naturally, you need an orange cloth. Already only in color it will be possible to guess who dressed up the child.

If the children's wardrobe has clothes of a fiery color - fine! Less work for you, and the baby will be more familiar in their outfits.

The fox costume, in fact, is defined in addition to color by several details. This fluffy tail, triangular ears and a fox mask or makeup on the face. It is best to use faux fur. Cut two ovals from it, sew on one end, sew, twist, stuff with any filler: cotton, foam rubber, synthetic winterizer ... There is no fur - you can sew the tail and ears of orange fabric, and paint the tip with white. Although the effect is no longer the same, the fox is a fox to flaunt its fluffy tail!

If the boy was wearing a fox costume, the actions are the same, only instead of a skirt you have to sew shorts. To do this, cut the square blanks, like for a skirt, approximately to the middle from the bottom to the center. After that, this incision is stitched, the hem is hemmed, and an elastic band in the belt.

Now - the top, he's the fox's face. You can again follow the path of least resistance, buy a fox mask in the store. But not every child will agree to wear it yet - not the most comfortable thing. So again we take out the fabric and pick up the needle.
