Hotel Yuhuayuan Hotel 4 * (China / Hainan Island / Sanya): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

One of the popular resorts of East Asian countries is the island of Sanya, which is part of China. Pure ecology, favorable climate and developed infrastructure allow travelers to come here on vacation year-round. The Yuhuayuan Hotel, rightfully deserving of four stars, is one of the most popular establishments for living in these parts.

general characteristics

Yuhuayuan Hotel 4 * (Sanya) reviews receive mostly positive. How did the hotel deserve such close attention of tourists? This is what we will try to find out. This is a relatively new hotel complex, which began to function in 2013. However, two years later he underwent renovation. The hotel area is compact, it is only 0.2 hectares. The main building is a five-story building, on which an amazing backlight turns on in the evening. There is an outdoor terrace around the perimeter of each floor. On the other expanses there are three-story villas for living.

After arriving at the airport, travelers need to cover a distance of 22 km. This proximity to the location has a positive effect on the weary travelers. The spacious park area with a swimming pool is filled with leafy palm trees, exotic plants and fragrant flowers.

It is not allowed to stay with pets and smoke in the apartment. Hotel Yuhuayuan Hotel 4 * (Hainan) accommodates guests with disabilities. Upon check-in, a deposit is payable for the entire stay.





Accommodation

In total there are 80 comfortable rooms to accommodate vacationers of all ages. They are divided into three categories: Premier Garden, Superior Sea, Deluxe Sea. All are one-room, differ only in size and view from the windows. The largest area leaves 42 squares, they are located in the main building.

The interior is dominated by decoration of a national orientation. Modern design creates a cozy home atmosphere. Light colors in combination with brown prevail in the color scheme. The walls are decorated with paintings. The floors are carpeted. It has access to an open balcony or private terrace. Illumination is created by numerous windows and unique floor lamps.

The maids are in charge of daily routine. There is the possibility of delivering food to the chambers. An extra bed is available upon request.





Hotel Conditions

The comfort of spending time in the walls of the rooms of Yuhuayuan Hotel is due to the availability of all necessary items. Double beds, bedside tables, a table, chairs and a wardrobe make up the furniture set. There is a coffee and tea set and an electric kettle that allows you to start the morning with strong coffee or aromatic tea: a good mood is provided for the whole day. An individual air conditioning system is able to provide the necessary temperature in the apartment. The TV allows you to keep abreast of all the latest developments. There are no Russian channels. A telephone providing communication with reception staff is involved in resolving emerging issues. There is a safe and a minibar, which is replenished upon arrival with drinking water.

The bathroom is equipped with a shower, sink and hairdryer, as well as the possibility of taking a bath. There is soap, shampoo and gel. Dressing gowns and disposable snow-white slippers contribute to the creation of comfort.

Wi-Fi is available throughout.





Nutrition

A feature of the Yuhuayuan Hotel 4 * hotel complex (China) is the provision of only breakfast, which is included in the price of the ticket. With an additional payment, vacationers are given the opportunity to visit the Chinese Restaurant, which operates on the A la Carte system, for lunch and dinner.

The lobby bar offers guests a wide range of pastries, muffins, snacks, coffee, soft drinks, which requires payment upon delivery.

The interior of the main restaurant is equipped with panoramic windows, a chic relief ceiling and unique chandeliers. The atmosphere, as noted by the guests, is warm and friendly, conducive to eating and easy communication with neighbors on the table. The floors are covered with patterned tiles, the furniture is made of dark wood.

High chairs are provided for the children.





Beach

The public beach is located 200 meters in a straight line. However, for the convenience of tourists in both directions, a bus runs every hour. The infrastructure is developed, represented by the possibility of renting umbrellas with sun loungers. There is a rescue service ready at any time to come to the aid of swimmers. You can diversify your time on the beach with water rides.

Entertainment for adults

The range of entertainment offered, as experienced, is relatively small. However, everyone can find something to their liking. The opportunity to rent a car. Going on a tour of local attractions in a comfortable car is pleasant and affordable. However, for those who want to hear a fascinating story of an interestingly narrating guide, an excursion reservation is available near the reception. Bicycle rental not only allows you to explore the surroundings, but also improve your body. There is an equipped gym for lovers of physical activity. Additional services

Yuhuayuan Hotel 4 * often receives positive reviews for additional services provided. Their list includes a transfer. Experienced drivers will quickly and safely deliver their customers to their designated destination. This service is quite in demand among the guests. For celebrations, there is a special banquet room where you can throw a grand celebration for each culprit of such events. It has a stylish design in a classic style: a unique combination of rigor and coziness will delight all guests. A business meeting or presentation is available in conference rooms that are equipped with all the necessary equipment: a projector, a screen, microphones and comfortable furniture.
