Hotels in Satka: addresses, description

Hotels in Satka are located in different areas of the city. Thus, guests of the city are given the opportunity to choose a suitable option so that it is convenient to get to their destination. The level of comfort and pricing in all Satka hotels is different. This allows tourists to choose their place of residence depending on their financial capabilities.

"Velvet"

The hostel is located in a beautiful area of ​​the city on Lenin Square, 2. It has comfortable rooms that can accommodate up to 6 people. The rooms have their own bathrooms and toilets.

At this Satka hotel, guests can choose one of their favorite rooms:

  • single;
  • double;
  • for 2 people with a large bed;
  • triple;
  • shared (up to 6 people) with bunk beds.

The rooms have modern furniture. The rooms are decorated in soothing colors. There are chairs, bedside tables, clothes hangers. Reception is open 24 hours a day.

Guests can use the free internet throughout. There is a ski run near the hostel. Also, tourists are offered a good selection of shops, cafes and bars in the area. The average cost of living per day per person is 550 rubles.

"Victoria"

Hotel in the city of Satka is located near a large park. The hotel is located on the street. Kalinina, 6. Rooms are equipped with different categories - from standard to luxury.

Each room has:

  • TV
  • refrigerator;
  • Electric kettle;
  • hair dryer;
  • disposable personal care kit.

Guests can use the Internet for free, the hotel has a laundry service and the reception is open 24 hours a day.

An infrared cabin and a café-bar are on site. Here you can organize a banquet or book a vacation for a small company of people.

The average cost of living per day is from 3000 rubles per room.

"Old city"

The hotel is located on the street. Bocharova, 10. The hotel "Old Town" in Satka equipped with all rooms in a classic style. It has modern wooden furniture. In the design of the rooms used expensive knitwear.

Each number is made in its own color scheme. The rooms have bedside tables, wardrobes, TV. The rooms have their own bathrooms and bathrooms. The luxury rooms are additionally equipped with soft corners and air conditioning.

All guests are provided with disposable hygiene kits. Free internet is available throughout. Reception is open 24 hours a day.

The building with a hotel in Satka has a modern beauty salon. Here guests can tidy themselves up and book relaxing treatments. The hotel also has a large restaurant. It is equipped with several rooms. One of them has an oriental-style interior and guests are invited to use a hookah.

The hotel has a modern sauna. Modern steam rooms are equipped here, there is a lounge. Guests can order here any items from the restaurant menu.

"Guest house on the Market"

The hotel is located on a street of the same name at 6. This small hotel in Satka, Chelyabinsk Region, is quite popular due to the affordable pricing policy for accommodation.

It offers comfortable rooms of economy class. They have new furniture and all the necessary equipment. In all rooms, tourists can use the free Internet.

Each room has its own toilet and shower. Guests can eat in the nearby cafes and dining areas of the city. Hotel guests can use the laundry service at an additional cost and rent an ironing board from the reception.

The average cost of living per day is 1600 rubles.

"Apartments" on the street Star, 2

This small hotel is located in a detached cottage in a quiet area of ​​the city. It offers beautiful mountain views. The hotel has two rooms, which can accommodate from 2 to 4 people.

The cottage has a kitchen area with all necessary equipment. There are also laundry and ironing facilities. On the street there is a dining area in a covered gazebo.

The cottage has several bathrooms and bathrooms. In winter, the heating system works great. Here is fenced personal territory where vacationers can have a picnic using the barbecue.

This option is perfect for families. Here, children will be expanse in the fresh air, and adults can enjoy peace and quiet on a spacious balcony with a cup of aromatic coffee.
