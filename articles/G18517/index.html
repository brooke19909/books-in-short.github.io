CPU

A modest processor with 4 cores of the A7 architecture of the MT6580 model from the company-developer of ARM-chips "MediaTek" is the basis of the Blackview A8. His characteristics are really modest: the maximum possible clock frequency of 1.3 GHz, the technological process for the production of a silicon crystal - 28 nm. The CPU itself was officially introduced in 2013. But for any budget device, its capabilities are more than enough. With all the main and most popular Blackview A8 does an excellent job. Reviews of the device in question on most forums and portals on this topic only confirm this.





The basis of the graphics subsystem

The Blackview A8 smartphone also has a separate graphics processor for processing on-screen images. His model is the Mali-400MP. It includes one pixel block and 4 shader processing blocks. The clock frequency of this component of the mobile device can reach 533 MHz. The graphic accelerator itself is located on the same semiconductor chip along with the CPU and is manufactured according to the standards of the 28-nm process. The computational capabilities of this video card are more than enough to display an image on a 720p display, with which it copes very successfully.

The memory subsystem and its capabilities

The Blackview A8 is equipped with only 1 GB of DDR3 standard RAM. A couple of years ago, with such a parameter, he would already belong to mid-range devices. Of these, about 4.5 GB are occupied by system software. There is also a slot for installing an additional memory card in the Blackview A8. The characteristics of this gadget indicate that the maximum volume of such a drive in this case can reach 32 GB. The autonomy of the gadget. Battery capacity

The Blackview A8 smartphone, as noted earlier, comes with a removable 2050 mAh battery. Therefore, it will be most correct to use third-party launchers for the Blackview A8. The firmware in this case will definitely be deprived of the two main previously indicated disadvantages. And the performance of the interface, as practice shows, will be much better. A set of pre-installed software in this case is typical:
