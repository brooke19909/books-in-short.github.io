How to make a chicken out of an egg: interesting ideas, description and recommendations

Chicken surrounded by colored eggs can be a decoration for any Easter table. We offer several options for such crafts, which can be made with your child. This article will tell you how to make a chicken out of an egg using a crochet hook, a glue gun and other simple devices.





Method one: what you need

To create such a handmade chick you need to have at hand:

  • yellow and white acrylic threads;
  • knitting hook;
  • beads for eyes and beak;
  • sintepuh;
  • one capsule from the Kinder egg;
  • a little very thick twisted yarn of orange (mustard) color.

How to Crochet an Easter Egg from an Egg

The crafts are made in the following order:

  • yarn of yellow color knits a chain of 2 air loops;
  • in the second loop from the hook make 6 SC (SCN);
  • go to the front row;
  • knit two sc in each of the already existing loops;
  • go to the second row;
  • do one sc and two sc in the next loop;
  • repeat to the end of the row;
  • knit one RLS in each of the 2 following loops and two RLS in the third;
  • repeat the previous action to the end of the row;
  • knit one sc in each of the next three loops and two sc in the fourth;
  • repeat to the end;
  • continue to knit, without adding loops, 5 rows;
  • insert an empty closed capsule from the Kinder egg;
  • change the color of yarn to white;
  • knit 2 same rows as the previous one;
  • bind each 4th and 5th loop with one RLS;
  • repeat to the end of the row and knit 2 of the same;
  • bind each 3rd and 4th loop with one RLS;
  • repeat to the end of the row and knit 2 of the same;
  • bind each 2nd and 3rd loop with one RLS;
  • put a little sintepuha over the egg to make it more round.
  • repeat to the end of the row and knit another one of the same;
  • bind each 2nd and 3rd loop with one RLS;
  • the thread is fixed, torn and tighten the hole.




Dressing

Now that you know how to make a chicken from Kinder Eggs, or rather its basis, you can proceed to decorate the crafts. First of all, you need to tie the edges of the "shell". To do this, again, you need a crochet hook and the remains of white yarn.

The procedure is as follows:

  • white yarn is fixed on the edge of the "shell" with a strong knot;
  • make 4 air loops;
  • fasten on the edge of the "shell", slightly departing from the nodule;
  • repeat the same steps to the end of the row;
  • paws are made of thick orange yarn;
  • stick black beads or beads instead of peepholes;

Second way

Want to learn how to make a chicken out of an egg with your own hands along with a preschool? Then you will need:

  • plasticine of yellow, red, black and white;
  • Kinder Surprise Capsule;
  • knife for working with plasticine;
  • silicone cutting board.

Operating procedure

To make such a chicken is very simple. An empty capsule from the Kinder Surprise is used as the body.

It remains:

  • to make a voluminous beak from plasticine of red color;
  • fasten on the capsule;
  • fashion 3 round "cakes" with a diameter of 1 cm;
  • split them with a knife in half;
  • fasten on the capsule;
  • fashion 2 flat oval details from yellow plasticine;
  • cut one edge in the form of a fringe;
  • to fix the resulting wings on the capsule so that the "feathers" slightly puffed;
  • two white plasticine circles are fixed near the beak and black pupils are glued on top.

How to Make Egg Chicken for Easter Using Corrugated Paper

For such an original craft you will need:

  • one empty capsule from Kinder Surprise;
  • satin ribbon;
  • 2 strips of yellow corrugated paper (the first is 5 by 20 cm and the second is 3 by 10 cm);
  • thin wire;
  • black beads or beads for the eye and red for the beak;
  • thread floss;
  • scissors;
  • orange ornamental cardboard for making paws and beak;
  • thermal gun.

The procedure for making crafts is as follows:

  • twist a strip of corrugated paper in the middle;
  • have a capsule inside;
  • straighten corrugated paper to make an egg;
  • the tail of the corrugation is twisted;
  • fasten with a thin wire;
  • twist the corrugated paper on both sides to form chicken wings;
  • close the tail with a thin strip of red paper, which will act as a bow;
  • paste wings on the body of the chicken;
  • stick paws, beak, eyes and jewelry.
