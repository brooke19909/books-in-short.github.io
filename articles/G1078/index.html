Park "Edelweiss" in Barnaul: description, mode of operation, reviews

Do you miss new sensations? Want to have fun with your whole family? Go to the Edelweiss Park in Barnaul. Thrills, positive emotions, a charge of energy for a long time - all this will be guaranteed to you!

Description

Edelweiss Park in Barnaul is a modern outdoor entertainment complex. There are no age restrictions. Everything will be fun: toddler, teenager, young and mature person. This is the largest park in the region by the presence of a variety of attractions. Each of them is able to give a storm of positive emotions.





Riding rides can be very tiring, besides such leisure burns a lot of calories. You can gain new strength in a cozy cafe, which is located in this territory. Experienced chefs prepare each gourmet dish only from the most delicious and healthy products.

The main feature of the Edelweiss amusement park in Barnaul is the availability of a 3D cinema.

Why exactly him?

If you still doubt whether it is worth spending your day off with the children in this place, here are some reasons. There are several of them:





  1. Every guest here is always welcome. The entertainment complex always has a cheerful and relaxed atmosphere. Children's laughter gives a charge of positive emotions, and good-natured staff will cheer up every visitor who comes here.
  2. A large stage has been created in the park. On holidays, large-scale concerts and events are held there. In the Christmas holidays, this place turns into a mysterious fairy tale.
  3. In the summertime, contests for the whole family are often organized here. Each participant can receive a valuable prize or a nice souvenir.
  4. It is a mistake to think that all events are organized in the summer. In winter, a large ice rink is created in the very center of the park.
  5. If you don‚Äôt want to learn the most fun and extreme attractions of the city, you can rent an electric car, bicycle, scooter or roller.

In addition, this place is famous for its beautiful nature. You can make a photo of the whole family in Barnaul Edelweiss Park. This picture will always remind you of a pleasant stay.

About rides

In the Edelweiss park, everyone can get a lot of positive emotions. The following types of attractions can give such sensations:





  • "Railway". Every boy must ride the railroad. He can visit not only the role of a passenger, but also a guide, he will have the opportunity to sit in the cabin of this vehicle.
  • The Fast and the Furious is the most extreme attraction that even adults are afraid of. A person rises high above the ground, and there is a feeling as if he is hanging in the air.
  • All children dream of having their own car. This place has a wonderful circuit. A young driver can choose the most suitable car model for himself and ride on it. If the parents are not confident in the strength of their baby, then they can help him, drive a vehicle using a special device.
  • The smallest children can ride on mechanical horses. They will rise up and down. The young rider has the feeling that he really saddled a horse.

The UFO, Catapult, Funky Hills, Waltz and the cartodrome are very popular among visitors.

Only in this place are the most unusual trampolines installed in the form of houses, fabulous animals, and even with an impromptu slide. In the very center of the entertainment complex there is a children's playground where young visitors can play absolutely free.

How to get there

Having told about all the advantages of this wonderful place, it‚Äôs worth telling how to get to the Edelweiss Park in Barnaul.





  1. First of all, of course, you need to come to this city (if you are not its resident).
  2. From the station you can get to this place by trolleybus number 6 or by minibus at number 58 or 54.
  3. By public transport you need to get to the stop "House of Pioneers". A children's entertainment complex can be reached on foot in 15 minutes.
  4. Not only from the station, but also from any other place, you can get to the Leninsky district of the city by public or personal vehicle.

It remains to indicate where the Edelweiss Park is located in Barnaul. This wonderful place is located at: Yurina street, house 275 b. You can visit it at any time of the year, on any day of the week from 10.00 to 21.00.

Positive feedback from visitors

Almost every visitor is satisfied with a trip to the Edelweiss Park in Barnaul. Reviews of it are found in the following content:





  • People praise a large selection of attractions for every taste, both for those who just want to unwind, and for fans of extreme sports.
  • All children are delighted with this place. In the summertime, a variety of events are held here.
  • On site you can relax and have a meal in the cozy cafeterias. There are also several stalls selling popcorn, cotton candy and ice cream.

In a word, this is a great place where you can fully relax with the whole family.

Negative reviews

What might a person not like in the Edelweiss park in Barnaul? Of course, prices that are sometimes biting. To many people here it seems too noisy: music, the noise of mechanisms and children's laughter can sometimes be tiring. Another drawback is associated with long lines, especially during the holidays and summertime.

Despite the small flaws, this place is madly loved by all the locals from small to large and tourists.
