restaurants and bars

If you relax in Bali, time flies by. Nevertheless, a feeling of hunger makes itself felt. Tasty refreshments and a refreshing drink can be in such institutions:

Negative reviews

During a holiday in Bali, time flies by unnoticed. Nevertheless, tourists notice a number of disadvantages inherent in the hotel in question. The most significant can be considered such negative points:

  • dishonest housekeeping;
  • maids rarely change bedding;
  • if you order food in your room, then you have to take out the dishes yourself;
  • gray washed towels;
  • high prices for drinks in restaurants and in the beach bar;
  • when checking out, you need to carefully check the check, as services that you did not use may be included there (though, if guests notice this, the reception staff immediately remove them from the general list);
  • between the two parts of the hotel runs a busy highway;
  • poor sound insulation in rooms (strong audibility from the street, from the corridor and from neighboring rooms);
  • hotel staff do not take into account the wishes of guests about large or twin beds indicated at the time of booking;
  • uncomfortable beds (the mattress is constantly moving out, as it lies on a slippery wooden board);
  • the plumbing in the bathroom is quite old, and therefore leaking;
  • Before checking in the guests, the employees do not check the serviceability of the equipment in the room, and therefore they have to constantly inform the reception about problems;
  • despite the fact that breakfast should start at 6:30, in fact the dishes begin to be displayed no earlier than 7:00, which is especially inconvenient if you leave early or go on an excursion;
  • inflated prices for SPA services;
  • waves of algae that no one removes;
  • when checking in, you need to make a deposit of $ 200 (if you do this by credit card, you have to wait a very long time to return);
  • on laziness of departure, the key card is blocked early in the morning, although the checkout time is scheduled for noon.
