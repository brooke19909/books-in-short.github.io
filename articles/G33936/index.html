Children's portfolio for kindergarten

A child‚Äôs portfolio is a kind of piggy bank, an archive of all the successes and achievements of the child, his development and results in any areas of activity. To make it or not, it is, of course, up to the parents to decide. But still, everyone is advised to have such a book, thanks to which you can follow the development of your child.

What is a portfolio for a child for?

Due to the fact that the portfolio will be carefully maintained and regularly replenished, it will be able to bring parents closer to the child, as they will constantly maintain interest in terms of the development of their baby.

Portfolio is able to show how much the child has developed in:

  • Socio-personal relationships.
  • Cognitive-speech plan.
  • Physical development.
  • Artistic and aesthetic plan.

Thanks to the portfolio, you can remember over time how the baby was, what first drawing he depicted on the album sheet, his first crafts, words and much more.

Portfolio Features

But still, many parents are wondering what a portfolio is for. The answer is simple, it has a function:

  • Purposeful. It supports the goals of learning something new.
  • Diagnostic Fixes all changes in external factors.
  • Motivational. Pushes the child to move on to his goal.
  • Meaningful. Shows and saves all the work and efforts of the child.
  • Developing.
  • Rating. Helps to see the number of skills, abilities and progress.

How to draw up a portfolio?

Since the portfolio is intended for the child, it must necessarily be bright and unusual. If properly designed, it can become the pride and the most beloved book of the child.

Before creating a portfolio, it is necessary to explain to the child what it is made for. After that, you can begin to design together.

It must be remembered that the brighter the ‚Äúbook‚Äù, the more interesting the baby will be to approach its replenishment.

You also don‚Äôt need to be afraid that the child will paint or try to decorate the portfolio with something of his own. On the contrary, you need to ask the baby to help, since absolutely all the details, all the awkward drawings of the child with a pencil, all this is a memory. How pleasant it will be in a few years to look at and remember how he sat and tried to decorate a small piece of his life.

It is strongly not recommended to restrict the child from creating his own portfolio, since his help will not only remain a memory, but also give the baby the opportunity to express himself and feel that he is actually trusted.

Proper title page design

The front page for a portfolio is the "clothing" of all the contents inside. He can also be called the ‚Äúface‚Äù of all subsequent work. Also, the first impression of all content will depend on the title page, because for all people the first impression is the foundation of a common, concluding opinion.

The main rule of the title page for children's portfolios is a minimum of information. There is no need to overload the cover with a huge amount of incomprehensible information, as it would seem that we are now facing a reference book or book. Instead of excessive informativeness, it would be better to do the design more carefully, since the children's portfolio should look appropriate to its small owner.

Yes, absolutely nothing should be written on the title page, but this does not mean that the portfolio should be ‚Äúnameless‚Äù. You can post all information about the owner inside. After all, it is much more interesting to read the details on bright, colorful pages decorated with photographs than on the title page, which is completely filled with letters.

Now the title page is drawn up mainly thanks to the computer, as new technologies can provide a huge number and variety of designs. You can choose ready-made children's portfolios or choose the font, drawings, and many other additions yourself. If the child wants to contribute a piece of his work to the design, then he should not forbid it. The kid will be pleased that he participated in creating the portfolio.

What sections do you need to add?

The portfolio must be carefully divided, since it must contain absolutely all information about the child. It is necessary to rationally distribute all sections so that it is more convenient to perceive the content.

The first section. "My life"

In this section you can enter any information that is important for the child.

  • Name (you can write the meaning of the name, why parents chose this name, and so on).
  • My family. It is necessary to enter all family members, tell them what their name is. Alternatively, you can attach a generic tree to the page. Thus, everything will look much more aesthetically pleasing.
  • My friends. It is compiled similarly to the previous one, but in addition it is necessary to write a brief information about friends, their hobbies and so on.
  • Where I live. It is necessary to write concise information about the hometown, its attractions. You can also attach photos.
  • My kindergarten. Tell about your kindergarten, add information about the group.

The second section. "My hobbies"

In this section you can write about the hobbies and hobbies of the child. For example, if a kid is fond of drawing, you can write that most of all he likes to draw and attach a couple of his works. You can also talk about what he does in the kindergarten, mug. If a child often spends time with his grandmother, you can talk about what, for example, crafts they most often do. Toy preferences also need to be entered.

If the baby likes to read, or listen to stories, you can write about his favorite book.

In general, this section should be fully devoted to the child‚Äôs favorite activities and hobbies.

The third section. "Holidays"

This section can be replenished with photos from various holidays. Be it birthdays, New Year, March 8th.

The fourth section. "I am growing up"

This section can include absolutely all indicators of child development. For example: annually add the outline of the palm, legs. You can add a table in which there will be all the indicators of the physical development of the baby, thanks to which you can observe changes in growth, weight.

Also an interesting ‚Äúheading‚Äù in the portfolio will be a list of the first phrases that the kid said.

Creative development is also included in this section. You can add various applications, papier-m√¢ch√©, drawings, modeling and absolutely all other creative results.

If the child has any serious hobby, participates in competitions, contests, and the like, you can post various letters, diplomas and other evidence of his work on the page "My records".

The fifth section. "My impressions"

In this section, you can add many different photos from theaters, travels, any discoveries. Even photos from ordinary parks will be appropriate in this section, since for all children going to the park is an unforgettable storm of emotions, because there you can run, jump, ride on the carousels and really have fun.

The sixth section. "Reviews and suggestions"

This section can be done as desired. If you want to add it to the portfolio, then you can distribute reviews into the categories of "Educators" (if the portfolio is for kindergarten), "Parents" and "Teachers".

Thanks to the positive reviews, comments, and various wishes, the child will develop self-confidence, determination. He will see that all his efforts are not in vain and will continue to succeed in the same spirit.
