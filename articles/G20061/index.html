Hotel Golden city Rayong 3 (Thailand): reviews and photos

Thailand is a fabulous kingdom in which many modern tourists dream of making a trip. They seek to relax on the coast of the warm ocean, enjoy the eastern sun and get acquainted with the unique places and objects of this country. Wellness and business tours to this kingdom are popular. However, whatever the purpose of the trip to Thailand, it is important to find a comfortable place to stay, and one of the most suitable options in this regard is the Golden City Rayong 3 * hotel.

Location and infrastructure

Golden City Rayong is located in Rayong, close to a shopping center and several markets. Utapao Airport is approximately 35 km from here, and Suvarnabhumi International Airport is 155 km away. It is easy to get here from other cities as well, as the bus service is very good.

Golden City Rayong 3 * consists of two multi-storey buildings. The hotel has a separate cozy area, decorated with greenery and flowers. Tourists traveling in their own vehicle can leave it in the hotel parking lot. The hotel provides dry cleaning, laundry, maid service, luggage storage and ironing. The reception at the hotel is open around the clock, so you can contact the hotel staff at any time with questions regarding your placement in Golden City Rayong Hotel 3 *, and rest in Thailand in general.





Terms for accommodation

For accommodation, the Golden City Hotel Rayong 3 * offers its guests 281 rooms of the following types: Superior Rooms, Deluxe B Rooms, Family Suites and Studios. Superior Rooms are equipped with a comfortable bed, desk, safe, LCD-TV, refrigerator, microwave, hairdryer. There is everything you need for making coffee and tea. This room has a private bathroom with shower.

Deluxe B Rooms and Studios are equipped like Superior Rooms, but they also have a kitchen with sink. Family Suites consist of a living room, two bathrooms and two bedrooms. There is also a kitchenette with sink. A TV, a desk, a hairdryer, a microwave, a refrigerator, an electric kettle - all this is at the service of the residents of this type of room.

In Golden City Rayong 3 * children under the age of three are provided with free hotel accommodation using existing beds. As for the placement of children 3-12 years old, an extra bed can be installed for them, but it is already necessary to pay extra.

Pets are not allowed in Golden City.





Nutrition

All hotel guests enjoy a complimentary breakfast served daily in the buffet restaurant from 6 to 10 in the morning. Breakfast can be of three types: continental, American and Asian. If desired, each person living in Golden City Rayong 3 can also have lunch at a restaurant for an additional fee, where a buffet is also served from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. In addition, throughout the day it is possible to order from the menu any dish of local or international cuisine. The restaurant has live music and karaoke. If you do not want to leave the room, for an additional fee, any of your order can be delivered from the kitchen directly to the room.

The hotel also has a bar, which is open daily from 6 AM to 2 AM. In this period of time, anyone can come here and quench their thirst with your favorite drink.





Business Opportunities

The hotel has nine fully equipped conference rooms where you can organize an event or meet with business partners. Some rooms have modern audio and video equipment, which is an excellent condition for holding a conference. From 30 to 2500 people can take part in the meeting at the same time. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, including conference rooms.

At the request of the customers of the conference rooms, they can be served drinks and light snacks, or a dining table is served in the hotel restaurant or other room.





Entertainment and sports

Swimming in the outdoor pool, classes in the fitness center, riding a rented bicycle - all this is provided by Golden City Rayong 3. Thailand is famous for its traditional massage throughout the world, so it is simply unacceptable that this hotel does not have such a service. Not a single hotel guest is able to leave the hotel without feeling the healing effects of Thai massage. Do not be an exception and you!

Near the hotel (about 15 minutes drive) there is a luxurious beach. You can get to it either on the local bus or on a rented scooter. The huge advantage of the local beach is its cleanliness and low population.





Local Attractions

Fans of cognitive pastime can also stay at Golden City Rayong 3. Rayong is a city rich in attractions, some of which are located in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.

Of particular interest is the Pra Chedi Klang Nam Temple, located on an island in the middle of the Rayong River. This ancient Buddhist temple can only be reached by boat. An equally interesting temple is the Wat Pa Prada pagoda, on the territory of which there is a twelve- meter statue of Buddha. Speaking about the sacred structures of Rayong, accessible to foreigners, it is also worth mentioning the ancient temple of Wat Lum Mahachai Chumphon.

The resort and its surroundings have many natural attractions, including mountain ranges, picturesque waterfalls in the middle of green forests, agricultural plantations with rubber trees and exotic fruits, coral reefs, a botanical garden and Khao Wong Cave.





Reviews and photos of tourists

Over the period of its existence, the Golden City Rayong 3 hotel received both positive and negative reviews. This is due, firstly, with the purpose of the trip, and secondly, with the personal preferences of each traveler.

The hotel is most praised by people who arrive in Thailand for business negotiations. It is noted for its silence, the convenience of equipped conference rooms, the cleanliness of rooms, a cozy area and friendly staff. However, on the issue of nutrition, opinions differ: some hotel guests were completely satisfied with them, while others chose to eat in nearby restaurants.

As for the tourists who arrived in Rayong on vacation, some of them were saddened by the small number of opportunities for fun and the lack of any entertainment program. Although families with children consider this a positive feature. No one bothers to sleep at night and enjoy a relaxing holiday during the day. And if you want to have fun, you can always go to the beach, take a walk in the city or visit nearby attractions.
