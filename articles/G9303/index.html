Warsaw hotels: descriptions, reviews of tourists. Holidays in Poland

Poland is one of those states in Europe where you want to relax again and again. Especially tourists like the capital of the country - Warsaw. Magnificent architecture, impeccably developed infrastructure, an abundance of attractions - all this has a good mood and a great pastime. And Warsaw hotels are simply created in order to lure tourists into their apartments. These are fashionable establishments offering guests all the best and comfortable for living. In the Polish capital there are both expensive and elite hotels, as well as cheaper ones belonging to the economy class.

General information about Warsaw hotels

Warsaw hotels are characterized by comfort, especially a welcoming atmosphere and the level of service that will make your stay in this metropolis ideal. Each of the hotels is trying in its own way to attract guests to their apartments. In addition to high-quality service, accommodation in the Polish capital offers guests a convenient location next to the most prominent sights, popular restaurants and entertainment venues.

There are budget Warsaw hotels in the Old Town. They are distinguished by comfort and homely hospitality. Most four- and five-star hotels during April and May, as well as September and October, are very expensive. In another period, on holidays and weekends, they even have a 40% discount. All this must be taken into account when planning a vacation in Poland, and Warsaw in particular.

Hotel located in the heart of the village

In the very heart of Warsaw, the Novotel Warszawa Centrum , famous among travelers, is a four-star hotel. To the main station from this placement - only half a kilometer. The venue is considered ideal for business meetings and business trips, as it has a fully equipped conference complex and unlimited access to the World Wide Web.

For their guests, hotel staff serves every morning breakfast with a huge assortment of various dishes. The hotel rooms have air conditioning, safes, work areas, flat-screen TVs, and all the necessary items for making coffee or tea. The apartments are equipped with sofas or soft chairs and a large bathroom with shower or bathtub and hairdryer. If the client needs a minibar, then he will be provided with it at an additional cost.



Hotel in old Warsaw

If you want to explore the old part of the capital of Poland, it is best to stay at the Intercontinental Warszawa hotel, a five-star establishment offering its guests a full range of services. This modern location was built in 2003, and six years later it was completely renovated. The city center from Intercontinental can be reached in ten minutes. Nearby are attractions such as the Old Town Market Square and Lazienki Park.

The hotel has a business center, internet connection and photocopy service. The institution has 410 rooms, each of which offers air conditioning, Wi-Fi, a minibar and a mini fridge. Some rooms have unmatched views of the village. All rooms have underfloor heating and comfortable furniture. Room rate includes daily breakfast.



Hotel near the Presidential Palace

Near the Presidential Palace is the Neo-Renaissance Hotel Bristol. The five-star Bristol, A Luxury Collection offers tourists the highest level of comfort, qualified service, hospitality and European service. The hotel has 168 luxurious rooms and 38 suites. Here are presented apartments of the following categories: executive, suites, classic and junior suites. Each room is equipped with the latest technology and is equipped with a marble bathroom. The hotel also has apartments for people with disabilities and rooms with balconies.

Hotel "Bristol" offers guests to taste delicious dishes in the restaurant "Marconi". It serves delicacies from international and national cuisine. In the territory of Bristol, A Luxury Collection, there is also a Bristol cafe, designed in a Viennese manner, which sells a lot of confectionery and desserts.



A chic stay at the Sheraton Warsaw Hotel 5 *

In the very center of Warsaw is the magnificent Sheraton Hotel, from which you can walk to the National Museum in just five minutes. The hotel is an excellent choice for a luxurious vacation in Poland. The polite Sheraton staff will help guests book the best tour of Warsaw and tickets to a variety of cultural programs. In the morning, a breakfast buffet is served, and during the day and evening several restaurants are located at the hotel.

In the fitness center of this accommodation you can keep fit and take a steam bath in the Finnish sauna or in the Russian bath. Since the reception is open around the clock, guests can solve the problem at any time. Sheraton Warsaw Hotel's staff responsibilities include arranging airport transfers for guests. The hotel has 350 rooms, including luxury apartments. Any room has everything you need for making tea and coffee.



Respectable design hotel

If you need a respectable vacation, the Hyatt Regency Hotel, located in the central part of Warsaw, can offer it to you. The institution is focused on discerning guests who know what real comfort and high-class service are. Each of the 250 bright and spacious rooms is decorated in a beige-gray palette and offers guests all the necessary amenities. The rooms have panoramic windows with unrivaled views of Warsaw. Satellite TV is also provided to diversify the time spent in the apartment.

The bathrooms are tiled with natural marble. A pleasant surprise for each guest who has just settled in will be fresh water and a basket of fresh fruits. The hotel features the sophisticated Venti-Tre Restaurant, which serves Mediterranean and Italian dishes. And if you need to truly relax, you can use the services of the Oasis Club spa.



Hotel Marriott

Another luxury hotel in Warsaw is the Marriott Hotel, located next to the Palace of Culture and Science. The location of the hotel is perfect for ordinary travelers and business travelers, as the institution is located in the center of the business and commercial quarter. The institution found a place in one of the highest buildings of the capital. In total, the building has 42 floors. For the "Marriott" allocated the first two and last 20 tiers. This modern stunning tower majestically rises above the landscape of the metropolis.

This hotel impresses with its welcoming, large-sized lobby, which has a large number of seating areas, magnificent flower arrangements, shops and a bar. The hotel has 11 restaurants, ranging from Italian establishments to local ones.



Three star hotels and hotels

Above we looked at hotels in Warsaw, with four and five stars. But there are less expensive hotels in the city. They have only three stars, but their level of service is also at a fairly high level. So, the most famous such placements include:

  • Metropol Hotel - Located in Warsaw's Srodmiesce district If the traveler sets as his main objective the sightseeing of the Polish capital, and not the test of the hotel business, then the Metropol Hotel is just what he needs. You can eat here in the international restaurant, while snacks and drinks are recommended in the local bar.
  • Ibis Warszawa Stare Miasto offers 333 guestrooms to choose from. They are equipped with air conditioning and a host of other amenities. Thanks to a free Internet connection, guests can always stay in touch with their relatives, and cable TV will allow you to pass the time when a trip to the city for any reason is not possible.

Listen to people

Any hotels in Warsaw get good reviews. Whether it is a five-star or a three-star institution, guests are always satisfied with it. Indeed, in Poland they know how to take care of their guests, and therefore people who lived in some Warsaw hotels say that everything in them is done at the highest level. And the kind staff of each of the placements is ready to fulfill any order. Customers note the chic equipment of the rooms of Warsaw hotels and the purity prevailing in them.
