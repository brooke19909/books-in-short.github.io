How to connect a smart watch to the phone: step by step instructions

A smart watch is a multifunctional wrist electronics, which has recently gained wide popularity. Well-known world brands began to produce all kinds of device modifications. But in order to make full use of their functionality, you need to decide how to connect the smart watch to the phone and make a full setup. The procedure may have some differences depending on the model of smart watches, however, the sequence of actions is subject to independent execution.

Preparatory Activities

After acquiring smart watches, their owner must familiarize themselves with the attached instructions and perform the following actions:

  1. To study the device, its capabilities and control methods.
  2. Determine the location of the connectors and control buttons.
  3. With the support of the SIM card device, thoroughly familiarize yourself with the installation diagram.
  4. Check battery health and contact integrity.

Different models are characterized by a peculiar technique and charging time. Therefore, to charge the device is used:

  • wire charging (contact) with power supply;
  • wireless charger;
  • magnetic charging (docking station).

As a rule, it is not recommended that the battery be completely discharged in the first 3-5 charging cycles. It is advisable to put the gadget on charge at a minimum level and charge up to 100%. Install SIM card and setup communication

Some watch models are equipped with a function that allows you to work with the card of any telecom operator. These include, for example, smart watches for children or citizens of advanced age. So, before connecting a children's smart watch to the phone, it is important to adhere to a number of rules:

  • It is strongly recommended that you select a reliable telecom operator that has standard network support that does not require difficult steps to set up communications.
  • Determine the tariff. It is desirable that it includes an option to prepay Internet traffic of at least 1 GB.
  • To track the balance by phone number, you should create a personal account on the website of the telecom operator.
  • You will need to check whether the card is activated through the phone, and also does not have a PIN code.
  • Be sure to replenish the balance, because otherwise the Internet connection will not function.

If the SIM card is installed correctly, then after a while an indicator will appear on the device‚Äôs display showing the signal reception quality.

Set date and time

Many smartphones after the first start-up involve setting the time in a smart watch, which is set based on the time zone. Some devices do not automatically suggest the date and time. In such cases, you must open the "Settings" and perform the appropriate actions. More specific information can be found in the device manual.

Smartphone Sync

Before connecting a smart watch to a phone, it should be noted that each model is equipped with software that allows you to connect to a smartphone. Smart devices can only connect to the Android or iOS platform. However, a large number of smart watches are cross-platform.

A step-by-step instruction on how to connect a smart watch to an Android phone consists of the following steps:

  1. Installing applications on the phone for a suitable watch model.
  2. Make sure that Bluetooth is activated on the clock, and on some Wi-Fi models.
  3. Select the name of the watch from the list that opens.
  4. Receive a notification with a secret code on device screens.

Initiate pairing using the Connect button if the codes on both devices match. On the gadget screen, after completing all the actions, the inscription "Connected" should be displayed. In case of failure, the system will also respond with a message.

Installing the application on a smart watch

Using a paired smartphone is one of the easiest methods to install additional applications on a smart watch. Many models of modern devices boast an abundance of both paid and free third-party programs. To install, it is enough, using a smartphone, to find the necessary application for Android Wear in the Google Play store, download and install it. Upon completion of the process, the add-on can be used on a watch by opening the installed application.

Connecting a smart watch to an Android smartphone: step-by-step instructions

As a rule, most modern gadgets support the Android operating system. Therefore, users should not have a lot of questions about how to connect smart watches to Samsung, Lenovo, HTC and other models. The principle is one and the same.

To connect a smart watch to a phone with the Android operating system, you must:

  • Download the free Android Wear app from the Play Market.

Connecting smart watches to your iOS phone

Before you connect a smartwatch to your iPhone, open and activate Bluetooth. After the gadget is detected by the program, you must select the name displayed on the smartwatch display. After that, you will need to specify the code, which will also be displayed on the dial.

Sometimes the watch and the iOS device do not detect each other. In this case, you should reset all the settings to the factory settings and try again. Once connected, the application will ask you to sign in to your Gmail account.
