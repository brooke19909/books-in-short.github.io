Exotic Palawan (Philippines) - a place where you want to return

One of the most beautiful places on our planet is located in the west of the Philippine archipelago. Tourists compare the exotic and little-studied island with a paradise on earth, admitting that they have never seen such fantastically beautiful landscapes. Thousands of travelers flock here to get acquainted with local attractions that are known far beyond the borders of the state.

Heaven on earth

The beautiful Palawan (Philippines), consisting of different islands, not very inhabited, is an ideal place for tourists who dream of solitude. In the kingdom of untouched nature, dense tropical forests and limestone cliffs that protect the paradise from all sides are combined with a unique marine world.



As if a pearl of the archipelago of people from different countries beckons with a magnet. The sparsely populated island will surprise the native people who are not spoiled by the harmful influence of civilization and who live in their own world.

Underground river

The main attraction of the resort, lost in the sea, is not in vain considered fabulously beautiful nature. In 1967, Palawan (Philippines) was recognized as a national reserve. The most beautiful place on earth falls in love with everyone who has arrived to enjoy amazing landscapes.

What awaits tourists on the territory of a colorful island? Of course, the underground river located in the karst zone and flowing through a mysterious cave will delight everyone. Winding in grottoes with colonies of limestone formations of Puerto Princesa takes its place in the list of modern wonders of the world.

During a fascinating tour of the underground labyrinth hidden from the human eye, tourists admire a huge cave, the halls of which are decorated with stalagmites and stalactites of the most fantastic forms. By the light of the boatman‚Äôs lantern, the emerald expanse of water shimmers with a scattering of lights, and a bizarre play of light and shadow conjures up the imagination of visitors arriving in Palawan (Philippines) with unusual images.



Considered the longest underground artery in the world, Puerto Princesa makes you admire Mother Nature, who created a unique masterpiece. Twenty-four years ago, the local authorities turned the river and its adjacent territory into a national park, which was included in the UNESCO World Heritage List.

Tabon Caves

In the south-west of the magic corner there are Tabon caves, which became famous after archaeologists found in the halls the remains of a primitive man who lived here more than twenty thousand years ago. The underground kingdom, formed due to the influence of water on karst rocks, opens up an amazing world for all visitors, which makes you stiff in admiration. Out of almost two hundred caves, only seven are open for tourists, and scientists have studied about thirty halls.



Anyone without special equipment can get inside the natural attractions of which the island of Palawan (Philippines) is proud. Interesting guests of the popular resort caves belong to the national museum of the state. The beauty of the underworld, created several million years ago, will remain in the memory of every visitor.

El nido

One of the main tourist destinations is the El Nido Nature Reserve, recognized as the most beautiful in all of Asia. Consisting of many islands that are not similar to each other, the picturesque land area is famous for its breathtaking picture of natural grandeur. A striking example of the diverse flora and fauna of Palawan has received the status of a conservation zone.

High cliffs, ancient grottoes, lagoons with delightful beaches, tropical forests, a colorful underwater world will surprise even the discerning traveler who has seen many countries.



The best place for water sports will not let anyone get bored. Tourists are invited to stay in comfortable hotels located in fabulously beautiful bays. Wildlife lovers admit that they have not seen anything more beautiful than the wonderful islands of El Nido, which is famous throughout the world for Palawan (Philippines). A photo of a wonderful corner, charming at first sight, will make you forget with admiration the hearts of all travelers.

Coral reef

Another popular tourist route is the Tubbatah coral reef, consisting of two atolls that are separated by a strait. With over a thousand underwater inhabitants, the UNESCO World Heritage Site rushes from all corners of the globe to divers to explore the so-called coral walls - dangerous places where the reef goes very steeply into the depths.

An outstanding attraction made famous the island of Palawan (Philippines). The incredible popularity of this place is evidenced by the fact that all tourist vessels that have the necessary diving equipment are booked for the whole year in advance.

A magic corner delights with a huge number of rare marine life and a wonderful coral garden.



Tours to Palawan (Philippines)

The best time to relax at the resort is spring, since in summer there is a high probability of drought, and in winter tourists note increased humidity. The air temperature does not drop below 25 degrees, which can not but rejoice all the guests of the island.

I must admit that tours to the distant Palawan are not budget options, but those who seek to open exotic corners of the world are happy to choose this destination.

Tourists reviews

Travel agencies say that every year holidays on the colorful island of Palawan (Philippines) are gaining popularity . The reviews of guests who have visited a paradise are full of admiration.

Everyone notes that this is an ideal place for families with children and a romantic trip. Amazing nature and hospitality of local residents give a feeling of genuine happiness.

Ecotourism lovers with pleasure repeat the fascinating journey to an exotic corner, located far from civilization, explaining their choice by the fact that here you can feel like a free person and immerse yourself in the atmosphere of peace.
