Complex "Guest Rooms"

Motel Cross Mountain

An ideal hotel option for Kandalaksha ski lovers. The motel is located on the slopes of the Cross Mountain. Only 5 km to the railway station, and a transfer can be ordered from the airport in Kirovsk (120 km).

The cottage is completely wooden, and the rooms are decorated in a mountain chalet style. Each has its own shower / toilet, TV, comfortable beds. The balconies offer a panorama of snow-capped mountains.

The motel has:

  • sauna;
  • large terrace;
  • place for barbecue;
  • free internet and parking.

Vacationers can cook in the kitchen, order imported breakfast from the cafe, visit the bar.

Since most of the guests come for skiing, the motel has rental and storage facilities for ski equipment. Convenient movement around the territory in equipment and access to the hillside are provided. The administrator sells ski passes.

The hotel organizes bike rental, diving equipment, fishing.
