Polar Zorye Hotels: addresses, descriptions, reviews and travel tips

Polar Dawns is a working town, the main enterprise of which is the Kola NPP. But tourists are familiar with it primarily due to the ski complex "Salma". There are only six hotels in the Polar Zory of the Murmansk Region . You can get acquainted with the basic information about each of them later in the article.

Hotel "Niva shores"

This is the most popular hotel of the Polar Dawns. It is located in the very center of the city, on Lomonosov street, 1.

The hotel is a modern building, on the ground floor of which there is a restaurant of European and Russian cuisine, and on the second and third - 37 comfortable rooms.

Categories of apartments:

  • single with shared bathroom;
  • double economy class;
  • two-room junior suites;
  • two-room and three-room suites.

Room rates - from 900 rubles. It includes buffet breakfast and parking. Wi-fi is free.

Hotel "Imandra"

Located at 16 Lomonosov Street, not far from the Salma ski resort, in the very center of Polyarnye Zori.

The hotel has double, triple and quadruple rooms of economy class. Showers and a bathroom are provided on the block. Self-catering.

According to guest reviews, Imandra is quite popular among groups of tourists who came to the Polar Dawns to visit the ski resort.

Hotel "Dawns"

This hotel began to function only a few years ago, but has already established itself highly among residents of the city and among tourists. Most vacationers who come to the ski resort stay here mainly with working visits.

In total, the Zori Hotel has 22 comfortable rooms for 40 people. Categories of apartments:

  • economy;
  • single, double and triple standard;
  • Suite

All rooms are equipped with a bathroom with shower. The price includes breakfast.

Guest reviews positively note the presence of billiards and a cafe on the hotel territory, as well as the fact that Wi-Fi works not only near the reception, but in every room. Guests note that the rooms here are clean and comfortable, special areas are provided for smokers. The hotel has an ironing room, and if necessary, you can use the services of staff. Round-the-clock registration is possible.

An important advantage of the hotel is that the ski complex is nearby. Her address: Polyarnye Zori, 4 Lomonosov Street.

Mini-Hotel Zapolyarye

Another hotel of the Polar Zory is located on Lomonosov Street 16.

The hotel has small but comfortable rooms in the following categories:

  • single with amenities;
  • family with a kitchen area;
  • single, double, triple and quadruple economy class.

The bed and breakfast has satellite TV and a shared kitchen with a stove, fridge, sink and dining table.

The cost of hotel accommodation is from 600 rubles.

Guest reviews about the "Arctic" note its following features:

  • Clean and comfortable rooms.
  • Convenient location.
  • Nearby are cafes and shops, transport stops.
  • The ability to order food and drinks in the rooms.
  • Reasonable prices.
  • Wi-Fi is available throughout the mini-hotel.
  • Guestrooms provide complimentary toiletries.
  • Near the hotel there is free parking.
  • Smoking is not allowed on site or in rooms. For this purpose a place is specially reserved.

Hotel "City"

Famous hotel in the city of Polar Dawns, which offers guests of the city cozy rooms of economy class. It is designed for 26 places.

The rooms are equipped with single beds and other necessary furniture. The bathroom and showers are designed for a block. The kitchen is shared. It has two four-burner electric stoves, a microwave, two sinks, a set of necessary dishes and even a washing machine.

"City" is quite popular among tourists who came to relax in the ski resort. They note the following important factors that influence the choice of this hotel:

  • affordable price;
  • the presence of a kitchen;
  • transport stop in three minutes walk;
  • clean and bright rooms;
  • friendly staff.

The hotel is located in the very center of the city on Lomonosov street, 16 (3rd floor).

Guest house Victoria

It is not located in the Polar Dawns themselves, but ten kilometers from the city. But it was worth mentioning this guest house because of its location near the Salma ski resort. After all, most tourists come to the Polar Dawns specifically for skiing.

The cost of living is from 1900 rubles per day. Comfortable two-, three-, four- and six-bed rooms are available for vacationers. They are equipped with all necessary furniture, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV with satellite antenna.

For the comfort of guests in the guest house "Victoria" has a kitchen and guest area. The kitchen has a stove, fridge, microwave, electric kettle, dishes. A TV is installed in the living room, a DVD player is connected, and a couch is installed. Victoria also has a sauna and a gazebo with barbecue facilities.

Guest reviews about the guest house note the following:

  • For vacationers in the ski resort "Victoria" - one of the best offers.
  • The rooms are comfortable, spacious and clean.
  • It offers stunning mountain views.
  • The guest house is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay: there is a kitchen, living room, sauna, barbecue.
  • Prices for accommodation are affordable.
  • Salma is within walking distance.
  • There are cafes and shops in the vicinity of Victoria.

Despite the small number of hotels in this city, visitors can easily find their own accommodation, depending on financial capabilities and needs. If a visit to the city is connected with work matters, then the most suitable lodging options are the Niva Shores and Dawns hotels.
