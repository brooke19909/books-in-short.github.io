MOSFET Transistor. Application of MOSFET transistors in electronics

MOSFETs are often used in chip manufacturing. These elements are designed to control the voltage of the circuit. Devices work on the principle of changing polarity. To date, many modifications have been released, which differ in the parameter of output resistance, sensitivity and conductivity. They are similar in design.

Low conductivity models consist of two cells. Conductors are installed in the lower part of the housing. Inside the element are channels with diodes. The scope of transistors is very extensive. Most often they are found in power supplies.





IRG4BC10K Series Transistors

This designation of transistors suggests that they are suitable for switches. They are installed on microcircuits with high current conductivity. The operation modes of the transistor can be adjusted by changing the frequency in the circuit. In this case, the limit sensitivity index is 5 mV. Submariners are able to withstand the output voltage of 12 V. If we consider modifications with connectors, then there transistors are connected through a modulator. Capacitors for improving conductivity are used only pulse type.

To solve problems with negative polarity, varicaps are needed. It is also important to note that these transistors are suitable for video senders. In this case, the elements are able to work only with field capacitors. In this case, the current conductivity will not exceed 10 microns. In power supplies, the use of transistors is limited to models up to 15 V.





Parameters of the IRG4BC8K Series Transistor

The presented MOSFET series N-channel transistor is in great demand. First of all, it is important to note that it belongs to the class of high-frequency elements. The sensitivity parameter for the models is 6 mV. Current conductivity averages 12 microns. For switches, models do not fit well. They also quickly overheat in the sides of the food.

Devices can work only with absorbing filters. Most often, modifications are found in controllers and regulators. Microcircuits for them are selected by the PP20 series. If we consider a standard controller with the indicated transistor, then the capacitors are used of a loop-through type. Filters in this case are taken with a lining. If we consider the regulator circuit, then the transistor is installed behind open capacitors. The conductivity should be no more than 15 microns. The maximum permissible current overload is 3 A.





Application of models IRG4BC17K

This designation of transistors suggests that they are used for switches and receivers. In this case, the current conductivity fluctuates around 5.5 microns. The sensitivity of the modification depends on the type of capacitors selected. If we consider the scheme of a standard receiver, then they are used of the field type. In this case, the sensitivity of the element fluctuates around 16 mV. It is also important to note that filters are only allowed to be of the absorbing type.

The permissible overload level in this situation will not exceed 3.5 A. The output voltage of these transistors in the receivers can withstand 14 V. If we consider a circuit with a switch, then the capacitors are pulse type. In total, two filters are required for the device. Directly the transistor is installed behind the winding. The current conductivity must be no more than 8 microns.





If we consider the modification with operational capacitors, then the above parameter will not exceed 10 microns. How to check a mosfet transistor? This can be done using a conventional tester. The specified device will immediately show a violation of the integrity of the conductors.

Features of the IRG4BC15K model

Powerful transistors of the presented series are suitable for PP20 chips. They are used in various regulators to control engines. Modes of operation of the transistor are easy to adjust due to a change in the frequency in the circuit. If we consider the circuit of a conventional model, the output voltage on the conductors is 15 V. On average, the current conductivity is 4.5 microns.

The sensitivity of the element depends on the capacitors, as well as the adapter. It is also important to consider the output impedance in the circuit. If we consider the modification with a grid adapter, then the sensitivity of the element is not more than 20 mV. Do not use triodes in the circuit. In order to increase the conductivity of the transistor, rectifiers are used.

If we consider the regulator on a broadband adapter, then the sensitivity indicator is not more than 15 mV. It is also important to note that the output voltage fluctuates around 10 V. In this case, the threshold resistance is about 20 ohms. In power units, the use of transistors is limited to devices up to 15 V.





Scope of the transistor IRG4BC3K

The transistors of the presented series are suitable for switches of various power. Also, devices are actively used in receivers. The throughput of the modifications varies around 7 microns. In this case, the sensitivity depends on the capacitors. If we consider a standard switch, then they use a single-junction type in it. In this case, the sensitivity index does not exceed 3 mV. If we consider devices with two-junction capacitors, then in this case the above parameter can reach 6 mV.

It is also important to note that the transistor is capable of working only with transition adapters. In some cases, insulators are installed to increase voltage stability. Filters are most often used as a conductor type. If we consider the receiver circuit with the indicated transistors, then the output voltage should not exceed 12 V. In this case, it is more advisable to select the capacitors of the operational type. The average sensitivity will be 12 mV.

Installing a transistor in an electric drive

A MOSFET transistor in electric drives of small power is allowed to be installed through adapters. In this case, capacitors are used with filters. The converter for normal operation of the system is selected without a rectifier. In some cases, a dinistor is installed.

If we consider a 10 kW drive, then the transistor should be with a kenotron. The maximum output voltage will reach 15 V. However, the resistance in the circuit should also be taken into account. On average, the specified parameter does not exceed 50 ohms.





Transistor in a 5 V power supply

In 5 V power supplies, the MOSFET can be installed without filters. Directly adapters are selected control type. Some modifications use a damper. In this case, the conductivity parameter does not exceed 5.5 microns. Sensitivity, in turn, depends on the type of capacitors. For 5 V units, they are often used as an integral type. There are also modifications with impulse elements. How to replace the transistor in the power supply with 5 V? If necessary, this can always be done by installing an expander.

10 V block transistors

In 10 V power supplies, the MOSFET is mounted with absorbing filters. Capacitors are most often used pulse type. The output impedance in the circuit must not exceed 50 ohms. It is also important to note that open adapters are not allowed. In this case, they can be replaced by a comparator. The negative resistance index will not exceed 40 Ohms.





Devices in a 15 V unit

In a 15 V power supply, a MOSFET can be installed with high bandwidth. If we consider modifications without amplifiers, then they are selected with an adapter. Capacitors for the circuit, many experts recommend taking a duplex type. In this case, the sensitivity of the element will be 35 mV. In turn, the overload indicator will be no more than 2.5 A.

In order to increase the current conductivity, pulse capacitors are used. However, it is important to note that they consume a lot of electricity. Pulse-type capacitors also provide additional load on the converter. To solve the presented problem, a triode is installed next to the transistor. It is more expedient to use a grid-type triode. Also on the market are modifications with an inverter.

Transistors in dimmers

Lighting controllers often use low sensitivity transistors. All this is necessary in order to solve problems with sudden changes in temperature. In this case, the negative resistance should not exceed 50 Ohms. Capacitors for systems are selected binary type. Many experts do not recommend the use of duplex adapters.
