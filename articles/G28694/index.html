How to recover a MTPL discount: step-by-step instructions

Currently, a huge number of accidents involving vehicles are taking place on the roads of our country. In order to somehow protect themselves in an accident, the driver needs to take out an insurance policy.

Every year, more and more motorists are wondering how to restore the CTP insurance discount. This is due to the fact that when renewing a car citizen, the cost of the policy is calculated without taking into account the discount. This is a rather unpleasant situation, especially if the driver has a large continuous accident-free experience and the discount amount reaches 50 percent.

The burning of accumulated bonuses may seem like a hopeless situation, however, there are several ways by which you can restore the KBM ( bonus-malus coefficient) . This and much more will be discussed in this article.

Why can MSC be calculated incorrectly?



Before talking about how to restore the CTP insurance discount (Rosgosstrakh offers it to its customers), you first need to understand the main reasons because of which the insurance coefficient may not be calculated correctly. This will greatly simplify the discount recovery process and will save a lot of time.

All information about insurance experience and previous policies is stored in a single electronic database AIS, which is under the jurisdiction of the PCA. When making changes to the database and updating data on a particular driver, various errors can be made that will cause an incorrectly calculated discount on car insurance.

In addition, there are a number of other reasons for the reduction of MSC, among which the most common are:

  1. Replacement of a driver‚Äôs license or data about an individual.
  2. The insurer did not transmit driver information to RSA.
  3. Mistakes made by the insurance agent when filling out the policy.
  4. Change in insurance program.
  5. Interruption of trouble-free experience.
  6. Permission to drive a car to several drivers.

Regardless of the reason for the burning of insurance points, each driver can return the overpayment for compulsory motor third-party liability insurance and restore the discount in one of several ways, which will be discussed a little further, but for now, each reason should be considered in more detail.

Replacing a driver‚Äôs license or personal data

One of the most frequently asked questions for many drivers is: "How to restore the CTP insurance discount after replacing a license?" And this is not surprising, because every motorist has changed his driver‚Äôs license at least once in his life. Each document is assigned an individual number, which must be entered into a single electronic database. If this is not done, then the insurance history is reset, and you will not receive any discounts when applying for the policy. The same applies to changing the name.

The insurer did not transmit to the PCA the necessary information about the client



Employees of insurance companies are the same people as you and I are, therefore, due to their negligence, carelessness or other human factors, they may not transfer data to the AIS about the policyholder. A similar situation may arise during the liquidation of the insurance company. How to restore the CTP insurance discount (Rosgosstrakh offers its customers such an opportunity) will be discussed a little later.

Incorrectly filled out insurance policy

Even the smallest mistake made when filling out the insurance contract can lead to the return of KBM to the base value. This is due to the fact that if incorrect data are entered into the policy, then they will also go to the AIS, as a result of which the driver will be perceived as a completely different person.

Admission to driving a few persons

This situation applies mainly to corporate transport, which is used by several company employees. In this case, when registering the MTPL policy, the smallest size of KBM will be taken into account.

Change in insurance program or interruption of trouble-free service

If the driver has not been involved in traffic accidents for one full year, then he will be credited with insurance points that provide a discount when renewing the policy. If the insurance program has been changed or the driver has not been driving a car for more than one year, then all the accumulated points are burned up and the KBM is reset. How to recover discounts on compulsory motor third party liability insurance in this case? Unfortunately, nothing.

Is it worth restoring insurance points?



The amount of the discount depends on many factors, among which the main ones are the accident-free driving period and the number of accidents involving the driver. If the car owner regularly gets into an accident, then when applying for an insurance policy, a coefficient of 2.45 is charged to him. It follows that he will have to pay almost 2.5 times more for the insurance of his vehicle than the average driver.

If a car enthusiast observes the speed limit and traffic rules, and also does not get into an accident, then over time he can accumulate insurance points that will allow you to get almost a 50 percent discount on insurance services.

Thus, the question of how to restore the CTP insurance discount in Rosgosstrakh is very relevant nowadays, since KBM allows you to significantly save on motor citizens. In addition, if the insurer incorrectly calculated the coefficient for previous policies, then when restoring points, the driver will receive compensation.

The main methods of recovery of the KBM

So, you turned to the insurance company to renew the policy and found that it has risen in price. What to do in this case, and how to restore the CTP insurance discount? VSK offers its customers help, other insurance companies - too. The first thing to calm down, because there is nothing wrong with that.

You can return the previous discount by:

  • applying to the UK;
  • appeals to the Central Bank of the Russian Federation;
  • compiling a complaint to the PCA.

Each of the methods has certain nuances, therefore, it is necessary to dwell on each of them in more detail.

Solving a problem through an insurer

Many drivers are interested in the question: "How to restore the CMTPL discount after replacing the rights?" The easiest and fastest way will be to contact your insurer. Current legislation obliges insurance companies to consider all complaints and appeals of clients related to CTP.

In this regard, on the websites of most UK there is even a special section in which you can file a complaint online. Scans of the previous policy or a certificate certifying the absence of insurance cases from the client issued by the insurer will need to be attached to the form. If you do not have access to the Internet, you can write a written application and send it by registered mail to the physical address of the company. The application must be submitted in duplicate, and also photocopies of the above documents are attached to it.



The secretary will have to register the appeal in a special journal, and on one of the applications put down the registration number and seal, and then send it by mail to the client. According to the legislation of the Russian Federation, the insurance company has 10 days, during which it must establish the problem of combustion of KBM and solve this problem. And how to restore the CTP insurance discount, if a year has passed, you ask? Unfortunately, this case does not provide for the possibility of restoring the MSC.

What if the insurance company was liquidated?

If the insurer with whom you issued the insurance policy no longer operates, then you can restore the MSC through the CBR or RSA. You will learn more about the procedure for applying and submitting documents to these authorities.

Applying to the Central Bank

How to recover a CTP discount through the Central Bank of the Russian Federation? It is very simple, however, it is necessary to take into account the fact that this instance considers only those appeals of citizens submitted under compulsory motor third-party liability insurance that have completed their operation no more than one year ago.

To restore the KBM, you need to go to the Internet reception of the Central Bank and fill out an online form, making all the necessary information and specifying ‚ÄúIncorrect calculation of the KBM‚Äù as the reason. It will also be necessary to attach a scan of the previous policy or a certificate confirming the absence of insurance payments to the form. This method is very convenient, since the driver will receive notifications about the status of his application for e-mail. The time it takes for the Central Bank to make a decision can take up to 30 days, during which the authorities will check the relevance of the information provided by the driver, establish the cause of the problem and make a final decision.

Now you know how to recover a CTP discount. Alfastrakhovanie and the Central Bank of the Russian Federation help to solve this problem on the same principle. If suddenly, for any reason, contacting these instances does not bring any results, then you should file a complaint with the PCA, which is the most powerful tool in solving this problem.

PCA appeal



RSA is one of the most influential organizations in the world of automobile insurance. It can not only exert significant pressure on the UK, but also deprive them of a license, which every insurer is afraid of. Therefore, when mentioning only the name of this instance, any insurer will strive by all means to solve the client's problem. It is worth noting that the PCA is not entitled to make any changes to a single electronic database, since this is the responsibility of insurance companies.

So, how to restore CTP insurance discounts in RSA? The official Internet resource of this instance provides detailed instructions on how to restore discounts on the CTP policy. You can also find the corresponding application form and contacts for advice.
