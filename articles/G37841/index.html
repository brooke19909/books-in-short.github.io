Intelligent charger for car battery: review, description, reviews

Eliminate problems with starting the car in cold weather will allow an intelligent charger. Thanks to this device, you will not need to seek help from other drivers and worry about the comfort of passengers. The onset of cold increases the likelihood of problems with the battery by an order of magnitude. Consider the features and characteristics of devices that will help to avoid these misunderstandings.





general information

Before purchasing an intelligent charger for a car battery, you need to familiarize yourself with the characteristics of the installed battery and the possibilities of its interaction with charging. Mostly the mass of AB is classified as a type of lead-acid action. Other parameters are indicated on the battery label.

Transformer modifications are gradually becoming obsolete due to their large size and weight. The best option is a pulse smart battery chargers. Consider the capabilities and features of these units.

Principle of operation

Pulse devices for charging batteries consist of a power supply that operates at very high frequencies. In this regard, it became possible to reduce the dimensions and weight of the device, as well as reliably protect them from moisture and possible short circuits. The smart device in question is as automated as possible. Its use is optimal in comparison with other analogues.

The principle of operation of all battery charges is almost the same. The device is powered from a standard network, lowering the voltage to the required value by rectifying the current depending on the characteristics of the battery. The unit scheme is quite complicated, but in practice it is enough to turn on the operating mode and wait for the signaling device to notify about the end of the operation.





Description

The smart charger is based on high-tech electronics. The designers of these devices were able to achieve a fully automated work process. The built-in microcontroller allows you to use various programs, as well as all kinds of protection.

The user just needs to connect the battery to the charging connected to the network. Further operation takes place automatically. The end of work is indicated by a light indicator or the corresponding inscription on the monitor.

Features

Intelligent car battery charger requires certain operating rules, as well as its analogues. Acid-lead modifications, it is advisable not to bring to complete zeroing. Frequent recharging is only good for them. Alkaline options, on the contrary, need to be fully discharged, since they have a certain charge memory effect. Common among both variations is the fact that they must be charged to the maximum position.





Modernization

For the optimal choice of a charging device, it is necessary to take into account not only its parameters, but also the characteristics of the battery (type, capacity, nominal charge value). The modern smart charger has a lifespan of at least five years.

The dream of all motorists is an eternal battery. However, today it is still from the realm of fantasy. No matter how high-quality an element is, it needs regular recharging. The considered modifications will satisfy the interests of almost any, even the most picky consumer.

Pros and cons

An intelligent charger has many advantages. The main ones are compactness and light weight. Modern modifications have a mass of 600-700 grams. It is worth noting that these devices require a certain time to charge. Since programming elements does not provide an instant effect, check its condition in advance. The average inspection period is at least once a month.





The disadvantages of the device in question include its repair. The unit can only be resumed in specialized workshops. Some models cannot even be taken apart on their own, as they are sealed at all seams.

Technical plan options

The modern intelligent car charger has good build quality. Depending on the modification, the charging mode is faster or slower, but the final goal is always achieved. By types, the devices under consideration are divided into multistage, automatic or intelligent options.

Below are the technical specifications of the latter category:

  • The maximum battery capacity is up to 300 A / h.
  • Charging current - 2/5/8/12 A.
  • Standby time (voltage indicator) - 0.1 A.
  • The maximum battery capacity is 9.
  • Rechargeable battery voltage - 12 V.
  • The supported battery types are helium, calcium, antimony, and AGM.
  • Cable length - 1800 mm.
  • Mode - charging and support.
  • Work Monitor - LCD or analog monitor.

Among the additional options can be noted the possibility of switching to winter mode, high performance and long working life.





Criterias of choice

The choice of a smart charger for batteries depends on the technical features of the device, as well as on consumer feedback. When purchasing devices in the domestic market, pay attention to the following points:

  • Give preference to models equipped with a certain reserve of charging current.
  • Consider the possibility of a combined function.
  • Ability to work in standard mode and as a stabilizer.
  • Size, design and indicators of the device.

When choosing an intelligent battery charger , pay attention to the manufacturer. The most quality recognized models from well-known foreign and domestic manufacturers.

Model Overview

Below is a brief description of the most popular chargers in the domestic market.

Pennant-27 2045. The device is a budget model that operates in automatic mode. The consumer has options for turning on and adjusting the current strength. Information on operating parameters is displayed on a digital monitor.

QUATTRO ELEMENTI i-Charge 10. This unit does not require supervision of the owner and special actions. If necessary, it is possible to adjust the supplied current from 2 to 10 amperes. A battery with a maximum capacity of 100 A can interact with this device.

SMART-POWER SP-25N Professional. This smart charger (AA class) is suitable for professional use in automotive services. It supports a voltage of 24 Volts, operates in automatic mode, not only charges the battery, but also restores its properties.

Telwin Leader 150 Start. This modification is used to charge high capacity cells. In addition, the device is designed to start the power unit. A feature of the model is the lack of automation, all modes must be manually configured.

Pennant-32 2043. Charger equipped with a Boost system, which allows you to charge the battery in an accelerated manner. It is worth noting that in this mode, the battery may lose some of its properties. The device is compact, equipped with infinitely adjustable current strength.

Fubag FORCE 420. Powerful starter-charger. The starting operating current reaches a maximum of 360 A. This allows you to charge the most capacious battery of a passenger car or truck.





Consumer Reviews

Users of intelligent car chargers respond mostly in a positive way. Owners note the versatility, practicality and durability of the unit. The compact device does not require a large storage area, it is convenient in transportation, and automation minimizes human participation in its work. Consumer call a single minus the high cost. It is worth noting that the price is determined by the decent quality and characteristics of the device.
