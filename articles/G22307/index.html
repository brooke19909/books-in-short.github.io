Interest deduction

Mortgage property buyers may receive a tax deduction on interest on the mortgage and on the cost of housing. It must be borne in mind that since 2014, an amendment to the Tax Code has been in force, which now indicates that interest rates are limited to three million rubles. This circumstance does not apply to those who have such a right before 2014. Thus, starting in 2014, interest deductions are limited to the indicated amount.

  • A completed tax return.
  • Submission of an application for a tax refund with the details of the account of the institution to which the applicant must transfer the money.
  • Issued income statement for the year for which the buyer wishes to return the tax.
  • Presentation of a contract for the sale of housing.
  • Documentation confirming the date of registration of property rights. In cases of investment in real estate under construction by agreement of equity participation will need an act of transfer of the apartment.

  • Bank reference on the actual payment of interest for the year. Each bank is given a certificate in its own form. The main thing is that it should contain the amount of interest paid for the year in rubles.
  • Documentation confirming payment (we are talking about cash receipts, receipts, payment orders, statements). Is it possible to receive a deduction at once from two mortgage apartments?

Starting from 2014, deduction limits are determined not per property (for example, per apartment), but per person, that is, directly to the buyer who receives it. Until now, many people believe that only the spouse for whom the property is registered can count on receiving this service. In fact, both husband and wife can file a tax deduction.

In addition, it can be obtained in several apartments. True, the limit of two million rubles applies to absolutely all objects in the aggregate, in this regard, such an innovation is only able to buyers of inexpensive real estate. For example, a person purchased two apartments of one and a half million rubles. Previously, he could receive a deduction only from this amount, and now from two million. In some cases, it can be obtained from three apartments, each of which cost seven hundred thousand.

Receipt options

There will be two ways to pick it up. The first option is to receive a property deduction in one payment at the end of the year. The entire amount is returned through the tax office. In the process of registration, a tax person will need a declaration. We are talking about the form of 3-personal income tax for the year when the apartment was purchased. After the documentation has passed the due diligence, the applicant receives the returned tax on the personal current account.

On the official portal of the Federal Tax Service there is a video instruction on how to fill out such a declaration. There you can find a deduction form and see approximate filling options.

The second way is registration through the employer. To do this, you should require a tax notice with a document confirming the right to property deduction. After receiving such a notice, the employer will be obliged to pay the person income without deducting thirteen percent of his salary until the end of the calendar year, as well as reimburse the amount previously deducted for the past months.

Mortgage to the unemployed

We learn how to get tax deductions for a mortgage to an unemployed: can this be done at all?

The cost of real estate, unfortunately, is increasing every year. Despite the constant rise in prices, people regularly need personal housing. The best option for buying a property is a mortgage, as accumulating the required amount is far from always possible. Mortgage loans can be issued by any domestic bank. At the same time, each of them has its own rules with the terms of registration of contracts. Many of those who plan to purchase real estate are wondering whether it is possible for the unemployed to take a mortgage, and if such an opportunity exists, then how to do it.

So, how can you get a mortgage for an unemployed person or someone who is informally employed and gets his salary in envelopes? It is worth noting that a few years ago mortgages were available only to officially employed citizens, but today the situation has changed dramatically. An increase in the number of credit institutions leads to increased competition between them. For this reason, many organizations have ceased to require documents from their clients confirming income.

In addition, many professions that are in demand at the moment do not oblige people to formal employment at all. In this regard, banking institutions are trying to keep up to date and trying to be loyal to their customers, so now there is the opportunity to take a mortgage for an unemployed person. However, credit state organizations do not issue funds without providing full documentation.

Mortgages for the unemployed can be issued by banking institutions without providing official certificates from the place of work. To obtain the required loan, you can today turn to one of the following three most common options, for example:

  • A mortgage is issued to the unemployed in the event that the person who issues the loan acts as an individual entrepreneur or conducts his own private practice.   • One of the most common options for obtaining a mortgage loan is also a guarantee. Friends and relatives may act as guarantors. Banks are provided with those documents that confirm the fact that the guarantors have real estate. The downside is that few people agree to become a surety for another person.
  • The provision of payment of half the amount of mortgage lending directly on the day of its registration. The remaining half is divided into parts that need to be repaid within a certain period.
