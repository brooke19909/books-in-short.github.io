Izmir Hotel, Sudak: description and photos

Crimean peninsula is a unique place. In a relatively small area there are completely dissimilar landscapes. Every corner of Crimea is filled with sights - historical, natural and architectural. The southern coast is known for its subtropical climate, mountainous terrain, exotic plants and graceful palaces of the royal era. The Western Crimea is famous for its wide sandy beaches and shallow warm sea, and the East Bank is a cozy picturesque bays, mysterious grottoes, historical monuments and sunny valleys valleys.

Each resort village of the peninsula is interesting in its own way. Wherever you come, bright and unforgettable impressions await you everywhere.

Rest in the Pike perch - southeast Crimea

The resort town of Sudak is located in the southeast of the peninsula and is popular with vacationers. From the north side it is protected from the wind and cold by mountains covered with dense forest, so the local climate is very mild, and the weather is almost always good.

Sudak is located on the shore of a cozy bay, along which nature has created gorgeous small-pebble and sandy beaches with distinctive nuances - quartz sand, when heated, favorably affects health, restores immunity and improves metabolism.

Izmir Hotel in Sudak

In the resort corner of Crimea on the very shore of the purest Black Sea at the foot of the famous Genoese fortress, an unusual Izmir hotel is located. It is named after the ancient Turkish city of the same name.

Izmir is a hotel (Sudak), which is a complex of two-story buildings in the eastern Turkish style, located around the perimeter. The highlight of Izmir is a cozy patio. The rooms for vacationers are located on the first and second floors of the building and each of them opens onto the courtyard.

The hotel offers magnificent views of the historic towers and the sea. If you want to wake up in the morning and enjoy the endless sea, picturesque mountains and a magnificent fortress, then you should definitely visit the Izmir Hotel.

Sudak, as you know, is famous for its healing beaches. Izmir Hotel is located twenty meters from the sandy beach, which is equipped with all necessary equipment: umbrellas and sunbeds.

The sea pier of the village is very close. Every day, seasonally, pleasure boats and yachts go on an excursion from the marina. Everyone can take a fascinating boat trip and see the beauties of Crimea from the sea.

Accommodation for vacationers

Tourists of the Izmir Hotel are accommodated in rooms of three categories: standard, junior suite and suite. Each room has its own individual characteristics, but there are common features.

So, for example, all rooms have their own access to the courtyard. In each of them there is a bathroom with all amenities - shower, toilet, toiletries.





All rooms, without exception, have a small refrigerator. On hot days, air conditioning provides guests with coolness. For those who like to keep abreast of all the news, each room has a TV with satellite channels.

On the ground floor of the building there are cozy standard double and triple rooms of the hotel. There are special recreational areas at the entrance. The rooms of the "standard" category are one-room, but quite spacious. All necessary furniture is available. The interior of the rooms is made in oriental style, in pastel colors and contributes to calm and conciliation.

Junior suites are larger than standard rooms. Junior suites on the ground floor have at their disposal cozy covered gazebos for relaxation. The second rooms have access to a private balcony equipped with a table and chairs.





The suites are located on the second floor of the building. They are two-room. On the spacious balcony of such apartments there is a real Turkish teahouse.

For large families or companies, the Izmir Hotel can offer three-room rooms with equipped kitchens.

Accommodation prices

Prices at the hotel, as well as at other Crimean vacation spots, depend on the time of year and season. From mid-April to mid-October, the Izmir Hotel accepts tourists. Sudak is an amazing resort. The swimming season here begins in late May and lasts until the beginning of October.

  • The price of a standard room is from 2800 to 4900 rubles per day.
  • Junior suite with a gazebo and a platform costs from 3000 to 5900 rubles per day.
  • Junior suites and suites with a balcony are more expensive - from 3400 to 6400 rubles per day.
  • Two-room suite - from 4600 - 7600 rubles per day.
  • Three-room suite with a kitchen - from 4900 to 9000 rubles per day.

For early bookings, the hotel offers a twenty percent discount. The price includes breakfast for each main guest.

Hotel infrastructure

The magnificent courtyard is a special advantage that the Izmir Hotel boasts. Sudak is famous for its infrastructure, but sometimes it is not needed at all. There are evenings when there is no desire to leave the hotel. Izmir has everything to prevent guests from getting bored at such moments.





There is a playground for children in the courtyard of the hotel. A lot of shops and swings, a place for barbecue, beautiful flower beds and exotic plants - all this is created for the convenience of vacationers.

The hotel has an excellent cafe with oriental and European cuisine. It is here that guests are served breakfast. If desired, the cafe can order lunch or a romantic dinner.

The Izmir Hotel has prepared a wonderful vacation with all amenities. Sudak is rich in attractions and all of them are nearby. Three hundred meters away is a water park, many restaurants and cafes, the city promenade.

Tourists reviews

What kind of rest will the Izmir Hotel (Sudak) give vacationers? Reviews of tourists will help to clarify and understand the positive and negative aspects of this place of rest.

Advantages of the hotel:

  • great location;
  • cozy courtyard;

Cons of the hotel:
