Sights and interesting places in Ufa: photos and descriptions, what to see, tips

Ufa is the capital of Bashkiria, a million-plus city. It is located on the banks of the Belaya River, on the Pribelskaya Plain. This is a rapidly developing city in which there is a construction boom. The capital of Bashkiria is very beautiful. Hundreds of tourists visit it with interest.

Walks in the central part

To get acquainted with interesting places in Ufa, we begin our virtual walk along the main street named after Lenin. She appeared in the XVIII century. Here is a historic building, which now houses the library named after Zaki Validi.

And at the intersection with Pushkin Street is the Bashkir Opera and Ballet Theater, and near the Academy of Arts of Bashkiria.

In the evening, you should definitely go to the promenade, where there are also a huge number of ancient buildings, monuments, cultural and entertainment venues.

Of course, you should ride the Ferris wheel, which is located near the shopping center Iremel, 137 Mendeleev Street. This is the wheel where the booths are closed and its height is 60 meters.

Cultural rest

If you are a theater lover, you should definitely go to the M. Gafuri Academic Drama Theater (34 Zaki Validi Street), although all the productions here are exclusively in the Bashkir language. The building itself is an interesting place in Ufa. It was built in the style of eclecticism, with elements of oriental and Russian architecture.

You can also go to the opera and ballet theater, where there are always many interesting productions.

The best and most interesting places in Ufa

Very beautiful mosque-madrassah "Lyalya-Tulip" in the city. It is located on the outskirts, at the entrance to the city from Chernivka (Komarova street, 5). These are two tall white minarets, with red "tulip buds" at the top. The mosque is visible from many points of the city. Minarets have a height of 53 meters.

They built a mosque in 1998. Her prayer room is designed for 500 visitors. For the female half of the faithful, a private balcony is equipped here, with a capacity of 200 people.

Beautiful carpets lie on the floor of the building, and the walls are inlaid with marble. The mosque itself is not only a house for prayers, but also the educational and cultural center at which the school operates, there is a dining room, a conference room and a hostel. All this is surrounded by a green park.

It is in this mosque that the Mufti of Russia reads sermons.

Cathedral Mosque

The list of interesting places in Ufa includes the first cathedral mosque, the construction of which began in 1830. Today its building is an architectural monument. It was built in the classical style on the donations of the merchant Tagirova-Khazmitev.

In 1890, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out. As a result, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe mosque was significantly increased.

The cathedral mosque is located on a hill, on the banks of the Belaya River, along Tukaev Street, 52.

Monument to Salavat Yulaev

It is with the image of this monument that souvenirs from Bashkiria are most often brought. Salavat Yulaev is the national hero of the republic, an associate of Pugachev.

The monument weighs 40 tons, its height reaches 10 meters. This is the largest equestrian sculpture in Russia and Europe. A monument was created in 1967. It is made of cast iron with a thin coating of bronze. The sculpture is located on the eponymous square where mass events are held.

On the observation deck near the monument, you can admire the landscapes of the city with the help of observation binoculars.

Museum of Archeology and Ethnography

This is an interesting place in Ufa for tourists of all ages. On an ongoing basis, it has 4 expositions where you can see artifacts from the Paleolithic era. This is where the real skeleton of the Khazar mammoth is located.

In the museum you can get acquainted with the culture and life of local peoples, starting from the 19th century, see in which yurts they lived. Be sure to look at the exhibition "Sarmatian Gold", where there is a golden figurine of a deer, and many exhibits date back to the 4th century BC. The museum is located on Karl Marx Street, 6.

Hippodrome "Akbuzat"

Horse enthusiasts should visit the hippodrome, because not every city boasts such an institution. Bashkortostan residents have a love of horses in blood. The first such institution in the city appeared in 1883, and the modern complex was built in 1982. He is considered one of the best in Russia. International competitions have been held here more than once. The hippodrome is located at: Mendeleev street, 217A.

Communication with animals

The Contact Zoo, located on 205A Mendeleev Street in the building of the Bashkorstan Shopping Center, is an interesting place in Ufa for teenagers and children. This zoo is called the Forest Embassy. The Moscow Dolphinarium is an unforgettable experience of the performance of representatives of the deep sea. The dolphinarium is located on the street Novozhenova, 92V in the park "Kashkadan". Interesting places near Ufa

Love the untouched and beautiful nature? Go to Lake Aslikul. This is the largest lake in the whole republic. Located near the town of Davlekanovo.

This is a reservoir of karst-disastrous origin, located in a wide pit between the spurs of the Belebeyov Upland. The lake is fed by meltwater, rainfall and underground keys. The water in it is slightly salty, high in minerals. Reeds and cattail grow on the banks.

Another most interesting place in Ufa and the republic is Mount Kuzguntash, however, it is located 189 kilometers from the capital, between the villages of Umetbaevo and Khakhilovo. The height of the mountain is 987 meters. Translated from the Bashkir language, its name means "black stone". The mountain is under state protection, being a natural monument.

Not afraid of heights? Then go to the Pervushino airfield. Here you can fly by helicopter or plane and see Ufa as it is seen by birds. Parachute jumps are carried out on the basis of the flying club both in tandem with an instructor, and independent. After a successful jump, you can relax in the cafe of the same name. You can also play paintball at the airport. That is, all the conditions are created here to increase the amount of adrenaline and relax actively.
