Transport accessibility

Future buyers are attracted by the fact that there will be a metro station near the complex, and it is not a problem to come here by ground public transport. Now the metro is far away, not reachable on foot. The construction site and sales office are 5.5 km from Ryazansky Prospekt Station. But ground transportation helps out, as several bus and trolleybus routes follow here. You can use minibuses (No. 63, 463, 351).

By the time the object is commissioned, they are planning to launch the Ryazansky interchange hub. Then there will be no need for an extra transplant, and the residents of the Sreda complex will have several routes to other parts of the city and beyond. In the future, an overpass will appear next to the complex. However, it cannot be said that this is a convenient place for car owners. The most logical way to come here is along Ryazan Avenue, because the rest of the roads are very narrow. A large freeway is always busy, so traffic jams are a common occurrence.
