Hotel Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 * (Krasnodar Territory, Krasnaya Polyana): room description, service, reviews

After the Olympic Games, the city of Sochi became a popular vacation destination not only among Russian tourists, but also among Europeans. Of course, the level of local service has also increased. Even budget hotels try to serve their guests in the highest class. If in summer tourists flock to the Black Sea coast, in winter they choose Krasnaya Polyana, which has become the center of skiing in recent years. In recent years, many hotels and infrastructure facilities of an international level have also appeared here. For example, often tourists from Russia and Europe stay at the Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 * hotel in Krasnaya Polyana. It is about this hotel that the article will be discussed.

Where is the hotel situated?

As mentioned above, the hotel is located in the ski village of Krasnaya Polyana in Sochi. It has an extremely favorable position for tourists. From all sides, the village is surrounded by mountains covered with dense forests, which makes the local air useful. Therefore, most often they come here to improve their health and ski. And in the summer in the village you can stay in one of the many resorts.

The hotel itself is located in the vast Rosa Khutor complex, which after the Olympics received the status of one of the best in Russia. It was built right on the embankment of the Mzymta River. The residential building of the hotel is located just 200 meters from the ski lifts, delivering tourists directly to the mountainside, where they can ski. Next to it, all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay has been created: shops, restaurants, cafes and bars work. The central part of Sochi is approximately 65 km away from here. Near the hotel, tourists can visit the ski museum and the ethnographic center. On the other side of the promenade is a supermarket. On foot you can reach the health trail, created for lovers of walks in the forest.

But the hotel is at a decent distance from the international airport. He is in Adler. To get to the place of rest from there, tourists will have to overcome about 40 km. The train station is 10 km away. If necessary, hotel staff can arrange a shuttle service for their guests.

general information

Hotel Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 * opened its doors to tourists in 2013. It is perfect for both single occupancy and family vacations. This hotel accepts guests throughout the year. Tourists often come here not only from Russia, but also from European countries. They can be housed in a five-story European-style hotel building. In total, 148 comfortable apartments are offered to guests here. The hotel staff speaks not only Russian, but also English.

Krasnaya Polyana in Sochi is a resort of international level, therefore, the requirements for guests when checking in at the hotel are standard. The rooms are suitable for children of any age, including newborns. When registering, residents of Russia must present a valid passport to the administrator. When arriving with children under 14 years of age, their birth certificates must be provided. Foreign guests will need not only a passport, but also a visa and a migration card. Tourists who come to rest with pets will be denied check-in.

The ski resort has prepared for its guests spacious and modern apartments of various categories. Among them there are options for both individual and family accommodation. The rooms are freshly renovated, put new furniture and plumbing. Each of them also has a standard set of hotel amenities, which will be described below. The hotel windows overlook the river and promenade or the vast mountain range.

The best option for accommodation are standard rooms designed to accommodate two adults and a child. They consist of a separate bedroom, a bathroom with a toilet and an open balcony. Additional equipment for living rooms

Since the Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 * hotel is an international level complex, all its rooms are equipped with a standard set of additional amenities. Although it is worth noting that some of them are still provided only for a fee. In the room, guests can find the following equipment:

  • individual air conditioning, which can be used in the summer, as well as heating in the winter;
  • a plasma TV to which a set of cable channels is connected, most of which are broadcast in Russian;
  • mini-bar for storing water and drinks, its filling is made at an additional cost;
  • a hairdryer in the bathroom, as well as a set of towels and hygiene items.

Guests can also expect daily housekeeping. Maids change bedding and towels several times a week. For an additional fee, delivery of food and drinks to the living rooms is provided.

Hotel infrastructure

Hotel Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 *, judging by the reviews of guests, is of high quality service. A full-fledged tourist infrastructure has been created on its territory, helping to make hotel accommodation as comfortable and cozy as possible. For free or for a fee, hotel guests can use the following facilities, services and facilities:

  • laundry and dry cleaning;
  • individual ski and equipment storage places;
  • car rental, yachts and boats;
  • free wireless internet throughout the complex;
  • 24-hour front desk, where you can also rent a luggage storage room and a safe;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • a business center with four conference rooms;
  • paid covered parking near the hotel;
  • newsstand, souvenir and flower shop.

As a rule, only breakfast is included in the price. They are served in the main restaurant of the Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 * hotel, which specializes in European cuisine and is located on the ground floor. For tourists, a buffet is served every morning. There is also a banquet hall. If necessary, guests can pay not only breakfast but also lunch. The restaurant is open daily until 23:00. On-site leisure options

The Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 * hotel is considered a ski resort, so it cannot be said that it offers a large selection of entertainment for its guests. However, there is still a minimal set of leisure options here. For example, tourists can visit the bath complex with a Finnish sauna. He works all day, and his services are included in the price. For sports enthusiasts, the hotel has a modern gym with treadmills. If desired, hotel guests can contact the tour desk and purchase excursions to the sights of Krasnaya Polyana and Sochi. In summer, a free shuttle service is available to the local beach.

Conditions for staying with small children

The hotel Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 *, according to tourists, is well suited for families with young children. Guests under 14 years old stay here at a discount. However, a large selection of amenities and entertainment for children in the complex is not provided.

Free hotel guests can also get a separate crib. It is issued if tourists call in with kids under two years old. In the restaurant you can request not only high chairs for feeding, but also a children's menu. Children can play in the outdoor area, equipped with slides, swings and a sandbox. There are no other entertainment options for kids at the hotel.

Positive feedback from tourists

As a rule, tourists liked their stay at the Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 * hotel; they leave mostly positive reviews about the time spent here. They note that the local service does correspond to the European level. At the same time, the cost of living can not be called very high. Tourists recommend it for a vacation in Krasnaya Polyana. In the reviews, they indicate the following advantages of this hotel:

  • clean and spacious rooms that have recently been redecorated;
  • varied breakfast meals; the buffet serves meat dishes, cereals, seasonal vegetables and fruits;
  • the rooms have good sound insulation, so tourists do not suffer from noise from the street and from neighboring living rooms;
  • excellent location, there is a grocery store nearby, and the ski lifts can be reached in 2-3 minutes;
  • Polite and qualified hotel staff.

Negative reviews

However, some tourists in their reviews criticize the service at the Tulip Inn Rosa Khutor 3 * hotel. The shortcomings discovered during the holiday by the guests turned out to be quite serious and spoiled the impression. So, hotel guests recommend that tourists before buying a ticket to the hotel pay attention to the following service deficiencies:

  • the cooling system in some rooms is too noisy;
  • hotel administrators sometimes behave unprofessionally, they can even be rude;
  • from hygiene items only liquid soap is provided;
  • due to poor ventilation, sometimes the room smells of tobacco smoke;
  • in the restaurant at breakfast, tables can be littered with dirty dishes that do not have time to clean up on time.

Despite these shortcomings, the hotel is worth recommending for a relaxing pastime with your family at the resort. Guests, often vacationing here, note that the service is better than in other hotels of Krasnaya Polyana.
