Hotel Bellevue Park Hotel 4 * (Tunisia / Sousse): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is a real treasure for tourists who prefer a relaxing holiday on the beach and immersion in a new interesting culture. Here you can relax not only in the summer season, so many travelers come to this country for a kind of wintering. The exotic beauty of nature, mild climate, extraordinary culture and traditions, and, of course, high-class service - all this, and even more, Tunisia can offer tourists.

Naturally, on the coast of the country there are many resort cities, including Sousse, which is considered one of the most popular beach holiday destinations. Naturally, many hotels are being built and opened on the territory of the city. And a good place to spend a vacation is considered the Bellevue Park Hotel.

During the travel planning period, people naturally try to get the maximum amount of information about the chosen place. For example, is the hotel location convenient and does it take far to go to the sea? What do living rooms look like and do they provide children with appropriate amenities? Is the hotel well-fed? How do other travelers respond about it? The answers to these questions are relevant.

Where to look for a hotel?





The location of the Bellevue Park Hotel 4 * hotel can be considered one of its advantages. It is located in the picturesque resort village of Port El Kantaoui, just a kilometer from its center and a few meters from the coast. Nearby is the more lively and famous tourist town of Sousse (distance is about 14 km). At 23 km is another popular resort - Monastir.

It is worth saying that just 200 meters from the hotel there is a public transport stop, which runs here constantly and regularly. With it, you can get to almost any town in the vicinity, and relatively inexpensive. Nearby are shops and malls where you can buy almost everything, including food, souvenirs and household items.

The distance to Enfid - Hammamet (the nearest international airport) is 22 km, which means that after arrival you will not have to get to the hotel for several hours.

What do the rooms look like? Photos and brief info





What can guests of Club Hotel Riu Bellevue Park 4 * count on? Tunisia is a country famous for its comfortable hotels and first-class service. And this hotel complex is no exception.

On the territory there are 387 rooms. For the most part, these are standard rooms, but there are also deluxe rooms with sea views, as well as family rooms designed for parents with children or large companies.

Regardless of which room you got, you can be sure that everything is arranged here to create the most comfortable pastime. The room, of course, has the necessary furniture, including large beds with orthopedic mattresses (by the way, linen is changed three times a week).

Guests are provided with a standard set of household appliances. In particular, there is a TV with a connection to a satellite dish (there are several Russian-language and music channels), as well as a telephone that allows you to dial a number directly. Valuable items can be left in a small but reliable safe. A minibar is filled upon request (drinking water is free).

You can count on a well-equipped bathroom with a shower. In addition, there is a sink, toilet, a large mirror, as well as a set of basic hygiene items. The owners also worried about the presence of a hair dryer. Bath towels are changed every day during cleaning. By the way, maids work well - the rooms always look clean and tidy.

Dining on site





Good food is the key to a truly quality vacation. After all, during the vacation you want to treat yourself to delicacies that are not at home, as well as strengthen the body with healthy food. The Club Hotel Riu Bellevue Park hotel complex operates according to the all-inclusive scheme already familiar to tourists - guests are provided not only with full three meals a day, but also snacks throughout the day and drinks from a local producer (alcohol is included in the price).

According to reviews, the food at the Club Hotel Bellevue Park is really good. Every day guests are offered a huge selection of dishes, including appetizers, salads, fresh fruits and vegetables, side dishes, dishes from different varieties of meat and fish. And, of course, an abundance of fresh fruits awaits you, as well as delicious desserts and pastries. It offers not only familiar international food, but also some of the most popular dishes of Tunisian and oriental cuisine. There is also a barbecue - cafe where you can order meat, vegetables and seafood, grilled.

Where to look for a beach? Distance to the sea and description

Tunisia for many people is associated with sandy beaches and soft sea waves. It is only natural that people choose this country just for relaxing on the seashore. What can the hotel complex Hotel Bellevue Park ex 4 * offer?





As already mentioned, the hotel was built directly on the seashore - the distance from the main building to the beach is no more than 100 meters. This section of the coast belongs to the hotel, which means that only guests rest here and their vacation is overshadowed by the presence of strangers or by the accumulation of a huge number of tourists.

Reviews say that the beach here is really beautiful - it is covered with soft fine sand and is cleaned daily. The entrance to the sea is convenient, cleared of stones and algae, and the depth increases gradually. On the beach, guests can use large sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas for free. Here you can get clean beach towels for free (issued on bail, which is returned after the delivery of towels). This is really a cozy place for leisurely enjoyment of the beauties of nature.

Water recreation: how are tourists having fun?

Of course, many travelers prefer to spend time more actively. The Bellevue Park Hotel has a large water sports and leisure center. Here guests can rent a catamaran or a boat (prices are quite reasonable). If you wish, you can ride a banana or water ski, having received a lot of adrenaline and bright, positive emotions. Tourists often engage in parasailing and sailing.

A favorite activity of travelers is swimming with a mask. The water here is clean, and the underwater world is diverse and rich, so you are guaranteed a pleasant experience. There is also a diving center where tourists with certificates can rent equipment and suits necessary for diving. Beginners are offered to take training courses and make their first voyage in the company of an experienced instructor.

Sports facilities for volleyball and football are equipped on the beach. Regardless of your preferences, you can be sure that this beach has everything for a truly interesting and eventful vacation.

Entertainment at the hotel: how do guests spend their free time?





Despite the fact that the area in which the hotel is located is relatively quiet, you can still have fun and actively. The hotel offers a completely diverse entertainment, so everyone will find something different.

In the center of the courtyard there is a spacious pool, around which there are enough sun loungers and umbrellas. There is even a bar where you can wet your throat. By the way, water aerobics classes are regularly held here. There is a smaller pool - it is equipped with a heating system and is mainly used in bad weather.

You should definitely visit the spa. Here, for a relatively small fee, guests are offered a huge selection of various health and beauty treatments, including massages, peels, body wraps, etc. Here you can also enjoy a sauna or a traditional oriental hammam.

There is a team of animators working at the hotel, so there is no need to get bored - in the daytime contests and competitions are held, and in the evenings various shows and discos are organized. Guests can play bowling, billiards, table tennis or have a good time in the room with slot machines. Near the hotel there are tennis courts and golf courses. Tourists can also go horse riding or rent a bike for walking. There is a library for guests with a large collection of books in different languages. There is a gym and a fitness center - this will appeal to travelers who prefer to always stay in good physical shape.

Do not forget that you can have fun outside the Bellevue Park Hotel. Tunisia is an incredibly interesting country, which is ideal for a rich excursion vacation. You can visit the ruins of ancient cities, enjoy the incredible beauties of nature and even spend a few days in the Sahara. Tours can be purchased both in the city and in the hotel itself, which is very convenient. A vacation spent in Tunisia can be one of the highlights of your life.

Conditions for a vacation with a child

Parents planning a vacation with their children, of course, are interested in the availability of additional amenities at the hotel. So what can Club Hotel Riu Bellevue Park 4 * offer to young tourists?

In fact, this complex is designed specifically for family vacations - not only comfort, but also a lot of entertainment awaits the children. As for amenities, parents can count on an extra rollaway bed, which can be quickly delivered to the room upon request. There is no special children's buffet in the restaurant, but among the huge number of dishes you can always choose something useful for the child, not to mention the desserts adored by the children.

There is a special shallow pool in the courtyard for the little one - the water here is always warmed up, so the guys can splash around and have fun games. There is also a small but well-equipped playground with swings and sports equipment. And every day, young travelers are invited to a mini club where they can play, get to know each other, organize team games, and engage in art. Of course, teachers and funny animators are always with the children, who not only keep order and safety, but also come up with more and more entertainments for the children.

Hotel Bellevue Park Hotel: traveler reviews

Reviews of people who have already managed to spend several days in a particular place can sometimes provide a lot of useful information. So what are they saying about the Bellevue Park Hotel?

For the most part, reviews are positive or neutral. The hotel is cozy and well-equipped, and its calm design creates suitable conditions for relaxing. The territory is very beautiful and clean - you can always see several cleaners and gardeners, suggestive of beauty. By the way, the staff is very polite, sociable and friendly - this fact is decisive for many travelers when choosing a hotel.

The rooms are quite spacious, they are cleaned every day, and the equipment works here. The food is not bad - there is always a choice of dishes. Of course, tourists who spent a lot of time here complain about a certain uniformity in the menu. On the other hand, guests are not hungry.

A good advantage is the proximity to the beach, however, experienced travelers do not recommend relaxing here in the rainy season. The town is calm here - you can walk along the clean streets and visit the traditional oriental bazaar.

Today, many people choose the hotel Hotel Bellevue Park ex 4 * as a stop. Tunisia is a country that is ideal for beach, wellness and sightseeing holidays.
