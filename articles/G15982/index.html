Traditional ways of investing money - deposits of individuals

There are many ways to invest in passive income. Some people buy currency and wait for its value to rise. Others make money on Forex, invest in PAMM accounts, trade binary options, trying to earn income.

All these methods of obtaining passive income allow you to get a fairly high profit, but they are associated with certain risks. Term deposits in banks, offered to individuals, and in the modern world remain the most traditional and reliable way to invest their own savings in Russia.
