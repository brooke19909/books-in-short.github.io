Do-it-yourself motorcycle diapers

A wonderful gift for the baby to discharge are diapers. All mothers will be happy to receive them - quite expensive and necessary for hygiene. But just packing diapers is boring and uninteresting. And you can turn on fantasy and create a motorcycle from diapers. Here in this form, the gift will look much more fun and original!

Now we will learn how to make a motorcycle from diapers with your own hands.

Tools and materials

To create a motorcycle from diapers, we need to purchase:

  • A pack of thirty-six diapers (it is better to take one).
  • 4 large stationery erasers.
  • Satin ribbons, needles with beads.
  • 2 small diapers.
  • Towel.
  • Bibs, socks, or scratches.
  • Bottle with a pacifier.
  • A bit of cotton.

How to make a motorcycle from diapers with your own hands? Step by Step Description

To get started, you will need to thoroughly wash a small pot with low walls (you can take a baking dish). Diapers (approximately 17 pieces per wheel) are placed quite tightly in it so that their direction goes from the middle to the walls. We fix with rubber bands so that the "spare parts" do not fall apart. We take it out of the form and fasten it with a ribbon, turning in the middle.

Now it's the turn of creating the frame. Take a diaper and turn it into a tube. We align the tips, place a bottle-nipple (headlight) between them, cover it with a bib (wing) and fix the entire structure with a bundle of diapers, simply beautifully tying it with a ribbon or fixing with clamps. We put a little cotton in socks or scratches to give extra volume and put them on the steering wheel. We fix another bib wing over the rear wheel, and our work is ready for the baby! You can use a small soft toy as a motorcyclist.

Tips for the proper manufacture of diaper crafts

Do not take diapers for the manufacture of motorcycles, the size of which is less than 1. They must be sterile, since the wound on the navel has not yet healed in the newborn. Pampers-units are used for older children, and here it is possible to present them nicely!

Before working with diapers, wash your hands and all accessories thoroughly. Ideally, gloves should be assembled. It is better to lay a clean blanket on the work surface.

All pets must be removed from the premises.

For a large craft, purchase a larger number of diapers, first by specifying which size and brand of diapers are desired.

No need to turn the diaper inside out during the assembly of the gift.

Treat all the dishes for the baby that comes with the kit with an antiseptic solution.

If the gift includes children's clothing, be sure to iron it with a hot iron.
