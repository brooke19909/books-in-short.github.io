Relaxation

Holidays in Sedovo can be spent in one of the reserves or reserves. There are also entertainment in the village for lovers of active pastime: an amusement park and a mini water park. Also on the beach you can rent "bananas", scooters.



Housing selection

The largest selection of housing options in 2016 in this urban-type settlement is represented by the private sector. Here you can find rooms of the class "Economy" and comfortable rooms "Luxury" in villas on the coast of the sea. When choosing housing, you should pay attention to the fact that Komsomolskaya and Shkolnaya streets are closest to the beach strip. Indeed, it is the right choice of place of residence that will provide a good rest in Sedovo, the prices for which can be found below.

"At Uncle Fedi"

One of the popular options for rental housing in Sedovo are private mini-pensions. Living conditions in these institutions are no worse than in well-known recreation centers, and prices are much lower. Such an example would be a private boarding house "Uncle Fedi", located at: st. Nardekova, 15. The hotel offers comfortable rooms with amenities that will ensure a good stay in Sedovo. Pensions here, including Uncle Fedi, if they are far from the public beach, they always have several pools on their territory, you can also rent a sun lounger. The rooms have all the necessary amenities, air conditioning, refrigerator. A triple room in this hotel will cost 220 UAH (560 rubles)., And a 2-room suite - 400 UAH (1027 rubles).



"Quiet corner"

For lovers of a relaxing holiday there is a worthy option - a mini-hotel "Quiet Area", which is located at: Komsomolskaya, 30A. The beach is a 10-minute walk away. Near the Quiet Corner there is a market and shops. The hotel provides junior suites (200 UAH or 513 rubles) and a suite (300 UAH or 770 rubles), which are equipped with new furniture. Also in the rooms are TV, refrigerator, shower, air conditioning. Another plus of the pension is a free Wi-fi zone. The last criterion will clearly enrich the vacation in Sedovo, if not all, then every second tourist.

"Anniversary"

For those who do not like to go far to the beaches and cook on vacation on their own, rooms in coastal pensions and recreation centers are more suitable. One of the most famous in Sedovo is Yubileiny. Its main plus is its own beach, equipped with sun loungers and awnings, which is located just 20 meters from the building. On the territory of the institution there are cafes, there are 2 swimming pools. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, shower and toilet. The pension also provides vacationers with round-the-clock meals. Room prices in the "Jubilee" range from 300 UAH. up to 550 UAH (770 - 1283 rubles). The majority who came to rest in Sedovo choose this complex.



"Crystal"

Also popular for vacationers is the boarding house "Crystal", located 50 meters from the sea. The complex offers rooms "Suite" and "Standard", each of which has a shower, toilet and electric kettle.

"Metallurgist"

A profitable option for a vacation in 2016 is also the Metallurg boarding house, which is located in the first line from the sea. The complex offers 2 swimming pools, a game town, a dining room, a bar, a parking lot and 2 cinemas. The pension has a huge equipped sandy beach, which guarantees an excellent holiday in Sedovo. The cost of one day of hotel accommodation is from 270 UAH (693 rubles) to 370 UAH / person. (950 rub.) Depending on the season.



Helios

Luxurious rooms equipped according to European requirements are offered by Hotel Helios, which has access to a huge private beach equipped with sun loungers and awnings. Helios provides vacationers with three meals a day. The rooms have everything you need: separate toilet, shower, fridge, TV. On the beach of the complex there is a cafe-bar, a net for beach volleyball, tennis tables. Prices for a hotel room from 110 UAH (282 rubles).



Thus, the resort village in 2016 provides a huge selection of accommodation for tourists and is looking forward to vacationers! It is better for average families to choose boarding houses and hotels that will definitely provide a good rest. In Sedovo, the private sector is suitable only for wealthy people, since the cost of rooms and houses is too high.
