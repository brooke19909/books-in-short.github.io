LCD "Kantemirovsky": reviews, infrastructure, construction progress, deadline

By the beginning of 2017, at the intersection of Marshal Blucher Ave and Kuleshovskaya Road, the last house of the planned eight brick-monolithic high-rise buildings, designed for 4,500 apartments from economy class to luxury, was commissioned. It ends with the construction of the residential complex "Kantemirovsky." We will provide reviews about it below.

Developer

The CDS group of companies (CC CDS) has been on the real estate market of the Leningrad Region for more than eighteen years. She carries out construction work on a full cycle. Today, the company is among the top five developers in the region in terms of rental housing. Projects developed by the organization are in different market segments: from economy class apartments to luxury residential buildings and commercial buildings.

The main mission of the developer is affordable housing. It was this company that suggested the concept of full-fledged apartments of small footage, which would satisfy a buyer with modest income. Almost all the residential complexes of the CC CDS have decent apartments at a low price with a mandatory balcony and a kitchen of 8 m 2 .

In addition, the developer was the first in the region to switch from sealing to integrated construction, which makes it possible to create a comfortable living environment in the complex being built. Vivid examples of such buildings - LCD "Pargolovo", "Murino" and "Kudrino". The company is proud of each of its forty projects with a total area of ‚Äã‚Äãmore than 3.5 million m 2 .





The CDS group of companies collaborates with Russian Capital Bank, VTB Bank of Moscow, Absolut Bank, Bank Vozrozhdenie, VTB 24, Sberbank, Promsvyazbank, Binbank, Otkrytie FC, Russian Agricultural Bank and others.

The company, even in difficult times for the country's economy, met the deadlines, and the Kantemirovsky residential complex is no exception.

The quarter being built by the CDS Group of Companies in the Kalininsky District is part of a large-scale project to form a new residential space. Characteristics of a new building

The walls of the houses are made of monolithic concrete, and the partitions and external cladding are made of brick. Houses built in this way do not settle. In addition, they are fireproof and durable.

The developer offers one-, two- and three-room apartments, including judges. Odnushki vary in the range of 25-43 m 2 , two-room - from 50 to 61 m 2 , and three-ruble - a maximum of 79 m 2 . Their layouts in the residential complex "Kantemirovsky" are presented in the article. The design solutions of the apartments are made in a classic rectangular style, but there are also options with a beveled wall. Three-room apartments have an elongated loggia connecting two rooms and an additional bathroom.

Each living room is equipped with innovative double-glazed windows, metal entrance doors, water purification filters, intercom with the function of communication with the security guard.

The developer pays special attention to the security of the complex. The system of which includes access control to the territory of Kantemirovsky. Each tenant is given an individual key to enter. Metal doors were installed in the front doors, alarm, intercom and communication with the concierge were made.

Promotions and discounts

On all apartments of the residential complex "Kantemirovsky", reviews confirm this, the action applies. Buyers are provided with interest-free installments for three years.

Each of the partner banks offers a mortgage. The three leaders are VTB 24, Alfa Bank and Sberbank. The latter has a limit of 8 million rubles, a rate of 0.8%, and a down payment of 15% of the price of the apartment. Alfa-Bank offers a mortgage without a down payment of up to 60 million rubles. And VTB 24 - up to 30 million rubles, at 6.2% and with a down payment of 10% of the cost of housing.

Individually, with each buyer, options are discussed with the use of maternity capital, a housing subsidy for military personnel, or targeted city programs.

Plus, the developer offers discounts for 100% payment of a one-room apartment - 20%, two-and three-room apartments - 15%. If the housing is bought by installments, then with a down payment of 30% for three years, interest-free installments (without discounts) are provided, with a down payment of 40% - the same installments, but with a discount of 10% from the down payment.

Information for new settlers

To design an apartment in the residential complex "Kantemirovsky", you must contact any office of the MFC of the city. To register the right to acquired property on the basis of a share contribution agreement, the following documents must be submitted (two originals):

  • share contribution agreement;
  • the act of acceptance of the apartment;
  • additional agreements (if any);
  • certificate of payment of the share fee;
  • acceptance certificate (attached to the investment contract);
  • state duty receipt;
  • power of attorney (if necessary);
  • two certificates of full repayment of a mortgage and a loan agreement (in the case of full repayment of a mortgage loan).

If the right to acquired property is registered on the basis of an equity participation agreement (DDU), then the following package of documents is required:

  • the original DDU with a stamp on registration at the Rosreestr (one copy);
  • originals of supplementary agreements (if any);
  • Originals of the Act on the acceptance of the apartment (two copies);
  • power of attorney (if necessary);
  • state duty receipt.

Post scriptum

New settlers are extremely active online. A lot of reviews on the official portal of the developer and other sites. Most of the residents talk about the repair, which was quick and easy thanks to the quality work of the construction crews.

Acceptance of apartments was organized at a high level. Payment of all bills passed through the application, which saved a lot of time.

All doors, windows and various technical units are operational. Many newcomers separately thank the developer for an even floor screed.

Infrastructure in the residential complex "Kantemirovsky" is just getting better. But construction is proceeding at such a pace that the residents of the new quarter are confident that the promised school, kindergarten, clinic, shops and shopping centers will open wide soon.

Pleases the owners of apartments, even the smallest, the presence of a balcony. An undoubted plus is the location of the complex, of course, this is not the city center, but also far from the outskirts.

Slips in customer reviews and the theme of the cost of apartments. Many people think that in Kantemirovsky the prices are very attractive, especially in comparison with the nearby complexes.

A significant part of the new settlers noted in their reviews the reliability of the builder. Some do not like the one-sidedness of two-room apartments of 52 m2.

There are also negative points. The transport situation is considered by residents of the complex rather complicated. The nearest stop is almost half a kilometer away. Any metro station is 1.5 km away. And this, according to residents, is 25 minutes in an average step. And, by the way, there is no metro station near this residential quarter, even in the long-term plans.

The streets adjacent to the LCD are almost always in traffic jams, especially the Kushelevskaya road and Marshal Blucher ave. Departure from Kantemirovsky is possible only on one side of the Kushelevskaya road going north. That is, those who need to go in the opposite direction will need to overcome the entire overpass for a U-turn. The situation will not be resolved until the city authorities extend beyond Bestuzhevskaya Street, which should pass along the northern border of the quarter. But the timing of these works is unknown. It is also unclear when the work to create an intra-quarter road network, sidewalks and bicycle paths will be completed.

Judging by the reviews of the owners of the apartments, the developer in the brochures and on the website does not warn about the proximity of the concrete plant. It is also unknown to buyers that the territory with a gas station, garages and a snow melting point adjoins the residential complex on the north side. If you look at the map farther north, you can see the railway with the Theological Cemetery located behind it.

Also, few people know about future significant expenses before buying. In addition to the surcharge for the increased area (this is prescribed in the contract), no one warns that, for example, to spend electricity in a one-room apartment costs about 12 thousand rubles, in a two-room apartment - 15 thousand. For a car place, you need to pay 500 thousand rubles, and then a thousand every month.

Another topic of reviews is the conditions of purchase. To purchase housing, you must join a housing cooperative. The contract, signed at the same time, does not make it possible to make claims in case of non-compliance with the terms of settlement, transfer of keys or delivery of the object. The share fee is equal to the cost of the purchased apartment. Plus to this (it is spelled out in the contract), an entry fee (10 thousand rubles) is necessary, which, by the way, according to clause 5.5 of the contract is not returned under any conditions. The amount of the target contribution varies from 11 800 to 22 400 rubles. If a shareholder, for his reasons, terminates the contract, then the unit contribution returned to him is reduced by 15%.
