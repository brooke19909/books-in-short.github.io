Documents for the purchase of OSAGO

The acquisition of a policy in the Renaissance does not differ from the execution of insurance in other organizations. To buy or extend the CTP policy in the Renaissance, you need:

  1. Passport.
  2. Driver's license.
  3. Diagnostic card or coupon confirming passing inspection. The condition is relevant for vehicles whose service life has exceeded 3 years.
  4. Certificate of registration cars or passport, if the car is not yet registered.
  5. Statement. When registering at the office, the client fills out together with the insurance agent, and on an online purchase - on their own.

If the insurance in the Renaissance is drawn up for several drivers (an unlimited policy), everyone should bring a passport and driver's license with them.
