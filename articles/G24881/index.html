DIY gift box in a few steps

A gift is, of course, good. But it will be even better if you hand it in the form of a surprise, and for this you need to successfully hide it. You can simply wrap it with gift paper, or you can make a beautiful box and put a present in it. So you will make his presentation more mysterious, because a person will not immediately understand what is inside. DIY gift box is a great option. It is not difficult to make it, but in it you put your soul and mood, decorating as you see fit.

Before you start, select the color of paper for design. Gift packaging made of cardboard is a more boring option, but if you are going to make it from this material, you will have to come up with a colorful design for it in the future. Or at least paste over with bright paper. But first things first.

DIY gift box: step by step instructions

  1. So, take cardboard or paper. If you start with cardboard, take one that is not very thick and can bend well. And if the paper is not very thin, since the box from it will turn out to be very fragile.
  2. From paper you need to cut a square of the required size. Fold it in the middle as shown in the image. As a result, you should have two lines intersecting in the center at an angle of 90 degrees.
  3. The next step is to fold the corners to the middle.
  4. Now we add two opposite midpoints so that they touch each other exactly in the center.
  5. We do the same with two other sides on top of the already folded ones.
  6. We unbend everything and return to the first point.
  7. We turn two opposite sides.
  8. Then we fold the corner between adjacent sides as shown in the figure.
  9. We bend the sides inward to form the walls of the box.
  10. We fix them with glue so that the structure does not fall apart.

Our DIY gift box is almost ready. It remains only to make her a cover. Please note that it should be slightly larger in size of the bottom. Or less if the lid goes inside the box. To do this, you can cut out two squares in advance, and one will be smaller than the other. From the smaller we make the bottom of the box, and from the larger - the lid (or vice versa).



The main stage can be considered finished, but if the gift box you created looks somehow boring and uninteresting, come up with an interesting design for it. For example, on the cover you can create a composition of flowers, stick ribbons, beads, buttons, write something. The more original the box is designed, the more it will intrigue the one in whose hands it will soon be. She will want to look into it as soon as possible in order to find out what is hiding in her.

In this way, a DIY gift box can be made in this way . Its size can be any. If you are going to give a piece of jewelry, you can limit yourself to a small box, inside which you need to put something soft and, preferably, decorated with a cloth. Well, for a larger gift, you will have to make a more capacious box, also thinking about how to make it festive.
