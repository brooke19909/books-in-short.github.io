Noginsk: Hotels, motels and hostels

Noginsk is a regional center in the Moscow region, located 35 kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road in the eastern direction from Moscow. The city has developed industrial production, military-industrial enterprises, machine-building, food, furniture and light industry enterprises are operating, the service sector and trade, culture and sports are actively developing here.

What attracts guests to Noginsk?

The city has a favorable location - it is located near Moscow, good transport links with the capital and other settlements of the Moscow region are organized here. In summer, the city has a lot of green spaces and flower beds. The Epiphany Cathedral, the building of the girls' gymnasium, the shopping arcade, the modern complex of fountains are far from all the sights that Noginsk is rich in. Hotels, of which there are a sufficient number, welcome guests and provide decent living conditions. Here everyone will find a hotel that meets financial capabilities and wishes.



Noginsk Economy Hotels

In the category of budget hotels there is a good choice. Business travelers who come to Noginsk choose hotels based on their financial capabilities, and often prefer budget accommodation options.

Hotel "Girus" offers guests paid parking for trucks and cars. The rooms here are equipped with everything for a comfortable overnight stay, each of them has bathrooms with a shower, a TV and a refrigerator. Possible settlement from one to four people. Regular customers are offered discounts for services.

The Sportivnaya Baza Hotel provides budget rooms with bedding, towels. The rooms have washbasins, a wardrobe with hangers for clothes. Toilets and shared showers are located on the floor. Here are waiting for guests without children and animals.

Noginsk: Hotels of a middle price category

For lovers of modern goods, there are hotels offering a wider range of services. The average price for accommodation is typical for most hotels. So, the average cost per night varies between 700-2000 rubles per person. Affordable prices - this is one of the main reasons why tourists choose Noginsk. Hotels of this category are in demand by guests of the city. High ratings and reviews of former guests become decisive in choosing a hotel.

Tourists who have arrived in Noginsk choose hotels closer to the city center - this is the Ekotel Bogorodsk Hotel and the Gusi-Swan Wellness Center. Here, accommodation is offered in comfortable rooms with the necessary appliances and comfortable furniture. Own bathrooms have high-quality modern sanitary ware, guests are offered hygiene products and accessories.





Ecotel Bogorodsk offers a 24-hour restaurant, swimming pool, bowling alley and spa services. The hotel is suitable for both corporate and family vacations, it is also chosen as a place to sleep on a business trip.

Hotel "Geese-Swans" positions itself as a bathing resort. Guests have a great time in the steam room, here you can do massage, visit the restaurant. There is a rental of sports equipment.

Premium Hotel in Noginsk

Not far from Noginsk, next to Lake Kovershi, there is a premium Yakhont hotel. Guests will find spacious rooms with a refined decor and all the attributes that ensure their comfort.



A restaurant, a karaoke bar, summer cafes, a spa, a swimming pool, various types of massage, horseback riding, paintball and bowling are an incomplete list of entertainment that guests can choose at their discretion. It is possible to stay with families with children, corporate clients and guests traveling alone.
