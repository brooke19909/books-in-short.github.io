Options for financing a startup in Russia: features, stages and conditions

The development of various industries makes it possible to increase the volume of products, as well as improve its quality. This is only one of the profitable areas in the implementation of new technologies in business. Each industry seeks to improve or modernize its functionality. Such types of optimization bring financial success to any project. More and more young people, having a sufficient level of knowledge, are trying to develop one of the priority areas in business. This may be information technology, programming, and more.

Startup Challenges

Thanks to such innovations, many companies with branches around the world are developing. However, at the initial stage, such initiative people are not always noticed. This leads to the fact that a business idea, which has a positive economic effect, is lost, without having received a real implementation. It is all about financing the project. Not everyone can raise the right amount to launch their own startup. Therefore, you have to look for opportunities on the side, attracting investors, sponsors, investors.

Financing a startup in our country is a complex and multi-stage process. Old-school businessmen are not yet fully open to modern technology and information innovation. Therefore, they do not always meet the suggestions of young and novice entrepreneurs. Another snag is the legislative regulation of all business activities. If the system of startups in the West has been developing for more than 10 years, then in the territory of the post-Soviet space it only has to go through all the stages in order to realize the model of development and financing of every really worthwhile idea.

What is a startup

If you do not delve into the economy, a startup is actually a start-up company that has a good idea for development and can make a profit in the future. However, the nuance of such an organization is that it does not have adequate funding for the full implementation of its project. This is how young firms created by novice businessmen perceive in Europe and America.

Financing a startup is a complex and phased process. The problem is the individuality of each solution. Depending on the direction of the idea and the business model, investing in it can be full or partial. Not so rare cases when several investors become willing to become participants and be in share. In addition, it is necessary to draw up an accurate economic model, which allows you to calculate all the possible risks when investing and the stages when the cost part goes to zero.

Russian startups are almost no different from European ones. The only difference is that domestic start-up projects appeared relatively late, which allows you to create companies based on the experience of Western colleagues. In reality, each system, although it has common roots, has great differences due to financial and legislative regulation, as well as the mentality of entrepreneurs themselves.

Innovative technologies in business development

Startup financing is especially active in new and less competitive industries. The constant modernization of the digital space leads to the fact that new ideas appear in the synthesis with information technology and science. Thanks to this combination, own software developments appear more and more on the domestic market.

Computerization allows you to develop a business and raise profitability to a new level. It is this industry in startups that is most attractive for investment. Due to the large number of different types of operating enterprises in the country, it is possible to improve the quality of their work by introducing modern systems and automating many work processes.

Phased development

Startup financing is a multifaceted process. Here it is necessary to rely not only on future economic indicators, but also on the period of relevance of the idea in its implementation. Often, already at the stage of financing and launching a project, it loses its liquidity, so it is necessary to calculate a similar nuance at the planning stage.

Each stage of the project has its own purpose. Simple and less costly is the creation of a company and the design of the very future object for investment. It was during this period that a team of young entrepreneurs created an exhibition model of the investment model for potential investors and those who would later want to become a participant in the development of this concept.

Types of investment in the project

Financing startups in Russia has its own characteristics, like any other investment industry. Young business is not without advantages and disadvantages. All investment options are divided by the amount of costs. For example, in order to receive an amount of $ 1 million (approximately 67 million rubles) for the development of a project, you need to turn to business angel communities.

These are successful entrepreneurs whose main task is to invest their own funds in various projects with the goal of further earnings on the development of new production models and the information business. Such an action will provide an opportunity to develop a project to a certain stage. For further promotion, larger amounts may be required, which can be obtained from banks or venture funds.

Financing technology

Financing start-ups in Russia needs to be protected by civil law. This type of investment refers to a certain type of transaction. Thanks to this, it becomes possible to legally regulate and vest each party with rights and obligations. The main type of investing in startups is fan.

The conditions for financing a startup of this type are that entrepreneurs invest not in a project, but in a business idea. Moreover, the amount of investment is small and calculated on the basis of potential future profits. Another form of investment is deferred. It resembles a framework agreement in the type of action, when an amount of money already transferred from an investor is already allocated for each stage of business development.

Investor selection

Financing start-up projects is complicated by the fact that it is necessary to deal independently with the search for investors. In Russia, online platforms are not well developed yet, where entrepreneurs with free funds select various start-up sites for investing in them. The founders of the young company themselves, based on the necessary amount, should look for those who would invest in it.

Venture capital funds are one of the most frequent models of investing in young but profitable projects. However, they set a number of serious requirements when concluding a contract. Therefore, it is important to assess all the risks and understand whether it will be profitable to cooperate with such structural units in the future, when the company begins to make a net profit.

Promotion through exchanges and the Internet

Startup funding sources can also be found over the Internet. This is a more complex process, however, many successful young companies received their first sources of funds through online platforms. There are whole forums and exchanges that are created for communication between investors and those who are looking for sources of financing.

However, each online platform also has a number of its requirements. It is worthwhile to familiarize yourself with the concept of patents and copyright in order not to become a victim of scammers who promise to invest after reading internal information or the process. Usually, after the transfer of data, such citizens or companies simply disappear.

Granting

Initial funding for startups can also be funded from the state or regional budget. Large domestic corporations are ready to become investors for many young and ambitious projects. All that is needed is to find the appropriate event and register according to the specified rules.

The presentation of the idea may vary. Therefore, it is worthwhile to familiarize yourself with all the requirements. Grants can reach up to 100 million rubles. However, only research institutes that can seriously approach the creation and modeling of a project can count on such funding.

Development loan

How to find financing for a startup if entrepreneurs refuse to invest in it? This is a difficult question. However, banks, noting the activity associated with the development of entrepreneurship among the young population of the country, began to develop suitable financing programs for them. Large institutions form extended credit limits for business development. All that is needed is to submit a properly executed project.

Funding amounts may vary. It all depends on the evaluation of the idea presented for consideration. It is important to send to the bank not only the model of future technology itself, but also conduct a small study aimed at assessing all possible risks. A business plan will also be useful, which indicates all items of expenditure, as well as the timing of the first profit and exit of the business from net loss.

Responsibility of the Parties

How to spend startup financing and develop a project? Practice around the world shows that not always young entrepreneurs who have received large funds for the development of their successful project tend to immediately get down to business. There are many high-profile cases where startups closed and their founders disappeared. As a result, they simply stole money and left for other countries, taking advantage of legislative flaws.

However, today the financing model due to such incidents has largely undergone changes in a positive direction. Each of the parties to the transaction has new rights and responsibilities. In case of misuse of funds, startup participants can be brought to court. It is also noted that after several such cases, when unfortunate entrepreneurs received real terms for fraud, the rest became more accurate in spending the allocated funds.

What to look for

An investor with full funding for a startup is a rather rare element of a young project. But there are exceptions. Having formed your own project, you need to find a niche for its implementation. Often successful are cases when young specialists create a separate company to develop an innovative solution for a specific application in another organization. This allows you to find the target audience, where the technology will be needed in the future.

When the concept is formed, you can send out proposals to participate in the project to all potential consumers of this technology. This will not only attract attention to the project, but also create competition. According to the rule of a market economy, demand always creates supply.
