How does the Samsung microwave work?

The Samsung microwave oven by the principle of its functioning does not differ from other devices of this type. Its design includes a magnetron, which generates microwave waves acting on water in food. All water molecules on Earth tend to orient themselves on the magnetic field of the planet, and microwaves in the furnace tend to deflect them from this state.
