Additional booking information

This is the last thing I would like to mention. So, check-in is from 14:00 to 19:00, people can check out from six in the morning until noon. And travelers who want to go on vacation with their children need to know that for such purposes they will have to find another option for the hotel, since the administration of the complex does not allow settling with minors.

You also need to be prepared for the fact that the administration of the complex can pre-authorize the credit card of potential guests within a minimum time after booking. And by the way, when registering on the spot at the hotel, potential guests are required to present the credit card that was used when booking. If it suddenly happened that she stayed at home, you will need to pay the entire cost of the stay in hotel in cash.

And the last: guests cannot bring drinks and food bought in other places to the hotel grounds. But this can not even be considered a drawback. Otherwise, the hotel is excellent, and thousands of tourists have already confirmed this.
