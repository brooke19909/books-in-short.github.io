For a long time, the Kurlevsky quarry became one of the favorite places for vacationers. The reviews of everyone who visited there indicate unsurpassed natural beauties, such as azure-colored water , snow-white sand, sunsets and sunrises that cannot be described.

Location

Kurlevskie quarry is not a natural lake, as some tourists believe. They were formed as a result of the extraction of powdered lime located there, which is known as drywall. The quarries left after excavation were filled with underground waters, and now they have become a great place for recreation and fishing. By the way, it is these soil streams that are the source of the Oredezh River.



Currently, this place belongs to protected natural areas located in the Leningrad region. And it is located in the Volosovsky district, about 80 km from St. Petersburg. If you have never heard of a reservoir under the name of K√ºrlevsky quarry, this does not mean that you do not know anything about this place, perhaps you know it under a different name - Lake Donzo. This water body belongs to the Russian part of the Narva river basin of the Baltic region. The pond area is small and is only 0.7 km 2 .

Road to quarries

If you are planning to go to Lake Donzo, it would be nice at first to find out what to expect from those places and whether it is worth spending money and time on the road. After all, if you live in St. Petersburg, then you will have to travel long enough to get into the Kurlevsky quarry. "How to get to this place?" - One of the most common questions for those who have not yet been to this lake. It is immediately worth noting that you can only get to it by road. It is necessary to move from St. Petersburg along the highway, which goes in a south-westerly direction.



Having passed the settlement of Gatchina, you need to begin to closely monitor the road. Immediately after this city you can turn towards Elizavetino. Having passed the specified settlement, you need to start searching for a place called Fifth Mountain. But there is another way, through the Kiev highway. On it, travelers reach the village of Vyra and turn towards Zamostya to the right. Having passed it, you can start to look, where there will be a turn to the pond of the Kurlevsky quarry. How to get there, every motorist will be able to figure out the road signs and prompts of local residents, but it is better, of course, to take a navigator on the road. The turn to the pond is right behind the bridge, which is thrown across the Oredezh River.

Description of places



Many outdoor enthusiasts who have not yet visited the quarries refuse to go to Lake Donets, because they do not reach it for a relatively long time, and they don‚Äôt know what to expect from this place. But according to the reviews of travelers who have been there, the Kurlevsky Quarry Pond is an amazing natural reserve. Absolutely clear water, a beach with white shores, an abundance of greenery on the shore, boulders of interesting shapes and various sizes attract the eyes of even experienced vacationers.

The depth of the pond is small, it does not exceed 4 meters. This is one of the main reasons why fishermen love the K√ºrlevsky quarry so much. Their reviews suggest that it is precisely the shallow depth, combined with clear water and rich vegetation at the bottom, that makes catching the local inhabitants amazing.

Travelers opinion

Is it worth it to talk about all the advantages of this conservation area, if everyone who has visited here wants to return? Ordinary vacationers do not get tired of admiring nature, not only from the shore, but also from the middle of the pond, swimming there on an air mattress. At the same time, they have a magnificent view of the rich underwater world of this place.



But the fishermen separately celebrate the upper reaches of the Oredezh River, because this is the only place where the local trout is found. Fans of avifauna are delighted with the arriving birds, among which there are river ducks, gulls, field moons, quail, nuthatch, kingfishers and many others. Swimming in the lake

Some vacationers say that the water in the pond is cool enough, which is natural for these northern latitudes. Others, on the contrary, argue that, due to its shallow depth, the pond warms up well on fine days. In favor of the latter is also evidenced by the fact that this is the place chosen by many families with children who are actively splashing in the lake. Also, many vacationers talk not only about the transparency of water, but also about a flat day, without silt deposits. True, at times the level of the pond rises, and to enter the reservoir you need to go not along the pleasant sand, but along the clay. Its particles rise and the water becomes cloudy in a matter of seconds. But they settle just as quickly.

Pond fishing



The K√ºrlevsky quarry is especially popular among lovers of catching aquatic inhabitants. Every fisherman can hope to catch perch, roach or pike. True, fishing on the lake has its own characteristics. Transparent water allows not only the fisherman to see the future catch, but also aquatic inhabitants can notice the hunter and leave. That is why experienced people try to throw spinning rods away. This allows you to go unnoticed on a pond with clear water called the K√ºrlevsky quarry. Fishing, as a rule, is not bad here, but finding large individuals is quite difficult.

All vacationers say that at any time of the year locals put nets on the pond. But visitors go for live bait. At the same time, they should be prepared that the weight of local representatives of the underwater world is on average within 300 grams. Roach, according to experienced fishermen, pecks well at maggots. The main thing is to choose the right place for fishing.

Underwater life
