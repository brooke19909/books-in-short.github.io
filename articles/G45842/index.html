Hotpoint-Ariston - Customer Reviews

Hotpoint-Ariston, whose reviews indicate its widespread popularity in the home appliance market, was born as a family business. The Merloni family from sunny Italy became famous all over the world not only for their excellent knowledge of the basics of the art of marketing and economic science, but also for understanding the preferences of customers.



The level of trust in Ariston products is high. Often, buyers acquire household appliances, only on the basis of their favorite design and individual functionality.





The founder of the company is Aristide Merloni, who in 1930 opened a small enterprise in the town of Fabriano. Initially, the company produced scales, which were sold mainly in the regional market. However, it soon became apparent that the company's product was not at all capable of generating revenue. The search for a range of products began, the sale of which could occupy a free niche in the consumer market. Fifteen years after its founding, the company began to produce electric water heaters and cylinders designed for liquefied gas. The son of Aristide Merloni - Vittorio - took over as president of the company in nineteen seventy-fifth. Beginning in 1989, after certain financial and marketing manipulations, Ariston merged with large European household appliance companies (Indesit, Hotpoint, etc.).

Since November two thousand and seven, the Hotpoint-Ariston brand has been finally formed, reviews of household appliances which confirm the presence of a powerful potential of British quality and Italian traditions. This brand has a wide range of diverse appliances needed in every modern home. The company produces refrigerators and washing machines, microwave ovens, dishwashers, etc.

Hotpoint-Ariston appliances, reviews of which speak of its maximum comfort and ease of use, are designed in such a way as to satisfy all customer requests. Hot Point Ariston company invests heavily, attracting the most qualified specialists from around the world to develop their new models. For example, the bompato design, which features smooth contours and rounded corners, was proposed by an architect from Japan. Currently, it has already become traditional.

The company's innovations include the use of a special Dialogic electronics system in the refrigerators it produces. Under the complete control of this device is the storage process of chilled products. Hotpoint-Ariston refrigerators, reviews of which satisfy the needs of the most demanding customers, are truly modern.
