MFP Canon Pixma MG2440: reviews, prices

Canon is one of the world leaders in the segment of the production of inkjet printers, as well as multifunction devices, which include an appropriate printing module. In Russia, the Canon Pixma MG2440 MFP model has gained tangible popularity (in particular, in one of the most common modifications - 8328b007). What is the specifics of this device?





Specifications

Specifications is the first aspect that we can start exploring the Canon Pixma MG2440 multifunction device . Reviews about him, due to its popularity, are present on a large number of thematic online portals.

The device belongs to the MFP class, that is, it combines the functions of a printer, scanner and copier. The paper size the device is designed for is A4. The corresponding tray can hold up to 60 sheets. The device uses inkjet printing technology using color cartridges. The maximum print resolution that the device provides is 4,800 by 600 dots. The speed of sheet processing in monochrome mode - 8 pages per minute, in color - 4. Supported print function without the use of margins. The device is compatible with photo paper. The type of connection to the computer is USB. The width of the device is 42.6 cm, the depth is 30.6 cm, the height is 14.6 cm.

Technology

Among the notable technologies implemented in the Canon Pixma MG2440 printer (the reviews of many users and specialists characterize the corresponding feature very positively), FINE, which allows the device to print photos and color images in the highest quality. More precisely, this technology is implemented specifically in the print head of the MFP.





In combination with the high resolution of the corresponding module - up to 4800 points, the FINE concept, as many experts say, really involves creating high-quality photos on the printer. It can be noted that for this MFP, a separate series of cartridges - XL FINE, characterized primarily by cost-effectiveness, has been released. So, according to some reports, they are approximately 30% more effective than standard printing components.

Software

Among the useful resources that become available to the user who has purchased the Canon MFP under consideration are specialized programs that come with the device. So, many customer reviews of the Canon Pixma MG2440 contain positive ratings from the My Image Garden program. This type of software has a wide range of capabilities for organizing and managing photographs. The program also allows you to use the MFP scanning modules and access various online resources.

The Auto Clip feature, which is performed by the Easy-WebPring EX extension, installed on Internet Explorer, is noteworthy. With it, the user can find the desired multimedia files on the Internet and print them. This program also allows you to combine the contents of several web pages on one sheet with subsequent printing or saving as a document in PDF format.

The owner of this Canon MFP is also available with the Creative Park Premium cloud service. It allows you to work with photos, create cards or, for example, calendars.





Scan Module Specifications

Let us now examine some of the parameters that characterize the Canon Pixma MG2440 scan module. The reviews of many users indicate that it is used no less intensively than the print unit. The type of scanner installed in the corresponding module is a flatbed one. The maximum resolution of the device in terms of the corresponding function is 600 by 1200 pixels. A color depth of 40 bits is provided.

Work in Xerox mode

The photocopy function, in turn, involves the use of both of the above modules - print and scan, which are equipped with a Canon Pixma MG2440 MFP. Reviews of digital enthusiasts who used the device, of course, also include the opinions of customers regarding the operation of the device in Xerox mode.





The capabilities of the corresponding module are such that it is difficult to compare it with a copy machine, if only because the printing technology in this case is inkjet, and the scanner of this MFP is not adapted by default for quick photocopying. Therefore, indicators reflecting the performance of the device when working in Xerox mode are very modest. The device processes about 1-2 pages per minute. However, as some reviews of the Canon Pixma MG2440 testify, the Xerox function is very necessary at a particular point in time, and even such indicators will be acceptable.
