Koralli Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece / Peloponnese): hotel description, services, reviews

Everything necessary for a comfortable Greek holiday is at the Koralli Beach Hotel 3 *. Despite the rather modest and even ascetic atmosphere, you can fully relax here.





Location

Koralli Beach Hotel is about 3 km from Niforeika . Peloponnese, Paralia, 25200 - the address of the resort. It is 10 km from the local airport Araxos. After a little over 25 km, you can visit such popular cities as Patra and Rio Antiriu. Killini seaport is located 50 km from the property.





What to visit

If you want to get to know the local attractions, one of the most convenient accommodation options will be Koralli Beach Hotel. Greece is rich in tourist sites. The closest to this institution are the following:

  • Alikes Beach (3 km);
  • Eleftheria Monument (4 km);
  • Kato Achaya Square (5 km);
  • Dimean Wall (16 km);
  • Kalogria Chapel (17 km).

Description of rooms

The category that corresponds to all rooms of the hotel Koralli Beach 3 * - Standard . At the same time, double, triple and quadruple accommodation is offered. Speaking about the amenities that guests can use in their apartments, it is worth noting compliance with modern standards. So, in the rooms of Koralli Beach Hotel 3 *, guests can use the following options:

  • single orthopedic beds;
  • individual air conditioning;
  • satellite television;
  • small fridge;
  • bathroom with shower;
  • own balcony with plastic furniture;
  • An electric kettle and hairdryer are available upon request.




Infrastructure

Despite the fact that Koralli Beach 3 * is a budget mini-establishment, there is everything you need for a comfortable stay and a good rest. The hotel infrastructure is as follows:

  • free car parking;
  • wireless internet in public areas;
  • a bar located on the terrace with sea views;
  • a tavern where guests can enjoy delicious food;
  • public pebble beach 50 m from the hotel;
  • terrace with sunbeds and sun umbrellas;
  • small garden;
  • a night club where every evening you can enjoy music from DJs;
  • playground for children in the open air;
  • reception desk, which works on a specific schedule.




Services

Hotel Koralli Beach will delight tourists with quality service. The following services are available to guests:

  • good nutrition;
  • daily quality housekeeping;
  • the formation of an individual menu (according to personal preferences or for medical reasons);
  • packed lunches for guests on an excursion, as well as for those who leave the hotel early.

Helpful information

Before going to Koralli Beach 3 *, you should familiarize yourself with a number of useful information about the rules of this institution. So, it is recommended to pay attention to such information:

  • Procedures related to the registration of newly arrived guests are carried out in the period from 14:00 to 22:00, and eviction - from 09:00 to noon;
  • children under 2 years stay free in the room with cots;
  • Children under 8 years stay free of charge when using existing beds.
  • pets are allowed upon prior request (this service is free);
  • upon check in, a cash deposit is charged;
  • Guests are required to present a valid ID with a current photo;
  • self-registration is possible from 16 years old;
  • Only cash is accepted;
  • The cost of breakfast is 5 euros.

General impression

This institution would be wrong to call a hotel. It is rather a guest house, which is designed for 9 rooms. Given the budget prices, guests should not expect an attractive interior and hope for quality service. Everything here is quite ascetic and more like a camp site. Furniture and repairs are so old that being in the room is extremely unpleasant. The food is too modest, but there are no alternative options nearby. The only positive thing is the proximity to the sea, which provides a beautiful panoramic view. But here there is a negative side, because during a storm your entire room may be flooded. And if you want to travel around the neighborhood, then you have to decently spend money on transport, because this area is quite remote.
