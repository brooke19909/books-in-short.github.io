Hotel TT Hotels Pegasos Royal 5 * (Turkey, Alanya): reviews

Determining the desired place for a long-awaited vacation or weekend, travelers often choose hot Turkey with its many resorts on the Mediterranean Sea, among which Alanya is quite common.

Advantages of Alanya

Rocky capes, sandy coast, shallow sea, numerous bays and affordable prices - all this attracts thousands of vacationers every year. This village is suitable for families.

A cozy place welcomes guests year-round. However, autumn and spring are most popular when the water temperature is significant and the weather is already less sultry. Here you can not only buy up, spend time diversely thanks to the developed infrastructure, but also expand your horizons and knowledge by visiting attractions accompanied by Russian-speaking guides.

The five-star TT Hotels Pegasos Royal 5 * in Turkey is a vivid and popular example of the hotel industry, combining high-quality service, comfortable accommodation, which is ensured through the concept of All Inclusive.





general characteristics

TT Hotels Pegasos Royal reviews are mostly positive. They not only come here to relax once, but also come back annually again. What has earned the hotel complex such attention? This we will discuss in the article.

The complex was built in 2004, the last renovation took place in 2013. Its location in an ecologically clean area, dotted with greenery and exotic palm trees, appeals to every guest, regardless of age. This is confirmed by numerous reviews from visitors. The total area is 31 thousand square meters. Tourists are allowed to use the amenities of the nearby TT Hotels Pegasos Club and TT Hotels Pegasos Resort.

Antalya International Airport is 135 km away. After the flight, the road to the hotel takes about two hours by bus. The main and only building is represented by a five-story building equipped with elevators.

Settlement Features

Pets are not allowed, smoking in rooms and in public areas is unacceptable. Here they take care of all categories of the population: conditions have been created for people with disabilities to live. Check-in time is not earlier than 14:00, while check-out should be no later than noon.

As guests in numerous reviews say, the staff here is friendly, smiling, ready to immediately help to solve problems. The possession of four languages, including Russian, English, German and Turkish, erase the language barrier in communication, which is also attributed by tourists to the advantages.

The provision of an extra bed and playpen is feasible upon request, requiring agreement with the administration. Payment is accepted by international banking systems.

The furniture includes a standard set: soft beds, a small desk, a luggage stand, a chair, bedside tables and a built-in wardrobe. Proper placement gives the room a sense of spaciousness, visually increasing its size.

Nutrition

Catering is carried out by serving meals according to the buffet four times a day according to a predetermined schedule. The buffet method allows each individual spa person to independently put in the plate such a portion volume that he can master. A variety of nutritious foods, including a huge selection of differently prepared meat, vegetables, cereals, fruits, fish, etc., will satisfy everyone's hunger without exception. According to numerous guests, even the most sophisticated visitor will not remain hungry here. For children there is a children's section, high chairs are provided.

Slender columns, multiple spotlights, sconces and embossed ceilings contribute to the creation of the classic style of the main restaurant. It has a covered area and an outdoor terrace. Table setting, friendly atmosphere contribute to the appearance of appetite and its satisfaction.

Beach

The 5-star TT Hotels Pegasos Royal is located on the beachfront, 50 meters from its own sandy beach. A gentle entry into the water determines the safety of games and bathing babies. The Mediterranean Sea in these environs has unprecedented transparency, which allows you to view the bottom with all its inhabitants.

Beach facilities include showers, changing rooms and a towel rack. You can enjoy sunbathing and find a dark tan, relaxing on a comfortable sun lounger with a soft mattress. For lovers of relaxation in the shade provided a sufficient number of umbrellas and awnings.

There is a rescue service and security on the beach, which constantly monitor the order on the beach and the safety of swimmers.

For those who prefer outdoor activities, there is a volleyball field where friendly teams compete for the championship, get to know each other and communicate easily. The beach is full of water activities, which include: banana, water skiing, windsurfing, parachuting for a boat, scooter and jet skiing. Diving center allows you to go on a journey through the amazing underwater world. It is available for both an experienced diver and a beginner who has undergone special training.

TT Hotels Pegasos Royal (ex. Suntopia Pegasos Royal) offers its guests free inflatable attractions that are located in the sea next to the pontoon. They will bring a lot of fun and unforgettable moments to their guests, will please not only children, but also adults.





Swimming spots

This hotel is equipped with a surprisingly large aquazone. The main pool contains an island with sunbeds, a volcano and slender towering palms. Here you can play water polo or do water aerobics. Light physical exercises from fitness instructors will give you a good mood, inspire a cheerful spirit and improve the health of everyone who decided to go in for sports. This pool is equipped with three water slides of different sizes and heights.

Nearby is a children's bathing area with an awning to protect from sunlight.

The second outdoor pool contains numerous water slopes for both young children and adults. Bright slides of various modifications lead to an unprecedented delight of everyone, regardless of age.

The use of sunbeds, mattresses, umbrellas and towels is free. Many fragrant flowers, tropical plants and palm trees with fruits surrounded the entire territory of the water park.

The covered aquazone is equipped with adult and children's pools with glass roofs.

Children's leisure activities

TT Hotels Pegasos Royal 5 * receives positive reviews from tourists who come to rest with children. Almost unanimously, the parents argue that the pastime of young guests is thought out to the smallest detail. The main focus is to take the child so that he feels happy, contented and joyful 24 hours a day. A TUI Tukan kids club is open for visiting, where a cheerful team of animators who know how and love to work with children will take their free time. Sports, contests and quizzes, culinary master classes and creative assignments will fill the day of the child with vivid emotions and unforgettable impressions.

In the fresh air, children can frolic in outdoor playgrounds equipped with the necessary equipment. They are safe, have a rubberized rug.

You can rent a stroller or use the services of an individual nanny at an additional cost.

Sport and leisure at the hotel

For lovers of mobile and sports activities, the TT Hotels Pegasos Royal 5 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya) has a well-equipped gym, basketball court, and tennis courts with a hard surface. Rental equipment and lighting are paid extra. It is possible to play bowling, billiards, darts or bocce. Dance classes, board games and chess will appeal to every guest.

In order to diversify the leisure of adults, throughout the day the team of animators tirelessly works on creating a holiday in the life of holidaymakers. Evening animation with a disco completes a beautiful day, full of new emotions and impressions.

An Internet cafe and a games room, a full-service spa and wellness center require additional payment and are available at any time of the day.
