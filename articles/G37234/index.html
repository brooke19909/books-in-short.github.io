Maritime Hotel, Rhodes, Greece: overview, features and reviews of tourists

Greek resorts are one of the most beloved among our compatriots. This country is associated with numerous islands, the Aegean and the Mediterranean Sea, clean beaches with fine sand, historical and cultural values, delicious local dishes with olives and olives, the sun and comfortable hotels. In Greece, you can relax even in a two-star hotel, where guests are offered quality food and great rooms. Aparthotel Maritime in Rhodes is one of them. In the article we will consider in detail (based on the reviews of tourists) this complex, its location and features, infrastructure, number of rooms, service.

What you need to know when traveling to Greece

Most travelers travel to islands such as Crete and Rhodes. Greece is a very friendly and safe country in relation to tourism. The first thing to do before traveling is to open a visa. This can be done through the consulate directly, as well as use the services of visa centers or travel agencies. Most often, Russians buy package tours to this country, which include round-trip flights, accommodation at the chosen hotel, insurance and visa support.





When passing customs control at airports (Heraklion, Chania, Diagoras, etc.) they may be asked to present an amount of 50 euros or equivalent in another currency. A tourist can carry 200 cigarettes and 1 liter of a strong alcoholic drink. It is recommended to keep all receipts for purchases made during the holidays. Duty-free, you can export goods from the country for a total amount of 50 euros, in other cases, you will need to pay tax.

Greeks are very good visitors. This is understandable, because the main source of state revenue is tourism. Travelers are often invited to visit, and denial may be perceived as an insult. Locals are very friendly, funny, they gesticulate a lot. The body language of the Greeks is somewhat different from Russian non-verbal communication. For example, our gesture of a clasped palm and a thumb raised up does not mean ‚Äúall is well‚Äù, but an insulting order to shut up. There are many differences, so when communicating it is better not to use facial expressions and body language.

When visiting public places, it should be remembered that although the Greeks honor Dionysus (the god of winemaking), one cannot drink obscene in bars, cafes and taverns. There are a lot of churches and temples in the country. Women should know that being in trousers or clothing with bare shoulders in such places is unacceptable.

Rhodes: climate, nature, characteristics

The resort town is located in the southeast of the island of the same name, whose area is slightly less than 1.5 thousand km, and is its administrative center. According to ancient Greek mythology, Rhodes is the home of Helios. That is why the island has 300 sunny days a year. In the middle of summer (July and August) the weather is very hot and dry. The best time to visit the island: the end of spring - the beginning of autumn.





Tourists who come here can swim in two seas at once: the Mediterranean (in the east) and the Aegean (in the west). The subtropical climate contributes to the lush growth of cypress and citrus trees, pines. Windsurfing is very popular on the northwest coast; fans of this active sport are attracted by the large waves of the Aegean Sea. The east of the island is perfect for a beach holiday, where the coast is covered by mountains. Here is a quiet and calm Mediterranean.

The city originated in the Middle Ages, so tourists are interested in considering the preserved fortress walls, castles and many other buildings from the time of the Knights. Rhodes is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, so there are a lot of interesting places to see.

Location and features Maritime Hotel 2 * (Rhodes)

Surrounded by a magnificent garden, the hotel is located in the Kremasti district of Rhodes. The area is famous for many attractions. To the beach (Gorgeous Beach) is just 7-10 minutes of calm walking. The center of the hotel is 9 km away, this distance can be easily overcome on foot or take the services of a taxi or bus. In Greece, it is best to rent a car, of course, if you have international rights and you are over 25 years old.

The tourist complex belongs to the category of apart-hotels and has 2 stars. Judging by the reviews of Maritime (Rhodes), it is chosen because of its proximity to the airport (4 km), clean interior, and rooms of rather large sizes. The staff is described as sweet, helpful and sincere.

Territory and beach

The main highlight of Maritime (Rhodes, Greece) is the presence of a chic garden. Here you can not just walk, but sit for hours, breathing in unique aromas and admiring the bright colors of subtropical plants.





The hotel area is very well maintained, ideal cleanliness will satisfy even the most ardent perfectionist. The distance to the beach is 300 m. The road gives great pleasure, beautiful flowers grow around, neatly trimmed shrubs, trees. The beach is municipal, pebble. You can use the sunbeds and umbrellas for a small nominal fee, there is a free shower. Many vacationers rent a car and explore other beaches of the island, among which Elli (very busy), the calm sandy coasts of Lindos, Faliraki (nightlife and hangouts) and some others are most famous.

Did the hotel guests enjoy the hotel infrastructure?

The snow-white building of the main building looks favorable against a background of green trees and a lawn. Nearby is a beautiful building, which houses a restaurant and bar. Guests have access to a free, secure car park.





In many reviews of Maritime 2 * (Rhodes, Greece), tourists write about the good conditions in the hotel for children. The kids were delighted with the pool and playground with bright swings, slides. For adults there is a volleyball court. It is possible to swim in the spacious pool or sunbathe on the sunbeds. Nearby there is a bar that is open until late in the evening.

Tourists characterize the hotel infrastructure as decent, excellent for a budget option. Most believe that Maritime in Rhodes is an ideal place for a family vacation. Some liked the area where you can organize a small picnic. Adults rested on the grass with snacks and amazing Greek wine, and happy kids frolic around.

Number of rooms

The only category of accommodation that the hotel offers is apartments, in total there are 46. The rooms are quite spacious, decorated in a classic minimalist style. The interior is dominated by bright colors. Each room is equipped with a small kitchen with a table and chairs, cutlery, utensils. In the sleeping area there are comfortable beds, TVs, air conditioners. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, sink, toilet, washing facilities.





For a reasonable fee, guests can use Wi-Fi. Many apartments have balconies with wonderful views of the island. Room service works efficiently. Vacationers were pleased with the cleanliness of the room, the regularity of cleaning and changing bed linen.

What are the tourists fed at the Maritime Hotel (Rhodes)?

You need to understand that this is a 2 * category, so you should not wait for culinary masterpieces and delights. If you are not a gourmet, then you will really like the food. The restaurant is open buffet style. You can choose the food option when booking the hotel, most often tourists prefer breakfast and dinner at the hotel. It is very comfortable. You can have a good breakfast with scrambled eggs, bacon, cereal or something else and spend the whole day on the beaches, on sightseeing trips. In the evening, dine with delicious hot (meat, chicken), Greek salad, souvlaki, stewed vegetables, etc. Guests are offered a wide assortment of fruits and berries: melons and watermelons, nectarines, tender peaches.





The bar is worth a try of local wines, especially the red Amorgiano. It is worth doing this for the reason that Rhodes drinks are not exported. You can try them and appreciate the taste only on the island.

Leisure options

The most popular type of tourism in Rhodes is amateur. Vacationers rent a car and drive around the island. Sightseeing on their own, dine in roadside cafes and taverns, swim on various beaches, visit disco bars and clubs. If it is not possible to rent a car, you can travel by bus. Opposite the hotel there is a public transport stop that runs quite regularly.

Not far from the Maritime Hotel (Rhodes) there are popular windsurfing centers. On the beaches near Ixia, lovers of various water sports gather. Here, water skiing, kite surfing, etc.

The most requested excursions

At the Maritime 2 * Hotel (Rhodes), staff is happy to talk about the main attractions of their island. Be sure to see Mount Filerimos with a cross that is visible even from the hotel. Nearby is Lindos with the famous Seven Springs. A popular excursion to the Valley of the Butterflies is popular with large and small tourists.





You can go to the Wine Tour with a tasting. You will be taken to a factory where they produce three main varieties of amazing Rhodes wines. Here you can buy unique products.

Maritime hotel rating by reviews

On the main booking sites, the figure is 4.1-4.6 points out of five possible. The majority of holidaymakers were satisfied with their stay at Maritime in Rhodes. People write that the atmosphere was sincere, the staff was responsive and friendly, the service was decent, and the rooms were comfortable.

As a rule, low ratings are given by people who were not ready to pay extra for Wi-Fi, sunbeds on the beach, etc. Others expected from Maritime 2 * (Rhodes, Greece) the pathos of a five-star hotel. All information about the complex is described in sufficient detail in various sources (including this article), many reliable reviews have been published. It is worthwhile to carefully and thoroughly familiarize yourself with them before the trip, so that the rest goes fully.
