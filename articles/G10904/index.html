How did I buy a health insurance policy

However, having looked at the necessary information on the Internet, he soon realized how things were going on in this matter. It turns out that medical policy is an independent document. It does not apply directly to compulsory health insurance (MHI). Nevertheless, such a policy provides an opportunity for the insured person to receive compensation for their costs of treatment and consultations with specialists.

In fact, VHI allows you to insure not health, as, for example, "in case of an accident", but the costs of a person for various medical care. Having understood this issue, I was surprised to learn that health insurance This type has very great advantages over compulsory medical insurance. In particular, you can choose the medical institution that is most suitable for the treatment of the disease. In this case, the insurance company monitors the quality of such a medical service. You can also choose a comprehensive version of VHI. It includes both the help of doctors in the clinic, and a number of dental services.

Advantageous option

On the Internet, I found all the necessary information about the largest and most reliable insurance companies. Asked off at work, I went to one of them.
