Hotel Description

The resort hotel "Albatros Palace Resort 5 *" (Hurghada) is just the perfect place to relax. It has everything you need to have a good time: clean pools with water slides, a modern large spa, as well as numerous restaurants, bars and other entertainment opportunities. Thanks to the professional and well-coordinated work of the animation team, guests will not have time for boredom. They will be constantly attracted to exciting sports or vibrant entertainment events. The incredible architecture of the Albatros Palace Resort 5 * hotel (Hurghada) complements the overall picture, made in the style of oriental palaces.





Hotel location

The hotel "Albatros Palace Resort 5 *" (Hurghada) is located quite conveniently relative to the main objects of the resort infrastructure. Hurghada Airport is not more than 20 km from the hotel, which is about 15 minutes. As for the city center, it is also about 20 km away.





Description of hotel rooms

Hotel "Albatros Palace Resort 5 *" (Hurghada) has a fairly impressive number of rooms, which is 621 guest rooms.





The most numerous category of apartments is a "double standard". There are rooms with views of the pool and the hotel garden, as well as those with a chic seascape. The apartment has all the necessary amenities: a bathroom with a shower, a small balcony (if the room is located on the ground floor, it will be a terrace), satellite TV (Russian-language channels are also present), as well as a full range of comfortable furniture, a safe and an international telephone.

Roomy family rooms, which are comparable in size to two standard ones, are very popular. The bedrooms are connected by a door. In all other respects, the apartments are absolutely identical to the standard ones.

Luxury rooms are two-room apartments consisting of a cozy bedroom and a spacious bright living room. Of the equipment there is a full range of modern designer furniture, satellite TV, a telephone and a minibar. The bathroom has a jacuzzi. Special attention should be paid to the terrace of the room, on which there are sun loungers, umbrellas and other garden furniture. There is access to a private pool.





Entertainment

The hot climate, proximity to the sea, as well as the opportunity to have fun - these are some of the most common reasons why tourists go to the resort city of Hurghada. "Albatross Palace Resort Spa 5 *" always makes sure that its guests spend time interestingly and actively. In this regard, the guests of the hotel complex are available the following elements of the entertainment program:

  • three modern tennis courts (it is worth paying attention that rackets, as well as spotlights for lighting, are provided only for a fee);
  • on the beach there is a special field for playing volleyball;
  • the hotel has a huge number of ping-pong tables;
  • the modern sports center is working round the clock, where you can get your body in shape on simulators, as well as do fitness under the supervision of a professional instructor;
  • one of the outdoor pools has a Jacuzzi;
  • sports animators conduct daily classes in gymnastics, yoga and aerobics;
  • throughout the day, as well as in the evening, for the purpose of entertaining guests, many sports and entertainment events are held;
  • in the hottest time of the year, themed parties are organized on the beach every night with dancing, music and a rich show program.

Providing children's rest

Not only adults, but also numerous young tourists annually come to Egypt (Hurghada). "Albatross Palace Resort 5 *" is well aware of the importance of the proper organization of children's vacations, so the following infrastructure and entertainment facilities are provided for the hotel for kids:

  • an entertainment and game program, as well as developmental and educational classes, are held daily in the kids club;
  • in one of the pools there is a children's area with a mini water park;
  • the hotel has a playground with many attractions and entertainment;
  • For a comfortable stay and rest of the kids, upon prior request, children's furniture is provided in the rooms, as well as in the hotel restaurants.

Restaurants

Every year more and more popular among tourists is the hotel "Albatross Palace Resort 5 *" (Hurghada). Tours to this resort are interesting for travelers not only in terms of entertainment and relaxation, but also in terms of the skill of local chefs. You can appreciate it by visiting restaurants that operate on the territory of the hotel complex:

  • Tagine is a chic restaurant of Moroccan cuisine, where everything is saturated with the atmosphere of an oriental tale.
  • L'asiatique - deciding to have dinner in this restaurant, you can try the most original and unexpected dishes, characteristic only for Asian cuisine.
  • Il Caminetto is an Italian restaurant that will appeal to everyone who cares for pizza and pasta.
  • The Mediterranean restaurant offers dozens of varieties of fish cooked in a variety of ways.
  • Salsa Mexican - in this institution you can fully experience the piquancy and piquancy of traditional Mexican dishes.
  • Zum Kaiser is a theme restaurant, the concept of which is to observe the best German traditions. Many beers, as well as spicy sausages with various side dishes, will certainly suit your taste.




Hotel Bars

With the words "carefree" and "entertainment" Hurghada is best associated. Hotel "Albatros Palace Resort Spa 5 *" maintains a similar atmosphere of relaxation, fun and relaxation thanks to the numerous bars on its territory:

  • Oriental Palace Bar is a lobby style establishment located in the lobby of the hotel. It is characterized by a restrained design and a relaxed atmosphere. This is an ideal place for business people or for those who just want to enjoy a friendly conversation in silence.
  • Le Pasha is a bright and noisy disco bar, where huge flows of music and dance lovers flock every evening.
  • Splash Beach Bar - This bar is located on the beach so that guests can quench their thirst with water at any time or try a lot of tropical cocktails.
  • There is another bar on the beach where you can eat delicious pancakes with a variety of fillings.
  • Mocambo is a real kingdom of fruit. Only here you can expect juices from exotic fruits.
  • Columbus is a bar with a refined interior where, in addition to drinks, you can also try different varieties of coffee.
  • There is also a bar on the terrace overlooking the sea , resembling a lobby in its atmosphere and assortment.




Positive reviews

For several years now, Albatross Palace Resort 5 * (Hurghada) has been considered one of the best hotels in Egypt. Reviews of tourists - this is perhaps one of the most important factors on which the reputation of the hotel complex is based. About this resort you can find the following positive reviews:

  • Polite and helpful front desk staff;
  • Free wireless internet in the lobby area. In the rooms located next to the bar, the network is also excellent.
  • Wonderful children's animation, as well as a lot of entertainment for kids on site.
  • Good varied food, as well as the presence of fresh juices on the menu, which is rare in Egypt hotels.
  • There are two large hotels in the neighborhood, on the territory of which you can also walk and visit infrastructure facilities and entertainment events.
  • Good shops on site with a large assortment of goods and accommodating sellers who are easy to bargain with at an affordable price.
  • Tourists arriving long before check-in are offered breakfast. Also, those who arrived at the hotel after dinner will not remain hungry.
  • Large, spacious rooms with fairly new furniture and working fixtures.
  • A pleasant moment is the ability to take food from restaurants to the room (which is prohibited in many other hotels).
  • A large area with many flowers, greenery and all kinds of decorative elements.
  • Beautiful clean beach, a nice addition to which will be corals and beautiful exotic fish.




Negative reviews

Quite high requirements are imposed on this hotel complex, since Albatros Palace Resort SPA is a 5 * hotel. Hurghada accepts many tourists, so the flow of people is large enough, which can lead to some interruptions in the work of the institution. Guests have the following complaints about this hotel:

  • surface cleaning, dust remains in many places, and the floors almost never wash at all;
  • the hotel has a problem with sewage, due to which a specific unpleasant odor periodically appears;
  • the hotel is not very well located (there are no large shops, markets or clubs nearby);
  • strange that there are no teaspoons in restaurants;
  • sometimes reception staff knowingly offer bad rooms so that guests pay extra for relocation to a better room;
  • Internet is very slow.
