Diving

Okurcalar has its own diving center. In it, a tourist can amuse his vanity by receiving an international diver diploma. What do the guests of the hotel in question say about him?

Prior to admission to courses in it, the tourist fills out a medical questionnaire and a compulsory statement in which he certifies the fact of understanding the risks when diving into the underwater world. Then the adherent will pick up a set of diving equipment for growth, followed by briefing on the basics of breathing under water and on safety when diving.

On the appointed day and time, a minibus will arrive at the hotel, which will take the tourist to the port of Alanya. Further, the yacht takes him aboard and floats to one of the 14 diving points scattered along the coast of the Alanya Peninsula. Diving is performed twice a day: before and after an inexpensive and tasty lunch, the cost of which is included in the payment of the courses. In the depths you can see starfish, octopus, moray eels, sea urchins, ancient amphoras.

After diving, the same minibus takes the diver to the hotel.
