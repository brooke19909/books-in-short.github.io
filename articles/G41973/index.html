Climatic conditions

The climate in the Dominican Republic is subtropical marine, which means it is humid and hot. Trade winds and breezes regularly blow here, which are a mitigating factor that helps to endure high temperatures with high humidity. From May to September there are frequent, but short showers, the same thing happens in the last two months of the year. The rest of the time the weather here is quite predictable and convenient for a beach holiday. Although, if you even bought a ticket to the Ifa Villas Bavaro Resort Spa 4 * hotel in the rainy season, do not despair, because the rains, as already noted, are short and the temperature of both air and sea water is always high. In addition, you can always swim in the pool.
