How long does it take to get from Antalya to Alanya?

Vacation is a small life, during which you want to have time to get maximum pleasure and experience. If you are going to relax in one of the popular resorts in Turkey, then why not get acquainted with the sights of the other too? Let's find out how much to travel from Antalya to Alanya. And also get acquainted with the most interesting places in these cities.





How long to travel from Antalya to Alanya?

With such a move will face all the tourists who plan to relax in Alanya, as the nearest airport is in Antalya. If you are resting in Antalya, but want to see the sights of another resort, then this question will also be relevant for you.

The distance between these two popular settlements is just over 133 km. What is the time to go from Antalya to Alanya? The answer to this question depends on how exactly you will get. If you go by car, then, as experienced travelers say, even with stops for a short rest, you can meet two hours. This is the fastest option. Everyone else will take more time. It:

  • transfer from travel agency;
  • bus;
  • Taxi;
  • car.

There is no railway connection between the cities. Consider all the available methods as detailed as possible.

If you purchased a tour

If you are a "package" tourist, then the travel agency will decide the question for you. At the exit from the airport a transfer bus will be waiting. How long it takes to get from Antalya to Alanya in this case will depend on the congestion of the road, as well as on how many hotels you need to transport arrivals. If you prefer to travel on your own, then you have to choose between a bus, taxi or car rental.





Riding a bus

To catch the intercity bus, you must first get from the airport to the Otogar Antalya bus station. You can do this on a local minibus. The stop is located at the exit of the airport's internal terminal The bus runs every half hour. Starts work at 5-30, ends at 01-00. For travel you need to pay about two euros. It will take about an hour to travel.

From the bus station in Alanya there are a lot of buses. They do not have numbers, but each says what city it should be in. Departure takes place every half hour. A ticket is purchased either at the box office or from the driver (then money must be given at the exit, not at the entrance), costs 8-10 euros per person.

An important point: buses do not go at night, the last flight leaves at 20-00, and the first - at 6-00. If you arrived late, you can either take a taxi or wait in the morning for the airport.

Turkish buses are as comfortable as airplanes. They have comfortable seats, air conditioning, personal lighting, small televisions with headphones. The driver's assistant will definitely warn you in advance about the stop. You will enjoy the ride.





We are taking a taxi

It will take no more than five minutes to search for a car; parking is located opposite the exit from the airport terminal building. And if you book in advance, the driver will meet you with a sign at the exit from the arrivals area.

The cost of the trip depends on the type of car. If the economy option, then from 60 euros, if you need a minibus - from 70 euros. In the case of traveling with a family or company, it will turn out even inexpensively.

How long to get from Antalya to Alanya by taxi? Travel time is 2.5 hours.

Driving a car

Two popular resorts are connected by the D-400 highway, which is considered one of the main roads in Turkey, so do not be surprised at the frantic traffic. The route follows along the seashore, crosses small towns where it will not work fast.





The road is boring and hard can only seem to the driver. Passengers will admire the magnificent scenery outside the window: the gentle sea, picturesque mountains and pines.

Since the entire road is equipped with detailed signs, you won‚Äôt get lost. The path will take, as mentioned above, 2-2.5 hours.

There is only one minus in this situation: gasoline is expensive in Turkey (in 2017 - about 85 rubles per liter). The budget of such a trip will be about 20 euros.

Since there is very little cafe on the way, take a snack with you.

Antalya and Alanya: which is better?

This is a question on which the debate will probably never subside. Therefore, we will only briefly consider the most interesting, both there and there.

Antalya is a large and noisy city, where there are many modern hotels, cafes and restaurants, shops and discos, water parks and amusement parks. Here life is in full swing. There are so many historical sights in Antalya that it can take a whole vacation to get to know them. This is the gate of Hadrian, which is already 2000 years old, the Hydyrlyk tower, Ataturk's house-museum, beautiful mosques.
