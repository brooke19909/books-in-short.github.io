Budget hostels in the heart of the Caucasus Mountains

The capital of North Ossetia - Alania, Vladikavkaz - is a city where life is in full swing. Travelers come here to see the beauty and sights of North Ossetia, they come here hoping to find business partners. The city is actively involved in sports and cultural activities, so young athletes or young talents who came to the next contest are not uncommon here.

A variety of hotels are open to many who come to the city, but there are also places like hostels. The Vladikavkaz hostel is an economist of a hotel with elements of home comfort, simplicity and the ability to communicate. The main plus of the hostel is the cost, which is what attracts many tourists.

Cozy "Horseshoe"

Hostel Podkova is located near the Republican Stadium Spartak (2 km). The one-story building is easy to find, it is located at: st. Markova, 52. Numerous tourists leave positive reviews about this place.

One of the important advantages of Podkova Hostel is the 24-hour reception. It doesn‚Äôt matter what time the guest arrives - there will always be a place for him.

The hostel offers the following options:

  • in the room, common for women and men, a bunk bed;
  • same bunk bed in male only room;
  • general number (men and women), designed for 4 places;
  • dormitory room (men and women) for 6 beds.

Hostel "Horseshoe" services

Each room includes a hairdryer, iron and ironing board, TV, safe, heating and air conditioning. Fluffy towels and clean linens are provided.

The toilet and bathroom are shared, and bathrooms and rooms are cleaned daily.

You can relax, watch TV, chat and play a variety of board games in the common living room. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hostel.

Many guests among the advantages note that in this hostel of Vladikavkaz you can always have a meal in the bar, where cooks will quickly prepare lunch or dinner, serve breakfast or coffee.

What are the advantages of this place tourists distinguish:

- there is a guarded parking lot in the territory;

- at guests' disposal - laundry room, where you can wash and refresh clothes.

The cost of staying in the Horseshoe

Accommodation in the "Horseshoe" hostel in Vladikavkaz will cost inexpensively - only 500 rubles. per night. This price is acceptable for students and those who do not want to spend money on a bed, spending all day in business or traveling. Horseshoe collaborates with organized excursion groups, and also hosts schoolchildren and athletes.

One: room description

Another Vladikavkaz hostel has the original name - One.

It offers comfortable rooms with modern furniture. The categories are as follows:

  1. Room for 8 beds. This is a spacious room with bunk metal beds. The window closes at night with a roller blind. There are nightstands, clothes hanger and hangers in the room.
  2. Designed for 6 people, the room has three beds in two tiers. For clothes and personal items, hangers, coat hanger, stands are provided.
  3. There is a room for 1 person. Comfort in the room is created by an easy chair, which can be turned into an extra bed, a wardrobe, a coffee table.

The hostel has two bathrooms, toilets and bathrooms, so there are no queues and problems.

You can come and stay in this hostel of Vladikavkaz at any time during the day.

The rooms and the shared kitchen-living room have a full set of necessary household appliances, you can make yourself tea or coffee, warm up dishes. By the way, tea and coffee in the hostel are poured for free.

Cost

Hostel "1" is a vacation without extra expenses. The cost of rooms is low and amounts to 400 rubles per person in rooms that accept 6-8 people, and 600 rubles in a single room.

It's nice that there are discounts in an economy class hotel! A group of 10 people can save - they will be given a 10% discount, a free shuttle service, and a banquet will be arranged at parting.

If the guest plans to stay for a period of 10 days or more, he is also entitled to a 10% discount.

And each visitor receives a memo, which shows the phone numbers of all services that deliver food.

One Services

So, the stay will be inexpensive, the hostel in Vladikavkaz One surrounds its guests with attention, creating pleasant amenities:

  • All rooms have free Wi-Fi.
  • The rooms include a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.
  • Every day a full cleaning of all rooms is carried out.

What else does this hostel in Vladikavkaz offer guests? There is free parking for guests, and video surveillance is available throughout, so that your stay is absolutely safe.

There are One services for an additional fee:

  • Deliver the ordered food.
  • Ossetian specialty cakes will be prepared on the way.
  • Laundry service is available.
  • Organized excursion programs in the region and the city, will help get to interesting natural sites.
  • Airport transfer.

This Vladikavkaz hostel is located at 82 Kutuzova Street.
