Sony KDL-40RE353 TV. Reviews, specifications and setup

This article will review the current model of the Sony KDL-40RE353 television device. Reviews of specialists and owners, technical specifications of this model, as well as its tuning algorithm will be further considered. In addition to this, the equipment of this multimedia center will be given and recommendations regarding its use will be given.

Equipment

Sony KDL-40RE353 was equipped with a pretty good supply list. The reviews of the owners of this television device include a list of equipment to its clear advantages. The leading Japanese manufacturer included the following main components in this list:

  1. TV.
  2. Comprehensive paper manual.
  3. Power cable for power supply of a multimedia system.
  4. A set of batteries for powering a remote control system.
  5. Complete support for mounting the TV on any accessible horizontal surface with a set of mounts.

main parameters

Fairly good selection and technical specifications of the Sony KDL-40RE353 TV. Reviews at the same time indicate a complete lack of shortcomings.

An integrated tuner is a fairly universal solution. He is able to receive both local programs and cable. Moreover, in digital or analog format.

The size of this screen is 40 ", and its resolution is 1920 x 1080 pixels. The type of matrix used is Direct LED.

The acoustic subsystem includes 2 speakers. Each of them can produce 5 watts of sound power. In total, you can already get 10 watts.

The communication list of the multimedia system includes one antenna input, 2 USB and HDMI ports each. There is also a CI slot for installing a digital TV decoding module and SCART and RCA connectors .

Setting procedure

Also, the advantages of this multimedia center include a very simple setup procedure, as evidenced by numerous customer reviews. Sony KDL-40RE353 TV lacks support for Smart TV technology . Therefore, its tuning algorithm consists of the following manipulations:

  1. We remove the multimedia system from the transport packaging along with the rest of the delivery elements.
  2. We lay the device on a soft and even horizontal surface with the screen down. We dismantle transport fasteners according to the attached instructions. Install the horizontal support using the complete elements.
  3. We put the device on a table or any other surface where it is planned to operate.
  4. We bring the power cord and connect the TV to the power network with its help. We also stretch the signal wire and switch it to the antenna input of the entertainment system.
  5. Turn on the multimedia center. Searching for TV shows using automatic search. First you need to specify the search parameters. We save the received current list of television programs and the device is then ready for use.

Owner reviews and cost

The advantages of this TV owners include:

  • reliability;
  • high quality pictures;
  • simple setup procedure;
  • low cost.

The downside of this entertainment center is only one - this is the lack of support for SmartTV at the software level. If necessary, you can understaff it mini-PC and solve this problem. The cost of such a device at the moment is 27 500 - 28 200 rubles.

Conclusion

As part of this review, the modern Sony KDL-40RE353 television device was reviewed. Reviews focus the attention of potential buyers on the fact that it does not have significant shortcomings. Such conditionally can only be attributed to the fact that the Smart TV function is not implemented in its software. But, as was previously noted, this drawback can be easily eliminated. And the advantages in this case include the low cost, excellent image and sound quality, high functionality of the integrated tuner, a simple tuning procedure and the reliability inherent in any product of this brand.
