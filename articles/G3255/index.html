general information

In order to take a break from endless problems, city bustle, noise and gas pollution, you should be alone with nature. It is in this way that you can feel the unique beauty of the world around you, get at least for a short time peace and tranquility.

Among the many wonderful places of relaxation, you can give preference to rest on the territory of the plateau Lago-Naki, which is one of the most picturesque corners of nature. "Mountain Village" (Adygea)

In Adygea, almost all tourist and excursion routes to the mountains begin from the village called Kamennomostsky. Although most often both visiting tourists and locals call him Hadzhokh. There is a good road from the capital of Adygea Maykop to Hadzhokh (a distance of about 48 kilometers), passing through a pass through a low Maikop ridge overgrown with forests.

There are wonderful recreation centers in these places, including a tourist complex with the name "Mountain Village", located in the fabulously beautiful valley of the Belaya River, at the foot of the Trezubets Mountain. From Maykop, the distance to it is 60 kilometers.

Holidays in Adygea in the "Mountain Village" - the most wonderful for romantics and nature lovers.



Location

Almost the entire territory of the Lagonaki plateau is covered by well-developed numerous types of tourism. It is active recreation - the main form of relaxation in these places. And in the tourist complex "Mountain Village" all the conditions for a quality rest of visitors are provided.

The territory of the base is located on the banks of the beautiful Belaya River, at the Granite Canyon, right at the foot of the Trezubets Mountain. The name of the canyon is associated with a ridge passing by the river.

The recreation center "Mountain Village" in Adygea is located in the wooded part of the river valley, not far from the village of Dakhovskaya. If you go from Maykop towards the south (to the village of Guzeripl), then the hotel of interest will be 6 km behind the village towards Guzeripl.

The base is located at a distance of 213 kilometers from Krasnodar (approximately 2.5 hours by car). Approximately the same ride from the M4 Don route leading to the Black Sea.

Previously, the village of Nickel was located here, but today this settlement is a group of hostels and modern cottages.



Rooms, Territory

The ‚Äúmountain village‚Äù of Adygea can be called both a forest hotel and a tourist base.

On the vast well-groomed territory of the complex, between the magnificent forest and the road, many log cabins are scattered.

Tourists can stay here in wooden cozy houses, made in the old Russian style and consisting of several rooms, which are equipped with separate exits. You can stay in them for 2-4 people. The houses are mainly two-story (3-4 rooms) with all amenities: cable TV, a bathroom with shower, refrigerator, etc. The rooms are all stylishly furnished.

In addition to the beautiful landscape design of the territory, the verandas offer a magnificent view of the Caucasus Mountains covered with dense forest.



Services

In the "Mountain Village" in Adygea for tourists there is the opportunity to use the services of a comprehensive meal in a cafe located on the territory (breakfast is included in the price). You can also have lunch and dinner, but by reservation. For visitors to the restaurant, decorated in a rustic style, the menu offers homemade Russian dishes.

There is also an outdoor pool with umbrellas and sun loungers for relaxing by the water. There are billiards, sports and children's playgrounds, barbecue facilities, a bath with a font and parking. For lovers of equestrian tourism there is the opportunity to make interesting horseback riding, for lovers of rafting - rafting, you can do canyoning. There are routes for professionals who love extreme sports and family walking.

There is a recreation center and a conference room in which you can conduct various corporate and business events. Parking for cars is free.

Mountain Village Chalet in Adygea is a great place to relax. Room rates include accommodation and breakfast, and in the summer it includes the use of the pool.



sights

The base is located at an altitude of 520 meters above sea level, surrounded by forest, so in the very peak of the summer heat it is a little cooler here than on the Kuban plain.

What kind of entertainment await tourists in such a fabulous place?

1. 50 meters along the road to the left, and then 300 meters down the forest road, you can go to the suspension bridge, thrown over the mountainous stormy river Belaya. Huge oaks and beeches grow in the forest. Behind the bridge there is a small farm where you can rent a horse.

2. If you go up from the eastern edge of the hotel along an old steep forest road, you can take a wonderful walk to the waterfall on the Syuk River. Further you can walk to the abandoned adits of the former Nickel mine.

2. At 20 kilometers in the direction of Maykop in the village of Kamenomostsky, you should definitely visit the Khojokh Gorge, which is a unique natural corner, where a canyon is cut through the river (length - 1 km, width - up to 7 meters, depth - 40-60 m). It is well fenced for excursions, but entrance to it is paid.

3. On the same road leading to the canyon, but a little closer - a cascade of beautiful waterfalls of the Bolshaya Rufabgo stream. Here you can spend longer time to fully enjoy the incredible landscape.

4. If you go up from the Mountain Village base (Adygea) towards Maykop and then turn left behind Dakhovskaya, after 20 kilometers you can find yourself on the famous Lago-Naki plateau, where you can get a lot of pleasant impressions from the beauties seen from a height of 1000 meters created by nature: the Big Azish cave, the Belaya river valley, the Stone Sea plateau, the snowy peak of Fishta, etc.

5. In Guzeripl there is a bridge to the reserve, where there is a museum, a large dolmen and a fabulous beauty of a fir forest.

6. The most striking and impressive entertainment for vacationers here is rafting on the river. White.



You can also do jipping (traveling on UAZs along mountain rivers and trails) and trips on a motor hang glider.

Once in the base of the "Mountain Village" in Adygea, you should definitely try to visit the magnificent Mount Trident.

Mount Trident

At a village in Adygea, Mount Trezubets rushed up from the bowels of the earth. In fact, there are several peaks, so the locals called them a trident.

The main path to it originates in the Blockhouse Glade (near the village of Hamyshki). After about 200 meters, the path rises to the spur and becomes a slightly noticeable path. The entire climb takes about an hour to the top at a height of 1050 meters.

From the top of the Trident there is a stunning view of the Belaya Valley, the village of Hamyshki and the village of Dakhovskaya, Lago-Naki, the Azish-Tau ridge, and Granite Gorge. Sometimes, being at the foot, you can see wild goats quickly galloping along the ridge, having settled in these places inaccessible to humans.

Trident - the beginning of the Du-Du-Gush ridge, which is one of the most beautiful corners of Adygea.

The general impression is a cozy and comfortable tourist complex. Rest in it is a great opportunity to spend time in nature, plunging into the atmosphere of unique beauty and freshness.
