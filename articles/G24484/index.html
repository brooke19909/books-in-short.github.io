TVs with Internet access: an overview

Those times when TVs were used only for their intended purpose are a thing of the past. Modern technologies have made it possible to turn them into the likeness of personal computers. And now you will not surprise anyone with a TV with Internet access.

Responsible for communication with the World Wide Web functional Smart TV. If the specification for the device has this line, then it supports access to the network. Televisions with the Internet and a Wi-Fi module are able to organize not just passive, but active leisure, where the user acts as not only a spectator, but also a direct participant in the events. This is mainly about game plan applications and other similar entertainment content.

Difficulty choosing

Televisions with Internet access are classified according to various parameters, but the only critical one is the operating system. The chosen platform directly affects usability, variety of content, as well as complexity of settings.

Many manufacturers offer their own branded OS, but judging by the numerous reviews of televisions with Internet access, there are only three options to stop. This is the familiar and universal Android platform, the development of Samsung - Tizen and the proprietary OS of LG - WebOS. As such, there is no brand binding to the operating system, so these shells can be seen among a wide range of manufacturers.

Issue price

To the question of how much does a TV with Internet access cost, there is no definite answer. The line "Smart TV" in the specification for the device does not affect the price tag so much. And the cost still depends on the usual qualities of the TV: matrix, brand, design, additional functionality, etc.
