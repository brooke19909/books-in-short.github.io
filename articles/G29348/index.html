How to use fax?



Almost every office, even the smallest one, is currently equipped with such a necessary machine as a fax machine. With it, copies of various documents are sent, ranging from important contracts to various images. Most often, faxing is entrusted to one person, mainly the secretary, who receives or sends various documents, monitors the performance of the machine, including controls the availability of paper in the appropriate compartment. But what if the person whose responsibilities it is to receive and send fax copies temporarily departed, and you were asked to look after the machine and at that time the phone rang. If you need to send a document, then first you need to fill it into the corresponding groove. Typically, a manufacturer takes care of its users and provides images of how to work with faxing. The main thing you should pay close attention to is which side you are inserting the sheet into the hole. Now you know how to use a fax, and you can easily accept and send documents without too much difficulty.
