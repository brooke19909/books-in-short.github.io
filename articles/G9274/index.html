Spring hat for girls (crochet): options for every taste

Accessories such as a hat and scarf can always be bought. But in such a situation, one cannot be sure of the composition of the used yarn. This can be avoided if the spring hat for the girl is crocheted by herself.





The first option: with a wide elastic band

It consists of two parts. Above is a round pattern. The bottom of the cap is a wide elastic band. At her expense, a spring hat for a girl (crocheted) does not slip and sits tightly on her head.

Start knitting relies on the tulle. An example circuit is located below. The circle must be made so that it completely covers the top of the head, then to move on to the elastic.





It is better to knit from embossed columns. They need to be alternated in two. First, two before work, then two more, but already at work. So until the end of the circle.

The second option: with ties

This spring hat for girls crocheted also fits with the crown. First, it is supposed to make a ring from a small number of loops. Then tie the required number of columns with a crochet. It should be a neat circle.

In the second row from the top of each column, two double crochet need to be made. In the third, connect two more columns between the two and divide them with an air loop. The fourth is formed by fans of three columns with a crochet in each arch of the previous row.

In the fifth row at each vertex of the columns of the fan, tie one more, only the extreme ones will be ordinary. A central relief before work. This embossed column will be the center of the pattern. In all other rows, it must be knitted again in relief in front of the canvas.

The sixth row repeats the fifth, only three columns of the fan are separated by air loops. Seventh, two columns should be connected at the edges of the embossed column. The eighth repeats the seventh with air loops along the edges of the embossed column.

If necessary, then you can continue to expand the pattern. Otherwise, continue to knit the eighth row without changes until a spring hat for the girl is crocheted of the required depth.

Then complete the edging. First, adjust the number of loops to such that it is divided into three. Then knit like this: three lifting stitches, three double crochets in the first lifting stitch, connecting stitch in the third stitch. Then repeat the same element until the end of the circle.





On the sides of the hats, tie a few long threads and weave two pigtails. They will serve as ties.

Third option: from a direct canvas

This is the perfect spring hat for girls (crochet) for beginner needlewomen. After the head circumference is measured, the number is supposed to be reduced by 1.5 cm. They will return by stretching the canvas.

Dial the initial chain at the desired length. Knit all rows with double crochets. Their number is calculated by the size of the girl‚Äôs head. Connect the web to the cylinder using half columns. Tie the upper and lower edges of the product.

Now you can connect the top. To do this, dial a chain of 50 air loops. Stretch them through the columns of the last row, tighten them and tie. Hide the ends inside.

From this option, you can easily get a spring hat for a girl with a crochet with ears. To do this, sew the corners of the cylinder, and pull the middle according to the described principle.





Fourth option: with a simple pattern

Even a novice craftswoman will succeed in crocheting such a spring hat for a girl. The master class of its manufacture begins with three air loops, which are supposed to be closed in a ring. Then tie 6 SC in it. This is if the yarn is dense enough. The number of columns can be increased at your discretion.

The second and fourth circles require that two single crochet columns be made at the top of each column. To rise in each of these rows, you need to knit one air loop.

Fifth circle: 2 loops for lifting, 3 double crochets, 2 double crochets in one loop. Repeat this alternation until the end of the circle.

The sixth is similar to the fifth. Only 5 double crochet and two in one loop alternate. In the seventh-tenth, a fan of two columns is knitted every 4.

The eleventh circle consists entirely of double crochets in each loop of the previous row.





Twelfth to fourteenth: 3 air loops, double crochet in the second loop of the previous circle, air, column in the next even loop. Repeat the alternation of the air loop and column until the end of the circle.

Fifteenth to sixteenth fully repeat the pattern of the eleventh.

The seventeenth to nineteenth circles must be knitted similarly to the twelfth.

Twenty-twenty-first: like the eleventh. Twenty-second: 2 lifting loops, double crochets at each vertex of the previous circle, skipping every sixth of them. This is where the decrease begins. If the yarn is thin, then an increase in the number of rows of the pattern may be required. This series can also be repeated if the cap is very loose.

The last circle: openwork strapping of the product.
