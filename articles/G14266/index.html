Evenia Montevista Hawai 3 * (Spain): description and reviews of tourists

If you plan to spend an unforgettable vacation in the Spanish resort town of Lloret de Mar, but do not want to spend much on accommodation, then the three-star Evenia Montevista Hawai hotel will become very worthy and worthy of attention. Read about our guests here, as well as the impressions of tourists who have already been here. Read our article.



Location

The hotel in question is located almost in the center of the major tourist center of Spain - the city of Lloret de Mar. In the vicinity of the hotel there are many bars, restaurants, shops and a variety of entertainment facilities. Evenia Montevista Hawai guests can walk to the promenade in just a few minutes at a leisurely pace. Public transport is 50 meters away. From Lloret de Mar you can travel to many cities in Spain. For example, you can get to Barcelona in just 1 hour. As for airports, the nearest air harbor is in Girona, 30 kilometers from the hotel. However, many guests stay here through the air harbor of Barcelona.



Evenia Montevista Hawaii (Spain, Lloret de Mar): description and photo

This 3-star hotel was built in 1973. However, several years ago a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here. So today, guests are expected to modern and equipped with everything necessary. Own territory of the hotel is small. However, there is an outdoor pool where you can have fun, if for some reason you do not want to go to the beach. The hotel has a 24-hour reception, a restaurant, a bar, laundry and dry cleaning services. According to many tourists, this hotel is ideal for relaxing people who prefer to spend most of their time on the beach and traveling to places of interest. Families with children and senior citizens may not like it here.

Housing stock

Evenia Montevista Hawai has at its disposal 180 rooms located in one building equipped with elevators. All apartments here are double. It is possible to install an extra or cot. Each room has comfortable beds, a spacious wardrobe, a TV with cable channels (including in Russian), a bathroom. A safe is available at an additional cost.



Evenia Montevista Hawaii Traveler Reviews

Since many modern tourists, when planning a vacation, carefully study not only official information about a particular hotel, but also the opinions of people who have already stayed in it, we decided to save you some time and give generalized impressions of our compatriots who happened to live during their holidays in "Evenia Montevista Hawaii."

First of all, many tourists note that this hotel not only draws three stars, but in many respects fully corresponds to a four-star hotel. In this regard, the ratio of price and quality more than pleased the guests.

According to our compatriots, despite the fact that Evenia Montevista Hawai is far from a new hotel, modern interiors and good furniture and appliances await guests here. So, many people are pleased to note very comfortable beds, the availability of all the necessary rooms and trouble-free plumbing. The guests were also pleased with the regular cleaning. Towels are also changed every day.

Despite the fact that some guests did not respond very well about the food at the hotel, the majority of the guests were quite satisfied with the quality and variety of food. So, according to the latter, there is always a fairly wide selection of well-prepared main dishes, side dishes and snacks. If you do not want to take risks, then book your stay without lunch and dinner. Then you can eat in one of the local restaurants.

Liked many travelers and the location of the hotel in question. So, according to them, the city center with a lot of entertainment can be reached in just a quarter of an hour. The road to the beach does not take more than 10 minutes. Moreover, the beach line here is very wide, and there is always enough space for everyone, even in the midst of the season. Many experienced travelers advise to buy a folding sun umbrella and mat on the first day so as not to pay for the rental of sunbeds (about 6 euros per day).
