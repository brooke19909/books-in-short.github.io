Beautiful crafts on the theme of "Autumn" from pasta

Very interesting crafts made from pasta. From different varieties, funny leaves, flowers, berries are obtained. The appearance of such crafts is unusual. Using products of different sizes, you can create exclusive items that will be a wonderful decoration or an original gift.

Bunch of grapes

Here is an example of a very simple craft on the theme "Autumn" from pasta, which can also be used as a Christmas tree toy. It is called a "bunch of grapes." We will prepare colored paper, a handful of horns in the shape of shells, a slice of serpentine, colored thread, glue, yellow varnish with sparkles. Getting to work. We cut out a piece of thick green paper and begin to paste grape-shells on it, forming a bunch. When the grape brush is ready, decorate with a serpentine mustache, we will make a loop from the thread. Let the crafts dry thoroughly. After that, apply a yellow glitter varnish to the grapes. Our craft is ready.

Hedgehog

First you need to fashion a hedgehog, make him ears, make eyes and a nose from natural material. And then decorate the back with pasta needles. Lamb

What else can you do crafts on the theme of "Autumn" from pasta? For example, a curly lamb that grew a long silky coat by autumn. To make crafts, we need a lamb contour cut out of brown paper. Mushroom basket

"Basket for mushrooms" is made from an unnecessary cap from a thermos and pasta in the form of scallops. Using a glue gun, starting from the bottom, glue the pasta in rows in a checkerboard pattern. To make a handle, scallops must be laid out in two rows. When the basket dries, apply a layer of brown paint from a spray can. Our basket is ready.
