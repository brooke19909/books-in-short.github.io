Hotel "Alpine Valley", Alushta: description, location, rooms and reviews of tourists

The cottage village "Alpine Valley" is located in the small village of Malorechenskoye, which is 27 km from Alushta. Surrounded by the picturesque mountain range of Demerdzhi, this hotel fully lives up to its name - just look at it once. The cottage village is ideal for those who want to spend time in silence, secluded with nature. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are several famous attractions such as Jur-Jur Falls, Ghost Valley, Lighthouse Temple, Funa Fortress, as well as some other interesting places that will not make you bored. The cottage village will give an unforgettable experience of relaxation, and you will definitely want to come back here!

Alpine Valley Hotel (Alushta, Crimea): description

The hotel is a real cottage village, consisting of several small houses in the Alpine style: they are built of wood and resemble Swiss chalets. They are surrounded by well-groomed territory: neat paths from paving slabs, various decorative elements for the lawn, landscaped areas shrubs and flowers. Each step of the guests in the dark is accompanied by bright stylish lights.

The interior of the rooms is calm, conducive to relaxation and complete peace, made in a modern style. It is dominated by neutral shades and natural materials. Public areas are also made in the style of a Swiss chalet.

Rooms: all amenities for guests

Alpine Valley (Alushta) has 56 rooms at its disposal, which are located in several cottages. All of them are comfortable for living, the only difference is that some are larger and, accordingly, equipped a little differently. But each has its own bathroom with constant hot and cold water, air conditioning, a small safe, refrigerator, TV and high-speed Internet.

Entertainment, opportunities for active and passive recreation

Alpine Valley is a fun place. In addition to strolling through the beautiful garden at any time, the hotel offers:

  • Visit the small pebble or VIP beach. It is 450 meters from the hotel, near the entertainment complex "Courage".
  • Take a dip in the pool at the Kamelot Hotel (extra charge).
  • Play checkers, billiards, backgammon, table tennis or chess.
  • Go to the SPA center, Turkish bath or Finnish sauna.
  • Watch a movie in the summer cinema of the entertainment center "Courage".
  • Get rid of excess calories in the gym.
  • Take advantage of the services of a tour desk, ordering an unforgettable walk through the sights.
  • Order a photo shoot.

Services for children

The Alpine Valley Hotel for children has a playground and a special children's menu in the restaurant. Wheelchairs available for walks with the baby. In the summer, animators work with children. Parents can take advantage of babysitting services if necessary.

Hotel for business and events

The Continent Hotel Group, of which this hotel is a part, offers 2 large conference rooms for events: one for 120 people, the second for 300. In addition, the Alpine Valley Hotel has the opportunity to organize a banquet.

Services offered in the complex

It features a restaurant, bar and cafeteria. The price includes meals (at the selected rate). Meals are served on the buffet system, as well as on the selected set menu. At other times, hotel guests can independently choose a place for lunch and dinner, as well as dishes offered by the chef.

  • Beach equipment.

  • Laundry.

Alpine Valley Hotel: reviews

Tourists write that the hotel is ideal for a vacation, here you can relax as your heart desires. Everything is clean and tidy, it can be seen that the staff takes good care of the buildings and the surrounding area, including the beach. The dishes are delicious, the buffet is varied. But there are a few drawbacks:

  • The remoteness of the beach.
  • The buffet serves breakfast only for two hours (from 8 to 10).
  • A small-sized village where it is difficult to find entertainment, so you have to go to Yalta or Alushta.

Useful hotel information

The hotel is open all year. The guest accommodation service is open around the clock, check-in time is from 14:00. Guests must check out no later than 12:00.

The cottage village "Alpine Valley" (Crimea), the reviews of which were written above, accepts children of any age. If the child is not yet 7 years old, he does not need a berth and food, he lives for free. You can also bring your pet with you, after prior coordination with the hotel.

The exact address: Bolshaya Alushta, the village of Malorechenskoye, street Alexey Dizh, 11. The cost of living starts from 3300 rubles per day per room.

To visit the Swiss Alps, it is not necessary to buy a plane ticket. This hotel will allow you to plunge into the mountain atmosphere, even though there is no ski resort here.
