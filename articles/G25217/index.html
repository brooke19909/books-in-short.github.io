Lexx (hotel, Koktebel, Crimea): description of rooms, services, reviews

In our article we want to talk about a new hotel in the Crimea with the interesting name Lexx. It is located in the southeast of the peninsula, not far from Feodosia. The complex was built not so long ago (in 2015), but has already taken a leading position among the best institutions in Crimea.

Location

Lexx is one of the best Crimean hotels. The complex is located in a wonderful place and is surrounded by two unique natural areas: the Kara-Dag nature reserve and Quiet Bay. The hotel, due to its architecture, harmoniously blends with local landscapes. Koktebel, in fact, is a unique place, which has no analogues in the whole world. The city is located at the intersection of the steppe, mountain and sea climate, which makes the air of this region healing.

How to get to the hotel?

It should be noted that the Lexx Hotel (Koktebel), whose location is unique in terms of natural areas, also has a convenient location relative to the transport interchange. Koktebel is located just twenty kilometers from Feodosia and one hundred and fifteen kilometers from Simferopol. The hotel organizes transfers for its guests. Experienced drivers who are well aware of the difficult Crimean tracks will meet tourists at the station or at the airport and quickly deliver to the place of rest.

Number of rooms

Lexx (hotel, Koktebel, Crimea) is ready to offer its guests one hundred and twenty spacious rooms. During the construction and decoration of the hotel, they tried as much as possible to use the best natural materials. Each number is executed in the same style. A characteristic feature for assigning a certain category to apartments is their location (cottage or main building), total area, number of rooms and, of course, the view from the windows.



Most of the one-room rooms are designed so that they can easily be transformed into two-room ones. This effect is achieved due to the presence of double doors in the apartments, which are installed according to the rules of the international system for designing individual rooms (doors have a strong sound insulation).

The rooms have the following equipment: terraces or balconies with high-quality summer furniture, refrigerator, air conditioning, bathroom and shower, slippers, bathrobes, mini-perfumes, safe, hairdryer, telephone, opaque curtains, orthopedic mattresses, beach towels, extra bed.

The price includes: meals at a certain rate, the use of towels for the beach, sun loungers, three swimming pools, parking, children's animation, luggage storage, wake-up service, wireless Internet.
