Matiate Park Hotel 3 * (Kemer, Beldibi): description of rooms, service, reviews

When the time for school holidays and long-awaited holidays is approaching, we are faced with the choice of a place for rest and travel. It's no secret that the most popular destination among Russian tourists in the summer season are considered resorts in Turkey and its entire coast. This country is happy to meet our brother on its territory and organizes for us a great vacation in its many hotels, one of which will be discussed in this article.

Hotel Description

If you consider yourself to be a category of undemanding tourists and want to relax inexpensively, but at the same time feel comfortable, then this accommodation option may well suit you. Matiate Park Hotel 3 * is a small family hotel for an economical vacation. Despite the fact that the hotel is a three-star hotel, it has everything necessary so that every guest does not need anything: an All Inclusive catering system, a swimming pool, animation, a gentle warm sea and a nice beach.

The hotel is located in the village of Beldibi, from which you can reach the center of the party Kemer by regular public transport in 20 minutes. Antalya Airport is only 40 kilometers from the hotel. The hotel consists of a four-story building, built in 2009, has a small landscaped area, the sea is a 10-minute walk. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is 1200 square meters. Two years ago, the building was completely renovated, the rooms were redecorated.

The hotel provides breakfast only. Lunch, dinner and other services according to the All Inclusive concept can be obtained at the 4 * hotel of the same name, which is 300 meters away.

Number of rooms

The rooms in the Matiate Park Hotel 3 * - Standard Room, there are 40 in total, each area is 25 square meters.





The rooms have:

- The balcony.

- Air conditioning.

- TV with Russian-language channels.

- Shower.

- Bath accessories.

- Hair dryer.

Beach area





Beach Matiate Park Hotel 3 * sand and gravel, about a hundred meters long. The distance from the hotel to the sea is about 400 meters. There is a road between the beach and the hotel. The beach area, although small, but clean, umbrellas, mattresses and deck chairs are provided free of charge, towels can be taken on deposit.

Catering

As mentioned above, the hotel provides guests only breakfast from 7 am to 9 and brunch until 10 in the morning. For lunch and dinner, guests have to go to the hotel, which is located next to the Matiate Park Hotel 3 * . In the reviews, many tourists note that this is not very convenient, but for economical citizens this option is quite tolerable. Hotels are located at a distance of about 500 meters from each other, but a shuttle always runs between them. For lunch and dinner, guests are taken by car.

Entertainment

Since the hotel is small and budget, it is not possible to carry out various animation programs for guests on its territory. But each guest of the Matiate Park Hotel 3 * can come to the Matiate Hotel in order to watch or take part in daytime and evening entertainment programs taking place on its territory.





Also, guests are not forbidden to swim in pools, ride with water slides, visit the hammam and the Matiate 4 * wellness center.

Infrastructure

As for the infrastructure of the Matiate Park Hotel 3 * hotel, it is not very different from other budget hotels in Turkey. Doctor services, internet connection, water sports, laundry are available for a fee. Free here you can use cots, high chairs in the restaurant, play table tennis. Also in the hotel there are adult and children's pools, shops, car parking, currency exchange. A neighboring club has a kids' club and a playground.

Tourists reviews

Reviews about Matiate Park Hotel 3 * say that this is a very modest hotel with a minimum number of services. Among the advantages, vacationers highlight the silence, calm and lack of clutter in the hotel, a small number of vacationers, the presence of warm pools and the absence of queues near the bar, their good location and gorgeous mountain views.

You have to eat and go to the hotel, which, according to tourists, is far away. The food is all monotonous, there is not enough fruit and pastries, you need to come to the dining room on time.





The beach at the hotel, according to vacationers, is small, the entrance to the sea is on stones or from the pier, special shoes are needed, umbrellas are broken.

Many people come here to save money, because the prices of this hotel are very attractive. A voucher for two with a weekly stay and all-inclusive meals can be purchased for 25-35 thousand rubles along with a flight. The Russians, vacationers here, compare their holidays with holidays in the Krasnodar Territory or in the Crimea and note that the time spent in this hotel reminds them of holidays in Russian resorts, only it costs much less.

Otherwise, tourists express a lot of dissatisfaction with the service Matiate Park Hotel 3 *. Many note that you have to wait a very long time to check in to the room, the rooms themselves are small, there are problems with the operation of the equipment, cleaning for the top three. The road to the beach and to Matiate 4 * takes a lot of time. There is nothing special to do in the hotel, only the warm sea and excursions save.

Excursions from the hotel

As for excursions, you can go anywhere from the Park Hotel . In the village itself, in addition to the Mosque and the Wild Beach, there is nothing to see, but there are a lot of field trips here. You can order them both at hotel guides and at street agencies. The quality is the same, but the prices vary greatly.

Most popular tours

Pamukkale

A pearl in the collection of Turkish excursions. You will get acquainted with a natural monument, a slope with snow-white sediment travertine filled with emerald water, swimming in the Cleopatra basin and visiting the ruins of the city of Hierapolis. The cost of the tour is 50 US dollars.





Demre - Mira - Kekova

A fascinating journey to the ancient city of Mira, a visit to the rock tombs and the Greco-Roman amphitheater. Then, on a yacht, you will be taken to the island of Kekova, where, scuba diving under water, you can see the ruins of settlements that were destroyed after the earthquake. The tour price is $ 35.

Cappadocia

The heart of Turkey. Here you can see volcanoes covered with snow, fertile valleys, rock formations of amazing shape, cave settlements and famous underground cities. The price of the program is $ 80.

Istanbul

The tour allows you to travel from Beldibi to the famous metropolis of Turkey. Here you will see the sights and monuments of the city, the Hagia Sophia Museum, Hippodrome Square. Also visit fur and leather factories, the Egyptian Bazaar. The tour price is about 200 dollars.

Termessos

The ancient city with the ruins of temples surrounded by dense deciduous forest. The ruins of the famous amphitheater can be explored on your own using taxi services or a rented car.

Phaselis

An archaeological park located near the village of Beldibi, where, according to the stories, Alexander the Great is buried.

Conclusion

If you are looking for an inexpensive hotel in Kemer or Beldibi, Matiate Park Hotel 3 * can suit you only if you consider yourself a completely undemanding tourist who came for a beach holiday and can close his eyes to a number of shortcomings. Judging by the reviews, this "troika" is suitable only for very unpretentious vacationers.
