Hotels of Engels, Saratov Region

On the left bank of the Volga, three kilometers from the regional city of Saratov, the small town of Engels is located. Some even consider it a continuation of Saratov, because they are separated from each other just by a bridge across the river. But in reality, life in Engels is calmer and more measured, and there are more picturesque green places. Perhaps this is why some tourists and business travelers prefer hotels in Engels to Saratov hotels .





4 or 5 stars

The stardom of Engels hotels is different. The bulk of them have three stars, but there are also Engels hotels of the "Lux" and "Super Business" class.

The Volzhino country recreation center (five stars) is located 6 km from the city center. Its feature is the placement of guests not only in the general hotel building, but the ability to choose a separate house from a timber. On the territory of Volzhino there is a restaurant and a bath complex. Since April, an outdoor pool with heated water has been operating. And in the cold season, guests can sit in the fireplace room, talk, have dinner or even sing karaoke. Volzhino is often chosen for corporate events and weddings.

The four-star Park-Hotel New Century offers its guests various types of rooms: from standard to premium rooms. The price varies from 950 rubles to 15,000 rubles. Even the simplest room is equipped with everything you need - a bed, a fridge, TV, a bathroom. In addition to ordinary rooms, the hotel has an equipped conference room for up to 60 people. In the "New Century" there is a restaurant and cafe, as well as karaoke and a sauna.





In addition, the park-hotel provides a service of exit registration of marriage with a beautiful arch and a tent, a red carpet and places for guests. All this pleasure will cost about 20 thousand rubles.

And here Russian stars stop: Nyusha, Margarita Sukhankina, Vlad Topalov and others.
