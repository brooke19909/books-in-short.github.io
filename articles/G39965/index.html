Old times

Okhotny Ryad is a metro station that got its name from the street of the same name. Previously, there was also an area with the same name. For 18-19 centuries, there were shops where hunters could share their catch. It was possible to get bird meat and excellent game.

During the nineteenth century they were engaged only in trade, they left goods in warehouses. There was an opportunity to live in a hotel or visit a tavern. When the year 1956 arrived, the square that was here was re-planned into the street, which from 1961 to 1990 was a segment of Marx Ave.
