Holidays in Sochi in February: photos and reviews

Winter Sochi pleasantly surprises with its desertness and tranquility. The streets, which in July were not crowded, freeze in anticipation of cold nights.

The period of numbness in the city lasts only a couple of months, because Sochi is a popular ski resort. What surprises did the winter city prepare for tourists? What can I see and where to go at this time of year?





In Sochi! In February?

For the first acquaintance with the capital of the Olympic Games in 2014, an unhurried walk through the historic quarter is the best choice. February Sochi impresses with the brightness of the flowers of the lush greenery of tropical vegetation.

Trees and palms that had withered in August prettified themselves and greet travelers with a dark gloss of fleshy leaves. Here and there, couples of lovers, languidly holding hands, stroll leisurely. Parents with children impressively walk.

Beach life

In Sochi, in February, beaches are as crowded and fun as in June. True, the colorful tents selling slates, towels and panamas replace the counters, clogged to the top with warm hats, gloves and scarves with a sports theme.

Cats scrawled from the southern sun scurry around, and seagulls dive into the sea somewhere nearby. Photos of tourists are the best proof of this.

No less noisy in seaside restaurants. They lure idle passersby with the spicy aroma of barbecue. Street verandas and terraces are still open.

Bicycle and scooter rentals are available. Barkers are inviting for boat trips, children's carousels are spinning and spinning.

For entertainment at the Riviera

If the rides on the embankment suddenly seem a little to you, feel free to go to the "Riviera". This is the central park of the city, which operates year-round. In Sochi, only water slides were closed in February.

The Ferris wheel, like in the summer, tirelessly uplifts vacationers into the blue abyss of the Caucasian sky. "Pirate ship" takes the children into the world of exciting and dizzying adventures.

A chain carousel dizzy with colorful ponies, donkeys and elephants harnessed to smart carts. Kids frolic on inflatable trampolines, ride in the colorful cars of the "Merry Train", screaming and laughing, riding horses.

Sochi National Park

Sochi National Park, according to the numerous reviews of travelers, is another point that must be included in the route when planning a tour to Sochi in February.

On its territory, the famous Vorontsov Mountains rise, in the depths of which mysterious caves are hidden from prying eyes. Here even in winter you can meet wild leopards, enjoy the contemplation of kites soaring in the sky, and see with your own eyes the bison.

The park is home to hundreds of birds, including graceful storks. Excursions are conducted by experienced guides.

Arboretum

The arboretum is part of the structural unit of the Sochi National Park. Residents of the city claim that winter in Sochi is by no means a reason to refuse to walk around the local reserves.

The heart of the arboretum is a swan lake. Graceful birds never leave their homes, admiring the audience with their snow-white plumage.

Even in February, you can wander along the arboretum's paths all day long. Oaks, palm trees, cypresses, pines and cacti grow in the reserve. Not far from the pond stands the villa of Sergei Nikolaevich Khudekov, the founding father of the park.

At the guests' disposal are four attractions at once, from which, without exaggeration, the heart beats. We are talking about the very "bungees" that are known to everyone. But here they hang right in the gorge, in the depths of which fog swirls.

Olympic Park

The Olympic Park, or Imereti Resort, is the last station of the Swallows, coming from Tuapse and Sochi. Regardless of the weather, the Olympic Park hosts competitions, concerts and even performances.

Within a short drive from sports facilities, the borders of the Southern Cultures Park, another unit of the Sochi National Reserve, stretch.

Guided tours that remain after the 2014 Winter Games are held both on foot and on electric vehicles. Experienced travelers recommend using the latter option or renting bicycles.
