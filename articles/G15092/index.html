Karon Cliff Bungalows 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): hotel description, services, reviews

In the magnificent resort city of Phuket, a huge number of hotel complexes and hotels are concentrated, which in terms of quality of service can only be compared with establishments for the rest of European tourists.

One of these places of accommodation for travelers is a hotel called Karon Cliff Contemporary Boutique Bungalows 3 * category 3 stars.

Short description

The hotel has a huge number of rooms, each room of which is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Very close to this territory is Karon Beach, the central part of Phuket Town and the Mali Embankment.
Externally, the Karon Cliff Contemporary Bungalows 3 * hotel is made in all the traditions of the modern stylistic direction, although it was built a long time ago, in 2009.

Great location

The epicenter of all the events of the city of Phuket is located at a distance of 2 kilometers from the hotel, which makes it possible to have a quiet stay for the audience of any age, but at the same time makes it easier to visit the sights, as tourist buses regularly move in this direction.

Literally a stone's throw from the hotel there are numerous museums and temples, and when you exit the street, a truly breathtaking mountain landscape opens up.

There is the possibility of flights by air, as the local airport is only half an hour away by car to the hotel.
