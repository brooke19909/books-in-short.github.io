Village Novomikhailovsky: beach, sea, vacation

The village of Novomikhailovsky has long gained authority among vacationers along with Tuapse and Gelendzhik. Its location is not far from the Nechepsuho River, which flows directly into the Black Sea. Also nearby is the famous Cape Bloodless. That Tuapse, that Gelendzhik are in the immediate vicinity of the village, namely 40 and 50 kilometers respectively. Vacationers will not have to get bored, because they have at their disposal a beach holiday on the magnificent sandy shore of the village of Novomikhailovsky (the beach here is considered one of the best in the Tuapse district), and an active type of vacation with fascinating excursions, the price of which, by the way, is quite acceptable. In addition, a wide variety of entertainment centers are offered to tourists.

Climatic conditions of the resort





Located between two large cities, the village of Novomikhailovsky combines a subtropical and hot climate, similar to the conditions on the Mediterranean Sea. Good weather here is almost all year round, starting from the end of April, and ending with the end of October. Rains are very rare, and their duration does not exceed several minutes. Novomikhailovsky has a huge number of places to accommodate vacationers, however, like the entire Tuapse district. Another point worth mentioning: the village is located between two hills that cover it from the wind, so there are never storms. As many as 240 days a year you can enjoy the warm sun here. This is what caused an unprecedented influx of tourists who want to improve their health and enjoy the rustle of waves and the sea breeze. There is another trump card in the village of Novomikhailovsky. The beach here is one of the cleanest.

Infrastructure

Novomikhailovsky is a very well-developed resort with a high level of service for tourists. On the seashore there are a huge number of cafes and restaurants with all the cuisines of the world, bars, shops, nightclubs and other entertainment venues. All along the coast, vacationers are available all kinds of recreation centers and pensions, where you can settle in very comfortably. In any hotel, regardless of class, there is access to a Wi-Fi network, which allows you to always stay in touch with family and friends.

Beaches of Novomikhailovsky





The biggest reason for pride of the indigenous people of Novomikhailovsky resort is the beach. Sandy beaches here are not inferior in cleanliness and comfort even to the famous Turkish with golden sand. The central beach of the village has a width of 35 meters and a length of 800 meters, respectively. The beach area is located at the place where the river flows into the Black Sea. In this place, the sea has a very flat bottom, which allows even small children to swim there (naturally, under the supervision of parents!) The beach has two types of coverings - pebbles and sand, which many vacationers like. There is everything you need for a good rest: boats, water skiing, scooters, catamarans, motor boats and other water vehicles, where you can ride and get a lot of positive emotions. On the promenade adjacent to the beach, there are a huge number of children's attractions, restaurants, cafes, nightclubs and other institutions.




The picturesque coastline is amazing! Between the steep cliffs there is a cable car and funicular, a ride on which you can enjoy a bird's-eye view of the panorama. Not far from this place there is a yacht club where you can rent a yacht and organize a romantic boat trip. For lovers of nudism a little further from the promenade is a wild beach in Novomikhailovsky, where you can take topless sunbathing, sitting on a comfortable sun lounger and covering your head with a special umbrella.

Beach "Gold Coast"





In order to describe this place, the use of lush vocabulary is inappropriate, since everything speaks for itself. Golden Beach (Novomikhailovsky) is equipped with everything necessary for a friendly or family, and for a romantic getaway. There are all the necessary attributes, sunbeds, sports equipment, catamarans and more. The beach is spread over as much as 10 kilometers, which allows everyone to find a corner of privacy.

Rest in the village of Novomikhailovsky has a special magnetism, and all because there is a place for everyone, including large families, and senior couples, and representatives of young people and middle-aged people. How to get there

There are 3 ways to get to this wonderful place: by bus with taxi transfers, by plane to Tuapse, then by taxi and by train.

Arriving in the village of Novomikhailovsky - the beach - the first thing that immediately catches your eye.

Having lived here for at least a few days, not a single person will remain indifferent, and even want to visit this place again! The magnificent landscape design of the resort, beautiful beaches, various forms of leisure, suitable for both adults and children of all ages - this is the formula for the best vacation! As for cash costs, the resort is quite economical when compared with the same Anapa or Sochi. Payment for services is made either in cash or by credit cards of any bank. Sanatoriums of the coast have a system of 5 meals a day, and some hotels offer guests the all-inclusive mode, which is popular when visiting Turkish and Egyptian resorts. All rooms are carefully cleaned daily, a service is provided to deliver food directly to the room (if a person cannot or does not want to go to the dining room). Many hotels have playrooms where parents can leave their children under qualified supervision for a while. Also, each hotel has its own medical center, where you can get timely medical care of any complexity. Arriving in Novomikhailovsky, a person will remember his vacation forever.
