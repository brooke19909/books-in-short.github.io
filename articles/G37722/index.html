Ski resort Bansko (Bulgaria). Ski resort Bansko: prices, reviews

Winter inspires dreams of snowy mountainsides and the possibility of skiing. On the eve of the most magical holiday, a trip to another country to one of the resorts will be most welcome. You just need to decide on the route. Why not book a tour to Bansko? Bulgaria's ski resort attracts many tourists - both experienced skiers and those who are just starting their friendship with this sport.

Where is Bansko

The resort, attracting many guests, is located near the Bulgarian capital. 160 km separates it from Sofia and 110 km from Plovdiv. And just 250 km are the popular Greek resorts, considered the highlight of the Aegean.

But they appreciate him not for being close to civilization, but for his amazing location. It is located at the foot of Pirin Mountain on the northeast side. This mountain range is known to many thanks to the Vihren peak, whose height is almost 3000 meters. And tourists are attracted here not only by convenient slopes and comfortable lifts, but also by the Pirinsky Reserve. Unesco even ignored it and took it under protection.

Climate and season features

Excellent conditions for recreation are offered by the ski resort of Bansko (Bulgaria). The reviews of those who have been there are full of words of admiration for the picturesque landscapes and the weather. The fact is that on three sides this city is surrounded by majestic mountain peaks. Therefore, there are practically no cold winds that interfere with skiing from the mountains. But the resort is always sunny, and the temperature on the plains very rarely drops below -3 degrees. In the mountains, it can reach -15.

About Bansko they say that here is the longest winter and the shortest summer. This affects the season. The ski resort of Bansko welcomes those wishing to ski from the mountainside from mid-December to mid-April. Snow cover here reaches two meters. And when the snow begins to melt, special guns come to the rescue, helping to extend the season.

How to get to the famous resort

Unfortunately, there are no direct flights. But due to the fact that the ski resort of Bansko is located in close proximity to major cities of the country, it will not be difficult to get to it.

Regular buses run from Sofia and Plovdiv. Travel time can be 3-4 hours. If you take a taxi, the trip will be faster. You can get to the resort in 2 hours.

  2. Banderishka Polyana. A place where excellent conditions for cross-country skiing are created. You can also do biathlon here.
  3. Shiligarnik. The highest area, which is conveniently located near the top of Todorin Vrakh. There are many tracks on which constant slalom competitions are held.

Lifts in Bansko

The developed infrastructure and the convenience of equipped tracks attract both Russians and Europeans. The ski resort of Bansko (Bulgaria) has as many as 26 lifts.



  • First of all, I want to note the gondola-type cableway along the Bansko-Banderishka route. It is designed for 8 passengers.
  • 8 chairlifts equipped with removable cabins. 5 of them are 4-seater, the rest are 3-seater.
  • 17 drag lifts.

In addition, there is also a halfpipe where you can make jumps and perform various tricks. Snowboarders will not be left idle either. A park has been created especially for them, and equipment can be rented.

In addition, the resort offers a test of strength in biathlon, you can also visit the gym and pool.

Features of children's leisure

If you have chosen the ski resort of Bansko (Bulgaria), bring your children with you. Little travelers will not be deprived of attention. Especially for them there are lifts. And at the station Banderishka Polyana there is even a ski kindergarten. There you can take the child if he is already over 4 years old. Also in this recreation area are the best attractions for children: moving paths, ski carousels.
