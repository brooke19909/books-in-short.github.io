How to get to the Asteroid DOL?

The camp is located in the city of Ussuriysk. There are several ways to get to the place:

  • It can be reached by public transport. both by train and by bus number 501. You can take a taxi directly to the camp.
  • To get there by car, you need to go along the route along the M60 highway.

In the Asteroid camp in Ussuriysk, travel and transfers were not organized. Therefore, you need to choose the most suitable option for delivering a son or daughter to the gates of the magic camp.

Children accommodation

Upon arrival at the health camp, children are assigned to groups according to age. Depending on which detachment the child is in, they are either in a four- or six-seater room. Each room is equipped with the following pieces of furniture:

  • Beds in how many children live in the room.
  • Bedside tables for storing personal hygiene items and things that you want to have on hand.
  • There is a large closet. Therefore, boys and girls will be able to fold all their clothes and not keep them in their travel bags.
  • Also in each room there is a sanitary unit.

Living in rooms is comfortable for children of all ages. After all, the premises are equipped in such a way that it is spacious and comfortable.

Package of documents for admission to the camp

When sending a child to the Asteroid camp in Ussuriysk, by phone you can find out the exact package of documents for joining a son or daughter in the ranks of vacationers. The standard list of securities is as follows:

  • Child's document (birth certificate or passport).
  • Proof of identity of the parent or guardian who brought the child to the camp.
  • Certificate from a medical institution. The certificate must indicate chronic diseases, paste the test results, and also indicate all the patient data that is necessary for the camp medical worker.
  • If the child is insured, be sure to present the original and provide a copy of the medical policy.

This is a standard package of documents. If necessary, the responsible DOL staff may request additional papers for admission of the child to the children's recreation camp.
