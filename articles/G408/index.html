Admiral Hotel in Arkhipo-Osipovka: description, reviews of tourists

Many tourists advise visiting the resort village of Arkhipo-Osipovka on the Black Sea. This southern settlement attracts vacationers with its lovely, cozy streets and courtyards, all kinds of entertainments and, of course, the clear sea and convenient beaches. The private sector in this village is represented by rather comfortable hotels. For example, tourists can rent rooms here at the Admiral Hotel. In Arkhipo-Osipovka this hotel is quite popular among vacationers.

Where is the complex located?

This hotel is located in the village, almost in its very center. Its exact address is as follows: Arkhipo-Osipovka, Gelendzhik, Sovetskaya, 46a. The nearest grocery store is about a 5-minute walk from this hotel. In 10 minutes, guests can reach the market. Public transport is also just a 10-minute walk from the complex. There is a pharmacy next to this hotel. Residents of the complex can reach it in about 8 minutes.





The Admiral Guest House is not too far from the beach. Its guests can reach the sea in about 10 minutes.

General description of the hotel

This hotel accepts residents in one stylishly decorated multi-storey building. The courtyard of the Admiral complex (Arkhipo-Osipovka) is tiled and well landscaped. Unfortunately, the pools are not equipped on the territory of most inexpensive mini-hotels of domestic Black Sea resorts. The Admiral complex is a pleasant exception in this regard. A swimming pool is available on its territory. Moreover, it is quite large in size.

On all sides, the courtyard of the hotel is surrounded by a reliable brick fence. This is especially convenient for hotel guests who have come to rest here with their children.

To book rooms in the Admiral complex, as well as practically in all other guest houses of Arkhipo-Osipovka , tourists should pre-season in season. There are enough people wishing to settle here in June-September. Residents, of course, should not observe any special rules on the territory of the hotel. The only thing - the administration of the complex forbids its guests to smoke in the rooms. Pets are also not allowed in this hotel.





What rooms are provided?

The size of the Admiral Hotel is relatively small. In total, 16 rooms are for rent in this hotel. Tourists can rent in this complex 1-4-bed rooms equipped with:

  • air conditioners;

  • TVs

  • refrigerators.

All rooms rented at the hotel include free Wi-Fi. Bathrooms in the hotel rooms are equipped with individual. Beach facilities can be enjoyed on the balconies.

Guest room reviews

The rooms in the Admiral Hotel (Arkhipo-Osipovka) , as noted by its guests, are for rent quite decent. Hotel workers cleaned up here quite often and carefully. In any case, the rooms of this complex do not look too dirty. Regarding the convenience of furniture, guests also have no complaints about the hotel administration. Beds, for example, are wide and soft. The maids at this hotel change linens frequently.

The availability of air conditioners and refrigerators in hotel rooms, of course, tourists also relate to its advantages.





Complex infrastructure

A certain number of amenities for holidaymakers who have chosen Arkhipo-Osipovka for their summer holidays, is offered at Admiral Mini - Hotel , of course, and directly on its territory. In addition to the pool, for the convenience of guests in this complex:

  • laundry;

  • Ironing

  • playground.

There is in this hotel, as in almost all other guest houses of Arkhipo-Osipovka, and its own parking. Tourists can leave cars here for free.

Guest House Admiral (Arkhipo-Osipovka): reviews on the service provided

In most cases, on the websites of tour operators offering hotel reservations for hotels in Black Sea resorts, the Admiral Hotel praises vacationers in their reviews. Tourists attribute the courtesy of its staff to the advantages of this complex. The hotel workers relate to their guests, judging by the reviews, quite friendly and helpful.





Most tourists, of course, consider the presence of a swimming pool a huge plus for this complex. Particularly pleased with the opportunity to swim on a hot day in cool water are vacationers who come to Arkhipo-Osipovka at the beginning or end of the season. That is, when the sea in this village is still cool. In the pool, the water in any case warms up during the day in the sun much faster.

Actually, the territory of this hotel itself is very large. Including thanks to this, tourists consider the rest in the Admiral (Arkhipo-Osipovka) quite convenient. Sun loungers and umbrellas installed next to the pool, guests of this hotel, of course, can use absolutely free.

Dining at the hotel

Residents of the Admiral complex (Arkhipo-Osipovka) can cook their meals directly on its territory. In addition, a large summer kitchen is equipped in the courtyard of the hotel. Also, hotel guests can fry themselves a barbecue or barbecue at any time. As in many other Black Sea hotels, the Admiral complex also includes a barbecue.

What is a hotel beach?

To the sea, residents of the Admiral complex in Arkhipo-Osipovka, as already mentioned, do not have to go too long. The beach opposite this hotel is located, judging by the reviews, quite comfortable - pebble. Tourists can sunbathe on the sea in this place, including simply on rugs. But if desired, vacationers have the opportunity to rent beach chairs and Arkhipo-Osipovka.





Tourists praise the sea itself in the village. Experienced vacationers advise travelers to Arkhipo-Osipovka to those travelers who do not like to swim in cold water. Near the coast in this place on the Black Sea there seems to be a warm current. Therefore, the water on the beach in this village usually has a very comfortable bathing temperature.
