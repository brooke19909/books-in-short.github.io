How to choose a tablet

Over the past two decades, technological progress has burst into a whirlwind and has not subsided. Even computer specialists cannot understand such an abundance of new products. This is an amazing fact, but many have to admit it. Ordinary consumers do not need so much knowledge. But when the decision matures to acquire a new thing, they involuntarily think and get lost among the huge selection of models offered by the industry. Let us try briefly and, in fact, to understand this difficult issue.


How is a tablet different from a computer or phone? Is this a small laptop? Some people think that the tablet is an advanced e-book. The young generation, born already adapted for new technologies, does not have difficulties with definitions, not everyone was so lucky. Therefore, we will deal seriously. The history of occurrence, transformation and processions around the world will be left out of the picture. Let's call it clear and simple. A tablet is a handheld computer. Computer and notepad. Choose a tablet you need to start with its size. Specialists call this screen size, measured in diagonals. A small diagonal of 5-6 is often for portable tablets, which, in essence, is no different from a phone, only with advanced features. Diagonal 7-8 is an average tablet, and it ceases to be a toy, it can already be used professionally to solve complex problems. The latter, which is considered, it is also with the largest diagonal of 9-10. This is a classic functional tablet. It has optimal dimensions.

In addition to the size of the diagonal, you must not forget to clarify the screen resolution. There are several standard ones, and the rule works here, the higher the better. For simplicity, you can pay attention to the technical characteristics of expensive models, and it is precisely in these parameters that you can choose. Because display quality is fifty percent of the success. This is the appearance and perception of the color gamut, contrast, glare.
