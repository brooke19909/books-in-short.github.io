Hotel "October", Kursk, Russia: description, rooms and reviews

If you like the classic style in the interior and a high level of comfort, then the Oktyabrskaya Hotel is just perfect for you. Kursk is a hospitable city that the staff of this hotel will happily demonstrate to you.



Brief description of the hotel

The Oktyabrskaya Hotel has a rather rich and interesting history. Kursk boasts a large number of ancient buildings, one of which is occupied by this hotel. Every year, hotel employees make it more and more modern and introduce more and more new services, thanks to which the stay of tourists becomes even more comfortable.





You do not have to deal with frayed walls, old furniture and leaking plumbing if you decide to stay here when you arrive in Kursk. Reconstruction of the hotel "October" was carried out relatively recently - in 2009. At the same time, the classic interior was completely preserved, which was so loved by the employees and guests of this institution. It is also worth noting that the rooms are equipped with all the necessary amenities that a modern traveler needs.

If we talk about nutrition, then the Oktyabrskaya Hotel (Kursk) also took care of this. Breakfast is served in the restaurant every morning, as well as the possibility of ordering a comprehensive meal. If you feel hungry during the day, light snacks and drinks will be available at the lobby bar. You also have the opportunity to organize a banquet in the restaurant of this hotel on the occasion of a holiday or special occasion.





Hotel Location

Quite a favorable location is characterized by the hotel "October". Kursk is a large city, and this institution is located in its very center, at 72 Lenin Street. Thus, you will have the opportunity to quickly access a variety of infrastructure and tourist facilities. The international airport is approximately 12 kilometers from the hotel.

Hotel Rooms

Hotel "October" (Kursk) offers its guests to stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard is a double one-room apartment, which is equipped with the minimum necessary set of amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. In addition to the bed, there is furniture such as a desk and chairs. The bathroom has a shower and a washbasin.

  • The family room is designed to accommodate three guests, but taking into account additional beds this indicator can be increased. There are two beds (double and one and a half), as well as folding upholstered furniture.

  • Studio apartments are a spacious room, which is divided into a bedroom and a living room by means of decorative zoning. The latter also has a small dining area with table and chairs. The bathroom is equipped with a jacuzzi.

  • Two-room apartments are a separate bedroom and living room. The first is quite compact and equipped with a double bed. If we talk about the living room, then its main feature can be considered a fireplace. Also, you can not ignore the presence of kitchen and dining areas. It is worth noting the presence of two bathrooms.

  • The suite category number consists of three full rooms. In the bedroom, in addition to a large bed, there is also a dressing table with a mirror. The living room is combined with the dining room as well as the kitchen area. The role of the third room is a full-fledged study (that is why this option of accommodation is often chosen by business people). It is worth noting that the room is equipped with two bathrooms.





The amenities provided in the apartment

If you like comfort, then you need to carefully choose the hotel. Oktyabrskaya Hotel (Kursk) offers its guests to use the following amenities in the apartments:

  • a full set of comfortable orthopedic furniture;
  • modern TV with a wide flat screen, which makes it possible to view several dozen information channels;
  • depending on the category of apartments, the bathroom is equipped with a modern shower with a hydromassage or a jacuzzi;
  • thanks to the presence of a refrigerator, your products will always remain fresh;
  • individual air conditioning allows you to independently adjust the temperature in your room;
  • Executive apartments have kitchen appliances, which include an electric kettle, microwave and stove for preparing full-value meals.




Hotel facilities

Many travelers and business people find such an accommodation option as the Oktyabrskaya 3 * Hotel attractive. Kursk offers many opportunities for guests, but the availability of developed infrastructure in the hotel itself is also extremely important. So, in this institution the following objects will be available to you:

  • a restaurant of traditional Russian cuisine, which can be considered one of the best in the whole city;
  • in the bar you can drink your favorite drinks and satisfy your hunger with light snacks;
  • throughout the hotel you can use wireless Internet absolutely free;
  • next to the hotel building there is a paid private parking with 24-hour security;
  • reception staff at any time of the day or night will help you to resolve issues related to accommodation;
  • there is an ATM in the lobby through which you can withdraw cash from your plastic cards;
  • in the laundry your clothes are quickly and efficiently washed and ironed;
  • There are several outlets where you can buy products, hygiene items, as well as souvenirs.

Facilities for hotel guests

Choosing an accommodation option like the Oktyabrskaya Hotel (Kursk, Russia), you can count on receiving the following list of services:

  • if necessary, part of bureaucratic procedures can be eliminated for an additional fee, which will significantly speed up the registration process;
  • You have the opportunity to order dishes from the restaurant directly to your room;
  • assistance is provided in the organization of sightseeing and study tours in the city;
  • if you need to purchase or book tickets, the hotel staff will help you solve this issue;
  • at the reception you can use the photocopying service, as well as send a message by fax;
  • for newlyweds a room with a romantic decor, as well as a present in the form of champagne and fruits;
  • if you are going on an excursion or if you are planning a busy working day, then order a lunch box in the restaurant to have a snack during the day;
  • if necessary, you can be provided with a transfer (paid service).




Useful information for guests

This hotel has the following rules regarding accommodation:

  • Check-out time at this hotel is set for noon (time can be changed individually for an additional fee if there are free rooms);
  • children under 6 years old stay at the hotel for free;
  • you have the opportunity to come with your pets to such an institution as the Oktyabrskaya Hotel, Kursk (phone for clarification of conditions - +7 (4712) 707-707);
  • guests have the opportunity to pay for services through plastic cards;
  • during a hot water outage, the cost of living is reduced.




Reviews: advantages

The following positive aspects can be distinguished in this hotel:

  • the hotel is characterized by a very convenient location in the central part of the city;
  • breakfasts are very tasty and hearty;
  • The rooms have special sets for making tea and coffee;
  • the bedding is new, clean and smells very nice;
  • polite and helpful staff who strive to please guests in every possible way;
  • the rooms are very bright and spacious;
  • Wireless Internet works quickly and without interruptions;
  • On each floor there is a cooler with cold and hot drinking water;
  • good soundproofing in the room;
  • high-quality daily cleaning;
  • quick check-in without unnecessary delays.




disadvantages

Of the negative comments, the following should be noted:

  • it seems that the hotel has been undergoing ongoing repairs for several years, which causes a lot of inconvenience to guests;
  • the hotel has a total lack of towels;
  • the drain in the bathroom does not work well;
  • in some rooms the air conditioners do not work (while the cost of living is not reduced);
  • the carpet in both the rooms and the corridors is very dirty;
  • the repair was done in very bad faith, and therefore wallpaper and false ceilings leave in some places;
  • ventilation works very poorly.
