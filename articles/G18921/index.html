Interesting places in Sochi: Mount Akhun, singing fountains, waterfalls, Riviera Park, Olympic Village

On the expanses of our country there are outlandish corners in which exotic palm trees and flowers of extraordinary beauty are basking in the southern sun. Mountains covered with greenery and blue waves - all this is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, which does not descend from the TV screens. Interesting places in Sochi can be described endlessly because they simply can not be counted.

This unique city combines relic groves, tea plantations, picturesque waterfalls, as well as museums and protected areas. We will try to describe in this article the most interesting places in Sochi.

Mount Akhun

Amazing beauty opens when you look at the city and its surroundings from a bird's eye view. Mount Akhun is best suited for this . How to get to this picturesque place will be described below. If you look at the green ridge from the side of the city of Sochi, then an association with the volcano may occur, but it will be erroneous. This is an anticline, which was formed from calcareous rocks and sandstones of the Matsesta and Khosta river valleys. The mountain rivers Khosta and Agur continued along their depressions.





Picturesque places

Matsesta springs rich in hydrogen sulfide are especially appreciated, the healing properties of which are famous for the Sochi resort. In the place where the Agura River leaves the gorge, there are similar healing springs. Of the mountain attractions of Sochi, the most interesting is Mount Akhun. How to get to its very top? You can do this in several ways: by car, by bus or on foot.

The flora of Mount Akhun is very diverse. From the southern slopes in the sun there is an oak forest (ash and linden trees are found in the lowlands). The shady north side is covered with beech (in some places there is an undergrowth in which laurel cherry and hazel grow). The mountain looks different from this, depending on where in the city you look at it: either a green ridge or a regular cone that crowns the surrounding low mountains.

origin of name

Singing Fountains

The most beautiful attraction is considered to be the singing fountains - Sochi is famous for such entertainments. This is truly a magnificent sight when, with the onset of evening, lights envelop a fiery bird, and the dance of water and music begins.

This is the legacy of the Olympics. When the holiday season begins, the show takes place every evening in the Olympic Park. Now the singing fountains are the hallmark of the city.





The fountain is represented by a large bowl (diameter 75 m), which the bird hugs. Hence the name - ‚ÄúOlympic Fire Bowl‚Äù. The construction of this facility was specially dedicated to the opening and closing ceremonies of the Sochi Olympic Games.

264 fountains make up this complex, and for each individual lighting is provided. The magic "bird" comes to life under the influence of the control panel.

Real fairy tale

Some interesting places in Sochi can be visited several times a day. The impression of them will be completely different. With the onset of the evening, the park of singing fountains is the embodiment of a fairy tale. Illuminated jets soar into the sky like fireworks. And the fog that forms above the surface of the water brings a mysterious atmosphere.





If you want to plunge into the magical world, the singing fountains (Sochi) will help in this: a fairy tale will come to your life to the melodies of The Nutcracker, Swan Lake, Ruslana and Lyudmila, Eugene Onegin, familiar from childhood.

Photography and video are allowed, but in order to feel all the beauty and scale, you need to see this spectacle live.

Park "Riviera"

Translated from Italian riviera - "coast". The park was founded by a well-known businessman, an honorary citizen of Yegoryevsk, Vasily Alekseevich Khludov, and now this place is one of the most popular among spa residents. The park is located in the very center of the city, on the coast of the Sochi River. This place is just created for those who want to enjoy the most delicious kebabs and fried red mullet.





Riviera Park is the best place to relax. In this park, anyone will find something to their taste: educational places - from cinemas, exhibition halls, a dolphinarium and a penguinarium to the library, as well as corners of romance - a reconciliation bench and a fountain of love. The Green Theater hosts various concerts and shows. There are also many cafes and bars where you can let go of yourself and dance to your heart's content.

Waterfalls

Describing the sights, one cannot but say about the waterfalls that are found throughout the entire Sochi Natural National Park and Krasnaya Polyana in the Agursky Gorge. These and other interesting places in Sochi, which are worth seeing, leave an imprint on your memory forever.

The peculiarity of Zmeykovsky waterfalls is that they, thanks to the nutrition of the springs, always function. More than others, the Emerald Falls, and especially its crystal drinking water.





The highest mountain is the canyon keeper waterfall. Women who dream of becoming mothers must definitely visit the Bowl of Rose waterfall, which has magical effects.

The most unstable of all - the tandem waterfall. During the spill of the river feeding it, it is divided into two full-flowing streams. Its waters are healing: if you take a shower under a stream of waterfall for 40 minutes, the healing effect will be equal to several hydromassage sessions.

A tour is the best option to not only see all the waterfalls, but also learn a lot of interesting things about these places.

Olympic Village

In order to see all the beauty of the Black Sea coast, you should go on vacation in Sochi. The Olympic village is ready to receive guests almost all year round. This hotel complex was built near the Olympic Park. This is a comfortable resort with 47 buildings that occupy an area of ‚Äã‚Äã72 hectares and are ready to accommodate 3,000 vacationers.





The Olympic village consists of recreational complexes and walking areas, landscapes and views of which delight tourists. Respectable restaurants, cafes and places of relaxation are provided, special attention is paid to the issue of comfort of people with disabilities.

What is the success of the Olympic Village?

Interesting places in Sochi are not only natural attractions and entertainment. There are still places saturated with a certain atmosphere. Of particular interest is the concept itself: the goal of the designers was to unravel the mystery of the soul of the Russian people and to present as much as possible the things and phenomena that are traditional for Russia. All this was presented in the form of a synthesis of the latest technology and classics.

On the territory of the Olympic Village, the official mascots of the Sochi Olympics in 2014 settled permanently: the modest Bunny, the purposeful Leopard and the good-natured White Bear. Residential complex "Olympic Village" is waiting for its guests from all over the world at any time.
