Phone Nokia RM 980: description, specifications, firmware, programs and reviews

Mobile devices manufactured by Nokia have long been considered one of the highest quality on the market. And if you take into account which phones are represented by other development companies, we can conclude that this is indeed so. Despite the fact that at the moment these devices are manufactured under the Microsoft brand, since the giant company acquired the Finnish brand, no one doubts the quality, stability of their work and attractive design.

In this article, we will characterize one very good phone that is present in the line of the described company. We are talking about the Nokia RM 980 - a smartphone that has been actively advertised relatively recently. How it differs from many other Nokia, as well as what it can offer its user, read on.





Alternative

First of all, we are interested in how this smartphone is presented on the market. And this is really important, because its concept is significantly different from other devices. Nokia RM 980 is the first phone from a Finnish company, managed by the Android operating system.

We all know that the vast majority of devices manufactured by Nokia run on a Windows system. Now we are talking, of course, about Windows Phone - a relatively new, but already very interesting system that won the hearts of many smartphone owners with the features of their work.

However, there is another side to the coin. Nokia developers have recorded a huge number of complaints about their products managed by this system. Already by what people who happened to use this or that device write, we can confidently say that they prefer working with another, the most popular OS in the world - Android. But it turned out that they had no choice, because every gadget from Nokia is primarily Windows Phone.

An experimental step designed to solve this problem (if not now, so in the future) was the development of the phone that we characterize today. Nokia RM 980 became an affordable alternative; it was sought after by fans of Nokia products as well as those who prefer to work with the Android system. As a result, their preferences converged in one device.





Appearance

You, of course, understand that the developers have limited their innovations to this model by the issue of the operating system that will be installed on the device. That is, in other aspects, they decided to stick to a more traditional Nokia installation, in order to save their customer.

Even in the matter of design, nothing has changed - here you can still observe a simple but very effective (as Nokia sales practice showed) appearance, consisting in bright color and simple plastic. Of course, this is a profitable solution: firstly, a similar concept is used on more expensive versions; secondly, the body of the device, made in this format, makes it more practical, increasing wear resistance and its hardness; thirdly, the model does not depart from the canons established by dozens of other generations of Nokia. This provides brand recognition, its advertising.

Nokia, as we know, does not skimp on screens: even the most low-cost versions have quite high-quality modules that can perform a wide range of user tasks. Their displays, as a rule, are distinguished by the clarity of the picture, the brightness of colors and a good response to user actions.

If we are talking about what Nokia RM 980 prepared for us, then this is a screen with not the largest resolution of 800 by 480 pixels. In practice, the double knock activation function is also implemented here. This makes it possible to turn on the display and start working with it much faster, without having to look for the screen unlock key.

Autonomy

Another weakness of Android gadgets, as we know, is autonomy. These devices are most often not optimized enough, which is why the battery charge in them is much higher.

As the characteristics describing the Nokia RM 980 model show, the device has a battery with a capacity of 1500 mAh. Considering not the highest smartphone performance, low RAM, low processor frequency and low screen resolution, we can conclude that this is quite enough. The phone is able to work one day in a fairly active mode. Most likely, daily charging will be normal for most Nokia Dual SIM RM 980 users.

And other Android phones cannot offer anything better.





Communication

The very name of the model shows that it is equipped with two SIM cards. This is very beneficial for those who want to combine tariff plans from different operators, getting the most affordable cost of certain services. Switching between cards is carried out in the usual way - in the settings (Android has a special menu for this).

Again, for greater user convenience, the phone has a special application that controls the flow of data. It will protect you from unforeseen expenses for mobile communication.

Interface

Those users who are used to working with other devices on the Android OS will be somewhat surprised to pick up the Nokia RM 980. The firmware of this smartphone provides a unique interface designed in the style of the already mentioned Windows Phone. We are talking about classic application tiles, which are organized differently than the standard Android icons.

Such an interface immediately plays a dual role. Firstly, it makes the shell more recognizable, different from other smartphones. Secondly, the execution logic is the same as on Windows Phone. This, in turn, will teach the user to work with this platform and will adapt it to switch to other models of the same line in the future besides Nokia RM 980.

The firmware, according to the statements of the representatives of the developer company, will support updates.

conclusions
