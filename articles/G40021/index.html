Weaving minions from rubber bands using a machine

Now we will figure out how to weave a minion from rubber bands on the machine.

To do this, we need the same set of rubber bands, a hook and a machine.





  1. We fix the machine evenly, the arrow should look down.

  2. We put one black elastic on the center row vertically.

  3. Then arrange two yellow ones horizontally, and then another two rubber bands in three vertical rows.

  4. On the extreme pegs of the second row we put on yellow rubber bands, and in the middle - black.

  5. In the third row we put on the edges two black ones, and in the middle - two white ones.

  6. On the fourth - two yellow on the edges and two black in the center.

  7. The fifth row consists only of yellow rubber bands.

  8. The sixth and seventh rows consist entirely of blue rubber bands.

  9. In the eighth row we put on black rubber bands only from the edges. And in the middle we‚Äôll leave a void.

  10. Then, twisting two black rubber bands three times (individually), we put them on the blue with a hook.

  11. At the joints of the yellow and blue rubber bands, we fix two more twisted black rubber bands.

  12. On black elastic bands at the end of the minion, on both sides we spin another black rubber band.

  13. Twist the blue gum in half and fix it on three pegs of the machine over black.

  14. In the same way, we fix the blue rubber bands over the blue ones, the yellow ones over the yellow ones, and the black ones over the black ones.
