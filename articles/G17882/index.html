Resorts of the Dead Sea. The Dead Sea in Israel. Hotels on the Dead Sea

Resorts in Israel with great power attract tourists seeking to visit this beautiful land with ancient history. The geographical position of the country allows you to choose between a vacation in the south, where the dry climate prevails, and numerous resorts in the north and center, known for quite variable weather. Today's tourists are very demanding on the choice, they take into account not only comfort, but also the opportunity to improve their health. That is why a lot of guests come to the Dead Sea resorts every year who want to improve their well-being, recover from ailments and just relax and spend time comfortably.





The uniqueness of the Dead Sea

The healing ability of this body of water has been known since ancient times. He was even credited with magical power. Aristotle admired him. Queen Cleopatra was looking for the source of eternal youth and beauty in the waters of the Dead Sea. And this is not at all surprising. Valuable cosmetics, perfumes and some medicines are made on the basis of plants of coastal oases.

Gentle sun

The Dead Sea is located at a uniquely low point on our globe, almost 412 meters below sea level. This place has the most saturated ozone layer, which completely excludes dangerous ultraviolet rays from the solar spectrum. Therefore, the resorts of the Dead Sea are a unique place for taking therapeutic sunbathing. Even prolonged exposure to the sun does not threaten burns.





Healing water

It is difficult to call the Dead Sea water "water", it is much more correct to say that it is a strong saline solution, since the salt concentration reaches almost 42%. The sea is enriched not only with salts, but also with minerals. Water, salts, and mud include magnesium, bromide, and calcium. Such minerals help to relax the body, smooth the skin, and stimulate blood circulation. The pushing force in these waters is so great that the Dead Sea in Israel is famous for the fact that it perfectly holds on the surface even that person who absolutely can not swim.

Fresh air

The climate is unique due to the mixing of two different air masses. Through the hundreds of kilometers of the desert, the Indian Ocean enriches the Dead Sea resorts with a dry, very clean, allergen-free air stream. It is met by evaporation from the surface of the sea, richly saturated with healing substances. Thanks to the hollow surrounded by mountains, the effect of combining air masses is greatly enhanced.





Mud

Healing mud is the strongest concentrate, there are no analogues on Earth. They have excellent cosmetic effects on the skin, have healing properties. Wraps are widely used to cleanse, relax muscles, improve blood circulation, and relieve rheumatic pains. Israel is a great place for people who seek not only to relax, but also to improve their health.

Dead Sea in Israel

Connoisseurs of unique natural landscapes will be amazed by the diversity of flora and fauna. A historian or archaeologist will be thrilled by the terrain that appeared before his eyes, which was described in a biblical narrative. Any tourist at will will be able to immerse himself not only in the Dead Sea in Israel, but also in the most significant events that took place in antiquity on this amazing land. By the way, hotels on the Dead Sea are designed for guests with different income levels.

Crowne plaza

The five-star hotel, located right in the heart of the Ein Bokek zone , has direct access to the Dead Sea beach. Around the hotel are wonderful attractions - the world famous Sedom Mountains and Hever Canyon.





Guests are accommodated in a 12-story building. The rooms are quite diverse: from standard to luxurious deluxe rooms. But they all have views of the Dead Sea. The apartments are equipped with satellite TV, minibar, telephone, voicemail. All rooms are equipped with bathroom, safe, air conditioning. You can make your own tea in the room or make coffee.

All guests are invited to use the outdoor pool, spa, phyto-bar, jacuzzi, gyms and a private beach. The opportunity for seminars, various meetings and conferences was taken into account. The business hall for such events is designed for 600 people. For the evenings, a number of entertainment programs have been developed with the participation of artists who do not give the opportunity to get bored.

Crowne Plaza is a wonderful oasis that allows you to relax not only with your body, but also with your soul. A wonderful hotel that allows you to relax, escape from everyday life and fully enjoy the tranquility.

Daniel Dead Sea

The hotel is located a few meters from the ancient biblical and historical area - the Massada fortress. The five-star Daniel Dead Sea Hotel shows a harmonious mix of cultures. At the same time, he retained a piece of the local, inimitable charm of the region both in design and in Israeli hospitality.





Guests are provided with 302 rooms, twelve of which are luxury rooms. Each room has views of the sea and desert. On holidays and weekends, additional places for tourists are provided.

The rooms are equipped with a minibar, TV, telephone, voice mail. Club Level rooms are equipped with wireless internet, a desk and a bathroom. Privilege rooms feature a living room, a bedroom with a huge bed and a bathroom with a Jacuzzi.

The hotel provides its guests the opportunity to enjoy the salt waters of the Dead Sea, unique mud baths, bowling, chilled beer in a pub, delicious food in the restaurant. Having experienced this wonderful vacation in Israel, you absolutely do not want to leave!

Isrotel dead sea

The modern, recently renovated five-star hotel offers guests a wide range of wellness services. The area in which the hotel is located is known for a unique geological phenomenon - desert landscape, rare fauna and beautiful historical sights.





The building consists of 9 floors. Tourists are offered 298 rooms, seventeen of them are suites. The hotel offers a wonderful vacation with a wide selection of diverse spa services. Spacious rooms offer views of the Dead Sea. It offers a magnificent swimming pool and a clean beach. Like other hotels on the Dead Sea, Isrotel Dead Sea offers spacious rooms with a telephone, TV, safe, air conditioning, and a minibar. Each room has a bath and a balcony. Rest at the hotel will leave the most vivid and rainbow memories.

Leonardo club

This 4-star hotel is located on the private beach of the Ein Bokek resort area. Not far from it are the beautiful national parks of Ein Gedi, Qumran and Massada.





Guests are offered 388 spacious rooms. From the windows of the hotel overlooking the mountains of Edom, appears the southern part of the Judean hills. Tourists can enjoy the beautiful beach, pool, rooftop solarium. Luxury rooms include spacious balconies and bathrooms with a jacuzzi. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, minibars, telephones, safes, cable TV. A bathroom with hairdryer is provided in each room. The guest is offered high-speed Internet access. This is a wonderful hotel that allows you to spend the most unforgettable vacation for the whole family.

Dead Sea in Jordan

How amazing and varied the resorts of the Dead Sea can be. Jordan offers guests a vacation on the east coast. Here, the stereotype of immobility, that is, of the static surface, is completely destroyed. The east coast will delight with waves, sometimes even very large. Their secret lies in the rapid flow of the waters of the Jordan - the only full-flowing river that flows directly into the Dead Sea.

Each hotel has a mud tub on the beach. If serious treatment is not planned, such dirt can be used for free. Just do not harm your health. The abuse of sufficiently active substances can lead to the opposite result.



Jordan Hotels

The east coast offers a variety of hotels. Jordan used the Dead Sea to organize beautiful health resorts. The most famous of them are Jordan Valley Marriott Resort & Spa (5 stars), Movenpick Resort & Spa Dead Sea (5 stars), Kempinski Hotel Ishtar (5 stars), Dead Sea SPA (4 stars). These hotels successfully treat skin, respiratory, muscle and neurological diseases. Guests who prefer outdoor activities can enjoy mountain climbing, jeep riding in the desert or even a camel. All hotels on the Dead Sea provide the opportunity to go to the sauna, enjoy swimming in the pool. Sports enthusiasts will be delighted with the fitness center, playing tennis.

Price policy

Despite the variety of hotels, all resorts of the Dead Sea have approximately the same cost of recreation. Prices rise in the high season - from the first of March to the end of May and from the beginning of September to the end of November. In low season, the cost drops significantly. The price starts at $ 88 per night (for the 3-star Tsell Harim Hotel).

Rest in the resorts of the Dead Sea is not cheap, but a competent approach and taking into account some factors will help to significantly reduce prices. For example, booking a room for more than 7 days will give decent discounts. In this case, the cost will be lower than in other world resorts.
