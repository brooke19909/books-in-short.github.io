Yalihan Aspendos Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

The most attractive destination for tourist trips, combining sea bathing and a variety of excursions, is Turkey. This is a state in which Western culture is combined with Eastern culture, Christians are united with Islamists, and multi-star hotels are located near historical sights.

Alanya is one of the resort areas that successfully combines price and quality. Golden sands, landscapes of the Toros mountains, clear transparent waters of the Mediterranean Sea will give unforgettable days of relaxation in this hot colorful country.

One of the budget accommodation options (about 7 thousand rubles per day), giving real comfort, warmth and cordiality from the first minutes of acquaintance, is a hotel in Avsallar - Yalihan Aspendos Hotel, which has three stars. Amazing holidays for children and holidays for the whole family, full of excellent mood, bubbling emotions and endless fun, can be realized within this hotel complex.

Satisfying the hunger of guests

Yalihan Aspendos Hotel adheres to the all-inclusive concept. One of its points is the organization of the food system. Meals are offered in buffet style. According to this food methodology, each guest independently chooses and puts in his plate a portion of those products that he prefers.

Meals are held in the main restaurant five times a day, according to the established schedule. The cuisine is predominantly international, with culinary delights from local chefs. Convenient and comfortable chairs with soft pillows will increase your appetite and allow you to relax a bit when you are full.
