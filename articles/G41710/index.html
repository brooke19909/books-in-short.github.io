iOS 11 on 5s: reviews. Is it worth updating?

iPhone 5s is Apple's smartphone introduced on September 10, 2013. The start of sales began in 10 days - September 20, 2013. It runs on the iOS 7 operating system and contains the Apple A7 64-bit processor. iOS 11 was introduced almost 4 years after the presentation of the iPhone 5s - on June 5, 2017 at the World wide Developers Conference. On iOS 7, the iPhone 5s felt really good, but on iOS 11, is it still the same?

Do I need iOS 11?

Reviews of iOS 11, reviews on 5s show that it has both global flaws and shortcomings that appear only on the iPhone 5s. Of the minuses of 5s, poor performance and a slightly reduced operating time can be noted, other errors can appear on any iPhone.

Despite these shortcomings, updating iOS 11 to 5s will bring more than 27 new features to your iPhone, such as an updated control center with individual settings, the ability to download the application cache in iCloud, the option to scan documents in notes, updated built-in maps from Apple, a completely redesigned AppStore , a quick screenshot editor. And this is just a small part of all the new features. All of them are very useful and work great.
