Modern shops: "Red Square" (Krasnodar). Shopping for the whole family

Want to know where to spend time in Krasnodar? You can make purchases and have fun in one of the largest shopping and entertainment centers of the region, where the best shops are collected. "Red Square" (Krasnodar) surprises with its variety and structured layout. Here you do not have to wander around the halls in search of the desired department, because they are all divided into sections by category. This makes the shopping center the most attractive shopping place for residents and visitors of the city. A staggering figure - about 1 million people visit it every month! So the "Red Square" can rightfully be called a favorite place for recreation and shopping among Krasnodar.

Entertainment and additional services

The shopping center has developed infrastructure and a large selection of entertainment. You can not only come here for shopping, but also spend fun time, take advantage of personal care services, and even solve business issues with documents. Of the important functions in the shopping center are presented postal services (post office of Russia), a multifunctional center "My Documents", where you can apply for the use of public services. The bank branch will help solve financial issues. Insurance and travel companies are also needed in this town inside the metropolis, everyone can use these services.

Families with small children may not have to worry about comfort, as the mall has a room for mother and child. Every week in the "Red Square" there are free children's performances on weekends. For visitors there is a food court with a large selection of restaurants and fast food chains. And visitors can even stay overnight at the hotel. Laundry facilities are also available. And in the exhibition room there are many interesting city events organized by trading companies.
