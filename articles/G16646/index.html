"Expanse" (ecopark, Omsk): photos, reviews, how to get

Residents of large cities just need to occasionally get out into the countryside in order to recharge with energy and throw off the accumulated fatigue from their shoulders. Unfortunately, this is not always possible, so the joy of the Omsk people, who already have the opportunity to fully relax, without wasting time on a long trip, is understandable. It appeared after the Razdolie complex (ecopark) began to operate in the city.

Omsk is a place where there are many interesting tourist sites and attractions, but the appearance of this outdoor recreation center can be considered a real gift for its residents and guests.





general information

"Expanse" (ecopark, Omsk) consists of a hotel, a banquet hall and bath patios, each of which is decorated in a specific style. Free parking is available for guests. All these objects are located on a rather spacious, fenced, landscaped and well-groomed territory. Its design was done by professionals who were able to create a magnificent landscape design, having beaten the features of the terrain and arranging artificial ponds.

On the territory of the eco-complex there is also a veranda "Fisherman's House". It is designed for a company of up to 20 people and is located right on the shore of a small picturesque lake.

You can go fishing from the veranda by renting fishing rods and cook kebabs, as there are barbecue facilities and tables for organizing gatherings in the fresh air.

"Expanse" (ecopark, Omsk): where is

The complex is located at: st. Mira, 112 A, building 1. It has excellent transport accessibility from all points of the city. Thus, "Razdole" offers outdoor recreation in the very center of Omsk, which is very rare.

To book a patio, banquet hall or order a service, you can call: +7 (3812) 40-55-77.





Banquet hall

In the "Expanse" (ecopark, Omsk) you can organize and hold any large-scale feasts (weddings, anniversaries, etc.). It features:

  • Banquet hall "Empress", designed for 140 guests and 32 seats on the summer terrace. It is a great place to organize a wedding feast.
  • Banquet room "Mr. Wright", which can accommodate 35 guests.

The menu includes dishes of Russian and European cuisine prepared by experienced chefs who strive to satisfy the taste preferences of guests of the eco-complex as much as possible.

Reviews

Judging by the entries left by visitors to the complex on online forums, they are usually satisfied with the level of service and the services provided by the Razdolye Ecopark (Omsk). Reviews indicate that there you can organize and hold a corporate party, as well as any other event with a large number of guests. Especially a lot of kind words can be heard about the high level of organization of weddings in the ecopark. In addition, the newlyweds are delighted with the gifts that the Directorate makes to them (free photo session with guests, renting a place for exit registration, subject to a banquet, wedding night in the suite).

As for dissatisfied visitors, their complaints, as a rule, relate to rising prices for services. However, according to the assurances of the management of the institution, this situation is a consequence of an increase in costs associated with an increase in the cost of utilities.
