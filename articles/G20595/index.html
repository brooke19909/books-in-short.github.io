New Year in Paris - reviews of vacationers

New Year is a favorite holiday of people all over the world. In different corners of the planet it is celebrated in different ways. Each nation has its own traditions and habits.
The Russians, as a rule, prefer to hold a New Year's meeting at home in front of the TV. At the same time, the table must certainly have traditional olivier and champagne. But a celebration of magic and fulfillment of desires, many of us would like to meet somewhere in Paris.





City of Dreams

The New Year in Paris must be celebrated at least once in a lifetime. The city is rich in fiction during these holidays , so there are a lot of scenarios of fun.

New Year in Paris is a bright and unforgettable sight. The city is decorated with a huge number of holiday trees, which can be found not only in the streets, but also in apartments, as well as in the entrances of houses. From the multi-colored shining lights decorating the capital on New Year's Eve, the streets are bright as day.

Colorful lanterns decorated and boutiques. Shops behind the scenes compete with each other for the most original design of festive shop windows. There is a special attraction in New Year's Paris, which is a curiosity for tourists. The windows of the largest Printemps and Lafayette shopping galleries come to life during the New Year holidays. In the facades of shops, residents and guests of Paris can enjoy puppetry. The smell of roasted chestnuts sold right there and stunning illumination will not leave anyone indifferent.

On December 31, after ten in the evening in Paris, one more traditional phenomenon can be observed. People begin to move towards the Champs Elysees and settle down in the areas adjacent to them. It seems that the boulevard, famous all over the world, is filled on New Year's Eve by tourists from all over the world. You can see among them our compatriots who decided to celebrate the New Year in Paris. The main attraction on this night is an unforgettable walk, the beginning of the route of which is located on Charles de Gaulle Square, and the final point is Concorde Square. Shops located on the Champs Elysees are open until midnight that day.

New year

Towards midnight, revival begins to be seen around the famous Eiffel Tower. There are no chimes near this building. The crowd mentally counts the blows on their watch. The New Year in Paris begins with the inclusion of hundreds of small flickering lights on the Eiffel Tower. Everyone who gathered near her pours champagne into plastic cups. Loving couples celebrate the arrival of the New Year with kisses. Parisians believe that if at the time of the New Year's arrival near the Eiffel Tower make a wish, then it must certainly come true.

The festive performance ends there. Around the tower, mass festivities and round dances are not held. Most of the restaurants are closed, and people disperse in nightclubs and entertainment cabarets. Some choose walks along empty streets.





Night clubs

New Year in Paris will certainly leave a lasting impression. Festive mood, no doubt, will give you clubs. The best institutions have everything that is necessary for such a case. The downside of nightclubs is only a large crowd of people. In this regard, there is a possibility that you simply will not get there. Finding another place in New Year's Paris is problematic due to a transportation problem. It is difficult to find a taxi, and the police cordon, which is exhibited in most fashionable places, as well as on the Champs Elysees and the surrounding streets, will not allow you to travel by your car.





Christmas story

Many people know the European Disneyland in Paris. New Year can be celebrated in it. It is simply impossible to imagine the best present for a child. Disneyland in Paris is a huge entertainment complex. He is the kingdom of beloved fairy tale heroes. The complex consists of two parks and Disney Village - an entertaining "village". Do not buy a one-day tour. The New Year in Paris, which you will want to celebrate at Disneyland, will bring you true pleasure when visiting attractions for at least two to three days. Only in this case you will have time to dine in the company of Pluto and Mickey Mouse, pay a visit to the castle of the famous Sleeping Beauty fairy tales, meet Jack Sparrow, see the festive fireworks and the grand New Year's show, and also get many other pleasant and unexpected surprises that will please you and your baby.

Disneyland tickets are not cheap. Their cost starts from fifty-four euros. However, you can save on the purchase by booking tickets on the official site of the entertainment complex.

Unusual holiday meeting

The Les Pavillons de Bercy Museum gives you the opportunity to become a member of a grand masquerade ball. It is there that you can unusually celebrate the New Year in Paris. Reviews of tourists indicate that this event is a real fairytale extravaganza. Guests dressed in luxurious costumes are delivered in gilded carriages on the Bercy estate. There they are met by Louis XIV.





New Year in Paris can be seen on a yacht that floats on the Seine. A candlelit dinner is organized for visitors , accompanied by a jazz orchestra. Such a New Year's Eve will be truly romantic.

Recreation facilities

New Year in Paris will be an unforgettable event if you meet it in the cabaret Paradis Latin. In this institution, on a festive night, visitors will find an enchanting show prepared in the best French traditions. Guests at this cabaret can enjoy a wonderful dinner and plenty of dancing. In the morning, the restaurant serves breakfast consisting of coffee and fresh croissants.

An unusual New Year celebration awaits visitors in the Kube Hotel's ice bar. In this institution, everything, even glasses, is made of real ice. Vodka, which is served here without restrictions, will help keep warm at a negative temperature, maintained at minus five.





Continuation of the holiday

In the early morning of January 1, a large parade with mummers is arranged in Paris. It can be seen on the streets of the city. This spectacle, in which giant brightly colored dolls participate, is accompanied by a laser show. From the first to the sixth of January in the capital of France, "Feasts of Fools" is held.

The beginning of the year is loved by all French fashionistas. Until the tenth-eleventh of January in Paris stores you can visit the festive sales. During this period, clothes produced by the most popular and well-known brands are sold at unprecedented discounts - up to ninety percent.
