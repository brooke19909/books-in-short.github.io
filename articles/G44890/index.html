How to make Minecraft paper figures

Usually for computer games you can buy figures in the store, because they are made of plastic, specially painted and decorated. However, if you look at Minecraft, you can quickly realize that it makes no sense to go to the store and spend money on the purchase of any items if you can make them yourself. After all, Minecraft is a minimalistic game in which everything consists of blocks, and you can easily reproduce them on paper. Thus, you can save money and do something yourself. Minecraft paper figurines can be very diverse - everything depends on you here, as well as on the wealth of relevant Internet resources. How to make such figures? This article will walk you through the process step by step.

Printout template





Of course, you can practice origami and try to create Minecraft paper figures yourself without any templates or blanks. But it is recommended that you contact the worldwide network, where you will find a huge number of various color blanks. All you have to do is print the one you like the most. To do this, you can use plain paper - if you are not satisfied with the strength of your figure, then you can just stick the paper on cardboard, and you get a more durable model. Minecraft paper figures everyone can do - most importantly, only take the necessary items. There are not so many of them - only scissors and glue, you will do the rest with your own hands.

Cutting elements





Minecraft paper figurines may consist of one or more elements, which at first glance may seem completely unlike what should result. Do not attach any particular importance to this, as it still has time to change a little later. For now, you need to carefully cut out all the parts along the contours, but do not cut them, but leave them in the state in which they were. It would seem that this is a fairly simple process, but in reality everything can be more complicated, given the abundance of various angles, as well as the fact that any inaccuracy as a result can affect how the figure looks in the final. Accuracy is very important here, and you need to pay special attention to this when you make paper figures from Minecraft. Schemes are for the most part freely available, so there should not be a problem with downloading and cutting.

Contour bending





So the long-awaited moment came when the variegated and strange flat sheets begin to gradually turn into the figure that you dreamed about. Naturally, any player in Minecraft would like to achieve this. Paper figures, schemes for which you have downloaded from the Internet, can decorate your desktop and the whole house, but you will have to try first. So, you need to bend all the available elements along all the marked contours, and doing this no less accurately than the cutting process itself. Initially, the figure may not take the desired shape, but you should not be upset, because there is yet another very large and very important step ahead, without which you will not be able to make Minecraft figures from paper.
