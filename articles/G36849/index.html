Ramenskoye, attractions: monuments, museums, nature

One of the ancient settlements located near Moscow is Ramenskoye. Its sights are not reduced only to architectural monuments. In Ramenskoye there are unusual fountains, figures from popular cartoons, picturesque building facades and much more.

The nature of Ramensky

Not only the sights, but also the nature in Ramenskoye attracts many tourists. The city has pine groves, and in the vicinity - forests. Many picturesque, albeit artificial ponds. Thanks to the forests surrounding the city and green spaces, the air in Ramenskoye is very clean. In the forested area there is an excellent ski run.

Museums

In Ramenskoye there is a museum of modern military equipment. It is located in a city park. The museum was created in memory of the soldiers who died in Chechnya and Afghanistan. On the equipped site exhibits of military heavy equipment used in battles. In the museum you can see BTR-70 (armored personnel carriers), rocket launchers, guns and a T-80 tank. Exhibits can not just be watched from the side. They are allowed to touch and climb inside. And not only for children.



Fountains

There are many fountains in Ramenskoye. For example, "Adam and Eve." This is a fountain of love. The second name is Creation. It was recently opened, opposite the city registry office. The sculpture of the fountain is made in the form of Adam and Eve, standing, holding hands, under the tree of Evil and Good.

Water flows down the branches and falls into a round bowl. Small jets strike vertically upwards around its circumference. The composition is made of bronze, concrete and granite. The height of the structure is almost 4 meters, and the diameter is about 6 m.

The fountain, called "The Seasons", is the largest in Ramenskoye. It is located on Sq. Youth. Created in neo-modern style with characteristic plant lines, naked bodies and mascarons.

Description of Borisoglebsky Lake

Borisoglebskoe Lake is one of the main attractions of Ramenskoye. This pond is its symbol, which can be seen on the city coat of arms. Two silver wave-like lines are drawn on it. The name of the lake appeared in honor of the holy princes Boris and Gleb. For the first time, the reservoir was mentioned in urban chronicles in the 16th century. Factories

Ramenskoye (Moscow region) is famous for its factory "Gzhel". This is one of the largest and oldest companies producing porcelain. Trinity Cathedral

The beautiful Trinity Cathedral in Ramenskoye was built back in 1852 at the expense of Princess Golitsyna. This is the main city church. Over time, the temple acquired additional extensions: refectory, aisles. They were later consecrated.

Currently, the cathedral has a Sunday school and a charity dining room. In the temple there are several very revered shrines, among which is the icon of the Savior with the relics of saints and others.

Cathedral of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God

The city has ancient churches and magnificent cathedrals, which Ramenskoye can rightfully be proud of. Sights must include in their list the Temple of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God. In 1998, the laying of the first stone took place. But there were difficulties with finances.



Initially, a wooden church was built on the site of the cathedral in honor of St. Matrona of Moscow. Later there was a separate belfry with 10 bells. And the chapel of St. Seraphim of Sarov is separated over time. There was a font and a holy spring in it.

Over time, the Cathedral of the Kazan Icon grew. It appeared Sunday school, which operates to this day. And in the preschool group "Ray" is preparing the kids for school. There is a pilgrimage service at the cathedral, and a separate newspaper is published every month.

Holy spring

The source of the Holy Trinity is located in the city Ramensky culture park. The retaining wall of the spring is lined with decorative tiles. Water drains through a metal pipe. Above the holy spring is a wall of colored mosaics. An icon of the Holy Trinity is mounted in it.

You can go down the stone steps to the source. In front of the spring there is a large platform, the perimeter of which is surrounded by a wide low wall. At the same time, it serves as a bench. The water of the holy spring helps to heal not only physical, but also mental wounds.



Parks

To see the unusual rope park-extreme, you need to visit Ramenskoye. Sights of it located not only in the city, but also in the surrounding area. For example, on Kratovsky lake. The unusual park opened only in 2016. And constantly filled with new attractions. Basically all the entertainment for children from six years old. Moreover, the rope park works even on holidays.

The ancient Marble Bridge has been restored on Kratovsky Lake. It has 4 spiers, two-meter balls and curly balusters. The project was created by Alexander Tamanyan. Initially, the bridge was reinforced concrete. Over time, it collapsed, but thanks to the efforts of residents it is now recreated anew. And from marble. Therefore, he received such a new name.

There is a large city park in Ramenskoye. It is located in the city center. Borisoglebskoye Lake is located in the same territory. The park has many different attractions, playgrounds, a dance floor and summer cafes.
