Many guests call the Happy Pushkin Hotel a great place to stay. You can go for a walk along the Promenade des Anglais at night: to watch how bridges are being built.

Many are also interested in its interior, like spacious, clean rooms, which are very different, but each is quite cozy in its own way. All numbers, the authors of reviews assure, with their own theme, are not the same. Residents argue that all the furniture in the rooms is solid, reliable. Many people like large windows and high ceilings; the rooms are very spacious and comfortable. They note the presence of comfortable double beds, good furniture with many shelves, and a minibar, which is replenished daily. Guests also like the fact that the bathroom has underfloor heating, and not every hotel in St. Petersburg differs in this feature. Fans of exotic note the presence of a working fireplace and firewood.

The reviewers also say that they have left a rather mixed impression of staying at the hotel. On the one hand, spacious rooms, proximity to the center, friendly staff.
