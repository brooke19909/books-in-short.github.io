Onatti Beach Resort (Egypt, Marsa Alam): description and photos

Are you planning to relax on the Red Sea, away from the noise of big cities and tourist resorts filled with tourists? Prefer a relaxing pastime on the beach or by the pool? Would you like to be able to admire the richest fauna of coral reefs? Do not want to spend much money on a luxury hotel, but still want to live in comfortable conditions? Then be sure to pay attention to the four-star Onatti Beach Resort (Egypt, Marsa Alam).



Location

The hotel in question is located on the very shore of the beloved by travelers from different countries of the Red Sea. The nearest town is El Quseir, which is 16 kilometers away. In the vicinity of Onatti Beach you will find only other hotel complexes. For shopping and various tourist attractions, you must go either to El Quseir or to Marsa Alam. You can get to these cities by taxi. As for the distance from the airport, the nearest air harbor is located in Marsa Alam. The journey to it takes about half an hour. However, many Russian tourists fly to Hurghada airport. It is removed from Onatti Beach Resort 4 * by almost 200 kilometers. So, about three hours are spent on the road for vacationers. Therefore, experienced travelers advise, if possible, to look for a tour that involves landing at the airport of Marsa Alam.

The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 204 comfortable rooms located in several one- and two-story buildings. There are apartments of categories "standard" and "family". All rooms have comfortable furniture, air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone, safe, bathroom with hairdryer, balcony or terrace. Rooms are cleaned every day. Towels and bed linen are updated several times a week. Also, according to our compatriots, if you leave a tip, the maids will build funny figures from towels on your beds.

The hotel has three restaurants: the main (buffet), as well as Italian and oriental ("a la carte"). Guests can also enjoy drinks and light snacks in the lobby bars, poolside and beach. During the day and evening, tourists are entertained by a team of animators.

In general, according to travelers who have been here, Onatti Beach Resort 4 * is perfect for a relaxing, relaxing holiday by the sea. If you are hoping for noisy entertainment, then it makes sense to consider other hotels.



Sea

The hotel in question has its own 130-meter private beach. According to tourists, it is very clean, with a small nice sand. When entering the water corals are found. However, for the convenience of vacationers there are cleared areas with a sandy bottom. Also in the sea you can go down the stairs from the pontoon. Near the coast there is a coral reef with diverse inhabitants. So, tourists with pleasure admired sea turtles, octopuses, huge moray eels and, of course, bright various fishes.

Guests at the beach have comfortable sun loungers and parasols, and there is a bar.
