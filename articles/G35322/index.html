Head unit specifications

Now the fun part. What is the Pioneer MVH-280FD car radio capable of? User reviews about this device are mixed. Someone likes the sound quality, and someone considers it flawed. In order to avoid disputes, it is necessary to consider technical specifications. So, the device has a MOSFET amplifier with a power of 100 watts per channel. There are four channels in total. One hundred watts is unrealistic a lot for the space that is available in the car. It is worth noting that the level of sound distortion for such power is ridiculously low. Engineers of the manufacturer used all their skills to ensure high-quality sound apparatus. Among other things, the radio boasts such options as the proprietary Advanced Sound Retriever Sound Enhancer, a five-band graphic equalizer, high and low pass filters, an FM tuner, support for Android and iOS devices. Supported Audio Formats

And here it is very interesting. What formats can the Pioneer MVH-280FD read? Reviews and review, alas, will not help to clarify the picture. All hope for information specified by the manufacturer. Well, on my own experience. The radio is completely deprived of the ability to play CDs. It‚Äôs right: the 21st century in the yard - ‚Äúdiscs‚Äù are a thing of the past. The device supports playing music from a USB drive. The head unit easily copes with formats such as MP3, WMA, WAV and FLAC. The latter is especially interesting, as it is an audio compression format without loss of quality. This means that in cars there will finally be high-quality sound. FLAC files weigh much more than usual MP3s, but the sound quality is several times better. For comparison: the maximum audio bitrate of an MP3 is 320 kilobits per second. While not even the highest quality FLAC easily produces 1000 kilobits per second and higher. Further comments are unnecessary.





Additional car radio interfaces

Let's take a closer look at the Pioneer MVH-280FD. Reviews and photos of the device make it clear that it is very different from standard car radios. So, this head unit has the ability to directly connect a subwoofer. This is very nice, since you do not need to spend money on additional amplifiers. And with the power that the radio is capable of delivering, the use of amplifiers is not required at all. Instead of a subwoofer, you can connect the rear speakers. Also on the rear panel of the radio there is a connector for connecting a wired remote control, which should be located on the steering wheel. However, it does not come in the kit. Will have to be purchased separately. Through the AUX jack on the front panel of the device, you can connect all kinds of analog audio playback devices. But that is not all. Using a special socket, the car radio easily works with smartphones on iOS and Android. Moreover, you can both control the device and answer calls.





Tuner specifications

Now consider the possibilities of searching for Pioneer MVH-280FD radio stations. User reviews in this regard are unambiguous: the radio is clearly not enough power built-in antenna. Therefore, you need to use the external. The rest of the tuner is very advanced. There is support for RTS, search for the best frequency, memory at 24 stations. But a particularly useful option for motorists is to inform about traffic density. The only trouble is that in our latitudes such a function is almost useless. You can also tune stations and the frequency range manually. You don‚Äôt have to rely on automation. In general, the quality of the tuner from the "Pioneer" is not bad. You just need to remember to attach an external antenna. Then everything will be fine.
