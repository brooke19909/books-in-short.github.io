La Perla Beldibi 4 Hotel, Kemer, Turkey

Description: The coast of the Mediterranean Sea in the region of the Turkish city of Kemer becomes mountainous. The local landscapes resemble Crimean ones - a wonderful place for those who want to remember a vacation in Yalta or Gurzuf, and at the same time get a higher level of tourist services for the same money. Not far from Kemer, in the town of Beldibi, on the first line of the beach is the resort La Perla Beldibi 4. This is a new hotel - its three-story building was erected only in 2004. A small park adjoins the building. The area is quiet, calm - ideal for a leisurely family vacation. A few kilometers from Kemer is the ancient city of Phaselis - there is an excursion pilgrimage for tourists from different countries. It takes about an hour to get to Antalya International Airport .





Hotel La Perla Beldibi 4 consists of: 88 rooms with individual temperature control systems. Eight of them belong to the Economy category: there is no balcony or window. Each guest room has satellite TV (one channel - Russian), a safe, a telephone and a minibar, which can be used as a refrigerator. Most rooms have private balconies. What is unusual for Turkish resort hotels is the availability of high-speed Wi-Fi for guests of all rooms. The rooms are carefully cleaned daily, and bed linen and towels are changed twice a week.



Meals: Hotel guests can choose the appropriate type of food: half board or all inclusive. La Perla Beldibi 4 has its own restaurant and bar. In the latter one can find wines from around the world, although wonderful local brands are recommended nevertheless. In the restaurant, guests will find a wide selection of dishes: fish, meat, vegetables and fruits. To please Russian tourists, pancakes are baked here for breakfast. Vegetarian and dietary requests are also satisfied.

Beach: The coast is pebbly, and there are practically no sandy beaches, but La Perla Beldibi 4 offers its guests an exclusive sand and pebble beach 75 meters long, with very small and smooth pebbles. All beach ammunition - umbrellas, deck chairs, sunbeds, towels and mattresses - is free of charge. In the daytime there is a bar with soft drinks and ice cream. On the territory of the hotel there is also an outdoor, indoor swimming pool (the latter with a sunbathing terrace), and a "paddling pool" with slides for children.



Additional information: La Perla 4 Resort offers a spa center with massages, a sauna and Turkish steam bath, tennis courts and a gym. For those who rent a car, the hotel provides a free private parking space. It is possible to wash and iron clothes in the laundry room. This resort can also be an excellent base for meetings or seminars: the conference room seats up to 60 participants. And young guests will certainly be entertained by the animators of the kids club. Reviews: Polite staff who knows several languages, which is nice to speak with; funny energetic animators; tasty varied food and interesting entertainment programs - this is how you can summarize the comments of visitors about the hotel La Perla Beldibi 4. Reviews also mention spacious and very comfortable rooms, which are clean and tidy. Tourists especially appreciated the work of maids - they cleaned their conscience, regardless of whether they left a tip or not. Guests were struck by such an amazing fact for hotels of this type as the abundance of free sunbeds on the beach.
