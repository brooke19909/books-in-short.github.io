Location

The magnificent five-star Sens Deluxe Hotel is located just four kilometers from the city center of Side and 1 kilometer from the village of Kumkoy. The resort village of Manavgat is located 7 kilometers from the hotel complex. The nearest airport is located in Antalya, 60 kilometers from the hotel. So upon arrival on the road it will take you a little over an hour. With regard to proximity to the sea, the hotel is located on the second coastline. Private beach is 200 meters away.
