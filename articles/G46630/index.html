Turban is the latest fashion trend

Turban is the headdress of Muslims. Islam attributes its followers to covering their hair, but men and women in different parts of the world do this in different ways.

What is turban

In fact, a turban or turban is a long piece of cloth wrapped around the head in a special way. Turban is common among the peoples of Africa, the Arabian Peninsula, India, Asia, and Russia.





On average, it takes six to eight meters of fabric to make a turban, but some species require twenty or more meters of fabric.

Turban in ancient times

In the East, there were more than 1000 methods of tying this headgear, and by its appearance it was possible to determine the status and type of activity of its owner.

Initially, the turban was the headdress of men in India and some Muslim peoples. She wore a skullcap on top. Men were forbidden to take off their hats with strangers.

In some sections of the population, turban was of practical importance. In ancient times, the turban of eastern warriors could reach up to 20 kg: they could carry small weapons and necessary things in campaigns in it.

Turban as a female headdress

Over time, this attribute also entered the women's wardrobe. Oriental women adorned their dresses with precious stones, and the turban itself was made of expensive fabric.



Depending on the region and its climatic features, fabric for turban is selected. Currently, not only people practicing Islam wear a turban, but also women of fashion of absolutely any faith.

Turban is fashionable, comfortable and practical. And those who live in cold climates have a good opportunity to wear hats that look like a turban in appearance.

Hat-turban: a fashionable hat

Hats in oriental style were a great success in the 80s of the last century. But, as they say, everything new is well forgotten old. It is not surprising that at present they have not only regained their former popularity, but also increased it.

Original accessories are very popular among movie stars and show business. To be stylish and fashionable, you can choose the model that suits you among those already offered or tie a turban on your own. And this is not as difficult as it might seem at first.



If you already have knitting experience, a similar headdress can be knitted in one evening. But even if this is a debut, creating a fashionable turban will not take you much time and effort. Yes, and knitting will be a way of manifesting your creativity.

What do you need to tie a turban on your own?

So, let's look at how to knit a turban hat with knitting needles for a woman. You will need the following materials:

  • yarn, in the composition of which there is wool and acrylic (or only acrylic - optional);
  • knitting needles, the format of which is indicated on the packaging for the threads;
  • hook;
  • needle with a big eye;
  • threads matching yarn;
  • scissors;
  • centimeter for taking measurements.

Knit a turban together

If you have prepared everything you need, feel free to get down to business. So, how to knit a turban hat with knitting needles? We‚Äôll review the description of the most convenient and quick way right now!

  1. First you need to determine the size of the product. Use a soft centimeter to measure head coverage. The measurement is made from one earlobe to another through the highest point of the head. Divide the received data into two, remember them or write down. Tie a sample of yarn - a 4 x 4 elastic band. The elastic band is knitted as follows: four front loops alternate with four purl loops to the end of the row. Then turn the product over with the other side (toward you), and continue knitting.
  2. Steam the resulting sample. Count how many loops are located on one centimeter. Multiply the figure by the data that determines half of the head circumference. Add two side loops to the hem.
  3. Dial the desired number of loops and start knitting. The algorithm is the same as when knitting a sample. Knit a flat cloth as if making a scarf. Try on the product periodically. On average, its length should be about 80-100 cm.
  4. When you tie the desired length, connect the two ends with a hook or needle with thread.
  5. Turn the product so that the connecting seam is further from you (it will run from the crown to the forehead). Connect the inner edges about 20 cm. You should get a hat with a rounded tail on the back.
  6. Women's turban is almost ready. Try it on yourself. Twist the loose loop from the back and pull it over the rim. You can wear a hat in different variations. You can rotate the turban forward, you can decorate with a brooch or other accessories of your choice.

If you plan to wear a hat in the winter, use thicker yarn. Acrylic or cotton yarn is excellent for the autumn-spring period.

Many modern hats are similar to each other, but a turban hat is always a unique thing, especially in its own performance.





It can be worn at any time of the year, even in summer, when it will protect your head from the scorching sun - this is the main function of a turban in some African regions.

You can knit a long scarf from suitable yarn and learn how to wind it around your head in the form of a turban, with a characteristic overlap. You can tie a hair band: it is suitable for both the warm season and cool weather.

A turban hat can also be tied to a girl - this headpiece is quite versatile and has no age restrictions.
