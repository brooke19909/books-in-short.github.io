Hotel Elysees Premier Hotel 4 * (Hurghada, Egypt): description and photos

If you are looking for an inexpensive hotel with comfortable rooms, decent service, located near the beach on the Red Sea, then Elysees Premier Hotel 4 * may suit you. About what is offered to guests here, and we will talk further.





Location

The hotel in question is located seven kilometers from the center of Hurghada (Egypt), a couple of hundred meters from the shores of the Red Sea. The hotel has at its disposal a large private beach equipped with sunbeds, umbrellas and a bar. The nearest airport to the hotel is in Hurghada, 10 kilometers away. So on the road from the air harbor it will take you about a quarter of an hour. This fact was especially appreciated by tourists arriving on vacation with young children. After all, after a long flight, they do not have to spend time on a long journey to the hotel. There is no special tourist infrastructure in the vicinity of the hotel in question. However, in the neighborhood there are several shops and shops where you can buy food, fruits and souvenirs. For shopping, it is better to go to the center of Hurghada. You can get here by taxi.

Elysees Premier Hotel 4 * (Hurghada): tourist reviews

As you know, many travelers, when choosing a hotel, are largely guided by the comments of those who have already visited a particular place. After all, former guests try to tell about the pros and cons of a particular hotel, based on their own experience. As for the Elysees Premier Hotel 4 *, the reviews of our compatriots are mostly positive. So, tourists from Russia and other CIS countries assure that the hotel is fully consistent with its category and cost of living. A confirmation of these words is the rating of the hotel, which is displayed on the basis of guest ratings. So, he makes four points out of the maximum possible five. In general, according to travelers, the hotel in question is an excellent option for relaxing a wide variety of categories of travelers - families with children, young people and older people. Here everyone will find entertainment to their taste.

Housing stock

As already mentioned, the hotel has 127 rooms. They are presented here in the following categories: standard (area 30-34 sq. M, accommodation - maximum 2 + 1 people, view of the pool or the sea) and family (64 sq. M, two connected rooms, maximum accommodation 4 + 2 guests) . Of the total number of standard apartments, several are non-smoking. In the rooms, according to tourists, there is everything necessary for living: comfortable furniture (especially guests allocate large beds), air conditioning, TV with cable channels (there are several in Russian), telephone, safe (free), minibar (for a fee), tea set, a bathroom with a hairdryer. Please note that balconies are not available in all apartments. If necessary, a cot can be added to the room. The apartments have ceramic tiles on the floor.

Rooms are cleaned every day. According to the guests, its quality here greatly depends on whether you leave a tip to the maid or not. Sheets and towels are updated several times a week.





Nutrition

In general, this item did not cause any particular complaints from tourists who visited the Elysees Premier Hotel 4 *. However, some guests claim that the menu could be more varied. But you could always find something to your taste. Some travelers did not really like the fact of catering on the day of departure. So, if you rent your room, as it should be, at noon, then you will have to pay extra for lunch and dinner (if you are still at the hotel).

Sea

Judging by the comments, almost all tourists were satisfied with the beach vacation. So, despite the fact that the hotel is on the second line, the coast can be reached in just a couple of minutes. In this case, the carriageway can be crossed by a ground crossing. The beach itself is quite large, clean, with enough sunbeds and umbrellas. There is also a bar with drinks and snacks, and a team of animators works throughout the day. As for the underwater world, it is off the coast of Hurghada, according to experienced travelers, is not as diverse as, for example, in Sharm el-Sheikh. However, bright fish and other interesting inhabitants of the Red Sea can be seen here.
