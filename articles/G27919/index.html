More on symptoms

Where can offspring come from? Is telegonia so manifested? Is it true or fiction all these provisions of the theory? The fact of the manifestation of such signs is explained as follows.





  1. Atavism. This is a case of an unpredictable appearance of a trait inherited from wild ancestors. For example, multisupport, excessive hairiness, the presence of a tail, wisdom teeth and so on. The phenomenon manifests itself as the result of a genetic reversal, that is, an unpredictable secondary mutation that restores the genome modified by the primary one.
  2. Phenotypic reversion. The phenomenon occurs when different genes interact. For example, the manifestation of resistance to antibiotics, a large need for amino acids or vitamins, a change in sensitivity to temperature, and so on.
  3. The manifestation of recessive traits as a result of cleavage with certain combinations of parental genotypes. Basically, the phenomenon manifests itself in parents with strong heterozygous lines.

Based on scientific data that have been obtained experimentally and repeatedly confirmed, we can say that the science of telegony has no foundation.

Scientific explanations of the phenomenon



What do scientists say about this? When asked about telegonia - what is it, geneticists answer that this is a manifestation of certain signs in the offspring, which the parents did not have, but the distant ancestors did. Thus, recessive signs are manifested, as well as atavisms, spontaneous secondary mutations that restore the genome modified by the primary ones. The fertilization process is accompanied by the birth of a zygote with a double set of chromosomes, which is inherited by each cell. Half of the genetic material is inherited from the producer of the egg, the other part from sperm. In the case of penetration of several sperm into the female reproductive cell (the phenomenon of polyspermia), the egg cell combines with only one sperm. Numerous experiments have shown that a genetically black embryo of an animal that develops inside a white mother always grows into a black individual, even if it was transported into the body of a non-native female. Thus, the law of telegonia has no support in the fields of genetics and reproduction. How many sciences - so many hypotheses.
