Why is my phone not charging?

Technology has firmly penetrated our lives. This is especially true for mobile phones, which already have all adults and almost every child. If just ten years ago they seemed like interesting new products, now even the most sophisticated mobile phone will actually not surprise anyone. However, with the complexity of the technique, the number of possible malfunctions increases. A client buys a mobile device, and at one point he begins to work poorly or refuses to work at all. One of the most common is the question: "Why does not the phone charge?".



A cell can quite easily fail on its own. This is a very common breakdown. It is with her that people mainly come to the cell repair masters. However, to find out why the phone does not charge, to conduct a thorough diagnosis is the most important task. Initially, you can determine the probable cause of the problem yourself. This is done in order to understand how much the owner of the cell will spend on its repair. In some cases, the problem can be eliminated even on their own, without resorting to the help of a specialist in the repair of equipment.

The main reasons why the phone does not charge, as well as some diagnostic and troubleshooting options, are as follows:





  1. Damage occurred due to malfunctioning charger. In this case, it is not difficult to verify. You need to take an identical charger from the exact same model of mobile phone. The main thing is to find exactly the same model. For example, the chargers for many models of Samsung and LG phones have identical connectors, but they have power supplied to different contacts. If you connect, for example, charging from Samsung to a phone from LG, then the processor of the phone or the battery may break. And if in the second case the repair (or rather the purchase of a new battery) is not so expensive, then in the event of a processor breakdown, its restoration (or the purchase of a new one) will fly to the client ‚Äúa pretty penny‚Äù. If the phone does not charge, it is easier and less problematic to buy a new one.
  2. The next common option is a malfunctioning charging connector. For diagnostics, you just need to move the phone jack. If in some position the charging has nevertheless gone, then you need to repair or change the socket. In this case, you can not do without the help of a specialist. Such a problem often occurs with owners of Samsung phones due to some design features of the charging sockets in their phones. Therefore, masters very often hear the problem that "the Samsung phone is not charging."
  3. Finally, software or some electronic components may fail. The cause of both software errors and circuit problems. Also, physical impact (shock) or water getting on the phone can lead to the phenomenon when the phone is not charging. In this case, it is strongly recommended that you do not assemble / disassemble the phone yourself, but consult a specialist.
