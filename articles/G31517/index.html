Belconti Resort Hotel 5 * (Belek, Turkey): description of rooms, service, reviews

Probably every experienced tourist at least once in his life spent a vacation in Turkey. This is one of the most popular resort countries, and it is deserved - here the European level of service is combined with traditional oriental flavor. When it comes to beach vacations, Belek is considered a good place to stop. This is a fairly large resort town, where there is everything you need for a comfortable pastime. Of course, there are plenty of hotels here, ranging from small, but cozy guest-houses to luxurious five-star complexes. And one of the most popular is Belconti Resort Hotel 5. Belek is a good place to spend a vacation, but before booking, you should learn more about the selected hotel.

Is the hotel location convenient? What living conditions can travelers expect? Can you hope for good nutrition? Is this a place to stay with your child? What do former hotel guests say? The answers to these questions worry many travelers.

Where to look for a hotel?

Of course, first of all, tourists are interested in the location of the hotel, because during the holidays you want to have everything at hand. Where is the Belconti Resort Hotel 5 located? Belek, namely a picturesque place near the beach - it is here that you will have the opportunity to spend a dream vacation.





By the way, this is a calm area, because only a garden and other hotels are nearby. On the other hand, the city center can be reached on foot, because the distance is only 800 meters. And here, tourists are already waiting for entertainment for every taste and budget. Antalya, one of the largest Turkish resorts, together with the international airport is approximately 35 km from Belconti Resort Hotel 5. Tours (package) already include the cost of a convenient transfer. The hotel itself also offers a similar service - for a small fee, you can order transport from / to the airport.

Hotel nutrition information and reviews

If you are going on vacation, you should take care of food, because even the most vivid entertainment does not seem attractive on an empty stomach. What can the Belconti Resort Hotel 5 offer? Turkey is a country that is famous for its all-inclusive service, and this hotel is no exception. It offers guests the main restaurant, designed for 1200 seats. There is a non-smoking room, as well as a spacious outdoor terrace with tables. It offers guests and five bars, in each of which you will be warmly welcomed and happy to prepare a delicious cocktail.

Beach and water activities

It's no secret that the vast majority of tourists go to Turkey, planning to have a good rest on the seashore. What can the Belconti Resort Hotel 5 offer in this regard? The beach is just a few meters from the main building and is part of the hotel grounds. This is a private coastal strip intended for rest of hotel guests. Of course, guests can use sun loungers, parasols and other beach accessories free of charge (towels are also included).





In their reviews, tourists note that the coast here is really luxurious - everything, including the entrance to the sea, is covered with soft sand, so there is no urgent need for special shoes. The beach is cleaned several times a day, so it is always a pleasure to spend time here.

By the way, residents are offered entertainment for every taste. You can always play volleyball, swim on a boat, ride a boat or pedalo. Near the shore there is a diving school. Tourists get a lot of fun riding a "banana" and water skiing. There are also children's attractions that will appeal to little travelers. In a word, all guests have fun here, regardless of age and gender.

Belconti Resort Hotel 5: entertainment and leisure for residents

On the territory of the hotel you can have some good fun - here even the most demanding traveler can find a lesson for himself. In the courtyard there are two large pools, which are a kind of center of social life. Here, guests communicate, swim, take sunbaths, do water aerobics, organize various team games. At certain hours of the day, water slides also work (there are four of them). There is also an indoor pool, which is ideal for relaxing in bad weather. You should definitely visit the SPA center - there is a sauna, a Russian steam room and, of course, a hammam. Experienced massage therapists are always ready to offer their services (you can sign up not only for relaxing, but also for wellness treatments).





Adherents of outdoor activities will not be bored either. The hotel has basketball and volleyball courts, as well as mini-golf and a full-fledged football field. It offers guests and ten tennis courts (racket rental, coach services, evening lights are additionally paid). In their free time, tourists enjoy playing billiards, bowling and ping pong, and compete in archery. There is a fitness center with a large gym. By the way, various group classes are regularly held here - you can sign up for yoga, Pilates, step aerobics, morning exercises.

As is customary, the hotel has animators who entertain guests throughout the day. And in the evenings you can count on a lot of positive emotions, because you can enjoy the performances of local groups, watch bright shows and performances. There is a disco club at night. There is also an open-air disco - young people (and not only) here have fun from the heart.

You can have a good time outside the territory of Belconti Resort Hotel 5. Belek is a wonderful place with its own attractions that are worth a look. In addition, the city has many travel agencies whose staff will help you organize a trip to the most interesting places in the country.
