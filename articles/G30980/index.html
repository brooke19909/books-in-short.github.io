Zlaten Rog 3 * (Bulgaria / Golden Sands) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

For several decades now, the Bulgarian Golden Sands resort has been one of the favorite vacation spots of our compatriots. Tourists from European countries often come here. The popularity of Golden Sands is explained quite simply: a short flight time, proximity to the airport, developed infrastructure, a wide hotel base, as well as a healthy climate, clear sea and magnificent beaches. Economy class hotels enjoy great success here, and almost everyone can afford a vacation in it. If you plan to spend a vacation in Golden Sands and are looking for an inexpensive accommodation option, then pay attention to Zlaten Rog 3 *. Today we suggest that you take a closer look at this hotel and find out what opinion tourists from Russia have already had on this hotel.





Location

The three-star hotel "Zlaten Rog" is located in a quiet place, next to the park area. The distance to the center of Golden Sands with a mass of shops, shops, supermarkets, restaurants, cafes and other infrastructure is about 400 meters. However, in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a couple of cafes, a supermarket and a bank branch. In addition, hotel guests can take a pleasant walk in the park. As for a beach holiday, the distance to the nearest beach is 400 meters. So guests of Golden Sands Zlaten Rog 3 * can be on the sea literally in 5-10 minutes. On the way, you need to cross the roadway (there is an underground passage).

Transport

The nearest airport to the hotel is in the city of Varna. The distance to it is only 25 kilometers. So upon arrival you can reach the hotel in just 20-25 minutes. You can move between neighboring settlements both by bus (the stop is located next to the hotel), and by taxi or rented car.





Housing stock

This hotel consists of 150 rooms located in one large five-story building with elevators. Zlaten Rog 3 * Standard Room (standard room) includes its own bathroom with shower and hairdryer, individual air conditioning, refrigerator, TV with Russian channels and a balcony. The floors in the rooms are either laminate or carpet. Part of the windows overlook a picturesque park area. Hotel guests can use in-room Wi-Fi for an additional fee. Apartments are cleaned daily. Maids renew their towels twice a week, and change their linen once every 7 days.

Nutrition

At their request, guests of Zlaten Rog 3 * can choose the appropriate food system. So, here you can order only breakfast, half board or full board. If you chose breakfast only, then during the day you can eat in one of the many restaurants of Golden Sands. According to tourists, they cook here very well, and the cost of the dishes is more than acceptable.





Infrastructure and entertainment

Hotel Zlaten Rog offers an outdoor pool with a section for children and an adjacent sun terrace. You can also have a good time playing billiards or table tennis, or by visiting the sauna.

Reception at the hotel is open 24 hours a day. Here you can leave valuables in a safe deposit box. The hotel also has a tour desk, car rental and laundry services.

Cost of living

Hotel Zlaten Rog 3 * fully justifies its status as a budget accommodation option. So, according to our compatriots, a seven-day tour in it can cost only 12 thousand rubles.

Hotel Zlaten Rog 3 *: traveler reviews of the location

According to the comments of our compatriots, this hotel is in a quiet place, next to the park area. The main infrastructure of Golden Sands can be reached in 10-15 minutes at a leisurely pace. However, near the hotel there is a cafe, a supermarket and a bank branch. By the way, experienced travelers are advised to change the currency here in banks, and not in private exchangers, since the latter offer a not very profitable rate. The distance to the beach is about 300-400 meters. You can reach the coast in 5-10 minutes. However, on the way back you will have to climb the hill, so it can take about a quarter of an hour. Please note that on the way to the beach you will need to cross a rather busy roadway. For the convenience and safety of pedestrians there is an underpass. A great advantage of the Zlaten Rog 3 * hotel, the reviews serve as proof of this, is its proximity to Varna, respectively, and to the airport. So, the journey from the air harbor to the hotel will take you only about twenty minutes. This fact was especially appreciated by travelers with children. In addition, many vacationers often traveled to Varna. This can be done either by bus (a stop is located next to the hotel) or by taxi. By the way, tourists warn that you should be very careful if you want to use taxi services. So, you must immediately negotiate with the driver the exact route and cost of the trip, otherwise you may be required to unreasonably large amount of money. Also, some travelers mention a pleasant walk to the Aladzhi monastery. The road to it runs through the park and takes about an hour.





Beach Vacation Impressions

About 300-400 meters separate the city beaches of Golden Sands from the Zlaten Rog 3 * hotel. Reviews of travelers who have been here come down to the fact that you can reach the coast in just 5-10 minutes by an unhurried step. But the way back will take a little longer, because you have to climb the hill. However, this fact practically did not bother anyone, since, according to tourists, it is always nice to take a little walk and burn calories gained at hearty lunches and dinners. The beaches here are in municipal ownership, so the use of sunbeds and umbrellas is paid. However, many of our compatriots did not rent chaise lounges, but conveniently settled on their mats or towels in unoccupied areas. By the way, the beaches here are sandy and very clean. The entrance to the water is gentle, so even children will be comfortable swimming. If you want to try ice cream, refresh yourself with a drink or have a bite to eat, then there are a lot of cafes, restaurants and fast foods on the promenade. In addition, there are shops, souvenir shops, a variety of attractions and much more. Please note that experienced travelers are not advised to buy food and souvenirs on the waterfront. This is due to the fact that as you move away from the shore the same things you can buy much cheaper. So if you want to buy something, then, leaving the hotel, go towards the market. According to our compatriots, the local shops have the most reasonable prices.
