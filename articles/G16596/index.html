Kuban - these are the best resorts in Russia. Rest in the Kuban

One of the most popular and best holiday destinations on the Black and Azov Seas is the resorts of the North Caucasus. Kuban is the Krasnodar Territory. This resort includes a huge number of interesting places.

Features of rest in the Kuban

Today this resort area is especially popular among residents of Russia, Ukraine and Belarus. Tourists claim that the service here is no worse than in European resorts. But the sanatoriums, which are located on the territory of the Kuban, are distinguished by their advantageous location, the quality of the services provided and relatively inexpensive prices. Kuban is a great health resort where everyone can improve their own health without a tangible blow to their pocket. Guests of the resort are offered a huge selection of recreation centers where the whole family can relax from the bustle of the city, think about their own problems in peace and solitude. Spend time surrounded by nature here at any time of the year. In addition, there is a great opportunity to organize various events in these places, such as weddings, birthdays or business negotiations. Very often in the Krasnodar Territory , rallies of scouts and various festivals are held.

Kuban is a container of various types of recreation. All kinds of sanatoriums, boarding houses, entire entertainment complexes provide their services here. Everyone can find something that suits his taste and possibilities. The resort attracts tourists with its affordable prices and promising development. Let's consider some resorts of Kuban in more detail.

Krasnodar





One of the largest cities in the Russian south is Krasnodar. It is located off the coast of the Kuban River. Krasnodar is famous all over the world for its unique, one of a kind sights. Upon arrival at this amazing resort, every tourist should visit a number of architectural monuments. The most popular place is, oddly enough, the water tower. Art lovers will love places like the Philharmonic Hall and the Arc de Triomphe. In addition, Krasnodar can please a number of attractions that are dedicated to certain topics. There are railway and military museums. But children can visit the menagerie, the aquarium and the planetarium. Kuban is a treasury of history. Truly, a unique place is the water tower. In the old days, about 2 hundred of the same towers were located throughout Russia, at the moment there are only 10 of them. And it is not known how many there will be in the next few years.

Gelendzhik





This picturesque place is located near Novorossiysk, at the very foot of the mountain range. It is this location that gives the city a certain cosiness and gives picturesque surrounding views, which the Kuban is rich in. Gelendzhik is rightfully considered her heart. The history of this small town dates back to the Stone Age. It is here that you can see the ancient buildings of the distant past. For a tourist it is better to come here in the summer. The city is turning into a huge entertainment center. There is a water area, amusement parks, the largest sports and dance floors open, themed cafes and restaurants. As for the latter, there are a huge number of them: you can choose an institution for every taste and budget. But the most important advantage of the resort of Gelendzhik is the sea. Clean beaches, azure water, the gentle sea - all this attracts thousands of tourists here.

Sights of Gelendzhik





The resort is distinguished by its natural assets. It is here that you can see the incredible beauty of the waterfalls, which will amaze with their fabulousness and grandeur. In these places the most beautiful photos are obtained.

In addition to natural attractions, the city is rich in museums. The holiday season is especially open here. Many tourists certainly want to get to this event. On the first July Saturday, all residents go to the carnival. This is an incredibly colorful and interesting event. The festive program includes a solemn procession in the city. Then, a concert is organized in the central square, where young and more experienced performers of various genres perform. You can also try your hand at mass sports competitions that are designed for all age categories. The bright end of the enchanting holiday is a magnificent salute, which adds even more solemnity. Not everyone knows that the Kuban is not only the sea and the sun, but also holidays, fun, amazing traditions.

Slavyansk-on-Kuban





This city is located on the banks of the picturesque Protoka River. It has a very mild climate, so people of any age category will be able to relax at the resort and will feel very comfortable. The sandy beaches are regularly cleaned. The city has a balneotherapy center where people from all over the world come to improve their health.

Interesting places





They visit this resort not only for relaxation, but also for spiritual enrichment. Slavyansk-on-Kuban is rich in interesting architectural monuments. These are cathedrals, art schools, and residential buildings that have been preserved since the 19th century.

Museums of the city are also interesting to visit. Here you can touch the history of the Kuban Cossacks, learn about their life and traditions.
