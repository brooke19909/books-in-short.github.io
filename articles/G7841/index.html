Intercontinental Abu Dhabi 5 (UAE): description and photo

A wonderful hotel, which is possible to spend the best vacation at the level of five stars, is Intercontinental Abu-Dhabi 5 *. Every year, only those travelers who value luxurious living conditions and top-level service relax in it.

general information

Intercontinental Abu-Dhabi 5 * was built quite a long time ago - in 1980, but such a long existence only contributes to the growth of the incredible popularity of this place for recreation. In order to maintain the beautiful and fresh appearance of its main building, various reconstructions and renovations are regularly held here. According to tourists, the hotel is well equipped from the technical side: all apartments have individual air conditioning, which allows each guest to feel comfortable, which rests in his apartment in any weather on the street.



Location

Intercontinental Abu-Dhabi 5 * is located in the central part of the largest metropolis, in the most valuable place for tourists - on the seashore. It is only 35 kilometers from the central airport in Abu Dhabi, and 160 kilometers from what is in Dubai. All guests who come from other countries have a ticket for flights to Abu Dhabi Airport in their packages - a car picks them up from here, which provides a transfer service.

All the best places for entertainment and beaches are concentrated in the vicinity of this hotel. Tourists who relax here often share their various experiences of visiting various places, among which local restaurants and bars, as well as night clubs are very popular. Often guests visit the spa, which is located next door to the hotel, as well as The Bayshore Beach Club.

Rooms

The official description of the Intercontinental Abu-Dhabi 5 * hotel contains 390 apartments, which are located in one large 19-story building. Of the total number of apartments listed, only 198 are non-smoking - the rest is allowed.

All apartments presented in the fund are divided into two categories: Deluxe and Club Room. Deluxe group apartments are also divided into two types, depending on which side the windows face (on the sea or in the neighborhood of the metropolis).

In all rooms, regardless of category, only modern furniture is installed, fresh repairs are also made here. Tourists note the beautiful interior that is presented in the rooms - it is made without the use of bright colors, with a lot of expensive materials: wood, velvet and glass.

The rooms without fail have a luxurious bathroom, which is located separately from the bathroom. It has an excellent bathing place with a built-in hydromassage, as well as several nightstands that store bath accessories, bathrobes and slippers. The towels presented here are replaced with new ones three times a week.

The open area has an aquazone. Judging by the reviews of Intercontinental Abu-Dhabi 5 *, tourists really like to visit it, especially on the hottest days.
