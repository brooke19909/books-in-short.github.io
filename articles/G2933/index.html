Hotel Ibis Pattaya 3 *, Pattaya - description, rooms and reviews

After the hot days spent on Turkish and European beaches, our compatriots are already starting to plan their next vacation and are considering Vietnam and Thailand as a suitable option. In these Asian countries it is possible to have an interesting and inexpensive rest when frosts and snowdrifts lie on the expanses of our country. If you want to simultaneously save and spend time at sea, then go to Pattaya. Here is a wide selection of hotels at affordable prices, among which it is very difficult to choose the best for yourself.

For those who plan to spend their time actively on vacation and return to their sleep-only room, we can recommend the Ibis Pattaya 3 * hotel in Pattaya. He receives high marks from tourists from Russia, so today we decided to devote an article to him. From it you can learn all the nuances of living in Ibis Pattaya 3 * in Pattaya, as well as its main features.





Description of the hotel complex

Ibis Pattaya 3 * in Pattaya is a network hotel. Moreover, almost all hotel complexes of the network stand out for comfort and coziness. They carefully monitor the design of rooms, and the modern design of hotels is always pleasing to the eye.

Ibis Pattaya Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) is a seven-story residential building located almost in the center of the resort. The hotel is one of the newest in Pattaya; it was commissioned about nine years ago. The building has three hundred rooms, a restaurant and bars.

The hotel complex has virtually no territory of its own; it is extremely modest. However, it housed a large and beautiful pool with a compact relaxation area.

Ibis Pattaya 3 * (Pattaya) is located on the second coastline. Tourists often write that it takes no more than ten minutes to get to the sea from here. If you plan to travel frequently to the nearby island of Koh Lan, then you need to walk about twenty minutes from the hotel to the ferry.

Hotel Location

From reviews of Ibis Pattaya 3 * you can find out that the hotel is located close to shops, shopping centers and main tourist attractions. For example, the distance to the aquarium does not exceed six kilometers, and the famous jewelry center is only five hundred meters from the hotel.

The direct location of Ibis Pattaya 3 * is Second Road or Second Street, as it is also called. In Pattaya, streets and alleys are called depending on their location relative to the sea coast. Therefore, it immediately becomes clear how long it takes to go to the beach.

The hotel is quite far from the international airport. After arrival, tourists will have to travel about two and a half hours; if there are traffic jams, the road will increase by about another hour. However, a similar path is carried out by everyone who travels from Bangkok to Pattaya.





Beach Feature

Almost all the beaches in the resort are municipal. Both visitors and local residents rest on them. The beach consists of small sand and, according to tourists, is quite clean. Sun loungers and umbrellas need to be rented, with some bargaining skills they can cost you no more than twenty baht. And when renting several sunbeds at the same time, you can throw off another ten to fifteen percent of the declared price.

Beach towels are not provided at the hotel, so take care of this yourself. Judging by the reviews of Ibis Pattaya Hotel 3 *, vacationers get everything they need, including towels, in the nearest shops.

Pay attention to infrastructure

We think you already understood that the hotel complex is not distinguished by an abundance of infrastructure. Therefore, it refers to the budget, although in many ways it resembles a good four.

Ibis Pattaya 3 * (Pattaya) has one restaurant and a bar. Mostly guests only have breakfast here, however, many liked the tasty and inexpensive dinners, which delight guests of the local chef.

The hotel complex has a large swimming pool. Its bottom is tiled, from which the image of turtles is formed. Around the pool are sun loungers and umbrellas. You can use them absolutely free, and towels are also freely given. However, note that taking them to the beach is prohibited.

The hotel has two conference rooms. Due to the fact that the hotel itself is quite compact, the capacity of the halls is also quite small - for twenty and fifty people. They are well equipped and it will be quite pleasant to hold business meetings in them.

Ibis Pattaya 3 * (Thailand / Second Road) has a cozy Internet cafe. It accommodates no more than ten people, but usually in a cafe there are five to six guests.

At the 24-hour front desk, tourists can exchange currency, rent a car or motobike, and use the safe. This service requires an additional charge.

Service level

First of all, Russian tourists are worried about whether there is free Wi-Fi in the hotel. We hasten to please you - all guests of Ibis Pattaya 3 * (Pattaya) can use the Internet at the hotel complex.

The hotel provides laundry and dry cleaning services. This service is paid, but its cost is quite acceptable for budget travelers.

If necessary, guests can check in luggage storage. This is done quite often, since the apartments in the hotel have a small area that should not be cluttered with things.

Maids clean their rooms daily. Guests in their reviews claim that they did not have any complaints about the quality of cleaning during their stay. Rather, on the contrary, the rooms were always perfectly clean, and the towels changed even when they had not yet been used.





Hotel compliments

Despite the fact that Ibis Pattaya 3 * belongs to budget hotel complexes, it is ready to provide its guests with two types of compliments:

  • in honor of a birthday;
  • newlyweds.

If you come to rest in your name day, you will definitely receive a festive cake from the hotel administration.

Newlyweds must notify the hotel of their celebration in advance at the booking stage. In this case, a surprise will be waiting for them in the room.

Vacation with children

Should I come to this hotel complex with children? If you focus on guest reviews, you can advise you to choose another hotel for a trip with your child. The fact is that here is a very small territory in which the kids will simply have nothing to occupy themselves. They can only spend time in the children's section of the shared pool.

When traveling to Thailand, keep in mind that there are no special conditions for children in these hotels. Many large chain hotels do not even have playgrounds and clubs. Therefore, be prepared to entertain your child on vacation on their own.

Also in Ibis Pattaya 3 * there are often noisy companies, because of which children will not be too comfortable. Be sure to consider this nuance when choosing a hotel complex for families.





Settling with children of different ages

The hotel provides several options for checking in rooms with children. For example, babies from birth to three years can live with their parents absolutely free. They are accommodated in existing beds or in a separate nursery.

A four-year-old child can be accommodated on an existing bed for free. Children from five to eleven years stay free of charge with their parents on the bed, and if you need an extra bed, it will cost about seven hundred and six baht per day.

A child from the age of twelve is checked in only if an extra bed is available. Parents will have to pay seven hundred and six baht per night.

Additional Information

The hotel complex offers accommodation with pets, so your pets can go on a trip instead of waiting for the owners of the house.

Check in at the hotel at two in the afternoon, check-out is from seven in the morning until noon. Before booking your apartment yourself, be sure to read the cancellation policy. In some cases, this will not be possible.





Dining at the hotel complex

All guests note that the restaurant in the hotel looks very cozy. Tourists can only take breakfast or breakfast and dinner. Most often, vacationers have lunch and dinner in the many cafes that are located in almost every corner in Pattaya.

Breakfast is served in buffet style. Guests often write in their reviews that they are pretty monotonous, but always fresh and tasty. Surprisingly, we did not track the guests dissatisfied with the food among the reviews. All of them noted that from time to time they had dinner with great pleasure in the hotel restaurant. The average bill in this case does not exceed six hundred and fifty baht.





A brief description of the number of rooms

Apartments in the hotel are divided into two categories:

  • standard;
  • family apartment.

In general, the rooms are identical, but family rooms have a large area and a cot for a child. In some cases, children are accommodated in bunk beds.

Each room is equipped with a safe, tea set, coffee maker, telephone and Internet access. They are also equipped with individual air conditioning and cable TV. Some rooms do not have a balcony, they overlook a busy road, so for security reasons they do not even open windows.

The bathrooms are quite simple, but they have a hairdryer and a shower. It is noteworthy that in the family apartments there is a game console for children.

The standard rooms in the hotel complex are two hundred and forty-six. Their area does not exceed twenty one square. It offers views of the pool or the city.

Family apartments have a slightly larger area - thirty-four square meters. They all go to the pool, four people can settle here.

Three rooms for people with disabilities are identical to standard apartments, but only two people can stay here instead of three.





Hotel Ibis Pattaya 3 *, Pattaya: reviews

What do people say about holidays in this hotel complex? Most of them are very pleased with their stay, but they also point to some of the hotel's shortcomings:

  • contingent consisting mainly of noisy and restless Indians;
  • small area;
  • lack of balconies in some rooms;
  • poor sound insulation in apartments located near the elevator;
  • poor knowledge of English by the staff.

Ibis Pattaya 3 * has a lot of advantages:

  • excellent location from all points of view;
  • impeccable cleanliness in the room;
  • delicious breakfasts;
  • nice pool;
  • Friendly staff
  • operational work of the restaurant and employees;
  • bicycles for rent.

The hotel is recommended for young people, fun companies and active couples who are ready to get to know Pattaya all day long.
