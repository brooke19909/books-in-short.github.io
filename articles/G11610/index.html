Tilda monkey patterns from fabric

Tilda's toys and dolls have recently become quite popular. They look funny, comfortable and cause interest not only in children. For many, making dolls has become a kind of hobby. In the new year, the decorative monkey Tilda is very popular. The pattern of the toy is quite simple and does not require special skills in sewing. With a little imagination, you can create a wonderful monkey and no one will definitely have such a second.





What to sew a doll

In order to make Tilda, even old clothes can be used, because it happens that a thing has not been worn for a long time, and it is a pity to throw it away, because the fabric is of high quality. Surely every housewife has such options. Pieces of chintz, fur, lace - all come in handy. The basis of the pattern of the monkey Tilda The original version of traditional dolls will be used.





Tools and materials

To get started, prepare the necessary materials and accessories for sewing. You will need the following:

  • paper for a pattern of Tilda's monkey;
  • fabric cuts, you can choose according to your taste;
  • scissors, needles, pins, threads;
  • sewing machine (you can without it);
  • synthetic winterizer for stuffing toys;
  • two black beads for the eye.

If you wish, you can make a boy and a girl of monkeys to make them more fun. For sewing the base (body), it is good to use knitwear, mainly brown or beige, you will still need a little lighter cotton for the muzzle. The girl will need to sew a dress, chintz with a small cheerful pattern, lace, satin ribbons will do. For a boy from soft denim, you will need to sew shorts, and a shirt from any bright material.

Preparation for cutting

Now you can arm yourself with a pencil, paper and begin to build a pattern. A full-size tilda monkey is rather bulky, you can make a toy of a size convenient for you, the main thing is to observe the main proportions. Transfer the pattern proposed in the photo onto the paper, you can simply redraw it by hand or print to the printer in the required size. Cutting out the tilda monkey pattern will not cause trouble, children can use scissors. It is useful to attract a child to help, participation in the process develops fine motor skills of hands, instills skills in needlework and gives a lot of pleasure.





If you print the diagram on sheet A4, the doll will turn out to be about 35 cm high. You do not have to try to make it a copy of the original, let it be your unique toy.





Tilda (monkey), pattern

A master class with detailed instructions is not so necessary, just attach a little imagination and creative mood. Step-by-step instructions for transferring patterns from paper to fabric:

  • fold the brown fabric in half so that it is convenient to cut paired details;
  • lay on it the parts of the pattern cut out of paper: body 1 pc., arms 2 pcs., legs 2 pcs.;
  • make one piece of dark color on the ears, and the inner pieces will look better from light material, also 1 pc each, and cut out the pattern of the muzzle from it;
  • circle the elements with small pieces and cut with an allowance of up to 0.5 cm so that the fabric does not climb when stitching.

The Tilda monkey pattern from the fabric is ready. You can proceed to the next step.





Connection parts

Start with the muzzle, this is an important moment to make your toy look like the original Tilda doll. The monkey will turn out wonderful!

We connect the patterns of the upper and lower parts, having previously stitched a tuck from above, thanks to it the face will look three-dimensional.

There is the final touch. Tilda traditional dolls have very small bead eyes. Therefore, small black beads can be sewn as an eye. Or just make a few stitches with black thread and tint around the seams with a simple pencil or felt-tip pen to give a natural look. Well, now everything is ready. Agree that the Tilda doll is easily sewn. The monkey (pattern and detailed explanation are given above) turned out like a living! Now you need to take care of her clothes.





Dress for Tilda

Do not hesitate, the children will be delighted with what is happening! At such a moment, they certainly will not depart from you. Choose together with the child the fabric for the dress of the monkey girl, figure out how to trim the edges with lace.

First, a pattern is drawn on paper, cut out, transferred to the material and cut out on fabric with allowances for seams. It is important to keep the dimensions for your monkey. If you made a big doll, then the dress needs to be made bigger, observing the proportions.

The outfit is sewn quite simply. It is necessary to stitch the side seams, then the shoulder. To process the edges with a seam in the hem; well, sew on lace and ribbons for beauty. You can sew several different outfits for different occasions, then your daughter will definitely not part with the toy for even a minute. Putting on a Tilda monkey dress is not difficult, it is easier to do through the legs, slightly bending the doll.





Clothes for a boy

Dressing up a little monkey boy is a little more difficult, since you need to sew both shorts and a shirt. For pants, you can add a rectangle of fabric, make an incision from the bottom, sew the edges. You can sew a narrow elastic band in the belt, especially if the boy will participate in the games of children. The shirt can be made according to the dress pattern, only a little shorter, in a boyish style - without lace and bows.

Clothing can be detailed and come up with a small collar or pocket.





Additions

And if imagination breaks out, you can think up a lot of additional things for monkeys. Sew winter options for clothing, hats, boots, knit a small scarf. Don't be afraid to experiment with doll makeup. To make it brighter, you can tint cheeks with blush or grow hair from threads. And by all means you will get the most beautiful monkey Tilda.

A bit about Tilda dolls

The Tilda doll is an original toy made of fabric, loved by many for its simplicity and warmth. The first doll appeared at the turn of the 21st century. Tilda soon became famous all over the world, and both children and adults adore her. In recent years, various versions of dolls have been invented: Tilda-hare, Tilda-kitty, Tilda-monkey (described above), as well as a slush doll for decorating little girls' bedrooms.

Distinctive features of dolls: natural body color with a slight tint of tan, long graceful limbs, small eyes, pink cheeks, elegant clothes sewn from natural fabrics, like the whole body.



The beauty of the toy is that it is created with your own hands, and you cannot meet two absolutely identical dolls. Having tried once to create a toy, it is already impossible to leave this hobby.

If you get the original Tilda doll (monkey), you can save the patterns for the collection. And try to sew other options for the toy.
