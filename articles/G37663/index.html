The best hotels of Tuscany: photos and reviews of tourists

Tuscany is a region of Italy known for its resorts. On its coast are Lido de Camaiore, Forte de Marmi, Marina de Pietrasanta, Viareggio. Tuscany has long established itself as a prestigious holiday destination in Italy. Its resorts have a well-developed infrastructure and the best hotels are located, among which you can find establishments for every taste: from rural villas to spa hotels. Tuscany is a prestigious holiday destination that every tourist would like to visit. We want to devote our article to the review of the best institutions of the resort.

Italian baths

Holidays in beautiful Italy are not only a beautiful sea, coast, but also an opportunity to improve your health at the thermal resorts known throughout the world. The best terms are located in Tuscany. Their healing properties have been in demand since ancient times, when the region was inhabited by Etruscans. There are thermal springs here, around which entire cities have developed and built over the centuries. Amazingly beautiful nature, pines and cypresses, clean air - all this has a rest for a full treatment and relaxation.

Among tourists who go on vacation, Tuscany hotels with thermal springs are incredibly in demand. Such institutions are good in that they allow holidaymakers to get better on vacation and at the same time spend time at sea. If the issue of recovery is more relevant for you, then you can go to one of the thermal hotels in Tuscany, not only in the summer, but also at another time of the year. Terme, like hotel establishments, operate year-round.

Hotels Montecatini Terme

The most famous thermal resort of the region is Montecatini Terme. It was the hot mineral springs that glorified the town in the center of Tuscany. It is located at the foot of the Ponteraite hill, 40 km from the Tyrrhenian Sea and 30 km from Florence. The picturesque town attracts with flowering streets, friendliness and tranquility of local residents, beautiful architecture.

It contains nine thermal complexes: Grocco, Redi, Excelsior, Tamerici, Salute, Torretta, Leopoldine, Tettukkio, Regina. The resort is relevant for those people who suffer from diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, musculoskeletal system, ENT organs.

The Tuscan thermal hotels offer first-class service and a wide range of services. They, as a rule, are located near sources so that vacationers do not have to go far. One such establishment is the Grand Hotel La Pace Spa. The five-star Tuscany hotel is near the Tettukkio and Excelsior. The institution was built in 1870 and is more like a palace than a medical complex. Its building is surrounded by a park with beautiful trees. There are two restaurants on site. The chefs of the institutions offer guests delicious dishes of European and Tuscan cuisine.

Inside, the hotel is decorated in an Art Deco style. The comfortable rooms are equipped with modern facilities. The nineteenth-century building itself served as a source of inspiration for artists. He was repeatedly filmed in feature films. The hotel's spa is equipped with a bathhouse, a heated pool, massage chairs, a hydro-shower and other treatment rooms.

According to tourists who have been here, the Grand Hotel La Pace Spa is one of the best spa hotels in Tuscany, where you can get a full range of quality services.

Grand Hotel Croce di Malta

Another wellness hotel in Tuscany is the Grand Hotel Croce di Malta. The 4-star complex can be safely called one of the best in the city. It was completely updated in 2010. In its equipment the smallest details were taken into account. The hotel has over one hundred rooms equipped with all modern amenities. Marble bathrooms feature the finest amenities.

The hotel complex offers accommodation in a completely new wing of the Prestige. The rooms are renovated and decorated by designers in the style of the latest fashion trends.

The hotel also has a gym, spa, pool with jacuzzi, massage rooms, showers. In the evenings, guests have the opportunity to relax in the bar and spend a pleasant time in the restaurant.

According to the guests, the hotel is very comfortable and pleasant in every way. Among the guests of the institution there are a lot of those who visit it every year, noting the good service and excellent living conditions.

Fonteverde Tuscan Resort Spa

Hotel Fonteverde is located near thermal springs. His building was built by decree of Archduke Ferdinand of the First Medici. The complex is located among the hills of the Val D'Orcia Valley.

Fonteverde is one of the hotels in Tuscany that has a huge spa with a wide range of treatments. Here you will find thermal and therapeutic pools, hydro-programs, massage parlors, programs to restore beauty and health. According to tourists, the complex is definitely worth a visit for those who want to get healthy and get a full range of procedures. The water complex of the hotel is really impressive in scope.

The rooms of the hotel, made in the classical style and equipped with all modern technologies, are no less attractive.

Tombolo Thalasso Resort

No less popular are hotels in Tuscany on the sea. Tombolo Thalasso Resort is one of the complexes on the Italian coast. The hotel has a free thalassotherapy center and a huge spa. It is surrounded by pristine nature, just a three-minute walk to the sea. According to tourists, Tombolo is one of the best hotels in Tuscany with its own beach. The length of its own plot on the coast is about 200 meters. The beach of the complex is deservedly awarded the Blue Flag.

The wellness center of the hotel is located in the grotto, after going to it, tourists can visit the hot tubs, sauna, thalassotherapy pool, Turkish bath, massage and beauty treatments. The complex also has fitness equipment.

For the kids, the hotel has a playroom. In the spa, you can also heal the child by ordering treatments. The restaurant serves delicious local cuisine. A buffet for tourists is served on the terrace with sea views.

"Pelican"

Among the luxury hotels of Tuscany in Italy there is the Pelican complex, which consists of a group of separate villas and one central building. All buildings are surrounded by greenery on the coast. Each room has comfortable rooms. The entire hotel can be called the exact opposite of traditional hotel complexes. Beautiful buildings, surrounded by greenery and flowers, resemble a paradise, devoid of the usual fuss. If you want peace and quiet, then Pelican is a great option. It is not for nothing that he has been chosen since 1965 for the rest of world-class stars.

The six cottages of the complex have 50 rooms. The interiors of each of them are individual and do not repeat. Simpler, rustic-style apartments are only present in the main building.

Of course, an institution of this level has its own spa and fitness room. At the request of the client, the staff selects treatment and wellness programs.

The hotel restaurant, awarded one Michelin star, is not inferior. Its chef invites tourists to enjoy Mediterranean cuisine. In summer, meals are served on the outdoor terrace. The hotel has its own wine cellar. Therefore, in the restaurant you can always order exquisite Tuscan wines or champagne. And an experienced sommelier will help make the right choice.

The hotel has a saltwater pool and its own private beach, accessible by elevator.
