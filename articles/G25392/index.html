"All for 600" (Beeline): description and reviews

So, today we will find out what the tariff "All for 600" ("Beeline") is. Unfortunately, if you look for information about this offer, then you will not find anything concrete. After all, now the company does not have such a tariff. Instead, it is now called "All for 500." But we will call it the old way. So let's study it. Is it worth it to connect to it? What features does the offer have? What do subscribers think about this offer?



general description

“All for 600” (“Beeline”) is a rather favorable tariff. The fact is that it belongs to the unlimited line of offers "Everything". Honestly, for this reason, many subscribers are interested in him. True, you should not think that you will be given incredible opportunities. Some restrictions apply.

In a general sense, “All for 600” (Beeline) is unlimited communication with all of Russia and in roaming. Messages and Internet traffic are included here. True, the offer has a lot of features. For example, all the limits offered to subscribers depend on what type of payment you have connected to yourself. It is worth considering them in more detail, and then choosing the best Beeline tariff for connection.

Prepayment

The first option that you can offer is prepayment. The cost of the proposal is 16.65 rubles. In some regions, this limit may be higher. This is a monthly fee. In most cases, according to subscribers, you will give 600 rubles a month.

In this case, the proposal may seem very tempting. If you were interested in the line "Everything", most likely, it has already become clear: this is the most favorable tariff for the Beeline company. 600 rubles per month - and you will receive 500 minutes per month of free calls to all numbers in the country. Also included in this price are SMS messages in the amount of 500 pieces, as well as 5 GB Internet traffic.



In principle, a rather interesting proposal. After all, after the limit is exhausted, local calls will remain free for you. And conversations in Russia and all SMS messages will cost only 1.5 rubles. MMS costs 5.5 rubles. As for Internet traffic, we can say that every 150 MB will cost 25 rubles. It is completely humane. For this, "All for 600" (Beeline) receives very good reviews from its subscribers.

Postpay

But do not forget about the second type of this tariff. There is a postpay. That is, you first spend all the possibilities, and then at the end of the month you pay for the contents of one or another connected package. Unlike the first option, this one offers more favorable conditions. The Beeline company created an unlimited tariff (600 rubles - its average cost) of this type especially for outgoing people. What can you enjoy with postpay?

Firstly, the monthly fee will be 500 rubles per month (it used to be 600, so the tariff was called “All for 600”). Moreover, the transition to the offer is absolutely free. In the same way as in the previous case.

Secondly, "All for 600" ("Beeline") with postpay raises all the limits. And this fact pleases many. We can say that all restrictions increase by 2 times. Unless the minutes of conversation remain in the same amount - 500. Traffic on the Internet is now given in the amount of 10 GB, and you receive 1000 SMS messages per month. Very interesting.

But this tariff has its own peculiarity. You will have to make a guarantee deposit when connecting: 700 rubles for the "International Communications" service. It turns out that the connection here is quite expensive. But this does not stop anyone. If you don’t know what tariff to connect to your mobile, Beeline will offer you a very good option - “All for 600”.



Connection

Next, you need to pay attention to the features of connecting tariff plans. The fact is that for post- and prepayment there are own approaches. And in this regard, “All for 600” (“Beeline”) receives mixed reviews from customers.

Why? The main disadvantage of postpay is that the connection can only occur directly at the Beeline office. You either give your mobile operator, which draws up an application, or buy a new SIM card with an offer already connected. Not very good prospects.

If you have chosen the prepaid option, you can only say that there is no negative connection with the connection. You can easily and easily call the Beeline operator at 0611 and ask for a request. Do not forget to say that they chose "All for 600" with a prepayment. In addition, an independent method for solving the problem is available to you. This is a special USSD team. In our case, it is 0674000500. It is also possible to connect via the official website. In principle, this option earns very good reviews. The main thing is to find "All for 600" in the list of offers.





Renouncement

But to abandon this tariff plan, as practice has shown, regardless of which payment option was chosen, it is easier. It is enough to come to the Beeline office and inform about your intentions to change the plan on the SIM card. In addition, at any time, you can absolutely completely abandon your tariff by switching to another one yourself.

It was the second option that earned a lot of subscribers' love. As you can see, connecting and disconnecting from All for 600 is very easy. But is it worth doing it at all? What is the general opinion of the customers on this proposal?

Practice

Honestly, it’s very difficult to decide. After all, someone is happy with this tariff, and someone is not very happy. In principle, Beeline lowered the bar of the monthly fee. Now, instead of 600 rubles per month, you have to pay only 500. This, of course, is good.



For the average subscriber, the cost of the tariff plan is too big. In this case, you can choose an unlimited tariff, which will be cheaper. All for 600 can be offered to those who communicate a lot (especially through messages) and work with the Internet on a mobile.
