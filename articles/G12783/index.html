Rostov the Great: how to get from Moscow by car, train, train, bus

One of the regional centers of the Yaroslavl region is the city of Rostov the Great. How to get from Moscow to this city? By Russian standards, the distance between cities is quite small, so you can get from one settlement to another in a few hours.

Way "Moscow - Rostov Veliky" by car

The distance between cities in a straight line is 194 kilometers, but in a straight line you can fly only by air. If you select the closest land route, then you will need to drive 214 kilometers by car. Driving about 1 kilometer per minute, you will reach your destination in 3 hours 23 minutes. What settlements will need to travel on the way from Moscow to Rostov the Great?





The journey from Mokva to the Brotherhood takes approximately 36 minutes (39 kilometers). After driving 22 km further, your car will reach the village of Leshkovo. On the way to this segment of the route will have to spend 20 minutes. Ryazans are located 3 kilometers from Leshkovo. The next stopping point is located at a distance of 14 km (we are talking about NIIRP, the travel time to which from Ryazantsev will not exceed 9 minutes). After the research institute, you will have to spend about 46 minutes on the highway until you reach the village of Novoe (44 km distant from the research institute). By the way, on this segment of the distance you will move from Moscow to Yaroslavl region.

After Novy Novy, after 8 kilometers your car will pass through the village of Glebovsky, and after another 4 kilometers the route will pass through Schelkanku. The next settlement on the way from Moscow to Rostov the Great will be Pereleski. From Schelkanki to this village 39 kilometers. In the middle of the path between Pereleski and Rostov is Petrovsk. From it to Perelesok 20 kilometers and to Rostov the Great 22 km. Well, here we have reached your destination!

Way to Rostov the Great: how to get from Moscow by bus?

Buses run from Moscow to Rostov the Great all day. I would like to note that the bus "Moscow - Rostov the Great" is a passing flight to Kostroma or to Rybinsk. The first flight towards Kostroma leaves from the Shchelkovo bus station in Moscow at 7 a.m. At 8:20 the transport leaves from Sergiev Posad AS, and at 9:35 it leaves from Pereyaslavl-Zalessky AS. The last stop in front of Rostov the Great is Petrovsk. The bus leaves from the Petrovsk bus station at 10:39, and arrives in Rostov Veliky at 11:05. On the same route and with a stop at the above bus stations, buses leave at 12:50, 10:30, 9:00, 14:30 (to Yaroslavl), 21:00, 23:00, 15:00. This is one of the ways to get to Rostov the Great.





How can I get from Moscow to Rostov by bus? Flights "Moscow - Rybinsk" depart from AS Shchelkovskaya in the capital of Russia at 12:00 and 16:00. Buses go to Rostov the Great 3 hours 34 minutes and pass the following stations: Sergiev Posad, Pereyaslav-Zalessky, Petrovsk. According to such a schedule, the Moscow-Rostov Veliky bus runs.

Railway connection

Trains also run through Rostov the Great. How to get from Moscow to the district center by train? In this direction, flights depart from the Yaroslavl station daily and at different times. Travel time (regardless of flight) will take 3 hours 4-5 minutes.





Now consider what trains go from Moscow to Rostov the Great? The flight Moscow-Syktyvkar (the capital of Yakutia) leaves for the route at 13:05. At 16:09, the train will arrive at Rostov the Great Station, making a one-minute stop in the city of Alexandrov (at 14:55). At 19:05 and 14:45, trains depart from the Yaroslavl station to the neighboring regional center - Yaroslavl. Passenger trains following this route reach the railway station we need in 2 hours 36 minutes, without making intermediate stops.

On Mondays at 20:20 the Moscow-Vologda train follows, and on Fridays the Moscow-Kotlas train leaves at the same time. These trains reach the Rostov Veliky station in three hours. There are no scheduled stops at small stations along the route of flights to Rostov the Great. The night train to Kostroma leaves the Yaroslavl station daily at 23:20. He arrives in Rostov the Great at 02:13. In addition to the above, trains to Arkhangelsk, Vorkuta, Vladivostok, Khabarovsk follow to Rostov the Great . They are sent daily both at night and during the day.

"Moscow - Rostov the Great": train

For those who want to get from the capital of Russia to the regional center of the neighboring Yaroslavl region, while incurring minimal material costs, we can offer electric trains. True, it will take a little longer to get there than by train, but the train "Moscow - Rostov Velikiy" will take you cheaper. Perhaps, for some, a small minus of such a trip will be the need to change trains in Aleksandrov, because commuter trains from Moscow to Veliky Rostov do not reach. For a more expeditious completion of the trip, you need to pay attention to the timetable of trains from Alexandrov to Rostov (from Moscow in this direction there are very frequent trains).



Electric trains from Alexandrov to Rostov the Great run daily 4 times. Departure time, according to the schedule of the Russian Railways, is: 08:29, 13:40, 16:18 and 19:29.

How to get from Rostov the Great to Moscow by bus?

Passengers need to know the transport schedule in order to return and back to the capital. Such buses pass through the Rostov Veliky bus station:

  • "Yaroslavl - Moscow" (daily at 16:20);
  • "Rybinsk - Moscow" (daily at 02:05 and 09:15);
  • most often - "Kostroma - Moscow" (at 00:50, 01:45, 09:35, 11:25, 12:50 and 18:50).

How to return from the district center to the capital?

Note that through Rostov a lot of passenger trains travel to the capital. We are talking about flights from Arkhangelsk, Vorkuta, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, Pechora, Cherepovets. Kotlas, Vologda, Yaroslavl, Syktyvkar and Sosnogorsk. Between Rostov the Great and Moscow, most trains do not stop at all. Travel time will take from 3 hours 11 minutes to 4 hours 44 minutes (depending on the route of the train and the speed of movement in individual sections). The Rostov-Moscow train leaves every 2-3 hours. A passenger will not have to stay at the station for a long time. It is important to note that the opportunity to leave Rostov the Great (how to get from Moscow to this city, we examined above) is provided at any time of the day.





Tickets for the Rostov-Moscow train can be purchased at ticket offices of railway stations, as well as online through the Internet. Each passenger can choose a way of traveling depending on their material well-being and personal preferences.
