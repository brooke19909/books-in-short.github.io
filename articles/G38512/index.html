Sanatorium "Krasnousolsk" (Bashkiria): rest, treatment, prices, photos and reviews

The Ural region has long been famous for its amazing taiga forests, rich in natural resources and valuable mineral springs, which have received worldwide recognition in balneological practice. Resorts of the Urals have always attracted active travelers and connoisseurs of Russian culture. The tourism industry in this region is at a high level.





Among the enchanting silence of the Ural mountains and pine forest, wellness centers, hotel complexes and dispensaries, functioning year-round, rise. Judging by the reviews of people, one of the best vacation spots in Russia is the resort "Krasnousolsk", located in the most beautiful corner of the Urals - the Republic of Bashkiria. The sanatorium is located on the banks of the ice-free Usolka River, near salt springs and healing mud.

A powerful medical center has been created on its territory, where you can undergo course therapy with qualified doctors. Visitors receive treatment with medications and natural factors aimed at maintaining health and speedy rehabilitation. The health resort is equipped with modern diagnostic equipment and German-made equipment that helps doctors to conduct a quality and error-free examination.

Accommodation





Inside the chic park, seven comfortable cottages have been built, each of which has its own facade design and interior. Residential buildings are surrounded by a fence and are under video surveillance around the clock. Comfort, European comfort and safety - this is Krasnousolsk.

The sanatorium (prices are adjusted depending on the date of arrival and the range of services) has standard rooms that are equipped with new appliances and furniture. Some rooms have cozy loggias with a view of the park area. For the convenience of guests, each room has a private bathroom, satellite TV, radio and a landline telephone with direct dialing.

Accommodation in a single room (day) will cost about 2500 rubles. For privileged and demanding tourists, spacious apartments with three separate rooms and a fireplace room are offered. In luxury rooms, the price policy is much higher - from 7000 rubles. If you have the opportunity to pay for such a pleasure, then do not deny yourself this. The sanatorium accepts up to 500 people at a time.

Diet





Sanatorium "Krasnousolsk" has spacious dining rooms with two rooms. Serving is carried out as a buffet. The menu is balanced and designed by experienced nutritionists. Dishes are always diverse, fresh, selected taking into account the individual characteristics of the patients.

Fresh vegetables and greens from their own vegetable garden all year round on tables. The health resort has its own bakery. The assortment of confectionery and bakery products is quite rich. Guests will also be able to try a variety of varieties of honey from the apiary, which began to be productively developed since 2006.





In addition to catering, the complex has a restaurant "Tabyn" with a banquet room. For dear guests, the chef prepares delicious delicacies of Caucasian and Russian cuisine. Celebrations and business events are organized here. It offers guests a cozy cafe "Kugush" with a rich wine collection and original snacks.

You can spend time in a pleasant company and enjoy the local scenery in the summer bar on the banks of the river. You will be offered barbecue dishes, barbecue, natural fruit drinks and delicious cocktails.

Medical profile





Sanatorium "Krasnousolsk" (Bashkiria) is rightfully proud of the healing gifts of nature. Thanks to unique salt springs and muds, balneotherapy, inhalations, baths and irrigation are performed to help restore health and improve vitality. The main profile of the health resort is metabolic pathology, gastrointestinal tract and musculoskeletal diseases.

Gynecological, urological and nervous diseases are also treated here. Provides professional medical assistance to patients suffering from various genitourinary infections, dermatological and respiratory ailments. Each tourist goes through a specialized consultation (neuropathologist, therapist, gastroenterologist, physiotherapist, gynecologist, nutritionist, etc.) in the examination room and passes the necessary tests. After receiving the results, the doctor prescribes the appropriate therapy.

Types of therapy





Health resort "Krasnousolsk" actively applies mud therapy with the addition of electric current. Therapy is carried out in the form of hydrogen sulfide and sodium chloride baths. According to individual indicators, mud applications and wraps are prescribed. Balneotherapy is widely used, which increases the body's defenses and reduces the negative impact of external factors (baths, tampons, irrigation, drinking).

Another effective method is hydrotherapy with the addition of sea salt, aromatic oils, plant extracts. To increase tone, mood, performance and metabolism, healing souls are prescribed. According to individual indicators, the specialist will carry out spinal traction, monitor bowel cleansing and physiotherapeutic procedures.

For youth and beauty





Sanatorium "Krasnousolsk", whose photo vividly illustrate the services provided, is equipped with a modern spa. Each woman will be able to fully relax in a real Turkish bath, infrared cabin and a hot tub, as well as visit the solarium and massage room. The center provides cosmetic services with a variety of treatments for face and body.

Experienced masters of the center of hardware cosmetology "Estet" will help you regain youth and health. Services are aimed at the study and treatment of the epidermis. Anti-aging injections, mesotherapy, contouring, vacuum cleaning, depilation, scar resurfacing and many other procedures are performed. All salon specialists are trained cosmetologists with vast experience in the field of beauty.

Leisure activities





The hospitable and mysterious Bashkiria ...

Sanatorium "Krasnousolsk" (prices for accommodation and treatment must be checked with the administrator) - this is the place where time slows down. In any season there is something to do and what to see. In winter - skiing, sledding, ice skating, snowboarding, playing hockey.





In summer - swimming in the river, pool, water activities, cycling and hiking tours. The territory is equipped with sports fields and paths where you can skate, engage in collective games, run and jump. In addition, the resort has a fitness center, a karaoke bar, bowling, table tennis, a dance club and a large library. Everyone can visit a small temple, art gallery and museum. The choice of entertainment is so diverse that "eyes run up."

What does the Krasnousolsk health resort offer to children?





For children of different ages, a development center works. Under the close attention of an experienced teacher, children will engage in interesting and informative games, watch animated films and communicate. Kids will appreciate the outdoor playground with swings, slides and horizontal bars. Animation shows, sports and creative events are organized daily.
