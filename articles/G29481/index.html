Amazing Montenegro - where is it? Popular tourist destinations of the country

Montenegro - where is it? After all, it seemed that ten years ago there could be no talk of an independent state. Today it is a modern European country located in the southwest of the Balkan Peninsula. Every self-respecting tourist knows where Montenegro is located, and probably already visited there. Today we will talk about the features of this amazing young state.



Montenegrin variety

Let this country occupy a small territory (close to fourteen thousand square kilometers), on its open spaces there is a wonderful sea, and picturesque mountains, and clean rivers with lakes. As of 2014, 621.5 thousand people live in the state. Even on such a small territory, temperate continental and Mediterranean climates are perfectly adjacent. Even the name of the state, which means “black mountains” in Russian, gives a sense of the wildness of this area, but in fact it is not.

How to get to Montenegro

Of course, the most comfortable, fast and convenient way is to fly by plane to the state of Montenegro. Where does this country have an air gate? There are two international airports: in the cities of Tivat and Podgorica. Both of them are small but comfortable, have everything necessary for high-quality passenger service.

Another way to get into the country is by ferry from Italy. It runs between states all year round, and looking at the map, you can once again make sure that the distance between sunny Italy and Montenegro is small, moreover, such a road will bring great pleasure: after all, the path passes through the cleanest Adriatic Sea. Traveling by car, too, has not been canceled, however, like regular buses from neighboring countries.



Real estate in Montenegro

Each tourist season attracts more and more tourists to the country of Montenegro. Where is it located, what sea is washing it, such questions can arise only for those who have never rested here before. Having come to Montenegro for the first time, many travelers want to return here more than once. Some even think about purchasing their own housing (of course, with financial opportunities). Indeed, every year, holidays in small Montenegro are gaining more and more popularity, and apartment rental prices and tours are growing.





Boka Kotor Bay - a popular tourist area of ​​Montenegro

That is what Boka Kotorska Gulf calls to itself in a country with the name Montenegro. Where located, photographs of this natural attraction are known to many seasoned travelers. Where else can you see how the unearthly beauty of the sea finds its way between high cliffs, and the steep slopes dotted with Greek and Roman ruins. In addition, Venetian and Gothic buildings are best balanced on the Adriatic, in the Boka Kotor Bay. After all, this small country is a kind of bastion between East and West. For five centuries, the ancestors of the modern inhabitants of Montenegro not only restrained the forces of Islam, but also resisted fascism during the Second World War.

The city of Perast (Montenegro) - where is it?

During a trip to the Boka Kotor Bay, the tiny town of Perast, which is under the protection of UNESCO, will certainly be the first stop. This maritime town rooted in the pre-Christian era, was restored by the Venetian seafarers as a strategic harbor. It will be useful to walk along the streets that are overgrown with vines, fig trees and oleanders.





The cultural history of the country

To see the cultural history of Montenegro, you need to drive inland, in the heart of the region. It is worth visiting the Orthodox monasteries of Moraca and Ostrog. Despite the fact that they are positioned as tourist attractions, monasteries in Montenegro remain active places of worship and pilgrimage.

Moraca Monastery

Here peaceful tourism and traditions coexist, monks and nuns with foreign travelers. Visitors will certainly be surprised by baroque frescoes in the Moraca Monastery. Strolling through its gardens with an abundance of roses, visitors and nuns coexist well.

Ostrog Monastery

Ostrog Monastery - a vertical complex of cave temples and monasteries carved in the rocks high above the Zeta River, is a more visited attraction. Pilgrims from all over the world come here to see this shrine, which has a reputation for healing.

Rafting, Montenegro. Where is this extreme form of entertainment located?

To extend the feeling of relaxation in Montenegro, you need to go rafting on the Tara River. Gliding downstream, in calm clear water, driving along the rapids, you can allow yourself to start the trip dry, and then soak in the water. At the very end, you can glide over a quiet area surrounded by the walls of the Tara Canyon, the deepest in Europe.

Good parting words

Let your every trip to friendly Montenegro be remembered only with positive and vivid emotions. Have a good trip and good mood!
