Olympus SZ-14 camera review

Now you can easily find on sale a large number of budget and no less good cameras. Similar companies are issued by such companies as Nikon, Kenon, Samsung. You can add Olympus to their list. The Olympus SZ-14 was recently introduced. It is compact, small in size, and many customers like the technical specifications. You can buy this camera at the moment in specialized stores for 5500 rubles.



Description

The camera is released immediately in several price options. The most popular model is gray. For girls, those options that are painted in red are more suitable. But you need to know that no matter what tone the camera is for sale, its lens will be standardly black. If you turn on the device, then this part extends, so that you can achieve a 24-fold increase.

Optics

Small dimensions did affect the optics. Unfortunately, when working with zoom, the aperture drops. The manufacturer at an official presentation said that this parameter has an indicator of F3-F6.9. A similar drop occurs due to the huge number of optical parts in the case and, of course, due to the minimum size of the camera itself. This effect occurs even with the fact that the device has special lenses of several types. But worry about such a defect is not worth it. Due to the strong zoom, the shutter speed may increase, but this is not a strong drawback. The device has an optical stabilization of the double type. It works by shifting the matrix. Due to this, the pictures will not be blurry.

Housing

The Olympus SZ-14 camera body, the price of which is low, received a "hump" on the top. Below it is a flash that can be turned on or off if necessary. She is quite powerful. With its help, you can light up to 7 meters closest to the camera.

On the back is an LCD type screen. He is bright. To the right of it are buttons that are responsible for the controls. These keys are different in size and have their own functions. In the center is a multi-position disc. Of course, the developers took care of the presence of the buttons for turning on the video recording. It is located above all others and has a decent size. To call help, the manufacturer created a separate key.



Display

It is necessary to describe the display. As already mentioned, it is installed LCD type. The manufacturer used a pretty good screen, which shows itself best among competitors from the budget category. The resolution of the Olympus SZ-14 display (price indicated above) is 430 thousand pixels. The picture is as clear as possible, so you can consider any detail in the picture taken. On the screen, the device shows itself perfectly. It does not fade and the image is visible well. There is no sensor coverage. Buyers note that it is not needed. The menu is well implemented without it. Even a novice without experience using this device will be able to quickly and without instructions figure out what's what. The viewfinder is missing. But this solution is quite understandable, since if it were, the cost would increase by 50% or more.
