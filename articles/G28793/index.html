Hotel Cuong Long Hotel 2 *, Vietnam, Nha Trang: overview, description, features and reviews

Cuong Long Hotel 2 * is a comfortable and fairly budget accommodation option in the resort town of Nha Trang.

Resort Summary

Hotel Cuong Long Hotel 2 * offers its guests inexpensive, but quite comfortable accommodation with a wide range of services. So, for example, guests can use car parking, as well as wireless Internet, absolutely free of charge. In addition, staying at this hotel complex, you can get to the main street of the city in a few minutes and see all its sights, as well as visit infrastructure facilities.

The rooms of the Cuong Long Hotel 2 * hotel are simple and modest. But, despite this, the apartment feels comfort and homeliness. It has high-quality and comfortable furniture, as well as all the necessary household appliances. Thanks to this, you will not feel that you are far from home. And due to the fact that the decor uses wooden elements, you can better learn the local interior traditions. In addition, you will probably be interested in observing the life of the Vietnamese, looking from the balcony to the street.

Hotel Cuong Long Hotel 2 * has a good restaurant, where, in addition to local dishes, European dishes are also served. Having thoroughly eaten in the morning, go on a fascinating tour organized by a tourist office. You also have the opportunity to rent a bike and ride your own surroundings. Reception staff will kindly assist you in purchasing tickets for any type of transport.





Where is the hotel situated

Cuong Long Hotel Nha Trang 2 * hotel is located in the area, which is located very close to the city center. Here you will find many markets, shops, as well as catering and entertainment centers. You can take the train if you walk to the station, which is just a 5-minute walk from the hotel. Near the hotel there is a sailing club, water park and other attractions. The airport is 30 km away from this resort.





Resort Resort Guest Apartments

Resort Cuong Long Hotel 2 * (NHA) offers its guests to stay in one of the 38 comfortable rooms. They are divided into several categories according to cost and level of comfort. The following accommodation options are available to travelers:

  • The standard room gives you the opportunity to stay in a small but very comfortable room in which there is one or a pair of double beds.
  • Superior room differs from the standard in size. It can comfortably accommodate three guests. In this case, all beds can be single or a combination option is available.
  • Deluxe apartments are considered the best in a hotel. Noteworthy is their exquisite interior and a large amount of living space. Also feature of this room can be considered a picturesque view from the window of the sea.




Apartment Amenities

All rooms at the Cuong Long Hotel 2 * Resort (Nha Trang) include the following amenities:

  • individual air conditioning, thanks to which your room will always have a comfortable temperature, no matter what surprises the weather may bring;
  • each apartment has its own small, but quite comfortable balcony (in some rooms it opens onto a busy city street, and from some you can enjoy the sea);
  • a modern TV broadcasts a fairly voluminous package of cable channels (entertainment and music prevail);
  • there is a minibar, which can also be used as a small refrigerator for the food you bought;
  • the bathroom has a shower, a set of bath accessories (bathrobe, slippers and a shower cap), as well as cosmetics.




Positive reviews

Sufficient quality and affordable services can be offered to travelers by the resort hotel Cuong Long Hotel 2 * (Vietnam). Reviews of tourists about this hotel contain a lot of positive and approving comments:

  • the location is quite favorable relative to the sea, and also pleases a calm area, from which you can quickly reach the center;
  • the staff is very polite, professional, and it is also nice that everyone is constantly smiling;
  • every day, cleaners change linens, as well as towels, a cosmetic set is constantly updated (and even toothbrushes are reported);
  • at the reception you can get absolutely free drinking water (one small bottle is given per person per day);
  • the lobby was recently renovated, and therefore the first impression of the hotel is very pleasant;
  • on the beds in the apartments - very comfortable mattresses, after sleep on which the back does not hurt at all;
  • right at the hotel there is a currency exchange office with a fairly good rate and a small commission (although in a neighboring hotel the conditions are more favorable);
  • room cleaning is very high quality;
  • so as not to worry about the safety of money during an absence in the room, they can be left in storage at the reception, previously folded into an envelope.




Negative hotel reviews

Of the negative aspects characteristic of this resort hotel, based on reviews, it is worth noting the following:

  • there is an electric kettle only at the reception, queues are constantly forming behind it, so it is better to take a boiler with you;
  • there are no Russian-language programs in the cable channel package;
  • the windows in the rooms are very small, in addition, some of them are almost adjacent to the neighboring building;
  • the carpet in the room is quite dirty, but due to the fact that guests are given rubber slippers, this drawback is partially compensated;
  • due to the fact that the furniture in the rooms is quite ancient, there is some unpleasant smell;
  • for the hotel, the problem of insects is relevant, and it is especially unpleasant to meet huge cockroaches in the rooms;
  • due to poor ventilation, as well as too small windows that poorly transmit light and air, the rooms are very damp and mold and an unpleasant smell constantly appear.

Be careful when booking a room and check in advance if there is a window in it.
