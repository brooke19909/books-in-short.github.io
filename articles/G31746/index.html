Microsoft Lumia 430 Mobile Phone: Overview, Features and Reviews

The mobile phone, which has become the subject of our review today, is not in vain called the most affordable of those models that are based on the Windows Phone operating system. And we are talking about the Lumia 430 Dual SIM. Currently, the approximate cost of this device is about sixty dollars. The user in exchange for this money receives basic abilities that are placed inside a modern smartphone, dressed in a small but stylish case. So, we present to your attention Lumia 430 Dual SIM - the most affordable and "insensitive" device running the Windows Phone operating system.

Specifications





First, briefly list the parameters so that it is clear what we have to deal with. So, what does the device have? On board it is installed the Windows Phone operating system version 8.1 with a custom shell called Lumia Denim. The dimensions of the device in three-dimensional space are as follows: it is 120.5 in height, 63.2 in width, and 10.6 mm in thickness. At the same time, the approximate mass of the smartphone is only 128 grams.

Microsoft Lumia 430 is equipped with a screen with a diagonal of four inches. The display resolution is 800 by 480 pixels. Density - not higher than 235 dpi. The matrix of the screen is made by LCD technology. The system can be controlled using separate keys that are not combined with the display.

To store user data, the owner of Microsoft Lumia 430 provided 8 gigabytes. The device supports the installation of an additional external drive. This is a microSD memory card. The maximum supported capacity is 128 gigabytes. At the same time, only 1 GB of RAM is provided for using applications and operating system operation. It will not be enough, but it is not so critical to discard the smartphone.

Nokia Lumia 430 will not please its owner with battery life. The battery that is built into the device is a removable type and is designed for a capacity of about 1,500 mAh. This suggests that in standby mode, the smartphone will "live" for several days, in a continuous conversation mode - 13 hours, and when listening to music - 46 hours. By the way, it was about working in a second generation cellular network. In 3G time will be reduced.

The set of wireless interfaces consists of Wi-Fi, operating in the b, g, n, "Bluetooth" bands of version 4.0, as well as GLONASS and GPS.

Well, it's time to complete the listing of technical specifications. We will do this by talking about cameras. So, the main one has a resolution of 2 megapixels. She shoots video at a resolution of 848 by 480 pixels, with a frequency of 30 frames per second. The front camera is present, but its quality is disgusting. It is only 0.3 megapixels.

Build quality





Usually, the Finnish manufacturer launches quite high-quality products on the smartphone market in this regard. However, in this case, the build quality does not please the owners of the device. Sometimes you can hear the crackling of the back cover. She bends inward under strong pressure. Some time after the start of use, she simply begins to sing, creak and generally do what she wants and how she wants. In conclusion, on this point: if you constantly care about what appearance your device has, and it is really important for you, do not rush to purchase this model. Otherwise, you risk being disappointed. Scratches on the case remain free, it is practically not protected by anything.

But the good thing is that fingerprints do not remain on the back cover of the device. In order to leave any traces on the subject of our review, you will need to really, really try. Nevertheless, this cannot be said about the screen coverage. Evaluate on a five-point scale, it can only be a solid unit, without any chance of raising the score. It is almost impossible to erase fingerprints from the display. The only thing that can save the owner of the device at such moments is only a special cloth made of the appropriate material. For example, microfiber.

Controls Left side





In general, all smartphone controls are located in their usual places. On the left side we have a double key that allows you to adjust the volume in third-party applications, as well as change the sound mode of the device itself. Double keys are also called a rocker. They are small in size, but very pleasant to the touch. The difference in the materials used to manufacture the entire case and the keys individually is striking, so you should not even worry about missing the button.

Top end

It accommodates a jack for connecting a wired headset standard 3.5 mm. Headphones can be used in third-party applications.

Bottom end

On the opposite side there is a connector for connecting a synchronization cable of the MicroUSB standard - USB 2.0. As mentioned earlier, the cord is not included in the package. If you are going to regularly connect the phone to a personal computer or laptop in order to exchange information between devices, you will need to purchase a cable in a mobile phone salon, for example.

Front Panel

From the front, we can see a screen having a diagonal of four inches. There are also three touch buttons. They denote the commands "search", "back", as well as "home". At the top of the screen are sensors and a front camera, which has far from the best resolution. In general, the set of sensors pleases, it is useful when using a smartphone.

Conclusion and reviews

To be honest, the Lumia 430 is exactly the device that does not cause absolutely any emotions when considering, buying and using it. It is unlikely that the owner of the phone will admire something for a long time. Unless only the first time, and even then only because this device is better than the previous one. You can call the device a remedy for smartphones. Why? Because, as soon as you buy it and start using it, any desire to continue to have a similar device on hand will immediately disappear.

In general, the subject of our today's review is not capable of anything other than voice calls, text messaging and correspondence on social networks. A weak battery, poor-quality assembly, a small screen and cameras that are not impressive in quality - all these are just the first items on the list of negative qualities of the device that its owners made. We are not even talking about hardware stuffing. However, if you are going to make a present to your child, then the best contender for this place is just Nokia Lumiya 430.
