Technical points

Currently, according to the official Polaroid website, in Russia you can buy three models of cameras with instant printing:

  • Polaroid Snap
  • Polaroid Snap Touch;
  • Polaroid 300.

These three models have different polaroid image sizes. But first, consider their differences.

Polaroid Snap is a compact camera that is capable of immediately producing a captured image using ink-free printing technology. The camera has a resolution of 10 megapixels, several shooting modes, the ability to set a timer and make a series of frames. In serial shooting, it is capable of shooting 6 frames in 10 seconds.

Polaroid Snap Touch has the same specifications as the little brother, but is equipped with a touch screen. And the Polaroid 300 is a modern version of the classic instant camera that we all once loved.
