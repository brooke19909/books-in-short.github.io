The best on-ear headphones: manufacturer ratings and reviews

Many fans of listening to music have repeatedly encountered the problem of choosing good and high-quality over-ear headphones. After all, not only attention should be paid to the brand, it is worth considering the sound quality, how comfortable the headphones are, what characteristics they have, and much more. Also an important parameter is the price, because expensive does not always mean cool. In today's review, we will look at 4 very good over-ear headphones that will appeal to even the most fastidious connoisseurs of sound.

JBL T450BT

Open the headphone rating - JBL T450BT. It‚Äôs worth saying right away that this model is wireless, although there is also a T450, which is already connected via a standard 3.5 mm cable. Let's take a closer look at this model.

Contents of delivery

The T450BT on-ear headphones are supplied in standard, translucent packaging, through which you can view the headset itself and get acquainted with some characteristics. Inside the box, the user will find the usual equipment, consisting of: headphones, a USB cable for the charger, warranty card and instructions.

Appearance and specifications

Headphones have a folding mechanism. The cups bend inward and turn to the side. Thus, the headphones can be easily transported in a backpack or a small bag.

All controls and connectors are located below on the right headphone cup. There are volume control buttons. Between them - play and pause. A little further - the on / off button, LED indicator and microphone hole.

Now, regarding the technical specifications. Closed- type headphones have a frequency range of 20 Hz-20 kHz and a resistance of 32 Ohms, the Bluetooth version is 4.0. As for the built-in battery, it allows you to listen to music for 11 hours without interruption from a full charge.

In terms of sound quality, you can say so - a solid four with a plus. There are good and rich bass. The high frequencies are clearly audible, but the middle ones pump up a little. Of course, you can stretch them out using the equalizer, but not everyone will do it.

Reviews and cost

The reviews on the JBL T450BT headphones are mostly positive, although there are some complaints from users. The first is not too comfortable headband. With prolonged listening, it begins to put pressure on the head. The second is the midrange, as already noted. And the third - buttons are hard to grope for, it takes time to get used to. Buy on-ear headphones JBL T450BT at the moment you can from 2500 to 3800 thousand rubles.

Sennheiser PX 200-II

The following are top Sennheiser PX 200-II earphones. At one time, the PX 200 was incredibly popular, primarily because of the high sound quality. The updated version is a good continuer of the line and will appeal to all music lovers.

Equipment

Headphones are delivered in a small box with a transparent plastic insert through which you can see the headset when folded. Also on the package are the main characteristics and "chips" of the model.

Inside, in addition to the overhead headphones themselves, there is also an instruction manual, a warranty card and a small branded carrying case.

Characteristics and appearance of the model

Externally, the headphones look stylish, but at the same time their design is strictly for the amateur. The headband has a folding structure and is additionally reinforced with a metal arc. When folded, the headphones take up very little space and can be carried even in the pocket of a shirt or jacket.

In order for the user to be comfortable listening to music for a long time, the headband at the headphones has soft lining made of high quality leatherette. Ear pads are made of the same material.

Since the PX 200-II are wired headphones, they connect through a standard 3.5 mm jack. For convenience, a manual volume control was also placed on the wire.

Now about the characteristics. Closed- type headphones have a frequency range of 10 Hz-21 kHz, a sensitivity of 115 dB and a resistance of 32 ohms.

The sound quality is at a very high level primarily due to the wide frequency range. The bass sounds good, but in moderation. Regarding midrange and treble - no complaints. Everything sounds very clear and balanced. Lovers of the classics will be clearly pleased, as well as connoisseurs of other musical genres.

This model can be safely called one of the best over-ear headphones in its segment.

Price and reviews

Judging by the reviews, the Sennheiser PX 200-II still has some drawbacks, but they are not related to sound quality. Users note a strange design that not everyone likes, incomplete noise isolation, in particular due to soft ear cushions and problems with the volume control on the wire. Over time, it begins to junk and when it is used, extraneous noises appear that drown out the music. Also, in some 1-2 years after the purchase, the sound in the right cup begins to disappear. Most likely, this is due to sloppy operation.

If we talk about the price, then you can buy Sennheiser PX 200-II for 4000-6000 thousand rubles.

Urbanears Plattan ADV Wireless

Another good and high-quality on-ear headphones - Urbanears Plattan ADV Wireless. Few are familiar with the products of the Swedish company Urbanears, but in vain. In addition to bright design solutions and high quality workmanship, the Scandinavian headphones have excellent sound and at the same time cost their money.

Model Options

Urbanears Plattan ADV Wireless is sold in a small box of thin cardboard. The headset itself is shown on the packaging, and on the back are the characteristics of the model.

Inside the box, everything is quite traditional: a warranty card, instructions, headphones, a charging cable and a removable cable 3.5 mm to 3.5 mm.

Characteristics and appearance

Headphones look very cool. Many color schemes are available to choose from. Urbanears Plattan ADV Wireless are foldable, but even when folded they look a bit bulky. The headband is made of metal and covered with a fabric roller, which can be easily removed and washed. The ear cushions here are also soft, they put enough pressure on the ear and do not cause discomfort.

Controls and connectors are standardly located on the right cup. At the bottom there is a power button and a charging slot. The indicator is located above.

There are no ordinary buttons for control here, instead of them is a touch panel, which is located on the outside of the right cup. It is enough just to move a finger over it to perform certain manipulations.

As for the technical characteristics, everything is simple: closed-type headphones, frequency range 20 Hz-20 kHz, sensitivity 103 dB, impedance 32 Ohms. The built-in battery allows you to enjoy music for 14 hours without recharging, but even if it is discharged, you can use the removable cable that comes with the kit and continue listening.

The sound quality of the Urbanears Plattan ADV Wireless wireless on-ear headphones is at a high level. They sound worthy and to find fault with something is extremely difficult. Basses are saturated and clear, the middle is not blocked up, high frequencies play out to the maximum.

Urbanears Plattan ADV Wireless is no problem for fans of all musical genres, from classical music to heavy Scandinavian metal.

User reviews and cost

User reviews about these overhead Bluetooth headphones show that the model has practically no drawbacks, with the exception of a couple. The first is a slightly tough headband. The problem goes away during the first week of operation - you need to "carry" the headphones. And the second - in places where many different radio signals can sometimes disappear for several seconds, the bluetooth connection with the playing device. In this case, the cable provided will help.

Buy Urbanears Plattan ADV Wireless at the moment you can for 5000-6500 thousand rubles, which is quite acceptable for this level of headphones.
