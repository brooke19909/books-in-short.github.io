Lermontovo: Hotels, motels and hostels

The village of Lermontovo is one of the most picturesque places on the entire coast of the Krasnodar Territory. Fans of camping have been fond of staying here for a long time, putting their cars right on the beach equipped for this. But the main part of the recreation area is reserved specifically for the guests of the village of Lermontovo, whose hotels are full of variety.

Hotel "Rostov"

In Lermontovo hotels you can find the taste and wallet of everyone. Rostov is a hotel located 400 meters from the sea. Holidays here are suitable for young couples, families with small children and for everyone who chooses a comfortable stay for little money. The hotel room stock includes 60 rooms.



They all have TVs, comfortable beds, refrigerators, air conditioning, bathrooms with modern fixtures, and balconies. Catering is organized in a cafe, in addition, there is a bar on site. Everywhere for the convenience of guests the Internet is available, there is an unguarded free parking. Judging by the reviews, at the request of vacationers, staff can also organize excursion services.

Hotel Marseille

In Lermontovo, private hotels are very popular. Thus, the Marseille Hotel is a great place for family, corporate and romantic getaways. The generous sun and the gentle, warm black sea along with the entertainment and service of the complex will give you a truly unforgettable pleasure. The hotel staff will always help you plunge into a light, comfortable atmosphere.



You can order lunch, coffee break, buffet or business lunch with the highest level of service, using the restaurant. Judging by the reviews, the waiters serve the banquet perfectly. They will contribute to creating a harmonious, pleasant atmosphere for guests. Here you will escape from the hustle and bustle of the city, feel the unity with nature, and enjoy the European service of this first-class hotel.

Hotel Aqua Villa

Hotel Aqua Villa is located in Lermontovo. Hotels, photos of which are presented in this article, here are pleasing with their diversity. The same institution is ready for you to open the doors of a first-class family vacation. The hotel employees will enthusiastically take care of creating comfortable conditions for rest: they will order transport, feed, organize a tour or excursion for you, clean up, etc. The hotel's interior is modern, so the feeling of comfort will not make you wait.



The territory of the institution is an excellent combination of comfort and relaxation, as evidenced by numerous reviews. For guests there are two swimming pools (adult and children's), there are relaxation areas with decorative trees and flowering lawns, gazebos with barbecue facilities for families. You can have a good time at this place. In addition, Internet is available throughout.

Hotel Golden Hills

Golden Hills Hotel is located in the bay, in an ecologically clean and picturesque place of the Black Sea. In addition to such a great location at this Lermontovo hotel , reviews of which can be found only positive, many other advantages. It is a modern five-story building with comfortable rooms of several categories. What pleases guests, the hotel is equipped with an elevator.

The gentle sun, the azure sea, the friendly attitude and the golden sand of the staff will make your vacation unforgettable and fabulous. On the territory of the institution, you can engage in various varieties of outdoor activities, for example, go to the water park.

Hotel Riviera Grand Hotel

This hotel is located on the seafront in the village of Lermontovo. Hotels here are ready to receive guests all year round. This institution is distinguished by a modern convenient layout, furniture made from environmental materials - all this creates the conditions for a carefree and comfortable stay, as evidenced by the reviews. The restaurant serves Caucasian dishes. Holidays in the Grand Riviera are pleasant, interesting and useful. Here you will be given a charge of positive and cheerful for the whole year.

Hotel "Mill", Lermontovo

Hotels for families are easy to find here. These include the "Mill". To the sea from it is only ten minutes of leisurely walking. The institution has comfortable rooms that are suitable for absolutely any company. Rest here is suitable for fans of unity with nature, peace of mind, as well as for large noisy companies. In this place they created a symbiosis of the purest air of juniper groves and comfort, the soft waves of the sea and the blue sky.

Judging by the reviews, the staff will provide a sincere and easy pastime. At the local cafe you can enjoy international cuisine. The pool and green terrace offer a relaxing break during the day.

Guest house "Mashuk"

Guest house "Mashuk" is located in the village of Lermontovo. It is a four-story, separate building, which contains 10 comfortable rooms of various capacities with all amenities. Reading hotel reviews, you can find out that there is access to free internet, a barbecue area and parking.

Guest House "Golden Lily"

7 minutes from the sea, in the center of the village of Lermontovo, the Golden Lily Hotel appeared. Hospitable owners could foresee all the needs, as well as take into account the wishes of vacationers.



Judging by the reviews, it offers guests spacious comfortable rooms with all the necessary amenities. On the territory of the institution there is a bar, its own dining room, grocery store, barbecue, shop, pharmacy.
