Hotel Crystal Sharm 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh): photos and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resort cities in Egypt is Sharm El Sheikh. Thousands of tourists from Russia and other CIS countries come here on vacation annually. If you plan to go here on vacation and are looking for a budget hotel with good quality service, then pay attention to Crystal Sharm 4 *. We offer further closer acquaintance with this hotel complex.



Location

The four-star Crystal Sharm Hotel is located at the very end of the bay called Sharks Bay. It is 20 kilometers from the center of Sharm el-Sheikh, and 15 kilometers from Naama Bay. The capital of Egypt, Cairo, is 485 kilometers from the hotel. The airport is located quite close. So, the distance to it is only 6 kilometers. Therefore, upon arrival, you can reach the hotel in a matter of minutes, which is very convenient after a tiring road. With regard to proximity to the sea, the hotel under consideration is located on the second coastline. Its own beach is 400 meters away.



Crystal Sharm 4 *: photo and description

Crystal Charm is a beautiful and stylish hotel built in the traditional Egyptian style. For the first time he opened its doors for guests in 2003. In close proximity is the Hotel Crystal Sirens. Together, two hotels form a single complex with shared beaches and territory. However, the infrastructure of each hotel has its own. Therefore, living, for example, in Crystal Charm, you will not be able to visit the bars and restaurants of a neighboring hotel. The hotel we are considering is perfect for both family vacations and spending time with older people, as well as fun youth.

Housing stock

Hotel Crystal Sharm 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt) has at its disposal 214 rooms. The entire housing stock is located in the main two-story building and three three-story additional blocks. There are rooms of the following categories: standard single and double, family, as well as suites. All apartments have recently been renovated. They are equipped with necessary furniture, TV, air conditioning, telephone, refrigerator. Each room has a bathroom with a hairdryer and shower, as well as bath and toiletries, stocks of which are regularly replenished. The apartments also have a balcony and a safe.



Nutrition

The four-star Hotel Crystal Sharm operates under the ol inclusive system. In the main restaurant of the hotel complex, a buffet is served three times a day. In addition, during the day, everyone can eat in the snack bars on site.

Entertainment and infrastructure

As already mentioned, the Crystal Charm and Crystal Siren hotels have a common sandy beach. It is located very close. It can be reached on foot in a few minutes or by bus provided by the hotel.

The hotel has several pools, including for children. Also, a team of animators works here all day.

If desired, guests can play volleyball, table tennis, darts, billiards, visit the gym, sauna, massage room. On the beach for an additional fee there is the possibility of diving.

As for access to the Internet, it is available at Crystal Charm for a fee. However, most of the guests, according to them, preferred to buy a local SIM card for a phone with an Internet package. It cost them much less.



Crystal Sharm Hotel 4 *: reviews of Russian tourists

As you know, modern travelers in the process of choosing a hotel in a particular country in the world are doing everything possible to collect as much information about it as possible. So, they study the official description of hotels, listen to the recommendations of managers from travel agencies. In addition, our compatriots are trying to get acquainted with the comments of people who have already happened to stay in their place of interest. After all, this allows us to put a picture more complete and closer to the real state of things. We decided to save you time and effort and provide generalized reviews of tourists from Russia who recently recently stayed at the Crystal Sharm 4 * hotel (Sharm El Sheikh) during their vacation in Egypt. Immediately, we note that most of the guests were satisfied with their choice. In their comments, they note that the cost of living is consistent with the conditions of accommodation, hotel category and quality of service. But we will understand everything in detail.



Hotel itself

In general, according to our compatriots, the rooms they provided at the Crystal Sharm Hotel are quite spacious and clean. Despite the fact that the hotel itself cannot be called new, the apartments are regularly redecorated. According to the guests, you will not find modern furniture and appliances here. However, everything is in normal working condition. If something fails, then you need to contact the reception, and the problem will be solved as soon as possible. Also, by the way, some hotel guests in their reviews mention the fact that if you do not like the room offered upon arrival, you can ask for a replacement. Moreover, sometimes you have to pay extra for this. The same applies to faster settlement. If you arrive early in the morning, in order not to wait two in the afternoon, you can pay about $ 20 and check in early.

According to tourists, when you check in the refrigerator you will find two half-liter bottles of water. Subsequently, its supplies are not replenished, so you yourself will either have to collect water in coolers or buy it. The latter option is quite expensive, so few people used it. As for the cleaning of the rooms, the guests were generally satisfied with its quality. Guests also note that several times during their stay the maids built funny figures from towels. Especially such elephants, monkeys, swans and other living creatures rejoiced children. Moreover, as guests note, the quality of cleaning and the desire of the staff to create such jewelry did not depend on whether they left them a tip or not.

Hotel territory and location

Reviews of our compatriots describe the territory of the Crystal Sharm 4 * hotel complex as large enough, clean, green and well-groomed. According to the guests, almost everywhere a beautiful view of the sea opens here.

As for the location of the hotel, it is located on the second coastline. You can literally walk to the beach in 7-10 minutes at a leisurely pace. Tourists also mention that a shuttle service to the coast is provided here. However, most of our compatriots did not see the problem of having to make a pleasant short walk to the sea.

Kitchen

As for nutrition, this issue always causes a lot of controversy among tourists. And this is true for hotels not only in Egypt, but also in other countries. The Crystal Sharm Hotel 4 * was no exception. So, some of the guests in their reviews claim that the food was meager and monotonous. However, most guests claim that this hotel is inexpensive and, therefore, you can‚Äôt count on a table full of exquisite dishes and delicacies. According to them, in the hotel restaurant you will always find something to eat, but the choice of dishes, of course, cannot be called very diverse. So, tourists note that for breakfast sausage, cheese, cottage cheese, cereal, yogurt, vegetables and herbs, milk, eggs, scrambled eggs, pancakes, a variety of delicious pastries, several types of sausages, grilled vegetables, various side dishes and snacks are offered. Coffee can be drunk from the machine, and tea is offered in bags or already brewed. For lunch, guests are offered two types of soup, side dishes, vegetables, meatballs, pancakes, as well as dishes of meat, fish and chicken. For dinner, the menu is about the same, but grilled meat is added to it, which the cooks cook on the street. In addition, you can always treat yourself to delicious desserts, including oriental sweets, as well as fresh fruits.

Beach Vacation Impressions

According to our compatriots, as already mentioned, the beach can be reached in a few minutes. It belongs to two hotels - Crystal Charm and Crystal Sirens, which is part of the same chain. There is a sufficient number of beds, you can rent equipment for water sports. The beach is sandy, entry into the sea too. So it is quite possible to do without rubber shoes. However, in their reviews, tourists pay attention to the fact that the reef is not alive here. Therefore, here you will not find such a large number of aquatic inhabitants, as in many other hotels in Egypt. But you can still enjoy beautiful fish and other marine inhabitants. Also on the beach there is a large pool, where you can also have a good time.



Entertainment at the hotel and beyond

The territory of Crystal Sharm Hotel 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt) has several pools. From morning to late evening, a team of animators works here. They offer everyone to take part in competitions, do gymnastics, water aerobics, play water polo, etc. In the evenings, animators arrange funny shows in the hotel's amphitheater. After that, everyone, accompanied by employees of the hotel complex, can go to one of the discos in the city.

Family tourists claimed that their children also had something to do at the hotel. So, in the afternoon they could attend a mini-club, and in the evening they organized a mini-disco.

In addition, experienced travelers recommend that everyone staying on holiday in Egypt do not spend whole days by the pool, but be sure to go at least for a couple of excursions. Indeed, in this country there are many places that are worth visiting. Moreover, according to our compatriots, it is most advantageous to buy tours not from the guide at the hotel, but from tour agencies in the city. This will save you a considerable amount of money.

The opinion of tourists about the staff

As for the employees of the four-star Crystal Sharm Hotel, on the whole, our compatriots were satisfied with them. So, hotel guests note that the workers here are friendly and attentive. They will always be happy to help and answer any question. In addition, our compatriots were pleased with the fact that most employees understand Russian well. So you will not have any communication problems here.
