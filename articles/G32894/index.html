Adriatic Sea. Italian Resorts

The most famous resort of Italy on the coast of the inland, closed on three sides of the sea, is called the Adriatic Riviera. This is perhaps the most democratic holiday destination in Europe. Many hotels of different classes attract tourists not because of their fashionability, but because of their accessibility.
What attracts tourists to the Adriatic Sea here? Resorts, in addition to accessibility, are distinguished by magnificent sandy beaches, an abundance of discos and nightclubs, and sports facilities of various kinds. Young people here can have fun day and night, people of more respectable age will enjoy the wonderful nature and excellent service. Parents with young children will also find something to do: Mirabilandia, Fiabilandia, Aquafan and many water parks will not let kids or adults get bored. Adriatic Sea. Croatian Resorts

They are also great for relaxing children and parents. There are many hotels here, the service is quite consistent with the price level. But the beaches here are not sandy, but pebble. In some places, there is not even pebbles: it is replaced by artificial concrete coating. Only in the Dubrovnik area can you find sand or sand with pebbles. However, despite this, the beaches of Croatia occupy one of the first places in terms of quality. Thalassotherapy is developed here.

Adriatic Sea. Resorts of Bosnia and Herzegovina

These countries account for only 20 km of the Adriatic coast. Here is the only resort - Neum. About how well to spend time in it, says the following: it is Neum that brings the largest revenues to the country's budget. Therefore, almost ideal conditions are created for tourists here.

The Adriatic Sea is beautiful in Albania too: it is shallow, warm, it has gently sloping sandy beaches. However, due to the instability of Albania, tourism there is not very developed.
