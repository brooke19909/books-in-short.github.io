The city of Suzdal: how to get from Moscow to Suzdal

One of the famous museum cities of Russia is Suzdal. How to get from Moscow to this city? There are several options: by train, by bus, by your own car. In the article we will consider each of them.





Museum City

Traveling along the Golden Ring of Russia involves visiting cities located in seven regions. Each of them has preserved unique historical and cultural monuments that attract tourists from Russia and abroad. One of these cities is Suzdal. How to get from Moscow to him is of interest to many residents of the capital. Tired of the fast-paced rhythm of the metropolis, they are in search of quiet, picturesque places to relax. And this is especially felt on weekends, when congestion on the Moscow Ring Road reaches the highest scores.

"Why are Muscovites rushing to Suzdal? How to get from Moscow to this city?" - questions that will be answered below. First, of course, it is worth talking about the city. And not only about the Kremlin and other historical monuments, but also about the shortcomings that vacationers face.

The population of Suzdal is about ten thousand people. Most of the locals are involved in the tourism, restaurant, hotel business. The main attraction of the city is the Kremlin. From it usually begin the inspection of Suzdal beauties. There are three churches on the territory of the Kremlin, one of which was founded in the seventeenth century. Others - in the XVIII century.

The list of monuments and museums that Suzdal has is quite extensive. "How to get from Moscow to this city?" - Such a question is usually asked by those who already have some information about local attractions. Only those who have already been there know the level of service in Suzdal hotels, the roads of this city, and the prices in restaurants.





By rail to Suzdal

"How to get from Moscow by train?" - a question that will be answered right now. But before you buy a ticket, you should book a hotel room. Suzdal is a tourist city. Prices here are not much different from Moscow. And most importantly, on weekends it is rather difficult to rent a room in a hotel house. Therefore, it is better to call and book in advance. When the issue with the hotel room has been resolved, you should find out the schedule of trains Moscow - Vladimir. It is Vladimir, because there is no direct route Moscow - Suzdal. How to get from Moscow by train? From Kursky station, electric trains depart daily for Vladimir. Travel time is about three hours. Ticket price - from 500 rubles.

From the regional center to Suzdal can be reached by bus. Departure interval is forty minutes. But the train station and bus station are not quite close to each other. You have to travel by public transport to the Torgovye Ryad stop. Or get to Suzdal by taxi. This option is quite tedious. But for people who are curious and hardy, it will do. Thus, you can visit the two cities of the Golden Ring. Some tourists prefer this route because in Vladimir there are also many attractions.

By bus

This method is much simpler. Every day, buses leave for the Suzdal and Vladimir buses from the Shchelkovo station . Ticket price is about five hundred rubles. Travel time is four hours.





By car

Most Muscovites visit Suzdal in the summer or on May holidays. Traffic jams cannot be avoided at this time. In addition, the roads in the Vladimir region leave much to be desired, and the repair work being carried out at many sites in the Moscow region can significantly increase the travel time. Suzdal is 220 km from the property. You can get from Moscow in 4-5 hours, depending on the time of day and the level of traffic jams. But even despite all these shortcomings, this is the most convenient way to get not only to Suzdal, but also to any other city of the Golden Ring.
