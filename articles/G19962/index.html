"Megaphone", "Pay when convenient." How to activate the Megafon Pay when convenient service?

Thanks to its tariffs, Megafon is undoubtedly popular among mobile subscribers. It has a lot of options that can be convenient for users. You may have heard about the Megafon service - “Pay when it’s convenient”, but you did not find a lot of information on the company's website. In order to have an idea of ​​the offer of the operator, you need to look at many sources. It will not be superfluous to look at the reviews. We tried to systematize everything we managed to find out.

When did this offer appear for subscribers

At the beginning of 2014, company employees started phoning subscribers, who offered to take advantage of the Megafon offer - “Pay when it is convenient”. Many subscribers were skeptical of the new feature, but those who decided to take the risk are quite happy with the service. Perhaps the operator’s calls seemed intrusive to someone, but these actions were aimed at conveying to their customers the conditions for the provision of a new company product.



If you recently use the services of this telecom operator, but you are interested in what the “Pay when convenient” option is, Megafon offers to visit the operator’s office or make a call yourself.

How to contact a company representative yourself



The Internet will help you choose the nearest office. In any search service, enter a request for the addresses of Megafon representative offices and you will be shown a list of addresses and a map. So you can easily find the closest to home or work, as well as find out a work schedule.

You can call the operator and find out details about this service at the number for subscribers free of charge: 0500. From a landline telephone, you can reach the same number, but in the format 8-800-550-05-00. This opportunity will save you if you don’t have your mobile at hand or if your account is currently negative.

Despite the fact that in both cases the calls will be free, the balance should be positive, and the number should not be blocked.

We use the official website to get information

If you can’t make a call and find out about the methods of providing the Megafon Pay When It Is convenient service, then you can use the official website. As mentioned above, there is no description of this service on it, so go to the "Help" tab and ask a question in a special form. Indicate your email inbox and contact number, it will not receive a response from a support worker.



If you do not like to write a lot, then there is the opportunity to make a video call. This can be done using the tool at the bottom of the page. So you can communicate with a company representative in person, and you will not need to dial a number.

What is the service “Pay when convenient” (“Megaphone”)

A service of this kind is characterized by one word - credit. The operator gives you the opportunity to call, and then pay the invoice. It is convenient for those who are used to paying for communication services once a month (for example, on the day of salary).



Thus, you get a tariff with a postpaid payment system, while the cost of calls, messages and megabytes of traffic will remain unchanged. In general, this is very convenient, but it is worth monitoring the status of the account so as not to go beyond the limits of the limit and not get an unpleasant surprise on the day of payment.

How to connect the opportunity "Megaphone" - "Pay when convenient"

For those who did not immediately decide to join the program after talking with a Call Center specialist, not everything is lost. At your request, this can be done at any time as the need arose. This is convenient for those who doubted whether he needs “Pay when convenient” (“Megaphone”) service, and decided to use it nevertheless.

The easiest way is to use the Service Guide menu and the personal account of the official site. In both cases, you need to select the option “Pay when convenient” in the list of services for connection. As soon as the service is activated, you will receive an SMS message with the result. The operator also acts if he rejects your request (the reason most often is not indicated in this case).

It is possible to send a message to 0500 (the official number of the operator) with the text "Connect" Pay when convenient "." If there is no answer, it is best to call the specialist hotline in the ways described above.

Advantages and disadvantages

Almost all subscribers can connect to the “Pay when it is convenient” service (Megafon), but there are a number of features that you need to know about, as company employees do not always notify of their availability.

For example, the balance must be recognized by the number * 149 #, since you need to look at the balance limit. Every month on the 6th day you will receive an SMS message with the amount payable. You have almost 3 weeks to pay, namely 20 days. If you cannot immediately pay off all the debt, then you can do it in parts.
The size of the loan provided to you by the company depends on your expenses in previous months. It can be in the amount of from 400 rubles to 1500. Also, the amount may vary due to the region of your residence.

The service “Pay when convenient” (“Megaphone”) reviews is quite contradictory. Those who are used to making payments for various services from a mobile account have reacted negatively to it. Once connected, this will not be possible. The credit limit is given only for communication services that are used on the number associated with this subscriber account.

On the other hand, those who use the number to work are positive about the service. In this case, “Pay when convenient” “Megaphone” receives the most positive reviews. All the opportunities previously available for your number will remain the same, but they won’t take money from you for providing a limit and changing the payment scheme. Take advantage of more features is completely free. Contrary to popular belief, disabling will also not cost you anything, so changing the payment scheme will not bring you any inconvenience or financial loss.





It is advantageous to use the service for those who pay for mobile Internet once a month. After all, it is not always possible to top up the balance on time precisely by the date of debiting. You will be able to deposit money for use later, when the invoice is already issued. Next, we’ll talk about how Megafon subscribers turn off Pay when convenient.

Options

The method of terminating the provision of a credit limit practically does not differ from the connection. You can also use:
• Call center.
• SMS to the official operator number.
• Personal Area.

The surest way to stop using the service is to call 0500 or 88005500500. In the first case, you just need to send an SMS message with the text: "Disable" Pay when convenient "." In the second, you need to get through and wait for a connection with a specialist. You may need passport data or a code word.

You can leave a request for disconnection in the Service Guide menu and in your account on the website. After that, you will receive a message with the result.
