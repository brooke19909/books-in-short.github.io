What are some entertainments in Vladimir for children?

The ancient city of Vladimir is quite rich in attractions. Here you can visit many places that will interest adults and children. Vacationers will receive a lot of positive emotions and plunge into the atmosphere of the ancient cultural center.

Museum-fairy tale "Babusi-Yagusi"

Which of the children does not like fairy tales? Yes, of course, everyone loves to listen and read exciting stories. Baba Yaga is a favorite character of many people since childhood. The museum is designed like a real fairy tale character house. Here you can see other heroes from your favorite fairy tales. The room is twilight, equipped with lighting effects. It is not recommended to bring children under five years old.

Aquapark

Active entertainment in Vladimir for children is considered a water park. Here, children can ride from the hills, run through the mazes. For adults, there is also entertainment. You can visit the sauna, and then swim in the pool. If you want a relaxing holiday, you can relax in the tea room on the soft sofa in front of the TV. The interior design of the water park premises resembles a tropical island.

Puppet show

If the vacation takes place together with the family, then you should definitely visit one of the most interesting entertainments in Vladimir for children - a puppet theater. Wonderful theatrical atmosphere, performances capture all the attention of children, making them worry along with the characters of the performance. The repertoire of the theater is composed of works by Russian and world classics, as well as from works by contemporary authors. Performances are held on a large stage in a cozy theater hall.

Planetarium

The unknown always attracts. In the planetarium, you can see a lot of interesting things, learn all about planets and stars, admire the sky through a special device. Of all the entertainment in Vladimir for children, the planetarium is the most mysterious and exciting place. It regularly hosts entertainment events, evenings on a specific topic. You can order the holding of any children's holiday.

Workshop "Chocolate"

The tour of the institution includes stories about the history of the appearance of your favorite treat, master classes on making chocolate are held. When choosing entertainment in Vladimir for children, you should pay attention to this workshop. Children will be interested in listening to the story and admiring the unusual exhibits in the windows. Chocolate figures have the most unusual shape. If you wish, you can take part in the Chocolate Workshop program.

Scientific and educational center

If the child shows a craving for knowledge and is interested in various experiments and experiments, then it is recommended to visit the scientific-educational center "Eureka". Here, children will be given fascinating guided tours of the halls, they will see the equipment through which various experiments are arranged, and they will also be able to participate in experiments in chemistry and physics. If you are looking for such entertainment for children in Vladimir, where to go, you can ask the guides.

Shopping and entertainment centers

There are many such universal entertainment places in Vladimir. While adults go shopping in the trading floors, cheerful animators will always be occupied here. The centers provide special game rooms for fun and active recreation of children. Extensive pools with balls, inflatable trampolines, different sizes of slides for skiing will attract the attention of a child for a long time. On weekends, interesting programs with prizes and fun and interesting contests are held here. Children of any age will find something to their liking.

Park of Culture and Rest

When you wanted to arrange a relaxing holiday in Vladimir for children, you can go with the whole friendly family to the central park of culture and rest. There are interesting attractions that will capture the attention of children, you can always relax from the bustle of the city among the green trees on the benches. If the rest fell on the winter period, then the children can enthusiastically ride the ice slide. From the Ferris wheel you can see the splendor of the old Russian city and admire the surroundings and wonderful landscapes.

Trampoline complex "Flight"

Young children will love to visit the trampoline complex, which is located in the city center. Here you can always run and jump as much as you like. Active children will especially enjoy the entertainment; they will not want to leave it. A climbing wall, foam pits and a trampoline zone await their visitors every day.

Upon arrival with his family in the city of Vladimir, entertainment for children is occupied by all parents during their holidays.
