How it works?

Naturally, users are interested in the principle of operation of this device. After all, the device is made in the form of a helmet, which must be worn on the head. It is immediately worth noting that the night vision device does not emit any radiation - everything is based on the physical laws of optics. In fact, the device enhances the infrared and ultraviolet rays that are invisible to the human eye.





Electron-optical converters pick up weak light reflected from objects and transmit its photocathode, which has a light-sensitive matrix (as in SLR cameras). The photocathode, in turn, transfers the image to a luminescent screen, which is able to increase the brightness of the image due to the constant movement of electrons from the cathode.

The convenience of this technology

The reader managed to notice that the market is dominated by solutions from foreign manufacturers, which are made in the form of a monocular. They are more reminiscent of an optical sight and are designed for mounting on a rifle or are used as a spyglass. And the PNV-57E device, whose price is an order of magnitude lower (10,000 rubles), is binocular, that is, it allows the user to see in the dark with two eyes in real time.

As users note in their reviews, this solution is not only convenient, but also allows the owner, through some manipulations with the optical device, to change the tactical and technical characteristics of the device. After all, this is military equipment that has been created for centuries. No expansion cards and tricky devices - a common designer for a Russian person: metal structures, optical instruments and several wires.

Disadvantages of the PNV-57E device

Naturally, any product of the military industrial complex has a number of disadvantages, which many users pay attention to. First of all, the disadvantage is the weight of the device. Nevertheless, the metal construction of the PNV-57E device makes itself felt. Low resolution of the image, which affects the detail of the image at large distances (over 20 meters). There are also problems with image sharpness when viewing objects at close range (up to 5 meters).

The performance characteristics

The PNV-57E device provides a field of view of at least 35 degrees - this is a serious advantage if compared with monocular systems. The optics of the device has a slight increase, which does not go beyond 1-1.2 times. Eyepieces have diopter settings that allow the user to adjust the sharpness of the picture in case of poor vision. The focal length of the lenses is 37 mm, and they consist of nine lenses. The optical axes of the PNV-57E device are equipped with a floating connection, as is realized with ordinary binoculars, so this device can be used by anyone, regardless of age.





As for the power supply system of solar cells and a luminescent screen, it is carried out at a voltage of 12-15 V or 24-30 V. Switching between power supplies is carried out automatically. The power consumption of the device at maximum load does not exceed 6 watts.

Oh, these Russians!

Probably only abroad it is customary to shout about dustproof devices operating in a humid environment and capable of experiencing shock loads. All users are familiar with the IP87 markings and their analogues. The PNV-57E device has several orders of magnitude higher performance.

  1. Dust protection. Judging by the reviews of the owners, who used the device for more than 100 hours within one year, when servicing the device, no dirt and dust were found in the optical elements and on the power boards. Only in the lubrication areas of moving parts is dirt accumulated.

Recommendations for hunters

Like any optical binoculars, the device has problems with detailing objects at close range. In fact, within 1-4 meters, image sharpness is low. Some enthusiasts suggest reducing focus by moving the lenses on the eyepiece. But then another problem will arise - the lack of detail over a long distance. This is unacceptable, especially if the device PNV-57E is used for hunting. There is a solution, it is interesting and was appreciated by many owners, judging by their reviews, positive.

You can move the lenses on one eyepiece, adjusting the focus so that the sharpness is 2-4 meters. And leave the second eyepiece unchanged. Yes, you need to get used to these settings, but it's worth it. With fast movement through the forest in pursuit of game, this solution allows you to better navigate the terrain and significantly saves the hunter‚Äôs time.

But the owners of the collimator a bunch of two devices obviously will like. If the optical device has autonomous power and backlight, then the user does not need to center the PNV-57E eyepiece with a collimator. It is enough to be able to look into the device at a large angle and see the aiming point.





Laser Guidance is even easier. Red target pointers are visible in the night vision device, like the Jedi swords. Naturally, the guidance point is easily located on the object, and any hunter will be pleased to lead the target with it. But the combined use of a laser and a collimator is better to immediately eliminate. The fact is that through the optical device, the red dot of the target pointer acquires a huge halo, which interferes with aiming.
