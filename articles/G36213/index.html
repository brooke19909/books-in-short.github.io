Hotel Himeros Club Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey): overview, description, features and reviews

Just a few tens of kilometers from the popular tourist center of Antalya is the cozy resort town of Kemer. For over a hundred years, it has been a port on the Mediterranean Sea and has attracted thousands of tourists from all over the world every year. The land, preserving the memories of Alexander the Great and the Roman emperors, entails the mystery of the East and the openness of the West, hospitality and a bright kaleidoscope of impressions.





The key to a good rest is the right choice of housing. One of the most popular hotels for tourists is Himeros Club Hotel (Grand Beauty Hotel) 4 *, located on the outskirts of the southern part of the city. A good combination of proximity to the sea, beach and historical places.

Hotel Location

The resort complex is located in a remote location, 1 km from the beach and the sea, but this is not a problem, since a shuttle regularly goes there, and for some vacationers this distance seems quite accessible for a leisurely walk. Antalya Airport is 50 km away. All residents, however, will appreciate the proximity of the historical and architectural monument - the ancient Lycian city (Phaselis), the distance to it is only 13 km.





Hotel Description

The three-story building was rebuilt in 2006, and in 2015, its last restoration was carried out. The Himeros Club Hotel 4 * (Kemer) has an interesting architectural design with a curved view (with a patio) with battlements above the windows of the top floor. The adjacent area is small and amounts to 2200 square meters. m. Small capacity - the hotel has 85 comfortable rooms of various categories, able to accommodate 210 guests.

Room Types

The interior of the rooms is quite simple. The decor is made in natural colors using wood, ceramic tiles on the floor. Most rooms have double and single beds, and some family apartments have sofa beds. The bathroom is standard: shower and bath, toilet, sink, towels and toiletries, hairdryer.





All rooms at Himeros Club Hotel (Grand Beauty Hotel) 4 * have a balcony with a view of the courtyard or the sea. In addition, it features a TV with satellite antenna and several Russian-language channels, individual air conditioning, a telephone with the ability to make international calls and a safe. A minibar and drinks are available for a fee, except for mineral water.

Room cleaning is done by hotel staff daily, linen change in the room - three times a week, individual service is available (for a fee).

Meals: restaurants and bars

The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. A daily small buffet restaurant is available for dinner, lunch and breakfast. Time is strictly limited. Guests are offered local drinks, fruits and vegetables, as well as dishes of national and European cuisines. On the territory of Himeros Club Hotel 4 * there are two bars. One of them is located next to the pool and is open until late in the evening. It offers visitors refreshments and spirits, ice cream and light snacks. The second bar is called "Vitamin", you can get freshly made refreshing drinks in it, and also have a snack during the afternoon snack. For children, a separate special menu is offered.





Himeros Club Hotel (ex. Grand Beauty) 4 *: sports, beauty and entertainment

Having a small number of rooms and a modest adjoining territory, the hotel still has a small pool with a children's section. Umbrellas and sunbeds are provided free of charge, but towels will have to be taken care of in advance.

Free use of the equipped gym, sauna and Turkish bath (hammam). For massage services, time spent in the jacuzzi, billiard room or steam room, you must pay extra. For entertainment, Himeros Club Hotel 4 * guests can enjoy table tennis, darts, and all water sports are organized on the city beach, the distance to it is about one kilometer, or you can use the shuttle.





For guests on site, a spa is open, which offers its visitors relaxing and therapeutic procedures, including mud therapy. Experienced staff will select a program that is suitable for all indications and organizes your leisure.

Who is the hotel targeted at?

Perhaps every traveler asks this question. Before you go on vacation, without exception, everyone is eager to learn more about the place, traditions, chosen housing. And this is absolutely correct. The hotel must be chosen in accordance with personal needs (for two, family with children, for a relaxing holiday, and so on). Only in this case can we count on comfortable conditions for everyone. Hotel Himeros Club Hotel 4 *, having a fairly good infrastructure, is still focused on adults, business trips and only then on family vacations. Certain conditions have been created for children: a branch in the pool, a playground for games, a mini club (for a fee), where for several hours they can do something interesting in the company of peers, as well as standard animation. If necessary, babysitting is possible, but not for free.

Himeros Club Hotel 4 *: reviews

The most valuable thing in a vacation is impressions that remain for a long time. Feel free to share them, including opinions about hotels and services, because they help future tourists and save them from unforeseen situations. So in this case, the impressions were not up to par. Most of the guests note that the declared four-star level has nothing to do with Himeros Club Hotel 4 *. Comments relate to the level of service; staff are often rude. In addition, the hotel rooms do not look the best, plumbing in poor condition.





The largest number of complaints was earned by the All Inclusive system, which is actively used in the hotel. Tourists note that the choice of food was limited without any gastronomic delights, and sometimes just tasteless. The poor quality of juices, presumably not fresh, but diluted from packets, as well as alcohol, was also noted.

The main advice given by former guests of Himeros Club Hotel 4 * is to spend more time on the sea, beach, excursions, walks. Well, you can get acquainted with Turkish cuisine and its main features in numerous restaurants and cafes nearby.

According to the Russians, one should not expect too much from the hotel, it fully justifies the low prices and last minute tours. Therefore, it will not be superfluous to think about how much you plan to spend time in it, if this is the minimum part of the day and night, then the option is quite acceptable.

Tourists recommend

A country with strong eastern roots and, nevertheless, European open and relaxed - this is Turkey. Himeros Club Hotel 4 * has a good location, despite the somewhat remoteness of the beach (about 20 minutes walk by foot), you can easily get to many places of interest from the point of view of culture and history (as well as entertainment).





We will draw the attention of readers to two amusement parks. To get to them from Himeros Club Hotel (ex. Grand Beauty) 4 * you can use public transport or a special transfer.

Firstly, Moonlight Park, which has a large territory with a city beach and magnificent views of the blue sea and snow-white yachts. It offers entertainment for children and adults: water slides, dolphinarium, cafes and restaurants, souvenir shops and just a pleasant walk in an interesting place.

Secondly, Folklorik Yoruk Parki, which will literally immerse you in the life of Turkish nomads, explaining and telling everything at a clear and affordable level. Add to this the picturesque views of the surroundings of Kemer. Many travelers recommend it for an independent visit, guides are often silent about this place, but meanwhile it is necessary to devote not an hour or two, but rather all day.

Well, actually, the city of Kemer itself. He is quite young, but very colorful and interesting. On it you can walk, ride, ride a bike. The main thing is to get to know him and breathe in the aroma of Turkish coffee and sweet pastries on its streets.
