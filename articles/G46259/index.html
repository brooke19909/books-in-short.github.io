The most popular hotels in Ples

Ples is a small town located on the picturesque bank of the Volga in the Ivanovo region. It was founded in the XII century, but during the period of the Tatar-Mongol invasion it was completely destroyed, therefore, the date of foundation is 1410. There are no large enterprises in this place, this town lives only due to tourism.
