Phone Motorola Aura: specifications, description, reviews

An unusual and vibrant cell phone is all about Motorola Aura. The device itself went on sale in 2009, and there is no need to talk about the full compliance of its technical specifications with modern requirements. But the design of the gadget itself remains relevant anyway. It is from this position that he is one of a kind.





Cell phone niche

At the start of sales of Motorola Aura It was a premium mobile phone. First of all, an unusual design performance indicates a similar positioning of the device. The second point is the metal case, which relates the device to the premium segment. To the above, you need to add the presence of the Linux operating system and 2GB of memory. Now, of course, this will not impress anyone. But in 2009, these were really phenomenal parameters. A good mobile device cannot be affordable, and as a result, this gadget was priced at two thousand dollars. It was this factor that scared away many potential buyers.

Equipment

Solid equipment even by today's standards boasts a Motorola Aura phone (the description is presented in the article). It included:

  • Operation manual and warranty card.

  • Stereo headset with high sound quality.

  • USB / microUSB interface cable.

  • Charger.

The only thing that was not included in the package of the mobile phone was the cover. Of course, it can be noted that the cell phone in question has a metal case, but, as experience shows, situations can be different. For example, if accidentally dropped, the gadget will not crash, but may bend. The cover is not an absolute panacea for possible falls, but you can feel much more comfortable with it. Moreover, the price of this phone is very solid.





Design

A key feature of Motorola Aura is the design. This phone boasts a rotatable housing and a round screen. Moreover, his menu was even partially optimized for the design of the device, and his items are also arranged in a circle. The phone case itself is made of stainless steel. On the upper part is the earpiece and display, and on the bottom is the keyboard. On the other side of the case is a single camera. There is also a cover behind which is a removable battery and a single SIM card slot.

Hardware specifications

Very, very modest technical parameters, as for today, the Motorola Aura phone boasts. The capacity characteristic of the built-in drive indicates the presence of only 2GB. For the described phone, this amount of memory is enough for comfortable work. But there is no additional slot for installing an external flash card. The amount of RAM is not specified. The same applies to the chip model on which this development of Motorola is based. Therefore, the detailed technical specifications of this device can only be guessed at. But in any case, now such parameters will be really modest. At the time of the start of sales of the device, they were not relevant. Most manufacturers did not specify them. So, Motorola was not unusual in this regard.





Autonomy

810 mAh is the battery capacity that came with the Motorola Aura phone. The technical characteristics of this component are really not impressive right now. Modern smartphones boast a battery capacity of 3000-4000 mAh. On the other hand, in this case, the diagonal of the display is 1.5 inches, and the presence of a resource-intensive filling is out of the question. As a result, the battery will definitely last for two to three days of battery life at an average load level. If you are a business person and communicate a lot, then this phone in such an intensive mode can last one day. Well, with a lower level of use of the device, you can count on even 4-5 days without recharging.

Camera

Not bad at the time of the release, the main camera was equipped with Motorola Aura. A review of technical specifications indicates the presence of a 2MP sensor. In 2009, this value made it possible to confidently attribute the device to a premium niche. That is why some experts called this device ‚ÄúMotorola Aura camera phone‚Äù. Now, only entry-level devices differ in the presence of such a sensor. This mobile phone could take photos with a resolution of 1600x1200. And the video, in turn, was recorded in VGA format.





Interfaces

An impressive set of interfaces for 2009 was implemented in the Motorola Aura phone.





A review of the technical specifications of this unit indicates the following methods of receiving and transmitting information:

  • The device has only one slot for a SIM card. The phone itself is equipped with
    GSM transmitter only. Accordingly, this device is capable of working in 2G networks, and the maximum data transfer rate in this case can reach 500 kb / s.

  • The main wired interface is microUSB, which allows you to charge the battery and synchronize the device with any personal computer.

  • Another important way to exchange information with the outside world is Bluetooth. With its help, you could synchronize this device with a PC or exchange data with similar mobile devices.

Now smartphones can boast a much larger list of supported interfaces, but in 2009 the above list was quite enough for comfortable work on any gadget, including this mobile phone from Motorola.

Software

In this case, Linux is the system software. More specifically, a special version of this software called MOTOMAXG, which is optimized for use in mobile devices with a small amount of memory. Alas, this platform cannot boast of a rich set of application software.

Reviews

The perfect combination of technical specifications and software is able to boast a Motorola Aura phone. Reviews indicate that in this regard, the described model of the mobile phone really has no shortcomings.





But what really prevented the widespread popularity of this gadget is the high cost. This device at the time of the start of sales cost two thousand dollars. It is this factor that ultimately led to the fact that only wealthy people could afford a phone.

Summary

This phone clearly shows how correctly the technical specialists of Motorola worked. The device has practically no shortcomings. The only minus of Motorola Aura is a clearly overpriced one. It was this drawback that ultimately led to the fact that the technically successful device was not widely used. For the rest, this is a great gadget that is far ahead of its time.
