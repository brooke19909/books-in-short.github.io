PIK Group, Salaryevo Park: apartments in a new residential complex

The PIK residential complexes are currently very popular. "Salaryevo Park" is a vivid representative of a modern, thoughtful complex located within walking distance from the metro station. A variety of planning solutions, the use of advanced technologies and unique materials, the affordable cost of real estate - all this attracts modern buyers.





about the project

Salaryevo Park ( residential complex, PIK) is a large-scale development project in the south-west of new Moscow. The concept provides for the construction of residential real estate and all necessary infrastructure in several stages.

The first stage involves the construction of 5 monolithic buildings. Among them are 3 single-section high-rises in 25 floors and two multi-section buildings of variable number of storeys. All buildings are erected using monolithic technology that provides the necessary strength and durability, are made in a pleasant pastel color scheme.

Location

The south-west of Moscow is currently developing, new buildings with spacious apartments and their own infrastructure are appearing here. At the same time, the absence of harmful production makes apartments in Salaryevo Park (PIK) as attractive as possible for young families. Clean air, forest belt, lakes and rivers - what else is needed for a quality life and parenting? And all this within walking distance from the metro station. At the moment, you can still buy an apartment in this area at a reasonable price, after a couple of years, housing here will cost an order of magnitude more expensive.

Finish

Potential buyers of apartments in Salaryevo Park residential complex (PIK) may consider the option with pre-finishing and turnkey finishing. The finishing treatment consists in preparing all surfaces (ceiling, walls, floor) for subsequent decorative design. These apartments have plastic windows, a glazed balcony, and electrical wiring. If you choose turnkey finishing, you will get a finished apartment with installed plumbing, wallpaper on the walls and ceiling and a laminate on the floor. Moreover, the company provides interest holders with the possibility of individual finishing according to a previously developed design project.

Terms of purchase

You can buy an apartment in the Salaryevo Park residential complex by depositing 100% of the cost or by using mortgage lending. For buyers who have made 40% of the cost as an initial payment, installment payment is provided for a period of three months. Cooperation with the largest banks allows us to offer favorable conditions for mortgage lending and the opportunity to participate in preferential programs.
