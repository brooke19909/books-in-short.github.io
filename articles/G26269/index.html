Bali Paradise Beach 4 * (Greece, Crete): hotel description, services, reviews

Crete is quite popular with lovers of beach resorts, and therefore in our article we want to talk about one of its hotels. Bali Paradise Beach 4 * is a four-star hotel on the coast, near the beach of Bali.

A bit about the hotel ...

Bali Paradise Beach 4 * is located on the vast green territory of the botanical garden and is made in the traditional Cretan style. The resort town of Bali is located thirty kilometers from the city of Rethymnon, fifty kilometers from Heraklion, eighty kilometers from Chania. The nearest international airport is Nikos Kazantzakis, 55 kilometers from the hotel.





The complex has been operating since 1991, and in 2014 it was partially restored. The hotel consists of a main building and several bungalows.

Number of rooms

Bali Paradise Beach 4 * has rooms in the following categories:

  1. Single & Doubles (area 20 square meters). The room is equipped with a double bed or two single beds.
  2. Family room (area 25 square meters). This is a single room with a double bed and two single beds.
  3. Superior Single & Double (area 30 square meters). The apartment is housed in a central building and was renovated in 2014.
  4. Villa Luxury (area 110 square meters). The apartments are 50 meters from the hotel and 150 meters from the reception. These rooms have upholstered furniture, two separate bedrooms and a fully equipped kitchen.

In total, Bali Paradise Beach 4 * has 227 rooms. All of them are made in classic style and equipped with good air conditioning. All apartments are equipped with a TV, seating area, bathtub, bath accessories. There are also balconies. The rooms create comfortable conditions for relaxation.

Hotel infrastructure

Bali Paradise Beach 4 * ( Crete, Rethymno, Bali) has a good infrastructure, thanks to which the hotel has everything that guests may need for a good rest. The complex offers its guests accommodation in the beautiful botanical garden in comfortable rooms.



The hotel has an internet cafe. Wi-Fi is available not only in the lobby, but also in the rooms. On the territory there is a currency exchange, a 24-hour front desk, a concierge, shops, a banquet hall, a conference room, and a tour desk. At the hotel you can rent a car and arrange a transfer if you do not want to get there on your own.

Dining at the hotel

What about food at Bali Paradise Beach 4 *? Meals are organized according to the All Inclusive system. The central restaurant offers its guests a buffet. Early breakfasts are served from five in the morning for those who go on excursions. Further, guests have the opportunity to have breakfast from 7:30 until 10:00. At a later time - from 10:00 to 12:00 - food is served in the pool bar and in the lobby bar.

Lunch and dinner are also served in the main restaurant. During dinner and lunch, guests are offered local drinks (water, draft beer, wine, soft drinks).

There is a lobby bar that discusses tourists from 8:00 to 01:00. Here guests can also order fruit juices, local beer and draft wine, cocktails, coffee and tea. Afternoon snacks are served from 16:00 to 17:30.





In addition, there is a poolside bar, which welcomes customers from 10:00 to 18:00.

All Inclusive is open only until midnight, after which guests of the hotel can order drinks for a surcharge. In addition, you should be aware that imported alcohol, expensive coffees, fresh juices and ice creams are paid separately and are not included in the all-inclusive concept.

Sports and Entertainment

The hotel administration takes care of the leisure of guests vacationing at Bali Paradise Beach 4 *. Tourists can play darts, billiards, visit a nightclub and sightseeing tours.

The hotel has a large swimming pool with an equipped relaxation area. The complex has its own spa center, which offers guests beauty and wellness treatments for body and face. For guests there is a fitness room. You can have a fruitful and active time playing basketball. The hotel has French-speaking animators.

Children's holiday

Children will find a children's pool with slides and a playground for games. The hotel has a mini club. A cot is available in the room upon request. The restaurant has a separate menu for babies and high chairs.

Beach

How far is the beach from Bali Paradise Beach 4 *? Tourists are always very interested in this issue. The complex is located a hundred meters from Bali beach, so you can get to the shore in a couple of minutes. The local coast is very clean and beautiful.





A bit about the resort

Crete is one of the Greek islands. However, it has its own history of tradition and folklore. Crete can be safely called the cradle of Greek culture.

Holidays in Bali are preferred by those tourists who are fans of peace and tranquility, as well as beautiful nature. The fishing village with a beautiful name is located near Rethymnon. The local slopes of the mountains are covered with continuous green carpets of plants, and secluded coves with clean clear water are located nearby. Holidays in Bali can be described as a leisurely calm pastime surrounded by beautiful nature.

It is necessary to take into account the fact that the local mountain slopes are quite steep. Therefore, trips to the sea are reduced to descents and ascents. For this reason, the resort may not be suitable for the elderly.

Transport

How to get to Bali Paradise Beach 4 *? The map shows that the distances are very small.





The hotel has a shuttle service, which will cost 60 EUR per person one way. But there is another way to get to the hotel. You can always take a taxi at the airport. Pre-reserve it is not worth it, because it costs money. Moreover, there are always free cars at the airport. Taxi from Heraklion (airport) to Bali costs about 60 EUR.

There are passing buses passing to Bali to Chania, Rethymnon, Heraklion. However, they all stop on the track. Go from it to the village itself for about two kilometers. You can use the local tram, but you need to analyze the schedule of its movement. In addition, you should not really hope for him, because he does not always go.

There are several companies in Bali where you can rent a car or a scooter. If you want to see the beautiful island of Crete, then the best option is a car. If you can not rent it, then it will be difficult to move around.

Resort beaches

In Bali, there are four main beaches located in the bays for tourists. The first of them is the most open and large. Decent waves rise here during the wind. The second bay is much smaller, but also calmer. But the central one is the busiest. There are moorings with fishing boats, pirate pleasure boats sailing here, which conduct excursions for tourists.



The most calm and quiet bay is called Evita (or Karavostasi). There are never waves, because it is located behind a mountain cape. This place is considered the most secluded and calm in Bali, since there is only one hotel and several taverns. Near the beach there are several small bays with beautiful grottoes, which can be reached by boat or by swimming.

And at the very top of the mountain there is an observation deck with a unique view. The highlight of Bali is the absence of waves. But it should be understood that during the high season (July and August) in windy weather, beaches can be crowded not only with vacationers, but also with residents of local villages in which the sea is not so calm.

Restaurants and Leisure in Bali

The village has a lot of souvenir shops, there are several supermarkets. There are many traditional taverns in Bali, which are famous not only for their excellent cuisine, but also for solid portions. One of the most popular places is Panorama, a port tavern.

Proponents of water sports can go diving, canoeing, go sea fishing.

There are no special attractions in Bali. If you want to see something worthwhile, then you can go around the island, which is really rich in interesting places. As for the village, there is Melidoni cave near it. Its worth a look. The entrance to it is located at an altitude of 230 meters above sea level. The main stone hall has spacious dimensions (44 x 55 meters), and the height of the cave ranges from ten to twenty-five meters. The cave is decorated with bizarre stalactites, illuminated by lights.

Bali Paradise Beach 4 *: reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I certainly want to refer to the reviews of tourists who managed to visit this beautiful place. Bali Paradise Beach 4 * (Crete) - an excellent hotel on the coast. The complex is French-speaking and more focused on the French. However, this does not spoil the rest. The bulk of holidaymakers are Europeans. The hotel is always quiet and calm, all guests behave culturally and with dignity. The hotel staff deserves special attention. Almost all employees are polite and attentive. However, no one knows the Russian language. According to our compatriots, some of the workers are more willing to serve the French than the Russians. But this, apparently, affects the human factor. In general, vacationers are very satisfied with both the service and the attitude of the staff.





The hotel has very nice rooms, especially in the main building. If you are unhappy with the apartment, you can always ask to replace the room. As a rule, at the reception they always willingly compromise. The situation in the rooms and the appliances are practically new, everything is working properly, there are no problems with plumbing at all. As for the quality of cleaning, almost everyone is satisfied, noting its high level. Towels and linen are changed very often, while it is clear that all sets are new and not washed. As for detergents in the bathroom, they are not (they are simply not provided), so it makes sense to take a minimal set of gels and shampoos with you.

During check-in, each guest room is left with wine and a fruit plate - a very nice bonus. But there is no drinking water in the apartment, so you need to buy it in the nearest store. Many rooms have not just balconies, but extensive equipped terraces with stunning sea views.

Dining at the hotel is organized on an all-inclusive basis, but no one bothers you to book half board. Each tourist has the right to choose for himself the most suitable option. Hotel guests are very pleased with the variety of buffets and the quality of food. Food is organized at a decent level. All Inclusive also implies the provision of free alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. And in the evening, free vending machines with tea, coffee, and cappuccino work in the lobby.

The real pride of the hotel is its beautiful park. Its territory is well-groomed and surrounded by greenery and flowers. The hotel has large pools. Children especially like the pond with a mini water park. There are also other activities at the hotel. Throughout the territory there are scattered playgrounds for games to which shady alleys lead. Animators work during the day and in the evenings. True, they conduct all programs and shows in French. Not knowing the language, all the replicas are incomprehensible, but the incendiary mood conveyed by the animators perfectly raises the tone.

The hotel has its own small beach on the coast. Go to him no more than five minutes. True, to find yourself on the coast, you need to go down the mountain. And vice versa, you have to go back to the hotel uphill. At the reception they talk about the existence of an electric car that takes guests ashore. It is hard to say how regular his flights are.





The coast itself is incredibly picturesque and clean. The beach is small, there are not many sun loungers on it. There are too many people here in season. But this is a solvable problem: you can take a walk further to a more spacious and free area. Entry into the water is gentle and comfortable everywhere. And the water is incredibly clean and warm.

Instead of an afterword

Bali Paradise Beach 4 * is the most beautiful hotel not only for families, but also for trips with friends. Bali Resort is an incredibly calm and quiet place that will appeal to absolutely all tourists. And the most active guests of the hotel can get acquainted with the ancient culture and sights of the island in more detail.
