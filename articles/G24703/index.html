MTS trust payment will not leave you at zero

It is very easy to find yourself in a situation when the money in your mobile phone account suddenly runs out, and you need to call urgently. There is not always the opportunity or desire to run in search of the nearest payment terminal (at night, for example). This does not mean that you have to sit with zero balance, you just need to remember about the service "Trust payment". MTS - one of the largest mobile operators - offers its customers such a convenient service.

How much money will you give?

It is important to understand how much you can receive using the "Trust payment" service. MTS provides a loan to its subscribers, depending on their monthly costs for paying for cellular communications. So, if you spend about 300 rubles a month, then you will be offered 200 rubles. You deposit in the aggregate from 30 to 500 rubles a month? Get 400 rubles of trust payment. For expenses of more than 500 rubles, you can count on 800 rubles. In this case, the subscriber can independently choose the amount of payment.





How to get a treasured payment?

So, you decided to use the MTS credit service. How to get a trust payment? Let's consider this question in more detail. There are three convenient ways to connect the promised payment.

The first is to call toll free number 1113. This is the telephone of information support for the MTS network. The second is to dial the combination * 111 * 23 # on the telephone keypad and press "Call". The third is to use the MTS Internet Assistant by finding the desired Promised Payment service in the "Payment" section.

Now that the service is connected, you can use it. Dial the combination * 111 * 123 #, send a call and select the amount of the trust payment. By default, the minimum amount is 50 rubles, and the maximum depends on your expenses for cellular communications. It should be clarified that if you have been serviced at MTS for less than two months, then the service is available to you only with a positive balance on the account.

MTS offers to repay the trust payment taken on credit within a week. Moreover, the cost of the service is 5 rubles.

Also note that you can activate the "Promised Payment" only if you do not use the "On Full Trust" service, as these types of loans are mutually exclusive. The status of the service can be easily checked by calling the toll-free number 11131, dialing the combination * 111 * 1230 # or via the Internet in your personal account.





MTS trust payment is a real lifesaver that will not allow you to remain without communication, wherever you are and whenever you need funds on your mobile phone account.
