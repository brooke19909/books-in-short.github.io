How to get to Vnukovo by public transport and by car

Airport "Vnukovo" occupies an important position in state air traffic. At present, Vnukovo is in 3rd place in Russia in terms of the number of passengers served. Not so long ago, the airport terminal has gained unprecedented popularity regarding the opening of a new international and domestic air service, the transportation of which is engaged in "Victory". For the Aeroflot subsidiary , Vnukovo Airport is the base airport in Moscow, and the flow of tourists who want to fly abroad at a favorable rate is simply endless. Because of this, many passengers who are accustomed to Domodedovo, and who have not been to the capital before, the question arises: "How to get to Vnukovo?"

History

The beginning of the history of Vnukovo Airport can be considered 1936, when its construction had just begun. The official opening of the airport took place on July 2, 1941. During the war years, Vnukovo Airport served as a strategically important facility, where the Moscow Special Purpose Aviation Group was located. It is also interesting that it was precisely at the Vnukovo airport that the plane landed on May 9, 1945, flying on a Berlin-Moscow flight and delivering the Act of Unconditional Surrender of Hitler Germany.

Location

The main feature of the Vnukovo airport terminal is its good geographical location. It is worth noting that it is located at a distance of only 27 kilometers from the historical center of the capital.

An equally important feature of Vnukovo is its location above sea level at an altitude of 205 meters - an additional bonus in case of adverse weather conditions.

How to get to Vnukovo airport?

There are several ways to get to this point from Moscow: by express train, bus or taxi. Of course, there is the option of renting a vehicle, but it is likely that you will stand in traffic jams.

Aeroexpress

High-speed trains and trains are the most reliable form of transport in this case. Having resorted to the services of the railway, you will not only save time, but will not worry in vain because of road traffic in the capital. Trains from the Kievsky station are regularly sent to Vnukovo between 6 a.m. and midnight. Travel time by aeroexpress will be about 40 minutes, which can be considered a very profitable option in a metropolis. It is also necessary to carefully plan the trip, as the express trains have non-working hours, which you should learn about in advance on the official website.

By personal transport or taxi

How to get to "Vnukovo" without any extra problems? Of course, on one of the authorized city taxis. This type of transport has its advantages, but despite this there are many disadvantages. Let's get it together.

Prosperous passengers mostly resort to taxi services, since taking into account Moscow prices the trip can cost a tidy sum. Rather, this option is more suitable for driving in the opposite direction from the terminal. A taxi or private car is always a huge risk of getting stuck in traffic jams. You can not only delay, but even be late for the flight.

When driving on a car yourself, use the Yandex.Navigator application, but do not forget to check the box that takes into account traffic jams - you will get a real picture of the situation on the roads.

Public transport

Can I save money to get to the Vnukovo airport? You can - take a bus or taxi. However, despite the budget of this option, it should be understood that the bus can get stuck in traffic and get stuck there for a long time. In good time, you can get to the terminal without traffic jams in 30-40 minutes, but always have behind you a couple of hours of emergency reserve. So, how to get to Vnukovo by public transport?

Public transport runs regularly, but from a specific point on the map. One of these points is the metro station "South-West".

Which bus goes to Vnukovo? From the Teply Stan metro station, city buses at number 611 and minibuses at number 611 depart, but with additional letters.

Bus number 566 makes over 40 stops, so the travel time can be about 1.5 hours without traffic jams. It comes from:

  • metro station "Rumyantsevo";
  • metro station "Salaryevo";
  • metro station "Troparevo".

At these stations, you can catch 611 buses following the metro station Yugo-Zapadnaya.

Parking spots in Vnukovo

A rather problematic issue of parking spaces is how a heavy load falls on the drivers head. When traveling by car, keep in mind that Vnukovo is an integral part of Moscow, which means that fines are imposed for violating parking rules.

Parking at the airport is paid and hourly. The cost of one hour of parking space is 100 rubles. If you plan to leave the car for a longer period, then it is better to use special daily rates after acquiring a club card. For boarding or disembarking passengers, you can enter the territory for free for a short-term period.
