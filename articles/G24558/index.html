Lake Saki: reviews of tourists

In the Republic of Crimea there is an amazing corner of nature, which is famous throughout the world for its unique healing effect - this is Lake Saki. It is called the "gift of the gods." And it is not surprising that this ecosystem has earned such a pathos status. Lake Saki is the place where people come to heal not only their body, but also their soul. It is located in the central part of the Crimean city of Saki.

Gift of nature

Lake Sakskoe is nothing but a salt estuary about eight kilometers long and three kilometers wide. Despite such a small size, this health laboratory can work wonders, saving a person from a vast arsenal of diseases. What is remarkable about this ecosystem? It's all about her healing mud. They help people to improve their health and cope with diseases that bother them for a long time.





In ancient times, Lake Saki was a Black Sea bay where table salt was mined. Over time, dark gray and black silts began to form on it, the upper layer of which had a healing effect.

As early as the 19th century, the first mud bath was established in Saki, thanks to which many soldiers and officers were able to save the lives of the Russian-Turkish war.

Lake in modern conditions

Currently, Saki Lake is divided into western and eastern parts, separated by a dam. The first is the raw material basis of one of the chemical enterprises, and the second is represented by sanatoriums and hospitals, where patients undergo rehabilitation courses. It is noteworthy that the water in the lake has a high density, so the risk of drowning in it is very small.

Unique properties of a natural laboratory

Saki Lake has such unique properties that its healing effect is considered stronger than the mud of the Dead Sea.

In terms of health promotion, they are indispensable. The mud of Lake Saki is a storehouse of minerals and nutrients. Iron, copper, cobalt, calcium - this is a small fraction of what they consist of. Arriving in this natural laboratory to promote health, after the procedures, people increase the tone of the body and completely restore its immune system.





What ailments help cure Saki mud

This miraculous lake enables people to get rid of a number of diseases. Cellulitis, arthrosis, osteochondrosis, gout, calcaneal spurs, infertility in women, colpitis, hormonal ovarian failure, prostatitis, erectile dysfunction - this list of ailments can be listed indefinitely. Saki mud lake really eliminates the above pathologies. Not only Russians come here to heal, but also residents from all over the world.

Sanatoriums Sak

Self treatment

Lake Sak mud, which has an amazing healing effect, attracts patients to come to this resort again. We should not forget that only experienced professionals are entitled to engage in mud therapy.





However, sometimes it happens that visitors begin to start medical procedures without the help of professionals. Such behavior does not stand up to criticism, since mud treatment with the wrong approach can not only not improve health, but, on the contrary, harm it. If you nevertheless at your own risk tried to treat yourself, then you should adhere to the following recommendation, namely: apply dirt to the skin using the alternation method. For example, on the first day you treat the joints of the legs, on the second - the hands, on the third - the chest area and so on. Applications should not be overexposed: the procedure should be approximately 20 minutes, after which the dirt should be washed off with warm water.

Contraindications

Well, and, of course, do not forget that not all people are recommended for mud therapy. In particular, we are talking about those who suffer from hypertension, oncology, diabetes mellitus, cardiovascular diseases, and epilepsy. Also, mud baths are contraindicated in pregnant women.
