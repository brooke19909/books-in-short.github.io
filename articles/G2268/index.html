Spa hotels in Krasnodar: list of names, rooms, services and reviews with photos

Walking along the streets of Krasnodar, of course, contribute to the unloading of the mind and allow you to enjoy beautiful views. But for those who want to relax in such a way as to feel like they are reborn, you should visit the local spa hotels. There are quite a lot of them in Krasnodar. A list of the best of them, with a detailed description and feedback from visitors, is presented in the article.

Top 5

In Krasnodar, hotels with a spa are especially popular. The most favorite places by the townspeople:

  • The Grand Spa Hotel in Krasnodar is located on Alexander Pokryshkin Street.
  • Hotel Congress Krasnodar - Gavrilova Street.
  • Art Villa on Leonid Lavrov Street.
  • "Paradise SPA" located on the Adygea embankment.
  • Amici Grand Hotel is located on Red Partisan Street.

These establishments are recommended for visiting both city guests and local residents. How to get relaxation in spa hotels of Krasnodar?

Hotel Grand SPA Hotel Avax

This spa hotel Krasnodar is very comfortable and cozy. Here is a wide selection of rooms of various types. The hotel management pays particular attention to the entertainment and relaxation of its guests. So, after paying for all rooms, all visitors have free access to spa and fitness areas, as well as swimming in the pool. The hotel rooms have air conditioning and TV. Toiletries, slippers and bathrobes are also provided. But not all spa treatments are free. Massages and salon treatments are available at an additional cost.

According to visitors' reviews, the spa area is simply gorgeous. But many are saddened by the fact that these procedures are permissible only for visits without children. Many note a very high-quality, relaxing whole body massage. Also, guests are pleased with delicious dishes in the menu of the local restaurant. As for the shortcomings, vacationers note the absence of dryers for swimwear. After working days, the residents of Krasnodar gladly go here.

Hotel Congress Krasnodar

This Krasnodar hotel and spa with a swimming pool is located near the shopping mall "City Center". The hotel has a large number of rooms, each of which is equipped with a TV, work desk, refrigerator and wardrobe. Particular attention is paid to spa treatments. Here, experienced professionals delight guests with massages, hydromassage complexes and a steam bath. Guests can also enjoy Thai massages and try exotic spas at the hotel. Hotel Krasnodar Congress pleases its customers with good service and friendly staff.

Visitors note the location of the spa center, and they are also fascinated by the huge and very comfortable rooms. The food and staff are also, in their opinion, at a very high level. As for the shortcomings, they consider the most important thing to be a high level of prices. But everyone considers them absolutely justified.

Art villa

Art Villa is a hotel located opposite the Oz Shopping Center. The airport is also close to this place: only five minutes away. All guests, in addition to good service and comfortable rooms, receive free parking. In addition, visitors can use the local pool, hot tub, solarium and sauna absolutely free of charge. All rooms are equipped with a good soundproofing system. Also in them without fail there are such benefits as air conditioning, TV and private toilet. Part of the room stock has beautiful balconies or terraces. The cafe at the hotel offers visitors dishes of various cuisines of the world.

The first thing that pleases the guests of this hotel is a very convenient location. They also note the friendliness of the staff. To the list of positive aspects, guests of Art Villa include a small room rate. And, of course, everyone praises this place for a chic pool.

"Paradise SPA"

Karaoke, billiards, massage, swimming pool, bathhouse, summer terrace - all these benefits await their guests at the Paradise SPA Hotel. The hotel rooms are made in a variety of styles. There is also a minimalist room in the loft style and cozy in the Baroque style. Also, guests can choose the classic comfortable or luxurious bourgeois options. Guests here enjoy not only the beautiful decoration of their homes, but also very exciting entertainments. Firstly, residents can take a steam bath in a wood-burning bath. In this direction there is an oriental hammam and a Russian bathhouse. All visitors receive the freshest fragrant brooms. And for those who wish to relax, the hotel offers a host of different spa treatments.

Spa Hotel Krasnodar "Paradise-SPA" offers the following services:

  1. Outdoor pool - will give a cheerfulness charge to each visitor.
  2. Bath tub - after the steam room, hotel guests can plunge into the bath with contrasting water. It is made in the unusual style of past years and is decorated with fresh flower petals.
  3. Massage for the sophisticated - relaxation of body and soul in an elegant setting.
  4. Massage of various parts of the body and general massage - contribute to the healing of the body.

Hotel guests respond very pleasantly about the steam room of this institution. They are also pleased with the advantageous loyalty system. Heated pool and delicious food in the cafe also left a pleasant impression on visitors. But there are those who have faced hanging prices for those services that visitors have not used.

Amici grand hotel

A luxurious hotel with an aqua-thermal complex, attracts guests with its scale and beauty. Also on the territory there is a restaurant and a hall for conducting business negotiations and business meetings. In total there are no more than ten types of rooms with excellent repair and good equipment. Each of them has in its arsenal comfortable beds and pillows, a safe for storing valuables, a telephone, a minibar, bathrooms with personal accessories and an innovative security system. Room amenities at this Krasnodar spa hotel include satellite TV, air conditioning, and blackout curtains. The price of each guest includes an aqua-thermal complex. It involves a pool and fitness.

Hotel guests can also book a hydromassage, sauna, body wraps, solarium and hammam. In their reviews, visitors write that the hotel is simply magnificent and they are ready to return here again and again. They note that all staff are helpful: from security to management. And luxurious rooms and the possibility of comfortable rest and relaxation make your stay in the hotel very comfortable.
