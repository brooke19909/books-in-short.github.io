"Morozovo" is a resort hotel. Recreation facilities in Novosibirsk

One cannot but agree that Siberian nature has a tremendous healing effect on the human body. Huge areas of pristine forests with their breathtaking fresh air, mountain springs and springs with mineral waters have miraculous power, allowing everyone to quickly improve their health. That is why in Siberia there is a huge number of diverse resorts, resorts and health resorts.





Recreation or corporate? Welcome!

One of these institutions is the recreation center "Morozovo". Novosibirsk, where the hotel in question is located, is a huge metropolis, not far from which landscapes of amazing beauty lie. Ecological purity of nature attracts many tourists to this area. For their comfortable stay, new hotels and tourist facilities are opened annually in the region.

Novosibirsk resorts are characterized by a wide range of services. Recreation, recreation, sports, leisure with family and friends - any institution will be happy to shelter travelers on its territory, regardless of the purpose of their visit.

Accommodation and way

"Morozovo" is a resort hotel located on the banks of the famous Ob Reservoir, near Akademgorodok. Getting to this place is very simple. From Novosibirsk you must follow to the regional center called Iskitim. From there, the path will run to Akademgorodok. The regional center and the hotel in question are separated by forty kilometers. Therefore, it is unlikely that the journey will take more than forty minutes. It is worth noting that the route to the resort is convenient and well-developed. Many recreation centers in Novosibirsk are located near the regional center. This is a huge plus for people who travel on vacation by public transport.

"Morozovo" - a resort hotel, spread out in a picturesque area, remote from the hustle and bustle of the city. The mighty forest, consisting of deciduous and pine trees, protects the recreation center from the outside world, allowing you to fully enjoy the peace and tranquility. This recreational institution is rightly called the pearl of the Ob reservoir. Helpful and hospitable staff is always happy to offer its guests a four-star service of a European level. A wide range of services will delight even the most avid skeptics.





Housing stock

"Morozovo" is a resort hotel with forty-two rooms on its territory, distinguished by increased comfort and coziness. Along with standard rooms, visitors can opt for luxurious luxury apartments. These rooms have a fantastic winter garden, which can be accessed from the private terrace. The classic interior of all rooms is the result of the fruitful work of designers. In winter, the rooms offer stunning views of the sleeping forest covered with sparkling snow. In summer, guests will be happy to watch the iridescent colors of emerald lawns and the water surface of the reservoir. A fabulous landscape, majestic and calm nature, a soft breeze from the side of an artificial lake create wonderful conditions for recreation in any direction: recreational, active, healing, family or even corporate.

Outdoor activities and horseback riding
