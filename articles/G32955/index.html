Memories Splash Punta Cana 5 * (Dominican Republic / Punta Cana): description, hotel infrastructure, room categories

We want to devote our article to the Dominican Republic, or rather, one of its hotels. Memories Splash Punta Cana 5 * is one of the hotels of the Blu Diamond Hotels & Resorts group. The complex operates on an interesting system - All in Luxury, originally conceived for those tourists who want to spend their holidays absolutely carefree. Hotel guests can use not only the infrastructure of this hotel, but also some of the amenities of the Royalton Punta Cana.

A bit about the hotel

Memories Splash Punta Cana 5 * was built in 2013 on the site of another hotel - Grand Paradise Bavaro. The complex is recommended for all categories of vacationers, including families with children. The main advantage of the hotel is the possibility of settling in adjacent apartments. The complex has a good location, the distance to the airport is only twenty seven kilometers. The hotel is a three-story building.



Memories Splash Punta Cana 5 * is located on the second line, a hundred meters from the coast. The length of the hotel's beach is 290 meters.

Hotel Rooms

Memories Splash 5 * (Dominican Republic, Punta Cana) is ready to offer 525 rooms to its guests. All of them are very diverse and have their own advantages. Apartment categories Memories Splash 5 *:

  1. Deluxe Room. These apartments have pool or garden views. The maximum number of people in a room is four. It is possible to combine apartments of this category.
  2. Deluxe Jacuzzi Room. An added bonus is the availability of a jacuzzi. It also has a terrace or balcony with pool or garden views. Some apartments have direct pool access. Designed for a settlement of four people.
  3. Deluxe Family Jacuzzi Suite. The room has a bedroom and a living room. The apartments are equipped with a large bed and a jacuzzi. Some rooms have access to the pool. The apartments are designed for four guests.

Memories Splash 5 *: premium numbers

The hotel has more spacious rooms for those guests who are accustomed to large spaces:

  1. Premium Room. These rooms are closer to the coast. This category of apartments can be combined.
  2. Premium Jacuzzi Junior Suite are large rooms with a jacuzzi and a terrace.
  3. Presidential Jacuzzi One Bedroom Suite - apartments with a living room and a bedroom, two bathrooms and balconies.

Climate of the Dominican Republic

Dominican Republic is a paradise country for relaxation. The reason for this is its climate, subtropical and humid. The heat is very easily tolerated, as there are soft trade winds and breezes.

The rainy season lasts from May to September. At this time there are frequent but short showers. Rains in December and November are possible. For the rest of the year, the weather in the Dominican Republic is very warm and dry. The best time to relax is the period from December to April. But if your vacation falls at a completely different time, do not despair. Showers in no way interfere with your vacation. The fact is that the rains here, although heavy, but short-term, literally in half an hour no trace of water can be seen.

If you want to go out during a rainstorm, the service will kindly offer you a huge umbrella. In such periods, an ocean storm can be observed. This is something awesome!



Dominican Republic is an amazing place. On a relatively small territory of the state there are snow-white spacious beaches, the ocean, the sea, and mountain peaks, arid deserts, moist forests. The temperature from +26 to +30 degrees is kept here all year round. At any time, tourists are waited by the warm ocean and the hot sun.

Sightseeing Tours

Dominican Republic is an exotic country rich in beautiful places and attractions.

Altos de Chavon is a city of artists, which is a copy of a medieval village. Here tourists can get acquainted with the culture of the country.



Costa del Barn is a very popular tourist area. It is also called the "Amber Coast." He became famous thanks to the abundance of amber reserves.

No less interesting for holidaymakers is Jose Bermudez Park. It is located in the central region of the island and borders two nature reserves. The park has the three highest peaks in the entire Dominican Republic. On the territory of the conservation zone immediately begin the twelve major rivers of the country. Here you can see deep gorges, beautiful landscapes, alpine meadows. It is local beauties that attract crowds of tourists here.

Amazing local reliefs create unique landscapes. And the vegetation is striking in its diversity, because at the foot of the mountains the real jungle grows. Palm trees, cedars, creepers, dates, mahogany, olive, juniper and many other plants grow here.

Wildlife lovers should visit the famous ecological park called Natural Eyes. In this place, before the eyes of a person, amazing, untouched by people tropical forests with all the diversity of fauna and flora appear. The park occupies more than six hundred hectares. On its territory there are giant and outlandish flowers, centuries-old trees, fanciful creepers. In total, the reserve has about five hundred species of wild plants, almost a hundred species of birds. And the beaches of the park were chosen by large sea ‚Äã‚Äãturtles, which lay their eggs in the hot sand.

Lovers of large sandy beaches will certainly enjoy the Samana Peninsula. In the most amazing way, large areas of virgin land were preserved intact here. On the peninsula you can see the largest desert beaches, caves, waterfalls, clean rivers and coconut groves. No less interesting are the sea depths in which whales, manatees and dolphins are found.

The famous "city of waterfalls" is impressive. Harabakoa collected an incredible number of waterfalls that form a whole water palette. And for mountaineering lovers, Duarte Peak is of genuine interest.



In general, to get acquainted with tourists with the sights of the Dominican Republic, you definitely need to start from the capital, Santo Domingo. In this wonderful city you can look into the Cathedral, visit the Museum of Tobacco, stroll through the picturesque streets.

But, undoubtedly, the most important attraction of the Dominican Republic is its endless sandy beaches. The most beautiful and most popular places are in the province of Puerto Plata, located in the north of the country.

We gave an example of only a few Dominican attractions that are worth seeing. In fact, they are much larger and, perhaps, one vacation is not enough to see them.
