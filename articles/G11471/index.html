An unforgettable vacation in Tunisia: Hotel Royal Jinene 4

Description: In Tunisia, near the city of Sousse, there is a cozy Hotel Royal Jinene 4, built in national style in 2004. This small hotel is a great place to relax with your family. It is located on a large landscaped area, at a distance of three kilometers from the central part of the city. Thanks to the all-inclusive program, good infrastructure, as well as the opportunity to play sports, the hotel is suitable for tourists of different ages.





Staying at the Hotel Royal Jinene, you can easily get to the main tourist destinations in Sousse.

For example, visit the Medina of Sousse, which is surrounded by a fortress wall, or the Old Town, which has many souvenir shops, as well as the catacombs of the Good Shepherd.

And you can plunge into the nightlife of Tunisia and visit the Hergla Park entertainment complex.

The hotel is a five-story building located on the first coastline.

Rooms: Hotel Royal Jinene 4 offers its guests 105 standard

rooms of 30 square meters, which can accommodate up to four people, and 5 suites of 60 square meters, consisting of two rooms and two bathrooms. Each living room has a TV and central air conditioning. Thanks to the available telephone, tourists can make a call either to the administrator or to the city. A minibar and luggage storage are available for a fee. The flooring used is tile. Almost every room has access to a terrace or balcony. Room service is available around the clock.

Meals: At the request of guests, Hotel Royal Jinene 4 offers a choice of full board or half board, including breakfast and dinner. You can also use the services of two restaurants and three bars, which offer both national and international dishes.



Beach: Hotel Royal Jinene 4 has three swimming pools, one of which is indoor. In the event that guests prefer to relax on the beach, they should use their own hotel, which is 50 meters long. Free umbrellas, deck chairs and mattresses will be offered here (paid).

Additional information: Hotel Royal Jinene 4 staff offers free use of the solarium, a disco and entertainment programs. Also, inventory for games such as tennis (table), darts and chess, volleyball, is freely available. For a fee, you can visit the fitness center or relax in the sauna or Turkish bath, treat yourself to a massage or relax in the jacuzzi. In addition, the hotel infrastructure is represented by a beauty salon, laundry, as well as a business center.

Digest: Tourists note that the linen in the rooms is changed daily. In addition, recently renovated at Hotel Royal Jinene 4. Reviews describe that the hotel staff is friendly and willing to help regardless of the time of day. The chef expresses special gratitude to the chef for delicious, varied food.

Tourists express special gratitude for the available children's entertainment programs and for the presence of a children's pool. Those who have been here at least once come back here again.
