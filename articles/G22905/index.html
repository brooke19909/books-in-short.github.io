How to wear sweatshirts with bare shoulders

This season, many eminent designers presented in their collections sweatshirts with open shoulders. Such things look very beautiful and romantic. A variety of shuttlecocks, assemblies, ruffles and lace are used as a trim for sweaters with open shoulders.



Of course, such clothes can not be attributed to one of the components of your classic wardrobe. But you do not live only work. Sweatshirts with open shoulders are perfect for a laid-back style, and will also look great in combination with casual clothes.
