Ski resort Belokurikha (Altai Territory): prices, reviews and photos

Altai is the pride of the Siberian region. Once you see the magnificent landscape of this region, you fall in love with it forever. The authorities emphasize the development of the tourism industry, as travelers from all over the world come here every year.





The region has the largest ski resort Belokurikha. The city is attractive with its history, diverse ethnological and archaeological structures, as well as inexhaustible healing springs and rich fauna. The resort area can rightfully be called a popular balneological health resort.

Mother Nature herself generously endowed these lands with magnificent mountain landscapes, forest valleys, healing springs and a comfortable healing climate. Man only helped to improve the benefits created before him: equipped slopes for safe skiing, built comfortable pensions.

Rest in the resort Belokurikha (Altai Territory)

All year round, visitors can enjoy the colorful landscapes of this city, inhale the fresh mountain air saturated with ozone, swim in the mineral radon waters. In addition to climate therapy and skiing, you will be offered fascinating hiking trails. You can ride horses in the local surroundings or book a bus tour of the Altai Mountains, see the legendary Aya Lake, Denisov Caves and other attractions.





Both professional climbers, titled snowboarders, and amateur beginners flock to the Belokurikha resort. Even an amateur will be able to quickly learn skiing by visiting a local school where real masters work. There are no age restrictions for conquering snow-covered slopes.

Belokurikha ski resort falls in love with itself from the first instant: extreme descents along the mountain peaks are breathtaking, inspire and inspire confidence in one's own abilities. This is much better than lying in front of the TV.

Where to stay?

The Grace complex was built at the foot of Tserkovka Mountain in the immediate vicinity of the mountain slopes. Here you can fully relax and get quality treatment. Periodically, the complex organizes real skiing sports. A 4-storey building has been built to accommodate.

The hotel has rope tows that take guests to the slopes. After an active day, the Grace complex will offer you to relax in the jacuzzi, sauna and solarium. Evening entertainment is organized. The complex has a pharmacy kiosk, a rental point for transport, a games room.

Katun





Belokurikha resort (Altai Territory) invites tourists to stay at the Katun sanatorium, which is comfortably located at an altitude of 230 meters above sea level. Nearby are the market square, water park, entertainment venues and shops. Nearby is a long trail (850 m), which can be reached by lift. Necessary sports equipment can be rented. Directly in the building of the sanatorium you will find a library, sauna, swimming pool, beauty salon, medical department. The minimum price for a double room is 2800 rubles per day.

Sanatorium "Altai-West"





Belokurikha ski resort is an international base that provides quality services for every tourist. On its territory there is a balneological dispensary "Altai" with European service and level of comfort. For guests there is a wide selection of rooms, leisure activities, including multilevel tracks and winding slopes. Highlighted safe gentle slopes of small length, designed for beginner skiers and children. The price for a room starts at 2800 rubles per day.

Complex "Aurora"

In the city of Belokurikha (Altai Territory) there is another no less interesting sanatorium under the intriguing name "Aurora". Next to it are built boarding houses "Northern Lights" and "Solar Hill". It is not crowded, so guests can relax without fuss and noise. The infrastructure and medical base are highly developed in the dispensary. A five-minute walk is the cable car, which is illuminated even at night. Accommodation in a double room in the spring and winter months will cost 3000 rubles per day. In summer, you will have to pay about 2700 rubles for a room.

Wellness center "Russia" (Belokurikha resort)





This is a large medical sanatorium with its own long route (green). The slope is focused on simple lovers, thirsting for vivid emotions and adrenaline. At the rental point you will be given special equipment, they will brief you on skiing. All slopes are covered with artificial snow, they are gentle and safe for children. The cost of living is from 2900 rubles per day.

Healing factors

Belokurikha resort (Altai Territory), whose photos illustrate the wealth of natural resources, is an internationally recognized balneological center. This status was given to the city due to thermal radon sources containing fluorine and silicon. Hot mineral waters contribute to the elimination of inflammatory processes, allergic reactions, produce analgesic effects. Studies have shown that radon baths improve the activity of the endocrine, cardiac, immune and nervous systems.





It is for the restoration of lost health that many aspire to the Belokurikha ski resort. In all of the above complexes is a professional therapy. Sanatoria are equipped with modern diagnostic facilities with high-quality equipment. Treatment is carried out in the field of gynecology, gastrointestinal tract, respiratory system. Complexes specialize in skin, urological and musculoskeletal pathologies.

Resort through the eyes of tourists

All vacationers speak with enthusiasm about the city. They like the peaceful atmosphere, pristine nature, high level of service, smooth tracks. Holidays spent in the Altai Territory, according to tourists, fills with positive, drive and gives fireworks indescribable emotions. In addition to a great vacation, there is an opportunity to heal. Numerous treatments enhance physical and psychological health.
