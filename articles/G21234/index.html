The most famous hotels of Izhevsk

Izhevsk is not only the capital of Udmurtia, but also one of the largest cities in the Russian Federation. In the world, the place is known as the center of engineering, metallurgy and defense industries. Therefore, it is not surprising that businessmen, business people and entrepreneurs regularly come here. And there are many sights in the city. Many tourists also come to see them. Thus, hotels in Izhevsk never lack guests. There are many hotels of different categories in the metropolis. You can settle in budget two-three-star establishments, as well as in luxurious four- and five-star apartments.

Popular Three Star City Locations

Among the guests, the most popular are the three-star hotels of Izhevsk, which offer quite comfortable holidays at an affordable cost. The most successful institutions include:

  • Amax Central is a comfortable hotel located next to the Udmurtia Exhibition Center and other museums. Amaks Central has been operating since 1967. The center of Izhevsk can be reached on foot in 20 minutes. Also nearby is the airport. The hotel has 210 rooms equipped with climate control systems, relaxation areas, balconies and safes.




  • "Hotel Narcissus" - a location remote from the central part of the village for six kilometers. Each room at Narcissus Hotel has a flat-screen TV and a dressing room. It offers guests an ATM, 24-hour reception, luggage storage and ironing service.
  • Some hotels in Izhevsk care so much about their guests that they are ready to offer them the most extensive range of services. So, in the Uralskaya Hotel, in addition to comfortably furnished rooms, there is a banquet hall, a copier, private free parking and a safe. The institution has 63 spacious rooms.

The best among three-star hotels

Hotel "Rainbow" (Izhevsk) is one of the best three-star hotels. The institution is a club hotel. It was opened relatively recently, in 2004, but two years later it was reconstructed. This is a country hotel, but it is only half an hour from the city from it. The complex has two two-story residential buildings, a restaurant, an administrative building, a football field and an outdoor pool. There is also a basketball / volleyball court and a tavern.

In the "Rainbow" provides 52 rooms belonging to the categories of economy, standard and standard +. But, regardless of class, each room has a TV, telephone, minibar and a bathroom equipped with a shower. The bathroom has a hairdryer and bath accessories. All entrance doors to rooms are equipped with electronic locks. In the rooms there is the possibility of placing a cot for a child and / or an extra adult bed.

The club-hotel has a restaurant that can accommodate 150 people, a bar and a tavern where they prepare delicious Russian pancakes.



Premier Hotel

Premier Hotel (Izhevsk) is located in the very center of the village. It is surrounded by restaurants and shopping centers. Its architectural design is similar to a mansion. The interior of the hotel is made in a restrained classic style. Since the Premier Hotel is a very small place, it manages to create a homely atmosphere for its guests, filled with peace and tranquility. The institution has twenty deluxe rooms, among which there are two-room and one-room apartments, cozy small and huge chic offers.

Premier Hotel (Izhevsk) offers free access to the World Wide Web. If you are on a business trip, then at your service is a conference room, which can accommodate forty people. The room is equipped with a TV, the diagonal of which reaches 60 inches.



You are welcome to "Deryabin"

The Deryabin Hotel (Izhevsk), which is also located in the center of the metropolis, always joyfully greets its guests. Depending on their financial abilities, guests can choose one of 66 rooms. But the rooms are furnished with all necessary things for relaxation and work. The price of any room includes internet connection and breakfast (buffet). The hotel building has a relaxation room equipped with a jacuzzi and a conference room.



4 star apartments

Izhevsk hotels take care of their guests, and therefore each of them seeks to stand out from the rest of the institutions. So, one of the most fashionable establishments of the city is Hotel Malina, which consists of only eight rooms. There are few rooms here, but all of them are equipped with household items: ironing board and iron, safe, individual air conditioning, telephone and microwave. Bathrooms include slippers, toiletries, and hair dryers.
