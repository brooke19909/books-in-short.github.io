Mythos Palace Resort SPA 4 *: tourist reviews, hotel rating, photo

Mythos Palace Resort SPA is a luxury establishment where you can immerse yourself in the atmosphere of a carefree vacation. Here you can forget about the bustle of the city and completely relax.





Brief description of the hotel

The resort and hotel complex Mythos Palace Resort SPA is a popular modern hotel, the buildings of which are decorated in the best traditions of Cretan architecture, which has an important effect on the general atmosphere of this institution. Also, you can not ignore the beautiful flowering gardens that occupy most of the territory of this resort. In addition, this hotel boasts a pristine private beach, which has been awarded a number of international awards.

It is impossible not to say about the comfortable apartments of the Mythos Palace Resort SPA. Their design is quite concise, but at the same time elegant and stylish. It is also worth noting the large number of amenities provided in all guest rooms. A feature of the hotel can be considered that from the windows of almost all apartments, guests can enjoy incredibly beautiful sea views.

At Mythos Palace Resort SPA, guests have access to a huge number of services that will provide you with a fun and active pastime. SPA treatments are also very popular, thanks to which you can feel complete relaxation and harmony with the outside world. Be sure to visit the hotel restaurant, which can be considered a real highlight of the dishes from the freshest seafood.





Where is the resort located

The resort and hotel complex Mythos Palace Resort SPA 4 * is located in the small resort town of Kavros, which is famous for its beautiful beaches and nature. From here, the nearest international airport is just over 50 kilometers away. But the sea is literally at hand.

Standard Apartment

The largest category of apartments of the Mythos Palace Resort SPA 4 * hotel is "Standard". These are small bungalows that can accommodate up to 4 guests. In addition to the beds (double or a pair of one and a half), the room also has a soft sofa and an armchair-bed, which can accommodate additional guests. You can choose for yourself apartments with sea views or a flowering garden. The second option will cost a little cheaper.





Junior Suites

Sea vacation is the goal of many tourists arriving at the Mythos Palace Resort SPA 4 * hotel. Crete is famous for its beaches, and therefore you will certainly be pleased with the opportunity to live in close proximity to one of them. Leaving the bungalow, you immediately find yourself on a clean coast. The house itself consists of two rooms. The bedroom in a large bed will comfortably accommodate two guests. The living room has folding upholstered furniture, which can also be used for resettlement. You will certainly have your own balcony or porch with comfortable rattan furniture, as well as a clothes dryer. There is also an option with a private pool.

Villas

Villa is the most luxurious and expensive hotel accommodation option. They can accommodate up to 6 guests thanks to their impressive floor space. The villa consists of two bedrooms, each of which has its own bathroom, as well as a living room with upholstered furniture and an attached toilet. Having settled in such a house, you will have the opportunity to use your own pool, around which there are sun loungers and parasols. The apartments of Mythos Palace Resort SPA 4 * are decorated in calm pastel colors. Photos taken by tourists demonstrate a classic interior with original decorative elements.

Room Amenities

In all apartments of the Mythos Palace Resort SPA 4 * resort complex (Greece, Georgioupolis) the following amenities are provided for a comfortable stay of guests:

  • the bathroom has a shower, as well as an electric hairdryer;
  • a complete set of bath, hygiene and cosmetic accessories is provided;
  • depending on the category and number of storeys, there is a private balcony or porch with comfortable rattan furniture, as well as a dryer for bath towels and bathing suits;
  • thanks to an individual air conditioner, you can cool or warm the air temperature in your room to a comfortable level;
  • landline telephone gives you the opportunity at any time to contact the reception, as well as make a call abroad;
  • a large flat-screen TV is connected to a satellite dish, which broadcasts dozens of channels, most of which are entertainment and music;
  • the minibar is filled with non-alcoholic refreshments for an additional fee, and can also serve as a small refrigerator for storing food;
  • so that you can be safe about the safety of your valuables, use a reliable safe with an electronic combination lock.

Resort Infrastructure

Guests of the Mythos Palace Resort SPA 4 * (Crete, Chania) can use the following infrastructure facilities:

  • a large clean outdoor pool, which operates in the warm season (heating function for cloudy weather is also provided);
  • in winter you will have the opportunity to use the indoor pool, the premises of which are heated;
  • a large green garden with benches and gazebos, which occupies a third of the entire hotel;
  • a restaurant serving breakfast and serving the most delicious traditional Greek cuisine;
  • a bar with a huge selection of drinks and light original snacks, as well as an evening entertainment program;
  • free wireless Internet (operates not only in public areas, but also in rooms, and even by the pool);
  • free parking with 24-hour security;
  • reception desk, which works round the clock for registration, as well as advising guests (you can contact in person or by phone);
  • in the business center you can access computer and copy equipment;
  • in the on-site shop you can buy souvenirs as a keepsake.




Entertainment and recreation opportunities

In this resort you can use the following services for entertainment and active pastime:

  • a large tennis court, which can be used not only during the day, but also at night, due to the presence of lighting;
  • in the billiard room, in addition to the equipment for the game, there is also a small bar and a plasma TV;
  • in the garden, as well as on the beach there are several ping-pong tables;
  • if you follow your figure and want to keep fit even on vacation, be sure to visit the gym, where you can work out with modern equipment, as well as consult a trainer;
  • After a hard workout, be sure to visit the sauna, which will allow you to relax both physically and emotionally;
  • in the SPA center you can use a huge number of procedures both for maintaining beauty and youth, and for healing the body as a whole;
  • Such a service as a hot tub is very popular;
  • visiting a massage room, you will be able to appreciate the diversity of techniques, as well as the professionalism of local masters;
  • an excellent alternative to a traditional bath will be the Turkish hammam;
  • by renting a bike, you will get not only an effective simulator, but also a good means of transportation;
  • for the smallest in the shade of the garden there is a large playground;
  • every day you will be entertained by animation staff, and in the evenings you will be able to participate in incendiary show programs.




Positive reviews

Mythos Palace Resort SPA will not leave anyone indifferent. Reviews of tourists about this institution are full of vivid impressions and approving comments:

  • a very beautiful and clean beach with a large walking path for those who do not want to swim, but want to breathe fresh sea air;
  • There is a very good transport interchange near the hotel, which gives you the opportunity to independently go on an excursion to anywhere in the island;
  • due to the fact that the hotel has very few rooms, it is quiet and peaceful on its territory;
  • there are several pools of different depths, thanks to which everyone can choose the right one for themselves;
  • the hotel is slightly removed from the busy street, which will save you from noise, but at the same time a trip to the resort center will not take you more than five minutes;
  • very comfortable and spacious terraces where you can sunbathe while admiring the sea or the garden.




Negative reviews

In addition to the advantages, there are also some disadvantages in the operation of the Mythos Palace Resort SPA 4 * hotel. Guest reviews are especially noted for the following:

  • insufficiently clean room cleaning even with good tips;
  • very monotonous breakfasts that quickly get boring;
  • the prices in the restaurant are unreasonably high, and the quality of the dishes is very mediocre, and therefore, if you want to get acquainted with traditional Greek cuisine, it is better to visit neighboring cafes (there are cheaper and tastier);
  • very rarely change towels (and then, after a personal request);
  • the decoration of the rooms is already quite worn, and therefore requires repair and even replacement of some interior items;
  • from the windows of the apartments the sea is very poorly visible, and therefore it is better not to overpay and take rooms with garden views.




General impression

A rather positive impression is being made about the Mythos Palace Resort SPA. Reviews of tourists are generally positive in color. Claims are not very significant. Basically, guests are not satisfied with the quality of cleaning, but it depends on who is lucky with the maid. It is also worth noting that the food prices in the restaurant are too high, and the quality of the dishes is below average. But this is not critical, because near the hotel there are a huge number of catering establishments. In general, the hotel is very popular because of the quiet atmosphere and beautiful green area, similar to a real Garden of Eden. On a five-point scale, tourists rate it at four.

This hotel has an excellent location. It is such that from most bungalows located on the territory of the hotel, guests go directly to the beach. It is clean enough. It is also important that the swimming area is completely cleared of sharp traumatic stones, as well as sea urchins and stars. Near the hotel there is a bus station and a train station. This gives you a unique opportunity to independently go anywhere in the island for an independent tour. After this, be sure to visit the spa, where you can relax.
