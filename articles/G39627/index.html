Hotel Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 * (Goa, India): traveler reviews, description and photos of the hotel

Goa is famous all over the world for its snow-white beaches, exciting entertainment and sincere hospitality. On the coast you will find a large selection of hotels of different sizes and categories that are ready to shelter travelers from all over the world. One of them is the hotel Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 *? What does the hotel look like and what conditions does it offer? How do tourists respond about it?

Description of location and territory





First of all, tourists are interested in the question of exactly where the Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 * hotel is located. Candolim (Candolim) - a quiet area of ​​villas and resorts. It was here in 2011 that this hotel was built. By the way, the distance to the sea is about 900 meters. But the nearest airport is in the city of Dabolim, at a distance of 45 kilometers. Fortunately, the hotel provides its guests with a shuttle service, so there is no need to worry about moving.

The hotel is quite small, its total area is about two thousand square meters. Here, in the middle of an ever-green and blooming garden, there are small four- and three-story buildings, which, in fact, are reserved for residential rooms.

Hotel Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 * (India): description and photos of apartments





This hotel is considered relatively small, since there are only 50 rooms on its territory. Depending on your needs, you can choose from:

  • 40 Standard rooms (small but comfortable rooms with an area of ​​15 square meters);
  • 6 Deluxe rooms (rooms with an area of ​​22 square meters, which are divided into sleeping and living areas);
  • 4 Family rooms (rooms consist of two bedrooms and a living area, the total area is 30 square meters).

As you can see, the rooms in the hotel are not too big, but fully furnished and comfortable. There is a necessary set of furniture and household appliances. For example, you can watch TV and create the most comfortable temperature using a modern air conditioning system. There is also an electric kettle and a set of dishes, which allows you to make tea and coffee yourself. The minibar will be filled only at your request - these drinks are not included in the price. There is access to the Internet, which is quite convenient.

The bathroom is also equipped with modern fixtures. There is a toilet, a washbasin with a wall mirror and, of course, a spacious shower. Hygiene supplies are constantly replenished. You can also count on clean towels. By the way, room cleaning is available upon request - for this you need to leave the keys at the reception.

Meals: where to eat at the hotel?





Like most Goa hotels, Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 * provides its guests with free breakfasts, which are held in a local restaurant. In the mornings, you will have a buffet with a fairly large selection of dishes, including toast with homemade jam, pancakes, light Indian soups, cereals, exotic fruits and, of course, aromatic drinks. In the same restaurant you can have lunch and dinner, however, already at an additional cost. The menu includes dishes of international and Indian cuisine.

By the way, there are a sufficient number of cafes and other catering facilities near the hotel, so choosing a place to your liking is not difficult. And almost every beach has large bars where they offer not only drinks, but also all kinds of snacks. And dinner on the seashore and in the light of the setting sun will definitely be remembered for a long time.

Beach: location and description





As already mentioned, the distance from the Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 * hotel to the beach is about 900 m. A convenient road leads to the seashore, and during the walk you can enjoy the beauty of nature and the elegant architecture of local villas. The beach here is public, and therefore always lively and crowded. Nevertheless, for a small fee, you can rent a comfortable sunbed and relax under an umbrella.

The sea is calm, the entrance is convenient, there is also a shallow area, which is perfect for bathing children. In addition, travelers can ride boats and boats, engage in other water sports, including windsurfing.

Leisure, additional service and entertainment for guests

Hotel Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 * provides its guests with the necessary conditions for a comfortable and pleasant stay. For starters, it's worth mentioning the amenities. The hotel has ample parking, luggage storage, as well as laundry and currency exchange with a very favorable rate. Despite the lack of a medical center, you can count on the help of a doctor in case of health problems.





In the courtyard there is a large outdoor pool with a children's section. By the way, a playground is also equipped for children. For a small fee, guests can visit the Ayurveda center, spa and massage rooms. From time to time, various singing and dancing groups perform at the hotel. And between the hotel and the city constantly runs a bus.

Hotel Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 * (Goa): what do tourists say?

The opinion of people who have already managed to visit a particular hotel is always a source of valuable facts and relevant information. What do tourists say about the Ginger Tree Beach Resort 3 * hotel? Reviews are mostly positive. Yes, the territory is small, but beautiful and clean. The rooms are also kept clean, the furniture is comfortable, and the equipment is working properly. Maids are cleaned well, but it is worth considering that this service is available on request.

Breakfasts also deserve praise, as they are quite varied. And many guests stay in the restaurant for lunch and dinner, because the cook here is really delicious, and the prices are not overpriced. Despite the certain remoteness of the beach, you can get to it in a few minutes. As the travelers themselves say, the hotel is perfect for a relaxing beach holiday. And the prices here are quite reasonable, especially considering the high quality of service.
