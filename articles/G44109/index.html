Thermostat for a water heater: what is it and how does it work?

The temperature controller for the water heater is a fairly important part. It is he who is responsible for maintaining a certain temperature at a constant level. The temperature controller for the water heater is capable of slow temperature control, and it is also equipped with a device that protects it from cooling and overheating. If necessary, it turns on and off the tubular type electric heater , that is, PETN.



This device gives the user the ability to set a specific mode of operation, and also maintains the temperature level in automatic mode. If the water temperature drops, it turns on the PETN, due to which the temperature rises to a certain predetermined level.

The temperature regulator for a water heater has a certain principle of operation. It is worth saying that it is similar for all types of water heaters. On the screw that each thermostat is equipped with, a certain required water temperature is set. When the swelling valve is twisted, hot and cold water starts to flow into the mixer system, and then the adjustment process starts. The temperature of the water is measured in the chamber, after which warm water is mixed with cold water in the required proportions. When supplying water for mixing, hot has an advantage, so it will flow until its temperature is above the set value. After that, the process of mixing it with cold water will be launched. Thanks to this fact, it can be explained that the thermostat for the water heater does not react so quickly. However, the user usually does not experience any particular inconvenience.





If the temperature regulator for a water heater is mounted in a system where there is no circulation of warm water, then cold water cannot enter the incoming stream at the initial stage. In systems where there is circulation of warm water, the thermostat adjusts the required temperature in an instant.

Storage and flow units are usually equipped with different heating elements. Typically, Tenes in flow models have higher power. That is why the choice should be made only after you decide on its purpose.

An electric thermostat may have a normally closed or normally open bakelite. Often the owners of water heaters decide on self-repair. The temperature controller for the water heater "Ariston" and other companies is an electrical part, so it is worth remembering that the tank itself and the warranty are different. If you need to replace any element of the water heater or repair it, you should contact the service center or the manufacturer directly, where you can find out if it is subject to warranty repair. It is important that an appropriate temperature controller is available.



If you are interested in where you can buy this device when your failed, then it is worth noting that most of the companies involved in the production of such equipment provide delivery throughout the country. For example, a temperature regulator for a Termeks water heater you can well order at home. It is easy to replace it yourself, using simple recommendations.
