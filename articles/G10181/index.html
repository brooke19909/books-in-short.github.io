Holiday Village "Milky Way" (Novosibirsk): description, reviews

Novosibirsk is a large industrial city with a developed infrastructure. During the working year, the inhabitants of the metropolis get so tired that they want to escape from the hustle, bustle, minor problems and life troubles for at least a couple of days and find themselves in a quiet paradise. The recreation center "Milky Way" (Novosibirsk) is located in a picturesque place on the banks of the Siberian river Ob, surrounded by centuries-old pines. If you are looking for an opportunity to have a great time, enjoy the beautiful and enchanting coniferous aroma, this place is ideal for you and your family.





Description

The Milky Way is a hotel complex in Novosibirsk, which can be a great place to organize a wedding, anniversary, any celebration and your holiday. The opportunity to stay away from the hustle and bustle of the city, wander through the woods, enjoy the birds singing, you will find here.

Territory

Going on vacation, it would be nice to read reviews about the Milky Way Hotel (Novosibirsk). So, they confirm the presence of an impressive territory for walking. For a pleasant and comfortable stay on a fresh holiday, cozy gazebos with tables and barbecue facilities are provided - here you can sit in a big friendly company and cook kebabs. If desired, you can organize a picnic on the banks of the river. Vacationers love to stroll in the morning. It's no secret that air. saturated with aromas of coniferous plants, heals the body. Do not deny yourself the opportunity to be in a real health resort in Novosibirsk.





Rooms

The recreation center "Milky Way" (Novosibirsk) offers guests to stay in one of 28 comfortable rooms of various categories. At the same time, up to 70 people can live here. Most of the rooms are located in the main 4th building, the rest are scattered throughout the territory in the form of small separate houses. You are waiting for the rooms of the following category:

  • Economy - a small cozy room, designed for 2 people. If necessary, you can equip an extra bed. Combined sanitary unit with shower and a set of necessary bath accessories. The furnishings include modern furniture and satellite TV.
  • Standard - accommodation in separate houses trimmed with natural wood. This room can accommodate 2 people, an extra bed is provided for an additional fee. The interior is represented by modern comfortable furniture, a TV. In the sanitary center, guests will find underfloor heating, modern plumbing and a set of bath accessories.
  • One-room suite - a spacious room with decoration. In addition to the bed, the room has a wardrobe, a table with chairs, an armchair, a set of dishes, a kettle, a TV with 40 channels. The bathroom has underfloor heating, a set of all the necessary bath accessories.




  • Two-room suite - superior rooms, which can simultaneously accommodate up to 4 people. There is a living room with a dining area, a bedroom and a luxurious balcony overlooking local beauty. Each room has a TV with satellite channels, stylish modern furniture. The room provides for the presence of two bathrooms: in the first - a toilet with a jacuzzi, in the other there is a shower and toilet. All bathrooms have underfloor heating.
  • Apartments - a room for discerning natures, large friendly companies. The apartments are designed for 4 guests, they are waiting for two spacious bedrooms, a fireplace with a TV, daily accommodation in the room involves an hourly rental of a sauna, breakfast and lunch are included in the price.

Some vacationers complain about problems and interruptions in the supply of hot water in the rooms, a malfunction of plumbing and equipment. The recreation center "Milky Way" (Novosibirsk) is a rather young project. Nevertheless, it is quite difficult to avoid some shortcomings, breakdowns with such an influx of guests and vacationers. At the first call, employees try to eliminate all the inconvenience.
