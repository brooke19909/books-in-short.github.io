Snowy finland. Levy and all his charms

One of the most suitable countries for winter holidays is Scandinavian and frosty Finland. Levi is the most popular and well-developed ski resort, which is located 160 km north of the Arctic Circle. The local infrastructure is more like an alpine recreation area - dense buildings (mainly hotels and cottage villages), proximity to the mountains themselves, a large number of ski slopes. Spending your winter holidays here, you will definitely not get bored. In Lapland, there are entertainment programs, and cozy cafes, and, of course, schools in which you will be taught to conquer the snowy peaks.



To begin with, we note some features of this region, as well as consider its infrastructure. In this mountain range there are many hotel complexes of various categories, as well as adjacent restaurants and cafes. They should try first of all those dishes that Finland is famous for. This ski resort is far from what its analogues are in Finland. Levy is the only place where the gondola cableway is built, to climb which is a fairy tale for everyone. As mentioned above, there are schools in the region where local instructors teach children and adults how to ski and snowboard. Also, specially for skiers of various levels in the mountains, various slopes were opened. Experienced athletes in this matter most often descend on the eleventh or thirteenth. Often, professionals ride on the fifth and sixth slopes. Children and beginners are recommended to start with the first three runs, and the eighth is ideal for them.

Being in this cozy winter place, you can fully experience what real Finland is. Levy is both an entertainment center of the country and a historical one. In local markets, you can always purchase very valuable items that are not released anywhere else. Visitors are also offered wine cellars, which store real hot masterpieces made from premium grapes. Well, you can relax in a family way, have fun with friends and enjoy the holiday atmosphere in the Hulu-Poro-Areena entertainment complex.



Based on this, we can say with full confidence that the best place for a winter vacation is precisely the country of Finland, Levi. Hotels in this area are designed for people with various incomes. Therefore, their configuration begins with a three-star hotel multi-storey complexes and ends with fashionable cottage villages. Almost every hotel has its own small spa resort, pool and gym.

By the way, the most visited winter resort by children is Levi (Finland). The reviews of the kids who have been there are full of bright colors and unforgettable impressions. That is why everyone who loves winter is recommended to visit this wonderful place.
