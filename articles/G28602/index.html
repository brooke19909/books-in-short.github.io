Hotel "Central", Kirov. Reviews, prices

Booking a hotel room when planning a trip for cultural or business purposes is one of the important organizational aspects of travel. The overall impression of the city and the efficiency of work depend on how correctly the choice is made.

If the travel geography includes a visit to Kirov, then the Central Hotel would be the best choice. Kirov is a city with a rich history and cultural life. Living in a historical building, whose birth date is 1937, will allow you to feel ownership of the great historical realities of this harsh settlement.





Description

The hotel building, designed by the Russian architect Charushin, is a historical object of national importance. It is located in the historical and business part of the city. At 50 meters from the hotel there is a public transport stop, from where you can easily reach any urban area. Transfer in half an hour will bring guests from the airport "Pobedilovo", which is located 26 kilometers from the "Central". Hotel address: Russia, 610000, Kirov, st. Lenin, 80.

A river port is located a kilometer from the hotel, and a railway station is 2.5 kilometers away. On foot you can walk to the historical sites of the city: Holy Assumption Cathedral (0.6 km.), Vasnetsov Museum (0.7 km), Holy Assumption Trifonov Monastery (0.6 km).
