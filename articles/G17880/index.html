Hotel Naxos Beach 4 * (Sicily, Italy): photos and reviews of tourists

If you are tired of standard tours to Turkey or Egypt, then pay attention to the resort complexes of Italy. This ancient country will amaze you with its quality of service, magnificent nature and sunny weather. Along the Italian coast there are many diverse resort complexes. One of the most popular of them was Atahotel Naxos Beach Resort 4 * (Sicily). This hotel will amaze you with its amazing views, a beautiful park area and the cleanest sea coast.

The hotel is designed for families with children. Also, if you wish, you can bring your pets with you. This service is paid, and you must notify the hotel about this when you make a voucher. Location

Hotel Naxos Beach 4 * (Sicily) boasts an ideal location. It is located on the territory of the Italian commune of Giardini Naxos, which is one of the most popular tourist resorts not only in Sicily, but throughout Italy. The city is famous for its huge number of restaurants, ready to treat tourists with masterpieces of Italian and Sicilian cuisine. Shopping lovers will also like it here. Many well-known global brands have opened their stores in Naxos.





The hotel itself is located near the foot of Mount Etna - the highest in Europe (3300 kilometers). Within walking distance from the complex is its own sandy beach, the road to which will take about 10-15 minutes. The nearest airport is in Catania. Go to it for about 45 minutes. At 5 kilometers from the hotel is another large city in Italy - Taormina. This is a perfectly preserved medieval city, which is definitely worth a visit to all lovers of ancient history.

Number of rooms

Hotel Naxos Beach Resort 4 * (Sicily) provides its guests with comfortable living conditions. It provides for single occupancy and whole family apartments for 4 people. The rooms offer amazing views: either the villas or the sea. Some of them have their own terrace. When choosing an apartment, it is worth considering that here the floors are considered from top to bottom. That is, the 1st floor in the hotel will be the highest.





The complex has spacious rooms with modern furniture and freshly renovated. All rooms are decorated in pastel colors. Other elements of the decor also have a calm color. In addition to beds, each room has a desk, a chair, a TV stand, bedside tables and table lamps. The walls are decorated with paintings of Italian landscapes, as well as elegant wall sconces. Some apartments have their own dining tables. Customers are provided with daily room cleaning, as well as timely change of towels and sheets.
