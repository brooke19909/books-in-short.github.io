How to transfer money from a passbook to a card: we study ways

A savings book is a valuable document confirming the opening of a deposit and a bank account. It has existed for a very long time, since those times when there was no concept of a mobile bank and ATMs. Employee notes on the passbook pages served customers as a bank statement. Until now, this tool is in demand among the population, although more and more people prefer plastic cards, as it is more convenient than standing in line for a bank employee. Therefore, many customers are wondering how to transfer money from a passbook to a card. It is pretty simple.

Transfer from a savings book in a bank office

Money can be transferred from a savings book to a Sberbank card using the following tools:

  • At a branch of Sberbank with the help of an operator.
  • Through an ATM.
  • Through a personal account on the website of Sberbank.
  • Using a mobile bank.

How to make a transfer at an ATM

How to transfer money from a passbook to a card at an ATM? The question is simple. Upon receipt of a plastic card, the client binds it at an ATM to Internet banking. Thus, the card holder gains access to your personal account. If at the same time he owns a savings book, then the current account is automatically reflected in the personal account of the client.

The Shortest Way - Through Internet Banking

All accounts owned by the client are available in your personal account, the savings book in Sberbank Online is also reflected. Sberbank Online web service is located on the official website of the credit organization of the same name. To access your personal account, you must enter the login and password that the client received at the ATM when he connected to this service. In the office you can see a list of your current accounts, including a passbook and a card that you are going to replenish. Then it remains to perform the following actions:

  1. Open the menu "Payments and transfers".
  2. Select "Transfer between your accounts and cards."
  3. Fill in the details for the transfer - indicate the amount, choose a card account.
  4. Confirm action.
