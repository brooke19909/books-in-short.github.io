Mastryukovsky lakes as they are

The natural objects that are discussed in our article are the ancient rivers of the ancient Volga River. This is a real expanse for a vacation in nature. The shores of Mastryukovsky reservoirs drowned in vegetation of marvelous beauty. Here you can find extraordinary thickets of reeds, moss and sedge. Almost all lakes have many springs. They serve as the main fillers of natural water reservoirs. Mostrukovskaya water is unusually clean. And touching it, you will feel its softness and tenderness. It is as if she caresses a person, making him a wonderful healing massage. The current here is weak, and this is just what you need for a vacation with children. The fauna and flora in this area is simply amazing in its abundance. A variety of mollusks can often be observed in the coastal zone. There are all conditions for their existence: warm and clean water. Mastryukovskie lakes, the map of which attracts tourists with one of its kind, is not only a water surface, but also an uncountable number of meadows where the simplest but at the same time beautiful flowers delight the eye for the whole summer; these are forest glades with old and mighty oaks and delightful pine trees that smell all over the region ...





Local fishing

Relax on the Mastryukovskie lakes and not go fishing - it means spending time in vain. Most Russian men come here precisely for this purpose. Indeed, fish in local water bodies are not so much - many. Lake guests are crucians and pikes. Therefore, to catch them is not at all difficult. Large bream and other river inhabitants are also quite often caught on a hook. How to fish is a matter of taste for holidaymakers themselves. This can be done either from the shore or from a boat rented. In shops located on the banks, they rent out all the necessary equipment for successful fishing. Fishing and recreation on Mastryukovsky Lakes is a single, inextricable concept.

The object under consideration was a certain innovation in the organization of sports. Not so long ago, this place became a gathering of wakeboarders. This is one of the extreme sports that combines acrobatic elements, jumping and water skiing slalom. Grushinsky festival

A wonderful place - Mastryukovsky lakes. Everyone who has ever heard of the Grushinsky festival knows how to get to them. Since 1968, in the Samara region organized a festival of songwriter. V. Grushina. The guy was educated at the Kuibyshev Aviation Institute, was fond of original compositions, but in 1967 the Uda River took his life. She took her away when a student rescued drowning children. The founders of the festival were comrades Valery. The event was held with extraordinary success, and therefore it was decided to organize it annually. Each year, more than a hundred thousand people arrive at the festival.





How is the holiday

The Grushinsky festival on Mastryukovsky lakes always adheres to a single scenario. So, his main attraction is a floating stage resembling a guitar. It is located on the lake surface. All musicians must play the guitars, and behind them is the sail, which also plays the role of a screen. The audience hall is also very famous among fans: it is a mountain with a fortified slope. The main event of the festival is a guitar concert, held under the night sky, dotted with stars.
