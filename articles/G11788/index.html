How to make a beet costume

There are costumes that are popular. These include the images of Cinderella, Spider-Man, snowflakes, princesses. But when parents are instructed to dress their child in a beet suit in kindergarten or in the lower grades of school, mothers are perplexed. How to make such a non-trivial outfit? Read about this in this article.

Simple beet suit

The easiest way to collect a daughter for a matinee is to wear an elegant dress on her. But how to make a suit of beets from it? Everything is simple. Choose a dress in dark cherry or red. We put a green hat on our head. And to make it clear that your daughter depicts beets, not cherries, you can cut a couple of leaves from felt. Sew them to the hat - and the image is ready.

Costume with dress





Now we will slightly upgrade the previous image. Let's make the beetroot costume more realistic. To do this, we again need a dark cherry dress. It is desirable that it has a tulip style. From tulle we cut a strip and sew it to the hem. But the headgear will have to be made. To do this, we must sew a hat, like the Sultan. We make a thin strip that will fit to the head green, and the headdress itself will be dark cherry. From the scraps of fabric we build a flower. But we do it in an unusual way. The bud will be green, and the leaves should be dark red. We pin such a decoration to the hat, and you can also put the flower on your hand or pin it to your belt. By the way, shoes can also be decorated with this unusual accessory.

Costume for the matinee

Only mom or grandmother who are good friends with the sewing machine can sew such an outfit. The beet costume for the girl will consist of a dress, hat and bag. Let's start sewing with a skirt. It will be made in the shape of a tulip, but with one unusual detail. The front and back of the skirt will need to sew a triangular ponytail. We sew a bodice of a dress with sleeves lanterns. A joint with a skirt can not be veiled, but rather, highlight it by inserting an elastic band.





It remains to make leaves. From green velvet material we cut 6 leaves. We try to draw them at least approximately similar to those possessed by beets. Sew the leaves together. As a result, we should get 3 sheets. Two of them will adorn the shoulders. But the third one can be made decorative or sewn into it a zipper, and you get a small handbag. Using thin strips of velvet material we connect the sheets together. The beet suit for the girl is almost ready. It remains to find or sew a suitable hat of cherry color and make a tail of real leaves on it.
