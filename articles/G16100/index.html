Jack Pumpkin Workshop

The cartoon character Jack has long been a symbol of Halloween evening. His portraits are hung in the form of posters near the houses, children want to put on his costume for a party, and adults are wondering how to cut a pumpkin with its terrifying grimace.

In order for you to get the correct and beautifully carved pumpkin with Jack, it is worth remembering some of the differences when cutting. Before cutting, stock up not only with a knife, but also with a needle with a dense thread. It is better to take the largest needle that is found. If this is not in the house, then the awl will come down, but then grab a lighter as well. All this is necessary for the "special mouth", which, as we can see in the drawings, the cartoon character is sewn up. We can only cut a small narrow gap and, having outlined the same distance in a couple of centimeters, make vertical stitches with thread.
