Disco ball - accessory for a home party

A disco ball is not a household essential. But! If you are a hospitable and cheerful person who likes to arrange real parties with dances in your home, then such an accessory is what you simply need. It will help to recreate the atmosphere of a modern club or, conversely, will plunge into the atmosphere of discos of the 80-90s.

Interested in? Then consider in more detail such a gadget as a disco ball.

How does it work?

What is the first thing you see when you visit a real disco or party? Shine! Bright, colored, shimmering from one color to another, blinking. This effect can be repeated at home by purchasing a disco ball.

Despite its compactness, the ball is able to cover with light a room with a large area. Optionally, you can turn on the music on a separate audio system, and activate the ball separately. Or the gadget can be configured to play music, and to emit light.

A feature of the device is that its light, or rather the rhythm with which it blinks, responds to the beat of the music, and it does not matter what source it sounds from: from the audio system or from the ball itself.

Also, light emission can be programmed, regardless of the musical rhythm.

Destination

Still consider a disco ball a waste of money? Perhaps if noisy holidays are rare in your home. But in other situations, the gadget may come in handy. For example, it is perfect:

  1. For a home holiday. If you are not a fan of a calm solemn atmosphere, but prefer dancing and music, then the disco ball will become an unusual addition to the holiday.
  2. Bars and small entertainment venues. The owners of such institutions acquire 2-3 balls to recreate a full-fledged incendiary atmosphere. Visitors like this design, and the owners can‚Äôt afford it. It is also convenient that in such institutions gadgets can be hung from the ceiling or wall.
  3. Outdoor parties. Be it dancing in the courtyard of your own house or cottage, in nature or on the beach. The combination of music and a disco ball will decorate the atmosphere and add fun.
  4. For corporate evenings in offices.
  5. For educational institutions: schools, universities, lyceums, etc. A disco ball or even several can be purchased for assembly halls and used during dancing. Pupils and students will be delighted with this design.

What are the possibilities of a disco ball with a flash drive and with music?

So what is this compact gadget capable of?

  • Perform the functions of a stroboscope - a device that emits light pulses.
  • "Issue" various light variations in the form of color transitions, patterns, and radiation power.
  • Automatically adjusts to the bass and rhythms of the sounding music.
  • Play MP3 from a flash drive through the built-in speaker.

Equipment

Options disco ball is not limited to one. In addition to the apparatus, the following is attached to it:

  1. Instructions that will help you understand the device and the management of the gadget.
  2. Remote control for a disco ball. With it, you can switch the light and music on the gadget, being at a distance from it.
  3. Power cable.
  4. 8 GB flash drive for downloading MP3 music.
  5. Mount for ceiling or wall mounting. Convenient function for small rooms.

Product description

The ball for the disco is made of plastic. The material does not differ in increased strength, so you need to treat it carefully. The built-in speaker is not capable of boasting clear sound, especially with loud sound. Therefore, the reviews of the owners suggest that it is better to use a laptop, audio system or music center as a sound source.

The dimensions of the device are compact: 170x150 mm, so the ball will not take up much space and will fit perfectly into any room.

Power Consumption: 25 watts. Light angle: 120 ¬∞.

Acting as a strobe, a disco ball is capable of emitting several light emissions of various colors: blue, green, red, white, yellow, pink.

Conclusion

Buying a disco ball with music will not harm the family budget. His cost does not exceed 3000 rubles, but the delight from his work will be immeasurable.

So, if there is a desire to somehow brighten up a home party and cause a storm of emotions among guests, then a disco ball is what you need.
