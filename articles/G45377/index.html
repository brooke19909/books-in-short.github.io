Hotel Atlantica Miramare Beach 4 * (Limassol, Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

Atlantica Miramare is an excellent hotel belonging to the well-known hotel chain, whose complexes are located in Greece, the Republic of Cyprus and Egypt. It is very popular - people who want to relax here have to book apartments in advance.

Atlantica Miramare is located in Cyprus, in the ancient city of Limassol, which today is a famous and visited resort. It is located in the southern part of this island state, on the shores of Akrotiri Bay. The nearest airport, located in Larnaca, is about 80 kilometers away.

Service

Atlantica Miramare has everything a person on vacation might need. In addition to free Wi-Fi, 24-hour reception, laundry, dry cleaning and other services that are available in any self-respecting hotel, the hotel offers much more.

On the territory there is a medical office. You can also call a doctor in the room if there is some kind of emergency sad case.

Another hotel is adapted for people with disabilities. There are ramps, elevators for access to the upper floors, parking spaces and separate rooms that fully meet the requirements of such guests.

Atlantica Miramare also has a currency exchange, shops (souvenir and jewelry), an internet corner with computers, and car rental. If something is needed, you can contact the staff: since the hotel staff is actively studying Russian, no one should have problems.





Special services

Atlantica Miramare Beach 4 * (Limassol) has a small business center and three conference rooms. They are often rented for various events. Their organization is carried out by highly qualified hotel specialists who pay attention to every detail.

And also there are ideal conditions for a wedding. Many newlyweds decide to arrange their holiday here. And wedding organizations involved in decoration are ready to offer several options for the possible development of the celebration.

What else is there in the hotel?

Atlantica Miramare Beach has a beauty salon, a fitness center with modern fitness equipment, a jacuzzi, a sauna, a steam room, a hairdresser ... And this is not the whole list of everything that the hotel has!

There is also an indoor swimming pool, ideal for strengthening and toning muscles. In the winter months it warms up. There is also a professional and floodlit tennis court. Guests can also rent bicycles and explore local attractions with this mode of transport.

Many people like to spend time on the terrace near the outdoor pool. And some are actively involved in animation programs, which include all sorts of entertainment like water aerobics, gymnastics, competitions and tournaments.
