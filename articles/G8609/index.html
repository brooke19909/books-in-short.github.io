At the moment, the company is engaged in the production of budget speakers, systems for home theater, headphones and headsets, as well as other computer peripherals. Appearance and design

The appearance of the column can be attributed to the classic. There are no designer frills here. And it is right. For all sorts of "prettiness" and unusual forms only spoil the sound. True, this statement is true only for speakers of the same class. The casing of the columns is made of MDF coated with black or brown veneer (depending on the color scheme). Specifications

It is time for boring numbers and obscure abbreviations. No numbers anywhere. According to the passport data, these speakers have a rated output power of 40 watts. At first glance, not bad. But everyone knows perfectly well what the "western" Watts are worth. The number should be divided by two. But 20 watts is enough to sound a room of standard sizes. Computer speakers Sven SPS-702 has a two-way design. In the arsenal of each speaker is a low-frequency and high-frequency speakers. There is no special speaker for reproducing mid frequencies. The frequency range of the speakers starts at around 40 Hz and ends at 22,000 Hz. This is a standard feature for speakers of this class. The active speaker also has a headphone output. The connection between the active and passive speakers is via RCA connectors (tulips). Here, in principle, all the characteristics.

Sound features

Now let's analyze the sound quality. It should be noted right away that one should not expect divine reproduction from these columns. The class is not the same. Nevertheless, the Sven SPS-702 acoustics copes well with the mid frequencies (which is strange, given the lack of a special speaker) and reproduces the high ones quite well. But with the low, in general, trouble. The shallow ‚Äúbass‚Äù is clearly not enough to show the full depth of the bass. And if you unscrew the low-frequency control to the maximum, then the speakers begin to choke. This is either a miscalculation of constructors, or class features. In any case, when watching a movie, the speakers will not be able to provide a reliable sound of the explosion or rumble of a rocket turbine. But the speakers cope well with simple music. They especially succeed in genres in which powerful and deep bass is not required: rock, metal, country and so on. Problems with dubstep, acid, and grooving can occur. But overall, the sound is acceptable. Unassuming listeners will be satisfied.

As for watching movies, here the feelings are twofold. On the one hand, the speakers provide good quality playback of dialogs and music. There is even an illusion of volume, which is not observed in other speakers of this level. However, the sounds of explosions, the roar of engines, the rumble of turbines in an airplane speakers clearly fail. There is a problem when playing moments saturated with deep low frequencies. However, this option is much better than headphones or cheap Chinese "tweeters." The proper depth of low sounds will not be, but the quality of the rest of the voice acting will be on top. And therefore, this speaker system is quite suitable for viewing the masterpieces of world cinema. Unassuming users will be thrilled. Especially after cheap Chinese "tweeters" or the sound of laptop speakers.

Noise level

But this is one of the few drawbacks of the Sven SPS-702. User reviews are full of negativity in this regard. Indeed, in idle mode, the speakers mercilessly "fonit". Extraneous noise is also noticeable when listening to music at minimum volume. But, based on the experience of testing such speaker systems, we can safely say that this situation is typical for Sven. In speakers of this class, the simplest amplifiers are used, which create a similar background. It is also worth considering that the speakers do not have magnetic shielding. And this factor also negatively affects the noise level. Particularly advanced owners of these speakers have found a way to deal with this problem. They simply turn off the built-in amplifier and connect speakers through a separate receiver. This allows you to get rid of noise and improve the sound several times. But this option is not for everyone. However, given the price of this speaker system, a small background noise is quite acceptable for the money.

Column Setup

Fine tuning of the speakers is necessary only if the user wants to achieve the highest quality sound. It is worth noting immediately that each style of music requires its own settings. But if the user is an adherent of any one musical direction, then there will be no problems with this. So, let's begin. First of all, it is worth taking care of the correct reproduction of high frequencies. Here you can safely twist the appropriate controller to the maximum. In the standard position there is a clear lack of high. This is a feature of the Sven SPS-702. The characteristics of the tweeter are such that it cannot reliably reproduce high without a little help. Now they are medium: nothing can be done with them, since there is no corresponding regulator. However, if you listen to music through a computer, you can amplify the midrange using the software equalizer of the player. This will add a sound of credibility. Now low. You have to be careful here. The knob should be twisted to a minimum, checking the sound at each stage. If it is noticeable that the speaker begins to wheeze and choke, then it is worth lowering the level of bass. This is the only way to find the ideal value. If everything is done correctly, then the sound of these speakers will be quite acceptable.
