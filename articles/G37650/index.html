Winter and summer holidays in the Kemerovo region: from ski to catamaran

It's no secret that the nature of Kuzbass has magical powers that help get rid of many ailments and have a great rest. Alpine ridges, sparkling rivers and springs with icy, but so delicious water, and air intoxicating to stupor have a magical effect on humans. And if you add here the unimaginable beauty of landscapes, then we can say with confidence that Kuzbass is an amazing place that is worth a visit. On this territory there are many boarding houses and resorts ready to accept the tired residents of megacities in their halls.





Useful sources

Very popular is the rest in the Kemerovo region. Saturated with the aromatic smell of coniferous trees, air, mountain ranges, rocky cliffs and fantastic untouched nature help people fill their bodies with new forces. Increased immunity, treatment and prevention of skin diseases, respiratory problems and cardiovascular ailments - all this is offered to tourists by sanatoriums, health resorts and other places of recreation in the Kemerovo region.

Kuzbass is famous for its sources of mineral waters and deposits of healing mud. Some of them are located in the Kemerovo region. The largest mineral water deposit found in the river valley, called the Upper Ters. This place produces a popular and very healthy drink called Tersinka. The second field of water enriched with mineral salts is located near the village of Borisovsky. It also produces a bottled drink with the same name.





Those wishing to improve their health

It is not surprising that near healing springs there are establishments offering travelers not only high-quality, but also very useful rest in the Kemerovo region. These, without a doubt, include the Borisovsky sanatorium. Here, everyone can not only relax, but also improve their health. The best doctors of the region help fight diseases of the digestive system, skin, nervous, genitourinary, endocrine, cardiovascular and other systems. There are a great many similar establishments throughout the region. Those who wish can purchase a ticket to their favorite resort.





Mountain Shoria

Not only is the recreational service aimed at combating various diseases. There are also many opportunities for outdoor activities. In the Kemerovo region there are many different boarding houses and tourist centers offering their customers various options for summer and winter pastimes. A place like Mountain Shoria enjoys great popularity among fans of snowboards and skis. In winter, many skiing and snowboarding tracks are laid in the area under consideration. It is noteworthy that even beginners will find gentle slopes for training here. Professional instructors from a specialized school will come to their aid. More advanced skiers can hone their skills on extreme tracks. Any necessary equipment will be provided for rental.

"Three Rivers" is a recreation center in the Kemerovo region, which is located in the Gornaya Shoria district in a picturesque location near the Pyzas and Mras-Su rivers. It can accommodate up to twenty people. Tourists are expected to ride a snowmobile, skiing from the mountainsides, winter fishing, excursions. It is safe to say that in Mountain Shoria the most intense winter holiday in the Kemerovo region is offered. In summer, rafting and swimming in mountain springs are added to visiting sights and fishing.





The recreation center "New Unga"

Like blood vessels, numerous rivers pass through the territory of the region in question. Almost every body of water sheltered a boarding house or sanatorium on its banks. The recreation center "New Unga" is located in a picturesque birch grove, located by a quiet backwaters of the river Unga. This area is ecologically clean. Addictive fresh air helps relieve stress, relaxes and fills the body with new forces. Here you can have a wonderful rest with your whole family or noisy company. It is easily accessible from the regional center - the journey takes less than an hour. Fishing, ATV and snowmobiling, swimming in the river, walking in the woods, gatherings by the barbecue, celebrations and many other entertainments are offered at the Novaya Unga recreation center. At any time of the year, they are happy to provide a cozy room for travelers tired of everyday life.





On vacation in the countryside

Another place popularizing holidays in the Kemerovo region is the tourist base "Hippo". This object is surrounded on three sides by a mixed forest. From the fourth it is washed by the waters of the Tom River. The absence of any extraneous noise helps travelers to fully experience the splendor of Kuzbass nature. Badminton, fishing, tennis, bath procedures, spacious gazebos for gatherings ... Here you can have a great time not only on weekends, but also on holidays.
