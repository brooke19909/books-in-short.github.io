Refund of personal income tax for an apartment: procedure, necessary documents and calculation of the amount of tax deduction

In Russia, citizens have a variety of benefits, as well as rights and state support measures. For example, you can reimburse yourself part of the costs of some transactions and services. Today we will be interested in the return of personal income tax for an apartment. When is it possible? What does that require? And how long does it take to wait for the appropriate cash? Answers to all these questions will be presented below. With proper preliminary preparation, no difficulties will arise.

Definition

What is a personal income tax return for an apartment? Before demanding something from the state, it is important to realize what a citizen can count on. Otherwise, unjustified expectations are not ruled out.

Income tax refund is called a tax deduction. It allows you not to pay personal income tax or reimburse yourself part of the costs incurred for some services and transactions. In our case, we are talking about the acquisition of real estate, in particular, housing.

Why you can claim a tax refund

When buying an apartment, personal income tax is far from always available. Conversely, in some cases, people can count on two property deductions at once.

When purchasing housing, citizens usually face:

  • basic property deduction;
  • mortgage repayment.

Accordingly, if a person takes an apartment in a mortgage, he has the right to two refunds at once. In the case of buying a home without a loan, the applicant will be offered only the main personal income tax deduction.

Refundable amount

What kind of amount can you expect in a particular case? Unfortunately, giving a definite answer to this question is problematic.

The thing is that the size of tax type deductions is 13 percent of the amounts prescribed in the relevant agreements. But there are certain limitations. Once they are achieved, the right to a tax refund will be lost.

To date, you can return for the purchase of housing:

  • 260 000 rubles - in the form of the main property deduction;
  • 390,000 rubles - for mortgage interest.

There is nothing difficult to remember in the figures presented. As soon as the indicated amounts are returned, a person will not be able to issue a refund for the purchase of housing.

Calculation of funds is quite simple. You can fill out a deduction application only for the amount of personal income tax paid in a given tax period. As a result, by submitting documents for several years in a row, you can collect the required amount of cash.

Conditions for registration

The return of personal income tax when buying an apartment in a mortgage is not much different from the registration of the main property deduction. These two operations are subject to the same conditions for their demand in authorized bodies.

Not everyone who bought housing has the right to income tax refund for the relevant transaction. To do this, you will need to make sure that the established requirements for filing tax type deductions are met.

These include:

  • the applicant has Russian citizenship;
  • the transaction for which they want to demand money has been completed on behalf of the potential applicant and for his money;
  • the citizen has an official place of employment;
  • a person requiring income tax reimbursement has income taxed by personal income tax in the amount of 13%;
  • the applicant has a residence permit in the Russian Federation;
  • the property for which you want to return the money is executed by a potential applicant.

It follows that the unemployed, as well as people working informally or recognized as retirees, cannot apply for a tax deduction. But it is not so.

For pensioners and unemployed

An example of the return of personal income tax for an apartment will be presented later. First you need to familiarize yourself with all the features and nuances of submitting applications to authorized bodies. Otherwise, a person may simply be denied the appropriate service.

Pensioners and the unemployed can also apply for a property tax deduction for the purchase of residential real estate. They are entitled to the so-called transfer of personal income tax.

In this case, when calculating the amounts returned for a real estate transaction, taxes that were paid for the last three years will be accepted. The countdown starts from the moment the applicant appeals to the Federal Tax Service minus.

This means that the right to reimburse expenses for real estate against income tax from a pensioner or simply a non-working citizen is abolished 36 months after the official dismissal. It resumes immediately after the emergence of a new legal income taxed with 13% of personal income tax.

Entrepreneurship and returns

How to fill out 3-income tax return tax? Buying an apartment is a transaction that entails significant costs. And reimbursement of even thirteen percent of the corresponding expenses is an excellent state bonus. Can entrepreneurs use it?

Yes, but not always. Typically, IPs work with special taxation regimes. And the entrepreneur will only be entitled to personal income tax for the purchase of housing upon payment of 13 percent of income in the form of income tax. Perhaps this is only on the basis.

It follows that under special regimes, an entrepreneur will not be able to claim a tax deduction. And not only for housing. If you work on a common taxation system, there will be no refusal by the tax inspectorate.

Time to call

An application for the return of personal income tax when buying an apartment must be submitted at a certain time. Otherwise, you may be faced with a denial of the corresponding service.

The limitation period for income tax refunds at the moment is only three years. During this time, you can apply for the return of personal income tax for the purchase of housing or for other reasons.

Moreover, you can request cash immediately for all 36 previous months. This technique is often used when buying a home on a mortgage or in the case of registration of personal income tax for educational services.

Quick Guide - Go to the Federal Tax Service

How to make a deduction for housing? It all depends on the specific situation. Suppose that a person personally addresses an authorized body (MFC or Federal Tax Service) for a service.

Then he needs to perform the following actions:

  1. Prepare a specific set of documents for consideration of a deduction. Its components vary depending on the specific case.
  2. Fill out the form 3-NDFL. Refund for the purchase of an apartment without a tax return cannot be requested.
  3. Carry out the filling out of an application of a standard form for personal income tax refund.
  4. Submit an application with pre-prepared documents to the Federal Tax Service or the MFC at the place of registration of the applicant.
  5. Wait for a response from the tax authority regarding the provision of income tax reimbursement services.
  6. Wait for the transfer of money to the account indicated in the application.

It sounds like there is nothing difficult in the operation. In fact, preparing for the registration of the return of personal income tax for an apartment is a lot of trouble. This is especially true for the formation of a package of documents for the implementation of the task.

For mortgage

So, income tax refund in Russia is a responsible process that requires great attention and preliminary preparation.

If there is a family

People who have families will have to face more paperwork. They need to prepare an additional list of documents.

Typically, these include:

In addition, you can bring to the authorized bodies documents indicating the fact of the use of personal funds during the transaction. Then the right to property return will be only with the spouse whose money went to purchase a home. Otherwise, both the wife and the husband have the appropriate powers.

Through employer

As already mentioned, the return of personal income tax for an apartment can be carried out through the employer. This is not the most popular service, but it still exists.

To take advantage of such a proposal, it is recommended to adhere to the following algorithm of actions:

  1. To carry out the formation of a package of documents for registration of a tax deduction. We have already familiarized ourselves with its possible components.
  2. Fill out the application in the prescribed form.

After that, if all the documents are filled out correctly, and also provided to the employer in a complete set, you can wait for the exemption of salary from income tax.

Request Processing Time

Are you planning a return for the purchase of an apartment? 3-NDFL - that document that you must fill out yourself or contact a commercial company providing such services. Such a tax return of a standard form is submitted to the Federal Tax Service in one copy.

How fast is tax deduction? This is far from the fastest operation. It takes about 1.5-2 months to consider the application in the established form. The same amount of time will pass before the transfer of money to the beneficiary's bank account.

In the case of registration of the deduction for housing through the employer, the process will take much less time - only one month. But this technique is not too common in practice.

Can refuse

Is a return to the apartment executed? 3-NDFL completed and attached to the appropriate package of documents? Then, most likely, the request will be satisfied. But can they deny a tax deduction?

Yes, but only if there are good reasons. Such a decision must be justified in writing by the tax authorities.

They can refuse a property deduction if:

  • an incomplete or invalid package of documents has been submitted;
  • there is no tax return or it is framed with errors;
  • the transaction is made on behalf of another person;
  • the statute of limitations has passed;
  • a citizen does not meet the established criteria under which the right to a deduction appears;
  • the established limits on refunds for property have been exhausted.

What to do if a person is denied a tax deduction? It all depends on the specific life situation. Often, within a month, you can correct the cause of the refusal and re-apply to the Federal Tax Service minus. In this case, you will not have to apply again for personal income tax.
