Mini-hotel "Albatross" (Adler): description of rooms, reviews

Adler is the southernmost resort in Russia, the younger brother of Sochi and a good neighbor of Krasnaya Polyana. The city is located on the Black Sea coast and on both banks of Mzymta, a lush mountain river. The resort subordinate to Sochi is divided into 15 settlements and micro-districts, many of which are distinguished by their special flavor. Two rivers form the peculiar Adler border: Kudepstoy resort is separated from Sochi, and separated from Abkhazia by the Psou river.

In Soviet times, sanatoriums and resorts actively developed on this territory, based on the use of the beneficial properties of local silt mud enriched with hydrogen sulfide. People came here to be treated for digestive, nervous and female diseases, cardiovascular diseases, problems in the musculoskeletal system, etc.





The new impetus for the development of the city was the Winter Olympics in 2014. Thanks to the Games, not only upscale sports facilities appeared in the city, but the infrastructure was thoroughly updated, new hotels and hotels, shopping and entertainment centers, cafes and restaurants were opened. To accommodate tourists there are a wide variety of options. One of the most popular guest accommodations in Adler is the Albatross Mini-Hotel.
