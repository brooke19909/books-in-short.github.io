Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The new and promising complex Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 * (Turkey) has recently been assigned the classification of the Quartet. This is a very interesting hotel, designed for tourists who came to sunbathe on the beach and explore local attractions. It provides a lot of excellent services, the ideal cleanliness and friendly staff, and the trip will not make you lay out a fortune for the rest. A team of professionals provides first-class services. At least that's what tourists say in their reviews.



Where is this hotel located?

Hotel Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 * is located very close to the beach of the Anatolian Riviera. Depending on which building tourists settle in to get to the sea, they need to overcome four hundred or five hundred meters. The nearest settlement to the hotel is the amazing resort of Side. Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 * (S. Demirel Boulevard) is located in its area called Kumkoy. The center of this resort is just a stone's throw away. And to the old part of the famous Side - a little further, four kilometers at least. If you walk tiring, you will need to use transport. To Manavgat - seven kilometers. Approximately the same distance from the hotel to Antalya and its international airport is 65-70 km.



How to get to Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 * (Turkey, Side)

Many guests choose this hotel for independent travel, especially after travel agencies have stopped their work recently. Therefore, it is important to know how to arrive at the hotel. To get here, you need to fly to Antalya (or at least to Istanbul, and then by bus). A shuttle service to the hotel can be ordered from the international airport. If not, then many minibuses go from Antalya bus station. True, you need to do a transplant. Most public transport goes to Manavgat. And almost all intercity buses to Alanya pass through Side. Then you need to transfer to a minibus. Dolmushi also travels from Side to the hotel. Their stop is opposite the hotel.



Side Resort

It is also called an open-air museum. If you settled in the Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 * (Side), it means that at your service is one of the most popular archaeological sites in Turkey, where there are antique ruins at every turn. And to the west and east of the old center are excellent beaches. One of them, Kumkoy, where, in fact, the hotel is located, has recently gained a very high rating among travelers. There is clear sea, soft sand, few sharp stones and a good entrance for children. Especially the gentle beach is famous for the western beach. And oriental swimmers and divers love it. The archaeological complex of the old city will present you with an ancient street with columns, and agora, and temples. Only the amphitheater and museum of antiquities, which are located directly in the Roman baths, have a paid entrance. Those who want to party in the evenings will like Side discos. For example, "Oxide", where you can dance and swim in the pools.

Infrastructure

The hotel Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 * has several buildings - one main and four additional buildings. They are of different storeys and surrounded by a garden. This hotel is brand new, it was built in 2012, and last year many rooms were completely renovated. The main building has an elevator. The hotel has a seminar room. On the territory of a shopping mall: a lot of shops with souvenirs and products, many pharmacies. There is parking for tourists cars. In general, the hotel is very cozy and comfortable. The territory is small - only five square kilometers in area, but is kept clean and in good order.





Reviews about the conditions of residence

The hotel Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 *, reviews of which we are considering, more than one hundred and seventy rooms. Standard rooms are located in the main building. Larger rooms and family rooms are mainly located in annexes. As the guests say, the rooms are bright, air-conditioned, with a plasma TV, minibar. The TV screen is huge, there are five or six Russian channels. Huge balconies with french windows. Two rooms are designed for people with mobility problems. Reception of tourists works around the clock. They immediately lodge, put on bracelets and help carry luggage to the room. According to tourists, living in a room is comfortable. They clean every day and very often change towels and bed linen. Everything is fresh, clean, without spots. Use of the safe in the room is paid. Furniture in pastel colors, all new, with wardrobe. Plumbing - excellent, works without any glitches. In the minibar (part-time fridge) every day there is a bottle of drinking water. Toiletries are placed once upon arrival. The best numbers are in additional buildings, especially in the fourth, fifth and sixth.

Now a little about what is the tourist service in the Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 *. Guest reviews tell us that you can rent a car here. A hairdresser and laundry are open. If necessary, you can send a fax, make copies of documents, use a computer to access the network. In addition, wireless internet is available on site, but it is best caught in the lobby. Hams with a program to cleanse the face and body are very popular with visitors. In general, the service was awarded excellent marks.

What to see

From the Merve Sun Hotel Spa 4 * hotel to the main attractions of Side - the ancient ruins - within walking distance. The journey will take you from half an hour to forty minutes, depending on your strength and energy. If you are reluctant to stomp your feet, take the minibus (dolmush). In Side, you will be amazed by the vast territory with many ancient ruins. In almost all places you can walk for free. It is very beautiful there, especially in the evening, with backlight.
