But the other part of vacationers can not imagine a vacation without the opportunity to plunge into the pool, even if there is a sea nearby. This is explained by several reasons: it is much harder to go to the beach in the heat, and the water in the sea is sometimes dirty, but in the pools it is always clean. The best hotels with a pool in Crimea

Going to visit the peninsula, each vacationer first of all looks through all the possible options for their accommodation. Based on prices, the proposed service and the location of hotels, tourists select for themselves the most preferred options and go on vacation.

Five Star Pearl of Yalta

Only in August 2014 the magnificent five-star complex Mriya Resort & SPA was opened, but it has already managed to fall in love with the guests of the peninsula and even become a laureate of several awards. This hotel is located in Yalta, in the village of Opolznevoy, right on the Black Sea. Everything is amazing here: the unique main building, whose bird's-eye view resembles a lotus flower, and exquisite rooms that meet the needs of the most demanding guests, and the list of services available at this hotel.





This hotel complex has everything to make the rest of every guest of Crimea unforgettable. Three different restaurants are responsible for food at the hotel, where you can find Asian, Mediterranean cuisine and a rich buffet. Sports fans can use two swimming pools, tennis courts, billiards and a fitness club. Own ballroom, a spa with a wide selection of services, a nightclub, conference rooms and cinema halls - all this magnificence is able to satisfy the needs of every vacationer. For younger guests of the hotel there is a special entertainment center, so that they can definitely forget about boredom here.

The spirit of the Mediterranean in Crimea

Crimea hotels with a swimming pool boast not only good infrastructure, but also an original design. For example, among the ordinary houses of the Black Sea Sudak, there is an amazing place that will transfer each guest to the distant and ancient town of Italy. This place is the Soldaya Grand Hotel - a hotel built here in 2014 near the Genoese fortress.





This hotel is made in the form of a whole Italian street, along which many will want not only to walk, but also to live on it. The design of the hotel impresses with its luxury: it seems that the guests are in a medieval castle, but equipped in a very modern way. There is a conference room, an excellent restaurant and grill bar, indoor and outdoor pools, a bath complex, a spa and a club for children with a play area. The city's promenade is just a stone's throw away, so each hotel guest can quickly be near the sea.

Living on an Italian street

Hotel Soldaya Grand Hotel itself is small: it has only 76 rooms. However, once in one of them, tourists probably will not want to leave it. The rooms were decorated with medieval, Mediterranean and even oriental styles. The windows offer magnificent views of the Black Sea, the fortress, as well as the territory of the hotel itself.





The hotel offers rooms of different categories: from one-room rooms of the categories Standard, Comfort and Junior Suite to two-room family junior suites and two-story duplexes. There are also special rooms: romantic suites, one of which is equipped with two terraces, and the other is in the tower and consists of three levels, and two-room royal suites that amaze the imagination with their decor and sophisticated design.

Crimean Riviera Gold

Finding Crimea hotels with a swimming pool is easy enough. Among them there are great options that offer not only a comfortable stay, but also an all-inclusive food system. It is this service that attracts many tourists to hotels in Turkey and Egypt, and it is so great that now you do not need to go very far behind it.





Just visit the wonderful Crimean city of Alushta, in the center of which is the three-star Golden Resort Hotel. In addition to a rich and varied all-day buffet, a pizzeria and a bar with a variety of drinks, guests can enjoy a variety of spa programs or health courses, swimming pools, a gym, a sauna and thermal baths. A kids room and magnificent playgrounds with attractions are always open for kids, there is even animation for all generations of holidaymakers. Hotel guests are also offered walking tours of the city, car parking, concierge service and a shuttle service to the equipped beach are available.

Rooms at Golden Resort

Everyone can stay at this wonderful hotel in Alushta. It is perfect for both individual relaxation, and for a romantic vacation or family trip. At Golden Resort, there are as many as 7 different room types. There are single and double standard rooms equipped with two single beds, comfort rooms with two 1.5-double beds, family rooms with three single beds, spacious junior suites and two-room suites with wide and comfortable double beds.

Most rooms have balconies from which you can enjoy wonderful views of the coast and the city. Also in all rooms you will find everything you need for a first-class holiday.





Great stay in Koktebel

Not far from Feodosia, in a wonderful place called Koktebel, you can also find excellent Crimea hotels with a pool. And not even with one pool, but with three! Guests can also choose all-inclusive meals, take advantage of the spa and all kinds of activities for children and adults. We are talking about the Lexx Club Hotel, which offers an excellent holiday to all those who decide to enjoy the beauties of these places.

The hotel has an active leisure club, thanks to which anyone can please themselves with quite interesting and unusual activities. Guests can enjoy diving, fishing, quad biking, aeronautics, horseback riding and all kinds of boat trips.





Lexx Club offers spacious deluxe rooms with balconies, as well as magnificent cottages with terraces. Rest in such a place will surely be remembered by many for a long time.

The variety of places in Crimea where you can relax, makes this peninsula a real treasure for many tourists. Excellent hotels with swimming pools and clean beaches, which also provide a host of additional services, can easily compete with well-known foreign resorts. That is why every year the number of holidaymakers in these places increases markedly.
