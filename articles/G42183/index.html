Number of rooms

It's time to talk about the options for living, which are offered at the recreation center "Sun" to everyone:

  • One-storey cottages. There are two rooms here. One is for resting and sleeping. In the second one you can play board games, read books, receive guests, watch TV. There is everything you need to ensure that your vacation is not overshadowed by any domestic problems. Comfortable, single beds, bedside tables, wardrobe, mirror, shower, toilet, air conditioning. All rooms are bright and clean. This option is liked by newlyweds or couples with children.
  • Wooden houses with all amenities. It also has everything you need for relaxation and living. On TV, you can watch a large number of sports and entertainment channels.
  • There are also houses with partial amenities. What does this mean? You can choose this option, if you are not annoyed to take a shower in the common base. There are only toilets in the houses.
  • Two-storey cottage. It can accommodate about forty guests. The cottage has double and triple rooms. Guests usually leave only the most positive reviews when choosing this option.
  • Cottages with verandas. It has all the necessary furniture: beds, wardrobe, chairs, bedside tables. There is also a toilet with shower. If you like to take morning coffee or tea in the fresh air, then you can go to the veranda. You can also read a book or look through a magazine here.

Holiday Village "Sun": prices

Let's see what amount you need to have in order to stay here. Depending on the level of comfort, rooms cost from 400 to 1200 rubles per day. Higher prices for accommodation in the cottage. Meals must be paid separately.

Crimea (Kacha), recreation center "Sun": reviews

How can I get there?

It is very interesting to know how you can get to the recreation center "Sun", as well as its exact location. To get here you need to come by train (or fly by plane) to the city of Sevastopol. Then you can take a bus or route to a small village, which has an interesting name - Kacha. Travel time will be no more than thirty minutes. In the village you will need to find Udarnaya street, 52. Local residents know very well where the recreation center is, so it will not be difficult to find it.
