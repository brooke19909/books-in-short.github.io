So, the subject of today's review is the Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge: specifications, reviews, and the advantages of a smartphone along with its shortcomings.

Appearance

If you put the S6 model in one palm and our respondent in the other, then you will hardly see any difference between the last flagship and the brand new Samsung Galaxy S7. Owner reviews played a significant role in this. Users appreciated the successful design and ergonomics of the predecessor, so the brand listened to the opinions of its fans and practically did not change the appearance of the gadget.





The new flagship still has glass front and back panels, and a nice metal frame runs along the entire perimeter. The most distinctive innovation is the beveled edges on the rear panel - the smartphone has now become a little more ergonomic, and just holding the gadget in your hand is noticeably more pleasant.

Design features

The rear camera eye sticks out a little less, and on the right you can see the familiar LED flash coupled with a heart rate sensor (detects blood oxygen saturation). At the top of the front of the device is a conversational speaker, just to the right of the front camera eye, as well as light and proximity indicators. In the lower part you can see the already familiar functional Home button, which is also responsible for the fingerprint scanner.

On the right side is the power off button, and on the left - buttons for adjusting the sound volume, and without any rockers, which can be called a hallmark of the Samsung Galaxy S7. Screen

At Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge, the screen's characteristics are quite impressive: an AMOLED display with a luxurious sweep of 2560 √ó 1440 pixels of the QHD standard, as well as a high pixel density of 577 ppi. Trying to make out individual points is simply useless - the picture is as realistic as possible for a smartphone. However, the Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge display features burdened with its fly in the ointment. Performance

The company has launched several variable models with different processors. We will not consider the version with the Qualcomm chipset, because in Russia it is less popular, unlike the Samsung Exynos 8890 Octa proprietary suite. The chipset runs on 64 bits with ARM big.LITTLE architecture . Four cores from Mongoose and as many from Cortex are responsible for speed. The first set operates at frequencies of 2.4 GHz, the second - 1.3 GHz. For the graphic component, a rather nimble chipset from "Mali" from the T-880 series is responsible.





On board there are 4 gigabytes of RAM, as well as 64 GB of internal (optionally 32 GB). Moreover, the strips are very nimble, because they are made using UHS 2.0 technology, and today it is the fastest standard that can be accessed by the ordinary user of the Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge. The model description tells in some detail where, and specifically why, every kilobyte of memory is used. But in any case, if you are not satisfied with the built-in volume, you can always expand the space for your needs with a third-party SD card (up to 200 GB). In addition, due to the OTG interface, it is possible to connect a third-party external drive to the gadget, and this is already terabytes of data.
