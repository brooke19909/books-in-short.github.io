Kul Sharif Mosque

Unfortunately, the Algorithm hotel administration does not sell excursions. However, its guests can easily explore significant places of Kazan independently. Hotel guests should take this opportunity to visit the Kul Sharif Mosque. This is the main religious building of the Republic of Tatarstan. It was built on the site of the legendary mosque of the Kazan Khanate, destroyed in 1552 by the troops of Ivan the Terrible. This building is named after one of the defenders of the city at that time - Imam Kul Sharif.
