Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * ("Sorra De Or Beach Club") - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Spain has long been one of the places where tourists from all over the world gather to relax and gain strength to continue their work. Catalonia is considered a real gem among all the resorts of this country. It is here that the best beaches are collected, and it is here that there is an amazing town called Malgrat de Mar. In order to relax in this resort place, you need to choose a good hotel for yourself. In this regard, a three-star hotel Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * will be an excellent and inexpensive option



Building's facade

The hotel was built in the last century, but is still one of the most popular in the whole city. At first glance at it, you can understand that the style in which it is built is no different from other buildings in this place. All forms of the building are slightly rounded, and bricks, granite and marble were used in the construction and facade cladding. It is impossible to say that the view of the Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * Malgrat-de-Mar hotel does not attract attention. Looking at her, you can feel a surge of desire to go inside. Despite the fact that there are 270 rooms here, the hotel attracts with its cosiness. Perhaps the whole point is that combinations of white and brown, which create the effect of warmth and homeliness, were used as primary colors.

Hotel location

Malgrat de Mar is located in a Spanish province called Catalonia. By itself, this town is not big, and therefore the presence of such a huge hotel by local standards as Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * automatically makes it almost the main attraction. In order to get to the city center of Malgrat de Mar from the hotel, you must overcome the path of only 400 meters. In addition, in the immediate vicinity of it is a railway station. As for the beach, it is 25 meters from the entrance to the hotel. After checking into the room, you can go to the balcony of your room and enjoy the stunning views of the sea. Near the hotel you can find all kinds of restaurants, taverns, bars, shops and shops where you can taste delicious Spanish cuisine and purchase interesting souvenirs.



How to get to the hotel

If the question arose about how to get to Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * (Costa Brava), then this indicates that he has already interested you. The hotel is located in the small town of Malgrat de Mar, which does not have its own airport. Therefore, in order to get to the hotel, you must first overcome the path from Moscow or any other city to Barcelona. From Moscow there are regular flights to the capital of Catalonia. The flight will take about 4 hours, after which it will land in Barcelona's air harbor. There are several ways to get to the city of Malgrat de Mar. So, you can go to the train station and buy a ticket to the resort city. The second way is to go to the Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * hotel directly from the airport by taxi. This option will be the most expensive. The third way is to go to Malgrat de Mar by bus. In addition, the Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * hotel (Spain) can provide a transfer service directly from Barcelona Airport. This service is paid, and it should be ordered in advance by writing a letter with the corresponding request from the hotel administration.



Services available at the hotel

The range of services at Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * can be called standard. Almost all of them are characteristic of any other hotel. As the main services that you can get by checking into this hotel, you can highlight the provision of a comfortable room with air conditioning and a minibar. You can also find a safe in the room, but you can use it only for a fee. There is a TV in every room of the hotel, where you can watch cable TV. For the convenience of guests in each room there is a telephone. The only minus in this hotel, some guests considered the lack of free wireless Internet. While in most modern hotels this option is taken for granted. But at the Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * hotel it is absent, which must be borne in mind when checking in.



Rest and leisure

If you want to spend time actively in the company of other tourists, then you can play beach volleyball on the beach. Hotel guests can do this for free. If volleyball is not to your liking, then you can always go to the billiard room and compete with other vacationers in this game. In addition, as another option for leisure activities, a video game is offered. In addition, in the territory of Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * Malgrat-de-Mar, reviews confirm this fact, there is a large pool. If you want to relax near the pool, then you will be provided with umbrellas and sun loungers so that this process is as convenient as possible. By the way, umbrellas and sun loungers can be used not only by the pool, but also on the beach. Hotel Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * owns a stretch of beach on the Mediterranean coast, so you can relax here in complete peace of mind.



Dining at the hotel

The hotel has a large restaurant serving breakfast and dinner. As a rule, in the morning you can eat refreshments in the form of sandwiches or sandwiches along with freshly squeezed juices or tea. Dinner in terms of dishes is more varied. So, you will be offered to taste dishes of both national and international cuisine: hot, salads, snacks and dessert. Also, if desired, guests can not go down to the restaurant for dinner, but order food in the room. If for any reason you do not want to eat in the hotel restaurant, you can always go to one of the catering establishments located in the vicinity of the hotel. Almost every one of them is excellently prepared, and you can try a variety of dishes of traditional Spanish cuisine.

Hotel rooms

In terms of size, Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 *, reviews of which can be found in the last part of our article, is quite large. And if you look at it in the context of the city, then it seems completely huge. The hotel has a total of 270 rooms. All of them are divided into three main categories: standard apartments, as well as deluxe and deluxe rooms. The standard rooms in the hotel are most. They differ from the other two categories in their smaller size, as well as simpler furniture. But even so, all rooms look very neat and comfortable. Each room has its own refrigerator, cable TV and shower room, in which, in turn, you can find a bath towel and bathrobe. All hotel rooms are cleaned daily at the moment when the guests went to the pool, sea or any other place.

Prices for accommodation in this hotel depend on how many people are going to check in, as well as on which category of room you prefer. If the room is standard for two people, then the cost of one night in it will be 65 US dollars. The price of a deluxe apartment for two will be already 98 US dollars. A suite will cost guests $ 140. What interesting places are nearby

Costa Brava is one of the most interesting places to travel, not only for a beach holiday, but also for excursions. On the beach there are a large number of centers that provide equipment for scuba diving. In addition, tourists can visit the water park, which is located a couple of kilometers from the hotel. History and culture buffs will also find things to do. So, on the territory of the Costa Brava there are a large number of historical attractions (the lighthouse of San Sebastian, the old quarter in Tossa de Mar and much more). In order to fully immerse yourself in the nature that dominates the Costa Brava, you can go to the botanical gardens of Santa Clotilde, Pinha de Rosa and Marimurtra. You can go on excursions both independently and as part of an organized group. By the way, you can also book an excursion to a particular place in the hotel itself. Here, reception administrators will be happy to advise you on car or bicycle rentals, and also recommend how to make a route for this or that trip. Most importantly, remember that Spain is an interesting country, rich in many amazing places. Therefore, do not spend your entire vacation at the hotel and on the beach, but be sure to travel in any way possible!

But the vast majority of tourists did not see a significant drawback in this.

In general, according to travelers from Russia and other countries of the former Soviet Union, the hotel is a good place to relax as close to the sea as possible. The only small drawback when checking into this hotel may be the lack of Internet, but this flaw is more than covered by many advantages that Sorra D'or Beach Club 3 * undoubtedly possesses. Among them are good service, and excellent living conditions, and excellent cuisine in the restaurant. Moreover, almost all guests say they are ready to return here again, and plan to recommend this hotel to all their friends and acquaintances.
