A great option for a summer vacation is the city of Izberbash. Holidays at sea with decent conditions of comfort at the lowest prices - this is how you can briefly describe this resort.

A young city with a very rich history

In the 30s of the last century, on the territory of the Republic of Dagestan, geological expeditions searched for the places most suitable for industrial oil production. In 1936, a tent camp was set up on the site of the modern city of Izberbash. It was here that the legendary oil well No. 8 was drilled, with which the history of the production of liquid "gold" in Dagestan begins. The city of Izberbash itself appeared on maps only in 1950, as a large industrial center. Where did such an unusual name of the settlement come from? According to one of the popular versions, the whole point is that once the local railway was built by the Germans and they designated their location in their native language as das ist Berg (‚Äúthis is a mountain‚Äù - a literal translation). It is believed that it was for this reason that the local mountain was named Izberg-Tau, and the city that grew in the immediate vicinity was Izberbash.





The industrial center is turning into a tourist center!

The times when Izberbash (Dagestan) was famous as a major center of the oil industry are a thing of the past. Today, the largest and most significant enterprises in the region are considered to be: a wine and brandy factory, a confectionery factory and a factory that produces drinking drinks. At the same time, the city is located in an area with favorable environmental conditions and picturesque nature. Tourism in this region is just beginning to develop, but despite this fact, today you can relax here comfortably. A sufficient number of hotels and recreation centers, a favorable local climate, gentle sun - all this is offered to its guests Izberbash. Holidays at sea in this city will give you a lot of positive emotions and will be remembered for a long time. Sandy beaches and a gently sloping seabed make local resorts a great place for families, because even children who are just starting to swim can swim here.





Izberbash: rest at sea with comfort

The sea in the vicinity of the town with a very unusual name is gentle and calm, severe storms are rare here. The beaches are well-maintained, they are clean enough, you can always find a free sunbed and an umbrella from the sun. Tourists are offered here traditional entertainments - cafes and fast-food points on the coast, mini-water parks, boat trips are organized. Many local hotels and recreation centers have their own private beaches, on the territory of which you can safely swim and sunbathe in the company of a few vacationers of the selected complex.

Accommodation options

Having chosen the city of Izberbash as a vacation destination, tourists can stay in one of the local hotels or rent a room / lodge at a recreation center. Before booking accommodation, try to determine your requirements for comfort and the location of temporary housing. It is on the combination of these two parameters that the room price depends. If you are planning your vacation in Izberbash for the first time, it will be useful to know that 3-4 * hotels are most often found here. On average, the cost of a room in them starts from 1500 rubles / day. Many local hotels are family oriented, which means that finding a room with children's furniture is no problem.





Since tourism in the region is just beginning to develop, almost all hotels and recreation centers have been built recently. This means that even for the lowest cost, a recently renovated room with modern furniture awaits you. An alternative accommodation option at the resort is the rental of guest houses and rooms with local residents.

Entertainment for tourists

What to do during the holidays? There are no ancient sights here, since not even 100 years have passed since the city of Izberbash appeared on the map. Holidays at sea without excursions may seem boring, but you can always go with an organized group to neighboring settlements and visit the most interesting and famous sights of Dagestan.





In Izberbash itself, two things are worth doing: enjoy local dishes and admire Mount Izberberg-Tau, which even locals most often call simply: Pushkin-Tau. Tourists are also invited to participate in sea fishing, scuba dive to the seabed and engage in other extreme sports.
