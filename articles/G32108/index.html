"Radisson", Moscow: description, services and reviews

Almost every day new people come to Moscow. Someone comes to the capital of Russia on a business visit, someone visits friends or relatives, and numerous tourists come here to explore this unusually beautiful city. Indeed, in Moscow there are many interesting places that are definitely worth a visit and see with your own eyes.

The capital of the Russian Federation offers its guests not only unique sights and fascinating excursions, but also a variety of hotels where you can stay both for a short time and for a long time. Tourists who prefer world-class upscale hotels should definitely pay attention to the hotels of the famous Radisson chain. Moscow and its region can offer as many as four such hotels, which we will discuss in detail below.

What Radisson hotels are in Moscow?

In the capital of the Russian Federation are three comfortable hotels belonging to the international hotel chain "Radisson". This is one five-star hotel "Radisson Royal" and two four-star hotels "Radisson Slavyanskaya Hotel and Business Center" and "Radisson Blu Belorusskaya". Another hotel of this chain is located in the suburbs of the capital, right on the territory of Sheremetyevo Airport. It is called "Radisson Blu Sheremetyevo Airport Moscow".





Those who dream not to relax, but to work in one of the world hotel chains, will surely have no trouble finding the right vacancies in these hotels. Radisson (Moscow), like any other employer, is always interested in qualified personnel.

Best Five Star Hotel

In the capital of our large state there is a sufficient number of hotels for every taste and affluence, including five-star ones. However, if guests of the capital are looking for the best hotel in the city, then this will certainly be the Radisson Hotel. Moscow with great pleasure invites all visitors to the fashionable Radisson Royal Hotel, which is located in the building of the former Ukraina Hotel. This famous skyscraper, which was built back in the 50s of the 20th century, today offers luxurious rooms and apartments, six restaurants and three bars in which anyone can find their favorite dish. Guests can have a great time in the huge Wellness Club, where there is a 50-meter-long swimming pool, a gym with professional equipment and spacious rooms, various spa and beauty salons, massage rooms, a real Russian bath, a hammam and a sauna. There is also a very convenient express laundry, which allows you to clean clothes in just 3 hours. Fans of shopping will find on the ground floor a whole gallery of shops, where you can walk and find new things.

The address at which the Radisson Royal Hotel (Moscow) is located is likely to be given to many residents of the capital, especially if you remind them of the Ukraine Hotel. You should look for this hotel on Kutuzovsky Prospekt, Building 2/1, Building 1. Next to the hotel is the Moskva River, where there is a whole park of yachts for river walks owned by the hotel.





Enthusiastic reviews from guests of the Radisson Royal confirm that this hotel is considered the best in the city. However, nothing is ideal in our world, and some guests note that the staff here sometimes behaves unfriendly and even rude.

Large selection of rooms at the Radisson Royal

The variety of room categories available at this 5-star Radisson Hotel (Moscow) is breathtaking. There are classic, superior, deluxe rooms and as many as six types of suites, including the presidential and ambassadorial ones. All are equipped with comfortable furniture, heated floors, large bathrooms and safes. There are also spacious apartments from which you can enjoy panoramic views of Moscow, as well as enjoy interesting designs and layouts.





Along the Moscow River with the Radisson

Not far from the Radisson Royal there is a whole parking of yachts owned by the hotel. Both residents and guests of the capital can enjoy exciting river walks and excursions, as well as taste amazing dishes from the local chef during the cruise. Also on such a yacht you can organize some kind of holiday event or even a business meeting. After all, there is nothing impossible for the Radisson. By the way, these yachts are capable of sailing along the Moscow River all year round, so now you can arrange a walk on a water bus even in winter.
