What is the weather like in Egypt in February?

The end of winter is the most suitable time for tourists who need to not only sunbathe on the beaches of the Red Sea, but also to visit the sights of the country of the pyramids. Although the weather in Egypt in February is considered the coolest of the year, nevertheless, during this period you can swim and sunbathe. Events in Egypt

Despite the cold weather, in Egypt in late February there is a celebration of the sun in the rocks of Abu Simbel. Inhabitants of this region arrange stormy dances and chants. The best way to get to this place, as tourists suggest, is water transport - a boat. In addition, February 15 in Egypt is a national holiday - the Day of the Prophet.

Of course, tourists can celebrate on the Red Sea coast not only local holidays, but also Valentine's Day - February 14, as well as Defenders of the Fatherland Day - on the 23rd. If the vacation is extended to March, you can also mark International Women's Day (March 8).

In addition, this period is perfect for sightseeing and educational trips. Indeed, in winter, unlike summer, there is no intense heat and sun. Next, consider what you can see at the resorts of Sharm El Sheikh and Hurghada this month.

Hurghada in February

Due to the political situation, the demand for tours to Egypt has significantly decreased - more than 6 times. However, there are still enough people wishing to visit the country. Among Russian tourists, Hurghada is one of the most popular places for families. The holiday season here lasts all year, and the cost of the permit is quite affordable.

In addition to swimming on a sandy beach in Hurghada are the main tourist sites of Egypt - the Valley of the Kings with the tombs of the pharaohs and the temple in the city of Edfu.

Places of rest in the resort of Sharm El Sheikh

The cost of the February holiday in Sharm

So, if tourists decided in February to go to Sharm El Sheikh, the cost of the trip will be lower than in the summer, vacation period. This is because in winter the flow of tourists is significantly reduced, and the demand for resorts is small. Service still remains at the proper level. Since hotels in Sharm are oriented towards European standards of service, here the quality and comfort of visitors are always an important aspect of their service.

In relatively cold weather in Egypt in February, the cost of a ticket will be reduced by more than half (50%). For example, for a weekly vacation for two people the cost of a three-star hotel will be from $ 600 to $ 800, and for a five-star hotel - from $ 950 to $ 1,350.

The February holiday in Sharm is perfect for a family holiday - the air is less stuffy than in the summer heat. In addition, various excursions in winter will also be discounted.

Conclusion

If you consider the most suitable climate option for February in Egypt - this is Sharm El Sheikh. Here, tourists are offered a choice of hundreds of hotels with excellent quality of service. Accommodation in this period will be much cheaper than in the summer season. Regardless, when traveling to Sharm El Sheikh, in the winter or in the summer, this is a wonderful resort with coral reefs, a trip to which will leave a lot of pleasant impressions.

If the trip is more tourist, then you can go to Hurghada - visit the ancient cities and temples. In addition, there are sandy beaches in which there are no corals, which is both a disadvantage and an advantage, since the sandy beach in Hurghada is smooth and comfortable for swimming.
