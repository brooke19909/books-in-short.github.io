Petrozavodsk - a city of 5 seas

Petrozavodsk is the largest city in the Republic of Karelia, it is considered its capital. The city is located in the northwestern part of Russia on the shores of Lake Onega. Now Petrozavodsk is developing and gaining popularity among tourists. The city is located in close proximity to Finland and St. Petersburg.

The center with small streets and ancient buildings is of interest to visitors. In Petrozavodsk for art lovers there are many theaters, clubs and exhibition halls. In the fresh air there are also places where you can relax. These are parks with picturesque rivers and lakes.

The architecture is interesting for its outlines; there are several monuments. On Lenin Square, the Eternal Flame burns.
