Hotel Calypso Hotel Siviri 2 * (Greece, Kassandra Peninsula): description, services, reviews

When you want to relax on the coast of the azure sea and at the same time plunge into history, the first thing we remember about Greece. At the same time, there is a large selection of resorts. So, you can go to any of the islands of the Greek archipelago, you can visit one of the coastal cities of mainland Greece, or you can buy a tour to a hotel, for example Calypso Hotel Siviri, which is located on the Halkidiki Peninsula - in one of the most heavenly places on the planet.

In the northern Aegean, the land cuts into the azure expanses in a very interesting way. If you look at it from above, you can see that this peninsula has the shape of a trident, exactly the same as that of the lord of the seas, god Poseidon. In a word, Halkidiki consists of three long and narrow peninsulas with abundant vegetation: Sithonia, Agion Oros and Kassandra. I would like to tell in more detail about the last of them.

Kassandra Peninsula: holiday features

This piece of sushi not only has a romantic name, but is truly a paradise. It is located in the west and is the most densely populated of all three "fingers" of Halkidiki. The tourist infrastructure is well developed here: traditional Greek taverns, clubs, bars, discos, souvenir stalls, boutiques, etc. There are many hotels of all categories: from chic five-star hotels, which, as a rule, belong to world hotel chains, to the most democratic dvushka ‚Äù, for example Calypso Hotel Siviri 2 * Standard. Halkidiki as a whole is famous for its large number of historical sights, and the Kassandra peninsula is no exception, and some of them are located almost on the territory of hotels. In a word, everyone who wants to combine a beach resort with an educational one will be able to enjoy the first and second equally. In Kassandra, you can meet newlyweds, families with children, youth companies, and pensioners. All this speaks of the universality of the peninsula as a seaside resort. A pleasant, mild, very comfortable climate contributes to a pleasant stay. The duration of the beach season lasts about 5 months - from May to the end of October.





Characteristics and Legends

The Kassandra Peninsula, as already noted, has the shape of an elongated finger, the length of which is 50 kilometers. But in width it is quite narrow: only 15 km. The highest point on the island is 355 m above sea level. According to legend, in the past the peninsula was called Flegra, that is, ‚Äúburning land‚Äù, and its first inhabitants were the Giants - the sons of Uranus and Gaia. It seems to many that Kassandra is a female name, but the peninsula got its name in honor of Tsar Kassandra - son-in-law of Alexander the Great. They say that the first settlement on the peninsula was founded by the great Macedonian as early as the 4th century BC. Life here was in full swing, the inhabitants were engaged in trade, a port acted on the shore.

Siviri

Today in Kassandra there are 44 settlements, each of which has its own special charm. One of the best resorts not only in Kassandra, but throughout Greece is Siviri. It is here that the aforementioned Calypso Hotel Siviri is located. The resort can be called emerald. It is completely immersed in the green of exotic plants and variegated flowers. Closer to the water's aquamarine edge, the beach is strewn with golden sand. The local landscape also includes picturesque mountains covered with a lush green carpet, as well as bizarre rock formations. The village is distinguished by an abundance of taverns, restaurants and bars, which serve seafood skillfully prepared by local chefs, as well as bakeries, the fragrant aroma of which is mixed with the smell of olive and citrus plantations. Especially lucky for those tourists who come to Greece during the Kassandra festival, which takes place in the famous Siviri amphitheater. Having settled in Calypso Hotel Siviri 2 * (Greece, Halkidiki), they will be able to enjoy various interesting events on the festival‚Äôs agenda: theatrical performances, concerts, comic performances, which by the end of the day will be crowned with watching the unreal beauty of the sunsets.



Calypso Siviri 2 *: general description

This hotel is located in a dense pine forest. It was built at the dawn of the new millennium, and despite the fact that only 2 stars are lit on its sign, comfort is the main priority of the hotel. During the construction of the building new materials were used, and during the reconstruction, which was carried out in 2011, the interior was improved in order to meet the requirements of the time. Calypso Hotel Siviri consists of two buildings - one two-story and one three-story.



Location

Hotel ‚ÄúCalypso‚Äù is located 250 meters from the beach, that is, on the second line. Within walking distance from it is a supermarket and a bus stop. Siviri resort center is about 200 meters away. Around there are night clubs, taverns, discos, where you will find quite affordable prices for all types of meals. At 8 kilometers, in the village of Kallithea, there is a well-equipped bowling club.

Number of rooms

The Calypso Hotel Siviri has a total of 22 comfortable rooms of three categories. Of these, 2 are deluxe, 3 are junior suites, and the remaining 17 are the most common standard rooms. In each of the 27 rooms, regardless of their type, there is a telephone with direct international dialing, a mini fridge, a safe, a TV with satellite channels, air conditioning / split system, in the bathrooms you will find a set of towels, a hairdryer, toiletries (soap, toilet paper, shampoo, etc.). In addition, all rooms have balconies with outdoor furniture. The deluxe rooms have a kitchenette with tea / coffee making facilities, a set of dishes, and a sitting area (sofa, armchairs, coffee table). Cleaning is done once a week. As you can see, despite the fact that the hotel is a two-star hotel, there is everything you need for a comfortable stay for guests, and most importantly, at Calypso you will find affordable prices acceptable for people with modest income.



Infrastructure

Free parking is available on site. You can leave your rental car here. By the way, it is very convenient, and, being on wheels, you can independently go around all the sights of the island. The rental price starts from 30 euros per day, while you need to have an international driving license with you. The hotel's infrastructure also includes a pool with a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols, as well as a restaurant and a bar located by the pool. There is free internet throughout the hotel.

Nutrition

The hotel operates on the BB system. This means that for all hotel guests, regardless of the cost of the tour, breakfast is provided in the morning. For him there is a hall specially equipped for the buffet. Breakfast can not be called very diverse, but there is plenty of everything. There are always fresh fruits and vegetables, fresh pastries, locally made dairy products (very tasty), cheeses, meat snacks. Hot eggs can be prepared on request, for children you can ask to cook porridge.



Beach

As already noted, the hotel is located on the second beach line, and the beach can be reached in 7-10 minutes, which, admit, not so much. The water in the sea is clean, just magnificent, the beach is gentle. Sharp stones can be found in the water, so it is better to buy rubber special shoes. Since the beach is municipal and entrance is free, the use of umbrellas and sun loungers is paid. Here you will certainly be offered a wide range of water activities: yachting, catamarans, scooters, bananas, water skiing, parachuting, and, of course, diving lessons.
