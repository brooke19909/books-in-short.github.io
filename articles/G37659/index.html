LCD "21 Century", Kazan: features and location

Kazan is one of the most beautiful cities in the country, a cultural and historical center. It is here that tourists come not only from Russia, but also from various corners of the globe. There is nothing surprising in the fact that the city is growing and developing, new residential complexes, entire microdistricts appear, where excellent conditions for a comfortable life are created. "Kazan 21 Century" is a revolutionary project of integrated housing development, which uses best practices, innovations. It reflects the modern concept of living space. Therefore, it deserves special attention.





about the project

The comfortable complex "Kazan 21st Century" fully lives up to its name. Several buildings erected using advanced technologies form a separate microdistrict with developed urban infrastructure.

Location

For the construction of the residential complex Kazan 21 Century, the territory of the former airfield was chosen. And do not be confused by this fact: the place is excellent, spacious and free from harmful industries. Comfort class buildings are built in several stages at the intersection of Albert Kamaleev Avenue and Sedov and A. Kutuya Streets. This is a quiet and peaceful place, not without access to all objects of developed urban infrastructure.

You can get to the city center in just ten minutes, both by personal and public transport. The nearest metro station "Gorki" is four kilometers from the complex, you can get to it using buses and minibuses.





Technology

The residential complex " Kazan 21 Century" is being built using advanced technologies, which make it possible to achieve impeccable quality and high speed construction. The process is based on several stages, each of which can be tracked on the official website of the developer. Enthusiastic reviews of the first tenants and their photos speak of impeccable quality of work. The use of energy-saving technologies can significantly reduce the cost of electricity consumption in each apartment.

Infrastructure

At the moment, active construction of the 2nd stage of Kazan 21st Century is underway . The project provides for the construction of all necessary infrastructure. On the territory there will be kindergartens and a comprehensive school, playing and sports grounds, necessary for organizing leisure activities. In addition, well-developed recreation areas, walking and bicycle paths, green areas for picnics and outdoor recreation, as well as ample parking lots for residents of the complex and its guests, are under development.





Flats

When planning the future apartments, all preferences and requirements of modern buyers were taken into account. They are offered one-, two-, three- and four-room apartments with a turnkey finish.

Here you will find small, but cozy and comfortable apartments for newlyweds, represented by a small kitchen, a living room and a full bedroom. By combining the bath and the bathroom, it was possible to free the necessary square meters of area. At the same time, a large friendly family will choose the best option for themselves, each member of which will receive his own isolated room. The developer proposed apartments of European layout, providing for the integration of the kitchen and guest areas into a single space and isolation of the bedrooms.
