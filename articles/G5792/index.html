Reviews

Tourists who choose Turkey for vacation share in their reviews that the purpose of their choice was to get as much sun, tasty food as possible during their stay at the resort, to experience the benefits of a pleasant and comfortable hotel service and clean beaches equipped with modern equipment. The Xanthe Resort & Spa 5 * (reviews assure of this) in many respects met their expectations.





About location

Guests of The Xanthe Resort & Spa 5 * hotel (Side, Turkey) note as one of its indisputable advantages a convenient location near the market and shops. The market, guests share, is 5 minutes from the hotel, you can buy everything here: swimwear, clothes, shoes, fruits, etc. Tourists recommend always bargaining at the market, while up to 60% of the cost of goods can be saved.

About Territory

According to reviews, the territory of the hotel is quite spacious, comfortable, it has a lot of greenery. All flowers, trees, bushes located on the territory are signed: plates with Latin names are installed near them. The authors of the reviews share that at night, hedgehogs run through the hotel. Along the promenade near each hotel there is a cat cafe where the staff feeds cats, to the delight of children. Tourists say that they really like the wide, long promenade, which is convenient to walk in the evenings. The minus of vacationers believe that the bars on the beach close the view of the sea. According to reviews, in the children's Olympic pool in the territory there is always cold water. They note the presence of many other amenities: the main large pool with children's slides, and a beautifully designed kids club, a pool (indoor) near the spa.

Many guests are happy with the necessary infrastructure available in the territory: a store with various little things, a jewelry, gym, spa, cinema, and beauty salon.

Near the hotel, the authors of reviews share, a river flows. Many turtles and fish swim in it.

About Check In

It is not populated immediately, vacationers share. If you arrive early in the morning, you will have to wait until 14.00. Visitors are handed bracelets, luggage can be left at the reception and immediately go to breakfast, and then walk around the hotel.

About rooms

The rooms on the 4th floor (with sloping ceilings) are equipped with spacious balconies with sea views, which is a big plus for those who like to enjoy the nightly seascape.

The rooms on the ground floor, especially if they face the road, guests wake up at night from the noise of buses bringing new tourists to the reception. In addition, in the morning, gardeners unceremoniously peek into the room, cutting trees under the windows.

The administration may refuse to change the number, motivating them by the lack of free places. The authors of the reviews recommend describing their preferences in advance, even when buying a tour to the hotel.

Some guests report poor housekeeping. If you put a bill with a hint on a dusty TV that it would be nice to wipe off this dust from it, the bill may disappear and the dust may remain.

Family rooms are called cozy. These are two-room apartments with double and bunk beds. They have two televisions, two watering cans per shower. The minuses call vacationers significant audibility in the evenings, as well as noise from neighboring rooms.

Vacationers note that the beaches in Side are usually jam-packed. About 3-5 minutes walk from the hotel to the sea. Living beach is called good, its surface is strewn with yellow sand, the water in the sea is very clear. The beach is shallow, vacationers say, which is very convenient for bathing children.

Unfortunately, hotel guests share, despite the presence of garbage containers, there are a lot of cigarette butts and other garbage on the beach. According to reviews, tourists have never seen a beach cleaned. Sun beds, according to reviewers, are often busy with towels, not vacationers. Tourists note a comfortable gentle sandy entry into the sea, without stones and breakwaters. On the beach there is a bar, vacationers share.
