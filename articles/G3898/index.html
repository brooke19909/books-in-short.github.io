Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * (Turkey, Side, Kumkoy): description, photos and reviews

Side is one of the largest resort areas in Turkey. Not far from this settlement is a beautiful hotel called Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 *, which, according to the recommendations of tourists, is often visited by their friends and acquaintances. This place receives a lot of positive feedback because it justifies its five-star status, offering guests first-class living conditions and an excellent level of service.



general information

Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * was built relatively long ago - in 1996. On its territory at the time of opening there was a large building and a swimming pool. There was also a small garden on site. Over time, there have been some changes in the hotel that have made this place to stay much better. At present, its territory is almost 11.5 thousand square meters. m. It has a large building, which has a very beautiful and modern look, as well as several additional buildings, which are represented by three- and five-story buildings.

The international airport of Antalya is located relatively close to this place - 60 kilometers. In each tour package, a shuttle service is provided, which, tourists say, is carried out in a very comfortable car, equipped with air conditioning, which on hot days saves tourists from the heat while they are on the road.

Rooms

In total, the hotel has 320 apartments, which are divided into two groups: "standard" and "family". All of them offer different living conditions, however, there are many common features.

First of all, all apartments have a beautiful bathroom equipped with plumbing, where there is a bathing place in the form of a bath or shower, a washbasin, as well as a bathroom. Each tourist is provided with a set of bath accessories at the entrance, which without fail includes a bathrobe, towel and slippers. In addition, sets of bathing accessories are installed in the room - their number is equal to the number of guests.

The apartments also have individual air conditioners, thanks to which you can maintain the optimum temperature, regardless of the weather, which is on the street.

"Standard"

Guests of Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * say in their stories about staying at the hotel that in case of relaxing here together the best option for living will be a standard room, which is equipped with everything necessary to achieve a state of complete comfort, and at the same time he is a cheaper option than a "family".

It has a double berth, which is represented by either a double French bed or two single beds. Near them are bedside tables on which vacationers can lay out their small things. If among them there are valuable, then the hotel provides the opportunity to rent an electronic safe, in which they can be folded. Guests can put their clothes in a large wardrobe, which is made of wood and, as guests note, it looks very elegant.

In rooms of this class there is a folding sofa, which can serve as an extra bed for another person. Near it is a small table, at which you can drink a cup of your favorite hot drink, prepared using the substation provided in the room.

The room features a large plasma TV with satellite channels, many of which are Russian-language.



"Family"

The rooms of this class, in the opinion of guests, are more likely intended for tourists who come to relax with their whole family, in some cases with children. All this is because, upon request, a playpen-type bed can be installed for a child under 12 years old. As for the rest of the furniture equipment, it is designed for four guests, which can accommodate in two rooms, the decor of which is the same. Each room has a plasma TV, on which you can watch your favorite shows.

In the room, as in the "standard" there is a tea substation for making hot drinks, which, of course, is positively evaluated by the hotel guests.



Conditions for children

Often guests of Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * (Turkey / Side) are families who prefer to go on vacation in the company of kids or with teenagers. Guests rate this hotel ideal for a stay in this category of guests, due to a number of factors contributing to their comfortable stay.

First of all, it is the presence of zones for children's entertainment. In particular, this is a pool located in an open area - it was built in accordance with all safety standards, has a shallow depth and a coating that prevents slipping. Also next to it there is a small but well-equipped playground, where there are several exercise machines and barricades for active pastime of kids.

Parents in their reviews of Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * often make a positive mark that it is possible to provide babysitting services. They are paid, but, despite this, very popular. If desired, kids can be sent to a mini club, where animators will spend time with them.



Entertainment

The guests of Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * (Side) constantly repeat in their comments that the hotel is always fun, there is something to entertain yourself even in the afternoon, at night. To ensure that guests do not get bored, a cheerful team of animators works here, the representatives of which, for the most part, speak good Russian. They organize show programs with dance, vocal and other entertaining elements, which undoubtedly please hotel guests.

In the daytime, sports and recreational activities are held in the aquazone area, and in the evening, real fun begins here - a disco that lasts all night.

Nutrition

Guests who live in the Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * on the all-inclusive concept can eat in catering establishments, in particular, in restaurants where a buffet is served. They very often leave feedback on the level of preparation of dishes, as well as their vibrant presentation.

Three restaurants are presented to the attention of guests, in which they can surprise guests with dishes prepared in the best traditions of Italy, the Mediterranean and seafood. All of them work exclusively during the holiday season.

In addition to restaurants, the hotel has three bars, one of which is open around the clock. You can taste delicious snacks, original desserts, as well as delicious and unusual cocktails.



Aquazone

Most tourists in their comments note that most of their time they spend in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãpools, which represent a considerable aquazone. Its total area is 730 square meters. m. One of the pools is equipped with high slides, where guests have the opportunity to cheerfully spend their time. As they note in their comments, the water in all three niches is constantly crystal clear, but they are heated by sunlight, due to the lack of a system that would automate this process.

If desired, tourists can spend their free time on the sun terrace, which is located around the pools. For their convenience, it is furnished with sun loungers and umbrellas, which are able to protect vacationers from the harmful effects of sunlight.

There is a bar near the pool where guests can enjoy original and colorful cocktails.



Sport

All guests who monitor their health in reviews of the Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * (Kumkoy) tell about the fact that there are all the conditions necessary to maintain your body in shape even while on vacation. One of such places is a large hall, which is equipped on the top floor of the building. It has a large number of units that allow you to work with different muscle groups. Some guests prefer to spend their time in the halls, which are equipped for playing table tennis. For a fee, you can visit the billiard room, which has several tables for this game.

An open area for tourists created a platform for team games. Among them there are venues intended for team games, there is even a tennis court, the game on which is paid - about $ 10 per hour.

SPA

Fans of relaxation give positive comments to the spa area, which is located in Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * (Side / Kumkey). They indicate that it consists of several parts, one of which is massage. It is a large room with a number of tables for working with a client. Tourists note that among the staff there are only masters of massage art who know how to immerse a vacationer in a state of complete relaxation. Tourists say that when you leave this place, you can participate yourself completely updated. Here you can also conduct a number of spa treatments that help rejuvenate the skin and the whole body.

The second part of the SPA zone is its thermal part, which consists of a large sauna and hammam. Visiting these places is completely free, which leads to their high demand.



Beach

Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * has its own beach on the Mediterranean coast, which is literally 50 meters from the main building. The beach line is covered with sand. As tourists note, it is always clean here, and it also offers an excellent overview of the surrounding nature: endless sea expanses and flowering tropical plantings.

Throughout its territory, there are evenly arranged resting places in the form of sun loungers, which are covered by large beach umbrellas. In the center of their congestion there is a large bar where guests can cool off with a cocktail or a delicious drink.

Staying at Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * (Kumkoy), tourists can use the toilets, changing rooms, as well as the public showers that are available in this place.

In their reviews, travelers generally rate this place to stay, they are always ready to recommend it to their friends and acquaintances. A week-long stay at Trendy Hotel Palm Beach 5 * (Side / Kumkoy) will cost about 55,000 rubles, which is quite affordable for many residents of Russia.
