Golden Taurus Park Resort (Spain / Costa del Maresme): photos and reviews of tourists

In our article, we want to talk about one of the Costa del Maresme hotels called Golden Taurus Park Resort. Spanish resorts have long gained popularity among tourists from all over the world. However, they are not cheap compared to budget options such as Turkey and Egypt.

A bit about the resort

Costa del Maresme stretches south of the Costa Brava near Barcelona. The main resorts are considered: Sant Pol de Mar, Pineda de Mar, Callela, Santa Susanna, Malgrat de Mar. These places are known for their wide sandy beaches. Costa del Maresme is considered the most economical region on the entire Spanish coast on the Mediterranean Sea. It should be noted that the vast majority of local hotels offer apartments at relatively inexpensive prices.

Once in these places there were only peasant and fishing settlements. But in the middle of the twentieth century, the rich inhabitants of Barcelona began to build up the coast with villas and hotels for themselves and visiting guests. Since then, the infrastructure of the Spanish resort has been developing all the time. And now it is a fully equipped and modern town for tourists. There are modern hotel complexes of various levels, entertainment centers and much more, which may be interesting to vacationers. Costa del Maresme is considered one of the most beautiful places on the Spanish coast.

Hotel Location

The hotel has a good location, fifty meters from it is the beach. From the center of Pineda de Mar, no more than three hundred meters to Golden Taurus Park Resort 4 * (Spain). Barcelona and its airport are 75 kilometers from the hotel. The closest to the hotel is the airport of the town of Girona.



Golden Taurus Park Resort has a long history. It was built in 1963 and since then has safely hosted guests within its walls. The last reconstruction was carried out in 2007.

Number of rooms

Golden Taurus Park Resort consists of four different buildings (buildings have from three to eight floors). The hotel is not very diverse. In total, the complex has 461 Standard rooms, 20 Single room apartments, 6 rooms equipped for people with disabilities.

The comfortable and spacious rooms at Golden Taurus Park Resort come with comfortable, modern furniture. Some of them have a view of the sea coast. The complex has double and quadruple apartments. All are connected to the central heating system, have air conditioning, safe, telephone, hairdryer and bath accessories.

The hotel rooms do not have any special differences among themselves. The largest of them can accommodate no more than four people. The complex is aimed at families with small children. It works all year round: in the summer, vacationers come here to swim in the sea and sunbathe, and in winter to ski in the mountains.

Hotel infrastructure

Golden Taurus Park Resort is located a little away from the center of the town, but there are bars, restaurants and cafes next to it. The hotel has a garden, a conference room and a playroom for children. The complex has six pools, near which there is a relaxation area with free sun loungers and umbrellas. Baby cots are available upon request, and babysitting services are paid. The hotel also has a spa with a jacuzzi and massage rooms. Paid services include: laundry service, parking, bicycle rental. There is also a beauty salon.

Dining at the hotel

How are meals at the hotel Golden Taurus Park Resort 4 * (Spain)? Agency tours usually include full board or continental breakfast. Basically, you can choose any option that suits you best. The fact is that next to the hotel there are quite a lot of restaurants and cafes, where you can also eat well.



As for the hotel, in the main restaurant the food is organized as a buffet. It serves dinner, breakfast and lunch. In addition, there is a snack bar on site, which offers a large assortment of hot dishes, salads, fast food, ice cream and other desserts. All hotel bars offer refreshing drinks and cocktails.

Sports, entertainment and the beach

Vacationers of the Golden Taurus Park Resort 4 * hotel (Spain) can visit the tennis court, do water sports, play mini-golf. The complex is located in the first line, which is a huge advantage for tourists. Hotel guests can use the Pineda de Mar Municipal Beach, which is one of the most equipped on this coast. There are good changing cabins, fountains with drinking water, bicycle parking and bike paths (this type of transport is very popular here), a large number of bars and shops. The beach infrastructure is a small but pleasant addition to the clear sea and the beautiful coast.



As in any resort, there is a wide selection of water activities for every taste: from the simplest to the most extreme.

Resort Features

The Costa del Maresme resort has gained popularity due to the mild marine climate, which is perfect for treatment and beach holidays. The area is surrounded by mountain ranges of the Pyrenees, which cover it from the winds. The result is such a wonderful climate. The sea season lasts from April to the end of October. Costa del Maresme is also called the fruit beach, because here they harvest as many as three crops a year. The slopes are completely covered with orchards and strawberry plantations.

Excursions

Resting in Golden Taurus Park Resort 4 * (Spain), it is simply impossible not to visit local attractions. Spain is a beautiful country with a rich history, there are unique beauty places and historical monuments.

Golden Taurus Park Resort 4 * hotel always offers its guests sightseeing tours, but do not rush to purchase them. In Catalonia, everything is so close to each other that you can independently get to all interesting places by public transport, which runs regularly.



Barcelona is a popular tourist destination, which is certainly worth a look. There are many museums and architectural attractions.

No less interesting will be a trip to Girona. In season, pleasure boats offer a boat trip between the Costa del Maresme and the famous city of Costa Brava. One way road will cost you from 15 to 25 euros, but it's worth it, you will get a lot of impressions.

Most of the vacationers Golden Taurus Park Resort (Spain) are families with children, they will certainly like the water amusement park called Marineland. The water park is designed not only for adults, but also for children. It has a special area for those who are not yet six years old. There is also a mini zoo and an exhibition of birds of prey. In addition, the water park is famous for performances of dolphins.

In Barcelona there is a Park Guell, which has gained worldwide fame. It is so unusual that it is worth seeing with your own eyes. Eusebi Guell once bought land on the outskirts of the city, planning to create a popular garden city at that time. But something went wrong and the project closed. Now in the park is the house of Gaudi, in which he lived for twenty years. Currently, there is a museum.

The spectacle so harmoniously combines light, music and water that every evening it gathers crowds of grateful spectators, including not only tourists, but also residents of Barcelona.
