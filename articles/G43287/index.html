Which good tablet is best to choose?

The main requirements that a good tablet must meet are a low price combined with a rich set of technical specifications. To date, three software platforms for these devices are widely used - these are Windows RT, iOS and Android. The first of them is presented only by Microsoft Surface. But its second version has already been announced. The tablet from the legendary Finnish manufacturer Nokia 1520 also runs this mobile operating system. Apple products have one significant drawback - closedness. Its essence is that there is no direct access to the file system. It can only be obtained by hacking. But this does not interfere with devices based on it. But Android is the most promising operating system, which is practically devoid of various kinds of shortcomings. But this does not mean that a good tablet works only under the control of this OS. It all depends on the requirements that are put forward to him, and, starting from them, you can make the right choice.





Windows RT

Microsoft Surface is the first tablet PC of this manufacturer. Talking about the prospects of this platform is quite problematic. Repeatedly, the largest software developer was forced to lower the price of his device. But this did not solve the problem - the demand for it did not increase. Its second modification, most likely, will not solve this problem. The strength of Microsoft is that it has a large set of software for x86 processors. Already announced devices that will combine the classic "Windows" and such CPUs. Before they appear on this platform, it makes no sense to pay attention, and only after such radical changes, perhaps a good tablet will appear among this group of devices.





iOS

This operating system is represented by a whole group of iPad tablets. There is also a miniature mini with a diagonal of 7.9 inches, and its 10-inch older relatives of versions 2, 3 and 4. The quality of Apple products speaks for itself. A large set of optimized software is an additional plus. But the minus of such devices is the high cost. Another drawback is the closed file system. If these two minuses do not play a significant role for you, then a good tablet from Apple will become your friend and assistant.

Android





High competition among Android devices has led to the fact that here you can really find high-quality and reliable products. First of all, pay attention to the Samsung Galaxy Note 10.1. An excellent solution, in which the minus is that the CPU is 2-core. A little higher in technical specifications is Nexus from Google. Its disadvantage is a lower resolution compared to the winner. The best tablet of 2013 is the Asus Transformer Pad Model TF700. Other things being equal, high resolution in Full HD format leaves no chance for competitors. If it is still supplemented with a docking station, then it turns into a full-fledged laptop. Of course, its price is comparable to Apple products, but the open architecture in this case is an additional advantage. This is the perfect tablet to date.

conclusions

As part of this material, you can get the answer to the question of which good tablet is best to choose today. One thing is certain. Despite the great variety, the leaders in this segment of mobile devices are Android devices: Galaxy Note from Samsung with a diagonal of 10.1, Nexus from Google with any diagonal and the Transformer Pad model TF700 from Asus. They can be recommended for purchase in the near future.
