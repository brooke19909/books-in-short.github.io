Zenkovsky Park (Prokopyevsk) - an oasis in the city

In the industrial city, which is the largest coal-mining center in Russia, there is a wonderful corner with beautiful nature. This park is like a breath of clean air in a city in which gray snow will fall even in winter. Among the dust and permitted structures, there is such a mini-paradise in Prokopyevsk where you can relax with your body and soul surrounded by nature.

Briefly about the park

Zenkovsky Park (Prokopyevsk) was opened a long time ago - back in 1933. On its territory there is a forest and a lake. There is everything for a good rest: attractions, playgrounds for sports, alleys with comfortable benches. The park was arranged by the townspeople. Now there are two sanatoriums operating on its territory: Shakhtar and Prokopyevsky. The latter is considered the best health resort of Kuzbass.

In summer, the park organizes entertainment. You can ride a horse or see the city from a bird's eye view by taking a helicopter ride. Those who enjoy outdoor activities are invited to the football stadium and ski-rental base, which operates only in winter.

In the Zenkovsky park (Prokopyevsk) the hotel complex "Aurora" works. In it you can not only have a good rest, but also go for a drive on a skating rink (in winter) or play tennis on spacious courts.

Children will also be interested in visiting the park. On its territory sculptures of fairy-tale heroes, playgrounds were built. Serves the park municipal institution "Pearl".

Reconstruction

Zenkovsky Park (Prokopyevsk) was first reconstructed in the 50s. The initiators were the workers of the Central mine. They attracted local people.
