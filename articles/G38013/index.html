Hotel Atlantica Thalassa 4 * (Greece, Kos): description, rooms and reviews

This article is about the Atlantica Thalassa 4. This is a chain hotel. It belongs to the Atlantic Hotels and Resorts chain. Its representative office on the Greek island of Kos is located in the resort of Lambi. What is this place, and what is the hotel - we will tell below. It should be said that the material for the description of the hotel is taken from the reviews of ordinary tourists.





Lambi Resort specifics

The capital of the island of Kos (Greece, the Dodecanese archipelago) is a city with the same name. The international airport, which accepts flights from Athens, lies twenty-five kilometers from the city. Kos island is located very close to the coast of Turkey. And its capital is located on the east coast. Therefore, in the morning (even without binoculars) you can see how Turkey wakes up.

Kos is famous for its sandy beaches. But in his capital they are pebble. But the water in the sea is transparent, like a tear. The earthquake of one thousand nine hundred and thirty-third year completely destroyed the medieval city of Kos, but laid bare the ancient ruins. The capital of the island is small, it is quite comfortable to walk on it. For tourists around the city, two types of trams run. A blue trailer will take you to the ancient monument of archeology Asklepion. Green - ride around the city with a sightseeing tour.

Kos, despite its metropolitan status, remained a quiet town. But he has an outskirts called Lambi. It is in this suburb that the whole evening and stormy life of the capital of Kos has moved. Amid this fun, across the road from its own private beach, stands the Atlantica Thalassa 4 Hotel. Literally at its entrance there is a city bus stop. The center of Kos is four kilometers away. The beach at the northern tip of the island is one hundred and fifty meters.



Territory and buildings

Atlantica Thalassa Hotel 4 is a small hotel complex. It consists of three buildings. The buildings were built in the year nineteen eighty-seven and completely restored in the first two thousand. The hotel is not open year-round, but only during the tourist season (May-November).

Judging by the reviews of tourists, the territory of the hotel is small, stylized as a park. Between the buildings spread green lawns. There are two swimming pools with fresh water (one of them is for children), a sun terrace. To keep everything at hand, a hotel with all kinds of stuff is located on the territory of the hotel complex.

Guests really like the fact that the hotel has several sports facilities: basketball, volleyball, mini-golf, table tennis and tennis. The hotel is surrounded by beautiful gardens. Across the road, a two-minute walk, is the private beach of the complex.

How to feed at the hotel?

Atlantica Thalassa Hotel 4 (Kos) has fully implemented the concept of catering and all-inclusive service. Guests are fed in the main restaurant, which serves buffet meals. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the chefs of this establishment specialize in European cuisine.

In addition to the main restaurant, on the territory of the Atlantic Talassa there is also the Greek Tavern, stylized as a national flavor. The poolside bar will delight guests with refreshments and light snacks. It is beautifully designed and sets for a cloudless holiday.

In the lobby of the hotel there is a cozy lobby bar with an extensive wine list. Wine and beer of local production are poured free of charge. You can also try ouzo, mastic, crayfish and other Greek vodkas. The bartender will mix excellent cocktails for you. The restaurant serves live music in the evenings.

Beach and Pools at Atlantica Thalassa 4

Kos (Greece) is famous for its wide sandy coasts. But at the Atlantic Talassa hotel, which is located on the northern tip of the island, the beach, according to tourists, is the best. It is, of course, sandy, very clean and well-maintained. It is located across a small road from the hotel. But sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach are paid - three euros per day. However, the hotel does not mind if you stay right on the sand.

The hotel provides beach towels for deposit, which can be changed once a day. The hotel has two freshwater swimming pools - one for adults and one for children. On the territory of the hotel you can also take sunbaths. Umbrellas and sun loungers on the sun terrace are absolutely free. There is a bar next to the pool during the daytime.

Hotel Services

Atlantica Thalassa 4 (Greece) is not in vain carries four stars on its sign. The hotel has many free and paid services. One of the first is access to the Internet - Wi-Fi pulls even in rooms. Services for children

A gentle entry into the sea and sandy beaches make the island of Kos a favorite place for family vacations. The talk of the town has become a sincere concern, with which the Greeks surround children, no matter their friends or strangers. Hotel Atlantica Thalassa 4 has created all the conditions for its youngest guests to feel comfortable on vacation and have fun. Firstly, they have their own shallow pool. Secondly, there is an equipped playground on the territory. And if the weather does not allow frolic in the fresh air, there is a room full of toys.

Small guests can be recorded in the mini club. There, animators will be engaged with children. The restaurant has high chairs for babies. Chefs take into account the needs and requirements of children's diet. If you notify the hotel in advance, a cradle will be installed in your room for the baby.
