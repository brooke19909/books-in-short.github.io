Several ways to transfer money from Tele2 to Sberbank card

A modern mobile phone has long been not just a device for making calls and sending SMS. Today, gadgets play the role of a handheld computer, and even a means of payment. Not so long ago, additional earnings became available using a mobile phone. Every day there are all new applications that allow you to organize additional income in any free time, regardless of the user's location. The money earned is sufficient not only for the use of mobile communications, but also for other needs. Therefore, users have become more interested in how to withdraw received money? Today, almost all operators provide such a service. This article will address the issue of how to transfer money from Tele2 to a Sberbank card.

New service of the federal operator Tele2

  • making money transfers to Contact, Unistream, Leader, Anelik and Close systems.

It is possible to transfer Tele2 not only to Russia, but also to the countries of the former Soviet Union.

How to use the new option to withdraw money from Tele2 to a Sberbank card?





The service works in two ways. Transactions can be made via the Internet and via SMS. It is worth noting that there are special payments that are made only through SMS.

Withdraw money via the Internet

In order to transfer money from Tele2 to a Sberbank card, you need to log in to the operator‚Äôs official website. In the bookmark, select with what the transfer is executed, then click on the label of the corresponding plastic card and fill in the proposed fields and forms. On the same page, you must specify the subscriber number from which the funds are debited.

There are no particular difficulties here. Each field has a tooltip. At the end, the full amount debited will be indicated taking into account the commission. Additional information is placed on the sides of the main field. The website of the service from Tele2 is located at www.money.tele2.ru. To fill out the form, you need to use the tooltips.





You can also transfer Tele2 by direct link - card-transfer.tele2.ru. After filling out the standard form:

  • phone number from which money will be debited;
  • amount of payment;
  • plastic card number (any payment systems are available: MasterCard and VISA; cards can be credit and debit).

One of the fields contains the commission for the provided service. After checking the correctness of the completed fields, click the appropriate button, and the money transfer will be sent.

Money transfer via SMS

In order to transfer money from Tele2 to a Sberbank card using short messages, you must do one of the following:

  • send a short message to the number 159 with the text: "card (space) 1234567812345678 (space) amount";
  • execute the USSD-command "* 159 * 1 * card_number * amount #";
  • send a message with the following content: ‚Äúrek (space) amount (space) account number (space) name of the recipient (space) bank details‚Äù to number 159.

The amount in all cases is indicated without cents. There is no charge for SMS to 159.

Transfer money in the office of a mobile operator





This is an additional way. In the Tele2 office of the consultant, fill out a form in which to indicate your own data and the size of the transfer. After processing the application, the money will be sent. The transaction will take no more than five business days.

Agreed limits and tariffs

There are restrictions on all transfers from Tele2 to a bank account . The minimum amount allowed for a transaction is 50 rubles. The maximum transfer (during the day) is 15,000 rubles. No more than ten transfers can be made per day. More than ten rubles should remain on the mobile account after the operation, in order to avoid blocking the phone.

The operator charges a fee for providing this service. Usually it is 5.75%, plus forty rubles. That is, transferring small amounts is unprofitable. The commission does not reduce the payment, but is calculated in excess of it.

The speed of receipt of money to the account depends on the selected method of sending. If the transfer is postal, then the money will go to the bank not earlier than in four days, the maximum period is eight days. The rest of the transfers can take two to three days, but usually arrive in a matter of minutes.

The operator warns that only those that have been credited to the account can transfer money from Tele2 to a Sberbank card. A variety of bonuses and other bonus points during the transaction will be unavailable. You can find out the amount that Tele2 is ready to transfer from a specific number using the command * 104 #.

The Money Transfer service is available to Tele2 subscribers with prepaid tariffication and a SIM card usage period of at least 60 days. Customers who pay bills after the fact cannot use this service.
