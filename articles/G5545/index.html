Crimea, "Homeland" (sanatorium): description, prices, reviews of vacationers

Even in tsarist times, rest in the Crimea was considered elite. In Soviet times, the attitude to this resort has not changed, and for many, a trip here remains one of the best memories of youth.

Now that the peninsula has again become part of Russia, it is quickly becoming one of the most popular holiday destinations. There are many sanatoriums, holiday homes, hotels and private pensions, which offer fairly comfortable living conditions for the period of beach holidays.

One of the best places of rest that Crimea offers is Rodina, a sanatorium where you can enjoy a comfortable rest and a warm sea outside.

Sanatorium "Homeland", Crimea. Description

We began to build this elite health resort in 1950. The construction of the main building took five years, after which a snow-white palace rose above Yalta. Massive columns, magnificent stucco molding, and also spacious viewing platforms give resemblance to a royal structure to a sanatorium. The architects did everything to give the building a unique grandeur, which even today leaves no one indifferent.



Then, in turn, three more buildings were erected, the last of which is located right on the promenade, by the sea.

The Crimea is famous for its magnificent nature. "Homeland" is a sanatorium that will amaze travelers with its vegetation. A health park of 10 hectares, on each meter of which a well-groomed tree grows, gradually descends to the coast. Here you can enjoy a leisurely stroll along the cypress and palm alleys, disconnecting from everyday worries.

You can go to the sea through numerous stairs, cascading down to the coast, among graceful statues located in specially installed niches.

How to get to the health resort

Crimea - Gaspra - sanatorium "Rodina", this is how the route to the recreation center looks like. Arriving on the peninsula in Simferopol, you need to get to Yalta. This can be done by bus or trolley. In Yalta, you should take bus number 102, which will take you to the stop "Sanatorium Rodina". Thus, getting to a health resort on your own is not difficult.

In addition to public transport, you can always use taxi services that will take you from Simferopol directly to the recreation center. A transfer from the sanatorium is also possible by prior arrangement.
