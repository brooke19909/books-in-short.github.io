Cyprus island, Ayia Napa, hotels

The quiet fishing village of Ayia Napa is a beautiful corner in Cyprus, which has turned into a popular resort with hotels, restaurants and discos. A fun and hectic life continues here around the clock.

Ayia Napa is surrounded by clear aquamarine water with pristine bays and beaches.





This place is especially popular among young people. Between shops and pizzerias is the main attraction of Ayia Napa - the Ancient Monastery, and the Museum of Prehistoric Tools. The taverns offer delightful Cypriot wine and traditional cuisine.

Cyprus Island, Ayia Napa: hotels

On the most famous beach of Nissi Beach is a very popular hotel - Adams Beach 5 *. This hotel is perfect for a quiet holiday with children thanks to a comfortable furnished area and the quality of services provided. In 2012, a new wing with luxury rooms was added for those customers who want to relax in complete privacy.





On the island of Cyprus, Ayia Napa, hotels are located in the tourist area, close to the central part of the city and the white sandy beach.

Hotel grecian sands

The modern and comfortable Grecian Sands Hotel was built in 1985, a hundred meters from the beach line. A cozy hotel with five floors is located in a private park with tropical vegetation on the seafront in Ayia Napa.

The hotel offers its customers excellent service, modern living conditions and a large number of leisure activities. The city center is just a 15-minute walk from the hotel. Consequently, your vacation will take place in the immediate vicinity of shops, restaurants and bars.





Nissi beach resort

Ayia Napa Hotels Cyprus is renowned for its first-class service. Nissi Beach Resort, built in 1972, is not an exception and a popular hotel complex among holidaymakers. This hotel is visited by tourists from all over the world. It is surrounded by a vast territory of a magnificent tropical park with colorful flowers and exotic palm trees. The hotel complex is considered to be a real paradise for tourists. The hotel is located on the sandy coast, in a beautiful bay, just two kilometers from the center of Ayia Napa.

Hotel limanaki beach

Popular hotels in Cyprus, Ayia Napa, is the very stylish Limanaki Beach Hotel, which is located just fifty meters from the beach. It can be confidently attributed to the highest level due to its convenient layout and quality service. The hotel is suitable for a quiet holiday with the family, and for active youth. It is surrounded by a large number of pubs, bars, shops and restaurants. An amusement park and nightlife are within walking distance.





On the island of Cyprus, Ayia Napa, hotels are booked from the first days of the opening season. Such incredible popularity is due to the developed infrastructure of the resort, suitable for vacationers of any age.

Despite the large number of pubs, clubs, restaurants and other things, Ayia Napa is also popular among lovers of a relaxing family holiday, since most of the hotels are located far from nightlife. Therefore, the noisy nightlife will not disturb those who prefer a quiet vacation.

On the island of Cyprus, Ayia Napa, hotels are as diverse as the resort itself. The summer season lasts from June to September. The least rainfall occurs in August.
