Montenegro, Budva, Riviera

This country and this city will not leave anyone indifferent. Everyone will find here something for themselves that sinks right into their hearts and will never forget it. Budva is a place for those who are tired of the usual beach holiday. It is very good for lovers of walks and antiquities. This resort on the Adriatic offers tourists beautiful sandy and pebble beaches, beautiful Mediterranean architecture that fits perfectly into the unique landscape. How to get there

You can get to the Grand Palazzo Hotel from two airports at once - Podgorica and Tivat. The first of them is sixty-five kilometers, the second - twenty-seven. Through the city runs one of the busiest routes in the country - the Jadran tract, which mainly drives all vehicles. This road goes straight to Greece. Tourists who used the services of Russian tour operators mostly get by transfer. The hotel is located in the New Town, where most of the hotels are located, so if you go by taxi from the airport, you will not be looking for this area for a long time. There is a railway here too, but it connects Budva with Belgrade, since it was built back in the times of the former Yugoslavia. From the airports, the Old Town bus station can also be reached by public transport, but from there you still have to take a taxi.
