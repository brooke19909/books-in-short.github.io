All details about Panasonic Lumix DMC TZ35

The Panasonic Lumix DMC TZ35 compact digital camera is more suitable for those people who have already mastered the basics of shooting. A wide range of different settings and simple management of them help to create the highest quality images without even having professional skills. When creating the camera, the previous TZ40 model was taken as the basis, the only difference is the lack of navigation, wireless connection and a touch screen. The Panasonic Lumix DMC TZ35 features a 20x optical zoom and a wide-angle lens. Compact cameras allow you to take pictures of close-ups or landscapes, they have already managed to achieve recognition in the international market and are loved by a huge number of users.



Comparison

The camera has similar characteristics of the processor, matrix and lens with the TZ40 model, but the screen resolution is significantly lower than that of the flagship, and there is no sensor in the device. When shooting video, the sound is recorded on a monaural microphone, there is no way to shoot in Full HD quality. This allowed to reduce the cost of the Panasonic Lumix DMC TZ35, now it will be 4000 rubles lower than the previous ultrasound model. You can save a lot on the camera. In other characteristics, it is in no way inferior to the TZ40.

Assembly

The package includes a battery, USB-cable, charger for Panasonic Lumix DMC TZ35, user manual, charging, lanyard for mounting, as well as a disk for downloading additional software.



In appearance, the digital camera is not much different from the previous model, but there is only one microphone, while the TZ40 has two. The power slider has changed to a regular key, its dimensions are identical to the previous options. The switch for shooting and playing back the captured video has changed, now to exit the view mode, press the shutter button. The camera was assembled efficiently, the case is not afraid of scratches, dirt and abrasions, when pressed, it does not emit crackles and creaks, for constant use it is completely reliable.

Camera structure

On the front of the Panasonic Lumix DMC TZ35 is a lens, flash and autofocus backlight, which additionally performs the functions of an automatic shutter timer. On the back side there is a multi-function button for entering the device menu, a key to switch to the view mode of the captured photo or video, exposure, changing shooting modes and deleting unnecessary files. The latter function helps not only to erase the data, but also quickly return to the menu and change one level in its functionality.

On the bottom there are connectors for a charger and a memory card, a special metal hole for mounting the camera on a tripod, and a plastic sticker informing that this device was assembled in Japan at a factory in Osaka.



On the upper part of the case we can see the drive for selecting operating modes, a microphone, a release button, a system speaker, power and video buttons, as well as additional zoom levers. On the right side panel there are connectors hidden by a plastic panel for connecting USB cables and charging, as well as an opening for a fixing cord.

Panasonic Lumix DMC TZ35 device reviews

The camera as a whole is quite functional, it allows you to take high-quality pictures in any light. Manufacturers tried to improve the model of a handheld digital device; it turned out to be very interesting and worthy of attention.

Some flaws in the device are still there. For example, slow autofocus, errors in metering and the lack of a GPS module, but in general, the solution can compete with similar cameras from other manufacturers.
