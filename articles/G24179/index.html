Kabardinka - relaxation for every taste and budget

It is safe to say that Kabardinka is a vacation that will appeal to every health resort, ranging from young couples with children to elderly couples. But have you ever wondered what attracts them here? What is the history of this world famous place?

Kabardinka. Relaxation. General information about the village



The village of Kabardinka is located fourteen kilometers from Gelendzhik on the banks of the Tsemess bay opposite the coast of Novorossiysk. At the height of the season there are many tourists. Shallow, and in some places sandy beaches, shallow depth, safe descent to the sea - an ideal place for families with children.

So what attracts Kabardinka? Relaxation? Prices? It is safe to say that both. In the modern resort town there are five-star hotels with high quality services, as well as budget hotels.

Most tourists still prefer to sunbathe on the central beach. Fans of a more secluded pastime choose distant corners: Cape Doob or Victoria. And for those who are accustomed to not quite a standard vacation, the nudist beach, the most famous on the entire Black Sea coast, is ideal.

The unique Old Park is the main attraction of Kabardinka. This is a real architectural monument of various times. Here you can get acquainted with Ancient Egypt, ancient classics, the periods of the Middle Ages, Art Nouveau and Baroque. All this is decorated with sculptures, fountains, an unusual landscape.

If you want to see the city of Novorossiysk in all its glory from above, you just need to climb the peaks of Mount Doob with the symbolic names Vera, Nadezhda, Lyubov.

Admirers of legends should visit the juniper reserve, where the Kastalsky font is located. An ancient legend about the love of the beautiful Nereid, daughter of Tsar Nerey, to the argonaut Dmitry is connected with this place.

Kabardinka. Relaxation. Historical reference

The ancient tribes who built the burial structures lived here in the Bronze Age. Monotone plate dolmens located on mountain slopes and ridges can be seen even now.

And in the 16th century, tribes of Circassians and Circassians settled in the vicinity of Kabardinka. Their burial mound has been preserved in the southeastern part of Kabardinka on the territory of the Pearl of the Sea boarding house.

In 1829, the Ottoman Empire signed a peace treaty with Russia, under which Turkey returned the Black Sea coast that it had seized. So the village of Kabardinka turned into one of the coastline fortresses. And in 1836 the first fleet of Alexandria arose here, which was later renamed Kabardinsky.

Since 1920, people began to come to rest in Kabardinka in large numbers. Tourists are attracted not only by the wonderful nature and sea, but also by affordable prices.

Kabardinka. Relaxation. Nature and local features

Kabardinka is considered the most favorable place not only for relaxation, but also for treatment. The sea, forests and clean mountain air of moderate humidity are very useful for people suffering from bronchopulmonary diseases.

The village is surrounded by a mountain range on three sides, this is a serious obstacle to the cold weather, so here is the longest holiday season. Tourists begin to come here in May, and until the end of November people sunbathe on the beaches. Hot weather in Kabardinka persists for seven months.

Due to the strong wind, only grass grows on the peaks of the Markhot Ridge, undersized trees begin to come across only closer to the slope. The soil of the region is very fertile, it has numerous orchards and vineyards.

The southern slope is covered with juniper forests, the species of these trees are listed in the Red Book. Plantings enrich the air with essential oils, which has a beneficial effect on the health of local residents and vacationers.
