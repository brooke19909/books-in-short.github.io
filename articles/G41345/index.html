Home workshop: do-it-yourself pencil cases for pens and pens

If you and your child like to do homework, make a pencil case in which you will put pencils, felt-tip pens, pens so that they do not hide on the drawers of the desk and are always in sight. Such a device would be quite acceptable for home use and for school.

Shape and appearance



What can be pencil cases? With their own hands they are made of cardboard, dense fabric, and other materials. The appearance, shape and other design elements depend on your taste and desire. The main thing is to do everything carefully, choose the right format so that it is convenient, and the thing lasts a long time.

Cardboard Case



To begin, discuss how to craft paper pencil cases. With your own hands it’s enough just to glue them from cardboard. First, put in a row the existing pencils or felt-tip pens, pens. It is better to make your own case for each type of writing medium. Now measure the occupied space with a ruler. Its length will determine the length of the cardboard, which is required for pencil cases. With your own hands, set aside the required number of centimeters on a sheet of cardboard.

Add on the right, left and bottom another 2.5-3.5 centimeters per sizing and internal volume. Draw a fold line with a simple pencil. Cut off excess paper. In a similar way, make the second blank-side under the pencil cases with your own hands. Only do not measure extra centimeters - they are no longer needed. On pencil lines bend inward seam allowances.

Spread with glue, attach the second blank on top, glue both parts. You got a box. Decorating such pencil cases with your own hands is no more difficult than making them. You can buy in the store a special dense self-adhesive paper “marbled” or with a different pattern. Perfectly wrapping paper with golden inclusions, embossed ornaments, etc. Or confine yourself to applications from fabric, colored paper, a ready-made set. Now you just have to make and attach the cap. That's all the tricks of how to make a pencil case with your own hands out of cardboard!

Fabric case



The second option for making a pencil case is from fabric, for example, denim. However, the material will suit another, and the main thing is tougher: velveteen, teak, etc. In addition to writing objects, you can also hold a notebook in such a case.

To make such a pencil case with your own hands, prepare 2 pieces from a denim panel of 45 by 36 cm for the base; one - 21 by 30 cm for the pocket; another 30 by 24 cm for a pencil pocket; 9 to 16 - lock parameters.

The technology is as follows: wash a pocket cut from old jeans trousers on the base material. From one of the outer edges, it should retreat 3 centimeters. You can insert a notebook into it. Sew now a pencil base pocket. Sew along the entire length into separate compartments where you will insert your objects for drawing. Do this on the left, wrong side of the case. Then flip to the front and sew. At the end, insert the zipper. Fill the pencil case and use the little thing! Happy needlework!
