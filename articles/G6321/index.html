Hotel Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 *, about. Phuket: description, rooms and reviews

Phuket is a great place for a beach holiday, full of entertainment and pleasant experiences. Here, tourists can enjoy the incredible beauty of nature, the warm sea, interesting culture and, of course, high-quality service.

In Phuket, especially when it comes to the coast, there are a huge number of hotels, pensions and guest houses. And the Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 is considered to be a rather popular tourist stopping place. Let's take a closer look at where the hotel is located and what services it offers? Is it suitable for family holidays? The answers to these questions are sought by many travelers.

Where is the hotel located and what does it look like?

The first thing that interests guests is the location of the Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4. Phuket, namely the west coast, is the territory of the picturesque resort village of Kamana, where the hotel was built.





By the way, the location of the hotel complex is quite convenient. For example, the village center can be reached in 10 minutes. The seashore is also nearby, and in 10 minutes you can take a taxi or bus to the famous Thai Patong Beach. A golf club with a huge field is a 15-minute drive away. The airport is also not too far away - only 30-40 minutes. By the way, guests provide a convenient transfer.

The hotel itself is a beautiful place located in the middle of a picturesque garden. Here you will find yourself in a real exotic tale. The hotel area is large, with pools, terraces, sports fields.

Hotel Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 (Phuket, Kamala): description and photos of rooms

This hotel is a great place for a comfortable stay. Guests are offered 289 luxuriously furnished rooms of different categories:

  • Studio - apartments that stand out with an elegant design. Their area is 44-56 square meters, and they consist of a bedroom and a living room.
  • Happy Baby Studio - rooms that are specially designed for families traveling with small children. In addition to the basic set of furniture, there is a cot and a chair for feeding, as well as a bath, a stroller and special children's dishes.
  • Royal Studio - spacious rooms with direct access to the pool.
  • Family Suites - large two-room rooms, consisting of two bedrooms, 1 or 2 bathrooms and a living area, which, incidentally, is also furnished with a soft folding bed.




Of course, you can count on the necessary household appliances, including a telephone, a TV, a powerful air conditioning system. There is a small safe and a comfortable desk. Almost all rooms have a well-equipped kitchenette with a coffee maker, microwave, electric kettle, and a set of tableware. The bathroom will please with modern plumbing - you can always take a shower here, and high-quality hygiene products are available.

Reviews indicate that cleaning is really carried out every day, and with high quality. Clean towels are always waiting for you in your room.

Restaurants and bars on site

For a truly comfortable stay, food is very important. What can guests at Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 count on? Paying for the room, tourists are also entitled to daily breakfasts, which are held in the form of a buffet. Buffets are served every morning at the main Fino restaurant. The reviews suggest that the menu here is quite diverse - they serve toasts, sandwiches, salads, light snacks, meat dishes and, of course, desserts.





Here you can have lunch or dinner at any convenient time. Next to one of the pools there is a bar with a summer terrace, where guests are offered chilled cocktails, spirits, snacks, desserts and ice cream loved by children.

Where is the beach? Water recreation

Hotel complex Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 is located very close to the sea coast. You can reach it in a few minutes. The beach here is covered with soft, golden sand, as well as the seabed. Despite the fact that this is a public stretch of coast, it is not always crowded here. For a small fee, you can rent sun loungers and umbrellas for the whole day, as well as get clean beach towels. Near the shore there are several cafes where you can eat or order a chilled cocktail.

Fans of more active leisure will also be able to find a pleasant pastime. Here you can ride a boat, pedalo or motorcycle, as well as water skiing, banana and even take a short trip on a boat or yacht. Travelers also enjoy sailing and windsurfing.

Do guests offer additional services?

Of course, guests at Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 can count on some additional services. For example, here you can exchange currency at a favorable rate. A mini-market is constantly working, where you can buy various household trifles, as well as food products, including fresh fruits and vegetables, as well as interesting souvenirs that will remind you of pleasant moments for many years.





There is wireless internet access in the lobby. Guests traveling without appropriate devices can use guest computers, which is very convenient. The wellness spa has a clinic with qualified medical staff. Here you can get medical help at any time.

Hotel Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 (Phuket, Kamala): how do guests have fun?

Naturally, most tourists come to Phuket in search of a beach holiday. Nevertheless, you can have a good time on the territory of the Sunwing Resort Spa Kamala Beach 4 hotel itself. For example, there are as many as seven pools of different sizes and depths. You can relax both by the large lively pool with water slides, and relax in a secluded, artificial lagoon in the middle of the garden.





A fitness center is constantly working, where not only a gym with modern equipment is at your disposal, but also professional trainers who will always help with advice or even draw up a special training program.

Guests can use the spa. Here you will be offered a huge selection of services, ranging from different types of massage, visits to the jacuzzi and sauna, and ending with various wellness, cosmetic and anti-aging treatments.

Yes, and do not forget about sightseeing holidays, because the island of Phuket is replete with incredibly beautiful places and, of course, historical monuments.

Conditions for relaxing with a child

For parents planning a trip with their children, questions about the availability of appropriate amenities become extremely important. What does Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 offer families? In fact, this hotel complex is designed primarily for family vacations. For example, choosing a room marked Happy baby, parents will have at their disposal a small covered terrace where children can play and relax. In the restaurant you can easily pick up delicious food, and for the youngest travelers there is a counter with quality baby food.





Kids can splash around in local pools and relax on the beach, because the entrance to the sea is gentle. The guys also have a great time on a large playground with swings, sandboxes and even a children's Swedish wall. Despite the fact that there is no mini-club in the hotel, experienced, funny animators always entertain children. Your child will be able to take part in team games, competitions and theatrical productions.

How do tourists respond to a hotel?

Of course, talking to people who have already managed to visit a particular place, you can get a lot of useful information. What do the Sunwing Resort Kamala Beach 4 say? Traveler reviews are only positive: the hotel is really beautiful, with a large well-groomed territory and all possible amenities.





The rooms are equipped with modern technology that works properly. The local restaurant serves delicious breakfasts, and you can have dinner here, and the prices are not too high. The advantage is the proximity of the beach, as well as the presence of various attractions and entertainment on the territory of the hotel. According to the reviews, all the hotel staff are very pleasant in communication, hospitable and attentive, so travelers feel at home.
