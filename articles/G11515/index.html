Sea tour in Azerbaijan: reviews. Tours to the Caspian Sea

Every year to go on vacation to Azerbaijan is a tradition, and not only among residents of Russia. Tourists come from all over the world to enjoy the light and mild climate of this beautiful country. Throughout the year, you can buy tours to Azerbaijan, but the best time for swimming and sunbathing is from the beginning of May to the end of September.

Based on the purpose for which each tourist goes on a trip, and determine the direction of movement. To improve health with thermal water, it is better to go to Lankaran, to be coated with medicinal oil - welcome to Naftalan. It is better to spend a quiet vacation in unity with nature in Kusary, Masall or Nabran-sel. But if the Russian soul requires a celebration of life with festivities and fireworks, it is better to choose Baku. Where to improve health?

Azerbaijan is famous for its health resorts. Consider the best of them.

Lankaran

Sea tour in Azerbaijan reviews on the restoration of health through thermal waters is positive. There are thermal springs not far from the city of Lankaran, in the Gaftoninsky forests. In the city itself there are several resorts that specialize in various diseases of the musculoskeletal system, nervous and cardiovascular systems, as well as the restoration of immunity.

Lankaran stands on the swampy coast of the Caspian Sea. As archaeological excavations prove, people settled here already in the Bronze Age, in other words, 2-3 thousand years before the advent of our era. The city itself arose already in the 10th century AD.

In addition to medical procedures, it is worth taking a closer look at the beauty of the city. Many very well-preserved attractions date back to the 8th century. Particular attention should be paid to the Lankaran fortress and Khanig.

Lankaran fortress

It is a round structure with high stone walls topped with rows of sharp teeth. Previously, a couple of bazaars were located in the fortress, but over time they were abolished.

Haniga

Behind the fortress wall are several mosques and mausoleums that have become famous for their magnificent architectural decoration - beautiful stone carvings. The main buildings of Hanigi were erected in the XI-XIV centuries, but over time they were repeatedly rebuilt. A mosque is located next to Haniga, in which is located the tomb of Pir-Hussein. The mihrab is a great pride of the mosque - a niche in the wall, consisting of two columns and an arch, decorated with kufic inscriptions and in an ornament of multi-colored tiles.





Naftalan

Holidays at sea in Azerbaijan reviews about treatment in Naftalan are extremely enthusiastic. Naftalan rightfully bears the title of the most unusual medical resort in the world. He gained his popularity thanks to the deposits of "liquid black gold", which can cure many diseases. Naphthalan baths and smears are used to treat more than 70 types of various diseases of the body: musculoskeletal tissue, nervous system, improve the condition of the skin of the body and face, alleviate gynecological and urological diseases. Naphthalan also helps with inflammatory processes, is able to alleviate pain, improve blood circulation, and is also an excellent stimulator of metabolic processes in the body. The patient, depending on the severity of the disease, prescribes from 10 to 15 baths. There should be rest periods between the procedures, so the entire course of treatment is about 20 days. The patient is immersed for 10 minutes in a bath filled with oil, the temperature of which is in the range 36-38 0 . Through the enlarged pores from heat, oil partially penetrates into the blood, then to leave the body along with toxins and toxins. Such a vacation in the Caspian is not only useful, but also unique.

Historical culture: welcome to history

Those who do not like to just lie on the beach will be happy to explore the ancient sights of the country. We list the main ones.

Shamakhi

Holidays in the Caspian are a great opportunity to expand your knowledge, get acquainted with the ancient culture and traditions of the country.

Archaeological excavations have shown that in the northwestern part of the city there used to be a settlement whose age dates back to the 4th-5th centuries BC. e.

In 1222, Shemakhia was in a long siege, but could not resist for a long time, so the Tatar-Mongol troops captured and destroyed the fortress.

The location of the city (it was on the intersection of several roads) contributed to the fact that for a very long time Shemakhia was attacked by its neighbors. They constantly robbed and destroyed the city.

Later, the Great Silk Road began to run through the city. And all because in Shamakhi there were schools of carpet weaving and miniatures. Silk was traded between representatives of different countries: Azerbaijan and Iran, Russia and India, as well as Arab, West European and Central Asian merchants.

The most famous building of Shemakha is the Gulistan fortress, erected in the XII century. On its territory, archaeologists have found several items whose age was determined by the IX century. Consequently, the fortress has more than 1000 years. For several centuries, the walls of the fortress defended the inhabitants from the attacks of the troops of the Mongols, Arabs and Ottomans. The main feature of the Gulistan fortress is the secret passage that leads from it to the gorge. Gulistan was used until the 16th century, but was subsequently destroyed by numerous earthquakes almost to the ground. Currently, it has remained from the picturesque ruins, which are located on the outskirts of the city.

Juma mosque

Built in 744 and is the oldest mosque in the Caucasus. During its construction, a unique architectural solution was applied: 3 halls for prayers, which were interconnected by wide openings.

Many wars and earthquakes led to the fact that the mosque had to be rebuilt several times. But during its reconstruction, the basic principles of construction were preserved: one central hall, located under a large dome, and two side ones with smaller domes. As before, each hall has its own separate entrance and mihrab.

Sheki

When buying tours to Azerbaijan in Sheki, you should know that this is one of the oldest cities in Transcaucasia. In the old days, Sheki was the capital of the Sheki Khanate, which was subsequently annexed to Russia in 1805. And with the death of the last of the khans, Ismail, in 1819 became the Transcaucasian province of Tsarist Russia. Like all resorts on the Caspian Sea, Sheki also boasts memorials, but there are not many of them.

Sheki Khans Palace

It was built in the 18th century. Window openings are decorated with openwork ligature, and on the walls are depicted images of Nizami.

The Telesen-Geresen fortress (‚Äúcome-see‚Äù) for a long time defended its inaccessible walls of the inhabitants from the attack of various enemies.

The ancient city of Sheki used to be a major shopping center. Here were located the caravanserais that have survived to this day: Lezgi, Isfahan, Tabriz, as well as hotels and inns. Silk was produced, which was famous in other countries. Today, silk factory, silk-weaving and silk-spinning factories are engaged in its production.

Due to numerous earthquakes, Sheki is not rich in historical monuments, but the city is still famous for the production of copper utensils, embroidery, embossing and jewelry. Famous silk scarves are loved by women all over the world.





Nakhichevan

The age of the city is estimated at almost five thousand years. And proof of this are cave paintings, letters, archaeological finds.

The cave paintings that have come down to our time can be seen today on the slope of Mount Gapydzhik. Ancient Azerbaijanis living at the foot of the mountain painted images of various animals on the stones: dogs, wolves, goats, deer, as well as symbols and fantastic images of people. Drawings were performed in a single version and in pairs. Nowadays, one can admire the preserved images of hunters with bows and dance couples that were knocked out by someone else's hand.

Mausoleum of Noah

There are many monuments in Azerbaijan, therefore, in order to carefully examine everything, it is better to travel to resorts on the Caspian Sea more than once.

The tomb of Noah belongs to the IX-XII centuries and is located in the southern part of the city, not far from the ancient fortress. According to legend, after a long voyage, the ark of Noah found its refuge at the top of the mountain Gapydzhik.





Turn around the soul: Baku city, Republic of Azerbaijan

Baku is the capital and one of the largest cities in the Caucasus. You can visit the capital all year round, the weather is favorable: dry and hot summers, cool winters with little rainfall. For aesthetic pleasure, you can stroll along the narrow ancient streets, explore the fountains and embankments, walk through the squares and ancient palaces. Beach vacations in Azerbaijan in Baku involve, in addition to sunbathing and sea bathing, diving, boat trips on the turquoise sea on scooters and water skiing. The city has 2 categories of beaches: free, on which there is not much sand, and paid, equipped, respectively, according to all the rules. Entrance to the second is free, but you have to pay for the sunbed. In addition, it is forbidden to bring food and drinks to the beach.





Azerbaijan on the map does not occupy a very large area, but this does not mean that there are no interesting places for every taste. In Baku, a large area is occupied by shopping centers and fashion stores. In shopping centers there are very good discounts, so when you go shopping, you can please yourself with inexpensive things and funny trinkets. Prices in Azerbaijan are quite high: a tourist spends about 110-130 dollars a day, and for a low quality of service this is quite a lot. Of course, you can save money, but for this you will have to rent housing on the outskirts of Baku, where public services leave much to be desired.
