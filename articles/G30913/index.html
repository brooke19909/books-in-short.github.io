Hotel Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort 4 *: overview, description, features and reviews

Hotel Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort 4 * is a unique resort complex, which can be considered a true paradise for golf lovers. It is impossible not to say about architecture, thanks to which you can get to know the Arab traditions closer.

Briefly about the hotel

Hotel Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort 4 * is the perfect combination of beautiful architecture, quality service, many opportunities for relaxation and the relatively affordable cost of the tour. The territory impresses with oriental luxury. Blue pools, exotic palm trees, as well as exquisite decor - all this creates an unforgettable atmosphere.

Hotel rooms deserve special attention. They are very spacious and bright. They are made in a rather modest and concise, but at the same time refined design. Some apartments offer views of the Persian Gulf.

Hotel Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort 4 * is characterized by a developed resort infrastructure. Guests are available and sports entertainment, and wellness treatments, and many gastronomic pleasures in the restaurants of this resort complex.





Hotel location

Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort 4 * is located in some distance from large settlements. So, Dubai is located about 75 km, and Ras al-Khaimah - about 25 km from this resort. Nearby (about 400 m) there is another large hotel, a visit to the territory of which is also available for guests of this complex. The hotel is located between a large golf course and a luxurious sandy beach.





Rooms

Hotel Al Hamra Village Golf And Beach Resort 4 * (United Arab Emirates) has 219 rooms in the following categories:

  • Junior Suite is a spacious elegant apartment consisting of a bedroom and a living room. Guests can sit both on the large bed and on a folding soft sofa. An important feature of the room is the terrace with private access to the pool and garden. It offers views of the green, well-kept golf course.
  • Standard rooms are small in size, but very comfortable rooms, designed to accommodate two people (an extra bed is possible by prior arrangement). The room does not have a balcony or terrace, but this is offset by a chic view from the window, which can overlook the garden, pool or golf course.
  • Classic room - in terms of area, decoration and capacity, these apartments are absolutely identical to the standard category. Perhaps the only and most important difference between the two is the presence of a balcony.
  • The suite is a small room, which comfortably accommodates two people in a large king size bed or two single beds. There is a private balcony. It offers views of the picturesque grounds of the neighboring hotel.




Room Amenities

Hotel Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort 4 * offers its guests accommodation in comfortable rooms, each of which is equipped with a full range of all necessary amenities, among which are the following:

  • individual air conditioning and air temperature regulation;
  • orthopedic beds;
  • a minibar, which is replenished daily with refreshing drinks;
  • TV with satellite antenna connection (catches a signal of several Russian channels);
  • safe with electronic combination lock;
  • international telephone line;
  • a bathroom, which is equipped with both a bathtub and a modern built-in shower;
  • each room has a hairdryer;
  • Wi-Fi connection available
  • an extra adult or baby bed can be provided upon prior request;
  • at your request, a fresh local news newspaper will be delivered to your room daily.




Infrastructure

Hotel Al Hamra Village Golf Resort (Ras al-Khaimah, United Arab Emirates) has a modern developed resort infrastructure, so that the vacation of tourists becomes full and most comfortable. The guests are presented with:

  • the ability to travel to the territory of a neighboring hotel on a modern environmentally friendly electric car;
  • A spacious conference room with everything you need for business meetings or meetings. At your request, the room can be converted into a formal banquet;
  • parking area, as well as several guarded garages;
  • laundry and dry cleaning;
  • shops with groceries, souvenirs, clothes and jewelry;
  • a large fresh water pool, which is heated to a comfortable temperature in cold weather;
  • several tennis courts;
  • spacious golf course;
  • Wellness center with sauna, bathhouse, jacuzzi, massage and beauty parlor;
  • a beauty salon, where skilled masters of manicure, pedicure and hairdressing work;
  • billiard club;
  • diving school;
  • many restaurants and bars;
  • private beach, the coastline of which is 1 km long;
  • free buses to Dubai twice a week;
  • 24 hour reception.




Services for children

In addition to adults who are accustomed to the luxury of tourists, the Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Ras al-Khaimah) annually has a lot of children who also need well-developed infrastructure. The following opportunities and services are provided to the youngest tourists:

  • the pool has a special children's compartment with entertainment and slides;
  • a mini-club is opened for kids, where children's animators work, as well as babysitters, so you can safely leave your child here and devote time to yourself;
  • the restaurant will offer children a separate menu;
  • upon prior request, you can order individual babysitting services for the child;
  • so that children in the restaurant are comfortable, there are special high chairs and chairs;
  • In the garden of the hotel there is a large playground with several play areas;
  • The children's animation team has developed an interesting entertainment program that is updated every day.

Hotel Restaurants and Bars

Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort (Ras-al-Khaimah) is famous for its catering establishments, which employ the best chefs from around the world. So, guests of this resort complex are recommended to visit the following restaurants and bars that operate on its territory:

  • The Village Restaurant - This is the main restaurant, which provides three meals a day for hotel guests. The menu is simply amazing with the abundance and variety of dishes that belong to the most diverse cuisines of the world. The main feature of this restaurant is a spacious outdoor terrace, thanks to which you can enjoy al fresco dining while admiring the scenic views.
  • La Cabana Beach Restauran is an original restaurant that is located on the beach, right by the sea. He specializes in meat dishes (namely, steaks), as well as in all kinds of salads: from light diet to high-calorie ones. A feature of the institution can be considered theme nights, which are held on Thursdays.
  • Molly Malone Pub is a cozy pub made in the best of Irish traditions. Here you can not only enjoy a variety of beers, but also try traditional, original snacks.
  • Club Malibu is a modern nightclub where entertainment with dancing and music is held every evening. This place is very popular due to the remoteness of the hotel from major entertainment centers.

Positive reviews

Many tourists are eager to get to the Al Hamra Village Golf & Beach Resort in Ras Al Khaimah. Reviews about this resort complex are full of thanks and positive impressions:

  • quick check-in regardless of the time of arrival (if available rooms);
  • after receiving the keys to the guest room, the hotel employee takes the hotel to an electric car, which eliminates a lot of inconvenience with luggage and orientation on the ground;
  • every morning, maids bring potable water, as well as coffee, tea and sugar;
  • the cleaning schedule is agreed with the maids individually;
  • high-quality wooden furniture made in a traditional Arabic style;
  • not far from the hotel there is a water park;
  • Russian-speaking staff at the reception;
  • a beautiful pool, around which there are many palm trees;
  • a large number of sunbeds both on the beach and near the pool;
  • a good gym with a large number of simulators, as well as with the opportunity to get advice from an instructor;
  • relatively low cost of the tour;
  • Beautiful, clean, well-kept beach.




Comments and complaints about the hotel

Despite its solid status and high-quality service, tourists have some comments on the Al Hamra Village Golf And Beach Resort 4 * hotel in Ras Al Khaimah, namely:

  • since it is located next to the road, in some rooms it is quite noisy;
  • there is no natural meat in the breakfast menu (only sausages);
  • almost no entertainment;
  • the hotel is located far from the sea (a quick step will take at least 15 minutes);
  • there are a lot of cars in the territory;
  • insufficient amount of greenery;
  • poor soundproofing in the rooms;
  • linen and towels are quite old, which is why they have a characteristic gray color and scuffs;
  • despite the fact that the hotel provides a free shuttle service to Dubai, in fact, it is not possible to use this service, since the recording is several weeks in advance;
  • old (and sometimes faulty) plumbing.
