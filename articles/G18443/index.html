Hotel Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort SPA 5 *: description, rating, reviews, photos

Luxurious luxury hotel Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort & SPA 5 *, which was opened in November 2015, impressed guests with its richness and beauty from the first minutes. If you dream of an unforgettable vacation, hospitality of the East and first-class service, then this option is suitable for you.

The Emirate of Sharjah is the third largest territorial district in the UAE and the only one with a spacious coastline in the Gulf of Oman and the Arabian Gulf at the same time. All tourists and vacationers can admire the magnificent and majestic mosques, historical and cultural heritage of the country, as well as traditional oriental markets and bazaars.

The distance to the airport is 16 km, but in a comfortable transfer the road does not seem burdensome to you, especially since the window is a deserted, but very picturesque desert. restaurants and bars

Of course, a first-class hotel with five stars should be perfect in everything, including in terms of gastronomy. After all, it is here that guests enjoy traditional Arabic cuisine and immerse themselves in the atmosphere of the fabulous East. In the hotel you will find several bars and restaurants for every taste. The main one is Al Qubtan, specializing in international cuisine. Gusti restaurant offers its visitors still amazing sweets and pastries, a variety of cakes and pastries, and cookies and chocolate will satisfy the taste of the most fastidious sweet tooth. For traditional oriental cuisine, head to Arjwan, and seafood and fish to Marasea.

In addition, for all guests of the Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort & SPA 5 * hotel (Sharjah) there is a bar near the pool with soft drinks and non-alcoholic cocktails, as well as Link Sheraton cafe, where you can not only enjoy delicious coffee or tea, but also use a computer to work or entertainment and communication.

Sport, beauty and health

The hotel has one of the best salons in the region and the largest (on two floors, its area is 1100 m 2 ) - Shine SPA. Make your vacation unforgettable and be sure to take the time to visit it to relax in the jacuzzi, sauna or steam room, Turkish hammam. The salon uses for its customers only the best and natural products that beneficially affect the condition of not only the body, but also the soul.

To stay in good physical shape even on vacation will help the sports center, working around the clock. However, one of the main advantages that the Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort & SPA 5 * hotel has is its huge pools. Two of them are in the open air, the water surface area is 500 m 2 and 250 m 2 . This allows you to accommodate all hotel guests without creating difficulties and inconveniences for them. Umbrellas, mattresses, sun loungers and towels by the pools are absolutely free.





The hotel is focused more on resort holidays, however, those who came here on a working visit will also be pleasantly surprised. The complex has eleven conference rooms of various sizes and with a capacity of 20 to 200 people.

Facilities for children

Traveling with children should be comfortable, for this you should carefully select the option of accommodation. Note that the Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort & SPA 5 * resort has all the amenities needed for younger guests. At the request of parents, children up to three years old can be provided with a cot in the room, as well as children's menus and highchairs in restaurants at no extra charge.

On the territory for children there is a special pool, an indoor playground for games and entertainment, as well as a club (for children from 3 to 11 years old). Specially trained and professional staff will surely find something to occupy even the most capricious visitors, while adults can devote a little time to themselves. A babysitting service can be provided upon request and at a surcharge.





Wedding organization

Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort & SPA 5 * hotel, reviews of which are mostly positive, offers an exclusive service - organization and holding of weddings. Here you will get the perfect place and conditions for the most important event in life. The resort complex offers for this two spacious rooms with high ceilings and their own lobby, from where you can directly get into the wedding room for the newlyweds. It has all the necessary audio and video equipment, luxurious design. Upon request, the chefs of the hotel restaurants can plan a banquet, surprise guests with the most exquisite dishes of traditional Oriental and European cuisine.
