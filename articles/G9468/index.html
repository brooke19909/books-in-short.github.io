A pager is a receiver for a personal call. It is made in such a way that it makes it possible to receive small text messages using the paging network. The first pager model was launched in 1956 by Motorolla. He immediately gained popularity among couriers, taxi drivers, nurses, in other words, among those people who, on duty, should always be in touch.





What is a pager and what is its history

The most popular pagers were in our country in the nineties. But due to the universal availability and proliferation of mobile phones, these devices have lost demand and have practically disappeared from the lives of citizens.

How does a classic pager work?

To send a message, you need to call the operator, call the subscriber number and dictate a text message. It is possible to notify several subscribers at once during group forwarding. With a modern pager, you can send messages to your mobile phone or email. If you wish, you can organize a hotline or receive alerts from different points. In general, today there are extensive opportunities for using a pager.

Where are these devices used today?

There is the possibility of using this device in different situations. In addition, a pager is a gadget whose price is much lower than the cost of a mobile phone. Outwardly, it looks like a rectangular box with a pair of buttons. Learning to manage it will be easy for everyone. Due to the fact that pagers have long gone out of fashion, it is possible to purchase them through private advertisements or paging network operators.





What other areas of use are known

In addition, today the meaning of the word "pager" is somewhat expanded and involves various functions.

So, in alarm systems, certain device models are used. For example, a GSM pager functions so that when the alarm is turned on, a small text message is sent to it. This is convenient, as time is given to take measures to prevent theft or allows you to save the contents in a safe.





The small size and convenience of the pager make it a practical electronic device. It has resistance to shock and mechanical stress. Using the pager in accordance with the instructions guarantees a long service life.
