Sanya resort town in China: description, tourist season, beaches, hotels, reviews

The resort town of Sanya is the tourist center of Hainan. The region has a well-developed infrastructure, tropical climate and good beaches. The resort welcomes guests throughout the year. Let's see what is attractive for tourists resort Sanya in China?

A bit about the resort region

Sanya resort (China) is attractive for vacationers for many reasons. All categories of tourists will like to relax here. A rich underwater world, clear water - ideal conditions for diving. A large selection of hotels allows you to choose accommodation to your liking. Lush tropical vegetation creates stunning conditions for relaxation. Sanya resort in China is a great place to relax for Russians. Our compatriots are always welcome here. In many places you can see signs with inscriptions in Russian.

Sanya is a resort located on Hainan Island. The population of the city is more than 600,000 people. For China, this is a modest figure, but for the island it is quite decent. The resort is convenient in that it has its own international airport. Therefore, getting to the place of rest is not at all difficult. The airport is called Sanya Phoenix. It is located just 15 kilometers from the city. This is very convenient, since the road to any hotel takes a few minutes. But at the same time, airplanes will regularly fly over your head on vacation.

Climatic conditions

The resort of Sanya (China), or rather the island of Hainan, is sometimes called "Eastern Hawaii", because they are located at the same latitude. The island is washed by the waters of the South China Sea. Here, more than 300 days a year, sunny and clear weather reigns, so the resort receives tourists year-round. The main feature of the region is that it is the only place in China with a tropical climate. The average air temperature in the resort throughout the year ranges from + 24 ... + 26 degrees. The hottest months on the island are August and July. The temperature during this period reaches +29 degrees. The summer months are hot enough. If you do not like high temperatures, it is better to choose another time to relax. The golden period for a holiday in the resort of Sanya (China) is considered to be November-May. With typhoons in the region, the island is an ideal place for windsurfing.

When it is better to go to the resort of Sanya (China), it depends on your preferences and goals of relaxation.

Weather in Dudanghai Bay

At Sanya Resort in China, summer reigns throughout the year. However, the high season in Dudanhai Bay lasts from November to May. There is no bad weather at this time. In winter there is no rain, but the temperature ranges from + 20 ... + 25 degrees. At night, the temperature can drop to +15 degrees. Many tourists consider summer not the best time to relax in Dudanhai Bay, as it often rains and the temperature reaches +35 degrees. Even the sea warms up to +30 degrees.

Yalongwan Bay

Yalongwan Bay is considered the most expensive and respectable place to stay in the resort of Sanya on the island of Hainan (China). Its coast stretches for 7.5 km and has the shape of a crescent. Local beaches are sandy. And the clear water off the coast in sunny weather becomes turquoise. The bottom is visible at a depth of ten meters. Since there are coral reefs, the conditions for diving and snorkeling are just perfect.

The beaches of Yalongwan Bay are open to visitors at any time of the day, unlike the coast of Dadonghai. The width of the coast reaches 30-50 meters. There is shallow water, convenient for families with children. Sanya resort in China, including the coast of Yalongwan Bay, is family-friendly. Local beaches are good even in winter, as the water temperature here never drops below +21 degrees. The best time to visit the resort is November-March.

The coast has a developed infrastructure and is equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay. The swimming area is fenced off by buoys, for which it is forbidden to swim. Rescue workers are always working on the coast, who are watching the tourists vigilantly. Beach accessories are available to everyone.

There are cafes and restaurants along the coast, however, the prices are much higher than in the city center or in Dadonghai. Behind them stands a green park area with lush tropical vegetation. Tall trees form a shady alley through which it is very pleasant to walk in hot weather. There are also children's carousels and a rope park.

The best time to relax in the resort

The holiday season in the resort of Sanya in China continues throughout the year, due to its favorable geographical position. No wonder that the region is considered the main health resort of the country. Despite the fact that the island is warm at any time of the year, they still emit high and low season. What time is better to relax, it's up to you. The first surge of tourist activity in the resort begins in late September. High season lasts until May. At this time, a lot of tourists come to the island, and prices for accommodation and food, of course, are skyrocketing.

If you want to save, then you can buy a summer tour. Due to rain and heat, few tourists come to the resort, so the cost of trips is reduced, as evidenced by numerous reviews. Sanya (China) is a great place to relax. However, experienced travelers do not recommend coming here in February. The fact is that at this time, local residents celebrate the New Year. Holidays last long enough, so many institutions do not work for a long time, which affects the quality of rest.

Our compatriots often prefer to spend holidays away from their homeland. Therefore, they leave for Sanya in January. The weather at this time in the resort is cold by the standards of the island. But for us it is quite acceptable for relaxation. January is a good time for a vacation if you want to not only swim, but also see the sights of the region. There is no heat and rain at this time. In the middle of winter, swimming is quite possible, but in the daytime, since it can be a bit cool in the morning and evening hours, the air temperature drops to + 14 ... + 16 degrees. Another feature of the rest in December and January is the cold currents. In general, at this time, the swimming season is unstable.

Experienced travelers believe that March is the best time to relax at the resort. It is at this time that the most comfortable temperature is observed. In the daytime, moderate warmth pleases guests, which favors sightseeing trips, and in the evening pleasant coolness comes. The sea in March warms up to +28 degrees, creating the best conditions for a beach holiday.

Do not forget that the island is not just a resort, but also a health resort. Very often people come to Sanya not only for the sake of the sea, but also for healing. The island has thermal springs that treat gastrointestinal ailments, rheumatism, osteochondrosis and skin diseases. In addition, the climatic conditions of the region have a beneficial effect on the health of people with chronic problems. If you want to take a wellness course of procedures, it is better to come to Sanya in the winter.

In general, everyone chooses the best time to relax for himself, depending on the purpose of the trip and financial opportunities.
