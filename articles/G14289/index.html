Hotel

Hotel Eden Beach Resort Hotel 4 * consists of two buildings. One of them is closer to the beach and near the road, the other is at a distance, to go to it for about five minutes, climbing the stairs. In the second building, the sea is visible only from the upper floors, but there it is quieter, since it is removed from the highway.

The residential buildings of the hotel complex delight the eye with the design of public areas. Their modern style, decoration made of light artificial stone of noble pastel yellow color, arches, columns, matte stylish lighting look very harmonious. Tourists, relaxing in such an aesthetically pleasing environment, feel like welcome guests.

The hotel has long been chosen by vacationers from Western Europe. Tours here are bought mainly by the French, as well as many Russians and Poles. It is better to rent rooms on the upper floors, solely because of the contemplation of sea sunsets: in the Mediterranean Sea this is a real show that should be seen with a glass of wine and in a pleasant company.

Infrastructure

The hotel has a small shop (more precisely, a kiosk), free parking for vehicles, currency exchange, laundry.

On the contrary, across the road, there is a bus stop, almost all guests use it. For food, it is better to go to the city of Saronida, which is two kilometers away, where the prices in the supermarket are much lower. On excursions, tourists go to Athens.

In the hotel you can spend your free time relaxing by the pool. There is an opportunity to play billiards. There are also table tennis tables. Wi-Fi works well in the hotel complex, and tourists do not feel deprived of the usual amenities.

The hotel is not a five-star hotel, so children's animation is not regular, there is no mini-club. But for families with children, a cot is provided free of charge. In addition, near the pool area there is a playground with slides, carousels and a games room.

The hotel has long been chosen by financial and commercial companies as a venue for conferences and symposia. For business travelers, the hotel complex has a conference center with four rooms, each of which is designed for 200 people.

Rooms

Eden Beach Resort Hotel 4 * has 286 rooms with sea and garden views. Among them are the budget Comfort, Economy Superior and luxury suites.

The rooms are compact but comfortable. They are furnished with two single beds, a pair of armchairs, chairs, a desk and a coffee table, a wardrobe, bedside tables. Of the appliances they have air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, hairdryer, telephone. There is also a safe and clothes dryer.





The design of budget rooms is dominated by two colors: white (walls) and light gray (floor, upholstered furniture, curtains). The color scheme of economy rooms is orange-pastel, they are one and a half times more spacious. The deluxe rooms have a solid Euro renovation and are furnished with solid wood furniture with leather.

The hotel bathrooms are compact enough, the plumbing installed in them is solid. Shampoos and gels are regularly updated by staff. Rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen and towels are replaced every other day.

On the balconies there is a table with a pair of chairs. It is substantial on the Mediterranean coast. In the evenings, sitting on the balcony, it is pleasant to contemplate the sunset, sipping the local aromatic wine.

The water of the Saronic Gulf is wonderful: blue and clear. When leaving land for it (2-3 meters), the feet pleasantly massage small pebbles, and then the classic sandy beach begins.

Nutrition

Dining guests Eden Beach Resort Hotel Anavyssos 4 * provide the main restaurant, two restaurants by appointment and two bars. In the restaurant itself, the guests are very worthy of service: the waiters are quick and polite, tablecloths on the tables are always clean and starched, plates are neatly folded and cutlery is laid out. Tourists impose their own food, choosing what they like from the buffet.

Two or three meat dishes and 1-2 fish dishes are prepared for lunch and dinner. As side dishes cook rice, potatoes, pastas, smoked vegetables. The meat is diverse: pork, beef, turkey, lamb, chicken. The restaurant has wonderful risotto and kebab.





Breakfast is light: scrambled eggs, cheeses, sausages, fresh orange, muffins, croissants, granola. Porridge is rarely cooked.

For dinner as part of the all-inclusive for dinner, you can take a bottle of Sprite. In general, guests positively evaluate the work of the Eden Beach Resort Hotel 4 * restaurant.
