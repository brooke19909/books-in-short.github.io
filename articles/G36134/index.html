Friendship Park Tambov: what to do in winter and summer

Friendship Park in Tambov is one of the most beloved vacation spots of citizens. This colorful corner of wildlife is located on the banks of the Tsna River, in a forest park. Here you can relax from the hustle and bustle of the city as a big noisy company, and in a close family circle.





Where is the Friendship Park in Tambov?

This picturesque corner is located in the southern part of the city, very close to the housing estate. It is surrounded by the Tsna River and the Tambov Reservoir. The park is one of the most favorite places for walking.

The park is accessible by public transport. You can do this on any bus passing along Sovetskaya Street, it is one of the central ones. You need to get off at the Pervomaiskaya stop. Then walk on foot, not more than ten minutes, to the Embankment, to the Thesikov bridge. What to do in the summer?

Finding something to your liking in the summertime in the park will not be any problem. Nature lovers and those who just want to relax from the bustle of the city can walk around the park itself, go down to the pond and enjoy the silence of nature. For mothers with children in the park there is an equipped playground.

But lovers of outdoor activities can ride bicycles or roller skates. For this, special tracks were built here. By the way, to make morning or evening runs on them is also very convenient. All tracks are well lit.

For those who do not have their own bicycle, a bicycle rental is open in the park. It works from morning to late evening, and prices will suit absolutely everyone.

In addition, the park has a small football field, tennis court and volleyball court.

And if you go into the depths of the park, then you can fry the meat on coals with a fun company, swim in the pond.

The Tambov Friendship Park recently opened the People's Museum. It is in the open. In general, any visitor will certainly not be bored.

Winter fun

Everyone understands that in the summer park of Tambov's "Friendship" there is always something to do. But what about winter? What can you see and what to do in the cold season?

Even in the coldest season in this place you can find entertainment for yourself. Not so long ago, a modern ski base was opened in the Tambov Friendship Park. It is equipped with the latest technology. Anyone can come here and go skiing on specially equipped paths.

You can come to the park not only with your skis, but also rent them. You can ride in comfort even in the evening, when it starts to get dark. All ski tracks are lit by lanterns. Ski hire starts its work from 8.30 in the morning and runs until almost nine in the evening. Of course, during the holidays the schedule changes a bit.
