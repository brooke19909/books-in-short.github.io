Black Sea vacation. Guest houses (Novomikhailovsky)

Guest houses (GD) are increasingly competing with hotels in the spa business. Unlike a hotel, DGs are the property of the owners of such a business. Such activities flourish among the entrepreneurial locals of the resort regions. Guest houses often make a worthy competition to hotels, including the level of comfort and service. In fairness, it is worth noting that a similar format of relaxation, close to the usual way of life, is optimal for some tourists tied to a purely domestic form of recreation.

Briefly about the village

A similar business is successfully developing in the village of Novomikhailovsky, located in the northern part of the Tuapse district of the Krasnodar Territory. Here are the budget hotels and guest houses. Novomikhailovsky is located on the Black Sea coast off the southern slope of the Caucasus Range, 22 kilometers from Tuapse and 87 from Krasnodar.





The climate in Novomikhailovsky is resort: mild and humid, transitional from temperate to subtropical. The valley of the Nechepsuho River, where the village is located, is surrounded by picturesque mountains covered with forest. PGT is geographically extended along the river and almost all of its infrastructure is located on the embankment. There is a sufficient number of small shops selling food and everything you need. Guest house "Edelweiss"

Guest house "Edelweiss" (Novomikhailovsky) was built in a cozy spacious two-story house with terraces intertwined with grapes. The sea is within walking distance of the second line (8 - 10 minutes walk). Nearby is a shop and market.



It offers furnished double, triple and quadruple rooms. The territory is landscaped with paved stone terraces, lawns with manicured climbing plants, trees, shrubs.

Vacationers eat in gazebos aesthetically arranged by the hosts, located among the greenery and terraces, where food is brought from the nearby summer kitchen. The latter is a neat one-story building, built according to a specialized project.



The hotel rooms are decorated in a homely style and equipped with air conditioning, TV. Vacationers celebrate the peaceful atmosphere. Near a lot of greenery, there is a forest, other guest houses. Novomikhailovsky, according to vacationers, has obvious potential for the further development of the resort business.

Guest house "Lyudmila"

The development of the resort infrastructure of the village is clearly demonstrated by those DGs that exceed two-star hotels in terms of service. They are motivated to develop their infrastructure, which is noted in their reviews by their guests. One of such private mini-hotels is the guest house "Lyudmila" (Novomikhailovsky). This resort establishment does not resemble a rural residential building.





Everything is arranged according to the canons of the resort business. Modern rooms with good household appliances, a terrace with steps, equipped for meals, in an altanka with columns built according to the classical canons, aesthetically decorated.

A comfortable pool with two bowls and terraces for relaxation, equipped umbrellas and sun loungers. Wood Sauna. Solid, stone-built brazier. Parking for cars is also available at this State Duma, and it is guarded.

After reading the reviews of visitors, it seems that some guest houses (Novomikhailovsky) are more preferable than "average" budget hotels that do not leave much impression. It is gratifying that when contemplating their design, a solid design and design of the architect is visible.

Guest House Versailles

Another DG, which is not inferior in service to a budget hotel, is also in demand among holidaymakers. This is the guest house Versailles (Novomikhailovsky). In a solid, purpose-built four-story building, budget-class rooms are equipped.





The territory of the guest house is equipped with a swimming pool, terraces, a gazebo equipped for relaxing and eating. The rooms have a bathroom, which includes a bathroom and toilet. The furniture in them is selected in the same style, worn out, fresh linen. The rooms have televisions, air conditioning, refrigerators. There is a hairdryer. This DG is furnished according to the canons of a budget hotel.





Vacationers note a good, wholesome home food, adequate, caring, friendly attitude of the owners.

Conclusion

The guest houses (Novomikhailovsky) mentioned in this brief description are located in the largest Russian resort village of Tuapse district. This area is known to many generations of people because for many decades there has been a children's camp "Eaglet". The holiday season here lasts from June to the end of September, the beach is pebble. The main infrastructure (discos, restaurants, dance floors, parties on the coast) is located near the promenade, where expensive hotels are built.
