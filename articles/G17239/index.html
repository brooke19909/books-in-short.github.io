Hotel Borodino.

The four-star Borodino Hotel is located in close proximity to the Sokolniki metro station, which is why it is often called the Borodino Sokolniki. Many Moscow hotels located in close proximity to metro stations. The external architecture of the Borodino building is fully consistent with all the features of the construction of buildings in the business center of the capital.
When designing the building in which hotel "Borodino" , beautiful and effective solutions were used, for example: panoramic elevators, roof gardening, a light-flooded atrium and spacious beautiful halls. The hotel's decor is in the style of the classic Renaissance.

The number of rooms of the Borodino Sokolniki Hotel is two hundred and thirty rooms of various categories.
The interior of the rooms of the Studio category is made in strict red colors. The rooms have the following furniture: a comfortable sofa, a coffee table, several armchairs and chairs, hangers, a wardrobe, a mirror, and cabinets. It also has air conditioning, a safe, a telephone, a flat-screen TV, a minibar and free Wi-Fi internet access. The bathroom in the Studio is equipped with a spacious bathtub and shower, bidet and hairdryer. Each such room is equipped with noise canceling double-glazed windows and electronic locks on the doors. In total, the hotel has ten rooms of this category.
Two-room suite (three in total at the hotel) is also made in red. The price of such a room includes breakfast in the excellent restaurant of the Borodino Moscow Hotel, access to the pool and sauna, as well as a number of additional services.
The vast majority of rooms at Borodino are Economy and Standard rooms.
The pride of the hotel is its Presidential Room. Only one is available. Two-room "Presidential" is made in the royal golden-white color scheme. The furniture that the room has: a spacious bed, several nightstands at the head of the bed, sofa, coffee table, armchairs and chairs, a desk, a functional wardrobe.

The hotel has many restaurants.
Kutuzov Restaurant is a high-class establishment specializing in Russian cuisine. It is designed for one hundred and eighty places. A breakfast buffet is served here every morning, as well as business lunches, as needed. A wide selection of delicacies of fish and meat, cold and hot appetizers, hot dishes, fresh pastries and fruits will appeal to every capricious client.
Restaurant "Denis Davydov" is a more compact restaurant on site. Impeccable service and interior in the Renaissance style, traditional and original dishes - that is the emphasis on which many customers and visitors draw attention. The lobby bar called Barclay is a cozy lobby bar with a capacity of sixty seats. It is an ideal place for a business meeting and socializing.
