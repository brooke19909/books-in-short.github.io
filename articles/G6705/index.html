Private hotel "Acropolis", Divnomorskoe: description, reviews

Do you want to have a good rest on the Black Sea coast and enjoy the local hospitality? Then you should definitely visit a small village called Divnomorskoe. Hotels are available for every taste and budget, and due to the fact that this place itself is not very large, the road to the sea will not be long in any case.

Marvelous village on the sea

Located in the suburbs of the well-known resort city of Gelendzhik, the village of Divnomorskoye is a fairly visited place on the coast of the Krasnodar Territory.

The name of the village speaks for itself. In this wonderful and comfortable place, everything is created so that the relaxation of each guest who comes here becomes the best. A diverse infrastructure allows you to meet any needs of tourists: there are a variety of cafes and restaurants, shops, souvenir shops, tour agencies, an amusement park and even a water park.





In the village of Divnomorskoe, hotels are located mainly in its center, along the streets of Kirov and Lenin. Here are beautiful beaches covered with small pebbles. Water in the sea in the village is considered one of the cleanest in the region, due to which this resort is very popular among vacationers. A beautiful promenade with a snow-white fence stretched along the coast, somewhat reminiscent of the Gelendzhik embankment. It is pleasant to walk along it both in the afternoon and in the evening in the light of stars and lamps.

It is in the midst of all this beauty and wonderful air filled with the aromas of Pitsunda pine trees that the private hotel Acropolis (Divnomorskoe) is located, which can be a great place for any tourist to relax.

Acropolis near Gelendzhik

This hotel opens its doors to all comers on May 1 and continues to operate until October 15. Here, couples in love, families with children of all ages and groups of friends will be happy to meet.





Hotel "Acropolis" (Divnomorskoe) includes two residential buildings located in a fenced cozy area. Both of them are four-story modern buildings with very beautiful architecture. The hotel received its proud name from all the famous attractions of the city of Athens. In a small landscaped area, which has "Acropolis" (Divnomorskoe) there are various attractions that are suitable for guests of any age.

The hotel does not have its own beach, however, like most hotels and guest houses in Divnomorskoye. But to the beautiful city beach from the hotel to go just 5-7 minutes. On the way, guests will meet all kinds of shops and cafes.

We are looking for the "Acropolis" in Divnomorskoye

Hotel "Acropolis" (Divnomorskoe) is located at the intersection of Kirov and Black Sea. There are other guest houses nearby, but the inscription at the top of the building can unmistakably highlight the hotel of interest to tourists.

You can get to the "Acropolis" both by personal transport and by public transport. You must drive your own car along the M-4 federal highway through Krasnodar and Dzhubga, follow the directions for Divnomorsky, before reaching Gelendzhik. If tourists travel from Novorossiysk, then they will need to drive Gelendzhik and after it turn towards the village.

The fastest way to arrive at the Acropol Hotel (Divnomorskoye) will be by plane to the airport in Gelendzhik, and then a taxi or transfer ordered from the hotel itself. Travelers who like to travel by train can reach the railway station of Novorossiysk, and from there by public transport arrive in Divnomorskoye. Also in Gelendzhik there is a bus station where you can get by bus from different cities of Russia.

Hotel address and telephone

Tourists will not have any problems with arriving at the Acropolis Hotel if they write down their exact address. It is located in the village of Divnomorskoe on the street of the Black Sea, in the house 10 / 1a. If necessary, the hotel can be contacted by the following numbers: 8-86141-632-37 and 8-918-311-14-55.

Hotel Rooms

The rooms of the Acropolis Hotel (Divnomorskoe) are located in both buildings. Among them there are various options for standard, family rooms and junior suites. Any of them will offer guests all the necessary furniture for living, air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, equipped bathroom. All rooms located above the ground floor have balconies.

Standard rooms in the hotel have one-room and two-room. One-room is double and triple. Each has a double bed, a folding armchair, bedside tables, a built-in wardrobe, clothes dryer and a table with chairs. The double seat is an extra seat. Two-room standards are designed for four people. One room here is a living room with a sofa, the other a bedroom with a wide bed.





Junior suites are divided into double and triple, with additional space in each of them. They are equipped with a galoshnik, a wardrobe, a sofa and an armchair, an individual safe, a table with dishes and an electric kettle. The bathroom also has a hairdryer.

Family rooms consist of one or two rooms. Both types are designed for four guests in the main places, but in the two-room apartments an extra bed is provided. This category of rooms is equipped almost like a junior suite. However, they also have a dressing table, microwave and DVD player.

How much money do you need

The cost of living will pleasantly please all those who have chosen the seaside village of Divnomorskoye for their holidays. "Acropolis" set prices for its rooms depending on the period of stay in them.

So, the cheapest vacation time here is the whole of May. The cost of rooms at this time starts from 1500 rubles. for a double standard and reaches 3000 for any family room.





In the first half of June, the cost of standard rooms is 2200 rubles. for a double, 2800 rub. for triple and 3000 rubles. for two rooms. Junior suites will cost 2800 and 3300 rubles, and family rooms at 4100 rubles. The second half of June is more expensive: the price per day starts from 2800 rubles. in a standard double room and reaches 4700 rubles. in the family.

In the first half of July, you will have to pay 3000 rubles for rooms. for standard for two up to 5500 rubles. for family.

From July 15 to August 25, the most expensive season continues at the hotel:

- a double standard costs 3700 rubles., two-room - 5500 rubles .;

- a triple standard and a double junior suite will cost 4200 rubles .;

- junior suite for three costs 4,800 rubles .;

- family one-room - 6000 rubles., two-room - 6600 rubles.

From August 26 to September 9, the price range is as follows: from 2500 rubles. for a double standard up to 4300 rubles. for a family room. At the end of September and until the end of the hotel, the cost of living is 1700 rubles. in the cheapest standard room and 3300 rubles. in the family.

What guests of the Acropolis can use

Everyone who is relaxing at the Acropolis Hotel is provided with luggage storage services and free internet access. Tourists can use the local pool, children's play area, barbecue area and kitchen as much as they like, jump on trampolines and play board games available at the hotel. There is also a library, iron and ironing board. For children, discos are organized, and for the smallest, a cot is provided for free in the room.





For a fee, the hotel also has a sauna, tennis and billiards. You will also have to pay for parking the car.

Guest reviews about the Acropolis

All guests of the Acropol Hotel (Divnomorskoye) leave their comments about their vacation and impressions of their stay with pleasure. Tourists who have been here especially note that this place is perfect for children, as there is a lot of entertainment for them. Our compatriots like the friendly staff of the hotel and tidy rooms with good furniture.





Some were slightly upset by the balconies, which for the most part have exits from several rooms. It is also often quite noisy here, as a busy intersection is located nearby.
