Surin Beach (Phuket): advantages and disadvantages

Phuket is a large Thai island with a huge number of beaches. It is quite popular among Russians. Here, tourists are offered the most diverse vacation - unpredictable, extreme, measured family - any, but not boring. There are so many interesting places on the island that one visit is not enough to see everything.





Beach holidays in Phuket - the most popular. Most hotels are built directly on the beach, so often the choice of hotel primarily depends on which beach it is located on. Most of the coast is very clean and with convenient access to the water.

Phuket Holidays

The beaches of the island differ mainly in the number of hotels, as well as places where tourists can have fun. And already depends on the development of infrastructure is expensive or cheap vacation. Phuket's beaches are plentiful, but the eleven largest are the most popular among tourists. It was they who liked our compatriots. Among them are Patong, Kata, Karon, and, of course, Surin Beach. Phuket, reviews of staying on which almost everything is positive, is snow-white sand, emerald waves rolling on the shore, amazing nature, exotic food and hotels for every taste and affluence.

The best season for arriving in Phuket is considered the time from November to April. In these months there is the most favorable weather for relaxation, and the waves are not high. The cost of tours to the island depends on the season and, of course, on the chosen hotel. Here you can find a cheap accommodation option - for only 300 baht per day (about 440 rubles). But there are apartments, the cost of which starts with thirty thousand (about 49 thousand rubles).





According to experts, the best beaches of this Thai island are located on its west coast. They have developed infrastructure and convenient access to the water. And one of them is Surin Beach. Many people choose Phuket because of its highly developed tourist infrastructure. Here you can not only relax comfortably, but also see the local attractions that this exotic island is so rich in.

Description of "Surin"

Of course, Patong is considered the most popular, especially among young people. There are all the possibilities for entertainment. In Patong you can find a hotel for every taste and wealth.

In contrast, Surin Beach (Phuket) is a place where it is difficult to find cheap hotels. Located on the west coast, it is more suitable for high-income people. This is a fairly calm resort area, somewhat reminiscent of a small coastal village. Tourists are waiting for the purest white sand, which looks especially impressive against the background of the turquoise sea water. Where is

Surin Beach (Phuket), the advantages of which have been appreciated by our compatriots for a long time, is filled in the high season not only by lovers of swimming and sunbathing, but also by those who cannot imagine themselves without surfing, boogie boarding, etc. It is located on west coast of the island, half an hour from Patong towards the north. To get to it, you need to drive through Kamala, past the turn to Laem Singh Beach, at the fork with three roads, turn left. A few minutes later, the beach "Surin". Phuket is a place that many rich people have chosen for themselves. And one of the places where you can see the most luxurious villas is its west coast.

Benefits

The beach "Surin" has a length of a little less than a kilometer. Impressive stones are set in the water along its borders. It is quite wide, although in high season the beach, densely lined with sunbeds and umbrellas, does not seem spacious. The ebbs and flows in this part of the coast are not as pronounced as, for example, in the neighboring Bang Tao. Here the water does not go several tens of meters. Another advantage of Surin is sand. Depending on the illumination, its color changes from yellow to snow-white. Water in the sea is transparent azure. There is practically no silt. The combination of white sand, the emerald sea and bright umbrellas with frills fluttering in the wind, judging by the reviews, creates a kind of festive atmosphere.





Minuses

Many Europeans choose the Surin Beach (Phuket) for their holidays. The disadvantages of this resort area are few. Like many other beaches located on the west coast of Phuket, Surin also abounds with quite strong undercurrents, especially in the rainy season. Therefore, tourists have to be extremely careful when bathing from April to November. And of course, another disadvantage of Surin is its high cost.
