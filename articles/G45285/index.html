A bit about the hotel

Esperides Sofras Hotel Bungalows 3 * is one of 8 hotels that belong to the famous Esperides Sofras chain. The institution was opened back in 1989, and by 2010 a major reconstruction was completed. One of the main features of this hotel is the decoration in the traditional Greek style.

In one of the most popular resort areas is Esperides Sofras Hotel Bungalows 3 *. Greece, Thassos, Glykadi, 64004 - the address of this institution. It is only 3 km from the central part of Limenas. Thassos Main Seaport is just under 20 km away. Distance from the international airport - 220 km.

Guest rooms

Guests of Esperides Sofras Hotel Bungalows 3 * can enjoy a full and comfortable stay. The following numbers are offered for accommodation:

  • Standard double room accommodates 2 guests with the possibility of additional accommodation for 1 child. The room is simply decorated using natural materials. There is access to a furnished balcony with breathtaking sea views.
  • Double Premier is an improved standard room. Its capacity is the same as in the case of the previous category. The main difference is the elegant design. An additional advantage is the sea view from your own balcony.
  • Superior not only has an increased area, but also an elegant interior. The main feature is the designer forged four-poster bed. There is also a small sofa for relaxing. The balcony overlooks the sea coast.
  • Deluxe was loved by guests due to the spacious area and romantic design. The interior is dominated by natural wood and imitation silver. The large bed and folding sofas can accommodate 2 adults and 2 children.




Apartments

Tourists who are used to relaxing in luxury should pay attention to the Esperides Sofras Hotel Bungalows 3 * apartments. The photo shows a luxurious interior and competent layout. The following options are available for placement:

  • Classic apartments are designed for 2 people with the possibility of additional accommodation for 2 children. The room is a combination of a bedroom and a living room, which are combined in one living space. You can enjoy mountain views from your private furnished balcony.
  • Deluxe is a luxury themed apartment. The emphasis is on light colors, natural wood and forged decorative elements. The double bed is equipped with a canopy. There is also a couch with a coffee table and access to a private terrace with sun loungers.
  • Superior is a detached villa that can accommodate up to 4 guests, as well as additional guests. It has two separate bedrooms and a spacious living room with kitchen area. The combined bathroom is equipped with a bathtub and shower. Private terrace provides panoramic sea views.
  • The classic villa is decorated in calm pastel colors with bright accents. The bedroom has a large bed, as well as a soft sofa. There is a kitchen with a dining table and appliances. The terrace offers mountain views.
