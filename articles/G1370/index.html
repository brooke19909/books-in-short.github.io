"Gold Coast" (Odessa): description and infrastructure of the beach

Odessa is the most colorful city in Ukraine, and its main advantage is the ability to surprise. In the summer, trains and buses drop out of their doors many lovers of relaxation at sea.

There are a lot of beaches in this city, the most popular, but also loaded among them are those located closer to the center. If you leave the railway station on tram 18 towards the Bolshoy Fontan station and get to the very end of the Health Trail, you will see a beautiful promenade and beach with amazingly soft and clean sand. This is the "Golden Coast". Odessa, thanks to this place, is able to surprise travelers every time.

Beach description

If you get by tram or minibus (No. 215, 223) to station 16 "Bolshoy Fontan", you can see a picturesque promenade located parallel to it. Beautiful and well-kept flowerbeds, a large number of green spaces and comfortable benches, cleanliness and the blue expanse of the sea - all this makes the ‚ÄúGolden Coast‚Äù (Odessa) not only a stunningly attractive stretch of coast, but also a place where you can spend time in the evening.

This small beach always seems full of people, although in fact it is loaded much less than its more popular holiday destinations. This is due to the fact that you need to get to it from the city center for about 30 minutes by public transport. But the purest sand and water of an unusual blue color, which can not be found on other beaches, fully compensate for the time spent.





The guests and residents of the city are greeted by 2 proud snow-white stone lions, near which tourists like to be photographed. The embankment was recently restored, and now the beach "Golden Coast" (Odessa) looks like a cozy coastal corner in a European coastal city.

Beach infrastructure

In this place, everything is done for a comfortable stay.

  • On the way to the beach, tourists are met by numerous small stalls where you can buy bathing accessories and toys, funny T-shirts and ice cream, beer and shawarma, sparkling water and southern Ukrainian fruits.
  • For those who want to really eat, there is a cafe on the promenade with very low prices and a high level of service. Restaurant "Gold Coast" (Odessa, st. Gold Coast, 37) from 10.00 to 23.00 waiting for lovers of Mediterranean cuisine. Banquets and weddings are held here, for which a hall for 200 people is arranged in it. There is also a cozy bathhouse.



  • Here you can buy a city tour or sign up for scuba diving lessons, and for experienced drivers a visit to the underwater museum and video shooting is organized.
  • On the beach itself there are dressing rooms, a toilet and showers. You can rent a deck chair or umbrella.
  • A small section of the beach is allocated for children, where there are entertaining attractions and an inflatable slide.
  • Adults can enjoy beach volleyball, catamarans, you can rent a bike and explore the surroundings.

That's so plain, but sincerely meets its guests the beach "Golden Coast" (Odessa). A photo cannot convey the whiteness, purity of its sand and the blue of the sea, it is better to come and see everything with your own eyes.

Private sector

Travelers in this place are warmly welcomed by representatives of the private sector, who offer to rent a room or outhouse. To "soak" Odessa, it is worth living in its quiet streets, covered with dusted greenery.

In the private sector, you can find both a cozy separate summer kitchen and rent a room. Such a vacation by the sea will cost from 120 UAH per day, although there are offers to rent a whole house or cottage with a swimming pool, a recreation area and a barbecue, a sauna and a garage. Such options cost from 800 UAH / day.

This type of rest allows you to solve the issues of nutrition and pastime on your own, according to your taste and preferences.

Hotels of the "Gold Coast"

If you choose housing "Gold Coast" (Odessa), then in the immediate vicinity of the beach there are only 4 options:

  • Villa Pinia is an eco-hotel that combines SPA and beauty services, European and Ukrainian cuisine, 16 comfortable rooms, decorated with environmentally friendly materials.
  • Hotel Asiya is completely new (2016), but it has already conquered its first customers. It offers a sauna, massages, a terrace for sunbathing, a cozy garden and 8 rooms.
  • Hotel "Cozy" fully lives up to its name. In addition to comfortable rooms, decorated in a classic style, guests will find a sauna and a jacuzzi, massage and relaxation area.



  • Katran is located just 50 meters from the coast. There are 7 rooms decorated with wood and a restaurant, while there is a playground for children.

To relax on the beach "Golden Coast" (Odessa), you can pre-book a place in one of these hotels.
