How to connect threads when knitting: basic techniques

For needlewomen who are just starting to knit things that require a large amount of yarn, the question arises of how to connect the threads when knitting so as not to spoil the look of the product. I want the thing, the manufacture of which took a lot of time and effort, to look no worse than what was bought in the store. The need to introduce a new thread may arise if the skein is over or you need to use a different color.

For those who work crochet

The double crochet is crocheted to the point where two loops remain on the hook. A new thread is introduced through them. This method is used both at the beginning and in the middle of the row.





If the colors in the product will alternate in no more than four columns, you can not cut the thread, but stretch it inside the column, picking up the hook. If the distance is greater or contrasting colors were used, it is better to use a separate ball instead of a broach.

How to connect a thread when knitting

If it is necessary to make a connection at the beginning of a row, a new thread is introduced when knitting the edge loop of the previous one. The loop is performed with a double thread, at the beginning of the row it is removed in the usual way.

In the middle, a connection can be made in several ways:

  • Double twist knot. The first stage of tying is done in the usual way (as on shoe laces), and at the second, the thread is twisted twice. The knot turns out small and imperceptible, does not untie.




  • Felting (applies only to yarn made from natural materials). It is enough to take the two ends of the threads and rub them together in the palms of your hands.
  • Crosslinking with a tapestry needle.

To make a beautiful product, you need to know how to connect the threads. When knitting from yarn mixed with synthetics, the third method of joining will be optimal. It, like the second one, creates a subtle thickening, but is the most universal, since it allows you to connect any kind of yarn.

Tapestry Connection

This option is a bit more complicated than the previous ones, but it gives the most accurate and beautiful result.

  • The thread is inserted into the needle and slightly decompressed (for this you can twist it in the fingers in the direction opposite to twisting).
  • When the tension relaxes, a needle is inserted between the fibers and stretched so that a loop is formed.
  • The thread to be attached is threaded into this loop.
  • Repeat steps 1-2 with a new thread.
  • Gently tighten the hinges.




Color change

When working in different colors, several skeins of yarn are used, so it is important to know how to connect the threads when knitting.

If a different color is entered at the beginning of the row, you can knit the edge loops with two threads. The edge will be thicker than usual, but no less neat. The second option: do not remove the edge loop at the beginning of the row, but knit the front, but of a different color. You get a decorative effect. With this method, the edge loop on the other side of the web should be performed in the same way to eliminate the difference in tension.

In order not to use several balls or to think about how to connect the threads when knitting, if you have to often change color, you can make broaches. If necessary, you need to skip at least four loops, the second thread from the wrong side is interwoven with the working. This will avoid inconvenience when putting on things. If the colors in the product alternate through a large number of rows, you can cut the thread each time or guide it through the broaches between the penultimate and edge loops.

Knowing how to knit yarn when knitting without knots is necessary not only for professional knitters creating things for sale. It is useful to everyone who wants to create beautiful products.
