How to catch perch in winter

Fishing, no matter what alternative points of view are expressed, is, without a doubt, one of the most acceptable ways to spend your leisure time, primarily for men. The winter season is no exception, moreover, there is a whole galaxy of fishermen who activate their hobby precisely in this period of the year. At the same time, beginning fishermen often have a question about how to catch perch in winter, since, we can safely say, for beginners this is the most acceptable option, at least in the beginning.

If you talk about why it is best to choose perch for fishing for lovers who are just starting their way, then it is necessary to focus on the habits of fish. The fact is that in the case of biting there are practically no gatherings; specimens of this predator grab the bait sharply, boldly and without options. Although, as in any business, it is very useful to possess at least basic knowledge and skills when cutting perch. At the same time, it is worthwhile to understand that there are no classical technologies, and even more so, techniques in this business, the secrets of catching perch in winter for each fisherman have their own and vary depending on the traditions of one or another region of the country. However, there are general tips that all fishermen should consider.

The question of how to catch perch in winter should start with natural-climatic analytical calculations. First of all, it should be noted that, as practice shows, the most intense biting is in the first weeks after the formation of ice in the reservoir, and, accordingly, closer to spring or even in spring, when the pre-spawning zhor begins in the vast majority of fish in our reservoirs. In addition, during the winter itself, fish activity may depend on natural fluctuations in atmospheric pressure. It is clear that the longer the constant pressure will stand on time, the higher the probability of a rich catch.

Now a little about daily fluctuations. It is clear that perch, like any other fish, is most active in the morning, when it was just dawn. At the same time, the presence of a little frost is optimal, for the central zone of Russia it is no more than 10 degrees Celsius. But nevertheless, if the fisherman left the pond all day, with or without bite, you should still wait for the first twilight.
