How to color a craft

We already mentioned earlier: the described doll has a special sign - a beautiful chocolate tan. And experienced needlewomen know how to achieve it. But newcomers very often rush to the shops, unsuccessfully trying to find material of a suitable color. Therefore, in the presented master class for beginners ‚ÄúHow to sew a doll‚Äù, we are obliged to touch on this topic.

In order to make a real Tilda, you must:

  • to purchase a piece of snow-white fabric in the department of goods for needlework;
  • then go to the grocery store and find the cheapest instant coffee there (note that the price is a very important indicator: the lower it is, the more stable the color can be achieved);
  • now we return home and pour one liter of water into the basin (the temperature should be quite hot so that the coffee dissolves better);
  • add one tablespoon of ordinary salt to it;
  • and then pour two and a half tablespoons of coffee;
  • mix everything well, achieving complete dissolution of both components;
  • then we lower the fabric into the prepared solution: it is very important that it is completely in the water;
  • in any case, we do not leave the material, but constantly turn it over so that the color lies evenly, we continue similar manipulations for fifteen to twenty minutes (depending on the desired saturation of the doll‚Äôs skin tone);
  • after which we pull out the fabric and rinse it in cold water;
  • wring out, not much twisting;
  • and hang to dry, attaching clothespins to the edge, but only slightly.
