Hotel Melia Varadero Marina 5 *, Cuba, Varadero: description, reviews

Melia Varadero Marina 5 * is not just a hotel, it is a full-fledged luxury complex that will give you all the shades of pleasure available on vacation at the resort. This is that piece of paradise that has been lost on earth and is inviting real lovers of the sea, sea voyages and all kinds of adventures on the crest of the wave to visit. A family vacation in the territory of Melia Marina Varadero Hotel Apartments 5 * or the long-awaited honeymoon will remain in your memory not just a vivid memory, but perhaps one of the most unforgettable discoveries. I would like to return to such a hotel again and again, because every weekend will be filled with the brightest colors, and a new visit here will be completely different from the previous one.



Fairytale location

Melia Varadero Marina 5 * hotel can be called the most beautiful part of the sports port, near which it has spread. The proximity to a clean, well-kept beach is striking - only 300 m. This recreation complex is unique because of its location on the territory of the ecological reserve, which is why it is unrealistic to see many plants that guests can admire from the windows of their rooms in the rest of Varadero or even Cuba as a whole.

This is an ideal place to relax with children: they will be delighted with the opportunity to visit the dolphinarium every day, which is also located next to Melia Marina Varadero 5 *. Reviews of admiration from the companies of young people who come here on the weekend most often cause caves, which can also be easily reached on foot.

From the city of Matanzas you will be separated only 50 km, and getting there will not be a problem. Tourists from the hotel often come here to visit the Tropicana Matansas - a unique and inimitable cabaret, the performances in which have not left anyone indifferent. The nearest city is Varadero, and it is only 15 km away.



The Pearl of Melia Hotels International

Melia Marina Varadero 5 * hotel is considered not only one of the most beautiful hotels in the city of Varadero and the surrounding areas, but also one of the best and most colorful establishments of a huge group of hotels - Melia Hotels International. This is a fairly new complex for luxury recreation for tourists from all over the world, it was rebuilt and put into operation in the near 2013. But in such a short time, a network of regular customers has already been built up here, thanks to the feedback of which the flow of guests who want to relax here for the first time does not dry out.

Hotel rooms are located in several buildings, which belong either to the Vistamares zone, or to La Marina. Each zone has its own separate infrastructure.

Melia Varadero Marina 5 * is a fairly large hotel, which consists of 423 rooms of different classes and 348 apartments.

Talk about restaurants

Since Melia Marina Varadero is a rather large complex, 9 restaurants work immediately on its territory. Six of them are in the hotel with rooms. The extravaganza of cuisines is the most diverse: from the eccentric Caribbean to the beloved continental. The reviews also often mention that a table should be reserved in advance to visit exactly the restaurant that you like today.



Near the apartment there are three more restaurants, whose cuisine is Mexican-Argentinean with a marine accent.

How is it without entertainment?

In addition to a clean, modern pool, Melia Marina Varadero pleases its guests with the presence of a pool with a jacuzzi, it is a must to visit it. At your service - car rental, a chic banquet room, the opportunity to relax under an umbrella on the sunbeds. There is, of course, a modern gym with exercise equipment, each of which will be at your disposal. Sports activities that are available to you also include:

Interesting evening shows (different every evening) will make your sea adventure in Melia Marina Varadero truly unforgettable.

Comfortable stay with children

Since the vast majority of guests are couples with children, the administration of the complex made sure that small tourists are comfortable here.

A children's menu is available on site and is available on request.
