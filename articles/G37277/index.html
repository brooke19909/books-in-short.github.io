Hotel "Irtysh", Pavlodar: location, description of rooms, hotel infrastructure, photos and reviews

The Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar is rightfully considered one of the most popular. This is not surprising, because in this institution there is everything necessary for the rest of travelers to be comfortable and full. In addition to living in comfortable rooms, guests are provided with access to a host of additional services.

Address of the Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar

The institution in question is located only a kilometer from the banks of the Irtysh River at 79 Akademika Bekturova Street. It is about 4 km from the railway station and 16 km from the airport. A 10-minute walk from the hotel is a local attraction - Mashkhur Zhusup Mosque. Within walking distance are two large supermarkets, as well as several catering establishments.

Contacts of the Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar

If you are interested in information about accommodation and other hotel services, you can always ask questions by phone or e-mail. The telephone number of the Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar can be found on their official website, the email address is reception@irtysh-hotel.kz.

Contacts of other hotel services are also given on the official website of the organization.

Accommodation options

The Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar provides guests with comfortable living conditions. Here are the categories of rooms presented to your attention:

  • Standard room is 26 square meters. m is equipped with a pair of one and a half or one large bed. The room also has a coffee table and wardrobe. Most rooms have views of the Irtysh embankment and Victory Park.
  • Junior Suite 46 sq. M. m - a spacious studio. In addition to a large bed, the room also has upholstered furniture for relaxation and additional accommodation. Windows overlook the riverbank and Victory Park.
  • 56 sqm suite m is a spacious two-room suite consisting of a bedroom and a living room. There is a large bed, as well as a set of upholstered furniture for relaxation and additional accommodation. The room is designed for two guests with the possibility of sharing two children. The windows overlook the Irtysh and Victory Park.
  • Presidential Apartment 118 sqm m are distinguished by high functionality and stylish interior. In addition to the luxurious bedroom, the room has a living room, study, meeting room and two bathrooms.

Room Amenities

The rooms of the Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar provide everything you need for a comfortable stay for guests. Here are the amenities in question:

  • combined bathroom with shower or bath;
  • bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • shaving and makeup mirror with zoom function;
  • built-in hair dryer;
  • access to wireless internet;
  • TV;
  • cable TV;
  • landline phone;
  • air conditioning system;
  • small fridge;
  • cot for babies up to three years (on request).

Catering establishments

At the Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar you can always eat and enjoy your favorite drinks. This is possible thanks to such institutions:

  • The Nobel is a cozy restaurant serving European cuisine. The dining room will delight you with elegant surroundings and beautiful city views. In the hall with 50 seats you can eat, drink quality wine and other drinks. The facility is open from noon until 02:00.
  • The lobby bar is located on the ground floor of the hotel. In a cozy atmosphere, you can enjoy signature snacks, signature desserts, as well as a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. The institution is decorated in a restrained classic style and attracts with a relaxed atmosphere.

Breakfast

From 07:00 to 10:30 a buffet breakfast is served in the restaurant's dining room. The distribution presents the following assortment of dishes:

  • two types of milk porridges;
  • fried eggs or omelet with sausages;
  • cheese and sausage slices;
  • a wide range of cold and hot snacks;
  • fresh vegetables;
  • several types of sauces;
  • fruit salads;
  • a set of dairy products (cottage cheese, yogurt, kefir);
  • granola and instant breakfast cereals;
  • bakery products of own production (rolls, croissants, pancakes);
  • drinking water, juices and hot drinks.

Other hotel services

In addition to rooms and catering establishments, there is also other infrastructure at the Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar. The description is as follows:

  • Two banquet rooms with a capacity of 100 and 400 people. They are equipped with music and lighting equipment, as well as a projector. Can be decorated according to your desire.
  • Two conference rooms for 15 and 70 people for business events. The halls are equipped with projectors, televisions, microphones, and there is access to wireless Internet.
  • Gym with a dumbbell, a cardio zone, a punching bag and many other modern equipment.
  • The pool is 14 meters long and 1.5 meters deep.
  • Three saunas (male, female and infrared).
  • Spa "Ginger", where you can go through a lot of procedures for healing, rejuvenation and relaxation.

Positive reviews

Only from the reviews of tourists can you get objective information about the Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar. Photos and other information on the official website do not always give a complete picture. Here are some positive comments the institution has received:

  • Convenient location in close proximity to the embankment and Victory Park;
  • spacious rooms;
  • decent breakfast menu;
  • during breakfast, pleasant music plays in the dining room, which charges you with a good mood at the beginning of the day;
  • a good spa (massage deserves special attention, which is very professionally done by masters from Thailand);
  • within walking distance there is a large supermarket;
  • polite and competent reception staff;
  • in the cold season rooms are very well heated;
  • beautiful panoramic views of the city open from the windows of the restaurant, located on the eighth floor;
  • excellent gym with a lot of modern equipment;
  • good soundproofing in the rooms - neither neighbors' conversations, nor clatter in the hallway, nor street noise interfere with the rest;
  • well-trained and very polite waiters in a restaurant;
  • free drinking water in the room;
  • comfortable orthopedic mattresses on the beds, as well as good soft pillows (you can fully sleep without pain in the back and neck);
  • on each floor there are coolers with drinking water;
  • Room service is available around the clock.

Negative reviews

It would seem that everything necessary for a good rest is at the Irtysh Hotel in Pavlodar - a pool, a sauna, a spa, a restaurant ... Nevertheless, guests note a number of shortcomings and shortcomings. You can learn about them from such negative comments:

  • old TV with a small screen and poor image quality;
  • empty minibar in the room;
  • inflated prices in the hotel restaurant (and there are no worthy alternatives in the district);
  • the staff is very focused and unsmiling, and sometimes you want simple human communication;
  • there is a fountain in the hall, from which the room is very damp and there is the smell of stagnant water;
  • it takes a long time to drain the water in the shower before it starts to heat up;
  • there are no cosmetic accessories in the bathroom except soap;
  • unstable wireless internet;
  • free access to the gym and spa area is provided only for those guests who live in the new building (no one warns about this when booking);
  • if the hotel has few guests, instead of a buffet for breakfast they offer a limited set of products at the discretion of the administration;
  • not the best quality of cleaning the rooms;
  • high prices for accommodation;
  • the rooms are very dark - a little light penetrates through the windows, lighting fixtures also burn quite dimly;
  • there is no bin in the room (only in the bathroom, but it is very small);
  • at early check-in, guests are not fed breakfast (you have to pay separately);
  • noisy operation of the air conditioner (you have to turn it off at night to sleep well);
  • on the upper floors of the hotel is very poor water pressure;
  • heaters that are installed in rooms in the cold season are very noisy.
