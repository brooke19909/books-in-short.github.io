Hotel SM Resort 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): reviews and description

Holidays in Thailand leave many tourists with a very vivid and pleasant experience. Here you can see many amazing places, whether it be fabulous oases of wildlife or man-made sights. In most cases, the conditions of accommodation offered by local hotels will not disappoint guests. Choosing the right option, you should pay attention to SM Resort 3 *, located on the island of Phuket. However, making the final decision, it will be useful to find out what tourists who have been here before think about this establishment.

Location

Hotel SM Resort 3 * is located in one of the most beautiful cities of Phuket - Patong. It is located on the second line from the sea, which can be reached in a matter of minutes. Around a lot of picturesque landscapes. It will be nice to walk along the lovely streets, where there are many cafeterias, shops and massage parlors.



The distance to the hotel from the international airport is only 40 km. Guests are quickly delivered to their place of residence. If desired, tourists booking a tour at SM Resort 3 * can immediately book a transfer from the airport, which will cost a small amount.

Hotel Description

The hotel consists of one modern six-story building decorated in Thai style. It is located in a very quiet area, which allows tourists who come here to relax from the busy city life. On the sixth floor there is a terrace where vacationers can admire the beauty of the nearby mountain, which is considered the main attraction here.



Hotel SM Resort 3 * is located in a small area. But even they managed to make the existing small space pretty pretty. Near the hotel there is a small pool decorated with very beautiful figures and fountains. Its cleanliness is closely monitored, so guests will be pleased to swim here. If desired, guests can use the sun loungers and parasols, which are provided free of charge. A towel is available at reception.

The interior of the hotel is designed in Thai style. Everything is very beautifully decorated, decorated with many plants. Even walking along the corridors of the room, you can admire the beautiful paintings hung here.

Infrastructure

Guests are offered various services that will contribute to a comfortable and exciting vacation. The reception of the SM Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket, Patong) is ready to receive guests around the clock and solve their problems. Even with complete ignorance of the Russian language, all employees try to understand the guests and quickly deal with all issues. Upon arrival, guests will be offered the opportunity to leave valuables or bulky items in the storage room, for which no fee is provided.



The hotel offers tourists the opportunity to rent motorbikes. Since many different interesting places are located at a respectful distance, this service allows guests to easily reach them at any convenient time.

For an additional fee, tourists are offered their services by the SPA-salon, which operates at the hotel. Here you can order various cosmetic procedures that will refresh and rejuvenate your face. An excellent Thai massage, which is done by experienced specialists, will help to relax. Guests in the salon can also straighten their hairstyle.

The SM Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket) offers laundry or dry cleaning services for an additional fee. For a certain price, hotel guests can use for their own purposes a business center equipped with all the necessary facilities that will be needed during a business meeting or negotiations.

Description of rooms

Guests arriving at the SM Resort 3 * hotel are waiting for 50 well-equipped rooms. Based on their own preferences, tourists can settle in the apartments they like most.

All rooms are quite spacious, so guests will feel quite comfortable here. Furniture that does not have a new but very neat appearance will not disappoint. Each room has a computer connected to the global network, which allows vacationers to always be in touch with their loved ones. Wi-Fi is also available here.



In the rooms, the guests will not have to be exhausted from the heat, because there are installed powerful individual air conditioners, which can be configured according to personal preference. The minibar is always filled with various drinks. But guests should remember that there is an additional charge for used drinks.

The bathrooms are separated by glass partitions, which gives the rooms a special sophistication. They include a hairdryer and necessary toiletries. From water procedures, you can only take a shower.

Cleaners visit rooms daily, leaving everything in perfect cleanliness. True, some vacationers sometimes complain about the presence of garbage even after the cleaning just done. But such reviews are not very common.

Food quality

The hotel provides its guests with a tasty and hearty breakfast for free. The menu consists of 7-8 dishes that will appeal to the most demanding guests. Fruits, tea or coffee are served immediately. Many vacationers are pleased with the agility of the waiters, who quickly bring dishes, so that you do not have to waste time in painful waiting.



The hall of the main restaurant is quite cozy. Everywhere is very clean. You can eat on the terrace, arranged on the street.

Lunches and dinners served in the hotel restaurant will also not disappoint guests. Here you can give preference to dishes of European and Thai cuisine. The menu is very diverse, but the prices are a bit overpriced. In neighboring cafeterias you can have a meal much cheaper. Near the hotel there is a market where vacationers can purchase various products. But storing food in the rooms is not recommended, as the ants will quickly find the available food.

Beach vacation

Hotel guests can use the city beach, located 200 meters from SM Resort 3 *. Reviews of tourists confirm that this is a very beautiful place, bordered by beautiful tropical vegetation. Despite the fact that everyone is allowed access to the beach, it‚Äôs pretty clean and there are always free places. You can sit on sun loungers under umbrellas, the rental of which will cost a certain amount. Towels are provided free of charge at the reception desk before going to the sea.



The beach will delight guests with its singing white sands, which make interesting sounds when walking. The water near the shore is pretty clean, so you can swim enough here. It is convenient to enter the sea. The depth is located near the coast, which is very pleasing to fans of swimming.

Entertainment

The hotel SM Resort 3 * (Phuket) will not offer vacationers a huge selection of entertainment. Here guests can only play billiards, for which you will need to pay. But since there are so many fascinating places nearby, the main attraction for tourists is excursions, which can be booked at the bureau or sightseeing yourself.

Phuket Island has many waterfalls surrounded by lush vegetation. Visiting such places for a long time will be remembered by any person. No less exciting will be visiting the nearby islands, where you can also admire the beauty of tropical plants.



All tourists will be able to find a lot of interesting things for themselves in Patong: shopping fans will find various shops and markets here, history buffs will be interested to get acquainted with the cultural heritage of the city, and for those who want to have fun their services are offered by numerous nightclubs.

Hotel SM Resort 3 * (Phuket): traveler reviews

According to people who have already managed to visit the hotel, here you can spend your holidays in good conditions. The advantage of the institution is its convenient location. It will be very nice to stay in spacious and comfortable rooms. The smile and friendliness of the friendly staff will contribute to a good mood.

But, despite all the advantages, the hotel SM Resort 3 * (Phuket) has its drawbacks. Despite the fact that the institution is recommended for families, children here will not be very comfortable. The hotel itself does not provide entertainment for kids, and parents will have to organize their children's leisure activities on their own.
