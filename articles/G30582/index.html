Tax deduction for a child: what is it and to whom is it due?

Child tax deduction - what is it? Questions of this kind arise in many family citizens. The thing is that the presence of children in the cell of society allows us to use special forms of state support. For example, tax deductions. We will talk about them later. Who can qualify for these? How much government assistance is provided? And what do you need to know about her request? Answers to these questions will greatly facilitate the life of all modern parents. Especially for those who have the right to deduction for children. It is possible that the bulk of citizens are endowed with relevant rights.

Description

What is a tax deduction for a child? So in Russia it is customary to call a special bonus offered to family officially working citizens. It is made out only when there are children in the cell of society, including adopted children.

The tax deduction per child is a way to reduce the tax base when calculating personal income tax. A certain amount will be taken from the salary before tax. Due to this, the size of personal income tax will be slightly reduced.

Conditions for request

What is a tax deduction for a child? Some form of state support, allowing to reduce the income tax levied on salaries. True, it is not for everyone.

To be eligible for such a deduction, a person must:

  • have minor children;
  • possess citizenship of the Russian Federation;
  • be officially employed.

In addition, certain restrictions are imposed on the annual income of the applicant. But this issue should be given special attention.

Salary limit

Tax deduction for children up to what amount of earnings will be provided? The thing is that in Russia, as already mentioned, certain restrictions are imposed. Citizens receiving high incomes cannot apply for a standard tax deduction. This is simply not provided for by applicable law.

At the moment, the standard tax return "per child" is terminated if the applicant's annual income exceeds the mark of 350,000 rubles. Up to this point, both the mother and the father of the child can demand a reduction in the tax base when calculating personal income tax on salaries.

How much will be returned

The amount of tax deduction for children is another point that interests many citizens. As a rule, the demand for state aid is accompanied by paperwork. And not always done training justifies the expectations of man.

What is the size of the tax deduction for children in one case or another? The answer depends on the number of offspring brought up, as well as on their state of health. You can focus on the following data:

  • 1,400 rubles - for one and two children;
  • 3,000 rubles - for three or more;
  • 6,000 rubles to adoptive parents for a disabled child;
  • 12,000 rubles - for each disabled child (parents).

It is worth noting that these returns are put on each child. So, if the family has 2 healthy babies, you can get a total of 2,800 rubles in the form of a deduction of the tax type of the standard form.

When does the standard deduction stop?

Want to take advantage of the above features? Then it is important to understand that this will not always succeed. Especially when it comes to standard tax deductions for children. The age of the descendants plays an important role in this matter.
