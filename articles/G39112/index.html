Pattern "Bag over the shoulder" for all occasions

The most useful and necessary women's accessory is a shoulder bag. The pattern that we will offer you in the article, a beautiful fabric, threads, fantasy - and you will have a unique thing!

For a walk





So how to do do-it-yourself shoulder bag? The pattern is designed to ensure that the finished product is not very large. Its size is suitable to put a phone, a wallet and a couple of necessary things, such as cosmetic bags or books. Such an accessory is very useful for a walk. What do we have to do:

  • Print a full-size pattern. If you don‚Äôt have such an opportunity, then draw it yourself, it‚Äôs easy, and you can adjust the template to the size that suits you.
  • On the fabric, cut out two details on the pattern, leaving space for the seams.
  • Fold both parts in half and sew together, connecting the edges and creating the bottom of the bag.
  • Having retreated 3 centimeters from the corner, sew obliquely, cut off the excess.
  • Do all of the above with a fabric that will be used as a lining.
  • Cut a pocket of the necessary size. Overlock one side and sew the rest to the lining.
  • Make a buttonhole that will cover the bag. To do this, cut a piece of fabric two times wider than necessary. Fold in half and sew the edges.
  • With safety pins, connect the lining and bag. Insert a loop between two layers. Sew it all together, leaving some free space for turning the bag around.
  • Level the product.
  • Sew the two ends of the handle together. Sew on the button.

So the shoulder bag is ready . The pattern is made in a rough form, but is still understandable.

For shopping

A shopping bag should be as convenient as possible, so it is good if the strap can be worn not only over the shoulder, but also in the hands. Such a pattern of a bag over a shoulder made of fabric is a little more complicated. Working process:

  1. Use the finished pattern or, based on it, create your own.
  2. Transfer the pattern to the wrong side of the fabric. The stock for seams is already taken into account.
  3. Fold the corners (shown by triangles in the figure). Sew the edges in dashed lines.
  4. Cut the required pocket size. Sew on the outside. Add a lock. To do this, connect one part of the lock to the inside of the pocket, and the second to the bag.
  5. Do the same with the lining.
  6. Sew inside the pocket, leaving one side free. Sew in the lock.
  7. Cut the handle for the bag twice as wide as the required length. Bend the edges to the center, sew on the wrong side. Turn to the front side.
  8. Connect the lining to the main fabric with safety pins. Insert the strap between the two layers on the side.
  9. Cut a rectangle that could cover the empty space of the bag, leave space on the seams. Cut it in half and sew a lock.
  10. Insert the finished part between the layers.
  11. Sew it all together.

For such a product, choose a thick fabric. Ready shoulder bag. Pattern By the way, it can also be used to create a fashion clutch, if you reduce the size.
