Through terminal

Putting money into an account through the official terminals of this company is as easy as shelling pears. Here is the procedure:

  1. Visit any point of sale of MTS products.
  2. Go to one of several terminals.
  3. Select "Pay for cellular communications."
  4. Enter mobile number.
  5. Enter the amount.
  6. Deposit money into the device.
  7. Wait for verification of their authenticity.
  8. Continue topping up the balance.
  9. Take a check.

Through the official website

  2. Find the official website of the MTS mobile operator - it is usually located at mts.ru.
  3. Select the "Financial Services and Payments" form on the main page of the MTS website. Then wait for the download.
  4. In the new window that opens, select the "Payments" form, and then the "Cellular" category.

  10. Enter your email address so that the check arrives there.
  11. Click the "Pay" button. Waiting for the receipt of money in your account.
