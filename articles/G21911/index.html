Holidays in the Maldives - exotic in all its manifestations

Holidays in the Maldives plunge headlong into a warm, fairytale world full of exoticism and solitude with nature. The Maldives is located almost at the equator, many of these islands are washed by the Indian Ocean. The hot sun's rays warm the golden sand almost all year round, and the emerald waves of the ocean resemble fresh milk. Flora and fauna beckon here romantics and newlyweds, who seek to spend here a memorable honeymoon for life .

In the Maldives there are upscale hotels where you can visit SPA procedures and massages if you wish. After all, there are SPA centers in almost every hotel, but each of these centers is unlike the other. So in one of the hotels the SPA-center is located under water, and during the procedures, it is possible to observe the underwater world through the window-porthole.

And you can sit on one of the many islands in a cozy bungalow on stilts. Here, no one bothers to enjoy the gentle sunshine and hot sand, as well as the virgin beauty of the island's forests.

And if there is a desire to spend time actively, it would be nice to go diving or snorkeling. In the Maldives, you can go down to the bottom of the ocean with a scuba diving or visit an old wreck, explore the indescribable beauty of coral reefs or watch the reef shark very close.

Holidays in the Maldives. Combined tours

In the Maldives, it is possible to combine different types of recreation if desired . You can combine a beach holiday with excursion programs, or a beach holiday and a dive safari. Since, when flying to the Maldives, a plane of some airlines lands in Dubai, it would be wise to rest there for a couple of days, to combine the rest in the Maldives with the rest in the UAE.

Whatever tour you choose, you can book it in advance, but if you want to save, it is better to buy last-minute tours to the Maldives. In some travel agencies, it is even possible to purchase last-minute packages to the Maldives in advance.
