How to create dolls by Tatyana Connee: pattern and master class

Prepare the fabric. It can be puppet knitwear, cotton, linen. Take the patterns, pin them to the fabric with pins, circle. Without cutting out the details, sew them on the sewing machine. Remember to leave openings for the filler.

Below is a pattern of Tatyana Konne dolls, in which the head is a separate part.





Now you should cut the details, leaving 0.5 cm at the edges. If you have zigzag scissors in your handicraft arsenal, then treat them with the edges. Then the material will not crumble.

Now every detail needs to be filled with holofiber or another filler to your taste.

The soles of shoes need to be strengthened: stick several layers of non-woven on them. Pin this part with pins to the legs. Stitch the elements leaving a hole for stuffing.

From any dense fabric (for example, felt) to cut shoes. Shoes should be slightly larger than the legs themselves. Connect the parts and put them on the toy.

The process of collecting the doll looks like this:

  1. The head is sewn first to the body (if the head is cut out separately).
  2. Sew limbs with a button fastener.
  3. Fasten the hair.
  4. We draw eyes on the face.
  5. Tint the cheeks with pastel or cosmetic blush.
  6. Dress up the doll.




Now you know the steps of assembling a doll based on Tatyana Konne. You can additionally see patterns below. There are several options for patterns: one-piece head and body, as well as when it is sewn separately.

Filling for dolls

Filler plays a huge role in the creation of textile toys. It depends on him how much the doll will serve its owners. The filler is also responsible for the smoothness of the ‚Äúskin‚Äù of a textile person or animal.

In handicraft stores, you will find a large number of fillers, but in fact it comes down to the fact that only one or two is suitable. In this part of the article, we will analyze everything that manufacturers offer, and the choice is yours. So, all fillers are divided into natural and synthetic.

Natural fillers

These include:

  • cotton wool
  • wool,
  • sawdust,
  • natural herbs
  • cereals.

Vata. Its only advantage is that it can be bought at any pharmacy. The fact that it consists entirely of cotton will never cause an allergy, and even if a child guts a toy and eats a piece, then nothing bad will happen.

There are a lot of disadvantages of cotton wool as a filler:

  • The toy cannot be washed.
  • Mold may appear in the filler, as it absorbs moisture from the air.
  • It is very difficult for her to evenly fill the product.

Verdict: stuffing toys with cotton can only be done if you are ready to change it often. This product can only be filled with a product from a very dense fabric, otherwise ugly tubercles on the surface are inevitable.

Wool (sliver). This is pure combed wool of alpine thoroughbred sheep. It is not painted, has no odors, is pleasant to the touch and even has healing properties.

The huge minus of this filler is the high price. In addition, it is sold in bobbins. Acquiring it to fill one product is expensive. This filler is mainly used in the manufacture of Waldorf pupae. Remember that products with wool inside must not be washed. The filler will have to be changed once a year.
