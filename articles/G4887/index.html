What is necessary to see in Odessa?

Black Sea Babylon, A Pearl by the Sea, a Hero City! Odessa is so beautiful and attractive that it does not leave anyone indifferent. Everyone wants to visit this resort, take a walk along its wide avenues, and experience its charming atmosphere. The taste of the sea on the lips and a unique dialect give the city a special charm. Do you know what to see in Odessa in the first place?





As the famous song says, the whole of Odessa is very great. It is literally filled with architectural monuments and natural beauties, humor and fun. Her special charm cannot be overlooked. What is a must see in Odessa? First of all, it is the Opera and Ballet Theater, built in a unique Viennese Baroque style by architects inspired by the Dresden Opera. At the building do not miss the sculptural group "Hippolytus". Here at different times great people performed - composers, conductors and singers N. Rimsky-Korsakov, F. Chaliapin, P. Tchaikovsky, S.
