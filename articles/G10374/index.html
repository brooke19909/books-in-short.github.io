Jordan, resorts on the Red Sea: review, rating, prices

Jordan is a unique country that is able to surprise every guest. Travelers from all over the world seek to visit the sacred land of Jordan at least once. The memory in their hearts will remain for many years. Jordan is an Arab state with its own culture, traditions, and customs. All this attracts and attracts tourists so much that every year the resorts of Jordan are simply packed to capacity with those who want to experience indescribable emotions, get a beautiful skin tone under the gentle Arabian sun, and plunge into the warm waters of the Red Sea. Hospitable Jordan is ready to share all this. Resorts on the Red Sea are among the best.





Where to go on vacation?

Sea breeze, gentle sun, magical atmosphere - all this sounds tempting. Jordan, the Red Sea ... The main resorts can give it all in the best possible way and in unlimited quantities. In Jordan, almost every city is one solid attraction. Moreover, here you can improve your health. Today it is one of the most visited countries for the treatment of many diseases.

Resorts in Jordan are noteworthy in that they can be visited all year round. The water temperature in the sea never drops below 24 degrees. A particularly favorable period is from the beginning of September to the end of December. At this time, the air temperature is just perfect for a comfortable perception (about 27 degrees).

The best resorts in Jordan for relaxation will be described in more detail below. So, let's start considering the rating.

Aqaba

The resort is located on the Red Sea coast. This is a favorite place for many tourists. In addition to a wonderful beach holiday , you can enjoy the atmosphere of antiquity, immerse yourself in the history of the past and visit many unusual places.





All year round, the territory of Aqaba is open to everyone. In winter, the resort is especially beautiful. The Gulf of Aqaba is unrivaled for its purity and beauty. It is surrounded by magnificent rocks, the color of which is constantly changing, which causes indescribable sensations. The waters of Aqaba are fraught with many mysteries. The unique underwater world wins the hearts of divers from all over the world. In the waters of the Gulf of Aqaba, a huge number of unique species of fish, corals and other marine inhabitants live. That is why scuba diving is considered the main attraction here. Fishing, water skiing and sailing are also practiced.

Those in whose soul a passion for history lives will find here a lot of interesting things. Aqaba is a resort with a rich history. There are objects that are 5000 years old. In the past, the resort was a strategic point connecting land and sea routes of Europe, Asia and Africa. Aqaba is also rich in medieval monuments of archeology. Here is the famous fortress, built in the XVI century. Of particular interest is the house of Hussein Ben Ali. It houses a museum.

Aqaba occupies the northeast sleeve of the Red Sea coast. From almost all sides it is surrounded by mountains, which makes its climate unusually favorable for recreation. The swimming season is never interrupted here. Water temperature does not fall below 22 degrees in the winter months. In summer, it is especially hot only in the afternoon. But this time can be spent on vacation, and in the morning and evening enjoy the warm, not scorching sun and sea.

Places to visit in Aqaba

Mamluka Fort, built by Kansuh Al Gauri in the 16th century, majestically stands on the seashore. From all sides palm trees surround it, which makes it even more picturesque.

In the center of the bay is the island of Pharaoh, on which the fortress of Saladin is built. This place is famous for the battles of the Crusaders, led by Richard the Lionheart. Aqaba also houses the oldest Christian temple. The Byzantine church was built in the IV century. Today it looks more like ruins, but some parts still survived. In the museum of the great-grandfather of the ruler of Jordan - King Hussein - you can find out the history of these places.

Beach Activities in Aqaba

Of course, the main place to visit in Aqaba are the beautiful beaches. They are intended not only for swimming. Fans of outdoor activities feel a special surge of energy here. The resort has several diving centers offering 24 diving spots.





Aqaba's coral reefs are inhabited by the most amazing representatives of the underwater fauna. Here you can find clown fish, and parrot fish, and sea dogs. Also here is the largest fish in the world - a whale shark. Those who do not really like to dive under water can see all the beauty of the underwater world on land. Aqaba has one of the most famous marine scientific centers with a huge swimming pool.

In addition to marine life, divers can explore the remains of the Lebanese ship Cedar Pride. Such entertainment is available even to beginners. Professionals can go to the crash site of the barge.





For those who cannot sleep, Aqaba offers night dives. The Rainbow Reef area is best suited for this. A huge number of starfish and spiny lobsters live here. Riding a boat with a transparent bottom is perfect as daytime entertainment.

The cost of rest at this resort is from 170 thousand for 10 days per person.

Tala Bay Resort

Not only Aqaba is famous for Jordan. The resorts of Jordan in the Red Sea are also represented by Tala Bay. From there you can bring an unforgettable experience. Jordan is rich in such places. Resorts on the Red Sea are striking in their hospitality, beauty. Adventurers can get an unforgettable adrenaline rush by trying all kinds of water sports in the waters of this marvelous place called Tala Bay. Fans of a relaxing holiday will also find a lot of interesting things here. Tala Bay Resort offers the best facilities for families with children. There are many safe entertainment options for them, some of which do not even require the presence of parents. Therefore, while the children have fun, adults can relax.





On warm late evenings, you can spend time in cozy restaurants with unusually delicious cuisine. You can just listen to the sound of the surf on the endless beach. The scenery and architecture of these places are breathtaking. Tala Bay combines the traditions of the Middle Ages with modern architecture.

The road to the resort is no less interesting. The Royal Highway is surrounded on all sides by majestic mountains. There are legends that the Edomite god Dushar, who is considered the patron saint of the mountains, lives in these mountains. Here is such an amazing country, Jordan. Resorts on the Red Sea are not only beaches, but also mountains and historical monuments.

Best restaurants and eateries in Tala Bay

Just amazing country Jordan! Resorts in Jordan have many places to relax. Tala Bay has many restaurants and eateries. In the institution Kensis you can taste delicious sandwiches. Crispy French bun or Arabic bread with various fillings - all that is needed to completely satisfy your hunger.

Restaurant "Nadzhel" offers its guests the best portioned dishes. Here you can have a delicious breakfast, a hearty lunch and dinner. This place is famous for a variety of salads. Also here are delicious snacks and desserts not only in Arabic cuisine. There is also an Italian restaurant called Casalingo. Here you can enjoy Italian cuisine, delicious hot pizzas baked in a traditional wood-burning oven.

In the Sezhan Bayside Al Fresco restaurant you can taste the world-famous Movenpic ice cream. It also serves the best desserts, juices and cocktails. Coffee lovers can stop by the Baraka Lobby Lounge Bar, which offers a wide range of coffees and other drinks. Restaurant "Solero" is famous for its pastry masterpieces. Here you can also enjoy dishes from almost all countries of the world.

Beach Holidays in Tala Bay

The city of Marina is famous for the fact that it houses the center of beach relaxation - "Beach Club". The Red Sea at Tala Bay is warm throughout the year. Connoisseurs of water entertainment consider this place a paradise on Earth. Here live dazzling beauty corals, colorful fish and many other marine life. In combination with bright turquoise sea water, this beauty delights even the most sophisticated divers.





The Beach Club employs professional instructors. Beginners can fully trust them and complete their first dive in life. Anyone who wants to independently explore the depths of the sea or just do some kind of sport can also contact the "Club". It is proposed to get equipment for water skiing, windsurfing, parachuting. You can order a whole range of events. It is available for both beginners and advanced athletes.

Where to stay?

Hotels in Jordan, located on the Red Sea, receive thousands of guests all year round. Some of them are entire hotel complexes that provide a lot of services to their visitors. The best hotels in Jordan will be described below. The rating of hotels and hotels can change, as each complex is constantly being improved in improving the quality of service for its guests. This is what Jordan is famous for. Even middle-income people can visit the famous resorts of Jordan on the Red Sea. Prices vary according to season and place of residence. On average, a ten-day vacation will cost 150 thousand rubles.

DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Aqaba

The hotel is famous for its swimming pool, which offers scenic views of the Gulf of Aqaba. Visitors can use the fitness center and sauna. The hotel is 11 km from Hussein International Airport.





All rooms are equipped with the best linens. The apartments also have a work desk, plasma TV. There is a balcony. At the restaurant you can taste delicious dishes of modern cuisine made exclusively from fresh products. At the hotel there is a restaurant "Infinity Grill Terrace", offering meat, fish and chicken dishes. The Infinity Chill Out Bar offers beautiful views of Aqaba Bay. Here you can enjoy exotic cocktails and energy drinks. A 5-minute walk to the center of Aqaba. The port is 7 km away.

Inter Continental Aqaba

The hotel is famous for its private beach on the shores of the Gulf of Aqaba. The hotel complex has a chic lagoon pool. From the balconies of the rooms you can see this beauty, as well as the splendor of the Red Sea. Hotel guests can use the services of the spa, which even has a Russian bath. Includes a minibar and a coffee maker for making drinks. Hotel guests can rent snorkeling equipment and other water sports.





Amazing sea, gentle sun, hospitable residents, luxury hotels, picturesque landscapes - this is the most common review in Jordan. Holidays on the Red Sea are really beautiful. It is unlikely to be forgotten. For every traveler, Jordan is re-opened. Resorts on the Red Sea will leave a lot of impressions for a lifetime.
