Hotels in Volgodonsk: overview, addresses, rating

Volgodonsk is the easternmost city of the Rostov region. The village is located in the Salsk steppes between Rostov-on-Don and Volgograd. Despite the fact that the city is quite young, it attracts many tourists and business people. Hotels in Volgodonsk offer all of them a warm welcome and comfortable living conditions. In our brief review, we arranged them according to the rating compiled from the guest reviews.

"Art City"

Hotel "Art City" in Volgodonsk is conveniently located. This is the very center of the city near Victory Park and DC "October". The train and bus station are a 5-minute walk away.

Guests can take advantage of the following benefits:

  • A restaurant.
  • SPA area.
  • Conference hall.
  • Parking.
  • Ironing room.
  • Laundry.

For guests, the following accommodation options are provided:

  • Single "Standard" - 3000 rubles. per day.
  • Double "Standard" - 3000 rubles. per day.
  • "Comfort" - 3500 rubles. per day.
  • "Comfort Twin" - 4000 rubles. per day.
  • "Suite" - 4500 rubles. per day.
  • "Lux Comfort" - 6000 rubles. per day.

Hotel address: 52 A. Lenin Street

Reviews about the hotel "Art City"

Art City in Volgodonsk was rated by 7.8 out of 10 guests. Guests leave such positive feedback:

  • Good spa for reasonable money.
  • Quiet and calm atmosphere, contributing to a good rest.
  • Comfortable orthopedic beds where you can sleep well.
  • Friendly and helpful staff who quickly respond to requests and complaints.
  • Good hearty breakfasts.
  • The presence of an elevator.

And such negative ones:

  • Due to poor ventilation, the smell of sewage is present in the bathroom.
  • The mixer in the shower works poorly (it is difficult to set the optimum temperature).
  • Outdated room design.
  • Tasteful instant coffee for breakfast.
  • Weak hair dryer power - it takes a very long time to dry your hair.
  • Wireless Internet outages.
