"Anavadia Hotel 4", Rhodes - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Even in the recent past, the small village of Kolymbia, halfway between the cities of Rhodes and Lindos, was a modest fishing village. Today, new hotels are being erected here and infrastructure is being developed, which annually attracts more and more tourists. If you want to spend your vacation in Kolymbia, then consider Anavadia Hotel 4 * as an option for your stay.

Location and Description

This hotel complex is located in a cozy and quiet place in the resort village of Kolymbia. Just a few kilometers away is the city of Faliraki. The distance to the island's capital - the city of Rhodes - is 27 kilometers, and to Lindos - 25 kilometers.

The hotel itself is small. So, his housing stock is represented by 40 standard and family rooms. However, several more buildings are under construction here. Each of the rooms has a private bathroom, a TV with cable channels (including in Russian), a hairdryer, a telephone, a minibar, a terrace or a balcony, a safe (for an additional fee). Some apartments also have a jacuzzi. Meals at Anavadia Hotel 4 * are carried out on an all-inclusive basis.

Hotel guests have at their disposal several swimming pools, sun terraces, bars with a wide selection of drinks, billiards, parking, currency exchange and a tour desk.





Prices

As for the cost of accommodation in Anavadia Hotel 4 * (Rhodes), then, according to travelers, it is consistent with the quality of the service provided here and the category of the hotel. So, a seven-day stay in the midst of the holiday season (July and August) will cost you from 1,100 euros for a single room, from 1,260 euros for a double room and from 1,450 euros for a triple. Please note that these prices include all inclusive meals.

Anavadia Hotel 4 * (Rhodes): Traveler Reviews from Russia

In order to facilitate the process of choosing a hotel, we decided to give generalized comments from our compatriots who stayed at Anavadia Hotel in Kolymbia (Rhodes island) in 2014. Looking ahead a bit, we note that most of the tourists liked this hotel complex. Some disadvantages were noted, but overall the hotel makes a very good impression. But we will understand in more detail.





Comments about the hotel itself and its location

Since Anavadia Hotel 4 * is located in the small resort village of Kolymbia, experienced travelers recommend a vacation to those who love nature and want to spend a vacation away from city noise. Near the hotel there is a supermarket and rental cars and scooters. Also in the vicinity of Anavadia there are several bars, taverns, shops and excursion bureaus. The journey from the airport takes only half an hour, and along the way you can enjoy magnificent views.

As for the rooms, most of the guests found them quite spacious and pleasantly decorated. According to vacationers, furniture and appliances in some apartments are new, and in some - quite old. The reason for this situation is that now the hotel is building new buildings. Therefore, if you are accommodated in a newly built building, then the conditions here will differ from older buildings. If for some reason you will miss some piece of furniture, then tell the receptionist about this. You will certainly be delivered everything you need. Also, many guests note such a pleasant fact as the presence in the bathrooms of some of the jacuzzi rooms.

The quality and regularity of cleaning at Anavadia Hotel 4 * (Rhodes), most of the guests were satisfied. Linens and towels here, according to vacationers, are changed several times a week.





Hotel Nutrition Opinion

On the whole, the majority of our compatriots were satisfied with the quality of food at Anavadia Hotel. According to them, breakfasts are standard here. For lunch and dinner, a large selection of dishes is offered, including from different types of meat and fish. Also always on the menu are fresh vegetables and fruits, delicious desserts. During the day, you can enjoy pizza, sandwiches, a bun and other pastries in the snack bar. Later in the evening, you can also enjoy ice cream here.

Impressions of tourists about the hotel staff

According to our compatriots, all employees of Anavadia Hotel 4 * (Greece) are very helpful, hospitable and pleasant people. They quickly respond to problems. If something does not suit you, and you would like, for example, to change the number, then the reception administrators will always meet you and do everything to make the guests happy. The only minus some tourists from our country considered the fact that few of the hotel staff speak Russian. Therefore, if you want to stay here, you will have to recall a little the course of the school curriculum of the English language.





Anavadia Hotel 4 *: reviews on beach vacations and other attractions

According to the guests, the distance from the hotel to the sea is about 1 kilometer. The path runs along one of the paved paths. You can easily walk to the beach in a quarter of an hour at leisurely pace. A shuttle service to the shore can also be arranged upon request. If you would like to use this service, then inform the reception in advance.

As for the local beaches, they are pebble, but there are also sandy areas. Since there are stones in some places at the bottom, swimming in special shoes is recommended. The sea here is very clean and pleasant, there are practically no waves.

Since the beaches in Kolymbia are municipal, you need to pay for the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas (about 8 euros per day). However, you can always sit on a mat or towel brought with you.

As for other entertainment, then on the territory of Anavadia Hotel 4 * there are two swimming pools for adults and the same for children. There is a sun terrace, where, according to our compatriots, sun loungers and umbrellas are always enough for everyone. There is no animation in Anavadia Hotel (as in most other hotels in Greece).
