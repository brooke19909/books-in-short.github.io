Holidays in the Mediterranean Sea: Okurcalar Resort, Turkey

In one of the warmest regions of Turkey there is a resort called Okurcalar.

Turkey is administratively divided into 7 regions. Okurcalar is located in the west of the country, in the Alanya region. The resort is young. The population of the village is only 4,500 people. The village is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, the climate there is subtropical. This place is suitable for a relaxing budget holiday. There are many different hotels here, and the infrastructure is not very well developed. Alara river flows along the western border of the village. If you cross it over the bridge, you will find yourself in a neighboring region, in the resort of Chenger.





In the village of Okurcalar (Turkey) there is no inherent in popular resorts a wide range of entertainment. There are only a few cafes, shops and restaurants. There is a bazaar on Fridays and Tuesdays where you can buy souvenirs and bargain.

How to get to Okurcalar

This village is the most western of all resorts in the Alanya region. It is 32 km from the city of Alanya, and 85 km from the airport of Antalya . Next to Okurcalar are the resorts of Incekum and Avsallar. The resorts are united by a route along which shuttles run every 15 minutes during the holiday season. Minibuses in Turkey are called "dolmushi". The fare in them costs from 3 to 8 lire per person. By bus, the journey takes 1.5 hours. From the airport with this transport it is easy to get to Okurcalar (Turkey).

Weather

The climate of this region is dry and hot in summer, humid and mild in winter. In the village of Okurcalar (Turkey), the weather is most conducive to relaxation in August and July. In these months, the temperature here during the day is + 30 ¬∞ C. Reception of vacationers lasts from May to mid-November. The water temperature in May is usually + 20 ¬∞ C, in August + 25 ¬∞ C.

Beaches

There are many bays and bays in the village. The coastline is 6.5 km long. Sandy and pebble beaches. The entrance to the water is smooth, convenient. This is especially convenient if you are relaxing with children. The beaches have banana rides and diving classes. For parents there are other entertainments: a sauna, a Turkish bath, massage. In the village there is a five-star hotel Water Planet Resort & Aquapark with a foam disco and a water park. For a certain amount, guests of other hotels are also allowed there.





Hotels

In the village of Okurcalar (Turkey), hotels are located on well-groomed islets of greenery, covered with palm trees and flowers. Okurcalar is not a residential, but a resort village, only tourists and hotel staff live in it. This makes it quite safe at night. Hotels here have a very different level, from 3 to 5 stars. There are few three-star hotels here, mainly at the level of 4-5 stars. Separate bungalows are also for rent for those who do not like to live in hotels. Many hotels have tropical gardens, beach soccer, tennis, volleyball, bowling, water polo, billiards. Also, hotels often provide guests with gyms, Turkish baths, saunas, and medical services. Most of the hotels here are located on the very shores of the Mediterranean Sea.

The best hotel in the village is considered Delphin De Luxe Resort. The most budgetary - Santa Barbara 3 *.

Attractions in the resort of Okurcalar, Turkey

Where to go and what to visit from local attractions? It is very simple. Start with a walk through the fruit plantations. Pomegranates and bananas are grown here. Then it is worth exploring the ruins of the ancient port city of Giustiniano Napoli. A few years ago in the sea off the coast found a sarcophagus of ancient times, II-III centuries. BC e. The find revealed the image of the god Eros. Now the sarcophagus has been transferred to the museum.





You can rent a catamaran at the fishermen's marina and go for a walk on a small island in the sea.

The village has shops with textile, leather, jewelry and convenience stores with fruit.

On the bank of a mountain river, in the west of the village, there is Alara fortress with a mosque and a caravanserai. The river valley is covered with pine trees. In the west of Okurcalar there is also a bus fleet, they daily carry tourists to Antalya, Alanya, Side, Manavgat.

In the neighboring cities of Side and Alanya, you can see the ruins of ancient temples of ancient times, walk through the ancient amphitheater, photograph the fortress located on the mountain. There you can go on an excursion to the underground kingdom of caves or rafting along a mountain river. Near the city of Izmir are the legendary ancient Miletus and Ephesus. This is the birthplace of world-famous ancient philosophers.
