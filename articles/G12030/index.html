Xperia Grand Bali Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists

Alanya is a popular Turkish resort located on the Mediterranean coast. Usually companies of young people and families with children come here on vacation. Alanya's beaches are marked with blue flags, which indicates their purity and beauty. If you want to have a good and inexpensive vacation at the resort, then pay attention to the Xperia Grand Bali Hotel 4 * complex.

Where is the hotel situated?

A good location of the hotel is the key to a successful stay. Therefore, it is often a fundamental factor influencing the choice of hotel. Xperia Grand Bali Hotel - Adults Only - compares favorably with its competitors because it is located in the center of the resort and at the same time is not very far from the coast. Cleopatra - the famous city beach of Antalya is located just 150 meters from the complex. Tourists can independently reach it in 5-10 minutes.

Xperia Grand Bali Hotel is located on a busy city street in the heart of Alanya's resort. Near the complex, tourists can find many restaurants, bars and shops. Nearby are nightclubs, the noise from which can interfere with the rest of tourists with small children.

The only minus of the hotel is its distance from the airport. To get to the hotel, tourists will have to cover a distance of 120 km, so the road can take several hours. The nearest international airport is in Antalya. A comfortable bus takes the guests from there, the cost of which is usually included in the price of the ticket. If desired, tourists can refuse this service and get to the complex by taxi or car.

General Hotel Information

This cozy city hotel was opened for tourists in 1996. On its small territory is a seven-story building, built in the classical style, as well as an outdoor pool and an extensive garden. At the same time, the Xperia Grand Bali Hotel 4 * complex does not look old or dilapidated at all - the last major overhaul of all the premises was carried out here in 2010. The hotel cannot be called large because it offers only 96 rooms. In total, about 250 tourists can relax here at the same time.





The hotel has one interesting feature - check-in with children is not provided here. Only teenagers over 16 years old can stay at the hotel. Therefore, it is often chosen by youth companies and single tourists. Families with young children, in contrast, will have to pick another hotel. In addition, pets are not allowed in the hotel. Smoking in the Xperia Grand Bali Hotel is also strictly prohibited.

It is worth knowing that the registration of guests here begins only from 14:00. They must leave their rooms after the end of the vacation before noon.

Room amenities

To ensure their guests a comfortable stay throughout their stay, the creators of the Xperia Grand Bali Hotel have equipped rooms with a host of useful amenities. Most of them are provided free of charge, but some still have to pay.

We list the main amenities:

  • individual air conditioning for each room - tourists themselves can control the level of cooling;
  • Plasma TV with a set of cable channels, including Russian-speaking;
  • free minibar - daily employees replenish it with drinking water and soft drinks;
  • a telephone intended for communication with the administrator and international calls;
  • a safe for storing valuables, money and documents - is provided for a fee;
  • built-in hair dryer, towels, a set of shower accessories (soap, gel for washing, shampoo);
  • free wireless internet;
  • electric kettle - only available in some rooms;
  • 24-hour room service, including food, drinks and newspapers.

Hotel catering

As a rule, the price includes not only accommodation and transfer to the hotel, but also payment for meals. Hotel Xperia Grand Bali Hotel 4 * in Alanya offers its guests an all-inclusive concept. She works every day from 10:00 until midnight, so it is difficult to stay hungry at the hotel.

They serve food according to a special schedule, which includes the following meals:

  1. Breakfast. Held from 08:00 and lasts until 10:00. Tourists can eat traditional European morning dishes, such as scrambled eggs, fried sausages, sandwiches with hot drinks, cereals and soups.
  2. Late breakfast. It lasts about half an hour after the end of the morning meal. In his time, guests can try light snacks: sandwiches, pastries, desserts, fresh fruits.
  3. Dinner. It runs from noon until 14:00. Guests are offered a wide selection of meat and fish dishes, fresh vegetables and fruits, hot and cold snacks.
  4. Dinner. It is held from 19:00 to 21:00. Tourists can try traditional dishes of European and Turkish cuisine, as well as desserts, fast food.
  5. Late dinner (until midnight). During the last meal, soup is served.




All meals are served in the form of a plentiful buffet from which you can take as much food as you need. However, it is strictly forbidden to take her with you out of the room. There is a main restaurant on site for free use, as well as a poolside bar and a cafe in the lobby. Cold drinks and light snacks are available there. It is worth knowing that the price does not include freshly squeezed juices, as well as imported alcohol. For a fee, tourists can visit the fish restaurant in the evenings. Table reservation price - 10 euros per person.

Description of infrastructure

Reviews of the Xperia Grand Bali Hotel tell not only about the good condition of the rooms and the varied food, but also about the quality of service organization. For guests there are many amenities that make your vacation truly comfortable.

For example, tourists can always take a car for a fee to travel around the resort. You can park your car in a guarded parking lot, which is free of charge.

To access the Internet, you can connect wireless Wi-Fi. The connection speed is not too high, but it is enough to view social networks. If you want to download something, you can rent a place in the Internet corner. Its cost is about 3 euros per hour.





At the reception, tourists can solve all questions with a Russian-speaking administrator. Currency exchange is also made here. For an additional amount, you can take the clothes to the laundry, visit the hairdresser and beauty salon. The hotel has several shops selling beachwear, cosmetics and souvenirs.

Finally, if necessary, you can call the doctor‚Äôs room. The cost of his services is not included in the insurance, therefore, it is paid directly during the visit.

Beach and Pool

Xperia Grand Bali Hotel does not have a private beach area. Tourists independently get to the sea, which is located 150 meters from the hotel. There is the famous Cleopatra beach. To get to it, you need to overcome a busy road. The underpass is not equipped here. The beach is completely sandy. Entrance to it is free, and for a nominal amount guests can rent sunbeds equipped with sun umbrellas. Towels are issued upon deposit.





If you wish, you can stay on site and relax by the outdoor pool. Its area is 50 square meters. m. It is open daily until 18:00. Free sun loungers, mattresses and parasols are available on the terrace next to it.

Sports and SPA

The complex has a modern SPA-salon, which you can visit only for a fee. There tourists can order a complex of health and beauty treatments, go for a massage. There is also a spacious sauna and Turkish bath.





You can play sports in the gym, which is open every day. There is no need to pay for using the equipment. If desired, on the beach you can play volleyball in the sand.

Other entertainments

A not too rich selection of entertainment is offered by the Xperia Grand Bali Hotel. In addition to the leisure options described above, tourists can play darts and billiards for free. On some days, live music is played on site. The hotel does not have its own animation program, but guests can visit it for free in a neighboring hotel. For a relaxing stay on the ground floor there is an open room equipped with TVs and air conditioning.

Conditions for relaxing with children

An ideal place for a vacation with your family will be Turkey. Unfortunately, Xperia Grand Bali Hotel is not suitable for families with children. As mentioned above, tourists over 16 years old are welcome here. Therefore, no recreational and infrastructure facilities for guests with children are not provided.

Positive feedback on the Xperia Grand Bali Hotel 4 * in Alanya

Guests, as a rule, respond well to rest in this place. They note that the hotel is suitable for single tourists who love active entertainment, and youth companies who are not afraid of the noise of city streets.

Guests indicate the following advantages of living in this complex:

  • Great location. Shops, restaurants and a Turkish bazaar are within walking distance. You can walk to the beach in 5 minutes, and to the port in half an hour. Near the hotel there is also a climb to the mountain, where the famous Alanya fortress is located.
  • Spacious rooms in which quality repairs are made. They have comfortable beds, new furniture, well-functioning plumbing.
  • Polite and friendly staff. Some employees speak Russian well. Hotel employees are always sympathetic to the problems that have arisen and are trying to quickly solve them.
  • Housekeepers regularly replenish the minibar and cosmetics in the bathroom.
  • Varied and tasty food. Every day at the buffet you can see a huge amount of vegetables and fruits, meat and fish. There are desserts, oriental sweets and ice cream.




Negative reviews about Xperia Grand Bali Hotel 4 *

But not without criticism. Some of the guests say that the hotel does not correspond to the declared category, when compared with European hotels.

Guests note the following disadvantages of relaxation in this place:

  • Lack of sound insulation. In the room you can hear not only noise from the street, but also from neighboring rooms.
  • Rare cleaning. Hotel staff may forget to change towels, and sometimes do not tidy up for several days. However, after a reminder, they eliminate their mistake in the shortest possible time.
  • Low speed internet. Due to the heavy workload in the evening, it is impossible to update even social networks.
  • Poor transfer organization. After arriving at the airport, tourists could not find a bus, so they had to call the hotel. Only after that, a car came for them for an hour.
  • The small pool is in poor condition and needs repair, so almost no one swims in it.

Despite the shortcomings found, tourists still recommend a vacation in this place, indicating the low cost of living.
