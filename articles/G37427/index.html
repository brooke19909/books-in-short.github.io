Coral Beach Resort Hotel (Egypt / Hurghada): Travel Reviews

The chic resort complex Coral Beach Resort is located on the seashore and looks like a real green oasis, where tourists feel comfortable, relaxed and carefree.





Hotel Information

Coral Beach Resort is a true paradise for lovers of water. Guests are invited to plunge into the gentle waters of the Red Sea, as well as swim in the cleanest pool, which is located in the courtyard of the hotel. Particular attention should be paid to rooms that boast not only an exquisite oriental interior, but also incredibly picturesque views.





It is impossible to know the culture of any country without having studied all the features of its cuisine. At the Coral Beach Resort you will be invited to visit several restaurants where the best and most professional chefs prepare only the freshest and highest quality products. You can cheer yourself up during the day by drinking delicious alcoholic or refreshing cocktails in the many hotel bars.

This hotel will especially appeal to those tourists who cannot imagine their vacation without sports and active pastime. Be sure to visit the gym to work out using the most modern equipment. In addition, you can play tennis, basketball, soccer and beach volleyball. And in order to relieve physical and emotional stress, be sure to visit the Turkish hammam, SPA center or use the services of massage therapists.





Resort Location

The resort and hotel complex Coral Beach Resort 4 * is located in Hurghada, in its secluded Sharjah district, which is about 25 km from the center. The international airport will have to drive about 18 km from here. The hotel is located on the very shore of the Red Sea, which is an undoubted advantage for lovers of sea holidays. In addition, there are very beautiful corals.

Room features

Coral Beach Resort Hotel provides its guests with the opportunity to take advantage of the following amenities and technical facilities in the rooms:

  • depending on what class of room you are staying in, you will have a modern shower or bathtub with hydromassage;
  • You can have fun watching cable channels on a modern widescreen TV (several Russian-language programs are also available);
  • the room has a minibar, which will be replenished daily with various drinks, subject to payment for this service (otherwise you can use it as a refrigerator);
  • so that you are calm about the safety of your money and valuables, safes with a combination lock are installed in the rooms;
  • You can make a call abroad or contact the reception staff using your phone;
  • the bathroom has a large mirror, a washbasin and a hairdryer, as well as bath accessories and a set of disposable cosmetics for the shower;
  • so that the Egyptian heat does not penetrate your apartment, use individual air conditioning;
  • You can enjoy the surrounding landscapes, as well as dry swimsuits and towels on the balcony.
