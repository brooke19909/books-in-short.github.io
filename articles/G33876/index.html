Hotel Lino Mare 3 * (Greece, Crete): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Greece has long been famous for its ancient history and culture. Along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea there are many hotels that are suitable for both budget and respectable holidays. Here you can find complexes that are suitable for tourists who prefer to explore the country on their own. They need a hotel only for an overnight stay. Such travelers often stay in a small hotel Lino Mare 3 * (Greece, Crete, Heraklion).

Hotel Location

The hotel is located in the center of the resort village of Amoudara. It is famous for its wide beaches and extremely low prices, which attract tourists who want to save money. Accommodation here is cheaper due to the large power station located on the western outskirts of the village. It not only spoils the local landscape, but also is a source of unpleasant odors, which tourists often complain about. Heraklion can be reached from here by taxi or bus. It is located 7 km from the hotel, so the road to the center will take about half an hour. The nearest airport, which is located near Heraklion, is only 10 km away.

Resort Lino Mare 3 * (Greece) is located on the second beach line. To get to the seashore, you have to walk 150 meters. The hotel and the beach are separated by a road. A bus stop is 50 meters away. Nearby is a large supermarket where you can buy food, alcohol, household chemicals and clothing at affordable prices. There is a Greek tavern opposite the hotel.

basic information

The construction of this small hotel was completed in 1994. It offers tourists only 20 small rooms that are suitable for two adults. All of them are located in a cozy three-story building, the facade of which is decorated with stone cladding. The hotel has virtually no territory. Its total area is only 800 square meters. Most often, small families with children live here, who are looking for an inexpensive place to stay. Hotel Lino Mare Hotel 3 * (Crete) welcomes even infants. For them, an extra free crib is offered in the room.





Pets are not allowed in the hotel. The staff speaks only Greek and English. You can get your room keys no earlier than 14:00 local time. At the end of the rest, you must leave your room no later than noon. You can pay for accommodation and additional services using Visa and MasterCard bank cards. The hotel will surely give birthday cake and champagne to those birthday and newlyweds. To receive a compliment, you need to notify the administration in advance when making a ticket.

Room Amenities

The hotel offers vacationers to use the amenities that will make living here much more comfortable. All rooms are a small studio with a balcony and bath. The main room has a sleeping area and a kitchenette. It is equipped with a small fridge, an electric kettle and kitchenware. There is no stove and microwave, so guests can only cook cold snacks. Tea and coffee making facilities are also provided free of charge. Wi-Fi works in all apartments. You can connect to it for free at any time during your vacation.





It is worth noting that individual air conditioning in Lino Mare Hotel 3 * is included only at an additional cost. You can find out the latest news using the TV, which is connected to many satellite channels. Among them, you can find several Russian speakers. There is no telephone in the room, but there is a mechanical safe. Tourists can use clean towels, a shower set and a hairdryer in the bathroom for free.

Local infrastructure

The hotel has a well-developed infrastructure, but it is far from five-star complexes. For tourists, a 24-hour reception desk is open daily, where you can exchange rubles for euros. There is also a luggage room for storing large luggage. Guests can leave large bags and suitcases here so that they do not take up much space in the room. Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel. Guests can connect to it by taking data at the reception. Also passwords hang on each floor of the building. Computer and high-speed wired internet are available at an additional cost.





Near the hotel Lino Mare 3 * there is public parking. You can leave your car here for free without prior reservation. There are soft sofas, armchairs, tables and a TV, as well as periodically turned on air conditioning.

Entertainment at the hotel

Hotel Lino Mare 3 * offers tourists a budget vacation at low prices. Most often it is used as a place to sleep, so there is not much entertainment here. On the roof there is a small pool with a jacuzzi, around which there are several wooden sunbeds. It is filled with fresh water. There is no heating, so it is open only in fine weather. Sand and pebble beach is a 5-minute walk away. There is a water entertainment center where you can rent surfing equipment for an additional fee. Tourists can play beach volleyball. A tour desk is also open next to the hotel, where travelers can book excursions to anywhere in Crete or Greece.





Positive reviews

Before making a tour, you should definitely read the opinions of previous guests. They help to understand the real situation at the hotel. About the hotel Lino Mare 3 * reviews are written both positive and negative. Tourists note that the complex justifies its three-star status. In their opinion, it has a lot of advantages. We list the main ones:

  • Great location. The hotel is located in the heart of the resort, so there are shops, restaurants, nightclubs, and a bus stop nearby.
  • Outgoing and polite staff. The guests especially liked the hotel owner, who often works as an administrator.
  • The rooms have new furniture, among which stands out a huge double bed. High-quality and fresh linens are provided.
  • Both in public places, and in premises it is always cleaned and clean. It offers views of neighboring hotels and buildings.
  • Free sunbeds and umbrellas are provided on the beach if you buy any drink or cocktail at the bar.
  • There are almost never many people in the rooftop pool. You can sit in the company of friends in a relaxing environment.




Negative reviews

Hotel Lino Mare 3 * (Crete) receives negative reviews not very often. But it was not without them. Some of the guests were dissatisfied with the quality of the services provided. In their opinion, the administration should pay attention to the following disadvantages:

  • Drain in the shower works very poorly, the water does not leave and floods the floor, so you have to wipe it every time.
  • Breakfast in the restaurant is monotonous. Every day they cook the same set of dishes, which quickly bothers.
  • The promised satellite television broadcasts only one Greek channel in terrible quality.
  • Maids do not clean the room every day. You have to constantly remind them of the need to replace bedding.
  • The Internet sometimes works very poorly. In the evening, the speed becomes very low, so the browser refreshes one page for several minutes.
  • Some rooms overlook a noisy street. At night, the sounds of cars and the cries of people prevent sleep.

Instead of an afterword

Hotel Lino Mare 3 * can be recommended to picky tourists who want to save on accommodation. They will appreciate the cleanliness of the rooms, the friendly staff and the good location. When coming here with children, it is worth remembering that at night the rooms can be quite noisy due to the highway. Most often, adult couples stay here who need a room only for an overnight stay.
