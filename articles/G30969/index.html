TVs with integrated satellite receiver: how to choose and configure

Today, TVs in which there are receivers are very popular. Using these devices, you can connect satellite dishes. The built-in receivers in the documents are displayed as a DVB-S / S2 receiver. As a rule, most models with this function are available in liquid crystal. The most common manufacturers are LG and Samsung.





How to choose a TV with a receiver?

In the case of built-in receivers, pay attention to the back of the TV. Regardless of the model, there should be an LNB IN connector. It is intended for connecting a satellite dish. Additionally, the LNB OUT output must be present. With it, you can connect a second receiver to the TV.

There is a VIDEO connector for video. His responsibilities include ensuring average image quality. Without AUDIO, you will not be able to hear a beep at the same time. The stereo headset connects directly to a TV or amplifier. The Internet port allows you to take advantage of the local network. Additionally, you can connect to a personal computer. Last of all, Smart TV is checked on the TV. An audio signal comes through this connector. In turn, the picture on the screen should be of good quality.





TV setup with receiver

Configuring the satellite DVB-S2 tuner built into the TV is quite simple. In different models the menu is slightly different, but in general the instruction is one. Setting up satellite TV in Samsung TVs is as follows. First of all, go to the menu. There should be a tab "Broadcast". Through it, you can go directly to the channel settings. When choosing a subsection of the satellite system, the TV will ask for the owner‚Äôs PIN code. By default, manufacturers specify 0000.

After a successful transition, you can select the LNB settings. At this stage it is necessary to check whether the satellite signal is found by the system. If this does not happen, then you need to select DiSEqC mode. Then you can enter the menu and select the satellite signal. After all that has been done, all the settings are saved without fail.
