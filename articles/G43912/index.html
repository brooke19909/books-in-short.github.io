So what is Tinkoff Credit Systems bank? It was founded by Oleg Tinkov, and it happened in 2006. The bank has no offices, branches or points of sale. Credit cards are issued remotely. In other words, you communicate with bank employees via a toll-free telephone line or via the Internet.

All agreements and credit cards are delivered to you by courier or Russian post. For registration you will need only one document - your passport. Income certificates are not required. This is confirmed by reviews of the Tinkoff credit card.



Account statements are provided. They are sent by e-mail or by mail to Russia. Give preference to the second option, it is more reliable.
