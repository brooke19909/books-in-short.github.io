We knit ourselves: a crocheted summer panama hat for a girl



Despite their young age, little princesses always want to look magical and beautiful. Often the whole image that a little girl invented for herself spoils an absurdly picked up headdress, so the baby does not want to wear it in any way, despite the terrible cold or, on the contrary, the scorching sun. In order to prevent such incidents from happening to children, we recommend choosing accessories and jewelry in accordance with their wishes. Not only blouses and dresses should be beautiful, but also hats. In this article, we will consider such an important detail of the image as a panama hat for a girl.

Most often such products are crocheted. Subject to simple rules, the result often exceeds expectations, and even the little and spoiled princess with joy will put on a hat that perfectly harmonizes with her sundress or summer costume. So how to knit this accessory? Now we will consider a brief instruction and get acquainted with some of the nuances in this work.



It’s worth starting with the girth of the child’s head. It is clear that you can choose elastic yarn, and then you get a universal panama for the girl. Crocheting, however, it is not always possible to tie such a thing, since they work mainly with natural threads. Therefore, it is advisable to determine all the parameters in advance. And only after that start work. Now proceed to the selection of the yarn itself. Since we have a summer hat, it is advisable to choose cotton threads that let air through, therefore, the child will not be hot in such a hat. It is recommended to work with such yarn with hook number 1.5 (if one is not available, get one).



So, where does the creation of such a masterpiece as a panama hat for a girl begin? We crochet a little ring formed from a thread, we enclose it in 9 single crochet stitches. After that we tighten the result and tie everything with a connecting column. So we get the first row, which in the future will become the crown of the hat. Now we knit the second row. It begins with three lifting air loops, followed by the final air loop and a double crochet knitted in the next loop. This is the basis with which any summer panama begins to be built. First, the air loops are crocheted, and then a pattern is attached to them - the one you choose.



Most often, hats for little princesses are bell-shaped. Such a model is universal and very comfortable to wear, in addition, such a hat is very easy to knit. It is important only with each row to increase the number of columns and loops in equal proportions, and make sure that the pattern turns out to be even and not distorted. Following these simple rules, you can very quickly make crocheted knitted panama hat. The schemes for such products are very simple, and even a beginner needlewoman will be able to understand them.

It is worth noting that the more air loops are provided in your pattern, the more delicate and delicate the accessory will turn out. However, doing an explicit search with them is also not worth it, because otherwise the hat will not fulfill its main function - to protect the child from sunlight. In principle, a panama hat for a girl is crocheted very simply, the main thing is to choose the right hook, yarn and deal with the scheme.
