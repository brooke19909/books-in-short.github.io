High Beach 4 *: (Crete): tourist photos and reviews

Why do many people who decide to go to Greece stay in High Beach 4 *? Crete is a place in which there are a huge number of guest houses and hotels. And this hotel can be considered one of the best for many reasons. All worth briefly telling.





Location

Hotel High Beach 4 * (Crete) is located on the first beach line. You can reach the sea in just a minute. And all because the hotel is located on its own beach.

The hotel was decided to build in Malia - this is what the ancient Cretan city of the Minoan state is called today. It is an ideal holiday destination. Here lovers of history and sights (which are worth only the local palace, excavations and city ruins), as well as those people who want to devote their holidays to sea recreation, will find something for themselves to their liking. Local beaches, by the way, are awarded the EU Blue Flag for cleanliness.

The nearest airport is in Heraklion - it is approximately 30-35 kilometers away. Relatively close.

The hotel is an ideal place for people who dream of peace and tranquility. On its territory, as well as on the adjacent, peace reigns. But if you suddenly want to plunge headlong into a busy life, you can go to the village of Malia.

The hotel itself is divided into six buildings. In total, the High Beach 4 * fund (Crete) has a little more than a hundred different apartments. All are decorated in a contemporary style and decorated with paintings by Greek artists. There are Superior room apartments, VIP rooms, family rooms, as well as Junior and Honeymoon. However, this is a separate issue. And about the apartments, as well as the amenities offered will be discussed a little later.

What is in the hotel?

In High Beach 4 * (Crete) there is everything you need for relaxation. On the street there is an outdoor pool and sun terrace, as well as a garden and barbecue. In the evening, the hotel hosts animated shows and programs. But the most interesting is not this.

Guests can enjoy water sports, snorkelling or diving. There is everything necessary for this. You can also rent a bike and ride it in the vicinity. Or take a canoe instead.

In addition, the hotel has a SPA center with a Turkish bath, a sauna, a massage room, a jacuzzi and three freshwater pools. You can also go to the spa for manicures, pedicures and facial and body treatments.

Lovers of an active lifestyle will appreciate the presence of a tennis court, a billiard room, a gym, and a golf course. Other facilities at the hotel include karaoke, darts, fishing and a library. By the way, you can still visit the dance hall Delia.

Guests who come here with children may not worry that their kids will be bored. For the youngest guests there is a separate swimming pool, a mini club with nannies and carers, as well as a playground. You can call it a small kindergarten. There you can give the child if you need to go somewhere.





Services

Hotel High Beach 4 * (Crete) receives mostly positive reviews . And you can understand why. After all, it offers a wide variety of services.

Naturally, there is free private parking and Wi-Fi. Concierge services, a doctor, a 24-hour reception desk, food, newspapers, drinks and babysitting are available upon request. There is a tour desk, currency exchange, photocopy and fax services, a left-luggage office, dry cleaning and laundry with ironing, and a common lounge.

In addition to the above, the hotel has a mini market, places for smoking, shops, car rental, a souvenir stall, a hairdresser with a beauty salon, and even its own temple. Guests can also use the transfer, heating, VIP-services, ask for a hypoallergenic or family room, and also, if necessary, amenities for people with disabilities.

And a couple of words worth saying about the staff working in High Beach 4 * (Crete, Malia). People working at the hotel are not only responsible specialists in their field. They also speak different languages. At the hotel you can meet staff who speak English, German, Greek, French, Italian, Dutch and even Russian.





restaurants and bars

One of the main features of High Beach 4 * (Crete, Heraklion) is that this hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. Largely because of this, potential tourists choose it for their temporary residence on vacation.

The hotel has a children's buffet, several bars, as well as a restaurant that works according to the buffet and menu. By the way, they also prepare dietary dishes. The main restaurant is called Estia. It serves gourmet Mediterranean and Greek cuisine. In general, in these institutions you can try a lot of unusual food. For example, cuttlefish or squid in a sauce with herbs, salads with shrimp, soups, mashed potatoes, meat. Everyone will find something that will appeal to him.

The Tropicana Bar, located on the beach, serves a variety of cocktails and refreshments.

There is also a second restaurant. It is called Seaside. There, guests can enjoy a variety of seafood dishes. There is also a bar of the same name. It offers light snacks, cocktails and cold drinks. The third bar is called Aki-Aki.

Booking and other nuances

This is also a topic that should be noted by attention, telling about High Beach 4 *. Crete (Crete), Malia (Malia) - a popular place. Therefore, closer to the beginning of the season there are practically no free apartments left. The hotel has a lot of positive characteristics, and this can be seen by looking at the photos and the description above. And it is better to reserve the apartment in advance, a few months before the trip.

The hotel allows children with any age. If the child is less than seven years old, then there is no need to pay extra for his stay. But he will only have to sleep on that bed, which is in the room. Extra bed is not available.

If the child is 7-12 years old, then for it will need to pay 35% of the cost of the apartment. An extra bed will also be provided. Children over 12 years old need to pay extra 50%. To get an extra bed, you need to make a request for this service, and in advance. Preferably when applying for a reservation.

By the way, in this hotel, check-in is started from 12:00 to 16:00. And departure - from 10:00 to 11:00. But since the reception is open around the clock, you can arrange an appointment at the reception.
