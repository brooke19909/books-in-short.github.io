Afimall City: how to get by metro and public transport

Located in the center of the ensemble of the Moscow City business center, the Afimall City shopping center opened its doors to visitors in 2011 and does not cease to amaze with newfangled introductions. The grand architectural object with an area of ​​more than 280 m 2 has six aboveground levels and three underground levels and has no analogues in Russia.





At its disposal are more than four hundred boutiques, fifty restaurants and an underground parking lot with a capacity of more than 2.6 thousand cars. This center is unique in that it contains all the most popular and sought-after brands represented in the capital, here you can visit the Formula Kino movie theater and watch movies in the innovative IMAX format, as well as visit fairs and interactive entertainment.

Further in the article you will learn about interesting places in the shopping center and how to get to Afimall City by metro and car.

How to get to Afimall City by metro from the Moscow Region

From Mitino:

  • The trip will be approximately an hour. From the Mitino metro station on the Arbatsko-Pokrovskaya line, you need to go 8 stations to the Pobedy Park metro station and transfer to the Kalininsko-Solntsevskaya line to the Delovoy Tsentr station, walk 430 m.

From Khimki:

  • You need to get to the metro station Zamoskvoretskaya line Rechnoy Vokzal (minibus 342), go to the station Teatralnaya and transfer to Arbatsko-Pokrovskaya line, go 4 stations to metro station Park Pobedy, then transfer to Kalininskaya line and get to the station . "Business Center", walk 430 m.




How to get to Afimall City by metro from Dolgoprudny:

  • The trip will be approximately 1 hour 50 minutes. It is necessary to make two transfers and you will have to get from the Altufyevo metro, having reached there by minibus 545, then going down the Serpukhov-Timiryazevskaya line metro, driving three stations to Vladykino metro station and going to the Moscow Central Ring, driving 10 stations to metro "Business Center", walk 800 m.

From Mytishchi:

  • You need to take an electric train from the Taininskaya station to the Yaroslavl station, walk 500 m to the Sokolnicheskaya line, then go 5 stations to the Lenin Library metro station, go to the Filevskaya line and go 4 more stations to Vystavochnaya metro station, go 370 m.

From Balashikha:

You need to get to the Izmaylovskaya metro station (minibus 1215), go down to the Arbatsko-Pokrovskaya line, go 10 stations to the Pobedy Park metro station and go to the Kalininsko-Solntsevskaya line, go to the Delovoy centr metro station, then go on foot 430 m.
