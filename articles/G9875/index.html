Sanatorium "Energetik" (Perm): photos and reviews of tourists

Sanatorium "Energetik" is located in the city of Perm. Its location very organically combines the ability to enjoy all the benefits of a developed urban infrastructure and at the same time enjoy nature. The multidisciplinary health complex is located in the protected zone - Chernyaevsky forest park.

  • for the nervous system;
  • digestive organs;
  • endocrine system;
  • gynecology;

  • in urology;
  • dermatology;
  • for the musculoskeletal system.

Sanatorium "Energetik" (Perm) is a diversified health-improving complex, staffed by highly qualified specialists in their field.
