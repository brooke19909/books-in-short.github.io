Morocco - a country of powerful waves and sandy beaches

Morocco is a country of mysterious puzzles and oriental exoticism. Here you can meet amazing landscapes and mountain landscapes, it is famous for powerful waves and picturesque beaches. Combines the ancient culture of the African and Arab peoples of Morocco. The country on the map is located in the westernmost part of North Africa.

Climate of the country

Throughout the country, the Mediterranean climate prevails, characterized by hot, dry summers and humid warm winters. Marrakech is famous for the highest temperature, here in the summer months it exceeds forty degrees. The coast near Casablanca has a cooler climate, in this place the cold currents of the Atlantic influence the weather.



Capital and major resorts

Every year, Morocco is visited by many tourists from all over the world, the state attracts with its unique natural attractions and rich historical past. The capital of Morocco - Rabat is an ancient city and the second largest in the country. In Rabat, many architectural monuments and museums of Moroccan art are concentrated. Among the attractions should be noted the Great Mosque and the Church of the Resurrection, the royal palace Dar al-Mahsen and the Andalusian gardens. Morocco is a country of exotic resorts, gorgeous beaches and amazing waves of the Atlantic and the Mediterranean Sea. The main resorts are cities such as Agadir and Marrakesh, Casablanca and Essaouira, Tangier and Al Jadida. Traveling around the country, you can use shuttle buses, this type of transport is very developed here. In addition, a central railway network operates well in central and northern Morocco.



Beaches

Beautiful sandy beaches stretch across the coastline. Basically, they are municipal, but there are also few private beaches, equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds. One of the most famous is the beach of Legzira, which is located near the resort town of Agadir. This place is amazingly beautiful and attracts millions of photographers from around the world. Throughout its length, there are wide beach areas with beautiful cliffs with stone arches sliding right into the ocean waters.

Hotels

Morocco is a country with luxury hotels and hotel complexes, made in the Arabic style. Most of them are located on the coast, just a few tens of meters from the seashore. Many hotels have spa resorts and fitness centers. Here, tourists will be offered unique facial and body treatments. Relaxing massages, aroma procedures, thalassotherapy will help restore strength, restore youth and beauty.



sights

A huge number of attractions in Morocco. The country, whose photos of exotic places will not leave anyone indifferent, is filled with amazing energy of the East. Casablanca is famous for Notre Dame Cathedral, the Great Mosque of Hassan and the ruins of the ancient city of Anfa. In the ancient capital of Marrakech, there is a unique square of Djemaa el Fna, here you can see real Moroccan exoticism.
