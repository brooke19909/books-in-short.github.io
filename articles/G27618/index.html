Hotel Phuket Diamond 4 and its Bulgarian counterpart

Thai hotel in Phuket - Diamond 4 - is located on a hill overlooking the sea. This rock separates the two famous beaches - Karon and Kata, so that from the hotel you can easily reach both. Phuket Airport is fifty kilometers from the hotel. Here we will make a brief overview of this hotel, its services and rooms. You will also get to know what the guests of the hotel think about this hotel. Interestingly, the hotel has a similar name, which is located on the other side of the globe, in Eastern Europe, namely in Bulgaria. Bulgarian fellow

Diamond 4 (Sunny Beach) is a resort hotel located in the village of this Eastern European country, known since Soviet times. Despite the fact that this hotel is usually positioned as a "second line", from it to the sea, as they say, is within easy reach. In addition, since the hotel is located in the very center of the resort, there are a lot of entertainments awaiting tourists right outside its walls - bars, discos, restaurants, boutiques and so on. Most of all, according to tourists, it is suitable for young people and couples with children. The nearest airport to Sunny Beach is Burgas. Before him no more than forty kilometers. Varna is located 120 km from the village. The Bulgarian hotel Diamond 4 is a four-story building. It has one hundred and forty-nine standard rooms, twelve studios and four luxurious apartments. The rooms are air-conditioned and the bathrooms have a hairdryer. Rooms also include a small fridge. In the hotel you can park your car, put things in a storage room, and valuables - in a safe. The hotel provides rental bicycles. Internet access is available for a fee. There are shops and a hairdresser in the lobby.
