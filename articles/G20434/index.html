Deposits

Keeping money at home, as most residents of our country did many years ago, is irrelevant today, if only because of inflationary processes in the country. To prevent depreciation of funds, citizens prefer to keep their funds on deposits in commercial banks. Deposits are different depending on the main conditions of the contract: minimum and maximum amount, interest rate, term, terms of prolongation, replenishment and withdrawal of funds.

Today, the banking system more than ever depends on the behavior of the population, the inflow or outflow of deposits from credit institutions. The share of deposits in the liabilities of financial institutions is constantly increasing. This has a positive effect on the economy, on the one hand, and the risk of dependence on the mood of the masses, on the other hand. Foreign and domestic experience of our country has repeatedly confirmed that the appearance of any rumors about the worsening situation in the country contributes to the outflow of savings from the financial sector, which automatically contributes to the banking crisis. A deposit insurance system has been invented as an internal mechanism to curb outflow of deposits. Today, deposit insurance systems operate in more than seventy countries around the world. Fifteen years ago, the insurance system was created in our country. She identified the main ways to protect the savings of the inhabitants of the country.
