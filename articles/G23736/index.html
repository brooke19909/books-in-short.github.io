Mud and bathing

The volcano resembles a lake (this is a crater). Various recreation centers and pensions located nearby offer to visit it. Mud

Mud volcano Tizdar is otherwise called "Blue Beam". Gray-blue mud became the basis for this second name. The source of this useful substance is the crater, and its reserves are constantly replenished from the bowels of the volcano. Scientists have calculated that the depth of the source is about twenty-five meters, but there is no way for a simple person to check it: as already mentioned, it is impossible to dive into the mud. But floundering on the surface of the source is truly good for health and spirit!





Dirt can be washed off not only in the sea. The tourist complex also has modern showers where you can wash yourself with fresh water. Water procedures are doubly pleasant, because the body gets tired after fighting with therapeutic mud, and it is irresistible to sleep. The shower can both calm and cheer.
