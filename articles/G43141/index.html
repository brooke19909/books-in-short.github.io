The best washing machine: choosing the latest technology

Choosing a washing machine is not easy. After all, every manufacturer claims that his devices are the best. Let's turn to the craftsmen who work in repair shops. These professionals know exactly which washing machine is the best.



Even 15 years ago, Bosch, Zanussi, Siemens and Miele were considered the most reliable. The best Bosch washing machine assembled in Germany could work without prevention for 20-25 years. Cars of this brand were considered the most expensive and reliable. Therefore, a proprietary washing machine, used, can still serve well.

Perhaps the best washing machine is going to Korea today. Repair masters are advised to pay attention to Samsung and LG. Why Korea Because there is high-quality industrial production and serious control at the Quality Control Department. Korean household appliance production lines are also successfully operating in the CIS. There are many service centers in Russia, and warranties and services are made at a high level.

If you follow the latest technological advances, then pay attention to LG. By the way, five years ago, the production of washing machines was not the main one for LG, but now their number is growing rapidly. It was LG that first developed and introduced direct drive. Therefore, the best washing machine today is marked by the words Direct Drive. Direct Drive Washers - A New Generation That Does Not Use A Pulley And Drive Belt. Fewer parts are a guarantee of reliability. By the way, the warranty on the engine installed in LG washing machines is 10 years. In addition, in comparison with previous generation machines, direct-drive washing machines practically do not vibrate and do not make noise, which indicates good balancing. In the drums of such machines, clothes are not tangled.



Recently, LG began mass production of direct-drive washing machines that use DD Plus technology. Such devices themselves choose the optimal washing algorithm, based on the weight of the laundry, dispense the supply of water and washing powder.

If a child is expected in the family, then you should buy a LG washing machine with a vertical loading. The best top-loading washing machine using LG True SteamTM steam laundry technology was awarded the U.S. Asthma and Allergy Foundation award in 2013. This machine uses less water and successfully fights dust mites. In addition, things can not be ironed after steaming. Today it is the best washing machine for baby clothes, officially recognized as the product of the year in the United States.

The new LG True SteamTM washing machine itself updates software over the Internet. This device can also be controlled using a computer or smartphone.



In addition to Korean washing machines, today there are many devices on sale developed in other countries. Each machine should take a closer look, study reviews on the Internet.

In order for the washing machine to serve for a long time, you should buy it in a specialized store. Only in this case you will be provided with free service during the warranty period.
