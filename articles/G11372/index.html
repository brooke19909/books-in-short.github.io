How to make do-it-yourself towel cakes gift arrangements? Master class in three versions

A towel is a practical and demanded item in the household. Therefore, this particular item can be a good and necessary gift for both a woman and a man. But so that this present does not look simple and ordinary, we suggest you arrange it in the form of an interesting composition. You can make a cake out of towels with your own hands easily and quickly. Such a gift will pleasantly surprise and delight the hero of the occasion. How is such a composition performed? You can learn the technology for making terry desserts from the following instructions.



New Year's cakes from towels (master class). Stage of preparation for the creative process

In order to perform a similar composition, you will need the following materials:

  • 1 bottle of champagne;
  • tailor pins;
  • thick cardboard or plastic base from under this cake;

  • double-sided tape;

Stage of the New Year's composition of towels in the form of a cake

Lay out a towel on the table. We put one more on top of it. Now we fold them along several times to get a strip about 10 centimeters wide. We wrap this blank around a bottle of champagne. The edges are fixed with pins. We frame the product with a ribbon and tie it on a bow. The lower layer of the composition "Cake of towels" with your own hands is made.

We do the upper tier of the product in exactly the same way, only in the work we use one terry item. We decorate the gift with decorative elements. Spiral out the tinsel. Over the satin ribbon we let the beads. Decorate the top of the bottle of champagne with artificial snowflakes, attaching them to tape. Optionally, you can supplement the composition with other New Year's decorative ornaments.

We transfer the product to the base made of cardboard. The bottom part of the plastic packaging from under the real cake is also ideal as a stand. From a roll of transparent wrapping paper, cut a large piece. We put a composition on it in the center. We lift the corners of the paper and fix it with tape. So, a beautiful and original gift is ready - a cake from towels. You can see photos of such products in this article and focus on them while doing your own work.

Wedding Present: "dessert" of terry compositions. We prepare materials for creativity

Towel cakes, the master class on the manufacture of which you can read below, look very elegant, beautiful and spectacular. In order to make such a gift for the newlyweds, you will need such items and tools:

  • white towels 50x100 cm in size - 4 pieces;
  • a white lace napkin with a diameter of at least 35 cm - 1 piece;
  • lace napkins 20 cm in diameter - 2 pieces;
  • thick cardboard;
  • gum stationery;
  • satin tape;
  • artificial flowers (roses);
  • double-sided tape;
  • decorative figures of the bride and groom.




Workshop on the implementation of a wedding gift from terry towels

Want to learn how to make a wedding cake towel towel? Study the following description.

We put all the towels three times along. Twist the roll of three towels. We fix it with an elastic band. From above we close it with a satin ribbon, tie a magnificent bow. From the fourth terry item we make a small "roll" and fix it in the same way as the previous one.

From cardboard we cut out a round stand. We put a large lace napkin on it. We set the lower tier of the cake on top. We cover it with a smaller napkin. We put a small towel blank on it. We decorate the top of the product with the second same napkin. Using double-sided tape, we attach decorative figures to the newlyweds on it. We trim the artificial roses to the desired size and make out the composition with them. We pack a cake of towels, made as a gift for a wedding, in transparent wrapping paper in the same way as described in the previous part of the article.



Gift arrangement of terry towel "Cupcake". Learning to make such a cake. What is needed for this?

You can complete a cute little composition from one terry item in just a few minutes. Such a ‚Äúdessert‚Äù will be an excellent present for work colleagues or girlfriends. For its implementation, we prepare the materials indicated in the list:

  • double sided tape;

  • a wide spool of thread or other similar object.

We perform the composition ‚ÄúCupcake‚Äù from a rushnyk

Learning to make towel cakes. The master class presented to your attention tells how to "turn" a small terry object into a cute and tender piece of cake in the form of a cupcake.



We wrap the cup with the paper of the desired color and fix it with tape. Lay out a towel on the table. We bend its edges to the center. It turns out the blank in the form of an envelope. We do the same manipulations with its tips again. The square should be so large as to fit in a cup. We put the spool of thread in the middle of the terry piece.

Turn the towel over, while holding the reel so that it does not move. We embrace the product with the palm of your hand so that we get such a "bag". We remove the coil. We try to do this very carefully so that the workpiece from the bench hammer does not change its shape. Insert the corners of the towel into the cup. On top of a double-sided tape, attach a decorative berry or small fruit. We pack the product in transparent paper and draw up a ribbon. A cupcake from a towel is ready.
