How to Change Date in Galaxy S III - Beginner's Guide

Date and time are the initial settings for each phone. They are familiar to all people working with mobile devices. Next, we will talk about how to change the date in the Galaxy S III. Owners of Samsung phones often have problems with this task. Why do they appear? What needs to be done to resolve date and time failures? How is the learning option configured? Answering all these questions is easier than it sounds!

First steps

How to change date in Galaxy S III mini? It is important to understand that before this you will have to carry out one small operation. It will help fix problems with changing the time and calendar.

It's about disabling the synchronization of the parameters of the smartphone with the Internet. If this option is enabled, then setting the date and time manually will fail. All settings will be lost.



How to change the date in Galaxy S III ? To have this opportunity, before starting the procedure, you must:

  1. Open the function menu of the device. Find the image of the gear there and click on it. This is the "Settings" item.
  2. Select the line "Date in time".
  3. Click on the first button in the list that appears. It is called "Time and Date."
  4. Several functions will appear on the screen. It is necessary to put a check mark next to the "Turn off" operation.

Saving these changes will disable the synchronization of the date and time of the gadget with the Internet. Now we can confidently say that the process of adjusting the parameters will succeed.

Customize date

How to change the date in Galaxy S III ? Once the synchronization is turned off, you can easily put the idea into practice.

Each user must adhere to the following instructions:

  1. Turn on cellphone. Go to the main menu of the gadget.
  2. Select "Applications" - "Settings".
  3. Go to the "Date and Time" section.
  4. Click on "Set Date".
  5. Using the switches, enter the necessary parameters and click on "Install".

Time is changing in a similar way. On this all the manipulations end. You can close the phone settings and enjoy the result.

  1. Go to "Applications" - "Settings".
  2. Open the menu item "Date and time".
  3. Uncheck the boxes next to "Automatic time detection" and "Automatic belt detection".
  4. Click on "Set Date / Time". Sometimes the corresponding buttons are simply called "Date" and "Time."
  5. Set the necessary parameters. Click on "Save / Install".
