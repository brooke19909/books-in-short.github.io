General impression

Fans of the sea and the sun should pay attention to such a tourist destination as Sochi, Adler. The hotel "Velvet Seasons", "Catherine's Quarter" - this is not just a hotel, but a full-fledged resort town, consisting of several "districts". In addition, the developed infrastructure allows you to find everything you need for a comfortable stay and good rest, without leaving the complex. The only thing lacking in all this splendor is a friendly and warm attitude from the staff. Unfortunately, arrogance than participation is more likely to be encountered here.

But such a problem as the movement of cars across the territory is likely to remain. Given that not all drivers comply with speed limits, it would not hurt to install more speed bumps or involve traffic controllers.

Nevertheless, from the windows of the rooms it is not always possible to enjoy the azure waves, and the reason for this is the dense development. Be sure to specify when booking where the windows of the apartments go, so that instead of beautiful landscapes you do not contemplate the concrete walls of the neighboring building.
