How to make a pirate hat out of paper - several options

Boys love to portray heroic heroes, among which pirates occupy a prominent place. The costume of your favorite character can be worn for any occasion, a matinee in kindergarten or school. Often parents rent a suit in the studio, throwing large amounts of money in a single day. If you make a minimum of effort, then you yourself can in a short period of time make an excellent outfit that will be unique.

In the article, we will consider how to make a pirate hat out of paper, because this is the main attribute of the costume. A vest and black pants can be bought at the store, but a headpiece will have to be created independently. There are several options for its manufacture, we will figure out further how to make each of them step by step. The photographs presented in the article will help to complete the work faster and verify the result with the samples.

Easiest option

You can make a pirate hat yourself from paper by cutting it out along the drawn contours. First you need to measure the circumference of the child‚Äôs head and make a hoop out of thick paper, on which the front part of the hat will be attached. Before connecting the ends of the tape, try it on the boy‚Äôs head so as not to redo the work later.

Separate from a black or red thick paper cut out the shape of the headgear, as in the photo above. In the center is a small rounded elevation, which on both sides drops to a thin strip. In the middle of the hat, a white circle is glued on which the pirate emblem is painted - "Jolly Roger".

How to make a pirate hat out of paper, you already understood. It remains only to attach the front of the hat to the paper hoop. This can be done with a stapler, with paper clips or glued with a glue gun, PVA glue is also suitable.

Origami Pirate Hat

You can clearly see how to make a pirate hat out of paper with your own hands for boys in the photo located below in the article. From a sheet of paper by folding according to this scheme, a triangular hat is made, which is decorated and put on a child‚Äôs head. Let's look at how to make such a hat step by step.

In order to choose the right paper size for folding, first make a hat from the newspaper, folding origami from a whole sheet. If the size fits the boy, then take thick paper in A-3 or A-2 format. Act accordingly to ordinal numbers on the circuit. First, the sheet is folded in half, then the corners are bent, connecting them in the center of the workpiece. The lower excess rectangles of paper rise up on one side and the other. Then, with your hand inside, expand the future hat, making a square. It remains only to tuck the lower corners up, bending the square diagonally. After trying on, start decorating.

Hat design

How to make a pirate hat out of paper, you already know, now we will consider its decoration. Most often, when designing such an attribute, black is used. The finished origami hat can either be pasted over with black colored paper, or painted with gouache with a brush. The pirate emblem is attached with applique or painted with white paint after the background dries.

It looks interesting hat, which left a bend from below. To do this, in work on origami, folds are completed on the diagram under number 7. To prevent the sheet from unfolding, the edges of the paper sticking out to the sides are coated with PVA glue. Connect in the same way the ends of the rectangular parts from the bottom of the hat. The semicircular cut of paper in the center of the strip, highlighted by a white border, looks original.

Double hat

Next, consider the option of how to make a pirate paper hat with your own hands for boys from two halves. The drawing below shows how to draw them. First, a stencil is made, then it is circled on thick black paper and two identical parts are cut out. On the one that will be front, attach the logo of the "jolly Roger".

The narrow edges of the patterns are interconnected with paper clips or glue. It is more convenient to place a bezel from a strip of paper 5-6 cm wide in the middle. In this way, the hat will stay better on the boy‚Äôs head and will not slip while moving in the eyes.

In the center of the picture there is a blindfold pattern. If desired, you can cut it and attach it on a rope. So that the paper does not rub the child, it is better to make such an accessory from a sheet of felt. It is soft and pleasant to the skin of the face.

Ready product

After a written explanation of how to make a pirate hat out of paper, compare your craft with a finished hat. As you can see, you can slightly modify the shape, making the narrow edges higher. The top of the hat has a few centimeters on a flat surface.

They draw a pirate emblem on a white sheet, and then draw a black marker around the contours to make it look voluminous. If you do not have artistic abilities - it does not matter, you can print such a popular drawing on a printer by taking the image of a skull with bones on the Internet.
