Location

For the construction of a new residential complex, a promising, and nowadays, prestigious direction of the south-west of New Moscow was chosen. Many of those who are in the process of choosing a new building are wondering how to get to the Nikolin Park residential complex. Everything is extremely simple: the owners of their own cars will be able to get to the complex along the Kaluga or Kiev highway (it is only 3 km from the Moscow Ring Road). Moreover, a separate public transport route has been launched from the Teply Stan metro station in the direction of the new microdistrict.

Ecology

Reviews about the project are positive. All those who managed to visit the site are impressed by its scope and the zeal with which the developer is trying to maintain the forest belt. Indeed, construction work is carried out extremely carefully and carefully in order to save every square meter of forest. And all this for the comfort of the inhabitants of dusty and crowded Moscow. You no longer have to dream about your own country house or cottage, because apartments in the residential complex "Nikolin Park" have incorporated all the advantages of urban and suburban life. The air is excellent, just a few minutes walk from the complex flows the river Sosenka.





Transport accessibility

The development plan of the Leninsky district has already laid the construction of the metro. Moreover, the reconstruction of the Kaluga highway is ongoing : new interchanges will appear that minimize the load on the highway.

Infrastructure

Own infrastructure is what attracted interest holders and current residents of the Nicolin Park residential complex. Photos of the facility and owners' reviews confirm that in addition to playgrounds and sports grounds, a recreation area and a barbecue, a kindergarten and a medical center where real professionals work were built on its own fenced area. Moreover, for all car owners here is an excellent garage complex, designed for 730 cars. Guest parking for 210 cars will save your friends and relatives from the need to look for a free place. At the stage when the facility was commissioned, almost all apartments are populated, we can draw conclusions about how successful the project turned out. The developer really thought through everything to the smallest detail, making the life of the residents of the complex convenient, as comfortable and safe as possible.





On the territory of the residential complex there is an excellent kindergarten with a swimming pool and its own territory of 1 hectare - children have places to play and frolic. There are no problems with placing children in this institution. Shops, pharmacies, restaurants and cafes, a car wash - all this really is in the complex. In terms of infrastructure, the Leninsky district is attractive for life: at present, there are several educational schools in the immediate vicinity, as well as the most prestigious universities of the capital.

Security

Living in the middle of the forest is not entirely safe. That is why many were skeptical of the complex. But all doubts instantly disappeared when the developer not only fenced off the territory, but also equipped it with security posts with a round-the-clock video surveillance system. Moreover, residents of the complex note that entry into the territory is by passes.
