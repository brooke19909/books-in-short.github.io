After Crimea was annexed to Russia, it became fashionable among wealthy people to buy land in the south of the country and erect buildings for summer vacations there. Castle history

For the first time, this building was erected from wood for a retired general after the Russo-Turkish war. It was a kind of cottage, which later changed both the owners and their appearance. The general, who owned this site and the construction of the first wooden house, called it the castle of love.

The look that this castle now has was given to it by the industrialist Steingel. Many believe that the German by birth missed his homeland so much that he wanted to have something in his eyes that could remind her of it. That is why Baron von Steingel approved the sketch and began construction. The Gothic castle resembled the culture of the Middle Ages and the knightly buildings of that time.

During the period when the First World War began, the castle was redone from the living quarters into a restaurant. Due to its unique location, it was in great demand among holidaymakers. "Swallow's Nest" had a lot of positive feedback from the officers and their ladies. During the Civil War, the building was abandoned. After the Soviet regime came to the peninsula, this building was transferred to the common possession.

Castle reconstruction

In the thirties of the last century, an earthquake brought down a part of the garden and a platform near the house, but the building itself remained intact. In the second half of the twentieth century, the authorities found funds for the reconstruction of the castle. This was done by strengthening the cliff and building a new observation deck. The building was opened for tourists only at the beginning of the twenty-first century. Over time, this castle became one of the symbols of the Crimea.

In 2015, the state of the castle was once again declared in emergency condition; specially allocated money was spent on its repair. Currently, the palace-castle "Swallow's Nest" is open to the public. Tourists who want to see this beautiful building with their own eyes aspire here. The cultural purpose of the castle

In addition to excursions and a small cafe in the castle, a spontaneous souvenir market opened. Images with a recognizable castle are sold on various objects, in miniature and in huge size. The Ministry of the Republic has ideas about how this monument of history and architecture will be used in the future. Tips of tourists who visited Crimea

  • It is necessary to give preference to cash rather than bank cards, as in some parts of the peninsula there are still problems with installing ATMs, third-party banks charge a fee for withdrawing money, not all stores have equipment for cashless payments.
  • You need to have several types of sunscreen with you, since there is not much shade on the beaches - it is better to take care of sun protection in advance.
  • Correctly develop your routes, ask the local population, it happens that the information posted on Internet resources, greatly reduces the distance from one object to another.
  • It is better to study information about insects and plants of the Crimea, there are spiders, the bite of which is very unpleasant; There are plants that can cause skin burns.
  • When searching for places where it is better to stay, you need to focus on the reviews of tourists or mentioning a specific place in a search engine.
  • The best way to find a place to stay is to book. No need to expect that a good guesthouse can be found upon arrival. As a rule, rooms in such places are busy.
  • Bus connections are well developed in Crimea, therefore it is better not to use the services of unfamiliar taxi drivers.
  • Be careful about your food choices. Do not try dishes from unknown fish, buy only fresh fruits.
