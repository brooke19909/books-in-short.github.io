The best hotels of Koblevo: photos and reviews of tourists

In Ukraine, the village of Koblevo is located on the Black Sea. Administratively, it refers to the Berezansky district of the Nikolaev region. It includes one of the largest resorts, bearing the same name with the village. At the same time, more than 25,000 tourists can relax here.

The benefits of a holiday in Koblevo

People come to Koblevo from different countries: Belarus, Russia, Poland, Moldova and others. To explain such popularity is quite simple. In addition to relaxing on the coast, here you can visit the dolphinarium, restaurants, water parks and other entertainment venues. You can also go on an excursion to the famous vineyards, which are located on the banks of the Tiligul estuary. Hotels in Koblevo are very popular . Reviews of many of them are mostly positive. Guests note a convenient location, usually on the coast, a large list of services, cleanliness and comfort of rooms. Also, special attention should be paid to prices. They change throughout the year. The highest are at the height of the beach season. Let's look at the hotels that received the most positive reviews.

Koblevo hotels: prices

The question of the cost of housing in Koblevo is worth dwelling in more detail. First of all, here prices fluctuate depending on the season. For example, in May or September, accommodation will cost about half the price. At the height of the season, economy class rooms will have to pay more than 1000 rubles. Tourists, who often relax in Koblevo, are advised to book accommodation in advance. Thanks to this, you can save a decent amount. So, let's look at the rates in the best hotels of this resort.

  • "Paradise." This hotel offers deluxe and junior suites. The cost will be approximately from 1000 to 1300 rubles.
  • The Atlantic. The hotel fund has two-room rooms, as well as double and quadruple rooms. Accommodation prices rise from July 1 to August 25. They make up 1700 - 3700 rubles. By the end of summer, they are declining: the minimum is 1000 rubles, the maximum is 1900 rubles.
  • "Elysium". Average prices at this park-hotel start from 4000 rubles. For the class number, the standard will need to pay 4600 rubles.
  • "Dolphin". In the face value of the hotel there is a villa, family suite, deluxe suite, standard double and triple. For the accommodation of one person per day, it will be necessary to pay about 1400 rubles. Breakfast is already included in this price.
  • "Birch". This park-hotel offers a one-room attic-type room, as well as a standard class, European standard, junior suite, family. The cost varies from 1100 to 3300 rubles.
  • "Cote d'Azur 2". In the face value of this hotel there are luxury and standard rooms, as well as cottages. The cost of the latter in September is about 800 rubles. from one person. Living in a triple "standard" will cost about 500 rubles. per room.
  • "VIP hotel." In this hotel you can rent two-room and one-room suites. Prices start at 2,900 rubles. The price includes breakfast.
  • Brazil You can rent deluxe and junior suites at this hotel. The cost of living varies from 990 to 1300 rubles.

"Paradise"

Hotel Paradise (Koblevo) is 5 minutes from the sea. It consists of three buildings. For residents, 200 rooms of various categories are provided. All of them are equipped with comfortable modern furniture, TV, shower stalls. Each has a balcony. Water supply, both cold and hot, is carried out around the clock. On the territory of the guests can relax in the gazebos, work out on sports fields, a play town is provided for children. The hotel has a large conference room and a concert stage. Guests can eat in the dining room or cafe. Discounts apply for children. Without extra bed, a child under 12 years old is settled for free.



The visitors rated the work of the staff, room comfort, cleanliness and additional services the highest rated. Some of the tourists noted the inconvenience regarding the request for cleaning the room. In order for it to be produced, it is necessary to leave a request at the reception.

Atlantic

Where is it better to settle, coming on vacation to Koblevo? Atlantic is a hotel located directly near the Black Sea. The building in which the rooms are located has 8 floors. It was built just 3 years ago. The rooms are equipped with a refrigerator, air conditioning, TV. They have a bathroom and a bathroom. The hotel offers beautiful sea views. There is a swimming pool on site, there is a parking place, but you can use it for an additional fee. The building has an elevator. Nearby is an amusement park.

The overall impression of visitors is mostly positive. The only thing that some of them highlighted was the lack of dishes in the rooms, loud music in the evenings. A significant advantage is the ban on smoking in the hotel building, free internet, the presence of a tennis court and inexpensive shops in the area.



"Elysium"

Elysium Hotel (Koblevo) has 55 rooms. Family-style rooms are available for guests. Each room has a minibar, air conditioning, TV. On the territory there is a swimming pool, restaurant, free Wi-Fi is distributed. Laundry and dry cleaning services are also available. The hotel organizes a shuttle bus. Taxi calls are free. Considering that there are no five-star hotels in Koblevo, the park-hotel Elysium can still satisfy any requirements of each client at the highest level. On the territory there is a gorgeous swimming pool, parking, free internet is available.

Feedback from visitors noted the unusual design of the rooms. Many of them have several levels. For example, on the first tier there is a dressing room, the next is the bathroom, and on the podium - a berth. Own beach, located 2 steps from the building, also did not leave tourists indifferent. The entire hotel area is perfectly clean, no admittance is allowed. The presence of a safe and secure parking is a significant advantage.



"Dolphin"

Hotel "Dolphin" (Koblevo) provides equipped rooms with modern furniture, LED TV, air conditioning. Each has a balcony with sea or pool views. Mosquito nets are installed on the windows. There are all accessories for ironing. The rooms are equipped with a kettle. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer and towels. Check-in with pets is possible by prior arrangement. This service is paid extra. Cleaning is done daily. A massage service is available on site for guests. Payments can only be made in cash.

The hotel received high marks in the following areas: cleanliness, staff work, and comfort.



"Birch"

Hotel "Birch" (Koblevo) has more than 150 rooms. At the same time, about 600 people can live in it. A huge advantage is its own beach, whose length is 100 meters. Near the hotel there is a water park. The hotel is surrounded by pine forest. Thanks to this plantation and the proximity of the sea, the air here is clean and healing. The rooms provided for the stay of tourists are equipped with comfortable furniture, a TV, a refrigerator. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout. There is a balcony with sea or forest views. In summer, you can undergo wellness treatments. There is a bar, restaurant, a conference room and a parking lot. Guests have access to sports facilities, a swimming pool and childcare facilities.



"Cote d'Azur-2"

Like many other hotels in Koblevo, "Cote d'Azur-2" is located next to the sea coast. It is 20 meters away. There are 50 rooms for living. All are equipped with necessary furniture, air conditioning, a refrigerator and a TV. Some rooms have a private bathroom. According to reviews, all the staff are qualified and friendly. The rooms are clean, the repair is fresh, the design is done in soft pastel colors. Cleaning is done daily. The hotel organizes hiking, there are parking spaces on site. Children under 6 years stay free of charge. An extra bed is subject to a ten percent discount. Only one extra bed can be accommodated in one room. Many visitors noted the good infrastructure near the hotel. There are numerous clubs, supermarkets, pharmacies and a market. The economy class room has a washbasin.

VIP hotel





This hotel is built directly on the beach. For accommodation there are only 13 rooms. All of them are made in modern design, equipped with comfortable new furniture, necessary household appliances, a safe. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant or bar. Convenience stores are located next to the hotel. Those who want to actively spend time at sea are invited to rent a jet ski or ride a banana. In the reviews, visitors noted good service, qualified staff, high-speed Wi-Fi, comfortable and modern furniture, good food.

Brazil





Hotel Brazil is just 30 meters from the beach. For accommodation, 15 equipped rooms are provided. They are equipped with showers, air conditioning and modern furniture. For your convenience, the rooms are equipped with a fridge and satellite TV. Like many other Koblevo hotels, Brazil offers sports and playgrounds for entertainment. There are rides for children, there is an entertainment complex Bora-Bora. The beaches are equipped with sun loungers, parasols, changing cabins. On the territory there are bars, cafes, restaurants. Guests can visit the beauty salon and hairdresser. Children under 7 years old are accommodated for free. If you need an extra bed in the room, you will need to pay about 400 rubles.
