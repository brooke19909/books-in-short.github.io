Fishing in the waters of Lake Gladyshevsky. The reserved places of Karelia

Unfortunately, and perhaps fortunately, not all fishermen know about the hidden places of Gladyshevsky Lake. It is not in the top lists and lists of fishing reservoirs, the organizers of the tours have just begun to develop these places. But local Karelian lovers to sit with a fishing rod and the most desperate members of St. Petersburg fishing clubs for many years come to this lake for a rich catch. It also attracts connoisseurs from more distant lands. But still this corner remains one of the most mysterious fishing places. However, like many other natural resources of the inaccessible Karelia.





History and geography of Lake Gladyshevsky

The lake lies in a hollow among steep, coniferous hills. Its depth reaches 14 meters, but there is information about depressions up to 24 meters. From the north, the Great River flows into the lake, which connects it with Lake Nakhimovsky. In the southeast, the Gladyshevka River flows from the lake, carrying water to the Chernaya River, and then to the Gulf of Finland. This river is replete with rapids, waterfalls, dams, and even at the very Gladyshevsky lake the character is rather complicated: numerous ice keys are breaking from its bottom, giving rise to cold undercurrents. But these northern places have always attracted visitors with amazing natural beauty and purity. At different times in the villages scattered along the shores of the lake, cultural and art workers stayed: writers M. E. Saltykov-Shchedrin and A. M. Gorky, poets Demyan Bedny and V. V. Mayakovsky, artist I. N. Kramskoy. The outstanding Russian doctor S.P. Botkin lived and worked in the vicinity of the lake. And the famous revolutionary, researcher and correspondent VD Bonch-Bruevich describes in his chronicles how he met V.I. Lenin, who was having a rest in the party cottage, in these places.





The underwater world of Gladyshevsky lake

Solitude and natural purity easily explain the abundance of fish living in lake waters. Perch, roach, ruff, scavenger - the easiest fishing prey that pecks on baubles, worms, cut dace. But to get zander, trout or pike, you need a lot of skill, the right equipment, the right bait. By the way, the pike here is of unprecedented size, there are specimens up to five kilograms. On Gladyshevsky lake lovers of all legal types of fishing will find a thing to their liking. Here you can sit with a fishing rod on the bridge, set up night snacks, swim away on a punt or a rubber boat and try your luck with a spinning rod.





Non-stop Fish Inspection

Without leaving a single chance to poachers, several departmental boats constantly plow Lake Gladyshevsky. Fishing here is so good in many respects thanks to the efforts of fish supervision. It is forbidden to set nets on the lake, and there really are none. You can only travel by rowing boat, the use of motors is not allowed. Strict rules for catching fish during spawning: warning signs located everywhere, careful checks, constant patrolling of the water area - all to maintain the proper level of fish population. Solidarity is also shown by fishermen: they catch only in permitted places, release the trifle that was caught back, so that it grows by the next season.
