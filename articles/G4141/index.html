Hotel Siagas Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece / Peloponnese): photos, reviews

Planning a vacation in sunny Greece? Do you like a leisurely beach holiday? Is it uncritical for you the lack of a dozen noisy discos, bars and restaurants just around the corner from the hotel? Would you like to spend a vacation enjoying nature and the sea, while not planning to spend much on a hotel? Then, as a place to relax, be sure to consider the three-star Ciagas Beach Hotel.



Siagas Beach Hotel 3 *: photo, description

"Siagas Beach" is located on the very shore of the Saronic Gulf, 60 kilometers from Athens and 20 km from Loutraki. It is located in the countryside, away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Athens Airport is 75 kilometers away. There is a bus stop near the hotel. From here you can easily get to Loutraki and the capital of Greece. Buses run according to the schedule, which can be taken from the administrator at the reception.

Siagas Beach is not a new hotel. However, it regularly carries out repairs. Therefore, it is quite well maintained and clean. The hotel building accommodates 101 rooms. There are 1-, 2- and 3-bed apartments. All of them are equipped with the necessary furniture, TV, telephone, air conditioning, mini fridge, bathroom, balcony. All rooms have free Wi-Fi. Cleaning is carried out three times a week.

The hotel offers the following types of food: all inclusive, half board, full board or breakfast only. So you can choose what suits you best. The hotel has an outdoor pool, a terrace with sun loungers, a bar. Very close is the hotel's own beach. Also at Siagas Beach you can rent a car or book an excursion. In addition, the hotel offers its guests an airport shuttle service for an additional fee.

An excellent source of useful information about hotels are reviews of real tourists. In them they describe their personal experience of living in a particular hotel. Food

The hotel offers its guests several dining options. However, most tourists are advised to choose half board (breakfast and dinner) when booking.
