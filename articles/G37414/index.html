A winning poker hand

There are many varieties of poker. The Texas species is the most popular. Numerous competitions are held both in America and around the world. Often Texas poker tournaments are broadcast on television. It will be discussed in the article.

Players compete among themselves, the one with the most powerful combination wins. You can take a bank in two cases:

  • money goes to the last player left with the cards (the rest are discarded);
  • all opened up - the one with the strongest combination of cards in poker won.

Sometimes such competitions bring huge profits, and sometimes ruin gambling people.

Poker. Game rules and combinations

The process is carried out clockwise. Landmark - dealer, he is giving out cards. According to the rules of the game, it changes clockwise on each deal of cards. The two closest opponents who sit to the left of the dealer are called the first blind and the second blind. They place bets automatically. If the established con of the game is 1/2 dollar, then the first bets $ 1, the second - $ 2.

Texas poker is divided into 4 stages:

  • preflop;
  • flop
  • Thorne
  • river.

Preflop Everyone is dealt 2 cards. The player who sits after the second blind starts the circle, ends the second blind. Everyone is allowed to fold their cards (fold). You can make a bet commensurate with the current (call) and raise it (raise).



Flop 3 cards are dealt to the center of the table. They are common to all. These 3 and their 2 (2 + 3 = 5) make up the combination. Based on it, a move is made: you can skip (if no one made a bet), level, raise or reset.

Thorne. Fourth community card. A new betting circle is being made.

River 5 (last) card on the table. The final round of betting, which decides everything in this distribution (party).

Next comes the showdown. If at the table there are 2 or more players who have not dropped, then they must open them, showing them to everyone. The one who has the highest combination of cards in poker has won. If they are the same, the bank is divided.



Next begins a new batch. The dealer becomes the next in a circle. The game at the table continues while people remain. At any time, a new person can join the process, sitting on an empty seat. However, he will be required to immediately place a bet that is equal to the amount of the second blind. Each player can also stop the game at any time by discarding cards and leaving the table.

Texas poker. Senior card combinations

  • Royal flash - 5 cards of the same suit from ace to tens in a row.
  • Straight Flash - in a row of 5 cards of the same suit.
  • Four of a kind - 4 identical cards (4 queens, 4 triples, etc.).
  • Full house - 2 cards of one rank and 3 of another (2 deuces and 3 triples).
  • Flash - 5 cards of the same suit.
  • Street - 5 cards in a row (6, 7, 8, 9, 10).
  • Set - 3 identical cards (3 ladies, 3 sevens).
  • Two pairs - 2 identical cards plus 2 more identical (2 fives and 2 aces).
  • One pair - 2 identical cards.
  • The best card - if there are no combinations, then the one with the highest card wins.
