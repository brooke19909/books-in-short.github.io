MOMs 3 * Hotel (Kemer): review, description, reviews

Turkey is the undoubted, undeniable leader of the tourist market in the Mediterranean region. This country was able to occupy and maintain such top positions by canceling visas for citizens of the CIS countries, honing services, introducing the All Inclusive system and, of course, encouraging the tourism business through state subsidies. Thus, Turkey, especially the Mediterranean coast, has become a kind of overseas homeland for our compatriots. According to statistics, 73% of all Russian and Ukrainian travelers who are planning a vacation over the hill for the first time fly to Turkey.

MOMs Hotel 3 *, Kemer. general information

This small, cozy hotel is located 5 km from the city of Kemer, the center of the resort of the same name, 100 meters from its own beach. Near the hotel there is a pretty park and green spaces. The hotel has been operating since 2003. The area around the hotel is 1.4 square meters. km The complex has a swimming pool, a terrace for relaxation, umbrellas, sunbeds, a platform for animation, basketball, its own private beach, restaurant, pool bar.

Hotel MOMs 3 * has no facilities for disabled guests.

Pets are not allowed.

The official check-in time is 12:00, eviction is 14:00.





Hotel location

MOMs Hotel 3 * is located in the Camyuva district, almost in the center of the picturesque mountain region of Kemer. The main city of Kemer is only 5 km away. Antalya International Airport is 55 km away. It is important to note that by Turkish standards this is a very modest distance. Most hotels in western Kemer or Alanya are 200-300 km away from the airport. So the shuttle bus can take half a day.



It will take a little more than an hour to visit the Kursunlu Falls and Nature Reserve or the city of Antalya with all its attractions. The most famous sights of Turkey - Pamukkale Cave - drive 2.5 hours.

100 m from the hotel has its own private beach.

The nearest hotel is Asdem Garden.

Nutrition

The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. In Turkey, it is customary even in the most budget hotels to provide a decent set of services and feed guests according to All Inclusive. Slavic travelers, who often like not only to eat unlimited, but also to drink unlimited, have chosen this food system very much. Of course, in hotels of such a budget price category as Hotel Moms Bang Teens 3 *, the All inclusive concept does not imply daily gluttony marathons with gourmet dishes and delicacies. But at the same time, a decent selection of dishes is provided to guests.



All-inclusive at this hotel includes breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main buffet restaurant, local soft drinks and alcoholic drinks (beer, local home-made wine, crayfish) in the pool bar. The time frame of All Inclusive is 08:00 - 22:00.

Service

On the Antalya coast of Turkey, which is very carefully massively occupied by Russians and Ukrainians in the warm season, it is customary that even the most budget hotels work on All Inclusive, have their own beach, animation and several entertainment options. There is also a side effect of such needs, it is that hotel owners still need to save on something, and they often save on rooms. For example, the Hotel Teen MOM 3 * hotel has rather small apartments by European standards, but the range of services and entertainment is not inferior to four-star hotels.

Beach of MOMs Hotel 3 *

The hotel has its own pebble beach, which is 100 meters away from its territory. According to tourists' reviews, a walk to / from the beach takes about 6-7 minutes. On the beach there are free umbrellas and sunbeds, which are enough for everyone. Mattresses are paid. The entrance to the sea is pebble, gentle. The water is very clean, clear. The beach offers beautiful mountain views.



Hotel reviews

Kemer resort is well treated by Russian tourists. Probably does not exist on the entire long coastline of the hotel, where our compatriots would not rest. This has its advantages. Firstly, the hotels have studied the Russian consumer and can now sharpen their service to their needs. And secondly, for tourists who are going to some hotel for the first time, there is no problem collecting information about this place. An important source of useful, albeit slightly subjective information, is the reviews of tourists. Hotel MOMs 3 * has an average guest rating.



Their authors most praise the beach, uncrowded, guarded, with clean water, beautiful views. Also, the authors of positive reviews praise the beautiful, small, very cozy territory of the MOMs 3 * complex, a good location in a quiet place. The authors of negative and neutral reviews have a number of complaints about a varied diet, small rooms with fairly ancient appliances and furniture.
