Crocheted tops and t-shirts with patterns: photo, work order

With the onset of spring and summer, the attention of many knitters is aimed at finding a worthy model of an openwork top, shirt or blouse. There are quite a lot of them on the network, but unfortunately, many contain a description or diagram that does not fit the materials selected by the craftswoman. Do not be upset, because there are general rules and patterns, the use of which will allow you to create a summer product yourself.

Types of Tops

Top called a T-shirt on the straps with the most open neck and armholes. Crocheted tops and T-shirts (with schemes, of course, much easier to work with), are quite simple to manufacture. Based on the type of canvas they can be divided into the following types:

  • Simple non-fitted.
  • With wide straps.
  • With a bodice.

The product model is selected individually, taking into account personal preferences and capabilities of the knitter. The first type is most often preferred by women in adulthood, therefore patterns with a moderate number of holes are suitable for them.

Summer tops, crochet T-shirts for girls can be knitted from almost any yarn and with the use of an interesting ornament.

Simple top

The simple top model is considered the most universal.

It consists of two rectangular parts sewn along the side seams and on the shoulders. Each of the parts is a typesetting canvas. Square motifs - a universal pattern. They are easy to knit and connect.

Crocheted tops and T-shirts with patterns of continuous paintings or motifs are significantly different. The solid pattern does not require special attention of the knitter, while the typesetting takes a lot of time and effort.

The diagram shows a diagram of a fragment and openwork strapping, which is equipped with the lower edge of the product, the neck and armholes.



The sizes of each motif are also indicated here, but they can be different when using yarn with different parameters.

Crocheted tops and T-shirts with patterns consisting of motifs can be assembled during the manufacturing process or at the end of the work.



Stitching ready-made motives, rather than tying them in the last row, avoids blooming if a mistake is made.

Openwork tops, shirts, crochet blouses: models with a bodice

The presence of a bodice allows the product to more closely fit the silhouette and emphasizes its femininity. You can crochet a bodice in two ways:

  • Volumetric cups.
  • Yoke.

Details of the cups are knitted from the center, their shape takes into account the anatomical features of the female figure as much as possible. When using the coquette, the bodice is more even, but this method is much simpler.

The photo below shows a top with a bodice and an open back. The line of the chest is underlined with a lace, and instead of strapless ties are used.



The manufacture of such a product is not difficult, since the main fabric is rectangular, and the bodice is connected by a continuous fabric according to the pattern.

The basis for success for such models is a beautiful hem on the bottom and neat knitting.

T-shirt with straps

Such products include wide or narrow straps. As can be seen in the photo, the canvas has a rectangular shape with triangular sections along the upper edge.



In this case, the canvas is assembled from motifs located at an angle. This design allows you to achieve the necessary triangular edges. Crocheted tops and T-shirts with solid canvas patterns require a pattern. Abbreviations are performed by applying knitting to the drawn contour.

When the necessary shape is obtained, straps are sewn to the tops of the triangles and they are strapped to all open edges.

Straps can also be connected in the process of tying the top.
