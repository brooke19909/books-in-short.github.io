History of Belgorod. Description, sights of the city and interesting facts

The history of Belgorod contains a lot of different significant events, despite the fact that the city itself is quite small in area. A variety of events took place on its territory, which significantly influenced the development of all of Russia. A special mission near the city is related to defense. He was repeatedly subjected to the onslaught not only during the time of the Nazis, but also since the predominance of the Crimean Tatars.

Historical information

Historical chronicles say that by the gracious order of Tsar Fyodor Ioanovich, a city was built near the White Mountains, which should serve as a fortress to protect the southern territories from capture by the Tatars. So Belgorod appeared. The history of the city is inextricably linked with its original purpose.

It is known that the area was repeatedly raided by Crimean enemies. But the city successfully repelled the attacks led by governor Ivan Tataev.

Initially, the Belgorod fortress was located on the right bank of the North Donets. But during the Time of Troubles, it was completely destroyed. Almost all the inhabitants died, the city, like many others, was burned. Only in 1613, but on the left bank Belgorod was rebuilt again.

After the fire that happened in 1766 and destroyed many buildings, a new plan was adopted to restore the city. The main condition was that all the main buildings will be made of stone.

The history of Belgorod is briefly studied at school. Pupils learn that starting from the 19th century, industry is booming in the city and even real water supply appears.

Belgorod is becoming not only a beautiful, flourishing city, but also quite prosperous. However, the Great Patriotic War made adjustments. There were fierce battles in this area. Later, the city was awarded the Order of the Patriotic War.

Modern streets

It is curious that in the city you can walk along the streets of Gazovikov or Surgut. This is due to the fact that this area was developed by internally displaced persons from northern cities. They gave such names in memory of their former homeland.

The history of Belgorod is inextricably linked with the famous fellow countryman Vatutin. When a wide avenue was laid near Kharkov Mountain, it was decided to give him the name of the hero. But the catch was that there already existed the street of the same name, which they wanted to rename Rabochaya. Residents opposed, so in Belgorod is now a street and avenue to them. Vatutina.





Sculptures and history of Belgorod

The monuments contain many events that occurred in the area. So, the largest sculpture is the statue of Vladimir the Great. The monument is the largest composition in the world dedicated to the prince.

Despite the fact that historical information does not confirm the fact, it is with his figure that the foundation of Belgorod is connected. In the 90s, it was decided to erect a monument. Vladimir Krasnoy Sunny is depicted in a laurel wreath, holds the cross with his right hand, in the other - the shield, it seems to protect the people.





A monument was erected in memory of the Chernobyl accident in Belgorod. The sculpture is located on B. Khmelnitsky Avenue. The monument is a man leaning back and spreading his arms, as if trying to stop some kind of evil. Behind the figure are two sails and an image of an atom. The whole composition is made of copper.

Residents and the war in Afghanistan recall with pain. A memorial complex is dedicated to all the dead. It is located near the Battle of Kursk Museum and is crowned with a cross of black marble. The composition itself consists of a huge cube with entrance arches. In the center are the bells, symbolizing the loss. There are signs of citizens who have not returned from that war. At night, it is very moving and beautiful because of the backlight.

Recently, the streets of Belgorod are becoming more comfortable, sculptures of ordinary characters, frozen in everyday poses, also contribute to this. Such memos quickly became popular and symbolize good luck. One has only to touch them.

The janitor's composition became a symbolic sculpture. It was installed in 2006. A man has a broom in his hands, and a cat is nearby. There is a belief that if you pet the animal, then the desire will certainly come true.

No need to be surprised at the unusual decision to choose a janitor to immortalize in bronze. In 2009, Belgorod received the title of the cleanest city. An invaluable merit in this of its residents and, of course, hardworking janitors.





Some interesting

The history of Belgorod includes a curious fact. The city was originally famous as a shopping center selling vodka and lard. It is also called the area where the people first saw and heard the fireworks. The event occurred after the liberation from the Nazi invaders. Soldiers simultaneously fired all the regiments from weapons, paying tribute to what had happened.

No less interesting is the fact that Belgorod is famous for people who are able to look into the future and know how to heal. This is associated with the times of the Russian Tsar Alexei Fedorovich. The monarch equated such people with sorcerers, and everyone who was suspected of a witchcraft was sent to exile in Belgorod.

Belgorod now

The history of Belgorod is very curious, all the most interesting is connected with its defensive functions and expressed in sculptures. Now the city has a developed infrastructure and may well attract tourists.

Old buildings, beautiful streets, picturesque avenues, cozy boulevards and unusual monuments will be interesting to view.

Belgorod has an art and literary museum that you must visit. The Battle of Kursk Diorama collects many visitors every day. It is based on the reproduction of the famous tank battle near the village of Prokhorovka. The building itself is also of interest, it resembles a sail.

For recreation of citizens and tourists, the best place is the central park. The undoubted advantage is the fountain "Round dance", which previously housed an ordinary playground. After the reconstruction, a whole town appeared for entertainment.

Conclusion

The history of Belgorod allows you to see in miniature the development of Russia. Fires, raids, the development of trade and industry - all these are significant events for any Russian city.

For all the time there has been a lot of everything that is now immortalized in sculptures. Wandering the streets and looking at architecture and monuments, you can literally plunge into the past.

Now the city is developing dynamically. He is recognized as the cleanest and most comfortable, and this is a considerable merit of the inhabitants of Belgorod. On the streets, antiquity blends antiquity with modernity, and classic architecture decisions with innovative thoughts.
