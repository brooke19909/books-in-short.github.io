Agoy, hotels. Inns, Agoy. Private Hotels

Picturesque streets descending to the Black Sea, the southern sun and a favorable climate - this is what attracts many tourists to Agoy. The hotels of the village are also able to surprise their guests with comfortable rooms, convenient location, and most importantly - a high level of service, not inferior to European.

Location of the village of Agoy

The village of Agoy is located in the Krasnodar Territory, 12 km from Tuapse. It is located at the foot of the pass at the mouth of the Agoy River. Clean pebble beaches and sea water are the main advantages of the village. Agoya Pass separates Aguoy Pass from Tuapse. At its top is a monument to the fighters of the Taman army. There is also an observation deck, from which magnificent views of the coastal mountains, the Black Sea and the Agoy river valley open. Private hotels from here are also clearly visible, and many tourists staying in the hotels of the village like to look for their guest house with their eyes.





You can get to Agoy from Tuapse, using the services of regular buses, minibuses or taxis. Tuapse, in turn, has a train and bus connection with almost all cities of Russia, so everyone can get to the Black Sea for little money, where they will find the best conditions for spending summer vacations, not least of which mini-hotels . Agoy is able to give an unforgettable experience to both an adult and a child.

Hotel "Aphrodite": location

Aphrodite Hotel is a new five-story house located on the seashore. Near the hotel there is a water park and a dolphinarium, so vacationers can diversify their vacation with their visit at any time. The hotel has a grocery and gift shops, as well as parking, although paid.

50 meters from the Aphrodite is a pebble beach, which is famous for Aga. Hotels located near the sea, of course, are more expensive than those located in the middle of the village, but they are worth it.

35 meters from the Black Sea in a building erected in 2014, is located the Hotel Chocolate (Agoy). Hotel Maritel

Hotel Maritel (Agoy) is located at a distance of 150 meters from the pebbled Black Sea beach. For your stay the hotel offers 86 rooms of various categories, including suites, junior suites, standards and attics. All rooms, without exception, are equipped with comfortable beds, satellite TV, a telephone, free Wi-Fi, a refrigerator, a set of dishes, as well as a bathroom.

The hotel has a swimming pool, fitness center, Finnish sauna and parking. The price includes three meals a day. In addition, the hotel has a restaurant where vacationers can enjoy delicious dishes from its menu at any time. There are also a number of services that can be ordered at an additional cost. Among them: laundry service, luggage storage, ironing service, as well as a shuttle service.

Opinions of tourists about the rest in the village

Tourists who once rested in Agoy leave extremely positive reviews about this village. They are pleased with the spacious and sparsely populated beaches, the clear sea, the abundance of greenery, the absence of a railway, the presence of two rivers with blue clay, many shops and cafes. If negative reviews about Agoy appear, they are usually associated with hotel stays. Therefore, if you want to avoid such a problem, carefully approach the choice of place of residence.





In addition, the village offers a huge number of opportunities for excursions. After all, nearby are attractions such as the Kiseleva rock, a sandbank and a pass with an observation deck.
