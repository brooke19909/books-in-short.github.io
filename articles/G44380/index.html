5 * hotels: AMC Royal Resort (Egypt / Hurghada), photos and reviews of tourists

The 5 * AMC Royal Resort hotel will give its guests an unforgettable vacation on the Red Sea with a lot of entertainment and high quality service.

Where is the hotel situated

5 * AMC Royal Resort Resort is located in a picturesque area, which is located between Hurghada and El Gouna (the distance is 11 km and 13 km, respectively). The sea is literally at hand, because the hotel is located on a spacious private sandy beach.

Guest rooms

The hotel complex has 395 rooms, which are located in two five-story buildings. Guests of the 5 * AMC Royal Resort resort can enjoy several categories of apartments:

  • Suites are large two-room suites. The spacious bedroom has a huge bed and also has access to a private balcony overlooking a private sandy beach. The living room has large panoramic windows (also with sea views) and a set of comfortable upholstered furniture, which is laid out and can be used as an extra bed. The bathroom is equipped with a modern shower with the possibility of vertical hydromassage.
  • The family room is one spacious room. There are three single beds (or a small plus a double), as well as a folding soft sofa. The bathroom is equipped with a large bath.
  • Standard rooms are equipped with a king-size bed or two small ones. There is also a small sofa bed to accommodate an extra guest. The bathroom is equipped with a bath and a bidet.




Room features

Hotel AMC Royal Resort 5 * (Hurghada) made sure that the guest rooms had everything necessary for the complete comfort of holidaymakers. So, in the apartment the following amenities are available:

  • individual air conditioning system;
  • each room has access to a balcony or terrace (depending on the floor);
  • satellite television with support for Russian programs;
  • a telephone, which serves not only to contact the hotel administration, but also for international calls;
  • in the bathroom, without fail, there is a hairdryer;
  • the minibar is replenished daily with drinking water and sweet carbonated drinks;
  • electronic safe;
  • a special set for making tea and coffee (electric kettle, service, drinks).

Resort infrastructure

Despite the fact that the main attribute of any southern resort is the beach, this is not enough to satisfy the needs of tourists, who are increasing every year. The resort and hotel complex AMC Royal Resort 5 * (Hurghada) provided its guests with an extensive tourist infrastructure, which consists of the following objects:

  • restaurants are responsible for providing good and tasty food;
  • drinks and snacks of tourists are supplied by a network of bars in the hotel;
  • for maximum comfort of guests in the buildings there is a spacious elevator;
  • In addition to the safe in the room, you can also deposit your valuables at the reception;
  • a spacious bright room combines a meeting room and a banquet hall;
  • in the business center you can rent an office, use office equipment, as well as get a number of such common services as copying, scanning, laminating documents and sending them by fax;
  • laundry is responsible for the cleanliness and tidiness of your belongings;
  • You can buy something to remember about the rest in the gift shop;
  • if you want to explore the surroundings and local attractions on your own, you can rent a car;
  • 50 meters from the hotel there is a sandy beach with free sunbeds and umbrellas;
  • a tour desk will direct you to the main tourist destinations and organize study tours.




Leisure activities

Sooner or later, every tourist gets tired of lying on the beach or in a sun lounger by the pool. Then it is time for entertainment and sports. In this regard, the AMC Royal Resort 5 * hotel (Egypt, Hurghada) can offer its guests the following features:

  • a huge outdoor pool with clean fresh water, in which there is a special children's compartment of shallow depth;
  • you can relieve stress in the sauna;
  • thanks to a modern spa, you can feel completely transformed and updated;
  • professionals in their field will conduct for you a course of wellness, modeling or relaxing massage;
  • on the beach you can go water skiing, a motorbike, canoeing, as well as learn all the intricacies of surfing;
  • for those who have always dreamed of exploring the depths of the sea, there is a diving center;
  • in the fitness center, together with professional instructors, you can develop a training program to maintain your figure in shape;
  • You can pass the time for such popular games as table tennis, billiards, futsal, beach volleyball, darts.




Loyalty program

Oriental hospitality is one of the main reasons why many tourists choose Egypt. AMC Royal Resort 5 *, in accordance with its loyalty program, prepared its guests the following pleasant surprises:

  • Newlyweds receive a small holiday cake as a gift from the hotel. To do this, when purchasing a tour, indicate the purpose of the visit: "Honeymoon", as well as present a marriage certificate to the reception staff.
  • Birthday at this hotel is an occasion to get a surprise in the form of a birthday cake. When booking, as well as during registration, do not forget to provide this information.
  • Guests celebrating their wedding anniversary at the hotel will also not be left without a festive cake. A prerequisite is the presence of a marriage certificate (or a copy thereof), as well as the corresponding note in the voucher of the travel agency through which you bought the ticket.
  • Regular guests of the hotel during check-in receive a large fruit basket. If the hotel is not full, such tourists are also entitled to upgrade their rooms, as well as the opportunity to use the late check out service.

The hotel will not disregard any guest who arrived at the hotel. Mineral water will be provided in your room, and while waiting for registration you can have a cup of tea or coffee.

Positive guest reviews
