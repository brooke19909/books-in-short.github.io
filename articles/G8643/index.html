The land of fantastic legends

It will be especially interesting to spend the holidays here for children. They will certainly be interested in the history of this lake, because it is full of mysteries and secrets.

Today, scientists offer two versions of the origin of this fantastic reservoir. The first claims that the volcano was the cause of the formation of the pit, which raged in this territory for many centuries. The second hypothesis says that many years ago an asteroid fell on this place. He formed a cavity, which was subsequently filled with water. The Finns called this pond "rabbit lake", which sounded in their language as "yanisyarvi". The hotel will become a real territory for adventure.





The extraterrestrial origin of the pool can be confirmed by the story of a St. Petersburg student who witnessed the fall of another meteorite into the water. This happened in 1908. Instructions for hunters

Of course, you can catch prey in any part of the reservoir. But if you want a specific type of fish, you will have to try and remember a few rules. For example, whitefish does not like great depths and swims along the coast, where the mark is no more than 5 meters.

Perch, pike and zander are not tied to a specific territory, so you can catch them everywhere. But the latter species is very rare, and if it appears in a bucket, then consider that you were lucky.

Rest on Lake Yanisyarvi is impossible to imagine without fishing. Most often people come here for trophies such as trout and salmon. The first species does not like noise, so it is better to ambush it in quiet company. The second lives on a sandy and stone bottom. In order to get such a boot, you should choose the right place for the boat.
