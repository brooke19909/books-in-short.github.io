Hotel Resta Reef Resort 4 * (Egypt / Marsa Alam): description, photos and reviews

The beach hotel Resta Reef Resort 4 * is a small, cozy, upscale resort complex designed for diving, snorkeling and just relaxing on the sea. The Arabian style of the building blends perfectly with modern amenities and modern design. The hotel specializes in family vacations and also invites young couples for a romantic pastime.





The hotel belongs to the famous Italian line of Besta Hotels and Resorts. Europeans and Americans love her, especially for a calm and serene pastime and low cost of living. Tourists value the hotel highly enough, as evidenced in their feedback about this place.

Marsa Alam region - why is it interesting?

The coastline of this new Egyptian resort stretches for seventy kilometers. And mainly four- and five-star hotels are being built along the sea. One of them is Resta Reef Resort 4 * (Egypt, Marsa Alam). From all corners of the earth, those who cannot live without diving and snorkeling are trying to come here. The clear turquoise water of the sea, the richest coral reefs are the main wealth of this resort. Its only drawback, from the point of view of some tourists, is too strong isolation. There is virtually no infrastructure, clubs, cafes and parties. Therefore, hotels are trying to make up for this shortcoming on their own territory. But excellent service, comfortable accommodation, deserted beaches and beautiful fish - this is such a bonus of Marsa Alam, which, perhaps, no other Egyptian resort can boast of. However, prices bite here, and the region is designed primarily for wealthy tourists. Therefore, according to reviews, Resta Reef Resort is good because its prices are acceptable for middle-income people.

Sea, beach, pools

The hotel has its own sandy beach. Nearby is a coral reef. There is a pier from which the sea abysses open. The water is very clean and clear. This attracts tourists to the Resta Reef Resort 4 * hotel. Reviews in 2015 assure that the corals are alive, and during snorkeling you can observe such rare fish as angels, butterflies. Near the open sea is a lagoon, where moray eels, rays, octopuses live. The beach has a diving center and sports facilities. But if you just swim with a mask, then the sensations, according to tourists, are simply indescribable. The fish themselves surround you and swim very close. In the open sea, the depth is very large, water is visible for tens of meters. If the weather is nice, then you can swim near the pier, and when big waves - then in the lagoon. In strong winds, a ladder on the pier is hoisted and a black flag hangs. The lagoon is up to two meters deep, the water temperature there is always above twenty-four degrees Celsius. However, the sea can be entered only in "coral slippers." There are two pools on the territory of the Resta Reef Resort Marsa Alam 4 * hotel. Both of them are in the open air, and one of them is heated.

Prices and Shopping

Of course, those who come to the Resta Reef Resort Marsa Alam 4 * hotel are eager to bring something with them as a souvenir. So, experienced tourists do not advise buying souvenirs at the hotel itself, but recommend hiring a taxi for twenty dollars and going to Port Ghalib. Here you can buy masks, and flippers, and coral slippers, and various Egyptian little things five times cheaper than in shops at the hotel. In addition, here you can buy everything that is usually brought from Hurghada - towels, shells, books, spices, jackets and t-shirts made of cotton, magnets, Bedouin scarves, galabeys and so on. Eastern sweets are also good in Port Galib, which you need to buy not in the bazaar or in the supermarket, but in special pastry shops in order to avoid fakes. A trip to Wadi Hammamat will cost about thirty dollars. ATV excursion in the desert - about sixty.
