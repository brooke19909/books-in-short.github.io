Slavic patterns and their history

Since ancient times, the art of the Slavs was distinguished by its mystery and faith in magical powers. All this is clearly expressed by the Slavic patterns that were used for various purposes and according to them, separate schemes and compositions were created. It was believed that each pattern has a special and individual magical power that can protect, strengthen, or even make richer anyone who wears a robe with it.

Mysterious Slavic patterns and their meanings

As everyone knows, art and religion are opposite poles, which, however, are not able to exist without each other. It is funny that the religion of our ancestors had very interesting origins: they include Slavic patterns and other symbolism, which later became churchly. Even ancient Russian temples and divine fortresses were completely decorated with special ornaments. The most interesting thing was the production of shirts and sundresses, the decoration of which served as Slavic patterns. Schemes were selected directly for the person who later wore the thing. As a rule, such ornaments were created with the aim of making the character of their owner stronger and more resilient. For example, if a boy was born weak, then he needed a pattern that gives strength, and if the girl was untidy, then, accordingly, the pattern should give her purity and accuracy. ...
