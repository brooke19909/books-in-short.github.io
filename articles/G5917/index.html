Turkey. Aspendos Hotel - Excellent Service at the Best Price

The Aspendos Hotel (Turkey) is 100 km from Antalya Airport , and 22 km from the city of Alanya. It is located directly on the coast, in the village of Incekum. 150 kilometers from the complex is the seaside village of Avsallar. Turkey, Aspendos Hotel. general description



The complex consists of two three- and four-story buildings and accepts tourists from March to November. During the overhaul, all furniture and appliances were replaced. The hotel buildings are built and decorated in the Art Nouveau style, the area is surrounded by greenery. The length of its own beach is 200 m. Here, guests have access to showers, sun loungers, umbrellas, mattresses, changing cabins. Beach towels are not provided.

Turkey, Aspendos Hotel. Accommodation

Guests are offered accommodation in 194 modern comfortable rooms:

  • 144 standard areas from 18 to 24 square meters. m each. All have one room, a bathroom.
  • 50 family rooms, each of which has an area of ‚Äã‚Äã30-32 square meters. m. Such rooms have excellent views from the window and balcony.


All standard rooms have a double bed or two standard beds (optional), an extra cot is available on request. Rooms are cleaned and towels are changed daily, and bed linen is changed 4 times a week. The rooms have satellite TV, telephone, minibar (not filled), air conditioning, hairdryer, shower.

Turkey, Aspendos Hotel. Nutrition

The hotel has a main restaurant serving international cuisine on an all-inclusive basis. The closed section can accommodate 300 people at a time, the open - 200. The hotel has four bars: by the pool; snack bar; Lobby Bar disco bar. Afternoon snacks are available at the snack bar or at the poolside bar. After 01:30 drinks are served for a fee.

Turkey, Aspendos Hotel. Services and entertainment



For an absolutely comfortable stay, the complex has an outdoor pool and a water slide. All guests can use the sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas by the pool absolutely free. The doctor is available only two hours a day, parking is available around the clock, it is possible to use the services of a photo studio and laundry, and rent a car. Children can have a good time in the children's pool, on the playground or in the games room. Adults are available aerobics classes, ping pong, sauna. The hotel offers a tennis court, a beach volleyball court, hammam, therapeutic massage and water sports. Paid services include: Internet cafe, hairdresser, sauna, safe, laundry, rental of tennis balls and rackets, water sports on the beach, tennis court lighting.

Aspendos Hotel (Turkey): reviews

Reviews about this complex are extremely positive. Many tourists note that even though the hotel has three stars, it is much better than some Turkish four-star hotels. From here, guests often travel to Alanya. Have a pleasant and memorable vacation!
