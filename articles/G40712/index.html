Insurance in Turkey: cost, selection tips and reviews

Over the past few years, travel to Turkey has become popular among Russians. As practice shows, during rest, unpleasant situations associated with poor health can occur, and also a tourist can get a banal injury. In this situation, the tourist must take advantage of pre-arranged insurance. Next, we consider the main features of the policy and the nuances that should be taken into account.

Do I need to buy insurance?

Many tourists who plan their trip to Turkey are worried about the need to buy insurance. Practice shows that novice travelers prefer to abandon this opportunity, and experienced tourists make it mandatory. What is the reason for this? As they themselves note in their reviews, primarily with the fact that insurance provides a whole range of opportunities and additional guarantees of assistance in the event of an insured event.

Persons who plan to stay within the country for more than a year need to draw up this document without fail - this requirement has been established since 2012.

What is insurance for?

The main importance of health insurance in Turkey is that the availability of this document for citizens and persons staying in the country for a long period allows you to eliminate possible unrest in the state healthcare system. Also, this document provides free access to medical services of various fields, the provision of which may be required by guests of the country during their entire stay on its territory. Other owls, Russians who have taken care of arranging insurance in Turkey in advance, can afford to contact the country's medical institution at any time in order to undergo an examination and get the necessary help. Medical services are provided to such citizens absolutely free of charge, since the cost of the policy fully covers them.

For whom insurance is required

Who needs insurance in Turkey? Among those persons, except citizens of the country, are those who plan to stay in the state for 12 months or more, as well as migrants who have arrived in the country for the purpose of employment. Persons who are on a business trip or with the goal of developing their own business also require an insurance policy.

Special attention in the country is drawn to those who came to marry a Turkish citizen, as well as those who applied for a residence permit in the country - insurance is also compulsory for them, allowing you to use the full range of medical services.

What does

It should be noted that the effect of insurance in Turkey extends to a rather wide range of cases, but by no means to all situations requiring medical intervention.

The insurance issued by tourists before traveling to Turkey is able to cover all types of services that are provided in state hospitals in cities of the country. So, tourists can even treat her for the treatment of diseases of a chronic nature. Moreover, all medicines prescribed by a doctor are included in its price. However, all of the listed rules apply only to those tourists who draw up state insurance. In the event that the traveler purchases a policy in a private company, as Russians often do before leaving for the country, you should first consult with an agent regarding the conditions of the document. As practice shows, such documents do not cover the costs associated with the treatment of chronic, sexually transmitted diseases, as well as injuries that were received as a result of participation in public events. So, if the disease belongs to these categories, then for its treatment, most likely, you will have to pay on your own.

About package insurance

Considering the topic of insurance in Turkey, you need to pay attention to their separate group - package offers. These offers are usually included in the price of the tour and are offered to travelers under the guise of its obligatory element.

Reviews of this type of insurance say that they are far from the best option, as they provide for the coverage of a minimum number of insurance cases. So, in the number of insurance events provided for them, injuries sustained during outdoor activities are never included. In other words, if a tourist unsuccessfully slides down the hill at the water park and gets injured as a result, payment for his treatment will not be covered by insurance - the traveler will have to pay from personal funds.

Packaged insurance medical services, according to tourists, are of poor quality. In the comments left by tourists, it is often said that the provision of necessary assistance to its owners is rather slow, and for the implementation of any actions by doctors, confirmation from the insurance company is required, which usually comes after a long time.

Additional options

Insurance of the most reliable companies includes not only coverage of cases related to health problems, but also some other options that you should pay attention to. These may include cases of loss of luggage, as well as travel restrictions. In addition, when studying the conditions of the insurance policy, you should definitely clarify whether it covers the costs associated with accidents that occurred during active sports - in decent companies this item is automatically included in the policy.

Cost

The price of the insurance policy may vary depending on various factors. So, among them, we must first highlight the breadth of the list of insurance claims covered by it, as well as the validity of the policy. Moreover, the gender and age of the tourist affect the price of insurance in Turkey.

As practice shows, the average cost of a policy for young men is about 7000, and for women - 6200 rubles. If we consider travelers of a more mature age, then the cost of their health insurance is about 15,000 rubles for men and the same for women.

Insurance Nuances

It should be noted that there are certain nuances that must be taken into account during the acquisition of the policy and its use. So, experienced tourists do not recommend concluding an agreement with an insurance company if the total amount of coverage for it is less than $ 50,000. Practice shows that most prestigious companies do not enter into transactions with a smaller amount.

In the event that a blood alcohol is detected in a tourist upon the occurrence of an insured event, there is no provision for repaying expenses at the expense of the policy - these are the rules.

What insurance in Turkey to choose? Experienced tourists do not recommend using those offers, the terms of which provide for reimbursement of expenses after the traveler returns to Russia - only scammers work this way.

After that, the company should send a response indicating the hospital to which you should go. Next, the tourist should go to the specified clinic for treatment. Payment for services provided by doctors should not be charged. In the event of any disputes, the tourist needs to contact the staff of the UK to clarify issues of interest.
