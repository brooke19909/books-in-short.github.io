Standard

For an on-air digital receiver to be used to watch twenty free television channels in Russia, you need to make sure that it is designed to receive a signal in dvb t2 format. If the instructions for the device do not have this abbreviation, then it may be unsuitable for watching TV. Also, it will not be superfluous to pay attention to the existence of a warranty period for the product you like.

It should be taken into account the available opportunity to return the goods you do not like in a timely manner. Currently, it is equal to two weeks from the date of purchase. Therefore, it is best to check the purchased digital airtime receiver as quickly as possible, and if it does not meet your requirements, take it back to the store.
