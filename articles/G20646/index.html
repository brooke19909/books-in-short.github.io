Location

Atrium Shopping Center, whose address is Zemlyanoy Val, 33, was built in the very center of the Russian capital. It is located directly on the Garden Ring. If you decide to visit the Atrium shopping center, how to get to it? It will not be difficult.





Two minutes walk from the Atrium shopping center are two metro stations - Chkalovskaya and Kurskaya. There is a pedestrian overpass here. It combines the complex with the nearby Kursk Station. There is a parking near the Atrium shopping center. This is an underground structure designed to accommodate seven hundred cars.

Zemlyanoy Val, 33 - Atrium Shopping Center invites movie lovers to visit. The cinema where films are shown is equipped with nine halls. Two of them are VIP. The same number of rooms has equipment that allows you to watch movies in 3D.
