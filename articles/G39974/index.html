Hotel Amara Club Marine Nature 5 * (Turkey, Kemer): reviews

Among the many resorts in Turkey, Beldibi, a small village located just west of Antalya, enjoys incredible popularity among travelers. It represents a narrow strip of fashionable hotels and a wide range of various entertainments. Here, absolutely everyone will find something to their liking. The mild climate, clear waters of the Mediterranean and minimal rainfall allow tourists to come here all year round.

The hotel complex Amara Club Marine Nature (Turkey), which rightfully has five stars, offers its guests comfortable living conditions and first-class service. Every minute spent within the walls of this institution will give you unforgettable emotions and a good mood.

Kemer is 15 km away. The journey to the airport will take about an hour. A short transfer has a positive effect on the mood of new arrivals.
