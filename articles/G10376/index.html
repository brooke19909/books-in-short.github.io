Hotel Club Hotel Beldiana 4 *: tourist reviews

There are many attractive holiday towns in Turkey. And one of the most popular among Russian tourists, of course, is Beldibi. In this village, our compatriots are waiting for an endless blue sky, picturesque mountains, sea and sun. Rest here will certainly be comfortable and memorable. Moreover, there are many comfortable hotels in Beldibi. The main thing is to approach the choice of a hotel more responsibly. In this article we will deal with what are available about the Club Hotel Beldiana 4 * complex of tourist reviews.

Location Features

This hotel is located in Beldibi right on the beach, on the first beach line. This is the majority of tourists, of course, attributed to its undoubted advantages. The nearest city, Kemer, is 13 km from this complex.





Russian tourists arrive in Turkey, of course, most often by plane. The airport closest to Beldibi is approximately 42 km away. So the tourists who rented rooms in this complex will have to get to the hotel by tour operator bus, taxi or a rented car for quite some time. Of course, according to many vacationers, this is not too convenient. However, buses Russian tour operators working with this hotel, their customers, judging by the reviews, still usually provide quite comfortable. From the center of Beldibi itself, the hotel is literally 10 minutes. walk.

General description and reviews of the territory

The Hotel Club Hotel Beldiana 4 * accepts guests , reviews of which are available, both good and not very, in several multi-storey buildings built in the Turkish style. The dark pink facades of these buildings are simply perfectly combined with the light paving tiles of the courtyard of the same shade and the blue smooth surface of the large pool.

The territory of the hotel is large and at the same time, judging by the reviews, well-maintained and landscaped . In the evening, the courtyard of the complex is illuminated. In this regard, tourists have no special complaints about the hotel. The very place in which the hotel is located is considered by most vacationers to be very picturesque. The windows of the hotel can offer truly magnificent views of the sea or the mountains covered with forests.





Shops and umbrellas are on site, and there is a bar. Hotel staff usually monitor the lawns carefully. But sometimes, as some former hotel guests say, lawns do not water. And this, of course, negatively affects the appearance of the courtyard of the complex.

Number of rooms

In total, the Club Hotel Beldiana 4 * hotel , reviews in 2017 about which there are more or less good, offers its guests 128 comfortable rooms. If you wish, tourists who choose Beldibi for their holidays can rent rooms of class park , family room or standard here. Most of the apartments in the hotel are double. In each room for the convenience of guests are provided:

  • safe;
  • TV.

Also in the hotel rooms there are balconies on which guests can sit, for example, in the evening, admiring the surroundings, or simply dry beach items. Of course, the rooms of this hotel are equipped with air conditioning. Therefore, residents can spend time in them comfortably.

As for the furniture, it is installed quite old here. However, beds, wardrobes and bedside tables look quite decent. There are no scratches and scuffs on them. The maids at this hotel change bed linen, unfortunately, not too willingly. But even on very dirty sheets the residents of the complex still do not have to sleep. If desired, parents can order a cot in the room.
