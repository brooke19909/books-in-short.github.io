Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 * (Budva, Montenegro): description, service, reviews

Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 * is a luxury hotel, which provides everything for a comfortable stay in Montenegro and for tourists to be remembered exclusively from a positive side. Villa "Monte Royal" has a good location in terms of distance to the beach and the main attractions of Budva. In order for you to get acquainted with other advantages and possible disadvantages of the hotel, we have prepared this article.





Hotel location and surroundings

The villa is located on the tranquil landscaped street Nikole Tesle, in the heart of Budva - in the Old Town. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are important city attractions that keep centuries of history. These are the preserved remains of the fortress walls, as well as temples and other buildings frozen in time. In addition, surrounded by Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 * there are restaurants, cafes, shops, a post office, a bank, mobile communication offices, a bus station, a gas station, a city market, a night club and a shopping and business center.

The distance to the popular Slavic beach is approximately 400 m, and the modern city center is only 10 minutes walk. A bus stop is 50 meters away, Tivat Airport is 27 km and Podgorica is 65 km away.

Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 *: description

The hotel was built in 2008. It is a three-story villa with a modern exterior and a cozy interior. The windows offer a charming view of the surroundings. The interior of the hotel is calm, it is spacious in common areas and rooms, modern high-quality furniture is installed, made mainly of wenge wood. The hotel is rightly considered to be luxury, as it will satisfy the wishes of even the most demanding customer. Ideal for a relaxing holiday, for families with children, groups of friends and couples in love.





Villa Room Categories

Prices for holidays in Montenegro, namely in the Monte Royal Hotel, depend on the selected room category. The villa has ten rooms, which are located on the second and third floors:

  • Two rooms category DBL Room. The area is 28 m¬≤. It is divided into a bedroom and a bathroom and is designed for two guests.
  • Four rooms of STUDIO 03 category. The area is from 30 to 36 m¬≤, on which there is a bedroom, a kitchen and a bathroom. Holds up to three people.
  • Two rooms of category STUDIO 02. Has the same characteristics as the previous version, only provided for two guests.
  • Two rooms of category Apartment 04. The area is 55 m¬≤. The apartments are functionally divided into a bedroom, a bathroom and a living room. Accommodation for two guests is provided, if necessary, you can organize 2 more beds.

The rooms have a comfortable layout and a single original style. Almost all of them have a private balcony with views of the city and mountains. Kitchens are equipped with the most necessary items: utensils, an electric kettle, a refrigerator, a sink and an electric stove. In the living area there is a sofa and a TV for a comfortable stay, and in the bedroom there is a comfortable bed, bedside tables and a wardrobe. In addition, each room is equipped with:

  • a safe;
  • air conditioning;
  • personal hygiene kits;
  • towels, bathrobes, slippers;
  • a hairdryer.

The rooms are equipped with cable TV with Russian-language channels, as well as high-speed Internet - both are available for a fee. The rooms are decorated in a classic style, the interior is calm and functional - what you need for a comfortable and relaxing stay. The furniture is modern, made of high quality solid wood.





Infrastructure and services at the Monte Royale hotel

The infrastructure of Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 * includes:

  • parking;
  • Laundry
  • garage;
  • car rental
  • a restaurant;
  • garden;
  • tour agency.

The range of services, in addition to the ability to connect high-speed Internet and cable TV, includes regular cleaning, a cot is available on request.

Leisure and entertainment at the hotel

Entertainment in Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 * is not enough - excursions and hiking. But such a disadvantage is compensated by a good location, since there are a lot of entertainment venues around the villa. In walking distance (5 minutes) - a sandy beach where you can rent sun umbrellas and sun loungers. This is a city beach that is not recommended by tourists and locals. It is better to go to Mogren, located about 1 km from the hotel. It has a clean beach and clear water, more pleasant for swimming.

It is possible to rent a car for comfortable movement around the city. In order not to waste time searching for the sights of Budva, it is proposed to take an excursion tour and enjoy a trip accompanied by guide stories.





Dining at a four star hotel

Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 * (Budva) offers accommodation without meals or with breakfast - the choice of vacationers. Meals are served in the villa by prior arrangement with staff. About 100 meters away is the LM restaurant, which is open from 7am to 10pm. Service is carried out according to the "menu-order" type.

Near the villa there is a large supermarket and small grocery stores, as well as a market where you can buy fresh products and prepare meals in your room on your own. Those who do not want to mess around in the kitchen, besides LM, are invited to visit other cafes, which are quite a lot in the vicinity of the hotel.





Cost of living, service and maintenance

Prices for holidays in Montenegro at the Monte Royal Hotel peak in July and August. At this time, the cost of living is from 80 to 140 euros per day, depending on the type of room. A little cheaper in September - approximately 64-118 euros. The cheapest months are from October to May. At this time, the price drops to 52-95 euros per day.

In addition, a mandatory tourist tax of 1 euro is paid for adults and 0.5 for adolescents 12-18 years old. Young children are not charged.

When booking a studio or apartment, a 40 percent prepayment is required. In the future, this amount does not change even with increasing prices for accommodation. When canceling a reservation, money will not be refunded.

All children are welcome. The hotel does not allow pets. It is also important to know that, if necessary, you can order a transfer - the owner of the villa will meet you at the appointed time.





Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 *: reviews

Hotel rating by reviews - solid four. According to most vacationers, the Monte Royal Villa is one of the best options for a tourist trip in Budva. The location of the hotel plays an important role in this issue, because within walking distance from it there is everything that a tourist needs: cafes and restaurants, shops and a market, beaches and even a bakery.

The four-star Monte Royale itself is a pleasant place to relax. The rooms are modern and comfortable to accommodate several people. The apartments have a lot of space to be able to cook, watch TV, sleep and just relax with your favorite book.

Food is the main minus that occurs in reviews. Almost all tourists write that breakfast was constantly cold, and even after requests to serve at least hot coffee, nothing changed.

The service in Villa Monte Royale (Cat. LUX) 4 * deserves special attention. The hosts are hospitable and helpful, always ready to help, if necessary, they will be happy to meet you at the airport, show the town and tell you everything you need to know about Budva. They speak excellent Russian, so you will not have to experience the difficulties of the language barrier. In general, Monte Royal is a good hotel with a high level of service and comfortable living conditions.
