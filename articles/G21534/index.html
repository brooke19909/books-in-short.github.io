Hotel Ibis Samui Bophut 3 * (Thailand, Bophut, Samui): traveler reviews

Bophut Beach (one of the spellings: ‚ÄúBo Phut‚Äù) is located in the north of Koh Samui between the famous Bang Rak and Maenam beaches. Translated from Thai "bo phut" (bo phut) means: "cadaveric lily." This is the name of one of the largest flowers in the world - rafflesia. This huge, pungent smelling flower once grew in these places, but with the advent of tourism the island disappeared.

The beach, named after this strange plant, combines the activity of the partying Chaweng and Lamai and the tranquility of the quiet Bang Rak and Chongmon. Here you can relax as lovers of romantic solitude in the shade of palm trees, so lovers of bright and noisy leisure time in luxury and club restaurants. Both are abundantly represented in this part of the island. To accommodate tourists on the beach, there are many hotels of various levels: from inexpensive guesthouses for backpackers to luxury five-star hotels that can satisfy the most demanding tourists. One of the most popular options is the Ibis Samui Bophut 3 * hotel.

Getting to know the resort

When choosing a hotel here, you should remember that Bophut Beach is part of the administrative district of the same name, including a number of other beaches. Often in booking sites you can find confusion in their description.

Bophut beach itself has a length of about 3 km, the closest major supermarkets are Macro, BigC and TescoLotus. Whichever of the hotels here was chosen, as the reviews assure - Ibis Samui Bophut 3 * or any other, in each of them the guests will find the opportunity to eat and relax.

There are few entertainments nearby (i.e., within walking distance), but those who wish can easily get to Chaweng by tuk-tuk or moped. The resort is recommended mainly for lovers of a relaxing holiday. More active will have to make forays to Chaweng and other ‚Äúparty‚Äù places on the island.

What is the hotel Ibis Samui Bophut 3 * (Bophut)?

Location

Ibis Samui Bophut Hotel 3 * is located, according to reviews, in a great place where vacationers can swim, sunbathe, engage in water sports and enjoy delicious dishes. The institution is 6 km from Samui Airport, Chaweng Beach is 7 km from the hotel, and the coast of Bophut Beach is 100 meters from it. At a distance of 500 m from the hotel is the city center, the pier is a 5-minute walk.

Towels are changed regularly; cleaning is done every day. There is a small balcony with a table and an armchair.

According to reviews, the walls in the rooms are quite thin, you can often hear everything that happens with the neighbors. The rooms, according to residents, are equipped with everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay.

Vacationers are advised when choosing a room to prefer rooms overlooking the pool and the sea. It should be borne in mind that the cost of such rooms is more expensive.
