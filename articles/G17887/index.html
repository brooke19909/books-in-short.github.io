Silver Boulevard - one of the streets named after Russian aviation

In 1973, a new residential neighborhood with typical apartment buildings was built on the site of the former Commandant airfield . Silver Boulevard, like many other local highways, in their names preserved the memory of the previous object, which had a glorious history.

Territory History

The Black River, originating in a swamp adjacent to Lake Dolgoy and flowing into the Bolshaya Nevka, has a rather unfortunate reputation in connection with the duel of A.S. Pushkin, which took place here in 1837. But due to the picturesque nature of the river, its surroundings were built up by government summer residences from the 17th century.

Tribute to the memory

Silver Boulevard was originally conceived as a tribute to the former airfield. It had to be planted with silver willows, so that everything here was associated and resembled the wings of airplanes.

Great unrealized idea. Planned as a boulevard, it essentially remained a rather narrow street. But the name remains, and it is closely connected with the history of the city.
