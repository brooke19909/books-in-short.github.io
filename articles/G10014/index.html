Real estate in Montenegro: pros and cons, selection tips, reviews

For most Russians, it is no secret that Montenegro is one of the most comfortable places in Europe, where there is wonderful ecology, excellent climatic conditions and picturesque nature. Who dreams of buying a house or apartment on the beach in this country? Very, very many. The route Russia - Montenegro is quite popular with our tourists. It would seem that if you buy real estate, it is better in Bulgaria. But it turns out that from the point of view of investment attractiveness Montenegro is no worse.





Why exactly this country?

It has everything for a comfortable stay: the gentle sea, warm climate, clean air. In addition, Russia, Montenegro are countries with a common culture, and a lot of them have in common. Over the years, our compatriots have come here to rest.

This means that potential business investors need to pay closer attention to the real estate here. First of all, objects located in the Bay of Kotor, the Budva Riviera and in the south of the country are considered to be a profitable acquisition. Those entrepreneurs who bought apartments or a house on the Lustica peninsula, where a powerful construction boom has unfolded, will also benefit.

Housing costs are relatively small

Another significant advantage of making a transaction to buy a house or apartment in the above country is that the costs of selling, acquiring and maintaining housing here are considered to be one of acceptable in Europe. Potential owners of real estate in Montenegro can pre-calculate the degree of profitability of the transaction. For a used apartment, you need to pay 3% of its value.

Every year you need to pay real estate tax (from 0.1 to 1% of the market price for 1 m 2 ). The amount of utility bills, when it comes to an apartment of 50 m 2 , averages 50 euros per month in summer and 100 euros in winter (water - 1.5 euros per m 3 , electricity - 10 euros per 1 kW, household waste - 10 euros )

And of course, Montenegro is one of the ecologically clean countries of the planet, which is also a significant advantage for Russian businessmen. This year, the country becomes a member of the European Union, and this fact will strengthen its investment attractiveness.





disadvantages
