Hotel Santana Beach Resort 3 * (India / Goa): photos and reviews

Holidays in the resort state of Goa (India) became popular with the light hands of European hippies who had chosen this region in the 70s. They were attracted not only to the local magnificent beaches with white sand and the sea of ‚Äã‚Äãwine; not only an amazing culture and a special, deep and friendly perception of life by Indians. Times are changing, since then significant international investments have been made in Goa, and developed resort infrastructure has grown. Our story is about one small fragment of it: the three-star hotel Santana Beach Resort 3 *. It is for tourists a wonderful comfortable portal to India, the starting point for exploring it.





In Goa, the taste of real, false freedom is felt in everything; musicians from the legendary Liverpool Four loved to relax here. The northern part of the state is a pronounced youth, party resort. Goa regulars believe that nowhere else in the world do holiday-goers feel such emancipation with such force. This special feeling is inherent in the resort town of Candolim.

Goan Beach Candolim

In the vicinity of the coastal village of Candolim, you will not find either garbage or slums, so familiar to other parts of North Goa. Here, on the neat streets, lovely houses are built, the courtyards are well-groomed and cows almost do not roam the village. True, there are ubiquitous dogs. But here they are some kind of non-aggressive, peaceful, and tourists willingly feed them.





Candolim is also called a cozy suburban beach, a segment of a huge ribbon of coastal beaches of Goa, stretching along the coast of the Arabian Sea from the beginning of Bagi Beach to the end of Sinquerima Beach. (In fact, there are no strict borders between the beaches on this coast of the Arabian Sea, but each of them has features in its infrastructure.)

In particular, both the village and the Candolim beach are considered the most respectable and clean in North Goa. Just in this well-kept mini-resort there is also a small cozy hotel Santana Beach Resort 3 * considered by us.

Climate and spa vacation

The rest of the hotel guests is determined by the seasonality of the weather in Goa. The dry season, lasting from October to May, is most attractive to tourists from Western Europe and America. It coincides with the local resort high season.
The period from June to September is characterized by humidity close to 100% and prolonged heavy rains. This is a low season at the resort. Tourists leave, and resort infrastructure collapses until the next high season.



Also an important factor determining the specifics of a beach holiday is the height of the waves. If the sea surface allows even children to swim, then high waves can drag an inexperienced swimmer into the sea. Therefore, the rather hectic Candolima beach, according to its characteristics, serves for swimming adults and well-kept people.

Excursions

Guests of Santana Beach Resort 3 * (India, North Goa) immediately upon arrival offer a whole list of standard excursions. Among them - a visit to the six-meter Dulsagar waterfall, trips to the spice plantation, boat trips. Excursions in old Goa (price $ 100 per person) hotel guests give a low rating. After all, an experienced taxi driver for only $ 40 will be able to show the sights to four tourists at once.

The coolest excursion here is considered to be a trip to the village of Hampi in the neighboring state of Karnataka, located 260 km from Goa. There are well-preserved ruins of the capital of the ancient Vijayanagar Empire, belonging to the UNESCO World Heritage.

The empire that controls all of South India lasted from the 13th to the 15th centuries AD and was destroyed by the Arabs. The majestic remains of the buildings of her capital are located in a mountainous area on an area of ‚Äã‚Äã26 km 2 : Rama Temple, Virupaksha Temple, sacred wells, Krishna Temple, and the palace complex.
