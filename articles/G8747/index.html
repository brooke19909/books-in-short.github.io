Slovenian seaside resorts: list, attractions and photos

Where to spend a vacation? The choice of option depends on financial capabilities. To have a good and inexpensive vacation, you can visit Slovenia. The country is famous for its sights and natural beauty. Slovenian seaside resorts are perfect for those who decide to spend a vacation with their families.

Country basics

The state has been independent since 1991. It is located on the border of central and southern Europe. The modern capital Ljubljana is an interesting and vibrant city with its own characteristics and traditions. There are still the best resorts of Slovenia on the sea. The country is washed in the southwestern part of the Adriatic Sea. The promenade attracts tourists with a huge number of attractions and recreation facilities.



In addition to the capital, the largest cities of a small state are Kranj, Bovec, Maribor, Tserkno. Here, domestic tourists can find a lot of interesting things for themselves. Modern culture borders on ancient buildings, which cannot but bewitch.

Slovenian resorts on the sea are very popular among Russian tourists. The trip from Moscow by plane takes only 3 hours. In addition, hotel reviews can only be heard positive. Below will be described the most popular resorts of Slovenia on the sea.

Isola

A small old town with developed infrastructure attracts European tourists. Reviews show that families with small children, as well as older people, can perfectly relax here. Narrow streets, local market, original buildings - all this is the face of Slovenia resort. Isola is the heart of yachting and windsurfing. It is no coincidence that the resort town is chosen by lovers of extreme relaxation.



Isola has its own passenger sea port. Thanks to this, tourists can easily get to Venice. And this is an additional plus of the resort. In addition, by purchasing a ferry ticket, you can reach the resort towns of nearby countries. Thus, a vacation in Slovenia can be truly diverse.

Slovenian seaside resorts are characterized by a mild climate, which is perfect for young children. Young parents can safely plan a vacation here, after choosing one of the popular hotels.

sights

The historical center of the resort town informs tourists that in the past it was part of the Republic of Venice. The 18th-century Besengi-Delhi-Ugi Palace is one of the oldest buildings, which is an example of the Baroque style. An interesting building for tourists in the style of the Venetian Gothic - the palace of Manzoli of the 15th century. The real architectural symbol of Izola can be considered the church of St. Moor. Its bell tower rises above the roofs of modern buildings and is visible from almost anywhere in the town.



What else attract Slovenian resorts to the sea? Of course, with its embankments! The coast in Izola is a long parking of private yachts. A boat trip on a summer evening will bring great pleasure to tourists. In addition, the Izoly embankment is full of small cafes, restaurants and bars. Everyone chooses for himself - to spend the evening culturally or have fun in a noisy company of friends in one of the karaoke clubs.

Portoroz

Great for youth holidays Slovenia. Holidays at sea, resorts, interesting sights - all this cannot but attract modern students. Low prices in local hotels is one of the advantages. Reviews show that the young generation chooses the town of Portoroz. Nightlife here is in full swing. Guests of the city can stay in one of the comfortable hotels located in close proximity to the sandy beach.



What else can Slovenian seaside resorts boast? Portoroz, for example, attracts with its thermal pools and spa centers. People from all over Europe come here to improve their health. The flow of tourists does not stop even in winter. But in the summer, of course, there are much more vacationers here.

The most attractive promenade in August. The water here warms up to 25 degrees Celsius. An interesting fact is that after seven in the evening in the resort town it is forbidden to swim. All attempts to violate the ban are prevented by the embankment guard. Such measures, according to the local administration, will help save the lives of tourists and locals who are intoxicated.

Piran

If we talk about the resorts of Slovenia on the sea, you can not help but remember about this small town, which in the past was also part of the Venetian Republic. Locals still consider themselves a little Italians today. In particular, it is easier for older people to communicate in Italian than in English. Piran is worth a visit for those who seek not only to spend time on the beach, but also to study the ancient architecture. The city has virtually no modern buildings.



The city starts from the sea and ends on the mountain. The population is quite small. Piran survives only at the expense of tourists. The settlement is distinguished by small cozy gated courtyards. Private areas are lined with ancient statues and twined with flower plantings.

The central square is considered the heart of the city. Here is the Municipal Council, as well as St. Peter's Basilica. Those who are going to Slovenia for shopping should not plan a vacation in Piran. Many tourists call the settlement an open-air museum. Large shopping centers would spoil the original old architecture.

Sights of Piran

Tartini Square is one of the city's most popular attractions. Reviews of tourists about her can be heard only positive. The square is located near the embankment, it is always clean here, you will not be able to meet vagrants. Cafes and restaurants located around contribute to a great stay. You can meet many families with young children. At night, the illumination of buildings is switched on, which adds more mystery to the historical area.



It is no coincidence that many tourists choose Slovenian seaside resorts. Reviews show that here you can not only relax from everyday life on one of the comfortable beaches, but also recharge with positive emotions, just walking along the historical streets. While in Piran, every hour you can enjoy the ringing of the bells of St. George's Cathedral - another attraction of the city. The cathedral is on a hill. Near it opens a gorgeous view of the city and the coast.

Koper

A small island is connected to the continental part of Slovenia by a mound of sand. In the past, the town was also subordinate to the Republic of Venice. Italian color reigns here to this day. Despite the fact that the settlement has a very small stretch of coast (only 30 km), the flow of tourists in the summer does not stop.



Koper is connected with the capital of Slovenia by a modern highway. Therefore, getting to the resort town will be possible without much difficulty. Shuttle buses and taxis run from the airport. Accommodation in Koper will be able to people with different financial capabilities. For those who want to save money, local residents rent rooms in private cottages. It is also possible to rent a room in one of the fashionable hotels on the waterfront.

Sights of Koper

Many monuments and objects of architecture tell us that in the past the seaside resorts of Slovenia belonged to the Venetian Republic. Photos of the city really impress with their beauty. The pride of Koper is the Pretoria Palace, which was built in 1464. The architectural structure is located on the main street of the resort town. The Loggia Palace will also be of interest to tourists. Today it houses an art gallery.

It is no coincidence that many families with small children visit Slovenian resorts on the sea. Description of the zoo in Koper will cause interest among parents. This place is definitely worth a visit on a summer evening. Here you can not only watch numerous animals, but also have fun on one of the attractions.

A huge water amusement park is another attraction of Koper. Attractions are offered not only for adults, but also for children.

Resorts in Slovenia impress with their diversity. Any of the cities described is suitable for an excellent holiday with family or friends.
