"VIP-hotel" (Koblevo, Ukraine): description, services, reviews

Koblevo - one of the most fun, eventful, popular and affordable resorts of the Black Sea coast. The warm sea, the gentle, flat Koblevo beach, fine sand, delicious spicy wines, beautiful nature, well-developed infrastructure, as well as the tempting opportunity to relax on a budget, attract crowds of tourists.

The main entertainments there are various entertainments on the beach, night clubs, markets, restaurants, water parks. Attractions in this region are few. Those who want to fill their vacation in Koblevo with something other than swimming and acquiring a bronze tan can go on an excursion to the Tiligul landscape park, the Koblevo winery or frolic in an extreme rope park. In addition to the colorful, active and rich in entertainment of Odessa, only some 50 km.

Tips for relaxing and choosing a hotel in Koblevo

Tourists often get the impression that every free centimeter of land along the coastline in Koblevo is lined with hotels. Partly the way it is. There are a lot of hotels, recreation centers, hostels and guesthouses in this region. But all of them are not able to cope with the continuous flow of tourists in the summer. Climatic features of the coast limit the possibilities of a beach holiday in the area. The holiday season here lasts from mid-May to mid-September. The rest of the time, Koblevo rests from tourists, and prepares for a new massive summer invasion. Basically, Ukrainians, Russians, Belarusians, travelers from Moldova rest there. According to data for 2015, the total number of tourists who visited Koblevo in three months is 53 thousand people.





So the most sober advice on choosing a hotel in Koblevo is to make this very choice as early as possible and book rooms in advance. And in this case, ‚Äúin advance‚Äù means a month or two before the date of arrival.

Koblevo is a fairly compact resort. In the very center there are many night clubs, shops, cafes, wine shops, bustling youth. Holidays in Koblevo during the summer holidays are the dream of every student. Nightclubs here thunder until dawn, wine flows like water, young people have fun, dance, get to know each other. So for those who wish to have a sweet rest at night, they should look for a hotel 300 m further from the center of entertainment.

Along the entire coastline there are many private apartments, recreation centers and sanatoriums from factories (the remnants of Soviet traditions).

The resort is located near the Tiligul estuary, on the territory of Koblevo there are several therapeutic mud springs. This makes the resort popular not only among tourists, but also among mosquitoes. So the main advice to vacationers in the summer is to generously stock up on repellents.

"VIP-hotel" (Koblevo) is located almost in the center of the resort area. There is a bus stop 30 meters from the hotel. Koblevo Beach is only 25 meters from the hotel. The hotel terrace smoothly passes into it. In the evening and at night, the coastal area is illuminated. The whole territory is protected.

Nightclubs, wine markets, restaurants and shopping centers are just 200 meters from the hotel.

"VIP-hotel", Koblevo. Accommodation prices

By international standards, the price of accommodation in a hotel of this category and with this range of services is more than affordable. But in Ukraine, the cost of living is many times lower than in European resorts. Total prices for accommodation in a VIP-hotel are average, but by European standards they are very low.

The hotel is open all year. The price of accommodation varies significantly, depending on the season. In the off-season, the price per night in a deluxe room is $ 12; for a two-room apartment, $ 19. In the summer months, the price for a suite is $ 30; for an apartment, $ 45.





You can pay by cash or credit card upon arrival. In the summer months, the administration requires an advance payment.

Rooms

The hotel has 27 rooms. Of them:

  • 21 suites. Maximum occupancy is 4 people. In each room: shower + toilet, TV, hairdryer, safe, set of toiletries and mini-cosmetics, refrigerator, air conditioning, kettle, dishes, tea / coffee making facilities.




  • 6 rooms of category apartments. Maximum occupancy is 5 people. Apartment with sea view. Each room of this category has a balcony-terrace with furniture, shower + toilet, safe, air conditioning, refrigerator, hairdryer, set of toiletries and mini-cosmetics, kettle, tea / coffee making facilities, dishes.

Room cleaning and towel changes are available on request. Sheets are changed once a week.

Nutrition

The VIP-Hotel 3 * hotel has developed various food systems. Each guest orders a guesthouse option based on their own preferences. The hotel does not work on the all-inclusive concept. Each tourist can order only breakfast (BB), two meals a day (HB) or full board (FB) both at the stage of booking in the room or directly on the spot.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in buffet style in a restaurant with sea views. Soft drinks, coffee, tea are included in the price.





Also in the complex there is a grocery store and a cafe.

Entertainment and sports

VIP-hotel (Koblevo) fully keeps up with European standards of holiday entertainment and actively organizes sports leisure opportunities for its guests. The hotel has an outdoor fitness club, table tennis, a beach volleyball court, fitness equipment, and children's attractions.





In addition, in Koblevo there are a lot of different opportunities for outdoor activities. Water scooters, slides, windsurfing, paragliding, beach soccer, volleyball, trampolines, parks with obstacle courses. Naturally, sports activities on the beach are paid, but VIP-Hotel 3 * offers discounts for renting jet skis, jet skis and banana boats for guests.

2 km from the hotel is the second largest water park in Ukraine. This is a large complex, a paradise for lovers of water rides. The water park is open only from May to September.

Beach

"VIP-hotel" (Koblevo) - a real coastal "Kurkul". It has a wonderful private beach just 25 meters from the hotel. The beach is very well equipped, has a platform, paths, umbrellas, sun awnings, deck chairs. All of these options are free for hotel guests. The beach itself is clean, smooth and beautiful, the sand is fine and pleasant. The entrance to the sea is gentle, ideal for swimming with children.
