Hotel Palace Kranevo 3 * (Bulgaria, Albena): photos and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria as a venue for summer vacations is chosen by many Russian tourists. The resorts of this country have a developed infrastructure, and relaxing by the sea is usually really convenient here. One of the most popular seaside villages in Bulgaria among domestic holidaymakers is Kranevo. Wide sandy beaches, a warm sea and, of course, the opportunity to rent inexpensive comfortable housing attract tourists here. The area of ​​Kranevo village is small and there are not so many hotels here. One of the most convenient of all available for tourists is Palace Kranevo 3 * .

Location Features

This hotel is located 50 meters from the center of Kranevo and about 200 meters from the coast. The distance to the nearest airport from Hotel Palace Kranevo 3 * is about 45 km. It takes about an hour for tourists arriving by plane to reach the destination. If desired, you can order a transfer directly to the hotel.





The village of Kranevo itself is located on the Black Sea, 4 km from Albena and 12 km from Golden Sands. Reviews about this village from tourists are mostly only good. Kranevo is by far one of the most affordable holiday resort in Bulgaria. This village is also praised by vacationers for the lack of crowds of tourists and well-developed infrastructure. Holidaymakers consider the street appearance not too good as a certain disadvantage of Kranevo. Many tourists even compare this village with the Soviet-era Adler. It is also believed that Kranevo is not particularly suitable for young people. Unfortunately, there are not too many entertainment facilities here. Thus, mostly families with children and elderly people prefer to rent accommodation at the Palace Kranevo hotel .

Palace Kranevo 3 *: general hotel description

This hotel consists of only one building. The architecture of the hotel building, according to many tourists, is quite original and beautiful. The facades of the three-story building of the hotel with the attic are painted in a pleasant pink color for the eyes. Against their background, balconies of an unusual form with white parapets look very interesting.

The area occupied by the hotel in total is 1000 m 2. The hotel courtyard is decorated, like the building itself, is very beautiful. The hotel area is covered with pink (the color of the facades of the building) tiles and well landscaped. From all sides, the hotel courtyard is fenced with a reliable fence of beautiful design. This is especially popular with tourists with children.

Guests are not allowed to smoke in the rooms of this hotel. But at the same time, guests of Kranevo can completely freely relax in a hotel with pets. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day.





Number of rooms

36 comfortable rooms - this is what Hotel Kranevo Hotel 3 * offers tourists who have chosen to spend their holidays in Kranevo. The photos of this hotel, presented on the page, demonstrate the convenience of the rooms rented out here. If desired, the hotel can rent standard rooms (15 m 2 ) or apartments (25 m 2 ). In all rooms, tourists can use:

  • telephone

  • mini-bar;

  • a safe;

  • a refrigerator;

  • TV.

The floors in the hotel rooms are tiled. Television at the hotel is satellite. If desired, tourists can watch one of the two Russian channels available at the hotel. Showers in this hotel in each room are individual. Among the free services in Palace Kranevo 3 * (Bulgaria) includes the use of a hairdryer. But guests should keep in mind that they bring it only on demand.

In addition, each room has a small balcony. If desired, beach items can be dried here. The views from the balconies of the hotel open, unfortunately, not at sea.





Guest room reviews

The opinion of the hotel guests about the rooms rented in it was simply excellent. In the network of rooms Palace Kranevo 3 * (Bulgaria, Kranevo, Albena) there is practically not a single bad review. Most tourists find the rooms in this hotel very comfortable, beautifully designed and clean. Cleaning maids in the rooms spend once every 2-3 days. Bed linen and towels in the shower in the hotel are also changed often - 2 times a week.

The beds in the hotel, according to many guests, are very comfortable. Household appliances in the hotel rooms are working properly. Some complaints are caused only by air conditioners in the rooms. The hotel is equipped with a centralized climate system. Apparently, therefore, the air conditioners in the rooms of Palace Kranevo 3 * do not cool the air very effectively. Also, tourists believe that the maids come to clean the rooms too early, at 9-10 in the morning, as some disadvantage of the hotel.

The advantages of the hotel, many tourists, among other things, include the availability of free WiFi. True in the rooms the Internet catches very badly. You can use it mainly only in the courtyard or in the garden.

There are no flying bloodsucking insects in the hotel rooms. This, of course, can also be attributed to the advantages of the hotel. Palace Kranevo rooms are equipped with mosquito nets.





Hotel infrastructure

The hotel rooms, according to most tourists, are equipped very well. Good service is also what deserves good hotel Palace Kranevo 3 * (Bulgaria) reviews . There are really a lot of amenities for tourists here. For example, guests can use the following services:

  • outdoor pool;

  • car rental

  • free parking.

The pool on site is not too large. However, the water in it is always warm and clean, and therefore it is pleasant to swim here. There are comfortable sun loungers and large umbrellas near the pool. You can use them for free. Parking at the hotel is unguarded.

The hotel also has a small conference room and a photocopy machine. Residents living on the upper floors have the opportunity to access the rooms by elevator. A small haberdashery shop also works on site.

Services for children

The care in the Hotel Palace Kranevo 3 * (Bulgaria, Albena, Kranevo) is shown not only about adult guests, but also about small ones. For children in the hotel are provided:

  • playground;

  • high chairs in the dining room.

If necessary, parents can rent a crib for the child at this hotel.





Nutrition

It is possible to rent rooms in the Palace Kranevo 3 * hotel with breakfast or with full board. Most tourists are advised to book the first option. For lunch and dinner, hotel guests most often prefer it in the city. In Kranevo there are many inexpensive cafes with a very good menu.

Breakfasts at the Palace Hotel Kranevo 3 * are served according to the "buffet" system. Food at the hotel, according to most guests, is delicious. In particular, tourists praise dairy products served in the hotel's dining room. For example, many vacationers like to take local cheese for breakfast. Tourists do not particularly praise meat dishes in the hotel's dining room. They are rarely served and in most cases, unfortunately, they are soybeans. Many tourists also consider the uniformity of dishes to be some lack of food at the hotel. For holidaymakers who have bought tours to this hotel for a long period, food in their dining room is usually boring.

Of course, guests sometimes order lunches with dinners at this hotel. Such vacationers to the advantages of the hotel, among other things, include the low cost of food. The prices for food at this hotel are small and, as many tourists have noted, have remained unchanged for many years.

The chef at the Hotel Palace Hotel has also been working a very experienced and conscientious woman for a very long time. Before that, she worked in the same specialty in kindergarten. She cooks, according to many guests, very well. The food in this hotel, albeit not too varied, but far from meager. Many hotel guests take only one portion for two or even three. And this is enough to eat.

In addition to the dining room, the hotel also has a small bar. Here tourists can order all kinds of drinks and snacks.

Palace Kranevo 3 *: beach reviews

This hotel is located, as already mentioned, quite close to the sea. Walking to the city beach Kranevo leisurely step tourists have no more than 5-7 minutes. On the way to the sea, guests of the Palace Kranevo have the opportunity to shop in shops of various specializations (souvenir, beach items, grocery items, etc.).

Actually, the Kranevo beach itself, according to vacationers, is very convenient - wide and not crowded. The sand here is soft and clean. Pass tourists on the beach completely free and free. If desired, vacationers can rent a sun lounger or umbrella here. This service is not very expensive (2 and 5 levs).

The water in the sea on the city beach of Kranevo is usually clean. However, after a storm, algae can swim in it. But they usually disappear very quickly. The fact that the water in the sea near the village is clean, is indicated, among other things, by the presence of a large number of different living creatures (mainly crabs and jellyfish).





Infrastructure of the village near the hotel

There are a lot of various catering establishments and shops in the immediate vicinity of the Palace Kranevo 3 * hotel. Especially very popular among tourists is the cafe "Bulgarian" located a stone's throw from the hotel. There are several fast foods near the hotel.

Unfortunately, there is no market nearby. However, hotel guests also have the opportunity to buy fruits and vegetables right next to her. Near the entrance to the hotel almost always work two trays. Fruits and vegetables are sold at a very low price. Most hotel guests are advised to buy them from Mary, a merchant and hawker.

In addition to fruit, hotel residents have the opportunity to purchase delicious ice cream in the immediate vicinity. Also near the hotel there are several cafes with wonderful cakes. In the Rosa-Market store, many hotel guests buy quality Bulgarian cosmetics.

The staff of the Palace Hotel Kranevo 3 * tourists consider it very polite. The local maids and administrators treat their guests with care and concern. Available Attractions

Of course, the guests of the Palace Kranevo 3 * hotel (Bulgaria), the description of which was given in full detail in the article above, have the opportunity to see various sorts of interesting places in Kranevo, Albena and their environs. The most popular attractions for vacationers at these resorts are:

  • caves at cape Kaliakra;

  • cooper's workshop;

  • Baltata reserve, etc.

Cape Kaliakra once housed a large medieval city. It even had its own mint. In 1791, a large-scale battle took place between the Ottoman Turks and the army of General Ushakov, which ended in the victory of the Russians. Today in one of the caves of the cape there is a museum dedicated entirely to this event.





Tourists visit the cooper's workshop in Kranevo primarily to get acquainted with the process of making barrels. This business, as you know, is rather complicated. At the end of the tour, the group independently collects a mock barrel.

The Baltata Nature Reserve is one of the most beautiful natural areas of the Black Sea coast of Bulgaria. It is a forest flooded with water. In total, more than 16 species of fish and about 250 species of plants live in this reserve.
