Royal Crown Hotel Palm Spa Resort, Phuket, Thailand: photos and reviews of tourists

In this short article we will discuss one wonderful hotel located in India. If you want to have a good rest, be sure to come here, you will definitely like it!

Location

Royal Crown Hotel Palm & Spa Resort is a hotel located 58 kilometers from the airport, on a mountainside, in a green, scenic area. Its height from sea level is thirty-five meters. The territory is as much as 10 acres (4 hectares) of dense exotic jungle, which can rightfully be called a tropical garden. It is so easy to feel your unity with nature: a gentle breeze blowing from the sea, quietly rustling leaves, and unseen exotic birds.



You will need to walk about a kilometer to the sea coast (the center of Patong Bay), since the hotel is located on the third coastline. However, guests do not need to get to the sea on foot, because in the afternoon (from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.) a special shuttle bus runs every two hours to the beach.

The Royal Crown Hotel Palm & Spa Resort is very quiet and comfortable, despite being located very close to the main attractions of Patong, where you can find nightclubs, as well as bars and shops. The Jungceylon Shopping Center can be reached on foot (it only takes a few minutes) or by special bus.

Hotel Residential Fund

Like any self-respecting three-star hotel, Royal Crown Hotel Palm & Spa Resort has everything you need for a comfortable and relaxing stay. There are 63 bungalows here, as well as a residential building consisting of four floors. The dining room building is located separately, to go to it, you need to climb a little higher up the hill.

Each of the 154 standard rooms has:

  • One double or two single beds. If necessary, they can put additional clamshells.
  • Desk, mirror, stool and chairs. If you wish, you can read a book or sit at a laptop, or maybe write down your impressions of rest in a diary while they are still fresh.
  • Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with free toiletries and a hairdryer.
  • Air conditioning, so hotel guests do not feel discomfort from being in a hot tropical area.
  • Balcony where you can sit, breathe fresh air and enjoy the views of the beautiful tropical garden, sea or pool at any convenient time.
  • Satellite TV and telephone, if you do not want to completely break away from your usual life.
  • Minibar with free bottled water.
  • Safe, in which you can safely leave money and documents.


If you want privacy and do not need neighbors behind the wall, then you can stay in one of 63 bungalows surrounded by palm trees and all kinds of greenery. Only there you will truly feel alone with nature. With all this, the bungalows have all the necessary amenities for a civilized person, including air conditioning.

Entertainment

In addition to the main entertainment of all tourists coming to Phuket - the sea with a sandy beach - Royal Crown Hotel Palm & Spa Resort offers other ways of entertainment:

  • The restaurant near the pool is ready to offer a huge selection of Thai cuisine, and for those who prefer something more familiar - European dishes.
  • An outdoor fitness center for those who, even on vacation, do not allow themselves to forget about training and keep fit.
  • Tennis court.
  • At Royal Crown Hotel Palm & Spa Resort 3 * you can also do table tennis or snooker.
  • To feel the full relaxation, you can go to a session of traditional Thai massage, visit a sauna or a steam room.
  • On the territory there are four pools: a large, children's, "Palm Resort", as well as "Three Seasons".


In addition, Royal Crown Hotel Palm & Spa Resort can offer its guests the following additional services:

  • Business center for business meetings and events.

sights

One kilometer from the Royal Crown Hotel & Palm Spa Resort is located almost the most famous beach in Phuket - Patong (Patong). This is a bay in which about 3 kilometers of coastline is strewn with white sand. There are sun loungers all over the beach, they can be rented for the whole day for about 100 Thai baht.

There is a good service on the beach. For example, if you want to order drinks, then the friendly waiters will bring them to the chaise lounge without any problems, you can continue your vacation further. Merchants come here who can inexpensively buy souvenirs or some goodies.

Along the entire beach is a road. If you go to its opposite side, you find yourself on a city street with restaurants, bars, shops and other fruits of civilization.

The best beaches

Patong Beach is the most popular in Phuket, and therefore many tourists are provided with various ways of entertainment: rent a surfboard, go skiing or banana. If you wish, you can rent (or even buy) snorkeling equipment to enjoy the underwater world of a coral reef.



This beach has a slight minus. Since it is located in the bay, after past storms the agitated water remains quite dirty for some time.

Another quite popular, but less crowded beach is Karon, located 5 kilometers from the Royal Crown Hotel & Palm Spa Resort. Its length is about four kilometers. The entire coast, even part of the road passing by, is strewn with golden sand. There is a peaceful atmosphere of solitude, although there are many hotels nearby. As in Patong, tourists are offered classic types of beach fun and entertainment.

It is worth remembering that in the rainy season Karon Beach is fraught with many dangers for vacationers. Suddenly emerging undercurrents are able to carry into the open sea even those who can call themselves an experienced swimmer. However, beach rescuers vigilantly monitor the situation and in case of danger put red flags on the beach.

Of course, beaches are very good. But where to go after sunset for tourists eager for vibrant nightlife? The answer to this question is very simple. Just a kilometer from the Royal Crown Hotel & Palm Spa Resort (Phuket) is the famous entertainment street called Bangla Road. Rhythmic music attracts amusement institutions of various kinds.

Walking on the right side of Bangla Road, you can look into a cafe with exotic and European cuisine, you can sit in a restaurant with live music. Quite often, Filipino groups arrange their performances here. In addition, there are sports bars in which you can watch sports broadcasts on a large screen with a glass of beer along with other fans.

Walking on the left side of Bangla Road, tourists fall into the world of adult entertainment: go-go and strip bars are found at every step. Dressed in sexy and enticing outfits (and sometimes even without clothes), the beauties dance at the pole. Sometimes these poles are right on the bar. Despite the free entrance to such establishments, vacationers here have to order alcohol without fail. However, in order to see something interesting, sometimes it is not even necessary to go inside the institution, because many artists and dancers perform right on the street.

Guest reviews

Tourists leave reviews about the Royal Crown Hotel & Palm Spa Resort varied, but mostly positive. They are noted by the politeness and friendliness of the staff and the cleanliness of the rooms (every day they clean).



Despite the fact that the path to the sea at first may seem distant (about one and a half kilometers), hotel guests note this rather as a plus. After all, remoteness from the beach means privacy and silence, and walking for 15 minutes is not a big problem. In addition, you can always get to the beach from the hotel either by a free bus, or rent a bike and get there with the breeze. This location of the hotel, according to tourists, has another positive aspect: very close is the whole city party scene with cafes, clubs, restaurants and shops.

Vacationers at the Royal Crown Hotel & Palm Spa Resort 3 * really enjoy the views from the rooms and bungalows. Due to the fact that the hotel is located on a small hill, from the balcony you can admire the sea or the greenery of the forest. If desired, you can choose rooms that overlook the pool. And the photos left by guests at the Royal Crown Hotel & Palm Spa Resort are unlikely to leave anyone indifferent.

In general, according to tourists who visited this hotel, it is fully consistent with its three stars. It is recommended for those who want to relax on a budget and get a vivid impression of an exotic country.
