The recreation center "Brook", Dzhubga: description, features and reviews

For those who want to have a good rest on the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory, it is important to know that the resorts of this area are not limited to the well-known to all Sochi, Gelendzhik and Anapa. The small village of Dzhubga is also a wonderful place, created in order to spend your vacation and enjoy the southern nature. A good option for living here can be a small recreation center "Trickle" (Dzhubga), which is well located away from the highway.

Rest in Dzhubga

Located in the Tuapse district of the Krasnodar Territory, the village of Dzhubga is the first settlement on the Black Sea coast that is found by all those traveling along the highway towards Sochi. Local people often relax here, but tourists from other cities of our country also come.





Dzhubga is famous for its beautiful nature, clear sea and a large beach, near which there is enough space not only for hotels, but also for campsites. So even the most demanding guest can find their accommodation option here.

Entertainment in the village is also enough. There is a water park with a variety of slides and a dolphinarium, in which you can watch performances with the participation of marine animals. You can take a trip to the local dolmen, to waterfalls or just admire the mountain beauties. And all kinds of dining rooms, cafes and restaurants, which are quite numerous in Dzhubga, will not leave a single tourist who came here hungry.

Recreation center by the sea

Finding housing in Dzhubga, as well as in other resort towns of the Krasnodar Territory, is not at all a problem. There are good hotels, guest houses and recreation facilities, so vacationers will have plenty to choose from. True, some of them are located very close to the federal highway, which goes towards Sochi, and for many tourists this is clearly not to their liking.

However, there is a quiet and cozy place, a vacation in which all lovers of nature, peace and solitude will enjoy. It is called "Trickle" - a recreation center (Dzhubga, Tuapse), which is located among a real forest with half-century trees at the very foot of the mountain. Is the vacation in "Brook" expensive?

The recreation center "Trickle" (Dzhubga) is waiting for guests every year from early May to late September. Depending on the month, the cost of the offered rooms also varies. More expensive rest in the "Brook" will cost in July and August, and more budget can be accommodated in May, September and June.





  • In 2017, vacationers will have to pay 1600 rubles for a stay in a double Suite in the low season and 2000 in the high season. "Suite" for four guests will cost 3,000 and 3,600, respectively.
  • Comfort rooms for two guests will cost 1200-1400 rubles per night, for three - 1800-2100, for four - 2400-2800, respectively.
  • In rooms of the type "Economy" accommodation is the cheapest: a double will cost 700-900 rubles per night depending on the season, triple - 1050-1350, four - 1400-1800, respectively.

How is room reservation done?

Tourists planning to come to the Rucheek recreation center should take care in advance to get the desired rooms. To do this, there is a reservation system, which can be used online or by one of the phone numbers listed above.





It is worth knowing that if guests want to make a room reservation, they will have to make an advance payment of 50% of the cost of the entire stay. If for some reason tourists refuse their reservation, then they can return the money only if this happened no later than 30 days before the planned arrival.

Guest reviews about "Brook"

The recreation center "Trickle" (Dzhubga), reviews of which present it as a very good place for spending a budget vacation, is popular not only among guests of the Krasnodar Territory, but also among those who simply come to the sea for the weekend. Many guests of this place really like its location due to the tall trees and its distance from the road. The neighboring camp for children does not interfere with a quiet and relaxing holiday here. Tourists note the friendly staff, comfortable rooms and also a great opportunity to take a barbecue for their needs.





However, almost every review of the recreation center "Trickle" (Dzhubga) also contains information about the shortcomings of this place. There are very few dishes in the shared kitchen, so experienced tourists recommend taking their own. Also, sometimes hot water is turned off here, which causes discontent among vacationers. For children, there is not enough of any playground where they could spend time.
