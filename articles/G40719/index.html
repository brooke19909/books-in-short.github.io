Android Wear watches: review and reviews

In 1966, the magazine "Technique of Youth" was published with smart watches on the cover. Already at that time, everything was predicted and a foregone conclusion, and they were not mistaken, something like this these futuristic gadgets from the past look like now. Electronics really became wearable. It is gaining momentum and becoming more popular, people love modern gadgets, especially interesting ones like fitness trackers or smart watches. Therefore, manufacturers are increasingly looking at this market, introducing more and more new products.

Android Wear watches: what is it and why?

Android Wear is one of the most promising and advanced platforms for wearable electronics. This is an operating system for watches, created for unification in the same way as regular Android is created to provide all manufacturers with a single platform. Initially, this platform was experimental, like most Google products, and was created as the main competitor to Apple Watch. It has all the features characteristic of a smart watch for communication, receiving notifications, playing sports and many other features. We'll talk about all that Android Wear watches are capable of next.





First of all, it‚Äôs a watch.

The smartphone, to which the watch is connected, is responsible for the accuracy of time, data is synchronized between two devices. When changing the time zone, the time on the watch will change too. This is possible thanks to geolocation services (the first important advantage compared to conventional watches).

The clock display does not light constantly (although it may be at the request of the user), it usually lights up only when the owner raises his hand.

An important feature of the platform and the main difference from the Apple Watch is the presence of a dial store. There are plenty of them on Google Play, any user will definitely find something to their taste. The collection includes both dials imitating well-known brands and completely unusual solutions, such as using only images or text.

Google Now: All the information on your wrist

One of the best uses for watches is, of course, using a voice assistant or assistant like Google Now or Siri. In the case of Android Wear, it‚Äôs still more interesting, because Google Now knows a lot of information about you and knows how to present it correctly. It is enough to raise your wrist, as you will see: weather forecast, results of recent matches, new release, scheduled meetings and much more, which may be useful in a particular situation.

As for the calendar, Google Now collects information not only about your meetings, but also about the venue, which means that by looking at your watch you will also see how much time will be spent on the road and where you need to move.

Here it is the future, all the information on the wrist.

Programs and applications

The number of applications is growing, and growing very quickly, both large developers and beginners, who decided to try themselves in this field, pulled themselves into the Google Play store.

At the moment, I want to distinguish three categories of applications that are of particular popularity.

Organizers and task managers. The first thing that watch owners think about is productivity. Many popular programs have already been ported to Android Wear watches, for example, Evernote - a popular cross-platform notebook or Todoist - to-do manager with a capital letter.

Dials. Although they have already been discussed, it is still one of the most popular categories, people really like to change their dials.

Fitness Applications for sports, fortunately, have gained quite a high popularity among owners of wearable electronics. Smart watches Android Wear offer a whole catalog of programs for running, cycling and even powerlifting.

It is also worth noting that the watch is great for ordering a taxi or eating at home, searching for information on the Internet or listening to music. Complete with Bluetooth headphones, the Android Wear watch can be an excellent replacement for the player. If you take into account the fact that the watch received GPS and 3G-modules, then the situation is quite real when you leave your giant smartphone at home and go for a light walk, because your watch is now as smart as your phone.
