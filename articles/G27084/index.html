What to do in Minsk: an overview of entertainment centers, cinemas, museums, interesting cafes, reviews

Upper city

Perhaps you have only one weekend at your disposal and you don‚Äôt know what to do? There are a lot of events at the weekend in Minsk. Be sure to visit the Upper Town.

The upper city has always been considered the main district of Minsk. From the earliest times, only the wealthiest people of the capital lived here. So it remains to this day. In addition, the best entertainment is now concentrated in the Upper Town.

There are always a lot of street musicians here at the weekend. Performers of jazz, classical, local cover bands. In the afternoon in the Upper Town there are also a lot of people. The city's attractions

Arriving in Minsk, be sure to familiarize yourself with the history of the city. The easiest option to explore the past capital is to visit local attractions. Looking for something interesting in Minsk? Where to go to learn something new about the city? A visit to the most beautiful historical places of the city will definitely satisfy your curiosity. This option of spending time in the capital is also advantageous in that it does not require special expenses.

Be sure to look at the Palace of the Republic. The opinion of tourists about this place is very mixed. Many admire the architecture of the building, but there are those who consider the Palace of the Republic one of the most ugly buildings in the city. It‚Äôs worth a visit at least in order to form your own opinion about the structure.

An interesting place in Minsk is also considered Independence Avenue. Many are impressed by the architecture of the buildings here. None of the buildings is like another, but despite this, all the houses look very organic. The avenue has many squares, including Independence Square.

On the same avenue is the Red Church or, as it is also called, the Church of Saints Simeon and Elena. The temple was built in 1901. During the reign of Soviet power, a theater was located here. Now here again are the church services. In addition, various performances, organ music concerts and other events are often held here. The architecture of the building is truly impressive.

The Minsk City Hall is also on the list of beautiful architectural buildings of the city that are worth a look. In summer, the building often hosts performances by various musical groups.

Trinity Suburb is one of the few areas of the city that were virtually unaffected during the war, and therefore the atmosphere of ancient Belarus is preserved here. Now it is also very beautiful here, it is a great place for photographs.

In Minsk you can see many unusual monuments. For example, near the Komarovsky market there is a monument to small business - a grandmother with seeds, who is waiting for her customers. Buyers also have a monument. Near the central department store you can see sculptures of two children and their parents. While a boy and a girl are looking at shop windows, mom and dad turn their pockets up in search of money.

"DINOSAURIA"

Where to go with children in Minsk? Both in the summer and in the winter, the Dinosaur Entertainment Center operates in the city. Everything is provided for recreation for both children and adults.

DINOSAURIA is one of the largest entertainment centers for children in Minsk. Every day there is a "DINO-SHOW": an exhibition of dinosaur figures that can move and make sounds. A small educational program is immediately carried out, which tells in a game form about dinosaurs, their varieties, what they ate, where and when they lived, etc. This part of the show continues to develop, so that the program is constantly being improved for the pleasure of visitors.

Rest in "DINOSAURI" is provided for both children and adults. The entire entertainment center is divided into two zones: a nursery and an adult. Visitors' comments about "DINOSAURI" are mostly positive. Tourists like a variety of entertainments here, various show programs, master classes. Among the interesting places in Minsk where tourists should go, there is a museum-gallery of the artist Felix Yanushevich. This place is definitely not like the galleries and art exhibitions that you had to visit before.

The gallery museum is one of the most unusual institutions in Minsk. It is located in a small suburb called Rakov. Once the founder of the gallery Felix Yanushevich opened it in order to show the world his own works. In addition, he is a collector of unusual art objects from around the world. A visit to the museum will surely appeal to lovers of mystical stories. These legends tell about the past of Rakov, who was previously considered the "capital of smugglers."

National Library of Belarus

Among the ideas of what to do in Minsk is a visit to the library of Belarus. Do not rush to be surprised. The fact is that the library building claims to be the best architectural structure of the country. This place is especially beautiful at nightfall, when a large cube begins to glow with hundreds of lights. A visit to the library will definitely provide you with a huge number of beautiful photos.

Of course, there was always a lot of controversy surrounding such a bold architectural decision. As already written earlier, the library building is made in the form of a large cube, more precisely, a rhombocuboctahedron twenty-three floors high. In the building you can see very rare and valuable manuscripts, books. The library holds various excursions, events, in addition, you can arrange a holiday here. Also, be sure to go up to the observation deck on the roof of the building. The view from there opens up simply incredible.
