Cross stitch unicorn

Each of us in childhood heard many tales. It was from them that we learned about a large number of unusual and magical creatures. Who in childhood did not dream to ride a pegasus or unicorn? The latter will be discussed in our article. We will show you several patterns of unicorn embroidery, and one of them will inspire you to create a masterpiece with your own hands.

We will mainly talk about cross-stitch, but we will also touch on topics such as embroidery with beads. You will learn what materials are best used and how to embroider so that the result will please you for many years.

Necessary materials for embroidery unicorn cross-stitch

To embroider a cross, you will need a hoop, cotton or wool floss thread , a needle with a wide hole, a canvas, as well as decor elements (rhinestones and sequins). For the unicorn itself, it is best to use woolen threads, as they will add more volume to the fabulous creation. But for the horns, hooves, mane and tail, you can take a floss with a metallic effect.

Embroidery hoops are best chosen from wood or plastic, but with a metal lock. Before buying, be sure to check the mechanism. Also get some embroidery needles.

Simple unicorn patterns

If you are new to cross-stitching, it is best for the first time to take a simple unicorn embroidery pattern. For example, you can take as a basis the following.

The size of the finished embroidery is approximately 20 by 20 centimeters. The final value depends on the size of the cells. You should start embroidering from the middle. To find it, divide all sides in half and connect the opposite with an imaginary line. The intersection point of these lines will be the center of your unicorn embroidery.





Note that the unicorn in the pattern is white. If you wish, you can not embroider white cells, but we strongly advise you not to be too lazy and embroider the whole beast. So it will be more voluminous. After you have finished embroidering, buy a frame and pull the fabric. You can also embroider a bag, pillowcase, children's wardrobe or any other piece of furniture.

Emblem with unicorn cross stitch

To make such an emblem, prepare a small piece of canvas, a piece of thick felt, glue, floss threads of gray, white, green, light green, yellow and dark gray, hoop and needle.

Define the center first, and then start embroidering. After you have finished cross-stitching, embroider the outline with the dark gray threads as shown in the image.





Carefully thread all the threads on the back. If necessary, fix the threads with a drop of glue. Cut a piece of felt that fits the embroidery. Next, sew or glue the logo on the felt. You can also make a brooch from this craft: for this you need to buy a special base and sew it to the wrong side.

Complex and multicolor cross stitch unicorn

If you have already embroidered at one time, then you can take a more complex pattern. For works using a large number of shades of floss, it is better to buy a product from a French manufacturer than from a Chinese one. The difference is in the number of shades: as a rule, Chinese floss do not differ in their wealth.

First hoop the canvas. The size of the finished embroidery is 20 by 30 centimeters. Embroider from the middle. After you have finished cross-stitching, pay attention to the fact that the unicorn and flowers with leaves located below have an outline. For the unicorn, make the outline a dark gray thread, for the grass dark green, and for the flowers orange.





After you have finished embroidering, you need to make a frame. It can be bought at a needlework store or ordered at a baguette workshop. It is better to hide the embroidered picture for safety under glass. When buying a frame, pay attention to the mount. It is better that the picture is hung on the wall with a steel cable. A simple aluminum hook can be bent over time, and the picture risks falling.

If you do not want to make a picture, you can decorate a bag or pillowcase with such embroidery. To do this, carefully thread the threads on the back side, and then bend the edges of the embroidery slightly and sew it to the fabric. Items with embroidered fragments do not need special care: they can also be washed and worn without fear that your favorite thing will go bad.

What does a unicorn in embroidery mean?

Many people know who a unicorn is, but few know what this fabulous beast symbolizes. The unicorn is a symbol of prudence and caution, prudence and purity, severity, as well as integrity and severity.





Unicorns can be seen in films about the wizard Harry Potter and the magical land of Narnia. In addition, unicorns are mentioned in the famous novel Game of Thrones.

Unicorn beadwork

If you want to embroider with beads, then you will also need a special fabric, nylon thread and pencil. And, of course, beads. Now there are many different companies specializing in the production of beads. We advise you to purchase beads of either Czech or Japanese origin. But in no case Chinese (he usually has a very poor calibration).

First pull the fabric over the frame or hoop. If you work with a hoop, then try to choose their size so that you do not have to change the position of the fabric. Then apply a pencil drawing on the fabric. Designate colors so as not to confuse them in the process. Also indicate color transitions.





Then start sewing one bead to the fabric. This method of creating bead paintings is called pattern embroidery.

There is another option for creating bead paintings. You can buy either a set with a scheme and beads, or one scheme. In this case, you will embroider according to an already finished pattern, and you will not have to choose colors, since beads, as a rule, are already included in the set.

The finished work of beads must be enclosed in a frame, hiding it under glass (beads have the ability to collect dust very well). If you do not want to insert a picture under the glass, you will have to wipe it with a slightly damp sponge from time to time. The fastenings for it should be strong, since the picture embroidered with beads is much heavier than the embroidered mouline thread.
