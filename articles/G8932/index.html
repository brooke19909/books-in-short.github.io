Muine De Century 4 * (Vietnam / Phan Thiet): reviews and photos, description of the hotel

Vietnam is a fairly popular place that is visited by lovers of beach and sightseeing holidays. Naturally, in recent years, hundreds of hotels and resorts have been built on the coast. And quite popular today is Muine de Century. Where is the hotel located? What living conditions await travelers? What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for? These questions are asked by many travelers.

Where is the hotel Muine de Century Resort 4 *?





It is worth noting that this hotel is famous for its advantageous location. For example, just 16 kilometers (10-15 minutes by taxi) is the city of Phan Thiet. Muine de Century 4 * is located just 200 meters from Mui Ne Beach, on the first coastline - breathtaking views of the sea open from almost anywhere in the hotel complex. The distance to Khomishin Airport, however, is much larger - 216 kilometers, but this problem can be solved, since the hotel, like travel agencies, offers a shuttle service.

Hotel Muine de Century 4 * is located in a quiet, calm area. But the main street passes nearby - here you will find a lot of shops, cafes, shopping centers, night clubs and other entertainment venues, so you will not be cut off from civilization.

Brief description of the territory and infrastructure

To begin with, it is worth saying that the Muine de Century 4 * hotel is a large complex - its total area is 4 hectares. Here there are both multi-storey buildings and small cozy villas that will appeal to lovers of a quiet secluded holiday.





The territory of the hotel is well-groomed - here modern amenities combine well with the breathtaking beauty of nature. Neatly trimmed lawns, bright flowering bushes, tall palm trees, cozy courtyards and comfortable walking paths - all this will appeal to even the most demanding traveler. Naturally, there are pools, comfortable gazebos in the shade of palm trees, as well as sports fields and much more.

What rooms are available in the hotel?

89 rooms of various categories are what the Muine de Century 4 * hotel complex can offer. The photo shows all the charm of the rooms - they are really spacious, clean, with large windows and comfortable furniture. On the territory of the hotel there are both multi-storey residential buildings and small villas built in the Vietnamese style. Depending on your personal preferences, you can choose a room with a view of the sea and the beach, courtyard or garden. Some villas are designed for 4-5 people, which is convenient if you travel with the whole family.





What awaits you in the room itself? Naturally, there is a necessary set of furniture, including beds, tables, chairs, bedside tables, wardrobes and even a large wooden desk. Upon request, a cot will be brought to the room, which is also convenient.

From household appliances there is a TV (there are satellite channels, including one Russian), a telephone (international and long distance calls for an additional fee), as well as a safe with an electronic lock, a set for making hot drinks, air conditioning. The minibar can be filled, but this service is paid separately - for every tourist two bottles of drinking water are put for free per day.

A spacious bathroom will delight any tourist. Depending on the room category, there is a shower, bathtub, washbasin, mirror, a set of clean towels and bathrobes. Every day, maids replenish hygiene supplies - travelers are provided with shampoo, soap, toothpaste, etc. There is also a hairdryer.

Food scheme for tourists





The hotel complex Muine de Century 4 * offers tourists a choice of two meal plans - BB (breakfast only) and HB (breakfast and dinner). Buffets are served twice a day in the main restaurant . The choice of dishes here is quite large and will delight travelers, especially fans of Vietnamese cuisine - many fish and seafood dishes, as well as light desserts, vegetables and fresh fruits. More familiar European cuisine is also available. By the way, the restaurant offers breathtaking views of the beach and the sea.

In addition, there are two bars on site that offer grilled dishes, light snacks and drinks. In any case, do not worry about food, as there are many cafes and restaurants on the main street where you can dine at a very reasonable price.

Water recreation and beach





The beach can be reached in just a few minutes, as it is located very close. The beach here is covered with soft, clean golden sand. The entrance to the sea is smooth, the bottom is also sandy, so the place is perfect for bathing children. By the way, the 200-meter-long site belongs to the hotel - only guests can be in the private area. There are sun loungers and umbrellas, for the use of which you do not have to pay.

Of course, in addition to swimming and sunbathing, there are other activities. For example, you can ride a boat, canoe, boat, water ski or surf.

Additional services offered by the hotel

Of course, on the territory of the Muine de Century 4 * hotel they tried to create all the conditions for the most convenient and cloudless vacation. To begin with, it is worth saying that there is a parking lot with 24-hour security. If you do not have your own car, then if you wish, you can easily rent a transport - the city is full of rental points.

If necessary, you can take your clothes to the local laundry and dry cleaning. The hotel has Internet access - you can use it for free. For a small amount, you can call a qualified doctor in your room. The hotel also provides hotel services. By the way, there is a small business center and a conference room that can accommodate 200 people.

Entertainment and leisure guests





Almost the entire courtyard is occupied by pools, around which deck chairs and umbrellas are placed. There are both shallow pools and places that are perfect for divers. There is also a small shallow area for children. Also, a small pool located in the middle of the garden will delight tourists who settled in the villas. There is also an outdoor jacuzzi.

Guests can visit the local fitness club. By the way, the gym is also noteworthy - simulators and equipment are exhibited here on the site, in the open air. There is also the opportunity to sign up for a massage - the hotel has excellent professional massage therapists.

Lovers of more active activities can visit the local hard tennis courts. Here you can rent rackets and other tennis equipment. By the way, there is a hotel complex with large golf courses nearby - you can use them for a relatively small fee. And, of course, lovers of excursion vacations will also not be bored, because on the coast there are a lot of interesting places worthy of your attention.

Hotel complex Muine de Century 4 *: traveler reviews





It's no secret that a lot of useful information about a particular place can be gleaned from the reviews left by travelers. Today, many tourists choose Vietnam for their holidays . Muine de Century 4 * has a pretty good reputation.

For example, former guests speak well of living conditions. The rooms have everything you need, household appliances are in good condition, and the rooms are cleaned every day. The hotel staff are all polite and helpful, help solve any problem and speak good English (some problems may arise with the Russian language). The beach is clean and secluded, the hotel area is well maintained, well equipped and worthy of praise. As for nutrition, this is a real paradise for fans of oriental and Vietnamese cuisine - every day something new appears on the menu.

You can come to rest here with a child. Fans of noisy parties will also not have to be bored, as there are many establishments of this type near the hotel. Often holidays, seminars and even weddings are organized here. The hotel is suitable for almost any type of vacation. Moreover, the prices here are relatively affordable, which is good news.
