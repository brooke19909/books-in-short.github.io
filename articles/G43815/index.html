Differences iPhone XS from iPhone X

In terms of design, both phones are exact copies of each other, the display also has no differences, and its diagonal is 5.8 inches. The screen resolution, number of pixels, contrast and brightness have not changed. Here are just manufacturers specify that the dynamic range has increased by as much as 60%. Unfortunately, users are unlikely to notice this.

There is a difference in weight, but not significant - XS is heavier by as much as 3 grams. Most likely, it arose due to the improved and strengthened glass both from the side of the screen and from the back side.

The processor in XS is better and able to save battery power, performance has increased by 15% and it is better tuned to work with neural networks. In addition, the improved processor now has not a 2-core coprocessor, but an 8-core one.

We expanded the main memory of the device. If on model X there was a maximum of 256 GB, then in XS it became 512 GB. This is enough to store 100,000 photographs.

The camera remained unchanged, the difference is only in the increased size of the sensor from 1.2 to 1.4 microns. This allowed about 50% more light to pass through.

Among the less significant differences, you can specify the following:

  • Face ID has become faster
  • the audio adapter was removed from the package;
  • autonomy increased by 30 minutes;
  • manufacturers modified the NFC sensor.

As you can see, the changes are quite insignificant, but the price difference between these models is from 20,000 rubles.

Competitors

There is a large selection of smartphones with the same or approximate characteristics, but with prices an order of magnitude lower. The iPhone competitors are on the alert and every spring delight people with new products.

Samsung Galaxy Note 9

The cost of this smartphone is from 69,000 rubles. Samsung has a large screen diagonal, increased RAM, a stylus, a standard headphone jack, a memory card slot and faster charging. Not without cons. It is only one; it consists in a less efficient processor. That's just it is almost not felt.

According to buyers, this Korean device is currently the most advanced.

The screen is 6.5 inches with a resolution of 2960x1440. It does not have a recess above the sensors. The camera is dual with a post-focus, Samsung Exynos 9810 processor, more RAM (6 GB). The main advantage of this device is the stylus, in addition, it still plays the role of a remote control (remote). He is able to rewind music and video, as well as release the shutter on the camera and at the same time everything happens without contact with the smartphone.

Note 9 can be called the main competitor of the iPhone XS.

Samsung Galaxy S9 +

The cost of the phone is approximately 66,000 rubles. This model has a fairly large screen with increased resolution, it does not have a notch in the upper part under the camera, sensors and a speaker, more RAM, there is a standard headphone jack, the kit includes fast charging and there is a slot for expanding memory.

This competitor of "iPhone 10" and XS has minor disadvantages, but this helps to save a few thousand rubles. The screen is 2.10 inches smaller diagonally, there is no stylus, the battery is 500 mAh less, and in the basic version of the memory it is 64 GB less.

LG G7 ThinQ

This representative of competitors "iPhone" costs 59,000 rubles. Its performance is lower due to the average processor, however, this is not very noticeable for ordinary users.

The screen of this model has a higher resolution, there is a connector (standard) for headphones, the kit provides for fast charging and there is a slot for a memory card. In addition, the headphones have a very clear and loud sound.

However, despite its advantages, this model is not very popular. Many believe that this is due to the strange and disproportionate form of the screen (19.5x9) and the camera, which takes not very good photos in automatic mode. However, during manual shooting this problem is not. The smartphone has one significant advantage in the form of an additional wide-angle lens. This few models can boast of.
