Rostov Dolphinarium is waiting for guests!

The Rostov Dolphinarium cordially opened its doors in June 2009. Its building was built taking into account the features of the content and all the needs of marine inhabitants.

Description

In its arsenal, the Rostov Dolphinarium has three pools, in one there are performances, and in the other two live entertainers (a family of dolphins and pinnipeds) live and relax. The central hall with a swimming pool, where performances are held, has three stands, a central one, a right and a left one with four rows of comfortable seats. The hall is decorated with colorful decorations that cheer up even before the show. Seats located in the forefront make it possible to enjoy the performance and the spray of water, which amusing entertainers generously drench around the audience.

Show program

The Rostov Dolphinarium boasts professional trainers who constantly update the water show program. Their pets are happy to perform complex tricks: they jump through rings and bars, play basketball, dance, perform dizzying somersaults, throw up and ride coaches, and most importantly - they amuse the audience not only with the planned performance, but also with constant improvisation, which is typical for real artists. Interestingly, dolphin talents are not limited to performing tricks, they are also great artists. Scientists have long been aware of the beneficial effects of dolphin creativity on the human psyche. Therefore, it is worth taking the opportunity to participate in the auction and acquire for yourself such a therapeutic picture.

The duration of the show is from 45 to 50 minutes. The program looks at one go, of course, time flies like one minute. It is not necessary to leave the dolphinarium immediately after the performance, with the permission of the trainer and absolutely free of charge, you can stroke marine animals, for an additional fee, take a picture with them.

Swimming with sea animals

Also, the Rostov Dolphinarium for a very reasonable fee provides an excellent opportunity to swim in the pool with dolphins. These amazing animals will please not only children, but also adults with their friendliness, playfulness and kindness, swimming in such a wonderful company will bring great pleasure, and the positive effect of dolphin therapy will help strengthen psychological and physical health.

Additional entertainment

The dolphinarium is located in the picturesque park "Fairy Tale", where you can take a walk before the performance, relax at the fountain, visit the rides or ride horses and ponies. You can also have a good time and grab a bite to eat at the cafe, which is cozy in the park.

Prices

A wonderful place is the Rostov Dolphinarium, the prices for the performance and other services are quite reasonable. The ticket price for adults is 300 rubles. Children under five can watch the show for free. A photograph with dolphins costs 300 rubles.

Rostov Dolphinarium. How can I get to?

Finding a dolphinarium is not difficult, it is located on the territory of the Skazka park, Sovetsky district, Rostov-on-Don, Communist prospect, 36, building 4.

Landmarks: Church of St. George the Victorious, Cinema Plaza Cinema, Perekrestok store, tennis court.

The nearest transport stop is located near the Skazka park.

This is followed by buses No. 34, 98, 96, 94, trolleybus No. 8 and minibuses No. 34, 98, 96, 25, 94.

Schedule and acceptance of applications

Tickets for the Rostov Dolphinarium can be purchased at the box office, which are located in the building of the water arena and operate from ten in the morning until seven in the evening, telephone for inquiries and applications: (863) 270-68-69.

Dolphin therapy: +7 928 125-79-04.

Performances are held on Saturday and Sunday at 12.00 and 16.00. Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday - 12.00, only with group applications.
