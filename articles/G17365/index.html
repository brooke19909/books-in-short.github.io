Chelyabinsk region. "Zyuratkul" (recreation center)

In the Chelyabinsk region, Lake Zyuratkul is located. A huge number of rivers and streams flow into it. The coastline of the reservoir extends for 26 km, its length is 7 km. Water is striking in its purity due to water exchange, which takes place during the year. Once the pond was natural, at the present time it is formed by a dam built on the Great Satka.



Geographic location

The lake is located in the National Park, which has the same name, and is one of the highest alpine reservoirs in the Southern Urals. Around it are mountain ranges that cover coniferous forests. South-west is the highest peak in the Chelyabinsk region - Nurgush. The main river supplying the lake is the Big Satka, near the source of which is the village of Zyuratkul. The recreation center, and not one located on the territory, annually attracts a huge number of vacationers.

Features

There is a recreation center "Lake of Heaven" in the village of Zyuratkul. It is located in the National Park, in the alpine zone of the Chelyabinsk region. There are 15 natural monuments, about 650 species of different plants grow, more than 40 species of mammals and 150 species of birds are found. From the animals on the territory you can meet bears, hares, martens, moose, squirrels and foxes. But the main advantage of the park is Lake Zyuratkul, located at an altitude of 724 km above sea level.



Conditions for tourists

The recreation center on Zyuratkul is constantly filled with visitors. And this is no accident. Conditions for outdoor activities, sports tourism and fishing - all this provides a recreation center. Zyuratkul Lake is a picturesque place, here every tourist will find something to his liking. In addition, parking services, a fishing instructor are provided, as well as the possibility of renting a boat, a brazier, barbecue and fishing equipment. You can order excursions, both one-day and multi-day.

Camping camp "Zyuratkul"

The recreation center, which is described above, is not the only place hosting tourists in the national park. Guests can spend their leisure time in a comfortable environment surrounded by nature. A hundred meters from the shore of the pond is a camping camp "Zyuratkul". The recreation center, which will be discussed later, is equipped with everything that is necessary for an excellent pastime. In the campsite, you can fully spend your holidays for both children and adults. Vacationers are accommodated in German tents. They are installed on wooden floors and have two rooms. All visitors are provided with good bedding. Each is provided with a foam, pillow, mattress and blanket. Fresh air of the middle mountains has a beneficial effect on the human body, increasing its immunity and resistance to colds. In addition, the campsite is equipped with a covered area designed for discos, street lighting, convenient walking paths. For everyone, a Russian bath works every day.
