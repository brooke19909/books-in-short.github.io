"Tele 2", "Promised payment": how to activate the service?

Tele 2 is a telecom operator that offers subscribers competitive prices for telecommunication services. The brand focuses on saving when using its own tariffs with bright colored names ("Orange", "Infinitely black") and a policy of honesty towards customers.

Who can use the option "Tele 2" "Promised payment"?

The Promised Payment service is available for Tele 2 subscribers using a prepaid recharge system.

A prepaid method of payment involves replenishing the mobile balance before using the tariff plan: in order to call / send an SMS notification, you must first make a payment.

An alternative method of replenishing the balance is postpay, when subscribers pay with their mobile operator at the end of the period: on the fact of sent SMS messages and minutes of conversation, a debt is charged for repayment. Service "Tele 2" "Promised payment" does not support postpaid tariff.
