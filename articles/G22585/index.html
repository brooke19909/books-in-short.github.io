LEGO Competitions





LEGO regularly organizes competitions among young fans of its products, and there are many in all countries of the world. The designer is very useful for acquiring initial technical skills; he develops attentiveness, ingenuity and a sense of design composition. From a huge number of details, children choose the right elements, make quick decisions and put them into practice, showing imagination and imagination. Courageous forms, graceful decisions and unusual approach can sometimes be envied by professional designers. To take part in such a competition, it is not enough to know how to make a car from Lego according to the instructions, you need to be able to create something of your own, which no one has thought of yet. This is not given to everyone, and the abundance of details is not the main condition, rather, on the contrary, their lack sometimes makes you look for a way out and develops non-standard thinking.
