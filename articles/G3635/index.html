Lavender Nha Trang Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam): description and photos

Planning to discover Vietnam? Have you decided to choose the city of Nha Trang as your vacation spot? Looking for a budget modern hotel with comfortable rooms and a good location? In this case, Lavender Nha Trang Hotel 3 * may be a good option for you.





Location

Almost all tourists who left Lavender Nha Trang Hotel 3 * reviews, in their comments claim that the hotel boasts an excellent location. So, only the road separates it from the beach. True, according to our compatriots, crossing it can be problematic, since here practically none of the drivers passes pedestrians, even if they are moving along the zebra. Therefore, cross the road should be very careful. Also near the hotel there is a large park with several pools of different depths, shapes and sizes, sun terraces, bars, etc. So if you do not want to go to the beach, you can have a great time here.

In addition, the hotel in question is located near the main tourist attractions of Nha Trang. So, the city center can be reached in 10-15 minutes by leisurely step. In addition, in the vicinity of the hotel there are many cafes, shops, supermarkets, a market, shops, spas and much more.

As for the proximity to the airport, the distance to Camran Air Harbor is only 35 kilometers. So upon arrival in Vietnam you do not need to spend a lot of time on the road to the hotel. If necessary, you can always order an airport shuttle directly at the hotel for an additional fee. Also, if you want to be met upon arrival in Nha Trang, please notify the property.





General information, photo

Lavender Nha Trang Hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang) began its work in 2014. It is a 10-story modern building. He has no own territory. However, this situation is typical for most hotels in Nha Trang. After all, here is very expensive land, so the owners of the objects try to use every meter of it, building tall buildings on a small area.

The housing stock of the hotel consists of 45 comfortable modern rooms. The building is equipped with an elevator for the convenience of guests. In addition to the apartments, it houses a restaurant, lounge and reception.

The Lavender Nha Trang Hotel, the reviews of our compatriots about which are overwhelmingly positive, is perfect for a wide variety of tourists. So, it will be comfortable here both for families with children, active youth, and for older people. By the way, this hotel boasts a very high rating based on the ratings of travelers who have been here. So, it is 4.5 points out of five possible. This suggests that the vast majority of guests choosing this hotel were not disappointed in their decision and did not regret the money spent.



Rooms

As mentioned above, the hotel has a total of 45 apartments. They are represented by the following categories: superior and deluxe, and are designed for two or three guests. According to our compatriots, the rooms have everything you may need: a modern TV, a refrigerator with a minibar (you can use its contents for an additional fee), air conditioning, a hairdryer, an electric kettle, a set of dishes for tea drinking, a bathroom with a set of personal facilities hygiene (soap, shampoo, shower gel, toothpastes and brushes, etc.). Please note that balconies are not available in all apartments. However, travelers say that even if your room is equipped with a balcony, you will not be able to sit on it. The fact is that its width will be only about 25 centimeters. So you can only stand on it. However, this did not bother the guests - they used balconies to dry wet things.





Cleaning service

In general, judging by the reviews of tourists, there were no special complaints about the work of the maids of the Lavender Nha Trang Hotel 3 * hotel. According to the guests, the rooms are cleaned every day. True, it can not be called very thorough, but in the rooms and the bathroom was always clean. Towels are also updated every day. In addition, as expenditures, the maids will replenish their personal hygiene products and bring to your room drinking water (per bottle per person). Bed linen at the hotel is changed twice a week.

As for the rest of the hotel staff, our compatriots did not express any complaints about them. True, some guests note that no one speaks Russian here. In addition, the staff is not particularly knowledgeable and English. However, this situation is typical for many hotels in Vietnam.

Nutrition

The price for this hotel includes breakfast only. They run from 6:30 a.m. to 10 a.m. in the hotel restaurant. In general, guests considered them standard. So, in the morning you can eat eggs cooked in different variations, soup, meat or chicken dishes, pastries, vegetables, fruits, cereals. During the day, meals can also be enjoyed in the hotel restaurant. It works until 10 pm.





Sea

As already mentioned, the great advantage of Lavender Nha Trang Hotel 3 * is its proximity to the beach. You can reach it in just a couple of minutes. The beach here is municipal and, according to tourists, very clean and well maintained. For an additional fee, you can rent a sunbed and sun umbrella. Or you can sit right on the sand, laying a mat or towel. A characteristic feature here is that waves are very frequent at sea. Therefore, in most cases, tourists manage to swim only in the morning. However, you can always compensate for this by going to a nearby park with lots of pools and water slides. So, our compatriots staying at this hotel did not have any complaints about beach vacations.
