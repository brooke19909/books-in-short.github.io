Life-size patterns for Baby Bon: Description, Patterns and Recommendations

The best toy for a little girl is a baby doll. It helps to educate the correct behavior model in the younger generation. Do you need a lot of the same type of dolls? They only take up space and leave little room for imagination. The best solution is one doll, but with a lot of accessories and clothes. A wardrobe for a daughter's baby is quite within the power of creating a mom. Life-size patterns for Baby Bon will help her with this.

Panties

Doll clothes are an important part of the game. The most popular baby dolls are Baby Bon and Baby Anabel. Both dolls are interactive, but their set of functions is different. For sewing clothes, dimensions are much more important. Baby Bon is 43 cm tall, Anabel is 3 cm taller. Despite the small difference in height, the patterns of clothes for Baby Bon and Anabel do not differ, and the clothes fit both dolls.

There is another characteristic important for sewing. Baby Bon has a plastic body, so any clothes will do. Anabel has a soft, stuffed torso, so body and simple panties are not suitable for her. T-shirts on Anabel also do not look very good, therefore, under sundresses or overalls with ties, you should definitely consider a blouse or a T-shirt.

Pattern panties for dolls such as Baby Born is one of the simplest. We cut out 4 identical parts and perform all the seams. We insert an elastic band at the top, you can insert them on the bottom, in each leg.





The basic model can be improved a bit. The patterns for the front part should be extended by 1.5 cm. Due to this, we make a small fold in the knee bend area. Such pants will look natural and stylish. To turn regular trousers into trousers, sew pockets and sew an additional line on the side to create the illusion of a dense fabric.

Overalls

The most popular clothes for the baby are jumpsuits, and, of course, the baby doll can‚Äôt do without him either. On the pattern there is no allowance for seams, therefore, after transferring to the fabric, you need to add 1 cm on each side at the place where the fastener will be fastened - 15 mm.

To translate the patterns for Baby Bon in full size, you need to take measurements from the doll and enlarge the picture on the screen to the desired value. If you plan to sew a warm jumpsuit, from a very thick or quilted material, the pattern needs to be made a little more, taking into account the thickness of the material.





First we do all the seams, the last step is sewing on the fastener. It can be anything - lightning, Velcro, buttons, classic buttons. Additionally, you can decorate the product with embroidery, applique, various stripes and ribbons.

Baby's undershirt

The undershirt is one of the simplest elements in the wardrobe. It is enough to make side seams and process the edges, and the product will be ready. Despite its simplicity, there are many options: a short sleeve, a long, closing fingers.





The pattern of the baby's undershirt is easy to adapt and sew a blouse, t-shirt. You can use any additional elements and decorations. If you expand the sleeves to the bottom and insert the elastic, you get an elegant blouse.

Costume for the girl

If the boy only suits pants and blouses, then the wardrobe of the doll-girl will be much more diverse. Take, for example, a pretty suit from a sundress and a pantalon. Life-size patterns for Baby Bon will help him sew.





Knickers are sewn in much the same way as pants, only shorter and wider. Extra width allows you to make beautiful builds. The elastic should be sewn 12 mm from the edge to get beautiful ruffles. Panties look even nicer with lace around the edge.

To sew a sundress, we initially perform side seams. The bottom and armholes are turned with a slanting inlay. The necklace from the neck is not trimmed, but stitched further, turning into ties. Not everyone can make a slanting collar on their own, this work is painstaking and delicate, so it‚Äôs easier to buy a ready-made version. The suit can be worn in this way or supplemented with a blouse under a sundress. So, the clothes for Baby Bon-girl are ready. Patterns can be supplemented and modified at your discretion.

Footwear

You can sew not only clothes, but also shoes yourself. To make patterns for Baby Bohn in full size, you can simply attach a foot to the screen and adjust the size of the pattern, taking into account the seams.

The main material can be felt, leather or leatherette. Sew the cotton lining on the upper part with the back seam. When the shoes are worn, the lining is practically invisible, but it will be easier to put on with it, and on the shelf the shoes look much more aesthetically pleasing.





We decorate the bow with embroidery, beads or applique. Fastening can serve as satin ribbons, laces, ribbons. The main seam is made directly on the front side. You can replace the ties with the creation of a one-piece strap on one side, and attach a button or hook to the other.

Creative Ideas

You can buy material for clothes or you can use old children's clothes. Saving on quality is not worth it, because girls have a lot of contact with the toy, and natural fabrics are much more pleasant. The advantages of cutting from children's clothes are that you can use ready-made pictures, the result is indistinguishable from industrial suits.

If the doll is intended for a little girl, then you should avoid small elements: a button, beads, rhinestone, sequins. Children often tear off these details, and there is a chance of swallowing. Such jewelry should not be used up to 3 years.

To sew clothes for dolls, you can use patterns for children's clothing. They need to be slightly reduced, in accordance with the standards of the baby doll, and even simplified. No need to worry about making the doll comfortable, so you can remove additional tucks and other small cut elements.

Overview of clothing patterns for Baby Bon allows you to create an extensive wardrobe. Basic models are easy to add or change a bit, and get a new cut. Girls always like to look after their dolls, and beautiful clothes will help to instill good taste and add variety to the gameplay.
