Spain, Costa Dorada. Hotel Playa de Oro Park 3 *: description and reviews of tourists

Spain is one of the most popular tourist countries in Europe. After all, there is everything you need for a quality vacation: warm waters of the sea, golden beaches, huge cities and picturesque coasts with a huge number of hotels for every taste. And the Playa de Oro Park 3 * hotel is considered quite popular today. This hotel is located almost on the coast and offers tourists decent living conditions.

Hotel Playa de Oro Park 3 *: location





People going on vacation are primarily interested in information about the chosen hotel. Hotel Playa de Oro Park 3 * (Spain) is located in the resort area of ​​Costa Daurada, very close to the seashore, in the small coastal city of Salou.

Note that the location of the hotel is its undoubted advantage. Why? To begin with, it is worth noting that the hotel complex overlooks the sea and is surrounded by forest on both sides - the nature here is really amazing. In addition, very close to the hotel there are lots of shops, bars, cafes, there is even a pharmacy and a shopping center. They can be reached in a few minutes.

Near the hotel there is a public transport stop - vacationers can easily travel around the area. For example, the tourist center of Salou is just 800 meters from the hotel. Tarragona is 20 km away. The nearest airport is 15 km away in Regus. The distance to Barcelona is a little more - 110 km.

Short hotel description





Hotel Playa de Oro Park 3 * (Costa Daurada, Salou) has a small territory of its own (compared to other, larger hotel complexes). Nevertheless, the infrastructure is carefully thought out, and the territory itself is decorated with a mass of decorative flowers, exotic plants and trees.

A large eight-story building is located in the center - this is the residential complex where the reception and guest rooms are located. The hotel has an elevator, so do not be afraid to check into the upper floors.

Description of hotel rooms





The hotel has 175 spacious, beautifully furnished rooms. Here is what the Playa de Oro Park 3 * hotel can offer residents:

  • Standard room. These rooms are 18 square meters. It has a spacious double bedroom, as well as a room with a bathtub, shower, washbasin and large wall mirror. The room also has a toilet for individual use. This room can accommodate two adults and two children with an extra bed.
  • There are also triple and quadruple standard rooms.
  • The suit rooms consist of a small living room and bedroom, as well as a bathroom.
  • There are also seven rooms specially equipped for disabled guests.

It should be noted that regardless of the room category, each is furnished with necessary furniture and household appliances. It has air conditioning with individual settings, as well as a TV for watching satellite channels and a telephone. There is a safe, but you have to pay for use.

Rooms are cleaned daily. Clean towels await you every day in the bathroom. Bed linen is changed every three days. Incidentally, some rooms have balconies. Rooms overlooking the sea are a bit more expensive.

Power scheme





There is a decent restaurant on site, where guests are treated to delicious dishes of local international cuisine. Tourists can choose their own food system. For example, you can only pay for breakfast and dinner. Or prefer three meals a day. In addition, there is an all-inclusive scheme. That is, guests have the right not only to three meals a day, but also to light snacks during the day and local drinks. The restaurant serves a buffet, where each visitor can choose their favorite dishes and drinks. Almost daily on the menu there are various seafood dishes. By the way, if you leave for an excursion in the morning, then the hotel staff can pack you breakfast with you.

In addition, very close to the hotel building there are many shops and small cafes where you can decently eat or buy the necessary products.

Does the hotel have its own beach?





Very close to the Playa de Oro Park 3 * hotel (Costa Dorada) there is a small secluded beach where guests can relax. The advantage of this place, of course, is the isolation and absence of a large number of vacationers. But here it is not too convenient, since the descent into the sea is steep, rocky.

That is why guests prefer to go down to the public beach, which is completely covered with golden soft sand. A convenient staircase leads to it. You can relax and swim here for free, but you will have to fork out for umbrellas, sunbeds and towels. By the way, on the beach there are changing cabins and showers, which is very convenient.

As on any other coast, tourists are offered a lot of ways to have fun. Starting from beach volleyball and catamarans and ending with a boat trip or water skiing.

Conditions for staying with children





Hotel Playa de Oro Park 3 * (Salou) provides decent conditions for guests who come to relax with their children. Even the smallest tourists here will be comfortable and fun.

For starters, it's worth talking about the comfort of the rooms. For example, parents with a small child will definitely be offered to put a special crib in the room, which can be used for free. Additional services are offered by the restaurant. Here you will certainly be offered a special children's menu. Tasty, exotic, but simple and extremely healthy dishes will appeal to even the most fastidious child. And the kids are provided with special highchairs.

Older children, of course, will be able to have fun with might and main. For example, there is a special shallow pool where children can have a great time under the supervision of hotel staff. There is also a games room with colorful toys that will also appeal to children. In addition, there is a kids club on the territory of the hotel, where experienced teachers work with the guys. And during the day a group of talented animators works here, who will help kids get to know each other, organize fun children's games and playful competitions.

Infrastructure and additional services





Of course, the Playa de Oro Park 3 * hotel has a developed infrastructure and offers its guests a lot of services. For example, Wi-Fi is free in the lobby.

In addition, the hotel has its own small parking lot, which is convenient for tourists traveling on their own or rented vehicles. There is also an Internet cafe, for the services of which you have to pay. On the territory of the Playa de Oro Park 3 * hotel there is a medical office where a qualified doctor will provide you with assistance for a small amount. There is a bar in the lobby. There is a luggage room, as well as a currency exchange.

Entertainment and sports at the hotel

What else can the tourist offer Playa de Oro Park 3 * hotel? Spain is a country with a rich history, culture and traditions. Therefore, interesting excursions organized by hotel workers will appeal to everyone.

In addition, you can have a great time on the territory of the hotel itself, because there are all conditions for fun and relaxation. As for the free services, there is a large freshwater pool - sunbeds and umbrellas can be used for free. By the way, various entertainment events and games are regularly organized in the pool. Also free of charge you can go to aerobics, gymnastics or yoga classes, play darts and table tennis. Every evening, local animators organize lively shows, helping guests relax and have fun.

As for the paid services, the hotel has relaxing massage rooms, a bicycle rental service, and a room with slot machines.

Hotel Playa de Oro Park 3 *: tourist reviews

In fact, most of the reviews left by hotel guests are positive. Tourists primarily note the spacious and really clean rooms, where it is convenient to relax after an eventful day. The local restaurant is mentioned separately - there is always a huge selection of dishes, including seafood, various types of meat, as well as fresh vegetables and fruits. Guests thank the staff: all the staff here are really polite, helpful and always ready to help. In addition, most of the employees to one degree or another speak Russian, so there will be no problems with communication. The atmosphere here is pleasant, relaxing, in a word, this place is perfect for a beach holiday.
