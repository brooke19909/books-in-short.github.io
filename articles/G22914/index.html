Pioneer Lake in Barnaul: how to get, reviews

Barnaul is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, having diverse sights. Its main advantage is its magnificent nature. Transparent reservoirs, high mountains, mysterious caves, immense forests, mineral healing springs. Being in these places not only contributes to better health, but also cheers you up. Camping is the most suitable place for relaxation and fun with your family. Regardless of where the person goes - to the forest, mountains, to the lake. Relaxing in the fresh air is always beneficial.

General description. Where is the pioneer lake

Pioneer Lake in Barnaul is called a reservoir, which is located near the city. It is famous for its miraculous qualities, which are due to the healing mud and highly mineralized water that are in it. This advantage is used to prevent various diseases. Previously, a pioneer camp was located on its shore (hence the name of the lake), now there is a children's health resort here.

The population of the coastal part of the reservoir is quite high. On the shore of Lake Pioneer in Barnaul, houses, entertainment centers, recreation centers are under construction, within which are created areas for playing volleyball, basketball, tennis. Also, everyone can rent skis, a boat or a catamaran. Sometimes on this lake in the autumn or winter season, you can see people who want to strengthen their health with the help of hardening.

How to get there

Getting to Pioneer Lake in Barnaul is easy. This can be done both by personal transport and by public transport. Get to the stop "Ozernaya" by bus 25 and 38 of the route or by taxi. The terrain from the road is clearly visible, so it is easy to see where Pionerskoye Lake is in Barnaul. Everyone has the right to come here and have a great time. Moreover, this place is suitable not only for family vacations, but also for tourism.

Reviews

Reviews of Pioneer Lake in Barnaul are very diverse. Of course, it is impossible not to pay attention to the beautiful nature. Most people consider this one of the best places to stay. It is very convenient to drive up to the shore, there are tables and benches, and there is sand at the bottom of the reservoir.

Pioneer Lake in Barnaul is a great place where all conditions are created so that not only adults but also children can have a great time. There is fish in the pond, so you can take a fishing rod if you wish. The lake itself is covered by small hills, which is very beautiful.

Some, unfortunately, leave a lot of garbage after a rest, even not considering it necessary to remove it from the table, get into the car and drive home. As for the children, they do not pay attention to the mess: they enjoy swimming, sunbathing. Many noted that being here is not only pleasant, but also beneficial, because clean air improves health. Therefore, many enjoy taking walks, relaxing by the water. By the way, near the lake there is a shop and a kiosk where you can buy food.

Thus, Pioneer Lake in the city of Barnaul is an excellent option for a free vacation. It is only necessary to respect nature and keep clean.
