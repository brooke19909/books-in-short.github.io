Hotel Sunshine Park 3 * (Lloret de Mar / Spain): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Without a doubt, the Costa Brava is one of the most picturesque places in Spain. The resort is located on the northern coast of Catalonia, stretching from the border of France to the city of Blanes. Thousands of tourists come here every year to enjoy their holidays on unusually beautiful beaches.

As one of the largest, most famous and most visited resorts of the coast of Spain , Lloret de Mar is known, in the center of which there are hotels with pools, guest houses, apartments. One of the most popular accommodation options is Sunshine Park 3 * (Lloret de Mar).





About the resort

Tourists who have chosen the hotel Sunshine Park (Lloret de Mar) as their vacation destination are pleased to respond to the resort. On the busy coast of the Costa Brava, vacationers share, with the resort of Lloret de Mar at the foot of the mountains, luxurious beaches and entertainment for all ages. On the street Sant Pere has a large number of stores in which anyone can buy products of international brands, exclusive jewelry stores. The city is known for its vibrant nightlife: it has many bars, restaurants, as well as one of the largest casinos on the coast.

Sightseeing lovers will no doubt be interested to know that the Verdager cultural center has artifacts from Pog de Castellet (an Iberian settlement of 250 century BC). For lovers of outdoor activities, Water World, one of the largest water parks in Europe, is offered.

At sea, vacationers can do kayaking, diving, snorkeling. The resort also has go-karting and several golf courses.

In the city center are apartments, guest houses, hotels with pools, among which the hotel Sunshine Park 3 * (Lloret de Mar), which occupies one of the first positions in the popularity rating.

From the hills of the resort town offers a magnificent view of the beaches - Lloret and Fenals. Climbing the cliffs, you can enjoy views of the spa town and the Mediterranean Sea.

Lloret de Mar is separated from Girona Airport by a half hour drive.

Sunshine Park 3 * (Lloret de Mar, Costa Brava, Spain): Acquaintance
