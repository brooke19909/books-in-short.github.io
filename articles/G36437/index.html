How to make flowers in a box with your own hands?

To surprise you, you can create flowers in a box with your own hands for a holiday, which will present a good mood and will be remembered for a long time. Such a gift is easy to make, the main thing is to choose the right materials. Use in the manufacture of fresh flowers.



Necessary materials

First of all, pick up flowers and plants. It can be roses and gerberas, field daisies, spruce or pine branches. Fruits and vegetables will help to complete the composition.

For registration you will need a box that you can make yourself or purchase ready-made in the store for floristry. Its size depends on the number of plants and additional decorative elements. The box should be made of thick cardboard. To strengthen the bottom, florists often use wooden stands.

For handmade fresh flowers in a box to please you with aroma and beauty for a long time, you will need a floral sponge. It retains moisture and prolongs the life of plants.

You may also need: packaging film, greens, artificial decor, ribbons and beads. Rhinestones and sequins are used to give shine.

Romantic gift

For a birthday or Valentine's Day, you can please your beloved woman with a handmade bouquet of flowers in a box. Such an unusual gift will leave fond memories.



The first thing you need to charge a good mood and a bit of patience. And then prepare the necessary materials for creativity:

  • A box in the form of a heart or round.
  • Floral green sponge.
  • 15 roses (peonies can be used).
  • Satin ribbon.
  • At will greens and small flowers.

Manufacturing process:

  1. Roses must be processed. Remove thorns and leaves. Use a pruner to shorten them to the level of the box.
  2. Prepare a floral sponge. To do this, it is cut into the shape of a box and put inside. The bottom must be lined with polyethylene or cling film.
  3. The sponge is slightly moistened with water and roses are inserted into it.
  4. The space between the buds is complemented by greenery and small flowers.
  5. Close the box with a lid and bandage with a satin ribbon.

Sweet bouquet

In the world of floristry, classic bouquets of flowers are still relevant. But sometimes you want to surprise a person with an unusual gift. Making a box of flowers and sweets with your own hands is quite simple.

Necessary materials:

  • The white box is small.
  • Flowers: roses, daisies, gerberas.
  • Floral sponge of a green shade.
  • Macaroons cakes - 5-6 pieces.

The process of making a delicious bouquet:

  1. Cakes are placed in the box on the right side. Be sure to cover the bottom with parchment.
  2. Flowers are cut to the required length.
  3. The floral sponge is given the form necessary to put it in a box next to the cakes. Wet it with water.
  4. Gently distribute flowers so that there are no gaps between them.

The resulting box is decorated with a satin ribbon and a small name card is applied. We must not forget that the shelf life of such a presentation is short-lived, since sweet cakes are used.

DIY handmade flower box for flowers

Original and exquisite bouquets are very popular. Hats were previously stored in hat boxes, and now it has become an attribute of floristry. She gives the gift a special charm and aristocracy.



Necessary materials for manufacturing:

  • Large and small flowers in a single color scheme.
  • Hat box of a gentle shade.
  • Wide satin ribbon.
  • Green floral sponge.
  • Plastic bag or cling film.
  • Scissors.

How to make a hat box for flowers with your own hands?

We offer you to read the instructions:

  1. Creating a composition begins with a choice of colors. It can be roses, peonies, alstroemeria and others.
  2. The floral sponge is cut so that it fits into the box. Fill it with water.
  3. At the bottom of the box is lined with polyethylene or cling film. This is necessary so that the water from the sponge does not wet the cardboard.
  4. Flowers begin to be inserted into the resulting oasis so that they fill the entire space. They begin to form a composition from the center, gradually filling up empty seats in a circle.
  5. The hat box, after it is ready, is tied with a wide satin ribbon.

It is important to remember that to create a composition of flowers, you must choose a container made of thick cardboard. To keep the buds longer, a special agent is added to the water.

Little surprise

Do-it-yourself flowers in small boxes will be a memorable present. In the composition you can put the main gift - a ring or a brooch.



It is necessary to prepare floral materials:

  • A small square box with a lid.
  • Large rose - 3-5 pieces.
  • Floral sponge.
  • Cling film.
  • Scissors.

How to make flowers in a box with your own hands?

  1. At the bottom of the box is lined with cling film.
  2. The floral sponge is cut out in the shape of a container and saturated with water.
  3. The flowers are cut off the stem so that they fit the height of the box. This is necessary to close the lid.
  4. The sponge is inserted into the box and decorated with flowers.

If desired, the box is decorated with a ribbon. Closing the lid, you can complement the composition with a flower bud.

Florist Tips

The main component of the composition is the flowers. When choosing them, follow the rules:

  • The buds should be half-open, dense. This indicates their freshness.
  • The stem of the flower should also be dense, without plaque and damage. Leaves are not limp and resilient.
  • A rotten slice of a plant suggests that it has long been standing in water.
  • The color scheme is selected depending on who the bouquet will be presented to. Young girls choose pink shades, women - red, burgundy. For men, dark colors of plants.

Matching the box is just as important as choosing the flower. The cardboard from which it is made must be dense. It is better to choose a box in tone to a flower oasis or lighter. This will help emphasize the beauty and charm of the composition.

Do-it-yourself flowers in a box will be a memorable gift. Such compositions have replaced the classic bouquets. Hat boxes for flowers are a fashion trend in floristry. They have an unusual and original design.
