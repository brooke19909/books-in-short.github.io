How to make an automatic machine out of paper? Practical tips

The more you are fond of creating paper crafts, the more you are captivated and carried away by this ancient occupation. There are objects that can be easily and fairly quickly made thanks to the art of origami, and this does not require a huge effort from you. But there are such things, the manufacture of which will require not only a lot of time, but also a lot of patience and effort. However, no matter how difficult the process of making this or that paper craft seems to you, origami will ultimately give you an unforgettable feeling of joy that cannot be compared with anything. Do you know, for example, how to make an automatic machine out of paper?



To begin with, we need A4 paper for crafts, a sheet of which we will place the long side to ourselves, and then roll up, but not too narrow. To achieve the most even surface, you can use a rolling pin, which must be put on paper after you fold the sheet. Later it will be possible to remove it and seal the edges and the middle of the sheet with tape.

Now we need another sheet of paper of the same size. To do this, put the first sheet on it and again roll it into a tube, securing it with tape. As a result, we have two finished rolls of paper, one of which should easily fit into the other. They are nested within a few centimeters, after which they are fixed. In general, everything is quite simple, even if you did not know before about how to make an automatic machine out of paper.



These steps must be repeated so that we have two identical long rolls that will form the basis of the barrel of our machine. Putting them side by side and placing the edges at the same distance, we fix them with tape, and then cut a piece of 7-12 cm in one of the rolls. This is necessary to separate the machine into the barrel and piston.



Next, we have to make a store and a butt, for which we will act according to the previous scheme, folding A4 sheet from the long side and fixing its ends with tape. The resulting roll must be flattened by laying on the table, since we need to get square, not rounded, elements. A quarter of the part obtained is cut off, after which a scissor cuts obliquely from either side to allow the part to be attached to the barrel. Attaching it to the base, we get a butt. With the store, which we will make of a longer part, you need to do the same procedure, then securing it then in the middle of the machine using adhesive tape.

The manufacture of our machine is completed. Now you know how to make a paper machine. In general, its manufacture does not require much time. To achieve a more natural appearance, it is best to use yellow, brown, or black sheets of paper. After completion, you can colorize it with markers. If you consider yourself an experienced origami lover and have enough free time, along with perseverance and patience, then you can refer to special literature and learn more about how to make an automatic machine out of paper.
