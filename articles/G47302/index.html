Rest in the hotel "Solnechnaya" (Ryazan): prices, services, guest reviews

If you plan to go on a business trip, business trip or just to get acquainted with the many attractions of the city of Ryazan, then you will definitely choose a place for housing.

This review offers you an acquaintance with one of the most famous inexpensive hotels in Ryazan - Solnechnaya. Photos of the accommodation with this warm name, coordinates and a brief description are given below in this article.

For all guests of Ryazan, this information will certainly become useful and necessary, since many, once in a foreign city, often ask themselves the question of choosing a good place to sleep.

What is a property

Judging by the general description, the Solnechnaya hotel in Ryazan is a budget option for an overnight stay. Here guests are offered rooms of various categories for a very reasonable price. Among the rooms there are ordinary single and double rooms, Standard rooms, triple rooms and suites. The total number of rooms, according to many sources, is 33. The building was built in 1986.

All rooms are comfortable, cozy, clean, have a warm atmosphere. There are rooms with private facilities both on the floor and inside. The interior design is dominated by pleasant colors and calm tones. Inside the apartment there is the necessary furniture, a fridge, a kettle, a TV, comfortable beds.

The hotel has a cozy cafe where meals are organized for guests. Daily experienced chefs prepare delicious national dishes for reasonable prices for guests.

Featured Services

The Solnechnaya Hotel (Ryazan) offers its guests a standard set of services, which include the following:

  • First of all, it is worth noting that near the hotel building there is free ample parking, which is very convenient for people who come here in their car.
  • Another important service is the provision of free Internet with a fairly good signal throughout the hotel.
  • For business people who have no time to look for suitable places for food in the city, the hotel has a cafe where you can have a hearty breakfast.
  • Rooms are cleaned daily.
  • Reception is open 24 hours a day.

The hotel provides guests with quality service, everyone can call a taxi, give a first-aid kit for use, provide a hairdryer, iron, ironing board, wake up at the right time. The shared kitchen has a microwave, cutlery, and dishes. In addition, pets are not prohibited here.

Among the services that are provided in the "Solar" for an additional fee, laundry and dry cleaning.

Indicative prices

The hotel offers guests to stay in both budget and more expensive rooms. The cost of living for one or two people in ordinary rooms at the Solnechnaya Hotel (Ryazan) starts at 800 rubles per person. A day in a standard room costs 1,500 rubles. When placing in a room of category "Suite" you will have to pay 2 100 rubles.

The hotel also offers you to order hearty breakfasts at a price of only 100 rubles per person.

When checking in, the time of arrival and departure from the hotel should be taken into account, the check-out time at the hotel starts from noon.

Reviews for the hotel "Solar" (Ryazan)

Among the reviews about this place you can find both positive comments and not very flattering.

If we talk about the advantages of the organization, first of all, customers evaluate its moderate prices and excellent location. Also, many note silence, comfort and the ability to sleep, comfortable beds. Here, the ideal ratio of price and quality, a great option for business trips. As for the hotel staff, judging by the reviews, all employees are polite and attentive, trying to quickly solve all problems that have arisen and answer questions.

Among the minuses, guests pay attention to old furniture, shabby bedding, odors in the room. Also, many do not like the fact that the shower in individual rooms is located on the floor. According to tourists, for the money one could find a more comfortable place to stay.

One nuance is also worth noting: judging by the reviews, there is no cafe in the hotel. Although the descriptions of the hotel on many Internet resources include a phrase about the availability of food and a dining room. Therefore, customers who plan to eat without leaving the hotel should clarify this detail when booking a room.

Conclusion

Summing up, we can say that the Solnechnaya mini-hotel in Ryazan is an ordinary middle-class hotel, where you can only stay for the night. It is aimed at unpretentious tourists with an average income, who do not like to look for cons, and are used to living positively.
