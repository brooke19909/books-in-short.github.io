Lamps for giving solar-powered: overview, characteristics

Solar-powered luminaires are widely used in landscape design. They issue cottages, cottages and house territories. The illuminated walkway or part of the landscape highlighted by street garden lights looks very beautiful.

Today's market offers an impressive assortment of stand-alone solar powered lighting devices. Getting confused in all this diversity is quite easy, especially for inexperienced consumers.

We will try to understand this issue and designate the most popular solar lamps for summer cottages. We also consider the main characteristics of the models and critical points that you must definitely pay attention to when buying such devices.

Difficulty choosing

Almost all solar-powered cottage lamps can be classified according to four criteria: material of manufacture, type of battery, type of silicon, and installation method. These parameters must be taken into account before purchase.

Material

The length of the operating period depends on the material, as well as the stability of the solar lamp for the garden to climatic changes and some physical influences. Devices can be made of ordinary plastic, wood materials, steel, alloys, as well as bronze.

To increase the protective characteristics, a layer of some chemical elements can be additionally applied, which helps protect wood from rotting, metal from rust, etc. Plafonds of solar luminaires for summer cottages are made of ordinary or tempered glass. The latter option will cost much more, as well as the use of noble alloys in the case itself.

Battery

Solar lamps for summer cottages are equipped with either nickel-cadmium batteries or nickel-metal hydride. The latter have a longer operational period, they are noticeably more expensive and together, but much more practical than the former. The power of rechargeable batteries in most luminaires ranges from 600-700 mAh.

Kind of silicon

In total, there are three main varieties: polycrystalline, multicrystalline and monocrystalline. The first is used in budget products, the second in mid-budget products, and the third in premium devices.

When choosing, it is necessary to take into account the specifics of using a solar lamp for a summer residence. If the device will be actively used all year round, it is better to focus on single and multicrystalline options. And for seasonal decoration, you can take inexpensive polycrystalline devices.
