Several ways to get from Stavropol to Krasnodar

Krasnodar is a city that is called little Paris. It preserved some special culture of kindness, courtesy and understanding. In Stavropol, a huge number of ancient architectural monuments that simply can not help but admire their grace. Needless to say, why so often people are interested in how to get from Krasnodar to Stavropol or make a route in the opposite direction? There may be several options for moving. It is worth choosing the most convenient of them and heading to another city in order to enjoy its beauties.



Features of the city of Krasnodar

One of the most interesting places in Krasnodar is the Moorish house. Every tourist who visits its territory will definitely hear a beautiful story about pure and bright love. The Botanical Garden deserves special attention, where you can not only study the most exotic species of plants, but also buy a live souvenir for yourself. Visitors do not deny themselves the pleasure of walking through an extreme rope park. This trip for a long time will charge the body with positive emotions. It is worth coming to this city in order to enjoy the view of unusual monuments, towers, mansions, parks and picturesque streets. Tourists are advised to taste the most juicy vegetables and fruits.



Features of the city of Stavropol

Stavropol Territory has always been considered one of the most beautiful places in the country. One can talk endlessly about its sights. The Burukshun estuaries, representing a whole system of various water bodies, are a real natural asset. In Stavropol, the largest number of temples, churches and chapels are located, which were repeatedly restored after the hostilities. Each visitor must strive to approach the "Cold Spring" and lay flowers with him in memory of the dead.

Route Features

As it turned out, Krasnodar and Stavropol are two similar cities, with identical culture, nature and architecture. Perhaps for this reason, the inhabitants of each of them make a trip to another city. The route between them, if you look at the map, represents one solid line. The total distance is approximately 299 km. This is a relatively small distance, which can be overcome with several modes of transport.

Car

The distance from Krasnodar to Stavropol does not exceed 300 km, so it will not be difficult to overcome it by personal transport. Asphalt was recently updated along the entire route and bright markings appeared, which makes the trip easy and comfortable. Between these two cities there are two settlements (the city of Ust-Labinsk and Kropotkin). If you wish, you can stop here to relax, have a bite and refuel your car. If you move from one point to another without stops, breakdowns and traffic jams, you can make a move in just 4 hours.



A train

Unfortunately, there is no direct train from Krasnodar to Stavropol. A trip on this type of transport can be made only with transfers. Most passengers prefer a flight through Tikhoretsk station. This is due to the fact that there will be the shortest period of time between the departure of one and the arrival of another train.

In general, the total travel time is about 8 hours. As a rule, in most cases, night moving is carried out. That is, at 21.00 the train leaves from the railway station of the city of Stavropol, located on the street. Vokzalnaya, d. 5, and already at 5.00 he arrives in the glorious city of Krasnodar. Tickets for it can only be bought at the box office. The approximate cost, taking into account the transplant, will be 1100 rubles.

Airplane





Another theoretically possible route from Krasnodar to Stavropol is by plane. At a distance of about 10-15 km from the center of each city is an airfield. Next to it is a taxi parking, so there will be no problems with moving to the desired point. It is worth noting that there is no direct flight either, you will have to make a transfer, for example, in Moscow, having gone there on business. The approximate cost of such a flight will be 4800 rubles, while at least 5 hours will have to be spent on the road.

Bus

If there is no personal transport, then the best way to travel from Stavropol to Krasnodar is by bus. At the bus station of each city there is a schedule, from which it follows that about 10 times a day there is a direct flight between these two points. The first departure takes place early in the morning - around 5.00. There are several more options to get from one point to another with transfers, but few people are happy with them. The ticket price is about 700 rubles per person. Total travel time does not exceed 6 hours.

Krasnodar and Stavropol are two great cities of Russia that will delight everyone. The distance between them is small, which allows you to quickly get from one point to another using any convenient vehicle. There are several more beautiful places between them, so the impression of the trip will be the best.
