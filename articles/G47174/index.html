Ski complex "Malskaya Valley", Pskov: photos and reviews of tourists

Ski complex "Malskaya Dolina" is a developed tourist center, functioning since 1998. Winter lovers, professional athletes and those who just want to enjoy a secluded vacation flock here. The main advantage of this place is its location - in a picturesque area, on the territory of the state natural museum-reserve under the poetic name Izborsk (Pechora district).





The unique landscape resources untouched by man delight with their extraordinary beauty. In the forests there are many different game and large animals. A favorite place among avid hunters is the Malskaya Dolina complex. The recreation center is located near the ancient Izborsk fortress, dating from the 14th century. The environment is conducive to pacifying, cognitive and active leisure.

Here, as nowhere else, the Russian spirit is felt, there is no bustle of the metropolis, there is no noisy highway and crowds of people. A comfortable climate allows you to spend a vacation in both summer and winter. Locals come here on weekends to combine outdoor activities with fishing. The territory of the base is fully landscaped for sports entertainment.

Things to do?





The Malskaya Dolina complex (Pskov) regularly organizes competitions between skiers and snowboarders. They can be attended by ordinary lay beginners and real professionals. There are safe tracks for skiing of different difficulty levels: basic, children’s, medium, free and cheesecake. To maintain the snow, special devices are switched on.

Modern lifts were built, one of which works in the summer - for cyclists. Tourists do not have to bring sports equipment and equipment with them - everything can be rented on site. Those who are not inspired by cross-country skiing can take rubber cheesecakes and rush along a steep hill with a breeze. Such entertainment is absolutely safe, it will give a vivid positive experience.

Very young children will also not be bored, for kids there is a small slope. Sledge with skates are for rent. Fun, joy and laughter will be the main fellow travelers for the whole weekend.





For those who wish, a school is open, where an experienced instructor will show and tell you in a short time how to properly manage a snowboard, slide on skis. Children from five years are accepted. Classes are held on the street under the strict guidance of the master.

If you feel unwell or injured, you can always contact the medical office, which operates on the territory of the Malskaya Dolina complex. It should be noted that the service in the center is constantly being improved. In terms of comfort, he can compete with almost any foreign resort. And pricing remains affordable for different social strata.

Food concept

The infrastructure of the Malskaya Dolina base (Pskov) provides not only skiing opportunities, but also a comfortable pastime. After an active day, an abundance of sports entertainment, you will undoubtedly play a brutal appetite. The management took care of this too - a cozy cafe with a panoramic view of the picturesque surroundings was built on the territory.





The menu is designed for different tastes in the Malskaya Dolina complex (Pskov). The cost of dishes pleasantly surprises - a plate (three hundred grams) of soup costs about 100-150 rubles. The most expensive dish is trout with lemon (300 rubles). Chefs prepare delicacies of Italian and Russian cuisine. Large selection of baby food: soups, chicken, juices. In the cafe you can arrange banquet events, celebrate significant events and hold romantic dates.

Where to stay for the night?

All nonresident guests can comfortably stay in the Malskaya Dolina complex (Pskov). The cost of a guest cottage ranges from 2000-4500 rubles. All communications are carried out in the houses, there is a bathroom and modern furniture. In summer, many people rent tents, such a pleasure will cost much less - 400-500 rubles. Conditions - general, on the street. Additionally, you can rent a gazebo with barbecue and benches. Such a detachment from civilization cleanses thoughts, stabilizes emotional balance and improves mood.

Entertainment Services





At the recreation center "Malskaya Dolina" there will always be classes for the soul and interests. If in winter you will find cheesecakes with skis and all-terrain vehicles, then in summer you can enjoy swimming in the lake, a boat ride, badminton, basketball, football, cycling and hiking tours. The Russian steam room with a swimming pool is open year-round .

Campaigns for mushrooms and berries are arranged. There is an opportunity to go fishing. The local lake has more than 40 species of individuals: pike, zander, roach, vendace, perch, bream and whitefish. This is a real holiday for lovers of fishing. After a rich catch, you can personally fry, bake or boil your ear from a freshly caught carcass.

Route

To get to the base with your own car is not difficult. The main thing is to remember the name of the village of Rogovo (Pechora district). We are moving out of the city of Pskov, driving straight along the Riga highway, when you see the Pechora sign - turn off. After about two kilometers, another signboard with the name of the Malskaya Dolina complex will be displayed.





Turn right, drive through the village of Maly and get to the very recreation center. The path can be set on the navigator. The road will also be prompted by local residents. If you don’t have a car or you decide to take a bus from Pskov, then the path will be a little more difficult. Get off at the Maly Village stop, and then go your way to your destination.

What do tourists say about holidays in the Pskov region?

Unforgettable moments are remembered from a vacation in this amazing place. There are only positive reviews about the base. Most of all, vacationers were pleased with the ski slopes, snow-covered slopes and stunning nature, alluring with its rich resources. Coming here, as if you find yourself in a magical world surrounded by a white fluffy veil. The peaceful atmosphere of this region immediately sets in a positive mood. I don’t want to leave from here.
