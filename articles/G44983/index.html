Many factors affect human health, and one of them is the correct air humidity. But in modern apartments, especially in the heating season, the balance is upset. Many residents try to fix this with various humidifiers. In connection with their use, a number of questions may arise, so we will consider which air humidifiers are produced. Ultrasonic humidifier

There are currently three types of humidifiers available on the market:

  1. Traditional (cold steam).
  2. Steam humidifier.
  3. Ultrasonic (can combine a cleaning function).

Ultrasonic devices are equipped with a more efficient air humidification system. The design has a piezoelectric element, which, thanks to high-frequency vibrations, turns water into tiny droplets resembling fog. The design has a replaceable filter that removes impurities and minerals in the water. Such devices include the Polaris PUH 3204 humidifier , which has recently been popular among consumers.



It is important to note that the filter cartridge, which is present in all ultrasonic devices, requires replacement over time, and its cost is very high. Significantly extend its service can be, if you use already purified or distilled water.

Humidifiers with a cleaning system or a system of "washing" air from the environment remove tobacco smoke, dust and other impurities harmful to the lungs.

Household humidifiers: the benefits and harms of appliances

Each individual type of humidifier has its own harmful effect. If we talk about ultrasonic devices, their negative effect is associated with the release of harmful substances along with moisture. Often the filter does not cope with the task and may not completely clear the liquid from salts and other substances. But this is easily avoided by adding distilled water to the device.

It is also necessary to control the humidity level of the room, since excess sputum is also harmful. For control in devices there is a hygrometer. But some models are not equipped with it, for example, a Polaris PUH 3204 air humidifier, so you need to additionally purchase a device for measuring humidity.



Using this device in your apartment, you can avoid many diseases, such as otitis media, bronchitis, sinusitis and similar ailments. The likelihood of a baby developing asthma and rhinitis will also be minimized. In addition, sufficient moisture is well reflected in the human skin condition, and fatigue will occur less frequently. The right climate will preserve wooden furniture and improve the condition of plants.
