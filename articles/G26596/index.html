Hotel Pafiana Heights Luxury Resort Spa 4 (Paphos, Cyprus): location, description and reviews of tourists

Paphos is a great place for tourists who love to combine beach and sightseeing holidays. Naturally, in the city there are lots of hotels of various categories, so everyone can choose something suitable for themselves. And the Pafiana Heights Luxury Resort Spa 4 * hotel complex is considered a pretty good place . What is a hotel? What accommodation conditions does it offer? Can I take children with me? This information will be useful to many travelers.



Short description and location of the hotel

The large hotel complex Pafiana Heights Luxury Resort Spa 4 * is located in the legendary Paphos. It is located on a hill, so from anywhere in the hotel offers a wonderful panorama of the city and the sea coast.

The hotel consists of several small buildings, as well as separate cozy villas. The beach is about 4 km away and the city center is 5 km away. But you will not have problems with transportation, as it is easy to rent a car here, and the public transport stop is only a few minutes away.

Hotel complex Pafiana Heights Luxury Resort Spa 4 * (Cyprus): description and photos of rooms

The hotel complex offers its customers rooms of the following categories:

  • Studio Suite Apartment, designed for two people.
  • One Bedroom Apartment Suite (there is a living room, kitchen, bedroom with a lockable door), suitable for four travelers at the same time.
  • Two Bedroom Apartment Suite (for six people).
  • The spacious Three Bedroom Pristige Apartment is perfect for a large family or company, as it allows you to comfortably accommodate up to 8 guests at a time.




Regardless of the category, all rooms are cozy, spacious and fully equipped with the necessary equipment. In the summer, air conditioning saves you from the heat, and in the cold season a central heating system works. In addition, there is a safe, a TV with a disc player, as well as an iron, ironing board and telephone with the ability to make international calls.

All rooms have a fully equipped kitchen with the necessary utensils, stove, refrigerator and other accessories - here you can freely cook dinners on your own. The bathroom is also quite spacious. Towels and hygiene products are replaced every day, during the scheduled cleaning of the rooms.

Meals for guests

The hotel complex Pafiana Heights Luxury Resort Spa 4 * offers guests different food schemes. In addition, in a local restaurant you can always have lunch or dinner for a relatively small fee, even if you did not pay for this option when buying a tour. Of course, you can cook yourself, as the apartments, as already mentioned, have a fully equipped kitchen with everything you need.

Near the hotel there are many catering establishments, ranging from small cozy cafes to luxurious exotic restaurants. And, of course, you can buy products in markets or in supermarkets.



Beach and water recreation

It is worth saying right away that the Pafiana Heights Luxury Resort Spa 4 * hotel is located a bit far from the sea shore - the distance to the beach is about 4 kilometers. A walk takes 40 minutes, and can be reached by public transport in 10. In addition, a bus leaves the hotel twice a day and quickly brings guests to the shore.

The beach here is public, but quite secluded. The entrance to the sea is convenient, the beach is covered with soft sand. There are sun loungers and deck chairs, which can be used for a very affordable fee. Here you can also play volleyball, ride boats, boats, catamarans. The coast is a great place for windsurfing.

Additional services for guests

You are unlikely to be bored at this hotel. If you do not want to go to the beach, you can swim in one of the two outdoor pools. In cold weather, tourists use the heated indoor tank. There is also a sauna, jacuzzi, massage rooms. If desired, guests can visit the gym, billiard room, hairdresser or have fun on sports fields and tennis courts.





For convenience, the hotel has its own laundry, dry cleaning, car rental and currency exchange. There is also parking. Customers are given the opportunity to use the Internet for free. Hotel staff also organizes conferences, weddings and parties.

Hotel Pafiana Heights Luxury Resort Spa 4 * (Paphos): traveler reviews

Tourists speak well of this hotel complex. Many travelers note the breathtaking beauty of nature and excellent views of the city, which opens from the hill. Naturally, the remoteness of the beach can be considered some inconvenience, but, on the other hand, you can always use the bus or rent a car (by the way, many tourists do this).

The rooms here are spacious and clean. A fully equipped kitchenette eliminates all food problems, especially if you don't like local delicacies. The hotel staff is polite, friendly and fluent in English and Russian. In addition, it is one of the most budget four-star hotels, which is also important.



The hotel is perfect for tourists who choose Cyprus, Paphos as their destination . The photos presented to your attention demonstrate all the charms and amenities of this resort.
