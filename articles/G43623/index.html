Where to go in St. Petersburg: choose a place

One of the most beloved tourist destinations in Russia is excursions to the "cultural capital" of our homeland, St. Petersburg. This city is famous for its sights, well courtyards, wet weather, culture and white nights. Peter is a very diverse city, everyone will find in it something interesting for himself. It remains only to plan the route, where to go in St. Petersburg, to spend time with benefit and interest.



To decide which places are worth visiting in the northern capital, clearly tell yourself: what exactly will you be interested in? For example, if you are interested in architecture, ancient monuments, history, then the choice of places to go to St. Petersburg will definitely include the Hermitage, the Yusupovs house, the Admiralty building, the Pillar of Alexandria and museums, which are very numerous in St. Petersburg. Of course, such a famous place as the Pushkin Museum-Apartment should be included in this list.

For lovers of not only history, but also religious buildings, the first place you should definitely go to St. Petersburg is the State Museum of the History of Religions. From there, you should register a visit to St. Isaac's Cathedral in the route, listen to the service in the Kazan Cathedral, get a free tour of the Savior on Spilled Blood, and also visit the world's northernmost Buddhist temple, which is located near the metro station "Old Village".

Fans of the revolutionary and military history of this city will also find where to go in St. Petersburg. Visit the famous Winter Palace, go on an excursion to the cruiser "Aurora" and look at the Artillery Museum.



And of course, Peter is a paradise for fans of cultural activities. Book tickets to the Alexandrinsky Theater, Maly Theater. For fans of jazz culture, we can recommend the GFC jazz cafe - until 22:00, the best musicians of the city play improvisations there. And those who are a supporter of contemporary art, will appreciate the loft project "Floors" on Ligovka - the mainstay of contemporary art in St. Petersburg.
