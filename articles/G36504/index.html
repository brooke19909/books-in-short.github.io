What is fractal analysis?

Fractal analysis is a kind of artistic perception of an object within the boundaries of identical geometric shapes.

For example, the hostess bakes a pie in the oven on a rectangular baking sheet. The finished product cuts into identical rectangles, reminiscent of the shape of the whole product. The portioned pieces have the same color, taste and composition as the whole pie. A piece of cake and a whole cake are identical in everything. A cut pie can be called a fractal. Because the whole consists of pieces similar to itself.



Entity entity

The principle of similarity applies to exchange trading. Prices of market instruments are viewed on long and short time frames and find striking similarities in the behavior of the chart for minutes and the other day, for example.

The theory of fractal analysis sounded in the last quarter of the twentieth century. A new word was introduced into the everyday life of market science by the mathematician Benoit Mandelbrot. The researcher replaced the word "recursion" with a sore mouth with Latin "fractal". The American and French scientist gave the financial world a way to study by sequentially dividing the observation intervals and finding symmetry on the graphs of long and short price movements.

Understand geometry and win

A fractal as an indicator shows the minimum and maximum price on an odd number of periods of the selected time interval. The rational number of periods that have taken root in practice is five.



For a combination of 5 candles, a fractal is displayed if a symmetrical figure is fixed:

  • for the green ‚Äúup‚Äù fractal, the extreme two candles to the left and to the right of the central one display prices below the maximum;
  • for the red fractal ‚Äúdown‚Äù, the left and right pair of candles should show above the minimum.

The number of candles in combination can be increased. Obviously, for 9 candles, groups of candles on the left and right consist of four periods.

When superimposed on a chart, fractal prices are indicated by a short arrow. The "up" fractal shows the price increase to a maximum for a selected period of time, and the "down" fractal displays a minimum at the same time interval.

The practice of trading systematized fractals into:

  • standard, when the middle candle indicates the achievement of one extreme;
  • non-standard, when the average candle reflects both extremes.

The appearance of a non-standard candle signals a conflict of interests between sellers and buyers. The market at this moment should choose one of the directions of movement.

Fractal Analysis Methods

To describe the price evolutions of the exchange asset and the subsequent forecast of the price course over any time periods, fractal mathematics based on the R / S analysis algorithm is used.

The dimensionless ratio of the magnitude R of the observed parameter to the standard observation delta S is the basis of the normalized analysis method.

You can evaluate the strength of a trend on an asset chart and the level of random price fluctuations by analyzing a normalized range for exceeding a value of 0.5.

The meaning of calculations and constructions is to determine the color of the market information noise. The algorithm of the method involves the calculation of the Hurst index H - the fractal characteristic of the analyzed time period - as well as the noise quality:

Advantages and disadvantages

The advantage of a fractal market analysis is its applicability to any instrument:

  • stocks and bonds;
  • goods;
  • Currencies
  • futures.


At any time interval, you can search and crush. But the accuracy of the forecast decreases with decreasing period: the prediction in the daily patterns is much more accurate than in five-minute ones.

The disadvantage is that the indicator is delayed. A fractal is formed only after the appearance of the candle following the minimum or maximum. That is, an incorrect reaction to volatility is possible. The fractal method is best suited for long periods when inevitable kickbacks are not critical for the player. Correction can be used to change your own volumes. At short intervals, you will have to use the figures in combination with other forecasting methods.
