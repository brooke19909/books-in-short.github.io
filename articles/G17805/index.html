Location and history of Georgia

Before talking about the best hotels in Georgia, you need to know the climate and the place where this state is located.

Georgia is a country located in the western part of Transcaucasia on the eastern coast of the Black Sea. In addition, it belongs to the Near East and the Middle East. By the way, Georgia is at the junction of Asia and Europe, and in some sources it is part of it. The capital of the state is the city of Tbilisi. It is located on the banks of the Kuru River, is the main cultural, historical and economic center.

It is worth noting that before Georgia was part of the USSR, after its collapse and several wars it became an independent state. The 1990s and 2000s were incredibly difficult for the country. At that time, several wars were fought, the most brutal and bloody war was with Abkhazia, after which it also became an independent state. Now Georgia is developing rapidly, economic growth is 12.4% per year. The economy is based on the food industry, metal ore mining, and the production of metal and non-metal products.

In the central and eastern part of Georgia, the climate is dry, continental, in the west - milder and wetter. In winter, the thermometer columns do not fall below +5 ... + 6 degrees, there is little rainfall, mainly in the form of rain. In summer it is quite hot, the average temperature is about +25 ... + 30 degrees.
