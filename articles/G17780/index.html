Water flows from the air conditioner: what to do?

Air conditioners are being installed everywhere, and due to their constant and intense work, frequent damage to these devices occurs. This material reveals the reasons why water flows from the air conditioner.



There are many devices that produce cold, and all of them work according to one method, which is called the Rankine reverse cycle. Among such units, one can distinguish a refrigerator, freezer, air conditioning and others. These devices may periodically become unusable. Today we will talk about an unpleasant situation when water flows from the air conditioner.



In this case, there are several common causes:

  1. Air conditioning drain is clogged. The back of such devices has openings for water drainage, and if the drain hole becomes clogged, then water flows. This is due to the fact that there is no regular cleaning or repair is in progress, which also contributes to clogging.
  2. The second reason that water flows from the air conditioner may be an insufficient amount of freon in the system. Here, most often, the air conditioner flows after turning off, as the ice begins to thaw and, overflowing the tank, spills out. In such a situation, it is necessary to fill the device with freon, which can be done by the corresponding service departments.
  3. The third reason may be an incorrect installation, in which the back is higher than the front. But vice versa, as water must flow into the back of the appliance. This can happen if people decide to install the air conditioner themselves, without having the appropriate knowledge.
  4. The fourth reason for the situation in which water flows from the air conditioner can be icing. Often, many people believe that such a malfunction in the system occurs in the cold regions of our country. However, this circumstance directly indicates problems with the cooling system. It is advisable to remove the grill during operation of the device and check for the presence of ice - if there is any, then repair is necessary.
  5. The fifth reason that water flows from the air conditioner may be clogging of the internal drains. This is because there are grooves inside the air conditioner that allow water to move from the front of the device to the back. If clogging occurs, then water will begin to collect in the front of the appliance and flow to the floor.

So, we presented to your attention the main reasons why water flows from the air conditioner. In any case, it is not recommended to open the air conditioners on their own, look for the causes of the breakdown and try to eliminate them, since such actions can lead to serious damage and significant financial costs subsequently. For such events, there are special services that employ professionals in this field. Service centers offering such services are open in all cities of Russia, and therefore finding them is not a problem.
