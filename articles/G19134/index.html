Hostels in Pushkino: list, description, price

There are not so many hostels in Pushkino, but still there is enough choice for tourists to find a room for living depending on their needs and financial capabilities. Most of these hotels have pretty pleasant living conditions, and guests feel comfortable.

Hostel "Pioneer"

The hotel is located 5 km from Pushkino, in Ivanteevka. The railway station is located nearby (5 minutes walk), so getting to the city will not be difficult. The hostel offers a variety of rooms to choose from:

  • 4-seater;
  • 6-seater;
  • 2-seater.

The rooms have wooden bunk beds or single beds. There are also small lockers for things that are locked. Includes a desk, TV and chairs.

Guests at the hostel in Pushkino can use free showers and toilets, which are equipped in a common corridor. The hotel has a lounge room. There is a large TV, upholstered furniture and shelves with books.

The hostel has a kitchen with a microwave, an electric kettle and a dining area. Vacationers can use an iron and ironing board at any time. The hotel also has an automatic washing machine.

Not far from the establishment there is a grocery store and an inexpensive cafe. The average cost of a bed in a hotel is 600 rubles.

"Town"

Directly hostels in Pushkino, Moscow Region are quite problematic to find. But in nearby settlements this is not difficult. In Ivanteyevka there is another hostel, which is distinguished by its affordable prices for accommodation. The average cost of space in a room is 500 rubles.

Here you can book a bed in a dormitory room for women and men or in a gender-divided client. The hotel also has comfortable family rooms with a double bed. They include private showers, microwaves and electric kettles.

The rooms have bunk or single beds. There are wardrobes for clothes and TV. The shared corridors are equipped with toilets and showers. There is a kitchen with the necessary appliances.

The hotel has a room where you can wash clothes and iron them. Free parking is available near the hotel. Near the hotel there is a cafe and a shop.

Pushkino

This hotel is located directly in the city at the address: st. Working, 1/1, in the Mammoth neighborhood. The hostel in Pushkino, Moscow Region is not distinguished by its original design, and the atmosphere is more like a student dormitory.

It features rooms that can accommodate from 4 to 10 people. They have old-style bunk metal beds, a TV and tables, and a wardrobe.

The cost of living in a single room is an average of 450 rubles. The toilet and showers are equipped in a common corridor, and there is also a kitchen with an electric kettle, microwave and a dining area.

The hostel has a washing machine and there is a room for drying things. Hotel guests can use free internet in common areas.

Hostel Lemon

In St. Petersburg there are also several institutions of this type in Pushkin. The most popular is Lemon. The hostel in Pushkin of St. Petersburg is located on Railway Station Square, 2.

The hotel has the opportunity to rent fully double rooms or beds in 3-, 4-, 5-bed rooms. The dormitory rooms have wooden bunk beds and a wardrobe. Almost all the rooms here are decorated in green shades.

In 2-, 3-bed rooms - single places for rest, TV, shelves for things and their own washbasin. The hostel has toilets and showers in the common corridors, as well as a kitchen with all necessary appliances and utensils.

All guests are provided with a clean set of bed linen and disposable hygiene shower sets. Hotel guests can use the free Internet, watch TV in the common lounge area.

The average cost of living at a hotel is 600 rubles per person. This includes free use of the laundry area, kitchen, lounge, where you can enjoy free tea and coffee.

By prior arrangement, transfers from the airport or train station can be arranged. Also for 450 rubles it is possible to organize an extra bed - a folding bed.
