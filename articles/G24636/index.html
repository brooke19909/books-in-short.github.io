Hotel Rena Apartments 3 * (Crete, Greece): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Rena Apartments is a three-star hotel located on the beautiful island of Crete, in one of the Greek cities - Heraklion (Gouves village). The airport is 15 kilometers from the hotel. The equipped beach of the hotel is 900 meters from its territory. Here tourists can take any sunbed and umbrella. The hotel is ready to offer vacationers one of its 27 rooms. All of them offer beautiful views of the amazing garden. There is a bus stop near the hotel. Here, at regular intervals, buses arrive to help vacationers reach the central places of the city. On the territory of the city there are also a lot of amazing corners in which a tourist should visit. 2 kilometers from the hotel is the old, but very picturesque village of Gouves.

Reviews of guests of the Rena Apartments 3 * hotel (Greece, Crete)

Reviews about this hotel are mostly positive. According to the guests, the hotel is a decent place, perfect for people who lead an active lifestyle, who love walking, as well as traveling. There is a car rental on site for a ride and to see the whole island. The place is suitable for middle-aged people and families. The hotel has very little entertainment for young people, so such people here will be quite bored. Hotel Rena Apartments 3 * (Greece, Gouves, Crete, Heraklion) is able to give tourists a quiet and relaxing holiday. This is a great choice for couples with children. The cozy atmosphere will allow a person to feel at home. Thanks to the active transport system, people can save on trips to famous sights. Buses appear at the bus stop every 30 minutes. From this place you can get to any attraction. If you purchase tickets for a trip to the station, then the cost will be much lower. The beach is located 900 meters from the hotel. It is well equipped, tourists will be pleased with this sandy beach with a large gentle entrance and clear water. I would also like to note the hotel staff, these attentive people will make the rest even more enjoyable. The prices in the stores at the hotel are adequate, it is very pleasing to tourists, because they can buy everything they need at an affordable cost.

Of course, there were some shortcomings. They are not significant, but still some guests mark them. One of the minuses of this hotel can be called a weak connection and Wi-Fi.
