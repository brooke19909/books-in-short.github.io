Samsung Tablet: advantages and disadvantages





Modern mobile and portable devices have quite extensive requirements. They must be reliable, high-quality and resistant to shake and minor physical impacts. At the same time, special attention is paid to functionality, battery capacity and screen quality. That is why the Samsung Galaxy Tab 2 tablet is very successful, not only among students and pupils, but also among businessmen. This device is able to perform almost any task, and even at the same time makes it possible to remain constantly mobile.

This line from Samsung uses the Android 4.0 operating system for its work. It has already proved to be quite good on devices of various types, it has a lot of add-ons and advantages over earlier versions. It is worth noting that the second generation Samsung Galaxy Tab tablet has a rather small price in the presence of a large number of additional functions and excellent quality.





The devices of this line have capacitive screens that match the size of a particular model. However, they all use multi-touch technology. As processors, a dual-core TI OMAP 4430 with a frequency of 1 GHz is installed on this line. The RAM used by the second-generation Samsung tablet corresponds to a volume of 1 GB, and the front camera - 3MP. Such parameters have not been called advanced for a long time, but they are much higher than the budget versions, and with a detailed study of the market, we can conclude that such a combination of additional functions and the availability of additional features is available at the best price only at Samsung.

Many skeptics believe that when they buy a Samsung tablet, they overpay for the brand. However, if you take into account the presence on the device of such functions as a light sensor, an orientation sensor , USB 2.0, Bluetooth and 3G, then it immediately becomes clear that such an amount is justified. It is also worth paying attention to the fact that the company gives a pretty good guarantee for all its equipment, which once again speaks about quality.

When choosing a Samsung tablet, it is possible to purchase the right model directly in the store. This is due to the fact that this company is very popular, and its products are constantly in great demand. Therefore, many stores monitor the release of new models and constantly replenish their assortment.





It is worth noting that the Samsung tablet is made in such a way as to facilitate the task for the user at work. All applications from the market are freely installed on it, and at the same time, some developers release software created specifically for this type of device. Also, these portable devices have a universal appearance, which makes it possible to use it together with various accessories and additional devices. This is especially true for covers of various types and protective films on the screen.

That is why if you need to purchase a reliable and high-quality device for work and leisure, then this line of Samsung will be the most optimal solution when choosing.
