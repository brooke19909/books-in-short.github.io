LCD "Barberry" in the immediate suburbs

For those who want to live not in Moscow itself, but nevertheless at the closest distance to it, RC "Barberry" offers comfortable apartments at real prices near Moscow.

The developer has chosen Mytischi for the construction of LCD "Barberry". Just twenty minutes on foot - and you get into the subway. But his address is Moscow Region, the city of Mytishchi. What makes the cost of housing affordable for citizens, because the developer is forced to sell it not at Moscow prices, but at Moscow region. And they are not an example below.





LCD "Barberry" is located on Kolpakova street and occupied a plot of more than ninety-four hundred parts. The nearby Pirogovsky Forest Park and the Borisovka River provide an opportunity for residents to enjoy communication with nature in their free time, fry kebabs and swim.

Moscow or not

In the suburban city of Mytishchi, LCD "Barberry" is comfortably located . Moscow is practically visible from the windows of the house. A metro is within walking distance. But territorially it is still not the capital. And therefore, the cost per square meter is also not metropolitan. That allows people to purchase it.

And the rest is just like in the main city of the country. Many supermarkets for every taste and budget. For children there is a school and kindergartens. If you suddenly need medical help, a network of medical centers, a clinic and a hospital are at your service.

The construction of a residential complex in an already built-up area has its undeniable advantages: all infrastructure is already available, and there is no need to wait for its construction.

The same thing is with the subway, or rather with its availability. The complex offers easy access to the nearest metro station. Since it is located only six kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road. Moreover, the public transport stop is located right next to the complex. In the future, it is planned to build a subway, the station of which will be located near the complex. The new metro station will contribute to a significant improvement in the quality of life of the residents of the complex and increase comfort. But at the same time, it will increase the cost of housing.
