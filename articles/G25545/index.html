Hotel "Azimut"

A welcoming meeting will be provided by two establishments of the Azimut network (Vladivostok). Hotels will please you with a good location and a high level of service. One of them is located right on the shore of the Amur Bay. In 2015, a complete renovation was carried out, thanks to which the hotel was brought into line with international standards.

The second institution is located on the territory of a large business center. This hotel is the largest in the city and includes 378 rooms. It is worth noting that all apartments are equipped in accordance with the Smart concept. This refers to the "smart layout", which implies ergonomic furniture, comfortable color design and good technical equipment of the apartments.

Reviews about the hotel "Azimut"

If you are interested in modern and luxurious hotels in Vladivostok, Azimut will be a great choice. Experienced tourists leave such positive reviews about the establishment:

  • stylish modern design of the hotel;
  • good soundproofing in the rooms (no neighbors are heard, no sounds from the corridor, no noise from the street);
  • beautiful views of the city and the sea;
  • daily good housekeeping.

And here are some negative ones:

  • uncomfortable pillows, after sleep on which the back hurts;
