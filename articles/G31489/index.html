The resolution of this screen is 2048x1536 pixels. This is significantly more than some monitors. Retina Display technology is an innovation in the tablet market. The work done by Apple engineers is worthy of respect.





Previously, people worked with such an accurate and high-quality picture, using only professional monitors (for example, hi-end computer monitors from various companies), the cost of which was mostly over $ 9.000, but today you can enjoy excellent image clarity just by buying a tablet on which Retina Display is installed.

Especially for iPad

The iPad tablet with Retina display uses IPS technology. Because of this system, we get a wider viewing angle. Thanks to this technology, no matter what the position of the iPad, you will always see a great image. Also, the contrast of the Retina display is much higher than the previous ones. Because of this, the white color is much brighter, and the black one looks much darker, and as a result the whole image looks much better. According to Apple engineers, it was quite difficult to make a display with 3.1 million pixels. To achieve the highest image quality, the developers used the separation of the system logic of the Retina iPad display into two areas. The pixels themselves remained at the top, and the signal generation circuit for each subpixel, which determines green, red or blue, was placed at the bottom.
