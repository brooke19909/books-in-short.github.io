Hotel in Belorussky Train Station in Moscow "Gorodotel 2 *": description of rooms, service, reviews

Belorussky Train Station is a place where many trains of various directions come. This is one of the nine stations in the capital, which is located on the Tverskaya Zastava Square. The station has a developed service sector. There are shops, a cafe, a passenger lounge, the Internet, a medical center, and a hotel.

The hotel at the Belorussky Train Station in Moscow is a place for recreation for guests of the city and its residents. The hotel offers comfortable and affordable accommodation. The article will focus on the Hotel City Hotel, its location, address and description of the rooms will be considered.

Overview

"City Hotel" - a network of hotels that are known throughout Russia. The main direction of their activity is the organization of places for overnight at railway stations or in their immediate vicinity. The hotel at the Belorussky Train Station in Moscow began operations in 2012, and in 2016 it was assigned the category "2 stars".

To accommodate guests, the hotel has 23 rooms, in which 46 guests can comfortably stay. All rooms are of different capacities and comforts.

A big plus is that from this point you can easily get to Sheremetyevo Airport. By Aeroexpress you can reach the international airport in 35 minutes. Red Square and the Kremlin - the main city attractions - are also in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. They can be easily and quickly reached by metro (2 stops). For evening walks, you can choose the busy street of the capital - Tverskaya. It is two minutes walk from the hotel. In the station building and in the immediate vicinity there are many catering points. shops and boutiques.

Interior

The hotel at the Belorussky Train Station in Moscow "City Hotel 2 *" is made in the classical style. No frills or frills. Everything is simple and tasteful. At the entrance to the hotel, visitors are greeted by a spacious bright hall with a reception desk. The building has an ATM, currency exchange, bar, cafe, medical station, pharmacy and entertainment centers. The hotel has the ability to order food and drinks.

All rooms are bright and airy. In the shared kitchen several families or companies can easily fit. Classic upholstered furniture (armchairs, sofas, tables and chairs). Beds with orthopedic mattresses. Light walls in rooms of discreet tones (beige, green, pink and yellow) are in harmony with plain curtains on the windows. Parquet floors. The bathroom and toilet are tiled.

Address and Location

"City Hotel 2 *" is a hotel at the Belorussky Train Station in Moscow, which is located on the 3rd floor of the railway station building. It is located in the first building, 7th house, on Tverskaya Zastava street . Nearby there are restaurants: Yakitoria (about 300 meters away), Goodman (750 meters), Japanese City (500 meters). From this point to the airports of Vnukovo and Sheremetyevo about 30-40 kilometers.

Hotel at Belorussky Train Station: room description

To accommodate visitors, the Hotel City Hotel offers several rooms of different comforts, capacities and prices. All apartments have a TV, a sleeping place and a wardrobe for storing clothes and personal belongings. The cost of living is from 1000 rubles.

Bed in dormitory room

This is one of the budget types of accommodation in this hotel. Accommodation per day will cost guests 1000 rubles. The rooms are divided into female and male, and are designed for 10 residents. Each room has 5 bunk beds. Near each there is a bedside table and a lamp. There is a work desk and several stools. A TV with multi-channel TV is available to all guests.

Each dormitory room has a dining area, which includes a microwave, kettle, fridge and kitchenware. Here guests can warm up meals and have tea parties.

The rooms also have a bathroom and toilet. They are combined. Each guest is provided with personal hygiene items (towel, soap, toilet paper).

Also in the rooms there is a telephone by which you can contact the hotel administrator or order a wake-up call. Long-distance calls are also available for a fee.

Outlets are common to all guests and are located near the nightstands and tables. Lighting is general and separate near each bed (reading lamp). Beds without screens and curtains.

Double Room

The apartment is designed for 2 guests. This number costs about 6000 rubles per day. It has everything you need for living: a bathroom, bedding, a TV, air conditioning, a fridge, a microwave and a kettle. The bed can be a large double or bunk. There are rooms with two single beds.

A hairdryer, free toiletries and sockets are also provided. The room is located on the second floor. No elevator.

Single Room

The hotel at the Belorussky Train Station, which provides accommodation for children and adolescents, offers extra beds in family and single rooms. The hotel is positioning this single room as an apartment for a mother with a child. The room has a single bed, as well as a cot and a changing table (folding). As in previous rooms, this one has a TV, fridge, kettle and microwave. A basic set of dishes is provided.

The hotel also has standard single rooms with all amenities without children's furniture. The cost of living is from 5000 rubles per day.

Rooms with shared facilities

Hotel at the Belorussky Train Station in Moscow - "City Hotel 2 *" offers low-cost beds in double and triple rooms with shared facilities. A shower room, toilet and sinks are located on the floor and are shared with neighboring rooms. Accommodation in such apartments costs from 4000 rubles per day. The hotel at the Belorussky Train Station, reviews of which will be lower, provides discounts to customers who are staying in rooms for a long time.

Suite and Junior Suite

Accommodation in comfortable spacious suites will cost guests 7,000 rubles. The rooms have a large double bed or two small beds, a TV, a fridge, air conditioning, and a private bathroom and toilet. For the convenience of guests in the rooms there are comfortable wardrobes and cabinets for clothes and personal items. There is a telephone and a shoe rack. A standard set of dishes and kitchen appliances is also present. The hotel at the Belorussky Train Station may change the cost of a suite and a junior suite, depending on the demand. For example, during the Olympic Games, the average price policy for all apartments was increased by 20-30%.

This hotel meets all quality standards of service and the provision of services. At the front desk, administrators will help you find solutions to any issue. Here you can order a taxi or deliver food to your room, check the train schedules, and also book a ticket for the desired flight.

Free high-speed internet is available throughout the hotel. The access code is issued by the administrator after registration. A 24-hour safe and personal luggage storage are available at the front desk. Concierge duties and currency exchange - all these services are provided by City Hotel 2 * to its guests at any time of the day. You can reserve a transfer to the airport on a paid and free basis (details must be checked with the administrator).

Reviews

This hotel at the Belorussky Train Station in Moscow, according to guest reviews, earned a rating of 8 points out of 10 possible. Many guests believe that its main advantage is its convenient location. For a transit holiday, this is ideal. Those who are going to fly out of Sheremetyevo should definitely stay here - the guests say.

A large number of reviews suggests that the hotel is in demand among tourists and visitors. Guests from different countries stay here. The friendly staff at the front desk answers all questions both by phone and in person. The ability to communicate in English eliminates possible discomfort when settling foreign citizens. Near the Belorussky railway station, the Belorusskaya metro station, Red Square and the Kremlin - all this attracts customers.

In their reviews, guests express satisfaction with the hotel and the interior. A big plus is the convenient location. You can immediately check into the room from the train and relax. For those who still have the strength to walk - near Tverskaya street and Red Square. As for food, in the station building itself there are many small points with fast food. For those who prefer more sophisticated lunches and dinners, several restaurants are close by until late at night. You can always order food to your room.

In their reviews, guests of the Hotel City Hotel were pleased with the rooms. they are spacious and comfortable. However, many furniture and appliances require replacement (it is outdated). There are also shortcomings in the quality of bedding (used, with small holes). The reading lamp in the room was also broken. Bath and toilet are clean. They are shared by two rooms. There was no queue. Sometimes at night, extraneous sounds are heard from the station (train announcements). However, this did not interfere with rest. In the room for three people there were two sets of dishes (I had to use them in turn).

Of the shortcomings in all reviews there is only one thing - the lack of an elevator to the third floor. Guests write that it is very difficult to climb stairs with filled suitcases and bags. In reviews and comments, they ask the hotel administration to place convenient ramps for strollers and suitcases. Belorussky railway station, Belorusskaya metro station and Tverskaya street in the immediate vicinity of the hotel are both plus and minus. After all, all these busy places bring extraneous noise, which prevents hotel guests from relaxing.

Reviews of guests who stayed at the hotel for a transit stay are mostly positive. All the nuances of accommodation were instantly decided with the administrator. The rooms are clean and comfortable. There is everything you need to eat. As for the cost, then relative to other hotels near the station - this is the most budget option (as customers write in reviews).

The hotel on the 3rd floor of the Belorussky railway station building is in demand by many guests of the city. This is a great option for relaxing between trains or planes. From this place it is convenient to get anywhere in the capital. "City Hotel" is in the middle price category. Living conditions are comfortable, standard. A wide range of services.

Conclusion

From all of the above reviews it follows that the Hotel City Hotel at the Belorussky Train Station in Moscow is in demand and popular. Many believe that it would be nice to carry out ongoing cosmetic repairs, replace furniture and utensils. The work of administrators is carried out efficiently and professionally.
