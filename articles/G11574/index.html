Universal Studio Park in Osaka: description, attractions, how to get, reviews

Japan has always been a mysterious country. It amazingly combines respect for traditions, respect for nature and modern technology. One of the interesting sights is the Universal Studios Park in Osaka. His dream is to visit not only children but also adults.

Short description

Universal Studios Park in Osaka is a place where visitors can feel like in Hollywood movies such as Jaws or Jurassic Park. There you can relax with the whole family by visiting the rides in any of the thematic areas, where entertainment for every taste and for visitors of any age is presented:

  • The Wizarding World of Harry Potter.
  • "Wonderland".
  • "Park of the minions."
  • "New York".
  • "San Francisco".
  • "Hollywood".
  • "Jurassic Park".
  • Amity Village.
  • "The water world."

This place of entertainment will make a worthy competition to the famous Disneyland. The rides at the Universal Studios Park in Osaka are more modern. In addition to them, there are restaurants, cafes and shops, as well as arrange interesting show programs with the participation of professional stuntmen. There you can meet your favorite characters and chat with them. The park was opened in 2001 and became the first Universal Park in Asia. Opening hours and ticket prices vary, so this information needs to be clarified on their official website.

The description of the park of Universal Studios in Osaka should start from the street leading to the entrance to this beautiful place. It has a large number of restaurants and shops, attracting attention with bright signs. This street immediately sets visitors on an adventure. Each visitor is a welcome guest, whom everyone greets with smiles. A shopping center is also nearby. How to get and buy tickets

Universal Park - is one of the most important attractions in Japan. Having been there, you can become a hero of the famous Hollywood blockbusters. How to get to the Universal Studios Park in Osaka? This can be done using the subway and train.

The nearest railway station is called Universal City. If you need to get from Osaka International Airport, you need to catch a train and go to the Hotaruike station. Then transfer to the Hankyu railway line Takarazuka and go to Umeda Osaka Station.

Next, you need to make another change on the JR ring line and get to the Nishikudze station. Then you need to go to the Yumeaki JR line and go to Universal City.

You can also get from Kansai Airport. You need to catch the JR train following the Hanva line to the Nishikudze station. There you need to make a change to the Yumezaki line and get to Universal City. There are also buses from the amusement park to Kansai and Osaka International Airports.

There are several categories of admission tickets to Universal Studio Park:

  • standard;
  • annual pass - with it you can visit the park without restrictions;
  • express pass - allows you to reduce the waiting time in the queues for popular attractions;
  • ticket for a park tour - a visit with a guide;
  • Premiere tickets.

Tickets can be bought at the entrance on the day of the visit or ordered online. A simple ticket costs about 7,200 yen.
