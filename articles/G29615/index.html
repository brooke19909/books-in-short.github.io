What is a shutter speed? In what cases is this function used and is it needed in the camera?

High-quality photographs cannot be obtained automatically with standard settings. Of course, they can be beautiful and interesting for a certain circle of people, but it is difficult to call them art work.

A function such as shutter speed in the camera is required for shooting moving objects, to obtain clarity, sharpness, or, conversely, blur of certain elements (for example, water flow).

So what is a shutter speed? From a technical point of view, this is the period of time during which light rays pass the lens aperture, falling on a photosensitive element.

From a scientific point of view, we explained what exposure is. And now let's take a closer look at what it happens and how to achieve the desired effect.





This parameter is calculated in seconds: 1 / 30sec, 1 / 60sec. Often, only the denominator is displayed on the camera, so don't be scared. In compact cameras, the row is displayed as follows: 1.1 / 2, ¬º, 1/8, and so on.

In any apparatus for photographing, there is such a function as shutter priority. This means that the aperture is automatically adjusted to the selected value.

The shutter speed is of particular importance when shooting water, regardless of whether it is a fountain or a river. A shutter too fast can create a frozen surface effect, and too long a lack of sharpness. So, for slow rivers and streams, a degree from 1/30 to 1 / 125s is suitable. In windy weather, set the parameter from 1/125 to 1/250. Rapid flows must be removed at about 1 / 1000s.

If you have a high-quality SLR or professional camera, you can shoot the night landscape of the starry sky with a shutter speed of up to several hours. So you get not only an interesting photo, but also the trajectory of the Earth. Do not forget about photosensitivity, otherwise the noise can be simply unbearable.

Underwater shooting uses short shutter speeds of no more than 1 / 30s, since you are constantly moving, which means that it is likely to get a blurry image.
