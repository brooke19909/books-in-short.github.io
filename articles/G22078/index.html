The best hotels of Belokurikha: information and reviews

Due to financial difficulties, compatriots have to refuse trips abroad. Unfortunately, many people do not even suspect that you can have a good rest without going outside of Russia.

This is not only about the Black Sea coast or the North Caucasus - recently, the hotels of Belokurikha located in the marvelous Altai Territory are gaining popularity.

For dignitaries

The resort village was founded on the site of the village in the 1920s. Ten years later, sleeping quarters, a solarium, an outpatient clinic, a clinic and a hydropathic center were built on the territory. In Belokurikha, state leaders rested and improved their health.

Studies of thermal waters, in which various trace elements (fluorine, radon, silicic acid, nitrogen, and others), played a large role in the development of a large resort in the east of the country. Healing mountain air contains light air ions, the amount of which exceeds the same figures in the famous Swiss Davos.

The climate is considered favorable for health: non-hot summers, warm autumn and spring months, mild winters with short-term frosts and thaws.



Today, Belokurikha hotels receive more than 200 thousand tourists a year. Travelers are interested not only in the magnificent nature and therapeutic procedures. For lovers of outdoor activities in the resort are equipped with ski slopes.

The Belovodye Hotel often comes with small children, because for them there is the most important entertainment here - a water park. Pools, slides and water attractions for young visitors, a thermal complex with a jacuzzi, saunas and even a cave with snow for adults.

"Grace"

In the immediate vicinity of the Katun-2 and Katun ski lifts is located the Grace Hotel (Belokurikha, 77 Slavskogo St.). On the territory there are a restaurant, rental, sports equipment storage room, secure parking and a small health center.

Several categories of rooms are available for booking: from a modest attic to spacious apartments with two bedrooms and a kitchenette. Regardless of the cost, all rooms are equipped with necessary furniture, a set of dishes, a kettle, a hairdryer and a TV.



The location of the hotel, according to guests, is ideal for winter sports. Nearby there is a ski school and, of course, magnificent slopes designed for any level of training.

For traveling with young children, guests often choose the Echo Hotel (34 Belokurikha, Slavskogo St.), which belongs to the Rossiya sanatorium.

The building, resembling a chalet in Switzerland, offers only 24 rooms for booking. A cozy atmosphere is created by handmade interior details.

Near the hotel there is a ski slope for beginners, you can rent the necessary equipment in winter and bicycles in the warm season.
