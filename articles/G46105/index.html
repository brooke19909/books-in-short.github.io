Yekaterinburg, Lake Chusovskoye - treatment and fishing in the city

Yekaterinburg (formerly Sverdlovsk) is the administrative center of the Urals District. This is the fourth largest city in Russia and one of the centers of industry, culture, connects the European and Asian parts of the country. The city has 7 districts. In one of them (Verkh-Isetsky) there is a unique place - the village of Chusovskoye Lake.

Village

The village is part of the city and is located in a forested area. There is a lake Chusovskoye in the village . Yekaterinburg (central part) is only 20 km from the village.

The village is built up mainly by private houses. Today, a cottage village of the same name is populated here. On the territory there are sanatoriums, shops, a children's institution and a feldsher-midwife station.

About 700 people live in the village (as of 2010). Mostly they are Russians.

Historical reference

On the shore of Lake Chusovsky (Yekaterinburg), the remains of a primitive man were discovered. At the beginning of the XVIII century, an Old Believer monastery, and a female one, acted on the territory of the village. Already from the 40s of the last century, sanatoriums began to be erected here.





Lake

In fact, the official name of the lake is Chusovoe. But the people call it Chusovskoye Lake in Yekaterinburg due to the fact that this is the name of the village.

The reservoir belongs to the Kama basin. An unnamed stream flows from the lake (in the south-west). From the north flows the Patrushikha River, which is assigned to the Irtysh basin. In fact, the lake is located on the watershed of two catchment areas.

It feeds on a pond from the mountains, which are located on the southern and northern banks. Also, food is due to precipitation.

It is believed that the age of the lake is about 320 million years. On the banks of the reservoir found parking primitive people. Russian came here only in the XVII-XVIII centuries. Bashkirs called Lake Boltou.

The width of the reservoir is about 1 km, in length - 2 km. The bottom here is mostly muddy, in some places rocky. The maximum depth is 4 meters.

Fishing

Fish is found on Lake Chusovsky in Yekaterinburg - these are pike, tench, perch, minnow, chebak and crucian carp. But the largest percentage falls on perch and roach, this fish can be caught here all year round. Net fishing is prohibited, only with fishing rods and spinning.

Here you can settle on the lake in a tent. It is believed that water can be drunk from here, it has a low degree of mineralization, fresh and artesian. However, previously it is still recommended to boil it.





OTsMR "Lake Chusovskoe"

Near Lake Chusovsky (Yekaterinburg) is a medical complex. Today it has the most modern equipment and highly qualified personnel. In addition, there is a well-groomed and landscaped area. There are specially designated buildings that contain bedridden patients undergoing rehabilitation after spinal injuries. However, the services that the vacationer wants to use and are not included in the conclusion of the MES are paid extra.

The following services are provided in the sanatorium:

  • manual therapy;
  • psychotherapy;
  • physiotherapy.

Here you can go through functional diagnostics, ultrasound and x-ray studies. There is also a biochemical and clinical laboratory.

The main direction of the Lake Chusovsky sanatorium in Yekaterinburg is rehabilitation measures after suffering neurological and cardiovascular diseases. That is, all activity is aimed at the full restoration of the health and working capacity of patients.

On the territory there are sports grounds, a mud bath, a gym and exercise therapy, a hydropathic. If desired, you can visit the dentist and beautician. Sanatorium treatment services are not designed for children.





Scientific activity

The sanatorium is also famous for scientific research in the field of medicine. And this is not only theoretical research, but clinical practice, which can improve the promising development of the hospital complex.

The employees of the sanatorium monitor diagnostic measures, study the role of drug therapy, the influence of pathophysiological aspects of physical activity. All these events allowed to create a complex of a new type, which includes traditional therapy, modern technology and comfortable living.
