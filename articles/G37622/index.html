Greece, hotel Kassandra Mare Hotel: photos and reviews

Greece has always been and is a favorite vacation spot for tourists from all over the world. After all, it was on this promised land that the heroes of Greek myths lived. Here, every stone, every piece of land are witnesses of ancient history. In a word, the main advantage of Greece as a tourist destination is the possibility of combined tours: beach and sightseeing, extreme and ecotour, in addition, the culinary orientation is also an obligatory component.



Halkidiki - the legendary trident

Greece, as you know, is divided into mainland and island. In addition, there are many resorts that are located on the peninsulas. And there are a great many of those along the coastline of Greece. And among them, perhaps the most popular is Halkidiki. There is a huge number of hotels of all categories, ranging from the magnificent "fives", ending with hotels with one star or without them at all (the so-called guest houses). However, the Russians mainly choose triples. They are comfortable, decent and offer tourists an upscale vacation, such as the Kassandra Mare Hotel 3 (Halkidiki / Kassandra).

This beautiful peninsula is located on the northern shores of the Aegean Sea. It has the shape of a trident or three fingers. Each of the teeth is also a peninsula. They bear very beautiful names: Kassandra, Agio Oros and Sithonia. All of them are rich in vegetation, which attracts lovers of flora and fauna here. In addition, Halkidiki is considered an excellent vacation spot for tourists vacationing with families.



How to get there?

The closest Greek airport to Halkidiki is Thessaloniki. Regular and charter flights fly from Moscow there. Flight time is about three and a half hours. From other large Russian cities from time to time, mainly in season, charter planes fly. Well, from Thessaloniki to the desired resort, you can go either by bus or by car. The distance is 56 kilometers, which means that you will have to be 40-60 minutes on the road. If your destination is a deluxe hotel, then the transfer from the airport to the hotel is usually included in the price of the tour, and if your choice is a three-star, for example, the hotel Kassandra Mare Hotel 3 * (Halkidiki / Kassandra / Nea Potidea) , then when you make a tour it is better to pay for the transfer immediately. Then, after landing at the airport of Thessaloniki, you do not have to solve the problem of moving to the resort on your own.

By the way, there is another good option - preliminary car rental. If you have a driver‚Äôs license of an international standard, you can rent a pretty decent car for only 30 euros per day, and then it will be possible to make long trips every day around the peninsula.



Climatic conditions

The average annual air temperature in these places is almost the same as in our Black Sea resorts. This means that the swimming season here lasts from May to early October. The holiday season is from May to September inclusive. On average, even in the hot months (July ‚Äì August), the air warms up to 30 degrees, but there are also higher rates - 35 or more. Therefore, tourists always feel comfortable in the room, but on the beach, under the umbrella, no heat is terrible.



Beaches on Halkidiki

Of course, the bulk of tourists (more than 95%) come to Greece for a beach holiday. And sightseeing is a secondary goal, so the condition of the beaches is important for almost everyone. By the way, almost all land areas adjacent to the sea, regardless of whether the hotel is in the immediate vicinity or not, belong to the municipality. In terms of cleanliness, everyone can be calm, since the Greeks are very careful about their land, including beaches. They are all perfectly maintained, you will not find garbage anywhere, everywhere there is beach equipment - changing rooms, showers, deck chairs, umbrellas, etc. True, you need to pay for some amenities. But the entrance to the beach is free. The beaches on Halkidiki are covered with snow-white sand mixed with small pebbles. For lovers of water entertainment there is a wide selection of attractions.



Kassandra

So, this time, Greece attracted you as a country for summer holidays. 3 * Kassandra Mare Hotel is an ideal option for an economical vacation. The conditions in it are acceptable and quite consistent with the price. In addition, you can freely use all the amenities of the neighboring five-star hotels (some for a fee). This hotel is located on one of the three teeth of the Halkidiki Peninsula, namely Kassandra. According to legend, here from heaven and earth the first of the giants was born - Alkionei. Kassandra is one of the favorite vacation spots not only for tourists from different countries, but also the Greeks themselves, who come here for the weekend. There are 44 settlements on the peninsula, among them Nea Potidea, in which the aforementioned Kassandra Mare Hotel is located.

Sometimes it seems that the most attractive in this peninsula is its name. Cassandra - for our ears, it sounds romantic and evokes associations with a beautiful sultry girl. Nevertheless, the peninsula got its name from the name of Tsar Kassandra - son-in-law of Alexander the Great (Macedonian). The beaches on the peninsula are the best among mainland Greece. Many of them have been awarded the azure flag of the EU more than once. By the way, for those who came to Greece not only for a beach holiday, but also for sightseeing, here is a real paradise.



Nea Potidea

It is near this village, located on the Kassandra peninsula, that Kassandra Mare Hotel 3 * is located. The biggest advantage of this resort are its magnificent sandy beaches. Near the resort are the ruins of a Corinthian colony, bearing the same name. In a word, the resort can become a delightful vacation spot for both adults and children. Its name comes from the name of the God of the seas of Poseidon. Enemy ships that wanted to attack the city were defeated due to the tides. Residents of the city believed that they were protected by Poseidon.
