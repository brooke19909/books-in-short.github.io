What can VDNH offer us? Amusement park!

It's hard to come up with a more wonderful weekend than visiting an amusement park that will allow you to relax and have fun with your family. VDNH, whose park is rightfully considered one of the best, offers a huge selection of entertainment for any age and for every taste. And it is not so important whether you are a guest of the capital or live in it. When you visit this stunning complex, everyone feels great.



At any age there should be a place for joyful entertainment. It is very useful to relax a bit and rejoice from any kind of children's pranks from the heart. Psychologists recommend visiting various entertainment centers as a psychological relief , and VDNH (park) is the most suitable place for a celebration of the soul.



In order to get to know a person better, it is enough to look at him in an unusual situation. By inviting your colleagues for a corporate holiday in one of the most beautiful and largest amusement parks, you can get to know them from the most unexpected side. Relaxing, people change, become softer and kinder. Therefore, such unloading will be useful for the entire office: it will have a positive effect on the work and the implementation of tasks. The main thing is to approach the rest wisely.

The park at VDNH can be a great place for a romantic date. Everyone will want to spend time with a loved one in such a cheerful and pleasant atmosphere. You can please your soulmate with a ticket to her favorite attraction - not a single gift is valued as highly as receiving positive emotions. After all, they leave the brightest imprint and are stored in memory for a long period of time.

A weekend with children is the time that every parent dreams of. In a modern world full of cares and fuss, in the endless pursuit of a secure future for their child, many do not have enough free minutes to fully communicate with children, not to mention spending time together. Attractions in Moscow (VDNH) will lead to the same delight both the child and the parents. This is a great opportunity for all family members to get close and share a pleasant experience.





In addition to the attractions, the park has a lot of simply beautiful and picturesque places, looking at which you can relax and enjoy the national heritage. Fountain Alley can impress even the most unromantic people. At VDNH, the park opens its eyes to unprecedented beauties and acquaints with man-made art, which was created over the centuries and required painstaking work.

At VDNH, the park offers fun at affordable prices. And all the proceeds go to the reconstruction of monuments, fountains, as well as the construction of new attractions.
