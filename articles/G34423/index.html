Mexico-Moscow time - what's the difference?

A huge country located between two oceans is divided into four time zones. These are UTC - 5 (East), - 6 (Central), - 7 (Pacific) and - 8 (Northwest). And Moscow is in the UTC +3 time zone. In addition, despite being close to the equator, Mexico, with the exception of the extreme southern state of Sonora, is switching to daylight saving time. We remind you that in Russia the clock hands on the first Sunday of April are not translated. The country constantly lives in the "winter" time. And the flight schedule always indicates the arrival and departure of the local timepieces. Thus, it is extremely difficult to calculate how long it takes to fly to Mexico from Moscow using the airline tickets available.
