The One Hotel Ex. Club Alinn 4 * (Turkey) - photos and reviews of tourists

It's no secret that Turkey is one of the most popular holiday destinations among our compatriots. A mild climate, clear sea waves, golden beaches and thousands of diverse hotels - this is what the country can offer to any tourist. And the hotel complex The One Hotel (Ex. Club Alinn) 4 *, located in a secluded spot on the territory of the sea coast, is considered quite popular. What conditions does this hotel offer? What type of vacation is it for? Is it possible to come here with children? This information is of interest to many travelers.

Hotel Location





Of course, tourists are primarily interested in the location of the hotel complex The One Hotel (Ex. Club Alinn) 4 *. Turkey is a great place to relax. And on one of the picturesque coasts of the country, on the territory of Sarigerme, this small but very cozy hotel is located.

The hotel first opened its doors to tourists in 2008. And in 2013, the last thorough reconstruction was carried out, after which the hotel complex became even more cozy and convenient. The nearest airport is Dalaman Airport, 18 km from the property. The hotel is located 16 km from the center of the famous resort town of Dalaman. And 12 km is the picturesque small village of Ortach.

What rooms does the hotel offer?





The hotel has 72 rooms:

  • 48 standard rooms, consisting of a spacious bedroom and bathroom;
  • 24 family rooms for 4-5 people; they consist of a living room, bedroom and bathroom.

All rooms in the hotel are very spacious and stand out with a modern but discreet design. The room has the necessary set of furniture, including a table, chairs, bed, mirror, wardrobe, sofa, etc. There is access to a terrace or a private small balcony.

From household appliances in the room there is an individual air conditioning, a TV (there are popular music channels, as well as three Russian channels), a telephone and a hairdryer. A safe is available for a small fee. A minibar is also available, but its content is not included in the price.

The ceramic-coated bathroom offers a spacious shower and 24-hour hot and cold water. Towels are changed every day during cleaning. Bed linen is changed three times a week.

The scheme of food at the hotel





It is immediately worth noting that the hotel complex The One Hotel (Ex. Club Alinn) 4 * operates on an all-inclusive basis. All residents of the hotel are provided with breakfast, lunch and dinner, which are held in the main restaurant of the hotel. It is worth noting that the menu here is very diverse - you will find an abundance of fresh fruits and vegetable salads, as well as a huge selection of snacks, meat, dishes prepared on the grill. The local restaurant is also famous for delicious pastries and homemade ice cream. Coffee, tea, soft drinks, as well as locally produced alcohol are free of charge for hotel guests. But for imported alcoholic drinks you need to pay separately.

Beach and water recreation for tourists

Many travelers are interested in questions about how good a beach holiday hotel offers The One Hotel (Ex Club Alinn) 4 *. Dalamen, Sarigerme is the right place to fully enjoy the natural beauty of nature, warm water and soft sand.

The hotel owns a private beach stretch of a thousand meters. The beach is located about one kilometer from the hotel building - a road and a picturesque promenade leads to it. If someone does not want to overcome the distance each time on foot, then a free mini-bus runs regularly for them.

The beach is clean, well maintained and closed to outsiders. There are even rows of sun loungers and umbrellas that you can use for free. You can also get a soft beach towel.

Naturally, lovers of a more active holiday will not have to be bored either. The beach has a volleyball court. You can also ride catamarans, water skiing or learn scuba diving. In addition, there is the opportunity to surf.

Leisure and entertainment at The One Hotel (ex. Club Alinn) 4 *, Dalaman





The hotel complex offers its guests all possible amenities for a pleasant and fun pastime. The courtyard has an outdoor pool of 325 square meters and a maximum depth of 1.4 meters. There is also a small water slide, on which both adults and their children can have fun.

What other entertainment does the One Hotel (Ex. Club Alinn) 4 * offer? Dalaman is a small cozy place famous for the breathtaking beauty of nature. Here you can enjoy cycling or hiking, hiking in the mountains, etc.
