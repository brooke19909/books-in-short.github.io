Pension "Nico" in Vityazevo: description, prices, reviews

Pension "Nico" is located on the seashore of the village of Vityazevo. The minimum distance to the beach and developed infrastructure make this place popular for vacationers. Pools, playgrounds and complexes, animation for children - all this is provided to vacationers by the Niko boarding house. Anapa is located from the recreation center at a distance of 18 km. What number of rooms does the establishment have for recreation? Are guests satisfied with the quality of service and living conditions?

general description

Pension "Niko" is located in a quiet place between the Black Sea and the Vityazevsky estuary. Thus, vacationers can lie on the beach in the morning, and take mud baths in the estuary in the evening. Despite the remoteness from the benefits of large cities, the boarding house has everything you need on its territory and in rooms. Every vacationer can appreciate it.

Pension "Niko" (Vityazevo) is constantly improving its infrastructure. At the moment, the territory of the recreation center has:

  • Pools (in quantity - 3) connected in one complex.
  • Vast territory with flowers and dense greens.
  • Two catering establishments.
  • Two playgrounds, a territory for sports.
  • Sauna with a capacity of 4-6 people.
  • Bureau for booking excursions.
  • Car parking in a designated place.
  • An entertaining entertainment program for children daily.

Pension "Niko" (Vityazevo) is a beautiful place for active and passive relaxation.
