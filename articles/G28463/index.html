How to make sultans with your own hands? Several different options

At festive performances in kindergarten and school, the presence of sultans is often required. They can also be used to decorate rooms for the New Year or for children's parties at home. What is a sultan? This is a bunch of bright stripes connected together. If you need to hold them in your hand, then this bundle is attached to a stick or the edges are tied with thread, rope, and adhesive tape. In the article, we will consider in detail how to make sultans with your own hands from different materials.

Bunch of ribbons

Sultans made of satin ribbons look beautiful . Tapes can be taken monophonic, but can be of different colors. Watching why they are needed. If the child is dancing the Kalinkui dance at the matinee, then the red sultans do it, if the dance of sunlight is yellow or bright orange. To make it convenient for the child to hold the product in his hands, before making the sultans with his own hands, you need to pick up a small round stick (from the old flag or cut a tree branch, in extreme cases, you can always use a pencil).





To keep the bunch together, the ribbons can be sewn with thread or glued to tape around the stick. The holder is wrapped with a colored ribbon. Everything, you can go on a holiday.

New Year sultans

If you need lush, shiny bundles for the New Year holiday, then we will tell you in detail how to make sultanas out of the rain with your own hands. It is possible to use a rain both monophonic, and multi-colored. If a girl dances a dance of snowflakes, then it is better to make silver sultans, if this is a dance of Parsley, then it is possible and colored. Such brilliant products are best made lush, as a half-empty bunch will look deplorable. Smooth and thin rain stripes are sold. This is convenient because making sultanas with your own hands will be easier.





The average length of the product is approximately 20 cm. The rain is laid out on the table in even stripes, the length of which is 40-42 cm. Then we find the middle of the segments and tie the bundle with a dense thread, a thin rope or rain. Then we raise the beam in the middle up, and the edges smooth down. In order for the child to hold them in his hand, you need to tape several times around the knot. If the rain has moved out of place, the edges can be trimmed with scissors, cutting off excess threads. Knowing how to make rain sultanas with your own hands, you can decorate a group room in a kindergarten or a school class with bright beams and hang them on a fishing line under the ceiling or on a blackboard.

We use bags

You can make very magnificent sultans from plastic bags, for example, at an autumn matinee. It is in the fall in kindergarten that often music workers enter the roles of rain and cloud into the script, especially in younger groups. For such dances will have to do lush translucent bunches. We will talk about how to make sultans from packages yourself. First you need to purchase two packages of the simplest packages without a picture of white and light blue.





If there are pens on them, they must be cut off immediately without printing the packaging. Then, several packets (6-7) of blue color and the same amount of white are cut into strips with scissors. But not to the end. A whole strip is left at the end, which, after incision, will need to be twisted into a tight cylinder and tied with a thread. Then we wrap the edge, over which the child will hold the sultan, with white tape. If you put the bags so that the colors alternate, you get a beautiful multi-colored bunch.

Paper products

Next, we will consider how to make sultans with your own hands out of paper. To do this, it is better to buy sets of tissue corrugated paper in stationery stores. They need to be deployed and folded in half in width. If the paper is too large, then cut it yourself into rectangles of the desired width. It should turn out exactly as in the photo. The expanded strip of paper is folded into a roll, and the middle is tied with a thread. Then we connect both sides from the bottom together, and around the knot we tie another thread. It turns out a bunch with a loop on top. Such a paper sultan can be hung by a loop on a rope to decorate a room for a holiday or many sultans can be hung on a fishing line above the ceiling. It will turn out a beautiful multi-colored magnificent garland.

Felt Sultans

Now needlewomen often use felt sheets in their crafts. They have bright and saturated colors. The material is convenient to use. It can be cut, glued, stitched. Before you make sultans with your own hands from felt, you need to buy several sheets in sewing accessories stores.





Further, the process is repeated on the model of the manufacture of paper sultans. If you need a bundle of cut ribbons to have a holder, then use plastic sticks for balloons. It is convenient, they look beautiful. If the stick is long, then it can be cut in half.

Christmas tinsel

Girls on holidays like to dress up as princesses, sorceresses and fairies. For such a New Year's costume, we will advise how to make sultanions from tinsel with your own hands. Tinsel is a silvered or gilded thread that decorates a Christmas tree for the New Year. For a fairy, you can make a magic wand decorated with an asterisk and tinsel, assembled in a sultan.

For such an item does not need great splendor, like a regular sultan. Here you can take just a few threads, but a longer length. Attach to the edge of a long stick and decorate it, for example, with a colored cardboard star. With the wave of a magic wand, the tinsel strings will fly up beautifully into the air and sparkle in the light of the lights.
