Do-it-yourself do-it-yourself topiary

Very soon we will celebrate the coming of the New Year and Christmas. In this article we will talk about how to create New Year's topiary with your own hands. Such beautiful and original crafts will be an excellent gift for colleagues, family members and friends. We bring to your attention two simple master classes on making "trees of happiness". We decorate one topiary with pine cones, and the second with Christmas balls and tinsel. So, let's start creating chic holiday souvenirs.

DIY New Year's topiary: decorate with "cones of happiness" cones



To make the craft you will need the following materials and tools: plasticine, foil, twig or stick, newspaper, glue gun and PVA glue, wrapping paper, tinsel, pine cones of small size, bow, shiny acrylic paint, a glass with thick walls with a capacity of 100 ml.

We begin the manufacture of New Year's topiary with the design of a glass. She will serve as a pot for our tree. We wrap the glass with beautiful wrapping paper, fixing it with glue. We fill the half of the container with plasticine, and lay a layer of tinsel on top. Now we make a tree trunk: we cover a branch with acrylic paint and leave to dry. We form a ball of arbitrary shape from foil. Do not crush the foil too tight, otherwise the crown of the topiary will be too heavy. We paste over the foil with a newspaper and apply the desired color to the blank. After the paint dries, we make a small hole in the ball and put it on the barrel, which, in turn, is placed in an already decorated pot. Our New Year's topiary (photo you can see in the article) is almost ready. Now it remains to decorate the tree with cones. It is first necessary to cover them with acrylic paints. Glue the cones to the ball using a glue gun. Important! Natural material must be placed tightly, so that the crown becomes lush and voluminous, without gaps. After that, it remains to decorate the topiary table with a bright bow. That's it, our work is completed!

Do-it-yourself New Year's topiary: decorate the tree of happiness with Christmas balls



In order to create a festive topiary with Christmas balls, you will need such tools and materials:

  • foam newspaper or ball;
  • threads
  • acrylic paint;
  • Christmas balls (it is desirable that they be of different sizes, colors, textures);
  • stick or twig for the trunk;
  • a small pot of ceramics;
  • glue;
  • gypsum;
  • glue gun;
  • decorative elements: beads, spruce branches, tinsel, sparkles.

First we make a ball of newspapers, and then wrap it with thread, Now we insert a branch into it, fixing it with glue. It is necessary to paint the ball in the desired color with acrylic paints. The turn of Christmas balls has come - we glue them to the ball of the topiary with a glue gun. Next, proceed to the design of the pot. We make a gypsum mortar and pour it into a ceramic container. Once the gypsum has set, you must insert a tree trunk into it . After this, you need to wait until the gypsum is completely dry. It remains to decorate the "tree of happiness" and the base of the pot with decorative branches of thuja or spruce, beads, tinsel. That's all, our New Year‚Äôs do-it-yourself topiary is ready! In addition to such festive trees, you can make a charming Christmas tree.

In order to make a figured trunk, it is necessary to prepare a cone from thick cardboard and arrange it with pine or spruce branches, then decorate with beads, small Christmas balls and tinsel. The Christmas tree topiary will look very pretty and cheerful. It will definitely decorate any interior and bring a sense of celebration.
