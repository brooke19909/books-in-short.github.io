Wild beaches of Anapa: photos, description and reviews

What attracts vacationers to wild beaches? The answer is simple: untouched nature, rare plants, numerous representatives of the animal world. The most frequent ‚Äúneighbors‚Äù on a wild beach are sand, shells, seagulls, sea wind and salty waves.





The location of such secluded places is not customary to advertise, because if every tourist knows about such beaches, they simply cease to be wild, lose their charm. We will unveil the veil of secrecy and tell you how to find wild beaches in Anapa. Photos and reviews are also waiting for you in this article.

Beach of the 40th anniversary of Victory

One of the least populated beaches of Anapa, located within the resort city, is the beach of 40 years of Victory. It is located on the High Bank. Entrance to the beach is free. Vacationers note that a convenient road equipped with sidewalks leads to the pebble shore. On the way to the sea, tourists will meet many stalls with food and local souvenirs.





The working hours of this wild Anapa beach are from eight in the morning until eight in the evening. Here you can rent a sun lounger, sun protection umbrellas and other beach equipment. Despite the fact that this beach is considered wild, there is a lifeguard post, water approaches are equipped.

Lighthouse

Another wild beach of Anapa - Lighthouse - is located on the second coastline. The shore, strewn with small pebbles, adjoins the territory of the Mayak sanatorium. The name was given to him by local residents. By the way, they do not recommend visiting this beach in a storm. The fact is that he is surrounded by large stones, and there is a risk of a collapse. Finding a beach is easy, it is located between two others - the Golden Bay and the Small Bay. The entrance is equipped with various rental points.

Wild beach towards Succo

The beach, chosen by lovers of secluded relaxation, stretches from the outskirts of Anapa towards the villages of Sukko and Supsekh. The length of this coast exceeds five kilometers. The beach is characterized by crystal clear water, it is here that you can feel a real unity with nature.





True, this wild Anapa beach has its drawbacks. Vacationers note that the entrance to the sea is rocky and not very convenient. And the descents to the very shore are not equipped at all, and therefore only young people and extreme people can choose this place for relaxation. Most often, he is chosen by couples who want to hide from prying eyes, and people who prefer to sunbathe without clothes. Those who are afraid to go down to the sea along a steep coast, but still want to visit this beach, should walk on it, right along the coast from the beach of the 40th anniversary of Victory.

From the Anapka River to Dzhemete

Another popular wild beach of Anapa is the one located in the direction of the resort village of Dzhemete. It stretches along the entire Pioneer Avenue. This place was loved by vacationers due to the very soft natural sand. In addition, the beach is surrounded by picturesque dunes. Holidays on this beach are chosen even by families with children. And this is not surprising, because the sea is shallow, its depth is growing little by little. Yes, and getting here is easy - on foot or by public transport.





Despite the fact that the beach is considered wild, service is well developed here. Vacationers are pleased that rentals work here, where you can pick up sun loungers, there are various water attractions.

Highland

The wild beach of Anapa under the popular name High Coast is located in the city. The coast is covered with pebbles. Its width here does not exceed 30 meters (earlier, by the way, it was less than half). That is why the beach is not crowded. Recently, the High Coast began to ennoble. Tourists note that dry closets, lifeguard towers, changing rooms, rental of necessary equipment and a left-luggage office appeared.





The only drawback of this beautiful place is the inconvenient entry into the water. The seabed is strewn with slippery cobblestones covered with silt. Where is the wild beach in Anapa located? Three descents lead to it from the Lesser Cove to the Walnut Grove.
