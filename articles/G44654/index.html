Nice beaches in Pattaya: overview, photos and reviews

What is the ideal vacation for you? Some will answer that it is a lot of activity, and prefer, for example, hiking in the mountains, rafting on the river. Others will tell you that for them, an ideal vacation is a visit to historical and cultural attractions. More than half of people will say that they prefer to spend their holidays in warm countries with white sand, emerald sea and luxury hotels. True, they often have a question, where can I go?

If outside the window you have snow, cold and frost, then most of the Asian destinations will suit you. For example, the state of Thailand, which is one of the most popular resorts for residents of Russia and the CIS countries. This is no coincidence. Firstly, the weather is amazing in winter, there are hot days, there is no rainfall. Secondly, everything is so well-equipped here that everyone, even the most fastidious tourist, will be able to find a place for themselves. Thirdly, in Thailand, you can quickly replace lying on the beach with cultural relaxation by taking excursions to the best palaces and temples.

This state is washed from almost all sides by the waters of the oceans, but not all beaches have become a favorite destination for tourists. Where can you lie down and enjoy the beauty of nature? It is to this question that we have to answer today. We will make a list of the best beaches in Pattaya, get acquainted with their infrastructure. So let's get started.

How to find the perfect beach? Criterias of choice

It turns out that finding a good beach in Pattaya, and in any other area, is not so simple, because not all of them are adapted for tourists to visit. Now we will find out the main criteria by which you can easily find the option you need:

  1. The cleanliness of the beaches. One of the most important criteria, because no one wants to swim in the muddy water. It is worth mentioning that the beaches in the city are not the cleanest, sometimes trash floats in the water. That is why most tourists could not give them a rating above 4 points.
  2. Scenery. Pattaya Province is multifaceted, it is almost impossible to find the same beaches. On some you can meet densely planted tropical trees, on others the beach is surrounded by gentle hills.
  3. Infrastructure. For many, an important criterion is enough, because almost no one wants to go far for delicious food and alcohol. Most of the famous beaches are visited by many sellers with various Thai food, which they sell at 2-3 more expensive than in street cafes. It is worth saying that some beaches are located so that to small shops with alcohol and food to go very close.
  4. Fame. The best beaches are a bit far from the city, there are not a huge number of tourists, but the nature is incredibly beautiful.

Main, central beach (Pattaya Beach)

9th place in the list of good beaches in Pattaya is the main and central beach of the province. It is located almost in the center, so it is convenient to get to it from all sides. We will evaluate according to the following criteria:

  1. Purity. There are many shops near the beach, cars often drive by, so there is a lot of garbage. Fortunately, the janitors try to remove all the garbage as quickly as possible.
  2. Scenery. It is quite beautiful here, you can settle down both at the very shore and under the shade of tropical trees.
  3. Infrastructure. Here is a real abundance of everything, many options for food and drink. Moreover, there are many cafes nearby where you can order any dish you like.
  4. Fame. Since the beach is central, there are many people here, but everyone has a place.

So, let's talk about the best hotels in Pattaya with its own beach located on Pattaya Beach:

  1. The Bayview Hotel Pattaya. A luxurious four-star hotel with incredibly beautiful rooms. They offer wonderful views of the private beach. The average cost of a room is 15-20 thousand rubles. Since the beach is fenced off from the public, the water and sand here are much cleaner.
  2. Royal Twins Palace Hotel. One of the best hotels in Pattaya with its own beach. The infrastructure is remarkably developed here, the rooms are clean and beautifully decorated. Minimum room rate - 10,000 rubles per day.

Jomtien Beach

8th place in the list of good beaches in Pattaya is Jomtien Beach. It is located a little further from the noisy center, so it is quieter and more comfortable here:

  1. Purity. The beach is also along the road, but there are about 40% less cars here. The water in the sea is cleaner and more beautiful, the beach is just as soft, with gray fine sand. There is not much garbage here, most of all the algae thrown ashore by the stream.
  2. Scenery. Many locals and tourists like to meet sunsets and sunrises here, because the sky is not blocked by tall buildings, in the distance you can only see a lonely island.
  3. Infrastructure. There are many different cafes and restaurants here, you can try both Thai and European cuisine in them. By the way, prices are much lower compared to the central beach. Along the shore there are small shops with food and drinks, there are massage rooms where Thai people make a great massage.
  4. Fame. The beach is off-center, it is popular with locals and tourists, but not so much.

The best hotel in Pattaya with its beach, located on Jomtien Beach, called the Grand Jomtien Palace Hotel. It has its own pool, gym, a wonderful breakfast is included in the price. The minimum price for a double room is 10-12 thousand rubles per day.

Dongtan

7th place in the list of the best beaches in Pattaya is Dong Tarn Beach. It is located away from the hustle and bustle, it is quiet and comfortable. This place has its own unique feature: you can often meet representatives of sexual minorities. For them, this is a real paradise where no one dares to condemn them:

  1. Purity. The beach is far from the roads, so it is much cleaner and more pleasant to be here. The water is not cloudy, acquires a beautiful, bluish tint. The sand is a little lighter, clean, even there is not much algae here.
  2. Scenery. The beach is surrounded by dense tropical trees, which are an obstacle to noise and most people. There is no incredible beauty here, but the distant horizon, connecting with the sky, is already incredibly attractive to most people.
  3. Infrastructure. Where there are no more crowds of tourists, there are fewer restaurants and cafes, you should not be upset, the closest of them can be reached in 4-5 minutes. Food and drink shops are a little closer.
  4. Fame. As many might have guessed, there are much fewer people here, especially Russians, Ukrainians, and Belarusians. Most of all are representatives of sexual minorities. If you are not embarrassed by such people, then be sure to visit here at least once.

If you are looking for the best Pattaya beach with hotels, then be sure to pay attention to Dong Tarn Beach.

Cozie beach

A stunning beach, further away from the bustling city center. It is ideal for most tourists. If you are wondering where is the best Pattaya beach for families with children, then Cozy Beach will be a great option:

  1. Purity. Here is blue clear water. The sand is light beige in color, there is almost no debris on the beach, because there are large waste tanks nearby. Unfortunately, sometimes some algae are thrown ashore.
  2. Scenery. This beach is especially beautiful, with a beautiful view of the surroundings from its shore. On the one hand, the territory is surrounded by the foot of the gentle hill Pratamnak. On the other side you can see the distant line of the beach and dense tropical trees. It is especially beautiful here at the time of sunsets and sunrises, when the sky is painted in bright colors, thanks to sunlight.
  3. Infrastructure. For cheap food you will have to climb up a gentle staircase for about 2-4 minutes. If you do not want to go far, then you can buy food from passing traders, however, it will cost an order of magnitude more.
  4. Fame. There are much fewer people here. But at the peak of the tourist season, of course, a little more vacationers come.

Hotels such as Cozy Beach Hotel are located on this beach. There you can rent stunning apartments with an incredible design and a private plunge pool. The minimum cost of a room is approximately 13-15 thousand rubles.

In addition, there is a hotel Emerald Palace. A nice, cozy and fairly budget option, where the minimum cost of living is 8,000 rubles.

Wongamat

If you are wondering which Pattaya beach is better, then pay attention to this option. Wongamat Beach is a wonderful place that is located in the north of the province:

  1. Purity. One of the few beaches in the region that really can be called incredibly clean. The sand here is small, has an almost white color. It is carefully cleaned every day, because along the Wongamat Beach stretches a huge line of expensive and luxurious hotels. The water here is very clean, there is almost no debris.
  2. Scenery. The view of Wongamat Beach is bewitchingly beautiful, especially in the evening when the sun gives shade, and you can see many islands of the Gulf of Thailand in a reddish haze.
  3. Infrastructure. The only negative, which is due to the availability of expensive hotels, is that you need to go a fairly long distance to most shops and cafes. It is worth saying that those who want to spend time in silence and cleanliness must be prepared for this. Fortunately, if the budget allows, then you can settle in one of the hotels, then you will have to go to bars and restaurants in only 1-2 minutes.
  4. Fame. Most of the tourists here are tourists from hotels, so there are relatively few people.

Wongamat Beach - one of the best beaches in Pattaya with hotels. 5 * The most popular here is the hotel complex Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya. It has incredibly beautiful and spacious rooms, several restaurants with great food and plenty of entertainment.

Ban Ampur

The next place in the list of the best beaches in Pattaya for children is Ban Ampur. It really can be considered one of the most beautiful and clean. It is removed from the main city of the province and is located 15 km south. It can be reached by private car or tuk-tuk (the so-called fast public transport in Thailand). Features of the beach:

  1. Purity. It is very clean, the sand has a beautiful golden color. True, the entry into the sea is a little harsh, so almost immediately it will be pretty deep. The beach is surrounded by beautiful green palm trees, as in the advertisement of the well-known chocolate with bounty coconut, under their leaves you can successfully hide from bright sunlight.
  2. Scenery. It is very nice here, tall buildings are not an obstacle to a beautiful view.
  3. Infrastructure. She is very well developed here. On the beach there are saleswomen who deliver exotic fruits, unusual dishes and drinks of Thai cuisine. In the shade of palm trees you can lie down on a deck chair or in a hammock. By the way, there are several masseuses right on the beach who can do a magnificent relaxing massage.
  4. Fame. Many tourists know the beach, but not all reach it. That is why there are few people here.

Bang Saray

The next best beach near Pattaya is located 30 km south of the main city of the province. This is the main highlight of the small fishing town of the same name:

  1. Purity. Since there are no major roads nearby, the town specializes only in fishing and tourism, the beach here is incredibly clean. The sand has a slightly yellow hue, rather small and soft. Sunset at sea is very smooth, with a gradual increase in depth. The beach is perfect for relaxing with young children.
  2. Scenery. It offers a pretty nice view, especially if you look slightly from the side, in front we will see a long line of the beach, which is enveloped by the sea on one side and high palm trees limit it on the other. The best landscape here, of course, at sunset and dawn.
  3. Infrastructure. There are many small hotels in Bang Saray with shops. Very close are cozy restaurants with delicious Thai food. In addition, if you do not want to lie in the sand, you can rent a deck chair.
  4. Fame. The beach is very far from most entertainment, but there are also many different hotels that take part of the territory for their guests. Fortunately, even independent tourists can find a secluded corner here without extra people and stay alone with nature.

Koh Lan Island

As we already said, not far from Pattaya is the picturesque island of Koh Lan, on which there are beaches with crystal clear emerald green water and white sand. There you can feel what a real paradise is. That is why, if you are wondering where is the best beach in Pattaya to relax, then be sure to look at this island.

You can get to Koh Lan very quickly, for example, by ferry, which runs several times a day. Travel time no more than 25 minutes. Also note the following features:

  1. Purity. There is magnificent water, beautiful sand. The beaches are simultaneously surrounded by rocky hills and tropical trees. Sunset at sea is smooth, the depth of 1-1.5 meters begins only after 5-10 meters from the coast.
  2. Scenery. Mountains, tall palm trees, the azure sea and white sand together create a stunning picture, like from advertisements on TV. The landscape of this area will delight many, even the most experienced tourists.
  3. Infrastructure. Life on the island is very developed. There are many hotels, clubs and other entertainment venues, so there are no problems with the variety of shops, cafes and restaurants.
  4. Fame. There are many tourists, but the beaches are huge, so everyone is comfortable.

If you decide to ever visit the island of Koh Lan, then be sure to visit the beaches of Monkey Beach and Samae Beach. They are very similar to each other, however, the first is somewhat smaller and more deserted.

Crescent Beach

The place with an incredibly romantic name is located north of Wongamat, it can be reached by public transport - tuk-tuk. Travel time is approximately 15-20 minutes. In fact, it is relatively small, stretching for about 500 meters. The crescent beach is located in a small bay, so the sea is very quiet and clean, there is no garbage at all. The view here is incredibly beautiful, because the territory is surrounded on all sides by low green hills. There are beautiful hotels right on the coast that give the local landscape a certain charm and color.

If we talk about infrastructure, then here it is very well developed. There are many cozy restaurants and cafes on the shore. There are not many people here, even in the tourist season, therefore, on the beach of the crescent moon, with 100% confidence, you can spend time alone with nature, enjoy the peace and beauty.
