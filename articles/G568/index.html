"Sunny Valley" in Minsk: rest all year round

"Sunny Valley" in Minsk is a place to relax for the whole family all year round. The ski resort will give a vivid experience to all visitors of any age.

"Sunny Valley" in Minsk: how to get there and how it works

The recreation center is located surrounded by a beautiful landscape in the fresh air. After the bustle of the city, this place will seem like a real paradise in peace and quiet. On the river bank in a quiet area is the ski resort "Solar Valley" in Minsk.

The recreation center is open on weekdays from 12.00 to 22.00, on weekends and holidays - from until 22.00. Tickets are sold at the box office until 21.00. The inventory rental shop is open until 20.30.





There are several ways to get to the "Sunny Valley" in Minsk. The address of the resort is st. Kazintsa, 81. On a private car you need to move to Kazintsa Square on Independence Avenue.

In this area there is public transport, the stop "Cinema Electron":

  • Buses
  • trolleybuses;
  • minibus taxi.

Also near the resort complex is the stop "Kozintseva Square". The transport listed above also goes to her.

other services

"Sunny Valley" takes orders for corporate events. To do this, you can reserve a stay in the gazebos. Administration at the request of customers can take on an entertainment program. Animators will hold entertaining contests and relay races. The local cafe offers a delicious menu.

Often, large corporations hold sports competitions here among their employees. Thus, the team rallies and actively rests after difficult weekdays.

The complex has organized places for camping. So, guests can spend several days here. On the base you can purchase a gift certificate for a certain amount. Such a gift will be a pleasant surprise and an occasion to begin to actively relax in the fresh air.

Visitors reviews

You can find many reviews about the work of the complex. Basically, they are negative - visitors complain about sloping tracks and rude communication between employees and customers.

Guests also note the untidy area around the gazebos. For visitors who want to leave the territory of the complex earlier due to inconvenience, money is not returned.





Guests also complain about worn inventory. Parents believe that the tubing track is quite dangerous. They are outraged by the fact that there is no medical center. Also, the guests of the complex are surprised by the situation in which it is necessary to negotiate with the instructors personally by telephone, and not through the administration.

Nevertheless, for objectivity, it must be said that there are also positive reviews. Most often they come from people who do not pay attention to service and come here to relax and enjoy the beautiful nature.
