The eye rejoices

Sights of the island of Mauritius, reviews of which are always excellent, as a rule, are of natural origin. The capital of this small island nation is Port Louis. The city was founded by the French. On the one hand, the city is surrounded by mountains, on the other - there is a bay, the picturesqueness of which will not leave tourists indifferent. The observation deck, which is located in Fort Adelaide, is also popular with them. It was erected by the colonialists and today is used for colorful shows. The administrative buildings in the colonial style and located in the city center Arms Square are part of urban architecture, which captivates the views of vacationers. There are also Chinese and Muslim quarters, and they are also of interest to tourists. Leo Bay, with the Lion Mountain above it, is another attraction of the city. The plaster statue of Laval, touching which you can count on luck, is another attraction of the place where the tomb of the founder of Catholicism in Mauritius is also located.
