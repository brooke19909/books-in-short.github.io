Shvets Alexander - Moscow wedding photographer

Shvets Alexander is by far the most popular Moscow photographer. Alexander has been improving himself in this direction for many years. In 1998, he opened his own personal photo studio. The main directions in his work became the wedding genre, as well as the genres "Love Story" and "Family Story." In a professional studio you can also take pictures in the style of "reportage". Alexander Shvets loves working with people in his profession, and he does it very well.



Experience and Achievements

Shvets Alexander has extensive experience in working with photography. In 1994, he was educated at VGIK as a cameraman. For several years after that he worked as a full-time photographer at Mosfilm. Alexander was also a member of the international wedding program, which was organized by the Kodak company. And in 2011 he was invited by Konstantin Dyachkov to the Parsuna individual project. In this project, they together held a series of photo sessions of group portraits in historical style. This project was recognized by the Russian Orthodox Church as particularly significant for the history of Russia.



Today, Shvets Alexander also specializes in creating beautiful photo albums and photo books on any number of pages.

You can agree in advance on the sketch of the future publication, its form and select all the best photos. Further work will be done by experienced studio designers.

Reviews on the work of Alexander Shvets

On the Internet, Alexander Shvets, a photographer, receives huge reviews. Almost always, these are positive feedback from satisfied customers. There you can also find a lot of his works and see in what unusual but very beautiful style he captures such important moments for everyone as a wedding, engagement, anniversary, etc.

Alexander is very anxious and painstakingly approaches work with each client separately. He carefully discusses all the nuances of a future photo shoot so that the client could not have any further disappointment. Thanks to this, all the photographs taken by Alexander are high-quality, vivid, and the emotions of the main characters in them look very lively and real.

The cost of work

The contract with Alexander for 4 hours will be approximately 26 thousand rubles. You can hire him to capture your entire holiday or conduct a solitary photo shoot away from prying eyes. Also, Alexander can go to work in another city or even in another country with you, but the cost of such work will need to be discussed separately.
