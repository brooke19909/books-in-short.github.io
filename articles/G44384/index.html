Selfie stick: what phones are suitable and what types are

Every day more and more people become fans of social networks. Here everyone can choose the settings to their taste, however, almost all profiles are invited to set a photo. In the social media news feed, users are used to sharing parts of their lives and uploading a lot of photos. One of the most popular ways to create a photo is selfies. For those who are not familiar with this concept, we explain: selfies are photographing oneself.

Selfies

There are several types of such photos: in the mirror or using the front camera. But very often it happens that, holding the phone, tablet, camera or other device in hand, it is not possible to capture the whole background on which you want to take a beautiful photo. Or, say, a very large group of friends wants to take a picture, but not all fit into the frame.

Then a selfie stick comes to the rescue. Another name for the device is a monopod. So, which selfie stick is suitable for which phones and what kind of miracle invention is it? We will talk about this in the article.





Varieties of fixtures

Before answering the question for which phones the selfie stick is suitable, you need to determine the type of device that you are going to purchase or that already exists. And there are several varieties of monopod. Briefly tell about the most popular of them.

Simple tripod

The first type of monopod to be discussed is a tripod. In fact, this is an ordinary stick, made of more or less durable material with a device for attaching the phone to the end. In general, these are all its advantages, if you do not take into account the fact that the cost of such a selfie stick will be quite cheap compared to electronic devices. But for a budget option is very suitable.

However, be prepared for the fact that whenever you want to take a picture, you will have to set a timer on the camera. What is good - you can use such a tripod even if you do not have a modern gadget and you use a simple phone with a camera or a camera.





The gadget is fixed at the top and bottom, but it is worthwhile to warn that the device may fall out, so it is better to keep it horizontal and try not to rotate. These are the limitations that this selfie stick implies. What phones is this object suitable for? For almost anyone, if only it could be fixed at the end of the tripod, and it is desirable that there is a timer on the camera.





Stick with button

It is the same tripod, but the kit comes with a small remote with two buttons. For which phones is a selfie stick with a button suitable? In this case, there are nuances, because far from all devices this product fits. To understand whether your device is compatible with this model, you need to clarify this circumstance directly at the place of purchase from competent people. For example, from sales consultants, if a monopod is purchased at an electronics store or a mobile phone store.

We strongly recommend not ordering a selfie stick on unfamiliar sites or without the possibility of a return, because the client risks that the monopod and gadget will be incompatible, respectively, the selfie stick will not work.

But back to the remote. Two buttons are standardly located on it - one for devices running Android, the other for iOS. It is important not to confuse when photographing something at a crucial moment.
