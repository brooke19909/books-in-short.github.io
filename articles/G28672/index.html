Holidays on the Black Sea coast has a lot of advantages. The village of Veselovka is the best place for a bright and informal vacation



In the Temryuk district of the Krasnodar Territory, between Taman and Anapa, there is a real oasis, ideal for a laid-back summer vacation - a village with the speaking name Veselovka. The Krasnodar Territory seems to have been created for spending holidays and enjoying life, but this place captivates with its own special atmosphere. Especially this village is liked by fans of "wild" relaxation and lovers of peace and tranquility. Wide sandy beaches, plenty of greenery, lack of ultramodern entertainment centers and 5 * hotels. This place is suitable for tourists of all ages and will delight everyone with a variety of pastime options.

What to do during the holidays?



The village of Veselovka (Krasnodar Territory), despite its reputation as a quiet and calm place, from time to time boasts an active life. This is where the Taman bike show and the KUBANA music festival are held annually. Of course, these events last a few days, and after their completion, the village returns to its usual calm. Well-maintained and spontaneous campsites, excellent opportunities for windsurfing and kitesurfing - all this can be offered to its guests by Veselovka. Krasnodar Territory is a place where everyone will find something to their liking. Due to the lack of large entertainment centers and nightclubs, Veselovka is also suitable for recreation of older people. However, in the tourist season there are enough cafes and bars, mini-water parks for children, and all the traditional entertainment on the beach.

Cozy hotels, resorts and private accommodation



Despite the small size of the settlement itself, Veselovka (Krasnodar Territory) offers a sufficient number of options for accommodating holidaymakers. Here there are cottage complexes, and small cozy hotels with decent service, as well as holiday homes and guest houses. Many locals offer rental housing for visitors on very favorable terms. It remains only to choose according to the level of comfort, price and distance from the beach. If you are used to maximum convenience, a great option for living is a recreation center. Veselovka (Krasnodar Territory) is a village in which there are enough such complexes designed for the seasonal accommodation of vacationers, there are also motels and pensions. Almost all recreation centers in the village delight vacationers with fresh repairs, good living conditions, a landscaped area and the possibility of accommodation with meals.

Tourists reviews



A huge number of tourists from all over our country come to Veselovka every year to rest. Most of them are satisfied with their vacation spent in this place. The village pleases with clean beaches and its provincial atmosphere. It is incredibly cozy, if you wish, you can fully enjoy the beauties of nature, and if it becomes boring, go to a disco, to one of the local cafes or walk through noisy tourist shops. Locals, like most southerners, are quite hospitable. If, on the contrary, you like fun and always be in the thick of things, do not be too lazy to book accommodation in advance and go on vacation during one of the local festivals. A lot of new impressions and interesting acquaintances are provided to you. The village of Veselovka (Krasnodar Territory) offers a varied vacation. And if you are tired of the most popular tourist cities of the Black Sea coast, be sure to try to relax in the local "province".
