Fairytale vacation in Lazarevsky

Lazarevskoye resort is a beautiful and hospitable place, one of the largest areas of Sochi. It is located a little north of the central part of the Olympic city, at a distance of only 70 km. All the beaches of Lazarevsky, whose length is approximately 7 kilometers, are covered with pebbles.
