"Leader Park" (Mytishchi) - a modern residential quarter located near the main city of the state

Moscow is a huge metropolis located in the heart of the state. People from other cities flock here in the hope of earning money or moving to a permanent place of residence. But the cost per square meter of housing in the capital is too high for a simple layman. Therefore, many prefer to purchase housing outside the Moscow Ring Road, within transport accessibility. New buildings in the Moscow region are growing very fast. For example, "Leader Park" (Mytishchi).

Location of the residential complex

The developer of the complex chose a very good place for his project. Just a little more than six kilometers separates the complex from the Moscow Ring Road. An undoubted advantage is the possibility of a direct exit to the Volkovskoye highway, which connects with the Moscow Ring Road. Travel time does not take more than 15 minutes. You can get to the LCD "Leader Park" in Mytishchi and on other highways - Yaroslavsky and Ostashkovsky.

In addition, the future residents of the complex are offered picturesque views of the Rupasovsky Ponds, Pirogovsky Forest Park, where it is so nice to relax with your family in the fresh air, fry the barbecue. Not far from the "Leader of the Park" in Mytishchi, the Klyazma River flows, where you can plunge on a hot day.
