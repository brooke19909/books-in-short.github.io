Lunapark (Odessa) - vacation for the whole family

Every adult knows a place where he again turns into a child. This is a lunapark. Odessa offers residents and visitors to visit some of the best entertainment places in Ukraine. Thousands of visitors flock here every day. A variety of entertainment, fireworks of emotions, an ocean of joy and the magical nature of the Black Sea coast - all this can be found here.

Lunapark (Odessa) offers attractions both for the smallest visitors and for their mothers, dads and even grandparents. It is so difficult to resist the atmosphere of universal fun and not ride a huge Ferris wheel or experience nerves on a roller coaster. And this is not a complete list of what the lunapark (Odessa) offers.

Address and Location

This entertainment center is located in Shevchenko Park on Uspenskaya street, 1. It is almost the center of the city, and in close proximity to the famous Langeron beach.





You can get to the park by any convenient means of transport from anywhere in the city. Convenient location in the center, and even not far from the coast makes it even more popular with visitors.

Lunapark (Odessa), whose working hours are from 11 a.m. to 10 p.m., is open all year round.

All this time in the park, a cheerful laugh does not cease, because here, in addition to a variety of attractions, entertaining visitors are funny clowns, funny mimes, the characters of their favorite cartoons, as well as other easily recognizable heroes.

For the smallest

Kids in the lunapark (Odessa) are in a hurry with pleasure, because there for them there are electric cars, various carousels for children, fascinating labyrinths and bright trampolines. You can even control a huge waterfowl in a large pool. Swan boats are available for preschool kids only when accompanied by adults.

For older

Not only children will be interested in an amusement park. The organizers also did not forget about adolescents and adults. Every year new entertainments appear for them, so that each new trip with children to the park gives a new experience. Roller coasters, a Ferris wheel, a room of fear, bomber jacks, electric cars - all this will please and give an excellent mood for a long time.

Ferris wheel

The highest wheel in Ukraine is located in Odessa. It is called very romantic "South Palmyra." Its height is 45 meters. Lunapark (Odessa), whose bird's-eye view is breathtaking, is visible in the palm of your hand. A little further - the endless sea, a wonderful panorama of the pearl of the coast ...

Roller coaster

One look at this attraction induces involuntary fear. But it is worth overcoming the first bend, as he is replaced by a surge of adrenaline, and a wave of fun covers. After hire inexpressible lightness and euphoria are felt.

In the evening, this adventure becomes even more exciting, because the attraction burns with bright lights, adding a pungent sensation. Employees conduct regular inspections of equipment, which is performed by analogy with the best modern attractions in Europe and America.

Lunapark in winter

Even in winter, the lunapark is not empty. The rink works in it. It is easy to get to the city center, so there are lines of people who want to skate in the fresh air.





The park has many cafes and restaurants where you can relax and gain strength for new adventures.

Locals love to visit the lunapark (Odessa). Reviews say that children like different carousels and trampolines. Adults also have something to do while the kids have fun. Some, however, complain that ticket prices are high, although compared to other amusement parks they are not higher, and sometimes even lower. All equipment undergoes continuous testing and maintenance and complies with strict European safety standards.

In the lunapark, children and adults fall into a fairy tale. They get joy by spending time together and enjoying the rest.
