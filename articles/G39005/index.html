Prestige Garden Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Marmaris): traveler reviews

When choosing a place for a long-awaited annual vacation or spending summer family vacations, be aware that one of the worthy options is the resort of Marmaris. The perfect combination of a favorable climate with no hot heat, a warm calm sea, located in a closed bay, attract tourists annually. The coastline is separated from the open waters of the Aegean Sea by islands and a peninsula.

The unlimited mountain cascades give a special charm to the tourist center. Picturesque secluded bays, surrounded by forests, allow you to enjoy the untouched nature and amazing relief forms.

Here is one of the longest embankments in Turkey, dotted with cafes and bars. Here everyone will find something to their liking. In addition to party disco in the night, you can visit a number of attractions in the daytime. The offered housing corresponds to different income levels: there are both economical three-star and five-star luxury hotels.





Where to live?

Hotel Prestige Garden Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) is located on the second coastline. It is represented by a single multi-story building. The main entrance is well lit at night and surrounded by greenery in the form of stunted palm trees and exotic shrubs. The exterior of the seven-story building in the form of the letter "G" has a traditional design without frills and additional elements. On the territory there are two pools and a mini water park. For the convenience of travelers, the building is equipped with elevators. Pets are strictly forbidden, as well as smoking in rooms. Check in is done at standard time - 14:00.

In order to get to the hotel complex after the flight, it is necessary to overcome a distance of 100 km. The city market is a little further than 2 km away. The large Blue Port shopping center, located within walking distance, will diversify leisure activities and make purchases.





Conditions and comfort

The room fund of the Prestige Garden Hotel 4 * hotel (Marmaris) includes 100 Standard rooms. There are family apartments. The interior has a decent decoration and expensive decoration. The walls are decorated with works by local artists. The floors are covered with white ceramic tiles of good quality. In the color palette, preference is given to pastel colors. There is access to a balcony equipped with plastic furniture for taking air baths and comfortable morning tea drinking. French glazing fills the room with light and visually enlarges the space.

Housekeeping is provided daily by the hotel maid. They change linen twice a week. There is the possibility of ordering food in the room and the wake-up service, which allows those who like to sleep to wake up at the scheduled time.

What is in the room?

In order to make your stay comfortable and carefree, there is everything you need for a pleasant stay in the apartment. The presence of an individual split system allows you to configure the desired climate. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is featured. The phone contributes to lightning fast problem solving, thanks to the possibility of communication with the reception. The safe will keep important documents and money safe and sound. The kitchen area includes a suite with a microwave, an electric kettle, a sink, a refrigerator and a set of dishes.

The furniture set consists of soft beds, armchairs, a sofa, a coffee table, countertops, chairs, a wardrobe, a curbstone and bedside tables. Some have bunk beds.

The bathroom is equipped with a shower, sink and hairdryer. Toiletries and towels are provided. Wi-Fi - free, available throughout. Ironing facilities are available upon request.





Nutrition

According to the current All-Inclusive system at the Prestige Garden Hotel 4 * (Turkey), meals are served according to the buffet system three times a day. A wide range of meat, vegetable and fish dishes, fruits and cereals, desserts and snacks can be enjoyed in the main restaurant. As guests note in their positive reviews, the food here is amazingly delicious. The restaurant has an indoor lounge and an outdoor terrace. Bulk floors, multi-level relief ceilings, spotlights, exclusive curtains and elegant serving will delight every visitor of this institution.

There is also an à la carte restaurant, a snack bar and a poolside bar. Rest in these establishments will satisfy your hunger, thirst and enjoy wonderful cocktails from bartenders, able to surprise the taste characteristics of drinks of each guest.

Highchairs are provided for all babies. There is a special diet menu.





Aquazone

The undoubted highlight of this hotel complex, as guests note in their reviews of the Prestige Garden Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), is the extensive swimming area, which includes a main swimming pool of 150 m2. It is filled with fresh water, has a gradual increase in depth and convenient steps for descent. Nearby is a children's pool with a shallow depth. This allows both parents and children to simultaneously enjoy the long-awaited vacation. They are surrounded in a circle by comfortable sun loungers, which contribute to getting a tan and enjoying relaxation. For lovers of shadow spaces - umbrellas.

A distinctive feature is the presence on a compact territory of a mini-water park, which includes two bright descents. The possibility of skiing gives vacationers rainbow emotions and a good mood.





Beach

Marmaris is a great option for healing kids. The entrance to the water is gentle, which allows children to run and frolic at shallow water with safety for their health. The coast is represented by coarse sand. Infrastructure is developed: there is a toilet, a dressing room and showers. You can diversify your time by playing volleyball or visiting water attractions, the most popular of which are scooters, banana rides and snorkeling.

Entertainment

For outdoor enthusiasts, it is proposed to visit the gym, equipped with modern equipment in the form of cardiovascular equipment and treadmills. Instructors will help in organizing the correct load on the body, which will not only improve it, but also help to find elegant forms. On the territory of the Prestige Garden Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) there is a sauna, a bathhouse and a hammam. Many guests prefer to diversify the evening with a game of billiards or table tennis. For kids, a game complex in the fresh air is installed.





Additional services

Prestige Garden Hotel 4 * reviews the positive plan very often gets for the quality of the additional services provided. Inside the main building there is a currency exchange and a tour desk. Dry cleaning and laundry services are paid extra. For all arrivals by car - free parking. It is possible to accommodate people with disabilities.
