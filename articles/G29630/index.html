Yamaha: synthesizer as a piano and piano combination

Long before the appearance of articulate words, as well as familiar phrases, mankind came to know music. This wonderful combination of sounds of various instruments pleases its fans now. There is not much difference: whether you are a man or woman, an adult or a child, how old you are or what your status is, everyone worships music. The difference is only in the fans attracted by the directions. Currently, a variety of musical groups delight us with their creativity. Moreover, the number of instruments used in ensembles also varies. The well-known electric guitar, drum kit or keyboards are adjacent to exotic banhu, mridanga and djembe. Moreover, many of the tools used are produced in a single copy and especially for this artist.



Pioneer Keyboard Devices

There are a huge number of companies specializing in the production of musical devices and mechanisms. One of these fairly popular brands is Yamaha. A synthesizer, guitar, violin, piano and many other musical instruments were released under the logo of the concern. It is noteworthy that this Japanese company is famous not only as a manufacturer of high-quality melodic accessories - sports salons, showrooms for two-wheeled vehicles and electronic equipment stores are full of goods under this brand.

Almost 127 years ago, the first organ that Torakasu Yamaha designed was released. The synthesizer appeared much later. However, it was 1887 that marked the beginning of the development of the production of keyboards of excellent quality under this name.



Japanese Character Synthesizers

It should be noted that many modern companies occupying a similar market niche cannot boast such excellent quality products, tested by time and consumers, which corresponds to Yamaha tools. The synthesizer of this brand has come a long way in its development. The line of the lineup of this musical instrument is quite wide and diverse. There are models for both beginners and high-class specialists. The main distinguishing advantage of any of the synthesizers is its high quality. It is noteworthy that even the instruments produced in Korean branches fully comply with the stated requirements. It should be noted that many companies offer only breadboard models of instruments for a small price, while Yamaha is a full-size synthesizer and a complete set. Moreover, the sound of this Japanese instrument can be envied even by higher standing analogues.



"Ancestor" of world hits

The most popular model released under the Japanese brand is the Yamaha DX7 synthesizer. The date of birth of this instrument refers to the eighties of the twentieth century. Low cost, classic design, excellent sound and quite convenient use - these are the characteristics that distinguished this synthesizer from a number of competitive devices. The sound of the musical keyboard mechanism is based on the combination of the sounds of a piano and a piano. For the described model, the unity of tones was invented at Stanford University in 1970 and was called “Fm – synthesis”. Thanks to this instrument, a huge number of amazing hits were recorded, which, without exaggeration, can be called world hits. With the help of 16-voice polyphony, the world saw the tunes of foreign Queen groups (“Show must go on”, “Who wants to live forever”), Modern Talking (“Doctor for my heart”) and others. Artists of the domestic variety also used the Yamaha DX7 synthesizer in their work. Thanks to this instrument, the public fell in love with the works from the albums of Alla Pugacheva “Happiness in Personal Life”, Igor Nikolaev “Mill”, Sofia Rotaru “It Was, But Passed” and many others.



Synthesizers CS

Currently, the range of Yamaha synthesizers has greatly increased and represents a huge variety of devices for composing music in any direction. Moreover, each subspecies of the tools represents a "family", united under a common name. So, the CS series synthesizers are a great option for those who want to know themselves in writing modern, full of rhythms and bits of dance music. This device is perfectly adapted to work with a mixer, computer, and other devices. The synthesizers of this “family” are great for various club parties.

The most common and popular among buyers is the second-generation model. Thanks to about thirty percussion instruments and more than seven hundred timbres, this device is an excellent device for creating electronic music.

DGX Series Tools

According to consumers, the most successful series of Yamaha synthesizers is a group united under the common name DGX. A wide and comfortable keyboard will be an ideal platform for those who are accustomed to using notes when creating their own quiet tunes or playing world classics. Why exactly this direction? Because calm tones and melodic polyphony lure to play only sensual and lyrical masterpieces.





"Families" PSR, MO, Motif

The Yamaha PSR synthesizers are another series of musical instruments that synthesize the bewitching piano sounds and powerful piano power. Devices of this "family" are most often more in demand for home use. The reason for this is the presence of all the necessary functions for training (auto accompaniment, polyphonic row, sequencer and others) and a surprisingly low cost. Moreover, the quality remains at the highest level. Having this tool and acquiring the "Synthesizer Game Tutorial", you can easily master the magic of this tool. In addition to devices available to any buyer, this series includes musical mechanisms of the highest categories. Synthesizers 1100, 1500, 2100 and other "thousandth" copies stand apart in the PSR model range. They are great for professional musicians and recording studios.

The new series that have only recently appeared on the market are the MO and Motif series. The tools of both the first and second categories are ultra-modern mechanisms for professional work on stage, in the studio or even at home.
