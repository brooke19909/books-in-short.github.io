Home appliance installation

The washing machine Beko WKB 51031 PTMA reviews are called convenient and practical. According to users, it has all the necessary washing modes, and when correctly installed, it works completely silently.

Before starting the device in operation, the machine must be installed. It will be better if the installation of the unit is carried out by an experienced specialist. Before installation, make sure that the drain and water supply in the house are functioning properly.

Before installation, the washing machine is inspected for defects. If they are found, you must contact the service center and do not install the device. When moving the washing machine to the installation location, the power cord cannot be bent, torn or pinched. This also applies to water hoses.

The washing machine is installed only on a hard surface. It must not be placed on a carpet or soft surface. Do not place the device on the power cord in places where the temperature may drop below 0 ¬∞ C. The gap between the appliance and the rest of the furniture in the room should be at least 1 cm.

Before starting up the appliance, the packaging fasteners must be removed. To do this, the machine is tilted back, and then pulled by the packing tape. The transport bolts are removed only after removing the packaging fasteners. Operation of the washing machine with transport bolts is strictly prohibited. Their dismantling is carried out in the following sequence:

  • Use a wrench to loosen the bolts so that they hang loose.
  • After loosening the transport bolts, they are carefully turned and removed.
  • Plastic plugs cover the formed holes.

The washing machine functions stably at a water pressure of 1-10 bar. At this pressure, the water flow rate is 10-80 liters per minute. At higher pressure, a pressure reducing valve is installed in the water supply network.

The hoses included in the kit are connected to the water inlets of the washing machine. The red hose is connected on the left to where the pipe for hot water is located. It withstands temperatures up to 90 ¬∞ C. Blue is connected to the right, to the cold water pipe. It withstands temperatures up to 25 ¬∞ C. After connecting the hoses, all nuts are tightened manually without using a wrench.

After all the hoses are connected, turn on the water and check their connection for leaks. If a leak occurs, unscrew the nut, check the gasket and tighten it again, more tightly.

The end of the drain hose is connected to the sewer or lowered into the sink. The hose is attached to the sewer at a height of 40-100 cm. At a lower position, the water will be drained with difficulty, and the laundry will remain damp in the tank. The end of the hose must not be submerged more than 15 cm into the drain hole. With greater immersion, dirty water will flow back into the machine.

If necessary, a short hose can be extended. When building, use a clamp. The total length of the drain hose must not exceed 3.2 m.

For quiet operation of the washing machine, without strong vibrations, you should adjust the position of the household appliance. To do this, loosen the locknuts on the legs and adjust the height of the legs by level, so that the device does not stagger. After proper installation of the machine, all nuts are tightened by hand. The machine should only work on earthed outlets equipped with a fuse.
