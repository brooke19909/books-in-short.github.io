Location

Toila Spa Hotel is located in Estonia. Estonia is a country in Northern Europe, located on the eastern coast of the Baltic Sea, washed by the waters of the Gulf of Finland and Riga. More specifically, Toila Spa Hotel (Estonia) is located in Toila. Toila is a small village located on the southern shore of the bay. It is a state seaside resort.

The holiday season here is relatively short. It begins in mid-June and ends in mid-August. Despite the low temperatures, the plant world is rich, a lot of greenery. That is why there is where to walk and enjoy nature.

The hotel itself is located in the center of the village, 300 meters from the main attraction. This attraction, of course, is the shore of the Gulf of Finland. The coast is steep, rises 30 meters above sea level. The hotel is located at: Ranna 12, 41702, Toila, Estonia. Many people wonder how to get from Narva to Toila Spa Hotel? Just by bus number 106 or taxi.

Number of rooms

It's time to talk about the Toila Spa Hotel rooms. First, I want to note that the hotel has 3 stars. It was built in 1989, in 2000 a complete restoration was carried out. This hotel is a white building in a modern style, 9 floors high.

The hotel has 166 rooms, all of which are executed in a classic hotel style in soothing colors. Premium rooms have a more stylish and thoughtful design.

  1. Double Room. It accommodates two adults, and a child under 5 years old is also allowed. Room size is 15 m 2 . There are options, both with two single beds, and with one large double bed. This room has a private bathroom, satellite TV, ironing facilities and a hairdryer. The cost of such a number starts at 7300 rubles.
  2. Single Room.
    A small room in bright colors for one, its area is 15 m 2 . The cost starts from 5300 rubles.
  3. Junior Suite Spacious room for two. It has a TV, a private bathroom and a living area. Room rates start at 10 500 rubles.

Nutrition

So, good and tasty food always indicates the high status of the hotel. The Club Spa Toila has several food systems.

  • Meals are not included in the price. This is the most budget option, because you will not have to pay for food at the hotel.
  • Good breakfast included. Breakfast is served buffet style in the hotel restaurant. Guests can choose what they will eat. Breakfast includes dishes for vegetarians and those who are losing weight. It can be various cereals, vegetables, fruits, desserts, yogurts, cereals, omelettes and cheesecakes, as well as various freshly squeezed juices, teas and coffee.
  • Breakfast and dinner included. The most expensive food option, but here you get two meals at once on the buffet system. For dinner, side dishes, salads and desserts are served, as well as a variety of drinks.

Paid services

Here, as in any other hotel, there are paid services that may be necessary for guests.

  1. Mini golf. There is a golf course on site. For this entertainment you will have to pay a certain amount of money.
  2. Billiards. In addition to golf, the hotel provides the opportunity to play billiards. It is worth saying that you can rent several tables at once.
  3. Massage. Each guest can use the paid massage service. Qualified masters will do any massage, for example, therapeutic, tonic, relaxing, anti-cellulite. In addition, guests themselves can choose the duration of the massage, for example, 10, 30, 45, 60 minutes.
  4. SPA-center. The hotel has a fairly large spa, each vacationer can use it by paying a certain amount. It provides cosmetic services, makeup artist or hairdresser. There is also a huge selection of caring procedures.

Free services

Did you think that the hotel paid absolutely everything? No, you were mistaken, it also offers a huge range of services for free.

  1. Fitness Centre. Each hotel guest can use the modern gym for free and without any restrictions. It has treadmills and exercise bikes, elliptical settings, a set of dumbbells, as well as exercise machines for working out individual muscle groups.
  2. Indoor pool.
    Since the weather is not always hot, and you want to relax in the water, the hotel provides its guests with the opportunity to visit the pool with a waterfall for free.
  3. Free wireless internet. It works around the clock throughout.

Near the hotel

What is a significant advantage of any hotel? This, of course, is a good location. That is why now we will talk about the attractions that are very close by.

  • Ontika Limestone cliff.
    Incredibly beautiful place. When you look at it, it‚Äôs hard to imagine what nature did. This place is a picturesque cape, located high above the sea. From its top offers a very beautiful view of the bay and the surrounding area. From our hotel it is only 12 km.
  • Meie Grocery Store.
    A large supermarket where you can buy absolutely everything. Huge selection of meat and fish, vegetables and fruits.
  • Toila Oru Park. This park was built in 1897-1899 by the St. Petersburg merchant Grigory Eliseev. Previously, the park was located near the merchant's palace, but during the Second World War it was completely destroyed. Now more than 250 species of plants grow in the park, not only European, but also exotic. Alleys, fountains, arbors and pavilions, that is what you can see here.

Reviews

Well, it's time to discuss the reviews of Toila Spa Hotel (Estonia). It is worth saying right away that there are both positive and negative assessments.

  • Positive reviews. Toila Spa Hotel reviews say that the hotel is always very friendly and friendly staff. A stunning location is also noted, next to the pine forest. Many enjoyed the breakfast, in particular the freshness of the ingredients and the large selection. In addition, guests really liked the spa, especially hydromassages.
  • Negative feedback. Vacationers have no strong complaints, there are only small flaws. Many tourists really lacked a kettle and drinking water in the room. The modesty of the rooms and the lack of direct access to the sea were also noted.
