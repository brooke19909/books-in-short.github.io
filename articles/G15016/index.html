In the residential complex, all objects of social and commercial-commercial infrastructure have been created: kindergartens, a school, shopping and entertainment centers, a clinic, banking departments, cafes and much more. All apartments are for rent with a fine finish, so buyers reduce their costs for subsequent interior decoration. The cost of apartments in the residential complex of St. Petersburg "Sunny City" starts from 1,900,000 rubles, which for St. Petersburg can be called a fairly affordable price.
