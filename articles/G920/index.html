"White Lake" (Moscow): rest, beach, bus schedule and reviews about the sanatorium

If you are bored with the bustle of the city or if you want to improve your health and gain strength - come to the departmental sanatorium "White Lake" (Moscow). This is not only the beautiful scenery of a picturesque lake, but also the opportunity to meet the sunset, appreciate the generosity of local waters and get an unforgettable experience of quality service.





Verify the veracity of this statement will help numerous reviews of tourists. The therapeutic and diagnostic boarding house (its official name is "White Lake") is rightfully considered one of the best in Russia. Travel packages are enormously popular. Sergei Esenin once wrote about the beauty and purity of the Meshchera lowland of the Shatursky district - there is truth in his poems. This is truly a legendary and highly sought after resort, designed both for budget holidays and for discerning tourists.

The main advantage of this place is its remoteness from the gas-polluted city, dusty highways and industrial plants. The sanatorium "White Lake" of the Moscow City Hall was built in the most often coniferous-deciduous park, about 150 km from the capital. The resort is unique in its pure ecology and the presence of mixed flora.

Number of rooms

The recreation center "White Lake" (Moscow) consists of a number of one-story houses called "Russian Village" and two multi-story buildings. The sanatorium is designed for 1233 people.

  • Building No. 1 is a 9-story building equipped with air conditioning. Almost all rooms have balconies overlooking the vegetation park. The rooms have a landline telephone, a private bathroom with hygiene and toilet facilities, as well as a kettle and a refrigerator.
  • Building No. 2 is a five-story building with several elevators and comfortable rooms. In the rooms you will find a small kitchen area and household appliances. There are spacious three-room apartments. Near the building is a children's play area.




  • Cottages "Russian Village": small villas are located separately from the rest of the buildings. Guests can choose both standard-class rooms and deluxe rooms with two rooms. All rooms have almost the same list of amenities and furniture.

Bars and Restaurants

The cost of a 10-day trip includes three meals a day, ordered meals. It should be noted that each residential building "White Lake" (Moscow) has its own dining area. The menu is developed by a nutritionist based on your diagnosis. There is also a restaurant and a bar in the health resort.





Services

Infrastructure dispensary provides a combination of modern and classic leisure. For relaxing relaxation, there is a four-lane pool, a bathhouse and a Finnish sauna. For beauty and health - SPA center with a variety of procedures: anti-cellulite wraps, mud and chocolate applications, vibrosaunas, stone therapy, massage. All cosmetics are natural.

To maintain fitness, the White Lake health resort (Moscow) offers to use the gym, you can seek help from an instructor. It offers vacationers - sports facilities, bowling, table tennis, rental point for rollers, bicycles, sledges, skis and skates. For those who wish, water aerobics is carried out in the pool.

Every day, musical, poetry, dance and cultural evenings are held on site. Organized contests, sweepstakes and humorous programs with gifts. For an additional fee, you can book a sightseeing tour or visit the cinema. For a secluded and relaxing pastime, pavilions were built in the middle of a pine forest, on the lake, where it is allowed to fry kebabs.





No less interesting and informative program is prepared for kids and adolescents. A large children's town was built, as well as a sports ground. There is a development club, a buffet and a hall for watching cartoons.

Medical profile

The diagnostic center is equipped according to European standards. People with musculoskeletal, musculoskeletal, and endocrinological pathologies are being treated. The health resort specializes in ophthalmic, nervous, gynecological, cardiological, dental and skin diseases.

The White Lake Prevention Center (Moscow) regularly receives thank-you comments on treatment and quality of service. The medical center has departments for functional examination, X-ray, ultrasound, ECG, biochemical and clinical laboratories. Therapy includes:





  • full professional consultation;
  • laboratory diagnostics;
  • taking healing mineral water;
  • therapeutic exercises, dosed walking, swimming, shower;
  • local massage;
  • heat and physiotherapy;
  • phytotherapy;
  • inhalation;
  • balneotherapy and hydrotherapy.

Effective cleansing, cosmetology and rehabilitation course programs are carried out.

Directions: Bus Schedules

White Lake (Moscow) is located 60 km from Shatura and 150 km from MKAD. There are several ways to get to it:

  • By public transport: drive to Shatura, from there a bus daily runs from the bus station at 12:15 and 21:15 (it is better to specify).
  • By electric train: we go to the Kazan station, take the train and get off at Shatura station - then by bus.
  • By private car: we move along the Egerovsky highway, turn off in the Kasimovsky direction on the ring and follow to the 151st km. There you will see signs towards the boarding house.

The medical dispensary has advanced technologies of foreign and domestic medicine, which makes it the best in its direction.
