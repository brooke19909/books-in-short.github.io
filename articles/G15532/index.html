Baan Vanida Garden Resort 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): overview, features, reviews of tourists

Baan Vanida Garden Resort 3 * hotel is located on the Thai island of Phuket, in the Karon area, in the resort village of Kata Beach. Its service and location attract many travelers. Closest to it is Karon Beach, and for entertainment it is best to go to Kata. People come here for a quiet life, relaxation and relaxation at sea, as well as for entertainment and adventure. The hotel is family-type, managed by husband and wife. Therefore, the atmosphere of home comfort prevails here, and in just a few days you feel as if you spent your whole life here.



Phuket, Kata Beach Area: Features

This third most popular area in Phuket (after Patong and Karon) is traditionally divided into three parts. Kata-Yai is a party district with nightlife, clubs and discos. The beaches here are also crowded, with developed infrastructure, often filled with surfers and divers. But the Kata Noi district, next to which is the Baan Vanida Garden Resort Karon 3 * hotel (Phuket), is quite quiet and calm. It is perfect for families with children. Nightlife is almost non-existent here. Not far from the beach is the so-called "Dinopark". This is a golf course with life-size sculptures of prehistoric animals. People come here not only to play, but also to take a walk, especially with children who are only happy to be in this Jurassic Park. Moreover, the dinosaurs are very realistic. There are several markets on Katya, including nightly. So, although there is less entertainment here than in Patong, tourists are not bored. Travelers from the Russian Federation often relax here, so you can find signs in Russian everywhere. And a kilometer from Kata, on a high hill, is the famous statue of the so-called Big Buddha, which can be seen even from the beach. Sightseeing tours in Phuket specially bring tourists to look at it. Nearby is the Wat Chalong Temple, where you can meditate, chat with the monks, and also tell fortunes for the future.



How to get there

From Phuket International Airport, you need to go to Karon for about 45 minutes if you order a private transfer or taxi. But in the case of public transport, you will have to spend more time. For example, to get from the hub to the Baan Vanida Garden Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket), you need about an hour and a half. It costs about 500 baht (approximately 1000 rubles). Reception desks selling tickets of different carriers are located directly in the arrivals hall. And taxi drivers are on duty right at the exit from the airport.

Territory

The building of the Baan Vanida Garden Resort 3 * hotel is surrounded by a garden with palm trees and tropical vegetation. The hotel has a parking for guests. The hotel area is small, but green, beautiful and tastefully decorated. Many exotic plants, large and small sculptures and other architectural forms. There are various viewing platforms where it is good to conduct photo shoots. In short, a miniature botanical garden that is carefully and daily looked after. The hotel is surrounded by small Thai houses. It is located in a residential area, far from any noise. It consists of several bungalows scattered throughout the territory, and a residential building with an elevator. Everything is very beautiful, decorated in Thai national style. Nearby are many shops, pharmacies, everything you need is a stone's throw away.



Rooms

Hotel Baan Vanida Garden Resort 3 * is very small. There are only 18 rooms. It has standard, superior rooms and suites. The rooms are good, air conditioned. LCD TV on the wall, satellite channels, good Wi-Fi. One Russian channel. There is a safe and a fridge in the room. The rooms are cleaned every day. Towels are also often changed. The rooms are very spacious and bright. Porcelain tiles on the floor, well polished. The beds are large and comfortable. There is an entrance hall, there are a lot of hangers in the wardrobe. Big mirror. There is an electric kettle, coffee, tea leaves, sugar, cups with spoons and saucers, glasses. Large balconies with a table, chairs and a clothes dryer. The air conditioner is comfortable, such as a "chiller", does not blow on the bed. There are many different lamps, so you can change the lighting mode in the room. The bathroom has a hairdryer and two types of shower - ordinary and tropical. Blackout curtains, so if you close them, you will get complete darkness even in bright sunshine.



Service and staff

Registration of guests and reception at the Baan Vanida Garden Resort Karon 3 * hotel are open 24 hours a day. At the hotel you can book a guided tour, rent a moped or scooter, and take the laundry to the laundry. A good massage parlor is open. A Spitz lives at the hotel, who loves tourists and, according to their reviews, looks like a living soft toy. The staff are very friendly, smiling. To communicate with them, you need to know English at least at the school level. Well, if absolutely nothing, just take a picture of what you need and show it at the reception. They will immediately understand and help you. If there are mosquitoes, you will be given an electrofumigator.



Nutrition

The restaurant of the Baan Vanida Garden Resort Karon 3 * hotel is called Plo Sai. There, for guests, breakfast is included in the price. They give fried eggs in different forms, ham, sausages, toast, rice, chicken, shrimp, yogurt, coffee from the machine. The fruit is mainly watermelon. Breakfast includes quiet music for relaxation. At very reasonable prices, you can also have lunch and dinner there. But many tourists prefer to have meals in other places, just for fun. Nearby there are a lot of places to “kill the worm”, as well as to eat fully, there are fruit stalls, ice cream for sale. Nearby there are Family Markt and Seven-Ileven chain stores, as well as a Macro supermarket. Tourists called the bonus of staying here that you can eat not only in a cafe or on the street, but also bring to the hotel what you bought, say, from the macaques, and are at the hotel tables. None of the staff will be in a complaint. You can also have dinner on the balcony in your room - there is a lot of space and not hot.



Sea

Hotel Baan Vanida Garden Resort 3 * is located on the second line. To the beach "Karon" from her walk about ten minutes. Get a little longer to Kata Beach. The first often has waves. The second is better to relax with children. But tourists claim that the "singing" the smallest sand of "Karon" can not be compared with anything. However, the "Kata" beach is very clean. The sand is white, good water and excellent infrastructure. There are many casuarine trees under which you can hide from the sun. Many sun loungers for rent at 100 butts (200 rubles) per day. They belong to local bars, so if you want something to drink or have a meal, the waiters will bring you all this directly to the beach. The most affordable restaurants - with real seafood - are located on the southern coast. There is also a bar where Ska night parties are held. It is built around a huge tree, and its doors open directly to the beach. Kata is not only a beautiful place, but also suitable for entertainment. Towels for sunbathing are issued at the reception. The hotel also has an outdoor pool with jacuzzi. There are sun loungers on the terrace. It is very cozy and has a homely atmosphere. There is a water massage and rain shower. In the evenings, poolside parties can be arranged.

Excursions

If you have already arrived in Phuket, then you should not sit all day even in such a cozy hotel as Baan Vanida Garden Resort 3 *. Reviews assure that you can get excellent excursions at local travel agencies, so your trip will be not only entertaining, but also informative. First of all, these are walks on the islands of the Andaman Sea, where you can get acquainted with cave temples, where monkeys live, visit the villages of local pearl hunters on stilts, admire the bizarre cliffs that wash the waves. A lot of time is given for swimming and relaxing on a beautiful landscaped beach. You can also visit the mysterious islands of the neighboring province of Krabi with its fabulous nature. But the most popular destination from Phuket is the Phang Nga region. It was in these places that the famous film from the series about agent James Bond was shot. Such excursions usually take all day. But there are longer trips, for 2-3 days, for example, to the Similan Islands. A Phuket sightseeing tour is most fun to do on motorbikes with a personal guide. So one day you can go around the most beautiful places, see the best beaches and swim in the waterfalls.



Baan Vanida Garden Resort Karon 3 *: reviews

Despite the modest "star", the rating of the hotel is quite high. Many tourists, including Russian ones, write that they would love to come back here again. The hotel is authentic, has its own style. Always quiet, no fuss, no drunken shouts, everything is calm. This is a great combination of exoticism and comfort. The atmosphere in Baan Vanida Garden Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) is pleasant and relaxing, the hotel is really created for a real holiday.
