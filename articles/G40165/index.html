Hotel - a monument of architecture

Park Hotel Romanova (Yevpatoriya) is a unique place to relax. The fact is that the hotel occupies the building of a two-story old mansion built in the distant 1914. In 2016, all buildings here were reconstructed, but the appearance of the facades and the interior of the mansion and the buildings adjacent to it were preserved.





The name "Romanova" (Park Hotel 3 *) was not accidental. Thanks to him, we are transported to 1916, when the whole imperial family arrived in Yevpatoria by train from Tsarskoye Selo. At that time, the hospital was located here, and the Empress Alexandra Fedorovna personally decided to take up the dressing of all the wounded soldiers who participated in the battles of the First World War. It was in honor of the wife of Emperor Nicholas II that the modern hotel got its name.

Grand opening

When all the work related to the reconstruction of the buildings and the preparation of the hotel for the reception of guests was completed, the grand opening of a new holiday destination for guests of Yevpatoria took place.

The festive cutting of the red ribbon took place on May 16, 2016. The event was also dedicated to the 100th anniversary of the visit of Emperor Nicholas II and his family to the Crimean resort. Besides the fact that the park-hotel Romanova (Yevpatoriya) was officially opened on this day, various festive events were held in the city in honor of this anniversary.

On this occasion, the head of the Russian Imperial House, Grand Duchess and Crown Prince, even arrived on the peninsula. Both of them participated in the opening of the hotel and were impressed not only by the organization of the holiday, but also by the beauty of the buildings, the surrounding area and the magnificent interiors in the rooms.

The way of vacationers to the hotel

The road of any tourist who chose to rest in Crimea and decided to stay at the Romanova Park Hotel will most likely begin with an airplane that will take him to the airport of Simferopol. Already from here it will be necessary to drive about 70 km to Yevpatoriya in order to be directly at the hotel.

For this, vacationers will need to use the intercity bus, which can be found at the Kurortnaya bus station. Once in Evpatoria, at the city bus station, you should transfer to a fixed-route taxi or bus at number 10 and take the bus to the Sanatorium Helios stop on it.

You can also always order a taxi that will take tourists straight to the hotel. You only need to tell the driver the exact address of the hotel: Yevpatoriya city, Kievskaya street, 48. However, this will cost more than a trip by public transport.

Great rooms to stay at the Romanova Hotel

For those who are looking for a relaxing and cozy holiday with all amenities, this hotel (Crimea, Yevpatoriya) will be an excellent choice. Park Hotel Romanova offers its guests a small number of bright rooms that will become a pleasant place for sleeping vacationers throughout their vacation.
