Description of the sanatorium in Yarovoy "Chemist"

Near the lake Big Yarovoye there is a sanatorium "Chemist". All year round they offer relaxation to everyone who wants to enjoy the beauties of the surrounding nature, take a break from city noise and get healthy.

general information





The sanatorium in Yarovoy has been open for over 46 years. For all this time, the health resort has established itself as one of the best, which has no analogues from the Far East to Siberia. The sanatorium accepts for rest and treatment all comers with children from the age of five years. The voucher is designed for 14-21 days.

Around the health resort is a large landscaped area, which is pleasant to walk not only in the evening, but also in the afternoon. And nearby is the famous Lake Yarovoye. It is only a hundred meters from the sanatorium. In summer, a sandy beach is accessible on its shore, where you can sunbathe or swim in the healing salt water. In winter, guests can enjoy skiing or horse riding in the area.

Number of rooms





To accommodate guests in the sanatorium in Yarovoy, two residential buildings are provided. One is four-story and the other consists of only one floor. In total, 150 beds are available in the sanatorium. Vacationers can check into the following rooms:

  • single and double "economy";
  • single and double first category;
  • double "studios";
  • double two-bedroom apartment category.

All rooms have a minimum set of furniture necessary for a comfortable stay: single and double beds, wardrobe, bedside tables. There is also a TV and a small fridge. Facilities on the floor are for economy category only. The remaining rooms are equipped with a bathroom with shower. Hot and cold water is supplied around the clock.

Services and entertainment





Tour arrivals take place on Thursdays, and on arrivals - on Mondays. The price includes accommodation, as well as food and basic medical procedures.

Among the services available to guests of the sanatorium in Yarovoy are:

  • free guarded parking;
  • wireless Internet;
  • cafe bar;
  • laundry;
  • taxi ordering;
  • purchase of air and train tickets;
  • transfer from the city of Omsk;
  • organization of excursions in the surroundings on a comfortable bus;
  • various cultural and entertainment programs;
  • library;
  • children's room;
  • babysitting services (only in summer).

For vacationers of the sanatorium there are various options for leisure activities:

  • gym;
  • sauna;
  • billiards;
  • Volleyball Court;
  • table tennis;
  • board games (chess, backgammon, checkers, dominoes);
  • small cinema hall;
  • concert and entertainment programs;
  • solarium;
  • cosmetology and manicure rooms;
  • horseback riding.

Among the available excursions are:

  • visiting the museum of the city of Slavgorod, the museum of the history of the village of Podsosnovo, as well as a mosque, church, the embankment of the Irtysh river and other attractions of Pavlodar;
  • aquaterrarium in the city of Slavgorod;
  • visiting the historical and cultural centers of Slavgorod.

Nutrition in the sanatorium "Chemist" (Spring) provides four meals a day diet. It is possible to place an order on a separate menu. Meals are served in the large dining room, open on the ground floor in the main building. Six meals a day are provided for children. On the second floor is the "Tea Room". From morning to evening, various teas and coffee are served here.

Health-improving measures





On the territory of the sanatorium "Chemist" (Yarovoye) work:

  • mud baths;
  • Ignalatorium
  • physiotherapy department;
  • various treatment rooms;
  • sauna with a small pool;
  • phytobar;
  • salt caves.

A freshwater swimming pool is located next to the dispensary.

The main directions of the sanatorium in Yarovoy are diseases of the skin and subcutaneous tissue, as well as the musculoskeletal system and joints. The sanatorium also provides therapeutic and preventive measures for patients with problems in the field of gynecology, respiratory organs, and the genitourinary system.

All vacationers are offered to undergo a course of mud therapy, physiotherapy, balneotherapy, herbal medicine, exercise therapy and galvanic mud therapy, hirudotherapy and so on. A large staff of medical personnel (doctors and nurses) observes the vacationers.

Location

There is a sanatorium "Chemist" at the address: Altai Territory, the city of Yarovoye, Lenin Street 19. You can get to the health resort from anywhere in Russia with flights to the cities of Barnaul or Novosibirsk. From there you need to catch a train or regular bus, which goes to Yarovoye. It can be Novosibirsk-Yarovoye, Omsk-Yarovoye, Belokurikha-Yarovoye and so on. By bus from the railway station of the city of Slavgorod, you can get a minibus number 2, next to the stop "Comfort". Just 50 meters from it are the main gate of the sanatorium. You can also take a taxi to the health resort.
