How to fold paper for snowflakes? Now let's figure it out.





1) Cut out a square of any size from paper.

2) Bend it diagonally. The neighboring edges should be clearly superimposed on each other, and the sheet should acquire the shape of a triangle.

3) Now fold it in the middle so that the shorter sides of the figure are in contact, and the long one is divided into two identical halves.

4) Draw with a pencil a barely noticeable dash that does not completely intersect the triangle. It should go from the fold line and not slightly reach the opposite side.

5) Carefully walk the scissors to the marked place.

6) Fully unfold the paper. Before you again should be a square.

7) Glue together the tips of the corners located in the center.

8) And now in the same way connect the external parts, but at the same time turn them in the opposite direction. That is, they should be like a mirror image of the first fragment. The first ray is ready.

9) Make six more parts in the same way and connect them in the center with a stapler.

10) Fix the elements in contact with each other with glue.

Everything, a beautiful snowflake-pinwheel is ready! You can make it white or make each ray of paper of a different color. And decorating the product with paints or felt-tip pens will be an excellent entertainment for children.
