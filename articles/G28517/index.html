Buryatia - the land of mountains and beauty

Bizarre mountains or deep pits, snow caps at the peaks and flowering greens at the foot, turbulent rivers and clean air - these unique conditions are presented to us by the hospitable Buryatia. The climate in these parts is sharply continental: in winter it is very cold, but summer gives so much sun that you will feel like on the coast of the warm southern sea. The stormy river Kyngarka, which boasts clear waters and unbridled waterfalls, escapes from the high mountains to the Tunkinskaya valley. Here, where the Sayan mountains lowered their slopes, and the sanatorium "Arshan" was located.

Generous rocks give a person unique mineral water and healthy mud, which eliminates many diseases. Powerful pine forests surround the sanatorium, and an impenetrable taiga is very close.

It is as if nature specially gathered in one place all the conditions for man, even the air here has a powerful effect on the body.

A bit of history

Sanatorium "Arshan" was founded almost 90 years ago. Patients came here not only from the immediate vicinity, but also from all over Russia. The unique mountain climate, carbonic water with different mineral contents, unique landscapes, silt sulfide mud - all these factors made the hospital absolutely unique. The fact is that nowhere else in the country can one encounter such a symbiosis of nature and its healing gifts. The Tunkinsky valley is located at an altitude of almost 900 m above sea level - and this fact allows vacationers to breathe the purest ionized air all year round.

The sanatorium was often reconstructed and new rooms and laboratories were added, the last repair was carried out in 2009. Such an event made it possible to provide better medical services to vacationers: new equipment was installed, and recreation facilities were improved.

At present, the sanatorium is positioning itself as a mountain-climatic and balneological resort, which spreads over 193 hectares of virgin forests and mountain valleys.
