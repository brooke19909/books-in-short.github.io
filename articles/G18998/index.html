Hotels of Kargopol. Description. Reviews

The ancient Russian city of Kargopol has a rich centuries-old history. Many tourists come here annually to admire beautiful examples of Russian white-stone architecture. It gathers craftsmen from all over the country at the festival of folk craftsmen of Russia. Kargopol hotels will offer guests of the city comfortable conditions for relaxation after an eventful day.

Hotel Kargopol

The location of the comfortable hotel "Kargopol" is the very center of the city. You need to go just a few minutes to walk along the banks of the Onega River. Guests can make use of the sauna and tour desk. There is an ATM at the hotel. In a small shop you can buy souvenirs. Meals are organized in a private cafe, which is located in the basement. Access to free wireless internet is in the halls on the first and second floors. Car owners enjoy free public parking spaces. The hotel consists of 17 rooms, including the following options:

  • standard double with two single beds - 3000 rubles per day for two, and 2600 rubles per day for one person;
  • double Deluxe with a large double bed - respectively 3900 and 3600 rubles per day;
  • Suite with a large double bed - 4950 rubles per day for two, and 4500 rubles per day for one person.

The price is based on a good breakfast. All rooms are equipped with cable TV, a telephone and a safe. This is a great option for those tourists who are interested in Kargopol hotels in the city center.

The Kargopol Hotel is located at 23 Akulova Street.

Hotel Kargopolochka

If you are looking for a budget option among Kargopol hotels, the Kargopolochka Hotel invites you. Major attractions are within walking distance. The hotel has an ATM and a terminal. There is no parking place for vehicles. Wi-Fi is free throughout. Meals are organized in the own restaurant. There is luggage storage and a gift shop at the property. Laundry and ironing services are available at a surcharge. The hotel allows pets, for which a preliminary request needs to be made. For accommodation, several categories of rooms are offered:

  • general five-seater for men and women - 550 rubles per day per person;
  • general triple for men and women - 750 rubles per day per person;
  • single budget - 1050 rubles per day;
  • double budget - 2400 rubles per day for two, and 1750 rubles per day for one person;
  • double standard - 2600 and 2000 rubles per day, respectively;
  • double superior - 3200 rubles per day for two, and 2400 rubles per day for one person;
  • family - from 3300 to 4200 rubles per day, depending on the number of residents.

Upon request, breakfast can be included in the room rate. The Kargopolochka Hotel is located in Kargopol at the address: Lenin Street, 83.

Guest House "Pagnuev House"

If you want to plunge into the atmosphere of an old Russian city, then, among the hotels and guest houses of Kargopol, pay attention to the guest house of the Pagnuyevs. Nearby is a large shopping center. You can park your car in free public parking. There is free WIFI. Pets are allowed at no extra charge, prior request is required. The hotel also offers bike hire and ski equipment hire. Guests can make use of ticketing and tour services. Upon request, a shuttle service can be arranged. The guest house has two rooms:

  • triple budget - 700 rubles per day per person;
  • single budget - 1000 rubles per day.

The guest house of the Pagnuyevs is located at the address: street of the 3rd International, house 61.
