What is remarkable about the Mendeleevskaya metro station?

Mendeleevskaya metro station began operating at the end of 1988. As you see, this happened a long time ago. It is located between the stations "Savelovskaya" and "Tsvetnoy Boulevard" on the Serpukhov-Timiryazevskaya line.

Description

This is a deep-bed station, notable for its beautiful columns, which, like the traveling walls, are faced with snow-white marble. As for the subway platform, black and gray granite were laid on it. To decorate the walls, insets were added on which you can see drawings depicting all kinds of chemicals, atoms and molecules. Lamps are very similar to crystal lattices. That's how unusual and interesting the Mendeleevskaya metro station is. But the most important detail that attracts everyone's attention is a monument to homeless dogs located here. Looking at him, many recall the difficult fate of stray dogs and decide to help them. People who are poorly oriented in the capital, but who want to get to the station, need to see where the Mendeleevskaya metro station is located on the metro map. Now, not everyone will risk going to an unfamiliar area, hoping only for themselves. If you have a card, then you should use it. Looking at it, you can immediately understand everything: where to get out of transport, and how to get to the right place. For many people who can navigate the map well, just a quick glance is enough.



No technical tricks on the "Mendeleev" is not observed. There is the only underground lobby, the escalator of which takes people to the platform and in the opposite direction. There is access to Novoslobodskaya street.

Transitions, operating mode

The main hall of the station is equipped with marching flights of stairs, which serve as transitions between points. From the "Mendeleev", which is located on the Serpukhov-Timiryazevskaya line, you can go to the "Novoslobodskaya" located on the Ring branch.

The lobby and station are open from 5:35 to 1:00. This mode is quite suitable for most passengers. Mendeleevskaya metro station rarely becomes the object of their complaints. And really, everything is pretty decent and convenient here, nothing causes discontent. The design is just at the height - everything is so beautiful and original, besides it reminds of the great science - chemistry. Nothing like this can be seen at any other Moscow metro stop. Of course, they are all interesting in their own way, but Mendeleevskaya stands apart, standing out for its unusualness and thoughtfulness. However, many people who are often here no longer pay attention to the surrounding beauty. For them, this is commonplace.





Coffee Shops and Restaurants

Near the Mendeleevskaya station, life is in full swing. Many coffee houses and restaurants have been erected and operate here, the doors of which are wide open for visitors. Everyone can eat or drink, if on a sultry day, thirst suddenly tormented. And in the cold season, you can go here to warm up.

Beauty and fitness club

For those who adhere to the correct lifestyle and want to always look great, an elite beauty salon, as well as a fitness club, are built near the station. The Mendeleevskaya metro area is very convenient for living, there is everything a person needs. Calculating people buy apartments here. And I must say, do not regret it later.





Museums, cinema and theaters

There are people who want to get food for the mind, to learn something new. Several museums are located for them, located very close to the metro. The distance from the station to its destination is only 400 meters, or even less. However, many museums are cool, and wish to relax. Well, then you can visit the cinema, which has multimedia viewing capabilities. Here you can choose a movie for every taste. There are also several theaters in which a variety of performances are staged - from light comedies to gloomy tragedies. Here is a cultural rest possible near the Mendeleevskaya metro station. Novoslobodskaya Street, in turn, allows you to get the aesthetic pleasure of a walk: here the beautiful architecture, in particular, the magnificent Sorrow Church monastery attracts attention. Some specially come here to admire him. This is truly an impressive architectural monument, from which it is impossible to take your eyes off. Local residents are lucky that every day they can contemplate such beauty. Many are proud that they live near such a luxurious attraction that attracts Muscovites and tourists to Novoslobodskaya. There are always crowds on this street. Day and even night, life is in full swing here. However, the same can be said about all of Moscow.





Educational institutions

Not far from the facility under consideration, there is a university, as well as a college. Music and sports schools have also opened their doors wide for everyone. Here you can learn dancing, and people willingly take this opportunity. Mendeleevskaya metro station sees a lot of young people daily, who rush about their business. Some go to fitness, others go dancing. And many want to learn how to drive, in which case you can contact a driving school, which is also available here. And for guests of the capital near the station a good hotel is open. She is very cozy and nice, almost everyone is happy with her. The Mendeleevskaya metro station literally attracts Muscovites and guests of the capital, as there are many opportunities for an interesting and useful pastime.
