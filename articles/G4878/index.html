Refrigerator in the car: review, description, reviews of the best models

There are many refrigerators for cars. They differ in power, energy consumption and size. Some modifications are installed near the armrest. There are models specifically for luggage carriers. Also emit devices that can be stored simply on the car seat.

In order to choose an excellent refrigerator, you need to get acquainted with the leading manufacturers and find out about the most popular models.

How to choose a model?

The optimal power of the refrigerator is 15 V. The devices differ in size, and this should be taken into account. Some are very compact and cannot be placed in large cans. The optimal chamber volume is 30 liters. At the same time, the best car refrigerators weigh about 6 kg. For transportation, the models are equipped with a handle. Modern devices are made with regulators. In this way, the cooling capacity can be adjusted. Charging refrigerators is mainly carried out from the cigarette lighter.





Compact devices

If you select a simple model, it is more advisable to stay on a compact refrigerator. The regulator must be of manual type. When choosing a device, you should pay attention to the case material. Modern cameras are made of polymer. Devices with electronic controllers are very expensive. For cars, such models are overkill. The energy consumption indicator for such models is low.

If we consider the Waeco car refrigerator, then its specified parameter is 10 kW. The refrigerator weighs about 4.5 kg. Its lower part should be lined. Additionally, pay attention to the cover, which should be firmly fixed.





Inexpensive models

Inexpensive refrigeration units are most often made with tonic upholstery. At the same time, plastic is used not of the best quality. Some models are made with low-quality regulators. As a result, cooling capacity is greatly reduced. The average volume of the chamber is 25 liters. This is enough to store liter eggplants. The flow rate in the devices is high, so such models are not economical.

12v models

12V car refrigerators are produced in various sizes. Many models are equipped with manual temperature controls. It is also worth noting that there are devices on the stands. To recharge the refrigerator you will need a cigarette lighter. The energy consumption indicator depends on the thickness of the protective layer. As a rule, models are suitable for short-term storage of drinks, and they should not be taken on tourist trips. However, it is worth noting reasonable prices for the goods.





15 V devices

15 V refrigeration units are found with and without shelves. Some models are made with manual controls. Most refrigerators are powered by a compressor. It is also worth noting that they use plastic frames. Covers are installed in different sizes. If you select devices for tourist trips, then you need to give preference to models with low noise. It is also important to note the seal that is under the cover. Modern models are made with several locks. Seals are made of foam. The pads in the devices are double walled. To reduce the noise level, a protective tape is used.

20 V refrigerators

20V car refrigerators consume a lot of energy. The devices use capacitors that are recharged from the cigarette lighter of the car. In this case, the device can be stored in the back seat. Some refrigerators are made without regulators. They do not have a sensor, they do not stand out with high cooling capacity.

It is also worth noting that there are high-quality devices with a plug. Models work silently and are very popular. The chamber volume of such refrigerators is approximately 40 liters. Energy consumption starts at 4 kW. Cameras can be with shelves and without them. There are also modifications with special departments for banks.





20 liter car refrigerators

20 liter refrigerators are great for small cars. On average, they have a cooling capacity of 45 BTUs. It is also worth noting that some models stand out with high-quality sensors. In this case, the regulators are installed manual and digital type. Sealers most often fit in two layers. The most popular are refrigerators with bags. They are very easy to transport. There are also lined models. They have a low power, but they are able to maintain the temperature of the drink for a long time and are very economical. In this case, much depends on the manufacturer of the refrigerator.





30 liter models

30 liter devices allow you to pack drinks in a 1.5 liter container. Many models have product departments. It is also worth noting that the devices are made with straps. The average weight of the model per 30 liters is 5.5 kg. The dimensions of the device are different.

If we consider models with dehumidifiers, then they have rather thin walls. However, modern refrigerators are made with several compressors. It is also worth noting that the models use air vents. When choosing a device, it is important to evaluate the strength of the cover, check the inner lining. There is a good model for 30 liters of about 6 thousand rubles.

Ezetil Models

This company is famous for its compact refrigerators. They have excellent cooling capacity. In this case, the seals are mainly made of foam. Many devices operate in an economical mode and do not consume much energy. Recharging the refrigerator does not take much time. There are budget devices in this series. They use simple plastic, which is not able to hold the temperature for a long time.

Regulators, as a rule, are installed on the model of a rotary type. It is also worth noting that there are devices on the stands that are very easy to transport. Additionally, customers choose models for quality covers. They use excellent capacitors. On average, such a refrigerator in a car costs 6 thousand rubles.
