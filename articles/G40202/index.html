New buildings in Vsevolozhsk: description, features

Vsevolozhsk is a small town in the Leningrad Region. The proximity to the Northern capital, remotely from the hustle and bustle, makes it attractive to modern construction companies. Over the past few years, several residential complexes have been built in the city, and this is only the beginning. We suggest, within the framework of this material, to evaluate the best new buildings in Vsevolozhsk and determine how suitable they are for life.

LCD "North Waltz"

Never before have new buildings in Vsevolozhsk from the developer been so large-scale and modern. If you are looking for a comfort class apartment for your family in an area with developed infrastructure and excellent ecology, pay special attention to the Northern Waltz residential complex. Brick-monolithic buildings of variable number of storeys with spacious studios, one- and two-bedroom apartments are unlikely to leave indifferent even the most demanding and demanding real estate buyers.





On the territory of the complex there is a place for kindergartens, schools, modern playgrounds and sports grounds, green areas for walking and outdoor recreation. The urban development plan for the development of the microdistrict also implies the construction of a shopping and entertainment center, which will become a favorite place for leisure for residents: here you can not only make the necessary purchases, but also have a great time in the company of relatives.

If you are looking for a place where all the necessary conditions for a comfortable life will be created, be sure to look at this project, which takes into account the wishes of modern residents.

LCD "Rumbolovo City"

Just 15 km from St. Petersburg is an excellent residential complex for those who value comfort. Apartments in new buildings of Vsevolozhsk from the builder are really a great investment of their funds, an opportunity to save on the purchase of real estate.

Low-rise building projects have recently become incredibly popular, and all because modern residents of the metropolis are looking for all possible ways to escape from the hustle and bustle of endless noise, to get rid of numerous neighbors, sometimes disturbing the peace.

Living in a small house surrounded by beautiful nature, you can fully experience a comfortable suburban life. In this case, you do not have to give up civilization, because everything necessary for comfort is literally at hand.





Five three-story buildings were erected using monolithic-brick technology, adding quality and external aesthetics. The choice of buyers are offered small studios, as well as one and two bedroom apartments of improved layout. The project does not provide for the construction of its own infrastructure, but all those who purchase an apartment here will have access to the developed infrastructure of the district.

LCD "Vakhrusheva"

New buildings in Vsevolozhsk could not do without LCD "Vakhrusheva". In this case, it is a small, compact three-story house for 36 comfort class apartments. A closed house area, parking spaces for all residents, modern planning solutions with spacious kitchens and isolated rooms, independent heating - these are the advantages that attract tremendous attention.





In the adjoining territory of the new building of Vsevolozhsk it was possible to place a modern playground, and plant rare trees around the perimeter, which, according to the plan of the developer, should create a "hedge" in a few years.

LCD "Smolny"

If you are interested in townhouses, new buildings in Vsevolozhsk will offer you a great option - LCD "Smolny". At the moment, this is a completed and commissioned project, which in no way detracts from its merits.





Townhouses in the modern sense combine the advantages of a country house and the comfort of an apartment in the very center of the city. They have long acquired tremendous popularity among those who value space, independence, dream of their own housing, but for today it was considered his purchase an unacceptable luxury.

Excellent town houses with a land plot - what could be better? The developer offers several layout options , two-level apartments with an area of ​​62 square meters. The use of advanced technologies and innovations ensures perfect quality for all buildings, and the availability of communications - comfort for all residents.

To summarize

We presented to your attention the best new buildings in Vsevolozhsk. For such a small town, this is a sufficient number of residential complexes, which potential residents need to pay attention to. Clean fresh air, access to the facilities of the developed metropolitan infrastructure is what sets the new buildings of Vsevolozhsk district apart from other projects.

If you have been choosing an apartment in St. Petersburg for a long time, but still cannot afford such an expensive purchase, pay attention to the new residential buildings that are rapidly being rebuilt in Vsevolozhsk.
