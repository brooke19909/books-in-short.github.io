Design





It has been noted more than once that the company clearly needs new, more creative designers. Either they have absolutely no imagination, or they have serious problems with free time. Why is this thesis true? If you look, for example, on the U and Solo models, and then on the object of our review, then distinguishing them immediately will be quite problematic. In the case of Sony Xperia Miro, the reviews suggest that the difference between the named models is played only by a small curvature that takes place under the screen of the device. Users who have already more or less begun to navigate the screen sizes can also note a reduced diagonal and a reduced resolution.

Maybe for some it will look ridiculous, but Sony engineers firmly believe that if the hardware keys were moved to new positions or simply changed, then this parameter is already considered a new design. In fact, this can be argued, but this is not about that.

A look on the edge





Under the screen, you can find the control and navigation keys. In a situation with Sony Xperia Miro, reviews say that the keys are touch, not mechanical. Above the screen is the front camera. You can notice it without any problems, because the engineers did not even think to hide it. A negative point for the model is the fact that there is no light sensor in the device.

At the top there is a jack for a wired headset standard 3.5 mm. There is also a power button with which you can turn the phone on and off, and also lock the screen.

On the left flank we have a connector for synchronizing with a PC. It is standardly type MicroUSB. On the opposite side is the key with which you can adjust the volume of the device and play music.

On the bottom face is the main microphone. There is also a small indent. Its purpose is to make it convenient for the user to remove the cover. With the help of indentation, it can be easily pry.

Well, the rear camera has a resolution of 5 megapixels. Next to it is an LED-type flash and an additional speakerphone.

The design can not be called original. There is a small update in this regard. It consists of a light indicator that has been integrated into the box under the hardware keys. If a text message arrives on the phone or an incoming call is fixed, the indicator will begin to pulse. But it is performed at an ordinary level, without access to the settings. As they say, cheap and cheerful.

Nutrition





The battery of the device has a capacity of 1460 mAh. Sony Xperia Miro, whose characteristics are technically modestly evaluated, does not work with such autonomous power for a long time. However, an ordinary user will be able to spend the whole day with the phone without worrying about recharging it.

Strange things sometimes happen to the battery of this model. At times, the Sony Xperia Miro ST23I does not turn on, whatever the user would do. In such situations, do not worry prematurely. You just need to check the reliability of the connection of the contacts. It should be said right away that a similar problem was observed in the case of other Sony models.

In general, according to the logic of things, the power consumption of the phone should not be so high. The fact is that the hardware stuffing of the smartphone is clearly not the best, therefore, it will not just consume energy just like that.

Having correctly distributed the load, in principle, you can squeeze out two days of battery life from the device. But it is better for active users to turn their eyes to more powerful models, although one of the few advantages that distinguish Miro is its operating time.

As we can see, the smartphone Sony ST23I Xperia Miro has nothing special in communication stuffing. But this is not surprising, since the device belongs to the budget type. But in general, everything is not bad, especially for ordinary users.

Of course, it was slightly modified and modernized. So, the processor frequency was increased, the architecture was changed, the work was optimized in some way. But it could have been better. Memory





The RAM built into the device is 512 MB. Some users ask what to do if the Sony Xperia Miro does not turn on. RAM can contribute here if the phone was in working condition. The fact is that it sometimes freezes and therefore does not respond to user requests. The phone seems to be turned off, but it is not. For personal needs of the user in the device allocated 2 GB of long-term memory. The phone has a slot for microSD memory cards. The maximum size of flash drives that are supported by Miro can be 32 GB.

Camera

The rear camera has a resolution of 5 megapixels. It is equipped with auto focus. Also, next to the camera is an LED flash. The user interface has not changed.
