Technical version

Before the crashes, one official site (qiwi.com) worked, where there was an English-language and a Russian-language version. In the spring, the Qiwi wallet team began work on another new site in the .ru domain zone (qiwi.ru). That is why it was impossible to enter the official site. However, technical work was not carried out around the clock and without warning its customers.

Those customers who subscribed to the QIWI-wallet news were notified of the possible difficulties with working with the site. Other users at the entrance to the wallet saw a warning about technical work and an apology for causing inconvenience. Now both sites work without problems.

Also, many difficulties arose due to browsers. That is why programmers advise updating old versions to new ones so that any service works, including the Qiwi wallet. The QIWI site does not work in this case due to a browser inconsistency. In this case, you need to clear cookies or log into your wallet through another browser (best of all, Google Chrome).
