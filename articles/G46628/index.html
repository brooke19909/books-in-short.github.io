Complex origami: principles, materials, benefits

Origami is called ancient Japanese art, which consists in folding paper figures. Even despite the fact that the paper itself first appeared in China, it was in the Land of the Rising Sun that they guessed to make interesting and beautiful figures from this material. More than one generation of Japanese masters has contributed to the development of this art, inheriting the ability to turn a flat sheet into an unusual craft.

Paper Engineering: Fundamentals

The list of necessary tools for classic origami has only three positions: a square sheet of paper, scissors and glue. You won’t be able to create without it. It is possible that for one figure you need not one, but several sheets.





A popular variety is modular origami, where one craft consists of a large number of identical parts, or modules, each of which is folded according to the classical canons of paper construction. After the master has made the required number of modules, he collects the figure, putting the finished parts into each other. Even the younger student will be able to create these modules, sometimes glancing at the diagram.

Features of paper design

What is complicated origami in the modern world? Nothing but one type of art from a large number of varieties of arts and crafts. It firmly entered and established itself in the life of many peoples of the world. It is a pity that now origami no longer carries the original semantic load. Anyone who wants to try to do this interesting way of communicating with paper, spend their free time for the benefit of both the mind and hands, will be able to do it without any difficulties. But before you cross the threshold of this unique paper craftsmanship, you need to choose the right material for creativity.

Construction paper

In classical art, ordinary paper is used for folding crafts. However, if desired, the master can take color. Under certain circumstances, even foil is suitable. The same figure may look different depending on what material it will be made of. For example, for more refined and sophisticated work, transparent paper is suitable. Just remember how the famous "Crane" looks from the inside!





Another unusual material is rainbow paper. She is very motley, but this only adorns the work.

Complex origami: what's the use?

First of all, origami classes contribute to the development of human creativity. This is especially manifested in the process of inventing new possibilities for folding paper so that something beautiful happens. In addition, many artists paint their products or pick up paper in various colors.

Complex origami contributes to the development of fine motor skills. And she, in turn, affects speech development. In addition, complex origami improves the quality of the brain. This activity allows you to develop spatial thinking. Many believe that it would be useful in schools to introduce a course in this art, especially before studying geometry.

Origami more difficult contributes to the development of the ability to concentrate on the process. Many figures are either quite complex, or consist of tens or hundreds of parts. Here you can not do without attention and perseverance! Sophisticated paper origami requires the master to be able to follow instructions and work consistently.





Psychologists highlight another useful effect of origami classes - this is calm and relieve stress, anxiety. In addition, doing this kind of art, a person receives great aesthetic pleasure, especially if the result exceeded his expectations. The latter, in turn, also increases self-esteem, as well as uplifting.
