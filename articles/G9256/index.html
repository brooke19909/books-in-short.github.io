TV LG 40LF570V: reviews, specifications, settings

An excellent mid-range device is considered to be the LG 40LF570V TV. Reviews about this product, its technical characteristics and capabilities will be described in great detail in the future.





TV niche

First you need to pay attention to how much the TV of this model costs . The price for it ranges from 18,000 to 20,000 rubles. Expect any phenomenal characteristics at this cost certainly not necessary. And the manufacturer himself initially refers this TV to middle-class solutions. It is perfect for home use. Also, the function of the panel or media player is quite capable of dealing with the product in question. The only important feature that you need to pay attention to before buying is the lack of support for the highly demanded SMART technology. Alas, the developers for some reason did not implement it within the framework of this device.

Pick list

The LG 40LF570V TV in question cannot really boast of really good equipment . Reviews highlight this disadvantage of this device. The manufacturer included the following in the kit:

  • TV.

  • Warranty card.

  • A quick guide to connecting , configuring and using.

  • Power Cord.

  • Set of batteries for the remote.

  • Remote control.

Definitely, the above list lacks communication wires. This is HDMI, and SCART, and RCA. Since the TV is a South Korean manufacturer refers to the middle class products, then the equipment should be appropriate. And the lack of such components in the original configuration is a significant drawback.





Specifications

The advantages in this case include the technical specifications that stand out from the analogs of the LG 40LF570V TV. His characteristics are really good. Two signal receivers are integrated into the device at once. One of them is analog, and the second is digital. In the first case, it is possible to view 60 analog channels. In the second case, there is support for both DVB-T and DVB-T2. In addition to this, the TV has an expansion slot in which you can install a decoder card for decoding digital channels. In this case, there is no integrated satellite receiver - to view such programs for the device in question, you will need to additionally purchase a receiver. When installed on a stand, the device‚Äôs dimensions are 911x581x189. If you use a wall mount to install the TV (you must buy it separately for an additional fee), then the dimensions will decrease to 911x528x55.3.

Screen Specifications

Indeed, the LG 40LF570V LED TV is equipped with a fairly high-quality screen. It is made, as the name implies, using LED technology. Its maximum possible resolution is 1920x1080. That is, in this mode of operation, this TV displays an image in 1080 p format, or, as it is also sometimes called, FullHD. Accordingly, in terms of image quality, this device is a typical middle peasant. Below it are TVs with an HD-matrix and a resolution of 1366x768, but the quality is better in the output picture with 4K format. Viewing angles by the manufacturer are declared at the level of 178 0/178 0 . That is, when viewing from any possible point of image distortion will not occur. In addition, it should be noted that on the screen the image is updated with a frequency of 300 Hz. As a result, to notice with the ordinary eye any flicker of the image certainly will not work.





Sound characteristics

The LG 40LF570V TV is equipped with a fairly good sound system. Reviews emit a good quality of its sound. Such a performance of the speaker system is provided by 2 speakers with a rated power of 20 watts. For the organization of high-quality sound accompaniment this is quite enough in most cases. If you need to improve the sound capabilities, then you can connect external speakers or even a subwoofer. Everything necessary for this in this device is a complete set of audio ports.





Communication list

Before we tell you how to set up LG TV of this model, we will understand its communication list. The list of ports for connection in this device consists of the following:

  • 1 antenna port.

  • 2 HDMI ports.

  • 1 SCART port.

  • 1 RCA port.

  • 1 USB port

  • A set of audio ports.

  • One slot for installing a T2 channel decoding card.

The only negative of the above list is the lack of RJ-45. This port would allow you to connect a TV directly to the computer network and, due to this, directly organize the output of the television picture. The second minus is the lack of support for wifi. On the other hand, the presence of such interfaces would significantly increase the cost of the solution in question, which would make it not so attractive against the background of analogues.

Connection and setup

Now let's figure out how to set up your LG TV this model. The algorithm in this situation consists of the following stages:

  • We take the device out of the box. Dismantle the transport locks.

  • We install the TV in a permanent place of use.

  • We connect the supply cord with one end to the TV, and the other to the outlet of the power supply system.

  • Cable from antenna to appropriate socket.

  • Install the batteries in the control panel.

  • Turn on the TV.

  • Through the menu, we search for channels.
  • Exit the menu.




Cost

Now consider how much this TV model costs . This universal device, as noted earlier, can be purchased at a price of 18,000 to 20,000 rubles. Higher cost of TV in electronics supermarkets and small shops in this area. The advantages of the purchase in this case are obvious: the device can really be seen, evaluated and decided on how much it really suits you. The more modest price is in online stores. But here the purchase in this case, essentially, is carried out blindly. Therefore, it is most optimal to watch live TV in an electronics supermarket, and to buy it in an online store at a low cost.

Reviews

Quite controversial, according to buyers, turned out to be the LG 40LF570V TV. Its price clearly relates to mid-range products. But at the same time he has a number of significant shortcomings. First of all, it is necessary to highlight a very, very modest configuration. The manufacturer saved on interface wires - after purchasing this solution, you must immediately additionally purchase such accessories. Also, the device does not have an RJ-45 port and there is no support for such a wireless method of receiving data as Wi-Fi. And their presence would allow using a computer network to display the image directly on the TV. But there are also positive points in this decision. This is the high quality of the displayed image, and high-quality sound, and an impressive interface set.





Summary

Despite a number of shortcomings, the LG 40LF570V TV turned out to be quite good and very balanced. Reviews for the most part really characterize it on the positive side. The quality of the built-in tuners is not satisfactory. Video and audio recordings are also reproduced in high quality. A good communication set in this case allows you to solve most problems. Even installing a mini PC in such a TV is possible. So for home use it's just a great device with a fairly large screen size.
