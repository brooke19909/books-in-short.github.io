State Farm "Russia" (Adler): features of rest in the southernmost region of Sochi

State Farm "Russia" (Adler) is the unofficial name of the resort micro-district of Greater Sochi. It is located two kilometers from the border with Abkhazia. Thus, it is the southernmost resort of the Krasnodar Territory. Why does he have such a strange name? The fact is that in Soviet times there really was an agricultural enterprise under that name. But now it has ceased to exist, and the district is traditionally called the "State Farm" Russia "." Adler, expanding, included these lands in his territory, and recently it has practically merged with the Olympic Park.



Location

This area is popular with tourists due to its extremely favorable location. It is located near Adler, with its water park, oceanarium, parks, shopping and entertainment. In addition, Abkhazia, its beautiful nature and various excursions are very close by. Getting to Adler from here is easy. In the center of the neighborhood there is a bus stop. Almost all flights from here go to Adler. Drive about ten minutes. If we talk about the immediate neighbors of the resort, the closest village to the side of Adler is the Olympic Village in the Imereti Lowland. There are the famous stadiums, the Ice Palace and part of the Formula 1 track. And near Abkhazia itself the village of Vesyoloye is located. State Farm "Russia" (Adler) used to be its agricultural land, and now - the coastal region.
