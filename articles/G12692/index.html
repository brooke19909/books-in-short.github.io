How to restart "Android": basic methods

Most modern smartphones are equipped with an inexpensive and convenient operating system - Android. Perhaps, there are no owners of the operating system who have not encountered temporary crashes, errors, and freezes of Android.

Many do not know how to cope with such a problem, sometimes they do not even try to do it. But if the error is caused by accident, then the solution is quite simple - reboot your mobile device. Therefore, you need to know the algorithm of how to restart Android.

For what purpose is a reboot necessary

There are a number of objective reasons why you need to restart your smartphone:

  • It happens that after a certain time the phone starts to freeze, the operating system slows down. This is considered normal and occurs due to the accumulation of temporary files, so you need to perform a scheduled reboot of your device.
  • Slowing down applications and systems can also be due to system crashes. In such situations, it is advisable to use proven methods and restart Android with the ability to restore data.
  • Rebooting can open access to new smartphone features, such as Recovery (a menu on the phone that allows you to reset all settings to factory defaults).

The reboot process can help in case of other problems. If this does not help, it may be possible to correct the situation by switching to safe mode. Do not panic without understanding the problem to the end and reset the phone to factory settings. It is possible that the problem is solved by rebooting.

The device freezes: how to safely restart the "Android"?

Sometimes the smartphone freezes so much that the usual way to restart the phone does not work (pressing the lock button), because the shutdown menu is not displayed. With a similar problem, you need to hold the lock button for a little more than 10 seconds until the device starts to reboot. In the end, the device will reboot even with a very strong hang of Android.

If the phone has a removable battery, then there is another algorithm. You just need to remove the cover, pull out the battery, wait a few seconds, insert it back and restart the phone. In the same way, you can restart the laptop, which hangs badly and does not respond to clicks. Just hold down the power button and wait for the laptop to reboot.

Quick and easy reboot without resetting to factory settings

So, how to restart Android, if it freezes, but the problem is not fatal, that is, the phone hangs slightly, but performs its main functions, then the following reset method is suitable:

  1. Hold the power button on the phone and hold for a few seconds.
  2. We wait until the shutdown menu appears, where several items will be registered.
  3. We find the item "Restart the device", click and wait for the reboot.

The speed of the appearance of the menu on different devices is different, on average it is 5-10 seconds. This restart method works on all modern popular smartphones, if there are no problems with the screen and phone display, and the shutdown buttons respond to pressing. If the phone does not respond to keystrokes, then to save data on the device you will have to resort to the battery method.

A radical way to reboot your phone with deleting data

It is worth completely rebooting Android with deleting data in the event that the errors are serious and cannot be eliminated by a normal reboot. A similar problem is caused by caught viruses, third-party applications, errors during any actions and certain functions. This method of troubleshooting involves deleting all data on the device without restoring it, but it is recommended that you save the data by backing up information from the phone‚Äôs memory.

The algorithm of this method is convenient in that it remains unchanged no matter what device you have. It is simple and involves a sequential chain of simple actions:

  1. Go to the device settings.
  2. Select the item with the name "Archiving and resetting data."
  3. Click and wait for the device to reboot.

This method of restarting Android will return the device to its original state. All data, including passwords and logins, will be deleted. Previously installed applications will also not be available. The smartphone will not be inhibited, and applications will stop hanging. Finally

If the performance of the mobile device has decreased, and it often freezes, then it is advisable to use the power button to quickly reboot or reset to factory settings. Before restarting Android, if it freezes during a reset and working with system files, it is important to remember the need to back up to save all important information to a laptop or flash drive.
