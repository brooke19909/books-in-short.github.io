A trip to Zurich is sure to be remembered for a long time. Making a plan for walking around this city is very simple, because the sights there are on almost every street. Some of them deserve special attention.

History Center

The sights of Zurich, the description, photos of which attract many tourists, include the Old Town. In many European cities there are such corners of antiquity in which narrow streets and buildings that have not changed for several centuries have been preserved. In Zurich, you can find houses that are more than ten centuries old, and people still live in them. An interesting fact is also associated with them. Before Napoleon came, the locals gave the houses names, not numbers.

The French invaders could not memorize complex German words and numbered the buildings in their own way. This numbering has been preserved to this day. Zurich, whose sights are quite diverse, can boast of ancient Roman baths.
