Country Park Hotel "Shishka" (Novosibirsk): description, photos, reviews

The city of Novosibirsk is an industrial and transport hub of our country. Despite the abundance of cold days, tourists from different regions and countries like to visit here. The city has many interesting places for an active weekend and children's leisure. In winter and summer there are lots of interesting and extreme activities for every taste and budget.

For a pleasant pastime and solitude from the benefits of civilization, the Shishka country park hotel (Novosibirsk) is ideally suited. This is a compact hotel, conveniently located in the Dzerzhinsky district. Its main advantage is its remoteness from the motorway and noisy entertainment venues.





The distance to the city center is about 6-7 km, to the airport - 24 km. A popular place surrounded by greenery, suitable for corporate, romantic, family or business holidays. The popular complex invites adults and children to enjoy nature, ski and get the most out of their stay here.

Accommodations

Park Hotel Shishka (Novosibirsk), the photo of which can be viewed in the material, offers comfortable accommodation in hotel-type rooms and wooden cottages for 25 people. The rooms are divided into categories, there are luxury apartments with two zones. It should be noted that all rooms have private bathrooms with showers and underfloor heating.





The interior is decorated in pastel colors, so that guests feel comfortable and can fully relax after an intense day. All rooms have high-speed Internet, an air conditioning system (cold-heat) is installed. For the convenience of visitors - a TV with a liquid crystal screen, providing the opportunity to watch at least two hundred channels. There is a bar filled with drinks of different strengths, which are available for a fee.

If you come to the complex as a large friendly company, we recommend renting a cottage built of natural wood. Near the house is a lake. The building is equipped with a small pool, sauna, open veranda with barbecue, banquet facilities. No one and nothing will disturb you.

Food concept

Arriving at the Shishka Park Hotel (Novosibirsk), in addition to a cozy room, you get a delicious continental breakfast served in a luxurious restaurant (on schedule). The institution practices European and Russian cuisine. The menu is replete with a variety of delicacies, delicious desserts and elite drinks.





There is an open area for 250 seats. The restaurant also has a small hall for celebrations and business events. It often hosts weddings, anniversaries, corporate meetings for 70 people. For larger festivities - a large hall with 100 seats with a kitchen area and a dressing room.

Leisure and entertainment

What a vacation without fun and sports? Country Park Hotel Shishka (Novosibirsk) does everything possible for its customers, constantly improving its services. The hotel has a fitness center with exercise equipment. A professional instructor works in the hall, who will develop an individual training program and tell you how to quickly and easily correct your figure without harm to your health.





The undoubted advantage of the hotel is the presence of Turkish and Finnish saunas. A visit to the steam room will improve and cleanse the body, as well as relax mentally and physically. A billiard room is equipped for guests, sports grounds are located on the territory. Children will be delighted with the swings and slides.

In warm weather, you will enjoy swimming in the lake, hiking and cycling. In winter, people come to the Shishka Park Hotel (Novosibirsk) for skiing. Skiing on snowy peaks, skiing, cheesecakes, snowboarding, ice skating, and sledding will bring unthinkable pleasure, give you the opportunity to again feel like a child and get vivid emotions.

Active exercises perfectly relieve tension in the muscles, improve physical condition and cheer up. The ski resort is unique under the snow cover of sparkling snowflakes. By the way, there is a rental center for sports equipment and equipment.

Internal service

The complex has a parking lot, a laundry and dry cleaning department. There is also a 24-hour front desk. Guests can use the paid shuttle service, book train and air tickets. There is a left-luggage office, a safe at the reception.
