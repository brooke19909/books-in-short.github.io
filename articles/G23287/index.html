Hotel Grand Central Hotel 4 * (Dubai): reviews, descriptions and reviews of tourists

Dubai is a city that offers an excellent vacation for tourists who come on a business trip, as well as just for those who want to explore the city, relax and spend time with your loved one. There are a huge number of hotels, as well as a small coastline, which is mainly occupied by entertainment venues and places to stay. One of them is the Grand Central Hotel Dubai 4 *.

Location

This place for overnight and leisure is located in the central part of Dubai. Not far from it are huge shopping centers, in which there are many places for entertainment. Often, tourists who relax here recommend to visit such places without fail and use the services that are offered here. Near the Grand Central Hotel Dubai 4 * there are a number of attractions, among which City Tours, Safari Desert, as well as Dhow Cruise are especially popular.

The nearest airport (Dubai) is literally a 7-minute drive from the hotel.

The hotel was built for tourists who wish to spend time in the city in order to solve some issues related to doing business, as well as for those who would like to spend some time in its luxurious rooms. Very often small reconstructions are carried out in the building, with the help of which its modern appearance is still preserved, as well as the technical equipment of rooms and the building is becoming more and more new and high-quality.

All rooms have a personal air conditioning that regulates the temperature conditions in the rooms to the optimum level regardless of the weather situation that occurs on the street. The apartments of Grand Central Hotel Dubai 4 * have private bathrooms, which are equipped with a full set of sanitary ware, as well as bath accessories, which tourists can find in special lockers.

Nutrition

In reviews of tourists about the hotel Grand Central 4 * (United Arab Emirates / Dubai), it is often mentioned about the level of service in the catering facilities available in this place for recreation. The attention of guests is often drawn to a service that is provided for guests living on the all-inclusive concept - this is a buffet, where everyone is invited three times a day. Guests note that they serve dishes prepared in Eastern, European and Mediterranean traditions, which have excellent taste. In total, the Grand Central Hotel 4 * (Dubai) has two restaurants, where, according to travelers, they are served at the highest level.

In addition, everyone can visit any of the two bars. The menu that is available in them offers a wide selection of appetizers and salads, as well as oriental sweets, which undoubtedly win the hearts of Russian gourmets. Great attention is paid to the selection of cocktails and drinks, among which there are both alcoholic and non-alcoholic.



Fitness and sports

Supporters of a healthy lifestyle do not miss the opportunity to visit the gym, which is located on the top floor of the Grand Central Hotel Dubai 4 * complex. In the reviews of tourists it is often mentioned that this place for its placement was chosen very well, since it is from here that you can observe all the beauty of the metropolis. You can visit this center from 8 a.m. to 6 p.m., on any day of the week.

SPA

A large number of guests prefer to spend their time in the SPA center, where you can not only carry out a wide range of cosmetic procedures, but also attend a massage session. A large number of reviews about the Grand Central Hotel 4 stars (Dubai) say that if you want to improve the condition of your skin and respiratory system, you definitely need to visit the thermal zone, where it is fashionable to meet a team of real professionals. A sauna and a bath are presented to guests.

If you want to do water procedures, tourists have the opportunity to visit the aquazone, which is located in an open area. It is represented by a large pool and a terrace around it. It seems possible to order drinks and food from the main restaurant - travelers consider this opportunity a great solution.



Additional services

The hotel administration regularly makes sure that each guest of Grand Central Hotel Dubai 4 * can feel as comfortable as possible while staying on its territory. That is why there are a number of services that are provided to tourists, but are not included in the price of a standard ticket. All of them can be used for a fee.

So, one of the most popular is provided with dry cleaning and laundry services - this is washing and ironing. Vacationers in reviews sometimes note that professionals use modern means to ensure that things get their original appearance. Here you can also take advantage of the possibility of renting vehicles, where experienced travelers can go to explore the surroundings and local attractions.

Absolutely all hotel guests also have the opportunity to use a number of free services. Among them is a room service, which is mostly commented on in the comments. In addition, any tourists can always contact the administrator at the front desk with questions of interest regarding accommodation and the provision of services. If necessary, a car from a taxi service can be called from here, as well as a newspaper ordered.



Prices

The cost of a ticket to this place is relatively low, especially if you take into account the whole range of conditions that are provided for guests. In reviews of the Grand Central Hotel 4 stars (United Arab Emirates / Dubai), figures are often given about 60,000 rubles, which are designed for a weekly stay of two tourists. Very often, customers of operators who choose this hotel for their trip are young couples who love outdoor activities, as well as fun.

According to travelers, a standard ticket to a hotel in the city of Dubai necessarily includes round-trip flights and room rental. Everything else, including transfer, can be included for an additional fee and only at the request of the client.

Often, different travel agents recommend using the services of tours to the sights of the UAE and, in particular, the city of Dubai. However, travelers who have been there more than once are advised to refuse "attractive" offers, as they will be much cheaper at the vacation spot.

Very often, tourists recommend visiting this hotel to their friends and relatives, recommending it because it is in this direction that excellent service and low cost of the tour are perfectly combined.
