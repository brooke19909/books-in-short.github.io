We knit the jumpsuit with knitting needles for newborns. Universal model

Things for a newborn baby should be particularly tender and comfortable. And how else, if not with my mother‚Äôs hands, can you realize such high demands on the wardrobe.



If you are already an experienced needlewoman, then it will not be difficult for you to make any clothes for the crumbs, but for beginner craftswomen there are several simple options, for example, knit the jumpsuit with knitting needles for newborns. It can only seem complicated at first glance. But do not be alarmed, you can knit a jumpsuit for a newborn with knitting needles without assistance, guided by the instructions below.

A very simple way to knit overalls for babies from 3 months to 1 year

Depending on the size, you may need from 3 to 5 skeins of yarn, as well as knitting needles and two buttons. Used pattern - garter stitch (all rows are knitted with facial loops). This is a very simple knitted jumpsuit for a newborn. The scheme of this model is designed specifically for needlewomen taking the first steps in the manufacture of clothing. So do not be afraid, proceed boldly.

We knit the back

Start knitting from the back of the jumpsuit. The entire back will be fully completed in one piece. We start knitting from the bottom. For half of the left leg, we knit 27 needles on the knitting needles (for the smallest size). We continue knitting with facial loops (or a pattern as desired) for 13 rows, after which we lay the connected fragment to the side. Now you need to tie the same part, only for half of the right leg (13 rows). Both parts must be transferred to one spoke, without forgetting to dial another 6 loops between them. On your knitting needles a total of 60 loops should be obtained. Knitting continues until the product reaches a height of 30 centimeters, after which on each side it is necessary to make reductions as follows. In each even row, the first time 5 loops are reduced, the second 4 loops, the third 3 loops and another 3 times 1 loop. Next, we continue to knit the remaining loops until the total height of the product is 36 cm. Now we close 6 loops located in the center and finish knitting each side separately, making reductions in the place of the future neck. To do this, we reduce 4 loops for the first time, in the next row - 2 loops and in two rows 1 loop each. Having reached a height of 41 cm, you can finish knitting and close the loops. You should have 5 loops on each side.

Knitting front

The overalls shelf is knitted similarly to a back. The difference will be in the length of the straps. After the height of the product is 42 cm, a loop for the fastener is made in each strap, corresponding to the size of the buttons you choose. After that, knitting lasts up to 44 cm. Just like when knitting the back of the overalls, 5 loops on each side should remain on the knitting needles. Close them and the front of the product is ready. Knitted jumpsuit with knitting needles for newborns in your design is almost ready, it remains to connect all the parts.

Product assembly

Two parts, front and back, are folded facing each other and stitched together on the sides. In the same way legs are connected to each other. Now you can make edges along the neck, armhole and along the cut of the legs. To do this, you can use the hook and tie the above edges with a decorative pattern or just columns without a crochet. Also, the design of the edges can be done using knitting needles: having typed around the edges of the loop, knit several rows with an elastic band or front stitch.

Knitted jumpsuit for newborns: design options

Above was described a model for beginners with a simple knit. Using the same knitting pattern with a different, more complex pattern, you will get a completely new option. For example, when using embossed knitting, the jumpsuit acquires a unique style and sophistication. But experiments with different colors will add its appearance to bright colors, which kids really like.



Knitted overalls with knitting needles for newborns may look different, for example, have sleeves and closed legs. Such a model looks very beautiful, but for manufacturing it requires special skills and great experience. Having dealt with an easier task to begin with, you will feel more confident in your future work. Good luck and beautiful things to your crumbs!
