What you need to know to sew a sundress for a girl

Modern mothers are needlewomen. They just love to do everything with their own hands. So why not try and dress up your beautiful daughter yourself? To do this, you just need to sew a sundress for the girl.





Blanks

Before you sit down to work, you need to have some supplies. So, what may be needed to sew a sundress for a girl? The fabric from which the product will be made, threads. A working tool will also be needed. You can, of course, sew the dress manually, using only one needle, but it will be quite difficult. It is better to do everything with a sewing machine.

Pattern

In order to sew a sundress for a girl, you may need a pattern where all sizes of a children's dress will be marked. You need to know the length of the product, as well as the volume of the chest. Since for girls dresses and sundresses are mostly free cut, it is not necessary to know the size of the child‚Äôs waist. The pattern will mainly consist of one or two parts, depending on whether a product is created from a single or separate piece of fabric.

Cut





The next step is if the mother wants to sew a sundress for the girl. Now you need to redraw the pattern on the fabric. To do this, the sample is simply circled on the wrong side of the product. You can do this with crayons, a special marker or good old remnants - pieces of used soap. By the way, the last method is most successful, because stripes from soap disappear when steaming with an iron. Drawings from the chalk need to be washed off the finished product. After the pattern is outlined, you need to add allowances for the seams - about one and a half to two centimeters. Next, the fabric is cut out, and everything is ready for stitching.

Sewing

There are two ways to sew a sundress. From a single piece of fabric, when the product is almost ready, and you only need to sew the sides, and also process the neck, armholes (if required) and the bottom of the product. And another way, when a sundress, in addition to processing armholes, the neck (if required) and the bottom, you also need to sew a yoke with a skirt. The choice of pattern depends on the desire of the mother. If a woman wants to sew a summer sundress for a girl, it can be generally of a simple cut, from a single piece of fabric with a special elastic band on the chest and straps sewn onto the shoulders. If you need to sew a school dress for a girl, there will be a little more work, because you will need to properly handle the neck and armholes, securing them with a prepared oblique trim.





rules

A few rules for sewing: before work, the fabric must be ironed to avoid shrinkage; the edges need to be processed with an overlock or zigzag, the seams should be smoothed. Also, do not forget to fix the beginning and end of the seams so that they do not diverge later. So that the neck and armholes turn out beautiful and not stretched, before processing them with an oblique inlay, the fabric must be planted, slightly tightened.

Jewelry

Having sewed a sundress for a girl, you can also decorate it. They do this with the help of ready-made ribbons, ribbons, home-made flowers and simply cut pieces of fabric sewn to the product in any way. The main thing is to show imagination and make the product for the girl original and unique.
