Mia Resort Nha Trang 5 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang, Nha Trang): room description, service, reviews

Vietnam is a very friendly country. The Russians are always welcome here. Therefore, our compatriots are happy to come here to rest. One of the best Vietnamese resorts is considered Nha Trang. Thanks to the mild climate and unique natural beauties, tourism is developing very rapidly here. We offer to relax in Nha Trang choose the wonderful hotel Mia Resort Nha Trang 5 *. Its employees make every effort so that tourists staying in Vietnam will leave only good and bright memories.

Location

If you look at the map, you can see that Vietnam has a long narrow strip encircling the Indochina Peninsula from its southeast side. Such a geographical feature causes completely different climatic conditions in the north and south of the country. Nha Trang is located closer to the south. It is 439 km from Ho Chi Minh City, and 1280 km from Hanoi. Mia Resort Nha Trang is built about half an hour from the resort center. This is a quiet and unusually beautiful place in a valley surrounded by hills on one side and the South China Sea on the other.

How to get there

In the reviews, almost all tourists indicate that the Mia Resort Nha Trang is located near the modern Kamran Airport. The distance is only 30 km. The road to the hotel takes about half an hour. If you are traveling from a travel agency, a comfortable transfer will be waiting for you at the airport. All tourists are offered wet towels and a bottle of cool water. If you reserved a room yourself, you can get from the airport to your destination by taxi. As evidenced by the reviews of tourists, private cabs take expensive. The most profitable taxi here is Vivasun.

Description of the Territory

The Mia Resort Nha Trang Hotel occupies a plot of only 10 thousand square meters. This is not very much for hotels of this level. However, the territory of this hotel is too elongated along the coast, so all the buildings and structures are at some distance from each other. In addition, all the tracks are paved with stone tiles, which makes it difficult to move in high-heeled shoes. Tourists in their reviews report that it‚Äôs rather inconvenient to go from your building, for example, to a restaurant. Hotel management solved this problem. For all guests free buggies (small open cars) are provided, with which you can move around the territory.

Mia Resort Nha Trang is surrounded by greenery and flowers. Perfect cleanliness is maintained around. The whole territory is divided into sections. Each has its own name. On these sites are villas and three two-story buildings, as well as a number of facilities for recreation and entertainment of tourists, the administrative building, bars and restaurants. A motorway encircles the hotel grounds. However, the noise from passing vehicles is absolutely inaudible.

Hotel Description

Mia Resort Nha Trang is a modern complex, built in 2011. The last renovation was held in 2016, so everything shines with novelty and cleanliness. Arriving at the transfer, tourists enter the spacious cozy hall of the main building. Here is the reception, working around the clock. The staff speak excellent English. Almost no one knows the Russian language.

Each arriving tourist is offered yogurt and juice or a local drink. A hotel card is also issued on which the building or villa where the guest will live is indicated. Things are delivered to the room on a buggy. All tourists in their reviews note the cordiality of the staff, friendliness, helpfulness, desire to help. At the reception you can book an excursion, tickets for a bus, train or plane, call a taxi or a doctor, and take personal items to the laundry.

In the lobby of the hotel there is a souvenir shop where you can buy some clothes, cosmetics, goods for a beach holiday. A spa center is located on site, located near the main entrance to the territory. It offers many treatments that restore strength and health.

This hotel is comfortable not only to relax, but also to conduct business events. For this, a conference room is equipped here, in which there is modern equipment necessary for business events.

Description of rooms

Mia Resort Nha Trang has all the conditions for a relaxing, harmonious stay. In two-story buildings, double rooms with one large or two standard beds are provided. There are also family quadruple rooms. Their area is from 40 square meters.

Villas in the hotel have a private pool and without it. Each has a small terrace with outdoor furniture and a garden. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe villas is from 55 square meters. The hotel also has one suite. Its area is 175 m 2 .

All rooms have a stylish contemporary design. The colors are dominated by bright, but unobtrusive, which helps to relax and have a great rest. Each room has beds, on one side of which (at the head) there is a desk with writing utensils, and on the other - a low soft sofa with pillows. To save space in the walls, niches are made where cabinets with hangers and various shelves are equipped. Here, tourists will find bathrobes and flip flops.

The hygiene room has spacious bathtubs, a washbasin and a toilet. Tourists are offered funds for taking a shower, as well as a suntan lotion, a spray against insects (mosquitoes and midges), napkins.

Each room has a TV (cable TV), air conditioning, hairdryer, safe, tea and coffee service, excellent wireless Internet. Upon arrival, tourists are given a welcome gift in the form of a fruit basket and a set of chocolates.

Service at the Mia Resort Nha Trang at a high level. Upon request, tourists can order breakfast, newspapers and groceries in the room. Hygiene products are regularly replenished by employees.

They clean the rooms very high quality. Some tourists write that the services of workers are somewhat intrusive, as they come to wash floors and take out trash several times a day. The ‚ÄúDo Not Disturb‚Äù sign solves the problem and needs to be hung on the door to the room.

Nutrition

The hotel has Mojito's and Sandals restaurants. The first is located near the reception, and the second is on the beach. Both serve breakfast. Their cost is included in the payment per room. As for the menu, the opinions of tourists are different. Some believe that a lot of dishes are offered, everything is tasty and fresh. Others say the food is monotonous.

For breakfast, the assortment of dishes is: cheese and sausage slices, deli meats with different sauces, soups, Vietnamese dumplings with different fillings, omelettes (cooked in front of tourists), seafood, shrimps, fish, salads, rice in various variations, fresh and baked, pastries, yogurts, fruits (mango, pineapple, grapes, agave, bananas), waffles, coffee, tea, juices. Type of food - buffet.

The hotel also has an Italian restaurant, where you can get customized dishes. In addition, there is a bar in the lobby, on the beach and on site. Here you can buy light snacks and sweets. Sandals Restaurant offers barbecue lunches and dinners every day, but for a fee. Two people can eat an average of 80-90 dollars (about 5500-6000 rubles). Since the prices are very high, the service is perfect. Waiters are ready to fulfill any request of customers with lightning speed, and dishes, tablecloths, cutlery sparkle with cleanliness.

Services for children

The hotel is always happy for young guests. The rooms are equipped with cots. A children's pool and a playground are on site.

Entertainment for adults

Many tourists like to relax in Vietnam. Mia Resort Nha Trang provides an opportunity to get acquainted with the life and cultural traditions of the country. In the lobby of the hotel you can buy a sightseeing tour of Nha Trang, a trip to the ethnic village of masters, on the island of Hong Mieu, where a huge aquarium in the form of a ship is installed, in the oceanarium. Tourists are advised to visit the mud baths and Nha Trang markets. Free buses are provided to the city three times a day. Those who wish can visit the golf club, located 4 km from the hotel.

But in the territory of the Mia Resort Nha Trang everyone can find something to their liking. The hotel has a wonderful fitness center. Upon arrival, all tourists are given free certificates for attending several spa treatments. Reviews about them are very good. The spa staff is polite, friendly, does everything with high quality.

Guests can enjoy free yoga classes on the beach on Saturdays. On Sundays, the masters conduct tai chi gymnastics classes, and on Mondays - Vietnamese language courses.

On the territory there are several large pools (except those that are near the villas). Around you can always find a free deck chair, umbrella. Also near the pools are inflatable balls and mattresses.

Sea

Many go to Vietnam for a beach holiday. The weather in Nha Trang in February is quite at its disposal. Since the resort is surrounded by mountains on land, there are no strong hurricanes and typhoons. The air temperature even in the winter months is in the range +22 ... + 24 ¬∞ C. The water temperature in the coastal waters is about 24 degrees. Therefore, you can swim here all year round.

The beach at the hotel is magnificent. Its coating is pure white sand. Entry into the water is quite convenient, there are no stones. From the most remote villa to the beach, go no longer than 2-3 minutes. Some villas are located, one might say, on the beach itself.

The weather in Nha Trang in February is good. It is not so much rains as in December and January, and they go most often at night. Such heat, which is observed in the summer months (air temperature can reach up to +40 degrees Celsius), is also not there. Humidity is moderate. You can swim in the sea at this time of the year every day. Jellyfish in the water is not observed.

The hotel's beach is especially suitable for families with children. It is completely safe for small guests.

Opinions of tourists

There are very good reviews about Mia Resort Nha Trang. Tourists give him a rating of 4.88 out of 5 possible points.

Noted advantages:

  • Friendly and helpful staff.
  • Convenient location.
  • Beautiful, clean, well-groomed area.
  • Spacious rooms.
  • New furniture, plumbing.
  • The villas have private pools.
  • Wonderful spa.
  • The hotel is practically on the beach.
  • Many free certificates for visiting the restaurant and spa.
  • Very tasty dishes, a wide range.

According to tourists, the hotel has no shortcomings, there are only minor flaws:

  • The staff does not speak Russian.
  • Not all villas have a pool.
  • Very high prices in the restaurant.
  • Buggy sometimes has to wait too long.

Conclusion: Mia Resort Nha Trang is ideal for a relaxing holiday for tourists who value comfort, luxury, excellent service. However, for those who want to visit noisy discos and nightclubs, as well as make extreme excursions during their holidays, it is better to choose another hotel.
