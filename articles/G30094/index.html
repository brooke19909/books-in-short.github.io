Everyone dreams of such a vacation.

Travels. There is nothing more pleasant than a vacation. Perhaps, only a good rest will leave pleasant memories, will give you strength and a charge of vivacity in these difficult working days. And the excellent service, of course, will make you return to your favorite place again and again. Each presents an unforgettable vacation in its own way. However, when it comes to holidays in the United Arab Emirates, tourists from all countries choose hotels in the Jumeirah line.

Who does not dream to spend a weekend in Dubai? This is one of the most beautiful cities on earth, the largest metropolis of the UAE and just a magnet for tourists from all over the world! The climate here is very hot and there is almost no rainfall. In summer, the temperature in Dubai exceeds 40 ¬∞ C, and in winter it does not drop below 18, which makes it favorable for tourists to visit the city all year round. To make your vacation in Dubai a fantastic and truly unforgettable experience, Jumeirah hotels are always happy to offer their services. Special attention of visitors attracts seven star hotel in Dubai designed in the shape of a huge sail. This place is called Burj Al Arab and it is undoubtedly well-known to everyone. The hotel invites you to appreciate its truly mesmerizing architecture, taste excellent food of national cuisine in six spacious restaurants, as well as enjoy luxurious suites with views of the coastline. It offers 24-hour car rental, a butler call, a spacious private beach and a free water park.

Those who prefer to settle right off the coast will also be pleased with the following beach hotels in Dubai . Jumeirah Beach Hotel is one of the best hotels in the city, located directly by the water. On the territory there is a huge number of various restaurants, five swimming pools, spacious and comfortable rooms with sea views, as well as a climbing wall and a diving club.
Another very popular name among beachfront hotels in Dubai is Jumeirah Zabeel Saray Royal. This place is rightly called the royal residences! 38 unique buildings decorated with unusually beautiful Turkish marble are located among the lush gardens around the lagoon pool on the island of Palma Jumeirah. The territory of Jumeirah Zabeel Saray Royal will not leave indifferent even the most fastidious tourist! Residences boast magnificent views of the Persian Gulf, as well as please with their architecture and amazing service. It features a private beach and a small but fairly spacious pool.

Jumeirah is a relatively new luxury hotel chain, which was founded in the UAE in 1997. The name "Jumeirah" quickly became popular in the hospitality industry, and by the beginning of the 2000s, many knew about it. In 2004, Jumeirah joined the largest company in Dubai Holding and began to expand actively. Today, hotels are ready to receive tourists not only in Dubai, but also in cities such as New York, Baku, Frankfurt, Istanbul, London, the Maldives, Mallorca, Rome and Shanghai. The list of cities is constantly updated. Jumeirah is famous for the highest standards of service that brought the company not only a huge number of regular customers, but also worldwide recognition. Whatever your vacation in duration, with Jumeirah hotels every day will be remembered for many, many years.
