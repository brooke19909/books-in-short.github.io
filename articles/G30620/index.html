Green Park Hotel, Pattaya - overview, features, services and reviews

Resorts in Thailand are incredibly appreciated by beach lovers. Exotic nature combined with the sea is a real paradise for vacationers. The most popular and famous resort of Thailand is Pattaya, which has long been loved by travelers. An incredible number of hotels of different levels have been built on its coast. Among them, you can find not only fashionable establishments, but also more budget options. In our article we want to talk about the three-star hotel in Pattaya "Green Park".

A bit about the complex ...

Green Park Hotel (Pattaya) is located in the heart of the resort. It was built in 1995 and has been open to visitors since then. The hotel complex is designed in modern Thai style and offers accommodation in comfortable rooms. The city center can be reached on foot in just ten minutes. Near the hotel there is a reserve and a park.

The hotel is built in an exotic garden with many palm trees and other tropical plants. The beautiful area has a relaxing holiday. The complex consists of three three-story buildings and 18 one-story cottages. The last repair in the hotel was carried out in 2014. Catering

The Green Park Hotel (Pattaya) has a 24-hour restaurant serving Thai dishes. The snack bar serves snacks and drinks throughout the day. As the hotel is located in a busy place, there are many cafes nearby where you can eat well.

In addition, all hotel rooms are equipped with kitchens, which makes it possible at any time to cook food yourself. In the immediate vicinity are grocery stores and a small market. 350 meters from the hotel there is a restaurant-bar "Mantra" and the institution "Copacobana", which tourists recommend visiting.

Complex infrastructure

The Green Park Resort Hotel (Pattaya) has a good infrastructure for a comfortable stay. A restaurant and poolside bar are on site. In the evenings, live music is available. The hotel has a fitness room, currency exchange, free parking, conference room, sauna.

A public sandy beach is 200 meters from the hotel.

Guest Services

Green Park Hotel (Thailand, Pattaya) offers the following services for its guests: dry cleaning, room service, daily cleaning, laundry, newspaper service, and ordering excursions.

A little bit about the resort ...

Pattaya is an incredibly lively and dynamic resort in Thailand. On its territory you can not only plunge into the night life, but also relax in a quiet secluded place, as well as admire the amazing Thai temples and other beauties. Pattaya is the place where you can find everything you want.

The famous resort attracts tourists with more affordable prices than Samui or Phuket. Just a few hours away is the airport, from which low-cost airlines fly to all parts of Thailand, which allows you to see the whole country.

Pattaya is divided into several areas. Green Park Hotel is located in its northern part near Naklua Beach. Rest here is more calm than in the central part, since there are fewer entertainment venues. This area is preferred by couples.

Tourists reviews

Continuing the review of The Green Park Resort 3 * (Thailand), I want to pay attention to the reviews of tourists who have already visited it. Should I recommend it for a vacation?

It is worth noting that the positive reviews about the Green Park Resort Hotel (Pattaya) characterize it from the very best side. Of course, the institution has its drawbacks, but they are not so significant. In general, guests of the complex highly recommend it as a budget vacation spot.

One of the main advantages of the hotel is its good location. It is located in a quiet area, not far from the center of Pattaya. Near the hotel there are cafes and shops, massage parlors. By bus you can quickly get to other areas.

The room fund of the Green Park Resort 3 * hotel in Pattaya (the description is given in the article) is represented by different apartments. Tourists can choose for themselves rooms in buildings or one-story bungalows. Here you need to focus on your own preferences. However, guests of the complex recommend booking standards, as they are much larger in size and more beautifully decorated than superior apartments.

The rooms located on the first floor of the buildings have a view of the pool. Bungalows are located in the back of the garden. In general, the hotel itself is built in a tropical garden and is surrounded by greenery. During the rest, the impression is that you are surrounded by a jungle. General impression of the complex
