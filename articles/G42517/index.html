Hotel 3 * Jungle Resort (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Jungle Resort 3 * (Pattaya) - one of the hotels visited by Russian-speaking tourists in Thailand. And today we will talk in detail about this institution!

Location and infrastructure

3 * Jungle Resort Hotel is located in the southern part of Thailand's most active and vibrant resort - Pattaya. Thousands of tourists from different countries relax here every year!



You can get to the Jungle Resort Pattaya 3 * from different areas of the city by tuk-tuk, taxi or motorcycle taxi. As in many resorts in the world, the fare will depend not only on the mode of transport and distance, but also on the time of year.

From Bangkok airport, if the transfer service was not provided by your tour operator or you are relaxing on your own, it is easier to get by taxi, the distance is about 120 km. Because it is very difficult and long to get to the hotel by buses, and during the day it is also unbearably hot.

Description

Hotel Jungle Resort 3 * is focused mainly on family visitors and is a small 3-storey mini-complex consisting of its own outdoor pool in the courtyard, parking, barbecue, bar and restaurant.

Rooms are accessible from the outdoor terraces. Rooms with facilities for disabled guests are available. The hotel policy includes free accommodation for children under 3 years old with two adults in one double bed. Pets are allowed.

Interior

Despite its impressive size, the 3 * Jungle Resort has fairly spacious rooms. The vast majority of visitors are delighted with the provided studios. They are closed with ordinary keys, but it is impossible to use electrical appliances and turn on the lights in the room without a key card, which will be issued together with the key to the front door during check-in.



The interior and filling are tasteful. The standard room includes a spacious double or single bed, a modern flat-screen cable TV, dressing and writing tables, a kitchenette with a fridge and a kettle, a bathroom with a shower, a washbasin, a bidet and a toilet.

There are no cutlery in the room, but you can easily get them in the restaurant of the mini-complex. It is possible to provide an extra bed at the request of the vacationer.

The rooms are equipped with modern appliances and equipment, including air conditioning. In the room, behind the panoramic sliding windows, there is a private balcony with a seating area equipped with a view of the pool and the exotic garden.

Absolutely all floors at the 3 * Jungle Resort are tiled. It is important to remember that the refrigerator during the absence of visitors will also not work, accordingly, it is necessary to exclude the content of perishable products in it during long walks outside the hotel. It will not work to leave a key card in the room for the whole day: the staff will still return it to the reception during cleaning.

Services

Registration for accommodation usually takes place very quickly, and after filling out the questionnaire and taking photocopies from the documents of the living, the staff issues a key and a key card from the room.



Customer service is provided in Thai, English and even Russian, which greatly simplifies the vacation for Russian-speaking tourists. The complex provides 24-hour security. A free shuttle to Jomtien Beach is provided. Room service, linen and towels are also free.

The rest of the service is carried out for a small tip. Valuables can be deposited in a cell in the registration lobby. Guestrooms provide complimentary bottled drinking water and complimentary fresh hot coffee / tea in the lobby.

There is a laundry room on site, where visitors to the mini-complex can refresh their wardrobe, paying only a rental washing machine and a portion of washing powder.

There is no hairdryer and iron in the room, you can ask the administration. There is a massage room. Upon indicating this information, upon registration, the hotel will prepare a surprise for the newlyweds, and those having a birthday will be pleased with a gift from the team - a company cake.

Entertainment

Like many hotels, the 3 * Jungle Resort Hotel has a 24-hour free Internet access for guests through a wireless Wi-Fi connection.





For lovers of sports activities in the courtyard of the complex there is a table tennis table, billiards, darts, which are free hotel options, as well as bicycle rental.

There is a playground for the entertainment of the youngest visitors. Also, for leisure activities, it is possible to use the barbecue area for free.

At a 5-minute distance there is a lake on which you can fish for a fee, however, the catch will still have to be released back.

Nutrition

Hotel 3 * Jungle Resort provides only a complimentary breakfast in the local bar restaurant Inc Bar, other meals are offered by the restaurant and cafe for a fee. Agree, these are pretty good conditions.



Visitors to the Jungle Resort 3 * have a variety of breakfast reviews. To some, he seemed modest, but to others it was quite enough before going to the beach. Breakfast can be chosen to your taste from several proposed options.

The surrounding area has shops and supermarkets where you can buy the necessary products and cook your own meals. If you get to the local market, then the products can be bought even cheaper than in stores. Also nearby are restaurants and cafes where tourists have the opportunity to try a variety of Thai dishes and local specialties.

Nearby Attractions

Just 700 meters from the Jungle Resort Pattaya 3 *, where several times a day a free shuttle service is provided, there is the longest beach in the district - Jomtien, the main attractions of which are the 56-story tower of Pattaya Park with an observation deck and an entertainment complex with a water park, a playground for scooters and monorail.



At a short distance (15-20 minutes walk) is the floating market of Pattaya. Also in the vicinity of the hotel you can find and visit the Tuxedo Magic Theater, which is Thailand's first magic show performed by first-class magicians and illusionists, as well as the Bottle Art Museum, which due to its originality can be attributed to one of the most unusual places in Thailand.

Visitors reviews

Tourists who visited the 3 * Jungle Resort hotel, for the most part, leave warm and kind comments. Most customers respond positively to this institution.

The reviews left by visitors to the Jungle Resort 3 * hotel (Pattaya) constantly attract new people who want to relax there, respectively, in general, visitors like the hotel.

Address and schedule

Phone: (499) 272-20-42.



The reception is on schedule. Registration of new visitors begins at 14.00 local time. It is recommended to leave the room on the day of departure until 12.00 in order to clean and prepare the room for meeting new guests.
