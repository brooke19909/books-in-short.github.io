Hotel Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * (Turkey, Marmaris): reviews

Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * is located directly on the Mediterranean coastline. It has a very modern look and offers a wide range of upscale services. Residents who are here, as a rule, evaluate the living conditions provided in this place at 9.5 out of 10 points and leave comments on sites related to the vacation process.

general information

Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * (Marmaris) was built in 1998. Since that time, a number of transformations have been carried out on its territory, during which the main building was updated, as well as some positive changes were made to the technical equipment of the apartments. As of the current moment, such an extreme renovation was carried out in 2011.

Location

The main feature of Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * is that the hotel is located on the seashore. At a short distance, literally three kilometers from it, is the central part of the resort city of Marmaris, where there is a lot of entertainment, you can go to a club, a cinema, a restaurant, a bar, karaoke and other institutions.

The airport is very far from here - 90 km, so all Russian travel agencies include a transfer service from the point of arrival to the hotel in the price of the ticket.



Rooms

In reviews of the Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 *, guests often mention the comfort of his rooms, saying that they are very bright, comfortable and completely conducive to a good rest. On the positive side, guests are considering that they have made fresh repairs.

In total, the hotel has 75 apartments, arranged in a modern style using a minimal amount of decor. All of them are divided into two groups: "standard" and "superior", and the second category is divided into three subgroups, depending on the type of color scheme in which the interior is made (red, purple and brown).

Each room has a mandatory bathroom, which has a full set of plumbing and bath accessories for showering. Each guest who comes here receives a white bathrobe, a large towel and slippers, which are replaced regularly.



All apartments have individual air conditioning, which helps many tourists in the hot season to cope with the heat and warm the air in a cooler period. Thanks to this device, the guest will feel comfortable here at any time of the year or day.

"Standard"

The rooms of the "standard" group in Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * (Turkey / Marmaris) are presented in one color scheme, which harmoniously combines coffee and white shades. Each room of this type has a private balcony with a wonderful view of the surroundings and the sea coast.

As for the furniture, the room has two berths, presented in the form of either one large bed or two small beds. There is a large table on which to write, as well as a chair. In addition, the apartment has a pair of nightstands.

The room has an electric kettle and a TV. If desired, guests can request an electronic safe and a minibar in the room, however these services are paid here. The floors of the room are covered with high-quality laminate, and the ceilings have beautiful lighting, which makes the interior of the room visually more expensive.



Superior

Apartments of this class present to guests the conditions of increased comfort. They can also accommodate a couple of guests at a time, for which there is one large French bed with an orthopedic mattress, covered with expensive linen. If desired, guests can put their things in a large wardrobe, which is also among the interior details.

The interior of this group of rooms is decorated more than in the "standard". Here on the ceilings you can find various drawings, along the walls in some apartments there are flowerbeds with living flowering plants, and in such rooms there are more lighting fixtures.

Tourists who are accommodated here can immerse themselves in the atmosphere of romance, enjoying each other and admiring the magnificent view from the window or from the personal balcony. For a pleasant tea party, there is an electric substation on which everything is installed for making hot drinks, as well as a small table made of light wood and a pair of soft half-chairs in the interior. On the photo of Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 *, made by tourists during the trip, you can observe the beauty and luxury of the interior that is available here.



Accommodation with children

While at the hotel, many tourists often conclude that it is not suitable for living with children and is more likely to spend time with your soulmate. However, the administration still offers the installation of cots, which are designed for guests under the age of 12 years, their installation is completely free. Also, in the open area for children, a small pool was organized, the depth of which is 0.5 m, the coating with which its sides are treated prevents slipping.

In public catering establishments for this category of guests there are special chairs that are more comfortable for sitting. There is also a separate menu for them, which contains dishes that can interest little gourmets not only in their appearance, but also in their taste. In their comments, parents often share the fact that children are very positive about the brightest ideas.

Nutrition

Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * (Turkey) has several catering establishments, which, according to guest reviews, serve delicious dishes. In particular, one of them is the restaurant, which is the main hotel. It serves a daily buffet, which guests are invited to stay in all types of apartments. The comments often say that the dishes of Turkish cuisine prepared here are especially delicious, despite the fact that not all of them are familiar to the Russian citizen.

If desired, each guest can visit any of the two bars, one of which is located in the hotel building, and the second - on the beach. And in one, and in another institution offers a very large selection of soft drinks and various drinks, among which there are both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. In addition, tourists in their reviews of Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * in 2017 noted the appearance of a decent assortment of desserts, among which there are traditional Turkish.

Aquazone

The hotel has a small aquazone, which every day brings together almost all the guests who prefer water treatments. It is presented in the form of a small pool, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is 77 sq.m. The water in it is constantly clean thanks to the installed automated system, but there is no heating here, the optimum temperature is achieved under the influence of sunlight.

If you want to relax, guests can stay on free white sun loungers over which beach umbrellas are installed that can protect from direct sunlight.

Hotel Services

On an ongoing basis, guests who relax here can take advantage of a number of services that are provided by the staff and the administration. In particular, for free, guests can always consult the administrator at the reception desk regarding the provision of conditions and the location of city objects, if necessary, they can call a taxi from here for the tourist.

The apartments are cleaned every day, which helps keep them clean. Three times a week, towels are replaced with fresh ones.

As regards paid features, dry cleaning, laundry and ironing services are provided here. Also, if necessary, guests can use the paid services of a doctor who is on duty at the hotel around the clock. On a free basis, all vacationers can use Internet access (Wi-Fi), which is available throughout.

If you have your own car, guests can leave it in a secure open parking lot for free.



Beach

The coastline of the Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * (Turkey) is located almost on its territory, in order to get to it, you need to overcome the road, which can be done through the underpass. The length of the beach is about 50 meters, the width is the same. This allows guests to settle in complete comfort and enjoy the rest, as well as beautiful nature. If desired, guests can take sun loungers, which are under huge umbrellas. If necessary, use a beach towel, you can take it at a special point, leaving a deposit of $ 10. Air mattresses are completely free.

As for the beach cover, it consists of medium-sized pebbles and sand, which is very convenient, a gentle descent into the sea.



Entertainment

In the Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * there is no animation, there are no night discos here, however, everyone can take part in excursions that are sold on the ground floor of the building, in a special bureau. All tours that are available here are paid, but their cost is relatively small. Reviews of Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * often say that all trips are accompanied by a professional guide who speaks Russian and has a talent to beautifully describe the sights and their history.

The hotel has a large tennis court where you can enjoy your favorite sport. Also here, tourists are offered halls for playing table tennis and billiards - their attendance is quite high. If desired, tourists can rent a bicycle at the hotel, where you can go explore the surroundings.

If you want to take part in night discos, you can go to the city center, where all this is available.

Voucher cost

A tour to the Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 * in each region of Russia can cost differently. For example, for residents of Moscow a ticket on average costs 50,000 rubles, which many can afford. Tourists note that operators for such a price offer a standard package of services, which, in addition to flight and accommodation for two, includes a set of medical insurance. Also, transfer is obligatory included in this price, as the hotel is far from the nearest international airport - 90 km (Dalaman). Experienced tourists remind you that residents of Russia must travel to Turkey on their own to get medical insurance, the cost of which in case of force majeure will pay off in full.



Hotel Accommodation Concept

In Dora Beach Hotel Marmaris 4 *, as in other places of relaxation, there are some conditions for accommodation, in violation of which the client can be evicted from the hotel in the appropriate manner. They are simple, but compliance with them is mandatory.

First of all, pets of any size are not allowed here. Secondly, rooms for people with disabilities are not provided here, and finally, thirdly, smoking is strictly prohibited in the apartments.
