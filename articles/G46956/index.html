Puerto de Santiago (Tenerife) - a place of freedom and entertainment

This seaside town is located at an altitude of 25 m above sea level, spreads 13 km along the coastline, there are approximately 6 thousand inhabitants. Puerto de Santiago has a very comfortable geographical location: the city is located in a natural bay. The harbor is protected from waves by a natural rocky breakwater, and is also artificially strengthened during urban planning.

In recent years, Puerto de Santiago has become the most beautiful tourist resort in Tenerife. Here the so-called quiet rest flourishes, for people eschewing busy tourist centers. The main attractions of Puerto de Santiago (Tenerife) are the cliffs of Los Gigantes, which reach a height of 600 m above sea level, and the world famous beach shore of Playa de la Arena, lined with black volcanic sand.

The resort of Puerto de Santiago has its own rather interesting and memorable attractions.

Espiritu De Buceo Padi 5 Star Dive Center

At this place you can enjoy scuba diving or snorkelling. All the equipment there is new, and the instructors are professionals in their field. Under water, you can see stingrays, crabs, moray eels and much more.

Route 66

Great bar, whose interior is made in American style. A delicious menu, reasonable prices, and beautiful live music.

Francy's bar

Wonderful bar, its menu is very diverse. You can sit both inside the institution and on the street. Prices will pleasantly surprise you.

Santiago Sporting Center

Interesting entertainment center. On its territory there are a large number of slot machines, bars, there is a tennis court, billiards, in addition, you can rent a bike or roller skates.

Highland Paddy 2 - Playa De La Arena

A youth place where you can have a good time with a cup of coffee after a beach holiday. In the evenings, cheerful music is played, the menu contains a variety of cocktails and light snacks.

Bar cafeteria plaza

This bar is loved by local Spaniards, the administration is friendly. There is a lot of cold beer and ready-made tasty dishes. Prices are not high and good staff.

Tobacco Vigilia Park

A nice gift shop that also sells a lot of delicacies. But most importantly, high-quality tobacco products. The prices are nice and the staff are nice.

Mega planet

This place is good for children to relax. Large playground, trampolines and balls. Playa de la Arena is a local black sand beach. There are places where the coastline strongly goes into the ocean and strong waves form from this, which are entertainment for vacationers. Also on the beach are a variety of public places. It is considered one of the best beaches in Spain.

If Activ - Tours & Trips

For excursions from Puerto de Santiago (Tenerife) you can contact If Activ - Tours & Trips, both walking and bus excursions with various stops are organized there. You can go on an excursion to Mount Teide. But this is for lovers of early rise. At 6:30, the bus stops in a cozy cafe on the slope for an early breakfast, and then goes directly to the mountain itself. If the weather is not windy, you can climb the cable car, enjoying enchanting views along the way. Many films were made in these places.

By bus, you can also go to Siam Park, drive up and pick it up directly from the hotel. On the territory and in the immediate vicinity of the resort there are several good hotels. Further we will talk about the most popular of them.

Dragos del sur

Puerto de Santiago (Tenerife) features free WiFi throughout the property. Breakfast is buffet style. There is a pool, air-conditioned rooms, non-smoking rooms, family rooms. The hotel has three stars. All furniture is new, there is a small kitchen.

For guests arriving by car, there is free parking. Within walking distance are cafes and bars, shops.

Globales Tamaimo Tropical

First, the pros and cons. The positive reviews of Puerto de Santiago (Tenerife) include the fact that this is one of the cheapest hotels in the resort. Each room has a kettle, fridge, microwave and kitchenware. Great animators work with guests. There is a car rental near the hotel, which is very convenient.

Cons: in the rooms there is no air conditioning, there is only a regular fan. There is no beach in the immediate vicinity, the ocean is very far to go. If you have valuables with you, you can only put them in storage in paid safes. Free Wi-Fi practically does not work, but paid catches well, but it is not cheap. It is difficult to find a free place in the hotel for parking a car.

Blue sea lagos de cesar

From the airport to this hotel can be reached in an hour. It is worth saying a little about this hotel. Check-in to the rooms is very quick yet, and deliciously feed at the same time. The staff is smiling and friendly, trying to help vacationers on any issues. The food, although not very diverse, but very tasty. Furniture, although not the newest, is in good condition. The ocean and beaches are within walking distance, walk no more than five minutes. Near the hotel there is a supermarket and a stop.

Hard Rock Hotel Tenerife

To begin with, if you are going to relax here with older children, then you will find a surprise. Most rooms, with the exception of superior rooms, do not have a bathroom. So if you are not ready to take a bath, or smear with sunblock in the presence of your matured children, you need to be very careful about choosing a room.

Otherwise, the hotel has many positive aspects. The rooms are very spacious, comfortable mattresses, good cosmetic sets in the bathroom, helpful staff, excellent restaurants (albeit quite expensive). Live music is pleasing, which plays not only in the evening, but also throughout the day. Absolutely amazing bar on the 16th floor. Of the minuses:

  • next to the hotel is a large construction site, which greatly spoils the view and creates noise;
  • music in the lobby plays late and prevents you from going to bed early;
  • minor flaws in the room, for example, the complete absence of hooks in the bathroom;
  • lack of a beach with access to the ocean and unrealistically dirty water near the shore.
