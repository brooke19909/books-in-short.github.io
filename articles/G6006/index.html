Scarf with braids to knitting: pattern, description of work

The use of harnesses for knitting a scarf has both positive and negative sides. Among the advantages is the undoubted attractiveness of wonderful patterns with braids. On the other side of the scale is the presence of a wrong side.

As a rule, it is customary to use bilateral ornaments when knitting scarves , as this accessory wraps around the neck several times. But if you really want to knit a scarf with braids with knitting needles, you can make a snood or wear the product correctly.

Which model to choose

Depending on what role the scarf is called to play, it is knitted from warm or cotton threads using a dense or openwork pattern. The photo shows a classic scarf with braids knitting. The patterns of such ornaments are familiar to many knitters.



The next model is more original, as it is a rather short fragment of a knitted fabric, as for a scarf. Its feature is the large width and fastener connecting the edges. If desired, instead of a placket with buttons, a simple knit stitch can be made.



Original scarf pattern

Working according to the proposed scheme, you can knit both a traditional (long), and a circular scarf with braids knitting.





The beauty of this pattern is that it is suitable for knitting thin and fairly thick yarn. Further description will be given for yarn with a thickness of 280 m / 100 grams.

Knitting : Scarf with braids

The rapports of this ornament are placed in a checkerboard pattern.





The width of each of them is 16 loops, and the height is 18 rows. The diagram shows all the rows, but only the front ones will be described, since the wrong ones are knitted according to the figure. The described sequence for each row should be repeated 4 times (for the formation of 8 rapports).

To get started, you need to dial on the knitting needles the number of loops, a multiple of 16 + 2 (for the edge). At the beginning of each row, remove the first hemline without knitting, and the last always knit the front one.

Stages of knitting

A beautiful scarf with braids with knitting needles can be knitted according to this scheme:

  1. I: three PPPs, three P knit together (P + P + P), two PERSONS P, N, 1 PERSON, N, 1 PERS, N, 1 PERSON P, N, two PERSONS, three P knit together with an inclination to the left: Take off the 1st one, knit the next two together, stretch the resulting P through the removed (PP + P), II: three PPIs, six LICPs, 1 PPI, six LICPs.
  2. I: three PPIs, (P + P + P), 1 PERSON, N, 1 PERSON P, N, 1 PERSON, 1 PPP, 1 PERSON, N, 1 PERSON, N, 1 PERSON P, (PP + P), II : three PPIs, six LIPPs, 1 PPI, six LIPPs.
  3. I: three PPIs, (P + P + P), N, 1 PERSON, N, two PERSON P, 1 PPP, two PERSONS P, N, 1 PERSON, N, (PP + P), II: three PPI, six P cross to the right, 1 PPN, six P cross to the left.
  4. Repeat rows 1-3.
  5. Repeat row 1.
  6. I: three PPIs, (P + P + P), 1 PERSON P, N, 1 PERSON, N, 1 PERSON P, 1 PERS, 1 PERSON, N, 1 PERSON, N, 1 PERSON P, (PP + P), II: three PPIs, six Ps cross to the right, 1 PPPs, six Ps cross to the left.
  7. Repeat row 3.

Further knitting should be done by changing the location of the rapports. First you need to perform the second half of the row (II), then the first (I).

Work should be continued until the blade reaches the desired length. Here, all the loops are closed so that both ends of the scarf look the same.

Shutdown

Such a scarf with braids with needles can have any length that will be convenient for its owner. Most often, the craftswomen stop at 1.5 m. Such an accessory can simply be wrapped around the neck or tied in a knot.

In the case when a circular scarf is planned for one revolution, they knit a cloth about 50 cm high. After the binding is completed, its height will increase by 4-5 cm. The width of this product must be at least 30-40 cm. To correctly determine the number of loops to set, you need to tie a control sample.

Ready scarf with braids with needles must be washed gently in warm water and dried unfolded. In the same way he is looked after during use.

When knitting with woolen threads, you need to purchase special detergents, since ordinary powder will give the product rigidity.
