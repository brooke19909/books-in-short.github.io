Hotel Sphinx Beach Resort Aqua Park 5 * (Egypt / Hurghada): photos and reviews of tourists

Egypt is famous not only for the pyramids, but also for the fabulously beautiful sea, wonderful coast, and wonderful hotels. Sphinx Aqua Park Beach Resort 5 * is on the list of the best. Perhaps this is because the owner of the Sphinx, dear Mr. Tamer, literally lives in his hotel. He very zealously monitors the order, as well as the performance of official duties by staff. According to tourists, Mr. Tamer is omnipresent. He can be seen in restaurants, on-site, and on the beach, and everywhere he is interested in vacationers, is everything all right. His second brainchild is the neighboring King Tut 4 * hotel, located a couple of tens of meters from the Sphinx Aqua Park Resort. Both guests have the opportunity to use the common infrastructure for free - it turns out as if you were relaxing in two hotels at once. They even have a common entrance through King Tut. We offer you to take a virtual walk along the Sphinx Beach Resort Aqua Park 5 *, look into the rooms, visit the restaurant, see which beach is here, get acquainted with the reviews of tourists.

Location

The city and at the same time the best Egyptian resort of Hurghada is located in the western waters of the Red Sea, 500 kilometers southeast of Cairo. Sphinx Aqua Park Beach Resort 5 * hotel is located in the most convenient place, from where it is close to the new part of the city and to the popular old Dahar district, which has preserved Arabic flavor. There are plenty of shops, shops, a large market and tourist police. The city center is about 2 km from the hotel, which can be walked or overcome by minibus. The highway and one of the main streets of Hurghada Al-Bahr runs very close. The international airport, where planes arrive from Russia, is located from Sphinx Beach Resort Aqua Park 5 * about 10 km. The neighboring sister hotel King Tut is within 100 meters. This is where tourists get to, coming from the airport or returning from the city, that is, first you need to go through King Tut, and thus get into the Sphinx Aqua Park Resort. They bring guests on special open cars.



Infrastructure

Sphinx Aqua Park Beach Resort 5 * (Hurghada) received its first guests in 2009, and the last update was held here in 2011. Its territory is compact, but unusually pleasant for walking, decorated with statues and architectural forms that mimic Egyptian antiquities, which can be seen in Giza or in museums. This design feature is very popular for photo shoots. Also on the territory there are unusually a lot of bright and lushly flowering shrubs, waving buildings, palm trees, giving coolness, green lawns, pleasing to the eye. At night, it all shines with colorful lights. Three pools, shops with various Egyptian products, restaurants, bars, a playground and a small zoo were found on the compact territory of the hotel.

Reception at Sphinx Beach Resort Aqua Park 5 * is open 24 hours a day. The staff perfectly understands and speaks Russian. It is located in a separate two-story building, in a spacious cozy lobby. Nearby is a lobby bar, Egyptian-style soft sofas and armchairs are installed, there are also modern plastic armchairs and tables, Wi-Fi catches well, a shop and a pharmacy are functioning. At the reception you can hand over things to the left-luggage office, and valuables to the safe, call a doctor, order a taxi and transfer to the airport, rent a car (parking at the King Tut Hotel), buy sightseeing tours, and exchange currency.

Sea

The Red Sea and wonderful beaches - this is why many go to Egypt. Curiously, some travel agencies position this hotel as Sphinx Aqua Park Beach Resort 4 stars, while others as 5 stars, which significantly affects the price of tickets. Hence the guests dissatisfied with the service. But a beach holiday is not satisfactory for anyone. This is because there are two different beaches at once, as they say, for every taste. The one assigned to the Sphinx Aqua Park Resort is equipped with stone-paved steps (no railing) leading directly into the water. The depth here begins right away, which swimmers really like. In addition to the steps, there is also a small pier. You can go snorkelling and diving in this part of the sea, or windsurf in the wind. The necessary equipment is provided at the diving center of the hotel. There you can take scuba diving lessons. Of particular interest to tourists are living corals and their fabulously beautiful inhabitants, which can be seen from the pier.

The beach, assigned to King Tut, is sandy, with a gentle approach to the sea and a shallow depth off the coast. There are sun loungers and umbrellas, always available. Hotel workers clean the sand every day early in the morning, but due to dishonesty of some tourists, they already find cigarette butts and other small garbage in it by noon.

On the beach you can ride a camel, and in the sea - on a catamaran, banana, parachute. Many excursions are also offered here.
