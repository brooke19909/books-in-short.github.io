Where to stay in Crimea? "Boar Pass" - recreation center (Crimea).

Holidays in Crimea are attractive because here you can always find entertainment for every taste. Tired of the heat? You need to climb a little higher into the mountains and enjoy the morning freshness. Tired of climbing the peaks? Well, the gentle sand and the alluring sea await below. Extreme walks, a rich excursion program, monuments of architecture and nature - in Crimea there is everything. But the most important thing when planning a vacation on the peninsula is to know where to stay. Today we consider "Boar Pass" - a recreation center in Crimea No. 1 in the southern part of the mountain range.

Location

If you are tired of the bustle of the city and the noisy beaches and you want to find solitude in nature, feel free to go to the Bakhchisarai district. This is a great place where you can not only touch the most ancient remnants of the Khan's culture, but also relax mentally, enjoying the coolness and freshness of the mountain air. It is in this area that Kabaniy Pass is located - a recreation center (Crimea).

Tourist camping is located on the pass of the same name, between the Baidar Valley and the Belbek River Valley . Here is a picturesque lake, on the banks of which houses on stilts are built, which are guest rooms, a restaurant and simple gazebos for relaxation.

"Boar Pass" is a recreation center of the Crimean Peninsula, it is located a bit off the road, between the villages of Polyana and Putilovka. It will not be difficult to find her by following the signs on the road.



What is on the base

"Boar Pass" - a recreation center (Crimea), where there is a great opportunity to stay overnight. It is suitable for both family vacations and fun companies. The only caveat - rooms need to be booked in advance. All guests will be accommodated in small apartments by the lake. The houses are mainly built on stilts, so in the morning you will enjoy a beautiful mountain view and breathe in cool freshness.

In order to have a snack or dinner, you do not need to travel far. There is also a small restaurant with an outdoor area and lake views. It is possible to fry kebabs on the grills or just have a picnic in one of the gazebos.

Among the services offered are also:

- fishing (all gear is available for hire);

- riding on ATVs;

- catamarans and boats;

- hunting for animals;

- quiet hunting for mushrooms;

- fireworks and other entertainment at will.

Also on the territory there is a playground and parking for vehicles.

Reviews

Most tourists like the "Boar Pass" recreation center (Crimea). They leave positive reviews. Of the advantages, of course, nature is noted, proximity to the main attractions, both natural and man-made, good infrastructure, responsive and kind staff. Almost everyone notes that this place is ideal for a family weekend. Here you can relax your body and soul, and at the same time fry the kebabs and ride on the mirror surface of the water, surrounded by picturesque mountains.

But cons were found. Basically, tourists found fault with the quality of the rooms. Some lacked hot water, although the booklet said that the rooms had a boiler. He was, but did not work. It was also unpleasant to be in a dirty room, which either did not have time, or did not want to clean. There are a lot of people at the recreation center all the time. You need to book an overnight stay at least one month in advance. In the gazebos it is very rare to freely and the staff often just do not have time to clean up after all. But this is a problem for people themselves who need to be more careful with nature and take out the trash.





We offer you to enjoy the beautiful views that abound "Boar Pass" recreation center (Crimea). Although photos will not convey the whole atmosphere, they will help you to feel the grandeur of the highlands.

Majestic mountains and palaces

The pearl of the Crimea is Mount Ai-Petri. From the "Boar Pass" to her handshake. Do not miss the opportunity to enjoy beautiful views from the highest point of the peninsula. And down down the cable car.

Well, on the way to the "Kabaniy Pass" (recreation center, Crimea), be sure to drop into Bakhchisaray itself and visit the Khan's Palace.
