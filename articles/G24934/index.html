Photoshoot with friends: ideas, recommendations

Human memory holds the best moments, but some events are worth it to perpetuate them in photographs. A photoshoot with friends will not only help you get beautiful pictures, but it can be a great idea for an interesting pastime in a warm company.





You can arrange a shooting on the occasion of a pre-wedding bachelorette party, a party on the occasion of graduation, or give a certificate for a photo shoot to a loved one for the holiday. A small selection of ideas will help you perfectly prepare for this event, so that the shooting result does not disappoint, but gives only pleasant memories.

Concept selection

Today, creativity is relevant in everything. If you want to get really cool photos, consider in what way the shooting will take place. Thoroughly discuss this with your friends, trying to ensure that every voice is heard. Your friendly company will look at the pictures as a whole, if you can develop a common concept. It is not necessary to dress the same at all, but the images must be combined harmoniously. Even if each of the girlfriends prefers a different style of dress, this idea can be beaten. Underlining the identity of each of the girls is a great idea for a photo shoot.





It will not be superfluous to heed the advice of the photographer. Initiate it in your plans in advance. His advice can be very helpful.

Shooting preparation

Many professional photographers do not work with models that have not passed through the skillful hands of a makeup artist and hairdresser. Even if you are fluent in the art of everyday makeup, you should trust a professional before shooting.

If you want the result of a photo session with your friends to be high-quality pictures, you can not do without a competent make-up and styling.

What should be the makeup for the photo shoot? Sculpting the face is not a mandatory step, but a smooth matte skin tone, masking imperfections and emphasizing the beauty of the eyes are simply necessary. In the hairstyle, the main thing is accuracy. Knocked out of the total mass of hairs can make the image untidy.

Time and place

A professional photo studio is a place where you can realize many ideas for a photo shoot with a girlfriend. In summer, you can take photos even on the street. Both options are good, but the first one will require significant costs, but it will be possible to shoot at any time convenient for you.

But in order to get beautiful shots in nature or in the city, you may have to get up early. Under direct sunlight, the shadows look sharper, and people are older. But the rays of sunset or dawn can be an amazing backdrop for shooting a company of pretty girls.

Or maybe you have a special place? Go to places where you loved to walk in your youth, skipped school lessons, gossiped about the boys.





A good idea would be a home pajama-style home shoot. Emphasize the ease and sincerity that is inherent in your relationship, put on pajamas or sportswear, arrange a pillow fight.

Image creation

It's great if you have the opportunity to rent beautiful dresses in which your company will become like a flock of fairies. But this is not the only option.

Consider other ideas for a photo shoot with friends. Photos can be made in the style of mafiosi of the early twentieth century, dude, hippie, pin-up.

Entourage and props

Of course, you should take care of the props. A photoshoot with your friends will turn out to be colorful if you agree to rent a retro car, a wicker rocking chair, and a hanging swing.

Of course, money is not necessary at all. It is possible to emphasize the power of true friendship, which is not afraid of adversity, even with the help of an ordinary cozy plaid or a large umbrella.

Naturalness in trend

Do not try to repeat what you have already seen on other people's photos, and do not be afraid to try to do something completely unusual by ordering a photo shoot with your friends. The ideas of each of you are worthy of implementation.

But do not abuse images, because the purpose of the shooting is to capture and preserve the moments that really were. Do not turn a photo shoot into a carnival. Be yourself, this is the main decoration of every girl.

Emotions





A smile on duty, even a very beautiful one, is not the best idea for a photo shoot with friends.

Surprise, be cunning, frightened, angry, tease each other, laughing with all your heart! Do not be afraid of hugs and kisses (of course, if your relationship is characterized by such manifestations of feelings). Show emotions, then your joint pictures will turn out truly sincere.
