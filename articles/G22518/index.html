Bouquets of sweets for Easter - a nice sign of attention

The Easter holiday is celebrated not on any particular day, but according to the lunar calendar. This holiday is a symbol of the victory of good. He keeps the memory of the historical sacrifice of Jesus Christ in the name of all mankind and its sacred resurrection. Celebrate the holiday, usually presenting each other Easter cakes and colored eggs, as well as gifts made by their own hands. Bouquets of sweets for Easter - a nice gift that will surprise and delight your family.



Easter holiday

Easter is preceded by the Great seven-week fast. At this time, believers abide by certain rules, restricting themselves to food. The day before Easter, on Holy Saturday, people go to church, pray, bring food to the temple to light it. And on Sunday, once a year, they put on the table consecrated food: Easter cake, Easter cottage cheese and colored eggs.

Saturday is replaced by bright Sunday, and after a seven-week fast you can already eat any dishes - salads, snacks, cakes, sweets.

If you are going to visit, consider candy bouquets (for Easter) as a gift option. This simple "design" will serve as a decoration for the festive table.

Easter Gifts

As a rule, on Easter it is customary to exchange colored eggs. The egg in ancient times before Jesus Christ was considered a symbol of the world, the universe in miniature. Some ancient peoples believed that it was the Sun and Life, and as a sign of worship and respect, they painted eggs.





Now this tradition has been preserved. People continue to paint eggs in every way, however, Happy Easter comes after a long seven-week fast, so it is customary to give small gifts like this for any other holiday. Bouquets of sweets for Easter will be very handy. Such gifts can be given to women of any age.

You can make bouquets of sweets for Easter with your own hands, it is enough to stock up with paper that bends easily, with wire, glue and, in fact, a box of sweets.

Bouquets of sweets are a great gift

Want to surprise your loved ones? To make a holiday gift memorable? Order a bouquet of sweets for them. This wonderful sweet gift will not leave anyone indifferent. For the Spring Festival (on March 8), a bouquet of Tulips candies is just the way to go. A very delicate multi-colored bouquet will delight any woman, and sweet tooth will be delighted.



You can also give a bouquet of sweets for Easter. It can be very small or huge, for mom, bride or brother. A whole basket, topiary or one delicate flower - a huge number of options! You must admit that a box of chocolates or a bar of chocolate usually looks banal, and a spectacular gift in the form of a sweet bouquet will impress anyone.

DIY candy bouquets

It is enough to know a little about floristry to create beautiful bouquets of handmade sweets. It is important to be able to control color, everything should look harmonious. Creative and delicious dessert should be pleasing to the eye, be bright and noticeable.



To create a bouquet you need to make a number of important decisions:

  • Determine the type of composition itself. It can be roses, tulips, snowdrops. You need to build on the preferences of the person to whom the gift is intended.
  • Decide which design will look advantageous. A box, tree or basket, bouquet or topiary.
  • Find out which candies the recipient prefers and try to guess, because different sweets can come in handy to create a variety of flowers. For snowdrops, small sweets are needed, for tulips and roses - more.
  • Think through the decor of the composition. Perhaps it will be butterflies or hearts, ribbons or leaflets. Everything to your taste, the main thing is that it looks harmonious.
  • Buy tools for creating a bouquet (glue gun, corrugated paper, rhinestones or ribbons).

Creating a bouquet of roses

Perhaps the most common bouquet is a bouquet of roses. To create it you need:

  1. Corrugated paper.
  2. Scotch.
  3. Glue gun.
  4. Scissors.
  5. Candy.
  6. Wooden stick.

Cut out the petals of different sizes, you can have different colors. Put the sweets in the petal and wrap the edges. Then we glue them on a wooden stick with a gun and continue to glue the remaining petals in a circle. Decorate the resulting rose with petals.

A bouquet of Roses candies is a wonderful gift for any occasion.

Whatever gift you give, your attention is always more expensive. And if this attention is accompanied by a handmade gift, it will delight your loved one.
