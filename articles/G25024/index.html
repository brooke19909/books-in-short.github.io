Learning to knit an elegant star pattern with knitting needles

Many patterns and ornaments made by knitting needles have already been invented. Many of them amaze with their splendor and originality. Looking at some drawings, I wonder how you can create such beauty with your own hands. To the category of "best-most" without a doubt include the pattern of "stars". Knitting it, it turns out, is not at all difficult. In this article, we will present you a detailed description of the methodology for performing this figure. In addition, the knitting pattern of the "star" with knitting needles in the technique of jacquard ornament and applique is also shown here. We hope you find this information useful.

Asterisk pattern. With knitting needles we learn how to knit a relief fabric

In front of you you see a photo of a "star" picture. Various items of clothing perform it, but it looks especially good on dresses, sweaters, sweaters and hats. This is not to say that this pattern is only suitable for women's things. It is quite textured, so it is clearly visible on the canvas of a dark color, from which men's clothing is usually made. So, master the knitting - pattern "stars".

Dial 25 stitches. 1st row: remove the edge, * 1 front loop, knit 3 * of three stitches, repeat from * - * icons to the end of the row, sew the last loop from the wrong. 2nd and all other even rows to perform purl. 3rd row: remove the hem line, 2 knit the front loops, * 1 front, 3 out of 3, 3 *, repeat from * to * to the end of the row. In the fifth row, start repeating the pattern as in the first.

For beginner needlewomen, we explain this method of knitting in detail. Here are 3 loops. Insert a knitting needle into them, grab the working thread, pull the front, then double crochet, knit the front again, and only then fold all the loops from the right knitting needle.



Jacquard pattern

The "star" pattern with knitting needles can be made in the form of an ornament. An example of one of them you can see in the photo. How do such drawings fit ? To complete this option, two threads will be used in the work at once - light and dark. All loops in all rows are made face. In the diagram, one square is equal to one loop, one horizontal line to one row. The pattern is knitted from top to bottom. In places where the ornament passes from one color to another, the threads are twisted together from the wrong side, and a different color of yarn is introduced into the work. When performing jacquards, many needlewomen complain that the fabric is compressed. This is because during knitting with a thread of one color, the other remaining on the wrong side is tightened. This cannot be done. She should hang freely.



Knitted applique

The "star" pattern can be made with knitting needles and in this version, as shown in the photo. The technology is the same as in the design of jacquard. The diagram shows all rows and loops. To make the pattern neat and beautiful, it is important that the yarn of both colors be the same thickness. Such a star will look good on children's clothes, scarves, mittens.

Let the process of creating knitted stars bring you pleasure and only positive emotions!
