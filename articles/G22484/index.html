Hotel "Aurora" In Chelyabinsk.

Just ten minutes walk from the main railway station of Chelyabinsk and half an hour drive to the international airport, on the street Dzerzhinsky is modern and comfortable Hotel Aurora , near which there is a park named after Pushkin and the central district of the city. A two-minute drive away is the M5 motorway. Aurora is one of the new Chelyabinsk hotels , which was opened in 2007 and is located in the building of the new business center of the city.

The Hotel Chelyabinsk offers sixty comfortably furnished rooms that are divided into different categories: single standard room, double standard, double superior and deluxe rooms. Each of these rooms will provide its guests with a telephone with long-distance and international calls, a flat-screen TV, cable TV, a minibar, as well as a trouser press and parquet floor . Junior suites are more chic because they have a separate dining room, and slippers and a bathrobe are provided. In addition, the rooms are much more spacious than the rest of the standard rooms - their area is 46 square meters - against 36 square meters. Superior double rooms have a separate dressing room. In all types of hotel rooms you can easily connect to the Internet, and also completely free.

In the morning, guests at the Aurora Hotel can enjoy a wholesome breakfast with wholesome food - a buffet breakfast (which is included in the room rate), and in the evening enjoy delicious food at the Honey restaurant, which serves international and European cuisine. Also, the price of the room also includes secure parking. Hotel guests have their own spa center, where you can enjoy a sauna, relaxing massages and a solarium. If you come with a small child, then you should urgently use the services of a nanny at the hotel. There are also conference rooms, the atmosphere of which is suitable for business meetings and meetings (for 350 people, for 120 people and for 30 people), a meeting room (for 25 people). Aurora is one of those hotels in Chelyabinsk, which Suitable for businessmen, and just for tourists, and for newlyweds, and for large families.
