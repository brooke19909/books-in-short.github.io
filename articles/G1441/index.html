Original DIY tea bouquets

Gift decoration takes on new interesting forms. The direction of suite design, which includes the compilation of beautiful compositions from sweets, is becoming increasingly popular. And where there are desserts, there are drinks. Bouquets of tea or coffee - no less than an original gift. Moreover, you can perform such a composition yourself.

Collection of basic and additional materials

To work, you will need:

  • a set of tea bags in individual packaging;
  • chocolate candies, truffles in foil;
  • artificial flowers or skewers;
  • tea mug or cup;
  • crepe paper;
  • thin wire or thread.

The process of making flowers from tea bags

First you need to navigate in multi-colored packages and decide what color your bouquets of tea will be. You can make the petals multi-colored or select all of the same shade, for example, gold. Lay out on the table the outlines of future inflorescences and make a choice.

Free artificial flowers from buds, leaving only cuttings with upper small leaves. Or prepare another material for the stems - skewers or rods of copper wire. Even plastic straws for drinks will do.

Wrap sweets in an additional layer of foil with sharp tips outwards, so that it looks like unopened buds.

Bouquets of tea consist of several flowers. To get them, you need to bend the two upper corners of each bag towards each other, forming the shape of a petal. Connect a few petals on the back with a stapler, tape or paper clips.

You can chop a lot of rectangular petals in the form of an open fan, glue it with scotch tape or hot glue using a floristic gun.

Insert a candy in the middle of the tea flower , leaving a long bundle of twisted foil on the back. Fasten this long end by wrapping it around the stem. Secure with a thin wire or thread.

Gather several flowers together and put in a mug or porcelain cup. In the latter version, you need to put a piece of plasticine at the bottom so that the stems stand evenly and beautifully without falling apart in different directions.

The simplest bouquets of tea at this stage can be considered complete. You can make them quickly, and almost any person will cope with the task.





Crepe paper accessory

If the primitive version does not satisfy, you can diversify it with decorative additions from corrugated paper, which is often used in floristic compositions and remains after flowers. A handmade bouquet of tea can be a real work of art.

Paper must be cut into wide stripes. Stretch each such tape on one side, spreading the folds. Then wrap around each flower or make paper buds separately. To collect all in one composition and arrange in a bag of floral paper.





Such an exquisite bouquet of tea can be presented to colleagues, presented to a lover of tart drink, or simply used as a decoration for a festive table. Additionally, chocolate, sweets, coffee, artificial flowers and leaves are optionally added to the composition. The scope for self-expression is unlimited, this is a great way to make a simple but effective DIY gift.
