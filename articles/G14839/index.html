Reason for creativity: do-it-yourself dresses for girls

Every mother wants her daughter to be the most beautiful and elegant. Domestic and foreign manufacturers strive to surpass each other, realizing this desire. And to choose an outfit for a little princess today is not difficult. But much more interesting when mothers create dresses for girls with their own hands! Moreover, for this it is not necessary to have the skills of a professional seamstress. There is enough knowledge once acquired at school in labor lessons, and the desire to give the baby an original thing.



Dresses for girls are created using a variety of technologies, depending on what the mother-needlewoman prefers. They are knitted and crocheted, sewn from whole fabric and shreds, combine fabric and yarn. When a thing is done with love (and how else can you create for your own daughter?), It always turns out to be very beautiful. Well, if these are your first steps in needlework and not everything turns out as you would like, it's okay! Children grow up quickly, and after some six months, clothes for girls will need new ones.

Where to get a pattern?

Of course, you can choose your favorite item in the fashion magazine for needlework. There will be not only a pattern, but also a detailed description of the process. However, today, as before, not all needlewomen are satisfied with the proposals of fashion designers. Indeed, most often dresses for girls with their own hands are created for every day, and not for a single show on the catwalk. In addition, it is impossible to find the right model immediately. And the price of quality magazines is high enough to buy them in large quantities. Actually, this makes sense only if there is an intention to make the creation of children's clothing a source of income.

What material to choose?

It all depends on your imagination, financial capabilities and the ultimate goal. It is obvious that dense woolen fabric is not suitable for a summer dress , and silk or chiffon is not very suitable for winter. It is best to choose natural fabrics in bright colors. For the warmer months, cotton and linen are a great choice. For cold weather - wool, fleece, jeans. You can choose fabrics with colorful prints. And you can use plain, decorating the finished thing with lace, applique, embroidery. The main thing here is not to overdo it so that beauty does not turn into bad taste.

If you are not going to sew, but to knit a dress for your girl, then here, preference is given to natural fibers. Synthetic yarn, even the most spectacular and wearable, is best left for outerwear that is worn for short periods of time. After all, it is assumed that the dress is worn for the whole day, and the body needs to breathe.
