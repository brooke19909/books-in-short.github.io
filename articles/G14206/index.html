Needlework lessons: knitting slippers with needles (with a description of the implementation of all parts of the product)

How nice it is to return home from the street in the cold season. The first thing we do when we go into the apartment is change our shoes into slippers. Indoor shoes should be comfortable, cozy and very warm. Knitted slippers can be a good alternative to factory slippers. If you are "friends" with knitting needles, then to make this kind of slippers on your own is not difficult. In this article, a master class is presented to the attention of the craftswomen, which describes how to perform knitting with slippers. For beginners, this instruction will be a real find, because the technology for performing this product is set out in great detail and clearly.



Track slippers. Beginning of work

Knitting needles with slippers (with a description of the performance of all the elements) we begin with the design of the back of the product. We collect 40 loops on the knitting needles. We knit a fabric 6-7 centimeters high with a 1x1 elastic band. Next, we will form the heel. For this, we divide the loops into three parts (13, 14, 13). We knit only the central 14 stitches. At the same time, at the end of each row, we perform the last loop of the middle part together with the first loop of the side of the canvas. Thus, we knit until only 14 central loops remain on the knitting needles.

Foot

We continue to master knitting with slippers. A description of the implementation of the lower part of the trace is given below. On the sides of the middle part of the product we raise 10 loops to the needles. Next, we make out the foot, knitting all 34 loops with a direct canvas. Try on a slipper on the foot. When its length reaches the little finger, proceed to the implementation of the sock. It is knitted according to the heel principle: the loops are divided into three parts (11, 12, 11) and are formed by the reduction method at the end of each row, 2 together with the central and lateral parts. When 12 loops remain on the spoke, work on the design of the bottom of the track will be completed.



Slippers Upper - Rise

We studied the technology of the heel and foot. We continue to familiarize ourselves with such a craft as knitting with needles of slippers (with a description of all processes), namely with the methodology for performing the upper part of the product. Knit the next row according to the instructions below. Grab one loop from the side of the track. Now there are 13 loops on the needles. Knit 5 loops face, then perform a decrease (3 loops together), the next 5 loops - face and grab the last loop of the side. Turn the job over. Knit all the loops wrong, at the end of the row grab one loop. The next row is performed in the same way, only in the middle the reduction from 3 loops into one is formed again. Thus, a strip is formed, which is the upper part of the trace. Shape it to your desired length. Due to the "tack" of the side loops, the product is solid, and it will not need to be stitched.

Master knitting: slippers with two knitting needles. Tips for beginner needlewomen

In order for slippers in the form of tracks to be warm, you should choose the appropriate yarn. It is desirable that these be woolen threads or half-wool, in the composition of which acrylic is no more than 50%. Do not take thin yarn for making slippers. The ideal option is 300 meters in 100 grams of thread.



As for the patterns, the foot is best done with front stitch or garter stitch. These drawings are simple in design, have a smooth structure and good density. The product made by them is very warm.

In this article, you familiarized yourself with the technology of such a direction in needlework as knitting slippers (with a description of the design of all the details). We hope that this master class will help you make beautiful and warm slippers.
