Sheraton Sharm - Holidays in Egypt

Description: Sheraton Sharm Hotel is located in the famous resort center of Sharm El Sheikh, on the shores of the beautiful bay of Sharm El Maya. It is located in close proximity to the Old Town. It is 20 kilometers from Sharm El Sheikh Airport. The hotel is surrounded by emerald greenery and flowers, among which there are ten 2-storey bungalows. All this is in perfect harmony with the nearby fabulous cliffs, creating an amazing atmosphere.




Rooms: The Sheraton Sharm Hotel Foundation consists of 312 comfortable rooms, including 298 standard, 10 family and 7 "suites". All of them are spacious and comfortable, decorated in bright colors. All rooms are air-conditioned. There is a bath, shower, hairdryer. The telephone line is up. There is a TV with satellite TV (1 Russian-language channel). There is a minibar that is filled with free water every day. There is a safe. One of the rooms is carpeted on the floor , and the second is ceramic. Not all rooms have a balcony. If necessary, one extra bed can be installed.


Meals: Sheraton Sharm Hotel offers All Inclusive meals ("Buffet"). Covered in the main restaurant. In addition to the main hall, there is a summer playground. Upon reservation, breakfast can be served directly in the room. In addition, throughout the day, all bars offer free snacks and all kinds of local drinks. As for ice cream and freshly squeezed juices, you will have to pay for them. On the shore there is a fish restaurant decorated as a fisherman‚Äôs hut. There is a bar on the beach. There is a disco bar at night.

Beaches: Sheraton Sharm El Sheikh Hotel has its own beach, which is 250 meters long. It is covered with fine golden sand. The beach has a shower and changing rooms. The palm grove stretching along it gives a special flavor to the beach. It allows vacationers to take refuge in the shade of picturesque trees during the hottest daytime hours. On the beach, tourists are offered all kinds of entertainment: a jet ski ride, a boat trip, scuba diving and just playing beach volleyball.

Information for tourists: if you choose to relax in Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh Sheraton is a place where you can have a great vacation and relax from the bustle of the city. There are 2 pools of different depths, 3 water slides of varying degrees of difficulty. Reception is open around the clock. The administrator has a safe where guests can store their valuables during their stay at the hotel. For those who decide to combine leisure with work, a conference room with modern equipment is provided, which can accommodate up to 60 people.



For sports, there are two excellent tennis courts. In addition, you can play table tennis, volleyball, darts, billiards (for a fee), as well as engage in archery. The hotel has a shopping center, spa, laundry. For those who wish to be irresistible during their holidays, a beauty salon is open. If necessary, you can use the services of a doctor (paid). In the evening there is animation.

And for those who participated in various excursion trips throughout the day, a sauna, Turkish bath or Jacuzzi will help to relax.

Reviews: Sheraton Sharm Hotel has excellent green areas. A comfortable beach with a palm grove where you can hide from the scorching sun.

Accommodation in almost a separate bungalow allows you to feel the comfort of home, which the hotel tried to create for its guests. The rooms are spacious and comfortable, with comfortable furniture.

The food here is excellent. A huge selection of all kinds of dishes that you want to try. Well, what kind of hotel without an attentive and polite staff? He is ready to always listen to you and, if necessary, help.
