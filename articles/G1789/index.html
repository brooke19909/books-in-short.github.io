Location

The tourist complex Wanbo Health Club Hotel 3 * is located in a picturesque area on Hainan Island, in an ecologically clean corner of the world - Sanya Bay. From the hotel to the city center - about 15 minutes drive to the Phoenix Airport - only 9 kilometers. The distance from the hotel to the beach is one hundred meters. The hotel is a five-minute drive from West Island and Cape Tianya Haijiao. Half an hour from here you can drive to Nanshan Temple and the duty-free shopping complex. Hotel guests are provided with free car parking.

The food system in Wanbo Health Club Hotel 3 * provides various options: you can order breakfast only, then the tour price will be significantly lower, or you can purchase a ticket with breakfast, lunch and dinner included and relax just like in the resorts of Turkey or Egypt, without thinking about finding local cafes and restaurants.

It should be noted right away that when choosing this hotel, and indeed rest in the resorts of China, it should be understood that the service here is very different from the service in countries that have long been loved by Russian tourists. Excursions in China

Hainan is a very picturesque island, and visiting it, you must definitely explore the most interesting places. Directly in the Sanya area from the Wanbo Health Club Hotel 3 * you can go on various excursions, which are a huge choice. Here are the most requested ones.

Sightseeing tour

Sanya is the southernmost settlement of China and the country's only tropical resort. According to environmental indicators, it ranks 1st in China and 2nd in the world.





A city tour gives tourists the opportunity to get acquainted with its history and population, visit the legendary Oleniha Mountain, see its landscape park, visit the Hainan deer nursery, get acquainted with local myths.

Monkey island

A fascinating tour that is worth a visit to vacationers with children. The tour includes a visit to the island, where about 2100 monkeys live, climbing a funicular and watching the monkey circus.

Oceanarium

Marine Chinese Zoo will introduce tourists to the life of marine life. There is also a crocodile show, a presentation of sea lions and trained parrots.

Excursion to the "End of the World"

"End of the World" is a park on the seashore with an unusually beautiful landscape. On a sandy beach, stone blocks of various shapes rise here.

Nanshan

The largest center of Buddhism in Asia. An ancient temple has been restored and a beautiful landscape park has been laid out, and in a separate pavilion there is the largest golden statue on earth (140 kg), decorated with precious stones.

Radon sources

The tour is in demand among fans of various bathtubs.

Pirate Island

Here tourists are waiting for white sand beaches, coral reefs, azure water and a full range of entertainment.

Also, many are interested in excursions to the botanical garden, the folk village, Haitan Bay, the Butterfly Museum, the Zoo of Tigers and Crocodiles, the Bird Park, the Dong Tien temple complex, museums of crystal and pearls, a silk salon, to the hot springs of Tianyuan and Nantian, sea fishing and others.
