Heviz Lake, Hungary - a healing pearl framed by the green edge of the Hungarian forests

Heviz Lake, Hungary is the largest geothermal reservoir of natural origin in the world. This place is a real gem framed by evergreen pine forests. Healing steam of warm thermal waters always swirls over the smooth surface of the lake. The very name "hévíz" translated from Hungarian means "current warm spring." Lake Héviz, Hungary - this is the bewitching beauty of landscapes, the silence of the surroundings, the cleanest air and healing thermal waters. Here everyone has the opportunity to gain peace of mind, calm and relax from the hustle and bustle of megacities.

Heviz Lake (Hungary) and its secrets



The stunning balneological resort located 200 km from the capital is open all year round. Secrets of its increased popularity:

  • truly healing waters saturated with minerals, gases;
  • natural dirt containing minerals.

From summer to late autumn, vacationers can watch Lake Heviz drowning in beautiful pink and gray colors. Hungary gave the world the largest warm lake of volcanic origin. The total surface area is 47.5 square meters and a depth of 38 m. The reservoir is completely renewed in 3.5 days, and its temperature never drops below 24-26 ° C. Heviz is fed by 10 sources - eight cold (17 ° C) and two hot (40 ° C). The temperature difference between the deep layers and the upper makes water constantly circulate. Due to this, a bathing temperature and an optimal concentration of healing substances are maintained. The combination of forest air saturated with ozone and intensive vaporization above the surface of the lake creates a favorable healing climate, which is very important for those who came to these places for treatment.

Heviz Lake, Hungary. Rheumatology Clinic named after St. Andras



The baths erected by the family of Count Festetics became the basis for the creation of the first building of the clinic of St. Andrash. It was headed by Dr. Schulhof Wilmosch in 1906, and in 1911 opened two modern departments - Roentgen and Zander. Here he received patients who needed correction of the musculoskeletal system.

Soon Wilmos joined the work of his brother - Schulhof Eden, a specialist in physical therapy and radiology. The brothers' achievements were so significant that on January 1, 1952, the Ministry of Health established the State Thermal Clinic on the basis of the complex, the heart of which was Lake Heviz, Hungary. Reviews of numerous tourists indicate an exclusively positive effect of preventive procedures carried out at the clinic. People with impaired posture, inflammatory pathologies of the musculoskeletal system, rheumatic diseases of the organs of motion, diseases of the vertebral cartilage, rheumatism of soft tissues, secondary pathologies of the joints and other ailments are sent here.

The combination of useful with cognitive



Rest on the lake can be combined with an excursion program. Nearby is the small town of Keszthely. It is here that the largest estate in Hungary is located - the Festetics white stone palace, created in the Baroque style. Tours to Lake Heviz, Hungary - this is an opportunity:

  • visit the medieval castle of Sümege, take part in a knightly tournament and celebrate the victory with a delicious dinner accompanied by the tart wine of the Badacsone region;
  • visit the capital of Styria;
  • take a tour of Budapest, Vienna;
  • go on a trip along one of the routes: Sopron-Fertd; Keszthely - Tihany - Balatonfured; Veszprem - Herend; Gyor - Bratislava.

Local beer festivals, operetta evenings and interesting street balls are often organized for all visitors to Heviz.
