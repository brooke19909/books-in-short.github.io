Are you planning to visit Egypt? Marsalam is a worthy place for your visit. This is a young modern resort located in the Red Sea. Despite the significant remoteness of the city from Hurghada (more than 270 km) and El Quseira (over 130 km), this place attracts an increasing number of tourists from different countries.

What attracts tourists to the city?

Do you admire Egypt? Marsalam is the city that will amaze you even more. As a resort, it is located 70 km along the picturesque shores of the Red Sea. This place was glorified by unique representatives of the local flora and fauna.

Tourists can witness the amazing life of dolphins, dugong, manta rays and hammerhead sharks. Here the layman will plunge into the indescribable beauty of the pristine wealth of coral reefs, hiding the secrets of millennia under the thickness of clear blue waters. Of interest to visitors are thickets of mangroves. Holidays in Egypt (Marsalam) - an unforgettable and exciting experience for divers from all over the world seeking to visit one of the major centers of the Red Sea.

From tourists who visited the city of Marsalam (Egypt), reviews received enthusiastic. After all, this area is the warmest climatic zone on the entire coast of the Red Sea.

The location of the city allows tourists to explore the historical monuments of Upper Egypt. From here, vacationers are offered excursions to Luxor, Edfa and Abu Simbel, although these trips take more than one hour due to the need to travel through Safaga.

Egypt has many historical secrets. Marsalam will offer visitors the main attraction - Wadi Hammamat, which has long dried up the channel of the right tributary of the Nile. On the shortest path from Thebes to the sea coast, the gaze of stunning beauty opens to the eyes of vacationers. Entertainment

Ideal conditions for active pastime is Egypt. Marsalam offers tourists the most breathtaking view among the "land" types of leisure - a ride on quads. The unique relief of the local desert, the hills that are insignificant in height will provide thrill-seekers with an exciting safari. Anyone can join the ranks of extremals, the Egyptians are ready to teach the basics of driving any guest of the country.





The most popular beach for vacationers is the bay of Abu Dabab, where in the picturesque lagoon you can see turtles of gigantic sizes and local natives - dugongs. This place is the home of the royal moray eel.

Tourists will remember an interesting and informative boat trip to the national reserve on the Kulan Islands. It includes four islands in its territory, three of which are available for snorkeling. The fourth is closed to visitors, as it is a nesting place for eagles. The guide will tell visitors interesting facts from the life of these birds of prey and offer to watch them with binoculars.
