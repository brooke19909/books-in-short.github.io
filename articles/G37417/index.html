The best recreation centers in Chemal

Altai Krai is landscapes of incredible beauty, mountain air, forests and gorgeous rivers. In Chemal, the famous Altai resort town, you can relax at any of the many recreation centers or health-improving complexes.

As a resort, Chemal became famous in the 19th century. Many famous figures of culture and art came here. Today we will discuss in detail bases for recreation in Chemal!

Resort Chemal

In the Soviet Union, recreation facilities in Chemal were actively being built, and many of them exist and are still working. Of course, new recreational centers are also available here. And the prices of recreation facilities in Chemal are quite affordable. Therefore, having any budget in hand, you can have a great rest here, enjoying the magnificent mountain nature.





Also one of the main attractions of Chemal is a hydroelectric power station built by prisoners of Siberian camps in the thirties. Now the hydroelectric station does not work, but tours are conducted on it, and many souvenir tents and cafes have been built around it.

The Altai center of Chemal hosts expositions and exhibitions telling about the life, beliefs and history of the settlers of the Altai Territory.

The economy of Chemal is based on tourism. Why? Yes, because this is the main income of the city treasury.
