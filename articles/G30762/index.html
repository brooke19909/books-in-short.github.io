Bright felt alphabet: do-it-yourself pattern

Any parent wants only the best for his child. Therefore, it is not surprising that mothers often sew toys for babies and do all kinds of crafts. You can buy anything in the store, but a hand-made product has some special energy. In fact, any hand-made thing is a luxury!

There are many educational games for children. These include the felt alphabet. The pattern of each letter is quite simple. To make the product look elegant and attractive, it can be decorated at the end of the work.

Bright letters for the baby: the choice of fabrics

For the manufacture of letters, it is better to use materials that will not be strewed. Felt is ideal for such purposes.



You should not use letters to divide letters into vowels and consonants. Indeed, the concepts of soft, voiced, deaf, hissing, and many other definitions also apply to these symbols. Let all the letters be colorful! The more shades, the brighter the felt alphabet will be. Pattern can be of different sizes, but it is desirable that it fit on a sheet of A-4 format.

What will be required for work?

The main material that is needed in the work is felt. It comes in different thicknesses: from 1 to 5 mm. For the flat alphabet, it is desirable to use the densest of the available options, for the volume alphabet - a soft thin felt. Pay attention to the quality of the fabric: acrylic material creaks. There are also options with the addition of wool. If the baby has prerequisites for allergic reactions, it is better not to use felt for the manufacture of the alphabet.



Pattern is the easiest step in tailoring fun multi-colored letters. For work, you will need threads that will fit the tone of the felt as much as possible, unless you want to make a contrasting stroke. Such an author‚Äôs idea also looks original and attractive.

Needles, pins, scissors and a textile marker will be good helpers to the craftswoman in working on patterns of letters of the Russian alphabet. You can take any filler, the synthetic winterizer is optimal. To conveniently fill volume letters, you can use a sushi stick.

Independent work on a pattern of letters

If you could not find a pattern of such letters on the Internet as you would like, you can do it yourself. There is nothing complicated in this; in a text document you need to configure the desired font style and size, and then print it. All! Can sew alphabet from felt. Pattern for future products is ready!



For the volumetric alphabet, it is recommended to choose characters with rounded edges so that there are no thin elements on them, otherwise it will be almost impossible to sew and fill them.

For a flat alphabet, it is undesirable to choose fonts with notches, since they will constantly jam, so it will be inconvenient to work with them.

We make flat letters from felt

This is the easiest option to use. The letters are pinned to the felt, circled and cut. You can make several identical products, they will come in handy when it comes to folding syllables and words, and letters can be repeated in them.





There is another interesting option for making flat letters. For the base, you can take a dense felt, use softer material on the upper part. When stitching patterns, you need to make one blank of the font of the desired size, and the second font is the same, but 2 sizes smaller.





The main letters will be multi-colored, and those that are smaller in the same color. Next, with a silicone gun, lower letters should be glued to large ones. At the edges we leave an equal distance. In such letters as ‚ÄúA‚Äù, ‚ÄúO‚Äù, the inside of the upper and lower letters is compared.



There is another original and fairly simple option. Using patterns of letters of the Russian alphabet, you can very quickly make a whole set with duplicates. Finished figures cut out of felt are glued onto the felt sheet with silicone glue and cut out, at the edges you need to leave 2-3 mm. Contrast colors look good.

Volume letters from felt

To sew the Russian volumetric alphabet, the pattern must be large. About 2 mm will go to the side seams, after stuffing the letter will gain volume and look smaller.



It is necessary to cut two identical details of each letter, for this the pattern is pinned and outlined. It doesn‚Äôt matter which side of the felt is encircled by the pattern; there is no face or wrong side in the fabric. In letters with holes, the first thing you need to do is internal seams, and then go to the edges. Stuffing letters is necessary gradually, filling in the stitched places. After the letter is sewn, you need to fix the knot, and remove the needle on the other side of the product and cut the thread there, then the knot will not be noticeable.

Most often they sew the felt alphabet with animals so that the child develops associative thinking.

The letters from felt are practical, they can be washed in the machine at low temperatures. Products will dry quickly and will not lose their shape. You can also wash it manually in warm water with shampoo.
