Steam generator for clothes: a choice

A functional device for giving clothes a neat and fresh look by steaming, a high-power iron with a constant supply of steam, is called a steam generator. Such a household appliance is much more effective than an ordinary iron, it is used both in a house, a store, and in production.

The operation of the steam generator is based on the effect of steam on the fabric, which impregnates it, at the same time smoothing and cleaning without using cleaning agents. Is applied steam generator for clothes , curtains, bedding from a variety of fabrics. Processing of the product is carried out with dry steam at a temperature of 140 - 160 degrees. Moreover, no special efforts are needed, as well as spending a lot of time.

An important advantage of the device is that it does not leave fingerprints, stains, does not create defects, does not stretch the material, therefore it is suitable for ironing silk, clothes with decorative elements as applications, beads, and drawings.

Types of Steam Generators

  • Horizontal, that is, to smooth the material, the product must be placed on an ironing board or other horizontal surface.
  • Vertical can iron products in any position. The vertical smoothing steam generator is small in size, which allows you to process curtains, disinfect various surfaces in the house: furniture, mattresses, toys, etc., destroying pathogenic bacteria and molds.

Types of steam generators

  • Products with a separate boiler used to generate steam. Preparation for the operation of such a steam generator requires a lot of time, since the required amount of water is poured immediately and it must be completely heated.
  • A steam generator with the ability to instantly generate steam by supplying water to a heated heat element.
  • A device that is equipped with two boilers, the first of which delivers water to the second, where steam is generated. Topping up water is carried out in the process.

If the steam generator is selected for the needs of the enterprise, then a boiler type device is used. For steaming clothes in salons, sewing workshops, it is advantageous to use a vertical steam generator, and at home you can use any type and type of this product.
