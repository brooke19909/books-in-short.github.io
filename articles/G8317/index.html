Voyage Belek Golf & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Belek): room description, service, reviews

The quality of rest in Turkey largely depends on the status of the hotel. This is people going to Arkhipo-Osipovka for the sake of the sea. Or there in Gelendzhik. And to Turkey - for the sake of the sea, the beach, comfortable rooms, delicious food according to the All-Inclusive concept, animation and incendiary entertainment. And almost all of this depends entirely on the hotel. You can live in a modest "troika" and come to the room "just spend the night", satisfying hunger in cafes on the promenade. But if you want to relax without worries, choose a hotel with only five stars. In this article we will characterize one of them - Voyage Belek Golf & Spa 5 *.

We built our description in many respects on the reviews of tourists. In Turkey, many hotels have a distinct focus. There are so-called party hotels where the main emphasis is on animation and youth entertainment. There are hotels for lovers of sports and spa treatments. As you might guess by the name, our hotel just belongs to this category. But she also has one more specificity. This is the maximum of services for children, including for infants.



Where is the hotel located?

Where else could there be a hotel called Voyage Belek if not in Belek? This resort is young and dynamic. It is famous as a youth vacation spot. But for the guests of the Voyage Belek Hotel, all conditions are created for silence, tranquility, and stay in languid bliss. The hotel is not in the town itself, but on its outskirts. More precisely, two kilometers from Belek. This distance is called the best reviews. You can slowly walk barefoot along the edge of the sea or along the promenade. In half an hour you will already be in the historical center of Belek. Voyage Belek is a first-line hotel. It has its own beach, which we will devote more information below. Many tourists want to know how long it will take to get to the place of rest from the airport. It is less than an hour from Antalya, where the air gates of the entire Mediterranean coast of Turkey are located. After all, both cities are separated by only forty kilometers.

What are VIP services?

What are the benefits of occupants of superior rooms over other vacationers at Voyage Belek? The service for VIP guests is thought out to the smallest detail and begins literally from the moment of arrival. Such vacationers are greeted with flowers, they are accommodated in a room without a queue, and the employee of the department of geography is escorting them to the door of the apartment. Upon arrival, a bottle of wine is delivered to the room. And every day they bring guests: fresh fruit, newspapers, beach towels, and from 17 to 18 hours - sweet pastries for tea.

An additional service is supposed - preparation of the room for bed. The minibar in these apartments is replenished better than in ordinary ones. Guests can not only refresh themselves with soft drinks, but also enjoy chocolate milk, try crayfish, whiskey, gin, etc. A special attitude to such guests is manifested even in cosmetic accessories. They are branded - from Bulgari. Upon request, breakfast is delivered directly to your room.



Hotel Voyage Belek: food

Many people know how to feed in Turkey on the program "All inclusive." But the Voyage Belek five-star hotel has the concept of Ultra All Inclusive. And this means that the food there is on the highest level. First of all, reviews report a competent division of the hotel infrastructure into two zones - adult and family. There are two main restaurants in the hotel (buffet service). But in one of them the entrance to persons under 16 years of age is ordered. But in the second there is a whole compartment for younger guests. Children can choose their own meals - just like adults. Five-star hotels must have a la carte restaurants. And in Voyage Belek there are as many as eight of them! Five of them are free for guests. These are restaurants of Mexican ("Grazias"), fish ("Deep"), Turkish ("Kebaba"), Greek ("El Ala") and Thai ("Tai Tea") cuisines. You will have to pay for visiting three more a la carte restaurants. In them you can taste dishes of Japanese ("Yaki-Yaki") and Italian ("Manyamo") cuisines, as well as enjoy well-fried or with blood (if you wish) steak in Steak House.
