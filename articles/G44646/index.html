Do-it-yourself diy satin ribbon crafts

Creativity helps a person to find peace of mind, to reveal his hidden possibilities and inspires new achievements. DIY items can be a great gift for friends or an original piece of home decor. Crafts from satin ribbon are easy to manufacture and do not require significant financial costs. Let's see how to make a beautiful rose in just a few minutes?





Material for work

For crafts from satin ribbons you will need scissors, a ruler, a lighter, a needle, threads, a glue gun. Choose the color of the tape based on your personal preferences. Artificial roses, unlike real plants, can be not only red, white or pink, but also blue, violet and even gold. The width of the ribbon can also be varied in accordance with the desired flower size, say 5 centimeters. Miniature roses can decorate children's clothing. Large flowers are ideal for decorating an apartment. You will also need a green ribbon for leaflets.

Manufacturing instruction

1. To make crafts from a satin ribbon, you need to cut 5 segments 8 centimeters long and 5 segments 13 centimeters long. Also, two green parts of 15 centimeters should be prepared in advance.

2. Gently burn the edges of all segments with a lighter. The material is easy to melt. Do not bring the fire close to the braid, otherwise you risk spoiling the future masterpiece.





3. Now each petal needs to be made separately. To do this, fold the prepared segments with a corner to the middle, secure with a pin, and then sew a line along the lower edge. After that, pull the thread tight, fixing it with a bundle. Repeat this procedure with all segments. Now all the details for crafts from satin ribbons (you can see their photos here) are ready.

4. Next, you can proceed with the assembly. All petals need to be put together in a bud, starting with small ones. Use a glue gun for assembly.

5. At the end of the work, glue a piece of tape around in a circle to hide all sections. Your pretty rose is ready.

Create and create

So, as you can see, creating original crafts from a satin ribbon is not difficult at all. A little patience is enough to learn how to perform vivid romantic compositions from flowing satin braid. The variety of colors of the material will inspire you to create original models that will be a great gift for any occasion. For example, try making crafts from kanzashi satin ribbons. To do this, you can attach a hairpin to the finished flower, which is convenient to wear every day, combining your hairstyle. Make several kanzashi patterns with roses of various colors and wear a hair clip that matches with your clothes. Use flowers to decorate a dress or business suit. A well-chosen part will become a highlight for creating a new image. Create, experiment, and then every day will be a small holiday for you.
