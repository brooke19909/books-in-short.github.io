Location, how to get there

Sunrise All Suites Resort is 3.4 km from Obzor, north of it. Nearby there are almost no other objects, only a couple of other hotels, but the excellent asphalt road, near which the hotel in question is located, can lead to Obzor, to Varna, to Burgas and to any other corner of Bulgaria. The hotel is surrounded by a small grove and meadow, through which you need to walk less than 5 hundred meters to get to the Black Sea coast. Thus, the Sunrise All Suites Resort will be most comfortable for those who are looking for a relaxing holiday away from fun and enchantingly vibrant, but such noisy entertainment facilities.

Getting to the hotel is easy. The overview is approximately halfway between the two international airports in Bulgaria. To one, called "Varna" and located in the city of the same name, 65 km, and to the second, called "Burgas" and, accordingly, located in the same city, a little more than 70 km. From both airports, the hotel transfers tourists to the hotel.

Rooms

Sunrise All Suites Resort has 179 apartments in its fund. Each of them can be called a separate apartment. Each is equipped with one or two balconies. The views from the windows, depending on the location of the building, can be at sea, on the territory of the hotel or on the surroundings. "Apartments" are offered with one, two or three bedrooms. Each room has a living room, kitchen, hallway and hygiene room. The room decor is rich, stylish and beautiful. From furniture there are bedroom sets, soft sofas, armchairs, coffee tables, decor elements: paintings, vases, figurines. Each room has air conditioning, telephone, split system, modern TV, safe. The kitchens are equipped with modern appliances: stove, washing machine, electric kettle, furnished with kitchen sets. But there are no dishes except two cups. If guests have a need to cook something for themselves, plates, pots and pans can be taken at the reception. Tea and coffee sets, sugar, cream, 2 bottles of beer and water, juice are provided in each room.

The hygiene rooms have toilet bowls, large recumbent bathtubs with showers, self-contained boilers, washbasins. All standard hygiene products are provided on the day of arrival of tourists and replenished regularly.

Hotel for toddlers

In Bulgaria, it was always good to heal children. The Sunrise Hotel continues this wonderful tradition. There are three separate, quite spacious children's pools up to 60 cm deep. Two of them are equipped with fungi with trickling water, and small water slides are installed. Between the buildings there is an outdoor playground and a trampoline, and in the central building there is a mini club. With kids, they spend all day animating, there are water rides. In the rooms you can order the provision of a cot, and for older children, folding beds. For a child under 12 years of age, payment is not made, and for a child from 13 years of age and for adults when placed on folding beds, the payment is 40 euros per day.

Leisure at the hotel

The nearest place with entertainment facilities is Obzor. Some guests go there on foot, which takes about 40 minutes. You can also drive into the city by bus. But the bulk of the guests, especially those who came with small children, spend their leisure time at the hotel. There are three large pools with free sun loungers and umbrellas. Sports enthusiasts can entertain themselves in the billiard room, on the darts board or at the tennis tables. For wellness, the hotel built a wonderful wellness center with a fitness room, sauna, bathhouse, massage room, and a beauty salon.



Sea

Obzor has long been known as a wonderful Bulgarian resort on the Black Sea coast. Hotel Sunrise is located at a distance of 300-500 meters from the sea, depending on the building number. The fact that the coastline is not nearby can be considered a plus, since near the sea from the afternoon there is strong wind almost every day, and the weather is always acceptable on the territory of the hotel. You need to go to the beach through the meadow, along a rocky path. The beach at the Sunrise Hotel is private, sandy, very wide and always clean. The only drawback is cigarette butts that irresponsible smokers (smoking is allowed throughout the hotel) are buried in the sand instead of being thrown into bins all over the place.
