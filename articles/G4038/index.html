The best music smartphone: review, types, models, specifications and owner reviews

A musical smartphone is a multimedia gadget whose main purpose is to provide its owner with high-quality sound. Let's try to figure out which device best copes with the task, taking into account the opinions of experts and reviews of ordinary owners.





Quite a lot of people mistakenly believe that the most musical smartphone should be estimated by the volume of the built-in speakers, but this is a big mistake, because the maximum decibel is not the main selection factor. Of primary importance is not the volume, but the sound quality - accuracy, frequency and saturation. Based on these indicators, the rating indicated below was compiled.

In fact, the entire list is represented by brands that managed to succeed in this difficult and very capricious multimedia field. Therefore, the most distinguished model can be distinguished from the general list from each company / corporation.
