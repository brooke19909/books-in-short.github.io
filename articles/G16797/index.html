How to make a shark out of paper - detailed description

Today we will tell you how to make a shark out of paper. The scheme of this animal belongs to the average level of complexity. This topic will be especially interesting to people who love the sea and unusual types of origami. Of the many predators that live on the planet, a shark is especially frightening to humans. Let's start by preparing a sheet of paper.

Training



We turn to the practical part of solving the question of how to make a shark out of paper. A square sheet of paper with a side of 15 cm is best for us. If you are new to origami, you can use a larger blank. In the latter case, it will be much easier to decide how to make a shark out of paper. The fact is that the fish as a result will be large, but it will be easier to fold it. To make the figure as realistic as possible, you should carefully select the color of the paper. Do not be afraid to experiment by creating your marine resident.

Instruction manual



As soon as the desired option of gray color is selected, we proceed to the next stage of solving the question of how to make a shark out of paper. First we add the fish, giving it a basic shape. Next, bend the workpiece in half. Turn it over. In the next step, we bend the corner of origami. We do it in a special way. The angle should go slightly beyond the bounds of the figure. At the next stage, we bend it along the planned lines. Next, go to the left side. There we bend the angle-tail. We carry out a similar operation with the right side. Next, bend the corner in place of future fins. Flatten the place where the pocket is located. In this case, the parts obtained must be unequal.

Finishing touches

At this stage of the work, the question of how to make a shark out of paper will be completely resolved. Next, take the scissors. At the corner we make an incision. We bend the bottom of the pocket from ourselves. It is important to remember one subtlety. In the lower part, you need to bend a little lower, compared with the top. At the next stage, we open the figure. We pass to the upper corner. We reveal it. At the next stage, go to the left side of the corner. We make an incision. Pull the corner. Leave one paper layer. Close the figure. We remove the corners on the head of our shark. Go to the next step. Now we are interested in the corners under the tail. At the same time we bend them with all layers of paper. After that, open the tail. It remains to deal with the fins. We install them on the side of our fish, while maintaining a right angle.
