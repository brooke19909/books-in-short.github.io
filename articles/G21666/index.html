Smart TV set-top box: how to connect and how to use

The set-top box Smart TV is very popular. She is able to turn a regular TV into a gaming platform or Internet browser. Thanks to her, you can freely watch videos, photos, and also communicate on social networks. But there is one important question about Smart TV: "How to connect to a TV?"

Many people are interested in the question of the advantages of this device, since its cost is far from small.





The prefix to the Smart TV has a great future. Therefore, you need to get acquainted with this equipment.

general information

TV through Smart TV is far from perfect. Every year it will be modernized and made better. However, the advantages cover all the disadvantages. Most people would like to see the Smart TV set-top box running on the Android OS. Connecting and setting up this device are two different things. In order for it to function better, it is necessary to configure all possible features and programs. Most of these devices run on the Android operating system.

What is an Android console ?

Typically, this is a small device that connects to the TV through a special connector. The power supply is a regular 220 V power supply network. If the set-top box is connected to the Smart TV, it is connected via a USB connector, and it receives power directly from the TV. The design of the device provides Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. This device can be compared with a modern tablet or phone.

This is the most common model to date, as it advantageously combines cost and functional performance. This Smart TV model, the price of which is 4,500 rubles, is freely connected to any TVs. Device connection

First you need to connect the TV via Smart TV. Next, the device is connected to the mains. If there is no USB connector for the power supply, you must use the special adapter that comes with the kit.



This connector is used to connect the mouse to perform settings, as well as to connect drives to save the necessary information or watch videos.

How to set up a Smart TV?

After the set-top box is connected to the TV, it is necessary to select the appropriate operating mode. You should not immediately connect to the Internet, since you will not see the desired result. First, go to the HDMI section located in the menu. This procedure will make it possible to see the image. After that, the active port is selected. This procedure occurs if there are many ports. If one, then everything happens by default. Next, the main menu appears on the screen, where other functions are configured.

How to use the prefix?

You bought Smart TV. How to use? If connected correctly, 6 bookmarks will appear on the main screen. First you need to turn on the wireless network. This can be done by going to the "Settings". It uses the same settings as on modern phones and tablets. Therefore, to accomplish this is not difficult. All main parameters have their own name. It is worth noting that working with a wireless network does not cause problems.

In this situation, the only drawback is the operating system itself (Android).





If you have made the wrong date and time settings, a notification will constantly pop up on the screen. Therefore, this issue must be resolved immediately.

Auto tuning

It is worth noting that the set date and time settings may be reset. Therefore, it is best to use automatic ones. To do this, specify the screen extension and set the refresh rate. Other settings will be the same as on Android devices.

Main sections

Among the main functions that are in the console, the following can be distinguished:

  1. Media This section contains programs that allow you to watch videos, listen to music and watch photos while connecting the drive. A special player is already installed in the section - MX Player. It is the most common and suitable for this console. After turning off the drive, all files can be played. If the built-in player does not fit, you can install another.
  2. TV. This section contains programs with which you can view video files online. One of these utilities is YouTube.
  3. Browsers There are programs for accessing the Internet, thanks to which you can freely access social networks, download videos, music, photos and so on. What to do if the standard browser is not satisfied? To do this, go to the Apps section and install a new one. If necessary, it can be downloaded from the Internet.
  4. Games. As a rule, in this section there are some of the most popular games. To expand this list, you need to download games through certain applications.
  5. Apps In addition to many browsers, in this section there are other programs that are aimed at simplifying the work. For example, cards, mail, etc. Also on the menu is "Play Market", where you can download a lot of useful applications and games. The notification center is located in the lower right corner.
  6. Watching TV. If all the programs and sections are tired, then you can just watch your favorite TV shows on TV. A lot of channels will be presented to your attention, on which you can watch everything from films to various sporting events.

On the main screen, the time and date, the status of the wireless network, the status of the connection of drives are displayed. On the right side of the screen there is a menu where you can get acquainted with other functions of the operating system. For example, adjust the volume or turn off unnecessary devices. After pressing the shutdown button, the prefix does not turn off completely, but goes into standby mode. You can activate it with the mouse movement.

The disadvantages of the prefix "Smart TV"

In addition to the mass of positive qualities, there are negative points:

  • When viewing video in MKV or AVI format, there are problems with the image, as well as sound, it is written on the screen that the format was not found, although the prefix should play all formats.
  • Games of rather poor quality, they are significantly different from those that are available for computers or consoles such as Sony Playstation.
  • You can watch some films and videos only upon request, that is, to watch them you will need to pay a certain amount.
  • The problem with the browser, since the standard browser is not suitable for social networks, it is inconvenient to watch news and photos, as well as chat with friends. We can say that the control function is not finalized. In addition, movies and videos cannot be played. The main reason is the insufficient amount of internal memory on the Smart TV set-top box.

Another plus is the use of a drive. It is difficult to find a TV that meets these requirements. All these positive qualities overshadow any flaws. The prefix Smart TV, reviews of which are positive, is an indispensable device for your TV.

Smart TV, the price of which ranges from four to five thousand rubles, has many possibilities. Thanks to this set-top box, you can watch movies and videos online, listen and download music, and view photos.



Thanks to the rich menu, this device can be used both for work and entertainment purposes. Using the Internet you can download interesting games and applications. But first of all, you need to find out important questions about Smart TV: how to use, configure and connect. A significant advantage is the work based on the Android operating system.
