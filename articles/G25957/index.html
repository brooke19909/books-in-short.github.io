How to make a screenshot on Nokia?

How can I take a screenshot of the display on Nokia to capture the information that you want, for example, to use in the future? A similar question interests many people who have purchased devices from a Finnish manufacturer. Many of them ask how to take a screenshot of the display on Nokia. At the same time, users do not even realize that the combination for capturing the screen from the buttons is quite simple, and it could be detected by an ordinary experimental method. So how to make a screenshot of the display on Nokia?





Fundamentals and models of phones

A similar function (we are talking about creating a screenshot) is laid in the software foundations of many models of mobile devices released by the Finnish manufacturer. It is logical that this does not require the installation of additional utilities. Although they still exist. Nevertheless, the Finns adopted a similar trend from the South Korean company Samsung, which began to integrate the function of creating a screenshot into the shell of the operating system. The answer to the question of how to take a screenshot of the display on Nokia is quite simple: you need to press a certain key combination.

What models does this rule apply to?

A lot of users ask how to take a screenshot on Nokia Lumia. However, they all have different models of devices. It can be a series of 500, 600, 900 and so on. But this is not important, since the basis of the work of all these devices is one operating system. Therefore, the principle of creating screenshots will be the same for us.





So, let's move on to the question of how to take a screenshot on a Nokia phone.

Method One: Use the Logo

If you carefully look down the screen of your device, you will notice the logo of Windows. To take a screenshot and capture the information on it, just hold the logo for a few seconds. At the same time, we use the power control key. If you do this at the same time and hold down the buttons in the active position for a few seconds, you will notice a small flash that will notify you that the screenshot was successfully taken and placed in a folder. On this, in fact, it all ends.

How to take a screenshot on the Nokia X

Although the device in this series is slightly different from its counterparts, if you can call them that, taking a screen shot on it is no more difficult. The actions underlying the creation of screenshots on this device are extremely simple. Again, the user is only required to briefly hold down several buttons simultaneously.





After the picture is taken, the operating system will save it to the gallery built into the software. From there, you can already perform various manipulations with the file: copy, delete, move, change using the editor or share it using a multimedia message or on social networks. In general, the choice of action remains after the screenshot is taken by the user himself.

Sequencing

Open the application or program at the moment that you would like to take a picture. Then press several buttons at once. These are the power and volume down keys. Once again we draw your attention to the fact that this must be done simultaneously. If you squeeze the buttons individually or sequentially, creating a screenshot may cause problems. Rather, the system simply will not properly respond to such a combination and therefore will not take the screenshot itself.





If it is created, you will be immediately notified by the operating system that the manipulation was successfully completed. The corresponding context will appear at the top of the screen. To view what you photographed, you can move to the device‚Äôs gallery. Find a folder called Screenshots there. All screenshots are saved after creation there.

Conclusion

As you can see, there is nothing so complicated about creating screen captures. What did we find out during this article? The Lumiy software and individual phones of the Finnish manufacturer allows you to take a screenshot without using third-party programs. This can be done by using a combination of keys and the Windows logo.
