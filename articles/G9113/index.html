Turkey. Green Hill Hotel is the best choice for outdoor activities!

Green Hill Hotel (Turkey) is an ideal place for those who prefer an active beach holiday to any other. The complex is 115 km away from Antalya International Airport , 10 km from the center of Alanya and 2 km from the village of Konakli. Its area is 150,000 square meters, where, in addition to the resort complex, there is also a mini-zoo for children. You can go down to the beach by funicular or through the many steps of the stairs.

"Green Hill Hotel." Turkey. Photo of accommodation and room description



The hotel offers accommodation in one of 330 comfortable rooms, 166 of which are standard, 165 are suites. Most of the rooms have panoramic views of the sea and nature. Each of the rooms is equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone, balcony, bathroom with shower, safe (for an additional fee), minibar, hairdryer. Rooms are cleaned daily. Room service is available. Accepted credit cards: Visa, Mastercard.

Turkey. Green Hill Hotel. Nutrition



The hotel has all-inclusive indoor and outdoor restaurants. Opening hours: from 7:00 to 00:00. It serves a variety of dishes of national Turkish and European cuisine, as well as alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Snack bars are located by the outdoor pool and on the beach. Here you can order a hamburger, spaghetti, soup, salad, pizza, hot sandwiches, alcoholic or non-alcoholic local drinks. The lobby bar also offers light snacks and refreshing cocktails. The most delicious Turkish coffee, freshly squeezed juice or original local drinks offers an aquapool and relaxation pool. The terrace bar, open 24 hours a day, will easily satisfy the most varied tastes of holidaymakers.

Turkey. Green Hill Hotel. Pools and beach



The hotel has several pools: landscaped outdoor (umbrellas, sunbeds, mattresses, etc.), a pool with water slides, a children's swimming pool. The mini-water park located in the outdoor pool will not leave indifferent either children or adults. Rest on the water always brings a lot of pleasure and gives a huge energy boost. The hotel has its own sand and pebble beach. Sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas are available to guests free of charge, beach towels - at an additional cost.

Turkey. Green Hill Hotel. Services

Vacationers are free to use the sauna, Turkish bath, gym, tennis court. You can have fun at a disco, water aerobics classes, playing volleyball on the beach, darts. In addition, the hotel has a team of animators who will not let anyone get bored!

Turkey. Green Hill Hotel. Children's leisure

The nanny can sit with the smallest. Older children can have fun on the playground, in the mini club, frolic in the children's pool and usefully spend time in the company of children's animators. Absolutely everyone will enjoy visiting the mini zoo located on the hotel premises. Especially for the little ones, a children's menu has been developed, which they will be happy to offer in the restaurant. Have a pleasant and memorable vacation!
