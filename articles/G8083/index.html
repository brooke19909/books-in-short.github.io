Hotel Notion Kesre Beach Hotel & Spa Hotel (Turkey / Ozdere) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

Notion Kesre Beach & Spa Hotel is a great place for an exotic vacation, recommended by many tour operators. It is here, according to them, that you can relax relatively inexpensively and in excellent conditions. The hotel administration is always doing everything possible to ensure that each guest feels as comfortable as possible here.



general information

Notion Kesre Beach & Spa Hotel 4 * was built relatively recently - in 2015. That is why its territory is very modernly designed, which also applies to buildings. The hotel also has modern equipment.

Its total area is 11,000 square meters. m, on which are located two modern and beautifully decorated buildings, as well as several places for recreation and entertainment. As guests of Notion Kesre Beach Resort note in their reviews of the hotel, it is perfect for guests who wish to relax with all their family members.



Location

The hotel is located in one of the largest resort regions of Turkey - Kusadasi, in its village called Ozdere. Here, according to vacationers, it‚Äôs quite quiet, you can sit with your children with great comfort. The center of Ozdere is 4 kilometers from the hotel, and in order to get to the central part of Kusadasi, you need to overcome 30 km. The nearest airport is also quite far away - 40 km, in the town of Izmir.

The hotel is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, where during the holiday season a rather hot temperature reigns, significantly mitigated by the influence of air masses coming from the seashore.



Rooms

The hotel‚Äôs apartments, of which there are only 164 in its buildings, are divided into three categories, depending on the conditions provided for living: standard, family and suite.

Each room, regardless of its level of comfort, has a private bathroom, which is equipped with everything that is necessary to maintain hygiene. Here, each guest is given a standard set of bath accessories, a personal bathrobe, a large towel, as well as a pair of slippers.

The apartments also have air conditioning equipment, which allows guests to feel as comfortable as possible under any temperature conditions.



Standards

Speaking of standard apartments in their reviews of Notion Kesre Beach & Spa Hotel, tourists often share their impressions of the interior that is available here. According to them, the interior of the rooms is very airy, allowing you to completely relax and enjoy a pleasant, very comfortable atmosphere. All the furniture installed here is quite modern and comfortable.

The main place here is occupied by a huge bed, designed for two guests, made of light wood. It has a large orthopedic mattress, as well as expensive bedding, which has hypoallergenic properties. Tourists relaxing in this type of room can put their personal belongings in a large closet, which is installed here, as well as use a dressing table and a mirror. The room has a luxurious sitting area, located near a large window - it is represented by a pair of white armchairs and a small table, at which it is very convenient to drink coffee and enjoy the beautiful sea expanses. At the same time, guests can watch satellite TV. According to hotel guests, many of them are Russian-speaking.



Family apartments

Family rooms, according to vacationers, are perfect for relaxing an impressive number of guests (up to 4 people), among whom there are small children or teenagers. Here, the room is divided into two small rooms, which are sleeping areas. Each of them has two berths, as well as one closet designed for storing clothes. This type of apartment has one landline telephone and a plasma TV. Guests can rent an electronic safe and a minibar during their stay at the hotel - they are paid by the day.

Suites

In Notion Kesre Beach & Spa Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi) there are several rooms that are classified as suites. Each of them is divided into two separate large bedrooms. The comments left by tourists about this place to stay often say that these rooms are very light, which is achieved by the presence of white and blue details in their interior, as well as a large number of lighting fixtures. In addition, the suites have huge windows all over the wall, from which you can see the surrounding expanses.

Each room has a huge double bed with two side tables. They have personal phones with the ability to connect to an international communication line. The apartments are equipped with relaxation areas in the form of a pair of large soft chairs and a tea table. There is also one tea substation in two rooms, with which you can prepare your favorite hot drinks, the ingredients for which are also provided.

If necessary, guests can use the paid options for installing a safe and a minibar in the room.



Nutrition

A large number of reviews of Notion Kesre Beach & Spa Hotel 4 * say that the hotel provides guests with an all-inclusive concept, excellent three meals a day. To take advantage of this opportunity, you can visit the main restaurant, which serves a buffet. According to tourists, it offers original and delicious dishes that are prepared in Turkish and European style.

In addition to the restaurant, you can visit three bars and one club, the entrance to which is paid. They can offer a huge selection of different drinks, including tea, coffee, cocktails, alcohol, soft drinks, juices, fruit drinks, etc. In addition, guests recommend staying here to taste local desserts. The menu has a large assortment of snacks, among which, according to vacationers, those made from fish are especially tasty.

Aquazone

Notion Kesre Beach & Spa Hotel has a large aquazone, which every day attracts the attention of a huge number of guests who are fans of water treatments.

Its territory consists of a large sun terrace, where there are places for guests to relax. According to tourists, the sun loungers installed here are quite comfortable, and the umbrellas located above them perfectly protect from the harmful effects of sunlight. They often recommend, while staying here, to read their favorite book or just relax with a glass of cooling drink in their hands.

In the center of the terrace there is one large outdoor pool with crystal clear water, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is 475 square meters. m. Next to it is a small niche that is designed for bathing children. There are two slides in the large pool where guests can have fun and very actively spend their time.

Spa

A huge number of positive comments left by travelers on social networks are addressed to the spa area located in the main building. Many tourists visit it every day, as they notice the beneficial effect of the procedures.

The spa area is divided into two large parts: the thermal, which consists of a hammam and a sauna, as well as massage, where you can perform a number of cosmetic procedures and immerse yourself in a state of complete relaxation under the influence of skillful manipulations by masters of their craft. The services of massage therapists and cosmetologists working here are paid, and you can visit the hammam for free.

Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle, while staying at Notion Kesre Beach & Spa Hotel, are often surprised by the presence of excellent conditions for keeping their body in shape. They note in their comments that, if desired, it seems possible to visit the large gym, which can be done completely free of charge at any time.

On the open square there are several sports areas equipped with everything necessary for playing futsal or basketball. On the beach there is a place for playing volleyball. A tennis court has been built near the aquazone.

If desired, guests can engage in more relaxed games - in billiards or table tennis.

Entertainment

Guests of Notion Kesre Beach Resort (Turkey / Izmir / Ozdere) receive a considerable amount of impressions by participating in evening events that are held at the hotel every day. Often during their conduct, guests are entertained by a team of well-trained animators who can impress both children and adults. Guests very often take a positive view of the fact that almost every member of this team speaks excellent Russian, which greatly facilitates communication with them for those tourists who are residents of the CIS countries.

The hotel hosts a considerable number of children's performances and show programs, however, all of them take place mainly in the daytime.



Beach

The coastline is a short distance from the Notion Kesre Beach & Spa Hotel 4 * (Kusadasi). This resting place has its own plot, which is closed to visitors by non-residents.

According to the guests, this beach area is quite well equipped for recreation of representatives of different age categories. Often, parents who are here on vacation with their children note the convenience and safety of the beach for kids - this is due to the gradual descent into the sea and a rather soft sandy coating of the coastline.

Large sun loungers, as well as umbrellas, are placed here throughout the area. In addition, the beach has a bar where vacationers can enjoy delicious cocktails. For guests' convenience, there are several locker rooms, public toilets and showers. If desired, tourists can take a towel or mattress for temporary use at a special point absolutely free of charge.

Along the coastline, you can find a considerable amount of entertainment that tourists will certainly enjoy.
