Waterpark "Limkor", Saratov: description, prices, reviews

When the sultry summer comes, many residents of Saratov rush to the beaches on the banks of the Volga. And where can I splash around at another time of the year? For these purposes, there is a water park "Limkor". Saratov has two similar complexes. There is also the Volzhsky Dali water park in Pristannoye, which is also a good vacation spot. But "Limkor" is recognized by users as the best in the city. Why? You will know this if you read our article. We will tell you about the leisure infrastructure in this water park, about its slides, prices and other services. Our story will be confirmed by photographs of this complex. Guest reviews have become our main informative base.



Where is it located and how to get there

Saying "Limkor complex", we did not say a word. The water park is indeed part of a large structure. It has a synthetic ice rink, gyms and other gyms, and even a hotel for nonresidents. There is a water park and the whole complex around the clock. And it is very convenient for those who want to relax or get an adrenaline zone after work. Slides

The first thing that interests visitors to all water parks is the rides. How many slides in the Saratov "Limkor"? Based on reviews and photos, there are only two for adults and one for children. The first is called "Kamikaze." A straight and steep descent from this yellow slide causes an adrenaline rush. Children are allowed on it, but only accompanied by adults. The second slide is called the Black Hole. It is a dashingly twisted blue pipe through which a skater in complete darkness slides into a 1.8 meter deep pool. Water park "Limkor" (Saratov) is two-level. On the second floor there is a zone for children. It consists of a pool 60 centimeters deep and a small slide, on which even the most timid kids will not be afraid to go down. Recall that the water in the pools is very warm. The temperature is maintained there all year round + 26-29 degrees. Water park indoor. Therefore, the air temperature is also approaching + 30.



Security

All the water in the Limkor water park (Saratov) passes through a chlorinated purification system, which is helped by bactericidal lamps. Before going to the pools and slides, the visitor must wash in the shower. The water park also has comfortable locker rooms designed for men and women. Reviews claim that they are clean and warm. But, reviews say, the floors near the pools are convex, imitated under pebbles. This complements the feeling of relaxation on the seashore.

Water park "Limkor" (Saratov): schedule, prices

The sports complex works seven days a week. Nevertheless, the water park is closed for cleaning pools and grounds, as well as for the prevention of night rides. The last visitors are asked to leave at ten in the evening. And already from ten in the morning the water park opens its doors again. What is the cost of admission to the water park "Limkor" (Saratov). Visitors call the prices too high. A regular adult ticket on weekdays costs (for August 2017) 1,200 rubles, and on weekends - 1,300. Children from four to six years of age pay 600 and 650 rubles each. respectively. But, knowledgeable people say, you can buy a ticket to Limkor at half price. If you have a birthday, grab a document proving this. From time to time, coupons can be purchased at the water park website. With them, the ticket also costs half the price. But what visitors did not like at the water park was their attitude towards people with disabilities. On the one hand, there is a discount for them.
