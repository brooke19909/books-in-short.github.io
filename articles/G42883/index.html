Vase from cardboard (do it yourself). Manufacturing process

A vase is an interior item that allows you to place accents and create an atmosphere of comfort. Of course, the range of such products is quite large. If desired, you can purchase a vessel of any size and shape. However, refined and elegant products are not cheap. How to decorate a room without spending a lot of money? A vase from cardboard will help out. With your own hands, such a product can be created by everyone. What is needed for this?



What is needed for a simple vase?

In order for a vase made of cardboard, created with your own hands, to be neat, you should prepare all the materials and tools in advance. For work you will need:

  1. Cardboard box. It should be rectangular in shape and small in size.
  2. Glue.
  3. Two sheets of corrugated cardboard.
  4. Putty.
  5. Paints.

The whole manufacturing process takes a little time. As a result of the work, a neat and beautiful product is obtained.

Manufacturing process

So, how is a vase made of cardboard with your own hands? The master class is useful to those who want to save money and get an original product. First of all, it is necessary to form a figure with six faces from a cardboard box of small sizes. This will be the base of the vase.

After that, you can start decorating it. Triangles should be cut from corrugated cardboard. They will be slightly smaller than the width of one of the sides of the workpiece. It is not necessary to make them isosceles and the same. When the elements are ready, it is worth putting glue on the verge. Each side of the workpiece should be glued with triangles of corrugated cardboard, leaving small gaps between them: a width of not more than 0.5 centimeters.



Final stage

Do-it-yourself vase from cardboard can be made in almost any shape. The main thing is to follow the sequence. When the workpiece is completely glued with triangles, fill the gaps between them with putty. When the material dries, you need to paint the vase. In this case, contrasting colors should be used. The gaps between the triangles should be covered with a darker composition than the geometric figures themselves. That's all. The vase is ready. If desired, the product can be varnished.

DIY cardboard vase

If necessary, a floor vase can be made of cardboard that fits perfectly into the interior of any room. To create such an accessory, you will need:

  1. A box of cardboard in large sizes.
  2. Pencil.
  3. Scotch.
  4. Paper.
  5. PVA glue.
  6. Paper clips.
  7. Glue for ceiling tiles.
  8. Pistachio shell.

Where to start?

So, how to make a vase of cardboard with your own hands from the listed materials? First of all, it is necessary to create a pattern for the future product. Since the vase will be outdoor, its height should be at least 60 centimeters. An accessory requires 4 faces. Two of them should be rectangular, and two should be curly. However, the width of the central part of the workpieces should be the same. The resulting patterns should be connected to each other in the middle using tape. After that, it is necessary to cut the bottom of the appropriate shape and size.

Now you can use tape to fix the edges by connecting them in the ribs. To make the parts more securely fixed, you can use strips of paper smeared with PVA glue. They are also worth attaching to the neck of the product. This can be done with staples.





In conclusion

The product is ready. It remains only to decorate it. Now you know how to make floor vases from cardboard. With your own hands you can create a real masterpiece. In this case, to decorate the accessory, you can use the shell from pistachios. Such an ornament will look extraordinary and very original. It is enough to put a little glue on the shell for the ceiling tile, and then fix it on the surface of the face. From this material, you can create an ornament. If desired, the shell can be glued randomly.

Use other materials to decorate the vase. You can decorate the product with colorful yarn, small shells, buttons and even beads. If desired, the vase is made plain, covered with a special coloring composition. The main thing is that the product fits perfectly into the interior of the room and emphasizes its features.
