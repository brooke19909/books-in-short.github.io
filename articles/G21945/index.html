Hotels in Tushino: description, reviews and photos

Tushino district is located in the north-west of Moscow. Today, this territory consists of three small areas: Northern and Southern Tushino, as well as Pokrovskoye-Streshnevo. In the last district is the Tushinskaya metro station, which is known to everyone and is located in the historical center of the city.

In this metropolitan area there are many attractions, temples, memorials and entertainment centers. For example, the Church of the Intercession of the Blessed Virgin Mary on Volokolamsk Highway or the church of the Holy Apostle and Evangelist John the Theologian. Tushino area hotels

Visitors to the city and its residents who want to find an overnight stay near the Tushinskaya metro station may encounter some difficulties. For example, lack of seats or poor location relative to the metro. Hotels in Tushino must be booked in advance, otherwise you may simply not find a place. If you need an inexpensive overnight stay in a hostel, then it makes sense to get ready for the features of such a vacation. First of all, it is worthwhile to understand that the bathroom and toilet will be shared - one toilet on the floor or for several rooms. Cheap hotels in Tushino offer beds in shared rooms, which means that you will have neighbors, and they can be people of different nationalities.

In order to stay overnight in the historic district of the capital, you need to prepare in advance. Inexpensive hotels in Tushino can also be found, however, you need to book a place in advance and study customer reviews.

Hostel "Start"

Hostel "Start" is located at the following address: 13A / 1 Vishnevaya Street. Spartak Stadium can be reached in 10-15 minutes. Nearby is the Dynamo Stadium, as well as the Botanical Garden and the Academy of Sciences. The hostel offers free Wi-Fi, as well as a bathtub, washing machine, ironing facilities and a kitchen. The cost of living here is from 400 rubles per day.

The interior of the hostel is simple but comfortable. Simple beds harmonize with light walls. The bathroom with shower is shared and is located on the floor. In the building where the mini-hotel is located, there is a spa center, which can be visited by guests of the hostel (for a fee).

Visitors are positive about this hotel. Good location (near the metro, shops and major attractions), friendly staff and comfortable conditions - that is what attracts many tourists. Hotels in Tushino (Moscow) should be similar to this hostel - guests say so.

Boutique Hotel "Sretensky Dvor"

The hotel is located at 26/1 Sretenka Street. This is a more expensive option than a hostel or guest house. The cost of living here starts at 3,500 rubles. There are rooms of different comforts.

The interior of the boutique hotel is very interesting, the walls are decorated with unusual paintings: a chest of drawers and a hat, a painted cat with a ball of thread jumping from a chair, a floor lamp and a table are also "illusory".

Each apartment has everything you need for a comfortable stay: a private bathroom with cosmetics and hygiene products, as well as towels, slippers and bathrobes. The room has a spacious bed with a comfortable mattress, a closet for clothes and bedside tables for small items - this is all that can be found in a standard room.

In the morning you can order breakfast at the hotel. There are many cafes and restaurants nearby, so there is a choice of where to eat. Friendly staff will tell and show everything that is in the hotel.

Guests speak well of this place. Many people notice that the rooms are always clean and comfortable. The apartments are spacious, bathrooms too. Some minor flaws (lack of bottled water in the rooms and difficulties in finding a hotel) are brightened up by friendly and caring staff. Guests recommend this place to stay.

Hostel "Nice"

Everyone can stay in this mini-hotel on Krasnoprudnaya street, 24, building 1. The cost of living is 580 rubles per day.

The interior of the hostel is designed in a youth style. Brick walls are in perfect harmony with wooden racks and shelves. Minimalism and innovation - these are the main directions in the interior.

All apartments have beds, bedside tables and shelves, lamps. The bathroom on the floor and the kitchen for cooking are shared. There are no air conditioners in the rooms, but they are in all other rooms.

A small gym and a common area for watching TV are made for the convenience and comfort of guests. The kitchen has several refrigerators and everything you need for cooking.

Guests speak well of the Mini Inn. In their comments, they say that it is a clean and comfortable place. There are rooms for 4 people with ordinary beds, and there are double rooms with screens. On the floor are common amenities (4 showers and several toilets with mirrors, guests do not have to queue in the morning. Loft-style repairs are very organic and stylish. It can be seen that there are a lot of guests in the hostel, as modern furniture has already been spoiled a bit.

Hotel "Taiga"

At the address Volokolamsk highway, house 89, building 1, the Tayezhny hotel is located. The cost of hotel accommodation is from 3000 rubles per day. There are rooms of different comforts.

The interior of the hotel and apartments is made in oriental style. However, he is unobtrusive, no frills. The rooms are made in different colors, so all guests can choose something to their liking. Comfortable beds, air conditioning, TV and a private bathroom - this is the list that is in all apartments. Most rooms do not have windows, so those for whom this is important should talk with the administrator.

A big plus in the hotel is the presence of a spa complex. Baths, saunas and massage services are available for guests. On the ground floor there is a cafe where you can have breakfast or lunch. For dinner, local dishes are also available.

In their reviews, the guests kindly mention this place of rest: it is close to the metro station and main attractions (Spartak Stadium, Academy of Sciences). Unfortunately, in the near access there are no shops and restaurants. This, of course, is a minus. Some guests are unhappy that breakfast cannot be ordered in the room. The lack of windows is also a bit unusual. But overall the hotel is quite comfortable and clean.

Family Guest House

This mini-hotel opens its doors on 11. Bolshaya Naberezhnaya Street. Accommodation for guests will cost from 500 rubles. If you are looking for cheap hotels in Tushino, then you should pay attention to this guest house.

At its core, this house is a spacious three-room apartment in which everything necessary for guests to stay is installed. All apartments have a bed, bedside tables, a table and chairs, a TV and bedding. Bathroom and toilet are shared by all guests. You will not find a certain style in the interior in the guest house "Family". Everything is simple, clean and comfortable. Walls in bright colors, large windows. The kitchen has everything you need for cooking and heating food, as well as for brewing tea and coffee.

The big minus is that the kitchen closes at 22:00 (the administrator sleeps there) - all guests note this in their reviews. There is a washing machine (a one-time rental costs 100 rubles), however, only the hostess can include it. In general, the guest house meets all the requirements of price and quality. It is quiet and calm. The staff is attentive, sometimes even redundant. Nearby there are shops and cafes. You can also note a convenient location relative to the metro and the main significant objects of the city.

Hostel Wood Owl

On Bolshaya Naberezhnaya Street, in Building 19, Building 2, this guest house opened.

The interior is created in a classic style with decorative elements in the form of owls on pillows and panels. Spacious shared kitchen with everything you need for cooking. There is also a microwave, kettle, refrigerator. Each room (with different capacities) has bunk beds with screens. Each guest has a locker, which is locked with a key.

There are rooms with 2 and 4 simple beds. Common facilities are on the floor. Free internet and breakfast for a fee can brighten up your stay in this place.

Guests praise this hostel. Its main advantage is its location. The mini-hotel is located in the green zone of Tushino, therefore it is always quiet and pleasantly smells of freshness.

The apartments are good for guest reviews. Each has its own cell for storing things, near the bed there is a socket and a shelf for small things. The screen closes the bed and creates an intimate atmosphere.

The shower room and toilet are always clean, but require repair or replacement of plumbing. In the kitchen you can cook and drink tea. Breakfast is cheap, but tourists do not like it. Many people prefer food in a cafe.

Hotel "Labor Reserves" in Tushino

Those who at least once visited Dosflot Avenue and saw the 10th house (building 1), or rather, an abandoned hotel, will have indelible impressions. The large five-story building behind the iron fence, surrounded by trees and shrubs, has been empty for more than 10 years. Many in their reviews say that before it was one of the largest hotels in Tushino.
