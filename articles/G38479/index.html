Hill Fresco 3 * (Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

Pattaya is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Thailand. And, of course, there are a lot of hotels of various categories on the territory of the city, among which each traveler can choose a place that suits his own needs. Pretty good is the hotel Hill Fresco 3 *. Where is the hotel located? What living conditions await a tourist here? Can I take children with me? What do hotel customers say? Answers to these questions will be useful to many.

Hotel Location





This resort is located in Pattaya. Pattaya Hill Fresco 3 * - a great place for a relaxing beach or a busy sightseeing holiday. The hotel building is just 400 meters from the beach. The distance to the city center is only 2.5 kilometers - can be reached by taxi. Suvarnabhumi International Airport is 128 km from Bangkok City.

Brief infrastructure description

In fact, Hill Fresco is a mid-sized hotel complex. It consists of two five-story buildings - the old and the new. By the way, this hotel is relatively young, as it was opened in 2007.

The hotel area, although small, is beautifully decorated and very well-groomed. Workers clean in the yard every day. The courtyard itself is a flowering garden that strikes with the colors of exotic plants. By the way, there is even a small artificial pond with lotuses, where goldfish and rare species of frogs live. In a word, it is always pleasant to spend time here, enjoying the fresh air, the shade of palm trees and the breathtaking beauty of nature.

Meals for guests





Many tourists say that this hotel has incorporated the best that Pattaya has to offer. Pattaya Hill Fresco 3 * is a great place where all the conditions for a really good vacation are created. When you check in, they will ask you what type of food suits you - BB (breakfast only) or HB (breakfast and dinner). At the reception you will be given special coupons for which you will enter the restaurant.

It is worth noting that the choice of dishes here is really large. You will be offered both international and traditional Thai cuisine, so there will be plenty to choose from. By the way, the restaurant will surely appeal to lovers of seafood, as here they are on the menu every day.

If you bought breakfast only when buying a tour, you can still have dinner at the hotel if you wish - the prices here are more than affordable. In addition, in the city, in particular, in the area next to the hotel, there are many cafes and other catering establishments . And some products can be bought in supermarkets.

Beach and water activities





The hotel complex Hill Fresco 3 * is located on the second beach line - the distance to the beach is about 400 meters. The beach here is municipal, but not too busy. Here, for a modest fee, you can rent a sun lounger and an umbrella for the whole day, as well as buy soft drinks and even snacks. Of course, you will have the opportunity to engage in motorized and non-motorized water activities, including boating, catamarans, water skiing and boats.

Entertainment for tourists

Of course, you will have the opportunity to have fun and usefully. One of the advantages of the hotel is the presence of a large pool with warm clean water. By the way, here, in the open air, there is a jacuzzi, which guests can use for free. And on comfortable sun loungers you can relax and take sun baths.

If you wish, you can visit the local gym and fitness center, where you will be sure to find the right training system or offer to participate in group classes. Also, the hotel has massage rooms every day, where experienced professionals offer their services.





What else can you offer Pattaya? Hill Fresco 3 * has its own tour desk, where they will tell you about the most interesting sights of the area, help you get a tour or make a route for an independent trip.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?

Since many people prefer to travel with the whole family, one of the most important issues is the availability of amenities for the child. What can the hotel offer Hill Fresco Pattaya ex 3 *?

In fact, the hotel has everything to make a child's stay comfortable and fun. Immediately upon arrival, a baby bed will be brought to your room. The local restaurant has a special children's menu - the food for the guys is not only healthy, but also delicious.

And there is plenty of entertainment here. For example, there is a separate section in the pool with a depth of 0.7 m, which is designed for bathing children. Your child will have a great time at the playground with various swings and sports fields. And if you need to leave for a while, then the baby can be safely left with a professional nanny - her services are not included in the price, but the price is quite affordable.
