Families with children, newlyweds, people of retirement age, lovers of active and quiet relaxation at the 3-star Lambi Hotel are waiting for good service, organic food, sports, sightseeing, cocktail bars, and a gift shop.

Climate

The island climate is created mainly by sunny weather and clear skies. Summer is hot, snowfall in winter. The favorable period for rest is April-June, September-October, when the average daily temperature is within + 25 ¬∞. In July, August - the heat is about 38 ¬∞.

Hotel Lambi 3 *, Ammoudara, Crete

"Lambi" 3 * is a modern architecture with snow-white balconies, a central columned portal surrounded by tented and open terraces with tables. White-cream facades immersed in tropical greenery.

Guests are greeted by a spacious air-conditioned hall, traditionally from hot countries, lined with cool ceramic tiles. Here you can sit down at the bar, have a cold drink, and then move into soft chairs. Windows on the floor offer beautiful views of the hotel. The whole atmosphere speaks of calmness and serenity of life here, sets up for pacifying relaxation.

Registration and check-in takes place without delay.



Description of rooms

Standard 1-3-bed rooms are simple and convenient. In Greece, it is not customary to create pompous interiors. White walls, tiled floors, pastel colors in the design. Each of the 108 rooms is equipped with a bath or shower, toilet. Toiletries, hairdryer. It features:

  • air conditioning;
  • TV, radio;
  • safe;
  • telephone.

If the room does not have a refrigerator, it will be provided upon request.

The furniture consists of comfortable beds, bedside and dressing tables with a mirror, benches, a wardrobe.

There is also a table and plastic chairs on the balcony. Here you can relax after a swim, enjoy the contemplation of flowering tropical plants, a panorama of the sea, mountains.

Lambi 3 * (Ammoudara) provides free round-the-clock service. Guests will appreciate the courtesy, friendliness of the staff.

Paid service includes parking, room service.



Nutrition

3 meals a day in the hotel restaurant consists of light breakfasts, hearty lunches and dinners. According to the reviews of guests, everyone is happy with the quality and quantity of dishes, the cordiality and attention of the staff.

In the resort village of Amudara there are many taverns, bars, restaurants of different price categories, including those with national cuisine, so tourists will not have to get bored of the monotonous food.

Hotel compliments

Compliments - small gifts - provided:

  • for newlyweds;
  • on the wedding anniversary;
  • on a birthday;
  • in honor of the arrival of a regular guest.

It is necessary to notify the hotelier about his status at the time of booking, and upon registration, submit the appropriate document. The fact of the second visit will be confirmed by computer information about the guests.



Entertainment at the hotel

On the territory of Lambi 3 * (Ammoudara) there is a large pool. From the walkways it is pleasant to dive into the clear blue water. On the other hand there is a staircase with handrails. The pool is spacious enough so that children can frolic with inflatable boats, without interfering with swimming adults.

On the tiled perimeter and a little further, on the lawns, in the shade of flowering trees and shrubs, you can soak up in comfortable sun loungers.

In the evening, from the illuminated turquoise mirror of the pool, it blows freshness. Mixing with the warm evening air, it adds a comfortable feeling to visitors to a nearby bar, to those who relax on sun loungers or balconies.

For general use there is a TV-room, a games room. Separately, there is a playground surrounded by palm trees, flowering tropical shrubs.

There are places for volleyball, table tennis.



Entertainment outside the hotel

Many excursion tours will introduce visitors to the hotel with historical places and the natural beauty of the island:

  • ancient ruins;
  • preserved fortresses, churches, palaces;
  • archaeological museum in the capital of Crete, Heraklion;
  • August feast of raisins in vineyards.

Trips around the island can be made individually in a rented car. Renting a car is easy.

Beach

The clean sandy beach of the Lambi 3 * hotel (Ammoudara) is 250 meters away. This is one of the 6-kilometer strip of the best shores of the island, stretching to Heraklion. The azure warm sea is safe for children and adults. The shore is never crowded.

Salty sea baths, aerotherapy with oxygenated air, healthy ions for the body, walks along forest and mountain trails will provide a boost of vigor and health for at least a year.

The hotel has a cocktail bar on the coastal area, where you can order both cool alcoholic drinks for adults and freshly squeezed juices for children.

Transfer

Crete can only be reached by plane. From the airport in the capital Heraklion there is a transfer - taxi to the hotel. Travel time is 15 minutes, so guests do not have to spend much time on the road.

Lambi 3 * (Ammoudara): reviews

3 stars is a pleasant stay with a good level of comfort. Guests with modest inquiries regarding everyday life appreciated primarily the healing air, clean sea, the ability to heal children and improve their health, shielding themselves from the city bustle.

To combine the trip with good shopping, the Lambi 3 * guests recommend visiting the supermarkets "Karfur" and "Leeds" within half an hour walk. The prices there, according to reviews, are lower than city and duty free. There is an opportunity to enjoy plenty of local fruits, buy good olive oil, wine, souvenirs.

Newlyweds, birthday people were pleased with the compliments from the hotel in the form of a beautiful bouquet and dishes with fresh chopped fruits.

In addition to this, a lounger with Wi-Fi access was taken for a day at a nearby beach cafe, buying an inexpensive cocktail from them, which did not burden guests at all.

At the end of the holiday, it is worth throwing a coin into the azure sea waves in order to return to the environmentally friendly natural oasis Lambi 3 * (Ammoudara) next year.
