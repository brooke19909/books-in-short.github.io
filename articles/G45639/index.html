Mallorca Hotels

Incredibly clear sea, perfect sandy beaches, chic hotels in Mallorca make it especially attractive not only for ordinary tourists, but even for the king of Spain. The royal family comes to enjoy their holidays on the island every year. This is an ideal place to feel on another planet, free from everyday life. Stunning nature combines forests, mountains and wide valleys. The sun shines almost every day of the year, the average temperature is about 28 degrees Celsius.

Holidays in Mallorca are a gift to romantic natures and creative people who value the novelty and unusualness of the surrounding reality.

On the island you will find a huge amount of entertainment, opportunities for water sports, visits to water parks.

There are also many beautifully preserved historical and cultural monuments, most of which date back to the 14th century.

Majorca hotels are generally characterized by the European level of service and the high quality of the services provided. Hotel reviews of Mallorca are generally positive. Nevertheless, among the many hotels on the island there are those that have been very popular for many years, and which tourists are always satisfied with.

Recommended Mallorca Hotels

Saratoga 4 *

This luxury hotel is located in Palma de Mallorca. It is located just a few steps from Jaime III - an elite commercial avenue. Nearby is the Museum of Modern Art, the port and the Cathedral of Palma.

All rooms with balconies are equipped with satellite TV, wireless internet.

At your service are two pools, a sauna, a fitness center, and a massage room.

It is noteworthy that the hotel has its own jazz club and you can listen to live music almost every evening. The local restaurant has a wide selection of Spanish and international dishes.

Playa Golf 4 *

This hotel is on the resort beach of Playa de Palma. All rooms have private balconies and air conditioning. The hotel has an outdoor pool, fitness and spa.

The hotel is located on the resort beach of Playa de Palma, about 10 minutes from Palma. It features an outdoor pool, an excellent spa, fitness club and air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony.

The hotel rooms are very comfortable, the decor is restrained and austere. The rooms have private bathrooms, satellite TV.

The hotel restaurant operates on a buffet basis.

Leisure activities offered by the hotel include a golf course, indoor pool and tennis courts. Car rental services are also available here.

Son Caliu Hotel Spa Oasis

Very picturesquely located in a secluded cove, surrounded by subtropical trees. The hotel is located next to an exclusive coastal area characterized by an international atmosphere.

The rooms are double, designer decorated. Guests have access to the award-winning spa, which offers a wide range of body care services . Guests have free access to the thermal pools, fitness room and pool.

Around the hotel are six top-level golf courses.

Tryp Bellver 4 *

Hotel of the same name chain. Located on the promenade of Palma de Mallorca, overlooks the bay. The hotel has a gym, outdoor pool, several golf courses.

All rooms have a track, soundproofed, air-conditioned. Internet access is available at an additional cost.

The buffet restaurant serves breakfast.

The city center is a 15-minute walk away.

Las Gaviotas Suites Hotel 4 *

The hotel is located 100 meters from the Playa de Muro resort beach in Mallorca. This is a luxury hotel. It is surrounded by a stunning garden of 15,000 m¬≤, which offers guests a sun terrace and swimming pool.

The hotel has standard rooms and suites. The latter have a balcony, free internet, flat-screen TV.

Not far from the hotel is the beautiful S'Albufera Nature Reserve.

On the territory of the hotel complex there is a gym, jacuzzi, massage room, snack bar. Representatives of the tour desk, currency exchange, car rental.

Four-star hotels in Mallorca - this is the best option for the ratio of price and quality of services. They are excellently suited for top-level recreation, while being more economical than five-star hotels.
