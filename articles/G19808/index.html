Island of Brac in Croatia: Attractions

There are many wonderful places to relax in the world. One of them is located on the Adriatic Sea. Pine forests, olive plantations, beautiful beaches and luxury hotels are the distinguishing features of the island of Brac in Croatia. How to get to it and what there are attractions, read on.



Description

On all sides, the island of Brac is surrounded by the sea, and its area is more than 400 square meters. km Of all the islands of Croatia, and there are a huge number of them, Brac is the most beloved and popular among holidaymakers. Reaching it is very easy. First by plane or train to Croatia, and then by any water transport to the island of Brac. One of the convenient options is to fly by plane to the city of Split, and then to the city of Bol, which has an international airport.

Sights of the island of Brac in Croatia

Not only beautiful landscapes and mild climate attract tourists here. On the island of Brac in Croatia there are a large number of entertainment venues and attractions. Here once the rich Romans lived, and archaeologists found priceless artifacts. The ability to touch the distant past is always of interest to many people. On the island of Brac in Croatia, you can not only relax in luxury hotels, but also walk through historical places, as well as get acquainted with interesting sights.

Dragon cave

Undoubtedly, the name of this place intrigues many travelers. Local residents can tell you about him a large number of legends and legends. The first information about the cave dates back to the fifteenth century. Dragon Cave is located in Murvice - one of the cities of the island of Brac in Croatia.

Here once lived a monk. All his time he devoted meditation and kept records for posterity. In them he talked about Christianity and the structure of the world. Many documents are well preserved and have survived to this day. In the cave you can see the inscriptions and signs on the walls. Unfortunately, they could not be deciphered. Tourists who have been here on excursions say that the cave has an amazing atmosphere, and that time seems to freeze on the spot. You begin to dream of something more elevated and inaccessible.





Vidova Gora

Another amazing place that is definitely worth a visit. The name of this mountain speaks for itself. It offers the most stunning views of the sea and the island of Brac in Croatia. Photos taken here will warm the soul for a long time.

Vidova Gora is the highest not only on the island, but also on the Adriatic coast. In the 13th century, a church was built at this place, in our days only ruins remain from it. At the top of the mountain there is a restaurant where you can relax and eat before the return road. Indeed, in order to climb to the very top of the mountain, which offers a beautiful overview of the island, you have to walk a couple of kilometers. Therefore, do not forget to wear comfortable shoes and hats, and be sure to bring soft drinks with you. You can not only climb the mountain on foot, but also drive in by car or bicycle along a special road.

Village Museum Shkrip

One of the oldest settlements on the island. Here, ancient houses and small palaces have been preserved in almost original form. Of particular interest to tourists is the archaeological museum located in the tower. Among its exhibits can be noted objects of the Stone Age, ancient gusli, ancient amphoras and sculptures, there are other valuable artifacts.

Only about two hundred residents live here, who are very good-natured and hospitable. The place is a real open-air museum where you can see unique architectural and historical monuments.





Bol

If you came to Croatia on the island of Brac, then by all means visit this amazing city. Once there was only a small village where fishermen and sailors lived. Today, Bol is one of the most popular resorts in Croatia. What is interesting here?

  • The beach "Golden Cape" is considered one of the best on the coast. What is its uniqueness? Under the influence of wind and sea, the beach is constantly changing its shape. Around it is a pine forest, so here even in the hottest weather you can find a cooler place. The beach is very popular with windsurfers.
  • In restaurants you can taste very tasty and healthy dishes. A large assortment of fish, shellfish, sardine and anchovy snacks and much more. Please note that the portions here are very large, be sure to consider this when ordering meals.
  • The art gallery is located in the palace building. Tourists have the opportunity to get acquainted with the creations of famous artists.
  • If you like incendiary music and a lot of fun, then definitely go to a disco. She is considered one of the best on the island.

Problems with transport in Bol, as a rule, do not arise. You can get to either end of the village by bus or private transport.



Milna

A small resort village is ideal for families with small children. There are all conditions for a carefree vacation. Warm sea, comfortable beaches, pine forests, restaurants and cafes. Many tourists come here to go diving. Milna was founded in the sixteenth century. Once imperial flotillas moored in its cozy harbors, and today - the most modern yachts and high-speed ships. Also in Milna there is a very rare example of architectural art - the ruins of the Gothic church of St. Joseph. Your vacation will take place in a calm and romantic atmosphere.



Blac Monastery

This amazing monument of human stamina and endurance was founded in the 16th century. Here lived the monks who escaped from the Turkish invaders. In difficult conditions, for many years they grew olive groves and cultivated vineyards. The monastery has a unique library, which was collected by the hermit Don Nikol Milisevich , who once lived here. This attraction can only be reached on foot.

What else can you do here?

What are the most popular activities for tourists and locals? So, a list of the most popular activities on the island of Brac in Croatia:

  • surfing;
  • diving;
  • unique sightseeing;
  • walk in historical places;
  • jet ski or water skiing;
  • study of exhibits of the archaeological museum in the city of Shkrip;
  • climb to Vidova Gora;
  • game of tennis;
  • visits to local restaurants and other catering establishments;
  • rest on the beaches of the island.




Paradise vacation spot

Many tourists love the mild Mediterranean climate and the beautiful scenery of the island of Brac in Croatia. Reviews of people vacationing here are full of enthusiasm and admiration. This place seems like paradise: the azure-colored sea, beautiful plants and flowers that emit amazing aromas, as well as a complete lack of heat. The restaurants will offer you the most exquisite dishes of Croatian cuisine: roast, smoked ham, sheep cheese, as well as delicious grape wine.

Here you can admire the beauty of the surrounding nature, take a photo for memory and take a fascinating excursion into the distant past. Holidays on the island of Brac in Croatia are a real relaxation for body and soul. Discovering these amazing places, it is simply impossible not to fall in love with them.
