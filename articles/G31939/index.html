How much make-up artists receive: salary level, working conditions and reviews

Modern life dictates its own conditions. Girls try to look attractive, competing with each other. Therefore, the work of makeup artists is more than enough. If earlier ladies themselves did makeup, and professional make-up artists exclusively did it, now everything has changed. Many women also want to get flawless makeup, but not everyone knows this art. As you know, demand creates supply. Therefore, make-up artists appeared on the services market.

Profession popularity

The demand for specialists who can do professional makeup is increasing from year to year. Makeup artists are people who have creative talent and a delicate taste. Due to the abundance of all kinds of techniques and tools, a professional is able to significantly change the appearance of the client.

Makeup artists are different ...

One common name combines several directions at once:

  • The makeup artist is exclusively engaged in decorative cosmetics. His task is to gently apply shadows, blush, tone, etc.
  • Makeup artist-stylist provides a wider range of services. In addition to makeup, he can pick up a hairstyle, accessories, give tips on applying decorative cosmetics.
  • Make-up cosmetologist works not only with decorative, but also with makeup products. He must be well versed in the types of skin and be able to choose the right tools.

Who can become a makeup artist?

In this profession, not only special skills, but also personal qualities are of great importance:

  • Sociability. Extroverts are more likely to find and communicate with customers. Are you burdened by someone else's society, and the need to contact with unfamiliar people turns into a hateful duty? Perhaps it is worth giving up the career of a makeup artist.
  • Stress resistance. A make-up specialist constantly has to communicate with people. Some of them will be capricious and even with a successful result will remain unhappy. We must not lose our temper and not give up.
  • Appearance. Clients trust a well-groomed master more than a slobber with sloppy manicure or hair regrowth.

How much do makeup artists get?

This question interests many. However, a definite answer simply does not exist. In order to somehow form an impression of the salaries of makeup artists, you need to delve deeper into this topic in more detail.

Income depends on:

  • places of work;
  • type of employment;
  • number of customers;
  • cost of services;
  • demand for the master.

How much does a makeup artist receive per month? The average income of a Russian makeup artist is 36 thousand rubles. The spread of salaries is from 15 to 60 thousand rubles. Interestingly, according to statistics, Moscow was not the leader in terms of income. It is difficult for a makeup artist to earn more than 50 thousand rubles a month. This is due to the abundance of craftsmen who, in order to attract customers, significantly reduce prices. Other masters, faced with a lack of orders, are forced to follow the example of their colleagues. Ultimately, this reduces the income of all makeup artists on the market.

Where do makeup artists work?

Even for a newly made make-up master, there are many vacancies. Such employees are recruited to beauty salons, photo studios, theaters and television. If you have experience, you can qualify for participation in professional photo shoots and fashion shows. Due to the irregular schedule, the work of a makeup artist can be combined with training, permanent employment or a life full of other events.

Having settled down in a beauty salon or photo studio, you can engage in private practice or undergo training and get an additional skill. You can not only do makeup, but also engage in the selection of accessories, compiling the entire bow. Having learned how much a stylist, make-up artist receives, you decide to seriously consider this option. After all, income can be about 200 thousand rubles. This largely depends on the number and generosity of customers. However, the presence of a goal always makes you develop and strive for more.

How much make-up artists get in St. Petersburg

Going to makeup courses, future specialists are confident that immediately after receiving the certificate they will quickly find a stable job. However, not all salons are ready to hire an inexperienced master without a portfolio. Even if the employer arranges a certificate obtained at the courses of visage, it is not necessary to count immediately on a large income.

To get serious experience in the profession, the makeup artist will have to work 1-2 years with a fixed salary of 20-30 thousand rubles. This period should be regarded as a paid practice. Serious income should not be expected, because it is work for the future.

How much do makeup artists get? By compiling a portfolio and building up a client base, the master can seriously increase his income - up to 50-60 thousand rubles, which is about two times more than the initial rates.

How much do makeup artists pay in Moscow?

Revenues in the capital will not differ much from the figures indicated above. It is curious that Moscow make-up artists can earn even less because of high competition. However, do not forget about the ability to independently find customers, and not work at a fixed rate.

How much do makeup artists get in Moscow? Having a stable flow of customers and setting favorable rates for himself, the master can earn 100 thousand rubles or more. However, the final figure is affected by seasonality. The disadvantage of self-employment is that you can never predict the level of income for sure.

How much do makeup artists get? In the wedding season, a sought-after professional can earn about 300 thousand rubles a month. However, one must be prepared for a subsequent recession and reduction in income.

What do makeup artists think of their work?

Probably, there is not a single profession whose representatives would be completely satisfied. Reviews of makeup artists are no exception. Among the shortcomings, for example, noted complex customers and physical activity. However, the possibility of creative realization becomes a big plus for many. Experienced experts believe that almost the main quality of a good makeup artist is the art of making contact with any person.
