Interesting about Baikal

No one can exactly answer the question of where the name Baikal came from. This topic was devoted to a lot of scientific works and literary publications. But not a single scientist could present peremptory evidence regarding at least one theoretical conjecture.

Where Baikal is located, beliefs and legends live. In the past, the peoples living near Lake Baikal in their own way called the lake: "a large body of water" or "rich water." If we take into account the age of the reservoir, then, according to scientific research, Baikal was formed more than 20 million years ago.

Usually, ice age lakes live for about 15 thousand years, and then gradually disappear from the face of the earth. However, Baikal does not show any signs of aging. Judging by recent studies, geophysicists have suggested that Lake Baikal may well be a nascent ocean.

Advice

Holidays on the famous Lake Baikal are quite diverse. If you dream about swimming, summer fishing and other entertainments in the hot season, then it is more advisable to go on vacation in July-August. At the beginning of summer, it is still cold on Lake Baikal, and the water was not warm enough. And in the northern part of the reservoir there are even floating ice floes. The end of August can overshadow the pastime of travelers with gusty winds. Water during this period is mixed, and its temperature decreases. Extreme vacation





So, you have come to Baikal! Where is the place that will give the traveler a drive? What else will conquer this famous vacationer territory? Baikal is able to provide the traveler with an extreme form of relaxation that can cause him a special surge of emotions and adrenaline. This is especially promoted by the dynamic rafting along the mountain rivers of the territory of Lake Baikal. Such water travels are noteworthy in that, after such a trip, the tourist is not indifferent to this type of rest.

It is worth at least once to see the wild nature of the Siberian taiga, rafting along a stormy river with a group of comrades. Memories of the euphoria of such a trip will accompany many years. So, remember that mountain rivers originate in the upper reaches of the mountains. River layers of water rush down from there - to the plateaus and plains. Due to sudden changes in altitude, the flow rate of mountain rivers ranges from 25 to 30 kilometers per hour. The number of obstacles encountered along the course of the course (rocks, waterfalls) determines the category of complexity of the water alloy.

The first category includes calm water, the sixth - obstacles with waterfalls and rapids reaching up to 15 meters. Tourists can conquer such mountain rivers on rafts. This is the most popular boat for such an extreme form of recreation. It is an inflatable boat. Its bottom contains holes for draining the water that has got inside the boat.

Raft differs from ordinary inflatable boats in that its design is reinforced with a durable shell, special fastenings for travelers' legs and two-layer balloons. Raft is used when passing water obstacles of 1-5 category of difficulty.

To get more acute emotions when passing rapids, tourists use a catamaran. These are two inflatable balloons made of very durable material, which are interconnected by an aluminum partition. A catamaran on the water is well stable and gives a stronger feeling than a raft.

To control the catamaran, more precise actions are needed. Usually it accommodates 2-4 people. On such a device, the passage of water obstacles from 1 to 6 categories of complexity is possible.

If you ask yourself where to go to Lake Baikal, then you should make a trip to the top of Mount Munku - Sardyk. Such a trip can be unforgettable, as this place is considered the highest point in Eastern Siberia. Tourists who have reached the snow-white summit will enjoy stunning views of the lakes frozen under the ice. At this moment, the nature of Lake Baikal will seem incredible! The best time to enjoy such beauty is April and May.

Tourists who have visited Baikal more than once note that, most likely, this trip will change a beginner. The mystery of this Siberian place can affect the subtle soul strings of a person, awakening in him a sense of novelty and harmony.
