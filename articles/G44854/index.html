Hotel Island View Resort (ex Sunrise Island View Resort 5)

It has a huge territory, but the whole life of vacationers is concentrated around Soho Square, in the area with the slightly frightening name Sharks Bay (Shark Bay). Fifty meters from this busy place with cafes, restaurants and shopping centers, which is famous for with its singing fountains and duty-free shops, the Island View Resort is located. Complex Island View Resort - ex Sunrise Island View - is very significant in size. It consists of a large number of residential premises - 492 air-conditioned rooms. 164 of them belong to the Double Standard category, and 296 belong to the Junior Suite (junior suite). Another twelve Triple rooms can accommodate three guests, fifteen Family Rooms are designed for a family of four, there are also five Executive Suite suites. Beach: For some reason, the name of the White Knight Beach has been fixed to the coast on which the hotel is located. Maybe because nearby is the eponymous restaurant. Island View Resort - ex Sunrise Island View Resort 5 - has a fenced area for swimming. There are a lot of sunbeds and umbrellas, all of them are free. Beach towels are also given, and not for money.
