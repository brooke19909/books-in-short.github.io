Resort Pernera Beach Hotel 3 * (Protaras, Cyprus): hotel description

When planning a vacation, it is important not to make a mistake in choosing a hotel. So, many travelers have chosen for themselves Pernera Beach Hotel 3 *. Protaras, Cyprus, 5310, Ifestou, 17 - address of the institution. This is one of the best resort places in the southeast of Cyprus. In a quiet tourist area you can fully enjoy the natural beauties and unique atmosphere.

Initially, it was a modest family business. Now it is a three-star hotel, which has 233 rooms of different categories for a complete rest of guests. Developed infrastructure includes swimming pools, restaurants, spa centers and many other facilities. And a 5-minute walk from the hotel is a lot of entertainment and other tourist sites.

It is worth noting that in 1978 the history of the Pernera Beach Hotel 3 * began. Protaras, Cyprus was already popular among travelers then. After a complete reconstruction in 2013, the institution was brought into line with international standards and modern ideas about the resort hotel. In 2015, the number of rooms was expanded and new infrastructure facilities were added.





How to get there

The closest to this resort hotel is Larnaca International Airport. The institution is a little more than 60 km away from it. There are several ways to get to your destination:

  • If you are renting a car upon arrival, you need to take the A3 motorway towards Ayia Napa. Upon reaching the end of the freeway, at the roundabout, take the exit to Protaras. After the nearest intersection, you will see signs for the hotel that you need to follow until the end of the journey.
  • Leaving the airport lounge, on the left you will see a taxi rank. Each of the cars is designed for 4-7 passengers. Transfer can be booked in advance by contacting the hotel manager.
  • If you are traveling in a group, it is most convenient to book a minibus in advance. do it better at the booking stage.
