Preliminary educational program

For some, St. Petersburg is saturated with the spirit of the Russian monarchy. The ancient capital of Russian emperors beckons historians, adventurers, dreamers. This city has a rich history, it is a child of many eras. A preliminary acquaintance with the history of the city will certainly help determine the route. If you are going to St. Petersburg for a weekend from Moscow, do not even hope to see all of his beauties in such a short time.

The city was laid by Peter I in 1703 on lands conquered from the Swedes. First, in the middle of the swamps, the fortress of St. Petersburg grew, and later a city formed around it. Just ten years later, the royal court moved here. The infrastructure began to develop: educational scientific institutions, theaters and museums, palaces and gardens were built.





By the end of the 18th century, with the light hand of Empress Catherine II, the Admiralty appeared here, the Fontanka and Moika embankments settled down, the beautiful Nevsky Prospect, the Winter Palace appeared. St. Isaac's Cathedral was built after victory in the war with Napoleon.

The revolution of 1917 brought the city not only a new name - Leningrad, but also greatly influenced its development. A whole cultural layer is associated with it. What is the mention of Smolny alone. Unfortunately, the First World War and subsequent tragic events provoked a significant decrease in the number of inhabitants. Part died, someone emigrated.

But Leningrad continued to live and develop. Soon the first film studio Lenfilm was opened in the country. Huge tests brought the Second World War. The city survived the blockade.

In Soviet times, the glory of the cultural capital for Peter finally entrenched. The natives of here glorified Soviet art and science throughout the world. After the collapse of the USSR, the city was returned the historical name - St. Petersburg. In 2003, he magnificently celebrated the 300th anniversary. And in the fall of 2012, a five millionth resident was born in St. Petersburg.

What cultural layer attracts you? What is causing more interest? Be sure to keep this in mind when planning your trip. Then your excursion to St. Petersburg for the weekend from Moscow will bring maximum pleasure.

There are several ways to get to St. Petersburg from the capital. The train ride will take several hours. Several flights depart daily in both directions. Who has not heard of the legendary Red Arrow? Traveling on this extraordinary train is an adventure in itself. It runs between Moscow and St. Petersburg for over 80 years, but today its technical equipment is the most progressive. The cars are equipped with double-glazed windows, the latest furniture, comfortable bathrooms. In addition to the usual coupe, a place in which will cost about 2 800 rubles, in the "Red Arrow" there are wagons of increased comfort.
