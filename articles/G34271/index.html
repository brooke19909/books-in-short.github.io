Dar Khayam 3 * (Hammamet, Tunisia): description, services, food, reviews

Hammamet is a European-class resort, one of the most respectable in Tunisia. Considered to be calm and conservative, Hammamet is widely known for its sandy beaches and a thalassotherapy center equipped in accordance with modern requirements. A quiet, measured rest harmoniously combines here with a vibrant nightlife, represented by a mass of upscale restaurants, discos and bars. Most tourists come here to combine relaxation with the passage of anti-aging wellness treatments.





Fans of a comfortable and respectable holiday can safely recommend accommodation in 3 * and 4 * Hammamet hotels. Dar Khayam 3 *, popular among tourists, also belongs to high-level establishments.
