Ski resorts in Italy: Cervinia. Trails, hotels, reviews

Italy is one of the best places for an active winter holiday. Such an industry has been developing in this country for a long time, so today all the conditions for a comfortable vacation in the winter are created here. The beautiful slopes of the Italian Alps are so striking in their majestic beauty that in 2009 they were listed by UNESCO as a natural heritage of the planet. One of the most beautiful places famous for the ski resorts of Italy is Cervinia.

Not only experienced athletes, but also careful beginners will be able to find the track here. People who have already visited these places say that prices for holidays in the Italian Alps are pleasing - they are much lower than in French or Swiss ski resorts.

Cervinia is especially popular. Italy is a ski resort, tours for which are most in demand among foreigners. Developed infrastructure is another advantage of Italian resort complexes. Here you can find everything you need to relax after intense skiing on the mountain slopes. Hotels, night clubs, various restaurants with excellent Italian cuisine, SPA salons, cafes, bars, shops selling ski equipment, and many other interesting places await skiers who decide to organize their winter holidays in Italy. Here you can relax perfectly on your own or with the whole family.

Cervinia

At the foot of the tall peak of the Matterhorn lies the highest Italian ski resort - Cervinia. Almost everyone who decides to visit the ski resorts in Italy comes here . Cervinia is a small settlement, as if "stolen" from a New Year's card. Lovely toy houses and majestic mountain landscapes have made this place the most popular ski resort in Italy. Despite its small size, the town has restaurants, shops and supermarkets. The town lives off tourism and is therefore trying so hard to please vacationers, creating ideal conditions for a winter holiday.



Service

Like many other ski resorts in Italy, Cervinia provides the highest quality customer service. For the benefit of athletes, there are 8 funiculars, as well as chairlifts, ski tows and cabin lifts. These leisurely devices are the only reliable means of transportation on the snowy slopes of the Italian Alps.

The population of Cervinia is only a thousand people, however, the village can accommodate up to 15 thousand tourists at the same time. The environment here has not yet felt the harmful effects of the industrial era, and the ban on the use of vehicles allows you to keep the mountain air clean and healthy.

Trails

For beginners in skiing, or for those who have recently started their acquaintance with this exciting sport, the best that the Italian ski resorts can offer is Cervinia. Reviews of those who have already visited this magical place will melt even the most callous hearts. The tracks here are of considerable length, so the descent along them rarely takes less than 20 minutes. There are many blue slopes, which will allow beginners to diversify their training in skiing, instead of training on the same track.



But experienced athletes Cervinia is also ready to provide slopes on which they will not have to be bored: red and black are also found here, albeit less often than blue.

The best that the Italian ski resorts offer is Cervinia. The circuit diagram here resembles a web that has entangled the slopes of Matterhorn peak. In such a variety it is difficult not to find a ski track for your skill in skiing.

Reviews for Cervinia and Resort Hotels

The place that glorified the Italian ski resorts is Cervinia. Hotels are almost at every step, so everyone can choose something to their liking. Tourists claim that this resort offers many hotels of different price categories.



Bucaneve

Hotel Bucaneve is a beautiful four-star hotel in the very center of the spa town. Vacationers delighted with it. This is a real paradise for lovers of wooden finishes and traditional Italian outfits. A very nice place that gives the impression of antiquity. However, the amenities here are the most modern. There is a restaurant, a bar, a parking lot, a left-luggage office, a hydromassage, a sauna, a gym and many other delights of civilization. Another plus of this hotel is that it is located near the ski lift. But the cost of living in Bucaneve will cost fans of skiing a round sum.

Europe

A huge number of wealthy people are attracted to Cervinia, Italy. A ski resort of this level is simply obliged to have expensive and comfortable complexes, such as Europe. This four-star hotel is the largest in Cervinia. It has about 80 comfortable rooms. Hotel Europe is always ready to please visitors with friendly staff and a wide range of services. There is a sauna, a swimming pool, a hydromassage, a solarium, a gym - in general, everything you need for a healthy, high-quality vacation.



Astoria

Another hotel that incorporates all the best that the ski resorts of Italy have. Cervinia provides vacationers the opportunity to stay at the cozy three-star Hotel Astoria. There is a bar, a solarium, a games room, a living room, and a spacious lounge will delight the eye. Vacationers say that the hotel is located next to the ski lifts, also very close to many shops, local cafes, restaurants and a swimming pool.

Punta maquignaz

The 4-star Hotel Punta Maquignaz is the most luxurious hotel Cervinia, Italy can provide. The ski resort, the photo of which is so amazing, is visible from here in the best possible way. On one side of the hotel offers a wonderful view of the city, and on the other - you can enjoy the view of the mountain peaks.



The hotel is centrally located and looks luxurious, reminiscent of a French chalet. The rooms are also distinguished by chic decoration, and excellent service has long been the hallmark of the Punta Maquignaz hotel. Here you can find a hydromassage, a reading room, a spacious fireplace room with a view of the Matterhorn, a restaurant, a bar, a garage, a gym. If you have the necessary money, this hotel will be a great place for a winter holiday in the Italian Alps.

Breithorn

Great place Cervinia! Italy, the ski resort of which has earned worldwide recognition, has created all the conditions for a comfortable stay of vacationers. For example, the two-star Breithorn Hotel is a great place to relax in Cervinia. It is located near the ski lifts and is made in the Alpine style, which gives it a special charm.

Tourists note that there are not as many amenities as in expensive hotels, but there is everything you need: a bar, a restaurant, a solarium, a garage, and a parking lot. Prices are lower than in four-star luxury hotels, which makes this small hotel even more attractive.



Chalet valdotain

If you don‚Äôt like the noise and the crowded streets of Cervinia, then this small alpine-style hotel is perfect, located at some distance from the center of the spa town. Chalet Valdotain impresses guests with a cozy atmosphere and spectacular views. The hotel is located next to the fabulous landscape of the Blue Lake. Therefore, here all year round you can observe spectacular views of mountain nature.

This hotel has excellent cuisine and many interesting entertainments. There is an indoor pool, baths and saunas, as well as a jacuzzi and a gym. There is a piano bar, a solarium, parking, garage, games room.

Chalet Valdotain is suitable for those who value comfort and leisure, peace and solitude. It is pleasant to relax here not only in winter, but also at any other time of the year, since the views of the mountain slopes, overgrown with mighty coniferous trees, impress vacationers in winter and summer.
