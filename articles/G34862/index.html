Comfort sheep

For the smallest, you can also make a sheep, which will be in the baby's crib. Toys for such crumbs are called comforters. They are a piece of fabric sewn in two layers, with the toy head attached to it in the center or on one side. A self-made sheep pattern can be a good help in this work. For sewing you will need:

  • children's fleece blanket;
  • a toy sheep bought in a store;
