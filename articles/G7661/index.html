Crochet flower for beginners, use it as a decor

Flowers are the most beautiful thing that nature has come up with. But it is unfortunate that they so quickly lose their beauty and attractive appearance. So, it‚Äôs impossible to decorate your wardrobe or its individual parts with fresh flowers. But there is a way out - crochet the flowers. For beginners, you can choose simpler models, and experienced knitters will be able to do complex and original options. There are many different patterns for crocheting flowers, but even without knowing any of them, you can come up with something of your own, not like everyone else. Your skill and imagination will help you with this. To begin, consider knitting the simplest flower.

Crochet Flower for Beginners

A flower connected in this way can be used to decorate any knitted products, for example, hats. So, for its implementation you will not need a large amount of yarn, just take a small ball of residues. The threads can be any: both woolen and cotton, the main thing is that they meet your idea and correspond to the size of the hook. Next, consider how to crochet flowers.





Schemes, descriptions and ideas for decor

Knitting begins with five air loops and connecting them into a ring. Next, we tie the resulting circle with crochets. The number of columns should be a multiple of the number of petals. In our case, this is 15 single crochet. In the next row, you need to tie the base for the petals. These are air loops. We knit a chain of 4 air loops and attach it, passing two single crochet posts. You should get 5 rings of air loops. In the next row, we begin to knit the petals. To do this, we knit 2 single crochet, 3 double crochet, 1 double crochet, 3 single crochet, 2 single crochet, into a ring. One petal is ready. In the next ring, we repeat knitting in the above order. Having connected the last petal, we cut the thread and fix it.



Crochet flower for beginners is ready! It remains only to place it at the planned place of the finished product. And by tying such flowers in large numbers and combining them with each other, you can create unique beauty items - blouses, boleros, topics and so on.

Another crocheted flower option

This flower model is also not very difficult to perform. I would like to note that crocheting such a flower for beginners will not be difficult, since the technique of knitting it is not very different from the previous version. The inner part (middle) is performed according to the above scheme, but this does not stop knitting.

Further, on the underside of the flower, we will continue knitting, performing rings of air loops in the same way as before. The difference is only in the number of air loops (there should be more) and in the method of attachment. If in the first case we fixed the air loops to the single crochet, now we will fasten them at the junction of the two already connected petals. This is done as follows: first, the air loops are knitted, and then the hook from the lower side is inserted into the hole of the petal and lowered into the next petal, the working thread is captured, stretched under the flower and knitted there with a half-column.

In the next row you need to fill in the resulting petals. The scheme is as follows - 3 single crochet, 4 double crochet, 2 double crochet, 4 single crochet, 3 single crochet. The lower petals are larger than the previous ones and look out from under them. We knit the third row of petals similarly to the second. At this stage, we finish knitting and fasten the thread on the wrong side.





Very simple and original option

Crocheting flowers is a very exciting experience. After all, with their help you can create unique masterpieces. It can be elegant clothes for children and adults or some home furnishings, such as pillows, rugs, napkins, and much more.

The option that will be discussed later is very simple to execute, and using different shades of yarn, you can achieve a very original combination of the pattern.

Like the first two options, such a crochet flower for beginners will also be able to master.

First you need to tie a chain of five air loops and connect them into a ring. The following is a single crochet. Do not forget to change the colors in the process of knitting so that they overlap with the overall color scheme.



The next step is to knit the base for the petals. To do this, we knit 10-13 air loops and fix the ring on the adjacent column. Next, we knit several single crochet columns (the amount depends on the size of the ring) and make the next petal. There should be four of them. Make sure they are evenly spaced throughout the middle ring.

Now we knit inside the petals double crochet, as much as fits. The main thing is that the petal should be smooth, not tightened or, conversely, wavy. And in the last row we tie the resulting flower in single crochet columns. The color can be left the same as the petals, or you can change the contrast. In both cases, the flower looks very beautiful.
