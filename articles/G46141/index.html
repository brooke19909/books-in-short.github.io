Coral Bay: description, features, nature and interesting facts

Translated from the Arabic language, Sharm el-Sheikh means "Sheikh Bay." The emergence of this name of the city is associated with the presence of an extensive coral reef off its coast. This Egyptian resort city gained worldwide fame after repeated studies of local depths by the famous oceanographer Jacques-Yves Cousteau.

Sharm El Sheikh: Coral Bay in the Red Sea





The warm waters of the Egyptian Red Sea are home to some of the most breathtakingly beautiful coral reefs. Sea life boils in it, and here you can observe one of the most beautiful underwater landscapes on the planet. Ancient wrecks, coral gardens with hundreds of marine species, spectacular walls, canyons and caves are waiting for brave divers who flock from all over the world to the Red Sea. Here you can find over 400 species of coral and about 1,500 species of fish, turtles, sharks and dolphins. Each dive is an adventure, and no one ever knows what surprises await under the surface of the water.

What is interesting Coral bay

A typical resort picture is characteristic of the area near the Coral Bay: along the coast are hotel complexes, water parks, evergreen palm trees and snow-white beaches. For a long time, local resorts were considered the best place for those who prefer a relaxed vacation in the hot sun.

Coastal hotels with cool pool water, fun animations, lazy bliss of hammams - all this has long been a hallmark of Egyptian resorts.

Of particular interest to vacationers is the beach "Coral Bay". In the Sharks Bay area, the beach consists entirely of coral chips, and you can go into the water by going through a long row of pontoons. This place is very popular with diving enthusiasts. The only place where there is no coral in the beach sand is the Sharm El Maya area.

Sharm El Sheikh - the best resort in the Sinai

The most popular and expensive resort in the southern part of the Sinai Peninsula is Egyptian Sharm El Sheikh. He is considered the most Europeanized.

The coast near Sharm el-Sheikh is completely cut by bays: Sharm el-Maya, Gadenz Bay, Naama Bay, Pasha Bay, Ras Umm al-Sid, Ras Nasrani, Nabq, Sharks Bay. Almost all of them are protected by the state, which means that landscaping is prohibited here. In order to swim in their waters, you will need special shoes so as not to injure your feet on the coral bottom.

Windsurfing enthusiasts prefer Nabq Bay for relaxation - windy weather and high waves often occur here. You can calmly swim in the bays of Naama Bay and Sharm El Maya, as they are protected from the wind.

Amazing underwater world

Coral Bay is considered to be one of the best places on the planet for exploring the underwater world. Due to the fact that rivers do not flow into the Red Sea, it is distinguished by its crystal clearness. Therefore, its clear waters have become the center of attraction for many species of fish and other marine animals.

The marine fauna and flora of the Coral Bay is striking in its abundance: here you can see a variety of corals, sea turtles, dolphins, sharks, sand moray eels, bright angel fish, sultanoks.





The unique and extremely picturesque underwater world, which the Coral Bay is so rich in, has become a source of attraction for tourists. The entire Egyptian coast of the Red Sea is incredibly popular with scuba diving enthusiasts due to the long coral reefs.

The most beautiful coral reefs have different contours: round, branched, flat, and sometimes they take an unusual bizarre shape. Their color scheme is also interesting - from bright yellow and pink shades to dark blue and black.

Interesting facts about Coral bay

The water in the Coral Bay is particularly transparent, so divers who decide to make an underwater trip in its waters can be seen from the side of the vessel, even if they plunged to a depth of 15 meters. Such purity of water is explained by its strong salinity, the absence of flowing rivers and the fact that representatives of marine flora and fauna living under the water column constantly purify it.





The color of water Coral bay can tell a lot of interesting things:

  • The bright blue color indicates the location of corals very close to the surface of the water, and also to a depth of not more than 20 meters.
  • The dark blue and almost black color of the water indicates that the depth exceeds 50 meters.

Hotels along the coast of the Coral Bay

In the resort of Sharm el-Sheikh there is a great variety of hotels for every taste and budget. The most popular hotels are those that have their own beach off the coast of the Coral Bay or are in close proximity to it.

On the first line from the bay is the Coral Sea Waterworld 5 * hotel, which offers its guests classes in numerous water sports, including windsurfing, water skiing, and it also has a well-equipped diving center.

Domina Coral Bay Oasis Garden Hotel 5 * is located just a few steps from the Coral Bay. Hotel guests can endlessly admire the views of the coral reef and the nearby island of Tiran from the windows of their rooms. A private sandy beach, the possibility of diving and snorkeling are included in the hotel infrastructure and the list of services provided.

Coral Beach Resort Tiran 4 * hotel attracts tourists with relatively inexpensive prices for accommodation and the proximity of the beautiful coral reef, which is located just 2 meters from the pier stairs. Here you can see the whole variety of coral and fish of the Coral Bay. The property organizes yacht excursions around the bay.

Beach coral bay reviews

Those who had the pleasure of relaxing in Sharm el-Sheikh, always speak with enthusiasm about the Coral Bay. Tourists are struck by unimaginable beauty, a huge number of moray eels, stingrays and other bright fish. Many divers recognize the Coral Bay as one of the best places on the planet for diving.





In addition to coastal and underwater beauties, tourists note the high level of service provided by local hotels. This is a resort of European level, where comfort is of great importance. In the daytime, the animators hold many entertainment events, and in the evening - a variety of show programs. Therefore, guests will not be bored.
