Museums of Novosibirsk: list, description and photo

Novosibirsk is the largest industrial and cultural center of Siberia. A whole mass of attractions are concentrated here that can interest any tourist. In the presented material, I would like to consider the museums of Novosibirsk, a list, the addresses of the best institutions of the presented plan.

Museum of Railway Engineering

We will begin to survey the museums of Novosibirsk, (a list of the best of them), from the exhibition site, which is located near the Sower station. This museum was founded in 2000. Its founder is the leadership of the West Siberian Railway. The author of the idea is a well-known railwayman in several generations N. A. Akulinin. It was this figure who for nine years was the permanent director of the institution.





The expositions of the railway museum are concentrated in the open air on a stretch of about 3 kilometers. Here visitors are offered old diesel locomotives, steam locomotives, wagons, railway equipment, cars of past eras. The total number of exhibits today exceeds 100 units.

Particular attention of museum visitors is attracted by the “Agile” steam locomotive, which was in operation as early as the mid-19th century. Among the expositions, the Victory, Moskvich, and Volga cars are also of interest. The museum even features an old American Dodge.

Art Museum

Considering the museums of Novosibirsk, a list of the most interesting places, it is worth noting the State Art Museum. It is the largest exhibition center not only in Siberia, but also beyond the Urals.

The main part of the expositions are old icons. You can also see here the masterpieces of domestic artists, in particular, the works of Vasnetsov, Aivazovsky, Shishkin. The museum presents paintings by English, Flemish, Italian and French masters.





In addition to the works of artists of past eras, the museum regularly exhibits the fruits of the works of modern Siberian painters, organizes excursions, competitions, lectures with the participation of famous personalities.

Museum of Funeral Culture

Looking at the museums of Novosibirsk (list with description), one cannot ignore the unique exhibition site, whose expositions are devoted to the funerary culture of the peoples of the world. Here you can see engravings, paintings, sculptures, crafts, which are dedicated to the art of burial of the dead.





Of particular interest to museum visitors is the antiques and memorials that were stored in the homes of wealthy Europeans. Items, among which the main place is occupied by the personal belongings of the deceased, at one time were on the estates of the nobility in the most prominent places. With the advent of photography, tradition has gradually become a thing of the past.

How to find a museum? It is located in the nearby city crematorium in the Memory Park.

USSR Museum

Continuing to browse the museums of the city of Novosibirsk, a list of the best of them is worth paying attention to the exhibition area dedicated to the culture and life of the Soviet Union. Expositions are presented by household items that are familiar to every person who was born in the middle of the last century. Here you can see clothes of a past era, household appliances, all kinds of medals, badges, uniforms, portraits of politicians and leaders, posters, etc.





The museum staff offers the visitor to take part in costumed tours, part of which is initiation into pioneers, familiarity with the features of the service in government bodies of the Soviet period.

Where is the museum? You can find it by reaching the Zheleznodorozhny district of the city, and then proceeding to the following address: 16 Maxim Gorky Street.

Sun Museum

Reviewing the museums of Novosibirsk, a list of the most interesting places in the city, it is worth noting the site, completely dedicated to the Sun, as well as its symbolism. In separate rooms you can see symbols of the celestial bodies of different eras and images of the gods worshiped by the peoples of the region.

Within the walls of the museum is a large collection of historical and scientific literature, poems, songs, tales dedicated to the Sun. In addition, there is a creative workshop where residents of the city study applied art.

You can find the museum at the following address: 11A Ivanova Street.

Museum of Communications

The exhibition site, which is located in the very center of Novosibirsk, offers visitors a whole lot of exhibits that are dedicated to the development of communications in the region. Here are examples of the first telegraph devices, antique telephones, radio equipment, television sets.

It is recommended to visit this place to everyone who wants to broaden their horizons, to learn the history of the development of technologies, and also to understand how humanity has come to modern achievements in science.

Finally

So we looked at the museums of Novosibirsk, a list of places that are able to interest every visitor to the city. Our top not all sites where exhibits of cultural and historical significance are displayed. However, it is these museums that are most popular among tourists.
