Meizu M1 Note: reviews and specifications

Today we will tell you about the Meizu M1 Note smartphone , the reviews of the owners of which confirm that the device is worthy of attention. The manufacturer of our hero was a very ambitious company from China.

Representation



At the presentation, the manufacturer noted that he was creating a new brand called Blue, especially for a separate line of devices. In other words, in our native China, our hero is called differently. There it is customary to call it Blue M1 Note. As for the English-speaking resources of the manufacturer, the name has remained familiar there, we can conclude that the new brand is designed exclusively for the domestic market.

Another round in development

The key advantage of Meizu M1 Note is the price, because, according to the manufacturer, this device allows him to enter a new market segment of extremely inexpensive smartphones. If the whole series will be such as the device we are describing, we can say that these are not just advertising slogans, but reality, because in your homeland a smartphone costs only 1,000 yuan and at the same time has a very good design and characteristics. Disadvantages, of course, exist, but the overall impression is not spoiled. If you look at the opinion of the owners of Meizu M1 Note, reviews indicate that the device lacks originality. And now we will try to explain what is the matter.

Design

You should start a detailed review of Meizu M1 Note with a description of the appearance of the device. At first glance, the phone can be called an enlarged copy of the iPhone 5c from Apple. Curiously, in the description of the M1 Note, which was made on the presentation of the device, the iPhone 5c was directly mentioned. Smartphones are similar to the smallest detail: a choice of colors, a speaker, a tray for SIM cards, a unibody case, glossy plastic. The only significant external difference is the camera, which in the Chinese version is located in the center, while in the American prototype this element can be found in the corner of the case.





Other analogies

However, this is not all the external features of the Meizu M1 Note, reviews by observant users convince us that the device is no less similar to previous models of the Chinese manufacturer. And in fact, if we put MX3 or MX4 next to our hero, you can notice a similar form of the case, under the screen there is a special touch circle, which is the company's signature touch, the implementation of the back panel and camera, which was mentioned above.

The Meizu M1 Note smartphone is very comfortable to hold in your hand. The shape of the case is chosen successfully. The transitions between the planes are smooth. The overall impression of the appearance of the device is pleasant. It can be called spectacular. With regard to the shortcomings of Meizu M1 Note, user reviews pay attention to the tendency of the device to quickly become dirty, but its prototype ‚Äú5c‚Äù suffers from the same problem.

Technical details

As for the features of using a smartphone, they include the ability to hold to activate and deactivate it. A quick transition to the camera and the main screen is also available.





Overview Meizu M1 Note will continue to describe the display. The smartphone received a 5.5-inch screen (1920 x 1080 pixels). The aspect ratio is standard, which can be called unusual for this manufacturer. The screen is quality. It may seem that the image is drawn on top of the glass, the maximum viewing angles, excellent margin of brightness, color reproduction, is close to natural. Perhaps some users will notice a slight bias towards cold tones, but this is a common feature of Meizu screens.

The device received an eight-core 64-bit platform Mediatek MT6752 (1.7 GHz). Graphics Accelerator - Mali T720MP2. The speed of the interface is not satisfactory, everything is very fast and smooth, no hints of delay. There is 2 Gigabytes of RAM, 16 or 32 GB of flash memory is built into the phone to store data, there is no slot for the drive.

The main camera of 13 megapixels, authorship of Samsung, f / 2.2, is complemented by a flash of a pair of LEDs of various tones. Frontal - 5 MP. It is characterized by an angle of 69 degrees, the possibility of superimposing effects and improving face images in real time. The result of processing can be seen before creating a snapshot.





The device software is supplemented by the Flyme shell. The add-on can be called beautiful, neat, simple and moderately customizable. It should also be said about wireless interfaces. It supports 4G, Bluetooth and dual-band Wi-Fi. The smartphone has a single tray for a pair of SIM cards, the first slot for the GSM standard, the second is also designed for WCDMA, LTE and data transfer.

The battery capacity is 3140 mAh, according to the developers, this is enough to ensure the battery life of the smartphone for two days in conditions of active use.

We can say with confidence that the Chinese company managed to release a budget and very attractive device. The device has a high-quality display, a beautiful and simple shell, high speed. All these indicators are on a par with more expensive devices. Another feature worthy of attention in the Meizu M1 Note is the price. You can purchase a device in the range from 11,000 to 14,000 rubles, depending on the place of purchase.
