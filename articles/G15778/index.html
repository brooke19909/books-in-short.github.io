Services of the trampoline center of Kaliningrad

Amplitude (Kaliningrad - trampoline center) provides a number of services. Among them:

  • Classes accompanied by a professional trainer.
  • Activities for children on a trampoline.
  • Jumping fitness.
  • Acrobatics for beginners and professionals.

The fitness center bar for twenty people can organize a corporate party or birthday.

The cost of visiting the "Amplitude"
