Beautiful crafts from plastic cups: interesting ideas

Any options and methods of artistic mastery contribute to the comprehensive development of children, so the more diverse creative tasks you can offer your baby, the better and faster his imagination, the ability to analyze, and solve problems will develop. Crafts from plastic cups: ideas, tips

All such products can be divided into two groups:

  1. Made from one cup, maximum 2-3 pieces.
  2. Made from a large number of blanks.

The first type includes the following options:

  • animals;
  • birds;
  • flowers
  • baskets and pails;
  • bells.

These are miniature figurines that are obtained by adding parts from paper, cardboard, plasticine, plastic, fabric and other materials to the main form.



The second group consists of large crafts made of plastic cups, they can even be called interior. They are made of several tens or even hundreds of units of plastic containers. In this way, the following products are performed:

  • Christmas trees;
  • snowmen;
  • balls, including lamps.

Materials and Tools

To perform interesting crafts from plastic cups, you need the following:

  • cups themselves (white, transparent, multi-colored);
  • stapler (for connecting elements together);
  • scissors;
  • glue;
  • plasticine;
  • colored paper;
  • the cloth;
  • paints with a brush;
  • varnish;
  • other decor (beads, rhinestones, stickers, tinsel, ribbons, bows, decorative tape, napkins with thematic patterns).

All materials are available, and if you are engaged in needlework and artistic creation, most likely, you already have the necessary accessories.





Funny plastic farm

Small children's crafts made of plastic cups made in the form of figures of animals, birds or even fairy-tale characters look very unusual and original. The easiest way to make stylized animals. If the cup has a suitable color (not transparent, but, for example, yellow or white), you can only supplement it with small elements characterizing a particular animal (beak, scallop cockerel; ears, muzzle, specks of a Dalmatian). If your container is only white or transparent, it is easy to paint it in the desired shade, for example, with acrylic paints. As a final coating, transparent varnish is ideal, which fixes the paint layer and makes the appearance of the product more attractive.

Flowers and baskets

These items are carried out using scissors. The sequence of work is as follows:

  1. Cut the cup along the perimeter into strips (1-2 cm wide) or directly on the petals.
  2. Tighten them with scissors or a core so that the workpieces become curved.
  3. Connect several elements between each other (a basket and a pen) or set the resulting "flowers" on the stems and add leaves.

Products made from colored transparent cups look very impressive, although any others will do. Ready-made souvenirs can be decorated with sparkles, beads, make the middle of beads.
