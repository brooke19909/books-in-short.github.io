Hotel "Crimea" (Sevastopol): address, photo, reviews

Crimea is considered to be the day of the favorite vacation spot for Russians from the second half of the 19th century. After the peninsula returned to the Russian Federation, the number of people wishing to spend their holidays there increased sharply. That is why many are interested in information about hotels in Yalta, Sevastopol, Yevpatoriya, Sudak and other Crimean resorts. At the moment, active construction of new sanatoriums and rest houses has begun there. At the same time, tourist facilities that were built during the Soviet period continue to be popular. Among them is the hotel "Crimea" (Sevastopol). It is considered the best accommodation option among economy class hotels and has an extremely convenient location.





general information

The three-star hotel "Crimea" (Sevastopol) was commissioned in 1979 and at that time was considered one of the most equipped on the peninsula. It consists of a single 14-story building with several elevators. It offers guests single, double, triple and quadruple rooms of different sizes (from 15 to 28 sq. M) with the possibility of installing additional space (folding bed), for a total of 408.

In addition to a quadruple, each of them has a TV and a refrigerator. 2-room rooms have a telephone.

The hotel is designed for simultaneous accommodation of 408 people. At the same time, during the holiday season it is difficult to find a free place there, since low prices and a convenient location put it beyond competition among similar tourist sites.

Children are accepted from the age of five. Accommodation and meals are paid. For settlement, a birth certificate, a certificate of the epidemic environment will be required. If they came to rest without parents, then the attendant should have a power of attorney.

There are no discounts at the hotel.





Services

Meals at the hotel are organized in the air-conditioned restaurant. It is not included in the price. Restaurant prices are as follows:

  • breakfast about 150 rubles;
  • lunch - 188 rubles;
  • dinner - 166 rubles.

As in many other hotels in Crimea, guests will have to get used to the idea that hot water will be delivered to rooms according to the schedule: in the morning from 07:00 to 09:00, and in the evening from 20:00 to 22:00.

Of the additional services, guests are invited to use the first-aid post if necessary. In addition, they can rent a cell in a deposit safe or leave things in a luggage room. Guests are expected at the hairdresser and in the Internet club, where, as in the lobby, Wi-Fi internet is available.

Hotel "Crimea" (Sevastopol) has its own spacious parking lot, and you can rent a car at the hotel to make excursions to the sights of Crimea located in the vicinity of the city.





Infrastructure

Hotel "Crimea" (Sevastopol) has a developed infrastructure. On the territory of:

  • a restaurant;
  • a conference room with 90 seats for various outdoor meetings, business and scientific conferences and other similar events;
  • bar;
  • billiards;
  • night club;
  • cosmetology and massage rooms;
  • barbershop.

In addition, those who choose this hotel to stay will have access to the entire developed infrastructure of the center of Sevastopol, including beauty salons, restaurants, entertainment venues and shopping centers.

There are also many medical institutions, for example, dental clinics, located next to the Square of the Risen, therefore, in emergency situations, guests will not be left without qualified assistance.

Beach and Fun

As already mentioned, the hotel "Crimea" (Sevastopol) belongs to the category of "urban". From it to the municipal pebble free beach, located about 1 km, can be reached by trolley bus or minibus number 2 and 6. As for other entertainment, 200 meters from the hotel is one of the largest sports complexes in the city, which has a swimming pool, tennis courts and stadiums. You can do sports in the hotel itself. In particular, the gym in the hotel "Crimea" (Sevastopol) is distinguished by the availability of modern equipment for those who prefer active leisure.





Hotel "Crimea" Sevastopol: reviews

Those who have already stayed at this hotel note that in general they were satisfied with the level of service. At the same time, the reviews indicate that this hotel is suitable for those who come to Sevastopol to explore local attractions and want to use it as a starting point for excursions to other cities of the peninsula located nearby. This opinion is based on the fact that the hotel is quite far from the nearest beach and belongs to the category of ‚Äúurban‚Äù. As for the service, there are no complaints about the work of the maintenance staff in the reviews. At the same time, some tourists were dissatisfied with the food and noted the uniformity of the menu and the lack of choice. Some guests did not like the fact that Sevastopol was busy in the summer. Hotel "Crimea" (photo below) is a quiet place, in the lobby and corridors during some hours it is quite noisy. However, it would be strange to expect something else in the holiday season.

Nearby Attractions

As already mentioned, most tourists believe that the best place to stay for lovers of interesting excursions is the Crimea Hotel. Sevastopol is famous for its attractions, and some of them are within walking distance or a short drive from the hotel. In particular, not far from the "Crimea" is the Kommunarov Park. It is a reminder of the turbulent revolutionary past of Sevastopol. The famous lieutenant P. Schmidt, many famous Soviet admirals and scientists are buried there. Monuments to the crew of the Kursk, the defenders of Sevastopol, etc.

From the hotel you can easily reach the Cathedral of Christ the Savior, built in the early 20th century.





Hotel "Crimea" (Sevastopol): address

The hotel is located almost in the city center, near the Risen Square. Its address is 6th Bastionnaya St., 46. The nearest public transport stop is a 3-minute walk away.

To get to the hotel "Crimea", first of all, you need to get to the Sevastopol bus or train station. Then it will be possible to use one of the minibuses plying on the routes No. 4, 107, 84, 109, 105, 110, 112 to a stop on the Risen Square.
