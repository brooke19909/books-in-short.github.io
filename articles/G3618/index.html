Subwoofers "Pioneer" - truly high quality!



Pioneer subwoofers are separate speaker systems specifically designed to reproduce the so-called low frequencies of the sound range. This brand is far from the first year in the audio equipment market and during this time managed to win the trust of numerous buyers and a worthy place among competitors. Basically, Pioneer subwoofers reproduce frequencies in the range from 20 to 120 Hz. Recently, however, the company has begun to rapidly expand its range and deliver models with higher functional characteristics to the market . All this, of course, makes the subwoofers of this brand one of the most popular today.

The main models of subwoofers



The Pioneer speaker system includes about a hundred different devices. All of them differ from each other both in characteristics and in price. That is why every motorist will be able to quickly and easily choose for himself the most optimal option. For example, one of the most popular today is the TS-WX304T. This is the Pioneer subwoofer (1300 watts maximum power), whose frequency range is from 10 to 360 Hz. One of the most inexpensive popular models is the TS-W310 speaker system . Its maximum power is 1000 watts, and the frequency range is 20-250 Hz. And finally, the third popular and very popular model among consumers is the TS-SW2502S4 system. This is the Pioneer subwoofer (1200 watts maximum power), which has a sensitivity of 91 dB and a frequency range from 20 to 125 Hz.

Advantages of Pioneer Subwoofers



If we talk about the main advantages that speakers of this brand have, then for a start it should be noted their attractive appearance and fairly compact size. Secondly, it is necessary to emphasize the very high quality that all Pioneer subwoofers possess without exception. They give the opportunity to get a louder and more realistic bass output. In addition, the double-type centering washer, which is installed in most models, helps to increase the sensitivity of the speaker and improve the clarity of the resulting sound. As a result, distortion is reduced several times, and the bass is highly detailed. Buying Pioneer Subwoofers

Acoustic systems of this brand can now be purchased in almost all car dealerships, as well as in specialized companies and service centers. At all points of sales, the client will usually be offered a professional installation of the purchased equipment. The latter, by the way, really, is best entrusted to specialists. Although it is worth noting that, if necessary, absolutely everyone will be able to install the Pioneer subwoofers quite quickly, thanks to the detailed instructions that are always included.
