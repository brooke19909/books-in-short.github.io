Modeling mass `` Squash '': benefits for children and adults

Modern children have great potential in terms of creative implementation. For this, a wide variety of devices and materials were invented.

Plasticine, resembling soft dough, solidifying in the right form, kinetic sand that holds better than usual and is much more pleasant to the touch, ceramic clay and much more - all these are the realities of modern children, and they use all the possibilities of civilization.

"Squash" - a new word in children's crafts

Recently, in the domestic markets specializing in children's leisure, a product such as Squash mass has appeared. This is a special consistency that combines the capabilities of plasticine and sand.

The mass for sculpting "Squash" can stretch and crumble. Amazing abilities, attractive appearance and nice structure - for this mass has already managed to fall in love with children.





Indeed, the mass is soft, and therefore easy to deform, which is important for the smallest children who still do not have the strength to knead ordinary plasticine. And assistive devices facilitate the task, and contribute to a more diverse creative process.

Squash Set

Squash molding mass for children usually comes in a standard set, which includes:

  • 2 containers with weight;
  • large roller (has 2 rollers, five patterns each);
  • molds;
  • instruction for use.




This mass is similar to ordinary plasticine, you can use it for modeling. The difference is that it can change its structure, being, as necessary, either in solid or in liquid state.

Remove the mass from the packaging and begin to knead in your hand for a while, the clay will immediately become soft and supple. Then you can begin to stretch it, it is very elastic.

To fully unlock the potential of the game mass and get the most out of it, you can fantasize and experiment with your child.

Purpose of the squash mass

Mass for sculpting "Squash" allows you to build different objects (by the way, you can use the molds included in the kit), and show tricks, passing it through your fingers (it will crumble like sand).



This amazing substance is suitable for games, perfectly develops fine motor skills, and also develops spatial imagination.
