Conflict is a misunderstanding of the parties, which often develops into a military conflict. Conflicts in the world and in Russia

When it comes to power and money, people often become unprincipled, cruel and aggressive. They try to tear off a piece of the pie for themselves more, therefore they often sacrifice principles and friendship. There are scandals, conflicts, sometimes wars. If at the same time representatives of one estate, community or state take up arms, this is a real tragedy.

Concept and its meaning

Conflict is a misunderstanding, a clash of interests and views, which often develops into conflict. People of the same nationality, religion, upbringing take up arms. They live in a common country, but in different areas, provinces, districts. Conflicts are a cyclical phenomenon. They have always been and will die only with the death of humanity. European feudal lords constantly fought for a place under the sun in the dark Middle Ages. Similar wars were fought between the emirs in Syria, the dukes of the Holy Roman Empire - in Germany, the noble families of Tyra and Minamoto - in Japan, the princes of the same blood - in England.



Conflicts are always a tragedy. Often on the battlefield, a person could recognize in the ranks of the enemy his father, neighbor, and friend. In each country where similar conflicts occurred, they played a different role. But the result has always remained deplorable. Even if it was possible to conquer the land, plant noble rulers on the throne and achieve justice, the goal did not justify the loss. After all, close people died, blood brothers.

Conflicts in Russia

In our time, there are also strife - these are disagreements between political parties, leaders, public figures, regions in the state. If a military conflict arises between them, it is called a civil war, separatism or rebellion. Similar conflicts in our country have been since the times of Kievan Rus. Prince Igor, for example, for many years tried to stifle the uprisings of the drevlyans, who were not satisfied with the policy of the ruler.



Many strife arose after the death of the great Mstislav, the eldest son of Vladimir Monomakh. Then the state created by the enormous efforts of the ancestors literally fell apart. People living in neighboring cities killed each other, robbed villages, set fire to houses. Conflicts are always a tragedy. But if at the time of other princes - Rurik, Oleg, Igor, Vladimir, Yaroslav the Wise - they were more moderate, then after the death of Mstislav really troublesome times came. Russia was torn to pieces by different ethnic groups.

Conclusion

Conflict is the beginning of the end. If the government lacks a strong, smart and self-confident ruler capable of crushing the rebellion and pacifying the dissatisfied, the state disintegrates and ceases to exist. What happened later with Kievan Rus.
