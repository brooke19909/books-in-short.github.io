Cologne hotels list: description and reviews of tourists

Cologne is located in Germany and is one of the most important cities of the country from a historical point of view. It is also incredibly popular among tourists who visit it throughout the year. Visitors can choose a suitable hotel in Cologne, based on location and cost of living.

List of the most popular hotels

The hotel business is very developed in German cities, and Cologne is no exception. It has more than 1000 hotels that are located throughout the territory and have different pricing policies.

Each tourist will always find a suitable option. It is worth paying attention to:

  1. Hotel Lyskirchen.
  2. Ameron Hotel Regent.
  3. Hotel Santo.
  4. Airport Business Hotel.
  5. Hotel Black.
  6. Hotel Leonardo.

This list is based on reviews from guests of the city and includes the best hotel in Cologne for families with children, as well as an option located near the airport.

The hotel provides tourists with breakfast, which is included in the room rate. Most of all, guests are pleased with the availability of free high-speed Internet.

The interior of Hotel Lyskirchen 4 * has a pleasant pastime and does not bother for a few days. All of Cologne's air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a spacious bathroom and a TV. Large windows offer wonderful views of the promenade. Around the complex there is a huge number of attractions that can be visited both independently and as part of excursions.

Airport Business Hotel

For people who travel frequently, the Hotel Cologne hospitably opens its doors, located in the immediate vicinity of the airport. The three-star complex is equipped with everything necessary for rest before the flight. Transport in this area runs quite often, and taxi drivers will quickly deliver the traveler to their destination.

The spacious, clean rooms have a large bed, private bathroom, air conditioning, and free internet. A buffet breakfast is included each morning at the Cologne Airport hotel, which is included in the price. On the territory of Airport Business Hotel Koeln 3 * there is a sauna, a meat restaurant offering a huge variety of steaks, and a cozy bar. All hotel services are aimed at relaxing the tourist before waiting for his plane and the next trip. Cultural and entertainment venues are densely located around the territory - a golf club, a theater, a fruit museum and a shopping market.

The hotel staff is friendly and welcoming with all tourists. The clerk will help with check-in, answer all questions and arrange transport to anywhere in Cologne. Reviews of tourists show that the rooms are very clean, the maids perform their work efficiently. Hotel black

The incredibly colorful hotel of Cologne attracts the attention of a huge number of tourists from all over the world. Interior design in black is a unique feature of this complex. All rooms, furniture, walls, ceiling and decor elements are made only in black and white and give coolness to the room, which is fully offset by the sincere friendliness of the staff, providing guests with a comfortable stay at Hotel Black 4 *.

The hotel has 4 stars and is located in the very center of the city. Within walking distance there is a stadium, theater, cathedral, museums and a large number of shops for a successful shopping. Hotel Black 4 * has a small number of rooms that are in great demand; it is sometimes difficult to book free. Hotel leonardo

This is another hotel option at Cologne Airport. It is in close proximity to it, as well as from the train station. Spacious, comfortable and clean rooms provide guests with a good rest and a great pastime. The complex has an indoor pool for residents and a restaurant with excellent cuisine. Hotel staff can supervise children for an extra charge and provide a cot upon request. The cost of rooms varies from 6 to 10 thousand rubles a day with a buffet for breakfast.

More than a hundred spacious rooms, made in different styles, are designed to relax on their own or with family. The hotel's services include the provision of executive car rentals for independent travel around the city and exploring the sights.

Conclusion

A large number of Cologne hotels make it easy to make a choice and find an option that fits all parameters. The complexes are located in the center and near the airport. The list of services in Cologne hotels is incredibly large and will satisfy the needs of any tourist.
