Hospital 20 at the "Babushkinskaya": how to get there? Reviews

If it happens that you have to seek medical help from specialists, we recommend that you familiarize yourself with the clinic in more detail before you pay a visit. Hospital 20 at Babushkinskaya has a long history and good patient reviews. Let us consider in more detail this medical institution. In what areas does it work?

General information





The State Clinical Hospital named after Eramishantsev (20 hospital on "Babushkinskaya") is located in the north-east of the capital, it is the largest healthcare institution. Highly qualified specialists provide the population with professional services through advanced technologies, modern techniques, the latest equipment. Annually, 66 thousand patients receive inpatient care here, over 160 thousand outpatient treatment, 800 in a day hospital. Hospital beds 1040. The hospital fund is represented by the following profiles: cardiological (myocardial infarction), therapeutic, nephrological, endocrinological, neurological (ONMK ), surgical (purulent, pure), neurosurgery, vascular surgery, urology, traumatology, psychiatry, obstetrics and gynecology.

Hospital History

The hospital at "Babushkinskaya" has a long history, throughout which the establishment of a medical institution, the growth of staff professionalism took place. The famous doctors who worked within the walls of the hospital made many feats and scientific discoveries, which made an indisputable contribution to the development of not only Moscow, but also Russian medicine. Over the entire period of operation, Hospital 20 at Babushkinskaya has become a symbol of the high professionalism of physicians, assistance to all those in need, a scientific and competent approach to healing.

For more than fifty years, hospital staff have been providing medical care not only to residents of the capital, but also visitors, both in urgent cases and as planned. In addition, the staff carries out the widest educational and preventive work among the population. The first admission of patients the 20th hospital began back in 1964. In those days, the institution was run by the city of Babushkina. Only in 1968 the institution was transformed into City Clinical Hospital No. 20 in Moscow. The reason was that the borders of the Moscow region changed, the city of Babushkin entered the territorial composition of the capital and became one of its areas.

The high professionalism of the staff is confirmed by the fact that it was here in 1990 that the USSR‚Äôs first orthopedic liver transplant operation was performed.

In 2014, the Department of Health renamed the hospital into the State Clinical Hospital named after Eramishantsev.

Branches

20 hospital on the "Babushkinskaya" operates in the most popular areas. It has many departments and qualified specialists who will help to cope with many problems related to your health.

The nephrology department is located in building No. 4, on the fifth floor. Today there are 60 beds. In the day hospital - 10 beds. The department staff advises patients with kidney problems and sends them to the hospital for treatment. Patients undergoing hemodialysis are treated. Vascular surgeons, urologists, surgeons are able to perform a biopsy, implant a catheter, provide vascular access during the hemodialysis procedure.

The surgical department serves many patients with problems in this area. Surgery is divided here into four departments, each of which works in a specific area. The first and second department deals with:

  • acute abdominal pathologies that require immediate intervention;
  • diffuse, nodular, mixed types of thyroid goiter;
  • varicose veins and its complications on the legs;
  • benign neoplasms;
  • ulcerative pathologies;
  • gallstone disease.

The third department is abdominal surgery.

The fourth branch - purulent surgery, diabetic foot.

The Urology Department is the only one in the North-West Administrative District of Moscow specializing in this profile. It is designed for 60 beds, but in fact it serves a lot more patients. The department has all the necessary equipment for conducting complex operations of the urological direction.

The key specialization of the trauma unit is gerontological traumatology. When solving problems, minimally invasive techniques are used. Joint arthroplasty is practiced at a professional level, while implants of both Russian and foreign manufacturers are used. Conservative and operational techniques are used in the treatment of degenerative-dystrophic diseases.

The neurological department deals with problems of circulatory disorders of the brain. This is one of the departments dealing with stroke patients. Reconstructive treatment, early neurorehabilitation - his areas of activity. Modern medicines, the latest examination methods, ultrasound dopplerography of arteries are used.

The endocrinology department treats patients with endocrine thyroid pathologies, as well as patients with diabetes mellitus. Daily glucose monitoring is performed. The latest medical equipment allows for full-fledged research. Collaboration with surgery makes it possible to perform operations that preserve organs.

Patients who have had a myocardial infarction, or those who are diagnosed with heart rhythm disorders, go to the cardiology department. Modern equipment allows you to conduct the necessary research and monitoring of cardiac activity. The hospital has 50 beds, full-time - 12.

In the department of psychiatry you can get psychiatric help without resorting to the services of a psychiatric hospital. The department operates in open door mode, like a sanatorium. Help can be given to people who are tired of life, depressed, and even those who are suicidal. They refuse to admit only to people with acute psychosis, alcohol and drug addiction, with severe neurological and somatic diseases.

The hospital also has a consultative and diagnostic department, portal hypertension, ultrasound, X-ray, radioisotope research methods, CDC, blood transfusion, maternity ward.

Maternity ward





Hospital 20 at Babushkinskaya has its own maternity ward with high-tech equipment. Professional medical staff provides expectant mothers with qualified assistance. At the first threats, specialists will prevent any danger to the life of the mother and child. Throughout pregnancy, women are under strict control. The individual examination of the patient includes a complete examination, ultrasound, analysis, observation of narrow specialists. Antenatal training courses help future mothers to overcome fears, acquire skills and generic skills. A program such as family delivery has also been introduced in the hospital. This technique has become widely popular among couples, where dads are also preparing for such a significant event in the family as childbirth.
