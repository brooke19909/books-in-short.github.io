Resort-Darasun - a great place for relaxation and wellness

Resort-Darasun is located in the Karymsky district of the Transbaikal Territory. He received his status as a resort village of urban type in 1957. This settlement gained national fame thanks to special mineral springs rich in hydrocarbonate, calcium, and magnesium.



Description of the village

It got its name from the Darasunka River, on the coast of which it is located. Translated from Buryat into Russian, this word means "red water", and this is completely true. The fact is that when standing for a long time, the water acquires the corresponding color due to the considerable iron content.

Darasun resort is located in the low-mountain zone, at an altitude of 750 meters above sea level, 132 kilometers from Chita. The mountains surrounding it are covered with forests (birch, pine and larch).

The local climate is sharply continental. On average in summer, the air temperature is +15 ¬∞ C. Winter is long, there is little snow, but it can be very cold. During this period, the temperature drops to -22 ¬∞ C.



Healing water

Mineral water, which is famous for the Resort-Darasun, in taste and content resemble the no less famous "Narzan", the source of which is located in Kislovodsk. However, its main difference is that it has fewer minerals and no sulfates at all. Local water is used in the treatment of many diseases. They can list the following:

  • Diseases of the female reproductive system.
  • Diseases of the genitourinary system.
  • Violation of the gastrointestinal tract.
  • Diseases of the circulatory system.
  • Problems with the musculoskeletal system (osteochondrosis, arthrosis).

Sanatoriums

In the village of Resort-Darasun there are two sanatoriums. One of them is the Darasun medical rehabilitation center, which is considered to be the oldest health resort in the Trans-Baikal Territory.

The second is the military sanatorium "Darasunsky". Despite the name, anyone who wishes can rest in it. The rehabilitation center and the sanatorium military (Resort-Darasun), in addition to excellent treatment with mineral waters, provide other services. People can just come here on vacation, enjoy the magnificent landscapes, clean air and a relaxed atmosphere.



Treatment and living conditions

Sanatoria Resort Darasuna offer vacationers the following conditions:

  • For treatment, drinking and therapeutic mineral water is used.
  • The main methods of healing include bathing with coniferous and carbonic waters, sea salt, showers, hydromassage.
  • The resort offers beauty treatments.
  • There is also a swimming pool, steam room and sauna.
  • For leisure, they offer games in billiards, a non-alcoholic bar, a cinema hall, a concert hall, and fitness equipment.
  • Types of outdoor activities: walks in forest areas, a tennis court, volleyball courts, as well as hockey.
  • For regular customers there is a system of discounts and bonuses.
  • You can relax with children for whom special conditions are provided.
  • In addition to standard single and double rooms, there are also deluxe rooms.
  • And most importantly - the prices for rest in sanatoriums are acceptable (from 1700 rubles).

Darasun military sanatorium

One of the sanatoriums of the Resort-Darasun, bearing the rank of military, was founded in the thirties of the last century and is located in the southwestern part of the mountains, which are covered with forests. It offers all the possible conditions for effective treatment and relaxation. Pleased in this area and the weather.

Resort-Darasun has excellent climatic conditions, which allows to achieve the most positive mood of vacationers. This resort specializes in diseases of the circulatory and endocrine system, as well as the gastrointestinal tract. The main methods of treatment include climatotherapy, mineral baths, hydro and physiotherapy, specialized nutrition and drinking. You can relax here with children from the age of four. For the smallest in the military sanatorium there is a games room.

At leisure, vacationers can read in the library, dance, play billiards, volleyball, tennis, steam in the sauna and Russian bath, swim in the pool. In the warmer months, you can take walks to local attractions, take part in sightseeing trips, and go ice-skating and skiing in winter (there is an ice rink and a ski base in the sanatorium for this).



Medical Rehabilitation Center "Darasun"

The medical rehabilitation center "Darasun" is one of the most attractive resorts in Transbaikalia. She attracts visitors not only with her services for treating diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, respiratory organs, genitourinary, nervous system, but also with beautiful clean mountain air, coniferous forests and flower meadows, as well as mineral water, which not only restores health, but also preserves health youth. That is why many seek to visit the Resort-Darasun (Transbaikalia).

The center conducts medical procedures only under the supervision of highly qualified health professionals and offers not only traditional methods of recovery, but also using modern technologies. In addition to treatment, there are also cosmetic services. And for regular customers the rehabilitation center "Darasun" offers a system of bonuses and seasonal discounts. This significantly increases the flow of vacationers.
