Arkaim is a place of power with magical appeal. In the 3-2 millennium BC, there was a fortified settlement on this territory. It belonged to the "Land of Cities." Arkaim on the map of Russia is located eight kilometers north of the village of Amursky (Bredinsky district of the Chelyabinsk region). It is located on a high cape, which was formed as a result of the confluence of the rivers Utyaganka and Big Karakaganki. This settlement is a famous historical, archaeological and natural landscape reserve. Ancient defensive structures and synchronous burial grounds are very well preserved on the territory of the considered monument. The historical landscape is characterized by maximum integrity.



How did the discovery of the amazing monument

Ancient Arkaim was randomly discovered in the summer of 1987 by members of the Ural-Kazakhstan archaeological expedition. The survey of the area began because of the need to build the Bolshe-Karagan reservoir, which would provide for the needs of the region's agricultural enterprises. At that time, they already adhered to the rules on compulsory archaeological exploration of the territories on which it was supposed to deploy large-scale construction. At first, experts did not regard the study of this area as promising. It was believed that it would be necessary to examine the steppe zone, which was not very interesting from the point of view of archaeological discoveries. However, later findings have amazed many.

Ongoing research

Large-scale excavations have been ongoing since 1991. They are led by G. B. Zdanovich. At the same time, natural science methods are actively used, including radiocarbon dating, geological and mineralogical, paleo-soil and geological-morphological studies. During the excavations, approximately half of the settlement (eight thousand square meters of territory) was discovered. Thus, it was possible to completely restore the original layout of the monument. A method of reconstruction, new to Trans-Urals, was also applied here. L. L. Gurevich painted how the settlement might have looked in ancient times.



What did you manage to find on the territory of an ancient monument called Arkaim? The place of power revealed some secrets. The remains of chariots, tools of saddlers and potters, harnesses, arrowheads, and also forms in which metal products were cast were found. Of particular interest are water supply and sewage systems. One of the most important finds is the remains of people and animals.

Detailed description of the complex

Arkaim - a place of power, including a fortified city, two necropolises, the remains of pastures and corrals. The village was built according to the radial scheme. Its diameter is about one hundred and seventy meters. There are two walls to which the rooms are attached in the form of a circular sector. In fact, the city of Arkaim was a fortress, on the territory of which there were two "apartment buildings". People lived and worked there. Cattle grazed outside the settlement, including in specially organized pens.

The walls of the dwellings and fortresses were erected from clay-clogged logs and unbaked bricks. There were premises for both personal and public use, as well as workshops. Judging by the found artifacts, metallurgical production was organized in some buildings.

In the center of Arkaim was a square. The city had a storm sewage system. Water was diverted outside the settlement.

Residents of the village were representatives of the Caucasian race. This was established thanks to the skulls found in the burial grounds. Arkaim (on the map of Russia it can be seen on the territory of Bashkiria) was destroyed by fire. The cause of the spread of fire was never determined. There are three versions: the city was set on fire either by enemies, or by local residents themselves, or a fire started by accident.

Scientific direction

Currently, Arkaim is the largest scientific center in the country. Existing employees and invited experts constantly conduct comprehensive research in the humanitarian and natural sciences. In the center of the study, of course, are the monuments of Sintashta culture - ‚ÄúCountry of Cities‚Äù. This is the main secret of Arkaim. The process is carried out with the involvement of a wide range of different techniques - natural science and archaeological, as well as remote (including decryption of images, geophysics).

In 1992, a year after the creation of the reserve, a team of natural scientists and archaeologists developed a program according to which the territory of Arkaim is considered as a testing ground for studying the processes of interaction between nature and man in perspective, retrospective, and also at the present stage. As part of this program, archaeological monuments were studied not only in the Arkaim valley, but also in its environs, as well as local biological diversity and soil composition. Of particular interest is information on the geological structure of the underlying rocks and their influence on the nature of farming and the choice of housing for the ancient inhabitants of the settlement in question.

Thanks to active museum activities, Arkaim became a platform for organizing a variety of experiments and reconstructions. Among them are areas such as ancient pottery, weaving and metallurgy, as well as fortification and construction of structures of various kinds.

The aim of scientific research conducted in Arkaim is to establish a dialogue of cultures, actualization of antiquity for the present, the formation of an ecological worldview of a new type through studies of cultural and natural spheres in their historical relationship.
