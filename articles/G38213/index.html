Metro station "Strogino". District Strogino

The metro station "Strogino" is located on the Arbat-Pokrovskaya line. The opening took place in 2008. The article describes the history of the Moscow metro station, as well as information about ground facilities located next to this metro.





Construction

For twelve years the metro station "Strogino" was designated on the diagram as being under construction. The point, of course, is not that the work process dragged on for such a long time. In the early nineties, the country was gripped by an unprecedented economic crisis, as a result of which the further construction of new metro stations in the capital became impossible.

The metro station "Strogino", according to the original plan, was to be part of the Filevskaya line, indicated in blue on the diagram. But in 1993, the construction was postponed indefinitely. When, after more than a decade, they returned to the project, Moscow looked completely different in the north-west . Metro "Strogino", "Volokolamskaya", "Myakinino" decided to include in the blue line, thereby providing underground transport for residents of new areas. However, the houses located in the vicinity of the station discussed in this article were built before the new millennium. The area in which the Strogino metro station is located will be described later.

The opening took place on New Year's Eve. For two years, the Strogino metro station was the terminal on the Arbatsko-Pokrovskaya line. In 2008, the construction of the next station of the blue branch, Myakinino, was completed.





Krylatskoye - Strogino

Travel time from one station to another in the Moscow Metro is an average of three minutes. But there is an exception. The train from Strogino to Krylatskaya travels for almost eight minutes. Passengers using the Arbat-Pokrovskaya line are aware of this feature. But why is there such a great distance between these stations? Few people think about this.

The fact is that between the "Strogino" and the "Krylatskoye" is located the "Trinity-Lykovo". This is a technical station that is currently used only by subway employees. It is also called "promising." That is, it is possible that someday it will be open.

Near the station "Trinity-Lykovo" is the village of the same name. Subway construction can only be resumed if there is a housing estate that can provide the subway with the required number of passengers. But representatives of the metropolitan authorities recently compiled a list of villages to be demolished. Trinity-Lukovo was not included in this list. We will return to the metro station "Strogino". What streets do you leave from this station?





Area

In historical documents of the sixteenth century, the first mention of the village of Strogino was discovered. But then this settlement was called differently: Ostrog. The village received its modern name in the 19th century, in the second half of the 20th century it became part of Moscow. In the mid-seventies, the construction of multi-storey buildings began here. Exits from the metro are carried out on Stroginsky Boulevard. Other streets in the area:

  • Isakovsky Street.
  • Street Marshal Katukov.
  • Street of Twardo.
  • Tallinn Street.
  • Kulakova street.

Ground transportation

Bus and tram stops are located near the metro station "Strogino". How to get from this station to Voikovskaya, located in the north of Moscow? This metro station runs tram number 30. By bus you can get to the metro station Mitino, Pyatnitskoye Shosse, Volokolamskaya.





Restaurants and shops

Near the metro station "Strogino" there are several large shopping centers and catering facilities. A few meters from one of the exits is "Stroginskaya harbor". This complex includes not only a restaurant, but also a sauna. The following cafes and restaurants are also located near the metro: "WOK Sushi", "Pizza Factory", "Picturesque Bay", "Taste Line", "Chateau Myakinino", "Vgosti".

Exit from the western lobby leads to the shopping centers "Daria", "Lukomorye." Shopping center "Daria" was opened in 2009. Located at: Stroginsky Boulevard, Building 1. The three-story building has many shops, organizations that provide services to the population, on the top floor there are several catering facilities. Lukomorye is also one of the largest complexes whose branches are located in Moscow near almost every metro station. There is a digital equipment store, a jewelry store, a pharmacy, and several cafes.

In order to be at the Yantar stadium, you should go from the eastern entrance hall of the Strogino metro station to Marshal Katukov street.
