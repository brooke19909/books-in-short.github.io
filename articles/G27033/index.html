Rest in the winter

Due to the peculiar location of the mountains, the ski season is long. It begins in December and ends in late May. In winter, thirty kilometers of slopes await guests at the Gorki Gorod complex. Trails (reviews - direct confirmation of this) have various levels of difficulty, among them there are even trails illuminated for evening skiing, and with artificial turf.



In winter, there are eleven chairlift and gondola cable cars. For tourists at the resort there are ski rental shops, instructor schools, as well as a kids club. Caring for the safety of guests, the resort administration made sure that only highly qualified specialists work in the rescue groups.

Summer rest

The complex is of interest not only in winter. In the warm season, guests can enjoy all kinds of activities to make it interesting to stay in such a wonderful place as Gorki Gorod (Sochi). Active tourists have a unique opportunity to go on foot along tourist routes of various levels of difficulty. Guests of the resort can watch alpine meadows, go to the Bear Falls, visit the relict forest and the Polikara Falls.

In addition, the whole family can go to the rope park. There are not only simple trails, but also extreme routes, everyone will find interesting entertainment for themselves. And for the kids developed their own routes that do not require special training.

Experienced instructors work in the cable park, who will certainly teach how to use the equipment properly. For the most courageous and experienced guests, the Mountain Panorama and Sky Bike routes will be a real test. Their passage will give unforgettable impressions and emotions.



Among the active entertainments are ATV excursions, during which tourists are invited to visit the most beautiful places in Sochi Park.
