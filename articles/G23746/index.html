"Pecherka" (yarn): description, reviews, prices

Despite the abundance of finished knitwear for every taste and budget, interest in knitting does not wane. Such a love for knitting needles and a hook is very simply explained: clothes, accessories, home-made items of interior design are always favorite and precious things. And it helps to create them "Pekhorka" - domestic yarn.





History of creation

A textile factory, which established a line for the production of knitting threads, was established in pre-revolutionary times, back in 1905. The location of the enterprise and gave the sonorous name "Pechorka". Yarn is named after the river on the banks of which the factory is located.

Her creator fathers were the Korzinny brothers, they founded a business that has successfully existed to this day. A rich assortment, high quality products and their availability - these are the main criteria by which the enterprise works today, and Pekhorka yarn (reviews, a high level of sales are confirmation of this) - really good raw materials loved by many needlewomen.

Chic choice

Is it possible to imagine wet dank autumn without a cozy cardigan, and a cold winter without a warm scarf and mittens? Of course not! The requirements for these products are the most stringent, because they must be warm, comfortable, and durability plays a role.

"Pehorka" - yarn that will satisfy the most fastidious and demanding customer. Finding the right composition, color and texture is not difficult.





Attention needlewomen presented samples from one hundred percent natural raw materials, as well as with various synthetic additives.

Cotton, sheep, merino, camel hair, alpaca, acrylic, mixed compounds - all these species, as well as many others (and there are more than 100) are created with love and care. Threads without knots and irregularities are very easy to use in knitting by hand and on special knitting machines.

Color spectrum

It is striking how huge a number of flowers in the assortment the Pecherka can please. The yarn of this company is monophonic and melange. At the same time, in each series there are up to several tens of various shades.

With the help of melange yarn, knitwear is obtained with a pattern. It can be clear contrasting stripes, a colorful multi-color canvas or a smooth transition from one tone to another. This effect is achieved due to sectional dyeing of the thread in a strictly specified order.

Monochrome dyeing is done using high-quality and persistent dyes. Knits are absolutely safe if their base is Pekhorka yarn. Reviews about it are very good, it is used for knitting adult and children's clothes, a variety of home textiles (rugs, toys, tablecloths, napkins).

Summer

Most often, knitwear is associated with the cold season. But this is a serious misconception! Pekhorsky factory offers needlewomen a large selection of yarn to create a summer wardrobe. It can be clothes both adult and children's. "Pehorka" is a universal yarn, and its use as a basis for children's costumes, sweaters, dresses and demi-season hats or coats will be an excellent solution.

The summer assortment is rich in natural yarn. This is primarily mercerized, gas-filled and plain cotton, linen and rayon. Clothing made of such material is light, breathable and hygroscopic, and these are exactly the qualities that things should have in hot weather.

All the best for children

Every mother wants the best for her baby. And who of the mothers and loving grandmothers did not knit cute booties, lace hats and cozy suits? And although the Pekhorka yarn for children is a novelty, such a line appeared in the assortment relatively recently, it quickly won its customer.





The delicate texture of the thread, ease of knitting, a variety of colors, naturalness - these are the qualities that the children's assortment of yarn for knitting boasts.

In addition, the positive characteristics of the threads are manifested in finished products. Children's things are very dirty, they must be washed regularly. Penny ruble saves

Pehorka is high quality yarn, but at the same time it has a very affordable price. In many ways, it depends on the composition. So, acrylic threads in skeins weighing 50 g are within 100 rubles per pack of 5 skeins. Most often, manufacturers use just such a weight. But yarn with a special weave or the addition of decorative elements is sold in skeins weighing 100 g each. Sophisticated production technology, as well as double the weight, make it more expensive (300-500 rubles per package).

The record holder for prices are natural wool threads. The more its composition and exotic origin of raw materials (alpaca, llama, merino, camel hair), the higher the cost of such yarn. On average, budget types of threads range from 500 rubles for 5 skeins weighing 100 g each.
