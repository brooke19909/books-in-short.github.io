Meet: five-star hotel Aska Lara Resort & Spa (ex. Aska Lara Deluxe)

The hotel is a 20-minute drive from Antalya Airport. This is a completely new five-star hotel, which received its first visitors this year. The start was successful. In a single complex there are ten swimming pools (in two of them the water is heated), four restaurants with bookings by appointment (Turkish, Oriental, Italian cuisine), four fast food restaurants (buffet), spa, six bars, a mini club, children's and sports grounds.

Features

Built in the relatively calm "family" resort center of Lara, the most popular in the Mediterranean region of Antalya, the Aska Lara Deluxe 5 * hotel (now called differently) meets the highest five-star hotel business standards. Zoning

The hotel project involves the zoning of a rather impressive boarding house (62 thousand m 2 ) into two differently functional parts.





In the first, relatively small, is the main stylish, modern seven-story hotel building. It has a horseshoe-shaped architecture, i.e. the project involves two wings. Communication between the floors is carried out by seven elevators, four of which are panoramic. Each of the elevators has a capacity of up to thirteen people. The building itself is spacious and bright, in its public area there are restaurants, bars, spas, a well-equipped indoor heated pool, a specialized modern fitness room. Here, in a single complex, there are massage rooms, a fully functional Turkish bath - a hammam, a Russian bath, a sauna. Table tennis tables are equipped. Near the hotel there are paved terraces for walking, originally decorated by landscape designers.

The hotel is served by a well-coordinated team of professionals. According to reviews, most of them understand the Russian language. The staff of guest relations work well. Lara (hotel, Antalya) is serviced by employees around the clock, many of them are Russian-speaking. Attentive attitude towards hotel guests: all issues are immediately resolved. Reviews indicate that at the request of visitors, towels are replaced in a matter of minutes. Bed linens are always new and perfectly clean.

Water park area

The second zone of the Aska Lara Deluxe 5 * hotel is much larger in area, it is designed for a more dynamic, active vacation. It has a large, fully-featured Wet'n Wild water park. In fact, it can be called "three in one", because it combines three arrays. The first is equipped with high slides with a depth of about 135 cm, designed for adults and adolescents. The second - for less extreme adults and children of primary school age with slides 80 cm deep. The third array of small slides, up to 35 cm deep, is suitable for the smallest children and for people of age.





Also on the second platform of the Aska Lara Deluxe 5 * hotel there is a theme park with attractions, restaurants. All these objects smoothly pass into a fairly large hotel beach (the distance from it to the hotel is 350 meters).

Physiotherapy

Hotel guests, especially middle-aged and older ones, regularly visit the hotel spa. Here, visitors significantly restore their strength thanks to the warmth of the Turkish bath (hammam), baths with rose petals, therapeutic massage. With the help of steam, the airways are warmed up, muscles are relaxed, and diseases of the cardiovascular and respiratory systems of the body are prevented. In fact, the Turkish bath is a slightly modified ancient Roman one. Vacationers unanimously reiterate: "She must visit!"





Clients of the spa have the opportunity to choose the peeling appropriate to their tastes: from algae, fruit, coffee, saline, etc. By means of peeling, hotel cosmetologists achieve high-quality cleaning of skin pores, removal of dead cells, and impurities. Diving

Many vacationers at the hotel have a happy opportunity (of course, at an amateur level) to join in an exciting lesson - diving. Scuba diving, observing marine photographs and photographing them, searching at the bottom of archaeological finds - all this attracts people. It is easy for hotel guests to choose their favorite diving courses from the many offers. Among the courses popular in Antalya, there are oriented both for beginners (general rules for diving, breathing features, standard appropriate actions in various situations), and for people with diving experience (deep sea dives, boat dives , underwater shooting).

The Aska Lara Deluxe 5 * hotel has a creative team of children's animators. Young girls and guys are able to communicate with small hotel customers, they know how to interest them.
