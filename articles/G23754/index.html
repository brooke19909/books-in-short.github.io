Air washers Boneco: reviews, instructions. Which air washer is better - Electrolux or Boneco?

The presence of a household air washer will ensure comfortable microclimate parameters, and at the same time perform cleaning with saturated moisture vapor. Compared to conventional humidifiers, sinks place particular emphasis on maintaining a healthy indoor hygiene background. This is achieved by various means, but at least allergens and polluting particles leave even low-cost models of the device in their filter. In turn, Boneco air washers have gone far in development from a basic set of functions. Swiss developers are actively using the latest technologies of filtration systems, not forgetting also about modern requests in terms of ergonomics.





Features of Boneco sinks

The concept of a small device that performs certain functions of regulating the microclimate has long been established, defining the main technical, structural and operational features. However, with the example of the Boneco product in question, one can trace the tendency to change this philosophy - at least in several aspects. For example, the company has practically abandoned the use of replaceable filters and generally seeks to get rid of consumables. The function of the active cleaning element is performed by a proprietary system with "honeycomb" absorbent surfaces on plastic discs. During the operation of the Boneco air washer, the filter rotates due to the drum design, immersing part of the wall in water. As torsion stains, other elements are washed away with water. In parallel with this process, a kind of restoration of the filter walls occurs due to the influence of clean air currents. Some modifications provide a water disinfection system. For this, the ISS antibacterial system is used, which is also based on an ionizing silver rod.





Reviews about the model AOS 2055

The device attracts many with its design, pleasant construction and practical controls. As for working qualities, the majority of users value the humidification function highly - dust and other foreign particles are quickly eliminated. The design allows you to place a large tank, which makes it possible to use the device for a long time, actively supporting the function of cleaning the air. Separately, the additional functionality that this Boneco air washer provides is also noted. Reviews emphasize the presence of an ionizing effect and aromatization. In terms of model weaknesses, there are comments on sound insulation. At short intervals of 10-15 minutes, the device gurgles.





2055D Model Reviews

By the way, on the control panel there are indicators of two main parameters - humidity and cleaning. This allows the owner to monitor the current air condition without special measuring devices. Instructions for washing

The device should be placed on a flat, dry and stable surface in a horizontal position. It should be freely accessible for maintenance. To achieve optimum performance, it is advisable not to have heating equipment nearby - it negatively affects both the work process itself and the device itself. Immediately before starting work, a water tank is installed on the pallet, which complements the Boneco air washer. The manual notes that only clean and cold water should be used to fill the tank. The use of mineral water, for example, can lead to an increase in pressure in the tank and damage to the apparatus. Also, additives should not be added to water, even if they are aimed at improving the cleaning effect. As the water flows, the device will signal the level in the tank through the indicator, but complete emptying will lead to automatic shutdown.

Instructions for maintenance





Although the washers of this brand do not require filter updates, this does not mean that the user completely gets rid of maintenance activities. Regular cleaning of the components and surfaces of the device is the key to high-quality and durable operation. For care it is necessary to disassemble the device, having previously disconnected it from the network. The disassembly process itself is the removal of the lid, the pan with the tank and providing access to the fan. After that, all the main surfaces that need to be cleaned will open. For this procedure, it is recommended to use wet wipes, but without potent chemicals. Regarding frequency, Boneco air washers are serviced on average once a month. Most attention should be paid to the water tank and the pallet. With intensive use, they should be cleaned twice a month. The manufacturer recommends cleaning the upper part of the case with a fan annually, and the moisturizing discs are serviced monthly.

Which air washer is better - Electrolux or Boneco?

The Electrolux company in the market directly competes with Boneco in the segment of washers, although unlike a Swiss company it does not have a narrow specialization in this profile. As a result, we can immediately emphasize the technological superiority of the products in question. The point here is not only the lack of replaceable consumables that are still used in Electrolux, but also as a moisturizer with the aroma effect. Nevertheless, the Boneco air washers, with all the advantages of ergonomics, are not so perfect in monitoring and control configurations. Nevertheless, this part is universal and elevated to a high level in the entire line of Electrolux household appliances for various purposes and sinks are no exception. It is enough to note the abundance of automatic systems with elements of intelligent control.





Conclusion

In search of multifunctional climate control, of course, a responsible housewife will think about buying a humidifier or a washer. In addition, today there is the opportunity to purchase a combined apparatus, which allows you to save. But is the availability of this technique justified in principle? As practice shows, in an urban environment , the Boneco humidifier sink-air radically changes the microclimatic background. Even if there is no need to adjust the air characteristics for comfort, getting rid of dust and the disinfection function will certainly not be superfluous. In addition, the manufacturer Boneco offers the possibility of ionization, which also adds appeal to devices of this family.
