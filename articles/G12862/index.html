Kem city

The regional center of Kem is located on the eponymous river near the place where it flows into the White Sea. Amazingly beautiful land, however, does not pamper residents and guests with a hospitable climate.





Here is a very short and cool summer, which lasts a maximum of 1.5 months - from July to mid-August. Despite this, there is no end to tourists, since the main attractions of the city and the surrounding area are clean air, nature unspoiled by human activity and wonderful people.

The city of Kem, whose hotels are ready to receive guests year-round, is the starting point for Solovki, which can be reached by boat. The Solovetsky Islands, on which the monastery‚Äôs monastery is located, is a historical place given to the monks Marfa Boretskaya in the 15th century.

In the 16th century, the Kemsky volost was ravaged twice - first by the Finns, and then by the Swedes. In 1591, a fortified prison was built on Solovki, and from the end of the 18th century, Kem received the status of a county town.

During the Soviet era, the famous monastery became the place of imprisonment of political prisoners, and a concentration camp was built in Kem where they were waiting for departure to the islands.





Today, not camps, but hotels (Kem, Karelia) give shelter to travelers, and the Solovetsky Monastery is a museum-reserve open to visitors. Its historical value and sad history attract thousands of tourists every year, which makes it possible to support the monastery financially.

Mushroom and berry hunting

Since Karelia is a region that does not have large industrial enterprises, it is a real paradise for mushroom pickers, since you can not be afraid that the mushrooms found can be poisoned with chemicals.

Fishing

For fishermen, this region is also a paradise. Forest lake will delight them with the catch of pike, pink salmon and even salmon, and in the White Sea you can catch sea cod with a net. Fishing requires a license, which is easy to obtain. Travel companies even organize weekend tours for fishermen, therefore the Hotel ‚ÄúPrichal‚Äù is not empty in Kem either in winter or in summer.





Catfish, flounder, sea bass, goby and other fish become trophies of such tours. On odd years, real herds of pink salmon fish come to spawn to the White Sea, whose caviar can be enjoyed from July to August.

For the most advanced fishermen who are ready to spend time on long fishing, fishing camps are organized, which can be reached by boat.

Mini-hotel "Kem"

In addition to the Prichal cottage complex, guests are welcomed by the Kemi Mini-Hotel of the same name. It is located in close proximity to the railway station and offers travelers not only comfortable rooms for relaxation, but also round-the-clock meals.

The cost of rooms from 800 rubles per day makes it popular with tourists traveling to the Solovetsky Islands. A bus departs from the inn, delivering passengers to the seaport.

Economy rooms do not have their own amenities (they are located on the floor), and the standard category has its own toilet and shower.

All rooms have a private balcony with city views.
