Needlework lessons. How to knit a hat with knitting needles for a girl?

How lucky the girls whose mothers know how to knit. The wardrobe of little fashionistas is regularly updated with new original handmade things. Craftswomen do not tire of coming up with more and more knitted models of clothes for young ladies. In this article, we will share with you information about how a beautiful double hat for a girl is made with knitting needles. The description is given in such detail that even a novice needlewoman can knit such an accessory. The size of this cap is designed for a head circumference of 52 centimeters. But the product can be made in a smaller format, and in a larger one. This is regulated by the number of initially dialed loops. We are preparing materials

To do the headgear, we need 150 grams of wool / acrylic yarn (150 m / 50 g). So that the product does not prick, does not cause skin irritation and the child is comfortable in it, take care of acquiring threads marked on the label for children. This is a specially processed yarn for the manufacture of products for babies. In the work we will use circular knitting needles No. 2.5 (on the fishing line).

Pearl pattern - simple and beautiful

For knitting such a headgear, you can use any pattern. For beginners, we propose to fulfill it with a pearl pattern. 1 row: 1 front and 1 wrong loops knit alternately. 2 row and all the rest are even: performed as the loops look. 3 row: 1 wrong and 1 front loops knit alternately.



How to knit a hat with knitting needles for a girl (top layer)

The product is carried out in circular rows with a thread in two additions. Dial 90 stitches and start knitting cuffs with a height of 7-8 centimeters with a 3x3 elastic band. In the next row we carry out increases. To do this, after every 6 loops, make one double crochet, as a result of which 105 needles will be on the knitting needles. Knit them directly until the product reaches a height of 20 cm. We round the caps by the method of reducing loops. How to do it? In each second row, knit 2 loops together every 6, then every 5, 4, and so on. When only 8-10 loops remain on the knitting needles, pull them off, cut the thread and fasten.

Knit the bottom of the headgear

So that the small head does not freeze, the hat with knitting needles (for the girl) will be double. In order to perform its inner layer, type 87 loops along the inner edge of the elastic band of the finished part. Knit them with a single thread with the front surface. When the product reaches a height of 17 centimeters, do the reduction only in the first three rows: first every 6 loops, then after 5 and after 4. Close the remaining loops, but do not tighten them. We can assume that the knitting cap for the girl is knitted. It remains to draw up the little things.

Assembly and decoration



Sew the inside of the cap to the outside carefully. This is done with the help of a large needle and thread, from which the headpiece is connected. Run the pompom and attach it to the crown. You can decorate the product with rhinestones, textile or knitted applique.

This is how simple the hat is for knitting with needles. We hope that this master class will be adopted by the mother knitters and very soon new handmade hats will appear in the wardrobe of young ladies.
