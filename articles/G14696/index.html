House carving, art of decorating a wooden house

Wooden Russia ... From time immemorial, houses, huts, and whole villages of wood were built. This was done for human life, so that it was where the Russian person lay his head, and wintered in the warmth. And live yourself, and attach the cattle. And the stone was in abundance, and there was enough clay, but everything was built of wood, since wood is a warm, lively material, infinitely grateful, cool in the hut in summer, warm in winter. So entire wooden cities, villages and villages appeared on the expanses of Russians. And where people live, churches are built there, large and small, cathedrals with chapels, parishes and presence.



Life requires beauty. A peasant's wooden house, although well built, but looks simple and unpretentious. Carpenters began to invent ornaments, compose ornaments, boards with carved patterns, "towels", gaps and cleats. There was a house carving. Of the tool, the architect had only an ax, and a chisel with a chisel. However, hunting is more than captivity, since one wants to cut something beautiful, then the hatchet can be sharpened so that it will ring, and the thread from under it will come out thin, but intricate. Real craftsmen appeared and with them house carving, an applied art that gave carvers a wide field for creativity. Competitions began, who cuts wood better, cleaner and faster.



It was all in time immemorial. Today, wood carvers, specialists in such an art form as house carving, have a wonderful set of tools, and there are no impossible tasks for them. Technologies for fine artistic processing of wood are diverse, from simple flat-surface carvings to through ornamental with a complex interweaving of the pattern. The art of carving "Tatyanka" is indispensable for the embodiment in a tree of natural scenes with leaves, grass, flowers. Baroque carving is used in the frame of church iconostases. And the facades of residential wooden houses are decorated with slotted boards-gaps.



Each owner of his own home is concerned about the attractiveness of the facade of his home. I would like the house to be decorated on the principle of exclusivity with unique carved patterns. The so-called Gorodets home carving gives the greatest degree of uniqueness. This technique combines a hollow embossed "Tatyanka" with its natural plots that are reflected in Gorodets carvings in the form of floral ornaments. The rich plant filling of Gorodets carvings is the most demanded when decorating the facades of wooden houses.





Some masters also willingly use art nouveau ornaments from a stone architecture order in their work. Their elegance and practicality perfectly meet the tasks of finishing wooden houses.

Currently, wooden carvings are coming into fashion in the form of bas-relief sculptures carved from hard wood. The image is half recessed into the surface of the wall or panel of the house. The effect of visual surround perception is amazing. This technique makes it possible to reproduce bas-reliefs on any topic, Chinese and Japanese, African and Latin American, the choice of plots is not limited. Sculptural bas-reliefs can be combined with other carvings, for example, artistic lace. Also for the exterior design of the house is a laid on home thread without a through slot, the elements of which are glued with waterproof glue.
