Ekaterinburg Hotels - Holidays for Everyone.

Russia is a state full of interesting places. Our country makes it possible to enjoy beautiful landscapes, visit historical places, study the history of one of the most ancient civilizations. To spend a vacation it is not at all necessary to travel abroad - the cities of our homeland carry practically all the possibilities for a good rest. One of the most attractive places for tourists is the city of Yekaterinburg - one of the largest cities in Russia, the capital of the Ural Federal District.

Holidays in Yekaterinburg can be called part of the passing and short-term that everyone cherishes, this is an opportunity to break out of the harsh gray reality. A trip to Yekaterinburg is almost always an original, unforgettable and vibrant event, which energizes as well as new experiences for a long time.

Although Yekaterinburg receives a lot of guests annually, problems with accommodation usually do not arise. Yekaterinburg hotels gladly open their doors to guests and offer many accommodation options - from inexpensive rooms to VIP apartments. The most popular hotels in Yekaterinburg are described below.

Grand Avenue Hotel

The Grand Avenue Hotel is located in the very center of Yekaterinburg and has a 3 star category. This place is ideal for people who visit the city to solve business problems. The hotel offers its guests a variety of options - from inexpensive Standard apartments to Presidential rooms.

In Grand Avenue, all possible business services are provided, represented by a conference room, meeting rooms, a business center. There is also a restaurant complex offering banquet and buffet service. In addition, there are additional services provided by Wi-Fi network, round-the-clock free room service, visa support, parking, concierge. Such hotels in Yekaterinburg make your stay in the city really comfortable.

Hermitage Hotel

The Hermitage Hotel is located in the countryside - on the shores of Lake Shartash. From the center of Yekaterinburg to go to this hotel only 20 minutes. The hotel has all the conditions so that the guests rest is of high quality, and the events held in this place are successful.

Hotel Iset

This hotel is recognized as an architectural monument of the twentieth century. One of the first hotels in the city offers a democratic pricing policy, which goes well with a wide range of business services, as well as developed infrastructure.

Hotel Ekaterinburg-Central

This historic hotel is located in the very center of Yekaterinburg. Category 3 stars implies the presence of a large number of rooms, represented by 96 rooms of various levels - from Standard to Suite. The hotel also has conference rooms, 3 restaurants, a coffee shop, 2 bars, and a cabaret. The hotel has a centuries-old history and carries a wonderful combination of interiors in a historical style and excellent technical equipment, as well as the highest level of service.

Premier Hotel

Premier Inn is located in the center of Yekaterinburg. The hotel has 19 rooms overlooking one of the most elite neighborhoods in the city. Nearby are many cultural and business facilities. Here you can see office centers, various administrative buildings, and then visit cinemas, galleries and restaurants

In the Premier Hotel itself, guests are invited to visit the cafe, use the services of the conference hall, business center, concierge service, as well as a full list of additional services.

Visiting the hotels of Yekaterinburg, you can see - a full and comfortable rest is available without leaving the country.
