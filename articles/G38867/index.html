Eilat: beaches, weather, holidays, reviews

One of the most expensive resort cities in Israel is Eilat. Its beaches are simply magnificent, and here you can not only enjoy a lazy beach vacation, but also engage in treatment and prevention using thalassotherapy, various spa treatments and simply healing air. It is for this purpose that hundreds of thousands or even millions of tourists from all over the world come here every day. For Russians, the popularity of this place has increased many times over the past few years.



Geographic location

Eilat is located in the south of Israel, moreover, it is the southernmost point of the country. It stretches along the shores of the picturesque Gulf of Aqaba, or Gulf of Eilat, in the Red Sea. This city is far removed from other cities in Israel. However, if you wish, you can organize excursions to the sights of the country. In Eilat, there is a port that connects it with countries spread along the coast of the Indian and Pacific Oceans. It is from here that the gifts of the Red Sea go to the countries of the Indian and Pacific Basin.

How to get

In addition to the sea route, you can get to Eilat through the air gate. In the very center of the city there is an airport of the same name. Until recently, it served only domestic flights, but with the increasing popularity of Eilat as a seaside health resort, the airport began to accept international flights. So, from Russia you can get here from a number of large cities: Voronezh, Kazan, Irkutsk, Yekaterinburg, etc. on charter flights. Ten flights from Aeroflot airlines ($ 1,400 round-trip is the most expensive option), El Al ($ 400 round-trip), LOT (the price is constantly changing depending on the time of the year) are daily made from Moscow to the resort every day. ) It is not as easy to get from the Northern capital to Eilat as from Moscow: there are only a couple of flights of the Polish LOT airline per week. You can also get here by bus, some tourists do so to save money. The nearest international airport is in the city of Uvda, which is located 60 km north of Eilat, whose beaches attract crowds of tourists.



Climate

If you look from a bird's eye view, then the Gulf of Eilat will seem to you a blue heart in the center of the golden desert. Along the beaches of Eilat, mountains rise. Experts say that this is the reason for the high volatility, as a result of which the salt concentration in the bay reaches 4.1%, which is much higher than in the ocean. Here, even in winter, it is warm enough to swim. Many tourists share that they are going to Israel in the cold season because of the wonderful weather in Eilat. The lowest water temperature in the bay is at around +22 degrees. All of these factors lead to coral formation. They make up a fairly wide band here.



Tourism development

According to statistics, about 50,000 people live permanently in Eilat. Of these, about 85% of the adult population are involved in the tourism sector. There are many hotels of various levels - both five- and four-star luxury, belonging to well-known hotel chains, as well as the most affordable hostels, guest houses, apart-hotels, etc. Thanks to the wonderful weather in Eilat, tourist life is full of life all year round. Wellness tourism is also developing very well at the resort, when citizens of other countries come not only for relaxation, but also for treatment and prevention.

sights

For tourists who come here, in the first place a beach holiday, treatment and complete relaxation. There are no interesting historical sights in this area. If you like to combine a beach holiday with an excursion program, then for this you can buy a walking tour to other cities in Israel. Here and in the vicinity, the biggest attraction is the beaches of Eilat, which are officially considered a nature reserve, in particular because of the strip of coral reefs encircling the entire coast. It is clear that some tourists are lovers of diving and snorkeling. Although climbers and fans of mountain trekking will also find many interesting routes here. And for those tourists who still come to the coast of the Eilat Gulf for a beach holiday, there are all the necessary conditions, the most important of which is the complete absence of bad weather in our understanding. The rains here are from the category of miracles, so that none of your days at the resort will be spoiled by bad weather.

Reefs

There are many beautiful reefs on the southern beach of the Eilat Gulf. Each of them has a bizarre shape and its own name. So, for example, there is a dolphin reef in Eilat. Its area is more than 10 square meters. The dolphin reef has the shape of a horseshoe. Today it is an amusement park. Bottlenose dolphins live in this part of the coast - a special species of dolphins. They communicate well with people, especially with children. Floating pontoons and towers are installed along the coast, smart animals swim towards them, jump to the people standing on the tower. Everyone can swim here with a mask, as well as a ride, catching on a dolphin.

Other animals also live in coastal waters, for example, angelfish, cuttlefish, lionfish, various stingrays, including spotted ones. The Dolphin Reef entertainment center has many other attractions, its own sandy beach, bars and cafes, a dance floor once a week, several artificial pools, etc. There is a diving center that offers both individual and group dives. and underwater walks. The entertainment center operates on all days of the week according to the following schedule: the first 4 days of the week + Sunday from 9:00 to 17:00, and on Friday and Saturday, as well as on holidays - from 9:00 to 16:30.



What is the best beach?

This question is probably of interest to everyone who is going on vacation to Israel. There are a great many wonderful areas for relaxation. However, many prefer the coral beach in Eilat. It is otherwise called the water area. At any time of the year you can meet diving fans here. The underwater coastal world is striking in its beauty and diversity of inhabitants. The coast is about 3 kilometers long. It stretches from the center to Taba - the southern border of Eilat. Because of corals, entering the sea without special shoes is impossible.

Reviews about holidays in Eilat

Tourists in this resort are primarily struck by sterile cleanliness. In addition, there is a chic infrastructure. And 4- and 5-star hotels operating on an all-inclusive basis are ready to treat their guests with the most exquisite dishes at any time. There are a lot of swimming pools in the hotel territories, which sets up for a beach holiday, for sunbathing on sun loungers. Of course, all tourists, without exception, are invited to go diving and, plunging into the water, observe the beauties of coral gardens. The city also lives a cultural life, but for all you need to pay a lot of money. No wonder they say that Eilat is the most expensive resort in Israel.
