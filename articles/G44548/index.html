Rest in Crimea in tents: the best places by the sea

With the advent of summer, every resident of the metropolis dreams of how to bask on the sea coast, dive into the cool waters that relieve accumulated fatigue. Is there a place in these dreams for crowded beaches with intrusive merchants and loud music? Hardly. Every year, wild tourism is becoming increasingly popular among urban residents. In Crimea, there are wonderful places for camping. But not everyone knows about them.





On the most part of the Crimean coast there are practically no landscapes untouched by civilization . And there are even fewer wild ones. Vacationers, having spent time on a picturesque beach, leave a memory of themselves: empty bags, bottles, plastic plates, snack wrappers and watermelon peels. Before organizing a vacation in Crimea in tents , it is worth asking you a question: " Am I ready to take all the garbage with me? " Containers for drinks and labels on the beach should not be - special containers are provided for them.

It was a small preachy introduction. Let's move on to the main topic of today's article - to rest in the Crimea in tents .

Wild tourism on the peninsula is popular primarily among local residents. "Interesting " spa visitors know about interesting picturesque places. For obvious reasons, the indigenous inhabitants of the peninsula are reluctant to talk about suitable beaches for relaxing with tents in the Crimea. But thanks to lovers of travel and camping, information about such areas is still there. We name the beaches on which fans of wild tourism regularly visit.

Lyubimovka

The village is located on the seashore, in the north of Sevastopol. There are many capes on the coast of the city. There are beaches both ennobled and wild. The latter is also located in Lyubimovka . The beach is a fifteen minute walk from the village. In the village itself there are recreation centers, campsites, and shops. As you know, there are many entertainments in the Crimea. For relaxing with a tent, quiet and secluded, the beach, located near Lyubimovka , is one of the best places.





Cape Chersonesos

In the southern part of the peninsula, in the west of Sevastopol, there is a lighthouse built back in 1816. For two centuries, it provides the approach of ships and ships to the port. The height of the lighthouse is more than thirty meters. Cape Chersonese is known, of course , not only for this attraction, but also for picturesque places and interesting history. During the Second World War, fierce battles were fought here. Today it is visited not only by history buffs, but also fans of relaxation with a tent.

Traveling around the Crimea by car is quite interesting. True, only for those who are not tired of the long road. The distance from Sevastopol to Chersonesos can be covered by car in seven hours (530 km). In the morning it is better to see the sights of the legendary city, in the evening - spend on the wild coast, setting up a tent. Rest in the Crimea and without a car will bring a lot of positive emotions. However, in this case, from the nearest settlements to the rocky coast, you will have to travel by public transport, which is quite tiring.

It is not difficult for an experienced driver to get to Cape Chersonese with his own car. It is better to buy water and provisions in Sevastopol. Here it is worth stocking up with firewood, without which tent rest is impossible. The lights of sea lighthouses, ships sailing in the distance , the sea surface during the hours of sunny sunset - all these are components of picturesque landscapes, which you can see when visiting the Crimea. Reviews about the rest in tents suggest that one of the most beautiful and convenient beaches is the one located in the Blue Bay.

This bay is located in the southeast of Chersonesos. A reference point is a black and white tower. It was no coincidence that Blue Bay got its name. Here the water is surprisingly bright blue. Even the sand at the bottom may appear slightly bluish. The beach is quite diverse. In its central part there are cafes, toilets, and mini-scenes. But after only three hundred meters, you can find yourself in a virtually uninhabited area. For those who are just exploring a wild vacation in Crimea with tents, Blue Bay is perhaps the most suitable place.





Violet tape

Rest in a tent in the Crimea , in the forest or in the steppe zone, is suitable for hardy, patient people. So, one of the most popular places on the peninsula is Cape Fiolent . The local beach is known primarily for its impeccably clear sea. But in order to get to it, you have to overcome the path of nine hundred steps. It is not easy to do this for a person laden with tents, personal belongings, food. According to reviews, on average, it takes more than five hours to go down and climb the stairs.

The cape is located fifteen kilometers from the center of Sevastopol. In 1996, a national reserve was created here. Extreme Fiolent is very fond of. Here they make jumps from the rocks, engage in sea kayaking . Among the beaches located on the cape, it should be distinguished Lighthouse, Tsarskoye Selo, Jashmovy.

How to get to the beaches that stretch from Balaclava to Cape Ayia ? Holidays in the Crimea with tents, many tourists prefer to organize in this area, and it is most convenient to get here by boat. Between the entrance to the Balaklava Bay and the Jasper Beach is Vasili. Boats go, according to the schedule, only once per hour. In fact, you can leave the beach almost at any time. Owing to its close location in the Balaklava Bay, owners of small boats, acting as a sea taxi, are constantly scurrying here.

The length of Vasili beach is about three hundred meters. Width - no more than forty meters. The beach got its name due to its location. It is located on the Vasilievsky beam. This beach is a wonderful place to relax with a tent. A huge number of sports tourism lovers visit Crimea annually. Most of them are directed specifically to Vasily.

It is impossible not to talk about Tarkhankut when it comes to the rest of the savages in tents. In Crimea, the most pristine, uninhabited places are located precisely in the west. This part of the peninsula deserves special attention.





Cape Tarkhankut

Rest in Crimea with tents by car attracts tourists not only with romance, but also with low cost. Even if we are talking about staying on the territory of paid camping. Camping, in addition, helps to relax as much as possible, forget from the bustle of the city. You can, of course, organize a vacation with tents in the south-east of Crimea. But here there are much fewer beaches untouched by civilization.

Sophisticated travelers prefer the western coast of Crimea, because it is here that you can learn all the charms of the steppe area. Not only mountains can be beautiful - it is precisely this thought that comes to mind when you first visit Tarhankut. There are no intrusive sellers and photographers, loud music and other elements present on the south coast on the cape.

In order to get to the cape, you must keep track of the village of Chernomorskoye, and from there follow along the coast. The tent here can be put anywhere. According to reviews, there is no such clear, transparent water on other coasts of Crimea. This is a great place for diving and underwater photography. What should I take on the road? First of all, a gas bottle. Many vacationers take braziers with them. Packages of coal in this case will not hurt, because there is no firewood in the steppe. You can build a refrigerator yourself by tearing a deep hole under the machine and loading a special refrigerator bag there.

Tents should be carefully strengthened. A hurricane wind is not uncommon in the steppe. Stakes, as recommended , should be checked every night. The tips given above will be useful to those who plan a vacation in the south or south-west of Crimea. Below are the places that are also suitable for camping, although not so popular. Told and in more detail about the most popular village of the Tarkhankut Peninsula.





Cape

There are many parking lots for camping in Crimea with tents. Someone prefers complete solitude. Someone enjoys organized campsites with a minimum of amenities. Theoretically, you can pitch a tent in the Crimea anywhere. A suitable place for wild tourism is the village of Mysovoye . It is located near the Feodosia-Kerch highway, near Cape Kazantip . This place is as if created for a secluded , romantic getaway . The sandy beach is not crowded, its silence is only broken by the cry of seagulls and a surge of waves. The sea is shallow and warm.

Quiet bay

From Koktebel to these picturesque places, which are still almost untouched by civilization, you can walk for forty minutes. Quiet bay is known for clay, which has healing properties. This part of the coast was once a favorite place of Soviet filmmakers. Today, an elemental tent camp is placed here every year. Parking is free.

Fox bay

This area is called "Crimean Goa ." Fox Bay is very popular among informals and nudists. For family holidays, these places are not the best option. Tourists enjoy drinking water from a spring on Echki Dag , which can be reached from the coast in twenty minutes. Nearby is a small market, many cafes, several toilets. The stock of products can be replenished in villages located near the Fox Bay.





Half wild vacation in Alushta

What are the tourists who prefer a tent vacation, but are not able to abandon civilization? They are sent to Alushta. Here is the tent town " Vympel " , which, due to its convenient location, is suitable for tourists arriving by public transport. There is drinking water, electricity, a toilet, and even a dining room. The camp is located in a forest, near the Sudak ring. In the tent camp, tourists can rent a tent, sleeping bag and other equipment. However, the sea is far away: you will have to go at least twenty minutes to the nearest beach.

Laspi

The bay is very popular among adherents of wild rest. It is reliably sheltered by the mountains, in some places the coast is covered with dense thickets of juniper. Paid camping with toilets and showers is not here yet. Vacationers stop with tents on their own. The nearest paid campsite is located near Cape Sarych. And next to Laspi is the village of the same name , where tourists replenish supplies of food and drinking water. The lack of relaxation in these places is a rather tedious descent to the sea.

Deer

This locality has already been mentioned above. It is located at the westernmost point of the Crimea. Here are amazing landscapes and great opportunities for camping. It is Olenevka that is considered the center of wild tourism on Tarhankut. Like other beaches on the west coast, the water here is surprisingly clear. True, the territory is open. The beach near Olenevka is characterized by scarcity of greenery, which amazes even those who grew up in the steppe. Near the village there is a paid campsite, which has not only electricity, a toilet and shower, but also wi-fi. You can rent a bike in the village, then explore the surroundings, rich in historical sights.





Bakalskaya Spit

At the beginning of the Bakalskaya Spit, a small tent town with all the necessary things is set up not so much for the wild as for a comfortable stay. There is a toilet, shower, garbage collection organized. Tourists replenish food supplies in the village of Stereguschey, located two kilometers from the campsite. The cost of placement is 150 rubles. According to reviews, one of the shortcomings of rest on the Bakalskaya Spit is annoying mosquitoes.

Evpatoria

On the Cote d'Azur, camping is a large outdoor area. Nearby are nightlife. There is a dining room on site. This is not the best place for lovers of silence, it is more suitable for young people. The campsite is equipped with modern showers, electricity is provided. The cost of living is a little more than a hundred rubles per person per day. Rent a house from reeds - 1000 rubles.

Camping is a budget type of vacation, which can be organized in two ways. Some choose the most secluded places. Others prefer equipped campsites. In both the first and second cases, the time spent on the Crimean coast will bring a lot of positive emotions and energize for the coming year.
