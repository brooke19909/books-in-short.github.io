Hydronym

There are two versions of the origin of the name of the river. According to one of them, the name of the reservoir was due to the Karelian word "deep". And according to another version - from the Old Slavonic "left." In the past, this river flowed through Finland, and the locals called it a swamp.

Water quality in the Shuya river (Karelia)

The water itself is mostly dark, reminiscent of tea in hue. But this does not mean that it is dirty - quite the contrary, even very clean. A characteristic dark color is obtained due to peat bogs, which are surrounded by a river. The quality of the water is soft, has a small salinity.

Flora and fauna

Shuya is a river in which the underwater world is not very rich. However, fishermen manage to catch a pike or perch. The riverbed has become a favorite place for salmon. It is here that this species of fish spawns.

Cloudberries, blueberries, lingonberries and mushrooms (ceps, aspen, boletus) grow in coastal forests in summer. Therefore, locals or vacationers come here for the harvest. However, during hiking in the forest you need to be extremely careful, as vipers can get caught.





Rafting on the Shuya river in Karelia

River rafting is very popular. Of course, it is held in the summer, as well as in late spring and early fall. The route is 105 km long. The main watercraft include six- or eight-seater rafts. If potential customers wish, they can ride on existing catamarans, which are designed for 2, 4 and 6 people, or in double kayaks.

Rafting on the Shuya river in Karelia does not last at all one day, but as much as 8 days and 7 nights. In other words, this is a weekly water tour. Impressions from such a trip will remain for a long time. Of course, it will be difficult for tourists in the early days due to unaccustomed and without acclimatization. But thanks to experienced instructors, these problems can be easily dealt with in order to continue to conquer the water element.

It is impossible to spend the whole week in the river, therefore, there will periodically be landings; there are a lot of parking lots along this route. During the days you can enjoy nature, fresh air, fishing, barbecue, relaxing by the fire. Equipment for the duration of the alloy is rented. At the end of the tour, excursions are also offered, for example, to Kivach waterfall or Kizhi island.
