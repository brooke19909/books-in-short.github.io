Hotels in Lyubertsy: addresses, reviews, room description

Why are Lyubertsy so popular with tourists? Because this city is an important part of the annals of Russian history. If you are interested in the heritage of your ancestors, the history of your country, then you are simply obliged to get acquainted with the sights of this glorious city. Moreover, their diversity is simply stunning. Monasteries and temples, churches and monuments ... One day is not enough to even get to know them in passing. However, hotels in Lyubertsy solve all problems with accommodation, so you can stay here for the whole vacation. We will introduce you closer to the most famous hotels and hostels of the city so that you can choose where you better stay.

Budget vacation

Not all tourists can expect to stay in expensive, five-star hotels. Fortunately, there are a huge number of alternative options. There are hotels in Lyubertsy for every taste, and we will begin our acquaintance with inexpensive hotels. The most affordable hostel is My Sunny Grandmother. It is located on Kolkhoznaya street, house number 3, to the city center - only 2.8 km. The atmosphere here is unique, homely, warm and bright. The hostel is relatively small, but everyone will find a cozy corner to retire and enjoy their vacation in peace. The rooms are large, populate here from three or more people. They have six or eight bunk beds, bedside tables, mirrors. The cost of rest is only 400 rubles per day without meals. Judging by the reviews of tourists, it is very clean and comfortable. Embroidered curtains and wicker chairs contribute to this . Here you feel like visiting your beloved grandmother.



Inexpensive hotel in the city center

If you are looking for hotels in Lyubertsy, located closer to the center, then pay attention to "Moscow Nights". A small and comfortable hotel is located in the heart of the Moscow Region city, at the address: 213 Oktyabrsky Prospekt. Nearby are the railway station and metro station, a city park with attractions, as well as the Sports Palace. Despite the fact that the building looks modest from the outside, inside it can even be called refined. The number of rooms is varied, you can rent a modest room for 800 rubles or luxurious luxury apartments for 4500 rubles. At the same time, prices are indicated without meals, judging by the reviews of guests, there is everything you need here. Guests repeatedly emphasize the beautiful interior decoration of the hotel and rooms, convenience and comfort. In addition, the location of the hotel is very captivating. Hotel "Podmoskovnaya" (Lyubertsy) - this is a lot of options for an excellent holiday for everyone.





Hotel "Leo"

If the choice is made exclusively at a price, then, of course, the first two options are beyond competition. However, tourists often note that inexpensive hostels and hotels do not provide the level of comfort they would like. If you have the same opinion, we recommend that you consider other hotels in Lyubertsy. Not far from the two largest metro stations ("Zhulebino" and "Lermontovsky Prospekt"), at the address: 142 Oktyabrsky Prospekt, there is a mini-hotel "Lev". It consists of only nine rooms, but you will certainly be pleased with comfortable suites and junior suites. Refined interiors, high-quality furniture and all necessary household appliances will allow you to spend time just fine. The price starts from 3300 rubles per room per day, this price includes a delicious breakfast, an excursion program. The advantages of this hotel are obvious, it is very cozy, elegant, and also quiet. Judging by the reviews, this is exactly the hotel where you want to return again and again.





LikeFlat Apartments

The hotel is located in Lyubertsy, at the address: Uritsky, 5. Only five minutes separates it from Natashinsky Park, where it is so good to spend evenings. Metro stations are approximately three kilometers away. The hotel has free Wi-Fi. I would like to pay special attention to the interiors of this hotel. The brightly decorated rooms feature a balcony, washing machine and flat-screen TV. Each room has a private bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries. Each room has a kitchen equipped with the latest technology, where you can cook or just have a tea party. The cost of renting a room starts at 3,500 rubles, while assessing your vacation, guests put a solid nine points out of ten possible. Judging by the guest reviews, this is the most modern and comfortable hotel in the city.





Hotel Iskra

This particularly cozy hotel is located three kilometers from Lermontovsky Prospekt Metro Station, at 8a Marta Street, 4a. Hotel "Iskra" offers you many beautiful rooms, but they do not include children, there are no special cots. Each room has a fridge and a TV, while some have a private bathroom. Sheremetyevo International Airport is 48 km away. The hotel offers guests a sauna and minimarket. A concierge is on duty 24 hours a day to answer all your questions. The cost of rest starts at 3,500 rubles per day, which is the average cost for the city of Lyubertsy. Pets are allowed upon prior request. Tourists say that this is a fairly simple hotel, which is nothing special, but where you can spend the night with comfort.

Hotel Through the Looking Glass

This is an original hotel for organizing wonderful leisure activities, located at: st. Kirova, 3. A pleasant stay will be provided by caring staff and an unusual interior, wonderful atmosphere for happy friends. At your service are mirrored rooms and a cozy bar, a luxurious sauna with a swimming pool. Even if you need a room for just a few hours, the hotel administration will meet you. There are only five rooms, but the hotel is not designed for a massive flow of customers. Rather, on the contrary, each guest is valued here and his individual wishes are taken into account. A day's stay will cost you 3,500 rubles. Judging by the reviews, this is a hotel for a special evening that you want to spend in unusual conditions. But for a regular business trip, it is not suitable.





Elite hotel Sino Guest House

We have already given you the most popular hotels in Lyubertsy. For a day you can settle in any of them, the most important thing is to decide what exactly you are looking for. Economy hostel, budget mini-hotel, luxury penthouse - there are any options here, and it all depends on your desire. However, we can not separately mention the Sino Guest House. This is a luxurious guest house, which is located in the city of Lyubertsy, just 20 km from Moscow, at 15 Voznesensky Lane. The interior of the hotel is very impressive, everything is done with an incomparable taste. Each room has an impeccable bathroom with a large shower. The sleeping area is striking in luxury, while functional furniture is striking in convenience. All rooms include a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Slippers and fluffy bathrobes await you in each room. The nearest airport is Domodedovo Airport, 31 km from the property. The cost of renting a room is from 3800 rubles. Judging by the reviews, this is far from the most affordable, but very beautiful and comfortable hotel, so choose it if there are no budget restrictions.





Apartments on Oktyabrsky

This is a real fairy tale, a dream come true. There are many similar options in the city, but there is only one of them. It is located in an ordinary four-room apartment, at the address: Oktyabrsky Avenue, Building 5, that is, this is the only number offered. It has air conditioning and underfloor heating. In the living room you will find a widescreen plasma panel, the kitchen is equipped with a coffee machine and a dishwasher, an oven and a kettle. The bathroom includes a bathtub and bidet, as well as all the necessary cosmetics. Based on the reviews of vacationers here, it should be said that this is an amazing place for a family vacation and a small corporate party.
