Hotel Princess Residence 4 * (Bulgaria, Kiten): description, reviews of tourists

Planning a summer vacation in Bulgaria? Are you more inclined to a measured beach holiday than to constant trips on excursions? In this case, consider the Princess Residence 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Kiten) as an option for accommodation .



Description, location

Princess Residence Hotel is located in the Bulgarian town of Kiten on the very shore of the sea. Burgas Airport is 65 km away. Thus, upon arrival on the road to the hotel, it will take you about an hour and a half.

This hotel was opened in 2009. It is a six-story building with 154 rooms. The hotel has a small territory on which, in addition to the residential building, there is a restaurant, swimming pool, sun terrace. It also offers parking, a business center, a 24-hour reception and much more.

The housing stock of the hotel is represented by studios, standards (sea or garden view) and one-bedroom apartments. All of them are equipped with necessary furniture and appliances. So, in each room there is air conditioning, a safe (for an additional fee), a TV (cable channels), a refrigerator, a telephone, a bathroom, a hairdryer, a terrace or a balcony.



Princess Residence 4 *: tourist reviews

Moreover, very close attention should be paid to the choice of a hotel where you plan to stay for several days or even weeks. After all, the overall impressions of the rest will depend on how comfortable you will be. Therefore, long before the holidays, it makes sense to start collecting information about the hotels of the place you are interested in. Moreover, it is very important not only to study the description of a particular hotel, but also to read the reviews left by tourists who have already been here. After all, former guests talk about what they had to face first hand. Today we offer to find out the opinion of our compatriots about the Princess Residence Hotel (Kiten, Bulgaria). Immediately, it can be noted that, judging by the rating of the hotel, most of the guests stayed here quite a lot. So, the average rating of the hotel is 4 points out of the maximum possible five. However, we learn about everything in more detail.



Rooms, infrastructure

Almost all guests who commented paid attention to the fact that they were satisfied with the apartments offered by him in Princess Residence 4 *. So, the rooms here, according to them, are spacious, pleasantly decorated, comfortable and clean. The furniture is not new, but high-quality, in excellent condition. The same goes for technology. Everything works without interruption. Those who came here in families or companies of 4-5 people also note the fact that there are two bathrooms in the large rooms. This is very convenient when everyone returns to their room from the beach at the same time, because in this case you do not have to wait long for your turn to take a shower.

Tourists also included regular high-quality cleaning and a daily change of towels (both bath and beach ones). The maids here update their bedding twice a week. As for the shortcomings, a number of complaints about the rooms, which overlook the pool and disco bar of a neighboring hotel. The fact is that quite loud music plays there until late, which can interfere with the relaxation of those who came on vacation with a small child, or those who are used to going to bed early. Therefore, upon arrival, you can immediately ask not to occupy you in such apartments.



Nutrition

Most tourists note in reviews that when booking accommodation in Princess Residence 4 * (Bulgaria), they paid only for breakfast and did not lose. So, according to travelers, in the vicinity of the hotel there are many cafes where you can have lunch and dinner much cheaper than in the Princess Residence. As for breakfast, their guests considered monotonous. So, in the morning you can eat a fried egg or scrambled eggs, bacon, cheese, granola, toast, tea, coffee, etc. However, the set of products and dishes every day remains unchanged. Therefore, some guests sometimes preferred not to go down for breakfast to the hotel restaurant, but to buy something in a supermarket or a bakery.

Sea

According to most of our compatriots who have visited the Princess Residence, this hotel is perfect for lovers of beach relax. So, you can reach the sea in just a couple of minutes. The beach here is municipal, so you will have to pay extra for renting sun loungers and umbrellas (about nine euros per day). Those who wish to save money can settle down on a site not occupied by sun loungers on mats or towels brought with them. No one will require payment for this. When entering the water, according to tourists, there are stones. Depth increases gradually, so families with children will be comfortable here. On the beach, vacationers are offered a lot of entertainment - riding a "banana", water slides, etc.
