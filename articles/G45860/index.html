All about the hotel Maria Apartments (Cyprus / Ayia Napa)

Cyprus is a place for a hectic holiday, discos and beach parties until the morning, and Ayia Napa is one of the most beloved youth resorts. Many young tourists and students often stay in inexpensive hotels, but lovers of comfortable relaxation and quality service choose Maria Apartments (Cyprus / Ayia Napa).

A bit about the resort

Ayia Napa is a place where you can relax and fully plunge into the nightlife. The presence of a large number of bars, discos and taverns will completely forget about everyday affairs and boring work. Often this resort is chosen by students and couples in love, in their opinion, Ayia Napa can easily be called the second Ibiza.





In addition, Ayia Napa also attracts couples with children. The beaches here are wide and safe, and the shallow depth of the sea allows parents not to worry about their child.

Maria Apartments (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): Hotel Location

The hotel is located near the center of the resort. In just a few minutes, you can reach the vibrant Ayia Napa area with many bars and restaurants. The sea is also nearby, 5-10 minutes before it can be reached by transport or walk, the beach is located a kilometer from the hotel. By the way, many vacationers spend time on the famous sandy beach of Nissi, which is famous for its clear waters and white clean sand.

Tourists who plan to get to the hotel on their own should be aware that the hotel is 48 km from Larnaca International Airport. It will not be very convenient to stay at Maria Apartments 4 * (Cyprus) for those who flew to Paphos, this airport is located 170 km from the resort.

Hotel Description





The apartments are located near a public beach. Despite the high star rating of the hotel, the prices for holidays here are quite reasonable.

Externally Maria Apartments (Cyprus / Ayia Napa) It is a cozy two-story building with a small territory. Near the hotel there is an outdoor pool with sun loungers and sun umbrellas. By the way, on the territory of the hotel you can use them absolutely free, unlike the beach. The hotel is considered medium in size and is part of the famous hotel chain in Cyprus - Tsokkos.

Description of rooms

Ayia Napa apartments have 74 rooms, they are divided into three main types: studio, single and double rooms. A maximum of 3 guests can stay in the studio, in single apartments - up to 4 people, in a double - up to 6.





Service and room service is at a high level. Regular cleaning is carried out by hotel staff 5 times a week. Each room has air conditioning, bathroom, shower, toilet, mini-bar, satellite and cable TV. By the way, tourists from Russia will enjoy a pleasant surprise - several Russian channels. A kitchenette, fridge, electric kettle or coffee maker, and tea / coffee maker are available upon request. You will have to pay for using a safe, a phone and Wi-Fi, a hairdryer can be obtained at the reception with a small deposit.

The furniture in the rooms of Maria Hotel Apartments (Cyprus) is new, the rooms are spacious and bright, the flooring is wooden.

As for the view from the rooms, most of the windows look onto the territory of Maria Apartments (Cyprus / Ayia Napa). A balcony or terrace is required.

Hotel infrastructure

As in many hotels, additional services are not free. Vacationers can use the laundry and dry cleaning services, Internet access, and car rental. Naturally, all this can be done for a fee. Free Wi-Fi can be found in the lobby bar, which is open 24 hours a day. By the way, for the services of vacationers - paid parking and currency exchange.





There are not so many entertainments in the hotel itself - live music, billiards, darts, table tennis, day and evening animation. There is luggage room.

For tourists who come to relax with their children, there is everything you need - a children's room, babysitting services (for a fee) and a playground. Chairs in the hotel restaurants with high backs, there is a children's menu. A baby cot is available upon request.

Hotel reviews

Analyzing the opinions of tourists about the place to spend a vacation, we can conclude that many people choose trips to Cyprus. Maria Apartments, reviews of which in most cases are only positive, are breaking records for the attendance of tourists from Russia.

Vacationers note the high level of service, friendly and friendly staff who warmly welcome all travelers. Many hotel workers speak good Russian.

Tourists are satisfied with their stay at the hotel, the rooms correspond to the photos provided by tour operators.

As for the kitchen, here, as they say, there are no comrades for the taste and color. To some, the assortment of dishes seemed scarce, someone only had breakfast at the hotel, so the issue of nutrition did not arise so sharply.

The proximity to the entertainment center of Ayia Napa for many tourists turned out to be an indisputable advantage when choosing a hotel, but there were also those vacationers who were not satisfied. The reason for the negative impression was the constantly heard sounds of music from restaurants.

In any case, everyone chooses a hotel based on their desires, tastes and preferences. Maria Apartments is an option for young and energetic tourists who crave adventure and fun.
