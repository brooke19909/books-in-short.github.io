ZTE Blade AF 3 - device features

Today we will discuss the phone ZTE Blade AF 3. Its characteristics will be described below. Before us is a cheap smartphone that costs a little more than 3,000 rubles, but is characterized by excellent performance and the presence of all the functions necessary for a modern mobile device.

Appearance



Phone ZTE Blade AF 3 is simple, without any frills. Available to almost any customer. Such a phone can be an excellent solution for schoolchildren, students and other representatives of the low-income population. Now let's talk about the appearance of the smartphone ZTE Blade AF 3. Design characteristics are presented below. It is both simple and modern. Buyers have the opportunity to purchase a model in black or white. The back cover of the phone is made with imitation of the appearance of the skin. The dimensions of the smartphone are quite compact. It can be conveniently used by both a child and an adult. The ZTE Blade AF 3 also weighs a little, which is very convenient. The case has a metal insert and looks pretty solid. Dropping the phone is not recommended, but by its appearance it can be said that minor drops for this model are not scary. The cover can be removed very easily, all buttons are conveniently located on the right side of the case. Connectors for connecting the charger and headphones are on top.

Speaker and cameras



Now let's talk about the sound of the ZTE Blade AF 3. We will consider the characteristics of the speaker further. It is quite loud and high quality. Also, the model is distinguished by the presence of a sensitive microphone. Thanks to him, the interlocutor will be able to perfectly hear the owner of the phone. The quality of the speaker and microphone is higher than that of many other cheap models.

Now let's talk about the screen of the phone ZTE Blade AF 3. Its characteristics are very worthy. The touch screen of the TFT 4.0 device has a diagonal of 4 inches. The image quality is quite high, the picture is excellently readable, as well as saturated with colors. The image can easily be viewed even in very bright conditions. The sensitivity of the sensor is excellent. The only drawbacks may be a relatively weak backlight, as well as the lack of a protective film for the device screen. Smartphone ZTE Blade AF3 may surprise customers with the presence of two cameras at once. The main camera with 5 megapixels allows you to receive photos of excellent quality. The flash allows you to shoot in the dark, it can also be used as a convenient flashlight. 2 megapixel front camera. It allows you to take high-quality selfies, as well as communicate via Skype. Given the low cost of the ZTE Blade AF3, the quality of the cameras is much higher than you would expect from such a cheap device.

Communication



Now let's talk about the communication capabilities that the ZTE Blade AF 3 smartphone provides. Communication media features include all the components needed for a modern mobile device. The smartphone supports 3G Internet at high speed. Wi-Fi networks and data transfer via Bluetooth are also available. The device supports satellite navigation, and also has a built-in radio. In the ZTE Blade AF3 smartphone, everything works quite quickly and without failures. The performance of the device is quite acceptable. Naturally, the phone will not pull too demanding on the game system, but its power is quite enough for the stable operation of any necessary applications and watching videos without freezes. The phone's performance is provided by 512 megabytes of RAM, as well as 4 cores of the Spreadtrum SC7731 processor, which has a capacity of 1.2 GHz. The built-in memory capacity for data storage is 4 GB. This is enough for media files and installing simple applications. But if you need more memory, you can easily increase it by installing additional media.

Platform



Works ZTE Blade AF3 based on the most popular operating system Android 4.4 among phone owners. The software platform has a very user-friendly interface and works stably. The phone received several factory installed applications that may seem quite useful to users. Most popular programs do not crash or freeze. ZTE Blade AF3 is the best option in terms of value for money. The buyer can easily get a multifunctional and productive device for only 3000 rubles. So we described the smartphone ZTE Blade AF 3. Its characteristics are presented in great detail.
