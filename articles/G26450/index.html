What kind of scratches and where do they come from?

As noted, their occurrence is due to frequent contact with various surfaces. Both in the pocket of the trousers and in the bag, with each movement, particles of materials interact with the screen of the smartphone.

Especially detrimental to lying down with the screen. For example, you come home and put the phone on a shelf or table, without thinking about its position in space. If this is done so often, then after a few weeks you will notice small scratches that are clearly visible in sunlight.

The second way. Toothpaste

It perfectly saves the smartphone from minor scratches and gives the screen a freshness. For those who did not know, it is generally very versatile. It not only gives the oral cavity a pleasant smell, but also perfectly cleans dirt.

The paste removes scratches from the phone, but only if they are shallow. Only regular toothpaste is suitable, none with microparticles or gel. They will disturb your screen more than they will help.

And so, the procedure is as follows:
