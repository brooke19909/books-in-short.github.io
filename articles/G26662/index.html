Water park in Phuket: description, photos and reviews of visitors

For many vacationers, it is important that the resort, in addition to the warm sea and the gentle sun, have the opportunity to relax in a good water park. That is why tourists traveling to Thailand ask tour operators the question: is there a water park in Phuket? The island has an excellent water amusement park, which is worth a visit to all vacationers.



A bit of history

Despite the fact that the island of Phuket is famous for its marvelous nature, luxurious well-groomed beaches, locals and guests have long been waiting for the appearance of a park here, in which you could slide off a dizzying water slide or swim in the pool. Their dream came true in 2010, when a new water park in Phuket - Splash Jungle was opened. Its name can be translated as "spray of the jungle" or "splash of the jungle."

A water park was built in Thailand (Phuket) by a company that was famous at the time for the construction of the famous Disneyland, so it is not surprising that the attractions there are of the highest level. The creators of this amazing building used the latest technologies in the field of construction, computer animation, the latest materials.



Today, any resident, as well as guests of the island of Phuket can visit this wonderful water amusement park, receiving vivid impressions that will long remain in their memory. Splash Jungle can accommodate more than two thousand people at a time. Water in the water park comes from an artesian well. Previously, it goes through several stages of purification. This avoids various skin irritations that can be caused by too much chlorine in the water.

The water park in Phuket is distinguished by its magnificent and diverse attractions, original designer finds and the unusual atmosphere that reigns here from the moment of opening.

This was appreciated by tourists from Europe, Russia and other countries. The entire space of the park is divided into several thematic sections. Each of them is dedicated to one country or part of the world. Continents reflect the culture of different countries at a certain point in their development.



Surely it will be interesting for vacationers to travel to the jungles of Africa, find themselves on the cold ice of Antarctica or try to unravel the mysterious world of the ancient Mayan civilization. Continents can be visited on a rubber ring on the Lazy River. Its length is three hundred thirty-five meters, and it carries its waters along a route that will show you the full beauty of each country.

However, do not hope that you will just calmly swim along the river. Behind each turn you can expect something new and unusual - a rumbling waterfall or picturesque grottoes and mysterious caves.

Rides

The rides at Splash Jungle are for park visitors of all ages. There are about fifteen of them. For adults, there are five large slides. Let's take a closer look at the two most popular attractions.

At Superbowl you will be offered to ride on a rubber ring on a tube. From it you will fall into a huge rotating bowl. You ride a little in it, and then a large funnel in the middle of the bowl will suck you in. A few seconds of flight, then fall - and you find yourself in the pool, which is located under the bowl.



Boomerango is a huge slide, shaped like the letter U. Guests come down from it on a cylinder (there are double cylinders). At first you will fall vertically down, and then rapidly soar up. And so in a row several times. Sometimes it seems that you can leave the limits of the attraction. But do not worry: everything is designed perfectly here. Children below 122 cm tall will not be allowed into such an attraction.

The water park in Phuket also has other slides, pipes (closed and open). In closed structures, absolutely nothing is visible, you can only guess where the top is and where the bottom is. In open pipes, the descent is calmer.

Today, many tourists come with children to Thailand. Waterpark (Phuket) has provided entertainment for younger guests. A special territory is equipped for them here. Here, children of any age will find suitable entertainment for themselves. On this territory there are three slides in a fabulous town with waterfalls, ladders, fountains and a huge barrel. As soon as it is filled with water, it turns over and the children find themselves under a grand shower. Parents can be calm: experienced instructors and lifeguards watch the children.



Sooner or later you will get tired of the sun and water. Therefore, the SPA-salon located in the park can please with a variety of procedures and massages performed by experienced masseuses. Here you can visit the sauna, which is made in the form of an Eskimo yurt.

Cafe

Everyone knows that after an active holiday you always want to eat. The water park in Phuket, the photo of which you can see in this article, offers guests to visit several cozy restaurants and cafes in the complex. Here you can have a bite to eat fast food or order a full dinner consisting of several dishes. It should be noted that all cafes and restaurants have a special children's menu. In addition, a huge assortment of cold and hot drinks is available here. If you have not tried the famous Thai milk tea (orange), do not miss the moment, be sure to order it. In addition to Thai dishes, guests are offered a variety of European dishes.

In the bar located here, adult visitors can order low-alcohol cocktails, beer. You should know that if you are going to visit the water park in Phuket, then you should not come here with your drinks and products. Those who violate this rule will pay a fine of one thousand baht (local currency).



Water park in Phuket: how to get there?

This wonderful complex is open seven days a week. He meets the first visitors at 10 a.m. And the last lovers of water rides leave him at 18.00.

Every morning, a transfer from the water park picks up tourists from the most popular beaches of the island. The first bus makes such a flight at 9.30 from Kata Beach. After that, he calls in Patong, Kamala, Karon, Mai Khao, Nai Yang. The second shuttle leaves from Kata Beach at 10.30.

You can call a taxi, which quickly takes you to the park, but in this case, the trip will cost you much more.

What to bring with you?

Experienced tourists recommend visiting the water park in Phuket in the morning. You should take a towel and swimsuit, slippers, soap, tanning products with you. Of course, they can be purchased on the territory of the complex, but there they are much more expensive.

In the locker rooms of the park you will be offered special lockers where you can leave personal items for storage. You should not take money and valuables with you. All visitors receive a bracelet on their hand, which shows the account number, so if you buy something, the amount will be entered into your number. You can pay it when you exit the park. You can pay by credit card and cash.

Guest reviews

According to the vast majority of tourists, this is a great place for a day's rest with family and children. All that is necessary for a good pastime is provided by the water park in Phuket. Reviews of vacationers about this place are simply enthusiastic. Many note that, despite its relatively small size, the complex is well planned, so it accommodates everything necessary for relaxation: attractions themselves, bars and cafes, a spa area, pools.

Some tourists are sure that all visitors to Thailand water park (Phuket) must visit. True, many vacationers believe that it has rather high prices, but when you consider that this island is the most expensive for recreation in the country, then everything falls into place. At the same time, it is not inferior in quality of entertainment to more famous structures of this type (in Singapore or Kuala Lumpur). Vacationers believe that if you have happened to be in other water parks, then you will not be disappointed, and if you have not been, you will get a lot of positive emotions from the rest.
