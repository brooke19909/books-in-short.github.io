Kyzikul Lake: tourist reviews

Beautiful places always have touching stories or legends. There is a legend about two Kyzikul lakes. Translated from Turkic - they are "beautiful lakes", and the Khakass claim that they are "girl's lakes." The legend tells of two girls, orphaned sisters, whom the stepmother hated. The girls decided that it would be better to drown, the further they would endure the stepmothers of bullying, and rushed into the river. The river has turned into two beautiful Kyzikul lakes. Rest on these lakes will be discussed in the article.

Lakes Location

There is a scientific point of view of the formation of these beautiful lakes. They appeared many millennia ago, during the melting of glaciers. Melting water filled the basins, including Minusinskaya. Kyzikul lakes (Small and Big) were formed precisely then. Both lakes are located in the Krasnoyarsk Territory, not far (36 km) from Minusinsk. Surrounds them is a pine-deciduous Insky pine forest.

Small Kyzykul oval with a surface of about 1.7 square meters. kilometers. 10 kilometers higher is the Big Kyzikul Lake. Both lakes are interconnected by the Kyzikulka river. The surface of this lake is 6 square meters. kilometers.

"Recreation on Lake Big Kyzikul"

Not far from the recreation center "Fisherman's House" is a beautiful suburban base "Zaimka on Lake Big Kyzikul". It is small, designed for 11 people, who are accommodated in 3 houses. Arriving at this base, tourists will feel the complete absence of civilization pressure and the approach to life in nature.

There is a sauna with a pier on the shore of Lake Kyzikul. You can use the order for a certain time. The cost of living includes the use of a guarded parking lot, barbecue, kitchen and utensils, as well as the use of four hours of electricity (generator). If you need more electricity, you can get it for an additional fee.



Basically, rest at this base is pleasant for those who want to live a few days without fuss and noise, enjoying the silence, the chatter of grasshoppers, the rustling surf of lake waves.

Pension "Small Kyzikul"

In the Minusinsky district on the Kyzikul lake there is a boarding house that works year-round and organizes family, children and corporate vacations. From the city of Minusinsk to Maly Kyzykul you can take a bus to the Malaya Inya stop. From it another 2 kilometers to the boarding house. Its territory occupies about 15 hectares, which include a pine forest and the coastal strip of Lake Maly Kyzikul.

The housing stock of the boarding house is represented by two two-story and two one-story brick buildings, a summer wooden house and a one-story wooden case. The rooms have everything for a comfortable stay in a relaxing environment. There are kitchen areas for cooking. Three meals a day only in the summer and is included in the price. In winter, vacationers are cooking in their kitchen areas.

Holiday Village "Salute"

On Lake Maly Kyzykul in a mixed pine-birch massif there is an excellent tourist base for schoolchildren and students "Salute". She works all year round. In the summer, mostly schoolchildren rest. The base at this time turns into a health camp. The hulls are located almost on the shore of Lake Kyzikul, where almost all summers children are sunbathing and swimming on the beach. In winter, it is a base for recreation. At this time, you can ride a skating rink, not far from the buildings, go jogging through the forest. All sports equipment is in the rental point "Salute". By the way, skating is free.



Tourists are accommodated in two brick buildings, heated in the winter. In the first building on the ground floor there are seven rooms designed for accommodation in them for 10 people (2 rooms), 4 people (3 rooms), 3 people (2 rooms). The same number of rooms on the second floor. The building also has a table tennis hall, a kitchen, one bathroom on the floor, and an inventory rental point. In the second building there are 10 four-bed rooms. Food is prepared in the kitchen, where there is everything necessary for this. Refrigerator, electric stove, microwave, electric kettles - all this is in the kitchen.
