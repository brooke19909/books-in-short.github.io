Hotel "Sea Breeze", Dzhemete: address, description, reviews

Guest House "Sea Breeze" is located on the sandy beach in the village of Dzhemete. His address: Pervy proezd, house 1. These are two modern 4-storey buildings with a good design and all the amenities created for a wonderful holiday on the Black Sea. Several entertainment complexes are located nearby, and the infrastructure of the place allows you to forget about the worries and enjoy your vacation at 100%.



Dzhemete village

In 1828, when the Turkish army was driven back from the Black Sea coast, the fortification of Dzhemete was founded. It served as a road protection from Anapa to Taman.

Until the end of the 90s of the 19th century, the village of Dzhemete remained only a military object, until one of the generals decided to grow nearby grapes. Since then, the territory has been successfully used to produce excellent wine, as well as an excellent holiday on the Black Sea.

The October Revolution changed the state of affairs in the village for several decades. But in the end, wine production was resumed, and the goods, as before, are of excellent quality.

The territory of Dzhemete has a good infrastructure. Here you can find a hotel to taste and a suitable price.



How to get there

There are 2 ways to get to the hotel: by plane and by train. Minibus to the stop "Market Dzhemete": No. 134, No. 114, No. 138. A meeting is possible at Vityazevo airport and at Anapa railway station. It is only necessary to previously warn the hotel employees about such a need. Service is available at an additional cost.

When you settle, you must have with you a passport and birth certificate for minor children.

The Sea Breeze in Dzhemete has a kitchen and organizes a buffet for its guests. The price for a 3-time visit to the restaurant is approximately 600-650 rubles per day. Meals can be included in the room rate when booking or paid on the spot.
