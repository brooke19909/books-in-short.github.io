Litsa Efi 2 *: hotel description and guest reviews

Relax at once on three seas, get a wonderful relaxation on the coast of the cleanest sea and sandy beaches, touch the ancient European culture - you can all get together while relaxing on the island of Crete (Greece).



The season here is quite long - from April to November. At the beginning and at the end of the season, there may be cool nights, but the water temperature is quite comfortable. Crete is the largest Greek island with a developed infrastructure. Island holidays, as a rule, are expensive. Therefore, we will get acquainted with the budget aparthotel Litsa Efi 2 *, to save money on living on entertainment, excursions and souvenirs.

Which of them are the most popular on the island for those who first decided to visit it?

  1. Knossos Palace. The memory of the Minoan civilization (2600-1100 BC). A visit to the palace will refresh knowledge about myths: King Minas, Ariadne, Theseus.
  2. A trip to the "Palm Paradise." Wai Town. Thanks to the picturesque landscapes and the beautiful coast, this place can be called just that. The road passes through the city of Sitia. It houses the famous monastery of the Holy Virgin Mary Toplou, which is also worthy of attention.
  3. Cave of Zeus. Located at an altitude of more than 1000 meters above sea level, this cave is considered one of the most beautiful on the island. In the cave you can see stalactite rocks (Mantle of Zeus), find out other sacred facts from the past of Ancient Greece.
  4. Drive to Water City Waterpark. A great opportunity to get an experience for both adults and children. Attractions are designed for all age groups.
