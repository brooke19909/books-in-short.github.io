Hotel Florida Beach Hotel 4 * (Ayia Napa): reviews, prices

The 4-star Florida Beach Hotel is located in Ayia Napa, Cyprus' most popular resort. Ayia Napa is located in the Famagusta area and used to be a simple fishing village, similar to most island settlements.





Ayia Napa is approximately 50 kilometers from Larnaca Airport. Weather conditions in Cyprus have a year-round tourist season. In summer, the air temperature can reach +40, and the sea - +30 degrees Celsius. In winter, the temperature of air and water does not fall below +20.

History of Famagusta

In ancient times, the locality of Famagusta was a popular resort in Cyprus - the city of Varosha. This continued until 1974, and then the Turkish troops captured Varosha, after which the Greek Cypriots living in the north of the island were forced to leave their native places and settle south. Today's Cyprus is divided into Greek and Turkish (northern) territories. Tourists who rest mainly in the Greek part of the island are not recommended independent trips to the north, only with tour guides to visit the sights.
