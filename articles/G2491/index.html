Plaza Grand Hotel 4 *, Egypt, Hurghada: reviews, photos

Holidays on the shores of the Red Sea, where you can enjoy plenty of bathing in warm water and sunbathe on beautiful beaches, has always attracted tourists. That is why many choose Hurghada to spend their holidays. Here, travelers are waiting for beautiful hotels that offer a wide variety of services that allow you to spend every minute excitingly. One of the places where any guest can feel the charm of a vacation in Egypt is the Plaza Grand Hotel 4 *. Based on the reviews of tourists who visited it, it can be understood that the holidays spent here will not make you regret your choice.

Hotel location

The popularity of the hotel Grand Plaza Hotel (Hurghada), reviews of which in most cases are positive, is due to its good location. It is located on the shore of a piece-made bay, which captivates with its beauty. The sea is within a few minutes walk, so guests can enjoy swimming without any difficulties during the day, and return to their rooms if they wish.

The international airport is only seven kilometers away, so the road to the hotel does not take much time. You can get on the provided transport. If you want a more respectable ride to the place of residence, you can order a transfer, for which you need to pay.

Hurghada is 10 km away. Since this town is a tourist town, travelers will be interested to visit it. Here you can admire various shows, among which the most popular singing fountains and performances of "A Thousand and One Nights." Fans of shopping in Hurghada offer their services to numerous shops and shopping centers. According to tourists, prices even for branded goods will pleasantly surprise. Hotel guests can reach the city by systematically running public transport or by taxi.





For tourists who have long dreamed of visiting Egypt, the Grand Plaza Hotel is an excellent choice. The rest spent here will be remembered for a long time and will allow you to get a charge of vivacity and positive.

Description of the hotel

From the first glance at the magnificent gardens surrounding the Grand Plaza Hotel 4 *, you can understand how much attention is paid here to the care of the territory. According to vacationers who had the pleasure of visiting this heavenly place, the staff throughout the day is engaged in the improvement of lawns of shrubs and trees. Their work is rewarded with an amazing picture: the hotel buildings are buried in luxurious greenery.

Guests will enjoy the beautiful, always trimmed lawns, decorative trees of strict forms. Magnificent palm trees spread everywhere, thanks to which the area is filled with oriental flavor.



Walking around is very nice. Tiled tracks are everywhere. The hotel made sure that during the walk the guests were not exhausted from the heat: all the passages are equipped with peculiar arbors, which are also buried in shrubs and flowers.

Near the hotel there is a huge pool. Guests here can relax on the sun loungers located in the shade of umbrellas. There are always plenty of free places, and there are no complaints about the lack of sunbeds. The pool water is clean and free of bleach. In the morning, the employees of the Plaza Grand Hotel 4 * prepare a pond, carrying out cleaning work. The hotel also has a winter pool, in which the water is heated. Guests can also make use of the nearby Grand Plaza Resort, which has water slides.

Room Amenities and Service

In the rooms of the Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * you can use the new plasma TV, safe, electric kettle. There is also a minibar with paid drinks. Only 1.5 liters of water are given free of charge. Central air conditioning. There are no complaints about his work, but the air conditioning system is quite noisy. The bathroom has a shower or bath, hairdryer. As for toiletries, they can sometimes be forgotten at the hotel. Only after applying to the reception, vacationers are provided with everything they need.

Telephone and internet in rooms are available at an additional cost. But, according to vacationers, Wi-Fi in the rooms is practically absent. You can use the internet only in the hotel lobby.

Each room has a balcony or terrace, from which you can enjoy sea, garden or pool views. Somewhat spoils the picture of the construction, which is carried out near the hotel. Tourists are advised not to pay attention to it and enjoy the beauty of the rampant greens.



Fitted with tiled flooring. There are also oriental-style rugs. The furniture is not new, but it looks well-groomed. Tourists respond very well about comfortable and soft beds.

Rooms are cleaned daily. Often the quality of the cleaners is not satisfactory. But still, in some reviews of tourists, the presence of dust and hair on the furniture and floor is indicated even after the cleaners visit the room. Linen is also changed daily.

In the rooms you can find insects. Administrators of the Plaza Grand Hotel 4 * warn guests that food cannot be stored in the rooms, as this attracts ants. If you ignore this remark, then the insects will really quickly detect the presence of provisions, and then only processing the number with chemicals will help them cope.

Hotel infrastructure

The hotel Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada) created the most comfortable conditions that will allow you to spend your holidays in a fun and profitable way. Holidaymakers who prefer to have their own personal transport at hand are provided with parking spaces at the hotel. For trips here you can rent executive cars for an additional fee.

For people who have arrived in the country on business, the hotel provides the opportunity to use the services of a business center or meeting room. If you need to organize a business event, you can count on the full support of the administration. Here you can use the services of a secretary, but only for an additional fee.

Shopping lovers will not be disappointed either. On the territory of the hotel there are various shops where various souvenirs, oils and other goods are offered. There is also a hairdresser.

Take care of your appearance will help in the beauty salon. All services here are provided only for money. The hotel guests can visit the office of massage therapists, who own different massage techniques. No less enjoyable is a visit to the Turkish bath or jacuzzi.



You can put your clothes in order at a local laundry or dry cleaning service. They can also iron clothes at the request of the client. These services are provided only for money.

If you have health problems, tourists can consult doctors who provide medical care around the clock. Their services, including advice, are provided at an additional cost.

Catering

Dining at the Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada) causes a lot of discrepancies in the reviews of tourists. According to some guests, the menu here is absolutely monotonous and very few fruits. Others argue that no one will remain hungry, and the dishes are all completely edible. But regarding drinks, guests express the same opinion. Soft drinks, which are served free of charge, are prepared from powders. At the same time, no one observes the observance of proportions, which affects quality. Alcohol is not advised to drink at all, as it is very diluted, and its use can lead to serious poisoning. The only drink that all vacationers liked was beer.

Meals are provided by the main hotel of the restaurant, which is located in the main building. The hall is well ventilated, so you can eat here in fairly comfortable conditions. The design of the room is simply mesmerizing: everywhere there is a lot of greenery, and through the large glass wall you can contemplate a beautiful view. Free tables in the hall can be found even in high season with full occupancy of the hotel. The staff copes well with their duties, so queues are very rare here.



The hotel has two more restaurants, one of which operates on the A la Carte system. This is an Italian restaurant serving various types of pizza and pasta. Drinks and snacks are available at the three bars that are open at the hotel. The quality of food in them is not satisfactory.

Beach vacation

The main attraction that the Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * has at its disposal is a very cozy and beautiful beach. This is a truly unique place. A special fence that separates the bay from the sea prevents garbage from entering the beach, which is why it is always very clean.

There are always enough sun loungers on the beach, so vacationers do not need to occupy free places in the morning. In windy weather, special protective barriers are issued. There is also a dressing room on the beach where towels are provided.

The beach is sandy, so the kids really like to play here. The depth of the bay is suitable for swimming adults and children. There are stones at the bottom, but you can swim without protective shoes, since there is practically no chance of injury. Because of the fence, the inhabitants of the Red Sea rarely swim to the shore, but still you can see small fish here.



To snorkel and admire the coral groves and wildlife of the sea, vacationers can walk along the constructed 70-meter pontoon to deeper places.

There is one small minus for a beach holiday at the Grand Plaza Hotel. Reviews from tourists suggest that local merchants can be very persistent in offering their goods to vacationers. To protect yourself from annoying offers, you need to install special red flags on deckchairs, which can be obtained with towels. These attributes tell the locals that the tourist is not interested in any purchases and is left alone.

Conditions for children

The hotel Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * (Hurghada) offers very good leisure facilities for the youngest guests. Upon arrival, parents at the reception may request a cot to be delivered to the room. For such a service surcharge is not provided. If necessary, an experienced nanny can look after the kids, but this will cost a certain amount.

Children can spend time on the playground very exciting. Everything is equipped here for a fun pastime: there are swings, houses, various toys. In addition, animators constantly come up with contests and fun that the young audience really likes.



Guests 6-12 years old can engage in a mini-club, working at the hotel, where qualified teachers will teach them all kinds of needlework. They have a lot of fun here, learning to paint various objects or make souvenirs with their own hands.
