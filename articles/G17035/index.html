Seven-pointed star made of paper: volumetric diagram

A simple craft for Christmas or any other holiday can be done using completely simple and inexpensive materials. One of these straightforward projects is a paper star. The volumetric scheme allows you to make a very cute craft, quickly and without much effort.



DIY paper decoration

Such a decoration as a paper star, the three-dimensional scheme of which will be described in this article, will be able to decorate not only the Christmas tree. This can be a very appropriate item on a handmade gift box.



Quick and easy

For the manufacture you will need the following materials and tools:

  • medium density paper;
  • scissors;
  • glue stick;
  • clothespins or stationery clips;
  • beads to decorate the center of the star (optional).




Choose size

How is a star made of paper? The volumetric design scheme creates a small 3-D effect, and the picture is already not so flat. First, you need to determine the size, and then cut out seven identical strips cut at the edges at an angle of about 45 degrees.

Step 1

We start making the petals. To do this, put a strip of paper with the narrower side up. Then we put glue on the right end and press the ends of the paper strip, laying them on top of each other.

Step 2

So that the ends do not diverge, you can add more glue and additionally fasten it with an ordinary clothespin and a stationery clip, which will hold the end parts until the glue completely dries. Such simple manipulations must be done with all the ends of which our paper star will consist. The volumetric scheme suggests making a seven-point model. When the details are ready, you can proceed to step number 3, their connection together.

Step 3

After complete drying, you need to glue together all the petals, arranging them in a circle so that the tops of the paper cones form the central part of the star.



So, by connecting the two elements, gluing their sides, you also need to temporarily fix them with clothespins.



So, with the gradual bonding of all the elements, an improvised sun of petals and clothespins will be obtained.



Step 4

After all sides are firmly glued to each other, you can remove the clothespins.



Step 5

Well, that star of paper is ready. The seven-point volumetric scheme can be made in a variety of shades and sizes. As an additional decoration, you can glue several small beads or sparkles in the center of the star. Color can also be varied using different shades of paper stripes in the same star. This jewelry can be safely used as an element on a gift box, Christmas tree decoration or decoration for anything.

Paper Star - 3D

Crafts made of paper in the hands of the master can be just amazing. As for the stars, you can make an entire galaxy and decorate it with a room, for example, at Christmas.



Such volumetric stars are made very simple. Need paper, glue and scissors. You can experiment with different colors and textures to create a variety of effects. Finished stars can be decorated with glitter and tiny buttons. Having made simple geometric measurements and following simple instructions, you can make quite nice 3-D figures.



Fantasy to help you!

In the work for measurement, you can use a ruler. To draw a circle, you need a pair of compasses or a simple glass.

At the same distance from each other, draw points on the circle, then connect them with lines, but not adjacent, but through one. Thus, two triangles are formed inside the circle - a kind of pictogram. Then you need to cut the shape. To give volume, it is necessary to make bends, as if dividing all the small triangles in the figure into two halves.



This is how a one-sided star is made of paper. The volumetric scheme for the Christmas tree is done in the same way, only two halves are glued together. Having shown a share of imagination, you can decorate with such crafts not only the walls and the New Year tree. Products can perfectly serve as elements of gift decor. By attaching to them a piece of multi-colored ribbon or thread, you can decorate a gift bottle of wine with such home-made stars, as well as form a whole colorful garland. Any volumetric star made of paper can be decorated with additional drawings and applications.
