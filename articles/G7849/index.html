Hotel Restaurants

Fans of delicious and quality food will be pleased with their stay at the Dolphin Deluxe Resort. Turkey, like any other country, has unusual culinary traditions. You can get acquainted with dishes of different nations of the world in the following institutions:

  • In the Turkish restaurant you can not only enjoy traditional dishes, but also immerse yourself in the enchanting atmosphere of Ottoman palaces. A similar effect is created thanks to the unique thematic interior, as well as the authentic musical accompaniment.
  • Margarita is a cozy Mexican restaurant with panoramic windows overlooking the pool. The sharpness and spice of spices emphasize red, orange and terracotta colors, which are basic in the interior.
  • Mr. Fish Fish Restaurant is a favorite place for seafood fans. The facility is located on the outdoor terrace overlooking the palm garden. Furniture made from natural materials creates an atmosphere of unity with nature.
  • If you are a fan of pizza and pasta, visit the Italian restaurant Dolce Vita. Here you can enjoy not only delicious dishes, but also a warm homely atmosphere.
  • Original dishes can be found in the Chinese restaurant. You will be able to feel the atmosphere of this mysterious country thanks to the thematic design and characteristic music.
  • The Japanese restaurant "Tepenyaki" offers you to try many types of rolls, sushi and sashimi, as well as traditional soups and other dishes. The facility is located outdoors and is well suited for romantic dates.
  • Mediterranean - An elegant restaurant specializing in Mediterranean cuisine. The luxurious room is decorated in white and blue colors, which creates a feeling of the ocean breeze. You can also eat on the outdoor terrace, located right in the garden.
