5 Star All Inclusive Vietnam Hotels. Is it realistic to find a replacement for your beloved Turkey?

Vietnam is an ideal holiday destination for tourists who appreciate the magnificent natural landscapes and the possibility of a relaxing pastime. The high level of service in hotels of the highest categories is noted by many travelers. However, there are relatively few complexes with five stars in this country, they can be literally counted on the fingers, and 5 star hotels in Vietnam (All Inclusive) are generally few. Consider some of them located on the coast.

Riviera Beach Resort and SPA (5 stars)

The list titled "The Best Hotels in Vietnam, 5 Stars, All Inclusive" is rightfully headed by the Riviera, as this is the only hotel that works fully according to the favorite food system of Russians.



The complex itself is completely new; it was opened in 2015. At the moment, he offers tourists great prices for accommodation in the framework of the action to attract attention. That is why in the near future it is worth a visit.

It is successfully located on the sparsely populated Baizai beach remote from Nha Trang with fine white sand and a gentle entrance to the sea. Its total territory is more than ten hectares! It is worth knowing that you will have to get at least half an hour to all the main infrastructure. However, travelers will not suffer from this, as the hotel has an uncountable amount of entertainment.

Entertainment at the hotel

  1. For children, the Angel Club works from 9 am to 8 pm with Russian-speaking girls-educators.
  2. Adult tourists can head to the modern gym.
  3. Throughout the entire complex, wireless internet is available around the clock.
  4. All 5 star Vietnam hotels (All Inclusive), photos of which confirm this fact, have a majestic well-groomed green area, and the Riviera is no exception. This will be a significant plus when choosing a complex for families - there will always be where to walk in the evening or during the day.
  5. There is a private spa complex with saunas, baths and a jacuzzi.
  6. On the beach you can enjoy water sports and ride a catamaran or a water bike.
  7. In your free time there is a unique opportunity to go on an underwater tour with an instructor.
  8. The hotel has two spacious swimming pools.

Service in the complex

The management of the Riviera Hotel cares about the comfort of its guests:

  • 4 times a day a free shuttle bass goes to the center of Nha Trang;
  • corporate rides can rent a beautiful conference room with a total capacity of up to 100 people;
  • There is an own store with all the little things necessary for traveling.

Since the Riviera is on the list of "Vietnam Hotels, 5 stars, all inclusive", it would be wrong to say nothing about food.





For tourists there are two main restaurants and a bar with refreshments and snacks. In addition to the main food and snacks, tourists can take unlimited soft alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.

Magnificent Winperl

Another place of honor in the list of "Hotels of Vietnam, 5 stars, all inclusive" is the chain of hotels "Winperl", which are located on the island of the same name near Nha Trang. Tourists can get here by cable car.

There are buildings for every taste: a bungalow type away from the resettlement of children with the possibility of a relaxing holiday, there are just big new buildings with active animation for kids in the area.





One of the features of the complex is the presence on this same island of a huge amusement park "Winperl", which is visited by travelers from all over the country.

The all-inclusive tour package also includes a visit to this park in an unlimited amount of time. Here you can find an amazing show of fountains, a dolphinarium, an amusement park and a quality water park with new slides.

Since this is a new country for the tourism segment, Vietnam's 5 star (All Inclusive) hotels, reviews of which are most often positive, are necessarily newly built buildings with the highest quality service in all of Asia. Therefore, you can choose absolutely any hotel: it will be difficult to make mistakes and regret your choice.
