How to switch to another MegaFon tariff plan and save on calls?

Even if the subscriber chose the most suitable tariff when connecting, then all the same, over time, you may have to change it. And there can be many reasons for this: the needs of the client have changed, more interesting offers from the company have appeared and much more. But, before changing the tariff, MegaFon offers to pick it up on the site on its own.



To do this, you can use the special calculator "Choose a tariff". Here you need to specify the average duration of your calls and the numbers of which operators most often make calls, as well as the frequency of use of additional services. After some thought, the program will give you a list of suitable rates. If you carefully read all the options, you can choose the best offer.

After choosing the appropriate option, it remains only to learn about how to switch to another MegaFon tariff. If the subscriber is absolutely sure of his choice, he has no questions, then it is easier and more convenient to do it yourself. This can be done either through the Service Guide, or through USSD * 105 * 20 #. You can also leave a request on the company's website to change the tariff plan. Whatever method is chosen in order to switch to another MegaFon tariff, the change will occur no earlier than the next day according to Moscow time. And if at the old tariff prepaid traffic was provided, then only from the beginning of next month. Before changing the tariff, it is worth clarifying whether all previously connected services will work. If they are not supported by the new tariff, they will be automatically disabled. If the tariff has already stopped connecting, then the change is not possible. And most importantly: since each subscriber can switch to another MegaFon tariff, this service is provided absolutely free of charge, regardless of the chosen method of change.

But still, do not rush to change the tariff. You can reduce your costs in another way by simply connecting tariff options. Today the company offers its subscribers more than a dozen of them. They allow you to optimize the existing tariff for your needs, sometimes even short-term ones. For example, if you need unlimited Internet for a day or you need discounts for calls in roaming, there is no need to find out how to switch to another MegaFon tariff.





You can simply connect the desired option. And as soon as the need for such services becomes unnecessary - disconnect. The tariff will remain unchanged.
