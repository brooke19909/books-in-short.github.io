Yacht Club "Admiral": the beach. Yacht club "Admiral" (Moscow): reviews, prices

The Admiral Yacht Club (Moscow) is the first water sports recreational center in our country organized according to the European type. On its territory combined fresh air with the benefits of civilization, classic urban entertainment and proximity to nature.

general information





The Admiral yacht club, a beach, swimming pools, a night club with numerous halls that will not leave anyone indifferent, is a cozy corner with a view of the Klyazma reservoir. In recent years, it has become very popular with residents of the capital. The range of services that the yacht club Admiral (Dmitrovskoye Shosse) offers is quite wide. Here you can relax in the great outdoors, take advantage of various attractions and entertainments, eat deliciously and generally just have a good weekend.

Infrastructure

The Admiral Marine Yacht Club, whose address is the seventh kilometer of Dmitrovskoye Shosse, is located outside the village of Gribki. You can get to it according to the signs, having reached the bridge over the Klyazma and turning right at the sign. Just two hundred meters away, a huge territory opens up on which the Admiral yacht club is equipped.

A sandy beach along the entire coastline, piers, summer parking for boats, jet skis and yachts, as well as equipped moorings - this is not a complete list of services that can be used on the territory of the complex. There is also a sink, a repair shop for maritime transport. There is the opportunity to take advantage of the service, raising and launching of yachts and boats, it is also possible here to store snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles. The Admiral Yacht Club, whose beach is considered one of the best on Klyazma, is guarded around the clock. Customers are guaranteed complete confidentiality.

Residence





One of the main buildings of the club is the Admiral Marina Club fleet. This modern hotel complex is located completely on the water. In addition to rooms of various categories, a restaurant and a sauna are provided in it. The location of the fleet is chosen in such a way that from the windows customers can see both the Klyazma reservoir and the Admiral yacht club itself.

For visitors, there are other - more original options for living. These are the cabins on the ship under the name "Admiral", as well as small cottages that are located in the forest in the club. In them, as well as in the fleet shop and on the ship, round-the-clock service.

Residents of such a metropolis as Moscow often need silence and tranquility, which, combined with clean air, charge for a long time with vivacity and energy. And it is precisely this opportunity that the hotel complex of the Admiral Club provides, where many people come, running away from the city rush to enjoy nature. And it is possible here at any time of the year.

The Admiral Beach Hotel complex pleasantly surprises with the variety of its number of rooms. Guest houses located in the park area are provided for lovers of privacy and tranquility, and respectable apartments on the ship are preferred by those who like to relax in unusual conditions. In total, the hotel fund of the Admiral Yacht Club has twenty-three rooms of the categories "economy", "standard" and "luxury".

Holidays

Reviews

Opinions of visitors to the Admiral Yacht Club are mostly positive. Guests liked the classic-style rooms, well-designed grounds, swimming pools and, of course, the beach, which saves many Muscovites in hot weather.

Klyazma reservoir - one of the favorite places of recreation for residents of the capital - is beyond praise. The club staff was also highly appreciated; real professionals work here.

Some reviews indicate that people are somewhat annoyed by high prices, but Admiral is the first of its kind water sports center for recreation, and the first one, as you know, cannot be cheap.
