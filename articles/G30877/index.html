How to knit a large knit sweater

Hand-made jerseys have always been in fashion. It is especially important to have in your wardrobe such cozy warm clothes in spring and autumn. Consider how to weave a large, viscous female sweater using knitting needles . The proposed model can be changed, refreshing the picture with a variety of braids and delicate motifs.





Large sweaters are the trend of the season

Sweatshirts and jumpers with a relief pattern were always in the wardrobe of a fashionable woman in a place of honor. Last season, women's and men's large knit sweaters also remained at the peak of popularity, eclipsing even thin leather jackets with their extravagance. And surely in the near future the situation will not change. Due to the variety of patterns that simply amazes the imagination, and practicality, knitwear will also be loved by fashionistas. Everyone will find the most acceptable option for themselves. So, adherents of sports style give their preference, as a rule, to elongated models in the form of tunics. And more sophisticated people flaunt with pleasure in fitted mini-cardigans decorated with weaving, ribbons and original braids. In sweaters of large knitting, regardless of the model, the pattern used, the combination of textures and ornaments, you can always look at the same time simple, elegant and fashionable.

A variety of drawings allows you to create unique models





For a beginner needlewoman, it usually seems very difficult to make large knit sweaters. The schemes, which, as a rule, always accompany a detailed description of the work, make it very easy and quick to understand the order and technology of pattern execution. More experienced craftswomen can skillfully combine several motives at the same time. This allows you to create real masterpieces of weaving with intricate ornaments. What is the main feature of all products with large viscous? Which of the motives clearly characterize these models? The first thing that usually catches your eye immediately is the presence of a different type of braids. This can be a single tourniquet located only in the center of the front of the product, or several similar weaves that symmetrically adorn the entire surface of the model, including sleeves. In the absence of experience and sufficient skills, try to start with a simple product, a detailed description of which is presented below.





Fashionable women's large viscous sweater: description of the ornament and calculation of loops

The proposed model is quite simple to implement. The manufacturing techniques of the shelves and backs differ from each other only in the design of their necks. The model is decorated with a wide braid of ordinary large weaving. In the center, the arrangement of two symmetrical patterns looks like a wide ornament. The same is repeated around the edges. In the intervals used "rubber" knitting. Despite the simplicity of execution, the product looks very voluminous and elegant. The canvas, on which the braids are present, is obtained, as a rule, in width somewhat narrower than the usual one, made with a front or wrong surface. Therefore, to calculate the loops, perform a small workpiece consisting of a single braid and several gum rapports. In this way, you can easily calculate the desired number of loops for a product of any size. You can make the fabric of the desired width by adding rubber bands at the edges from the braids.

The technology of the shelves and backs

  2. Then, on the front side, proceed to the ornament: 1 chrome., 3 rapport gum from 2 out. and 1 liter., 2 out., braid of 10 liters., 3 rapport gum from 2 out. and 1 liter., 2 out., braid from 10 liters., 1 out., braid from 10 liters., 3 rapport of gum from 2 out. and 1 liter., 2 out., braid of 10 liters., 3 rapport gum from 2 out. and 1 liter., 2 out., 1 chrome.
  3. Weave every 10 rows.
  4. After completing 25 cm of openwork fabric, knit the armhole, gradually closing 10 loops on both sides.

  6. The back is made out in this way almost imperceptibly. Start tying the neckline after 25 cm.
  7. The shoulders close straight, without lowering.
