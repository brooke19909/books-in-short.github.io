Unique design

Thanks to an interesting modern design, this object of the park has turned into a light installation. These bright luminous towers greet guests at the entrance to the rink in Sokolniki.

Children's groups

People often come to the rink in Sokolniki with the whole family. So that the kids are not afraid to fall, there are special walkers in the form of a penguin on skates. The child clings to him, and it is easier for him to move on a slippery surface. Also, classes for teaching children ice skating were organized here. It is called the training center "Snake." Accept children from 4 years. Experienced trainers help children gain confidence on the ice, learn to take their first steps, turns, fall correctly, so as not to hurt, keep balance. Services are also available for adult beginners.

Equipment rental

People who do not have skates can come to the rink in Sokolniki. All the necessary equipment can be rented directly on the territory of the ice entertainment complex. Hire and sharpening skates will cost 200 rubles in two hours. For schoolchildren, students and senior citizens there is a system of discounts. For young children who are uncertainly skating, you can take protective equipment: a helmet, knee pads, elbow pieces, gloves that protect your wrists.





Sharpening your skates will cost 300 rubles, and if they are completely new, they will charge you 500. You can rent a penguin or other educational toy for 200 rubles.

Services

Ice skating rink (Sokolniki) provides an opportunity, without taking off your skates, to warm yourself over a cup of fragrant tea or coffee in a tent, which is located directly on the ice. There is also the opportunity to relax, without removing the equipment, in an Italian cafe called "Mercato". There, in addition to hot drinks, you can eat several types of freshly baked pizza.

Benefits and Cost

You can come to Sokolniki to the rink from 10.00 to 24.00. Since there are fewer visitors on weekdays , the cost of the admission ticket is only 250 rubles. And when the influx of visitors is large, then the price increases. For example, on Friday, on weekends and on holidays, the cost of one ticket will be 350 rubles.



For the preferential category of citizens has a discount, but only on weekdays. They will have to pay 150 rubles. Children are free from 3 to 6 years. Pensioners can go to Sokolniki to the rink for free only once a week, on Tuesdays from 10 to 12 and from 13 to 15 hours. You can also visit the skating rink for large families, orphans, and disabled people for free. But you must submit the necessary supporting documents.

How to get there

The skating rink is located in Moscow at the address: Sokolnichy Val Street , Building 1, 1. It is best to get to the park by metro to the Sokolniki station. Then to the park 5 minutes on foot along the alley to the entrance. It is approximately 400 meters.
