Where is Khrustalnoye Lake located

The beautiful is often nearby, only you need to pay attention to it. Not far from Komsomolsk-on-Amur in the Khabarovsk Territory, in a picturesque place, the amazing Khrustalnoye Lake is located.

The former industrial quarry was turned into an artificial reservoir, which received a beautiful name, and a recreation center was later named. Find this place is not difficult, because it is located between the village of Solnechny and the city of Komsomolsk-on-Amur. Tourists, wondering where the Khrustalnoye lake is located, always get an exhaustive answer, because the local population has long chosen it for hanging out with close people. Explaining in detail how to get there, well-wishers help any vacationer to get to the picturesque shore. Residents of the city and surrounding villages are happy to spend their free time here, regardless of the time of year.
