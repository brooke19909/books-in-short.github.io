Tyagachev ski club: winter and summer sports, year-round recreation

The ski center, which we will talk about in this article, is the oldest in the suburbs. Here, traditionally held training on snowboarding and skiing of the Russian national teams. Many eminent athletes practiced here on the basis of the children's and youth sports school of the Olympic reserve, which was opened in the sixties at the highest point of the Klinsko-Dmitrov Ridge. Only 40 km from Moscow are 95 hectares, fully equipped for many types of active not only winter, but also all-weather recreation. And all this is the Leonid Tyagachev ski club in Shukolovo, with whom we hasten to introduce you more closely.

Winter sports: downhill, cross-country skiing, snowboarding

One of the priority areas of the complex is winter recreation. Season - November 28-April 15. Let's see what the ski club in Shukolovo can offer skiers and snowboarders at this time:

  • 7 illuminated tracks of various difficulty levels (for children, very simple, simple, complex, very complex) with lengths from 42.5 to 415 meters. The difference in height is 91-187 meters. Meet FIS standards, fully suitable for slalom competitions.
  • A special track for the snowboard.
  • 5 towing cable lifts and a baby lift.
  • Possibility of evening and night skiing.
  • Children's ski town.
  • Possibility of skiing for beginners - independent and under the guidance of base instructors.




The pride of the Tyagachev Club ski resort is considered to be a snowboard park built with the participation of the Da Industries team - Urban Park. The structures are located on a separate slope, and a lift leads to them. A team of experienced shapers constantly monitors what is happening, the entrance to Urban Park is free for visitors. Here you can try your hand at the following figures:

  • seven-meter springboard roll;
  • spine;
  • nine-meter direct rail;
  • direct boxing - 7.5 m;
  • double-kick boxing - 12 m;
  • bonk;
  • double king rail - 11 m.

In the Tyagachev ski club there is a great opportunity to become a skier or snowboarder under the guidance of coaches of the Olympic reserve school, as well as titled athletes - former and practitioners. The training is conducted according to the Austrian system. There is also a rental service for visitors, where they are waiting for modern equipment of foreign production.

The ski resort is pleased to welcome other fans of winter holidays:

  • A free 5-kilometer trail for lovers of both classic and cross-country skiing. A five meter wide ski track is partially lit and completely free. It is regularly serviced by snow groomers. There is a rental workshop, where you can paraffin, polish, and incise your skis. For beginners, experienced instructors will help.
  • Snowmobile and ATV rental. You can either rush along the paved route, and go to conquer the dashing off-road.
  • For skate and hockey lovers - an ice town with the rental of necessary equipment.

Summer sport

Ski club "Gorki Tyagacheva" is open in the summer. What can amateurs of active lifestyle at this time:

  • Adventure in a 12-step rope park.
  • Karting site.
  • Fishing. Carps are constantly fed in the lake, so a good catch is provided. It is possible to rent fishing equipment. Only fishing here is paid.
  • Horse routes of varying difficulty.
  • Playgrounds for volleyball, basketball, tennis, beach volleyball.
  • Pneumatic shooting gallery.
  • Health trails with varied coverage. Walking on them barefoot, depending on the terrain, has a diverse effect on the body - tonic, soothing, exciting.




Recreation - individual and corporate

At any time of the year, you can just come to the Tyagachev ski club to relax from the bustle of the city. You can spend your time in a variety of ways:

  • Swimming in the outdoor pool 14x6 meters with showers, changing rooms and sun loungers, in the indoor pool 6x8 meters with lighting, hydromassage, waterfall. And after swimming, you can take a walk in the conservatory.
  • Sauna with dry steam with showers, tea room.
  • Russian, American billiards, bowling.
  • Karaoke bar.
  • Children's room.
  • Mangal grounds: a gazebo by the lake and in the meadow, a large tent that seats up to 50 people.
  • Contact mini zoo.
  • Beach by the lake.

It is also possible to hold weddings with a celebration in a large tent near the lake or in a restaurant and fireworks program. For young people - a deluxe room, for guests - a hotel and cozy chalet houses.

Opportunities for corporate relaxation in the Tyagachev ski club:

  • a platform for teambuilding;
  • conference rooms for 300 and 90 people;
  • banquet hall for 350 visitors;
  • multifunctional sports ground;
  • hotel complex for 156 residents;
  • restaurants for 70 and 100 visitors.

Accommodation and meals

Guests of the Tyagachev ski club can choose one of several accommodation options:

  • In the cottage: 2-, 3-bed room.
  • In the hotel: 2-, 3-, 4-, 5-, 7-, 10-bed room.




It is difficult to stay hungry in the complex: two restaurants, cafes, bars, fast food - Russian, European, home-made cuisine, an Irish pub.

Contact Information

Tyagachev Ski Club is located in the Dmitrovsky district of the Moscow region, near the village of Shukolovo. Access options are as follows:

  • Public transport: electric train from Savelovsky station to the Tourist platform. Next take a taxi to the club for 3 hours.
  • Personal transport: move along Dmitrovskoe highway up to 39 km from MKAD. Then turn left to Shukolovo. Follow the signs for the club.




Tyagachev Ski Club in Shukolovo is not only an inexpensive and modernly equipped area for skiers and snowboarders. This is a favorite pastime of supporters of various sports, as well as lovers of rural recreation.
