Best hotels in Punta Cana (Dominican Republic)

Many beach lovers are admirers of the beautiful beaches of the Dominican Republic. Punta Cana Resort invites guests to enjoy relaxing on the bounty-style beaches. Perhaps nowhere else in the world can one find such pure white sand and green palms bent over water. If you decide to go to the resort, then you will probably be interested in information about the best hotels in Punta Cana. We want to talk about them in our article.

A little bit about the resort ...

If you decide to go on vacation to the Dominican Republic, then you will surely choose the most famous and popular resort of the country - Punta Cana. On its territory is the largest number of hotels and all kinds of tourist attractions. The resort has gained such popularity due to its stunning snow-white beaches, stretching thirty kilometers along the coast. Any tourist who is planning a vacation will not be bothered by comprehensive information about the best hotels in Punta Cana. This will help you make the right choice and enjoy your vacation.

A feature of the resort is the weather. It practically remains unchanged throughout the year. The thermometer here is rarely less than thirty degrees. Even the rainy season, which lasts from May to July, is not an obstacle to relaxation, because the rains, although strong, are short-lived.



The best hotels in Punta Cana are large complexes with many attractions. As a rule, hotels offer active animation, dance courses, casinos and other entertainment events. The best hotels in Punta Cana are equipped with a la carte restaurants, several pools, bars. Residential buildings of complexes represent two- and three-story bungalows. Hotels can also consist of separate villas.

Discussing the best hotels in Punta Cana, it is worth recalling their features. Very often in one territory several complexes of the same network are located at once. As a rule, all of them are designed for holidaymakers with different financial capabilities. So, for example, the five-star Grand Palladium Bavaro Resort is located on the territory of the Grand Palladium network. But the Luxuri Bahia Principle Barn hotel 5 * is located on a common territory with other institutions of the Bahia Principle Hotels & Resort chain, but it does not accept guests under eighteen years of age. All hotels have a clear focus. And this is worth considering when choosing the best hotel in Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) for vacation. It is also worth considering the features of local beaches. Of course, all of them are good, but still differ from each other: on some it is often windy, why there are high waves, while others are more calm for relaxation, because they are protected by reefs. For your first acquaintance, we have selected for you the 10 best hotels in Punta Cana.

Five Star Hotels

The best hotels of Putna Kana 5 * - these are world-class establishments. They fully meet all standards. Many of them are representatives of world famous networks. The hotels are surrounded by a picturesque area with tropical plants and exotic birds and animals (peacocks, parrots and flamingos). Each complex has a very vast territory, on which you can get around by trams. Hotels have excellent infrastructure. On their territory are located spa centers, pools, massage parlors, fitness centers and much more. All kinds of marine activities are offered for guests: snorkeling, fishing, diving, windsurfing and much more. No less good and varied are restaurants that serve dishes from different cuisines of the world. The advantage of a holiday in the Dominican Republic is that local hotels offer a very versatile and comfortable stay.

Our range of facilities will help you figure out which hotel in Punta Cana is better.

Barcelo Bavaro Palace Deluxe 5 *

Barcelo Bavaro Palace is one of the best luxury 5 star hotels in Punta Cana (Dominican Republic). If you want to relax on a snow-white beach surrounded by tropical vegetation, then you are definitely here. Warm clear water, coral reefs and comfortable conditions - all this is in the hotel. Its territory is located on one of the ten best beaches in the world. The hotel will give you a carefree vacation, comfortable apartments and a wide range of entertainment: spa, gym, shopping center, 11 a la carte restaurants, swimming pools, a kids club and more.



The complex works according to the all-inclusive concept. It is focused on family vacations, and therefore for children all kinds of entertainment are provided. Among them is a large water park with pools, waterfalls, lagoons and slides. The hotel is presented with ultramodern rooms with new technology and beautiful surroundings.

According to tourists, the complex really deserves the attention of even the most demanding tourists. It is rightly called one of the best hotels in the resort. High standard of service and good food complete the overall good impression.

Bee Liv Grand 5 *

One of the best hotels in Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) can also be attributed to Bee Liv Grand. The complex is located on the beautiful beach of Bavaro. It is good for families and for youth. There is a mini-club for young guests on site, as well as various entertainment programs.

The beach next to the hotel is surrounded by coral reefs. For this reason, divers like to relax here. The rooms overlook the ocean. All apartments are equipped with modern appliances and new furniture. Guests can use the pools, visit the fitness center, spa, sauna, golf club, beauty salon, tennis courts and much more. The hotel has an excellent infrastructure. On its territory there is absolutely everything that guests may need. According to reviews, the best hotels in Punta Cana (5 stars) are equipped in such a way that you can spend your entire vacation without leaving the complex, as it has everything you need. Such is the Beast Liv Grand. The complex works according to the all-inclusive concept. In addition to the main restaurant, seven more establishments operate on its territory, serving a wide variety of dishes from around the world.



The high level of the hotel is confirmed by numerous positive reviews about it.

Grand Bahia Esmeralda Principle 5 *

Among the top hotels in Punta Cana is the Grand Bahia Esmeralda Principle. The luxury complex belongs to the Bahia Principle network along with the institutions of the Grand Bahia Principle of Bavaro and the Grand Bahia Principle of Punta Cana. Guests of any hotel of a well-known chain can use the entire infrastructure.

The hotel rooms are designed in accordance with modern requirements and have beautiful interiors. In addition, the staff of the institution offers a high level of service for a comfortable stay. All apartments in the complex are equipped with modern equipment, so staying at the hotel allows you to completely disengage from all domestic problems. In addition, the hotel has excellent infrastructure with a kids club, shops, pools and other facilities.



According to tourists, the hotel is good for relaxation. Cleanliness is the main motto of the staff. And this applies not only to the rooms themselves, but also to the entire territory and restaurants. In hot climates this is very important. You can see how the staff is busy daily cleaning. Special disinfectors for hands are even located throughout the territory, which is very useful for tourists. The hotel service is at its best, which attracts many guests here.

"Catalonia Bavaro Golf and Casino Resort" 5 *

Catalonia Bavaro Golf and Casino Resort is also listed in the ratings of the best hotels in Punta Cana. The complex consists of a number of cottages. The hotel belongs to the Catalonia Hotels chain. Luxury complex built on the first line. It consists of thirty three-story villas located around the lake. Not so long ago, in 2013, the hotel was updated. Now the complex offers 711 rooms for guests. They all have modern facilities and beautiful interiors.

"Catalonia Bavaro Golf" is good for families, as it works on the concept of "all inclusive". In addition, on its territory there are many attractions for tourists. The hotel is located near its own beach. The distance from the most remote cottage to the sea does not exceed 200 meters. Four a la carte restaurants and five bars allow you to try a variety of dishes and not be limited to the buffet of the main restaurant.



According to tourists, the hotel has a very beautiful green area with numerous pools and ponds. Beautiful rooms and excellent food make your stay at the resort even more enjoyable. The luxury hotel is perfect for families. But young people may want a more active pastime.

The Ossidental Grand Punta Cana

"Osidental Grand Punta Cana" 5 * was built back in 1999. The hotel consists of 17 three-story buildings. It is located on the coast, near the airport. The first line allows tourists to enjoy the beautiful coast of the resort and the ocean during the whole vacation. The buildings of the complex are built in a palm grove. The hotel area, although not very large, according to tourists, but well maintained and clean. It has three swimming pools, one of which is intended for young vacationers.

The complex offers not only to take advantage of the all-inclusive concept, but also to visit ten restaurants and nine bars. Each of the food establishments has its own characteristics and offers national food from around the world.



There are also several clubs for children, a fitness center, shops, a spa, a massage parlor and much more. According to vacationers, the hotel is suitable for a luxurious relaxing holiday. If you want to spend carefree time enjoying the ocean and nature, then you are definitely here.

Iberostar Grand Hotel Bavaro 5 *

Iberostar Grand Hotel Bavaro 5 * is another high-level hotel in Punta Cana. It is significantly different from other resort complexes, since it consists of only one building, in which not only all rooms are located, but also most of the entertainment infrastructure. The unique layout made it possible to place all the apartments on one side of the building, so that they all have ocean views. The chic interiors and the beautiful territory can amaze even the most sophisticated travelers. However, it must be remembered that the hotel does not accept guests under the age of eighteen, and therefore is not suitable for families with children. The rest of the institution, according to tourists, is ideal for a luxurious vacation.

Paradisus Palma Real Resort 5 *

Paradisus Palma Real Resort is one of the hotels of the large Spanish chain Melia. The complex consists of one main building and a number of villas. According to tourists, the hotel has a beautiful area. Buildings are literally buried in tropical greenery. And on the open sandy beach there is nothing but white sand and incredibly tall coconut palms.

The hotel offers chic rooms, evening entertainment, a network of pools and other entertainment.

Nau Larimar Punta Cana

Nau Larimar Punta Cana cannot boast of luxury apartments, but the hotel offers a very high level of service. The atmosphere of the establishment will certainly appeal to those people who crave fun and entertainment on vacation. By the way, the animation team at the hotel works all day. It is especially pleasant that activists also conduct programs in Russian.



According to guest reviews, the hotel is good for an active vacation, especially if you are not a supporter of lying on the beach. One of the advantages of the complex is its beach, protected from winds and waves by reefs, which attracts tourists with children here.

Paradises Punta Cana Reserve

The Paradises Punta Cana Reserve is another great hotel for families. Beautiful rooms and excellent service attract many tourists. Vacationers recommend this hotel even for families with kids, which is the best recommendation for the establishment.

Barcelo Bavaro Beach Premium 5 *

Barcelo Bavaro Beach Premium 5 * is a hotel of the Barcelo chain. Only nine complexes of the famous brand were built on the island. All of them have an excellent reputation and are loved by customers.

The hotel will be appreciated by sports enthusiasts as it has golf courses, basketball courts, tennis courts and even a football field. The territory of the complex is small. The hotel has only three restaurants, which is quite unusual for Punta Cana, most of the hotels here have numerous dining establishments. The complex is aimed at guests over 18 years old.
