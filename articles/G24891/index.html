Dzhemete: hotels and reviews about them

With the approach of summer, many residents of Russia think about a vacation at sea, and often the village of Dzhemete becomes their choice. Hotels located in this village are very popular among holidaymakers, since most of them are distinguished by their favorable location, quality service and reasonable prices. In addition, guests of this village can at any time go on an excursion to Anapa, which is literally nearby.

White Sand Hotel

White Sand is one of the most popular hotels in Dzhemete. The hotels located nearby also often attract the attention of tourists, but it is this one that attracts vacationers with two swimming pools and a sunbathing area located on the roof of the building. It also offers great sea views.

The hotel itself is presented in the form of a four-story building, in which comfortable rooms are located. It offers vacationers one-room standards and two-room suites. The rooms of both categories are equipped with the highest level. At the request of guests, they can be settled into a room with a balcony. For an additional fee, tourists can enjoy meals in the local restaurant.

The hotel is located on the first coastline, so vacationers can be on the beach in a few minutes. Near the hotel there is a tour desk, a dolphinarium, souvenir shops, a pharmacy.





Atlas Hotel

At a distance of 100 meters from the sea this hotel is located. Dzhemete beach, where guests of the Atlas can be in just 5 minutes, is able to please their opportunities for a luxurious vacation. And the hotel itself will surprise you with modern comfortable rooms, an outdoor pool, as well as the proximity of numerous entertainment facilities, including a dolphinarium, many cafes and a tour desk.

The rooms in the Atlas are presented in the standard category, however they can be with or without a balcony. All rooms are equipped with electronic locks and beds with orthopedic mattresses. There is also a private bathroom, TV, hairdryer, fridge and safe. Each room can accommodate an extra bed.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are not included in the price, however, it is possible to order a comprehensive meal for an additional fee. At the same time, hotel guests receive a huge selection of dishes from meat, fish, seafood, salads, fruits and wines.

Hotel "Southern Night"

Southern Night is another noteworthy hotel in Dzhemete. Hotels located on the second coastline are not always less convenient, and this rule applies to this hotel. The entire hotel courtyard is in the shade of the vine, which is its indisputable advantage, especially on hot summer days. There are also many flower beds with roses and a bar.

The hotel is represented by 4 buildings, in which there are 69 standard and studio rooms. There are double and triple rooms, equipped with all necessary furniture and household appliances. The hotel offers three meals a day, swimming pools, sun loungers, a parking lot, a children's playground, as well as an order for a transfer.





Jennifer Hotel

The Jennifer Hotel offers comfortable rooms, hospitable hosts and the proximity to the location of the famous Black Sea resort of Anapa. Hotel Dzhemete has been on its territory relatively recently, but during this time many tourists have already managed to relax here and remain satisfied with the living conditions in it.

Hotel "Jennifer" is represented by a four-story building, which is equipped with an elevator. The beach is nearby, so guests can visit it every day as many times as they like. At the same time, they have the opportunity to enjoy the clear water and the abundance of the sun, which Dzhemete differs. The hotel receives extremely positive reviews, as its owners spend a lot of effort to ensure that each guest here is comfortable.
