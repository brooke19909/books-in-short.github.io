Shopping center "Armada" in Orenburg

Shopping and entertainment center "Armada" in Orenburg is the largest regional facility, covering an area of ​​300,000 square meters. m. Located on the outskirts of the city at the address: 1. Sharlykskoye Shosse. On a huge territory, there are several hundred shops and entertainment facilities, an international exhibition center, an innovative business center, a cinema, a hotel.

"Armada" in Orenburg is a favorite vacation spot of many residents of the city and its guests. Here, on the territory of the facility, an intercity bus station is located, and there is a stop for electric trains nearby. “Armada” is a world of great opportunities!

The shops

Stores of popular brands, including Carlo Pazolini, Salamander, KARI, Caterina Leman, Mascotte, Basic Edition, Lawine and others, will satisfy any customer needs. The domestic manufacturer is represented by the retail outlets “Furniture of the Black Earth Region”, “Snow Queen”, “Detsky Mir”, “Linda Kitchen”, “Milavitsa”, “Dyatkovo”, “Lapis Lazuli”, etc. Goods for every taste: food, household appliances, shoes and clothes, furniture - in the Armada shopping center in Orenburg there is everything.

Construction hypermarkets "LEO" and Castorama will delight you with a huge assortment, quality service and interesting offers. Promotions are constantly held in stores, allowing customers to significantly save their budget.



For all the fair sex, the fact that the largest store of cosmetics and perfumes is located in the mall "Armada" in Orenburg is curious. "Rive Gauche" covers an area of ​​500 square meters and offers the widest selection of goods for every taste. A network of stores throughout Russia is famous for the constant updating of the range and high quality products.

One of the most popular sporting goods stores is also located in the Armada shopping center (Orenburg). Decathlon is the largest European hypermarket with an area of ​​2500 square meters. Here you will find the necessary equipment for any sport, and responsive staff will competently advise and help determine the purchase.

Food zone

In 2016, the famous fast food restaurant McDonald's began to operate in the mall. This is a place where you can enjoy branded hamburgers and crispy fries according to the original recipe of the institution. The opening of McDonald's was a jubilee, and now the food court has 10 restaurants with different cuisines that will appeal to anyone. These are Country-chicken, Cinnabon, Sbarro, Subway, Baby Potato, The Pasha, Oriental Bazaar, Russian Pancakes, Burger King and, of course, McDonalds.



Entertainment

In the shopping center "Armada" in Orenburg you can find entertainment for every taste and budget. For children and their parents, we recommend visiting the Living Planet zoo center, where various species of animals and birds are represented: iguanas, raccoons, parrots, owls, etc. You can not only watch the life of the zoo center pets, but also feed them yourself. Prices will also delight you: an adult ticket costs 200 rubles, a child ticket costs 150 rubles. Children under 3 years old have free admission.

For fans of extreme sports, we can offer modern karting, which has no analogues in the city. You will have a wonderful time at PRO KARTING, and if you are still in doubt, we recommend that you sign up for a free trial and check your strengths. Prices are very affordable, vary depending on the day of the week and time of visit: from 190 to 350 rubles. per session.



Crazy Park is a holiday every day. This is a place where you can have fun and have fun with your whole family. Various master classes are held here daily, including on face painting techniques. Good and responsive animators are at your service. A visit to the park is guaranteed to remain in your memory, and you will definitely want to come back here again.

Cinema

Cinema "Armada" in Orenburg - these are spacious halls with new, modern equipment. Huge screens with high image quality will not leave anyone indifferent, and the excellent Dolby sound will take you to the fantastic world of cinema. "Kinofresh" in "Armada" (Orenburg) is the best cinema in the entire region, capable of accommodating up to a thousand people in nine halls, two of which have 3D and IMax 3D functions.

Hotel

Hotel "Armada" in Orenburg is the best place to accommodate guests of the city. A cozy hotel with 45 rooms of various levels of comfort, an ideal place for relaxation and business meetings. And the prices of "Armada Comfort Hotel" comply with European standards and quality service. In addition, there is a flexible system of discounts on accommodation when ordering online. Room rates range from 1600 to 6600 rubles per day, depending on the service offered.

In 2016, the hotel immediately won three prestigious awards in the hotel industry. These are decals from Booking, TripAdvisor and 101 Hotels. On the Armada Comfort Hotel website visitors have already left about three hundred reviews, most of them positive.

The hotel offers all kinds of services: Wi-fi, car rental, parking, room reservation, restaurant, billiards and many others. The staff is always friendly and responsive to the requests and wishes of guests. The high level of comfort at the hotel will allow you to have a good time here and leave only a pleasant impression on your trip to Orenburg.
