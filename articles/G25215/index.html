Scope of phones with increased protection

High-security gadgets, such as the Chinese Hammer phones, are used in specific areas such as construction or rescue. It is there that the communications equipment requires good dust protection and moisture resistance. The conditions in which such gadgets are typically operated are significantly different from normal. If, for example, you consider a phone such as the Chinese Hammer H1, manufacturers indicate the degree of protection of IP57, and in some sources IP67 may be indicated . What are these numbers talking about?






The first digit after the letters (5) indicates that dust can still get into the phone, but this will not affect its performance. The second digit (7) is that the phone is not afraid of short-term (up to half an hour) immersion in water to a depth of one meter. If the first digit is 6, this indicates complete protection from dust.
