Hotel Ozkaymak Select Resort Hotel 5 *, Turkey, Alanya: overview, features and reviews

The hotel complex Ozkaymak Select Resort, according to experienced, justifiably awarded five stars. First-class service, comfortable accommodation, culinary masterpieces and developed infrastructure will make your stay here a series of memorable events and emotions.

Benefits of Alanya

A favorite place for tourists to realize their long-awaited annual vacation is the amazing resort of Turkey - Alanya. It is located on the Mediterranean coast at the foot of the Taurus Mountains. It is a small and cozy town, with a warm sea and clean air. Massifs protect from the blow of cold northerly winds. Here you can not only soak up in the bright sunshine, gaining a dark tan, but also visit a number of attractions, the most famous of which are the archaeological museum, Sialanya Park and caves of Alanya.





general characteristics

Hotel Ozkaymak Select Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya) is relatively new, as it first opened its doors to receive guests in 2012. On the territory, whose area is 30,000 square meters, there is the main and only building with seven floors. It has the shape of the letter Y and is equipped with panoramic elevators. The park area is always clean and well-groomed, dotted with numerous alleys, fragrant flower beds, spreading trees, exotic palm trees and green lawns.

The distance to the nearby international airport is a little less than 100 km. Transfer is paid extra: experienced drivers will deliver their guests quickly and comfortably at any time convenient for them.

Pets are not allowed. Smoking is allowed only in designated areas. It is permissible to check in people with disabilities. Guests can use all the facilities at Ozkaymak Incekum World, with the exception of restaurants.





Accommodation

The room fund of the Ozkaymak Select Resort Hotel 5 * hotel consists of 267 apartments, which are divided into the following categories:

  1. Promo Room.
  2. Family Room.

The interior of the chambers of the Ozkaymak Select Resort Hotel 5 * (Alanya) has a similar design, which is characterized by simplicity and homely warmth. They vary in size, views from the windows and the maximum possible number of people placed. There is no central chandelier, however it is compensated by numerous wall sconces. The floors are carpeted. The predominant color scheme is pastel warm colors.

There is access to the balcony only in family quarters. It is equipped with plastic chairs and a table. Here you can enjoy the picturesque scenery of the surroundings and breathe in the fresh air of untouched nature.

Cleaning is carried out daily at a time when guests are not available in the rooms. The food and press delivery service requires an additional charge and is available 24 hours a day. Bed linen is changed three times a week.

Equipment

A carefree stay is facilitated by the benefits and amenities that each room is equipped with. These include central air conditioning, which changes the climate according to the weather outside the hotel. Watching Russian-language channels on an LCD TV will brighten up the time before going to bed and inform you about the latest events in your native land. The phone allows you to keep in touch with the reception, which instantly solves the issues. Wi-Fi is ubiquitous.

The use of a safe is included in the price, which will allow you to keep documents and money safe and sound. An electric kettle is available upon request at an additional cost. The minibar is updated daily with drinking water and other drinks.

The furniture set includes soft beds, a sofa, a desk, a chair and a spacious wardrobe.

The bathroom is decorated with ceramic tiles, equipped with shower, sink, complemented by a set of bath accessories and personal hygiene products. There is a hairdryer and terry towels of the required quantity, disposable slippers and a bathrobe.





Diet

Ozkaymak Select Resort Hotel Alanya 5 * hotel (Alanya) offers five meals a day according to the Ultra All Inclusive system in the main restaurant, organized according to the buffet principle. The buffet serving of dishes allows vacationers to independently decide what the soul is lying for and put on the plate as much as a person can consume.

The restaurant has an indoor hall with many columns, a multi-level ceiling with soffits, a marble floor and an open summer terrace in the fresh air. The dishes are mainly represented by Ottoman cuisine, prepared by local chefs who love their profession unlimitedly.

Additional dining options

You can taste the masterpieces of fish and Turkish cuisine in the Sunset restaurant, which is open for visits in the evening. Dress code is required: you will not be able to enter swimming trunks and swimsuits. Once his visit is included in the price of the permit, however, requires a preliminary reservation.

During the day, from 11 am to 6 pm, snacks are served to quench the feeling of incipient hunger. Coffee breaks and sweets, ice cream and pastries are available in the Vitamin Bar and Amphi Bar. Local drinks are included in the Ultra AI concept.

In addition to the mentioned bars, there are similar establishments by the pool, in a disco, in the lobby. The latter meets customers around the clock.





Beach

Local beaches are characterized by their small depth and safety. Perfect for bathing babies and the implementation of a full family holiday. Ozkaymak Select Resort is located on the first coastline, directly by the sea. The length of the sand and pebble beach is 250 meters.

Comfortable chaise lounges with soft mattresses allow you to relax and enjoy the light breath of the wind and the barely audible sounds of the surf of the waves. For those who want to stay in the shade, the beach is equipped with umbrellas to shelter from mercilessly scorching sunlight. Towels are provided free of charge. Changing rooms, showers and toilets are part of the beach infrastructure.

Here you can entertain yourself with a game of volleyball and futsal, the grounds for which are equipped with everything you need. Friendly teams of barely familiar tourists fight for the championship. A wide variety of water attractions allows you to enjoy a beach holiday to the fullest. The most popular are: banana, canoe, parachute flying after a boat, snorkeling and diving. Pleasure yachts that approach the concrete pier take travelers to the open sea for fishing and diving to the picturesque bottom of the Mediterranean.

Children leisure

Ozkaymak Select Resort Hotel 5 * reviews rarely get negative reviews. Mostly they are positive. The key mass of admired responses is left by tourists who come on vacation with their children. They argue that in order for the children not to be bored and their parents to fully enjoy the long-awaited vacation, the doors of the children's mini-club are open throughout the day, which accepts children aged 4 to 12 years. A varied entertainment program includes contests and quizzes led by animators in the outfits of fairy-tale characters, scenes and theatrical productions, sports and competitions. Under the supervision of qualified teachers, kids will make unusual crafts, take part in entertaining master classes, draw amazing paintings and play with a variety of toys. And after that they will collectively start watching their favorite cartoons.

Open areas with game complexes will allow you to jump, run and frolic in the fresh air, which is so necessary and useful for children. Slides and swings, labyrinths and ladders, Swedish walls and exercise machines physically develop babies, increasing their immunity.

The evening ends with a mini-disco, where the kids spend their last energy before the upcoming night's sleep.

Restaurants are equipped with high chairs, a children's menu. There is no rental of pots and prams. Manege in the room is installed on demand. Using the services of a nanny, parents can not worry about pastime of their child, relax and have fun.





Adult Activities

Every tourist in his spare time, according to numerous reviews, will find something to his liking. The diverse structure of entertainment is designed to create a good mood, positive emotions and unforgettable experiences. For lovers of a relaxing pastime, you are invited to visit the free cinema.

In the evening, in the amphitheater of the Ozkaymak Select Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya), a professional team of animators creates a small celebration for each traveler. Experienced actors and talented dancers daily offer to watch a unique show program with many humorous scenes, acrobatic performances and national dances.

Table tennis, billiards, darts, badminton and archery will diversify the activities of the adult generation. Lighting, rental equipment and coach services for a clay tennis court require an additional charge.

Additional services provided by the hotel

The complex has five accessible conference rooms of various capacities. They are equipped with innovative equipment and modern design. Meetings and business negotiations held under similar conditions will certainly bring a positive result.

By renting a car or bicycle, you can get acquainted with the beauties of local nature, regardless of the guide and excursion group. Parking - guarded and lighted. A paid bus runs from the front door to the city center.

Beauty salon is happy for every client, regardless of gender and age. For those who have created a new image and made unique pictures of travelers, the desire to return here will certainly settle in the soul again. Laundry and doctor services are paid extra.

Summing up all the above information, we can conclude that the Ozkaymak Select Resort 5 * hotel (Turkey, Alanya) is definitely worthy of the attention of tourists who visited Alanya in the company of friends or with their family. Have a nice rest!
