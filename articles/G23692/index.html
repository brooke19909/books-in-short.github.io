Why do I need a checking account for individual entrepreneurs?

For this category of figures, the presence of such an instrument in the bank provides an opportunity to carry out two forms of settlements: in a cashless format and in cash. Why is such an IP account required:

  • it allows you to conclude agreements of a civil and legal nature, making payments on them;
  • through the account you can make all kinds of transfers of large amounts, which is prohibited for the sphere of cash circulation;
  • there are no restrictions on the time of sending money in favor of legal entities;
  • the obligation to staff can be settled in cashless form.

In some situations, opening an account at a banking institution is not a right, but an obligation of entrepreneurs. Do I need such an IP bill? What other benefits does a current account have?

What are the conditions for opening a current account for an individual entrepreneur?

Cash payments lose their relevance to business due to the high cost of servicing them. The bank charges a commission for each cash withdrawal and transfer transaction to counterparties. Is a business account required for individual entrepreneurs? Of course, since its launch allows you to reduce costs by lowering interest on commission services. An additional plus is perpetuity. A card for personal use needs to be updated regularly (re-release of plastic takes up to two weeks, and, at times, is a paid option).

Conducting settlement operations using personal plastic is associated with an increased risk of tax security. One of the advantages of the considered payment instrument is to minimize the risks of disputes with the Federal Tax Service and the separation of the financial flow of work and personal nature. The controlling body, if there is no separate account for the individual entrepreneur, can credit all receipts on the card to income. This entails underpayment of taxes and the occurrence of various lawsuits.

The tax authorities have the right to make requests for movement on the accounts of all business entities. Based on the information received from the financial institution, FTS specialists may make additional assessments on taxes. Entrepreneurs will have to prove the need to exclude personal income from the tax base in the framework of the judicial procedure (which is not required if there is a separate payment instrument). If it is impossible for the court to present compelling arguments with documentary evidence for absolutely every operation, a conclusion can be made in favor of the fiscal authorities. Tax Checks

Sometimes people are interested in whether this body can check the current account. It is worth saying that on certain grounds, of course, it can. That's why she and the state official service. But there are a number of significant nuances:

  • who owns the payment instrument (individuals, individual entrepreneurs or LLC);
  • legislative basis;
  • cases when inspectors have the right to inspect.

So how does the tax check the current account of the individual entrepreneur? According to the Tax Code, the inspection service has the right to request the following information from a financial institution:

  • opening and closing an account;
  • Actual balance at the time of the request;
  • statement of debit transaction;
  • certificate of funds transfer.

The Bank undertakes to provide the requested data within three days. Verification can also be carried out on the basis of Article No. 122 of the Tax Code for non-payment of income tax, as well as other offenses.
