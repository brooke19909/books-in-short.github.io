"Kazinka", Stavropol Territory. Recreation centers in the Stavropol Territory: reviews, photos

Stavropol Territory is famous for its clean nature and the diversity of water bodies. Here you can catch fish, and just soak up the sun. Tourists with financial disabilities can choose one of the many bases located in the area for recreation. There are more expensive options for picky handfuls. Tourists prefer to relax in the Stavropol Territory in the warm season. Despite this, many bases are happy to welcome guests in the winter.

Holiday Village "Kazinka"

The holiday home is located 40 km from Stavropol, not far from the village of the same name Kazinka. Tourists who get to rest on their own car can get into the boarding house, moving along the Rostov highway. In the summer, finding a free room here is not so simple. It is advisable to book the apartment in advance. Not far from the holiday home is a clean lake with a white sandy coast. Vacationers note that the promenade here is practically no different from the sea.

Wooden apartments in the summer keep a cozy cool. In winter, houses are additionally heated. Double, triple and quadruple rooms are available. It is possible to install additional beds.

In the boarding house people from various age categories can have a great time. For fishing enthusiasts, all the necessary equipment is offered. It will be possible to rent it for an additional fee. For young people in the evening there is an open-air disco.

"Blue Wave"

On the banks of the Yegorlyksky reservoir , 30 km from the Stavropol Territory, a cozy guesthouse welcomes guests. Like other recreation centers in Kazinka (Stavropol Territory), Blue Wave offers accommodation in comfortable rooms with all amenities. In addition, the infrastructure is well developed here. There is a grocery store, if you wish, you can play billiards. From the gates of the boarding house, a bus runs regularly to Stavropol.



The holiday home will appeal to lovers of outdoor activities. Tourists who were fortunate enough to visit here note that the most memorable will be a walk through the local reservoir on a catamaran. In addition, in the pension there is a volleyball court, outdoor fitness equipment, there is the opportunity to play table tennis.

For corporate evenings and weddings, Kazinka (Stavropol Territory) is also perfect. The recreation centers offer excellent banquet facilities. In the boarding house "Blue Wave" you can also rent a summer canopy for celebrations.

Pension "Zemzulka"

The holiday home got its interesting name due to the river flowing in the immediate vicinity of the same name. Guests are only invited in the warm season. In winter, you will not be able to spend time here. In this case, you can plan a vacation at the recreation center "Kazinka" in the Stavropol Territory. This guesthouse accepts guests all year round.



Guests are invited to the Zemzylka Holiday House for picnics with all the necessary infrastructure. On the territory there are gazebos and wooden houses. The apartments are designed for both vacationers and large companies. It is possible to install additional beds. You can buy coal, firewood, mosquito repellents. For a fee you can go fishing in one of three located near water bodies.

"Forest Lake"

Only 10 km from Stavropol, the employees of the Lesnoye Ozero recreation center are happy to welcome guests. Here you can have a great vacation in the company of friends or with your beloved family. Guests are accepted at any time of the year. The base is located in an ecologically clean area, surrounded by forests. There is a lake nearby. The boarding house area is ennobled by beautiful statues and flower beds. All this has a good rest for tourists.

The pension offers the same conditions as the recreation center "Kazinka" (Stavropol Territory). Guest reviews show that guest houses with a bath are more popular. It will be possible to rent such apartments for 5000 rubles per day. A little cheaper is a comfortable wooden house without a bath. At the same time it can accommodate up to 5 people. It is possible to install additional beds.

"The Recreational Beam"

Only 10 minutes by bus from Stavropol, and every tourist can get into the territory of a cozy holiday home. This place was especially fond of fishing lovers. Here all the necessary conditions are created for enjoying your favorite hobby. For accommodation, double and quadruple wooden houses are offered. There is also a large brick building, which can simultaneously accommodate up to 20 people. Guests are welcome in the winter, as in the Kazinka boarding house (Stavropol Territory). Recreation facilities in the cold season are perfectly heated.



Pension Verbovaya Balka has its own sandy beach, equipped with sunshades and sun loungers. Outsiders are not allowed here. Additionally, the beach has several large gazebos. You can rent barbecue and skewers for rent. A festive event can be organized in nature. According to vacationers, on weekends you can often watch the wedding ceremonies here.

"Beach of the sun"

The country recreation center is located 70 km from Stavropol. The territory of the boarding house is fenced and guarded. It will not be possible to get here without prior agreement. Comfortable rooms are offered for accommodation, as in the Kazinka boarding house (Stavropol Territory). The recreation center demonstrates photos of their rooms on the official website. Detached cottages with all amenities are very popular with tourists. No more than 5 people can be accommodated in the houses. Premium rooms with a large double bed, leather seating area and plasma TV are also available.



The boarding house has everything you need for a bright holiday. There are several sports fields, a zone with swings and trampolines for the smallest guests. Water and sports equipment is being rented. You can also rent fishing equipment.

There are two cafes on the territory of the holiday home. The variety of dishes has always surprised tourists. There is even a special menu for kids. It is possible to order breakfast directly in the room. Meals are not included in the price.

Pelagiada

The boarding house is open all year and is perfect for a bright summer vacation, and for a memorable winter weekend. The base is located in a picturesque, ecologically clean place. All houses are built in the same style. At the same time in each of them can be up to 6 guests. If necessary, an extra bed for the child is installed. Each house has a shower and toilet, as in the Kazinka boarding house (Stavropol Territory). Recreation facilities with comfortable rooms attract tourists from other regions of the country much more.



Near the boarding house there are two reservoirs with clean spring water. This is a great place to go fishing. For an additional fee, you can rent all the necessary accessories for your favorite hobby, as well as barbecue and skewers. There are several comfortable gazebos on the beach.

Holiday Village "New Venice"

On the banks of the Novotroitsk reservoir near Stavropol, a cozy guesthouse is happy to welcome guests. From any point of the base a gorgeous view of the water opens. There is a private comfortable beach. Additionally operates a fishing pier. The water in the reservoir warms up very well. Therefore, vacationers open the beach season in early May. The base offers comfortable accommodation for both single tourists and corporate clients.

Those who planned a vacation at the recreation center "Kazinka" (Stavropol Territory), but did not manage to book a room on time, should not despair. Pension "New Venice" is happy to welcome tourists at any time of the year. At the same time there can be up to 500 people.

"Exclusive fishing"

Not far from the Novotroitsk reservoir there is a specialized recreation center for fishing lovers. Although tourists come here not only for hanging out on the shore of a reservoir. In any weather, in a boarding house you can perfectly relax in the circle of friends and relatives. It is advisable to book rooms for accommodation in advance, as in the Kazinka boarding house (Stavropol Territory). The recreation center phone for booking indicates the main stand at the entrance. Tourists note that it is advisable to call a few weeks before the holidays.



Cozy wooden houses are offered for living, at the same time up to 5 people can live in them. Most often avid fishermen with families come to the boarding house. While the head of the family enjoys his favorite hobby, wives with children can have a great time on the beach. And in the evening you can have fun in the nearest cafe.

Reviews of tourists about the rest in the Stavropol Territory

The region is rich in cozy bases and pensions. Everyone will be able to find an option for themselves. Tourists note that rest here can be classified as inexpensive. For only 500-1000 rubles a day, you can rent a cozy wooden house with a minimum set of amenities. Luxury rooms are also offered to vacationers, which are no different from apartments in expensive overseas hotels.

The most popular among foreign tourists is the Kazinka boarding house (Stavropol Territory). Recreation facilities (how to get to them, you can check with the tour operator) offer the best conditions for guests. This area was especially fond of the Poles and Belarusians.
