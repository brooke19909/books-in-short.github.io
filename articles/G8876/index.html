A little about the company

Mystery equipment belongs to the budget segment of the domestic market. How to set up a Mystery TV?

Very often in the reviews there are complaints about the complexity of tuning the TV to digital channels. Some buyers, after a series of fruitless attempts, even begin to consider their equipment malfunctioning. Before you set up your Mystery TV, make sure that the selected model supports the DVB-T / T2 and DVB-C standards, and that digital signals are transmitted in your area.

The first time you turn it on, the setup goes in several stages:

  1. When the "Setup Wizard" appears, you need to select the menu language.
  2. The type of antenna is set: DVB-C (cable) or DVB-T / T2 (terrestrial).
  3. After selecting the type of antenna, the channel search will begin. After a while, the number of programs found will be displayed on the screen.
  4. At the next stage, choose the type of connection to the Internet. If communication will be via an HDMI cable, select ‚ÄúWired‚Äù, if via Wi-Fi - ‚ÄúWireless‚Äù.
  5. Subsequent configuration is carried out taking into account the information of the provider and data from the router.
  6. When the setup phase is completed, the TV will be ready to go.
