Madinat Jumeirah Hotels, United Arab Emirates: Travel Reviews

Despite such a peculiar oriental name, the Jumeirah network is present all over the world. The Maldives or the Balearic Islands, London or Frankfurt, Shanghai and even Baku - at all these resorts and cities you can find a chain of hotels that amazes the imagination with luxury buildings, room decoration, sophisticated cuisine, and the highest quality of services. But most of all Jumeirah hotels in its "homeland" are in the United Arab Emirates. And their number is off the charts in Dubai. In this city, there are lonely hotels and entire hotel complexes. One such thing, Madinat Jumeirah, will be discussed today. Prices for accommodation in such palaces are rather big, but the rest in them is focused on the most demanding, you might say, capricious customers. Here we will study their opinion. What can I get by paying a ticket to a luxury hotel of the Jumeirah chain, such as Madinat (Dubai, UAE)?



Madinat Jumeirah Hotels

As the name implies in our article, we are not talking about one hotel. The Madinat of the Jumeirah network is a whole town of oriental comfort and sophisticated luxury. Like many other chain operations in Dubai, this complex stands on the prestigious Jumeirah beach coast. What hotels are included in the resort "Madinat"? First of all, these are the huge Al Qvasr, Al Nasim and Mina A'Salam. Near these boutique hotels, consisting of multi-storey buildings, there is also Dar Al Masyaf. It consists of twenty-nine bungalows. And closer to the promenade and the sea are seven luxurious cottages, united in the hotel "Jumeirah Malakiya Villas." The layout of buildings of buildings and bungalows, restaurants and other buildings recreates the ancient Arabian town, where the colorful architecture organically fits into the landscape, with its blooming oases, canals (their total length is about three kilometers) and hanging gardens.



Hotels or resort?

Relaxing in one of the buildings or villas of the hotel complex, you will not feel the need to go to Dubai. Hotel "Madinat Jumeirah" (photos of travelers clearly demonstrate this) is a full-fledged resort with developed infrastructure. Guests of the complex have the opportunity to visit all the bars and restaurants available on its territory, and there are as many as forty. All bathing pleasures are available at Talise Spa. For sports there is a Talis Fitness with the most modern fitness equipment. And even for the sake of shopping, you do not need to leave the territory of the complex. Shopping center ‚ÄúSouk Madinat Jumeirah‚Äù is designed as an oriental bazaar. What city without transport? On the territory of "Madinata" electric cars run around the clock. The guest of the complex can use them for free. Moreover, such golf carts will take Madinat guests to such Dubai attractions as Bourges Al Arab, Wild Wadi Waterpark and Jumeirah Beach Hotel. And if you want to get somewhere else, a free bus will take you to the nearest metro station. Abrah water taxis run through an extensive network of canals.



Where is the hotel complex located

It occupies a key position in the resort city of Dubai. Hotel Madinat Jumeirah rises on the shores of the Persian Gulf. From here it is very easy to get to attractions such as Internet City, Dubai Marina and the famous man-made island "Palma". By the way, the Jumeirah International chain owns such sights of the city as the Wild Wadi water park, the Burj Al Arab skyscrapers, the Jumeirah Beach Hotel and the Ward Trade Center Residence. The hotel complex is located half an hour from Dubai International Airport. Golden Souk is the city‚Äôs most famous bazaar, located nineteen kilometers from Madinat. And 6 km there is a golf club and a huge shopping center. As you can see, the Madinat complex of the Jumeirah hotel chain is a city in the city. It has everything you need for a good rest. Even the most demanding customers were satisfied with the location of the complex and its territory with developed infrastructure.

Hotel "Jumeirah Al Qasr"

Now we will consider in order all the resort hotels. Al Qasr is the main jewel in the crown of the Madinat Jumeirah complex. The photo represents the real residence of the Sheikh, where traditional architecture is successfully combined with the modern concept of comfort. If the word "Madinat" in Arabic means "city", then "Qasr" is a "palace". The name is quite justified. One main entrance to the hotel looks no worse than the site in front of Versailles. The wide avenue is decorated with gilded statues of Arabian stallions. Tall palm trees lined up in two rows, like a company of guardsmen. We pass into the hall blinding with luxury and rise to the rooms. Some category names speak for themselves: Royal Suite, Ocean Deluxe King, Executive Arabian, Junior Suite Suite, etc. Each bedroom has antique furniture. Each bathroom has a noble marble finish. The design of the rooms does not let you forget that you are in the East. And luxurious decoration allows you to feel like a hero of the Thousand and One Nights.

Hotel Dar Al Masyaf

Tourists called this hotel a calm haven. In the Madinat Jumeirah complex, it is like an oasis of serenity and tranquility. The hotel consists of twenty-nine detached two-story houses, made according to the canons of Arabian architecture. Each of them has its own patio. Dar Al Masyaf Villas are surrounded by lushly flowered gardens and water channels. Between the villas, paths meander along which it is very pleasant to walk. You can feel in the Arabic "Venice", a ride on the abre. In a word, the hotel "Dar Al Masyaf" is chosen by lovers of a refined, but calm and secluded relaxation of a premium class. If sheikhs are supposed to live in the previous Madinata Jumeirah hotel, then they live in the villas ... they just got out into the countryside. A butler is attached to each such bungalow, ready to take care of all the little things of life.

Rooms at Dar Al Masyaf

Refined luxury reigns in the bright and airy rooms of the villas. The rooms on the upper floors have balconies. The lower level rooms are adjacent terraces on which you can sunbathe. The rooms in the villas are also divided into categories. There are also those that do not consist of one bedroom with a bathroom. But in the simplest room there is antique furniture. The design of the rooms is made in the Arabic style with warm, golden-red shades. The bathroom has a rain shower and marble trim. Some of the villas that host the suites, such as Arabian Summer House or Golf Ocean Suite, have their own pools. Villas "Malakia" are filled with special luxury. They are the pearl of the Madinat Jumeirah, an oasis of solitude and relaxation. Room in the "Malakie" consists of two bedrooms. Each of them has its own balcony. The bathroom has a jacuzzi. There is also a kitchen corner with a bar. And the villa has its own swimming pool, lawn and gazebo. Inhabitants of "Malakia" are also members of prestigious clubs and are entitled to services as VIP clients.

It surrounds a water harbor where trees grow on artificial islands. It should be said that this hotel is located closer to the sea than all the others in the complex. From the spacious and bright rooms, the Persian Gulf is not only visible, but also audible. Sea breezes and smells of iodine flies in the windows. Also visible is the skyscraper ‚ÄúBourj Khalifa‚Äù.
