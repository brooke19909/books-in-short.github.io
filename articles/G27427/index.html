We make an amulet of luck with our own hands

Everyone knows about the existence of certain amulets for good luck, health or love. Many use them in their daily lives and believe that they will certainly make Fortune smile at the most crucial and important moments in life. So what is an amulet of luck? And how does it work?

An amulet is an item, the wearing of which contributes to success in any field of human activity, for example, in finance, career, love, health. This intimate thing must be made individually for the person. It is for this reason that purchased talismans work so poorly. Many mistakenly believe that you cannot do them yourself. But in fact, the luck mascot works best with its own hands.



All amulets can be divided into the following types:

1. Amulet to protect against negative external influences. These are talismans, protecting from evil eye, disease and evil spirits. This option is the most common.

2. Charms-symbols. This type of amulets helps people choose the right path in life, get rid of addictions and meet good reliable people. They are quite rare, but have tremendous magical power.

3. The amulet for good luck. It affects the emotional mood of a person, contributes to luck and success both in love affairs and in business.



Most likely, many remember how their children looked for special pebbles on the beaches with a hole in the middle (the so-called "chicken gods"), bringing unthinkable luck to their owners. Those who found such rare river stones can indeed be called lucky already for this. The rest remains to learn how to make an amulet of luck on their own. Talismans are easiest to make from natural wood. To work with metals, certain skills are required, and stones are very difficult to process. But it will be fair to say that it is from stones that the most powerful talismans are obtained.



Among the Slavic peoples, the most revered trees were birch, oak and willow. From their wood (as well as bark or branches) very effective amulets are obtained. Suitable trees can be found in any forest or city park. Do not break living branches, it is better to pay attention to those that are under the tree. If you want to make a really strong amulet for good luck, then use a birch. She has always been a symbol of worldly happiness, joy and carelessness. Its leaves and bark are endowed with valuable healing properties, so that the power of the amulet will be doubled.

The most common amulet of birch is made in the form of a ringlet. He needs to be worn during depression or when a streak of failure sets in. Of course, making a ring with your own hands is not easy, so you can use the services of a good carpenter.

Sometimes an amulet of luck can be inherited. And sometimes a mascot becomes a random little thing, which has always been next to you. Sometimes luck brings a keychain or a rare coin. Anything can become a talisman: pebbles, pendants, earrings, bracelets, twigs and even cereal grains. The main thing is to believe that this amulet has power, thereby you give it energy, and in the future it will really bring good luck at the most crucial moment in your life.
