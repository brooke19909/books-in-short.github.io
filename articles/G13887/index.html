How to make furniture for dolls with your own hands at home?

Girls are very fond of playing with dolls. But after buying such a toy, you need a lot of additional devices to play with it. In addition to a variety of clothes for your "child", you will also want a doll house, and furniture in it. If you buy furniture in a store, it will cost parents a round sum.

In the article, we will tell you how to make furniture for a doll with your own hands, what you need for work, how to decorate furniture for a doll's room. The materials used are the most familiar and cheap that are available in each apartment. These are plastic bottles and foam sponges for washing dishes, packaging boxes made of corrugated cardboard, cardboard packaging for eggs and matchboxes, ice cream sticks and other materials.

How to make furniture for dolls with your own hands at home? First you need to prepare the necessary materials, think about how to fasten the structural parts to each other. You can use PVA glue to work with paper and cardboard. To work with other materials, a stronger connection is required, for example, a glue gun or liquid nails, it holds well bonded parts and transparent Crystal glue.

Bed for a doll

If you still have a tight packing box, then now we will tell you how to make doll furniture with your hands, see the photo below. You can make a single bed, or you can make a stretching second bed from below. If the berth only needs one, then from below you can make a drawer made of cardboard, in which the child can fold the bedding for the doll's crib.

How to make furniture for a doll with your own hands from cardboard with step-by-step instructions, read on. First you need to measure the height of the doll, add 2-3 cm for the pillow and get the required size for the bed.

Next, a drawing of the future piece of furniture is created with a simple pencil. A long line is drawn, from it the length of the bed is measured downward, to the left is the depth of the furniture with a cutout under the drawer. On the right you need to measure the depth of the bed. To the left and right, add a few centimeters per hem. Then everything is cut out along the contours. Further, the sweep needs to be bent along the fold lines. You get a bent part with the letter "P" with curved bases.

Then, a box for linen is made in the same way, there is no need to make reserves for bending, but you need to add side strips to the drawing. Further along the folds, a rectangular box is formed and a handle is attached to the front panel. It can be made after decorating furniture, for example, from a button, bead or strong thread, on which several pieces of beads were strung.

But that is not all. To make furniture for your Barbie doll with your own hands, you also need to cut out the side parts - the headboards. Again we take up a simple pencil and ruler and draw a rectangle in which the length is equal to the height of two backs plus the length of the bed itself. After bending in the right places, an inverted letter "P" is obtained, on the bottom of which the bends of the main part of the structure are glued to the PVA.

Decoration work

You have already learned how to make doll furniture for your dolls from cardboard and paper, now let's see how it can be decorated. There are several ways to paste cardboard: colored paper, it is advisable to use printed glossy with a beautiful floral print, fabric or felt sheets. The fabric adheres perfectly to the cardboard after spreading with PVA glue, similarly glues felt.

On the backs you can cut curly lines, and attach a heart at the head. But it is not necessary to do this from corrugated cardboard, you can decorate the headboard and applique of paper or fabric, stick a voluminous heart cut out of felt. It is a soft material with bright colors.

The handle on the drawer can be made of a shiny pebble or a beautiful button on the bow. Then it will be more convenient to pull it forward. Of course, then for the bed you will also have to sew bed linen, make a pillow and a blanket out of sintepon or cotton wool.

Matchbox chest of drawers

If you decide how to make furniture for dolls with your own hands from improvised materials, then you can use the following tip. From the matchboxes glued together, you can create a beautiful and stylish chest of drawers in which the child will place small items for the doll: a comb, a mirror, shoes or clothes for a toy.

First, the boxes are glued together with PVA glue. Then they need to be painted or pasted over with colored paper so that they are colorful. Loose sides can be covered with printed paper.

From matchboxes you can also make a dressing table with a central mirror. To do this, we will tell you how to make furniture for the doll with your own hands. 6 boxes are glued together: 3 from the bottom, 3 from the top. All boxes are glued together with thick paper or self-adhesive with a wood pattern. As legs for furniture use beads or plastic squares. They can be purchased at the hardware store.

On the back side, a strip of thick cardboard of any shape is attached and a mirror is cut out of the foil. Each drawer is painted with gouache or pasted over with colored paper. At the end attach the handles. To do this, use loops of thread woven together in a pigtail.

Foam sofa

We continue to consider how to make furniture for dolls with your own hands from improvised materials. For the manufacture of its soft options use the basis of corrugated cardboard. To do this, measure the length of the sofa, its width and height of the back. According to the drawing, the contours of the future design are drawn. When cutting, it is necessary to leave a few centimeters on the folds so that the back can be installed at an angle. You can use scotch tape for this. Soft pillows and sidewalls are cut from a piece of foam or a sponge for dishes.

At the end of the work, each foam part is sheathed with fabric from all sides. You can sew cushions from sintepon.

Wine cork trestle bed

If you have wine corks in your house , you can collect a small trestle bed from them. How to make furniture for the doll with your own hands, we will describe further. First, the base is made of corrugated or thick cardboard. Then they attach the first row of corks cut with flat plates. The second row is trimmed. so that there are no voids between the elements.

The legs for the frame are made of wire. From the top of the corks where the drawing is placed, pillows for the head are cut out.

Egg packaging

Now we will tell you how to make furniture for a doll with your own hands from a packing box for eggs. This is a temporary option for playing a child, for example, in the country.

Armchairs and supports under them are cut out from the bulges. The table is a cover. Sofa - inverted box. You can give your daughter to paint white details with markers or pencils.

Bottle Chairs

If you still have not chosen how to make furniture for the doll with your own hands, then you can use the following option. Such beautiful armchairs are just a decorated bottom from a plastic one and a half liter bottle.

First, the outlines of the chair are cut with sharp scissors. Then a cantik is sewn along the contours of matter. Bending the side arches, buttons are sewn into the center of the fold. For beauty, you can pick up a fabric and make a flower.

A pillow sewn from the same fabric is put on the bottom. Both cotton wool and synthetic winterizer can be used as internal filler. A similar pillow is sewn onto the back of the chair, only a smaller one. She is also sewn on the button.

Bookcase made of wood cuts

Furniture for dolls can also be made from natural materials, for example, from sawn wood. A thick branch is cut into thin "pancakes", a central rod is made from a flat branch. In all details except the top, a hole is drilled to the size of a stick.

Then the saw cuts are put on and are located at an equal distance from one another. To prevent them from moving, glue is smeared on the inside of the hole, and a small screw is screwed into the bottom of each shelf. In the upper saw, a through hole is not drilled and a piece of wood is simply pulled onto a stick.

Each saw cut must be pre-treated with sandpaper. If you do not want to use varnishes and paints, then after cleaning you can rub the saw cuts with teak oil or wax for wood. It is safer for the baby.

Wire furniture

From strong knitting wire, you can make chairs and a table for a doll's kitchen or dining room. For work, you will need pliers, since it will be hard to bend it evenly with your hands. The shape of the chairs can be any. The ends of the wire are intertwined. Such a joint is hidden under the fabric. So that the child does not cut his hand, the joint must be wrapped with tape.

The backs are sewn from matter and tightly stretched to the wire back. For the seat you need to put a solid base in the cover. It can be made both from dense cardboard, and from foam.

Ice Cream Sticks Chair

Thin wooden sticks are an excellent material for crafts. From them you can make chairs, and stools, and tables, and a bed. You can cut them with simple tailor scissors.

For bonding use contact adhesive for wood. If desired, the product can be painted with acrylic paints.

From the article you learned how to make furniture for dolls with your own hands from cardboard and paper (the photo is in the article), from plastic bottles, from wood and wire, using sponges and ice cream sticks. Decide for yourself what suits you best. Making crafts is easy, and with the money saved, you can buy a girlfriend for her doll.
