Holidays for every taste and budget - South Ozereyka, Novorossiysk

A small resort village on the Black Sea coast with the romantic name South Ozereyevka is waiting for its guests. Administratively, it is part of the urban district of Novorossiysk, although it is 10 km from it. One letter ‚Äúe‚Äù has long disappeared from the official name, and the village, conveniently located in the valley, is familiar to everyone as South Ozereyka.



Leisure facilities

The climate in these places is Mediterranean with little rainfall and almost no winds. The beach, located between the mountains, is only about two kilometers. Behind the rocks there are also recreation areas, only wild ones.

South Ozereyka, due to its characteristics, even in the peak season does not have a large influx of tourists. There are many entertainments on the beach for the pleasure of vacationers, including the rental of catamarans and jet skis.



Cozy atmosphere

The quiet, measured life of the village is exactly what you need after a noisy and bustling metropolis. Belonging to the city of Novorossiysk South Ozereyka - these are excellent mini-hotels, recreation centers, cozy guest houses and many private offers. And, it should be noted, prices are much lower than large resorts.

Non-boring zone

Entertainment in the village, except beach, abound. Open to all comers dive center. Experienced instructors conduct training courses and dive with groups to wrecks.

The fascinating equestrian historical show ‚ÄúRome‚Äù leaves no one indifferent. A riding club is open here for those who wish. Lessons are held regularly and horse riding groups gather.



About private wine production, known far beyond the borders of the village of South Ozereyka, reviews can be read only enthusiastic. The hosts invite you to a tour of their own vineyards and winery. Upon completion, a mandatory tasting takes place. All drinks are of amazing quality and taste.

There is a great opportunity to organize sea fishing. All necessary is issued for rent.

A tour desk is open in the village. There are many options: interesting tours to Novorossiysk, Abrau-Durso, Gelendzhik and other resort towns. It also offers evening and night romantic excursions.

Tired of a beach holiday, guests make exciting trips to the dolphinarium and water park of Novorossiysk or Anapa. An excursion with an indispensable tasting at the famous Abrau-Durso champagne factory does not leave anyone indifferent.

Residence

With an increase in the flow of tourists, South Ozereyka began to be built up with mini-hotels and guest houses. Most of them are located on two main streets - Peace and Freedom. The price depends on the conditions in the room and the distance from the sea.

One of the most popular guest houses is South Yard. The rooms here are double and several quadruple suites. Each apartment can arrange an extra bed. The rooms have comfortable beds, bedside tables, wardrobes and bathrooms with showers. Bed sheets with towels are provided upon check-in.



On the territory of the guest house there is a guarded parking lot and a cafe, which serves excellent breakfasts, lunches and dinners. For a comfortable day or evening, there is a covered terrace and a balcony. An entertaining town was built for children, and for adults there are power simulators in an open area.

Children are welcome here from 0 years old, and animals by prior arrangement.

The guest house of increased comfort "Asteria" offers its guests a rest in large comfortable rooms equipped with comfortable furniture and modern fixtures. Each apartment has a roomy refrigerator, split system, flat-screen TV with satellite antenna and free wi-fi.

For younger guests on the territory of the guest house there is a playground with sandboxes and slides.

The accommodation fee includes the use of barbecue areas with cozy covered gazebos, a kitchen and all necessary cooking equipment and a parking lot.

The guest house has a small pool with a relaxation area. It is equipped with shade tents, parasols and sun loungers.

The owners of "Asteria" by prior arrangement organize a transfer of their guests.

There are several children's recreation camps and subordinate bases in the village of Yuzhnaya Ozereyka.

The best holiday complex

One of the wonderful holiday offers in the village of South Ozereyka is the Ozereyka recreation center. It is designed for 350 seats and is located on the so-called first line. The windows of comfortable rooms look at the sea surface and the mountain slopes covered with relict forests.

Guests are accommodated in two-story buildings or in luxury cottages for four people.



On the territory of the recreation center there is a cozy dining room offering delicious three meals a day, two cafes, a snack bar with an excellent selection of alcoholic drinks. For active vacationers, basketball and volleyball courts, treadmills, a football field and a tennis court are provided. There are also billiard rooms and an open disco.

Inside the base and on the beach there are points for renting sports and game equipment. There is a guarded parking lot and a tour desk.

Within walking distance are a market with local products and shops.

Camping

Camping is located ten kilometers from the city of Novorossiysk (South Ozereyka). The infrastructure of the town is well organized. Shops and cafes are within easy reach. The beach is only 20 m. On the territory there are dry closets, a kitchen and mangal zones. In addition to the tent, you can rent a small house for four people.
