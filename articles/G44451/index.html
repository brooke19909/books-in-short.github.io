Montenegro is a holiday destination for unpretentious tourists mainly from the countries of the former socialist camp. On the other hand, Montenegro (as this country is known all over the world) possesses such vast natural resources - a beautiful landscape, magnificent sea beaches, cozy bays, etc., that real connoisseurs of beauty seek here. However, the country has long been under a socialist regime, so hotel infrastructure is not well developed here. Although in recent years a lot of magnificent hotels have been built, less often 5 *, more often - 4 and 3-star hotels, for example, Obala Zelena 3 *. Climate

The climate of Montenegro is just perfect for a beach holiday. On the Adriatic coast, it is Mediterranean. The central part is a bit cooler, and the coast is warm and not damp. High humidity is observed only in winter - the rainy season. Since the end of April, Montenegro resorts are open to beach lovers. This continues until the end of October. That is, you can swim in the sea for as long as 7 months. So you can buy the Obala Zelena 3 * tour for both mid-spring and mid-autumn. Naturally, during these periods prices are much lower. And do not be afraid of storms, strong waves, since all the beaches of Montenegro are hidden by rocks from the winds.



Obala Zelena 3: General Description and Location

This hotel is very cozy for lovers of a quiet and secluded holiday. It is located near the village of Rafailovichi. From Tivat airport you can get here in 15-20 minutes. The nearby major resort is Budva. This hotel is not on the beachfront. To the sea you need to go about 250 meters.

Number of rooms

Obala Zelena 3 is a very small hotel. The number of rooms consists of 50 rooms. They have everything necessary for the comfort of visitors: modern furniture, a TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, a balcony, a bathroom with a shower, toiletries and a hairdryer, a minibar for an extra charge. As for the room service, the hotel is cleaned daily, and linen is changed every other day. Room service is provided for tourists at an additional cost.



Types of placements

Obala Zelena 3 has the following accommodation types:

  • single;
  • double room;
  • double (+1 place additionally).

For couples with children under 2 years old a cradle is provided , for children under 7 years old - a cot, and for older children - a cot.

Sea and beach

From the hotel to the beach about 300 meters. Since it is public, the use of equipment - umbrellas, deck chairs, mattresses - is possible only for a fee. The coast is quite clean, the beach is covered with small pebbles. There are a large number of beach activities: catamarans, boats, bananas, scooters, etc.

Obala Zelena 3: tourists reviews and ratings

Tourists who left reviews about this hotel can be divided into two groups: some are satisfied with everything, while others are dissatisfied with the service. Some complain that this hotel is far from the main building, where the restaurant and other administrative points are located. This is not very convenient, especially if the weather is bad: people have to give up their dinner or breakfast. In addition, the rooms do not have telephones, and in order to communicate with the hotel administration, you must go to the central building each time. But the reviews are pretty good about the kitchen. This means that the chefs work wonderfully and treat guests with delicious dishes of both local and international cuisine.
