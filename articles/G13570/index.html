Felt Decor

The most common way to decorate a bottle for the New Year is to sew a felt cap. This bottle decor is sewn in a couple of minutes, and will last for more than one year.

So, let's try to sew a cap in the form of Santa Claus.

For this we need:

  • Felt red and white.
  • Beads or eyes for hand-made.
  • Glue.
  • Needle, thread, scissors.
  • Pencil and paper.

To begin with, we need to make a template by which to cut the felt. The cap will be in the form of an elongated isosceles triangle. Draw an elongated triangle along the length of the hypotenuse bottle of approximately 15 cm. Take an extra centimeter into account. Prepare a fluffy beard template in advance to simulate Santa Claus's beard.

Put the large triangle pattern on the felt and cut out two triangles. Cut off the upper corner, backing off about 5-6 cm. Do not throw it away, this is the future hat.

Sew the cap on the sides. Turn and try on a bottle. The hat also needs to be sewn and twisted.

From white felt, cut a beard and a thin strip for a hat.

After trying on the hat, place the eyes on the main part and glue it. Sew fluffy beard and strip to the cap with white threads below.

If desired, you can decorate the hat with a white strip.

Santa Claus is ready.

Imagine. Several bottles can be decorated with caps in the form of a snow maiden and a snowman, as well as make deer or a symbol of the coming year. They will keep company with your Santa Claus at the festive table.

In addition, the simplest bags with embroidery are also popular from felt. You can also put knitted jewelry on the bottle, but a felt cap is the fastest way to decorate.

Decor with ribbons and threads

Bottles in the interior are a good idea, especially if they are beautifully designed. Let's try to repeat the master class on the decor of bottles of ribbons and threads. Below is an option on how to make a few bottles that will look good in the living room or hallway.

What do we need for this bottle decor with our own hands:

  • Twine or woolen threads.
  • Glue.
  • A few buttons or pearl beads.
  • Lace ribbon.
  • Ribbon for flower or dried flower.

Before working with the bottle, it must be degreased.

The decor with twine has several nuances, however, this will not bring much complexity to the process.

First, you need to start wrapping the bottle from the bottom. Lubricate it with glue and twist the thread with an even snake. Be careful with the rib. On it, too, you need to glue the twine so that the bottle is stable.

Secondly, after the bottom, the process goes from bottom to top, the thread must go parallel to the bottom. Make sure that the rope does not bend, otherwise it will ruin the whole picture (of course, if this is not intended).

Carefully consider attaching the thread at the neck. A loose end may result in destruction of the entire composition.

After you have done the main work with threads, start decorating. Look at the photo. Among other things, you can decorate the finished work with additional flowers and sequins, sparkles and beads, as well as put flowers or wire shapes in bottles.

Note: Twine can be replaced with a crocheted pigtail. Experiment!

Simple, fast and incredibly beautiful. This is an easy way to decorate a bottle, for example, to a festive table.

Decor paints

Speaking of pictures, we cannot pass by and not tell how to paint bottles with acrylic paints and how to draw on bottles.

There are several ways to color a bottle. The first way is to paint from the inside. Using a syringe, diluted acrylic paint is introduced into the bottle and thoroughly smeared. The finished products are dried upside down to make the glass paint.

This method has its own advantage - quickly, simply, and most importantly, the bottle will not lose its glass sheen. But, on the other hand, you will not be able to draw a picture.

The second method allows you to draw on a bottle. For a simple and original pattern, electrical tape can be useful. To get perfectly even stripes, stick on electrical tape and paint on top.

If you want to draw an image on the surface or create an entire composition, it is best to prime the bottle before work.
