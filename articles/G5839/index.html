Lake shira





Khakassia, holidays in the territory of which is gradually becoming more and more popular, has a sharply continental climate with cold winters and short but warm summers. That is why with the onset of the season tourists come here to have time to recover their body.

Lake Shira, located in the Minusinsk depression, one hundred and sixty kilometers from Abakan, has a maximum depth of twenty meters. Its bottom rises with ledges to the gentle shores, covered with pebbles and a layer of sand, as a result of which quite comfortable beaches are formed. Mineral water of the lake with a high concentration of salts and silt mud have healing properties.

The healing power of Lake Shira is mentioned in ancient Khakass legends and legends. The first vacationers came here in 1873. After excellent treatment results, the popularity of the reservoir began to grow. And in 1891, the authorities opened the first resort in the province here, and today Lake Shira has become one of the most famous treatment zones in Siberia. On its shore there is a year-round sanatorium with 910 beds. In summer, patients come here to treat glaucoma. There is also a children's medical institution that accepts children from the age of six.
