Hotel Harman Hotel Sanya 5 * (China / Hainan Island / Sanya): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Many travelers seek to know all the facets of life by visiting resorts around the world. One of the exclusive places for an unforgettable vacation or a long-awaited vacation is spending time on the Chinese island of Hainan. Paradise life in a quiet secluded corner allows you to relax and distract from the problems and city bustle. The five-star hotel complex Harman Hotel Sanya is characterized by comfortable accommodation and friendly staff, which is pleased to welcome guests all year round.

Accommodation

The room fund of the Harman Hotel Sanya 5 * hotel (in Dadonghai) is represented by 485 rooms, which differ from each other by the offered views and dimensions. All of them belong to the class standard and have a modern design. Pastel colors prevail in the design. Numerous decorative elements contribute to a comfortable living: wall paintings, interesting flowers in vases and original coasters for them. The floors are covered with carpet or ceramic tiles. The saturation of light is provided by numerous sconces, spotlights and French glazing, which allows you to admire the surrounding landscapes without leaving the room. Each apartment has a balcony equipped with plastic furniture.

Tidying up and changing towels is a daily procedure performed by tidy maids. Lingerie is subject to change twice a week. It is possible to order food in the room around the clock for an additional fee. For those who wake up with difficulty, a wake-up service is provided.

Wi-Fi - freely available 24 hours a day.

The bathroom is open. Nearby are slippers, a hairdryer, bathrobes and personal hygiene products that contribute to the feeling of being at home.

Additional space is possible, but requires payment.

Bathing for babies

The kids will also have fun here: inflatable toys, circles and mattresses, slides, a basketball hoop and mini scooters will allow them to frolic to the maximum. The unique bright complex of descents, spraying water and flowing water flows surprises not only the kids, but also their parents like it.

Beach

Considering that the Harman Hotel Sanya 5 * hotel (Hainan) is located 1 km from the coastal zone, for the convenience of guests, a shuttle has been launched, which runs in both directions during the day. You can get there on foot by crossing the roadway. The beach is municipal; for guests of the Harman Hotel Sanya, sunbeds, umbrellas and towels are absolutely free. Enjoy the sultry rays of the sun and air baths, giving an excellent dark tan, - the goal of many who came here. Beach facilities include showers, a toilet and changing cabins. The rescue service tirelessly watches over the order on the beach and the safety of swimmers.

The shore is exclusively sandy, which allows kids to play with the little ones by building sand castles. A gentle entry into the water, a crystal-clear sea surface, sometimes replaced by surging waves, inspire every guest.

In order to diversify your leisure, you can play volleyball. Friendly teams every day fight for the championship, acquiring new acquaintances and comrades. Different types of water rides will amuse every guest. The most popular are scooters, banana riding and windsurfing.





For little guests

Pastime of young guests is planned by the administration of the Harman Resort Hotel Sanya 5 * to the smallest detail. In addition to endless water entertainment, there is a mini-club for the age group from 4 to 12 years. The spacious games room, hung with numerous balloons, is furnished with tables with chairs for board games and creative activities. Here you can draw, decorate or attend interesting workshops. Rubberized rug with many obstacles allows you to arrange sports. Tolokaras, cars, toys, a pool with balls, a swing and a slide - something that will not let the kids get bored. A cheerful and cheerful team of animators daily creates a holiday for children, transforming into different fairy-tale characters. The evening ends with a mini-disco where kids spend their last energy before a healthy night's sleep.

In restaurants - high chairs for free access. Babysitting services are provided for a fee. A qualified teacher will take the baby's time with exciting games and educational activities.





Sport and beauty

For those who prefer outdoor activities, it is proposed to visit the well-equipped fitness room, which is equipped with exercise machines, treadmills, equipped with dumbbells and other equipment. Exercise will help boost immunity, lose extra pounds and normalize processes in the body. Individual trainers will draw up a schedule of individual classes for more productive results.

A visit to the wellness center - the spa - will help relax your body and rejuvenate your appearance. A variety of procedures from wraps to peelings conducted by first-class professionals will appeal to all visitors. Sea water, mud and algae have a positive effect on strengthening immunity.
