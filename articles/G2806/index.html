Deer farm in Antsiferovo: description, history of creation. How to get to the farm?

In the modern world, being in a stone jungle, a person seeks to reunite with nature and its cloisters. It is believed that communication with her helps to relax and gives peace of mind. The desire of people did not go unnoticed.

There is one place in the Moscow region that is worth a visit - a deer farm in Antsiferovo. Previously, the village was the simplest and almost unknown to anyone. But today it has become one of the popular holiday destinations in the Moscow region. Tourists who know about it share positive reviews and recommendations.

Acquaintance with the village

You already know what is remarkable about the village of Antsiferovo. Here is a unique deer farm, which anyone can visit. Today this place is filled with tourists, although 10 years ago no one knew about it. It all began in 2003, when one trucker named Alexander Bondarchuk saved two deer from imminent death. He bought the animals that were prepared for slaughter and organized a nursery for them under the name "Reindeer." Antsiferovo from this moment began to gradually change, today it looks like a nature reserve.





Reindeer - who is he?

Animals that are so often found in children's tales have a fairly attractive appearance. The opportunity to get to know them better is opened by a deer farm in Antsiferovo.

The main advantage of these animals is beautiful long branchy horns. The height of the animals is from one meter to two, weight - from one hundred kilograms to two hundred. In the summer they are brown, in winter they turn gray with a white mane. The body is covered with short hair, but it is very thick and therefore warm.

At the first cold snap, males drop their horns. In females, this occurs at the birth of offspring, in the hot season. The horns of these animals can be very different. They differ in bends, trunk length, and size of processes.

Only domesticated individuals are not afraid of people, they can easily come closer to a person and even allow themselves to be stroked.





Features

Farm "Reindeer" in the village of Antsiferovo near Moscow began its work in 2007. Today it can be called part of the small North. Thanks to the work of employees, care and love for pets, animals quickly settled in this environment. The entire territory currently occupies approximately three hectares of land. Immediately upon entry, tourists enjoy a magnificent view. Nearby is a parking lot where you can park your car. Upon arrival at the place you will be met by a guide who is easy to notice because he is dressed in a national costume. This person will tell many interesting stories about wild animals, their appearance in the village and later life.

Excursions

In the nursery you will be met by a trained person - a musher who will tell you about the traditions and customs of the peoples of the North. His responsibilities include sled management and customer safety. The instructor is dressed in a national outfit, on animals - traditional harnesses and handmade jewelry.

A deer farm in Antsiferovo is open all year round for visitors. The tour will be unforgettable both in the summer and in the winter. Especially memorable will be the New Year program. During this period, you can meet fabulous deer, Santa Claus and the harmful Baba Yaga, who will steal one of the animals. In order to find it, you will go on an exciting journey. For success, you will have to show all your dexterity and quick wit. As in any fairy tale, the end will be happy, and, of course, no one will leave without gifts.

The deer farm in Antsiferovo is open daily from 11:00 to 17:00.





Territory rules and infrastructure

When visiting a nursery, people should familiarize themselves with the laws of this place. Consider a short list of what you need to consider when going on an excursion.

First, pay attention - you can feed animals only with those products that employees have provided to you. Secondly, in no case should you scare animals and touch their horns. For your own safety you should not go over fences. If you come with children, then you can not leave them unattended. Do not spoil the trees, bushes and flowers growing here. Be sure to keep clean.

If you want to take photographs or make a video for memory with animals, it is better to do this in the presence of a guide. It is forbidden to enter the farm with your pets. There is a designated area for smokers. You can not bring alcoholic beverages and be drunk on the farm.

Here you can visit a souvenir shop, buy souvenirs or crafts from deer skins and horns as a keepsake.

For lovers of tasty food there is "Yaranga" - a cozy cafe. They serve dinners, delicious hot tea with many different sweets.

And those who like to relax closer to nature, can take advantage of additional services, which include rental arbors and barbecue facilities. The meat for him is sold at Yaranga. You can also be trained in archery. It is also worth noting that there is a lake on the farm. If desired, you can fish there.

On weekends, visitors are provided with horseback riding, which they bring to the reindeer farm. Antsiferovo will leave you only positive emotions.





How to get there

Owners of personal vehicles need to go to Yegoryevskoye Shosse, drive through the large Moscow Ring and, not reaching fifty kilometers, turn left following the sign. On the right side will be Antsiferovo. "Deer Farm - How to get to this entertainment complex?" - Local residents will definitely answer this question. Special signs that are located on the outskirts of the village will also help, so it is simply impossible not to find it.
