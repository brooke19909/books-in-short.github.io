Hotel Grande Mare 4 * (Greece / Corfu Island): description, photos, reviews of tourists

The island part of Greece is famous for its tourist infrastructure and incredible natural beauties. That is why every year this country is visited by thousands of tourists who want to enjoy not only the mild climate and warm sea, but also the indescribable atmosphere of ancient history and high-quality service.

One of the most famous and popular Greek islands is Corfu. Despite its relatively small size, travelers are offered all possible amenities here. If we talk about hotels, then the Grande Mare 4 * hotel complex is considered a pretty good place to stop. But, of course, before booking, you should still learn more about this place and the living conditions that tourists offer here.





Features of the hotel complex location

When choosing a hotel, many tourists are guided, first of all, by the convenience of its location. So where to look for the hotel Grande Mare 4 *? Benitses, a picturesque and colorful fishing village - here, on a hillside, among dozens of other guest houses, painted in all colors of the rainbow, you have to spend a vacation.

By the way, close to within walking distance not only the beach, but also shops, restaurants and some attractions. For example, only 800 meters away is the Sissi Palace. The distance to the airport is also small - only 9 km. Therefore, do not worry about a long trip. The more vibrant city of Corfu (the island's capital) is approximately 12 km away and the distance to the seaport is no more than 14 km.





What does the hotel look like? Information about the territory

Hotel Grande Mare 4 * is not too big. It consists of a pair of cascading buildings, which in appearance resemble a beautiful country villa. The hotel area is very attractive - there are walking paths covered with old pavers, and terraces for relaxation, and areas for physical activity. Naturally, the entire area is generously decorated with Mediterranean plants, tall cypress trees, bright, flowering bushes.

The reviews confirm that it is really pleasant to spend time at the hotel. By the way, tourists have been welcomed here for decades, because the hotel was opened in 1980. The buildings, as well as the infrastructure itself, are constantly being improved and updated, and today this small cozy guesthouse can satisfy the needs of even the most demanding, fastidious tourists.





Hotel Grande Mare 4 * (Corfu): photo and additional info on rooms

Travelers respond positively about this place, confirming that the level of comfort and service here is pleasantly surprising. The Grande Mare Hotel Wellness 4 * itself is relatively small and consists of 48 spacious rooms, each of which has its own balcony or a cozy veranda (the views from the windows are really amazing).

Guests can count on a set of necessary furniture, as well as modern equipment, without which the life of a modern traveler is difficult to imagine. It offers a powerful air conditioning system, as well as a telephone, a TV (there are satellite channels, although not Russian-speaking) and a small safe in which documents are mainly stored. There is also a small refrigerator, which is very convenient.

The bathroom, although small, is functional and clean. Residents can take a shower, use the toilet and washbasin. Of course, you can count on clean towels and basic hygiene products. By the way, they clean here really every day, and very well.





Food: what do guests offer?

A good vacation is hard to imagine without delicious food, especially if you are in Greece, whose cuisine is famous all over the world. What can you expect a guest at the Grande Mare 4 * hotel? The reviews confirm that you can always eat at the hotel, and tourists are fed here deliciously.

As a rule, hotel accommodation includes breakfast, which is held as a buffet in a hotel restaurant. The menu is not bad - there are snacks, slices, salads, side dishes and fresh fruit. You can have lunch and dinner here - guests are offered both local and international dishes and, of course, different varieties of fish and seafood.

Nearby there are several cafes and traditional island taverns, where you can eat at any time of the day, enjoying not only the cooking of the local chefs, but also the color. Food in any case is not a problem for the tourist.





Leisure and amenities by the sea

Of course, arriving on the island, the traveler expects to enjoy an unhurried vacation on the seashore. Hotel Grande Mare 4 * is located on a hillside, and its territory faces directly to the beach - the distance here is no more than 30-50 meters.

According to reviews, the coast is beautiful. The entrance to the sea is convenient, and big waves do not rise so often. Not only adult travelers, but also kids feel comfortable here. Of course, you can use sun loungers and umbrellas, and beach towels will be given to you at the hotel.

Fans of more active leisure on the water are not bored here, because for a small fee you can ride a boat, rent a boat or a catamaran, and get a lot of vivid impressions from parasailing and water skiing. Some tourists swim with masks. They also learn the basics of diving and windsurfing.

Should I count on an additional service?

Of course, settling in the Grand Mare 4 * hotel, you can count on high quality service. For example, it is not difficult to exchange money at the hotel. There is a left-luggage office where many travelers leave luggage for a while. Laundry services are available.

Almost throughout the territory there is access to the Internet, however, the speed is not always good. The hotel also has its own conference room, where they organize not only business events, but also various holidays.

Guests can use the parking lot for free, and in the village itself you can easily rent a transport. Many tourists do just that, because it greatly facilitates movement around the island. The hotel does not have its own medical office, but in case of any problems, employees will definitely call a specialist.





How do tourists spend their leisure time on site?

They come to the Grande Mare 4 * hotel to relax, and here all the conditions are created for this. The territory of the hotel is large, and there is a pool in the courtyard. Guests can sunbathe here or relax on a sun lounger and enjoy a chilled cocktail.

The hotel has a large SPA center, where customers can choose from a variety of treatments for healing, relaxation and rejuvenation. By the way, the prices here are not too high, and local workers are experienced and qualified specialists. Lovers of fitness always and everywhere will appreciate the fitness center.

Arriving in Corfu, you just can not refuse excursions, because there are a lot of interesting places on the island. By the way, the hotel will tell you about where to go in the first place, and even help you get an interesting sightseeing tour.

Hotel Grande Mare 4 * ( Corfu Island, Benitses): conditions for holidays with children

As you know, many tourists today take their children with them, because kids also need rest, vivid impressions and wellness. For such guests, questions of comfort come to the fore. And reviews confirm that the Grande Mare 4 * hotel is really suitable for a vacation with the whole family.

Upon request, a large, folding bed with a soft mattress is delivered to the room without delay. In the restaurant you can always choose something tasty for the child, and nearby, in stores, they sell special cereals for the smallest tourists.

Traveler reviews and recommendations

Official information is not everything. It is much more interesting to find out what tourists themselves think about the hotel. Immediately it is worth saying that travelers leave positive reviews, and the hotel rating is quite high. Cozy, comfortable rooms, colorful grounds and a picturesque beach a few steps away are just what any tourist needs.

The service staff is experienced and qualified. Moreover, the employees are polite and always sincerely ready to solve any problems that arose. The food is also good. The breakfast menu is not very varied, but you are unlikely to be hungry. Lunches and dinners are delicious, because cooks really strive to pleasantly surprise travelers.

An advantage is the location in a quiet area - here you can relax from the noise of roads and city bustle. On the other hand, you can get to lively resorts and interesting sights relatively quickly. Tourists recommend the hotel Grande Mare 4 *. Corfu is a suitable place for beach and sightseeing holidays.
