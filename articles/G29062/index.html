Hotel Saint Thomas 5 * (Bulgaria, Sozopol): description, photos, reviews

Do you want to spend a vacation on the sea, admiring the azure surface and untouched nature? Accustomed to comfort and excellent service? Would you like to relax in Bulgaria? In this case, be sure to consider the hotel Saint Thomas 5 * (Sozopol) as a place to stay.



Description

The five-star Saint Thomas Hotel is 12 km from the town of Sozoplya and 15 km from Primorsko. The nearest airport is 60 km away.

The hotel complex was built in 2008. It includes 80 rooms located in several three- and five-story small buildings. The hotel has two restaurants, a 24-hour reception, a spa, four pools, bars, a souvenir shop, a grocery store, a children's playground and much more.



Saint Thomas 5 *: reviews

Since the hotel becomes literally our second home for the vacation period, it is very important not to make a mistake with his choice. After all, if you will not have a hotel on many points, then the impressions of a long-awaited vacation will not be so rosy. Therefore, experienced travelers recommend long before the expected date of departure to start collecting information about the hotels of a city. It is very useful to pay increased attention to the reviews left by those tourists who have recently been here. After all, former guests are trying to share information about what they had to face while living in a particular hotel. Thus, you can decide whether a particular hotel is suitable for you or not.

As for the Saint Thomas 5 * considered by us, the majority of our compatriots respond very warmly about him. This is confirmed by the rating of the hotel, which is compiled on the basis of guest ratings. It makes 4.3 points out of 5 maximum possible. Most of all, tourists praised the infrastructure, equipment of rooms and the beach of the hotel. Some complaints about the level of service. But we learn more about everything.



Housing, location

Many travelers, judging by their comments, were truly enthusiastic when they arrived at St. Thomas. After all, the hotel is located on a cliff surrounded by the azure sea. The hotel complex offers stunning views of the water surface.

As for the number of rooms, there were practically no complaints from the guests. So, the guests were pleased with the spacious, stylishly decorated and equipped with all necessary apartments. Especially many were pleased with the availability of mini-kitchens with a full set of dishes in the rooms. So, if you wish, you can always not only drink coffee or tea directly in the room, but also prepare something tasty by buying fresh products at the local market or in the supermarket.

Infrastructure

According to our compatriots, Saint Thomas 5 * (Bulgaria, Sozopol) is perfect for families with children of different ages, as well as for a romantic vacation for lovers. So, guests really liked the four pools with different depths. Next to them are equipped with sun terraces with comfortable sun loungers, a bar with various drinks and snacks. Guests are also pleased with the hotel's spa and gym. Tourists who arrived on vacation with young children, note that with entertainment for their children there were no difficulties. So, the hotel has a well-equipped playground, a games room. Also, the youngest guests are entertained by animators during the day, and in the evening a mini-disco is held.



Food

So, according to them, excellent cooks work at the hotel, and the administration does not save on products. The menu always has dishes of meat, fish, seafood, a variety of side dishes, fresh vegetables and fruits, delicious pastries, sweets. Sea

Judging by the comments of tourists, a beach holiday with former St. Thomas guests was definitely a success! According to them, the sea here is simply magnificent. The hotel has two beaches. One of them is located under the rock, you can go down the stairs to it. However, now it is not in its best form due to the collapse of its part.
