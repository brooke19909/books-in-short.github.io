Roaming Beeline. Information and instructions

Today, the technological world is constantly improving itself. What happened yesterday, today is getting better, faster, more powerful, better. In our era of technological progress and cellular communications must keep up with the times. Most of the world's mobile operators provide their customers with services that allow them to be in touch, being almost anywhere in the world. Every day they improve and become more accessible.

The Beeline company provides roaming services in 213 countries of the world. If you are going to travel and take your phone with you, then your number will not change. You can call relatives and friends at any time to share your impressions of the trip with them.

Connection of international roaming

The Beeline roaming service is activated automatically if there is a positive balance in the account. It is recommended to register in the networks with which online roaming will be opened. In this case, the subscriber can control his current balance in real time.

If you register in networks that do not support online roaming, you need to have an amount in excess of 600 rubles on your phone account. When lowering the balance threshold to 300 rubles, the service is automatically disabled. Before traveling abroad, you must connect international roaming.

Check your balance and make sure there are no bills to pay. To find out about the latter, you need to dial a simple command * 110 * 04 #. Then a guarantee fee of 1,500 rubles should be paid. Then make a call to 067409131 or dial * 110 * 131 #. In a few minutes, a message will be sent to your phone that confirms the successful connection of a service such as Beeline roaming.



Or you can come to any office of the company, where employees will provide the necessary assistance in connecting.

How are calls to Beeline roaming

Before you leave, inform your family that your mobile number remains unchanged. You should call just like they did before.

When roaming, numbers are dialed in the international format: +, country and city code, phone number. Example: +7 314 98765432.



Calls to the CPC (help)

While abroad, the subscriber can make calls to the support center. They are made strictly in an international format like this: +7 495 9748888. The call is absolutely free anywhere in the world.

Registration in roaming

Make sure that the country you are traveling to supports Beeline roaming, and that the city you intend to visit is served by your local cellular company.

Upon arrival, you do not need to type any additional commands - turn on the phone, and it will automatically register in the network of the Beeline partner. You will see the name of the local operator on the screen. If this does not happen, you must manually select the network through the menu of the mobile device.

In some cases, the frequency range of networks may differ from the Beeline range. Make sure your phone supports the frequencies of the manually selected guest network.
