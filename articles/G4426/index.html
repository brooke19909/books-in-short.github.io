Manos Palace 3 * (Greece / Crete) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If you want to spend your vacation in a picturesque, calm place by the sea, away from the noise of a big city and surrounded by magnificent nature, then come to the resort village of Crete called Bali. Yes Yes! Do not be surprised that Greece has its own Bali, which is a real paradise for travelers. If at the same time you want to save some money on accommodation, but stay in a comfortable hotel with excellent service, then feel free to choose Manos Palace 3 *. We will talk about what awaits guests here, as well as the impressions of our compatriots who have already stayed at this hotel.





Location

Manos Palace is located at the very beginning of the small tourist village of Crete - Bali. There are several taverns and restaurants where you can taste delicious dishes of national cuisine, as well as shops, pharmacies and other infrastructure. In addition, just a couple of hundred meters from the hotel there is a magnificent sandy beach. If you want vivid entertainment, then go to the neighboring town of Rethymnon. It is only 25 kilometers from Bali, which can be overcome both by bus and taxi. Relatively close to the hotel and to the airport. So, the air harbor is located in Heraklion, which is 50 kilometers from Bali. So after landing in Crete, you can reach the hotel in less than an hour.





Crete, Greece - Manos Palace 3 *: photo and description

This hotel is very small. So, it includes only ten apartments and nine studios. All of them are decorated in pleasant calm colors and equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Due to the small size and hospitable attitude of the owners and employees, the hotel creates almost a homely atmosphere. Family people, as well as tourists who want to relax in a calm, peaceful atmosphere, will surely like it very much. It features a small outdoor pool, Wi-Fi, a shop, and parking.





Rooms

All 19 rooms of the Manos Palace 3 * hotel (Crete, Bali) are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of guests. So, each of them has its own bathroom, TV with Russian channels, telephone, air conditioning and a balcony. Most apartments offer scenic sea views. Rooms are cleaned every day. Bed linen and towels are changed by the maids twice a week.

Nutrition

The cost of living in Manos Palace 3 * (Crete) includes breakfast. They pass in a cozy room on the fourth floor, from whose windows a magnificent view of the sea and the surrounding landscape opens. Here, guests will be offered delicious hot dishes, as well as a large selection of fresh fruits, vegetables and delicious pastries. Tourists can have lunch and dinner at taverns and restaurants nearby. According to our compatriots, they cook here very well, and the prices are very affordable.





Beach holiday

Manos Palace 3 * will become a real paradise for lovers of beach holidays. So, guests can walk to the nearest luxurious wide sandy beach in just three minutes. Here you can either rent a sun lounger and an umbrella, or sit completely free of charge on the sand on a beach towel brought with you .

Cost of living

As for the prices for accommodation in Manos Palace, they, according to tourists, are more than attractive for this level of service and living conditions. So, a seven-day stay of 2 people in a hotel in the month of August will cost from 400 euros.

Travel Vacation Reviews

Judging by the comments of our compatriots, the Manos Palace 3 * hotel is a great place for lovers of beach holidays. So, the nearest magnificent sandy beach is 200 meters from the hotel. The water here is clean, the area is equipped with comfortable sunbeds and umbrellas, as well as equipment for entertainment and sports on the water. If you want to soak up the sand, then renting a deck chair is not necessary. So, you can walk a few tens of meters to a site not occupied by beach equipment, and conveniently sit on a rug or towel brought with you. Along the coastline there are lots of taverns, cafes and bars, so you can have a bite to eat and refresh yourself with your favorite drink.

Since Manos Palace has a very small territory of its own, it cannot offer any special entertainment to its guests. So, there is only a small outdoor pool. Experienced travelers recommend not to get hung up on beach vacations and sunbathing, but to travel to Crete.
