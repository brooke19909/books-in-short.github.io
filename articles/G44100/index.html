How to make a phone case from paper: diagrams, instructions

There are times when there is no way to buy a finished phone case. But human imagination is limitless, and therefore do not despair. In this article, we will tell you how to make a phone case from paper in various ways.

Glossy openwork cover



It may seem unexpected to some, but a lot of things can be done from paper. And all of them will be not only beautiful, but also useful. You are probably already used to the silicone case for your phone. Today you will learn how to change the boring phone shell to a bright and original one.

How to make a phone case out of paper? First you need to draw a sketch. For example, you can use a floral ornament. If you do not know what to draw, then try to create a pattern of wavy lines. And then just make them thicker. Now we cut out the pattern and transfer it to thick paper or cardboard. And cut it out again. In the corners, you will need to make incisions and give the workpiece a phone shape. By the way, the latter can be done already on a smartphone to precisely fit the dimensions.

Cassette cover



The easiest way to build a portable case is to make it like some kind of packaging. For example, covers for cassettes. Such an accessory will definitely attract the attention of others with its originality.

How to make a phone case out of paper? First of all, we need to draw a scan of the future cover. We put the phone in the middle of a clean sheet and circle. We retreat one centimeter and circle the phone again. Well, of course, the cover should have a cover. Therefore, without retreating, we circle the phone for the third time. Now we cut the blank, on the new sheet we draw the same shape, only 1 cm wider. It remains to draw the sides. We add one centimeter above and below our pattern and cut it out. It remains to bend the resulting product to get a cover, as in the picture.

Hard case

In fact, making a more complex case is simple, however, it will take much more time. But if you select a scheme, then a novice will be able to cope with this task.



Consider how to make a phone case according to the scheme. It can be printed on a printer and slightly adapted to the model of your mobile phone. The case to protect your device is going to quickly if you correctly cut the patterns.
By the way, it is better to make them out of cardboard or thick paper, since a regular landscape sheet will not be able to hold its shape for a long time. Brown paper case



One of the simple and effective ways to decorate your mobile phone is to wrap it with beautiful packaging. For this purpose, it is not necessary to take paper in which gifts are wrapped; any color sheet is suitable. On it later it will be possible to stick funny stickers or to paint with felt-tip pens. The wrapping paper mobile phone case will be assembled on the basis of the existing silicone bumper. It is to him that we will stick colored sheets. How to do it? We put the phone on paper and wrap it so that there are quite distinguishable contours. We take out the phone, cut out the layout of the cover from paper to size. We attach it to the silicone case with a glue gun or double-sided tape. We bend the edges of the paper under the bumper and fix it there with glue.

Envelope Cover



One of the easiest ways to protect your phone from the environment is to put your smartphone in a protective case. Which can be a paper envelope. Why not? In addition, this version of the case can be made in various variations. In the first case, it will be just a paper case for the phone, and in the second case with a window. Case with window



Such a case can be made independently, or you can simply redo the existing packaging. We will tell you how to make a do-it- yourself phone case from paper. First we need to make a pattern.
We put the phone on paper and circle it. We retreat 1 cm and circle the phone again. And again, we retreat 1 cm. From the bottom you need to make the indent smaller, and from above it should be 5 cm. We cut out the resulting pattern and transfer it to thick paper. You can use cardboard for this purpose. In the front part you need to outline a hole. It should copy the shape of the phone screen. To make it easier to do this, you can make a pattern. Cut a hole. Now we need to fold our case. It will resemble a box. If desired, you can make a hole for charging.

If you do not want to go in such a difficult way, then you can ease your task. To do this, you need to find a box that is ideal for the size of your phone. If it is not very beautiful, then you can glue it with wrapping paper or paint it. It is advisable to use a spray can. Apply carefully and evenly. And the last - we cut a window to fit the screen.

Smart case made of paper



Such a device will simultaneously protect your phone and serve as a stand for it. How to make it? Again, you have to make a pattern. It will be 3 rectangles. After we have made a pattern, we transfer it to a beautiful thick paper. It is advisable to make the rectangles a little larger than the phone. Cut out the cover. Now, using double-sided tape, glue one part to the lid. Our smart case is ready.

If you plan to watch movies on the phone, you will need to make a deep rut in the front of the case. So the cover can perform not only a protective function, but also act as a stand. What the design should look like, you can look at the photos presented in the article.
