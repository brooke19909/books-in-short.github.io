How to make the steering wheel skin?

Are you a frequent visitor to a car service and used to trust your iron friend to mechanics? But there is not always a need to visit auto repair shops. Some small work, such as, for example, arranging a salon, can be done independently. So, hauling a steering wheel with leather with your own hands is very easy, and it is available even for a novice motorist. Let's consider several options for how to turn the main control subject into a rather attractive and at the same time convenient little thing.





Classic - black color

The first thing to think about when deciding to make the steering wheel with leather by replacing the cover is the choice of material color. But this problem is quite easy to solve. Better to focus on the classics. Black color in this case will be ideal. With any stylistic orientation, such a noble leather steering wheel will look exquisite and at the same time restrained. After deciding on the color, familiarize yourself with the properties of different materials. Of all, the most practical, supple and soft is perforated leather. Although it is not so sore as smooth, but in comfort it is simply impossible to compare with it. If such material is right at your fingertips, you can safely get to work, guided by further instructions.





We make the hauling steering wheel in our own template

  1. Remove the steering wheel from the base. To do this, first unscrew the screws from the housing, and then the fastening nut from the steering shaft.
  2. If there was already a tight covering, remove it.
  3. Then make the layout as follows:

    - Wrap the steering wheel with a thick film (or simple store bags);
    - cover the plastic layer with paper sticky tape;
    - draw a future cut line with a marker or pen;
    - with a sharp knife, carefully dissect the film layer stretched over the steering wheel according to the drawn marks, resulting in several large parts;
    - perform cutting by applying the material to the paper and circling taking into account the allowances for the seams (15-18 mm), as the hauling of the skin on the steering wheel is carried out with a certain supply of material.

  4. Try the received parts on the steering wheel to make sure that the cutting is correct.

Making a new steering wheel: stitching parts and stretching

  1. Fold all received parts in the necessary sequence and stitch. At the same time, adjust the machine so that it turns out not too frequent, but not rare stitches. It is advisable to overcast all open sections to avoid scattering of the edge. For work, you need to take threads containing kapron (for greater strength).
  2. Pull the semi-finished leather cover onto the steering wheel. The canvas should lie easily and freely. But at the same time, the part must necessarily measure the body tightly.
  3. Smooth wrinkles and bumps.
  4. In conclusion, do the most important work - connecting the edges of the canvas.
  5. You can use one of the three sewing techniques for this - macram√©, pigtail, or sports stitch. Any of the options looks ready-made neatly, moreover, they are all very reliable.
  6. Establish the sheathed steering wheel in its place and check the strength of fastening.

We use factory preparation

It is much faster and easier to carry out the hauling of the steering wheel with leather when using a ready-made cover made at the factory. By purchasing a blank specifically designed for your car brand, you will save yourself from the painstaking work of making patterns, patterns, and sizing by adjusting seam allowances. In this case, the steering wheel skin can be done in just an hour and a half. Therefore, if you have the opportunity to purchase a factory part, choose this option for decorating the steering wheel.
