Raw materials and additional materials

It should be understood that the source material (also called a T-shirt) should not be faded, heavily washed or gaping with picturesque holes, otherwise it would be better to look for a workshop on weaving rugs from knitted flagella. Worn clothes, even after a very high-quality alteration, will look untidy.

An ideal option would be a new T-shirt, but not the right size, of course, that it cannot be small for its owner, rather, on the contrary, most of the works suggest ideas on how to make a dress out of a large T-shirt.

Before you start work, it is important to figure out whether you have enough material, to consider not only the style of the dress itself, but also whether there will be decor on it. You may need a few more T-shirts, which should be combined with each other in color and texture of the fabric, so that the finished product looks organic and natural.

For decoration, lace, rhinestones, buttons or decals are suitable. Those who have artistic talent can try to make a pattern or drawing on the fabric. Handmade variations

There are several ways of making a dress from a T-shirt with your own hands, and each of them can be finalized by adding your own exclusive touches to the craft. The simplest and most affordable option is to take very large clothes and, lowering the T-shirt to the chest through the neck, tuck both sleeves inward. This method does not require cutting and sewing, so you can not be afraid to spoil the thing. As a result, you can get a great strapless home or beach dress . A loose style, the absence of strapless and straps will not allow to evaporate even on the hottest day.





Other methods require reshaping and stitching the details of the future dress among themselves, so for their manufacture you need a sewing machine and some skills. Note that knitted fabric is quite delicate and specific in work. To facilitate the process of laying stitches, it is better to use a special needle for knitwear, its feature is that it does not cut through interwoven threads, but gently passes through them. Due to this, ugly holes are not formed in the material, which eventually let the arrows.

Work technology

A fitted T-shirt or short-sleeved T-shirt that needs to be cut along the waistline will act as the top of the future dress. Next, sheets of other colors are hemmed to it. Bright sundress from nothing

The following method demonstrates how to make a dress from a men's t-shirt. Of course, you can take a female one, but you should look for a rather large size, because the alteration requires cutting out details that, after stitching, will become a dress. As you can see in the photo below, this method requires trimming the sleeves and neck. The rest of the t-shirt needs to be cut taking into account the bends of the female figure, making a chest and waist line on the workpiece.





The next step will be trimming the part that will hold the dress on his chest so that it does not fall off, you need to insert an elastic band into it. It can be a narrow elastic band in the upper and lower parts of the chest. As an alternative, seamstresses often use rubberized thread, which is sewn with a sewing machine and will turn the entire part into a wide elastic band. Having sewed the bottom and top together, laying the side lines, you get a cute dress made by yourself.
