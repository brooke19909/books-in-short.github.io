Autumn picture of leaves. DIY beauty

With the advent of autumn, the eye is pleased with the riot of colors. I want to enjoy this beauty longer. And this is quite real, remember: back in kindergarten, as soon as the leaves began to fall, you as a whole group went to the site in search of the most beautiful elements for the herbarium. Soon, the wall in the room of the parents or in the hall was decorated with paintings of dried leaves.



This article is mainly devoted to parents whose children attend kindergarten or school, as the annual tasks are not much different, and the fantasy ends very quickly. Here you can find interesting ideas for autumn crafts, for example, such as a picture of leaves. With your own hands to create beauty that pleases the eye throughout the year is not very difficult.

Crafts for Kids

It is easy and informative to work with natural material. For the smallest, leaf crafts can be an interesting fairy tale or a fun adventure.

The first stage is the search for suitable leaves and seeds. It is not necessary to use only maple leaves, although they affect a variety of colors and shades. From colored material, you can try to make a bird, a fungus, a typewriter or a fish. Choose simple ideas so your child can do most of the work on his own.

Not only yellow, red and brown materials can be used; add green color and you will see how your picture from leaves will be transformed. With your own hands, you can create a real masterpiece using improvised means. Do not leave flowers aside - even when dried, they look great! Note: dry rose petals look great on the tail of a peacock.

For schoolchildren and their parents





Agree, if the student brings a butterfly or an elephant made of colorful leaves, then it will be too simple. And our task is to surprise the teacher and get an excellent grade.

So, a picture of leaves (any schoolchild can do it with his own hands) can become a story if you use, say, colored paper. Try making a hedgehog carrying an apple, or a goldfish emerging from the ocean with a present for the old man. Everything that is difficult to lay out from the leaves (animal faces, small parts, etc.) can be made of colored paper, cardboard or plasticine. So your picture of leaves with your own hands will look not only neat, but also spectacular.

Another version of the craft is to cut letters from the leaves, which later easily add up to words. Attention: fresh leaves should be cut, as otherwise they will crumble.

Ideas for Parents

To decorate your home interior, dry leaves are a great option. You can make a bouquet, but it is commonplace. Try to twist roses out of leaves not yet dried. Such a bouquet will surprise all guests and will be remembered by everyone for a long time. This is unusual and interesting. You can decorate a boring photo frame by sticking small leaves with a special gun. Bright colors of autumn will bring a pleasant variety to your interior.



Pictures of leaves, photos of which are presented in the article, can be performed by both children and parents. This activity will give pleasure and give a good mood.
