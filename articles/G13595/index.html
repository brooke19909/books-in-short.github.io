The first stage, how to tie a bow from a satin ribbon "Butterfly"

Wrap the ribbon with the loop up. Then, holding your finger, pull the free part to the intersection of the ends. The loops formed on both sides should be the same size. It is very important to reliably hold the work so that there is no bias and asymmetry of the picture.



The second stage, how to tie a bow from a satin ribbon "Butterfly"

Clutching the workpiece firmly with your fingers, wrap one of the free ends, forming an additional loop. Do the same with the other side. Turn the work a hundred and eighty degrees so that the free ends hang down. Secure the joint with a thin and flexible wire. Then decorate the middle with the fabric used and carefully process the seam from the wrong side. Cut the loose ends a bit diagonally and lightly burn with a match. The simplest ribbon bow is ready.
