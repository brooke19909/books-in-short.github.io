Kaluga-Tula route: planning a trip wisely

Tula and Kaluga are the administrative centers of two neighboring regions. Both cities are rich in historical sights and various economic objects. Accordingly, there is nothing surprising in the fact that many residents of Kaluga periodically visit Tula on personal or work matters, just as the Tula people often visit Kaluga residents. The distance between cities is only 110 kilometers. Regional centers are connected by the Kaluga-Tula highway and railways. Which route is the most convenient?

Driving a car



The Kaluga-Tula route is not at all difficult to overcome on a personal vehicle. It‚Äôs not necessary to take a navigator on this trip, since the road is simple and direct. All you need is to leave the city on the Kaluga-Ryazan highway and then follow the signs. There are practically no sharp turns and difficult road junctions. The route runs through such settlements as the village of Akhlebinino, the village of Plastovo, the village of Old Petrishchevo, the village of Yamny, the working village of Plekhanovo. If your trip is of a tourist nature, many interesting sights can be seen along the way. Those who are in a hurry for some business and are interested in getting there faster, we advise you to go straight and carefully look at the signs. Travel time will be one and a half to two hours.

Intercity Buses

Regular buses of Kaluga-Tula leave daily from the city bus station. Tickets can be purchased at the box office in advance or for the next flight. If you are not satisfied with the departure time for direct flights, you should consider the bus schedule from other regions making a stop in Kaluga. The Smolensk-Tula route is quite convenient in this regard. You can also get to the city of gunsmiths on Kaluga buses passing through it to other cities. These are Kaluga-Voronezh and Kaluga-Oryol. Travel time will be about two to two and a half hours. Regular intercity buses operate with at least one stop at a gas station, where passengers can visit the store and toilet.

What type of transport is the most profitable?





For successful travel planning and the right choice of a particular type of transport, all significant factors should be considered. This is the duration and duration of the visit to the city, its purpose, the presence of baggage and some other conditions. Let the Tula-Kaluga highway be fairly simple, but if you are traveling with a minimal amount of things and aiming to relax, it makes sense to seriously think about choosing a bus or electric train. But to transport large luggage between neighboring cities is most convenient, on the contrary, in a private car. Another important parameter is travel time. It turns out that the fastest way you will reach is by car, a little longer by regular bus, and the longest journey will take by train. If you have chosen some type of transport from public, be sure to call the station in advance and check the schedule. It is also useful to pre-purchase tickets, especially if it is important for you to leave on a specific day and time.
