Where to relax in July at sea? Beach holidays in July

Where to relax in July at sea? A similar question at the beginning of the holiday season is one of the most relevant for the vast majority of tourists. The desire to plan a route in advance and determine the country of visit is quite understandable. In the middle of summer, comfortable conditions for recreation do not exist in every region. What, then, to stop at, where to go? What to choose: domestic south or direction a little further?

General recommendations: beginning of the month

So where to go in July to the sea? If you are going to relax in the early days of the month, it is recommended to pay attention to countries such as Turkey, Montenegro, Greece and Cyprus. In the first decade of July it is still moderately hot, and the sea heats up to the most optimal temperature. In just a couple of weeks, the air will reach maximum levels, and being in resorts can hardly be pleasant, especially if tourists and children go on a trip.





More about resorts

Let's talk a little more about these countries. Of course, the most popular destination is Turkey, where hundreds of thousands of Russians from all over our vast Motherland rest here every year. The main advantages of local recreation include:

  • Excellent July weather (water - 26 degrees, air - up to 35 degrees).
  • Developed hotel chain (accommodation options for every taste and budget).
  • An abundance of all kinds of entertainment, high-quality animation (leisure for both adults and children).
  • Fairly reasonable prices.

Cyprus is also considered a popular, but more European destination. In addition to an upscale beach holiday, here tourists can enjoy excellent cuisine, natural beauties and monuments of local culture. Young people prefer to relax in Ayia Napa - this small city is famous for its nightclubs and other entertainment venues, in addition, there is a water park for children and adolescents.

Do not like to "bother" with visas and documents? Welcome to Montenegro: European holidays and a minimum of bureaucratic delays have made this destination a real find for tourists from Russia. In Montenegro, many couples with children rest, this trend is explained by the excellent ecological situation and the unique local nature.





General recommendations: mid-month

For those who are looking for tours to the sea in July, implying a vacation in the middle of the month, it is recommended to pay attention to directions closer to Russia. So, Bulgaria may be an ideal solution. Its distinctive features include a comfortable climate and well-developed infrastructure.

Tourists who prefer a moderate temperature rather than a hot heat may come from the Baltic countries (for example, Lithuania or Latvia). Here you can swim, if necessary, sunbathe or visit the rich excursion program to local attractions. In addition, such resorts are distinguished by excellent environmental conditions.

In mid-July, the high season begins in domestic resorts - in the Crimea, on the shores of the Azov and Black Seas. We will talk about them in more detail later.





Where better to go at the end of the month

Toward the end of July, you can comfortably relax in a number of European cities. Excellent sunny weather during this period usually reigns in Spain, France, and also in the south of Italy. Holidays on the coast here can be quite successfully combined with sightseeing programs and shopping tours.

The advantages of a European beach holiday can be enumerated endlessly. Here, excellent service, good environmental conditions and clean water, plenty of entertainment and opportunities for organizing interesting leisure activities. Where to relax in July at sea in Europe? In addition to the states mentioned above, Portugal or Croatia can become a worthy solution.





Where better not to go?

Answering the question of where to go to the sea in July, it is important to immediately discard the least comfortable tourist destinations. A large number of tourists arrives very short-sighted and acquires permits in the most popular countries, not taking into account their climatic features. It's about Egypt and Thailand. In the first case, in mid-summer the air temperature reaches its maximum, and intolerable heat reigns everywhere (about 45 degrees, the air is very hot and dry). Such conditions for an unprepared person, and even more so a child, can be not only uncomfortable, but also dangerous to health.

In the second case (Thailand) - over the most part of the country there are prolonged rains that make relaxing on the beach a very unpleasant activity. Of course, there are exceptions to the stated rules. So, the island of Koh Samui, belonging to the territory of the Kingdom, - may be an ideal option for a vacation in the month of July.





Domestic resort Anapa

With the development of the hotel and tourism industry in our country, more and more Russians prefer local resorts. For such trips, you do not need to change the currency, learn the language and make a visa. In addition, such options are great for parents with children, because the kids do not need to undergo prolonged acclimatization.

A good option might be the decision to visit the sea in Anapa. In July, the most favorable weather prevails in the city, the sun shines almost every day, and a couple of rains only knock down moderate heat. In the daytime, the air temperature warms up to almost 30 degrees, at night, it fluctuates around 20-22, with regard to water temperature, the monthly average is usually about 25 degrees.

What else can you do if you have already appreciated the sea in Anapa? In July, the tourist season reaches its peak, so any tourist will definitely be able to find entertainment for themselves. So, supporters of an active beach holiday will be able to enjoy:

  • diving
  • sailing;
  • a banana ride
  • riding extreme slides.

Fans of culture and history will certainly be inspired by a visit to the local knight's castle or reconstruction of an African village. The youngest travelers will enjoy visiting the dolphinarium.





Russian resort Gelendzhik

You can have a good rest in the middle of summer not only in Anapa. Many lovers of domestic tourism go to appreciate the sea in Gelendzhik in July. The city is picturesque and has excellent climatic conditions, there are forests, and waterfalls, and mineral waters, as well as real vineyards. The urban resort area occupies about 100 kilometers of the sea coast, the air temperature is slightly higher than the water temperature, which provides excellent conditions for sunbathing and water procedures.

Those who want to relax can either stay in comfortable hotels or choose a more affordable offer in the private sector, as well as purchase a ticket for an examination and further treatment in a sanatorium.

Other resorts

It should be noted immediately that the Black Sea in July is most popular, at this time the peak of the season begins in almost all resort towns. It usually lasts about two weeks, and then it slowly decreases. In the middle of the month, water in the sea can warm up to a record 27 degrees, swimming in it is a pleasure.

In addition to the above-mentioned resorts, tourists prefer to visit cities such as Tuapse and Dagomys on the Black Sea coast . For an interesting vacation with children, Anapa or Adler are more suitable, however, the southern capital of Russia, Sochi, is considered a real star. In addition to the coast, the city has a huge amount of entertainment. Going here, be sure to visit the arboretum, amusement park, Park Riviera.

Starting this year, more and more Russian tourists come to rest in Crimea. The most popular cities are Yalta and Alushta. July is considered the most popular holiday month.





The sea is inexpensive

Where can I relax economically? The answer to this question is simple - visit Abkhazia. Despite the fact that it is customary to classify a trip to this amazing country as economic trips, you are unlikely to forget such a vacation soon. Its main advantages include:

  • clean sea;
  • lack of crowds of tourists;
  • subtropical climate, which makes it easy to tolerate high temperatures;
  • clean air and an abundance of pine trees, making it incredibly useful;
  • reasonable prices;
  • lack of visas;
  • numerous ways to enter the country;
  • the opportunity to communicate in Russian;
  • Attractions and cultural centers available for sightseeing.

Where to relax in July at sea in Abkhazia? The most popular local resorts are Pitsunda, Gagra and Sukhum.

Instead of a conclusion

Now you know where to relax in July at sea. It should be noted that each traveler chooses the ideal route for himself, starting from his own expectations, preferences and financial opportunities.
