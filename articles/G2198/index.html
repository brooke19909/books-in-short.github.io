DIY crafts from natural material: several options

The fantasy of children is limitless. Very useful classes for making various souvenirs. Among them, crafts from natural materials occupy a huge place . Do it yourself with your own hands is not difficult if, with the onset of autumn, stock up on everything you need. The material can serve as leaves of different trees, cones, twigs, acorns, plant seeds, shells, chestnuts, pebbles, shells, needles, bulrushes. Let's try to make various crafts from natural material with our own hands. For work, you may also need colored paper, threads, glue, an awl, scissors, various ribbons.





How to make crafts from natural material with your own hands? Interesting ideas

Panel

For a basis, take thick paper or cardboard. Fantasy, lay out the available material - cones, shells, pebbles, plant seeds. Glue with strong glue. After drying, it is recommended to open the panel with varnish from above.

Snails

Blind small snails from the dark brown plasticine. Attach a sea shell on top. Such a composition, decorated with pebbles, will look very original in a flower pot.

Funny fox

To make this autumn applique, you need small dry leaves of yellow or orange shades of different sizes. Skillfully arranging them on a paper basis, glue it with glue. With a felt-tip pen, draw a muzzle of a fox.

Unusual hedgehog

The basis of the hedgehog is plasticine. And the needles will be ordinary sunflower seeds. Decorate the toy with a beautiful leaf.
