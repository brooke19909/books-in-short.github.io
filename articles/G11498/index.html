Adress Beach Hotel - quality and comfort at an affordable price

The small hotel called Adress Beach Hotel is located in Beldiri. The nearest major city of Kemer is just over 15 km, more than 40 km will have to go to the international airport in Antalya. At the same time, the sea is only 70 meters away.





Despite its modest size, Adress Beach Hotel attracts guests with a cozy design of rooms, a good location, as well as relatively low prices for accommodation. The four-story building of the hotel was built in 2001. The total area occupied by the complex is about 2.5 thousand square meters.

In total, Adress Beach Hotel has 61 rooms open to guests, 55 of them are standard for two or three people, and 6 rooms can accommodate four people, suitable for families. From the windows of the hotel, vacationers can enjoy beautiful views of either the sea or the mountains of amazing beauty, as well as the courtyard with a pool and garden. All rooms must have a TV with a package of satellite TV channels, including Russian, telephone, air conditioning, toilet and shower, a hairdryer and a small balcony. Linen, towels and room cleaning are done 3 times a week.

Adress Beach Hotel has its own pebble beach in close proximity to the building, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided for free, but you will have to pay extra for a towel. Unfortunately, a beach bar is not provided.





Meals at Adress Beach Hotel are organized according to the all-inclusive principle that has become classic for Turkish hotels. For breakfast, lunch and dinner, guests can choose any dish they like, in between light snacks are available, and guests can enjoy alcoholic and non-alcoholic local and imported drinks.



The hotel has two outdoor pools: one for adults and one for children. For children, entertainment programs are organized with the involvement of animators. Babysitting services are available at an additional cost. Of the free entertainment you can note darts, TV lounge, table tennis. In the lobby area there is the opportunity to access the Internet using Wi-Fi. Also, for a fee, guests can enjoy water sports on the coast, a sauna, a Turkish bath and massage, a disco. Since the hotel is located in a small town, a short distance from major tourist centers, the cost of living is oriented towards vacationers with an average income. At the same time, the level of service is not lower than in eminent hotels. Among Russians, this complex is popular with couples with children who prefer a relaxing holiday by the sea for a moderate fee with a fairly wide list of services included. Booking a room is not difficult, as the hotel can be found on the popular sites of Russian tour operators.
