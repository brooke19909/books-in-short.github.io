Which is better: single-compressor or two-compressor device?

It is known that the compressor is the ‚Äúheart‚Äù of the refrigerator, pumping and compressing freon steam. From this compression, the vapor condenses into a liquid state, and then, getting into the evaporator, evaporates, absorbing the heat of the internal space of the refrigerator. Two-chamber refrigerators are one- and two-compressor.



In two-compressor machines, one compressor works for the refrigerator, and the other for the freezer. How does he deal with two refrigerated compartments at the same time? He is helped by dividing one stream of cold into two (into the freezer and refrigerator), the distribution solenoid valve. Thus, the Liebherr two-chamber refrigerator can be either single- or double-compressor.

Refrigerator cooling system, cost-effective

Refrigerator buyers have an alternative choice between the two types of cooling systems. The more traditional jargon is called the ‚Äúcrying evaporator‚Äù (we will consider the principle of its operation below), and the newer is called the forced ventilation system.

They coexist, since the traditional system also has its strengths: greater simplicity and, as a consequence, profitability.

Imagine the principle of the "crying evaporator". When the compressor is operating, frost is formed on it. After the compressor stops, this frost melts and goes into the drainage system in the form of water. When the compressor is turned on again, the resulting water evaporates again, while absorbing heat.



The NoFrost system works on simultaneous inclusion together with the fan compressor. Her freeze is drier. However, it is more expensive, more energy-intensive, and its operation is accompanied by fan noise. The main advantage of NoFrost is a better temperature distribution than when cooling with a ‚Äúcrying evaporator‚Äù.

The level of energy-saving technologies used in the design of a particular brand of refrigerator was reflected in the energy consumption indicator. A, A + and A ++ (as you know, A ++ is the most economical class).

Unfortunately, today this problem is typical not only for Liebherr, but also for other large manufacturers of refrigerators: while there are practically no complaints about the unit itself, there are enough questions about its handles (which break during operation). (By the way, there are perhaps fewer similar claims to the company we are considering than to others.)

Why, with Liebherr‚Äôs high market reputation, does the refrigerator handle still break? What are the causes of this phenomenon? Indeed, in modern refrigerators, the handle does not look like a "bulwark of reliability." Indeed, its working plastic fragment half a centimeter thick has a fairly significant load, especially when opening the freezer, the door of which under the influence of internal low temperature has the property of "sticking".

Are manufacturers, reputable companies, allowed the problem of pens to drift?

Not at all. The indicated bottleneck is not a structural defect in the handle, but a violation of the technology for the production of plastic from which it is made. As you know, when assembling by an orthodox German manufacturer of the company Liebherr, the handle for the refrigerator must contain a marking like plastic. It is the material approved by the technologists that ensures the regular functioning of this part and frees users from unnecessary headaches. Cautious people on the eve of the purchase clarify this point and necessarily carry out a ‚Äúface control‚Äù of the original marked pens. Agree that this prevention reduces the likelihood that you will come across the specified part of inadequate quality.

The reason for the marriage was caused by the ‚Äúdaughters‚Äù of the parent company, namely, their negligent fulfillment of the function of localizing production, that is, the manufacture of some components at home. Perhaps homegrown technologists send unsuitable plastic to the injection machine.

What to do if after buying the Liebherr unit the handle for the refrigerator still broke. There are two ways: contact the warranty repair line (a shorter way, but you will get a pen of the same quality) or take the trouble to take a photograph of the breakdown, send it with a letter to the parent company and wait for the package with the original pen.
