Briefly about the hotel

This hotel complex is located in Sharjah. Historically, this city was one of the richest in the entire region. It is also quite ancient, since the first settlements were founded here more than five millennia ago. Now it is the third largest metropolis in all the Emirates.

The city of Sharjah is located on the shores of the Persian Gulf, near Dubai and Ajman. Accordingly, the airport can be reached from here quickly, which is a plus in terms of location. Yes, and Dubai with Ajman is interesting to many to see. From Swiss Belhotel Sharjah 4 * you can get anywhere, because there is a well-developed transport connection.

But, of course, the most important thing is the proximity to the sea. You can walk to the beach in just a few minutes. And this is a huge plus for tourists, since most of them go to the UAE precisely for the sake of the sea.

The list of services that Swiss Belhotel Sharjah 4 * offers its guests is quite wide. This hotel complex has everything that can make your stay more comfortable and carefree - from parking to free Wi-Fi.
