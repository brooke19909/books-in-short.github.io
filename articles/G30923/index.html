Coin "Matron Moscow": types, value, cost

The acquisition of valuable metals is a very profitable investment. Exclusive coins not only do not lose their cost during inflation, but over time are appreciated even more by collectors. We will present you one of these items. This is the Moscow Matron coin.

Varieties

So, more details. Today, there are three variations of an exclusive product.

One of the varieties of the Matron Moskovskaya coin was released in 2015 in the Russian Federation by the Imperial Mint. It is made entirely of silver of the 925th test. On its obverse ("tails", front side) the saint's face is depicted, and on the reverse ("eagle", the reverse side) is a brief prayer to Matrona. The uniqueness of the coin lies in the fact that on its surface with a diameter of 23 mm, the smallest details of the image in the "proof-like" ("mirror side") technique are meticulously executed. The weight of pure silver in the product is 28.76 g.
