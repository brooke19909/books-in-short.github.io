Car refrigerator: how to choose a quality product



Motorists who travel frequently have repeatedly thought about how to maintain the quality of their products. Going on a country trip or on a long journey, take a car refrigerator with you. It will help you avoid a lot of problems with the products. Car refrigerator - how to choose and what to pay attention to first of all? If you are used to traveling with your family - great. Make a map of the road and mark shops and markets on it. Now it will become clear to you that you do not need to take a huge amount of products with you, because along the route you can replenish them.

The main task of an auto-refrigerator is to protect food from high temperatures and prevent them from deteriorating.

Car refrigerator. How to choose the right one? What you should pay attention to?

There is a lot to consider in your choice: first, appearance. If there are already any scratches or dents on it, then it is better not to take such a device, despite the favorable discount from the store. Suddenly he will fail at the most inopportune moment, and he will have to repair it or buy a new one. Why pay twice? Secondly, it must be resistant to shocks, because you will not always ride on a perfectly smooth road. Therefore, an auto-refrigerator should keep products in an appropriate form. Since it is in direct contact with food, it must be environmentally friendly.

Car refrigerator. How to choose, given the volume and shape?

A family of three is enough to buy a car refrigerator with a volume of 30 liters, because even when traveling long distances you will have the opportunity to replenish food along the route. Take care of its size. If you want to put a two-liter bottle with a drink there, then it should fit there. Consider the dimensions of the refrigerator.

Car refrigerator. How to choose, given the internal system of the mechanism

Most auto-refrigerators operate on a cigarette lighter and are constantly recharged from the battery, which can lead to its complete discharge. To avoid this, the car refrigerator should be equipped with a device that automatically blocks the flow of energy. You must consider the length of the cord, as you may need to place it at the rear of the car.

Car refrigerator. Which type to choose?

Types of automobile refrigerators: absorption, thermoelectric or compressor.

When choosing the type of power supply for an auto-refrigerator, you need to decide how much energy you can allocate for the proper operation of this device. The most economical are compressor refrigerators. They quickly gain the set temperature, but are very sensitive to external vibrations. Absorption refrigerators do not work on freon, but on a water-ammonia solution. They can run on gas. It is more economical. They work silently, but are excessively sensitive to roll of the car.

If your chosen refrigerator has two sections, then when you open one of them, you do not let heat into the second, and thereby save its energy. Pay attention to the equipment cover. It should fit snugly, otherwise the cold will seep through the slotted holes of the structure.

For car refrigerators, the price is different. Absorption refrigerators are cheaper (from 12 thousand rubles) than compressor ones (from 25 thousand). Refrigerators that can heat food are more expensive than everyone else.
