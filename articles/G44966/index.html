How to get Sportmaster bonuses yourself?

Sportmaster is an example of a company with a well-developed marketing department. Marketers "Sportmaster" have developed an excellent customer program that encourages you to return to the store again and again and make purchases.

How to become a member of the Sportmaster family?

Get bonuses from the "Sportmaster" is actually very easy and accessible to anyone, if he is not too lazy. Profitable transactions can be made not only in the store, but also directly on the official website. From this, neither the amount nor the number of charges will change.



The formula is simple. 1 point on the card is equal to 1 ruble of the Russian Federation in the amount of the purchase. If the goods were purchased in the amount of 1000 rubles, then 1000 points will be credited to the card. A plastic card is issued free of charge. You can get it on hand after making your first purchase at the Sportmaster store. Filling out the form and getting 500 bonuses is all that needs to be done. How to register online?

You can get Sportmaster bonuses on the company's website for free. There is a special section on registration. If you go into it and go through the standard questionnaire, the first 500 bonuses will be automatically credited to the virtual bonus card.

The loyalty program is not only a point accumulation system. The more purchases, the more you can get Sportmaster bonuses. At the same time, program participants have many privileges. Some discounts are only valid for those who have a card. The product return system and warranty repair conditions are also simplified.



If you buy a simple ball worth 300 rubles, you will be surprised why points were not counted. Please note that bonuses are awarded on goods whose value exceeds 1000 rubles. Blue card

The first step in the loyalty system is to get a simple blue card. They are issued to everyone who made a purchase of 1000 rubles or more. This is the easiest way to receive Sportmaster bonuses. At the same time, of all types of cards, this one gives the lowest interest.

Silver card

This is the second, more advanced level. You can get such a card when the total amount of purchases for the entire period of your cooperation with Sportmaster is 15,000 rubles. This is the only company in Russia that provides such benefits.

A silver card is very beneficial. With each 1000 rubles of purchase, it is no longer awarded 50, but 70 points. The warranty period is extended for another 30 days, and you can get services with a 50% discount. And most importantly - you can pay for goods with bonuses in an amount equal to 50% of its value. No longer need to wait for the summer, but feel free to take your favorite jacket immediately for 2-3 thousand.

Gold card

And the highest level is a gold card. It is issued to those who are a true fan of Sportmaster and have made purchases in the amount of 150,000 rubles. For every thousand such customers receive 100 bonuses and can use them to fully pay for their new purchase.

A little about the company itself
