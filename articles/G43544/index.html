Flowers are different: yellow and red, large and fragrant, small, fluffy ... But they are the same in one: each flower is a unique creation of nature, each is good in its own way, but always pleasant. In general, the presence of flowers in the house favorably affects the mood of its inhabitants, creates a unique atmosphere of comfort. But where can I get fresh flowers all the time? If you do not belong to the oligarchs, then surely this question will arise with you. And there is a way out - you can create flowers yourself! And, believe me, skillfully made, exhibited in exclusive, made by their own hands, vases, such flowers will delight and delight at the same time: after all, in addition to aesthetic decoration, they will be decoration and design.

Origami flowers are the embodiment of nature's perfection in paper. This is a double perfection: first, the mother earth creates beauty, then a person with the help of his genius repeats the natural beauty in other materials and using special techniques. Paper flowers are pleasing to the eye and warm the soul just like living flowers. The only way they differ significantly is the lack of smell. But they serve the owners much longer and protect nature. In addition, work on a small masterpiece will unite the whole family for several evenings, and it is worth a lot!
