The easiest outfit

Any girl dreams of being a fairy or a princess, a Snow Maiden or a sorceress at a holiday. All this is easy to bring to life by putting on a child a magnificent dress and making small accessories. So, to create a New Year 's princess costume for a girl , it is enough to make an original crown. To do this, you need a sheet of thick cardboard, a simple pencil, glossy colored paper (it is best to use golden or silver), PVA glue, a brush, tulle, scissors, a stapler, decorative elements.

The contours of the crown of any shape are drawn on cardboard. Even the simplest option, as in the photo above, will look great if it is beautifully decorated. You can make a crown around the circumference of the head, or you can make a small one that will be held on a hoop or elastic band. For a small part, you can take a cardboard sleeve from napkins or toilet paper. The edges of the cut out element are connected by a stapler, previously bright paper or fabric is glued to the cardboard.

To decorate this original accessory for the New Year's costume for girls, rhinestones, half beads or pebbles are glued to the crown. The bottom edge can be decorated with tulle, picking up its edges in the center. The crown is fixed on the head with an elastic band, you can attach the glue gun to the hoop both in the center and a little on the side.

Snowman costume

If for the dance of snowmen you need to make a New Year's costume for the girl with your own hands, do not start to panic. It is not necessary to pack your daughter in balls of different sizes, you can wear a white turtleneck or blouse, a long-sleeve gymnastics leotard is also suitable, over which a pack of white tulle is worn.

Making it is very simple. You must purchase a white tulle fabric and a wide elastic band of the same color. It wraps around the waist and is cut to size, you just need to add a couple of centimeters to the seams. Then you need to cut the strips of fabric and, folding them in half, stretch the ends into a loop through an elastic band. Thus, strips are passed around the entire elastic band until the tulle fills the entire space of the future skirt. The more pieces of fabric used, the more magnificent the pack.
