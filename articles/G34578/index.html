Hostel in Surgut: overview and prices

It is not so difficult to choose a hostel in Surgut. You only need to understand what services are needed, and decide on the maximum room rate per day. In the article, we consider the most profitable options in which you can settle down, having arrived in the city. When booking through special sites, it is better to additionally contact the hostel and clarify all the nuances.

Like

There is also a seating area and shower.

The room can order delivery of food or food and drinks. A play area is open for children, and there is also a playground on the street. Shops are available on site. There are non-smoking rooms, family type. Heating works.

In the reviews they write that the staff, the atmosphere, the service are all at the highest level. The bathtub has underfloor heating.

Veles

This hostel will cost an average of 700 rubles. Free internet is available. The rooms have a TV, wardrobe. There is also a shared bathroom on each floor. Hostel Veles in Surgut has its own kitchen. You can always make tea or coffee on it for free. The administration can tell where it is better to go on an excursion, it will also sell tickets for the chosen route.
