"Volga Pearl" of Yaroslavl: how to get, reviews

The Volzhskaya Pearl of Yaroslavl is a unique hotel located on the water. This place is especially fond of foreign tourists, as well as those people who are accustomed to comfort and high standards of service.

Brief description of the hotel

The Volzhskaya Zhemchuzhina Hotel of Yaroslavl offers its guests comfortable living conditions, as well as quality service. Here you can visit the traditional Finnish sauna, use the private parking area, and Wi-Fi, which is included in the price.

As for the number of rooms, the Volzhskaya Pearl of Yaroslavl offers you to live in beautifully decorated rooms, which are decorated in a concise, but very cozy style. Also, modern orthopedic furniture and household appliances are installed here, which will provide the most comfortable living conditions.

As for nutrition, here you can try the best dishes of European cuisine. Restaurant "Volzhskaya Pearl" of Yaroslavl is distinguished by its original design using antique furniture, as well as beautiful paintings. Fans of hops should visit the hotel bar.





Hotel Location

Many tourists and business people come to Yaroslavl. The Volga Pearl is characterized by a rather convenient location, because this hotel is located just a few minutes drive from the railway station or the international airport. To find the hotel, you need to get to the Central Embankment. The reference point for you is the Arsenal Tower. Nearby is the Historical Museum, as well as one of the main attractions - the Kremlin.





Hotel Rooms

Comfortable living conditions should provide guests with a hotel. Yaroslavl, Volzhskaya Pearl offers you the following accommodation options:

  • Standard rooms offer single and double occupancy on one and a half orthopedic beds. The apartments are decorated in light colors, making them appear more spacious.
  • Superior rooms feature a fairly large area, as well as an elegant design. Here, in addition to the bedroom, there is also a small sitting area, which is represented by soft chairs, a coffee table and a floor lamp.
  • Luxury apartments consist of two rooms. The cozy bedroom has a large bed, as well as a dressing table. The spacious living room is equipped with a soft sofa, which, when unfolded, can serve to accommodate an additional guest.
  • Attic apartments feature a private terrace from which you can enjoy views of the Volga. The room itself is designed as a studio, in which the bedroom is separated from the recreation area by the bar counter.
  • Penthouse rooms are located on the top floor. In many ways they are similar to suites. A distinctive feature is the presence of small windows in the ceiling, which serve as an additional source of light.

It is worth noting especially the design of the rooms of the Volzhskaya Pearl Hotel (Yaroslavl). The photo shows a restrained classic style in the interior.





The amenities provided in the apartment

If you are accustomed to comfort, then the Volzhskaya Pearl Hotel is ideal for you. Yaroslavl attracts many tourists, as well as business people who need a good rest after a busy day. This condition is met due to the following amenities in the apartment:

  • each room has a small balcony or veranda with plastic garden furniture;
  • a flat-screen TV connected to a satellite dish will give you the opportunity to watch many channels;
  • All rooms have high-speed wireless Internet access (no extra charge);
  • the minibar will always have a supply of drinking water, as well as alcoholic and refreshing drinks;
  • thanks to the presence of a refrigerator, the products you bought will remain fresh for a long time;
  • air conditioning allows you to adjust the air temperature in your room according to your needs;
  • a reliable safe with an electronic combination lock will ensure the safety of your valuables;
  • thanks to the presence of a landline telephone, you can call the reception at any time, as well as use the long-distance line;
  • In the bathroom, equipped with a shower, a hairdryer, a set of bath and cosmetic accessories are also provided.




Hotel Infrastructure

Quite attractive in both tourist and business terms is Yaroslavl. Hotel on the water "Volzhskaya Zhemchuzhina" offers you to use the following list of infrastructure objects:

  • throughout the hotel operates a wireless Internet signal;
  • All guests who arrive in their own or rented car are provided with a parking space;
  • if the safe provided in your room is not enough for you, then you can rent an additional cell at the reception;
  • in the hotel building there is a souvenir shop where you can buy trinkets to commemorate the city and the hotel;
  • special smoking areas are provided;
  • on the terrace there are sun loungers for sunbathing, as well as benches and tables;
  • a steam sauna allows you to relieve emotional and physical fatigue;
  • if you have bulky suitcases or valuable luggage, then you can put it in a storage room;
  • For business events, a modern conference room is provided;
  • the tour desk can help you find an interesting excursion program.




Catering establishments

The following establishments are responsible for full and quality food in this hotel:

  • Restaurant Volzhskaya Pearl (Yaroslavl) specializes in Russian and European cuisines. The wine list deserves special attention. Also here you can taste a variety of teas from around the globe. If you plan to organize a banquet in this restaurant, it will be useful to know that the hall can accommodate up to 100 people. The institution opens at 12 noon and is open until midnight.
  • The lobby bar specializes not only in alcoholic beverages, but also in various types of coffee. The highlight of the institution can be considered a huge assortment of original desserts from the titled chef.
  • A favorite place for hotel guests, as well as residents of Yaroslavl is an outdoor summer cafe, which is located on the terrace. Here you can enjoy delicious dishes from the restaurant menu, admiring the river landscapes.

Facilities and services for guests

One of the most affable cities of Russia is considered Yaroslavl. Hotel on the water "Volzhskaya Pearl" provides guests with the following list of services:

  • delivery of dishes from the restaurant directly to the room;
  • if you are going to work or on a long excursion, then a lunch box may be formed for you;
  • for an additional fee, you can be provided with a shuttle to the airport or train station, as well as to any other place;
  • if you are interested in the latest news, then order a daily delivery of fresh press;
  • for fishing enthusiasts, an event can be organized;
  • the reception is open around the clock, and therefore at any time you can get answers to your questions about accommodation;
  • guests are assisted in organizing banquets and celebrations.




Useful information for guests

This hotel has the following policies:

  • check-in is after 14:00, and check-out is strictly until noon;
  • the rooms do not provide the possibility of providing extra beds;
  • upon prior request, pets can be accommodated (paid service);
  • guests can use plastic cards to pay for accommodation and related services;
  • When booking an apartment, guests are required to pay a prepayment in the amount of the full cost of the stay.




"Volga pearl" (Yaroslavl) - reviews, advantages

Hotel guests note a number of positive points, namely:

  • original location on the river;
  • the central part of the city with good transport interchange, as well as close to the main infrastructure facilities;
  • friendly and helpful staff;
  • there are armchairs on the balcony, and therefore enjoy the beautiful scenery with comfort;
  • hearty and delicious breakfasts; good assortment in the restaurant;
  • There is a bicycle rental company near the hotel.

Negative reviews

The following can be considered negative characteristics of the hotel:

  • the rooms have poor sound insulation (especially noisy in those apartments located next to the restaurant);
  • standard rooms are so small that it is difficult to move around;
  • when weddings are held in the restaurant, the whole hotel shudders from the noise;
  • old, washed towels;
  • the carpets in the rooms are very dirty;
  • lamps were twisted in half of the lighting fixtures; the view from the windows of the rooms on the river is blocked by neighboring buildings;
  • there are no mosquito nets on the windows, and therefore there are always clouds of mosquitoes in the room;
  • the rooms do not have dishes (but they can be taken for free at the bar).

General impression

Volga Pearl is one of the most popular hotels in Yaroslavl. And this is not surprising, because it is located directly on the water. It would seem that nothing should stop the guests from enjoying beautiful views, but if your windows are not facing the embankment, then most likely the landscape will be obscured by various kinds of buildings. It is also worth noting that the rooms are too small (especially standard ones). Nevertheless, many people choose this particular accommodation option, due to its convenient location in the central part of the city and proximity to the main infrastructure and tourist facilities.

One of the features of the hotel is its restaurant, which is popular not only among tourists, but also among local residents. A huge assortment of dishes is presented here, and the original interior deserves special attention. Nevertheless, for hotel guests, a restaurant is a source of noise that keeps you awake until late at night (and especially if any banquet or wedding is held), because the rooms are just terrible soundproofing. Therefore, if you are used to going to bed early, then this accommodation option will not suit you.
