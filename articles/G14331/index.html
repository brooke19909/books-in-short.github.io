Residential complex "Yaroslavl" (Chelyabinsk): characteristics, decoration, builder, reviews

In the Soviet district of Chelyabinsk , the construction of a new residential complex "Yaroslavl". The plot allocated for construction is located in the territory limited by Kuznetsova, Yaroslavskaya, Tolbukhin and Charchan Streets. The attractiveness of the Yaroslavsky residential complex from the NIKS construction company in Chelyabinsk is explained by the uniqueness of this project and the good environmental situation. Nearby is a pine forest and Shershnevskoe reservoir.



Project Features

According to the general plan, the residential complex consists of a six-section house of different floors (16, 18 and 22 floors). The project was developed by experienced architects and designers who managed to combine the original design with the latest technologies in the field of construction.

The residential complex "Yaroslavsky" in Chelyabinsk is:

  • total area reaching 9716 sq. m;
  • the number of apartments in residential buildings - 150;
  • unique architectural solution for each section of the house;
  • the use of monolithic-frame construction technology that ensures the strength and durability of buildings;
  • three-layer wall construction for increased thermal insulation;

Apartment renovation

The construction company offers to buy apartments in Chelyabinsk in the Yaroslavsky residential complex with a fine finish. This option is considered the most convenient, as new residents will not have to spend time on repairs and preparing the apartment for living. You can enter immediately after receiving the keys.

What is included in the finish of the apartment:

  • alignment of floor and walls;
  • ready-made wiring with switches and sockets;
  • linoleum flooring in living quarters and in the kitchen;
  • water and sewage system wiring;
  • installation of high-quality plumbing fixtures (sink, toilet, bathtub);
  • installation of suspended ceilings;
  • pasting the walls with modern high-quality wallpaper of famous brands;
  • covering the surfaces of the bathroom with tiles and water-dispersion paints;
  • installation of a reliable front door;
  • installation of interior doors.

Internal Infrastructure LCD

At the planning stage of the Yaroslavsky residential complex in Chelyabinsk, even trifles were thought out. All conditions for comfortable living are created.

  • Particular attention is paid to the safety of residents. There is a fenced area, cameras around the perimeter, strict access control to the site, security service.
  • Closed courtyards provide maximum security and silence.
  • Car parking is available on site.
  • The first floors of the buildings are commercial areas. They will house shops, a concierge room, offices of service companies.
  • Yards are equipped with children's, sports grounds and places for recreation.




In addition, residents of the Yaroslavl residential complex in Chelyabinsk will not have problems with access to external infrastructure. Nearby are schools, kindergartens, pharmacies, clinics, supermarkets, fitness rooms, beauty salons.

Reviews about the residential complex of Chelyabinsk - "Yaroslavl"

Among the advantages of this residential complex, buyers who managed to enter the apartments are called:

  • good environmental situation in the area;
  • protected area, which is very important for families with children;
  • good transport accessibility.

However, this project was not able to avoid the complaints of new residents. Most often, complaints about the layout are expressed. According to some buyers, separate rooms turned out to be less than on the plan (in this case, the developer recounts the cost).
