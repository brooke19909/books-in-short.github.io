Arkhangelsk, hotel "Dvina": location, photos and reviews

Arkhangelsk is a wonderful city with many amazing places and attractions. But, before planning a vacation here, it is worth thinking about a place to stay. Excellent reviews from tourists can be heard about the hotel "Dvina". The hotel is located in an area with convenient infrastructure and will delight guests with quality service.

Hotel Services

Tourists will have only the most vivid impressions about Arkhangelsk. Hotel "Dvina" provides all services for a comfortable stay of guests. This is primarily a luxury apartment with a beautiful view of the city. It includes a shower or a bar. If the tourist has perishable products, they can be left in the refrigerator. Room rates include iron rental, internet, local telephone connection, as well as round-the-clock taxi service. This is especially convenient for those guests who came to work in Arkhangelsk. Hotel "Dvina" is ready to offer a room for negotiations. Also in close proximity is the large Iceberg Business Center. His address: st. Embankment of the Northern Dvina, 30. Arkhangelsk is a city in which there is everything for a comfortable stay of people of various categories.



Other services can also be provided at an extra charge. Guests who want to have breakfast, lunch and dinner right at the hotel can be offered a full board for only 975 rubles. The number can be provided only after the person submits an identity document. This can be a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation or a passport. Rules of residence

The rooms should be kept in order and the minimum security requirements. Ironing is not allowed directly in the apartment. There are special rooms for this. To get the keys to the ironing room, you must contact the maid. For an additional fee, hotel staff will be able to put things in order.

In most cases, a hair dryer is included. If he was not in the apartment, you should contact the maid, who is responsible for the floor. A hairdryer can be provided for a short time.

It offers guests individual guest safes in which you can store valuables and important documents. To reserve a cell, it is necessary to contact the staff at the internal hotel number 100 (or 101).

For guests who come to Arkhangelsk by car, the Dvina Hotel offers a comfortable parking. If you need a place to place a car, you should call the internal hotel number 111.

Smoking in the walls of the hotel

The hotel staff does not condemn guests for bad habits and treats smokers with understanding. But from June 1, 2014, smoking in public places was prohibited. Therefore, the walls of the hotel you can use only electronic cigarettes. Additionally, special smoking areas were equipped . Finding them is not difficult, as they are marked with special icons.

It is worth remembering that, in violation of the law, smokers endanger not only themselves, but also other guests. In case of violations, the staff has the right to evict an unscrupulous guest without the right to re-check.

Safety of living

Hotel "Dvina" (Arkhangelsk) is equipped with the latest security systems. Fire detectors are installed in all rooms and corridors, which allows timely response to a possible danger. The hotel staff also asks guests to observe the simplest rules. It is worth remembering that the mains voltage is 220 V. After using the devices, they should definitely be disconnected from the mains.

Compliance with the basic rules is constantly monitored by the security service. If any violations are detected by the guests, appropriate measures are immediately taken. Initially, a warning follows. In the future, guests may be asked to leave the walls of the hotel. If any problems are identified, the security service asks to be contacted by internal numbers 100, 101 or 111.
