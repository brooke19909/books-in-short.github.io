Hotel Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Side, Turkey): overview, description and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular hotels in Turkey, where you can relax inexpensively, but with taste - Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side). It is located in a wonderful climate zone, where almost all year round sunny and hot weather reigns, perfectly mitigated by the influence of air masses coming from the Mediterranean. It is often recommended to be visited by many tour operators around the world for holidays, motivating travelers with low prices and an excellent level of service.

general information

The date of construction of Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side) is considered to be the year when the first tourists spent their holidays within its walls - 2004. The hotel is quite modern and offers travelers a decent vacation by all the best European standards. Now its building and adjacent places for entertainment are located on a considerable territory of 12,000 sq.m.



Location

The hotel is located in one of the brightest and most favorable places on the Mediterranean coast - in the large resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãSide, which has long been recognized as an entertainment center. The nearest settlement is the town of Kyzylot, which is famous for the picturesque nature of it, which is everywhere. At almost every step, vacationers can admire the beauty of the growing exotic gardens, which throughout the resort period delight travelers with their vibrant flowering.

The capital of Turkey, where the airport where foreigners arrive, is located at a distance of 75 kilometers from the Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Side / Kyzylot).

Rooms

The hotel offers tourists a rather impressive number of rooms, which consists of 180 modern comfortable apartments. Each of the rooms is currently undergoing modern renovation, and there is also a stylish interior made in pastel shades. All rooms are located in one building, which extends up to three floors.

Each room has a new air conditioner, which guests can control independently, adjusting the temperature in their space, based on personal preferences. In addition, the apartments have a bathroom with modern fixtures. Here, every traveled traveler receives for individual use a large bath towel, slippers and a bathrobe. In addition, the hotel provides a set of soap supplies.

The apartments Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Side / Kizilot) is strictly prohibited smoking and pets with any type.





Standard

Among the total number of rooms there are more than one and a half hundred rooms of the standard group, which provide for the placement of a pair of guests on their space. A large bed is provided for them (in some apartments there are two single beds instead). If desired, another guest can be settled here, for whom one more sleeping place is established. The rooms of this category have a small balcony, from where tourists can watch a very beautiful view of the sea.

Hotel guests who stayed in standard-class rooms, in their reviews, recommend that, if there are valuable things, rent a small safe, the cost of which is about $ 2 per day. If desired, guests can request a minibar in the room, which will provide soft drinks.

Family

Guests who wish to relax with their family or just a big company, at the time of the tour, are often recommended to give preference to family-type apartments, which are a room divided into two separate rooms. Each of them has a beautiful bedroom, consisting of a pair of places for a comfortable sleep, as well as a pair of nightstands and lighting fixtures, with which you can create dim lights.

One room has a large flat-screen TV that broadcasts Russian-language satellite channels. A safe is installed here on a paid basis, and if desired, vacationers can call by phone, which is charged separately.

Nutrition

Guests staying at the Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side) can eat in the restaurant located in the hotel. Five times a day you can visit the buffet, which offers delicious dishes of Turkish and Mediterranean cuisine - they are prepared only by professional chefs according to traditional recipes. In addition, there are two bars in the resting place, where a considerable selection of snacks and sweets is presented, the main place here is occupied by chilled drinks and cocktails. Some travelers in reviews of Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side) definitely recommend tasting oriental sweets that have a unique taste.





Entertainment

Tourists staying at the hotel never have to get bored, as there are various entertainment programs throughout the day. Most of them are organized by a small group of animators. According to tourists, some members of this team speak Russian well, which is quite convenient for travelers who come from the CIS countries.

Starting in the morning, sporting events and relay races are held in the aquazone area, in which almost all guests take part. With the onset of the evening in the same place you can have fun at the disco, which usually ends at the very morning.





Pool

The Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Side) has a large aquazone designed for active recreation of its guests. There is a large outdoor terrace lined with sun loungers and beach umbrellas. On its territory there is also a bar that offers refreshing cocktails, which are in special demand on hot days.

In the very center of the terrace there are two swimming pools, one of which has one water slide, on which vacationers can have fun.





Spa and sports

Lovers of relaxation do not miss the opportunity to visit the large center located in the building of the complex. Its territory is divided into two parts: thermal and massage. In the thermal room you can visit the sauna and the traditional hammam, where a whole range of cosmetic procedures that are useful for the whole body can be offered by parmeisters.

The beautifully arranged massage room has several large and comfortable couches where tourists can relax while enjoying the talented manipulations of professional massage therapists.

Fans of a sports lifestyle have the opportunity to go to the volleyball court located on the beach. If desired, travelers can rent a bicycle and go on it to inspect nearby attractions. Many vacationers recommend traveling in groups in this way.

Beach

Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side) has its own beach on a large and clean sandy area. Here you can take a sun lounger for free and take advantage of a large umbrella that can protect from the harmful effects of sunlight. For a small rent, you can take a large towel and mattress.



There are also several types of water activities that appeal to tourists of different ages. Reviews of Avalon Beach Hotel 4 * (Side) often say that it is here that you can rent a yacht or boat and make a short boat trip with your loved one. The most extreme vacationers do not miss the opportunity to ride a hovercraft or water ski, and only a few decide to dive under scuba diving.
