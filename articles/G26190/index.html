Kstovo hotels: differences and hotel services

Not far from Nizhny Novgorod on the banks of the Volga is the city of Kstovo. This is a city settlement of regional significance, there is a railway and the Volga highway of federal significance, there are large enterprises of the chemical, processing and food industries, significant sporting events are held.

Distinctive features

There are several hotels in the city . Kstovo hotels differ by the following criteria:

  • Number of rooms;
  • cost of living;
  • belonging to private or municipal property;
  • quality of service and level of comfort for guests.

Most of Kstovo hotels are conveniently located, located near the federal highway and the city center. Some of them are located at the exit from it, not far from large industrial enterprises and on the way to the regional center - Nizhny Novgorod.

G. Kstovo, whose hotels often host athletes from all over the country and abroad, is famous for its World Sambo Academy. On its basis, competitions and training camps on sambo, various types of wrestling, rhythmic gymnastics and other sports are held. Therefore, the presence in the city of a sufficient number of hotels, mini-hotels and hostels is an urgent need.



Popular hotels in Kstovo

Guests of the city often choose a place to stay by looking at the rating and hotel reviews in various sources. Popular and well-established among travelers in Kstovo are institutions: "Kstovo", "Beloved City" and "Calypso". These small hotels have a lot in common:

  • they are conveniently located, have good transport links with train stations, industrial and other enterprises and organizations;
  • recently put into operation;
  • the number of rooms is represented by rooms of different classes, the choice depends on the financial capabilities of the guests;
  • the furniture in the rooms is modern, its completeness meets the needs of guests, the beds have orthopedic mattresses;

Economy Hotels

In the economy category, Kstovo hotels offer their services: Aelita, Sim-Sim and Motel mini-pensions (on Magistralnaya St.) and others. Guests often rent rooms in these establishments for one night, budget tourists and their organized groups - sports teams, students and youth, business travelers settle here. It is possible to check in a room from two to four people, the Aelita Hotel offers six- and eight-bed rooms with bunk beds. Guests have access to shared bathrooms with showers, hygiene products and towels. In the hotel "Aelita" you can pay three meals a day. The motel is located next to a cafe. All listed institutions have a convenient location in the city.





Kstovo hotels: prices

The cost of a bed per night in an economy class hotel is from 350 rubles and above, depending on the number of places to stay in the room. Higher-class hotels charge an average of 1,700 rubles per person per night for their services, depending on the category of room provided. The institutions have their own discount system, which allows you to save up to 20% of the room price.
