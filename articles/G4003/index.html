Sights of the Tuapse district: description and photo

Tourists who come to rest to the Black Sea often get bored of a monotonous vacation on the beach. For example, Tuapse is a famous domestic resort. However, not every tourist is interested in the culture and history of the city and the whole region, which really deserves attention. Traveling here, it is worth visiting the attractions of the Tuapse district, photos and descriptions of which you will find below.

City attractions

Walking around the city, you should definitely turn to K. Marx Street, which houses the famous alley called Platanovaya. It begins in the city center and stretches for more than one kilometer. Alley landed at the beginning of the last century.

The sights of the Tuapse district and the city are inextricably linked with the sea, promenade and port. Therefore, you need to turn onto Primorsky Boulevard, where you will find an architectural monument - the Pilot House.

Historical dolmens

The territory of the Tuapse district covers historical dolmens. They are located near the suburban village, on the slope of Mount Bogatyrka. Dolmens is the name of ancient burial structures, which consisted of several plates. Moreover, one of them was as a roof, and the other served as a passage to the burial chamber. As a rule, the weight of the plates was quite large (the severity of one plate is estimated at several tons). Researchers concluded that the dating of these dolmens dates back to the 3rd-2nd millennium BC. e. By visiting these places, you will see how diverse the sights of the Tuapse district are. Excursions to dolmens are optional, you can go to them yourself. However, professionals will tell you about the names of the monuments, their history and show the route.





It is generally accepted that every dolmen has the power to charge a person with energy and fulfill dreams. In order for dreams to come true, small rituals are required, which guides will tell visitors about. But if you still decide that you can do it alone, do not worry - next to each dolmen there is detailed information about them.

33 meter waterfall

This high waterfall has a different name. It is customary to call it Perun. This threshold is the largest in the Tuapse region, and it is located on the left bank of the river, at the Kazhenny stream. The passage to the waterfall is not easy, as it is not so easy to find compared to excursion routes. So, before you decide to go for a walk to the threshold of Perun, you need to clarify how to get to it from the locals. There is a waterfall behind a village called Krasnoe, to which buses and trains go.





Rock of tears

Attractions of the Tuapse region also include the Kiseleva rock, or, as it is also called, the Tears rock, which is located between the river named Aga and Cape Kadosh, four kilometers from Tuapse itself. The name of the cliff is connected with the beautiful legend of the obstinate girl, through whose fault her lover died in this place, and she could not stand the grief and dumped herself into the sea. The height of the rock of Tears, which is visible above the sea, reaches forty-three meters. It organizes excursions during which you can enjoy the fresh air and the picturesque landscape.
