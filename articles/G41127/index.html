Iberostar Playa Pilar Hotel 5 * (Cuba, Cayo Guillermo): description of rooms, service, reviews

Cuba is a fabulous country in which each of us would like to visit. Going to Liberty Island, you need to find a good hotel. In our article we want to talk about one of the hotels - Iberostar Playa Pilar, located near the beach "Playa Pilar".

A bit about the hotel

Iberostar Playa Pilar is built on one of the islands of the Jardines del Rey archipelago. A wonderful hotel will allow you to relax in one of the best corners of the planet. Here, guests invariably expect endless sandy beaches, silence, coconut palms and the turquoise sea. What else is needed for happiness?

The five-star Iberostar Playa Pilar offers its guests luxurious facilities. Here you can have a good time not only in the company of friends, but also with the whole family. The advantage of the establishment is that it is located on the territory of the best resort near the stunning Pilar beach by the Caribbean Sea.

Hotel Rooms

The Iberostar Playa Pilar offers comfortable apartments with excellent lighting and five-star service. The bright rooms are decorated in a Caribbean style. In total, the hotel has 482 rooms, which are located in 15 buildings located on the coast.

Among the presented apartments, you can choose the suitable option:

  1. Double Room. This is a great option to stay. The apartment consists of a well-equipped bedroom and a bathroom. The room has a balcony or terrace with a unique view. The rooms are equipped with a minibar, tea and coffee sets, TV, ironing set, safe. This room can accommodate three adults or two children and two adults.
  2. Double Apartment with Caribbean Sea View. The rooms have the same amenities as the rooms of other categories. Their main advantage is the stunning scenery outside the window.
  3. Junior Suite is a multi-functional and spacious room, which is comfortable for adults and children. Its residents have free access to the spa. The apartment consists of a bathroom, bedroom, sitting area and terrace.
  4. Suites are the best hotel rooms that are equipped for the most demanding guests. The apartments have an original design. Their layout is very convenient. The room has a bedroom and a seating area, as well as a bedroom.

Among such a variety of apartments you can always choose something suitable for yourself.

Catering

Relaxing in Iberostar Playa Pilar 5 *, you can enjoy the amazing taste of local dishes. The hotel has five restaurants and as many bars where you can taste the best Caribbean dishes. Restaurant chefs also offer guests all kinds of masterpieces of international cuisine. Four theme restaurants allow you to have fun evenings. In the daytime, you can enjoy your time in one of the five bars or have a bite to eat at the snack bar.

Restaurant "Buffet" is the main institution of the hotel. A comprehensive breakfast awaits you here: hot and cold snacks, delicious sweets, natural juices, fruits and coffee. For lunch you will find more variety, and dinner will not disappoint.

Gourmet Restaurant offers visitors to taste traditional Cuban cuisine. Everyone has the opportunity to visit an institution with Japanese cuisine, which prepares light dishes made from natural products. Here you can taste delicious sushi.

No less attractive is the restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine, where you can order desserts, salads, meat and other dishes. La Parilla serves local specialties. Here you can try piccadillo, chicken, beans and much more from Creole cuisine. People who adhere to the dress code are admitted to the institution.

Hotel infrastructure

In Iberostar Playa Pilar 5 *, services for a fun-free holiday are widely represented. Tourists can visit the spa, wellness treatments, beauty treatments and massages, aerobics classes.

For tourists, there are swimming pools and a water park. The hotel's spa is considered one of the best in Cuba. Wellness facilities are available here.

Services for children

In Iberostar Playa Pilar (Cuba), they are very kind to the children who are considered the main characters here. For them, built a water park, a kids club, and in the evenings, kids will have mini-shows. In addition, the hotel has clubs, workshops and other events.

Entertainment for adults

The Iberostar Playa Pilar offers recreational activities for adults. A comprehensive program is provided for tourists of all ages.

The hotel guests are guaranteed not to be bored during the holidays, because funny animators offer dancing, aerobics, organize team games and sporting events. A fun holiday on the coast of a sunny country will be unforgettable.

Tourists reviews

Tours at Iberostar Playa Pilar are well-deserved. Cuba attracts many tourists with its exoticism. But rest on Liberty Island has its own characteristics. Many travelers warn that it will be fabulous only if you do not rely on high-quality service. In Cuba, you will not wait for such a service as in the Dominican Republic, but this is not so important. Locals are very nice and kind, they welcome guests. Smile is the main weapon of tourists.

Service at Iberostar Playa Pilar is perfectly acceptable for a good stay. And the beautiful ocean and stunning views will make your vacation unforgettable. According to tourists, the hotel is in a quiet and peaceful place off the coast. It has a vast territory, which is well equipped and regularly cleaned.

Many tourists who travel often note that the hotel can be safely called one of the best in Cuba.

Room Reviews

Description of rooms Iberostar Playa Pilar allows us to say that they are well equipped and have beautiful interiors. The rooms are spacious in size. All rooms have furnished terraces or balconies. As for cleaning, reviews about its quality are quite contradictory. Experienced tourists recommend putting aside small tips that significantly improve the quality of service. On holiday you can take all kinds of trinkets and chocolates that you can use as souvenirs. Locals are very happy with such presentations. Such modest gifts will not burden you, but the quality of your room service will noticeably improve.

The apartments are cleaned daily and bed linen and towels are regularly replaced. The bathrooms have a minimal set of detergents and cosmetics. In general, there are no problems with the service. Tourists note that on the ground floor in the rooms you can see insects (cockroaches and scorpions). But for exotic countries with a hot climate, this is quite normal. In general, the apartments are new, clean and well equipped.

Nutrition Reviews

According to reviews, in Iberostar Playa Pilar food is established at a very decent level. If you want to treat yourself to delights, then go to Cuba. Local cuisine is awesome. The hotel has several main restaurants serving the most delicious Cuban cuisine. In addition, the institution has four a la carte restaurants. Tourists strongly recommend visiting each of them. Guests are given the opportunity to spend four nights in beautiful places for free. It is difficult to name the best restaurant. Delicious food will be offered to you in each of them. Especially pleasantly surprised by the service in the institutions. Here you can feel like a special guest, about whom the waiters are so anxious. Such a service is possible only in Cuba.

In the main restaurant, according to tourists, the food is very tasty and varied. There are always a lot of seafood on the tables, including grilled ones. Where else can you enjoy such wonderful lobsters and shrimp?

Tourists appreciate the quality of drinks in the bars. Bartenders make great cocktails. And local sweets are no less impressive. To fully appreciate the taste of local food, you need to try it.

General impression of the hotel

The hotel has an excellent location. It is located on the very coast of the ocean. Indeed, for the sake of a beach holiday, tourists come here. Directly near the hotel, the beach is equipped with pontoons, from which there are convenient calls to the sea. Here you can relax and swim very comfortably. The coast is equipped with a large number of sunbeds, on which there is always enough space.

The famous Pilaya beach with fine sand is a ten minute walk from the hotel. Such a beautiful place is difficult to find elsewhere. The coast has picturesque views. Most tourists prefer to relax on the Pilaya beach. There is a smooth and shallow entry into the sea, very convenient for children.

Tourists go to Cuba exclusively for positive emotions and exoticism. All of this is available here. Delicious food, plenty of sunshine, fiery animation and a beautiful ocean - all this favors a wonderful vacation.

According to tourists, the hotel was created for a comfortable stay. If you want to plunge into the world of beauty and exotic, then you should visit Cuba. The island of freedom differs from other countries, which is reflected in all spheres of life. Rest in this beautiful country is a real holiday that will give you a lot of pleasant experiences.

Iberostar Playa Pilar can be safely called one of the best hotels in the country. The facility is well equipped. The room fund has beautiful interiors. The hotel boasts good staff. All customer wishes are fulfilled upon request. Especially many positive reviews are left by tourists regarding food. A variety of restaurants with excellent cuisine allow you to enjoy gastronomic delights. Of course, the hotel has minor flaws, but they are very insignificant and do not spoil the general impression of this fabulous place.
