Smartphone Explay Atom: reviews, prices and specifications

An entry-level smartphone with unusual features is the Explay Atom. Reviews parameters and other important information about this interesting device will be given in this review material. This smart phone has one important option that sets it apart from its competitors.





Kit

The usual equipment, as for an economy class gadget, is Explay Atom White. Reviews of him indicate this feature. The kit includes:

  • The device itself.

  • Battery.

  • Charger.

  • Cord with microUSB connector.

Among the complete list of documentation, one can single out an instruction manual and, of course, a warranty card.

The design of the gadget and its ergonomics

Expect some frills in terms of design from an entry-level smartphone is not necessary. This is a regular smartphone in a single-block design and with support for touch input. It has a length of 126.7 mm, a width of 64.4 mm, and a thickness of 12.5 mm. The weight of this gadget is 139 grams. The diagonal of the screen is 4 inches. Under it are three ‚Äúfamiliar‚Äù buttons. In turn, the physical buttons are displayed on the right edge. This is the lock button and swing volume control device. Above the screen are the front camera and earpiece. The main camera is displayed on the back side (there is an LED backlight next to it) and a loud speaker.





CPU

A very modest central processor is installed in Explay Atom. Reviews about it indicate a rather low level of performance. It's about MTK6572. This is a dual-core chip, which operates at a frequency of 1 GHz in maximum computing load mode. Each of its modules is based on the rather outdated architecture of the A7. In reality, its computing power is enough for almost all tasks. But what he definitely can‚Äôt do is play high-quality videos and the latest versions of the most demanding games.

Graphics

A very modest graphics subsystem in Explay Atom Black. Reviews of this smartphone model confirm this once again. He does not have a separate graphics adapter. Therefore, the computational load associated with image processing is shifted to the central processor, which already does not have a high level of performance.





As noted earlier, the display diagonal of this gadget is only 4 inches. This seems to be not enough at the moment, but you can not expect more from an economy class device. Its resolution is only 800 pixels in length and 480 pixels in width. In general, the graphics subsystem of this smart phone cannot boast of anything unusual.

Photo and video

A very modest main camera is installed in the Explay Atom phone. Reviews speak of the low quality of photos and videos that are obtained with its help. Indeed, it is not necessary to expect outstanding performance from a 3 megapixel sensor element. There is an opportunity to receive photos, but their quality will obviously not be ideal. A similar situation with the video. There is an opportunity to shoot videos, but their quality will be far from the best. There is also a front camera, the sensitive element of which is based on a 0.3 megapixel sensor. This is quite enough for comfortable making video calls. And, by the way, this is exactly what it is for.

Memory

The minimum amount of memory required for comfortable work is integrated into Explay Atom. Reviews indicate this flaw. Only 512 megabytes of RAM capacity. In turn, the amount of internal storage in this case is 4 GB. Of these, 2 GB the user can use to install their programs or store their information. If necessary, you can install an external drive in the device. The corresponding slot is in this smartphone. At the same time, its maximum size can be 32 GB.





Autonomy

Users note a good battery capacity, which is installed in the Explay Atom smartphone. Reviews suggest that one of its charges is quite enough for 2-3 days of battery life with an average level of use. With maximum savings, this value will increase to 4 days. But with a peak intensity of its use, this indicator will decrease to 1 day. In general, this smartphone model cannot boast of something remarkable in terms of autonomy.

OS and other software

As an OS, it acts on this Android device. In the boxed version, version 4.2 is installed. But after the first connection to the global web, this value will change to 4.4., Since an unusual set of application software is installed on the Explay Atom smartphone. Reviews highlight this feature.





In addition to the usual applications from Google and social services, it also has programs from Yandex. These are maps and a browser.

That is, he has three slots for installing SIM cards. Controversial, of course, is the decision of the developers, but today the device has no competitors in terms of this indicator. The device itself can work in networks of the 2nd and 3rd generation. Experts and owners: reviews on this smart phone

Experts and owners agree on a device like the Explay Atom smartphone. Reviews about it is once again confirmed. It certainly will not get wide distribution. The price with such a filling is clearly overpriced. This cost is explained simply - 3 slots for installing SIM cards. It is for users who are carrying three phones with them, and this gadget is aimed. Of course, there are not so many of them, but they still exist. In other cases, it is impractical to buy such a device. With fewer SIM cards, you can buy a gadget with similar characteristics much cheaper. For comparison: the price of this smart phone at the moment is 110 dollars, and if you take its analogue, but with two slots for SIM cards, for example, you will have to pay about 90 dollars.





Summary

Now let's summarize the prospects for Explay Atom. Reviews distinguish 2 advantages from competitors. The first of them is the ability to work simultaneously in three mobile networks. Indeed, for this indicator, the device has no competitors at the moment. And the second is a set of applications from Yandex. But this is not so significant. After all, all this can be installed without any problems from the Play Market. This smart phone is interesting only for those who need an inexpensive smartphone that can work simultaneously with three mobile operators. In other cases, the purchase of this device is not entirely justified.
