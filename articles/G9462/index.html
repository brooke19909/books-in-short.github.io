Resorts of Spain near Barcelona - combine business with pleasure

Spain is a place of quality relaxation, but this is not the most popular destination for tourists. In many respects it depends on the cost: rest in Spain cannot be attributed to economical. However, you need to go to Spain at least once.

Especially attractive are the resorts of Spain near Barcelona. Holidays will be unforgettable thanks to the combination of beach holidays and cultural. Barcelona is a city praised by poets and singers, portrayed by artists. It attracts with all kinds of cultural attractions and historical monuments.



The resorts of Spain near Barcelona are not very numerous, but there is a choice. The largest is the Costa Brava. This resort is famous for its unusual nature. The name of the resort, which translates as "rocky mountain", explains its unusual location. The mountains here are so close to the sea that the presence of sandy beaches is surprising. The panorama of the Costa Brava looks very unusual: the blue waters of the sea seem to break inaccessible cliffs in some places, forming bays with sandy beaches. From the rear, the beach is protected by high mountains.

A map of Spain with resorts shows that on the territory of the Costa Brava several small towns and villages are conveniently located, in which tourists can easily find themselves. Perhaps none of the beaches around the Costa Brava can be called wild. Each village has a well-developed infrastructure, and there are many attractions for tourists.

The town of Lloret de Mar has more than 1000 years. Here you can visit the medieval church of San Roma, the castle of San Joan, etc. But no matter how interesting the historical monuments of the cities are, the most attractive are the resorts of Spain on the sea. Lloret de Mar has 5 beaches. It has its own water park, the vibrant nightlife (bars, discos) and sports activities are offered.



At any resort, you can book an excursion to the cities of Spain and already there you can enjoy walks around the sights there.

One can not help but mention such resorts in Spain near Barcelona as Girona and Tossa de Mar. The latter is located on the edge of sheer cliffs. The city is surrounded by an ancient stone fortress, against which it is possible to take magnificent photographs. The city of Girona is an islet of the Middle Ages in the middle of the civilized world. To this day, he has preserved his old layout: narrow streets, ancient buildings, sculptures and castles. In addition, Girona is famous for its gardens and coniferous forests - a combination of stunning colors, green, blue and rocky gray.



You should definitely visit the city of Blanes - this is the oldest city in all of Spain. It was built BC, during the existence of Ancient Rome. Local attractions include the seven-century castle of San Juan, the Church of St. Barbara and the fountain. Once it was a fishing town, but now it joyfully welcomes tourists with comfortable hotels. And, of course, calling the resorts of Spain near Barcelona, ​​you can not ignore Figeyras - a museum city. You definitely need to go there, even if you relax in another place. Here are the Salvador Dali Museum, the Toy Museum and the Empord Museum.
