The beginning of the biography

In the family of Nikolai Troshev, a military pilot, and a resident of Grozny, Nadezhda Mikhailovna, in March 1947, the first-born was born, who was named Gennady. The boy was born in Germany, but all his childhood will pass in the Caucasus - the motherland. In addition to him, two girls were born in the family, whose upbringing Nadezhda Mikhailovna was engaged in alone after the death of her husband at the age of 43. This was preceded by his reduction from the army under the law of Nikita Khrushchev from 1960. More than a million soldiers and officers were fired from the Armed Forces, with which Nikolai Troshev could not reconcile until the end of his life, bequeathing to his son not to connect his life with the military profession.





Being a leader by nature, the future General Troshev, whose biography began with the leadership in the game ‚ÄúCossacks-robbers‚Äù on the streets of Grozny, was a creative person. He went to the tank school of the city of Kazan in order to be on full state support and not to depend on outside help. The desire to be the best in everything led him to the Academy of Armored Forces, and then to the Academy of the General Staff.

Army career

While serving in the tank troops, the future general Gennady Troshev did not have time to count the stars on uniform. So rapidly developed his army career. All of it is connected exclusively with the North Caucasian Military District (North Caucasian Military District). In 1994, Troshev rose to the rank of commander of the army corps, becoming the commander of the 58th army during the First Chechen (1994-1996) , gradually leading the United Group of Forces and receiving the rank of lieutenant general. After graduation, he became deputy commander of the North Caucasus Military District.





In the CTO (counter-terrorist operation in the North Caucasus) since August 1999, he led the federal forces that repelled the attack on Dagestan by militants. Then he headed the Vostok group, under the command of Viktor Kazantsev, commander of the United Federal Forces in the North Caucasus, replacing him in this post in April 2000, having received the rank of Colonel-General the day before. Until December 2002 he was commander of the North Caucasian Military District.

Play with death

There were legends about the courage of Troshev. During the fighting, he flew by helicopter, showing personal courage. During the battle for Argun, he called on soldiers and commanders to rise on the offensive, controlling the battle from the window of the porthole. They hit the machine with heavy machine guns. The state of emergency pursued him constantly: in 1999 a helicopter in the fog almost ran into high-voltage wires, and only the art of the pilot Alexander Dziuba, who passed Afghanistan, saved the commander's life. During the fighting, the military uniform became the second skin, General Troshev did not sleep for days, sharing with the soldiers all the hardships of military service. Playing with death, he emerged from the war without a scratch.

Hero of Russia

Brought up on Chechen soil, the general did everything to avoid bloodshed. His efforts were especially pronounced during the active phase of the CTO (1999‚Äì2000). For the operation in Dagestan, which marked the beginning of the CTO, and the courage shown during the hostilities in Chechnya, General Troshev was introduced to the title of Hero of Russia.
