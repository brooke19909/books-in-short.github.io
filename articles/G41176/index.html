Hotel La Madrugada Beach Resort 3 * (Tanzania, Zanzibar): description, reviews

Holidays in the very center of Africa, namely in Tanzania, will surely give tourists a lot of memories and positive emotions. On the territory of this amazing country, a huge number of outlandish varieties of flora grow, and the richness of the fauna is simply amazing. This country is also known for many fascinating stories and comic tales about Aibolit, who sat under a baobab and treated animals. The most famous symbol of Tanzania is Mount Kilimanjaro. A popular resort is Zanzibar. Tours here are not cheap, but the trip will give truly unforgettable emotions.

Zanzibar - a separate country

As for the political system, this state has long had sovereignty, as well as its own president, parliament and other necessary authorities. Zanzibar is not considered to be a separate territorial unit, however, this island has long been fighting for freedom and, it must be said, is not unsuccessful. Many representatives of the population of Zanzibar have long been separated from the main people, considering themselves only Zanzibar people and no one else. Speaking of culture, it is worth noting the similarity with the Tanzanian one, nevertheless, differences can still be found in something.

Holidays in Zanzibar

As for the organization of recreation and leisure, the island of Zanzibar is the best option for these purposes. Diverse sightseeing trips are regularly held here, the most interesting of which is a trip to visit plantations where many common spices are grown. Guides organize various tours, during which everyone can learn the intricacies of growing seasonings such as cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, etc.

Beaches

Zanzibar is ready to offer visitors about 25 cleanest beaches with an azure sea bottom and dazzling white sandy beaches. I must say that it is on the territory of the beach areas of Zanzibar that the lion's share of the most beautiful pictures that get on the Internet is removed, and many visitors to sites with skepticism believe that photoshop is involved here.

Information about the fact that the beaches of Zanzibar are firmly entrenched in the top five best beaches in the world both in the beauty of nature and in the level of service will not be superfluous.

Underwater world

Visiting tourists are very fond of diving on the island, since the underwater world of this area is extremely rich and diverse. There are beautiful corals, and outlandish fish, and amazing aquatic plants, and much more. There is even an opinion that many pearls were lost at the bottom of the ocean in this place.

The visibility level under water is from 10 to 30 meters, which makes it easy to find under the water column and electric rays, and moray eels, and jellyfish, and crabs, and other bizarre marine settlers. The best places, according to many professional divers, are the reefs of Boribo and Morogo, on the territory of one of which is located a mysterious turtle cave. In addition, here is the habitat of the giant Mantoux stingray. Very close to this place a long time ago sank a tourist ship, long overgrown with moss. There is a path between the lost islands of Pange and Bave.

sights

Speaking of attractions, it is worth mentioning such an amazing place as Stone Town, another name for which is the stone city. This city was built in the first half of the 20th century by the Sultan of Zanzibar Island. By the way, this city is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The territory of the stone city is literally completely built up with a variety of shelters for believers. There are 51 mosques, 6 churches for Jews and 2 churches for Catholics. Among other things, on the territory of the stone city there is a huge number of buildings made of stone rocks, the oldest of which is the fort of the Arabs. The main highlight of this place is a huge number (about five hundred) of doors carved from wood, which have brass upholstery and are a kind of heart of this city.

Another amazing place that you should definitely visit is the palace of ceremonies of the Sultan, once rightfully called the "House of Miracles". The thing is that the outlandish phenomena in this house were those amenities that no apartment building can do without now: this is an elevator, hot and cold water, and sewage.

A very famous place is the island of Prizon, which has a huge number of prisons for the mass of prisoners. Now these buildings have stopped working, and remained only as visiting places for visitors. Giant turtles, whose age is measured in several hundred years, are in great demand here. These turtles were introduced directly from the Seychelles, where they appeared initially.

There are a lot of hotels in Zanzibar. One of them is a complex called La Madrugada Beach Hotel Resort 3 *.

Description

This magnificent, comfortable and very cozy hotel is located on the coast of the resort town of Mankunduchi, located east of Zanzibar.

Each room of this hotel complex has beautiful rooms equipped according to all the laws of the tourism business and having wide panoramic windows overlooking the sea shore and beautiful exotic palm trees.

Infrastructure





For a complete leisure, vacationers are offered a clean beach, 2 swimming pools in the heated area and a jacuzzi, all kinds of sports and a restaurant with a varied menu.

Each room at La Madrugada Beach Resort (Zanzibar) has a guest corner, a fan or air conditioning, a bathroom with a shower or bathtub and everything you need, as well as TV, beds and access to the World Wide Web. For children there is a games room, classes with animators and babysitting services.

Guest Comments
