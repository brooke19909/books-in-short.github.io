Evelyn Beach 4 * (Greece / Crete) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Crete today is one of the most visited by Russian tourists islands that are part of the Hellenic Republic. It attracts travelers with its magnificent nature, excellent beaches, warm sea, as well as all the necessary infrastructure for an unforgettable vacation. If you are looking for a comfortable hotel in Crete, but at the same time do not want to overpay for accommodation, we recommend that you pay attention to Evelyn Beach 4 *.

Description and photo of the hotel

The four-star Evelyn Beach Hotel was built in 1993. The last large-scale reconstruction was carried out in 2000. Today it is an elegant hotel, located in a fairly quiet place, close to all the attractions of the resort village of Hersonissos (Crete, Greece). The beach is just around the corner from the hotel. As for the distance from the airport, the nearest air harbor to the hotel is located in Heraklion, 26 kilometers away. So upon arrival in Crete, the hotel can be reached within literally half an hour. Evelyn Beach 4 * ( Crete, Hersonissos) has a restaurant and a bar. Here guests are offered a choice of meals according to the following schemes: only breakfast, half board or all inclusive. This hotel is well suited for active tourists who are accustomed to combine beach vacations with visits to clubs, discos and trips to the surroundings and attractions.





Rooms

The housing stock of the four-star Evelyn Beach Hotel consists of 76 rooms located in one building. It offers standard single, double and triple rooms. Some apartments have sea views. All rooms are spacious, comfortable, pleasantly decorated and equipped with everything necessary. So, here there is air conditioning, TV (with Russian-language channels), a refrigerator, a large bathroom with a hairdryer, a safe and a balcony or terrace. Rooms are cleaned every day. Towels are refreshed every two days, and bed linen twice a week.

Prices

The cost of accommodation in this hotel, according to many of our compatriots, is quite consistent with both its category and the level of service and living conditions. So, a week of stay at Evelyn Beach in the midst of summer holidays will cost you from 371 euros for a standard room for one, from 616 euros for a standard double room, from 665 euros for a standard double room with sea view and from 826 Euro for a triple room. Prices shown include breakfast. Half-board and all inclusive systems will have to pay extra for meals.

Impressions of our compatriots about the hotel, the territory and the number of rooms

According to tourists reviews, Evelyn Beach 4 * is a standard city hotel. It has virtually no territory of its own, so you will not find a pool or a playground here. The main contingent of vacationers in Evelyn Beach consists of young people and couples or couples of various age categories.

As for the housing stock, the travelers found the hotel rooms to be nice, comfortable and quite spacious. Some apartments have sea views. Guestrooms feature pay-per-view safes. On TV you can watch one Russian-language channel. The bathroom in the rooms is spacious, there is no interruption with hot water. Also in the rooms there is a hairdryer, so you can not take it with you from home. You will not find an iron in the room, but you can always ask for temporary use at the reception. Wi-Fi is only available in the hotel lobby for an additional fee.

For cleaning the rooms of Evelyn Beach 4 * (Crete), guests had no special complaints. True, according to some tourists, sometimes they forgot to add soap or shampoo during the cleaning process, but it was worth saying this to the maids or the administrator, and the situation was quickly corrected. Towels were changed every other day, and bed linen was updated twice a week.





Hotel location comments

According to our compatriots, Evelyn Beach 4 * has a very favorable location. It is located in a fairly quiet place, but just a stone's throw from the entire infrastructure of the resort village of Hersonissos. So, you can walk to the main street with shops, cafes and bars in just five minutes. The beach closest to the hotel is a three-minute walk away. You can also take a pleasant walk along the promenade, where there are shops, restaurants and clubs.

Guest opinion on meals at Evelyn Beach

According to tourists, the variety of the menu Evelyn Beach 4 * (Greece), of course, can not be compared with five-star hotels, but the choice of dishes and their quality are very good. Meat, chicken and fish dishes are served daily. There are always a lot of fresh fruits and different salads. Also, those who wish can enjoy delicious desserts and ice cream. The restaurant is always clean and tidy, the dishes are good, there are no queues. By the way, you can eat both in the indoor restaurant and on the terrace in the fresh air with a gorgeous view of the sea.
