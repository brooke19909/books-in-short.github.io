Hotel Ras Al Khaimah Hotel 4 *, Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates: reviews and photos of tourists

The United Arab Emirates is a region of the world that is incredibly popular among tourists with the luxury of its hotels, as well as high quality services. One of the middle-class hotels is located on a hill and has the same name with it - Ras Al Khaimah Hotel (Ras Al Khaimah). Tourists in their reviews very often note that from its windows you can clearly see all the surrounding nature, which is represented by tropical plantations, also from its terraces you can watch the sea.



general information

The history of the Ras Al Khaimah Hotel dates back to 1971, the date of its construction. On the territory of the hotel almost every year (during the absence of tourists) construction work is carried out, with the help of which the hotel currently has a modern look, and its technical equipment keeps up to date. One of the last such renovations was made in 2012.

Location

The hotel is located in a fairly secluded place on a hill, surrounded by beautiful nature. The coastline is located at a small distance from it (about 3 km), which allows you to admire it from the windows of the rooms. The entertainment and leisure center (the city of Dubai) is an hour's drive from here, which undoubtedly pleases tourists who prefer an active nightlife.

The hotel can be reached from three airports that are approximately the same distance from it: Sharjah, Dubai and Ras Al Khaimah. It will take them about 40 minutes to get there.

Not far from this hotel is another popular holiday destination - Mangrove Hotel Ras Al Khaimah 4 * (United Arab Emirates). It is also often recommended by many travel agencies for vacations. The hotel in question in terms of providing accommodation also competes well with Doubletree by Hilton Hotel Ras Al Khaimah, which for many years has won the hearts of guests with an atmosphere of ease, fun and luxury.

Rooms

All apartments that are offered to guests are located in one large building, which rises to four floors. It has a modern and quite beautiful appearance, which is often mentioned in their reviews by vacationers. There are 92 rooms that are arranged quite comfortably, which tourists also appreciate.

Each room has a private bathroom with modern fixtures. It includes a large bathtub, shower, sink, as well as a bathroom. In free-standing drawers, each tourist is offered to use a bathrobe, towel, standard set of bath accessories and slippers. The rooms also have individual air conditioning, which guests can use if necessary to adjust the temperature in the room.

All apartments are arranged in the same style - classic. They contain rather expensive interior details, most of which are made of wood. The rooms are divided into several categories: Superior, Julphar, Executive, as well as Presidental.

Business

At Ras Al Khaimah Hotel (UAE), as in other hotels in this country, much attention is paid to creating the conditions for doing business. This is due to the fact that the main flow of tourists who relax here is divided into two groups: people who came to relax, and also those who are on a business visit. It often happens that a tourist belongs to one and the second category at the same time.

For this category of people, the hotel has two conference rooms, which are arranged in such a way that are ideal for meetings, presentations and various business events. They have very comfortable furniture for guests, as well as modern lighting and video equipment that allows you to make the event as bright as possible.

Lease of such places is paid, it is charged hourly.

Nutrition

Hotel guests often respond positively to the work of the main restaurant, which is located in its main building. It works from 6:30 a.m. to 10:30 p.m. All guests who are staying according to the all-inclusive concept can visit it. Sitting comfortably at a table, guests can enjoy magnificent views of the sea and flowering gardens. According to tourists, it serves delicious, beautiful and very nutritious dishes prepared according to the best traditions of Eastern and European cuisines. If desired, guests can place an order for room service.

In addition to a large restaurant, there is also a pastry shop. It offers the best sweets prepared according to the exquisite traditions of the East. Tourists who managed to taste them note that they have not only excellent taste, but also a pleasant spicy aroma.

Sports and SPA

Ras Al Khaimah Hotel has a beautiful spa area, which is always reviewed by guests. They talk about the fact that there is an excellent massage room where professionals can immerse any client in a state of complete relaxation. They also recommend visiting a local sauna, after which it is appropriate to perform a number of cosmetic procedures to maintain the excellent condition of their skin and the whole body.

Sports guests are certainly happy to visit the hall of Ras Al Khaimah Hotel 4 * (UAE), which has the best fitness equipment for different parts of the body. It also offers the option of playing tennis on the modern court. These services are paid, their cost can be checked with the administrator. Experienced travelers recommend using the services of a tennis instructor, which are provided for a fee.

Prices

Reviews of Ras Al Khaimah Hotel often refer to the cost of staying there. It can vary significantly depending on the desired living conditions. So, in the case of a week-long stay for two in a Superior room, the price of a tourist package with an all-inclusive concept will be about 55,000 rubles, which is quite acceptable for this country. Travelers recommend taking a standard package, and all other services that are needed are advised to pay at the place of rest, explaining that the cost of the same opportunities can vary significantly.



Hotel guests also recommend that you know the schedule for the bus to the beach in advance. Travelers are advised to pay attention that the entrance to the beach is free only for two. If the company is large, then, starting from the second guest, a fee of 10 dinars will be charged.
