We will tell you how to make animals from beads. With the scheme and a step-by-step description of the work - two master classes for beginner needlewomen

From beads and glass beads you can make not only complex interior items, flowers, jewelry, jewelry for shoes, hair and clothes, but also simple crafts - figures of animals, fish, birds and insects.

If you want to learn beadwork, start with the easiest way to make such small flat figures. In this article we will tell you how to make animals from beads by spending a minimum of time and effort . With the diagram and step-by-step descriptions presented in this article, you will surely succeed. So, let's make a fly and a cute bugle from bugles together with you!

How to make a beautiful product? Brooch "Fly"

To make this stylish craft, you will need to prepare the following materials and tools:

  • beads and long bugles silver and black;
  • two large shiny beads of dark gray color;
  • thin wire (0.43 mm);
  • scissors.

Having acquired the necessary materials and organized a workplace, get to work. We will perform the fly using the following schemes for beads.

Animals, as a rule, are made of separate parts, which are then assembled into a single structure. First, we will make four wings of a fly (see Fig. A). Take a piece of wire about 20 cm long, beads and glass beads of silver color. The first row of the wing will be made of 1 pc. bugles. Then we collect beads and bugles, we extend the ends of the wires towards each other. The third row is made of one bugle and two beads. Strictly adhere to the scheme of work, stringing beads, make sure that they are always from the same edge as in the previous row. The fourth row - the middle of the wing - is made of one bugle and three beads. The fifth row is made of the same number of beads as the previous one, but they are typed from the other end. Further, work continues with a decrease in the number of beads by one in each subsequent row. The last row of the element is made of bugles (1 pc.). Everything, the first wing of the fly is ready. By analogy, we carry out three more exactly the same details.

Now we start weaving the body of a fly. We take a piece of wire 25 cm long and black beads. We begin to perform the torso from the back, guided by rice. b . The first row is made of one black bead. The second - of the two, letting the ends of the working wire towards each other, and the third - of the three. Next, we perform three rows with four beads, without magnifications. It will be the abdomen of our fly. Now we carry out three more rows of three beads in each. Here we will mount the wings. Next, we string two large shiny beads on the ends of the wire. The eyes of the fly are ready. It remains to make a proboscis of two black beads and complete the part with bugles (1 pc.). The ends of the wires are fixed with a twist around the last bead. Our fly is almost ready. It remains to make paws and assemble all the parts together.

We will begin the work by making the ear from white and pink beads (see Fig. A). By analogy, we will perform the second ear, and then connect the parts as shown in Fig. b . At the remaining end of the wire of the first ear, we lower three white beads and draw it through the holes of two beads of the last row of the second ear.
