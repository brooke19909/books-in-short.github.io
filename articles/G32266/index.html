Brokerage companies in Moscow: rating, list of the best. Credit brokerage companies, Moscow: assistance in obtaining a loan

Finding a bank, getting a loan, and most importantly, getting it is not an easy task, especially when it comes to rather impressive amounts. Nevertheless, brokerage companies in Moscow can help in this matter, the rating of which is annually compiled on the basis of consumer feedback. Before choosing the appropriate office for the provision of such services, it is worthwhile to understand in more detail who the brokers are. How safe is it to work with them? And is there any benefit to this?

Who is a loan broker?

In fact, this person is an intermediary who helps in obtaining a loan for a certain monetary reward. By and large, the loan broker negotiates with the bank and represents the interests of the client, the latter can only come to the credit institution at the appointed time and put a signature in the agreement.





However, it should be borne in mind that credit brokerage companies in Moscow cannot guarantee a 100% probability of obtaining a loan to those customers who previously had delays in loans. Simply put, if a borrower has a bad credit history, even the best broker in the city will not be able to completely clear it.

How does collaboration work?

Before you consider the rating of brokerage companies in Moscow, it is worth a little more to study the procedure for contacting a specialist. It should be noted right away that today in the Russian Federation there are more than 3 thousand companies providing such services. Some are officially registered, while others operate without a license.

If a client seeks help in obtaining a loan from Moscow brokerage companies, then cooperation is built according to the following algorithm:

  1. A person makes an appointment with a specialist.
  2. The broker reviews the credit history of the client and evaluates his approximate chances of a successful loan.
  3. The specialist draws up a list of banking institutions that are most likely to agree to provide a borrower with a loan for a certain amount.
  4. The client chooses the most interesting conditions and the bank with which he wants to start cooperation.
  5. The broker agrees with the credit institution and informs the borrower where he will need to drive to conclude the agreement.
  6. The borrower signs a contract and receives a sum of money, after which his specialist is awarded a fee.




Choosing the best brokerage companies in Moscow, you should immediately clarify whether a company employee wants to get his percentage before the transaction is completed. If the answer is yes, then it is better not to contact such an office. Since if the bank refuses, the client will not receive an advance payment back. Most often, scammers act in this way, which disappear immediately after receiving a reward.

Employees of reliable companies never ask for any prepayment. And the amount of interest is usually not more than 2% of the loan received. Nevertheless, it all depends on the complexity of the situation. If the borrower has a very bad credit history, then it will not be so easy to find a bank that agrees to start cooperation. In this case, the percentage of remuneration increases.

Can a broker really help in obtaining a loan?

Most often, those borrowers who already have problems with credit organizations turn to specialists. Usually these are people who, due to some circumstances or simple reluctance, did not pay the previous loan on time, which in turn led to a decrease in the credit line.





It is worth saying that the lists of brokerage companies in Moscow can be found on every corner. However, this does not mean that any of the proposed organizations can really help in obtaining loans. Most of these companies employ inexperienced specialists who do not know all the intricacies of obtaining loans, provided that the client does not have a good history of loans.

If you apply to a brokerage company with a high rating and positive reviews, then the chances of receiving money at a percentage increase significantly.

Black loan brokers

Today, activities of such organizations are not regulated, therefore, a large number of fraudsters can be found on the market, cooperation with which can lead to disastrous consequences.

You can identify a black loan broker by the following criteria:

  • High commission. Very often, scammers demand too high rewards.
  • Lack of company information. Fraudsters never register companies officially. In addition, they often do not have a fixed landline telephone, as they do not rent offices.




  • Paid services. If they require money for a consultation with a specialist, then this is the first sign of fraud. The same applies to the paid preparation of a package of documents for the bank. In trusted organizations, all of these services are free.

Before contacting such an organization, it is worthwhile to inquire about it and get acquainted with customer reviews.
