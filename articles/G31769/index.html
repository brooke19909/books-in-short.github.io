Power: what is measured, how is applied, calculation formulas

What is strength and power? What is measured by this indicator, what devices are used in this case, and how the named physical quantities are applied in practice, we will consider further in the article.





Force

In the world, all bodies of a physical nature begin to move due to force. When it is exposed, with a passing or opposite direction of body movement, work is done. Thus, a force acts on the body.

So, the bike starts moving due to the force of the legs of a person, and the traction force of the electric locomotive acts on the train. A similar effect occurs with any movement. The work of force is the value in which the modulus of force is multiplied, the modulus of displacement of the point of its application and the cosine of the angle between the vectors of these indicators. Power





What is this quantity measured in? To begin with, we will analyze what it is. It is clear that the body begins to move due to the force that performs mechanical work. However, in practice, in addition to this, it is necessary to know how it is performed.

Work can be completed at different times. For example, a small motor or a large electric motor can perform the same action. The only question is how long it will be produced. The quantity responsible for such a task is power. In what it is measured, it becomes clear from the definition - this is the ratio of work for a specific time to its value:

Watt and other power units

Watt means power, where one joule is produced in one second. The last unit was named after the Englishman J. Watt, who invented and built the first steam engine. But at the same time he used a different value - horsepower, which is still used today. One horsepower is approximately equal to 735.5 watts.

Thus, in addition to Watt, power is measured in metric horsepower. And with a very small value, Erg is also used, equal to ten in minus the seventh power of Watt. It is also possible to measure in one unit of mass / force / meters per second, which is equal to 9.81 watts.





Engine power

The named value is one of the most important in any motor, which happens to be of very different power. For example, an electric razor has hundredths of a kilowatt, and a spacecraft rocket has millions.

For different loads, different power is needed to maintain a certain speed. For example, a car will become heavier if more cargo is placed in it. Then the friction force on the road will increase. Therefore, in order to maintain the same speed as in the unloaded state, more power is required. Accordingly, the engine will eat more fuel. This fact is known to all drivers.

But at high speed, the inertia of the machine, which is directly proportional to its mass, is also important. Experienced drivers who are aware of this fact find a better combination of fuel and speed when driving, so that less gasoline is used.

Current power
