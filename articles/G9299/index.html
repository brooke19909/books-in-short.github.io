Holidays in Bulgaria: review of experienced tourists

Picturesque mountain peaks, long sandy beaches, the warm Black Sea, the possibility of healing, a lot of the most pleasant experiences - all this promises a vacation in Bulgaria. Golden Sands, Sunny Beach, Nessebar, Burgas and Varna - these seaside resorts have already reached the level of tourist infrastructure that will satisfy the most discerning travelers. And at the same time, the prices for travel to this wonderful country remain low compared to the same neighboring Turkey. The absence of trouble with a visa, a short flight, budget holidays, close to the Russian language of the hospitable population - all this attracts Russian tourists to Bulgaria. From Moscow airports you can reach not only the capital of Sofia, but also directly to the resorts: to Burgas and Varna.



Holidays in Bulgaria: review of a curious tourist

There is something to see in this country. Ancient Rome, incorporating the modern territory of this country into the empire, left a lot of antique sights here. Byzantium built in Bulgaria many temples and monastery complexes, which are now included in the UNESCO World Heritage List: Rila Monastery, Ivanovo churches in the rocks, Boyana Church. Among other world famous monuments can be called Sveshtarskaya and Kazanlak tombs, Madara Horseman. This open-air museum is an ancient city of Nessebar. Nature lovers will discover many new things in the Srebarna Nature Reserve and the Pirin National Park.

Holidays in Bulgaria: a review of a skier



In this southern country in winter you can ride on the mountain slopes! Three ski resorts: Pamporovo, Borovets and Bansko - have made Bulgaria famous all over the world. Of course, this is not Andorra and Switzerland, you should not expect miracles from the local service. In addition, the season in these mountains lasts less than in the Alps and Pyrenees - you can confidently ride here only in January-February. But Bulgaria pleases with an abundance of slopes for beginner skiers and those who prefer slopes of medium difficulty. Snowboarders can also find suitable slopes in the Bulgarian mountain resorts .
