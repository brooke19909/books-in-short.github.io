Imperial 4 * (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Where is the Republic of Bulgaria located? This state is located in Southeast Europe. It occupies 22% of the Balkan Peninsula. The country was named in accordance with the ethnonym of the people - the Bulgarians. By 2011, the population was 7,364,570 people. In the European Union, Bulgaria ranks eleventh in territory and sixteenth in number of inhabitants. Its capital is Sofia, the official language is Bulgarian. This country is ruled by parliament. In 2012, Rosen Asenov Plevneliev was elected President of Bulgaria. The country's lands are divided into twenty-eight small regions, which are divided into 264 regions.

This wonderful state in the east is washed by the Black Sea, in the south it borders with Turkey and Greece, in the west - with Serbia and Macedonia, and in the north - with Romania. According to the constitution, Bulgaria is a secular state. 78% of the inhabitants profess Orthodoxy.

Tourism in Bulgaria

Tourism brings huge profits to Bulgaria. A large number of tourists visit either a summer or a winter resort. And magnificent 4 * hotels (including the Imperial Hotel) always welcome guests! Tourism in Bulgaria is developing rapidly, because the country owns both natural and cultural attractions. By the way, in this state, only two months are unsuitable for rest in the resorts - October and November. The Black Sea March is marked by a confident spring with flowering trees, and from June to September, vacationers are splashing with might and main into the sea.





Black Sea coast of Bulgaria

The coastal zone of Bulgaria contributes to the rapid development of beach tourism. This country is one of the most important resorts for Eastern Europe. In the 1990s, the flow of tourists decreased, but now their number is growing steadily. Most of the guests come from Scandinavia, Germany, European countries, Ukraine, Russia and the UK. The most visited Black Sea resorts are Sunny Beach, Obzor, Riviera, St. Constantine and Elena, Elenite, Sozopol, Albena, St. Vlas, Golden Sands. The balneal resorts include Hisar, Sandanski and Velingrad. With pleasure, tourists visit the ski resorts: Pamporovo, Borovets, Bansko.

The National Statistical Institute estimates that in 2012, 8,339,477 tourists visited Bulgaria.

sunny Beach

The largest seaside resort in eastern Bulgaria is Sunny Beach. It is located by the bay in the Black Sea. Its ten-kilometer-long beach in the central part is one hundred meters wide. It is covered with fine yellow sand. Sunny Beach is located between the cities of Burgas and Varna, is part of the community of Nessebar. City buses run between ancient Nessebar and Sunny Beach. This wonderful resort has been awarded the Blue Flag. The municipal beach of Sunny Beach is clean and well equipped. Most of it is occupied by sun loungers under umbrellas. Equipment is serviced by concessionaires-entrepreneurs. Here you can relax for free only in free areas, since you must pay for the use of umbrellas and sun loungers. The seabed is sandy, smoothly going into the depths.

Hotel Imperial

What is the famous Bulgarian hotel Imperial 4 *?
