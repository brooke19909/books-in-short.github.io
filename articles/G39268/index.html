Mirage Suite Hotel - general information

This is a city hotel, which, of course, does not stand directly on the beach. It is located in the very center of Alanya. This is a five-story building with an elevator. It was opened in 1997, rebuilt in 2005, and in 2010 it underwent a major overhaul. The hotel is small, but it has everything you need for relaxation, including a swimming pool with a sun terrace. The territory is small but cozy. This is a budget hotel with all its features. It has a private car park. It is in a quiet place, and at the same time close to everything. You can see all the attractions, saving on guides and transport. In addition, all the shops and shops nearby are many times cheaper than on the waterfront.

Location

Located Mirage Suite Hotel 3 in the resort town of Alanya, near the Ataturk Monument, four hundred meters from the sea. This means that you can get to Antalya Airport from it farthest. Tourists who have a shuttle should be prepared for a long trip.
