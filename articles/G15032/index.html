China Yuhai International Resort Apartment SPA 5 * (China / Hainan Island): photos and reviews of tourists

China has long been a popular destination for Russian tourists. Many go on the beaten track for inexpensive goods. And those who crave relaxation by the warm sea, choose the island of Hainan. Tourism infrastructure is developed there much better than in the rest of the PRC. Many high-class hotels, entertainment and excursions will be a pleasant addition to a vacation on beautiful well-groomed beaches by the warm South China Sea. Where to stay to spend a vacation on about. Hainan? Yuhai International Resort Apartment SPA - a five-star hotel - became the topic of this article. By the way, most recently it was renamed to Azur Resort 5 *. What conditions await holidaymakers in this hotel? Where is it? What is nearby? Our article will tell about all this. We were based not only on information provided by the hotelier himself. We studied the reviews that tourists have already visited the resort hotel.



Hainan Island The nuances of relaxation

Previously, this island, whose name can be translated as "In the South of the Sea", was exiled to the emperor of the Celestial Empire who fell into disgrace. Now it is called "Chinese Hawaii." The island also has a middle name. Due to the large number of thermal mineral springs, Hainan is called the "Health Resort of China." This is the second largest (only Taiwan surpasses it) the island of China. But Hainan is the only one in the country lying in tropical latitudes. Therefore, as an application to beaches and the sea, tourists get access to bananas, lychees and other exotic fruits, which the region is rich in. You can relax in Hainan, where the Yuhai International Resort Apartment SPA (China) is located, in any season. Three hundred days a year are clear, the average air temperature is +24, and the sea +26 degrees. But the ideal time to visit Hainan is spring and autumn. In the summer months it is too hot, and in the winter there are those same 65 cloudy days.

Sanya. Resort Features

If Hainan is the most chic island in China, then Sanya is the most prestigious holiday destination on it. The resort is located at the southernmost tip. All that China has achieved in modern architecture and hi-tech can be seen in Sanya. This resort meets all needs: shopaholics - because of the abundance of shops and fashion boutiques; parents with children - due to the gentle entrance to the sea protected from storms; divers - because of the wealth of the underwater world off the coast; youth - because of the huge selection of entertainment. As for hotels, most of them belong to a high category of service. Among them is Azur Resort 5 * (formerly Yuhai International Resort Apartment SPA). But you can also find budget hotels in Sanya.

The resort has many shopping centers where they sell clothes and shoes of famous brands. But there is a place for craftsmen and craftsmen. The main souvenirs that are brought from Sanya are pearl jewelry. This industry was developed here even before Hainan turned into a resort island. For shopping, it is not necessary to sacrifice the beach: shops and markets are open until ten in the evening.

Where to eat outside the hotel

Near the beach you can also have a snack. Sanya has long been a favorite resort for Russians. Therefore, in cafes and restaurants you will not have a language barrier with the waiters. Beach and sea

Directly to the coast you need to go about a hundred meters. But taking into account the bypass of the buildings in front and the intersection of the highway, the path will take about ten minutes. The nearest city beach is very clean and, importantly, free. This is a feature of the Sanya resort. Yuhai International Resort Apartment SPA does not have a dedicated territory on the beach. Yes, it is not needed, since toilets and showers can be used for free. The beach in Sanyavan is very spacious, several kilometers long. It is covered with dense light sand. In Sanyavan, the beach is ideal for bathing young children, since the entry into the sea is very gentle. Reviews report that there are few sun loungers and umbrellas. But along the road there is a row of palm trees, in the shade of which you can hide from the sun. Many travelers have tried other Sanya beaches. If you come in winter, head to Dadonghai District. In its bay, the water is warmer than on neighboring beaches.



Pool

An alternative to lush beach life with ever-scurrying merchants can be a relaxing rest by fresh water. How many pools are there at Yuhai International Resort Apartment SPA? Reviews report two. One, smaller, is equipped specifically for children. The swimming pools are very clean. The water in them warms up well. At the children's pool there is a small slide and a decorative mill. On the sun terrace around these ponds there are many sun loungers and umbrellas, rescuers are on duty. The only negative - there is no bar near the pool. With drinks you have to go from the lobby of the hotel where this institution works. If a group of Russian or Chinese tourists comes to the hotel, the pool becomes noisy.

Excursions in Sanya and around the island

Tourists strongly recommend that you do not spend all days by the sea or in the Yuhai International Resort Apartment SPA 5 * hotel. Hainan is a fairly large island, and it has something to surprise a traveler. The only inconvenience is that all historical sights are concentrated in the north, near Haikou. The capital of the island itself is also interesting, where disgraced nobles settled in exile. In addition to the ancient houses of the Ming era, you can visit the tomb of Hai Zhuya, Wugong and Nanshan temples. And in the vicinity of Haikou, there is the Ma-An paleovolcano with a viewing platform on the very edge of the crater. Sanya is a completely new city, but there you can find entertainment. This is diving, paragliding, surfing and sea fishing. Children will be interested in visiting the island of monkeys, where visitors get by cable through the strait. Tourists are also taken to the Bethel Nath ethnopark (30 kilometers from Sanya). There, representatives of local meow nationalities and whether demonstrate their life and culture.

Treatment in Sanya

Hainan's thermal waters help to cope with diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, hypertension, osteochondrosis, rheumatism, urological and skin ailments. If the procedures provided in the SPA-center of the Yuhai International Resort Apartment SPA 5 * hotel seemed inadequate to you, reviews recommend visiting in the Dadonghai district. There, on the basis of the Pear River Garden Hotel, there is a center of Chinese traditional medicine, Longevity Garden. The best balneological hospital in Sanya is considered SanYiTang. Its branch was opened in the Cactus Resort Hotel in the Yalongwan District. Treatment in these medical centers is carried out exclusively with natural medicines, acupuncture, manual therapy. Reviews also praise the Nantian, Yu-Kang, and Taiji thermal mineral springs.
