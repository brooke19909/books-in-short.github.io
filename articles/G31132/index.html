Where exactly is the hotel located and what interesting things can be found near it?

Pattaya Park Beach Resort has earned admiration for its location in the first place. Pattaya Park Beach Resort is not just a hotel, it is a whole complex, the main parts of which are two buildings of the hotel - Pattaya Park (a more romantic part of the hotel, which is partially hidden from human eyes and is suitable for those who come to relax from any fuss and want to reunite with nature) and Pattaya Park Tower (this is a high-rise building, which, in addition to hotel rooms, also includes many entertainment options that those tourists who are tired of sitting in boring offices dream of so much).

There is literally a stone's throw to the coastline, only two hundred meters will separate you from the warm waves. What is in the room?

Pattaya Park Beach Resort cares about its guests, so the feedback that it has air conditioning, a landline telephone, a large TV and a hairdryer is true.
