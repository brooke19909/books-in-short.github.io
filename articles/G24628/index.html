How to make an autumn bouquet of sweets with your own hands?

DIY gifts will always be appreciated, of course, if they are made efficiently and beautifully. Species for their manufacture do not require special resources, the main thing is imagination. Do-it- yourself autumn bouquets of sweets are not only beautiful, but also delicious. However, such work requires time, attention, accuracy and great desire. In addition, outside the window, autumn, and this is the richest season for gifts, you can find a large amount of free exclusive material for your idea.

If you really decided to translate such a congratulation into reality, then it is worth considering the cost of the necessary materials. First of all, you need to study everything well in order to create a really beautiful and interesting gift that is suitable for any autumn holiday.

Necessary tools and materials

You cannot list a specific list of materials and tools, it all depends on your imagination. To decorate an autumn bouquet of sweets, you can use anything you want, we draw your attention only to its main components:

  • corrugated paper;
  • candy;
  • leaves, acorns, small twigs;
  • scissors;
  • decorative tape;
  • wire;
  • frame (polystyrene foam, foam, hardened mounting foam);
  • decoration items;
  • glue (preferably using a glue gun, it greatly simplifies the work).

You can choose everything that seems interesting and suitable for the composition. Use various ribbons, bows, decorative flowers, beads, buttons, exclusive threads, fabric. Everything that comes to your mind can be embodied in reality, the main thing is not to overdo it. Now there are many different stores for needlewomen, you can find the necessary tools there, or just order them on the Internet.

If you use wire as a frame, be sure to think about how to disguise it.

Cardboard, dishes, baskets may be suitable for the base. You can apply various home decoration. If you want your autumn bouquet of sweets to be unique, do not use banal materials, turn on fantasy.

How to make flowers?

First of all, you need to learn how to make buds. This is the basis of any composition. Skilled craftsmen sometimes create real masterpieces that are difficult to distinguish from natural colors. Here your imagination is unlimited. For example, you can take as a basis a rose - a queen of flowers, delicate lilies will look original, carnations are perfect for the holiday on May 9, peonies add creativity to your work, or you can collect several types of flowers in one bouquet. Feel free to realize your fantasies. For example, we give a couple of options for a photo of an autumn bouquet of sweets.

Phased flower creation

Creating an autumn bouquet of sweets and corrugated paper is not as difficult as it might seem at first glance. We bring to your attention one of the options for creating a flower with a step-by-step guide.





Prepare the material and cut it into wide strips.

Fasten each part in the middle with a thread or wire, fold it in half. On the opposite side, cut the edges in a semicircle and twist using a knitting needle or pencil.





In the next step, you will need candy. Give preference to sweets of a round or oval form. Treats need to be attached to a toothpick (you can use a wire, a branch or any other material). Cut the petal in half at the place where the thread was, and wrap around the candy. Secure corrugated paper with wire, thread or glue.





Continue sticking the petals over each other. After the flower is ready, complete it with green paper, thereby hiding the not-so-beautiful places for attaching the petals, and also add the leaves.





The principle of creating a flower is simple and straightforward. Taking it as a basis, you can create a wide variety of compositions, changing the color, material, number of petals, etc.

Teacher's Day

If you decide to conquer your child‚Äôs teacher, you can create a unique gift for the teacher together with the child. Its creation is worth the effort, but it is worth it. How to create an autumn bouquet of sweets for a teacher?

Necessary materials:

  • sweets;
  • autumn leaves;
  • double-sided and decorative tape;
  • ribbons, decorative threads, lace (which is at hand);
  • corrugated multi-colored paper;
  • scissors;
  • colored foil;
  • glue;
  • base materials;
  • wire;
  • colour pencils.

We begin our work. The process does not take much time, but requires special care and attention.

  1. Choose a paper color. Cut it into strips (width and height depend on the size of the flower). Then cut one side of the fence.
  2. We wrap the candy in the resulting part and fix it on the end with an elastic band. The protruding cola is slightly rounded to give a resemblance to real flowers.
  3. We make as many flowers as you need for a bouquet.
  4. We take paper of any other shade. We make five hearts, and cut off their pointed tips. Stretch the middle. With the help of knitting needles we wrap beautifully the edges. We pack the candy in the resulting petals. We lay one on top of another to make a rose.
  5. We cut one long and several short pieces of wire. We fix on the end of each sweets. We wrap our future ‚Äúberry‚Äù with red foil, and below we fix and decorate the whole branch with green tape. We connect all the components.
  6. We make a cone-shaped frame from wire or take it ready-made.
  7. From the middle to the edges on the inside, we lay live leaves and fix them.
  8. Add the stem to our buds, using wire and decorative tape.
  9. We collect the bouquet on the frame. Add decorations.
  10. We attach colored pencils to the wire and insert them into the composition, among the flowers.
  11. We frame the frame with a shade of corrugated paper, similar to that which decorates living plants.
  12. Around the wrapper where the stems are located, glue the pencils on the double-sided tape.
  13. We beautifully bind the composition with a ribbon and the autumn bouquet of sweets is ready!

You can add whatever you want there. Just stick to your chosen style and remember who this souvenir is for. The advantages of such creativity is that you can cook it in advance for the holiday. After all, a paper gift will not be able to deteriorate, even in the absence of water.

For children

It is important to consider what sweets the child likes. Naturally, it is better not to use treats with the addition of liquor or cognac. All children love sweets, which means you need to put more sweets, chocolates and lollipops, but less paper and various fillers.

For men

Completely the opposite situation. In the composition, which is intended for a man, it is worth using candies and dark chocolate, with cognac or liquor. You can also decorate the bouquet with a miniature bottle of Jack Daniels. If desired, you can make a combined bouquet, diluting sweets with banknotes.
