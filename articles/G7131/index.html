Planet Park in Astrakhan - a sea of ​​entertainment for everyone

In Astrakhan there are many attractions and places where you can go with the whole family. But one of the most popular with the townspeople and guests of the city is the Planet Park (Astrakhan) and this is not surprising. Its owners are constantly striving to surprise their visitors, expanding the number of attractions and improving their quality.

The largest park in the city

Park Planet (Astrakhan) is the largest amusement park in the city. This applies to the area and the number of attractions. In addition to the park there are two ponds where you can ride on catamarans and enjoy the picturesque waterfall.

For animal lovers, a contact zoo is open. A large stage has been equipped where concerts of musical groups and other city events are held. On a huge screen broadcast sports, collecting a large number of fans.

For the smallest

Planet Park in Astrakhan pays great attention to kids, because children are the main visitors to the park. There is a closed playground for children's games. Here they can play, learn a lot of new and informative, have fun. Funny animators will capture the attention of the kids, and adults at this time can relax or go about their business. Under the close supervision of educators, children are completely safe.



There is also an outdoor playground where kids can swing on a swing, ride a hill. Outdoor games in the company of friends - what else do children need ?!

Well, and, of course, a variety of trampolines, musical horses, funny trains and other colorful carousels will delight children. And riding a real pony is an unforgettable entertainment for any kid.

Entertainment for teens

For older children in the Planet Park in Astrakhan there is also plenty of entertainment. Among them are spectacular chain flights, riding electric and cycle cars, sailing on bumper boats. And many more exciting rides.



Contact zoo

City children are removed from wildlife. But in the “Planeta” park in Astrakhan, the “Merry Farm” contact zoo is open here, where kids can not only look at different animals, but also feed and pet them. Pink pigs, fluffy rabbits, active kids, funny zebras, luxurious peacocks, farm chickens and important turkeys will bring a smile not only to children, but also to their parents.

Recreation for adults

The organizers of the rest in the Planet Park in Astrakhan did not forget about adult visitors. Extreme sportsmen will be able to test their strength on the Hip-Hop ride, drive on go-karts or a race track. From the height of the Ferris wheel you can admire the city.

Ponds

The schedule of the Planet Park in Astrakhan allows you to visit it at any convenient time. It is so pleasant here on a hot day or on a warm evening to stroll along the shore of the pond, watch the swimming swans or feed them by hand. Or admire the “floating” roses, which have become a living attraction of the city. For their red color and beauty, locals lovingly call them scarlet flowers.



Cafe by the water

You can relax from active entertainment in a cozy cafe, located on the beach. Here you can order dishes of Russian, Ukrainian, Caucasian or Oriental cuisine. Fishing enthusiasts can relax by throwing a fishing rod, and the cafe chef will prepare the catch. Tasty dishes, cozy atmosphere, sounding music have a rest.

Helpful information

In general, on the territory of the Planet Park in Astrakhan, whose address is Ostrovsky Street, 146, there are many places to relax. Boat stations, bungalows, gazebos, benches, open and closed areas, attractions for people of all ages.



A card system has been introduced in the park. At the box office, visitors replenish the card and can use any attraction of their choice by swiping a card through the terminal. This is very convenient - no need to defend the queue in front of each carousel. And you can top up the balance at the cash desk at any time. Entertainment prices are quite affordable. Depending on the chosen attraction, they range from 20 to 100 rubles per ticket.

The working hours of Planet Park in Astrakhan are convenient for visitors. It is open daily from 10 am to midnight. You can always find time to relax after a hard day to spend it with your family. Getting to the park is easy. From all over the city there are buses, shuttles and trolleybuses. Near the park, the supermarket has a large parking lot where you can leave your family car and go have fun.

Park Planet is an ideal place for families. Here you will find activities for everyone and kids, and teens, and adults of all ages. In the park, anyone can again feel like a child or experience new sensations at extreme attractions.

Park staff is constantly increasing the amount of entertainment so that visitors find something new for themselves. New carousels and attractions are installed, and the old ones are restored and repaired on time, so they are always ready to go.

Park Planet will give an unforgettable vacation to the whole family.
