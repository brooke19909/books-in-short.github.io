We sew summer shorts with our own hands for a little fashionista

A beautiful skirt always looks very feminine on a girl of any age. There are various options for this wardrobe item: pencil skirt , wrap skirt, tulip skirt and short skirt. The latter option is very suitable for little fashionistas, because children always lead an active lifestyle, and in a regular skirt it is not always convenient. In summer, shortened pants look very easy and airy, and therefore any girl will surely like it. You do not have to buy all the clothes in the store, you can sew summer shorts with your own hands.





Pick the right fabric

You can choose a different fabric for the product: silk, denim , satin, velvet and even wool. Please note: if you want to sew shorts with your own hands for wearing in the summer, choose lighter materials. In addition, natural fabrics such as cotton or satin should be purchased for children.

Pattern

If you find the right material, you can safely proceed to the pattern. As a basis, you should take the scheme of a short skirt. The pattern needs to be expanded and divided into three parts. To make women's shorts with your own hands, draw a line from the fold, separating the first third. This line should be transferred to the tuck. On the back of the structure, you need to carry out the same procedure. Next, along the vertical lines made, cut the parts, moving them 3-4 cm. Calculate the length of the shorts depending on the height of the girl, but remember that this piece of clothing should be short so that it is not hot in the summer. The length of the cut will depend on the size of the tuck - the deeper it is, the smaller the cut should be. If you plan to make pockets, then you need to cut them out separately.





Do it yourself shorts

When the pattern is ready, you can proceed to the most important stage of work. According to the patterns, make the details of the future masterpiece by transferring the scheme from paper to fabric. Remember to leave a seam allowance. Pin the finished parts together with pins so that they do not move when sewing. If you plan to make shorts with pockets, then it is better to sew them before assembling the main parts.

Now you can start sewing shorts with your own hands. Connect the sides first. Then sew the gum channel. Tuck the bottom of the product at the very end and sew. It remains to process the internal seams with an overlock. Now do-it-yourself shorts are ready! Unpack them to iron all seams. You can decorate the model with decorative ribbon, fringe or embroidery. Shorts will look very nice with a strap. If you choose this option, then just sew the loops for the belt.





Finally

Do-it-yourself shorts, photos of which are presented in the article, are sewn very quickly and easily. Even the beginning dressmaker will cope with this model. The main thing is to correctly transfer the pattern to the fabric and make neat seams. You can come up with jewelry for your product with your child. It can be a fun applique or bright large buttons. You can sew a ribbon and pockets. Now your fashionista will show off in original new shorts that no one else has.
