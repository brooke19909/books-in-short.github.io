IPad mini: user reviews

In October 2012, Apple introduced another new product to the market. She became a smaller copy of the "apple" laptop iPad mini. Such a model could appear only after the death of the founder of the company, Steve Jobs, since it was he who opposed the release of tablets whose screen sizes would be less than 10 inches. It is this display size that allows everyone to work

specially designed applications without crashes and freezes. But current Apple executives bypassed the ban of the guru, and the iPad Mini came into being , the user reviews of which we now consider.

The quality of the aluminum case is not in doubt. Knowing the material from which the laptop is made, it is easy to understand how the manufacturer achieved its small weight. The dimensions of the tablet allow you to wrap it around with your palm so that the device is in your hand securely, and your fingers do not block the screen. Users are happy to change iPad 2 to this baby. The small size of the display does not cause discomfort in operation. The extension of 1024x768 dpi does not strain vision, does not allow eyes to get tired. The disadvantages include the same display. Many users find that Retina is better.

IPad mini: reviews of women



In the first place, any woman would put convenience. The small size allows you to carry the device in your purse. The small weight (only 310 grams) does not interfere with taking it with you wherever you go: to work, to visit, in a cafe. Agree that three hundred grams is better than a pound. Great camera. Rate it allow the screen paint - juicy, bright colors. You can print at high speed. The iPad mini does not freeze and does not slow down. The price seems great for such a baby. But later you can make sure that the tablet fully justifies it. The laptop looks stylish and solid, despite the size.



The small size makes it very convenient to place it in a female hand. The tablet itself is very light. The display is good: the pictures are bright, colorful, juicy. Applications of previous versions work perfectly: they do not freeze, they do not slow down. The price is quite reasonable for a laptop of this class. The iPad mini-2 will soon appear on the market, the difference from the previous version is the Retina matrix. Experienced tablet users praise her for the high quality display of graphic information. If you do not play games, then there is no need to change the iPad mini, reviews of which are pretty good. The only thing that can push to replace the laptop is a more powerful processor, which manufacturers promise to equip the device with.

The battery life is also a significant plus. After active work for 7 hours, the laptop uses only 40% of the energy. Another advantage over the big "brothers" is the short charging time. Meet about iPad mini reviews can be different. But of all the shortcomings, only one is significant - the display. With the Retina matrix, previous versions of the tablets featured brighter pictures. However, one drawback could be attributed to one feature - incompatibility with laptop chargers of earlier versions of laptops. But this is not a very big drawback, and it is unlikely that it can affect the choice of a tablet.
