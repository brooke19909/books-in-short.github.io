Interesting ideas on how to make a flower out of fabric for a headband

Probably many of us were faced with the problem of where to put on old T-shirts and unnecessary knitwear from our family members. Do not rush to throw them away!

We present you some original ideas on how to use this very nice material. How to make a headband for yourself or a child and how to make a flower from a fabric to decorate it. You will also learn how to do other useful and cute things with your own hands.

Creating a blank for a headband



Before you start thinking about how to make a flower from a fabric from an old T-shirt, prepare the material from which you will create your little masterpieces: wash and dry old T-shirts or other things from stretch fabric. Cut the strips of knitwear in the direction shown in the figure. If the strips are thin enough, then you can get blanks resembling cords. They can easily make an original jewelry that looks like a necklace. Cords of various lengths and differing in tone look beautiful. Gather them to your taste and attach flowers from fabric and ribbons at the junction - in this unusual necklace you will not go unnoticed.







For a headband, cut a wider tape. Its length should be approximately 5 cm less than the circumference of the head. This is necessary so that the elastic bandage fits tightly during wear. There is no need to process the edges of the tape - the knitwear will curl around the edges a little and form a beautiful roller on both sides. Connect the ends of the dressing with a simple seam on a machine or manually. A flower will be placed at the seam site, which will close the junction of the dressing.

If you like weaving, then try to weave the base for a headband from knitted cords. The tape can be three or more, as shown in the photo. Wrap the junction with a piece of fabric so that the bandage has a neat appearance, and decorate with a flower.
