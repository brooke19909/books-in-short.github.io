Preparation for work

These instructions are the simplest examples of how to make shoes for Monster High dolls . But even simple DIY work requires careful preparation of the workplace. The surface on which the process will take place must be disposed of from excess objects and then wiped - dust and dirt can seriously ruin the shoes.

Polymer clay doll shoes

Polymer clay is an excellent modern material that exists in all kinds of colors and shades, pleasant and easy to use. How to make shoes for Monster High dolls with your own hands? Yes, very easy! It will only take:

  • polymer clay of the right color, suitable for the outfit of a doll;
  • any model knife (suitable for clerical).

From clay it is necessary to mold the base in the form of a wedge or platform, with one edge raised - where the heel of the Monster High doll should be. We put a leg on it and cut off the unnecessary excess on the sides.





Then we cut out two strips about 1-2 mm thick. These strips must be attached to the back of the heel in the form of a backdrop and in front to form the toe of the shoe. Carefully align the material so that there are no seams or creases.

We take the Monster High leg from the workpiece and, if a heel is needed, just cut it out of the wedge heel - the base of the shoes is ready!

Now you can start decorating. From the same clay, you can make straps, stick flowers or apply patterns with a toothpick. After that, the shoes are baked in the oven. It is worth carefully reading the instructions for polymer clay - the manufacturer usually leaves recommendations on the required temperature and baking time.

Papier mache shoes

The quickest and easiest way to make doll shoes is made of modern flexible polymer clay. But such universal material still needs to be bought, and new accessories are needed now! How to make shoes for Monster High dolls from improvised materials that everyone has in their house?

We will need:

  • napkins;
  • newspaper;
  • PVA glue;
  • stationery eraser;
  • hand cream.

The pupal leg is smeared with cream, and then glued with a napkin in three layers. From above it is necessary to stick several layers of small pieces of newspapers, plentifully moistened in PVA. Then you need to again wrap the legs with a napkin in two or three layers. It is better to take a white napkin, so that later it is easier to paint the finished shoes in any color.

A wedge or platform under the sock is cut from the eraser. If the shoes are on the heel, then for its formation it is necessary to trim the thick edge of the toothpick. Details are carefully glued to the workpiece on the Monster High leg, and then wrapped with a PVA napkin in a couple of layers.





Carefully remove the base for the shoes from the doll‚Äôs legs. Now you can cut the desired shape from the workpiece and proceed to the decor. Then everything can be limited only by the imagination of the author! For coloring shoes, you can use sparkles, nail polishes or acrylic paints.
