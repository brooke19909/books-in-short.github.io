Pensions Olginka: "Svetlana", "Orbit", "Impulse"

At the word "Olginka" everyone represents a cozy resort village in the Krasnodar Territory on the Black Sea coast. A small village located just 25 km from the center of Tuapse, since Soviet times, has won the status of one of the most environmentally friendly and green areas. In this picturesque area the healing aroma of coniferous trees, the transparent sea and well-maintained beaches harmoniously intertwine. Also pleasing are affordable housing prices.

An even highway runs to the village, so it is not difficult to get here. A huge advantage is the lack of industrial and chemical enterprises in the resort. Due to this, the air in the area is saturated with volatile and ozone, which has a beneficial effect on human health. That is why the hotels and pensions Olginka enjoy tremendous success.

Many tourists relax here more often than in larger resorts. To accommodate guests in the village, many luxurious hotels and health resorts have been built with their own dendrological park and pristine beach. The largest and most popular boarding houses in Olginka are Impulse, Svetlana, Orbit, Olginka. Let's get to know each hotel in more detail.

Rest house "Impulse"

An excellent dispensary, designed for an audience of different ages, is located on the first coastline (own beach). It works on the European type of "all inclusive". Guests have access to a spacious dining room and restaurants. Nutrition is developed by experienced nutritionists and taking into account individual medical indicators. Fresh greens, sour-milk products and healthy mineral water are always present in the diet.





Pension "Impulse" (Olginka) provides excellent conditions for therapeutic and beach holidays. In the enclosed enclosed area there is a large pool with amenities and water slides. In the cold season, the hotel does not stop receiving guests and does not limit services. Instead of the beach area, there is an indoor pool.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas. For relaxation and relaxation there are comfortable benches arranged around the entire perimeter of the complex, buried in lush vegetation - tall palm trees and bright colors. In the middle of the tropical park stands a comfortable three-story residential building and two-story cottages with a well-maintained lounge and a 24-hour reception.





All rooms are decorated in light pastel colors and equipped with modern furniture. Even the standard rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, as well as a refrigerator and a TV. The suites are air-conditioned. From their rooms, guests can enjoy stunning views of the Caucasus Mountains or the Black Sea.

Wellness at the Impuls Hotel

For an additional payment, everyone will be able to heal their health, get the advice of an experienced doctor and undergo a complete diagnosis in a modern medical center. The complex specializes in pathologies of the respiratory, musculoskeletal, digestive and cardiovascular systems. Professional assistance is provided to patients with skin diseases.

The basis of the treatment is physiotherapy, aromatherapy, halochamber, massage, physical education, inhalation and diet. Oxygen cocktails with various plant components (mountain ash, wild rose, sea buckthorn) are offered daily.

The cost of the resort voucher includes three meals a day as a buffet. The housing stock consists of a number of three-story cottages. There are rooms with two rooms and superior comfort. Absolutely all rooms have air conditioning, telephone, bar and ironing facilities.





For a beach holiday boarding house "Svetlana" (Olginka) has a small pebble coast with sun loungers, awnings and a cafeteria. Near the residential complexes built a swimming pool, children's play areas, sports fields and a gym. The administration arranges excursion routes to interesting sights.
