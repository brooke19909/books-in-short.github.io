Sultans Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Camyuva): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Larissa Hotels is a dynamically developing hotel chain with its head office in Turkey, specializing in providing a relatively inexpensive guesthouse (about $ 60 per day) to its guests. This economical pricing policy allowed her to consistently open three- and four-star high-quality and cost-effective hotels in promising growing resorts.





On the Antalya coast in Turkey, for example, there are 11 hotels from Larissa. This network launches its boarding houses in other countries. In this way, a corporate strategy is implemented - to grow into a rating network of hotels not on a regional, but on a global level.

The subject of this article is a comparison of four-star hotel complexes named the same: Larissa Sultan Hotel - and built in Turkey (Camyuva village) and Egypt (Hurghada city). Using their example, we see promising management from Larissa: to build standard rating hotels that provide classic level services at econom class prices. However, each of the hotel complexes of the same name has its own characteristics, which come from the economy of the country where it is located. At least, after reading the article, you yourself will come to the conclusion: which of the hotels of the same name - in Camyuva or in Hurghada - is more suitable for you.

How to buy a tour

Buying excursions for vacationers in Turkey and Egypt has common features. The pricing strategy of the hotel agents is expressed in one of the known types of financial transactions - speculation.

Suppose you in an Egyptian hotel decide to take a sightseeing tour of the island of Tobia (it is also called Paradise Island). You can save. For example, right at the hotel, his guest can buy a ticket for this tour for $ 20-25. And with quick wits, he can find a travel agency outside the hotel that offers the same ticket for $ 14. This option is preferred. At the same time, we do not recommend using the services of overtly dumping artisanal entrepreneurs offering a price of $ 10. In order not to return to this financial aspect further, we summarize: we still recommend, before buying any excursions at the Sultan Beach Hotel (Hurghada), to compare their cost with market prices from several tour agencies. Save money, do not overpay one and a half to two times.
