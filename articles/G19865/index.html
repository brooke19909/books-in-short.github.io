The uniqueness of the tract

The soil structure here is such that moisture at the roots of the trees persists for a long time, so the slopes of the mountains are covered with bright greenery. Actually, the gorge is of interest precisely because of its vegetation, because here trees grow that are no longer anywhere in the world - for example, the sacred Vitex, it is also an Abraham tree, and others. Most importantly, the Wide Beam has a wonderful, even climate - the summer is not hot, the winter is cold (left-handed bloom). It is the mild climate that provides incomparable comfort - the air temperature in July is +28 degrees, and water - +27.



All this led to the formation of the sanatorium complex "Wide Beam" here, rest and treatment in which turned the suburban summer cottage village of Novorossiysk into a resort of federal significance. A bit of history

The traces of the Greek settlements, which archaeologists find along the banks of the gorge itself, confirm the historical information that these places are from the end of the VI century BC to the IV century BC. e. were part of the state of Sindica, and then, since 480 BC. e., - in the Bosporus kingdom. So, in 1898, on the territory of the Gorny Rodnik base (then these lands belonged to the landowner Kuleshevich), a bronze bust of the granddaughter of Mithridates the Great, the Bosporan Queen of Dynami (stored in the Hermitage) was found. Later, these lands passed from hand to hand; here are traces of Genoese fortresses. During the war years, heavy battles were fought for these lands on these territories.
