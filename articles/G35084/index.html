How to crochet a baby shirt shirt?

Knitwear is always in fashion. They become especially popular in the cold season. After all, how pleasant it is to wrap yourself in a warm scarf or to feel the fluffy softness of mittens on your hands.





One of these useful gizmos is the shirt shirt. Crocheted or knitted, it is not important.

The main thing is that the product reliably covers the neck from the cold. Manishka is a successful replacement for a scarf, especially for children, as it fits tightly around the neck and does not unwind. At the same time, this part of the wardrobe can become an ornament if it is made of thin threads with an openwork pattern for which knitting needles or a hook are used. Knitting a shirt front will not take so much time, it all depends on the chosen style and thickness of the threads.

Different models of shirt-fronts

There are many variations: voluminous and small collar-sized shirts, dense of thick yarn and easy to use in combination with spring raincoats. The shirt-front can be put on over the head or have a fastener. The latter are more practical, especially for children. Clasp is the most changing element. The use of the option with small loops on the shoulder or back is popular. The shirt-button can be buttoned up completely or partially. An interesting element is the use of hidden zippers instead of buttons. According to the color scheme, products are usually combined with other wardrobe items - a hat and mittens.





Crocheted shirt-front: the simplest option

We offer you one of the ways a baby shirt shirt fits . Crocheting this model can be done in 2-3 hours. It is not difficult for experienced craftswomen to make a similar product with knitting needles or use a different pattern when working. A combination of several colors and decoration with decorative elements look bright.

To work, you need 150-180 grams of wool blend yarn and hook number 3. This model is suitable for girls 7-10 years old. Crochet a shirt shirt is quite simple.

We collect 50 loops of the chain. Next, follow the conventions and instructions:

  • BN column - single crochet,
  • CH column - double crochet,
  • column C2H - double crochet,
  • VP - air loop.

1 - 6 row - columns of BN.

7 row - columns of CH.





7, 8 row - columns of CH, after about four columns we knit two in one to expand the shirt front.

9 row - * column C2H, 2 VP, skip one loop of the previous row *, then repeat the repeat ** to the end of the row.

10 row - in each arch from 2VP of the last row, knit three C2H. Between the "fans" - 2VP.

11-13 row - knit as 10 row.

14th row - * a column of BN is knitted into the arch from 2VP of the last row, then 7 VP *, then repeat rapport ** to the end of the row.

15 row - * in the BN column, knit the BN column, 5VP, the BN column in the 4th loop of the arch from the VP of the last row, 5VP *, then repeat the repeat ** to the end of the row.

Such a crocheted crochet shirt can be decorated with tassels on the bottom or with a multi-colored lace with small pompons. The final step in the work is the processing of the fastener. To do this, each of the two front slats must first be tied with BN columns, and then with fans of five CH columns. At the same time, do not forget to make button loops, which can also be crocheted.

Having shown imagination and skill, you can easily do the job. Let your children enjoy new things and wear them with pleasure.
