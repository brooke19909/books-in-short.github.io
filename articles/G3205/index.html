SVEN HA-1410T: review, comparison with competitors and customer reviews

Modern powerful 5.1 speakers that work directly with a computer or DVD player are becoming more popular. There are more and more lovers of quality sound, but not all of them have money for an advanced speaker system, so many manufacturers have begun to create sets of speakers at a lower price than classic high-end options. SVEN brand

Now the Scandinavian brand SVEN was created in 1991 in the USSR. As part of the conversion, the company was engaged in the development and sale of multimedia devices (portable acoustics and speaker systems) and various computer peripherals (headphones, keyboards, headsets). Throughout its history, the company has followed the only principle - to use the cheapest materials, to limit the functionality of the devices, but at the same time to put forth all efforts to achieve high quality of the main function of the device. Thus, the manufacturer managed to create a market filled with high-quality, and most importantly, inexpensive speakers and headphones. Next, we turn to the review of SVEN HA-1410T, the actual speaker system itself.

What is active acoustics 5.1?

To begin with, it is worth deciding what active acoustics 5.1 is. The number 5.1 means a complete set. The user receives a ready-made set of 5 speakers (often referred to as ‚Äútweeters‚Äù or ‚Äúsatellites‚Äù), which are responsible for the upper and middle tones, and 1 subwoofer, which is responsible for the depth of sound and bass. The subwoofer also acts as a kind of ‚Äúhub‚Äù to which speakers are connected, as well as a media player or computer. The word ‚Äúactive‚Äù means that the system does not need a separate amplifier, the speakers are already equipped with built-in options.

Appearance of the speaker system and components

The visual design of the speakers is impressive. They look really worthy. There are several color options that can be selected for a specific room. Thanks to the minimalist and austere look, they can easily fit into even the most sophisticated interior. The material for the columns is a fiberboard, the so-called MDF (transliteration). The thickness of the plate is 15 millimeters. A synthetic winterizer is laid in the inner part, which affects the acoustic characteristics of the ‚Äúsatellites‚Äù, which reduces the level of vibrations. To connect the cables, screw sockets with a small amount of gold spraying are used. To avoid confusion, the connectors are color coded.

The subwoofer is made of the same materials. It looks pretty neat, without any prominent elements. On the front panel there was a place for a compact display. On it looms information about the reproduced content and other useful information. The speakers and power jacks are located on the back. The subwoofer rests on four metal spiked legs. This design contributes to the stability of the subwoofer, but may damage the flooring. The manufacturer thought about this and put rubber tips in the kit.

The remote control is not convenient. It can be seen that the bias was made towards the accepted external design and compliance with the general style of the entire speaker system. It is very thin and light, but the number of options on it is limited. In fairness, it is worth noting that the available settings will suffice for the vast majority of users.





Speaker design

Each SVEN Audio HA-1410T speaker is equipped with a bass reflex and a small plastic tube located in the back of the chassis. Two-way speakers protected by a Butterworth filter are used. Thanks to this filter, the amplitude-frequency characteristic of the speakers remains smoother and almost disappears at the frequencies of the suppression band. Mid-range speakers, their cone is made of polypropylene, and its suspension is made of rubber, which is not very stiff, which is good for sound. The high-frequency elements are in the shape of a small dome and made of silk. There is a powerful magnetic system with full shielding. The whole mechanism has a fairly wide stroke. Another important detail is the acoustic impedance level (this characteristic indicates how many ohms are in the SVEN HA-1410T). It is 6 ohms.

Subwoofer design

Among the design features of the subwoofer, one can distinguish a large radiator grill that maintains the low temperature of the amplifiers. The diffuser is made of dense material of an unusual shape with a large number of recesses, which favorably affects the level of resonance in the sound. The bass reflex and two wide pipes are led out to the bottom panel.

In general, the 5.1 SVEN HA-1410T acoustics is a truly ‚Äúserious‚Äù device. It can be seen that the manufacturer wisely approached the creation of the device, focusing on the maximum level of quality. An LM4731TA chip is involved, on which a stereo amplifier with a large power reserve is installed. Among the shortcomings, it is worth highlighting a limitation of 20 watts, after the intersection of which there are obvious distortions that hit the sound quality. Nevertheless, for such a set of speakers this power is usually more than enough, the main thing is not to overdo it.





Listening experience

The speaker system perfectly copes with any music, no matter what genre you prefer. The sound is beautiful and powerful, thanks to the advanced speakers and characteristics of the SVEN HA-1410T as a whole. Even if you bring the power to full, it is hardly possible to notice any serious changes in the sound. There are certainly distortions, but you can put up with them. Mids are impressive. The sound detailing and its saturation far beyond the price segment of the monitored acoustics, a higher level is heard worthy of many Hi-Fi systems. High frequencies also bring a lot of pleasure, since they do not spoil the impression of a typical background whistle, wheezing and hissing typical for such systems. The bass in the SVEN HA-1410T deserves a standing ovation. Regardless of the length of the deep bass, the system copes with it with a bang. Hard metalcore blast beats and lingering ‚Äúexplosions‚Äù of hip-hop music sound great.

Of course, a lot depends on the location of the speakers in the room. The speaker system is designed for installation in a room of about 30 square meters. With the proper placement of all elements, an immersive presence effect can be achieved. It is important that when you switch from another set of speakers to the brainchild of SVEN, you can discover new compositions for yourself that you have listened to for years. Everything that used to seem familiar and hackneyed appears in new colors and again surprises.

It is difficult to single out any drawbacks, since there is no even a frequently encountered problem with an excess of intrinsic noise in the columns. A slight noise can be noticed only if the volume is set to maximum, and even then you have to snuggle up close to the speaker to notice it. Nevertheless, the system is very sensitive to interference, so when you turn on other electronics, you can hear crackling and noise in the speakers. Again, these clicks are hardly noticeable.

Measurements

The analysis data of the amplitude-frequency characteristics (hereinafter AFC) are encouraging. To begin with, it is worth clarifying that when collecting data, frequencies in the interval 130-500 Hertz were intentionally overwhelmed. The characteristics of the speaker system were taken at "zero" settings. The fact is that the subwoofer has a wide working band, the upper threshold of which is 500 hertz. Therefore, the data was shot in this way to combine the subwoofer and the ‚Äúsatellites‚Äù.

Data was taken in a straight axis at an angle of 45 degrees. The frequency response was uniform, with a slight deviation within 6 decibels.
