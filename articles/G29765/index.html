Hotel Dream Vacation Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): overview, description, features and reviews

The topic of this article is the hotel Dreams Vacation Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 *. This luxurious five-star complex is considered among tourists the best among competitors in its price category. In our article today, we will try to consider all the most important nuances for relaxation and answer the main questions of tourists.





Description

Dreams Vacation Resort Sharm El Sheikh (Egypt) shares its resort territory with another hotel - Dreams Beach Resort. Guests of both hotels enjoy shared services and entertainment facilities. Both hotels are located in the prestigious area of ‚Äã‚ÄãSharm el-Sheikh called Umm al-Sid. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe complex is seventy thousand square meters. The hotel we are interested in accepts guests in comfortable two-story buildings. There are nine such buildings in total, plus one administrative building.

Beach area

Beach Dream Vacation Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * also shares with Dreams Beach Resort. The entrance to the sea is coral, so you should get slippers. The question immediately arises: is there a place for young children to enter the sea? Tourists are advised to wait for the tide and swim on a small sandy island, but still wear slippers. But the views of the coral reefs in these places are simply magnificent.

The relaxation area on the beach is completely sandy. Free sun loungers, mattresses, beach towels and umbrellas are provided for free. Entrance to the sea is from a pontoon or pier.

To get to the beach, you need to go a fairly large distance from the residential complexes, and this despite the fact that the hotel is on the first coastline. A rather steep and long descent leads to the sea, because of which the management of the complex was forced to build an elevator. But its minus is that it is located at the intersection of the borders of two hotels, so it is not convenient for all guests to use it. For example, if you settled in the left wing of Dream Vacation Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 *, then you will quickly get to the sea if the sender is on foot.

Hotel facilities

The infrastructure of the complex is huge. As a self-respecting five-star hotel, Dream Vacation Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * provides free parking and a safe at the reception. Guests can visit one of six restaurants, three of which are a la carte, located in the Dreams Beach Resort. Additionally, there are three bars, as many conference rooms and outdoor pools.

Also, hotel guests can visit the laundry, beauty salon, first-aid post. Internet access is available for a fee. In 2015, thirty minutes online cost six dollars. Many people prefer to buy a local SIM card and thus save on gigabytes.





For children

Dreams Vacation Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt) is a good choice if you are relaxing with the kids. The restaurants have a children's menu and you can even find milk. And so that the child feels comfortable, he will be provided with a personal high chair.

Animators also work actively, amusing kids while parents enjoy diving in the Red Sea. If you are too lazy to go to the beach, you can offer your beloved child to ride on a water slide. There are two children's pools.

Entertainment and sports for adults

Adults also have something to do, so as not to lie on the sand. You can play tennis, as there are two fully prepared clay courts in the area. Of the active entertainments, two large water slides are at the guests' disposal.

You can choose from a sauna, a bathhouse, a hammam, a jacuzzi and massage. Seasoned vacationers are advised to attend at least one massage course. Its cost is thirty dollars, but all the other times you will visit the sauna for free. Yes, and get an unearthly pleasure from the work of a professional.

Dream Vacation Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * offers a lot of water activities. The most popular, of course, is diving.

You can play table tennis, billiards, volleyball, soccer for free, visit the fitness center and disco.





Number of rooms

The hotel has 319 rooms in all nine buildings. The hotel was built in 2003, and during this time the number of rooms has been restored more than once. Recent updates took place in 2014, but not all rooms have been restored to normal.

Standard in each room there is a bath or shower, hairdryer, minibar, air conditioning, cable or satellite TV, telephone and a balcony or terrace. Some rooms already have Wi-Fi.

Room Reviews

There are few negative comments about the Dreams Vacation (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh). The reviews of most people are generally positive. But not about the rooms. Unfortunately, there are more minuses than pluses.

The poor, even dead state of the rooms is not surprising. The hotel has existed since 2003, and there is simply not enough time to repair all 319 rooms. The influx of people is large, and the hotel would simply lose its income if the buildings were closed. And nobody wants that. Partial repairs were already started in 2014, and those who settled in the restored rooms were very lucky.

According to some tourists, they expected more from a five-star hotel. The rooms are not very impressive guests. Very old plumbing, everything flows, the shower practically does not work, because it flows out of the water hose more than from the divider, the toilet bowl is loose. But the worst thing is the black fungus on the tile. After the request to change the number, they will not always go towards you, and if they do, then it is not a fact that the number will be better than it was. The staff tries to repair non-working equipment immediately, but, unfortunately, one has to put up with many shortcomings.

Housekeeping is good. Workers try, change linen and towels daily. Be sure to leave a tip, otherwise you will not get rid of the cleaner until he gets his.
