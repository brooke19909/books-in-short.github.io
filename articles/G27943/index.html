Aransia Resort Hotel (Turkey / Alanya): everyone can afford a relaxing holiday

Dreaming of a serene vacation on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea? Then welcome to the sunny tourist complex "Aransia Resort" (Turkey). This is a real paradise nest with a huge landscaped area and a tropical park.





The hotel is located away from the motorway - in a resort ecologically clean area in the village of Konakli near the center of Alanya. You can get to the town from Antalya Airport (110 km). The hotel is quite new, founded in 2006 and during this time has undergone reconstruction more than once.

The Aransia Resort (Turkey) complex is oriented towards a calm, measured and laid-back family vacation with children. Conditions have been created for people with physical disabilities, for this category of tourists there are special rooms and paths.





Number of rooms

The hotel's 7-story building housed more than 400 rooms, half of them with stunning views of the coast. The rooms are divided into classes. Minimum room size is 26 square meters. For especially demanding tourists, family 2-room rooms with a living and sleeping area are provided. There is a balcony and a private bathroom.

For a comfortable stay, all rooms have air conditioning. A TV with Russian television is available. A telephone line has been made to contact the administration of Aransia Resort Hotel (Turkey). A safe, Internet and a bar with drinks (free mineral water) are available for a fee. Wet cleaning is carried out regularly . Bed linen is changed on certain days. On request, a baby cot will be delivered.





Nutrition

Three meals a day according to the European all-inclusive system are waiting for guests. There is a separate menu for vegetarians and children. Hot and cold snacks, sweets and drinks, including alcoholic drinks, are served at the afternoon snack.

If you want to enjoy Italian dishes, then visit the restaurant A La Carte, it works on a preliminary reservation. Guests are allowed a free one-time visit. Fish and Turkish restaurants, 6 bars with a variety of desserts and delicacies are open. In the evenings, it serves a wine bar for connoisseurs of elite alcohol.





Beach

Aransia Resort Hotel (Turkey) is located just 50 meters from the beach line. The shore is pebble and sand, well maintained and equipped. All beach items (mattresses, sunshades, towels and sunbeds) are free of charge. From morning till night there is a bar where you can buy soft drinks and ice cream.

Additional Information

In the adjoining territory of the tourist center "Aransia Resort" (Turkey) there are two external pools with slides. Another is located in the hotel building. Sports services are provided: tennis court, parachuting, billiards room. Customers can attend tennis, diving and surfing lessons at any time, play bowling, do aerobics and belly dancing.





For moviegoers open cinema. Those who wish to improve their health and put their body in order will appreciate the gym, Turkish bath, solarium, massage rooms, SPA-salon with cosmetic procedures. Businessmen and those who come to work will always be able to comfortably hold any kind of meeting and negotiations in one of three conference rooms (up to 500 seats). Children's leisure includes a playground with equipment (swings, slides), a development club up to 12 years old and a swimming pool.

Digest





Positive emotions, lots of vivid and unforgettable impressions await tourists at the Aransia Resort Hotel (Turkey). Prices for accommodation here range from 1700 rubles. up to 5900 rub. per day. The hotel is one of the most popular in Turkey - quality service combined with reasonable cost will not leave anyone indifferent.

Guest reviews in 90% of cases are positive. Guests were satisfied with the rest and service. The contingent of vacationers is mainly couples with children, so the atmosphere in the guesthouse is calm. The guests unanimously praised the amazing and varied food, well-maintained and modern rooms, a clean beach and a polite attitude from the whole staff.
