Hisense TVs: reviews, specs and specs

As part of this material, Hisense TVs of two models will be considered. One of them is marked N42K391 and belongs to the middle segment of such devices. The second is designated 55M7030UWG and belongs to the niche of premium solutions.





Briefly about the manufacturer. Device positioning. Equipment

In 1969, Hisense debuted in the Chinese market. Televisions, HVAC equipment, refrigerators, mobile phones and smartphones are far from a complete range of products from this manufacturer today. The production facilities of this company are located in China. But its products are sold worldwide.

As noted earlier, Hisense LED TVs considered within this material belong to the middle (model N42K391) and premium (devices labeled 55M7030UWG) segments.

The delivery list of devices of this brand includes the following:

  1. Multimedia Center.

  2. A set of supports with a mounting system.

  3. Power cord, with which the power supply of such a solution is implemented.

  4. Corporate control panel with a set of batteries.

  5. Guide for connecting, configuring and operating.

  6. Warranty card with a period of company obligations 1 year.





main parameters

The younger model of Hisense N42K391 TV has a 42 ‚Äùscreen diagonal. It is capable of displaying an image in 1080p format, or, as it is sometimes also called, FullHD. The type of matrix installed is EdgeLED. The image type is widescreen, and its aspect ratio is 16: 9. The characteristics of the integrated tuner indicate that it can work with any type of input signal, except satellite.

The sound system includes 2 stereo speakers. Its total power is 10 watts, and each of the built-in speakers can produce 5 watts. Additionally, at the software level, AVL technology is implemented. With it, you can improve the quality of sound.

The software shell of this solution is based on the VIDAA U2 operating system, which, in turn, is based on the Android OS. The communication list of this multimedia device includes all the necessary connectors, among which are USB, HDMI, SCART and RCA. There is also a wireless way to get Wi-Fi information.

The availability of more advanced technical specifications boasts Hisense flagship televisions. Reviews at the same time focus on an affordable price.

In the model 55M7030UWG, the screen is enlarged to 55 ‚Äù, and its resolution can be 4K, or 2160r. The type of matrix installed is ULED. The aspect ratio of the screen is identical, as well as the technical specifications of the built-in tuner.

Built-in acoustics is implemented in a similar way. But here the total power of the built-in speakers is increased to 20 watts. A single speaker can produce 10 watts of sound.

The software shell is similar - VIDAA U2. More precisely, all devices with Smart TV technology from this manufacturer are equipped with just such software.





Owner reviews. Device price

Hisense TVs amid competing multimedia devices boast an advantageous combination of price and technical specifications.

The younger model can be purchased for 42000-45000 rubles. Again, the N42K391 is discontinued. Therefore, you can only buy it from stocks. The advantages of this model include functionality, the presence of an advanced software shell, an expanded communication list, a simple configuration procedure and reliability. Also a significant advantage of this solution is the special AVL technology, which allows to improve the quality of the output sound. The minus of this device is that this Chinese brand is not widely known.

Hisense flagship televisions boast even more advanced specs. The reviews highlight such advantages for the model marked 55M7030UWG in comparison with the mid-range solution:

  1. Support 4K.

  2. Improved acoustics.

  3. An even better communications list.

  4. The advanced matrix that underlies the screen.

Moreover, the cost does not exceed 50,000 rubles.





Conclusion

This article describes two models of Hisense TVs. Both the youngest N42K391 and the more advanced 55M7030UWG have excellent specifications and support the Smart TV option. Also, their cost is quite affordable. As a result, such devices are great for home use and allow you to create full-fledged multimedia centers. In other cases, such functional devices are impractical to use for the reason that their capabilities will not be fully used.
