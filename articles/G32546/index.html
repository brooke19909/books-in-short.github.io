The best hotels of Crimea for visitors

For a long time, holidays in the Crimea have attracted not only compatriots, but also guests from near and far abroad. This is not surprising, because the cleanest sea, fresh mountain air, tempting landscapes are the best that you can think of for a perfect holiday. In addition, visitors are waiting for the best hotels in Crimea, designed for vacationers with different financial capabilities. Comfortable rooms, friendly staff, convenient location - all this puts them on a par with hotels of elite resorts. And, of course, among the whole variety of hotels, one can single out the best of the best, which will make your vacation unforgettable.

Budget vacation option





More budget, but no less comfortable - hotel "Aquamarine". This hotel is the pride of the western coast of Crimea. It is located in the beach zone "Omega", takes a leading place in the list of "Crimea hotels" all inclusive "" and holds a leading position for a long time. Aquamarine has 126 rooms of various categories. The main feature of the hotel is that it has rooms for visitors with physical disabilities. There is everything to enjoy your vacation to the fullest. Restaurants, bars, phyto-bars, water park, fitness center, playgrounds. In addition, the hotel has a wide range of services: a medical center, dry cleaning, laundry, shops, currency exchange, taxi. For those with a limited vacation budget, this establishment is perfect.

The most popular hotel of Feodosia





The list "Best Hotels of Crimea" can safely replenish the hotel "Scarlet Sails", located on the seafront in Feodosia. More than 50 comfortable rooms of the highest category, made in the classical style, will allow you to have a great time with the whole family. And spacious lobbies, business centers, conference rooms will be an ideal place for business negotiations and meetings with partners. The complex has bars, restaurants, clubs for lovers of card games, elite drinks and cigars, a children's room, heated pools and a private beach located in close proximity to the hotel. Feodosia is a fairly popular Crimean resort, so it is quite difficult to book places in the summer at this hotel. This issue needs to be addressed in advance.

Four star hotel of Crimea

Another attraction of the western coast of Crimea is the Ukraine Palace Hotel. This hotel falls into the category "Best Hotels of Crimea (four stars)". The hotel rooms are designed in the style of modern classics, equipped with the latest technology to make every guest feel at home. On the territory of Ukraine Palace there are three swimming pools, a children's playground, where professional animators, bars, restaurants are engaged with the kids. And for lovers of romance, a beautiful panorama of the resort town opens from the attic of the hotel. The beach is 800 meters away. But do not be upset, because this is an ideal opportunity to enjoy the surrounding attractions. The rooms are simply amazing, they are always clean, comfortable. Each vacationer can completely relax from their workdays. The presence of air conditioners will allow you to set the optimum air temperature in the room.
