When and where to go in Greece

Even from school, we heard about Greece - the cradle of European civilization. Athens, Sparta, Delphi, Mount Olympus, Corinth and Crete - all these names attract an exciting journey back centuries. But not only historical sights does this country give to guests. Amazing nature, hot climate, beaches and the sea, developed tourist infrastructure - all this can be found in any local resort. Therefore, the question of where to go in Greece, need to be approached, considering each specific situation. When are you going to visit this country? What does the concept of "rest" mean for you - excursions, the beach, shopping, incendiary parties?



Where to go in Greece in winter

Despite the reputation of a hot country, there are still ski resorts in Greece. All of them concentrated in the northern, mainland part. The most famous are Kaymaktsalan, located in the highest point of the state (2480 m), Kalavrita on Mount Helmos and many smaller resorts in the Greek province of Macedonia. Where to go in Greece to heal





Health resorts are very numerous in this country. A health resort of national importance is Loutraki, almost on the coast of the Gulf of Corinth. Fresh sea air and healing mineral waters make real miracles. According to legend, even the gods descended from Olympus to drink water from Lutraki springs. Now there has grown a real wellness center where, under the supervision of qualified specialists, you can undergo a course of treatment and simply increase the overall tone of the body. SPA-procedures, therapeutic exercises, massage relieve many health problems and give an amazing effect.

Where to go in Greece with young children

If you are going on a summer trip, it is better to choose the island as your vacation destination . Zakynthos, Rhodes, Corfu, Santorini or Crete - the most favorable conditions will be created everywhere for you and for the little traveler. Fresh sea breezes will soften the heat that mainland Greece is exposed to in July. Where to go with baby? Almost every island resort has a water park with slides or a dolphinarium. And if the child is already old enough to become interested in the myths and real history of ancient Greece, you can take him on a tour of the ancient cities. By the way, local hotels provide all services so that parents can enjoy each other's travels or company, having handed over the child to the care of a qualified nanny.





Where to go to Greece youth

Vivid impressions, outdoor activities, entertainment and reasonable prices - all this restless youth will find on the island of Crete. In addition to the stunning cultural attractions that have been preserved since the Crito-Mycenaean civilization, you will find wide sandy beaches and a warm sea, as well as an adventurous nightlife of seaside resorts. A favorite place for young people is also the island of Mykonos in the Aegean Sea. Discos, water parks, night clubs and outdoor activities on the beach, conditions for diving - everyone will find their own relaxation here.
