Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 *: hotel reviews

One of the most popular places for tourists is the Turkish city of Kemer. Here you can count on living in a comfortable hotel, a rich beach and sightseeing vacation. One of the pretty good options for the traveler is the hotel Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 *. So what is this place and for what type of vacation is it suitable?

Where is the hotel situated? Description of infrastructure





The picturesque small hotel Mira Golden Resort Hotel 4 * is located almost in the center of Kemer, not far from the sea coast. This is a cozy place where you can relax, enjoying the beauty of nature and views of the mighty mountains. The distance to the nearest international airport, which is located in Antalya, is about 45 km, but the hotel, as well as travel agencies, as a rule, provides its customers with a convenient transfer. By the way, the center of Antalya with all its attractions and entertainment for tourists is 55 km away.

The hotel itself is small, but very comfortable. The territory here is generously decorated with greenery, so walking along it is a pleasure. In the center is a large four-story building. In the courtyard there is a swimming pool, sports fields, places for relaxation and much more.

Hotel complex Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): what do the rooms look like?





This hotel complex by the standards of Turkey is considered quite small - there are 67 rooms. For the most part these are standard rooms, there are several connected apartments that are perfect for families and large companies.

All rooms are bright, with large windows, spacious enough for a comfortable stay. The rooms are decorated in a simple style with comfortable furniture. Of course, the guests are provided with the necessary equipment, including air conditioning, a TV (you can watch some Russian channels), a telephone, a safe (you can use it for a small fee), a minibar with cooling (empty, filled on request).

The bathroom is tiled and equipped with modern fixtures. There is a mirror, sink, bidet, hair dryer. Of course, guests receive clean towels, which are changed every day.

Food for hotel guests





For many tourists, the issue of nutrition is important. What does the Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 * offer? Guest reviews suggest that there should be no problems with food. Like most Turkish hotels, this one offers an all-inclusive system, which is very convenient, as it includes good nutrition, as well as drinks and snacks throughout the day.

Three meals a day are served at the hotel restaurant. The choice of food is quite large, they serve traditional international dishes, as well as masterpieces of Egyptian cuisine and sweet oriental pastries. There are always plenty of vegetable salads and fresh fruits. From time to time, guests are offered fish and seafood dishes. In hotel bars, residents can also get light snacks, order chilled drinks, exotic cocktails or alcohol.

Naturally, on the beach, and in the city itself, there are other catering establishments where you can eat, chat with new people and have a good time.

Where is the beach? Are there any conditions for water recreation?





As already mentioned, the hotel complex Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 * is located near the sea, on the second beach line. The hotel has its own beach, but it is at some distance, namely, at 1700 m. Of course, some people prefer to walk to the sea on foot. But most tourists do not like this prospect. That is why during the day a large comfortable bus runs from the hotel to the beach and back.

The beach is clean, well maintained. Sunset in the sea is gradual, covered with soft sand - both children and adults can swim here. On the shore you will find comfortable sun loungers with mattresses and pillows, hammocks, umbrellas. There are locker rooms. A beach towel can be obtained free of charge, but it is issued on bail, which is then returned. It also has a spacious beach volleyball court.

Besides bathing and sunbathing, there are other ways to have fun. In particular, tourists can go boating, catamarans, water skiing, windsurfing or sailing. Reviews indicate that the beach here is really good.

Vacation with a child: are there any conditions?





Many parents are interested in the question of whether to take a child with them to the Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 *. Kemer is a great place for young tourists, and the hotel provides some facilities. Immediately it is worth noting that they do not put extra beds in the rooms, but almost all rooms have comfortable soft sofas that are perfect for a child of any age. For the youngest guests in restaurants there are high chairs. You can rent a stroller. The menu always contains delicious, dietary dishes, as well as pastries that all children adore.

Hotel Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 * offers young travelers and the opportunity to have fun. During the day, they can splash around in the pool or have fun in the open air with a swing. A mini-club is constantly working, where kids can meet new friends, play, draw, take part in various team games and other events.

Hotel Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 * (Kemer): list of additional services

Despite its relatively modest size, the hotel offers its guests the necessary services. For example, clothes can be taken to the laundry if necessary. On the territory there is a large parking with a security system. By the way, in the city you can easily rent a car, if you have documents, of course.

You can also exchange currency at the hotel, and the rate is pretty good. For a small fee, you can use the Internet access almost throughout the hotel. A hairdresser is also constantly working, where you can "bring beauty" for a small fee. If there are health problems, a professional doctor is quickly called to the guest.

Leisure of tourists: how to have fun at the hotel?





The hotel has the opportunity to have fun. In the courtyard there is a rather large swimming pool with clean fresh water. Guests bathe, relax on comfortable sun loungers, communicate with each other, in a word, have a good time. In the pool periodically held classes in water aerobics, and next to it - morning group gymnastics. And from time to time, fun games of water polo are organized.

You can relax and improve your well-being in the spa. Here guests are offered to visit the Turkish bath, relax in the sauna and jacuzzi. The hotel has several professional massage therapists.

Tourists have the opportunity to play futsal, have fun playing a game of table tennis or have a good time with friends playing a game of billiards.

Animators who work here constantly entertain guests throughout the day. In the evenings, everyone is invited to interesting performances and exciting shows. Evenings of live music are held where you can dance in a relaxed atmosphere. There are no discotheques and nightclubs in the hotel, but fans of nightlife can visit one of the city's venues.

This is not all that Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 * has to offer. Turkey is a country with an incredibly rich history, interesting architecture, and deep culture. This place is ideal for a rich excursion vacation. The hotel will certainly help you pick up and buy a guided tour that will bring a lot of pleasure.

Hotel Traveler Reviews





A lot of relevant information can be obtained by talking with people who have already managed to visit this hotel. Reviews about Mira Garden Resort Hotel 4 * are mostly positive. There really is nothing to complain about, because the rooms are clean and spacious, household appliances work, there is constant water in the shower, and indeed they are cleaned according to a schedule, every day.

The food is also at a good level, there is always something to choose from. Despite the fact that some travelers complain about the monotony, the food is always fresh and no one remains hungry. The disadvantages include the remoteness of the beaches, although this problem can be solved if you use a bus or rented transport. The sea is beautiful and clean, it's nice to spend time on the beach.

The animation at the hotel is unobtrusive, but you can have a good time with the guys if you wish. The employees here are polite and decent, they speak Russian, so there will be no special problems with communication. The hotel is more suitable for families with children, as well as fans of a relaxing holiday. But for young people who prefer noisy attractions and nightclubs, it can be a little boring here.
