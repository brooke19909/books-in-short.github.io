How to make a frog out of plastic bottles in various ways?





This material describes how to make a frog out of plastic bottles on your own using a common set of tools. Two methods for creating such crafts are given. One of them is to create a magnificent casket for a modern fashionista. And the second is a figurine for your personal plot or playground. As in the first case, and in the second, the procedure is very simple and a novice master can even cope with them without problems. Which option to make a frog out of plastic bottles to choose is a personal matter for everyone. But if you need a simple and reliable box, then the first option has no alternative. But if it is necessary to create a special decoration, the second option is preferable.

Excellent box - pencil case

Want to make your child special and unique? Then, how to make a frog out of plastic bottles in the form of a pencil case or casket is exactly what you need. Such a thing will be a special attribute with which your child will be

stand out from the rest of the children. To create such a fake, we will need 2 containers. The recommended volume is 1 liter. With a smaller size, it will be inconvenient to create this small masterpiece. But containers with a larger size will lead to the fact that it will no longer be a pencil case, but a roomy box. We cut off these containers at a distance of 5-7 centimeters from the lower edge, previously marking with a marker. One of the parts received will be the bottom, and the second - the top. You need to make a choice and decide which part will perform which function. Immediately put the bottom of the pencil case and then install the second component on top of it. The teeth in this case should clearly coincide opposite each other. Make a horizontal mark at the beginning and end of the lightning with a marker. A zipper is laid along the inner perimeter of each part and sewn with fishing line or a strong thread. Top tape is glued to the seams on each side. All this is then opened with green paint. After the paint dries with the help of a marker or felt-tip pens, the necessary decorative design is performed. That's all. Now you know how to make a frog out of plastic bottles in the form of a pencil case or casket, the created craft can be used as intended.

Elegant decoration





The second option for creating such a craft is figurines, which are most often seen on a playground or a personal plot. Now let's figure out how the frog princess is made from plastic bottles. The procedure for creating it is similar. Take 2 containers with a volume of 1 or 2 liters. Cut at a distance of 5-7 cm from the bottom. They are applied to each other strictly symmetrically and are connected using adhesive tape. All this is painted in the same green color. While the frog dries, of the three containers of 0.5 liters, it is necessary to make paws and a crown. The first are obtained by cutting the conical part of each bottle. They are painted in all the same color. The crown is obtained from the cylindrical part by cutting the bottom and cone. A saw is cut from one side, which makes it look like such a symbol of power. It is opened already in yellow, and then with the use of adhesive tape it is fixed on top, and legs are set along the edges and fastened in the same way. A set of felt-tip pens or markers decorates the figurine (eyes, mouth). At the final stage, two pairs of paws are cut from the residues. They are also painted green. One pair is placed at the base of the body, and the second continues the legs. They connect with the use of adhesive tape.

Crafts from bottles are becoming more and more popular now. A frog is one of the most common and simplest options. It is simple and affordable. To create such an amphibian is possible even for a novice master.
