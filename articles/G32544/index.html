Terminal Assignment

Answering the question of how to send money to the card through the terminal, you must first understand what this device is. Advantage of using

Putting money on a bank card through the terminal is not difficult. The device is equipped with an intuitive interface. If we consider ATMs, then most of them are equipped with physical buttons. This is necessary in order to register all user actions in order to prevent a possible risk of theft of money from a stolen card.

After making cash, the device sends information to the server through a wireless data channel, where a payment order is generated. All actions take a few seconds. The participation of people in this process is minimal; therefore, the written algorithm is responsible for the uninterrupted operation and speed of money transfer.

In addition, a convenient menu with a large selection of options allows you to transfer funds to various card formats and bank categories. Typically, funds are received within 15 minutes after sending data to the server.

Account replenishment procedure

Now you need to figure out how to put money on the card through the terminal. It is important to make out each step. The instruction is universal for almost all types of ATMs.

The algorithm for crediting funds to the balance is simple:

  1. Enter the main menu of the terminal. It is worth noting that if an ATM is used, it will be necessary to insert a card to which funds will be sent and enter a PIN code from it.
  2. In the menu, select "Top-up" or "Top-up a bank card."
  3. Next, you will need to enter the card details. To do this, enter its number, consisting of 16 digits on the front side, the validity period and the name of the holder, if necessary. If an ATM is used for replenishment, then after entering a PIN code, data on the card holder and account number appear automatically.
  4. After entering all the necessary data, you need to click "Cash deposit".
  5. Usually in terminals or ATMs, the bill acceptor is located on the right side. It can be either vertical or horizontal type. It is necessary to carefully deposit paper money into it in one banknote.
  6. Next, the system will recount the deposited money and issue the amount on the screen. At the end, you will need to confirm the operation.

It is worth noting that the terminals and ATMs use the same conditions for crediting cash.
