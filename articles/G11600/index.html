Family trip

Rest in St. Petersburg with children involves the preparation of a special excursion route. A visit to the Leningrad Zoo will surely appeal to young travelers. Here you can admire predatory animals and primates, rodents and ungulates, as well as many other representatives of the fauna. Often horse shows are organized for visitors. It is possible to ride children on a pony. The zoo is open on weekdays from ten in the morning to six in the evening, and on weekends from eleven to five. It is located near the metro station called Gorkovskaya. The cost of tickets is low: they will take 350 rubles from adults, 100 from schoolchildren, 50 from children from four to seven, and children under this age are entitled to free admission. The zoo has its own website where you can find out even more information about this interesting place.

Green islands of the metropolis

You can penetrate the charms of nature even in such a large and noisy city as St. Petersburg. One of the places suitable for this is Moscow Victory Park. It is popular for its picturesque grounds and beautiful ponds. The park is just perfect for a leisurely family vacation. If you want to have fun, you can ride the rides. Another wonderful place is the Alexander Garden. It was defeated in the nineteenth century. Health promotion

Residents of metropolitan areas exposed to constant stress, just need to play sports. Fatigue from the daily hustle and bustle is much more effectively removed, for example, by jogging or swimming, rather than lying on the couch. At the same time, not only Petersburgers themselves, but also city guests increasingly prefer to spend their free time not in bars and restaurants, but on horseback. In the sports club "Prometheus" everyone can ride a trotter at their pleasure. The program provides for horse trips in wooded or open areas. Such a vacation in St. Petersburg is becoming increasingly popular. Reviews of guests of the Prometheus club about such pastime are mostly enthusiastic. People note that communication with animals and unity with nature cleanse the soul, relieve stress and give strength.

To get to this club, you need to get to the Leninsky Prospekt metro station, after which you transfer to the 639th minibus. She will take you to the turn to Kaporskoye. You will need to walk fifteen minutes to your destination. The club works every day, but for complete confidence that they are waiting for you, it is recommended that you first contact the manager of Prometheus. You can get all the necessary information by calling 8-901-307-55-97.

Recreation facilities in St. Petersburg

In the suburbs of the Northern capital there are many places to relax in the lap of nature. One of the most popular recreation centers is the "Old Mill". It is located on the shores of the famous Gulf of Finland. These places attract tourists with intoxicating fresh air and spectacular views.

Tourists are accommodated in furnished cottages with air conditioning, TV, refrigerator and all amenities. The capacity of the restaurant "Cheremsha" is one hundred and fifty people. Customers enjoy using the sauna, swimming pool, Turkish or Russian baths. In addition, it is possible to ride horses, bicycles or ATVs, even shoot from a real crossbow. The institution is located on the forty-first kilometer of Primorsky highway.
