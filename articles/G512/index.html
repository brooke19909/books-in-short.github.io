Front-mounted controls





The front panel contains keys for controlling the phone. They decided to cover them with a plastic cover that slides down for added protection. By the way, after you open it, you can fix it in two different positions. In the first of them, the user will have access exclusively to the function keys. In the second, absolutely all buttons will be open for use.

Back side





On the back of the Nokia 8110, the review of which we are bringing to your attention, you can notice two miniature tracks. This is actually nothing more than contacts that are routed directly to the speaker. For some reason, their designers did not cover anything. If we assume only theoretically, then the conclusion suggests that these components can be damaged by physical or mechanical stress. Nevertheless, many phone tests ended without such excesses. The cover can be moved relative to the housing. At the same time, it will move along the components that have been nicknamed by users "rails". Here one more oversight of designers immediately comes to light. When dirt gets into the "rails", the movement of the lid along them becomes significantly heavier. Nevertheless, such a solution will be more practical than the opening lid, which was universally used in the entire product line from Ericsson. The fact is that the mechanism does not become so loose over time that it provides a lower percentage of failures of such a system.

Microphone location





This element is located directly inside the cover, in its lower part. To protect it from mechanical influences of an external nature, the designers added a special plastic plate to the design. Interestingly, even with this approach, audibility remains at a high level. The shape of the device allows you to negotiate conveniently and comfortably without moving the phone during the call process. The effect of the barrel, by the way, is completely absent in the device. And for this, we should thank the developers of the phone, who provided for the possibility of the corresponding problem and timely work to eliminate this drawback.
