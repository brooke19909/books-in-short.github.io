Types of rooms in hotels: choose a vacation to taste

Vacation is a wonderful part of every person‚Äôs life. With impatience, each employee expects a treasured date when it will be possible not to go to work, but instead get on a plane and wave to hell.

Today, tour operators offer, as a rule, two main types of recreation: sightseeing and beach. Going on a tour of the first program, you may not think much about the hotel, but rather focus on getting positive emotions from sightseeing. But in the case of a beach holiday, you choose his choice with special attention. Everyone knows that hotels are classified by the number of stars, however, each room has different types of rooms.

Rooms for every taste

To meet the requirements of any category of vacationers, hotels offer various types of rooms. In hotels you can rent a standard (standart), superior room (superior), deluxe (de luxe), studio or apartment, family (family room), suite (suit), honeymoon room (honeymoon room), presidential (president room) , two-story or maisonette (duplex, maisonette). Obviously, their cost will be different.

Standart

As a rule, this is the most inexpensive hotel room with the smallest quadrature and modest decor. It consists of one room, bathroom and toilet. There are 1-bed and 2-bed standard rooms, but in some hotels it is possible to put an extra bed -

extra bed for triple occupancy.

Superior

Improved types of rooms in hotels - this is superior: more than a standard room, sometimes closer to the sea (if we are talking about club hotels or hotels of a combined type, when there is a main building, a high-rise building, and separate bungalows for several families). Otherwise, there are essentially no differences.

Family apartments

Family types of rooms in hotels in Turkey are of two types: family room and family studio. The first option is a one-room apartment, larger than a standard room. They can accommodate up to 4 people (usually). A family studio consists of two rooms and accommodates more guests than a regular family.

Suites

The category of suit rooms can also be of different types: ordinary suite, junior suite, executive suite, suite senior. These types of rooms in hotels in Greece, as well as in any other country, differ from other categories in their quadrature (they are larger and usually consist of a living room and bedroom), can have two bathrooms, stand out with their original design (this most likely refers to executive suite), and the furniture is expensive. It is also worth paying attention to their location in the case: as a rule, suites have a good view from the window.

Presidential Apartment

These are the most expensive hotel room types:

located in the most prestigious place (for example, almost under the roof), with a huge quadrature, several bedrooms and living rooms and more than one bathroom. The president room is furnished with high quality furniture. Often, guests choosing these types of hotel rooms are given certain privileges that brighten up their holidays: free use of the minibar, infrastructure, which is paid for other vacationers, etc. Needless to say, the level of service in such apartments is simply unbelievable?

Obviously, with such a luxurious assortment of rooms, each tourist can choose for himself exactly the type that will satisfy all his requirements and wishes.
