Sugar Marina Art 4 * (Thailand / Phuket about): description, photos and reviews

Sugar Marina Art 4 * is a unique resort complex, which can be considered not just a hotel, but also a real art object. The original decor on the territory and in the interior makes your stay not only comfortable, but also incredibly beautiful.





a brief description of

If you not only appreciate the quality service, but also love the original surroundings, then Sugar Marina Art 4 * will be an ideal place for your relaxation. This is a unique hotel whose concept is more like a museum of modern art. On the territory you can admire the original installations, and picturesque paintings are hung on the walls of the halls. And, of course, each of the hotel rooms has a unique zest from the designers.

Sugar Marina Art 4 * Resort was opened in 2007. Until August 2013, it was called Sugar Palm Karon, after which it was rebranded. And by 2014, the restoration was completed, as a result of which the hotel acquired its current appearance. Guests are accommodated in 2 modern buildings.





Location

Sugar Marina Art 4 * Resort is literally a 5-minute walk from the popular Karon Beach and a 10-minute drive from Patong Coast. It takes less than an hour to get to Phuket International Airport.

We can talk about a rather favorable location of Sugar Marina Art 4 *. Thailand, Phuket, Karon, Patak Road, 542 - The address of this institution. Resting here, be sure to visit the following tourist sites:

  • Suwan Khiri Ket Temple (1 km);
  • night market (1 km);
  • Dino Park Golf Club (2 km);
  • Plaza Kata shopping center (3 km);
  • Simon Cabaret (3 km);
  • Chalong Temple (5 km).

Standard rooms

The standard rooms of the Sugar Marina Art Karon Beach 4 * hotel are characterized by a spacious area, a high level of comfort and an original interior. Accommodation is made on two single beds, which can be shifted if necessary. A plasma panel is installed opposite, and a workplace is equipped. There is a large wardrobe. Thanks to the panoramic window, which occupies almost the entire wall, a lot of sunlight penetrates the room, which in combination with white walls visually expands the space.

Deluxe

Deluxe gives guests Sugar Marina Art Karon Beach 4 * absolute comfort, plenty of light and living space, as well as aesthetic pleasure. The interior is made in classic black and white with bright accents of sky blue. Opposite the king size bed there is a soft sofa, which can serve as an extra bed. There is a workplace and a built-in wardrobe. In addition to decorative elements, the highlight of the room can also be considered that the bathroom is separated from the bedroom by a frosted glass wall. The spacious balcony overlooks the hotel's pool and flowering garden.





Suite

The suite is largely reminiscent of the previous category (in all that relates to the layout). Nevertheless, it has its own zest. The most important feature is that through a sliding glass door, guests enter the spacious terrace, which borders the pool. Another important point - a plasma TV, in addition to its main purpose, can also be used to access the Internet.

Apartment Amenities

Going on vacation to Sugar Marina Art Karon Beach 4 * (Phuket), you can be sure that you will be provided with comfortable living conditions. Homeliness is ensured by the following list of amenities in the rooms:

  • a plasma TV connected to a satellite dish, which broadcasts several Russian-language channels;
  • the bathroom is equipped with a shower, as well as a hairdryer, bath accessories and hygiene products;
  • An individual air conditioning system allows you to set a comfortable microclimate in the apartment (it works only for cooling);
  • a free minibar, which is daily filled with drinking water, beer, sodas and juices;
  • wireless Internet;
  • reliable safe with a combination lock (located in a wardrobe).




Hotel Infrastructure

You can enjoy a full rest in the Sugar Marina Art 4 * hotel (Phuket). Karon is a rather specific resort. Unfortunately, the tourism infrastructure is underdeveloped. That is why it is important for guests to take advantage of the maximum number of facilities at their hotel. Speaking about this institution, it is worth noting the following options:

  • a luxurious green garden with many walking paths;
  • a spacious terrace with sunbeds and parasols;
  • a buffet where guests can have a bite to eat or order a meal;
  • car parking, the use of which is included in the price;
  • the bar offers a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks;

  • a left-luggage office where you can leave your luggage waiting for check-in or after eviction;
  • the reception is open around the clock to advise guests and process applications;

  • Thai Restaurant




Entertainment

Entertainment is an integral part of any holiday. The Sugar Marina Resort Art Karon Beach 4 * hotel offers guests the following:

  • outdoor swimming pool;
  • a small sports hall, where the necessary equipment for strength and other types of training is collected;
  • If you want to get to know Phuket better, you should definitely use the services of a tour desk, where an exciting tour will be organized for you;

Facilities and service

Sugar Marina Resort Art Karon 4 * is guided by the highest international standards in service of guests. The following service is available to guests:

  • special towels are provided for use by the pool and beach;
  • room service;
  • assistance in booking and purchasing tickets;
  • your nanny can look after your children while you are on excursions;

  • Guests can use the cashless payment system to pay for the services received.

Additional Information

Before you go on vacation to the Sugar Marina Resort Art 4 * hotel, travelers should familiarize themselves with the basic rules of accommodation. So, the following points can be considered the most significant:

  • check-in and check-in is possible only after 14:00 (unless there are prepared rooms available);
  • to solve all organizational issues related to eviction, it is before noon (otherwise, the guest will be charged hourly for overtime in the room);
  • Children under 12 years are charged 600 baht when using existing beds;
  • an extra bed for a child is provided for 800 baht, and for an adult - for 1200 baht per night;
  • The hotel does not provide facilities for pets;
  • upon registration, the guest is required to present the credit card with which the prepayment for the reservation was made (otherwise the blocked funds will be returned and the full amount of the stay will be charged from the new card);
  • for registration and check-in, the guest must present an identity card with a photograph;
  • When specifying special requests regarding a room when booking, consider the fact that additional charges may apply for their implementation;
  • In order to minimize the waiting time for arrival, inform the property in advance of your estimated time of arrival;
  • newlyweds are provided with romantic rooms, as well as a bottle of champagne and fruits;
  • birthday cake is presented to the guest.

A lot of positive reviews are left by travelers about the Sugar Marina Art Karon 4 * hotel. Based on these comments, you can talk about the following advantages of this hotel:

  • the staff is polite, friendly and helpful;
  • after registration, without any additional requests, one of the hotel staff will help you bring things to your room;
  • in the presence of free rooms, guests are settled without delay, without waiting for the checkout time;

Negative reviews

A number of disadvantages are inherent in the Sugar Marina Art 4 * hotel. Reviews focus on the following points:

  • the pool is not deep enough for a comfortable swim;
  • bed linen is changed irregularly;
  • overpriced food prices;
  • construction is underway near the hotel, from which there is a lot of noise and dust;
  • the situation in the hotel is somewhat outdated, and therefore redecorating is required;
  • it takes about 10 minutes to get to the sea;
  • special requests that are indicated when booking are almost never fulfilled;
  • the key card often enough moves away from the slot, because of which the number is de-energized;
  • cleaning is not thorough enough (tips do not help to fix the situation);
  • air conditioning is very noisy;
  • the hotel area is too small;
  • the gym is a cramped stuffy room where several exercise machines are standing end to end;
  • there is a translucent glass wall between the bedroom and the bathroom, which not everyone will like (besides it is all in drips and stains);
  • problems with hot water supply (slightly warm water is pouring from the tap);
  • the pool smells too much of bleach;
  • Wireless Internet in the rooms practically does not work, and in the lobby it can be used only when there are few people;
  • from moisture in the bathroom mold appeared;
  • the hotel has no Russian-speaking staff (and they do not speak English very well);
  • the windows of the first floor are located directly opposite the terrace with sunbeds, and therefore you have to close them with curtains so as not to be in sight of everyone;
  • the breakfast menu is repeated every day;
  • there is no lock or hook on the door to the bathroom (that is, it does not close);
  • there are few lighting devices in the room (evening reading will be very difficult);
  • there is practically no entertainment for children (with the exception of one slide in the yard);
  • old leaking plumbing;
  • upon arrival, carefully check the serviceability of the room equipment, otherwise during the eviction you may be presented with a breakdown and require monetary compensation.

General impression

In order to objectively evaluate the quality of services at the hotel, you should know the specifics of such a tourist destination as Thailand. This country is not for hotel holidays. Here you need to enjoy beautiful beaches and rich excursions. For an active tourist, this accommodation option will be just perfect. Despite some shortcomings (scuffed walls, old plumbing), you will be satisfied with the rest. Of particular interest are art objects on a well-groomed green area, as well as interesting design elements in the rooms.

If you nevertheless scrupulously relate to the living conditions, then pay attention to the fact that the hotel has 2 buildings. The rooms in the new building are more spacious, and the repairs in them are more recent. In addition, it is worth paying attention to the presence of a balcony and a view from the window. If you do not want to contemplate the wall of a neighboring hotel, then book a room with a view of the pool (or do not spare money for a surcharge for relocation). As for nutrition, limit yourself to breakfast. Cheaper and tasty food can be found in the many cafes and restaurants in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.
