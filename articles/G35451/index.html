Penguin family



Take eggplant for example. They can make original compositions from vegetables under the name ‚ÄúPenguin Family‚Äù. What is needed for this: 5-6 blue different sizes and intense dark color, without spots and damage, always with green tails. Several large cauliflower inflorescences or a couple of ordinary white cabbage heads. We begin the preparation of our vegetable composition with eggplant processing. Point the ends of the tails at them, these will be the beaks. With a knife, carefully make incisions in them where you can insert small dark buttons-eyes. So that the eggplant composition made of vegetables, made with your own hands, does not darken the pulp without the peel, wipe it with vinegar. Cute sheep

Let's start with the main element of the craft of vegetables (photo attached) - lambs or lambs, as you like. At the cabbage, cut exactly the cuttings, which will play the role of legs. Sheep must graze in the meadow! For mushrooms, cut the legs closer to the hats so that they look like sheep's muzzles. With the inner side forward, attach the champignons to the heads of cabbage using matches that can mimic the eyes. And curly inflorescences will completely pass for curls of sheep's wool. Using the same matches or toothpicks, fasten each lamb on the foam. Now make holes on the sheet where you insert the branches of greenery in groups. So you have a wonderful meadow - a real Swiss idyll!

Cucumber Wahns and Sharks



Thinking over various crafts, compositions from vegetables, use associative connections. With whom, for example, do you associate a cucumber? Of course, with green frogs and crocodiles. And even with sharks! Cute little animals will cause a storm of delight among your young children, and even if they have problems with appetite, they will agree to eat an unloved vegetable without long persuasion. Where do we start? Place the cabbage leaf on a plate. Take some small pickles. Cut each crosswise into 2 halves: one large, the other smaller. Divide the smaller one in half, trim by making the paws. At the larger end, make a small slit-like incision - the mouth. To make your cucumber quack really resemble the real one, make eyes out of blackcurrant berries. With matchsticks, fix the paws and eyes in the appropriate places and place the frog on the cabbage leaf. And get down to the next. Just remember to take out the matches when the child eats vegetables!

Edible bouquet



If your child is lucky to be born in the fall, you will find useful compositions from vegetables, made by yourself with your own hands for kindergarten, especially in combination with a variety of fruits. Children will eat these ‚Äúbouquets‚Äù with pleasure as a treat from the birthday person. True, they need to be cooked immediately before serving. You will need: thin wooden skewers, tomatoes and cucumbers, apples, plums, oranges, large grapes, watermelon, melon, pineapple. Wash all products well. From melon, watermelon, pineapple, cut pieces of medium-sized pulp. Cut apples into slices. Peel the oranges, also divide into slices. Remove grapes from brushes. Cut cucumbers and tomatoes in large circles. Place the foam sheets on the trays. On plates - flowers, cucumbers with tomatoes, garnish with drops of mayonnaise. String the fruit platter in random order on the skewers, and then stick them into the foam. Place trays with fruit and vegetable compositions on the tables during lunch for children. And congratulate with them the hero of the occasion!

Potato crafts



Speaking about vegetable crafts, listing various source materials, we undeservedly ignored potatoes. And, strangely enough, from it, dear, you can also create a lot of interesting things. For example, make fabulous piglets. Wash medium-sized potatoes and boil in their skins until cooked. Take out, let cool. Boil a few carrots. Peel the vegetables. Cut the carrot into "wheels" a couple of centimeters thick. It will be a patch of stigmas. Stick them with matches to potatoes. Insert into each tuber 4 matches-legs, set on plates. Arrange between them cucumber glades and invite the children to have dinner!
