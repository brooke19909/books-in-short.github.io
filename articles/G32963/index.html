Beaches Ordzhonikidze, Crimea: photos and reviews

The Crimean village of Ordzhonikidze is washed from three sides by the sea, so the choice of beaches here is great. In Ordzhonikidze there are sandy, and pebble, and rocky, and wild beaches. Village Ordzhonikidze

Ordzhonikidze is a small urban-type settlement with a population of about 5 thousand people. It has become a resort area relatively recently. The location of Ordzhonikidze is unique in that one part is surrounded by small mountains, and the second is located on a gentle plain. Feodosia is located just 14 kilometers from the village.

Due to the fact that Ordzhonikidze is not crowded, and there is very little transport on the streets, rest here attracts tourists with calm and measured. Summer on the Crimean peninsula is always sultry, and the water is warm.

Central beach

The most important and developed beach is Ordzhonikidze Central Beach. It is located near the city center and is considered the most ‚Äúparty‚Äù place. The beach stretches for a distance of 1 km. The sandy part of the beach is about 300 m, and the rest is concrete. The concrete section has a steep descent to the sea, above it is a mini promenade.





The sandy section of the Central Beach is equipped with restaurants, cafes, bars and various kinds of entertainment for adults and children. The beach is equipped with changing rooms, toilets, vacationers can rent an umbrella or deck chair. The sand on the beach is large and golden, the bottom is sandy and clean, but large stones can come across at a distance of 10 m from the shore.

Beaches of the Two-Anchor Bay

The beaches of Ordzhonikidze (Crimea), which are located in the Two-anchor bay, are located in the easternmost point of the village. There are pebble and sandy beaches, which can be reached by car.

Wild beaches stretched along the coast of Ordzhonikidze. They are located in secluded bays, therefore they are hidden from prying eyes. On some beaches, the water is cloudy, as healing volcanic clay comes out on it. The wild beaches of Ordzhonikidze are not crowded, they are randomly scattered around the village. The most famous among them is the beach "Krasnyachka". It is a stone and pebble beach with a rocky bottom. Most diving enthusiasts come here.





Another famous beach of Dvuyakornaya bay is called "Under the Saddle". Here is a local attraction - a rock, which in its shape resembles a saddle. The beach is directly below it. The coast is strewn with large pebbles and stones. Here lovers can swim and dive with a mask. If you go around the rock, then next to the bay there is another small wild and cozy beach.

On the east coast there are Katran and Start boathouses, where tourists are offered a comfortable but not cheap vacation. Boathouses have their own clean and well-equipped beaches. The downside is the lack of entertainment and walks, for which you have to get to the village.

Provato Bay beaches

The most famous among them is Agate Beach. It is located under the mountain Vasyukovka on the shore of the Koktebel Bay. Scenic views open from the coast, water strikes vacationers with transparency and purity. The beach is named after the agate stones that can be found along the coastline.

The seabed on the Agate beach is rocky, and the coast is pebble-rocky. The descent to the beach is very steep, so there is a ladder specially for the descent and ascent. There is a cafe near the start of the descent, and you can rent sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach. This place is famous for the underwater ridge of reefs, which is located immediately at the entrance to the sea.

In the bay of Provato there are also not very cozy narrow beaches with large pebbles. They are called Factory. Despite the fact that access to them is difficult, a small number of holidaymakers can be seen here.

Other beaches

In the west of the village of Ordzhonikidze, between small toes there are small beaches visited. They are famous enough, and many seek here to retire in private and snorkel. These beaches do not have names, they are simply given numbers. There is no access to them. You can get to the beaches along the paths or along the coastal strip. Due to the privacy and secrecy from the eyes, these beaches were chosen by lovers of sunbathing naked. Here you can also meet campers.





The most romantic are the beaches of Ordzhonikidze, which are located in Goat Bay. They get here only by water, so vacationers on the beach can be rarely seen. Those who wish to retire are taken here by boat, and then taken away. Romantic couples often stay here overnight, taking a tent with them. The beach is covered by rocks on three sides, the beach is pebble, and the water is crystal clear.

Reviews

More and more people choose the beaches of Ordzhonikidze for holidays in Russia. Reviews about them are found only the most positive. In their comments, tourists leave the following information:

  • Ordzhonikidze has a very large selection of beaches, there are pebbles, sand and stones.
  • Sea water is very clean.
  • The beaches of Ordzhonikidze are a great choice for scuba diving.
  • The beaches are quiet and calm, there is an atmosphere of comfort and relaxation, in contrast to the noisy beaches of famous resorts.
  • The beaches are in beautiful and picturesque places.

Beaches in the Crimean village of Ordzhonikidze are a good option for a vacation with the whole family.
