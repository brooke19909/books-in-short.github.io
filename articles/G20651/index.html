Mortgage insurance at VTB 24

Mortgage insurance is a fairly simplified procedure. The first thing you need to do is contact the nearest branch of the insurance company and write a statement. It must include personal information, data on the mortgage apartment, the loan term and the value of the purchased property. An employee of the company will accept the application, on the basis of which he will calculate and announce the necessary list of documents.





The price of the insurance contract is determined individually for each client and in most cases does not exceed 1.5% of the value of the collateral.
