Divnomorskoe: pensions and places to relax

One of the most popular resorts in the Krasnodar Territory is deservedly considered the village of Divnomorskoye. Pensions, motels and rest houses of this "paradise" place are visited annually by tens of thousands of tourists from all over Russia.

Village Divnomorskoe





Why are these places so attractive for tourists? The undoubted advantage of the village is that it is located away from highways, railways and seaports. Coniferous forests surround it on all sides, and in the south begins the forest of the Pitsunda pine listed in the Red Book of Russia . That is why there is always clean and fresh air with a healing effect. Due to the fact that the resort village is located on the shores of the open sea, the water here is much cleaner than in other places. The steep cliffs that cover the coast from prying eyes attract here nudists who have long been fond of these places for a "wild" vacation in Divnomorskoe. Pensions, created for people who prefer comfort and coziness, offer all the conditions to spend a pleasant time on vacation. Some of them carry out spa treatment for a moderate fee.

Pension "Torch"





Pension "Fakel" (Divnomorskoe) is designed for a simultaneous stay of 360 people. For this, seven modern three-story residential buildings were built on its territory, in which single, double and triple rooms of various categories are located. From standard with conveniences on the floor to suites with split systems and balconies with sea views. Football and volleyball courts, tennis courts, billiards, bowling and an outdoor pool are for lovers of a healthy lifestyle. Those people who need to carry out prophylaxis for their body can count on a modern medical complex with doctors of various specialties. A well-maintained pebble beach is literally 50 meters from the sleeping quarters. Here the usual standard of services: umbrellas, deck chairs, changing rooms and showers. It is possible to rent water rides. For those who like to sit with a fishing rod in the shade of coniferous trees and enjoy the clean mountain air, there is the opportunity to fish in an artificial pond on the territory of the sanatorium.

Pension "Flamingo"





If you decide to relax with your children, then you should pay attention to the boarding house "Flamingo" (Divnomorskoe). It is located in the depths of the village, away from the noisy promenade and loud night discos. A 20-minute walk from the emerald sea. The rooms are designed for living with all the amenities of the standard, junior and deluxe categories. The price includes three meals a day, as well as the diagnosis and treatment of various diseases. The boarding house has a modern sports complex with pools, playgrounds for games, gyms and fitness rooms. For children, animators work, who organize a holiday and diversify their holidays in Divnomorskoe. Pensions with such a level of comfort and coziness usually do not have vacant seats in the summer season. Therefore, the purchase of a ticket to the sanatorium must be taken care of earlier.

Things to do in Divnomorskoye





The Divnomorskie boarding houses are largely intended for families with children. Hence, most of the entertainment for children on the embankment: swings and slides, trampolines and shooting at the shooting range, playgrounds and even a mini-water park. Adults can relax by visiting the Kakadu nightclub or the Rus entertainment complex, which are located in the center of the resort village of Divnomorskoye. Pensions and motels offer their customers various excursion routes. This is a boat trip on a yacht. And horseback riding in the surrounding forests. Visiting waterfalls, dolmens and mud volcanoes. Tasting and acquaintance with the production of Kuban wines in the village of Abrau - Durso. A trip to the Gelendzhik water park "Golden Bay" and the amusement park "Olympus". We can confidently say that every vacationer will receive a lot of positive emotions and pleasant experiences.

How to get to Divnomorskoye

The village of Divnomorskoye does not have a railway station, much less an airport. By plane you can fly to Anapa or Gelendzhik. Take the train to Novorossiysk. From there, take buses, shuttles or private taxis along the Gelendzhik-Divnomorskoe road. Pensions and motels sometimes include a transfer (meeting and escorting a tourist) in the price of the vouchers, or provide such a service for a fee. In any case, it will be convenient for those who first went on vacation to Divnomorskoe.
