All details about Nokia Asha 305

Nokia has long been one of the leading market players in the mobile phone market. She never ceases to amaze customers with more and more new models of communication devices. Nokia mobile phones differ in their price category, so any buyer can choose for themselves a high-quality and inexpensive communicator.

One of these budget solutions is the Nokia Asha 305, which houses a TFT touch screen displaying 65 thousand colors, a 2 megapixel camera, a 3 ‚Äùscreen diagonal, and the ability to use two SIM cards that can work simultaneously. The downside of this phone is its weak 1100 mAh battery, capable of withstanding 14 hours of talk time and 528 hours of standby time without recharging. For high-quality communication, the manufacturer placed a Web browser, a stereo bluetooth, a player and a 3.5 mm audio output into the device to connect it. The Nokia Asha 305 mobile phone will certainly be able to find its buyer. Often users prefer a high-quality and reliable device for communication, without any expensive frills.

Nokia Asha 305 firmware: strengths and weaknesses of the platform



The manufacturer tried to apply the Android idea for the smartphone, using the popular platform as a prototype. Such a solution was practically unsuccessful, because with a 3-inch resistive display and the ability to reproduce only 65 thousand colors, the device has low sensitivity, which adversely affects the capabilities of the touchscreen. In this regard, the Nokia Asha 305 mobile phone does not reach a full-fledged smartphone in its characteristics, because even the manufacturer did not take proper care to adjust the color. In bright conditions, color fading appears, which cannot be eliminated even with the help of special settings.

Appearance



The design of the mobile phone is quite simple, there are no special frills that are present in expensive models. Such a device is more suitable for work than for entertainment, and for teenagers it will seem quite boring at all.

The case consists of glossy plastic, it is not afraid of bumps and drops, because the appearance is already unpretentious. The plastic base helped reduce the weight of the phone, which in the end is only 98 grams. The thickened back panel of the device does not look very harmonious, but it allows you to comfortably hold the phone in your hand.

On the side , volume controls, a SIM card switch, several buttons for controlling the main functions of the device and a screen lock are very conveniently placed. You can not restart the phone to connect a second SIM card, because using a special button, the connection is made automatically.

Interface



The Nokia Asha 305 mobile phone is available in four color categories: blue, silver, red and white. In this case, the buyer is given the opportunity to choose according to personal preference.

The interface resembles "Android" only in appearance. The screen itself is divided into several desktops, they perform the function of facilitating the use of the phone. The first window opens all the available shortcuts, the next is a directory application, and the last allows you to display the music being played on the screen, set up auto redial or radio.

The use of a mobile phone is made difficult by the low sensitivity of the touchscreen, so it‚Äôs impossible to call a quality interface. For those who are used to the high speed of turning on various settings and applications, this device will seem like a real torment. Nevertheless, the undoubted advantage of the Nokia Asha 305 is the ability to print on a full-screen keyboard, despite its very small size.

Nokia Asha 305: reviews and conclusions



For those who care about the high speed of the Internet, this phone is not recommended. Scrolling through pages and updating them is rather slow. Such a communicator is suitable for communicating on social networks that are fully supported by the device. Built-in bluetooth is used to transfer files and download them from another device or computer. Speaking generally about Nokia Asha 305, user reviews suggest that we are talking about a very reliable phone, designed for people who can not imagine their life without communication.
