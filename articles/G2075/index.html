Hotel (Novocherkassk): names and description

Today we will tell you about hotels in Novocherkassk (Rostov Region). We will consider the features of each of them. We also talk about the number of rooms, services. The opinions of people who have already lived in a particular hotel will also be described.

Dynasty Hotel (Novocherkassk): description

The hotel offers guests rooms of high comfort. The hotel is perfect for active people who know how to manage their time.





It features a restaurant, bar and terrace. It is also possible to use the ticket delivery service (rail, air).

It has secure parking, luggage storage and a sauna. Ironing and laundry services are also available. All rooms are equipped with minibar, telephone, modern furniture and TV.





Hotel Rooms

1. Single (standard). The room has a bed, bedside tables, satellite TV, telephone, chair, work desk, satellite TV, etc.
2. The double room is equipped in the same way as the standard.
3. Suite. The room has a safe, satellite TV, desk, bed. The bathroom is fully equipped with everything necessary (hairdryer, slippers, bathrobe, toilet and more).

The average price of a room is 3,500 rubles. Breakfast is not included. It should be paid separately. Estimated time is twelve days.

Most people are happy with this hotel. True, some had problems with Wi-Fi. The rooms in this hotel are clean, the staff is friendly.

Hotel "Spark"

This hotel (Novocherkassk) is located in the center of the Donskoy microdistrict. This accommodation is located in a very convenient location. The rooms here are of different categories (from economy to luxury).





It offers free internet. The hotel also has a beauty salon and parking.

Number of rooms of the hotel:

1. There is a single room with a double bed. Also there is a split system, table, chair, wardrobe and refrigerator.

2. The triple room has three single beds. There is also a table, chairs, TV, fridge.

3. There is a junior suite.





The average room in this hotel is 1200 rubles. Breakfast is not included in this price. Check out time is eleven in the morning. Check-in time is 12:00.

Platov na Dubovskogo - a good hotel (Novocherkassk)

This hotel is located in the city. Guests can use the public parking, it is under constant video surveillance.





The hotel has 22 comfortable rooms. The interiors are decorated in a classic style. Appliances in the rooms, like furniture, are new. The rooms also have everything you need to make guests feel most comfortable.

An additional service is breakfast. It should be paid separately. The cafe offers dishes of Russian cuisine. Near the hotel there is a grocery store. It is very comfortable.





This hotel (Novocherkassk) is located near the main street. Nearby is the theater, the Ataman Palace and the Museum of the Don Cossacks.

The average cost of a room is three thousand rubles. Visitors are satisfied with this hotel. The service, as guests say, is up to par here. The rooms are clean and comfortable. Also, hotel guests note its very good location.

Little conclusion

We examined different housing options for local residents and visitors. We hope that at least one hotel is suitable for you. Novocherkassk is a city in which there are not very many hotels. But to choose the most suitable option for yourself, of course, is possible.
