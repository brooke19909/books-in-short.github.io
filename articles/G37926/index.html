Hotel Nissiana Hotel & Bungalows 3 *: reviews

If you plan to relax in Cyprus and are looking for a budget option to stay near the famous Nissi Beach, be sure to pay attention to the Nissiana Hotel & Bungalows 3 *.





Description

This hotel was built in 2002. It includes 141 numbers. The apartments are located in the main three-story building and several two-story bungalows. When booking accommodation, you can choose the option of food that suits you - only breakfast, half board or all inclusive.



Nissiana Hotel & Bungalows Traveler Reviews

Every experienced tourist knows that one of the most important points for planning a trip abroad is the choice of hotel. After all, impressions from the rest will largely depend on this. And even if you consider yourself a very unpretentious traveler, you still probably want to be able to live in comfortable conditions, eat deliciously and count on the attentive attitude of the staff. Therefore, it is very important to be responsible for choosing a hotel.

Invaluable help in this matter, according to experienced travelers, is provided by studying the reviews of those people who have recently stayed in a particular place recently. After all, guests, based on their own experience, share information on the advantages and disadvantages of a particular hotel. Therefore, you can make a more complete and closer to reality picture of what will await you during your vacation.

Today we offer together to learn about the impression left by our compatriots in the three-star Cypriot hotel Nissiana Hotel & Bungalows. Just note that almost all the reviews of tourists about this hotel are exclusively positive. A confirmation of this is the hotel rating, compiled on the estimates of its guests. So, this indicator is 4.5 points out of the maximum possible five. But we learn about everything in more detail.





Location

In general, the location of the hotel in question, our compatriots were satisfied. So, the main factor for many was the proximity to the best beach in Cyprus - Nissi Beach. According to tourists, it can be reached in just five minutes by an unhurried step. And along the way you need to cross the roadway. There are no difficulties with this, since the traffic here is not busy. Also, as travelers note, from this point in Ayia Napa it is convenient to go on a variety of excursions. The area in which Nissiana Hotel & Bungalows 3 * is located has a well-developed infrastructure. So, next to the hotel you will find supermarkets, shops, pharmacies, cafes and restaurants, travel agencies, car rental points and much more.

According to tourists, walking to the center of Ayia Napa is far away. However, you can always get here by bus or taxi. The journey takes no more than 10 minutes. In the city center you will find a lot of entertainment for every taste (there are night clubs, bars, discos, amusement park, etc.). There are also many shopping establishments where you can buy both food and clothing, as well as souvenirs, and everything that you may need during your vacation.





Hotel itself

According to tourists from Russia, Nissiana Hotel & Bungalows 3 * seemed to them very neat and well-groomed. The hotel cannot boast of its own large territory, however there are several outdoor pools (adults and children), a sun terrace, a spa (indoor pool, sauna, massage rooms), a gym, a tennis court, a playground for children, a conference hall, restaurant and bar. The hotel has a lot of greenery.

Housing stock

Since the Nissiana Hotel belongs to the economy category, here you will not find luxurious apartments with a breathtaking design and modern technology. However, as the hotel guests note, the rooms here are very cozy and comfortable. Recently noticeable cosmetic repairs are noticeable in them. As for furniture and items of equipment, everything is not new, but very high quality and in working condition. If something breaks, then after contacting the administrator at the reception, hotel staff will quickly eliminate all problems that have arisen.

Some of the complaints of tourists concerned only apartments with windows overlooking the pool. According to vacationers living here, the rooms were too wet. As for the pluses, many travelers note in their reviews the fact of the availability of free wireless Internet both in rooms and throughout the hotel. Moreover, the connection speed is high enough, which allows you to always stay in touch with your friends and relatives staying at home. As for the equipment of the rooms, there are no complaints. Please note, by default, most apartments do not have electric kettles. But they can always be taken at the reception, leaving a deposit of 10 euros. The dishes in the rooms are always in sufficient quantities.





Population

Despite the fact that, in accordance with the internal rules of the Nissiana Hotel & Bungalows (Cyprus), the accommodation of newly arrived guests in the apartments they have reserved must be carried out after two in the afternoon, in most cases this formality is not performed. So, if you arrive here in the morning, and the hotel has available rooms corresponding to the category you reserved, they will populate you almost immediately, they will not doom you to tedious waiting. Many travelers appreciated this attention to guests on the part of the administration. As for the day of departure, you should hand over the room keys before noon. If your departure from the hotel is planned at a later time, then you can vacate the apartment, leave things in the luggage room and continue to rest until the time comes to go to the airport.

Service, staff

Many tourists in their comments mention how satisfied they were with the staff of this hotel. So, according to them, the staff here are very friendly, attentive and always ready to help. A big plus for our compatriots was the fact that almost all the hotel staff speak Russian. So you will not have any difficulties in communication here.

As for the quality of service for guests, here, too, no complaints were noted. So, guests say that the maids clean the rooms every day and do it very high quality. Towels are also updated daily, and bed linen is changed twice a week.





Nutrition

The guests of the Nissiana Hotel & Bungalows (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) were mostly satisfied with the level of organization of the restaurant. According to them, despite the fact that the choice of dishes here is not as diverse as, for example, in hotels in Turkey and Egypt, the quality of their preparation does not cause any complaints. For this, our compatriots express special thanks to the hotel chefs. In addition, for lunch and dinner, you can always choose something to your taste. The menu always has meat, fish, poultry dishes, several side dishes, delicious fresh pastries, vegetables, fruits, desserts.

Beach vacation

As for this point, here the hotel in question was awarded the highest ratings from the guests. After all, as already mentioned, it is located within walking distance from the best beach in Cyprus - Nissi Beach. It will be comfortable for both adults and children to relax. The beach itself is fine sand, the entrance to the sea is gentle, without stones. The sea in this place is very clean and beautiful. Since the beach is in municipal ownership, renting sun loungers will require additional payment. But you can always sit right on the sand, laying the mats that you brought with you and installing an umbrella.
