Appearance, dimensions and features

This model definitely looks very interesting in appearance. She immediately catches the eye among a wide range of smartphones. The case is made quite neatly. The dimensions of the gadget are compact:

  • thickness - 7.9 mm;
  • height - 143 mm;
  • width - 71 mm.

However, the interaction may cause discomfort, especially for those with a small palm. The reason is the shape of the case. Massiveness of the device is added by smooth ends and slightly rounded corners. Even the small weight, which is only 136 g, does not save from inconvenience.

An indisputable advantage is the abundance of colors. The frame that runs along the perimeter of the entire case is silver and gold. As for the latter, it is installed with cases of brown, white, pink, black. There is also a model completely made in gold tones. Fans of strict decisions can choose a classic black instance with a silver frame.

Unfortunately, the phone case is made of plastic, which is only disguised as metal. With careful handling, it is impossible to completely avoid scratches, so it is recommended to use covers. The design of the phone is collapsible. In order to access the SIM slots, memory card and battery BQ 5070 Magic, just remove the back cover. It holds pretty tight due to special latches. On the back there is the name of the manufacturer. At the bottom there is a speaker hole, and above it there is an inscription - Magic. The main camera lens is round, displayed at the top. Next to it is a flash.

The indisputable advantage of this model is a separate slot for an external drive. In order to install it, there is no need to remove the battery. But replacing the "SIM cards" will not work quickly, since the slots (micro-SIM and nano-SIM) are located directly above the battery.

On the front panel are all the necessary elements. Above the screen is a proximity sensor, a speaker hole and a selfie camera. The control keys are virtual, so the bottom of the panel is completely free.

Connectors for charging and headphones are displayed on the upper end. On the opposite side is only a small microphone hole. You can adjust the volume by the oblong key, which is located on the left side face. For the power button, a standard place was chosen - on the right side.
