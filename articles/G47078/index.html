Hotel Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 *, Vietnam: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Vietnam is a country that is becoming more and more popular among tourists. After all, it is here that you will find picturesque landscapes, not too crowded clean beaches and, of course, gentle sea waves. Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort is located almost on the seashore, which makes it so popular. So what does this place look like and is it worth spending a vacation here?

Description of the territory and location





The small hotel Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * is located on the territory of the popular resort town of Phan Thiet, on a picturesque seashore. This is a quiet area, which is perfect for lovers of secluded relaxation. By the way, 10 km is the more famous and lively city of Mui Ne, which can easily be reached by bus, which leaves directly from the hotel grounds. But the distance to Ho Chi Minh International Airport is quite large and is 217 km. Of course, the hotel provides a shuttle service, but you should prepare for a long trip.

Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort is a comfortable hotel with a small but very beautiful area. The hotel consists of a main building and several small bungalows. Everything here is decorated with lush greenery and exotic plants. There is even a small garden where you can relax.

Hotel Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * (Vietnam): what do the rooms look like?





This hotel is not too big. On its territory there are 62 rooms of the following categories:

  • Superior Garden View - Rooms are 30 square meters. m overlooking the garden.
  • Superior Pool View, which overlooks the courtyard with pool.
  • Deluxe Ocean View with stunning coastal views.
  • Deluxe Garden View.
  • Villas Garden View - Spacious rooms are 65 square meters. m, which consist of several rooms.
  • Villas Beach Front - This room has direct access to the beach.

Regardless of the room you choose, you can count on modern comfort and homeliness. Of course, in the rooms you will find the necessary comfortable furniture and a set of appliances that will make your stay more convenient. In particular, you will have the opportunity to watch satellite channels, use a telephone and air conditioning. There is a minibar with a small refrigerator, however, it is filled on request, which is not included in the price. Guests can use the safe for free.

The spacious bathroom has a bathtub with hydromassage system and shower (depending on the room category). There is a hairdryer for drying hair, a large mirror, a dressing table with a sink, a set of hygiene products. Rooms at Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort are cleaned daily, linen is changed 1-2 times a week.

Dining at the hotel





Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * hotel operates on the "BB" system. This means that paying for hotel accommodation, tourists also get the right to free breakfast. Reception is arranged in the hotel restaurant. The choice of dishes is quite large, and guests are also treated to delicious pastries.

In the same restaurant you can have lunch or dinner, but at an additional cost. Dishes are prepared to order, and the menu is quite large. Guests can enjoy masterpieces of Asian and traditional Vietnamese cuisine, as well as more familiar international dishes.

Nevertheless, you can have a delicious lunch on the territory of Mui Ne, where you can get there in 20 minutes by bus or taxi. Here, for a small fee, you can enjoy fresh seafood, fish dishes and masterpieces of Vietnamese cuisine.

Is it possible to relax on the beach?

As already mentioned, the hotel is located on a picturesque seashore - you can walk to it in a few minutes. What does the hotel Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 * offer? Reviews indicate that the beach here is quite clean and well-groomed, and not crowded. Therefore, the hotel usually stops tourists who want to relax, enjoying the peace and breathtaking beauty of nature.

Sun loungers and sun umbrellas are placed on the shore, so guests can relax and take sun baths. It is possible to ride boats, boats, go sailing and windsurfing. And of course, you can always visit the more lively public beaches in the city, which offer more entertainment.

Additional services and entertainment for guests





Naturally, the owners tried to make the stay of tourists in the territory of Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort as comfortable as possible. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available. The hotel has a car, bicycle and motorbike rental service, which is most often used by guests who are going to spend time outside the hotel. Free parking is nearby, which is also convenient.

Internet access is available throughout. The hotel also has a good business center. There is even a small conference room for business events and celebrations.

The courtyard has a swimming pool with clean water, and sun loungers and parasols are arranged around the guest terrace. Here you can swim, relax, sunbathe and chat with neighbors. There is a gym, as well as a spa center, which offers beauty and wellness treatments, as well as various types of massage. If you want to get acquainted with the sights, traditions and culture of the country, then you will be helped with this in the hotel excursion center.

Hotel Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort 4 *: reviews





Naturally, after talking with tourists who have already managed to visit one place or another, you can get a lot of valuable information. What do Madamcuc Saigon Emerald Resort say? Reviews indicate that this is a really beautiful hotel, which offers not only good service, but also real home comfort. Here everyone knows each other and communicates without problems. The staff speak English and are always ready to help.

The rooms are spacious and clean, all appliances are in good condition. You can relax by the pool, and the beach is incredibly beautiful. The food is good, and tourists like to eat on the outdoor terrace, enjoying the fresh air and views of the picturesque garden.

The only drawbacks of reviews include some privacy, as shops and restaurants are located at some distance. On the other hand, here you can use buses and taxis, which are not so expensive. And the hotel is more designed for people who prefer a relaxing beach holiday. Young people who prefer nightlife, as well as children here may be a little bored.
