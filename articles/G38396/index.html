Italy in November: weather, holidays, where to go

One of the most beloved countries for holidays is Italy. This country has magnificent beaches, excellent resorts, a large number of unique historical monuments. In addition, Italy is washed by five different seas. Therefore, it is not surprising that tens of thousands of tourists from all over the world come here every year. This country is good at any time of the year. But today we will tell you about holidays in Italy in November. You will find out what the weather is like here at this time. What is the best place to go and which tours to Italy in November are the most popular.

Great place to relax at any time of the year

Many tourists like to come to this amazing country in the summer. Bask in the warm sun, swim in the gentle sea, go shopping and enjoy the monuments of architecture and art. But if you want to save on travel, it is best to go to Italy in November. Further we will tell you about all the charm of such a holiday. But first, about the features of the weather at this time of year.

Climatic conditions

It will be interesting to know what weather awaits tourists in late autumn in Italy. Traveling to different cities of this wonderful country, you can bask in the warm sun (Sicily, Palermo), as well as get caught in the rain (Venice, Genoa). But, fortunately, bad weather is not worth several days. After the rain, as a rule, the sun peeks out.

Bologna Jazz Festival

A person who attended this event will definitely want to see and hear it again. This amazingly beautiful music festival lasts for several weeks. The most talented musicians from all over Italy and other countries of the world come to him. It is considered one of the oldest and most popular festivals. It takes place not only in Bologna, but also in other cities.

Old bonfire celebration

You can witness one of the ancient rituals if you come to Italy in November. In one of the cities in the region, Emilia Romagna, a huge bonfire is lit in the town square. Witnesses of this spectacle are thousands of tourists from around the world. This holiday has long been remembered as one of the most spectacular and unusual.

Italy in November: where to go?

There are many amazing places to stay in this country. Let's find out what tours to Italy in November are offered by travel agencies. Let us dwell only on the most popular.

  • Milan. In this city you can visit a large number of attractions, as well as shopping centers. Here is the Royal Palace, which was built in the XII century. Inside the building is decorated with painted murals and mosaics. Today, art exhibitions are held here. Another ancient building dates back to the Middle Ages - Sforza Castle. Initially, it was built as a fortress, protecting residents of the city from raids by enemies. The great artist Leonardo da Vinci worked on the design of its interiors. In the XX century, the castle was completely renovated and still pleases tourists with its impressive size. If you come to Milan, then by all means visit the Brera art gallery. Here you can see the masterpieces of the most famous artists of the world. Also pay attention to the Vittorio Emmanuele II Gallery. The place impresses with its amazing beauty and sophistication. It connects the central squares of the city. Here you can see beautiful sculptures, as well as visit restaurants, cafes and shops.
  • Sicily. The weather here in November is quite warm. And if you're lucky, then you can even swim. Also here you can make various excursions and see a large number of attractions.
  • San Miniato. Choosing this place to relax you can become a member of a large number of gastronomic festivals that take place in the fall.

Some tips

If you are going on vacation to another country, you need to remember several important points, which we will tell you about further:

  • In order not to waste time waiting in lines at museums and other cultural institutions, use the ticket reservation service on special sites. This will significantly save your time and be sure that you will definitely get to the place you dreamed about.
  • Before planning a vacation, remember that the weather in Italy in November is quite windy and rainy. Therefore, be sure to bring warm clothes, waterproof shoes and an umbrella.
  • Remember that you can make very profitable purchases if you find in advance information about sales, which are often arranged in many stores in Italy.

Shopping and souvenirs

Have you decided to go on holiday to Italy in November? You have made the right choice. Indeed, at this time you can buy a large number of good things and souvenirs at more favorable prices. We offer you an approximate list of products that are worth purchasing:

  • Dreaming of a chic fur coat or a fur vest from the collection of famous European fashion designers? You think this is too expensive a purchase? In November, you can make such purchases with very large discounts.
  • A bottle or even some good wine. At this time, the festival takes place in the Italian city of Merno. You can not only make a bargain from well-known wine producers, but also taste many varieties.
  • Pay attention to branded clothes and leather shoes. We think that you will undoubtedly like the offered prices.
  • Household items: photo frames, interesting figurines, wall clocks and much more.

Tourists reviews

Many tourists believe that Italy in November is a great option for a family budget. Moreover, you can not only save money, but also go shopping. To summarize

You should not be afraid that the weather in Italy in November will spoil your entire vacation. Firstly, you can choose more sunny cities at this time. And secondly, in addition to a beach holiday, there are so many interesting entertainments that worrying about windy or rainy weather you simply will have neither time nor desire. Have a pleasant and exciting trip to you at any time of the year!
