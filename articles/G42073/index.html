Tobolsk hotels: description and reviews

Tobolsk is a picturesque city in the north of the Tyumen region. In the distant 1590, Tobolsk became the first settlement of Siberia, which received the status of a city. therefore there are many historical sights and natural beauties, as well as business opportunities. All this makes the city attractive for both tourists and business people. Tobolsk hotels will provide them with a warm welcome. Living conditions and the level of service will pleasantly surprise you.

Hotel "Slavyanskaya"

Hotel "Slavyanskaya" in Tobolsk is located at 9 microdistrict, building 1. This is the first hotel of a European level in the city. Her story began in 1994. To accommodate guests provided 184 rooms of various categories of comfort. Namely:

  • Single economy - from 2600 rubles per day.
  • Single standard - from 3800 rubles per day.
  • Wedding number - from 4500 rubles per day.
  • Double standard - from 5000 rubles per day.
  • Junior Suite - from 6800 rubles per day.
  • Luxury - from 7400 rubles per day.
  • Apartments - from 800 rubles per day.

Reviews

About this hotel in Tobolsk guests leave a lot of approving comments:

  • good breakfasts;
  • the bathroom comes with a full range of cosmetic accessories;
  • new perfectly clean towels;
  • Friendly staff who carry out their work in good faith and promptly;
  • convenient location.

But they also notice the flaws:

  • bad smell in rooms;
  • number of rooms needs redecorating;
  • distinct smell of tobacco smoke in rooms and corridors;
  • small windows through which little sunlight penetrates;
  • high prices for accommodation that do not correspond to the level of comfort and quality of service.

Hotel "Seasons"

In the very center of the city is the Four Seasons Hotel in Tobolsk. The institution is located on Revolutionary Street 9. The hotel rooms will delight you with their original design in the theme of four seasons. Guests can take advantage of the following services:

  • Accommodation in a junior suite (5000 rubles);
  • wireless Internet;
  • Private parking
  • safe at the administrator;
  • ironing facilities;
  • non smoking rooms.

Reviews

You can hear a lot of good things about this Tobolsk hotel:

  • pleasant chamber atmosphere associated with the small size of the hotel (only four rooms);
  • convenient location near the Tobolsk Kremlin;
  • delicious and hearty breakfasts;
  • nice common sitting area by the fireplace.

But not without negativity:

  • there is no kettle or refrigerator in the room (only in the shared kitchen);
  • uncomfortable beds - too soft mattresses;
  • TV does not work well - channels show with interference and constantly "slow down";
  • weak wireless internet signal;
  • there are two mattresses on the large bed, which are constantly moving apart.

Hotel "Siberia"

Sibir Hotel in Tobolsk is located at S. Remezov Square, 1. This is the historical part of the city. The facility is equipped in a small three-story building, which was built in 1963. The 37 rooms can accommodate up to 54 guests. The cost of living is from 2000 rubles per person per day.

Also, the institution will delight you with the luxurious restaurant "At Nikolsky Vzvoz". The facility has a beautifully designed dining room for 50 seats and a banquet room for 10 guests. Here you can try traditional Russian cuisine.

Reviews

About this hotel in Tobolsk, travelers leave such approving reviews:

  • convenient location in the historical center of the city near the main attractions;
  • pleasant interior of rooms and the hotel as a whole;
  • friendly and helpful staff;
  • good transport interchange near the hotel.

As well as negative comments of this content:

  • in the rooms, new furniture is adjacent to the old Soviet, which looks very inorganic;
  • even with a heater in the rooms it is very cold;
  • assortment of breakfasts leaves much to be desired;
  • high prices for food in the restaurant;
  • Wifi in rooms is poor;
  • weak pressure of water in the shower;
  • poor sound insulation;
  • intermittent interruptions with hot water supply.

Reviews

If you want to get an objective impression of the quality of service in the hotel in question, pay attention to the reviews. From them you can get the following positive information:

  • Near the hotel there is a large shopping center;

Hotel "Georgievskaya"

The new modern Hotel Georgievskaya in Tobolsk is located in the historical center of the city near the Kremlin, the Church of Peter and Paul and many other attractions. The address of the establishment is Leninskaya street, 35. Guests can take advantage of the following advantages:

  • 69 rooms of different categories of comfort (from Standard to Premium Suite);
  • breakfast is included in the price;
  • free wireless internet access;
  • guarded parking for guests' cars;
  • transfer organization;
  • sale of souvenirs;
  • a beauty salon with a male and female hall;
  • Luggage storage (you can leave your suitcases before check-in and after eviction);
  • washing and ironing of things of guests;
  • booking and selling tickets for air and rail transport;
  • provision of cots (upon prior request);
  • round-the-clock video surveillance at the hotel;
  • wake up service;
  • post services;
  • conducting excursions;
  • business and special events;
  • food in a restaurant or tavern;
  • conference room rental;
  • rent a banquet hall.

Reviews

About this hotel you can meet such positive opinions:

  • beautiful design of the hotel (especially the restaurant);
  • attentive staff responds to all requests and comments of guests;
  • good hearty buffet breakfasts are included in the price;
  • convenient location near the main attractions;
  • good quality room cleaning;
  • there is a cooler in the lobby with free drinking water.

And such negative ones:

  • strong audibility between numbers - you can hear how neighbors talk, wash, and walk;
  • uncomfortable too soft mattresses, from which springs stick out and painfully dig into the back;
  • few lighting fixtures in the room;
  • I would like to have slippers in the rooms;
  • little old tvs.
