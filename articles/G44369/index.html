Sony C3: specifications, review and reviews

Last August, the Japanese manufacturer Sony released a gift for its users, selfie lovers. The Sony C3 smartphone with a large diagonal and a high-quality front camera, which has an LED flash, appeared on the market. Let us consider in more detail the design of Sony C3, the characteristics and capabilities of the optics of this model.

Appearance

The body of the gadget is made of plastic. Three color options are available for users: black, white and green (peppermint color).

On the front panel of the device, a large area is occupied by a screen. Neither touch, nor mechanical keys are provided on the case. Above the screen there is a movement sensor and a light warning indicator, as well as a business card of the device - a front camera with a flash.





On the right side of the phone is a slot for micro-USB and a compartment for a memory card; on the left side there is a camera activation button, a device lock key, a volume rocker and a compartment for two SIM cards.

At the top there is a 3.5 mm headset jack. On the back of the device are a microphone, speaker, NFC coverage area, camera and a second LED flash.

The overall dimensions of the device are 155.2 √ó 78.7 √ó 7.6 mm, weight - 149.7 g.

Sony C3 screen: specifications and resolution

The screen is designed using a standard TFT-matrix and does not have an oleophobic coating. Display diagonal 5.5 inches with a resolution of 720p. 16 million colors produce a bright and attractive picture, but when you look at the display from different angles, it becomes noticeable that the image fades somewhat. The multi-touch here is pretty good, and supports up to 10 touches at the same time.





Specifications

Now let's talk about the Sony C3 filling. The characteristics are as follows: the Qualcomm MSM8926 Snapdragon 400 quad-core processor, clocked at 1.2 GHz, also on board the model provides 1 GB of RAM and 8 GB of memory for storing data. The phone supports microSD (TransFlash) flash drives with a capacity of up to 32 GB. Adreno 305 graphics accelerator is responsible for the picture , which allows you to run high-quality 3D games. Of the additional features available are Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC, DLNA and USB 2.0. The platform is Android 4.4.2.

In terms of functionality, there are no special complaints about the device: information is processed quite quickly, the device practically does not slow down, the system copes well with fairly resource-intensive applications.

Cameras

Now we will consider the most interesting features of Sony C3 - the characteristics of optics. The front camera has 8 MP, autofocus, LED flash, image stabilizer and the ability to shoot video in Full HD. The asset of the front camera is 5 megapixels, a front camera and the ability to shoot HD clips. Among the settings there are the following functions: HDR, recognition of smiles and faces, geo-tags, panoramic shooting, visual effects, touch focus and more.

Capturing the picture is fast enough, we also note the good work of the image stabilizer and autofocus. The front camera also behaves qualitatively, under which, in fact, this device was imprisoned. The received pictures can be immediately posted on a social network.

Multimedia

The Sony C3 smartphone, the characteristics of which allow it to be used as a player, supports many codecs for both audio and video. The speaker is loud, in addition, the xLoud option is available in the settings, which is able to further increase the sound level. On a bright screen, movies and movies with HD resolution look great.





Battery

The model has acquired a non-removable lithium-ion battery with a capacity of 2500 mAh. In standby mode, the battery will last about 960 hours, in talk mode - 11 hours, and in listening to music tracks - 65 hours.

Price

So, we examined in detail the Sony C3. Characteristics, a review of which indicates the strengths and weaknesses of the gadget, should give users the final answer to the main question. Is a smartphone worth its 14,580 rubles or not?

Sony C3: specifications, reviews and ratings

The camera, although it is a calling card of the smartphone, received mixed reviews.
