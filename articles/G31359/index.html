Hotel "Ugra Valley" in Khanty-Mansiysk

Khanty-Mansiysk is the administrative center of Russia with a population of about fifty thousand people. The foundation of the city dates back to the end of the 16th century, to the times of the development of Siberia by Ermak's squads. It received its name from local nationalities. The city is located on the banks of the Irtysh River in the West Siberian Plain. These vast lands are also called Ugra. Hence, the large modern hotel complex "Ugra Valley" took its name.

Khanty-Mansiysk is the cultural center of the Autonomous Okrug, where you can relax. Public libraries, five state museums, three theaters, many entertainment venues will brighten up leisure and add good mood. Particular attention is paid to sports. And given the good, snowy winters, the emphasis is on its winter views. Modern ski slopes and a biathlon center provide an opportunity to hold world-class competitions.



Hotel "Ugra Valley" (Khanty-Mansiysk)
