Lenovo A526 review: reviews and specifications

In search of a small classic candy bar, many found for themselves the Lenovo Ideaphone A526, the reviews and specifications of which are not only pleasing to the eye, but also fully correspond to its low price. This smartphone was released in 2014 and to this day pleases its owners.





Dimensions

This phone lies perfectly in the hand, because its dimensions are 67.59 x 132 x 11.1, and its weight barely reaches the mark of 145 grams. A miniature smartphone compared to modern models looks organically both in a gentle female hand and in a brutal man.

If we talk about the screen, then its size is 4.5 inches, resolution is 480 x 854 pixels, which is a pretty good indicator. The matrix costs TFT with Multitouch support, as evidenced by the Lenovo A526 reviews, the viewing angles are very low, which causes discomfort when working with the device outdoors in sunny weather. In principle, this type of matrix few people will please.

CPU

Regarding the processor installed in the Lenovo A526, the reviews are the most flattering. The manufacturer used the MediaTek MT6582M with a clock frequency of 1300 MHz. These are proven quad-core processors, which are put on budget smartphones. They consume a small amount of energy and contribute to the smooth operation of the device. His only receiver, which was able to supplant the MTK6582M, was the recently appeared MTK6580. It is put on the latest models. Leave the most different about Lenovo A526 reviews. On the device, you can watch high-quality video and play most classic games freely. The processor can be safely attributed to the advantages of this smartphone.





Appearance

As mentioned earlier, the smartphone is a candy bar with three touch keys at the bottom. On the side panels there is a volume rocker and a shutdown / lock button. Below, under the back button, you can see a small hole for the microphone. Everything is very organic and neat.

On top of the display, the proximity sensor and lighting, the front camera are clearly visible. By the way, the camera has a rather low quality, so for an avid selfie lover it does not fit. But we'll talk about cameras, which are two in the device, later.

All necessary connectors are on the top panel. They are standard. What does it mean? If the cable for the charger is suddenly damaged or you want to change the in-ear headphones to vacuum ones, then there will be no problems.

I want to pay special attention to the back cover. In addition to the company logo and main camera located on it, the holes for a fairly loud speaker are very neatly inscribed. The cover itself has a corrugated surface, which allows the smartphone not to slip in the palm of your hand and minimize fingerprints. In general, in appearance there are no complaints.





Memory

The phone has 1024 megabytes of RAM, which is an excellent indicator for a budget smartphone. This model cannot boast of large built-in memory - only 4 gigabytes. But you should not be upset, as it can be expanded by purchasing a memory card.

Cameras

As mentioned earlier, the photos are of medium quality. This is due to the fact that it has a front camera of only 0.3 megapixels, and the main camera has 5 megapixels. Lenovo A526, whose photo is presented in the article, does not have a flash. Such indicators guarantee high-quality images exclusively in good daylight. Otherwise, they are for video calls. Whether this applies to negative points is up to you.

Functionality

The developers installed Android 4.2 as a system. Some craftsmen try to flash the device on 5.0 and 5.1. However, in this case, usually Lenovo A526, reviews confirm this, is not subject to recovery. So it's better not to take risks.

From the standard set of programs you can find:

  • Social networks: Skype and other clients.
  • Media: player, radio and so on.
  • Office applications: notepad, calendar or calculator.

The smartphone supports two SIM cards, but there is only one radio module. It is possible to access the Internet in parallel with telephone communication. Of course, in 2016 you will hardly surprise anyone, but on the Lenovo A526 (4.5 "4gb), reviews often mention this.





Battery

The battery in this smartphone is only 2000 mAh. This figure will allow the smartphone to hold a charge for 5 hours of conversation and several weeks in standby mode. For a device with such a price, this is more than normal. If this is not enough, you can always purchase a portable external battery.

conclusions

About Lenovo A526 reviews leave both positive and negative. But this does not prevent the smartphone and to this day to be popular.

When buying in the kit, you will find the smartphone itself with a battery, a warranty card, a brief instruction, a charger with a USB cable. As a bonus - headphones. Let the sound in them wish to leave the best, to get such a gift is always nice.

Whether to purchase this device is your personal choice, but remember that at the moment the world market is filled with many other models of smartphones with a lot of features. For fans of the classics "Lenovo" A526 will be the perfect assistant.
