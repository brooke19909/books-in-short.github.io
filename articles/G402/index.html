"Moscow. City Park 1": reviews, prices

Moscow is a huge metropolis that attracts those wishing to settle in the city and enjoy all the advantages of the capital's inhabitants. Therefore, for developers, the city is promising, including the territory of the Novomoskovsk administrative district. After all, it is there that the construction of the Moskovsky. City Park 1 ". You will learn more about it from today's article.





Location of the residential complex

At a distance of about 7 km from the Moscow Ring Road along the Kiev highway and 7 km from the Salaryevo metro station in Moscow, a new microdistrict is located - the residential complex Pervy Moskovsky. City Park. " You can get to it from the Moscow Ring Road in a few minutes using your own vehicles. There is another option - from the metro stations “Yugo-Zapadnaya”, “Troparevo”, “Salaryevo”, “Teply Stan” on urban public transport routes: buses and fixed-route taxis.





Description of the complex

After completion of the construction of the residential complex “First Moscow. City Park ”will be a residential complex divided into microdistricts and quarters. It will include 65 panel multi-storey buildings erected in compliance with a single architectural style. The houses belong to the comfort class, in each there are from 17 to 25 floors. In some apartments, the developer provides for repairs in English, Scandinavian, modern or classic styles. Other living areas are offered in draft finish.





Layouts of apartments and their cost

Almost all apartment layouts are provided in the residential complex : one-room, two-room and three-room. The smallest apartment is 40 m2, and the largest apartments are a little over 100 m2.

The developer offers per square meters of the “First Moscow. Cities of the Park ”prices are very moderate. Traditionally, the most expensive are one-room apartments. Their cost is about 136 thousand rubles per square meter of living space. For three-room apartments, the price drops to 98 thousand. This is relatively inexpensive compared to the cost of housing in the capital itself. In Moscow, prices start at 150 thousand, and the upper limit of the cost is missing.

Internal and external infrastructure

“Moscow. Park 1 City, like most residential complexes under construction in the capital or in the immediate vicinity, has a developed infrastructure. We are talking not only about the internal, but also about the external arrangement. In the quarter, general education and sports schools, several kindergartens with pools, many shops, a shopping and entertainment center will be erected. Owners of their own vehicles will be pleased with garages and parking lots.

In the courtyards there will be children's playgrounds and sports grounds with landscape gardening. Security will be provided by security personnel.





In the immediate vicinity of the residential complex "Moscow. City Park 1 ”are the supermarkets of the federal retail chain Pyaterochka, a medical center, and a clinic. Vnukovo Airport is approximately 6 km away.

Ecological situation

She is in this area more favorable than in the metropolis itself. The residential complex is not located on the fastest Kiev highway, but a little distance from it. Therefore, residents do not hear the noise of passing vehicles. In addition, “Moscow. City Park 1 ”is surrounded on all sides by a forest, which makes it possible to breathe fresh air.

Reviews of local residents

There are both positive and negative opinions about life in a complex from people who bought apartments in it. Negative reviews add up mainly due to the quality of the houses built. Negative opinions are due to an uncomfortable exit from the Kiev highway and its congestion during rush hours.

However, they are much more common about the multi-apartment complex “First Moscow. City Park "positive reviews. After moving, the local population will be in the city limits and will be metropolitan residents with all the privileges arising from this status. The buyers of apartments like the surrounding area of ​​the complex with forest areas. In addition, the developer provides a well-thought-out and developed infrastructure for use, so that you can not travel outside the complex. Enthusiastic reviews deserve the opportunity to choose an apartment with both full interior decoration and without it.
