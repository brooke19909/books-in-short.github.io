Hotel Palazzo di Zante Hotel (Greece / Zakynthos): Travel Reviews

In the north of Zakynthos is the Hotel Palazzo di Zante Hotel. The Vasiliskos area is considered one of the most picturesque in all of Greece. Excellent complement to beautiful nature will be impeccable service and comfort.





Accommodation

An important criterion when choosing a hotel is the living conditions. At Hotel Palazzo di Zante Hotel, guests can enjoy the following rooms:

  • Of the 167 rooms of this institution, 146 belong to the standard category. They are a compact bedroom with large or twin beds. It is possible to accommodate an extra guest. Features a private furnished balcony.
  • For family accommodation, the hotel provides 18 two-room rooms. It can accommodate from 2 to 4 people. One bedroom has a French and the other twin beds. Guests can enjoy the pool from the furnished balcony.
  • Each of the 2 suites overlooks the pool. They consist of a separate living room and bedroom. This room can accommodate 2 main and 2 additional guests. In addition to beds, the room has a sofa and an organizer for storing guests.
  • The presidential suite in terms of comfort is comparable to a full-fledged apartment. There are 3 separate bedrooms with individual bathrooms and a private exit to a spacious furnished terrace. There is also a living room with upholstered furniture and a dining area. The main advantage of the room is its own kitchen.




Infrastructure

At the Hotel Palazzo di Zante Hotel you can find everything that a modern tourist needs for relaxation. So, the hotel infrastructure is represented by the following objects:

  • free access to wireless Internet in the reception area;
  • Internet cafe in the lobby;
  • luggage room;
  • private car parking;
  • conference room for 150 people;
  • round-the-clock reception;
  • a business center with photocopying and fax services.




Services

Hotel Palazzo di Zante Hotel is guided by high standards of service. It is worth paying attention to access to such services:

  • laptop and tablet rental;
  • washing and ironing;
  • wake up service;
  • qualified nanny (service is ordered no later than one day);
  • rental cars and mopeds;
  • doctor's call and medical care;
  • organization of business negotiations and conferences;
  • safe rental;
  • the ability to pay for services in non-cash form;
  • irons and ironing boards are available on request (for temporary use, in order of priority).

  • outdoor swimming pool;

  • gym;

  • karaoke;
  • Themed Greek Nights
  • indoor heated pool;

  • visit to the amusement park located in a neighboring hotel;

Catering establishments

At Palazzo di Zante Hotel, close attention is paid to the full nutrition of guests. This function is performed by such institutions:

  • Appolo is the main restaurant, which serves a comprehensive buffet. The dining room is decorated in a classic style with elements of a traditional Greek decor. There is also the opportunity to enjoy delicious food in the fresh air.
  • A la Carte Restaurant specializes in traditional Greek cuisine. The main emphasis is on seafood dishes. There is also an extensive wine list, where both local and imported drinks are presented.
  • Aphrodite is a lobby bar located in the lobby of the main building. The institution is characterized by a calm atmosphere, which was created thanks to a cozy interior and calm music.
  • Channel is a poolside bar. His main specialization is cocktails and refreshments. It also serves light snacks and hearty fast food.
  • The disco bar is open at night. Until two in the morning, guests can enjoy incendiary dances. Musical accompaniment is provided by popular DJs.




All Inclusive

The resort hotel Palazzo di Zante Hotel 4 * provides guests with the opportunity to eat according to their favorite all-inclusive system. This concept includes the following points:

  • breakfast in the main restaurant from 07:00 to 10:00;
  • lunch is held from 12:30 to 14:30;
  • from 16:00 for an hour, guests can enjoy fresh tea or coffee, pastries, pastries, as well as refreshments with sandwiches;
  • from 19:30 to 21:30 in the main restaurant dinner is served;
  • During main meals, guests are also provided with drinking water, beer, local wine and refreshing drinks;
  • from 10:00 to 23:00 in all bars, guests can enjoy alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, tea and coffee, as well as ice cream.

Positive feedback on the Palazzo di Zante Hotel 4 *

The main goal of any holiday is to get positive impressions and vivid memories. Based on the opinions of travelers, we can say that the Palazzo di Zante Hotel completely copes with this task. Reviews contain information about the merits of the institution. These include the following:

  • the main restaurant is very nicely decorated (there is an artificial waterfall with night illumination on the open terrace);
  • antique furniture is installed in the lobby area (although it is not in the best condition);
  • the hotel is surrounded by a picturesque olive grove;
  • food, although not very diverse, but quite high quality and tasty;
  • quality local wine is served;
  • inexpensive car and motorcycle rental;
  • pricing policy is consistent with the quality of service;
  • gentle entry into the sea, which is especially pleasing to vacationers with small children;
  • the hotel has a fairly large territory, which is nice to walk in the evening;
  • an active animation program for both adults and children;
  • good children's infrastructure, and therefore kids always have something to do;
  • You can get acquainted with the local flavor during themed Greek evenings (held twice a week).




Negative reviews about Palazzo di Zante Hotel 4 * (Zakynthos Island)

Do not neglect information about the negative aspects of relaxation in a particular resort. Speaking about the Palazzo di Zante Hotel (Zakynthos), it is worth noting the following disadvantages:

  • old repairs in the room (nevertheless, the decor is quite pleasant and comfortable);
  • almost no fruit in the restaurant menu;
  • sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach are paid;
  • given that the hotel is on a raised platform, it is rather problematic to return from the sea or from the city;
  • tiles in the bathroom have traces of mold;
  • there are no free drinks on the beach;
  • inflated prices for excursions (it is cheaper to rent a car and go around on your own);
