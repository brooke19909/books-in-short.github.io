New World, Crimea. Tourists reviews

Many of us choose products, clothes and equipment, having previously read reviews about them on the Internet. Recently, it is also customary to choose vacation spots by studying the reviews of tourists. So, most vacationers are advised to visit the New World, Crimea. Reviews about this amazing place are only positive.

Lost among the rocks

The New World is a tiny village in the Crimea with a permanent population of just over a thousand inhabitants. It is located on the banks of the picturesque Green Bay. On both sides of the coast of the New World were surrounded by two rocks: Falcon and Eagle. The beaches in the New World are sandy with good access to the sea.



In the vicinity of the village is a botanical reserve. Mostly relic groves of juniper and pine of Stankevich grow here. The healing air in the reserve favorably affects patients suffering from asthma, hypertension and cardiovascular diseases.

Prince who loved champagne

Relaxing in the New World and not visiting the factory of champagne wines is the same as standing at the window of a candy store and not going there. Factory "New World" in 1878 was organized by Prince Golitsyn. By the way, you can stay in the house where Lev Golitsyn lived and find out about his interesting life. In the basement of the former estate of the prince there is a tasting room, which houses a wonderful collection of champagne wines.

Two company stores are opened in the village where you can buy champagne or wine produced by the New World factory. Champagne wine factory is another good reason that attracts tourists to visit the New World, Crimea. Reviews about the products of this plant are more than enthusiastic.



Trail between sky and water

Another attraction of the New World is the Golitsyn trail. This trail will lead you through the most beautiful corners of the resort village. The Golitsyn trail begins its winding path from the Green Bay and leads to a large natural grotto. Its total length is 5470 meters.

Many tourists who at least once walked along the Golitsyn trail dream of returning here again. As you can see, the Golitsyn trail is the second foundation to come to the New World, Crimea. Guest reviews are direct evidence of this.

Musical grotto

The Golitsyn Grotto (also called the Chaliapin Grotto) is a rather large natural grotto created by the sea element in Mount Koba-Kaya. According to one legend, the famous Chaliapin once sang in this grotto, who smashed a glass of champagne with his voice. Later, in the stone arches that are located in the grotto, Lev Golitsyn kept this divine drink.

A path leading to the Royal Beach is laid through the grotto. Variety Grotto (another name for it) is a unique place that should encourage you to visit the New World, Crimea. Rest in this truly paradise will give a lot of incredible impressions.



Resort infrastructure

Many tourists are unhappy with the prices and quality of service in the village. Most cafes and shops put up exorbitant prices for no reason. Of course, you should not go to the New World if you are waiting for such a service as in Turkey or Egypt. But if you want to be alone with nature and just enjoy the enchanting views - feel free to go to the Crimea, the New World. Rest in 2013 promises to fill you with happiness and peace.

There is another unpleasant moment, which is indicated by vacationers who visited the New World, Crimea. Traveler reviews say that this village is unlikely to be suitable for youth recreation. In the New World, there is only one disco, which works up to a maximum of 2 a.m. To have fun, young people usually travel to Sudak.

If all these inconveniences are trifles for you, then the New World is the resort for you.
