Gelendzhik, water park "Golden Bay". Prices and working hours

The water park operates from the beginning of June to the end of September, daily from 9 am to 7 pm. Ticket prices change throughout the season and peak in mid-summer. The cost of an adult ticket in July and August for a full day is 1200 rubles. Children - 650 rubles. For half a day, from 12 hours before closing time - 1000 and 400 rubles, respectively. In June and September, the tariff is reduced by 200 rubles for adults and by 100 rubles for children.
