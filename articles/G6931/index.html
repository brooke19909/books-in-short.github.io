Sergios Hotel 3 * (Crete, Hersonissos): description of rooms, service, reviews

The resort town of Hersonissos, located on the northern coast of the largest Greek island of Crete, is known not only for its rich history dating back to more than one millennium, but also for its vibrant nightlife. Apparently therefore, there is always an accumulation of youth resting in an atmosphere of carefree fun. Hersonissos on the island has a very good location: it is located only twenty-five kilometers from Heraklion, the capital of Crete, where the international airport is located.

The resort is washed by the turquoise waters of Malia Bay, hidden between the Aegean and Mediterranean Seas. Due to the fact that Hersonissos is located on a hill, tourists have the opportunity to enjoy breathtaking views. The city has many hotels of various categories. For some time now, Russians have chosen three- and four-star accommodation options, for example, Sergios Hotel. Crete has long been chosen by our compatriots as a destination convenient for spending a youth beach holiday. And this hotel is perfectly suited for this.



general information

Sergios Hotel 3 * was built in 1998. Since then, it has been repaired more than once. The last renovation was carried out in 2015. This modern hotel combines comfort with a carefree atmosphere. Sergios Hotel has a compact cozy area. It is located on the main street of the small resort town of Hersonissos. Within walking distance from it there are many cafes and taverns, inexpensive souvenir shops. Vacationers can independently get to Star BeachWater Park - an amusement park. Tour operators recommend this hotel, both for active family vacations, and for youth.

Nutrition

Sergios Hotel (Hersonissos) operates on an all-inclusive basis. Breakfast can be provided in three types: from 07:30 to 10:00 in the main restaurant, early continental (04: 00-07: 30) and late (10: 00-11: 00). Of the drinks in the morning, cocoa, filtered coffee, milk, espresso and cappuccino, juices (except freshly squeezed), tea and water from the apparatus are offered. Buffet lunch is available from 12:30 to 14:30 in the main restaurant. From 11 a.m. to 6 p.m. in the snack bar located by the pool, you can get a variety of snacks, as well as toasts, pizza, popcorn and ice cream. Coffee, tea and cookies are freely available in the main bar from 16:00 to 17:30. Dinner runs from 19:00 to 21:30 in the main restaurant. For children

Sergios Hotel, the service in which is quite limited for children, is offered by tour operators primarily to those tourists who come for outdoor activities. For younger customers, this hotel offers only a children's section in the pool, a cot in the room, available upon request, and a highchair.

Paid service

A massage service is available on site. If desired, residents can play billiards, electronic games in the Internet cafe, as well as engage in water sports - banana, parasurfing, water cycling and skiing - on the beach. All services aimed at facial and body care at this hotel are provided at an additional cost.

The same can be said of excursions. You can buy tours both in the office working at the hotel and on the street in numerous offices. The cost of excursions is different: for example, you can go to the neighboring island of Corfu for 60 euros, to the junction of the three seas for 150, and a safari jeep throughout Crete with lunch on the Libyan Sea for 80 cu.

Free service

On the territory of Sergios Hotel 3 * there is a small pool with fresh water. Sunbeds and umbrellas around it can be borrowed for free. Towels should be paid extra. You can also play darts and table tennis at the hotel.

Beach

Sergios Hotel does not have its own swimming area. The beach is at a distance of about one hundred and fifty meters. It is urban, so the use of trestle beds and umbrellas must be paid separately. Everything is provided for a comfortable pastime, there are also showers and a toilet. The coating is pebble and sand, therefore, judging by the reviews, the presence of special rubber shoes is necessary. Those who did not bring with them should not despair, because it can be purchased at any nearby store, including the hotel. Paid is also a beach bar.





Sergios Hotel: reviews

Many Russians share their impressions that initially, even before arriving at this hotel, they were very worried about the low category of the Sergios hotel. But their fears on the spot immediately dissipated. Moreover, many regretted not buying a longer tour. The ratio of value to the quality of the service offered is simply amazing.

The rooms are comfortable. Many Russians liked the presence of a balcony from which an excellent panorama opened. The furniture is new, the plumbing works fine. The staff, judging by the reviews, works great. Everything is clean, bedding just shines, towels are changed even in some cases twice a day.

As for food, there are also no complaints. Tasty, healthy and varied - this is how our compatriots who have already had time to relax at Sergios Hotel speak about food. I especially liked the pastries, in particular cakes. In general, there are practically no complaints about the hotel itself, except perhaps about its small territory, as well as the lack of any entertainment on site. Therefore, many advise young people to come here, who will spend most of their time in the city.





The discontent among our compatriots was caused by a group transfer. And although Sergios Hotel is only 25 kilometers from the Heraklion airport, guests arrived at the hotel only forty minutes later. The bus was stuffy, and there were a lot of people in it. But this is a minus of the host.

In the end, we can say that the vast majority of Russians hope to return to this hotel yet to spend their holidays just as well.
