Credit card "Tinkoff Platinum": conditions, design, reviews

Tinkoff Bank Joint Stock Company is a financial company that specializes in remote servicing. The first and only banking institution operating without its own banking terminals and offices. The key areas of the organization when working with individuals are Tinkoff Platinum debit and credit cards.





Tinkoff Platinum Credit Card

Tinkoff Platinum credit card is a classic financial product with a grace period. It is convenient to use every day.

Tinkoff Platinum Credit Card - conditions:

  • The interest-free period is 55 days.
  • The maximum limit in rubles is 300 thousand.
  • Monthly payment - 8% of the amount spent, but not less than 600 rubles.
  • Annual maintenance of the main / additional credit card - 590 rubles each.
  • Participation in the insurance program - 0.89% of the amount owed.
  • SMS notification - 59 rubles every month.

Step-by-step instructions for obtaining a credit card

You do not know how to issue Tinkoff Platinum? To get it, you need to perform a few simple steps:

  • Fill out and send an application on the official portal of the bank (passport information will be required).
  • Wait for a decision that is made in a few minutes. The applicant may be called from the bank if there are additional questions.
  • If the decision is positive, the card will be delivered by the bank employee at a convenient place and time. Delivery is carried out within one week.

The credit card is transferred to the client in an inactive state, that is, you cannot pay for goods and services, nor can you withdraw cash from it. In order to activate an account, you need:

  • Call the hotline number or do it on the site.
  • After activation, you need to save the PIN code.
  • Put a signature on the "plastic".

Now the card is ready to go.

Interest free period

The grace / interest-free period is the period during which it is required to pay the amount of the debt so as not to overpay interest on the use of borrowed funds. For a Tinkoff Platinum credit card, this period is 55 days.

The countdown of the interest-free period does not begin from the day of spending the funds from the card account, but from the moment of receiving the next monthly statement. For example, if a client received it on May 11, then the grace period will be valid until July 4 (55 days). The monthly period from May 11 to June 11 is provided for spending funds from a credit account, from June 11 to July 3 - for debt repayment.

After the operation is completed, the owners of the Tinkoff Platinum card have 120 days to deposit funds to pay off the debt when interest will not be charged.

How to deposit funds to the card

The direction of Tinkoff Bank is remote work with clients. In addition to the fact that the organization does not have its own offices, they also do not have ATMs. But this is not a problem for closing credit card debt, because the organization has a whole network of partners, which includes 350 thousand payment points with the possibility of depositing money.

The simplest option to recharge a Tinkoff Platinum card (according to reviews) is a debit card of this or any other bank. In addition, it is possible to replenish the account by bank transfer or by depositing cash through payment terminals. All these methods do not imply payment of a bank commission, but in some cases it will be necessary to pay a partner, therefore, in order to save money, it is worth examining the conditions before the operation.

In addition, do not forget about the timing of crediting money. Transferring from one card to another or depositing funds through the terminal is usually done in a few minutes, but a bank transfer can take several days. This circumstance should be taken into account in order to prevent late payment.





Card financial transactions

The procedure for transferring funds to a bank card with a Tinkoff Platinum credit limit can be performed in different ways:

  • Bank transaction. Before sending money in this way, you need to make sure that the selected financial institution does not charge a transaction fee and is really a Tinkoff partner of the bank. There are companies that charge fees, but do so purely symbolically. For example, Avangard Bank charges 10 rubles for a bank transfer, regardless of the amount.
  • Transfer from a card of another bank. Tinkoff Bank itself does not charge a commission for such a procedure, but the other party may charge a fee for the service. This point should be clarified before sending money. When sending funds from a Tinkoff debit card to a credit operation, it is executed instantly and without any fees. Internet banking has a separate module for conducting transfers.
  • Transfer through partner banks. The full list of partners is published on the official website of the organization, in the Internet bank, as well as in the mobile application. Transfer fees are most often not charged, but there are exceptions. For the operation, it is necessary to provide a loan agreement number, sometimes a passport is required.

In addition, you can transfer funds to any other from a Tinkoff Platinum credit card, but you will need to pay a fee of 2.9% of the transfer amount plus 290 rubles for the transaction. Since the interest-free period extends solely to payment for purchases, then interest on spending credit funds will be accrued.

Once all debts have been closed for credit, you should block your credit card if you do not intend to use it in the future.
