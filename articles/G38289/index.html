Hotel Sueno Hotels Beach Side 5 *: reviews and photos of tourists

Turkey is one of the most popular tourist countries, as it offers a comfortable beach holiday at relatively low prices. One of the most popular resort cities in the country is Side. It is here, almost on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, that Hotel Sueno Hotels Beach Side is located - a luxurious hotel complex offering high-quality service and decent living conditions.

So where exactly is the hotel located in the city? What do the rooms look like and what quality of accommodation can you expect? Are there any conditions for a fun holiday with children? How do travelers respond about this place? This information is sought by many tourists planning a trip to Turkey in the near future.

Where is the hotel complex located?





The luxurious Sueno Hotels Beach Side is located on the seashore, about one kilometer from Side. Not so far is another famous resort town called Manavgat - the distance to it is about 10 kilometers.

But the international airport is 60 km away, in the city of Antalya. You should not worry about the distance, as the hotel and travel agencies provide their customers with a convenient transfer. Nevertheless, it will take several hours to get to the hotel.

Photo and description of rooms in the main building





The main building of the hotel has 606 rooms of the following categories:

  • 495 standard rooms;
  • 7 suites (there is a separate bedroom and living room);
  • 95 family rooms (a bedroom separated by a door and a spacious living room with soft folding furniture, there are also two-bedroom rooms for a large family);
  • 1 king suite
  • 8 rooms suitable for guests with reduced mobility.

Regardless of the category, you will find spacious bright rooms with balconies or private terraces where you can relax and enjoy garden views. The floors are carpeted. There is central air conditioning, a small safe, and a TV on which you can watch almost all Russian satellite channels. Welcome minibars are waiting for you in the minibar with cooling system upon arrival. Their stock is replenished daily.

The bathroom is spacious, has a toilet, shower and washbasin. It will be convenient to have a hair dryer for drying hair. Also, guests are provided with the required number of towels and branded cosmetic products from the hotel. Cleaning is carried out every day.

What do villas offer?





Immediately it is worth saying that each villa has a small cozy garden and a private pool. In total, the villas offer 134 standard rooms and 20 family rooms (consist of two bedrooms).

The floors are covered with ceramic tiles, there is a necessary set of furniture. From the equipment in the room there is a TV, air conditioning, safe with electronic lock, telephone. There is also a minibar, which is filled every day, during cleaning. The rooms on the ground floor have a terrace, while the second floor has spacious balconies. The bathroom always has hot and cold water.

Turkey, Sueno Hotels Beach Side 5 *: beach holidays

Naturally, they primarily go to Turkey for a fun holiday on the seashore. The territory of Hotel Sueno Hotels Beach Side goes directly to the beach - you can walk to it in a few minutes. The beach is clean, covered with soft warm sand. There is a convenient entrance to the sea, although it is worth buying special beach shoes. Also built a long pier for those wishing to swim at a depth or just sit, enjoying the soft sound of the waves.





Plank beds with soft mattresses and umbrellas are on the shore in sufficient numbers. Once a day, you can get a beach towel for free . Here, on the beach, there is a bar where guests are offered light snacks and cold drinks.

Water activities for tourists

Of course, fans of more active pastime in the hotel complex Sueno Hotels Beach Side will not be bored. For a small fee, you can rent a boat, go canoeing and catamarans, get a dose of adrenaline while walking on a boat and water ski, and just go for a swim with a mask while enjoying the beauty of the underwater world.

A windsurfing school is organized at the hotel. If you have a license, then here you can easily pick up a board and rent other necessary things. If you do not have experience in such a sport, then the staff will definitely offer to take some private lessons from a professional surfer.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?

Since many parents prefer to travel with children, the question of what the Sueno Hotels Beach Side Hotel has to offer their children becomes important. Reviews of tourists, as well as information from travel agents, indicate that this place is adapted primarily for the rest of parents with children of different ages.

For starters, it's worth discussing amenities. An extra bed is provided in the room, and a high chair is provided at the restaurant. The menu has a huge number of dishes that will appeal to the child, regardless of age.

In terms of entertainment, Hotel Sueno Hotels Beach Side will also not disappoint. There is an outdoor pool in the courtyard. In cold weather, you can swim in the children's indoor pool with a heating system - there is even a closed section for babies and their mothers. Older children will surely like water slides - there are nine children's attractions here, and for different ages.

There is a spacious playground with swings, slides, sandboxes and houses. For children aged 4 to 12 years, a mini club is open every day. There is also a junior club for teens from 12 to 16 years old. Here teachers and animators spend time with the children, organizing various games and performances. There is even a large amusement park with swings and other attractions. If necessary, the child can be left with a professional nanny.

Leisure and entertainment on site





You are unlikely to be bored on site, as there is everything you need for a truly eventful vacation. For example, there are four outdoor pools, and in the evenings and in cold weather, tourists relax by the indoor, heated water.

The hotel has a large spa area, which offers a variety of services, including massage, sauna, solarium, Turkish bath, peeling and skin rejuvenation. The presence of a large gym with modern equipment will also please. Group fitness classes are regularly held at the fitness center.

On the territory there are also five tennis courts with a hard surface, basketball, football and mini-golf courts, table tennis and billiards. There is a small cinema. This is not all Sueno Hotels Beach Side has to offer. Turkey is a country with a rich history, ancient traditions and a huge number of interesting sights.
