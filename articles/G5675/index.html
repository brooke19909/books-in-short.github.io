Knitting for women: photo with description

Do you want to occupy yourself with something in your free time? And do not know what? Then try knitting. In this matter, besides desire, great patience is also important. What if you like it? After all, models for women, knitted, will become an original addition to the image or the main decoration of the wardrobe. And then knitting will become your hobby.

Knitting for women (with a description of the process)

Knitting should begin with a selection of knitting needles and threads. Knitting needles come in different sizes - short, long, complete and individually, there are also circular knitting needles. Even in knitting use markers, a hook and a needle. And how beautiful the names of the patterns sound - pearls, arana, jacquard. From some names it is already curious how these patterns look. There is an interest in trying to connect them.

Newbie Tips

A beginner needs to train in a set of loops, then in knitting the front and back loops, how to close the loops. You can tie a small probe - a square of 10 x 10 cm. Such probes (they are called samples) are usually knitted to calculate the loops when they are going to create any thing. Only after that you can try to knit a scarf.

Having become skilled in this matter, they are moving on to a more complex technique - knitting braids, tows. And also they try to knit with crochets, to knit several from one loop.

If everything works out for you, then you can try knitting a vest or sweater. It will not be difficult to even make a skirt. When you have more practice, you can tie footprints or socks. And when you become a master of your craft, then you will be able to connect not only yourself and your family, but also your friends. Remember that knitting requires patience, perseverance and attention.

Knitting for women: fashion models

Cape poncho is a comfortable and cozy thing that many people like because of its practicality and convenience. For women, knitting a poncho cape is not an easy task, especially for beginners. This can be an elongated and short poncho, large or small knitting - all in the hands of the master.

Knitted pullover is a fashionable and ageless wardrobe element of all fashionistas and lovers of comfort. Knitting of such a model for women can be found in special needlework magazines or on thematic resources.

Cardigan is one of the fashionable models of knitting for women, a practical and irreplaceable wardrobe item. For knitting a cardigan, wool or half-wool is ideal. It is also important to consider that in 2018 patch pockets and voluminous long sleeves are in fashion.

Knitted hat - not only retain heat, but also emphasize the image. In 2018, at the peak of fashion, hats with large viscous. A knitted hat can emphasize a business or casual look. If there is still not enough experience to create it, then with a fashionable bandage there should not be global problems.

Knitted sweater is a warm element of the wardrobe, which easily combines with its other elements. The variety of models will allow you to choose the perfect option for yourself, emphasize the merits and hide all the flaws. Large knitting and a long sleeve are the trend of 2018, do not forget this.

The shortened model of the sweater is called a crop sweater. It is suitable for any figure, and especially for the figure "triangle" (with narrow hips and broad shoulders). Also, free models are still in demand.

Knit for children

Knitting is an ideal activity for women. It soothes, helps to save on winter things. Children and family are the highest value in the life of any person. Often, grandmothers and mothers for grandchildren and children knit the first booties in their lives, overalls, hats, mittens, putting love, tenderness and care into their products.

Yarn selection

For a baby, you need to knit things from a certain yarn. Comfort and warmth - this is what is important for a newborn. Moms always care about the naturalness of materials, so cotton yarn is an excellent choice. This yarn almost does not stretch, designed for tight knitting, but the baby is comfortable in it. It does not fade under the sun, it is painted with high quality in bright colors, but it is not easy to knit products from such yarn. They also use yarn half made of cotton and half made of acrylic or viscose.

The most common yarn for knitting children's things is wool. Woolen fiber is lighter and more flexible. Unfortunately, there is a minus of this yarn - it often rolls. And mohair or Angora cannot be used on products for babies.

Sometimes knitted from silk yarn. This yarn is very light, thin and stretches well. No spools are formed on the finished products, and the material is pleasant to the body. Knitting with needles is very simple, quick and easy, you can learn even the most from video lessons or master classes.

Types of products for children

A woman who is waiting for a baby begins to knit booties, blouses, hats, overalls for him. Sometimes they even knit an envelope or kit for a newborn's discharge. It must be remembered that the baby spends a lot of time in the crib, stroller and arms, so there should be no iron zippers or rough seams on the clothes.

The child should feel comfortable and free. Today there will be no difficulties to buy yarn of any color and texture. Manufacturers always print on the label the composition of the yarn, the length in meters, the thread consumption and the size of the knitting needles or hook suitable for the particular yarn.

From thin threads, products turn out to be openwork, lighter. But this lesson will take more time. It is better to store accessories and tools for knitting in covers. You can knit from a thin thread, but we must remember that the consumption with this knitting will increase significantly.

It has become fashionable to bring tied bonnets or booties, and sometimes even whole blankets or envelopes, to the discharge from the hospital.

Every mommy wants to dress a newborn in all the most fashionable and beautiful. Now very common children's knitwear, knitted with their own hands. Yes, and yarn is in any color scheme. The choice is huge, and you can dress up babies, starting with diapers.

Muzzle caps of animals and just with ears are now at the peak of popularity. Also, baptismal sets, rugs, a hat with a scarf and gloves, and much more brought a variety to fashion. Of course, before dressing the baby in knitwear, they must be washed or treated with hot steam.

Knitting is not just a hobby, it positively affects both physical health and emotional comfort, it also allows you to show your creative thinking, to add variety to a simple scheme.
