Hedef Beach Resort & SPA 5 * (Turkey / Alanya): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Alanya has long been one of the most popular Turkish resorts among residents of Russia and other CIS countries. This is not surprising, because local hotels of different stars have a developed infrastructure and are pleased to offer their guests a comfortable and interesting vacation. Today we offer a closer look at one of the five-star hotels in Alanya. It is located in the resort village of Konakli and is called Hedef Beach Resort & SPA 5 *. We offer to find out what the hotel is and what services it provides to its guests. We will also find out what impression our compatriots left on living in it.





Hedef Beach Resort & SPA 5 *: photo of the hotel and its location

This hotel complex has a very good location on the first coastline in the resort village of Konakli. The beach here is literally a few tens of meters. The distance to the nearest major city - Alanya is only 12 kilometers. From the international airport of Antalya, the village of Konakli is a hundred kilometers away. Thus, the journey from the air harbor to the Hedef Beach Resort & SPA 5 * hotel will take you about an hour and a half.

Entertainment





On the territory of the hotel, guests have at their disposal an outdoor pool and an adjacent sunbathing terrace, as well as an indoor pool where you can swim in any weather. Hedef Beach Resort hotel and SPA 5 *: 2014 reviews

Many tourists decide on the choice of a hotel, including on the basis of the impressions of travelers who have already stayed here. Therefore, we bring to your attention the generalized reviews of our compatriots who spent their holidays in Hedef Beach Resort & SPA in 2014.

In general, tourists found this hotel appropriate for its price and declared star rating. Many guests appreciated the friendly and efficient staff, well-decorated clean rooms and a large swimming pool with slides, which not only kids but also adults enjoyed riding.

As for the beach, opinions are divided here. Someone was very pleased with the comfortable awning, the proximity of the sea and clean water. Someone was disappointed that garbage was constantly visible in the sand. However, the latter fact is associated not with the sluggishness of local cleaners, but with the lack of manners of some vacationers who throw cigarette butts and other garbage directly onto the ground, without bothering to walk a couple of meters to the ballot box.

All guests who came to rest with families appreciated the children's animation at the hotel. So, according to them, pleasant young people entertained the children from morning till night, and the kids were delighted with this. As for adult animation, most travelers would rate it as a solid "four."
