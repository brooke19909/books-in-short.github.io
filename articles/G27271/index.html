How to get to Abkhazia in different ways

Abkhazia is a fairly narrow strip of land between the sea in the south and the mountain slopes of the Caucasus Range in the north. This is an amazing place with a mild and comfortable climate for a person from the middle strip. The mountains are covered here with dense relict coniferous forests, approaching very close to the sea itself. By a whim of geographical circumstances, the subtropics closest to Russia are located precisely on this small strip of the Black Sea coast. The question of how to get to Abkhazia is interesting to a large number of tourists from many Russian regions. Their flow in this direction is growing steadily every year. This small country lives mainly due to the reception of tourists, and therefore all visitors are welcome here. The general price level is one of the lowest among all resorts of the Black Sea coast. The main tourist infrastructure has been preserved here since the Soviet era. In recent years, much has been done for its restoration and development.



How to get to Abkhazia from different cities of Russia

To get to Abkhazia, you first have to get to the city of Sochi. By air you will find yourself at the international airport of the famous resort, and by rail it is best to go to the Adler station, which is territorially part of Greater Sochi. From Adler, eight kilometers have to be overcome to the Psou River, which is the natural border of Abkhazia. A border checkpoint operates around the clock. When planning a vacation on this coast and figuring out how to get to Abkhazia, it should be borne in mind that entry into this sovereign country is visa-free for citizens of the Russian Federation. You only need to have a passport with you, no matter - domestic or international. And a small amount of money for health insurance. Passing border and customs formalities usually takes about an hour, depending on the crowds at the checkpoint. On both sides of the border, taxi drivers are at your service ready to deliver a passenger to almost anywhere in a small country. The question of how to get to Abkhazia also includes the endpoint on its territory that you are trying to get to. It is best to decide on this geographical name in advance and call it a taxi driver.



Abkhazia: how to get by train to its capital

You can do this by train, which every morning leaves from Adler in the direction of Sukhumi. And in the afternoon, more often, an additional train leaves for Gagra station. The schedule here sometimes changes depending on the number of passengers.



Abkhazia: how to get from Moscow without transfers

There are a number of interesting features in this matter. You can get to Sukhumi without transfers from Moscow from the Kursk station by train number 75/76. Only according to the schedule, he does not go there. The ticket should be taken to the Vesyoloye station in the last cars. And already on the way at the conductor you should buy an additional ticket to Sukhumi. The surcharge is from 300 to 600 rubles, depending on the type of car. Parking and customs clearance at the border takes about an hour.
