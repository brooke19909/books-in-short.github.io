Figures of Warhammer 40,000. Miniatures, figurines of the Primarchs

Surprisingly, board games are still popular in the digital and VR era. And those who left not far from the primitive soldiers, but considered to be an expensive pleasure. It's about the Warhammer 40,000 techno-fantasy project, the figures for which (together with painting) cost a lot of money.

Thumbnails

An integral component of any desktop is the soldiers. There are a great many of these in Warhammer. The first game was published in 1987 thanks to the efforts of the Games Workshop company, and was greatly loved by the fans thanks to a well-thought-out balance and a bewitching atmosphere.

But in fact, most of the fans were attracted due to the detailed elaboration of miniatures. Each formation, looking from the covers of the rule book, entices with unique characteristics. The Warhammer 40,000 figures are so bright, colorful and sometimes brutal, that an outsider is unlikely to believe that all this was created by the hands of talented masters who have a good command of the brush.

Buying Primarch Figures

In Warhammer 40,000 there are characters whose images have a place not on the cells of the playing field, but on the collector's shelf. It's about the leaders of the Astartes legions.

The prices are impressive, for example, a miniature of Mortarion will cost about 6800 rubles. A figurine of Lorgar, Primarch of the Legion Bearers legion, is not very numerous, but the price is about 7000-8000 r.

We are talking about ready-made figures of Warhammer 40,000. The primarch of the "World Eaters" Angron in human form exhibited for about 8000 p. Reincarnated Magnus Red - 6000 r. Prices may vary depending on the point of sale.

Verdict

Warhammer 40,000 figures are popular with desktop gamers who can afford such an expensive pleasure. Otherwise, you have to do with paper analogues of your own production.

Or even go into reading books. In any case, connoisseurs of Warhammer 40,000 figures will always be found. It is a pity that Primarch miniatures are rarely released.
