Kaya Maris 4 * (Turkey / Marmaris) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Turkish resorts receive tourists all year round thanks to the excellent service, low cost of living, a special food system and an extensive entertainment program. Hotel Kaya Maris 4 in Marmaris offers excellent conditions to those vacationers who are configured to receive positive emotions. Boat trips and sightseeing, beach vacations, extreme sports and even skiing - choose what you like!

Location

Hotel Kaya Maris 4 is located in the small village of Siteller, near the resort town of Marmaris (3 km from its center). Dalaman Central Airport is 90 km away. The surroundings are magnificent: there are many attractions nearby, a picturesque area, a wide promenade with many bars and cafes, various shops, a supermarket and a grocery market.

Rules of residence

Check-in at Kaya Maris 4 Hotel (Marmaris) starts at 14.00. Documents for departure are drawn up before noon (12.00). Of course, the staff goes to meet the vacationers, because it is not always convenient to wait for a settlement if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning. Employees try to quickly solve the issue of determining the number so that tourists can feel comfortable.

The hotel does not allow pets. The tourist reception service for various issues works around the clock. If for some reason the number does not suit you, you can ask the employee at the reception to replace it.

Among the vacationers there are not many Russians. Almost half of the tourists are residents of Sweden, Ireland and England, however, there are Germans and Turks. The staff does not speak Russian, and therefore knowledge of English will not hurt tourists. The staff takes a tip gratefully. Salaries of maids, bartenders and waiters are small, but for a small fee you will be treated more carefully. But do not spoil too much, a few liras or $ 1-2.

Service

Kaya Maris Hotel 4 provides both free and paid services. At no extra charge, you can visit the gym or aerobics classes, play mini-golf, table tennis, and billiards. Wireless internet is available in the lobby. There is a TV room, there is a library, parking is available for residents and their guests. Vacationers can use the pool (separately for adults and children), sun loungers and umbrellas are provided by the hotel, towels need to be rented or your own.

Laundry and ironing services are available at a surcharge. There is a hairdresser and massage room. Car rental is provided. There is a sauna and hammam. About the latter in more detail. The service costs $ 15 and includes the following: sit in a steam room for 20 minutes, then a hammam worker cleanses your skin with a special glove (the procedure resembles scrubbing), then shower and massage for about 20 minutes. In general, the entire course lasts about 1.5 hours. Additionally, you can pay for mud masks, honey massage and herbal tea. In principle, not bad.

The hotel has a doctor, but he is called in case of emergency. At the reception you can rent a paid safe for storing valuables. If parents want to spend time together or for a short time, you can use the services of a private nanny working at the hotel. When calculating use cash or credit cards MasterCard and Visa.

Nutrition

The traditional food option in many Turkish hotels is the All Inclusive system. Marmaris Kaya Maris 4 is no exception. The system is valid from 10.00 to 23.00. For breakfast, lunch and dinner - buffet meals.

They offer many vegetables and fruits (grapes, oranges, apples, melons and watermelons). It can be whole fruits, slices or a variety of salads. Breakfast includes yoghurts, granola, cheese, cold cuts, and pancakes or delicious egg omelettes can be prepared at the request of guests. At lunch, the menu is more nutritious and expanded: mashed soups, meat dishes, sausages, rabbit, beef and chicken, boiled rice and vegetables. Several times a week cook fish fried in batter. Grilled chicken with vegetables is often served for dinner. Striking a wide selection of desserts.

Kaya Maris 4 serves mainly local drinks (this applies to the all-inclusive system). You can order wines (red, pink and white), light beer, as well as brandy and gin. The quality of the drinks is not bad, and the food is excellent. Fresh can be ordered from 8.00 to 21.00, ice cream is served from 10.00 to 20.30 (optional). Imported drinks are expensive.

There are 2 restaurants: Therfore, which serves dishes from different cuisines, and Cordon Blue, a restaurant dominated by Turkish cuisine.

Especially for guests of the hotel there is an indoor air-conditioned room and an outdoor terrace. The smallest visitors to the restaurant are provided with a highchair. There is a poolside bar where you can also have a bite to eat. There is a lobby bar in the lobby, there is a snack bar serving light snacks from 16.30 to 17.30.

Description of rooms

IN Kaya Maris Hotel 4 (Marmaris) 120 standard rooms are equipped. The area is small, 20-23 m 2 . Maximum occupancy in the room: 2 adults and 1 child. 65 rooms are equipped with 1 single bed, 55 rooms have 2 single beds. You can install an extra bed.

If necessary, order a crib for a small child. The balcony offers a wonderful view of the garden or the sea (it is advisable to occupy rooms on the 5th floor, because in front of the Kaya Maris 4 there is also a hotel that hides the view).

On the floor there is tile or carpet, split-air cooling system is functioning. There is a TV, Russian channels work. There is a shower room, a hairdryer, and it is better to bring bath accessories with you. Towels and bed linen are changed several times a week, room cleaning - daily. For an additional fee, you can order a safe in your room, telephone and ask to fill a minibar. The hotel has 1 room, specially equipped for the disabled. The cost of living starts at 1900 rubles per night.

Excursions

You will be guaranteed a great experience if you stay at Kaya Maris Hotel 4. The reviews are mostly positive. But the most interesting thing is that you can book excursions not only with tour operators, but also with local agencies in Marmaris. It is much more profitable and completely safe. In addition, employees of local agencies often reward their customers by issuing free invitations to evening walks by sea. True, for this you need to order several excursions.

So, about $ 100 is a trip to Ephesus-Pamukkale, designed for 2 days.

Check out early at 6.30 (by the way, you can take breakfast with you in the restaurant and eat on the way). Be sure to take bottled water: a tour of the ancient city ‚Äã‚Äãof Ephesus lasts more than 2 hours. Then they drive to the monastery of the Virgin Mary, and then to the hotel, where you can have dinner and spend the night. On the second day they drive to Pamukkale. Immediately go to the Cleopatra's pool, walk along the travertines. If you plan everything carefully, you can manage to visit the Hierapolis Amphitheater and take a couple of shots.

If you plan to relax for a long time, book a trip to Dalyan. The road is a little tiring, but for $ 30 you get a lot of pleasant experiences. In terms of inspection, the radon pool, acquaintance with a huge turtle and Lycian tombs. The area is well known for its rare blue crabs. On the way back, you will have the opportunity to try their meat for $ 9. Do not deny yourself the pleasure, be sure to order this dish.

An excursion to the Aegean islands will cost $ 20.

Tourists are taken by boat to the sea, to the islands. They are allowed to swim and dive, feed, serve water, beer and wine. You can visit Cappadocia, where there are many cave monasteries, visit the ancient ruins of Mira and the ancient city of Knidos. Some directions can be mastered independently if you rent a car at the hotel.

Where to buy gifts and souvenirs?

Sweets can be purchased at nearby stores. If you ask, then sellers may allow you to try the product and pack it when you buy. Souvenirs are best purchased in supermarkets, as well as in small shops and shops.

By the way, during excursions, especially in Ephesus-Pamukkale, they constantly offer to visit some factories where they produce sweets and souvenirs. As a rule, the prices here are overpriced, and the quality does not differ at all from what they sell at retail in the city. Pay attention to small shopping facilities: here, as a rule, adequate prices for souvenirs, magnets and other trifles.

Hotel Pros

In conclusion, I want to summarize and highlight the features of the hotel Kaya Maris 4. Reviews tourists will help to do this most accurately.

  • Excellent cuisine and a wide range of dishes.
  • Polite, helpful and helpful staff.
  • The rooms are comfortable, regular cleaning.
  • Vacationers are mainly foreigners aged 45-55 years. Whether this is an advantage or a disadvantage should be decided by the tourists themselves, but the hotel is more suitable for a relaxing family vacation.

Minuses

  • Small rooms.
  • Unsatisfactory work of plumbing: either the taps are leaking or the toilet.
  • There are few animation programs in the hotel itself.
  • There is no private beach.
  • The staff does not speak Russian.

Despite all the pros and cons, most tourists are inclined to believe that the hotel fully justifies its 4 stars. There are those who do not agree, but there are much fewer of them.
