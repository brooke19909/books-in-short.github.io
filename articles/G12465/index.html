Installation



To solve the issue of how to install iOS 8 on iPhone 4, there are two ways. You can achieve the desired result using Wi-Fi on the smartphone itself, or by accessing iTunes on the computer. Now we will take a closer look at how to install iOS 8 on iPhone 4 through a wireless connection. This approach can be considered the most simple. However, it cannot be considered one hundred percent effective. Another problem is the long download time via Wi-Fi of the firmware, the size of which is 1.1 GB. Also, before starting the action, you should take into account the battery level. His indicator should not be below the mark of 50%. If this condition is not met, the battery may be discharged directly during the installation of the update, and the smartphone will turn off. In this case, you will need to connect the device to the computer and restore its operation through iTunes. To perform the update (if all of the above conditions are met), you will need to perform a series of actions. You must first connect your iPhone to Wi-Fi. It is better to check the possibility of accessing the Internet, for example, through Safari. Next, go to "Settings". Select the item "Basic". We use the "Software Update" section. Select the "Download and Install" function. Downloading is automatic. When the data is received, select ‚ÄúInstall‚Äù. Next, an agreement is to be adopted.
