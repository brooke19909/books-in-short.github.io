Hotel Fidan, Sochi: address, description of rooms, service, reviews

Sochi is a famous resort on the territory of which a large number of hotels have been built. Therefore, tourists are offered a wide selection of accommodation options. One of the hotel complexes worthy of attention is the Fidan Hotel in Sochi.

Location

Mini-hotel "Fidan" in Sochi has a very convenient location. It is just a three-minute walk from Kurortny Prospekt. The airport is 32 km away and the train station is 3 km away.

Walking distance to the Winter Theater, Seaport and Central Museum. Guests only need a few minutes to reach the sea and the beach. The convenient location makes the hotel a convenient place to stay.

The hotel complex is good not only for those tourists who come on vacation, but also for people who have arrived on a business trip. The hotel is open all year round, unlike many other establishments operating seasonally. The hotel rooms are heated, therefore suitable for placement in the cold season.

Description of rooms at the Fidan Hotel in Sochi

The mini-hotel has only 15 rooms. All of them are made in bright colors and have an unusual design.

Hotel rooms are presented in the following categories:

  1. Double Room. There is a TV, air conditioning, a minibar and an electric kettle. The bathroom is equipped with toiletries, bathrobes and a hairdryer.
  2. Deluxe This room features a balcony and a safety deposit box.
  3. Exclusive Suite - this apartment has a balcony, satellite channels, TV, safe, electric kettle and minibar. The room is spacious, designed by designers.
  4. Two-room deluxe suite is made in warm colors. It is equipped with a TV, air conditioning, minibar, satellite channels, safe. The bathroom includes toiletries and a hairdryer. Spacious apartment with a bedroom and a living room with a dining area.
  5. Royal Suite is also done in bright colors. It is equipped with every comfort. Among other things, there are two bathrooms. The living room has a dining area with all necessary furniture and utensils. Luxury apartments are quite large.

Restaurant "Fidan"

The hotel has a restaurant, which was given the same name. Within its walls, guests can taste the dishes of Azerbaijani cuisine, because it is considered the oldest in Asia. Its characteristic features, according to experts, took shape a millennium before the beginning of our era. Over the next centuries, traditions were formed and stored, and therefore survived to this day.

In the restaurant of the Fidan Hotel in Sochi you can taste dishes that have absorbed the ancient traditions of the East. The skilled chefs of the establishment prepare excellent food from poultry, vegetables and fruits. Guests should definitely try the Baku cuisine, which cannot but please. The taste of her delicious dishes is remembered forever.

In the restaurant you can taste more than 30 varieties of barbecue, eggplant snacks, desserts and much more. Azerbaijani desserts are no less beautiful - baklava, jam, dates. No one can refuse such a treat.

The restaurant of the Fidan Hotel in Sochi accepts orders for corporate events. There is a separate menu for organizing such events. The banquet hall of the institution can accommodate up to 45 guests. All the colors of the East are collected in its interior. Murals on the walls and Ottoman landscapes impress guests. The interiors of the room are made in golden shades. The same tones dominate the dishes. Restaurant guests are invariably fascinated by the sunny atmosphere of the East. Every Tuesday and Thursday in the institution sounds live music.

The hotel also has a bar where you can enjoy your time.

Organization of holidays

The hotel is always open for guests, including those people who have a desire to organize a banquet or some other special occasion. To do this, the institution has everything you need, including a banquet hall. The hotel accepts applications for the New Year holidays, receptions, corporate parties and other celebrations. For such events, the restaurant provides a separate menu with the best dishes of Russian and Azerbaijani cuisine.

In addition, the institution provides the ability to deliver banquet and buffet dishes at the destination. Such a service is relevant if you are celebrating some kind of celebration or corporate party in a narrow circle. At the same time, you absolutely do not need waiters and a banquet room, but you need delicious food. In this case, you just need to phone the administration of the institution and discuss the menu. Ready meals will be delivered to you at the appointed time. In addition, staff can provide cutlery, textiles, dishes and other accessories for table decoration.

When to visit Sochi?

When is the best time to go on vacation to this beautiful city? It all depends on the purpose of visiting the resort. If you are planning a beach holiday, then you should go in July, August or September. In the middle and at the end of summer, the largest number of tourists accumulate on the coast. But during this period the best weather conditions for a beach holiday are observed.

Summer in Sochi pleases with stable weather and ample opportunities for travel. The warmest weather in Sochi in August, the air temperature at this time reaches +30 degrees. And the water warms up to +27. In August, the sea is the warmest during the entire swimming season. It is for this reason that there are an incredible amount of people at the resort at this time. Tourists with small children come here to experience all the delights of a beach holiday.

Since the resort is located in the region of the subtropical climate, in autumn the temperature is set here from +14 to +16 degrees in the daytime. At night, it drops to +5 - +7 degrees. Compared to other regions of Russia, it is very warm.

Many tourists come to Sochi in the fall for sightseeing. At this time, vacation prices are much lower than in summer. If September and October are pleased with stable weather, then in November it becomes moody. Not all days are pleasing with the sun, very often it drizzles with rain, which is accompanied by strong gusts of wind. Going to the resort at the end of summer, you should have a set of warm clothes with you. Due to the proximity of the sea in the city there is a high humidity.
