Simple full-size tilde patterns

You think to sew a cute tilde, but do not know where to start? The first step is to buy a suitable fabric. And if the material is already ready? In this case, draw a pattern. If you are deprived of artistic talent or simply do not want to waste your time on this, then our article will help you a lot. It contains full-size tilde patterns.

Simple tilde

If you have never sewed toys before, then you should start with the simplest option. The full-size tilde pattern is shown above. The toy consists of only 8 parts. Her body, head and legs are all-cut, only her hands and heart are sewn separately. We print out the tilde doll pattern. It is made of bodily material. We cut out the details from it and begin to sew them together. The first thing to do is make the body. Then we sew and stuff the hands and attach them to the shoulders. Now we sew a heart. It should be cut out of any printed fabric. For example, it may be red material with white polka dots or a rosette. If it seems to you that putting a tilde in your hands is too trivial, then you can supply the doll with a bouquet of flowers.

Tilda angel

Full-legged long-legged dolls fell in love with many girls. Tildas adorn bedside tables, coffee tables, and sometimes even sofas. Naive dolls can be used for its intended purpose, that is, for the game. A full-size tilde pattern is presented above. This angel will be able to decorate any interior, if you just wear it correctly. But more on that later. We print out the tilde doll pattern. The best option would be to place the pattern on two A4 sheets. Thus, you will not get a miniature toy, but a full-fledged doll. If you do not know how to scale the image on the computer, you can do it by hand. When the pattern is ready, cut it out of bodily material. Well, more precisely, the body, legs and arms should be cut out of it. The wings should be made white, but choose the color of the vest yourself. The options look interesting when the tilde clothes are made of printed fabric.

Sew together the body, arms and legs. If you want the doll's limbs to bend, then in the elbows and knees you need to make additional seams already on the stuffed part. Sew legs and arms to the body, then attach the wings and dress the doll with a vest. It remains to make the toy hair from woolen threads or satin ribbons and embroider eye-dots.

Tilda hare

Such a doll looks much more original than standard girls. A cute bunny will appeal to children of both sexes. A full-size tilde pattern is attached above. It needs to be printed and cut. Now select the color of the toy. It is worth sticking to the classics and make the body in beige or sand tones. Cut out all the patterns from the material. The first step is to stitch the body. It consists of three parts. First you need to sew the side parts to the back, and only then to each other. Moreover, the toy should be gradually turned out and stuffed during sewing. Otherwise, it will then be difficult to evenly distribute the synthetic winterizer. After the body is ready, you can begin to make hands and ears. The limbs should be stuffed and sewn to the shoulders. But ears should not be stuffed. Instead of synthetic winterizer, cardboard or wire frame should be placed in the part. This is done so that the ears of the hare stand, not lie. After the toy is ready, the hare should embroider or sew eyes and brown the cheeks with pastel.
