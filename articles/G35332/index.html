Terracina, Italy: city attractions. Holidays in Terracina

The picturesque resort of Terracina (Italy) is located on the Tyrrhenian Sea and belongs to the Lazio region. The city is considered one of the most famous resorts in this region and is famous for its excellent sandy beaches. In addition to its natural resources, Terracina boasts a rich historical and cultural heritage. There are many unique structures that have survived from ancient times.



Colorful city

The city is divided into Lower and Upper Terracina and is located on the path of the famous Appian Way. In the upper part of the settlement are buildings of the medieval period, fragments of fortress walls, defensive fortified towers, as well as other structures from the time of the Roman Empire. The lower city is a modern neighborhood with a large number of shopping centers, shops, fashionable hotels and luxurious sandy beaches. The infrastructure in this area is well developed and favorable conditions for recreation are created. In addition to water sports and sunbathing, the resort offers travelers sightseeing programs, visits to discos, boat trips and many other attractions. Terracina (Italy) means beautiful and breathtaking landscapes, beautiful architecture, mild climate, clean air, which is very rich in iodine. All these riches are integral attributes of romantic Terracina.

Historical reference

Terracina existed before our era and is mentioned a couple of times in historical chronicles that date back to the time of Carthage. After the entry of this settlement into the Roman Empire, it became the largest port in the region and already in those years was a popular resort on a local scale. In the era of the Romans, the Appian Way was paved here, the largest structures were erected. According to legend, it was here that the apostle Paul met Jesus Christ, and then went to Rome in order to promote the covenants of Christianity. The Byzantine era also made a significant impact on the city, which influenced its cultural development. The status of the resort city of Terracina in Italy received in the middle of the last century. It developed in the shortest possible time and today has gained a reputation as a well-maintained tourist center in the Lazio region.



Climate

The weather conditions in this region are mild. The Mediterranean climate provides hot summers; the thermometer thermometer in the summer usually does not fall below +25 degrees. Precipitation in Terracina is usually observed from November to March. In cold weather, it usually does not fall below +6 degrees C. The swimming season at this resort is quite long - from May to October. These are the favorable conditions that Terracina (Italy) can provide. Reviews about the resort are generally positive.

Route

You can get to this luxurious resort from Naples or Rome (these cities have international airports). From there, the resort can be reached by bus or train, which constantly go in this direction. In a word, you should not have problems getting to the resort.





Beaches

Which of us does not dream of going on holiday in Italy? Terracina is a great place to spend your summer vacation with the whole family. The coastline of the resort boasts quite wide and simply chic places for a peaceful vacation. The beaches in Terracina are well-maintained - there is everything that is needed for complete comfort: sunbeds, umbrellas, showers, sports fields, changing cabins, rental points for various water vehicles, discos, etc. The most active tourists are invited to learn diving skills and dive into fascinating underwater world. If you do not want to dive deep into the deeps of the sea, you can take a jet ski with a breeze.

Sights in Terracina, Italy

The main property of Terracina is the historical district of the city. You can enter it through the majestic Neapolitan gate. On the central square of the city are theaters and temples of the Roman Republic. In this resort town you can also see charming mansions of the Middle Ages. Among all these ancient structures, St. Cesare's Cathedral stands out especially. The floor of this temple is decorated with a unique mosaic that dates from the 5th century AD.

If you have decided that Terracina (Italy) will be your vacation destination, then do not forget to admire the elegant Wenditti Castle, which is located next to the arch on the Appian Way. Opposite the palace is the ancient Tower of Roses and the Bishop's Palace. All these ancient buildings cannot but impress you, because it is simply impossible to not feel the spirit of that ancient era here. In addition to all these amazing architectural monuments, Terracina will also present to your eyes the ancient temple of Jupiter - its columns majestically rise directly on the seashore.





Water activities

Monuments of antiquity - not all that Terracina (Italy) can present to you. Sightseeing is, of course, very exciting, but I really want a little extreme! Vacationers have a great opportunity to join the water sports. You can also head to the local Miami Beach water park and play beach volleyball.

Romantics will like exciting boat trips, and for fishing lovers they organize whole tours to remote areas. There you can also swim in the open sea or even dive with scuba diving. In order to diversify your leisure time, we suggest you drop by the evening in a local restaurant or at a disco.
