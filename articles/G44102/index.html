Virginia - Sea, Nature, and Fun Laws

The state of Virginia is located in the eastern part of the United States and is the tenth in a row in the state. This is a pretty picturesque part of America - here are the Appalachian mountains, and numerous rivers, and tall trees. Anyway, I must say, there is something to see.





general information

The state capital is Richmond. Its population is only 200 thousand people. But the largest city is Virginia Beach, about half a million inhabitants live there. By the way, this is a resort city where thousands of tourists come annually to enjoy the waves of the Atlantic Ocean and the bright sun. There are a huge number of hotels and beaches, so there is where to relax. Interestingly, in the eastern part of the state, the terrain is very boggy. On the western side, this is not observed, since it is on that side that the Appalachian mountains are located - they stretch for two thousand kilometers! By the way, Virginia is on the 12th place in terms of population density (out of 51). In total, more than eight million people live there. An interesting fact is that quite a lot of Germans live in this state - more than 12%. About 20% are African-Americans, about 11% are British, 10% are Irish, and only 11.5% are Americans. At the same time, the percentage of Native Americans living is less than one! Although, if we turn to history, we can find out that originally Indian states lived on the territory of the state .

Places for rest

Virginia is a place where you can really rest nicely. And for everyone there is an occupation. For example, Virginia Beach (a city included in the Guinness Book of Records as a resort with the longest beach in the world) is a place for outdoor enthusiasts. The sea, the sun, parties, clubs, shopping centers - all this is here. Or you can go to Norfolk - a port city. And fans of a quiet pastime will like Hampton. In general, a vacation in Virginia is designed for those people who want to escape from the daily rush and enjoy the tranquility.





Main attraction

As mentioned earlier, in these places there is something to see. There really is a mass of interesting sights, but the most important is the great gloomy swamp (Virginia, USA). This is a wetland located in the southeastern part of the state on the plain. It is safe to say that this small corner is one of the last in all of America that people have not touched. This is a national park that covers almost 500 km of dense forests and water. The Great Gloomy Swamp (Virginia, USA) has a completely unique ecosystem. Unlimited water resources, rich flora and fauna, diverse nature, mysterious atmosphere - all this attracts the attention of tourists and local residents. By the way, the Great Swamp is included in the list of unique places in the USA. Scientists have a version that it was formed due to the last shift of the continental plume.
