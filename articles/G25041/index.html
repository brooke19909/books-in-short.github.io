Sunrise Garden Beach Hotel. Hurghada. Description

Surrounded by a magnificent garden on the very shore of a sandy beach, the exclusive Sunrise Garden Beach Hotel (Hurghada) is located. It offers its guests luxurious living conditions, quality service and a host of recreational activities. Here you can admire stunning sunsets, mountains, exotic landscapes of the desert and the azure waters of the sea.

Description

Sunrise Garden Beach (Hurghada) was opened relatively recently, in 2003. The complex consists of the main building, in which the reception is located, and a complex of three-story modern buildings. The hotel is focused on family and youth recreation. It is located seven kilometers from the airport and 15 km from the tourist town of Hurghada.

Rooms

In total, the hotel has 480 apartments of various categories, decorated in accordance with the modern trends of the hotel industry: standard (45 sq. M.), Family (60 sq. M.) And Suites, consisting of two bedrooms, a kitchen and a bathroom. Their area is more than 90 square meters. All rooms are equipped with a shower room, a minibar, air conditioning, satellite TV, a safe, a telephone, a hairdryer, a balcony and Internet access (fee). Cleaning and linen change are regular.

Nutrition

Sunrise Garden Beach Hotel (Hurghada) serves its guests around the clock using the All Inclusive system. Breakfast, dinner and lunch - buffet. Throughout the day, the bars offer local soft drinks and alcoholic drinks, as well as some types of imported alcohol. In addition, tourists can visit the fish and Italian a la carte restaurants twice.

Entertainment

Sunrise Garden Beach (Hurghada) offers its guests to attend aerobics classes, a gym. Tourists can play beach volleyball, darts, billiards, bocce, football, tennis. In the evenings and in the afternoon, entertainment programs are held, high-quality live music sounds. For a fee, the following is available: a jacuzzi, a steam room, a sauna, a spa, a massage room, banana, catamaran, windsurfing, diving, camel or horse riding. Tourists can also go on a quad or jeep safari, visit the cheap markets of Hurghada or go on an excursion to Luxor and Cairo. Possible cruises in the Mediterranean Sea.

Territory

The hotel has three swimming pools, one of which is heated, water slides, currency exchange, shops, Internet cafes, taxis, parking, laundry, medical office, conference room, dry cleaning and hairdresser.

For children



For kids in the outdoor pool there are two special departments. Also, a playground, a club (for children from three to ten years old) are constantly working on the territory of the hotel. Babysitting services are available upon prior request . For the convenience of families with children, a cot for a child can be provided in the room.

Beach

The warm Red Sea, picturesque landscapes and clean sand - this is what Hurghada attracts tourists from year to year. Sunrise Garden Beach, a photo of which is presented on this page, has its own beach. It has umbrellas, towels, sun loungers and swimming mattresses. Entrance to the sea through pontoons. Near the beach are coral reefs.
