Bulgaria, Kranevo - hotels

Recently, Bulgaria has become more and more popular. Kranevo, for example, is another confirmation of this. The name of this young, but already managed to fall in love with many resort, it seems, lurks a certain distance from civilization. Perhaps this is due to a certain consonance with the Russian word "edge".

So how is it really? Who would like to rest on the coast of this country? What to expect when going there on vacation? This will be discussed in this article, but first of all, I would like to interest readers with information that enthusiastic reviews about such a direction as Bulgaria are far from rare. Kranevo high quality service only confirms their veracity.

Section 1. General description of the resort



It should be noted that this small but very cozy settlement has a very advantageous location: between the famous Golden Sands and Albena. But, compared with the neighboring resorts, the rest here is more economical and quite calm.

Hotels in Kranevo (Bulgaria) are usually surrounded by green hills and a forest. The climate is perfect for recreation with the goal of healing, both for adults and young travelers.

Here you can find many different options for accommodation: pensions, cheap hotels, as well as private apartments.

Among other things, several youth and children's camps were opened in the village at once. In general, it should be noted that the resort of Kranevo (Bulgaria) is an ideal place for those who prefer to relax with children, although it is also suitable for single tourists who want to be alone with themselves.

Section 2. Budget hotel



As already noted above, this Bulgarian village can provide relaxation, as they say, for every taste and budget.

For example, as an athletic base for additional training, adherents of an active lifestyle choose a hotel called Aqualife. Kranevo (Bulgaria) will help to save money by providing a decent vacation for relatively little money.

In this village is located the hotel "Rendezvous", opened in 2006. It is located near the airport, approximately thirty kilometers. This is a rather small institution, unlike the Aqualife Hotel (Kranevo, Bulgaria). There are only 15 rooms, ten of which are doubles, and 5 are triple. However, tourists characterize it as a real paradise, designed to relax in a close company of friends or in the family circle.

Each room has a private bathroom with shower, Wi-Fi access, cable TV and a fridge. You can have fun at discos organized, and in the daytime - with the help of water and beach sports.

The Rendezvous Hotel has a restaurant with a summer terrace. The menu offers traditional dishes of Bulgarian and European cuisines, vegetarian dishes and barbecue.

Section 3. Hotels for middle-income vacationers

Going to Kranevo (Bulgaria)? Hotels of different categories are waiting for you. Here are examples of places that will surely appeal to those who love comfort, appreciate convenience, but at the same time are not ready to spend a fabulous sum of money on a seven- or ten-day vacation by the sea.

Hotel "Harmony Hills" - a resort complex that operates throughout the year and is designed for a great vacation and exciting entertainment. Its main advantage is that it is located away from the city, in the countryside, which is characterized by picturesque views. Nearby is the national reserve, and, in principle, you can reach on foot.

All the houses and buildings in Harmony Hills (Kranevo, Bulgaria) are tastefully equipped. Guest reviews are another confirmation of this.

Today, the villas and apartments are located in the complex so that the windows offer beautiful views of the Black Sea.

There are many different types of rooms: one and two bedroom apartments, a double villa, a three bedroom villa and a studio. In all, of course, there are cable TV, air conditioning, high-speed Internet. The balconies in the apartments and the courtyards with lawns in the villas are equipped with comfortable chairs and modern tables.

Shops, a mini market, landscaped garden and terrace are also located on site. The hotel complex has a lot of entertainment: spa, gym, pool, billiards, jacuzzi, sauna, hammam. Everyone can also sing karaoke, go on a bike ride or go horse riding.

The hotel restaurant has an indoor lounge and an outdoor terrace. The menu includes Bulgarian and European cuisines, as well as wines of local producers.

There is also a bar located near the pool. They offer refreshing alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, juices and several types of coffee.

Section 4. Luxurious Holidays for the Rich



What category of tourists is suitable Kranevo (Bulgaria)? Reviews of grateful vacationers come not only from budget hotels. In this country, lovers of luxury and special comfort will definitely like it.

Ready to spend a substantial amount of money in exchange for a high level of service and genuine European service? In this case, welcome to an amazing place called Therma Palace, which is deservedly called unique. What is the secret of such originality? The thing is that in this recreation area not only all the traditions of mud therapy in Bulgaria, which have been collected for centuries, are successfully combined, but also the modern achievements of technology and medicine. Every year the Bulgarians themselves come here, as well as guests from near and far abroad. The supply of strength, energy and positive emotions is guaranteed for a long time.

The hotel itself is a five-story building. It has mineral water pools and a medical center, restaurant, bar and cafe, banquet and conference rooms, laundry, dry cleaning, as well as a garden and park. All conditions for the stay of the kids are created: a swimming pool, a playground, a children's room, special chairs in the restaurant and cots in the rooms.

Entertainment and sports facilities can be found for every taste: billiards, darts, bowling, volleyball, tennis court, soccer field, spa, massage, jacuzzi, bathhouse, sauna, hammam.

Section 5. Features of local beaches



In fact, this place is amazing - Kranevo (Bulgaria). The hotels here, as we have seen, are also magnificent. What about the beaches? Is there a possibility of a good rest on the seashore? "Surely!" - answer everyone who is lucky to visit this place.

It should be noted that the length of the coastal zone is about six kilometers, a strip of sand has a width of 100 meters.

On the beach, as a rule, there are umbrellas and deck chairs, rent them separately and for a fee. The sand is soft, shallow and very clean. In general, Bulgaria, in particular, Kranevo boasts the presence of a wonderful sea - shallow, clean and absolutely free of algae and sharp stones.

During the beach season, catamarans, jet skis, and surfboards are available for hire. You can go water skiing, boat and banana. The beach has a tennis court and volleyball court.

Section 6. Entertainment and Attractions



Kranevo has a large number of cultural and historical attractions that are located in the vicinity: nature reserves, Orthodox churches and monasteries, fortresses and caves, an archaeological museum and a dolphinarium.

The resort center has a very symbolic name - "Black Sea". It is a street where there are restaurants, cafes and souvenir shops. By the way, many will find it useful to know that it is here that the currency exchange points and the post office are located.

Hungry? Good cuisine can please in the restaurants "Dune", "Izvora", "At Ivan".

Section 7. Positive feedback about the resort



In general, a country like Bulgaria (Kranevo in particular) cannot but like it. And the prerequisites for enthusiastic emotions abound.

Medical boarding houses are very welcome by travelers. Why not? The pursuit of excellence, quality service, developed children's infrastructure, a variety of entertainment and polite staff - are these not the components of an ideal vacation?

Unusually low prices are also pleasing.

Ancient historical sights, many souvenir shops, where everyone can find something special for themselves, are attracted and left indifferent. The restaurants pleasantly surprise the variety of dishes, their prices and large portions.

Section 8. A fly in the ointment ...



In general, it should be noted that there are no constant and general complaints. Basically, these are rare cases when someone is not lucky to settle with young children in a room near a restaurant, bar or disco.

Although here, too, as they say, a double-edged sword. Kranevo is a quiet corner for relaxation, so young people sometimes get bored, because even at a disco the distance is not less than a kilometer.

A little upset by the small number of local residents who understand Russian. Someone is bothered by insects on the beaches.

There have been cases of fraud during the exchange of dollars, but there are very few of them. Usually everything happens honestly and openly.

In general, it is worth noting that holidays in Bulgaria (Kranevo) cannot fail to please both adults and small vacationers.
