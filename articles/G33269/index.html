The Tale of How to Flash a Phone

A cell phone has long ceased to be a luxury and has become a daily necessity. But there are situations when this tool also needs help.

Situation times. You bought a brand new cell phone, and with trembling hands you turn it on for the first time. But ... Such a long-awaited feeling of happiness is replaced by disappointment. The first thing that comes to mind is the Internet. And with trembling hands of indignation you clog your cry of the soul in a search engine (see questions above). Yes, the Internet knows everything: both how to flash a phone, and how to flash your phone. But do you know what the word firmware means? And this is a complete replacement for the entire operating system and software of your beloved phone. The firmware can either restore the original system or improve certain functions of the phone: fix errors of previous versions, add new themes, update software, improve performance, improve signal quality and much, much more.
