Recreation center Chusovaya. Description. Reviews

The recreation center "Chusovaya" is located on the banks of the river of the same name near the village of Sloboda. There used to be a tannery here, but in the first half of the last century it was converted to a camp site, and already in 1934 the first tourists came here. Now the hostel welcomes its guests all year round.





The recreation center "Chusovaya". Description

So that vacationers can fully enjoy the surrounding beauties and the benefits of outdoor activities, the most comfortable conditions are created for them at the camp site. Just one minute walk from the residential buildings is the beach. For young tourists on the territory there is a children's town. In the evening, guests can relax in the cozy wooden gazebos. Picnic lovers are waiting for the area with barbecue. There is a cafe where, for a fee, those who wish are provided with three meals a day. The hotel has a bath and sauna, as well as a wellness center.

In summer, you can sunbathe and go fishing, go for a bike ride, and in winter go ice-skating and skiing. Fishing gear and sports equipment are available at the rental office. The hotel offers various excursions. You can use the services of the horse club "Consul" to practice horseback riding. The hotel has a museum where you will be introduced to its history, and will also offer to try your hand at the ancient ability to carry buckets on the rocker arms. You can leave your photo in the museum, which will become a part of the history of the recreation center.

Paid secure parking is equipped for vacationers' personal vehicles on the hotel territory. The recreation center "Chusovaya" (photo below) is located in the shade of tall pines, between the trees there are cozy benches where you can relax and breathe in tarry coniferous air.





Guest Accommodation

The recreation center "Chusovaya" offers its guests a choice of several accommodation options.

  • There are six buildings that are designed to accommodate families, participants in organized seminars, small groups of students and schoolchildren. The rooms have a refrigerator, TV, bathroom.
  • Six separate cottages are designed to accommodate large families and companies. In the cottage "Cranes" at the same time can accommodate 8 people, there is one extra bed. Most vacationers (44 people) can be accommodated in the Lebyazhy cottage. All houses are built according to different designs, made mainly of wood. One cottage has several bedrooms, as well as a shared kitchen, living room and bathrooms.

All living areas are finished with wood. Some rooms have terraces and balconies with wonderful views. Most rooms include breakfast.





Rest by the river

The Chusovaya River at the recreation center attracts tourists not only with the opportunity to go fishing and boating. This is one of the most beautiful rivers in the Urals, and the only one that flows through two parts of the world. It begins on the eastern slope of the Urals in Asia and ends on the western slope in Europe. Along its shores, travelers can see picturesque cliffs, colorful Russian villages, working villages, as well as large industrial cities.

There are a lot of fish in the river. Fishermen catch perch, pike, bream, grayling here. The warmest on Chusovaya in July, the air temperature at this time can reach thirty degrees. The water in the river is clear. The beach has a very convenient approach to the water. A bathing area with a sandy bottom is equipped. On the shore there are sports playgrounds and a sunbathing area. Tourists can rent a boat or catamaran. In winter, the river freezes, and from the cliff where the camp site is located, views of its magnificent snow-covered bends surrounded by pines and firs.





Chusovaya River at the recreation center. Reviews

  • The territory of the base is clean, well-groomed, in the houses there are small cozy rooms.

  • I remember fishing very much. Fish can be caught both from the shore and on the water from a catamaran.

  • The descent from the recreation center to the river goes along a very beautiful staircase.
  • By prior arrangement with the hotel, you can arrange river rafting on catamarans. Tourists are accompanied by experienced instructors.
  • In the forest you can pick mushrooms, there are quite a lot of them.
  • The recreation center "Chusovaya" has its own cafe, which is very hearty and deliciously fed.

Where else can you relax on the Chusovaya River

On the banks of the picturesque Chusovaya River there are several recreation centers where you can relax in peace from the bustle of the city.

  • The tourist base "Ust-Koiva" is located in a small village of the same name. It offers comfortable holiday homes as well as economical accommodation options. Interesting excursions await tourists here, experienced instructors will go hiking with you, and also organize rafting on the river. Fans of a walk in the woods can enjoy picking berries and mushrooms. Well, the most interesting thing on the river, of course, is fishing.
  • The recreation center "Polyana" is located in the forest (50 km from Perm). Guests can relax in silence in the cozy cottages and townhouses. The Chusovaya River is two hundred meters from the camp site. On the river bank you can go fishing and sunbathing on the beach. There is a modern restaurant on the territory of Polyany.
  • The recreation center "Bor-NEO" is located on the outskirts of the village of Kosy Brod in a pine forest and is a modern cottage. The Chusovaya River is located six hundred meters from the base. Comfortable rooms, clean air and fishing - here you will find everything you need for relaxation.
