Hotel Angela Hotel 3 * Vietnam, Nha Trang: overview, features, rooms and reviews

Recently, Asian exotic destinations are becoming increasingly popular among Russian tourists. Vietnam is no exception. This country today has turned into a developed state, where you can relax and see many amazing sights.



general information

The beach capital of Vietnam is Nha Trang. This definition for travelers is quite enough to understand the essence of the resort. Nha Trang, although not very large, is a cheerful and noisy town with a developed tourist infrastructure. It is no accident that it attracts more and more tourists every year, including our compatriots. It is difficult to name one thing that is important, which is why it is worth coming here, overcoming thousands of kilometers. Some love Nha Trang for the azure sea and the white sand beaches, as if necklaces surrounded by green hills of all colors, for tropical islands growing in the middle of the bay. And it's all here - a reality, as if descended from a commercial. Others choose this resort because of its vibrant nightlife. And conservative tourists who like the Sybarite infrastructure of Nha Trang - mud baths, first-class spa treatments, stay in chic five-star hotels, which here fully correspond to the declared category.



Overview of Nha Trang hotels

In general, there are plenty of places to stay here. Nha Trang, being the most popular resort in South Vietnam, is one of the thirty most beautiful bays in the world. There are chic hotels occupying small islands, four-star hotels with a full range of services, and, of course, treshki with air conditioning and a private pool. One of them is Angela Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang). Do not confuse it with a hotel that has almost the same name, but is located in a distant Greece. We are talking about Angela Suites Lobby Hotel 3 * (Rhodes). Vietnamese hotel - in the center of Nha Trang on Tran Hung Dao. And it is about him, and not about the Rhodes Angela Suites Lobby Hotel 3 * that will be discussed. So, we are going to Vietnam, on the coast of the South China Sea.

Description

Angela Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) is a cozy hotel with comfortable rooms not pretending to luxury. It has a warm, almost homely atmosphere. The hotel is an amazing combination of cleanliness and maximum accuracy, cleanliness, which is in harmony with professional service and an incredibly friendly welcome. It was built in 2009. The last complete renovation took place in 2015. Angella Hotel 3 * is an eight-story building. It is located on the second line from the sea in the center of Nha Trang. Next to it is the most diverse infrastructure - restaurants and night clubs, cafes. Angella Hotel 3 * specializes in organizing and conducting wedding ceremonies.



In half an hour you can get to the international airport, located in Nha Trang, and in three minutes - to the railway station. Today, many tourist operators for an economical vacation in such an exotic country as Vietnam offer Angela Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang). Reviews indicate that it is suitable for any category of travelers.

Infrastructure

This hotel, despite its three-star category, offers a fairly wide and, most importantly, high-quality range of services. It is clean everywhere, in all public places you can connect to the Internet for free. The hotel provides ironing services, newspapers and concierge services. The staff is mostly multilingual. There are several small shops, a currency exchange office, gift shop, shoe shine and laundry service in the lobby. There is a modern elevator between the floors.

The registration desk is open around the clock. Here you can use the depositary safe, buy air and train tickets, order a transfer. Angela Hotel 3 * has a small private parking. There is a car and motorbike rental service in the lobby, as well as a tour desk. The front desk has fax and photocopying services. The infrastructure also includes a conference hall with a capacity of 300 seats and a business center.

Nutrition

Angela Hotel 3 *, reviews of accommodation in which many Russians leave immediately upon arrival from Vietnam, offers only a continental buffet breakfast. The hotel also has a restaurant and cafe. The cuisine is mostly European. At the restaurant you can enjoy both familiar dishes and delicious grilled specialties. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many inexpensive establishments where you can have a tasty meal. Judging by the reviews, many liked the food from the mobile shops.

Beach

The beaches of Nha Trang are mostly municipal. The only exceptions are the bathing areas, which are owned by luxury five-star hotels. In Nha Trang, seven kilometers of white beaches along which the boulevard stretches, completely surrounded by coconut trees. From the Angela Hotel 3 * to the sea is only one hundred and fifty meters. The sand on the beaches of this Vietnamese resort is the smallest fragments of multi-colored shells, which is probably why the water here is very clean and absolutely clear. The entrance to the sea is comfortable, medium gently sloping. All amenities for a beach holiday - umbrellas, trestle beds and sun loungers - are provided for a fee. There are many entertainments on the shore, including snorkeling, water skiing, sailing and boating.



Hotel Features

Angella Hotel 3 * offers to spend a vacation immersed in the subtleties of national culture. In his SPA-center you can buy a variety of treatments for relaxation. Professional specialists, individually suited to each visitor, use old techniques and modern drugs. The wonders they create make customers look compelling. The hotel has an outdoor freshwater pool. Its area is 62 square meters. The hotel offers wedding services.

What can be seen

Vietnam has many ancient attractions. It is in order to see them that many Europeans come here. In the hotel lobby you can buy sightseeing tours to the Oceanographic Museum, Yangbai Waterfall and numerous temples, as well as a huge stone Buddha statue sitting on a lotus. On foot, tourists can walk to the Tyam Towers of Po Nagar. Many of those who live in Angela Hotel 3 * prefer to rent a car, motobike or bicycle inexpensively and explore local attractions on their own. And some, to carelessly plunge into the history of Vietnam, accompanied by a guide, buy sightseeing tours right at the hotel.



Angela Hotel 3 *, Vietnam: reviews

Most of the Russians staying at this hotel were satisfied with their stay. Convenient location Angela Hotel is one of its main advantages: it is the very center of Nha Trang. Near many shops and cafes, there is a very inexpensive night market. Many of our compatriots dined outside the hotel, for example, in the nearest barbecue, or bought food in mobile shops.

The rooms are all new. Especially a lot of positive feedback about the work of staff - maids. Everywhere is clean and tidy. And although only workers at the reception speak several languages, the rest nonetheless communicate well in sign language. But the most important thing is the responsiveness and friendliness of the staff.

As for excursions, many are advised to go to Hon Tam Island and Dalat. Those traveling with young children should not go on tours in the mountains of Vietnam because the road is exhausting.

Many liked the fact that the rooms have tea accessories, as well as the code on the telephone set for free access to the Internet.

The food at the hotel is not bad, although some Asian cuisine was not to their liking, so they preferred to eat at nearby cafes. Nevertheless, most Russians liked the breakfast, there are even tips to try local coffee.

Among the shortcomings, our compatriots note the very small size of the pool, as well as the fact that the windows in the rooms do not open. Some were annoyed that when checking in at the reception they took their passports, but, as they were told, this was done for the benefit of the tourists themselves, who very often lose their documents and with great difficulty restore them. Another reason for dissatisfaction is the noise during weddings, which are held in this hotel a lot.

Finally

If to summarize based on the reviews of Russians, we can say that the vast majority of our compatriots Angela Hotel 3 *, located in the Vietnamese resort of Nha Trang, really liked it. The average rating for this hotel is four and a half points.
