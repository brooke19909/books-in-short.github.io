Berlin Green Park 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands): hotel description, reviews

Berlin Green Park 4 * - one of the best Bulgarian "fours" in its price category. The hotel is located in Golden Sands, a popular beach resort. It has a large green area, excellent location. Citizens of European countries prefer to go to this hotel - mainly Germans and British. The hotel is located on a hill, and therefore its guests can enjoy wonderful panoramic views of the Black Sea coast. It belongs to the line of Berlin Hotels. Tourists leave good reviews about this hotel, and now we will see why they value it so much.



Holidays in Golden Sands

The Golden Sands region of Bulgaria has become a resort not so long ago. This happened in the fifties of the last century. There is everything you need for a real European resort. The cleanest beach with the Blue Flag, thermal healing springs, excellent infrastructure - cafes, discos, nightclubs, lots of entertainment for vacationers of all ages. Golden Sands is the widest and longest coastal strip in Bulgaria. Along this flat, as if drawn under a ruler, beach, villas, pensions, hotels and recreational facilities were built. The resort is surrounded by a protected forest. No wonder he is among the top three in popularity among tourists. Vacationers here like a harmonious combination of nature and civilization, a mild climate when water and air are almost at the same temperature - 24-27 degrees Celsius, and also that it is one of the largest ‚Äúhangout‚Äù places on the entire Bulgarian coast. Therefore, if you settle almost on the beach, then consider that it will be noisy. And at the Berlin Green Park Hotel you will find the perfect combination of comfort and silence.

How to get and where is located

If you arrive in Varna, you will reach the place quickly. But Golden Sands are quite far from other airports. Therefore, in some cases, for example from Burgas, transfer by transfer can be quite long - about three to four hours. The hotel is located in the northern part of the resort village. From the building of the hotel Berlin Green Park 4 * to the beach, promenade and marina with snow-white yachts, walk no more than fifteen minutes. In the evenings, you get to not only the promenade along the sea, but also to the amusement park, a huge number of restaurants, nightclubs and discos that are just waiting for their customers.



Territory

In the building of the hotel Berlin Green Park 4 * six floors. It stands on a hill where a beautiful English-style park is laid out. Stairs, arbors, snow-white arches fit perfectly into the classical landscape. The hotel building is also very pretty. The park has many exotic trees, picturesque bushes with fragrant flowers, as well as well-groomed flower beds. On the territory of the hotel there are shops with souvenirs and various beach trifles, an exchange office, a reception lobby are located on the ground floor. There is a parking place for tourists. But parking is paid.



Rooms

This hotel has two hundred and sixteen guest rooms located on six floors. And, of course, the higher you are settled, the better the view from the windows. Therefore, the upper floors are always more in demand. The most common category of rooms in the Berlin Green Park 4 * hotel is Standard. Golden Sands is also considered one of the best family resorts. Therefore, the hotel has special facilities for families with children or large companies. These are family rooms, studios and apartments. All rooms are elegantly furnished with comfortable high-quality furniture and air-conditioned. Most rooms have sea views, but there are also those with mountain views. Everywhere has furnished balconies. The rooms are spacious, clean, the beds are large. There are blankets. The rooms are equipped with armchairs, coffee tables. Use of the safe and minibar is at an additional cost. The apartments are most suitable for families with children - there are two bedrooms and as many bathrooms, one with a bathtub and the other with a shower. Satellite TV with four Russian channels. Cleaning is done at a time convenient for tourists when everything is at sea.



Service and entertainment

In the hotel Berlin Green Park Hotel 4 * catches free Wi-Fi, not only in the lobby or rooms, but even on the beach. The hotel has a lot of sports activities - mini-golf, the opportunity to play tennis on the court, as well as handball and volleyball. There is a billiard room. If necessary, you can call a doctor, take things to the laundry. Many services are intended for children - playground, mini club, slides. There is a good SPA center with massage room and sauna, as well as a fitness room. The hotel staff speaks not only Bulgarian, English and German, it also knows Russian. The employees are mostly young people. They do their job well. Newlyweds are entitled to champagne or cake upon arrival. The main thing is to inform the managers in advance. A special hall has been adapted for evening animation, shows and concerts. Children really like the mini disco. The animation for the kids is amazing - every day there are games, quests, fun with pirates, Indians, treasure hunts, master classes.



Nutrition

At Hotel Berlin Green Park 4 * guests are fed in two restaurants. One of them works like a buffet (the all inclusive system is practiced there), and the second - a la carte. The main restaurant is spacious with a beautiful outdoor terrace. There are also several bars here - in the lobby, by the pool, on the beach (there are two of them). An interesting fitness-style diner. One of the bars is around the clock. On the beach you can not only dine, but also try both soft and alcoholic drinks, which you rely on the all-inclusive system - beer, wine, raki and gin. The food in the hotel is varied - there is meat, fish, chicken. many side dishes. Bulgarian cuisine with lots of spices predominates. In the afternoon, a cart with ice cream is rolled out, and sweets are laid out near the restaurant. For lunch - delicious soups. Every day they cook something on the grill. Pancakes and barbecue meat are very popular among tourists - they even line up for them. They give fresh fruits (peaches, apples, oranges, bananas, melons, watermelons), cheeses, delicious desserts. For vacationers with kids there is a special children's table.



Sea, pools, beach

As already mentioned, from the hotel "Berlin Green Park" to the beach to go no more than a quarter of an hour. To do this, go down the stairs that go through the park, and then make a small promenade along the promenade. But if this is too far for you, then the hotel provides a minibus that runs to the sea and back quite regularly. The hotel has its own beach. This is quite a rarity for Bulgarian hotels. It belongs to a hotel of the same chain, but located on the first line. On the beach there are new sunbeds, bamboo umbrellas. The entrance to the water is good, sandy, convenient for children. Often small fish swim in flocks, sometimes dolphins can be seen. If you see that someone is taking more than two sun loungers under an umbrella, contact a beach worker who will solve this problem. Rescuers very vigilantly monitor safety, especially children. Moreover, they work both on the beach and by the pools. The hotel can take special towels for sunbathing, but only on deposit. The complex has two pools - under the roof and in the air. Both of them are filled with mineral water, clean, do not smell like bleach. Around the outdoor pool there is a terrace with umbrellas and sun loungers. In addition, guests of this hotel can use some of the services of a five-star fellow. For example, the pools and territory of Berlin Beach.

You can visit the thermal springs and mineral pools on your own, especially since they do not charge for the use of them. The chapel of St. John the Baptist is also popular.
