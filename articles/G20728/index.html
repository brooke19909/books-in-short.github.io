Hotel-Park "Olympus", Kolomna: photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel-Park Olympus is located in one of the most beautiful corners of the Moscow Region. The Kolomna district is not in vain attracted by the great attention of tourists. Beautiful nature, fresh air, as well as the opportunity to spend time with benefit, studying the cultural heritage of the people of Kolomna - all this attracts new guests here. But today in the center of our attention is the hotel-park "Olympus". This is a modern complex, perfect for holding a variety of events, ranging from corporate events and ending with a noisy wedding.





Location

Hotel-Park "Olympus" is not difficult to find, just open the navigator of the Moscow region. Kolomensky district, the village of Maloe Uvarovo - then you should focus on signs. We will tell you more about how not to get lost.

  • By train, you can travel from the Kazan station to the Golutvin station. There is a bus stop number 22, which will take you to the village of Maloe Uvarovo.
  • By bus number 460 (Kolomna) you will need to get to the same station, the only difference is that it will depart from the Vykhino metro station.
  • Another bus No. 333 departs from this metro station (the city of Lakes), it will immediately take you directly to the village of Maloe Uvarovo.

Number of rooms





The hotel has 141 rooms, which are located in the main building and the youth center "Sunny Olympus". In total, about 350 people can be accommodated here at the same time. Here are rooms of various levels of comfort. In addition, you can choose 4 ticket options: without meals, with breakfast included, half board (two meals a day) or full board. Guests are invited to:

  • 16 rooms of economy class.
  • 68 standard rooms.
  • 32 categories of "comfort".
  • 21 "suites" and 4 wedding "suites".

Regardless of class, all rooms are equipped with necessary appliances. This is a TV, fridge, telephone, air conditioning. Each has a bathroom and a loggia. Bathrobes and slippers are provided.

Service

Park Hotel Olympus (Kolomna) takes care of the comfort of its guests. Laundry, ironing, laundry and drying services are available. Wi-Fi is available throughout. A receptionist is on duty at the reception desk, which also provides safe deposit boxes. In the morning, guests are awakened using the "Alarm Clock" service.





You are given the rental of any equipment. It can be household appliances: a fan, an iron and a kettle, sports equipment or beach accessories. Towels are cleaned and replaced daily. If you require additional services, you can contact the administrator.

Catering

Park Hotel Olympus (Kolomna District) gives you the right to choose a food system. The "Buffet" is used when at least 30 guests have a simultaneous check-in (provided that all tourists agree with this). In other cases, you can order a room without meals and go to a restaurant on your own or go to Kolomna for lunch. In addition, you are offered the organization of one-time, two-time or three meals a day. Among Muscovites, the Olimp Park Hotel is very popular. Reviews of tourists rate the catering in the hotel very highly, it takes into account the most diverse gastronomic requests.





Entertainment and beach vacations

No need to strive abroad, better come to the suburbs. Park Hotel Olympus is no different from European resorts. There is everything that can interest even the most demanding customer. Summer activities must include relaxation on the water. There are all conditions for this. The equipped beach on the Kolomenka River allows you to sunbathe at any time. If swimming in an open water does not suit you, then there is an indoor pool with slides for this.

There are a number of other activities on the river that you can take advantage of. For example, rent a pedalo or a catamaran, go on a wonderful fishing trip or play water polo. Separately, it should be noted the activities of the yacht club, you can become a captain and go on a trip.

If you have never been here before, you will be amazed at the abundance of entertainment that the Olimp Park Hotel (Kolomna) offers. Reviews confirm that even in a week it is impossible to have time to visit every corner of this huge entertainment complex.





Sporting young people here will be even more interesting, especially if you come with a big company. For you there are special platforms for playing soccer, polo, volleyball, tennis, badminton. Tracks for riding bicycles and roller skates are created. There are billiards and bowling. The gym and gymnasium will allow you to stay in good shape, and instructors will conduct horse riding training for you. This is only part of what is here, it is simply impossible to disclose all the charms of this hotel in a short article.

Children's holiday

Many tourists come with their children, because they are also interested in the Moscow region. Park Hotel Olympus has not forgotten about its young guests. Especially for them, the restaurant has developed a unique menu that takes into account age-related features. The mini-zoo, built on the territory of the hotel, will capture the attention of your fidgets for a long time.

There is also a contact living area where children can chat with the inhabitants. Playgrounds here are simply amazing. This applies to the indoor games room and outdoor areas located next to the hotel. Here you can jump, climb, run and play. For older children, a whole town was built with rope walls, trampolines and labyrinths.





Cost of rest

We will indicate the cost of full board, including a comprehensive breakfast, lunch and dinner. With the exclusion of food, the price decreases slightly. A standard double room will cost you 5500 rubles (per day). It has two twin beds. The comfort rooms are equipped with a double, family bed, the cost is the same. A double "suite" with air conditioning costs 8900 rubles, and a two-room "suite" - 9700. Wedding "suite" is a little cheaper - 8900 rubles.

Tourists reviews
