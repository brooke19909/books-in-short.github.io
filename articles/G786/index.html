Overview of the best cafes in Kolomna (Moscow region): photos, addresses

Kolomna is a city in the suburbs, rich in attractions. A large number of tourists who come here are interested in the history of the country, meet with friends or just stay here on a business trip.

Among the inhabitants of the city and its guests are very popular various cafes in Kolomna, the best of which are discussed in this article.

"Mill"

This is one of the best venues for hosting various banquets and events throughout the city. There is a cafe "Mill" (Kolomna) in the Pervomaisky district of the city, on Zaitseva street, 2.

On the territory there is a wonderful banquet hall and a picturesque summer playground, sitting on which you can watch a view of the city greenery and streets. Indoors, guests can admire the chic interior, the main element of which is an expensive dark wood - massive chairs with high backs, as well as benches and tables are made of it.

The Kolomna cafe menu offers a wide selection of Russian dishes. It also presents works of confectionery art, many of which were invented personally by the chef.

"Mill" is open every day of the week from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m.





"City"

Cafe City in Kolomna is located in the building of a local hotel, which is known to every resident. Visitors often stop there. The facility is located in the city center, next to the main infrastructure.

The menu of this cafe offers visitors to taste amazing dishes of Japanese, Russian and European cuisine, as well as original desserts that can win the heart of even the most demanding sweet tooth.

Inside, everything is made in a classic style. The interior of the establishment is dominated by a combination of brown, beige and gold shades. The brightness of the overall picture is given by soft seats for seats made in turquoise and lilac colors. The hall also has an aquarium in which swimming fish can make a good impression on visitors.

The institution offers the opportunity to conduct various events on its square. For regular visitors a huge number of promotions and discounts.

The cafe is located at Kolomna, Sovetskaya Square, 2. You can visit it any day from 10 a.m. to midnight.



"Hints"

A wonderful art cafe in Kolomna delights its residents and visitors. Inside, it resembles a small art gallery, which exhibited the work of the best photographers. The walls of the institution are decorated with large pictures taken with high-quality lenses.

Inside, guests can stay in a small room, done in black and white. Near small dark tables here are wooden chairs, which are also painted black. Each guest area is separated from the others by sliding screen partitions.

"Prince"

The cafe, loved by many residents of Kolomna, is located on Lazhechnikova Street 1. Its doors are open for guests any day of the week from 11 a.m. to midnight, and on Saturday it works from noon.

Guests resting here have a great opportunity to sit in huge wards - it is in this style that everything is done here. The ceilings of the halls are decorated with stucco and frescoes, as is done in mansions. Massive wooden chairs and the same tables covered with red tablecloths are provided as places for guests here.

The restaurant's menu offers dishes traditional for Russian cuisine. They can also surprise you with excellently prepared meat on charcoal and grill.





"Monty"

An excellent institution that specializes in preparing delicious dishes for true gourmets is Cafe Monti. It is also often referred to as fusion. This place is something between a cafe and a restaurant.





Inside, everything is very original and tasteful. The interior has many details that are made of light wood. In particular, these are tables and chairs, which are decorated with soft linings. The hall has several large elements of drywall, with which the hall is divided into several zones. One of them has a decorative fireplace made of light brick.

The cafe menu offers visitors to taste expertly prepared dishes of Russian and European cuisine. You can find Italian pasta, pizza, as well as excellent dishes prepared on the grill. In addition, for customers of the cafe there is always a wide selection of snacks for different tastes and light salads.

Cafe in Kolomna "Monti" is located at the address: Lenin street, 69. It seems possible to visit it any day of the week from noon to midnight, on Fridays and Saturdays the institution closes at one in the morning.
