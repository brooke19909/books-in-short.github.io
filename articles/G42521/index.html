Petrovskaya spit: clubs, yacht club, restaurants

Summer, sun, beach - what could be more wonderful while relaxing? Going on a trip or a traditional vacation, everyone wants to experience new emotions, relax and have fun with all their heart. To do this, it is recommended to probe the soil in advance, so to speak, that is, get as much information as possible about the place where you are actually going. One of the most amazing places where you can have a wonderful rest with your family and friends, is the Petrovskaya Spit. It is located in St. Petersburg (Russia) and is very popular with both locals and visitors.



Map location

The spit is located in the Petrograd district of the city. It runs from the main square to the yacht club. For some time, the Petrovskaya Spit was included in the avenue passing nearby. Since 1940, for unknown reasons, she was expelled from there. Driving in the city is often associated with the attractions that are next to it. For example, many have heard about the charming Nobel house, the western (guest) harbor, St. Petersburg yachting community. In addition, not far from the spit is the shipbuilding company Almaz and the shipyard NP Project Standart.

Night clubs

Quite often, tourists, and locals visit clubs. Petrovskaya Spit is an ideal place for entertainment. Among the most popular nightlife establishments, the Cote d'Azur and SYDNEY Beach Club stand out. Also, those who wish to have fun may be interested in the clubs “Air”, “Savage” and “ArcticA”.

SYDNEY Beach Club is considered one of the best and most popular. It is located on the beach and works mainly in the summer. The unique restaurant offers its visitors to enjoy excellent sound and lighting equipment, try the best dishes from the restaurant chef and feel a surge of energy and joy from a party in the fresh air. And although at first glance it is an ordinary tent, but it is equipped so well that it can compete with the best institutions of the city. In addition, you can visit other clubs. Petrovskaya Spit offers a glimpse into the equally popular "Cote d'Azur", which is located on the territory of the Central Yacht Institution. It also works in the summer and is noticeably transformed on Fridays and Saturdays. The Cote d'Azur will allow guests to relax and have fun, leaving only positive emotions.



Project "Site"

Petrovskaya Spit is a magical place that is constantly being restored and improved. So, more recently, it was decided to build a green area, taking as an example the “New Greenland”. It is intended primarily for outdoor sports. In addition, the platform (Petrovskaya Spit) will be available for families and friends: you can sunbathe on the sunbeds, play mini-golf, look at the movie screen at night and, of course, attend parties. This is the main feature of the fabulous place.

The organizers claim that guests can also play basketball, table tennis, attend yoga classes and relax in hammocks. The project "Site" (Petrovskaya Spit) was launched in June and will work all summer. A stage has been built on its territory that will allow you to enjoy a night movie screening or a concert, and the audience, by the way, will be able to watch the event, sitting comfortably on sunbeds with soft pillows. The townspeople liked a wonderful project, so they plan to return to it every summer.



Petrovskaya Spit Restaurants

And, of course, one cannot fail to mention the chic restaurants that are located in the Petrovskaya Spit area. Among them, the most popular are Sails and More. Also, tourists can visit the institutions "Rynda", "Yalta" and BeOpen! Bar.

The Sails restaurant is especially different. It is located almost in the center of St. Petersburg and at the same time - on the territory of the yacht club. The guests of the establishment can enjoy the stunning sunset, the sound of the surf and the screams of seagulls, as well as enjoy the snow-white yachts and the lively promenade. The glass walls of the restaurant allow you to keep abreast of events taking place outside and enjoy a charming, unforgettable atmosphere. Visitors can order dishes of Italian, European, Russian and Japanese cuisine. Three days a week, the restaurant has professional backing vocalists and DJs. Sometimes you can get to the performance of popular stars.

Restaurant More allows guests to enjoy seafood, the best wines of the Mediterranean and enjoy the bewitching landscape. The chef offers to try dishes from mussels, squid, oysters, shrimp. In addition, the visitor can order steamed fish, grilled or aromatic herbs.

Finally, we recommend visiting BeOpen! Bar. A comfortable terrace is located so that you can enjoy the calm Neva. The institution focuses on European cuisine and offers to try the largest cocktail in the city - one and a half liter “Mojito”. Thus, Petrovskaya Spit, the restaurants of which will feed any gourmet, is a center of entertainment and fun.





Other entertainment on Petrovskaya Spit

Peter Spit is known to many thanks to the huge number of yacht clubs. This place is saturated with positive energy and adrenaline. It is very easy to find entertainment to your liking. Almost all establishments are in the fresh air, which further seduces visitors. For example, at Petrovskaya spit, 9, there is a wonderful river yacht club that anyone can visit. The berths are designed for more than 350 ships, and the fleet has more than a hundred sailing yachts.

Advantages of the Central Yacht Club

On the territory of the popular yacht club are located: berths, slipways, cranes, workshops, parking lots, showers, cafes, restaurants, helipad, warm and cold boathouses (designed for storing yachts), classrooms, a conference room, shops, service companies , venues for corporate events, celebrations and exhibitions. This is the best St. Petersburg yacht club (Petrovskaya Spit), where events, holidays, concerts are held. Today it occupies 12 hectares of land.



Club Spit Parties

On Petrovskaya Spit, club parties are a fairly common form of entertainment. A wonderful place in the open air can be visited in any month of the summer season. Many restaurants, clubs, venues offer a noisy and fun party. So, the institution "Summer" offers to enjoy live music, the work of DJs and watch the dancing girls Go-Go. In addition, many institutions have an equipped dance floor, organize show programs and provide visitors with the opportunity to sing karaoke or read interesting magazines.



Visitor Features

In many restaurants, guests have the right to participate in tea ceremonies, order dishes on the grill or grill, take a walk along the pier, admiring the magnificent scenery and use the services of English-speaking staff. Each institution is trying to create the best conditions for the rest of customers - local and visitors. In addition, the guest has the opportunity to order an original cake, hookah and try unforgettable desserts. But the most important thing is to enjoy the beautiful landscapes and the surrounding history, breathe fresh air to the fullest and admire the sunset reflected in the smooth surface.
