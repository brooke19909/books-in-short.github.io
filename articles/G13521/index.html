Hotel "Grand Peterhof SPA": reviews of tourists

Choosing a place to travel, many stay in the hot ocean countries. However, for fans of sightseeing holidays and the history of the era of Peter I, a visit to Peterhof is perfect. The greatness of the fountains, courtyards and fresh air are what attract thousands of tourists here. The four-star Grand Peterhof SPA Hotel in Peterhof offers its guests high-quality service and comfortable accommodation. Having been here once, as guests note, the desire to return here reappears for everyone.

general characteristics

The hotel complex began welcoming visitors in 2009. Maintaining it in perfect condition is achieved through the coordinated work of responsible personnel and creative leadership. The main feature is a large-scale spa, which attracts thousands of guests from all over the world. Picturesque open spaces and proximity to outstanding sights, as guests note, make the rest in these parts saturated and aesthetically filled.

The main and only building is a two-story yellow-white building. Living here will allow you to plunge into the time of Peter I and become part of the era of kings and balls. The courtyard is full of greenery, fragrant flowers and sculptural ensembles.

Location and features of settlement

The four-star Grand Peterhof SPA Hotel, a photo of which is presented to your attention in the article, is located less than five kilometers from Strelna and 15 km from St. Petersburg. The nearest airport is 16 km away. It is impossible to accommodate pets in the walls of this establishment. Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout. Upon check-in, a deposit payment is required by credit card or cash. The reception is open 24 hours a day. One child under five years old stays free in a room with parents without providing an extra bed. Check-in time is 14:00. Leave the chambers before noon. Parking spaces are provided free of charge. It is possible to place cars under surveillance cameras.
