What can be done from an old mobile phone: a couple of original ideas

No matter how good and high-quality any thing may be, over time it inevitably breaks, and often so that it is impossible to restore it. And sometimes it happens that in honor of the birthday (or just for no reason) you get a novelty as a gift, and then you puzzle over what application to find an old, but quite suitable thing. Just throwing it away is a pity, but storing it without any benefit is uninteresting. In this article we will tell you what you can do from an old mobile phone. Perhaps some of our ideas will seem quite interesting to you.



Sale

If everything is in order with the phone, it can be used for the second SIM card or sold. In the latter case, it makes sense to look for a store that accepts old mobile phones for parts. Reception prices are low there, but if they offer too little, you can try posting an ad on the forum or in the newspaper. In the latter case, you have to wait until there is someone who wants it (if he still exists), but more money will be obtained.



What can be done from an old mobile phone if the case is damaged

In this case, it will be very original to come up with an exclusive design from improvised devices. The new case can, for example, be knitted or made from an ordinary pack of cigarettes. Here, as they say, anyone has enough imagination. As a result, no one will have such an apparatus. You can try to make from it a usb camera for communication on the network or an alarm for a car.

What can be done from an old mobile phone if the display is damaged

Oddly enough, this part of the cell breaks down very often. Either manufacturers specifically limit the life of their products in this way, or it does not occur to them to improve the strength of their devices. If the phone falls to the ground one or several times, and in the best case there will be an ugly scratch, and in the worst case, the display will cease to show anything at all. If this is just your situation, do not rush to be upset. Firstly, the display can be completely replaced with another, and in older models the cost of such repairs is relatively small. Secondly, such an apparatus can be used as an independent mobile device for listening to uplifting music.

The only things that need to be taken care of are the speakers and, possibly, if the model is old, the adapter from the headphone jack on the old phone to a regular 3.5 mm diameter.
