Seabel Alhambra Beach Golf & Spa 4 * (Tunisia, Sousse): description of the hotel, services, reviews

Tunisia, which is part of North Africa, has recently become increasingly popular as a resort. Many people who dream of a budget vacation on the seashore decide to go here. And the main question of interest to tourists before the upcoming trip is to choose a hotel. Well, there are a lot of them, but with special attention I would like to mention Seabel Alhambra Beach Golf & Spa 4 *.





general information

This complex is located in the port city called Sousse. For ease of orientation, we can note the fact that it was located on the Mediterranean coast of Tunisia. It is also not only a popular seaside resort, but also a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Therefore, both lovers of beach vacations and lovers of historical sights come here.

Location

Seabel Alhambra Beach Golf & Spa 4 * is located in close proximity to the sea. You can walk to the beach in a couple of minutes. It takes about 20 minutes to get to the airport (about 27 kilometers). Very close is a bus stop. So it is not necessary to spend money on a taxi to get somewhere.

By the way, in 5-15 minutes you can reach various interesting places. For example, to Friguia Park. It is a colorful place, the first wild zoo in Tunisia. Also nearby is the entertainment park Medinat Alzahra Parc, the private museum of Dar Essid, the Ribat, the Grand Mosque and much more.
