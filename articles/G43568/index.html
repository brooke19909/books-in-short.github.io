Master class: how animals are made in origami style from paper

What can be done in the origami technique? Animals are excellent at paper. You can also involve children in this lesson. Assembly schemes are quite simple, kids will be delighted with the new hobby.

Origami animals from paper: assembly schemes

We offer to make a fox first.





  1. Prepare a sheet of paper and fold it first once diagonally and then a second (Figures 1 and 2).
  2. Then wrap the left and right corners of the resulting triangle from the center line (Figure 3).
  3. Fold the part in half (Figure 4).
  4. Unscrew the upper half of the figure a little and divide its top into two parts (Figure 5).
  5. Spread the spread and rotate the part onto the barrel (Figure 6).
  6. Bend the right corner (Figure 7).

To complete the work, draw a muzzle to the chanterelle.

Cute origami paper pig

The master class will tell you how to make origami pigs from paper.





Animals can be absolutely any color. It will be better for the child if you take bright colored paper.

First, a rectangular blank is made. For this, a sheet of paper is folded in half in length. To make the pig beautiful, it is better to use a regular leaflet, rather than a square one.

To facilitate the further assembly of crafts from the workpiece, make an accordion in three gates. Then straighten the rectangle. As a result, you should have four fold lines.

Now pull the left edges so that the bottom and top of this part are straightened. The purpose of this movement is to make the pointed shape of a rectangle. Repeat the same action with the other side (picture 1).

Flip over to do the back and fold it in half in length (picture 2).

Unscrew the sides of the side triangle (picture 3).

Do the same on the other side.

Wrap the right triangle inward (picture 4).

Wrap the tip of the left triangle inward (picture 5).

Lift the bottom of the figure, as in picture 6.

Draw a face and flatten the paper.

Our origami pig is ready!

Origami from paper: savannah animals

In this technique, you can make not only cute animals, but also real predators. We offer you a master class on how to make origami out of paper.





Savannah animals are zebras, giraffes, lions and many others. We think that it will be interesting for children to make a lion. Therefore, take the yellow paper.

  1. Make a diagonal fold line (picture 1).
  2. Turn the upper and lower parts to the center line (picture 2).
  3. Bend the corners of the figure a little bit (picture 3). This is done diagonally.
  4. On the reverse side, diagonally wrap the upper and lower parts of the figure (see Figure 4 for more details).
  5. Bend the half of the figure back (picture 5).
  6. Visually draw a diagonal line that intersects the two halves of the figure. Wrap inward the front of the part along this line (Figure 6).
  7. Fold the half of the right side (picture 7).
  8. Fold down the part shown in picture 8.
  9. Fold the bottom triangle of the part (picture 9).
  10. Wrap the right side diagonally (picture 10).
  11. Fold back half the tip (picture 11).
  12. Fold half the tip back in the opposite direction (Figure 12).
  13. Tuck a small corner (picture 13).

Cute predator is ready. It remains only to draw his eyes, mouth, nose and mustache.

Tip for creating paper crafts: origami (savannah animals) will look better if you take sheets that are white on one side and orange on the other. So the mane stands out more clearly.

Paper rabbit





To make a rabbit, you need to collect the usual blank (pictures 1, 2 and 3). Then turn the protruding triangle two times in opposite directions and turn the resulting figure over (pictures 4, 5 and 6). Now fold the part in half, first in length and then in width (picture 7 and 8).
