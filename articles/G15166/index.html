Features of rest in Marmaris

Marmaris is one of the best and most "European" resort of the Turkish coast. Until recently, the main part of vacationers here were residents of Northern Europe. And they, as you know, know a lot about high-quality leisure. What attracts tourists to Marmaris? The main advantage of the resort is mild climate. It is ideal for a beach holiday. In addition, this town lies in a fenced island and a peninsular bay, as a result of which there are almost never high waves and strong winds. Holidays in Turunc

In principle, this small resort village is unremarkable. However, the places here are simply amazing, natural landscapes amaze with their picturesqueness. Dense coniferous forests cover the mountains, which gradually descend to the shores of the Aegean Sea, framed by golden sand. It is always quiet and calm. There is not so much entertainment, except for dance floors, where grandiose discos are organized in the evenings. On the beaches you can meet families with children. However, on the private beach of the Turunc Dream Boutique Hotel, you are unlikely to see kids, because, according to the concept of this hotel, there is no place for little vacationers.
