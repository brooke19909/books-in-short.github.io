Gazprombank: partner banks. Cash withdrawal without commission

Gazprombank is one of the leading in Russia due to its activities. The country has many branches. The bank was founded 27 years ago to serve Gazprom. Gradually, new areas of activity began to appear. Gazprombank also has partner banks that offer their customers preferential terms.

Now customers can receive various types of cards, the validity of which is 3 years. Their holders can order additional cards, receive insurance policies at an affordable cost, and participate in soft loans. To do this, contact Gazprombank. Partner banks allow you to withdraw cash without commissions or with minimal fees.



How to draw up a card?

Customers can receive a Gazprombank card via the Internet, as they have a convenient remote service. You can send the questionnaire through the "My Account".

New customers should contact the sales office by filling out the form. The requirements for registration are minimal, since there is no credit limit on the card. The minimum age is 20 years, a passport is required. The employee will inform you about the readiness of the card, and when it can already be picked up, an agreement is signed on the design and maintenance of the account. Production time is from 5 working days. There is no service charge, you only need to pay for the issue.

Use of cards

How to use funds on the card? In order not to pay a commission, you must:

  • use ATMs of Gazprombank and its partners;
  • pay for purchases in stores, supermarkets, deposit funds for various services without cashing out;
  • withdraw funds at the cash desk of Gazprombank;
  • transfer money to a card of another bank through online banking.

Using funds without a commission will only work with debit cards. If the card is credit, then interest will be charged. This feature exists in almost every bank. The commission is set based on the amount.
