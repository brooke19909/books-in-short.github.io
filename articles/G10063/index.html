How to knit bactus with knitting needles?

The knitting process begins with a set of 4 loops on the knitting needles. Openwork bactus with knitting needles is knitted with a garter stitch , that is, all rows will be knitted either only in the front or only on the wrong side. Knitting bactus knitting pattern

You need to knit both the first and last stitches, as well as the entire row, with any chosen knitting option. You also need to knit 3 next rows, while you do not need to add or reduce loops. Now now, only with 4 rows is it necessary to make a yarn, which in the 5th row must be tied with a loop at the back wall to get rid of the hole.
