Who killed Kennedy?



Few believe that someday light will be shed on such a mysterious story as the Kennedy assassination, the news of which instantly spread all over America, and then other countries. Residents could not believe that the young president, though not an ideal ruler, but beloved and respected by all, had died. No one knew for sure who killed Kennedy, no one knows this now. Too much time has passed, too many traces have been noticed.

Witnesses do not like to remember that day: everything was unexpected, however, the president himself predicted in a few hours. Of course, he hardly realized that he saw his own future and gave his words crushing significance, but they turned out to be prophetic. The assassination of the president, according to John himself, is quite possible. To do this, you need to take a rifle with an optical sight, climb onto the roof of a tall building and take a couple of shots. There was irony in his voice.

The assassination of John Kennedy was attributed to the lone psycho Lee Harvey Oswald, who later refused to admit his guilt, but his words did not matter - the case was sewn with white thread.

Indeed, no one took into account many obvious inconsistencies, and if some evidence leaked to the press, they were immediately destroyed. Persons with power know how to cover their tracks, and apparently no one will answer the question of who killed Kennedy.
