Stencil is an indispensable tool in decorating the interior

Stencil is A convenient tool for those who can not draw or do not want to spend a lot of time on what can be done much faster and with less effort. It can be made independently or purchased in a store.





What are the stencils

These devices differ depending on where you plan to use them. The main types:

  1. Volume. It is usually made of thick plastic and is reusable. Designed to create volumetric images using a special paste or plaster.
  2. Glue. This is a picture cut out of a self-adhesive film. The factory version has a substrate that must be removed before use. You can make a stencil yourself from vinyl. it will save a lot of money. It is mainly used on glass and ceramics.
  3. Sandblasting. Made of especially durable materials, used for decorating mirrors and glasses.
  4. The simplest and most accessible option for everyone is paper.

Where can I use them?

With the help of ready-made templates, you can decorate almost everything that is around. Common situations in which stencils can be used for cutting:

  1. Garland making. In the cut out silhouettes, holes are made through the hole through which the cord passes.
  2. Drawing a pattern on windows.
  3. Drawing volumetric image on culinary products.
  4. Dressing clothes (you should use special paints that can be purchased at art stores).
  5. In the decoupage technique.
  6. Under repair.




Popular Templates

To decorate the interior, floral patterns and ornaments are often used. Symmetric images look interesting. In addition, they can use one stencil. it convenient when templates are bought: just flip the tool and you get a symmetrical pattern. This eliminates the need to buy a second template.

During the New Year holidays, images of the animal, the symbol of the coming year, become relevant. But this is a short-term home decoration; for him it is not necessary to purchase durable patterns and use resistant paints. The best solution would be paper stencil and gouache.

Flowers, symbolic images of heraldic elements and ornaments will always be relevant. Using different colors in the work can make it more interesting.

When choosing a ready-made template, you should remember that the stencil is tool, and it must correspond to the planned type of work in order to get a high-quality result.
