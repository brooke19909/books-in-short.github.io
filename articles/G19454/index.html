Hotel Residence Parco Mare Monte 3 * (Ischia, Italy): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Hotel Parco Mare Monte 3 * is the best suited for people who are going to travel in Italy. This is a budget hotel that stands on a high hill with panoramic views. From there you can go down to the sea. The hotel is surrounded by a Mediterranean forest with cedars and chestnuts and hills planted with vineyards. The hotel's terraces offer wonderful views of Ischia's sights such as the port of Casamicciola, the beaches of Laco Ameno Bay, and the endless expanse of water.





How to get to the hotel? What is nearby

In order to get to the "Parco Mare Monte", you need to fly to Naples, and from there you will be delivered by ferry to the island. Since Ischia is located forty kilometers from Naples, the boat overcomes this distance in just an hour.

Hotel Parco Mare Monte 3 * is located in the northern part of the island. on a hill seventy-five meters high. The nearest city to the hotel is Casamicciola. It is one and a half kilometers from the hotel. A little further - a comfortable beach. Before him, about two kilometers.

There is a bus stop right at the entrance to the Residence Parco Mare Monte 3 * hotel (Ischia). By public transport you can reach the port of Casamiccioli, as well as the city center and shops. And three kilometers from the hotel are the terms famous for the whole island. In the presence of a tourist transfer, this hotel is the first to arrive, as it is very close to the port. A forest path through cedar groves will lead you directly to the city in fifteen to twenty minutes.

The same amount of time to get to the nearest supermarket "Deco". If you come here in September, you can pick up full baskets of ceps. Within walking distance through the forest is one of the thermal parks.





Ischia Volcanic Island

This is one of the best resorts in Italy, where they go not so much to relax on the sea, but to use healing mud. Therefore, the main attraction of the island, where the Parco Mare Monte Casamicciola 3 * hotel is located, are thermal parks and pools. A mixture of mineral sulphurous water and clay is called the "royal mess" here.

In addition, people come to Ischia to travel, enjoy the scenery, relax with children. After all, there is a very calm, family, you can say home-like atmosphere, and such tourist pearls and temptations of Italy, like Naples or Capri, are very close.

Tourists note that the island has a very peculiar architecture - a riot of colorful color, houses with red shutters. A lot of greenery that grows on volcanic soil - grapes, figs, lemons, cacti ...

Territory and services

Hotel Parco Mare Monte Casamicciola 3 * (Ischia) was built in 1983. However, in 2003 and 2008, she underwent a major reconstruction. Another wing was completed.

Now it is a two-story building, consisting of connected buildings. The hotel has a parking where guests can leave their cars for free. The hotel building is surrounded by a garden with large terraces and many terracotta statues.

On the ground floor there is a 24-hour reception. You can come with pets to the hotel, and there is no additional charge for them. In the lobby near the administration he catches a free Wi-Fi. There are safes where you can hide valuables during your absence.

The hotel has a spa center with a free sauna. Water and steam (about 90 degrees Celsius) come from pipes drilled into the underground depths. So this sauna is of natural origin.

Number of rooms

This hotel is small. It has only about forty rooms. Hotel Parco Mare Monte 3 * offers standard single and double rooms and apartments.





The rooms can include air conditioning, but they are paid. Many say that they simply slept with an open window, since the hotel is located on a hill, where a breeze always blows. Practically in all residential premises of the hotel there are balconies with sea views (at least partially). Tourists claim that the sunsets on the island are especially visible from the hotel windows.

The apartments are equipped with kitchens and have one, two or three bedrooms. There is satellite TV, and a hairdryer can be requested at the reception. Rooms are cleaned every day, and linen and towels are changed whenever you want. If necessary, they can put a cot in the room. The rooms and apartments are very beautiful, especially with blue furniture and forged beds.

Where can hotel guests sunbathe and swim?

Hotel Residence Parco Mare Monte 3 * has its own pool with thermal water. Its temperature in winter and summer is about thirty degrees Celsius. It is surrounded by a sunbathing terrace with umbrellas and sunbeds, and nearby are beautiful sculptures from the eighteenth century. The pool has a hot tub, which all guests can use. Water comes from thermal springs located at a depth of eighty meters in volcanic rocks.





The sea is about a quarter of an hour's walk. True, to go up for longer, about forty minutes. Therefore, if it is difficult for you to walk, it is better to take bus number sixteen. He goes to the port no more than five minutes. You can get to both wild and equipped (paid) beaches.

The best coast for relaxation is considered Laco Ameno. It is there that you can visit for free. The same piece of the coast with volcanic stones, where you do not need to give money for entry, is located in the port area.

Where to go and what to bring?

In the evening, it is best to go to Laco Ameno. There is a beautiful promenade with a fountain, good music, many discount stores and inexpensive restaurants. When traveling by bus, it is best to buy combined tickets - they are cheaper. Local transport is advised to travel to the cities of Ischia and Santo Angelo. It is possible in this way to get to the gardens of Mortella, Negombo.

Most of the tourists who lived in the Hotel Parco Mare Monte 3 * (Italy, Ischia Island) recommend going to the Amalfi Coast. There you can see many small authentic towns with a unique atmosphere. These are Sorrento, Amalfi, Positano and others. But keep in mind that you have to take a bus along serpentine roads for quite some time. You will come across amazing landscapes along the way, but it can rock you.

You can ride around the island on a boat for ten euros - great impressions and beautiful photos will fully pay for this price. Hotel guests also visit three local thermal parks, although they say that the healing effect at the hotel pool is most noticeable. Good local ceramics can be brought from Ischia.



Parco Mare Monte 3 *: reviews

Rest in this hotel, many tourists call fabulous. And not without reason. The hotel is beautiful, built in the style of an Italian country villa. Great fresh food. Good-natured and helpful host.

Due to the fact that the hotel is small, all guests in a couple of days already know each other and tell who was where and what they saw. Tourists claim that it is best to spend romantic evenings in this hotel on terraces in the fresh air with a bottle of wine. It is quiet, there are no noisy roads nearby.

Especially noted around the clock accessible thermal pool. If you leave early in the morning, you will be offered hot coffee in the thermos in the evening and delivered to the bus suitcases. This hotel is for you if you are healthy and like to walk a lot. She is preferred by active young people and middle-aged guests.
