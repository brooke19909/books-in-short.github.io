Krasnogorsk, Moscow region, hotels: rating, description and reviews

In Krasnogorsk, Moscow Region, hotels are quite in demand, as many business people come here to visit the Crocus Expo exhibition complex. Of course, while on a business trip, it is important to relax at the end of an eventful day. Consider several hotel options that offer guests of the city modern comfortable conditions.

1. Hotel Aquarium

The three-star Aquarium Hotel has the highest rating among hotels located in Krasnogorsk, Moscow Region. Its location is directly the Crocus Expo international exhibition center. All rooms are equipped with a TV and air conditioning, some with river views. The hotel's restaurant serves European cuisine. Breakfast is served in the buffet. The hotel has an ATM, luggage storage, free internet and free parking. For accommodation, guests are offered the following types of rooms:

  • double standard (view of the pavilion) - 3900 rubles per day if two people live, and 3400 rubles for one person;
  • double standard (river view) - respectively 4700 rubles and 3900 rubles;
  • Studio - 5000 rubles for two people and 4000 rubles per day if one person is accommodated.

The Aquarium Hotel is located at: st. International, house 20.

2. Hotel Krocus Star

If you are considering economy hotels in Krasnogorsk, Moscow Region, pay attention to the Krocus Star Hotel. The Crocus Expo Exhibition Center and the Crocus City Trade and Exhibition Center are just 15 minutes away. Sports complex "Snezhkom" is located at a distance of 900 meters from the hotel. Each air-conditioned room has a TV and free Wi-Fi. And the windows of most of the rooms visible embankment of the Moscow river. For accommodation, guests are offered several categories of rooms:

  • single (one single bed) - from 3000 rubles per day;
  • double (two separate beds) - from 4000 rubles per day;
  • double (large double bed) - from 4500 rubles per day;
  • Superior Studio - from 5000 rubles per day.

Toiletries, cooler water, coffee and tea are provided free of charge. The Krocus Star Hotel is located at Krasnogorsky Boulevard, 24.

3. Hummingbird Hotel

European quality and Russian hospitality await you at the three-star Kolibri hotel, which is only 17 minutes walk to the Crocus Expo international exhibition center. The Snezhkom ski complex is a 20-minute walk from the hotel. Within walking distance are a supermarket, cafes and restaurants. If you are staying at a Hummingbird, a hotel in the city of Krasnogorsk, Moscow Region, you can park your car in a free public parking area nearby. The rooms are well soundproofed and have free Wi-Fi access. The mini-hotel offers several payment options:

  • daily rent;
  • overnight;
  • hourly.

For accommodation, tourists are offered several options for rooms:

  • double (with one double or two single beds) - 8000 rubles per day;
  • triple family - 10 172 rubles per day;
  • deluxe double - 9250 rubles;
  • Deluxe double with a king-size bed - 9500 rubles per day.

All rooms include a flat-screen TV. The hummingbird mini-hotel is located at the address: Moscow boulevard, house 14.

7. Erzi Club Hotel

Among the economy class Krasnogorsk hotels in the Moscow Region located in the city center, the Erzi Club Hotel can be noted. In walking distance - the Moscow River, a supermarket.
