Where is Belgorod, one of the cities of Russia

If you make a population survey about attractive places in Russia, then, perhaps, in most cases, the list will not include such a city as Belgorod. But there are a huge number of interesting places and picturesque corners!

It will not be a mistake to say that most of the inhabitants of Russia will not even answer the question of where Belgorod is located. Why? Perhaps this is due to the fact that the town is small. Its population hardly reaches half a million people. The city is inhabited by people of about 100 different nationalities and nationalities. Of course, the majority are Russians. They account for 92%. The second step in the list is occupied by Ukrainians, which make up 1.5%.



For those who do not know where Belgorod is located: it is located in the southern part of the Central Russian Upland, 697 km from Moscow towards the south. From Ukraine it is separated by a distance of 40 km. The city looks great on picturesque hills, which are rugged by branchy ravines. Most of it is located on the right bank of the Seversky Donets (right tributary of the Don).



In addition to answering the question of where Belgorod is located, it is recommended that you familiarize yourself with its most interesting history. In short, the city was founded in September 1596 according to the decree of Tsar Fyodor Ioannovich. Then there was a 70-meter hill, on which the first border fortress was built. Thanks to her, from 1658 Belgorod was considered one of the main areas that had an 800-km defensive line. He defended the Moscow kingdom from the invasion of the Crimean Tatars. The strategic importance of the city decreased at the beginning of the XVIII century. And after the conquest of Crimea, he was generally expelled from the existing fortresses. From 1727 to 1779 the city was the center of the Belgorod province, and from 1954 - the regional center.



Just knowing where Belgorod is is not enough. It is recommended to visit this city and see its beauty. There are many interesting places, including the Smolensky Cathedral, located on Prospect of Glory. An equally attractive architectural structure is the Transfiguration Cathedral, which stores the relics of St. Joasaph. Here is the miraculous icon of Nikolai Ratny. This temple is located on Preobrazhenskaya street. It should be noted Marfo-Mariinsky Convent, located near the Intercession Church.



Interesting places include not only the architectural structures of the city. A curious place is Belgorod Cathedral Square, which is the heart of the city. Here is the Shchepkin Theater, the Eternal Flame and a monument to Lenin. In the center of the square is a memorial sign of the Belgorod region. Immediately beyond the theater is Victory Park. Here, vacationers discover the picturesque banks of the Vyazelka River. The decoration of the park is a magnificent fountain. Of interest is the pool, which is decorated with two bowls. In one there is a ball rotating under streams of water, and in the second there is a sculpture of a lion, which is a symbol of the city.

In addition, the city has many museums. For those who go on vacation with children, it will be interesting to visit the local zoo, which is located on the banks of the Vyazelka River opposite the park.



Sights are known not only the city, but the whole Belogorodskaya region. So, it is recommended to see the famous oak growing in the village of Oak. It was planted more than 300 years ago by Bogdan Khmelnitsky himself . It is thanks to this tree that the village has such a name. Many residents of Belgorod seek to come here to take a break from the bustle of the city and take a walk in the cleanest manicured park.
