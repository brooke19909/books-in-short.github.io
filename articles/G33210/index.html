Homemade weapons: how to make paper nunchucks

Many types of ancient weapons to this day are in great demand. One of them is the nunchucks. However, it is worth mentioning that this type of weapon is prohibited. Master Class



Making nunchucks at home is not difficult, the main thing is to prepare everything you need and follow the instructions. To make them you will need:

  • A4 sheets of paper;
  • Scotch;
  • unnecessary newspapers;
  • twine;
  • glue;
  • rope.

After all the material is collected, you can begin to manufacture the nunchak. Roll an A4 sheet into a tube. Its diameter should not exceed 5 cm. So that the workpiece does not unfold, it must be bound with adhesive tape.

Twist the newspaper into a tight tube. It is important that she can calmly enter the workpiece. As soon as both blanks are ready, start decorating the product.

How to make nunchucks out of paper so that they look believable and last as long as possible? To do this, tightly wrap the parts with twine. If you put it on glue, you can be sure that it does not unwind after the active use of homemade weapons. When both blanks are wrapped, the ends of the rope must be sealed with tape.



Home-made weapons in the form of nunchucks are often used to conduct costumed games. In this case, you need to remember that the handle should be comfortable and properly positioned in the hand. At its end, you need to make a ring. Such a detail will perform not only a decorative function. Its main purpose is the convenient use of weapons.

So, you need to do nunchucks with your own hands. How to make nunchucks with a ring - the question is quite relevant. To do this, just twist the paper tube. Once the ring is made, you need to attach it to the product using tape or glue.

Next, you need to fasten both sticks with a strong rope. It is advisable to stretch it through one blank completely and tie a knot. Then the rope must be inserted into the second stick and tied tightly at the end.

Baby paper nunchucks

As mentioned above, the nunchucks are considered edged weapons, so for children‚Äôs games they are, of course, prohibited. But if you know how to make paper nunchucks for a child, you can please your child and let him reincarnate for a while as a ninja turtle or as a favorite karate hero.



To make children's nunchucks, you need to prepare:

  • wide adhesive tape;
  • 2 old magazines;
  • strong rope;
  • scissors.

Both magazines need to be rolled up and secured with tape on the ends. You need to turn so that a rope can be passed into it. After the blanks are ready, you need to wrap them on top with colored tape. Then through 1 stick you should stretch the rope and tie a knot. The same actions need to be done with the second workpiece.

A bit of history





So, how to make paper combat nunchucks, we figured it out. Now it's time to plunge a little into history and find out where and when this edged weapon appeared. Previously, it was assumed that their homeland is Japan, but soon this version was denied.

Scientists came to the conclusion that the combat nunchucks came to us from China, since it was from this country that the prototype of the nunchucks was brought. After Japan took control of Okinawa, officials tightened laws. The peasants were forbidden to carry weapons, so the former soldiers were forced to invent new ways of protection from improvised means. So the nunchucks were born, which are still in demand to this day. Foreign films made a big contribution to their popularity, in particular those where the legendary Bruce Lee was shot.
