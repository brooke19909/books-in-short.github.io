Where can I send and where to pick up?





The availability of such a service as the transfer of Hummingbirds through Sberbank to the majority of the population of our country is due to the geographical distribution of the network of branch offices. Registration fees

As mentioned earlier, the fee for the transfer of Hummingbirds from Sberbank is quite loyal, it makes up only 1.5% of the amount sent. The minimum and maximum thresholds of the commission fee are also envisaged - 150 and 1000 rubles, respectively.

An additional part of the commission is paid by the recipient in the case of an international transfer.

And even if the sender decided to return the already sent transfer back, he will receive it minus the commission that he paid during the transfer, and he will also have to pay a penalty to the bank in the amount of 150 rubles.

What documents are needed for registration

The minimum set of documents is another undoubted advantage of such a service as a hummingbird transfer. Sberbank does not oblige you to open accounts, e-wallets, cards, etc. No need to store a lot of information in your head. All that should be with you is your passport!

How to make and receive a transfer?

In order to make the treasured translation, you need to know only the surname, name and patronymic of the recipient. Here you need to be careful, the data must fully correspond to what is written in the passport. Danger lurks, for example, in the pairs "II", "E-E", "b-b" and so on. Remember that Sberbank may not translate Hummingbirds if a spelling error is detected.

In exchange for this data, the sender is issued a receipt in which the transfer code is indicated. The recipient is left to come to the Sberbank branch with a passport, name the code received from the sender, and you're done!

Thus, a minimum of time and effort is required if Sberbank makes an internal Russian or international transfer of Hummingbird. How to get the transfer amount? Upon presentation of a passport at any office of the bank, money will be easily issued to the recipient 10 minutes after departure in Russia and an hour after departure from abroad.
