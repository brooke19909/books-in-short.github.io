How to put money on "Tele2" through a bank card? Tips & Tricks

Today, most payments are made through bank cards. These payments include salaries, remittances, scholarships, as well as many other transfers. In order to pay for goods and services, there is no need to withdraw money from an ATM, just pay them through the terminal using a plastic card. One of the types of such payments is the recharge of your cell phone. How to deposit money on Tele2 through a bank card will be described in this article.



How to pay "Tele2" using a card?

In order to replenish the balance, you can use any bank card issued by a Russian bank. Before this procedure, you should clarify in advance with your operator servicing the hotline of a banking organization the possibility of such replenishment. The fact is that some organizations specifically block such functions. It is important to remember that to unlock it you need to do everything yourself. Namely: enter your mobile phone number, which is planned to be replenished with a cash amount. Next, you will need to enter the parameters of the plastic card. After that, you should once again check all the completed data regarding the payment and confirm the transfer. All of the above actions will need to be carried out through the official website of your bank, where, if necessary, you will need to enter additional information regarding the owner of the credit card. This applies to cases when the card is untethered. In subsequent cases, the replenishment of the balance will be many times faster.

The main thing to do is to check on the ATM display all the completed fields and make sure that there are no errors. So, it becomes clear: there is nothing complicated in depositing money on Tele2 through a bank card. It is enough to find the nearest device and complete the operation.



How to fund your account using the Internet?

In order to replenish the balance via the Internet, you need to go to the website of the mobile operator, and then go to the payment menu. Next, you need to select the payment by card and indicate the mobile phone number and the amount of the payment. After this, it is necessary to proceed to filling in the details of the card itself, namely, indicate the credit card number, expiration date, name of the holder and CVV2 / CVC2 code. When all the data has been filled in and checked for errors, the payment should be confirmed. Money must arrive instantly. For example, if you put money on "Tele2" through a bank card of Sberbank, then you first need to attach the card to your phone number. This step will simplify further payments and save time.

How to deposit money on "Tele2" through a bank card without commission?

The most convenient way that does not even require a commission is payment by SMS. Using this service, you can deposit money to "Tele2" through a bank card by sending only one message to number 109. The amount of payment should be indicated in the text of the SMS message. In this case, the number should be in rubles and without cents. This opportunity is provided to subscribers whose card account is tied to a mobile number. Each message sent to number 109 is not charged, so it costs the cardholder free of charge. Thus, to save money, you can put money on Tele2 through a bank card without a commission.
