Industrial city Angarsk: hotels for visitors

Angarsk is a young city, it was founded in 1945. It is located in the Irkutsk region, its population is about 240 thousand people.

What is famous for Angarsk?

The city has well-developed industrial production. Here are oil refineries, there are factories that produce household chemicals, polymer products, fertilizers, building materials, nitric acid and ammonium nitrate. Many city residents work at the large enterprise of the nuclear industry - the Angarsk electrolysis chemical plant. The product of its production is enriched uranium.

The activities of enterprises not only bring revenues to the city budget, but also cause significant damage to its ecology. The environmental situation in Angarsk is tense, the city has the status of a settlement, occupying one of the places in the ten most environmentally dangerous cities in Russia. But in spite of everything here they care about the comfort of its inhabitants, they are actively engaged in beautification. The city has a railway and bus station, 10 km from the city is Irkutsk airport.



Angarsk - hotels of the city

A high level of industry allows us to develop the necessary infrastructure, which the guests of the city of Angarsk offer. Hotels here are represented by a sufficient number - there are more than ten of them. Other accommodation facilities provide hostel services - hostels, private apartments. Due to the adverse ecology in the vicinity, tourists practically do not come to Angarsk.



Hotels here are focused on accommodation of people who have come to the city on a business trip. Based on the specifics of the work, many of them require a fairly long stay in the city, so the hotels of Angarsk are cheap, but despite this fact, guests are pleasant and comfortable to live in.

Many hotel establishments are located near the city center, for example, the Pushkin Hotel and a small complex with the corresponding name Small Hotel. Here it is possible to get to the required place by public transport, which is very convenient, especially for people who do not know the city of Angarsk.

Auto-Stop and Old Castle hotels are often chosen by overnight travelers as they drive through the city to rest on Lake Baikal. After all, they are conveniently located near adjacent highways.
