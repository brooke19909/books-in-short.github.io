Sony's new flagship smartphone: Xperia Z5 with advanced features

A modern smartphone is the gadget that accompanies its owner literally around the clock and is intensively used by him to perform a variety of actions: conducting telephone conversations, sending emails, accessing the Internet, receiving photos and the like. Such multifunctionality provided these devices with very high popularity, and the mass of offers from manufacturing companies set a very high standard of requirements for both individual parameters and the device as a whole.

Sony Xperia Z5 Sony's development perfectly fulfills all the functions of a modern smartphone. To a large extent, this is the implementation of its electronic circuits based on an eight-core 64-bit processor. Its computational capabilities, among other things, provide a very high degree of noise reduction, which makes it comfortable to conduct a conversation even on a noisy street.





3-gigabyte RAM provides normal performance even for complex applications and is supplemented by a built-in flash memory of 32 gigabytes. With a lack of regular memory, it can be increased with removable microSD format media, which is installed in the side sliding slot.

A 5-inch touch screen with a pixel density of more than four hundred dpi has the option to automatically adjust the brightness, useful in different levels of lighting. The multi-touch system buffer captures up to ten consecutively entered commands. The screen locks when the smartphone approaches your ear, which prevents accidental disconnection when you touch the end button with your cheek.

More details about other parameters of the device and prices can be found on the website http://www.foxtrot.com.ua.

The unconditional skate of the new Sony is its camera. The 23-megapixel matrix in combination with hybrid autofocus with a guaranteed response time of three hundredths of a second allows you to take very clear photos literally on the fly and on the first try. The camera works great both in normal lighting and at night. Fivefold scaling makes it possible to provide a large increase in the image without losing its quality.

The capacity of a regular battery does not reach three thousand mAh a little, which, however, is enough for normal operation of the device for two days without recharging due to the economy of electronics.

The Xperia Z5 enclosure provides IP65 protection against harmful environmental factors. This means that he is not afraid of spray during a conversation in the rain, spilled tea and even an accidental fall into the sink.
