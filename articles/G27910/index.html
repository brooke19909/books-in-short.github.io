Dessole Olympos Beach Resort 4 * (Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Greece is a country chosen by tourists from all over the world. After all, the mild Mediterranean climate, the exotic of nature and the atmosphere of ancient civilizations are perfectly combined here. The Greek islands are world famous for their picturesque beaches, and therefore travelers often choose the legendary island of Rhodes as their stopping place. It is here that the cozy Dessole Olympos Beach Resort 4 * hotel is located.

Before the trip, every tourist tries to find the maximum amount of information about a particular place. So where is the hotel located and what conditions can be expected? Are there any conditions for relaxing with a child? Is it possible to have fun here? How do other tourists comment on this place? The answers to these questions will be of interest to many readers.

Location Description





Naturally, the first important point is the location of the Dessole Olympos Beach Resort 4 * hotel . Faliraki, a picturesque resort town on the island of Rhodes - it was here, almost on the very shore, that a small hotel was built, which had already managed to shelter hundreds of thousands of tourists.

By the way, the location of the hotel is very convenient. Just a few meters away is a secluded pebble and sand beach. Faliraki city center, with all its shops, restaurants and entertainment, can be reached in a few minutes. The ancient city of Rhodes is only 12 km away. By the way, there is a bus stop across the road from the hotel, so you can freely move around the neighborhood, because public transport here runs regularly.

What does the hotel look like?

Dessole Olympos Beach Resort 4 * - a picturesque, small hotel, which consists of one six-story building. By the way, it was built in 1992. And in 2013, the last cardinal reconstruction was carried out, and therefore the building and rooms fully comply with all the standards and requirements of a modern traveler. The hotel has several elevators, and the infrastructure is well thought out.

The hotel area is small, but incredibly beautiful. Here you can hide in the shade of trees, enjoy the breathtaking views of the surroundings, admire the beautifully arranged flower beds, and just relax, breathing in the sea air.

What kind of food is offered by the guest?





Delicious food is one of the attributes of quality relaxation. What can the Dessole Olympos Beach Resort 4 * hotel offer in this regard? Greece is a country famous for its delicious cuisine and true hospitality. The hotel offers its guests to choose any convenient meal plan, but the all-inclusive system is especially popular.

Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served as a buffet in the central hotel restaurant. Reviews suggest that the menu here is varied - guests are offered the most delicious dishes of local, as well as Mediterranean and international cuisine. From time to time, theme nights are held here dedicated to the cuisine and culture of a country.

On the beach there is a cozy, colorful tavern, where during the day you can get drinks, cocktails, snacks, desserts and even fresh, delicious pastries. Another small bar is located by the pool - the menu here mainly drinks and cocktails, but sometimes serves ice cream and some snacks.

Relax on the beach: what does the hotel offer?





Naturally, tourists gathering on the island of Rhodes, primarily rely on a fun beach holiday. Dessole Olympos Beach Resort 4 * hotel is located almost on the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea. This stretch of coast is relatively secluded; hotel guests spend their time here mainly.

According to reviews, the entrance to the sea is relatively convenient, but there is a strip of small pebbles, but because it is still worth getting beach shoes. Guests at this hotel can count on free sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as clean beach towels. There is a tavern on the seashore, where at any time of the day you can have a good snack and ask for a chilled drink.

Fans of more active pastime will also not have to be bored. Tourists can go boating, boat, water skiing, parasailing and sailing, enjoy the beauty of the underwater world, thanks to the diving center. Naturally, such attractions and services are not included in the price, but the prices here are not higher than on other beaches of the island.

Range of additional services for guests





Of course, hotel guests can count on a certain range of additional services. To begin with, it is worth noting that in public areas there is access to the Internet, which is very important for most tourists. Sometimes the hotel is used for small business meetings, seminars or even celebrations - for this there is a comfortable conference room for 40 people.

Guests can use the hotel parking. Nearby in the city you can easily rent a car, bicycle or other type of transport. Also, tourists can rent a safe from the administrator for a small fee, and hand over unnecessary things to the luggage storage room (especially important for tourists who travel around the island without stopping anywhere else).

Dessole Olympos Beach Resort 4 * (Greece, Faliraki): what entertainment does the hotel offer?

Of course, the main attraction of the hotel is the picturesque beach. Nevertheless, a fun lesson can be found on the territory of the hotel itself. For example, there is a swimming pool, and on the terrace with sun loungers, guests relax, sunbathe and just chat with each other.





Tennis fans have a good time on local courts. Here you can rent balls, rackets, and also pay for lighting at night. Guests can play billiards and table tennis. You will have the opportunity to do aerobics and some other sports. And guests love to relax in the spa, where customers are offered a variety of anti-aging and wellness treatments, as well as several types of massage. The hotel also has a team of animators who know how to amuse travelers. In the evenings, live music is often played here, and various performances and shows are also organized for guests.

Vacation with children: are there appropriate conditions?

For parents traveling with children, some other issues become a priority. In particular, they are interested in what conditions for a child's rest can be offered at the Dessole Olympos Beach Resort 4 * hotel.





This hotel is primarily designed for family vacations, and therefore parents can count on some additional services, including, for example, a chair in the restaurant and a folding bed. And the guys do not have to be bored. In addition to beach activities, children can find interesting activities on the territory of the hotel itself.

For example, in the pool there is a small shallow section where children can swim, but only under the supervision of their parents. There is also a fairly spacious playground where the guys can have fun in the company of peers. A children's club is also constantly working, which accepts small tourists aged 4 to 12 years. According to reviews, children have a great time on site.
