Mobile Transfer

If you start to describe how to transfer money from MegaFon to MegaFon, you need to start with the main service - Mobile Transfer. It is provided directly by MegaFon and is completely legal.





In order to send money, you will need to send a USSD request. That is, in the field for dialing, enter the following: * 133 * transfer amount * recipient number #. To make it more clear, we give an example example. Suppose you are thinking of sending 500 rubles to your friend, and his number is 89264985612. In this case, your USSD request should look like this: * 133 * 500 * 89264985612 #. After that, press the call key.

Please note that there are certain limits on the transfer of money, all of them are different depending on the location of the addressee, so contact technical support for more detailed information. You can find out about the transfer commission there.

Translation on the MegaFon website

Let's look at another way how to transfer money from MegaFon to MegaFon without commission . As you can understand by the name of the subtitle, we will be sending through the official MegaFon website. To do this, type money.megafon.ru. From the main menu, click on the link "To another phone."
