Hilton Taba Resort 5 *, Taba, Egypt: tourist reviews

This article is dedicated to the Hilton Taba Resort 5 * hotel. Taba is the name of the city where the hotel is located. Taba‚Äôs border status does not at all preclude its merits as a resort. On the contrary, it makes it a great starting point for excursions in Israel, Jordan and Saudi Arabia. Taking into account the fact that many Russian tourists are now arriving in Egypt in a roundabout way, the Hilton Taba Resort 5 * border location is very advantageous. From Israeli customs to the hotel reception stomp about two hundred meters. And from the Egyptian one even closer. And from the windows of the room (if it is located on the upper floors of the building), you can contemplate the Holy Land and Jordan. From the airport of the Israeli city of Eilat to the hotel - about ten kilometers. Those tourists who are already familiar with the high standards of services in the Hilton hotel chain were not disappointed with its representation in Taba.





Territory

In fact, the full name of the hotel is Hilton Taba Resort 5 * & Taba Nelson Village. It consists of two parts. Hilton Taba Resort occupies a ten-story high-rise building with elevators. And "Nelson Village" is a scattering of two-story nice cottages. But all these buildings are located on a common territory, which is a huge well-groomed garden. Beautiful winding paths trace green lawns, flickering between flowering bushes and trembling palm trees. To prevent guests from getting lost in the vast territory, there are signs at the crossroads. The large snow-white building is built in such a way that most of its windows face the amazingly blue Aqaba Bay in the Red Sea. The building was erected in 2000, but still, thanks to constant renovations, shines with novelty. Most rooms have balconies. If you ordered the cheapest standard - suggest reviews - you can officially get a terrace for fifteen dollars.





Where live

Four hundred and ten rooms in the Hilton Taba Resort 5 * (Taba, Egypt) are divided into categories with fancy names. It's not so easy to figure out all of these king sunset suites and queen family hilton deluxe. The reviews give us a little hint. All numbers that have the word "nelson" in their name, refer to the bungalow. In the building, guest rooms are oriented to the beach with pools, sunset and east. By price category, rooms are divided into guest rooms (which corresponds to the standard), suites and deluxe. But, as befits a five-star hotel, it gives coziness and comfort to all its guests. All rooms have marble bathrooms with a bidet, hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers. The bedrooms have quiet air conditioning, a TV with numerous channels (including one Russian). In the queen guest room, in addition to the balcony, there is a desk and Wi-Fi. Deluxe rooms have a minibar and a safe. The suites are complemented by living rooms. The last two categories have balconies or terraces with sea views.

Beach and Pools

Many tourists choose the Hilton Taba Resort 5 * hotel as a convenient strategic point for traveling to Egypt, Israel, Jordan and Saudi Arabia. But also the hotel is chosen by lovers of beach holidays. The hotel has a private sandy beach. Maybe the divers who visited the Ras Muhamed nature reserve will disappoint the underwater world near the shore of the hotel. But compensation will serve as an excursion to the island of Pharaoh. It is located only eight kilometers from the coast. In addition to coral gardens on the island, you can see the medieval fortress of Saladdin. Another seven kilometers away is a bay called the Fjord, which also has many underwater caves and interesting inhabitants. On the beach of the hotel there is all the necessary infrastructure for recreation. Water activities are offered. An instructor will teach you how to curb a board with a sail. The hotel itself has two outdoor pools. One of them is filled with fresh, and the other with sea water.



How to entertain

Many tourists look forward to a relaxing beach holiday in Taba. And in this sense, the animation team at Hilton is fully consistent with the aspirations of the guests. Guys and girls work, but so as not to impose their services on those who want to take a quiet nap or relax with beach reading. In the evenings, animators arrange all kinds of shows, hold funny competitions and lotteries. By the way, children also have a mini club. There, special animators draw with them, conduct master classes and play. Well, for curious tourists there is a tour desk at the hotel.

Excursions

Reviews highly insistently recommend not to lie on the beach with a seal, but to go explore the sights that Taba (resort) generously gives. Hilton Taba Resort is located door to door with an Israeli border, so when can you touch the relics of the Holy Land now? Jordanian Petra is also waiting for her guests. But in the Egyptian part of Sinai there are many attractions. And the first of them is the already mentioned island of Pharaoh. By the way, a jeep trip to the Colored Canyon or a pilgrimage to Mount Moses is much more convenient to make from Taba than from Sharm. You can also book an excursion to the smallest diocese in the world - the monastery of St. Catherine. Only need to dress more modestly. The main attraction of this monastery is the indestructible dome. Numerous early Christian texts are also kept here.
