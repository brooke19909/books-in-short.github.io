Beach: amenities and entertainment

What in terms of beach holidays can the hotel complex ‚ÄúBahia Lara‚Äù offer? Antalya is a resort that is primarily famous for its picturesque coast. The distance from the main building to the beach is no more than 100 meters.

This is a private coastal strip 150 meters long - only hotel guests have a rest here. It features wide sun loungers with mattresses and umbrellas, as well as changing cabins. Additional service for tourists

This is a five star hotel, so guests can count on all possible amenities. To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel has its own laundry and dry cleaning services, there is a currency exchange office, and bank cards are accepted for payment.

Guests enjoy free hotel parking, which is guarded round the clock. The hotel also offers to rent a car (if you have documents), a moped or a mountain bike - having your own transport opens up a lot of opportunities for tourists.

All public areas have Wi-Fi, which is very convenient. In addition, you can always sit in a cozy and quiet Internet cafe.

The hotel often becomes the venue for various business events. If necessary, guests can use office equipment or a computer. There is a small meeting room, as well as three conference rooms with a capacity of 100 to 600 people. Of course, the hotel staff takes care of the organization of events. By the way, the premises are used for holidays. If you wish, you can use the services of a professional photographer (the hotel has its own photo studio).





Entertainment: how to organize leisure?

At the hotel, every tourist will find something to their liking. As is customary, most of the courtyard is reserved for the pool, around which sunbeds and umbrellas are arranged in even rows. Nearby there is a swimming pool for sports swimming.

It offers sports facilities, in particular, a place for playing football, basketball and volleyball. There is a fitness center with a gym, as well as experienced instructors who are always ready to advise. By the way, they regularly conduct various sports for groups of tourists. Guests enjoy playing darts and boccia, practicing archery. There are two tennis courts, next to which the school is located - you can take several lessons from professionals.

The hotel offers a bowling club, billiards tables, a room with slot machines. There is also a spacious amphitheater, on the territory of which various shows are held every evening - you can enjoy dancing and acrobatic performances, performances by local groups and even fashion shows. And at night everyone is invited to a disco - admission is free, only drinks are paid extra.

You can have fun outside the Baia Lara Hotel. Turkey is a country with an ancient history and interesting traditions. The surrounding area is full of museums and attractions that are worth seeing, so do not refuse the services of a tour desk that works directly in the hotel (there are Russian-speaking guides).
