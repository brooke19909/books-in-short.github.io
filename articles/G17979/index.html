A new format has come to Vladivostok: Illusion Park cancels standards

The new interactive entertainment center was acquired by Vladivostok. "Illusion Park" opened in the shopping and entertainment complex "Cheryomushki".





Object design

When planning, key attention was paid to family leisure. While adults play bowling, children can plunge into the world of virtual reality, and teenagers will appreciate real quests.

After exciting entertainments, you can have a delicious meal in a variety of catering establishments. Companies such as Yummy Pizza & Kebabs, Dumpling Republic, Ukushu, Gelato Italiano, Toki Sushi and others work here. Fans of aromatic drinks will appreciate a visit to the coffee shop with delicious pastries.





The network of the complex includes the cinemas "Ussuri", "Ocean" and the eponymous "Illusion", which Vladivostok is so proud of now. "Illusion Park" also combined cinematographic complexes in Nakhodka. The entire network is managed by Cinema LLC.

Grand opening

"Illusion Park" (Vladivostok) held the opening on a grand scale. On the fifth and sixth floors of the Cheryomushki shopping center there are many entertainment venues. The center combines several different-format services at once:

  • cinema halls with widescreen screens;
  • bowling;
  • quest park;
  • movie bar.




"Illusion Park" (Vladivostok) on September 9, 2016 was opened by the famous showman Sergei Svetlakov. He brought the film "Groom" to the audience, where he plays the main role. The actor was also given the honor to cut the red ribbon for the audience.

Fascinating contests were organized for all those present and interesting entertainments were prepared. It seemed that on this day in the center gathered all of Vladivostok. "Illusion Park" met the most daring expectations of visitors.

Movie premieres

The opening of Illusion Park in Vladivostok took place with the premiere of the film, where S. Svetlakov played the main role. In the film "Groom" he also worked as a screenwriter and performed producer functions.

True, only the elite who had invitations could get to the premiere of the comedy on the opening day. As Svetlakov himself said, the heroes of his film have very real prototypes. He emphasizes with his plot that you do not need to look back at the West or America, but just look around. It was in the Russians living in the outback that the mentality was preserved in which humor, kindness and many other qualities were combined.





Before the show, S. Svetlakov talked with the audience, admired the city (while his wife was in Vladivostok for the first time).

You can watch the film, but without meeting the star actor, in the cinema halls of the Illusion Park institution (Vladivostok). Tickets were not sold on September 9, but now any premiere can be seen in the forefront.

Beginning September 10, 2016, the latest news from Russian and foreign cinema was included in the network of displays. For the convenience of the audience and taking into account the different wealth of visitors, tickets are divided into categories: regular and VIP.

Illusion Park holds various events. Films included in the program of the Pacific Pacific Meridian International Film Festival were shown at its base.

More about entertainment areas

For residents of the city and its guests, the complex is equipped with as many as five auditoriums, which are combined by the Illusion Park (Cheryomushki). Vladivostok first received cinemas in the premises of the trading premises.

Fans of watching films under the rustling of popcorn appreciated the self-service cinema bar, unique for the city. Here you can buy your favorite treat and various drinks on your own.

According to the founders of Illusion Park, they wanted to put the entertainment center on a whole new level of perception. For this to happen, completely different areas of leisure were gathered in one place.

Innovation for Vladivostok

The founders tell why the shopping and entertainment center was chosen as the site for the opening of the center. Modern man does not have time to devote time to entertainment. Therefore, entrepreneurs are trying to combine different types of services in one place, and a shopping center where people make purchases is an ideal option. If such formats are familiar to many large cities, then for Vladivostok this is an innovation.

"Illusion Park" allows people to relax after hard work, escape from everyday hustle and bustle, amaze children and diversify leisure activities for teenagers.

Currently, the institution organizes large-scale film festivals, holds interesting events. The halls of cinemas are equipped with the most modern equipment, which allows showing films in any accessible format.

In addition, there are entertainment areas, the uniqueness of which is recognized throughout the Far Eastern District. Despite this, the park is developing and striving to give its visitors even more. For this, the founders and employees attract new partners and introduce the most fashionable innovations.
