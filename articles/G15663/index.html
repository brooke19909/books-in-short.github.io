Georgia, resort of Kobuleti: reviews of hotels and resorts

Kobuleti is a popular Georgian resort located 24 km from Batumi. Thousands of tourists visit this small town annually, mainly from Armenia, Belarus and Ukraine. Recently, this resort village is becoming increasingly popular among Russian vacationers. According to most tourists, Kobuleti is best suited for a quiet family vacation.

Location Features

To get to Kobuleti, the reviews of which tourists are wonderful, from Batumi you can either by minibus or taxi. The road takes no more than half an hour. The fare on the minibus is $ 1-1.5 (63-93 rubles.), By taxi - about $ 15 (940 rubles.). Kobuleti is located right next to the sea. In the immediate vicinity of it is another popular Georgian resort - Ureki, famous for its healing magnetic sands.





Resort Benefits

The town of Kobuleti is small. Only about 20-25 thousand people live here. Judging by the reviews available on the Web, the townspeople are very friendly. Not too clean, but not particularly littered streets - this is what, among other things, is characterized by Kobuleti. Reviews of tourists about the appearance of this city are mostly good.

The main advantage of the resort is not crowded. Vacationers here are much fewer than in Batumi, and therefore, housing can be rented much cheaper. Holidaymakers praise this resort also for a very clear sea. As many tourists say, in Batumi it is much dirtier.

Another advantage of the resort of Kobuleti, according to most vacationers, is the ability to inexpensively and at the same time have a tasty lunch or breakfast. There are not too many cafes and restaurants in Kobuleti. But in most cases, the menu in them is very diverse, and dishes are prepared with the freshest ingredients.

Getting to the sea from almost any Kobuleti hotel is not too long. The fact is that this town literally stretched along the coast. And therefore, all the streets are located in close proximity to the sea.





Rest in Kobuleti: reviews of the infrastructure of the town

Most tourists find this town beautiful and convenient for spending holidays. One of the streets of Kobuleti, David Agmashenebeli, during the USSR was the longest in the country. Today, of course, she has already lost this status. However st. David Agmashenebeli is still the longest in Georgia (14 km). It is she who is considered the center of the resort life of Kobuleti. If you wish, you can visit a cafe or restaurant, go to the shooting range, a karaoke bar, make purchases in a supermarket, etc.

Climate in Kobuleti

The weather in the summertime at this resort is just great. The climate here is the same as in most other places on the Black Sea coast. That is warm subtropical. The only thing that can overshadow the vacation in Kobuleti (Georgia), whose climate reviews are mostly good, are summer heavy rains. However, the sky in this town in summer is quite rare. In any case, it does not rain for too long.

The holiday season in this part of the Black Sea coast lasts from late May to mid-November. It was at this time that the most comfortable holiday in Kobuleti (Georgia) was considered. Reviews about the holidays spent at this resort during the summer season among its guests are only excellent. However, most tourists believe that it is best to come here in the second half of June. At this time, there are not so many vacationers in the town, and the sea is already warmed up enough. Housing in Kobuleti

According to many tourists, there are not so many very good high-class hotels in this town. However, there are simply a huge number of inexpensive budget mini-hotels here. Because of this, some vacationers even consider that Kobuleti is very similar to a huge communal apartment. Nevertheless, according to most tourists, housing in this town, though not fashionable, but quite comfortable and convenient. You can rent a good apartment even in high season at this resort for no more than $ 10 per day (this is about 630 rubles).

In the hotels of the town, approximately the same as in Sochi, there is everything you need - showers, bathrooms, sometimes swimming pools. The rooms are equipped, depending on the cost, with air conditioning or fans, televisions, refrigerators.





Experienced tourists advise beginners to rent a room in Kobuleti, reviews of which are very good, including due to low housing prices, not on the main street, because it is very noisy here, but in the city itself. It is better to book rooms at this resort in advance. Quickly finding a suitable inexpensive room in high season here is quite difficult.

The best hotels of Kobuleti

The cost of a room in the most expensive hotels of this resort can reach up to $ 100 per person per day (approximately 6300 rubles). However, most tourists, as already mentioned, prefer to rent rooms for living in ordinary mini-hotels of Kobuleti. Reviews of all these small hotels are not bad. But Ponto, Kobuleti and Chveni Ezo are considered the most convenient of the inexpensive (average price category).

The opinion of tourists about the hotel "Ponto"

This hotel has earned good reviews from tourists primarily for its convenient location and friendly staff. You can get to the sea from the territory of this hotel in just 3 minutes. In the courtyard of the hotel there is a pool with artificial waterfall, a bar and parking. Tourists have the opportunity to use the Wi-Fi network for free. The hotel grounds are guarded around the clock. If desired, guests can also take a walk in the garden with exotic plants.

The Ponto Hotel has its own restaurant, among other things. Here vacationers can order dishes of national and European cuisine. Buffet breakfast is included in the price. Each room has a modern cable TV. Most rooms have air conditioning.

A room in this hotel can only be booked by booking. Moreover, the administration requires an advance payment 5 days before arrival by transfer to a bank account. If the person who wants to rent a room does not transfer money, he may be refused accommodation. The rooms in this hotel are about 2000 rubles. per person per day.





Chveni Ezo Hotel reviews

Hotel "Chveni Ezo" is also very popular among vacationers vacationing in Kobuleti. Tourists respond about it as being very clean and comfortable. Each room has air conditioning, shower, refrigerator, TV. Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel. According to tourists vacationing in this hotel, linen in the rooms is changed every 3 days. The hotel staff is very friendly to its guests.

In general, many tourists advise vacationers to rent a room in this hotel in the resort of Kobuleti. Reviews of 2016 about this hotel are in any case very good. The hotel "Chveni Ezo" earned some complaints from tourists only for the noise. Room prices at this hotel start at 1,500 rubles per person per day.

Is it possible to heal?

Thus, many tourists find hotels in Kobuleti very convenient. Vacationers have good reviews about mini-hotels and about middle and high class establishments in this resort. However, in Kobuleti you can not only relax well, but also heal. Once upon a time in this town for the first time in Russia a private sanatorium was built (in 1911). In Soviet times, Kobuleti, like many other settlements on the Black Sea coast, was considered a health resort. There were just a huge number of sanatoriums and dispensaries here. Kobuleti gradually turned into one of the best resorts in the USSR. During the collapse of the Soviet Union, the affairs of the city, of course, fell into decay. However, nothing stands still, and over time the tourist infrastructure of the resort began to develop again.





Today in Kobuleti, reviews of vacationers about which are not bad, including because of the opportunity to improve their health, there are many good resorts. Near this resort town beat mineral springs with healing water. It is used in the sanatoriums of the town as a medical and dining room. Many vacationers are advised to purchase a ticket in one of the local health centers to treat the gastrointestinal tract or kidneys. There are also sanatoriums in Kobuleti specializing in diseases of the cardiovascular system, pulmonary and nervous.

If desired, in the Kobuleti dispensaries can be treated, including tourists who came to rest savages. The cost of health courses is approximately $ 400 (25,000 rubles).

Reviews about the beach town

According to most tourists, a somewhat gloomy view is what distinguishes the beach in Kobuleti. Reviews about the coastline of this town, however, are pretty good. Swimming and sunbathing on Kobuleti beach is relatively convenient. The fact is that the coastline here is covered with small pebbles. You can even walk on it barefoot, which is uncharacteristic for this part of the Black Sea coast. For example, in the same Batumi, the coastline is strewn with rather large cobblestones, and it is simply impossible to walk along it without shoes.

A small number of people on the beach is what, among other things, is characterized by Kobuleti. Reviews on the coastline of this resort on the Net are mostly good. Some vacationers find the city beach of this town only a little muddy. However, if you wish, you can always rent a catamaran, boat or boat. In some places on the seashore there are inflatable slides for children. The resort administration promises to soon organize such entertainment for tourists as diving. If desired, you can also take a sun lounger and an umbrella on the beach. On the promenade of Kobuleti there are many small cafes and restaurants. Here, tourists are offered a huge number of delicious and inexpensive dishes and drinks.

Amusement park: reviews

Translated into Russian, the word "cicinatela" means "firefly." There is an amusement park in Kobuleti only at night. Every evening, thousands of lights are lit here. In this regard, the name of the park was chosen. If you wish, you can ride here on a big Ferris wheel or on some fun merry-go-round. Experienced tourists are advised to first go around the entire park and choose the most suitable attractions, and then return to the ticket office located at the exit and purchase tickets for each of them. Walking to the gates every time you want to ride on one or another carousel will simply be uncomfortable.

In general, the Tsitsinatela park has earned good reviews from tourists. Spending time here is pretty fun. It is best, of course, to come here with the whole family.

Peter Fortress

Many experienced tourists to vacationers vacationing in Kobuleti are advised to visit this interesting attraction. Reviews about the fortress of Peter on the Net are extremely positive. It is located in the Mtirala nature reserve in the territory of Tsikhisdziri village. There used to be a land road in this place, connecting western Georgia with Persia and Armenia. Therefore, it was here that Emperor Justinian built the fortified city.

The fortress got its name because it is located almost on a steep cliff. In Georgian, the word "Peter" is translated as "stone." From the fortress a wonderful view of the Black Sea opens.





How to get to Kobuleti

It is best to go to this resort through Batumi, where you can fly, for example, by plane. Direct flights are provided here from Moscow and Minsk. You can also get to Batumi by sea from Sochi. In addition, many tourists who want to relax in Kobuleti, book a ticket for a boat, following from the main resort of Russia to Poti. From this town to the destination, as well as from Batumi, the easiest way to get on a minibus. After registration of documents at customs, foreign citizens can stay in Georgia for up to 90 days.
