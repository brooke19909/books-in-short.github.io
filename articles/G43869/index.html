"Aminevskaya" - hotel (Moscow): address, description, reviews

Arriving in the capital, many pre-view the list of Moscow hotels in order to choose the most convenient accommodation option. I must say that in the main Russian city there are a lot of hotels for every taste and budget. Prices in them depend on the stardom, location and, of course, on the selected room. Among the proposed options there are many three-star hotels, which, being relatively inexpensive, offer a very wide range of services necessary for a comfortable stay. Among them is Aminievskaya. The hotel is located on the highway of the same name.



Address

It is located near the center of the capital, two kilometers from Kutuzovsky Prospekt, in the calm Ochakovo-Matveevsky district. "Aminievskaya" - a hotel whose address all residents of the Western District know - is located near the Novodevichy Convent and Borodino Panorama. Within walking distance - Vorobyovy Gory, Poklonnaya, Central Ring Road and Skolkovo (innovation center). Therefore, those who decide to stay in it can easily see all the nearby attractions. The exact address of the hotel is 5. Aminievskoye Shosse (Moscow), in just twenty minutes, you can drive from it to Red Square - the heart of the capital.

In addition, the hotel is conveniently located in relation to many business centers. For example, Vereyskaya Plaza can be reached on foot in just fifteen minutes. Within a few minutes you can drive to West Plaza, Kutuzoff Tower, West Park and Dorokhoff Plaza. This hotel on Aminievskoe highway is located near many medical centers - SC them. Bakulev Institute of Clinical Cardiology, Kurenkov Ophthalmology Clinic, Life Line Reproduction Center, located on Mozhayskoye Shosse, and the Dikul Medical Rehabilitation Center in Kuntsevo, which can be reached in twenty minutes. For guests who come to the capital and are sent to these medical facilities, the Aminievskaya Hotel (Moscow) provides special discounts.

Aminievskaya is a hotel that also offers round-the-clock accommodation in rooms, free Internet and the ability to rent a car. Hotel Policies

Despite the fact that the reception of the Aminievskaya Hotel operates according to a predetermined check-in and check-out schedule, nevertheless, upon arrival of guests at an unplanned time, paperwork and check-in are carried out immediately, of course, subject to availability.

Tourists with children can get one extra folding bed in the room. If you wish to smoke at the hotel, please inform the property in advance. No pets are allowed in this hotel.

Additional discounts are provided for those who need the Aminievskaya Hotel (Moscow) for a long stay.

Reviews
