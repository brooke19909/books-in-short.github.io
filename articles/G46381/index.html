Mycerin Pyramid in Cairo

The civilization of Egypt is one of the oldest. It was the Egyptians who built one of the seven wonders of the world - the Egyptian pyramids. Scientists still cannot understand how such a monumental structure was erected with accessible building technologies.

Valley of the Pyramids



In total, more than 100 buildings were found in Egypt, but the most famous of them are located near Cairo, in the Giza Valley. There are three ancient memorabilia here: Cheops, Chefren and the Pyramid of Mikerin. Also in the large funerary complex is the Sphinx and a museum with an ancient boat located inside. Egyptian pyramids, regardless of size, have great historical and cultural value. Interest in them does not fade today.

It was laid in the southwestern part of the Giza complex, at some distance from the tombs of Cheops and Chefren. Mykerin's tomb has the shape of a regular pyramid. Parameters of the tomb of Mykerin



The nearest pyramid of Chefren is 200 meters from the tomb. It rises by 62 meters, and the length of one side is 109 meters. Mykerin pyramid has the shape of a regular pyramid. Initially, it was 66 meters high, but time and the desert did their job. Moreover, due to the fact that the pyramid was partially protected by sand drift, the length of the sides and part of the outer lining at the base were preserved. As conceived by the designer, the pyramid was to have a base size of 60x60 meters. However, later it was decided to significantly increase the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe base. During the construction, the experience of building the previous pyramids was used. Unlike its counterparts, the Mykerin pyramid has in its foundation an artificial terrace of limestone blocks. Typically, the tombs were erected on a rocky natural base.

The outer lining significantly distinguished the Mycerin pyramid among others. It was made of the following types of material:

  • the lower part is lined with red granite;
  • in the middle part used turkish limestone;
  • the top is decorated with red granite.

Inside the tomb of Mykerin



The dimensions of the burial chamber are also modest and correspond to the size of the pyramid. Parameters: 6.5x2.35 meters, and a height of three and a half meters. The ceiling of the main room is made in the form of a semi-arch and is composed of two adjacent blocks, it creates the illusion of a vault. For facing the inner walls of the mausoleum, polished granite was used. He also laid out the walls of the corridor and the original tomb.

A staircase leads into the room with the afterlife. The pyramid was first examined in 1837 by the expedition of the British Colonel Howard Wens. An autopsy revealed a basalt sarcophagus with a wooden lid and bones. The sarcophagus was sent to study in London. However, the ship fell into a storm and sank. The lid of the coffin, made in the form of a human body, archaeologists attributed to the period of early Christianity. Also during excavations, an extensive collection of sculptures was discovered. The most valuable exhibits were exhibited at the Cairo Central Museum and at the Boston Museum of Art.

The whole block located inside the pyramid, which weighs about 200 tons, also speaks about the skill of the designers. This is the heaviest monolithic block brought to the valley of the Pharaohs. Also in the main part of the temple was found a large statue of the king.
