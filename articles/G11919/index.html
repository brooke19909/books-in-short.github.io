Hohenschwangau Village in Germany

This village in Germany is truly a national pride. After all, it houses the most beautiful castle of the country - Hohenschwangau. It is made of light beige stone. If you want to relax from the bustle of the city, want to completely plunge into the natural world, then this is definitely your place.
