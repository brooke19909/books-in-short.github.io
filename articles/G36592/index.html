What is the new resort region of Marsa Alam

Egypt is famous as a winter destination for budget travelers. But still this part of Africa is not the Dominican Republic and not Thailand. In winter, the traditional resorts of Egypt (in Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh) are windy and far from hot. But there are places that are not inferior in climate to real tropical countries. Marsa Alam is located almost in the very south of Egypt, on the same latitude with Luxor. And this means that even in January, tourists can count on +25 degrees in the shade and the warm, warm sea. From the west, the resort is covered by mountains from the cold desert winds. In addition, from Mars Alam closer to get to the historical sights of Egypt. Here you can choose from the Karnak Temple, the Valley of the Kings, and the Abu Simbel complex. And in the north of Al Quseir you can visit the Farran Mosque and the fortress, built in the sixteenth century by Sultan Selim. And in Marsa Alam itself, you can also have some fun. The local coral reefs are able to compete in beauty with Ras Mohammed. In addition, near Mars Alam (more precisely, a hundred kilometers from the Hilton Nubian Resort 5 *, Marsa Alam) there is a dolphin nursery where you can swim in company with these wonderful and friendly mammals.
