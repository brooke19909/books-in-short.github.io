Club Hotel Caretta Beach 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): reviews, photos

As you know, Turkey has long been one of the favorite destinations for summer holidays among Russians. The 3- and 4-star hotels are in great demand, offering comfortable accommodation, all-inclusive meals at affordable prices. One of these hotels is Club Hotel Caretta Beach 4 * (Turkey, Alanya). It is with him that we will get to know each other better today. Consider not only the description of the hotel, but also the reviews about it of our compatriots.



Location of the hotel complex

Club Hotel Caretta Beach is located in the village of Konakli, in one of the most popular tourist destinations in the Turkish region of Antalya, on the seashore. The nearest major city, Alanya, is only twelve kilometers away. Here are concentrated a lot of large shopping centers, markets, souvenir shops, supermarkets, excursion bureaus, bars, clubs, restaurants, etc. You can get here on foot, by taxi or by minibus. By the way, the stop is only 200 meters from the hotel.





The nearest airport to the hotel is located in Antalya. The distance to it is 110 kilometers. Thus, upon arrival on the way to the hotel you will spend about two hours.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal regulations adopted by the Club Hotel Caretta Beach, check-in of arriving guests takes place after two in the afternoon. However, in most cases, tourists are accommodated earlier, a few minutes after their arrival. But it should be borne in mind that you should not particularly hope for such an opportunity in the midst of the holiday season, when the hotel is filled with vacationers, as they say, to the eyeballs. On the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. In this case, it is necessary to hand over the keys and pay for the accommodation (if you have not paid the travel agency) and use additional services. You can use here both cash and cards of the Visa and Mastercard systems.

Since the Club Hotel Caretta Beach positions itself as a family hotel, children of any age are allowed here. Moreover, if you place your child under six years old on the existing sleeping places in the room, then you will not have to pay anything extra for it. Also free is the stay of babies up to two years. They are provided with playpens.





As for people who plan to go to Turkey in the company of their four-legged pets, their stay at Club Hotel Caretta Beach is not allowed. Therefore, you will either have to leave your pets at home during a trip to the sea, or find another hotel, the policy of which is more loyal to guests with animals.

Nutrition

Club Hotel Caretta Beach 4 * (Turkey): reviews of tourists from Russia
