How to make a tulip out of paper: our workshop will tell you

Spring is coming, time for love and flowers, spring holidays. Yes, and just want

to please your beloved women with flowers. However, this pleasure is not cheap now. What to do? The ancient Japanese art of origami will help us out. It has repeatedly helped lovers to originalize, and now it will help to make an unusual gift to your loved ones. We suggest you make a bouquet of delicate tulips using the origami technique. After all, as they say, the most unforgettable gift is a gift made by yourself.

Simply and easily

There are many options for making a tulip out of paper. From the simplest to the more intricate.

In this article, we will tell you one of the easiest ways to make a tulip out of paper. A small note: it is better to take thick tulip paper so that it can hold the volume well.

Material needed

To make a tulip, you will need to prepare:
- 1 sheet of paper for the tulip (paper format and color - solely at your discretion);
- 1 green sheet of paper for the stalk;
- PVA glue;
- pencil or pen.

Getting to work

So, a tulip from paper. The scheme is pretty simple.
Take a sheet of paper. If it is rectangular, bend diagonally so that the sheet is square. Cut off the excess part, and it will no longer be useful to you.



Fold the sheet in the other direction. You will get two lines intersecting a square sheet of paper crosswise.



Fold the sheet in half so that you get a rectangle.



Fold the triangle, bending both sides of the square inward.
Smooth the corners of the triangle.



Bend the corners of the triangle towards the top on both sides.
It turns out a four-sided figure.



Next, it is necessary to bend all four sides of our figure so that they are slightly further than the middle. This must be done from the side where there are incisions.

Both bent triangles must be inserted with the ends into each other. You got two kind of "little bag".



And the final stages

In the lower part we make a hole for the stem. We inflate a tulip so that it becomes voluminous.



A few more steps and you will learn how to make a tulip out of paper. We turn over the tulip and bend four petals from the tip.



Make a stalk

We take a green sheet of paper. Screw it tightly onto a pencil or pen. We glue the end of the leaf and twist. We take out a pencil. The resulting tube is inserted into the bud.



We make a leaf for a tulip from the same green paper and glue it to the stalk.

Here's how to make an origami (out of paper) tulip. The scheme, as you see, is straightforward. But not everyone succeeds the first time. Try again - and you will succeed.
How to make a tulip out of paper so that it is not boring? Of course, along with the children. The child will be happy to surprise her beloved mom with a bouquet of home-made tulips by March 8th. And simply, without any reason.



In conclusion

Such a craft is perfect for decorating your home and creating a spring mood for your whole family, and will bring a sense of celebration and freshness to your home. Moreover, such flowers will stand longer than living flowers. And there is no less joy from them.

We wish you success in all your endeavors.
