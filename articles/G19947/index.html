Full tulle skirt - how to sew? Pattern of a full skirt of tulle

Many girls and very young girls dream of having such a thing as a full tulle skirt in their wardrobe. "How to sew such beauty?" - This is the most common question, because in stores it is not so common, and it can be too expensive. Nevertheless, at the moment, there are many simple methods by which you can quickly and easily make this lovely little thing for yourself.

Full tulle skirt - how to sew?

Everyone knows that making a thing is much cheaper than buying it. In addition, hand-made clothing has a unique appeal. It is also worth paying attention to the fact that only the thing that is sewn by individual standards will ideally fit the figure.

One of the simplest and most spectacular wardrobe items that you can make yourself is a fluffy tulle skirt. How to sew? Nothing is easier! Before proceeding to its manufacture, you need to decide on the style. Imagine the color, length of the skirt, as well as the accessories that will adorn it. An important point is how magnificent you want to make it. Not only the amount of material used will depend on this, but also the way with which you will develop a pattern.





What will be needed for work

A fairly small amount of materials requires a fluffy tulle skirt for its manufacture. How to sew it, what materials are needed - all these questions you have to study. First, head to the sewing store to purchase the following items:

  • tulle of the color and texture you like;
  • threads to match the fabric;
  • an elastic band that will serve as the basis for your design (can be wide or thin;
  • Invisibility, with which you can simulate the style of the product before fastening the fabric;
  • all kinds of decor: ribbons, buttons, rhinestones - everything is at your discretion.




How much tulle does a fluffy skirt need

Before you start sewing a skirt, it is very important to correctly determine the amount of material. If you buy it too little, then it simply may not be enough to implement your idea. Otherwise, there will be too much unused waste, which can be considered a waste of money.

When answering the question of how much tulle you need for a fluffy skirt, you need to decide how magnificent you want to see your product. The number of layers and, accordingly, the cut length will depend on this. In addition, how tough the material will be plays an important role. The coarser the tulle, the more magnificent the skirt will turn out. This fabric is more suitable for holiday costumes, because it is not very pleasant to the body.

You also need to decide on the pattern. If you are going to sew a sun skirt, then you need to multiply the diameter of the circle by the number of layers. If you start to sew traditionally using an elastic band, then you need to multiply the circumference of your hips with a small margin for folds.

How to cut a skirt

Pattern of a full skirt of tulle can be of several types. Traditionally, the so-called sun is used for these products. In this case, you need to build a circle whose diameter will be equal to the sum of the length and half of the waist circumference. After the workpiece is completed, fold it, as well as a sheet of cloth four times and cut the parts. By the way, in order not to repeat this action repeatedly, you can cut several layers of the future skirt at a time. If you consider yourself a craftswoman in the field of sewing, then you can experiment and make the sun skirt asymmetrical.





Rectangle skirt

The second option is to cut out the rectangles from the fabric. In the future, an elastic band will be stretched through them. The height of the rectangle is equal to the estimated length of the product, but the width depends not only on the circumference of your waist, but also on the number of folds that will add fluffiness to the skirt. So a fluffy tulle skirt with your own hands will be cut much faster, and the process of sewing it will be much easier.

The working process

If you decide to sew a product like a fluffy tulle skirt with your own hands, then you need to adhere to the following algorithm:

  • To get started, purchase and prepare all the necessary materials and tools;
  • make the necessary measurements of the parameters of your figure and write them in legible handwriting;
  • proceed to build a pattern on a large sheet of paper;
  • transfer the pattern to the fabric and cut the blanks;
  • further, with the help of invisibility, form folds (if necessary), and also make an opening for the elastic or attach the fabric to a wide decorative belt;
  • bast fabric;
  • go through the machine line on the basting;
  • stretch and fasten the elastic;
  • decorate the skirt with decorative elements, if necessary.

One of the indisputable advantages of tulle skirts is that their edges do not need to be processed. The fabric is stiff enough and will not fray from below. The main thing is to cut it gently and evenly with sharp scissors.

Another point to consider when sewing a skirt is the transparency of the tulle material. If even through several layers the fabric shines through, then make sure to sew a lining under the lower layer. For this, a thin opaque material is suitable, which will be pleasant to the body, and also suitable for tulle in color.





A simple way to sew a skirt

Despite the simplicity of execution, not everyone is subject to sewing such a product as a fluffy tulle skirt. The master class posted in the article will help you effortlessly create a pretty product without using threads or needles, as well as a sewing machine. This method is perfect for making children's holiday costumes. Also, this method should be of interest to those who absolutely can not sew.

Do-it-yourself tulle skirt is made in several stages:

  • To begin with, you will need to measure the circumference of your waist and prepare in advance an elastic band of the appropriate diameter;
  • then decide on how long you would like to see the skirt;
  • take the fabric and cut it into strips about 15-20 centimeters wide;
  • the length of each of the strips should be twice as long as the estimated length of the skirt itself;
  • Now go back to the previously prepared elastic band and begin to tie to it the pieces of fabric you cut so that their ends are the same;
  • the number and thickness of the patches depends on how magnificent you want to see the skirt.

The advantage of this method is not only in its simplicity, but also in the fact that you have the opportunity to use fabric in different colors. So your skirt can sparkle with all the colors of the rainbow.





Where to wear

A tulle skirt is very gentle, feminine and beautiful. But for some reason, this thing may seem to many too extravagant. Nevertheless, she perfectly complements the image of both the most inveterate fashionista and the quiet modest. If the skirt is short and magnificent, like a ballerina, then it will be an excellent element of the wardrobe for the party. A long lined item can perfectly complement your everyday ensemble.





A fluffy tulle skirt for a girl will be an excellent basis for a festive costume. With this accessory on the matinee, the baby will look like a real princess or fairy. The main thing is that to sew such a thing is quite simple and not unprofitable, but because in the arsenal of a young fashionista there may be several tulle skirts of different colors and styles.
