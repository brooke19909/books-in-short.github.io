Hotel Marriott Mountain 5

Description: In the center of the tourist resort of Sharm El Sheikh, near the beach of the Marriott Front Hotel, Marriott Mountain 5 Hotel is located. It offers various restaurants and a spa. It offers breathtaking views of the resort.





The Marriott Mountain 5 rooms are soundproofed and have a modern interior. During leisure, you can visit the fitness center located on site. And within walking distance there is a water park and Adventure Ridge shop. The airport is only 15 minutes away by car.

Rooms: Even the standard rooms at this hotel are quite spacious. Each room has comfortable beds and air conditioning. Windows overlook either the garden or the pool. In addition to a standard set of towels, slippers and bathrobes are provided.

Each room has comfortable orthopedic beds and a spacious seating area. There is a modern cable TV, fridge and coffee / tea making facilities.

The room is cleaned twice a day.





Meals: Guests at the Marriott Mountain 5 can choose several options for basic meals - Breakfast / Dinner, Breakfast Only, All Inclusive. In addition to the main restaurant, there are also establishments with national Italian and Japanese cuisine. There is also a seafood restaurant.

The main restaurant serves traditional European cuisine, but on Fridays vacationers can also enjoy national Egyptian dishes. Type of service - buffet, drinks are ordered separately from the waiters.

Beach: The beach at Marriott Mountain 5 is across the road from a nearby hotel. Vacationers on the beach are provided with free sun loungers and towels. Useful information: If you compare Marriott Sharm Mountain with club hotels, then its territory is not very large. In the courtyard there is an adult pool with a bar. The bar is easily accessible by the bridge, and you can order a drink without leaving the water. There is also a pool for children. The entire hotel is decorated with evergreens.





Despite the fact that this hotel also provides services for children, it is more suitable for couples without children. Also, older people who prefer tranquility and comfort like to relax here. Most of the children's entertainment is in the Marriott Hotel, located next door.

Since both Marriott hotels belong to the same network, vacationers can use the services of both. For example, to watch daytime programs and evening shows, you need to go to the territory of a neighboring hotel, they are held only there. In the animation team there are always girls and boys speaking Russian.

Several times a week in the evening performances are invited artists. Basically, guests are offered to watch fakir shows, belly dancing, shows with snakes, etc.
