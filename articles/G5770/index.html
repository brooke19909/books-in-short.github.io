Goat tilda: workshop

Here you will find a description of the tailoring of the Goat in the tilde style. As a hairstyle, she will have a long thick braid. The toy reminds the heroine of the fairy tale ‚ÄúThe Wolf and the Seven Little Kids‚Äù and children will certainly like it. Below will be provided her pattern. Tilda Goat is easy to sew and is designed for an average level of skill.





Flax or cotton of gray, white, brown or flesh color is suitable for the product. Lay out the pattern on the fabric and circle it. Remove the paper and sew along the specified lines. Make small cuts at the bend points to make it easier to fill the product. Turn out all the details.

Stuffing

Fill the crafts best holofiber. Vata, although a natural material, is categorically not suitable for these purposes. After a short time, it goes astray, and the thing is deformed. The synthetic winterizer is also not the best choice, it lays unevenly, and creates a semblance of cellulite on the extremities of toys. We stuff the product with filler using a sushi stick or pencil.





Product assembly

We pin the horns with pins to the head and sew them with a hidden seam. Do the same with the ears. Sew the legs to the body using a button. This is a reliable mounting method. In addition, they will remain mobile.

Outfit for a goat

Now let's think about what our tilde doll Goat will wear. Pattern for a simple dress is not needed. It can be made of two rectangles or sew a more complex outfit for a toy. Decorate the bottom rectangles with lace. Put your hands in the sleeves and fasten them with buttons.

Top the dress with a thread and a needle. Sew it carefully to the neck of the Goat. Hands fasten with buttons. So the tilde Goat is ready . With your own hands, you can draw her eyes with paints or just sew on beads, as is customary in the manufacture of toys of this type.

Goat hairstyle

It is better to make a braid from a woolen thread. Take a book or notebook and wind the yarn around it. It's better. so that the threads are light shade and fluffy. Too thick braids should not be done. Wrap a small amount of thread on a notebook. Cut the yarn on one side. Braid one of them. Drag it with tape or elastic. If you want, you can cut the bangs.
