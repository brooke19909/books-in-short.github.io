The Kazzhol Hotel is a 4 * business hotel located in a new nine-story building in the financial and administrative center of the city.

The design of the rooms of this hotel meets the high standards of the European level.

It is also worth noting the additional services of this hotel in Almaty. There is a new business center built in full compliance with standards. It is a professionally equipped conference venue. Nine conference rooms, designed for a total of 450 people, are great for hosting events of various formats - from seminars and trainings to symposia and full-scale conferences.
