Installation Nuances

To ensure correct operation, the radar detector must be correctly placed in the cabin. In this case, certain installation requirements are taken into account:

  1. The radar detector is installed in the middle of the windshield as low as possible.
  2. You can not place the gadget behind the wipers or the upper sun stripe. Such elements are equipped with metal parts that degrade signal reception, which increases the response time of the device.
  3. The Sho-Me 520 radar detector must not come in contact with the windshield.
  4. Some synthetic coatings may affect radio reception.

Gadget installation

The radar detector is mounted in the passenger compartment in two possible ways. According to one of them, the device is mounted on the suction cups on the windshield. To this end, suction cups are attached to the bracket, after which the bracket and bumper are mounted on the windshield.

The second mounting option is to mount the radar detector on the dashboard with Velcro. The installation location of the radar detector must first be degreased, since it is attached using double-sided tape. The serial number must remain visible, otherwise the warranty will be terminated.
