Why choose Beldibi?

This is a very cozy and relatively calm resort village of a small area. Its main part is concentrated between the Toros Mountains and the Mediterranean coast. Nature and climate are similar to Kemer: pine trees are adjacent to palm trees, pomegranate and orange trees, clean large-pebble beaches with crystal clear water. Mountains protect the coastline of Beldibi from northerly winds and heavy storms. There is a very calm sea, and the entry into the water is smooth, gentle. You can swim from mid or late May to early October. The beach season begins a little earlier, sunbathers relax on the beaches from the end of April.





The resort infrastructure is well developed: there are shops with famous Turkish goods (leather handbags, clothes, yellow gold jewelry, oriental sweets, souvenirs, etc.), bars and restaurants, night clubs and, of course, hotels. As in other resorts, the most expensive respectable hotels are located almost on the seashore on a large private beach. The category of accommodation, which belongs to the golden mean, is also often located on the first coastline.

Hotel Belpinar in Beldibi: features, location

The distance from Antalya International Airport is 45 km, to Kemer - 15 km. Belpinar Hotel in Beldibi is located on the first coastline. To get to the beach, you need to go through the complex, and then go through the underpass to the sea.





The main building was commissioned in 1991, the hotel is regularly updated. Every year minor cosmetic repairs are carried out, the last major renovation was made in 2014. The hotel is positioned as a club, but the audience here is the most diverse. Many young people, active vacationers of middle age, families with children.

Buildings

The Belpinar hotel (Beldibi, Kemer) has four buildings (1, 2, 4 and 5 floors). The housing stock is about 350 rooms.
