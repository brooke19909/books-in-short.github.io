Residential complex "Bestuzhevsky Gardens" (Kharkov): customer reviews

Kharkov is the largest city in Ukraine, where new real estate is in unusually high demand. Therefore, the construction of comfortable, modern complexes that meet absolutely all international standards is very active here. One of the projects that is already being prepared for delivery is the LCD "Bestuzhevsky Gardens". Kharkov, of course, will be decorated with its bold architecture, and the unusually bright design of the complex will make this corner of the city brighter and more attractive.

Location, transport accessibility

Without exaggeration, we can say that a very good place was chosen for the implementation of the Bestuzhevsky Gardens project. Kharkov is a city in which one and a half million people live, and about the same number of cars rush through the streets. It is quite difficult to find a quiet, quiet corner here, and even next to important objects of urban infrastructure, but the developer of the housing complex under consideration succeeded.

The address of the complex is Bestuzheva street, building No. 11. This is a relatively quiet place, where there is no big fuss and cars rushing endlessly somewhere. But it is very easy to get to anywhere in Kharkov from here, as transport stops (minibuses, buses, trams) are located just 300 meters away. A little further, namely at 1,500 meters, are the metro stations "Kievskaya" and "Defenders of Ukraine", and at 2,000 meters - the metro station "Akademika Barabashova".

Ecology

It is difficult to say how satisfies the requirements of environmentalists LCD "Bestuzhevsky Gardens". Kharkov is an industrial city whose enterprises contribute to air pollution. In addition, many cars pollute the environment here every hour. According to specialists, the environmental situation is best on the outskirts of Kharkov, and the pollution index in the center is about 6. But the Bestuzhevsky Gardens LCD looks relatively good against the general background. It is far from industrial enterprises, high-rise buildings separate it from large transport arteries. The advantage of the complex is that the Memory Park is 700 meters away, and the Zhuravlevsky Hydropark is about 2.5 km away.

Infrastructure around

In principle, there is no great need to build other leisure facilities in the LCD "Bestuzhevsky Gardens" (Kharkov). The complex is located in the city center, so there is no lack of infrastructure here. Literally 650 meters from the building there is the popular French Boulevard shopping center, within a radius of 300-700 meters there are schools, a gymnasium, a hospital, kindergartens, several pharmacies and many shops. Also near the "Bestuzhevsky Gardens" are a church, a market, gas stations, ATMs and bank branches.

"Bestuzhevsky Gardens" (Kharkov): apartment plans

The building of the complex is being built using the technology of "monolithic-frame construction." The external walls here are three-layer - brick-mineral wool-brick. The internal partitions are also brick, but in one layer. On each floor from 20 apartments are located. They are all of the same type - studios. The difference is in the footage - from 11 to 40 m 2 . Bathrooms in the hygiene rooms are combined with a toilet, but in apartments up to 20 m2 there are pallets with a shower and a curtain, and in larger apartments there are standard bathtubs.



The so-called two-story apartments in the Bestuzhevsky Gardens complex (Kharkov) are also offered. Customer reviews about them are restrained. Features of this layout are higher ceilings and very large windows. On the first tier, it is planned to place a kitchen, living room, and hygiene room. Price and payment

The cost of apartments and even 1 m 2 in the complex is different and depends on the floor, layout, number of windows (one or two), and other factors. On average 1 m 2 costs from 653 at. e.

Unicredit and Kredobank banks are working on the project of the Bestuzhevsky Gardens complex. Making a deal is possible in two ways:

1. Immediately paid 100% of the cost.

2. An installment plan is drawn up. It is given for a period of up to 6 months subject to a down payment of 50%.

The developer for this project arranges promotions and discounts. You can learn more about them from sales managers.

Who builds and how

LCD "Bestuzhevsky Gardens" (Kharkov) is being erected by a developer with an interesting name "Sparrow Hills". The last project he completed was a complex of the same name, which received high marks from citizens. The developer in the housing construction market is only 6 years old, but it is already very popular and has earned a lot of thanks, because it builds efficiently and quickly.

The commissioning of the LCD "Bestuzhevsky Gardens" is scheduled for the II quarter of this year. In February, the construction of the hull and pouring of the roof are completed. Now internal work is underway, the connection of communications, the design of the entrance areas.



LCD "Bestuzhevsky Gardens" (Kharkiv): reviews
