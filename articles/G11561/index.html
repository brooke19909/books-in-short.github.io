Sanatorium "Baikal" on Lake Baikal: photos and reviews. Sanatoriums on Baikal

Lake Baikal ... This Siberian "pearl", an amazing place where landscapes caress your eyes and crystal clear air can even turn your head, offers tourists such a wide range of entertainment that sometimes there are not enough pages to list them.



But not everyone is interested in rest on Lake Baikal. Sanatoriums and hospitals built along its banks give people the opportunity to improve their health.

Wellness





The sanatoriums on Lake Baikal are so numerous that it is sometimes difficult for an ignorant tourist to make the right choice. Improvement in the climatic resorts of this largest freshwater reservoir in the world is primarily associated with an environmentally friendly environment, fresh air filled with the healing smells of the Siberian taiga and the aromas of many medicinal plants. In addition, the sanatoriums on the shore of Lake Baikal use local mineral waters and therapeutic muds for treatment and combine the methods of medical and alternative medicine.

The water in the lake has no organic impurities, while it is saturated with oxygen as much as possible. And by the amount of mineral salts dissolved in it, it is close to distilled. That is why many people consider Lake Baikal to be not just a pearl in Eastern Siberia - according to many, it is a real wealth and a special pride of Russia. The pond, which occupies a vast territory, the bulk of which falls on undeveloped and uninhabited places, is interesting from the point of view of a wellness holiday because of its excellent ecology.

Pensions and motels

Medical institutions are located, for the most part, along the southern shore of the lake. This makes it possible for tourists to easily reach them by rail - along the Trans-Siberian Railway.

Health resorts for the military, resort complexes - many of them annually take several thousand people. For the convenience of military personnel, as well as military pensioners and members of their families, reception departments function in Chita and Ulan-Ude. Here you can apply for rest and treatment, and nonresidents can send a letter by fax. For some categories, benefits apply.





Military sanatoriums on Lake Baikal offer the entire necessary range of procedures. For example, in the Molokovsky health resort, the musculoskeletal system and the nervous system are treated, they heal skin diseases, and they perform a physical education complex.

"Darasunsky" is a sanatorium that accepts the military with problems of the digestive and metabolic organs , as well as ailments of the endocrine system, diseases of the circulatory system. Here, servicemen and their families can not only improve their health, but also actively relax on Lake Baikal, go hiking, go rafting and just enjoy the beautiful scenery. Many resorts are located directly on the shore of the lake, but there are those that are built some distance from the beaches.

Sanatoriums on Lake Baikal, whose prices for services depend on many factors - location, offered level of comfort and infrastructure, are crowded in high season. People come here not only from the Irkutsk and Chita regions or Buryatia, but also from all over Russia. Many of our compatriots spend their holidays here year after year. Today, all comfortable pensions and motels on the shore of Lake Baikal are equipped with modern diagnostic equipment. They are located in Buryatia and in the Irkutsk region. Among them, the Baikal sanatorium on Lake Baikal is very popular.

general information





This resort and medical institution is located on the south-west coast of the lake, five kilometers from the source of the Angara near the village of Listvyanka. Sanatorium "Baikal" (on Lake Baikal) is located in a dense coniferous grove, which has a natural origin. Its complex of buildings and secondary structures was built in 1960. The sanatorium in those years was created as a state dacha, where the then US President D. Eisenhower was going to come. During the first few years of its existence, it was used as a place where senior foreign delegations received.

Infrastructure

Located on sixty-two hectares of parkland, this health resort was last renovated in 2011. Its infrastructure includes a pharmacy, a large sports, gym and cinema halls. The latter is designed for two hundred and fifty places. There is also a parquet dance floor, a billiard room, a pool with four paths twenty-five meters long, as well as a sauna with two small pools - cold and warm. Water in them comes directly from the lake.

There are a lot of spa and medical resorts on Lake Baikal, but the namesake of the lake is considered one of the most popular not only among military personnel, but also among those who buy vouchers on a commercial basis.

Cost

The health resort accepts for treatment both adults and children, starting from the age of four, with parents. The price of accommodation with treatment for a commercial package is from 1500 to 1800 rubles. The price includes three meals a day, diagnostics, procedures and the use of a certain infrastructure. You can get to the Baikal sanatorium on Lake Baikal by train or bus to Irkutsk, and from there by bus to Listvyanka.

Nutrition





An individual diet is selected for vacationers in a health resort. Three meals a day in the sanatorium. It is served in the restaurant according to the menu, based on the spa card of each resident, taking into account the contraindications indicated in it. There is also a bar and cafe on site.

Reviews

Those who rested in the sanatorium were mostly satisfied with their rest and treatment. The procedures and medical staff were highly rated. Many were pleased with the presence of an excursion company, which very cheaply organizes tours not only to the lake, but also to its surroundings. There is a phytobar where vitamin tea is made, based on the fees of local herbs. Food vacationers rated at "five with a minus", with half a point reduced due to dietary dishes. But there is nothing to be done, the sanatorium is a sanatorium.

Those who came here a few years ago note the fact that over the years the furniture has worn out a bit, but on vacation, according to many, the main thing is nature, healing water and a lake.
