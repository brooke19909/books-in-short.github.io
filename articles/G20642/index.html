Innovations in Russia

Do you pay tax when buying an apartment? Sellers - yes, buyers - no. This norm is spelled out at the legislative level. Moreover, since 2016, significant changes have been introduced to the Tax Code of the Russian Federation. They began to mislead the population.

The thing is that earlier, when calculating the tax when buying an apartment and selling it, the market value of the property was taken into account. Now this indicator has been abolished. From now on, citizens will have to prepare for the calculation of personal income tax according to the new rules. The cadastral value of housing will be taken as the tax base.

This means that it will depend a lot on such an indicator. Yes, the seller can indicate any value in the sales contract. Only tax authorities will take into account exactly the cadastral price tag when calculating personal income tax. Large deviations from the corresponding amount up or down will lead to huge costs. Long tenure

Property acquisition tax in Russia is not levied. Moreover, buyers may, under certain circumstances, rely on a property tax deduction. Taxes on the sale of real estate in the Russian Federation are paid only by sellers.

But not always. Long ownership of the property exempts owners from the need to pay personal income tax. What does it mean?

If the property was acquired after 2016, then it will not be necessary to pay income tax on the sale of this property after 5 years of owning housing. In our case, since 2021.
