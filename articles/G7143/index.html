Holiday Village "Donskoy Les", Lipetsk Region: photos and reviews

For lovers of relaxation on the river and in the forest there is such a place - this is the recreation center "Don forest". Lipetsk is located 46 kilometers from it. Great nature! The pine, tarry air of the forest, the lack of city bustle and silence - all this has a beneficial and healing effect on vacationers.

Base Description

The administration of the year-round working base with heated houses is glad for guests who come for a long vacation and on weekends. The recreation center "Donskoy Les" Zadonsky district is located in the forest. For those who come on weekends after a working week, this will be a real vacation. In the morning you can take a walk along the forest paths or along the river bank. At breakfast, you can discuss future plans with friends. In the summer, it can be just relaxing on the beach or doing any sport in the air. There are bicycles at the rental point, where you can also ride along forest paths.





In winter, the rental office offers sledding, skiing and ice skating. Of course, in nature, regardless of the time of year, a must-have item on the weekend is a barbecue in the gazebo with barbecue. Evening can be taken at will. This is a visit to a restaurant whose cuisine is not inferior to the city, or play billiards with friends.

Service and entertainment

It is worth noting that this base is a great place for outdoor activities. There is a tennis court for lovers of tennis, billiards, badminton and table tennis. An outdoor pool with artesian water invigorates the body. For those who like to take a steam bath at the recreation center there is a sauna with a pool, as well as a Russian wood-fired sauna with brooms.

For lovers of barbecue, there are gazebos at the recreation center "Don forest". They can accommodate up to 20 people, with large barbecue facilities. Those who wish can sit in the "Divan Teahouse", which offers delicious oriental dishes, hookahs, fragrant teas and sweets. For younger guests of the recreation center there is an outdoor pool with constantly heated water up to 30 ¬∞ C and a game town.



All visitors by private transport can use the free car park. The territory of the base is provided with round-the-clock video surveillance. For corporate events, there is a conference room and the necessary equipment, as well as wireless Internet (WI-FI).

Accommodation and meals

Accommodation of guests who arrived at the recreation center "Donskoy Les" is carried out at their request, corresponding to their budget. There are options.

Quadruple two-room cottage with access to the veranda. It offers guests furnished rooms standard guest and standard. The guest room has two single beds, and in the usual one double bed. The rooms are the same: LCD TV, air conditioning, wardrobe and fridge. There is a shower cabin in the bathroom.

Corporate cottage for 12 people with a minibar, TV, air conditioning.

VIP-complex for 15 people. Two rooms of improved layout and full of furniture for relaxation, three bedrooms and a kitchen. Guests can use an infrared sauna, a jacuzzi, a regular sauna with a pool.





Meals are provided in the restaurant. Breakfast is included. There is also the opportunity to cook food yourself in the kitchen or on equipped areas with barbecue facilities.

Corporate events and seminars

At the recreation center "Donskoy Les" all conditions for corporate events, including seminars, conferences and trainings, are created. Carrying out corporate parties is an image component of the base. To ensure the event, there are all conditions from video and audio equipment to the places of residence of people who came to participate. During such serious events as seminars, conferences and trainings, visitors are offered leisure options during breaks in classes. This is a coffee break and outdoor activities at the base.

If this is a corporate event on the occasion of the celebration of a significant date, the organizers will hold it at the highest level. Those present will be satisfied with the plan of the corporate meeting, and the attention of the attendants surrounding them. And, most likely, there will be a desire to return here again.





Holding a wedding

The recreation center "Don forest" of the Lipetsk region is a favorite and beautiful place for exit registration and weddings. The special romantic atmosphere of the registration and the magical forest around is something that the newlyweds like. Moreover, at any time of the year the forest is unusually beautiful. Here you can hold a unique photo shoot. Exit registration of marriage in the "Don Forest" is also good because there is no need to spend time on the road, moving from the registry office to the banquet hall and for a photo shoot. Everything is in one place.

At any time of the year, the organizers of exit registration create unique and original programs for each couple. Winter weddings are no worse than summer and autumn. Sledding, tea from a samovar, hot mulled wine. The rest of the seasons are interesting in their own way. Yellow carpet of leaves in autumn, delicate snowdrops in early spring.





On the territory of the recreation center "Donskoy Les" there are two banquet rooms and two sites for marriage registration. For the bride and groom, there are deluxe rooms, as well as rooms for guests who want to stay on the second day of the wedding.

Guest reviews

At the camp site, they relax in the family circle or spend time outdoors with friends. The reviews are very different: from everything is bad to everything is just wonderful. Why such a runaway in grades? Everyone just has their own criteria. Someone loves silence, and someone likes to spend the whole night in a disco. Not everyone likes the comfort that the administration of the base provides. Of course, the houses are much more comfortable, but here, mainly, visitors appreciated the rest.

In their reviews, people indicate the excellent location of the recreation center "Don Forest": the river is just around the corner, time passes in the forest zone, where the air is clean, which even makes you dizzy from an excess of oxygen. Complaints to the service staff no. There is no garbage on the territory, the grass is trimmed and always looks fresh, paths are laid everywhere, and in the dark the base is well lit.



One of the wishes was to somehow resolve the issue with the stairs leading to the beach. Very steep steps for the elderly, and it would not hurt to make a railing. But this is quite fixable, and the request is justified. There are claims to the bungalow, in which there are small rooms. But, as representatives of the base explained, rest here is not positioned as comfortable with deluxe rooms. The main thing here is nature, river, forest. And whoever comes to enjoy time in nature, leaves the base very pleased.

How to get to the recreation center

Since the base is located near Lipetsk, and the Zadonsky district has bus stations, you can come to the base by bus or by personal transport.

When traveling, a person often changes his perception of the world. Perhaps after a trip to the recreation center "Don Forest" something will change in your perception of the surrounding reality.
