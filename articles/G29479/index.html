H2O - water park for the whole family

A wonderful complex of water entertainment is located at Rostov-on-Don, M. Nagibin Avenue, Building 34. This is an H2O water park operating year-round. For the convenience of visitors, the center is divided into a thermal zone and an aqua zone. If you wish, you can purchase a comprehensive ticket or pay for your stay only in saunas, or only in pools with slides. The water park in Rostov H2O is ideal for families.

On the territory of the complex there is a children's town, many pools of various depths, "calm" slides and attractions for fans of extreme sports. The brand name of the aquacenter is an artificial beach with open-air pools on the roof. In the thermal zone you can visit the Finnish or Russian sauna, hammam, salt bath, cave with the effect of heavy rain. Massage rooms work here, and guests are offered a variety of spa treatments. The water park in Rostov H2O boasts a flexible pricing policy. On a weekday, a complex ticket for 4 hours for an adult costs 950 rubles, and for a child - 650 rubles. In the warmer months, another center of water entertainment invites you to swim in the pools and ride on the water attractions of residents and guests of Rostov-on-Don. Waterpark "Octopus" is in the open. The exact address of the water center: Rostov-on-Don, Komarova street, ownership 23. The guests of the complex are waiting for 7 slides of various heights and difficulty levels, 7 pools, an artificial beach, cafes and bars. Among the attractions there are especially extreme ones: Kamikaze (50 meters), Black Pipe (90 meters) and Wide Carpet.
