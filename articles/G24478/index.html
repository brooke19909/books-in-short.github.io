Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): description, service, reviews

In this review we will talk about one of the hotels in Vietnam, as well as about vacations and excursions in Nha Trang. It is worth noting that it is Nha Trang that is considered the most popular holiday destination in South Vietnam. Here are the best beaches, clear water and the most diverse selection of restaurants and nightclubs. As for hotels, there is also a wide selection of them: from budget three-star hotels to upscale five-star hotels. Below we suggest you to get acquainted with the new four-star hotel, which is not considered the best in the city, but it cannot be called bad either.





Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Vietnam)

First of all, it should be noted that the hotel is located in the center of an ocean resort, on the coast of one of the most beautiful bays in the world. In a place perfect for walking, entertainment, great shopping and dining. The hotel is designed at the level of four-star standards, has twenty floors, is very cozy, with spacious rooms decorated in a traditional style, it was opened in 2017.

When choosing this hotel, judging by the reviews, you will remember the rest in Nha Trang with a smile on your face. This very pleasant place, designed in a modern style, is a great choice for tourists. Arriving here, every vacationer will feel like a welcome guest thanks to the attentive and professional staff and the friendly local population.

Where is the hotel situated

Red Sun is located in Nha Trang (Khanh Hoa Province). It has a gorgeous view of the city. A beach and a brewery are located a two-minute walk away, and a night market is a three-minute walk. Seven hundred meters from the Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4 * a sailing center is arranged, one kilometer - the square. The airport is 25 kilometers from the hotel.

Rooms for tourists

The hotel has 81 equipped rooms. All rooms are equipped with an electric kettle, hot drinks set, flat-screen TV, air conditioning, telephone, safe, fridge, a private bathroom with bathrobes, slippers and toiletries, a bidet. Some rooms have a seating area.





Judging by the reviews, the Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4 * has very good rooms with new modern appliances and all the furniture necessary for a comfortable stay. All rooms have balconies or windows with sea and city views.

The hotel has family rooms as well as non-smoking rooms. The hotel concept prohibits the accommodation of tourists with pets. Check-in is from 2 p.m. to 11:30 p.m., check-out is from 1:00 a.m. to noon. Upon arrival, a passport of one of the tourists is taken as a deposit.

What services are offered in the hotel complex?

The hotel was built recently, and so far little is known about it. But if you believe the information that is available on various sites with descriptions of hotels, then it fully justifies its four stars.

Service Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4 provides many services for its guests. Guests are offered free internet throughout the hotel, a bar, a restaurant, an outdoor pool with a gorgeous view from above, a 24-hour front desk, a luggage room, a laundry, and a currency exchange office. In addition, hotel guests can rent a car or bicycle and get to know the nearest surroundings on their own, or purchase a variety of sightseeing tours at the hotel‚Äôs travel desk. The hotel accepts credit cards.

Beach strip

One hundred meters from Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4 there is a public beach with a very convenient sandy entry into the sea. Umbrellas and sun loungers are provided for a fee, towels to the beach can be taken at the reception for painting. Beach relaxation in the company of friends or family will relieve accumulated fatigue and make you forget about all the problems and worries. Vacationers here are caressed by the waves of the South China Sea, located between the Indian and Pacific Oceans. The water warms up well, so you can relax on this coast all year round. Relax in a hotel with children

Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4 welcomes children of all ages. When placing young travelers under six years of age, accommodation is free of charge (without extra bed). For children, the hotel has a pool.

For a fee, the hotel offers babysitting services.

Entertainment

Among the activities at Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4, there are only swimming pools (indoor and outdoor) and a fitness center. But it is worth noting that the hotel is in close proximity to various clubs, bars, restaurants and local attractions. The hotel also has a travel agency, where you can book many exciting excursions.

Excursion program

Relaxing in the hotel described in this article, you definitely need to visit at least a few excursions in Nha Trang. Despite the fact that this city is quite compact, there is where to go and what to see.





Tourists are given a wonderful opportunity to enjoy the majestic Baho Falls, visit Monkey Island, explore the Po Nagar towers, swim in the mineral and mud springs. Also in the city there is a beautiful Oceanarium - an island of entertainment - which can be reached by a long cable car; You can also go on a river or island cruise.

Tourists can visit many places in the city on their own and thereby save a lot. For instance:

- You can easily get to the Chama towers by taxi for 2-3 dollars or on a rented motorbike. The towers are more than 1000 years old, but they are perfectly preserved. Locals consider this place mystical.

- Thap Ba. The hot spring complex is located next to Long Son.

- Long Son Pagoda is a large Buddha temple. Here you can see a huge Buddha statue and bewitching views of the city.

- Entertainment Island Winperl. Many recommend it for a mandatory visit. This is a large separate island with a water park, an aquarium, an entertainment area, a VIP hotel, a laser show, restaurants and shops. There is a lot of entertainment for both children and adults. When you purchase a ticket, an all-inclusive system is available for all shows.

- Monkey island, islands of deer and orchids can also be visited independently. First you need to get to the boat station - from there every 30 minutes boats leave on these islands. From Monkey Island you can immediately go to the Baho waterfalls.

- Youngbay Park will be very interesting for children. Here you can meet real shamans, ride ostriches, see pig races and more.

Guided tours:

- The southern islands. With a guide, the trip will be cheaper, plus lunch is included.

- The city of Dalat. It is advisable to come here for a couple of days to have time to visit the waterfalls, visit Buddhist temples, Bao Dai Palace and Crazy House.





In order to make it easier to navigate in the city, at the reception at the hotel you need to take a free map of the city with all the marked attractions, restaurants and shops.

As for the visa to Vietnam, provided that the tourist will stay in the state for less than 15 days (including the day of arrival and departure), it is not needed. If your vacation is designed for a longer period, then you should take care of obtaining a visa.

High-quality service, daily and high-quality room cleaning, towels and shower accessories are changed every day.

A few words about Nha Trang

Nha Trang - the most popular resort in South Vietnam, has long been loved by tourists. Here, vacationers are provided with equipped beach areas, a clear sea, numerous SPA procedures, a huge selection of nightlife, massage parlors, cafes and restaurants. Sea water is clear, warm, turquoise, ideal for scuba diving. The city offers tourists many exciting excursions and places for shopping.

Judging by the reviews, it is better to organize a vacation in Nha Trang from February to May. At this time, there are more comfortable conditions, the sea is calm, warm, azure in color, there is no sweltering heat, rainfall is rare. It is during these months that children need to be introduced to Vietnam.

From October to December, the city begins the rainy season, and although the temperature of the air, like the water, remains warm, for some the rest at this time can be spoiled. Rather large waves rise to the sea, debris nails to the shore, and often a cool wind blows.

Conclusion

To summarize, we can say the following: judging by the description, Red Sun Nha Trang Hotel 4 is the best option for a vacation in Nha Trang for both young people and couples who come with children.





Here, every tourist, without a doubt, will rest comfortably at an attractive price and will remember a vacation for a long time.
