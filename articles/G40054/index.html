How to make an origami horse from modules?

This year, as you know, passes under the symbol of a very beautiful and graceful animal - Horses. Every home should have this totem that brings happiness and prosperity to the owners. Today we will learn how to make an original origami horse from modules. This craft can be put in a conspicuous place or presented to friends for any holiday this year.





Materials

For work, you will need standard blanks-modules, which are made of a square sheet of paper folded diagonally and connected to a second similar part. They are in the craft firmly connected to each other. The whole structure is assembled from these parts and is held only by them, without the use of glue. Therefore, before starting work, prepare 291 gray and 39 black modules in advance. The color of your symbol can be any, so you can make at least green, at least pink horse. Accordingly, the base parts of the craft choose the desired shade.

How to make origami from modules?

A horse, the assembly scheme of which is not the most complex, is carried out in stages from several rows of parts. To get started, take the first 20 gray modules and connect them, taking turns inserting finished parts into each other. The next two rows will also consist of 20 figures each. We form the shape of the body of our future horse. The fourth, fifth, sixth and seventh rows will also consist of 20 parts each. The eighth row - 2 black and 10 gray modules point outward. The ninth row - 3 black modules and 9 gray ones also point outward. Tenth row - 2 black modules 8 gray short part out. Eleventh row - 3 black and 6 gray modules. The twelfth row - 2 black and 6 gray. The thirteenth row - 3 black and 2 gray. And two more rows - 2 gray and one black module.





Make the head

Now we start assembling the head of an origami horse from triangular modules. We collect the horse carefully, carefully inserting all the details so that the structure does not fall apart. We start with three rows of 12 gray modules. Now we need to turn and turn the craft out. Next, we do the fourth, fifth, sixth and seventh rows also with 12 parts. Seventh row - 4 black and 4 gray modules (these are the ears of the future model). Now three more rows of 12 figures. We turn it back and glue the parts so that the structure holds well.





Make legs and tail

Our origami horse from the modules is almost ready. It remains to glue the body and head, and also make her legs. Each leg will consist of three black, two gray and three more gray modules. Accordingly, we need to make four such legs. Feet can be immediately glued to our craft. Of course, the origami horse from the modules will not be completed unless you attach a beautiful tail to it. We will make it from five black figures, and then glue it to the toy. You can decorate the work by drawing a craft eye or gluing the original mane of thread. Now your origami horse from the modules is completely ready. Put it in a conspicuous place so that happiness stays in your house all year round. Present a toy to your friends, and your work will be appreciated!
