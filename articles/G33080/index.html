Hotel DoubleTree By Hilton Ras Al Khaimah 4 * (Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates): photos and reviews of tourists

The UAE is not only a large federal state, but also a place highly loved by tourists. Emirates are washed by the waters of the Gulf of Oman and the Gulf, and there are numerous expensive hotels on their shores. One of those is DoubleTree By Hilton Ras Al Khaimah 4 *. I would like to tell you more about it.





Location

DoubleTree By Hilton Ras Al Khaimah 4 * is located in a place called Ras Al Khaimah. This is the emirate, located in the north of the UAE. And here it will be interesting to everyone - both people who are fond of exploring local attractions, and lovers of beach holidays. And each of them must visit the ancient city called Julfar.

The airport from the hotel can be reached in 20-25 minutes. The ideal distance is not very far, but at the same time enough not to hear the noise of landing and taking off planes.

And, of course, the beach is just a stone's throw away. You can walk in a minute. The beach is sandy, the entrance to the sea is very convenient, and there is also a bar, deck chairs and umbrellas.

Entertainment

DoubleTree By Hilton Ras Al Khaimah 4 * has tons of ways to have a good time. It features an outdoor swimming pool, garden and sun terrace. There is also a fitness center with a variety of fitness equipment, a nightclub with a DJ, and a golf course. In the evening, various entertainment shows are held on site.

The hotel also has a SPA center. There guests can enjoy wellness and beauty treatments and massages. There is also a sauna, a bathhouse and a jacuzzi inside. By the way, in SPA there are Precor cardio machines, which is a leading manufacturer of equipment for gyms. There are also beauty rooms where they do manicures and pedicures. However, the full range of services provided in the SPA can be found on the spot.





Service

In DoubleTree By Hilton Ras Al Khaimah 4 *, as in any other self-respecting hotel of this class, there are a lot of different services, the provision of which can make the rest of tourists as comfortable as possible. For example, the Internet. It is free in public areas. If you need it in the room, you will have to pay 80 dirhams per day or 40 - per hour. This, by our standards, is too expensive - 1,400 and 700 rubles, respectively. However, inflated prices for everything - this is perhaps the most important and only minus the UAE.

But there is free and private parking. In the lobby, guests are offered lockers, concierge services, and a tour desk. There is a ticket office, a currency exchange, a 24-hour reception. By the way, guests can enjoy express check-in as well as check-out.

Other guests can call the nanny so that she can sit with the children taken for rest, use the laundry, dry cleaning or ironing (in which shoes are also cleaned).

Guests are offered a shuttle service, car rental services, newspapers, food and drinks delivery to their apartments, as well as preparing packed lunches with them on the road.

On the territory there are shops (both food and souvenir) and places for smoking. Nutrition

DoubleTree By Hilton Ras Al Khaimah 4 * reviews receive a mostly positive character. Largely thanks to the restaurants and bars that are available on its territory. Guests assure: to stay hungry here is simply impossible. Breakfasts, lunches, dinners - all this is served here. Either for a separate payment, or by default (depending on the paid services).

The restaurant serves delicious hot breakfasts according to the "buffet" system - omelets, scrambled eggs, spaghetti, beans, warm Viennese waffles with maple syrup, mashed potatoes, cereal with milk. Of the drinks - juices, water, tea, coffee. A lot of fresh, soft pastries - both salty and sweet. A lot of meat - bacon, sausages, grilled sausages, ham. Cheese slices are also plentiful. There are even salads and fresh yogurts.

The bars serve a lot of different drinks and treats, but freshly squeezed fresh ones are especially tasty there. By the way, who goes on vacation with children, may not worry that the kids will remain hungry - there are a lot of not too salty and not spicy food.

For three people, the standard cost is 105 thousand rubles. under the same conditions. If you have discounts, you can also save about 20-25 thousand rubles.

This apartment can be booked by a single tourist, but this solution cannot be called profitable. 80 000 rubles without a discount. And with it - about 55 thousand rubles.

Booking and accommodation

This is also an important topic that should be noted attention. When making a reservation at DoubleTree By Hilton Ras Al Khaimah 4 *, you must indicate your credit card details. The hotel accepts Visa, Diners Club, American Express and Master Card. Some funds may be blocked in the account. This is a guarantee that people really intend to go on vacation.

Check-in starts at 15:00, and check-out lasts until noon. The hotel can accommodate children of any age. There are even special offers for them. For example, a child under 12 years old can sleep in places available in the apartment for free. And for a baby under three years old, they provide a baby cradle. Also free. If a person over 12 years old arrives with the guests declared in the reservation (it does not matter whether the child is or not), 100 dirhams per night (about 1740 rubles) must be paid for it.

If you need an extra bed or cradle, you must inform the property in advance, even when making the reservation request. Guests should receive a confirmation reply on the provision of these berths.

The hotel also recommends that you familiarize yourself with the visa application procedure in advance. And it is worth remembering that in the hotel food for children under the age of 6 years is provided for free. Those over six but under 12 are given 50 percent discounts in restaurants.

This is useful information to remember. In general, booking a room is long before the trip. Firstly, there is a chance to get a good discount. And secondly - prudence will not hurt. After all, the hotel is popular, and there may simply not be any free apartments by the scheduled dates.

Well, this hotel leaves a good impression according to guest stories and description. If you want to verify this yourself, you should go here on vacation.
