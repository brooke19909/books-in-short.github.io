Zaporozhye Hotels: General Overview

The city of Zaporozhye is a large industrial center of Ukraine. Located on both banks of the Dnieper River, it embraces the picturesque island of Khortitsa. Depending on the purpose and objectives, city guests can choose several accommodation options from more than forty hotels. Zaporozhye hotels can provide any accommodation option: from economy class to luxury apartments at various prices and taking into account any requirements of customers.





Hotels on the waterfront

Recently, new modern mini-hotels located on the Dnieper embankment, which have access to wide sandy beaches, have been in great demand. The Reikartz Zaporizhia Hotel (19 Mayakovsky Avenue) provides 69 rooms ranging from Standard to Suite and Studio. Residents also have at their disposal a restaurant, a conference room and a summer outdoor grill bar with an outdoor pool. In the immediate vicinity of the Zaporozhye-1 railway station and the main city bus station, there are three more hotels.

Among them are the Porto Riva complex (15 Lunacharsky St.). This hotel has 17 rooms, from the modest Standard to the Executive Suite. It features a restaurant on the sun terrace with an outdoor pool. The location of the hotel directly in front of the sandy beach provides an opportunity for comfortable sports. On request, boat rental and fishing can be arranged.

At the same address is the Vodograi Hotel, offering single rooms, semi-suites and cottages for 4-6 people. The rooms are decorated in Ukrainian style, which can be fully felt in the restaurant, tasting the national cuisine. Additional services include a sauna, bicycle rental and a children's playground.

Hotel "Sphinx" (st. 40th anniversary of Victory, 31) is part of the entertainment center "Sahara", where you can visit a nightclub, play billiards and take a steam bath in the sauna with a mini-pool.

Zaporozhye hotels located on the waterfront are represented by the huge and modern Khortitsa Palace Hotel (Shevchenko Boulevard, 71-a), which is located in the city center a five-minute walk from City Hall. The monolithic two-section building, shining with glass of facades, leans over the granite coast of the Dnieper. It rises above an artificial lake created by flooding a quarry. Seventeen-story buildings also contain a business center with underground parking. The hotel has 164 deluxe rooms providing VIP-class services. Several bars and restaurants will satisfy a variety of gastronomic delights, and during dinner from the height of open terraces you can observe the rapid flow of the Dnieper and the greatness of gray-haired Khortitsa. It is also very pleasant that pets are allowed in the hotel for free.





Hotels in the center

All significant and old hotels of Zaporozhye are located on the central avenue of the city. The closest to the train station is the Soborny Hotel (Soborny Avenue, 28), which provides visitors with 16 rooms of various classes, SPA-center services, a swimming pool, a sauna and hot tubs. Additional services include: free parking, room service, bar, pets allowed in coordination with the administration. A little further, at the intersection of Soborny Avenue with Chekistov Street, the Teatralny Hotel is located. It was built in the historical center of the city along with the musical and drama theater and retained the acting spirit that has been hovering in these places for almost half a century. After the reconstruction of 2014, the hotel was equipped with 109 rooms from "Standard" to business class, a conference room, 2 banquet rooms. There is an excellent restaurant where you can taste dishes popular in many countries of the world at any time of the day. For the convenience of guests, breakfast (buffet) is included in the price.

The largest Soviet-era hotel, Intourist, is located on the central Festivalnaya Square (135 Soborny Avenue), directly opposite the regional administration. Now in "Intourist" 136 rooms are different in comfort and price. Three restaurants and two bars provide an opportunity to enjoy Ukrainian, European and Japanese cuisine.

Departmental hotels

Zaporizhzhya hotels of departmental subordination are mainly located directly near numerous enterprises and organizations such as Zaporizhzhya Aluminum Plant, Zaporizhstal, Dnepro-energo, Zaporizhzhya Transformer Plant and others. The services of these hotels for a moderate fee will satisfy the needs of people seconded to Zaporozhye enterprises.
