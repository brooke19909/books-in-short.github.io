Choose fabric for the "sun"

Striped "sun"

Do not give preference to fabrics with stripes or in a cage if you are new to sewing. Only experienced seamstresses and tailors can sew a striped sun skirt so that it would not be embarrassing to put it on light later.

On one side of the seam, the pattern will turn out horizontally, and the other vertically. To fix this, you can flip the seams. Remove the side, and make the main front and rear. You can also cut the fabric not from the corner, but in the shared direction. Then it will be appropriate to make the seams in front and behind. The stripes will converge, visually forming a pattern with an angle of 90 degrees.

If you want to sew a sun-skirt from checkered fabric, be prepared for the fact that much more material will be needed to combine the pattern. The additional expense will be easy to calculate, knowing the height of the pattern repeat. The larger the cell or its pattern, the greater the expense. Add the height of the cell to each side of the pattern. Before you open a skirt-sun for a girl, try to attach square or striped patterns to each other. Estimate how to connect them most advantageously.

Getting to the pattern

So how to cut a sun skirt:

  • Fold the material in half and begin to draw radii on it. Folding the fabric in half, mark a point on the fold line in the middle of the length. From it we draw a radius of a waist. To draw a radius, measure line segments of length R1 from the starting point and put points on their end. Then just connect them together. The resulting semicircle will be the radius R1.




  • Similarly, draw the radius of the bottom of R2. Check the accuracy of the measurements by measuring the distance from the first radius to the second. It should be equal to the intended length of your skirt. Above the upper radius, leave a seam allowance of 0.5-1 cm, and under the lower radius 0.5 cm. The smaller the allowance from the bottom, the more accurate and effective the hem will be.

Half skirt

The process of how to cut a sun skirt and a half sun differs in the number of patterns and the consumption of fabric. For a half-sun skirt, more parts are needed, respectively, the number of seams will be more.

The algorithm of actions is exactly the same, with the exception of the first paragraph. The fabric is not folded in half, but cut in one piece.

To save material, some novice seamstresses make a small but annoying mistake. Two patterns must be placed on the fabric so that the left and right seams of both are located on the longitudinal and lobular sections, respectively. If you arrange the patterns in such a way that the left seam of one will be with a longitudinal section, and the other with a longitudinal section, then in the future this will badly affect the product. Such joints will look sloppy, seams will be noticeable, bumps will appear.

How to cut a skirt-sun on an elastic band

The top of the skirts and the sun and the sun can be varied. Someone uses a stitched belt, someone not. You can tie a beautiful ribbon or belt over it. Gum is inserted into the lung tissue. A skirt with a belt on an elastic band will favorably emphasize the figure of its owner.

Make a pattern of the future belt. Determine the width according to the chosen style, it can be different. Remember to leave allowances around the edges.

After processing the edges of the skirt, it is necessary to fasten the belt on it. Pin it on the skirt so that the front side of the belt is 1 centimeter below the top edge of the skirt. Sew in a way convenient for you.

There is nothing simpler than cutting out a sun skirt with an elastic band. It will look stylish and relevant, but it will take a little time, effort and money.





Useful Tips

What is the best way to sew a sun skirt with an elastic band:

  • If you want a very fluffy skirt with a lot of folds at the waist, draw a larger radius when patterning. Be sure to align all folds before flashing. For dense and heavy fabrics, wide folds look more advantageous.
  • Before sewing natural fabrics, in order to avoid deformation of the product, it is recommended to wash and iron.
  • Choose the right patterns on the fabric that will not enlarge the figure. A skirt-sun on an elastic band visually gives volume to the hips.
  • If the bottom of the skirt turns out to be uneven, ask someone to help you. Put on a skirt and start turning slowly in it in a circle, the second person can hold any flat part and draw a new edge on the product, with chalk, for example.
