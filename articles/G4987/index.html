How to make a dog out of a sausage ball? Technique, useful tips

Many circus spectators, watching how a clown deftly twists a long ball in his hands, creating figures in a couple of seconds, think about how to make a dog out of a sausage ball. The master is quick and easy because he trained for a long time, making many mistakes. If you wish, you can learn how to twist simple figures, but you need to master the technique of this process and listen to the necessary advice from professionals.

What is twisting?

Before we make a dog out of a long ball, let's find out where such art came from and where it is now applied. Modeling various figures from balloons is very popular in our time, although it arose long ago, at the beginning of the last century, in America. At first, only clowns in circus performances twisted, but soon the craftsmen surprised the kids in the amusement parks, selling figures for money. He came up with how to make a dog out of a ball, American Hermann Bonnert from Pennsylvania in 1939.

Now it is impossible to imagine a single children's holiday without figures of animals, butterflies or flowers from balloons. Entire decoration companies for anniversaries and birthdays, graduation parties and corporate parties, store openings and holidays in kindergarten appeared. However, the twisting technology is simple, if desired, everyone can learn how to make simple figures.

In the article we will tell you how to make a dog out of a ball. The diagrams presented in the article show the step-by-step execution of the work, and a detailed explanation of the process of twisting bubbles will help you cope with the task faster and easier.

Necessary materials

Over time, you will learn to collect the figures of any characters from balls of different shapes, but we will learn from special rubber products designed for modeling. This is a long and thin ball, which is popularly called the "sausage." The standard dimensions of such a product are 5 √ó 150 cm. They are available in different colors and densities.

When buying balls, pay attention to its type. Balls No. 260 or 260-2 are considered the most convenient for twisting, as they are strong, but at the same time elastic. During inflation, you do not need to worry that the product will crack and deflate.

Tips Before You Start

  1. Before starting work, pull the ball with your hands in different directions, then it will become more elastic and will not tear when pumped with air.
  2. When inflating, leave 15 cm of the ball not inflated so that when the parts are twisted, excess air can fill the void and the ball does not burst.
  3. After inflating, tie the end of the ball with a simple knot.
  4. Always start work with the head of the dog.
  5. Before you make a dog out of a ball, squeeze the bubble of each part a couple of times, and then twist it.
  6. All parts are twisted in only one direction.
  7. At a minimum, each bubble must be scrolled 360 ¬∞ around its axis, but more can be done.

At the end of the work, make two identical bubbles for the hind legs and twist them together in front of the tail. Now you know how to make a dog out of a sausage ball.

Work Options

After several training exercises, you will want to modify and diversify the performance of the dog. Depending on the breed, the shape of the ball, the number of bubbles and their size also change.
