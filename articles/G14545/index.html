Hotels in Monchegorsk (Murmansk region): overview, addresses, reviews

Located in the small city of Monchegorsk, located beyond the Arctic Circle, guests and tourists are attracted by the picturesque nature and many interesting sights. Amazingly beautiful terrain among the tundra, the Moncha River with its rapids and the Bolshaya Imandra Lake will not leave anyone indifferent. And so that you can relax in comfortable conditions after fascinating walks, you are invited to Monchegorsk hotels.

Hotel Lapland

In a multi-story building in the city center, the three-star Lapland Hotel is located. Having stayed here, you will get the opportunity to take a walk in the picturesque park, which is located nearby, as well as to visit the Monchegorsk Museum of Colored Stone. The hotel's own restaurant serves European cuisine. The wellness center offers guests its services at any time of the day. The hotel staff will help you choose a fascinating tour of the Kola Peninsula. It is possible to arrange transfers from or to the airport or train station. There is a bus stop nearby. A cash machine is installed on site, there is a luggage storage room, a place is provided where guests can store their skis. Parking and Wi-Fi are free. Pets are allowed here for an extra fee. The Laplandia Hotel in Monchegorsk offers several categories of rooms for accommodating guests:

  • single standard - 4145 rubles per day;
  • single comfort - 4585 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 5100 rubles per day for two people and 4750 rubles if one person lives;
  • a suite with two single beds - 8250 rubles per day for three people, 7150 rubles for two people and 6800 rubles for one person;
  • Suite with a large double bed - from 7500 to 10 050 rubles per day, depending on the number of residents.

Price includes continental breakfast. All rooms offer views of Lake Bolshaya Imandra and the Khibiny Mountains. The Laplandia Hotel is located at 32 Metallurgov Avenue.
