Kanzashi: all kinds of petals

How many varieties of leaves, no needlewoman knows, as new forms are constantly being created. Conventionally, they can be divided into two subgroups:



  • in the shape of a petal - round, sharp;
  • in color - plain, two-tone, multi-color.

Let's get started with a simple round kanzashi petal.

  • Fold the square in half along the diagonal line.
  • The result is a triangle. Its top is turned towards you.
  • On each side, bend the edges to the top.
  • The result is a square that you bend, creating the shape of a petal.
  • Cut off the tip obliquely, singe with a candle, press with your fingers.
  • Flip the petal so that the rounded portion is turned down. Cut the wrong side to form a neat leaf. Singe it, press it with tweezers.
  • Spread the petal. The result was a boat shape.

How to make bicolor kanzashi petals? MK is the same, only two squares of different colors are taken. On top lay the material that will lie inside the petal. Fold them individually with triangles that overlap each other. All other actions will be the same.

Wide wavy petal

How to make a two-color wide leaflet? For the basis you take the above workshop. Fold the squares with triangles, lay them on top of each other. Fold the edges to the top, bend the petal, cut off the tip. Singe it, press it with your fingers. Next, turn the petal over without cutting anything, singe each side individually, pressing with tweezers.

Sharp petals

Sharp forms can be created in many ways. They are used in the creation of leaves, flowers (asters, carnations, chrysanthemums). We start parsing a simple sheet with a sharp top.



  • Fold the square diagonally.
  • The result is a triangle that folds in half.
  • The sides of the small figure are folded again, clamped with tweezers.
  • Cut the tail, singe fire.
  • The result is a leaf with space.

If you want a fused, sharp kanzashi petal, then you need to turn the leaf over, cut a little from the wrong side, scorch it with fire, press it with tweezers. Two-color elements are very beautiful. To do this, add two squares separately twice. Next, place the triangles on top of each other, fold the sides, cut the tail with the "wrong side", singe.

A two-color sheet can be made in another way. When fold the squares once, then grasp the other with one triangle. That is, the first vertex looks down (in the arm), then the second figure is directed to the right. Then the opposite sides of the figures alternately clasp each other. It turns out a triangle from which you form a leaf. The rest of the actions will be the same. It turns out a beautiful two-color sharp petal. Kanzashi with a combination of sharp and round petals looks incomparable.

As a last step, the wire trunk is glued to the hairpin, rim, comb and decorated with a cord or other petals.
