As befits, under it are other accessories. This is a charger, headphones, battery and manual. We can conclude that the developers did not stint in the issue of equipping the phone.





Of course, equipping the phone with such additions as a headset and a spare battery does not play a special role, but nevertheless it's nice to see some efforts by the developer to expand the proposed package.

Battery

Lenovo smartphones with powerful equipment, judging by the reviews, often suffer from low endurance, having batteries of small capacity. So, according to technical data, Lenovo A7000 (8gb), which we were looking for reviews, has at its disposal a 2900 mAh battery. Of course, the site presents the results of official measurements of the endurance of this battery; however, they, as we know, do not always correspond to reality. In order not to repeat them, we will note more simply - when interacting with the phone in normal (everyday) mode (which provides 3-4 hours of active screen glow and periodic work with 3G / LTE networks for 30-40 minutes), the device can last up to two days on a single charge. Of course, if you use it more actively, this figure will decrease. In any case, reviews describing the Lenovo A7000 Dual will call the device insufficiently hardy - this is normal, given the level of charge on such smartphones.
