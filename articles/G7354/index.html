Hotel Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea 3 * Israel: photos, reviews

The Dead Sea is one of the most mysterious places on our planet. It is located at the lowest point on Earth.

Here, their laws reign, attracting many people who want to admire the extraordinary beauty of Israel and improve their health on the healing mud of the Dead Sea. Several hundred thousand tourists from all over the world come here every year, including our compatriots. In our article, we will talk about one of the most pleasant and affordable hotels located on the coast itself - Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea. We will give you general information about this hotel complex, its number of rooms and the cost of living.





Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea 3: hotel description

This hotel complex belongs to the network and until 2011 had a different name. This is a small hotel that is distinguished by calm and comfort. Here you can have a great time and improve your health far from the hustle and bustle.

Many of those who have already visited the Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea 3, in the description specify that the hotel is located in a beautiful garden, where beautiful peacocks roam and exotic flowers grow.

The hotel complex belongs to the small and far from new Israeli hotels. It opened in 1981, the last reconstruction was completed seven years ago. Therefore, many tourists note that the hotel complex is quite modest, but clean and filled with some kind of home comfort.

Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea is a three-story building, surrounded by greenery. It has ninety-six practically identical rooms, ten of them are suitable for non-smokers.

Hotel Location

Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea 3 is located in a very beautiful and popular resort town - Ein Bokek. The resort is located on the south coast of the Dead Sea and is almost year-round filled with noisy crowds of tourists.

The hotel is almost on the shore, so you can walk to the beach in just four minutes. Very close to the Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea is a large shopping center that sells a lot of products, including popular cosmetics with Dead Sea mud.

The hotel is just over forty kilometers from the airport. If you are planning a trip to Jerusalem, then know that you will have to overcome about one hundred and fifteen kilometers. Also, from the hotel complex you can go on an excursion to the city of Arad (this is forty-five kilometers) or to Tel Aviv (one hundred eighty-five kilometers).





Infrastructure

Most tourists go to the Dead Sea with a completely banal goal - to improve their health and undergo rejuvenation procedures. Here they are very common, and miracles have long been told about the capabilities of Israeli doctors and cosmetologists. Therefore, choosing a hotel on the Promised Land, tourists are very interested in its infrastructure. So what can Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea 3 offer its guests?

The hotel offers ample parking where you can leave a rented car. Indeed, many tourists who want to see all the main attractions of Israel, rent a car and travel around different cities and memorable places with pleasure.

The Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea 3 has two pools in a small area. One of them is filled with sea water, which is heated to a comfortable temperature. The second pool is smaller, filled with fresh water.

To ensure that guests do not have to climb steep stairs to the hotel, architects provided an elevator. It greatly facilitates the lives of older tourists who come to Israel in large numbers.

The hotel has a small and cozy restaurant, and by the pool you can sit down and have a cocktail, which the local bartender, judging by the reviews of hotel guests, is a great master. Here you can have a light bite before dinner, because hot and cold snacks in the cafe at the restaurant will please any gourmet.

It is hard to imagine a modern hotel complex without laundry and dry cleaning. Naturally, they are also present at the Leonardo Inn Hotel Dead Sea 3. Guests can always return dirty clothes there (at an additional cost) and get things clean and well ironed.

When the purpose of your trip is business, the hotel complex staff will help you organize an event of any complexity in the conference room for one hundred people. Here you can hold business negotiations or a seminar.

Garden of the hotel complex

Talking about the hotel, it is difficult not to mention its garden. All visitors to the hotel complex talk about him with great admiration. SPA-center

Number of rooms

As we have already specified, the hotel has only ninety-six rooms. They are made in approximately the same design and practically do not differ from each other. Guests of the hotel complex note that in the rooms there is battered furniture and the same appliances. The air conditioner often buzzes, the refrigerator with its noise also does not allow you to relax at night. The furniture is shabby, it is noticeable on tables, chairs and many other items. But in general, the rooms are always clean and tidy, they are fully consistent with the declared category of three stars.

Settlement takes place at three in the afternoon, and it is necessary to evict before eleven in the morning. Please note that pets are not allowed in the hotel. This is strictly prohibited by the hotel complex. If you arrive at the hotel on Saturday or a Jewish holiday, you can only check in after sunset. This is a peculiar feature of many Israeli hotel complexes.
