Daniel Negreanu is a professional poker player: biography, interesting facts

The most famous and successful poker player, Daniel Negreanu, prophesied the fate of a professional snooker. He spent most of his time in the billiard room of the city of Toronto, where, constantly in the atmosphere of gambling, including poker, he decided to try his luck. This circumstance became decisive in his future life. Success and skill immediately manifested themselves in the youth. Toronto's gaming halls soon became cramped with him, and he moved to Las Vegas.





The most famous player in the world

Today , Daniel Negreanu is the most famous and recognizable poker player. He has a large number of admirers, the number of which is constantly growing due to the openness and friendliness of the player. His success is associated not only with professional abilities, but also with his amazing sociability. He had long been known as a wonderful storyteller. Daniel can capture in an exciting and colorful way any, even completely unremarkable, event that happened at the poker table. Negreanu Biography

Daniel Negreanu, whose biography does not differ from millions of others, lived in a simple and well-to-do family. The future millionaire was born in one of the Canadian states, in the city of Toronto, in 1974, on July 29. His parents were immigrants who, eight years before his birth, moved from Romania to Canada for permanent residence. About mother Annie and father Konstantin there is practically no data. Only one thing is known that they prophesied to his son a professional career as a player in billiards.





Playing billiards, he was constantly close to poker players, and by the age of 15 he had already learned to play it perfectly. The game immediately completely absorbed Daniel, he even sacrificed his studies and, without graduating from high school, switched to the professional level of playing poker, and began to regularly attend poker tournaments. In his city, he meets Evelyn Ng, a professional poker player at that time, adopting strategies and gaining invaluable experience.

The tragic events of 1996 prompted the young man to think about his future and take everything into his own hands. His father is dying this year, and Daniel Negreanu decides to move to Las Vegas. The first major tournament

After analyzing his capabilities and participating in several tournaments organized by the owners of the largest Las Vegas casinos, having gained experience, Daniel realized that he was ready for something more. Professional masters could envy his abilities. Having gained even more experience and knowledge, in 1997 he participated in a competition called World Poker Finals, held in Foxwood. The youngest player
