Hotel Thapsus Aquapark 4 * (Tunisia, Mahdia): photos, reviews of tourists

Tunisia can hardly be called a tourist center for beach holidays. This country, with its distinctive culture, seems to be in the past, despite all the latest technologies, cars and airplanes. It is just as quiet and peaceful as it was 100 and 200 years ago. Rather, it is good for those who want to really relax from the noise, crowds and crowds. If you belong to this category of travelers, then the Thapsus Aquapark 4 * hotel will be a good place to spend time with your family.

Mahdia

This small town with a stretch can be called a resort, since it does not yet have those features by which you recognize similar settlements around the world:

  • There is a huge number of people scurrying back and forth on business and without work.
  • There is no usual noise of voices, crying or cries of numerous children and parents screaming at them.
  • Nobody grabs hands and tries to sell some consumer goods on the go.

Perhaps all these signs will someday become the property of Mahdia, but the guests of this city write in their reviews about him that they would not want this to happen with such a nice and cozy village. The only drawback that none of the clients of the Thapsus Aquapark 4 * hotel in Tunisia could not keep silent about is the incredible dirt on the way to the city, and even in it. You should be aware that choosing the Thapsus Aquapark 4 * hotel as a place to relax in Mahdia in Tunisia, you agree to a quiet relaxing holiday without exciting attractions, spectacular shows, discos and foam parties. What to bring from Tunisia

Mahdia is a famous silk production center throughout Tunisia.
