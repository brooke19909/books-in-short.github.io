"Alexandros Hotel 4" in Corfu - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Our compatriots have long appreciated both Greece and one of the most famous and most beautiful islands of this country - Corfu. Thousands of tourists from all over the world come here every year to enjoy the magnificent sea, magnificent beaches, picturesque nature, as well as to touch historical and cultural monuments. So, if you plan to go on vacation to the marvelous Greek island of Corfu, then the Alexandros Hotel 4 * will be a good option for your stay. We suggest today to find out what this hotel offers its guests, and also to find out how much the vacation here will cost and what impressions the tourists from Russia staying at this hotel have.





Where is the hotel situated?

Alexandros Hotel 4 * is located in a small resort village of Corfu called Perama. The distance to both the city of Corfu and the airport is five kilometers. Thus, upon arrival, you can drive to your hotel in just a quarter of an hour. Also, six kilometers from Perama, there is a port from where you can make a sea voyage. It is interesting that the guests of the Alexandros Hotel do not have to go far in search of any attractions. So, one of the most important architectural monuments of the island of Corfu - the palace of Achillion - is within walking distance. With regard to proximity to the sea, the Alexandros Hotel 4 * is located on the second coastline. In order to get to the beach, you must cross the road.

What is a hotel like?

This small hotel offers its guests a comfortable and interesting vacation. The hotel has 102 rooms equipped with everything necessary, and has a magnificent panoramic view of the Ionian Sea and the famous islet of Pontikonisi. The last large-scale reconstruction in the hotel was carried out in 2007. The number of stars assigned to the hotel is 4. Alexandros Hotel (Perama) today is a great place for a comfortable holiday for family people with children and youth. The hotel has a swimming pool for adults and children, a tavern with a wide selection of drinks, a restaurant where chefs are ready to offer you delicious dishes of both national and international cuisine, a sun terrace, as well as a shaded garden. The local staff works around the clock to make the stay as comfortable as possible.





Greece: hotel Alexandros Hotel 4 *: accommodation rules

In accordance with the rules of this hotel complex, the settlement of newly staying guests occurs after two in the afternoon. If you arrived earlier than this date, and the hotel has available rooms, then you will be settled immediately. However, if the previous guests have not yet vacated the rooms, and the staff did not have time to do the cleaning (this usually happens at the height of the tourist season), then you will be asked to leave your suitcases and bags in the storage room and go for a snack, swim in the pool, sunbathe on the terrace or take a walk around the hotel and its surroundings. On the day of departure from the hotel, you should vacate the room before noon. Then it is necessary to pay for the entire period of residence and use of additional services. If you have already paid for accommodation in Alexandros Hotel 4 * with your travel agency, then at check out you will only have to pay for additional services if you used them. You can pay here both in cash and with plastic cards of the Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Diners Club systems. Also note that when booking a room via the Internet, the hotel reserves the right to block part of the cost of living. In this case, at discharge you only have to pay the balance for the entire placement period.





Accommodation for children and animals

Since Alexandros Hotel 4 * (Corfu Island) is a family hotel, children of any age are allowed here. Please note that in each room maximum, only one cot or extra bed can be accommodated. Moreover, if you place a baby up to two years old in a crib, his stay will be free. Please inform the hotel in advance if an extra bed is required.

As for the accommodation of pets, then, to the delight of people traveling in the company of their four-legged pets, the hotel allows their accommodation in rooms. However, it is necessary to preliminary coordinate this possibility with the administration of Alexandros Hotel 4 *. In this case, payment for the placement of animals is not charged.

Sea beach

Alexandros Hotel 4 * (Perama) is located on the second coastline and does not have its own beach. You will have to walk a couple of hundred meters to the nearest municipal beach, crossing the road along the way. The beach is small pebble, with a gentle entry into the water. Here, for an additional fee, you can use sun loungers, parasols and towels. Also on the beach there is everything you need for water sports.





Pool

There is a freshwater pool on site. Near it there is a sun terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas, the use of which is free for hotel guests. There is also a bar where you can order your favorite soft or alcoholic drinks.

Entertainment

In order to diversify the rest of the guests of Alexandros Hotel 4 * (Greece), there are animators who entertain tourists throughout the day and arrange evening shows. Once a week there is a thematic Greek evening. You can also play billiards and basketball at the hotel.

Alexandros Hotel 4 * (Corfu): reviews

Since for many travelers when choosing a hotel it is very important to know the opinions of people who have already been here, we have summarized the comments of vacationers to present you with the big picture. So, how did our compatriots rate the four-star Alexandros hotel (Corfu island, Greece) ?

I must say that in general, most tourists were satisfied with their stay. As for the hotel itself, many vacationers quite liked the rooms provided to them. However, some guests had poor air conditioning in their rooms. Also, many tourists pay attention that the bathrooms do not have a hairdryer. If you need it, then you can contact the reception, where they will give it to you. Some guests also expressed dissatisfaction with their rooms located on the ground floor near the pool, as loud music was heard in them until late. Otherwise, there are no particular complaints about the apartments. On the contrary, many guests noted the magnificent view from the balconies and terraces of the hotel.

Conflicting reviews relate to food at the Alexandros Hotel 4 *. So, people who had previously rested in hotels in Turkey and Egypt and got used to the all-inclusive system adopted there, were a little disappointed. However, experienced travelers are skeptical of such comments, arguing that ‚Äúall inclusive‚Äù in all hotels in Greece differs markedly from the above countries. That is, it is very reckless to count on the same choice of dishes as in Turkey and Egypt. Nevertheless, most guests noted that the food at the Alexandros Hotel is very tasty, and the portions are quite large. In addition, there are a lot of restaurants near the hotel where you can also eat very tasty, tasting dishes of both national Greek and international cuisine.

Extremely positive emotions were left by tourists by the hotel staff. So, Alexandros 4 * employees are always ready to help you in solving any issue. Also, travelers note the friendliness and attentiveness of the staff to them. Many of our compatriots were surprised by the fact that the maids at the hotel do not tip, and the quality of cleaning is at the same time high. The only minor drawback is the fact that the hotel staff does not speak Russian. However, many of them are fluent in English, so you can always explain.

As for the pools, there were no special complaints from tourists. So, many note the fact that both the adult and the children's pool are cleaned daily. However, some of our compatriots were saddened by the fact that having come to the sunbathing terrace after breakfast, they could not find free sunbeds. Therefore, tourists who wanted to spend several hours near the pool had to get up, as they say, no light or dawn, and take a deck chair for themselves and family members.

In general, most Russians were satisfied with their stay at the four-star Alexandros Hotel, located on the Greek island of Corfu. They do not exclude the possibility of returning here again, and are also ready to recommend it to their friends and relatives.
