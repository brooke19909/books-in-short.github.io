Camping lantern - a warm little light on the way

Lovers of summer outdoor recreation - camping trips, fishing - will appreciate such a necessary item on the road as a portable flashlight. He will give a feeling of comfort while stopping for the night, a feeling of comfort will appear from his light.

A variety of camping lights



You can buy a camping lamp in the shops for tourists. And you will be pleased with a variety of models that are presented there. They differ not only in design. Devices can operate on battery or batteries, provide lighting thanks to LEDs. With a portable lamp, you can safely get to the tourist camp in the dark, in its warm light it is pleasant to relax and read a fairy tale to children.





Description of models

Camping LED flashlight is very convenient in operation, it is economical and reliable. Its body is made of stainless steel and resembles old kerosene lamps, which gives it a special charm. The flashlight runs on three AA batteries. They last for twenty-eight hours. The modern device is equipped with twelve LED-LEDs and provides illumination of 40 lumens. These are the characteristics of the Vango model. For the convenience of controlling the lamp, an infrared remote control is attached to it. To switch the camping lamp into remote control mode, a large button is located on the case. The device can be carried by the folding handle. In order to suspend the device, for example, in a tent, it has a loop. The biggest advantage of such models is their durability and efficiency. LEDs do not require frequent replacement, and their work is enough for quite a long time. Their diffused light is bright, pleasing to the eyes and illuminates a large area.





Folding lights

Depending on the model, the camping lamp can be attached to the surface using a special magnet, which is located on the back of the device. For example, such is the ERA-K48 lamp, which has a flat shape and is ideal for use in the country or during a picnic in nature. As a rule, lamps are of good quality and meet a lot of positive feedback from customers. In addition, specialized stores offer their rich assortment. For example, you can meet a camping lamp, which folds and turns into a handheld device. It is very convenient to take with you on the road. If you pull the handle located on top, it stretches out and takes on a shape resembling an old gas lamp. In this form, the lamp can be suspended in a tent. It gives an even and bright light. The device is very light. Its weight is one hundred forty grams and its dimensions are 85 x 132 x 50 mm.

Rechargeable flashlights

The life of the fixtures depends on the type of power supply. Camping rechargeable flashlight is more reliable, since its operating time is longer than that of devices that operate on batteries. There are several options. There are lamps that charge manually or from a car cigarette lighter. In some cases, you have to replace the battery. A wonderful choice will be a dual-mode flashlight. The device combines two in one: a table lamp and a rechargeable floodlight. The lantern is equipped with one powerful 1 Watt LED and 15 bright elements.
