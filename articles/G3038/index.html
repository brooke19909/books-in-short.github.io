Crafts from apples to decorate the Christmas tree. Since time immemorial in Russia it has been customary to decorate Christmas trees with different sweets. For this purpose they used chocolate and sweets, various gingerbread cookies, nuts and fruits - fresh and candied. The Christmas tree pleased the kids not only with its beauty, but also with sweet surprises that could easily be found in its dense branches. Let's revive this tradition and make DIY crafts from apples to decorate the Christmas tree. Dress up the apple in a New Year's mask. To do this, we cut a narrow mask with cuts for the eyes from colored paper on the size of an apple. Decorate it with colored sparkles. From another sheet of paper, roll up a cone (like a familiar candy bag). Its shape should be narrow and long. Glue so that the cone does not turn. On the wide side, at the base with scissors, make small cuts, bend them, glue them with glue and glue in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe nose to the prepared mask. It turned out a mask with a long nose, like Pinocchio. On the sides we attach the ties to it and put on an apple. Tie a loop to an apple stick, then hang it on a Christmas tree.



DIY crafts from apples for a festive decor. One of the symbols of Christmas is a festive wreath that is hung on a door or wall. Bright red apples are an excellent material for its manufacture. We will need small apples of equal size, fir cones, nuts, a round base made of plastic or plywood, glue. Glue a large fir cone on the base in the center , then in a circle fill its entire surface with rows of apples and nuts. The last row of fir cones is placed along the edge of the base in the form of large petals. On the reverse side we fasten the red satin ribbon with a stapler, tie it with a beautiful bow and hang a wreath on the door or on the wall.
