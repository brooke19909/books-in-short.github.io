Climate and ecology

The climate of the Altai Republic is sharply continental. Characteristic for him is a hot, but very short summer, as well as a long, frosty winter. During the summer it often rains, but if you manage to catch the moment, the rest will go well. The best time to visit Altai is June-July. Already in August it becomes cold on its territory, and precipitation appears every day.

As for ecology, in the place where the camp site is located, tourists will be able to enjoy the cleanest air. Since there are no large industrial cities nearby, the area is not polluted by chemical waste.
