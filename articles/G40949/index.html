Recreation center "Ob open spaces" in Novosibirsk: clean air and unity with nature

The recreation center "Ob open spaces" in Novosibirsk is an unforgettable vacation all year round. Pure fresh air and green pine forest will charge any guest of the complex with the forces necessary for a busy city life for several months in advance.

The recreation center "Ob open spaces" in Novosibirsk: how to get there, working hours

The boarding house is located in the Horde district. From Novosibirsk, the distance to the complex is approximately 130 km. From the city by car you need to leave on the street. Trolley. Then you need to go to the Horde highway.

You need to move towards the village of Kirza. It is necessary to drive through this settlement, after which there will be a sign on the road to a boarding house near a turn in the forest zone. This dirt road will lead to the gates of the recreation center "Ob open spaces" in Novosibirsk.





The complex operates year-round. You can book a room by phone listed on the official website of the company, or online there.

Accommodations

All buildings are decorated in a boarding house in the Russian traditional style. Guests can choose their own room according to their preferences:

  • In the building "Sun" rooms with two single beds. Designed only for summer holidays. There is no heating system, there is a fridge, microwave and TV (one per room), a bathroom and shower.
  • House number 6 - a two-story cottage, has separate entrances for every two rooms with a veranda. On the ground floor there are 4 rooms with three single beds, on the second floor there are two rooms with four beds, each room has a microwave and electric kettle, a bathroom and a shower cubicle are one in two. The case is intended only for summer holidays.
  • Houses number 7 and number 8 - wooden, 4 rooms each with a separate entrance for every two and a veranda. The rooms have three single beds, each room has a microwave and electric kettle; one bathroom and shower in two rooms.
  • House number 9 is intended for recreation all year round (there is a heating system). It has two large rooms with separate entrances and verandas. The rooms are fully equipped with necessary furniture, in each room there is a microwave oven, an electric kettle and a set of dishes, a bathroom and shower, TV and Internet connection.




Near each cottage there is a spacious clearing with a place and equipment for grilling.

Entertainment and features

On the territory of the recreation center "Ob open spaces" (Novosibirsk) organized several sports grounds for playing soccer and volleyball. Also close to this area are outdoor exercise machines for sports.

Fans of fishing are given the opportunity to do their hobby on the Ob Sea. At the recreation center there is a small motor boat for access to the water. It also has a sandy beach where you can sunbathe and swim.

The complex has an artificial lake with fish. Fans of extreme sports are invited to rent ATVs and speed bicycles.

For a large company, paintball games are arranged. For this, a special zone has been allocated and the necessary equipment is available.

On the base of the rest "Ob open spaces" in Novosibirsk built a real Russian bath with all the attributes. It has a separate relaxation area where you can relax after visiting the steam room.

A small petting zoo is located on site. Goats, ducks, rabbits, calves and small dogs live in it. Absolutely all adults and children can stroke and play with them.

The complex has covered gazebos with cooking equipment on the fire. Here you can fry aromatic barbecue or cook cabbage soup in a Russian oven.





In winter, visitors are offered skiing, ice skating, sledding and tubing. In summer, you can pick mushrooms and berries in the forests.

The recreation center "Ob open spaces" in Novosibirsk: reviews

Despite the fact that the rooms in the complex do not have a high level of comfort, most of the guests really enjoyed their stay here. Visitors note that the territory of the base is always clean and the staff instantly responds to the wishes of vacationers.





The rooms are always cleaned on time, in the dining room very tasty food. Young visitors enjoy playing with animals at the zoo. Adults also have no time to be bored.

Fresh brooms always lie in the bathhouse and the staff melts it qualitatively. Equipment for entertainment is operational and in stock, as stated on the site. In cool weather, electric heaters are placed in the buildings and guests feel comfortable.
