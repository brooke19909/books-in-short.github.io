Short review of the Triva Hotel (Moscow)

Hotel Triva (Moscow) is one of the most popular places for temporary residence in the Russian capital. Next, we consider the main features of this hotel, as well as some reviews of hotel guests.

Location

Hotel Triva is located in the Russian capital, within walking distance from metro station Krasnoselskaya. Many of the reviews left for this place of rest say that the hotel is located in an easily accessible place, which can be easily reached using taxi services or public transport. For guests who arrived at the hotel by car, free parking is available on the territory of the place for recreation.

Within walking distance from the Triva Hotel (Moscow) are some city attractions, including the Church of the Monk Alexy, the theater and several monuments to prominent political figures - Melnikov and Lenin.

Address: 3rd Krasnoselsky Lane, 5.

Rooms

This place for recreation and temporary residence provides comfortable apartments in which there is everything you need for a comfortable stay. Reviews of the Triva Hotel (Moscow) often say that the price set for staying in hotel rooms (from 4,500 rubles per day) is fully justified.

The rooms of the hotel in question are equipped with modern comfortable furniture, which includes: a large bed (in some apartments the double has been replaced with two single beds), bedside tables, as well as a work area and a corner for relaxing, as well as conducting warm tea drinking. Here, guests are presented with a large plasma TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system, as well as an electronic safe and a minibar. Guest rooms at Triva Hotel have large wardrobes suitable for storing clothes.

The apartments of the hotel in question are equipped with modern air conditioning, able to maintain the air temperature at an optimal level regardless of the season. Each room also has a well-equipped bathroom, where guests are provided with bath amenities.

Service

The reviews of many guests of the Triva Hotel (Moscow) often say that the considered place for living has excellent infrastructure. In particular, in the hotel building there is a small restaurant where not only guests, but also casual guests can have lunch and dinner.

The hotel has a laundry room, which also provides ironing services. Moreover, if you wish, you can visit the beauty salon, which is located on the first floor of the building.

Tourists wishing to check into the Triva Hotel can pre-book a transfer from their arrival point to the place of accommodation - this service is paid (1,300 rubles) and is provided on comfortable vehicles with air conditioning. At the reception, guests can call a taxi, as well as ask to wake up at a certain time.

In reviews of the hotel, his former guests often note that this place is ideal for families. This is due, first of all, to the fact that in the Triva building there is a small children's club, where babies and animators can look after the kids for a fee. The local restaurant has a separate menu for children, which is also very convenient for those who prefer to feed their child not only delicious and beautiful, but also healthy dishes.

Prices

Accommodation in the hotel in question is quite inexpensive. Guests note in their reviews that the average cost of a daily rental of single rooms of the Standard class costs about 3,000 rubles, and doubles of the order of 4,500. Moreover, the hotel offers accommodation in apartments of the type "Junior Suite" and "Suite". The cost of living in the first of them is 6,500, and in the second - 8,000 rubles.
