Hotel Kerkyra Village Studios 3 * (Greece, Corfu): reviews

The island of Corfu, or, as the locals exaltedly call it, Kerkyra is the most northern of the islands of Greece. The natural beauty of the area is difficult to exaggerate. This paradise, fertile place pleases with orange and olive groves, which dizzy with the aroma of spring and happiness, and huge vineyards, where raw materials for fine Greek wines grow.

In 2007, the island was given the status of a UNESCO World Heritage Site.



Picturesque, atmospheric Kerkyra

Kerkyra is the capital of the island of the same name. Corfu itself over a long history has been attacked and conquered several times by different civilizations. For a rather long historical period, Corfu was ruled by Italy, which undoubtedly left an impressive imprint on the cultural and architectural features of the region.

Kerkyra is more like a magnificent medieval city than the modern capital. Impressive cultural diversity makes it a suitable place not only for a relaxing, abstract holiday, but also for an informative, cultural pastime.



Hotels in Corfu differ from the stereotype common among our compatriots. You rarely see animators, rows of buffets or crowds of guides with offers of excursions.

Hotel Kerkyra Village Studios 3 * in Greece, Corfu

This is an apartment type hotel. This is the way the hotel business is spread and popular in Corfu. The hotel consists of 18 family apartments.

The villas are located in a picturesque garden, which is a great place for relaxing walks. There are also green spaces and relaxation areas with sea views on the roof of the hotel.

Kerkyra Village Studios 3 * - a budget hotel, one of the cheapest among those that offer domestic tour operators. A double room with breakfast per day costs 29 euros .



There are no facilities for disabled guests at the hotel. The complex accepts visitors with pets by prior arrangement.

Check-in time 14:00, eviction before 13:00 . Credit cards are accepted at the reception, and there are ATMs within 100 meters of the hotel where cash can be withdrawn.

Hotel Location

The hotel is 200 meters from the picturesque village of Benitses, 12 km from the capital of Kerkyra and 13 km from Corfu International Airport.

Benitses a dozen years ago was a modest fishing village, picturesque and peaceful, but with the development of tourism in Corfu, various objects of tourist infrastructure appeared in large numbers in Benitses. The silence and harmony of such a place, as well as the proximity to the capital, further stimulate the growth of popularity of this small resort. It is very likely that soon this territory will cease to be a peaceful vacation haven for travelers tired of urban life, and will turn into a bustling coastal town.



At 130 meters from Kerkyra Village Studios 3 * is the city beach. He was awarded the European Union Blue Flag for cleanliness and safety.

Group bus excursions to Corfu are just as unpopular as hotel animations. Tourists prefer to set off on their own to discover and explore the island. A popular way of traveling over short distances is cycling. Two-wheeled vehicles can be rented at numerous offices located throughout the island. The issue price is about 3 euros per day.

Corfu is a hilly island, so it is not advisable to go for a bike ride on the mountains, it is better to take a car. There are also a lot of rental offices on the island, the average price for a Skoda or Ford car is 45 euros per day.



Description and classification of numbers

All rooms at the Kerkyra Village Studios Apartments 3 * studio type: double and triple. The apartments have a balcony or terrace. Some rooms have sea views.



The room has a stove, electric kettle, dishes, washing machine, air conditioning, refrigerator, bath or shower, TV.

Room cleaning - 6 times a week, towel change every two days, bed linen - once a week. There is no room service in the hotel.

Hotel service

In Kerkyra hotels the service is not as diverse as in the resort complexes of Bulgaria or Turkey, but this does not affect its quality. The hotel does not have a mini-club, room service, animation, SPA center and other options, but the minimum services necessary for a normal stay at Kerkyra Village Studios 3 * are still provided.

  • Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the rooms and rooms.
  • Free car parking is available.
  • There is no laundry or dry cleaning at the hotel; rooms have washing machines.
  • There is a small freshwater pool, as well as a playground and a garden for walks.




  • There is a safe at the reception, use is free.

Hotel beach

At 120 meters from Corfu Kerkyra Village Studios 3 * there is a municipal beach of Benitses. The beach is luxurious, well-maintained, the water is clean, the shore is wide and flat. Not for nothing, this beach is awarded the Blue Flag. Given that the beach is public, sometimes in the high season, especially on weekends, it is crowded.

Umbrellas and sunbeds on the beach are paid, or rather, conditionally paid. Those who do not want to pay for a lounger can be advised to buy a cocktail or ice cream at the beach bar, then sun loungers near this establishment will be free for the client all day.

The beach is pebble, the entrance to the sea is also small pebbles, the water is clear and clean even at an impressive depth.
