How to make a hat with a visor out of a newspaper: manufacturing nuances



If you are looking for something to cover your head from the scorching sun in the summer on the street or how to hide hair from paint with plaster during repair, then you can remember how to make a hat out of a newspaper. This, incidentally, is also a good way to entertain and entertain a child of almost any age - kids are very interested in doing something with their own hands.

Probably everyone knows how to make a hat in the shape of a cap from the newspaper, because this is the most common option. Take a sheet of the right size, fold it in half. Put the resulting paper with the fold away from you, wrap the sides inward so that 2 identical triangles come out. At the same time, their sides should be in contact, and the angle that turned out during bending is the top of the cap. A small strip of paper remains below, it must be wrapped up, as if wrapping a triangle formed on both sides. The edges of the strip must be wrapped to strengthen the structure and give it a more aesthetic appearance.



This is probably the most common and easy way to make a hat out of a newspaper. But for those who know how to work with paper and love creativity, other options for hats were invented. So, some prefer to make an army cap instead of a regular cap, the technology of its manufacture is a little more complicated, but it turns out to be a more interesting and non-standard option. But many are interested in how a hat from a newspaper with a visor can be made, because this is an indispensable option for working in the country, for relaxing in the forest or at sea. In such a cap, you will definitely be different from others and you can entice children for some time who will definitely want to make caps for themselves.

Before you start work, you must find the right material. For such a hat, you will need a large spread from a newspaper or a sheet of A1 format . The beginning is no different from the manufacture of a cap: fold it in half, make two symmetrical triangles from the fold. But the remaining edge of the newspaper must be tucked not once, but 2 times. The base for the cap is ready. This is a universal technology, how to make a hat from a newspaper, because the option is suitable for making a cap, army or cowboy hat.



In order to continue the production of the headgear with a visor, one double gate must be deployed, do not touch the other fold - it will go behind the product and serve as its supporting base (the so-called band). Continue like this: reduce the angles of the resulting triangle to the center and try on the head of the future owner of the cap. Usually in a cap for children, they will overlap each other, and in adults they barely touch. At the next stage, create a visor: bend in half the rectangle formed at the bottom of the future heading, this will be the visor. To give it a shape, it is necessary to roll two long triangles from the edges so that their corners can be fixed under the bearing base of the cap.

Tuck the top of the product under the flap on the back. For beauty, wrap the corners of the cap. Now you are aware of how to make a hat out of a newspaper and still not look like a worker who has escaped from a construction site.
