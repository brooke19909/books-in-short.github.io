Armas Labada Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Kemer, Camyuva): description, service, reviews

With each summer season, interest in Turkey and its sea coasts does not fade away, but, on the contrary, flares up with renewed vigor. Our compatriots have great love for this country and its hotels, because it is there for quite reasonable money that you can get not only a wonderful vacation, but also high-quality service, which is almost always at its best.

Each of the resorts in Turkey offers tourists a huge number of options for accommodation, and it is sometimes very difficult to choose a specific one from them. Some travelers follow the advice of tour operators, someone selects a hotel on a whim, and some study the options in great detail in advance and make an informed and informed choice. And in this they are helped by detailed hotel reviews.

One of the popular holiday destinations in Turkey is a city called Kemer. Here, in the small village of Camyuva, the Armas Labada Hotel 5 * is located, with a description of which it will be interesting to familiarize any future tourist of this sunny country.

Old hotel names

Its current name "Armas Labada Hotel" this hotel received recently, only at the beginning of 2017. It was at this time that the hotel became part of the Turkish group Armas Hotels. At the same time, the resort complex has acquired a team of professionals who, with particular care, try to satisfy all the wishes of their guests.





Prior to this, the hotel, built back in 2006, bore the following names "Asdem Beach", "Labada Beach Hotel" and "Asdem Labada Hotel".

Convenient location of the hotel

For lovers of a relaxing beach holiday and those who do not like long walks to the sea, the Armas Labada Hotel 5 * hotel is perfect. After all, it was built on the first coastal line closest to the water, so guests of this place will not have to spend a lot of time to get from their rooms to the gentle waves.

Just five kilometers away is the center of Kemer, where you can easily go for a walk or go shopping. The famous large resort town of Antalya is 50 km away from the hotel, and you will have to drive a little further to its international airport - 60 km.

Several accommodation options

Armas Labada Hotel 5 * (Camyuva) is ready to offer its dear guests 186 bright and fairly spacious rooms in which every tourist can completely discard all his everyday activities and really enjoy a great vacation.

There are not many categories of rooms in the hotel, there are only two of them: standard rooms and junior suites. The windows offer unforgettable views of the sea, garden and mountains. Each guest is always entitled to choose for himself an apartment with the landscape outside the window that he likes the most. In Armas Labada Hotel 5 * accommodation prices vary: "junior suites" are more expensive.

For a comfortable stay, all rooms are equipped with private bathrooms with a shower or bathtub, necessary furniture, a minibar, which is replenished daily, central air conditioning, TV with satellite channels, a telephone for communication with the hotel reception, a safe for safe storage of documents and other valuables belonging to guests. Tasty meals by the sea

Armas Labada Hotel, like any five-star hotel in Turkey, offers all-inclusive meals. Throughout the day, guests can enjoy food and drinks at several restaurants and bars located on site.

The main meals are held in the main restaurant of the hotel, and they are organized in the form of a beloved "buffet". Here tourists are waiting for:

  • brunch, lasting only half an hour in the morning, starting at 10.00;
  • lunch starting at 12.30 and ending at 14.00;
  • dinner in the period from 19.00 to 21.00 hours;
  • a special meal for night owls, starting at 23.30 and lasting only half an hour - night soup.




The dishes presented in this restaurant apply to both Turkish and international cuisine, so any guest will surely find dishes to their liking. Also, on specific days of the week, theme nights are organized that always delight vacationers.

You can enjoy cool ice cream from 3 p.m. to 4 p.m., from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. there is a chance to taste the wonderful cakes of Gozleme, and you can just drink coffee or tea with fresh pastries from 4 p.m. to 5.30 p.m.

A wide range of entertainment at Armas Labada Hotel

In addition to the joys of sea spray and the sun, Armas Labada Hotel offers many activities that will make your stay in this place even more unforgettable.

Firstly, it is an opportunity to play sports at any time. To do this, you need a whole gym, swimming pools (including indoor), two tennis courts, a beach volleyball court and the ability to use non-motorized and motorized water equipment (boats, motorcycles). Table tennis, billiards, darts and water polo games are organized, and in the morning everyone can do gymnastics or aerobics. There is also a small water park, consisting of three slides.





Secondly, these are all kinds of show programs conducted by animators in the evenings, discos for vacationers of all ages and live music that can be enjoyed several times during the week.

And the little guests are welcome here

Young visitors who came to rest with their parents will not have to miss the hotel. A mini-club was created especially for them, in which professional animators will delight kids every day with their unique entertainment program. Children are waiting for all kinds of games and sports events and even a special disco, designed specifically for young dancers.

The mini-club has two age groups: from 4 to 12 years old and from 12 to 16 years old. All sorts of theatrical games, fun contests, colorings and even dance lessons are organized for younger children. Older children are also waiting for sports games in which they can prove themselves in all their glory.





On the territory there is a special playground where children can freely climb, ride the slides and run. In the restaurant they are waiting for highchairs, a children's menu and even a children's buffet, which operates during lunch and dinner. Adults can also use a blender and microwave if they need to cook special foods or warm them up for their little ones.

At any time, for a fee, you can rent a pram and even use the services of a nanny. True, this option is only possible for children from 4 years.

What vacationers say about the hotel

Of course, studying information about the hotel before the trip, one cannot but get acquainted with the impressions of tourists who have already visited Armas Labada Hotel. Reviews about this place make it clear that the hotel does not yet comply with the stated 5 *. Many argue that the rooms require repair and equipment upgrades, as, for example, they have old TVs. The air conditioning system in the rooms is central, so you cannot set the temperature yourself. Some point out that in public places it is rather dirty, even on the beach. And a lot of complaints concern paid Internet in the territory: its cost is high, but the signal level and speed leave much to be desired.





At the same time, of course, guests also find positive moments of relaxation at Armas Labada Hotel.
