Hotel Emir Fosse Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Alania for many Russians has become a place where you can have an excellent and relatively inexpensive vacation. This small resort town in southern Turkey is almost immersed in the green gardens of lemon and orange trees. A citrus aroma literally floats in the air. Alanya has become famous not only for its wonderful beaches and good service in hotels. Next to it are numerous monuments of cultural heritage. After all, Alanya, which lies at the foot of the Toros Mountains, has a very long history. Today, all tourists are told that it was this piece of the Mediterranean coast that Mark Antony presented to his beloved Cleopatra at one time. For the queen, he turned into that very favorite place where she came to rest.



general information

Today, tours to Alanya are breaking records for popularity. I must say that tourism brings the main profit to this resort city. And this is not at all surprising. This popularity of Alanya among the average traveler is understandable. Firstly, getting to the resort is simple and very quick, since the nearest airport is relatively close. Secondly, tours in this direction are considered inexpensive. Hotels in Alanya are much more affordable at prices than in other nearby resorts.

In addition, the resort offers all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay. In turn, the mild climate contributes to the fact that both elderly people and families with children come to Alanya. As for hotels, here the most popular are three-star hotels. I must say that in this resort, "star rating" is far from always the criterion by which tourists choose a place to stay. Most hotels in Alanya, belonging to the category of "3 *", offer a fairly wide range of services. One of such hotel complexes is Emir Fosse Beach Hotel 3 *.



Description and photo

The hotel is located two and a half kilometers from Alanya in a small village called Oba. The resort center is four kilometers away. It is separated from the sea by a freeway leading from Antalya to Mersin. The hotel is only fifty meters from the beach. An underground passage is provided for the safety of tourists through the highway. Emir Fosse Beach Hotel is considered an urban hotel. It was opened in 2003. It was reconstructed several times. The last major overhaul was carried out in 2015. The hotel area is small, however, despite its compactness, it is fully landscaped.

This mid-range hotel complex in recent years has become very popular among Russians. It is positioned by tour operators as a hotel, suitable both for a relaxing beach holiday, and for sightseeing. It is one six-story building, in which rooms are equipped. The administrative block is also located here. The nearest bus stop is just a hundred meters away, and the airport is one hundred and thirty kilometers away.



Infrastructure

Emir Fosse Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya) offers its guests free parking spaces, as well as rooms for the disabled. Its infrastructure includes a small business center with paid fax, laundry, and photographer services. On the territory there is a small souvenir kiosk and a shop where you can purchase basic necessities. The registration desk, open around the clock, at the request of arrivals carries out express registration, here you can also purchase a transfer in any direction and order a taxi. Upon arrival, tourists receive a printed list of all paid and free services provided at the hotel, as well as restaurant hours.

Concierge services are available at Emir Fosse Beach Hotel upon request, and a depository safe can be rented for a fee at the reception desk. Next to the registry, on the ground floor of the main building, there is a tour desk, there is also a car rental point.

There are many ATMs near the hotel. For the convenience of arrivals, especially those tourists who arrive ahead of time, a cozy lobby is provided.

Housing stock

The hospitable, almost homely atmosphere prevailing at the Emir Fosse Beach Hotel makes staying at this hotel quite comfortable for residents. According to its housing stock, it is considered average. It has a total of eighty-six standard rooms, two of which are equipped for those customers who cannot move independently. At the same time, the hotel can receive up to two hundred and thirty tourists. The maximum number of guests in each room is 2 + 1 people.



About rooms

The cozy atmosphere in the rooms, climate control provided by a split system, furnished balconies that open onto both the patio and the pool, round-the-clock quality service, as well as numerous types of services provided upon request - this list makes accommodation for tourists at Emir Fosse Beach Hotel (Alanya) as comfortable as possible.

The rooms have a standard set of furniture. There are non-slip tiles on the floor; doors to the balcony are closed with blackout curtains. The color scheme of the rooms has a relaxing holiday. A small built-in safe is available for a fee. The cost of rent is five dollars a day.

Bathrooms in this hotel are combined. All spanging equipment, judging by the reviews, is working properly. A daily replenishment of toiletries does not include shampoos and hair balms.

Services for children

There are always a lot of kids at Emir Fosse Beach Hotel 3 * (Alanya). Many families from Russia come here. And this is not surprising. Excursions

Tour operators who have arrived in Alanya are strongly advised not to lie all day in the sun by the sea, but be sure to see local attractions.
