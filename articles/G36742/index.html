Guam hotels: overview, service, reviews

It should be noted that Guam is an island that, thanks to its landscapes and views, will not leave anyone indifferent. On its territory there are a large number of hotels. There are cheaper options, but there are more expensive ones. However, they all correspond to their quality. Let's consider interesting options.

Wyndham garden guam

Cost per day: 5 thousand rubles.

The hotel is located near the center of Tamuning - just 20 minutes. Near the complex there is a bus stop, which makes it easy to get to any part of the city. Guam Hotel is 5 km from the airport.

The complex has 142 rooms. They have a balcony, kitchen, telephone, ironing facilities. The more expensive rooms offer beautiful views. The bathroom has a hairdryer and shower. Internet is free for all guests. There is no need to pay for parking. No pets allowed. If desired, you can order a transfer. The front desk is open 24/7. There is a gym, pool.

Days Inn Guam-Tamuning

Cost per day: from 4500 rub.

Many tourists are interested in holidays near the bays and the sea. On the map of hotels you can see that some inexpensive options are located right on the coast. It is about such a complex that will be discussed later. This Tamuninga hotel is just 10 minutes from the airport.

The rooms have cable and satellite TV, safe, toilet, ironing. The bathroom has a shower, hairdryer. Non-smoking rooms are available. At the local restaurant you can relax with a drink or a meal of any cuisine. Several cafes are directly a 5-minute walk from Guam Hotel.

There are only 46 rooms. Internet is free of charge. Reception is open around the clock. There is parking, a transfer booking service is available.

Sky garden

Cost per day: 11 thousand rubles.

The described hotel is located on the territory of Dededo. It offers air-conditioned rooms with free internet. The apartments have a dining area. TV set. The expensive rooms have a terrace or balcony. The kitchen is equipped with an oven, stove, fridge, toaster and coffee machine. The bathroom has either a shower or bathtub. Parking is free for guests.

The airport is 4 km from Sky Garden Hotel. The staff speaks Filipino, Korean, English. Pets are not allowed. There is no elevator, so you have to go upstairs to the upper floors.

Lotte Hotel Guam

Cost per day: about 17 thousand rubles.

The hotel is near Tumon Bay. On its territory there is also a swimming pool, fitness room, restaurant, shops, cafes. The rooms have free internet. The view from each room is amazing.

The complex is located just a 10-minute drive from the airport. Guests are allowed to visit the gym, where treadmills and exercise bikes are installed. You can come with children. A play area is equipped for them. The hotel has a pool for children.

The rooms are equipped with a refrigerator, safe, TV. The bathroom has slippers, a toothbrush and a paste. Tasty international dishes can be enjoyed at the restaurant, and drinks can be ordered at the bars. The staff at this Guam hotel speaks Filipino, Korean, Japanese and English. No pets allowed.

Sheraton Laguna Guam Resort

Cost per day: about 10 thousand rubles.

The hotel is located 10 minutes from the airport. Many residents are pleased that the complex is located on the coast of the Philippine Sea. On the map it will be easy to find the exact location of the hotel. The hotel has a fitness center, pool, restaurants, bars. Internet is free.

Guestrooms have televisions and CD players. A minibar and coffee maker are also included. The bathroom has a bathrobe and slippers. The expensive rooms have ocean views. It serves international cuisine, including American, Japanese and local. The rooms are serviced around the clock. Dry cleaning and laundry services are also available. The staff speaks Japanese, Korean, Filipino and English.

Onward beach resort

Cost per day: about 30 thousand rubles.

A wonderful hotel, which is located near the city of Hagatna (Guam). Located just 2 minutes from the beach. The complex has a restaurant, bar, spa, gym. Free internet is provided.

The water park has a swimming pool for both adults and children. You can ride water jets. The rooms have air conditioning, TVs. The balcony offers views of either the city or the ocean. At the local restaurants you can enjoy delicious Japanese and Italian cuisine.

This Guam hotel is located 10 minutes from the airport with breakfast. The nearest shopping center is 4 km away. The staff speaks Korean, Japanese, English. No pets allowed.

Hilton Guam Resort & Spa

Cost per day: about 14 thousand rubles.

Not all people who come to rest on Guam try to find a hotel near the Philippine Sea. It is clearly visible on the map: the Pacific Ocean is nearby. It is he who attracts many. The beach is just 6 minutes away. The complex has a tropical garden. Guests are invited to the gym, where you can play tennis, a water park with 5 pools and the Internet.

From the hotel to the airport - only 10 minutes. If you wish, you can order a transfer service, it is free. There is a special zone for children. There is a fitness center. Each air-conditioned room has a fridge, kettle and slippers.

No pets allowed. The staff speaks Korean, Japanese and English.

Holiday Resort & Spa Guam

Cost per day: about 10 thousand rubles.

Another good hotel in Guam. It is located just 1 minute from the beach. The complex has restaurants, a bar, spa and fitness center, pool. The rooms have a TV and a balcony.

The airport is just 10 minutes away. Famous shopping centers are nearby. The rooms have air conditioning, refrigerator. A safe is installed, there are dishes. The bathroom includes slippers and toiletries.

A play area for children is equipped. There are restaurants on site serving breakfast. You can purchase dishes of any cuisine, including Korean. Pets are not allowed. The staff speaks Japanese, Filipino and English.

Dusit thani guam resort

Cost per day: about 25 thousand rubles.

Guam has a large number of hotels, and all of them are popular. The described option is located just two minutes from the beach. The rooms have beautiful panoramic views of the bay. The hotel is surrounded by palm trees. On its territory there are restaurants, a fitness center, a pool.

The rooms are decorated in Thai style. They have air conditioning, TV. If you are interested in expensive options, then they are suites or a villa. There is also a kettle and hygiene products.

The on-site restaurants serve a variety of cuisines. If you wish, you can sign up for a massage or regain your strength in the hot tub. A special zone is open for children, it is possible to book a tour. There are 9 rooms where conferences and other events are held. The staff speaks Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Thai, Filipino, English.

The westin resort guam

Cost per day: about 20 thousand rubles.

This hotel is a three-minute walk from the beach. The complex has a fitness center, spa. Most of the rooms have a balcony, the view from it opens onto the pool or the ocean (depending on the cost).

The rooms are large rooms, have air conditioning, minibar. The bathroom has both a shower and a bathtub. Guests can go diving, fishing, and kayaking. In addition, there is a conference room. There is a Starbucks cafe next to the hotel. Car rental is allowed. A Japanese restaurant is located next to the hotel. It is cozy and has ocean views. There is also a bar nearby. A golf course is a 30-minute drive away. The airport is 6 km away. The staff speaks Russian, English, Chinese, Filipino, Japanese, Korean. Pets are allowed, but this should be discussed with the property in advance. A similar service may be charged.

Hyatt Regency Guam

Cost per day: about 20 thousand rubles.

Another hotel whose rooms have large rooms. It is located near a private beach. The complex has swimming pools, restaurants, a tennis court, a spa. Some balconies have ocean views.

Within a two-minute walk from the hotel there are bars, shops, restaurants. The airport is just 10 minutes away. The rooms have air conditioning, coffee machine, TV. The bathroom has an entire cosmetic kit.

Guests can enjoy various sports, including water sports. Internet and parking are free. The restaurants serve a variety of international dishes, including Japanese and Italian. Conference rooms are designed for 900 people. The staff speaks Japanese, Chinese, Korean, and English.

Summary

Judging by the reviews of vacationers, the above options can satisfy the desires of even the most demanding customers. You can book rooms online on the hotel's website or on specialized resources. You can come to some with animals. In order for visitors not to be bored, various entertainment events and excursions are organized.
