Ski resort of Georgia, Bakuriani: description, features and reviews of tourists

Everyone who once came to relax at the Georgian ski resort of Bakuriani, remains impressed for a long time and wants to return here again. Staying here is not only skiing. Every tourist here has the opportunity to enjoy the national color, to see the local attractions, and there are plenty of them, as well as to visit guests, because everyone knows that Georgians are the most hospitable people. Being in the mountains, you can spend hours admiring the beauty of mountain peaks, alpine meadows, breathing in the scents of pine forests, enjoying the clear mountain air.





Resort Overview

One of the most popular places for lovers of winter activities is the ski resort of Georgia Bakuriani. It is located in the mountains of the Caucasus on the slope of the Trialeti Range. Nearby, 37 km away is the famous resort of Borjomi, which has long been famous for its mineral springs. Bakuriani rises above sea level by 1700 m. Clean mountain air, the beauty of coniferous forests in the 19th century attracted members of the imperial family to these parts. The royal palace is still preserved here. Mountain serpentine - a narrow-gauge road connecting Borjomi and Bakuriani, it was built under the leadership of the French engineer Eiffel.

The ski resort attracts thousands of tourists with its many sunny days, the season here lasts a long time. In Soviet times, these places were simply a ‚ÄúMecca‚Äù for politicians. In the 30s, partisan A. Zhemchuzhnikov was one of the first to ski here, after which the whole elite came to rest here. Later, all the Soviet national teams of skiers trained at this base, the most famous athletes, Olympic champions, visited these places.

Nearby is a rare beauty Botanical Garden, which belongs to the Georgian Academy of Sciences. Recently, among the Europeans, ski resorts of Georgia are very popular, Bakuriani is one of such places. What attracts extreme people here? Freeride - downhill on unpaved, untucked slopes. At Tskhratskaro pass there is a track for cross-country skiing enthusiasts, there are three ski jumps, as well as a track for sledges.





Weather in Bakuriani

For all lovers of mountain recreation, skiing, the weather in the resort of Bakuriani is just perfect. The ski season begins here in November and lasts until March. Winter is snowy enough, but very mild. The ski resort of Georgia Bakuriani can always boast of an excess of sunlight, it is always bright and joyful here, tourists are accompanied by a great mood. But when going to the mountains, do not forget to wear sunglasses. Among locals, this place is called nothing more than the "Sun Valley". In mid-winter, in January, the average air temperature is only -7 degrees. There are practically no winds, so it seems that it is always warm here. Often you can find skiers riding swimsuits on the mountain slopes. Snow cover reaches 60 centimeters. Snowfalls are common here, but avalanches are extremely rare. Summer at the resort is quite hot. The average temperature in July reaches only +15 degrees.





Ski resort of Georgia Bakuriani. Scheme of trails and cable cars

The main area for skiing is the slopes of Mount Kohta. The difference in height is from 1800 to 2850 meters. The resort has two functional ski slopes. "Kohta-1" is designed for skilled skiers, professionals. The track has a length of 1.5 km, two steps. The first part of the route is 400 meters down at an angle of 52 degrees, the second step (red), more gentle, has a length of 1 km. The Kokhta-2 track (red and blue) is 3 km long, also has two stages, it has a variety of sections, both steep and gentle for easier skiing. For those who first went skiing in Georgia in winter, there is a more gentle Plateau track. Its length is only 300 meters, and the angle of inclination is 10 degrees. Ski tows operate here . The nearest Mount Didveli is being mastered in the resort, where the elevation is from 1780 m to 2670 m. There are black, blue, red tracks here. Lifts of both gondola and chair-type work. The luge track in the resort has a length of 1350 m.





Where to stay in Bakuriani?

Where is it better to stay for those who decide to visit the ski resorts of Georgia? Bakuriani, reviews of which all who have been here leave only enthusiastic, offers several comfortable hotels.

Bakuriani Hills . The hotel is located near Bakurian Park, designed for 45 people. Three-story cozy building. It includes all the amenities for a comfortable stay. There is a private bar, a dining room, secure parking, drying and ironing rooms. Friendly staff creates homeliness and warmth.

Premier Palace . Modern hotel for 225 visitors. It has an Asian, European bar, an Internet cafe, a night club, a children's room (with a nanny), table tennis, and billiards. A ski instructor works, ski equipment rental.

" Eureka . " The hotel has a prestigious location near the slopes. At the disposal of tourists 25 rooms of various classes. There is a winter garden, a bar, a conference room, a sauna, a pool, billiards, a gym, exercise machines, a jacuzzi. Tours are organized in Borjomi, to other historical places of the region.

According to tourists, the infrastructure here is not yet fully developed, but skiing itself leaves an amazing feeling. Nature, beauty, mountain clean air - all this is just breathtaking. Gudauri tracks

Each season, the ski resorts of Georgia, Bakuriani is either Gudauri or visited by thousands of tourists. It involves romance and a desire to get your dose of adrenaline. Gudauri tracks are special, have different lengths. They lie on the slopes of the mountains Sadzele, Kudebi. The average length is from 2 to 3 km. The length of the longest is 7 km.
