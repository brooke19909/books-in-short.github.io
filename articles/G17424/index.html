Park Inn Hotel (Kazan): address, description of rooms, infrastructure, features, photos and reviews

The four-star Park Inn Hotel (Kazan) is partly owned by the Radisson international line. Therefore, you have the right to expect from him the whole range of services that this network is famous for.

The hotel is located in the center of the capital of Tatarstan. The address of the Park Inn Hotel in Kazan is Lesgafta Street 7. This is a quiet place in the historical district.

The hotel belongs to the type of city, and at the same time it is removed from the noisy highways and busy streets. Reviews about her served as the source of our essay.

Let's get acquainted with what the hotel offers its guests. After all, this hotel is suitable for people who come on business, and a traveler in a hurry to get acquainted with the sights of the city.

How to get there

The hotel is 24 km from Kazan International Airport and three kilometers from the railway station. But if you came to this particular hotel, then you are not worried about how to get there.

If you have a car, you just go to the address and leave it in the parking lot. But what about those who come without a car? The Park Inn hotel (Kazan) provides such guests with a transfer both from the airport and from the station.

From the international hub to the hotel can be reached in about half an hour.

What is nearby

The area in which the Hotel Park Inn 4 * Hotel in Kazan is located is elite. Almost all of the city's historical sights are within walking distance.

The hotel is located half an hour walk from the Kazan Kremlin. Not too far away are the shopping centers, the Opera House and several good restaurants. The nearest metro station is on Tukaya Square, ten minutes walk.

But there is the opportunity to take another public transport. The bus stop is just 200 meters from the hotel exit. It is called "Volkova Street."

Walking can begin as early as a 10-minute walk from the hotel. There is a pedestrian Bauman street. Directly near the hotel is Chekhov Square.
