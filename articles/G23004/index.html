Stitch embroidery: patterns for beginners

Stitch embroidery is loved by needlewomen from small to large. Passion for it will allow you to show your creative abilities and have a good time. If you find simple patterns for embroidery, you can safely start work, and no matter how old you are.

Stitching can decorate clothes for adults and children, towels, curtains, pillowcases, napkins and tablecloths. Various accessories and interior items look much richer if they are decorated with embroidery. Postcards, small pictures - this is a cute gift that even a child can make with his own hands. An excellent task for children's creativity is embroidery stitch. Schemes for beginners are available on the Internet and magazines. Experienced embroiderers create whole pictures. Of course, such work requires time and painstaking work.

But to learn to embroider a stitch is easy, it takes only a little patience and knowledge of some secrets.

Distinctive features of stitch embroidery

Stitch is one of the needlewomen's favorite embroidery techniques; it allows you to create incredibly realistic compositions.

It consists of tightly fitting stitches that completely cover the fabric. The direction of the stitches can be different, the effect depends on this. Stitching with stitch is more difficult than cross-stitching, but this technique allows you to make elegant fragments and accurate transmission of details and color transitions. When embroidering satin stitch, it is customary to rely on patterns, but experienced craftswomen can create over time without using them.

Start with simple motives for beginners, and you will quickly learn this technique. Flowers are the favorite stitch embroidery of many. The patterns are the outline of the pattern, which is subsequently filled with stitches according to the instructions.





Tips for beginner embroiderers

Looking at the beautiful works of the craftswomen, the novice needlewoman wants to quickly learn how to create the same masterpieces. It is quite possible, but you need to follow the steps.

The basis of the basics is to learn how to sew different types of stitches and stitches. A great start is a simple stitch embroidery. Schemes for beginners are sometimes elementary, but they allow you to perfectly train on small details. Time will pass, and you will intuitively perform color transitions without even looking at the diagram.

For stitch embroidery, you will need the following materials:

  • base fabric;
  • hoop;
  • embroidery needle;
  • threads (usually a mouline thread);
  • special small scissors;
  • a thimble to protect your fingers.

How to choose a needle and thread for embroidery stitch

Almost any thread is suitable for stitch embroidery. Cotton and silk are used for delicate fabrics.





The most common stitch embroidery threads are mouline thread. Each thread (skein) consists of 6 thin threads, which are easy to separate and use separately. Floss manufacturers offer the widest palette - up to 400 shades.

Cotton fabric ‚ÄúIris‚Äù or wool looks good on dense fabrics, but the number of shades is very small. Sewing threads for embroidery are not used because of their strong twisting.

As for the selection of needles, there are no special requirements, you just need to rely on the thickness of the thread and fabric. A thinner base fabric will require embroidery in 1-2 threads of floss. Make sure that the eye of the needle is not too wide so that the fabric does not deform. Coarser fabrics are embroidered with full skein of floss or wool.

Numbers of needles for embroidery of thin fabrics - 1-3, for thick cotton - 4-8 number, for dense wool - 9-12.

Satin stitch rules

  1. Thick enough fabric (not knitwear). In case of embroidery on knitwear, use non-woven fabric or another sealant, which can then be trimmed. If the fabric is stretched well, the pattern will not deform.
  2. Ways to transfer the picture: carbon paper, tracing paper, special washable markers. You can use a thermal transfer pencil or a finished drawing, which is transferred to the base with an iron.
  3. Hoop the fabric carefully.
  4. Preferred threads are mouline thread or silk. Hue cards of well-known manufacturers - DMC, Madeira, Anchor - have hundreds of names and will allow you to choose and perform the most delicate transitions, especially if it is embroidery with a stitch of colors. You need to choose proven schemes, then your work will be incredibly realistic.
  5. The outline of the pattern should be hidden, so embroider a little beyond its borders.
  6. The stitches should close the material so that it is not visible.
  7. Different directions of stitches will allow you to achieve interesting color effects.

Where to look for stitch embroidery patterns for beginners

If you decide to learn satin stitch embroidery on your own and bought everything you need, you can easily find many patterns in needlework books. Step-by-step workshops describe in detail how to do the work. Stitch patterns for embroidery - flowers - have remained the most popular for a long time. You can find an option for every taste.



Many books for beginner needlewomen are devoted to this kind of creativity, such as stitch embroidery. The schemes are beautifully illustrated, so it is very convenient to work with them, especially if you are outside the house.

Also on sale you can find ready-made kits for beginners, they already have threads of the right colors, fixed on the organizer, a detailed diagram, fabric, needle.

Simple stitch embroidery technology

In a simple smooth fashion, the stitches are parallel to each other, they tightly fill the part, while the edges of the pattern are even. One of the types of needlework that requires special care is stitch embroidery. The schemes look simple, but the implementation of motives requires accuracy.





The simple surface is double-sided, so the face and the inside of the product look the same. It looks very beautiful and allows you to use this technique everywhere. The ends of the thread are hidden under the main fabric of stitches, but knots are not made.

The thread is fastened as follows. Inside the element, you need to make several stitches ‚Äúforward needle‚Äù, then stretch the thread so that the end remains on the surface of the pattern. Next, the embroidery stitches will close the end of the thread, and it will not be visible.

Simple patterns for embroidery: flowers

After the working thread is fixed, the main pattern is sewn with tight stitches, exactly along the line of the pattern. This requires accuracy and patience.

The stitches can overlap at different angles, but always parallel to each other. Watch the thread tension.

Floral motifs - a very popular stitch embroidery. Schemes are very diverse, of different levels of complexity.

For example, you can embroider a simple flower using colored threads.





Getting started is the center of the flower. We use a simple smooth surface, in the same way we execute leaves. The antennae are embroidered with a stitch seam. As a result, you get a beautiful smooth double-sided pattern.

Clothes decoration

The most popular motif for decorating clothes is stitch embroidery of flowers. Schemes may be the simplest, but the result is simply amazing. Folk costumes have long been decorated with rich embroidery. Popular stitch embroidery patterns on clothes will allow you to turn modern things into trendy in just a couple of hours.

For example, we will decorate a simple T-shirt with embroidery, we will use a floral motif.

We will need:

  • white t-shirt ;
  • pattern for embroidery stitch;
  • floss;
  • small plastic hoops;
  • washable marker for translation;
  • needle;
  • hoop.

We transfer the circuit by the method of "transparency" using a marker. We fasten the fabric in the hoop and proceed to embroidery.

First we embroider the branches, seam ‚Äúforward the needle‚Äù.

The contours of the leaves go around the stalk-like seam, then fill them with a simple smooth surface.

We also embroider flowers with a simple stitch according to the pattern.

When the entire hoop pattern is completed, move them to a new place.

And here is the result: a fashionable embroidered T-shirt.



Clothing decorated with embroidery has a special character, because it is unique. This is a wonderful gift for loved ones or for your beloved.

Thus, stitch embroidery is a wonderful needlework, which will give you many pleasant minutes.
