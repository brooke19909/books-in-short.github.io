Varadero (Cuba) hotels - what they are

Going on vacation? At your service are the hospitable hotels of Varadero, Cuba in all its glory and unlimited possibilities! Hotels of the legendary resort will welcome everyone, so solving the issue of living on the island is quite simple. There you can not only have a good rest, but also hold confidential meetings, conferences, negotiations at a decent level. And also arrange a romantic date and even meet your love. It seems that the hotels of Varadero (Cuba) are simply created for this. Every minute you can occupy yourself with something, and when you want peace, just watch the local natural beauties while drinking a delicious cocktail.



Like the whole of sunny Cuba, Varadero, whose hotels are ideal for a comfortable stay, especially boasts some of them. For example, the Melia Hotel stands out. From the airport you can get here in about five minutes. For family vacations, perhaps, there is no better place. Four restaurants, as well as cafes and five bars (including a karaoke club) are successfully operating on the vast territory. That is why there are no problems with food at all. Local chefs will offer you international and Chinese cuisines. There is a separate restaurant with a buffet in order to ensure that guests are as satisfied as possible and always full.





Families with children will no doubt be happy with their visit to Melia Varadero, because here every member of the family will find an interesting activity for themselves. Adults will love the golf course, jacuzzi, massage. Children will be offered to swim in a small pool, watch interesting animated performances, as well as spend time in a house with toys (for children up to five years old) and slot machines (for children from seven to twelve years old).

Hotels of Varadero (Cuba) can offer a good rest for young people. So, the Beaches hotel is ready to provide more than 350 rooms. At the same time, you can choose the standard option or a comfortable three-story villa for your stay. Just like in Melia Varadero, meals are served on an all-inclusive basis. However, Beaches will not accommodate guests who are less than 16 years old, because the conditions of stay are designed for adults. The hotel has a lot of active entertainment. You can enjoy a professional massage, learn how to swim in a diving club, increase muscle tone in a fitness center or take an interesting excursion. In demand are riding water bicycles, bananas, windsurfing, kayaks and much more.





Varadero (Cuba) hotels also offer spacious meeting rooms for business meetings. Among them is the Iberostar hotel. It is here that 5 modern halls function, each of which is equipped with new audiovisual equipment. The smallest room is designed for 28 people, and the largest - for 442 people. After the meetings, participants can relax in the local restaurants or cozy bars.

On the island of Cuba, Varadero hotels have their own private beaches, strewn with gray or white sand. Small pubs are comfortably located on their territory, deck chairs and umbrellas are provided for free.

Given all of the above, we assert boldly: among the various cities of the island of Varadero is the best and is popular among tourists from all over the world.
