rose flower

How to make a flower from sausage balls so that it looks like a rose? At first glance, this venture is very difficult, but there is nothing complicated in it. For such a lovely flower, three balls are needed - one for the stem and two for the bud. They begin to create like this: a sausage for a flower is inflated, made of its small length and form a core from it. To do this, twist the ball in two places to get a circle in the center and two barrels at the edges. After the kegs are twisted together. The second balloon is inflated to its full length and tied into a knot around the finished element, wrapping the free ends in a loop until they become short, after they are connected at the bottom of the bud. The final stage is the formation of the stem. For him, at the inflated ball, two rings are made from one edge, twisting them together, and the flower head is attached to them.



Chamomile

How to make a flower from sausage balls so that it looks like a camomile? To begin with, a green ball is inflated, twisted at the top, forming a core. After proceeding to the petals, the ball is visually divided into 5-6 parts, of which the loops are then twisted and connected in the middle. After they start the core.

Tulip

Spring flowers from balls look very original and may well become a gift by March 8 for a teacher, girlfriend or mother. To make a tulip, you need only one long ball, which is inflated, expelled into the nose and tightly tied, forming a bloated bud. After slightly inflating the main part of the ball, which will act as a stem.
