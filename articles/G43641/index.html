Game progress

Having learned the purpose of the game, you can begin to find out in more detail how to play Uno, the rules of which, by the way, are quite simple. Next, one card from the deferred deck is turned face up and placed right next to it, so that cards are put on it in the end. After that, the first player can begin to walk from any card, for example, "Green Two." Uno Spin Rules

When the creators of the Uno game noticed that their game was already boring to the players, they decided to slightly diversify and supplement it, so the Uno Spin desktop appeared, which instantly got a bunch of fans. This game has all the same rules as the classic Uno, however it is also supplemented by a small drum with an arrow and nine sectors on it, which each player spins before making his move. And depending on which picture the arrow points to, the player, or even all players without exception, is obliged to complete one or another task. Perhaps the player will have to draw cards from the deck until he finds the card of the desired color, or, conversely, he will be able to fold all cards of the same face value, or maybe he will have to fight with other players to lay out the largest card. In any case, all these tasks are extremely exciting, so playing this type of board game Uno will be even more interesting.

Uno seven-zero

Even more exciting will be the Uno game "seven-zero", the rules of which, of course, fully coincide with the classic version of this board game. If a player lays out a card with a face value of 7, then he can exchange his cards with any other player at the table at his discretion.
