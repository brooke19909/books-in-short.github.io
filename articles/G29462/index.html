The beach in Krasnoyarsk. Where to relax in the summer?

Summer in Siberia is very short, which is why every warm day I want to spend on the shore of a reservoir: plunge into the cool water, soak up the sun in the bright rays of sand. Local residents of Krasnoyarsk and city guests enjoy spending time on the beaches. There are several bodies of water in this region that are suitable for swimming. However, there are those on which beach vacations are prohibited.

The opening date of the summer season can be considered June 10-20. Although the water does not warm up much, the inhabitants of the Siberian region are already trying to spend time on ponds. Locals, of course, know where the beaches in Krasnoyarsk are the best. But city guests quickly find it quite difficult to navigate. Let's look at the most popular holiday destinations in this region.

Emelyanovo

An excellent summer vacation will give Lake Semiradsky. The beach in Emelyanovo (Krasnoyarsk) is paid, the entrance is 300 rubles. Also have to pay for sunbeds. If you arrive early in the morning, you can take free places. A football and volleyball field are equipped on site. There is a special parking place for 2500 seats. Barkhatovo

The beach in Barkhatovo (Krasnoyarsk) is located on the lake. Locals call it Velvet. It works from 8:00 to 23:00. The beach area is equipped, there are platforms for active games. There are also attractions. Those who wish can ride a catamaran, a jet ski, a banana. There are changing cabins and toilets on the beach. Those who walk into the territory on foot need to pay 100 rubles. Entrance by car - 300 rubles., Regardless of the number of people in the cabin. Children who are under the age of 12 are free. You can often see tent camps in the beach area. There is also a vip zone. It is equipped with comfortable houses with a kitchen.



Beach complex "Mirage"

In the Beaver Log park, the Mirage complex is located. This beach in Krasnoyarsk is located not by the pond, but by the pool. For the convenience of visitors, sun loungers are located on three-tier terraces. Pool water is heated. A small shop and cafe are located on site. For adults, one day of rest will cost 800-850 rubles. depending on the day of the week, for children aged 7 to 14 years will have to pay about 600 rubles. Preschool children visit the beach for free, but they are not provided with a sunbed.



Ecopark "Admiral"

Not everyone prefers the beach in Krasnoyarsk in the open water, so you can go to Divnogorsk. Here is the Admiral Ecopark. It is located in Shumikha Bay (Krasnoyarsk Sea). Here, vacationers can not only sunbathe on the beach, but also take a walk in the coniferous forest. All tourists will appreciate the cleanest fragrant air. Bicycles and baby cars are available for hire on site at an additional cost. There are also many water attractions, which include water skiing and a boat trip.

The park has a sauna, a bathhouse, barbecue facilities, a billiard room and table tennis. In order to relax in this beautiful place, you need to pay. An adult ticket costs 800 rubles, a child ticket costs 500 rubles. For this money, showers, towels, sun loungers and dressing rooms are provided. Also, two swimming pools are equipped on the territory (adult and children's), swimming in it is included in the ticket price.

Tatyshev Island

The most popular beach in Krasnoyarsk is located on the island of Tatyshev. To explain this is quite simple - admission here is free. However, recently there has been hung a sign "Swimming is prohibited." However, this does not stop the Siberians. That is why the city beach still opens on June 20.
