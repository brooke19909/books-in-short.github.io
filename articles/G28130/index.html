San Marin Hotel 4. Description, reviews

San Marin Hotel is perfect for parents with children, and for youth companies. The hotel focuses on customers who prefer a decent level of service at reasonable prices.

Rooms: The hotel has 159 rooms, of which 118 are standard rooms and 41 are family apartments with 2 bedrooms and a living room with sofas.

The rooms have double or single beds with the possibility of a baby cot. Spacious furnished balconies are a great place to dine or relax. The apartments come with ceramic flooring, air conditioning and several Russian TV channels. You can use the electronic safe for a fee.

The room has an empty minibar, which at the request of the guests will be filled for a surcharge.

Meals: San Marin Hotel 4 offers an all-inclusive service. Guests are invited to the self-service restaurant, which consists of an indoor hall and an open terrace with a total capacity of up to 700 people. They serve European cuisine, signature treats from Turkish cooks, local spirits and soft drinks.

The hotel has three bars, where there is always tea, coffee, soft drinks, pastries, desserts, snacks, fruits.

Beach: You need to walk less than a hundred meters to the sandy beach with a gentle entrance to the sea. On the shore there is all the necessary beach equipment.

Additional information: San Marin Hotel 4 guarantees customers a carefree holiday, providing domestic, transport, entertainment and business services.



The hotel has parking, car rental, laundry, medical office. Presentations and business meetings are held in a modern conference room for 125 people. Wi-Fi is available throughout. The fitness center offers gym classes, beauty treatments and massages. You can visit the Turkish bath or sauna.

San Marin Hotel 4 has a small area with a pool and water slides. In the afternoon, vacationers spend time by the pool or on the beach, where they do aerobics, play volleyball and water polo. In the evening, guests will enjoy musical performances and quizzes.

For children, there is a playground, a small compartment in the pool and a mini club where training classes are held.



Digest: A good vacation at a reasonable price is offered by the San Marin Hotel 4. Hotel reviews are numerous and varied, but most tourists note changes for the better after the repair.

According to the stories of vacationers, guests are accommodated in simple, modern, cleanly furnished rooms with a view of the green courtyard with a swimming pool. Daily replenish supplies of detergents and give fresh towels.

Responsive staff pays attention to each guest and never refuses help.

The food is plentiful and varied. A particularly large selection of dishes is offered for lunch and dinner. Every day they serve fish, various types of meat, fruits, sweets, pastries and drinks without restrictions.
