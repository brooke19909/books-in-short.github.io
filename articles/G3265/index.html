Learning to hem pants correctly

Surely everyone came across a situation where a thing just bought needs a little correction. For example, you need to sew something, or to hem pants. But sometimes there is not enough time to carry the new thing in the studio. If you have a sewing machine at home, then do it yourself is not at all difficult. For work you will need a trouser braid, ruler and a piece of chalk.

Classic men's pants: how to hem



This model of trousers has its own characteristics. In it, the braid can be sewn only on the back half of the product. This is necessary so that the underlying tissue does not wear out.

First, a man must wear trousers to determine the exact length. You can try on in shoes, then the lower edge of the leg should be located between the heel and the heel. If you measure the length without shoes, then the border of the hem should be five millimeters above the floor. Do not forget to make a mark with chalk or pin the leg with pins.

Mark the desired length

Before hemming trousers, it is necessary to make precise marking. To do this, lay them on a flat surface. Draw two lines with soap or chalk. Do this with a ruler. The first is the cut line, the second is the finished trousers length. The margin for hem should be approximately five centimeters. The thickness of the fabric is also taken into account. The thinner it is, the less margin needs to be left. On the bottom line, cut off the excess fabric and use the overlock to process the bottom edge. The same can be done using the zigzag. Sew trouser braid two millimeters above the fold line.



You should know that a new trouser tape must be soaked in hot water, let it cool, then steam it well. It is impossible to hem the trousers with unprocessed new braid - during the washing of the garment it can shrink and tighten the bottom of the trousers. Fold the fabric so that two millimeters of tape remain on the front side. This is necessary so that the pants do not wear out ahead of time. You can use duct tape, laying it with the main fabric and hem, then ironing with steam. True, this method has a significant drawback - the tape may peel off during the washing process. Therefore, it is safer just to hem the trousers by hand with a thin needle with a thin thread.





Shutdown

When the main work is finished, without removing the budget, iron both trousers, then remove the excess thread and iron the bottom of the trousers again.

Important details
