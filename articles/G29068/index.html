Bank cells in Sberbank: conclusion of a lease agreement, advantages and disadvantages, user reviews

Most people living in Russia are tormented by two questions: "how to make a lot of money" and "where to store them later." Some still at their own risk continue to keep their savings at home. But in the modern world there are a large number of options for storing funds, and one of them is the use of a bank cell. This service has long been used in the process of buying and selling real estate and for the duration of a long trip. Next, you will learn all about bank cells from Sberbank.

All about bank cells

Bank safes are standard metal boxes stored in financial institutions. The dimensions of the provided cell are different, depending on the needs of the client. They are located in specially equipped rooms, most often basements. Safe rooms are securely guarded with several armored doors and alarms.

The security service of a financial institution imposes very strict requirements on citizens who wish to find themselves in a vault. These conditions apply not only to customers who have rented a cell, but also to bank employees. The latter must obtain special permission for this and present it upon request. Visitors are allowed to visit the safe depository an unlimited number of times, but at any visit, the client must present a personal passport and put a mark in the visit log.

There are two types of safes: with two sets of keys (one is stored at the client, the second at the bank branch) or with a combination lock (the character set is known only to the tenant). When choosing the second one you will have to pay a lot more, but his level of protection is much higher.

Lease agreement

Between the tenant and the financial institution, two agreements are concluded: for the rental of a safe and for storage, in order to fully regulate all parties to the contract. The main points of the document are:

In addition, the text of the agreement spells out all the rights and obligations of the parties. The main ones are the absolute liability of the banking institution and the need for the tenant to follow all the conditions for making payment and access to content.

The agreement guarantees confidentiality of contents for other employees of the institution who are not authorized to participate in the transaction.
