Guest house "Elena" (Adler, state farm "Russia"): description, services, reviews

An affordable and comfortable option for summer holidays is the guest house "Elena". Adler is a great place to spend a vacation with family or friends. Here you will have the opportunity to enjoy the warm sea, beautiful nature and quality service.

Guest house "Elena" (Adler, state farm "Russia") - short description

The Black Sea coast attracts thousands of tourists, because there are so many opportunities for entertainment. If you do not like the hustle and bustle, go to the guest house "Elena". Adler will give you the sea and beautiful nature, and this hotel - comfort and quality service. In addition, it is worth noting the incredibly convenient location.

The hotel consists of two new buildings. The two-story building was commissioned in 2008. It has 8 comfortable rooms. The second building, built in 2011, offers guests 22 comfortable rooms.

The hotel area is closed, and therefore you can not worry about the presence of strangers. There is a small courtyard with a garden, as well as private parking. Guest house "Elena" (Adler) is located in the private sector, and therefore the noise of roads and nightclubs will not interfere with your vacation.





Hotel address

The sufficiently favorable location is characterized by the guest house "Elena". Adler, Field, 17 - the address of the hotel. It is very close to the public beach (3-5 minutes). Nearby are many shops, cafes and restaurants, and the road to a large market takes about 5 minutes. This is the central part of the city with numerous parks and walking alleys. Adler Train Station is 11 kilometers from the hotel. To get to Sochi International Airport, you have to overcome about 13 km. The nearest attractions are as follows:

  • Fisht Stadium;
  • Iceberg Palace;
  • Olympic Park.




Hotel Rooms

If you plan to spend your vacation at sea, go to the guest house "Elena". Adler is a popular tourist destination and all travelers need comfortable living conditions. The following room categories are available for accommodation:

  • Double standard room is a small room. Guests can sit on a huge French bed or two twin. Due to the fact that the interior is decorated in bright colors, the room seems more spacious. The balcony has chairs and a tea table in the fresh air.
  • Superior standard room has an impressive area, so triple accommodation is possible. The room has a large bed, as well as a soft folding chair. This room has access to a furnished balcony.
  • The family room can accommodate four guests. To do this, there is a double bed, as well as a large folding sofa. The room also has a full-fledged workplace. There is access to a private balcony with garden furniture and a tumble dryer.




Apartment Amenities

The guest rooms at the hotel include the following amenities:

  • thanks to the air conditioner, you can set a comfortable temperature in the room;
  • a wide-screen TV will fill your leisure time watching several dozens of satellite channels;
  • the safe is responsible for the safety of your valuables and documents;
  • An electric kettle will allow you to make a hot drink at any time.

It is worth noting that all guests can use the shared kitchen, barbecue, washing machine, as well as free wireless Internet.

Opportunities for children

For the full relaxation of young tourists in this hotel the following services are available:

  • baby cots or playpens may be provided;
  • in the evenings, cartoons will be watched for kids;
  • the shared kitchen has special high chairs for children;
  • in the courtyard there is a playground with swings, slides and a sandbox;
  • there is a large children's library.

Rules of residence

The following rules apply in the guest house:

  • Children under 4 years old stay free of charge if they can stay in existing beds;
  • this hotel does not have terminals for payment by credit card;
  • guests who book a room are required to make an advance payment within 5 days from the date of application;
  • if you plan to relax in the hotel for more than 5 days, then as a bonus you will be provided with a free transfer to Adler station or to the airport.
