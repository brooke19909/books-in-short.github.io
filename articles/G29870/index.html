How to crochet a pillow. Crocheted Pillows for Beginners

At all times, do-it-yourself things not only delighted others, but were also an exquisite and stylish decoration. This category also includes various crocheted knitted pillows . Couch, nursery, large and small - this is a very convenient accessory for the home. It can be warm and fluffy options, plain lace or made from the remains of multi-colored yarn. And children's pillow-toys, knitted with a hook, will please their little owners for a long time, protecting their sleep. And in this article you will learn how to crochet a pillow quickly, beautifully and easily.

Crocheted sofa cushion made from leftover yarn

By tying this pillow option, you will kill two birds with one stone. Bring bright notes into the interior of the room and get rid of a large number of multi-colored glomeruli, which are a pity to throw out, but it is not always possible to find a worthy use for them.



So, as it has already become clear, for the manufacture of a sofa cushion you will need the remains of multi-colored yarn and a hook. Design will depend only on your imagination and skill. Such pillows are knitted from individual elements. They can be square, rectangular or in the form of hexagons. Size may also vary. If you are an experienced needlewoman, you can come up with your own flow chart. Alternating various shades, you will get bright and eye-catching parts of the future pillow. Do not forget that you can link a lot of small elements or several large ones. For example, the photo shows examples of both crocheted pillows. It is very easy to figure out the schemes, even looking at the photo. You need to start knitting from the middle with 5 air loops connected in a ring. And then we alternate three double crochet and 3 air loops. In the next row, in the corners of the element, you already need to tie six columns with a crochet, separated by air loops. And on the sides of the square, alternating staggered air loops and 3 double crochets. This is the easiest option. Please note that you can crochet a pillow as a bright gift for any holiday.



After the required number of parts is connected, you can proceed to the assembly. You can connect all the pillow elements together using a hook or thread with a needle. The back of the pillow can be made in the same technique as the front, or have a plain surface connected with columns with a crochet or without. In any case, the pillow, tied with one's own hand, will turn out to be unique and your loved ones will surely like it.

The way to make a round pillow

Knitting of such a pillow begins with 4 air loops connected in a ring. Further knitting continues in a circle. The main thing is to make uniform increases (two double crochets in one loop), so that until the desired size is reached, the canvas is smooth and does not bend. After you have connected the circle of the required size, the increments are not made. Knitting continues without adding, and with each round it will increasingly look like a pillow. In this way, knitting continues until the pillow height of your choice is obtained. Next, we again knit a smooth round surface, only now it is necessary to carry out the reduction (skipping the loops in the bottom row). The photo shows how the knitting of such a pillow ends. For its internal filling, foam rubber or other material, for example, more dense, can be used. In this case, you get an excellent floor pillow that your children will like. And having knitted several in different colors, you will get stylish accessories for the children's room. Agree, crocheted crocheted pillows will add to the children's room coziness and warmth.
