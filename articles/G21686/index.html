Hotel President 4 * (Mallorca, Spain): photos, reviews

Everyone at least once should go on vacation abroad. Many have legal holidays for the summer, so people go to the seaside resort. And a very popular option is Spain. Namely Mallorca. This resort has a huge number of excellent hotels. And one of those is President 4 *. Majorca receives tens of thousands of tourists every year, and many decide to stay there. Well, therefore, it is necessary to tell in more detail about what the hotel offers its guests.

Location

In a very good location is the hotel HSM President 4 *. Mallorca is Spain's largest island by area. There is a picturesque and beautiful village called Puerto de Alcudia. It is located in the northern part of the island. This is where the hotel is located. It is only 15 meters from the beach. The hotel accepts guests from May to October.





Near the building there is a picturesque forest, and literally a couple of meters away - a small bay. The village center is a 10-minute walk away. It takes about an hour to reach Palma (a major tourist center and the main port of the Balearic Islands). The airport is far away - 70 kilometers from the village. But then the planes do not interfere with their takeoffs and landings.

There is a bus stop next to the hotel, so you can leave almost anywhere.

Service

Now in more detail about what services are offered in President 4 * (Mallorca). Wi-Fi in public areas is free, and you need to pay extra for in-room internet. An office building with fax and photocopy machines, as well as dry cleaning and car rental are located in the hotel building. True, there are no parking lots in the complex.

The registration desk here works round the clock, so check-in can be carried out not at 14:00, as according to the rules, but earlier. But this must be negotiated. The administration will meet the guests if, by the time they arrive, the room will be free from previous guests, and the staff will have time to prepare it and put it in order.

If people gather somewhere for the whole day (for an excursion, for example), they can go to the reception the day before and order lunches with them. By the way, the staff speaks not only Spanish, but also German and English. It is worth taking a phrase book with you to better explain if you have problems with languages.





Entertainment

The main occupation of people staying at a resort in Spain is a beach vacation and swimming in the sea. However, in President 4 * (Mallorca) there are several more ways to brighten up your leisure time. Naturally, there is an outdoor swimming pool and a sun terrace nearby. Lovers of an active lifestyle will appreciate the presence of a tennis court, as well as the opportunity to play futsal. A golf course is a 5-minute walk away. And here, near the pool, various entertainment programs are held in the evening.

The hotel also has a billiard room, table tennis, the opportunity to play volleyball, as well as rent a bicycle. Those who wish can go diving, however, this is at an additional cost.

Also in HSM President 4 * (Mallorca) there is a separate SPA-center. Many people like to spend their free time here. SPA has a gym, massage room, sauna, bath, hammam and jacuzzi. Customers also have the opportunity to do aerobics. Immediately spend contrasting and therapeutic souls.

Nutrition

President 4 * (Spain, Mallorca) has a bar and restaurant. Guests are served buffet style at the latest establishment. Themed dinners and cooking shows are also held periodically.

There is diversity, and indeed it is. Every day, several types of meat, fish, seafood, squid, mussels. Breakfast is also hearty and delicious. There are hot dishes - these are pancakes, scrambled eggs with bacon, scrambled eggs, jams, cereals, various pastries and cold cuts. In the morning they even serve champagne, which, in principle, is the norm for these places.

The restaurant also serves a variety of fresh fruits, original desserts, french fries, spaghetti, juices, wine, ice cream. Any cocktail can be ordered at the bar, but tourists recommend sangria. This is a drink made from wine, in which sugar, pieces of fruit and berries, as well as some mineral water, spices and brandy are added.





Standard 2-bed rooms

President Majorca 4 * (Spain, Mallorca) has apartments of various categories. However, it is worth telling more about each of them.

The most budget option (and, accordingly, the most popular) is the 2-seater standards, whose area is 22 square meters. m. Inside there are two single beds, which are shifted tightly to each other, so it seems that this is one bed. The interior is pleasant, the floor is parquet, there is nothing superfluous inside. There is a balcony, plasma TV, powerful air conditioning, dressing room. Naturally, the room has a plumbing unit with a bath, toilet and toiletries. A fridge, hairdryer and telephone with safe are also included.

7 days with breakfast will cost two guests about 42,000 rubles. If you order the all-inclusive service, you will have to pay about 63 thousand rubles. This is a better option. The restaurant and bar serves good, quality food and drinks. And besides, few people want to look for a cafe in the vicinity of the hot sun, where they could have a bite. And so everything is nearby.

Improved options

The HSM President 4 * (Mallorca, Alcudia) has an even more expensive 2-bed apartment. Conditions - all the same, only from the furnished balcony guests enjoy amazing sea views. For this you need to overpay. A week for two with the all-inclusive service will cost about 70,000 rubles.

There are also apartments for 2 adults and one child up to 12 years old. In style they resemble rooms of the very first category. But inside there are three single beds. This option with the all-inclusive service will cost 80,000 rubles per week.

And of course, there are economy rooms for three adults. Their price is 90,000 rubles.





Junior Suite

Apartments of this category in HSM President 4 * (Spain, Mallorca, Alcudia) are more spacious than all of the above. Their area is 30 square meters. m. Inside there are two single beds and one sofa bed. A room for two adults and one child is calculated.

These apartments are divided into zones. There is a bedroom and a living room, which are separated by a sliding compartment door. There is a balcony, as well as a bathroom. Everything that was listed earlier is also available to people staying in a junior suite. 95 000 rubles - this is the price of these apartments with the all-inclusive service for 7 days.

There is also such a room for three adults. Inside, everything is the same. And the balcony offers views of the mesmerizing seascape. However, for three adult guests the apartments will cost more. With the same conditions, you will have to pay about 110,000 rubles.

By the way, you can book a room with a cancellation service. However, it will be more expensive. The plus is that in case of change of plans for canceling the reservation, a fine will not be charged, as it would be in a normal situation. About staying at the hotel

In HMS President Hotel 4 * (Mallorca) you can stay with children of any age. If the baby is not two years old, then specially for him they will put a cot in the room, and it will be free. Need an extra bed for someone older? Then you will have to pay 50% of the total cost of living for this service. But if the child is younger than 12. Do guests have an older child? Or did a third older guest come with them? In these cases, it will be necessary to pay 85% of the total cost of living per person.

If an extra bed or crib is needed, you must inform the property in advance. The administration must send a response to the guests.

By the way, only persons over 16 years old are allowed into the SPA center, which the President 4 * hotel has (Mallorca). And you also need to bring a bathing cap and slippers with you.

Hotel guests

HSM President Hotel 4 * (Mallorca) gets a variety of reviews . But most of them are positive. First of all, tourists talk about the location. Who should go

And finally, a few words about who the President Majorca 4 * hotel is intended for (Spain, Mallorca). If you believe the reviews and the description, then this is a hotel for young people, single tourists and those who want to spend their long-awaited vacation in a quiet place. Compared to other noisy resorts, where dozens or even hundreds of thousands of tourists come every year, it is really quite quiet here.

By the way, there are few Russians here, which plays into the hands of people who want a break from their home and everything that is there. The main contingent is visitors from Germany and the Poles.

In general, if you want to have a good time, enjoying a sea holiday and good food, you should go here.
