Holidays in Germany cannot be forgotten

Germany is located in the central part of Western Europe. In the South, Germany is surrounded by the Alps, and from the North it is washed by the North and Baltic Seas. Every year, thousands of tourists come to this country on vacation.

The tourist season in Germany lasts all year round. Here everyone can find something to their liking: you can get acquainted with local traditions and customs, as well as national cuisine, visit museums, admire magnificent alpine landscapes, ancient castles or engage in various active sports. Perhaps that is why for every person who has visited this large and interesting country, a vacation in Germany becomes a real fairy tale.
Germany consists of sixteen lands having not only a diverse landscape, but also its history, culture and national cuisine. In Germany there are many ancient monuments: churches, monasteries, palaces and castles. Holidays in Germany are an opportunity to enjoy the beauties of this amazing country, to get to know its culture and history more closely.

Going on vacation to this developed European country, you can choose the most suitable vacation plan for yourself, and by purchasing last-minute trips to Germany, you can save money and spend it, for example, on shopping or on additional one-day excursions to Brussels or Amsterdam. Having bought last-minute tours to Germany to Berlin, you can visit the show in the famous theater, which is located on Potsdamer Platz, the thermal complex Crystal or the SPA center Liquidrom.

Holidays in Germany must be connected with a visit to medieval cities surrounded by stone fortified walls, wandering around narrow cobbled streets. In Dusseldorf and Cologne, tourists get acquainted with the famous Rhine castles. Holidays in Germany are perfectly combined with a cultural and entertainment program. Beer lovers can take an active part in the traditional Oktoberfest beer festival, which is held annually in Munich. A huge number of beers are sold at the festival , most of which can be tasted here and nowhere else.

This is explained by the fact that each region of Germany, with rare exceptions, produces dozens of varieties of this popular drink. Munich is considered not only the capital of Bavaria, but also the beer capital of Germany. However, not only beer is famous for this city. What are walks in the night city or the famous pinakothek, which are held here!

Germany is also a great place to improve their health, which has been pretty shaky for a busy working year. On the territory of the country there are famous balneological resorts, motels and pensions with friendly and experienced staff in small and clean resort towns famous for clean air.

All hospitals are equipped with the latest technology. Rest and treatment in boarding houses and sanatoriums are very diverse and complemented by extensive entertainment and sports programs. Germany also has great opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts. Surfing, swimming pools, golf, tennis courts and kayaks - everything makes a vacation in Germany unforgettable.

Every tourist who once visited this developed European country wants to return here again.
