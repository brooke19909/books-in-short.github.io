How to get to Lake Borovoe (Noginsky district)?

The Moscow region is densely dotted with rivers (about 2 thousand), lakes (up to 350), swamps (about 5% of the entire territory). All lakes of the region account for 7.8 thousand hectares.

Lake Borovoe

In the eastern part of the Moscow region, in the Noginsk district there is a large network of lakes that are contemporaries of the glaciation era. Today, they are all at varying degrees of overgrowing.

Lake Borovoye in the Noginsk region is surrounded by forests, in which there are many berries and mushrooms. The pond itself "sheltered" many species of fish.

The water in the reservoir is clean. According to its hydrochemical composition, it is one of the cleanest in the region. The lake belongs to the basin of the Timkov Lake Group. The pond is located in the valley of the river Sherny.

On the south coast are summer cottages, in the northeast - the village of Karavaevo. The northern part is surrounded by a dense mixed forest.

Today, Lake Borovoye in the Noginsky district is leased by the Ministry of Defense to serve the recreation area.





Geography and how to get

The lake is located 9 kilometers from the village of Noginsk, on the east side. On the south side, at a distance of 2 km, is the Gorky highway. Near the reservoir there is a settlement Bunkovskoe, the village of Karavaevo. The Big Moscow Ring is located 14 km from the north-eastern side , and 10 km to the west in the small direction.

How to get to Lake Borovoe, Noginsk district? There are several ways:

  • by bus No. 39, from the Noginsk railway station (from the Kursk station) to the village of Bolshoy Bunkovo. Further northward to the Karavaevskaya factory, about 2 km. Within walking distance (0.5 km) is the lake;
  • by car, along the Gorky highway, to the village of Bunkova. GPS coordinates: N55.87308 E38.59463.

Ichthyofauna and fishing

Lake Borovoye in the Noginsk region is rich in carps, breams, crucians, perches. You can catch crayfish. Previously, almost all reservoirs in the Moscow region could surprise even the most experienced fisherman with an assortment of fish. There was a sterlet, a whitefish and even a sturgeon. But the sewage from nearby plants and factories greatly polluted the water bodies of the Moscow region. Periodically, measures are taken to improve the situation:

  • treatment plants of industrial enterprises are being modernized;
  • the fight against poachers;
  • a ban on net fishing has been introduced.

In addition, fry of various species of fish are periodically released into the waters. The sufficiently large depth of Lake Borovoe in the Noginsky district is almost a 100% guarantee of catch. There is always a good bite in any season.

Many fishing enthusiasts come to this particular reservoir, due to the fact that there is night fishing here. Shores covered with algae are decorated with lanterns, schools of fish are visible - this is a delightful sight not only for fishermen, but also for children who will definitely be delighted.





Country holiday

In the village of Bolshoy Bunkovo, on the shore of the lake, there is a departmental recreation center Borovoye surrounded by a pristine pine forest. Sherna River is located 500 meters from the base.

The number of rooms in the base is represented by apartments with all amenities. In addition to accommodation in five-story brick buildings, a campsite is also available for holidaymakers and there is a special camping area. There are only six brick buildings, each of them has a working elevator. If desired, meals can be organized for visitors according to the "buffet" system. Three meals a day, and the dining room is designed for 300 vacationers.

The room has all the amenities, a balcony. Superior rooms have a refrigerator and a TV. Deluxe rooms have a bedroom and a living room.

Reviews about the recreation center on Lake Borovoye (Noginsky District) are mixed. Everything suits one people, others are outraged that the declared comfort is not in the rooms. Everything is very reminiscent of the Soviet Union. At the same time, to relax on the couch, you can stay at home, and if you go to nature, you need to walk, swim and actively relax.

At the services of visitors to the base there are:

  • a gym with exercise equipment and instructors;
  • tennis court;
  • catamarans;
  • sauna;
  • rental of equipment for sports (bicycles and skis, accessories for water trips);
  • skating rink (in winter);
  • bonfire site.

The administration of the Borovoye BO regularly organizes excursions by bicycle and ski (in winter) in the local district. Canoeing. For children on a regular basis animation programs. Excursions in the surroundings can be one-day or multi-day, with overnight stays in tents.

The most important holiday, which is celebrated on the territory of the base, is the Day of the tourist. On this day, mass sports are organized in tennis, volleyball and basketball. That is, the area in front of the building turns into one large sports ground.

For those who do not want to fuss and run, a sandy beach is always open. You can go to the pine forest for mushrooms. The recreation center is open year-round.

On the territory of the base there is a conference room for business events. The hall has modern lighting equipment, appliances and air conditioning.

Diving

On Lake Borovoye of the Noginsk district, this is one of the available entertainment. The organization of diving is the scuba diving club Odyssey.

The lake differs from most reservoirs in the Moscow Region in that it reaches 10 meters in visibility, that is, it is ideal for diving and practicing skills. Everyone can rent diving equipment, take a brief briefing and enjoy the underwater beauty of the lake. There is an opportunity to carry out spearfishing. Winter diving is very popular, as visibility is greatly improved.

On the territory of the center there is a compressor room, a kitchen and a relaxation room, a sanitary unit.





Sherna River

Vacationers who are bored by the lake can go to the Shern River. This is a small rivulet, not more than 89 kilometers in length. However, she is one of the cleanest in the Noginsk region. The river is characterized by a fast flow, on the banks of beavers and otters, in recent years, they are rapidly recovering their population. According to local old-timers, earlier on the banks of the river it was possible to meet many martens and ermines.
