Master class "Lotus Flower"

Until you have completed carving courses in Moscow, start with the simplest things. "Lotus Flower" will help you develop basic carving skills. Cook a large onion, carrots, cabbage leaf and a very sharp knife.



Step-by-step instruction

  1. Cut the onion in half. Then around the edge, outline where you will cut the triangles. The width of the side along the round edge should be 0.5 - 0.7 cm. Cut into the bulbs into the depths of 1.5 cm.
  2. Trim 0.6 - 0.8 cm from the bottom edge of the onion and divide it into layers. Inner smaller layers will end up with blunt ends. They must be cut to make sharp.
  3. From a carrot, cut a circle 1 - 1.3 cm wide and cut perpendicular V-grooves.
  4. Take 3 layers of the bulb and put them into each other so that the petals do not fit together. Put the carrot stamen inside.
  5. Pour water into a wide plate. Then put 2 - 3 cabbage leaves on which plant a flower. To keep it better, you can hide an inverted coffee cup under the leaves of cabbage.
