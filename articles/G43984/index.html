We are going from Kazan to Saratov

Russia is a rich country. This is not about the financial issue, but about beautiful places located on its territory. Two amazing cities - Saratov and Kazan - are famous for their ancient architecture, magnificent natural parks and a huge number of entertainment centers of various subjects. What else can a tourist see in these regions? There may be several reasons for coming here.





Interesting places in the city of Kazan

Many travel agencies offer their customers a bus tour to the city of Kazan. What objects are worth paying attention to? There may be several options.





Not far from the metro station "North Station" is the famous Uritsky Park. He was recognized as the largest and most well-groomed in the whole country. You can walk for hours on its territory and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

In the capital of Tatarstan there are many ancient temples whose architecture fascinates, for example, such is the Temple of all religions.

Almost every tour offers an excursion to the famous Syuyumbike Tower. An amazing feature is that every year it tends to land more and more, but it does not fall.

The most extreme people should visit the rope park. Its highest point is located at the level of 8 meters. The view from it is bewitching.

The ten most beautiful places in this city include the Bauman pedestrian street. Locals make haste on it every day. Tourists can stay here for hours, examining architectural monuments.

There are many entertainment places in Kazan. There are a huge number of shopping centers, museums, zoos, concert halls, you can visit the circus. Of particular note is the cinema, in which the screen is located on the ceiling.

Interesting places in the city of Saratov

Saratov is a fairly small city. Despite this, annually a huge number of tourists arrive in it. There are several objects worthy of attention in this city.

On Radishchev Street is the museum of the same name. It still has pre-war exhibits with an interesting history. The staff respects every visitor and tries to convey the most useful information.

You should definitely look into the local conservatory. From its windows you can hear the sound of an organ. Tourists can stand at this building for hours, enjoying the melody.

Visiting people must visit Victory Park, monuments of military subjects are installed in it.

At Kumysnaya Polyana there are several clean springs where you can get healing water.

In the summer, you should go to the sandy beach.

Favorite place for all residents is the promenade. It is where all city events are held. On weekdays, here you can go to the amusement park, square or sit in a cozy cafe.

Two cities are tourist centers, where a huge number of people come annually. It is not necessary to travel as a group, you can do it yourself. It is important to study in advance the question of how to get to Kazan from Saratov and make a route in the opposite direction.

Method number 1: by car

The distance from Kazan to Saratov is 676 kilometers. To overcome it on your own vehicle, of course, is difficult, but possible. In addition, the whole path is one straight line, it is simply impossible to get lost. Approximate travel time is about 10 hours. It will be quite difficult for the driver to maintain such a distance, it is recommended to think over a place to stop beforehand.

Leaving Saratov, you can drive in two possible ways: on the left or on the right side of the Volga. It is better to give preference to the second option, since on this route the road surface is much better. Next, you need to follow the direct route, passing through three large cities: Balakovo, Syzran, Ulyanovsk.

On the way you can see a huge number of gas stations, grocery stores and cafes.

Method 2: by bus

There is a unique opportunity - to get a direct flight from Saratov to Kazan by bus. Unfortunately, he only walks three times a week: Monday, Friday, and Sunday. Total travel time is 13 hours and 30 minutes. That is, at 19:50 departure from the Saratov international station, and already at 7:20 the vehicle arrives at the central station of Kazan. The cost of a ticket per person is approximately 1150 rubles.

Method 3: by train





There are no trains from Saratov to Kazan. You can buy a ticket for passing, following from Kislovodsk or Volgograd. Departure takes place at 7:00 and 23:46. The minimum ticket price is 906 rubles.

Method 4: by plane

Near the settlements there is an airport. This suggests that you can get from Saratov to Kazan quickly - in 1 hour and 30 minutes. It is worth noting that the flight is carried out only twice a week: on Saturday and Sunday, early in the morning at 7:00. In the summer, it is recommended to buy tickets in advance, the cost per person will be approximately 3450 rubles.





There are several ways to get from Kazan to Saratov. They are affordable for every person. It is worth choosing the most optimal option for yourself.
