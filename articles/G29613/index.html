Do-it-yourself rugs from old jeans. Useful needlework

Jeans fashion has not passed for several decades. More than one generation wears denim pants. The clothes are comfortable, the fabric is durable. But fashion is changing, I want to buy new ones, but where to put on worn pants? You can make rugs from old jeans with your own hands.





Non-waste production

Nothing needs to be thrown away, everything will come in handy. Just attach a little imagination, pick up scissors, a needle and thread, and you will get a new masterpiece of home production.

This is one of the best materials for crafts and the manufacture of various products. We offer you five options for a rug from this fabric. You can put it in a room or give it to your creative friends. Additional pants can be requested from them.

Tapes can be selected alternating in color - front, wrong side. You can connect a fantasy and roll each tape into a roller, and secure the ends from the wrong side. Now you know what to sew from old jeans, and how to do it.

The rug is jeans massage

Give a second life to unnecessary jeans. Show your imagination and master the simplest manufacturing techniques. The mat is useful for you in the bathroom, nursery or bedroom.





Manufacturing steps:

  1. Cut as many 8 x 8 squares as possible. It will be great if the whole family, especially the children, will be engaged in their preparation, especially since there is no need to persuade the children.
  2. Everything will depend on what format will be the rugs from old jeans with their own hands.
  3. Prepare the base from the old legs, make a rectangle from them. How to sew a rug from old jeans is a popular question, but such things are done quite simply.
  4. Fold each cut square in the shape of a triangle and scribble, attaching it to the base.
  5. Pick a color scheme from triangles.
  6. Alternately lay triangular rags on each other "herringbone".
  7. Stitch to the base, doing row after row.

Rugs from old jeans with their own hands in this design will be an adornment of any country house.

Denim wicker mat

This rug is quick-made and less expensive in materials:

  • cut jeans ribbons so that all strips are the same width;
  • pick light and dark blue fabric for weaving;
  • connect the strips together in three hanks and begin to weave a pigtail out of each of them;
  • secure each pigtail at the base with sewing pins or ribbons;
  • choose any shape you like - oval, circle, square;
  • in the course of work, sew each braid with the next, in a certain order;
  • select the threads in the color of denim.

A beautiful interior item will be a rug from old jeans. A master class will help to understand its manufacture.





Floor mat

Choose denim jeans. Do not forget to get rid of thick seams, pockets, belts. The fabric should be approximately the same density:

  • cut jeans in length into narrow strips up to 7 mm wide;
  • cutting the fabric, do not reach the edge of about 4-5 mm;
  • each strip is cut in the forward and reverse directions;
  • at the junction of the strips you will get an angle and a slight thickening, but this will not be noticeable in the finished product;
  • twist the resulting yarn into a skein, so it will be easier to weave;
  • crochet air loops, the length of which will be 6-7 cm less than the total width of the mat;
  • knit each row with a single crochet, replace the first column of the row with 2 lifting air loops;
  • knit columns for both arches of the columns of the penultimate row, this will give it strength;
  • to connect a new skein, sew the ends of the ribbons with strong threads;
  • heterogeneous material can give a small wave at the edges, correct by tying with crochet columns around the entire edge of the mat; for this you need the extra 5-7 mm left;
  • Tie each corner with three single crochet columns from one loop; in each subsequent row, remove the wave along the edges and knit two columns in one cavity in one point, and knit two columns on the ledges, combining them.

Do-it-yourself rugs from old jeans will appeal to all lovers of handmade art.





Sports mat

Denim trousers and jackets will go into business, the main thing is to choose them by color and the same structure:

  1. Spread all the products at the seams and cut into two strips.
  2. Sew all the strips in width, alternating them in color in a checkerboard pattern.
  3. Sew the flaps into one fabric.
  4. Align the edges and shape the rug.
  5. Prepare a strong, lining fabric in a suitable color.
  6. Chip off the lining and mat with pins.
  7. Sew on the sewing machine around the entire perimeter of the mat, leaving a gap of 15-20 cm to turn it out.
  8. Finish the finished rug with a slanting inlay, with the wrong side inward.

So you can give old things a new life with their own hands.

Label Carpet

Mats from jeans clothing labels will look great, with images of symbols, flags of different countries. For density, it is better to sew labels on top of each other. It is better to sew along the perimeter, along the edge with which it was sewn to the product. It is possible to sew to a rug a fir-tree, a grid or a spiral. The chaotic arrangement of labels will look spectacular and expressive.
