Let's talk about how to crochet napkins: a workshop for beginners

Anyone can learn to crochet napkins . In fact, it is simple and does not take much time. In this article, we will tell you how to create such a delicate miracle with your own hands, which can become a magnificent decor element.

Using napkins, you can transform the interior of the room, making it more comfortable and enjoyable. In addition, having mastered the technique of manufacturing these products, you can use the knowledge gained in knitting blankets , tablecloths, bedspreads and other exquisite things. We advise beginning masters to try crocheting napkins, this will help you quickly and easily learn to understand any patterns.

Plenty of room for imagination



Knitted napkins can vary in size and shape. They can be square, and round, and triangular, and even hexagonal. Napkins are made using a variety of techniques, including Irish lace, patchwork or Brugge lace. Often in one product several different knitting methods are originally combined.

The choice of shape, size, color performance depends on the preferences of the master and the functional purpose of the thing. There are no restrictions in the manufacture of napkins - any author can rely on his imagination and sense of beauty. But you should remember that first you need to decide on the knitting pattern of the product. It is important to use ready-made options. If you come up with a drawing in the course of work, then the product may turn out to be uneven and not beautiful enough.

In this article, we will tell you how to crochet a napkin like floral motifs to decorate a coffee table. We will make several identical fragments in the shape of flowers and combine them into a single design.

Large napkins (crocheted) - an elegant decoration for your home

For work you will need white threads and a hook. So, we crochet openwork napkins: first, pick up the air loops (6 pcs.) And connect them into a ring. Make 2 double crochets and then add 1 air loop. Repeat 8 times.

After knitting the first, we proceed to the manufacture of the second row: we make 1 double crochet and we knit 3 air loops. Now again, make a double crochet and make one air loop. Repeat the item 8 times.

We start the third row with two air loops. We knit 1 double crochet, 2 stitches, again double crochet and 2 stitches. After that, we perform a single crochet. Repeat this 8 times.

We start the fourth row with two air loops. We knit 3 double crochets, 1 air loop, then again repeat 3 double crochets. Make 2 stitches and complete the crochet element with a single crochet.

We knit the fourth row to the end, repeating this element of the scheme 8 times. You should get a small openwork flower.

Crochet napkins - do-it-yourself home decoration

After one flower is ready, tie a few more fragments and connect them. If you want to make a large napkin, tie 6 or 8 fragments, if small, four will be enough. After connecting the parts, your craft is ready! Now you can create a charming napkin with your own hands, which will definitely decorate any table or cabinet.

In fact, crocheting napkins is not so difficult. A novice master will be able to cope with this task. Good luck and success!
