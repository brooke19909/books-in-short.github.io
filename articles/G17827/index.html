Lan tester: description, purpose

Lan tester is a device designed to verify the integrity of telecommunication networks. Typically, such a device consists of a main part and a removable one (most often one, but there are models in which several removable parts are provided). Lan-tester allows you to "ring" computer networks, telephone communications, crimped connectors.



The network tester greatly facilitates the work and saves the time of cable specialists. After all, their job is not only to extend communications, but also to debug the entire system. Visually, it is practically impossible to determine the quality of the crimp of the connector, and in order to ring it with the help of a tester, it will take a lot of time and an assistant.
