Barcelona is a major tourist destination in the Mediterranean. The capital of Catalonia has all the amenities for a comfortable stay. The infrastructure here is very developed. Barcelona hotels, accommodation prices which depend on the level of service, are dispersed throughout the city. General information about the city

Barcelona is a little different from Rome or Paris. She only recently gained wide popularity. This happened after the Olympics in 1992. Since then, the capital of Catalonia has changed. The best beaches of Barcelona invariably attract more and more tourists from year to year. A lot of hotel complexes appeared in the city. However, the owners of these institutions do not have time to cope with a large influx of visitors. More often than not, in high season rooms are not enough. Reservations must be made in advance. This becomes a necessity if guests plan to stay at the establishment for the weekend. In many hotels, these days, a system of discounts. It should be said about the undoubted advantages that Barcelona has: the sea, the well-groomed coast, a large number of entertainment centers for active pastime.

Hotel classification

Hotel complexes are located in each district of the city. However, most of them are concentrated in the center of Barcelona, where there are many popular places.

Expensive establishments

The best hotels in Barcelona are concentrated mainly near Plaza Catalunya, as well as Paseo de Gracia and La Rambla. Many establishments of a high level of comfort are located in the Forum area. All of the above areas of the city are next to each other. If you stay at any local hotel, then the surrounding attractions and the best beaches of Barcelona will be within walking distance. Not far from the Sants station is located no less popular urban place. Here are mainly apartment hotels and four-star hotels. Guests can rent not only a separate room, but also an entire apartment. The Camp Nou district, where the legendary stadium is located, also has a similar specialization. Local hotel complexes provide the highest level of service. Those who prefer a luxurious stay most often stop here.

Location Features

Barcelona is not a very big metropolis. Even from the most remote hotel complex, using a taxi, you can get to any of its points quickly enough. Many tourists prefer to walk. Bed & breakfast

Hotels of this category, as well as hostels, are a more budget option for accommodation. A place in such institutions, as a rule, costs no more than 20 euros. The rooms are more expensive. In some hotels, the price for them can go up to 35 euros. Most hostels equipped with private rooms can well compete with two-star hotels in the quality of the service provided. In addition, many of them are located in the city center. This allows guests to significantly reduce transportation costs. In total there are about two hundred similar institutions of various categories.

Hostels

Establishments of this category are divided into official and private. The first are part of the Hostelling International network. Formal institutions require customers to have a membership card. It can be purchased at every hostel that is part of the network. Official establishments also differ in that they close for several hours during the day and all night. At the same time, guests must observe various restrictions. In contrast, private establishments are open around the clock. The standards for their service are not regulated by anyone. A rather cheerful atmosphere reigns here. In official hostels, as a rule, many European schoolchildren stay. Private establishments accommodate travelers who travel to Timbuktu via Barcelona.

Apartments

These establishments are a great alternative to hotels. Many apartments are located in different areas of the city. Every year they are becoming increasingly popular, especially in Spain. The choice of such establishments is truly huge. Guests can stay in simple studios or spacious rooms that include up to five bedrooms. When choosing an apartment, it is worth considering the fact that they are most often rented for longer stays. Reservations for apartments are provided for a minimum of three nights. The apartments are populated mainly by large groups of travelers. Also, establishments of this category are popular with those who are willing to save on room service and other services inherent in hotels. Apartment for two will cost guests about 80 euros per night. Luxury rooms will cost more.

The most economical accommodation

The noise of a crowded city will not bother them. However, in this case, the path to the sights will not be close.
