How to choose a wall fan heater. Fan heaters for home: reviews

A person is quite sensitive to any changes in ambient temperature. At all times, people have sought to live in mild climatic zones, but the growth of the population on the planet has led to the need to populate more and more new territories, in particular, regions where the climate was not so favorable. Since then, they always face the problem of heating their homes. For most of the history, heating was provided by fairly primitive natural means: bonfires, fireplaces, hearths, stoves. Open fire is not only warm, but also a lot of fears and inconveniences. With the development of science and technology, a big step has been taken in solving the heating problem. Electricity is the main source of heat in our time.





A modern approach to heating

It is worth considering a wall-mounted fan heater as an interesting representative of electric heating appliances. According to the principle of action, it is a heat gun, that is, a device that converts electrical energy into heat, and then radiates this heat to the environment. In a constructive plan, the fan heater is a combination of the following elements: heating part, control device, fan, housing, protection device.

The wall-mounted electric fan heater uses spiral heaters as a heating element , where the heat source is an open spiral made of special materials, electric heaters and plate-type ceramic-metal heaters. Many consumers - according to reviews - agree that the best solution is devices with the latest type of heaters, since they are the safest and most reliable in terms of operation. You can consider each element of the device individually.

Fan

The wall fan heater has such an important element as a fan, which provides the maximum coefficient of efficiency in the entire family of devices. Its task is to ensure forced air circulation, due to which the room warms up many times faster and more efficiently. Modern industry offers a huge selection of fans, featuring quiet operation, low power consumption and a wide range of speed control.





Device control

The wall fan heater can work with manual or automatic control. In most modern devices, the second option is used, which allows you to maintain specified temperature conditions without human intervention. A distinctive feature of a manual device is lower cost, but its inconvenience is that the user must constantly participate in the process of his work. Many models have remote controls, through which, according to the owners, it is possible to improve the operation of the device.

Equipment protection

First of all, this element is focused on protecting the fan heater from overheating. Human protection can be ensured by the presence of a residual current device, which works when damage to the insulation of live parts. However, it is important to understand that the wall-mounted fan heater is an equipment of increased danger, therefore, its operation must occur in compliance with safety regulations.





Housing

The type of housing design has a direct impact on the nature of the installation of the device. In this case, it allows you to mount the fan heater directly on the wall of the room. According to users, this saves space in the room, eliminates the possibility of unwanted contacts with the device, organically fits it into the design of the room. For modern heating appliances, housings are made of materials that comply with all environmental, aesthetic and technical standards.





Features of use

The wall fan heater is a device used to heat apartments and public premises. In terms of design, this is a fan in which air passes through the heating elements. The principle of operation of the device is quite simple. The fan heater creates an air stream passing through special heating elements. Next, warm air is evenly distributed throughout the room. The thermostat ensures its heating to the required temperature. After this happens, the device turns off. When the room temperature drops, it turns on again.

The fan heater supplies not only warm but also cold air, which is ensured by turning off the heating element. Therefore, fan heaters for the home can be used as conventional fans.





Features of different types of devices

Equipment in this category can be ceramic and spiral. The first option is considered more practical and reliable, it is characterized by a long service life. Exploitation

The wall fan heater, reviews of which indicate its convenience and practicality, is well suited not only for the living room or bedroom, but also for the bathroom. For this room there are special types of appliances that are not afraid of moisture. In the basic configuration of such equipment, a heated towel rail is often found. Thanks to this, the device will help not only warm up, but also dry things.

Devices differ in power. Most often, the wall-mounted fan heater, reviews of which speak of its functionality, is characterized by a power of up to 2 kW. High-performance devices are commonly referred to as heat guns. Such equipment most often runs on electricity, but there are also models that operate on diesel fuel or kerosene. However, such options are usually not used in everyday life.

An interesting solution is the wall-mounted Timberk fan heater with an integrated air humidifier. With constant work, the air in the room becomes overdried, and this option makes it softer, allowing you to maintain health and immunity.





conclusions

Wall fan heater is a good solution, with which you can warm up at any time of the year. It quickly warms up the room, characterized by compactness, low weight and ease of installation. It can be moved to another room without much effort. Modern instrument models combine a whole range of diverse functions, so they replace several devices.
