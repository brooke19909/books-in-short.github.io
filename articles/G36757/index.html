Airotel Achaia Beach 4 *: description of the hotel, rooms and offered services

On the shore of the Gulf of Patraikos, which is washed by the Peloponnese (the southern part of the Balkan Peninsula) from the north-western side, there is a wonderful city called Patras. It is very popular among tourists. And it is here, in the immediate vicinity of the beach and just a 5-minute drive from the city center, that the 4-star Airotel Achaia Beach Hotel is located, which should be described.





Services offered

At Airotel Achaia Beach, there is everything that visitors may need. Naturally, this is free Wi-Fi in the complex and in the rooms. Private parking is also available. By the way, you do not need to rent a car through an agency in advance - this can be done at the hotel. He has such an opportunity.

There is also a currency exchange and tour desk, dry cleaning with laundry and even a business center with a large conference room.

In addition to these services, the administration can arrange the delivery of drinks and food directly to the apartments. By the way, the hotel has not only rooms for honeymooners, but also with increased sound insulation. Guests also have the opportunity to request non-smoking apartments.

Entertainment

The vast majority of tourists coming here are engaged exclusively in beach vacations. The sea is clean, pleasant and even literally located at arm's length. But, if you do not want to leave the Airotel Achaia Beach, you can stay sunbathe on the terrace near the large swimming pool.

There are few entertainments in the hotel, but there is a sauna, a good fitness center and the possibility to rent a bicycle. For children in this small but comfortable hotel there are no conditions. People who want to take their kids with them on vacation should know about this in order to choose another, more suitable option.





Nutrition

Airotel Achaia Beach has one restaurant and bar. When booking a room, people can choose one of two food options - either BB (breakfast) or HB (half board). It is better to take the latter option, since there are few establishments nearby where you can eat - basically everything is in the center.

The food is varied, everyone will find something to their taste. There are cereals, all kinds of fruits, hot dishes (omelets, pancakes and fried eggs, they are cooked in the presence of visitors), delicious fresh vegetables, pies, pastries, as well as cheeses, sausages and even dried fruits.

For dinner, a variety of dishes are also served. Lovers of satisfying food will enjoy the variety of meat - they serve pork, lamb, beef, fish, poultry, squid. You can enjoy hot pasta with mushrooms, shrimp or cheese. And of course, sweets - different puddings, fruits, pastries. The only thing you need to remember is drinks (except water) paid for dinner. But the prices are reasonable. For example, for half a liter of red wine you will need to pay 3.5 euros.





About budget rooms

In Airotel Achaia Beach 4 * there are 85 different apartments. All are furnished with good, quality furniture and are furnished in a cozy style. The most economical option is the Standard for two guests. The room is small (15 sq. M), but has everything you need for comfort. They have powerful air conditioning, satellite TV and even a coffee maker with safe. The room itself opens onto a balcony, from where you can admire the picturesque mountain scenery, sitting in comfortable armchairs. Inside there is everything you need to iron clothes, an alarm clock, a hairdryer, a telephone and, of course, a bathroom.

Room prices may vary. It all depends on the tour operator, season, as well as the presence / absence of shares. In general, a week of stay in these apartments costs 25-30 thousand rubles. for two (with breakfast).





More spacious options

In Airotel Achaia Beach 4 * there is also the so-called family apartment. They are slightly larger in area and are designed for 2 adults with two children. Inside, there are two single beds and one bunk. The amenities are all the same as mentioned a bit above. And the weekly price of the room is ~ 43,000 p.

By the way, apartments designed for three adult guests will cost the same. Only inside, in addition to two single beds, there will be a folding sofa.
