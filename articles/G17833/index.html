MFP "Samsung SCX - 4200". Practical recommendations for use

Samsung SCX-4200 is one of the most affordable multifunction devices, which, among other things, is based on the laser method of obtaining prints. This universal peripheral device is perfect for implementing ultra-compact workflow systems. And it is about this printer from the eminent South Korean manufacturer that will be discussed further.





Key Features of the All-In-One

As previously noted, the Samsung SCX-4200 printer uses the laser method to print fingerprints. Its essence boils down to the fact that electromagnetic field lines are distributed on the surface of a selenium drum. Under their influence, toner is unevenly distributed on its surface. Then this drum heats up and the toner powder ‚Äúfuses‚Äù into the paper.

This method allows you to get a large print limit and increased speed. But the quality of the output documents is more modest than that of inkjet MFPs and printers. Therefore, this solution is excellent for those cases where print volume and speed are important. But quality fades into the background. Most often, such an MFP can be found in small offices.

How to reuse a cartridge

The Samsung SCX-4200 cartridge, when fully filled with toner, can output up to 3,000 sheets. After that, the manufacturer recommends the use of a new cartridge, and in the old there is a blockage, which can still be unofficially circumvented. And two completely different methods. One of them is to use a new microcircuit, and the second is to update the MFP software shell.





Microchip

The easiest way for a novice user to extend the cartridge life for Samsung SCX-4200 is to install a new chip. This method is hardware. Before its implementation, it is necessary to purchase a special refill kit, which consists of monochromatic toner and a special counter chip.

The procedure for performing such a hardware method for extending the life of a laser printing cartridge is as follows:

  1. We remove the cartridge from the MFP.
  2. We disassemble it. For this, it is first necessary to prepare the workplace, which should be covered and equipped with all the necessary tools.
  3. After disassembling, clean the ink container with a rag.
  4. Using a scalpel and tweezers, dismantle the old chip and install the new one in the same way.
  5. Fill the container with new toner.
  6. Putting the cartridge together.
  7. We return it to the MFP and check the performance.

The key disadvantage of this method is the need to purchase a chip and replace it with each refueling.

Shell replacement

An alternative to the hardware solution to this problem is the software method, which consists in installing a special shell. In this case, a special third-party software environment is sewn up in Samsung SCX-4200. It is identical to the branded one and differs only in that the cartridge resource check is disabled in it. This allows you to reuse the cartridge without using a chip. In this case, only monochromatic toner is included in the refill kit. But in the additional acquisition of a new chip in the described case, there is no need.





Which method is better in practice?

Both of the previously described ways to reuse the cartridge can often be found in practice. As a rule, novice computer experts opt for the hardware method, since although it is more expensive, it is simpler. And the second option for extending the cartridge service for the Samsung SCX-4200 MFP is already chosen by experienced users. It is more complex, but economically more profitable. It is recommended that it be used whenever possible.





The results. MFP cost and reviews

Judging by the reviews, the Samsung MFP SCX-4200 does not have significant minuses and shortcomings. Some experts only occasionally point out the minimum set of possible ways to connect this device. The main one is universal USB. If the MFP is equipped with a network adapter, then you can even directly connect it to a computer network.

The pluses often include speed, the possible volume of documentation output per month and cost. The latter is in the range of 7,500 to 9,000 rubles.
