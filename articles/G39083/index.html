Hotel Radisson Blu Tala Bay Resort 5 * (Aqaba, Jordan): photos and reviews of tourists

Radisson Blu Tala Bay Resort is a very popular hotel in Jordan. There are many reasons for this, and each one should be briefly described.





Location

Radisson Blu Tala Bay Resort is located in Jordan. This is an Arab state located in the Middle East. Recently, not as many tourists come here as before. Jordan borders on Syria and Iraq - many are afraid of the situation there. However, all resort hotels are far from the borders - this can be said the opposite end of the state, which borders on the Gulf of Aqaba.

In these places the hotel is located. More specifically, at a tourist resort called Tala Bay. It can be reached from Aqaba (the seaport of the same name) for 15 minutes by car. It takes about 20 minutes to get to the airport. The hotel itself is located in close proximity to the beach.

By the way, the hotel is relatively new - it was built in 2008. And for 8 full years of work, he managed to become very popular and visited.

There is also a laundry, ironing and dry cleaning service, where guests can still clean their shoes if they ask. There is also a business center, an office with fax and photocopying services, a spacious conference room and various events.

About accommodation and booking

For many people, one of the most likely holiday destinations is Jordan. Radisson Blu Tala Bay Resort 5 * is the hotel that most of them choose. Therefore, in order to catch a vacation right here, to this hotel, it is better to send a reservation request a couple of months before the planned trip.
