Hotel Arkutino Family Resort (Bulgaria / Arkutino): photos, reviews

Arkutino Family Resort is a wonderful hotel located in one of the most popular and, accordingly, visited resorts. The hotel has a lot of advantages. And I would like to talk about all of them in as much detail as possible.





general information

Arkutino Family Resort is located in the modern resort of Duni. This is the southern coast of Bulgaria. Dune is a failure of a well-groomed and cozy village, the visiting card of which is pedestrian streets paved with stone, and shady picturesque alleys. It is an ideal place for a relaxing and peaceful stay.

The hotel itself is a couple of minutes walk from a private beach. The airport is also relatively close. It is located in Burgas - it is about 40-50 kilometers away. There is also an airport in Varna, but it takes longer to get there (about 170 km).

The hotel is in a good location - attractions can be reached quickly. Ropotamo Nature Reserve is approximately 1.7 kilometers away. The local water park is approximately an 8-minute drive away. Sozopol port is about the same. A 10-minute drive takes guests to the Apolonia Amphitheater. In general, the location for tourists is advantageous in all plans, this is a fact.

About Services

Arkutino Family Resort has the perfect setting to make your guest feel comfortable. Everything is thought out here to the smallest detail - starting with free wireless Internet, ending with private parking.

A 24-hour front desk, tour desk and currency exchange are available in the lobby. In addition, the hotel has such standard services as laundry and dry cleaning, where guests are still ironed. There is a small business center in the hotel.

Transfer services are also provided here - both to and from the airport. But if a person needs personal transport, then this is not a problem, since the hotel has a car rental point. In addition to the above, the hotel has a beauty salon, hairdresser and special services for the bride and groom. By the way, the staff here speaks not only Bulgarian, but also English, German and, most importantly, Russian. Therefore, no one should have communication problems.

Relaxation

Most people come to Arkutino Family Resort 4 * to enjoy the atmosphere of the resort and, of course, a beach holiday. But if you do not want to leave the hotel, you still will not be bored. For children, by the way, there is a separate playroom and playground. And they are also entertained by animators. So the smallest on vacation will be something to do.
