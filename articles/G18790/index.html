LCD "Symphony" (Yekaterinburg): description

Yekaterinburg is the capital of the Urals, a million-plus city, whose residents need new residential quarters and neighborhoods. LCD "Symphony" - a project of modern high-rise buildings, offering all citizens the highest level of comfort and safety. It will be a small residential area in a huge city, with its infrastructure and a recreation area.

about the project

The new residential quarter, built up with comfort class buildings - all this is the Symphony LCD. The project envisages the construction of 24 monolithic buildings of various storeys, landscaping and greening of the adjacent territory, as well as the construction of some objects of its own infrastructure. Currently, the first phase of the project is being implemented - a 25-storey building.





Location

The Zheleznodorozhny District of Yekaterinburg was chosen for the construction of the complex . This is an attractive place for modern construction companies, because all residents of the district will have access to the infrastructure facilities of the district. The air here, which is rare for an industrial city, is clean and fresh, within walking distance of new buildings is Verkh-Isetsky Pond, which will definitely become a popular place for picnics and outdoor recreation.

Technology

The Symphony residential complex is being built using monolithic brick technology: monolithic concrete provides the necessary strength, reliability, resistance to shrinkage, and brick floors and cladding give not only an external aesthetics to the complex, but also provide it with excellent heat and sound insulation properties.





Infrastructure

The quarter was originally conceived as a full-fledged place for the life of modern families. The developer tried to take into account all the wishes of the residents. The territory adjacent to the complex, according to the plan, is literally immersed in greenery: as the object is commissioned, trees and shrubs will be planted on the territory, flower beds and flower beds will be broken.

Playgrounds and sports grounds with modern equipment for children of various ages, their own kindergarten, parking for all residents - all this is the Symphony residential complex. Reviews of those who have already managed to purchase an apartment here for themselves, focus on comfort. The lower floors of residential buildings will be given to the missing infrastructure: bank branches, shops, laundries, dry cleaners and much more.

Apartments, decoration

The residents are offered a choice of apartments with a fine finish, which they like, according to their reviews, as it will allow you to make repairs to your liking. All balconies and loggias will be glazed in the same style in order to maintain the integrity of the overall architectural ensemble.
