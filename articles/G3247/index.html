Rest in Kerch: boarding houses, a private sector, recreation facilities, prices

Kerch is a unique place in many respects, one of the oldest cities not only in Russia or in Europe, but also in the world, because it was founded almost 100 years before Rome. In addition, Kerch is full of attractions and colorful places, and is also unique in that it has access to two seas at once - the Black and Azov Sea. According to locals, visiting Crimea and not coming to Kerch is about the same as coming to Paris and not climbing the Eiffel Tower. Where to stay, having arrived in this city, we will tell below.

Features of rest in Kerch

Despite the fact that rest in Kerch has great tourist potential, it is one of the most non-tourist cities in Crimea. They rarely come here for a long time. Basically, Kerch is a transit point on the way to Kazantip, or a one-day resort where people go to see the sights. Therefore, the local hotel fund leaves much to be desired - several mini-hotels and mostly private pensions. There are several resorts.





There are housing offers from budget to quite expensive, but in most cases for a little money you will be offered a comfortable room and good service. Accommodation prices on average here are slightly lower than on the rest of the peninsula, which is attractive for budget travelers.

Pensions Kerch

There are several pensions located on the coast. They offer their guests half board or full board, good cuisine, comfortable clean rooms, evening entertainment and discos. The boarding house staff also organizes excursions and offers a shuttle service to the local market. This is an option for those who want to pay once and get a good rest. True, all these resorts of Kerch are located outside the city, but it is a pleasure to be on their territory - they have access to the sea, a landscaped area, a park area, arbors and walking paths.

Recreation facilities

The city of Kerch attracts tourists with its nature. Recreation facilities are a great option for those who do not plan treatment, but only want to enjoy the beauties of Crimea. The cost of living in them is significantly lower compared to boarding houses, which allows you to fully enjoy the sea and nature of Crimea.





Holidays in Kerch are possible at one of a large number of different bases, many of which position themselves as places for family vacations. It is convenient to come here with children - the beach is clean and gentle, there are no high waves at the sea, there are playgrounds and options for the children's menu. Also, recreation facilities, as a rule, offer accommodation not in large buildings, but in cozy cottages or bungalows.

Holidays in Kerch at such bases usually cost from 2500 rubles per day for 1 person.

Since they come to Kerch either for a couple of days, or to a sanatorium or a boarding house, there are almost no large hotels here. These are mainly private mini-hotels, but there are also a couple of large ones built back in Soviet times.

Recreation: private sector

This is an inexpensive type of accommodation suitable for those traveling to the Crimea as a "savage" or for a long term. You can rent a whole house or cottage, as well as an apartment or even a room in the city center, by the sea or in the suburbs of Kerch. If you want to save money - look for ads for accommodation in the city center, do not want to go to the beach for a long time every day and are ready to pay more - consider housing offers on the first line.

The cost of living in a manor or guest house starts from 800 rubles per day per person. Many hosts offer food, it can be tastier and more profitable than going to a cafe or restaurant. The average price level is about 700 rubles for three meals a day for one. Children are usually given good discounts.
