Ice Palace in Balashikha: description, address, mode of operation

Modern sports complexes are distinguished by their multifunctionality. Various tournaments are held in them, as well as sections and groups are opened. The Balashikha Ice Palace often becomes a venue for hockey and figure skating competitions. Its impressive size allows exhibitions and other events to be held here.

General information

The Ice Palace (Balashikha) is a universal center in which various sports tournaments, performances, and concerts are held. He has been working since 2007. The complex was built so that the population could lead a more active lifestyle. He is regularly visited by many people who are interested or involved in sports. For games and performances, the complex is equipped with modern technology for transmitting light and sound. A special screen of quite impressive size is installed for guests. In Europe, there are only a few such displays.

The main arena in the Ice Palace (Balashikha) regularly becomes the venue for hockey matches. Also there are competitions in futsal, boxing, basketball, volleyball and other sports. In a short period of time, the site can be redone for the necessary event. It is often used for shows and performances. Here V. Meladze, S. Mikhailov, N. Kadysheva and other artists sang in front of the audience. Many citizens were lucky to see the famous "Ice Age".

The complex has space for six thousand spectators. Vip-class lodges are also provided. They are designed for 200 people. For people with disabilities, special places are designed, separate elevators function. In addition to the main arena, there is also a training for athletes. Significant events regularly take place at the main venue. They hosted the Gagarin Cup, held the 4 Nations Tournaments. Previously, the complex was engaged in the HC MVD.

The center is famous for the presence of a restaurant, from which the entire sports ground is perfectly visible. There is also a cafe for visitors, which has its own separate entrance. For sports enthusiasts, a store with sports attributes has been opened. Tickets for the arena cost from 1000 rubles. The cost is affected not only by the popularity of the team, but also by the time at which the match takes place.

Where is

Many citizens know a popular place in the city. Therefore, they can always tell tourists where to find it. Ice Palace Address:

  • Park street, building 2.

Nearby is a large green area. This is the Pechorka park, which is named after the river flowing nearby. It can be an excellent guide to search.

There are various ways to get to the sports complex. Near him stop:

  • Buses 10, 16, 22, 32, 110, 337.
  • Minibuses 4k, 5k, 7k, 22k, 104k, 1132, 1176.

You can come to the Ice Palace (Balashikha) from Moscow and other cities. The best way would be the railway. Tourists need to go to the station called "Gorenki". Not far from it is the Gorenka Platform stop. From there, you can take a 7k minibus to the complex itself.

Mode of operation

The ice palace in Balashikha is open every day. It opens at 9.00. On some days it is available to visit until 18.00. But often it is open much longer. For the work of each sports section, you must ask on the spot or by phone listed on the official website.

Additional Information

The sports complex is very popular among people of all ages. Some come here to watch hockey, while others come to work out in various groups and sections. In the center it is easy to find something to your liking. Therefore, the address of the Ice Palace knows most of the inhabitants of the city.

The complex can enroll in a school-studio "Todes", which is very popular. There are also groups in rhythmic gymnastics and hockey, a boxing section, gyms. A chess club has been opened. Available to visit the Internet cafe with its own bar. For guests of the complex who have come to the competition, a hotel is nearby. In it, you can comfortably stay at the right time.

Ice skating enthusiasts regularly come here from all over the city. The ice rink in the Balashikha Ice Palace is very popular.

Mass skating takes place several times a week. On Wednesday and Friday, sessions begin at 20.00. On Saturday, come to ride is available from 18.00 to 2.00. On Sunday - from 18.00 to 21.00. Each session lasts one hour. There is a skate rental service on site, so they can be rented right on site.
