Hotel Citrus Hikkaduwa 4 * (Sri Lanka): photos, reviews

Accommodation

The main building of the Citrus Hikkaduwa 4 * hotel , the photo of which you have the opportunity to see in the article, contains 90 apartments of an increased level of comfort. All of them are divided into two categories: Superior and Deluxe. The rooms differ in size, the smaller of which is 27 square meters, located on the lower floors, the largest - 35, located on the 4th and 5th floors. Maximum occupancy is 4 people. There are rooms for the disabled.

The interior of the chambers is decorated taking into account modern trends in the art of repair. Multilevel ceilings, spotlights and original lighting add status and chic. A laminate or tile is laid on the floor. The color palette of the walls is represented by all shades of white. Bright pillows and curtains make the room bright and colorful. One of the walls is occupied by a large mirror.

All rooms of the Citrus Hikkaduwa 4 * hotel have an exit to the balcony, where there are comfortable wooden chairs for taking air baths. Housekeepers daily clean up and maintain cleanliness. Linen is changed twice a week. You can order food in your room or wake up service at the scheduled time.





Equipment

The furniture set is made of dark wood and includes a bed, armchairs, a coffee table, a desk and a wardrobe. The presence of a tea and coffee set along with an electric kettle allows you to meet the morning cheerfully and in a good mood. A telephone is available for transport with a 24-hour reception. The availability of a safe will allow you to enjoy a carefree stay on the island. There is a multi-channel TV and a minibar filled with drinking water. Individual split-system easily adapts to changes in climatic conditions. Ironing board and iron are available upon request. Wi-Fi access is free throughout.

A shower cabin, a sink and a hairdryer are equipped with a bathroom. Its decoration has a simple modern look. Near the mirror is a set of personal care products. White bathrobes and towels are available.





Nutrition

When checking in, each guest independently chooses a suitable catering system: only breakfast, half board, board or All Inclusive. The organization of meals is carried out according to the buffet system. The required number and preferred variety of dishes each guest forms independently. The offered dishes are represented by international and local cuisines. The Lemon Fish Restaurant, which resorts eat, has a cozy atmosphere, an interesting interior and a beautiful table setting. As guests note in their positive reviews, the catering here is at a high level. Even the most sophisticated tourist will choose a dish to taste for himself.

In the open air is the Summer Salt Bar, where every vacationer can quench their thirst all day. Professional bartenders will treat visitors with unusual cocktails, fresh fresh and other soft drinks.

The High Tide Pub offers a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as a number of delicious snacks.





Beach

Getting to the waters of the Indian Ocean is easy. A distance of 50 meters separates the Citrus Hikkaduwa hotel complex from the coast. The entrance to the water is gentle. A gradual increase in depth allows young children to frolic aground. Slender tropical palm trees and comfortable pavilions with sun loungers are located on the shore. Each guest can use them.

The most popular here are windsurfing and diving. To conquer the surging waves, standing on the board, not every traveler can do it. But diving to the bottom of the Indian Ocean, enjoying the underwater beauties and a unique combination of flora and fauna, is available to everyone: both a beginner and a diver with experience.

In addition, many people prefer diving with a mask and riding various water attractions.

It does not have a heating system. Numerous sun loungers with mattresses surround him: getting an excellent dark-skinned tan is not difficult for both adults and children. Numerous umbrellas are set here for those who want to hide from the hot sun. The view of the spacious oceanic distances inspires every guest. Towels are provided free of charge.
