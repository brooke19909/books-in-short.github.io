The best hotels in the center of Kemer

Kemer is a port city, a resort city in Turkey. The most common destination among Russian tourists for recreation. The Mediterranean coast of Kemer attracts thousands of Russians.





Tekirova and Camyuve

In Kemer, there are five main areas: Tekirova, Beldibi, Goynuk, Kirish, Camyuve.

Families with children should go to Tekirova. Here you will find the best beaches, tangerine trees, pine groves, underwater caves with dolphins.

The most beautiful bays are located in the fishing village of Camyuve. Due to the presence in the water of mollusks with a cover of phosphorus, it seems that at night the water is highlighted. Great place for a romantic trip. Here you can ride a quad bike and go on a safari.

If you went on vacation with your family, children in Kemer will not be bored either. The largest dinopark in Europe will meet you with a large number of exhibits. Many dinosaurs move and growl.

Goynuk, Kirish and Beldibi

Going to Goynuk, you will not regret it either. Here is Mount Tahtali, whose height above sea level is 2365 meters. From here you can explore the surroundings and enjoy the beauties of the Mediterranean coast. You can swim in the bays with clear water. A huge number of fish restaurants will offer you their menu.

Fans of diving should stay in Kirish.

In Beldibi you can go rafting, there is also coniferous fresh air and pebble beaches.

The best hotels in the city center

For lovers of luxury relaxation, you can offer five-star hotels in the center of Kemer.

  1. Queen's Park Le Jardin Resort. Babysitting services, tennis court, mini market, sauna, spa, Turkish bath, library, currency exchange, parking, bar.
  2. Crystal Aura Beach Resort & Spa. Room service, buffet, Turkish bath, private beach area, babysitting services, gift shop, mini market, hairdresser.
  3. Simena Hotel. Free parking, laundry, dry cleaning, bar, restaurant, snack bar, night club.




You can find 4 star hotels in the center of Kemer:

  1. NOVIA GELIDONYA. The hotel is located on the second line. More suitable for youth recreation. Meals are organized on an all-inclusive basis. Private beach with free sunbeds, umbrellas. SPA center and Turkish bath.
  2. Garden Resort Bergamot. An excellent hotel complex for a family or romantic getaway. While staying at the hotel, you can take walks in the orange garden. An Italian restaurant, a patisserie on the terrace and 5 bars are at your service.
  3. AMBASSADOR PLAZA. An excellent complex with its own garden is suitable for a romantic getaway. Buffet, three meals a day, round-the-clock bars. The rooms are single, double and economy.

Cheap hotels in the center of Kemer

Many people prefer an economical vacation. Then it is best to stay in a hotel in Turkey, in the center of Kemer, under the name Armeria Hotel. The three-star hotel is designed for 120 guests. On the territory there is a restaurant with local and European cuisine, a bar, a Turkish bath, a playground. Around night clubs, bars, cafes, souvenir shops.





There is also a mini-hotel Idyras Hotel. Single, double, triple rooms are offered by the hotel in the center of Kemer to its visitors. The hotel has a patio with exotic trees. It offers guests - a playground, bicycles for walking and good food.

Inexpensive hotel in Kemer - "Camyuva Beach Hotel". The price includes breakfast. There are rooms with a beach view, the rooms are air-conditioned, there are non-smoking rooms. The hotel is not located on the beach, you have to walk to the sea, but there are pools on the territory, which is suitable for lovers of "lazy" relaxation. The cheapest standard room with free internet, a minibar and air conditioning.

A much cheaper cost will cost a vacation at the Orange Garden Hotel. The hotel is given only one star, but it has its own pool, free parking, restaurant, non-smoking rooms.
