Demarcation criteria

In the article, we will divide the entire smartphone industry into 4 parts: budget devices, the middle class, above the average, as well as top gadgets. If we talk about digital expression, then the scope is approximately the following: up to 10 thousand, from 10 to 20, from 20 to 30 and over 30 thousand rubles per device.

Budgetary

Obviously, the most popular smartphones in this category are the best-selling, in principle, due to the low cost of one device. Judging by the reviews on the electronics sites, as well as on the statistics published on the mobile retailer sites, representatives of the brands Samsung, Lenovo, Nokia, and LG are popular.

On the one hand, the gadget has a very affordable price (quite capable of competing with the cost of Chinese smartphones). On the other hand, this is the Samsung brand, known worldwide for the high quality of its products. These two factors determining the choice in favor of Star Plus are complemented by a 4-inch screen with a resolution of 800 by 480, support for two SIM cards, as well as preinstalled Android 4.1.
