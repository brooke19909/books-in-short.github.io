USB heated mug: specs, reviews

A mug of your favorite drink is the most desirable for those who have to sit at the computer for a long time or for drivers who are stuck in traffic for several hours. Of course, you can use a thermos, but it is large and bulky and it is not always convenient to carry with you.

Today, engineers in the field of IT-sphere offer a wide range of useful trifles that facilitate a long stay at a PC. A modern gadget is not only a phone or tablet, it can still be such an inexpensive, but very necessary accessory, like a USB heated mug. If you still do not know what it is and how it works, it's time to introduce you to this miracle of technology. In addition, this is a great gift for people spending a lot of time at the computer.





USB Heated Mug - Great

Do you know the situation when you sit at a computer for a long time and forget about the brewed coffee, which soon cools down and you have to get up, go to the kitchen to wash the mug and make a fresh hot drink? Agree, this is very inconvenient, especially if you have to postpone an important matter that does not accept delay, and you, as luck would have it, lose a lot of time. The solution may be a mug with USB heating, which can be of two types.

The first option is the simplest - a mug with a built-in heater. It is enough to connect it to the computer, and your drink will be hot for a long time. Only in this case you will have to endure the presence of the cable.

If you want to get something more practical, take a look at the coasters to the mugs, which provide long-term preservation of temperature in a cordless way. For example, there is a more advanced version - a stand for mugs with heating from USB. We will talk more about it below.





What is a stand for heating mugs?

In IT-souvenir shops you can find a great variety of varieties of such coasters: backlit, with connectors for other devices, and even complete with their own mug, but the essence remains the same for everyone. The device is a stand, power switch and a USB cable with a length of a meter. All you need to do is connect the device to the PC connector and put a mug of hot drink on top.

The gadget is designed to maintain the temperature of a hot drink, and not boil it again, so you should not expect that the stand for a cup with a USB heating will make your tea hot again if it has completely cooled down.

Another important point - the mug for such a stand should be with a flat metal bottom, so that there is better heat transfer. In this regard, the circles included in the gadget kit are good.
