Villa "Magic", Crimea: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Each of us dreams of relaxation. At least once a year, you need to change the situation, forget about problems and life troubles, turn off your mobile phone and enjoy the silence, calm and measured. Such a vacation can only give the sea. The sea calms, gives a sense of harmony, becomes a source of energy and vitality. All roads are open for a modern tourist: he can fly to Asia, enjoy the exotic and color of Asian resorts, be imbued with local philosophy and culture, he can give preference to Europe with stunning beaches, luxurious resorts and lots of attractions, and he can relax quite well without leaving the country.

Crimea is what we want to offer you. It is famous for its unique nature, majestic mountains, cozy bays, immersed in the green of unspoiled nature and wide beaches with clear sea water. Of course, some time ago, to visit the Crimea, it was necessary to draw up a passport and cross the border, but now that it has become a Russian republic, the situation has changed radically. Moreover, tourism in Crimea is on the rise: new hotels are being rebuilt, and the infrastructure of resorts is developing. Of course, in many respects the quality of rest depends on how carefully and responsibly the potential tourist approaches the choice of hotel or hotel. Even if you plan only to spend the night in the room, the cleanliness in it, the order and availability of the necessary equipment, a separate bathroom with hot water are the minimum that every tourist counts on. How do you like the villa? We offer a detailed and objective review of a rather popular option - Villa "Magic" (Crimea). What living conditions can she offer to modern tourists? What can they count on?

Description

So, Villa "Magic" is a 3 * hotel (Crimea, Saki), located in the small Crimean village of Novofedorovka. The villa is located on the second coastline, that is, it is only 300 meters from the coast. For Crimea, this is rare, so be sure to pay attention to proximity to the sea. The village itself is located in the western region of Crimea, which largely determines the specifics of rest in it. The hot steppes in combination with the sea breeze create excellent conditions for an unforgettable beach holiday.





In fact, the location is hardly a strong point of the hotel. Tourists complain that all the attractions are far enough away. The only way to get to them is by public transport. Of course, you can use the hotel shuttle, but in this case, each trip will be quite expensive for you. That is why more and more tourists come here with personal cars.

Territory

The territory of the villa, although small, but quite beautiful, well-groomed, literally immersed in greenery. Many gazebos for vacationers, there is a platform for cooking barbecue. The garden staff take care of the garden every day, put it in order, fight weeds, plant new plants.

Description of rooms

If you liked the Villa "Magic" (Crimea), you decided to stay here, you are probably interested in a detailed description of the rooms with an indication of the technical equipment. So, each of them has a private bathroom with shower and hairdryer, a set of bath towels and cosmetics. All vacationers can count on a TV with satellite channels, air conditioning and wireless high-speed Internet. Vacationers note that the signal level is excellent, the speed is enough even to watch movies and programs in real time. Moreover, each room has an ironing board and an iron, now you can bring your things in order at any convenient time, and completely free of charge. The rooms have a minimal set of dishes and a kettle. A huge drawback is the lack of a mosquito net on the windows, even in the season of mosquitoes there are so many that guests will have to stock up on protective equipment.

There is not much furniture, but it is all functional and practical: a wardrobe, beds and bedside tables, upholstered furniture. Not all rooms have a balcony, which is why if it is important for you to be sure to express your wishes when booking a room. Each room has a rather large mirror, which was noted by almost all vacationers. The furniture is mostly clean and modern. Of course, some rooms need redecorating.





Plumbing in the bathroom is working, sometimes there are problems with the supply of hot water, but they are temporary.

Infrastructure

Villa "Magic" (3 *, Crimea) boasts a good infrastructure. In addition to the 24-hour reception, the villa has a fairly spacious parking lot, which is under 24-hour security. Now guests arriving in a private car will not have to worry about parking and safety of their car. The territory of the villa, the path is not too big, but it has a well-equipped barbecue area. If you liked the Villa "Magic" (3 * hotel, Crimea), you can use it. Vacationers note that quite a lot of hotel guests gather here in the evening who have a great time and share their emotions from the rest with each other.





A huge drawback of the villa from several buildings is the lack of an elevator - vacationers after a tiring road have to carry things themselves up the steep stairs.

Nutrition

If you want to taste delicious dishes of national cuisine, enjoy ripe and juicy fruits, come to Crimea, Saki. Villa "Magic" offers meals on the system "boarding house". Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the villa's restaurant. Here you can taste dishes of national, European and Russian cuisine. They cook well, the menu is not the most varied, but certainly no one will remain hungry.

All vacationers can visit local restaurants, cafes and taste delicious national delicacies there, by the way, very inexpensively.

Service

Hotels in Crimea are just starting to develop, while the level of service offered is difficult to compare with what you will be offered at foreign resorts. Nevertheless, the villa "Magic" (Crimea) offers excellent service. All tourists are waited by the friendly and responsible staff, the villa staff are ready to answer all questions and organize the rest of a dream. At the information desk you can get to know the resort better, get a detailed map and even sign up for an excursion program. For an additional fee, you can use the transport to the airport or train station. And all this is Villa "Magic" (Crimea). Reviews of real vacationers also focus on room service. They are cleaned daily: all surfaces are put in order, plumbing is cleaned, towels and bed linen are changed.





Entertainment and sports

The hotel has enough entertainment for every taste - you will definitely not be bored here. All fans can play billiards, visit the sauna and take a steam bath with a broom. By the way, if you come to Crimea (Saki), the hotel-villa "Magic" will offer you the services of a professional massage therapist.

On the territory of the villa there are two swimming pools: outdoor and indoor heated. For Crimea, this is a rarity; not all hotels have such luxury. If you arrive here in early spring or fall, a heated pool will be a great alternative to water. Thanks to this, the Villa "Magic" (Crimea) can receive vacationers all year round and always ensure a high quality of relaxation. The only thing, some vacationers note the presence of chlorine in the water, which only negatively affects swimming procedures. The administration of the villa needs to be more attentive to the issue of cleanliness and disinfection, and not to spoil the quality of the water with chlorine, which causes an allergic skin reaction. There is an excellent playground for children on site.





Sea and beach

The sea is only 300 meters from the villa, so all vacationers can enjoy the sunsets and sunrises. The sea is clean, the beach is quite large, even in season there is enough space for all vacationers. Sports equipment and water sports equipment rental is available on the beach. Moreover, here you can get scuba diving lessons, board riding from real professionals. For an additional fee on the beach you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas - the prices are reasonable. Literally every 20 meters on the beach there are locker rooms, showers, toilets, there is also a fountain with drinking water.

Sunset in the sea is gentle, the bottom is sandy, so families with small children will be comfortable resting on the coast. More recently, an excellent promenade was built along the coast, which has become a kind of local attraction.

To summarize

In general, the Villa "Magic" (Crimea) is a good option for vacationers in the village of Novofedorovka. Of course, there is enormous potential and growth opportunities. It is necessary to work on the service and quality of service, to carry out small cosmetic repairs in some rooms, to replace appliances and plumbing. In general, this is a fairly budget hotel with good infrastructure and excellent prospects, located near the coast. We advise you to pay attention to this option if you decide to spend your vacation in Crimea. We sincerely hope that you will like Magic Villa (3 * Hotel, Crimea, Saki) and you will have a great time here.
