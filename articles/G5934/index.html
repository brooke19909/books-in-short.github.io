The benefits of a holiday in the village Dolzhanskaya

This place is ideal for relaxation, so most of the village is occupied by hotels. Dolzhanskaya is in great demand not only among Russian tourists, it is quite popular with foreign tourists. The main advantage of this resort area is its unique climate and clean air, because within a radius of several tens of kilometers there are no industrial enterprises.



The salty breeze coming from the sea saturates the air with ions and makes it extremely favorable for the health of vacationers. In summer, the weather is dry and warm, precipitation rarely occurs, the temperature is stable, about 30 degrees. Due to the lack of high humidity, breathing here is much easier than in other resort areas. The beach season lasts several months. Dolzhanskaya welcomes guests to its hotels from May to September.
