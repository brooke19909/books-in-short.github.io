Stocks

A stock is a type of securities that certifies the right to a share of its owner (shareholder) in the ownership of the company and makes it possible to manage it and receive dividends (income).

Bonds

The bond fixes the debt obligations of the company to the owner of the paper. A bond holder, unlike a shareholder, does not manage the company and does not have the right to receive dividends from its profits. The owners of this security are paid interest income, and at the end of the maturity or at maturity, a nominal value is issued.

Risk comparison

Stocks are a riskier investment than bonds, since dividends depend only on the successful development of the company. The bonds are secured by the asset and property of the enterprise.

Is it worth investing in a business?

In order to answer the question, you need to understand that by investing in a business, you get a certain percentage of its profit. It is also worth noting that you can invest both in your own and in someone else's business. Thus, even if you do not have any ideas for starting your own business, you can invest in someone else's. Money can be invested both in a ready-made business, and make investments in business projects. These are the so-called startups. The funds invested in such projects are venture capital investments. What it is? This is the name of the funds that are invested in young risk projects, usually banks refuse to cooperate with them, therefore private investors are attracted. Typically, a process is characterized by long-term investment in a company. Revenue generation may also vary. Some investors receive only passive income (percent of profits), while others receive active income (they directly manage the business). Do not worry if you do not have the full amount to contribute to the business, because you can give not 100%, but for example 20-30% and join other investors (equity participation). In general, 100% investing in a business is a very risky business, it is much safer to share. Investing in a business has both a number of advantages and disadvantages.
