Model description

The Indesit IWUC 4105 washing machine has a classic design. It is made in white. Type of loading frontal. The drum is made of stainless steel. In one cycle, you can wash 4 kg of dry laundry. On average, about 40 liters of water and up to 1 kW of electricity are consumed.

The Indesit IWUC 4105 model belongs to the small-sized type. Its width is 33 cm. The total weight of the device is about 52 kg. The efficiency of the washing machine is fully consistent with modern requirements. The energy and washing class is A, the spin cycle is C. The height is 85 cm, however it can be reduced with a removable cover. This point is very important for those who purchase a device for installation in furniture.

Cons washing machine

So, the time has come to talk about the shortcomings of the Indesit trademark device. The first thing that distinguishes the largest part of buyers is the noise. During washing, the machine is almost inaudible, but when you turn on the rinse and spin mode, the machine starts to produce too loud sound. And sometimes it even turns into a whistle. Also among buyers there are complaints about the quality of rinsing. Unfortunately, very often a lot of detergent remains in things, which is why you have to constantly use the Super Rinse function, and this leads to additional energy costs. Significant disadvantages include vibration, its level is significantly increased if the spin mode on the Indesit IWUC 4105 washing machine is turned on.
