Hotel Sunny Hotel Nha Trang (Nha Trang, Vietnam): overview, description and reviews

Vietnam is a country that has recently opened its doors to tourists. Nevertheless, the infrastructure is constantly developing here, and every year more and more travelers come here. If we are talking about relaxing on the beach, then one of the most popular resorts is the city of Nha Trang. When choosing a place to stay, many people prefer the Sunny Hotel Nha Trang.

Of course, for travelers it is important to get all the information about the hotel before giving preference to a particular place. So what can a hotel really offer a tourist? Is it possible to spend a really pleasant vacation here?

Vietnam, hotel Sunny Hotel Nha Trang: description of the territory and location





Naturally, the location, as well as the infrastructure, are important details for many tourists. Where is the hotel Sunny Hotel Nha Trang 3 * located? Nha Trang - a picturesque resort town on the seashore. It was here, almost in the center, that a hotel was built. This is a lively area where there are always a lot of vacationers. Therefore, you can count on a developed infrastructure: shops, restaurants and cafes, public transport stops within walking distance. The international airport is approximately 38 km away.

The hotel itself is small, and it is a seven-story building, built in a modern style. Own territory is available, but it is small. Nevertheless, the building itself has everything you need, including even a rooftop pool.

What rooms offer tourists?





This hotel is not too big: on its territory there are 50 rooms. All of them are quite spacious and bright, with large windows, balconies or terraces (depending on the floor). From the room you can enjoy views of the city and the sea.

Of course, there is the necessary furniture, including large beds, wardrobes, bedside tables, soft sofas. Guests are provided with a standard set of household appliances, which provides the maximum level of comfort. For example, all rooms are equipped with a modern air conditioning system, you can also watch TV, however, there are few satellite channels, mostly local TV shows.

Guests can also use a telephone and a convenient safe with a secure lock. As for the minibar, it is available, but only non-carbonated drinking water is filled for free.

The bathroom is attached to the room, where you will find a comfortable shower and modern plumbing. Guests are provided with clean towels (changed during daily cleaning), as well as shampoo, soap and other hygiene products. Comfortable slippers are provided.

Dining at the hotel: what to count on?





The rooms at Sunny Hotel Nha Trang include daily breakfast. They take place in a cozy room of the main restaurant in the form of receptions. According to reviews, the menu here is quite good: there are muesli, fresh fruits, vegetables, hot and meat dishes, as well as some traditional meat snacks. Of course, you can count on chilled drinks, coffee and tea. By the way, in the lobby there is a small bar where they offer really high-quality drinks and cocktails, prepared according to original recipes.

In addition, you can go to lunch or dinner at the same restaurant, however, you will be prepared to order, respectively, you need to pay for food separately.

Relax by the water: beach, amenities and affordable entertainment





Many tourists choose this place to spend their holidays, counting on a relaxing holiday on the seashore. What does the Sunny Hotel Nha Trang offer in this regard? Vietnam is a country famous for its incredibly beautiful beaches. One of them is located just 100 meters from the hotel, a convenient trail leads to the shore.

This is a public site. On the other hand, it is always clean, despite the abundance of tourists, you can always find a cozy and even secluded corner. For a small fee, you can use a large comfortable sun lounger. But beach towels are issued on site.

Travelers can count on all kinds of water sports: boating, boating, water skiing, windsurfing and diving lessons, parasailing and sailing, beach volleyball, in short, whatever you like.

Additional services for hotel guests





Will the accommodation at the hotel Sunny Hotel Nha Trang 3 * be comfortable? Guest reviews confirm that here you can find everything for a pleasant pastime. For example, the presence of dry cleaning and laundry removes the burden of domestic problems. By the way, for little money you can even give clothes for ironing.

Despite the small territory of its own, the hotel is equipped with parking. Nearby there is a rental point where you can rent a car or any other type of transport, including bicycles. This service is not so expensive, but it greatly simplifies the life of travelers, opening up new opportunities for exploring the country. Parking, by the way, is free.

At the hotel you can exchange currency, and the rate is quite favorable. Also, guests can use the luggage storage room, leaving unnecessary things there for a while.

The hotel is often used for the so-called business leisure, so there is a well-equipped business center where you can use computer equipment, send a fax or even a letter through the post office. By the way, throughout the territory there is access to the Internet - this is one of the important points for almost every traveler.

How to spend time at the hotel?





As already mentioned, the hotel has a small but well-equipped pool. Water is cleaned every day, and around on a spacious terrace there are wide sun loungers and sun umbrellas. This is a place where guests not only take sunbaths and relax, here they get to know each other and have fun.

The hotel also has a tour desk. Vietnam is a country that is of interest to a traveler. A rich history, a peculiar culture and a lot of architectural sights are what many tourists like. The hotel will tell you about the most interesting places in the vicinity, as well as help you choose and purchase a guided tour. By the way, the prices here are about the same as in city agencies.

Family fun: baby-friendly

Parents planning a vacation with their children, of course, are primarily interested in the availability of amenities. To begin with, it is worth saying that for small tourists there are different discounts on accommodation (depends on the age of the child and some other factors).

Sunny Hotel Nha Trang has family apartments, but if you are accommodated in a standard room, you can still count on an extra bed (folding bed). As for nutrition, the menu has healthy and wholesome dishes, so you can pick up something tasty for the child.

The on-site swimming pool has a small children's section. There is a shallow depth, and the water is clean and warm, so the children can splash and play while their parents are resting nearby. You can count on a beach holiday, because the entrance to the sea is gradual - the shore is perfect for bathing a child. Well, of course, in the city center there are many entertainment venues where your child can get a lot of vivid emotions.

Hotel Sunny Hotel Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam): traveler reviews





Probably everyone knows that in just a few minutes of talking with people who have already managed to visit a particular hotel, you can find out a lot of useful and even secret information. What do they say about the hotel Sunny Hotel Nha Trang?

Feedback is only positive. The hotel is small, but quiet and comfortable, and the territory is beautifully furnished and decorated. It is always a pleasure to spend time here. The rooms themselves are comfortable, there is the necessary furniture, working equipment and a constant supply of hot water. By the way, supplies of towels, soaps, shampoos and other hygiene products are replenished every day.

Travelers pay attention to the professionalism and courtesy of the staff, who will always greet you with a smile, help you solve problems, tell you about the most interesting places, and go up to your room right after the call. Cleaning is carried out very carefully, no one complains about the presence of dirt.

The food is quite decent. The menu may not be very diverse, but the products are always fresh, food is served in sufficient quantities. Tourists recommend the Sunny Hotel Nha Trang for a fun and relaxing holiday by the sea.
