Hotel The Three By Apk 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Phuket Island is a world famous resort. It has everything: beaches with white sand, unreal beauty of the sea, iridescent with thousands of emeralds, palm groves that cast a shadow on tired travelers, the gentle sun, a kitchen that exudes exotic.





General description of the island

The island occupies the southeastern part of Thailand. Outwardly, it looks like an uneven pearl. Phuket is famous for its unusual beaches. The clear sea, cliffs and amazing coral reefs attract thousands of tourists to these shores. The size of the island is considerable. Most of it is buried in tropical greenery. There are also coconut plantations, many waterfalls and beautiful parks. Together, all this is an ideal place for an unforgettable vacation.

But the island is not only famous for its unique nature. And this is confirmed by numerous reviews. Good, welcoming, hospitable people live here. Local attractions, unusual architecture, cozy comfortable hotels will make your stay truly unique. Phuket Island is considered one of the largest in Thailand. Moreover, he was awarded the status of a province.





How to get there

The island is 892 km from Bangkok. It only takes an hour to reach a place on the plane. In the same way, you can get here from Singapore, Malaysia and Hong Kong. The mainland and the island are connected by a dam along which the highway is laid.

What to see?

We can say that the island is a separate universe. Here, tourists are fascinated by unique architectural structures. Especially colorful in the capital of the same name. The air is saturated with notes of India, China and Portugal. These countries are reflected in the local architecture. The capital of the island has a fairly developed infrastructure. There are numerous shops, cozy cafes, restaurants and bars. You can also have fun in one of the entertainment centers. Local natural beauties are reflected in numerous green areas with impenetrable jungle. The whole world famous park located on the very edge of the southern part of the island. It offers magnificent sunset views.

There are other attractions here. You can visit the aquarium of the Biological Center or see with your own eyes how pearls are grown on marine plantations. You can go to an unusual village where folklore events take place twice a day, allowing you to plunge deeper into the atmosphere of Thailand. The most ancient inhabitants of the island are the so-called sea gypsies. You will definitely see their settlement in Phuket.

This island is truly famous for its rich history. There are many places, the visit of which will leave an unforgettable mark in your memory. Numerous attractions will help you get acquainted with the history of Thailand. Naturally, a trip to such a distant place should be carefully planned. You need to take care of living on the island in advance. A wonderful hotel will be described below, a stay in which will leave a lot of positive impressions. This is also indicated by the positive reviews of the lucky ones who have already stayed here.





Hotel Description The Three By Apk 3 *

The hotel is relatively new. More recently, it was made available for public viewing by the Razot Group agro-industrial complex. Staying at a hotel, guests find themselves in the heart of Patong Beach's life. After all, The Three By Apk 3 is located right next to this beach. Staying here will be especially appreciated by young energetic personalities. They will undoubtedly be delighted with what is happening here days and nights.

From the hotel you can walk to the Paradise Complex, Jungseilong Shopping Center, Ban Zan Fresh Market and, of course, Patong Beach. All this is a 5-10 minute walk from the hotel. The whole nightlife of Phuket is centered on Bangla Road. This place is also within walking distance of The Three By Apk 3. It can be reached in 20 minutes.





Number of rooms

The hotel offers 185 rooms. All of them are divided into three categories: "Superior" (there is a shower or bath), "Deluxe" and family-type rooms. Each has a 32-inch LCD panel. Almost all international channels are available for viewing. Air conditioning, bath and tea making facilities are also provided.

Superior Room

The apartments are 20 square meters. m. It can comfortably accommodate two adults and one child. No extra bed available. The room is decorated with exquisite taste. You can choose between twin beds or a large double. The room is equipped with air conditioning, telephone, plasma screen with cable TV, mini fridge, safe, table and chairs. The bathroom has a shower, hairdryer. Free tea / coffee making facilities are also provided. It is possible to connect the Internet for an additional fee. The number is locked with an electronic key. The apartments have a balcony. In the form of a bonus, two bottles of clean water await guests in the room.





Deluxe Room

These apartments at The Three By Apk 3 are more spacious than the previous ones. Room occupies 28 square meters. It has a balcony with a jacuzzi. From here amazing city landscapes and mountains open. The room can accommodate three adults (a rollaway bed is provided) or two adults with a child. Extra bed is not provided. All rooms are equipped with a large double bed. Comfortable temperature is maintained due to the presence of air conditioning.

Guests can contact staff via the telephone in their room. There is also a TV, small fridge, microwave, safe, dining table. On the balcony there is a small table with chairs. Guests can make their own coffee directly in the room, for this there are all the necessary accessories and drinking water. In addition to a jacuzzi, the bathroom has a shower. A hairdryer, slippers and bathrobes are also provided free of charge. An electronic room key is issued at the reception. For an additional fee, you can get a wi-fi password.





Family Room

Vacationers have at their disposal 35 squares of the square. The room is designed to accommodate three adults (two adults with one child are possible). Room does not provide extra bed. But there is a full dining room, a spacious bedroom with three single beds. Also in the room there are all those attributes for a comfortable stay, as in the apartments described above.

Hotel Infrastructure

Three by Apk Patong Phuket 3 has a well-developed infrastructure. In all recreation areas you will be able to access the Internet for free. In the rooms, as already mentioned, connection is provided for a nominal fee. The hotel has a rooftop luxury swimming pool and sunbathing area. There is a bar by the water bowl. Tasty lunch or dinner can be in the restaurant located on site.

Room service is only available during daylight hours. Laundry and postal services are available. A van with a driver and a bellboy are available upon request. The hotel has a well-equipped conference room. You can also visit the local spa. Reception is open around the clock. It is possible to rent a moped, if necessary, call a doctor or ambulance.





Pool Reviews

The pool is located directly on the roof of The Three by Apk 3 Hotel (Phuket). Guest reviews about this place deserve special attention. Many say that they received indescribable emotions from observing how the sun sets and night falls on the city. The rooftop pool offers amazing views. From here you can watch how life flows in the village, as well as enjoy unforgettable sunsets. The pool is located on the roof of a nine-story building. The stay here is just breathtaking. The pool is open from eight in the morning until seven in the evening.

Description of the conference room

The Three by Apk 3 Hotel (3, Phuket) has a well-equipped room for hosting various events. Moreover, it can be either business conferences, or private parties or solemn meetings. The hall can accommodate up to 80 people, if, for example, a cocktail party is planned. Seats are designed for 60 people. In the hall there is all the necessary audio and video equipment, there is access to the Internet.

Nutrition

The Three by Apk 3 has great dining options. The main one is on the ground floor of the hotel. From here visitors can enjoy the contemplation of the busiest street located on the waterfront. The restaurant serves predominantly traditional Thai dishes. Surprisingly, recipes were collected from all over Thailand. Here vacationers are happy to enjoy the unique tastes of wholesome food. The restaurant can comfortably accommodate up to 80 people. The institution operates from 6.30 to 23.00.

The Three by Apk 3 also has a cozy lounge bar. Here you can have a great time with friends, having missed a glass of another favorite wine. The range of cocktails, juices and other soft and strong drinks is amazing. The hotel bar is open from half past seven in the morning until eleven in the evening, the pool bar from 10.00 to 19.00. What do vacationers say about The Three by Apk 3? Reviews about this place are sometimes contradictory, but mostly positive.

What you should pay attention to?

When checking into the room, a deposit of $ 100 is required, which is then returned. It is recommended to immediately rent a motorbike for the entire period of rest. So it turns out much cheaper (about 200 baht per day). Scooter is best taken in specialized stores. Street vendors are best avoided. Almost all of them are scammers. You can do this at the bike rental service, which is located near The Three by Apk 3 (Thailand, Phuket). There are many positive reviews about this rental. Its owner is a decent honest man. The passport is left as a deposit. Upon delivery of the vehicle, the document is returned. For rented bikes, parking is provided on site. At night, they are under the reliable guard of staff.

It is necessary to refuel a scooter at a gas station. It can be safely left on the street or beach. There are parking spaces for this vehicle everywhere. The main thing is that the steering wheel is closed. People who visited Thailand claim that without an iron horse, rest would be boring. On the bike you can go around almost all the beaches of the island. And without it, you will have to be content with visiting only the swimming area located near the hotel. Phuket is a huge island and difficult to get around on foot. On the beach, you also need to discuss in advance the cost of certain services. Thais are cunning people who cheat at every step.
