Hotels in Evpatoria by the sea: a description of the best

Pleasant climate, healthy air, saturated with aromas of steppe grasses and the sea, a long strip of sandy beaches, the sea warmed up in June and the treatment base remaining from the Soviet years made modern Evpatoria a popular and popular resort.

It is important that the city stretched along the line of the sea. Therefore, almost all hotels in Yevpatoria on the beach or within walking distance from it.





general information

Evpatoria is an ancient city located along the Kalamitsky Bay of the Black Sea. Conventionally, the city is divided into several zones, where there are almost all hotels in Evpatoria on the seashore:

  • Simferopolskaya street is located at the entrance to the city, on one side there is a strip of sandy beaches, on the other, the salt lake Sasyk borders;
  • Tereshkova embankment - the historical part, the so-called Old Town, where the main attractions are concentrated;
  • Gorky embankment leads along the beach line to the center of Yevpatoriya, most of the health resorts and children's sanatoriums are located here;
  • In recent years, many modern hotels have been built around the salt-water healing lake of Moinaki.

Depending on the desire to undergo treatment in a sanatorium, to explore local attractions or just relax, it is worth choosing one of the city areas.

Hotels on Simferopolskaya Street

Simferopolskaya street is the 1st coastline. Many hotels are located directly on the beaches.

Hotel Ribera Resort & Spa provides guests with rooms of different classes and levels of comfort - from the "standard" to the "Grand Suite".

The hotel is located on a sandy beach, where guests are provided with sun loungers and umbrellas. Own sports corner and playground, as well as a small cozy promenade allow you to spend all your free time on the street.

The hotel has several restaurants and bars, including on the beach and poolside.

The large aquatic complex attracts attention to the hotel even in winter. Sauna, steam bath, hammam, massage and beauty treatments and a large indoor pool are located in a separate hotel building.

Among the best hotels of Yevpatoriya on the seashore with its own beach, the Silver Breeze Villa is always called. Single and duplex suites, breakfasts and a playground will make your stay a pleasant one. No need to go to the sea - the villa is located directly on the coast.

On Simferopolskaya street, among the many hotels, the mini-hotel Lyon is popular. After all, the hotel offers its guests rooms with sea views away from the noisy city. From the threshold of the room to the sea there are literally two steps, where tourists are waiting for sunbeds, awnings and a bar. Full meals may be included in the room rate.

On the Simferopolskaya street, hotels go one after another: Laguna, Breeze, Arsenal, Oasis and others. In the same area there are excellent hotels "On the Squadron" and "Dzheval".

You can get to the city center by tram, minibuses or taxi.





Hotels on Tereshkova Embankment

Tereshkova embankment in the evening becomes a place of crowded festivities. There are many attractions, a yacht club and a jetty, museums and architectural monuments, restaurants and cafes are located nearby.

Such hotels in Yevpatoria on the seashore, such as "Trekhgorka", Ritsk, Argo, "Crimea" and others, provide their guests with the opportunity to relax every evening on the wonderful promenade.

The newly built Ritsk Hotel offers 22 rooms of various categories; the hotel has a restaurant and a pastry shop.

A small park with southern plants adorns the territory of the Trekhgorka boarding house. Pergolas, children's play areas, barbecue - all this is created for the convenience of guests. The guesthouse's own beach is nearby, you can get there on a fun tram or take a walk.

Argo Hotel is a small hotel located next to the Crimean War Museum and the Black Sea Pirate Museum. The pizzeria restaurant, the nearby beach and the attractions attract guests with children.





Hotels on Gorky Embankment

The best hotels in Evpatoria by the sea are located in the central part of the city.

The hotel complex "Ukraine Palace" is located at the beginning of the walking pedestrian street Frunze leading to the sea. The hotel deservedly has 4 stars, offering guests:

  • breakfast included; children's menu;
  • cafes, bars, restaurant, pizzeria;
  • aqua complex (outdoor pools, jacuzzi, children's areas);
  • children's playground, working animators;
  • simulator, beauty parlor;
  • night club.

The high level of service and convenient location of the hotel allow you to enjoy your vacation without any problems.

Comfortable rooms are offered to vacationers by a small, only 64 rooms, hotel TES-hotel Resort & SPA, which is located in a park area. Not only the pool, the nearby beach, saunas and the bath complex attract guests to the resort, but also the polite staff, cleanliness and comfort in the rooms.

The rooms of various categories (super apartments, VIP, luxury, 1- and 2-room apartments) are offered to vacationers by the modern Liana hotel. On the summer playground there is a swimming pool, a gazebo for relaxing, for kids - a play area and a slide for descending into the pool. For those who prefer the sea, nearby is the hotel beach with everything you need. The spa complex of the Liana Hotel welcomes guests all year round.

Service is gradually developing in the resort city of Yevpatoriya: hotels on the beach "all inclusive" are becoming more and more popular. One of such fashionable complexes is the Empire Hotel. The 4-storey building of the hotel is located 60 meters from the beach and the central city park. The hotel offers:

  • rooms of different categories (standard, luxury, apartments);
  • all-inclusive meals, including alcoholic drinks and fruits in the bars, cafes and restaurants of the hotel;
  • access to the pool, sauna and beach;
  • animation for children and adults;
  • children's and sports ground.

To date, in Yevpatoria (Crimea), all-inclusive hotels on the seafront with pools and spa complexes are no longer rare. Among these comfortable hotels, Juliana, Grotto, Julia, Russian Sea, Breeze and others have proven themselves well.

Hotels in Moinaki

In the resort part of Yevpatoriya, hotels by the sea with a swimming pool are located near Lake Moynaki. This is a quiet picturesque area, where there are many children's health resorts. Getting to the center from there is not difficult.

One of the best modern hotel complexes in Yevpatoria is the Romanova Park Hotel, where modern infrastructure is hidden behind the old facade.

Wonderful comfortable rooms accommodate about 180 people. The private park has a pool where you can swim even at night. Sports grounds, a sauna, massage and beauty parlors, a children's playroom - all this makes the rest carefree and enjoyable. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis.

In the same area there is a boarding house "Lake of Dreams", which offers guests comfortable accommodation, service in the medical center, swimming pool, spa complex, tennis court and diving center.

In the area of Lake Moinaki, there are such hotels as Thalassa, Nevsky Bereg, Bee and others.





2017 prices

In 2017, the holiday season was pleased with some decrease in prices for vacations in Yevpatoriya.

The cheapest time is to relax in May-June: hotels in Yevpatoriya on the beach offer prices from 800 rubles per room.

The most expensive month is August. The cost of a room with all the amenities in a good hotel with a pool, a private beach and breakfast included is 5-15 thousand rubles.
