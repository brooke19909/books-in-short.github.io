Hotel Sofia 3 *, Krasnaya Polyana: overview, description, rooms and reviews

Krasnaya Polyana is a developing Russian resort with a mild climate and excellent ski slopes in winter. And in the summer there is a diversity of the plant world and fresh mountain air, heady with the aromas of flowers, mineral springs and many, many other natural attractions. One should try to find such a unique place on our planet.

Residents and guests of the Krasnodar Territory were lucky. They can get here from the city of Adler in almost half an hour along the modern freeway built for the 2014 Olympic Games, and stay for a few days in one of the resort's cozy hotels. For example, you can choose the hotel Sofia 3 * (Krasnaya Polyana).

Short description

Sofia is a 3 * hotel (Russia / Sochi / Krasnaya Polyana), located in a five-story building with 17 comfortable rooms, a cozy restaurant and an Alpine-style hall with a fireplace.





Reception is located on the ground floor. There is also an area with upholstered furniture for guests to relax.

From the second to the fourth floor there are 15 residential rooms of various layouts. The top floor of the attic type is occupied by two luxury rooms.

For a complete rest of the guests at the hotel there are indoor gazebos, a bath complex and an outdoor pool, a playground for children, a fountain with live fish, a decorative well and a mill. A barbecue area has been allocated and improved.

The hotel supports the concept of a healthy lifestyle, for this reason smoking is prohibited on its territory.

Hotel Location

Sofia is a 3 * hotel (Krasnaya Polyana), which is located in a picturesque and environmentally friendly village, at the foot of Mount Achishkho, at an altitude of 600 meters above sea level and 700 meters from a federal road.





The distance to the center of Sochi is 70 kilometers, to the Adler airport - 35 kilometers, to the sea - 40 kilometers.

Hotel "Sofia" 3 * (Krasnaya Polyana) is removed from the cable cars. Closest to the hotel is the lower lift of the Gornaya Karusel ski resort (6 km), the closest to the hotel is Rosa Khutor (8 km). Almost at the same distance (6.7 and 6.9 km) from the hotel are the Gazprom (Laura) and Alpika-Service lifts, respectively.

To make it easier to get from the hotel with ski equipment to the ski resorts in winter, a free shuttle service is organized.

Number of rooms

Sofia is a 3 * hotel (Russia / Sochi / Krasnaya Polyana), which invites its guests to rooms of two categories:

  • one-room standard;
  • two-room suite.

Each hotel room has two twin beds or one double, wardrobe, nightstand, TV (5 channels), telephone, table with mirror, wall lamps and chairs.

The bathroom is equipped with a shower or bathtub, sink and toilet, a set of towels and hygiene items are prepared on the shelves.

In addition to accommodation, the price of the room also includes the use of wireless Internet throughout the hotel, an outdoor pool in the summer and a parking lot.

The maid in the rooms is cleaned every day, towels and bed linen are changed every three days.

Nutrition

Sofia is a 3 * hotel (Sochi / Krasnaya Polyana), which offers its vacationers to try dishes prepared according to Russian and European recipes in its own restaurant-bar on the ground floor of the building.





In winter, breakfast is included in the price and runs from 8 to 10 hours. Lunches and dinners on the menu for an additional fee. In the evening, the restaurant closes after the last customer leaves.

It features a widescreen TV, DVD, karaoke and hookah. Discos, corporate banquets and family celebrations are organized.

Hotel infrastructure

On its territory, the 3 * Sofia Hotel (Krasnaya Polyana) has a large outdoor unheated pool (25 sq. M.), Open during the summer, a sunbathing area with sun loungers, umbrellas, tables, chairs and a bath complex.



On the ground floor of the last there is a Russian bathhouse with a capacity of up to 10 people with various steam programs and professional massage, on the second floor there are exercise machines, table tennis, games, and a hookah. There is also a laundry room where you can give things for washing and professional ironing. At the same time, the cost of these services will not affect the family budget.

In winter, a storage dryer for sports equipment was organized. Bicycle rental works for vacationers in the summer, interesting excursions are organized by jeeps along mountain roads, by ATVs along the bottom of canyons, by horses along gorges, and much more.

What did vacationers like at the hotel?

Focusing on the brochures of travel agencies and information on the Internet, many Russians choose this hotel. They like Sofia (3 * hotel, Krasnaya Polyana). Rosa Khutor with its lifts, although far enough from it, but the Mountain Carousel complex is close. The cost of Skype there is lower, and the route is no worse.





Among the comments made by tourists on the Internet, there are many positive ones. Liked:

  • spacious and bright rooms with good soundproofing, almost new furniture;
  • delicious and nutritious breakfast, which is included in the price in winter;
  • nice and friendly staff;
  • surrounding silence;
  • the availability of grocery stores within walking distance;
  • presence on each floor of the ironing board and iron.

What caused negative emotions in the hotel guests?

There are also negative points that tourists note. Not everyone likes Sofia (3 * Hotel, Krasnaya Polyana). Reviews speak of the following disadvantages of the hotel, which you need to be prepared for when planning to stay here:

  • From public transport stops you need to climb to the hotel on the road uphill with heavy things, for many it is quite difficult. An inconvenient location is not a problem on your vehicle.
  • Lack of evening organized transfer from the ski lifts to the hotel. Since the location of the hotel is inconvenient, you have to take a taxi, and this entails additional costs.
  • The Wi-Fi channel cannot handle the heavy load. With a large number of users, the speed of the Internet decreases, it is impossible to work.
  • Limited number of television channels received.
  • Lack of a five-story building elevator. Climbing the upper floors with heavy things up steep stairs is difficult and uncomfortable.




In general, the Sofia Hotel 3 * (Krasnaya Polyana) is a nice small hotel with beautiful green territory and good service. Therefore, despite some negative reviews regarding the unsuccessful location, the hotel will be a good accommodation option for a budget holiday in the mountains both in summer and in winter.
