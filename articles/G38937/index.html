How to distinguish Chinese "iPhone" from the original: external differences

The next step in recognizing a copy is to examine the smartphone itself and work with it. First of all, we will evaluate its appearance. We already mentioned the highest build quality of the original Apple technology. If you remember this, then all kinds of defects on the phone case, even subtle, will indicate to you that there is a Chinese fake in front of you. The original is distinguished by the impeccable quality of the fit of all panels, the absence of backlashes, gaps, chips, creaks or depressions. The front panel must have a factory protective film. As a rule, the original iPhone model has a perforated tongue at the bottom. It is needed for quick and convenient removal of protection from the display.

And the most important feature that the original phone from Apple cannot have is a removable back panel! This iPhone is a monolithic device that cannot be disassembled manually. If you have a deliberately original iPhone, you can advise putting two devices side by side and compare them visually. "Chinese" will stand out with a more elongated case format.
