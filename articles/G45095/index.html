Business trip, family vacation, romantic weekend, corporate event - Pushkin Park Hotel in Buzuluk is ideally suited for these and many other purposes. Here you can relax from the hustle and bustle of big cities and everyday worries, enjoying quality service and admiring the beautiful surroundings.

Location

Park Hotel "Pushkin" in Buzuluk is located at the following address: May 1, 1a. It is a 5-minute walk from the city center, within walking distance of the Samara River and the picturesque park. Less than a kilometer from the hotel is a supermarket.
