Mobile phones Ritzviva S500C: reviews, descriptions, specifications, features

More and more often recently smartphones of unknown brands appear on store shelves. Every year their range is significantly increasing. However, how do you decide whether these models are worth the money? In order to dispel the uncertainty of one of these devices, this review was compiled, the hero of which was the RitzViva S500C mobile phone. The reviews of owners and specialists will help to understand the features and characteristics of this gadget.

The model went on sale in November 2015. During this time, many users have already been able to test it in operation. The most active of them shared their impressions, on the basis of which it became possible to determine all the strengths and weaknesses of this gadget.



Equipment

What can a customer expect when purchasing a RitzViva S500? Mobile phones of this manufacturer are equipped with a box of presentable appearance. This gadget was no exception. Externally, it is framed in white. On the front panel is a photograph of the device itself. At the top there is the brand name, and at the bottom - the model.

Inside the box there is a rigid substrate in which the apparatus lies. Underneath are packed a network adapter, a USB cable, and documentation. There is also a wired stereo headset. Additional elements are not provided, so it is recommended that the buyer immediately pay attention to accessories.





Design

There are several color solutions on sale in which the manufacturer introduced the RitzViva S500C mobile phone: golden, black, white, dark blue. In this matter, the buyer will have no problems choosing the most suitable option.

The arrangement of all elements is quite typical for a modern gadget. On the front panel, most of the screen. The perimeter frame is rather narrow. It becomes wider only in the upper and lower parts. This decision was made in order to place the necessary elements. Above the screen there is a hole in the earpiece, proximity sensor, front camera lens. The control panel consists of three touch buttons, it is located at the bottom of the gadget.

Case type - monoblock. The manufacturer used plastic for its manufacture. A silver frame runs along the perimeter of the side face. The phone weighs 170 g. The average dimensions are 145 √ó 71.5 √ó 9.2 mm. The model uses modern technology, thanks to which the developers were able to increase the level of impact resistance and dust / moisture resistance. Without mechanical buttons could not do. They are located on the right side edge, perform the function of locking and volume control. A mini-jack (3.5 mm) for connecting a wired stereo headset and a USB port are brought to the top end.

The back cover is less informative. It has only a two-position flash, the main camera lens and the company logo.

All owners of the Ritzviva S500C reviews in most cases leave positive. There are no special comments on the design. The only thing that can be seen in the exterior design is the ordinary style and lack of originality. If we talk about a holistic picture, then the gadget looks pretty typical.





Communications

What best describes the RitzViva S500C smartphone? Owner reviews! Most advanced users liked the availability of synchronization with a PC. Data can be transferred in several ways: using a USB cable and Bluetooth. The device supports Wi-Fi (802.11 channel). Also, the Internet can be accessed using GPRS and 3G.

The model has two slots for SIM cards. One of them is a standard format, the second is micro. It supports a 2G and 3G network device. Considering that 4G is still unstable, such characteristics are quite enough to ensure uninterrupted communication.

Ritzviva S500C: display reviews

When testing this model, experts drew buyers' attention to the type of matrix. This device uses the best technology - IPS. The screen has a diagonal of 5 inches. The image is displayed in good quality. Pixelization with the naked eye is invisible, since the density is 294 ppi. The display resolution is 1280 √ó 720 pixels. Screen type - capacitive, touch. It displays over 16 million shades. The developers have provided multi-touch support.

The owners noticed that on the street in any light, the information displayed on the screen is viewed quite clearly. The level of contrast is within normal limits. The color is realistic and saturated.





Hardware stuffing

What about the performance received smartphone RitzViva S500C (golden) reviews? It is worth noting that most of them are satisfactory.

The functionality of the gadget is provided by the Spreadtrum SC7731 chipset. It is based on four cores (Cortex-A7) operating at a frequency of 1300 MHz. The amount of RAM is sufficient. It is 1024 MB. Such storage provides uninterrupted operation of the device and relatively fast processing of existing processes.

The native memory in the device is 8192 Mb. According to many users, this is not enough for modern tasks. However, the developers provided an extension method and installed a slot for microSD and microSDHC memory cards in the device. The gadget supports an external drive up to 32 GB.

Battery

A rather powerful battery is equipped with a RitzViva S500C smartphone. Reviews that relate to this particular indicator are laudatory. Many buyers were pleased with the battery capacity of 3500 mAh. Its type is lithium polymer.

Often users with gadgets running on the Android operating system are faced with the problem of freezes. Therefore, it is very important when the battery is removable. This helps to cope in situations where the phone does not respond to button presses. Therefore, for the S500C, this is an undeniable advantage.





Operating System and Multimedia

The base of this model is the popular Android operating system. The developers used the current version 5.1. The interface is quite convenient, the icons are the optimal size. Buyers have no complaints about their location on the desktop. There is a curtain on which all the important elements are displayed. There is also a brightness control for the display. Quick access to this feature saves battery life.

Most users have no serious media comments. The device is equipped with a video and audio player. There is an FM radio, ringtones are set to mp3 format. To use a wired headset, the developers installed a standard type 3.5 mm jack. There is no need to use adapters.

As for the speakers, many users wanted the sound level to be slightly higher. However, this drawback cannot be called particularly critical.





Summarize

To compile a review of the RitzViva S500C smartphone, user reviews were taken as a basis. This allowed the reader to present a truthful list of advantages and disadvantages.

Benefits:

  • Great large screen with high brightness.
  • Standard, flawless design.
  • A wide range of colors.
  • Sufficient performance to complete all tasks.
  • The presence of cameras with a resolution of 2 and 5 megapixels.
  • Support for two SIM cards.

Disadvantages:

  • The lack of backlight on the control buttons.
  • Sensor support for only two simultaneous touches.
  • Poor back cover.
  • At high load the processor heats up.
  • A large number of factory defects.
