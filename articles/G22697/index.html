Great stay on the Baikal savages

Vacationers spend their vacations in different ways: some strive for the warm sea, others go to exotic countries, and others simply remain to cultivate their summer cottages. But there is another amazing category of people - travelers, tramps, simply - savages. All year they imagine how they get together and walk along uncharted paths, comprehend the secrets of nature, admire its beauty, conquer turbulent rivers and mountain passes. And then on the halt, cozy tents will stand side by side, songs will be sung with a guitar, and smoke will draw fire ...





There are many places in Russia where you could go for such romance. And one of them is Lake Baikal. Even a short vacation on Lake Baikal by savages will be remembered for a long time. The lake is beautiful at any time of the year. Short Baikal summer attracts with wonderful fishing, swimming, autumn colors the Baikal shores with bright watercolor. It is in the fall that a favorable season begins for beauty lovers - photographers. In winter, Baikal is a solid paradise for skiers, lovers of winter fishing and dog sledding.

Along the coast of the lake and on the islands there are many tourist camps, hotels, campsites, but rest on Lake Baikal by savages is no less attractive. Truly, it can be appreciated only by those who are ready to give up at least for a while the benefits of civilization and merge together with nature. Peschanaya Bay is just waiting for such hermits, philosophers, to immerse them in silence and silence. Here, even time seems to stop. And the island of Olkhon will present wonderful fishing and will surprise you with unspoiled virgin nature.





Yes, the rest of the savages on Lake Baikal is devoid of many amenities, but on the other hand, it requires less cost. You just need to select the routes of interest in advance, study the local transport schedule well, stock up on detailed maps of the area, as well as good tents, sleeping bags, fishing tackle, necessary equipment and food supplies. Of course, this option is not suitable for everyone, not everyone is able to carry a tangible load on their shoulders for several days. If you are just one of these, you are more likely to stay in the private sector. Prices there are much cheaper than in a hotel, and you can also bargain with the owners. For a separate low fee, you can go to a real Siberian steam bath, exuding an incomparable coniferous smell. By the way, while rest on Lake Baikal in 2013 savage even in summer was relatively inexpensive, then in the autumn and winter seasons prices are even lower.





The private sector of Baikal villages will welcome travelers: it is Listvyanka with its majestic nature, Big Goloustnoye with heady forest aromas, Arshan with healing mineral springs. The local population treats visitors very well, because tourists are a good source of income for them. Believe me, you will remember a vacation on Lake Baikal as a savage, while prices will pleasantly surprise you.

Everyone chooses a trip within their means and, of course, to their liking. In this sense, rest on Lake Baikal by savages is suitable in all respects. Baikal is a real Siberian pearl; it attracts true connoisseurs of severe beauty with their exoticism, who are ready to come there again and again and explore unexplored routes.
