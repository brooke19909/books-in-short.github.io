Gallery Avenue (hotel, Moscow): reviews

A welcoming reception for Moscow guests is provided by Gallery Avenue. The hotel is guided by the highest standards of service in its work .





a brief description of

You can feel the atmosphere of home comfort at the Gallery Avenue Hotel. The hotel adheres to classical canons in the design of rooms and other premises. In each of the 170 rooms, in addition to comfort, you can experience aesthetic pleasure. A peculiarity of the hotel‚Äôs interior is that the walls of its rooms are decorated with colorful reproductions of Renaissance masterpieces.





Where is

In the very center of Moscow is "Gallery Avenue" (hotel). Its address is 32 Shchepkina Street. It is about a 3-minute walk from Prospekt Mira metro station. The Kremlin and Red Square need to be walked a little less than 4 km. Also in close proximity are the following tourist sites:

  • Moscow Cathedral (1 km).
  • Sports complex "Olympic" (1 km).
  • Pharmaceutical garden (1 km).
  • Catherine Park (1 km).
  • Eliseevsky department store (2 km).
  • Bolshoi Theater (2 km).
  • Luzhniki Stadium (9 km).

How to get there

Near the main thoroughfares of Moscow is "Gallery Avenue" (hotel). Hotel staff will tell you how to get there if you contact them by phone at 8 (495) 510-67-37 upon arrival.

From the Prospect Mira metro station, you need to walk about 500 meters parallel to Durov Street. From the international airports "Vnukovo", "Domodedovo" and "Sheremetyevo" you need to travel about 40 km by taxi. You can also get on the shuttle bus, reaching the stop "Prospect Mira".

Room Amenities

A feeling of homeliness and comfort will accompany guests of the Gallery Avenue hotel. The hotel provides the following amenities in each of the 170 rooms:

  • a minibar, which is filled for an additional fee, and is also used as a refrigerator;
  • the bathroom is equipped with the necessary fixtures, a hairdryer and bath accessories;
  • you can store your valuables in a safe with an electronic combination lock;
  • TV provides the ability to view many cable and satellite channels;
  • air conditioning system that helps you create a comfortable microclimate in the room;
  • landline phone for communication with the reception and calls to other cities;
  • a desk with a lamp.




Loyalty program "Favorite guest"

For regular guests, Gallery Avenue (Hotel) offers a loyalty program. It applies to those tourists who stay at the hotel 2 or more times, and also book a room directly through the official website. The following privileges are available under the Beloved Guest program:

  • early registration and late eviction (until 15:00) subject to availability);
  • free upgrade of the category of apartments (also if there are enough available rooms);
  • providing a company bathrobe and slippers in the room;
  • a bottle of drinking water every day;
  • a toothpaste and a brush are attached to the main hygiene set;
  • an electric kettle and tea bags with hot drinks and sugar;
  • Welcome plate of fruit on check in day.

Positive reviews

Before your trip, it is useful to get acquainted with the opinions of travelers about the institution "Gallery Avenue" (hotel, Moscow). Photos and comments of tourists testify to the many advantages of this institution. So, it is worth paying attention to the following points:

  • advantageous location - in the very center of the city, not far from the metro station;
  • due to the fact that a few minutes walk from the hotel is the Meshchansky registry office, this place is great for weddings;
  • original interior;
  • the bathroom is kept in perfect cleanliness;
  • Considering that the hotel is located away from busy streets, the atmosphere here is quite calm;
  • large comfortable rooms;
  • the staff is polite, friendly and attentive;
  • new household appliances in the room;
  • good shower, which is designed for tall people;
  • there is an ironing room on each floor;
  • quick settlement without unnecessary bureaucratic delays;
  • the staff instantly responds to all comments and requests of guests;
  • delicious and hearty breakfasts.




Negative reviews

Not only positive, but also negative aspects are characterized by "Gallery Avenue" (hotel, Moscow). Reviews indicate the following disadvantages of this institution:

  • due to poor sound insulation, noise from the street, as well as from the corridor and neighboring rooms, may interfere with your relaxation;
  • the heating in the rooms is very weak, and for heaters you have to pay extra;
  • electronic keys work every other time;
  • too soft mattresses on the beds, due to which the back hurts in the morning;
  • there is no heated towel rail in the bathroom;
  • the windows of some rooms overlook a noisy and dusty construction site, work on which does not stop even at night;
  • there is no way to refuse to include breakfast in the price;
  • special wishes of guests, which they indicate when booking, in most cases are not taken into account;
  • the breakfast menu is repeated every day;
  • few towels (one for each guest);
  • the hotel does not have its own parking lot, and therefore you have to use a paid parking lot (if you leave the car on the sidewalk, it will be evacuated without warning);
  • maids do not replenish supplies of cosmetics (you have to ask for it yourself);
  • the bathroom has a strong smell of sewage;
  • on crystal lamps - a thick layer of dust;
  • poor lighting in the rooms (you can add floor lamps or table lamps);
  • there is no special makeup mirror in the bathroom;
  • the refrigerator is too loud;
  • it is impossible to get through to the reception (you have to go down to the lobby to ask questions of interest);
  • in some places the elaborate design of the hotel looks cheap;
  • in standard rooms there are no bedside tables;
  • breakfast starts at 7:30, which is late enough for business people.

The Gallery Avenue (hotel) offers quite comfortable living conditions. Moscow will be in front of you at a glance due to its convenient location in the city center. This institution will delight you with its original interior and high standards of service.
