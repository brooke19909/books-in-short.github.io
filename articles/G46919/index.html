Pearl of China on the Pearl River

Guangzhou after Beijing and Shanghai is the third largest and fourth largest city in the Middle Kingdom. It was founded in the 8th century BC and is considered one of the oldest cities in the world. Despite this, he managed to preserve his history, rich culture and unique flavor to the present day. At the same time, Guangzhou is today an ultramodern commercial and industrial center of world significance. According to history, it was from here, from the port of Guangzhou that the legendary Silk Road began. True, in those days, the city was called differently - Canton.

Historical value

What attracts Guangzhou tourists? Any guide will tell you that here in the city are concentrated more than 150 different attractions - natural and historical monuments. In another beautiful park, Haichung is the oldest monastery in Guangzhou.
