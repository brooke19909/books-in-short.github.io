How to understand which is the best refrigerator?

To determine which is the best refrigerator, you must first select the quality criteria that interest you. It is important to understand what price you are willing to pay for it, as well as what you will store in it and in what volumes?



To refrigerators intended for freezing, storing and cooling food and drinks it is customary to include: refrigerators and wine cabinets, built-in and non-built-in refrigerators and freezers, portable and transport devices. Ice makers, ice makers for ice, and coolers for drinks are adjacent to the above units. The range of refrigerators includes a wide range of models, as well as a variety of prices. And here the question naturally arises of which is the best refrigerator?



You can consider the characteristics of these units to determine the appropriate type of devices. The first characteristic is profitability. International standards provide nine classes for this criterion from A ++ to G. Classes A ++, A +, A, and B are assigned to very economical models, C and D receive devices with moderate electrical energy consumption. The economy of the refrigerator depends on such parameters as design features, workmanship, as well as operating conditions. Another important indicator of a good unit is reliability and durability. If the device has a long official warranty period, then this will allow you to insure you against unnecessary expenses for a long time in case of failure or if any problems are found. European manufacturers usually give a year of warranty on their products, and Russian and Belarusian - for three years.

Determining which is the best refrigerator, it is worth looking at the models presented in different price ranges. In the lower and middle price ranges with a volume of up to 400 liters, there are many brands of aggregates, both domestic and foreign. You can name brands such as Smolensk, Saratov, Pozis, Turquoise, Nord, Atlant, Hansa, AEG, Kaiser, Bosch, Siemens, Liebherr, Ardo, Indesit, Ariston, Beko, Vestel, Fagor.

The more expensive and convenient to use include American- made refrigerators , which have built-in door bars and convenient dispensers - Amana, Northland, General Electric, Mayyag, Frigidaire, Viking, as well as European models - Bosch, AEG, Gaggenau, Liebherr , Siemens. The dimensions of the refrigerator depend on many indicators, including the capacity and the availability of additional options. The choice of all this depends only on individual needs.

If we talk about what is the best refrigerator, then it is worth mentioning Japanese and Chinese manufacturers who install simplified ice generators operating in their semi-automatic mode on their devices. On the market are elite models made in the "retro" style, which are produced in limited quantities. Elite models have many features and capabilities: there is a built-in control panel that allows you to control modes, activate a lot of functions without opening doors; power switch, which allows you to turn off the unit without pulling the plug out of the socket. An electronic assistant can give recommendations on cooking from existing products and more. Of course, the cheapest refrigerator will not be equipped with such features.
