Serpukhov Hotels: Overview

Since the 15th century, Serpukhov was the specific principality of the Moscow kingdom. He served as a fortress during the Tatar-Mongol raids and wars with the Lithuanian-Polish invaders. At all times, the Kremlin and fortified monasteries were a reliable outpost on the Oka borders of Muscovy. It was in Serpukhov that Ivan IV, Boris Godunov, Vasily Shuisky and Ivan Bolotnikov gathered and based their troops. Modern Serpukhov is a small administrative center with almost 130 thousand inhabitants. It is located in a picturesque area on the Nara River , a hundred kilometers south of Moscow. The presence of 150 enterprises and factories, more than 10 higher educational institutions, as well as a rich history and unique architecture led to the development of hotel infrastructure. Hotels in Serpukhov can provide accommodation of different comforts and taking into account various financial possibilities.



Cheap hotels, hostels, apartments

  • Hotel "Pearl" (Serpukhov, st. Kaluzhskaya, 5) offers 16 spacious rooms, the cost of living - from 1900 p. per day. The hotel is located in the historic city center, surrounded by several temples. It features a restaurant and a shared kitchen. Breakfast is included in the room rate, facilities for people with disabilities are provided, pets are allowed.
  • Hostel "Serpeyka" (Serpukhov, Nizhnyaya Serpeyka St., 13). There are 6 rooms with a room rate of 400 r. per day. Guests have access to a shared kitchen, a bathroom, and there is a barbecue area with all utensils in the shady garden.
  • Apartments on Moskovskoye Shosse (Serpukhov, Moskovskoye Shosse, 53/17) are located on the bypass road, which is very convenient for drivers of transit vehicles. The cost of living is from 1500 p. per day. The capacity of the apartment is up to four people, they have at their disposal a complete set of kitchen and household appliances.
  • Apartments on Frunze (Serpukhov, 6 Frunze St.). The maximum number of guests is four people, the cost is from 1500 p. per day. The apartments have such furniture: the bedroom has a double bed, the living room has a sofa bed and a fully equipped kitchen.




Gostiny Dvor "Rus"

Hotel "Rus" (Serpukhov, 2-ya Moskovskaya St., 2/20) is located in a historic building, built more than 200 years ago. The room fund consists of 50 rooms with a cost of living from 2200 p. per day, and there are also suites and studios. It offers a lobby bar, a restaurant serving beer from its own brewery, breakfast (buffet), an extensive menu in the evening, including a dietary one. The hotel has a souvenir shop, tour desk, ticket service. Airport shuttle is available at an additional cost. Pets are allowed.

Hotel "Noble"

Hotel "Noble" (Serpukhov, Voroshilova St., 56) has 23 rooms, decorated in a classic style, the cost of living - from 2900 p. The rooms have a standard set of high-quality furniture, a minibar, a toilet room with a bathtub and shower. The restaurant serves European and Russian cuisine. The hotel has a sauna, on-site free parking, pets are allowed.

Hotel and business center "Oka"

Hotel "Oka" (Serpukhov, Voroshilova St., 128) is located directly in the business center of the city near banks and institutions, shopping and entertainment centers. Offers leasing of offices and premises for organizations and enterprises. The room fund is located on three floors of a nine-story building and offers 38 rooms with a cost of living from 1900 p. per day. The rooms have a standard set of furniture, a work desk and a refrigerator. Pets not allowed, no parking.



Hotel Korston

Hotel "Korston" (Serpukhov, Borisov highway, 1). This is a huge ultramodern complex with many shops, restaurants and bars. It also has a business center with offices and 4 conference rooms that can accommodate from 30 to 400 people. On the fifteenth floor there is a panoramic restaurant, which offers magnificent views of the whole city. For lovers on the second floor there is an Italian restaurant and a pizzeria. Under the glass roof there is a cafe where you can spend a romantic evening under the stars. The central restaurant serves breakfast (buffet), and there is also a Japanese restaurant. Several lounge cafes offer a variety of drinks, cocktails and gourmet sweets.

Club "Hipsters" brighten up your leisure time with an interesting music show program with contests and draws. The complex includes: the best cinema with four halls and 2D-, 3D-equipment, bowling, billiard room, children's park, where the child will have fun with qualified animators.

The hotel has 79 rooms equipped with the most modern furniture and appliances. Cost - from 3000 r. per night. The rooms include safes and free toiletries. Parking requires a reservation (the service may be chargeable), pets are allowed.



Country hotels in Serpukhov

  • The recreation complex "Luzhki" (Luzhki village) has 91 comfortable rooms equipped with new furniture. Cost of living - from 2000 p. per day. It offers a parking lot, a playground, a sauna, a restaurant, a conference room, and smoking areas are provided. For active recreation there are tennis tables, billiards, bike rental.
  • Mini-hotel "Palisad" (Vskhody-2 village) has 8 comfortable, staffed rooms. Cost of living - from 2000 p. per day. Guests are offered active leisure: hiking, cycling, horse riding, skiing. There is a free car park, a barbecue area, a children's playground. Fishing and picnics are available for a fee.

VIP Hotels

Hotels in Serpukhov are always happy to meet travelers, provide comfort and high level service for all categories of city guests.
