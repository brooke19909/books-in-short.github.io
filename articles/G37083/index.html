How much is an apartment in Moscow? One bedroom apartment in Moscow: price

Despite the fall of the national currency and the economic crisis, people continue to sell and buy real estate in the state. For many years they have been saving money for the long-awaited apartment. The most expensive property is considered to be in Moscow. But here, the final price is affected by many factors.

The price is determined by location

Real estate has such a name because it cannot be moved. As in other cities of the Russian Federation, prices for apartments in Moscow depend on the area in which they are located. Housing with the same layout, number of storeys in different areas of the city can have completely different costs. It is possible to purchase a luxury apartment several floors outside the capital at an affordable price. For the same money, one-room apartments in the center will be offered.



The lowest prices for apartments in Moscow are offered in remote areas of the city. At the same time, you will have to get to the metro for at least 20 minutes by public transport. The least prestigious area is the Moscow Ring Road. And even cheaper, you can get your own housing if you go to the suburbs. Many save significantly by purchasing apartments here. It should be borne in mind that you will have to get to work for at least two hours. Those who have their own car win. But the daily costs of gasoline and staying in traffic jams can discourage any desire to purchase housing outside the capital.

In which area to buy housing?

Former working outskirts are becoming less attractive to the younger generation of Muscovites. Apartments have significantly decreased in price in areas such as Lyublino, Tekstilshchiki, Hammer and Sickle, Lianozovo, etc. These areas scare buyers not only from their remoteness from the center. Neighbors in many cases are a decisive factor. Many are not interested in how much an apartment in Moscow is in a remote area. But the environment and traffic intersection are very important. Is it worth it to buy housing in the area in which it is scary to be on the street in the dark?



Such areas as Patriarch's Ponds, Tverskaya, Arbat, Kutuzovsky Prospekt, Vorobyovy Gory are considered safer. The price of a one-room apartment in Moscow can be really high. But if the area is well lit, there are special places for walking with children, and kindergartens and schools are nearby, the question of price does not arise.

Before deciding exactly where to buy an apartment in Moscow, it is worth clarifying the information by district. Each district has its own reputation with an established level of prices and housing classes. Given their financial capabilities and basic requirements, everyone will be able to make the appropriate choice.
