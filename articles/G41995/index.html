Which speaker system to choose for home?

Many suffocating cages of the apartment prefer the spaciousness of their own country house. In addition to the mass of various advantages, it also allows you to safely enjoy your favorite music, its sound. Therefore, you should be very careful when choosing a speaker system. After all, complete sound happiness will not appear if you just get a good music center. It is necessary to pay attention to a number of technical parameters. A true music lover will certainly need an acoustic system (AS), in other words, a whole set of excellent sound emitters that can reproduce each of its own part of the sound range.



Kinds

The speaker system can be divided into 2 classes: Hi-End and Hi-Fi. The first marking indicates that you have no more and no less elite sound and amplifying equipment. It sounds great, but it costs significantly more than Hi-Fi. This term from English literally means high accuracy. In the language of acoustic technology, this suggests that this equipment meets all accepted international quality standards, and the reproduced sound will be very close to the original. To date, 3 types of speakers are produced: floor-mounted speakers (what they are - it's clear from the name), shelf and speaker systems that are built into the ceiling or walls. The first two types are considered the most convenient, since they can be moved to any other room at any time. The built-in speaker system cannot be called mobile. It requires serious installation work, and after installation, of course, cannot be moved anywhere else.



Features

When choosing speakers, pay particular attention to the number of speakers. Single-band systems have only one such device, which, of course, cannot promise any good sound. A higher level is two-way speakers. Their feature is not only the number of speakers. It is also important that they are capable of separately producing high and low sounds. Perhaps the ideal can be called a speaker system with three-way speakers. In them, as a rule, high, medium and low frequencies give out different speakers.



Dimensions

In terms of dimensions, the speakers differ in large and small. Before buying speakers for the home, be sure to measure the room where you plan to install sound equipment. So, for example, it is not very reasonable to put two-meter speakers in a small room. However, much here still depends on the characteristics of the premises, decoration and furniture. Typically, small designs distort the sound very much, especially during loud sound. Today in stores a huge number of speakers. Naturally, they cost differently.
