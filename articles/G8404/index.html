Mirage World Hotel 5 * (Marmaris, Turkey): description, service, reviews

In this article we will talk about the Mirage World Hotel 5 * hotel. For the most part, we built its description on the reviews of tourists who visited there in the 2017 season. Looking ahead, it can be noted that this Turkish "five" left a good impression. Despite the fact that she is on the second line, reviews praise her location. The hotel offers guests a high level of service, comfortable, bright and spacious rooms, a variety of entertainment for adults and children. The hotel positions itself as a place of quiet beach family vacation. Many tourists note a good value for money.



Icmeler. Features

Many tourists, having decided to go on vacation to Turkey, are torn between noisy resorts with vibrant evening life and quiet secluded places with beautiful nature. What to choose? Of course, Icmeler! This village surrounded by greenery at the foot of the mountains is located just eight kilometers from the big city of Marmaris. To get to the famous Bar Street, you need to take a shuttle bus for about ten minutes. And in Icmeler itself, nature resembles Eden: pine trees, lush Mediterranean vegetation and, of course, the gentle Mediterranean Sea. In a few kilometers, waves of another water area are rolling ashore.

Swim in one day in the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas and feel the difference between them - isn't that a miracle? Therefore, Icmeler, which some reviews call the Marmaris region, and others a separate town, is considered a prestigious holiday destination. There are mainly hotels with five stars. And Mirage World Hotel 5 * is one of them. You can walk to Marmaris on the beautiful promenade. The whole journey will take about an hour. Or take the city shuttle in ten minutes. Barov Street, where young people from all over Marmaris flock in the evenings, is located near the Old Fortress. Dalaman Airport is 110 kilometers from the hotel. The general transfer to the hotel will take more than two hours, because first tourists will be transported, vacationing in Marmaris itself.



Hotel Location

All resort life in quiet Icmeler is concentrated on the promenade. There is the lion's share of restaurants and bars. Therefore, many vacationers so highly appreciated the location of the Mirage World Hotel 5 * hotel. It is on the second line. On the one hand, no unwanted noises from outside enter the hotel grounds. On the other hand, it takes about 3-5 minutes to go to the sea. The beaches in Icmeler are all municipal, the beds and umbrellas on them are paid. Therefore, the first or second line - there is no fundamental difference. And "sea view" opens from the upper floors of the hotel we are describing. The Mirage World Hotel is surrounded by small shops selling fruit or excellent Turkish textiles. Nearby there is a bus stop dolmushe - minibuses with which you can get to the center of Marmaris. Also within walking distance there are several tour agencies.

Hotel Territory

The Mirage World Hotel 5 * hotel (Marmaris) was built in 2001. The last time he was renovated in 2014. Reviews note a fresh whitewashing of the facades and new furniture with appliances in the rooms. Tourists also give top marks to landscape designers and the efforts of gardeners. Everywhere, wherever you look, flowering oleander bushes can be seen on the emerald lawns, coconut palms and banana trees languidly swinging leaves under the breeze. The Mirage World Hotel has a vast territory of 7800 square meters. There are four 4-story buildings, built so that the sea can be seen from the maximum number of windows. All these buildings have elevators. When choosing a room, tourists are advised to ask for building A. Building C is considered the worst, since some of the rooms have a view of the wall and back yard of the hotel. Between the buildings are swimming pools. A little far away are other hotel buildings - a snack bar, a playground. The hotel is not too far from the beach. The distance separating it from the sea is only eighty meters. It takes about three minutes to reach him.



Where guests are accommodated

Of the 250 rooms of the Mirage World Hotel 5 *, two hundred are double standards. Some have twin beds, others have one wide matrimonial bed. There are several rooms, including standard categories, fully adapted to the special needs of persons with disabilities. They are located on the ground floor. There are other room categories. For families, the hotel can offer 17 family rooms. This number consists of one parent bedroom and a living room, in which there are two folding sofas. There are two balconies adjacent to the family room. Both living rooms have air conditioning. The highest category of rooms at Mirage World Hotel are suites. These rooms are designed for 4-5 guests. Choosing this category for relaxation, reviews recommend taking a corner suite. These corner rooms are more spacious and bright. Also, tourists are advised to ask for the upper floors of the buildings - from their windows you can see either the sea, or the beautiful mountains covered with pines.



What's in the rooms

The equipment of rooms in the Mirage World Hotel 5 * hotel is fully consistent with its five-star status. Southern heat reliably leaves a split-system of a new generation behind the windows. But if you want to open a balcony, then at your service will be a table and two soft chairs. The flooring in the rooms is tiled, which makes it very pleasant to walk barefoot in hot weather. But if you drive at the beginning or end of the season, bring slippers with you! In the bedroom, guests will find a minibar with replenished drinking water, a TV with satellite channels (among them there are Russian ones). The bathroom comes with a shower and hairdryer. Toiletries and cosmetics are replenished daily. Paid services include the use of a safe, as well as filling the minibar (excluding drinking water). Maids clean the rooms every day. According to reviews, in good conscience. They sweep and wash the floors, clean the bed, change towels. Clean linen is laid every other day.



Mirage World Hotel 5 *: food

Is it worth it to paint the Turkish all inclusive system? Anyone who has ever visited an all-inclusive hotel on the Anatolian coast earned a lifelong nostalgia for these endlessly lasting feasts. The hotel we are describing is by no means an exception. From seven to ten in the main restaurant breakfast. Who overslept or who thought it was not enough, can eat up to 11:00 in a tent by the pool. Another hour - and guests are invited to dinner. Then tea or coffee with sweets. A little more time, and a very plentiful dinner awaits the guests. Anyone who has a bad habit of eating up before bedtime can satisfy her - from midnight to an hour, a snack is provided in the restaurant. And between all these meals, a variety of drinks are served in the hotel bars and snacks are served. Imported alcohol, ice cream and fresh juices are paid. Everything else is in the public domain.



What nutrition reviews say

At the Mirage World Hotel 5 * (Marmaris), the food is very tasty and varied. There is a choice for those who prefer the usual European food, and for those who want to try Turkish national dishes. Parents assure that the assortment is sufficient to feed a capricious child. Pancakes are baked for breakfast, there are yoghurts and cereal. Many grilled dishes that children love so much. Mashed potatoes and pasta are often served on the side dish. Tourists make odes to the desserts served at the hotel. In short, if you want to lose weight, you should not go to this hotel. Bartenders are very friendly, remember the preferences of customers, maintain a friendly conversation.

Beach, Pools

But tourists are not alien to other interests besides drinking and eating. The sea in Icmeler is said to be very clean and warm. Depth setting is good for bathing children, but watch out, parents, do not relax! The beach is a soft, clean sand of golden color. Sun loungers and umbrellas are paid. Some beach equipment is owned by coastal cafes. If you order something in them, you can sunbathe on their sunbeds at least the whole day. The Mirage World Hotel 5 * (Turkey) has freshwater swimming pools for adults and children. Two slides increase the pleasure of swimming: one is straight and the other is coiled. The sun terrace around the pools is lined with sunbeds and umbrellas - free for guests. Beach towels are provided at the hotel.



Mirage World Hotel 5 *: service

Wi-Fi at the hotel is free. But it operates only near the reception, which, by the way, works around the clock. Children from three to twelve years old can be sent to a mini club, where they will be engaged in professional educators. For babies, you can hire a babysitter. Free services at the hotel include: sauna, hammam, car parking, library, gym. The hotel has a children's playground, currency exchange and a gift shop. For adults, a separate animation team works. The guys are trying very hard to entertain the guests with sports games and water aerobics during the day, in the evenings they organize shows on their own. Sometimes professional artists are invited to Mirage World Hotel 5 *. Reviews mention belly dancing, performances by magicians and dancers, and fire shows.
