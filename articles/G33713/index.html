Hotel Love Hotel Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): reviews, rating, description, photo

Nha Trang is the beach capital of Vietnam. This definition is enough to understand the life essence of this resort. It is no coincidence that a cheerful, noisy, full of energy and aplomb town attracts thousands of tourists every year. Someone in Nha Trang likes the white sandy beach of the beach, bordered by amazing azure water, a necklace of hills shimmering with different green shades, and exotic islands in the middle of the bay. Someone is attracted here by the vibrant nightlife. The most peaceful and conservative tourists appreciate the opportunity to visit first-class spa treatments and mud baths, go diving and get acquainted with ancient sights.





To accommodate tourists in Nha Trang, a wide variety of hotels is provided: from 5 *, 4 *, 3 * (located in descending order of the luxury offered to guests), equipped with swimming pools and air conditioning, to hotels of low categories. One of the popular accommodation options for visitors to Nha Trang is the Love Hotel Nha Trang 3 * hotel.

Location

Love Hotel Nha Trang 3 * is located in the vicinity of Chang Fu Beach (Nha Trang). The nearest beach is 200 m away. The international airport in Kamrani is 26 km away.

How do guests respond to the location of the hotel?

Tourists believe that the institution is located very well - in the center, but in the alley, so noise from the highway does not reach it, and residents have the opportunity to relax in silence. Nearby, authors of reviews share, there are popular cafes and restaurants with reasonable prices, shops, shops where you can buy fruits and much more.

Acquaintance

The main popularity of Love Hotel Nha Trang 3 * (Nha Trang) is based on the proximity of the establishment to the sea. In addition, many tourists also like the romantic design of its rooms. The hotel offers impeccable service, all the necessary conditions for a good stay. The numerous amenities provided at the establishment can satisfy the most demanding guests.

The hotel offers a conference room, a 24-hour reception, a currency exchange and a laundry service. The restaurant serves European and Asian cuisines. Guests can use wireless internet for free. For guests who want to relax, a sauna with a massage session is provided. For add. A fee is offered for car rental.

Cozy comfortable rooms are equipped with TV, safe, bathroom with shower, split system. Room service is available (on request). Many consider the hotel a worthy option for relaxing by the sea.

How do guests respond about the rooms?

Hotel guests describe the Deluxe rooms as spacious, bright, clean, with balconies or huge panoramic windows all over the wall, imitating a balcony, which offer magnificent views of the mountains and the city. Plumbing in the rooms is all new, but it often leaks. According to tourists, they clean the rooms cleanly and completely imperceptibly. Lingerie changes daily.

The rooms have a lot of ants, guests complain. Cleaning is done every day, water is updated, laundry services are inexpensive. The downside of some Standard rooms is called the absence of a "dressing room." Right next to the door are beds, a small nightstand and a small wardrobe. The peculiarity of the hotel is called by guests that during the rain, hot water disappears here. You have to wash cool. The administration of the institution is not able to solve this problem. Tourists share in their reviews that many found a way out of this situation in the following. As soon as the weather deteriorates, they are sent to mineral springs. They bask there, because the water is warm there.
