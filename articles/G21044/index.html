Gorokhovets hotels (Vladimir region): addresses, description of rooms, reviews

Those who travel quite often move to various cities of the country for work purposes, they are probably well aware that they should prepare in advance for each trip. Large companies have created special departments responsible for booking hotels.

If you are traveling to a large developed city, it is unlikely that you will have difficulties with accommodation. But if you find yourself in a small provincial town, there will not be many options for living. Take, for example, Gorokhovets (Vladimir region) - an old Russian city with a rather interesting and impressive history. There are not so many hotels and hotels, or rather, very few, but you definitely will not stay on the street. As part of this material, we will evaluate the best Gorokhovets hotels, describe the conditions of their accommodation, and also provide feedback from those who stayed in them.

Features of accommodation

Small provincial cities, like Gorokhovets (Vladimir region), due to the stereotypes around them, few people are attracted to. But in fact, they can be a great place to spend a weekend with your family - it is so quiet and calm here that all problems literally disappear in an instant. And if you want, you can find a great hotel, it will be comfortable not only to spend the night, but also spend several days. The main and undeniable advantage in this case is a small demand - you do not have to book a room for several weeks or months, just do it before the trip to get a room of the selected category. And the prices for hotel accommodation are much lower.

Labyrinth

If you plan to immerse yourself in the atmosphere of the city, enjoy fresh air and stunning views from the window of your room every morning, pay attention to this option. Hotel "Labyrinth" in Gorokhovets has already managed to fall in love with many guests of the city, and all because it is located in a picturesque place, not far from the Klyazma coast and the famous Lysa Gora - the pride of local residents. At the same time, the city center is only 2 km away.

The hotel's rooms are represented by standard and luxury rooms, where there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay: a bathroom, modern furniture, appliances, a set of dishes. The interior of the rooms will satisfy taste preferences: from simple classic options to warm and comfortable rooms decorated with natural wood. At the hotel, each guest can use the high-speed wireless Internet for free, as well as taste traditional Russian cuisine in the restaurant. Vacationers focus on the hospitality of the staff, the possibility of settlement at any time of the day or night, as well as a high level of comfort.

The cost of daily living in the rooms of this hotel starts from 1800 rubles.

Address: Gorokhovets, st. Gorky, 86.

"Merchant's estate"

This option provides entire complexes for recreation, and not just a hotel room in Gorokhovets. The reviews of those who managed to stay at the "Merchant's estate" confirm that the rest here becomes unforgettable. The complex is located in the very center of the city, therefore it provides access to all the necessary infrastructure: shops, public transport, all interesting sights are within walking distance.

In addition to comfortable rooms, the territory has everything you need for a comfortable stay: a billiard room, a bath and a sauna, ample secure parking, a relaxation room with karaoke, a bar and a cinema. All guests can use the wireless Internet, as well as a shuttle service to the hotel. The employees of the complex take on the entertainment program of their guests. Vacationers respond quite positively to the rich and varied excursion program.

Surely you are interested in the conditions that this Gorokhovets hotel has to offer. In the description of the rooms of Merchants' Estate, they focus on the original Russian traditions in which they are sustained: an abundance of natural materials, primarily wood, woven carpets and rugs, embroidered bedspreads. In such a room, it is homely cozy and warm, which is so valuable on vacation or on a business trip. At the same time, the hotel administration equipped all the rooms with modern furniture, appliances, and electronics. The cost of daily living in a hotel starts from 2500 rubles, it is better to reserve a place in advance.

Address: Gorokhovets, st. Lenin, 44.

"Aquarius"

If you are used to spending time in chic apartments, put comfort and arrangement of rooms above all else, your attention will probably be attracted to such a Gorokhovets hotel as Aquarius. All those who choose her come back here again. But is this not the main indicator of quality?

One of the main advantages is the location. The hotel is located directly on the M7 federal highway connecting Moscow and Kazan, which is why not only guests of the city so often stop here, but also those who are looking for a comfortable overnight stay. At the same time, the center of Gorokhovets is only 1.1 km, you can reach all attractions in just 5-7 minutes. Comfortable rooms, modern technical equipment, high-speed wireless Internet, as well as satellite TV - all this will be at the complete disposal of guests.

The hotel is designed for simultaneous accommodation of 48 people, is part of the ski complex, so it will become not only an excellent overnight stay, but also a place for organizing leisure activities, spending weekends with friends and relatives. The cost of daily hotel accommodation starts from 1800 rubles.

Address: Gorokhovets, st. Krasnoarmeyskaya, 1.

Wild spruce

Gorokhovets hotels are not numerous, but each of them deserves special attention. Take, for example, the guest house "Wild Fir" - a quiet, calm and remote place from noise and fuss.

This option is ideal for city guests who decide to spend their weekend here. Comfortable spacious rooms with a kitchen and a veranda, their own secure parking, a rich and varied entertainment program from the staff, the possibility of staying with pets are the advantages that guests have already appreciated. The hotel is ideal for families: a spacious modern games room, children's books and cartoons, animation, ski slopes, barbecue areas.

The cost of daily living in a hotel starts from 2500 rubles.

Address: Gorokhovets, Krasnoarmeyskaya, 22.

"Vladimir region"
