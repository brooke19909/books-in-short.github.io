How to disable the radio in an apartment in St. Petersburg. Turn off the radio

Radio point - this word refers to the usual wired radio, the socket for which you are likely to be able to find in your kitchen.

Few people know that its main function is not to broadcast radio programs, but to alert residents of the city about emergency situations. After all, a wired radio is autonomous from the main electric networks and can function with a total outage. This important point allows you to use it to convey important information about potential threats. Due to this fact, radio dots are required in residential premises. Both old houses and new buildings - all of them are necessarily connected to a wired radio. Without the presence of a radio point in each apartment, the state commission does not issue permission to operate the house. But despite this, residents have the right to refuse to use it in their apartment.



The choice of radio stations for broadcasting near a wired radio is small: Mayak, Radio Rossii, Says Moscow. Radio stations on air from 6 am to midnight. But for three holidays - New Year, Christmas and Easter, you can listen to the radio until 3.00.

Using Radio Points

As mentioned above, three radio stations are available for listening. The well-known "Radio of Russia" broadcasts on sound frequencies and is available for the least functional radio receiver. To be able to listen to the other two stations, you need an apparatus with an amplifier that works in the high-frequency range.



You can purchase a radio when concluding an order for connecting a radio point. The simplest device, having a range of one channel, costs around 170-180 rubles. High-frequency radio can be bought for 800 rubles and above. It should be noted that the loudspeakers mentioned are produced only by domestic manufacturers, there are no foreign ones (unless you are lucky enough to buy an old receiver from the time of socialism).

Although wired radio has an important function, it does not have many fans. However, the fact that you are not listening to him does not mean that you are not paying for it. Payment is required and occurs monthly, in St. Petersburg, for example, fluctuates around 100 rubles. But you have the right not to pay for the service that you do not use. How to disable the radio in an apartment in St. Petersburg. Quick way

You need to contact the FUP RS SP, located at Rasstannaya street, house 18 B. There you will have to write a statement about the refusal of the radio point, after which it will be turned off by the master in your apartment. You must have a passport and a document confirming your ownership of the apartment with you. Without these documents, you will be denied services.

How to disable the radio in an apartment in St. Petersburg. Slow option

Here, to disable the radio point (SPB), First you need to make photocopies of your passport and the last document on your payment of utilities. Then make a free-style statement where you must indicate the reason for not using the radio. The following information is also required in it: the address of your place of residence and contact details, by which you will always be able to contact. After these steps, fax the documents to (812) 240 44 55. After some time, specialists will contact you to arrange a visit to the master. Upon arrival, he will give you a receipt to pay for disconnecting the apartment from the radio point, the price of such a service is 100 rubles. After the receipt is paid, the wizard will visit you again to turn off the wired radio. He will draw up a certificate of disabling the service and deliver it to the accounting department of the management company serving your home.



This was the main algorithm of actions regarding how to turn off the radio, St. Petersburg was an example.

And this is not all the actions on how to turn off the radio channel in an apartment in St. Petersburg. Recalculation will be done to you only after all the necessary certificates are in the accounting department of the settlement center. And the master must write you an act in which it should be written that from now on you do not have a radio point. This document must be submitted to the EIRC. And after all these actions, the line "Wired Radio" will be finally deleted from your payment receipt.

For those who are puzzled by the question of how to turn off the radio channel in an apartment in St. Petersburg and do it legally, recall the Consumer Protection Law, Clause 3, Article 16: you are not obligated to pay for those services for which you did not consent. According to this article, all the described actions are completely legal.

How to restore the radio channel?

If for some reason you began to feel the need for an already disconnected radio stream, then the algorithm for reconnecting it is simple. First write an application for its restoration, and then buy the radio you need, which the wizard will install in response to your application.



Worth thinking

In conclusion, one can focus on the fact that a radio point can sometimes be the only means of warning the population about an impending disaster, martial law, or a natural disaster. In its absence, you can lose a very chance of salvation, however absurd it may sound in our peaceful everyday lives. You should think about it well before writing a document to refuse the broadcasting service in your apartment. Ultimately, a hundred rubles a month is not so noticeable loss to deprive yourself and your loved ones of the opportunity to avoid unforeseen danger because of it.
