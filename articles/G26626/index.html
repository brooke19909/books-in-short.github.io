Create a holiday. How to make a flower out of balls



What kind of figures do masters not make from ordinary balloons - a favorite childhood toy. And how wonderful they look, creating a cheerful mood at the holiday! It is difficult to look away from such a sight, but it is easy to create it yourself. Yes, making a flower out of balloons is pretty easy.

Before creating your first masterpiece, you need to stock up on materials. How to make a flower out of balls?

Take two long balls of green and red, a hand pump (since it is quite difficult to inflate them with your mouth) and your own imagination, backed up by patience. First, we inflate the red ball for modeling until only the tip 3 cm long remains free of air. Next, you need to tie the ends of the rubber product into two knots, bend it in half, and twist it in the middle twice. Then gently twist the ball two times in such a way as to divide it into three even parts. Next, from this design we make an accordion, with our hands we form three petals.

How to make a flower out of balls on a stalk? The petals are ready, we proceed to the stem. Loosely inflate the green ball and tie it. Next, leave about 10 cm from the end and twist the stem. After this, the green ball should be connected with the red one, inserting it into the middle of the flower. We give the composition the desired shape. From these outlandish plants, you can make a multi-colored bouquet that will delight both the baby and the adult.

Previously described was a method of creating a composition using special long balloons. You can make flowers from the usual round products.



How? Stock up on cardboard, a ruler, an office knife, scissors, a felt-tip pen, a pencil, one ball of blue and four yellow. And you also need a tape, a hand pump or Rocket-AS.

How to make a flower out of balls? First cut a square out of cardboard. This will be a template with which you can inflate the balls to the same size for the petals. On the cardboard should draw a figure of the required size and cut. The hole will be a measure: if the ball does not pass, it should be deflated a little, if it passes too freely, pump it up with air.

At first two yellow balls should be inflated without tying them. Then they should be fastened together for tails. To do this, twist the tail of one ball twice around the tail of the second. Fasten the second pair of yellow balls in the same way. After that, lay one pair on the other crosswise and twist them together. The base of the flower is ready.





For the middle, slightly inflate the blue ball (approximately in diameter up to 8 cm), tie it and put it in the center between the petals. We connect the blue tail with the yellow ones. From several of these flowers you can make a bouquet.

How to make a flower out of balls? The answer to this question can now be given by any student who enjoys playing with these weightless, but very bright toys. Try it and you will succeed. Having mastered the technique of simple shapes, you can safely and easily move on to creating more complex ones.
