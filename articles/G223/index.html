Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Alanya / Mahmutlar): hotel description and photos

Mahmutlar is a young but actively developing Mediterranean resort. It is located on the coast of Turkey. The nearest large settlement, Gazipasha, is thirty kilometers from it. The village is surrounded by pine-covered mountains. Water in these parts has a characteristic greenish tint. Moreover, it is transparent and clean.





Turkish coast

Air flights arrive at the international airport every hour. After the meeting, tourists are placed in comfortable buses and brought to the 3 * Milano Beach Family Hotel. Mahmutlar is shrouded in a network of highways that connect it with other resorts in the region.

Public transportation is concentrated within a short drive from the hotel. They are located on the streets of Kargicak, Ataturk and on Barbaros Avenue. Minibuses stop on demand right on the highway. Private carriers stop working at 23:00. After midnight, the resort can only be reached by taxi.





The swimming season at Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * opens at the end of May. At this time, the weather is already hot in summer. Water warms up to 20 ° C. In June, this indicator is 23 ° C, in July - 25 ° C, in August - 27 ° C. In September, the coast gradually empties. Although the sea is still warm. Its temperature is 26 ° C. Even in October, many vacationers continue to swim. Water finally cools in November.

Key points





Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * occupies the first line. To the beach at hand. Eight kilometers away is the Dim Dam. Vacationers have at their disposal a spacious deep pool with fresh water, accessories for self-cooking meat on an open fire and charcoal, a sun terrace and aerotherapy.

The aquazone is served by a bar. It offers refreshing drinks and ice cream. The pool offers breathtaking views of the expanse of the Mediterranean Sea. The coast is also visible from the hotel rooms. The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable pastime.

Rules of residence

The reception of Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 *, whose staff is responsible for receiving new customers, is open 24 hours a day. It features a private beach, free rental bicycles and sports equipment. Employees of the information service assist in finding profitable rental offers for cars, yachts and pleasure boats. Near the hotel there is a car parking. Entrance to it without passes.

The hotel practices early reception. Check-in starts at 08:00. You need to leave the room from 10:30 to 12:00. At the reception, cards are accepted that are connected to the international systems American Express, Visa, MasterCard, Maestro. To pay for the number you need to present an identification card. Judging by the reviews of Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 *, the special wishes of travelers are accepted, but they are far from always fulfilled. This point must be taken into account.

Service





Hotel service corresponds to the declared category. It offers guests a common area equipped with a TV and upholstered furniture. There are vending machines selling sandwiches, appetizers, chips and crackers in the lobby. Next to them you can buy drinks and soda in bottles and cans.

Smoking tobacco throughout the Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * (Alanya) is strictly prohibited. Violation of this requirement faces an administrative penalty in the form of a fine. The modes and functioning of the air conditioning and heating systems are controlled by the administration. The rise to the upper floors of the building is carried out by a ventilated silent elevator.

The hotel staff speaks Russian, English and Turkish. Maids clean guest rooms daily. Sheets are changed regularly. Clean up the shared kitchen Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * (Mahmutlar). Take care of the garden and the beach. They monitor the health of sun loungers, umbrellas and sports equipment.

Gastronomy





Meals at the hotel are sold as part of the buffet system. All dishes offered at the distribution desk are included in the price of the vouchers. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the main dining room. The restaurant is open at scheduled times. Intermediate meals, tea parties and snacks await guests at the bar and bistro.

In reviews of Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * (Turkey), travelers say positively about the menu. There was an abundance of vegetables, fish, dishes of chicken and beef. The range of sweets can impress even the most spoiled gourmet.

Entertainment

During the holiday "Turkish Night", guests indulged in traditional oriental dishes. Spectators were entertained by dancers and musicians, singers, acrobats and gymnasts.

At Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey), bars are located by the pool and on the beach. Alcohol in them is sold only to persons over 18 years of age. There is no need to pay for beer and local wine. But for imported whiskey, cognac, vodka and gin will have to fork out. Ice cream is prepared in the bistro at the hours set by the administration. In high season, a long line is built up to the counter.

Around the hotel there are a large number of restaurants of various levels. They try dishes of European, Mediterranean, Turkish and Arabian cuisines.

Aquacomplex





Opposite the residential building is a swimming pool. It is filled with fresh water, which does not heat up. In their reviews of Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * (Turkey), customers complained that there were no free sunbeds at lunch. Had to take sun loungers in the morning.

Around the bowl is laid ceramic tile that does not slip. A fluid drainage system is installed around the perimeter. Depth increases gradually. Entrance is provided by metal stairs placed on both sides. Mattresses and umbrellas are issued by hotel staff.

A miniature bar is located under the stairs. It is a wide table full of drinks. An employee of Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey) pours tea, brews coffee, treats with lemonades. Service ends at 18:00. The work of the institution is seasonal. In autumn, winter and spring the bar is closed.

There is also a children's pool. It is round, shallow and separated from the common area by the sides. The water in it is always warm. No towel changes in the aquazone of the complex.

Mediterranean Sea

The beach is owned by the hotel. It is located opposite the residential building. From the Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * to the sea there is a well-groomed path. The beach is covered with yellow sand. The entrance to the water is flat and gentle. There are no large boulders and sharp stones.

Guests can use free sun loungers and umbrellas for free. At the height of the tourist season, there are not enough sunbeds for everyone. To take your place, you need to arrive before 09:00.

The beach is empty after 18:00. At this time, turn off showers, close toilets. Collect plastic furniture. Clean up the garbage and put things in order. In the morning, the beach will again be ready to receive vacationers. There are practically no waves in these places. Only autumn winds bring short-term storms to the water area of ​​Mahmutlar.

During the day, barkers from local cafes and eateries are constantly walking along the shore. Actively, and sometimes even obsessively advertise their services, photographers and animators. Tour agencies are happy to introduce travelers to their offers.

Additional services

At the disposal of guests Milano Beach Family Hotel 3 * (Mahmutlar) car rental. The choice of cars is satisfactory. These are mainly models of the budget category of famous Asian and European brands. For a private transfer to anywhere in Turkey you need to pay extra. The hotel accepts cash and plastic cards.

Bicycle rental is included in the tour price. Hourly and daily rent is provided. All vehicles are in good condition and in excellent technical condition. An office is equipped for business people. It has computer and copy equipment. Access to the hall for banquets and conferences is by prior arrangement.

The service of organizing holidays and family celebrations on the Mediterranean coast is paid separately. It is possible to rent a yacht or a pleasure boat. It offers a comprehensive restaurant service, performances by musicians, animation.

When planning an important event, do not forget to invite a professional photographer. He will not only perform thematic shooting, but also provide high-quality image processing.

Reviews





Despite the colorful description of the hotel, hotel reviews are not so optimistic. Guests complain about dilapidated furniture, leaking taps, inoperative household appliances. Travelers noted dirt near the pools and in the restaurant. Room cleaning was also of poor quality.

In July, the number of rooms booked exceeds the number of rooms available. Hence the mass of dissatisfied customers. They are placed in other hotels. The staff is almost not fluent in English. Nobody knows Russian. Everyone speaks Turkish. The maids and waiters do not wear uniforms. Employees are rarely at their jobs. They can easily fall apart on the couch and talk with friends in the lobby or restaurant.

Requests vacationers ignored. Breakdowns in rooms are eliminated after numerous reminders. The attitude is indifferent, sometimes rude. Cooks enter into an open confrontation with visitors, behave aggressively and defiantly.

For many, it turned out to be an unpleasant surprise that the Internet in the rooms was paid. Open access was only in the lobby and in the courtyard of the hotel. Instead of single occupancy, they practice the settlement of two vacationers in one room. It is almost impossible to get the number indicated in the ticket. The Turks paid for the apartments paid by the Russians. Some for a week, others for a day.

Excursions

In Mahmutlar itself, there is nothing to see. Travelers walk in the municipal park. It has a lot of exotic flowers. There are benches and fountains. In the evenings, Barbaros Avenue becomes especially noisy and crowded. Street artists and musicians are loudly and expressively inviting customers.

Craftsmen lay out their "treasures" right on the sidewalk. In their collections are coins, crafts made from pumpkins, wood, metal and textiles. Do not flatter yourself, these things can not claim authenticity and originality. These are just fakes that cost a penny. Therefore, bargain, and if in doubt, pass by.

Any sightseeing tour begins on the banks of the Dimchay River. It carries its crystal waters from the high peaks. In the midst of rains, her meek character is replaced by a stormy disposition. Its current sweeps everything in its path, carrying trees, bushes and colossal boulders torn to the sea into the sea. A surprisingly tasty trout is found in the river. The dishes from her meat have a delicate texture and a bright aroma.

The cost of visiting the remains of the fortress of Mamur with a tour of the ruins of the ancient settlement Anamurium is $ 250. The price is for a group of four adults. You can arrange your travel dates at the hotel reception or at the travel agency.
