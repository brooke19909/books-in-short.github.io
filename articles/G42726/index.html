Knitting blouse from the neck with a front closure

Spring is getting closer, more and more lovely dear ladies are reviewing their demi-season wardrobe and are tormented by thoughts that there is nothing in it ... Stunning! And the beautiful women of fashion want to amaze everyone every day with their originality and charm. And the best way to turn your clothes into an amazing collection of exclusive patterns is to knit. In general, knitting blouse spokes is easiest to do seamlessly - on circular knitting needles from the collar. For this model, you should draw as many loops on the knitting needles as required based on the circumference of the neck plus 5 loops on the clasp. How to calculate the number of loops, you probably know.



So, the knitting of women's blouses from the gate begins with a collar elastic of arbitrary width. Although, if you wish, you can execute the model without it, but then you should take care after completing the basic steps of crocheting the neck of the neck. You can also tie the collar and sew it after the model.



The knitting of this blouse model is not done in a circular fashion, but after each row the product is turned inside out. Then the model itself is knitted with the chosen pattern also with a knit revolution, but on each side of the product (at the end and beginning of the rows) 6 loops are sewn with an elastic band for fastening.



Immediately after the transition to the main panel, you should start making raglan. But before knitting blouses with needles gets its practical implementation, it is necessary to make the necessary calculations: the number of all loops (let it be, for example, 40) must be reduced to a specific calculated number, that is, subtract the hem and one side of the fastener, since it overlaps and does not participate in width. In our case, it looks like this: 40 - 2 (hem) - 6 (fastener) = 32. Now we divide this number by 4, 32: 4 = 8 - this is the number of loops of the back and each sleeve. Before us, it has a fastened clasp, so on both shelves there are 8 + 6 + 2 = 16, and each individually 16: 2 = 8 - together with the hem.
