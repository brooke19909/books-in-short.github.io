Hardware platform

Communication

Battery Life

Owners of the DIGMA HIT Q400 3G device in reviews often touch on the topic of autonomy. The developers equipped the smartphone with a 1400 mAh battery. This time is significantly reduced if you use the Internet, play or listen to music. In intensive operation, the battery will last for about 3 hours.
