Hotel Gafy Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh)

One of the most popular resorts in Egypt is Sharm El Sheikh. Here, tourists will enjoy a magnificent beach holiday, as well as hotels for every taste and budget. If you are planning to spend your holidays in Sharm El Sheikh and looking for a hotel offering comfortable accommodation at a reasonable price, then pay attention to the four-star Gafy Resort.



Location

Hotel "Gafi Resort 4 *" is located at the very end of Naama Bay in the resort of Sharm El Sheikh. The Old Town is 8 kilometers away. The center of Naama Bay can be reached on foot in just five minutes. The nearest airport is 15 kilometers from the hotel. So upon arrival you can quickly get to the hotel.



Gafi Resort: description, photo

Hotel Gafy Resort 4 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh) was opened in 1995. The hotel complex consists of a main two-story building and several 1- and 2-story blocks.

The housing stock of the hotel includes 204 rooms of the following categories: standard, family, superior and suites. Some of the apartments offer magnificent views of the Red Sea. Regardless of the category, each room has all the necessary furniture, air conditioning, cable TV, telephone, safe, minibar, bathroom and a furnished balcony or terrace. Cleaning is done every day, linen and towels are updated several times a week. For an additional fee, guests have the opportunity to use wireless Internet access and order room service.

Meals at the hotel "Gafi Resort" is carried out according to the "ol inclusive" system. During the day, guests can eat in the hotel restaurant, as well as in snack bars. Also, vacationers can order non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks in bars.

Gafi Resort has its own sandy beach, located 50 meters from the hotel. There is a sufficient number of sun loungers for everyone, there is a bar. Near the sea is a densely populated coral reef.

On the territory of the hotel you can spend time at the pools (there are five of them). In addition, the hotel complex has a spa center, gym, beauty salon, massage room and diving center.
