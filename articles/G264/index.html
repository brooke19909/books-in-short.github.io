There are several undeniable arguments in favor of the fact that you need to choose a vacation in Serbia. Of course, it is definitely worth a visit to Belgrade. The heart of this city is the Kalemegdan fortress, which is considered one of the oldest in Europe. And around it is a delightful park. Not far from the fortress is the oldest part of Belgrade. Here, museum lovers will find something to their liking. Be sure to visit the Skadarlie quarter, Ethnographic and National Museums, Ada Siganliya Park, the Old Palace, the Museum of Modern Art and the Bayrakli-Jamiya Mosque.

Holidays in Serbia are good because you can come here all year round. Some prefer winter, namely a trip to Mount Kopaonik, which is the pearl of the country. On Kopaonik, 60 km of pistes and 24 ski lifts are at the disposal of tourists, so skiing is only a pleasure. In addition, there is a very fun and active nightlife.
