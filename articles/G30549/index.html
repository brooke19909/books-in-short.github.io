How to get from Cologne to Amsterdam: transport choice, timetables, tickets, travel tips and recommendations

How to get from Cologne to Amsterdam? This is not an idle question. Cologne is located on the western border of Germany, not far from France and Holland. Therefore, tourists use this city when traveling around the countries of Europe as a transshipment point for traveling around the Benelux countries. After seeing the sights of Cologne, you can visit Amsterdam and other capitals of the Old World.

Distance from Cologne to Amsterdam

By Russian standards, these cities are located close to each other, the distance will be only 270 kilometers. If you chose the railway, then the travel time will be from 2.5 to 3 hours. The time spent on the bus will be much greater, about 5 hours, but there is an advantage here. Using the bus Cologne-Amsterdam, you can immediately see the city and its attractions if you buy a sightseeing tour.

These cities, despite being nearby, are completely different. Having spent not so much time, to be in another country, which in comparison with Germany is a contrast in essence a tempting idea. Therefore, many tourists take this opportunity, being in Cologne.

Travel by bus

This will be the cheapest way to get from Cologne to Amsterdam. As for the train, discounts are provided for pensioners, students and schoolchildren. The cost of one way can be around 19 euros. The price will depend on the carrier. Round trip can be reached at the price of a one-way train ride. But there is one drawback. Travel time can be up to 5 hours. It also depends on traffic congestion. If you get stuck in traffic, the time spent on the road can increase significantly. A bus ticket to Cologne-Amsterdam can also be booked online.

Bus tour

If you do not travel to Amsterdam by train, then you should use a sightseeing bus, which includes the cost of a round trip, city tours. The cost of such a trip will cost 54 euros. You can book a tour for 2-3 days. Most importantly, there is no need to decide how to get from Cologne to Amsterdam on your own. This trip can be booked online.

From Cologne to Amsterdam by car

Traveling along the Cologne-Amsterdam route by car yourself will bring a lot of pleasure. The driver learns about the border crossing by signs located on the side of the road or SMS messages coming to the mobile phone. You can book a car on reseller sites scanning offers from car rental companies.

Separately, it should be said about highways. On the territory of Germany should go along the E-25, in the Netherlands A-1, A-3-, A-12. The coating quality is excellent. They have several lanes along which traffic is carried in both directions. Route A-1 has peak lanes that open during busy hours.

On freeways, you should be extremely careful, as they are equipped with security cameras. If you exceed the recommended speed or other violations, you may be fined. The size of European fines exceeds Russian several times. There are gas stations and cafes along the slopes where you can eat.
