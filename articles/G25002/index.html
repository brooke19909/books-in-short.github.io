Defne Defnem Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Side is a Turkish resort that is visited by millions of tourists from all over the world during the year. And in these places there really is something to see. Arched gates, the ancient city, the picturesque Manavgat River, endless beaches, temples, theaters - these are just a small list of what you should pay attention to while staying at this resort. But for the rest to be complete, you need to find a decent hotel. And one of the best is rightfully considered Defne Defnem Hotel 5.





Good location

The location of the hotel is very important for a person who has come to rest. It is advisable to be closer to the center and near the sea. And I must say, Defne Defnem Hotel 5 in this regard is an excellent option. Firstly, the beach is located very close to the complex - literally three minutes walk. Side is also a little from the city center - 3 kilometers, this is a maximum of 10 minutes by car or minibus. Manavgat is quite close - the hotel is 8 km from its center. The airport is 62 km away. By the way, Antalya is not so far (72 kilometers). Staying on vacation in Turkey, you should at least once go to this city, which is the main resort of the country.

We can say with confidence: if you do not like to sit in a hotel and want to visit not only the beach, you should check into Defne Defnem Hotel 5, because in terms of location this is an excellent option.





Architectural features

If we talk about the most original and beautiful hotels in Turkey, then the Top 10 will definitely include Defne Defnem Hotel 5. Photos of this complex are simply fascinating. The snow-white ideal building of the hotel attracts the eyes of everyone - both guests of Turkey and local residents. It looks like a huge temple, made in the ancient Greek style, with arches, high columns, stunning picturesque sculptures. Defne Defnem Hotel 5 cannot be attributed to ordinary seaside hotels. This is a real work of architectural art, which perfectly combines oriental and Greek stylistic features. Also impressive is the height of the first floors - about five meters! And all this is decorated with real stone and artwork. In general, everything is done in the best ancient traditions, and all the guests like to be inside such splendor.





Accommodation options

The hotel has 351 rooms to choose from potential guests. There are 311 of them - standard, 20 - family (a bit larger than the first ones), 10 - Family Rooms type 2 and the same number of Family Rooms type 1. Apartments differ in size, in general they are equipped with everything you need for comfortable living and full healthy sleep. They have comfortable new beds, bedside tables, chairs, mirrors, etc. I must say that everything is new in hotel rooms, since the complex itself was opened only in 2013. Therefore, the apartments are beautiful, spacious, with perfect repair and modernly equipped. In these, you really can fully relax.

Room features

In addition to the fact that the apartments are made in the same style, which aesthetes will definitely like, in addition to the furniture they have, there is also the rest of the equipment that every person living in modern conditions is used to. The rooms of Defne Defnem Hotel 5 are equipped with high-definition plasma TVs, a balcony from which you can enjoy picturesque views of the resort, wireless high-speed Internet (naturally, free of charge), central air conditioning. And, of course, a bathroom with all the necessary accessories - it even has a telephone and a hairdryer. Also in the rooms are minibars. And finally, it is worth noting the attention of such pleasant nuances as a perfectly flat laminated floor, satellite TV, a safe and another phone (only in the room).





Sports activities

Defne Defnem Hotel 5 will definitely appeal to people who enjoy an active lifestyle. The hotel takes into account all the wishes of the guests. The facility has a tennis court, a room for table tennis, the ability to play volleyball or billiards. Of course, all this is completely free. Guests can also enjoy water sports (right on the beach), a gym and a fitness center. Moreover, this is not just rooms with a Swedish wall, mats and mirrors. These are fully equipped halls in which even professional bodybuilders will like to engage. You can also ride on a water slide, play golf or darts. Or just take a swim in the spacious, clean pool. Everyone will find such an occupation that will appeal to him. In an extreme case, you can just go to the shops (which are also available on the territory of the complex) and spend time shopping. Thus, it is quite possible to entertain yourself all day with entertainment without leaving the hotel.

You should not miss such a moment, being in the very center of Turkey! You can also soak in the jacuzzi or go to a professional massage therapist. The hotel also has a sauna and steam room. If you want to find a quick sunny tan, then for this on the territory of the complex there is a solarium. Representatives of the beautiful half of humanity will enjoy spending time in the SPA salon or in the Welness center. And also on the territory of Defne Defnem Hotel 5 (Side) there is a wellness salon where you can gain strength and rejuvenate by going through a set of specially designed procedures.
