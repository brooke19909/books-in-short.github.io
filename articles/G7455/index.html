Superb stay at the Titanic Palace Hotel (Hurghada)

In order to have a great vacation, it is very important to choose the right hotel. Hurghada offers its guests many types of accommodation. One of the most popular is the hotel "Titanic Palace" (Hurghada), which has a category of 5 stars. It is located 17 kilometers from the Old Town, on the seashore. It is 10 kilometers from the city airport. This is a fairly young hotel opened in 2007. Its guests can use not only the services of this hotel, but also the Titanic Beach complex, in whose territory there is a large water park with 14 slides.



Guests of the Titanic Palace Hotel (Hurghada) can stay both in a three-story building and in one-story bungalows. Those rooms that are located on the ground floor have direct access to the pool. Children are not allowed in them. The floor is tiled. In addition, 47 bungalows are available for guests. Rooms are 35 square meters. Due to the easy access to the pool, they are also not allowed to check in with children.

All rooms have a balcony or terrace, air conditioning and a safe. A telephone with an international line has been let down. There is a TV, a minibar, in the bathroom - a hairdryer, bath accessories.



For its guests, the Titanic Palace (Hurghada) provides All Inclusive meals - a buffet. Breakfast is available in the room upon reservation. Once a week free admission to the a-la-carte restaurant is allowed. There are several restaurants on site, including Italian. Here you can order an excellent dinner. There is an open-air fish restaurant on the beach. In addition, there is also a bar where in the daytime you can buy light snacks and drinks. Another bar is located near the hotel, there is also a lobby bar, a coffee shop.

"Titanic Palace" (Hurghada) has its own sandy beach. It is located just 500 meters from the hotel. For swimming, it is recommended to use special shoes so as not to injure your legs. Coral sandbank has a length of up to 500 meters. Beach equipment is provided free of charge. There is a pier about half a kilometer long, along which an electric car runs.





On the territory of the Titanic Palace Hotel (Hurghada) there is parking, all kinds of shops. For luggage guests have a luggage room. For those who came not only to relax, but also to work, an equipped conference room is provided, and there is Internet access. The hotel has a lot of paid services, including medical assistance, laundry, car rental, dry cleaning, currency exchange. For those who care about their beauty and health while relaxing, there is a fitness center, jacuzzi, steam bath and sauna. For an additional fee you can visit the SPA, use the services of a massage therapist.





Those who have already rested at the Titanic Palace Hotel (Hurghada) leave reviews mostly positive. The predominant factor is that there are quite a lot of sports and other entertainments. So, if you wish, you can go diving or surfing. You can ride a water slide, do water aerobics. The hotel has both an outdoor outdoor pool and an indoor one. An important plus is the varied leisure: daytime animation, a movie room, an entertainment center, an amphitheater, video games, table football. There is a tennis court, a gym, a volleyball court, billiards, bowling, a basketball court. A variety of services are provided for small vacationers.
