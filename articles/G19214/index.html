Making a devil costume for a girl with your own hands

A tight-fitting red dress, a formidable trident, small horns and defiant makeup - there is no doubt that this is a devil costume. The girl dressed in it looks feminine, mysterious and defiant, so this very outfit is very popular. Women love to wear it, since men cannot take their eyes off them.

Devil costume

The main detail of the costume is the scarlet color, which is a symbol of the infernal flame. It goes well with black, which is considered the color of evil and dark forces.





It is not necessary to go to the store for a bright outfit, for a start you can examine your wardrobe. There are likely to be things in red. You can choose a fluffy skirt with a petticoat, and a tight-fitting top or corset to it. If there is nothing suitable, then you can sew clothes by looking for a suitable pattern. The inherent attributes of the devil

You can wear anything you like, be it pants, a skirt or a dress, but if there are horns, a ponytail and a trident in the outfit, then no one will doubt that this is a devilish outfit.

To make all the attributes of the costume will be the strength of everyone who knows how to hold a thread with a needle in their hands. From a piece of black or red fabric, you can make a devil's cloak. The material just needs to be sewn or sewn by hand at the edges. To make it look like a cloak, in one of the ends you need to insert a lace or ribbon and tie it around the neck.

You can go a little further and make a cloak with a collar. In this case, the tape must not be passed along the edge, but at a distance of about 20 cm from it. When assembling the fabric, a kind of collar is obtained, and if you use dense matter, it will be more resistant.





Creating a devil costume for Halloween, you must take care of the horns. Of course, they can be purchased at the store, or you can save money by making the attribute yourself. The easiest option is to twist the small foil horns, wrap them with masking tape and leather (leatherette). They need to be attached to a hoop or pins.

The next thing to do is make the tail. Its base is made of wire, wrapped with synthetic winterizer and thread, giving the tail the desired shape. Next, sew a cover of red fabric (faux leather or fur is suitable). The finished cover is put on the base and a loop is made so that it can be attached to a belt or clothing.
