Crochet dresses: the secrets of creating fashionable summer things

DIY jerseys are still fashionable and popular. If we crochet dresses, it is worth considering some secrets. This will be discussed in this article.





Crochet dresses for summer 2014

In anticipation of the warm season, you need to worry in advance about filling the wardrobe with stylish gizmos. Will the new season be different from the old? What exactly are fashion trends? What were the crocheted summer dresses last season? 2013 had one common feature - the predominance of floral motifs in the ornaments. To create them, in most cases, the fabric was sewn from separately made parts. The chaotic arrangement of elements and the filling of a free background with an impromptu viscous were also popular. Other fashion trends were beach and wedding dresses. When embodying the ideas, the craftswomen, no doubt, were in the spotlight, attracting those around with exquisite femininity and stylish charm. Do you want to try to make something really unique and exclusive with your own hands? Start with simple models, taking into account all the features of the work described below.





Top secrets

Want to be at the height of fashion? Then it is advisable to have crocheted dresses in the wardrobe (photo). Schemes of most summer models consist of separately made openwork elements. Since the fabric is reticulated, a thin lining is almost always made. As a rule, light fabrics or a knitted fabric are used for sewing it. By planting a knitted blank on such a basis, you don‚Äôt have to worry about deformation. Therefore, this option is the most acceptable and practical. The exception is beach dresses. They are specially matched to the type of coarse mesh. It is their ability to shine through that makes such holiday clothing very fashionable and popular. You can also make not only beach products monolayer. This technology is suitable if the pattern is not too openwork. When we crochet dresses, we can periodically try on, as there are no interfering knitting needles. Before starting work, the selected pattern must be tried in practice. After completing the preparation, you may have to make some adjustments.





We create a unique summer little thing. Operating procedure

The first stage is the creation of a product pattern. Different measurements may be required depending on the model. The main ones are the thigh circumference, the chest circumference and the length of the product. The width of the pattern will be equal to half of one of the first two dimensions, depending on which one is larger. As a rule, most dresses have a simple cut without additional tucks. Therefore, in order to get a fashionable thing in the simplest way, you need to knit a canvas in the form of a rectangle to the level of the armhole. Next, adhere to the pattern, reducing the width for the design of the armhole or expanding and getting a one-piece product. Openwork has a tendency to stretch, so use this property in your work. When we crochet dresses, we need to constantly monitor the compliance of the workpiece with a pattern, taking into account adjustments for deformation. The last stage of the work is the edging of the armhole and neck with several rows of impromptu inlay. Imagine and create unique fashionable models!
