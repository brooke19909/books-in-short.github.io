Holiday Village "Svetlaya Polyana" (Kazan): description, prices, reviews

To have a good time in nature, it is not necessary to have a country house. You can go to the recreation center "Svetlaya Polyana".

Description





Rest with family or friends, holding weekends, holidays or various events - the Svetlaya Polyana recreation center (Kazan) has all this. You can rent a house in a picturesque place on the banks of the Volga both for a day and for several days and weeks.

On a vast territory you can fry kebabs and cook other dishes on the grill and bonfire, relax on the sandy beach and fish, walk in the woods. Also at the recreation center there is a bath / sauna. For lovers of fishing there is the opportunity to rent a boat, or you can come with your own.

Cost of accommodation and additional services





You can stay at the recreation center "Svetlaya Polyana" all year round. Accommodation in houses of two types of comfort. The usual houses are double and quadruple. They contain everything you need: furniture, a small refrigerator, and a shower. Superior houses are designed for ten and five vacationers. In addition to the standard set of comfortable furniture (beds, bedside tables, table and chairs, wardrobe and so on) they have a fully equipped bathroom (toilet and shower with hot and cold water), satellite TV, heating and air conditioning, microwave and refrigerator . For cooking, a minimum set of dishes, glasses and cups is provided. That is all to make your stay as comfortable as possible.

Accessible rest for everyone - this is how the recreation center Svetlaya Polyana (Kazan) positions itself. Prices for economy-class houses start from 500 rubles per day. Rental of luxury homes varies from 3 to 7.5 thousand rubles. It depends on the number of people, duration and date of arrival. Prices are indicated at the end of 2016 - beginning of 2017.

The cost of a bathhouse, rental of fishing tackle and other additional services must be recognized on the spot.

Where is





The recreation center "Svetlaya Polyana" (Kazan) is located in the village of Borovoye Matyushino. It is only twenty kilometers from the city. By your car you need to go onto the R-239 highway, and then turn right into the village of Voronovka. Having passed it along the Matyushinsky tract, you will reach the goal.
