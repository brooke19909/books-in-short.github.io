Hotel Gunes House Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Alanya): description, reviews of tourists

It's no secret that Turkey is one of the most popular tourist countries. And today, many tourists choose a resort town called Alanya as a vacation spot. This is a wonderful place, which is famous not only for the clear sea and wonderful beaches, but also for a huge number of modern hotel and entertainment complexes. One of them is the hotel Gunes House Hotel 3 *. Where exactly is this hotel located? What conditions of rest can you count on? The answers to these questions are interesting to many travelers.

Where is the hotel?





For many travelers, an important issue is the location of a hotel. And in this regard, the hotel complex Gunes House Hotel 3 * stands out because it is located in the very center of the popular resort city of Alanya, and only 100 meters from the sea shore. Nearby are shops, restaurants, night clubs, entertainment complexes and interesting sights.

The nearest international airport is located in the territory of another large tourist city - Antalya. The distance to it is about 126 km, but do not worry, because travel agencies, and the hotels themselves, provide a shuttle service.

Brief description of the territory and infrastructure

The hotel complex Gunes House Hotel 3 * is relatively small, but its territory is clean, well-groomed and competently decorated. The main part is a five-story hotel building, built in a modern style. The courtyard is reserved for the pool, sun terrace. Despite its rather modest size, the area is decorated with palm trees, figured bushes, beautiful flower beds and bright exotic plants - reviews of tourists indicate that it is always a pleasure to spend time here.

Hotel complex Gunes House Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): room description





This hotel is considered relatively small - in its territory there are 68 spacious standard rooms. All rooms are clean, spacious and bright. They are furnished with a sufficient amount of working and modern furniture. From the room there is access to a private balcony, from where you can enjoy views of the city, the sea or the indoor pool.

As for household appliances, the room has a telephone, a working air conditioning system, a radio, and a TV on which you can watch satellite channels.





The room comes with a fairly spacious bathroom, where you can take a shower or relax in the bath at any time of the day. There is also a toilet, sink, large wall mirror. Naturally, each guest receives a set of clean towels, which are changed daily.

What kind of food does the hotel offer?

Like most hotels in Turkey, Gunes House Hotel 3 * operates on an all-inclusive basis. Guests are offered full-fledged meals - they are invited to the main restaurant three times a day, where a buffet is served for them. The choice of dishes here is quite large - there are cold and hot snacks, meat and fish dishes, desserts and fresh fruits. In addition, in the restaurant and bar you can get drinks (of course, only Turkish production, you will need to pay extra for imported ones).

Beach and water recreation

The hotel offers its guests a visit to the public beach, which is located just 100 meters from the residential building - a direct road leads from here to the shore. The beach here is sandy and pebble. The entrance to the sea is convenient, but in some places there are stones near the shore, and the bottom is covered with pebbles, so it is recommended to use special shoes.





Plank beds and sun loungers are in sufficient quantity on the shore, but their rent is paid separately. On the other hand, their price is not so high. People who prefer a more active holiday will also not be bored, as here you can ride a boat, boat, water ski, do parasailing, snorkeling or even make a real scuba dive. In addition, you can visit other public beaches of the city, so you will surely find a place that will appeal to you.

Additional service

Of course, the hotel offers some additional services that will help make your vacation more comfortable. In particular, you can always use the dry cleaning and laundry services - they cost not so much. You can rent a safe from the administrator, where your valuables will be completely safe. The hotel has a fairly spacious conference room, which is suitable not only for meetings, gatherings and seminars, but also for various festivals.

Entertainment at the hotel complex Gunes House Hotel 3 * (Alanya)





Alanya is a great place to stay. This resort area is very popular among tourists, famous for its genuine hospitality, vibrant sights and modern comfort. As for the hotel itself, here you can have a good time.

The courtyard is reserved for a pool where you can swim and freshen up. Nearby there are a sufficient number of umbrellas and sunbeds. The hotel also has a games room. You can also play darts, chess and board games. You should definitely visit a real Turkish bath. You can relax in a more familiar sauna. Also, professional massage therapists work at the hotel.

Are there any conditions for living with children?

Since many parents today rest with their children, the issue of the availability of appropriate amenities is extremely important for them. Some of them are available at the hotel. In particular, a cot can be delivered to the room upon request. The restaurant has high chairs, and despite the lack of a special menu, you can easily pick up something really tasty and healthy for your child. For entertainment, there is also a small section of the pool, which is spacious enough, but not too deep - children can have a great time here. Mini-clubs and children's animation are not available on site. Nevertheless, there are a lot of entertainment centers for children in the city, where you should definitely bring children.
