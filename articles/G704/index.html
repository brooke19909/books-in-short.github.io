Headphones Philips SHC1300 - review and reviews

The leader in the middle price category of wireless headphones is the Philips SHC1300. It is about them that will be discussed in this article. Their cost is about 1300 rubles. Since they are wireless, sound is transmitted through the emission of infrared waves.



Specifications

Headphones dynamic, closed type. As already mentioned, they are wireless and overhead. The device is capable of reproducing frequencies from 18 to 20 thousand Hz. Weigh 410 g; have a sensitivity of 108 dB.

The mount is represented by a headband. The membrane in the design has a diameter of 32 mm. The sound signal is transmitted using infrared waves, which are received at a distance of 5 to 7 meters. This figure depends on the number of walls separating the Philips SHC1300 headphones (reviews below) from the sensor. The farther the device is, the worse the sound. The frequency range of the transmitter is 2.7-2.8 MHz. Since the headphones are wireless, there are no questions with a suitable connector for the device.

Food type AAA. The number of batteries is two. From the battery, the headphones can work up to 30 hours.



Appearance and convenience

Philips SHC1300 headphones are an on-ear device that has a standard appearance. There are no special design innovations here. The case is black. It looks quite massive.

When wearing the device does not bring discomfort, rather the opposite. You can use headphones to listen to music, talk on Skype, play games, work on a computer and so on. The headband is regulated by a slight movement, finding the right size is not difficult. The ear pads are made of soft foam, so even with prolonged wear they do not cause discomfort.

Sound

The Philips SHC1300 device has pretty good sound for wireless headphones. Bass and treble are heard well. The speaker is made of a ferrite-type magnet, its size is 32 mm. The frequency range is 18-20 thousand Hz.



Benefits

The Philips SHC1300 device has advantages that captivate many consumers. What are the advantages that force you to purchase these headphones? We are talking about a good sound, beautiful design, lack of wires, a base for fishing and signaling, which is included in the kit, the quality of all materials and parts, the availability of a regulator, excellent ergonomics, battery operation, compatibility with almost any type of equipment, low cost. In addition to the above, it is worth noting that the base can work with several pairs of headphones at the same time.

disadvantages

Like any device, the Philips SHC1300 has its drawbacks. Consider the main ones:

  • sound is transmitted only up to five meters;
  • ear pads wear out too quickly;
  • playback quality deteriorates if walls or partitions appear between the device and the sensor.

Reviews

Reviews about the device are somewhat controversial. Those who purchased headphones just to listen to music without inconvenience to their family are happy with the purchase. Those who expected too good sound and quality, respectively, no. There is little official information about the device, so you have to rely on the information that customers provide.

What comes with the Philips SHC1300? Instructions, batteries, adapter, adapter (6.5 mm) and transmitter. The manual describes everything quite informatively. Many of those who purchased these headphones confirm that you must always be parallel to the transmitter, otherwise there will be interference during playback.

The modulator looks modest. He has special bulbs that light up when turned on, as well as four legs that help him to stay steady.

Buyers note that the sound, in principle, is clear and good. You can adjust its quality and volume directly on the headphones themselves. It's comfortable. The device is lightweight, does not press on the ears. Warranty, as a rule, is given for them for a year, but for many these headphones work much longer.



Wireless devices have one feature. When the battery runs down, they suddenly begin to hiss and wheeze. However, the described model does not have this problem.

Ear pads, according to many, are terrible in quality. However, if you use the headphones carefully, you can significantly delay the moment when they become unusable. Some ear cushions tear after six months, while others - after a couple of years of use.

According to the manufacturer, the headphones are quite high quality and work well throughout the warranty period. Sound distortion is likely, but not a single device of this type is safe from this problem.
