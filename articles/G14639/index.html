Where to relax in Altai in the summer with the whole family?

Holidays in Russia are gaining more and more popularity; the inhabitants of our country no longer want to go abroad so much. The main reason for this is the crisis, so many Russians went to the Crimea and Altai in the summer of 2015. Where can I relax in the Altai Mountains in the summer? It is with this issue that potential vacationers turn to travel agencies.

Why Altai?





Despite the fact that holidays in Altai are not in great demand, it is here that you can enjoy the real Russian nature, which has not lost its originality and naturalness. Resting here is not a cheap pleasure, but it's worth it, you can easily forget about all your problems and unresolved tasks and relax.

All the fuss here recedes into the background, and a person learns to live in harmony with his own "I", which is why many people call Altai a magical place. The worldview is simplified so much that even simple things appear in a completely different perspective, many businessmen and busy people come here to dump their thoughts and just live aimlessly for several days.

Before the trip, it is best to clarify where you can relax in Altai in the summer at a low price, since the cost of a tourist permit may be higher than you expected. As an option - to go on vacation and the "savage", but this is best done in a friendly company than alone, it is also worth taking care of organizing your vacation in advance.

Teletskoye Lake and its surroundings





If you are thinking about where to relax in Altai in the summer, and are looking for the most suitable place, pay attention to the coast of Lake Teletskoye, one of the main attractions of Altai. There are a lot of campsites and tourist camps, but tourists are not spoiled by nature. More than a dozen different species of fish spawn here, in their usual habitat, for several hundred years.

Especially for tourists, boat trips are organized around the lake, during which you can see a large number of bays and gorges of the reservoir. The banks are steep, so it is not recommended to walk on them alone. Local guides advise tourists to definitely visit the Korbu waterfall, where the maximum depth of the lake is more than 320 meters. Pure clear water, saturated with oxygen, is the most suitable condition for the local fauna and flora, which you must definitely familiarize yourself with.

Local tourist centers offer comfortable rest for all comers at reasonable prices. The cost of a week of living for an adult in a comfortable cottage with all conditions ranges from 4.5 to 27 thousand rubles, for a child - from 2 to 14 thousand rubles, respectively. The price of accommodation directly depends on when exactly the tourist went to rest.

Katun

Another option for where to relax in Altai inexpensively in the summer is Katun, the largest river in the Altai Mountains. Chemal district, for the most part of which Katun flows, is located in the central part of the republic. The wonderful climate, forest and mountain landscapes, as well as its convenient location have turned the river banks into one of the most popular tourist resorts in the Altai Territory.

It is here that tourists are offered not only summer, but also winter vacations. Beaches, discos, sports, excursions, rafting - all this is open to tourists in the summer, while in winter they offer skiing, snowmobiling and snowmobiling. Fans of summer extreme sports will appreciate the bungee, which operates next to the Chemal hydroelectric station.

The prices here are more affordable, you can even find a place in a hotel for 450-500 rubles, this is the minimum cost of a daily stay, the average price here is 900-1000 rubles. The number of days of stay is not fixed, you can set it yourself by paying the appropriate amount.

Karakol lakes





If you still do not know where to relax in Altai in the summer, pay attention to the Karakol lakes. Despite the fact that you can get there only as part of an excursion group, you will be delighted with the tourist complex, located at an altitude of up to 2 kilometers above sea level. Lakes differ from each other in the chemical composition of water, its color and temperature.

Each pond is surrounded by a unique flora, here you can find cedar taiga, alpine tundra and alpine meadows - all this is concentrated on a space of several tens of square kilometers. Near the lakes there is a recreation center, consisting of several wooden houses.

For thrill lovers





If you like extreme holidays and are looking for where it is better to relax in Altai in the summer, you should think about climbing Mount Belukha. The height of the natural formation is more than 4.5 kilometers, it consists of 50% glaciers, therefore hot days are rarely given out here. Cases of abnormal colds in July were repeatedly recorded, the temperature dropped to -20 degrees Celsius.

It is here that you can travel through the forests, pick berries, as well as look at snow leopards, sometimes there are lynxes. It was on Belukha that the famous artist Roerich created most of his paintings, which became famous throughout the world. At first glance at his canvases, it may seem that such flowers simply do not exist, but this opinion will change as soon as you find yourself in Altai.

Holidays for the whole family

If you are wondering where to spend summer time with children in Altai, feel free to go to the Russian House, which is located near the village of Yazovo. The tourist base is decorated in the original Russian style, used several decades ago, thanks to this, all vacationers remember traditional values: the family and its foundations.

Especially for children, animators work at the tourist base, which constantly hold contests and support the work of local attractions. The whole family can visit the local farmyard to admire the local fauna and feed its representatives. Accommodation prices are very affordable, children under 3 years old can relax here for free.

Rest on your own





Some tourists like to relax on their own, however, such a vacation can be afford only in the summer. Where to rest in the Altai summer savage, everyone decides for himself. Experienced tourists recommend going deep into the republic, for example, to the Ulagansky pass, where you can relax and go fishing. There is no electricity, running water or mobile communications, this will allow you to relax and reunite with nature.

Also popular is the Lebed River, in which you can even swim in the summer, as the water warms up to high temperature. Near the village of Turochak there is a caravan park where you can rent all the tourist ammunition for rent, make a fire in the space specially designated for this, and also take a shower. You can spend the night in the park for only 50 rubles, which is very profitable.

Do you need treatment?

This resort city is also very popular, since it is in it that the tourism industry is actively developing. If we talk about where you can relax in Altai in the summer at low prices, Belokurikha is the best option. It is worth noting that the city has a huge number of resorts, where you can heal and put your body in order.

Of course, living in such resorts will require certain investments. The cost of one day in local sanatoriums ranges from 1900 to 3500 rubles, depending on the time of year. The price, as a rule, includes 2-3 meals a day, you can also use the services of your existing infrastructure (gyms, swimming pools, bowling alleys, etc.).

And if you want to swim?





If you still have not decided where to relax in Altai in the summer, and you want to swim and soak up the sun, you can go to Lake Aya, in which the water warms up to +25 degrees Celsius in summer. A large number of excursions, water activities and parties will allow you to relax and take a break from all your usual activities.

It is here that you can stay in small summer houses and expensive hotels, while the cost of living is relatively low. The cost of an overnight stay and housing can range from 350 to 4000 rubles, depending on the time of year. The easiest way to relax there in the summer is on weekdays, when there are not so many visitors.

History lovers

All those who are interested in antiquity do not even have to choose where to relax in Altai in the summer, the answer is obvious - Soloneshensky district. It is there that you can find architectural monuments of the Stone Ages and the Middle Ages. Unique natural complexes, animal and plant worlds attract tourists from Russia and the whole world.

Thanks to the nearby motorway, the tourist area is very much in demand. Here you can admire the largest waterfall in Altai, called the "Giraffe", its height is 72 meters. Tourists can settle in a local holiday complex, some villagers are also ready to provide guests of the region with accommodation for a moderate fee.

On a tour of Altai!





Travelers will surely enjoy walking, water, horse and combined tours in Altai. Where to relax in Altai in the summer is quite simple to decide, the main thing is to choose the most suitable ticket. The cost of such a trip can be completely different, everything will depend on the type of tour chosen.

Prices for walking tours range from 16 to 19 thousand rubles, water - from 17 to 22, horseback - from 15 to 30, and combined - from 18 to 25 thousand rubles. When buying a tour, it is necessary to clarify the complexity of the route, all the skills necessary for its passage, as well as get acquainted with the list of things that you need to take with you.

Everyone can also go to the rafting, which is organized every three days on local rivers. The event was assigned the III-IV category of complexity, since the rivers behave quite obstinately. However, the natural beauty, cave paintings, caves and barrows - all this will become a pleasant highlight in water travel, and will open up the secrets of the early civilizations that lived on these lands.

Summary

Those who rest in Altai always come back with a huge baggage of positive impressions, knowledge and even skills. So, where to relax in Altai in the summer? Reviews will tell you where amazing places are. We presented them in the article. Beautiful nature, well-developed infrastructure, the opportunity to meet new people and visit places that are still poorly understood - all this is worth a lot.

Most tourists come to Altai in the summer, so you need to buy tickets before the start of the season, then there is a chance to get a place with a beautiful view at a low cost. In winter, resorts are also in demand, but low temperatures do not always allow spending time the way tourists want. However, in the recreation areas they meet the guests and conduct festive and entertainment events right in the hotels.
