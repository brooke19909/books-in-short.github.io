Horn Irina is a talented, bright master. Knitting and Style Tips

Irina (Karpenko) Horn is a name, certainly, known among masters of hand knitting. The author of many enchanting models of clothing made by crocheting (Irina basically crochets it), a blogger who gives valuable advice to beginners and reveals the secrets of craftsmanship, stylist and model. Irina photographs her creations, talentedly composing them with the details of her own wardrobe, she always acts as a model herself.





Horn Irina, nee Karpenko, was born and raised in Omsk. Now she lives and works in Germany, in Hamburg, devoting almost all of her free time to her favorite hobby - knitting.

Many clothing models are cited by thousands of copycats and published in knitting magazines, such as:

  • "Ksenia."
  • "Everything for a woman."
  • On many sites dedicated to knitting, for example Knit-Crochet.ru.

On pages in all social networks, Irina has a lot of friends knitters and needlewomen who draw inspiration from ideas and author's avatars, photos and descriptions of which she generously shares.

Tops, pullovers

In her blog for those who are just mastering knitting, Irina Horn gives a description of many author's models and provides tips for making and combining to create a harmonious image. In the arsenal of the craftswoman there are quite a lot of tops and pullovers. Irina composes them with shorts, trousers, skirts, with shirts that can be put on over the top or put on under a pullover, cardigans and elongated vests, both knitted and sewn from fabric.





Many models made in soothing colors, for example, milky shades, Horn Irina prefers and uses them for many years, composing them in different ways. But bright and bold tones are present in her collections. Very interesting ethnic models of a dress, a sundress with a pattern combining rich colors, or a red skirt on the floor, or a green cardigan, or a purple jacket. In the hands of Irina, any thread of any color turns into a unique model that every craftswoman wants to repeat, and every fashionista has to have in her wardrobe.

Cardigans and vests

Among the latest works of Irina Horne, knitting of vests and cardigans is particularly distinguished by bright and juicy shades.





Bright red, juicy orange, bold pink cardigans look new, relevant and stylish in combination with matching trousers and blouses.

As always, the image is complemented by accessories: handbags, belt, necklace or an interestingly knitted scarf.

Extravaganza colors Irina Horn





I especially want to note the work with color. Irina Horn is incredibly talented in combining colors in sets. Knitted skirts, tunics, shorts, cardigans of various colors are combined with clothing items of a different texture in a very interesting color combination and complemented with accessories. In one of the blogs, Irina gave advice to beginners on how to combine colors. She said that if there is no intuition or confidence in how to correctly combine these or other shades, you can buy a woman‚Äôs scarf where there is a tone that needs to be arranged and boldly combined with any colors that are present on this scarf.

Accessories

A very interesting detail - neck knitted accessories, small scarves with many small details to match the skirt, for example, which are almost always present in photographs of knitted patterns by Irina Horn. They can be used both as scarves and as belts, depending on the mood and configuration of the outfit.

Irina has very interesting knitted handbags, which she can change and redo over time if she wants to revive her favorite model.





Stylist

For some time, Irina has been publishing notes on new items and trends in fashion on her blog. She writes her opinion and gives photographs from fashion shows, with tips and selections of experts on which colors, textures or models to prefer for different occasions to women of different ages and weight categories.

In general, we can safely say that Irina Horn and crochet are synonyms, even more so, talented and unique knitting, elevated to the rank of art.
