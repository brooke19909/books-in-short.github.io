Which fabric is better to choose

The easiest way to work with knitwear. The winning option is velor or velvet, which shimmers beautifully due to its fine shiny pile. It is this fabric that will look gorgeous in combination with fur. How to make a trouser pattern

We take the pants, turn them inside out and put one leg in the other, after we put the thing in this form directly on the wrong side of the fabric for cutting and first circle the back step and middle seams, and then the side and front step seams, while gradually unwrapping the thing so that it lay on a plane without folds. If this procedure seems unbearable, then you can take out of service pants and cut them along the front, back and step seams. After that, lay out the pants panel stitched along the side line and circle on the fabric with a shallow or remnant.

Wolf muzzle and tail are essential parts of a costume

How to make a scary wolf costume with your own hands? It is very important for the boy that the hero evokes at least admiration, and even better if he looks intimidating. For this outfit, the face of the wolf is mandatory - on a hat or hood.

So, first with a centimeter tape measure the circumference of the child‚Äôs head and the depth of the future cap - from the back of the head to the middle of the forehead. After they cut out the rectangle, sew it together, folding the smaller sides, and on the top of the cap make tucks like a six-link.



The wolf costume for the boy is almost ready. It remains to think about how the hat will hold on, because our children are such fidgets! And if you take into account outdoor games with Santa Claus and competitions ... By the way, the muzzle can be attached not only to the cap. An elastic bandage or hood is suitable for this purpose.

Then proceed to the design of the wolf face. In order to sew the mouth, four long semi-ovals are cut out, laying in the seam cutting triangles of felt, which will play the role of teeth. After these details are turned out and stuffed with synthetic winterizer. They are sewn together at the base, putting between them a half-felt of red felt, which will serve as a tongue. Further, one semicircle is cut out of fur and velor, having previously prepared triangular ears embedded in the seam. The part is turned out and stuffed with a synthetic winterizer, after which they sew a wolf's mouth to it .
Eyes are cut out of white felt, and pupils and nose are cut out of black. These elements are glued with hot glue or sewn with a blind seam. When the wolf‚Äôs head is ready, it is attached to a hat or hood.

A New Year's wolf costume is unthinkable without a tail. To complete this detail, you will need a rectangular section of fur 7-8 cm wide and length from the waist to the knees of the child. The tail can be sewn, or you can attach it to the button. The advantage of the second option is obvious - when the baby gets tired of this element, it can simply be unfastened.
