How to check the rest of the traffic on the "Megaphone"? How to find out the rest of the traffic on the Megafon modem?

This article will explain how to check the rest of the traffic on Megafon. All methods are absolutely free. Based on the positive and negative sides of each, recommendations will be given regarding their use.





What are the ways?

Today, there are many ways with which you can learn about how to check the rest of the traffic on Megafon. Among them are the following:

  • using a modem and its software;
  • using a special request;
  • call to the service center;
  • by sending SMS to a number specially reserved for these purposes;
  • on the official website of the mobile operator.


Each of them will be considered in detail in the framework of this material.

We use a modem

This method is most often used to find out the rest of the traffic. On the Megafon modem, this can be done using the software utility that comes with the kit. In its interface there is a button "Statistics". By clicking on it, you can find out the number of megabytes or gigabytes remaining. But at the same time, at the bottom of the screen there will be a message stating that the data is approximate and in order to obtain more accurate information, you need to use another method. This method works only on a computer or laptop and gives only an approximate amount of remaining traffic.

Phone inquiry

You can find out the rest of the traffic to Megafon using your phone or smartphone. For this, this operator has a special team. The order of its dialing is as follows: enter * 105 # and press the call button. In response, we get the rest of the traffic. In principle, this method also successfully works on some models of modems. Another nuance that needs to be taken into account is that such a request can be sent only from phones that can work in the network of this operator. That is, those mobile devices that are flashed under "Beeline", "MTS" or "TELE2" cannot not only send such a request, but even register on the Megafon network. This must be taken into account when trying to find out in this way the number of gigabytes or megabytes remaining.

Official site

Another way to check the rest of the traffic on Megafon is based on using the official website of this mobile operator. To be more precise, the Master Guide system is used. In this case, you need to perform the following manipulations:

  1. We launch any of the installed browsers on the PC.
  2. Using the search engine, we find the official website of this mobile operator.
  3. First, we go through the registration procedure in this system. We fill out a form with personal data. We indicate the password for access to the service "Master Guide" in it.
