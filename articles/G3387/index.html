How to make a patch at home

Have you torn your pants or sweatshirt? Do not throw away your favorite clothes. You can make an application. On how to make a patch with your own hands, read below.

Lemon

How to make a patch? The easiest way to make it is to embroider a beautiful pattern on a dense material. For example, you can make an application in the form of a lemon. An outline should be cut from felt. It can be a whole lemon or its slice. Cut the felt from the felt along the contour and wrap it around. This is most conveniently done with a sewing machine or overlock.





You can make a beautiful edge of the patch with your hands, for example, wrap the product with a hook or a seam over the edge. If you make an application in the form of a lemon, you should embroider a highlight on the product. If the patch looks like a lobule, then you need to make white streaks. They can also be embroidered in two ways: either on a typewriter, or manually. Do not forget to depict the bones with a few stitches.

Dog

How to make a patch of beads? First you need to draw the contour of the animal's face and cut it out of felt. Now we should outline the main features. The contour of the eyes, nose and ears is applied to the fabric with a pencil. Now we take beads of several shades (black and white) and get to work.





First, embroider a white border. Then we outline the circle of the muzzle with black beads. Need to embroider strictly in shape. That is, the forehead should form a semicircle, the nose - a drop, and the eyes - ovals. Look at the pattern attached above and repeat the shape.

Patch

This application looks very original. How to make a patch patch? We will make it from two materials: the first is felt, and the second is thin faux fur. We make patterns of two rectangles with rounded edges. One of them should be larger, the other less. From felt, we cut the part for a larger pattern, from fur - at least. Now you should place the blanks on top of each other and stitch them on a typewriter or sew them by hand.

We pass to the most interesting part - to the decor. In our case, it will be a broken heart. Embroidering on fur is inconvenient, so the first thing you need to do is make a substrate. On a typewriter, we sew a rectangle in the center of the patch and make a border for it. On it, with a pencil, we depict a broken heart. Now, by typewriter or manually, fill the contour with red threads. To make the patch more realistic, small circles should be embroidered in a chaotic manner on two sides of the center.

Ice cream

Today it is fashionable to decorate clothes with food images. How to make a patch of your own theme with your own hands? From felt you need to cut the outline of ice cream. The material should be used beige, as it will form the basis of the cup. Now you need to divide the workpiece into two parts. One of them will be a glass, the other - ice cream. We begin to make the application with the fact that with beads we sew it along the contour.





Now on the cup you need to depict a diamond-shaped pattern. Once it is ready, you can proceed to the decor of ice cream. Beads and beads should be sewn in a chaotic manner. Optionally, you can even glue rhinestones. The main task is to fill the entire space so that there are no gaps. You can decorate with a finished patch not only clothes, but even a bag.
