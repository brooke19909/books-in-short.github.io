What do iPad owners do?

Today, mobile technology has stepped far forward, and a huge number of subscribers of mobile carriers have iPad devices. IPad Settings

Find out the MTS SIM-card number, iPad settings will help. To do this, open the "Settings" menu, then go to the "Basic" tab, and then select the "About device" option. In the column "Number for cellular data" you will find the number of your SIM card.

Special MTS Service

To solve the above problem, you can use the MTS service, which the mobile operator has specifically developed for iPad owners.
