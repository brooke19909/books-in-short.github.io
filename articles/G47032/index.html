How to make a DIY choker tattoo

The name "choker" in English translates as "strangler." This immediately focuses on its features. This piece of jewelry should fit snugly around your neck. It is also called a ‚Äúchoker tattoo‚Äù, because from afar on the neck it looks like an openwork pattern.

Popular decoration of the 90s

Twenty years ago, the popularity of this simple jewelry knew no bounds. In any store you could find sets of collars, bracelets and rings. Chokers were black, white, rainbow, and sometimes even glowed in the dark. Such a trinket could be won in an amusement park or beach attraction. Fashionistas wore them regardless of the weather and with any wardrobe. And many even knew how to make a choker tattoo from a fishing line on their own. Since the creation of a choker does not take much time and does not require effort, craftsmen could make a lot of such jewelry in a day, and sometimes they even took orders from friends to make this popular jewelry.



What you need to make a DIY choker tattoo

This task will not require special skills from you. Even those who have never done needlework are guaranteed to get a good result. To make a tattoo choker with your own hands, you do not need special devices or rare materials. All we need is a fishing line of the desired color and thickness and an office clothespin in order to pinch it for a while.

If the fishing line is thin, you can weave in two or three threads. This allows you to combine different colors, and the product will turn out more openwork. Sometimes, instead of fishing line, other, similar materials are used: an elastic cord, a wire from the headphones, a rubber thread. Variations in the thickness and structure of the weaving base used make it possible to obtain diverse results.

If desired, you can use beads or pendants to decorate our homemade choker.

How to make a choker tattoo yourself

  • If the choker tattoo should be decorated with beads, you need to thread them through the fishing line when creating loops.

  • Upon completion, you need to carefully fasten the edges of the fishing line to prevent the opening of our product.   • If it is not possible to get crimp balls, you can tightly tie the fishing line or solder it by melting its edges on a fire. Such a choker will need to be worn over the head.

Is wearing a choker tattoo appropriate nowadays

We learned how to make a DIY choker tattoo out of ordinary fishing line. It turns out that this is not difficult at all. But is it worth wasting time on such needlework if the times of popularity of chokers are long gone? Is it appropriate to wear it now?

Naturally, those who adhere to the classic style in clothing will not be able to wear such an ornament. Also, it will be out of place at official meetings or in a formal working environment. The choker on owners of a short, dense neck simply looks ugly.

But there are many options when the choker is not just acceptable, but will be a great addition to the image. Admirers of the free style and casual style will appreciate such jewelry. Also, this accessory should well appeal to lovers of handmade. The choker looks perfect on female bikers and representatives of various informal movements.



Interesting needlework for adults and children

Even if such jewelry is not to your liking or does not suit the style, children and adolescents will definitely appreciate handmade jewelry. You can attract a child to the process by showing him how to make a choker tattoo. Curious kids are always interested in doing something with their own hands. The weaving process will give them real pleasure. And young women of fashion will be happy to wear intricate jewelry with their own hands.
