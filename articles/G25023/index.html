Moscow - Yakutsk: how to get and what to see

Many people visit Yakutsk annually. It has beautiful nature, a unique climate, a large number of attractions. A large number of tourists from Moscow come here, so it is worthwhile to analyze in detail the road, the difference in time zones between these cities, as well as the features of the route.

General information about the city

Yakutsk is a city that has a rich and ancient history. It is located in the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia). Interestingly, this is the largest city, which is located in the permafrost belt. Despite these harsh conditions, a large number of people live here. According to 2017, the population of the city is about 269 thousand people.





Yakutsk really boasts its own history. In 1632, the Yakutsk prison was first laid here. However, it was not where the modern city is now, but 70 km from it. Then there lived about 300 Cossacks. In 1643, the prison was renamed Yakutsk. After that, its active development and land development began.

Now Yakutsk is a trade, administrative and cultural center. Industry here is not very developed. The main enterprises of the city are aimed at maintaining its functioning. Among the energy enterprises, one can mention the Yakutsk State District Power Plant and the Yakutsk Thermal Power Plant. There is also a bakery, a metalwork factory, a poultry farm, a gas processing plant and other enterprises. Also, the city is rich in attractions.

Moscow - Yakutsk: route features and modes of transport

Many people would like to go to this wonderful city. However, getting from Moscow to Yakutsk is not as easy as it might seem. The fact that you can get in any convenient way is encouraging.

Airplanes depart from the capital to Yakutsk and trains to the cities nearest to it. If desired, can be reached by car. Many travelers overcame the route Moscow - Yakutsk by car.

The time difference between cities is 6 hours. Moscow is in the GMT + 03: 00 time zone, and Yakutsk is in the GMT + 09: 00 zone. Thus, when the capital is 12 o’clock in the morning, then in Yakutsk it is already 6 o’clock in the morning.

The distance between these cities is really great. It is 8,370 km. And now it is worthwhile to analyze in more detail all possible ways.

How to get from Moscow to Yakutsk by plane

When the distance between cities is so great, the easiest and fastest way to get there is by air. In such cases, the aircraft is usually not only faster, but sometimes cheaper. Therefore, most travelers depart by plane on the route Moscow - Yakutsk. How long it takes to fly depends on the flight you choose. Usually the flight lasts about 6 hours 10 minutes.

Flights are operated by 2 airlines: Aeroflot and S7. Departure is carried out from the capital's airports Vnukovo, Sheremetyevo and Domodedovo. Therefore, you can choose the most convenient of them. Ticket price is more dependent on the time of year. The highest prices for air tickets in the summer. For example, in August, the one-way price is about 32 thousand rubles, a round-trip ticket is more than 39 thousand rubles. The lowest prices are usually in January. A one-way ticket will cost 9,700 rubles., Round-trip - 19,400 rubles. Aircraft arrive at the airport of Yakutsk.





A plane is one of the fastest and most convenient ways to overcome such a large route.

Train ride

Many people prefer to travel quietly by train. This is quite natural, because sometimes tourists cannot fly by plane for many reasons. From Moscow to Yakutsk can not be reached by train directly. You can drive to the city of Neryungri, which is 660 km from Yakutsk, or to the city of Tommot, it is 389 km from Yakutsk. From Tommot and Neryungri you can reach the final destination by bus, taxi, train. From Neryungri to Yakutsk, you can even fly by plane.





The train from Moscow to Neryungri leaves from the Kazan station. Travel time is 5 days 4 hours. The price for a reserved seat ticket starts at 4,166 rubles, for a ticket in a compartment - from 9,002 rubles.

A train from Moscow to Tommot also leaves from the Kazan railway station. It turns out longer in time - 5 days 21 hours. It offers tickets only in a compartment, prices start at 12,317 rubles.

How to get there by car

Those who prefer to travel by car can go to Yakutsk on it. It is worth noting that this journey will be quite long. The distance by car is 8 370 km. Travel time without stops will be 135 hours 24 minutes. Of course, it is difficult to travel such a distance without stops, so these data are indicative. Stops can be made in the cities through which the road passes. They will not be difficult to find a hotel for the night.





Going on the route Moscow - Yakutsk by car, it is better to know in advance about the traffic situation and the condition of the road surface. For example, in the cold season in many areas it will be difficult or impossible to drive due to weather conditions.

What to see in Yakutsk

Going to Yakutsk, you should think in advance which sights are better to visit, since there are quite a lot of them. Be sure to go to the architectural complex called "Old Town". This is a whole historical quarter in which the appearance of the old city of Yakutsk is recreated. Most buildings are copies of previously destroyed wooden buildings. Here you can see the prison tower, the Transfiguration Church, the old shopping arcades and much more.
