MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya, Konakli): reviews and ratings

general information

The hotel was built a long time ago - in 1989, and during this time managed not only to earn, but also to maintain an impeccable reputation as a great place for a comfortable exotic vacation. Today, the building of its main six-story building has a very beautiful modern look, which was achieved thanks to regular repair and cosmetic work, the last of which was completed in 2015. Due to all the conditions, this place occupies a leading position in the ratings of vacation spots compiled among Konakli hotels. Tourists themselves on different sites with reviews rate his work at 9-9.5 points out of a possible 10.

Location

MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey) is located in one of the areas mass visited by Russian tourists - Konakli. It is located literally 10 kilometers from the large city of Alanya, which is also known for its well-developed tourist infrastructure. At a distance of 500 meters is the nearest shopping center where vacationers can buy interesting goods for themselves and their relatives.

The nearest airport is at a great distance from Konakli - 110 kilometers in Antalya. That is why in every trip necessarily includes a transfer of arrived tourists to the location of the hotel. Guests note that this service is provided in a rather comfortable car with air conditioning.



Rooms

All the apartments available in the hotel are located in a large six-story building. The guests of the hotel are presented with 320 rooms, divided into two categories: Standard and Family.

Each of them has access via individual cards, which ensures the safety of guests' belongings. They also have individual climate equipment. If desired, guests can adjust the air temperature in their room to the optimal level, regardless of the current situation on the street.

All apartments are equipped with private bathrooms, where there is not only a bathroom and a bathing place (bath or shower), but also a sink. In addition to plumbing, there is furniture - cabinets, in which there are bath accessories, bathrobes and slippers.

All apartments have free internet access, which is wireless and high-speed. This opportunity allows tourists to always stay in touch with their family and friends.



"Standard"

Apartments of this category are presented in a relatively small area - 24 square meters. m., on which there is only one room. According to tourists, everything is decorated in coffee and beige tones that are pleasant for the visual perception.

The furniture installed here is designed to accommodate a maximum of three people: for two of them there is a large double bed (or two single beds), and the extra bed is represented by a large folding bed of a European type. Guests note with positive comments the size of a wardrobe made of wood - here, according to them, you can put a large number of things. If desired, tourists can watch TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system.

Guests are very pleased with the excellent view from the window, which opens up beautiful sea open spaces.

"Family"

The numbers of such a group are represented on a much larger territory than the "Standard" - 36 square meters. m. Their area is divided into two rooms, each of which has two beds. If necessary, a folding bed for a fifth person can be installed in any of the rooms.

In their free time, guests can watch plasma TVs in the rooms. They broadcast satellite channels, many of which are Russian-speaking. The floors here are carpeted, which is comfortable not only for adults, but also for children living with their parents.

This category of apartments has a private balcony with a pair of armchairs and a table for guests. They note that it is very comfortable to watch the coast of the Mediterranean Sea, and in any weather.

Conditions for children

Guests of MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * (Konakli) often in their reviews of this vacation spot pay attention to the fact that the hotel is ideal for families with children. Such conclusions of tourists are caused not only by the availability of rooms that are perfectly suitable for a large number of guests (up to 5), but also by the presence of a wide range of conditions for small guests.

The first of them is the opportunity for children to visit the mini-club, in which everything is arranged so that they have fun and actively spend their time. Here, kids can meet new friends, animators are engaged with them. Parents note that in individual cases, you can request a babysitting service, but they are paid.

If desired, tourists can take their child to a small pool, which is located in an open area and is made to all safety standards. In addition, the MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * has a large and beautiful playground.

Entertainment

Tourists themselves, vacationing here, admit that the hotel does not have to be sad. A team of active and talented animators is always engaged in the entertainment of every traveler. By the way, many tourists from Russia note the pleasant fact that many animators are fluent in Russian.

In the daytime, MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * hosts sporting events: team games are held here, for which sports fields are equipped, as well as relay races in the aquazone.

In the evening, various shows with dances, songs and contests are organized. Also, there are night discos, entrance to which is free, but you will have to pay for drinks in the bar.

Nutrition

Fans of delicious food, according to most vacationers, will never remain indifferent to the food that is offered in the catering establishments of MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 *. The photo of many guests presents brightly and beautifully decorated dishes that are served in a local restaurant. The institution is ready to offer any tourist who lives on the all-inclusive concept food prepared in Turkish traditions, as well as several options for dishes of European cuisine. All are served at the buffet, which can be visited in the morning, afternoon or evening.

On the territory of MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * there are five large bars, most of which are concentrated near the water, one is in the main building. Menus offer a wide variety of appetizers and salads in all these places; sweet tooth prefer to choose desserts here. Bartenders are ready to surprise any guest with bright and unusual cocktails or offer simple chilled drinks.



Pool

Guests of MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * in their reviews note that the hotel has a huge aquazone, around which there is a large sun terrace designed for tourists. For their greater comfort there is a considerable number of sun loungers made of wood, as well as beach umbrellas.

In total, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe aquazone is 640 square meters. m. It is divided into three pools of different sizes, in one of which slides are installed for active pastime of guests: two of them are designed for adults, and two - for kids. You can visit the aquazone at any time of the year or day, regardless of weather conditions.



Sports and spa

Fans of a healthy lifestyle at the MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * prefer to visit not only the aquazone, but also the gym, which is located on the top floor and offers the possibility of training on units that affect different muscles. For a variety of sports activities, there are halls for playing table tennis and billiards. There is a bowling club on the ground floor of the building, where guests gather every evening.

The description of MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * says that here, under the guidance of a professional instructor, it is possible to engage in active water sports, which will certainly appeal to fans of extreme sports.



Tourists who relaxed in the hotel often mention in their positive comments the spa, which is located on one of the floors of the main building. Its territory is divided into a large thermal zone, consisting of a sauna and hammam, as well as a massage room, whose employees are able to immerse any client in a state of complete relaxation. All services provided here, except for visiting the hammam, are paid.



Beach

The description of the hotel MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 *, presented on the official website, states that there is an individual beach assigned to its territory, which can only be visited by guests of this place. Hotel guests often speak about the beauty of this sand and pebble beach, the territory of which is always clean.

According to tourists, the coastline is perfectly landscaped for tourists of different ages. For their comfort, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided here. If desired, guests can borrow air mattresses for temporary use, but towels will have to pay a rental fee.

Along the entire line of the beach, the total length of which is 200 meters, there are evenly distributed public toilets, changing cabins, as well as showers, which vacationers can always use.

Prices

Travelers who wish to go on vacation to MC Mahberi Beach Hotel 4 * can apply for a tour package to the agency. Here they can offer the best accommodation options suitable for different categories of guests. As a rule, the cost of a package for a week-long stay for two, taking into account accommodation in a Standard room, costs about 60,000 rubles. All agents include in this price standard conditions such as a two-way flight, accommodation, transfer, as well as medical insurance. If this package is available, the tourist should independently think about obtaining a visa to Turkey, the presence of which is necessary for travelers from Russia.
