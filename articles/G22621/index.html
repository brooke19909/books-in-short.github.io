H.Top Royal Star Hotel 4 * (Spain, Lloret de Mar): room description, service, reviews

This magnificent resort is located in the heart of the Costa Brava and is known as the main party place on the Mediterranean coast. Lloret de Mar attracts tourists from different countries with many discos, bars, night clubs with laser shows and incendiary dance programs. The history of this resort has more than a thousand years, during which a small fishing village has become one of the most elegant cities on the Spanish coast and has become a famous tourist center of Europe.

One of the popular tourist accommodation options is H. Top Royal Star Hotel 4 * hotel.

Acquaintance

The establishment is one of the objects of the H. Top Hotels Resorts Group hotel chain. Opening date - 1975. A complete reconstruction was carried out in 2001.

Location

According to tourists reviews, H. Top Royal Star Hotel 4 * is located in a cozy, quiet area. Address: 17310, Carretera Blanes a Lloret s / n, Lloret de Mar. From here, the beach can be reached in just 10 minutes. Barcelona is 90 km from the hotel, while Girona is 38 km away. There is a bus station nearby. The resort center is 700 meters, the beach is 600 meters.

Description

The cost of living at the H. Top Royal Star Hotel 4 * hotel is from 1,150 to 2,953 rubles (the average rate for the room is standard). Place in the rating of hotels in this resort:

H. Top Royal Star Hotel 4 * is a 7-story building with 397 rooms. The remoteness of the establishment from the sea is compensated by the presence of large pools, high-quality food, active animation and a full-fledged All inclusive system, including vintage drinks (some restrictions are provided). H. Top Royal Star Hotel 4 * offers additional accommodation options: Full board + and Half board + (includes: national wine, beer, water, soft drinks for lunch and dinner). Within walking distance is the main street, on which the main discotheques of the resort are located.

At H. Top Royal Star Hotel 4 * entertainment and sports are available to all lovers of active leisure activities. Guests of the institution can use:

Beach

Hotel guests can relax on the sandy and city beach. Mattresses, umbrellas and sun loungers are provided for a fee. Playa de Fenals Beach is 600 meters away. There is a road between them. According to vacationers, this place is suitable both for active family vacations and for spending leisure time in the company of friends. The beach is called excellent, cozy, quite spacious and calm, with clean sand and rich infrastructure. Here you can rent a boat, a catamaran, there are sports equipment, a free shower, a lot of cafes. Other beaches can be reached by boat from here, visitors are provided with a large paid parking. Many tourists like the location of the beach. On both sides of the Playa de Fenals are surrounded by hills with greenery (pine trees), thanks to which you can find a shadow here in the morning and evening. For those who do not want to roast in the sun all day, this option is the most optimal.





Guests note that the sea at Playa de Fenals is very clean and beautiful. But those who relax with children should be very careful, as the descent is quite steep - a significant depth begins literally in a couple of steps. The place for boats and catamarans, as vacationers testify, is fenced off by buoys. There are several rescue towers. Sometimes, in accordance with the weather, there are big waves. The beach is quite crowded - a very beautiful, warm sea and beautiful landscapes attract crowds of tourists. Several restaurants are located nearby.

Guest Impressions

This accommodation option is quite popular among tourists. Guests with pleasure recall their stay on vacation at this hotel:

  • Location Some in their reviews H. Top Royal Star Hotel 4 * called an optimistic hotel, located near the cemetery. A feature of the institution is its location in the immediate vicinity of the cemetery and the complex of funeral services, including a morgue, etc. But, as the authors of the reviews note, not a single hotel room faces this sad place, so only the most zealous Feng Shui adherents can feel some discomfort here. . The hotel is located quite far from the beach, guests say, but relatively close to shops and the bus station.
  • Excursions. There is a gathering place for excursion trips nearby. Employees of the travel agency "Anex Tour" provide all the information (in English) about bus routes to Sitges, Figueres, etc. Vacationers highly appreciate this service.
  • Nutrition. Many speak positively about the food provided at the hotel. The guests especially like melons, pineapples and watermelons, as well as snacks (fudge, marmalade, nuts and chips) and desserts. Portioned ice cream of 3 varieties is served during breakfast, lunch and dinner. Wine for lunch or dinner can be bought in bottles and left in the wine refrigerator for several days if it is not drunk immediately. The bartender will hang a tag with the guest number on the unfinished bottle and put it until the next time in the wine cabinet, where no one will touch it. For breakfast, according to guests, they cook a variety of smoothies made from various ingredients, including celery and green fresh cucumbers. The food at the hotel, according to vacationers, is very plentiful. Breakfasts are quite monotonous, lunches and dinners, on the contrary, are distinguished by an extraordinary wealth of assortment.




  • About entertainment. According to vacationers, the hotel in March looks like a pension for pensioners. At this time of year, for youth, entertainment is absent both in the hotel and in Lloret in general. Entertainment programs during this period are defined by reviewers as suitable for older people rather than youth.
  • SPA Residents are pleased to respond to the services of the spa complex, which is located on the ground floor and is equipped with several changing rooms with toilets and sound showers. The indoor pool, warm enough, roomy and shallow, can be used for free. Sauna and steam room can be used after payment (6 euros for half an hour stay for an adult). You should pre-register at the reception, where they issue a magnetic card, with which opens the door to the compartment with a steam room and a sauna. Tourists say that sometimes, taking advantage of the fact that often the children loosely close the door to the magnetic lock, other guests go there. Pretending they do not understand English, they can soar at someone else's expense until they are expelled. Magnetic cards in the spa, according to many tourists, are not very convenient. Often they jam or demagnetize, causing the door to jam.
  • The rooms. The guestrooms are ordinary standard rooms with a balcony. The audibility in them is very good, the residents are sharing, therefore, those who are not lucky with noisy neighbors will be lucky. Room cleaning, according to guests, as well as towels are changed daily, without reminders. A significant drawback of the stay is the presence of uncomfortable mattresses in the rooms.

  • The Internet. Wi-fi in this hotel is paid, its cost is 5 euros per day. You can use the Internet for free for 15 minutes a day. Guests are mostly dissatisfied with his work, they advise that it is better to buy a SIM card, which will be cheaper.
  • Terms of stay . Upon arrival, the hotel guests say, the city tax will be charged: up to 1 euro per person per day. She will not be returned back. The hotel staff does not speak and do not understand Russian. Communication is possible in English.

Finally

In general, guests are satisfied with their stay at this hotel. The vacationers include the convenient location of the institution, the cleanliness, high quality of the food provided, the friendliness of the staff. Cons consider rides on weekends of drunken Spaniards who behave very noisy, paid Wi-Fi, uncomfortable mattresses in the rooms. Nevertheless, the hotel is recommended to potential guests of the resort as a wonderful vacation option.
