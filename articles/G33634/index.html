Hotel Sberbank in Crimea "Mriya": address, description, service, reviews

If you dream of a luxurious premium vacation, pay attention to the "Mriya" - the hotel "Sberbank" in the Crimea. An international level institution has the potential for a comfortable stay, good nutrition, wellness and events. This is a great option for a family or corporate vacation.

Location

The address of the Hotel "Mriya" in Crimea is the village of Opozolnoe, General Ostryakova Street, 9. It is about 20 km from Yalta. While relaxing in this hotel, be sure to visit the following tourist sites:

  • Vorontsov Palace - 8 km;
  • Alupka Park - 9 km;
  • cable car to Ai-Petri mountain - 10 km;
  • Yusupov Palace - 12 km;
  • Foros Church - 13 km;
  • Rock Parus - 14 km;
  • Swallow's Nest - 15 km;
  • Wuchang-Su Waterfall - 16 km;
  • Chekhov House-Museum - 19 km.

From Simferopol, the hotel can be reached by the trolleybus route, which is the longest in the world. The road will take about 4 hours, but during this time you can enjoy the beautiful mountain scenery.

For those who travel by car, it is recommended to set the coordinates 44.397627 and 33.940114 in the navigator.

A shuttle service can also be arranged in advance. The hotel can provide a shuttle bus, a business car or even a helicopter.
