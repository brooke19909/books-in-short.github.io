Velana Beach Maldives 4 *: description and reviews

Maldives today is considered one of the most luxurious places to relax. Picturesque landscapes, secluded lagoons with azure waters, tropical parks and, of course, the best level of service - all this attracts tourists from all over the world to the islands.

By the way, one of the most popular resorts in the Maldives is Male and its surroundings. Many large hotels and cozy, secluded guest houses have been built here, where travelers enjoy peace and quiet. Many people prefer to stay at the small Velana Beach Maldives. Of course, before buying a tour or booking a room, you should familiarize yourself with all the available information.

Location Information





To begin with, it's worth figuring out the location of the hotel, because for many travelers, this is an important factor. Velana Beach Maldives Hotel is built on one of the shores of Mafushi Island (Kaafu Atoll). This is a secluded place where tourists can enjoy the beauties of the tropical garden, snow-white beaches and crystal clear, warm sea water.

The large spa town of Male is just 27 km away. The same distance to Ibrahim Nasir International Airport (this is where tourists most often land).

Brief information about the hotel infrastructure





Hotel Velana Beach Maldives is quite small. It is one two-story building, built in a modern style. On the ground floor there is a restaurant, bar and a spacious lounge where travelers often relax.

The hotel is located in a secluded place, surrounded by a tropical garden. The whole territory is literally surrounded by greenery. The park is ideal for romantic walks and for relaxing away from the noise of big cities.

By the way, this is a new hotel - tourists began to receive here in 2015. That is why travelers can enjoy the well-groomed territory, new furniture and equipment, cleanliness and excellent service.

Hotel Velana Beach Maldives 4 *: photos and additional room information





Of course, I want to spend my vacation in comfort. What does the hotel complex Velana Beach Maldives 4 * offer? Reviews confirm that the living conditions here are simply perfect. The rooms are large, bright, with a fresh renovation and access to the balcony (not from all rooms). The furniture is also new - there are large beds with orthopedic mattresses and plenty of soft pillows, large dressing rooms, work desks, in a word, all that is needed for comfort.

Naturally, tourists can rely on equipment, without which a really comfortable pastime is almost impossible to imagine. In the guest rooms there is a large-screen TV (you can relax without interrupting watching your favorite TV shows), a safe with a combination lock. A powerful air conditioner will help to survive the summer heat (the room has a functional control center). A minibar with a cooling system is regularly filled with drinking water - here you can store purchased drinks. Guests can use a coffee maker and an electric kettle (a set of dishes are included).

Dining at the hotel





Each experienced tourist before booking will be interested in food conditions. Velana Beach Maldives has its own restaurant with a terrace right on the snow-white beach. Here, polite waiters offer visitors delicious, fresh dishes, including some traditional Maldivian delicacies. In the evening, you can dine by candlelight on the seashore, enjoying the sound of the waves. By the way, on request, guests prepare boxes with lunches.

A small cafe is open around the clock, where polite bartenders are at any time ready to offer a delicious cocktail, sandwich or some other light snacks. In addition, there is a small supermarket on site where you can buy drinks, some products and local fruits. In short, nutrition is unlikely to be a problem for you.

Where is the beach located? Entertainment Information





The hotel building was built very close to the seashore. Of course, the beach belongs to the hotel, so it is never crowded here. It offers residents a small but comfortable deck chair. It enters the sea smoothly, the bottom is covered with soft sand, and the water is transparent like glass.

Of course, tourists are not bored here. Vacationers just love to swim with masks, because the water is clean, which makes it possible to fully enjoy the bright colors of the underwater kingdom. An incredibly popular sport is diving - tourists are offered training and rental equipment for diving near the hotel. For rent boats, canoes, catamarans. For an additional fee, travelers can take a small boat trip along the atoll. Tourists of any age have a great time here.

Description of additional services

This is certainly a very small hotel. Nevertheless, guests provide a high level of comfort.

Reception is open 24 hours a day. Employees are always ready to take on the search for tickets. In addition, the hotel guests are provided with a comfortable water transfer to the airport and back.

There are luggage storage rooms, which is very convenient. In the hotel, of course, you can exchange currency for local money. Things can be taken to the laundry room (for extra clothes, they are ironed here).

In the rooms, in the lounges and in the courtyard there is access to high-speed Internet - guests use it for free. If you are a business person who skillfully combines relaxation with work, then your service is fax, printer and some other office equipment. Travelers here (regardless of the purpose of their arrival on the island) have no shortage of anything.

Hotel Velana Beach Maldives 4 * (Maldives, Male): leisure travelers





This is a small hotel, which is ideal for complete relaxation. The territory does not have its own pool, and it is not needed, because the guests with great pleasure swim in the ocean. Nevertheless, there is a spacious terrace-solarium - here you can relax, lying on a comfortable mattress, take a sunbath and chat with other guests.

In the garden there are alleys for walking and morning jogging, secluded arbors, benches for relaxation - both adults and children have a good time here, hiding in the shade of palm trees.

Massage rooms are available on site. In the reviews, tourists say that the masseurs here are very good, real masters of their craft. By the way, if you wish, you can sign up for medical procedures.

Do not forget about excursions. Maldives is a wonderful place, so rich in natural beauties. Tiny islands, azure waters, jungles, noisy waterfalls, secluded caves - all worth seeing. The hotel has its own guide, ready to offer interesting tours at an affordable price.

Family vacation: facilities for children

Velana Beach Maldives Hotel is designed for a relaxing holiday on one of the most beautiful beaches in the world. Sometimes families with children stay here. It offers spacious triple rooms in which parents with children feel quite comfortable. Comfortable rollaway beds are brought to the room upon request. By the way, kids under two years of age live in the territory for free, for older children there is a system of discounts.

Unfortunately, there are no special entertainments for children at the hotel. Nevertheless, small tourists have a good time on the local beach - it is clean, cleaned, the entrance to the sea is convenient, and the depth increases gradually. The guys love to splash around in clear, warm water, build sand castles, play with peers. Of course, if other entertainment and the availability of facilities for children are important to you, it is better to look for another place.
