World Wide Web Fraud

Many motorists, wanting to save money, apply for an insurance policy in Internet reception rooms, where many promise to sell OSAGO form at a bargain price. At the same time, the so-called representatives of the insurance company offer not only a policy at a bargain price, but also free delivery to any place and time.



It would seem, why not take advantage of advantageous services. As practice shows, only scammers sell policies through the World Wide Web. These forms are false, and you will not be able to count on insurance benefits in case of an accident. Such forms can only be provided to police officers so as not to receive a fine.

Therefore, if you are looking for where it is better to insure the car for OSAGO, do not believe the attractive prices and attractive delivery services. It is worth checking the form and company carefully before purchasing it.

Differences between the fake form and the present

When purchasing a contract, you need to pay attention to the following main points:

  1. The first thing you should pay close attention to is paper. The original policy is dense and slightly rough. Fake printed on a regular A4 sheet.
  2. This form is cleaned with watermarks and the PCA logo. If you look at the form in the light, you can see the inscription PCA.
  3. A protective metal strip on the back of the policy, which is sewn into the form.
  4. The size of the policy. The fake has an A4 format, the original is a little longer.
  5. The form number is located in the upper right corner. In the original form, it is convex to the touch.
  6. Like banknotes, the original forms are protected by red and green villi. Reds can be easily seen in the clearance on the back of the form. Green villi can only be seen through ultraviolet light.
