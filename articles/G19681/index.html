Imperial Boutique Hotel, Abrau-Durso

Everyone with responsibility approaches the issue regarding where he will spend his next vacation. Some go abroad, while others decide to relax in one of the Russian resorts. There are many of them, and countless hotels are concentrated everywhere. However, now I would like to touch the attention of the Hotel Imperial (Abrau-Durso).





general information

This elegant hotel complex is located in the Novorossiysk district. To be more precise - in the rural district of Abrau-Durso. You can drive to Novorossiysk from there in 15-20 minutes. The pebble beaches of the Black Sea are only 6 kilometers away. But a 5-minute walk from the hotel is the freshwater lake Abrau - the largest in the entire Krasnodar Territory. Its depth is 11 meters, and the length is more than three kilometers.

Services

The Hotel Imperial (Abrau-Durso) has everything that guests can need. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the complex, there is private parking, a 24-hour reception, a concierge, the services of which everyone can use. The hotel also has a laundry room, where guests' items are not only washed, but also ironed.

The hotel has a small business center, a conference room and a meeting room - the resort is popular among business people who combine business with pleasure.

Other guests are offered transfer services, delivery of food, drinks and packed lunches on the road. In addition to all of the above, the hotel has a souvenir shop, a small church and a beauty salon with a hairdresser.





Special offers

Especially popular are the weekend programs that the Imperial Hotel offers potential guests. Abrau-Durso is a resort that is loved by connoisseurs of the so-called wine tourism. After all, it is here that the largest producer of wine and champagne in Russia is located. And tourists are actively visiting not only the factory! The Champagne Museum located here is popular.

And of course, the Imperial Hotel (Abrau-Durso) invites guests to spend champagne and a wine SPA weekend here. This is a unique offer. After all, this hotel is the first champagne-SPA in Russia - that is, a Champagne House with an atmosphere of relaxation!

Guests are greeted by the luxurious atmosphere of the hotel, made in the exquisite style of French Provence. Champagne baths, walks through the vineyards, romantic dinners, excursions to the wine factory, boat trips around the lake, relaxing SPA treatments with a glass of trousers and even helicopter rides are just a short list of things that can please guests.





Rest for body and soul

This boutique hotel receives extremely positive reviews. Not surprising, since there is everything that your heart desires. Private beach area by the lake, horse riding, fishing and cycling, Turkish bath, massage rooms, fitness center, sauna.

But the main highlight that Imperial Boutique Hotel boasts is a SPA center. The list of proposed procedures amounts to dozens of items. Everything is accompanied by carefully thought-out aroma and light therapy, pleasant background music, and the atmosphere pleases with burning candles and lush flowers.

Here you can try treatments with scrubs and peeling, body wraps and massages and visit the thermal zone, where clients will find a herbal and Finnish sauna, hammam, Kneipp path and rain shower. Honey-salt body masks, relaxation care for the head, wraps with seaweed, chocolate, honey, herbs; lymphatic drainage, anti-cellulite, can massage, stone therapy - this is not the whole list of procedures that are available here.

By the way, they use only high-quality cosmetics made from natural ingredients by the French companies Biologique Recherche and Caudalie. However, you can talk about the SPA-salon and its services for a long time, but it is better to familiarize yourself with this topic individually.





A restaurant

Particular attention is paid to the delights that treat guests who have decided to spend their holidays in Abrau-Durso. The Imperial Hotel truly has a luxurious restaurant. He specializes in French cuisine and unique author's delicacies from the chef, who has experience in institutions marked by the Michelin star.

Guests can enjoy foie gras pate with truffles, beef burgignon with aromatic champignons, Provencal rabbit stew and quail and homemade green noodle soup. It also serves delicate French creme brulee with ginger and almond flavors, as well as walnut-honey roasting and pear in red wine. In general, any foodie will find a favorite dish here. Indeed, the menu has really everything - from grape snails (escargos) to pancakes with crab meat.

By the way, even breakfasts here are not the same as in all other hotels. Instead of regular scrambled eggs and omelettes, guests are served homemade fresh pastries, French bread, champagne in misted glasses, red fish, freshly squeezed juices, blue cheese, yogurts and much more.
