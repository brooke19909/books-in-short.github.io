We make flowers for the garden from plastic bottles easy and simple





Plastic bottle garden flowers are a simple and elegant decoration. This craft can be made without any problems, and with minimal time costs. Materials and tools for its implementation are suitable from the usual set. It can easily be made as a fairly experienced master, as well as a beginner. In general, in short, such jewelry is accessible to everyone and they are very easily obtained from improvised waste. All this in the complex has led to the fact that flowers from plastic bottles for the garden are not so rare and are increasingly found in playgrounds and personal plots.

What will we need?

The main component is a container made of plastic. Color, the size of its special role does not play. The main condition is the absence of bends, patterns on the surface. Although, if desired, you can use such material. We also need fabric or oilcloth with bright colors and an extraordinary pattern. To cut out the template you need cardboard or whatman paper. Still need glue, adhesive tape or any other component with which you can connect everything together and fix it. Among the tool can be identified:





  • scissors or knife;
  • felt-tip pen or marker of any color;
  • ruler;
  • pattern.

When all this is available, it is very simple to make flowers for the garden from plastic bottles.

Components

The first step is to create templates. To do this, with the help of a marker and a pattern, the petals of the desired shape are drawn. Then they are cut out. For one flower, one blank will be enough. Next, we take a plastic container and apply a template to it. We circle it with a marker or felt-tip pen and cut it out either with a knife or with scissors. After the required number of petals is cut, proceed to the core. A button is used as its frame. It is necessary to cut such a scrap of matter so that it can be completely covered. Then, on one side, it is fixed with adhesive tape or glue. Similarly, petals are formed. Next, you need to combine all this and get ready-made flowers for the garden from plastic bottles.





Putting it all together

At this stage, you need to attach the petals around the entire perimeter of the core. To do this, the corresponding end of the second element is gently smeared with glue and attached from below to the desired point of the first, and so on, until the entire perimeter is completely gone. It is better to choose glue for this operation, and not adhesive tape, the use of which significantly complicates everything. Then it should dry. Similarly, the rest of the flowers for the garden are made from plastic bottles. At the final stage, they are installed in place. Then the work is over and you can enjoy the result.

Conclusion

From plastic bottles for the garden, you can make many decorations. One of the most common among them are flowers. Make them in the strength of any person. Moreover, this craft can be safely chosen for the first time. It is lightweight, done quickly and without any problems.
