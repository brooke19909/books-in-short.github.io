Treatment and rest in the Irkutsk region

If you want real Siberian health, be sure to pay attention to this region. So, what sanatoriums of the Irkutsk region really deserve attention? There are health facilities for every taste. So, for example, luck is to get a ticket to the sanatorium of the FSB "Baikal", located one and a half meters from the lake of the same name. People come here who need treatment of the musculoskeletal system, respiratory system, nervous, circulatory, genitourinary systems, as well as patients with metabolic and nutritional disorders. Of the procedures there are all kinds of physical procedures, among which are the treatment with local mineralized waters and sapropelic mud. Striking abundance of practices aimed at peace of mind, improving energy.



Among other sanatoriums, "Angara" stands out, in the list of specialized areas of which there is gerontology. This means that special attention will be paid to the elderly. However, the sanatorium is family-friendly, families with children from four years old are accepted. In addition to the standard set of procedures, they also offer apitherapy - treatment with bee stings.

Wellness center with the unusual name "Nukut Matsesta" definitely deserves attention. He specializes in skin diseases, gynecology and urology. Mineral waters are offered, both low-mineralized and sulphide waters for baths and massage.

Procedures

The resources used by the sanatorium are based on a microclimate and healing air. In addition, balneological resources are used, in other words, mineral waters. Sayan water "Ordai" is the basis of the procedures. There are two types of it. The first has a fairly broad specialization: heals blood, and skin, and the musculoskeletal system, and the problems of gynecology and urology. The second is aimed at combating diseases of the digestive system.

The set of physiotherapy procedures is quite wide. However, pay much more attention to bathtubs. They come here not only with mineral water, but also with turpentine and selenium. Pay attention to your own sanatorium speleoclimatic chamber with potassium-magnesium salts. Do not go past applications and wraps of peat mud. As in all sanatoriums, there are many mental therapies, procedures for influencing the psyche (color, aroma and so on).

Accommodations

Comfortable conditions will definitely not leave you unhappy. Each of the three buildings has a convenient elevator. The rooms come in different types: simple, junior suite, suite and apartments. The deluxe room, among other things, has a bathtub (the rest have showers). Naturally, they vary in price. However, tourists from simple rooms do not complain at all: all rooms have the necessary furniture. Halls are equipped with upholstered furniture. Four meals a day, and a different menu is offered: vitamin, diet, etc.
