How to make a flower from beads with your own hands

The beadwork will not leave anyone indifferent . A flower of small balls shimmering in the light will forever delight your loved ones and will never fade. In addition, it is also an excellent pastime - beadwork. Such an activity will easily relieve stress from everyday household problems and bring you a lot of pleasure.

How to make a flower from beads with your own hands





You can easily make yourself any bouquet you like. You can choose absolutely any beads. "Flowers" - patterns for beginners can be found quite easily in any needlework store. A bouquet of roses looks very nice. For this flower, you can use both standard pink or red colors, and purple, blue - in general, anyone that fits into your interior. If you learn to weave from beads using the basic techniques, you can easily create artificial flowers yourself.

Petal weaving

Such a flower from beads with their own hands can be made from colored beads and wire. We begin to weave the petals of the bud itself. Their number for one flower will be about eight to nine. We string twelve beads on a wire, we promote them to the middle. Pass one free end on the other hand through the entire row of beads and tighten the ends. Next, from any side we put on 10 beads, and pull the other end of the wire through a new row. We continue in the same technique with nine, seven and five beads. After that, we already string such an amount of beads on one end so that we get an arc surrounding the resulting petal, twist the two ends together.





Core

The middle of the rosette can be performed using various techniques. On one of them we pass three beads into the middle of the wire, twist the ends, retreat one centimeter and put on the same side the same number of beads, wrap the end under them from this side. We do the same on the other hand. We get three stamens in the core.

Rose leaves

We create them in the same technique as the petals, only without the last arc. Beads are strung in the following sequence: two, three, four, three, three, two. The number of leaves can be done any.





Flower assembly

Around the core, we begin to arrange the rose petals, alternately twisting the wire into the stem. We arrange the petals evenly, as in a real living flower. At the very end, we fasten the leaves to the stem. Our flower is made of beads with our own hands! You can use not only one color for a rose, but also several shades of one or a couple of different colors. For example, the color is scarlet in the middle, and gently pink along the edges. If you have a rose, you can make a few of the same and create a beautiful bouquet that will look great in a vase. The stem can be left in its original form or braided with a beautifully satin green ribbon. Such a flower made of beads, made with your own hands, will be nice to receive as a gift to any person, because it will be much better than bought. And when you gain experience in creating such colors, you can even fulfill orders - combine business with pleasure.
