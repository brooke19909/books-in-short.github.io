Golden Donaire Beach 3 * (Spain): photos and reviews of tourists

The Mediterranean coast of Spain in recent years has become increasingly popular among travelers from Russia and the CIS countries. They choose this country because of the relatively low cost of trips, rich culture and good, truly European service. However, living in five-star complexes is very expensive, so many tourists prefer to stay in budget hotels. For example, in La Pineda, the Golden Donaire Beach 3 * hotel is popular. It is about this inexpensive option for accommodation that will be discussed in this article.

Holidays in La Pineda

This small coastal resort is located in Catalonia and is famous for its clean air, sandy beaches that are not crowded with numerous tourists, as well as excellent conditions for families with small children. It got its name from the Spanish name for the Mediterranean pine - there are a lot of such trees here, which makes the local air truly healing. La Pineda is a relatively young resort that has just begun to gain popularity. Its promenade is considered the longest in Catalonia.

The sea here has a very flat, gentle entrance, which makes this place ideal for swimming with young children. The beaches in La Pineda, as a rule, are sandy, only occasionally they are interrupted by pebble sections of the coast. The infrastructure at the resort is also designed for family vacations - everywhere you can find open shallow pools and children's clubs, and restaurants and cafes provide a special menu for kids. Hotel Location Features

Golden Donaire Beach Hotel (Spain) is built right on the seashore, so tourists do not have to spend a lot of time walking to the beach and back. You can reach it in just 2-3 minutes. In addition, the complex is located just 300 meters from the main promenade of the resort, so in the evenings guests love to stroll along the promenade, visit the cafes, restaurants and taverns opened there. A bus stop is located next to the hotel. At the same time, tourists can choose a route that allows them to get to the major cities of Catalonia, for example, Tarragona or Barcelona. The nearest supermarket is just 100 meters away. Tourists with children are also advised to visit the local water park - the distance to it is approximately 500 meters.

But the airport from here is at a decent distance. The nearest of them is in Reus. The distance to it is only 15 km, but aircraft from Russia rarely land there. Typically, tourists choose to arrive at an airport near Barcelona. Flights go there much more often, and the cost of the ticket is ultimately much cheaper. That's just the airport is almost 110 km from La Pineda, so tourists will have to spend a lot of time on the road. But most of the amenities can be reduced to zero if you book a transfer to the hotel in advance.

General hotel information and check-in rules

Complex Golden Donaire Beach (Spain) was built in 1969, but do not worry about the state of its number of rooms. The last repair was completed here in 2015. At this time, all residential and public buildings were completely restored. The hotel consists of a large nine-story building, built in a modern style. Next to it is a spacious outdoor pool and sun deck. This complex is a place for mass recreation. In total, 410 budget rooms are offered here, so in the high season at the same time more than 1000 tourists can live on the hotel territory.

The hotel does not have any unique registration requirements for its guests. Upon check-in, they, like in other hotels, must present their passport or other document with an identification photo, as well as provide a credit card. It is worth noting that the hotel is not allowed accommodation with your favorite pets, including guide dogs. Registration of guests is carried out at strictly allotted hours for this - from 14:00 until midnight. After the end of the stay, tourists must leave the hotel between 07:00 and 11:00. It is also worth mentioning that the staff does not speak Russian here - the staff communicate with guests in Spanish or English.

Hotel Golden Donaire Beach. Description of rooms

Do not rely on luxury apartments when arriving at this affordable hotel. However, tourists note that the rooms here, although small, are comfortable and clean. There are 410 of them in the hotel. Six of them are equipped with all necessary amenities for guests with physical disabilities. But there are no adjacent apartments in the complex. Most of the rooms are standard. They are designed to accommodate two adult guests, as well as a small child. Their area is only 15 square meters. m. The room is divided into a bedroom, which has a living area, and a combined bathroom. The room has an open balcony. It offers rooms with views of the surrounding area, and for a fee you can buy apartments with sea views.

For tourists, family rooms are also available, which can accommodate 4 adults at once. If you come to rest alone, you can choose Single Room. This room is only 12 square meters. m is equipped with a single bed and a minimum set of amenities, but it costs much less. Of course, all the apartments are cleaned daily by the hotel staff. They also change linen and towels, but only 3 times a week.

Furniture and basic equipment of rooms

Of course, the Golden Donaire Beach (La Pineda) hotel cannot offer its guests luxurious interiors and extensive decoration. But guests note that for a budget holiday, the room has everything you need. When checking in, you can choose rooms with double or single beds. In addition to them, from furniture, tourists are provided with bedside tables, wardrobes, a desk, chairs, a mirror. On the balcony there is a dining set made of plastic, as well as a clothes dryer. The bathroom has a bath with shower, sink, large mirror and toilet. Here you can also find toilet paper, liquid soap, shampoo and shower gel. If necessary, tourists can use the built-in hairdryer to dry their hair.

Of the technical equipment, Golden Donaire Beach guests are provided with a small plasma TV located on the bedroom wall, to which a set of cable channels is connected - most of them are presented in English. You can contact the administrator or order room service by telephone. For a fee, international and local calls are also made outside the hotel. There is no individual air conditioning in the rooms; instead, a central cooling system works, which turns on in early June and turns off at the end of September. For a fee, you can rent a safe, which is locked with a mechanical key. Its cost is 4 euros (274 rubles) per day. But wireless internet in all rooms is provided for free.

What kind of catering concept does the hotel offer?

The hotel Golden Donaire Beach 3 * (La Pineda) has an all-inclusive concept. The price includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as soft drinks and mineral water. From alcohol serves white, red wine of local production, sangria and beer. All drinks are poured into plastic glasses. Minors are not allowed to drink alcohol. Between meals, the bar serves snacks: fast food, pizza, ice cream, tea and coffee. A gala gala dinner is held once a week. In addition, tourists can pay for breakfast only upon arrival, and the rest of the time they can eat at their own expense.

The hotel has one main restaurant, which serves a common buffet. Children, vegetarian and diet menus are available free of charge. Gluten and lactose free meals are served. An open kitchen is open daily. For a bite to eat, guests can enjoy a snack or a poolside bar.

Hotel infrastructure and its territory

The territory of the Golden Donaire Beach 3 * complex (Spain) cannot be called extensive, but there are all the necessary infrastructure facilities for recreation. We list the main ones:

  • paid hairdresser;
  • 24-hour reception, where you can exchange currency, rent a safe, order a taxi or an excursion;
  • Internet cafe, which offers its services for a fee;
  • luggage room;
  • therapist's office.

Separately, it is worth noting that the hotel does not have its own parking, so tourists with cars have to take care of the place for their accommodation in advance. There is also no laundry service in the complex.

Beach and Pool

Although the Golden Donaire Beach 3 * hotel is built right on the coast of the sea, it does not have its own stretch of beach. Instead, tourists visit the city beach, access to which is free. But for the rental of sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses an additional fee is charged. There is also a water entertainment center on the beach, windsurfing equipment is rented, and volleyball courts in the sand are equipped.

You can swim and sunbathe on site. For these purposes, a freshwater pool is open. It does not heat up, so you can swim in it only in warm weather. But next to it there is a sun terrace with free sunbeds and umbrellas. And in the cold, an indoor pool starts to work.

Other leisure options

The hotel Golden Donaire Beach 3 * (Spain) presents many options for active and relaxing leisure. Guests can relax in the sauna or jacuzzi. There are also open areas for playing table tennis and football, billiards and badminton. In the evening, entertainment shows are held. On the ground floor there is a relaxation room with slot machines. In the morning and in the evening, an English-speaking animation team works. Free aerobics classes are provided.

Conditions for placing with small children

Most of the guests come to the Golden Donaire Beach Hotel with young children, so there are many amenities prepared for them. For example, a cot is provided for free in the room. In the restaurant you can ask for high chairs and a special menu for kids. If necessary, a nanny can be called into the room.

The organization of children's leisure is also carefully thought out. Kids can swim in a shallow outdoor pool with warm water. In the evenings, animators hold children's discos and competitions. In the morning and afternoon, they at the hotel entertain children in a mini-club, which can be visited by all children aged 4 to 12 years. However, the animators only speak English.

Positive feedback about Golden Donaire Beach 3 *

This hotel is very popular among tourists who, after their vacation, are in a hurry to share their impressions of him. It is worth saying that many guests here really liked it, although they note that the hotel has its own minor flaws. But they recommend this place for a budget holiday in La Pineda. The Golden Donaire Beach complex, in their opinion, has the following advantages:

  • very friendly and friendly staff who always try to quickly solve the problems;
  • the choice of dishes in the restaurant is not very large, but all the food is very tasty and beautifully decorated;
  • great children's animation, which kids really like, so they themselves ask for a mini-club;
  • excellent location - the hotel is located directly on the beach;
  • Despite its small area, the rooms have enough space, and they are also regularly cleaned well.

Negative hotel reviews

Unfortunately, the Golden Donaire Beach complex reviews are different. There are negative ones among them. And although many tourists appreciated the level of service at the hotel, they could not help but notice the following serious shortcomings:

  • sometimes in a restaurant they can spread food on dirty plates;
  • There is a highway next to the hotel, therefore it is impossible to sleep at night with an open window - the noise of passing buses interferes;
  • not all rooms have fresh repairs;
  • the air conditioner does not work well, and sometimes it also makes a lot of noise;
  • next to the pool in the evenings it was very noisy, which prevented tourists whose windows overlooked the hotel courtyard.

So is it worth choosing this hotel for your vacation?

Of course, Golden Donaire Beach Hotel is not suitable for those travelers who are looking for a chic place to stay. However, for tourists with low requirements, it is quite possible to recommend it. It is worth noting that the complex is focused on couples with children, the elderly and youth companies who prefer to relax without noisy parties. For these categories of tourists there is everything that they may need.
