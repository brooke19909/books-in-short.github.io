Holidays in Cyprus: reviews of tourists

Many people when choosing a vacation destination abroad prefer Cyprus. Reviews of tourists who have already been here convincingly prove the benefits of this trip. This magnificent island attracts a huge number of people. A mild climate, a small amount of rainfall, exotic nature, excellent beaches and hotels - all this allows you to call Cyprus (reviews of tourists will be provided below) a paradise. Let's get to know him better.

Interesting Facts

If you are going to go on vacation to Cyprus, then it will be useful to get acquainted with the history of this island. When did the first people appear here? Which states are next to Cyprus? What is the name of the island connected with? Read more about this and much more.

  • Thanks to some surviving documents, it is known that the first people in Cyprus appeared about 12 thousand years ago.
  • More than a million people live here.
  • Cyprus is one of the largest islands in the Mediterranean Sea. Near it are countries such as Egypt, Syria and Turkey.
  • Cyprus constantly tried to capture different nations. Among them: Assyrians, Egyptians, Crusaders, Turks, Persians and others.
  • The weather in Cyprus (vacationers' reviews confirm this) is always warm, even in the winter months the thermometer does not fall below +15 during the day and +7 at night.
  • According to one version, this name of the island was given to him because of the large number of cypress trees that grow here.

What to do on vacation

Cyprus is not only a wonderful climatic conditions and magnificent beaches, but also a large number of options for spending free time. Among the most popular, you can list the following:

  • visits to night clubs, bars and restaurants;
  • horse riding lessons;
  • try a wide variety of Mediterranean dishes;
  • diving lessons with exploring the underwater sights of the island;
  • tasting famous wines and sweets;
  • visit to water parks;
  • acquaintance with the best sights of the island;
  • relaxing on beautiful beaches and much more.

Next, we will introduce you to the most popular resort towns of Cyprus. The reviews of tourists about the holidays, which we analyzed, helped us make this difficult choice.

The azure sea and the peaceful nature

Welcomes all visitors Protaras (Cyprus). Reviews of a large number of people resting here are full of enthusiastic emotions. One of the reasons for the great popularity of Protaras is the unique weather. Here you can relax and swim in the Mediterranean even in winter, as the air temperature does not drop below eighteen degrees Celsius. On the island you can find fig trees and rare plants. It will also be interesting for tourists to see shows of dancing fountains and beautiful bays. If you want to take a break from the hectic and hectic life of big cities, then Protaras is what you need. The calm flow of life in this place will allow you to enjoy every moment spent here.

Ayia Napa

It is best to go to lovers of an active lifestyle. It offers vacationers - a huge number of night discos, bars, restaurants and other institutions. A large number of water parks and a calm sea attract couples with children to the resort of Ayia Napa. You can stay in comfortable hotels or separate bungalows. The stunning nature of the resort and the opportunity to engage in water activities attract here a large number of tourists.

Pathos

There are a large number of architectural monuments and other attractions. If you want to not only swim in the warm sea and sunbathe on the beaches, as well as touch the treasures of world culture, then choose Paphos. In ancient times, the city was the capital of Cyprus. Here are located not only unique monuments, but also museums, parks, churches and monasteries.

Popular Attractions

Many people come to Cyprus to get acquainted with its rich history and legends. Among the large number of attractions of the island you should definitely see:

  • Knight's castle of Kolossi. This impressive building was built at the beginning of the 13th century.
  • Natural Park "Cavo Greco". On a vast territory collected a great many plants, many of which can only be seen here. Here you can not only relax in the shade of trees, but also ride a bike along the paths of the park.
  • Amphitheater of Kourion. It was built in the 2nd century BC. Antique mosaics have even been preserved here. Today, performances and musical performances are taking place in a huge arena. Unfortunately, it is impossible to tell in one article about all the sights worthy of the attention of visitors. Cyprus is not just an island, it is a place where you can touch the distant past. Further we suggest you to get acquainted with the best hotels.

  • Grecian Park 5 *. This option is suitable for people who love nature and comfortable rest. The complex is located in a tropical park. Trip to Cyprus with children: reviews

When choosing a place to stay, couples are guided by the following indicators:

  • climate;
  • clean beaches and water;
  • the availability of entertainment programs;
  • a large number of greens and flowers.

Going to another country with children, you first of all think about their comfort and entertainment. Resorts of Cyprus - an ideal option for a joint vacation of parents with their children. First of all, because there is a mild and dry climate, which has a beneficial effect on the children's body. Secondly, beautiful nature. Thirdly, a large amount of entertainment for children. It will be interesting to know what tourists write about this in their reviews of Cyprus. So, you can find the following statements:

  • In Cyprus there are a large number of hotels that provide accommodation with children. They have special pools, children's slides, water parks, playgrounds, separate meals for small picks, and much more.
  • Hotel workers are very friendly with families with children. They offer suitable excursions, give various recommendations and advice, and provide first aid.
  • In Cyprus, you can find a large number of cafes that offer a special menu and ice cream. The prices are quite affordable, and the quality of the dishes on offer is commendable.

Cyprus is a place where you can truly believe that heaven exists on earth. In any case, there are all conditions for a carefree vacation: warm sun, clear sea, chic beaches and hotels, a large number of attractions.
