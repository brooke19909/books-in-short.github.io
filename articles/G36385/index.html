Water park "Caribia" in Moscow: description, location and reviews

In order to feel in a sunny Caribbean resort, swim in the gentle waves, relax in a comfortable sun lounger on a sandy beach, it is not necessary to go to distant shores.

Holidays in the Caribbean in Moscow

Now you can, without leaving the noisy metropolis, visit the Caribia water park in Moscow. Reviews of guests of the water park provide an opportunity to judge what a wonderful vacation awaits you, your family and friends literally 20 minutes from the city center.





Here, under a glass dome, spreading over the aquazone, it seems to a person that he is indeed staying at a seaside resort. A lot of various entertainments, fascinating attractions and animations for young visitors, delicious cuisine from the chefs of local restaurants and cafes make visiting the entertainment center a real holiday. The original design of the water park is also impressive - it is decorated with the works of the artist Olga Motovilova, from which it blows light and warmth.

Water park "Caribia" - rest with the whole family

This amusement park has recently opened, but has already earned the love of Muscovites. The variety of water activities here is no less than in an upscale resort. There are several swimming pools for adults and children: with waves and geysers, a children's pool, safe even for the smallest swimmers, a quiet river water relaxation zone, a Jacuzzi more than a meter deep and a contrast bath.





The pools of the water park are filled with water that has passed a multi-stage cleaning system using the most modern technologies developed by the German company Best Water Technology. The complex cleaning process does not stop even for a minute, and visitors leave enthusiastic reviews about the Caribia water park as one of the cleanest water areas of the city.
