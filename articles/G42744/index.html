Number of rooms

In total, Zodiac 4 * (Tunisia / Hammamet) has 97 rooms. Of these, 81 are standard, 12 are for families of 3 or more people, and 4 rooms are upscale "suites". They are located on two levels (floors) and are equipped with everything necessary for a respectable vacation of premium tourists. In addition, there are facilities for the disabled and non-smokers.

Description and service

Standard rooms at the Zodiac Hotel are no worse than suites. Just their area is less. All rooms have air conditioning and heating, as the hotel is open year-round, and in the winter months it is sometimes necessary to heat the rooms. There are also TVs with satellite channels, telephone, Internet, safe, mini-bar (for a fee), comfortable furniture. The bathroom is equipped with a shower or bathtub. They have all the necessary bath and toiletries, as well as a hairdryer. Cleaning is done daily, and linen is changed 2-3 times a week. In some reviews, vacationers complain that, despite the advertised cleanliness of the hotel staff, linen and towels are changed very rarely. If for you cleanliness is one of the most important determinants, then check the declared information several times and only after that go to the Zodiac 4 * hotel (Tunisia). Prices are low, but can this justify the dishonesty of staff? As noted above, the cost of living at this hotel starts at $ 50 (per day).

Nutrition

Hotel guests can choose from various types of food: all-inclusive, full-board, half-board, or breakfast only. In the main restaurant of the Zodiac 4 * hotel (Tunisia), a buffet is served three times a day with a huge selection of hot dishes, drinks, salads, pastries and desserts. Although in some reviews, former guests complain about the meager menu, nevertheless, the restaurant chefs work wonderfully and wish to delight guests with their culinary masterpieces. Those vacationers who prefer to choose the all inclusive system can use the bars (free of charge) located on the hotel territory throughout the day. At 3 p.m. tea and pastries are served in a special room. In addition, daily, next to the pool, for guests, barbecue is fried in a barbecue cafe, and for gourmets there is always a counter with ice cream. The hotel also has a pizzeria and an oriental cafe.





Infrastructure

The hotel complex has a well-developed infrastructure. Since the Zodiac has a conference room, various forums, symposia, conferences and seminars take place here from time to time. At the services of participants in these events there is a business center with all the necessary devices (printer, copier, fax, etc.). In addition, there is a currency exchange, elevator, parking, first-aid post, dry cleaning, ironing and laundry services, a beauty salon, spa, massage room, sauna, etc.

Sports and Entertainment

For fans of an active lifestyle, the hotel has spacious swimming pools (indoor and outdoor), a fitness center and a gym, evening tennis courts, a pool table, mini-golf, volleyball, etc. There is also a games room. As already mentioned, a golf club is a 10-minute walk away. For those who can not imagine life without noisy entertainment, a disco, show animation, etc. is arranged in the evenings.

For children

Little vacationers have their own pool. They can find many interesting activities at the kids club. For those who like to play outdoors, there is a playground with slides and swings. In restaurants, children are provided with special chairs and several children's dishes.
