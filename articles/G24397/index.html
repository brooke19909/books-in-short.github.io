Residence

Atlantic Oasis Hotel consists of five buildings. 235 cozy rooms, decorated in a calm minimalist style, are different in categories. Atlantic Oasis (Cyprus) offers its guests an extensive entertainment program. The hotel has a gym, massage room, jacuzzi, TV room and karaoke.
