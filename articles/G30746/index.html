How to choose a charger for your phone: review, useful tips. Wireless Charger for Phone

In today's world, a person simply can not do without a cell phone. These devices have become very firmly entrenched in our lives, and even sometimes it is not clear how they could have been dispensed with before. The phone runs on battery power, and she needs the energy she receives through a special power supply. It is about them that we will talk today. We will figure out how to choose a charger for the phone. We begin to understand the topic in order.

How to choose a charger for your phone?

Many choose by price. If the charger is broken, then you need to go to the nearest store and buy the cheapest option - if only the connector fits. This is not the right approach to business. Yes, undoubtedly, the moment with a suitable connector is important, but there are some more nuances that deserve attention.

If you look closely at the model, then its parameters will be written in small print on it. The original device is ideal for charging the battery of your mobile phone. And it will be good if those memory devices that you buy instead of the broken original one will be identical to all of these characteristics.

If complete identity cannot be achieved, then it is worth choosing a model with the most suitable parameters. But you should understand that your battery can best be charged with the original. If the analog will have slightly changed parameters, then it will charge the battery worse.

If we are talking about a one-time case, for example, you lend a charger to one of your colleagues at work, then you can not worry about the above parameters. But if we are talking about memory for constant everyday use, then talking about the parameters is relevant.

Charger Options

When choosing a memory for your mobile device, it is extremely important to pay attention to the electrical characteristics of the future power supply. These include:

  • rated voltage;
  • current;
  • device power.

The charger converts alternating current with a voltage of 220-240 V and a current of 5-6 A into direct current with the necessary parameters (5-18 V and 0.5-2.1 A).

The current filling rate of the device‚Äôs battery will directly depend on the current strength. The larger the value of this parameter, the faster the memory will restore the battery supply of your device. But each power supply is rated for its current. So, high rates can ruin it completely. In this case, a low current can lead to the fact that the device will not be charged at all. You need to know about this.

2-2.1 A is the value that will suit almost all modern flagship devices, and 0.7 A is an option for the oldest phones. It is also worth mentioning that manufacturers of low-cost memory devices often overestimate the strength of the output current. For example, a device that says 1 A in fact produces only 0.5 A.

Almost all modern gadgets, if other information is not indicated on them, are designed for a voltage of 5 V. This standard is used, for example, in computer USB ports.

Original memory or equivalent?

As we just found out, "native" is the best option. How to choose a charger for the phone, if the original is very expensive? You can monitor prices at several points of sale. After all, it is possible that only one store is overpriced, for example, because it is located in a busy place. Or, conversely, where there are no other stores that could impose competition on it.

If it‚Äôs not the point of sale, but the brand or model of your phone and the charger for it is more expensive than you can afford, then you should consider analogues or turn to offers for the sale of used memory devices.

Secondary market

A used original power supply can almost always be found in popular services with ads. The price for them here depends directly on the requests of sellers. We can safely assume that prices here are more interesting than in stores for new original memory devices. How to choose a charger for the phone, buying it from hand? Need to check it out. That is, the purchase or sale should take place where there are power outlets to verify the purchase.

Also, it is not superfluous to check the integrity of the wire. Sometimes it can be broken or there are traces of teeth of pets on it. The whole should be not only the wire, but also the connector, which should sit tightly in the charging socket of your mobile device and work stably.

If everything is in order and there are no flaws, then you can safely purchase a power supply, of course, if the transaction price suits you. Now you know how to choose a good charger for your phone, bypassing the offers of stores.

New Wireless Charger

The principle of operation of such a gadget is very simple. It consists in the fact that you put your device on a special panel and that‚Äôs it (the charging process begins). The principle of operation of such an accessory is based on the operation of a conventional induction coil. But in the modern world, this technology has been dubbed Qi. Other modern solutions

Ecology is in fashion today. And this is beginning to affect the charging of mobile devices. The solar-powered memory, which we examined above, just falls into this number of environmentally friendly solutions. But if the option with solar energy already seems standard and mundane, then other modern and environmentally friendly options are simply amazing.

For example, there is data about such a charger for mobile phones, which is suitable for those who like to spend time outdoors. Although maybe someone will use it at home. The device is a foot air pump. Inside the enclosure is a mechanism that converts energy from the flow of air into electricity. In short, you connect the phone to the pump, and it charges while you pump air.

There is another solution, which is also connected with the flow of air. Option suitable for cyclists. The device is a kind of hard case for a mobile gadget, on top of which a fan is installed. Wind flows from driving will rotate the fan blades, and a small generator of electric energy is built into the fan. Thus, while you pedal, the phone is charging.

Here you can recall bicycle dynamos originally from the times of the USSR, which received rotation from the bicycle wheel, and this energy fed the lighting devices on it. You can refine this option for mobile devices.

And this is not a complete list of environmentally friendly, that is, the best chargers for the phone. The list of such solutions is constantly updated.
