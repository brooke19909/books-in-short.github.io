Review of the camera "Horizon"

Horizont cameras (positive feedback on this equipment) were produced on the conveyor of JSC Krasnogorsk Plant named after S. A. Zverev. The same enterprises previously produced panoramic cameras of the FT type.

The first small-format device was produced in 1967. The release continued until 1973. About 50 thousand units were put up for sale.

What shooting principle is used in this device? The frame is scanned with a lens that rotates. The exposure in the process can vary according to the width of the slit. It also directly depends on the speed of passage of the gap.



Specifications

The manufacturer is a mechanical plant in Krasnogorsk. It was made 6 years. 135 type film is used. The frame has a size of 24 √ó 58 mm. The shutter is mechanical type, the gap changes. The lens rotates, fixed design. Focus is set to hyperfocal distance. If desired, you can manually set the shutter speed. There are four meanings. Flash sync is not integrated. The viewfinder is removable and optical. The total mass of the Horizont camera is 1.1 kg.



Varieties

In 1989, the model was presented under the index 202. After it was presented - 205 (hit the series in 2000), 203 and modifications of the latter. The differences between them are mainly in the number of extracts.

Panoramic cameras "Horizon-202" and 205 are presented to a wide buyer with the help of intermediaries - an Italian company. Because of this model, they were distributed under a different name: PAN. The PAN-35 modification, which was an analogue of Horizon-202, went on sale. They distinguished themselves among themselves due to the design of the case.

In 2010, a presentation of a joint work of a Russian plant and an Italian company, the Horizont camera, was held in Cologne. The price of the device did not exceed 1150 euros. The full name of the model is Horizon-D-L3.

Horizon 205 pc

This camera was created for professional filming. An unperforated film with a width of 61.5 mm was used. In narrower circles, it is known as type 120 material.

The device has a ‚Äúshift‚Äù mechanism, which allows the lens to move vertically by 8 mm. This makes it possible to change the shooting points and increase the space that is included in the picture.

Thanks to the rotating design of the lens, you can achieve good picture quality. This is due to the destruction of optical distortion. Most often, a similar problem was encountered with devices with fixed wide-angle lenses.

Thanks to two levels that provide the most correct position, use the device for horizontal installation.

Due to the presence of long shutter speeds, you can shoot at night. One frame can be exposed several times.

Due to the presence of an optical viewfinder, you can see the full boundaries of the resulting image even with the lens moving.

Summary

Until now, this camera is in demand. Older models are collected by collectors, and new ones are used by amateur photographers. It should be noted the many advantages of these devices and reviews that fully confirm them.
