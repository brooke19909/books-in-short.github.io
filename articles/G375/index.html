The best hotels of South Goa: description, rating, photos and reviews

Everyone sometimes needs a good rest, especially when winter, cold and dullness are outside. After all, it helps to reboot, relax and distract from everyday routine and problems. Most residents of Russia and the CIS countries prefer to spend their holidays in warm countries, where there is a good beach and a warm sea.

Now a large number of countries with a favorable climate are trying to develop in the field of tourism. After all, it is incredibly profitable. Moreover, tourism provides a large number of jobs to residents of the country. Those destinations where you can fly to relax in the winter enjoy special demand. After all, such countries are much smaller. Where can one go if there is cold, snow and frost outside the window at home? What about India, a state located in East Asia? This article will focus on South Goa, a county in the Indian state of Goa. It is located on the Hindustan Peninsula, which is washed by the waters of the Arabian Sea. An interesting fact: here the proportion of literate adult population is 80%, which is relatively higher than the national average. So, what about the best hotels in South Goa, their infrastructure, location?

Below you will find the rating of the best hotels for vacation in this place, considering the price-quality ratio:

  1. Taj Exotica Resort & Spa.
  2. La Grace Resort.
  3. Azaya Beach Resort Goa.
  4. Joecons Beach Resort.
  5. Radisson Blu Resort.
  6. Luxury Collection Resort & Spa.
  7. Lotus Beach Resort.
  8. Planet Hollywood Beach Resort.
  9. Beleza By The Beach.
  10. Karma Royal Haathi Mahal.

Taj Exotica Resort & Spa

One of the best hotels in South Goa 5 stars. It is located in the small town of Benaulim, right on the shores of the Arabian Sea. Here guests can enjoy the beautiful white sand beach, relax by the huge cool pool. In addition, they have the opportunity to visit the spa, which offers a huge range of services. Moreover, six restaurants with a varied cuisine have been created especially for guests.

List of rooms most praised by tourists:

  1. Luxurious one-bedroom villa. The room is made in Asian style in a beautiful sand color. It has a large double bed, private garden, balcony, TV. Such a villa costs about 25,000 rubles.
  2. Deluxe room with a private exit. It is decorated in a contemporary style in gray and white. It has two single beds, a seating area and a small terrace. The cost of this placement option is 30,000 rubles.

In the reviews, guests say that in one of the best hotels in South Goa, Taj Exotica Resort & Spa has excellent cuisine, a huge, incredibly beautiful area. In addition, professional staff works.

La grace resort

Looking for the best 3 star hotel in South Goa? Then this is exactly your option. It is located in the very center of Benaulim. It features a small outdoor pool, a bar, several restaurants and a spa. By the way, young children can have fun at several playgrounds.

So let's get acquainted with the numbers:

  1. Apartments. Spacious room decorated in sandy orange tones. It has a living room with a dining area and a seating area, a fully equipped kitchen, 2 double bedrooms, a TV and a wardrobe. The cost of this apartment is 5000 rubles.
  2. Deluxe Triple Room. It is made in beige in a rustic style. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay: bed, air conditioning, TV. This number costs 5200 rubles per day.

Reviews of the best budget hotels in South Goa say that La Grace Resort is an excellent representative of this category. It has reasonable prices, clean and comfortable rooms, tasty and safe food.

Azaya Beach Resort Goa

If you want to visit India for a long time, then pay attention to one of the best hotels in South Goa called Azaya Beach Resort. This hotel fully embodies the concept of "oriental luxury." After all, the whole territory is dotted with greenery, the sidewalks are paved with white stone. Near the huge pool there is a magnificent bar, nearby there is a private beach and several restaurants.

Where can I stay here:

  1. Standard double room. Incredibly beautiful room, made in bright, warm colors. It has a huge four-poster bed, a TV, a minibar with a large selection of drinks and snacks, a bathroom with a shower. Such a number costs 12,000 rubles.
  2. Luxurious room with a private pool. It is actually a paradise on earth. The room itself is huge, there is a guest corner and a giant bed, panoramic windows allow you to enjoy the beauty of your own pool and terrace all day. This placement option costs 30,000 rubles.

Joecons beach resort

If you do not need a luxurious interior, vast territories, then this hotel will be the perfect option for you. This small and comfortable hotel is located in a real tropical forest. It features an outdoor swimming pool, playground, on-site restaurant, bar and parking. By the way, the price of most rooms includes a wonderful breakfast.

The most common hotel room is a deluxe double room. Nice, bright and spacious room, decorated in white and brown shades. It has a double bed, a wardrobe, a TV, air conditioning and a dressing table where you can put yourself in order. This placement option costs 4,000 rubles.

Reviews of the best 3-star hotels in South Goa say that Joecons Beach Resort is a great option for a quiet and relaxing holiday. The hotel has a good location, excellent territory and helpful staff.

Radisson Blu Resort

Do you dream to taste luxury? Then you definitely need to visit one of the best hotels in South Goa called Radisson Blu Resort. It has a huge, green area, a luxurious outdoor pool and a white sand beach. Most of the rooms are based on the all-inclusive system, which allows guests to eat free of charge in all restaurants in the territory of this hotel complex.

Here are the numbers our compatriots prefer:

  1. Suite A beautiful southern-style room in bright colors. It has a seating area, a comfortable large bed, a balcony with great views and a bathroom. This placement option costs 30,000 rubles.
  2. Superior double room. Spacious, bright room with panoramic windows, made in brown and white. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay: bathroom, balcony, minibar and comfortable bed. This number costs 8,000 rubles per day.

Luxury Collection Resort & Spa

One of the best hotels of South Goa in India, which is really incredibly beautiful. The territory is surrounded by green, exotic plants, the huge pool is striking in its grandeur, and the fine cuisine in the restaurants conquers from the first bite.

Tourists advise the category of rooms where the room is made in a beautiful olive color, it has a large terrace, four-poster bed, TV and air conditioning. This placement option costs 18,000 rubles.

Reviews of the best hotels in South Goa say that Luxury Collection Resort & Spa is the perfect place for a wonderful vacation. There is everything that a person may need on vacation, shops, restaurants, the beach and the pool.

Lotus beach resort

If you want to relax modestly without spending a fortune, then Lotus Beach Resort is the best option for you. This small hotel at first sight impresses with its territory, which is buried in beautiful green plants.

Where can I stay here:

  1. Standard double room. A cozy room decorated in a contemporary style in sand color. It has a wardrobe, safe, minibar, a good bathroom and a bed. This number costs 3,500 rubles per night.
  2. Suite This number is made in the same color scheme. The main difference is the presence of a spacious balcony and a slightly larger area. A deluxe room costs 9,000 rubles per day.

In the reviews, guests say that there is everything for a great stay: clean rooms, friendly staff and a good location.

Planet Hollywood Beach Resort

A huge and incredibly beautiful hotel with a magnificent pool, a private beach and a good fitness center. There is also a bar, spa and parking on site.

Let's get acquainted with the numbers:

  1. Superior double room. It has an incredibly stylish and unusual interior. The room is made in a modern style in black and gray. It has a large terrace, an oversized bed, a dressing table and air conditioning. This placement option costs 13,000 rubles.
  2. Luxurious room for two adults. The room is decorated in dark, warm shades in the English style, atmospheric posters and posters hang on the walls. The room has an excellent balcony with a magnificent view of the pool, bed, minibar and air conditioning. This placement option costs 19,000 rubles.
  3. Executive Suite. Room for true connoisseurs of beautiful design. The living room is made in a stylish brown color, the main bright nuance is a leather sofa in red. Such a suite costs 20,000 rubles per day.

The reviews say the hotel is incredibly cool. It is surrounded by greenery, has wonderful staff and a luxurious beach.

Beleza by the beach

The most popular accommodations are superior double rooms. The room is decorated in a modern style in beige and brown tones. It has a balcony with stunning views of the sea and the territory, a bed, a TV and a comfortable sofa. This number costs 10,000 rubles per day.

Karma Royal Haathi Mahal

The last hotel in Goa to be discussed. It has a huge pool, several restaurants and a spa.
