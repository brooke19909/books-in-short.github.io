Tire frog - a toy or a flower bed?

If you are interested in decorating your garden, but do not want to spend money, then this is the solution for you. Tire figures are one of the simple solutions you can apply. Firstly, you don‚Äôt need to buy anything for this, that is, the price will be very cheap. Secondly, you also spend a little effort. With your own hands you can make various crafts from tires. A frog is one of the simplest solutions that can be picked up. A variety of methods allow you to choose for yourself the most suitable option.



Decide first on the end result. What do you want to accomplish: a flower garden, a toy for a child or decoration for a garden. Each option is interesting in its own way, but the methodology and sequence of actions are different everywhere. An excellent frog from a tire will turn out if you dig one wheel vertically 1/3 into the ground. On the top of the rim, draw eyes and a mouth, and so we have a small frog. For garden decoration, it is enough to have only one or two blanks.

For a children's swing, a frog from a tire is made a little differently. Here it is necessary to prepare a seat for the child in advance. We cut a small tire into two halves and attach a cut board on top. If you use material that could injure a child, it is necessary to sheathe the seat with dense material. Then it will be more comfortable to sit, and the child will not get hurt.

A frog from a tire for a flower bed is made of several blanks. Better if there are three pieces of the same size. You can take two tires of the same diameter, and a third larger or, conversely, smaller. We lay two wheels side by side on the site of the future flower garden. We fill the empty cavities with earth - these are the future landing sites. We fix one more tire on top. If the dimensions of the wheels are large, then fixing the upper part is not necessary. The third tire is also covered with earth. So that nothing spills out of it, you can pre-cover the bottom with any improvised material.





Now, to make the frog out of the tire, add eyes. They can be made from plastic bottles by cutting off the bottom diagonally. Or come up with your own version. Having painted the tires in green, on the top we draw a red mouth, it is possible with a tongue. So we got a pretty frog out of tires. In the free zone we plant flowers. To give a greater resemblance, you can cut the legs from polystyrene or wood. Attach to a finished figure using a rope or an old hose.



Now, knowing how to make a frog out of a tire, you can handle any option yourself. Even from one blank, you get a pretty flowerbed. Especially if you have a big tire. We pour earth into it, draw a mouth and attach eyes. That's all. Ready is our beauty. If you don‚Äôt know what to make eyes from, don‚Äôt worry, you can simply draw them. The brighter your crafts, the prettier they will look in the garden.

Even from pieces of tire, you can make a frog by cutting out its legs and attaching it to a tree. To decorate the garden , just a stone will do if you paint it and draw a green frog on it. Your imagination will tell you what to use for decoration. Of course, your crafts can not be compared with nature, because she is the best artist. And the best decoration of your garden will be a lively croaker jumping along the paths. But this does not mean that it is not necessary to try to ennoble and embellish your lands. Hand-made figures for the garden are perfect for this role. Go for it! You will definitely succeed!
