Noginsk, hotel "Aniva": address, description of rooms, reviews

Beautiful nature and clean air attracts many tourists Noginsk. Hotel "Aniva" offers you comfortable conditions for relaxing in the country.





Short hotel description

Adherents of a healthy lifestyle often choose Noginsk for their holidays . Hotel "Aniva" offers you a beautiful territory, comfortable rooms, as well as the highest standards of service. Fifteen hectares of parkland are completely planted with greenery, and also contain a huge number of infrastructure. There are walking spots, a sports hall, a barbecue, and a lake for fishing.

Speaking of rooms, it is worth noting that this 4-star hotel offers comfortable accommodation. The rooms are very simple, but at the same time incredibly elegant. There is a lot of light and living space, as well as comfortable upholstered furniture. All apartments have a spacious bathroom, and the windows offer simply incredible scenery. In addition, all apartments in the hotel complex are covered by the wireless Internet signal.

Just the perfect place to relax in nature is Noginsk. Hotel Aniva, with its large green area, offers you many opportunities for fishing and barbecue. And if you prefer to enjoy ready-made dishes, then by all means visit the restaurant where you can taste the most exquisite dishes of European cuisine. In addition, a delicious and hearty breakfast is served each morning.





"Aniva" (hotel, Noginsk): address

Hotel Aniva is located in the village of Zhilino-Gorki. It is very close to Moscow. A trip to the center of the capital will not take you even an hour. The path to Noginsk will take you no more than a quarter of an hour. Nearby is a picturesque lake, which will delight lovers of fishing and just a relaxing holiday in nature.





Hotel Rooms

If you want to rent a room in the Aniva Hotel, you can choose one of the following options for yourself:

  • Single apartments are designed for single occupancy, and therefore their area is quite small. There is a minimum necessary for a comfortable stay: a bed, a TV, a desk and a number of amenities. The bathroom has a shower.
  • Double apartments are equipped with two single beds. A complete set of furniture and household appliances is provided for the most comfortable stay in the room.
  • Junior Suite has a large area and a high level of comfort. It is worth noting a luxurious, but at the same time concise design. The bathroom is equipped with a large bath, as well as bath accessories and a hairdryer.
  • Suite is the most luxurious apartment in this hotel. The rooms are decorated in a cozy country style. There are two rooms here, one of which has a double bed, and the second is equipped with a dining table and upholstered furniture. The bathroom includes a spa bath.




What facilities are provided in the rooms?

In this hotel the apartments are equipped with the following amenities:

  • modern comfortable furniture for your good rest;
  • ventilation and air conditioning system that helps create a comfortable microclimate in your apartment;
  • a bathroom with a bath or shower (depending on the category of the room), as well as a hairdryer and all necessary bath accessories;
  • modern widescreen TV connected to both satellite and cable antenna;
  • landline phone for communication with the reception, as well as for calls to other cities.

Negative reviews

Guests note the following flaws in the Aniva Hotel:

  • if for some reason you have to leave the hotel ahead of time, then the staff will do everything to not return the funds for an unused day;
  • It may come as a surprise to you that there are no cosmetic accessories in the bathroom, so take them with you (the nearest store is not so close);
  • if you require replacing bed linen before the due date, then you will have to pay extra for this;
  • When booking a room through special resources, you cannot be sure that your application has been accepted and you will be settled there (it is better to order directly);
  • despite the fact that wireless Internet is declared in the list of services, we can say that it is practically absent, since the speed and quality are almost at zero;
  • breakfast starts at 9:00, which is very late, because at this time many people go for walks and excursions;
  • despite the fact that the TV is connected to a satellite dish, it broadcasts only 2-3 channels (all the others are very "snowy");
  • if you order a banquet, you may find that the quality and quantity of dishes does not match the price that you paid, and therefore require that every item be written in the check for which you are charged.

General impression

The city of Noginsk attracts residents of Moscow and other cities with its beautiful nature and clean forest air. Hotel "Aniva" invites you to have a good time in comfortable rooms. This institution has a very beautiful territory, which also includes a large picturesque lake. Guests have access to fishing and barbecue facilities. The main advantage of the hotel is its silence (both on the territory and inside). But not without flaws. So, the organization of work of employees leaves much to be desired. It is also worth redecorating the apartments. Nevertheless, if you like a relaxing holiday, then you are advised to rent a room at the Aniva Hotel.
