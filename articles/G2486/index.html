Ippon Back Power Pro 600: model review and customer reviews

When the first computers were put into operation, it was assumed that they would work stably and continuously. If programs crashed and the PC shut down, then the user lost his data. When it comes to systems installed in production, here we can talk not only about the loss of information, but also about the large financial costs. In the nineties, along with the rapid development of computer technology, the problem of power outages became quite popular. Because of this, mass systems went out of service. Therefore, devices that can provide uninterrupted power came to protect the computer. Such devices can not only eliminate voltage drops, but also makes it possible to respond quickly when it is emergency shut off. The source has rechargeable batteries that allow you to keep the equipment in an active state. Thanks to this, a person can quickly save the results of his work and turn off the PC.

When the first uninterruptible power supplies became available , which spread rather quickly, the number of broken PCs sharply decreased, and working with them became as safe as possible. In today's market there is a wide range of devices that can solve any problem. This may be the operation of not only home computers, but also entire systems.

Uninterruptible model from Ippon

Ippon Back Power Pro 600 is one of the best representatives of the line of uninterrupted devices. It is quite powerful, and also affordable for any buyer. This model is sold in 2 variations: regular and Euro. The difference between them is minimal, they differ only in design.

Features

The uninterrupted power supply of the Ippon Back Power Pro 600 does not look too elaborate. Its appearance is classic. The device is a rectangular block. On the front of the device you can see the power button, USB port and the indicator itself. The decorative elements that are available with this device give it originality. On the side are special grilles. They protect the device from overheating. The back of the device is not much different from a device of this type. This UPS is suitable for home use only. There are three sockets on the rear panel, one of them is built-in to protect telephone lines. This kit is enough for home.

The UPS Ippon Back Power Pro 600 Euro has a different design. This device received a liquid crystal display. Due to this, the device displays more information. The design is stylized and has decorative elements. On the front panel are the keys to turn on. The grilles that allow air to circulate are smaller. There are slightly more ports on the rear panel than in the previous device. The first thing that is noticeable is a connector that meets the RS-232 standard. It is an alternative USB connection. There are also two sockets for plugs, they are protected from battery drops. There are two ports for connecting phones and modems. The last thing on this surface is the fuse.

Specifications

Specifications Ippon Back Power Pro 600 and its varieties are not much different from each other. The devices are designed for a maximum power of 600 watts. This is enough to work with a simple home computer. The mains voltage is 200 volts. The range at which the device operates is 162-268 V.

Of all the protective functions, it should be noted that the device is able to respond to a short circuit and filter the voltage that is supplied. The Ippon Back Power Pro 600 has a built-in battery that cannot be taken apart. There is no possibility of service, the system is designed for 7.2 Ah. 12-volt batteries. They have a lead-acid structure. In the characteristics of the device it is written that the device is able to cope with a high voltage drop, without even switching to a battery.

Reviews

The majority of consumers have a pretty good opinion about the device. Most buyers emphasize affordable cost as well as stability. However, there are negative reviews in which they talk about overheating of the device and a short life. Most often, the uninterruptible power supply Ippon Back Power Pro 600 fails after one and a half to three years. But still, many buyers recommend this device. It copes with its duties as much as possible, which deserves attention.

conclusions

The Ippon Back Power Pro 600 is a great device when it comes to the budget segment. The power will be enough for the computer to work stably and reliably. Judging by the reviews, it is convenient to manage. At the same time, both the keys and other physical elements have excellent fastenings. You can also use uninterruptible power using special software. The device is able to increase and decrease the voltage to certain ranges. This fixture can keep the system active for about 15 minutes after turning off the light.
