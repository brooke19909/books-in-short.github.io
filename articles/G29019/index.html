At the federal level

The thing is that some vehicles are exempt from taxation, regardless of where they are registered. Only not everyone knows when to take advantage of the relevant benefits.

At the moment, they do not pay at the federal level for:

  • public transport;
  • vehicles for special services;
  • boats with a motor no more than 5 horsepower;
  • rowing boats;
  • agricultural transport;
  • transport intended for the transport of goods and passengers;
  • a car belonging to the disabled or used for their transportation;
  • cars purchased with the support of social services, if they do not exceed the capacity of 100 "horses".

Nor do vessels that have been entered in the register of the Russian Federation provide for taxation. For other vehicles you have to pay in the manner prescribed by law.

Preparation for calculations

We have already studied the benefits of transport tax in Krasnoyarsk. We will find out later how to arrange them. First you have to understand how to calculate, pay and check the tax on the vehicle.

The formula for calculating tax on a car in Krasnoyarsk is used exactly the same as in other cities of the country. A citizen will need:

  1. Get the quotient from dividing the months of vehicle ownership in the year by 12.
  2. Multiply the tax rate on engine power, and then on the quotient of the division obtained in advance according to the principle proposed above.
  3. If necessary, multiply the resulting amount by the coefficient of tax increase on vehicles. This is necessary when it comes to luxury cars.

Perhaps this is all. This formula is used to calculate the transport tax in Krasnoyarsk and other regions of the Russian Federation in relation to individuals. Organizations use an advanced formula.

To do this, the previously obtained figure must be multiplied by the organization‚Äôs share in the ownership of the movable object, and then, if necessary, deduct the benefits.

Payment time

What is the time to pay the transport tax in the Krasnoyarsk Territory? At the moment, organizations in the respective region make advance payments. They are made at the end of each reporting period - 1, 2 and 3 quarters. Moreover, payments must be made no later than the end of the month following the end of the quarter.

With individuals, everything is much simpler. The thing is that the transport tax in Krasnoyarsk is paid until the first of December. And no later than that. The next day, the delay in tax accrual will begin.

If not pay

What will be the consequences of the resulting tax overdue? They depend on the specific case.

If tax delinquency is allowed unintentionally, a twenty percent penalty is imposed, otherwise a forty percent penalty. From the first day of delay in tax, a penalty will be calculated, and as soon as the amount of debt reaches significant amounts (from three thousand), the case can be transferred to the bailiffs. Where it leads?

Bailiffs can arrest and sell the property of the debtor for debts (not only in taxes), as well as block bank cards. The debtor is also often barred from leaving Russia.

The procedure for applying for benefits

The procedure and deadlines for paying transport tax in the Krasnoyarsk Territory need to know. But it is also important to remember how to properly apply for vehicle tax benefits. This with timely preparation will be a fairly simple task.

To achieve the desired goal, a citizen or organization requires:

  1. Prepare documents for further maintenance.
  2. Fill in the application of the established form for the appointment of the prescribed benefits.
