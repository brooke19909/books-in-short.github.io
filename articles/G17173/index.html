Ferrari Land Port Aventura - Theme Family Fun Theme Park

The conceptually unusual Ferrari Land Port Aventura is an exclusive theme park dedicated to the world-famous Italian brand Ferrari. Here, adults and children have the opportunity to immerse themselves in the history of one of the most prestigious automobile brands in the world. Divided into several zones and equipped with a wide variety of attractions, Ferrari Land today is considered the most popular park in the world. Perhaps only the Ferrari World of the same name, opened in the UAE in 2010, can be compared with it. Actually, it was only thanks to its wild popularity that it was decided to open the second Ferrari Land in the world, but already in Europe.

Location of the Ferrari Land Port Aventura

Ferrari Land is located in a larger park called Port Aventura, the first largest in the Mediterranean and the second in Europe. It, in turn, is located in the vicinity of Barcelona (100 km), in the city of Salou, on the Costa Daurada. The easiest way to get there is from the capital of Catalonia by bus or train. Also, almost on the coast there is a Reus airport, but a direct flight from Russia to fly there is very problematic.





History of the creation of Ferrari Land

In 2010, the first Ferrari Land was opened in Abu Dhabi (United Arab Emirates), which has become very popular - up to 5,000,000 people visit it annually. The purpose of the creation was to pay tribute to Enzo Ferrari - the genius who founded the automobile brand, with whose name speed, innovation and technology are associated. Here, every attraction, every detail is designed to convey the mood of Ferrari to guests.

Due to the frenzied popularity of the theme park in the UAE, the founders decided to open a similar entertainment complex in Europe. So, in Spain there was a Ferrari Land Port Aventura, the opening date of which occurred at the beginning of May 2017. 100 million euros were allocated for the construction. Therefore, the amusement park has a well-developed infrastructure, which includes many different objects.





Conceptual theme park solutions

Since Ferrari is an Italian brand, the park is inextricably linked with this country. Here you can feel the spirit of Italy, the birthplace of the legendary automobile brand. Each corner of Ferrari Land is stylized for a specific city in Italy. While in Spain, you can visit some of the sights of Milan, Rome and Venice. And of course, the founder's house is equipped here.

Tickets: cost and varieties

The price of a ticket at Ferrari Land Port Aventura for one day for people from 11 to 59 years old is 49 euros, for children from 4 to 10 years old and people of retirement age (from 60) - 41 euros. If you want to buy a ticket for 2 days, it will cost 69 and 56 euros, respectively, for 3 days - 90 and 73 euros.

There is an opportunity to buy tickets cheaper on the official website of the park. There you can familiarize yourself with promotions in order to save even more and relax even more!





Ferrari Land Port Aventura: reviews

Since the park opened recently, there are still very few reviews about it. However, those who managed to visit the Ferrari Land were satisfied with the rest. In particular, families with children, because the park has enough entertainment for kids and adults, there are areas for a relaxing holiday and cafes where you can eat.

There are also hotels in the park, so when planning a trip, you can book rooms in one of the hotels. This is very convenient, since you do not need to spend time traveling to the park. Moreover, hotel guests have privileges when visiting Ferrari Land Port Aventura.
