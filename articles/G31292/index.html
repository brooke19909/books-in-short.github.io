Ryazan-Sasovo, features of a trip along the route

If you look at the map of the Ryazan region, it is noticeable that, except for the regional center, there are no other major cities. This differs from neighboring regions. From the regional center, you can get different means of transport to the opposite end of the region - to the city of Sasovo. The distance from Ryazan to Sasovo is 200 kilometers.

Bus ride

The central bus station is located in Ryazan on the Moscow highway. Five buses leave from Sasovo almost every day:

  • 11:30 a.m.
  • 13:50.
  • 15:35.
  • 17:10.
  • 6 p.m.

The trip from Ryazan to Sasovo takes three hours, the ticket costs 350 rubles. Buses are small, usually Mercedes with 19 seats. The final destination for buses can be not only Sasovo, but also the village of Kadom.

In the opposite direction, from Sasovo to Ryazan, buses run from Monday to Saturday according to the following schedule:

  • 06:00 a.m.
  • 08:45.
  • 13:30.

The exception is Sunday. There is only one flight to Ryazan - at 13:30. It should be remembered that in the winter the buses are quite cold, sometimes passengers complain about it.

Rail ride

The distance between the settlements is small, so there are electric trains between Ryazan and Sasovo. The schedule of their departure from Ryazan-1 station looks like this:

  • 04:20.
  • 6:48 a.m.
  • 12:40.
  • 14:37.
  • 19:17.

The trip takes 3 hours 15 minutes. A ticket costs 423 rubles. Thus, the tariff is 2.1 rubles per kilometer.

In the opposite direction, from Sasovo to Ryazan, electric trains run according to the following schedule:

  • 06:07 a.m.
  • 08:33.
  • 13:12.
  • 13:31.
  • 14:56.
  • 18:40.

Ticket price and travel time do not differ. There will be about 20 stops in 1 minute along the way. The largest of them is the regional center of Shilovo.

Sasovo is located on the railway that connects Moscow and the Volga region with the Urals, so some long-distance trains also stop there.

Trains from Ryazan to Sasovo depart according to the following schedule:

  • 02:08 and 02:58. A rare passenger train to Ulyanovsk and an ambulance to Karaganda alternate. The latter is formed by the railways of Kazakhstan.
  • 05:02, 05:12 or 05:36. Named train from St. Petersburg to Samara. Civil, but expensive.
  • 15:31. Fast train from Moscow to Ufa. It has seating cars, and a trip for the price will be a little more expensive than a bus.
  • 18:52. A passenger train to Chelyabinsk with seated cars and an ambulance to Dimitrovgrad alternate.
  • 21:01. Signature double-decker train to Samara. The fastest, two hours on the road.
  • 22:35 or 22:40. Named train from Moscow to Ulyanovsk.

The trip takes from 2.5 to 3 hours. The cost depends on a number of factors: the type of train and wagon, the shares of Russian Railways, the seasonal tariff. You need to focus on such prices:

  • Sedentary. From 400 rubles.
  • Reserved seat. From 550 rubles.
  • The compartment. From 660 rubles.
  • Sleeping. From 2100 rubles.

In the opposite direction, from Sasovo to Ryazan, trains run according to the following schedule:

  • 00:22. Two-storey branded from Samara.
  • 03:32 and 03:35. Company composition from Ulyanovsk.
  • 05:10. Passenger from Chelyabinsk.
  • 09:16 and 09:32. Trains from Karaganda and Ulyanovsk alternate.
  • 12:10. Branding from Samara to St. Petersburg.
  • 23:10. Non-branded train from Ufa to Moscow.

Car trip

From Ryazan you need to go southeast along the E-30 highway and move along it to the city of Shatsk, where you need to turn north, onto the R-124 highway. It leads directly to the city center. It‚Äôs real to travel such a route in 2.5-3 hours, depending on the weather and the traffic load.

What to see in Sasovo?

Sights of Sasovo are hardly widely known. This is not Suzdal or Pereyaslavl-Zalessky.

The city preserved the cathedral of the beginning of the XIX century. If you drive from Sasovo 18 kilometers to the village of Kargashino, you can see the remains of the estate in the Gothic style.

In addition, it is worth paying attention to such objects:

  • Museum of Local Lore.
  • Museum of Russian Song.
  • Museum of the flight school.
  • Unexpected for these places, a monument to Ernest Hemingway, as well as railway workers and pilots (An-2).
  • Manor Troitsk.

And you can still admire the Tsna River.
