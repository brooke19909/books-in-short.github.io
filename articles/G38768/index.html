Benq W1070 Projector: Overview, Specifications and Reviews

Projectors for home use occupy a rather specific niche in the entertainment multimedia technology market. Firstly, this equipment is not cheap even in the initial segment, and secondly, the widespread distribution of such devices is hampered by serious competition with mini-cinemas. Nevertheless, projectors also have a sufficient audience of fans of high-quality "pictures", especially when it comes to multifunctional equipment with the inclusion of modern technology. In this regard, the short-focus model Benq W1070, an overview of which is presented below, is unique in just a combination of high-quality hardware, moderate for its price segment and a wide range of communication capabilities for connecting.

Projector Overview





The model is positioned as a mid-range DLP projector, which is designed for home use. However, the boundaries in the segments are quite blurred, so this device can be considered as a good solution for the office, and as a budget equipment for private use. Actually, both the cost and the poor equipment indicate that the equipment belongs to the initial level. The VGA cable, power cord, remote control, and software disc are the complete set that comes with the Benq W1070. The price of the device varies on average from 60 to 70 thousand rubles. This is inexpensive, given the active expansion of the class of multifunctional models from 100 thousand rubles. True, in the middle link there are enough competitors with high performance parameters.

Specifications





Against the background of the spread of cutting-edge image transmission formats such as UHD and 4K, the capabilities of this model look quite modest. Nevertheless, the high quality of the Benq W1070 projector and decent performance allow it to compete for class leaders. The characteristics of the device can be represented as follows:

  • Resolution - 1920x1080.
  • The power of the light source is 240 watts.
  • The operational life of the lamp is 3,500 hours.
  • The diagonal parameters are from 102 to 600 cm.
  • Frame scan - frequency from 23 to 120 Hz.
  • The power of light radiation is 2,000 Lm.
  • The contrast ratio is 10,000: 1.
  • The noise figure is 33 dB.
  • HDTV format - up to 1080p.
  • Dimensions - 31.2x10.9x24.4 cm.
  • Weight - 2.75 kg.

Design and controls





The developers of the model did not look for unusual formats for the design to attract the client with the original design and applied the traditional concept of a block projector. The case is made of glossy plastic, while the front part is decorated with a gray insert. On the same side, the Benq W1070 projector has a ventilation grill, a lens and a proximity sensor, which blocks the light supply if necessary. The main slots and connectors of the device are located on the back. Here, in particular, there is a special trigger for connecting motorized screens.

The sides, with the exception of the ventilation openings, have nothing. The bottom of the device is equipped with a grill for air passage, as well as threaded fastenings for the bracket with a support leg. The main external controls and power buttons Benq W1070 are located on the top panel. In the recess, where gray inserts are also present, rings are provided for adjusting the image scale and focus.

Interfaces and Communication





For several years audio and video equipment has been required to support the HDMI interface as the most modern means of connection. Although this cable is not included in the basic package, the projector provides for two whole sockets of this type. Also, the rear side of the device allows you to connect devices via composite input, VGA, S-VIDEO and the relatively new format of the RS-232 connectors. For sound, RCA channels and a standard mini-jack are provided. Benq W1070 also supports mini-USB technology. It is worth noting that modern projectors, despite the development of the hardware, often have problems with sound. The creators of this model thoroughly approached the implementation of the speaker system. The model is equipped with a 10 W speaker, which, judging by the reviews, does a good job of playing both the soundtrack in films and musical parts.

Operating modes

In addition to the basic set of formats with different settings, Benq also offers two original viewing modes - SmartEco and EcoBlank. In the first case, automatic adjustment of the lamp power of the device is proposed, supplemented by an intelligent brightness setting, taking into account the minimization of energy consumption. Even more economical EcoBlank mode automatically turns off the projected light output if the device is not used for a certain period of time. In this case, there is a gradual reduction in the power of the signal source, which allows to reduce energy consumption by almost 70%.

Positive feedback about the model





As the practice of operating this model shows, the manufacturer was able to maximize the operational capabilities of the hardware filling. Users note a decent image quality with a high brightness of the lamp. The advantages of a short focal length are also emphasized, which also provides advantages in the viewing process. The latest technological innovations Benq W1070 projector is not deprived, but the presence of 3D technology compensates for this flaw. In general, the owners of this device include ergonomics as the main advantages, a high level of which can be traced in every detail. But first of all it concerns external control elements of settings on the case and the remote control.

Negative reviews

Serious shortcomings from the point of view of the direct image translation function are not noted by users. As for the remaining nuances, many owners complain about the noise of the projector, which is due to the operation of the fans. Oddly enough, there are complaints about the presence of speakers. According to some users, projection equipment is not designed for acoustics, so the sound reproduction function seems redundant. However, there are other opinions on the speakers Benq W1070. Testimonials from users who value the versatility of multimedia technology, for example, highlight the benefits of a built-in speaker. So, if you plan to use the projector in the country, where there is no special sound equipment, such an addition is simply necessary.

Conclusion





Modern projectors have to wage a fierce battle in the market with plasma and LCD panels, which are successfully used as home theaters. Realizing the popularity of traditional solutions, manufacturers seek to surprise consumers with new features and communication capabilities. However, a completely different example is shown by the Benq W1070 projector. The price of the device is 60-70 thousand rubles. due not to a wide range of options, but to a high-quality elemental base and thoughtful technical stuffing. This option is not suitable for connoisseurs of high-tech fashion features, but for those who expect to get decent quality with minimal investment.
