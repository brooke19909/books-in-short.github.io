Aventura Park (Turkey) - an excellent hotel near Alanya

Hotel "Aventura Park" (Turkey, Antalya region) is a very successful example of a suburban "five". It is located right by the sea, near the very picturesque village of Avsallar. But to nearby large cities, and especially to amazing Alanya, you will have to get about forty kilometers by local minibus or taxi. Antalya is double the distance. However, the location of this hotel makes it possible to enjoy the clear sea and breathtaking landscapes, and the distances become invisible, since local transport is not very expensive, it runs often and regularly. It has been hosting Aventura Park guests for more than twenty years. Turkey is generally famous for its five-star complexes, but this particular hotel is worthy of special mention. However, everything is in order.

Hotel "Aventura Park" (Turkey, Antalya). Services and meals

The hotel has a restaurant and five bars (vitamin, in the lobby, on the beach and by the pool, with a disco), and the banquet room can also be used for various seminars. You can park your own car or rent a car. The hotel practices an all inclusive system. You can specially order seafood. A children's menu is available. From recreational activities, massage is offered, a special center for fitness. There are two outdoor pools with a jacuzzi. Hammam - for a fee. Water slides - for adults and children. And in bad weather, a bathhouse under the roof will come in handy. At the beach, as well as at the outdoor pool, umbrellas and sun loungers are available. Towels for sunbathing and mattresses are provided on bail. There is everything you need for fans to play table and tennis. A mini club for young guests accepts children under fourteen years of age.



Park Aventura Hotel (Turkey): reviews

Tourists who visited this hotel speak of a fairly high level of service. They like cozy rooms, a beautiful well-kept park, which is nice to walk on a hot day, a convenient location and proximity to the sea and transport, as well as an extreme variety of food and the skill of cooks. Their work was even called the "culinary show." Especially praised meat and fish grills and desserts. In addition, outside the hotel are very picturesque places, somewhat reminiscent of the Crimea - rocky capes and bays. The staff is well-trained, friendly, very memorable. In a word, this is the hotel where you want to return to hear the sound of the sea and feel the peace of mind. Some even cried when they left.
