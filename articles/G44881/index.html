Penza Pawnshops: addresses and schedule

A pawnshop is a credit institution that issues short-term interest-bearing loans that are accrued daily. In this case, property is taken as a pledge.

Pawn shops of Penza

In the Russian city of Penza, there are several dozen pawnshops. It is difficult to name their exact number.

Penza pawnshops provide secured loans:

  • silver;
  • gold;
  • mobile phones (you must bring documents to them);
  • household appliances.

The loan term is determined in each case individually. The client can repay the debt ahead of schedule, while the overpayment will be less. Interest on the use of money is accrued daily.
