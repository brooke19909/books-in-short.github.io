How to find out connected services?

And the first thing you should do is check on which subscriptions you are currently subscribed to. Only in this way can you be sure that you have disabled everything. In turn, the management of MTS subscriptions is carried out in several ways. We will cover all of them in this chapter.





So, the first option is to use a special team. To find out what you are subscribed to, dial * 152 * 2 #. This is the easiest way to check your MTS subscriptions. You can also call 152 from the phone connected to this operator, and then select button 2 in the voice menu.

Another option is through the site. How to check MTS subscriptions in this way is indicated on the official portal of the company. Just go to your personal account and select the "My Subscriptions" section. Right on the same page you will see a list of services for which you pay your money.

How to manage subscriptions?

It should be noted that MTS subscription management can be carried out in the same ways by which you can check what you are specifically subscribed to. But it allows you to completely turn off all subscriptions or unsubscribe them one at a time, managing individually.
