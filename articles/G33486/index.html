Veteran of Labor medal: popular recognition turned into an element of private collections

Despite the contradictory nature of the Soviet state, this period left us a great legacy. Today, the Communist Party is relentlessly criticized, often in the case. However, her reign marked itself with many positive aspects. After all, before the civil war, whatever you say, Russia was hopeless

backward from European leaders peasant country. Moreover, it is very likely that the fate of Iran, Turkey or China could have awaited her - also great once, who instilled fear in the whole world accessible, but by the beginning of the 20th century had turned into dependent colonies of Western states.

The great leap of economic recovery in the 1930s, and then in the post-war years, allowed us not only to avoid the fate of the third world countries, but to make it one of the most highly developed in the world. This could not have been achieved if there had been no colossal national industriousness and literally a cult of labor created in the Union. It is not surprising that the Veteran of Labor medal became not just an occasion for pride, but almost the most significant award of the country, recognition of the greatest merits for the good of the fatherland. In its significance, it did not surpass, perhaps, only military awards, celebrating the exploits of the Great Patriotic War.





Veteran of Labor medal and its history

The order establishing this award was adopted by the Soviet leadership in 1974. His strength remained until the collapse of the state in 1991. Interestingly, over all these years, the Veteran of Labor medal was published in more than 39 thousand copies. This number is very, very large. However, the mass does not detract from its merits. This regalia was a very valuable and honorable distinction. And such a large volume of circulation is explained by the following fact. The Veteran of Labor medal was received in all spheres of activity of the Soviet state: scientists, collective farmers, directors of machine-building enterprises and machine tool workers, officers and school teachers could be awarded this award.
