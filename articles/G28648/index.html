Bulgaria, Kamchia SOK: rest on Golden Sands

Very popular among Russians is Kamchia JC in Bulgaria. This resort complex is famous for its unique location, quality of service, perfect cleanliness and unusually warm attitude towards vacationers. There are two ways to get there. Firstly, there is a special program in Moscow, according to which children, and sometimes their parents, are sent to Kamchia for free. Secondly, you can just buy a ticket there.

Location





Bulgaria is rightfully proud of its largest tourist center Varna and the resort of Golden Sands. SOK Kamchia is very close to these attractions. Varna is about 30 kilometers from there, and another 18 kilometers to Golden Sands. The abbreviation SOK with a fruit drink is not connected in any way and is deciphered as follows: a health resort complex. Kamchia is the name of a rivulet flowing near the resort and flowing into the sea. You can reach the place of confluence in 15 minutes by boat or walk on foot. The water there has a significant temperature difference (+10 river and +22 sea). This property can be used as an exotic thermal massage for legs. Kamchia is a narrow and shallow river with very beautiful banks. Nearby is a nature reserve. An interesting fact - in Bulgaria there is the village of Kamchia, which lies far from the sea and has nothing to do with the described JUICE.

SOK Kamchia, Bulgaria. Camp Rainbow

There is a wonderful place on Earth where every child will be happy. This is a recreation camp "Rainbow", located in the Bulgarian "Kamchia".

Children are welcome in comfortable buildings with rooms equipped with a shower, toilet, air conditioning, TV and a set of necessary furniture. All of them are two-room. Each is designed for 4 people. Meals are organized four times a day. Children are offered juices and fruits every day. For leisure activities in the camp there is an entertaining and educational children's center with many interesting circles. Each child has the right to choose for himself two of them and at the end of the shift to demonstrate what he learned. Also for children there is a pool, a computer room, a cinema, a ballroom, a fitness center. The beach is next to the camp. By order of children are distributed according to age, but not more than 20 people. Experienced pioneer counselors and educators look after them during the shift, and on the beach there is an additional rescue service and paramedics. Russian money for Bulgarian officially changes several times a month. Valuables can be deposited with caregivers.
