Lappeenranta hotels: description, photos and reviews

The resort, which bears the interesting name of Lappeenranta, is a Finnish town that is rightfully owned by students. It is located very close to the borders of Russia. Many residents of St. Petersburg visit this city for the purpose of shopping in order to purchase high-quality and interesting things. But in addition to really profitable and wonderful shopping, here you can enjoy the magnificence of beautiful nature or make interesting acquaintances with foreign students who belong to the elite social classes.

Tours in Lappeenranta are very popular, especially among residents of the northern regions of Russia. Here you can observe simply stunning landscapes that remain in the imagination for a long time. The city has a special and main attraction, which is Lake Saimaa. This area pleases every tourist with its beautiful landscape at any time of the year. An interesting fact is that in winter you can ski here and just take a walk along the snowy shores.

Thanks to these factors, the city has become very popular among Russian tourists. Lappeenranta attracted particular attention by the fact that she can with undisguised pride boast her best spa complex in Finland. This hotel is very popular among tourists, because it provides really high-quality services. In addition, there are a number of excellent and high-quality hotel complexes in the city that can offer guests high-level services. Lappeenranta hotels in the city center are close to all points of interest. There are also more budget options that are suitable for every pocket.

The most popular placements in Lappeenranta will be featured in this article.

Spa in Lappeenranta





Among domestic tourists, spa tours to Lappeenranta are very popular. After all, here is the best complex in all of Finland. The hotel is located in the city center, on the shores of Lake Saimaa.

Here guests have free access to the pool and sauna. For vacationers who have arrived in their own car, there is a free parking zone, which is guarded around the clock, so the guest may not worry about his vehicle. Throughout the institution provided free internet access. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a TV, a work desk and a minibar. And in some rooms there are balconies on which there is furniture, here you can very comfortably stay and drink delicious tea.

Hotel Lappeenranta SPA is considered a historical building, since it was erected in 1912. Already at the reception you can sign up for certain spa treatments, which will certainly give the guest great pleasure. Guests can rent bicycles and other necessary sports equipment on site. It is possible to order tickets to the theater and other entertainment venues. There are several restaurants on site that serve breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as some specific exotic dishes. Not far from the hotel is the train station, so it will be absolutely easy to get there.
