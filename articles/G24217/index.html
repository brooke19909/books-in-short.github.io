How to sew a gypsy skirt: pattern, fabric selection, sewing order

Fashion, as you know, is cyclical, so modern designers often use the achievements of the past. And folk motifs in clothes today are more relevant than ever. Take at least such a popular wardrobe item as a fluffy skirt with ruffles. This idea is often embodied in the collections of the best fashion houses in the world, at least we see skirts in the floor in casual style.





It is noteworthy that most models of gypsy skirts are sewn on one of two basic patterns. The first is a sun skirt, and the second is tiers sewn together. Both patterns are easy to build, and tailoring will require only basic skills and accuracy. By and large, even a high school student, who listened carefully to a teacher of home economics, will cope with the task. And our article will help you figure out how to sew a gypsy skirt in a casual and carnival style, how to decorate it and what to wear with.

Features

In the silhouette of fluffy skirts, the influence of the hippie style is easily guessed. And the children of flowers, in turn, borrowed the idea (and not only this) from the gypsies. The national costume of these people amazes with a riot of colors and a wealth of decor. With the skillful selection of details, many gypsy motifs organically look today.





By learning how to build simple patterns, you can easily experiment with details to create new images. If you want to replenish your wardrobe with this elegant little thing, you only need a piece of fabric of the right size, slanting inlay, thread and elastic. And for work, the most ordinary sewing machine will be enough.

Gypsy dance costume

It is perhaps hard to imagine a more singing and dancing people. It is not surprising that national gypsy dances are so popular today, and in all corners of the earth. If you are studying or just planning to start doing this beautiful art, then you just need a gypsy skirt.





With your own hands you can sew it from the most catchy and elegant fabric. Still, the scene obliges. This is the case when you can not worry about moderation at all - the skirt simply must be smart, sparkling, ringing and rich. But still, there are some rules here. The main one is the compatibility of colors. When choosing a fabric, give preference to saturated basic shades: red, orange, purple, green, blue. Often in gypsy outfits there is also black. But there is very little white. Turquoise, coral, dusty plum and other complex newfangled shades - this is not about gypsies. There are better options for a traditional costume.

Suitable for skirts and fabric with a pattern. Large flowers will be the best option: roses, peonies, chrysanthemums. The larger the better. A small flower on a black background is also a great idea, like polka dot fabric. The strip and geometric pattern for traditional dresses are not typical, as are abstractions.

Choice of fabric for everyday style

If you plan to sew an elegant thing for everyday wear, it is not at all necessary to try to repeat the national gypsy costume.

This thing can be decorated in the style of hippies or boho, then preference should be given to natural fabrics of natural tones, printed material (for example, "paisley"), blue denim. A pure white cambric or linen skirt trimmed with lace will simply look luxurious.

A plain full-length skirt can be the main highlight of a laid-back urban style.

Tiered Gypsy Skirt

How to sew a gypsy skirt from several tiers is shown in the following diagram.





You need to measure your hips and do some simple calculations. Do not forget about allowances for seams, and in addition to them, leave a margin for the belt on the upper tier.

The height of the tiers can be any. You can simply divide the product length by 3 to get the same cuts. But you can make them different, for example, the upper is the largest, the lower ones are smaller. If you plan on more than three tiers, make each successive approximately one third wider than the previous one.

Getting started is easier from the bottom. Pick up the edge of the strip, form folds. Sew it to the flat edge of the smaller strip. Sew and sweep the edge.

Do the same until you reach the top. The easiest way to draw a skirt into the waist of such a skirt. To do this, bend the edge, stitch, leaving a small hole. Insert an elastic band into it whose length is equal to your OT.
