Which banks do not refuse credit - review, features, documents and reviews

Lending is one of the ways to quickly solve financial problems. To get a loan: it is enough to be over 18 years old, have a passport of the Russian Federation, registration in one of the regions, a good credit history and a stable income. But not always the borrower can boast of having all the parameters. And for those who previously allowed delinquencies in loans, 5 out of 6 banks are denied loans. In order not to waste time filling out a questionnaire where it is impossible to obtain a loan, you need to know which banks do not refuse credit.

What do I need to get a loan?

It is not difficult to obtain a loan, even if the borrower has previously made delinquencies under the contracts and has outstanding obligations. To do this:

  • choose a financial institution that guarantees a loan with a bad credit history;
  • provide a complete set of documents required by the lender;
  • choose a convenient type of loan (if there are several);
  • apply online; it is much faster and more profitable than a loan at the bank office;
  • wait for the decision of the bank.

But before applying, you need to compare banks that give loans with bad credit history. Not all companies are willing to provide a good loan to non-payers. In order not to overpay interest on a loan, it is better to spend time searching for a minimum rate and favorable conditions for maintaining a loan agreement.

List of creditors for bad credit payers

Of course, not a single bank will issue loans to all customers without failure. This is unprofitable and entails financial risks. But there are companies whose approval percentage for applications in which does not fall below 89%.

Which banks do not refuse a loan to almost anyone:

  1. Tinkoff
  2. "Setelem."
  3. "OTP Bank".
  4. "Renaissance".

Some of them, namely, Setelem and OTP Bank, specialize in drawing up loan agreements by installments. Sales points of such lenders are located in large shopping centers. They collaborate with furniture stores and networks of technical goods companies.

Others - Tinkoff and Renaissance - provide a wide range of credit services not related to the purchase of expensive goods. Clients can apply for a regular loan as well as a credit card with a bank limit.

Tinkoff Bank loan: features

One of the leaders in the Russian sector of online loans is Tinkoff. The Bank works exclusively through the Internet, therefore, obtaining a loan, from filing an application to withdrawing cash at the terminal, is carried out on the company's website.

This is one of the banks that give a loan with bad credit history and without it. A loan is issued on a plastic card of a client. The product delivers the courier to the home or work of the borrower.

A quick loan decision, understandable tariffs and an operational support service are the advantages that millions of Tinkoff customers in Russia have already appreciated.

Loan options at Tinkoff Bank

Bank lending conditions are as follows:

  1. Cash loan without security. Issued in the amount of up to 2 million rubles. The rate is from 12% per year for up to 3 years. An income statement is optional. No guarantee is required.
  2. A loan secured by a car. You can get up to 3 million rubles. Interest - from 11% per annum. Loan term - up to 5 years. The car remains with the owner for the entire duration of the contract.
  3. A loan secured by real estate. A loan on which you can get up to 15 million rubles. It is issued for a period of up to 15 years at a rate of 9.9% per annum. The apartment at the time of payment remains with the owner.
  4. Credit card with grace period. Tinkoff Platinum allows you to use the bank without interest for a period of 55 to 365 days. Interest for violation of the grace period - from 12% per annum. Issued with a limit of up to 300 thousand rubles.

To obtain a loan or credit card, the borrower must fill out a questionnaire on the bank's website. In the first case, he will meet with a specialist who will communicate the decision on the application and issue a debit card with cash if the loan is approved. In the second case, the courier will deliver the credit card to a place and time convenient for the client.

Tinkoff does not require documents to confirm income. You can apply for a loan and an installment card only by passport.

You can apply online. Consideration takes place during the day. If the decision is positive, the client must contact the selected bank branch for a loan.

Why can they refuse to issue a loan?

Having learned which banks do not refuse credit, borrowers are still afraid to hear that they did not approve the loan. None of the credit organizations will give a 100% guarantee that the loan will be approved, since the decision on each client is made individually.

To increase the chances of issuing, you need to know when the bank refuses the loan:

  1. Insufficient solvency.
  2. Very bad credit history.
  3. Existence of existing delays.
  4. Arrest on the accounts.
  5. Refusal to provide documents.

If there are at least 3 reasons, there is practically no chance of getting a loan at the same time. But to find out exactly why the bank refuses the loan, the borrower can from the lender. In addition, the client can request a credit history to check their reliability as a payer.
