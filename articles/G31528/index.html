Credit Cards with Instant Solution - Design Features, Conditions and Reviews

The simple receipt of funds no longer suits most customers of banking institutions. This is not surprising, because sometimes it is necessary to purchase a product or thing for the home very quickly (within a few days, and sometimes hours). In this case, it makes no sense to contact the bank for cash, as usually applications for such loans are considered on the order of a week, or even more.





But what to do in this situation? Issue a credit card with an instant decision of the bank, which will be issued directly immediately in its branch. Thanks to this, a huge amount of time can be saved, because in this case applications are considered from 15 minutes to 1 hour. After that, the client receives a standard registered QC, which is made directly at the bank branch. However, it is worth considering that there are certain conditions that a potential borrower must meet.

Features of obtaining a credit card with a passport with an instant decision

To get the coveted plastic, a person must be at least 21 years old at the time of application. In this case, most often it is required to additionally provide a certificate in the form of 2 personal income tax and confirm your work experience, which is at least six months at the last job.

However, some credit institutions have special programs that allow the issuance of a credit card with an instant decision without inquiries. This usually applies to those who are already a client of the bank. New customers are required to provide a full package of documents. For example, you can apply for a Sberbank credit card with an instant decision if a person already has an account with this financial institution.

There are also a number of other banks where you can get plastic without additional documents: Otkrytie, Home Credit, Renaissance Credit and many others. In some organizations, it is additionally required to confirm their solvency, but in certain situations it is possible to provide documents for a car or apartment, thanks to which a bank employee will be sure that in case of non-payment of a loan a person will be able to recover funds in court.





However, it should be borne in mind that when you make a QC, you should not count on a reduced credit rate. As a rule, in any bank, there is a certain grace period during which you can pay the debt without any commission. As practice shows, few people manage to cope with this. After the end of this interest-free period, the interest rate on the loan increases markedly, and in this case it is much more profitable to take a consumer target loan than to draw up a card. Therefore, it all depends on the specific situation.

Credit Card with Instant Bank Solution: Benefits

Products of this type have several advantages. First of all, this is a grace period, which was mentioned earlier. In different banks, it ranges from 30 to 100 days. During this time, the client can return the previously taken funds to the account without any problems, without paying any interest at all.

Keep in mind that money on the card will always be more secure than if the borrower receives it in cash. Among other things, thanks to QC, it is much more convenient to receive funds while abroad. In this case, the optimal exchange rate is selected, so that a person loses much less money.
