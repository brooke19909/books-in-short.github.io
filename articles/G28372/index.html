The recreation center "Cape of Good Hope": location





This holiday complex is located in one of the most picturesque corners of Poltava, very close to the river. The nearest village is the village of Petrovka. In fact, these are beautiful Gogol places, which, thanks to the stories of the famous writer, have become a real legend. Living conditions at the base: room description





The recreation center "Cape of Good Hope" (Petrovka) offers its guests check-in in two types of cottages - this is standard and luxury. There are double and quadruple standard rooms. These are spacious rooms with the necessary set of furniture, including comfortable single beds, a wardrobe and chairs.

As for the deluxe rooms, that is, cottages with two or three separate rooms. In addition, the cottage has a spacious glazed veranda and fireplace, which will make the evenings much more comfortable. Each such house is designed for 4-6 people.

It is worth noting that each room is equipped with the necessary set of household appliances. For example, there is a refrigerator, TV, safe, and a set of tableware. The luxury cottages also have a small kitchen, which is perfect for tourists who prefer to cook on their own. Each room has free Wi-Fi access.

There is also a toilet and a bathroom with shower, and a set of clean towels. By the way, the cost of living on the territory of the recreation center includes free entrance to the beach and breakfast in a local cafe.

Food scheme for vacationers

Near the cottages there is a large cafe, which is open from 8 am to 11 pm. It is located in a cozy meadow and is surrounded on all sides by a pine forest. In the morning there are tables for breakfast for holidaymakers. The rest of the time the cafe works on the menu. Here customers are offered dishes of Ukrainian and European cuisines. By the way, the institution is quite roomy and designed for 80 seats. This place is quite popular in the summer, as banquets and festivities are often organized here.

On the beach there is a well-equipped bar with a capacity of 120 seats. Here I offer guests drinks (both alcoholic and non-alcoholic), as well as light snacks, stockfish and grilled dishes.

Beach holidays and water activities

As already mentioned, the Cape of Good Hope base (Poltava, Petrovka) was built on the very bank of the river. And here they equipped a beautiful beach for relaxation and swimming. Convenient wooden sun loungers and umbrellas are located on the shore, but you need to pay for their use separately. After payment, you will be given a comfortable soft mattress. A beautiful waterslide has been built here, which will appeal to both children and their parents.





Near the beach there is also a grill area. There are 11 comfortable wooden arbors with tables and benches. Each gazebo can accommodate up to eight people. The central section is reserved for barbecues and washstands - guests who rented a tent can use them. By the way, firewood can also be purchased at the box office, which is very convenient.

Sports and entertainment at the base

Base "Cape of good hope" was created primarily for the convenience and comfort of vacationers. And, of course, on its territory there is everything you need for an unforgettable vacation. For example, there is parking near the beach - in most cases, vacationers arrive here by their own transport.

On the territory of the base there is also a large playground with a fence for greater security, there are slides, swings, and labyrinths. In a word, children will definitely like it. There is also a playground for adults - most often vacationers arrange volleyball or football tournaments here. By the way, the ball can be rented here at the box office. There is even table tennis.





The base also offers its guests the opportunity to rent a mountain bike - it regularly organizes walks in the surroundings and excursions for cyclists to interesting places. The leisure complex also has its own cable car, where fans of this sport can have fun and beginners can try themselves.

Since the river is very close, it is no wonder that the recreation center offers a lot of water activities. For example, here you can rent a boat or kayak to swim along the river, getting a lot of pleasure. It is also allowed to fish on the river - the payment for this is symbolic.

And, of course, do not forget about excursions to Dikanka and other interesting places, of which there are many.
