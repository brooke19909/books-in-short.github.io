The best mini-hotels of Sochi and St. Petersburg

Tourism business in the modern world is developing rapidly. Naturally, the demand for hotels is growing along with it. Large hotels do not increase the number of rooms fast enough and are not able to satisfy the increasing demand for hotels. And here mini-hotels come to the rescue, which have become quite popular recently. What it is? A small hotel, as a rule, is private, with a limited number of rooms, while providing the same services as a regular hotel. Comfort and convenience for little money - that's what attracts tourists here. We bring to your attention mini-hotels of St. Petersburg.

Belvedere Nevsky

Belvedere Nevsky has the status of a business hotel and is located in the center of St. Petersburg, in a building of incredible beauty, which was built in 1868-1869 by professor of architecture D.I. Grim and was the first five-story building in the Northern capital.

34 rooms of a mini-hotel of categories of comfort and standard, in which there is everything you need for a comfortable stay: air conditioning, TV, internet. There is a luggage room where you can leave bulky items that restrict freedom in the room.

For business people who have chosen Belvedere Nevsky to stay, the presence of a conference room in the hotel will be a big plus. Tourists who wish to appreciate the splendor of the famous city and see the sights will have a great opportunity to do this in the form of a walk. Elite clothing stores and the DLT shopping center located nearby will inspire and delight shopping lovers. If necessary, you can use the nearest metro stations - Nevsky Prospekt or Admiralteyskaya. "Nevsky coast 122"

Nevsky Bereg 122 is an elegant hotel in the best European traditions, located in the historical center of the city on Nevsky Prospekt. A wonderful mini-hotel in St. Petersburg, if you are interested in sights and entertainment at the same time. In walking distance from the hotel are the Moscow railway station and the metro station "Ploschad Vosstaniya", a little further - Summer Garden and the Hermitage. Well, and, of course, bars, restaurants, shopping centers - for every taste.

Guests "Nevsky Bereg 122" offers 38 rooms - from standards to apartments. They are all decorated in bright colors and designed in a classic style, each equipped with a bathroom (some with a jacuzzi), and such pleasant things as a refrigerator, TV, hairdryer, free Wi-Fi. There is no elevator, so baggage service is free.

A buffet breakfast is served every morning. In warmer months, the Sky Portal summer terrace is open for guests, equipped with a barbecue, sunbeds and swings. A great place to hold various events.

Chekhov

In the historical center of the city, amid the splendor of ancient buildings, one of the best mini-hotels of St. Petersburg is located - Chekhov. 8 rooms - single, double and triple - impress with their attractive price, comfortable atmosphere and windows on a quiet street. And the highlight of the hotel is an unusual lounge with a fireplace, which creates the comfort and atmosphere of an endless celebration for guests. A continental breakfast awaits you every morning.

The convenient location of the mini-hotel will allow you to enjoy the historical and cultural heritage of the Northern capital during a leisurely walk. And numerous restaurants and cafes will surprise any gourmet.

"Atrium'

In autumn 2008, one of the best mini-hotels of St. Petersburg Atrium was opened on Nevsky Prospekt. And since then it has endlessly delighted the guests of the city with an excellent level of service and magnificent rooms. The hotel surrounds the Alexander Nevsky Lavra Hotel, the Moscow Conference Complex and, of course, many cafes, bars and restaurants. Such a neighborhood is guaranteed to provide you with a cultural and entertainment program. 35 rooms of various categories and 6 apartments decorated in modern style will not leave indifferent even the most demanding guest. The sophistication of the interior will create an atmosphere of romance, and the most modern security systems will provide security. Where to stay in Sochi

Sochi is a leader among resorts on the coast of Russia. And if you are going to spend a vacation in this glorious city, you need to be puzzled not only by purchasing tickets in advance, but also choosing a place to stay. There are a lot of offers, but more and more tourists choose mini-hotels. The advantages are obvious: this is the price-quality ratio, and a small number of rooms, which guarantees relative peace and quiet. The priority factors when choosing a mini-hotel are: minimum distance from the sea and quality service at a low price. Sochi mini-hotels that meet these requirements will be presented below.

The guest house, consisting of only 4 rooms of different levels of comfort, is a business hotel in the best European traditions. He already managed to earn the approval of his clients, who appreciated spacious rooms, a modern interior, and company-specific features of the service. The hotel has all the attributes of a quality holiday: a comfortable sleep system, TVs, free Wi-Fi. The place for cooking is common, but so well equipped that it guarantees a lot of pleasant emotions during the culinary experiments. And if you do not consider the option of cooking on your own, you will be happy to feed cafes and restaurants that are adjacent to the hotel. The location of the hotel in the city center of Sochi will provide guests with a combination of cultural, entertainment and business life. Nearby are the Riviera Park, the Aquarium, the Green Theater, 1 km to the beach and the train station, 30 km to the airport.
