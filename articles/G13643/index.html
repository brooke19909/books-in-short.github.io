Beach facilities





Many tourists come to the country, wanting to soak up the gentle sun on the seashore. What does the hotel complex Movenpick Tala Bay Aqaba 5 * offer its guests? Jordan, Aqaba in particular, is famous for its beautiful beaches. And the hotel owns its own, private stretch of coast.

The beach is just 50 meters away. Only guests rest here, so you should not worry about the influx of tourists or the presence of strangers. You can freely use wide comfortable sun loungers and relax in the shade of umbrellas.

According to tourists, the coast here is covered with fine, really snow-white sand. The entrance to the sea is cleared, but since a coral reef is located nearby , guests are still recommended to use special shoes. There is a long wooden pontoon from which you can also go into the water.

Fun and relaxation on the water

Swim in the warm sea, relax on a comfortable mattress, enjoying the beautiful scenery - this is what many travelers count on. But what should tourists do who prefer a more active pastime?

On the shore there is a sports recreation center. Here for a small amount you can rent a boat, canoe or jet ski. You can get a lot of positive impressions by skiing, bananas, and a speedboat. Tourists also love parasailing, windsurfing and sailing - on the shore you can easily pick up and rent the necessary equipment.

Nearby there is a coral reef, but, alas, not too colorful (this is evidenced by the reviews of guests). Nevertheless, diving is one of the favorite activities of travelers. On the shore, you can take training courses and get an excursion to more beautiful reefs.
