5 star Mexico hotels, all inclusive: review, rating. Best resorts in Mexico

Mexico receives tourists from all over the world almost all year round. This is due to the fact that the country is located in the southern latitudes, therefore it is always warm here. Whether it is the capital of Mexico City, or Cabo San Lucas - tourists can optionally find a place for themselves not only for living and entertainment. Thanks to multimillion investments, all cities and islands located along the coastline have entire spa complexes and dozens of establishments of various classes.

Mexico hotels "5 stars" ("all inclusive") are in great demand among holidaymakers. Such a program allows, having paid for the full stay, to use all the entertainment located on the territory of the complex, absolutely free. This also includes meals and excursions.

The article will present a 5-star rating of Mexico hotels, which are in demand among tourists from all over the world and get the highest ratings.

Secrets Maroma Beach Riviera Cancun

Opens Mexico 5-star (all-inclusive) hotel rating of Secrets Maroma Beach Riviera Cancun 412-room villa. This romantic resort complex is mainly intended for adults, located on a private white sand beach with sun loungers, umbrellas and bars. Due to this location, people come here to enjoy a great view from the coast and get a bronze tan.

In addition to the buffet, there is a wide selection of high-quality restaurants, including Japanese teppanyaki, French-style establishments, a wine cellar with more than 200 different drinks and romantic dinners for two on the beach for an extra fee. Lovers are served various seafood delicacies.

The stunning wellness spa offers couples treatments in private outdoor lodges on the beach. The rooms are large and tastefully furnished with separate seating areas and balconies. The rooms on the ground floor are located next to the water. The hotel is especially popular for weddings, although numerous additional fees can make your stay here expensive.

Le blanc spa resort

The luxurious 260-seat Le Blanc leisure center is the best (and most expensive), where, after paying for your stay, the rest of the entertainment is at the expense of the establishment. It features a magnificent white sand beach in the center of the hotel zone, a spa (guests get free access to the hydrotherapy zone), stunning pools (two with bars).

All rooms have excellent service. Every day cleaning and replacement of bath accessories with new ones is carried out. The design of the hotel is mostly white. And the modern, bright rooms have spa baths for two. Most of the rooms have ocean or lagoon views. The chic restaurants serve excellent food and desserts. Fitness rooms will also impress fans of training, and there are several daily activities, including yoga and Pilates. Getting to the place where the hotel is located is better from Mexico City. A taxi ride will take no more than half an hour.

Playa mujeres

Mexico City also has chic, tropical-style apartments. The place inside is like a wild island. This luxurious adult resort offers a range of services with a large number of restaurants, beautiful pools, spas and other wonderful entertainment.

The rooms are large and modern, with spacious bathrooms, a jacuzzi and upscale amenities such as large flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and iPod docking stations. Some have a large rooftop terrace with a private plunge pool and ocean views. However, note that the beach is less attractive than others located in the south and that there are no attractions nearby. It is better to get to the hotel by flight Moscow - Mexico. Traveling takes no more than 9 hours.

ElDoradoCasitasRoyale, Karisma

El Dorado Casitas Royale, a closed, upscale division of El Dorado Royale, is an exclusive hotel for wealthy travelers, halfway between Cancun and Playa del Carmen. While the amenities on both sides of the resort are the same, El Dorado Casitas Royale is quieter and more secluded than the rest of the huge complex.

The rooms are large and luxurious, with wood-beamed ceilings, huge bathrooms with open-air showers and two-person spa baths. Some apartments have private terraces or balconies with spacious pools.

Most guests prefer to spend their days in one of the hotel‚Äôs several pools (some have a bar), because the beach is small and rocky, and it is not suitable for swimming. The hotel's restaurants serve a variety of dishes, but there are practically no gourmet dishes.

BlueBay Grand Esmeralda

In the middle of Mexico's 5-star (all-inclusive) hotel rating is BlueBay Grand Esmeralda. This sophisticated and fashionable boutique hotel has 109 rooms.

The entire building is a luxurious complex for wealthy tourists, which includes all the entertainment in the price, located in the Playa Mujeres region, north of Cancun. It is located on a beautiful long sandy beach and has three chic outdoor service pools and a bar.

The rooms are huge (starting at 290 square meters), completely white and have spacious bathtubs with hydromassage, as well as private balconies - many with ocean views, and some with small pools. The 10,000-square-meter spa is magnificent with an impressive hydrotherapy massage and fitness center.

The food in the four restaurants is excellent and you do not need to reserve tables. After checking in, all places are assigned to guests. 24-hour room service is also available. In Mexico, the 5-star BlueBay Grand Esmeralda hotel is in demand not only among tourists, but also among wealthy locals.

Secrets The Vine Cancun

Secrets The Vine Cancun is a luxurious all-inclusive 500-star hotel. Many apartments have stunning sea views. All rooms have exquisite interiors, such as Italian stone floors and beautiful teak furniture.

Guests can take advantage of a wide range of activities, from yoga to wine tasting, as well as feasting in one of the 6 restaurants without booking a table. You will be offered a world-wide list of dishes, including Italian, Mexican, Argentinean and Asian dishes.

Another way to relax is the on-site spa, which has 14 treatment rooms, a sauna and a steam bath, as well as four beautiful outdoor pools to choose from. The beachfront location is ideal for tourists looking to relax. If desired, you can visit local attractions, which are located within walking distance from the hotel.

Hyatt Zilara Cancun

The best resorts in Mexico include Hyatt Zilara Cancun. This 307-room estate-style resort is located on a pristine beach in the hotel area. It is one of the best luxury options for adults and children. The stylish colonial-style rooms feature balconies with stunning ocean views, which can also be enjoyed from the hot tubs.

Six restaurants serve excellent food, especially for an all-inclusive resort. It is worth noting that all dishes are prepared exclusively from environmentally friendly products that come from local farms and shops. The long and wide pool is equipped with a giant swimming bar in the center. A spa and a huge gym (complete with an outdoor pool for relaxation) allow you to usefully spend time.

It also impresses with many interesting events, from weekly parties and dances to tequila tastings and live music by local artists. Children here are not left aside. Professional animators work with them, rooms with games are equipped. Not far from the center is a large water park.

The royal playa del carmen

This luxurious all-inclusive 507-room hotel has become the favorite destination of many couples. A particular attraction of this resort is its proximity to nightlife in the center of Playa del Carmen, especially the lively Fifth Avenue, which is very popular among young people.

The magnificent beach and exquisite rooms with hot tubs for two people allow you to spend time away from noise, having a romantic date. The range of possibilities is extensive. The hotel has seven restaurants and five bars, an Olympic-sized pool, a wedding center and a spa. The young couple, if desired, can arrange with the administration to conduct a symbolic wedding ceremony according to local customs.

Hotel mousai

The most exclusive hotel in the Garza Blanca Nature Reserve, Mousai is a luxurious hotel complex with 74 modern suites and panoramic views of the coast, located near Cabo San Lucas. The interior design of the rooms is a bit angular, and the design style resembles a chessboard. The hall is made entirely of stone. Its entrance is decorated with columns. The whole territory is surrounded by dense thickets of local shrubs.

The all-inclusive system gives the hotel guests the full right to attend any institutions and events on its territory. A restaurant and two bars are located inside, but the entire Garza Blanca project is located along the coastline, including the beach (10 minutes walk down the hill). A large outdoor rooftop pool, magnificent views from the rooms and a fully equipped gym make this place even more attractive for relaxation.

Secrets Silversands Riviera Cancun

Rounds out Mexico's 5-star (all-inclusive) hotel rating for Secrets Silversands Riviera Cancun. The hotel is a 40-minute drive north of the city. The resort is smaller and quieter than many nearby establishments, but it has many amenities. Visitors are greeted by friendly and responsible staff. Each room offers a set of ready-made bedding and cosmetic accessories. The administrator can be asked to arrange a place for small children in the room.

The hotel has six restaurants, numerous bars, swimming pools, a spa with beach massage services and a modern fitness center. A wide selection of rooms are available, including suites with terraces with pool views. Nevertheless, the beach consists mainly of stone. So all the rest will have to be spent by the pool.
