Do-it-yourself children's costume Thor

The New Year is approaching, a series of matinees, Christmas trees and carnivals. If your son is a big fan of the movie "Thor" or Scandinavian mythology, in general, then this outfit will definitely appeal to him. In our article we will tell you how to make a costume of Thor with your own hands.

Hammer making

The main problem with most materials suitable for making a hammer is their weight. The child will probably get tired of carrying a massive thing, for example, of wood. There will be no such problems with our hammer, because it is practically weightless.

To make the main attribute of the Thor costume, we need:

  • Floral sponge.
  • Cut plastic pipe.
  • Newspapers.
  • Glue.
  • Tape for wrapping the handle.
  • Silver paint in a spray can.
  • Masking tape.

Step-by-step instruction:

  • We cut the floral sponge from the sides, giving it the shape of a hammer.
  • Since the sponge itself is very soft, wrinkles and crumbles easily, the structure must be strengthened. In the papier-m√¢ch√© technique, we glue the sponge into pieces of newspapers in several layers and leave it to dry completely.
  • We make a hole, insert a piece of pipe into it. We fix using masking tape.
  • From a thick cardboard we cut out a circle and glue it from the side opposite to the handle.
  • Paint the hammer with silver paint.
  • Wrap the handle with tape. At the end, for convenience, we make a loop.
  • Our super lightweight but formidable-looking hammer is ready.

We are preparing a Torah costume for children, but the hammer will look quite adult.

Thor's Helmet

The next important element is the helmet.

To make it we need:

  • Cardboard.
  • Glue.
  • Metallic color paint

The process of making a helmet for Thor's costume:

  • For a helmet, cardboard from packing cereals or any other box is suitable. Cut 7 elements in shape, as in the photo:
  • Fold the elements in a circle so that the edges of one figure slightly overlap another. We glue.

Another piece of Thor's costume is ready.

It remains to make a bib. With it, your child will definitely be successful at any matinee.
