Choosing a good gamepad for PC

Many have probably noticed that many game projects come out with an orientation to support the gamepad, under which control is configured. This is facilitated by several reasons: the popularization of game consoles, the quality and convenience of the device. But not all controllers of this type are comfortable and efficient. The purpose of this article is to highlight the most acceptable instances and, as a result, to designate the best gamepad for the PC.



Xbox 360 Controller

Perhaps one of the most successful joystick options in recent decades. The Microsoft PC Xbox 360 is comfortable and ideal in all situations. Smooth and pleasant contours of the device provide a complete fusion of the hand and joystick, lightness and simplicity of pressing minimize stress on the muscles of the hand. Drivers for this gamepad must be downloaded via the Internet on the official Microsoft website. It is very easy to get used to this joystick and it is hard to tear yourself away from it. 10 functionally assigned keys, progressive triggers and convenient button layout provide maximum convenience. In addition to the usual version (wired), there is a wireless model on the market, which is also perfect for playing on a personal computer. But in the first case, the length of the cord allows you to sit almost anywhere in the room. Wireless gamepads for PCs weigh slightly more than standard versions, but you may not even notice it, especially when climax gaming moments occur on the screen. Despite the rather high price (compared to its competitors), varying within 30 euros, this joystick is the best of its kind in almost all respects. Also a decent model for playing on a PC. Externally, the joystick very much resembles the first version of the controller for the Xbox, but its surface is much softer and more comfortable. Logitech Gamepad F710 Controller

This model features a comfortable soft plastic, made in black and gray colors. Ergonomic design certainly guarantees a strong and comfortable grip. It is worth noting that the joystick is wireless, which is one of the best of its kind. In addition, the model has a feedback vibration communication system. The batteries last for a fairly long time. This PC gamepad is one of the most successful options for any gamer who prefers a particular game genre.



Thus, 3 most suitable controller models were selected that successfully combined with games on a personal computer. Among them, a clear winner can be distinguished - a gamepad for PC Microsoft Xbox 360, which is rightfully considered one of the best. But this in no way impairs the quality and convenience of other models that can provide the user with maximum comfort from the process.
