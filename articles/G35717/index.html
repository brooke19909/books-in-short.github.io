Sights of Tchaikovsky, Perm Territory

When planning a tourist trip to the province, many tourists first choose the most ancient settlements. Indeed, the older the city, the richer its history and more historical architectural monuments can be seen in one trip. Nevertheless, there are exceptions to this rule. Take some free time to explore the sights of Tchaikovsky in the Perm Territory.

It is a very young city, founded in 1955. Today Tchaikovsky is a recognized cultural center and is considered one of the most interesting tourist cities in its region.

Port city and cultural center

Tchaikovsky was founded as a working village on the banks of the Kama River and the Votkinsk reservoir. It is easy to guess that the place was named after the brilliant Russian composer Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky. The village quickly grew to a small cozy town. Today, the unhurried atmosphere of the province is preserved here. The town pleases with a large number of landscaped squares and green spaces. Surrounded by water on both sides in the warmer months, it resembles a southern resort.

Sightseeing Tchaikovsky can start from the local promenade. The city has its own river station. During the season, you can take a boat trip or relax on the beach. Thanks to its famous name, Tchaikovsky since 1977 began to develop as a cultural center. It was then that the first music festival for children and adolescents was held here. The event is held annually, young talents from Russia and foreign countries take part in it. Today in Tchaikovsky there are several children's art schools and a music school, to which students from all over the country come to study.

Museum of Local Lore

What could be interesting in a museum dedicated to the history of a city that did not have time to celebrate its first centenary? A lot of things - visit the Tchaikovsky Museum of Local Lore and see for yourself. It is located on Mira Street, d. 19. The museum was founded in 1963. In it you can find out the history of the construction of the city and the main enterprise - Votkinsk hydroelectric station. A separate exposition is dedicated to peasant life in the 19th century. One of the significant events in the history of Tchaikovsky is the landing near the city of the Vostok spaceship, on board of which the dog Zvezdochka made a flight. You can learn more about this achievement of Soviet cosmonautics at the local museum of local lore. In addition to permanent exhibitions, various temporary exhibitions are regularly held here.

Picture gallery

A bright and interesting attraction of Tchaikovsky is the art gallery. Usually, wishing to see the masterpieces of world painting, we go to large cities. Meanwhile, a decent collection of paintings and sculptures has been collected in the Tchaikovsky art gallery. This unique museum begins its history in 1970. It was then that the Moscow collector A.S. Zhigalo donated his personal collection to the city. In 1993, the Tchaikovsky Gallery was presented by recognized artist I.P. Ruban. The collection continues to grow. Sometimes this museum is jokingly called the "gallery of gifts." You can admire the works of art at the following address: st. Mira, d. 21.

Ethnographic complex "Saigatka"

Once on the site of modern Tchaikovsky was the village of Saigatka, the first mention of which dates back to 1646. From the original village buildings, the central street and several lanes, succinctly inscribed in the city‚Äôs development plan, have been preserved. Tourists wishing to see all the sights of Tchaikovsky should definitely follow the Saigatka architectural and ethnographic complex. The tourist site occupies an original house of the XVIII century, built of wood without a single nail. The interior of the dwelling of an Old Believer peasant typical of the late 18th - early 19th centuries was reconstructed inside. The museum provides guided tours and interactive theatrical programs for guests of all ages. Ethnographic complex address: st. Gagarina, d. 98.

Temples of Tchaikovsky

Tchaikovsky is a young city; there are no ancient Russian cathedrals in it. The main city Orthodox church is located on Kosmonavtov highway, 1. The Church of St. George the Victorious was built in 2003. This young temple looks impressive and is one of the most beautiful buildings in the city. Other religious attractions of Tchaikovsky: Trinity Church, Assumption Church and the Church of St. Nicholas. All these Orthodox churches are operational. They are not remarkable for their outstanding architecture, but you can visit the service. A unique attraction of Tchaikovsky is the existing mosque. This temple is also operational today, it can only be viewed from the outside for tourist purposes today.

Other sights of Tchaikovsky

A great attraction for tourists is a walk along the streets of Tchaikovsky. There are several interesting monuments in the city. The most significant of them is a monument dedicated to the first builders and builders of the city. The sculptural composition has many small fragments that tell about various stages in the history of the village. In Tchaikovsky, a monument to the dog-astronaut Zvezdochka was erected. The monument was inaugurated in 2011 for the fiftieth anniversary of the Russian cosmonautics. On Peace Street you can see a monument to A.S. Pushkin. It was solemnly opened after the transfer of the sculpture of the composer Pyotr Ilyich to Lenin Street. Choose to visit the sights of Tchaikovsky to your personal taste. Active recreation and health tourism

Tchaikovsky surprises his guests with fresh air and a favorable ecological environment. In recent years, a passion for a healthy lifestyle has become increasingly popular among local residents. In the vicinity of the city there are several health resorts. A biathlon sports complex has recently been built outside the city. The real pride of the region is the Snezhinka ski base. These are so different Tchaikovsky sights. Where to go on an excursion and a walk in the evening, every tourist will find for himself here. Come for a walk and see for yourself! There are hotels in the city and housing for rent. If desired, tourists can stay here for a few days and have time to visit all the attractions without haste.
