Rest in Batumi. Hotels on the Black Sea

If you want to relax on the Black Sea, looking for affordable accommodation options, pay attention to Batumi. The hotels here are different - there are 3-5 star ones. You can stay in a mini-hotel and save even more.

From 740 rubles





That is how much currently costs per night in cheap hotels in Batumi. They are located close to the sea, they have everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Hotel Verona is a 5-7 minute walk from the Black Sea coast. The building has 5 rooms that are decorated in chocolate and white colors. Each is equipped with air conditioning, a fridge, a modern cable TV, and a bathroom with a shower and toiletries.

Cafes, bars, the nearest bus stop are a five-minute walk away. It offers views of Batumi. Hotels like Verona offer guests free parking, WiFi.

Lavro also has free parking for residents and Internet. This hotel has 12 rooms, each with a double bed. From the bus station you can reach the Lavro Hotel in just 6 minutes. The path to the sea will take a little longer, it is 350 meters away.

All Inclusive Batumi Hotels - Royal Hotel

In such comfortable hotels, buffet breakfast is included in the price. This also applies to the Royal Hotel. From it you will reach the beach in just 5 minutes, the center of Batumi is four km away. In a beautiful pink and white building - 30 rooms. They are bright and decorated in a classic style. The hotel has several types of rooms - with a large double or two separate one and a half beds; triple and single room, "honeymoon suite". Each has air conditioning, balcony, TV, fridge, bathroom.

The hotel restaurant serves Turkish and Georgian cuisine, there is an assortment of buffet dishes, a bar. The staff offers a free airport shuttle service and a fee. At the request of vacationers, they will be delivered food directly to their room.

Radisson blu batumi





There are other hotels in the city of Batumi where they provide not only the all-inclusive service, but also many others. So, the luxurious Radisson Blu Batumi hotel has indoor and outdoor pools, a rooftop restaurant, and free Wi-Fi. The sea is a 5-minute walk, it can be admired directly from the balconies, from the windows of the hotel.

Radisson Blu Batumi has steam baths, a sauna, a gym, and massage treatments are available.

There are standard, superior rooms, junior suites. In total - 168. In the mornings, the hotel restaurant serves a buffet, you can have a delicious and nutritious breakfast.

"Oasis"





If you want to have a great rest in Batumi, the Oasis Hotel will be happy to welcome you. It is located 13 km from Batumi, in the beautiful village of Chakvi, in a quiet location on the Black Sea. The hotel has its own 300-meter beach.

The hotel itself extends over 4 hectares. There was a place for outdoor and indoor pools, saunas, a fitness center. There is also a billiard room, a playground, outdoor tennis courts, a football field, a basketball court, beach volleyball, table tennis, and a badminton court. For you - spa, gym, jacuzzi, free parking, solarium. You can eat delicious food in any of the two restaurants of the hotel, have a snack and a cocktail in one of the four bars.

The hotel complex consists of 3 buildings, which have 210 rooms of various classes - from "economy", "standard" to "presidential".

Batumi city and the sea station can be reached by land from the hotel in 15 minutes. The train station is 10 minutes away.

Sheraton Batumi Hotel





If you want to spend a vacation on the Black Sea, live in a comfortable environment, surrounded by many amenities, then the 5-star Sharoton hotel is suitable for you.

It is located in the center of Batumi. The hotel has 202 rooms of various types, these are:

  • Executive Suite
  • penthouse suite;
  • Deluxe Double Room (1 large bed);
  • Junior Suite
  • club double (2 beds);
  • executive double room (1 large bed).

The hotel has a spa area with outdoor and indoor swimming pools. The rooms are equipped with everything you need, they have free Wi-Fi, a TV. Each room has a private bathroom with a shower and bath.

Breakfast is buffet style. You can enjoy European and national Georgian cuisine in the Veronica restaurant, decorated in red and purple. If you are a fan of classic style, then you will surely like the decoration of the Sunflower Restaurant.

It offers easy access to the zoo, just 800 meters away. You can visit the Drama Theater, breaking the path of 2 km.

Where else can I stay for a while in Batumi

According to vacationers, Royal Holiday Sea Towers Apartment deserves 9.8 out of ten, and My Warm Guest House - 9.4.

These Batumi hotels are located on the seashore, in beautiful places. They are equipped with everything necessary to make guests feel comfortable.

Guestrooms at My Warm Guest House have sea views and city views. The hotel has family rooms, 2-bed rooms, family suite, studio rooms.

Royal Holiday Sea Towers Apartment offers beautiful sea views.





It is convenient that the rooms have kitchens where you can cook your favorite dishes or food for young children. Guests also like the availability of washing machines, ironing boards.

In 3 minutes you can walk to the beautiful lake Ardagani, stroll along its coast, admire the picturesque places.

If you choose one of the proposed options, then guaranteed to get an unforgettable vacation in Batumi. Hotels here - for every taste, have different prices. Therefore, in this hospitable city, anyone can relax perfectly and unforgettably!
