Waterpark Skadovsk: description and photo

In our article we will talk about the water park in Skadovsk. Indeed, many people go on vacation to this city, so they will be interested to learn about such an entertainment complex for children and adults.

Description

The water park in Skadovsk is called the "Water Area". He positions himself as a complex of entertainment for people of different ages (both children and adults). There are slides, pools and various water attractions.

Particularly popular are some spiral slides. Closed and open rides are also in demand. Beginners and children will be able to ride on a more gentle slides. Extreme people prefer steep.

Infrastructure

The water park in Skadovsk has outdoor pools for more passive relaxation. There are sun loungers next to them. Guests can sunbathe at them. Anyone who is afraid to burn, can lie down on the sun loungers, which are in the shade. The complex has toilets, a cafe.

The location of the water park. How to get there

There is a water park in Skadovsk at the address: Volodarsky Street, 2a. It is not far from the promenade and the city beach - twenty meters away.

The water park itself is part of the famous Treasure Island complex. It can be reached from cities such as Lazurnoe, Bolshevik and Iron Port.

Opening hours and ticket price

When is it worth to visit the water park in Skadovsk? You can get into the complex from 9:00 to 20:00. Children under three years old can use the complex for free. The cost of a ticket for adults, if you enter the complex in the morning, is 200 hryvnias (about 500 rubles). If you plan to go to the water park after 14:00, then you will need to pay 150 hryvnias (about 380 rubles). On average, people in this complex spend three hours. Although some, the most outcasts, are ready to stay from opening to closing.

Conclusion
