Mini-washing - clean without problems

The mini-washer is a high-pressure apparatus designed to care for the car, which belongs to the category of non-professional equipment. Consider what this equipment is for.



The mini-sink is a universal device that helps to clean a variety of surfaces through a jet of water. It allows the use of interchangeable nozzles and special cleaning agents. The Karcher mini-washer has found the widest use in car cleaning, but this destination is not the only one. It can also help clean up terraces, garden paths, building facades, pools, roofs, sewage and flow pipes, and more. Thanks to its use, the work will be easier, and the amount of water used will be reduced by 80%.



The mini-sink has the following basic characteristics:

- The pressure. Domestic equipment is capable of delivering pressures up to 160 bar. Effective washing of the machine is quite possible at a pressure of about 130 bar.

- Performance. This characteristic shows how much water is consumed per minute or hour. On average, it takes 7-12 liters per minute.

- Voltage. The Karcher mini-sink for domestic use is powered by 220 volts.

- power consumption. In mini-sinks, an engine with a power of 1.5-2.5 kW is installed.

Kercher produces a wide range of domestic mini-sinks, from the primary to the semi-professional. All household appliances are usually classified in series from the second to the seventh. Typically, entry-level mini-sinks are reasonably affordable, small in size, light in weight, and compact. They are designed for short-term and infrequent use. Their main purpose is the occasional cleaning of small contaminated areas. The service life of such devices is limited, the working pressure is quite low - 100-110 bar. Typically, their pumps are made of durable industrial plastic, which significantly reduces the life of the device. Water must be supplied to the device under pressure, otherwise the performance will be significantly reduced.



The most popular is a middle-class mini-sink, which is most often used for washing cars. Such devices have a longer service life and performance than the previous class, and their pumps are made of silumin, and not plastic. At the outlet, the working pressure is at around 120-140 bar. Water can be supplied to the device by gravity or under pressure, which does not affect the performance of the equipment.

A mini-sink of the highest class is a semi-professional option designed to clean contaminants from large enough areas. Pumps made of brass are installed in such sinks, which significantly increases their service life. At the exit, you can get a pressure of up to 160 bar.

Typically, Karcher mini-sinks belonging to the same series have similar characteristics, only their design and appearance may differ, which does not affect performance in any way.
