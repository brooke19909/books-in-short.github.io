Eleana Hotel Apartments (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

Holidays in Cyprus are among the five most popular among our compatriots, and among the resorts of this island Ayia Napa takes the first place. Here you can find absolutely everything that an ordinary person associates with a good rest - luxurious beaches, cozy cafes, various entertainment establishments and hotels of any level. It is noteworthy that in Cyprus you can relax perfectly even in a small hotel with three stars. These include the popular among the Russians Eleana Hotel Apartments in Ayia Napa. It is often recommended to budget tourists, but it can also be comfortable for vacationers who are not constrained by financial means. If you doubt that this is possible, then we are ready to convince you. Today our article is devoted to Eleana Hotel Apartments and features of living in it.





Description of the hotel complex

Eleana Hotel Apartments amaze its guests with beautiful territory and a friendly welcome. Every guest is welcome here, and it is felt literally from the first minutes of your stay at the hotel. It is literally surrounded by greenery, which is a rarity for local hotels.

Eleana Hotel Apartments consists of three two-story buildings, in which twenty-one rooms are located. Basically, all apartments are similar to each other, the differences are only in the number of rooms and their equipment.

The hotel is classified as economical. Therefore, here you can meet many young people, as well as families with young children. They often choose Cyprus because of its gentle sea and wide beaches. And the hotel provides this category of tourists with excellent conditions for little money.





Location

Located Eleana Hotel Apartments in Cyprus in Ayia Napa. It is located almost in the very center of the city - only eight hundred meters from the main attractions and entertainment centers.

The hotel is located on the second coastline, only five hundred meters will separate the hotel guests from the beautiful beach of Limanaki. The coastal zone, like many others in Cyprus, is a municipal one. Therefore, sun loungers and umbrellas must be paid to all vacationers, regardless of their place of residence. Usually their cost does not exceed two and a half euros per day of stay on the beach.

Near Eleana Hotel Apartments there are many shops and cafes selling souvenirs and preparing delicious Mediterranean dishes. The hotel is forty-five kilometers from the international airport, but Nicosia, the capital of the island, will have to travel much longer. The city is located eighty kilometers from the hotel complex, however, many tourists use public transport to get to the capital.

In tourist catalogs the hotel is often called "urban" because of its proximity to the center of the resort. This allows its guests to save on rental transport, because any place can be reached on foot in twenty-twenty-five minutes.





The infrastructure of the hotel complex

Choosing for yourself Eleana Hotel Apartments, keep in mind that the hotel does not have too much entertainment and infrastructure. However, this is typical of Cypriot hotel complexes, they rarely provide their guests with a wide range of services, but this nuance is characteristic of Cyprus.

The hotel has a reception desk, twenty-four hours a day. Its employees are usually quite fluent in Russian, so guests can easily solve problems with staff. Luggage storage and safety deposit boxes are available at the front desk. These services are available at an additional cost.

Tourists in Cyprus often rent vehicles to move around the island freely. Therefore, they need parking, so as not to worry about the safety of their cars. In Eleana Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus), such a parking space is equipped, and vehicles here can be left completely free.

Hotel guests enjoy relaxing by the pool. It is open and large in size. There are always many guests who are too lazy to go to the beach. At the pool, they can enjoy cocktails, which the local bartender makes from the very early morning. Guests eat in a small restaurant where the cook prepares delicious Mediterranean dishes.

Eleana Hotel Apartments reviews provide information on the on-site shop. Here, prices are often lower than average in the city, so guests here prefer to buy souvenirs and Cypriot products, which are often taken away from the island as a present to relatives and friends.





Service and Facilities

It is nice that the hotel complex staff speaks several languages. This can be considered a rarity in Greece, where employees usually communicate only in their own language and a little in English.

The hotel has room service, so guests can order dishes from the restaurant without leaving their apartments. This service is very popular with newlyweds who prefer to spend most of their time alone with each other.

If necessary, tourists will be given the opportunity to send a fax or copy documents. There is no full-fledged conference room in the hotel, but the necessary equipment is at the reception.

Entertainment

You should not expect that in the hotel complex you will be constantly entertained. This is not accepted in Cyprus hotels. Of the main attractions, guests can relax at the pool, which has a wide sun terrace.

Guests can also ride bicycles. This pastime is quite popular among active youth, because in this way they get the opportunity to see the sights of the island.

In the evenings, many hotel guests gather around a pool table. International teams often organize competitions here.





Numbers: classification and description

We already mentioned at the beginning of the article that guests are accommodated in two-story cottages. At Eleana Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa), tourists can choose one-room or two-room apartments. They are inhabited by two to four people. Almost every room is equipped with a small kitchen, where it is nice to cook breakfast and lunch. Many tourists buy food in stores and prefer to eat in their own rooms.

All units are air conditioned and have a TV with several channels. Almost all of the windows overlook the swimming pool, respectively, spacious balconies also look here. They have light furniture (chairs and tables).

The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, toaster, microwave, kettle and stove. Many tourists are pleased that the hotel does not have problems with hot water. Keep in mind that this problem is very common in Cyprus.

Rooms are cleaned daily, maids change bed linen twice a week. I would like to clarify that the quality of cleaning is not satisfactory even for the most picky hotel guests.
