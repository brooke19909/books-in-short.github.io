Visit Gelendzhik! The sandy beaches are amazing there!

Summer, sea, sand ... Most people present their annual vacation in this way. That's just where to go - to the Black Sea or abroad - a question that torments every second vacationer. Now travel agencies can buy permits for every taste and budget. And it was nice to know that domestic resorts are not inferior to foreign ones. And the leading place rightfully belongs to the coastal town called Gelendzhik. The sandy beaches here are magnificent, so the resort is one of the most beloved and most visited for tourists from all over the world.

Gelendzhik

This city is located in the Krasnodar Territory, near Novorossiysk. It is very easy to get to it. You can by train, or by plane. Sanatoriums, dispensaries, recreation centers and private pensions are always ready to receive guests. The Gelendzhik resort zone includes villages located on the coast near the city, such as Betta Khutor, Divnomorskoe, Dzhanhot, Arkhipo-Osipovka, Praskoveevka, Kabardinka, Krinitsa. Now consider where in Gelendzhik sandy beaches.

Sand or pebbles?

The coastline of the city stretched over more than 100 km. Wild beaches alternate with fully equipped ones. Most of them are pebble with a flat sandy bottom. There are about 120 of them with a total length of more than 20 km. But if you wish, if you visit Gelendzhik, sandy beaches can also be found. Now we will tell you exactly where they are.





So, you are planning a vacation in Gelendzhik. The sandy beach in this town is located, for example, near the marine station. Its length is about 1.5 km, and the area is 5 ha. You can always take inventory here, but in the second half of summer there are a lot of vacationers on this beach. This should be taken into account by lovers lying on the sand.

Before choosing a health resort or vacation in a private boarding house, it is better to clarify which beach there is. After all, most go to lie in the sand, soak up the sun and swim in the warm sea. So, the choice of the beach should be given attention. Some people like sand, while others like small pebbles.

In addition to the urban sandy beach, in Gelendzhik there are those that are covered with small pebbles. You can meet them if you walk along the promenade towards the Thin Cape. Most resorts in the city have such beaches.





The city of Gelendzhik attracts many. Sandy beaches where else are they located? Now consider a few more of these places.

Do you plan to go on holiday with children in Gelendzhik? A sandy beach with a shallow bottom is what you need. Where is such a wonderful place? Such beaches are located in Blue Bay and Kabardinka. And on the site between Gelendzhik and Kabardinka you can relax on the pebble beaches that prevail here.

From Gelendzhik to Janhot, the coastline is in solid rocks. There are either no beaches at all, or they are very narrow and rocky. And yet, between Dzhanhotom and the village of Divnomorskoe, you can find among the rocks a convenient descent to a small area covered with pebbles or stones, where you can swim.





Further, between Dzhanhotom and Praskoveevka, you can find unique sea baths. They are formed by horizontal layers of rocks. The bottom of such natural bathtubs is completely smooth, sanded with water for many years. The beaches in this area are pebble, but closer to the settlements artificial sand banks are more and more common.

In Arkhipo-Osipovka pebble-sand beaches prevail. And in Betta, the coast is completely covered with small pebbles.

Those who prefer to relax in health resorts are advised to pay attention to the Blue Wave Sanatorium. Its beach has long been considered the best in the city. It is covered with small pebbles. The shallow sandy bottom is clean. There are a lot of shallows, so this beach is perfect for families. Children can be safely left in shallow water. On the beach, you can rent any equipment and equipment - from sun loungers with umbrellas to masks and equipment for sea fishing. In addition, a wide range of services is presented here - rental of boats, jet skis and "bananas". Entrance to the beach is free, you will need to pay for the rental of equipment and a shower. Vacationers in the resort need only present a guest card, the rest must pay.





For lovers of silence equipped pontoon islands. Payment for such rest is hourly. Since they are located in the very center of the bay, they can only be reached by boat or boat. For a fee, hot meals and cold snacks can be requested on the pontoon island.

It is advisable to take a seat on the beach until 10 a.m. Later, there may not be enough sun loungers, and even at sea it will be too crowded.

"Red Talca"

No less famous in the city is the beach of the resort "Krasnaya Talka". It is divided into two levels. On the upper there is a green cover and sun loungers - all the amenities for sunbathers, at the lower level there is only a green cover. There is a sandy beach and a rocky bottom. To protect against high waves that can lift debris and algae from the bottom, a special dam has been laid at a half-meter depth. On the beach there are toilets, a shower, a bar, a beach volleyball court. Diving enthusiasts can use the services of an experienced instructor diver. Entrance for guests of the sanatorium is free, as well as refreshments. It is enough to present a guest card. The rest will have to pay. Bored on vacation will not give experienced animators. Tourists who care about their figure should be aware that donuts, pancakes and other flour sweets are constantly being prepared on the beach, which may well add a few extra pounds.

Farm Betta

Betta Farm is a great place for a romantic getaway. This village is surrounded on all sides by orchards and vineyards, and coniferous aroma is constantly floating in the air. And this is not surprising. Indeed, in this area the most coniferous forests and thickets of juniper. The beach is pebbled and the seabed is rocky. And yet this is a favorite vacation spot with children. It has a lot of playgrounds. And the abundance of underwater gorges with rapans is a real paradise for divers.

In August, the sea on Betta Farm begins to glow and shimmer with all the colors of the rainbow. Small algae - noctiluki - are to blame. Only with them you need to be careful. Everything with which they come in contact also begins to glow.

Those who want to swim in perfectly clean water will have to leave the city and find a place on the shore away from sanatoriums and settlements.

In addition to the beaches, there are many attractions in Gelendzhik, the main of which is the monument to the White Bride. And the world's longest embankment is perfect for evening walks.

Conclusion

Now you know that there is a city like Gelendzhik. Sandy beaches on its territory are not bad, but they are few. Pebbles make up the bulk. If you want to relax, you can safely go to this seaside city, here everyone will be able to choose for themselves a place to stay, and to lie down on the seashore.
