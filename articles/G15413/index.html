Ostrovsky Park, Rostov-on-Don: address and photo

The bustle of the city, the noise of cars, workdays - all mixed up into one, and sometimes you just want to relax. It is such peace of mind and at the same time a fascinating pastime can be obtained in Ostrovsky Park. It is described in more detail later in the article.

Park history

This green corner was born at the end of the 19th century. The park was named in honor of the popular publicist of those times Nikolai Ostrovsky. Immediately planted groves of huge area, and the square began to slowly transform. Ostrovsky Park (Rostov-on-Don), whose address: 1a Selmash Ave, began its active life.



Very often the name of the park changed, and this went on for quite some time. And after more than 50 years, the square was given its current name - Nikolai Ostrovsky Park of Culture and Rest.

Its main feature is a huge number of unique green spaces. Many rare species of trees and shrubs. Some of them have long exceeded one hundred years.

Sports section

It is not necessary to go to fitness centers, look for exercise equipment and the like. Ostrovsky Park (Rostov-on-Don) is a kind of small sports state where a healthy lifestyle reigns. In it you can take a walk along the health path with a length of almost 1 kilometer. And accordingly, enjoy the fresh air.

Also in the park you can freely exercise and do athletics, while still getting a boost of energy and a wonderful mood.

Two years ago, the Svyazistov alley grew up in the park. On a traditional tree planting day, about 70 oaks were planted. From that moment, the alley was born. It has a workout area.





Inside two more parks

The square amazes all guests with its diversity. On its territory there are two more peculiar attractions: the rope park and the dinosaur square. Unforgettable impressions and positive emotions are guaranteed.

You can enjoy the mass of adventures on 6 routes of different difficulty levels in the rope park. To return to the past and meet with the inhabitants of the Mesozoic period, it is enough to drop by on a visit to Vesely Dino.

Ostrovsky Park (Rostov-on-Don) is the only place where you can see representatives of the ancient world. On a large territory there are more than 20 electromechanical figures that move, while making different sounds. Near each exhibit there is a special information board on which the name of the animal is written, its parameters and environment.

To relax in an interesting and instructive way, just visit Ostrovsky Park (Rostov-on-Don). Dinosaurs are very similar to real ones, due to the fact that elastic material was used in the manufacture.

Dinoland is a kind of visiting card of Rostov for tourists. This idea was born 13 years ago, but has not been implemented.

Creative moment

Visitors who like to have a good time can appreciate the work of the youth association. In it, participants organize various theatrical performances, and also hold children's and family holidays. The cultural leisure of the older generation takes place in the form of thematic meetings.

Each year the park hosts about 300 events. On holidays and weekends, visitors are pleased with theatrical performances, fun contests, concerts, entertainment and game programs.





Visitors advise

Regular guests of the park do not come without nuts. It is here that there is a mischievous squirrel, blue-winged jay, and pigeons and other representatives of the animal world also fly in packs.

Visitors are pleased that the park is not only beautiful, but well-groomed at the same time. To some it even resembles a forest, since there really is a lot of flora and fauna in it.

Satisfied visitors say that not only culturally, but also fun, you can spend the weekend coming to Ostrovsky Park (Rostov-on-Don), the attractions of which will give unforgettable emotions.

Guests love this place because there are many walking paths. Also, there will always be entertainment for both young visitors and adults. For children, there are many trampolines, cars, carousels.

However, despite the fact that the park is guarded, visitors are not advised to stay there at night, as there were robberies and robberies. Because of this, the reputation of the square is not very good, and guests strongly recommend improving security.
