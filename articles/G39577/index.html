Hotel Holiday International Sharjah 4 *: description, photo, reviews of tourists

If you decide to spend an unforgettable vacation in the UAE, and you consider the city of Sharjah as a place for relaxation, Hotel Holiday International Sharjah 4 * can be an excellent option for accommodation.





Location

This hotel is located almost in the center of Sharjah, on the shores of the Khalid lagoon. There are two airports within a 20-minute drive from the hotel. In the vicinity of the hotel there are lots of restaurants, cafes, shops. There is also a bazaar and a park.

Photo Description

Hotel Holiday International Hotel Sharjah 4 * was built in 1979. In 2015, a large-scale renovation was held here. The hotel consists of one large 14-story building. The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 253 rooms of the categories Standard, Deluxe, Junior Suite, Presidential Suite and Executive Suite. All apartments have: air conditioning, TV (there is one channel in Russian), minibar, safe, bathroom, balcony. Towels are cleaned and changed daily. According to tourists, wireless Internet ‚Äúcatches‚Äù well not in all rooms. However, you can use it in the lobby and in the hotel restaurants.

At the hotel there are: swimming pool, sun terrace, sports grounds, shop, currency exchange, tour desk, spa.

As for meals, when booking travelers can choose one of the options: breakfast only; lunch and breakfast (half board); full board (three meals a day).

The beach is a quarter hour drive away. For everyone, a free shuttle service is organized several times a day. You can also take a taxi to the shore.





Holiday International Hotel Sharjah 4 *: traveler reviews from the Russian Federation

Since many modern tourists are very careful to approach the issue of choosing a hotel in a particular country, they want to collect as much information as possible about their place of interest. Moreover, they are no longer just the official description of the hotel and the advice of the manager of a travel agency. So, travelers try to study the reviews of other people who have visited the hotel in question. This approach allows you to make a more complete and close to the real state of things view that will save you from disappointment upon arrival on a long-awaited vacation. In this regard, today we bring to your attention the generalized comments of our compatriots that they left after visiting the Hotel Holiday International Sharjah 4 *. Running a little ahead, we note that the vast majority of tourists in their choice were not disappointed. In their opinion, the hotel is fully consistent with the price declared for accommodation. But we learn about everything in more detail.





Hotel itself

Despite the fact that this hotel is not new, travelers were satisfied with both its appearance and interior design. So, according to our compatriots, fresh repair is noticeable here. As for the rooms offered to the guests, almost all tourists were satisfied with them. According to vacationers, all Holiday International Hotel Sharjah 4 * apartments are quite spacious, bright, pleasantly decorated. Guests note not new, but very high-quality and comfortable furniture, excellent working equipment. If you go on vacation with your children, then grab a USB flash drive with cartoons, because the rooms have TVs with a USB connector. Many tourists also note in their comments blackout curtains that allow you to sleep in the mornings (after all, they do not let in the rays of the early rising bright sun), as well as nice balconies where you can sit while admiring the surroundings. Travelers did not have complaints about cleaning.





Location

Many tourists preferred Hotel Holiday International Sharjah 4 * including due to its location. So, the hotel is located in the very center of the city. In its vicinity there are plenty of places for walking and shopping, which, judging by the comments of our compatriots, are deprived of hotels located directly on the beach.

Nutrition

Most of the guests when booking a stay at this hotel only ordered breakfast. According to them, in the morning the food is excellent here. Food and dishes are the same every day, but there is always something to choose from.

Tourists preferred to have lunch and dinner in cafes and restaurants of Sharjah or other places. According to our compatriots, there is a lot of fast food at an affordable price. However, if you want to treat yourself to national cuisine or some kind of delights, then the cost of a trip to a restaurant can be very significant.





Entertainment

The main fun for guests of Hotel Holiday International Sharjah 4 * is a beach holiday. According to our compatriots, it is not worth worrying about the fact that the hotel is at a considerable distance from the sea. Indeed, the beach can always be reached either by a free bus that runs several times a day, or by taxi, which will cost you quite inexpensively. A bus will bring you to the beach of one of the hotels. Entrance here is paid. It is desirable to purchase tickets at the reception of your hotel, it will cost less. On the beach there are sun loungers and parasols, a bar. Drinks must be paid separately. There is also a public beach nearby. No one will take money from you for his visit. However, there are no sun loungers, so you can settle down by laying a towel on the sand or a special rug.

Since it is very hot on the beach during the day, many guests of Hotel Holiday International Sharjah 4 * spent this time at the hotel. So, according to them, to hide from the heat here was possible not only in rooms, restaurants or bars, but also by the pool. It has a terrace with sun loungers. It is surrounded by trees giving a saving shadow. In addition, some guests enjoyed visiting the local sauna and hammam.
