Hotel Nirvana Lagoon Villas Suites SPA 5 * (Beldibi, Turkey): description and reviews of tourists

Surely everyone knows that Turkey, with its luxurious resorts, today enjoys great popularity among tourists who want to spend a vacation on the beach. And a pretty good place is considered a small resort village Beldibi. Many travelers prefer to stay at the luxurious Nirvana Lagoon Villas Suites SPA. So what is this hotel and what kind of living conditions await guests?





Location and description of the hotel

Nirvana Lagoon Villas Suites SPA is a luxurious hotel complex located in a picturesque corner of Beldibi, next to the seashore, surrounded by mountain peaks. The famous spa town of Kemer is just 16 km away. In addition, it is not so difficult to get to Antalya from here - it is located 26 km. The international airport is 36 km away.

It is worth noting that this hotel is completely new - it was opened in 2014 and, accordingly, can offer the traveler all the modern amenities. There is a luxurious garden, and the designers have done a great job on the entire territory of the hotel.





Hotel Nirvana Lagoon Villas Suites SPA: photo and description of rooms

This hotel complex is quite large - on its territory there are 560 rooms. These are standard and deluxe rooms (with a high level of comfort), spacious family apartments and even small secluded villas.

All rooms are spacious with large windows. The floors are covered with laminate flooring and the rooms themselves are furnished in an ultra-modern style. Naturally, there is a necessary set of equipment, including a TV, air conditioning, a small refrigerator, a DVD player. There is constant access to the Internet. Villas and family rooms have a separate kitchenette with a set of tableware and the necessary kitchen appliances. A spacious bathroom with round-the-clock hot water supply will also please (there is a shower cabin, some apartments also have a bathtub). Maids are cleaned every day, so the rooms are kept in order and clean.

Meal plan for guests

The hotel offers an all-inclusive scheme, which provides four meals a day, as well as snacks and drinks (but only locally made), which can be obtained during the day. Meals are served in the main restaurant of the hotel (buffet is served). According to reviews, the choice of dishes here is very large: fresh fruits, dishes from vegetables, meat, fish and, of course, traditional Turkish sweets and more familiar European desserts.



There are bars in the lobby, pool and beach. Alternatively, you can visit the local cafeteria and enjoy real Turkish coffee. You can also book a table at local restaurants a la carte.

Beach and water recreation

Nirvana Lagoon Villas Suites SPA owns a private section of the beach, which, incidentally, is located just 20 meters away - you can walk here in a matter of minutes. The beach is covered with sand that is cleaned daily. Along the coast is a lively coral reef. A comfortable wooden pontoon is equipped for launching into the sea. Naturally, there is a sufficient number of sunbeds and umbrellas, which guests can use for free. And you can get a refreshing drink at the beach bar.

Fans of more active pastime will not remain out of work, because the choice of entertainment is truly huge. Because of corals, the coast is popular with divers - here you can rent the equipment you need for scuba diving , as well as take training courses. In addition, tourists love to ride boats, boats, catamarans, water skiing. The beach has a well-equipped volleyball court.

Entertainment and additional services

Of course, the hotel tried to create all conditions for a comfortable and fun holiday. For the convenience of travelers, laundry, dry cleaning, currency exchange, as well as parking and car rental are constantly working here. If necessary, you can go to the doctor.

In the courtyard there is a large pool with sun loungers and parasols around. In addition, water slides work every day , where not only children but adults can have fun. You can also relax in the sauna or jacuzzi, go for a massage. Animators entertain the guests daily, and in the evening, exciting performances and interesting show programs await you.



Many parents are interested in questions about whether children can have fun at the hotel Nirvana Lagoon Villas Suites SPA 5 *. A kids club is open every day and there is an excellent playground in the garden. Kids will also be able to splash around in special pools and have fun on water slides.

Hotel complex Nirvana Lagoon Villas Suites SPA: reviews

Immediately I must say that this hotel is in good standing with tourists. Travelers leave exclusively positive reviews. Naturally, one of the advantages is the proximity to the sea and spectacular views of the mountains. The territory of the hotel itself is also well thought out and well designed.

Guests note good food - in the restaurant the food is just wonderful, there are always enough tasty dishes and drinks on the tables. All employees are polite, and most of them are fluent in Russian. The beach is clean and well maintained. Even children like to spend time here, because funny animators constantly arrange various performances and entertainment events. In short, the hotel complex Nirvana Lagoon Villas Suites SPA deserves its five stars.
