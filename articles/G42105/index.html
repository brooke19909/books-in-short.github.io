We crochet a small flower, knitting patterns

Huge popularity is won by small flowers crocheted according to the schemes of threads. They are created with a hook, and then they decorate items of clothing, hats, hairpins, bracelets, bags, etc. They make wonderful bouquets, necklaces and earrings from these flowers - all the fair sex will be happy to receive them as a gift.

There are a huge number of knitting methods - from narrow petals to oval, voluminous and flat inflorescences.

The use of knitted flowers

The size of knitted flowers is also diverse, they are both large and smaller. They can decorate absolutely everything - from hooks for curtains to coats. With their help, masterpieces are created!

Having connected many small flowers, you can make unique jewelry, a hair clip, a holiday card or a photo frame.

The advantage of these products is their great resistance to wet processing - washing, dry cleaning. They do not warp, perfectly retaining their original shape. They are attached to the material either with glue or sewing with threads.

In short, anything is possible with imagination and patience! Crochet small flowers according to patterns and decorate your favorite things!

Tools and materials

For work on knitting small flowers, there is no need for additional costs for new skeins of yarn, use the remnants of materials left over from old knitters! Each needlewoman has such a bag with glomeruli, which I really do not want to throw away. Now they will come in handy!

We also note that when working with different types of threads, completely different effects are obtained, even if the knitting follows the same pattern. Suppose a thin woolen yarn gives a fluffy soft texture to the flower, a cotton thread creates very clear outlines of the leaves, mercerized cotton will bring a slight glow to the finished product.

Each knitted flower should be crocheted in an appropriate size - it is selected to the thickness of the yarn. The most suitable for such work are the thinnest hooks - No. 1, 2 and 3.

Also, for the middle of the flowers, beads, beads, rhinestones are needed.

Crochet viola

Creating asymmetric flowers, for example, viola, is not at all complicated, as it seems at first glance. The base is the same as in the previously described options - a ring of loops with a pillar tied into it. without crochets. The only difference is knitting leaves.

There are 3 stages in the work.

On the first, a core of yellow yarn is knitted, which is then tied with half-columns.

The second step will be the joining of purple yarn and tying a pole in a circle. without crochets. The same thread is knitted arches from air loops.

Miniature layered flowers

Having mastered the simplest techniques for crocheting beautiful flowers according to patterns, you can begin to manufacture much more interesting inflorescences! After all, many flowers were created by nature with several rows of leaves. Creating voluminous flowers is easy using flat color schemes.

At the beginning of the work, they also knit a ring of air loops, tie it with single crochet columns, and then with the help of arches tied under the petals of the previous row, they increase another row of petals.

This row will be larger than the previous one by one loop. Each arch in the next tiers is gaining one more additional loop.
