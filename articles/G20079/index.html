Hotel and restaurant complex Amici Grand Hotel (Krasnodar): address, description of rooms, service, reviews

There is no shortage of quality hotels in Krasnodar. There are five, and budget hotels. But if you are looking for a hotel in the center of Krasnodar, and even such that the price meets the quality of services, then the choice is narrowed. The leader in popularity among visitors - both vacationers and business travelers - is the Amichi Grand 4 * hotel and restaurant complex.

In this article we will describe this establishment in detail. Its original, reminiscent of the donjon of a medieval castle, dark blue mirror glass building can be seen from afar. The address of Amici Grand Hotel is known to all local taxi drivers, and finding a hotel is easy. Let's see what rooms this complex offers its guests, and what is its infrastructure. An informative base for the review was the reviews of tourists.

Location

The hotel and restaurant complex is located in the heart of the city. Its address: 112 Krasniy Partizan Street. This location offers excellent opportunities for hotel guests. After all, within walking distance are both business districts and key attractions of Krasnodar. The streets of Turgenev and Herzen are considered shopping. There are many shops, boutiques, cafes and restaurants. Also, not far from the hotel, in two or three blocks there are shopping and entertainment centers such as Red Square and Gallery (on Krasnaya Street).

Krasnodar Airport is 20 kilometers away from the hotel, and the city railway station is 7 kilometers away. Businessmen who come to the largest exhibition complex in the south of Russia, Expograd, often stop at the hotel. They find the location of the hotel very good. Most tourists who arrived in Krasnodar for its sights agree with businessmen. The Kuban River flows nearby, there are several park areas nearby.

The high-rise Amici Grand Hotel was erected in 2016. Therefore, there are not so many reviews about this complex. But those that are available are the most enthusiastic. The tower of the modern building, according to travelers, resembles a multi-storey Tower. True, from the panoramic windows of the rooms you can see not London, but Krasnodar with a simple chess layout of the streets. Amici Grand is a typical city hotel. On the basement level of the building is a parking lot. There, guests can leave their vehicle for free. The porter will help unload the suitcases from the trunk and deliver them to the reception, and then - right in the room. In the multi-storey building of the hotel there are:

  • a magnificent lobby, reminiscent of luxurious decoration more like a throne room in the palace;
  • a cozy lobby where you can set up a business meeting with a partner;
  • conference room and several meeting rooms;
  • a wonderful Mediterranean restaurant and cafe;
  • pool;
  • a spa center, which, in addition to standard services, also has a salt room.

Room Description at Amici Grand Hotel

The architecture of the tower building, alas, does not include balconies. But everything else, hotel rooms will captivate travelers with their comfort. All "standards" have private bathrooms. There, guests will find a set of fluffy towels, a hairdryer, and daily replenished hygiene items. Soft, snow-white bathrobes hang in a wardrobe. Slippers are provided. Bathrooms are equipped with showers. Superior rooms also have scales.

Let's move on to the description of the bedrooms. All the services that should be in a 4 star hotel are there: cable TV, safe, minibar. The bed is covered with hypoallergenic bedding. The name of the hotel Amici Grand Hotel contains the Italian word for "friends." It is the key to hotel service. Here you are met as the most cordial friends. And this is manifested not only in the cordiality of the staff, but also in many small things. This, for example, a sewing kit and brushes for clothes and shoes. Both rooms are luxuriously cleaned, they reign solemn and at the same time cozy atmosphere.

Residents of the city often reserve these rooms for weddings and other special occasions.
