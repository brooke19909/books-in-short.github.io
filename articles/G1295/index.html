How to install Jailbreak and why do it at all?



Apple products are famous not only for their high price, reliability, and most importantly, thoughtful and user-friendly interface, but also for the fact that almost all applications, even the smallest and simplest, are installed only for money. And yet, the worst thing for many modern users, in the standard software environment there is no way to configure most of the device, the choice is limited to two or three points and nothing more. In order to fix this shortcoming, many are looking for information on how to install Jailbreak.

Before updating the software, you should consider the legal basis of this process. Apple spoke out once, but it‚Äôs very clear that it is extremely negative about this procedure. Any device that will have a modified software is automatically removed from the warranty. That is, if you just install Jailbreak, you can forget about free support in official services. Now you have to pay for any repairs, even the smallest ones.



Changing the software allows you to not only get more access to system settings. This is an absolute must for those who have purchased a phone or tablet with a 3G module in the USA, since installing Jailbreak is the only way to use the full functionality. Such devices were initially assigned to the operators of the country in which they were purchased. By inserting the SIM card of the local provider, you can see that the device refuses to work with it. The whole point is that such a restriction is laid at the program level. Jailbreak, the installation of which solves this problem, rewrites the necessary lines, allowing you to freely change operators and, accordingly, use the device purchased in the west and in other countries.

Before proceeding with the installation of new software, it is worth evaluating your own strengths and capabilities. Most often, the price of such services is about one thousand rubles, although it can vary from region to region. Given the cost of Apple products, even in the most minimal configuration, almost every owner of these devices will be able to afford this process.



In case you have knowledge about how to install Jailbreak yourself, you need to find out if there is a hack for a specific installed system on a tablet or phone. If the software version was released earlier than a month ago, then most likely there is no way to crack it. After downloading the desired firmware, you must disconnect the device and connect it to the computer. Then the special program RedSn0w starts, where you need to press the Jailbreak key. After that, an instruction will appear, following the instructions of which, it is necessary to transfer the device to DFU mode. After installation, turn off startup mode. Now the device is untied from the official system and works as the user needs.
