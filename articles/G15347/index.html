Possible malfunctions of the air conditioner

The air conditioner itself is a complex device, with many details and their precise fit. Perhaps that is why your air conditioning is so defenseless against dust, dirt and time. The malfunction may be due to the fact that the filters clog the dust, dirt adheres to the fans, the oil becomes obsolete and dirty. It becomes difficult for the device to work with such obstacles, and it turns out that everything seems to work, but there is no sense. Of course, the master will not return lost time, but he can clean the dirt and dust without difficulty.

One of the causes of breakdowns is poplar fluff. His cunning is that if moisture appears in the place where he accumulated, he immediately turns into a durable film, which affects the air conditioner badly. A malfunction may lead to the replacement of the device. And such a film boldly lays on the complex surface of the heat exchanger, it starts to lack air, and if the protective sensor does not work on time, the device stops working.



It happens that individual leaves or feathers (fluff) are sucked in by air to the heat exchanger, and this causes a breakdown. And what is the role of ordinary dust! It acts as cement and clogs the heat exchanger. And it also becomes an excellent environment for colonizing mold, bacteria and fungi, it can cause allergies and other diseases, the involuntary culprit of which will be air conditioning. The malfunction can be associated with cockroaches, flies, wasps, which can also damage your device, crawling into some small compartments or tubes and becoming a cork in them. Respect the device as a home and mice, absorbing the insulation of wires and dying in the back streets of technology.

A very common problem (relevant for smokers, bars, rooms of smokers) is kitchen fumes and tobacco smoke. Those harmful substances that settle in the body can become a binder for dust and dirt. They clog passages, glue moving parts. Even able to corrode isolation.

Unstable voltage (if there is no protective grounding), temperature changes (in the case of cheap models of air conditioning) are the enemies of any technology. The outside of the air conditioner can be damaged by a fallen icicle, or discarded household garbage, or even a piece of cracked stucco.
