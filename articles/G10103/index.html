Seamless footsteps

Before you start knitting the footrest with knitting needles, you need to prepare the necessary materials. This is any yarn (depending on your wishes), approximately 100-150 g, and number 3.5 knitting needles on a fishing line.

Walkthrough:

  1. Knitting of the followers with knitting needles begins with a set of a certain number of loops. Since we are giving a master class on foot size 36-37, we dial fifty-two loops. Carefully count each row so as not to make a mistake with the length.
  2. Knit four rows with an elastic band (one front, one wrong side). Tip: remove the first loop, and knit the last back; only then you will get a beautiful edge of the product, which is not a shame to show and convenient to use when stitching individual parts.
  3. Next, start knitting with the front stitch (in even rows all the loops are front, in odd rows the wrong) according to the following pattern: one row - one hem (remove), twenty-four front, yarn, two front, yarn, twenty-four front, one edge (wrong side) ) The second row - according to the figure, the third row - one hem, twenty-five facial, yarn, two facial, yarn, twenty-five facial, one hem. The fourth row - according to the drawing.
  4. The next row must be tied with purl loops. It turns out that you need to add loops along the central part of the canvas, and to do this very carefully and accurately. Therefore, it remains for you to continue knitting until you get six increase loops (eleven rows).

Front knit pattern

Next, proceed to the rest of the details. So, in order to connect the front part of the product, it is necessary to perform each step of the scheme in turn:

  1. Knit thirty-seven loops face, then combine the next two loops. After that, expand the knitting and, forming the wrong loops, knit twelve pieces, and combine the next two. Continue work until fourteen stitches remain on the knitting needles. After that, knit the row to the end with purl stitches. As a result of the manipulations you will get a practically ready track (it remains to make a beautiful heel).
  2. Make a set of purl loops from the remaining hem. You will get eight pieces on each side. Now you just need to continue knitting according to the scheme described earlier (decrease in the foot). Work continues until there are two loops left on each side intended for reduction.
  3. Continue to decrease, changing the front surface to an elastic band (one front loop, one back loop).
  4. Close the hinges in a way convenient for you, and hide the sticking tails with a hook or needle.
  5. So one follower is ready. The second one also fits. If desired, you can sew crocheted flowers to them.

That's so simple, and most importantly, without seams, you can knit the heels with knitting needles. If you easily repeated the master class and were satisfied with the result, then you can safely include imagination and create your masterpieces using famous patterns.

Recommendations

Be careful when sewing loops so as not to change the density of knitting. If you cannot connect the loops accurately, that is, without a hole, then dial plus one additional loop, and when approaching the next row, throw it on a new knitting needle, and already from it drop the loop on the main canvas.

Important: be careful with the foot, it should always remain even (smooth), so as not to cause discomfort when wearing the finished product. If you wish, you can strengthen it by adding an additional thread. This solution allows your product to remain intact longer.

Woolen yarn must be knitted in two threads. And when changing colors, cross the threads on the wrong side of the work. Do not change the direction of knitting until the toe.
