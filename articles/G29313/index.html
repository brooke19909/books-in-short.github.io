How to remove a person from a region in Minecraft: best ways

Playing Minecraft, we are constantly building something. But our buildings are always at risk, because some people like to break someone else's, not to get away from this. We create a region for our magnificent buildings, if we play with friends, we add them to the region, but there come moments when someone wants to be removed from our region. And here the question arises: how to remove a person from a region in Minecraft?

Creating a region in Minecraft





To add or remove people to a region, you must first create it. How to do this? First you need to make sure that the server on which we play plugins such as WorldGuard and WorldEdit are installed. We assume that they are installed. Now we need a wooden ax. We craft it or get the command // wand. There is an ax. Now we represent our region as a volumetric rectangle. Select one corner of this rectangle with the right mouse button and the other with the left mouse button. Just click the ax. Then we come up with a name for our region. And then we write the command: / rg claim <Region name>. Done, our region is created, and the building is protected.

Add people to a region

We have a region. No one can break anything and use it. But what if we have friends who also want to live with us? Just add them to our cool region. Before that, we have already created a region. It remains only to add a player to it. There are two ways: add it as an ordinary resident: / rg addmember <Region name> <Player nickname>, or as the owner of this region: / rg addowner <Region name> <Player nickname>. Everything seems to be simple here, but how does the owner differ from the resident? The owner can add new residents, but the resident cannot. But how to remove a person from the region in Minecraft if the resident we added turned out to be a vandal and a bad person who wants to break everything and smash it?

Removing people from a region

How to remove a person from the region? Minecraft has a lot of opportunities for this. It often happens that a friend is no longer a friend at all or that he just has his own house and wants to move, or one of hundreds of reasons. Removing a person from the region is a common thing, we can easily cope with it. If we have a created region and residents in it, then simply write the command / rg removemember <Site name> <Bad player name> if we want to remove the resident, and to remove the owner: / rg removeowner <Region name> <Name of the meager owner >. That's all, the player unwanted to us is removed from our wonderful region.

Just write the command / rg teleport <Region name>, as you instantly find yourself at home, in a safe region.
