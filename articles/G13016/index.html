Phu Quoc Island - Holidays in Vietnam

When planning a long-awaited vacation together, it is very difficult to make a specific choice of a place where you would like to go. Indeed, in the world there are many beautiful countries, cities and attractive islands. We propose to consider such a combination as recreation, Vietnam, islands. And this is not just a bunch of words. Try to combine them together and get the pleasure of a wonderful holiday on one of the Vietnamese islands.



The most popular tourist destination is the island of Phu Quoc, located in the very south of the country in the Gulf of Thailand. It is separated by a distance of 15 kilometers from the coast of Cambodia . It is home to more than 85,000 people. The monsoon subequatorial climate prevails on the island. The rainy season is very short, only one month. The rest of the time you can have a wonderful rest here. Beautiful beaches stretch along the coastline. Bai Dai Beach is considered the best.



Phu Quoc Island is a place where you can watch the sun go down over the sea horizon, because almost the entire coast of Vietnam looks to the east. It is very beautiful in here. The island is dotted with many low mountains and hills that adorn the virgin rainforests.

All hotels on the island are located near beaches. If we take the tourism infrastructure as a whole, then it is not yet sufficiently developed. Therefore, lovers of active and fun holidays here will be a little boring. In the future, it is planned to make the island of Phu Quoc as famous and attractive as Bali or Phuket. Its arrangement is moving rapidly. A diving center has already opened on the island. After all, as you know, the coastal underwater world is full of bright fish and beautiful corals.



Phu Quoc Island has its own attractions, the main of which is the pearl plantation. In addition, it is famous for its excellent fish sauce, which has no analogues in any country. It is made from ca com fish, which is rich in protein. The sauce has an unusual but very pleasant smell.

You can get to this island from Ho Chi Minh City. There are five flights per day. In addition, ferry runs from Ha Thien to An Thoi, located in Fukuoka. And from the city of Rach Zia to the island can be reached by speed boat.



Phu Quoc has its own story. Once there were rubber plantations founded by French colonialists. After the French left Indochina, the island belonged to Cambodia until the 80s of the 20th century, until it was presented to Vietnam in honor of the liberation of Pol Pot.

The islands of Vietnam are particularly eastern attractive. No less attractive is the island of Kondao, located in the place where the Mekong River flows into the South China Sea. The large territory of the island is part of a beautiful park. Here you can see various rare animals. Many tourists come here to look at the green turtle. Here you can also climb to the top of Thanh Zha Mountain. On the way there are rare animals and unique plants that grow only on this island.

Condao boasts superb white sand beaches. The most popular is Ong Dung. Hotels are also here, but very few. Currently, the island is under active construction.
