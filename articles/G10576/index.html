The best recreation center (Primorsky Krai): rest, description, services, living conditions

In order to have a good rest on the sea coast, it is not necessary to go to foreign resorts. There are also many places in Russia where you can get a lot of pleasant experiences. For example, Primorsky Krai. The nature here is peculiar and picturesque. For a good rest there are all the necessary conditions. Tourists can enjoy the coast of the Sea of ​​Japan, taiga landscapes and mountains. Everyone will find entertainment to their liking.

Any recreation center (Primorsky Territory) will be happy to welcome guests . Rest is mainly active here. Guests are invited to engage in sports such as surfing, diving, parachuting, kiting, skiing, paragliding, etc. Boat trips and horse trekking are also organized.

A wide range of services and a loyal pricing policy attract more and more tourists to the Primorsky Territory . Where to stay on vacation? Let's look at some popular recreation centers.

"Askora"

In the Northern Bay of the Sea of ​​Japan is located "Askora." This is a small recreation center. Primorsky Krai (holidays here are popular among residents of the Russian Federation) warmly welcomes guests from all over the country. There are always a lot of visitors at the Askora base. The most popular season is, of course, summer. However, the recreation center begins to work in the spring and closes in the fall.

Guests are accommodated in economy rooms. The building itself is located only 10 meters from the sea coast. It is worth noting that the beach is clean, sandy. For entertainment, an inflatable slide is installed. Also, the administration of the base has created all the conditions for diving. On the territory there is a volleyball court, there are gazebos with barbecue facilities. Private car can be parked in a guarded car park. It is proposed to rent sports equipment, sun loungers and diving equipment at the base.

Tourists are offered 3 double rooms and 2 four-bed rooms. All of them are equipped with the necessary furniture (bed, bedside table, shelf). The room has a place for an extra mattress or folding bed. The bathroom is on the street, there is a summer shower. A shared kitchen is equipped on the territory, it has a refrigerator, stoves, and dishes. Visitors are allowed to live with animals. Minimum room rate - 1000 rubles.



"Ocean"

The recreation center "Ocean" (Primorsky Territory) is located next to the reserve "Cedar Pad". It occupies a fairly large territory - 8 hectares. It has access to the sea coast. To stay here are equipped with double houses, a building for 4 people and a hotel offering 48 comfortable rooms. The minimum cost is 990 rubles. The recreation center "Ocean" offers a wide range of entertainment. This includes diving, fishing, billiards, surfing, disco and others. There are cafes and restaurants on site. The latter offer delicious seafood dishes. The administration organizes excursions, as well as boat trips. Guests can eat on the principle of a buffet 3 times a day. Hot dishes, salads, as well as specialized meals for children are offered. The recreation center works all 4 seasons.



Ba Gua

“Ba Gua” is a wonderful recreation center. Primorsky Krai (rest here is filled with calm and silence) will amaze with clean air and a favorable climate. “Ba Gua” can be compared with a paradise. Most often people come here for families with children. Being here, you can enjoy the unspoiled nature and comfortable conditions. For guests' stay, several detached houses and a cozy hotel are offered. All rooms of the "standard" class are equipped with a minimum set of furniture. A bathroom and a bathroom are located in the base near the hotel building. The houses are designed for 6 people. They have beds, televisions, refrigerators, bedside tables and wardrobes. The recreation center does not offer organized catering. Guests can cook in the summer kitchen. Minimum cost of living - 1600 rubles.

You won’t be bored here. The beach is equipped, there is a Russian bath. Tourists can rent a quad bike or jet ski. It also offers a parachute jump. For children, a playground has been organized, on which there is a swimming pool, trampoline, swings and other attractions.



"555"

Near the Trinity Bay there is a recreation center (Andreevka, Primorsky Territory). "555" is located near the mountainside on the banks of the river. To the sea coast about 10 minutes. Both couples and friendly companies come to this place. For accommodation, summer houses for 4 people and a two-story residential building with four and six-bed rooms are offered. A bathroom and a shower with hot water are located at the base. Children under 12 years old receive discounts. There is a playground, parking, a swimming pool, gazebos with barbecue facilities. The cost of living starts from 1200 rubles. There is no organized food, guests will have to cook on their own in the equipped kitchen.



Sun City

“Sun City” is an excellent recreation center (Slavyanka, Primorsky Territory), which is located on the shore of Khalda Bay. For your stay there is a large selection of rooms: from economy class to VIP. The minimum daily cost is 1,500 rubles. In the rooms of the class "economy" and "compact" bathroom and shower are absent. For them, a common room located on the territory is provided. The standard, comfort and VIP rooms have modern furniture, a toilet, a wash basin and a hot shower. The beach is equipped with sun loungers, there are water rides, the territory is constantly cleaned. Children can swim in the pool. In spring, summer and autumn, this recreation center (Primorsky Krai) operates. Rest in these places will leave a lot of pleasant impressions.
