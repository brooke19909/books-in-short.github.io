The best recreation centers in the Vladimir region

Spend a weekend or vacation at one of the recreation centers in the Vladimir region, which means combining a pleasant pastime in nature with the opportunity to visit the unique cities of the Golden Ring.

Recreation facilities near Vladimir

Recreation facilities are located in picturesque places, surrounded by forests and on the banks of ponds, closer or further from settlements and highways. It is possible to choose the most suitable option for you.





"Lakes"

On the shore of a forest lake, among the trees, away from the hustle and bustle, there are log houses in which from 4 to 10 people can live. The interior design is designed in accordance with the surrounding nature. Wooden walls, an interior in the folk Russian style create peace and tranquility. But this is organically combined with modern amenities that provide comfortable accommodation in cottages.

The recreation center "Ozerki" of the Vladimir region welcomes guests year-round, at any time there is an opportunity to interestingly organize your leisure. A Russian bathhouse, a jacuzzi and a swimming pool, a summer beach on the lake will be interesting to lovers of water procedures. Forest lake also attracts fishermen, and in winter turns into a skating rink. Have you ever skated on lake ice surrounded by forest trees?



You can do sports here at any time of the year. There are special areas, equipment rental, billiards. Or maybe you want to fight paintball with your friends, rafting on the Luh River or go hunting? At the recreation center, you can combine outdoor activities with relaxing massages.

Veles

If you go to organize your stay near Vladimir, the Veles Club Hotel is suitable. The recreation center of the Vladimir region is located in a pine forest and provides many services for a comfortable stay here. Residential buildings located on the territory with rooms of different price categories, as well as 17 wooden cottages with all amenities will allow you to choose the option that is acceptable for your wallet. If guests come to rest with children, then cots are provided for babies.



Residents of Vladimir have long chosen this place for organizing celebrations: weddings and anniversaries. But just a meeting with friends or a family trip will leave the most pleasant memories.

There is no need to worry about food. The restaurant complex provides for the possibility of acquiring vouchers for the All Inclusive system or only with paid breakfast. Several restaurants and bars in the recreation center in the Vladimir region will not allow guests to remain hungry.

For the organization of leisure, you can choose both outdoor activities and a visit to the spa, and children at this time will be under the supervision of teachers and animators.

"Nadezhdino"

The modern and well-equipped recreation center "Nadezhdino" of the Vladimir Region will make it possible to have a busy day or vacation with your family saturated and interesting. Its advantage is, first of all, that here you can relax completely. A small number of rooms and guests do not create discomfort and bustle.



For lovers of sports activities here expanse: billiards, table tennis, badminton, various games and walks in the fresh air. The administration of the recreation center in the Vladimir region organizes special walking routes with varying degrees of load. Here you will find pleasant surprises, for example, a tea party in the village from a samovar with jam and cookies. While adults are resting, children will be involved in sports or competitions. Cycling and skiing routes are designed with the possibility of walking around the scenic spots and swimming in the river in summer.

While the boys ride tubing or snowmobiles, girls can learn how to make herbal puppets or amulets. And guests are waiting for interesting excursion trips along the Golden Ring of Russia.

"Pines"

If you are tired of city noise and want to relax leisurely in private with nature, it is worth considering the recreation center "Sosenki" of the Vladimir region. It is located in the most ecologically clean area, away from city noise and smog, from smoking pipes. Forest silence and peace will quickly restore the strength of the body.

Living in a comfortable log cabin, guests can enjoy walks along the forest paths at any time of the year. A path running down will lead to the bank of the Klyazma River, where you can sit on the shore, swim in clear water or catch fish. Hunters will also find here an opportunity to do what they love.



But staying in this wonderful place does not mean at all that you will be divorced from your usual way of life. Just 40 kilometers away is the city of Vladimir, which can be visited at any time. And on the territory of the recreation center in the Vladimir region there is something to do.

Playgrounds for tennis, volleyball, football are almost never empty. Playing billiards or shooting at a local shooting range will appeal to many men. Water lovers can enjoy the outdoor pool or the sauna. As in the best sanatoriums of the Soviet times, there is a summer outdoor dance floor.
