Hotel WaterLoo in Loo: description and reviews

Some kind of French association for some reason arises at the first acquaintance, when you read on the pages of tour operators: the hotel "WaterLoo" in Loo. The address of this hotel complex, however, is purely Russian: Krasnodar Territory, the resort city of Sochi, the resort micro-district Loo, ul. Pogranichnaya, d. 3.





The name of the microdistrict - Loo - is borrowed from the Abazin language of local residents, the rivulet flowing here is also called. Outwardly, it rather resembles not a city microdistrict, but a separate village, stretching along the Black Sea coast. About 10 thousand people live in it. From its continental side, Loo rests on the picturesque foothills of the Caucasus, and its wide strip of beaches indicates a significant resort potential.

Hotel Location

From here, it is 18 kilometers to the center of Sochi, and the villages of Vardane and Dagomys are located in the neighborhood. Loo is built on a fertile place, which has been inhabited by humans since the 8th century AD. Here during the slave trade there was a large slave market, it was rich land. And it was ruled for many centuries by the Abkhaz feudal clan Low, who gave the village its name. The ruins of the Abkhaz-Alan temple serve as evidence of past greatness.

It is no coincidence that a whole block of hotel complexes (including the WaterLoo Hotel) in Loo was erected here. "How to get to it?" - Often asked in a hurry, newcomers. They are in a hurry to get better at this resort, which hosts up to one million people annually. The tourist infrastructure of Loo is not inferior to Sochi.

There are several options. From Sochi airport, where airplanes from all over Russia land, it is advisable to get to Loo by regular bus. As an option, you can first come to the Sochi railway station, and then take the train. In Loo there is a fairly large railway station, located along the route to Adler.

Weather features

The climate of this resort is humid subtropical, favorable for growing persimmons, tangerines, pomegranates, kiwi, medlar, figs. The stuffy, hot summers give way to wet and cold winters. The average annual air temperature in Loo is 14.2 ¬∞ . This resort is one of the few in Russia operating year-round.





In April - May, a revival of the local resort business is felt, and prices rise by 20%. The high tourist season in Loo is observed in the summer months, when the cost of hotel tours, as well as food, increases by 50 -80%. And after a lull from the second decade of October to the third of November, the resort opens its ski season.

About hotel

The transfer from the train station, which the WaterLoo Hotel provides to its guests in Loo (Sochi, Russia), is paid and costs 250 rubles per person.

The hotel area is safe and fenced. It is landscaped with lawns, palm trees, topiaries, seated masters of landscape design. Alleys along it are convenient for walking.

The building of the hotel complex is three-story and has an L-shaped shape, in the bend of which is located the pool and the surrounding recreation area. Arriving tourists are immediately accommodated in the rooms they paid.

In the pool area there is a terrace and lawns.

Service and Facilities

Guests recognize cozy "WaterLoo" - a hotel in Loo. Their reviews mark the conscientious work of its staff, the cleanliness of the rooms and the territory, the active and constructive position of the owners.





The service provided by the hotel complex includes the classic services of a three-star level: 24-hour reception, daily housekeeping and towel change, bed linen change twice a week, hotel guests breakfast in the hotel restaurant, transfer, beach equipment rental, car parking.

However, a review of the WaterLoo Hotel in Loo, conducted by guests in their reviews, revealed that in addition to the standard services, additional services are also provided. In particular, the evening bar is regularly organized, themed evenings (adult and children's discos), barbecue, and animation programs are held.

Rooms

The hotel has two types of rooms: standard (economy level) and superior (classic level). Both modifications are quite spacious. Each room has a bathroom with a shower and a balcony with a table and a pair of chairs. The furniture in the rooms (wardrobe, table, beds, chairs, bedside tables) is new and worn out. Removable bedding is also unworn, it is made from pleasant to the touch natural fabrics.

In addition to the refrigerator, telephone and TV present in standard rooms, the AirLoo rooms (hotel, Loo) are additionally equipped with air conditioning and a hairdryer. Photos of client rooms are not considered for economy class, but the economy comfort class of the rooms offered for settlement.

Moreover, pebbles are somewhat large. The hotel employees periodically clean the beach (municipal!) From construction waste (he was here before) and dangerous stones. However, for the lack of significant refinement of the beach through the importation of small pebbles, one cannot blame the owners of the hotel, because this is the competence of the municipalities.





In the meantime, it is more comfortable for beach goers to walk on it not barefoot, but in beach shoes, and for a comfortable tanning it is better to stock up on a rather thick mattress or use a rented sunbed.

Nuances of relaxation in Loo

Tourists, as a rule, have no complaints about the hotel most described in the article. He is comfortable, his food is wonderful. However, the infrastructure of the village of Loo should be further developed. Hotel WaterLoo in Loo, like other hotels in the region, could attract more customers after solving the problem of dumping sewage into local rivers by local authorities. For a long time, rivers should be turned into another tourist value of the resort, and it will become even more attractive. This all-Russian resort has the potential to become international.





Also, the WaterLoo Hotel in Loo (Russia), like its neighboring hotels, loses customers due to the rocky and uncomfortable walking surface of the municipal beach. On the part of the hotel, work was done to improve the beach. But this is not enough, we need a long-term municipal project for the gradual replacement of large pebbles and cobbles with a more convenient coating for beach people.

Conclusion

Hotel "WaterLoo" in Loo (Sochi) corresponds to the "three-star" level. Families with children and senior citizens like it. Its number of rooms, a recreation area by the pool, food in a restaurant, room and territory maintenance by staff deserve high marks. The guests of the hotel complex are provided with all the possibilities, including transfer and rental of beach equipment, to improve the local healing climate and familiarize themselves with the national color and classic life of local residents.

Prospects for the development of the hotel "WaterLoo" are associated with further improvement of the beach by the local municipality, overhaul of the drainage systems.
