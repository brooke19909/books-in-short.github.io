Hotel Anita 3 * (Montenegro / Becici): photos and reviews

Becici is a small village located on the Adriatic coast. Tourists are attracted by the availability of developed infrastructure, as well as the magnificent pebble beach, which is considered one of the best in Europe. Hotel Anita 3 * (Montenegro) is suitable for economical people who value the quality of the service provided. What services does this hotel offer?

Where is?

This small hotel is located on the coast of Becici . At 150 meters from it there is a municipal beach, along which there is a promenade equipped specifically for tourists. Here you can buy souvenirs and clothes, have a meal with Montenegrin dishes, watch the performances of magicians, musicians. Travelers prefer to stroll along it in the evening when the intense heat subsides. A little further removed the famous beach " Becici ". It is 250 meters away. Opposite the hotel is also the small Splendid Hotel, and a bus stop is 10 meters away.





The central part of Budva is 3 km from Hotel Anita 3 *. You can get to it by taxi or public transport. In addition, a special tourist train runs from Budva to Becici , which stops at each of the hotels. Shopping lovers should definitely visit the TQ Plaza shopping center, which is located in Budva . It is only 2.5 km away. Tivat International Airport is 23 km away. The road to Budva will take about half an hour. For travelers traveling around Montenegro by car, the Adriatic Track has been built. You can drive along it not only to Becici , but also to other major tourist centers of the country.

general information

Hotel Anita 3 * (Montenegro, Becici ) is considered a small hotel, because it has only 35 rooms. They are located in a small six-story building, built in a Mediterranean style. The hotel opened in 2006. Tourists choose it for a relaxing holiday or as a one-time place to stay. The last room repair was carried out in 2013. Hotel administrators speak Russian, English, German and Serbian. You can pay for accommodation and additional services at the hotel with Visa and MasterCard bank cards.





Registration of guests and their check-in at the apartments in Hotel Anita 3 * ( Becici ) starts from noon and lasts until midnight. Here you can live with children of any age. Moreover, cots are provided free of charge for children up to three years old. Pets with pets of any size are strictly prohibited, even at an additional cost. Tourists must leave their apartments between 05:00 and 10:00. However, if you wish, you can extend your stay for several hours if the hotel has free rooms.

Housing stock

As mentioned above, the hotel has only 35 rooms. They are small studios designed for 2-3 adults. In addition to the bedroom, they have a separate kitchen, a living area and an open balcony. The bathroom is equipped with a modern shower, washbasin, large mirror and toilet. The bedroom has a French bed. The hotel overlooks the city and the mountains. Some rooms have partial sea views. Local infrastructure

The presence of developed infrastructure is another advantage for which guests appreciate the hotel Becici Hotel Anita 3 *. With its help, comfortable accommodation for tourists during the holidays is provided. We list the main objects and services that the hotel offers:

  • Private car parking. No prior reservation is required. She is guarded around the clock. Paid, for renting a place daily 5 euros will be charged. You can also put the car in a closed garage, which is located on the ground floor.
  • Paid car rental is available at the reception. This service is available for tourists over 21 years old. A prerequisite is also the presence of an international driver's license.
  • Laundry service is available upon request and for a fee.
  • Luggage storage for large bags. Tourists can also leave their things in the safe at the reception.

What to do in the hotel?

Becici resort has prepared a wide variety of leisure options for tourists . Hotel Anita 3 *, on the contrary, focuses on a relaxing holiday, so it does not offer a wide selection of active entertainment. Water sports enthusiasts should head to the city beach. For a small amount, you can rent sun loungers and sun umbrellas here, and you can sunbathe for free on your own beach towel. The water entertainment center offers tourists to try windsurfing or diving. You can also always ride a catamaran or a banana or take a non-motorized boat for a while.





On the top floor of the hotel is a modern spa , which opened just a few years ago. Outdoors there is a small hydromassage pool. For a fee, tourists can visit the Turkish bath, salt room, sauna and massage room. The beauty salon offers wellness and anti-aging treatments for the face and body, as well as anti-cellulite programs. At the reception, guests can arrange a tour or rent a bicycle.

Positive reviews

Tourists who visited Hotel Anita 3 * (Montenegro) leave mixed reviews . Someone appreciated the quality of the services provided and the price of rest, while others, on the contrary, felt that the vacation was ruined. According to guests, the hotel has the following advantages:

  • Administrators are very friendly and smiling, speak good Russian, they try to fix all problems immediately after a request.
  • Good location. The sea is very close, and next to the hotel there is a good and inexpensive cafe, where many guests have lunch and dinner.
  • The rooms have excellent air conditioning. They cool silently without emitting any unpleasant odors.
  • Daily maid service. The maids make the beds, wash the floors and all the plumbing in the bathroom, wipe the dust. Linen and towels always change on time.
  • Hearty, albeit monotonous, breakfasts. Baking and slicing is always fresh. If necessary, dishes are replenished, so it is simply impossible to remain hungry.

Negative reviews

This is not to say that this hotel will be the perfect place to relax. Negative opinions of guests here are not uncommon . Therefore, many do not recommend staying at Hotel Anita 3 * ( Becici ). The reviews describe the following disadvantages:

  • Some rooms require renovation and cleaning. Paint drops off the walls , and mold is found in the bathroom. Plumbing is leaking, and there are not enough dishes in the kitchen.
  • The windows of the rooms partially overlook the busy road, so you cannot sleep with the windows open at night due to loud noise.
  • Rooms come with old bedding with stains and small holes.
  • The Internet sometimes does not work, and administrators cannot solve this problem.
  • The hotel area is almost not tidied up. There is always a lot of rubbish on the summer terrace.
  • Poor soundproofing of rooms.
