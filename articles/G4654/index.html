Tashkent hotels: prices, reviews and service level

When planning a trip or vacation to the capital of Uzbekistan, it will not be superfluous to find out about hotels in Tashkent to be sure of your place of residence. Strange, but not a single person here can show you the city center, so it is safe to say that many hostels, hotels and hotels are located in the central part of Tashkent.

The growing interest in the capital was the reason for improving the quality of service. Today, Tashkent hotels skillfully combine European standards and national interior design.

Indisputable Leader - Intercontinental

The construction of this 5-star hotel ended in 1997. "Intercontinental" is located in the most picturesque place of Tashkent: on the lake, near the Japanese Garden - and only 20 minutes to the airport. A large role of such popularity was played, of course, by the neighboring buildings.

Near the hotel there is a center for international exhibitions UzExpo, Business Center, as well as the National Bank of Uzbekistan. A water park and Tashkentland are located just a 5-minute walk from Intercontinental, and after walking a few more meters, you can see the TV tower. As for hotel services, there are 243 rooms, several bars, 4 restaurants, a solarium, a steam room, saunas, pools and this is not the whole list.





Prices here correspond to the prestige of the hotel. For example, for a day in a standard room for 1 person you will pay about $ 200, if you two have to pay an additional 50. The price of the presidential suite is $ 3300.

Hotel "Russia"

Tashkent is not the best city for learning the true oriental flavor, it more resembles a Soviet metropolis. It was during the Soviet era that the Russia Hotel was built, now called the Grand World Hotel. A complete reconstruction of the building took place in 2003, but the only thing that has remained unchanged is the low ceilings, which are the hallmark of the Soviet era. Unfortunately, there is no swimming pool, as in the previous hotel, but there is a well-equipped parking, airport shuttle, business center and round-the-clock service.

126 rooms have an oriental design, 11 of which are small suites and 6 are large. No matter what the cost of your room, the freshest fruits will be brought to you daily. Each room has a makeup table, safe, air conditioning, minibar, telephone and luggage rack.



As for entertainment, the Harem club is located on the top floor of the hotel, which attracts all visitors with a wide assortment of cocktails and magnificent views of Tashkent at night.

Tashkent Palace

Many hotels in Tashkent are located in the cultural part of the city, such as the Tashkent Palace. Next to it is the Shopping Center, Mustakillik Square (Independence) and the Alisher Navoi Opera and Ballet Theater. Very close to the airport and train station, such a neighborhood will significantly save your time.

The services provided by the hotel fully comply with international standards: 232 rooms, 60 of which are located on the 4th floor and are intended exclusively for non-smoking guests. Each room has a minibar, free Wi-Fi, air conditioning. It should be noted that the windows overlook the flowering garden (hotel property) and the opera and ballet theater.



If you lead a sports lifestyle, then a daily fitness center is open here from 8am to 10pm.

City celebrity

Hotel "Uzbekistan" in Tashkent is considered the most famous and one of the best. It is popular among tourists from the CIS countries. It is located in the heart of the business world of the capital, and its architecture was able to include Eastern and European style: 17 floors, 278 comfortable rooms, whose windows overlook the Forum Palace, the Timurids Museum and the famous Tamerlane statue.





Each room has a safe, and, according to the hotel administration, security here is far superior to banking. For guests with a private car, a 24-hour secure parking is provided. The hotel also has a medical center that will monitor your health 24 hours a day.

The cost of a single room with a shower is approximately 100 euros, and with a bath - 140.

Where is cheaper?

What to do if you need to find budget hotels in Tashkent? Cheap hostels will solve your problem. For example, you can stay in an art hostel on Zanzhirbog Street 3. The rooms here are not only spacious and comfortable, but also clean. You will also be pleasantly surprised by the presence of a pool. As for the bathroom, here it is common and is available on each floor.



Near the airport there is a hostel "Topchan", according to guest reviews, this is a pretty good place to stay. The rooms here are modest, but neat and clean. The staff is very kind and helpful. If you book a room via the Internet, then a useful letter will come to your mail from the hostel's administration, which will tell you about the sights of the city.
