Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria 4 * (China, Hainan, Sanya): hotel infrastructure, room description, service, reviews

Sanya is the most popular resort in Hainan, attracting tourists with young children, lovers of sightseeing holidays or diving. In the city there are many budget and expensive options for accommodation. If you prefer a comfortable stay at an affordable price, then pay attention to the large tourist complex Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria (Hainan). We will tell you more about its location, infrastructure, food, rooms and reviews in our article.

Where is this hotel located?

Advantageous location is one of the main advantages of this resort hotel. It is located in the picturesque bay of Yalongwan , which is considered a prestigious area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city. The most expensive hotels in Sanya are built here , but the Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria is considered the most budget of them. The complex is located on the second beach line away from the noisy road. On both sides it is surrounded by green hills and mountains. At the same time, getting to the sea is easy - every half hour free electric cars go there. They can be reached by the coast in 5 minutes. The hotel is significantly removed from the central part of Sanya . The distance to it is about 16 km. If desired, tourists can call a taxi to get to the center. Large shopping centers, restaurants and museums are located there. Within 10 minutes you can reach the Butterfly Garden or Tropical Paradise Forest Park.

Another advantage of this complex is its proximity to the airport. It is 26 km away, so you can get to the hotel from here in 1 hour. Taxis and buses run from the airport to the resort. However, most often hotel guests prefer to pay for a transfer when buying a ticket.

General information about the resort

The official opening of the hotel took place in 1998. It is located on a vast landscaped area, surrounded on one side by a pond, and on the other - high hills. The complex consists of one five-story building, which, in turn, is divided into the eastern and western wings. At the entrance to the hotel you can see a huge swimming pool with a water park and a spacious sun deck. The housing stock of Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria 4 * consists of 563 rooms of different price categories and spaciousness. The hotel accepts tourists with children of all ages. Their accommodation is usually provided at a discount. Guests with pets are not allowed here.





Most of the hotel staff are skilled workers who speak Chinese, English and Russian. Also in high season, there are Russian-speaking guides on site who can help with the translation. Registration of guests here starts at 15:00, however, if desired, guests can pay for expedited paperwork. After their rest, tourists must leave their apartments before 12:00.

Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria: room description

Comfortable and spacious rooms this hotel has prepared for its guests. The last renovation in them was carried out quite a long time ago - in 2005. In total, the complex has 563 rooms, each of which has an open balcony and a private bathroom. Some of the apartments are designed to accommodate tourists with disabilities. There are rooms for non-smokers. The hotel offers both budget and luxury room categories. As a rule, most rooms are standard, therefore they are designed for 2-3 people. If you are traveling in a large company, you can take multi-room rooms. Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria also has luxury suites with a jacuzzi on the balcony. The windows of the budget rooms, as a rule, face the pool or garden, the more expensive - the lake or mountains.





In addition to furniture, each accommodation option offers guests the following amenities:

  • individual air conditioning;
  • plasma TV with satellite television;
  • free wireless internet; cable connection is also available;
  • an electric kettle and utensils for tea and coffee;
  • telephone and wake-up service ;
  • radio and music center;
  • minibar without filling;
  • toiletries and cosmetics;
  • built-in hairdryer in the bathroom; slippers and bathrobes.

Employees clean rooms daily while tourists are absent. Change of linen and towels is done according to the ecosystem, that is, you need to put things on the floor so that the maid changes them.

Learn more about the nutrition concept.

When buying a ticket tourists can choose a suitable food concept. Guests can choose between the Breakfast Only and Full Board systems , including breakfast, lunch and dinner. It is also possible to arrange a tour without meals. All meals are covered mainly in the restaurant in the form of a common buffet. The menu here consists of international and local dishes, and for a fee, guests at Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria 4 * can try seafood delicacies and freshly squeezed juices.

The following catering establishments operate at the hotel:

  • Lobby Lounge Restaurant - specializes in European cuisine;
  • Resort Dinner Restaurant - serves Asian and Chinese dishes;
  • The Moon Bar - located by the pool, serves ice cream, fruits, light snacks, cold cocktails, including alcohol.




Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria: hotel infrastructure

Hotel guests can count on quality accommodation, as it has a developed infrastructure system. The complex has prepared for them everything that they may need during their vacation. We list the main objects and services that guests can use:

  • several conference rooms equipped with modern computer technology and accommodating up to 550 people;
  • gift shop and newsstand;
  • individual luggage lockers and lockers for a fee;
  • ATM and the ability to exchange your money for local currency around the clock;
  • concierge and porter
  • round-the-clock security of the complex, in addition, all public areas are under video surveillance;
  • laundry and dry cleaning; ironing service;
  • Private secure parking provided free of charge;
  • wireless internet in the hotel lobby;
  • separate smoking areas.

Beach and Pool

Hainan ( Sanya ) is an ideal place for a relaxing beach holiday. The sand here practically does not contain pebbles, and the entrance to the sea is flat and gentle, so it is perfect for bathing children. The hotel is located on the second beach line, so it does not have its own stretch of coast. However, electric cars constantly run from the complex throughout the day. Hotel guests can travel for free in both directions. In addition, they will not need to pay for sunbeds, umbrellas and towels on the urban beach area. For tourists, there is also a water entertainment center and a diving school.





Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria also has a large outdoor pool. It is open daily until 22:00. The water in it is not heated, but is regularly cleaned. Next to it is a sun terrace with umbrellas, sunbeds and mattresses. They are provided to guests free of charge.

Other entertainment options

But not only a relaxing beach holiday offers its guests Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria. Hotel service is also designed for tourists who prefer active leisure. The complex has prepared the following entertainment options for guests:

  • water sports: non-motorized and motor boats, boat trips or jet skis on the sea, diving, fishing - on the beach;
  • a spa offering a course of wellness treatments, there is also a mineral water pool, sauna, massage room, hairdresser;
  • fitness center with a spacious gym;
  • a tour desk where you can purchase sightseeing tours to the most significant sights of the island;
  • evening entertainment with live music;
  • grounds for playing mini-golf or futsal;
  • rental of mountain and pleasure bikes;
  • paid riding lessons;
  • beach volleyball and table tennis.




Hotel accommodation with children

Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria is a great place to relax with children. Small guests under 12 years old can stay at the hotel for free if they do not occupy a separate bed. Additionally, a cradle can be delivered to the room, if available. In the restaurant, children taller than 120 cm can eat for free. If they are above 120 cm, but below 140 cm, then the food is provided to them at a 50% discount.





On the territory of the pool there is a special children's section, the depth of which is only 30 cm. Additionally, it is equipped with colorful water slides. Outdoors, children can play on the playground with swings and slides. It is located on soft sand and fenced by a low fence. For older children there is a children's club where a contest, quizzes and training events are held.

Positive reviews

Tourists from Russia often choose this hotel as a place to stay. Those who managed to visit Cactus Resort Sanya by Gloria leave positive reviews . They believe that this is the best option for budget accommodation in Yalunvan Bay , and the complex itself is not inferior in terms of service to the more prestigious hotels located here. We list the main advantages of the hotel:

  • clean and comfortable rooms that are regularly carefully cleaned;
  • the hotel does not have fleas and other insects, which is quite rare in inexpensive hotels in China;
  • the windows offer magnificent views of Yalongwan Bay and the mountains;
  • clean and shallow sea and tidy beach;
  • electric cars to the coast ply very often;
  • the hotel is very quiet day and night, and drunken companies of tourists almost never occur;
  • A polite and sociable Russian-speaking employee works at the entrance to the hotel.




Negative reviews

Tourists note that flaws in the hotel are also present. However, they can hardly be called significant, because they will not spoil the rest of picky travelers. According to the guests of the complex, it has the following disadvantages:

  • the rooms are in need of repair, since the bathroom fixtures are in terrible condition;
  • small, monotonous and tasteless breakfasts;
  • in some rooms there is not enough light and smells damp;
  • on the beach, even with a light wind, a red flag is posted that prohibits swimming in the sea;
  • remoteness from the central part of the city;
  • getting to the beach on foot is quite difficult because there is no pedestrian road to it.

Should I come to rest in this hotel?

Hainan Island ( Sanya ) is a great option for an inexpensive beach holiday. The scenic Yalongwan Bay , where the hotel is located, is also recommended for visiting. The complex will be a good option for tourists who want to save on vacation, while receiving quality service. Coming here, it is worth considering that most often adult couples, families with children and friendly companies stay at the hotel. As a rule, the majority of vacationers come here from China, and tourists from Europe and Russia are much less likely to meet.
