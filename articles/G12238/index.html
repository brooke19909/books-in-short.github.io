Georgioupolis, Crete - attractions

The resort village of Georgioupolis (Greece) is located on the island of Crete. This quiet and beautiful place attracts many tourists with the cleanest ten-kilometer beach with golden sand washed by the gentle Mediterranean Sea. The main attractions of this picturesque resort will be discussed in this article.





Location

Several magnificent resort areas have sunny Greece. Crete (Georgioupolis) is one of them. The village is located in the western part of the island, on the coast of the sea, between two large settlements. It is located thirty-eight kilometers from the regional center of Chania and twenty-one kilometers from the city of Rethymnon. The beach, which is located directly in Georgioupolis, stretches along the coast for a distance of about two kilometers. Here you can find everything you need to relax: beach umbrellas and sunbeds, showers, changing cabins and numerous bars and cafes. On the beach there is the opportunity to do water sports and even ride a horse. Seven hundred meters east of the local port, the beach area is divided into two halves by the Perastikos River. This is a very beautiful place, overgrown with lush vegetation, but the sea here, unfortunately, is cold and unsuitable for swimming.





Climate

The mild Mediterranean climate prevails in the village of Georgioupolis. Crete is generally a great place to relax in the open air, under the gentle sunshine. The hottest months of the year on the island are July and August. Summer heat here is much easier to tolerate, thanks to the cooling proximity of the sea. In general, the climate in the resort, as well as throughout the island, is very favorable. The average air temperature in July is +32 degrees, in January the thermometer column fluctuates at around +11 degrees. In mid-summer, outdoor temperatures can reach +40 degrees, but the heat is neutralized by cool northerly winds blowing from the sea. At any time of the year, the resort has warm weather, comfortable for living and relaxing.

Infrastructure

Georgioupolis, whose attractions are described in this article, has a well-developed tourist infrastructure, since it is on it that the welfare of local residents is maintained. Branches of various banks work in the village until late in the evening. At the post office you can always buy a fresh newspaper or call a long-distance telephone. Georgioupolis stores sell a variety of products, from food to beach accessories. The local taverns offer excellent fish and seafood dishes. Live music always sounds here. Tourists can order a meal of fish caught in the local river with their own hands. A weekly concert is given on the main city square where folk songs are performed. Georgioupolis is buried in verdure: eucalyptus trees and cypresses grow everywhere, which delight the eye with its beauty. Despite the hectic tourist life, this place has retained its unique flavor and natural charm.





Hotels

All lovers of measured time in a quiet and cozy Mediterranean resort just need to visit Crete. Georgioupolis, whose hotels annually welcome thousands of guests from all over the world, is perfect for families. The largest complex in these parts is the Pilot Beach Resort, consisting of three- and four-star hotels, equipped with everything necessary. The hotel staff consists mainly of locals, very friendly and welcoming. The resort has small guest houses, such as Poseidon studios, located on a cozy sandy beach and has only eleven rooms. A very good hotel to stay is the three-star Hotel Corissia Beach. It is located near Lake Kurna. Among the four-star hotels can be noted Hotel Corissia Princess, Hotel Orpheas Resort, Hotel Annas House. The most famous five-star hotels in Georgioupolis are Hotel Pilot Beach, Hydramis Palace Beach Resort, Hotel Vantaris Palace.

In addition, vacationers can stay with local residents. Accommodation in this case will be relatively inexpensive, and the conditions of stay (cozy bungalow) will be very pleasant and comfortable.





Fun on the river

Georgioupolis (Crete) offers its guests a range of diverse entertainment. Vacationers can fish in the Almiros River, which flows through the very center of the village. There are special piers on its shores, and fishing gear can be purchased at nearby shops. Lovers of active pastime have the opportunity to go windsurfing. You can rent a canoe or catamaran for river walks. Such a promenade will delight tourists with beautiful sea views and coastal views. To protect vacationers from danger on the water, a rescue service operates around the clock in the village.





Excursions

The island of Crete, a map of which is published in this article, is famous for its attractions, which will be interesting to anyone to visit. The favorable location of Georgioupolis allows you to plan various excursion tours. Using a local branched transport network, tourists can travel without hindrance to the sights of Crete. For this, comfortable buses run daily from a stop on the central square of the village in all directions. You can visit Chania, the historical capital of Crete, admire the ancient buildings of this city, considered the architectural center of the Mediterranean. Or go on a tour of ancient Crete, visit the ancient city-states of Aptera, Polyrrinia and Falasarna.

Freshwater lake

The most beautiful and famous attraction that the city of Georgioupolis (Crete) boasts is Lake Kournas, located three kilometers from the city. The pond is surrounded by the White Mountains. The surrounding area is a local nature reserve. The weather on Kournas is always beautiful, even the slightest breath of breeze does not disturb the water surface . One side of the reservoir is overgrown with lush greenery, on the other a beach with umbrellas and sun loungers is organized. In the shade of the luxurious olive groves, there are various cafes and bars serving snacks and cold drinks. To vacationers could diversify their leisure, on the lake there are rental catamarans. Using these simple means of transportation, you can cross the lake and watch the local inhabitants: wild ducks and turtles. Near the pond is the Kournas Cave, which archaeologists discovered in 1961. A stunning spectacle opens before the eyes of everyone who falls into this world of stalagmites and stalactites.





sights

Several interesting and memorable places are in the village of Georgioupolis. Crete boasts magnificent architectural monuments. In a small resort village there are few of them, but their value does not become less significant from this. First of all, you need to visit the old promenade, paved with smooth pebbles, make a promenade along the fragrant eucalyptus alley, the first trees in which were planted back in the century before last. Excavations were carried out in the vicinity of Georgioupolis, during which researchers found traces of the Minoan settlements dating back to 1500-1400 BC. e. Now there are guided tours for lovers of archeology and history. In a very picturesque place stands the Church of St. Nicholas. It is built on sea cliffs, on the very edge of the breakwater, about a hundred meters from the island. The path to this church lies through a narrow stone path, laid between it and the shore.





Beaches

The main attraction of the resort described in this article is a magnificent ten-kilometer sandy beach. Georgioupolis has become a popular tourist destination precisely because of its incredibly beautiful and comfortable coast. All beaches here consist of the most delicate golden sand warmed by the hot sun. The shallow water makes entering the water in these parts absolutely safe, therefore tourists come here as families, along with small children. On the beaches there is everything you need for a civilized beach holiday: comfortable sunbeds, umbrellas, showers, cafes and bars. Directly in the village itself is Kalivaki beach. This is a very calm place, so visitors like to relax on it.

Georgioupolis is a quiet, cozy Mediterranean village with a developed tourist infrastructure, magnificent beaches and wonderful nature. Everyone who has ever visited this corner of the globe wants to come back here again.
