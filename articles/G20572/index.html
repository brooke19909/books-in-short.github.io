Bosch MAXX 4 washer: instruction manual

The well-known manufacturer Bosh, before introducing another new product to the world, conducts not only many tests, but also uses all available methods to ensure maximum safety. In order for the MAXX 4 washing machine to please its owner as long as possible, detailed instructions are included in the kit for it without fail to help ensure proper care and use.

It is worth noting that for some people it is easier to understand the material presented through visualization, therefore, to explain important issues specifically as a supplement, there are sketchy pictures. But before you start using the device, you must familiarize yourself with the safety instructions and general rules and recommendations of Bosch.



Safe operation

In accordance with the instructions for the Bosch MAXX 4 washing machine:

  1. The device must be connected exclusively to cold water supply. The lineup is intended for domestic use only.
  2. Keeping small children and pets away from the device is not recommended.
  3. Heating the glass of the protective cover during washing can reach high temperatures, therefore it is not recommended to touch it during the process.
  4. You can touch the washing machine‚Äôs control system and the electric cord with dry hands only.

In order to reduce the likelihood of premature failure of the device and harm to the health of consumers, it is necessary to comply with all the operating rules of the device, as described in the Bosch MAXX 4 instructions.

Installation Process: Features

When the washing machine is brought home, first of all, it is necessary to remove the transportation bolts. As a rule, they are installed in the store to maintain the proper condition of internal spare parts. The most commonly used four bolts. To get rid of them, you must use SW 13.





As for the connection, in accordance with the instructions for the Bosch MAXX 4 washing machine, there are two ways in which you can connect the hose to the water supply: using a separate pipe or using a mixer located in the bathroom or sink in the kitchen. In each case, the inlet hose must be screwed onto a special tee. The second part is connected to the typewriter.

As for the organization of the drain, in any case, the hole should be located at a distance of at least 60 cm from the beginning of the floor. Be sure to use a clamp that will help reduce the likelihood of leaks.

In accordance with the Bosch MAXX 4 WFC 2060 manual, the device must be installed at a level. For these purposes, a special construction line or an ordinary glass with water is suitable. The maximum slope should not be more than 2 degrees.

First start

The manufacturer in the Bosch MAXX 4 operating instructions recommends starting up the appliance for the first time without laundry. This can be done with a special starting agent or ordinary washing powder. The composition is poured into the compartment for the main wash and the standard cycle on the Cotton 60 program starts. Such measures will allow to clean the washing machine of industrial dirt and prepare it for loading laundry.





Programs and functions of a model range

The choice of the necessary program is provided here with the help of a universal knob that rotates in both directions. Speaking about the functionality of MAXX 4, with the help of various modes you can cope with serious and old dirt, wash delicate things and children's clothes, sports equipment and even shoes without any harm. And although each mode has its own temperature, if necessary, all the specified parameters can be changed independently.





Separately, you can also initiate a rinse and spin procedure. It all depends on the tasks set by the user. In addition, so that the machine does not vibrate much, the number of revolutions can be reduced up to 600. But in this case, the laundry will be more saturated with moisture. If the thing is delicate, then the additional rinse function will also always come in handy. The quick wash mode is usually used if clothes or underwear just need refreshment. In the instructions for the Bosch MAXX 4 WFC 2063 OE, you can read in more detail a table where the time is indicated for each mode. The maximum washing time is 135 minutes.

Launch

For those for whom this model is not the first washing machine, starting a wash will not be difficult. As a rule, first you need to make sure that it is really connected to the power supply. It happens that due to rush, consumers are mistaken in choosing a washing program. Do not despair, because choosing a different mode is still possible. To do this, carry out the following procedure:

  1. We shift the program selection knob to the zero mark.
  2. We wait a couple of seconds, after which we select the program that we planned to use earlier.
  3. Press start - and the washing machine starts the process in accordance with the newly selected mode.

Care

In the instructions for Bosch MAXX 4, the manufacturer notes that the device will last longer if it is properly maintained. It is also important to use it exclusively for its intended purpose.

The dispensers where the washing powder is placed, the fabric softener also needs to be washed periodically, since over time a coating will form, the powder will not be completely washed out of the compartment. As a rule, the procedure needs to be repeated every four washings.





According to the instructions, Bosch MAXX 4 is not recommended for washing with detergents not suitable for washing machines. For example, handwash powders create a rich foam. Also, you can not use grated finely laundry soap - it can clog the powder receiver.

Only strict compliance with the instructions Bosch MAXX 4 will allow you to evaluate all the advantages and high-quality assembly technology of a leading manufacturer of household appliances.
