Domina Hotels Worldwide

The Domina Hotel Group buildings are located in the most picturesque and popular holiday destinations around the planet and are well known to tourists from all over the world for their high level of service. Three Domina hotels are located in Russia: in St. Petersburg, in Kaliningrad and in Novosibirsk. In Italy there are 10 hotels, one is located in Germany, 7 - in Sharm El Sheikh in Egypt.

The four-star Domina Hotel in Novosibirsk is located 20 km from the airport, in the very center of the city. The hotel has 218 rooms of the following types:

The latter are equipped with two double beds, two bathrooms with bath and shower, living area. The room has satellite TV, soundproofing, and split systems.

The bathroom has underfloor heating, a hairdryer, a bidet, a large bathtub, and bath accessories. Hotel guests can relax in the sauna, jacuzzi, in the Thai massage parlor, visit the gym. According to tourists, the hotel has excellent food.

Hotel restaurant and bar

There is also a Tartufo restaurant serving original cuisine with an interesting interior and large panoramic windows. It accepts orders for various events. And the Brera Bar, open 24 hours a day, offers a rich cocktail menu. Adjacent to it is the VIP Lounge for 13 people.

To the delight of guests, the hotel allows pets.

Wedding celebrations at the hotel

The hotel has become a popular venue for weddings. It provides rental rooms for the bride and groom (for shooting preparations for the celebration), a restaurant and bar with lounge, and organizes exit registration of marriage.

Hotel Domina St. Petersburg

Domina Prestige Hotel is located in the very center of St. Petersburg in a historical building. Previously, this place housed the house of architect Andrei Kvasov, which was demolished, leaving only a modified facade, and in 2012 a modern hotel opened here.

Nearby are the House-Museum of Vladimir Nabokov, a monument to Nicholas I and the State Museum of the History of Religion. You can walk to Isaac Square.

109 rooms of the Domina Hotel with enhanced sound insulation are equipped with air conditioning, LED TVs, minibars, wonderful comfortable furniture, Wi-Fi.

Bathrooms feature deep soaking bathtubs and complimentary toiletries. The rooms have work desks, telephones for city communications.

The hotel is a great venue for business meetings, conferences. Here you can rent executive cars. An airport pick-up service is available at a surcharge. Smoking is not allowed throughout the hotel.

A visit to the sauna, Turkish bath, SPA-salon will help you to relax and regain your strength, and in the fitness center you can lose excess calories.

Food and drinks are provided in the hotel restaurant, bar, snack bar. Tourists can order special diet menus, packed lunches, room service.

The Domina Hotel has an elevator. Pets allowed.

Tourists staying at this hotel note excellent service, excellent food, excellent location in a quiet location in the city center.

Hotel Domina Coral Bay Prestige Hotel 5 *

Those wishing to find themselves in a true Arabian fairy tale are advised to stay at the Domina Coral Bay Prestige Hotel in Sharm El Sheikh, on the very shore of the Red Sea.

The reception is located in a one-story building, and tourists are accommodated in two-story buildings. According to guest reviews, the hotel is suitable for couples, romantic and youth holidays. The beach is just a few meters away. The length of the beach area is 1.8 km. On a huge territory they move in tuk-tuk.

Nearby is a magnificent coral reef with lots of fish. Sun loungers and parasols are provided free of charge.

Guests can use all the amenities of other Domina hotels in Sharm El Sheikh. According to tourists, the hotel can be a great place to relax, especially tasty and plentiful food. Guests are treated to a variety of meat and sea dishes, salads, desserts and fruits.

The hotel was opened in 2003. In the two-story buildings in the Moorish style, 46 rooms with modern equipment: air conditioning, digital TVs and direct Internet access.

The rooms have comfortable beds with orthopedic mattresses and canopies, which are covered with beautiful linens. Bathrooms have a jacuzzi or hydromassage shower. Bathrobes and slippers are provided. Children under 14 years old are not allowed into the SPA zone.

Conclusion

Domina hotels around the world provide comfortable conditions for relaxation. The rooms with all modern amenities and interesting interiors offer comfortable beds, cleanliness, and bathrooms with hairdryers and toiletries.

In different countries, excellent food is organized with dishes of national and Italian cuisine, a large selection of drinks, a relaxed atmosphere and polite staff.
