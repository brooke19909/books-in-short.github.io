Useful hobby: making flowers from bottles



Everyone chooses a hobby according to his taste, but everyone will agree that if it brings not only pleasure, but also benefit, then this is the best leisure possible. In this article, we will consider another option for creating beautiful things from improvised means. Old bottles accumulate exponentially. We throw them away or turn them in for processing, but meanwhile very beautiful flowers will turn out of them . With due diligence and dexterity, of course. So, we try to make flowers from bottles of various kinds.

The stalks

First, prepare the grounds for the flowers. To do this, cut the entire bottle in a spiral - from the neck to the base. You do not need to interrupt the spiral, just cut in a circle by turning the bottle. We get a very long strip of plastic. It needs to be heated and turned into sufficiently stable stems. The heated stem can be twisted to make something like an icicle. Flowers from bottles on such stems will look very pretty. Remember to just pick a green bottle.

Chrysanthemums



Let's start our floral bouquet with these flowers, because making them is very simple. Take an empty bottle of the desired color, cut it with "noodles" from the base to the neck. Now this thread needs to be tucked in a floral manner. It may need heating again. An ordinary candle is suitable for this. The center of the finished flower looks empty. Insert another similar twisted blank into it for density and volume. If you do not have a bottle of the right color, then you can always color the finished flower. Aerosol paint is best suited for this. You can also cover the flower with mother of pearl and decorate with sequins to create a brilliant effect.

Clove

For these flowers, we need to cut a circle from the bottle and cut it into petals. Further it is still simpler and more creative. Using a candle, heat the petals and wrap them beautifully. Please note that the plastic heats up very quickly and you can get burned. Use tweezers or gloves. You will also need tweezers for other masterpieces, so we recommend that you purchase it if you intend to seriously do this. If you are just interested in the answer to the question of how to make a flower out of a bottle, then, as you can see, this is quite simple. As soon as your workpiece with curls is done, we will prepare another one with a smaller radius according to the same principle. We glue them together, and in the center we insert a beautiful bead or rhinestones. Our flower is ready. It can be painted with special spray paint, no one will guess that such flowers come from bottles.



And much more

Cutting out the round petals, curling them with a candle and stringing them on the stem, you get an interesting rose or peony. Similarly, you can make flowers from bottles for a garden or home decor. And if you just randomly paint the bottles, and then cut them into sharp petals, then this will be gerberas, and Jerusalem artichoke flowers, and all that your fantasy tells you. So you can decorate your garden and make it "ever flowering." Flowers from bottles can look so elegant and bright that they are not ashamed to present them, put them in a vase, and decorate their house. Check it out from personal experience.
