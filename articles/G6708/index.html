Scandinavia: a visit to the northern gods!

There are quite interesting places on planet Earth, but the Scandinavian countries have always stood apart - a vacation in Scandinavia is something special, unique, bright. Fearless Viking sailors believed in their own northern warrior gods, fairy trolls, elves and gnomes. And, looking at the fjords of Norway, as if carved by the sword of a harsh deity in a stone monolith, at picturesque waterfalls and mirror lakes, at the endless taiga massifs and sparkling mountain peaks, it is believed that this is a magical land worthy of the gods.

Tours to Scandinavia They are popular at any time of the year and among people of various preferences. Romantics come here to take part in a sea regatta, enjoy the soft light of white nights or watch the mysterious northern lights. And the picturesque villages, ancient fortresses, lighthouses beckoning travelers, coastal dunes and flowering valleys will win the heart of a lover better than the most expensive jewelry.

Tireless adventurers can go kayaking, discover rafting, experience the power and beauty of rumbling waterfalls, and conquer the best snowy slopes.

Fans of a relaxing holiday will especially enjoy the measured life of the Scandinavians, and you can settle in one of the cottages with a sauna to abandon the hustle and bustle of big cities, to feel harmony and enjoy the pristine nature. Do not forget about this type of vacation as fishing in Norway .

But the most popular tours are sightseeing. You will find estates and castles covered in legends, ancient cities, famous sights, secrets of ancient cultures and modern museums ...

The possibilities of relaxation in Scandinavia are endless. You can choose a group tour or book an individual one, becoming the director of your own trip. For example, lovers of unusual and fish cuisine can arrange a gastronomic trip, and families with children spend unforgettable days in children's amusement parks.

Tours to Scandinavia were created in order to feel the positive energy of pristine nature, to imbue with the special spirit of the northern countries and to fall in love with the homeland of the Scandinavian gods!
