Hotel "Liner", Yakutsk: address, description, reviews and photos

Yakutsk is a large industrial and tourist city. Hundreds of guests come here daily. The city has its own airport, which, as expected, is located in the suburbs. Guests and tourists come to Yakutsk for relaxation and transit. Many need lodging near the airport. Hotel "Liner" (Yakutsk) is one of the largest in this city and is located 200 meters from the airport.

It is worth noting that for some this arrangement is a big minus, but for someone a big plus. The article will discuss what services this hotel provides, what interior is in the rooms and how guests respond about it.

Hotel "Liner" (Yakutsk): how to get, address and overview

Outwardly, the hotel looks quite ordinary. An inconspicuous brick building, gray walls and a large central entrance. The hotel itself is located in Yakutsk on Bykovsky street, house 7. You can get here by taxi, private transport or public (minibus, bus). The Liner Hotel in Yakutsk, the address of which was written above, is a few steps from the airport.

In total, the hotel has 140 rooms of different levels of comfort. The apartments are thought out to the smallest detail. The administration tried to open a hotel so that various categories of citizens could live in it. That is why the price range of apartments is so wide.

The Liner Hotel in Yakutsk, whose telephone number is on the official website, offers early booking options. Also, by prior arrangement, you can order a transfer from anywhere in the city or suburb.

Services

Car parking is available on site. Smoking in rooms and floors is strictly prohibited. Hotel rooms are located on five floors, an elevator. For a fee you can order luggage transportation to your room, as well as a shuttle service.

You can book a room in the Liner Hotel in Yakutsk both by phone and on the official website. The administrator will tell you all the information about the rooms, the staff will also explain the conditions for canceling the reservation of certain rooms.

There is a spacious gym in the hotel, which can be used by all guests. Luggage storage and a safety deposit box are also provided.

The city center is 7 km from the hotel. Therefore, it is often quite inconvenient to get to the city, especially if tourists are first in this place.

You can order food delivery from the local restaurant to your room (tea, coffee, breakfast and lunch). It is worth noting that after 20.00 the restaurant closes, and you can eat at a later time at the airport or city.

The reception on the ground floor is open around the clock. You can contact the administrator with a question or request at any time.

Hotel "Liner" (Yakutsk): rooms

As mentioned above, the hotel has only 140 rooms. Here you can stay in a luxury apartment, a twin or twin room, as well as a single room. In any room it is possible to deliver an extra bed, including a crib.

Standard Single Room

Spacious apartment in burgundy (wine) color. The room has everything you need for relaxation and work. Bed for one person, wardrobe, table and chair (workplace). They also have a fridge, TV and telephone. Private bathroom with everything you need. On the windows there are dark opaque curtains for a comfortable rest in the daytime.

The room also has air conditioning and a hairdryer. Above the bed is a nightlight with muted yellow. The cost of such apartments per day will cost about 4000-5000 rubles. The Liner Hotel in Yakutsk, the photo of which you will see below, offers standard equipment in all rooms.

There is free internet throughout the hotel and in the rooms. Guests can enjoy the delivery of food and drinks from the bar and restaurant, the menus of which are in each room.

For those who wish to relax, there is a massage chair and karaoke. You can rent bicycles and have fun.

The hotel has a gift shop where tourists can purchase souvenirs and small items. There is also an ATM in the lobby of the hotel for various operations with bank cards.

Cleaning and maintenance

Room cleaning is carried out daily or if necessary (at the request of the client). Change of bed linen and towels once every three days. If you need more often, just inform the reception or call by phone - linen will be changed immediately.

registration

On the official website of the hotel you can read the conditions under which you can cancel the reservation. It all depends on the specific room. Prepayment is not required at the time of booking. Check-in and eviction from the apartment takes place at 12.00. If you need to renew the room for a day or less, payment is charged per day.

Terms of Accommodation

The hotel allows the possibility of settling guests with pets, but subject to the availability of all documents. When guests stay with children, they will be provided with extra beds, but not more than one in the room. If the child is under two years old and sleeps in a crib, then his stay is carried out at no extra charge. Children under 12 years old sleep on an extra bed, subject to the payment of 500 rubles per day. Overnight for children over 12 years old will cost 1,500 rubles per day. The cost of accommodation at the hotel includes breakfast in the restaurant (buffet).

Standard Double Room

The apartment has two separate beds (per person). It, like the previous one, is decorated in red colors. There are rooms with the same equipment, but in beige and brown tones.

In addition to sleeping places with bedside tables, the apartment has a wardrobe, refrigerator and TV. On the windows, night curtains are green. On the wall (near each bed) are small nightlights that allow you to read when a neighbor is resting. Separate bathroom with shower. Bed linen and towels are provided. The room rate is 4000-5000 rubles per day.

Double room with a large bed

The apartments are designed in a classic style. Dark furniture blends well with light walls. The room has a large double bed with a comfortable wide mattress.

There is a working area (table, chair and table lamp). There is a wardrobe where you can place things. Shower room with towels and bathrobes for two. A refrigerator, a hairdryer, a telephone, a TV and air conditioning are standard equipment in all rooms. Room rate - 5000-6000 rubles.

Suite

This room has two rooms: a living room and a bedroom, there is also a small entrance hall. In the living room there is a leather sofa and an armchair, as well as a working area (table, chair and lamp). There is also a TV with a stand. The bedroom has a large bed for two, air conditioning, bedside tables and lamps on the walls. The shower room has all the necessary things (towel, bathrobe, hairdryer, shower accessories).

The entrance hall is equipped with a wardrobe and a shelf for shoes. The deluxe room has an electric kettle or coffee machine, as well as dishes (cups, saucers, glasses). The cost of this number is 7000-8000 rubles per day.

Apartments

This type of room has a kitchenette in its configuration - the main difference from other rooms. The room has a living room and bedroom. This room has a soft corner with a rounded shape, where you can comfortably sit down to relax. There is also a TV area and a desk. There is a small closet. The kitchenette is separated from the living room by a bar (used as a table). The kitchen has a kettle, stove, sink and kitchenware. The apartments have their own refrigerator, air conditioning, hairdryer and telephone.

The bedroom has a large bed for two and bedside tables. There are blackout curtains on the windows. In the hallway there is a shelf for shoes, a wardrobe and a large mirror. Bathroom as standard. Room rate - 10,000-12,000 rubles per day.

Restaurant "Liner"

This catering facility is part of the hotel complex and the airport. The restaurant is designed for more than 100 people. Banquets and special events are often held here, as well as negotiations and business lunches.

The restaurant is located at: Bykovsky street, house 5. The restaurant is open daily from 12.00 to 03.00.

This place has a pleasant interior and light atmosphere. Many bright colors and light make the halls visually even larger.

A small hall (for 15 people), decorated in a hunting style, is very fond of men. There is a special atmosphere and atmosphere that is conducive to serious conversations. Many people think that such rooms are suitable for business meetings.

There are small secluded rooms for friendly companies and family gatherings. Restaurant "Liner" is ready to meet and serve any guests, including foreign delegations.

As for visitor reviews, everyone talks about decent breakfasts. However, few people like lunch and dinner at this establishment.

Guests are dissatisfied with their reviews and believe that the Liner restaurant is more like a Soviet canteen. This quality of food does not correspond to the interior of the institution.

In their reviews, visitors say that this is a great place for celebrations and celebrations, especially if guests arrive from other cities. Inexpensive, but good service, and guests can be accommodated for the night in a hotel.
