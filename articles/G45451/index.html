Mobile phone Fly FF301: features and reviews

In the era of sophisticated and complex gadgets, button phones still do not lose their relevance. The Fly FF301 model is intended for people who need calls primarily in a mobile device, and then everything else. This device is fully compliant with such requirements: a nice design, comfortable keys, minimal functionality and, most importantly, low and affordable price make it very attractive for undemanding users.





Equipment

In the box with the device, in addition to the device itself, a headset is supplied, which is also an antenna for the FM receiver installed in the gadget. Here are the other accessories the Fly FF301 phone contains: instructions, certificate and charger.

Appearance

The case of the device is made of plastic and has a strict and attractive appearance. The user will be able to choose one of two color options: black or white. Build Fly FF301 is very high quality, the design does not play and does not stagger, it is also worth noting the minimum gaps.





On the front panel there is a display, a speaker for conversations and keys. The buttons came out large enough, so the risk of mistakes when typing is minimal. The navigation joystick and keys responsible for the main control turned out to be quite convenient.

On the back of the device is a camera, flash, also playing the role of a flashlight, and a music speaker. Side keys in the gadget are missing.

The upper part of the device is empty like sidewalls, at the bottom there is a micro-USB jack, a microphone and a 3.5 mm headphone jack.

The overall dimensions of the device are 56.8x129x11.8 mm, weight - 104 g. The Fly FF301 cell phone lies comfortably in the hand and does not slip out, and there are no fingerprints on the matte surface of the case.

Screen

The size of the Fly FF301 TFT-display is 3 inches, the resolution is ‚Äì240x320 pixels. With so many pixels, you should not expect any miracles in terms of color reproduction. Settings allow you to adjust the backlight level - this is useful when the brightness needs to be lowered to extend battery life.

The viewing angles turned out to be very bad: when you look at the screen from the side, it is almost impossible to parse the contents. The colors are very pale and nondescript, so you will not be able to fully enjoy the video or picture.

As for the proportions of the screen, then for such a phone they are selected almost perfectly. The display looks quite large, it contains a large amount of information. This will be a plus when browsing the Internet or any document.





Fly FF301: specifications and technical capabilities

Since the Fly FF301 mobile phone is not specially designed for any other tasks, except for calls and SMS messages, the processor is installed very modest here: its clock frequency is only 312 MHz. However, traveling through menu items and using certain standard options is quite simple and convenient: the system does not slow down or freeze. The processor quickly processes the photos taken by the camera. A nice feature is the ability to connect two SIM-cards at once. Their connectors are located next to the memory card slot. This will allow you to use the phone for communication even more efficiently by installing, say, two cards of different operators or different tariffs.

The flash will fit, rather, as a flashlight, since this LED is not able to properly illuminate the territory on which the survey is being performed. Other functions of the camera include brightness, contrast and various filters: black and white, sepia, and others.

Sound

Fly FF301 is capable of boasting a very loud and clear speaker. Thanks to this, it is almost impossible to miss an important call or to oversleep at work without hearing an alarm. The audio system functions solidly and produces quite decent sound quality for a budget category device.

The audio player plays MP3, but to download tracks, the user needs to purchase a memory card, which, unfortunately, is not included. Musical compositions listened through the headset have standard sound.

For those who like to spend time enjoying the radio stations, the developers have installed an FM receiver. The antenna for him is a headset, so without connecting it, radio activation is impossible.

Applications

A set of pre-installed applications is very scarce: we only have a calculator, a flashlight and a converter. The flash, although unsuitable for lighting images, copes well with the role of a flashlight that can illuminate a small area. So in those cases when you need to find the keys to the apartment in a bag, being in a dark entrance, you can count on the phone.





In the "Services" section there is an option to access the Internet. It is not possible to fully access the worldwide network on such an apparatus, but it is quite possible to look through some simple pages that do not require a large amount of system resources.

The Fly FF301 black mobile phone has three alarms for which you can set your own tunes, there is also a timer, world clock and other unimportant options.
