We make holiday attributes with our own hands. How to inflate balls with helium at home?

Today it is difficult to imagine a holiday without helium balloons. They decorate the banquet hall, hold various competitions with them, they are also attributes of children's entertainment at the celebration. And how beautiful is the rite of launching balloons into the sky by the newlyweds on the wedding day or by graduates at the school evening. What is the secret to the popularity of these vibrant rubber attributes? The fact that they are lightweight, fly perfectly, and most importantly - do not fall to the ground. And all this thanks to helium - a light special gas. In order to decorate the holiday with such attributes, you can contact the companies specializing in organizing events. They will issue balloons inflated with helium for you without any problems. The price of one copy will be about 4-5 rubles. Few people know that you can inflate rubber bubbles yourself. For this, simple and affordable means are used. How to inflate balls with helium at home and how can it be replaced? This topic is described in detail in the article.



Helium Ballooning Rules

In order to inflate a rubber bubble, you will need a balloon with this material. The ball is put on the tube, which is attached to the cylinder. The fingers of one hand hold the tail of the bubble, with the other hand, gently open the tap on the unit. When the ball reaches the required size, the helium flow is blocked by closing the tap. The tail is tied in a knot and a thread or tape is attached to it. Now you know how to inflate balls with helium at home? If you manage to get a special cylinder, then you will do it without problems.



But you can inflate balloons with the helium effect without this material. How? We will talk about this later.

Method number 1

To perform the ball inflation procedure, prepare the following materials:

  • plastic bottle;
  • glass;
  • funnel watering can;
  • balloon;
  • baking soda - 1 small spoon;
  • distilled water ;
  • lemon juice;
  • table vinegar - 3 large spoons;
  • Scotch.

How to inflate balls at home like helium? Follow these recommendations and everything will work out. Fill a bottle with water a quarter and dilute soda in it. Stir vinegar and lemon juice in a glass. Pour this liquid through the funnel into the bottle. Put the ball on the neck of the plastic container very quickly and fasten it with tape. As a result of the chemical reaction that occurs during the interaction of soda and acid, carbon dioxide is produced, which creates pressure and pumps the ball. Then carefully remove the adhesive tape and quickly tie the rubber bubble with thread. Now hold it tight so that it does not fly away.



Method number 2

How to inflate the balloons "helium" at home? Rock salt, aluminum foil, copper sulfate and water can help you with this. This procedure should be performed away from children, as hazardous materials are used in the work. Pour salt (about 80 grams) and copper sulfate into a glass bottle. Drop a small piece of foil here. Now fill the container with water (400 grams). The reaction begins instantly. To avoid burning your hands, put the bottle in a pot of cold water. Put the ball on the neck very quickly. It inflates in seconds. During the time during which the reaction occurs, two to three rubber bubbles can be inflated.
