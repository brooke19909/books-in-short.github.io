Anapa has two hotels with the same name. The first is the heroine of our story. It is located in the city center, not far from the central square. This hotel has five stars on its sign. In this article, we will also pay attention to the more modest "Valentina", which is located within Bolshaya Anapa, and more precisely in the resort village of Vityazevo. Grand Hotel Valentina (Anapa): Specialization

According to one description of the rooms, one can judge that this hotel is intended for an elite vacation. The hotel specializes in hosting high-ranking guests and foreign delegations. Nutrition

Many hotels on the Black Sea coast of Russia offer their customers only continental breakfasts. But the exception to the rule is the Grand Hotel Valentina. Anapa can be proud of this institution, because here dear guests will be offered half board. Moreover, each tourist has the right to decide for himself how and when to eat. He can stay at breakfast and lunch, or in the morning and evening meals. Many people choose this last option, since there are many different catering establishments on the Anapa embankment with a variety of menus and prices.

At the peak of the season, if there are many guests, the hotel practices three meals a day. In winter, it only offers breakfast. Tables are served at the hotel in the restaurant "Freesia", which is located on the ground floor of the building. Tourists leave exclusively laudatory reviews about the quality and variety of dishes in the hotel. Meals are prepared according to the "buffet" system, so you can always choose the right dishes for yourself. In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has a bar with a variety of alcoholic drinks, including Cuban ones. And the restaurant chefs specialize in Russian and Caucasian cuisine.



Infrastructure

Anapa invites everyone to the five-star Valentina Hotel. The hotel provides its discerning guests with the highest level of service. It features a spa. Here you can take a steam bath in a Russian bathhouse, calcinate bones in a Finnish sauna, soak in the jacuzzi, relax under the sensitive fingers of a masseur. Since the specialization of the hotel is the reception of businessmen and organization of business meetings, the building has a conference room fully equipped for such purposes and several meeting rooms in a narrow circle.

Needless to say that all rooms have Wi-Fi? So that customers can maintain their athletic form and keep themselves in good shape, they have a fitness room equipped with the latest fitness equipment. For leisure, there is a tour desk. The hotel has free and secure parking. Tourists often come to this hotel with household members. If you travel with a child, the baby can be left in the care of a nanny.



Pools and beach

The sea splashes only a seven-minute walk from the hotel, and it can be seen from the upper floors. Just go through a beautiful park and you will find yourself on the city promenade. Hotel Valentina (Anapa) has its own guarded pebble and sand beach. It is well equipped with awnings, sunbeds, umbrellas, a shower, changing cabins. The clerk gives the hotel guests beach towels. There is also a toilet and a drink bar.

So that the hot sand does not burn legs, carpet paths are laid on it. Rescuers are on duty all day on the beach. When the sea is cold or stormy, you can swim in another place. In addition to the SPA center, the hotel complex also has an outdoor swimming pool. Fresh water is heated in it (not in the summer). The sides of the pool are lined with sunbeds and umbrellas.
