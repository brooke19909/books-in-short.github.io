Hotel Magic Sunrise Club 4 * (Israel, Eilat): photos and reviews of tourists

Israel is not only an ancient state. Today it is also a country popular among tourists. Most come here to explore the local attractions. And others want to relax on the Red Sea. In any case, you need to choose a good hotel. There are many of them. But I would like to mention Magic Sunrise Club 4 * separately.





Location

On the shore of the Gulf of Aqaba is such a wonderful city as Eilat. He is very loved not only by tourists who adore the Red Sea, but also by connoisseurs of culture. After all, Eilat is a very ancient city that existed as far back as 900 BC. And this is where the Magic Sunrise Club 4 * is located.

This hotel complex was built in 2000. 12 years after the opening of the hotel, a restoration was carried out. There were no major repairs. Everything is already quite new and modern. But they regularly carry out cosmetic repairs.

The beach from the hotel can be reached in just 10 minutes. Although this is not close proximity to the sea, but there are all sorts of interesting sights nearby. For 3 minutes, for example, you can get to the Eilat Promenade, for 6-7 - to the Dolphin Reef.

And another undoubted advantage of this hotel is that to the airport here - just a stone's throw. Drive 5-7 minutes. Of course, some consider this a minus - many people have to get used to the noise of taking off and landing planes for some time. But, however, the apartments here have good sound insulation, because nothing will disturb the guests' relaxation.

Service and Facilities

Magic Sunrise Club 4 * has absolutely everything that is necessary for the comfort of guests, from free high-speed Internet to private parking. Naturally, there is a 24-hour reception, currency exchange, and a tour desk. There is also a room where guests can leave their luggage. Ticket service is also available.

The hotel also has such basic services as a laundry with dry cleaning, an office with fax and photocopying services, as well as a good conference room with all the necessary equipment for meetings and other events.

But of course that's not all. On the territory there are several shops, its own church and a beauty salon with a hairdresser.

Guests can book a non-smoking room or family apartment, take advantage of the delivery of drinks and food, or, if there is such a specific need, ask for amenities for people with disabilities.

By the way, talking about the services, it is impossible not to mention with special attention the staff working here. The fact is that the hotel staff speaks not only Hebrew, but also English, German, Arabic and Russian. The latter fact is especially pleasing.





Leisure

Many people, choosing a hotel for their temporary residence, are interested in - and what can be done there? Of course, Eilat is an interesting place, besides with great beaches. But some people want, while on vacation, to do something with themselves at the hotel.

In Magic Sunrise Club 4 * you can devote time to entertainment of almost any kind. It has a swimming pool and a terrace on which people sunbathe. In the evenings, interesting entertaining shows are held here. There is also an indoor pool. He, unlike the open, does not work seasonally, but throughout the year.

The hotel also has its own SPA-center, which operates a health complex and a gym with modern fitness equipment. The SPA offers a lot of procedures. Guests can check out their full list upon arrival. But the sauna, massage and beauty parlors, a Turkish bath and a jacuzzi are especially popular here.

Also in Magic Sunrise Club Eilat 4 * there is a tennis court and a billiard room. Guests can also engage in any active type of recreation - go kayaking, catamarans, "bananas" or motor boats, go horse riding, go on a desert jeep tour, take time for mountain hiking and bird watching. In general, there is a lot of entertainment. There is even a football and basketball field.

Bars and Restaurants

How well they are fed at the hotel is an important nuance. Given the fact that the Magic Sunrise Club Eilat 4 * serves its guests on an all-inclusive basis.

The hotel has several excellent facilities. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served at The Arava Restaurant. A varied buffet awaits guests there.

There is also Bar Cafeteria - a cafe where guests can enjoy both hot and cold drinks. Also, guests often drop in at The Moonlight Club. This is an entertainment hall where you can taste various drinks (including alcoholic drinks), enjoying the performances of the local music troupe. By the way, he works around the clock.

The hotel also has Lobby of the Depths. It is a pretty cozy and quiet place with a pleasant atmosphere. Guests can enjoy tranquility surrounded by large tropical fish aquariums.

And the last place that can be noted with attention is the snack bar. Various snacks and drinks are served there.





Hotel guests about meals

People who visited the Magic Sunrise Club Eilat 4 *, reviews about the restaurants and bars of the hotel leave extremely positive. All unanimously say - at this hotel they cook very tasty. And on Fridays, on Shabbat, chic festive tables are served here, which are bursting with delights prepared by local chefs.

Local establishments work non-stop, which fans like to eat at night. And the restaurant has a good ethnic assortment of dishes. Fricassee, sushi, falafel, buns with spundra, lamb sausages marges with anise and caraway seeds - here you can try a variety of delicacies. And for vegetarians there is a large selection of fresh salads and even a few hot dishes.

Waffles, creams, desserts, mousses, cookies, fruits, pizza, French pancakes - this is also enough here. Even a table with food for children is available. There are nuggets, french fries, hot dogs, onion rings, corn, etc. By the way, the "children's corner" is at the level of kids, so they can choose their own food.

In general, no one will remain hungry here. This is confirmed by many guests who have been here, assuring that delicious food is one of the main "highlights" that Magic Sunrise Club 4 * (Eilat) can boast.

Deluxe

Apartments of this category in Magic Sunrise Club 4 * (Israel, Eilat) are also quite popular. Their area is 30 square meters. m. Inside - all the same conditions as in the previously mentioned apartments. Only the area is divided into living and sleeping areas. And these apartments are located on the ground floors, so that they do not have a balcony, but a terrace. So the room has a direct exit to the garden.

The cost of a week of living in a deluxe room for two adults (another child is allowed to stay) will be about 190 tons. If the guests are only together, then they can book apartments with the full board service, and this will cost 155 tons.

Junior Suite

These rooms differ from deluxe rooms with a slightly smaller area (27 sq. M.) And a balcony with a beautiful view. And inside there is not one, but two 2-sleeping beds. But at the same time, the room is oriented on accommodation for a maximum of 2 guests.

For a week of accommodation in a junior suite (with full board), you will need to give about 175 tons But if you order "all inclusive", and take a child with you, then the price will increase to 210 tons. If you plan to stay 3 adult guests, you will need to pay about 230 tons And though the price is high, but this option is still the most profitable. Since 75 t. in this case.





Other options

In general, the apartments available at the Magic Sunrise Club 4 * hotel (Israel, Eilat) do not differ from each other in principle. Take, for example, a junior suite for 2 adults and 3 children. In terms of area and conditions - absolutely the same number as the apartments of the improved category (the most budget). But they are designed for three children (under 12 years old) and adults. Accordingly, inside there are two folding sofas and one 2-king size bed.

In this case, you will have to pay about 410 tons per week. And, if two adults take with them not 3 children, and 2, then the price will be 330 tons. In general, the rooms are not cheap, but it is worth remembering that the guest, paying this amount, pays for the all-inclusive service.

Helpful information

At Magic Sunrise Club 4 * (Israel, Eilat), check-in starts at 15:00. And the departure lasts until 11:00. Each guest should be aware that on Saturdays and those days when everyone in Israel is passionate about national or religious holidays, registration may be delayed. The apartments will be prepared for guests only two hours after sunset. In general, for your own comfort it is worth planning a trip so that it does not fall out on any such day.

In Magic Sunrise Club 4 * (Israel) you can live with children of any age. If guests brought a baby (up to two years old), they will be provided with an extra bed, but for $ 12 per day. Do guests come with children who are not 12? Then you have to pay on top of the entire amount another 25% of it. And if a person over 12 years old came with guests, then 40% of the room price will have to be paid for it.

By the way, when making a reservation, you will need to provide your credit card details. The hotel accepts Diners Club, American Express, Visa and Master Card.





Vacationers

Well, now you can pay attention to what travelers say, having been in such a wonderful place like Eilat. Eilat Magic Sunrise Club 4 * - the hotel is not bad and quite popular. And here, despite the rather high prices, it is practically never empty. That is why tourists are advised to go here in the winter season. At this time, Elight, like its surroundings, is as spacious as possible. And, most importantly, prices are falling.

Tourists say that Magic Sunrise is a great hotel for families with children. The animation team will not be difficult to occupy the kids for the whole day. Anyway, quite often there are quite interesting performances that will be good entertainment for adults as well. For example, a carnival with cooking classes, magic shows, boating and much more.

Naturally, many people note the excellent location of the hotel. On the one hand - this is a cozy hotel, located in a quiet environment, in a green area. But, on the other hand, if you want to plunge into the bustling life of Elight, it is enough to spend 5 minutes on a trip to the city center. And there will be no trace of silence. So this place is perfect for lovers of contrasts.

Cleaning

Naturally, each guest wants to live not only in comfortable, but also in clean rooms. Well, in this regard, Magic Sunrise does not cause any complaints. Good maids work here, who clean the rooms every day and demonstrate their skills in folding towels. Swans, crocodiles, bears - what amazing shapes they do not form from them.

Towels and linen are also changed regularly. And stocks of cosmetics replenish. In general, if you need something, you can contact the reception.





Interesting information

Magic Sunrise Club 4 * reviews receive mostly positive. Which is not surprising. Many guests claim that this is not a hotel, but a real separate resort. After all, the complex is located on 80 acres of land, with green spaces and dense grass.

People who came with children say that upon arrival, each baby is given unusual passports. There is detailed information on the activities carried out by local circles. And in each of them, where the child went, a stamp is put in the passport. All children who collected 10 seals receive gifts.

And many guests enthusiastically talk about the local small Luna Park. Children's railway, an inflatable trampoline, electric cars, carousels - all this can brighten up your leisure time.

In general, if we make a general conclusion, we can confidently say: this is a good, but not cheap hotel, aimed at people with children, as well as offering excellent service and amazingly delicious dishes. Many reviews have confirmed this, but if you want to make sure, then you should go here on vacation.
