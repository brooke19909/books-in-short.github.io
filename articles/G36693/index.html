We consider hotels. Ischia is a place to be healed

The large, beautiful island of Italy , covered with tropical greenery , Ischia is located in the Gulf of Naples, its area is about 47 sq.m. There are approximately 60 thousand locals. The tourism industry is well developed here, it is one of the best balneological resorts where millions of people come annually to not only sunbathe on the white sandy beaches, but also to improve their health in the healing springs, of which there are more than a hundred on the island. Thermal complexes and springs with medicinal water have many hotels on their territory. Ischia is a place where you can be healed.



The presence of such a large number of thermal complexes is associated with volcanic nature - the first healing springs were discovered by the ancient Romans. It has been proven more than once that the mud and water of this island help with skin diseases, psoriasis, diathesis, metabolic disorders, neuralgia, arthritis, rheumatism, gynecological and respiratory diseases. That is why in recent decades, Russian citizens have preferred local hotels. Ischia is rightfully considered one of the best islands. And the thermal parks built on it have no analogues in all of Italy.

Also, Ischia resort is famous for its orange and lemon groves, pristine nature, snow-white beaches, warm and comfortable climate. The season opens here in May, and the island is filled with fun. The most visited resort cities are Serrara Fontana, Ischia Porto, Forio, Lacco Ameno, Casamicciola and Barano. Each resort is a separate country, where there are many attractions, gorgeous beaches and luxury hotels.



In total there are more than three hundred pensions on the island, and almost all 4-star hotels have thermal pools and therapeutic SPA complexes - this is what Ischia Island is remarkable for. Three-star hotels are also equipped with thermal springs (but not all), for example, Riva Del-Sole Guesthouse, located in Forio. But if you want to spend your vacation as a privileged person, then choose luxury hotels, for example, Regina Isabella, here you will find royal apartments and luxurious conditions.

But there are also budget hotels. Ischia offers couples economy class hotels for couples with children, where they will offer you good conditions and delicious food, as well as organize leisure activities for your baby. But regardless of the category, almost all hotels have thermal treatments where you can rejuvenate and get rid of diseases. For people with obesity, they will offer to undergo a course of mud therapy and hydrotherapy. The beauty centers have professionals working to help guests restore and strengthen their health.



Hotels in Ischia provide a wide range of diverse treatments. The most famous and legendary tourist complex - L'Albergo-Della Regina-Isabella 5L * - is the embodiment of chic and glamor. This is a paradise for demanding tourists who prefer to spend their holidays in beautiful surroundings. Every year, the hotel hosts film festivals and concerts. Gourmets will have the opportunity to try the culinary masterpieces from the chef Palamaro.

Other hotels are no less in demand. Ischia is famous for such centers as the Grand Hotel Punta-Molino by the sea, II-Moresco with its own healing spring and L'Albergo San-Montano - a natural oasis of freshness. In fact, the island has many different hotels for every taste and budget. You are guaranteed an unforgettable vacation, effective treatment and a charge of positive energy.
