A beautiful and practical accessory is a headband. Crochet learn how to knit such a little thing!

This article will focus on such an accessory as a headband. They can be made of different materials: fabric, leather, ribbons. A special place among such accessories is occupied by bandages made of yarn. They can be wide and narrow, dense and openwork, winter and summer. Each needlewoman will be able to independently make such an accessory. And the master class presented in the article will help you in this matter. How to crochet a headband? Let's talk about this in more detail.



Destination

Dressings can fulfill two functions - a headdress and hair ornaments. The first option should be wide enough to cover your ears. In cool summer evenings and in the spring-autumn period, such a thing will be simply indispensable for the fair sex: you can see heat and a beautiful hairstyle.



Also, a headband crocheted can serve as just a decoration that performs the function of a hair holder, like a hoop. It is better to tie such an accessory with a thin one and decorate with various decorative elements: bows, flowers, beads.

Crochet a headband

This description will result in a wide and warm accessory. The knit is quite dense, so you can wear it in the cold season. For work, we need 30 grams of wool / acrylic yarn (135 m / 50 g), hook No. 3, a bead, a needle.

Measure the circumference of the head and, according to the obtained dimensions, dial a chain of air loops. Next, a bandage on the head is crocheted with ordinary columns without a crochet in circular rows. When the product reaches a height of 8-10 centimeters (you can also make it wider if desired), cut the thread, fasten the knitting, and hide the tip on the wrong side of the work. The base is ready, now let's do the decor.

Knitted flower on a bandage

Dial a chain of 6 air loops and lock it into a ring with a connecting column. We knit 1 row: 5 air stitches, one single crochet through the ring. It turns out a petal. Follow these four more. 2nd row: * one single crochet in the arch of the lower row air loops, 5 double crochet, single crochet *. Repeat the loop combination from * and to * four more times. As a result, you get a flower with five petals. Sew it on a bandage. Attach a bead to the center.

Narrow headband (crocheted)

This version of the product is worn as a hair hoop. To make such an accessory you will need a hook number 2, cotton yarn (different colors can be used), a needle, a decorative button in the form of a flower or beads.





Draw a chain of air loops with a length corresponding to the circumference of the head, and knit one row with double crochets. So that the edge of the product was wavy, we design the product further with the Veerochka pattern. Knit a single crochet column, skip 1 stitch, now do 4 single crochet columns in one stitch. Skip the loop again, and knit the single crochet column in the next. Continue this combination to the end of the row. At the place where the 4 double crochet stitches are made, fans will be formed. Cut the thread, tie it to the other (even) edge of the bandage and knit the pattern in the same way. Connect the ends of the product with a decorative button.
