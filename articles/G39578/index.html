Hotel "Noble Nest", Smolensk: description, reviews

The "Noble Nest" is a complex consisting of a hotel, restaurant, cafe-bar and bathhouse. The hotel occupies the structure of the former noble estate. It is located on the shore of a beautiful lake, among beautiful nature. If you want to take a break from the bustle of the city with comfort, come to the Noble Nest. Smolensk is only 15 minutes away by car from the hotel.





Hotel Description

Being in a former noble estate, the hotel complex managed to preserve Russian aristocratic traditions, an atmosphere of luxury and refined taste. The main decoration of the hotel is an old-fashioned arbor of the "Noble Nest" (Smolensk). The photo below demonstrates it clearly. The hotel has a category of 3 stars. Guests are presented with a choice of 33 comfortable rooms of various price categories. The price includes breakfast, which is prepared in the hotel restaurant as tasty as at home. You will be offered dishes of both Russian cuisine and copyright from the best chefs. In the new large hall, made in the noble style, guests can celebrate weddings, corporate parties, anniversaries. In the equipped conference room you can hold important business meetings. Hotel "Noble Nest" (Smolensk) is a protected facility. Round-the-clock video surveillance. The hotel complex has its own guarded parking lot. Check-in and check-out time is 12.00.





Accommodation

Hotel "Noble Nest" in Smolensk offers its guests comfortable accommodation in the following rooms:

  • Single with a cost of 2500 rubles per day.
  • Apartment for two guests with separate beds at a price of 3000 rubles per day.
  • Double with a double bed. The price is 3000 rubles per day.
  • Superior double with air conditioning. A day here will cost 3,500 rubles.
  • Two-room suite with air conditioning. The price of this apartment is 4800 rubles;
  • Superior double room with two beds and air conditioning will cost guests 3800 rubles.
  • One-room suite with air conditioning. The price of this number is 3800 rubles per day.
  • Two-story VIP room for four people. Its cost is about 10,000 rubles per day.

The hotel allows pets.





Room Amenities

The hotel rooms offer the following amenities:

  • television with Russian and foreign satellite channels;
  • free wireless internet;
  • air conditioning (in superior rooms);
  • held telephone;
  • there is a hairdryer and toiletries;
  • slippers and towels are provided;
  • extra bed or sofa.

All rooms and the hotel are non-smoking.
