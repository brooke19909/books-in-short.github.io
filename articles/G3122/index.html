How to put a ringtone on iPhone via iTunes: step-by-step description, instructions

When buying a new phone, each one sets it up for himself: he selects a picture on the main background, changes the melody that will play during the call, installs applications and much more. IPhone, like other mobile gadgets, has a predefined number of standard sounds. Any of them can be set to alert various events, such as SMS, notifications or incoming calls. But many people prefer to listen to their favorite melody during a call. In the iPhone, the installation of third-party sounds is slightly different from other phones. There are some nuances in this process. You can get detailed instructions on how to put a ringtone on an iPhone, and also find out all the possible ways by reading this article.

Installation methods

There are various ways to create and install ringtones on your phone. In ordinary smartphones, it is enough to transfer the melody via Wi-Fi, transfer it from the computer through the program for synchronization, or simply download it from the Internet. The process of creating and setting a ringtone for a call in iPhones is slightly different. There are several ways to put your favorite ringtone on Apple gadgets. You can buy a ringtone in the store, create it yourself, or use the standard application for synchronizing your iPhone and computer - iTunes.

Ringtone preparation

How to put a ringtone on an iPhone and set the music to ring? In order for the phone to recognize and play a passage of a song during a call, you must first convert it. As you know, ringtones in such smartphones should be of a special format - m4r. Such files are smaller due to special data compression methods. In addition, compression loss is minimal. That is why they are considered the best ringtones for iPhone phones.

Third party programs

How to set any ringtone to your iPhone ringtone? One way is to use special applications for editing music files. It can be such famous programs as Wave Editor or Audacity. Any of them can be downloaded free of charge on the Internet. Open the necessary file in the program, select the passage you like and cut the song. After that, save the melody in MP3 format. Next, the file must be converted to M4R. To do this, you can also download a third-party application or convert online on a website.

You can also go the simpler way. In this case, you need to download a program that can create and convert ringtones to the desired format for the iPhone. For example, NowSmart Cut. It is also free and available for download. After the melody is created and transformed, it must be transferred to the phone.

Web search

How to put a ringtone on iPhone via iTunes? If you do not want to waste time cutting and converting melodies, you can download the finished ringtone in m4r format. To do this, follow these steps:

  • Create the appropriate query in the search engine.
  • Listen and choose your favorite segment.
  • Download the music file.
  • Then transfer the sound to iTunes and save to iPhone.

Phone Sync

Earlier, in older versions of iTunes, in order for an iPhone to recognize a ringtone, it was necessary to perform a large number of actions. Now with the update these steps are no longer necessary. To add an already converted melody to an iPhone, it is recommended to do the following:

  • Open iTunes.
  • Connect your phone via USB cable or Wi-Fi.
  • Go to the "Sounds" tab in the pop-up menu of the phone in the program.
  • Transfer the finished sound file by holding down the right mouse button.

After performing these steps, the selected melody will appear in the list of phone ringtones.

How to put a ringtone on iPhone via iTunes? If you do not want to set one of the standard sounds available in the iPhone by default, this method can be simple and useful. The iTunes Store has a huge catalog of tunes created by both professionals and amateurs. After downloading one of them, you can change your ringtone to 8-bit performance of any song you like.
