"World without Borders" ("MTS"): description, disconnection, connection of the service

Well, today we will try to activate the World Without Borders (MTS) service with you. Unfortunately, now subscribers who are looking for this offer will not be able to find it. After all, the MTS company changed its name a little. Now this is Zero Without Borders. In principle, the meaning does not change. And it is precisely this tariff plan that we now have to connect, disconnect and study as a whole. What are we going to deal with?





Description

"World without Borders" ("MTS") is an offer that will seem beneficial to all subscribers of a mobile operator who travel a lot. After all, they have to talk while roaming. Incoming calls at this rate will be absolutely free, but only the first 10 minutes. Then in a minute give 25 rubles each in international roaming. But you will have to pay for outgoing calls according to the tariff in a particular country.

Here it is worth paying attention that this rule applies to the first and all subsequent minutes, starting from the sixth. From the second to the fifth fee will be only 25 rubles. We must not forget that the MTS service World Without Borders is paid. You will have to pay 95 rubles a day.

The operator to the rescue!

Well, we have become aware of a service called "World without Borders" ("MTS"). There are several ways to connect it. The first one is a call to the operator. On the mobile phone, dial 0890 and wait until they answer you. Next, inform the call center employee that you would like to connect to the option "Zero without borders" (or "World without borders", no difference).





They will tell you the details of the service. Agree with them and wait until you apply. The operator will inform you that you now need to wait for an SMS message with the result of the operation. If you have enough funds on your phone (the connection is free, but the balance must be positive), you will be notified of a successful roaming connection. In addition, please note that the operator can immediately charge you a daily subscription fee. When connecting, it is better to have about 100 rubles on the balance sheet.

Connection request

There is also a slightly different approach to resolving the issue. At "MTS" ("World without Borders"), the connection is able to be carried out by sending a special request. On a mobile device, you need to dial a certain combination and send it to processing.

In our case, this is * 111 * 4444 #. The request does not require any fee from you. After you call the team, the service will be connected. First you have to click on the button in the service menu. To connect, select the unit. Nothing special, right? It is this scenario that attracts subscribers the most. Even a novice user can handle it.

Personal visit

However, sometimes you do not really want to deal with the connection yourself. In this case, you can personally come to the office of the MTS operator and ask employees to connect you with one option or another. Sometimes you may be offered to purchase a separate SIM card with already connected roaming. Refuse and give your phone to an office worker.





Let us know that you would like to connect yourself to "World without Borders" ("MTS"). An application will be drawn up for you. The process takes about 10 minutes of time. After that, wait until the SMS notification about the successful completion of the operation arrives. Ready? You can leave the communications office and go about your business. In principle, this alignment is not particularly popular. After all, subscribers often try to get by with independently connecting / disconnecting services. Please note that you can also refuse our today's function directly at the MTS office.

"Personal Area"

The Internet is a great helper in solving many problems. And as soon as the question concerns the mobile operator, it can turn out to be simply irreplaceable. The thing is that you can turn off "World without Borders" ("MTS"), as well as connect this opportunity to yourself not only through trips to mobile phone stores. The official website of our operator will help in solving the problem.

Go to the MTS website and log in to it. You will be taken to the "My Account". This service allows you to manage certain services and packages from a computer. Its use is absolutely free. Take a look at the Services section. There, find the name of the tariff option "Zero without borders" (or "World without borders"). If you look at the corresponding line, you can notice how one of the two functions will be displayed there. In the absence of a connected package, this is Connect. Otherwise - "Disable".





Click on this inscription. You will receive an SMS message on your mobile phone. It will write a confirmation code for the operation. It is suitable for connecting a service, and for disabling it.
