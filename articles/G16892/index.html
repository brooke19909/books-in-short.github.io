525707 - robot vacuum cleaner from "Oriflame": reviews, description, specifications, instructions

This fully applies to the robot vacuum cleaner 525707 from Oriflame. Reviews of this miracle have filled the Internet. The happy owners of the smart unit enthusiastically describe its capabilities and are glad that now cleaning the apartment for them has turned into a pleasant lesson similar to a game. In fairness, it should be said that on the electrical market there are other models of robots - vacuum cleaners. The prices for them are still impressive, since these smart devices are really unique. The assistant - cleaner from Oriflame stands out from the general series for its low cost, especially when buying on one of the shares that are often arranged by the company. To whom to say thanks for the vacuum cleaner

Everyone knows that the Oriflame company sells robot vacuum cleaners of the 525707 series. But the aforementioned company is known as the largest distributor of cosmetics and some related products. What does electrical engineering have to do with it? Let's make it clear. The fact is that Oriflame is the customer of this series of products. That is, she does not produce them, but only buys and sells them. Move on. If you carefully study the labels on the packaging of the vacuum cleaner 525707, you can find out that this product is delivered to Oriflame, whose official office is located in Moscow, not directly from the manufacturer, but through another company called Trade Express LLC. Its main office is located in the city of Pushkin, Leningrad Region. Manufactures vacuum cleaners - 525707 series robots by Sharp Crown Development, located in China. For 8 years, it has been exporting similar products to different countries of the world. But the Sharp brand, as you know, is the largest Japanese corporation producing the most advanced electrical equipment, and vacuum cleaners are robots as well. As a result, it turns out that Oriflame robot vacuum cleaners 525707, reviews of which are so enthusiastic, are made in China, but using Japanese technology. In Russia, they are purchased by Trade Express LLC. She resells them to Oriflame, and already further this technology falls into the hands of consumers. Here is such a long journey that this intellectual unit takes before it takes refuge in your apartment.



Design

Description of the Oriflame robot vacuum cleaner 525707 in their reviews is provided by all respondents. It is round in shape, the size of a regular plate. The design of this wonderful technique is quite pleasant. The case of the vacuum cleaner is red-cherry, the lid and edging are gray. At the top there is a Start button. While working in a circle, red and blue lights are lit. The lid of the vacuum cleaner is opened by lightly pressing the special latch. Inside is a collapsible plastic container with a volume of 200 ml. Dry small garbage is collected in it. On the bottom of the case are castors, rollers and clamps into which triple brushes can be installed. Also on the bottom side of the vacuum cleaner you can attach a special panel, and a damp cloth to it, so that using your miracle machine not only collect dust, but also wash the floors.

Principle of operation

The technical characteristics of the Oriflame 525707 robot vacuum cleaner are as follows:

  • Powered by a rechargeable battery, which makes it possible to move in any direction at any distance.
  • Charges from the network.
  • The duration of continuous operation is 50 minutes, provided that the battery is charged for at least 4 hours.
  • It can ride straight, turn, follow in a circle, along obstacles, move along complex trajectories.

Instruction manual

In their reviews, absolutely all respondents note that using this unit is extremely simple. Instructions for the robot vacuum cleaner 525707 from "Oriflame" reports that you just need to turn it on. Then the car will do everything herself. Naturally, for it to work, its battery must be charged. If you want the vacuum cleaner to clean both carpets and parquet, it is better not to install brushes. With them, it works well only on smooth surfaces. As you fill, you need to empty the container. It is very simple to take out and install. If necessary, it can be cleaned with a special brush, but if the garbage is dry, just shake it out.



To clean the floors from the bottom of the case, you need to fix the panel, and on it - the wet rag included in the kit. But customers in their reviews report that the robot vacuum cleaner does not do very well with this function, since the rag does not hold well on the panel.

What are the guarantees for the Oriflame robot vacuum cleaner 525707

Of course, having a robot at home, even in the guise of a small vacuum cleaner, is interesting, prestigious and to some extent beneficial. Representatives of "Oriflame" assure that every person who paid money is guaranteed to receive their own vacuum cleaner. But before that, it is advisable to try to figure out why the 525707 Oriflame robot vacuum cleaner stopped working. Repair may not be difficult if your electronic assistant does not turn on or does not absorb dust for the following reasons:

  • They forgot to empty the container, and the garbage filled its entire volume.
  • The battery is not charged.
  • Sensors are clogged (dusty).
  • Some guides broke (they are plastic, therefore not very durable). In order for the vacuum cleaner to work again, they just need to be fastened with high-quality glue.

DIY repair

Clogged sensors are the most difficult nuisance that you can fix yourself. If the problem is in them, the robot vacuum cleaner will turn on, but will stop working correctly (it will start spinning in one place, for no reason to change the direction of movement). No need to panic right away. Try to disassemble the case (unscrew the screw with a screwdriver) and carefully clean the inside of your car with a normal vacuum cleaner.

Theoretically, more serious problems can happen with a robot vacuum cleaner, for example, such:

  • Dust clogged with sensors in wheels (not in rollers).
  • Breakage of teeth in plastic gears installed inside the housing.
  • Battery failure.

If something like this happened, it is better to take the vacuum cleaner to a service center for the repair of electrical equipment.
