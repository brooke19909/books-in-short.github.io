General Hotel Information

It began to function for the first time in 2007. And immediately began to be in great demand among vacationers. He is considered the most original corner in St. Petersburg. Even despite the remoteness from the central part of the city, the hotel offers comfortable living conditions, fascinating and multifaceted leisure.





The hotel officially received the status of a three-star tourist complex. The administration arranges a transfer from the airport and from the railway station, of course, for a certain monetary reward. You will be met in a comfortable car and taken to your destination. In the summer season for tourists staying at the hotel, Fort Konstantin offers a free shuttle bus to St. Petersburg.

The hotel itself has a tour desk with its own guide. For visitors, educational and interesting routes are organized around the suburbs and the sea defense line. An experienced guide will tell a fascinating story about the fortifications of the fortress, as well as about all nearby forts and the Gulf of Finland. Guests who have already visited this facility will respond warmly about it and recommend it to those who crave adventure, boat trips and unforgettable excursions.

Number of rooms





The floating hotel is fully equipped for living. Only 10 double and triple rooms with an original wood look are concentrated on it. From their cabins, customers will be able to watch the emerald bay and the marina. The hotel-fort "Constantine" is suitable for living in cold weather. In winter, heating is turned on.

Each room is equipped with a bathroom with hygiene products. There is a comfortable bed with linens, a small refrigerator and a TV with cable channels. High-speed Internet is included, which is included in the price. The cabins are regularly cleaned.

Supply system

Hotel Fort Konstantin prepares delicious breakfasts for its dear guests. Meals are served in the cozy cafeteria. Lunch and dinner will have to pay, but you will not be disappointed in the quality and sophistication of the products. Service





Customers who come to rest at Fort Konstantin in Kronstadt will not have to worry about the safety and security of their own car. For guests provided parking space is absolutely free.
