Guest house "Lyudmila" (Gelendzhik): description, reviews, photos

Many Russian tourists increasingly prefer a vacation on the Black Sea coast. The sunny and welcoming resort town of Gelendzhik in the Krasnodar Territory is a great place for rich and vibrant leisure activities for millions of our compatriots. Solving the problem of how to relax in Gelendzhik comfortably and not to the detriment of the seed budget, many settle in the private sector, choosing for themselves accommodation in one of the guest houses widely represented here. A popular option for vacationers is the guest house "Lyudmila" (Gelendzhik, Marine, 19).





Location

Guest House Lyudmila (Gelendzhik) is located in the center of the resort. A pharmacy, bars and cafes are within walking distance. From the guest house to the central city beach (sand and pebble) can be reached on foot in 10 minutes. Vacationers are provided with many interesting entertainments: hydro-bikes, parachute, jet skis, banana and boat rides. On the beach for the convenience of guests there are numerous bars and cafes, there are shadow canopies, showers, deck chairs.





Distances

Guest House Lyudmila (Gelendzhik) is located within walking distance to many of the most important resort infrastructure facilities.

  • Sandy beach - 7 minutes.
  • Pebble beach - 7 minutes.
  • The promenade is 7 minutes.
  • The entertainment center is 15 minutes away.
  • The center of Gelendzhik is 10 minutes.
  • To the water park "Dolphin" - 20 minutes.
  • To the market - 10 minutes.
  • To the store (grocery) - 1 minute.
  • To a minibus stop - less than 1 minute.
  • Prior to the "Golden Bay" (water park) - 10 minutes by taxi.

Description

Guest House Lyudmila (Gelendzhik) provides on-site parking and Wi-Fi internet. At the hotel, guests have the opportunity to cook for themselves and loved ones in a specially provided kitchen equipped with all necessary utensils. For those who do not want to bother with cooking, there is a cafe in the house. If necessary, a transfer can be arranged (for a fee).

  • Number of buildings: 3.
  • Storeys: 2.
  • Number of rooms: 7.
  • Parking capacity (free): 7 cars.

For catering:

  • kitchen in the room;
  • common kitchen (2): for self-cooking.

Services

Guest house "Lyudmila" (Gelendzhik) offers vacationers the following amenities and services:

  • organization of transfer (paid service);
  • use of a washing machine (free);
  • safe rental (at the owners);
  • barbecue facilities in the yard;
  • cooking barbecue;
  • rest in the green yard;
  • rest in the gazebo;

  • the use of microwave (in the kitchen).




Number of rooms

Gelendzhik greets vacationers with the richness and variety of comfortable living conditions. Guest house "Lyudmila" (Morskaya, 19) provides visitors with accommodation in rooms equipped with everything necessary. Room Types:

  • "Standard" (18 sq. M.). Cost of living: from 500-600 rubles.
  • 2-room (35 sq. M.). Cost of living: from 400-450 rubles.
  • 1 bedroom with kitchen (45 sq. M.). Cost of living: from 400-450 rubles.

In the rooms

  • Number of seats: 2-4.
  • The bathroom.
  • Shower.

  • The list of standard amenities consists of: the presence of an air conditioner or split system, TV, refrigerator, electric. kettle, dishes, balcony.

Conditions

  • Check-in: from 12 o’clock.
  • Departure: up to ten.
  • Accepted payments: Cash.
  • The hotel is non-smoking.
  • Before arrival, guests must make an advance payment (the amount is equal to the cost of living for 1 day).

Reviews

Guests staying at the hotel are very pleased. In numerous reviews, vacationers note the reality of the descriptions and photos given on the site.

Tourists like the location of the hotel near the sea, shops, market, dining room, bus station, etc.

Upon arrival, guests are provided with an air-conditioned room with TV, a refrigerator, an electric kettle and a small balcony. You can use a washing machine for free, shared by residents. There is a beautiful green courtyard, public kitchen.

Mistress Lyudmila gives visitors a warm welcome.

Guests thank the owners of the hotel for the created comfortable conditions for relaxation and recommend the institution to the attention of everyone who wants to spend their holidays in Gelendzhik as the most successful accommodation option.
