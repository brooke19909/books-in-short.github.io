Gallery Park Hotel: photos and reviews

If you like original solutions, and also appreciate comfort and quality service, then, arriving in Moscow, be sure to choose the Gallery Park Hotel for your accommodation.





Brief description of the hotel

Hotel "Gallery Park" is a special institution whose interior is mesmerizing at first sight. Here, classics are successfully combined with modern architectural and design trends. In addition, you will be provided with services such as wireless Internet, private parking, and a shuttle to the train station or airport can be provided for an additional fee.

Particular attention should be paid to the guest rooms available at the Gallery Park Hotel. All of them are characterized by original design and a high level of comfort. Here you will find amenities such as air conditioning, TV, refrigerator and much more that will immerse you in the atmosphere of home comfort. The bathroom has a modern shower with hydromassage.

Nutrition of guests is one of the paramount tasks set by the capital's Gallery Park Hotel. Every morning in the restaurant you will be served a sumptuous sumptuous buffet breakfast, where everyone will find their own food to their taste. During the day, you can taste the delicious dishes of Russian cuisine presented on the menu.





Hotel Location

In one of the central districts of Moscow is the Gallery Park Hotel. Derbenevskaya 11a is the address of this institution. Walking for a couple of minutes, you will find yourself on the picturesque embankment of the Moscow River. Also nearby are attractions such as the Kremlin, the Mausoleum, St. Basil's Cathedral and others.

The amenities provided in the apartment

Gallery Park Hotel (Moscow) provides for the following amenities in guest apartments:

  • individual air conditioning system, thanks to which your room will always maintain the optimal air temperature;
  • large modern flat-screen TV, which is connected to a satellite dish and broadcasts several dozens of news and entertainment channels;
  • there is a minibar in which drinking water is replenished every day (alcohol and soft drinks are provided for an additional fee);
  • a landline telephone is provided both for communication with the administration and the restaurant, and for making calls throughout Russia and abroad;
  • the bathroom has a modern shower with hydromassage, an electric hairdryer, and also provides all the necessary set of bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • in each of the rooms there is a reliable safe with a combination lock, which will ensure the complete safety of your money, documents and other valuables.

It is worth noting such an interesting feature that every morning, in addition to cleaning, quartzing of all rooms is also carried out, which helps to prevent the growth of bacteria and microbes.





Hotel facilities

Gallery Park Hotel on Paveletskaya offers its guests to use the following list of infrastructure facilities:

  • a restaurant of traditional Russian cuisine, where a delicious and satisfying breakfast is served every morning (it is possible to order dishes directly to your room, as well as the formation of lunch boxes);
  • in the bar you can always enjoy your favorite drinks to the sounds of relaxing music;
  • throughout the hotel, as well as in the rooms, you will have the opportunity to use wireless Internet absolutely free;
  • next to the hotel building there is a private parking area with video surveillance and 24-hour security;
  • if you have any questions regarding accommodation or have a problem, then at any time of the day you can contact the reception staff (in person or by phone);
  • there is a left-luggage office where it is possible to hand over bulky or valuable luggage;
  • if you need to quickly tidy up your soiled or wrinkled clothes, then use the services of dry cleaning;
  • At the reception you can be provided with services for scanning and copying documents.




Positive reviews

Many Moscow guests choose for themselves such an accommodation option as the Gallery Park Hotel. Reviews about this place are characterized by the following positive comments:

  • the rooms are very spacious, and all the details of the interior can be viewed for hours; the hotel has a very quiet and calm atmosphere;
  • Not far away is the Paveletskaya metro station, from where you can quickly get to anywhere in the city;
  • An excellent buffet with a large selection of delicious dishes is served for breakfast;
  • very polite, attentive and qualified staff;
  • daily cleaning and quartzing of rooms, which is extremely rare;
  • if you do not want to walk to the metro, you will be provided with a free shuttle.




Negative reviews

Hotel "Gallery Park Hotel" is characterized by the following disadvantages, from the point of view of tourists:

  • in those rooms in which there are no windows, there is a constant smell of mustiness and dampness; ventilation in the bathroom does not work well, because of which a harmful black fungus forms;
  • the mattresses on the beds are old, with protruding springs; if your windows overlook the courtyard, then be prepared for the fact that you have to constantly contemplate garbage cans, which also terribly stink;
  • even in double rooms, bath and toiletries are provided only for one person (after treatment, the deficiency is corrected);
  • some rooms do not have air conditioning.

General impression

"Gallery Park Hotel" is a pretty attractive place to stay in the capital. It is located in a quiet area near the metro station, as well as major attractions. In addition, it is worth noting especially the architectural and design solution of the hotel. Here, modern style, discreet classics and Baroque luxury are successfully combined, creating a unique atmosphere. The food provided in the restaurant deserves all praise. Of the shortcomings, it is worth noting, except that the dampness and mustiness in the rooms. This problem should be solved by repairing the ventilation. It would also be nice to remove garbage bins from under the windows, which not only spoil the view, but also emit an extremely unpleasant odor.
