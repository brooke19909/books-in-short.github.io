Hotel Narcissos (Cat. A) 4 *, Protaras, Cyprus - photos, prices and reviews

Narcissos (Cat. A) 4 * is a hotel belonging to the beach hotel chain called Aqua Sol Hotels. These complexes are located along the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. Hotels belonging to this network are in different resort corners of the Mediterranean - in Rhodes, Crete and Cyprus. And one of the hotels that belongs to Aqua Sol Hotels is Narcissos (Cat. A) 4 *, which is located in the town of Protaras.





About hotel

It can be said to be an ideal place for a relaxing family vacation. In the city of Protaras there are not so many noisy nightclubs, and those that are available are located at a sufficient distance from the hotels. Thus, it will be comfortable for people who crave silence and solitude, and those who want to have fun at night. What you should know about the hotel? First of all, it has been working for 18 years! And it is definitely trustworthy. For so much time, Narcissos (Cat. A) 4 * served tens of thousands of guests who returned more than once, and also advised the hotel to their friends. The restoration was carried out in 2005. The hotel itself is not very large - 94 rooms, but it is never empty. So if there is a desire to stay in this particular place, then you should take care of booking apartments in advance.





Location

When choosing a hotel, potential visitors are guided by several criteria. And one of the most important is where the hotel building is located, in which particular place. Protaras Narcissos (Cat. A) 4 * is located two kilometers from the city center. Only a five-minute drive away guests of this hotel from Ayia Napa. This, I must say, is a very popular holiday destination, because it was not without reason that they gave it a second name - Cyprus Las Vegas.

And, of course, one cannot fail to note the attention for the sake of which the overwhelming majority of tourists travel to the resorts - the sea. The hotel is 300 meters from the coastline. At any time you can go down to the clean sandy beach. And if you want to see local attractions, you can visit Cape Greco National Park - it takes only 10 minutes to get to it by transport. In general, if we talk about which hotel has the most favorable location, then Protaras Narcissos (Cat. A) 4 * will definitely be ahead of everyone. By the way, the airport is also not so far away - just 50 kilometers from Protaras.

Sports and recreation

Of course, few of the guests stay at the hotel, having arrived at the resort. But sometimes, especially towards the end of the vacation, there is a desire to spend time in the hotel. In Narcissos (Cat. A) 4 * (Cyprus), you can do anything. For example, swim in the outdoor pool, relax on the terrace, sunbathe. Lovers of an active lifestyle have the opportunity to play tennis, billiards or darts. You can also rent a bike and ride around the city, get to know the picturesque surroundings. There is even a basketball court. By the way, here, as in other resort towns, many are engaged in diving. The hotel offers this opportunity. It is worth using it, given that the lessons are given by a true professional in this sport.





Bars and Restaurants

The nutrition aspect is also very important. In Narcissos (Cat. A) 4 * (Protaras) guests are served by two systems: half board and all-inclusive. The hotel has two restaurants, where guests are offered fresh and delicious dishes of European and national cuisine. Every day they expect a variety of treats that can satisfy even the most refined taste. Main Restaurant - this is the main restaurant, which starts operating at seven in the morning and ends at 21:30. The second is restaurant a la carte, its working day lasts from 10:00 to 21:30. In fact, the food is very diverse - meat, bacon, sausages, eggs, sauces, salads, coffee, juices, tea, in general, everyone will find in the offered assortment what he likes. By the way, it should be noted that on an individual request, a dish can be prepared according to the dietary menu. In the evening, guests are offered cakes, fruits, watermelons and ice cream of various varieties. And, of course, the hotel has two bars with a wide range of drinks. Even if you want something else, then there are various cafes in the vicinity of the hotel where you can treat yourself to local dishes.





Accommodation options

Among the 94 rooms that are offered to guests, there are studio apartments, as well as One Bedroom. When booking, be sure to indicate which of the proposed options it was decided to choose. A maximum of two people can fit in a studio, five in One Bedroom. All rooms are tastefully furnished, the furniture is modern, the apartments themselves are very comfortable. The rooms have air conditioning, exit to the terrace or balcony, bathroom (which has everything you need for personal care), TV with Russian channels, telephone. And this is only the most basic. There is also a kitchen corner with tea / coffee making facilities, a coffee maker and kettle, and a fridge. Everything is thought out to the smallest detail - from the safe to the Internet access. True, the latter - for an additional fee. In general, the hotel rooms are characterized by increased comfort and convenience, ideal for a good rest.
