Feldman Zoo in Kharkov: photos, reviews, how to get to the zoo

Many believe that animals are the cutest creatures on earth. On the other hand, they manifest themselves as smart, smart, cunning and active organisms. That is why you can even learn from some species. Like it or not, the animal world is very interesting and unpredictable. It is obvious that we humans want to learn as much as possible about such unusual representatives of the wild. And, fortunately, each person has the opportunity to see some beautiful mammals by visiting, for example, the Feldman Zoo in Kharkov. It will be discussed in this article.

Feldman's free zoo

Under the wonderful city of Kharkov (not far from Alekseyevka) there is an equally stunning zoo that will become a godsend for young children and their parents. It is a great place to relax with family and friends. Feldman Zoo has existed for only two years, but managed to conquer thousands of hearts, as it contains the cutest, most beautiful and charming animals.



Park Features

The wonderful place is open for visiting from 10.00 to 19.00. The uniqueness of the park lies in the fact that the entrance is absolutely free, which is very attractive for different categories of the population. But the park still has its own rules, one of which is a ban on travel on bicycles, cars and other vehicles. Not surprisingly, the desire to look at exotic animals arises most often at the weekend. Therefore, if possible, it is best to visit the zoo on a weekday. Then the tourist will be able to enjoy the company of animals and take a walk in a relaxed atmosphere.



How to get there

Having heard about such an interesting place, I want to know how to get to the Feldman Zoo. We offer several options. The first is to use taxi services, as a result, a two-way trip (that is, there and back) will require 60 UAH. The second option is to get on your own by bicycle. It will take a little longer, but the trip will be richer and more interesting. Of course, there is another way - to walk, but few decide to walk 2 km long.

It should be noted that in the park it is forbidden to smoke, conduct photo shoots and call in on various types of transport. Near the institution is equipped with a guarded spacious parking lot.

Cozy town



Feldman's Zoo is considered the largest in Kharkov. Despite this, it is beautifully decorated. In general, this is a real paradise for children. Small ponds, interesting sites, fabulous statues and much more delight the children. And, of course, the inhabitants themselves are truly beautiful. The zoo management takes good care of them, so the animals feel at home.

Children and their parents will definitely like the stone park where the bear is located, and around it - mushrooms with snails. In addition to places for recreation, on the territory there are also washbasins, comfortable gazebos, attractions for little mischief-makers, table football, trampolines and slides. Kids can also find cartoon characters, an authentic llama, a rock garden, a Russian stove and much more. Naturally, every child will want to visit such a place, and when his desire is fulfilled, impressions will last for a whole month in advance. In this case, the parents have an obvious question: "How to get to the Feldman Zoo?"



Detailed route

You can get to the Feldman Zoo on a special bus that runs every hour, starting at 9.30 in the morning. The most interesting thing is that this is completely free transport, which the founders of the park provide to their guests. Those who are satisfied with this method of transportation need to look for route No. 1656 (AS Forest Park - Feldman Ecopark).

If a tourist decided to get to his destination by bicycle, he should follow this route: Alekseevka - Lesopark - soldier's grave - Pyatikhatki - golf club - Belgorod highway - Feldman's zoo. How to get to your destination by car? We will tell further.



Tourists who decide to drive their own car should use the following route. The course must be kept on the Alekseevsky market. Roughly we go along the district towards Saltovka. The driver must pass the Belgorod highway and after exactly 300 meters turn left. After 200 m you can see the former pioneer camp.

On average, a tourist will need 40 minutes to get to the park. If the driver has a navigator, then the task will be simplified several times, only it is worth knowing that the zoo is located on the site of the former Solnechnaya Polyana camp. Therefore, there is no need to get lost if the system indicates the route exactly to the former children's entertainment institution.



Zoo animals

Of course, tourists are interested in the Feldman Zoo (Kharkov), how to get there, which animals live there and so on. In addition, it is important for some visitors to see something unusual, otherwise the trip is considered a waste of time. Let us dwell on the inhabitants of the zoo.

Arriving at Feldman's park, tourists can see the most powerful and royal animals - tigers. Spacious enclosures are equipped for them, and the animals feel great. If you go a little further, visitors will be greeted by friendly rabbits and lemurs. A feature of the zoo is that many animals can be hand-fed. The cages are easy to open (only with the permission of the employee), and a wild goat or a small pony will enjoy delicacies from tourists. Recently, raccoon cubs have appeared in the park, you can also find exotic parrots, platypuses, unusual pigeons, cute monkeys.



In general, the zoo is one of the best places for entertainment with children, but many adults are also delighted with many adults. It is recommended to visit the islet of nature, both independently and with the kids, to chat with our smaller brothers.
