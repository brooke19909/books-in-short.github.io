Island Beach Resort Annex 2 * (Greece, Korfa): photo and reviews of tourists

In the summer season I want to have a good rest not only respectable adults, but also reckless youth who dream of the sun, sea, parties until the morning and new acquaintances. Unfortunately, in travel agencies of this category of tourists can not offer a large selection of options. Therefore, young people are forced to go to hotels in Turkey or Thailand, but they have a good alternative - the island of Corfu. Reviews about Island Beach Resort Annex 3 * contain information that you need to go here specifically for young people who are ready to hang out at night. This has not only the hotel complex, but also the resort village in which it is located. If this option interests you, then we are ready to tell you about the Island Beach Resort Annex and reveal its main secrets that will help to make the most accurate picture of the hotel.





A few words about the hotel complex

Hotel Island Beach Resort Annex is presented in a variety of tourist catalogs a little differently. Some speak of him as a three-star hotel, while others give only two stars. This causes some bewilderment of potential travelers who do not quite understand what star they still have to pay for. In reality, the hotel positions itself as a solid "three-ruble note", but its guests often say that it does not reach this level. However, this fact is not too frustrating for young people who come here for fun and good mood. After all, in the rooms they spend a minimal amount of time.

Island Beach Resort Annex 3 * is a fairly comfortable hotel complex, consisting of several buildings. They contain about three hundred rooms of eleven categories. Such a large selection allows guests to stay on the most suitable option for themselves. This can be attributed to the advantages of the hotel, because here often come big and noisy companies.

The hotel has a small but well-groomed area with everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Hotel Location

Located on the Island Beach Resort Annex in Kavos. This resort town is located on the seashore and is a place of pilgrimage for young people. If you are not ready to stay awake at night, listen to music until the morning and funny songs coming from the street, then you should opt for another hotel. After all, according to guests of Island Beach Resort Annex 2 *, every night here turns into a real holiday, with euphoria from which the entire resort village is embraced, without exception.

From Corfu Airport, the hotel complex can be reached by breaking about forty kilometers. About thirty meters from Island Beach Resort Annex 3 * (Corfu) is a public transport stop. From here you can easily go anywhere in the city.

The hotel is located on the first coastline, so the sea will not need to go too long. The distance depends on which building the guests are accommodated in. Four blocks are quite distant from each other, so the road to the beach is from fifty to four hundred meters. Naturally, this factor also affects the cost of rooms.





Infrastructure

The hotel has a fairly developed infrastructure, so guests are never bored or uncomfortable here. There are two restaurants and five bars on the territory, and they are located in such a way that each building has its own institution, where you could buy drinks and snacks.

Also, almost every building has its own pool, so some guests believe that you can not go beyond the limits of your block. After all, there is everything you need for a fun holiday.

The reception is open around the clock, which is very convenient for guests. There are safes here, where for a small surcharge vacationers are free to leave their belongings.

The hotel perfectly catches Wi-Fi, which is very important for young people who can not imagine their lives without the Internet. If desired, guests can rent a vehicle and leave it in free parking for any time period.

It is noteworthy that the hotel has a laundry service, the services of which are often used by young people. Indeed, in the interval between night and day parties it is so difficult to choose the time for self-washing clothes.





Additional Information

When checking into the hotel, you need to familiarize yourself with some rules, the implementation of which will make your life easier on vacation.

For example, throughout the hotel complex you can pay with bank and credit cards of well-known payment systems.

You can move into the room with your pet, so if you are used to taking your pet everywhere with you, this hotel is just made for you.

Please note that check-in takes place at two in the afternoon, and check-out must be done before noon. Judging by the reviews of the hotel, its staff is very responsible for this item - gape tourists can be evicted after the indicated hour.





Number of rooms

In the assessment of the numbers, our compatriots did not agree. However, you should not blame anyone for bias, just the condition of the apartments very much depends on the building in which the guest is accommodated.

We will not list all categories of rooms presented in the hotel complex, because for the most part they differ only in the number of beds. However, in addition to standards, the hotel complex has apartments with equipped kitchens. They are often booked by our compatriots.

Each room is equipped with a TV, telephone and air conditioning. But the latter only works at an additional cost. This is a fairly common occurrence in Greece. If you are not ready to pay for air conditioning, then you will have to sleep with an open balcony, from where bloodsucking insects will constantly fly.

The rooms are equipped with a bathroom with shower and hairdryer. Some apartments, as already mentioned, have a kitchenette. It is equipped with all necessary items, so you can cook breakfast, lunch and dinner on your own.





A bit about nutrition

Hotel Island Beach Resort Annex 3 * in Kavos offers travelers to book rooms with included breakfast. If necessary, you can completely refuse food, this nuance must be indicated when issuing a ticket.

It is worth saying that most of the guests cook themselves or eat in the numerous taverns of Kavos. There are a lot of them in the village, and almost all of them are family, which can guarantee vacationers tasty and fresh food.

Advantages of the hotel complex

It is noteworthy that the reviews about the hotel, left by young people and older people, are very different. The younger generation sees mainly only the advantages of the hotel complex:

  • convenient location;
  • the presence of a kitchen;
  • proximity to the sea;
  • a large number of discos in the hotel and in the area;
  • good housekeeping.

Summing up, we can conclude that the hotel complex Island Beach Resort Annex is an economical vacation option, ideal for those people who go on vacation for parties and fun.
