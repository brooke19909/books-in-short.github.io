Technology

All those who are watching the construction of the English Mile are probably familiar with the advanced technologies underlying the concept. It would be strange and surprising if the project did not differ from those of its closest competitors and was not as high-tech and modern as the English quarter should be.

So, the monolithic technology with brick cladding was not chosen by chance at all - it became an ideal option for the harsh and unpredictable climate of the Northern capital. The wall thickness is 45 cm - it is represented by the 20 cm wall itself, a 13 cm layer of mineral wool, a ventilated gap and a facing layer. From extraneous sounds and noise from the busy streets of the city, buildings are protected not only by rather thick walls, but also by high-quality double-glazed windows with maximum energy efficiency. All entrances are equipped with high-speed elevators, the operation of which is accompanied by a minimum level of noise.

In all apartments, horizontal heating was installed, which will save residents from pipes and will make it possible to realize even the most original design idea. Vertical risers are hidden in special mines located on the stairwell, they are freely accessible. The process of hot water formation takes place right in the house, water heaters are installed in the basement of the buildings, providing each inhabitant with water of a comfortable temperature. All apartments have water meters.
