The brightest sights of Dalian

Dalian is a city in the north-eastern part of China, there are a lot of different interesting places to visit. For example, Port Arthur is located 45 km from the city, on the way to it you can enjoy the scenery, see the sights of Dalian, which are mostly devoted to the Russian-Japanese war. The cemetery and memorial of the Russian military are the largest Asian burial places of Russians.

Considering the sights of Dalian, it should be noted the reserve "Ice Valley", whose total area is 5000 hectares. In a valley surrounded by mountains, you can observe pristine forests. Usually tourists are attracted by unique landscapes and crystal water. But the sights of Dalian do not end there - the museum of marine nature is also located here. It was built by the Russians in 1900 in a style typical of Northern Europe.

The Club of Sailors presents to visitors several expensive restaurants, as well as banquet rooms, which are located on the 2nd floor. Arriving in Dalian, the attractions of which attract thousands of tourists every year, you should see the Primorsky Road, which is 30 km long. She goes around the hills, curls with a serpentine. The road combined the Tigers park with an interesting Starfish park .

The area located in the east of st. Binhailu, nine kilometers from the heart of the city, has an amazing landscape. The area in the north is covered by mountains, with amazing sea views. Having looked closely, you can see the Sanshan island in the fog, reminiscent of a more fantastic object.

Binyu Landscape Area is located forty kilometers north of Zhuanghe. From here you need to move to the north-east of the city of Dalian. Sights, reviews of which you can only hear the most admired, are amazing in this place - this area is called "small Switzerland" - stone blocks and a mountain landscape create an amazing and unique view.

You can arrive at this place by public transport, which is a direct flight from the Dalian station. In addition to exploring the picturesque beautiful nature and sights of Dalian, you can also improve your health here. There is a good Medical Center in the city where professionals have been successfully treating all kinds of diseases for several years now.

There is also Japanese Street, where various architectural structures are concentrated, as well as trade enterprises characteristic of the country of the same name. This street was built in 2000 in memory of the Japanese presence in Dalian.
