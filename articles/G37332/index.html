Smartphone Meizu M5c 16 GB: reviews, description, specifications

Meizu introduced one of the cheapest smartphones in the line, called Meizu M5c. In many details, it resembles existing devices, but it has a large number of simplified options, as well as new functions.



Specifications

The screen received an IPS type matrix. It is 5-inch, its resolution is 1280 by 720 pixels. The device runs on a platform of 4 cores.

The RAM of the Meizu M5c smartphone is 2 GB, and the built-in memory is 16 gigabytes. Supports MicroSD Card operation. The main camera has 8 megapixels, the front camera is designed for 5 megapixels. Supports the operation of modules Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, MicroUSB, GPS, GLONASS. The battery is designed for 3 thousand mAh. There is an accelerometer, light sensor, proximity sensor. The phone weighs 135 g. The device runs on the Android 6.0 operating system.

Appearance

Smartphone Meizu M5c is part of a stylish and current budget line. Its body is streamlined, decorative elements are practically absent, the front part is quite simple, on it there is only a Home button. The device most closely resembles the previous Meizu 5M model. Thanks to the plastic case, the back of the phone is made of matte material. The sound and lock keys are located on the right side. At the bottom there is MicroUSB. There are also grilles, under one of which is a microphone, under the second - a speaker. The headphone port is at the top.

You can see massive blocks above and below the screen. The speaker grill is not in the center, so the phone can be attributed to the low-cost segment at the first glance. After all, such a solution is the lot of budget devices. 2D glass is missing. However, due to the flat design of the display, you can see that the device is protected from scratches if you put it with the back up. This is achieved using a threshold that is located around the glass perimeter. Reviews about Meizu M5c (16 GB) confirm this property.

There are no elaborate elements in the device, the black color looks corny, the assembly is excellent. The phone can be attributed to smartphones of a strict type. The design, whose elements are usually used in more expensive models, also plays along with a pleasant perception. Due to the presence of matte polycarbonate, the device retains a good appearance longer. It is also more pleasant to the touch than cheaper aluminum.

Brightness has a minimum level that users like, but the maximum is too low to work on a sunny day. The picture will quickly lose saturation. In the settings, you can easily adjust the color temperature, but this does not give the owner any particular benefits. Because when using this option, the colors are greatly distorted. Reviews of the Meizu M5c (16 GB) confirm this.
