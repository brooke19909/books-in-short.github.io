Unusual crafts. How to make a bat out of paper?

You can delight yourself and your loved ones for a holiday with an original paper craft, which will become an interesting decoration of the interior. So, for Halloween you can make a bat with your own hands. The manufacturing technology is so simple that you can create one toy or several dozen. The unusual decoration of the room where the animals will be hung will be remembered for a long time by all the guests of the party.





What do you need for crafts

Thinking about how to make a bat out of paper, you need to pay attention to the fact that the option presented below will be very easy, and even if a person has never used the origami technique, then you can create an unusual craft without any difficulty.

So, for work you need to have at hand:

  • scissors;
  • black paper.

Step-by-step instruction, or How to make an origami paper bat

First, cut a square from a sheet of black paper with each side equal to 9 cm.

The resulting sheet is folded along one of the diagonals, it is important not to forget to carefully smooth the bend. In the next step, you need to unfold the paper and perform a similar action with a different diagonal.

This square is folded so that the wrong side remains inside, again diagonally. The result is a triangle that needs to be positioned with the top towards you.

How to make a bat out of paper? The diagram shown in this material shows in detail the manufacturing process of the original craft. The upper part bends down - to a height of 2/3 of the height of the triangle.





The next step is to make wings. Here the oblique line is bent to the base of the lower part, which has a triangular shape, about one centimeter. All folds must be ironed carefully.

Both wings need to be bent back, for this the folds are shifted to the central part by several millimeters.

After this action, on top of the craft, you can see small triangles located on both sides. Along each side, which is located closer to the central part, it is necessary to make small cuts to the lines of each wing.

The central part, going between the cuts, should be bent inside out.

How to make a bat of paper voluminous? To do this, select a line located in the center of the body, not forgetting to select lines that run parallel to the wings.





What other option can be done

Even small children will be able to take part in the work on such a craft.

What do you need for work? Here you will need:

  • colored paper;
  • glue;
  • scissors.

Step-by-step instruction

Three colored semicircles of the same size should be cut from a colored sheet. One of these details will go to the formation of the head and body.

One of the semicircles needs to be folded in half and bent the semicircles diagonally. Then unfold the craft, and fold it so that one part protrudes over the other. The resulting protruding corners will serve as the ears of the future masterpiece.

The two remaining parts will go to the wings. To do this, wavy lines are cut out of each semicircle. Then it remains only to glue the wings to the craft and, at the last stage, glue the fangs with the eyes. Both of these details are elementary made of white paper.

The original version of the craft

Thinking about how to make a bat out of paper, you can turn to this method. Here you will need a cardboard base from toilet paper.





The base must be painted with black paint, and to give it a shine, it is best to take acrylic. White paint is needed to make the animal's face. Then you need to cut the ears out of black paper and stick to the inside of the cardboard.

Wrapping paper is great for making wings. With the help of scissors, two wings are cut, each of which should have a bend at one end, it must be glued to the base.
