Smartphone OnePlus One: reviews

The modern market of goods and services is filled with various models of mobile phones. Among them there are amazing flagships that have won the hearts of many people. Today, readers will be presented with a mobile phone called OnePlus One. Reviews of this product and its review will help to understand the quality of the device. How satisfied are subscribers with the gadget? What characteristics is he endowed with? Is there anything special? Having answered all these questions, it will be possible to speak with confidence about the quality of the device.



Description

What is a OnePlus One smartphone? Owner reviews indicate that this is a new flagship mobile phones. The thing is that this product is a Chinese modern gadget. This is a multifunctional smartphone of excellent quality, at least that is what users say.

OnePlus One has everything a modern consumer needs. Its distinctive feature is affordable price. For a small amount of money, it is proposed to buy a powerful and fashionable smartphone.

Contents of delivery

The first surprise that awaits customers is the equipment of the device. Especially when it comes to the Chinese assembly. OnePlus One is sold in two boxes at once. The first is a telephone, the second is a charger. Perhaps the presence of several boxes is the only drawback.

Otherwise, there is nothing surprising. There is no headset in the kit, but this fact has long surprised no one. In the boxes with the gadget you can find:

  • OnePlus One smartphone
  • phone battery;
  • Charger;
  • instruction manual;
  • a special paper clip to remove the SIM card;
  • cable for connecting to a computer (USB).

This configuration ends. A distinctive feature of the flagship is the presence of a universal charging unit. You can charge the OnePlus One battery both via USB (for example, by connecting it to a PC), or through a regular power outlet.



Appearance

An important point for many is the design of the smartphone. OnePlus One reviews in this area earns mostly good. All this is due to the fact that the model has no features. The appearance of the phone can be called standard, without frills. As some buyers say, the look of the gadget is concise.

The shape of the smartphone is standard, the back panel is slightly curved. Despite this, holding the gadget is still convenient. In general, the device is not scary. Some buyers note that the appearance of OnePlus One is not surprising. A typical smartphone without frills and features. Available in standard colors - white and black.

Body composition

The phone case consists of several components. The inner part is made of magnesium alloy. This component is hidden from the user's eyes. The outer case is made of plastic. In addition, the gadget has a metal protective frame and glass.

OnePlus One receives positive reviews because the plastic used is impact resistant. Matte finish allows you to maintain a pleasant appearance. On a smartphone, it is very problematic to make scratches. If they appear, they will obviously not be noticeable. OnePlus One is rough to the touch.

About assembly

Special attention is paid to the assembly of the device. Smartphone OnePlus One owner reviews are often called monolithic. But in fact, the back cover of the device is removed. It is assumed that the user does not disassemble the gadget too often. Therefore, in general, the lid is fixed. But at home, removing it is not difficult.

Of the moving parts in OnePlus One, only the keys and the space for the SIM card. The build quality of the phone is generally pleasing. The buttons fit snugly against each other, there are no gaps on the smartphone. It is felt that the smartphone being studied is a really reliable device that you can trust!



Dimensions

The OnePlus One smartphone receives positive reviews for its size. Not always, but very often. After all, the device as a whole has standard sizes. At first glance it seems that the device is very large. And indeed it is.

Some customers call VanPlus one of the largest smartphones on the market today for such products. Its dimensions are 75.9x152.9x8.9 mm. The device weighs 162 grams. This is even more than the iPhone 5S! A weighty and large gadget that will always be felt both in the hand and in the pocket.

According to consumers, at the very beginning of using OnePlus One it seems that it is simply huge. But over time, the hand gets used. Initially, the usability of the gadget depends on how much a person is used to large smartphones.

Many note that due to the weight of the device, wearing it in jeans or a handbag is convenient. But in summer shorts - no. After all, OnePlus One is a dimensional and heavy smartphone.

About controls

Like most modern smartphones, OnePlus One has several controls. They are provided for a minimum - "volume" on the left side of the gadget, power button, touch buttons (3 pieces) under the screen. This is where all the controls end.

It is noted that the buttons on the smartphone are not too convenient. The thing is that they are narrow, flat and small. Therefore, adjusting the volume and turning the gadget on / off will not be so simple. But you can get used to all of this. Especially considering the fact that the power button is used extremely rarely - it is allowed to unlock a mobile device without it, using the touch screen.

On the left side of the gadget there is a hole for the SIM card. To remove the slot, you must use a special paper clip. It is included with the OnePlus One. Reviews emphasize that such a solution is very convenient - you do not need to disassemble the phone to replace the SIM card.

The top end of the gadget is equipped with an additional microphone, as well as a standard headset jack. Below is a stereo speaker and a connector for connecting the device to a computer and a main microphone.

In addition, the front of the smartphone has a camera, proximity and light sensors, a spoken speaker. All this has not surprised customers for a long time - we can say that in general, OnePlus One has standard controls.

Now a little about the basic characteristics of the gadget. What makes the OnePlus One stand out? A feature that is considered one of the most important is the screen settings. It has already been said that the studied device as a whole can be called overall. Basically it is because of the size of the display.

OnePlus One is a 5.5-inch smartphone. A very large screen, on which everything is visible - this is what pleases almost all customers. You can play on such a smartphone, and browse the Internet, and read books. Eyes will not get tired even after a long work with the device.

Resolution also pleases - 1920 x 1080 pixels with a pixel density of 401 ppi. The display is protected with the optional Gorilla Glass 3 coating.

It is noted that according to the main characteristics of the image display, the smartphone can be called good. The image on the screen is bright, clear, saturated. The viewing angle also leaves no one indifferent - it is maximum. The color rendition of the phone is close to real (natural), there is no bias towards too bright or dark tones. Smartphone operation

An important point when choosing any phone is its battery life - without recharging. OnePlus One gets mixed reviews from users in this area. The phone has a Li-Pol battery with a capacity of 3100 mAh. This is a good indicator for a modern smartphone.

In practice, it is noted that the gadget works well. With the active use of the device, the battery lasts an average of a day. Therefore, you do not have to worry about the gadget shutting down at the most inopportune moment.

Despite this, there are still complaints about the flagship. For example, OnePlus One takes a long time to charge. Without active use, the device may lie for several days in working condition.



Memory and platform

The OnePlus One smartphone offers pretty good specs overall. The gadget has the latest Qualcomm Snapdragon 801 platform , as well as a powerful 4-core processor. Its capacity is 2.5 GHz.

RAM, according to users, is enough for comfortable work with modern games and programs - 3 GB. But to say how much space is allocated for storing user data is difficult. After all, OnePlus One provides models with 16 and 64 GB of memory.

It should be noted that the gadget does not have the ability to connect an additional memory card. Accordingly, before buying, you need to decide how much space on the phone is enough - 16 or 64 GB. The lack of the possibility of expanding the space on the smartphone causes a stormy reaction among buyers - this phenomenon is not all happy.

Performance

OnePlus One owners reviews earns a variety of. Many emphasize that the smartphone has excellent performance. When working with the gadget there are no brakes or "glitches". The phone works quickly, efficiently, without problems.

It is impossible not to emphasize the fact that the gadget does not heat up even with active work. The smartphone allows you to easily view FullHD video. All the latest gaming industry news can be played without brakes or delays.
