Hotel Coral Costa Caribe Resort, SPA & Casino 4 * (Dominican Republic): description and photos

Want to discover an exotic Dominican Republic? Are you looking for a not very expensive but comfortable hotel with a luxurious beach and lots of entertainment? Then Coral Costa Caribe Resort, SPA & Casino 4 * may be a suitable option for you. We offer you to find out what awaits tourists here.





Location

This hotel is located on the coast of the azure Caribbean Sea, on the beach of Juan Dolio. Nearby is the city of San Pedro de Macoris. Santo Domingo, the capital, is a 45-minute drive away. The journey from Punta Cana Airport takes about three hours. The hotel has its own sandy, well-equipped beach. Near the hotel there are shops, souvenir shops, pharmacies and other infrastructure. As for interesting places near the hotel, Los Marlins is 1.4 km, Playa Guayacanes Beach is 4.5 km, Guavaberry Golf and Country Club is 7 km, and Tetelo Vargas Stadium is 14 km away. and 24 km from Boca Chica.



general information

Coral Costa Caribe Resort, SPA & Casino, photos of which can be seen in this article, until April 2014 was called the Costa Caribbean Coral by Hilton. Now it belongs to a large chain of hotels "Coral".

The hotel has a large beautiful territory with a tropical garden, bright colors, picturesque bridges and figures. According to tourists, walking here is a pleasure. In addition, here you will find a lot of places in order to make magnificent photos in memory of a vacation in the Dominican Republic.

The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 548 rooms located in the main seven-story and additional four-story buildings. All buildings are equipped with elevators for the convenience of guests. The hotel has several pools with sun terraces, sports fields, a tour desk, a rental car, and a mini club. Also, tourists are offered both daytime and evening animation (there are programs for both adults and children).

Housing stock

As already mentioned, the Coral Costa Caribbean Resort, SPA & Casino Hotel (Dominican Republic) offers everyone to stay in one of 548 comfortable rooms located in modern seven- and four-story buildings with elevators. The apartments here are presented in superior and deluxe categories with sea or tropical garden views, as well as suites and junior suites located near the beach. Regardless of the type, all rooms are freshly renovated and decorated in a pleasant style. They have everything that guests may need during their vacation: comfortable beautiful furniture, air conditioning, ceiling fan, TV with cable channels (including in Russian), safe, coffee maker, tea set, iron and ironing board, balcony or terrace. Please note that most rooms do not have a refrigerator by default. However, if necessary, they will provide it to you (it is advisable to ask the hotel in advance).

A big plus, according to many tourists, is the fact that throughout the entire Coral Costa Caribe Resort, SPA & Casino 4 * (including rooms and the beach), guests can use free wireless Internet.
