Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey): description, service, photos and reviews

When planning an annual vacation or a romantic weekend, many prefer one of the most prestigious resorts in Turkey - Marmaris. Here you can not only soak up the rays of the scorching sun, but also diversify your vacation by visiting a number of historical attractions. The four-star hotel located here - Seray Deluxe Hotel offers its guests comfortable accommodation.

general characteristics

The opening of the Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * hotel complex, the description of which will be presented to your attention in the article, took place at the border of two millennia - in 2000. Over the years of its existence, many updates and renovations have been carried out, the last of which was implemented in 2012. On a compact territory of just over two thousand square meters, the main and only building with an aquazone is located. For the convenience of vacationers, it is equipped with an elevator. The exterior of the five-story building has a unique modern design that impresses guests at a glance.

To check into the hotel after the flight, it is additionally required to overcome the distance of 95 km. This does not upset tourists, because visually familiar with these warm edges to everyone's liking. The center of the tourist Marmaris - 1 kilometer.

Pets are not allowed in the house. There are no rooms for people with disabilities. Smoking is allowed only in special areas. Highchairs in restaurants - in the public domain. A cot in the room is available on request.





Accommodation

The room fund of the hotel Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Turkey) consists of 78 superior comfort rooms belonging to the Standard category. The view from the windows falls on the courtyard and the garden. Rooms are just over 20 square meters. The maximum number of people who can fit here is 3 people.

The interior has a pronounced strict style. Luxurious decoration will allow each guest to feel like an aristocrat in a chic castle. White shades predominate in the color scheme, which are diluted with the saturated gray color of curtains, pillows and bedspreads. The walls are hung with paintings, unique sconces and a dimensional mirror. The floors are covered with laminate.

All apartments have a balcony, where there are comfortable plastic chairs with a table for taking air baths. Cleaning and maintaining order is the daily duty of conscientious maids. Linen is changed three times a week. You can order food delivery to the rooms or wake-up service at the appointed time.





Equipment

The furniture set consists of a bed, chairs, a small coffee table, a countertop under the TV, a wardrobe and bedside tables. An individual split system is designed to create a favorable microclimate for each guest at the Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Marmaris). A flat-screen TV allows you to keep abreast of all current events in the world. The phone, thanks to which there is instant communication with the reception, contributes to the immediate solution of all problems. Use of the safe requires an additional charge. It allows you not to worry about the safety of important documents and financial resources. The minibar is empty at check-in, its replenishment is paid. Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel.

The bathroom has a shower and hairdryer. On the sink is a complete set of personal care products, represented by soap, shampoo, gel, balm and lotion. Taking a shower here is a real celebration for every guest.





Satisfying hunger

The concept of the organization of four meals a day, which adheres to the hotel Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 *, is "All inclusive." Vacationers are invited to independently compose their diet from the available dishes, which are represented by international and local cuisines. Excellent chefs produce real culinary masterpieces that are able to satisfy the needs of even the most capricious guests. The main restaurant has an indoor hall for 150 people and an open-air terrace for 250 visitors. The interior looks expensive: numerous spotlights, wall sconces, marble floors and wooden furniture. This design, as noted by tourists, has to satisfy the hunger and friendly atmosphere.

To quench their thirst, travelers are invited to visit two bars. The offered soft alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks will please with their quality and taste characteristics.

There is also a restaurant, which requires additional payment. The menu of the offered dishes can surprise every guest. Dress code required.





Beach

The coast is about 100 meters away. To overcome this distance under the force of both adults and children. On the way there will be a carriageway without an underpass. The sandy beach is public, but the infrastructure is developed and is at a decent level. There are sun loungers with mattresses for rent under umbrellas overlooking the mountain hills drowning in the abyss of water. The entrance to the sea is gentle, which allows children to frolic in the warm coastal waters of the Mediterranean.

The safety of water procedures is constantly monitored by the rescue service, ready at any moment to come to the rescue. There is security that ensures the order and tranquility of sunbathers.

You can entertain yourself by playing volleyball in the company of friendly neighbors or have fun on water rides, the most popular of which are canoeing, ring riding and scooters.

No less popular are diving and snorkeling. Everyone can learn the bottomless world of the unique flora with its inhabitants, after passing a preliminary briefing.





Aquazone

To maintain health and increase immunity, there is an outdoor swimming pool. Its area is 62 meters squared. It is surrounded by sun loungers with umbrellas. Filled with fresh water, without heating system. On the part of its perimeter there is a high fragrant landscaped fence. Towels are provided free of charge. The steps to enter the water are incredibly convenient.

A miniature shallow pool of six squares is provided for younger guests. However, the ability to splash around in the open air allows kids to recover, physically develop and become tempered.





Toddler Leisure

For a rich and lively pastime of children at the hotel complex Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) there is a children's playground. It is equipped with swings, carousels, slides and labyrinths. Here the kids spend their energy, amusing themselves with their peers, organize competitions to identify the most agile and fast participant, and simply spend their leisure time joyfully and cheerfully.

Sports and Entertainment

In addition to fascinating excursions, which are offered an uncountable amount, vacationers can diversify their pastime by playing darts, billiards or chess. Having rented a bicycle, holidaymakers go on a tour of the picturesque surroundings. An animation program is provided once a week. It includes Turkish night and live music in the restaurant.





Additional services

Laundry or dry cleaning services are available at the reception. Packed lunches are available for guests. The lobby is equipped with comfortable seating with tables for watching shows on a plasma TV. Here you can chat, play board games, making new friends.

Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 *: reviews

This hotel has changed its recent three stars to four. As experienced in their reviews, this is completely justified. The staff is always trying to please the guests. The atmosphere is sincere, everything is at home. Cleanliness, as many reviews say, is top notch. The proximity to the city center is to the liking of every spa person. In general, as guests note, Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * service offers a high level. There were no serious complaints. Many tourists recommend the hotel to their friends and acquaintances.
