Tariff "Everything is simple" ("Megaphone"): reviews and description

The mobile operator Megafon offers its subscribers a number of different tariff plans. Do you prefer to deposit money into your account once a month and not think about the balance during this period? Then choose the tariffs with the included minutes, messages, megabytes (or connect additional options). Prefer to pay upon the use of communication services? Then we bring to your attention the tariff "Everything is simple" ("Megaphone"), reviews of which other subscribers are mostly positive. Many customers are satisfied with the simplicity and extremely clear terms of use. In this article we will provide a description of this tariff plan and tell you why subscribers are happy to switch to it.





Features of TP "Everything is simple"

The tariff "Everything is simple" ("Megaphone"), reviews of which are found in a fairly large number, are distinguished by transparent conditions. In addition, on it:

  • there is no subscription fee - money is debited from the account only after using communication services;
  • fixed cost per minute of a call when making calls to any numbers in the home region (cellular and landline);
  • it is possible to activate the service “Per Second Billing” (by default, the connection duration is rounded up to minutes) for more beneficial communication and economical spending of funds on the room.

Many subscribers are motivated to switch to the “Everything is Simple” (“Megaphone”) tariff; reviews (mobile, Internet services, etc., users give a very high rating). Existing users of this tariff note not only profitable calls in their region, but also low prices for calls and sending text messages in Russia.





Description of TP “Everything is simple”

The conditions of the tariff under consideration are really simple "to the ugliness". You can call the number of any mobile operator providing services in the home region for 1.6 rubles. (per minute). These may be Tele2, VimpelCom, MTS, etc. subscribers. Please note that a call to a city ​​number in your region will also cost 1.60 rubles. per minute of conversation. The cost of sending a text message is 1.90 rubles. (3 rubles in Russia, 5 rubles to the CIS countries, Georgia, South Ossetia, Abkhazia, Ukraine). The tariff plan "Everything is simple" is perfect for those who have to call long distance. The cost of a minute of conversation is 3.9 rubles. (which is 3 times more profitable than with any other TP of this operator).

Be careful, as the cost of communication services may vary by region.

Internet cost

Accessible Internet with the XS package is included in the “Everything is Simple” (“Megaphone”) tariff, reviews described in this article by default. When switching to TP or purchasing a new SIM card, the option “XS-Internet” is activated automatically. The first week it is provided free of charge. After seven days, a monthly fee of 7 rubles will be debited. If the subscriber does not use the Internet and does not use more than 499 Kb during the promotional period, the package will be disabled automatically. After that, the cost of 1 megabyte will be 9.90 rubles.





Conditions for switching to the “Everything is simple” tariff

Before you switch to the selected tariff plan, you must deposit 300 rubles into the account. Despite the fact that the change in TP in this case is not charged, the amount must be present on the account not less than the previously indicated. In the future, these funds can be used to pay for communication services (calls, messages, Internet).





Tariff activation methods

If the subscriber needs to change the tariff plan, he can use any of the following methods.

  1. Sending a USSD request.
  2. Call / send SMS to service number.
  3. System "Service Guide".

In addition, there is always the opportunity to contact the support service and use the services of a specialist to carry out the necessary operation, in this case, to change the tariff plan.

Change TP from phone

Tariff “Everything is simple” (“Megaphone”): how to connect? Reviews about this TP are mostly positive. That is why subscribers are increasingly thinking about its activation. Via a mobile phone, this can be done through the USSD request * 105 * 0041 # by sending an empty message to service number 0500941 or by calling this number. Following the prompts of the system, you can change the tariff in a matter of minutes. Please note that if the required amount (300 rubles) is not available on the balance sheet, then it will not work to transfer to TP “Everything is simple”.

Change of TP through "Service Guide"

Through the Internet assistant - the Service Guide system - you can also activate the “Everything is simple” tariff (“Megaphone”). Reviews (communications, Internet) of current subscribers allow us to conclude that TP is optimal and is suitable for profitable communication, including inter-city calls. Using the self-service system offered by Megafon, you can perform any operations with a number, including changing the tariff. To use the Service Guide service, you need to go to the official portal of the Megafon company and click on the "My Account" link. Here, after authorization, you should select the item “Change tariff”, find “Everything is simple” in the list and confirm the change of TP. The changes will take effect in a few minutes. Please note that if “Everything is simple” is not in the list of available tariff plans, it means that it is impossible to switch to it from this number. The reason for this situation can be clarified with the customer service operator.

Tariff "Everything is simple" ("Megaphone"): customer reviews

For subscribers who do not like tariff plans with a monthly fee, this TP is really a godsend. Despite the fact that there are no included minute packages, SMS messages or Internet traffic, it is popular. After all, you need to pay for communication services only after the fact: talked, used the Internet - paid. Earlier, the MegaFon tariff “Easier than Easy” was in such demand (reviews about which it will not be possible to read, since it is in the archive), which also had fixed prices for certain communication services. Why is “Everything is Simple” TP suitable for many subscribers, what are its advantages?

  • No need to make monthly payments.
  • Write-off of funds occurs only after the commission of any paid action on the part of the subscriber.
  • Favorable calls both in the home region and to long-distance numbers (mobile and landline).
  • The bonus may be the already connected option “XS” (under its terms, for a daily subscription, 115 Mb of Internet is provided at maximum speed; after the limit has been reached, a speed limit of 64 Kb / s is set).

Conclusion

The “Everything is Simple” tariff plan will be a good choice for subscribers who carefully monitor their budget and do not accept the additional costs. You can go to this TP in any convenient way (via the Internet, using a mobile phone, through office or customer service staff). You can also purchase a new number with this tariff plan from authorized dealers, in Megafon sales salons or through an online store, on the operator’s website. Do not forget to take an identification card when purchasing a SIM card through the office of a mobile operator.
