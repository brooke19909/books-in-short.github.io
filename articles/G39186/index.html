Holiday Village "Mandarkhan Plus" - the best vacation option on the Small Sea

The recreation center "Mandarkhan Plus" is located eight hundred meters from the coast of Lake Baikal. It is conveniently located in a coniferous forest, not far from the protected area on the mountain. Not only companies with friends go fishing here with pleasure, but also families with children, including small ones. Getting to her is best from Irkutsk. Buses depart every day, at nine in the morning, from the main entrance of the Trud Stadium. Only four hours, and you are already on Lake Baikal. Now let's see how interesting this base is and how tourists rate it.

Residence

The recreation center "Mandarkhan Plus" is located in a coniferous forest, among the Baikal larch. The administration does not allow the installation of tents on its territory. But it offers tourists accommodation in standard rooms in wooden cottages and detached houses. There are well-equipped rooms with private facilities in the new cottage, built in 2011.

The rooms are designed for two, three and four people. There are beds, a fridge. Each room has a kitchen with utensils and an electric kettle. Showers and toilets are located on site. Heated water from a boiler (titanium). Electricity is supplied around the clock.

Children under five years old live at the base for free.

The cost of rest - from 750 to 1,500 rubles per day per person, depending on the degree of comfort. The houses and buildings are heated, so you can live on the base all year round. Each room has a separate entrance, near which there is a place for a fire, a column with drinking water and a table. Firewood is provided by the administration. The rooms on the second floors have balconies.

Water recreation and other services
