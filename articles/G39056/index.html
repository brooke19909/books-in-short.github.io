Tie a knot for memory

The very elegant lace-making technique, like many others, has its own zest in execution. Tatting is different in that it is braided by tying special knots on the main thread. This is done with the help of the shuttle, and the connection of the loops will require the help of a thin hook. Actually, the texture of the lace is formed by just a few elements:

  • Rings;
  • Half rings;
  • Arcs;
  • Pico Josephine (it differs from the usual trivial ring by weaving, created exclusively from straight knots) ;
  • Combined rings.
