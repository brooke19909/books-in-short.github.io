How to sew a Christmas candy costume for a girl

Soon New Year's party in kindergarten or school? Do not know what to dress your daughter with? If you have not decided on an idea yet, see how a candy suit is made. It is not very difficult. The main thing is to complete a hat of a certain shape, resembling a wrapped candy wrapper, and the dress can simply be matched to the color. This option is quite universal - it can be worn on a Christmas tree, and for a birthday, and for a thematic dance.





Christmas candy costume for girls: models and options

Such an outfit can be performed in the form of:

  • Dresses.
  • Skirts and blouses.
  • Blouses and panties.
  • Blouses and shorts.

The most important thing is that you have a headdress of the appropriate form. Everything else is optional. In general, you can limit yourself to a hat that resembles the wrapped tip of a candy wrapper. Make it to the color of the existing elegant dress - and the costume is ready.

You can complement the dress with a fabric applique in the form of candy, which can be easily sewn onto a purchased dress. To get an even more interesting candy costume, complete the outfit with a large souvenir bulk candy made of cardboard and corrugated paper or fabric. You can just give it to the girl in your hand or come up with a bag option.

DIY candy costume

If you have enough time and experience, sew the costume completely on any model yourself, so that each item is made of the same material. Here, pay special attention to the color and quality of the fabric: it should be bright, elegant, and even better - shiny, iridescent. As for the forms of things, you can choose any option - a dress or pants with a blouse. Decorate the lower edges of the legs, sleeves, skirts with frills from the same fabric or lace. This will resemble the shape of wrapped sweets.





Tools and materials

To sew a candy suit, you will need the following:

  • Paper for a pattern (graph paper, tracing paper, newsprint or any other).
  • Pencil and ruler or finished printed template.
  • Scissors.
  • The cloth.
  • Pins
  • Tailor's chalk or remnant.
  • Threads with a needle for sweeping.
  • Sewing machine.
  • Decorative elements (sequins, beads, beads, lace; satin ribbons, tinsel).

As you can see, you don‚Äôt need anything special. All that is required is for every needlewoman. You will only have to buy a suitable fabric and thread of the corresponding color.

How to make a hat

As already mentioned, a candy suit for the New Year can consist of both a dress and a blouse with a skirt, shorts or trousers, but in all cases a hat should be present.



It is done as follows:

  1. Measure the circumference of the child‚Äôs head at the widest point.
  2. Cut a strip of paper or cardboard that folds easily and does not break, that is, not corrugated from the box. You can choose the height at your discretion, so that the headpiece looks beautiful on the head of the child. The minimum is usually 10 cm. Take the length in accordance with the measured circumference of the head plus an allowance for gluing. Usually about 3 cm is added. If there is no strip of the desired length and width, you can glue a narrow strip from the back of the head.
  3. Fold the strip and glue overlap. You can use a stapler for greater strength.
  4. For decorative purposes, it is worth pasting the surface with beautiful paper or at least just a suitable color. This step makes sense to do, if you wrap the workpiece you will be a transparent material, if not - leave the brown cardboard as is.
  5. Take the prepared cloth. Try on what size flap you will need.
  6. Put the prepared material in one or several layers (you can make a workpiece from several rags of different colors, laid one on top of the other).
  7. Wrap the cardboard ring so that the free ends remain on one side, which will be the tip of the headgear.
  8. Tie loose parts of the fabric with satin ribbon or beautiful ribbon.
  9. Sew decorative elements onto the fabric wrapper. If the material is used transparent, jewelry can be glued, on the contrary, on a cardboard base. Use elements in the form of sweets cut out of colored paper, real small sweets, beads, circles - in a word, what is at hand or seems suitable for you.

The hat is ready.

How to make decorative candy

No matter how you make a candy costume with your own hands, you can decorate it with the help of an additional accessory - a big candy that the girl can pick up or put on if you can attach the strap as an original bag. You can make candy from polystyrene foam, a cardboard box of a suitable size and shape, or from a sheet of cardboard.



The work is done like this:

  1. Cut a piece of foam to a suitable size. If the thickness is small, make a workpiece of two identical thin ones. If you have a suitable box - just prepare it. If there is neither polystyrene foam, nor a box, take a sheet of cardboard and roll a tube out of it, as they did for a hat, glue the seam.
  2. Wrap the blank with corrugated paper.
  3. Tie the edges with ribbons to make a candy shape.
  4. If necessary, attach an eyelet or strap for easy wearing.

All done.

So, you saw how the candy suit is made. Choose your favorite option and manufacturing method. Create an exclusive outfit for your little caramel!
