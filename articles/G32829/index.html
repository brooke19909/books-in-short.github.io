Hotel "Green Max" 5 * (Turkey, Belek): photos and description, reviews of tourists

Turkey is considered one of those resort countries that offer tourists a variety of conditions for spending holidays. There are gorgeous beaches, ski resorts, historical sites that are not one hundred years old. Another advantage of a holiday in Turkey is the amazing climate, which is easily tolerated even by people with high blood pressure. The tourist infrastructure in the resorts of this country is well developed: impeccable service, the famous all-inclusive concept, real oriental hospitality - all this made this direction incredibly attractive for recreation.

general information

The cost of rest here is quite affordable, so several million people come to Turkey every year, most of which belong to the class of people with an average income level.
