Hotel Club Sun Heaven Family 4 * (Turkey, Alanya, Konakli): description, services, reviews

Club Sun Heaven Family Resort offers travelers a relaxing break on the shores of the gentle sea and enjoy quality service. In a small cozy area you will find everything you need for an unforgettable vacation.





Hotel Feature

Club Sun Heaven Family Hotel is spread over 27,000 square meters. m in close proximity to its own well-kept beach. In the courtyard for guests is equipped with a large clean pool. You can also hide from the Turkish heat in the shade of the trees that are present in abundance on the territory of this hotel.

Club Sun Heaven Family Hotel consists of 20 buildings, each of which is 5 floors high. It is worth noting that part of the buildings in 2014 was completely restored and reconstructed. In total, 358 rooms are available for guests, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay and relaxation.





Where is the hotel situated

If you can not imagine your vacation without the gentle sea and the hot sun, go to the Mediterranean coast. Turkey can offer you a lot of options for a comfortable pastime. One of the most popular hotels is located near the village of Konakli in the resort district of Payallar. Club Sun Heaven Family is just over 100 kilometers from Antalya International Airport. And if you want to walk around the center of Alanya, then you have to overcome no more than 15 km.

Guest rooms

Club Sun Heaven Family 4 * guest apartments are equipped with everything you need for a modern traveler for a comfortable stay. Guests can stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard rooms, despite their compactness, provide comfortable living conditions for two guests. In addition to a large or a pair of twin beds, there is also a soft armchair for reading and relaxing. It is also worth noting the presence of a balcony with plastic chairs.
  • Class A family room consists of two bedrooms, which are separated from each other by a sliding door. The rooms can be equipped with double or twin beds. The balcony is only in the "adult" bedroom, and the bathroom is in the common corridor for both rooms.
  • Family room class B is designed to accommodate 6 guests. Two bedrooms have three beds, as well as individual balconies and bathrooms. It also has a living room with a small kitchenette.

When booking rooms in Club Sun Heaven Family 4 *, please note that there are both old and new buildings on the territory that have undergone reconstruction. The cost of living in the renovated apartments is slightly higher.





Facilities

All guest rooms at San Haven (Turkey) boast modern facilities. In their apartments, guests can take advantage of a wide range of amenities, namely:

  • a bathroom with a shower or a spa bath, in which you will also find a hairdryer, bathrobes and towels, a set of hygienic cosmetics;
  • the room has a minibar, the filling of which is possible for an additional fee (many guests prefer to use it as a refrigerator);
  • air conditioning is responsible for the comfortable air temperature in the rooms;
  • You can pass the time by watching cable or satellite channels;
  • at any time, a landline phone will help to contact the administrator, as well as the restaurant;
  • bask in the sun and enjoy nature, without leaving your room, guests can go to the furnished balcony.

Club Sun Heaven Family (Alanya): reviews, negative comments

Going on vacation, first of all, you should get acquainted not with positive, but with negative reviews. This will save you from unpleasant surprises and disappointments. If we talk about such a resort institution as Club Sun Heaven Family 4 * (Alanya, Konakli), based on the reviews, the following negative points can be distinguished:

  • the hotel territory is quite small, and when you consider that two new buildings were built, there are even less places for walking and recreation;
  • the presence of Russian-speaking workers at the reception was always considered a positive moment, but not in this case (here they behave defiantly and boorishly with guests, and therefore it is better to try to find a common language with the Turks);
  • in rooms located in old buildings, furniture, plumbing and other equipment are in poor condition;
  • one gets the impression that the guests are specially lodged in the "killed" rooms, so that later they require an additional payment for changing the room;
  • very poor breakfasts (daily eggs, porridge, cakes and cheese slices are served);
  • Wireless Internet works with big interruptions, and if we talk about numbers, then it practically does not catch there;
  • beach towels are issued once for the entire period of rest, and if you want to change them to clean, you will have to pay 1 dollar for this service;
  • photos of rooms presented on the official website and booking services do not correspond to reality;
  • from maids and staff it is impossible to replenish stocks of shower gel, shampoos and other cosmetics (even after persistent demands and calls you will be assured of your wrong);
  • the playground is very dusty (it would be nice for the employees to periodically wash the coating);
  • very few seafood on the restaurant menu;
  • central air conditioning (that is, included only at certain hours);
  • to get to the beach, you need to cross the roadway;
  • if you order the transport in advance, then on the appointed day they may simply forget about you, and therefore it will be better if you remind yourself about the day before departure.

General impression

Club Sun Heaven Family 4 * hotel is exactly the case when the number of stars does not reflect either the living conditions or the quality of service. Unfortunately, many guests are not aware that the hotel has old buildings with a "worn out" room stock, and therefore they will be shocked and disappointed upon arrival. In addition, all attempts to resolve the situation will be futile, because the guardians of the hotel are the rude administrators. Naturally, it does not make sense to turn to them for a solution to a problem. If you still choose this hotel for yourself, then stock up on hygiene products (they will not be provided here), and also try to switch your attention to the beautiful nature, not paying attention to what is happening around.
