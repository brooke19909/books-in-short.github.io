Description of the complex

Hotel Carribean Hotel Soma Bay 5 * is a chic resort for lovers of sea holidays and luxurious surroundings. You will certainly enjoy the huge well-kept beach belonging to this hotel. Those who prefer not to deal with the sea elements can have a great time in one of the four pools, as well as visit their own small water park.

Carribean Hotel Soma Bay 5 * will give you a feeling of complete comfort and coziness thanks to the design, as well as the technical equipment of the apartments. Comfortable new furniture, modern electrical appliances and other amenities - this is an important moment, because for the rest period the hotel room becomes a kind of home for the traveler.

Carribean Hotel Soma Bay 5 * is suitable for both those who came to Egypt for bright and unrestrained entertainment, and those who want to just relax and put their health in order. There are bars, and clubs, and sports events, and a health center, and spa, and much, much more.





Location

In the resort town of Soma Bay, the hotel complex Carribean Hotel Soma Bay 5 * is located. TUI (travel agency) our compatriots who want to get to this hotel, most often choose. The hotel itself is located on the coast of the bay, and therefore is characterized by close proximity to the sea. Hurghada International Airport is approximately 45 km from the area. After driving 20 km, you will find yourself at the famous Naama Bay. The location of the hotel is due to the beautiful mountain and desert landscapes that guests can enjoy.





Rooms of the hotel and resort complex

Speaking about the apartments of the Caribbean World Soma Bay 5 * hotel (Egypt, Soma Bay), it is worth noting that there are 529 in all, and they are located in several cozy buildings three stories high. You can choose for yourself numbers from the following list:

  • Superior room is a room reminiscent of a standard room, but the living conditions in it are somewhat improved. This is achieved by increasing the living space, as well as adding some pieces of furniture.
  • Superior Deluxe is a room that is almost similar to the previous one in terms of area, equipment and design solutions. The status upgrade is due to the fact that the windows of these apartments are located on the sea side.
  • Family rooms are designed to receive at least four guests, and therefore consist of two rooms (bedrooms). An additional condition for comfort are two separate bathrooms.
  • Family Deluxe has earned an elevated status due to the fact that from its windows a bewitching seascape opens.




Apartment Amenities

A high level of comfort offers its guests Caribbean World Resort Soma Bay 5 *. The hotel provides guests with the opportunity to stay in rooms that are equipped with a host of amenities. Their list is as follows:

  • a small refrigerator in which you can store your grocery supplies, as well as dishes brought from the restaurant;
  • a modern flat-screen TV with access to a satellite dish (Russian, Belarusian and Ukrainian channels are provided);
  • air conditioning allows you to independently (regardless of the general hotel temperature) regulate the microclimate in your apartment;
  • a telephone, which is intended mainly for communication with administration staff, also has access to an international communication line;
  • there are several 220 V sockets to which you can connect your electrical appliances or charge a mobile phone;
  • on the balconies there is furniture (two chairs and a small table), as well as a rope or clothes dryer;
  • the bathroom provides for a toilet, shower or bathtub, sink, large mirror, hair dryer, as well as bath accessories and cosmetics;
  • an electronic safe is provided so that you can be calm about the safety of your belongings;
  • For your safety, the rooms have a fire alarm.




Restaurants and bars of the hotel complex

In the hotel you will be invited to visit the following facilities:

  • The main restaurant offers a huge selection of dishes presented in buffet style. There is also a special corner for children.
  • The barbecue restaurant specializes in meat and fish, as well as other products prepared in the fresh air. You can also try original marinades.
  • Tortuga is a beachfront establishment whose menu is dominated by grilled dishes. In addition, it offers Italian pizza, light salads and delicious desserts.
  • La Palma is an Italian restaurant that will reveal to you all the culinary secrets specific to this country.
  • Aloha is a lobby bar located in the lobby. It is characterized by a quiet and calm atmosphere, as well as an interesting design, the main highlight of which is live palm trees.
  • Jamaica Bar offers you the opportunity to enjoy fine drinks right in the pool.
  • Aladdin is a hookah bar where you can also enjoy traditional Egyptian drinks and snacks.
  • Gazebo is a favorite place for football fans. Here you can watch sports matches with a glass of beer and lively conversation.
  • Caribbeano - a bar in which an incendiary disco is held in the evening. The best drinks and the latest music await you.
  • Cuba is a beach bar where you can quench your thirst or hunger between diving in azure waves.
