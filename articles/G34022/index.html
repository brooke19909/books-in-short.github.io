Hotel Tirana Aqua Park Resort - relax in Egypt!

Egypt is a country of sun, sea, amazing culture and mysticism. But not only this is famous for the country. Egypt is a popular and beloved vacation spot. Increasingly, tourists choose the hotel Tirana Aqua Park Resort. Why does he so attract those who want to relax?



Location

Tirana Aqua Park Resort is an amazing place where you can relax with the whole family. The hotel is located only 20 km from the airport, so after a long flight you will not need to wait long for arrival: only 15-20 minutes - and you are there. The complex is located on the shores of the warm and beloved by many of the Red Sea, as well as just 5.2 km from the local airport of Sharm el-Sheikh. Not far from the hotel is the well-known Naama Bay area. This amazing place is famous for its beauty. Here you can stroll along the sea along the sandy beach, relax in the shade of trees, and also swim in the warm sea, cleared of sharp corals.

About hotel

The Tirana Aqua Park Resort has plenty of room for everyone. The total number of rooms is 510, of which 84 are family rooms, 418 standard rooms, as well as 8 luxury rooms. Each room in the hotel boasts its own comfort, a beautiful and simple interior, as well as modern furniture and access to the terrace. Room service is provided daily. You will be expected not only cleanliness, but also beautiful designs from bed linen, towels, for example, in the form of swans with rose petals. The room has air conditioning, satellite TV, Internet, telephone and minibar. Tirana Aqua Park Resort is a hotel where it is very cozy and comfortable!



Services

Hotel services are varied! For those who want to ride around the city and visit the sights, the hotel offers a car rental with driver. This way you will not get lost in unfamiliar alleys. And women will be pleased with the hairdresser's at the hotel and a beauty salon, a souvenir and jewelry store, spa, a Turkish traditional bath, a sauna, and a massage room.

Relax and have fun

Eight outdoor pools will delight children and adults with their diversity. For example, if you want to relax from the sea, you can go on water slides with your children or have fun in the pool with artificial waves. On the territory of Tirana Aquapark Resort Ex Sunrise Tirana there is a tennis court, a multifunctional field, as well as billiards, a gym. Girls will enjoy aerobics and water aerobics classes, and young people will have great fun at discos with competitions and shows. What will your children enjoy at the hotel? Here you can please the kids with a kids club called Nemo, as well as a special children's pool with entertaining slides, animations.



Nutrition

Of course, in a hotel of this level, food is not only varied, but also beautifully designed! Tirana Aquapark Resort is famous for its cuisine and chef. The all-inclusive system allows you to choose any food and drink of your choice at any time. In the main restaurant you can have breakfast, lunch and dinner on a grand scale: delicious sauces, fruits, sweets, hot dishes, soups and broths, dairy products, pastries, coffee and ice cream, salads and much more will be provided to you! Three restaurants will diversify your leisure: Italian, Lebanese and Chinese cuisine - all at the highest level. At the lobby bar you can enjoy alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as a cup of tea. Four of the eight pools have a bar, so you can choose whatever you want, without leaving the place of rest. Tirana Aqua Park Resort is a paradise where your vacation will be a real fairy tale!
