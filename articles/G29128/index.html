Do-it-yourself rim of ribbon and artificial flowers

We need a simple bezel of medium width, a ribbon of a suitable color (it is better to choose a satin green width of about one centimeter). A bouquet of small artificial flowers, the main thing is that the flowers are divided into twigs, and at the same time, the tip of each of them is long. Additionally, you need a needle and thread to match the tape, colorless glue. First we take the rim and gently glue the tape from the very tip so that it does not show either the beginning of the rim or the ends of the tape. Then we begin to gently wrap the ribbon around it, somewhere at ear level under the ribbon we lay out branches with flowers in a chaotic order. The tips should completely hide under it. We do this to the very end, where the ribbon is carefully hemmed with a needle and thread. The bezel is ready!
