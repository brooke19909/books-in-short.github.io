History of the town of Kuta

Kuta is one of the busiest and most fashionable resorts on the Indonesian island. It is an ideal place for cheerful and active people. The resort attracts vacationers with an active nightlife, an infectious cheerful mood, interesting winding streets resembling a maze, chic sandy beaches. Hippies were the first to notice Kuta, in the 60s of the 20th century they came here in large numbers, relaxed, held their own events. As the resort town began to function in 1967, from that time the first hotels began to appear. In just five years, an inconspicuous village has grown into one of the most popular holiday destinations in Bali.

What to do in Kuta?

For lovers of beach holidays and nightlife this resort is ideal. Kuta (Bali) has beaches with a length of about 8 km. On the delicate golden and very fine sand you can watch amazingly beautiful sunsets. Young people will love the night cafe-club Gado Gado, located on the beach and open until the morning, the stylish bar Caf Del Mar, which boasts the performance of the best DJs of the island. Also very popular is the Kori Restaurant & Bar, which conquers a calm and peaceful atmosphere. In the daytime, you can visit restaurants, cafes, bars. Nusa Dua and Bukit - Bali Beach Resorts

Chic and stylish hotels, surrounded by fountains, impeccable lawns, golf courses. The resort is also called tropical Switzerland. All hotels have tennis courts and fitness centers. Nusa Dua is a suitable option for vacationers who put comfort in the first place.



Bukit is also located in the southern part, famous for its wonderful beaches, Bali. Which resort to choose for professional and beginner surfers? Ubud and Seminyak - fashionable towns of Indonesia

To the north of Legian and Kuta is one of the fashionable Balinese resorts - Seminyak. Perhaps this is the only Indonesian city in which a huge number of foreigners come from Russia, Australia, Europe. Not all Bali resorts will be able to compete with Seminyak in terms of service and infrastructure. This is a center where nightlife is in full swing, numerous restaurants and cafes welcome guests at any time of the day, and shops, boutiques and supermarkets can offer customers what their soul desires.



If you want to know the real Bali, get acquainted with the culture, religion and art of the island, then you should visit Ubud. A trip to this amazing city of artists and craftsmen of all trades will broaden your horizons, contribute to the healing and purification of the body and soul from accumulated negativity. There are many places worth visiting in Ubud, among them there are both cultural and historical monuments, as well as natural attractions. If you highlight the best resorts in Bali, then this town, of course, will be located in the honorary three. Ubud attracts visitors to the island with picturesque landscapes, a creative atmosphere, the presence of unique places for relaxation and meditation, in addition, it is not as hot here as in other cities of the country.

Ideal surf spots

Kuta, Legian and Canggu are Bali's ideal resorts for surfers. Cities can not boast of luxury hotels, expensive villas, boutiques with clothes from world brands. They are more suitable for thrifty tourists, accustomed to enjoy spending time in nature, and not from the benefits of civilization. On the city beach there is a huge number of surf schools, ready to educate everyone. Kuta is more suitable for young people, because here it is noisy both day and night, bars and discos practically do not close for a break, a huge variety of cheap housing and food is offered. Want peace and quiet? Then you should stay in Legian or Canggu, hotels here are located away from entertainment venues.

Sanur - family friendly resort



Bali resorts offer not only a 24-hour drive, but also a calm, relaxing atmosphere. If you are planning to go on vacation with your whole family, then Sanur, located on the east coast of the island, is ideal for you. It attracts travelers with a measured, unhurried flow of life, a huge selection of water sports, a well-groomed beach, whose length is about 7 km. In Sanur, not only couples like to relax, but also elderly people and newlyweds, because there are many secluded, romantic places.

Karangasem - the search for inner harmony

Breathtaking landscapes attract tourists to the resorts of Bali. Description Karangasem can seduce even the most fastidious travelers. Rice terraces, panoramic views of the ocean, temples, Rajah palaces, bounty beaches, snorkeling and diving sites, amazing food - all this can be found in this beautiful town. Karangasem is perfect for those who have not yet decided on the purpose of a visit to Bali. The resort combines beach, sports and leisure activities. The town will warmly welcome couples, as there are many peaceful places, tours are organized.

Curious young people will like it here, because in Karangasem you can explore the surroundings and look at the daily life of the local population. Divers will be interested in exploring the wreck and coral reefs. Not far from the resort is the main place of Bali - the volcano Agung. In general, the town is ideal for lovers of photo and video, researchers of culture and life of the people of Bali. The map of resorts will not allow you to get lost and miss an interesting attraction.

North bali

Russian tourists rarely visit the northern part of Bali, but in vain. There are a lot of interesting places. Lovina is a combination of several small villages where villas and bungalows for budget holidays are built. If the purpose of the trip is to see the sights, study the culture and art of the people, relax on the ocean, then the north of the island is a wonderful place where you can fully experience the unity with nature.



Lovina attracts tourists with a relaxed, calm atmosphere, full calm on the sea, which lasts all year round, clean beaches with volcanic sand, walks with friendly dolphins. There is a dolphinarium in which everyone can swim with these cute creatures, as well as undergo the full course of dolphin therapy.

Mostly divers come to Pemuteran, there are several eco-resorts here. The town is ideal for everyone who wants to hide from the hustle, bustle, worries, noise, senseless chatter. The quiet sea, the high mountains of Java, looming on the horizon, amazingly beautiful hills and bays create an atmosphere of security and solitude. This resort is suitable for stress relief and energy recharge. In addition, in Pemuteran you can see the unique architecture of the temples and the natural beauty of waterfalls.

What to do on the island?

Bali is a paradise that can satisfy the needs of even the most demanding customers. Depending on the desired result, you should choose a place to relax. Active nightlife, quiet, secluded places, amazing landscapes, golden beaches, budget bungalows, luxurious luxury villas are offered by Bali resorts. Tour prices start at $ 450 per week.

On the island you can do meditation, improve your health in spa salons and natural springs, explore the life of the local population, get acquainted with the Indonesian culture, go on an excursion to the waterfalls, to the forest.
