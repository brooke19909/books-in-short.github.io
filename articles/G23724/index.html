Address and road map

Everything is thought out to the smallest detail in Sochi (Olimp shopping and entertainment center)! "How to get here?" - This is the most common question. The answer is simple. The center has a convenient location: there is a bypass road nearby, connecting the airport and the highway to Adler - another resort city. For guests who want to visit the shopping center, there is a convenient secure parking in front of the building and on its roof, accommodating 550 cars.





Everyone who wants to visit the Olimp shopping mall (Sochi) can do this by public transport. A minibus No. 47 runs from the train station all day with an interval of 10 minutes. Free transport is provided from the neighborhoods of Makarenko, Bytkha, Donskaya and Gagarin.
