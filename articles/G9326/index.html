Lara Dinc Hotel 4 * - ideal for lovers of comfort and coziness

Lara Dinc Hotel 4 * is a hotel located in one of the most popular resorts in Turkey. Antalya is famous for its ancient cozy streets, pebble and sandy beaches and magnificent hotels. Everyone, having come here, will find a place for themselves to their liking. For some, it will be Lara Beach, for others it will be green parks, while others will like to spend time in numerous shops and cafes.





Holidays for everyone

Lara Dinc Hotel 4 * is a beautiful building with six floors, made in a modern design. And the appearance in this case is not at all deceptive. This hotel complex is an ideal place to spend a good rest in it. Moreover, it is suitable for both young travelers and older couples. Excellent conditions and high-quality service will be appreciated by romantic couples, young families with children, cheerful carefree companies. It will really be comfortable for everyone, and this is one of the hallmarks of the Lara Dinc Hotel 4 *. This hotel offers a variety of services that can make your stay more productive, carefree and interesting. Although beaches are nearby, the hotel has an outdoor swimming pool. So at any time, guests can relax and sunbathe on the terrace. Particularly sophisticated guests will love the sauna, Turkish bath, massage and fitness center. In general, as you can see, Lara Dinc Hotel 4 * is a place where you can not only relax, but also have a good time.





Location and room features

The hotel is located near the center of Antalya - only 14 kilometers separate the hotel complex from the place where most visitors prefer to spend their leisure time. It should be noted that the hotel is in close proximity to the international airport, which is a positive point. People who come on vacation would like to check into their rooms as soon as possible, take things away and take a break, rather than go to the hotel for an hour and a half, before that, waiting for transport. In this case, there will be no problems with this. The hotel offers a choice of 48 standard rooms and four suites. All apartments are individually decorated with comfortable, comfortable furniture. The rooms really have everything you need for a carefree and cozy stay and a comfortable pastime. All rooms offer views of the stunning scenery - guests can admire the magnificent nature of Antalya.





Accommodation in apartments

In the Dinc Hotel Lara 4 * (Antalya), as already mentioned, there is everything you need. It offers a telephone, air conditioning, a TV, a minibar, as well as a toilet, and a bathroom equipped with hygiene products. There is also a hairdryer for drying hair. Speaking about accommodation, one should also note the cost of rooms in this hotel. Seven nights in a standard double room will cost about 22 thousand rubles for two. And this is in a room equipped with a bathroom, free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, modern comfortable furniture and a balcony with a chic view. A triple room with similar conditions will cost about 29,000 rubles - for three, with half board included in the price. Agree, this is a very reasonable price.

Service and maintenance

Restaurant Lara Dinc Hotel 4 * is really special. Here, guests are offered a menu that is made exclusively according to the preferences and tastes of hotel guests. Here you can taste international dishes prepared by excellent masters of culinary art. And of course, guests will be offered dishes of Turkish national cuisine, which, of course, will be appreciated by lovers of delicacies. If you wish, you can visit the snack bar, which serves desserts, light snacks and a variety of drinks. But that is not all. In the lobby and pool bar you can enjoy a cocktail or try new alcoholic drinks that are of high quality here.





Guest reviews and comments

In general, we can say that one of the best accommodation options combining high quality and low prices is Lara Dinc Hotel 4 *. Reviews confirm this. The first thing guests say is that in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a public beach. But many come here in order to devote their holidays to sea vacations. Nearby are also various scenic spots - parks, alleys, which are pleasant to walk on warm evenings. They also note that the hotel is very comfortable, at home. Many were pleasantly surprised by the fact that at the reception there is a Russian-speaking employee who will always prompt and help if guests are interested in something. It is always pleasant for a Russian person who finds himself in another country. And in general, guests note good service - daily cleaning, polite staff, good food and also a decent contingent. This fully characterizes the Lara Dinc Hotel 4 *. Reviews in general create a positive picture. Everything is decent and cultural, definitely, will appeal to lovers of just such a holiday. In short, a great combination of low price and good quality.
