The largest cannon in the world - awesome but useless

Martial artillery - one of the three oldest military branches - throughout its existence knew examples of the creation of unique weapons. Huge, powerful, unprecedented, they were announced as a weapon of victory and almost always remained out of work. Most likely, they were conceived as an indicator of the military might of a power, a demonstration of the genius of engineering.
