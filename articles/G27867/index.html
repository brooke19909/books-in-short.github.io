Sirius Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer, Tekirova): hotel description, service, reviews

One of the currently popular resorts in Turkey is the village of Tekirova. It is located sixty kilometers south of Antalya, close to the diverse local attractions of these surroundings. Clean sandy and pebble beaches, an abundance of pine forests, numerous parks with citrus fruits, developed infrastructure, a variety of shops, entertainment venues and a high level of service in hotel complexes make your stay here an amazing fairy tale on the Mediterranean coast. A bright representative providing rooms and high-level service is the Sirius Hotel, which has earned 4 stars.





History and number of rooms

The hotel was built in 1993. The total area is 6500 m 2 . Comprises:

  • Main building (three-story building with integrated panoramic elevator).
  • Annex building (one-story building).

Large-scale restoration was carried out in 2012. All chambers are presented in a single category - standard. The first building includes 54 rooms, the second - 10. One room is designed for people with disabilities and is equipped with all necessary appliances.

Tourists who have visited the Sirius Hotel, in the description of the rooms, characterize them as modest but cozy. All rooms have a relatively small area, but are equipped with everything necessary. The interior is classic, the color scheme of each individual room varies. Numerous mirrors in some apartments contribute to a visual increase in space. Furniture sets are of high quality and include beds, a table, a chair, a wardrobe and bedside tables. The abundance of various lamps fills the space with cosiness and light. The rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, split system, telephone, refrigerator and electric kettle. The bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer and is filled with all the necessary cosmetic kits and personal hygiene products.





Sirius Hotel: Dining

The concept of all inclusive involves the consumption of free drinks from 10:00 to 22:30. Three meals a day according to the buffet system are carried out according to the established schedule. Everything after 22:30 is paid. In the daytime, by the pool, to create a good mood among tourists, a buffet of Turkish pastries and tea is provided. The opportunity to enjoy ice cream and order drinks at the bar near the aquazone will delight every vacationer and allow you to enjoy the hot sun in a comfortable setting.

The minibar in each room is updated daily - one bottle of drinking water is added for free. The rooms have stickers with coffee and sugar, which will allow you to meet the dawn with a cup of tea and at the same time not to leave the chambers.

A distinctive feature of this hotel is that bottled drinks and freshly squeezed juices throughout the day are paid extra. It should be noted that for many tourists, according to their reviews, the buffet, sometimes located in the fresh air, is to their liking.





Sirius Hotel: Beach

Hotel Sirius is located in the first coastal zone, which is why it is popular with tourists. To the sea abyss about 100 meters. Own sand and pebble beach fifty meters long is equipped with sunbeds, mattresses, umbrellas and awnings. The bar located here allows you to cool with all kinds of drinks throughout the day. The administration of this hotel took care of the availability of towels on the beach. Changing cabins, a toilet and a shower contribute to a comfortable rest near the Mediterranean Sea.

Water activities for guests of all ages

There are two outdoor pools. One of them is equipped with a slide for adults in the form of a bright rainbow. The mood certainly rises at the sight of this playing and bright color. It is direct, but the descent along it diversifies the reception of monotonous sunbaths and swimming in the heated pool water. Its area is 350 square meters.

The second pool is for children, 100 square meters, equipped with a slide in the form of an elephant. Bathing in any weather will amuse the little guests, and active swimming will favor evening sleep. Numerous sunbeds, umbrellas and showers are located in the area.





Busy child - free parents

For little guests at Sirius Hotel, they did everything possible to make their summer vacations a memorable one. The presence of a playground in the open air captivates kids for a long time, since the presence of a variety of carousels, slides, transitions, swings and other equipment takes young guests and carries them into relentless climbing. During the day, the kids are entertained by animators, and the fairy-tale heroes who arrived help their good mood.

The main restaurant provides free high chairs for use. Cots in the room are also included in the list of paid services, they are brought at the request of customers.

This hotel offers babysitting services. However, this service is paid extra.

A mini-club for children from 4 to 12 years old, equipped with a variety of toys for children of any age, entertaining games that contribute to the development of the child, will certainly make it possible to turn a relaxing holiday into a useful development in a playful way.





Entertainment and infrastructure

This complex does not differ in a large number of entertainment provided, however, rest here will not be boring. Throughout the day, a variety of games and competitions are held in the pool. On the territory, artists perform with show programs and animators. In the evening, life in the complex will turn into an amazing fairy tale, filled with bright colors and ideas. The ensembles performing here delight with their professionalism, talents and desire to create a real holiday in the soul of every vacationer. The language of the animators is Russian.

Evening discos with entertainment programs will also appeal to every lover of movements and dances.

The administration provides parking spaces for everyone. This service is free and does not require prior coordination. Pets are strictly prohibited. However, the Sirius Hotel (Turkey, Kemer, Tekirova) does provide smoking rooms. Ironing, shoe shine and laundry are services requiring additional payment.

Allowed accommodation for a child of any age. It should be noted that children under the age of two who need a baby cot live free of charge. Children under 7 years old without a bed and adults with an extra bed in the amount of one unit stay for free. However, this accommodation requires prior approval and confirmation by the hotel of this service.

Phone, safe, laundry, doctor's call and Wi-Fi - paid services are paid extra.





Skin and body care

At the hotel Sirius 4 * you can use the services of massage therapists and a sauna. They are not included in the all-inclusive package and are paid extra. Skincare of guests is carried out by qualified professionals. The ability to heal the body in a sauna in the circle of a family and friendly company will color fast-flying resort days. Spa treatments of various kinds will give the body a pleasant pleasure.

Sport

For fans of sports entertainment and recreation, the opportunity to use the gym, equipped with various equipment. Table tennis, a Turkish bath, aerobics and a volleyball court on the coast will diversify leisure travelers. Diving and billiards here belong to the list of paid services. The Dart Gaming Salon will delight gamblers and let you enjoy the game in your free time. The beach offers scuba diving courses, various sports and diving.

Possible excursions near

The successful location of the hotel in the village of Tekirova made it possible for vacationers to visit various attractions. Ancient Phaselis - the oldest port city, founded in the VI century BC, will surprise with its ruins and delight everyone. Mount Tahtalidag with a height of more than two thousand meters will delight clean air and saturate the body of travelers with a charge of vivacity and freshness. The most mystical attraction of the whole world will be thrown into the unprecedented delight of everyone - Mount Chimera, where fires ignite all year round in different places.

Hotel Sirius 4 * is an amazing gem in southern Turkey, on the Mediterranean coast. Having once been here, enjoying a comfortable vacation and taking a lot of photos that will remind you of a delightful pastime, every traveler will feel a great desire to return to the already known places.
