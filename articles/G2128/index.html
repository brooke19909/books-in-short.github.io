Fascinating shopping in Vietnam





The trade industry is an indispensable attribute of modern East Asia. This region is famous for such shopping centers as Thailand, Taiwan, South Korea. At the same time, the level of service here is close to perfection. But next to such giants, there are other, not so widely advertised, places for shopping. Among them, Vietnam can be distinguished. Not so long ago, this state was not very interesting for shopping fans, but with the development of services and tourism, the situation is changing. At the same time, shopping in Vietnam has a number of advantages of its own, including low prices and a unique assortment of goods. The monetary unit of Vietnam is the Vietnamese Dong. You can purchase it for euros and US dollars in each exchanger of the country.

Going to shopping, in Vietnam, many want to buy pearls, which are sold here in almost all stores. But be careful - it is customary to fake this popular product here by selling it to unlucky tourists. If you buy a fake, instead of the original you will become the owner of a plastic ball treated with a special hard coating. Therefore, it is best to buy pearls near the points of its extraction. Having spent a little time on a trip to the pearl farms, you can save some money, as in these places it is sold much cheaper. At the same time, the highest quality pearls are mined on the island of Phu Quoc.





Shopping in Vietnam is also attracted by the opportunity to purchase porcelain. Especially popular in this place is the dishes, a large selection of which can be seen in the city of Bat Chang. Also visit the ceramic market between Halong and Hanoi. Its peculiarity is the fact that by purchasing ceramics, you can simultaneously arrange delivery to every corner of the world, including Russia. Also, sapphire and silver products are famous for everyone who loves shopping in Vietnam. The prices for these products are quite low. In addition, an incredible selection of gold products is offered to the attention of the buyer, but it is expensive by European standards. Jewelry made of precious materials can be purchased in the villages of Kat and Chau Khe. But lovers of natural silk go to the village of Nya Sa, the unspoken capital of silk.



More and more tourists choose shopping in Vietnam. Reviews about it for the most part are only positive. Shoes and clothes, which were produced right there, are becoming increasingly popular, but Thailand and China continue to be the undisputed leaders in this industry. The reason for the production boom of this type of product is that most global brands here prefer to place their own production facilities due to cheap labor. This is displayed, first of all, on the cost of goods, while tourists who have chosen shopping in Vietnam, note that in terms of quality / price, local products will be the best choice of all. Due to this, shoes and clothes that were produced in Vietnam are incredibly popular among tourists from Europe.
