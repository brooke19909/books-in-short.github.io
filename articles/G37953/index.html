Crock-pot "Dobrynya": with good to the table!

Many housewives, choosing household appliances for the kitchen, are used to seeing foreign brands on store shelves and will be pleasantly surprised to learn about the appearance of a Russian manufacturer in this niche. The company "Dobrynya" successfully competed with foreign firms, presenting a wide range of products to consumers, from stoves and irons to dishes and accessories. And, of course, manufacturers take into account the heavy workload of modern hostesses. In the care of women, they have developed a line of inexpensive, beautiful and functional multicookers.

Such a "miracle of technology" has already simplified the chores in the kitchen of more than one woman, but not every one of them is ready to overpay for the brand. Therefore, the Dobrynya crock-pot will become a find not only for housewives who want to save time, but also for those who want to save finances. Below we will talk about several models, they differ in advanced functionality and at the same time remain affordable.

Slow cooker "Dobrynya" DO-1005





This wonderful saucepan can easily cope with the most difficult recipe! Thanks to a large selection of functions, you can cook soups and cereals, vegetable stew and steamed fish in it. Pamper your child with freshly prepared appetizing porridge, and your spouse with fried juicy meat. At the same time, all that is required of you is to choose a recipe for cooking, buy ingredients, put them in a multicooker bowl and select the desired operating mode. This device supports 8 built-in programs, the ability to cook without oil. Its 4-liter bowl, thanks to its non-stick coating, will not spoil the food with unpleasant odors. Also, the Dobrynya multicooker will allow you to use the delayed start function, which is very convenient if you need to cook a dish for a certain time (for example, stew for the whole family for dinner). A discreet but attractive design will appeal to many housewives, and the low cost of the model will please no less.

Slow cooker "Dobrynya" DO-1010

If you are faced with the need to feed a large family, this model with a 5-liter bowl and non-stick Teflon coating will help you. With it, you have a great opportunity to cook both simple daily dishes and surprise your household with something like that! Thanks to a large selection of programs, the multicooker will equally well cook, fry, stew and even bake. Whatever gastronomic delights you decide to treat yourself to, this model will perfectly prepare the desired dish, you can rest assured. Its functionality will also please those people who adhere to a healthy diet, because in it you can cook steamed without a drop of oil. Heat treatment will save all the nutritional value of products, leaving them tasty, aromatic and healthy.



And that's not all!

In addition to the considered multicooker models, Dobrynya also produces other types of household appliances: electronic kettles, hot grills, household scales, hair care accessories, and much more. All products are decorated in the style of "Gzhel", "Khokhloma", which is the hallmark of this brand, indicating the country of origin - Russia.
