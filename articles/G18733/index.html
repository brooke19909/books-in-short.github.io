Pullman Pattaya G Hotel 5 * (Thailand / Pattaya): reviews, photos

Pullman Pattaya G Hotel 5 * is a popular hotel complex located in Thailand. Many tourists decide to stay in this place. And there are a number of objective reasons for this. The hotel is good, modern, comfortable - and these are only its main advantages.





Briefly about the hotel

The Pullman Pattaya G Hotel 5 * complex can be seen from afar - a spectacular multi-story building, made in accordance with traditional Thai architecture, very harmoniously fits into the environment. The hotel is modernly decorated, and the hotel rooms look really expensive and presentable. Just a few steps from the complex is a private beach - spacious and clean. All hotel guests relax here.

And in close proximity is the Temple of Truth. Before him on foot - about seven minutes. In addition, a variety of shops, cafes and souvenir kiosks can be found nearby. Anyway, the hotel is located in a very picturesque place, so that every evening walk in the vicinity can turn into a real event.

Bars and Restaurants

A considerable number of guests stayed at such a hotel as Pullman Pattaya Hotel G 5 *. The reviews they left about this hotel abound in details and positive emotions. Service, maintenance, comfort level ... but most of the comments affected the topic of nutrition. The guests assure that if the person who planned the trip here is worried about how they will feed there, then he can safely relax. The complex has a luxurious restaurant called Terrazzo. It serves delicious Thai cuisine. In the evening, various shows are organized here and musicians perform.

There is another restaurant - Bay. Decorated elegantly and stylishly, it is always cozy and cool. And guests who have been here assure that the menu has such dishes for which it is worth visiting this hotel.

A continental breakfast buffet is also available . And a secluded cafe next to the pool. Guests are strongly advised to visit it and try the desserts from the chef.





Service

Hotel Pullman Pattaya Hotel G 5 *, the photos of which are presented above - this is a hotel with excellent service. Tens of thousands of guests who have been here confirm this. In this complex - everything is for people. There are non-smoking rooms, for example, as well as apartments for people with disabilities. Fresh newspapers are also delivered to the rooms, and lovers can soak up the beach by the sea with sun loungers and umbrellas.

There is car parking, a shuttle to (or from) the airport is provided, you can even rent a car or bike. The hotel has a room in which luggage is stored, a currency exchange office, a shop with souvenirs and gifts, and even a laundry room. Hotel guests say that the staff is very nice - employees always try to help guests with something. So, for example, the administration often organizes excursions, banquets, meetings. If someone is sick, they can send a doctor. Needless to say, even the services of a secretary, translator and mail are available. So the service is on the level - and this cannot but rejoice.





Leisure and entertainment

What else can you tell about this wonderful hotel located in such a picturesque place as Pattaya? Pullman Pattaya Hotel G 5 * is a complex in which you can not only stay for the duration of your stay at the resort, but also have some good fun. The hotel has a sauna, spa and fitness room - those guests who like physical activity will especially enjoy this, and after them - relax in the steam room. There is also a gym and two outdoor pools. There are also places in the hotel where you can do yoga, go for a massage or take a jacuzzi. And lovers of active sports will be happy to know that water volleyball, diving and table tennis are still available from entertainment. So there is a lesson for everyone. In any case, the guests who have already stayed here assure that leisure is organized at the highest level.





What you need to know about booking

Finally, a few words about how to reserve a number. Naturally, this must be done in advance. The hotel is popular, so at least the booking process should be puzzled about two months before the trip. Fortunately there is a function of "payment on the spot" and "cancellation." So, check-in is done after two in the afternoon until midnight, and check-out is from nine in the morning and, accordingly, until 14:00.

It happens that guests come with children - then special conditions apply. If the child is under 12 years old, he will be placed on the existing bed in the apartment for free. If the baby is not even two years old, then a crib will be put in the room. Accommodation will also be free. But if the teenager is over 12 years old, then you will have to pay about 4,800 rubles (in Thai currency this is 2236 THB).

All in all, this is a great place to spend a truly luxurious vacation. Positive impressions guaranteed.
