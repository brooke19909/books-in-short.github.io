Hotel The Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel 5 * (Cyprus): description, rooms, prices, reviews

One of the best places to spend a vacation is Greece. Many people come here to get an unforgettable experience and relax properly. Before going on a trip, every tourist needs to choose a hotel in which he will live in the near future. The best option in Larnaca, which is one of the most popular resorts, is The Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel. Therefore, it is worth talking about all the advantages of this hotel.





Briefly about the complex

Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel was built in 2015. This is a brand new, modern complex, which is located on the coastline of Larnaca. Perfect location. The coastline stretches for 25 kilometers, there are clean sandy beaches, well-developed infrastructure, good sea. Also nearby is a large number of cafes, taverns and cozy restaurants.

This hotel is perfect not only for comfortable accommodation during a business trip, but also for the usual sea and beach vacations. You can walk to the beach from the hotel in a minute - and this is a definite plus. In addition, the airport is located very close by - just five minutes away by car from the complex.

Also within walking distance of the complex are local attractions. This is the church of St. Lazarus, Pattiqueio Theater and Larnaca Castle.

Restaurants

The Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel is a 5-star complex, so there are several presentable establishments on its territory where you can have a delicious lunch, go for breakfast or dinner. A restaurant called Enigma specializes in international dishes. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served there. He works every day, from seven in the morning until ten in the evening.

There is also a restaurant L`or. It works from 19:00 to 23:00. And besides him, the Grand Bar is located on the territory of the hotel, which serves lunches and dinners. The cuisine is also international. In addition to various delicacies, there you can order a variety of cocktails, strong drinks and snacks. By the way, you should definitely try the local wine. After all, everyone knows that it is special in Greece.

By the way, if a person planned his vacation at the end of December, then he can attend free gala dinners and dinners, which are held here on the 24th, 25th and 31st.

What is in the complex?

The Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel 5 * (Larnaca) is fully equipped for relaxation. On its territory there is everything that you may need. True, there is no pool. But the hotel has its own private zone on the nearby beach. And the hotel has a sunbathing terrace.

If guests want to have fun, they can rent a bicycle - rental is provided here. There is also free private parking on which you do not need to pre-book a place. By the way, car rental is also provided.

Concierge services, a tour desk are available. Guests can leave their belongings in the luggage storage room. If the guests did not manage to exchange the currency in their own country, they can do it in the hotel - there is a separate item for this. The registration desk is open around the clock; accelerated check-in or eviction is possible.

By the way, even guests are offered laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services. There is even a business center and fax and photocopying facilities.





Service

The Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel 5 * hotel is popular not only because it has all the conditions for a good rest. Many fell in love with him for his attitude towards visitors. The staff is not only polite and welcoming. The people working here are real professionals. They speak five languages, among which there is Russian, which cannot but rejoice. In addition to him, they also speak English, German, Greek and Swedish.

Also, if guests want, then they can be arranged to deliver drinks and food to the room. Non-smoking rooms are also available. There are even facilities for people with disabilities. And on the upper floors you do not need to get on foot - there is an elevator for this.

By the way, in brief it is worth mentioning one interesting feature that distinguishes the hotel The Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel. Cyprus is a place of rest. And many go to sea with children. So, The Ciao Stelio Deluxe is an ideal place for people who want to spend their holidays in peace and quiet, as it is not allowed to check in guests with children. Everything in the hotel is literally imprisoned for adults and, admittedly, wealthy people.

About arrival and booking

Hotel The Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel 5 * reviews receive mostly positive. Largely because of this, this complex has become even more popular, because many people want to experience for themselves what the experienced guests talk about.

But, since the hotel has only 52 rooms, you should think about booking a few months before the trip. Especially if the trip is planned when Greece is in the midst of the summer season (that is, from April to October).





Guest Comments

In the end, a few words are worth mentioning about the reviews that leave about The Ciao Stelio Deluxe Hotel. The hotel, as previously mentioned, is new, so not all travelers managed to visit it. But all those people who came here were impressed.

Special attention is paid to cleaning. Towels are changed daily, just like linen. If the pillow seems uncomfortable (too flat or, conversely, large), then you can always ask to be replaced. Aesthetes are especially pleased with the modern style in which all the rooms are decorated. The apartments look interesting, original, even fashionable. Linen, towels - you can see the hotel logo on everything.

Special attention should be paid to the dishes served in the restaurant. People who visited it assure that it is better to go there than to visit the institutions located in Larnaca itself. Undoubtedly, other restaurants are not bad either. But the one located in the hotel really corresponds to the level of five stars.

And still all guests note the excellent location of the hotel. And it's not just about the sea and the beach. The city center, an airport nearby - all you need, plus well-developed transport links. If anything, you can rent a car, the hotel provides such an opportunity for a very reasonable price.

In general, if you want your vacation to be unforgettable, you should go here. From such a holiday there will definitely be a lot of vivid impressions.
