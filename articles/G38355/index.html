How to make bouquets of soft toys? Beginner Tips

What woman doesn‚Äôt like to receive a bouquet of flowers as a present? With or without it, if you want to make the fair sex a pleasant surprise, give her such a present. Well, if you still want to be distinguished by originality and surprise the lady, then give her a bouquet of soft toys. Such compositions look spectacular, beautiful and unusual. In our article, you can read information on how to make bouquets of soft toys with your own hands. The tips and tricks presented to your attention will help you make an exclusive gift at home.



We prepare materials for creating a composition

To make original bouquets of soft toys, you need to decide what kind of figures it will consist of. The composition may include 3 large toys or 9-11 small ones. Also, the bouquet looks original in which one large figure is placed in the center, and around it there are many small ones. This material for making a soft gift can be picked up and bought in a store. But the most unique and unusual bouquets of soft toys are obtained from handmade elements. They can be sewn from fabric or knitted from yarn.

In order to make a composition of toys, prepare another basket, a piece of polystyrene (the size should coincide with the circumference of the bottom of this container), wooden sticks, thermal gun, wrapping paper, satin ribbon, scotch tape.

How to make bouquets of soft toys? Practical tips

At the bottom of the basket, put the foam, after applying a few drops of glue on it. This will fix the foundation.

Poke each toy with a wooden skewer below (where the tail is located). To keep the figure tighter, glue the tip of the stick with glue.

From the brown paper (or floral grid), cut two squares with side sizes up to ten centimeters, pierce them in the center with a skewer and put on each stick with a figure. Using your fingers to pick up paper near the puncture site, form a frill around the toy. Fix it with tape for the skewer. Around each soft figure you get a magnificent "skirt".



Lubricate the bottom edge of each stick with glue and insert into the foam base. Start filling the basket from the center to the edges. The voids around the toys can be filled with twigs of artificial plants, and even better - with sticks of candy.

When thinking about how to make bouquets of soft toys, take care of the decor for the basket itself. Her handle can be wrapped with tape or braid, tie a beautiful bow. Glue small artificial flowers or bows on the walls of the container on the outside. If possible - get iridescent rhinestones, beads, buttons.

How to make bouquets of soft toys with a pen holder?

To prepare such a base, prepare a piece of foam, a cardboard tube (from under a cling film or foil) or thick cardboard, a glue gun, wrapping paper, and adhesive tape.

Cut a circle out of the foam. Roll the cardboard into a roll and secure it with tape. Glue the tube to the round workpiece using a heat gun. Wrap the resulting structure with corrugated or wrapping paper, gluing the edges with tape.





Next, we carry out the fastening of toys. They can simply be ‚Äúplanted‚Äù on the foam adhesive. But in this case, the toys after disassembling the bouquet remain damaged. The best fixture for attaching figures in such a composition is wire. Gently pierce the toy from below with it, stretch and bend both ends down. Now stick them into the foam. Everything, the figure is firmly fixed. To make the bouquet look voluminous, do not forget to make a frill under each soft blank. When all the toys are seated in a bouquet, wrap it with a floral grid or wrapping paper. Tie a bow on the handle.

Now you know how to make bouquets of soft toys. Following the prompts presented in the article, you can easily build a beautiful, and most importantly, an unusual gift with your own hands.
