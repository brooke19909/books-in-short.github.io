The beaches of Sevastopol. Overview of the best places to stay

Resorts of the Crimean peninsula are very popular not only among Ukrainians, but also residents of near and far abroad countries. Here you can enjoy beautiful landscapes, breathe in the fragrant forest air, climb the mountains, swim enough in the sea. One of the cities that deserve special attention is Sevastopol. Beaches, reviews of which are mostly positive, are here for every taste: urban concrete, sandy, rocky, pebble. In addition, they are all absolutely free.



Sevastopol is washed by the sea on three sides, so there are plenty of places to relax. In the city you can not only get acquainted with architectural and historical sights, but also have a good rest on the beach. Most of them are superbly equipped and suitable for a pleasant pastime both for the company of adults and couples with children.

The beaches of Sevastopol are located both in the city center and in quite secluded places, so tourists are offered a wide selection.
