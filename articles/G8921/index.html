Delfin Nessebar Apartment (Bulgaria, Nessebar): hotel description, reviews

Today, resorts in Bulgaria are becoming increasingly popular. This is one of the oldest countries in Europe, which is famous for its centuries-old traditions and has a rich history. Bulgaria is located in the southeastern part of Europe, on the Balkan Peninsula. The country is very rich in cultural and historical attractions. Stunning resorts create excellent conditions for relaxation and unlimited opportunities for entertainment. The nature is unique and picturesque. It combines several opposites, Black Sea beaches and wonderful ski resorts.



Hotels on the coast

There are a lot of them, so even before you go on vacation, it is advisable to decide where you want to live. We suggest you pay attention to the resort town of Nessebar. This is one of the oldest cities in Bulgaria with numerous architectural sights. Nessebar has about 3,000 years. Today, most of it no longer exists, the city is swallowed by the sea. On a clear day, the remains of the walls can be seen at a depth of 80 meters. So the city is a real open-air museum.

If you want to spend your vacation with maximum comfort, but do not overpay, pay attention to the Delfin Nessebar Apartment. It is located in the southern part of Sunny Beach resort. Forms a common complex with the Sirena Hotel. A great combination of price and quality. The beach is separated by dunes.

Tourist entertainment

As already mentioned, Hotel Delfin is part of a huge tourist complex called Sunny Beach. This is a real paradise for tourists with lots of opportunities for a great vacation. The beach strip stretches here for 10 km, a width of about 40 meters. Further up are picturesque dunes. Golden sand stretches almost to the horizon. Beach holidays are a priority here, because the air temperature in the season is from 22 to 30 degrees, and the water is about 25. The sea near the shore is shallow, the slope is gentle, which is very convenient for families with children.

No matter what hotel you decide to stay at, all the entertainment of the resort area remains available to you. Especially many options for outdoor enthusiasts. The resort offers a variety of water sports. These are water skiing and bicycles, windsurfing and parachutes, hang gliders, yachts and boats, "bananas" and jets.

For lovers there is a large number of "earthly" entertainment. There are tennis courts and basketball courts, where several teams gather daily. Vacationers have access to beach volleyball and mini-golf, a bowling alley, bowling, bicycles and an equestrian base with thoroughbred horses. For lovers of historical competitions there is a shooting range for archery and crossbow. And 10 km from the resort opened a grandiose zoo.



Night life

Hotel "Dolphin" (Nessebar) is considered a family type hotel. It is very quiet, and therefore tourists eager for vivid impressions leave its cozy walls with the onset of the evening and rush to the entertainment centers. Sunny Beach is a real paradise, nightlife here is in full swing. There are over 250 restaurants on site. Among them there are classic places where you can taste delicious dishes. And for lovers of new products it is recommended to visit the attraction restaurants "Caligula" and "Khan's tent". In addition, you will find many entertainment venues, nightclubs and discos. It operates the largest casino variety show in the Balkans, there is a summer theater and open cinemas.

General description of the hotel

The Delfin Nessebar Apartment is one of the oldest on the coast. It was built in 1980, but in 2013 a complete reconstruction was carried out. Therefore, today you can count on a comfortable stay. The territory is about 5000 square meters. The hotel is a seven-story building. It offers stunning views of the Black Sea.

Delfin Nessebar Apartment is located on the beachfront. The beach is just 100 meters through the sand dunes. The hotel is 2.7 km from the center of Sunny Beach. If you want to visit Old Nessebar, then you can take a hiking trip, there are only 3 km. The hotel is 23 km from Burgas Airport , and by car you can get in just 15 minutes.



Hotel accommodation

The seven-story building has 66 rooms. The building is elegantly decorated with brickwork and decorated in a bluish-beige color scheme, built on a green, landscaped area. It has everything you need for a great vacation. Places for relaxation and sunbathing, a pool and restaurant, as well as a bar with refreshments.

Hotel Delfin Nessebar Apartment offers its tourists comfortable conditions for a leisurely holiday on the Black Sea. There are no noisy parties, but you can have a wonderful time with your family. Tourists are accommodated in comfortable rooms with sea views. All are equipped with modern furniture and household appliances. Depending on the composition of your family, you can choose a double or triple room. There are also eight-bed apartments for a large company or just lovers of comfort.

Beach

Hotel Delfin is located in the immediate vicinity. From the window you can enjoy panoramic views of the Black Sea coast. There is no private beach, but vacationers can enjoy a vacation on the municipal. The path to it lies through the promenade or promenade. The entrance to the sea is very gentle, there are sun loungers and umbrellas on the shore, you can order soft drinks.

In addition to the urban sandy beach, there is the possibility of relaxing by the pool. You are waiting for umbrellas and deck chairs, a relaxed atmosphere. Usually there are a lot more people on the beach, and here you will spend time in silence, enjoying the sunny heat. Please note that sun loungers and umbrellas near the pool can be used for free, and on the shore you will have to pay for a similar service.

Tours for tourists

On the third day, usually tourists have already studied all the activities that Delfin Nessebar Apartment offers.
