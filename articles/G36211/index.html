Packaging and equipment

The first thing that meets the buyer is packaging. It is framed in white. There are no familiar phone images on it. Instead, designers used a device diagram drawn with dashed lines. Technical capabilities are presented in the form of notation. On the front panel and side faces, you can see icons in the form of letters, bulbs, joysticks and others, which tell about what will be available to the user who will purchase this model.

Many buyers are no doubt wondering what exactly the ZTE Blade A510 comes with. The instruction manual and other documentation are necessarily included in the package. In addition to them, there is also a warranty card, which provides free after-sales service for one year. Also, inside the box, the buyer will find a power adapter and a cable with a USB connector. The latter is used not only to charge the device, but also to connect the gadget to other devices, for example, to a computer or laptop. Also in the box, the buyer will see an additional plastic cover. Unfortunately, the manufacturer does not provide a headset in the kit. However, you can buy it at any store. With the choice of a specific model of headphones, there will be no problems, since the phone is equipped with a standard audio port and works with a headset of any brand.

Ergonomics

According to most users, the cell phone ZTE Blade A510 leaves only a positive impression when using it. Due to the fact that it weighs only 130 g, the hand does not get tired even with a long conversation. Moreover, its body has such a shape that it is most convenient to interact with the gadget. Many users claim that you can wear the device not only in a bag, but also in the pockets of a shirt, trousers, jacket and other clothes.

It is especially necessary to highlight the thickness of the body. It is only 8.2 mm. This indicator can be considered the standard of modern mobile devices. It is not so small that the phone is damaged in the event of an accidental fall, but also not so large, due to which the user will get tired after a long interaction.

The build quality of the Chinese device exceeded all expectations. All parts are fitted as accurately as possible. There are no creaks and backlashes.

ZTE Blade A510 Phone: Display Features

The manufacturer in his offspring used a 5-inch matrix. Its quality can compete with any other brands. In the manufacture of the display used IPS technology. Due to the fact that there is no air gap, the image displayed on the screen is highly detailed. The most optimal for this diagonal size is a resolution of 1280x720 px. This is exactly what is used in this model. Image clarity also depends on pixel density. Its rate is 320 ppi. Even if you look at the picture in close proximity to the eyes, it is impossible to notice the "squares".

The manufacturer did not forget about such an option as multi-touch. This model supports up to 10 simultaneous touches. The sensor is highly sensitive. An instant reaction follows even after light touches. It was previously noted that the manufacturer used an oleophobic coating. It significantly reduced the brightness level of the screen, but this does not mean at all that fingerprints do not remain on the glass at all. But you should not worry, as they are easily erased by any soft cloth. The developers did not use a special film, explaining it this way: the properties of the glass are so high that the screen is protected without it too.

The display in this model is characterized by excellent color reproduction and maximum viewing angles. In any light, the user can count on a comfortable brightness. It changes automatically thanks to sensors. For example, when used outdoors in sunny weather, the brightness level rises to the maximum limit, and in the room it is set to average. In order to work comfortably in the dark, you can reduce the brightness to a minimum.



Camera

Like many other modern gadgets, the ZTE Blade A510 phone has two cameras. The main one is based on a 13-megapixel matrix. With its help, you can take pictures with a resolution of 4160x3120 px. According to this criterion, this smartphone model can compete even with devices of the middle segment. The developers used phase autofocus, which allows you to take the most detailed pictures. Only the main camera is equipped with a flash, as it is located on the back of the case. Going into video mode, you can record videos with a resolution of 1280x720 px. The change of frames per second is 30 units.

According to most users, the quality of the optics impressed everyone without exception. For such a price category this is more an exception than a rule. The best quality pictures are obtained in good light. They are distinguished by a high level of contrast and real color reproduction. A slight noise may appear in low light. This can be considered a drawback that spoils the high praise of optics.

For the front camera, the developer chose a fixed focus. It is based on a 5-megapixel sensor. The maximum resolution of photographs is 2592x1944 px. In their reviews, users often pay attention to the fact that it is not very suitable for a selfie, since the image is clearly visible in the pictures.

Hardware "stuffing"

Phone ZTE Blade A510 on the criterion of performance did not cause sharp comments from users. It is based on the chipset of the MediaTek MT6735P trademark. This processor runs on 4 computing modules of the Cortex-A53 type. The row of the system is 64 bits, which allows users to install the most modern applications on the gadget. The graphics card is the Mali-T720. She does an excellent job with all types of images, including animated ones.

Performance features are complemented by one gigabyte of RAM. This allows the operating system to freeze up. Each user can notice that the desktops smoothly and quickly switch. The orientation of the picture changes literally in one second. If you work with non-resource-intensive applications, then they open very easily. The indisputable advantage of this model is that many 3D-games run with a bang.

To work with the phone, the user is provided with 8 GB of integrated memory. Of course, not all of them will be available. A certain amount is occupied by system files. For those who will not have enough remaining capacity, it is recommended to install memory cards. With their help, the storage for downloading additional software will be expanded to 32 GB.

Autonomy

The ZTE Blade A510 is equipped with a rechargeable battery with a capacity of 2,200 milliamps per hour. It is worth noting that its characteristics are not outstanding. Communication opportunities

The communication manual ZTE Blade A510 will help you deal with communication capabilities. It says that the device is designed to work with two SIM-cards of mobile operators. There is support for fourth-generation networks. Also, using headphones, the device can be used as a radio station.
