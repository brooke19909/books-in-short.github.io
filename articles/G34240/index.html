Park Hotel Olympiets: how to get there

You can reach the hotel by public transport, taxi and private car. It is more convenient to take a taxi from Sheremetyevo Airport. The journey takes only a few minutes.

Getting here from Moscow by car, you need to get onto the Moscow Ring Road, and then follow either the M11 toll road or along the Leningradskoye Highway towards the airport. At the sign "Sheremetyevo-2" turn right, passing the airport, turn right again, follow the village of Klyazma and after about 2 km at the intersection turn left. The whole journey will take no more than 20 minutes.

Near the hotel there is a bus stop number 29, 43, 11, 14 and minibus number 62. They run in Khimki, where it is easy to get from the capital by electric train from the railway station, by bus number 345, departing from the Rechnoy Vokzal metro station, or by minibus No. 469, going from the Planetarnaya metro station.

Description

Olympiets is a park hotel occupying a vast territory of 49 hectares. Such a solid scale allowed designers to conveniently and harmoniously arrange the buildings, a separate guest house and all infrastructure facilities. The hotel was built in a pine forest, so the air around is filled with life-giving phytoncides.

This is an additional bonus for guests, because, relaxing in the Olympian, you can get free coniferous aromatherapy every day. In addition to magical aromas, neat paths and many different colors are pleasing to the eye. There is a spacious free parking zone for everyone who arrives at Olympiets by car, a wonderful playground is equipped for kids, there are several sports facilities for outdoor enthusiasts, and for the convenience of all guests, there are shops, an ATM, a summer outdoor pool , winter skating rink, multiple walking areas.

An important feature: this park hotel is a non-smoking area, but for those who can not part with this addiction, there are specially designated places.

Infrastructure

Olympiets is a park hotel where guests are welcomed at any time of the day, which is especially convenient for arrivals with evening and night flights to Sheremetyevo. Reception staff, located in the main building, are ready to meet and provide rooms for guests day and night. In the spacious and stylishly decorated lobby there are bars, restaurants, beautifully decorated relaxation areas with comfortable upholstered furniture and tables. At the reception you can order a taxi, hand over things for washing, ironing, dry cleaning, use a photocopy machine and fax machine. There are no elevators in the lobby. To use them, you need to go up to the second floor.

Park Hotel Olympiets in Khimki is ideal for business events. Entrepreneurs of all categories can rent any of the 12 conference rooms here. Among them are small, designed for 35 people, and large, accommodating up to 700 participants. In addition to the halls, the hotel has 11 classrooms. All these rooms are individually designed, furnished with modern furniture and equipped with all necessary equipment.





For celebrations and anniversaries, the Olympiets Hotel provides themed banquet rooms, as well as tents and outdoor summer areas for up to 900 guests.

Leisure

Muscovites go for a weekend in Khimki for outdoor activities. Park Hotel Olympiets is located a stone's throw from the paint club of the same name, which is an important advantage for many guests.
