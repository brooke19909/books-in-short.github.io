Transfer of finances using the Sberbank Online service

To transfer funds between Sberbank cards using your personal account, you will need access to the Internet, since the operation can be performed exclusively online. To carry out the action, you will need a plastic carrier tied to the specified service.

To connect your personal account:

  1. Go to the website of Sberbank.
  2. Fill out the registration form.
  3. Enter the card number in a special line.
  4. Wait for the arrival of SMS with an activation code.
  5. By entering the authorization code on the site, you can authorize your personal account and, accordingly, gain access to all the services provided.

What to do next?

After authorization in your account, you should perform certain actions:

  1. Go to the section of bank transfers and payments.
  2. Select a transfer to the card.
  3. Enter the number of the plastic where the funds should be transferred, the amount to be transferred, confirm the operation.

You can also transfer money to a Sberbank card at an ATM.

Transfer of finances through an ATM

To transfer money from one plastic card to another, you can use an ATM. The transfer operation is performed as follows:

  1. You need to insert your card into the ATM card reader.
  2. Select the section of money transfers of money, going to payments and transfers.
  3. Choose sending money.
  4. Enter the card number to which the funds should be transferred.
  5. Indicate the required transfer amount.
  6. Confirm the operation.

Crediting of funds during transfers using an ATM occurs instantly. However, the check issued by the device after the transaction is recommended to be stored until the receipt of money on the recipient's card. In case the money is not received, the check will help confirm the fact of performing the specified operation and debiting funds from your own payment plastic means.

Transfers made from cards to cards issued by Sberbank are carried out by a financial institution without charging a commission. If you send money to plastic media issued by another bank, the amount of commission will depend on the conditions of the company that issued the card to the recipient.

special instructions

When transferring money to a Sberbank of Russia card using an ATM, it is important to consider the following:

  1. To protect the pin code from prying eyes, it is recommended to cover the digital keyboard with a purse or palm during its entry. Such a precaution will not allow an attacker to see and remember the access code.
  2. With the help of an ATM, in addition to money transfers, you can perform many other operations - replenish the balance of a mobile phone, make a loan payment, pay traffic fines, deposit funds into the light of an electronic wallet, and replenish your own card balance.
  3. It must be remembered that the locks installed at the entrances to the premises with ATMs do not require entering access codes.
  4. Before using an ATM, it is recommended to inspect the card reader and numeric keypad for any foreign objects or devices attached.
  5. Only a limited amount can be transferred from one card to another through an ATM - at least 100 rubles, maximum 300,000 rubles. The amount of the limit depends on the class of the plastic card.
  6. It is worth considering that after the transaction is completed, it is impossible to cancel it. If you need a refund, you will have to contact their recipient.
