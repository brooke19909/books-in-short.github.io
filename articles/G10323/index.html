Description and address of the hotel "Slavyanka". Hotel "Slavyanka": reviews of tourists, photos

When visiting the capital of Russia, many have a question about where to settle. It is definitely impossible to answer it. Here, financial opportunities and personal preferences play a big role . But still there are hotels that can be recommended to people with different incomes and wishes. Hotel "Slavyanka" is a place where there are comfortable living conditions, high-class service, pleasant service and reasonable prices. The location of the hotel is also very convenient, and the reviews are only positive. What attracts guests?

A bit of history

The history of the Slavyanka Hotel is quite long. Perhaps this explains its high level. Very often star guests settle in it, choosing one of the best places to stay. The Slavyanka Hotel was founded in 1935. From the very beginning to today, it belonged to the Ministry of Defense. But the doors of the hotel are open not only for the military, but also for civilian visitors as well. Built a hotel on the site of the Church of John the Warrior.

Initially, it was called the hotel of the Central House of Artists, then it was renamed to the Central House of Artists. Only in 1995 she received the name "Slavyanka". Despite the strict and even a bit gloomy appearance, a pleasant atmosphere, coziness and modern design reigns inside. It is cleanliness, order and strict rules that attract guests here.

Location

The location of any hotel is important. Moscow guests wish to stay closer to the center, iconic sights and places of relaxation. Hotel "Slavyanka", the photo of which is in this article, is located very well. One of the advantages is the proximity of the metro. So, you can get to any corner of the capital. Dostoevskaya metro station is just a five-minute walk away. Near the hotel - Prospect Mira, Tsvetnoy Boulevard and Novoslobodskaya.

If you take a short walk, you can visit the Central Academic Theater of the Russian Army, the Olimpiysky Sports Complex, the Beasts Theater "Grandfather Durov's Corner", Catherine Park and the Central Museum of the Armed Forces. Catherine Park has a lot of entertainment: a court, a dance floor, a planetarium and a pond. Hotel "Slavyanka", reviews - confirmation of this, is located in a very picturesque place. Even from the window of a comfortable room you can admire the beauty of the surroundings. Not every hotel located in the bustling business capital is capable of boasting such an indicator. If you wish, you can visit the sights of Moscow and get to them without problems by metro or take a walk while admiring the local beauties. The Sheremetyevo Airport is 29 kilometers, the Riga Station is 1 kilometer, and the city center is only 3.5 kilometers.

Infrastructure

Hotel "Slavyanka" offers its guests both the organization of leisure and serious events. For this, the hotel has everything you need. A large conference room equipped with all modern requirements. There is also an additional room for events with 30 seats and a meeting room for 10 people. A small but very cozy restaurant for 50 people, located on the ground floor of the hotel, is always happy to offer its guests only the best dishes.

There is also a cafe, which is located on the 5th floor of the hotel. Here you can have an excellent breakfast and just spend time in a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere. It should be noted that breakfast is served for all guests absolutely free. For a fee, you can visit the wellness center, where there is everything for active and healthy relaxation for the body. It offers guests a swimming pool, sauna, hammam and professional massage therapist.

Description

Hotel Slavyanka is a strict building without any pathos, having 7 floors. Her appearance does not cause much enthusiasm. This is a solid building without architectural excesses, at the same time evoking a sense of reliability and order. In total, the hotel has 306 rooms. Guests can choose from rooms of category 1, 2 and 4, junior suites, suites and double apartments, consisting of three rooms. The average room rate is 2800 rubles per day. Hotel Slavyanka (reviews about the hotel are enthusiastic and speak for themselves) offers apartments for every taste. It should be noted that the room rate does not affect the quality of service. Free Wi-Fi and breakfast are available for guests.

Rooms

By choosing any room, you can get friendly service, high-class service and the whole range of services that the Slavyanka Hotel offers its guests. Moscow has hotels of different levels, but not all of them provide such guests with such a service. The rooms have access to free Internet and all means of communication (telephone). They are also equipped with a TV, air conditioning and fridge.

All rooms have a minibar, which can be used for a fee. For the convenience of visitors, a safe is provided for storing valuables and money. Upon request, a non-smoking room can be provided. Each guest receives the maximum attention of qualified staff. High-quality and unobtrusive hotel service is her calling card.

Apartments

The most luxurious are the apartments. They have three rooms, one of which is a living room. Two luxurious bedrooms amaze with their cosiness. The room has two bathrooms and a bath. The atmosphere meets all the requirements of a modern guest. The living room has a sofa for relaxing and a coffee table. This room also has a dining table for eating. The room has a safe with an electronic lock. Air conditioning will help to escape from the heat in the summer.

The apartments are equipped with telephone, refrigerator, TV. Each guest can use a hairdryer, which is also available. The minibar will help to relax, but its use is paid. After taking a bath, you can wear a cozy bathrobe and slippers provided by the hotel. There is a tea station that will help brighten up evenings after a hard day. The room costs around 13,000 rubles, but this is an affordable price.

Double Suite

The rooms of the Slavyanka Hotel are comfort and coziness. Suite double is not inferior in its decoration to apartments. It consists of two rooms: a living room and a bedroom. There is also a bathroom and bath. The living room has everything for a great vacation. It is equipped with a luxurious sofa and a coffee table. It has a large dining table where you can spend time tasting mouth-watering dishes from a local restaurant. The bedroom has a large double bed. As in all rooms, there is a TV, telephone, refrigerator and air conditioning. For the convenience of visitors, a safe is provided. You can use the tea station or minibar, comfortably sitting in the living room in a warm terry bathrobe and soft slippers. The cost of this number varies between 8000-9000 rubles.

Twin Suite

These numbers can be called an analogue of double. They also consist of two rooms (living room and bedroom). They have all the amenities that are provided by the hotel. Air conditioning is not available in all rooms, it is offered at the choice of a guest. The interior of the rooms is very cozy and made only of the best materials. It is designed in a classic style, which is distinguished by some luxury. Each room is cozy and comfortable.

The atmosphere is calm at home. Combined with courteous and attentive service, she receives positive reviews. Number can be rented in the range from 7000 to 8000 rubles.

Studios

They consist of one room, which is conditionally divided into zones. The sleeping area is equipped with a comfortable double bed. The guest part of the room has a luxurious sofa and a coffee table. Also in the room there is a bathroom, a bathroom with a shower. The only drawback is the lack of air conditioning. But in cold weather there is no need for it, and this does not become a problem. The room is equipped with everything for a comfortable stay. Such apartments can be rented for 5000-7000 rubles per day. The whole room is decorated in a luxurious classic style.

Standard rooms

These are no less comfortable rooms that differ only in their size. Each of them consists of one room, a bathroom and a bath. The room is divided into two zones: bedroom and living room. A cozy double bed and fine textiles will allow you to relax and unwind after a hard day.

The guest area is equipped with a sofa and a coffee table. The room has a TV, fridge or minibar (optional) and telephone. For convenience, a hairdryer and kettle are also provided. As with all hotel apartments, the standard ones have a safe for storing valuables. There is such a number from 4000 to 5000 rubles.

Budget options

The block is a room with a single bed, a TV and a telephone. This is a very budget option, the cost of which is about 2000 rubles per day. It is convenient to stay for a short time, having not very great financial opportunities. Room decoration is not particularly affected by the low price. Cozy discreet atmosphere, high-quality furniture, good textiles and friendly service - all this is guaranteed by the Slavyanka Hotel. Moscow is a large and popular city, and it is very difficult to find a place to stay in it at this price.

Service

In addition to the standard services associated with staying in rooms, the hotel offers its guests an excellent additional service. This is a mandatory ATM and gift shop. There is also a travel agency for those who wish to enjoy the beauties of the capital. If necessary, a photocopy of documents can be made at the hotel. Guests are offered to book airline or train tickets without leaving the hotel.

On the territory of the Slavyanka Hotel there is a medical center and a clothing repair bureau. At the request of the client transport services are provided. Public parking is available near the hotel. The rooms can be equipped with an extra bed. Hotel "Slavyanka", reviews of employees about which are also important, is one of the most convenient places to stay in the capital. It is not flashy luxury, but has a good location, polite staff, comfortable rooms and a range of services for visitors.

If you want to stay in a calm and welcoming place, then the Slavyanka Hotel will suit you. Employee reviews suggest that this is a great place. But if a person happily does his job, then he does it just fine. That is why the guests come back here, coming to Moscow.

Features to be aware of

So that some rules of the Slavyanka Hotel do not come as a surprise, you need to know about them in advance. Most importantly, pets are not allowed in the rooms. Therefore, fauna lovers should choose another hotel or place their pets in special institutions (hotels for pets).

Reservations are made around the clock, and check-in is from 12 to 14 hours. You can pay for your stay in cash or by credit card. Hotel address: Building 3, Building 2, Suvorov Square, Hotel Slavyanka. Here guests are always welcome, ready to give them maximum attention and provide the highest service.
