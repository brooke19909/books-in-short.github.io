Tips for tourists traveling to Turkey

1. In the hot season, you need to use sunscreen with an SPF level of more than 30, as well as sunblock. Be sure to wear a hat to avoid burns or sunstroke.

2. Do not forget to bring a first aid kit with all the necessary medicines.

3. For most products, the price is too high, so feel free to bargain.

4. Nudism is prohibited on any beaches in Turkey.

5. Refrain from buying gold items, they can be cheap, but be mixed with a lot of copper.

6. If you need a rental car, be sure to choose a car with insurance.

7. The religion of Turkey is Islam, so try to be respectful of both religion and the country's traditions.

8. Excursions are relatively expensive, so you need to have money with you. You may be persuaded to buy an amulet against the evil eye, but this is just a ploy, do not get fooled.

9. Smoking in public places in Turkey is strictly prohibited.

10. Do not drink tap water, it is better to limit yourself to bottled water.

11. The export of antiques from the country is prohibited.

12. Almost all stores accept credit cards, but you should still have cash with you.

13. Raki - Turkish alcoholic drink, alcohol content of 45%, so do not drink it like beer.

14. The rules of the road in Turkey are not a dogma, therefore, crossing the road, carefully look around.

15. When entering a house or mosque in Turkey, you need to take off your shoes.
