Resort Sentido Turan Prince Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Side): description and reviews of tourists

On the coast of one of the most popular resorts in the Mediterranean Sea, there is an excellent five-star Sentido Turan Prince Hotel 5 * hotel. The city of Side is very advantageously located between the larger Alanya and Antalya, which makes it suitable for a relaxing family vacation.





Location Features

This region is famous for its clean beaches and the most favorable climate. It was here that Turkish cuisine was greatly influenced by the best culinary traditions gathered throughout the Mediterranean - they prepare a variety of fish and seafood dishes according to recipes passed down from generation to generation.

Since the city was founded by the ancient Greeks, the atmosphere of antiquity was especially preserved here. Active tourists will be able to find many attractions that have remained from the first settlers. Ancient household items, an amphitheater, temples, a market square, all this will surely attract the attention of vacationers and leave pleasant memories of the trip.

Hotel info

Sentido Turan Prince Hotel 5 * belongs to one of the best hotel chains of SENTIDO, which guarantees a comfortable stay and professional service aimed at the maximum relaxation of holidaymakers.

The complex was built in 2004, the last renovation (full renovation) was carried out in 2015. The territory includes four 5-story residential buildings and thirteen 2-story Park Villa buildings, which are located across the road opposite the main building.

Vacationers with children are offered many options for pastime. Attentive and interesting animation, a water park, a rich green area, delicious cuisine and wonderful views - all this will become an integral part of busy days spent on the Turkish coast.

sights

Well, enough has been said about the rating of Sentido Turan Prince 5 * (Turkey, Side), as well as about its advantages and advantages. Now you can pay a little attention to the sights, for the sake of which, by the way, many people stay at this hotel - it is very convenient to get from them.

The Temple of Apollo (the patron saint of beauty and art) was erected in the 2nd century A.D. The antique building was badly damaged during the earthquake and today there are only a few columns of white marble and picturesque ruins. According to legend, the temple was erected by order of the military commander Anthony in honor of the Egyptian Queen Cleopatra. This place is a symbol of the city and one of the most romantic attractions of the country.

The Temple of Athena is located to the right of the Temple of Apollo and is a ruin, as if raised from the seabed. It offers stunning views of the sea, against which great pictures will be obtained.

The open-air amphitheater is one of the largest antique buildings with a capacity of 18,000 spectators. Sea battles, battles of gladiators with predators were organized here, and in the V-VI century the theater was used as a church.

The famous Manavgat waterfall is located near Side, and in fact is the threshold of the river, where observation platforms are installed at different levels. Crowds of tourists gather in this picturesque place to take amazing and spectacular photographs.

The ancient Roman aqueduct stretches along the northwest side of the city. At one time, it was used to transport water to the city from the Manavgat River.
