Guest house "Palladium" in Vityazevo: description and reviews

Where to relax in the summer at sea? A banal question, which every year on the eve of the hot season is asked by more than one person who dreams of a quality vacation. In the variety of offers on the tourism market, pay attention to Vityazevo.





This is a small village near Anapa (Krasnodar Territory), whose visit card has long been the open Black Sea and a wide sandy beach, stretching for almost a kilometer.

Guest house "Palladium" (Vityazevo). general information

The Palladium Guest House 2 * is ready to welcome visitors at any time of the day. The complex is three-story, modern, decorated with shell stone. Against the background of the rest of the buildings, the building stands out with huge stained-glass windows laid out of dark glass. The fenced area is paved with paving tiles. There are two pools.

Hotel "Palladium" in Vityazevo is located on the second resort line. Distance from the sea 400 meters. The place is quite quiet, despite the proximity of the promenade with lots of entertainment venues: cafes, restaurants, discos, an amusement park, bowling center. Therefore, here supporters of quiet leisure and lovers of active pastime will feel comfortable. Children can have fun in the Byzantium amusement park located next to the Palladium Hotel in Vityazevo.





The photo colorfully conveys the charming beauty of the complex and the territory on which it is located.

On a note

The friendliness of the staff, professionalism and willingness to help guests with any questions are reflected in numerous reviews about this institution. The price includes accommodation, use of the playground, pool.





Check-in time is 14:00, estimated time is 12:00. To regular customers the guest house provides a 5% discount. Hotel address: Krasnodar Territory, the city of Anapa, p. Vityazevo, per. Spartan, 3.

Description of rooms

"Palladium" in Vityazevo provides guests with a wide selection of 1-, 2-, 3-, 4-bed rooms of different levels of comfort. All of them are equipped with modern new furniture, selected in pastel colors (double sofa, sleeping set, bedside tables, dressing table), and appliances (TV, fridge). If necessary, a cot is provided free of charge.

The rooms are bright and comfortable, with balconies, equipped with air conditioning. On the floors - carpet. An iron with ironing board is on every floor. Towels are provided in units of 2 per 1 guest. Linen is changed on demand, no more than once every 5 days. Laundry service is available.

Internet access is round the clock.

Beach

The beach area, located a 15-20 minute walk from the Palladium guesthouse in Vityazevo, is fully equipped for a good and quality vacation. Outdoor playgrounds, cafes, covered decks, deckchairs - everything is created here so that relaxing at sea is comfortable. Passive leisure can be diversified by visiting the dolphinarium and the aquarium of exotic fish, jet skiing, water slides, catamarans. Olympia Water Park is a 7-minute walk.





The shallow shore, clear water, shallow sea. Water warms up from +22 to +28 . The period of the swimming season is May-October.

Excursions

There are a lot of proposals for visiting various attractions of the Krasnodar Territory. Guests of the resort have to choose the most interesting for themselves. Anapa, Gelendzhik, Abrau-Durso, Taman, Novorossiysk, Temryuk - each place is unique in its own way.

Anapa

The mud, climatic, balneological resort of Russia, located in the southwestern part of the Krasnodar Territory. A place at the junction of the Taman Peninsula and the Greater Caucasus. The rich natural landscape with forest foothills, lowland plains and sea estuaries, a unique climate where the air of the sea and dry steppes mixes, the largest number of sunny days per year among all the Black Sea resorts are key factors that attract visitors here not only from nearby regions, but also to the distant abroad. It even heals sand. Dry and hot, it quickly warms up the body, having a beneficial effect on all body systems. Arriving in Anapa, be sure to take a walk along the promenade, visit the excavations of the ancient city of Gorgippia, take a look at the Temple of St. Onufry the Great, take a picture near the Turkish Gate, take a boat trip on a yacht.

Novorossiysk

Hero City A place filled with significant events that took place during the years of World War II. You can penetrate the time when fathers and grandfathers selflessly defended their homeland, in the memorial complex Malaya Zemlya. About the formation and development of domestic shipbuilding will tell a tour of the cruiser "Mikhail Kutuzov." The tastiest wines are offered at the Myskhako winery.

Gelendzhik

The city that arose in 1831 with the goal of strengthening the southern borders of Russia. Be sure to visit the local safari park, get a lot of emotions in the Golden Bay water park, take a walk in the Old Park in Kabardinka - a unique place where all the architectural monuments of the world are presented in miniatures.

Abrau-Durso

Terrain with unique natural conditions for growing vineyards. At the Museum of Champagne Wines, you can learn about the process of producing champagne, taste several types of sparkling drink and, if you wish, buy your favorite brand.

We recommend visiting the holy Neberjaya springs and Pshad waterfalls. The unique attractions of these places and the proximity to nature will cause unforgettable emotions for every guest of the Krasnodar Territory.
