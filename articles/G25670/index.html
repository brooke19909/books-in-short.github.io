Hotel Side World Star Hotel 3 * (Side, Turkey): Tourist reviews

The popular Turkish resort of Side is famous among tourists, including well-developed infrastructure. In any case, renting affordable, affordable housing in this city will not be too difficult. For example, some tourists stay in Side at the Side World Star Hotel 3 * hotel.

Where is

This complex was built directly in the city of Side. Directly opposite it is a small peninsula with the ruins of the Temple of Apollo. Side city center is only about 2 km from this hotel. That is, by public transport, residents of the complex can get here in about 10-15 minutes. If necessary, hotel guests can walk to the center of Side on foot.





Tourists who rent rooms at Side World Star Hotel 3 * arrive , usually at Antalya Airport. It is located about 87 km from the hotel. On a shuttle bus of a tour operator from this airport, hotel guests can thus reach it in about an hour and a half.

This complex is built relatively far from the sea. On the sites of tour operators there is information that the distance to the beach from it is 800 meters. However, unfortunately, this information is not entirely correct. According to vacationers who have already rented rooms in this hotel, in fact, the distance to the beach of the hotel from its buildings is approximately 2 km. But in any case, for the convenience of tourists, the hotel also provides a transfer to the sea.
