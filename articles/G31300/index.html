How to get a mortgage with a small official salary: the necessary documents, the procedure and conditions for registration, terms of payment

One of the common reasons for refusing a mortgage is a low income client. What if the official income is lower than required, or it is not enough to get the right amount? What if the employer pays wages in an envelope? Is it possible to take a mortgage if there is no official income at all? How to get a mortgage with a small official salary? Consider options for correcting the situation.

How to calculate the payment?

Each bank has its own system for calculating the maximum possible customer payment. In some banking organizations, the amount of payment cannot exceed 40% of the average monthly income. In others, this threshold is 60%. There is a calculation scheme where the living wage in the region is taken as the basis. The amount of the subsistence minimum and all mandatory expenses of the client are deducted from the salary: maintenance and education of children, utilities and the like. Based on the remaining amount, the maximum loan payment is calculated. Some banks consider minor children as another minimum cost of living to the borrower's expenses. And others do not use this scheme and write this article of expenses according to the client. Surely you can find out how the bank will calculate the amount only in practice. The estimated income of the client should not be less than 30,000 rubles in the Moscow region. With a smaller amount, applications usually go into denial at the first stage of scoring. What to do if the amount is insufficient to receive a loan or the bank offers less than necessary? How to get a mortgage with a small official salary?

Bank form reference

Perhaps the easiest way to solve the problem with low official income. Each bank has a form that the employer must fill out "by hand", where the full income of the client will be indicated, taking into account the "gray" wages. What you need to specify in such a certificate:

1. Income must be stable and accrued at least three months prior to applying for a mortgage. Some banks need to provide information for at least six months, check this in advance. In an ideal form, in the certificate indicate income for the year. In a special table, each worked month and the amount of payment (in figures) are prescribed. In the last line write the word "TOTAL" and the summed income for the specified period.

2. All information fields are filled in, namely: the full name of the organization and its details, position and data of the employee for whom the certificate is issued. The details of the organization and the landline also appear. A fixed number is required by the requirements of most banks, this should be given special attention.

3. The amount of earnings is indicated in full: official and gray wages.

4. On the form are two signatures: an accountant and a manager. Both with decryption. And a round branded original organization seal.

Often, employees whose employer company works according to a gray scheme have problems getting help. The organization is afraid that the bank will transfer the data to the tax service and will be punished with a fine or liquidated. In fact, these fears have no basis. Firstly, the bank is not entitled to disclose data that carry financial secrets. This is not even in his interests, since he will lose the client (and not one if someone else from the company decides to apply for a loan). Guarantor

What is the difference between the concepts of co-borrower and guarantor in a mortgage? There may be several guarantors in real estate lending. These should be people with sufficient income to get a loan. Unlike the co-borrower, the guarantor does not have rights to a share in the credited housing. The guarantor performs exclusively the guarantee function of repayment of the debt. If the client does not have enough income to receive a mortgage, he has the right to attract guarantors. But, as a rule, they are difficult to find. In case of non-payment of monthly payments by the borrower, the bank may present claims against the guarantor. Military mortgage

For people related to public service and officially registered in the ranks of internal troops or agencies, there is a wonderful program - a military mortgage. The only negative of which is that it is better to start using it as soon as possible. Can I get a mortgage with a small salary?
