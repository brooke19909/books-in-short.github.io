Hotel description Perla Sun Park

The modern, intimate Hotel Perla Sun Park is the epitome of style and comfort. It is located in the Bulgarian resort town of Primorsko near the oak forest and the turbulent river called Devilskata. Near the complex is a modern water amusement park and sports center. The nine-story hotel building, built in 2007, is modernly designed and equipped in accordance with the latest hotel requirements. The luxurious and well-maintained Perla Sun Park is located in one of the most picturesque areas of the city with amazingly beautiful natural landscape. This hotel is perfect for tourists seeking a peaceful, relaxing holiday.



Rooms

Perla Sun Park is ready to offer 150 comfortable apartments of various categories: luxury (quadruple) and standard (double with one bed). The rooms are equipped with modern furniture, shower, washbasins, air conditioning, bathrooms, refrigerators, TV satellite and TV panels. Each has a terrace with amazing sea views. The apartments have all the necessary toiletries. Periodically cleaning and linen change.
