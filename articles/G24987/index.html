Lunskoye Lake, Nizhny Novgorod: a brief description

Nobody wants to spend a summer vacation in the city. For this, such places are selected in which there is a forest, river or lake. Surprisingly, the inhabitants of Nizhny Novgorod do not even have to travel far in order to enjoy unity with nature. In Sormovsky district there is a wonderful pond where in summer the water warms up to + 22 ... + 25 ¬∞ . Beach lovers, fishing lovers and just those who want to have a good time come to it. Probably, many have already guessed that we are talking about Lake Lunsky (Nizhny Novgorod). So, let's get closer acquainted with this part of nature, which is not touched by the hand of man.

About the lake

Lunskoye Lake is an open reservoir. You can relax on any shore. The reservoir itself has a slightly curved shape. The coastline is winding, in some places overgrown with reeds. But there are also beach areas with a sandy bottom. The depth here is different. There are shallow areas (up to 1 m), which are heavily overgrown with vegetation. They are located at the far end. Most often, fishermen catch fish near them. There are also deep-sea pits - up to seven meters.

The coastline of the reservoir is beautiful and picturesque, this can be seen in the photo. Lunskoye Lake (Nizhny Novgorod) is perfect for a beach holiday. There are gently sloping places with spacious sandy beaches. But they alternate with low slopes, which sharply descend into the water. A pine forest surrounds the pond. Closer to the shore, deciduous trees grow. Due to such vegetation in this area, a favorable climate for recreation. People enjoy clean fragrant air, a little moist, which is especially good in extreme heat.

Swimming in Lunskoye Lake (Nizhny Novgorod) is allowed. SES regularly takes samples of soil and water. They correspond to the norm. It is safe to rest here for both adults and children.

Fishing

In addition to a beach holiday, you can have fun with a fishing rod. However, in order to leave with the catch, you need to know some secrets. Fish in the pond is cunning, it must be fed. Such a method is the key to good fishing. In Lunskoye Lake (Nizhny Novgorod) there are pikes, perches, crucian carp, bream, roach and other species. Recommended fishing from a boat. Near the coast in the season there are always a lot of vacationers, because they, swimming, scare away the fish. It is important to know that you cannot use a boat during spawning.

Where is?

Getting to Lake Lunsky (Nizhny Novgorod) is easy. It is located in the industrial Sormovsky district. Public transport, in particular the 172nd route, goes to the reservoir. Using it, the lake can be reached from the city center. The journey takes no more than 40 minutes.
