Hotel Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 *: reviews and photos

It's no secret that Egypt is perhaps the most popular country among those tourists who crave the sea, sun and entertainment. In turn, one of the largest resort cities in the country is Hurghada. It is here that the large hotel complex called Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * is located. Many travelers are interested in additional information about this place, including service, quality of accommodation and food, proximity to the sea, etc.

Where is the hotel?





If you want to have a wonderful rest at the sea and enjoy all the delights of oriental culture, then you will surely like the Egyptian city of Hurghada. Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * - a large hotel complex, which is located just 300 meters from the sea shore. The distance to the historic city center is about 15 kilometers, to the nearest international airport - 10 kilometers. As a rule, a travel company (or the hotel itself) provides tourists with a transfer service to the hotel building, so do not worry about the road.

Brief infrastructure description

It is immediately worth noting that the Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * hotel is located next to the huge Jaz Aquamarine Resort. These hotels have a common area with sixteen swimming pools, beautiful gardens, bars, restaurants and shops that all guests can use. Here you will find three-story buildings built in a simple but elegant style. The territory is really huge, so you definitely will not be bored here. By the way, both hotels belong to the same owner.

Hotel complex Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 *: photo and description of rooms





In fact, this hotel can be considered relatively large - on its territory there are 300 spacious rooms. For the most part, these are standard options with a sleeping and living area, but there are also large family rooms designed to accommodate a large family with children or a group of friends.

Regardless of the category, you will find a large, bright and clean room with a luxurious modern setting, in which, however, there are some oriental motifs. Naturally, the rooms have enough new furniture, including large comfortable beds, bedside tables, wardrobes and sofas. There is also a private balcony or terrace with outdoor furniture - here you can breathe fresh air and enjoy views of the garden or indoor pools.

There is also a complex of necessary household appliances. In particular, with the help of an air conditioner, guests can create the most comfortable temperature conditions, which is simply necessary in the summer heat. There is also a minibar, but only drinking water is provided free of charge, all other drinks need to be paid. In a small personal safe you can store documents and other valuables, and watch satellite (even some Russian) channels on TV. You can make calls to other cities and countries by phone, but this is not included in the room rate.

Of course, each room has a spacious bathroom with a shower. There is also a toilet, sink, large toilet mirror. Also, guests are entitled to a set of towels and personal care products. By the way, cleaning is carried out every day, so you are unlikely to have to complain about cleanliness.

What kind of food does the hotel offer?





For many tourists, this issue is extremely important. So what kind of food scheme does the hotel Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * offer? Traveler reviews indicate that the food here is really at a decent level. Three times a day, guests are invited to the stylish main restaurant, where generous buffets are organized for them. The restaurant's menu is quite diverse: it offers dishes of international, Mediterranean, Italian and Egyptian cuisines, so there will be plenty to choose from. Naturally, on the tables there is always a sufficient amount of fresh fruits that tourists like so much, especially in the summer.

According to former guests, you should definitely visit the bar in the lobby. Here you will be offered soft drinks, exotic cocktails, as well as alcohol from an Egyptian manufacturer. Another bar is located by the pool - here, in addition to drinks, they give real homemade ice cream, which is liked not only by children, but also by adults.

Beach and water holidays for tourists





Only 300 meters away is a private beach that guests of the Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * and Jaz Aquamarine Resort can use. The beach here is sandy, but the entrance to the sea is not convenient everywhere, so you should stock up on special shoes. By the way, there is a large section of shallows near the shore, where children, as well as tourists who cannot swim, will feel comfortable. Of course, there are plenty of sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach, and you can also rent a beach towel.

Fans of more active types of recreation will not have to be bored either. In particular, a beautiful volleyball court is equipped on the beach, where guests often arrange fun competitions. Also at a very reasonable price you can rent a boat, a catamaran, go water-skiing, take a short boat ride, go snorkelling and even go fishing. In a word, here everyone will find something to their liking.

Hurghada (Hurghada), Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 *: additional service at the hotel





Naturally, the hotel has all the conditions for a truly comfortable stay without domestic problems. Dry cleaning and laundry services are constantly available here. If you rented a vehicle, you can leave it in the free parking lot at the hotel, which is very convenient. There is no medical assistance point on site, but in case of health problems you will definitely be called for a doctor, whose services will have to be paid separately.

In public areas, as well as hotel rooms, there is constant free Internet access, which is important for modern travelers. There are also shops and trading stalls where you can buy basic necessities, various trifles and interesting oriental souvenirs.

Entertainment and leisure guests





Naturally, tourists who prefer to spend the bulk of their time in the hotel complex will not be bored. To begin with, it is worth saying that there are several outdoor pools, as well as water slides for adults, who work every day. Tennis enthusiasts can rent a court at any convenient time for an affordable price.

There is a fitness center and a gym that will appeal to tourists who prefer to always stay in great shape. You can visit the SPA center, which offers a fairly wide range of cosmetic and relaxing procedures. Here you can visit the bathhouse, sauna, jacuzzi and massage rooms.

Naturally, this is not all that the city of Hurghada has to offer. Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * has its own tour desk, where they will help you choose an interesting tour and talk about the most interesting sights of the surroundings.

Also on the territory of the hotel there is a team of animators who entertain guests in the daytime, and in the evenings arrange exciting, memorable shows.

Are there any conditions for living with children?

Since today many parents travel with children, the priority issue for them is the availability of certain amenities. According to reviews, the hotel complex Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * is perfect for children. If necessary, an extra bed will be delivered to your room, and in the restaurant you can count on a special chair for feeding a small child.

Your child, you can rest assured that there will be something to do. On the territory of the hotel Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * there is a spacious but safe children's pool. It is definitely worth taking the child to a water slide, where everyone can have a great time. There is also a mini club for children, where professional educators work with them. There is also a well-equipped playground with sandboxes and swings. And, of course, every day your child will be entertained by animators who, according to the guests, really know their business.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?





For what category of vacationers is the hotel complex Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * suitable? Hurghada is a wonderful place for beach and sightseeing holidays, so travel lovers will be quite comfortable here. According to the guests, the hotel is perfect for families with children. And also this complex was chosen by youth companies, as well as newlyweds and even older couples. By the way, for the most part, Germans and residents of other European countries stay here, as well as local vacationers. Naturally, Russian tourists here also relax quite often, so you can find new friends.

Hotel Traveler Reviews

A huge amount of really useful information can be obtained by asking the opinion of those people who have already visited this or that place. So what do tourists say about the Jaz Bluemarine Resort 5 * hotel? Reviews are for the most part positive. Guests in the first place note the huge joint territory of hotel complexes, as well as truly comfortable and clean rooms.

A decent nutrition plan - everyone will be able to find dishes and drinks suitable for themselves. Sometimes, however, there are problems with free tables. The sea is very beautiful, but lovers of swimming in depth will have to go along the bottom for several minutes. And on the other hand, this is a huge plus for vacationers with young children. There are a lot of shops on the territory where, by the way, you can "shop" at very reasonable prices - reviews by experienced travelers say that in some tourist shops in the city the cost of the same goods is much higher.

The employees of the complex speak English, some employees speak Russian and German quite well, therefore, communication problems should not arise. The service, by the way, is also at a decent level, as the staff here are polite, always ready to help, but unobtrusive.
