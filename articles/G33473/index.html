Prague is a charming, colorful, even slightly magical city with an interesting history and culture. Its atmosphere, saturated with bohemian charm, mystical legends about Czech alchemists, leaves no one indifferent. Prague is a very non-trivial and multifaceted city. Even among other European capitals, many of which are very magnificent, Prague has a very competitive position. According to many ratings, publications and surveys, it ranks first among cities where tourists want to return again and again.





What visa is issued for a trip to the Czech Republic?

Immersion in the Prague fairy tale, however, can be overshadowed by some formalities. Given that you need a standard Schengen visa to the Czech Republic, it is impossible to plan a spontaneous weekend trip. In all other respects, the Czech Republic is open and accessible to almost all categories of tourists, its airports accept low-cost low-cost flights, the city is connected to Europe with a dense network of railways.



Moreover, a visa to the Czech Republic goes to our tourists easier than, say, to Germany or Spain. The consulate of this country is on the list of "loyal" to Russian citizens.
