Acer Iconia W511: the appearance of the tablet, the amount of memory, specifications, user manual, features of operation and owner reviews

Positioning

The manufacturer positions the Acer Iconia W511 tablet as a universal mobile device for a wide range of tasks. In principle, the way it is. Before us is a very good tablet computer, which will more than fit as an entertainment and multimedia center.

It is also a great surfing tool. The desktop version of the Windows 8 operating system involves the full use of all browsers and related software. That is, the user can work with any sites, including business portals, where multi-step authentication with digital signatures is required.

Well, in trains, the Acer Iconia W511 may well replace the ordinary laptop. Here you can run exactly the same applications as on laptops. In general, the appearance of such a platform can be called the second birth of netbooks, which no one has heard of for several years. Only in this case, the "filling" is noticeably more powerful, and more interesting in terms of usability.

The W5 series comes in two versions. One differ from the other only in the amount of internal storage and support for 3G protocols. The W510 version, alas, does not support 3G networks, while the Acer Iconia W511 feels quite comfortable in them. You can also see the letter "P" in the model name. This indicates a preinstalled professional version of Windows (Windows 8 Professional).
