H Top Royal Beach Hotel 4 * (Spain, Costa Brava): description of rooms, service, reviews

Usually people are used to thinking of Europe as a reserved and cold part of the world, but here you can also find a place for an ideal beach holiday, this place is considered to be Spain. Spain is washed by the waters of the Bay of Biscay, the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea, and in addition to the mainland, the Canary and Balearic Islands belong to the territory of the country of burning flamenco.

Weather in Spain

The climate of Spain can be called perhaps the most favorable for relaxation, summer reigns 280 days a year, you can swim from late spring to late autumn, in winter you can spend holidays in ski resorts.

In Spain, you can easily rent an apartment, villa or apartment. Also here are concentrated a lot of modern hotels and hotels for every taste and budget. One of these hotels will be discussed in this article.





H Top Royal Beach Hotel 4 * (Spain)

A four-star hotel complex with a two-year overall rating of 4.33 (according to the most popular hotel ratings site from around the world). The hotel received the highest score for accommodation, after food and service.

Royal Beach is perfect for families with children, as well as for youth.

Location

From the hotel to one of the best beaches in the town of Lloret - Fenals, no more than three minutes in a calm step. The city center, which is considered the Costa Brava party center, is about ten minutes walk. Note that the staff of the complex organize a free shuttle service to other hotels of their network. Near Royal Beach, there are golf courses, a water park, and a menagerie. Thirty kilometers away is the air harbor of Girona.

Briefly about the hotel

H Top Royal Beach Hotel 4 * opened in 1973, three years ago it was partially reconstructed. The hotel is part of the H Top Hotels Group and is considered a hotel with ancient traditions, which offers services that can satisfy the most fastidious travelers. The building is a seven-story building, divided into three buildings, which total 357 cozy rooms. An outdoor pool and rooftop jacuzzi are on site. The beach at the hotel is sandy, municipal, but equipped with everything necessary, is 150 meters from the hotel.

Beach

Lloret de Mar Resort has two popular golden sand beaches - Lloret and Fenals Beach. On the shore of the latter is the H Top Royal Beach Hotel 4 *. Fenals has a fairly large swimming area (about 700 meters long) and is divided into bays that shelter it from large waves and wind, due to which the sea is crystal clear and relatively calm. It is very comfortable for guests of Royal Beach to relax on the shores of Fenals, as the hotel administration took care of the availability of umbrellas, mattresses and deck chairs, although for a fee.





Fenals among locals is considered the best beach in the city for families with children, not far from it there is a pine forest where you can take a walk after long swims and breathe clean air. On the beach there are various attractions: water skiing, parasailing, kayaking. From July 1 to the end of summer, a children's club is open here. In addition, along the perimeter of the beach there are lockers for storing things, racks of lifeguards, there are showers.

Additionally

On the roof of H Top Royal Beach Hotel 4 * there is a bathtub with hydromassage and an outdoor pool with a relaxation area equipped with canopies and sunbeds. For children there is a special playground for games. In the evenings, the hotel hosts recreational activities. The hotel is surrounded by a picturesque green garden.

Cost of living

Royal Beach Hotel is considered one of the not expensive, but very good hotel complexes. The cost of living here depends on the type of food chosen and the time of year.

A weekly trip to this hotel in the midst of the season (without meals, per person) will cost from 30 thousand rubles, according to the All inclusive system from 45 000. This price includes accommodation in a double room, airline tickets, insurance and transfer.

Those wishing to live in Royal Beach in the winter, a similar ticket will cost 40 thousand rubles.

When you purchase a tour using the early booking system, the cost of the ticket will be approximately 20% lower.

Comments from travelers to H Top Royal Beach Hotel 4

Guest reviews at this hotel are very controversial, but as you know, no institution of this kind can have 100% positive feedback. There will always be a client who is dissatisfied with something. We will try to objectively look at all the advantages and disadvantages of this hotel, and whether or not to go to relax at H Top Royal Beach is everyone's business.

pros

According to many tourists, the hotel has a great location. Located in the small cozy town of Lloret (Costa Brava), from where it is easy to get to Barcelona or Madrid. It takes less than an hour from the airport to the hotel. Supermarkets, cafes, bus stop within walking distance. The path from the hotel to the beach takes three minutes. The beach is small, but equipped, well maintained and clean. Sunset in the sea is pleasant, the water is clean.

Judging by the reviews, immediately upon arrival at the hotel you feel that guests are always welcome here. The staff is very attentive, friendly, the accommodation in the room is fast, the room is cleaned daily and of high quality. The number of rooms is very decent, the rooms are clean and bright, floor-to-ceiling windows.

As for nutrition, everything here is also beyond praise: every day they serve fish, amazing meat, which is prepared in various variations, a huge selection of drinks, quality champagne for breakfast. In the evenings, the animation team entertains guests.





Minuses

Among the minuses, tourists note the lack of children's entertainment, paid umbrellas and sun loungers on the beach. Many guests note in their reviews poor soundproofing and the presence of insects in the rooms. The housekeeping staff does not speak English, the cleaning lady will not explain in any way that you are currently busy, and you need to come back later. Also embarrassing is the lack of Wi-Fi in the H Top Royal Beach Hotel 4. Food, according to some hotel guests, is not quite diverse.

Summarize

Spain is an ideal holiday destination, both for youth and couples with children, and for people of respectable age. When choosing a cheap hotel, you should consider the option H Top Royal Beach Hotel 4, the service, location, comfort and wide range of services which are unlikely to disappoint you.
