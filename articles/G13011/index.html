Photo Studio Air Rooms: description, services

Make a beautiful and high-quality photo these days is not a problem. A modern interior, the right light, makeup, hairstyle, elegant clothes - all these are components of a professional photo. Photo Studio Air Room in Moscow provides the services of a make-up artist, stylist, photographer, and also rents out studios. Want to get a series of great photos in your portfolio? Then you to the professionals.

Description

Air Rooms is an interior studio with bright walls and panoramic windows. It is perfect for shooting professional photos and videos, various events, as well as for master classes in photography, thematic trainings. Air Rooms has two rooms, a make-up room and a relaxation area.

It is important to note that in the halls of the studio every week the styles, colors and shades of the interior change. Mostly light, delicate tones and airiness prevail here. This photo studio stands out with a large space of halls, which was created due to windows and light. For holidays, such as New Year's Eve, an additional Christmas decor is available.

The studio is equipped with modern lighting fixtures from Hensel. Photographers have access to the necessary synchronization kits for SLR cameras from different manufacturers.

Rental rules

For photographers who wish to rent one of the rooms of the studio Air Rooms, certain rules apply. To book a room, you must contact the management by phone numbers indicated on the official website of this photo studio, or by e-mail. Another main rule is a fifty percent advance payment for the rental of premises. It must be paid within seventy-two hours from the time of the reservation. Prepayment is a guarantee that the client takes responsibility for the property of the studio, the equipment that is located there.

Before making a prepayment, it is necessary to write to the photo studio by e-mail, where it should be indicated: the amount of money paid, time of transfer, lease date, full name, name of the reserved room, that is, the hall.

If the client refuses to rent a few days before the appointed day of shooting, then the advance payment is not returned to him. There is also a system of penalties for canceling studio reservations. During shooting in the halls you need to be in removable shoes, and if there is none, then you should use slippers that are provided by the administrator. If the photographer rents the studio, then he is responsible for the cleanliness of the room, the safety of interior items.

Address

Air Rooms is located in the capital - in Moscow - at the address: st. Bolshaya Cherkizovskaya, 24a, building 6. The studio is located on the fourth floor, in the second entrance hall of the Etalon enterprise.

Prices

The cost of rent is one of the advantages of the capital's photo studio. The minimum booking time for one of the rooms is one hour. If the client rents room No. 1, then for an hour of time on weekdays you need to pay 1100 rubles, on weekends - 1300 rubles. (rental time from two hours). Renting the hall number 2 will cost 1300 rubles, at the weekend - 1400 rubles (from two hours). If the rent is increased by one hour, the surcharge is 200 rubles.

Reviews

Photographers who booked earlier locations in Air Rooms claim that the photo studio is not bad, but it is far from the center. Some point to expensive rent and strict rules, while others, on the contrary, believe that the cost of rent is minimal. Clients include elegant, sophisticated and bright interiors to the advantages of a photo studio. Taking pictures in such rooms is easy, and the photos are light and delicate. The rooms have a music center, so you can work in a relaxed atmosphere.

The disadvantages include a small entrance hall, ugly ceilings, spoiled props (for example, burned out light bulbs of garlands), studio equipment.

In addition, many complain that the photo studio is very difficult to find, as it is located in the courtyards. By agreement, the photographer can bring his props, and if it is heavy, then lift it on a freight elevator. Dressing room rental is a separate fee. All additional services must be paid separately. It is possible to hire a photographer who works there. Clothing for a photo shoot should be brought with you. If the photo shoot is thematic, then there is the opportunity to rent dresses and suits.
