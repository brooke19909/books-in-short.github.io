Lenovo S5000 tablet: specifications, features and reviews

The Lenovo S5000 is a budget computing solution. It is great for performing the simplest tasks. At the same time, the use of an energy-efficient microprocessor provides increased battery life. It is this mobile computer that will be discussed later in this review.

Specialization

As noted earlier, the Lenovo S5000 is a budget tablet. Therefore, it is great for reading books, editing documents, viewing Internet portals, playing videos and listening to audio recordings. Also this list can be supplemented with the most simple toys.

For something more, this mobile computer is not suitable.

Equipment

The Lenovo S5000 delivery list includes the following:

  • the tablet;
  • Charger;
  • user manual, which is supplemented by a warranty card;
  • interface wire.

But the manufacturer did not include a protective film and a cover in the package. There are also no headphones. These are the main disadvantages of this device. But, as previously noted, it belongs to the economy class segment and such a delivery list is typical for this niche.

Tablet Options

The MT8125 microprocessor is at the heart of the Lenovo S5000. Reviews indicate a low level of its performance. But at the same time, the energy efficiency of this chip is at an acceptable level. This CPU includes 4 processing cores that operate at a nominal frequency of 1.3 GHz. In this case, the silicon substrate of this microprocessor is manufactured using a 28 nm process.

The memory subsystem consists of 1 GB of RAM and 16 GB of internal storage. Pre-installed software takes 4 GB. Therefore, the newly made owner of such a tablet can only rely on 12 GB of disk space. But support for memory cards in this case is not implemented.

Finally

The Lenovo S5000 is a typical entry-level tablet. It has a low level of performance, but at the same time increased battery life. Also, the cost of this computing solution is quite affordable. It is perfect for creating a small home multimedia entertainment system. At least there is everything necessary for this in this device. This is how owners use it at home. But in the office such a tablet is extremely rare for the reason that usually in this case, companies tend to use more expensive premium solutions.
