Sol Sharm Hotel 4

Description: On the northern coast of the Red Sea, 20 kilometers from the popular resort of Sharm El Sheikh, Sol Sharm Hotel 4 is located. In about ten minutes, a transit bus will take tourists from the airport directly to the hotel. Located on the second beach line, Sol Sharm is ideal for divers who like diving in the water area near the uninhabited island of Tiran.



The Egyptian-style architectural complex was built in 2003 on the shores of the narrow Gulf of Aqaba, the legends of which make up the outlandish beauty of the underwater world. Sol Sharm Hotel 4 features beautiful, winding pools and green lawns with flowering shrubs.

Rooms: For accommodation, 214 rooms of the standard category are designed for one, two or three people. There are spacious apartments for families and comfortable suites. All rooms have access to the terrace, which serves as an excellent place to relax with views of the pools, garden or sea.

The interiors have comfortable wooden furniture and warm natural shades dominate. Each room has a work desk and an always-stocked minibar. The bathroom is equipped with a shower and a convenient washbasin with a mirror. Always available for shower and hygiene items.



Meals: Sol Sharm Hotel 4 has the popular All Inclusive system, which provides four meals a day, as well as drinks, desserts and snacks throughout the day.

The main restaurant is an abundance of Asian and international dishes. The cozy restaurant in the pool area will delight you with delicious Italian cuisine and seafood specialties. A lobby bar with a classic assortment is open around the clock. Hookah lovers like to visit the colorful Afrikan bar with a traditional Egyptian interior and a selection of national drinks. The pool and beach are always open bars with a wide range of water and juices.

Beach: Only a few hundred meters separates Sol Sharm from the sandy beach. Every 15 minutes a bus approaches the main building, delivering tourists directly to the sea. On the shore there are sun loungers and reed umbrellas.

Additional information: Sol Sharm Hotel 4 promises a perfect vacation, which is provided with a rich infrastructure and a wide selection of entertainment.

The hotel has shops and parking lots, a left-luggage office and a medical center, a safe at the reception and an exchange office. Standard amenities are offered, limousine rental, airport pick-up, taxi call, internet access.



Health club services include spa treatments, massages, exercise, yoga, baths and a jacuzzi.

Vacationers have a large selection of water activities, from swimming in the pool to extreme diving. On the territory of Sol Sharm Hotel there are sports fields, tennis tables, a billiard room, darts targets.

In the daytime, vacationers are waiting for contests and sports, in the evening - discos, music shows and dance evenings. For children, outdoor and educational games are organized, a shallow pool is equipped, cots are provided.

Digest: Many tourists choose to stay in Sharm El Sheikh Sol Sharm Hotel 4. Reviews filled with positive emotions leave no doubt about the high level of service.

Vacationers put a solid four in the hotel, admiring the dazzlingly beautiful grounds, clean spacious rooms and helpful staff.

Tourists of any category can relax at the hotel, including young travelers, for whom there is everything here: slides, swings, a small pool, cribs, children's food.

Restaurants and bars have a huge selection of fruits and vegetables, desserts and pastries, meat and fish dishes, light wines and spirits, juices and carbonated drinks.

And, of course, above all praise - the cleanest sea with amazing vegetation and unique inhabitants.
