Hotel Saphir Hotel 4 *, Turkey, Alanya: photos, reviews

Alanya is a small but atmospheric Mediterranean resort. Remoteness from international airports is a minus, which is more than compensated by the protected nature and excellent service of local hoteliers.





In captivity of the elements

Alanya beaches are highly rated by European experts. Some received the Blue Flag mark. The combination of exceptionally clean water, the sandy coast and affordable prices annually attracts hundreds of thousands of our compatriots.

In the center of the resort there are hotels focused on noisy youth companies. In the vicinity are concentrated complexes designed for family travelers. The abundance of historical attractions makes summer vacation a real adventure that boasts a lot of discoveries and surprises.

Tourists are introduced to the life and traditions of the region. They are lowered into the cool dusk of the famous caves of Alanya. The dungeons are decorated with centuries-old stalactites and stalagmites gleaming in the light. Absolute silence is interrupted by the subtle noise of lake waters.

Transport accessibility

Hotel Saphir is located in an area called Konakli. It is located away from large settlements. The nearest city is twenty kilometers away. The village center is a five minute walk. Antalya's air gateway, whose runways accept transnational flights, is 110 km away.

The population of Konakli is 30,000, but in the high season it increases several times due to tourists who have come to rest. The lion's share of the population is employed in the hospitality industry. The rest work in agricultural enterprises.

Description





Saphir Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) is a complex of detached buildings. The highest building has seven floors. The other buildings are lower, with six and five levels. Rise is carried out by silent ventilated elevators. The hotel took its first guests twenty-seven years ago. In 2011, a global reconstruction of residential and public areas was carried out.

The buildings are made of concrete. Their design is in the same modern style. The hotel has about 600 rooms of various levels. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive program. Since the complex is focused on the reception of families with children, it presents a wide range of entertainment not only for parents, but also for kids.

Reviews about Saphir Hotel 4 * (Turkey) indicate that the hotel has rooms designed for people with disabilities. Reception of guests with pets is not practiced. The property has a garden.
