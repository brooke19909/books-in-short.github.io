Rest in the city of Kazan. Amusement park "Klay"

The city park today acquired the status of a green island, where you can walk and relax. It can be botanical, zoological, forest park, culture and leisure, or others. Amusement parks are an integral part of the metropolis today. There is an island of relaxation and fun in the city of Kazan - the amusement park "Kirlay". Such places combine relaxation and entertainment, and give parents the opportunity to spend time with their children.

About the park

Where can you go for a walk and have fun outdoors in the city of Kazan? Amusement park "Klay" is the best suited for this type of vacation. There is everything for this. The location of the park belongs to the central part of the city - it is very easy to get to it by any means of transport. It is located in the historical part of the city - in front of it on the other side of the reservoir is the Kazan Kremlin.





The picturesque embankment is added by the embankment of the Kazanka River, which adjoins the park. And across the road is the Kazan Family Center, where solemn marriage registrations are held. Here you can safely take a walk with your child or with a group of friends.

Rides

The park is replete with a variety of recreational attractions. These devices for fun appeared a long time ago. At first it was just swings and slides, then carousels and comic fountains became popular. A breakthrough is considered the invention of the Russian scientist Andrei Nartov - "mechanical roller coaster." Abroad, this attraction was called "Roller coaster", we have it called "Roller coaster."

Amusement park "Klay" offers entertainment for both children and adults. The rides in the park are divided into children's, for family vacations and extreme.





Children can ride in a train, which looks like pirate boats, when moving they seem to swing on the waves. The Kangaroo Jumping Tower will amuse the kids. Carousel "Airport" has the appearance of airplanes with sound effects. The Tropicana game complex consists of stairs, slides, turnstiles and ropes, where children can run and frolic. As an entertainment, the park offers children to ride on a swing, circle on a carousel, jump on a trampoline, visit a children's ferris wheel and a circuit.

Fans of extreme sports are invited to ride on the "Flying Saucer" carousel, where rocking and rotation occur simultaneously, the attraction has several extreme modes. This attraction is the only one in all of Russia. The adrenaline rush will help the Tower of Free Fall. Its height is 18 meters. It simulates the effect of free fall. The attraction "Wild Dance" is a double cabins, the rotation of which occurs in three planes.

Children and adults can have fun together on the Tornado attraction, which has the appearance of slides with rail tracks, the cars either go down or go up, going into steep turns. On the circuit "Fast and the Furious" you can ride on electronic cars. Bumper boats are a type of water attraction where boats move through the water in the pool. A chain carousel will ride with a breeze both adults and children. This attraction will help dads and mothers to plunge into childhood memories.





The largest and highest attraction in the park is the Ferris Wheel. From a wheel 55 meters high, a beautiful panorama of the city opens - a view of the Kremlin and the Volga. In the ranking by height, the Kazan wheel ranks third in Russia. It can ride up to 3600 people per hour.

All these types of entertainments are offered by the place of rest and entertainment - Kirlay Park, Kazan. Rides have different prices, the cheapest are for children, the most expensive are extreme.

Park cafeterias

A walk in the fresh air, a lot of emotions, impressions develop an appetite. The city of Kazan is hospitable. Amusement park "Klay" is ready to please its guests with numerous cafeterias and a varied menu. Meat, vegetables, sweets, ice cream are served. Young visitors to the park are offered a children's menu.
