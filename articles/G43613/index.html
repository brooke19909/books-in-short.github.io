The unique city of Setenil de las Bodegas: how to survive among the rocks?

Charming Spain, which has become a center of world tourism, is famous for its unique sights. Every corner of it is fraught with something interesting for guests. The miniature town, famous for its originality, surprises even many travelers who have seen it. And only having been here, you can appreciate its charm. The picturesque Setenil de las Bodegas, dressed in all white, is waiting with open arms for vacationers, whose spirit dies at the sight of unusual and original dwellings.

City of Stone Jungle

Setenil de las Bodegas, located in the sunny province of Cadiz (Andalusia), is held in stone by the arms of powerful rocks. Famous for its unusual geographical position, it attracts enthusiastic tourists from different countries. Despite the fact that you can only get here by car, and excursions in the blessed village, spread in the channel of the most beautiful gorge, which was cut through by the turbulent river Trejo, are very rare, the flow of curious vacationers does not dry out. The attraction, which you should definitely get acquainted with, surprises with a special pacifying atmosphere.





Cliff dwellings

Built in the cliffs of Setenil de las Bodegas, having achieved prosperity through the tourism business, extends only 82 km 2 . A small settlement, the number of inhabitants of which does not exceed 3 thousand, is very ancient. Peaceful landowners once held back the attacks of militant crusaders trying to take over the town. It was they who planted the first vineyards. And now a wonderful corner is famous for delicious sparkling drinks.

Initially, the first dwellings appeared in caves. However, after the XII century, when a small settlement began to grow, the first huts, a cathedral, and even a magnificent castle, which collapsed with time, were built on the slopes of the mountains. The inhabitants of the medieval town did not hide from the enemies on the top of the hill, but sought refuge among the crevices of the rocks. As the settlement began to be actively populated, houses with a snow-white facade retreated further from the rocky landscape.

Fairytale place protected by stone blocks

Tourists exploring the quiet Setenil de las Bodegas are fearful. It seems to them that the overhanging stone arch, crushing the streets and houses located on them, is about to collapse on the ground. No one sees the blue sky or the twinkling stars. Residents are used to the fact that instead of the sun they are observing the frightening power of piled rocks. However, over 8 centuries, not a single accident was recorded in the pearl of Andalusia.





Natural basalt formations that appeared millions of years ago are not the result of a strong earthquake. Mother Nature herself made sure that the residents of Setenil de las Bodegas did not have problems with the choice of material for the roof. She did everything possible, and the inhabitants of a fabulous place are reliably protected from heavy rain, the rays of the scorching sun and strong wind. On the streets, more like underground tunnels, the weather is comfortable all year round, and basalt blocks replace walls and ceilings.

Paradise

The compact village, which is the most amazing corner of Andalusia, is so small that it has only a few narrow streets. On one, the name of which translates as "Solar Caves", it is light in the first half of the day, and on the other, crushed by a huge block, twilight reigns.





The famous Setenil de las Bodegas is a true gourmet paradise. Having won the title of the best gastronomic center of Andalusia, he will delight those who love delicious and high-calorie food. Fragrant pork sausages, numerous meat dishes, pies with various fillings, air cakes delight tourists. In addition, you can taste sparkling wines made according to old recipes, which are kept in the strictest confidence.

Tourists reviews

As recognized by tourists who have become acquainted with such an unusual attraction, at first it seems that this is the scenery for the Hollywood blockbuster. And many people think that the majestic cliffs hanging over residential buildings do not look real. However, vacationers who set foot on the land of the city of Setenil de las Bodegas in Spain, discover that the stunning landscapes are quite real.

A natural masterpiece ennobled by human hands is a fabulous place where residents engaged in daily affairs do not even notice the unusualness of their homes. The serene atmosphere attracts admired tourists here, promising to return to one of the most vibrant villages of the Spanish south.





Unlike any other city, the colorful Setenil de las Bodegas in Spain is ideal for those who are tired of the noisy life in gazed megalopolises. Ringing silence, overflowing peace and stunning views have a beneficial effect on tired travelers who dream of recharging with positive emotions.
