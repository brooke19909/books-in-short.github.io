Hotel Family Hotel Breza 3 * (Sunny Beach): overview, description, reviews

Bulgaria is the undoubted leader in the tourism market of the Black Sea region. Bulgarian resorts are especially popular among Russian tourists, and in the summer they even become centers of mass tourist pilgrimage.

In recent years, Bulgaria is not very active, but enters the European tourism market. Now, a Schengen visa is required to travel to this state, the hotel sector is being actively improved, many hotels operate on the All Inclusive system. But the sympathy of our compatriots for the Bulgarian resorts, which has reliable, yet pre-perestroika roots, does not fade.

A small barrier for Russian travelers who want to relax in Bulgaria can be a Bulgarian visa. The process of applying for it is not much different from applying for a Schengen visa to any other European country, but its cost is one of the highest in Europe - 55 euros.

General Hotel Information

Small family budget hotel Family Hotel Breza 3 * is located in the northern part of the large resort Sunny Beach in the Dolphin area, 100 meters from the sandy beach.

It is 30 km from Burgas International Airport.

Within a kilometer from the hotel is one of the largest water parks
Bulgaria.

The historical center, the city of Nessebar, is 8 km from the hotel.

The hotel has a small outdoor pool, fitness center, restaurant, bar, beautiful garden, free secure parking. Sun beds and umbrellas by the pool are free.





Hotel Family Hotel Breza 3 * (Bulgaria) does not accept tourists with pets.

The hotel accepts credit cards.

Rooms

Total in Family Hotel Breza 3 * one building, 20 rooms. Of these, 3 single, 15 double, 1 triple, and one quadruple Studio. The building has an elevator.

Regardless of the room category, each has: shower, toilet, telephone, TV, refrigerator, air conditioning, comfortable new furniture.

Internet in the room at an additional cost.



Rooms are cleaned daily. Towels are changed twice a week, bed linen once a week. Replenishment of bath accessories during each cleaning.

A safe deposit box and luggage storage are available at the reception. Luggage storage is free, safe rental costs 10 euros per week.

24 hour room service at an additional cost.

Nutrition

Family Hotel Breza 3 * (Sunny Beach) offers guests such food concepts: VO, VV, NV.

By default, the room is booked without meals. On request, for tourists who stay in the hotel for more than 10 days, and for tourist groups, breakfast and lunch can be ordered.

The hotel's √† la carte restaurant serves European and Bulgarian national dishes. The bar serves local wines, soft drinks, cocktails, tea and coffee.





The convenient location of the hotel eliminates all sorts of problems with food. The hotel guests have many options where to eat: cafes, restaurants, bars on the beach and in the city. All necessary infrastructure is within 100 m.

Beach

Family Hotel Breza 3 * is located 100 meters from the municipal public beach. Rental of sunbeds, umbrellas on the beach is paid by tourists additionally.



The beach is well equipped. There are rescue centers, shops, cafes, showers, toilets. The entrance to the sea is shallow and gentle. Suitable for bathing children.
