How tourists are fed at the hotel

Hotel Medplaya Hotel Santa Monica 3 * operates on several food systems. Here you can take rooms only with breakfast. Two meals a day are popular, where you can choose a second meal - lunch or dinner. Should beach lovers stay here?

To the sea from the hotel Medplaya Hotel Santa Monica 3 * (Spain) walk just two minutes. Across the road is a municipal sandy beach, equipped, clean, large, with paid sunbeds and umbrellas.
