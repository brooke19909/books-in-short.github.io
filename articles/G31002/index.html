Hotel Glarus Golden Sands 4 *: overview, features and reviews

Golden Sands is a popular resort on the Black Sea, which is visited annually by many tourists. On its territory built a considerable number of various hotels for every taste. In our article we want to talk about one of the four-star resort complexes - Glarus Golden Sands 4 *.

A bit about the hotel ...

Glarus Golden Sands 4 * is a beautiful hotel complex with panoramic views of the famous Golden Sands National Park. The hotel is slightly removed from the central part of the resort and is located in a quiet location on the sea coast. It was built in 2006, and in 2009 - completely renovated. The hotel consists of one 11-story building and another 4-story building.

It is located at: Bulgaria, 8240 Sunny Beach.

A little bit about the resort ...

The famous Golden Sands resort is located near Varna. It got a beautiful name thanks to the park, on the territory of which there are hotel complexes, beaches and entertainment clubs. The park occupies a very vast territory, which is a nature reserve. Here you can see forests, beautiful beaches and, of course, the sea with clear water.



Fifty hotels have been built in this region. All are located near the coast. It is worth noting that the resort has long gained popularity as the best place for families with young children. Each complex has children's clubs in which experienced animators work. Well, on the beaches for young tourists there are a variety of attractions.

The coast of the resort offers all kinds of entertainment for guests. Here you can go diving, hang gliding or ride a water bike. The beaches stretched along the entire resort, reaching in some places up to 100 meters wide. Fine-grained sand cover and shallow sea with a clean bottom are the pride of Golden Sands.

Local Attractions

Golden Sands is a fairly young resort, which has not yet managed to grow significant sights. In the list of interesting places there are several objects worthy of the attention of tourists.

One of them is Aladzha Monastery. It is located 15 kilometers from Varna, near the resort of Golden Sands. The monastery was once carved by hermit Christians in a sheer scale around the 4th century. The monastery lasted peacefully until the 14th century until it was plundered by the Ottomans. The monastery is one of the places visited by tourists in Bulgaria. The medieval structure is perfectly preserved to this day. It consists of two tiers. Here you can see the temple itself and the living quarters of the monks. Catacombs are located nearby, in which Christians hid from the Ottomans. Now the monastery is a museum and receives visitors all year round.



The main property of the resort can be considered the beautiful forests of the national park in whose territory it is located. Local authorities decided not to block these places from tourists, but, on the contrary, paved walking routes of different difficulty levels, constructing paths with places for halt, drinking fountains and observation platforms. There are trails for cyclists, children, senior citizens, you can even hire a guide who will help you overcome the route, and at the same time will tell a lot of interesting things about the park.
