Guest house "Salang", Lazarevskoye: description, service, reviews

Contrary to prevailing stereotypes, I want to say that on the Black Sea coast of Russia you can have a good rest - the main thing is to decide on a resort and a hotel. In recent years, the infrastructure of Russian sea resorts has undergone a number of significant changes: new hotels have been rebuilt, many new entertainments have appeared, all the beaches are now equipped with lifeguards, locker rooms and even showers, which makes them as comfortable as possible. Russian resorts are increasingly choosing for themselves families with small children, not ready for long and tiring flights. Rest on the territory of Russia allows you to independently decide on how you will get to the resort, where to live, how to organize your leisure. Accordingly, there is an opportunity to save.

Lazarevskoye is an excellent resort on the Black Sea, where neither children nor adults will be bored. We suggest you take a closer look at it if you plan your trip in the summer. There are enough chic hotels, there are more affordable options, tourists with limited means can settle in the private sector. Guesthouse "Salang" (Lazarevskoye) managed to gain a huge number of negative and sometimes terrifying reviews. What living conditions can he offer? Why did modern tourists so dislike him?





About the guest house

So, the guest house "Salang" (Lazarevskoye) is located in the resort village near the main attractions - water park and dolphinarium. In walking distance there is a good beach, the central for the resort - "Cote d'Azur". This option attracts potential tourists and vacationers with a good location and, most importantly, affordable accommodation prices. The hotel is really well located: shops, market, attractions, entertainment and, most importantly, the beach, are literally within walking distance.





Accommodation

Hotel "Salang" (Guesthouse 1 *, Lazarevskoye) is small, only 36 rooms. Tourists are invited to choose rooms of various categories:

  • "Standard" - rooms are designed for two and three people.
  • Superior - Room for four people.
  • "Suite" - two-room suite with a view of the coast.

Settlement is fast enough, but vacationers advise potential tourists to the Salang Hotel (Sochi, Lazarevskoye) to be extremely attentive. Quite often, the staff settles tourists in the first available room, which may not correspond to the description and what the tourist is counting on. Of course, hardly anyone wants to spoil their vacation on the first day, but in any case, the staff must place guests in the hotel that they have booked and pre-paid. That is why you should be extremely persistent and ask to settle you in the number that you managed to pay.

Nevertheless, every tourist wants to get a small but clean comfortable room in a quiet area, equipped with a private bathroom with hot water and all necessary bath accessories. Hotel infrastructure

The hotel has a small shop where you can purchase a minimal set of products. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are held in a small dining room, and check-in at the reception at the entrance to the Salang Guest House 1 * (Lazarevskoye).

All vacationers can spend time in the sauna, take a steam bath in excellent company. For entertainment, the hotel can offer only table tennis and rental of sports equipment. This is actually not necessary, because the beach has enough entertainment for every taste.

Nutrition

For an additional fee, you can purchase three meals a day at the hotel. If you trust the reviews of real vacationers, the condition of the products leaves much to be desired. There is a complete feeling that tourists are fed stale food, and they cook absolutely not tasty. Here you will never see fresh bread, pastries, aromatic coffee and especially fruit. Definitely not worth taking meals, it is better to eat in local cafes and restaurants - there are plenty of them here.





Service

The reception is open around the clock, all vacationers can contact the staff on any matter of interest to them. "Salang" is a hotel in Lazarevsky that offers good service. Shuttle services to the airport or train station can be arranged for an extra charge. At the reception you can get acquainted with the resort's rich excursion program, choose something optimal for yourself. So, for example, almost all tourists do not deny themselves pleasure and go for jipping - entertainment full of drive, risk and unforgettable emotions.

Sea and beach

There is a beach within a 10-minute walk of the hotel, but there are no complaints about it: cleanliness, developed infrastructure, gentle entry into the water. Of course, a lot of vacationers gather here in the season, of course, but you can always find a more secluded place if you walk along the coast.

To summarize

Hotel "Salang" 1 * (Russia, Lazarevskoye) is definitely not suitable for those tourists and vacationers who count on excellent service, room service, as well as comfort. The hotel is hardly suitable for an overnight stay, and then with varying degrees of success. Do not overpay for meals, it is better to have breakfast, lunch and dinner in local cafes and restaurants. You should not choose this hotel for families with small children: unsanitary conditions, dirt and not the best living conditions are definitely not suitable for you.
