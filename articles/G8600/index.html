Hotel Clumba Mar 4 (Mallorca / Can Picafort): description, photo

Mallorca is a major resort of the Balearic archipelago. Favorable conditions, picturesque nature and clean ecology have contributed to the huge popularity of the island among rich people and ordinary tourists. World celebrities such as Schumacher, Zeta Jones and Douglas have repeatedly visited here, who subsequently acquired property in this sunny region.

It is no coincidence that tourism in Mallorca is so in demand. The island has long since gained the fame of the most hospitable, democratic and romantic resort. Here the clear sea, gentle beaches and quality service. In addition, this territory is under the protection of municipal authorities - more than 40 protected parks are concentrated here.

Mallorca is attractive for older couples, travelers with children, inveterate party-goers, shopping enthusiasts and lovers of versatile excursion leisure. The resort has bribed millions of famous politicians and businessmen, as well as people who value natural beauty. Thick protected forests located on rocky archipelagos, picturesque bays stretching along a wide snow-white coast with numerous entertainment venues - all this is an incomparable Mallorca that can give literally every tourist the happiness and vivid impressions.





If you are going on a long-awaited trip to this paradise island, check out all the tours in advance. The main thing is to remember: each resort area is endowed with its own characteristics and traditions. For a relaxing and serene vacation, the place of Can Picafort on the coast of the beautiful bay of Alcudia is ideal. The sea is shallow and very warm, which is especially important for kids. This resort has built many hotels operating on the All Inclusive system . Among the variety of hotels we offer to choose a modern and inexpensive complex Clumba Mar 4.

general information

The hotel is well located in the heart of the area, near an abundance of business, entertainment and cultural attractions. About an hour away is the airport, from which it is easy to get to the residential complex. Pension Clumba Mar 4 (Mallorca) was built back in 1974 and during that time earned an excellent reputation and high rating.





For an affordable price, the management offers quality service and three meals a day. On the territory of the house there are many recreation areas and sports and entertainment facilities, including for children. Active tourists will not get bored here either - different centers and rental points are concentrated near the complex. A diving school and tour desk are available.

Housing stock

For guests, Clumba Mar 4 offers 235 fully-equipped and well-equipped rooms in a 6-story building. There are spacious rooms for people with disabilities. All rooms have split systems, safes and loggias with sea views.





In the individual use of each tourist bathrooms with a standard set of bath and toilet facilities. TVs are connected to satellite broadcasting. There is a landline telephone with direct dialing, a small bar with paid drinks and the Internet. Ironing facilities and a second cot are available upon request.

Daily cleaning and towel changes are provided. Bed linen is changed twice a week.

Supply system





If desired, guests of the Clumba Mar 4 hotel (Mallorca / Spain) can choose several food options: half board (breakfast, dinner) and all inclusive. Table setting is carried out in the restaurant as a buffet. Throughout the day, guests are free to eat local drinks and snacks. In addition, ice cream, juices and foreign alcohol products are paid. There are two bars in the hotel: one in the lobby, the other near the pool.

Coastline

A sandy and comfortable beach stretches 200 meters from the Clumba Mar 4 complex (Mallorca / Can Picafort). It belongs to the state, respectively, all the amenities on the coast are paid from your own pocket. There are cafeterias, bars, and catamaran and ATV rental centers on the shore.

Entertainment and services on site





Internet is available in all public areas (for a fee). For those who combine business with leisure, the administration offers technically equipped conference rooms, copy and postal services, as well as fax. Clumba Mar 4 boasts a library with a reading room and a TV lounge.

Twice a day - day and evening - tourists are entertained by professional animators, cheerful competitions are organized on the territory, and dances begin in the late afternoon. Several pools (indoor and outdoor) and massage rooms are free of charge.

Hotel Clumba Mar 4 has table tennis, billiards and slot machines. Car rental services, excursions and transfers are provided. A gym with new equipment was built on site. The building has a currency exchange desk and a medical office.

Children's leisure





A well-developed cultural and entertainment base for children of different ages. Separately built safe pool. Every day fun programs are held with jokes and contests conducted by a group of experienced animators. For kids there is a development club with different circles. Another center is open for older children. In the evening, a dance floor opens for children.

On this list of services is not limited. We can say with confidence that the Clumba Mar 4 hotel (Majorca) is fully consistent with the declared category . The hotel receives only positive reviews and ratings. Many noted the friendly atmosphere, cleanliness of rooms and varied food. I especially liked the quality service and relaxed atmosphere. There is no noise, endlessly loud music and nightclubs - the administration carefully monitors this.
