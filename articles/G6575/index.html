Nokia 222: specifications, reviews, settings. Mobile Phone Nokia 222 Dual Sim

Not so long ago, phones sold under the Nokia brand were in high demand. They are deservedly called the best, as they were distinguished by a high level of quality and battery life. But time does not stand still. Every year, many new models that are equipped with innovative technologies appear on the market. The Finnish company, striving to follow the fashion and saving on production costs, gradually slowed down, giving way to other brands such as Apple, Samsung and others. It is worth noting that even smartphones from Chinese manufacturers have become more attractive to buyers.

Finding a phone sold under the Nokia brand is currently quite difficult, as the company is now led by Microsoft. But in 2015, the new Nokia 222 model goes on sale. Specifications, reviews and a description can be read below. The phone is a typical button device. What technologies did the developers use? Is there anything interesting about him? Does it meet modern requirements? These questions are often asked by buyers who pick up a new mobile phone. Answers to them are contained in the article.



Model release

By the time this model was released, Microsoft had already acquired Nokia assets. However, she did not completely abandon the old brand name, using it for classic devices. As a rule, during their development, preference was given to proven technologies.

The Nokia 222 phone was produced in two versions. The first is classic. It is a typical all-in-one with a button keyboard. The model is designed to work with one sim-card. The second option looks no different from the first. The form factor corresponds to the classic design. The only thing is that it has two slots for sim cards. The manufacturer called this model Nokia 222 Dual Sim.

The devices received an updated operating system, which allows you to work with a wide range of different programs. The color screen and the convenient keyboard unit are undoubtedly an advantage. The target audience is children and the elderly. Also, this model is purchased as a second phone, since it is simple and high-quality.

Assembly is carried out in Vietnam. Such news will please those who have a negative attitude to products from the Middle Kingdom.



Design

Externally, the phone looks pretty pretty. The stores are presented in two classic colors - white and black. Light - the weight is only 80 g, the dimensions are optimally selected (116 √ó 50 √ó 13 mm). According to the instructions, such dimensions correspond to both a simple model with an index of 222, and a dual-SIM - Nokia 222 Dual Sim. Due to this, the phone lies perfectly in the hand, does not slip. In the lineup there are options with a matte and glossy body. The latter are marked with the prefix Rome.

The front panel is visually divided into two halves. At the top there is a screen, a speaker. The bottom is allotted for the keyboard unit. Under the "0" key is a hole for the microphone.

The back panel is simple. There are no unnecessary details. In the middle of the cover, the brand name is printed horizontally. At the top is the lens, and below it is the output speaker.



Keyboard

The button unit deserves special attention. It has an almost square shape. All buttons except the central one (joystick) are oval, the same size. This gives a special style. The numbers are large, clearly visible even to people with low vision. Soft keys and call accept / reset buttons are located in pairs under each other. How to set up quick access to applications in Nokia 222? The instruction will give answers to these questions. According to the information contained in it, for the soft keys and arrows of the joystick, individual settings are provided. Each user can, depending on personal preferences, change the purpose of these buttons.

As for the digital block, the owners have no comments on it. Button layout isolated from each other. Due to this, a clear relief appears, which allows you to clearly distinguish between the rows. Therefore, the phone is suitable for those who are accustomed to dial, as they say, blindly.

Battery

What points should I look for when choosing a phone like the Nokia 222? Specifications, reviews and, of course, battery life! Previously, the devices of this brand wore the title of centenarians, and this was well deserved. But can the new model boast high rates?

The phone is equipped with a BL-5C lithium-ion battery. Its capacity is 1100 mAh. For a push-button device with a weak screen, this is quite enough. This model can be actively used for 20 hours, and not only for making calls, but also for listening to music, watching videos, passing levels in your favorite game. The manufacturer claims that in standby mode a simple model will work for about one month, and the one that provides support for two SIM cards - within 20 days.

The owners claim that they have to charge the phone no more than once every three days. According to these data, the device is a worthy follower of the legendary Nokia models.
