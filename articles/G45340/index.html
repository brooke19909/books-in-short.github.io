Pension "Lazurny" (Kabardinka) - relaxation with health benefits

On the very shore of the Black Sea, at the foot of the Doob Mountain, not far from the Tsemess Bay , the Lazurny boarding house (Kabardinka) is located. There are excellent conditions for recreation and recreation with the whole family.

Description

The sanatorium, built in 1999, is located nine kilometers from Gelendzhik and 23 km from Novorossiysk.

The territory of the health resort is a park area where Pitsunda pine and juniper grow under natural conditions. Just twelve kilometers from the complex is located the Golden Bay Water Park, 14 km is the Captain Vrungel entertainment zone, and 15 is a dolphinarium. Pension "Lazurny" (Kabardinka) is open for tourists from May to September.

Recovery. Treatment

Kabardinka is considered the driest place among all the resort areas of the Black Sea coast, so the climate of this region has an exceptionally healing effect on vacationers. The treatment uses healing hydrogen sulfide mud and mineral water. In Kabardinka, you can get a number of wellness services for people with pathology of the cardiovascular system, diseases of the respiratory tract, peripheral and central systems, as well as movement organs. Pines, mountains of the Caucasus, mineral waters, the Black Sea - all these natural healing factors have a tremendous impact on the health of every tourist who came to this region. Pension "Lazurny" (Kabardinka), upon a preliminary request of vacationers, is ready to offer coursework for specialized treatment in medical institutions of Gelendzhik.



Rooms

The guesthouse offers 100 comfortable bungalows for vacationers. Among them there are buildings with double superior rooms (14 sq. M.) Equipped with all amenities, a refrigerator, a bathroom, a TV, a loggia. There is also a standard 2 or 3 bed apartment (12 sq. M.). They are equipped with a bathroom, TV and fridge. For guests of double rooms without amenities, a TV and a bathroom are in common areas. The room has only a refrigerator. If you have chosen the Lazurny boarding house (Kabardinka) for your vacation, the prices of 2013 can be found by contacting the administration of the institution directly.

Nutrition

Pension "Lazurny" (Kabardinka) provides meals in an integrated system. The cafe offers refreshing drinks, a variety of dishes, snacks, fruits and ice cream.

Leisure

If this year your place of relaxation was Kabardinka, the Lazurny boarding house, the prices of which are relatively low, is ready to offer a lot of entertainment. For extreme sports there are water skis, paragliders and motorbikes. You can also go diving and explore the depths of the sea. Fishermen are given the opportunity to go fishing in the open sea for a boat. Bearing, flounder, burbot, etc. are found in these parts. Gelendzhik has horse clubs where you can ride a horse or even go hiking in places of interest. The rest will be memorable and very interesting.
