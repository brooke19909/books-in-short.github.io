Goas Pearl 2 * (India, North Goa, Candolim): photos and reviews of tourists

Goa is the most famous resort in India. And as it often happens, there is a lot of something unlike other Indian states. Chic beaches, pleasant climate, calm and warm sea, picturesque nature, interesting cultural phenomena and culinary delights - these are just some of the reasons for the popularization of tourism in Goa.

In terms of tourism, the state is divided into two areas:

1. South Goa . There are flat beaches, white or golden sand, warm sea, beautiful landscapes. It is not as densely populated as other states of India. And more like a "resort in the square." In fact, this is an almost isolated piece of the Earth with a heap of expensive hotels, where only the wealthiest Indians, Americans and Europeans, lovers of a respectable, relaxing holiday, relax and have fun. It‚Äôs such a unisex resort, where at the exit from a five-star hotel it‚Äôs not immediately possible to understand which country this hotel is located in.

2. North Goa. Yields to the southern brother in beauty and natural wealth. The beaches here are not so beautiful: they mainly consist of sand of volcanic origin, it is a strange dirty gray color, it does not at all look like a golden beach "standard". The entrance to the sea is often quite steep, the terrain is hilly, and cities and towns are often crowded with Hindus and original tourists who crave for existential impressed, young and not-so-young hippies and other fun fans of a free lifestyle. Needless to say, that such tourists can appreciate all the delights of a holiday in the north of Goa. Most people are immediately struck not by a special atmosphere, unforgettable energy, unlimited possibilities for finding themselves, but by poverty on the verge of poverty, unsanitary conditions and baseness of morals.





How to choose a hotel in Goa?

With the choice of hotels there are no problems. In the area there are a lot of establishments of different price categories: from budget hostels and guesthouses to high-ranking luxury complexes with VIP services. Those who believe in the prejudices associated with holidays in India should pay attention to hotels in the south of Goa, preferably chain hotels. The concentration of inexpensive hotels and guesthouses in North Goa seems to be created for unpretentious and fun budget tourists. When choosing a place to stay in the north of the state, you must first pay attention to the city. Some villages in this region are suitable only for very specific categories of tourists. The main administrative center of Mapusa - the most "Indian" of the cities of Goa - is very densely populated and not picky about cleanliness. Anjuna and Calangute are real party Mecca. Candolim and Sinquerim are a bit like resorts in the southern Goa region. They are popular among foreigners, comfortable and not crowded, but, unlike the southern resorts, they are ready to accept non-pocket tourists.

Hotel location

According to the guests, the location of the Goas Pearl Hotel 2 * hotel is extremely successful. It is a two-minute walk from the Nerul River, a 10-minute walk from Candolim and Coco beaches . Near the hotel there is a bus parking. One way to the beach costs 5 rupees.





Panjim is just a 20-minute drive away. The city is very interesting, but not occupied by tourists. Lovers of quiet but attractive sights, colorful settlements should go to it. The most visited tourist sites are the Menezes Braganza Institute, the Church of the Immaculate Conception, the secretariat and the Church of St. Lawrence.

Goas Pearl 2 *: tourist reviews

Hotel reviews are an important source of information about hotels and holiday destinations for unprepared tourists. Adequate comments by experienced travelers and tourism industry workers are especially valuable.

The reviews about this hotel are very mixed. Some tourists praise the hotel, while others criticize tirelessly. Satisfied people note the low price of accommodation, good breakfasts, a very convenient location, free internet in the lobby, service to the beach. Most travel agents advise making an adjustment to the fact that, among other budget hotels in the north of Goa, the Goas Pearl 2 * guesthouse clearly wins.

The authors of negative reviews note that the pool is dirty, the rooms are very superficial, the towels are old, and the service is practically absent. Also, stones of discontented tourists fly towards food: they write that the food is tasteless and not always fresh.

Be that as it may, Goas Pearl 2 * is a very popular hotel, in the high season it is almost always filled with tourists, because it offers optimal conditions at such a low price.
