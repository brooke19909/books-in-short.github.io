Daphne Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos): description, services, rooms and reviews

Recently, many people who want to go somewhere on vacation, choose Greece as their destination, which is not surprising. After all, staying in Greece, you can enjoy not only the sea, but also historical and cultural attractions. Just before you go on vacation, you need to choose the exact direction and, of course, the hotel. It is desirable - more budget. Well, a good choice will be Daphne Hotel Apartments 3 *.





general information

The hotel was built in 1977, but was last renovated in 2007. It is located in the city of Paphos, which is located in the southwestern part of Cyprus. And quite close to the sea - you can walk to it in 10 minutes.

The airport is also nearby - eight kilometers from the hotel. But most importantly - in the immediate vicinity there are various interesting sights. In a minute you can walk to the church of Panahia Chrysopolitissa, for example. If you walk further, you will see the harbor of Paphos, the column of St. Paul, the castle of Saranta Colones and the church of Agia Solomon. And literally 800 meters from the hotel is the famous Kings Avenue Mall in Cyprus. In general, you can get anywhere from the complex. In the immediate vicinity there is a bus stop, and minibuses in various directions depart from there regularly.

Service

In Daphne Hotel Apartments 3 * well-developed infrastructure. There is everything that a tourist may need on vacation, starting from free parking located next to the building and ending with wireless Wi-Fi, which catches throughout the complex.

If guests want, then a shuttle service can be arranged for them. Do you want to have your own personal transport, do not depend on anyone? Then guests will be offered car rental. Or they can rent a bicycle - this is a fairly popular form of transport in Cyprus.

The lobby reception is open 24/7. There is also an ATM, an excursion bureau (where else they sell tickets, helping with their registration), a luggage storage room, and a currency exchange office with a favorable rate. Laundry with dry cleaning, where guests ironed clothes, the hotel also has.

If you have any questions, then you can contact the administrator. Fortunately, Daphne Hotel Apartments 3 * has multilingual staff who speaks not only Greek and English, but also Romanian and Russian.





What else is there in the hotel?

Daphne Hotel Apartments 4 * is not a resort hotel. There is little entertainment, which positively affects the cost of living. Nevertheless, while in a hotel, you can brighten up your leisure time. For example, swim in the pool, or sunbathe. If guests come with children, then those while their parents are resting can play in a separate area. By the way, the hotel also has a beauty salon and a hairdresser.

But, of course, the most important thing is the restaurant. Daphne Hotel Apartments 4 * offers its guests three food options - breakfast, half board and all inclusive. As the guests who were here assure, no one will remain hungry. The choice is not as wide as in fashionable hotels, but everything is fresh, satisfying and tasty. Everyone can find something to their taste - they serve salads, fruits, meat, fish, seafood, poultry and side dishes. But drinks are not included in the price of dinner. Important information

At Daphne Hotel Apartments (Paphos), check-in starts at 2:00 p.m., as in all Greek hotels. And departure - in the morning and lasts until noon.

The hotel is allowed to stay with children. If the guest arrived with a baby whose age does not exceed two years, then for him a cradle will be installed in the apartment. For all children older than 2, but under 12, you need to pay extra 5 euros per day. Extra beds can be installed for them, but their maximum number is two. For each adult you have to pay extra 10 euros per day.

If the guests know in advance that they will need an extra bed, you need to inform the property in advance. The administration will send a confirmation of the provision of this service.

Upon check-in, you will need to present an identity card, as well as your own credit card, the details of which were indicated when applying for a reservation. Allowed to use "Visa", "American Express", "Master Card" and "Maestro".

But inside there is everything - starting with sets for making tea and coffee, ending with the oven. The rooms here are conscientiously cleaned daily, changing towels and linen. Guests are advised to take only breakfast - they are enough to get enough, and for lunch and dinner you can look into one of the restaurants or cafes, which are numerous in Paphos.

In general, to summarize, the hotel will be an ideal option for people who dream of relaxing by the sea for modest money.
