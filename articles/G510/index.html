Explay Scream 3G: Feature Overview and User Reviews

Medium-cost tablets, like smartphones of the same price category, have recently hit all records for popularity. And this is absolutely understandable, because at an affordable cost you can get a device with excellent technical characteristics. The undisputed leader in this niche is Explay. Recently, this manufacturer introduced a device that embodied all the secret desires of tablet lovers. The gadget is called quite unusual - Scream 3G. As the name implies, it will make owners scream with joy. But let's look at what a device like Explay Scream 3G is, and is this gadget really the way the manufacturer introduced it.





Appearance and Ergonomics

At the first glance at the tablet, a feeling of fullness and maturity of this new product appears. The form factor is presented without unnecessary lines and the wrong proportions. In appearance, the Explay Scream 3G tablet is no different from other average ones. This is the most common rectangle with fairly strongly rounded corners. Despite the fact that it has many similarities with other gadgets of this kind, here you can find features, for example:

  • lack of any inserts and overlays;
  • color uniformity of the case and parts;
  • convenient arrangement of connectors and a white signature of each on the rear panel;
  • slight bevel of the edge of the cover around the perimeter.

As for ergonomics, Explay Scream 3G user reviews are good. According to them, there is nothing superfluous, and using the tablet is very convenient. Holding it in your hands is not a problem, while the side function buttons are not pressed. Naturally, with a 10 ‚Äùdisplay it is impossible to control the tablet with one hand. But thanks to the wide frame around the screen, it is easy to hold in any orientation. The second hand may be occupied with control.





Display

The display is one of the main "chips" that the Explay Scream 3G tablet has. This is a large ten-inch IPS matrix with a resolution of 1280x800 pixels. This indicator is not enough to talk about the clearest image, but the points, surprisingly, are weakly visible, and they can be seen only upon close examination. Multi-touch supports up to 5 units at a time. The sensor works smoothly, has not yet received complaints from users. Also very pleased with the Explay Scream 3G is a special grease repellent layer. Thanks to him, much less bold prints appear on the screen. The back panel, by the way, has exactly the same coating.





Performance, software and advanced features

Has an Explay Scream 3G tablet processor with 4 cores. But the manufacturer wanted to stop two of them. Why such a decision was made is unknown. But still, performance issues by users have not been noticed. Everything here works smartly and without hangs.

The tablet is equipped with 1 GB RAM (standard for the middle class). This is enough to store several "heavy" applications in the tray. Only 8 GB of internal memory is available, but thanks to the possibility of expanding to 32 GB using a microSD card, most of the problems with data storage are eliminated.

Explay Scream 3G works under Android OS version 4.2.2. The shell is standard and has not undergone major changes. The main feature of this gadget is the ability to work in a 3G wireless network, which is rarely seen among competitors. But, in addition to this, the tablet supports all other standard Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules.

Also, users were very pleased with the presence of two cameras: a front camera of 1 MP and a rear camera of 1.9 MP. The received frames are fully consistent with the declared parameters.
