The best hotels in Nefteyugansk

Nefteyugansk is a city of district significance in the Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug. This is the only city in the Middle Ob region, which is located on the island territory - it is washed by the Ob and the Yugansk Ob. This northern city is characterized by a developed industry (in particular, oil production). If you plan to visit Nefteyugansk, the hotels of the city will provide you with a warm welcome.
