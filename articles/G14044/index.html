How guests are accommodated

In all the buildings of Club Hotel Anjeliq there are more than three hundred and fifty standard rooms and forty-nine family rooms (two bedrooms connected by a door). Each of them has wide beds, a shower, toiletries. In any room, you can install an additional folding bed. There is a refrigerator where water is placed on the day of arrival. Satellite TV with several Russian channels. The safe in the room is paid. The hotel also provides special one-room suites for honeymooners. They have a jacuzzi on the terrace. The rooms are air-conditioned, with split system of individual control. The bathroom has a hairdryer. All rooms have balconies. Cleaned daily, and the towel and bed are changed twice a week.
