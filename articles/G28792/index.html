Private sector of Anapa: reviews, conditions, addresses. Where to stay in Anapa?

Every year, thousands of tourists from different parts of Russia and neighboring countries prefer to spend the summer in Anapa. In the private sector of this resort city, you can always rent housing, and absolutely on different conditions and with a varying degree of comfort. Many places to stay are aimed at families with children. Some of them offer a quiet and relaxing holiday, away from city noise and civilization, and some are located within walking distance from the main entertainment places of the city.





Why should I go to rest in Anapa?

This resort attracts tourists primarily for its beautiful and long beaches that stretch along the clean and warm Black Sea. The beaches here are mostly covered with pebbles and fine soft sand. Almost every coastal area is very well equipped for a comfortable holiday for tourists. There is also a huge amount of entertainment.

Also in this city, the infrastructure is well developed: there are a huge number of restaurants, clubs, bars, cafes, as well as other venues for a fun pastime. In addition, tourists who come here on vacation will always find a place where you can stay overnight or for a certain time. In the private sector of Anapa, daily rental housing is rented in almost every house - the income from tourism among local residents is very large and everyone wants to take the opportunity to increase it. Plus, prices in the private sector of Anapa will pleasantly surprise any traveler - they are low, and the living conditions are sometimes really chic.

In addition to all of the above, the climate here is simply excellent: the weather is hot, but the air is softened due to the proximity to the sea. There is also a large number of sunny days that will certainly delight tourists.

So where can you stay in Anapa? What hotels, inns, guest houses and apartments in the private sector are offered in this city? About it below.

Houses by the sea

Houses by the sea in Anapa's private sector are very popular among tourists. Guests of the city note that it is possible to stay in them for all those who do not want to get to the sea for a long time, as well as for families who relax with small children, because, as you know, it is not easy to overcome long distances with them.

So, the maximum number of positive reviews are received at their address by the sea in the private sector of Anapa, such as Sibiryak (along Severnaya Street), Zelenaya 21b and Novorossiyskaya 50 (the last two houses got their name due to their location.

Here, guests can be offered very good home conditions, in some cases even with a pool and a luxurious garden. Tourists can stay here with their children - for such cases there are special rooms equipped with extra beds, and some houses offer meals.

As for the cost, living in Anapa's private sector is inexpensive - its cost starts from 300 rubles per day from one person.

Best Economy Homes

Some tourists on vacation plan to save on accommodation. In their reviews, they say that even in budget class apartments also have all the necessary conditions for a normal stay. As a rule, in economy rooms in the private sector of Anapa there is a minimum amount of furniture: the necessary number of beds and a cupboard to store a minimum amount of clothing and personal belongings of guests. Some rooms have chairs or benches and private tables where guests can eat. Most tourists are happy with home furnishings in the courtyards of the private sector: here, as a rule, there are places for relaxation and beautiful green orchards with fruits.

The rooms in such houses, as a rule, are not divided into categories - there are only economy class rooms, the cost of which is about 250-300 rubles per person per day. As for children, it is usually free.





As for the location of houses, they can be located within walking distance from the sea, and at a long distance from the coast. Guests of the city note that it is advisable to pre-book places to stay in economy class houses, since a huge number of people come to Anapa who want to spend as little on housing as possible.

Here is a short list of addresses for cheap homes in the private sector (Anapa). The reviews of city guests often say that you can relax in good conditions, despite the low cost of living:

  • St. Crimean, 258 ("Cozy courtyard on the Crimean", from 250 rubles per day).
  • Green Street, 4 (from 250 rubles per day).
  • St. Workers, 189 (from 200 rubles per day).

In reviews of the private sector of Anapa, tourists often note that when choosing accommodation in economy rooms, tourists should always be ready to meet with a number of pitfalls. In particular, lovers of solitary pastime will not be happy to find a large number of neighbors nearby who will go about their business. The shared bathroom or the restroom may cause inconvenience to everyone, who, moreover, will be on the street. It should also be borne in mind that all vacationers who will live in the house will feel the need to visit the shower at about the same time - this will create small queues - they must be waited.
