Chalet "Fishing coast": photos and reviews

In a fabulous, uninhabited place near the Karelian Isthmus there is a recreation center "Fishing coast". This mini-village is located on the shores of Lake Otradnoe, surrounded by deciduous and pine trees.

Location

Chalet "Fishing coast" is located 106 km from St. Petersburg, in the village of Kutuzovskoye. People come here to relax on weekends, to celebrate an anniversary or a wedding, on long holidays and on vacation. The recreation center "Fishing shore" at any time of the year is ready to please its guests with entertainment.



Base "Fishing coast": photos of cottages confirm the description

On the basis of 8 cottages with a capacity of 4-12 people and 2 summer houses, which live in two. In the hotel building, 4 two-room rooms are organized, where 4 people live each. The necessary equipment for recreation: barbecue, grill, skewers and coal base "Fishing coast" provides free. Each of the cottages has its own number.

Summer Services

A hotly heated bath will remove the accumulated fatigue from the guests of the base, especially after active physical exertion or successful fishing. Base "Fishing coast" prepares for its guests two types of brooms: birch and oak. To cleanse the skin and make it healthier, improve breathing, soaring young birch branches. A dense oak broom is suitable for lovers of intense heat.

Kids club

It begins its work on the first summer day and ends on the last. An experienced teacher in an equipped children's room conducts developmental classes, competitions and games with children. The club is open from 10am to 10pm. The cost is 200 rubles per hour. From 11 to 13 and from 17 to 19 free classes are held.

Kitchen

On the basis of the "Fishing coast" there are two full kitchens, which serve cold and hot dishes. The first kitchen is located in a separate building, opposite the cottage number 1, the second - in the hotel building.





Watercraft

For lovers of fishing and those who want to make a water exercise, boats and boats are provided.

Shooting range

In a pneumatic dash, everyone checks accuracy. A pneumatic shooting range is a safe collective pastime. You can also find out how accurate your own eye is when playing darts.

Base "Fishing coast" rents bicycles and ATVs, for which it provides the necessary ammunition, which reliably protects against injuries.

Gazebo

In the glazed arbor, designed for a company of 20 people, it is pleasant to sit on cold autumn days. The covered gazebo is heated, so when it is cool outside, it is especially cozy.

Terrace

Renting a covered but open from the sides of a wooden terrace for up to 80 people will allow you to spend an unforgettable holiday, be it a birthday or a wedding. And let the weather be whimsical outside, relaxing under the arches of the terrace.





Games

Board games such as air hockey, soccer or tennis are waiting for guests to while away their leisure time at the Fisherman's Coast base. If the weather is warm outside, they play volleyball or football, rent a set for badminton.

Winter services

In winter, resting at the recreation center "Fishing Coast", you should use a snowmobile rental. Near the base there are several routes and tracks laid, the passage through which will give a pleasant feeling. More guests of the base enjoy skiing, play hockey, and ride the prepared artificial ice rink.
