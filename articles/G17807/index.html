What is important for a beginner to know?

If our reader is just learning the basics of knitting, then most likely he has no idea where to start. Therefore, in the current paragraph, we will explain in detail which preparatory steps precede knitting a hat:

  1. The first step is to determine which season requires the accessory.
  2. Then select the appropriate thread. That is, for the winter it is better to take those that are thicker and woolen, and for spring - thin and light.
  3. Then pick up the knitting needles. Experts advise focusing on the thickness of the thread. If you want to get a clear pattern ‚Äúwithout holes‚Äù, you should pick up the needles of the same thickness as the thread itself. If the knitting is planned to be loose or the person starting to knit tightens the loops very strongly, then the knitting needles can be two, or even three times thicker than the thread.
  4. Next, you should decide on the model of the header. For example, brighter, lacy options or those that consist of plaits or braids are suitable for women. Men, on the other hand, are calmer, rather, of dark colors, which are based on a simple elastic band. If we knit a children's hat with knitting needles, the fantasy has no boundaries, because in this case you can use any color combinations and patterns.
  5. Finally, it is very important to calculate the correct number of loops for a product that will look perfect on your head. How to do this, find out in the next paragraph.

How to determine how many loops to dial?

We figured out the model, threads, tools and decided which hat to knit. But just how do you know how many loops are required for a suitable-sized product? Actually very simple. And then our reader will be able to verify this.

To determine the correct number of loops, you must:

  1. Knit ten stitches.
  2. Knit ten rows with the selected pattern.
  3. Then, using a centimeter, measure the width of the resulting piece.
  4. Measure the width of the head.
  5. Divide the larger number into the smaller one.
  6. As a result, we get a certain figure.
  7. We multiply this figure by ten.
  8. And we get the number of loops for the perfect headgear.

Having solved this question, we proceed to the next step of the instruction "How to knit a hat with knitting needles for beginners."

How to make loops on knitting needles?

For a beginner who has just started learning the basics of knitting, it can be difficult to even dial loops. Therefore, we simply cannot ignore this issue.

To dial loops, you must:

  1. Unwind a small "tail" from the ball.
  2. Roll the thread into a loop.
  3. Then it should be put on the thumb and forefinger of the left hand.
  4. Now we take both knitting needles in the right hand.
  5. We put them under the thread that stretches between the fingers.
  6. We hook the thread lying on the thumb.
  7. We draw through the resulting loop the one on the index finger.
  8. As a result, two loops are immediately on the knitting needles.
  9. Then again we pick up the thread from the thumb.
  10. And we stretch the thread through it from the index finger.
  11. The two previous steps are repeated as many times as necessary. Until the right number of stitches appear on the knitting needles.

For beginners, the described process may seem too complicated, but this impression is misleading. After all, if you understand the technology, over time it will be performed automatically. Therefore, just in case, we will also provide step-by-step photo instructions. Thanks to her, the technology "how to knit a hat with knitting needles for beginners" will be fully understood.

What are the loops and how to knit them?

Before you start knitting the original hat, you need to figure out how to make different loops. After all, alternating them with different options, it will be possible to "depict" a lot of drawings.

There are only two types of main loops: purl and facial. To tie the first, you should throw a thread from which new loops are knitted from the front side of the fabric. Then, hook the first loop with the needle in the right hand and pull a new loop through it, as if ‚Äúdragging‚Äù it onto the right needle. Front loops fit in the same way. The only difference is that the main thread is behind the knitting needle in the left hand.

And also beginners should be aware that the first and last loops in the canvas are called edge. The first one just needs to be removed - transferred to the spoke in the right hand. And we knit the last loop as the wrong one.

When the loops are finished, we knit a hat with knitting needles. To do this, we understand the most beautiful, original and lightweight patterns.

Gum ordinary

This pattern is considered the most popular, because a product made in such technology knits very quickly and simply. But let's not get ahead of ourselves. Let us explain that the elastic and front loops alternate in an elastic band. The most advantageous options are:

  • 1 x 1;
  • 1 x 2;
  • 2 x 2;
  • 3 x 3.

However, it is not enough to choose the gum option. It is very important to correctly calculate the number of loops so that the pattern on the canvas looks finished. For example, if a reader chooses a 1 x 2 elastic, the total number of loops of a repeating pattern called rapport is three. This means that the total number of loops should consist of the following mathematical calculations: multiply the number of loops in the rapport by the number obtained during the calculations in the second paragraph of the current article, plus two edge loops.

Having figured out the counts, pattern and technology, we knit a hat with knitting needles.

Chess Pattern

The following option is very simple to perform if our reader understands the principle of knitting ordinary gum. After all, it can be said to be based on it. But his main secret is that the number of alternating loops and rows is equal. For example, a ‚Äúchess‚Äù pattern consists of a series of two wrong and two front loops. So, you need to knit the first row as follows:

  1. Remove the first loop.
  2. Knit the second and third, as facial.
  3. The third and fourth, as purl.
  4. Thus, the pattern repeat is two facial and two wrong loops.
  5. It should be repeated until the end.
  6. If the number of loops is calculated correctly, then at the end of the row there will be one "extra" loop.
  7. It should be knitted, like a wrong side.
  8. The second row fits in the pattern. Edge is removed, and then again follows a series of facial and wrong loops. The latter is the wrong side.

The hat connected, according to this instruction, is quite voluminous and very original.

Dots Pattern

Another interesting pattern is obtained by observing the following technology:

  1. The number of loops in the rapport is two. So, the total number of loops in a row must be even.
  2. Having typed the loop, we proceed to the execution of the pattern. To do this, remove the first loop.
  3. We knit the second, as a front one.
  4. The third, as the wrong side.
  5. Then alternate them.
  6. We knit the last loop, regardless of the pattern, as if it were the wrong one.
  7. We pass to the second row.
  8. We remove the edge loop.
  9. And then we knit according to the drawing. However, we do not repeat it, but, on the contrary, ‚Äúconfuse‚Äù it. That is, where the front knit purl and vice versa.
  10. In the third row, repeat the steps described for the first row.

Using this technology, we knit a children's hat or an adult. The pattern is suitable for both options. However, it looks best on female representatives. No matter how old they are.

Another rather unusual pattern will turn out if you follow the instructions below. The pattern repeat in this case is equal to four loops. Therefore, the total number of loops should consist of a multiple of four and two edge loops.

How to "pull off" a hat?

Many beginners encounter difficulties when reducing loops, finishing knitting hats. As a result, in some cases the product is small, while in others it is too bristling right on the top of the head. To prevent this, we will explain in detail how to reduce loops correctly:

  1. We determine the height of the headdress on our own and knit the hat with knitting needles for a woman, man or child, focusing on any of the patterns described above.
  2. Then, when the height is sufficient, that is, the cap covers the ears, and on the top of the head there is a small circle whose diameter is about eight to ten centimeters, you can begin to gradually remove the loops.
  3. Moreover, you can continue to knit a pattern, or you can go to knit facial loops.
  4. In the first row, remove the first loop, knit the next four according to the drawing, and then two together. So we move to the end. The last loop is the wrong one.
  5. In the second row and subsequent even, you should knit a hat with knitting needles, observing the pattern.
  6. In the third row, we also remove the first loop, knit the next three according to the drawing, after them two together. We continue until one edge loop remains.
  7. In the fifth row, we decrease one loop every two.
  8. In the seventh - through one.
  9. In the ninth and subsequent - every two loops, not counting the edge.
  10. When ten loops remain, we break off the main thread, stretch through them and tie.
  11. Then we sew the product and decorate it with a pompom.
