Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 * (Turkey / Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists

Alanya is the easternmost resort of Turkey, very popular with tourists around the world. One of the features of this area is affordable prices for services. This also applies to the cost of housing. There are a lot of cheap hotels in Alanya built. For unassuming tourists here , perhaps, a budget hotel Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 * will be a good choice .

Where is the complex located

This hotel is located in the very center of Alanya. Near him there are many shops, clubs, bars. In this regard, the majority of tourists consider the location of the hotel quite successful.





On the sites of tour operators it is reported that the Nergiz Hotel is located on the first coastline. In principle, the information is true. But unfortunately, next to the hotel are only paid, and quite expensive beaches. The good ones with free access are located far from it, as tourists note, far enough.

The nearest airport to the hotel is Gazipas, about 35 km from its buildings. This, of course, is not too far, but not particularly close. From the Antalya Airport, tourists who rented apartments in this hotel will have to get, of course, even longer. This transport air hub is located almost 100 km from the complex.

Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 *: hotel description

This hotel was built in the last century - in 1990. The last renovation here (in 2017), judging by the state of decoration, as vacationers note, was also carried out for a long time. Guests at the hotel are welcomed in one rather large five-story building.

Moreover, the price of rooms of different types, of course, varies. However, experienced tourists note the fact that all apartments rented at the hotel are of the standard class. So customers of Russian tour operators are free to rent economy rooms here at a lower price. The living conditions in the hotel will be the same in any case. At least, that was, judging by the reviews, in 2017.

What do tourists think about the rooms

The opinion about vacationers renting at Nergiz Hotel was unfortunately not very good. Many travelers advise renting rooms here only to those tourists who travel to Turkey solely for the sake of the sea and the sun. Unfortunately, unlikely vacationers will be able to relax in Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 * (Alanya) itself .

The majority of travelers attribute the disadvantages of hotel rooms to their very small size. For one person, hotel rooms in terms of spaciousness, as vacationers note, could still be acceptable. But usually at least two guests occupy apartments in this complex.

As for the cleanliness of the hotel rooms from tourists, there are also not too good reviews. The maids come to the rooms rented at the hotel quite often. But they do not do their work very carefully. Not too clean at this hotel, judging by the reviews, and the bedding. Sheets and towels at the hotel are provided for guests with washing and holes.

About showers in Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 * reviews on the Web, unfortunately, are also not too good. They are here, as noted, travelers, quite miserable. In addition, the plumbing in the bathrooms of the hotel is installed, unfortunately, not particularly high quality and at the same time also old.






Judging by the reviews, the air conditioners in the hotel apartments are not bad. In most rooms they do their job. The balconies in this complex, unfortunately, as tourists note, are too small. At least, it is unlikely that guests can comfortably relax on the balcony in the evening.

Hotel Infrastructure

On the territory of the hotel, Nergiz Hotel Sand City 3 * (Alanya) tour offers the following services:

  • open pool;

  • free parking;

  • Wi-fi

This property also has a sauna. Payment for various additional services at the hotel is accepted, including with cards.

Service Reviews

Despite the fact that the Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 * hotel (Turkey) belongs to the three-star class, the services on its territory to the guests, as you can see, are not particularly provided. Therefore, travelers respond to the service at this hotel without much enthusiasm.





The staff of this hotel, many tourists consider, unfortunately, too familiar. Hotel employees, for example, instead of working, can lie on sofas in the lobby, taking the place of vacationers, or try to entertain guests with flat jokes. Sometimes the hotel employees allow, judging by the reviews, in relation to the guests and all sorts of arrogant antics.

The only thing, many vacationers consider the father of the hotel owner to be a very sincere and friendly person. On the sites of tour operators this hotel is positioned as a family business. However, judging by the reviews, in reality this is not entirely true. This hotel is run mainly by the hostess. Moreover, she puts up prices for hotel services and room rental, according to many vacationers, overpriced.

How to feed at the hotel

If desired, the hotel can take half board or all-inclusive meals. About the dining room of the Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 *, unlike its rooms and service, there are mostly good reviews on the Web. The chef prepares the hotel restaurant, according to vacationers, very good. But on the products, according to tourists, the hotel owner still, unfortunately, is trying to save. Therefore, the menu in the restaurant of the complex is quite monotonous. In addition, guests who have rented rooms with half board in this hotel do not offer any drinks in the dining room. Such tourists cannot take tea or coffee in the restaurant of the complex. At the same time, they are given water only for a fee.

The beer in this hotel, as noted by guests who stayed in it on an all-inclusive basis, is offered not particularly high-quality. Pouring residents here is the cheapest. Moreover, in the end, the actual cost of beer at the hotel is equal to the price of the best brands in the city.





What is a hotel beach?

Hotel guests can reach the sea in just a couple of minutes. But, as tourists say, opposite to Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 * p, there is, unfortunately, the most expensive beach in the vicinity. Umbrellas and chaise lounges here cost 10 lire. On one of the neighboring beaches, a sunbed can be rented for 5 liras. Just sunbathing on a rug or towel on the sand by the sea next to the hotel will cost tourists as much as 2 lira. Also near the hotel there is a beach on which sunbeds are offered for any purchase in a cafe.

Judging by the reviews, the sea itself is not very clean, and the sea itself is next to the complex. The same goes for the beaches themselves. Cleaning on the coastline near the hotel is carried out only in those places where sun loungers are installed. Tourists resting on rugs are forced to sunbathe amidst small debris and cigarette butts.

The beaches next to the Nergiz Hotel are thus either too expensive or dirty. Therefore, many hotel guests prefer to leave on shuttles to sunbathe to other parts of the coastline of Alanya. For example, Cleopatra beach earned very good reviews from travelers. You can get here by buses No. 1 or No. 101. Tourists who decide to visit this beach should go in the direction to the right and get off at the Aquapark stop.

Buying excursions

There are many different places of interest for vacationers in Alanya itself. Of course, guests of the Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 * can visit various sights . However, experienced tourists here do not recommend taking excursions from the guides of their operators. Prices at large travel agencies for trips to Alanya are too high. In addition, hotel guests in some cases in order to quickly sell excursions may be provided with inaccurate information about it.





Instead of a conclusion

Thus, the reviews on the Web about Nergiz Hotel Sand and City 3 *, unfortunately, are not particularly good. Moreover, the cost of living in this hotel is not so cheap. Therefore, it is worth booking accommodation here only as a last resort, for example, if the trip was planned recently and there was no time to choose a hotel with better service. Perhaps someday the owners of this hotel will decide to spend money on its repair, as well as try to make the stay of tourists in it more convenient. But for 2017, this hotel can be considered, unfortunately, suitable for relaxation only for very unassuming travelers.
