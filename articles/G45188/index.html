What to get from Krasnodar to Novorossiysk?

Russia is an amazing country with many beautiful, picturesque places that you want to explore again and again. Every traveler is looking for something to travel: low prices for goods, ancient architecture, monuments, entertainment centers, unusual temples and much more. Lovers of beautiful nature often go on a short trip along the route Krasnodar - Novorossiysk.



These are two cities of amazing beauty. Fortunately, there are several types of transport that can facilitate the task - to get from one to another. Transport should be selected based on several factors.

Vehicle Selection Criteria

Before choosing a suitable vehicle, it is worth talking about what criteria you need to pay attention to:

  1. Health status. It is difficult for a sick and elderly person, a pregnant woman, a passenger with children to travel a few hours. Therefore, the train will be the ideal transport for them. In it you can change position, sleep and eat. The path will be as comfortable as possible. A good alternative is the train.
  2. If you want to get from one point to another in the shortest possible time, then you should give preference to the plane. But tickets for it can cost several times more than for any other vehicle.
  3. The cheapest but tedious way is to travel by bus.
  4. If during the road you need to call in at any places remote from the stops, then it is better to go by your own car or join friends who are traveling in this direction.




The distance from Krasnodar to Novorossiysk is only 150 kilometers, this allows you to use any convenient vehicle.

By car

150 kilometers from Krasnodar to Novorossiysk can be easily overcome by car. The total travel time is approximately 2 hours 35 minutes. If you look at the map of Russia, you can see that the route between these two points is one straight line. In addition, signs are installed along the entire route. You can get to the appointed point without even using the navigator.



On the way from one destination to another are two wonderful cities - Krymsk and Abinsk. Every traveler is recommended to visit them. On the road there are a huge number of gas stations, shops and cafes. Asphalt was updated not so long ago, so you can see the bright markings on it.

This track is always crowded. If suddenly a person goes astray, he can easily ask the passers-by for directions. The only drawback is the huge number of traffic jams throughout the day. Passengers usually stand at least three hours in them.



By train

The most convenient way to get from Krasnodar to Novorossiysk is by train. A direct flight is daily made between two settlements. Departure time: early in the morning - at 8 hours 50 minutes. Three hours later, the passenger is at the appointed place. The cost of a ticket for one person is only 280 rubles.

In addition to the train from Krasnodar to Novorossiysk, the train number 355U also passes. At about 6 in the morning, the flight leaves from Krasnodar 1 station. He arrives at the Novorossiysk station exactly 3 hours and 15 minutes later. The minimum ticket price is 495 rubles. On a branded train, ticket prices are slightly more expensive. You can buy them at the box office or order online.

By bus

Exactly 3 hours 30 minutes is the bus from Krasnodar to Novorossiysk. There will be no problems with buying tickets, as flights go several times a day. The first route leaves from the bus station, located at the following address: Privokzalnaya square, house 5, at 7 a.m. And already at 10 hours 31 minutes the bus arrives at the bus station of Novorossiysk (Tchaikovsky, 15). The fare is 310 rubles. However, if a passenger travels from one point to another with large luggage, he will need to pay 25 rubles for his transportation.



By plane

Many travelers, before setting off on a journey, find out the flight schedule. There is no direct flight with transfers from Krasnodar to Novorossiysk. And to use it would be unreasonable, since the distance between these two cities is completely insignificant.

Two beautiful cities - Krasnodar and Novorossiysk. You can talk about their sights endlessly, and yet it will not work to convey the beauty of these places in words. It can only be appreciated only with a personal acquaintance with these unique places.

What is the best way to move from one city to another? The choice can be made, relying only on the taste of the passenger. The most convenient is a car. If there is no own transport, then it is better to give preference to the train. Bus and train are the longest and most expensive option, but at the same time many locals of these settlements prefer them!
