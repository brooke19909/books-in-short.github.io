How to detect a hidden camera at home

Most likely, you heard a couple of stories about how a hidden camera was found where, by definition, it should not have been - in a public toilet, locker room or even a hotel room. There are many legitimate and legal reasons for installing hidden cameras:

  • Protection of your business (retail outlet, distribution points, wardrobes in public places, etc.)
  • Protection of a house or apartment (privately owned)
  • Property monitoring in courtyards and surrounding areas
  • Checking employees who work at your place (nanny for your child, cleaning lady or janitor)
