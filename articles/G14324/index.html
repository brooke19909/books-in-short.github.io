How to make modular origami "Flowers" with your own hands?

It turns out that beautiful tender flowers that will look original both on the desktop and in the bride‚Äôs bouquet can be made even by a child. The craft can be presented to a mom or girlfriend for a holiday, in any case, you simply can‚Äôt imagine a better gift.





Modular origami "Flowers": material

To make bright happiness, you will need colored and corrugated paper, glue, wire and floss threads.

Modular origami "Flowers": the basic scheme

Before you begin, you need to make a few simple blanks that are performed using standard techniques for many products. These are the so-called "modules". To make one, you will need a square sheet of paper (side - 5-7 centimeters). First, it should be bent in half, then bend the side corners to the top. Then again on the sides. Each corner must be bent from the middle out. Now we straighten the design. As a result, you should have a figure that looks like a shamrock. The vertices of the corner will have to be hidden inside a small triangle. Next, we bend them up again. It remains to glue the upper sides of the side triangles. The flower module is ready.





Modular origami "Flowers": collect crafts

The number of modules will depend on how many flowers in the bouquet you want to receive. Three plants will look great too. If you are preparing a product in kindergarten, then let each child make one module. The result is an impressive amount of flowers in your bouquet. For one product you need five modules. Thus, if you want to make 5 colors, prepare 25 parts. To assemble the product, glue the middle of the modules, grabbing the sides. That's all, one flower is ready. Rather, it is still his bud. Now make the stem. To do this, take the wire, push it into the middle of the flower, fix it in the bud. The wire should be wrapped with a thin strip of green corrugated paper or floss thread. It is better to smear it with glue in advance so that the craft is durable. You can also make sheets of corrugated paper. To do this, take a square sheet, fold it in half diagonally, attach it to the shape and glue it to the stem.





Modular origami "Flowers": a bouquet

The more modules you make, the more flowers there will be, which means a more elegant bouquet. Now you have to collect your products in a pile. It is better to fix the stems with thread floss, carefully wrapping them at the base of the buds. Spread the latter, giving the design a natural look. Now you know how to make modular origami. Vase (a master class on the manufacture of which you can already show yourself) can be performed in various techniques. The main thing is that it organically combines with your delicate flowers and does not spoil the overall romantic mood from the contemplation of your creation.

Conclusion

Presenting an origami on March 8 or a birthday is a great idea for a surprise. The bouquet is made simple, but it looks very unusual. Your relatives will not even guess that you spent no more than an hour on making a masterpiece.
