Screen

It should be noted immediately that a 5.5-inch display is installed on the Elephone P8000 4G LTE (mobile phone). We agree that a screen of this size is more inherent in phablets (something between a phone and a tablet computer), but, nevertheless, manufacturers opted for precisely such dimensions.
