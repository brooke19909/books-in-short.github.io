Kapetanios Hotel Limassol (Cyprus / Limassol): Travel Photos and Reviews

Summer is the time for a beach holiday. And in search of adventure on the beach, tourists often turn their eyes to Cyprus. This country is famous not only for its picturesque coast and mild climate, but also for its rich culture, hospitality and a huge number of hotels for every taste. One of them is the small complex Kapetanios Hotel Limassol.

Of course, choosing a hotel for relaxation, travelers are looking for any additional information. Where is the hotel located and will you have to go far to the beach? Do travelers offer additional services? Is this place suitable for a family vacation with a child? Do youth companies come here? Is it nice to spend time here? Are the rooms clean? The answers to these questions are important for every tourist who is going to spend time in the territory of this hotel.

Where is the hotel situated?





Of course, the important point when choosing a hotel becomes the location of its location. Kapetanios Hotel Limassol has an advantage in this regard, as it is located in one of the most prestigious areas of the city of Limassol. The luxurious beach is just 100 meters away. Near the hotel there is a zoo, the historical center of the city, the archaeological museum and other attractions of the resort town, which will be of interest to both adults and children. On the other hand, the place is quite calm - tourists can count on a serene pastime.

Kapetanios Limassol Hotel (Cyprus): general description

This is a small hotel, which consists of one multi-storey building. The last thorough repair was carried out here in 2002, but the hotel looks beautiful, and one can hope for the benefits of modern civilization.

A small but cozy area adjoins the building. Part of it is reserved for pools and terraces. Here you can stroll along neat paths and mowed lawns, admire the vibrant colors and exotic trees.

How many rooms are there in a hotel?

Hotel Kapetanios Limassol Hotel 3 * is small - on its territory there are 75 rooms. For the most part, these are spacious standard rooms with city or sea views. However, there are several large family apartments, which consist of a bedroom, a living area and a small but fully equipped kitchen, which is convenient, because sometimes children need to cook separately. By the way, the large room is designed for shared accommodation of two adults and two children.

Photo and a brief description of the amenities in the rooms





Guests at the Kapetanios Hotel Limassol can count on all the necessary amenities. The rooms here are spacious, most of them have balconies where you can enjoy the view and breathe in the healthy sea air while sitting on comfortable garden furniture. The design of the rooms is modern, but homely.

Of course, you can count on a set of equipment, without which you can‚Äôt imagine the rest of a modern tourist. Free time can be spent on watching satellite channels. There is an easy-to-manage climate control system that will help to cope with the summer heat or create comfortable conditions in the cool winter months. The phone makes it possible to directly make international calls, however, the bill will then need to be paid.

The room comes with a bathroom with round-the-clock water supply and working plumbing. Depending on the room category, guests have the opportunity to either take a shower or relax in the fragrant bath. You can count on clean towels (delivered every day) and toiletries.

The reviews of travelers who have already managed to spend several days here confirm that the rooms are in almost perfect condition - high-quality cleaning is carried out regularly, and therefore the rooms are clean and fresh.

What kind of food is offered to tourists?





Good nutrition is an integral part of a truly good vacation. At Kapetanios Limassol Hotel, guests are free to choose the food scheme that is convenient for them. Room rates include free daily breakfast. For an additional fee, you can also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant.

In the morning a delicious breakfast awaits you. Reviews of tourists indicate that the menu here is quite diverse, and the dishes are always fresh. In the evening, you can have dinner here, but food is already being prepared on order. Naturally, the city has a huge number of catering establishments.

Location and description of the beach





What can a Kapetanios Limassol Hotel 3 offer in terms of beach holidays? Limassol is a city that is ideal for a vacation by the sea. The hotel is located almost on the coast - the distance to the beach is no more than 100 meters.

The seashore and descent are covered with soft sand. Hotel guests are offered free sunbeds with soft mattresses and sun umbrellas. Reviews say that there are always enough sun loungers. Here you can get large beach towels, but you need to pay for their rent. By the way, there is a small bar on the beach where, for a reasonable price, you can enjoy a cocktail or even have lunch, enjoying the warm sea breeze.

Water activities

What should tourists do who prefer a more active holiday? Kapetanios Hotel Limassol has a sports center, which offers an impressive range of services. Here you can rent water transport, including canoes, boats, catamarans. Travelers love to ride jet skis, boats, bananas. You can get a shot of adrenaline by trying yourself in parasailing. And, of course, you can go windsurfing and scuba diving (there are even special training courses). Naturally, the services of the water recreation center are paid separately, but the prices are not overpriced.

Additional service for guests





Of course, even a household trifle can sometimes significantly ruin a vacation. Fortunately, Kapetanios Hotel Limassol offers everything you need for a comfortable stay. To begin with, it is worth saying that the reception is open around the clock, as well as the room service.

Guests can rent a safe from the administrator, which guarantees the safety of valuables. You can use the left-luggage office, which is very convenient if you intend to travel along the coast, changing hotels and at the moment you simply do not need all your things. The hotel constantly operates a currency exchange.

In addition, travelers can use the laundry and dry cleaning services for a reasonable fee. You can quickly rent a car on site (if you have the relevant documents, of course). If necessary, employees will arrange a transfer for you or call a taxi. By the way, the hotel has a spacious parking lot, where you can leave your transport for free.

Sometimes a hotel becomes a place of corporate relaxation. There is constant access to the Internet, a small business center where you can send a fax, as well as a conference room for 50 people. By the way, the room is sometimes used for holidays (weddings, birthdays).

Kapetanios Limassol Hotel 3 (Cyprus, Limassol): leisure and entertainment for travelers

How do guests spend their free time? In fact, this hotel was created for a calm, measured and comfortable stay. In the courtyard there is a large outdoor pool with clean water. Around him on the terrace are comfortable sun loungers with umbrellas. There is also a bar nearby where you can get light snacks or enjoy a chilled cocktail.





In cold weather, as well as in the winter months, guests can relax by the indoor pool with a heating system. You should definitely visit the small spa center, which offers wellness treatments. Here you can access the sauna for free. There is also a jacuzzi where you can relax. And skilled massage therapists will help you restore good health and improve your health.

You can have a good time playing a game of table tennis. From time to time among the guests organize bingo evenings. The hotel has a beautiful, multilingual library, which is also free to use. There is also a gym where travelers can keep fit.

In the evenings, live music is played on site - you can enjoy the beautiful sound or even dance. Cypriot folklore evenings are regularly organized for travelers. And do not forget that just a few minutes walk - the city center of Limassol with all its historical attractions and entertainment venues. No traveler is bored here.

Conditions for the rest of parents with children

Many travelers wonder if you can relax with your child at the Kapetanios Limassol Hotel. Limassol is a place that is ideal for children. The hotel itself offers all the necessary amenities so that a family vacation is fun and pleasant.

Naturally, parents can count on a rollaway bed in the room and a high chair in the restaurant. Young tourists, of course, will not be bored, because here they are waiting for bright entertainment. In the daytime there is a games room where animators look after the children. There is a shallow but spacious pool with heated water. Your child will be able to have fun in the open air with a swing. Yes, and evening performances give the kids a lot of positive emotions. And if necessary, parents can always use the services of a nanny.

Hotel Traveler Reviews





A lot of useful information can be gleaned by asking the opinion of tourists who have already managed to visit one or another place. So what are they saying about the Kapetanios Limassol Hotel? Reviews are by and large positive. Naturally, this is not a huge five-star hotel-city, but it has its own advantages.

The hotel is small, and therefore very comfortable. The staff greet each guest with sincere joy and do everything to make guests feel at home. The living conditions in the rooms are good, and you can eat delicious food at any time of the day. The hotel is quiet, so you can count on a quiet sleep and a relaxing holiday by the sea. No less important advantage is the relatively low cost of living, especially considering the level of service and convenient location.

By the way, the Kapetanios Limassol Hotel in most cases becomes the venue for family vacations, although youth companies and business people often stop here.
