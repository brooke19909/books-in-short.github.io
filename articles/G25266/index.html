Step-by-step master class

Before you sew a jacket for a doll, you need to cut it out on a piece of paper. Acting strictly according to the scheme, even a beginner can draw out the exact patterns for the doll:

  1. In the middle of the checkered sheet, draw a vertical line.
  2. On a vertical line, draw two horizontal lines parallel to each other, at a distance from the desired length of the future sweater.
  3. Mark the point on top of the vertical line bordering the horizontal. We apply the place of the neck. To do this, divide the neck circumference into two. If the OR is 6 cm, mark 3 cm, 1.5 centimeters on each side of the vertical line.
  4. Step back 4 cm - the length of the neck, and draw a horizontal line. Thus, the shoulders loom on the pattern. Do not forget to measure the desired width of the shoulders and mark on the pattern.
  5. Down, from the shoulder line, we retreat 4 cm. We draw a line. From the line mark the width, chest circumference. When building the shelf, we divide the resulting measurement into two: Exhaust gas - 14 cm, which means 14: 2 = 7 cm. It results in a half-girth of the chest.
  6. The armholes can be drawn by eye.
  7. We measure the width of the doll clothes by eye, if desired. If the jacket is fitted to the hips, then at the bottom of the pattern we measure the half circumference of the hips: we divide OB by two, for example: 12.5 cm: 2 = 6.25 cm.
  8. Having drawn the pattern, we apply the doll to the paper, "trying on" a pattern on our model.
  9. We carefully cut out the pattern and proceed to sewing clothes.

How to sew a jacket for a doll with your own hands

Before making clothes, the needlewoman must have experience with tools such as a sewing machine, needle and scissors. If you have such a skill, you can safely proceed to the creation of a new puppet wardrobe.

First, we will prepare the necessary materials:

  • sweatshirt pattern;
  • fabric, shreds;
  • remnant or chalk;
  • scissors;
  • pins
  • needle, thread;
  • sewing machine.

Work algorithm:

  1. We select the right fabric. It should be smooth and smooth. For tailoring a warm blouse, fleece, velvet, velor, knitwear are suitable. We impose a pattern on the prepared patch, fasten it with a pin and carefully outline with remnant or chalk.
  2. We cut the workpiece with sharp scissors, not forgetting the seam allowances. With manual sewing - 5 mm, with sewing stitch - at least 7 mm.
  3. Sew the cut parts manually or sew on a typewriter.
  4. Do not forget about the clasp. It can be located at the back, on the back, or in front of the sweatshirt, depending on your preferences and on the model of clothing.
  5. To sew the sleeve, we attach a piece of the flap to Barbie‚Äôs hand, fix it with pins and carefully draw the desired length and width. Cut out the part. Sew on the wrong side of the sleeve edge and sew it to the jacket.
  6. We turn the thing on the front side. The edges of the sleeves can be sewn with a blind seam, or decorated with a cuff, ribbon or lace.
  7. The neck of the sweatshirt is processed by sharpening or decorated with a collar. To make a collar, cut it out on paper. To do this, circle the neck of the jacket on the sheet, draw the desired model of the collar and cut it out. Sew to the neck from the inside.

The new blouse for Barbie is ready.

Sweatshirt for Monster High

Before you sew a jacket for a doll "Monster High", you need to build a pattern.

A drawing of a sweatshirt is constructed in the same way as a sweater pattern for a Barbie doll, but taking into account the characteristics of the Monster High parameters. The sizes and shapes of the mystic lady are slightly different from the figures of the American blonde - fashionistas.

Dimensions "Monster High":

  • breast volume - 7.5 cm;
  • width under the chest - 5.5 cm;
  • waist volume - 6 cm;
  • thigh volume - 10 cm;
  • line from shoulder to waist - 3 cm;
  • arm length from shoulder to hand - 6 cm.

Necessary materials to create a new thing:

  • fabric of two colors: for sweatshirt and trim (hood, pockets, cuffs);
  • needle and thread, sewing machine;
  • pins
  • scissors;
  • tissue marker or chalk;
  • pattern.

Step-by-step instruction on how to sew a hoodie for a doll:

  1. Fold evenly ironed fabric with the front side inward. Apply a pattern from above: front shelves, back, sleeves. Circle the patterns with a marker. Cut the back on the fold of the fabric, so that you get not two parts, but one.
  2. Cut the details with sharp scissors, not forgetting the allowances for 0.5 cm seams. Connect with pins and gently sweep with large stitches.
  3. Sew blanks manually or by machine. Unscrew on the front side and sew 1 mm from the shoulder seam, on two shelves.
  4. Fold the cuffs in half, attach the front side to the bottom of the sleeve. Then make a seam.
  5. Stitch the top okat in the same way to the armhole of the jacket.
  6. To tailor the hood, you need to measure the neck. First, draw a line on a piece of paper and use a protractor to draw a semicircle of the hood. From the fabric, cut two parts. Stitch facing inwards and weld with a zigzag or overlock.
  7. Stitch the hood to the sweatshirt and process the seam.
  8. Cut patch pockets. They can have both a square shape and a semicircular shape. Sew oblique or even sections of pockets beforehand, and then, bending the edges by 0, 2 cm, sew to the jacket. Lay a seam on top.
  9. At the bottom of the sweatshirt, sew on an elastic band or a hem according to the cuff principle.
  10. As a fastener use a small "zipper", Velcro, hooks.
  11. Without a hood and a fastener, you will get a sweatshirt for the doll. To do this, you need to tailor and stitch the front shelves according to the method of cutting the back.
  12. You can decorate the jacket with beads, sequins.
