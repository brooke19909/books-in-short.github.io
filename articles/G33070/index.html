A few words about the company

Not so long ago, in the commercials on the same federal entertainment channel, ZTE became the sponsor of the show. And there are several reasons for this. Firstly, the company is manufacturing budget smartphones with very high performance indicators. That is, it is really a worthy competitor in the modern market to such companies as Lenovo or Philips. Of course, the company can not yet compete with such a giant as Samsung, but it is possible that in the future it will surpass the top flagship models. Secondly, ZTE is a hybrid product manufactured by a Russian-Chinese company. Yes, the company is both Chinese and Russian at the same time. This is what marked its growing popularity in Russia.

RAM and internal memory

ZTE Blade AF 3 (t221), reviews of which say that the smartphone is really worth the money, works quite well even with a relatively small amount of memory. Although, if desired, it can be increased.
