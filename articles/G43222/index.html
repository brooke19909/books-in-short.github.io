Traditions of recognition of the fact of marriage in antiquity

Probably, few have heard the expression "consummate marriage" What does this mean in the tradition of many peoples of antiquity? In past centuries, according to historians, the role of the first wedding night between a woman and a man was important. Why? Because the term "consummation" from the Latin language is translated as "completion". This moment usually meant the first sexual intercourse between husband and wife.





What does it mean to consume marriage?
