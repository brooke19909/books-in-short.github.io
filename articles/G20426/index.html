Hotel "Deck", Kalyazin: how to get there? Reviews

Park Hotel "Deck" in Kalyazin is a wonderful place to relax and spend a great vacation. It is a whole complex of entertainment places that guests of neighboring cities and its indigenous people like to visit.

Location

This is a wonderful place to stay in the Tver region, in the Kalyazinsky district, along the highway that leads to Uglich. Around this place there are dense and green forests that make the air here very clean and pleasant. That is why tourists often come here for recreation. The hotel is located on the very banks of the large river Zhabnya, which is a right tributary of the Volga.



Rooms

On the territory of the park-hotel "Deck" in Kalyazin there is a large hotel where tourists can relax. It features 30 well-equipped rooms with modern furniture and appliances.

As for the level of comfort inside the apartment, it is always at its best. There are rooms of several categories: economy, standard, comfort, family, luxury, and even there are apartments with a personal sauna.

Each room has the required number of beds. As a rule, in many of them accommodation of two guests is provided, therefore the places are double, with new linen. In each room there is the possibility of installing an extra bed, which is made for a certain surcharge.

In addition to beds in the bedrooms there are cabinets and wardrobes for storing personal belongings. It also provides mirrors and dressing tables with chairs. Everywhere there is a plasma screen for watching satellite channels.

Each room has a private bathroom, there is a shower, sink and bathroom.



Conditions for children

Park Hotel "Deck" (Kalyazin) is a great place where clean air and beautiful nature reign. It is not surprising that families often spend their holidays here. By the way, the hotel administration creates all the necessary conditions for an increased level of comfort for children. Here, if necessary, a cot can be provided in the room, and this is done completely free of charge. The local restaurant has a personal menu for kids, which includes the most wholesome, but at the same time vibrant and mouth-watering dishes. There are also special comfortable highchairs.

Nutrition

The guests of this recreation center can eat in an excellent restaurant, the interior of which is made in the style of a loft. This establishment is located on the territory of the park-hotel "Deck" (Kalyazin). Photos taken within its walls confirm the presence of an amazing atmosphere that reigns in the room. You can watch them standing in the middle of the institution a luxurious fireplace, which performs not only a decorative function, it is valid and in the cold season warms guests with its warmth. Visitors here can sit on comfortable and soft chairs, at small, but very spectacular tables. On the walls of the institution there are plasma TVs on which music and sports broadcasts are held.

As for the cuisine, then, according to reviews of the Park Hotel "Deck" (Kalyazin), the dishes here are very tasty. Each dish served here is prepared in the best Russian and European traditions. Guests note the high professionalism of the chefs working in the local cuisine.

The main hall of the restaurant can serve as an excellent venue for a banquet, which in the warm season can also be arranged on the summer terrace.



Brewery

The institution has its own brewery, which brews a delicious drink according to company recipes. Guests at the Paluba Hotel (Kalyazin) often share comments on the original tastes of the local beer.

The large workshop has all the equipment necessary for the production of beer, starting from its earliest stage. As for the ingredients from which the drink is made, then, according to the manufacturer, the composition includes only natural ingredients: malt, yeast, water and hops. The final product is unfiltered and unpasteurized, which allows you to maintain its original taste.



Yacht Club

Everyone has the opportunity, staying at the Hotel Deck in Kalyazin, to rent a yacht. To do this, contact the local yacht club, which is located along the coast of the Zhabnya River. On its territory you can also leave your own ship - there are special places for this. Their rental is paid and is charged in accordance with the current price list, depending on the length of the side of the vessel.

The parking for river vessels is guarded during the day, it is also equipped with electricity and water supply.

Tourists who relax in the hotel can book a boat trip along the river surface. Such entertainment is often chosen in order to arrange a romantic date with a loved one. Fans of extreme sports can take the opportunity to ride water skiing, air cushions or wakeboards. However, this type of entertainment is more popular in the warm season.

Bathhouse

Fans of "hot" relaxation can visit the bathhouse, which is located on the clean and picturesque banks of the Zhabnya River, on the territory of the Hotel Deck in Kalyazin. In the bath complex, guests are offered a comfortable relaxation room, a bathroom, a swimming pool and, of course, a good steam room. The main hall has a small kitchen with accessories, seating areas, a large table, a TV. In the warm season, guests can sit on the veranda, which is located near the bathhouse - it has a large massive wooden table and several large benches. In the cold season, all seasoned ice-diving enthusiasts can do this after visiting the steam room. 15 meters from the bathhouse there is a river, where, if necessary, they can cut through the nast.

In such a bath, up to 8 people can steam at the same time. Bathrobes, towels and slippers are provided. If necessary, after paying an additional fee, guests can use the restaurant service.



Excursions

Everyone can take the opportunity to take part in excursion programs to the attractions that are in the district. They are designed for different age categories. Especially popular from all presented are such programs as the quest "Village Detective", a tour to the Kalyazin Ice Palace, a trip to a riding school, a visit to the Kashin Museum and Kashin traditions.

The hotel also has the ability to take helicopters, as there is a specially equipped area, ideal for their landing.

Beach

The hotel has a well-equipped beach where tourists can relax and sunbathe in the warm season. This area is equipped with comfortable sun loungers, umbrellas, public toilets and locker rooms. There are also several showers. The swimming place is well arranged, it is always clean, and the entrance to the water is gentle, which is very convenient for young children.

The beach also has a well-equipped volleyball court. In the event that vacationers live in hotel rooms, they can take all the equipment for the game free of charge.



Additional services and features

Guests of the hotel and complex can be provided with a number of services. In particular, the entire territory can become an excellent venue for various events. In the local restaurant you can arrange a lavish banquet or buffet, and outdoor festivities are often organized in the open air.

There is a large conference room, which is often used for events, business meetings and presentations. There is a separate room for playing billiards, it has several tables covered with green cloth.

Among the services at the Hotel Deck (Kalyazin) are horse riding.

Large companies here can rent entire gazebos, near which there are barbecue facilities. Vacationers can have a great time and fry barbecue on the beautiful bank of the river.



Reviews

On social networks and on the Internet, you can find a lot of reviews about how you can relax. In their comments, people share their impressions. They are generally positive. Many tourists recommend going here to relax with the whole family. Someone advises to spend time here creatively, while someone offers this zone in order to retire with a loved one. In general, as the tourists themselves say, this place is original, beautiful and suitable for completely different purposes.

Reviews of the Hotel Deck in Kalyazin often describe good service from staff. Many note the beauty of nature and purity throughout.



How to get there

Before you go on vacation, you must know how to get to the Hotel Deck in Kalyazin. Your route must be laid to the final destination - the village of Myshino, this is where this resort area is located. It is located near the highway that leads from Kalyazin to Uglich. It is very easy to get here by car. Alternatively, you can always order a taxi.
