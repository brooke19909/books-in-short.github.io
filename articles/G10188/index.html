What is it, the oldest civilization?

History is one of the most intricate subjects in the whole world. Probably no one will be able to tell with accuracy what the oldest civilization in the world was. Some argue that they were Arians, others called the aborigines of the Australian continent, and still others talk about Sumerians. Each theory has its own right to exist.



The Aryam is credited with the construction of the ancient city of Arkaim, which was found in the Urals. Excavations carried out on the site of the site showed that it was a well-defended settlement, which had walls and a moat. It is believed that this little-studied ancient people inhabited the entire continent of Eurasia. In Indian culture, much consists of Aryan sources.

We should not lose sight of the works of ancient historians. The legendary Troy was discovered by Schliemann precisely thanks to the works of Homer. Does this mean that we should pay more attention to the works of ancient scholars about Atlantis? Atlantes may be the oldest civilization in the world. The ancient Greeks attributed to them incredible abilities. Maybe these legends did not appear from scratch.

New findings in the Asian part of Eurasia provide evidence that the ancient civilization of China was more developed than we imagine. Terracotta warriors, the Great Wall of China and many other mysteries continue to excite the minds of scientists - archaeologists.

Each civilization has different paths of development. The history of Australian Aborigines dates back more than 40 thousand years. They have their own unique visual perception and some other differences of the physiological plan from Europeans. Aboriginal culture is not primitive, it is just different and is misunderstood by modern man.



The official point of view claims that the Sumerians are the oldest civilization on Earth. This people lived in separate groups in different cities that constantly waged internecine wars. Disagreements so weakened the country that it was soon captured by Sargon of Akkad. The Sumerians had an extremely complex religious system, each city was represented by one of the gods. The center of power was the high priests who ruled on behalf of the gods.

The findings of scientists on the South American continent indicate that the most ancient civilization could well exist on the territory of modern Brazil or Argentina. Mexican pyramids, according to some scholars, were not built by Indians. A detailed analysis of the buildings showed that the Aztecs only carried out "completion" of some elements of their cities. The age of the South American pyramids is difficult to establish, but it is believed that they are at least no younger than the Egyptian ones.



History holds many secrets and mysteries. For example, statues of Easter Island. The production and transportation of each of the figures should take a huge amount of time and effort. Even with current technologies, it is very difficult to perform such a large-scale work. The meaning of the performed actions is difficult for a modern person to understand. However, the view that the most ancient civilization was involved in the construction of these idols continues to intensify. Perhaps the Atlantean survivors thus tried to pay the last tribute to their people. Archaeologists continue their work and, perhaps, the answer to the question of what it is, the oldest civilization, will soon be given.
