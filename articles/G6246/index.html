About the manufacturer

Yarn is produced in Turkey, India, China. Perhaps this is subjective, but some reviews of Vita Brilliant yarn note that the quality of threads made in China is inferior to similar ones made in India or Turkey.

The symbol of yarn is a recognizable sign - a peacock feather on a dark green background.

The labels indicate: made in Turkey for OSBP GmbH, Richard Wagner Str., 3, Essen, Germany.

This suggests that Vita is a German trading brand, which, as modern manufacturers often do, delegates production functions to more convenient countries. Therefore, so often you can hear about the German yarn "Vita" or "Diamond".

But the search for data on the German company that owns the Vita brand yielded nothing. There is evidence that the rights to the brand belong to the Russian group of companies "Simpex", which since 1994 has been engaged in the wholesale of yarn in Russia. On the other hand, there is information that "Simplex" is simply a supplier of imported brands, including Vita. But then it is not clear why there is absolutely no information about this yarn on German sites and Ebay.

So it would be premature to call this yarn German.

What is a "laster"?

Translated from English luster means "shine." Ordinary shine wool is deprived, because, like any hair, it is covered with scales - it is they who are responsible for the causticity, the tendency to stall the finished fabric. In the process of special processing, the wool is first treated with acids (this eliminates the fiber ‚Äúfluffiness‚Äù), and then subjected to sizing (coating with a special composition containing adhesives, softeners, antiseptics). The applet is called HERCOSETT-125, the technology is patented.

After this treatment, the wool gains its luster, while not losing its solidity. Its texture remains noble, and the thread itself gains increased strength - things connected from wool last, perfectly tolerate machine wash. In addition, the laster:

  1. It is not peeling (that is, it is not covered with pellets).
  2. It does not shrink.
  3. After washing, it is not subject to felting (a cloth of ordinary wool is felted in the ‚Äúwet way‚Äù, it is wetting and subjecting the surface to friction, this is how felt is obtained).
  4. Does not irritate the skin and does not bite. The canvas is smooth and soft to the touch.

Please note: "laster" is an unofficial name. Another term for the same type of wool processing is superwash.

Pluses according to reviews

In reviews, the Vita Diamond yarn gives an average very high rating:

  1. Pleasant to the touch silky, elastic thread. It does not fluff, does not creak, moderately slippery - it‚Äôs comfortable to knit the fabric manually, it gives pleasure in the process. It is easy to work with a thread, it is not confused, it is easily pulled out of a skein.

Cons and criticism

As with any low-cost thread, the quality of Vita Brilliant yarn may vary by batch. Hence the contradictions in the reviews. Cons mentioned by knitters:

  1. There are places with a defective twist.
  2. Very rarely there are places with knots (1-2 knots per skein), with coarse fibers and garbage.
  3. Very rarely there are reviews of the discomfort that caused the canvas. Keep in mind that the level of skin sensitivity is different for everyone, and even despite the numerous reviews about the softness of this yarn, it may seem choppy.

Which products are best for

Many recommend this yarn for knitting in several additions or in one crochet addition. A product of size 44-46 with knitting needles in one thread will require a lot of time. The yarn holds well openwork and arana in one thread. A great choice for a shawl or sweater. Despite the fact that the yarn is positioned as winter, because of the thickness of the yarn, knitters often choose it for the manufacture of light things: tunics, dresses - and they are not mistaken. The canvas also shows itself perfectly in the summer - light, not prickly, not stuffy, plastic.





Features of working with yarn

Reviews about the ‚ÄúVita Diamond‚Äù yarn contain a lot of useful recommendations. What to consider before work:

  1. The thread may delaminate, so choose a needle with a blunt end.
  2. The color of the same article in different lots can vary greatly. Therefore, it is better not to buy yarn, but to purchase immediately the required number of skeins in one place.

Vita Diamond yarn products in operation

This is the most problematic when choosing a knitting yarn. Inexpensive thread often shows itself perfectly in the process of creativity, and the finished product cannot be worn. ‚ÄúVita Diamond‚Äù yarn is highly praised in terms of wear. Just consider the following points:

  1. It is possible to wash in the car in the gentle mode and not at high temperature (30-40 degrees).
  2. The product does not sit after washing. It is stretched slightly, and only if the thing is really large or has a solid length. Be prepared that the skirt to the floor of this yarn after washing will stretch by 20%.
  3. After several socks, it can roll up a bit in places of great friction: on the sides, under the armpits.
  4. In some reviews you can find information that bright shades shed. Unfortunately, this is a frequent drawback of inexpensive threads, and different shades, or even the same color in different batches, can behave differently. Before work, check that the thread does not fade: soak a small length and iron it through a thin cotton cloth with a hot iron. If traces of paint remain on the fabric, the product will fade.
