Hotel Palm Oasis Boutique Hotel 4 * is simply impossible not to notice. It stands out very much from the rest of the buildings and places available nearby. After all, this is a real botanical garden. The whole hotel is just surrounded by greenery and exotic vegetation! And the hotel itself was located in close proximity to Nai Harn Beach. And not so far is Phuket Airport. 35 kilometers is not a lot and not a little. But enough to not hear the noise of airplanes and still not go out a few hours before leaving home.

By the way, nearby is also Rawai beach, Promtep cape and Kata beach. These are the places that you just need to visit while staying here on vacation.
