Cottage Stamboli (Feodosia). History and Description

The famous estate of Stamboli is the architectural heritage of Feodosia. The monument is constantly in the spotlight and attracts with its own dissimilarity with other interesting structures of the resort metropolis. Built in the distant 1914, the cottage of Stamboli (Feodosia) felt and suffered battles and perestroika. Its beautiful buildings were enjoyed by more than one generation of local residents and visiting guests of the city. The cottage-palace of Stamboli (Feodosia) is made in the unsurpassed style of the East. Visually, the building immediately stands out among other buildings of the same importance for the resort town.

Hard times

Later, this two-story building acted as a spa complex. Here was opened the first sanatorium on the southern coast with 100 beds. It was named after I.V. Stalin. In wartime, the building of the sanatorium was again useful. It housed a hospital for Russian wounded soldiers, and during the occupation, the injured Germans were already brought here. The territory, which was near the dacha, was given for burial for soldiers and officers. At the end of the war, the building and the bridge nearby were mined; they planned to blow them up during the retreat, but nothing came of it. Strange, but the war, by some magical incident, passed over the building, although on the nearby avenue all the famous and historically important buildings were destroyed.

Since 1944, after the liberation of the city, a children's health camp was founded in the building. In 1952, the building was completely reconstructed; the 9th block building of the Vostok health resort was founded in it. And until the 60s of the twentieth century, the estate of Stamboli was the border of the city of Feodosia. Later, there was a drug rehabilitation center named after A.R. Dovzhenko. The changes that took place in the early 90s of the 20th century also affected this memorable monument. For some time, a commercial bank was located on the territory of this historical monument. In the future, a place here was taken by a cafe with the same name as the estate, which allows it to be quite popular.
