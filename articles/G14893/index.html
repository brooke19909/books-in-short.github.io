The Naturals Resort (Patong, Phuket, Thailand): review, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Localization

The Naturals Resort 3 * Resort is located in the center of Patong, on the island of Phuket, within walking distance from Patong Beach (5-7 minutes walk).



The distance from the sea is 200-1000 meters. The Naturals Resort 3 * hotel is a beachfront hotel located on the 2nd line. The institution was built on the territory of the Baantosai Garden Resort. The islands of Phi Phi, popular and popular with tourists, can be reached by boat in 2-2.5 hours.

Taxi rates to the hotel - from 2 thousand rubles, approximate travel time - 45-60 minutes.

Description of rooms

The total number of rooms is 214. The rooms are located in the bungalow or in 2- and 3-storey buildings. Super deluxe rooms (superior and overlooking the pool, the hotel grounds) are especially popular with visitors from Russia.



All rooms are spacious, comfortable. The rooms are equipped with comfortable new and comfortable furniture. All rooms have a balcony.

Each room has a bathroom with shower and all necessary amenities. Visitors are provided with 2 bottles of water per day for free.

Reviews

Hotel The Natural Resort (Phuket) has mostly positive reviews, of which we can highlight the following advantages and disadvantages.

The main plus is that the institution is located at some distance from busy streets, open discos and bars. This provides peace and quiet in the hotel itself, which gives residents the opportunity to enjoy their vacation. Walking access to many places of entertainment is also noted by residents as a big plus.



Visitors note the friendliness of the staff, the cleanliness of the hotel and the quality of housekeeping. There are no nutritional claims.
