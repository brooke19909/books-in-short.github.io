Where to go on vacation? Dzhanhot: recreation centers, living conditions, services

In the Krasnodar Territory there is a unique corner, where not only a full-fledged, but also useful rest will be - Janhot. The recreation facilities of this farm are located in an ecologically clean area. The air here is filled with essential oils of Pitsunda pine, which makes it healthy for the body. This farm is located near the famous resort of Gelendzhik. The prevailing mountainous terrain. Dzhanhot gained popularity relatively recently, but his fame is growing rapidly.

The Black Sea coast and pine forest - this is what the rest here is. Dzhanhot (recreation centers have an ideal ratio of quality and price) is a paradise where everyone can find the most suitable option for themselves. On the farm you can not only get close to the unique nature, but also have fun. After all, this is a settlement in which there are shops, cafes, restaurants and other institutions.

Arriving in these places, you can do spearfishing and fishing. The fauna is diverse, and the seabed will amaze with its beauty. Those who are going to come to this farm for the first time need to familiarize themselves with some recreation facilities.

"Ocean"

Considering that Dzhanhot is located near the open sea, the recreation center "Ocean" is only 500 meters away from the coast. It is located on the slope of the gorge, surrounded by a pine forest. Wooden houses and cottages are provided for your stay. All of them are double. Equipped with a fridge, beds, TV, wardrobe. The cottage also has a bathroom, a balcony and a safe.

To entertain guests, the administration equipped a playground and a sports ground. There is also table tennis. On the territory there are gazebos with a TV. For the preparation of barbecue there are special mangal zones. Tourists who come in a private car are provided with secure parking.



"Grace"

To tell you a good place where rest will be calm and full? Janhot! Recreation facilities located on the Black Sea coast offer many additional services that allow tourists to feel at ease and at ease. Just 10 minutes from the shore is the Grace complex. It occupies a large territory on which 4 buildings are built. They can rent standard, standard-studio, junior suite, and deluxe rooms. Each room has a refrigerator, TV, air conditioning, furniture, a bathroom and a shower with hot water. The deluxe rooms have a balcony and terrace, equipped with rattan furniture.

For guests, the administration has provided three meals a day on the basis of a buffet. On the territory there is a swimming pool, a gazebo with barbecue, a playground for children, sports equipment. Pebble beach, equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas, there are water attractions.



The recreation center "Zokio", Janhot

Between the two mountains in the gorge was built a recreation center, which was called "Zokio". Unique relict pines and many other green spaces grow on its territory. Accommodation is available in single-storey summer-type houses with a capacity of 2 to 4 people. Tourists with a private car can use the paid car park. There is a comprehensive nutrition. Those who wish to save money can cook on their own. The shared kitchen is equipped with all necessary appliances. On the territory there are mangal zones and a playground.

The property offers ironing and laundry services. The entire territory of the base is under round-the-clock video surveillance. For those who enjoy spending time in culture, excursions to local attractions are organized. You can also ride horses, visit the water park and the dolphinarium. According to most tourists, the rest at Zokio is excellent, but many did not like the beach. There are large and sharp stones on it, and there is not even the slightest hint of arrangement.
