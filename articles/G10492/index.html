How to make paper nails? Several variants

Nails are of different lengths, varnished, of different shapes, but they are real. Many girls love to needlework. Working process

The following is a description of how to make paper nails:

  1. A sheet is taken.
  2. On the left corner it folds diagonally.
  3. Then the same thing needs to be done on the right side.
  4. Once again, from the same angle, a sheet is folded diagonally.
  5. From the right side, the figure bends in half and begins to resemble a house.
  6. The angle from the bottom to the left is bent.
  7. The same thing is done on the right.
  8. The result is a rhombus that needs to be folded in half.
  9. Now the task is to give the nail a shape, for which a 30 ¬∞ angle is bent in a circle. This is done 3 times in a row.
  10. The last corner is wrapped and inserted inward, fixed.
  11. The nail is given a voluminous appearance and the pocket for the desired finger is already made directly.

Another way

Consider another option on how to make paper nails. For girls, this will be a very exciting activity. To make false nails you may need:

- multi-colored paper;

- pencils;

- manicure set;

- simple scissors;

- nail polish, preferably transparent.

Stages

Using a manicure set, finger nails are prepared: they are trimmed, filed, the remains of the old varnish, if any, are removed. Then, on a paper of good quality, each individual nail is drawn, even with a margin in size, so that later it would not be offensive if it turns out less than necessary. Drawn layouts are very neatly cut. Finger nails are coated with a clear varnish. If necessary, it can then be easily removed. The long-awaited moment has come: prepared paper nails, one by one, are superimposed on the prepared girl's hands. They must be held for 15 seconds, pressing them to the surface of the fingers. Here's how to make paper nails. For girls, this is not only an exciting activity, but also a matter of pride. You can show your work to a friend or mom.
