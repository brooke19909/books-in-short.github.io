The recreation center "Blue Bay". Kuchugury, Sea of ​​Azov

There is a rural settlement in the Krasnodar Territory with the interesting name Kuchugury. It has a very mild climate with warm and dry summers (average temperature in July +30 ° C), almost dry autumn (rains are rare guests), warm winters (average temperature in January -5 ...- 3 ° C, but there are frosts down to -20 ° C) and warm in spring. It is believed that this place has 280 warm days a year, and this is more than in Anapa!





Mud baths

Due to its favorable location, many tourists choose Kuchugury for their holidays. The recreation center "Blue Bay", which is located in this village, has everything you need for a full vacation both with friends and family. Clean sandy beaches, warm sea (sometimes the water temperature reaches +35 ° C) and plenty of entertainment - everything becomes available at the Blue Bay Hotel. Kuchugury will delight its guests with local attractions, among which there is even a volcano with blue clay, where you can take therapeutic mud baths.





Is it worth talking about the benefits of such procedures? No other product purchased in the store can be compared in healing properties with mud baths and their versatile effect on the body. Muds have a rich composition, which includes various salts, minerals, carbohydrates, acids and nitrogenous substances.





While taking a mud bath, hemoglobin rises, the body is cleansed of toxins, metabolic processes and the nervous system are normalized. The work of the heart and digestive system is also stabilized. That could be a vacation in Kuchugury! "Blue Bay" also prepared a lot of pleasant things for its guests.





More about this place

Kuchugury is a quiet and peaceful place. As accommodation, guests are provided with small cozy cottages, which have all the basic amenities. The hotel includes a large courtyard with a dining room. Not far from it there is a new playground with some attractions, and a sports ground for those who prefer to keep in shape even on vacation.





Evening "Blue Bay"

In the evening, the other side of the Blue Bay Hotel will open to the eyes of guests. Kuchugurs with the onset of darkness gain a warm, romantic atmosphere, and this recreation center is no exception.

In a wooden gazebo located in the courtyard of the base, you can relax and spend time in a warm company of friends over a glass of cold live beer. A stone barbecue is also equipped here, in which you can cook a real barbecue and some other appetizer.

For those who prefer a more active, eventful evening, there are some clubs and discos located near the Blue Bay base. Kuchugurs are rich in such establishments, and on the embankment it will be quite easy to find them.



Among other things, lovers of cognitive relaxation will draw a lot of interesting things for themselves here. After all, the history of these places is much more interesting than it seems at first glance. Having ordered a tour of this village, everyone will be able to find out its details. And guests can leave the car in a free guarded parking on site.

Fresh air, a lot of entertainment, the hot sand of a clean beach, picturesque landscapes and the warm Sea of ​​Azov - all this can be enjoyed at the Blue Bay, Kuchugury.
