How to get to the hotel cheaply by public transport

Theoretically, you can travel from Dabolim to Calangute by train. 5 km from the village is the city of Vasco da Gama, where there is a large train. D. Station. From there you need to follow to the city of Tivim, and then take a taxi to the hotel. In general, the road will cost up to 300 rupees. But trains in India for a European are quite extreme and not the most suitable mode of transport, as they are always crowded and late. Tickets for them must be ordered in advance, and often they are sold to several passengers in one seat.

You can get from Dabolim to Sea Breeze Inn 3 * and by bus. At 80 meters from the airport, buses stop right across the road for 10 rupees taking to the bus station in Vasco da Gamma, where you need to get a ticket to Panaji, and then transfer to the bus to Calangute. If you decide to visit North Goa during the low season, you will have to fly to Delhi, and preferably to Mumbai. From there it is most advantageous to take a plane ticket to Dabolim (price from $ 148), and then follow the scheme described above.

Buses and trains from Delhi and Mumbai to Goa also run, but travel time with all transfers takes almost the whole day, and the cost savings are very small.
