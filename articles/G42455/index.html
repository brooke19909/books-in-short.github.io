Construction technology

Let's dwell on this aspect in more detail. All objects in the LCD "Mayakovsky" (reviews of real customers you can study at the end of the article) are built in accordance with modern standards and requirements. The frame is a steel structure with aerated concrete walls, erected by monolithic technology. Partitions are not provided, and pylons that are installed in certain places are responsible for increasing the rigidity and strength of the entire structure.

The outside of the building is a ventilated facade that prevents moisture accumulation and extends the life of building materials. The buildings are sheathed with ceramic tiles, which are highly resistant to negative environmental factors, as well as providing good thermal insulation. The roof of the buildings is flat. It provides a drainage system.

The ventilation system in buildings is forced. It consists of shafts running along the entire height of the building parallel to the elevators, and the air circulates thanks to electric pumps. In order for residents to install domestic air conditioners in their apartments, an additional ventilation shaft is provided.
