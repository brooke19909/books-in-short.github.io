Hotel Marriott (Novosibirsk): address, phone, reviews, photo

Novosibirsk is the third largest Russian metropolis. One of the best zoos in the country, the longest avenue in the world, an avenue of bards and unusual monuments - travelers come to the Siberian capital for new experiences.

The real symbol of Novosibirsk is the Opera and Ballet Theater. Legends say that a whole city was built under the building, where during the Great Patriotic War exhibits from the Hermitage and the Tretyakov Gallery were stored.



By coincidence, just opposite the largest opera house in Russia is the five-star Marriott Hotel (Novosibirsk), which represents the world-famous Marriott chain.

Establishment of a business empire

The name of the Marriott Corporation today is associated with luxury and wealth. It is unlikely that in 1927, John Willard Marriott could imagine that his modest bar of non-alcoholic herbal drinks would become the founder of the business empire.

By investing all his money and energy in small catering establishments, John Marriott managed to reach a new level and become a supplier for three airlines. The first Marriott Hotel was opened in 1957 in Virginia. Seven years later, the founder‚Äôs son was appointed president of the company, and his restructuring benefited the business.

The following years were marked by rapid development. In addition to opening new hotels, John Marriott Jr. tried to earn income from providing various services to guests.

Marriott Corporation has 4,000 hotels in 80 countries. The first five-star hotel in Russia was opened in 1997. Today, travelers can see the high-quality Marriott service in Moscow, Kazan, St. Petersburg, Novosibirsk, Irkutsk and Krasnaya Polyana.

In the heart of the Siberian capital

The Marriott Hotel (Novosibirsk) is located in the heart of the city. The main attractions, administrative buildings and shopping and entertainment are within walking distance.



The Marriott Hotel (Novosibirsk) is ready to offer a magnificent view of the Opera and Ballet Theater. His address: Ordzhonikidze street, 31. The complex has several bars and a restaurant, a fitness center, SPA-salon, as well as modern venues for various events.
