Jadore Deluxe Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Side): description, service, reviews

Side is one of the most popular seaside resorts in the Turkish Mediterranean. This small town is located near two larger tourist destinations - Alanya and Antalya. On both sides of Side stretched strips of magnificent beaches and hotels.

One of the most popular accommodation options among holidaymakers is Jadore Deluxe Hotel & Spa 5 *.

Getting to know the resort: how the climate in Side differs

This ancient Greek city (now a popular resort) is located in the southwest of the Mediterranean coast of Antalya. The climate here is typical Mediterranean, characterized by dry, hot summers and mild, cool winters. In winter, the average temperature does not fall below + 10 ¬∞ C. In summer, the maximum air temperature reaches + 45 ¬∞ C. The time from May to October is considered to be the most comfortable for relaxing in Side, since during this period there is calm, hot, cloudless weather with the least amount of rainfall.

In this part of Turkey, Mediterranean culinary traditions have especially influenced national cuisine: local cafes and restaurants prepare many unusual seafood and fish dishes. Beach holidays here are considered the main area of ‚Äã‚Äãleisure activities. However, Side is also extremely attractive for fans of sightseeing tourism, due to the presence of a large number of ruins of ancient structures. The resort stretches for 20 km along the coast, in the middle of which is a small cape, which is the historical center of the ancient city. This place contains many evidence of the presence of the ancient Greeks. Amphitheaters, temples, a bathhouse, a market square, ruins of ancient walls, etc. are located here. Those who wish to combine a beach holiday with a sightseeing are advised to settle somewhere nearby.

Selection of Hotels in Side

To the west of the resort center lies an area that is especially popular with tourists. It is distinguished by the presence of clean and beautiful, but during the high season, very crowded beaches. There are many hotels and hotel complexes. The cost of housing in double rooms of hotels on the first coastline ranges from 300 to 1000 TRY (from 4844 to 16148 rubles). Solitary vacationers usually choose hotel accommodations east of the center of the peninsula. Accommodation there costs from 200 to 700 TRY (from 3229 to 11 303 rubles) for one night. The beaches in the area are less busy. There are also no problems with excursions, since it is not difficult to get to the historical center from here. Those wishing to save money, experts recommend settling closer to the historical center. In 5-15 minutes. walking from the coast, you can pick up housing for 70-120 TRY (approximately 1130 - 1937 rubles) per day.
