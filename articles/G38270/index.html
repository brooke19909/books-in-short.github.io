Recreation center "Bottlenose dolphin", Anapa: photos, reviews

The time has long passed when domestic tourists preferred overseas vacations to their native Black Sea. Great hotels, excellent service and a huge amount of entertainment can be found in the territory of his native country. What is the base of rest "Bottlenose dolphin". Anapa is a real paradise. It is no coincidence that people come here not only from the neighboring countries, but also from other continents.

Why choose Anapa?

This is not just a resort town. Here you can not only have a great time on the beach, but also see many sights, improve your health, gain strength before the new working year. A summer vacation trip to Anapa has become a good tradition for domestic families. After all, local beaches are great for young children. The coast is quite shallow and warm.



It is even better to relax if the place to stay is correctly selected. Excellent conditions are offered by the Afalina recreation center (Anapa). Well-developed infrastructure, comfortable apartments, delicious food - what else is needed? Anapa offers a huge range of entertainment for tourists of various ages. Nightlife is for young people, vibrant attractions are for kids and extreme lovers.

Rooms and prices at the Afalina complex

The recreation center (Anapa) offers various living conditions. The least expensive will be a double room "standard". In July and August, you will have to pay 2300 rubles for a daily rental, and in 1800, 1800 in May, June and September. The apartment will have two beds, a toilet, a table and several chairs. For more comfortable conditions you will have to pay 2900 rubles per day. This will be a double room "Euro standard". There will already be a refrigerator, a balcony with sea views, an armchair. The most expensive will be a room in a Finnish house, the category of "comfort". For rent at the height of the season you will have to pay 4000 rubles, in May, June and September - 3100 rubles.



Additionally, it is possible to install a berth in the room. The cost of the service is 700 rubles per day. Children under 3 years old are paid at a rate of 200 rubles per day. A child under 10 years old - 400 rubles per day.

Check-in is carried out daily at 12:00 if you have a ticket (reservation confirmation), passport (you can travel abroad) and a birth certificate for children under 14 years of age.

How can I book a room?

The recreation center "Afalina" (Anapa) has an official site. This greatly facilitates the selection and subsequent booking of apartments. All you need to do is go to the official resource and select a number that fully meets all the requirements. The site has a special form, filling in which you can send a reservation request. It is advisable to do this in advance. Usually in April experienced tourists already begin to look for rooms for July-August. Next you should expect a call. The manager calls back and clarifies the reservation information.

The necessary information on the application can be found by calling +7 (86133) 3 08 98. By contacting by phone, you can also book apartments for a specific date without a preliminary application.

Beach vacation

The coastal zone deserves special attention. Has its own beach recreation center "Afalina" (Anapa). The photo of the promenade is really impressive. Pools

The sea is a natural element that does not succumb to the desire of people. It often happens that the weather is perfect for relaxing on the beach, but huge waves do not allow you to come close to the promenade. In these conditions, there can be no question of swimming. Only desperate people enter the sea water. Has two wonderful pools recreation center "Afalina" (Anapa). Reviews show that here you can also have a great time when there is no desire or opportunity to go to the beach.



Pools vary in size and depth. The first is more suitable for adult vacationers. Here you can swim and dive. The second pool is designed for small tourists. The depth here is increasing gradually. However, the staff of the complex is not responsible for children. In water, babies should always be supervised by their parents. If you do not have your own beach equipment (vests and rubber rings), all this can be rented.

The area around the pool is well equipped. Sun loungers, umbrellas - all this is provided to vacationers for free. Both pools are equipped with a modern water treatment system. The temperature is comfortable enough to spend a long time at your favorite pastime - swimming.
