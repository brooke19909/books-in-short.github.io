Water park "Lava" (Yarovoye) - one of the largest in Siberia!

Waterpark "Lava" (Yarovoye) is the largest of its kind similar institution beyond the Urals. Since it is located in the open air, it works exclusively in the summer, starting from June and ending with the end of August. Big Yarovoye Lake is the location of this water park. There are always a lot of vacationers. Among them are both children and adults.

The main attractions





In the water park "Lava" there are 7 slides of various heights. Those who do not want to ride can enjoy the cool water in the pools. If desired, you can visit the sauna, bath.

The Black Hole is a slide 9 meters high. At the same time, a person will go down for 26 meters. The funnel attraction ends. Accordingly, having rolled down, a person spins in a spiral and enters the pool 1.8 meters deep.

Closed 12-meter slide - one of the highest attractions of the water park. In a closed pipe, the visitor descends for 80 meters. The slide ends with a special pool for splashdown.

Closed 9-meter slide is the longest here. The length of the descent is about 100 meters. At the same time, at the finish, a person develops a speed of up to 50 km / h.

An open 9-meter slide is the easiest option, ideal for beginners. There are almost no turns. And the length of the descent is 30 meters.

The wave pool is a unique construction of the Lava water park. It is capable of generating 2 meter waves. They occur every hour. The duration of such a wave attraction is 15 minutes. The rest of the time this is a regular pool.

There is a waterfall fungus in every water park. Lava is no exception.

A children's pool with a depth of 0.3 to 0.8 meters is a great place for kids to play, but under the mandatory supervision of parents.

Kamikaze is the highest 14-meter slide. She is a favorite and most popular among visitors. At the finish, a person develops a speed of up to 90 km / h.

There is a free swimming pool measuring 10x20 meters.

And finally, a three-armed slide 12 meters high. Only vacationers who have reached 16 years of age with a weight of not more than 120 kg can go down it.

Everything for the convenience of vacationers!





Water park "Lava" (Yarovoye) specifically for the convenience of its visitors offers:

  • non-cash payment system: you can pay for services not only in cash, but also with a card;
  • a left-luggage office, where you can safely leave your things while you are at the water park;
  • a souvenir shop with the opportunity to purchase products with the logo of the water park, as well as buy trinkets in memory of the resort;
  • sun loungers, mattresses, chaise lounges, umbrellas, rocking chairs, rugs - all this may be necessary for a long rest in the water park;
  • cafe, pizzeria, bar - food places are especially relevant for those who plan to stay here for the whole day.

In the evening, the Lava water park (Yarovoye) turns into karaoke, where there is all the necessary professional equipment. Therefore, the fun can continue until the morning.

A foam party

Every year, the water park "Lava" (Spring) holds a night foam party. It is remembered by each visitor for a long time. No wonder many vacationers come here just for her. A lot of foam, moonlight, splashes of water, a wave pool, fiery music - all this is filled with the event.

Of course, the main thing during this party is the invited stars. Not a single holiday like this can be done without professionals. The Beasts, Virus and many others have already managed to visit here.

Price policy





Waterpark "Lava" prices are low. A visit costs, on average, about 250-450 rubles per hour. For children, the price is lower than for adults. Special subscriptions are valid for 3, 4, 6 hours, as well as for the whole day (12 hours).

It is worth noting that at the beginning of June and the end of August the prices for visiting the Lava water park are lower than at the very height of the summer season, when a lot of tourists come to the Big Yarovoye lake.
