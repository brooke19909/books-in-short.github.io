Hotel "Tourist", Barnaul: photos and reviews of tourists

Are you going to Barnaul and want to remember the trip for a long time? Tired of boring gray walls and similar hotels? Then the hotel "Tourist" (Barnaul) was created so that you stay in it! Why this hotel deserves special attention, read below.





Short hotel description

Hotel "Tourist" (Barnaul) is a unique place. The hotel, located on the upper floors of a sixteen-story office building, where each room has its own name and its corresponding interior, and the hostel is dedicated to a yellow submarine, will definitely not leave anyone indifferent.

In addition to the unusual interior and comfortable rooms, the Tourist Hotel (Barnaul), the photo of it you see below, attracts with its convenient location. The fact is that in the shopping and office center in the building where it is located, there is everything so that you can’t get bored - from an ATM and a massage parlor to a supermarket and a solarium. And in order to get into them, you just need to go down several floors.





Who can stay here? The hotel management carefully thought out this question, and now the answer to it can be so - everyone. If you are a student with a limited amount of finance, then an ideal hostel for you would be a hostel that will delight you with comfort and design; in case of a business trip, it will be convenient for you to stay in studios; Well, if you decide to relax in ideal conditions - the suite will open its beautiful doors for you. Already packing up your suitcase? Wait, first find out more about the other benefits of the hotel.

Hotel "Tourist" (Barnaul) - address and location

The hotel is in the very center of the city. Therefore, a good location is another plus of such a place as the Tourist Hotel (Barnaul). Its address is 72, Krasnoarmeyskiy Avenue. There is a bus stop very close by, there are many grocery stores in the same building.

The hotel stands in the center of the traffic intersection, which means that you can get to any of Barnaul’s sights in less than ten minutes. In close proximity to the hotel are the Shukshin Theater, Musical Comedy Theater, Holy Protection Cathedral, Museum of Local Lore and the Railway Station.

Rooms

Hotel "Tourist" (Barnaul) provides rooms for its guests for every taste and budget. There are seven categories: single, double and triple standard, studio, apartments, suites and rooms in the hostel. The rooms of each variety have their own name and are exquisitely furnished. Experts worked a lot of time on their design and interior - it becomes noticeable immediately, as soon as you get into the room, everything is thought out and creative.

In addition, due to the location of the hotel on the fourteenth and fifteenth floors, the view from its windows offers simply stunning. A nice bonus will also be the fast wireless Internet available anywhere in the hotel.

Single

These are six types of one-room apartments with their own unique style under the names: “Pirates of the Caribbean”, “East”, “Mary”, “The Hobbits”, “A Man from the Capuchin Boulevard”, “Kin-dza-dza”.

All rooms include the following amenities: one bed of various sizes, TV, air conditioning, cable TV, telephone, desks of various types and chairs. There is also a small kitchen with the necessary minimum equipment and a toilet.

Double

There are seven types of rooms; they are also one-room and are called: “Mary”, “Revolutionary”, “Matrix”, “Frost”, “Khutorok”, “Diana”, “Military”. These rooms differ from single rooms only in that there are two beds instead of one, and in the Khutorok type there is one double bed. All other amenities are absolutely identical.





Triple

This is one type of room called Military. It differs from other rooms of the "standard" category in that it has three beds. There are also all the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay.

Studio

Includes three types of one-room numbers: "Rome", "Vine" and "Africa". The rooms differ in the number of beds: in the Rome studio there are two of them, the rest - one, as well as the types of amenities that are provided in the bathroom.

The most equipped is the bathroom in the Rome room, here you will find a bidet, shower and hairdryer, least of all - “Vine” - there is only a shower, and in “Africa”, besides it, there is also a bidet. The rooms are furnished in the same way as the Standard rooms.

Apartments

You can choose one of two types: "English" and "modern", both are two rooms and a separate bathroom.

The apartments differ in style and decoration, and insignificantly - in equipping the rooms: in "English" there is an electric fireplace, two TVs and a round table, in "modern" you will find a coffee table and one TV. In addition, in both types there is a double bed, air conditioning, wardrobes, upholstered furniture, telephone, chairs, cable TV.

In the bathrooms there is a shower, hairdryer, bidet. And to make you feel at home, a cozy terry bathrobe and slippers are provided, as well as hygiene products.





Suite

These chic two-room rooms are presented in two types: "Classic" and "Gothic". In terms of amenities, they differ only in the fact that in the bathroom in the "Classic" you will see a jacuzzi, and in the "Gothic" - a shower.

Also in each room there is air conditioning, a bed, upholstered furniture, a wardrobe, a TV, a telephone, cable TV, an electric fireplace, a table and chairs. Equipped with a small kitchen, which has a microwave, minibar, dishes, kettle and water filter.

Hostel "Yellow Submarine"

It was built six years ago and is one of the hotel’s business cards, because it got its name in honor of the Beatles song, as well as the children's cartoon. Despite the very pleasant cost of living, it has a fairly high level of comfort.

There are nine one-room rooms, which are made in the form of a submarine. Each room has two beds, air conditioning, TV, cable TV and a table. Cutlery, tea and coffee are provided. The bathroom is located separately, there are also latches and a galley.

Nutrition

The price of the ticket may include breakfast from the restaurant at the hotel, but please note that food will be brought directly to your room. For lunch and dinner, you can also in the restaurant at the hotel, but for a fee, or order groceries in the apartment. The menu consists of European and Russian cuisines.





It is also worth noting that all rooms are equipped with their own kitchen, and the hostel is shared, so preparing food directly in the room will not be difficult. Moreover, there are grocery stores just steps away. You can also go to restaurants, which are located in the vicinity of the hotel, if you want to taste local dishes.

Hotel facilities

Since the hotel is located in a building with a large number of organizations, guests can use their services, the Tourist Hotel itself (Barnaul) provides only the most necessary services. It:

  • Transfer to anywhere in the city - for a fee.
  • Transfer to and from the airport is also chargeable.
  • Luggage storage for your luggage.
  • Room service from the restaurant.
  • Special number of category "Suite" for newlyweds.
  • Free parking.
  • Ironing clothes. You can also ask for an iron at the reception if necessary.
  • Laundry.

Terms of accommodation

The hotel has pan-European rules for check-in and check-out: check-in is at 14:00, check-out is at 12:00. If you need an earlier check-in or late check-out, you must coordinate this with the hotel in advance. A surcharge for these services is likely to be required.

To book a room, you must pay the full price of the stay on the hotel’s official website or intermediary sites using a credit card or bank transfer. Please note that cancellation of the reservation is carried out without penalty only if at least three days remain before the planned arrival date. Otherwise, the hotel will deduct the cost of living for one night from the account. Refunds will be made to a credit card or bank account, depending on the payment method.

Hotel "Tourist" (Barnaul) does not allow pets.

Children under the age of one year are accommodated free of charge, provided that they do not request an extra bed. Cots are not available at the hotel.

Extra beds must be agreed upon upon booking, otherwise they are not provided.

Hotel "Tourist" (Barnaul) - organizations located in the same building with her

As mentioned above, the hotel has a great location - many companies work with it in the neighborhood. Here you can use the services of ATMs, a dental clinic, fax, photocopy and printout, hairdresser, massage parlor, solarium, sauna, pool, gym, library, three cafes, banquet and conference rooms, a grocery supermarket, billiards and a parking lot.





There are also other institutions located in the same house as the Turist Hotel (Barnaul): offices of auditors, designers and lawyers, travel agencies, insurance agents, ateliers, air and multicass offices, a jewelry workshop, a printing house, and a nail studio .

Jobs

The hotel is a very popular destination among workers in the tourism sector. Therefore, most often in an institution such as the Tourist Hotel (Barnaul), vacancies do not exist. And if you still really want to join the friendly team of hotel employees, you will have to be patient and wait for the opportunity, until some place is vacated.





Reviews

Hotel "Tourist" (Barnaul) collects reviews from guests, for the most part, positive. The people who stayed at the hotel were very pleased. But there are those who did not really like staying here. Let's start with the pros:

  • Attentive, helpful and polite staff. Even when checking in at night, I am ready to offer my services.
  • Excellent location - in the very center of the city, so you can quickly get to any objects.
  • An interesting and unusual design of rooms that is found in hotels of this level is extremely rare.
  • The rooms are spacious and bright, with a good repair.
  • The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay - from slippers to a minibar;
  • Beautiful view from the window, the city, at a glance.
  • Large and beautiful bathrooms in the rooms.
  • Cleaning is done every day, so the rooms are always clean.
  • The system of showers, bathrooms and kitchens is carefully thought out.
  • With a high level of service and room comfort - affordable price.
  • Spacious secure parking for which you do not need to pay extra.
  • Conveniently, the building has many organizations providing a wide range of services - no need to travel far.

Hotel "Tourist" (Barnaul) has its negative sides. Here's how the guests described them:

  • Poor insulation.
  • Bad smell of sewage from the toilet.
  • Lack of curtains on the windows.
  • The inconvenient location of the smoking area is on the 16th floor, which is very difficult to find and then come back to your room.
  • Breakfast is provided only to guests from rooms above economy class.
  • The beds in the rooms are old.
  • If you live in a hotel for a long time, towels and linen change much less frequently.
  • Air conditioning is not working well.

So, if you plan to visit the capital of the Altai Territory - the city of Barnaul, it will be difficult to find a more suitable place than the Tourist Hotel. You will remember the atmosphere of her rooms for a long time, because they have practically no analogues among other hotels in Russia . Well, a pleasant service, a wide range of services in organizations working nearby, a stunning view from the window and a good location will complement the picture.
