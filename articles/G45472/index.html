Olhuveli Beach & Spa Resort 4 *

The luxury SPA hotel is located at the very end of the archipelago - on the southern atoll Male. It features a white sandy beach and two infinity pools. The hotel complex itself is built on the territory of a beautiful tropical garden, in which there are 4 restaurants, 3 bars and a scuba diving school with all the necessary certificates.

It offers spacious air-conditioned apartments with wooden furniture. Each villa is equipped with a TV (cable TV), minibar and DVD player. Some have an outdoor hot tub with ocean views and a private terrace for sunbathing.

Olhuveli Beach is an hour boat ride from the capital's international airport.

Sun Spa offers all types of relaxing body massages, beauty treatments and flower baths. The staff speaks English, Chinese and Russian.

Sunset Restaurant serves a buffet. Lagoon serves Western food. For Asian delicacies, head to Four Spices and light snacks at Island Pizza.

The cost of a weekly tour (2 adults) all-inclusive to the Maldives at the 4 * Olhuveli Beach Hotel in the summer is about 157,000 rubles. Reservation is confirmed within 24 hours.
