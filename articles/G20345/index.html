Iphone constantly reboots: what to do?

It was in the hands of a brand new and stylish iPhone, which is so much talked about on television. At first, everything is fine, and the phone restarts only at the behest of its owner. But after a while, the unexpected can happen, and now the iPhone is constantly rebooting and does not allow you to perform a single operation. When this happens twice or thrice, there is nothing to worry about. But if multiple reboots are a reason for excitement.





Typically, such gadgets are easily replaced in official Apple stores. But what if the warranty has already expired? What if the iPhone constantly restarts, what should I do in this situation? First of all, do not panic. In most cases, this problem is solved without the use of specialized equipment and does not require any fantastic knowledge of electronics from the phone owner.

In fairness, it is worth noting that the problems associated with the fact that the iPhone is constantly restarting began after the new version of iOS. Most users who installed iOS 11 / 11.1.2 firmware noted such "quirks." However, the problems affected not only outdated phones. Many noted that the iPhone 5S, 6, 7 and 8 are constantly restarting by itself. Thus, if this version of the operating system is installed on the phone, then the firmware error is most likely.

It is quite possible that there are problems in the device itself if a short circuit occurs. However, most often the malfunction lies precisely in the software part. Accordingly, you can try to fix the damage at home on your own. However, before performing certain manipulations, it is worthwhile to carry out preparatory work.
