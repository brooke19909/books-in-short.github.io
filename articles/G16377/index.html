Hotel "Corinthia", St. Petersburg: address, phone, description of rooms, infrastructure, service, reviews

The northern capital attracts tourists from all over the world all year round, so on the Internet you can easily trace the interest in the hotels of this city. They go to St. Petersburg not only for sightseeing, but also for solving various business issues. Seminars and forums are often organized in the cultural capital of Russia, where foreign participants also come. Therefore, the city has a high demand for modern hotel complexes located in the historical part of St. Petersburg. Hotel Corinthia (St. Petersburg) is a special complex combining a high level of service with original Russian hospitality. Many of its guests consider the hotel to be one of a kind, especially since it received an ECO certificate, which other hotels in the city did not receive. Therefore, if you value comfort, dream of living in the very center of the Northern capital, prefer to book rooms of at least deluxe category and are used to eating tasty and proper food even on a trip, then you definitely need to choose the Corinthia Hotel for your stay in St. Petersburg. Let's talk about it in more detail.

Description of the hotel complex

Hotel Corinthia (St. Petersburg) consists of two buildings, both of which have their own interesting history. The hotel complex itself was opened about twenty-five years ago. For this new luxury hotel, the masters reconstructed two old houses. Both of them were built in the first half of the nineteenth century and keep within their walls many secrets of other people's destinies.

The first building is the brainchild of the famous architect Arthur Lange and overlooks Nevsky Prospect. Hotel guests write that a similar view opens today from several rooms of the hotel and restaurant, where breakfast is served.

The second building was designed by Mikhail Lange for an acting family, whose members have played in theaters of the Northern capital for more than a century and a half. The owners of the hotel set up a small museum dedicated to this acting dynasty. Today it is part of a luxury hotel complex and its hallmark.

It is interesting that from the first quarter of the last century on the site of the Corinthia Hotel (St. Petersburg) there were several hotel complexes. Therefore, we can say that, staying here, you can feel the "heartbeat" of the ancient city.

The hotel complex has almost four hundred rooms. Moreover, the hallmark of the Corinthia Hotel in St. Petersburg is a wide selection of rooms. In addition to standard apartments, guests can check into deluxe rooms, which, in turn, are divided into different categories.

Guests of the hotel in their reviews always note with admiration its extraordinary design. The design is designed in a simple, yet luxurious style. Therefore, every step crossed the threshold of the hotel complex immediately falls into a special atmosphere, which cannot leave anyone indifferent.

Corinthia Hotel (St. Petersburg): how to get to the hotel complex

Guests usually use the subway in order to get to Corinthia. After all, the Mayakovskaya metro station is located very close to the hotel. From the reviews we can conclude that the hotel is located very convenient for exploring local attractions. Just three kilometers away is the Hermitage, and five - the Peter and Paul Fortress. You can get here, even just walking along the streets of the city.

Tourists should know that in addition to the subway (Mayakovskaya metro station), they can get to the hotel by taxi. The distance from the airport and train station to Corinthia is small. For example, Pulkovo Airport is only seventeen kilometers from the hotel. And Moskovsky Station is located very close to the hotel - at a distance of four hundred meters. Therefore, a taxi is quite convenient, fast and, what is nice, an inexpensive way to quickly check into a hotel complex.

Hotel Corinthia (St. Petersburg): infrastructure

The hotel complex is a typical city hotel. Therefore, it has no adjacent territories, and the entire infrastructure of Corinthia is concentrated in the buildings.

Even the most sophisticated and satiated tourists will be absolutely satisfied when they check into this hotel. After all, it has a lot of features that make it unique.

The pride of the hotel is its conference room. It simultaneously accommodates up to one thousand people and is considered to be the best in St. Petersburg. The assembly hall is a room with mobile partitions, which makes it suitable for any event. The hall itself is made in the Art Deco style and can please any connoisseur of architecture and art.

Not the last place in infrastructure is occupied by the museum of the Samoilov family. The example of one family tells the fascinating story of the formation and development of ballet and theatrical art in the city on the Neva.

In addition to the listed infrastructure facilities of the complex, it is equipped with more than fifteen rooms for events of varying complexity. The hotel can organize a conference, banquet, corporate event, seminar, festival and many other events. Thus, the hospitality of the Corinthia Hotel (St. Petersburg) can be enjoyed not only by its guests, but also by people who came here for other purposes.

In the buildings of the hotel is a beauty salon. It offers a wide selection of treatments, including several types of massage. There is also a solarium.

In order to maintain good physical shape during the trip, a gym is equipped. Only the most modern simulators are installed in it, classes on which turn into a real pleasure.

Hotel guests can use covered and secure parking if desired. However, her services are paid separately and are not included in the price. A day of using a parking space will cost guests more than four thousand rubles.

At the reception, guests can leave their belongings in a left-luggage office. There is also a tour desk. If necessary, its employees organize an individual or group tour for guests of Corinthia.

A few words about the service

Even from the first minutes of communication with the staff at the reception, we can conclude that the high level of service they provide here. Judge for yourselves: employees speak six languages. It is worthy of the most luxurious European hotels.

Service at the Corinthia Hotel (St. Petersburg) allows you to turn even the most difficult work trip into a pleasant pastime. The maids are ready to bring the selected dishes and drinks to the rooms at any time of the day or night. Every day, each guest is put in the apartment a bottle of clean drinking water.

Upon request, a diet menu can be provided at the restaurant. A separate children's menu is also available at the hotel, however, dishes from it are not included in the total cost of breakfast.

If necessary, guests will be issued an accelerated check-out or check-in. Such a service is very much appreciated by business people and guests with small children.

Wi-Fi is excellent throughout the hotel, so guests will not have communication problems. This is very important for those who check into the hotel as part of a business trip.

If we talk about the service, it is important to note the ability to choose non-smoking rooms and hypoallergenic apartments. A pleasant bonus for guests will be a glass of soft drink during the paperwork at the reception.

Characteristics of paid services

In any luxury hotel complex there is a list of additional services. Hotel Corinthia (St. Petersburg) is no exception to the general rule. Due to such services, guests get the opportunity to make their stay in the complex even more comfortable.

For example, guests can still book a transfer to the hotel when booking a room. Also, with a small surcharge, the transport belonging to Corinthia will bring you to any specified point in the city.

If you need to look presentable every day, then laundry and dry cleaning will help. Thanks to these services, your image will be perfect, and business negotiations will be successful.

Corinthia provides services for honeymooners and couples in love. At the hotel they can spend time alone with each other, without sacrificing their comfort.

Short description of filling the apartments

In the hotel, the equipment of the rooms depends on their category, so we cannot describe everything. However, we give a general characteristic that allows us to conclude that they are comfortable.

In each room there is a plasma TV, showing, in addition to local channels, about twenty international. The apartments have individual or central air conditioning. The second is mainly mounted in standard rooms.

All apartments have a safe and minibar. In many rooms, in addition to a tea set, a capsule coffee machine is also installed. Extras include a hairdryer in the bathroom, slippers and bathrobes. Phones have direct access to international calls.

Some features of settlement and accommodation

Check-in is carried out at two in the afternoon, the guest must leave the occupied apartment before noon. After making a reservation, tourists can cancel their reservation, but each room category has its own rules.

Children up to twelve years of age can be accommodated in the hotel with the beds in the room, in which case an extra charge of one thousand rubles for breakfast is required. Children up to six years of age live and eat in the hotel complex for free.

Parents should be aware that extra beds for children are not available upon request.

The hotel provides registration services for guests of foreign origin. However, it is paid - about two hundred and fifty rubles.

If at the same time it is reserved from six rooms and more, then for such situations a special system of discounts has been worked out. Also, a similar category of guests provides additional services that are usually not included in the price.

The hotel administration reserves the right to request copies of documents of guests when booking rooms by phone.

How to find the Corinthia complex in the city on the Neva?

The address of the Corinthia Hotel in St. Petersburg is included in many directories, so it is easy to find. There is a hotel complex on Nevsky Prospekt in the house at number fifty seven. The area where Corinthia is located belongs to the central areas of the city.

By phone of the Corinthia Hotel (St. Petersburg) you can get a consultation around the clock.

Guest reviews

What do the guests write in their comments after the rest in Corinthia? Almost everyone is happy with their stay here and recommend the hotel for short business trips or weekend trips.

If we consider the disadvantages of the hotel, first of all, tourists include the cost of living here - it is extremely high. A day in a standard room will cost guests in the amount of eight thousand rubles.

Also, among the shortcomings in the hotel, guests of the complex emit noise in rooms on the seventh floor. It comes from an air conditioner located on the roof. If guests express their dissatisfaction with this fact, they will be moved to other apartments. Problems with this usually do not arise.

The downside is poor air conditioning. These complaints relate mainly to centralized air conditioning.

There are a lot of advantages of the hotel complex, noted in the reviews. Almost everyone notes the convenience of the location of Corinthia. By public transport, get to the hotel no more than an hour, which is considered the minimum time interval when it comes to a big city.

A pleasant surprise for many is the doorman at the entrance and a welcome drink. Most of the guests note that the indisputable advantage of the hotel is the ability to get almost all toiletries and other household items for free. This is done on an individual request.
