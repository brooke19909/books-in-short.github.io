Eternal Flame Memorial

And of course, as in many other parks dedicated to these events affecting every seven families, the eternal flame is his heart . The memorial is located near one of the entrances to the park.

It is surrounded by a pantheon, on the tablets of which the names of the inhabitants of Tatarstan, who gave their lives for their homeland, are depicted. Among them are 200 Heroes of the Soviet Union and 29 Knights of the Order of Glory. On the seven stela installed around the memorial, each of which is dedicated to one of the seven rifle divisions (they were created in Kazan), the names of the soldiers are immortalized. It is clear that the memorial is the main scene of the Victory Parades. All Kazan brings flowers here. Victory Park on this day is especially transformed. At the Eternal Flame, young residents of the capital of Tatarstan are on guard.

Place of worship

In order to form the necessary number of alleys, trees were planted in a certain way: it was originally planned that each of them would belong to a specific military branch. There is also an alley of mothers, widows, women in general, whose fates were cut short by the war.



But Victory Park is not only a place of sorrow and worship of the great feat of the Soviet people, the inhabitants of Tatarstan. Through the efforts of the authorities, it has become the favorite resting place of the townspeople, and this is the best memory of the victims.

Park ponds

There is always a lot of vacationers here, especially on Sundays. The park has a beautiful artificial lake. Citizens have a tradition of arranging radio-controlled ship competitions on its waters. And the tradition has taken root so well that in 2006 the Russian Championship for racing radio-controlled boats was held on this lake. Swimming in this lake is prohibited, but you can ride catamarans. There is another reservoir in the park, it is located in the north-eastern part. He is stocked and chosen by ducks.

Park restoration

The 70th anniversary of the Great Victory was very widely celebrated throughout the country. Almost a year in the capital of Tatarstan, restoration work was carried out in such a cult place as Victory Park (Kazan). The reconstruction was completed by May 9, 2015. She touched not only the pantheon, where new names of warriors appeared, who were previously considered missing.

The paving stones were replaced, the steps became granite, the slab under the Eternal Flame was made of solid granite. In addition, the Victory stela and the colonnades were faced with new travertine tiles. Work was carried out to improve the lighting of the park (in particular, a new highlight was installed on the stele), and completely military equipment was restored. Flower beds were added around the perimeter of the memorial. A beautiful English lawn appeared near the Avenue of Widows; now in a separate pavilion there is now an electronic book in memory of the victims. A lot has been done to the 70th anniversary of the Victory.
