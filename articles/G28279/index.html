Waterpark in Alushta. Description

One of the favorite vacation spots of many is the water park in Alushta. It is located on the waterfront (in the professor's corner). This wonderful water park opened in 2004. He immediately became very popular. Excellent landscape design is amazing and pleasing to the eye. Description

The water park in Alushta occupies a considerable territory (2 hectares) and accommodates 6 pools, 14 stunning water slides, several relaxation areas, numerous attractions, waterfalls, fountains, a jacuzzi, a river with a stream and even a VIP zone. All this will provide an unforgettable vacation and a lot of positive emotions. In addition, the territory of the water park has the following:

  • Safes for storing valuables.
  • Left-luggage offices.
  • Shower cabins.
  • Locker rooms.
  • Medical Center.
  • Toilets.

"Dolphin"

This bar is located on the open - air terrace with stunning views. Helpful and friendly staff, a cozy atmosphere and delicious food will make you return there again.

Wave pool

This pool is famous for the water park in Alushta. It perfectly alternates the sea breeze (imitation) and traveling sea waves. A 10-meter waterfall flows into a two-meter-deep pool. At intervals of 40 minutes, waves reaching 1 meter in height are fed.



"Python"

The attraction, consisting of 2 slides made in the form of a serpentine, a length of 133 meters. Height is 14 meters, depth - 1.1 meters. They descend from this unique slide on an inflatable circle, so even those who are afraid of heights ride this attraction, since the circle inspires confidence.

Gyurza

The peculiarity of this attraction is that when descending a serpentine hill (it is 82 meters long and reaches 9.5 meters in height), considerable speed develops. Thrill-seekers prefer the water park in Alushta precisely because the most vivid emotions can be experienced only on this hill.

"Green Island"

Under this attractive name is an entertaining and gaming pool with a waterfall and a fountain. Near the pool you can enjoy the cool shade on the sunbeds under the umbrellas.

"Boa"

Another attraction, consisting of two serpentine slides. Their length is 65 and 81 meters, respectively, depth - 1.2 meters, height - 9 meters.



"Anaconda"

This attraction consists of 5 high-speed slides and 3 high-speed tunnels (total length - 50 meters, height - 11.5 meters). Slides overlook the outdoor pool (depth - 1.1 meters). Alushta is famous for this attraction. Waterpark (photo) is presented in all its glory.

Children's swimming pool

It is specially adapted for the safe pastime of young and middle-aged children (up to 12 years old). Swimming areas are up to 50 centimeters deep. Waterfalls, slides and fountains will bring joy to your baby. Near the pool there is a zone for parents and a playground.

VIP zone

Separate cabin with sun loungers and umbrellas. Calm and secluded atmosphere allows you to relax and unwind.

When visiting this wonderful resort, you can choose as a place of residence "Water Park" (hotel). Alushta always attracts a large number of tourists and is very popular.
