Batteries ag13: description and specifications

The modern world can not be imagined without electronics, a variety of gadgets and devices. To power them, special cells or batteries are used when they fail and it is necessary to replace them. When buying batteries, the question arises of its labeling and the possibility of using analogues. Particularly difficult to find a battery in the form of tablets, such as, say, ag13 battery. It is worth considering the example of batteries.

Batteries ag13

This battery has a flattened appearance resembling a tablet. The lower part is distinguished by a slightly smaller diameter. This side is the negative pole. The upper part, having a slightly larger diameter, is the positive pole. It is marked on which, when installing the battery, you should be guided so as not to confuse the poles.

Specifications

The ag13 battery has the following features:

  1. The small size of this battery allows you to install it in various miniature devices and gadgets. The height is 5 mm and the diameter is 11.6 mm.
  2. Electricity, which gives the battery, is generated in it from an alkaline reaction. It follows that these batteries are alkaline.
  3. When buying a battery, it should be remembered that the voltage error here is 0.05 V. It absolutely does not affect the performance. It also cannot be called marriage. The rated voltage of the battery is 1.5 V.
  4. Due to their small size, the batteries do not have high power, but at the same time they have a current strength of 0.22 mA.
  5. The electrical capacity is 110 mA / h. This is worth remembering when installing in various devices. In powerful gadgets, the power consumption time will be small.
  6. The battery weighs 3 g.

Application

Often, manufacturers of various devices where it is necessary to use ag13 batteries indicate this in the operating instructions. Depending on the power of the electric gadget or device, the number of batteries varies from one to five.

The ag13 batteries are used to power various children's toys, where their miniature design is convenient. They are also installed in desktop, watches and wall clocks. They are used in laser pointers, as well as flashlights and calculators. The ag13 battery is used to power some low-power remote controls, as well as in electronic thermometers. They are also installed in pedometers and other similar gadgets.

Operational Features

When using the ag13 battery, some people have a question: is it possible to charge this battery. But manufacturers categorically forbid doing this. You can find many different ways to increase battery life on the Internet, but don't blindly believe this. Indeed, when heated, for example, it can simply explode, since the alkali inside will boil. It is also impossible to charge it (with the help of improvised chargers).

When using an ag13 battery, it is worth remembering that harmful substances are used to generate electricity, as well as various heavy metals, which can cause severe burns on the skin. It is not recommended to throw elements into the chute, there are special eco-receivers for disposal.

Experts note several important rules for using ag13 batteries:

  • it is forbidden to heat elements and keep them in the sun;
  • during the acquisition, you should pay attention to the expiration date: the fresher the battery, the longer it will last;
  • keep children and animals out of them;
  • Do not disassemble or deform the housing;
  • Never charge these batteries.
