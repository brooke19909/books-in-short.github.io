Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): tourist reviews

Russian tourists have recently discovered Vietnam. The mysterious Asian country was pushed aside by other giants of the tourism market: China and India. But gradually, tired of the usual countries, travelers switch to other resorts. One of them was Vietnam, whose tourism industry has risen to a first-class level in a few years. If you decide to visit this country, then pay attention to the new Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * hotel, located in a city such as Nha Trang. It will be a great option for those who want to explore the country without spending huge sums.

What are the benefits of a holiday in Vietnam?

Tourist complexes of this country can offer travelers excellent service at affordable prices. Vietnam is a beautiful country with a surprisingly hot climate, warm sea, well-groomed coast and a very ancient history. Tourists visiting this country will be able to see stunning temple complexes, national reserves with unspoiled nature and amazing flea market markets. In addition, in Vietnam, stunning national cuisine, which is famous for its rice and noodles dishes, as well as amazing delicacies. Here you can try, for example, fried frogs or even baked crocodile meat.

Perhaps the only drawback of the country that repels Russian tourists is a long flight. For example, to Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) from Moscow will have to fly for almost 14 hours, not counting the time spent on the transplant. But you can come to rest here in any month. Even in the winter months, the temperature does not drop below 24-26 degrees. In the summer months in Vietnam, there is an exhausting heat. The average temperature in July is 34 degrees. But the sea here is very warm, and your vacation will definitely not be spoiled by cloudy and rainy weather.

General information about the hotel

The hotel opened its doors to guests in 2014. On its territory is the latest 13-storey building, which houses 60 rooms. Available are small rooms for those who want to relax on a budget. There are entire family apartments in which a full-fledged family of four can live. The rooms overlook the streets of Nha Trang, as well as the sea. The staff can communicate in Russian or English.



Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * is considered a youth hotel, so children are not welcome here. Pets, including guide dogs, are also not allowed here. Check-in at the hotel starts no earlier than 14:00 Vietnamese time, and check-out must be done no later than 12:00. Upon request, the hotel can arrange a paid airport transfer for its guests. Upon arrival and registration at the reception you will be required to pay a deposit in cash or by credit card.

Hotel location and nearby places worth visiting

Golden Beach Hotel Nha Trang 3 * is located in the European quarter of Nha Trang. This is the second largest settlement in Vietnam, as well as the largest tourist center of the country. The hotel is very close to the public beach of the city. The distance to it is only 100 meters, and in time the journey will take only a couple of minutes. You can get here from Nha Trang Airport, which is located 26 kilometers from the city. It takes less than an hour to get to the hotel.





The hotel is within walking distance of major attractions of Nha Trang. Just 40 meters away is the Night Market, which opens at sunset. Here you can buy souvenirs, clothes and accessories. If you wish, you should also bargain with sellers, they can make a big discount. Three kilometers from the hotel is the Long Son Pagoda - the main Buddhist city center. It is believed that when you reach the top of the pagoda on foot, you can be cleansed of all your sins. Another important object that is worth visiting, staying at Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 *, is Bambu Island. It is 10 kilometers from the hotel.

What rooms can the hotel offer?

The 13-storey building of Golden Beach Nha Trang Bo 3 * Hotel has more than 50 comfortable apartments, ready to receive tourists in any month throughout the year. Standard rooms are designed for two people. Upon check-in, either two single beds or one double. The interior of the rooms is classic, designed in a single color scheme. The walls are dark beige and the furniture and decor are brown and orange. The floor in the room is wooden. Some apartments have a private small balcony.

In addition to the bed, from the furniture in the room you can find bedside tables, a coffee table, a sofa or chairs, wardrobes, a cosmetic table, a chair and a mirror. The rooms are decorated with paintings, and on the tables are fresh fruits or flowers in pots. Of the additional lighting in the rooms there are table lamps. If desired, the windows can be closed with blackout curtains. Sheets are decorated with the hotel logo.

Room features

The modern Golden Beach Hotel Nha Trang 3 * (Nha Trang) is committed to providing its guests with a first-class level of comfort. To do this, each room is equipped with a number of amenities that allow guests to feel at home. We list some of them:

  • Flat-screen TV with a wide diagonal, which hangs on the wall, not taking up space in the room. You can watch cable and Russian-language channels.
  • Electric kettle and complimentary tea or coffee making facilities. Thanks to them, guests can independently prepare these drinks at any time convenient for them.
  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi, located not only in the room, but throughout the hotel.
  • Each room has its own bathroom with shower. Free slippers and towels are provided. There is also a hairdryer, toiletries (liquid soap, shampoo).
  • The bedroom has autonomous air conditioning.
  • Paid safe in which visitors can leave things.
  • Paid mini-bar, the filling of which will also cost money.
  • Telephone.
  • Ironing board and iron are available upon request.
  • Room Service: daily housekeeping, regular change of linen and towels.




All about food: bars, restaurants, local cuisine

The price of accommodation at Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * Hotel (Vietnam) includes only daily breakfasts. They are served as a buffet in the main restaurant. Chefs prepare omelets and scrambled eggs for guests. Breakfast is not diverse, but you can always eat hearty. The menu also always contains vegetables (cucumbers, tomatoes), fruits, rice, sandwiches, noodles, dishes of national cuisine. For each person a small bottle of mineral water is given for a day.





In addition to the main restaurant, there are no more establishments in the complex. But nearby, in Nha Trang, tourists can visit small cafes specializing in national cuisine. A full meal here will cost quite inexpensively. In the European Quarter there is a chic Indian restaurant, which must be visited by all connoisseurs of Asian cuisine.

Can I stay here with a child?

Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * complex is considered a youth hotel, so there is not much entertainment for children. One child is allowed to stay for free if he is not older than 11 years old and if he does not occupy a separate bed. Also, hotel guests should be prepared for the fact that there is no cradle for babies. Do not hope that the baby can be left in a mini-club or given in the care of a nanny. In addition, there may be problems with the nutrition of the child. The hotel has the right not to give him a free breakfast if he lives for free.

Organization of leisure activities for children is not here, so guests will have to think about it on their own. Therefore, if you decide to choose this hotel for a family vacation with children, then it is unlikely to be a suitable option for you. It is better to stay in another complex that will focus on children's audiences.

Hotel infrastructure

At Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) they carefully monitor the quality of service, so the staff is committed to providing customers with all the necessary range of services. The hotel has a developed network of infrastructure facilities that guests can use at any time. We list the main services:

  • The hotel has a 24-hour reception and an administrator who is always ready to answer guests' questions.
  • At the reception for guests can arrange a paid excursion to any city sights.
  • The ability to exchange currencies in dollars, euros or Vietnamese dong at the most favorable rate.
  • Dry cleaning works, which for an additional fee can wash clothes, quickly dry them and stroke them.
  • For hotel guests, a business center provides their services.
  • Souvenir shop. For a small fee, guests can order in-room newspaper, food or drink delivery. Also, at the request of customers, they can be awakened by a phone call from the reception.
  • The hotel is equipped with a modern elevator.




Local entertainment

Golden Beach Nha Trang Boutique 3 * hotel does not have a large territory, so it can not offer its guests a wide selection of entertainment. On the thirteenth floor of the building there is a small outdoor pool in which guests can swim at any time. The hotel also provides bicycle rental, with which tourists can travel around the city. They can have a good time on the beach, which is located within walking distance of the hotel.





The hotel does not provide any other entertainment services. Therefore, guests themselves can go to the city to have a good rest. In half a kilometer from Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * there is a sailing club. A little further you can find a restaurant-brewery, city square, a large shopping center and city museum.

Positive hotel reviews

The opinions of tourists are a good way to find out the current state of affairs at the Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * hotel. Most of the reviews about him are positive, but they could not have done without significant shortcomings. Consider the advantages of the hotel, which were noted by guests staying here:

  • ideal location: the beach, shops, bars and restaurants are located very close;
  • spacious rooms with beds equipped with orthopedic mattresses, working fixtures and new renovations;
  • friendly staff who are fluent in English;
  • good housekeeping, regular linen change, no insects (ants) in the rooms;
  • high-quality and hearty breakfasts, regular replenishment of tea and coffee in the room.

Negative reviews and criticism

Most of the tourists who rested here, in general, liked the rest. They note that the quality of service corresponds to the price category of the hotel. However, there are those who were not satisfied with the rest. They note the following disadvantages:

  • a slow elevator that has to wait a few minutes;
  • they are constantly building something near the hotel, so the rooms are quite noisy in the morning and afternoon;
  • monotonous breakfasts, which are boring already for 2-3 days;
  • small beds with hard mattresses and uncomfortable pillows;
  • tourists are offered a view of the sea, but in fact it is absent: from a small window you can see only a small piece of the coast and the ventilation that hangs on the building;
  • the minibar is not replenished, although the staff was repeatedly reminded of this;
  • the hotel has only one Russian-speaking administrator who is not always present at the reception.




Cost of living

Golden Beach Nha Trang Boutique Hotel 3 * is considered a budget option for tourists visiting Vietnam. The cost of living will primarily depend on the time of year. The lowest room prices are in November and December, and by Catholic Christmas prices rise significantly. On average, the cheapest room in these months will cost 2500 rubles per night. By June and July, prices rise significantly: the minimum price rises to 4,000 rubles. Thus, a weekly stay for two in a standard room will cost guests 20-40 thousand rubles, depending on the season.

Should I choose this hotel?

Choosing this complex, it is worth considering that it is not designed for couples with children. But it will be a great option for those who plan their vacation on their own, so he needs a hotel only in order to sleep and gain strength for the next day. The location of the complex is perfect for exploring the city. Nearby is the beach, and attractions, and cafes, and shops.

Thus, this hotel is not suitable for those who want to relax at sea, being satisfied with the services provided by the host. But if you want to stay inexpensively in a modern hotel with high quality service, then Golden Beach Nha Trang 3 * complex will be an ideal find for you.
