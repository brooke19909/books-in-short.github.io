"Small Utrish" - base. Guest House "Small Utrish"

Maly Utrish is a very special place on the Black Sea coast, which is very popular among travelers. After all, here you will find a real adventure. The warm sea, colorful mountains and relict forests create a unique landscape and excellent conditions for relaxation. So where is this amazing place?

Small Utrish village: a brief description of the area





This small fishing village is located on the very shore of the Black Sea and is part of the State Dendrological and Marine Reserve Utrish. The distance to Anapa is only 20 kilometers, and to Novorossiysk - 30.

It is worth noting that getting to a secluded village is by no means easy: it can be done either by sea or by a not-so-convenient dirt road. Nevertheless, rest here is worth all the efforts and difficulties. After all, relict trees provide clean, fresh air, and the mountains protect the area from cold winds. By the way, the tourist season lasts from May to October. During this period, the temperature does not fall below 20 degrees.

In the village there are only two small bases for tourists. In addition, travelers can find accommodation in the private sector. There is also a tent camp.

The recreation center "Small Utrish"





A small resort center is located 100 meters from the beach. Here you will find picturesque cottages and cozy wooden houses scattered in the forest, they provide you with a decent overnight stay and a fun holiday.

"Small Utrish" is one of two tourist bases on the territory of this part of the coast. Therefore, rooms should be booked in advance. Naturally, the resort center has all the necessary amenities for tourists. For example, guests of the base can use the parking lot for free. And very close to the beach there is a shop where you can buy groceries and some essentials.

Accommodation conditions for tourists





In the photo of "Small Utrish" you can see that the base is really located in a picturesque secluded corner of the coast. Nevertheless, there is a rather impressive number of rooms. Wooden houses and cottages are divided into rooms of various capacities. If you travel with a large company, you can even rent a whole building.

What awaits guests in the room? Naturally, there is the necessary amount of furniture, including soft beds, wardrobes, tables, chairs and bedside tables. Each cottage is equipped with a bathroom and a bathroom. Some rooms have air conditioning and refrigerators. And at the rental point, you can rent an electric kettle or crock-pot.

Guest House "Small Utrish" provides its residents with the necessary share of comfort. It is regularly cleaned, bed linen and towels are changed, so it is unlikely to have to complain about the condition of the rooms.

Of course, tourists are also interested in the question of what kind of food scheme the Maly Utrish complex offers. The base provides its guests with full three meals a day. Tables are served in the spacious dining room. But for this pleasure you need to pay extra separately. In addition, the resort complex has a bar that sells ready-made snacks. There is also a common kitchen where everyone can cook for themselves. And on the summer terrace there are barbecue facilities for tourists who prefer freshly cooked food on fire.

Beach and water activities





As mentioned above, the recreation center is only 100 meters from the beach - you can walk to it in one minute. Secluded pebble beaches will delight any traveler. After all, there is never a large influx of people. You can enjoy sunbathing and the warm waves of the Black Sea as much as possible.

Leisure and entertainment for vacationers





The recreation center offers a wide range of entertainment and services. Of course, there are no noisy parties, markets or nightclubs. But lovers of outdoor activities will be happy to spend their holidays here, as the area is rich in various attractions.

In particular, various excursions and hiking in the mountains are regularly held, which will give travelers a lot of pleasure. Nearby there is a small spring with mineral water. At one time, they planned to create a sanatorium here and even drilled a deep well. And although this place has remained untouched, everyone can taste the healing water.

And in the mountains there is a small lake with clean water. Here you can not only swim, but also enjoy the magnificent panorama that opens from the peaks. This place will appeal to lovers of history and archeology, because not far from the base are the ruins of the ancient city.
