Sunny Spain. Almeria and its white beaches

If you are looking for an active, diverse and simply unforgettable vacation, then the resort that you should definitely pay attention to is Spain, Almeria. It is this corner of Europe, according to weather forecasters and geographers, that is the warmest and sunniest. You can relax here almost all year round, as colds rarely visit a country called Spain. Almeria, although it is located in the autonomous region of Andalusia, is significantly different from the nearby resorts, and this is what we are now trying to understand in detail.



The nature and landscape of this region is amazing. The city itself is located on low cliffs that smoothly descend to the waters of the sea or hang with harsh silhouettes over them. Some hills are covered with incredibly rich flora, and, walking along them, you can go crazy with a variety of herbs and flowers, shrubs and low tropical trees.
