How to get there

Exact location Exotic Park - 47 km Kaluga highway. The zoo is located 20 km from the Moscow Ring Road. The easiest way to get here is by car, following the signs on the route. The zoo also provides visitors with free access to the Teply Stan metro station.

Zoo inhabitants

There are many reasons why you should come to Exotic Park. The zoo on Kaluga highway is a unique place.
