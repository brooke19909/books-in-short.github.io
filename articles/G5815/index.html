Hotel Dessole Coral Hotel 3 * (Greece / Crete): tours, photos, reviews

An unforgettable Greek vacation awaits you in the resort Dessole Coral Hotel 3 *. Here you can enjoy beautiful nature, delicious food and quality service.





a brief description of

Agios Nikolaos is considered one of the most romantic resorts in Greece, and the Dessole Coral Hotel 3 * is one of the most popular accommodation options. It is worth noting that this is one of the very first hotels opened in this city, it started working back in 1970, and therefore, over many years of work, traditions of service and reception of guests have been developed. In 2006 and 2012, two waves of restoration were carried out. Speaking of the interior, it is worth noting that it combines traditional Cretan motifs and modern practicality. The hotel consists of a main two-story building and an additional five-story building. The total number of rooms is 165.

Location

On the shore of the picturesque Mirabello Bay, the resort complex Dessole Coral Hotel 3 * is located. Crete, Agios Nikolaos, 72100 - the address of this institution. From here to the central part of the resort no more than 500 meters. Heraklion International Airport is 60 km away.

Accommodation

Comfortable living conditions provides its guests with a resort complex Dessole Coral Hotel 3 * (Greece). All rooms of this hotel can be attributed to the standard category. They are designed to accommodate from 1 to 3 guests. The rooms include beds, as well as soft chairs for relaxation. There are desks. It is also worth noting the possibility of placing additional guests on folding beds.

Additional Information

In accordance with certain rules, Dessole Coral Hotel 3 * operates. Tours at this hotel are very popular. Before you go on vacation, you should familiarize yourself with the following information:

  • check-in is from 14:00 to midnight, and check-out is before noon;
  • Visa support is provided for an additional fee;
  • to confirm the reservation, guests are required to make a full prepayment for the entire stay;
  • upon registration, guests will be charged a deposit in cash or non-cash;
  • a prerequisite for checking into a hotel is the provision of a valid photo ID;
  • children under 16 years old are not allowed in the hotel;
  • pets are not allowed;
  • the amount of cash payments cannot exceed 1500 euros;
  • to check in, the guest is required to present a personal credit card, the details of which were indicated at the time of booking;
  • if a third party card is used, written permission is required.

  • rather cramped rooms;
  • too small a safe, where money and documents are hardly placed (equipment cannot be hidden in it);
  • almost no animation and any entertainment for active tourists; a small pool in which it is inconvenient to swim (vacationers constantly interfere with each other);
  • the hotel does not have its own beach, and on the municipal deck chairs and umbrellas are paid;
  • the entry into the sea is strewn with large pebbles, which painfully stabs his legs;
  • there is practically no greenery at the hotel (there is no territory as such);
  • there are very few sun loungers near the pool, and therefore they can be occupied only by those who are used to getting up early in the morning;

  • if your room is located above the restaurant, noise from a working hood may interfere with you;
