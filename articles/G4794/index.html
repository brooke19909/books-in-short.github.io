A little bit about the hotel building

Hotel "Rococo" is located in a historic building, which was built in 1916 by the famous architect Emil Kralichka. The building has undergone many reconstructions, the last of which occurred in 2005. Engineers managed to maintain the original system of light wells, thanks to which the passage in the daytime is flooded with sunlight. By the way, this is the only operating system of this type that has survived in Prague to this day.

Speaking of passages, it is worth noting that this is an important part of the development of Wenceslas Square. The passage of the Rococo Hotel is connected to two more old passages, under the roofs of which a whole town is hidden - streets, intersections, squares.

Theater

The Rococo Hotel in Prague is famous not only for its traditions of service, but also for the theater, which was equipped in the basement of the building back in 1915. Its first leader was the famous singer Karel Gashler. Many outstanding artists of that time performed here, and therefore the theater was popular.

In the 60s, the main specialization of the theater was musical and dramatic productions. Almost all Czech and foreign stars are on stage. The Soviet artists also shone on the stage.

Since 2005, the Rococo Theater has been included in the Prague City Theaters. The contemporary repertoire is based on classic dramatic productions performed by prominent actors. But there is a place on the stage for bold experimental ideas.
