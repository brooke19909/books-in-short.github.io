Selvamar 3 * (Spain / Costa Brava) - photos, prices and reviews

Costa Brava has long been one of the most popular resorts in Spain. The largest city in this region is Lloret de Mar. It is famous for its developed entertainment infrastructure and a wide hotel base, offering accommodation for every taste and budget. Three-star hotels are most popular here, offering travelers comfortable accommodation at an affordable price. Today we offer to consider one of the hotels of Lloret de Mar in this category called Selvamar 3 *. We learn what it offers its guests and at what price. We will also find out what impressions remained of our compatriots staying in it.





Location

The three-star Selvamar Hotel has a rather good location. So, from the center of the resort town of Lloret de Mar, it is only 500 meters away. Thus, all the attractions of the tourist capital of the Costa Brava are within walking distance. Since most travelers come to Costa Brava, including for beach activities, the distance to the beach plays a very significant role when choosing a hotel. As for the hotel in question, it does not have its own beach. The nearest beach is municipal and 800 meters away. Thus, you can get on it, having made a pleasant walking fifteen-minute walk. With regard to geographical location, the city of Lloret de Mar, in which Selvamar is located, is located in the southern part of the Costa Brava tourist region. The distance from Barcelona to it is only 75 kilometers. In addition, Lloret de Mar is located just a hundred kilometers from the French border. Therefore, if you stayed at the Selvamar 3 * hotel (Spain), then you can take a fascinating trip to neighboring France.





How to get to the hotel?

Typically, travelers staying on vacation in Lloret de Mar (Costa Brava) do this through the airports of Barcelona, Girona or Reus. If you want to spend as little time as possible on the way to the hotel, then try to find a flight to Girona. The airport of this city is only 30 kilometers from Lloret de Mar. Thus, after landing, your journey to the hotel will take only 30-40 minutes. Some tourists choose Barcelona as their arrival airport. The distance from this air harbor to the center of Costa Brava spa life is 75 kilometers. In this case, the journey will take you more than two hours. If you are planning a trip to Spain on your own, without the help of a travel agency, the important factor will also be how you will get from the airport to the Selvamar 3 * hotel. The most expensive, but simple and comfortable option is to order a transfer directly to the hotel, or hire a taxi. You can also rent a car in the air harbor of both Barcelona and Girona. If you want to save money, you can get to the hotel by bus (there is a well-established regular service between all the cities of Spain) or by train.

Therefore, today this hotel is quite modern, comfortable and has all the necessary infrastructure for an excellent holiday and accommodation. It consists of one large four-story building, which includes 243 rooms. Most of them are standard, but there are also suites. Hotel Policies

As in other hotels around the world, the Spanish three-star Selvamar has a check-in and check-out time. So, settling the staying tourists here is supposed to be after an hour of the day. However, this is not a strict rule, and in case of your earlier arrival, the hotel will do everything possible so that you can stay in your booked room as soon as possible. True, this is not always possible at the height of the tourist season, when the hotel is completely full. In this case, you will have to wait until the previous guests have vacated the room, and then the maids will prepare it for new guests. By the way, it is not necessary to sit in the hall next to your suitcases. So, you can leave your things in the luggage compartment and go for a snack in the restaurant, refresh yourself with drinks in the bar, have a nice time by the pool or take a walk around the hotel and its surroundings. As for the day of departure from Selvamar 3 * (Lloret de Mar, Spain), the occupied room should be vacated by 10 a.m. At the same time, it is necessary to hand over the keys to the reception and pay for the entire stay in the hotel and use additional services. If you have previously paid for the tour with your travel agency, then you only have to pay for additional services (if you resorted to them). By the way, both cash and credit cards of payment systems such as Mastercard, Visa, American Express and Diners Club are accepted for payment at the hotel.

Accommodation with children

Since the three-star hotel ‚ÄúSelvamar‚Äù is oriented to family tourists in many respects, guests who are on holidays with children of all ages are always welcome here. To accommodate children up to two years, the hotel provides the service of installing playpens in the rooms. You can place older children both on the existing beds in the rooms and on extra beds. Please inform the hotel in advance about the need for a crib or extra bed. In this case, be sure to wait for confirmation from the hotel and find out how much this service will cost you.





Pets welcome

This item should be taken into account for those travelers who do not want to part with their four-legged pets even during the holidays. Unfortunately, in accordance with the internal rules of the Selvamar 3 * hotel (Costa Brava), pets are not allowed here. Therefore, you will either have to leave your furry friend for a vacation at home, or to find another hotel from the Pets Friend category.

Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the Spanish three-star Hotel Selvamar, located in the city of Lloret de Mar (Costa Brava), consists of one rather large four-story building, accommodating 243 rooms. 240 rooms are standard (this also includes single rooms and rooms specially equipped for people with disabilities), and three more are suites. All rooms in the hotel, although they are small in size, are distinguished by comfort and convenience, and also have everything you need. So, in each room there is a private bathroom, TV, air conditioning, telephone and safe (for a fee). There is also wireless Internet access. As for the service, cleaning in the rooms of the Selvamar 3 * hotel (Spain) is carried out on a daily basis. Towels and bed linen are changed twice a week.





Nutrition

When booking a room at the three-star Selvamar Hotel, you can choose the type of food most suitable for you: only breakfast, half board or full board. The hotel's restaurant serves a variety of dishes, snacks and desserts of both international and local cuisine. By the way, the food in the Selvamar 3 * hotel , reviews of tourists serve as a weighty confirmation, it is quite diverse, tasty and of high quality. If you chose the type of food in the form of only breakfast or half board, then at your service there are a lot of restaurants and cafeterias located in the vicinity of the hotel.

In addition to the restaurant, the Selvamar Hotel has three bars with a wide selection of drinks for every taste.

Sea and beach

As mentioned above, the hotel in question does not have its own beach. And to plunge into the azure waters of the Mediterranean Sea, you have to walk about 800 meters to the nearest beach. Along the way, you need to cross the roadway and promenade, which stretches along the coast. The beach is municipal, and you will have to pay for using sun loungers and parasols. Also on the beach there is everything necessary for water entertainment and sports.





Entertainment

We can say with confidence that in Selvamar Hotel 3 * all conditions are created so that guests can have an interesting and fun time. So, there are two rather large pools. One of them is located in the open air, and the second is indoor. Near the pool there is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as a bar with a wide selection of a variety of both alcoholic and soft drinks.

For lovers of outdoor activities at the hotel are equipped with three tennis courts, as well as a field for indoor soccer. In addition, here you can play ping pong, billiards, visit the gym and aerobics classes. For relaxation, the hotel features a sauna and a jacuzzi.

In the evenings, Selvamar 3 * hosts various entertainment shows and events, and later an incendiary disco begins.

Infrastructure

Despite the fact that the Selvamar Hotel (Lloret de Mar, Costa Brava) is a three-star hotel, it has a developed infrastructure that allows its guests to feel very comfortable here. So, for example, the hotel reception is available around the clock, which allows tourists to quickly solve all problems.

For the convenience of guests, the hotel is equipped with elevators. It also has bars, a restaurant, a TV lounge, a conference room, a shop and laundry facilities. For a fee, hotel guests can use the parking lot.

In Selvamar 3 *, all the conditions for the relaxation of the youngest travelers are also created. So, there is a children's pool and a children's playground, a mini club is functioning, and in the evenings there is a mini disco.





Cost of living

As for the price of accommodation at the Selvamar hotel, accommodation in a standard double room in high season will cost about 1080 euros (with the type of food half board). In the low season, accommodation for the same period will cost significantly less, about 400 euros.
