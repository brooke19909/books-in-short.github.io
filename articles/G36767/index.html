What can I get from Voronezh to Kursk?

The road from Voronezh to Kursk is an almost straight line. Why do people need to make this move? There may be several reasons. Based on them, you should choose the most suitable vehicle.





Option for the most active travelers

If the main purpose of the trip is to travel to new objects, then the most indispensable transport will be a personal car. First of all, this is due to the fact that in another city it will be very difficult without it, because it is quite difficult to enjoy all the beauties of this place while traveling by public transport. On the way, you can also make many discoveries for yourself. To get from one point to another, it is worth driving past several interesting settlements. The road ahead is fairly smooth and unobstructed. Unfortunately, there are no cafeterias, restaurants and other catering facilities along the way. But you can buy a light snack in supermarkets. As for gas stations, there will be no problems with the purchase of fuel. At the entrance to the city of Kursk there will be a traffic police post, its employees can stop absolutely any car, check luggage and documents.





Cost calculation

The distance from Kursk to Voronezh is approximately 225 kilometers. If you move without stops and traffic jams, then you can overcome this distance in just four hours. Costs will be required only for gasoline. About 20 liters of fuel will be needed, which will cost a little more than 700 rubles. Basically, this calculation applies to domestic cars. Fuel consumption of a foreign car may be less. Modern youth before such a trip are looking for travel companions and sent by a large company, this helps to reduce costs several times.

The best way for those who do not have personal transport

Many people are wondering how to get from Voronezh to Kursk if there is no personal transport. Of course, the best option would be to travel by bus. For long trips, a modern type of transport is provided, it has a small TV, air conditioning and seats with adjustable backrests. Approximate travel time is 4 hours.





We should also talk about a very convenient bus schedule from Voronezh to Kursk. The flight is carried out every day with a time interval of about one hour. The first departure takes place at 6 a.m., and the last at 9 p.m. A ticket for one person will be about 500 rubles, you can buy it right before boarding at the station of Voronezh or Kursk. If necessary, you can also use transit transport, having made a transfer with several transfers.

The longest and most tedious way

The longest and most tedious, but at the same time very convenient and interesting way to get from Voronezh to Kursk is a train. Usually this option is chosen by pregnant women, parents with young children and the elderly. This is due to the fact that sitting for several hours in one position is terribly uncomfortable, and in the car you can walk, lie down and even sleep.





It is possible to buy a ticket for a passing train that runs daily. From the railway station of the city of Voronezh, he leaves at 6.30 and at noon arrives in Kursk. Accordingly, the entire travel time is just over 5 hours.

The cost of a ticket for this type of transport is very different. The cheapest seat ticket can be bought at a price of 470 rubles. Further, the cost is much higher: reserved seat 990 rubles, coupe 1060 rubles, luxury - 2065 rubles. Convenient discounts are provided for small passengers, children under 3 years old can travel for free, and up to 10 years old with a discount of 50 percent.

Most expensive way

Of course, flying from Voronezh to Kursk is unreasonable, since the distance between these two cities is not significant. However, many people still dream of using this particular vehicle. There is no direct regular flight between these two cities. If, nevertheless, for some reason such a need arose, then you should look for a route with transfers, for example, in Moscow, Yekaterinburg or St. Petersburg. It is worth considering immediately that at a cost this will turn out at least 10 thousand rubles, but there will be an amazing opportunity to visit several beautiful cities.

In conclusion, it is worth saying that it is possible to get from Voronezh to Kursk, and this can be done in several different ways. It is recommended that you still give preference to personal transport or a bus for several reasons. First of all, this is due to the fact that this can save time and money. In some situations, the train is also suitable. The most unreasonable way is to use an airplane, time to overcome the path will be spent several times more.

Approximately per person, the fare will be 500 rubles. Of course, the exception is the flight by plane, but absolutely none of the travelers currently use this method.
