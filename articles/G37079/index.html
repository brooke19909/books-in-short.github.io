Park-Hotel "White Sable" (Baikalsk): description with photos, guest reviews

In the city of Baikalsk, popular tourist routes intersect leading to the Tunka Valley, Buryatia and Mongolia. In addition, it is located on the shore of Lake Baikal. This factor attracts many tourists here at any time of the year. And guests can comfortably stay in the White Sable, located in the Baikalsk hotel, right at the foot of Sobolinaya Mountain.

Description

The hotel is located next to the Sobolinaya Mountain ski resort. In wintertime, lovers of skiing come here, and in summer fishing, hunting, hiking and boating await you. To Lake Baikal from the "White Sable" is only two kilometers. Ski lifts are located within walking distance from the hotel. From the bus stop "Sangorod" fifty meters from the hotel you can go to Baikalsk.

Meals are organized in the two-storey restaurant "Cranberry", which can be reached from the hotel via a warm passage. Here the hotel staff will help you organize a festive banquet. On specially equipped sites, picnic lovers can cook kebabs. After an active day, guests will find a sauna, massage room, a large outdoor pool.

Private guarded parking is free of charge for guests arriving by car. Tourists can also use wireless internet for free throughout. A sports equipment rental service is available. At the ski school, experienced instructors will teach you the basics of skiing. Young tourists can have fun in the playground or in the games room. Staff at the tour desk can help arrange sightseeing tours. Upon request, a transfer from Irkutsk airport can be arranged for you. The hotel provides rooms for accommodation, as well as two-story cottages. The hotel "White Sable" is located in Baikalsk at the address: 93 Krasny Klyuch microdistrict.

Accommodation of tourists in a hotel

To accommodate guests, the hotel offers the following room options:

  • single standard - 3000 rubles per day;
  • standard double with two single or one double bed - 3800 rubles per day;
  • Junior suite with two single beds and a sofa bed - 5000 rubles per day for two people;
  • Studio - 5500 rubles per day for two people;
  • apartments with a large double bed and a sofa bed - 7500 rubles per day for two people.

Prices for accommodation in the hotel "White Sable" are shown for low season. The price includes breakfast.

Accommodation in cottages

In the park-hotel "White Sable" for living are also offered comfortable two-story cottages and a detached house.

The ground floor of the cottage is a large living room, kitchen, dressing room and guest toilet. The living room has a plasma TV with karaoke and upholstered furniture, in the kitchen you will find all the necessary appliances and utensils. The dressing room provides storage space for ski equipment. On the second floor there are three bedrooms, one of which is designed to accommodate four people and two for two, as well as two bathrooms, each of which has a shower. If necessary, you can decide on the provision of additional beds. The cottage can accommodate twelve people at a time. The cost of living per day is 18,000 rubles.

The small detached double wooden house also has everything you need for a comfortable stay. The house can accommodate four people at a time, the cost of living is 3,500 rubles per day.

Bath and Sauna

At the White Sable Hotel in Baikalsk, guests have the opportunity to relax in a real Russian bath or sauna.

The bathhouse is a relaxation room with a dining table, steam room and shower. Extras include oak brooms. Upon request, you can order dishes from the restaurant. In the bath at the same time can accommodate no more than twelve people. The cost in the high season and New Year holidays is 2500 rubles per hour, in the low season - 2000 rubles per hour.

In the sauna, designed for 6-8 people at a time, there is a relaxation room with a large plasma TV and comfortable furniture (leather sofa, chairs and a table). Guests can use sheets, towels, tea set. Upon request, you can order dishes from the restaurant. The cost in the high season and New Year holidays is 2,000 rubles per hour, in the low season - 1,500 rubles per hour.

When ordering restaurant dishes in the bathhouse and in the sauna, an additional fee of 10% of the cost of the order is charged.

Hotel "White Sable" (Baikalsk). How to get there

From the airports of Irkutsk and Ulan-Ude, between which the resort is located, you can get to Baikalsk by two modes of transport - road and rail:

  • Buses and fixed-route taxis leave daily from the Irkutsk railway station to Baikalsk, the travel time is two to three hours, one trip costs about 250 rubles.
  • From Ulan-Ude, a fixed-route taxi to Baikalsk also leaves from the railway station from 8.00 to 20.00 with an interval of two hours. Travel time is 4.5-5 hours, the cost of one trip is about 600 rubles.
  • Also, by the nearest train or train, you can get to the Baikalsk station, and from there a regular bus, minibus or taxi will take you to the city. You will have to pay about 100 rubles for a taxi.
  • From Krasnoyarsk to Baikalsk you can get by rail.
  • If you get to the hotel by private car, then, not reaching five meters to the Sobolinaya Mountain checkpoint, you need to turn right.

Booking conditions

When booking a room in White Sable, the following rules apply:

  • Prepayment must be made within two days after confirmation is received.
  • Upon confirmation, you will be informed of the cancellation policy.
  • During public holidays, cancellations can be made free of charge 30 days before the declared arrival date, if cancellation is made in less than 30 days, a penalty of 100% prepayment will be imposed.
  • If the reservation is made through the official website of the hotel, a 20% discount is provided.
