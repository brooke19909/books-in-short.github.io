Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): photo with description, infrastructure, service, reviews of tourists

Vietnam is one of the most popular destinations for Russian tourists, especially in the winter, when the low season begins in Turkey. Not only exotic oriental culture attracts vacationers, but also low cost of living. For example, in Nha Trang along the coast a lot of cheap hotels have been opened. But not all complexes offer really high quality service at low prices. An extremely budget option in Nha Trang is considered Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * (Vietnam), the reviews about which tourists leave controversial. Should I choose it for relaxation? We will tell you about this resort in this article.

Where is the hotel situated?

Since this hotel is considered a place for cheap accommodation, it cannot offer tourists a favorable location. Nevertheless, the Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * hotel (Vietnam) was built in the central part of the city on a busy street. In the immediate vicinity there are many attractions, including the 2nd of April. Tourists can walk to it in 5 minutes. Near the hotel is the Longshon Pagoda and the Tyama Towers. If you want to buy souvenirs or gifts for loved ones, be sure to visit the Vietnamese Dan market. Tourists with children are advised to go to an amusement park. It will take you about 10 minutes by car to get there. The distance to the central part of Nha Trang is about 450 meters.

The main disadvantage of the location of the hotel is its distance from the beach. The hotel does not have its own stretch of coast, because it was built on the third line of the sea. The distance to the beach is about 750 meters. You can walk to it in 10 minutes. To get to the sea faster, many tourists also rent scooters.

The international airport is located near Nha Trang, so guests can get to the hotel in about 1 hour. It is 35 km away from the hotel. For convenience, when buying a tour, you can purchase a transfer from the airport. You can also take a taxi or public transport.

general information

The budget city hotel Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * was opened in Nha Trang in 2010. But do not think that his number of rooms is very outdated. The last full repair in the complex was carried out recently - in 2016. The hotel has no extensive territory, as it is located in the city center. It only houses a seven-story residential building. The housing stock has only 21 rooms, so this hotel is suitable for tourists who do not like crowded places to stay. After all, even with full occupancy, no more than 50 tourists live on its territory.

It is worth noting that the hotel staff does not speak Russian. The administrator speaks Vietnamese and English. You can come here with children of any age. It is also possible to check in with pets, however, this service is provided for a fee. It is necessary to warn the hotel management in advance of their intention to bring the animal and give them all the information about it.

Check-in at the hotel starts at noon local time and lasts until midnight. Tourists must leave their living rooms no later than 12:00. Upon registration, guests must give the administrator an international passport as a deposit.

Number of rooms Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang)

It has already been said above that this is a very small hotel, with a housing stock of only 21 rooms. Of course, they cannot boast of large sizes or an elegant interior, since the hotel is considered an extremely budget option for accommodation. But each room has everything that you might need for a comfortable night: comfortable beds, a bathroom with a shower, lockers for clothes.

Room features

And although Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * is considered a budget option for accommodation, all its rooms are equipped with a standard set of amenities needed for a comfortable stay. Having settled in the apartment, tourists can use:

  • individual air conditioning with temperature control panel;
  • a small TV, to which a package of satellite channels, including Russian, is connected;
  • direct telephone, which can be used both for communication with the administrator, and for international calls;
  • a refrigerator for storing food and soft drinks;
  • free wireless internet.

All guests receive bottled drinking water upon arrival. There is no safe in the room, but tourists can leave their valuables with the administrator at the reception.

Supply system

Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * does not offer a large selection of food concepts. As a rule, guests prefer to eat not in the hotel, but in small cafes and restaurants on Nha Trang Street. Therefore, they can purchase a ticket without food or pay only breakfast.

Morning meals are served at the on-site restaurant, which seats 30 visitors. A standard international breakfast is served. Its menu consists of omelet or scrambled eggs, sandwiches, sausage and cheese slices, fruits and vegetables.

Territory and infrastructure

There is no developed infrastructure in the hotel either, as it is often used exclusively for overnight stay. Nevertheless, guests can exchange currency or arrange car rental at the reception. Lockers for storing valuables are also rented here. For a fee, the administrator can arrange a shuttle service to the airport or hand over clothes and laundry.

Leisure options

Tourists choose this hotel for an overnight stay, and prefer to spend most of the time in the city. Therefore, no entertainment for guests at the hotel is not provided. If desired, they can book an excursion to the sights of Nha Trang at the reception or rent a bicycle and a scooter. Using the latter, it is very convenient to get to the municipal beach, which is 750 meters from the hotel.

Conditions for children

Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * complex (Vietnam, Nha Trang) accepts tourists with children of any age, but does not provide additional amenities for them. Children under 5 years old can stay in rooms for free. However, there are no cots for children, and this must be taken into account when arriving at the hotel with babies. Entertainment for young guests at the hotel is also not.

Positive feedback about Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 *

Like many budget hotels, this hotel is often criticized by guests. Of course, offering accommodation at a low cost, the administration cannot provide guests with a first-class level of service. However, many tourists leave positive reviews about the hotel. They believe that the cost of living is fully consistent with the proposed service. Yes, the hotel has quite a few shortcomings, but the prices here are not high. Tourists recommend this complex to picky travelers who are looking for an inexpensive place to stay in Nha Trang.

In positive reviews about the Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * hotel (Vietnam), guests indicate its following advantages:

  • Small rooms in which all the necessary furniture and equipment are compactly housed. The interior can not be called elegant, but the rooms are kept clean, so they look good.
  • On the way to the beach, there are many cafes, restaurants, shops. Even the market. It is very convenient to buy the necessary products and have a snack, returning from the sea.
  • Friendly and responsible hotel administrators. They always tell you where to go in Nha Trang, and also try to quickly solve all the problems of the guests.
  • The bathroom has replenished bath amenities. The hotel always has hot water.
  • Wireless internet works well throughout the complex.

Hotel criticism and conclusions

It cannot be denied that the complex has many shortcomings. Often, negative reviews about Ken Hotel Nha Trang 2 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) indicate really existing disadvantages of the hotel. But it is worthwhile to understand that you are choosing a two-star hotel for relaxation. Tourists recommend choosing a more expensive option for accommodation if you want to relax in Nha Trang with comfort.

What did guests dislike about the hotel the most? In their reviews, they ask management to pay attention to the following serious disadvantages:

  • Some rooms have no windows, and the lighting is rather dim, so the living rooms are very dark in the evening.
  • There are a lot of ants in the hotel that staff cannot handle. Therefore, tourists do not recommend leaving food on the table, as it attracts insects. Better put them in the refrigerator.
  • The windows overlook the busy traffic street, so sometimes it is quite noisy at night. Tourists have to sleep with the windows closed.
  • A slow elevator that has to wait a few minutes to go up to your floor.
  • Some rooms require general cleaning. There is a lot of dust under the carpets and furniture, stains are visible on the windows. Sometimes maids bring towels and bedding with small holes.

Of course, this hotel will not be an ideal place to relax in Vietnam. But for budget accommodation, it is perfect. Guests recommend coming here to young companies and those travelers who need a hotel room only for an overnight stay. After all, Nha Trang is a lively resort where absolutely any tourist can enjoy entertainment.
