The recreation center "Russian House" (Talmenka, Altai): recreation, prices and reviews

The picturesque and endless Mountain Altai is a great health resort of the Russian Federation, attracting tourists like a magnet. Clean air, pristine nature and beautiful lakes - that's what people like in this area. Here you will find diverse entertainment and activities for every taste. Altai Krai provides unlimited and versatile opportunities for travelers.

Over three hundred comfortable hotel complexes are concentrated in the expanses of the republic. Among the huge number of boarding houses, the Russian House recreation center deserves special attention. This is a paradise drowning in vegetation. On its territory you will find all kinds of entertainment for the adult audience and small children.





General information

The all-season recreation center "Russian House" in Altai is located in a nature-protected zone - surrounded by a thicket near the Chumysh River. In the beautiful territory you can see wooden sculptures of the heroes of Russian fairy tales (Babu Yaga, Leshii, The Little Mermaid and many other characters).

It is comfortable and fun to spend holidays with children, relax with large companies and hold corporate events. At certain times of the year, exciting programs are organized. Most vacationers in the summer months when the beach opens: you can swim, sunbathe, and in between water and sun baths, go picking mushrooms and berries in the forest. This is a real paradise for fans of calm, active and family leisure.





Terms of Accommodation

Comfortable conditions have been created for living in the Russian House complex. The recreation center (Talmenka) at a time can accommodate up to 144 people in the summer. The number of rooms includes several housing options - from comfortable rooms to Indian wigwam.

From wigwam to ship cabin

Native American wigwams are small yurts with stove heating, a toilet and a sink. The shower is located in another building. There are four yurts in the territory, and they are designed for three people. In winter, the house is heated with wood.



Brick cottages with a separate entrance are designed for 6 people (there are additional beds). The rooms are equipped with modern furniture and appliances. There is a private bathroom.

The Russian Village houses were built between 2010 and 2014. All rooms are decorated in an original Russian style. There are showers, a fridge and a plasma TV.

Wooden cottages (4 pcs.). Guests can choose heated rooms with private facilities or seasonal rooms with a shared bathroom.

Cabins in the ship "Love" are designed for 17 people (shower / toilet, TV, beds). There is a dance hall, a billiard room and a bar.



Three-story hotel "Terem" for 26 people. On the ground floor there is a cozy common fireplace room. There are rooms with and without amenities.

Rest house "Fish" with common facilities and a kitchen area.

The huts of the Petrovsky Village are concentrated on the other side of the river. They can be reached by boat in 15 minutes. Ideal for those who want to feel like a real villager and live away from the noisy metropolis. There is absolute silence and peace. The houses have stoves, so in winter it will be warm and cozy. Convenience, however, on the street, as befits such a dwelling. Communication with the base is via a walkie-talkie.

Every year in the spring and autumn, the recreation center "Russian House" provides discounts. Prices start at 1800 r / day for one person. The payment includes a balanced diet and basic leisure programs. If you intend to hold a business conference or other major event, the cost is discussed individually with the administrator.

Supply system

Country recreation center "Russian House" offers a comprehensive wholesome meal. The territory has its own compound, so the bulk of the products come from there, including meat and poultry. The menu will amaze you with its variety, taste and benefits.

Chefs prepare exclusively traditional Russian dishes. For small guests a separate diet. Outdoor picnics are organized: you will be served stunningly delicious grilled meat on the grill, fish soup and many more other delicacies. It is also allowed to cook on their own using their own products.





Coastline

The beach season opens in June and lasts until the month of October. The recreation center "Russian House" is located near the coast. The beach is clean, strewn with fine sand. A rich entertainment program is offered: boat riding and a pontoon in the vicinity, water skiing, "buns", rafting for thrill-seekers. You can ride on a "banana" along the surface of the river with a breeze and get unimaginable pleasure and vivid impressions.





Entertainment

Want to feel like an avid fisherman? Then come to the tourist complex "Russian House". The recreation center regularly receives rave reviews from people. Guests are satisfied with a truly homely and welcoming atmosphere. Many laudatory words have been said about the quality of food and living conditions. Being here, you get the strongest charge of positive emotions, a storm of impressions and most importantly - you are cleansed spiritually.

It is impossible to describe in words the sensations of riding horses. An experienced instructor will tell you how to properly manage animals. And after a busy and adventurous day, it's nice to visit a Russian bathhouse.





Active program

Sports grounds for team games are equipped on site. The necessary inventory is issued. Interesting excursion routes are organized in the local area. Guests are free to walk in the woods on their own, ride a bicycle, pick mushrooms and berries. In addition, the recreation center "Russian House" invites everyone to a disco, which is arranged on the deck of the ship.

In winter, fun adventures await you: riding real reindeer, sleighs, skates and skiing. By New Year, the whole territory is lit with colorful garlands and lights. Little guests of the complex will not be bored. There is a playground on site. Children can visit the animal corner (compound), feed its inhabitants and watch them.



An unbelievable pleasure will be provided by a walk on the real ship "Love", where children can stand at the helm. Discounts are provided for young tourists: up to three years, free accommodation, from three to six years - 75% of the cost of the ticket, from 7 to 14 years - 50%.
