Knitting horizontal patterns: description, application, photo

Knitting horizontal patterns with knitting needles is a very useful skill. This technique does not require much effort in the work, allows you to show creative imagination, makes it possible to regularly update your wardrobe and give original gifts to loved ones. Hand knitting is the oldest form of decorative art. Such work gives pleasure, calms the nervous system, develops motor skills, which positively affects the state of health and mental activity of a person.

Yarn selection

The choice of yarn is of utmost importance. Each thread is endowed with its own characteristics, which are usually written on the label. Do not ignore the manufacturer's recommendations regarding the care of yarn, since the life of the knitted product depends on this. The following types of yarn are presented in the assortment of specialized stores:

  • Woolen
  • half-woolen;
  • synthetic;
  • cotton;
  • mohair;
  • wool of home spinning.

Practical tips

Knitting horizontal patterns are very suitable for making wool products. They are perfectly worn and do not lose their shape with proper care of them. For knitting hats, sweaters, scarves or any outerwear, thick threads are suitable. Knitting dresses, blouses, vests, pullovers is recommended from fine wool, which is taken in three or four threads. Sports things should be knitted from a thick thread or pet yarn.

The yarn brought from the store should not be immediately rewound into balls. It must be washed. To do this, you need to collect water 30 - 40 degrees, put there a baby or bath soap, beat the foam. Then lower the yarn there, gently squeeze it with your hands (no need to rub), using a delicate wash. Rinse thoroughly in water and dry.

For knitting, you can also use second-hand yarn. To do this, it is necessary to undo the product parts, dissolve them (usually dissolve from top to bottom) and wind them into a skein (not to be confused with a ball). Then wash according to the above method and start a new knitting.

Simple patterns

Knitting horizontal patterns with knitting are very easy (even for beginners). There are some simple drawings that are suitable for almost any DIY product. Among them:

  1. Facial surface. The canvas fits as the loops look. For example, the first row is knitted with facial loops, the second - with the wrong ones. When the job is turned over, the front loops appear again, then the wrong ones again.
  2. Hosiery knitting. This is a simple knit, in which all the loops are knitted facial. At each turn of the work, knitting is obtained against the way the loops look, since the front loop on the other hand becomes seamy.
  3. Putanka. This knitting involves the alternation of one front and one wrong loop. When the job is overturned, the front loop is sewn on the wrong side, and the wrong loop - on the front.

Original stripes pattern

Knitting pattern "horizontal stripes" can be done with plain yarn, or you can make each strip color. You can alternately alternate two colors, you can repeat the pattern every three, four, etc., or you can make the product in the form of a gradient or rainbow. In the case of simple stripes, remember that the finished product will stretch in width. That is why for this method it is recommended to type a little less loops. It is recommended to choose an option that suits the taste of the customer:

  1. Knit the first row with facial loops, turning over, knit the wrong side. Then again, the face and the wrong side. The fifth row is knitted with the wrong side (against the way the loops look), then with the front ones, etc. The result should be a drawing in which four rows of facial and four rows of purl alternate.
  2. Similarly, knit six rows of front and back. It is thus possible to make 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, etc. It should be remembered that the more identical rows there are, the wider the strips.

Chain

The knitting pattern "horizontal chain" is knitted with the usual facial smoothness with delicate chains periodically running horizontally. This pattern can be used to separate some parts of the product or as the main canvas. Knitting should begin with the front surface with an arbitrary number of rows. Place the chain evenly through the same number of rows or make a drawing as you wish. The horizontal line in the form of a chain is as follows:

  1. Remove the edge loop without knitting.
  2. Add an extra loop by pulling.
  3. To put an additional loop on the left knitting needle.
  4. Knit the second loop on the left knitting needle behind the back wall (leave on the left knitting needle).
  5. Knit the first loop on the left knitting needle behind the front wall.
  6. Reset both loops (one of them turned out).
  7. Put the loop on the left knitting needle.
  8. In a similar way, knit to the end of the row.
  9. Knit the end of the chain (the last loop) together with the edge seam.
