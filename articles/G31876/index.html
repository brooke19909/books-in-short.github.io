How much a notary earns: salary level by region

Notaries are considered the fifth branch of government. This is the highest rung in the legal field. Working as a notary is attractive because they have virtually no competition in the market. Therefore, they have a good income from their activities. We will find out how much a notary earns and why many graduates of law schools dream of becoming one.

Who is a notary public?

A notary is a public institution that protects the rights and interests of individuals and legal entities. The corresponding functions in the Russian Federation are performed by notaries. They can be employees of government agencies or provide the necessary services privately.

The position is appointed in accordance with the rules approved by the federal executive body. Together with the Federal Notary Chamber, it exercises control and supervisory functions in the notarial sphere. He also determines the number of posts that are possible in a particular district.

The notary is vested with authority on the recommendations of the Federal Notary Chamber. Persons newly taking up their duties take an oath in which they undertake to fulfill their duties in accordance with the law, ensure the preservation of professional secrets, and be guided by the principles of a humane, respectful attitude to people in their work.

The exact income of notaries is hard to call. For some legal services, they can set their own price, which is several times higher than state tariffs. Functions

Sometimes it seems that a notary public employee sets high prices for elementary actions that do not require special efforts. But this is an erroneous opinion. The range of actions of a notary is very wide. Such a person should be well-versed in legislative acts in order to professionally advise clients and correctly draw up each document. The main functions of a notary public are as follows:

  • Registration of procedural actions of various nature.
  • Certificate of documents (including copies), extracts, signatures.
  • Providing evidence.
  • Drafting transactions.
  • Clarification of questions on notarial acts.

The amount of services rendered depends on how much a notary will earn per month.

Responsibilities

  1. To provide notarial services to individuals and legal entities.
  2. Explain the rights and obligations of citizens seeking help.
  3. Promote customer protection.
  4. Talk about the possible consequences of committed notarial acts.
  5. Refuse to perform a service if it does not comply with Russian law.
  6. Comply with the professional code of notaries of the Russian Federation.

What does it take to become a notary?

It can be a person who has a law degree and has passed a specific exam. The following requirements are established for a candidate for a notary public:

  • Russian citizenship.
  • Higher specialized education.
  • One-year internship at an experienced notary public.
  • Successful passing the qualification exam.
  • Obtaining a license to carry out professional activities.

Many want to become a notary. Even if the candidate meets all of the above requirements, he may not be hired if a stronger opponent wins the competition for the vacant position. Many agree to remain in the position of assistant notary until free space appears. Often the waiting period is delayed for several years. How much does a notary who has just begun to fulfill his duties earn? The earnings of a novice employee will be 2-3 times less than that of a practitioner.

Salary in the capital

How much do notaries in Moscow earn? Their official income in a large metropolis can reach 70,000 rubles per month. How much does a private notary earn? A beginner can have about $ 1000 per month. An experienced specialist, this amount increases several times.
