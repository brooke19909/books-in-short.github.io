Choose a Samsung vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter

Choose a Samsung vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter





Vacuum cleaners have firmly entered our lives, turning a tedious and lengthy cleaning into a simple and easy task. They are made by many companies. What company to stop at?

Samsung conquers the market

Samsung entered the market in 1969 and quickly gained the attention of fans from all over the world. Quality and reliability is famous for its household appliances. Samsung vacuum cleaners are a great example of modern home equipment. They are distinguished by reliability and ease of use at the same time at an affordable price.

What is the difference?

Vacuum cleaners have various designs, functions, power and, of course, prices. When choosing, you should pay attention to the suction power, which directly affects the quality of cleaning. The required power is calculated taking into account the size of the room and the amount of dust in it. The cleaning, which is carried out using this household appliance, can be dry and wet.

Vacuum cleaners for dry cleaning

Depending on the design, they are divided into 3 groups:

  • with a dust bag;
  • with aquafilter;
  • cyclone.

Vacuum cleaners with a dust collector collect dust and dirt in a paper (disposable) or fabric (reusable) bag. They are outdated due to the fact that they pass microparticles of dust into the air, but are still popular.





The Samsung vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter is characterized by high quality air purification, as it retains almost 100% of the dust entering the inside. This quality is possible due to the presence of a special cleaning system. It passes a polluted stream of air through a reservoir containing water. It is water that traps dust and debris. Purified air flows back into the environment and creates a feeling of freshness. Therefore, the Samsung vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter cleans not only surfaces, but also airspace. In addition, it moisturizes and aromatizes the air in the room. Also, the Samsung vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter is equipped with a turbo brush, which helps to remove special contaminants from the carpets, including animal hair. Another advantage is the aqua vacuum cleaner -

constant suction power regardless of the degree of filling of the container, unlike a vacuum cleaner with a dust collector. The disadvantages of this variety are high cost, large size and weight. Water filters in vacuum cleaners with an alpha filter can be separator and hookah (bubbling).

In the separator type, only water without porous filters is used. With the help of a powerful whirlpool, all trash is captured. After cleaning, drain the water. Due to the fact that there are no porous filters in the vacuum cleaner, they do not require washing and drying. But this vacuum cleaner is very expensive.

A hookah type vacuum cleaner is much cheaper, but its disadvantage is that after each cleaning, porous filters must be washed and dried so that bacteria do not multiply. In addition, filters require frequent replacement.

Samsung Cyclone Vacuum Cleaner is an ideal option for home use. It spirally swirls contaminated air in a special plastic tank. This ensures that dust is thrown onto the walls of the vessel and that clean air exits the vacuum cleaner.

Vacuum cleaners for wet cleaning

If you need not only to collect dust, but also to carry out wet cleaning, then it is better to buy a washing vacuum cleaner. It sprays water and then sucks wet dust and debris into a dust collector.
