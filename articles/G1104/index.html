The correct calculation of property tax: who pays, how much and for what?

According to the Federal Tax Service, the new calculation of property tax will be implemented starting in January 2014. To date, it has performed approximate calculations, for which the cadastral valuation of various objects located in 83 regions of the Russian Federation was taken as the basis. According to the tax authorities, the amount that will need to be paid to homeowners depends on its value. In addition, a number of representatives of the press acquainted the results of the work carried out by the Federal Tax Service.

How will the tax on property of individuals be calculated ? The fiscal service mentioned above offers to implement it on the basis of a rate of 1 or 0.5 percent. The first is valid for residential real estate, and the second - as you might guess, non-residential. Representatives of the Russian media did not want to stay away from the announced innovations: a number of publishers published approximate amounts of payments. During the calculations, it was found that in 2014 the number of objects for which citizens would have to pay would almost double. At the same time, fees will increase fivefold. For example, if previously the number of objects was estimated at about 35.4 million, then about 70.3 million will be subject to taxation in the new year. As you know, the maximum price of real estate is fixed in the capital. Here, a square meter of the apartment is estimated at about 165.5, at home - 109.1, office - 92.7 thousand rubles.
