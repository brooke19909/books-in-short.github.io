Bank History

The first branch of Sberbank was founded in Russia in 1841 by the Emperor Nicholas I. The financial institution at that time was necessary for the delivery of funds to the state in the most profitable and lawful way.

Over time, the network grew, forming a stable, reliable institution, which all residents of the Russian Federation knew about. Today, Sberbank is a large-scale, leading in the ratings reliable bank in Russia.
