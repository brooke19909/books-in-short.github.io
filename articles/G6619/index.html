Hotel Palma 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands): photos and reviews of tourists

What could be better than travel? Every year, hundreds of thousands of Russians go to the beach to soak up the sun and take a dip in warm water. World resorts collect tourists from all over the globe. Russians enjoy traveling to Turkey, Egypt, Crimea or Sochi. Bulgaria also does not lag behind world resorts. Her hospitality always attracts tourists, and well-groomed beaches make her come here with young children.

Bulgaria is a family, safe and cultural holiday. The best medical resorts of this country are located on the Black Sea coast. But the most popular holiday destinations not only for Russians, but also for tourists from other countries remain the Gold Coast, Borovets, Bansko and, of course, Golden Sands. This is where a great place for the whole family is located - Palma Hotel 4 *.

Golden Sands

To get to this resort, you need to fly to Varna. From the airport to Golden Sands go only 20-30 minutes. The distance is small, which is a plus for traveling with young children. Hotel Palma 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is located three hundred meters from the center of the famous town.



By the way, the resort got its name thanks to the Golden Sands Park. It is on its territory that there are hotels, beaches and, of course, entertainment complexes. Such a peculiar reserve attracts many tourists. Here, on the seashore, you can stroll through the forests and see unusual birds and animals.

In addition to the Palma Hotel, Golden Sands has 50 hotels. Each is close to the sea, which is a huge plus for children. Golden Sands is the perfect place to relax with the whole family. In addition to leisure centers, it is easy to find animators and educators who will occupy your children. On the beaches there are many attractions for kids. The shore itself is safe and received an award from the European Union as one of the cleanest and most well-groomed in the world. Hotel "Palma" (Bulgaria) is a great place to relax with the whole family. Now we will see this.

Where is the hotel situated?

As mentioned earlier, to get to the hotel, you need to fly to Varna airport, and then it takes 20 minutes to get to the hotel itself. Palma 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is located near the center. The hotel was completely renovated and modernized by 2010.

The building is located among the forest. To get to the beach, you need to walk 200-300 meters through the wonderful green spaces. The hotel is sometimes called the Palma Boutique Hotel, but this is wrong. In Golden Sands with a similar name are still hotels that can be confused. Palma Boutique Hotel is located 30 meters from the sea in the eastern part of the resort.

Hotel Description

The hotel opened in 2008, two years later it was reconstructed and changed its appearance dramatically. However, there are no bizarre forms in its architecture. This is a traditional family run hotel. Palma (Golden Sands) is a small hotel on 7 floors. Inside are 39 rooms. On the territory there are two large pools.



The hotel is divided into two parts. In one live those who have purchased apartments in a hotel. In another, tourists and city guests live. The hotel itself is compact. Despite the fact that it has 7 floors, it does not look huge.

Rules of residence

As in other hotels, in Palma 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) the rules of living are simple. Those who have just arrived at the hotel can check into their room after 14:00. If those who previously lived in it left the room, you will be settled immediately after cleaning. If you arrived earlier than 14:00, you can leave all your things in the storage room and go down to the pool or take a walk along the Golden Sands.

If you have a child from 2 to 11 years old, the cost of his stay is 70% of the payment for one adult tourist. If one parent arrives with two children, then the room is paid as double for two adults.

At the end of the rest, you should vacate the room before noon. Until this time, you must pay for both accommodation and all paid services that you used. If you have pre-paid the number in a travel agency, you just have to pay for the entertainment. All additional services can be paid in cash or with a Visa and Mastercard card.

To book a hotel room in Palma (Golden Sands), please note that a prepayment will be charged to the credit card, to which the rest will be added at the end of the stay. Cancellation of a reservation is possible without loss of funds, if you take care of it a week before arrival. If canceled later, the first night will be charged.

Children

The hotel really has a family holiday. In Palma Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) there are two elevators, this is especially nice for parents with young children and for older people.



Accommodation is possible for children of any age, but the rules still indicate that they accept babies from 8 months. Those who are not yet 3 years old will be provided with a crib, but for a fee. You can also use a stroller for a child for money. But the high chair in the restaurant will give you free.

On the territory of Palma Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) there is a playground and two outdoor pools. Only one is available for tourists, the second is occupied by those who purchased apartments in this hotel. In the adult pool there is a small area with slides for kids.

Pets

Many love to travel and relax with "our smaller brothers." Some hotels allow pets. But here in Palma 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) the description of the rules says that four-legged animals are forbidden. Perhaps if you call the hotel and clarify this issue, for a fee they will allow you to come with your favorite doggie.
