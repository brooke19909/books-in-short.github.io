LCD "Kalina Park". "Kalina-Park": how to get there, characteristics of houses and reviews

At a distance of more than 1300 kilometers from Moscow and about 100-120 kilometers from the Black and Azov seas, the city of Krasnodar freely stretched under the hot southern sun. The population in it is growing rapidly, so the city is considered a very attractive place among developers. The residential complex Kalina Park is a vivid example of this.





Krasnodar city

Krasnodar is the unofficial capital of the Kuban, the largest economic and cultural center of the North Caucasus. It ranks first in the ranking of cities with growing business activity. The city has a rather difficult environmental situation due to the large number of road transport.





Krasnodar is a large industrial center. About a third of the total population works at enterprises located in the city. This is instrumentation, metalworking, production of construction, sewing, furniture, food and other products.

In addition, Krasnodar is one of the three leaders, along with Moscow and St. Petersburg, in the pace and volume of construction of residential real estate. Such high rates are ensured, including by the developer of the residential complex Kalina-Park - StroyTrest.

Location of the complex

Kalina Park is located in the Prikubansky urban district of the city of Krasnodar, one of the best areas in terms of environmental conditions. The developer tried to create such a home for its customers that would please them with clean air, tranquility and a picturesque view from the window. Speaking specifically about the address at which Kalina-Park is located, this is the Russian village in the northern part of Krasnodar. In fact, this is the same city. The complex is located on the streets of General Petrov, Izmail and Komarov.





How to get to the complex

Getting to the Kalina Park residential complex is no problem or problem. For those who are accustomed to travel by their own vehicles, it is enough to leave the city center on the Rostov highway or on Rossiyskaya street. Then you need to turn onto Running in the first version or Shady in the second. They will lead to Komarova street directly to the residential complex. For those traveling by public transport minibuses are provided, the stop of which is located right next to the complex. The road from the center takes no more than 15-20 minutes.

Infrastructure: internal and external

According to the project of the developer, Kalina-Park (Krasnodar) is located in one of the most favorable areas of the city - the Russian village. From it you can quickly get to anywhere in the city, because there is a lot of public transport.

The territory will be equipped with everything necessary for children's games and adult relaxation. This includes the construction of children's playgrounds and sports grounds for children, walking alleys with comfortable benches along the route for parents. For owners of their own vehicles will be built two outdoor parking lots and parking for 201 cars.

As for the external infrastructure, since Kalina-Park is located in the city, it enjoys the full right to dispose of the entire developed social infrastructure of Krasnodar. On foot you can walk to a comprehensive school, kindergarten, pharmacy, shops. Also nearby are supermarkets and the only 24-hour fitness center in Krasnodar.





Reviews about the complex

"Kalina-Park" managed to collect reviews from residents in sufficient quantities. In any new building, they are both positive and negative.

The positive ones are more related to the excellent choice by the developer of the location of the complex away from industrial enterprises and from the polluted atmosphere of the city, near the lake and the forest. Also pleasing to buyers of apartments is that the houses are not very densely located, which allows the sun to penetrate the windows of the apartments, and the residents can easily walk along the alleys.

Negative reviews are related to construction delays. Some equity holders even turn to the courts to recover a penalty under an equity agreement. Some are not happy with the condition of the roads around the complex. But, probably, this problem will be subsequently solved by the developer.
