Hotel Best Semiramis 5 *: room description

This hotel is an 18-story building in which guests are equipped with 298 rooms. You can choose one of the following options for placement:

  • Standard rooms are comfortable and concise. Double or half beds can accommodate from 2 to 4 guests. There is access to a balcony with sea or mountain views.
  • Superior Senator rooms occupy floors 11 to 14, providing a panoramic view of the sea coast. The apartments are large in size and elegantly decorated. Being planned in the form of a studio, the room combines the areas of the bedroom and living room.

Special offers

The Best Semiramis 5 * hotel has developed a discount system for certain categories of guests. Tourists can take advantage of the following offers:

  • One child under the age of 6 years can stay in the room free of charge, subject to the use of existing beds;
  • booking a room for a period of more than 21 days, guests receive a right to a discount of 12%;
  • tourists over the age of 55 receive a 15% discount on accommodation subject to reservations for a period of 4 days or more;
  • for early bookings, guests are entitled to a 10-15% discount (depending on the season).

It should be borne in mind that guests can take advantage of only one offer that they consider most advantageous (discounts are not cumulative).





Helpful information

If you are going on vacation to the Best Semiramis 5 * hotel, you should familiarize yourself with some of the rules and conditions that apply in it. It is worth paying attention to the following information:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests is carried out from 13:00 to midnight, and eviction from 01:00 to noon;
  • hotel rules do not provide for folding beds in the room for additional accommodation;
  • pets are not allowed;
  • the use of air conditioning is available only from June 1 to September 30;
  • for corporate bookings of 5 or more rooms, it is possible to provide discounts;
  • upon registration, guests will be charged a cash deposit by blocking funds on a credit card;
  • a mandatory requirement for guests is the provision of an identity card with a current photo;
  • self-registration is possible only from the age of 18;
  • the maximum amount of cash payments should not exceed 2500 euros (local law requirement);
  • a prerequisite for visiting the restaurant is compliance with the dress code (for men, long trousers are required);
  • access to the restaurant and spa is possible only after prior booking;
  • The cost of using the safe in the room is 2 euros per day.
