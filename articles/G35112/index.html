Yacht club "Vodnik" (Dolgoprudny): description, photo, address, reviews

Sport, more romantic and status than sailing , probably simply does not exist. Of course, many people would like to learn to manage a yacht. And to do this in our time is completely uncomplicated. In order to learn how to sail, you just need to become a member of a yacht club. There are many such organizations in our country. In addition to the training itself, such sports complexes usually offer after-sales service for vessels owned by citizens. Provide yacht centers to the population and other services - boat trips, the organization of celebrations on ships, etc. One of the oldest such clubs in Russia is Vodnik.

Where is

Finding this sports center for residents and guests of the capital, if desired, will be completely uncomplicated. The club "Vodnik" is located in Dolgoprudny in the suburbs. The easiest way to get to this settlement from the capital is from Savelovsky Station. The distance between Dolgoprudny and this transport hub is only about 18 km.



It is not too difficult to get to the club for sailing enthusiasts or just citizens who want to take a boat trip, and will be in their own car. The distance from MKAD to the club is only 7 km. Motorists need to go to the club along Dmitrovskoye Shosse.

Dolgoprudny is located on the Klyazma River. In addition to Vodnik, there are several more yacht centers in this village. Actually, the Vodnik club itself is located on the banks of the Klyazma reservoir. It is located, according to many vacationers and athletes, in a very picturesque place. Citizens who wish to relax here can find this sports complex at the address: Dolgoprudny, Vodnik Yacht Club, ul. Embankment, 24.

A bit of history

The location of this sports center, most residents of Moscow, therefore, considers it quite convenient. You can get to it from the MKAD in literally 10-15 minutes. But what exactly is this complex itself?

The yacht club Vodnik has been operating in Dolgoprudny for almost a century. It was opened at the beginning of the last century, in 1937. The brothers Lobach-Zhuchenko, former pupils of the school of midshipmen of the imperial fleet, became the initiators of its organization. Thus, at the moment, the Vodnik yacht center is one of the oldest in the Moscow Region.

Club infrastructure for guests

On the territory of this complex, in addition to everything else, there are two hotels (one floating) . Here, those who wish to engage in water sports or simply sunbathe on the beach can rent quite comfortable rooms.

Also, the infrastructure of this club is represented by:

  • convenient outdoor pool;

  • team building area;

  • volleyball court;

  • a sauna;

  • billiard room.





If desired, guests of the complex can rent a yacht here, take part in races, cheer for their favorite team, or simply hold a festive photo shoot on any ship . Of course, there is parking on the territory of Vodnik. Club guests do not need to pay for it. Visitors to the complex can also use free WiFi on site.

In winter, there is a skating rink at the Vodnik Yacht Club (Dolgoprudny) . It also offers dog sledding. In summer, the club hosts animations.

What is offered to yacht owners

The owners of the vessels at the Vodnik Club are provided with the following services:

  • parking of boats and yachts;

  • ship repair up to 7 tons.

Among other things, the club also employs experienced mechanics who can be consulted for ship maintenance, breakdowns, etc. Also, of course, there are courses in navigational studies at Vodnik.

This club has two own harbors. Owners can leave yachts in Vodnik both in summer for short periods and in winter. In the cold season, the club's employees periodically clean snow from the moored vessels.
