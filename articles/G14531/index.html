Walkie-talkies Kenwood: manual, reviews

Kenwood walkie-talkies are one of the most popular accessories on the electronics market, featuring an excellent ratio of quality and price. The most popular are the Kenwood TH and Kenwood TK series - they leave the largest number of positive customer reviews.

The manufacturer strictly controls the quality, reliability, durability and safety of Kenwood walkie-talkies. All products are certified and meet the requirements for such devices. A striking example is the Kenwood Tk-3207 model: it was tested in the most severe operating conditions that meet American standards. The long process of testing the radio included the effects of high and low temperatures, humidity, earthquakes, salty environments and other factors.

A portable glass-type charging cable is supplied with the radios. The battery can be charged separately from the device thanks to the automatic control function. This feature of the radio is beneficial for those involved in mountain sports.

You can buy Kenwood radios both in ordinary stores and on the Internet.





Kenwood TK-F8 Dual

The power consumption of the radio of this model is 8 watts. The radio station has a compact size and the ability to work with dual-band frequencies. It is equipped with an energy-intensive battery with a capacity of 3 thousand mAh.

Like other Kenwood walkie-talkies, this model has a wide range of functions. Available settings for the names of radio stations, channel monitoring, coding, flashlight option. It differs in small dimensions - 5.8 x 11 x 3.2 centimeters. Each frequency can be assigned a specific name. You can use 25 networks and 128 memory channels at the same time. Available 9 levels of sensitivity. How to configure the Kenwood walkie-talkie is described in detail in the operating instructions supplied with the device.

A large and bright screen displays information about the battery charge, activated functions and the received frequency. The user can change the display backlight as desired.

The cost of the radio is 3 thousand rubles.

One of the best walkie-talkies Kenwood, reviews of which confirm its high quality work, affordable price and wide functionality. The device operates with a frequency range from 400 to 470 MHz. Equipped with an energy-intensive 2300 mAh battery. The Kenwood walkie-talkie works simultaneously with 128 channels, it is possible to store the most frequently used frequencies in the device memory. The functionality of the walkie-talkie includes a key lock and password setting for the selected frequencies. Kenwood walkie-talkie settings allow you to expand the range of received frequencies.

The device is equipped with a 2300 mAh lithium-ion battery. The most frequently used channels from the available 128 can be stored in the radio. Wide functionality allows you to lock the keyboard and password-protect individual frequencies. An extension of the reception range is available.

The price of the radio of this model is 3 thousand rubles.

Long battery life is guaranteed by a 3,000 mAh battery. The user can customize the design and change the transmitter power, enable the subscriber recognition function. When the call is displayed, the call sign of the caller. Additional functions of the device - timer, keypad lock, stopwatch, power saving.
