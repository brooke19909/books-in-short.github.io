Hotel Ambiente Hotel 4 (Turkey / Kemer)

Description: Ambiente Hotel 4 is a beachfront hotel with stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea, conveniently located within walking distance from the coast, in the picturesque village of Tekirova, just 23 km from the central part of Kemer. The abundance of greenery, stunning nature and mountains that protect the complex from the scorching sun give this place a special oriental charm and pacifying tranquility - this is a true paradise for lovers.



Hotel Ambiente Hotel 4 consists of a five-story building, which houses fully-equipped and designer-equipped rooms. The complex was opened in 2003 and has already undergone restoration several times - the last update was in 2006. The territory is small in size: beautiful palm trees and green shrubs grow on it. For recreation, two cozy bamboo terraces and an open-air swimming pool were built.

Pension Ambiente Hotel is a quiet, cozy corner where you can isolate yourself from the bustle of the city, enjoy the azure waters, and be alone with yourself. You will not hear the noise from the motorway, since it is far from the building - you are guaranteed peace and quiet. The distance to the airport is approximately 72 km.

Rooms (129): The hotel has large rooms for families with children, as well as luxury apartments. All rooms have balconies with sea views (not all). For a comfortable stay, each room has an individual climate control, a plasma TV (Russian channel is broadcast), and a direct dial telephone.



There is a small bar with drinks (mineral water and drinking water - free of charge), safe (paid service), kettle, hairdryer. In personal use - a bathroom. Cleaning - daily, bed replacement - several times a week.

Beach (private): Just a couple of steps from the main building there is a clean sand and pebble beach with a gentle entrance to the water. All amenities in the form of mattresses, sun loungers and umbrellas are free at Ambiente Hotel 4. Touristic reviews about the private beach are enthusiastic. Fans of diving were delighted with the rich sea world. Fans of outdoor activities appreciated water activities.



Additional information: The infrastructure of Ambiente Hotel 4 is multifaceted. The territory housed not only an outdoor pool, but also a spacious parking lot, a network of shops, car rental, a beauty salon, a hairdresser, a medical office. In addition, there is a reading room, a technically equipped conference room for 120 people.

Among the entertainment services: TV room, billiard room, table tennis, darts, disco club and wireless Internet. A gym, a Turkish bath, and a massage room are open. Aerobics classes are held daily. Program shows are also periodically organized.

We did not forget about the smallest and most active guests, for them a swimming pool, a large play area and a development club were built. A babysitting service is provided for babies.

Digest: A huge plus of the Ambiente Hotel 4 is a chic beach and friendly staff. According to most guests: the hotel is suitable for both business and family holidays. The rich internal infrastructure allows you to find a hobby for every tourist.

Many liked the high-quality and fresh food - there is always a lot of meat, fresh salads and fruits on the tables - you will not stay hungry! The complex is fully consistent with the declared category - excellent price / quality ratio.
