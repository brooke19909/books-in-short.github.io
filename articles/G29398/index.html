Kazan hotels: overview, description, addresses, photos and reviews

The capital of Tatarstan, Kazan is a beautiful and attractive city for tourists. If you want to admire its sights, you can safely come here. Kazan hotels, apartments and guest houses will welcome travelers and offer them comfortable and affordable accommodation conditions.

Hotel "Davydov"

If you want to live in the very center of the city, the Davydov Hotel is at your service. This modern three-star hotel complex offers comfortable rooms, free parking, and Tatar, Russian and European cuisines are presented in the restaurant. Walking 950 meters from the hotel, you can visit the wonderful zoological garden. The Kazan Kremlin is located 4.5 kilometers from the hospitable hotel. The Davydov Hotel in Kazan offers rooms of several categories:

  • Standard double with one double or two single beds. The cost of such a room is from 3000 rubles per one night;
  • standard triple - from 5100 rubles per day;
  • double superior - from 3400 rubles per day;
  • Suite - from 8000 rubles;
  • family suite - from 10,000 rubles;
  • Presidential Suite - from 15,000 rubles.

Each room is equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone, safe, there is access to free wireless Internet. The price includes breakfast, which is served in buffet style. Payment depends on arrival time. The hotel is located at: st. Nursultan Nazarbayev, house 35a.

Hotel "Lampa"

If you want to consider several options for hotels in the center of Kazan, pay attention to the "Lamp". You can walk to the Kazan Kremlin in just 25 minutes on foot. Nearby are such attractions of the city as the Peter and Paul Cathedral and the Church of the Exaltation of the Holy Cross. In the hotel cafe guests will always be pleased with national pastries, as well as European dishes, a children's menu is separately offered. Guests have access to free Wi-Fi throughout. Accommodation is made in the rooms of the following categories:

  • double - 3500 rubles per night;
  • double standard - 4000 rubles per day;
  • double superior - 4500 rubles per day;
  • Triple standard - 5000 rubles.

The price includes breakfast. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, refrigerator and TV, the interior in each of them has an original design solution. The hotel is located at: st. Mayakovsky, 30/16.

Hotel "Maxim Gorky"

Among the hotels in the center of Kazan, tourists and guests of the city often prefer the Maxim Gorky Hotel. Just a 15-minute walk from the hotel are the Kazan Kremlin, Kul Sharif Mosque and the Basket Hall sports arena. At a distance of 1 kilometer is Tukaya Square (the central square of the city). The hotel rooms are bright and spacious. You can dine in the restaurant or cook something in the shared kitchen. Car owners can park their cars in a free fenced parking lot. For accommodation, guests are offered several options:

  • double economy room - 2500 rubles per night;
  • standard double room - 2800 rubles per day;
  • comfort double room - 3200 rubles for one day;
  • Junior Suite - 4000 rubles per day;
  • Suite - 6000 rubles per day;
  • family room (designed for 4 people) - 4500 rubles per night.

All rooms are equipped with TV and air conditioning, there is access to free wireless internet. The hotel is located at: st. Maxim Gorky, house 6.

Hotel Don Quixote

Hotel Don Quixote in Kazan is located next to the railway station (5 minutes walk), tourists can reach the Kremlin in just 15 minutes. The hotel restaurant offers its guests Tatar, Russian and Spanish cuisine. In their free time, guests of the hotel can work out in the fitness center. Car parking is free. The hotel occupies a small beautiful two-story building in the city center and is considered one of the best hotels in Kazan in its location. For accommodation, rooms of the following categories are offered:

  • single standard - 2500 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 4100 rubles for one night;
  • Junior suite with an attic - 4100 rubles per day;
  • Junior Suite - 4500 rubles per day;
  • Luxury - 5100 rubles for some essentials of living.

The price includes breakfast. Each room has air conditioning, TV, minibar, free Wi-Fi. Check-in time is 14.00, checkout time is 12.00. The hotel is located at: st. Burkhan Shahidi, house 11/16.

Hotel Tatar Inn

Among the Kazan hotels located in the city center, I would also like to mention the Tatar Inn hotel. The famous Kazan Kremlin is a 25-minute walk away. The hotel has its own cafe. Parking, unfortunately, is not provided. The rooms are bright and decorated in warm colors. Here you will be offered the following conditions for accommodation:

  • single standard room - 1800 rubles for one day;
  • double standard room - 2600 rubles for one day of stay, if one person settles, the cost will be 2000 rubles;
  • Studio attic room - 3500 rubles per day;
  • luxury attic - 4000 rubles per night.

The price includes breakfast. Each room is equipped with a TV, air conditioning and a fridge. There is free wireless internet access. The hotel is located at: st. Shigabutdina Mardzhani, house 6.

Hotel "Bulgar"

Like other Kazan hotels, near the Bulgar Hotel there are city attractions, among which the popular Mushtari Street can be noted. Guests can relax in the indoor pool and sauna, at an additional cost. Breakfast in the morning is served in a large, bright restaurant. Free parking is available. For accommodation, several categories of rooms are offered:

  • double with a shared bathroom and a bathroom - 2800 rubles per one night;
  • double with its own bathroom - 3200 rubles per day;
  • family - 3,700 rubles per day;
  • Junior Suite - 3900 rubles per day;
  • double comfort - 3900 rubles per day;
  • single rooms are offered from 1700 to 2800 rubles per one night stay.

Room rates include breakfast. Free Wi-Fi is provided. The hotel is located at: st. Vishnevsky, house 21.
