Arriving in any country, I always want to explore the capital, because it is still the main city, the face of the state. Slovenia, Ljubljana ... Having already heard only the name, I want to go there.

The capital of the Republic of Slovenia - Ljubljana - is a small, cozy and real Slavic city. The river divides the settlement into two parts: New Town on the left bank and Old Town on the right.

It is customary to walk around Ljubljana, since urban vehicles are not allowed on the Old Town Square and on many streets. Right bank - a cluster of attractions. It is interesting to see the castle of Ljubljana Castle, which was founded at the turn of the 5-6 centuries and then rebuilt, giving it a new Baroque style, in the 17th century. The masonry of the castle still stores fragments of Illyrian, Celtic and ancient Roman structures. The panoramic platform of the castle is the best place to explore the entire capital from above. In addition, Slovenia, Ljubljana in particular, is famous for Pre≈°eren Square, where the Franciscan church of the 17th century is located. Its facade is made in the Baroque style. The covered city market is another place worth your visit.
The most central part of the capital is Tromostovye or three pedestrian bridges decorated with dragons and passing through the Ljubljana River. Ljubljana is especially beautiful in the evening - an endless variety of cozy cafes and restaurants, bars and discos. Even despite many years spent under the control of the Austro-Hungarian Empire, the city retained its unique flavor and atmosphere, and remained truly Slavic.

Cuisine and restaurants

Slovenia, Ljubljana in particular, will offer you to try real Slovenian cuisine. Try the local bread and soups: sour juha - from pork, chevapchichi - with sausages, vipavska iota - cabbage from sauerkraut, ribya wandering - ear. It is interesting to try dumplings, stuffed peppers, buckwheat porridge, pork sausages with horseradish and onions. And, of course, we must not forget about wine, because it is known all over the world.



Shopping

Anyone who comes here for shopping will not be disappointed, because here you can buy high-quality clothes of various designers, including Slovenian. Ljubljana sells many interesting souvenirs, dishes and antiques. Most stores are located in the northern part of the city. Here is also located one of the largest shopping centers in Europe, BTC City. On the covered market you can buy vegetables, spices, fruits and, of course, Slovenian delicacies. For those who love painting, we recommend visiting the galleries located in the city center. There you can purchase reproductions of famous paintings. Have a magical trip!
