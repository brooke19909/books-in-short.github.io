How to send a transfer

To send funds to relatives or friends, you must:

  1. Visit the office of a financial company that carries out transfers through the Contact system.
  2. Present a passport to an employee of the company.
  3. Provide information about the recipient to the financial company specialist: full name, country of dispatch, currency of receipt, amount to be sent, and contact details.
  4. Sign the application for the transfer of funds, having previously checked all personal information, deposit funds to the cashier and receive a check.

How to get a transfer



To receive a transfer, you must do the following:

  2. Present a passport or other document that is included in the liver of identity documents.

  5. Get cash at the checkout.

Money transfers "Contact"

Throughout the territory of the Russian Federation there is no limit on the amount of transfer. As for foreign currency, here the system sets restrictions - no more than 5,000 dollars of transfer per day.

Sending a transfer is carried out in ruble equivalent. In this case, the sender can convert and indicate any currency received.

Do not forget about the commission that is charged to the sender. The size of the commission is fixed and set by the tariffs of the monetary system, and its size directly depends on the amount of transfer.

Registration of a translation takes no more than 5 minutes. Immediately after sending, the recipient can contact any nearest branch and receive money.
