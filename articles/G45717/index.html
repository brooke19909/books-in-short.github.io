Do-it-yourself tablet case

Having purchased a tablet, you should definitely take care of its protection. To do this, you should buy a special case. He will be able to protect the gadget from dirt and dust. On sale there is a huge number of covers made of a wide variety of materials of various colors and sizes. It would seem that choosing the right product is not difficult. However, as practice shows, despite such an abundance, it is not so easy to find a model that completely suits the color, size and workmanship. If the suitable option was not found, then you can make a cover for the tablet with your own hands.



To begin with, it is very necessary to measure the size of the gadget. Treat this stage is with all responsibility.

After all, if a mistake is made, then the finished cover can either not be put on the tablet, or the gadget will drop out of it.

Having made a measurement, it is worth making a pattern, in accordance with which the cover for the tablet will be subsequently made with your own hands.

In this case, it is necessary to take into account not only the length and width of the gadget, but also its thickness.

You can make a tablet case for your tablet in various ways. If you own sewing, then you can use this method. To do this, you should purchase a suitable fabric. It is preferable to use natural materials such as cotton and linen for this purpose. The size of tissue acquired will depend on several factors. However, the main, of course, are the parameters of the gadget itself.



If the ability to sew beautifully and efficiently is not your main advantage, but you know the basics of knitting, you can make a cover for a 7-inch tablet in this way.

It doesn‚Äôt matter if you are good at knitting or crocheting.

Both of these knitting methods allow you to create a truly unique product.

The main thing is to choose the right yarn and choose the right pattern.

It is preferable that the composition of the material used includes the maximum possible number of natural ingredients, such as cotton, linen or wool.



You can tie the cover for the tablet with your own hands on four knitting needles. In this case, the finished product will not have side seams. If desired, you can use two knitting needles, and use the line along which the seam should be formed as a decorative element. To do this, you can form holes in advance along the very edge of the fabric, into which you will then need to insert a decorative lace.

A 10-inch tablet case, connected with a hook, looks more beautiful. Such a product, depending on the pattern, can turn out to be quite dense and tough. Especially if you tie it with a single crochet. However, if desired, you can use a different pattern. You can even give preference to lace, but the likelihood that various dust will fall on your gadget will be very high in this case.

You can decorate the finished product in various ways. An ideal option would be applique or embroidery. Such a cover will definitely turn out beautiful, and you might even want to give it to your loved one.
