Corfu, "Messonghi Beach Resort" (Messonghi Beach Resort 3 *): tourist reviews, photo rooms, tours

The Greek island of Corfu can present a real fairy tale to tourists . Messonghi Beach is a luxury hotel where everyone will feel at home. The hotel complex consists of three buildings of different floors, as well as several cozy bungalows. You will have a unique opportunity to relax on the beach.





Resort Location

Many dream of spending their holidays on the eastern shore of the Greek island of Corfu. "Messongi Beach" is located on the shores of the Ionian Sea in a beautiful area that is literally immersed in the greenery of exotic plants. In the tourist village of Messongi, guests have access to a developed resort infrastructure. The same can be said of the nearby village of Moraitika.

A bus stop is located near the Messongi Beach Hotel. In 45 minutes you will reach the picturesque Corfu, the fabulous Kavos or the mysterious Lefkimi. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many shops, cafes and entertainment venues that will open up great opportunities for you to relax.

Messongi Beach Hotel (Corfu) - rooms

A vacation does not happen with us so often, and therefore it must be carefully planned in order to receive only positive impressions. If you love the sea, the sun and exotic, then you should definitely go to relax on Corfu. Messongi Beach offers its guests to stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Double "Standard" offers its guests comfortable accommodation in small beds. Room size also allows you to place an extra bed or cot. The bathroom has a shower. From the balcony you can enjoy wonderful views of the garden or the sea.
  • Family standard provides a huge room, designed to accommodate four people. There is a double bed, as well as a bunk bed. Additional guests can comfortably sit on a fold-out sofa.
  • Superior family apartments consist of two rooms - children's and adult bedrooms. Each of them is adjoined by a separate bathroom, which creates additional comfort.
  • You can enjoy a comfortable secluded holiday in modern bungalows. They can be in the format of standard or family rooms. The main advantage of this accommodation option is its own terrace with sun loungers, as well as a small garden.

An absolute sense of comfort will accompany you at Messonghi Beach (hotel, Corfu). The photo of the rooms demonstrates a restrained authentic style, radiating warmth and homeliness.





Amenities at Guest Apartments

A warm welcome and an unforgettable vacation will give you the Greek island of Corfu. "Messongi Beach" takes care of the comfort of its guests, and therefore the following list of amenities is provided in the rooms:

  • spacious bathroom with shower, electric hairdryer, bath accessories and branded cosmetics;
  • own spacious terrace or balcony with comfortable garden furniture, as well as sunshades;

Sea vacation

Greece is a great place for sea vacations, and therefore summer tours are in great demand. Hotel "Messongi Beach" (Corfu) is located on the first coastline, which is its undeniable advantage. You will certainly be pleased with a huge and well-groomed sand and pebble beach. Due to the gentle entrance to the sea, the depth increases gradually, without sharp drops. This is important not only for children's recreation, but also for the safety of those tourists who do not swim very well. For an additional fee, you can rent a sun lounger (1.5 euros) for the whole day, as well as a parasol (6 euros).

There is a bar on the beach where you can buy refreshing drinks, as well as light snacks. Thanks to free Wi-Fi, you will always stay in touch with loved ones. Having crossed the bridge over the Messongu River, you will find yourself on the second beach, which is considered more civilized. Here, among other things, there are toilets and showers. If you do not want to overpay for the use of sunbeds and awnings, then you can simply swim on the beach, and you can take sunbaths near the pool, where inventory is provided absolutely free.

Opportunities for families with children

The mild climate, beautiful nature and fresh air are some of the main advantages of such a tourist destination as Greece, Corfu. Messongi Beach is often chosen for family vacations, because all the possibilities are provided here not only for adults, but also for children. So, the following infrastructure is available to young tourists:

  • two pools of shallow depth with rides and safe children's slides;
  • in the shade of the green garden a large playground with swings, a sandbox, horizontal bars and other entertainments is equipped;
  • for kids from 4 to 12 years old there is a mini-club in which they will have a great time in the company of funny animators;
  • if you need to leave, a qualified nanny will look after your children.

For families, Messonghi Beach is just the perfect option. You can not worry about the leisure and safety of your children. While young tourists will enjoy fun activities, you can relax and forget about household chores.
