How to disable ads on the "Android" in the phone: methods and instructions

There are not so many ways to disable ads on an Android device, but almost all of them imply knowledge of the platform, that is, they are intended for experienced users. Here we are talking about a thorough study of OS files for the presence of virus code and its subsequent destruction.

Well, for beginners and those who do not want to mess with system files and pick out viruses from the registry, there is a special software that allows you to remove ads on your Android phone. In our article, we will analyze the programs that allow you to disable ads on your Android phone. Consider the most popular, and most importantly, effective applications that have a large number of positive reviews from users.

It is immediately worth noting that such software allows you to disable and block, but not remove the adware virus from Android. For this, you need to turn to complex products like Kaspersky, Doctor Web, and similar. The solutions described below are precisely the blockers of windows, banners and other spam, and not a full-fledged antivirus that allows you to remove adware viruses from your Android phone.

Adblock Plus (ABP)

This is one of the most effective, and at the same time, popular products with lots of positive reviews. It helps to completely disable ads on the Android phone. The application is widely known as an extension for desktop browsers, but since 2012 a full version of the utility has appeared on mobile gadgets.

The application includes many filters, and very flexible. So if you do not need full protection from advertising, you will need to configure AdBlock Plus on Android. This part deals mainly with the fine-tuning of the proxy server. For some users, mainly those who are in the "enemy camp" and engaged in the layout of banners, this will be very helpful. In addition, not all advertising can be called aggressive, a small part of it can be really useful.

It is also worth noting that if you do not need a full-fledged blocker for applications, then you can use Adblock Plus as a plug-in for the browser. This application is no less popular than the previous utility, which allows you to completely block ads on your Android phone. Adguard can be called a complete solution. It includes not only a spam blocker, but also a firewall with an anti-phishing module.

Netguard

The developer positions his utility as a firewall for mobile devices running on the Android platform. The application allows you to flexibly configure the rules for both incoming and outgoing traffic.

With the latest updates, the product has an ad blocking feature. The utility protects your mobile gadget from pop-ups, malicious links and prevents accidental clicks on aggressive banners.

DNS66

This small utility also acts as a firewall, using a local VPN to disable ads. The application works with DNS protocols and filters traffic only when a connection is established. This approach has its pros and cons.

A clear advantage of running the program through DNS is a significant saving in battery power. That is, the application is activated and uses system resources, and with them the battery, only when your gadget is connected to the network.

The disadvantage is the fact that the utility has to be activated every time again, after the gadget goes into sleep mode.
