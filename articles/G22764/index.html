How to get CTP payments

A feature of compulsory liability insurance is that CTP does not insure the car, but the liability of its owner to third parties. That is, the policy will compensate the harm caused to other participants in the movement. The cost of repairing a car in an accident is not compensated for by this type of insurance.

Only the injured party will receive money from the insurance, and not the culprit of the accident. The only exception when, as a result of an accident, he can receive payments under compulsory motor third-party liability insurance is mutual guilt.

On April 19, 2013, the State Duma adopted a bill according to which payments under compulsory motor liability insurance will increase. The increase in insurance amounts will occur in stages from 160 to 500 thousand rubles per victim. Also, payments for compensation for damage to property will increase from 120 to 400 thousand rubles. Victims will have the right to choose whether to receive compensation in money or to be repaired at the service station.

It must be understood that in order to quickly and surely receive CTP payments from the insurance company, you need to start acting immediately after the accident. First of all, it is required to properly execute the accident, correctly write a statement, collect all the certificates and successfully pass the examination.

The more serious the accident, the more time all these procedures will take. Any error in the paperwork may become a reason for refusal of compensation.

In the event of a serious accident, it is important to immediately find witnesses to the accident. Call the traffic police immediately. Before their arrival, inspect the car yourself to know what damage should be described in the documents. If some of them are not reflected, CTP payments in your favor will be lower.

While waiting for the crew to call, call your insurance company. Explain the situation to the operator and follow his recommendations. That is, the amount indicated in the examination is likely to be greater than the amount that you can actually get from the insurance organization.
