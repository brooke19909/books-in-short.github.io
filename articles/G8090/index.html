Grand Seas Hostmark Resort, Hurghada, Egypt: overview, description, features and reviews

It is no secret that Hurghada is one of the most famous and popular resorts in Egypt. This is where travelers can count on comfort, entertainment and genuine hospitality. On the territory of this resort area are thousands of hotels of various categories. One of them is the Grand Seas Hostmark Resort complex. So what conditions and service does the hotel offer? Is it possible to have fun here with a child? What reviews do tourists who have already managed to visit here leave? Answers to these questions will be useful to many.

Location Description





The large hotel complex Grand Seas Hostmark 5 * is located almost on the very shore of the Red Sea, in a picturesque park, in the famous resort area called Hurghada. By the way, the distance to the city center with all its restaurants, historical sights and entertainment is 15 km - you can get here by taxi or using public transport. The advantage of the hotel is that the international airport is located only 10 km, which means that the transfer will take you to the hotel in 20-30 minutes.

Catering for tourists





The Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 5 * hotel, as well as most hotels in Hurghada, provides its guests with full-fledged meals. Breakfasts, lunches and dinners are held in the form of a buffet - buffet is served for guests, so each visitor can choose their own food. It serves international, Italian, American, Eastern and Mediterranean dishes. Of course, a sufficient amount of fresh vegetables, fruits and delicious oriental pastries are always served to the table. Quite often themed evenings are held on the territory of the main restaurant of the hotel, dedicated to the cuisine and traditions of a country.

Paying for accommodation at the Grand Seas Hostmark Resort, guests can receive free drinks and light snacks in the hotel bars, which are located on the beach, by the pool and in the lobby of the main building. By the way, you need to pay for imported drinks separately. You can spend the evening in the disco bar, enjoying not only oriental drinks, but also incendiary music.

Where is the beach located? Water leisure travelers





The warm sea and long clean beaches are what Hurghada is famous for the whole world. Grand Seas Resort Hostmark owns a private stretch of beach, which is located in close proximity - you can reach it in a few minutes. The beach, although not too big, but private, and only hotel guests rest here.

The coast is covered with sand, and the entry into the sea is cleared. Nevertheless, tourists are advised to wear special shoes, as a live coral reef is located nearby. On the shore there are comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas, which will come in handy during the heat of lunch. Here, guests receive clean beach towels. And on the beach there is a bar where you can order a refreshing drink and even a snack.

There will be something to do for fans of more active recreation. For example, the beach has an excellent volleyball court. Here, tourists at a reasonable price can ride a boat, jet skis, bicycles and ski, go sailing, try parasailing and windsurfing, admire the underwater world, snorkeling or making full dives in the company of experienced divers.

Conditions for families with children

For parents who prefer to travel with their children, questions about the availability of appropriate conditions are important. What entertainment can a child offer the Grand Seas Resort Hostmark 5 * hotel? Hurghada is a wonderful city for a full family vacation. On the territory of the hotel complex there are two playgrounds with swings, slides, sandboxes - children of different ages have a good time here. Every day there is a children's club where your child will be able to play, dance and draw, take part in competitions, contests and performances. In the central pool there is a special compartment where the smallest tourists can splash around - it is safe here, and the water is always well warmed up.

What additional services does the hotel offer?





Of course, guests are offered some additional services that can greatly facilitate their lives. For example, a currency exchange office is constantly working here. Guests are invited to visit the local shopping gallery, where you can buy almost everything from beach slippers and other household items, ending with model clothes, jewelry and original souvenirs.

Laundry or dry cleaning services are available at a reasonable price. There is a large parking area for cars. Free access to the Internet is in the main building. There is also an internet cafe. There is also a spacious conference room, designed for a simultaneous stay of 150 people - here employees will help to organize a business meeting or seminar, as well as a fun holiday or even a wedding.

Leisure and entertainment for guests





Some tourists indicate that the hotel is not so much entertainment. Nevertheless, everyone can find an occupation. The courtyard is occupied by a large freshwater pool where you can swim, freshen up or play water polo. On the terrace you can relax, relaxing on a sun lounger, or just chat with new neighbors.

Guests can also have a good time on one of the two tennis courts, which, incidentally, are illuminated in the evening. A fitness center is constantly working, where various group sports classes are regularly held. There is also a gym, a sauna, where you can steam muscles before training, a jacuzzi and massage rooms.

The hotel has a team of animators who entertain guests throughout the day, organizing various contests and fun competitions. In the evenings you can admire the performances of local groups and exotic oriental dances. In addition, you will always have the opportunity to have fun and interesting time in the city center. And do not forget that Egypt is a country with an ancient history, but because it is the best suited for sightseeing holidays.
