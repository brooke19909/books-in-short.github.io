Pamukkale

One of the most anticipated tours by tourists is to the Pamukkale Natural History Museum. This place, called the "cotton castle", is hard to miss. It stands out from afar with its white color. His beauty was appreciated before our era. An antique pool was built here. Numerous picturesque travertine baths in ancient times were surrounded by a portico with columns (time turned out to be stronger, the ancient structure was destroyed).



The water of the baths is exceptionally pure, mineral (with bubbles) and very warm. The main thermal spring of Pamukkale is romantically called the Cleopatra Basin. It is quite spacious, has a shallow and deep part. Outside there are natural baths - travertines. They are covered with picturesque snow-white deposits of salt. However, the legendary queen never visited this place. And her name brings a raid of mystery to this object. Water in the pool is heated by a geyser. Above it, the ancients built Roman baths, which, unfortunately, could not stand the test of time. Their ruined columns still lie here, creating a special flavor of antiquity to this miracle of nature.

Discovery Park

One of the favorite places for tourists to visit in Antalya is Discovery Park. Traditionally, adults and children are attracted by giant dinosaur figures. They are quite complex mechanized devices with automatic control elements. In particular, the breath of ancient dinosaurs is imitated. The motion sensors installed in their bodies capture the movement of oncoming spectators and make these giant mannequins move and make a sound. It looks impressive and the kids really like it.

In addition to demonstrating the models of giant dinosaurs who lived on Earth millions of years ago, an exhibition of sand figures is regularly shown at Discovery Park.





Here, in the combined terrarium-aquarium, viewers have the opportunity to personally see various snakes, spiders and very photogenic fish. There are children's attractions, there is where to eat.

In memory of their stay in this fabulous place, visitors take pictures with a parrot, iguana, lion cub.

Ich kale

The preserved Byzantine fortress Ich-Kale with powerful fortress walls erected around the perimeter of 7 km deserves special attention. Inside the ancient stronghold, visitors can see structures built 800 years ago: churches, castle ruins, water tanks.

Grotto Dalmatash

The creation of nature, created more than 15 thousand years ago and located in the vicinity of Alanya, is the Dalmatash Grotto. It is located below sea level. It is distinguished by a constant year-round temperature of + 22 0 C. In the grotto, the stunning spectacle of stalactites and stalagmites is enhanced by special lighting. This place has a story connected with piracy: here once corsairs hid from prying eyes.
