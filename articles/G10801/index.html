Lake Akakul (Chelyabinsk region). Recreation and fishing

Lake Akakul (Chelyabinsk region) is located in the Argayash region. It has an elongated shape: from north-west - to the southeast. The lake is quite large: length - about 5 kilometers, width - 3, surface area of ​​the water - more than 10 km 2 . Akakul is a sewage reservoir, through a system of channels, streams and swamps, it discharges its waters into Ulagach. The average depth is about five meters, the maximum is eleven. There are no large settlements on the shores of the lake. Lake Akakul (Chelyabinsk region) is a place of rest. On its shores are numerous children's camps and recreation centers. But on the south bank there is a water intake station, a subsidiary farm is located, stone quarries function. One kilometer from the lake is the Akakulsky tract - this is a wetland. Also, swamps and thickets of reeds are characteristic of the northwestern coast. The catchment area is more than 50 square kilometers.





Pond ecology

The water in the lake is clean. Mineralization - not more than 300 mg / liter. The chemical composition of water belongs to the hydrocarbon class according to the soda group and the magnesium group. By softness, water belongs to the first category. Around the lake grows a mixed forest (linden, pine and birch). It has a lot of mushrooms and berries, which can be collected directly on the territory of recreational facilities. Small rocks are scattered in the forest. Fruit-bearing trees and shrubs grow: mountain ash, cherry, apple tree, raspberry and bird cherry. In the forest, birds, squirrels and hedgehogs are found in large numbers. In summer, the water of the lake warms up well and remains warm until autumn colds. Due to the lack of industrial enterprises, the composition of water and a lot of vegetation, the air on Akakul is clean and fresh. It has a beneficial effect on the work of all organs and systems of the human body. The bottom of the reservoir is mostly muddy. On the beaches they clean it, there the bottom is sandy. The east coast is steep and rocky. There are 3 stone capes and islands covered with vegetation.





Fishing

Lake Akakul (Chelyabinsk region) is rich in fauna; fishing attracts lovers not only from the Southern Urals, but also from all over Russia. Pike, roach, ruff, ruff, whitefish, perch, crucian carp, ripus, bream and many others are found in huge numbers. Rakus and whitefish Akakul is stocked annually. It is believed that the largest number of fish species in the entire area is found in it. Crayfish are also found here. You can fish both from the shore and from a boat, but at a depth the production is larger. You can stay on the lake "savage" in a tent, and you can order a fisherman's house. This is a 2-storey cottage where even a large company will be spacious. There is a bathhouse, billiards and a barbecue area. You can rent boats. Lake Akagul (Chelyabinsk region) is loved by many fishermen, some come here annually.





Relaxation

Beautiful and picturesque lake Akakul (Chelyabinsk region), photos completely convey the charm of this reservoir. In addition to this factor, recreational use leads to high water quality. Numerous summer children's camps and recreation centers offer numerous entertainment and pastimes: swimming pools, baths, sports and playgrounds, dance floors, restaurants, coffee houses, shops, cafes, boat rental, catamarans, cars and much more. Almost all institutions allow you to bring pets with you. The price range is very wide, including depending on beautification and season. Friendly lake Akakul (Chelyabinsk region), reviews of vacationers are exclusively positive.





How to get there

Lake Akakul (Chelyabinsk region) is located near the cities of Ozersk and Kyshtym. This is the northwest of the area. Federal highways do not pass here, and you can get to the place only by personal transport. From Chelyabinsk, you must first follow the M5 highway to Yekaterinburg, pass the sign for Dolgoderevenskoye and turn on the very first sign directing to Argayash. In this village you need to turn towards Kyshtym, on the way to the city about 2/3 of the way there is a lake. It is about 80 kilometers from Chelyabinsk, that is, 1.5 hours of travel. From Yekaterinburg to the lake - 160 kilometers. Route of the road: Yekaterinburg-Bolshoi Istok-Oktyabrsky-Tyubuk-Sysert-Kasli-Kyshtym. Then turn to Argayash, where the lake is located on the left side.

Lake District

The Chelyabinsk region is rightly called the lake region, on its territory there are more than 3.7 thousand reservoirs of various sizes. Some of them are mountainous, others are forest-steppe. Lake Akakul (Chelyabinsk region) is one of the most attractive places for tourists and fishermen. There are no attractions here. But there is clean air, healthy water and lots of fish. The lack of civilization determines the ecological cleanliness of this beautiful place. Vacation packages to recreation facilities must be ordered in advance. And in the summer on the banks of the reservoir grows a tent city.
