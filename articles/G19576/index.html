"Edelweiss", Abzakovo: address, room selection, ease of booking, quality of service

The hotel complex "Edelweiss" (Abzakovo) is a ski hotel located in Bashkortostan, in the Southern Urals. But in the summer there is a very beautiful nature, which is favorable for walking.

This place is loved by snowboarders, skiers, and everyone who enjoys winter sports. Here you can be accommodated in the main building or guest houses, designed for large companies.

The hotel has an excellent Apre-ski service. Let's see what the guests say in the reviews about the Edelweiss in Abzakovo. After all, this small hotel has become popular with vacationers for several years.

How to get there

The nearest major city near this winter resort is Magnitogorsk. It is located 60 kilometers from Abzakovo.

The train station is a 20-minute walk from the Edelweiss Ski Hotel in Abzakovo. You can get there from Moscow by the Southern Ural train in a day and a half. The train leaves from the Kazan station.

The plane flies only to Magnitogorsk about 2.5 hours. There are flights from both Vnukovo and Sheremetyevo.

But from Magnitogorsk to Abzakovo can only be reached by taxi or order a transfer to the hotel. Otherwise, problems will arise. Private pick-ups are not common here, and taxis may be busy.

You can also drive to the resort's railway station by train from Magnitogorsk. There are minibuses from the airport.

Abzakovo

Where is the Edelweiss Hotel located? Abzakovo is a famous Russian ski resort. The hotel is 800 meters from its lifts. The ski center is located on the slopes of the South Ural Krykty-Tau ridge, not far from Shaitan Mountain.

In recent years, the entire leisure infrastructure has completely changed here, so Abzakovo has gained all-Russian popularity. They ride here from November to early May.

Professional athletes are training in Abzakovo. Various festivals and competitions are often held there.

Territory and infrastructure

Hotel "Edelweiss" in Abzakovo is a small town for relaxation. Its territory is completely fenced. It is guarded around the clock, so strangers will not get there.

The complex has a parking lot for guests' cars. The hotel often hosts seminars and conferences. For such purposes, the hotel administration can provide an equipped room for 40 people, where there is all the necessary equipment for presentations.

There are also rooms for business meetings or group work. The hotel has a 24-hour convenience store. Near the hotel there is a ski bass stop, which will take you to the ski lifts in a couple of minutes.

Reservation

Do you want to come here to relax together, with children, a large company or family? Then you should call the Edelweiss Hotel (Abzakovo) in advance by phone +7 (3519) 43-88-19. Then you can pre-order a roomy guest house or a room of your choice.

The fact is that in the season - both in winter and in summer - they are, as a rule, already occupied. You can book your stay online either on the website of the hotel itself or on tour operators. But travelers are still advised to book rooms directly by calling directly to the administration of the complex.

In order to book your stay on the hotel website, you need to enter the dates you need on the corresponding page. Then information will appear on what types of numbers are available and how much they cost at a given time.

Other types of housing

There are several guest houses in Edelweiss that are fully rented. On the ground floor of one of them there is a corridor, an entrance hall, an equipped kitchen, a bedroom, a sitting area with a TV and a sofa, as well as a separate bathroom with a shower.

Three more rooms and a living room are on the second level. There is another bathroom. In total, such a house can accommodate 16-20 people in 5 rooms. A microwave is available for hire.

The house has a Russian bath, but they use it for an additional fee. Linen is included, but towels are not provided.

The second guest house is designed for 20-25 people. It consists of 8 rooms and two floors. On the first floor there is not only an entrance hall, but also a kitchen connected to the dining room.

The house has 7 bedrooms, designed to accommodate from 2 to 5 people each, 3 bathrooms. Huge guest room with beautiful sofas and fireplace. It is good to spend evenings here. Dishes and bedding (except towels) are provided.

The complex also owns several houses outside the hotel, in the center of the village of Novoabzakovo, next to which there is a resort.

Excursions

The staff of the Edelweiss Hotel in Abzakovo developed special routes for summer guests, which allow them not only to have fun and get to know the wonderful local nature, but also to plunge into the world of Bashkir culture.

This type of service refers to additional services. Tourists write that such excursions give them the opportunity to learn about life, cuisine, traditions of this people, as well as listen to music.

There are many thematic group travels. These are panoramic excursions, with access to viewing platforms, and hiking in the woods and mountains, and bicycle tours. You can visit the ski centers that are nearby - "Abzakovo" and "Metallurg".

Those who wish can go on an excursion to Magnitogorsk. And for extremals a collective rafting on the Belaya River is provided.

Fans of historical and natural attractions remembered a trip to the archaeological sites of the South Urals, as well as to the Shulgan-Tash nature reserve.
