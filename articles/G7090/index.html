Hotel Himeros Life Hotel 4 *, Kemer, Turkey: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Homer once wrote in the Iliad that it is here that the Chimera lives - an absurd creature with a lion's head, goat's body and dragon's tail. Historians believe that it was from this monster that Kemer got its name. It is not known whether this amazing creature actually existed, but not far from this Turkish resort there really is a mountain called Chimera, the slopes of which are closed by burning gas coming out from the ground. Kemer has become famous among tourists relatively recently. The resort with developed infrastructure appeared only some two and a half decades ago. And in the sixties here, between the azure sea and the mountains, there was a very small fishing village. They say that even by car it was very difficult to come here, because there were simply no roads.



Overview

Another thing is the current Kemer. It is called the universal resort capital. The city has changed so much that today it can compete with many European counterparts. The tourist infrastructure here is quite developed. The range of entertainment provided by visitors is very wide: it includes foam parties, and ‚Äúacidic‚Äù discos, an abundance of hammams, where massage therapists practically work wonders, a dolphinarium, a small water park.

There are a lot of hotels in Kemer. The largest of them are located on the outskirts of the resort. In general, the city is surrounded by amazing nature. Some hotels are a true oasis for relaxation with progressive architecture and landscape design. Decent service, the availability of many entertainment for both adults and children, a variety of food - all this awaits guests here who choose their final choice on Kemer (Turkey).



Hotel Himeros Life Hotel

The 4-star hotel complex, which is positioned by tour operators as suitable for any category of tourists, is located in the very center of the resort, surrounded by a dense pine forest. The hotel was built back in 2004. Today it is beautifully renovated and fully corresponds to the category for which it is declared.

Himeros Life Hotel 4 * is one kilometer distant from the seashore. The center of neighboring Antalya is forty kilometers, to the nearby airport, located in the same place, is an hour's drive. The location of the hotel was chosen quite well: there are many shops, restaurants and inexpensive cafes nearby. Judging by the reviews, many Russians choose Kemer because of the mild climate. The heat here is almost not felt due to the surrounding mountains and pine forests.





The territory of the Himeros Life Hotel 4 * is small, but well-maintained. This "drawback" is characterized by all hotels located in the center of Kemer. The road to the sea passes along the main street, so some remoteness of the beach is practically not felt.

Infrastructure

Himeros Life Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer) is perfect for family people and business travelers. Its infrastructure includes a small conference room, a cozy lobby. There is also a depository safe, for the rental of cells of which you must pay separately, a left-luggage office, dry cleaning. Laundry and shoe shine facilities are available. On the ground floor of the only three-story building, there is an opportunity for currency exchange, there is an ATM, a coffee machine. Parking must be reserved in advance.

The reception is open around the clock. There is a service of express check-in. Check-in, according to the rules at the Himeros Life Hotel 4 *, is scheduled for fourteen, and check-out is at twelve in the afternoon.

On its territory there is a shop where residents can purchase basic necessities, including bathing accessories.

About nutrition

Himeros Life Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer) operates on an all-inclusive basis. Dining is provided in the main indoor restaurant. In addition, guests can dine on the terrace by prior reservation. Tables must be reserved at the reception desk. On the territory of the hotel, by the pool, there is a bar, where those who buy tours of the all-inclusive concept are provided with free snacks and drinks produced at local enterprises. At the bar there is a free apparatus for cold water. The buffet is always full of vegetables and dishes from them, a lot of fruit.

For children

Himeros Life Hotel (Kemer) is a 4-star property. There are also services for children. Parents can get an extra bed in the room upon request. In addition, upon check-in, it is necessary to warn the registrar that the child needs diet food.



For children in the main pool there is a shallow compartment and slides. If for some reason parents need to go about their business, they can use the paid babysitting service.

Excursions

Himeros Life Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Center) has an excellent location. This circumstance in their reviews was appreciated by those who prefer not only to relax, but also to combine idle pastime with the study of local attractions, which are abundant in this resort.

Sightseeing tours can be purchased both at the Himeros Life Hotel 4 * and from street vendors. Tourists are invited directly from the hotel to go on a jeep safari to the mountains or just ride around the donkeys. In the summer, many attended concerts by the famous singer Tarkan, who often tours along this coast during the high season.

Russians will enjoy an excursion to the ancient city of Mira. Here, judging by the chronicles, the bishop was St. Nicholas the Wonderworker. Some, having rented a car at the hotel, drove all day in Pamukkale, where you can swim in the miraculous mineral springs or just wander around the caves of Cappadocia.

Beach

Himeros Life Hotel 4 * (Kemer) is somewhat remote from the city beach. You need to go to him for half an hour. Judging by the reviews of vacationers, the road passes along the main street, past many shops and cafes, so it is not tiring. The beach is well equipped. Sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses are available for a fee. The beach is sandy, so no rubber slippers are needed. The entrance to the water is gentle, which is important for young children.
