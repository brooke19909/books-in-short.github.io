Goat pattern. How to sew a toy with your own hands. Master Class

Children's psychologists say that any baby must have soft toys. Their creation is a fascinating and interesting activity. Having acquired the fabric and the necessary sewing tools in a specialized store, you will be able to implement all your ideas without any help, following the instructions. In our article, a goat pattern is proposed, as well as instructions for sewing it.





Sewing Preparation

It is unlikely that at least one person in the world is found who would be indifferent to toys. Beautiful soft creatures attract the attention of both babies and older children. They cause exclamations of tenderness, delight and cheerful smiles. Toys that are sewn on their own are especially fond of. Little lambs, goats, sewn by the mother‚Äôs hands are the best gift for the baby, because she puts all the warmth of her own soul into it.

The article provides a pattern of a goat toy, using which you can sew a very cute Luibmitz to your child. Creating your own soft toy, you must decide what size it should be. In addition, the goat can move its legs, stand or just sit. A self-created goat pattern and a toy sewn with its help have a lot of advantages:

  • work with children brings family members together;
  • old stocks of shreds are reduced;
  • sewn soft toy is a great gift for loved ones.

When attracting kids to work, do not forget about safety. Needles are best stored in a box with a magnet and counted, it is advisable to thread a thread into each. Do not trust the guys with all the devices, especially scissors. It is better to give toys that have blunt ends, and keep the hot iron away.





What to prepare

To sew a toy you will need the following:

  • suitable soft fabric imitating wool;
  • material for the manufacture of ears, muzzles and paws;
  • filler - synthetic winterizer;
  • threads to match the fabric;
  • pins
  • buttons for the eye;
  • floss thread for embroidery muzzle;
  • a simple pencil or marker for drawing markings;
  • paper for a pattern;
  • scissors for fabric.

Redraw the silhouettes of all the necessary components of the shape you like on a sheet of paper (goat pattern is given in the article), enlarging it to fit your size. Cut out all the details carefully, then place the resulting parts on a fabric laid out on the wrong side up and circle around the contour. After that, cut out the details of the future goat from the material, departing from the created silhouette by 0.5-1 cm (stock for the seam). The size of the allowance depends on the volume of the components of the created toy. The goat pattern may be delayed for the next toy.

We sew a head





Before collecting the goat, sew on the place where there will be a muzzle, eyes, highlight the mouth, nose, eyebrows and eyelashes. Then sew the parts of the ears, folding the parts with the wrong sides. Along the seam, make notches with scissors so that there are no ugly assemblies on the outside. Next, turn your ears to the outside. Sutures need to be straightened from the inside with a narrow needle or pencil. Connect the external sections with each other and attach the ears with tailor pins to the top of the head with the face, sew along the edge, then turn the part of the head to the outside and smooth the seams. Often the pattern of a goat toy at this stage involves moderate filling of the head with syntepon to the base of the neck. An open cut is sutured.

Torso and legs





Align the parts of the body with the wrong sides facing out and sew together. Leave a hole so that you can easily stuff the toy. Turn the torso through an un sewn section and fill it with a synthetic winterizer. Sew the slit with a blind stitch. Fold 8 parts in pairs, which will make 4 legs. Leave a hole, turn out and fill with filler. Sew the cut, attach the legs with pins to the body, determining the location. Connect the limbs to the body with a hidden seam. Work with the tail in the same way. It remains to sew the head to the body - and your goat is ready.

The differences between the patterns

You have already noticed that the pattern of the goat, the sheep can be different. We examined sewing toys from several parts. But you can sew a product from one or two parts - the head is sewn separately, the body with legs is one part. The tail is created as desired.

If the goat pattern, made by yourself, consists of four parts, then sew one part of the abdomen with an external barrel, and also the second parts with the wrong sides facing out. Then you need to connect the two already created parts together, flash along the contour, leaving the hole only on the abdomen. Turn the resulting part out and fill the goat‚Äôs body with filler through the bottom hole. It remains to sew a gap on the abdomen and sew a head to the body, which is created similarly to the description above.
