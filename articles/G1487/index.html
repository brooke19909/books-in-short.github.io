Detailed instructions: how to throw money from MegaFon to MegaFon

In this article we will talk about how to throw money from MegaFon to MegaFon. This function of this operator is very useful, because it is not always possible to top up a mobile account through a terminal or voucher. Just in this case, it will be appropriate to contact a friend and ask him to send you money. Moreover, this can be done not only in one way. All will be discussed in this article, so that everyone will find a suitable way for themselves.





Mobile Transfer Service

Before you talk about how to save money from MegaFon to MegaFon, you must first clarify that this company has two official services, the first is called Money Transfer, and the second is Mobile Transfer. Their differences are insignificant and consist only in the size of the commission and restrictions. The first we will consider the service "Mobile Transfer" from "MegaFon".

So, initially let's talk about the very limitations and commission. Limitations (they are also called limits) are as follows:

  • during one calendar month, the subscriber can send a maximum of 5 thousand rubles to another subscriber in his region;
  • within a month, you can send a maximum of 15 thousand rubles to a subscriber who lives in another region;
  • when transferring in one payment, it will be possible to send a maximum of 500 rubles, only within a single region;
  • when transferring in one payment, it will be possible to send a maximum of 5 thousand rubles to a subscriber who lives in another region.

That's all with regard to limits, the commission is 5 rubles for subscribers of one region and 0 rubles for different ones.





Now let's move on to the topic of how to drop money from MegaFon to MegaFon. And you can do this using a USSD request. You will need to dial * 133 * initially, then indicate the transfer amount, put an asterisk (*), and then enter the recipient's number and put the lattice (#). It remains only to press the call key to send a USSD request.

Now on the mobile screen should be displayed a request for confirmation with the specified password. You will need to repeat the dialing of the USSD request, only now specifying that password. For example, the password was 999, which means that you will need to send a request of the following type: * 133 # 999 #. As soon as you do this, money will be debited from your number and transferred to the recipient's number.

In order to get money from phone to phone using the "Money Transfer" service from "MegaFon", unlike the previous method, you need to use SMS. Specifying the recipient number and the transfer amount, you need to send the text to 3116. The format is: "number" "amount". Example: 9234567890 150.

Using this service, you can throw money from the number to the number of MegaFon and other operators.

Transfer from Sberbank card

Now we will analyze in detail how to throw money from a card onto MegaFon. We will consider the card of Sberbank.

In order to replenish your account with a card, you need to use the Mobile Bank service from Sberbank. To do this, you will need to send SMS, about which a little later. By visiting the site, you can activate "Auto Payment". When this function is enabled, the account is replenished automatically at the time you specify.

Now let's go directly to SMS. You need to send it to the number 900, and in the text field, write the recipient's number, amount and the last four digits from the card. For example, 9234567890 150 4321.
