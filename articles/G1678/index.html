Mountain Keys of Primorsky Territory. Mountain Key Village: photos, description, resorts

Once upon a time, a monastery was erected in these places - Holy Trinity Nicholas. Near him the village spread out. And now, starting in 1965, this place is an urban resort town.

This is the village of Primorsky Krai - Gorny Klyuchi (Kirovsky district). The article provides some information about this mountain resort.

Geographical location and climate

This area is located in a natural park area, on the site of the southern slopes of the Sikhote-Alin ridge of the Ussuri taiga. In administrative terms, the village, which is the center of the Gornoklyuchevsky settlement, is located in the northern part of the Kirovsky district. The picturesque area is located at r. Ussuri, in the place where Draguchin (duct) flows into it along the left bank. The distance to the village of Kirov is about 16 kilometers.

The railway station Shmakovka is located 21 km from the Mountain Keys of the Primorsky Territory. The railway station Ruzhino, where many passenger trains stop, is located in the city of Lesozavodsk. In addition, from many cities of Primorye, as well as from the city of Khabarovsk, intercity buses pass through Gorny Klyuchi.

The climatic conditions of these places are sharply continental. Winters are quiet, sunny, but cold, with little rainfall. Since mid-November there has been a steady snow cover. Fogs are rare in these places, in July the air temperature is + 21.8 degrees, in January - minus 20.8.

Shmakovsky sanatorium

The military sanatorium of the Primorsky Territory in the village of Gorny Klyuchi is currently a powerful multidisciplinary treatment and prophylactic institution that can simultaneously receive about 500 people for treatment. The medical boarding house is equipped with the most modern physiotherapeutic and diagnostic equipment. The wonderful surrounding nature and clean air contribute to a favorable and relaxing holiday in it.

A bit about one historical attraction

Primorsky Krai (p. Mountain Keys) is famous for the oldest Holy Trinity Nicholas Monastery in the whole Far East, founded in 1893. It has been operating since 1895. Since then, it has become an important religious center of the region.

The monastery was closed in 1924, and in 1927-1991 it was used as a warehouse. Only in 1995, monastic life again resumed in the monastery, but part of the former monastery buildings began to belong to the Shmakovsky sanatorium for the military.

The Chapel of the Transfiguration of the Lord remained unfinished after the beginning of its construction in 1917. Having become the property of the sanatorium, it was transformed into an observation deck, due to its convenient location on a high hill, from where a magnificent panorama of the surroundings opens.

Finally

The exact date of the foundation of the village of Gorny Klyuchi of the Primorsky Territory is still not known. One of the versions about the beginning of the history of the village says that a settlement was laid down, as already noted above, in 1895 - from the date of the founding of the Shmakovsky monastery. Once, upon learning of this convenient and fertile place, migrants from other places gathered all their home belongings in carts and hit the road. The place is really very good: a river, fertile land, a monastery under construction and growing. This earth corner was a real dream.

However, things are a little different now. Most likely, it is thanks to the presence of such wonderful sanatoriums that the village keeps afloat.
