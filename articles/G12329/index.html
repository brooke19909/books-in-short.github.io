The mask of Darth Vader - a cult image

In the world of cinema, there are not so many images that remain popular even many years after the release of the film on the screen. One of them is Anakin Skywalker in the fantastic Star Wars saga. The most popular attribute of his image is the mask of Darth Vader, which he became by going to the "dark side of power." Only thanks to the sophisticated costume, which is essentially a portable life support system, it still supports life after injuries received from Obi-Wan Kenobi. The helmet of Darth Vader is made in the ancient traditions of the Sith. According to them, any warrior of the "dark side" should wear heavy armor.



Spectacular mask of Darth Vader

All enemies were in awe when communicating with this warrior. An important role in this was played by the most complicated apparatus for artificial respiration, which made very unpleasant sounds. Now on sale on some sites of online stores you can find helmets that are practically no different from what was used in the film. It can even make the sound of Darth Vader's heavy breathing. Such a helmet has only one significant drawback - it is expensive. Only real fans of this image and film can afford such a luxury, and deliver it only by reservation. But on some sites you can easily purchase watches made in the form of such a mask.

Costume and mask of Darth Vader

DIY Darth Vader mask



To make a helmet with a mask, you need thick cardboard, strong glue, black paint, colorless varnish. Of course, it is very difficult to repeat all the features of the mask and helmet of Darth Vader, but if you have a good photo, you can create a pretty decent drawing, according to which all the details of the helmet are cut out. On the cardboard note all the lines of bending and gluing, as well as the nodes connecting the detachable elements. A pattern of the required size is cut out of cardboard. In this case, allowances for bends and seams should be considered. With the help of glue, all parts are connected together. The mask and helmet are painted black. If the paint is not glossy, it is coated with a colorless varnish to obtain a characteristic metallic luster.
