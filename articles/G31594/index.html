Hotels of Shadrinsk. What is the price?

The city of Shadrinsk has a diverse range of hotel services with a flexible pricing policy for visitors and tourists. There are many options where to stay. The hotel industry offers both luxury elite and more budget hotel options for a low cost of living. In more detail about hotels of Shadrinsk of the Kurgan region - further.

Hotel Ural

Address: Shadrinsk, st. February, 105.

It is considered a visiting card of the city, where you can rent a good room with all amenities.

Hotel Shadrinsk "Ural" is located 5 minutes from the city center and 10 minutes from the railway station. On the ground floor is the excellent Mrs. Hudson Pub and Restaurant, equipped with a billiard room. All doors are equipped with electronic locks. Hotel "Ural" offers: luxury, junior suite, single, double and family rooms. Each room has free Internet, plasma TV, fridge, shower, toilet. The price includes breakfast. Laundry facilities are available for a fee.

The cost of living in a hotel in Shadrinsk per day is from 1650 rubles to 3980 rubles.

Hotel "House of Merchant"

The hotel of the city of Shadrinsk is located in the central part of the city. Previously, the house belonged to the merchant E. Maltsev and is currently a historical monument. Small hotel with family rooms. The cozy rooms are furnished in a modern style, with free Internet, air conditioning, a shower room and a bathroom.

On the territory of the mini-hotel there is a restaurant with European cuisine and a bar-lobby, parking. The cost per day is from 1800 rubles.

Motel Rublev

Address: Shadrinsk, 4th km of the Shadrinsk-Chelyabinsk highway.

Located 25 minutes from the city. Large and spacious rooms with a TV, fridge, microwave, fan and free internet. The bathroom is combined with a shower room. In front of the building there is a parking and a cafe where you can eat delicious European cuisine at reasonable prices. The daily rate is 1000 rubles.

Guest house "Alexandria"

Address: Shadrinsk, st. Efremova, 21.

The guest house is a 15-minute drive from the city. There are three two-room apartments with a separate entrance and kitchen. As well as three separate rooms with all amenities. There is a sauna on site, where guests staying in rooms receive a discount. In the courtyard there is a barbecue area, outdoor parking and a small banquet hall for 15 people. The hotel cooperates with the Barabas cafe, which delivers its products directly from the restaurant‚Äôs menu to the room. The cost of living per day is from 2700 rubles.

Sanatorium "Pearl of the Trans-Urals"

Address: Shadrinsk, st. Extreme, 17.

The sanatorium is located 15 minutes from the city center. The institution has two dormitories and one medical building, interconnected by a warm passage. Its capacity is 380 seats. Guests are offered comfortable single, double and triple rooms. Each is equipped with a shower, toilet, TV, safe, telephone and refrigerator. There is also a suite, junior suite and a special honeymoon room. For longer stays, seasonal discounts are offered. On the territory there is a cozy cafe, library, massage room and indoor year-round pool. For children, there is a playground and entertainment programs. The resort accepts vacationers with pets.

The cost of living per day is from 2000 rubles, children receive a discount.

Restaurant "Hunter"

Address: Shadrinsk, st. Demyan Poor, 11.

The hotel is located on the 220 kilometer of the Yekaterinburg-Kurgan highway. The cafe-hotel is equipped with five double rooms and a banquet hall for 50 people. Secure parking. The interior of the room corresponds to its name. Wooden furniture, stuffed animals and decoration in the style of a hunting lodge. The cost of the room can be clarified by phone at the reception

Rest house "On the Miass River"

The hotel is located in the village of Zarechny, 57 kilometers from the city center. It offers two separate bedrooms with a terrace, a kitchen with household appliances, a dining area. A sauna and parking are available on site. Leisure activities include fishing or hiking. Pets are also allowed. There is only one room in the hotel at a cost of 2,000 rubles.

Kiwi Hostel

Address: Shadrinsk Elevator Lane, 3.

The cozy inn is a 7-minute walk from the train station. The hostel is located on the second floor. Two-, four-, six-, eight-bed rooms for men and women are equipped with free internet, TV, library. For general use, they provide a shower room, toilet, washing machine, ironing board, hairdryer, and iron. There is also an equipped kitchen and a seating area. On the ground floor there is a shop and a vending machine. The hostel offers babysitting or caregiver services, children's TV channels are available. There is a terrace on site with barbecue facilities and a small garden. There are smoking areas. The hostel allows pets. Laundry and ironing services are available for a fee. The cost of living per day is from 350 rubles.
