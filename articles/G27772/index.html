Knitting for baby girls. Necessary and original models

Knitting for baby girls is one of the most exciting and enjoyable activities. Dressing your baby is doubly nice. With today's variety of yarn and decor elements, it is very easy to create a unique masterpiece.



The main thing is that the thing you connected is necessary, and does not lie on a shelf in the closet. Such clothes include a bag for sleeping and walking. A very convenient thing, and if it is made with your own hands, then it is also original, beautiful, comfortable. Let's find out how to link the simplest model.

Bag for sleeping and walking

To make it, you will need 3 skeins of yarn. Choose its thickness and composition depending on the season. In winter, you can use half-woolen yarn, in summer - cotton. Knitting for a newborn girl with knitting needles will bring you many pleasant minutes, and the finished thing will surely cause admiration for others.



Knitting a sleeping bag is very simple. First you need to measure the distance from the baby‚Äôs chest to the tips of the toes, add 3-4 cm to this value. This will be the height of the finished product. We start knitting from the bottom. The width can be different, but it is desirable that the bag is wider, since newborn children like to chat with their legs, and it will be more convenient for them to turn in a wide bag. So, we put on the knitting needles of the number of loops and knit the selected pattern to the line of the belt. Next, go to knitting with an elastic band and continue until we reach the desired height. Having finished knitting one half, in the same way we knit the second exactly the same. Now you can begin to perform strapless. On the back half along the edges we raise 10-13 loops (the amount depends on the desired width of the strapless) and we knit a garter stitch of the required length. At the end of each strap, make a button hole. Now it remains only to stitch the halves along the side and bottom seams. The bag is ready! Have a nice dream and walk your baby.

Knitting hats for baby girls



Another very necessary thing in the wardrobe of a newborn is a knitted hat. We have long been accustomed to the usual options for hats for babies. Most often these are bonnets and their various variations. And let your little one is very small, you still want to dress it in something original, not like everyone else. The variant of the cap described below is for such a case. Very fashionable, stylish and beautiful. Knitting for baby girls is a good way to diversify the wardrobe of your daughter or granddaughter.

First option

The hat is knitted easily enough. The number of loops should correspond to the distance from the forehead to the crown plus 3-4 cm. Further knitting continues as follows. The first 6-8 loops are knitted with a garter stitch, then a pattern with braids is performed. You can tie one wide braid, as in the photo, or several narrow ones.



Thus, knitting continues until a value equal to the circumference of the head is reached. The hinges close, a seam is sewn. In the upper part, it is necessary to collect all the loops using a hook or needle. Separately, a bright flower contrasting with the color of the cap is crocheted, which is fixed to the side with a needle. A stylish hat for your fashionista is ready. I want to note that knitting for baby girls will not take you much time, but, as noted above, will diversify the wardrobe.
