Base "Elk Island" (Izhevsk): description, reviews

Izhevsk region is famous for its beautiful nature and relatively good environmental conditions. Tourist bases of this region are always popular among the population. For example, residents of the region can have a good rest in the Elk Island complex. Izhevsk is located in the immediate vicinity of this base. And so in the city she is quite famous.

What is a complex

This recreation center is located in the northern suburbs on the banks of the Izhevsk Pond. He takes guests in two wooden houses, one of which is designed for simultaneous visits of 20 people, the second - 40. From all sides the base is surrounded by a pine forest, and its territory is overgrown with soft short green grass. The views from the windows of the rooms of the complex open simply magnificent - to the forest or to the pond.





Number of rooms

If desired, tourists can rent standard class rooms designed for one to three people in the houses of the Losiny Ostrov base (Izhevsk). In the rooms, vacationers can use:

  • comfortable beds;
  • bedside tables;
  • plasma TVs.

Showers are available in every room rented out at the base. Of course, guests have the opportunity to use the bathrooms.

Cottages for 40 people: infrastructure

Guests of the base who have rented rooms in this building can, among other things, visit:

  • a wood-fired bathhouse;
  • pool 5 * 4 * 1.50 m.

Also, guests of the building for 40 people have the opportunity to play Russian billiards. The water temperature in the pool of this house does not exceed 25 C. In addition to it, a large wooden font is installed in the bath complex of the building.





House for 20 people

The rooms in this building are also rented by many tourists who have chosen the Elk Island base (Izhevsk) for recreation. Photo banquet hall house for 20 people is presented below. In this room, among other things, there is an electric fireplace, microwave and karaoke DVD. There is also a plasma TV. The bathhouse in this house is located on the ground floor. Here, tourists can use the steam room, shower and a small pool. On the second floor of the building, guests have the opportunity to play American billiards.





Complex infrastructure

On the territory of the base itself there are:

  • Volleyball Court;
  • sports equipment rental.

In the summer, tourists can not only play volleyball, but also swim in the pond or go fishing. The beach at the base is very convenient for relaxation - sandy. You can fish in the pond, including from bridges. In winter, guests of the complex have the opportunity to go skating and skiing. The tracks laid on the basis of the track are suitable for both professionals and beginners and are well lit.

Elk Island (Izhevsk): guest reviews

Tourists have a relatively good opinion of this base. To the advantages of the complex, its guests include, first of all, comfortable, well-equipped baths. In particular, this base has earned good reviews from lovers of steam. The temperature in the baths of the complex is quite high. Also, most of the former guests of the center note the beautiful design of the halls, corridors and rooms of the houses.

The water in the basins of the base, according to many tourists, has a comfortable bathing temperature. In addition, it does not smell like bleach at all and does not irritate the skin.

But, of course, there are reviews about the Elk Island (Izhevsk) base and not very good reviews. Some of her former guests note the fact that the staff of the complex often refers to their guests, unfortunately, not too friendly. In addition, when renting a banquet hall at the base, excess money for additional services may be withdrawn from customers even after the conclusion of the contract.





How to get there

You can get to the Losiny Ostrov base from Izhevsk along the Yashkur-Bodinsky tract. You need to move along this road to the cafe "Elk". Here you should turn left through the parking lot and get to the first fork. Next, turn right and go straight. At the next fork, turn left. After some time, the sign "Elk Island" (Izhevsk) will be visible on the road. The base address is as follows: Yashkur-Bodinsky tract, 7 km. Rooms in the Elk Island complex should, of course, be booked in advance. This base is popular and there may simply not be any free spots on it.
