Beach holidays in Thailand - summer all year round

In Southeast Asia - on the Indochina Peninsula - the most beautiful country of Thailand is stretched. It attracts many tourists with its amazing nature, pristine beaches, ancient culture, centuries-old traditions, hospitable residents and interesting sights. In addition, vacation in this exotic country is relatively inexpensive.

For this reason, many Russians choose tours to Thailand. Beach holidays are considered the best way to improve the body. In addition, the warm sun makes it easy to get rid of depression and improve the functioning of all human organs, from the nervous system to the respiratory system. In addition, the bright colors of landscapes help to dilute the grayness of urban everyday life.


Beach vacations in Thailand can be carried out all year round. The country has such climatic conditions that allow you to take vacationers from January to December. However, it should be borne in mind that somewhere from mid-April the rainy season begins, which ends only by October. During this period, it rains every day, more often it is shallow and short-lived, a little less - lingering.

Beach holidays in Thailand have long been considered a global brand. Hundreds of thousands of tourists from different parts of the world flock here to rest. Their greatest influx is observed from December to May. Thai resorts offer a huge selection of beaches. The most popular are those located on the islands of Phuket, Samui and Phi Phi.



Phuket Island has 17 beaches, of which seven are located on the west coast. The center of resort life is called Patong. This is the most crowded beach, the main advantage of which is that everything here is quite close to each other: cafes, hotels, night clubs.

Young people who decide to enjoy a beach holiday in Thailand are advised to choose Karon. It is always noisy here, and large waves attract hundreds of surfers. Despite its tiny size, Kata Beach, located in the southwestern part of Phuket, is also popular.

It is quiet and calm. Kamala also boasts of such silence. It should also be noted such as Nai Harn, which is located in the south, and Mai Khao, which occupies the northern coastal territory of the island. Elite beaches are Pansy, Surin and Bang Tao. They are considered to be the most beautiful on the island.

Beach holidays in Thailand are not limited to the island of Phuket. The beaches of Lamai and Chaweng located on the island of Koh Samui are also popular. Chaweng is popular not only among Europeans, but also among Thais themselves. It is famous for its deep sea and incredibly white fine sand.

As for Lamai, this is a youth beach located near nightly entertainment centers. Other places to stay include Ban Taling Ngam, Bang Po and Nathon. There are also shallow areas for families with children, such as Bang Kao, Tong Krut and Hua Thanon.


In this country you can carry out other tours. Thailand, the beach vacation in which is in the first place, is ready to offer travelers a huge number of attractions, among which there are many unique architectural structures. For example, in Bangkok there are a large number of cultural monuments. The main attraction is the Royal Palace. Do not ignore the Temple of the Emerald Buddha.



Crocodile farm located in Samutprakarn is also popular with tourists. At a distance of 86 kilometers from Bangkok is Siam - the ancient capital. Ayutthaya historical park is also located here. You can list all the most interesting places for a long time, but this will be a different story.
