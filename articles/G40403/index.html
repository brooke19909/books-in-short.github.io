Viva Ulaslar Hotel in Alanya (Turkey)

Description

Viva Ulaslar Hotel was built in 1993 on the Turkish coast near Okurcalar, twenty kilometers from Alanya. Its previous name is Polat Alara. The completely forested area on which the hotel is built covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãfour thousand square meters.

The last major reconstruction was carried out in 2008. Many come here to relax after reading reviews of Turkish hotels that help make the right choice. The infrastructure of Viva Ulaslar Hotel includes a number of shops, including a souvenir shop, a left-luggage office, a small conference room, a medical office, a post office, elevators, photo services, a laundry room, a lobby, a beauty salon. The hotel has its own private parking, for the use of which you do not need to pay. The round-the-clock reception has a fax and photocopy service, a depository safe. Those who wish can arrange a transfer. The hotel is one hundred kilometers distant from Antalya . The nearest airport can be reached in an hour.

Rooms

The housing stock of Viva Ulaslar Hotel is 90 standard rooms and 15 - Family Room with a balcony for a maximum of four people. A minibar and safety deposit box must be paid extra. The bathrooms have showers, there are all necessary accessories to maintain hygiene, as well as a hairdryer. The hotel has one special room for people with special needs who need a wheelchair. Pets must be arranged in advance. The whole space is air-conditioned, on the floor - ceramic tiles.

Nutrition

Viva Ulaslar Hotel organizes meals for vacationers on an all-inclusive basis in the form of an open buffet, served on the terrace of the main restaurant. The full board includes an unlimited number of free soft drinks, juices, ice cream, coffee, brandy. After midnight you have to pay for everything. Toasts and pizza are not included in the food offered under this concept. There are also five bars, including the pool. Upon request, you can get a paid service such as room service.



Beach

Viva Ulaslar Hotel has its own sandy beach, which can be reached in three minutes, just by crossing the road. Everything you need for swimming and sunbathing - umbrellas, as well as sunbeds and towels - is free of charge. The sandy entrance to the sea is convenient.

Additional Information

Viva Ulaslar Hotel offers children relaxing in the hotel to swim in the children's pool with a slide, as well as have fun on the playground. In addition, an extra rollaway bed is available upon request. Adults, in addition to swimming in the pool and riding on adult water slides, you can play darts, table tennis. Tennis equipment can be rented at the reception. Those who wish can enjoy free water aerobics and rent tennis courts. The hotel has animators and a disco in the evenings.





What vacationers say

Reviews of tourists about hotels, especially about Turkish, are always contradictory. As for living at Viva Ulaslar Hotel, many agree that it is a very convenient in price and quite comfortable hotel, designed primarily for families with children. The staff is always helpful. The food is hearty, the restaurant is very organized. The rooms are excellently cleaned. The animation is very good. The only drawback is some call a small area.
