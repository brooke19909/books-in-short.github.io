Tariff change options

After the subscriber has decided on the tariff plan, which he would like to use on his number, he will have to perform an action to connect it. The tariff change on Tele2 can be done in several ways, we will talk about them in more detail below:

  • through a mobile device (dialing a short command, the My Tele2 mobile application, calling a service number);
  • through your personal account (here you should select the appropriate team, familiarize yourself with the list of available options and make a change; similar functionality is available through the mobile application);
  • through an employee of the contact center (you must specify the data of the owner of the SIM card).

You can switch to another Tele2 tariff using any of the above methods. At the same time, it is somewhat easier to do this via the Internet (personal account, mobile application), because here you just need to click on the option you need.

Of course, switching via a smartphone to another tariff without using the Internet is also not a difficult task. However, here you need to know which short command you need to enter to connect.





There is a short service number for changing TP - 630. By calling on it, the subscriber can listen to the overview information about the tariff plan and activate it at will.
