Yeisk - Hero City

Where is the city of Yeysk, we have already learned. But now we recall the facts from history and find out why this village is a real hero. During the Great Patriotic War, the city was constantly subjected to bombing from the air by German air forces. These attacks began in April 1942. The occupation of the city lasted until February 1943. This time was very difficult, many people died, including 214 children in the orphanage. Mass repressions destroyed not only human destinies, but also their small homeland. Where Yeysk is located, there still live the grandchildren and great-grandchildren of real heroes who laid down their lives in the struggle for freedom. This once again proves the versatility of the city. The small town absorbed both modern blessings and the cultural heritage of their ancestors.

The title of the resort town of Yeisk was awarded relatively recently, only in 2008. And to this day, he does not cease to amaze us with his spiritual kindness. This resort, in comparison with the cities of Sochi, Gelendzhik, Anapa and others, is the most economical in terms of spending on vacation. In Yeisk, food and accommodation are much cheaper, although in terms of comfort this small, friendly town is in no way inferior to the more famous Kuban resorts.
