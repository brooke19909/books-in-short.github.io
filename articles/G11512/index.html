Holiday Village "Salute" (Yeysk): description, features and reviews of tourists

On the Yeisk Spit on the coast of the Sea of ​​Azov there are many beaches and places of recreation. There is also a recreation center "Salute". Yeysk is famous for its beaches and shallow sea, which allows tourists with children to relax in this place. The recreation center itself offers vacationers a wide variety of entertainment and provides all the conditions for recreation for every taste.



Where is the recreation center "Salute"

Yeysk in Russia and abroad is not very famous as a place of rest. But in fact it is a rather large resort city on the Sea of ​​Azov. It is located near the Yeisk Spit, which leaves a narrow wedge in the sea from the port and extends for 3 km. On the one hand it is washed by the Taganrog Bay, and on the other is the Yeisk estuary. It is quite small and warm, so it is great for families with small children.

Yeisk Spit is the main resort area of ​​the city. There are many boarding houses, recreation centers, children's camps. In addition, there are numerous beaches, cafes and restaurants, attractions and playgrounds. Therefore, the recreation center "Salute" in Yeysk is so popular. It is located almost at the very tip of the Yeisk Spit, on the central street of Beach. From there, a magnificent view of the Sea of ​​Azov opens. You can get to the recreation center either by your own car or by one of the many fixed-route taxis. Those who arrived in Yeysk on their own, go to the recreation center nearby, only 4 km.



general characteristics

The recreation center "Salute" in Yeysk appeared recently - all the buildings were built in 2011. They are made entirely of wood, as well as small buildings: arbors, a playground. The base is located only 25 meters from the sea. Windows in almost all rooms overlook the vast expanses of the sea. The territory of the base itself is fenced, is under round-the-clock protection. But this does not cause vacationers any inconvenience, as the picturesque nature, fresh sea air and magnificent views make it possible to forget about everything.

The location of the recreation center is successful not only because it is close to the sea, in a picturesque corner of the Yeisk Spit. Almost next to it there are many cafes and restaurants, an amusement park, so vacationers will not be bored. Therefore, families with children and outdoor enthusiasts come here.



Beach vacation

The recreation center Salyut operates in Yeysk from April to October, since the sea occupies the main place in recreation here. It is located just 20 meters from the base. Therefore, vacationers have the opportunity to take the best places on the beach. It is worth taking a few steps from the building, and you can take sunbathing, enjoy the soft sand and warm healing water of the Sea of ​​Azov. Vacationers can swim at any time, even at night, when the water is especially warm.

A feature of a beach holiday on the Yeisk Spit is that the sea is not deep here, especially from the estuary. A smooth sandy bottom and a slight slope allow even small children who cannot swim to swim. Comfortable sun loungers, as well as 24-hour security allow you to relax. But not only a family with a child can have a good rest at the Salute base. Lovers of outdoor activities are waiting for riding on a catamaran, boat, banana or hoverboard. Every year, this place hosts surfing competitions.



Services offered

The recreation center "Salute" in Yeisk offers reasonable prices for everyone. One room, depending on the size and number of residents, costs from 2200 to 5000 rubles per day. The price includes breakfast, use of the beach and the infrastructure of the base. The following services are offered here:

  • free internet in the territory;
  • sun loungers and deck chairs on the beach;
  • parking;
  • playground;
  • arbors and places for cooking barbecue;
  • iron, electric kettle, split system.

All these services are included in the price. But you can take advantage of the additional infrastructure of the Yeisk Spit. There are numerous cafes and restaurants for every taste and wealth, an amusement park, a pharmacy, a savings bank, several shops. Of the attractions, the most popular are Emelya, the Ferris wheel, and children like the most diverse trampolines. Tourists are offered face painting, drawing on the body with henna, weaving braids, making souvenirs. Vacationers can book a boat trip or fishing on the high seas. Popular excursions to a desert island located 3 km from the spit.



Accommodation

The recreation center "Salute" in Yeysk offers accommodation in 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5-bed rooms. All of them are located in a new wooden two-story building. In total, the base offers 25 rooms of European level and two two-room economy rooms. At the same time, about 50 people can rest at the base. Each room has a toilet with a washbasin, a shower with hydromassage, a refrigerator, a split system, a TV, comfortable furniture, a mirror. But there are some features in different rooms.

Economy rooms overlook the common gallery of the second floor. The windows overlook the sea, but in the second room there are no windows at all. Each room has 4 or 5 single beds, a bathroom, a shower that does not have a hydromassage function, the necessary furniture and a balcony.

One room can be double or triple. In this case, in addition to the double bed, there is a chair bed. Shower in these rooms - with hydromassage, TV and fridge.

Two-room rooms are designed for 3, 4 or 5 people. They have wide double beds and chair beds.

Since the rooms are not very different from each other's comfort, each one can have a good rest, their cost is also not too different. It is advantageous to relax in Salute for both young couples and large families with children, especially since children under 5 can live almost for free - only a resort fee of 300 rubles is paid for them. Comfort of rest is explained not only by the availability of all amenities in the room. There is no hard mode at the base - you can sleep as much as you like, there is even such a service when the administrator wakes vacationers at the time they ask.



Reviews about the recreation center "Salute" in Yeysk

In most cases, guests enjoy a holiday at this recreation center. Almost everyone notes the cleanliness of the rooms, the comfort of living, the courtesy and diligence of the staff. Very many say that they will once again come to the recreation center "Salute" in Yeysk. Reviews also note that it is best for families with children to relax here. In addition to the developed infrastructure with children's entertainment, it is quiet and safe, and the sea is very warm and shallow. All vacationers like that for such a small price you can relax quite comfortably - with a TV, fridge, air conditioning and even a hydromassage shower. Therefore, the recreation center "Salute" is never empty, as it is popular.
