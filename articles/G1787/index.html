Tanzanite Beach Resort (Tanzania, Zanzibar): description, service, reviews

The southern country of Tanzania is becoming more and more popular with domestic beach lovers. On a world map, you need to look for it in the equator region in eastern Africa. Part of Tanzania is including the Zanzibar archipelago with the main large island bearing the same name.



This place is considered by many travelers to be truly paradise. Zanzibar attracts tourists from all over the world with its clear sea, comfortable beaches and magnificent nature. Resorts located in the north of this island are very popular among vacationers. The Nungwi region is no exception in this regard. Housing, for example, vacationers are offered quite comfortable here. Most local hotels have earned good reviews from vacationers. Included in their number and hotel Tanzanite Beach Resort.





Where is the hotel located

This small complex was built on the beach. In order to get to the beach, its guests just need to go down the short stairs (consisting of about ten steps). At high tide, the sea approaches, as tourists note, to the territory of Tanzanite Beach Resort itself.

The hotel is approximately an hour's drive from the nearest airport. In the absence of traffic jams in Stone Town, a shuttle bus can take tourists arriving by plane to this hotel faster. If desired, vacationers can order delivery from the airport directly at the hotel itself. A ticket service is also available on site.

Nungwi itself is a long strip of beach along which many cafes, shops, restaurants and souvenir shops are built. Hotel Tanzanite Beach is located not in the area itself, but somewhat in a distance from it (about 1.5 km). Hotel guests can reach Nungwi simply by moving along the beach (at first rather rocky) along the sea. Residents of the complex can also get here through the village.





There are no hotels and special infrastructure close to this complex. But the fact that the hotel is in a quiet place, many tourists attribute to its advantages. Obsessive merchants simply do not enter this place. And in the hotel itself, people usually do not rest too much. So, best of all, this hotel, according to tourists, is suitable for those who want to relax in the south in complete unity with nature.

Tanzanite Beach Resort: general description

Trips to Zanzibar to Russian vacationers are usually quite expensive. Tours in this country with accommodation at the Tanzanite hotel in this regard are a pleasant exception. The hotel is considered perhaps the most affordable price in Nungwi.

Take vacationers at this hotel in several small bungalows. Each of the houses is designed for two rooms. Rooms for tourists are offered in different categories. For example, guests of Nungwi can rent standard or family rooms. The hotel also has large rooms with panoramic windows overlooking the sea.

This complex belongs to the class of three stars. Service in Tanzanite Beach Resor t for tourists, judging by the reviews, is offered good and unobtrusive. The territory of this hotel is small, but, according to tourists, quite beautiful and comfortable. In addition to sun loungers, hammocks are located next to the bungalows in this complex.

Smoking is not allowed in the rooms of this hotel. There are designated areas for cigarette lovers at the hotel.





One of the features of the Tanzanite Beach Resort 3 * cottage complex is that pets can be accommodated here. But to warn the hotel owners about the desire to bring their pet with them is, of course, in advance. No extra fee for keeping animals in the rooms at this hotel.

What family tourists need to know

Children under two years old can stay in the complex for free. For small guests aged 3 to 12 years, 75% of the cost of living is charged. Unfortunately, extra beds are not available for the hotel guests. Check-in time at the hotel is possible at 13:00. Tourists must release their rooms at the end of their paid stay at 11:00.

Tanzanite Beach Resort: housing reviews

The rooms in the complex look quite neat, according to vacationers. The maids in the hotel rooms are cleaned quite often and carefully. The furniture is set in retro style, it is relatively comfortable and solid. The beds in the hotel are wide, closed with canopies, which gives the bedroom place additional comfort and coziness.

Tourists consider frequent power outages and hot water to be a disadvantage at Tanzanite Beach Hotel . Also, the vacationers attribute the presence of just a huge number of mosquitoes to the minuses of the hotel rooms. Resting in the rooms of the complex, these insects lodges guests very much. In addition, ordinary fumigators practically do not save from them. Experienced tourists recommend purchasing repellents in Africa, and not bring with them from Russia.

A serious drawback of the inexpensive rooms of this hotel, guests also consider the lack of air conditioning. The weather in Zanzibar during the season is very hot. The fans installed in the hotel rooms unfortunately do not save tourists from the heat of tourists.

The lack of refrigerators hotel guests are also attributed to the minuses of the rooms. In the heat, the residents of this hotel do not even have the opportunity to drink cold water.

What is the infrastructure of the hotel

As in any other southern three-star hotel, in Tanzanite Beach Resort (Zanzibar) , tristians have the opportunity to swim in the pool located on the territory. There is also free parking in the courtyard of this hotel. Booking a parking spot in advance is not necessary for tourists.





In front of each bungalow in the hotel is equipped with a small beautiful porch with a table and chairs. This complex also has its own laundry room. Here guests can wash dirty clothes for a fee. This hotel also has an exchange office and a small business center with a photocopy machine.

Reviews of tourists about the service at the hotel

Hotel rooms (however, as elsewhere in Zanzibar) in terms of comfort are significantly inferior to housing provided in Egyptian or Turkish hotels of a similar level. Even some Indian 3 * hotels offer vacationers more comfortable accommodation. However, there are practically no negative reviews about the Tanzanite Beach Resort 3 * complex on the Internet.

Saves the situation (in addition to the successful location of the hotel), according to tourists, the courtesy of its staff. The hotel staff are very sincere towards their guests, and it is pleasant to communicate with them, as many travelers say.

The pool on the territory of this hotel, according to guests, is equipped quite convenient for swimming. The water in it always has a comfortable bathing temperature. In addition, they clean it often enough. The only drawback is that the water in the hotel pool smells a bit like bleach.

Another advantage of this hotel tourists consider good Wi-Fi. Guests can use it not only on the territory itself, but even in some rooms.

A definite drawback of the Tanzanite Beach Resort hotel, t lawyers consider that there are no special entertainments for guests here. For example, there will simply be nothing for children on vacation at the hotel.

How to feed at the hotel

Tourists can book meals at Tanzanite Beach Resort . About breakfasts, lunches and dinners in this hotel on the Internet there are, unfortunately, basically only "cool" reviews. Cooks cook deliciously, but the hotel does not differ in a special variety of menus. Yes, and guests of this complex are forced to get up for breakfast early. Late tenants in the hotel dining room may not have enough dishes.





Everything else in the hotel restaurant, tourists can also get bored by whole clouds of flies. At the same time, the administration, as some vacationers note, does not take absolutely any measures to combat these insects.

Positive feedback from tourists earned only served at the hotel breakfasts. At this time, tourists can take seafood in the hotel restaurant. The cook of the hotel prepares them, as many say, is simply excellent.

Those tourists who are not satisfied with the food offered in the hotel's dining room can visit the restaurant of the nearest Essque Zalu Zanzibar 5 * hotel . Here, for a fee, guests of the island are offered simply excellent cuisine.

Beach Hotels

As already mentioned, the Tanzanite Beach Resort complex is built right on the seafront. Hotel guests can get to the beach in just a couple of minutes. Views of the ocean, even from the very territory of the hotel, as many tourists note, are simply magnificent. And the rocky beach of the hotel itself looks very picturesque. Photos here can be made just great.





The beach belonging to Tanzanite Beach Resort looks beautiful, but sunbathing and swimming is not very convenient here. Both the coast and the bottom of the sea in this place are rocky. In addition, a lot of sea urchins live on the beach of the hotel. Therefore, tourists can swim and sunbathe here only in special shoes. Among the pluses of the hotel, among other things, vacationers include the fact that beach slippers are given to them absolutely free of charge. The guests of the complex can take them right at the exit to the shore by the stairs. The sea itself near the hotel is clean, but not too transparent due to coral.

Useful advice

There are no comfortable beaches in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. In order to get the opportunity to relax and sunbathe with amenities, guests of the complex need to travel along the coast by public transport for several stops. The beaches of the Nungwi area, according to vacationers, are simply luxurious. The coast here is covered with light soft sand, and the sea is clean and absolutely transparent.

sights

Tanzania on the map is a rather small country, not to mention the archipelago and the island of Zanzibar. However, there are many interesting places worthy of visiting tourists in this African resort. Of course, guests of Tanzanite Hotel can also visit them .

This hotel is located , as already mentioned, in a deserted and deserted place. You cannot meet sellers of excursions on its beach, but guests of this hotel, of course, can buy any trips to Nungwi itself.

And of course, there are many noteworthy places on the island of Zanzibar. For example, hotel guests can see such sights as:

  1. Pemba. This coral island became part of Tanzania in the middle of the last century (as part of Zanzibar). In ancient times, it was known among traders as the "Green Island". Legends circulated about him as paradise on earth. On this island, on the wide deserted sandy beaches, tourists can enjoy peace and quiet surrounded by unspoiled nature.

  2. Reef "Baribi". This place is definitely worth visiting for diving enthusiasts. In the sea here, at a depth of about 20 meters, you can see not only numerous underwater animals, but also sunken ships.





Guests at Tanzanite Beach Resort can also visit Josani National Park on the island. This forest is famous for the fact that it is the only place on the planet where a very rare species of monkeys - red colobus - is still preserved.

Planning trips around the Zanzibar archipelago or around Tanzania can help guests at Tanzanite Beach Reso rt , including their staff . The hotel has its own tour desk.
