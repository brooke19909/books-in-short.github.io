Hotel Coral Sea Water World (Egypt): description, photos and reviews of tourists

The Egyptian resort of Sharm el Sheikh, located on the southern side of the Sinai Peninsula, attracts tourists with a developed entertainment infrastructure and unique natural conditions all year round. The mild climate with the maximum number of clear days is ideal for those who want to sunbathe, and scuba diving enthusiasts can enjoy the underwater scenery of coral reefs and tropical fish that you will not find anywhere else on the planet.

Among the hundreds of hotels offering their services to visitors from Europe, one of the first in the ranking is the hotel complex Coral Sea Water World 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh). The hotel belongs to the prestigious Coral Sea chain. A distinctive feature of this Middle Eastern brand is its strict adherence to European traditions of service.

How to get there

From Russia to the Egyptian resort you can get a direct flight. Regular communication is established from many large cities. If there are no tickets, an alternative is to fly to Hurghada. This air hub is connected to Sharm El Sheikh by regular bus routes. A one-month guest visa is issued directly at the customs terminal. The Coral Sea Water World hotel complex is located just 5 kilometers from the airport of Sharm El Sheikh, 24 kilometers from the Old Town on the very shore of the Red Sea. From the airport you can get by taxi or minibus. The hotel is located in one of the main streets, near the market. Within walking distance are many shopping centers and colorful cafes with local cuisine.

Number of rooms

Coral Sea Water World Hotel offers two types of rooms: Standard Room and Deluxe Room. The floors in the rooms are covered with ceramic tiles. The centralized air conditioning system provides for individual adjustment in each room.

During cold nights, the hotel is heated. Replacement of drinking water in minibars is carried out daily, the service is free. All rooms have a private bathroom, a safe, a hairdryer, a telephone with international direct dial access, and satellite TV with Russian channels. On request, a baby cot can be provided. Nutrition

Meals are organized according to the All inclusive system. Coral Sea Water World has three restaurants, two of which, Indian and Italian, provide A la Carte services . A buffet with a huge selection of dishes, drinks and fruits is available in only one. Three meals a day, during the day light snacks, ice cream and soft drinks, as well as local alcohol are offered. Of the six bars, one is a pub. The restaurants host themed evenings. The menu of the dishes of a country is announced by hanging the national flag, the staff also dresses accordingly. For an additional fee, meals can be delivered to the room. Diet menus, children's menus are available on request, and a baby chair is available in restaurants.

Hotel Services

Coral Sea Water World Hotel provides a standard package of services: a 24-hour front desk, as well as concierge services, check-in and check-out are accelerated, there is a left-luggage office, an ATM, a currency exchange office, and a spacious TV lounge. Airport shuttle is paid. The administration can call a taxi at any time. The services of the spa, massage room, Turkish bath can be used only for a fee. Billiard, tennis courts and rental equipment paid. The hotel has a gift shop and several convenience stores. It is possible to rent a car or scooter, use dry cleaning, laundry, repair and ironing, as well as babysitting services at any time of the day. Water sports equipment is available for rent. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas. There is a limousine service.

Beach holiday

Coral Sea Water World 5 * - hotel of the first coastline. To the sea - only a hundred meters. The width of the beach strip along the sea is 400 meters, it is located in a small natural bay. This is quite enough so that numerous guests do not interfere with each other.

To get to deep water, you should go to the end of the pier 180 meters long. Sun loungers, parasols, mattresses and towels are provided free of charge. Directly on the beach is a certified diving center where you can rent snorkeling equipment or snorkeling equipment. The entrance to the sea is sandy. Due to the abundance of mollusks, water should be entered in special shoes.

Entertainment

The entertainment industry is committed to creating a holiday atmosphere that they will never forget. It is truly a water world. Almost the entire territory of Coral Sea Water World is a huge water park. Six slides for every taste: from children's to extreme. A lifeguard is on duty near each. There is a swimming pool with artificial turmoil, as well as a site called the Lazy River (lazy river). Visit for guests is free.

The spacious outdoor pool in the middle has an islet with a bar and a shallow children's area. In addition, there are two more heated pools and one for children. Around the pools are numerous umbrellas and sun loungers. Every evening, a show program with animators is organized. Mostly they are English speakers. An entertainment program is held at the amphitheater.

For young tourists aged 4 to 12 years, the Popai Kids Club and a children's playground are provided. On the site with the children are engaged in animators. For guests who prefer outdoor activities, there are several areas for playing beach soccer and volleyball, as well as a water polo area.





For guests who have a birthday during their stay, the staff gives a special compliment: a greeting card and a small cake are presented. On Christmas holidays, the administration of the tourist complex provides a special entertainment program.
