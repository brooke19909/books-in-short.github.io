Adriatic! Sandy beaches of Croatia

In the west, Croatia is washed by the Adriatic Sea. And the length of the coast of this country is nearly 6,000 kilometers. And 4,000 kilometers falls on the coast of reefs, sea cliffs and islands. The bays and coasts of this country are incredibly good. There are quiet places where there is no hustle, bustle, omnipresent onlookers and noisy bazaars. There are no industrial enterprises in Croatia, so the water and air here are quite clean. This country is also famous for its beaches, which UNESCO has repeatedly marked with the Blue Flag. And most of them are rocky and rocky. But those who wish can easily find the sandy beaches of Croatia and at the same time make a trip to this country.





So in the Adriatic, in the very south of the country, is the island of Mljet. And those tourists who are interested in a relaxing holiday are happy to visit it . In Croatia, sandy beaches are rare, but there is one on this island, and it is located in its southeastern part. There is a small lagoon, the coast of which is covered with fine and clean sand. And not far from the beach there is a dense forest where you can hide from the heat. The island of Mlek is always calm, a transparent sea splashes on the shore and the whole local atmosphere promotes relaxation and positive emotions.

The sandy beaches of Croatia can also be found on other islands. For example, on the island of Lopud, which is located at a distance of 14 kilometers from Dubrovnik, there is Sun beach. For centuries, the rich inhabitants of this city have loved to relax on the island. And it is distinguished by unforgettable landscapes and rich vegetation. And Sun Beach is especially popular with tourists. It is well-groomed and long enough to prevent crowding. This beach is also divided into two parts. And in one of them nudists sunbathe, and in the other - people of more strict morals.
