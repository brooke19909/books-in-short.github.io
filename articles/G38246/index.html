Rating of the best budget hotels in Balashikha

It is best to stay at a hotel, so you are guaranteed not to suffer from scammers who carry out fraud with housing. And you can spend your vacation safely and comfortably. Especially so that you do not have to spend a lot of time looking for a good and inexpensive option, this article presents a list of the 5 best hotels in Balashikha. The choice of which one to stay in is yours.

  1. Hostel "Nikolsky". It is very popular with visitors to the city. This is a great option if you want to live in the center, but are not ready to pay a large amount of money for a hotel room. This gives him the right to take first place in this rating.
  2. Hotel "Pechorskaya". Responsive staff is always ready to help you and resolve any difficult situation. The rooms are clean and tidy.
  3. Hotel "Lilac". The atmosphere of home comfort reigns in it. Having visited this place once, you will not want to go to another hotel. This option is very fond of both residents and visitors.
  4. Zheldor Hostel is in a great location next to Balashikha Central Park. Here you can enjoy the silence and relax from the city noise.
  5. The hotel with the long name "On Shchelkovo Highway" closes the hit parade of inexpensive hotels in Balashikha. Here you can relax well with a large family or in the company of friends. Also, there are all conditions for diplomatic negotiations and important meetings. Another plus: nearby is a unique reserve "Elk Island". Hostel "Nikolsky"

This is a good option for housing in Balashikha. It is perfect for a large group of tourist friends.

Hostel "Nikolsky" is waiting for its visitors around the clock. Here you can rent a comfortable bed in a spacious room for 4 people. You can also redeem it completely if you do not want to live with strangers. The cost of one place: 800 rubles per day.

In total, the hostel has 3 rooms, and the shower and toilet are on the ground floor. Each room has two bunk beds, a wardrobe and a table. In addition, there are necessary appliances: kettle, microwave and refrigerator. You can cook in the large shared kitchen, which has everything you need to create a delicious meal. At the end of the day, you can visit the library and relax while reading a fascinating book.

Hostel Nikolsky is located at 1 Zheleznodorozhnaya, 8.

Mini-hotel "Silver"

This small but very comfortable hotel is deservedly popular among city guests. There are all conditions for comfortable rest and relaxation at the end of the day. You can rent one of the following rooms:

  1. Quadruple with shared bathroom. The room is in good repair, the walls are painted in a nice beige color, stretch ceilings. In the bathroom, all the necessary accessories for water procedures. There is a washing machine. Also in the room there is one double bed and two sofas, a modern TV, many nightstands and a spacious wardrobe. Cost: from 1700 per day.
  2. Double room with one bed. A good option for a pair of lovers. It has everything you need for a perfect holiday. A large bed, coffee maker, TV and many other furniture and appliances. Price per room: 2000 rubles.
  3. Family Suite. It comfortably accommodates 3 people. The rooms are very spacious. There is all the necessary furniture and appliances. The tables in the room are decorated with beautiful flowers. For this pleasure you need to pay 2500 rubles. per day of stay.

The hotel has a special place for guests to relax, where you can play table tennis or other interesting games. The hotel has a vending machine where you can buy chocolates, chips and soda.

This is a good option if you want to rent a room for an inexpensive night in a hotel in Balashikha. Address: Zarechnaya, 32.

Hotel "Lilac"

In a large three-story cottage on Shchelkovo highway, an inexpensive hotel with a memorable name is located in Balashikha. For guests there is parking and the opportunity to use the Internet for free at any time. Also on site is a small cafe with delicious ice cream.

You can call the hotel at any time, the reception is open around the clock. It is better to book a room in advance. You can select the following options:

  • economy double;
  • economy for three people;
  • standard.

The rooms are always tidied, a thorough cleaning is carried out every day. There are all necessary furniture and appliances. The standard rooms have a bathroom, in the economy - all the amenities are shared on the floor. Price: from 900 rubles per day. Address: Lilac, 15.

"Pehorskaya"

This is another hotel Balashikha (Moscow region). "Inexpensive and comfortable" is her main slogan.

The hotel has many shops and cafes, a large conference room and a common living room where you can play billiards or board games. Guests can also visit the beauty salon, massage room and sauna during the day. The rooms are decorated in an unusual high-tech design style. The furniture is very comfortable and has all the necessary equipment.

You can rent a room for both one and two people. Cost: from 1500 per day. You can also rent a cottage located nearby. It will comfortably accommodate 40 people.

Hotel address: Pekhorskaya, 9.
