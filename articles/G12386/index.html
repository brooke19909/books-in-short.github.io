Baltic countries. Recreation and tourism

The Baltic countries are great for leisure and tourism. Recreation areas with clean ecology, the European level of service, make rest in these beautiful places attractive and comfortable. In addition, Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania are interesting for their centuries-old history, cultural monuments, ancient traditions and customs. All these countries are beautiful and unique in their own way.



An ancient and mysterious country - Latvia. For several years tourism has been developed here. Latvia is a country of museums, monuments of architecture and history, which are always of interest to tourists. But here not only lovers of antiquity will be able to have a good rest. Latvia is famous for its beautiful sea resorts and well-groomed beaches. Lakes of extraordinary beauty, fresh sea air make the country's climate pleasant and comfortable.

The capital of Riga and the resort city of Jurmala are considered the most visited cities of the country. It is impossible to confuse Riga with any other European city. Her special charm in small, like fairy-tale houses, narrow streets, cozy, shady parks.

On the shore of the Gulf of Riga is the resort city of Jurmala. Everyone who wants to enjoy the fresh sea air, impeccably clean sea can have a great rest here. In addition, all Baltic countries have sanatoriums and boarding houses where health can be restored and strengthened. Recently in Latvia, rapidly

industrial tourism is developing. Old mills and windmills, a tram depot in Daugavpils, casemates in Liepaja - attract lovers of urban tourism to the country.

In Lithuania, you can also relax in the sea resorts, see wonderful lakes and dunes. All visitors to the country celebrate the amazing purity that reigns everywhere. You can spend the whole day on Lithuanian beaches - the service here is amazing.

Everyone who arrives here for the first time and wants to see the Baltic countries should definitely visit Kaunas Vilnius and Palanga. Majestic medieval castles, fortresses, churches and cathedrals make up a portrait of ancient Vilnius. In Palanga, in local sanatoriums, you can correct shaky health.
