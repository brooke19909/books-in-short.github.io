Useful legislative changes





There are several useful legislative changes that need to be taken into account during the policy process:

  • the maximum possible damage to property is 400,000 rubles, but this rule applies only to those contracts that were drawn up after October 1, 2014;
  • the maximum possible harm to health or life after an accident reaches 500,000 rubles, but this norm is relevant only for contracts drawn up after 01.04.2015;
  • reimbursement can be requested from your insurance company if only two people are involved in the accident, and the damage was caused exclusively to vehicles, while in other situations you will have to go directly to the insurance company of the person who is responsible for the accident;
  • the maximum possible amount of damage for which it is possible to contact the insurance company without having to call the traffic police is 50,000 rubles if both drivers' OSAGO agreements were concluded after 2.08.2014;
  • the deadline for resolving all issues after receipt of the application is 20 days, and the countdown begins immediately after the provision of a full package of documentation, and during this period the company must send a reasoned refusal, provide a direction for repair or make a payment;
  • if the insurance company has an agreement with a professional technical service station, then in this case the victim has the opportunity to choose between direct cash payment, as well as sending his vehicle for repair in the event that he entered into the MTPL agreement after 1.09.2014.

These rules apply even if you are not issuing OSAGO in a company other than Rosgosstrakh.
