General concept of the All Inclusive tariff

To begin with, Megafon has several offers called All Inclusive. They are distributed depending on the volume of services provided as part of the tariffs, as well as on the size of the monthly fee. These 5 plans are called XS, S, M, L and VIP.

This name was given to tariffs for the reason that within their framework the subscriber is provided with services of all kinds: from calls and messages to the Internet and international communications. Plus, again, we will not discard the marketing expediency of naming service packages in such a way as to assure the subscriber that he receives everything at once.





The cheapest XS package will cost the subscriber 199 rubles a month, while for the most expensive VIP tariff you will have to pay 2700 rubles. Accordingly, the differences between the volume of services provided for in the plans are equally large. However, we are most interested in package M.
