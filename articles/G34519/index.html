Description of the Sony Xperia line: all models that deserve respect, features and reviews

In order to make a choice, we suggest reading brief reviews on the most popular phones of this manufacturer.

The Sony Xperia line, all of which is famous for its spectacular design and advanced filling, is a combination of an interesting appearance and high-quality materials, convenient support and the latest technology. There are a lot of smartphones, so everyone can find an option to their taste.

Smartphone Model ZR

Screen resolution - 1280 x 720. The processor is quad-core, the frequency is 1.5 GHz. RAM is 2 GB, flash is 8 GB. It works on a software package called Android 4.1. In appearance, the Sony Xperia ZR turned out to be quite modest, which is noted by most consumers. He incorporated many features from previous models, this is noticeable even with the naked eye. There are no searches in appearance. The device is heavy, boring and uninteresting. The whole body is made of plastic, no metal.

Smartphone Sony Xperia XZ

Like other representatives of the Sony Xperia line, all models of which are in high demand, the next - XZ - runs on Android. The version of the "green man" is 6.0, the well-known "marshmallow". The main camera is 23 megapixels, the front camera is 18 megapixels. The battery is non-removable type, its power is 2900 mAh.

The appearance of the smartphone, like the Sony Xperia line itself, all of whose models are immediately recognizable, was created on the basis of old developments. The front camera received a flash and small functions that were not previously implemented. They allow you to make selfies even better. Both cameras work with autofocus.

Like other devices of the Sony Xperia line (all models are ranked high in online stores of household appliances), the described device is popular with consumers. It works stably, there are no failures, rarely when the system slows down.
