Shopping center "European" in Moscow: a brief overview, travel options, pros and cons

Shopping center "European" is truly considered one of the sights of the capital. The reasons for this are the impressive interior design using modern technologies, versatility, active participation in various events and holidays, constant draws and presentations from major brands. The advantage is the good location - in the immediate vicinity of the Kiev railway station, Europe Square and the Kievskaya metro station .

Nice to meet you, "European"!

The building of the Evropeisky shopping center in Moscow is primarily attracted by its unusual triangular shape. The total area is 180 thousand m 2 . It was erected by the architect Yu.P. Platonov. When creating his creation, he was guided by the principle of building ancient Russian ranks.

How to get to the Evropeisky shopping center in Moscow

The shopping center is very conveniently located at the intersection of the square of Kiev railway station and Dorogomilovskaya street. The address of the shopping center "European" is Moscow, pl. Kievsky station, d. 2. Five days a week the center is open from 10.00 to 22.00, and on Friday and Saturday - from 10.00 to 23.00. A number of supermarkets and entertainment areas are open around the clock.





The easiest way is to get to Evropeisky by metro - one of the exits of the Kievskaya station of the ring branch leads directly to the shopping center. Focusing on the signs in the subway, you can go to the brown Kievskaya from the stations of the same name on the blue and blue branches.

Travelers traveling by land should choose routes passing through Bolshaya Dorogomilovskaya Street. The final point will be the stop "Kiev Station". Another way to get into the "European" - electric trains arriving at the station.
