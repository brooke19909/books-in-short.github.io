Brief Specifications

The device operates in the GSM bands. The phone first appeared on the market in 2009. The matrix of the screen is made by TFT technology, the screen diagonal is 2.2 inches. The resolution is 240 x 320 pixels. Calls can be set polyphonic type, there is support for MP3 format. The size of RAM is 64 megabytes. The battery capacity is 1050 mAh. To access the international network, you can use the WAP and EDGE standards. To exchange multimedia information with other devices, a bluetooth function is provided. The camera resolution is 2 megapixels. Zoom is possible in four times.

Contents of delivery

The device comes with a lithium-ion battery, an external microSD memory card (flash card size is 1 gigabyte), as well as a charger and a wired stereo headset of fairly good quality. This also includes all the necessary documentation, including a warranty card and operating instructions.





Dimensions

The dimensions of the phone are as follows: in height - 115 millimeters with a width of 47 mm. The thickness of the device reaches 15.3 mm. Moreover, the mass of the device is 94 grams. According to such dimensions, to say that the model belongs to the class of protected devices is very, very difficult, but in fact everything is just that way.





Exterior Features

The back panel is made of matte plastic. There is also a special rotary screw. Around the case along its perimeter stretched a metal frame. Perhaps all of these components can be called characteristic features of the phone. Plastic, by the way, is made soundly enough. A similar solution was applied to some other devices from the class of inexpensive phones. Using your finger to pull out the locking screw does not work. To do this, you will need to use some subject. In the absence of specialized tools, even an ordinary coin can be used.





Build quality

The device is assembled quite high quality. All openings were sealed. There is an additional film that provides moisture resistance. However, something in more detail about her will not work.

Security level

Defining his device in the class of protected devices, the Finnish manufacturer did not win in his head. Even if we consider the most fragile part of the design (and in almost any phone it is a screen), we can immediately note that even it is protected very effectively. Such a device can be thrown as much as you want and anywhere, and at the same time it will not be particularly harmful. On the World Wide Web you can find videos on which the company's specialists captured the test of strength and other "bullying" committed on the model. Even by today's standards, the reliability of the device is at a good level. Needless to say, then this level delighted a huge number of users.

The company conducted the most interesting test when the phone was immersed in a glass of water. In practice, such an experiment will end for the device not with instant, but with gradual death. Yes, death for the device does not come right on the same day, but immersion in a liquid will significantly reduce the period of possible operation. Let us respect the protection standards and therefore, for the sake of fairness, we recall that IP54 was designed to protect against individual splashes. But this standard will not cope with immersion in a liquid, that's for sure. That's about something, and potential buyers of the device should not forget about it.

Color schemes

From the very beginning, it was assumed that the phone will be delivered in four colors to the mobile device market. However, when sales started, on the official website of the company, the model appeared in only two solutions. This, as always, is a classic of the genre.
