Negative hotel reviews

Despite the general positive picture, there are also some shortcomings in the work of the Ic Green Palace 5 * hotel (Antalya). Reviews of tourists indicate the following negative points:

  • almost all the rooms of the hotel have slippery marble floors, and therefore there is a big risk of slipping and falling (especially in the dining room, where they constantly pour something);
  • the hotel has some kind of selective attitude towards tourists - only European guests can bring suitcases to a room;
  • you have to take places on the beach in the early morning, otherwise you will then have to sunbathe on a towel;
  • the children's pool is too cold water, and the heating function is not provided;
  • there is no protection on the beach, and therefore everyone uses it as a common one (as a result - dirt and crowding);
  • there is no special coating on the playground, and therefore it is very dusty in the heat, and after the rain there are puddles and dirt;
  • not very conscientious cleaning of the rooms - the floors do not wipe and do not vacuum;
  • on the tables in the restaurant, not tablecloths, but some paper napkins that quickly get wet and torn;
  • when you check into the room, you may not find the promised fruit and wine baskets, and therefore do not hesitate to remind employees of this defect;
  • even in cool weather, the pools do not include heating;
  • the impression is that all guests are deliberately lodged in poor rooms, so that later they knock out an extra charge for changing the room;
  • Due to a poor hood, the bathroom constantly smells damp.
