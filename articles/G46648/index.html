What is the best recreation center in Primorye?

Primorye is a unique and picturesque region in Russia! Every year a large number of tourists come here in order to enjoy a vacation on the sea coast. Those who wish can also stroll through the taiga forests and mountains. Not everyone knows what is the best recreation center in Primorye. It is impossible to answer this question unequivocally. There are a lot of similar establishments that offer a wide range of services. Living conditions are directly dependent on the cost. You can relax in Primorye starting at 500 rubles. per day. Of course, you will have to sacrifice some services, but even the lack of certain amenities will not spoil your vacation.

Almost every recreation center in Primorye offers a wide entertainment program. This includes surfing, diving, excursions, horseback riding and more. Let's look at recreation centers that have the most positive reviews from tourists.

Bali

Trinity Bay is very popular among tourists from all over the country. Those wishing to relax in these places are recommended to pay attention to the "Bali". The recreation center is located in the center of the village of Andreevka, you will need to walk about 70 meters to the coast. For housing, there are two buildings with double, triple and quadruple rooms. The bathroom is on the street. Also, tourists can live in detached houses. There is no organized food. However, all dwellings have equipped kitchens. Also on the territory there are mangal zones. Tourists who do not want to cook their own meals can order dishes in a cafe. It specializes in Caucasian cuisine. On the territory there is a guarded parking lot, swimming pool, gazebos. All the recreation centers in Primorye (Andreevka) can boast such a variety. Reviews about "Bali" are mostly only positive. Many visitors highlight good living conditions, convenient location, a wide range of entertainment. Water activities such as banana, ski, ATV and more are organized on the beach. Vacationers can book a parachute flight. The administration of the base provides rental equipment for sea fishing and diving. For those wishing to visit natural attractions, excursions are organized. Minimum cost of living - 2 500 rubles.



"Coast of dreams"

The recreation center in Primorye "Dream Coast" is located in the bay of Vostok Bay. Next to it is the village Volchanets. It has everything you need for a family vacation. The houses for living are located on the first and second lines of the coast. Conditions, furnishings and amenities depend on the cost. Prices start at 2,000 rubles. A bathroom and a shower with hot water are only in four-bed houses. The rest of the tourists will have to use the public premises located on the territory.

"Shore of Dreams" works from spring to mid-autumn. It was at this time that the most comfortable swim in the sea. The base has access to a small sandy beach, in order to reach it, you need to overcome 20 meters. On the territory there is a playground, free parking. Near the base there is a grocery store and a kiosk with essential goods.
