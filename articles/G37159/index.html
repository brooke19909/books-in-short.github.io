Hotel Sentido Ixian Grand 5 * (Rhodes, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Sentido Ixian Grand is one of the most popular and, accordingly, the best hotels in the western part of Rhodes. Many people think so. And not in vain, because there are many reasons for this. Some of them should be told.





About location

People, choosing for themselves a hotel in which they would like to stay during their stay in a foreign country, often pay attention to the place where the hotel is located. This is indeed a very important criterion. For example, if a person travels to Greece with the aim of sunbathing and swimming in the sea, it is important for him that the hotel is as close to the beach as possible. By the way, here is the first advantage of the complex. You can literally walk to the beach in 1-2 minutes. Moreover, it is private, only for guests.

By the way, the locality in which the hotel is located is called Ixia. This is a resort located in the western part of Rhodes, a few kilometers from the capital. Accordingly, Diagoras Airport can also be reached quickly.

Still very close is Mount Filerimos, and beyond it is another interesting city - Ialyssos. It is worth noting that Ixia is the best place for people who like windsurfing and waves in general. Since here they are almost always available. But the sea is clear.

And finally, a few words about the hotel itself. It is a modern complex decorated in an attractive style. It was built a long time ago - in 1980, and in 2007 they held the last renovation. The hotel is positioned as a place for a peaceful, relaxing holiday for lovers. Therefore, families with children under the age of 16 are better off looking for other options: they will not be accepted here.

Fun and relaxation

While at Sentido Ixian Grand, people can brighten up their leisure activities in different ways. It has an outdoor pool and sun terrace, bike rental, a billiard room and even a library. There is also a gym and games room. But the main way to entertain yourself here is a trip to the SPA center.

In this place you can not only relax in the jacuzzi and visit the massage room - it offers full-fledged packages of services. And both for single clients, and for couples. Many lovers like a joint visit to cosmetic and wellness treatments. And SPA offers a wide range of them. Sauna, solarium, relaxation programs, deep muscle massage, mud baths, steam room, hammam, reflexology, aroma and thalassotherapy - all this and much more, clients of the Sentido Ixian Grand SPA-center can experience for themselves. Guest reviews strongly recommend spending time on such pleasures. Everything is done here - even Swedish and sports massage.





Services

In Sentido Ixian Grand 5 * there is everything that can make the guests rest more comfortable. Starting with wireless Internet (free of charge - in public areas), ending with parking.

Guests can make use of luggage storage, concierge services, a 24-hour reception, a tour desk and currency exchange. The hotel also has a laundry room, an office with fax and photocopying services, a mini market, several various shops, a car rental service and a meal delivery service for drinks in the apartment.

By the way, in addition to the above, the hotel offers amenities for guests with disabilities and apartments with increased sound insulation.

Bars and Restaurants

There are several establishments in the complex where hotel guests can quench their thirst and hunger. You can stop by the main restaurant Sentido Ixian Grand. Ixian All Suites is known for this establishment. There for breakfast arrange a variety of plentiful buffets. And for lunch and dinner, they also offer real Greek wines to choose from. In addition, this restaurant has a very pleasant atmosphere, conducive to a good pastime for delicious food.

There is also a bar in the lobby of the hotel. It attracts attention with a chic chandelier made of Italian glass, comfortable stylish furniture and mosaics. Very nice setting. Therefore, guests here like to spend time enjoying not only the atmosphere, but also delicious beer, original drinks and exotic cocktails. By the way, the bar has access to the outdoor terrace.

There is a second bar, it can be found near the pool. Its terrace is made in the original island style. Here, guests also like to spend their time enjoying their vacation to the sounds of live music.

About accommodation

Check-in at the hotel begins, as elsewhere, at 14:00. And the departure lasts until noon. However, the reception is open around the clock, so if you agree with the administration in advance, you will be able to check in earlier (or check out later).

Possible additional accommodation. For it will have to pay 25% of the total cost of living. The property will also arrange for an extra bed in the apartment.

Speaking of accommodation, it is worth noting that in the restaurant of the Sentido Ixian Grand Hotel (Rhodes) there is a dress code. Girls are encouraged to wear dresses, while men are encouraged to wear closed shoes, a shirt, and long trousers.

Upon arrival, each of the guests is required to present their identification card. And you will also need a credit card, the details of which were indicated at the time of booking. The hotel accepts Visa, Maestro, Master Card and American Express. Potential guests should be aware that the administration may block a certain amount in the account. This acts as a guarantee of the arrival of tourists.

Guests about accommodation

Hotel Sentido Ixian Grand 5 * reviews receive mostly positive. The rooms are nice - they correspond to their description and photos. In the event that guests arrive a little earlier than check-in, and if there are no ready-made rooms available for them, they can leave their things in the luggage storage room and go to the beach.

The apartments have everything you need - the air conditioning works well, the plumbing is working, the furniture is comfortable. Good TV, happy with the presence of several Russian-language channels. Many guests note a large selection of food and drinks. There is everything - from yoghurts and oatmeal to meat delicacies, wines, cocktails and more.

And of course, many leave comments about the beach of the Sentido Ixian Grand 5 * hotel. Greece (Ixia, Rhodes) is widely known as an excellent seaside resort. Many people come here to swim. So, the hotel has its own beach. There is a part with grass (artificial turf), on which there are sun loungers and umbrellas. And another area with large pebbles. The bottom of the sea is sandy, which pleases especially sensitive people. And the depth begins almost immediately: it is worth literally 5 meters to go - and you can immediately swim. No shallow water. Accordingly, the sea is almost always clean, even after cloudy weather and rains. By the way, guests are advised to bring swimming goggles with them. The underwater world here is not as luxurious as in the zone of tropical islands, but still, diving into the depths, it is nice to see the sparkling sand and small fish.
