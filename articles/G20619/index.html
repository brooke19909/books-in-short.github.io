How to choose a car from LG

Before you go to the immediate choice, you need to decide on the maximum capacity. If the family is large, then 8 kg will be enough.

The second question that needs to be paid attention is which method of loading laundry is preferable: frontal or vertical. Both have their advantages. If the LG washing machine is installed in the kitchen, it is logical that the hostess will use its upper part as a work surface. In this case, it is necessary to choose a model with a window (front type). For those rooms that do not have a large amount of free space, a machine with a vertical loading method is suitable. As a rule, such models are small in size.

When the time comes to consider the functionality, you need to pay attention to the option "Smoothing". When it exists, clothes during drying and washing themselves are not deformed and will not rumple.
