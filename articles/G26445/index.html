How to create an image?

A monkey costume for a boy can consist of pants and a jacket or overalls. It is only a little imagination and transfer all the planned details of the suit on paper, drawing a small sketch. This will help to navigate the stages of work in the process of creating an order.

Panties and T-shirt: a quick way to cut

It should be noted right away that for a dress it is best to give preference to knitted fabrics that stretch well. This will ensure a comfortable fit. You can sew a monkey costume with your own hands from such paintings as velor, velvet, diving, terry or fleece. Naturally, the color must be taken brown.

To create a pattern of pants and a T-shirt or overalls, it is not at all necessary to trouble yourself by taking measurements, complicated calculations and creating drawings. Otherwise, everything is quite simple:

  • cut patterns;
  • transfer to fabric;
  • to cut with allowance for seams;

It should be noted that the tail will need to be sewn into the middle seam of the panties or overalls in the appropriate place. For a playful image, you can beat the ass and the midsection of the character by stitching round patches on them.

Tutu skirt for girls

For a girl, a monkey lady will be a win-win. Elastic waist size, stripe stripes 10 cm wide and a length equal to double the length of the skirt will quickly turn into a chic skirt. To do this, just tie the fabric on an elastic band. You can also complement the outfit with brown knit socks and gloves. The tail should be attached to the elastic of the skirt and brought out between the elements of the tutu skirt.

The carnival costume of the monkey must be fun and elegant. And perhaps here you can not do without bright beads around the neck. This small detail is able to instantly transform the entire outfit.





It should be noted that the combination of colors is one of the important points. Since leggings, gloves and a T-shirt should be brown, it is better to choose orange, yellow, green, red or pink for the skirt. Although the option with a rainbow skirt is also good!

How to make a tail?

The tail is one of the easiest elements to perform. For him, 10 cm are cut out of the fabric, folded face inward, a line is laid and turned out. On one edge, you can tie a knot, and the second edge is sewn into pants or attached to the elastic of the skirt.
