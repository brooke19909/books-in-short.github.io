Hotel Bach Duong Hotel 2 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): overview, description and reviews

Exotic attracts modern tourists and travelers. Virgin nature, the sound of the surf, amazing culture, opportunities for a great vacation - all this will give you Vietnam. Hundreds of thousands of tourists from different parts of the world come here every year who want to enjoy the culture, philosophy of the country, but at the same time relax in a civilized way. Vietnam will delight all lovers of exoticism at its best. It is not only beautiful, but clean enough, cozy, comfortable. And, of course, Vietnam is open for tourists all year round. That is why if you want to be in a paradise in the middle of a cold and blizzard winter, Vietnam will be a great option for you.





Of course, as is the case with any other exotic country, tourists should be extremely careful in choosing a potential place for their stay. Quite often, the description of the hotel on the official website is not true: many are faced with dirt in the rooms, broken plumbing and other not the most pleasant inconveniences that you do not want to endure on vacation. We want to bring to the attention of all tourists Bach Duong Hotel 2 *. This is a fairly popular option, despite the low category. Based on real reviews from vacationers, we will try to give him the most objective assessment.

About hotel

Quite often, the Bach Duong Hotel 3 * is credited with this particular qualification, in fact, only 2 stars correspond to it. Despite this, the hotel is modern, comfortable, cozy and reasonably priced. It is located in one of the quietest and most remote areas from the center, on the shores of a picturesque bay. At the same time, Bach Duong Hotel 2 * does not fall out of the cultural life of the resort - just a 7-10 minute walk from it there is a busy street, popular among tourists and vacationers, with many cafes, bars and restaurants. The trip to the nearest airport takes no more than 30 minutes; by local standards, this is a short distance.

Hotel location

Many tourists emphasize that the hotel is conveniently located. Bach Duong Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang) is located in a side street, not far from a local park, literally immersed in the greenery of local tropical plants. Within walking distance are all the necessary entertainment on vacation, while the hotel continues to be quite quiet and calm. You can really relax here after an eventful day.





A number of inconveniences may be caused to tourists by a construction project unfolding on the coast. The walls of the hotel are really very thin, so if you have a sensitive dream, ask the hotel to settle you on the opposite side of the hotel.

Hotel Area

Bach Duong Hotel Nha Trang 2 * is quite small, but quite cozy: tourists are invited to stay in 8 standard and 12 superior rooms. The most picky and demanding holidaymakers will appreciate the 12 deluxe rooms.

Population

Check-in takes place from 14:00, eviction takes place until 12:00. Children under 5 stay at the Bach Duong Hotel 2 * completely free of charge. Unfortunately, the staff does not understand a word in Russian, some employees speak English. Quite often, the explanation is through gestures. In fact, the staff is friendly and friendly.





Quite often, if there are free and prepared rooms, the administration does not expect an official check-in time and issues the room keys within 10-15 minutes. Agree, after a long and tiring flight, this trifle becomes a huge advantage of the hotel, to which many tourists refer in their laudatory reviews.

Rooms

It's no secret that in many respects the comfort on vacation depends not only on the location of the hotel, but also on the condition of its rooms. So, if you go to Vietnam, the Bach Duong Hotel 2 * hotel will offer you quite comfortable conditions. The air condition is responsible for the microclimate in the rooms. Of course, minor breakdowns occur, which are quickly eliminated by specialists.

To someone, a TV with satellite channels may seem like an inadmissible luxury on vacation - you come here to enjoy the silence, the pacification. However, all apartments are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, including Russian ones. Moreover, each room has a telephone, minibar and a private bathroom. The furniture is not new, but in excellent condition.

Guests pay attention that the rooms here are quite bright, spacious and, most importantly, clean. The beds are made with snow-white bedding. In the bathroom there are not only towels, but also the necessary bath accessories, by the way, of good quality. Deluxe rooms have a stunning balcony with views of the coast and local beauty. Tourists love to settle down here in the evening with a glass of wine and enjoy the sunset.





Speaking about the shortcomings of the hotel, guests note thin walls and disgusting soundproofing of the rooms - literally every step of the neighbors is heard. Not quite a pleasant fact on vacation. The rooms do not have a safe, but for an additional fee you will be kindly provided with a place to store documents, valuables and money at the reception. These are perhaps the only drawbacks of the Bach Duong Hotel Nha Trang 2 *. Accommodation prices here are more than affordable. The cost of a standard double room starts from 1000-1200 rubles per night.

Hotel infrastructure

In Vietnam, there are many stunningly beautiful places with pristine nature, which is why tourists and vacationers rent bikes at the rental point - the most popular type of transport here. It is on it that guests move along the coast. In addition, the Bach Duong Hotel 2 * hotel offers all vacationers free parking.

Nutrition

Tourists notice that the hotel does not have its own restaurant, even typical Asian breakfast options you will not see here. You can eat in local restaurants and bars, where they prepare amazing dishes of national cuisine. Not far from Bach Duong Hotel 2 * there is a cafe Tuans, quite popular among vacationers.

Some tourists buy a tour, which includes breakfast. How so? Everything is extremely simple: in this case, upon checking into the Bach Duong Hotel 2 *, they receive food stamps in a neighboring hotel.

Service

Many hotels in Vietnam can not boast of first-class service and amazing service. A completely different matter - Bach Duong Hotel 2 * (Vietnam). Upon check-in, all vacationers will receive a password from the wireless Internet. The kind staff is always ready to help, answer all your questions.





Towels are changed every day, bed linen - as they become soiled. Wet cleaning in the room is carried out regularly, without interfering with a calm and measured rest. Attentive staff tries to carry out all the work during the absence of tourists, so upon returning to the room a feeling of cleanliness and freshness is created. And all this is Bach Duong Hotel 2 *.

Reviews of tourists emphasize how comfortable it is in the rooms. At the first request of visitors, they will receive beach towels at the reception.

Beach and sea

Vacationers note that the coast is literally at hand. Just 100 meters - and you are on the beach. The hotel is really located on the first coastline. It was impossible to dream of such luxury for such little money. If you prefer a beach holiday, you will definitely like the modest Bach Duong Hotel 2 *. Tourists emphasize how stunning and beautiful the beach is. Crystal clear sea water, snow-white even sand, tropical plants that create a pleasant shade - what else is needed for relaxation. There are not many tourists here even during the season, so you will get a unique opportunity to spend your holidays in seclusion, you can enjoy nature, climate, and be alone with your thoughts. After a busy metropolis and a frantic pace of life, this is really necessary.





Tourists note that the beach is cleaned daily and even sieved using special equipment. Here, for only 3-4 dollars, you can rent a lounger with a soft mattress and an umbrella hiding from the scorching sun. By the way, before the trip you need to stock up on sunscreens with a high protection factor that will prevent burning of the skin.

Entertainment

In the evening, it is fashionable to walk along the promenade - it is beautiful and well-maintained here, tropical trees are planted along it, fountains and playgrounds for children, exercise machines, so popular in our time, are being built. From the hotel you can take some pretty interesting and inexpensive excursions. The kind staff will help you find the right option. To the most prominent landmarks of the country far enough - 13 hours by local transport.





If your vacation is limited in time and you cannot tolerate long journeys, it is better to refuse this trip, especially since its cost is quite high. It is worth noting that in the vicinity of the resort there are quite beautiful, picturesque and noteworthy places. Most of them are natural. Sports equipment and diving are available on the beach. By the way, Vietnam is a great place for those who want to dive for the first time.

To summarize

For its stars, Bach Duong Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is a hotel that many will like. The deluxe room with a balcony, which offers stunning views of the coast, is in many ways superior to hotel rooms with more stardom. Again, recall that in Vietnam, many hotels simply do not fit their category in the description. The fact that for modest money you will get a great option will surely only please you.
