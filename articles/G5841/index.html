Naiades Almiros River 3 * (Crete): hotel description and reviews

Everyone from school knows about the legendary island of Crete, which is fraught with many ancient legends. And when the question arises, where to go on summer vacation, many want to visit this piece of land, among the azure waters of the most romantic of the seas - the Mediterranean. However, many people are stopped by the thought of the high cost of living in Crete. But if one approaches this question wisely, then a very acceptable solution can be found. In most three-star hotels, the price for accommodation is very low, and the conditions are quite comfortable. So, for example, the Naiades Almiros River 3 * hotel (Heraklion, Agios Nikolaos) in Crete offers tourists excellent conditions at very affordable prices.

In Crete, Agios Nikolaos is considered the local Saint-Tropez. This is a very comfortable and sought-after resort in the entire island of Greece. It mainly attracts tourists who, in addition to beach vacations, are drawn to nightlife, because there are many opportunities to spend time for every taste. Agios Nikolaos was once built by residents of other regions of Crete, who fled from the oppression of the Turks. Here they hid from the Mohammedans, who wanted to eradicate the local inhabitants of the island. Over time, the Greeks, who are famous for their optimistic nature and craving for fun and entertainment, turned this small village into a thriving resort. Today there are many hotels of various categories - from luxury hotels to the simplest 2 * and guesthouses. However, three-star hotels such as Naiades Almiros River 3 * (Crete, Agios Nikolaos) are most in demand among average Russians.

If you want to buy a tour to the Naiades Almiros River 3 * hotel, then, of course, you will be interested in for what period it is better to buy a ticket when the weather on the island is most comfortable. The beach season opens on the island from mid-May and ends at the end of October. The hottest is August, when the air temperature warms up to +35 or more degrees. But the sea water all the time of the beach season warms up to 22-25 degrees. There is no winter as such on the island. If you are a lover of not only beach, but also intellectual relaxation, then feel free to choose the island of Minotaur, buy a tour to the hotel Naiades Almiros River 3 *. Crete with all its legends will be able to satisfy your need for intellectual entertainment. Do you know that the birthplace of the supreme Greek God Zeus the Thunderer is precisely Crete? Hotel Description Naiades Almiros River 3 *

This is a family-run hotel built in traditional Greek style. It consists of several two-story buildings. Next to it (100 m) is the wonderful beach of Almiros. It should be noted that on the island all beaches are municipal and private. Agios Naikolaos town center is 2 kilometers away. Nearby is the picturesque river Almiros. These lands are very beautiful, while the primordial nature is preserved here. Those tourists come here who want to really relax. And the hotel staff will do everything so that tourists find out what constitutes true Greek hospitality. By the way, this country has a special attitude towards children. Locals are very helpful towards small tourists. Children under 12 years old stay at this hotel for free.

Number of rooms

Judging by the number of rooms, Naiades Almiros River hotel 3 * is quite small. It has only 19 comfortable rooms in the following categories: 2-seater - twin; 3-seater; 4-bed rooms; family two-room. All rooms have balconies with outdoor furniture that open either to the garden or pool, as well as a kitchenette where you can find a tea / coffee maker. The rooms have air conditioning, a TV that also shows Russian channels, a refrigerator. The bathroom has a shower or bath, 2 sets of towels, toiletries. Housekeeping and linen change regularly.

Hotel infrastructure

The Naiades Almiros River 3 * hotel has a parking where you can rent a car. By the way, there is also a car and bicycle rental point, as well as a round-the-clock taxi service. There is a library in the hotel building. For tourists, excursion programs are organized at the hotel, for this you can contact the tour desk and get information about upcoming bus tours to certain sights on the island. Free internet is available on site. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available upon request. For young tourists there is a playground with swings and slides.





Entertainment

Like most hotels in Crete, Naiades Almiros River 3 * has a fairly spacious pool, around which there are sun loungers and umbrellas, which can be used for free. There is also a pool bar. There is billiards in the lobby. For everyone who wants to learn diving, training is first conducted by the pool, and then tourists make diving excursions. The beach, by the way, is only 100 meters from the hotel. Here when using umbrellas and deck chairs you need to pay a certain amount.

Nutrition

In Naiades Almiros River 3 * tourists are offered two types of food: half board (HB) or full board (All). A buffet is served daily three times a day. The dishes that are offered to vacationers are mostly traditionally Greek. They are cooked in a wood-burning stove, and the smoke gives the dishes a unique flavor. Natural dairy products, cheeses, fruits and vegetables are also served.





Naiades Almiros River 3 *: vacationers reviews

Do tourists like to relax in this hotel? This is what they write in their reviews. The ratings of most couples are positive. After all, there is everything for a quiet measured family vacation. But the youth, hungry for entertainment, believes that the hotel in the evenings is very boring. However, there is plenty of entertainment in Agios Nikolaos, some of which are within walking distance of the hotel, while others can be reached by taxi. Almost all tourists complain about infrequent cleaning. There were even cases when the number was not cleaned for a whole week. There are also complaints about the restaurant menu. Although it is tasty, it is not diverse, and very soon dishes can get bored. Everything else (especially the beach, nature, the possibility of excursions) is rated by tourists as a solid five.
