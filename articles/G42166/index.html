Plasticine ninja turtles: master class

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles are the characters from the well-known cartoon that all children adore. They fell in love with the children for their dexterity, agility and invincibility. To please the boys with a new toy, it is not necessary to go to the store for it. Plasticine ninja turtles - a wonderful craft that kids can create with their own hands.

Crafts from plasticine - a joy for all children





Many kids love to create crafts from plasticine . Such work contributes to the perseverance of little fidgets. Boys aged 8-10 years can easily make plasticine ninja turtles on their own. Well, if something goes wrong, they can always call for help from their parents.

Master Class

How to mold ninja turtles from plasticine and what is needed for this?

1. Before you begin to work, you need to prepare the material for modeling. In order to make a bug, you will need plasticine in green, brown, beige, white and black. You must also have a bright plasticine. For example, yellow. It is with its help that it will be possible to determine each character, because the ninja turtles from plasticine will turn out the same, but their accessories and callsigns will be different.

2. Two pieces must be separated from the green bar of plasticine and the head and trunk should be made of them.

3. To connect both parts, you need to use fasteners. For this, a match or piece of wire is suitable.

4. From green plasticine, it is also necessary to fashion the legs, and then attach them to the torso of the turtle. For a reliable connection you need to use matches.

5. Likewise, it is necessary to blind and hands. They also need to be attached to the torso of the hero.

6. To mark the fingers and toes, you need a stack.

7. For further work, green plasticine is no longer required.

8. Next, you need to take a beige material and roll out a part resembling a heart shape from it. It must be attached to the chest of the turtle. Using the stacks on the torso of the turtle, muscles should be drawn.

9. From a yellow piece of plasticine, you need to mold a ribbon and tie the eyes of a brave hero with it.

10. Using all the same yellow color, you need to fashion the rings and put them on the arms and legs of the turtle.

11. Next came the turn of brown plasticine. It is necessary for sculpting the shell. The piece should be oval and drawn on it with stacks. In the process of sculpting, the carapace can be tried on to the back so that it turns out to be suitable in size.

Conclusion





At this, the master class on creating your favorite hero is over. As you can see, the plasticine ninja turtles are made very simply and quickly. A child can make several of these toys and enjoy an interesting game. To make the characters easy to recognize, the ribbon and rings must be made of different colors.

Plasticine ninja turtles - a great craft, from which all the boys will be just crazy.
