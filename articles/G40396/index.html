Spring photo shoots - interesting ideas, poses and recommendations of professionals

With the help of a special photo shoot in nature in the spring you can realize a large number of artistic ideas. It can be performed both separately and with family, a loved one and friends. Also, a huge number of places are available for taking pictures: street, park, sea and forest. It is in the spring season on a sunny day that you can take great pictures that will retain happy moments.

Organization and holding a photo shoot in the spring

In order to convey the desired mood and take original pictures, it is necessary to think over the following points in advance: the venue of the photoset, themes, clothes and accessories of the model, desired poses and necessary details.



There are so many reasons for spring photo shoots. These include: Shrovetide, March 8 and Easter. On such days, you can come up with thematic shootings. Lovers will look very romantic while walking under colored umbrellas in the rain in the park.

After choosing the right photography option, it is important to think through different types of poses and ideas for a spring photo shoot. The following poses are perfect for girls: with a book sitting on the grass; lying or sitting on the grass; standing with one hand on the belt and the other behind the head; peering out from behind a tree.



Spring is a time of revitalization of nature, so you can capture the beauty of the world around you: growing green grass, the first warm rays of the sun, blue bright sky. The selected landscape should be in harmony with the position and image of the shooting. It is very important to choose the right ideas for a photo shoot in the spring in nature.

You can organize photography with the help of friends and relatives, and by contacting specialists in this field. For a high-quality photo shoot of a girl in the spring and getting beautiful pictures, it is very important to view ads on the Internet or in newspapers, as well as use the services of a makeup artist or stylist. They will pick up accessories and clothes suitable for the photo shoot, make the necessary hairstyle and makeup. Using the services of a professional photographer, you can find new, memorable photography subjects.

Ideas for posing in nature

You need to choose the right angle for taking pictures using its theme. But there are general basic rules for posing that will help you find the perfect pose for outdoor photos. These include the following poses:

  1. Keep your posture straight. In this case, the back should not hunch over and slouch. This can be achieved in the following way: a properly placed leg should serve as a support to the whole body.
  2. It is also very important to remain in a comfortable, relaxed and relaxed state during the photographing process. This will help make great shots.
  3. A very important point when shooting - the toes of the shoes and shoulders should not be directed in one direction, otherwise this can lead to visual weight gain.
  4. It is advisable to adhere to asymmetry, that is, if one leg is slightly bent, then the other should be in a straight position.

Original poses for a spring photo shoot

Spring is a time of awakening of nature, flowering trees and romance. Pictures at this time of the year should be as light and gentle as possible. In this case, it is advisable to pay special attention to the choice of clothing for photography: you need to choose an outfit of bright colors. To get interesting pictures, you can go to verdant glades, flower beds and flower gardens. The most winning poses for shooting in the spring are:

  1. In a pose sitting next to blooming flowers. In this case, you can change the position of the hands.
  2. Lean on a tree, resting his foot on the trunk.
  3. Lying on the green grass with a book in his hands, pretending to be busy reading.
  4. To obtain dynamic photos, you can use a relaxed pose while riding on a swing.
  5. Portrait shot with blooming lilacs.
  6. Pose sitting or lying next to flowering plants.




Themed outdoor shooting

Wide open spaces of fantasy open spring photo shoots on the street. To get excellent shots, you will need to choose a suitable costume, decide on the location of the photo shoot and choose a shooting theme.

The choice of clothing for shooting on the street is huge: for this, both unusual and original outfits, and ordinary casual clothes. The photographer will help you choose the right props and decorations. You can choose any image for photography, the main thing in this matter is fantasy and professionalism.

There are several options for posing on the streets of the city:

  1. Shooting near tree trunks.
  2. Photoshoot on the background of flowering shrubs or trees.
  3. Posing with a book on a bench.
  4. Shooting at the wall of the building.

These images reflect the sunny and light spring mood.

Focus on decoration

The scenery can be beautiful places of nature, interesting and unusual landscapes, for example: a large tree, a large green field, an old overgrown pond. In such frames, where scenery plays the main role, the model fades into the background. It can be located on the side or in the middle of the picture.



To convey the beauty of the chosen landscape, the pose of the model should convey the mood of the place itself. An option that reflects the whole spectrum of feelings during the shooting: the model becomes half-face to the photographer, folds his hands on his chest with his palms to his neck, his eyes look down and are half-closed. You can throw your head back so that your face is better visible.

Focus on the model

To focus on the model itself, it is better to choose portrait poses and full-length poses at close range. Such a spring photo shoot is used to hide flaws and emphasize their unique features.



With this shooting, you can use the full-face pose of the photographer: you should bend over and throw your hair to one side, and fold your hands on your knees. Or a pose in profile to the photographer: throw one hand behind your head and put the other on your knee.

For portrait shooting, you do not need to look directly into the lens, it is better to lean your head slightly back or turn it to the side. You can also tilt your head forward, put it on your knees, lean it with your forehead or cheek against a tree. In addition, shooting from behind is very effective. In this case, the model can turn to the photographer in the waist or neck. In this case, it is better to look into the distance so that it seems more mysterious.
