Anapa, health resort "Rodnik": driving directions, reviews and photos of tourists

A warm sea, a favorable climate, well-developed infrastructure, the prospect of a pleasant pastime - all this can offer Anapa tourists. Sanatorium Rodnik, located 5 km from the center of Anapa, in addition to all of the above, offers its guests all the necessary equipment for high-quality diagnosis and treatment. Conditions for accommodation, food and interesting leisure in the sanatorium are also at a high level.





How to get there

Those wishing to spend an unforgettable vacation and at the same time improve their health must remember that the final point they need to arrive at should be Anapa. The Rodnik sanatorium located in the city will not be difficult to find at all - it is located at 30 Pionersky Prospekt. But you can get to Anapa in several ways. The city has air and rail links with many cities of Russia and the world. Tourists who choose the air mode of transport from Anapa airport to the bus station stop can be reached by bus or taxi. There are also regular buses to the station from the train station. Shuttles (No. 134, 114, 118) regularly depart from the bus station, where you can get to the place where the sanatorium is located. Health springs and other preventive measures are ready to receive arrivals in just a few minutes.

People who prefer traveling by car can reach the sanatorium moving towards Simferopol highway. Routing is necessary depending on the point of departure.

Photo "Spring"

Going to the Rodnik sanatorium, do not forget to pack a camera in your suitcase. After all, the favorable location of the sanatorium provides excellent opportunities for shooting. Thanks to the magnificent park located near the sea, a well-groomed territory with numerous flower beds and other attributes of a pleasant stay, you can replenish your home collection with new beautiful photographs. Upon returning home, you will remember for a long time the wonderful vacation that Anapa - sanatorium "Rodnik" gave you.





Photos of the territory, the dining room, rooms, procedural, local attractions, the beach and other objects typical of Anapa will also be needed if you want to recommend this sanatorium to your friends or relatives for rest and recreation. After all, it’s one thing to paint the benefits for a long time, and it’s quite another to show a photo where you can see everything with your own eyes.

Medical profile

On the territory of the sanatorium there is a medical building with an area of ​​5000 m², on the basis of which it is possible to conduct an accurate diagnosis and high-quality treatment. In addition, it is here that mineral water sources are located, attracting tourists, whose destination becomes Anapa. The spring sanatorium is not the only one. Firstly, this is source No. 5 of low-saline medicinal water, which has been supplied to the pump room, which has been working on the territory of the sanatorium since 2007. It is open to the thirtieth anniversary of the institution. Secondly, a source of highly mineralized hydrogen sulfide-sulfide water of the Matsesta type, which is used for medicinal baths, and thirdly, a source of highly mineralized iodine-bromine chloride-sodium water, reminiscent of the Dead Sea water for filling pools and baths.





To everyone who was confident that the sea is the only thing Anapa is famous for, the Rodnik sanatorium will prove the opposite. People come here with diseases of the nervous system, respiratory system, circulatory system, genitourinary system, diseases of the ears, throat, nose, female genital organs, metabolic disorders, urological diseases, and leave it in a much better condition.

Diagnostic and treatment base of the sanatorium

High-quality treatment is impossible without an accurate diagnosis, and for this the resort has all the necessary equipment, as well as experienced specialists, including an allergist-immunologist, therapist, gastroenterologist, surgeon, pediatrician, ultrasound doctors, neurologist, rehabilitation medicine doctor, otolaryngologist, functional diagnostics doctor, urologist, dentist, dermatologist, nutritionist and anesthetist-resuscitator.

As for the treatment, in addition to the basic procedures, there are many opportunities for additional ones, such as, for example, hydropathic showers, aroma, phyto, aero, thalasso, terrencuro, helio, speleo and oxygen therapy, whirlpool baths, massage procedures, co-therapy, colonoproctology, paraffin-ozokeritotherapy, drug electrophoresis, etc.

Clients of the sanatorium can undergo a course of procedures according to one of the main developed programs and ask doctors to draw up an individual treatment or rehabilitation program for them, the implementation of which can be provided by the sanatorium. Health springs, a unique climate and the proximity of the sea play a fundamental role in the healing of customers.





Documents required for arrival

Going to the Rodnik sanatorium (Anapa), you need to know in advance what documents are necessary for adults and children to check in. So, you will need a voucher, passport (required with a registration mark) or a birth certificate, if it is a child, a medical insurance policy and a spa card. When settling children, two additional certificates must be additionally provided - about vaccinations and the epidemiological environment.

Accommodation in a sanatorium

253 rooms in 8 dormitories are offered to guests by the Rodnik sanatorium. Anapa has other hotels on its territory where you can stay, but not every one of them can boast of the same convenient, equipped with all necessary territory and rich medical and diagnostic base, as this health resort. There are single and double rooms. The latter are divided into standard one-room and two-room superior comfort. At the same time, the sanatorium can accommodate up to 420 people.

Each room is equipped with a comfortable bed, TV, split system, shower, refrigerator, bathroom. Some rooms have exits to the balcony, loggia or terrace - depending on the type of room and the building in which it is located. Double rooms located in buildings 1, 2, 5 and 7, upon request, can be equipped with an extra bed - a sofa or an armchair-bed.





Depending on the room category, bed linen and towels are changed every 3 or 6 days. Some of the superior rooms are equipped with an iron and an electric kettle.

Nutrition

Tasty and varied dishes await everyone who has stayed in this comfortable institution, whose location is Anapa. Rodnik (sanatorium) is equipped with the most modern equipment for treatment, but they have not forgotten about healthy nutrition here. Residents of economy-class rooms and standard rooms are offered 4 meals a day according to the menu-order system. Vacationers who prefer superior rooms are served on a buffet. It is worth noting that residents of other rooms can also receive similar meals, subject to additional payment.

No matter which system you choose for nutrition, you can be sure of the quality of the food. After all, the sanatorium is not only a place of comfortable rest, but also treatment and recovery, and this is impossible without healthy regular meals. Moreover, Rodnik employees do not recommend eating outside the sanatorium, for example, during sightseeing, which Anapa is rich in. Sanatorium "Rodnik" takes care of the health of its guests and is responsible for them, which can not be said with confidence about other institutions.





Opportunities for leisure at the sanatorium

In the "Spring" there are all conditions not only for medical and recreational activities, but also for spending pleasant leisure time in free from procedures and other activities. On the territory of the sanatorium with an area of ​​5 hectares, there is a private park with many exotic plants and benches where you can sit in the shade and enjoy the surrounding nature.

Vacationers who can’t imagine their life without physical activity, Rodnik offers a modern gym, tennis court, 6 tables for playing table tennis, an outdoor pool with an area of ​​300 m², a basketball court.

Own beach with sunbeds and umbrellas at a distance of 200 m - this is another advantage that the Rodnik sanatorium has at its disposal. Anapa has a lot of attractions on its territory that you can see during an excursion organized from the sanatorium.

Holidays with children in the sanatorium "Rodnik"

The sanatorium "Rodnik" is designed as a place for recreation and treatment of the whole family. Children from 4 years old are admitted to the procedures. For children up to this age, a discount of 60% of the cost of the permit is provided, but without the provision of food and space. Children from 4 to 8 years old get a 40% discount on the ticket, and they are provided with a berth (folding bed) and meals. The discount for children 8-13 years old is 25%, a chair-bed and meals are provided. Children over 13 years old settle in the sanatorium under the same conditions as adults.

On the territory of the "Spring" there is a children's playground, a games room with teachers and a pool for children. There is also animation and a special menu for the youngest guests of the sanatorium. In the dining room you can ask for a high chair.





Reviews about the sanatorium

The opening date of the sanatorium is 1973, so today there are already quite a lot of people whose place of rest and treatment has become "Rodnik" (sanatorium, Anapa), reviews, respectively, have accumulated the most diverse over the years. It’s probably better to start with the good. So, everyone praises the well-groomed green territory of the sanatorium, the excellent medical base, the polite staff, the wealth (but also not too much!) Of fun, the presence of three mineral springs.

However, there are also shortcomings, which slightly overshadows the positive impressions that Anapa can give. The sanatorium "Rodnik" received negative reviews in 2014 due to uncomfortable rooms and poor nutrition. Many complain about the lack of fruit, the uniformity of the side dishes and too small portions. Although the food in this resort is a normal diet and, in principle, it is unlikely it can be different. People who suffer from a number of diseases remain satisfied with such a menu.

Additional services

Travelers in their own vehicle are provided with a parking space located in the resort. The Rodnik Hotel offers a library, a cinema and concert hall, day and evening animation, a dance floor, free Wi-Fi in Internet cafes and rooms, a bar and a restaurant. On the territory of the sanatorium there is a branch of Sberbank and an ATM, pharmacy, gift shop.

For an additional fee, guests can use the services of the aesthetic beauty center, organize business events in the conference room, visit the sauna, hammam, hairdresser. There is also a tour desk where you can sign up for educational trips to Anapa and other cities of the Black Sea coast.
