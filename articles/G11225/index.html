Residential complex "Cascade" (Nizhny Novgorod): sale of apartments with improved layout

Another three-sectional residential building with an interesting architectural concept will decorate a dynamically developing city. This is a residential complex "Cascade". Nizhny Novgorod, famous for its historical monuments and museums, is surrounded by functional multi-storey buildings that give it a modern look.

LCD "Cascade" is located in the center of the developed area at the address: st. Lopatin, 9. The house and the surrounding area are designed to provide high comfort for both adults and young children. The complex includes:

  • two levels of underground parking for 194 cars;
  • surface parking for 40 cars;
  • well-maintained playground;
  • recreation areas.

The successful location of the complex and developed infrastructure make living in it convenient: nearby are the main public transport routes, kindergartens, schools, numerous shops, salons, sports clubs, a hospital, a cinema and a shopping and entertainment center.





The residential complex "Cascade" (Nizhny Novgorod) is a fifteen minute drive from the city center. Trekking to the nearest stop will take no more than five minutes.

What kind of apartments will Cascade offer (residential complex)?

Nizhny Novgorod is a millionaire city that requires an appropriate standard of living for its citizens.

"Cascade" is ready to offer apartments of any type and price category, from one-room apartments with finishing "economy" to four-room spacious with decoration "comfort". Each apartment has a bright large kitchen, a balcony or a loggia, panoramic glazing. As a bonus - a separate place for the air conditioner.

The apartment is:

The residential complex "Cascade" (Nizhny Novgorod) has a typical apartment project. However, at the request of the owner, you can change the layout and make it individual.

Construction technology

The residential complex is being built taking into account modern building technologies. Monolithic frame walls are made of reinforced concrete with the use of gas silicate blocks, insulation and plaster. Partition walls made of gas ridge panels. The standard ceiling height is 2.65 m.





The residential complex "Cascade" (Nizhny Novgorod) is being built according to the latest technologies, quickly and efficiently, which increases the functional characteristics of the apartments.

Modern materials used to decorate the external walls make the facade beautiful and durable. The front rooms are equipped with passenger and freight elevators.
