Here, travelers are offered a wide base of comfortable hotels and a rich excursion program. If you plan to spend your vacation here and are looking for a great hotel, then pay attention to Serenity Makadi Beach (Hurghada) 5 *.

Travel Reviews: Serenity Makadi Beach (Hurghada)

Impressions of travelers about the hotel itself, as well as its location and territory

According to our compatriots, Serenity Makadi Beach (Hurghada) has a very large, green and well-groomed territory. There are many picturesque places, artificial ponds, many pools. So you will have a place to take a walk and hold several photo shoots. However, outside the hotel there is only desert, so there is nothing special to do there. Also, many tourists liked the proximity of the hotel complex to the airport. So, from Hurghada's air harbor, you can get here in about half an hour.

As for the housing stock, the hotel rooms are described as spacious, comfortable and well-decorated. According to them, they have everything they need, and the furniture and plumbing are in excellent condition. Everything works without interruption. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer, and bathrobes, slippers and a variety of bath amenities are regularly provided.

Cleaning in the apartments of Serenity Makadi Beach Hurghada (Egypt), reviews of tourists confirm this, is carried out daily. Linen and towels are always clean and white, without stains. They are updated several times a week. As for the Internet, it can be used free of charge in the hotel lobby. For an additional fee, you will be given access to Wi-Fi in the room. Travel Comments on Beach Vacations and Activities

According to our compatriots, despite the fact that two hotels use the beach here at once, there are no problems with sunbeds and umbrellas, since the beach is quite large and wide, as a result of which there is enough space for everyone. It is located directly next to the hotel complex. You can enter the sea here both from the pier (immediately deep), and from the shore (in this case, after a few meters, corals begin to meet at the bottom, so it is advisable to swim in special shoes). Experienced travelers are advised to everyone staying at Serenity Makadi Beach (Hurghada) to be sure to bring along or buy a mask on the spot. Indeed, in coastal waters there is a mass of very different marine animals, which is very interesting to watch.

As for entertainment, you will not have to miss the hotel. So, here you can play golf, tennis, volleyball, boccia, visit the gym and have a good time at one of several pools (some of them are filled with salt water, there is also a heated pool). In addition, you can go to the water park at a neighboring hotel (for a fee).

As for the animation in Serenity Makadi Beach (Hurghada), then, according to most tourists, it is rather weak. In general, this hotel is more suitable for people who want to spend a relaxing vacation on the shores of the beautiful sea. If you crave noisy entertainments and adventures, then it is better to find another option for accommodation.
