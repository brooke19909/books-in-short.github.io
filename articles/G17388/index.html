How to make a tree of paper and more!

There are so many ways to make a tree out of paper. This includes the most simple applications, which even a child in a kindergarten can afford. You can also make a volumetric model of napkins, for example. Another option for this kind of fakes is various souvenirs - decorative and voluminous. In general, this is a fairly large field for creativity, in which the main thing is not to limit the flight of one's imagination. This article will describe completely different options for how to make a tree out of paper. The simplest of them - this is the first - of napkins and thin wires. The other two are a little more complicated, but even a novice master can cope with them.





Most simple

The easiest way to create such crafts is from napkins, colored paper and thin wire. In addition to these materials, in the process, we will need side cutters, pliers, scissors, glue, a ruler, cotton wool, a pattern, a flower pot filled with soil or sand, and a simple pencil. Immediately make a wire frame of your choice. Next, the lower part of the trunk is installed in the pot and securely fixed. In order to hide the soil or sand, the top of the pot is covered with cotton wool. Then each of its branches and the trunk are wrapped with napkins. It will be better if they are brown. In extreme cases, they can be painted with watercolors. At the same time, they are attached with glue and small pieces of wire. Then apples or pears are cut out of colored paper. At the final stage of how to make a tree of paper in this case, the cut fruit is attached to its branches with glue. After the glue has dried, the craft is ready.





Volumetric tree

Volumetric tree made of paper is made according to a completely different algorithm. The set of materials and tools in this case is much simpler - it consists of glue, scissors, a set of colored paper, pencil, patterns and a ruler. At the first stage, several blanks are made. In this case, the right side of the first of them should be exactly the same as the second left. A similar situation with the right fragment of the second and left third. And so on, until the whole circle is completed. The last part of the tree, the right fragment should be the same as the first part of the tree. All blanks are bent strictly in the middle. Their identical sides are glued. After the glue has dried, the tree is decorated. The easiest way to make a Christmas tree.

Decorative tree

Another interesting craft is a decorative tree made of paper and wire. The set of tools and materials is exactly the same as in the first case, only it needs to be supplemented with tape or electrical tape. A pot of soil or sand is covered with cotton wool. Then a barrel of wire is installed in it. The same branches are attached to it. For greater reliability, they can be fixed with tape or tape. The petals are cut out of colored paper and are similarly mounted on branches. When the tree has the required number of leaves, the craft is ready.





Summary

This article has described three ways to make a tree out of paper. The first one is the simplest, the other two are a little more complicated. But still, all of them are affordable for any adult, regardless of level of training. Each of them, regardless of complexity, is quite spectacular, and such an craft can be a good gift.
