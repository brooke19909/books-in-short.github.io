DIY Christmas paraphernalia

With the onset of the New Year holidays, a special magical atmosphere appears in each house, because this time of year everyone is waiting for - both children and adults. Everyone knows how important the New Year paraphernalia during this period is to create the New Year mood. With the appearance of flickering garlands on the streets, the smell of hot mulled wine, Christmas songs and the smell of tangerines, I want to fill my house with magic. In this article, we will analyze the popular New Year paraphernalia in 2018, as well as how you can decorate your house for the holiday.

This is the most magical time of the year ...

Before the most New Year's and wonderful night of the year, it is necessary to proceed to decorate your home. For a whole year boxes with New Year paraphernalia stood on the mezzanine, and now is the time to get them! Take out the Christmas tree toys, hang the garlands, arrange Christmas installations and various themed figurines on the shelves. If you do not use an artificial Christmas tree, take care of the purity of your home and the longevity of the living tree - buy and decorate the New Year's beauty a day or two until the last day of the year.





Christmas paraphernalia is incredibly important for creating a special atmosphere in your home. Until you have installed a beautiful Christmas tree in the place of honor in the apartment, proceed to decorate the house with the help of purchased and home-made decorations.

Paper garlands - simple and beautiful

Agree, in order to feel the approach of the holiday, it is enough to decorate the house with winter decorations, for example, garlands. Hang an electric garland around the room. It is best to use battery-powered devices, as they are independent of the location of the power outlets in the rooms and save your energy costs. If you do not have such garlands, provide your wired jewelry with access to outlets.





After you have decorated the room with purchased garlands, proceed to create your own Christmas attributes. The simplest and most cost-effective paper garlands will be. You can create completely different decorations, wielding only with scissors, sparkles and glue, sometimes adding a stapler to your assistants. From paper you can create a wide variety of garlands - in the form of rings (as we were taught in childhood), snowflakes, Christmas trees, snowmen, as well as any other shapes. In addition to the shape, you can also choose the color, and the presence or absence of sparkles.

Needles in home decoration

New Year's attributes are not only something from artificial materials, but also various wreaths and garlands from coniferous branches. It is this smell that we associate with the onset of the New Year, and even products from this material bring comfort and warmth to the house.





In order to create an ornament of fir branches that will last you several weeks, you need to tie small twigs to the base of wire, cardboard or polystyrene using a fishing line or rope. After that, add some bright ribbons, small toys and artificial snow from the spray can to the ends of the branches, creating a truly Christmas decoration. You can hang such a garland or wreath above the door or fireplace and on the railing of the stairs, if you live in a private house.
