Getting Ready for the New Year Masquerade: Deer Costume

New Year is truly a magical holiday. This day of the year becomes especially long-awaited for kids: sweets, gifts, fun. They are looking forward to the tale, because in the New Year you can turn into a super-hero, an astronaut, a princess or a charming deer. Learn how to make a deer costume with your own hands from the article.

Jumpsuit for the little ones

New Year's masquerade is always very exciting. Creating an image, searching for clothes, finishing touches in the form of accessories and makeup - preparing for the festive event brings a lot of fun, especially for mothers, because it is they who first of all ask themselves how to make a New Year's costume with their own hands? So, if you want your baby to appear in the image of a deer on New Year's Eve, check out the instructions for creating an assistant Santa Claus costume .

Overalls suit

In general, a deer costume can be created from a ready-made jumpsuit. The main thing is that it should have a brownish tint, the fabric should be plain and soft (plush, wool, fur) - in general, it should resemble a deer skin as much as possible. You should also pay attention to the fact that there is a hood on the overalls that will fit snugly to the head.



Tip: put your baby in a diaper - this will prevent the occurrence of awkward situations.

Homemade Accessories

What is a deer costume without the good old horns?

Do not forget to warm the baby by putting on white tights and a turtleneck underneath. You can add a finishing touch to the hood by attaching the horn gloves made of two white cotton-filled gloves to the hood. The fur tail will be a charming addition to the costume.

Horns can also be made from a wide head hoop. On them, by means of a rigid wire, twist the deer horns, and then wrap them with material such as cotton wool or twine.
