Hotel "Ugra" (Khanty-Mansiysk): description of rooms, service, reviews

Hotel "Ugra" in Khanty-Mansiysk, in Western Siberia, has three stars on its facade. But, despite such a low classification, this is one of the best hotels in the city. It belongs to the Cronwell Inn line.

The hotel is located in the very center of the city, offers business travelers and travelers comfortable rooms with all amenities. The hotel is perfectly suited for business trips.

In this article we will tell you about how much a berth costs here, what are the living conditions, how is the hotel fed and convenient for travelers. We will also consider what guests have said about this hotel.

How to get and what is nearby

The hotel is located at: Khanty-Mansiysk, st. Komsomolskaya, 32.

It is best to get to the city by air. Khanty-Mansiysk Airport is located 5 km from the hotel. You can fly here from Moscow, Novosibirsk, Omsk, Tyumen, Surgut.

From the local hub go no more than 20 minutes by public transport. To do this, take the bus number 5 or minibuses number 12 and 16.

But if you decide to go by train, then it is more difficult. The nearest train station is almost three hundred kilometers from the hotel. So it will take a long time to get from the train.

But in the immediate vicinity of this hotel in Khanty-Mansiysk - just a 10-minute drive away - there is a city bus station. Here comes transport from Novy Urengoy, Nizhnevartovsk and other cities. And in the summer, ships go to the river Salekhard and Kondinsky.

Shopping centers and business districts from the hotel can be said to be close at hand. So, if you come on a business trip, you practically do not have to spend money on public transport. But if you need remote areas of the city, then near the entrance to the hotel there are bus stops of different routes.

Taxis are also on duty nearby. In walking distance a beautiful alley, "House of a journalist."

Khanty-Mansiysk

In recent years, this Russian city in the heart of Siberia has been turning from an ordinary taiga village into a real metropolis. The fact is that Khanty-Mansiysk was in the center of the oil industry.

This, in fact, led to the fact that a lot of people go to the city - both on business and just for fun. Here is the Cromwell Inn Ugra Hotel. Since the city itself was built mainly in the XXI century, the buildings here are planned in the original modernist style.

Interestingly, here, unlike most Russian cities, there is not a single monument to Lenin. The city is located at the confluence of the Ob and Irtysh rivers. There is an observation deck, as well as a monument to the pioneers of the Ugra region.

Hotels

Although this city is small (only 98 thousand people live in it), it is one of the richest industrial centers in Russia. Therefore, prices in Khanty-Mansiysk pretty bite.

A room in a hotel for a day can cost from 600-700 rubles (this is the most budget option, far from the center) to 5-6 thousand (this is for a chic complex with a thermal pool and sauna). As you can see, the scatter is great. Among the best hotels in Khanty-Mansiysk are called "Tarey", "On seven hills" and "Cromwell Inn".

These hotels offer the best value for money. Traveling tourists and middle-class tourists stop here. The city also rents many apartments for both one night and for longer stays.

Territory and infrastructure

Hotel "Ugra" in Khanty-Mansiysk is a five-story building with an elevator. Near it there is a parking place. On the ground floor of the hotel are the lobby, reception and a spacious lounge.

There is also a restaurant. The lobby has 2 ATMs, a gift shop and a left-luggage office for guests who have already rented a room.

The business center at the reception can help travelers to scan the necessary documents or send a fax, as well as work on the computer. Business center services are paid and depend on what you need to do.

Prices

To settle for a day at the Ugra Cronwell Inn Hotel, you need to pay at least 3250 rubles. This is the cost of a single room.

The minimum price for a comfort room is 3680 rubles. But you will not give this money away for one person, but for the whole room. The price of the studio starts at 5300 rubles, and the luxury starts at 6600.

If you want to include meals in the price, then half board will cost you 650 rubles. per person per day. You can eat at the hotel three times. Full board will cost 1300 rubles.

According to tourist reviews, accommodation prices are quite normal. But the guests described the cost of food and services as overpriced.

Hotel "Ugra" (Khanty-Mansiysk): reviews

Travelers think the hotel has a very good location. The hotel has many regular customers who leave positive feedback about it and often stay here.

However, there are critical reviews. Some claim that the hotel is in need of repair, the rooms are very "tired" and are just sleeping places. The shower cabin does not have partitions, water drains directly to the floor, into the hole. Therefore, special shoes are needed.

In addition, due to the fact that the building is panel, the rooms can be cool. But if you ask, bring a heater. Internet in the restaurant and lobby - with good speed. But in the rooms - where he catches - quite slow.

Nevertheless, most of the people who stayed here (both tourists and those who have come to the city on business) claim that this is one of the best hotels in the center of Khanty-Mansiysk.

The staff is polite, fed well and satisfying, although without frills. The rooms are clean. Parking is convenient. The best reviews about this hotel relate to its location and cuisine.
