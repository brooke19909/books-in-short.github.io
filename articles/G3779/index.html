Smartphones with a TV tuner: specifications and photos

Modern smartphones with a TV tuner include maximum features. Most issues related to the Internet can also be resolved using this unit if you have a network connection. Watching television broadcasts allows you to spend your free time. This function is not particularly popular, however, we will pay attention to devices with such an option. There are not many representatives on the domestic market selling such devices. The vast majority of them are of Chinese origin.





general information

The first smartphones with a TV tuner appeared long before the popularization of touch phones. The Finnish company Nokia has successfully produced devices with retractable antennas for receiving television signals. This brand was later overbought by competitors from China. After activating the Android operating system, the need for antennas has disappeared. Nevertheless, with the increase in the diagonal of the screens of gadgets, this "chip" again became relevant. Only now is the antenna optionally attached additionally. It can be hidden in the case or included in the design of the headset.

In some respects, watching television broadcasts is possible from almost any modern device. There is one thing but. These features appear only when connected to the Internet. In addition, a weak signal will not allow you to normally watch your favorite series or other program. Smartphones with a built-in TV tuner do not require mobile Internet or a Wi-Fi access point. Watching programs is carried out on the principle of a standard TV, only in a mobile version. There are not many similar models on the market right now. Consider the features and characteristics of the most popular variations.

ZTE Leo M1

These smartphones with TV tuner are made in China. At first glance, they look like standard devices. At the same time, the devices do not differ in their original design, advanced operating system and advanced processor.

This model stands out at a low price, the ability to use two SIM cards and a built-in analog tuner, which makes it possible to watch television programs in good quality. The receiving antenna is the headset supplied as standard. Watching television is done using the built-in application.





Smartphone Asus with a TV tuner

This mobile device allows you to watch digital television anywhere. The model is called ZenFone Go. The built-in tuner supports most TV formats. To make it possible to view channels, you must first connect to the device a special antenna that is supplied with the kit. This device is a kind of adapter for headphones with a diameter of 3.5 mm. At the same time, headphones themselves are optional. For a more comfortable use of the function in question, a special application is provided.

The main parameters of the Asus smartphone with a TV tuner are listed below:

  • Display - 5.5 inches with a resolution of 1280 * 720 pixels.
  • The processor is a quad-core version of the Qualcomm Snapdragon 400 type.
  • RAM - 2 GB.
  • The front camera is 12 megapixels with an f / 2.0 aperture.
  • The battery capacity is 3010 mAh.
  • System management - Android 5.1 Lollipop program with a proprietary shell from ASUS.




Smartphone with DVB T2 TV tuner

Geniatech Corporation introduces a miniature device on the market that allows you to watch television broadcasts on your tablet or smartphone. The tuner in question works on Android, at least version 4.1.2. The device is connected via the USB interface.

Such a device allows you to watch television programs where there is a connection to the DVB-T2 signal. Direct visibility of the TV tower is not required, but it should be nearby. To receive a signal outside the city, you will need to connect a powerful antenna, an adapter to which is included in the equipment package. The Geniatech PT360 device is a full-fledged type, it is suitable for operation in any conditions where a digital pulse of the desired range arrives. Broadcasting is much more efficient and easier in the city than in remote places where the use of powerful antennas is required.

Explay StarTV

This is another smartphone with a Chinese-made TV tuner. It was released in 2013, equipped with a 3.5-inch display. The modern device does not have particularly outstanding characteristics, but it is equipped with Android 2.3 software, cameras with a resolution of 0.3 and 3.2 megapixels. Especially communicate on the Internet on this unit will not work. It seems that the manufacturer released it for a specific purpose - to ensure stable television broadcasting. Nevertheless, the device found its consumer.





LG Optimus VU

The famous Korean company did not lag behind its competitors and released its mobile "TV". Below are the characteristics of this gadget:

  • The diagonal of the display is 5 inches.
  • Year of release - 2012.
  • Aspect ratio - 4/3.
  • The tuner is of the built-in type, it is great for watching television programs.

The model in question differs not only in that it allows you to enjoy digital television, but also performs well in other functions of the smartphone.

Texet TM607-TV

This modification can not boast of a high-quality display and heavy-duty processor. However, the smartphone is equipped with a retractable antenna, which gives it a unique uniqueness. Thanks to this innovation, you can easily watch your favorite movie or sports match on a 3.5-inch screen.

Alcatel Tribe 3041

Smartphones with a digital TV tuner from the French manufacturer are distinguished by good quality parameters. You can watch a television program at lunchtime, on the way to work or at another convenient time. The device differs from its competitors in a wide range of colors. The series presents 8 body colors, among which a girl or a man can choose the suitable modification.
