RN Bank: car loan reviews, order of receipt, repayment conditions

People who live in the capital or any other major city simply can not do without a car. This is not surprising, because it usually takes a lot of time to get from one point to another. Few people want to ride in a stuffy bus or hustle in the subway. Not everyone can afford a new high-quality car. In this case, people resort to a car loan service.

A similar type of loan is issued today by almost every bank. However, there are separate organizations that provide money on credit on more favorable terms. For example, RN Bank can be attributed to them, reviews of car loans in which differ mainly in positive ratings. This is primarily due to low interest rates and interesting programs for customers.

Affiliate program "Renault-Nissan"

Before considering reviews of car loans at RN Bank, it is worth paying attention to interesting information. This financial institution is a partner of the Renault concern, which, as you know, owns Nissan production.

It is not surprising that he provides the most favorable conditions for vehicles from these manufacturers. The rates for the program can vary greatly depending on the specific model, as well as the down payment and the period for which the loan will be issued. In addition, you need to consider the risks that the borrower will insure.

Features of lending

Reviews on car loans at RN Bank often say that not every car can be purchased in installments. But if you choose one of the car dealership offers, you can save a lot.

For example, in customer reviews, you can see that in some cases the overpayment is at all 0%. This is because the bank periodically holds shares, according to which overpayment on the loan is not charged. However, one must understand that in this case the nominal rate remains, which, as a rule, is slightly higher than when applying for another type of loan. But, regardless of this, the buyer still gets a very nice discount, due to which the overpayment is almost equal to 0.

Features of a car loan at RN Bank

First of all, it is worth noting that this financial institution does not have its own branches and branches. Accordingly, in order to get a loan, you must contact the seller in the salon, which will redirect the client to the loan specialist. He will help fill out a questionnaire and get a response from the bank.

If the user receives an approving response, then in this case a standard contract of sale is concluded. Since, in order to receive a car loan from PH Bank, Casco is a compulsory service, then after this insurance is issued.

At the next stage, the happy owner of the car must make an initial payment and only after that draw up a loan agreement. When all the formalities are completed, you can arrange the car for yourself and transfer it as a guarantee to a financial institution. This does not mean that you cannot use the car. Just the car owner does not have the right to sell this vehicle, as it is part of a contractual relationship.

If we talk about vehicles, then it is worth paying attention that for each brand of machine its own conditions are developed. Therefore, taking part in such an affiliate program, you should first study all the subtleties of processing credit relations and other conditions.

Features of lending

As a rule, when applying, the client can count on up to 4.5 million rubles. At the same time, a loan is issued for a period of up to 7 years, and the interest rate can be 0% if you successfully get into the stock. What documents will be required

According to reviews of car loans at RN Bank, no additional securities are required. First of all, you need a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation and proof of income in the form of an appropriate certificate.

Sometimes managers are asked to provide an additional work book or a photocopy of its pages. SNILS will also be required, and if a person has performed military service, then documents confirming this. Separately, it is worth considering the existing programs and tariffs for new customers.

Renault Credit

Under this program, lending conditions may vary, unlike a particular machine model. RN Bank: early repayment of a car loan

Like most bank loans, such lending involves the payment of the full amount until the end of the contract. But in this case, you need to pay attention to several nuances.

If we consider the reviews on the early repayment of a car loan at RN Bank, then it is worth paying attention that this procedure can also be performed online. Although the official portal of a financial institution does not allow you to arrange an initial loan online, this does not apply to contractual contributions. Therefore, you can repay the loan without leaving your home.

If we talk about how to early repay a car loan at RN Bank, then first of all, you need to notify the employee of the financial organization of his intention in 30 days. To do this, you can use the feedback on the bank's website. After drawing up the relevant application, the borrower receives an SMS with data on the amount that he must pay in order to close the loan.
