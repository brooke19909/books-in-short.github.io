India: photos, nature and attractions





If you want to see a truly stunning beauty, you should see a photo of India. Nature in this country is striking in its splendor, you can admire it endlessly. Vast forests are widespread in the state. The slopes of the Himalayas are also entirely covered with vegetation. The Hindustan Peninsula is famous for its tropical forests. They are characterized by fairly tall trees with dense crowns. Because of them, of course, the entire lower part of the forest is shaded. Making your way among the trees, you can find abundant bamboo thickets.

Mysterious india

India can be called a very mysterious country, at all times it attracted travelers precisely because of its mystery. And at present, people have not lost interest in her. Tourists admire the sights this country is so rich in. It is impossible to convey in words how beautiful India is. Photos, the sights on which look very tempting, amaze with their beauty.





Divine country

India is among those nations whose cultural heritage makes a strong impression on travelers. Therefore, it is very interesting to relax here. Each tourist is recommended to make a list of the most attractive places that you want to visit in advance, otherwise you can not calculate the time and do not have time to see everything.
If there is a country that can be called not just wonderful, but divine, then this is India. Photos, the sights of which are truly mesmerizing, tourists in large quantities post on their pages on the Internet, so that others have the opportunity to enjoy this paradise.

Amazing Architecture, Taj Mahal

Already on the first day of their stay in India, everyone notes the unusual architectural style that is prevalent here. Many places of worship are distinguished by their outstanding appearance: attractive forms and magnificent painting are also characteristic of them. Some tourists, having just arrived in the country, immediately seek to visit the Taj Mahal, located in Agra. Such zeal is justified, because this architectural monument has long been considered a symbol of the state, in addition, it is one of the newest seven wonders of the world. And day and night, the marble mausoleum, complemented by all kinds of semiprecious stones, attracts everyone's attention. India, whose photo is made to freeze with admiration, may seem like a fabulous place, so everything is unusual here.





Virupaksha Temple and Harmandir Sahib

Every tourist should admire the Virupakshi Temple located in Hampi. It is one of the oldest Hindu religious buildings in which ceremonies are still held today. Harmandir Sahib Temple is also beautiful, many are fascinated by its rich history. It is recommended to visit it in the evening, when special lighting is turned on, making it even more beautiful. In the darkness a golden radiance emanates from him, and it is not surprising that many tourists wish to see this. India, whose photo often motivates people to think about visiting this country, is famous for this wonderful temple.

What is Goa interesting for?

Once visiting Goa, it is impossible to forget all his life. There are many opportunities for relaxation. The first thing to do is to go to one of the beaches, of which there are many. Vagator and Palolem are distinguished by their extraordinary beauty. The highest concentration of tourists is observed on the latter.

When the beach holiday is boring, you can go to the Basilica of Jesus, by the way, it is a UNESCO heritage site. This architectural monument attracts many travelers to Goa. India, whose photo is a real delight, is generally rich in places of worship.

Dudhsagar Waterfall, Panaji City





An unforgettable impression on admirers of natural beauty is made by a waterfall called Dudhsagar, the name of which means "milk of the ocean." It was so named because the water in it has an unusual hue.

Those who are fortunate enough to visit the city of Panaji will also not be bored. In this village there is a street on June 18, which is a large market. Many visitors to the country tend to get here, because there is everything that a person needs: from all kinds of seasonings to jewelry. India, whose photo is usually full of bright colors, has many markets with a large assortment of goods.

This is only a small part of the interesting places recommended for visiting. However, the above attractions are considered the minimum, which should be familiar to all tourists. India, whose photo can often be called true works of art, leaves a deep mark in the heart of every traveler.
