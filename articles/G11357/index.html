The island of Cyprus and its resorts: overview, description, attractions and reviews of tourists

Cyprus is considered to be a fashionable island, a trip to which, although expensive, but will definitely leave a tourist an unforgettable experience. The capital of this state is the city of Nicosia. The island of Cyprus and its resorts offer a variety of leisure activities, both for those who seek pleasure at noisy sea parties, and for travelers who prefer cognitive leisure (or, for example, relaxing with children). This island is the third largest in the Mediterranean. And in this article you will find out what Cyprus can offer you. An overview of the island with resorts is presented below.





Important information

Half of the island is mountainous. The highest point in Cyprus is the famous peak - Olympus. In the north is a not very wide winding chain of mountains, which are five peaked peaks. If you climb a mountain, then you will have the opportunity to enjoy the picturesque view of the forest sinking in green, cozy villages, steep banks of mountain rivers. In addition, there are several salt lakes in this area.





Climate of Cyprus

In order to know how to choose the right resort in Cyprus for recreation, you need to consider the climatic factor. As a rule, the weather conditions here are considered to be among the most moderate and healthy on the planet, which is why there are such a large number of centenarians on the island. Most of the year Cyprus is exposed to the sun. In winter, the temperature drops to 15 degrees (the mountain climate at this time, of course, is more severe). In the summer season, the temperature ranges from 22 to 31 degrees. Reviews of tourists indicate that it is better to go to Cyprus in the summer. The warm season on the island begins a little earlier than in our region, namely in May. It is getting colder in this region in October. In the same period, the sea begins to cool.

Snow cover on the island can be seen only in a certain area and at a certain time. It is created artificially and used to ski. So, in the area near Olympos in winter there are winter resorts. Rainy time in Cyprus begins in the October days and ends in April. You can breathe absolutely clean and transparent air here, enjoying the picturesque scenery, especially in the spring.

Larnaca Resorts

This settlement is located in the southeast of the coast. The flight from the state capital to the city of Larnaca takes about three to three and a half hours. The main attraction of the resort is considered to be the views of the Finikoudes embankment, from which you can see yachts moored in the local bay. Most hotels are located in the tourist area, on the sandy shore of the bay, a few kilometers from the runways at the airport. The main number of hotels are establishments with two or three stars and inexpensive apartments.

Not far from the city of Larnaca is one of the oldest monasteries of the island - Stavrovuni. Traveler reviews indicate that it is good to relax here with young children, since there are no additional noisy entertainments, and the depth of the sea is very small. What are the prices for holidays in Larnaca? On average, accommodation in a cheap hotel will cost 250 euros per week. If you prefer fashionable apartments, then for 7 days of rent you need to pay at least 800. Lunch in a small restaurant will cost you 30 euros, and for entertainment and excursions you will have to give from 100 to 200 (depending on your wishes).





Limassol Resort

Limassol resort is located on the south coast of the island. The city is considered quite noisy and suitable for a fun holiday resort. Both young people and people of mature age can be entertained here. Respectable tourists choose holiday in Limassol. The beaches here are covered either with sand or pebbles. As the best recommend Ladis Mile. Among the excursions that you can order, pay attention to the expositions of the Cyprus Museum of the Middle Ages (Fort Limassol), the District Archaeological Museum, the Museum of Folk Art, as well as the Municipal Gallery. In the vicinity of the city are attractions such as Kolossi Castle, the Kourion Museum, Kourion itself, the temples of Apollo, Amathus, St. George and the monastery of Panagia.

Limassol offers entertainment that will not let you get bored at any time of the day. Here you have the opportunity to visit the chic restaurants and taverns where you can taste Cypriot wine. In addition, Limassol has a large number of nightlife of any kind, where you can dance and spend time fun and unforgettable.

The cost of the tour to Limassol depends on the number of days of stay at the resort and the people who arrived on vacation. On average, 7 days of vacation will cost you 20,000 rubles (per person). On the territory of the city you can see many magnificent holidays. In the February period, carnivals are held here, in which people in costumes participate. At a parade or procession, local residents and tourists drink wine, communicate. Thus, in Limassol it is good to relax for those who love the night life, which offers including the island of Cyprus and its resorts.





Paphos Resorts

The city is located in the southwestern part of the island. In this territory, one can observe the emergence of a unique microclimate. During the most sultry period you will not be tormented by heat: your face and body will cool with a fresh sea breeze. Paphos is washed on several sides by the warm waters of the Mediterranean, because the climatic conditions here are milder and suitable for any public. The hotels here are quite fashionable. Ancient cultural monuments are protected as the cultural heritage of the planet. If you want to relax without children, then this is a great place from those that offers the island of Cyprus and its resorts. Prices in Paphos are designed for prosperous tourists who like noisy clubs, restaurants and pubs. On average, 7 days of rest in the elite resort of Cyprus costs 20-23 thousand rubles (per person).





Holidays in Ayia Napa

The city is located on the southeast coast of the island. If you have chosen Ayia Napa as your vacation destination, you will enjoy the golden sand and clear sea water. Here, most three-star hotels are located on the territory of the first lines. Thus, there are no other buildings or roads in front of the hotel and along the road to the sea. The main attraction in Ayia Napa is considered to be the construction of a monastery of the 14th century, which is located in the central part among restaurants and places for entertainment by the water. Interesting for tourists is the exposition of the Museum of Marine Life. In it you can learn more about the life of the inhabitants of the Cyprus coast and the Mediterranean. In turn, in the folk museum you have a great opportunity to look at archaeological finds.

When night falls, the island of Cyprus and its resorts become more lively. And Ayia Napa is no exception. The night city offers a large number of various bars, clubs that are open until morning. On the beach you can also find entertainment for yourself. Tourists usually enjoy water sports in Ayia Napa. Many also rent yachts and boats. In addition to these types of leisure activities on the water, there is the opportunity to play tennis at the hotel, play golf, ride a bike or horse. The largest theme water park is the WaterWorld Waterpark, which is located in this city. Here you can find entertainment for both children and adults. A 7-day tour from Moscow to Ayia Napa will cost you 20-25 thousand rubles per person.
