Hotel rooms

More and more tourists prefer this accommodation option as Artemis Princess Hotel 4 *. Alanya will give you hospitality, a distinctive culture, beautiful landscapes and a warm climate. As for this hotel, it will provide accommodation in 119 comfortable rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard apartments include single, double and triple occupancy. In this case, double or twin beds can be used. The minimum necessary for a comfortable stay also includes bedside tables, a desk and a built-in wardrobe. There is access to a balcony furnished with plastic chairs.




  • Standard rooms consist of two rooms - for children and for adults. The first is equipped with a pair of twin beds and a sofa bed, which serves as an extra bed. The adult bedroom has a king size bed and a balcony. Both rooms are combined with bathrooms.
  • Suite is a great option for lovers of comfort and luxury. It is comfortable and functional. There is a living room and a bedroom, separated from each other by an interior door. There is a private terrace with outdoor furniture and a sunshade.
  • Room for guests with disabilities is equipped with special orthopedic furniture, and also has a convenient layout that allows you to move in a wheelchair. It is worth noting the wide doorways, handrails, a safe balcony, as well as the convenient location of sockets and switches.

Simplicity and elegance - the main characteristics of the interior rooms Artemis Princess Hotel 4 *. Photos show calm tones and bright accents.

Useful information for guests

In any hotel, certain rules are established that regulate accommodation and rest. If we talk about such an institution as Artemis Princess Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Obagel), then it will be useful for travelers to familiarize themselves with such information:

  • Registration of guests starts after 13:00, and all issues related to eviction, guests are required to resolve before noon (or make payment for late check-out);
