Samsung Galaxy J1. Smartphone Features





The device is delivered to stores and mobile phone stores with the Android operating system version 4.4.4. The matrix of the screen is made by TFT technology. The diagonal of the display is 4.3 inches. At the same time, its resolution is 480 by 800 pixels. There is no function to automatically adjust the backlight level. Density - 217 pixels per inch. Capacitive touch screen.

Samsung J100F Galaxy J1 is equipped with a lithium-ion type battery with a capacity of 1850 milliamps per hour. As the South Korean manufacturer assures potential buyers, the smartphone will be able to withstand about 10 hours of continuous talk in third-generation networks, 40 hours of non-stop music playback, 9 hours of watching video clips or movies. Internet time through Wi-Fi can be 9 hours with a minimum level of backlight.

The Samsung Galaxy J1 phone has a built-in processor of the Spreadtrum family. Two cores work simultaneously in it, the maximum clock frequency of which can reach 1.2 gigahertz. The amount of RAM is 512 megabytes. In the modification of the SM-J100F, it is increased to 768 MB. Not really a big difference in fact, but still it will be a little easier to cope with multitasking. To store personal data, the owner of the phone is allocated 4 gigabytes. There is support for external drives up to 128 GB.

The resolution of the main camera is 5 megapixels, the front one is only 2.
