Sights of Yakhroma: description, photo

On the canal named after Moscow and on the river of the same name, there is a city with an amazing name - Yakhroma. The sights of this small town are historical and religious monuments. Fans of outdoor activities Yakhroma is known as an excellent ski resort.

History of the city

The Finnish word "yahr" means "lake", hence the name of the city. Initially, there was a settlement of Finno-Ugric tribes. The history of the present Yakhroma dates back to 1841. At that time, already distant for us, there was a village at the cloth factory here. Later, with the advent of the manufactory, production began to expand. In 1901, near the village, the Yakhroma railway station was opened. The period 1932-1937 was marked by the construction of the Moscow - Volga Canal. Stalin. In October 1940, Yakhroma received city status. At the very end of November 1941, the Germans came to Yakhroma, but already on November 7, soldiers of the Red Army liberated the city. Yakhroma is the first city liberated from fascist invaders.

This is a major skiing center in the Moscow region. On its territory there are large sports and entertainment centers "Sorochany", "Volen", "Yakhroma". Which of the sights of Yakhroma to see in one day:

  • Trinity and Intercession churches.
  • Church of the Ascension.
  • Memorial to Soviet soldiers.
  • Gateway number 3 on the channel.
  • Amusement park "Yakhroma".

Temples

The first shrine built in the village of Peremilovo was the Church of the Ascension. It was built in the 16th century in honor of the Archangel Michael. In the 40s, the temple was reconstructed and consecrated in honor of the Ascension of the Lord. When in 1972 the cathedral passed into the possession of Stepan Apraksin, it was again reconstructed, and from wooden it turned into stone. The temple is made in the classical style, there are elements of the Gothic. The church is located on Peremilovskaya street, 93.

On a hill in the very center of the city (Konyarova St.) is another Yakhroma attraction - Trinity Church. According to architects, it was supposed to accommodate about 4 thousand people. Therefore, the cathedral is impressive in its size. The temple was erected in 1982-1985 according to the project of S.K. Rodionov. On the opening day of the shrine, the inhabitants of the village and factory workers were invited to a sumptuous dinner. In artistic form, the building is close to the era of classicism.

At the beginning of the XX century, the temple was closed, after which the property inside was stolen. Later, the room was used as a warehouse for food, furniture, even as a dining room. During the Great Patriotic War there was a hospital. In the late 90s, the cathedral was returned to the Russian Orthodox Church. The temple is being reconstructed at the present time.

Monument to the Soviet soldier

The monument is located at Peremilovskaya height. This is the main attraction of the city of Yakhroma. At the place where the monument rises, during the years of World War II, decisive fierce battles were fought for the city. The monument was built in 1966 - this is the largest project of its kind in the whole of Moscow Region. The height of the bronze sculpture is 13 meters. It depicts a soldier rushing to attack. The sculpture itself stands on a pedestal, whose height is 15 meters. At the foot there is a tablet with the names of warrior heroes and verses dedicated to them. The monument is located on an elevated platform with a picturesque view of the city.

Leisure park

Among the attractions of Yakhroma can be called a recreation park of the same name. This sports and entertainment zone of the Moscow Region is one of the most favorite places for a country vacation. The park is located in the Dmitrovsky district, which is famous for the beauty of landscapes. The Yakhroma has modern infrastructure, hotel complexes and a restaurant chain. The park organizes leisure activities for adults and children, there are exciting attractions. The most popular is Crazy Tobogan (an analogue of Roller Coaster). In winter, you can go skiing in the park, and bicycles in the summer.

Gateway number 3

This gateway is considered the most unusual and beautiful on the entire channel. A distinctive feature of this Yakhroma attraction in the Moscow region are two turrets along the edges of the gateway, built in the 30s of the XX century. The tower is crowned with two caravels "Santa Maria" made of red copper. Once this ship belonged to the famous Christopher Columbus. During the Second World War, the towers were blown up, but at its end they were restored. Caravels are also depicted on the coat of arms of Yakhroma. Gateway No. 3 is a visiting card of the city.

Ski resorts

Switzerland near Moscow - this is the name of the main sports attractions of Yakhroma. The photo shows the ski slopes of the resort "Sorochany". This is a great place for skiing.

The very center of the resort is crowned by a 225-meter mountain, and in the district there are 10 ski slopes of various difficulty levels. Lifts are equipped for visitors, there is also a small elevator for children. For them, a playground was built. Modern equipment allows you to keep the snow cover until April.

Another ski resort is called "Volen". It has 15 tracks of different difficulty levels. Sports equipment can be rented here, for those who wish, a school of instructors is open. All tracks of the resort are well lit, so you can ride at night. On the territory of "Volena" guest houses, a cafe, a playground are equipped. In the summer months, the resort opens its doors to lovers of golf, tennis, and basketball. Those who wish can ride a quad bike or bicycle.

These are the main attractions of Yakhroma that are worth seeing when visiting this wonderful Russian city.
