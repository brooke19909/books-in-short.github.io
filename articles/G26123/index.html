Hotel Description Golden Yasmin Mehari Hammamet

The hotel under the beautiful name Golden Yasmin Mehari Hammamet 5 *, opened in 1999, is part of the Golden Yasmin network, which operates under the Mehari brand. The complex is located in the heart of Yasmine resort, located in Hammamet. The Cleopatra Casino and the famous Citrus Golf Course are nearby. Carthage Land Amusement Park is within walking distance. One and a half kilometers there is a General store and Marina port, four kilometers stretches of disco street. The hotel itself is located on the first coastline and has its own sandy beach. Thus, the location of Golden Yasmin Mehari Hammamet is perfect for a relaxing beach holiday, for active pastime, as well as for conferences and seminars.





The room fund of the complex is represented by 205 comfortable apartments that have everything you need. These include 3 family rooms, 12 combined and one presidential. Each standard room at Golden Yasmin Mehari Hammamet has a TV, air conditioning, telephone, separate bathroom and toilet, safe, minibar, hairdryer and balcony.





As for food, guests can choose the All Inclusive system or HB and FB. There are four restaurants at the guests' disposal: the main one is L'Andalous (international cuisine), Le Bervecue (grilled goodies) and La Baron Chef (Italian cuisine). If you want soft or alcoholic drinks, you can visit one of the three Golden Yasmin Mehari Hammamet bars: a beach bar, a Carthage lounge bar or an American-style bar.

As for relaxation, hotel guests can always visit the magnificent sandy beach located across the road from the complex, on the territory of which sun loungers, deck chairs, umbrellas and mattresses are provided for free.





The hotel has two outdoor pools, which include pools for the youngest visitors to the complex, as well as two indoor pools equipped with heated water and a jacuzzi. Towels, sunbeds and umbrellas are provided free of charge. The business rooms of the complex are equipped with everything necessary for conferences, seminars and meetings. Golden Yasmin Mehari Hammamet offers its guests a balneotherapy center with a gym and a sauna, a hammam, a cosmetologist‚Äôs office and a massage room providing all types of massage. The complex has a Thalgo Vital health and beauty center, where you can enjoy a procedure such as a circular shower, a Jacuzzi, and a beauty salon with a full range of cosmetic procedures.

Fans of outdoor activities can use two tennis courts, ping-pong tables, an aerobics room, as well as play darts, bridge or beach volleyball. Guests staying at the All Inclusive hotel are provided with a free shuttle to the golf course (reservation must be made in advance).

The hotel's animators are tirelessly ready to entertain both kids and adults in the daytime and evening. Dance lovers are advised to visit a disco.

If you plan to spend an unforgettable, interesting and eventful vacation in Tunisia, then Golden Yasmin Mehari Hammamet will be an excellent option, reviews of admired tourists about which are found in huge numbers.
