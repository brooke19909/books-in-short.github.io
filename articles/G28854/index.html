Pandora 5910: car alarm review, reviews

Car alarm Pandora 5910 is a convenient security system that ensures the safety of the vehicle and guarantees its safety.





Car Alarm Features

Signaling system Pandora DXL 5910 effectively protects the car from theft and hacking thanks to its wide functionality. 16 independent security areas provide a high level of protection, prevent unauthorized entry into the car, starting the engine or attempted theft.

The use of an individual disarming code guarantees complete protection of the car: it is impossible to pick up the code even with the help of special devices, and evacuation of the vehicle will trigger an alarm. The control of most functions and options of the Pandora 5910 alarm is carried out both using a special key fob and using special software on a cell phone. The application allows you to view the history of events related to the car, up to each system operation.

A unique signaling radio path allows the owner to be in touch with the car at fairly large distances: according to reviews on the Pandora 5910, the keyfob's range is about 2 kilometers. At a greater distance, the car alarm is controlled by a mobile phone.





Alarm Benefits

An improved base unit of the Pandora 5910 system. The update of the alarm control unit is due to the introduction of innovative security and telemetry systems in modern vehicle models. Changes in the hardware affected the power supply system, making it more economical and increasing its resistance to high voltage.

The updated base unit received increased memory, which allows you to save more track information if it is not possible to download it to a separate portal using GPRS. This function is very effective for long trips to areas with expensive roaming of GPRS traffic or in the GSM network coverage area. All data after getting into the "home" network is loaded automatically when the memory buffer is cleared.

Changing the CAN bus driver system. The load response of the timer channel has increased significantly; changes have been made to the protection mechanism of the Pandora DXL 5910 system against overload. The alarm uses smart keys that do not affect each other.

Radio Channel 868 MHz. One of the most important characteristics of car alarms and other security systems is the duration of communication between the key fob and the car. The Ford Pandora 5910, which is installed on cars, is equipped with a GSM-channel with a voice interface, however, the standard radio channel has not lost its relevance - in the absence of GSM coverage, it makes it possible to control the car and control the security complex using a two-way keyfob.

The radio path used in car alarms has undergone significant changes. The Pandora 5910 package includes the advanced SI4463 transceiver. The radio range in which the new channel operates is 868 MHz, while it has 12 independent channels with a small bandwidth of no more than 10 KHz. The duration of communication and the security of the channel directly depend on the width of this band. Compared with previous versions of Pandora signaling, this model has almost twice as long communication channel duration.





Internet service

Free p-on internet service. Allows you to view the history of events that have occurred with the vehicle. All events are saved on the server with the current date and time. The Internet service not only indicates the coordinates of each specific event, but also gives the exact geographical location, resorting to Yandex Maps, Google Maps, OpenStreetMap, depending on the choice of the car owner.

The Pandora info app combines car alarms, cars and smartphones. For mobile devices on various operating systems (Android, iOS, Windows Phone), special software is available, the functions of which allow you to monitor, track and configure the security complex and control the vehicle from a distance.

GPS / GLONASS tracking and positioning

Prompt storage of vehicle coordinates, date and time synchronization. Any events occurring with the car Subaru XV, Pandora 5910 saves in history with coordinates. While the car is moving, the tracking function is activated, which allows you to get a full report of the travel time, maximum and average speed, and the path covered on the map.

Multi-system CAN interface

Turns the alarm into a part of the standard electrical equipment of the car and increases the anti-theft potential of the vehicle. The security complex can simultaneously work with two digital buses, supports K-line, LIN, K-Bus, W-Bus, P-Bus. High-speed multisystem CAN-interface allows you to set the alarm on cars of all makes and models equipped with digital buses. The data transfer rate is 1 Mbit / sec.

Dynamic dialog code

The modern AES encryption algorithm, coupled with a dynamic dialog code, guarantee the impossibility of intelligent hacking of the system during the exchange of service information. The manufacturer guarantees the impossibility of hacking the system in the next 15 years.

868 MHz multi-channel interface

The multi-speed multi-channel narrow-band interference-protected radio path supports a reliable system with car alarms, monitors the communication channel on an ongoing basis, providing fast delivery of alerts, convenient, quick and comfortable control.





Motor control and control

The Pandora 5910 alarm controls the fuel level in the car, and the ignition and turbo timer support function allow the turbocharged engines to be correctly and accurately operated. The car can remain without key protection in the ignition switch with the engine running.

2.4 GHz Radio Interface

The function of the immobilizer with wireless tags and the ability to control a wireless lock relay are based on this interface. Both functions significantly increase the car's resistance to theft and robbery. At the same time, synchronization with additional 2.4 GHz devices is supported: the RMP-03 programmer, GLONASS / GPS - the NAV-03 receiver. The wireless programmer allows you to make changes and configure the alarm software through a personal computer at a distance of 5-7 meters.

System energy efficiency

All requirements of modern car manufacturers are fully complied with by the Pandora DXL 5910 alarm system. The system's energy consumption is low; the battery of the machine is not wasted even during prolonged use of the security complex.

Work with heaters

The Pandora telemetry system allows you to control the preheater of the car engine. Connecting a car alarm to heaters allows you to automatically start the engine. This function is very convenient in the cold season.





Two-way keychain

The advanced two-way communication keychain is equipped with an inverse indicator based on transflective technology. The display of the key fob is liquid crystal, has a good level of contrast both in complete darkness and in bright conditions. The keychain operates on the basis of an interference-protected radio channel at a frequency of 868 MHz, which ensures the effectiveness and efficiency of two-way communication.

Car Owner Safety

Among all car alarm systems, Pandora DXL 5910 is the only system that provides a high level of safety for the vehicle owner. Automatic notifications

The security complex automatically detects a car collision in the event of an accident and sends a message to the security services and loved ones with the coordinates of the car. The fact of the accident is also recorded in the Internet service in the history of events.

Active security system

The Internet service on an ongoing basis monitors the coordinates of the vehicle and the presence of GSM-connection when the active protection mode is activated. The owner is instantly notified by the Internet service in the event of a loss of communication with the car or its towing.
