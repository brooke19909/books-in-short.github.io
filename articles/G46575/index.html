Hotel Xperience Sea Breeze Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt): description, price and photo

Would you like to have a great vacation on the Red Sea and have the opportunity to admire the amazing inhabitants of coral reefs? Are you planning to stay in a hotel with comfortable rooms and high-class service? In this case, Xperience Sea Breeze Resort 5 * may be perfect for you. About what awaits guests at this hotel, and find out further.





Location

The five-star X-Sea Breeze Resort is located in the Sharm El Sheikh Egyptian resort of Sharks Bay. The hotel has its own beach, just a hundred meters away. Naama Bay is 15 kilometers away. On this route, the hotel organizes a free shuttle service for its guests twice a day. You can also always get to Naami Bay using a taxi. The nearest international airport is just three kilometers away. So the path to the hotel will take you just a few minutes.





Description

Hotel Xperience Sea Breeze Resort 5 * was opened in 2011. It has a small, but, according to the guests, very well-groomed, green and beautiful territory, where there are many places for a pleasant pastime. A feature of this hotel is that only guests over 16 years old can stay here. Children are not allowed.

The housing stock is represented by 304 comfortable rooms located in two- and three-story buildings. Some apartments have a magnificent view of the beautiful Red Sea.

The hotel has five pools. Moreover, three of them are equipped with a water heating system, so you can swim in them even in the winter. Next to them are spacious terraces for sunbathing with comfortable sun loungers and parasols, there is a bar where you can either refresh yourself with a drink or have a snack during the day. Also, guests have the opportunity to visit the spa, gym, book an excursion, use the services, book a transfer to the airport, etc. Day and evening, entertainers are entertained by a team of animators. They hold funny contests, organize games. In the evenings, guests can watch an entertainment show, and then visit a disco.

Travelers also liked the hotel's own beach. You can reach it in just a couple of minutes. The beach here is quite spacious, clean, well equipped. There is a pier with stairs, from where you can immediately go down to the depths. Near the coast is a coral reef with a lot of interesting inhabitants. The beach, according to the guests, is equipped with a sufficient number of sun loungers and umbrellas for everyone. There is also a bar and restaurant where you can enjoy a drink, have a bite to eat fast food and even have a full lunch.





Apartments

As mentioned above, in all, the hotel in question has 304 rooms. All apartments are stylishly decorated, spacious, bright and comfortable. The rooms here are represented by the following categories: standard (34 sq. M, sea view, pool or garden, shower, balcony or terrace, maximum occupancy - 3 people) and deluxe (36 sq. M, sea view, can accommodate maximum three guests, balcony, shower and jacuzzi). All types of apartments have comfortable furniture, modern appliances (plasma TV with Russian-language channels, refrigerator with minibar, air conditioning, electronic safe), tea set, balcony or terrace. Flooring - ceramic tiles. Internet in the rooms is available at an additional cost. However, many tourists simply purchased SIM cards of local mobile operators with a package of mobile Internet.





Cost of living

According to many of our compatriots staying at the Xperience Sea Breeze Resort 5 *, the prices for accommodation here are quite consistent with the level of comfort and service offered by the hotel. So, the cost of accommodation for two for seven nights will be approximately 48 thousand rubles for a standard room (garden view), 58 thousand rubles for a standard room (sea view), 78 thousand rubles for a deluxe apartment with a sea view.

Opinion of tourists

Travelers who have left Xperience Sea Breeze Resort 5 * reviews generally do not regret their choice of hotel. So, according to them, it corresponds to its category and price. This can also be confirmed by the hotel rating based on guest ratings. So, he makes four and a half points out of the maximum possible five.
