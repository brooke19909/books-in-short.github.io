San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): description and reviews

Alanya is one of the most popular regions in Turkey, where resort life is seething. This settlement is located on the very coast of the Mediterranean Sea, which attracts guests to its resort hotels.

One of the most popular holiday destinations in this region is the San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 *, which is located across the road from the picturesque sea coast. You can find a lot of reviews about this place that leave tourists resting on its territory.

general information

San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * was built in 2006. From the moment of its foundation, its building already had a modern look, which has retained its freshness to this day thanks to regular repair and restoration work aimed at improving it. One of the latest renovations was made in 2012. At this time, a modern climate system was installed throughout the building, allowing tourists to enjoy the comfortable climatic conditions in the apartments.



Location

Hotel San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey) is located almost in the center of Alanya, on the first coastline, which provides tourists with walking access to the sea.

As for transport hubs, the nearest international airport is almost 35 kilometers from this place, and a large bus station can be found almost 7 km from San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 *.

Near the hotel there is a large city water park, which is often visited by whole families.

Rooms

In San Francisco Beach Hotel Alanya 3 * (Alanya) there is a small number of apartments. All of them are located in a modern five-story building. As noted by tourists who relax in the hotel, in each room and in the building made a fresh repair. In total, this resting place offers tourists 33 rooms, each of which is decorated with a high degree of comfort. All apartments, in accordance with international standards, belong to the same category - "standard".

Tourists note that all apartments have a large and comfortable bathroom with new, modern plumbing, which includes a bathing place (bath or shower), sink, and a bathroom. There are also several cabinets that store bath accessories (personalized for each guest), as well as bathrobes, slippers and large bath towels.



The rooms have excellent climate control equipment that allows you to maintain the air temperature in the room at the optimal level, regardless of how many degrees are currently on the street.

All rooms at San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Alanya) have a private balcony where vacationers can spend time sunbathing and admiring nature. It offers views of the endless expanses of the sea, the beach, as well as tropical gardens, which bloom in the midst of the spa season in a special way.

Room decoration

All apartments are made very elegantly, in their interior there is a good taste of the author of the design. The overall picture is represented by a good combination of white, brown and beige colors. Here, each room is visually divided into two parts - a sleeping area and a living room.

As for the furniture, the room has a French double bed, which can accommodate two guests. An extra bed for another person can be presented in the living room - on a folding sofa. Vacationers have the opportunity to put their things in a large closet, which is in the room, small cupboards located near the bed are also intended for this.

Tourists note with a stream of positive comments that the room has excellent lighting, which is created using the work of wall lights. Moreover, almost everyone notices that the lighting in the living room is brighter than in the bedroom.



In the recreation area, tourists can sit in a large armchair or on the couch and read their favorite book. At a small table here you can enjoy a cup of coffee or tea. In order for guests to spend their free time more diversely, the rooms have large plasma TVs that are connected to a satellite broadcasting system. By the way, guests from Russia in their reviews on the positive side consider the fact that a considerable number of Russian-language channels are broadcast here.

If desired, tourists can request the installation of an electronic safe in all apartments, in which you can store small but valuable things. Also, at the request of the client, the telephone installed in the room can be connected to an international line and call up with relatives and friends. In addition to all this, for a fee, guests can use the minibar, the range of which is updated every day with new soft drinks.

Nutrition

Hotel guests in their reviews of San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * often mention the possibility of food on its territory. For such purposes, the main building has a well-decorated restaurant where talented cooks can surprise you with excellently prepared dishes in the best traditions of Turkish cuisine. Hotel guests also often mention that the menu has a number of European dishes, but they are less popular among holidaymakers.

Every day, a buffet is organized for guests in the restaurant, where tourists living according to the all-inclusive concept are invited three times a day. According to guests, the dishes presented here are quite nutritious and served beautifully. The work of the waiters of the visitors is also pleasing - they are all attentive and responsive.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel has two bars. One of them is located in the hotel building, on the ground floor. There is free access to the Internet (Wi-Fi), and the menu offers a large selection of tea and coffee. In addition, guests here are surprised by delicious cocktails, as well as alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. If desired, guests can order light salads and several types of desserts here.

The second bar is located near the pool - it is popular in the daytime, when tourists spend their time in the aquazone. Here, much attention is paid to the selection of various drinks.

Entertainment

Reviews of San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey) often say that there is simply no time to be bored here. All this is associated with a considerable number of entertainment programs that are held on its territory. One of the most popular events for tourists is a night disco, which begins in the evening and can last until the morning. It is carried out in the open air, in the aquazone.

Fans of relaxation without noisy music can visit the large hall, equipped with everything necessary for playing billiards, or go on an excursion to local attractions, which are many in Alanya.



Beach

Many tourists prefer to spend a lot of free time on the beach, which is located across the street from the hotel. In the comments about the rest, guests very often talk about its well-being and excellent atmosphere. The water in the sea is clean and warm, and on the coast you can engage in active sports and have fun.



Organization of excursions

The hotel is in a very beautiful and convenient location. In its vicinity there are many attractions that tourists love to visit - paid tours are often organized there. Among the places for travel, the Dim river coast is especially popular, which, as tourists say, is very picturesque in itself, here you can feel a real unity with nature, feel the clean air. There is also a cave formed by nature with the same name.

Guests of the city are very fond of trips towards the Red Tower and the archaeological museum - these are real historical monuments that have been preserved since ancient times. Alanya Fortress is also a visited site. It can be seen on the images available on various souvenirs - residents are very proud of this place.

Sport

Fans of outdoor activities will certainly take the opportunity to visit the places for sports that are in the San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 *. One of the most popular is the gym, in which everything is arranged for active work on your body and maintaining the level of health. You can use the simulators for free, which makes this place even more popular.

If desired, guests have the opportunity to visit the table tennis room. The most active residents will prefer water sports, which are organized only on condition of payment for the services of an instructor.

A large number of hotel guests use bicycle rental services. As tourists note, they can go to explore the surroundings and explore the sights that are next to the hotel.



Services

Each guest who lives in San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * can use a number of services that the hotel provides for a fee. Among them, most often they request professional laundry in dry cleaning and laundry. Here, for a fee, you can simply iron your things, if suddenly they lost their even appearance in a suitcase.

Guests whose life is connected with doing business can use the conference room rental service, the stay in which is charged hourly. As guests note in their comments, this room is equipped with everything that is needed for a successful meeting or presentation: there are lighting equipment, as well as video equipment, tablets, laptops, photocopiers, a small number of stationery.

In San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * (Alanya), there is an offer to rent cars of different brands for the period of stay in this vacation spot. Tourists recommend using this opportunity without fail, since having a car at hand, you can always go independently to any place that is nearby and study its nature, features. Many guests in such a car go to neighboring cities in search of entertainment, as well as night and day vacations.



Prices

The cost of a ticket to the San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey) is quite small. Even tourists who come here on vacation notice this in their comments. According to the information received from the reviews, for a weekly stay in it together on the concept of "all inclusive" you will need to pay about 45-50 thousand rubles. Moreover, in this price, all operators include not only accommodation, but also a two-way flight for two and even a transfer from the airport. Guests note that it is carried out in a comfortable car.

In their reviews of San Francisco Beach Hotel 3 * (Alanya), tourists very often recommend going here to rest here to their subscribers on social networks, as well as relatives and friends. Vibrant photos are often attached to the comments, which show the whole atmosphere prevailing in this place.
