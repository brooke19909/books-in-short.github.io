Residence "Crimean Breeze": prices

The cost of staying at the hotel depends on the time of year and the chosen method of accommodation. The resort distinguishes between low, medium and high seasons.



During the low season (from the end of the first decade of January to the end of April and from the beginning of October to the end of December), the cost of rooms is from 12 thousand rubles per day. The minimum number of seats in a room is 2. The highest cost - at Villa Chaika: 150 thousand rubles for apartments for 8 people.

The average season includes the following periods:

- from January 3 to 10;

- from late April to June 20;

- from late August to early October;

- New Year holidays: from December 30 to January 8.

At this time, the minimum cost of rooms is 17 thousand rubles per day, the maximum - 250 thousand rubles.

The high season is considered to be the period from June 20 to the end of August. During this period, the cost of rooms starts from 25 thousand per day. The maximum cost of apartments at Villa Chaika is 300 thousand rubles per day.

Regardless of the season, the price of an extra bed is 7 thousand rubles per person. Children under 6 years old are accommodated in the hotel free of charge, from 6 to 12 years old the payment is for an extra bed.

This price includes breakfast in the restaurant, unlimited internet access and a parking space in the parking lot of the residence. Staying in the pools, aquazone of the beauty and health center, on the tennis court, sports fields and the promenade is not limited. Guests also have free access to the fitness center and fitness instructor.
