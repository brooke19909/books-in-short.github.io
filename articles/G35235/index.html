Hotels: Caravanserai. Caravanserai, Turkey

The huge hotel complex "Caravanserai" is located in one of the best resort areas in Turkey. This is Lara, Antalya - we can say the most prestigious and popular place on the Mediterranean coast of the country. "Caravanserai" does not apply to cheap hotels, but it has a high quality service. It is located on the seashore and was originally intended for training and recreation of athletes. This hotel of unusual architecture (in the style of high-tech) belongs to the line of Kervansaray International Holiday Courts.



How to get to this hotel? What surrounds him?

Lara is located near Antalya with its international airport. If you are traveling in a group, you will be provided with a transfer by the Caravan-Saray Hotel or your company. Independent travelers take a taxi richer. If you want to spend money on entertainments and excursions, then it is better to take a minibus or a bus - since they are constantly plying here. To go from the airport - no more than a quarter of an hour by transfer, and dolmush - about forty-five. Near the hotel there is the Lower Duden Waterfall, near which there is a beautiful sandy beach.



What's on the territory

The Hotel Caravanserai itself (Antalya, Lara) was built almost ten years ago. This is a multi-story building constructed in the shape of a horseshoe. The hotel has a vast territory - almost eleven square kilometers. In addition to the beautiful park, surrounded by greenery, there are two football fields, as well as six tennis courts. The hotel is intended, among other things, for holding large congresses, seminars and conferences - its Congress Hall can accommodate up to two thousand people. In addition, there are dance halls of a large area, as well as a large media center. The territory of the Caravanserai Hotel, the photo of which you see, is so large that you can wander through it from morning to evening, and you will always find something to do. A special amusement town is provided for children, but entry is paid.

Excursions and travel

Of course, many guests do not leave the hotel "Caravanserai". Turkey, however, attracts not only fans of "seal" pastime, but also those who want to see the nearby attractions. And the Lara region can offer a lot. The surrounding area is unusually picturesque. Therefore, the hotel organizes a variety of excursions. You can walk on foot to admire the Upper and Lower Duden waterfalls falling from huge rocks directly into the sea, and even drink coffee, contemplating this sight. You can take a ride in a revolving restaurant on Mount Tunektepe and treat yourself to an unusually delicious fish. Trips to Antalya and the Aqualand water park will entertain connoisseurs of antiquities and those who want to have fun. Lara is a great place for those who want to combine a beach holiday with shopping and entertainment in nightclubs.
