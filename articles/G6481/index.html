Hotel "Marine"



Mini-Hotel Morskaya (Novomikhailovsky) is open from May to October and is an economy class establishment. It was built in 2006. Guests are invited to relax in comfortable double rooms belonging to the Junior Suite. At the same time, the hotel can accommodate ninety-four people. Accommodation with children of any age is possible. Children under five years old are accommodated free of charge. Also, if necessary, provide additional child seats for a fee. All rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay: there are air conditioning, TVs, bathrooms with shower. Rooms are cleaned daily; bed linen is changed every five days.

Entertainment

All guests are offered three meals a day in the hotel restaurant, which is included in the price and is carried out on a buffet basis. Guests can also eat and have fun in the cafe or bar.

Hotel Morskaya (Novomikhailovsky, village) is located next to the sea, in the immediate vicinity of the center. Nearby are shops, a market, a pharmacy. For children in the center of the village opened an entertainment center "Jungle".

Before visiting the beach, tourists have the opportunity to purchase the necessary accessories in a shop located directly in the hotel. For motorists there is a free secure parking. Mini-Hotel Parus





Novomikhailovsky is a village that is famous for its picturesque places. In one of them on the shore of the beautiful bay of St. Elijah is the hotel "Parus". The complex includes twenty-one rooms ranging from twenty-one to forty-five square meters. The hotel is open all year. Vacationers can stay in a double or triple room with a balcony overlooking the courtyard or the sea. All apartments are equipped with modern air conditioning systems, furniture, two single beds, household appliances. Each room has a bathroom with shower.

For lovers of extra comfort, accommodation in a two-room suite is possible. In addition to modern household appliances, the room has a bedroom with a French-style bed. The living room is upholstered furniture. By prior arrangement, guests can stay with their pets.

Bedding is changed every five days, and cleaning is done daily.

Things to do?

Dining is carried out in the hotel cafe on the basis of the buffet. It is not included in the price, but is paid separately. Children under five eat free, taking into account the nutrition of parents. For older children (up to ten years) a discount of up to fifty percent is provided. On the territory there is a playground with barbecue.

The hotel has representatives of various tour operators who are always ready to offer vacationers interesting excursions. Trips to the water park and dolphinarium are popular. The hotel is located a ten minute walk to the sea. Vacationers have the opportunity to visit the village sand and gravel beach, equipped with sunbeds, awnings. Note!

The village has a very extensive infrastructure for accommodating tourists. The presence of numerous hotels of varying degrees of comfort and price category makes it possible to choose the best option for accommodation. All hotels are located in close proximity to the sea. However, many of them do not have their own beach, and guests are forced to visit the village. Therefore, choosing a hotel, this factor should be taken into account.

It is also necessary to pay attention to the availability of additional services in the form of organizing recreational activities for vacationers. Those who come with their children should take into account the opportunities available at the hotel for children (the presence of a club, playground and animation programs).

Not all hotels make payments in cashless form. In addition, when booking rooms, a prepayment is often required, reaching the amount of the stay per day. This can be an additional inconvenience for vacationers.

Separately, you should pay attention to the organization of catering, which can both be included in the cost of living, and paid separately. As a rule, meals that are not included in the price are somewhat more expensive. Therefore, you should not save on it. After all, the main thing on vacation is the most comfortable living conditions.

Given all the factors, you can safely plan a vacation trip to Novomikhaylovskoye (Tuapse district). Hotels here are always welcome new guests.
