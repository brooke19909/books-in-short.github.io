Charles Hyder (Dr. Hyder): hunger strike, biography, photo

There are many eccentrics among us who make this world less boring. Dr. Hyder, an astrophysicist scientist from America, belongs to the category of unique personalities. To be more precise, this person had no signs of eccentricity, which cannot be said of those who tried to present him as such to the public.





What made astrophysicist Charles Hyder go on a hunger strike?

After the Second World War, the rivalry between the two superpowers - the USA and the USSR - forced each of them to build up military resources and improve nuclear weapons. It was worth, for example, the United States to hint at the presence of another military know-how, then immediately after a short time from the opposite side there was a statement about the invention of a similar "toy". As a result, by the mid-80s of the last century, relations had sharply exacerbated, reaching, so to speak, a boiling point.

In such an environment, the figure of Dr. Hyder, a respectable, slightly flabby man, arose. The attribute of the clothes was a knitted ski hat with a large pompom on the top, which gave its owner the appearance of an impromptu Santa Claus.

Justice fighter not understood at home

It would seem that the mass media of a democratic state should have paid attention to an outstanding person who expressed protest against the policy of military confrontation. There was very little airtime allocated to American peace activist in such a way that only those citizens who suffered from insomnia could learn about the details of the action.





But in India and in countries that have chosen the socialist path of development, the topic of Hyder was being developed, as they say, to its fullest. Moreover, he was represented as a hero, devoted many publications on the pages of the newspaper "Izvestia" and television reports from the very heart of the United States. They clearly showed notes of sympathy, and Charles Hyder received his moral support.

Protest rally at the fence of the White House

As a protest, a hunger strike of 218 days was chosen, which was dubbed the marathon. It began on September 23, 1986, and Lafayette Park, located next to the residence of the president, was chosen as the venue. Dr. Hyder acted defiantly and confidently. Photos of men were published in all the newspapers of the world.





In a statement, a protester voiced the accusation of the then American president of spurring an arms race. Reagan will inevitably appear before the court of history. Astrophysicist Charles Hyder was determined uncompromisingly - any food will begin after the American government fulfills the following conditions:

  1. Nuclear warheads have no place in the United States.
  2. Military force should not be used as a lever in resolving issues related to international relations.

Proposal of the President of the USSR

Dr. Hyder received a letter from the Secretary General of the CPSU Central Committee asking him to stop further torture of the body. M. Gorbachev also promised that if a scientist decides to come to the Soviet Union, then research work will be provided.





Hyder did not take advantage of the last offer of the president of the USSR. Soon after the hunger strike ended, without any chance of success, he fought for the presidency as an independent candidate, the result of the campaign was the election of George W. Bush as president. But in any case, such a person as Dr. Hyder went down in history. A man‚Äôs hunger strike did not produce any results, but made a fuss in some states.

Early termination of shares

What did Hyder actually achieve by sitting at the fence of the White House? He was sitting on the lawn in the literal sense of the word, because the presidential administration restricted him in comfort by forbidding the installation of chairs and a table.

First of all, the protest had a positive effect on the state of the organism of the 56-year-old scientist. For seven months of a tough diet, Dr. Hyder dropped 45 kilograms, which he simply needed. Initially, body weight was 135 kilograms with a growth of 1 meter 88 centimeters, that is, there was a pronounced obesity!





Was there a hunger strike?

However, one photographer claimed that after three months the doctor did not seem to him to be a haggard man. Six months after the start of the action, further fasting was often carried out at home. The ex-wife controlled the process.

Hyder announced the early termination of the hunger strike on May 4, 1987, thus not reaching the planned deadline of 2 weeks. The motivation for such an act turned out to be banal - he simply changed his mind. But, despite this, the scientist Hyder caused a lot of talk. The hunger strike only benefited him!

Acting talent

Based on such considerations, Haider cannot be denied talent. Indeed, all this was more like a dramatization, the purpose of which was to create a beautiful, impressive picture. Before the eyes of the Soviet citizen appeared the image of a scientist who, without any support, at the risk of his health, is trying to stop the aggressive policy of President Reagan. None of the viewers even knew that the correspondent of the State Radio and Television had resorted to tricks. All day he shot stories, changing clothes in different costumes, changing ties, creating the illusion that every day he comes to the fence of the White House for filming. Ordinary viewers sincerely believed in this and watched with interest the fighter against a nuclear weapon.

Whatever the case, despite the tricks described, this man was not a swindler. PR was still not an end in itself, Dr. Hyder sincerely wanted to ensure that nuclear weapons were eliminated.
