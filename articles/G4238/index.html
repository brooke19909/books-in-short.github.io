Waterpark in Gudermes - the first water entertainment complex in the Chechen Republic

The first water park in the Chechen Republic was opened in the summer of 2009. Stunning with its size and an abundance of modern attractions, the complex is still one of the favorite vacation spots of local residents and tourists. What activities does the water park in Gudermes offer its guests?

Description of the water entertainment complex



Water Park them. Zelimkhan Kadyrov is located in Gudermes, the second largest city in the Chechen Republic. A water entertainment complex worthy of major resorts is built on 70 hectares. Earlier on this place was an unattractive wasteland. Today it is a great place for families: in the summer there is a children's amusement park, several catering points and a large open water park, divided into two zones.

The territory is landscaped, there are two large ponds where you can ride boats and catamarans. Decorative fish are found in the ponds, which is a pleasure to watch. In the warm season, beautiful flower beds are broken around the ponds. On the banks of the ponds there are comfortable benches in the shade of trees and shrubs.

The water park in Gudermes also has a “winter”, closed area with pools and water attractions. And this means that you can relax here at any time of the year. In total, there are 2 ponds, 3 large pools, more than 10 slides of various heights and complexity on the territory of the water entertainment complex. In terms of its infrastructure, quality and variety of attractions, the water park meets all European standards.

Traditions - Above All!



Gudermes water park is an ideal place for Muslims to relax. The entertainment complex is divided into separate female and male zones. The split visit rule must be observed by all guests of the center. It is noteworthy that many tourists from European countries who came to Gudermes like this tradition.

Women's water park is no less interesting than men's. At the same time, staying in an entertainment zone in the company of people of your gender allows you to completely relax and not be shy. In the cold season, the “winter” water park works according to the schedule: all even numbers are women's days, and odd ones are men's.

Particular attention in the water entertainment complex is paid to the safety of tourists. The entire water park in Gudermes, the women's and men 's zones , are under the supervision of professional lifeguards. Near each attraction there is a warning sign with the rules of use and restrictions on age / weight.

Affordable vacation for everyone!



The water park in Gudermes is notable for its low prices. The cost of visiting the water entertainment complex is from 200 rubles per person (children's ticket). If you wish, you can have fun and excitement to spend the whole day with family or friends at an affordable price. Disabled people and some other categories of beneficiaries have the right to visit the water attraction center for free. Waterpark in Gudermes regularly pleases guests with discounts and great offers. Healthy and enjoyable holidays can be affordable and affordable for every family!
