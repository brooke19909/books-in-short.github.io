Hotel La Bahia Principe La Romana 5 * (Dominican Republic / La Romana): photos and reviews of tourists

Many people dream of a carefree holiday on the Caribbean Sea. And most of them translate their fantasies into reality, going to the Dominican Republic. This sunny resort has a huge number of different hotels. And a very attractive complex is La Bahia Principe La Romana 5 *. Here he deserves special attention.





general information

First, a few words about the hotel. It is quite new, was built in 2007. Another name by which he is known is Grand Bahia Principe La Romana. This complex is located in La Romana. This is a fairly large city in the Dominican Republic and one of its seaports. In addition, La Romana has long been recognized as the administrative center of the province of the same name. It is located a hundred kilometers from Santa Domingo - the capital of the state.

The hotel is located in a very favorable location. From there, take about 45 minutes by car to Punta Cana Airport. But this is not the only option. La Romana Airport is a 30-minute drive away.

But the main advantage of this hotel is its close proximity to the cleanest beach. For his sake, in fact, most travelers go here.

In La Bahia Principe La Romana 5 * the conditions provided there allow you to stay comfortably and not to think about anything throughout your vacation. Only paid Wi-Fi, which costs $ 15 per day, can upset some guests. But if the Internet is badly needed, you can stay in the lobby and use it there for free.

There is a private parking, currency exchange, 24-hour reception, a beauty salon, a hairdresser and a laundry with dry cleaning. The hotel also has a spacious conference room (designed for 500 people), a small business center and two meeting rooms. And shops at the hotel are available. In La Bahia Principe La Romana 5 * even provide wedding ceremony services. In general, a full range of top-class services.

The staff, by the way, speaks not only Spanish, but also English.





Leisure activities

Being in La Bahia Principe La Romana 5 *, you certainly will not be bored. This hotel is not only for a comfortable stay, but also for a positive pastime. Firstly, water sports are available here. This is kayaking, surfing, snorkeling, bodyboarding and windsurfing.
