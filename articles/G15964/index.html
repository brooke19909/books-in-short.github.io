Hotel Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey): description and reviews

Alanya is known as one of the most atmospheric resort cities on the Mediterranean coast in Turkey. Despite the fact that the city is located far from airports, it is still very popular among vacationers. Tourists are attracted by the warm sea, magnificent clean beaches marked with the prestigious Blue Flag, as well as quite affordable prices. For everyone in Alanya, there is a holiday to their liking and financial opportunities. Fans of solitude can in the silence of a hotel far from partying relax on the beach or by the pool. Young people will enjoy having club hotels near the marina and in the resort center with their bright and noisy parties. For lovers of historical and cultural attractions, Alanya offers a wide selection of excursions. To accommodate guests in the resort built a lot of hotels of various categories. One of the popular budget options is Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya).

Acquaintance

In Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 *, Alanya (photo presented in the article), guests are offered to feel at home in one of the 60 rooms, equipped with air conditioning and other amenities necessary for a comfortable stay, including a refrigerator and an LCD TV. The description of Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya) on the websites of travel agencies contains information that all rooms for guests have private balconies. Guests can use the wireless Internet (paid!), With which you can constantly stay in touch. The presence of satellite TV will not let you get bored. En suite bathrooms have a shower and hairdryer.

Tourists who have provided a description of the hotel Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya) in their reviews, report that the hotel offers guests a variety of opportunities for an intensive leisure and entertainment. Among them - a pool (outdoor, seasonal) and a sauna. The hotel also provides amenities such as wireless Internet access (free of charge!), A TV in the public area and the organization of tour assistance for booking tickets.

Hungry, guests can visit the restaurant, which serves breakfast, lunch and dinner (√† la carte and buffet). For lovers of privacy, the hotel offers 24-hour room service. In the bar / lounge, you can quench your thirst with your favorite drinks if you wish.

For the convenience of guests, the hotel provides the opportunity to use free newspapers in the lobby, a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage service. Subject to an additional charge, guests can use the 24-hour airport shuttle. The hotel is recommended for families with children.

Hotel location

Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya) is located in the heart of the famous resort. The location of the hotel was recognized by many vacationers as very convenient: the institution is located near the center of Alanya (at a distance of 1.2 km, can be reached on foot in 10 minutes), near the state hospital, water park. Nearby are Ataturk Square and the House Museum, Dalmash and Dim Caves.

Hotel guests claim that the beach can be reached on foot in 7 minutes. It takes a little longer to get to Cleopatra Beach - it is located a 30-35 minute walk. A bus regularly runs here. Nearby is a market, various shops and shops.

Nutrition

The hotel's restaurant serves traditional Turkish cuisine (menus and buffets). At the pool bar, guests can enjoy delicious cocktails and cold drinks. The hotel's cafe serves cold and hot drinks.

How do guests respond about food?

Vacationers believe that it is better not to come to this hotel with young children, since the child will have practically nothing to eat here. Those traveling with children are advised to bring baby purees, cereals, and cookies with them. Here for the child it will be possible to purchase yoghurts and fruits. Dishes provided at the hotel, children eat very reluctantly.

Many reviewers call the local food meager and monotonous. Meat is rare (several times a week they serve chicken, chicken nuggets or a liver with offal), the rest of the dishes are from soy. A lot of vegetables are served both stewed, and in the form of slices or salads with mayonnaise. Judging by the reviews, guests like breakfast: scrambled eggs, boiled eggs, delicious cheeses, rolls, jams in boxes, cereals, chocolate balls, etc. You will not stay hungry at this hotel, tourists assure, but you will also get a special pleasure from the meal here hard. From alcohol, the authors of reviews share, you can only drink beer.

How do guests respond to staff and service?

The hotel staff Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya) living are called very welcoming. Particularly noted is the love of employees for children, to whom a lot of attention is paid to them. Guests respond positively to the service, but consider that the hotel staff is too overloaded. The authors of the posts on the networks speak with sympathy about the staff, which here simply falls off their feet from fatigue (there was even a case of the fainting of one of the waitresses). Tourists urge the hotel management to certainly increase staff.

How do tourists respond about rooms?

Guests leaving reviews about the hotel Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya) share their impressions of staying in rooms. The guest rooms are generally called relatively comfortable, equipped with a TV, air conditioning, a comfortable bed.

The bathroom has a hairdryer, usually all appliances are in working order, although some complain of damage. According to the residents, the shower in the room can be so full of holes that the water in it is sprayed throughout the bathroom. The drain in the bathroom is often clogged, making drainage difficult. Showers are different in size, in some rooms they are smaller, in others - more spacious. The same can be said of balconies.

A big minus of the hotel is poor insulation. Rooms are cleaned once a week, and in order to clean the room, it is necessary to negotiate specially at the reception. The linen is not completely clean: stains on sheets and towels show spots of unknown origin and mold. The windows of many rooms, guests share, overlook the windows of nearby residential buildings. A dryer on the balcony is often absent. The refrigerator cools doubtfully.

Beach

Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * (Alanya) does not have its own place on the beach. Guests are invited to relax at the nearest, located 600 m from the hotel (3rd line, 10 minutes walk). Beach towels are not issued to vacationers. You can use sun loungers and parasols.

How do guests respond to the beach?

Many vacationers call the city beach closest to the hotel rather dirty, noting that there are lids, cigarette butts, napkins lying on the sand here. The territory is covered with fine sand, but when entering the sea you can stumble on stones.

The authors of the reviews recommend not to be too lazy and walk to the Cleopatra beach (it will take 40-45 minutes to walk on foot). This beach all tourists do not get tired of praising. It is very beautiful, clean, the water is clear, a pleasant entry into the sea. You can use sunbeds with umbrellas for a fee (inexpensive), but you can sunbathe and lying on a towel.

Reviews

Guests who have left reviews about Alanya Pera Inn Hotel 3 * hotel (Alanya) characterize the restaurant rather ambiguously. According to them, throughout the entire time spent at the resort, they try every day to diversify their leisure time: they either sunbathe on the beach or attend various excursions, but they only return to the hotel to eat and sleep. Alanya, vacationers share, is a truly chic resort. But the Pera Inn Hotel is considered by many to be an institution for very unassuming tourists. Guests consider the hotel's advantages to be a convenient location, the presence of friendly staff, large rooms and balconies. As a minus indicate the lack of a hotel own beach, territory and animation, poor quality food and bad alcohol. Those who appreciate comfort and decent service are recommended to choose another hotel.
