It is so pleasant to find yourself in the arms of summer in the middle of winter! Hainan Island lies in the tropical zone, so the peak of the season falls on the period when Russia is cold, windy, snowy. Isn't that a fairy tale? Hainan Island is also popular among the Chinese themselves. Many people come from the mainland in season to enjoy sea bathing. The most lively resort on the island is the city of Sanya, and the most beautiful bay is Dadonghai (Great East Sea). There is a developed tourist infrastructure and a lot of entertainment.
