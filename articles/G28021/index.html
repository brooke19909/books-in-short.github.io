Three ways to transfer money from Beeline to Tele2

If you had situations when you did not know how to transfer money from Beeline to Tele2, this article will be useful to you. There are 3 simple methods how to transfer money from Beeline to Tele2, and in this article we will try to consider them and clearly explain to you. We advise you to fully read this work and choose the most suitable method for you.

Website translation





How to transfer money between numbers? Beeline mobile operator provides its users with several methods, of which the simplest is to transfer funds online. If you have access to the Internet, you can easily transfer money to Tele2 from the account of the Beeline operator. This service is carried out through your personal data on the site beeline.ru.

Having entered this site, in the menu you will find the "Payment" section and from it you will go to the "Money transfers" section. In this section, select the operator from whose balance you want to transfer funds. Next, fill out a kind of form with the data of the subscriber-recipient and indicate the amount of money that must be sent.

This operation is protected by a password that should come to your phone. This password must be entered in the questionnaire to approve the operation, after which the funds will be transferred to the account of the recipient.

Transfer via SMS





What should subscribers do who do not currently have Internet access? For this, Beeline mobile operator has provided a different way of transferring money. It is possible to execute data transfer of funds to the account of "Tele2" and other operators through simple messages.

It is necessary to send a wording in a certain form to short number 7878. For example: 7AAAAAAAAAA MMM, where A is the final ten digits of the number of the subscriber to whom you send money to Tele2, and M is the amount of money to replenish the account.

Attention! A space must be left between the mobile number (A) and the amount (M). After sending an SMS message, a money transfer will be completed for some time.

Transfer via USSD request





One of the elementary methods of transferring money from Beeline to Tele2 is mobile transfer. On a mobile phone, dial the command * 145 * AAAAAAAAAA * MMM # (A is the final ten digits of the subscriber's number, and M is the amount of money). For example, if you need to transfer 100 rubles to the number 89633815237, then the request will look like this: * 145 * 9633815237 * 100 #. Keep in mind that the number is entered without the initial digit 8. After sending this command, you will receive an SMS notification with a code to approve this operation. Further, this received code must be entered in the following form: * 145 * code #.

Nuances

Before transferring funds, you need to remember that there are some restrictions. For example, for a day the minimum amount that can be transferred is 10 rubles, and the maximum is 15,000 rubles. In this case, a month you can transfer no more than 30,000 rubles. In addition, there is a limit to the number of transfers. So, no more than 10 transfers are allowed per day, no more than 20 per week, no more than 50 per month.

In addition, the Beeline operator did not provide a free way to transfer money, therefore, with all methods, a commission of 4.95% of the transferred amount is charged. We hope you find the most suitable method for transferring money from Beeline to Tele2.
