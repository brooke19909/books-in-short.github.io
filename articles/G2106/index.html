Hammamet Garden Resort SPA (Tunisia, Hammamet): traveler reviews

Hammamet Garden Resort SPA is a true oriental tale. Here beautiful nature, traditional architecture, home comfort and quality service are organically interwoven. This is a good option for those who want to not only have a good rest, but also to improve their health.





Hotel Description

Hammamet Garden Resort SPA is a luxurious modern resort, where there is everything for a good rest. A rich tourist infrastructure and qualified staff is the unbreakable foundation on which the rules of quality service have been built for many years. The traditional Oriental architecture of the buildings, as well as the interior decoration of the rooms, leave a lasting impression. Well, of course, you simply must visit the wellness center, which will give you good health and attractive appearance.





Location

Hammamet Garden Resort SPA boasts a fairly convenient location. Just 400 meters away is a long beach strewn with golden sand. The road to the central part of Hammamet will take you no more than 10 minutes. As for the nearest international airport (Tunis-Carthage), it takes about an hour to get to it.

Hotel Rooms

Hammamet Garden Resort SPA has 300 comfortable rooms. The following categories can be distinguished:

  • Standard rooms can accommodate 2 or three guests. It is also possible to install additional cots. The compact one-room apartment has everything you need for a comfortable stay. Some rooms are adjoining (connected by a lockable interior door).
  • Superior is a spacious studio, where the main role is assigned to a huge canopy bed. Also in the room there is a small sitting area with a soft sofa, armchairs and a coffee table.
  • Family rooms consist of two rooms. The adult bedroom has a king size bed and a balcony. The room for children is equipped with two single beds and a sofa bed. Both bedrooms have a private bathroom.

Room Amenities

You will find all the amenities necessary for a comfortable stay in your apartment. Guests of Hammamet Garden Resort SPA 4 * can use the following options:

  • powerful air conditioning with individual control to create a comfortable microclimate in the room;
  • TV broadcasting a wide package of satellite channels;
  • landline telephone, which not only provides communication with the reception, but also makes it possible to make international calls;
  • reliable safe with a combination lock for storing valuables;
  • combined bathroom, which, in addition to plumbing, is equipped with a hairdryer and bath accessories (in some rooms there is a separate toilet);
  • mini-bar (filled for a fee, and can also be used as a refrigerator).




Service for guests

A wide range of services is provided to its guests by Hammamet Garden Resort SPA 4 *. Guests are provided with the following options:

  • laundry and ironing, as well as shoe shine;
  • currency exchange operations (you need to contact the reception);
  • office services (photocopy of documents, sending a fax, using a stationary PC, etc.);
  • safe box rental at the reception;
  • car and moped rental;
  • a personal concierge will help you navigate the hotel, as well as advise on city infrastructure and sightseeing routes;
  • emergency medical care (guests can also take a first-aid kit from the administrator);
  • delivery of orders from the restaurant to the room;
  • assistance in booking and purchasing plane or sea tickets.




Infrastructure facilities

Hammamet Garden Resort SPA 4 * hotel has a developed infrastructure, so it can be compared with a small tourist town. So, without leaving the hotel, you can use the following objects:

  • souvenir shops, the range of which consists of products of local craftsmen;
  • a common living room with a TV and comfortable upholstered furniture;
  • wireless internet in public areas;
  • kiosks where you can buy refreshing drinks, cigarettes, as well as the local press;
  • a large garden with flowering plants and trees creating a cool shade;
  • a business center where guests can use office equipment, as well as rent a meeting room;
  • a hairdresser, where there is both a male and female hall;
  • a left-luggage office where you can check in your luggage while you are waiting for check-in, or between eviction and departure.

  • a night club, where every evening after dinner an incendiary disco begins, which continues until the morning;
  • a gym where you can do fitness with a professional instructor or use fitness equipment;
  • two outdoor pools (one of them is intended for lovers of a relaxing holiday, while the other organizes water activities);

  • football and volleyball fields;
  • table tennis game;
  • training in archery and the organization of tournaments in this sport;
  • mini golf;
  • horse riding lessons and horseback riding in the surroundings;
  • indoor heated pool, the use of which is available in cold weather;
  • own beach area;

  • animation program.

Catering establishments

Quite a few catering establishments operate in the tourist town of Hammamet. Hammamet Garden Resort SPA 4 * invites guests to visit such restaurants and bars:

  • Nedra is the main dining room, where guests can enjoy a buffet with a huge selection of dishes. During dinner, cooks demonstrate their skills by arranging real cooking shows.
  • Green Peace is an institution whose name speaks for itself. This restaurant can eat guests who follow a strict diet, as well as vegetarians.
  • Les Oliviers is an outdoor grill located by the pool. Here you can try fish and meat cooked on the grill. Chefs are constantly experimenting with new marinades and sauces.
  • Les Palmiers is a cozy lobby bar located in the lobby of the hotel. Here you can taste local sweets, as well as calmly talk with friends over a cup of coffee.
  • In the cafe Ali Baba, guests can plunge into the atmosphere of the east. National interior, folk music, belly dance, herbal tea and hookah - what else is needed?
  • Jamaica is a cozy beach bar. Here guests are treated to beer, ice cream and sweet soda. For those who do not want to leave the beach even during lunch time, grilled dishes and fast food are prepared.
  • The Vitamin Bar is a favorite place for those who adhere to a healthy lifestyle and principles of good nutrition. Fresh fruits, diet salads, fresh juices - this and much more can be found here.
  • The pool bar specializes in cocktails. From their preparation, bartenders sometimes arrange a real show.

Additional Information

Tourists should familiarize themselves with certain information before going to the Hammamet Garden Resort SPA 4 * hotel. Traveler reviews say the following:

  • check-in and check-in of rooms is carried out from 14:00 to midnight;
  • on the day of departure, guests are required to leave their rooms and hand in the keys for reception until noon;
  • if any holiday falls during your stay at the hotel, a gala dinner is included in the price.

Positive reviews

Only on the basis of the opinions of experienced travelers can you get a real picture of your vacation at the Hammamet Garden Resort SPA 4 * hotel. The reviews contain such positive comments:

  • at late check-in (if guests missed dinner) dry rations are given, as well as hot drinks;
  • small, but very beautiful and well-groomed area;
  • air conditioning is very quiet;
  • excellent food (the dishes are very tasty and of high quality, and most importantly - varied);
  • on the way to the sea, oranges and other fruit trees grow (you can enjoy the fruits for free);
  • polite and smiling staff (however, in order to be treated favorably, you must also show friendliness and respect for employees);
  • despite the fact that it takes 5 minutes to go to the sea, guests are also given the opportunity to get to the beach in tuk-tuk;
  • the resort area around the hotel is cordoned off by the military, who conduct a thorough search and do not let outsiders;
  • they make candy floss for children (though not everyone gets it);
  • hotel staff constantly clean something, wash, vacuum;
  • satellite dish catches Russian channels.

General impression

Unfortunately, for the pampered and demanding tourist, the Hammamet Garden Resort SPA 4 * hotel (Tunisia) is not the best place to relax. Negative reviews are much more serious than positive ones. Of the advantages, perhaps, only food and a convenient location can be distinguished. In all other respects, the hotel is significantly behind Turkish and Egyptian counterparts. The number of rooms is outdated, savings are felt in everything, and the staff does not know their work well enough.

Thus, this hotel is in no way a place for lovers of a calm measured rest on the beach or by the pool. Rather, active travelers who come to get to know Tunisia better can stay here. If you are not going to spend the whole day in the territory, then this hotel will become a good place for an overnight stay and food.
