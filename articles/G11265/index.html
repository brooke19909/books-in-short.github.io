Dubai Nha Trang Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

Vietnam is a beautiful country, perfect for spending a vacation. The infrastructure at the resorts of this state is very well developed, and you can relax here comfortably. This also concerns one of the most popular tourist cities in Vietnam - Nha Trang. At this resort, vacationers are waiting not only for comfortable beaches, magnificent nature and all kinds of entertainment. If desired, in Nha Trang, you can easily rent including good comfortable housing. For example, Dubai Nha Trang Hotel 3 * offers relatively comfortable and at the same time not too expensive rooms for city guests.

Hotel Location Features

Most of the Russian tourists who have booked a room at Dubai Nha Trang, of course, prefer to get to the place by buying plane tickets. The nearest airport to the hotel is about 38 km from it - in the village of Kamran. Actually, domestic vacationers usually get to the Dubai Nha Trang by a tour operator's bus. But if necessary, you can get to the hotel and take a taxi.

Many tourists recommend using Nha Trang Taxi services in Nha Trang. Transportation prices for this company are fixed. Riding a taxi with a counter in Vietnam is usually much more costly.





If necessary, you can order a transfer in the hotel. This method is considered by many tourists as the most convenient.

Nha Trang city center is just about 500 meters from the hotel. It takes about two minutes to go to the coastline from its territory. At a distance of 400 m from the hotel there is a 2/4 square of Nha Trang city with a night market.

Most tourists consider the location of the hotel quite successful. However, the dead end lane in which it is located, unfortunately, has a very small width. Therefore, the shuttle bus never turns into it. On this narrow street, he simply will not be able to turn around. Tourists who rent rooms at Dubai Nha Trang should be aware of this.

What is a hotel

The Dubai Nha Trang Hotel 3 * hotel was built not so long ago - in 20 1 5 year. This hotel is a fifteen-story building with a rather original modern architecture. One of the advantages of the hotel , according to most of its former guests, is that they do not take a deposit from tourists at the entrance. Reviews of vacationers about the condition of the hotel building itself and about the design of its rooms exist mostly only good. The hotel is new, and therefore the decoration and furniture here look neat. Never breaks in the hotel and household appliances.

The Dubai Nha Trang is a three-star hotel. And, judging by the opinion of many tourists, it corresponds more or less to its status.

Dubai Nha Trang Hotel 3 *: hotel food reviews

Of course, on the territory of this hotel, like any other three-star hotel, there is a restaurant. Room rates include breakfast. Reviews of the food in this hotel on the network are both positive and negative. Dubai Nha Trang Hotel 3 * (Vietnam): beach hotel reviews

The coastline is just 300 meters from the hotel . The beach closest to the hotel is the central city of Nha Trang - Chang Fu. Its total length is 7 km. This beach, according to most tourists, is very convenient for relaxation. In addition, Chiang Fu is equipped just fine. The smallest particles of pink sea shells form sand here. Therefore, his structure is rather rough. Nevertheless, walking on the sand on the city beach of Nha Trang is extremely convenient. Many tourists feel even compare it with the Persian carpet. Of course, for the same reason, it is comfortable to sunbathe on this sand.

Along Chiang Fu Beach, the impressive city promenade of Nha Trang stretches along its entire length. There are many different catering establishments here - from tiny cheap cafes to large expensive restaurants.

Very popular with guests of the hotel D ubai Nha Trang Hotel 3 * in Nha Trang and the small beach of Paragon. It is located a little south of the city. Its total length is only 200 m. This bulk beach enjoys popularity primarily because it is located in a very picturesque place. Moreover, the tides in this part of the ocean are never strong. In the immediate vicinity of Paragon there are several very expensive five-star hotels. Therefore, mainly rich tourists rest here. Entrance to Paragon Beach, although it is considered to be elite, is completely free. However, sun loungers here, unfortunately, are quite expensive (about $ 3 per day).





Attractions Nha Trang

Among other things, the hotel D ubai Nha Trang Hotel 3 * also has a tour desk. There are many very interesting places worth exploring in Nha Trang. For example, many hotel guests visit :

  • towers of incense;

  • entertainment island Vinpearl Land;

  • Baho waterfalls.

Incense Tower

This attraction is only about 300 meters from D ubai Nha Trang Hotel 3 * . The tower is the Incense original architectural stylization of the healing tree of Aquilaria, revered by all Vietnamese. Inside the building is an exhibition dedicated to the achievements of various provinces of the country.

Vinpearl land

The entertainment island Vinpearl Land is perhaps the most important attraction of Nha Trang. You can get to it either by boat, or by the longest cable car in the world. The island has a lot of various attractions, to ride on which will be very interesting for both adults and children.

Waterfalls

Baho's natural attraction is worth a visit to vacationers who love extreme sports. These waterfalls are located deep in the forest and you need to get to them, overcoming a variety of obstacles of natural origin. In any case, only the most hardy tourists reach the last of them.
