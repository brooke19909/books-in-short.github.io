Routes from Perm to Chelyabinsk

What is a journey? This is a special kind of exploring the world. The first thing that bothers many people is the question of which is the best vehicle to choose for this exciting activity. For example, there are several options for moving from Perm to Chelyabinsk.

A train

Three flights are carried out daily from the city of St. Petersburg in different directions: to Chelyabinsk and Astana. You can get to any of them from one specified point to another. A route in the direction from Kotlas to Chelyabinsk is also suitable . Unfortunately, a direct train from Perm to Chelyabinsk does not currently exist.





The minimum ticket price will be 1,700 rubles. You can buy it at the box office of the city railway station. The entire move will have to spend more than 12 hours.

Advantages and disadvantages of this method

The positive thing is that the stations are located in the very center of the city. You do not have to spend time on an extra road to enjoy the sights. You can hold any size luggage on the train for free, without having to go through a grueling search. Moving is carried out at night, you can sleep on the road, so time will pass much faster.





The only drawback is that the train takes longer than other modes of transport.

Bus

A daily bus service is carried out from Perm to Chelyabinsk at 7 a.m. and 10 p.m. Both of them depart from the long-distance Perm station. Additionally, on Fridays and Saturdays, another route is carried out at 16:40.





As a rule, there will be no problems with buying tickets, they can be purchased before departure at the ticket office of the station. The approximate cost is 1,300 rubles per person.

Advantages and disadvantages of this vehicle

The main advantage of the bus as a vehicle is the low cost of the ticket. During the journey, he passes through the central part of the cities. Passengers can enjoy the beauty of uncharted places. If you wish, you can ask the driver to make an emergency stop.

The disadvantages of this method are much greater. The whole trip will be about 11 hours. To sit such a period of time in one position is very difficult. Buses are hot in the summer. If they do not have an air conditioner, then their health condition may deteriorate. Due to the limited amount of space, it is inconvenient to carry bulky baggage.

Car

If there is personal transport, then there will be no problems with traveling at all. The distance from Perm to Chelyabinsk is only 567 kilometers. It can be overcome by car in just 7 hours 25 minutes.





The journey will have to start with the departure from the city of Perm to the M5 highway. It represents a path from this city to Yekaterinburg. A distinctive feature is the evenness of the road, bright layout and width of the canvas. If the transfer falls at night, then you can stay at the hotel for the night.

The cost of the trip depends on the individual characteristics of the car.

Advantages and disadvantages of this vehicle

There are many advantages to traveling by car from Perm to Chelyabinsk. First of all, the plus is due to the fact that once in another city, you do not have to use public transport. You can also plan your route yourself, for example, drop in other places and get acquainted with the sights. You can also decide about stops yourself.

The disadvantage is the difficulty of travel, so you can‚Äôt do without a navigator.

Airplane

The fastest way to get from Perm to Chelyabinsk is by plane. Unfortunately, there is no direct flight, but you can choose for yourself several options with transfers, for example, in the city of Moscow, Samara or St. Petersburg. The total road time will be about 6 hours, along with a waiting period. It is better to purchase a ticket to the capital of Russia in advance. The approximate cost for the whole trip will be about 7,000 rubles.





Advantages and disadvantages of this vehicle

This type of transport is famous for speed and a high degree of comfort. You can see the sights of Moscow or Samara - these are two amazingly beautiful cities. Many people cannot stand idle traffic jams, and for this type of transport they are not terrible.

The main disadvantage is the high price compared to a bus or train - it is about three times more. Some passengers have a great fear of flying. Both airports are located at a distance of more than 5 kilometers from the central part of the city, you will have to spend extra money and time on ground public transport.

What can I get from Perm to Chelyabinsk? There are a lot of options. It is worth making your choice depending on whether there is a personal vehicle, how much the passenger is willing to spend money on buying a ticket and time on the road. Each person may have their own preferences. Most tourists choose a train.
