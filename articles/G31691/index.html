Plasticine penguin in different ways

Working with plasticine is not only interesting, but also useful. After all, this is the best material for the development of fine motor skills of the hands. Therefore, it will be useful to show the child how a plasticine penguin is made.

Natural materials





The lesson "Penguins from cones and plasticine" will step by step teach a child to combine different materials in his work. What do you need:

  • Pine cone.
  • Black, yellow and white plasticine.
  • White paint.
  • Brush

Working process:

  1. Carefully clean the cone from dirt, do not damage the shape.
  2. Paint the tips with white paint.
  3. Roll up a black ball that will serve as the head of the bird.
  4. Make a muzzle from white plasticine.
  5. Tear off some black material. Roll up two small circles and flatten them. Glue on the face. Eyes turned out.
  6. Roll up a short, thick tourniquet. Sharpen one edge and flatten - the beak is ready.
  7. Make two flat ovals for the legs.
  8. Roll black plasticine into a tourniquet, sharpen the edges, gently flatten. It turned out one wing. There should be two of them.
  9. Gather the details.

Done! A penguin made of cones and plasticine will look more interesting if you blind him a scarf from the same material or cut it out of felt.

Simple penguin





So it turns out a simple penguin from plasticine:

  1. From a small block of black plasticine, cut some material to create the wings of a bird. Cut one third of the remaining. Roll it into a ball (this is the head), roll the rest into an oval (this is the body).
  2. Blind the wings, giving the rectangular details a more rounded shape.
  3. From white plasticine, make a circle that can cover the whole body. Tear off the material of the same color a little for the eyes. Divide the torn part into two and give each shape an oval.
  4. Make pupils out of black plasticine.
  5. Roll a small oval from orange plasticine, sharpen the edge (this is the beak).
  6. Make circles from orange plasticine that will serve as the legs of the bird. With a toothpick, draw three lines from one edge.

It turned out a plasticine penguin that even a child can easily create!

Dressed Penguin





What to do to create a dressed up penguin? That's what:

  1. Roll the ball from black to create a head and the ball is one and a half times more white for the body.
  2. Roll a black tourniquet the thickness of a body. Flatten it and place it around a white ball. It should look like a penguin's wings.
  3. Glue the head to the body.
  4. Make a white ball slightly smaller than the head. Flatten the bottom. Make a rubber band for the cap from pink material and wrap it around the ball. With a toothpick, draw a knitted relief. Glue on the head.
  5. From a material of the same color, cut a strip of the length needed for the scarf. Wrap it around the penguin's neck so that it closes the joints between the head and body. At the end with a toothpick, make slots.
  6. Take the orange plasticine and roll two equal balls that will serve as legs. Flatten them and glue to the body. Make a beak, put a point with a toothpick at its base.
  7. Blind your eyes from black and white plasticine, rolling balls of equal size.

Show your child how to make a plasticine penguin. He will definitely like it, because modeling is a very exciting process. And changing the color and shape of accessories, you will constantly receive something new.
