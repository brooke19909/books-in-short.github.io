Resort Bulgaria. Sunny Beach - holiday reviews

If Bulgaria has never been your vacation destination , Sunny Beach reviews by tourists are recommended as the best place for a first acquaintance with this country. This is the largest seaside resort of the republic. Its beaches are about eight kilometers long along the clear Black Sea. As elsewhere in Bulgaria, they are golden sand. The best conditions for a beach holiday are created here: comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas, showers and gentle slopes to the sea, water slides and pools, inexpensive summer cafes, which Bulgaria is famous for.



Sunny Beach, reviews of vacationers here regularly claim that it differs qualitatively from other Bulgarian resorts in that here, at the foot of the Star Planina mountain range, like nowhere else in the country, ideal conditions for outdoor activities are created. This includes sailing, and surfing, water skiing, boats and scooters, volleyball, tennis, hang gliding, fitness, bowling and many other sports. Pleasant even climate, clean warm sea, sufficient remoteness from big cities and highways, developed tourist infrastructure made this resort the best of what Bulgaria can offer.



Sunny Beach reviews of tourists are characterized in different ways as a place for a family vacation. On the one hand, ideal conditions for children's vacations are created here: clean sandy beaches, relatively shallow sea off the coast, experienced children‚Äôs animators, many interesting entertainments and attractions. However, according to some tourists, Sunny Beach is too noisy at night to be attributed to comfortable resorts for families.

And there is definitely some truth to this. The resort has 130 (!) Hotels located along the sea line in four rows. Each of them has a developed infrastructure that does not stop its work for a minute. This is the most emotional holiday destination Bulgaria has to offer. Sunny Beach has the most positive reviews from young people. Many nightclubs, discos, all kinds of attractions for adults, which at any time of the day can charge people with a huge portion of adrenaline, attract young people from all over Europe. Especially a lot of students here, because in all respects European holidays here can be obtained much cheaper than in other countries of the Old World.



History lovers will find a lot of interesting things at this resort. On the north side adjoins the ancient city of Nessebar, an archaeological site for which Bulgaria is famous. Sunny Beach, whose map is rich in numerous excursion routes, can offer those who wish longer trips both domestically and in neighboring states - Turkey and Greece.

In general, if Bulgaria, Sunny Beach, whose photo confirms the European level of its infrastructure, attracts you as the next vacation destination, it will suit you perfectly. It is here that you will find the gentle sea, rich outdoor activities and high-level service for relatively little money.
