"Pineapple" - hotel in Turkey: description and photo

At a distance of 135 km from the international airport of the resort of Antalya is a five-story building of the tourist complex "Pineapple". Hotel Turkey is perfect for a beach, active and family vacation. The guesthouse is famous for its high service, convenient location and low cost. Near the building there are many diverse shopping centers, shops and entertainment venues.





Description

In 1989, the resort center "Pineapple" was founded. Turkey Hotel meets European quality standards and invites tourists to spend time on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, surrounded by picturesque and fragrant nature. Citrus trees, palm trees and flowering shrubs grow in a cozy area. Outdoor terraces with benches are organized for relaxation, where you can escape from the worldly bustle and enjoy the amazing panorama of the coast.

This is one of the most popular hotels in Alanya. The Pineapple Hotel (Turkey) is famous for its interesting leisure, rich infrastructure, neat and modern rooms. Ideal conditions for games and occupations are created for your kids. Without overpaying extra money, you get the opportunity to fully experience all the delights of this resort: to see stalactite caves, luminous grottoes, Byzantine fortresses, historical buildings and much more.





Number of rooms

Includes 176 comfortable rooms. More than half of them are overlooking the highlands and the Mediterranean Sea. The rooms have a sofa bed, telephone with international dialing. There is a TV with satellite TV, Russian and music channels.

Customers are provided with a cot, a safe, a small bar with drinks and free still water. Air conditioning (heat / cold). In the bathrooms you will find the necessary set of hygiene products and a hairdryer. The rooms are carpeted. A loggia is attached to the room. Several times a week high-quality cleaning is carried out, daily scheduled change of linen and towels.





Nutrition

According to the All Inclusive system, the Pineapple complex operates. Hotel Turkey offers full three meals a day in the main restaurant. Throughout the day, guests are treated to freshly baked pastries, juices, local spirits and snacks. There are also restaurants and bars serving hot meals and delicious desserts.

Beach

Within walking distance (100 m), there is a wide coastline owned by the Pineapple Hotel. Hotel Turkey offers beach amenities (free of charge) and plenty of water activities on the beach. There is a bar with refreshments. To get to the beach, you need to go through the underpass.





Additional Information

The hotel also provides conditions for business travelers: 5 conference rooms accommodating 200 people. Each room has technical equipment for high-quality meetings and negotiations. Free internet access in the hotel lobby.

For a relaxing holiday, two outdoor pools (one for children) were built. A modern fitness center is available. Aerobics and water gymnastics are held daily in the pool. In the afternoon and evening hours, animated shows are organized in different languages, including Russian. On certain days, concerts with live music and a disco take place.





In a paid mode massage treatments, medical center. The hotel has a Turkish bath, table tennis, laundry service, car rental and a chain of shops. There is a play area for children, entertainment programs and animations are held. A development club is open.

Digest

Hotel "Pineapple" (Turkey) (photo attached) is perfect for picky holidaymakers. You can fully enjoy beach amenities, the gentle sea, the warm sun and magnificent nature. Tourists who have already rested at this hotel were pleased with the attentive attitude and cheerful animation.

Some expressed dissatisfaction with the diet: according to them, the food is monotonous. Although there were few such reviews. In general, vacation in Alanya is an opportunity to spend time on a comfortable beach, see the country and get acquainted with its cultural and historical heritage.
