Hotel Seabel Aladin Djerba 3 * (Tunisia, Djerba): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Guests of Seabel Aladin Djerba 3 * can enjoy a hot exotic vacation. This is a real oasis of quality service and comfort in the middle of the desert.

Short hotel description

Seabel Aladin Djerba 3 * resort complex occupies a vast territory of 6 hectares, which also includes a luxurious private beach. The institution works in accordance with the concept of "All inclusive", which will make your vacation easy and carefree. Accommodation is made in the main three-story building, as well as 7 bungalows. In total, the hotel has 257 rooms, each of which is equipped with the latest technology, and is also characterized by an elegant design. The interior of the hotel corresponds to the Arabian interior traditions.
