Resorts of the Krasnoyarsk Territory: type of vacation, resort choice, hotels, services, addresses and reviews

Krasnoyarsk Territory has long been famous for its resorts. Here you can not only have a good rest, but also improve your health. The plus is that people with different financial capabilities will be able to choose an option for a quality vacation.

Krasnoyarsk

Resorts of the Krasnoyarsk Territory attract not only domestic tourists, but also foreign tourists. Krasnoyarsk is the largest economic and cultural center of Eastern Siberia. Tourists come here throughout the year. But the city is more popular from May to October.

Krasnoyarsk is famous for its natural resources. Despite the rapid development in economic terms, health centers are located in the environment of the city, located in ecologically clean zones. Here everyone can have a good rest in body and soul.

In close proximity to the Krasnoyarsk Territory is Khakassia. This is a republic within the Russian Federation, which also offers many opportunities for tourists. Local resorts are also very popular in the summer. In winter, there is virtually no snow. A trip to a local sanatorium is an opportunity to improve your health and forget about working days for a while.

Sanatorium "Shushensky"

Resorts of the Krasnoyarsk Territory and Khakassia - this is a unique opportunity to have a great rest, regardless of wealth. A lot of good reviews can be heard about the Shushensky sanatorium, which is located by the Oia River, surrounded by a pine forest. There are all conditions for high-quality rest with the whole family, as well as wellness. The exact address of the health resort is Krasnoyarsk Territory, Shushensky District, the village of Kazantsevo. The sanatorium operates throughout the year. You can get here through the Shushenskoye bus station. The nearest railway station is located 40 kilometers from the health resort.

In the Krasnoyarsk Territory, resorts and motels offer everything you need for a quality vacation. You can stay in comfortable double, triple and quadruple rooms. Superior apartments are also available. For a daily stay with treatment you will have to pay from 3,500 rubles. The health resort has organized high-quality buffet meals. A special menu is offered for people with diabetes and digestive system diseases.

Resorts of the Krasnoyarsk Territory are especially famous for their applied kinesiology and manual therapy. Thanks to the work of local specialists, many patients managed to get rid of serious diseases of the spine. Young children who were born with malformations are also brought for treatment.

Yenisei

If we consider a balneological resort in the Krasnoyarsk Territory, it is worth paying attention to the Yenisei health resort. The sanatorium has been functioning since 1986. It is located on the banks of the river of the same name. Cozy buildings are located among the majestic pines. Residents of the Krasnoyarsk Territory, as well as visitors, can recover at any time of the year. The exact address of the health resort is Krasnoyarsk, Tsimlyanskaya Street, 2.

It offers vacationers single and double rooms with all amenities. For a daily stay with treatment here you will have to pay from 3000 rubles. Vacationers are offered a wide range of medical services. There is an opportunity to undergo a full examination by specialized specialists, to pass tests. The sanatorium has its own computerized diagnostic system.

Health complex "Grenada"

Resorts in Krasnoyarsk and Krasnoyarsk Territory are valued for their unique natural resources. Health complex "Grenada" is an excellent place in which everyone can relax with good soul and body, undergo rehabilitation after injuries, and recover after an exacerbation of a chronic ailment.

It offers guests rooms of standard and deluxe category. For a daily stay here you have to pay from 2500 rubles. In addition to standard wellness programs, cosmetologist services are also offered here. On the territory there is a swimming pool, sauna, phytobarrels and much more. Reviews show that it is much more comfortable here to relax in the winter, when there is no large influx of tourists.

The health resort is located in the Sverdlovsk district of Krasnoyarsk, on Bazayskaya street, house 347/1.

Sanatorium "Balm"

Resorts of the Krasnoyarsk Territory are famous for their quality medical services. In no way inferior to its competitors, according to reviews, the resort "Balm". The health resort is located 7 km from the city of Minusinsk on the industrial site of the Electrocomplex. Adults, families, and also formed groups of children are accepted for rest. The cost of a day varies from 1300 to 2500 rubles.

The main medical profile includes diseases of the ENT organs, as well as muscles and bone apparatus. On the territory of the health resort, methods such as balneotherapy, inhalation, herbal medicine and other types of physiotherapy are used. Reviews show that for each patient, a wellness program is developed individually.
