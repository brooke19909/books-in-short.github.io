Hotels of Uralsk, Kazakhstan: overview, features and reviews of tourists

When traveling in Kazakhstan, be sure to visit the city of Uralsk. You will not have problems with accommodation. You can choose a place to stay both in the city center and in calmer areas. Uralsk hotels will welcome their guests.

Park Hotel Uralsk

The hotel complex is located on the picturesque bank of the Chagan River in an ecologically clean place. This is a favorite vacation spot for both city guests and its residents. Here, at a high level, business meetings are held in an equipped conference room for up to 100 people. The park hotel includes two buildings and seven cottages with separate rooms. Meals are organized in a two-level restaurant with 300 seats. Tourists can relax and have a good time in the sports and fitness center, Finnish sauna, Turkish bath. A massage room and a heated indoor pool with a self-cleaning system are available for you.

Non-Uralsk

If you need an inexpensive hotel room in Uralsk, you can choose from the following options:

  • double superior room - the cost is 2312 rubles per one night;
  • single standard room - the price for it is 1423 rubles per day.

The price includes breakfast. In addition, you can stay in suites and apartments. The total number of rooms of the park-hotel "Uralsk" consists of 46 rooms. Each guest has comfortable furniture, air conditioning, TV, telephone, minibar, free wireless internet. Car owners enjoy free private parking. The hotel complex is located at Samal district, 3.
