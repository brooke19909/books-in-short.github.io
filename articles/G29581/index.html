My Aegean Star Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kusadasi): description, service, reviews

On the Aegean coast of Turkey, at the very edge of the water, lies the famous resort of Kusadasi. It is not by chance that he enjoys crazy popularity with tourists. Its snow-white beaches, a huge number of historical attractions and a long swimming season are only a small part of what travelers expect here.

Turkey has long been a popular tourist destination for residents of many countries, and Russia is no exception in this regard. In this regard, the resort areas of the country are well prepared - the tourism infrastructure is well developed, the cost of the tour is often quite affordable, and the swimming season is quite long (from July to September, the weather in Kusadasi is perfect for the beach).

The Kusadasi area is also popular because it houses the most famous fashionable hotels. One of them is My Aegean Star Hotel 4 *.

Beach

Kusadasi Hotel My Aegean Star Hotel 4 * is located very close to the beach (only about 300 meters), so it will take 10-15 minutes to get to the road. The hotel does not have its own beach, so guests are offered to go to the city beach. This will not hinder a good rest, since the Kusadasi resort is famous for its snow-white sandy beaches far beyond the borders of the country.

  • Central. The central city beach (located very close to the hotel) is better equipped than others. There are a large number of umbrellas, deck chairs and mattresses, there are changing cabins. Guests can enjoy a cocktail at the local bar anytime.
  • Tusan. This beach is located some distance from the city (7 km from the center). It is worth going to all those who prefer not just to lie on a deck chair, but to have fun. Tourists can enjoy yachting and boating, water skiing, banana skiing, beach volleyball.
  • The beaches of the national park. They are quite far from the city (it takes about 20 minutes to travel there) and stretched for several kilometers. One of these beaches is sandy, 2 - sand and pebble. To see this splendor is worth all those who prefer a secluded holiday surrounded by natural beauty. A thin strip of beach is limited on one side by blue waves, and on the other by a pine forest.




Attractions in the area

Many tourists believe that Kusadasi is an exclusively seaside resort, which boasts only beaches and expensive hotels, but this is completely not the case. Kusadasi offers a large number of excursions to historical and natural attractions.

The Turkish hotel My Aegean Star Hotel 4 * is only 7 km away from the center of Kusadasi, which means that getting to most of the significant places in the city will not be so difficult.

  • Hill with a statue of Ataturk - 7.5 km away. At the top of the hill there is an observation deck, from where an amazing view of the surroundings opens.
  • Kaleici Mosque (7 km). This dilapidated mosque is located in the old part of the city.
  • Castle. This wonderful castle has been perfectly preserved to our days, despite its long history, which began in Byzantine times. This architectural landmark is located on the Pigeon Island. There is a museum, restaurant and disco club.
  • In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is another popular hotel - Alish Hotel.

When to go to Kusadasi

Travel time should be planned based on your own requirements for rest:

  • Beach vacation. If the main goal is to have a good time at sea, then it is better to take a ticket to June, July or August. In September, the weather in Kusadasi is also suitable for swimming, but the water is already somewhat colder, and waves and wind appear more often.
  • Excursion. For trips to attractions it is better, on the contrary, to choose the spring or autumn months in which there will be no sweltering heat.

Hotel My Aegean Star Hotel 4 *: reviews

As often happens, reviews of tourists were divided into laudatory and not very.

The list of good points was often called:

  • pleasant atmosphere in the rooms; cleanliness;

  • friendliness of staff (maids, waiters, administrators are always smiling and ready to help).

From the fact that many tourists did not like:

  • very small territory of the hotel (crowding occurs);
  • great distance from the sea;
  • lack of a private beach in the hotel.

In general, tourists resting here say that they will be happy to return to this hotel again. That is why you can safely consider the hotel for your own vacation and book tours to Kusadasi from Moscow and other cities.
