Description of the Territory

Not so long ago, the full name of the hotel was Costa Caribe Coral By Hilton 4 stars. Now the last words have been removed, but the chic inherent in the Hilton hotel chain has been preserved. The hotel area is very beautiful and exotic. Everything is buried in greenery and flowers, many palm trees, there is even a pond with fish and turtles. At the exit from the central building of the hotel (where the reception is), there are tennis courts, a casino, a small golf course. Near the hotel there is a golf club with a larger field. A shuttle service is available there. Right under the palm trees is a swimming pool, in the center of which is a full-day bar. For the convenience of guests, there are several shops on the territory (with clothes, souvenirs, food products). In addition, every day local residents are pulled to the central building with mangoes, bananas, decorations and more. Merchants are not imposed on anyone, and their services are quite out of place, since when making purchases you can bargain and significantly bring down prices, and other outlets are far away.



For children, Costa Caribe Coral 4 * provides a shallow paddling pool, a playground and a babysitting service.

A big plus of the hotel - on its territory there is a diving center where you can take equipment, and for beginners in scuba diving to receive lessons and training.

Rooms

The 4-star Coral Costa Caribe Hotel has 548 Superior, Deluxe, Suite and Junior Suites. All of them are quite spacious, with windows on the sea or on the garden and pool. The technical condition of the rooms is very different. The hotel is undergoing renovation. The rooms, in which the repair is already completed, have good furniture, well-functioning plumbing and electrical equipment. If you are accommodated in a room where something is wrong, it is easily changed to a suitable one.

For the services of guests are provided:

Hygiene rooms are equipped with a washbasin, shower, toilet. Shampoo, soap, toilet paper replenish as necessary throughout the race. Bottled water is also regularly brought. Towels are changed daily. The system is as follows: in the morning they are collected for washing, and then after 17 pm they are given out, and they strictly record in which room how many towels were taken. These works are performed not by maids, but by individual staff, so if you suddenly forgot to bring a towel or did not leave water, you need to contact the reception.



Concerning cleaning in Coral Costa Caribe 4 * the reviews are contradictory. Some tourists believe that it is clearly insufficient, others are very pleased that they clean cleanly and every day. Also, some tourists do not like that the towels are not new.

A distinctive feature of the rooms is that all the sockets in them are designed for a voltage of 110 w!

Sea and weather

Most tourists go to Coral Costa Caribe 4 * for the sake of the sea and beaches. The Dominican Republic is a paradise of our Earth, where it is always summer. Air temperature here rarely rarely drops below +30 in any month of the year. Water temperature is always + 26-28. The only difference in the weather is rain. Least of them are from December to April. The maximum is from August to October. But even in the relatively rainy May, June, July, beach vacations are always excellent, because the showers in the Dominican Republic are powerful, but short-term, they do not affect the temperature of air and water, only humidity. She is here about 80-90%. Almost all tourists tolerate the heat normally, as sea breezes blow, giving a feeling of freshness.

Juan Dolio resort is not crowded with tourists, it is always spacious on the beaches.



Coral Costa Caribe Hotel has its own beach area, which differs from the neighboring ones in that there are practically no waves on the sea. Sea water is warm and clean, there are few algae. If you go from the beach in any direction, you can find completely secluded places. There are no dangers in the person of local crime there. The only nuisance that can happen is to step on the sea urchin in the water.

Sunbeds on the hotel beach are free, but there are no umbrellas, only palm trees. This is also part of the exotic - white clean sand, azure waters, palm trees swinging with twigs, a light sea breeze. Violate this paradise only fruit and souvenir merchants who are present on any beach in the world. True, here they are not intrusive.

Leisure outside

All reviews about the Coral Costa Caribe 4 * hotel necessarily mention unique excursions. Even those tourists who spent their entire vacation on the beach write about them. You can buy sightseeing tours from Russian tour operators or from local ones (in the diving center of the hotel). The most popular and unforgettable trips are:

1. To the island of Saona. It is the largest in the Dominican Republic. Here you can admire unusual plants that do not grow anywhere else, amazing birds and animals, but the main trump card is starfish. With them during the tour, everyone conducts photo shoots. In addition, during this trip, they feed a delicious lunch with lobsters, treat them with drinks and cocktails, and have a dance party.

2. Samana . People come here to admire whales, which show real performances - they jump out of the water, make breathtaking somersaults, wave their tails. All this takes place in a natural setting, not far from the coast in the open ocean. Tourists admire all this beauty from the boat. It should be noted that whales come here only from January to March.

3. Horse ride to the waterfall. This tour is suitable only for those who are in good shape.

4. To the island of Catalina. He is small but fantastically beautiful. There are unique beaches even for the Dominican Republic, and Catalina is the best place for snorkeling. On an excursion, they are deliciously fed, rolled along the river flowing in the mangrove jungle. With any luck, here you can watch raccoons.

5. Santo Domingo. Not everyone likes the excursion to the capital of the Dominican Republic, although it is a unique city that takes its history from the end of the XV century. Here you can admire very beautiful buildings, chapels, castles, towers, monasteries, visit the tomb of Columbus, go to museums, which have seven in the city. The proximity of Santo Domingo from the hotel makes it possible to visit this city on your own, without buying a sightseeing tour.



If we talk about interesting objects near the hotel, they are not there. Only the beach. Therefore, in order to bring more impressions with you, visiting excursions is quite out of place.
