Cheap hotels in St. Petersburg: prices and description

St. Petersburg is the cultural capital of the Russian Federation. Therefore, every year the city receives a large number of tourists. Many are faced with the question of whether it is possible to rent a room in the center inexpensively. Hotels in St. Petersburg offer this opportunity. Consider the most popular options.

Cinema

Cost per day: about 350 rubles.

This hotel is located in the city center near the main attractions. The rooms have comfortable furniture that will allow you to spend a pleasant vacation. In the kitchen you can cook meals, warm food. Installed stove. The train station is 1.5 km from the hotel, and the airport is 16.8 km away.

A hairdryer and internet are available for free. A washing machine will cost 100 rubles. Shuttle services are available at an additional cost.

Resthostel

Cost per day: about 600 rubles.

This affordable hotel in St. Petersburg is centrally located. The rooms have comfortable beds. The interior of the room is modern. There are all necessary equipment and furniture.

The kitchen has appliances that allow you to cook various dishes. The dining area is as cozy and comfortable as possible. If desired, you can eat in cafes or bars. Restaurants are located near this inexpensive hotel in St. Petersburg.

Near the hotel there are attractions: theaters, museums, monuments. A metro line is a 6-minute walk away. The hotel provides translation and guide services. You can be settled both by foreigners and residents of Russia.

Aqua Hostel

Cost per day: about 700 rubles.

Another inexpensive hotel in St. Petersburg is AquaHostel. It is located near the Palace Square. Each room has furniture, a berth, appliances (TV, fridge), which allows you to spend time in comfort. Depending on the cost, the bathroom can be either in the room or shared, located on the floor.

The cafe has the opportunity to order dishes that are not on the menu. Bars and restaurants are also nearby. The airport is 26 km from the hotel, and the train station is 8.2 km away. You can watch TV in the common living room for free, play games, use the Internet or the Xbox console. If you need to call a taxi, then the hotel will pay the bill for it.

Lemur

Cost per day: about 800 rubles.

The described inexpensive hotel in St. Petersburg is located on Nevsky Prospekt. The staff is polite and welcoming. The rooms are cleaned in a timely manner.

Guests are accommodated in two-, four-, six-, and eight-bed rooms. Each of them has all the necessary equipment and accessories.

The shared kitchen has equipment that allows you to conveniently prepare breakfasts. The front desk is open 24/7. If you wish, this inexpensive hotel in St. Petersburg can provide a guide for the tour. Free hairdryer, air conditioning and internet. If you wish, you can speed up the check-in or check-out process. There is a playroom for children.

The metro line is 470 meters away, and the airport is 15 km away. And to the train station about 10 minutes by car.

Summary

The article considers the best inexpensive hotels in St. Petersburg. They please not only the cost per day, but also the quality of service. That is why, if you want to relax inexpensively (no more than 1 thousand rubles a day), then you need to consider the proposed options.
