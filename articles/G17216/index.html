Territorial borders

The cottage village "Dragoon Stream" is located 30 kilometers from St. Petersburg and is located near the Neva River. This is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the Leningrad region. At the same time, it is located relatively close to large cities and very close to the village to them. Morozova.

Base "Dragoon Stream" consists of 40 cottages, each of which is built using high quality materials using innovative building technologies. Guests are welcome here all year round. The nearest village from the cottage village is 3.5 kilometers away. There you can buy food and medicine and, if necessary, visit the clinic. Particular attention at the recreation center "Dragoon Stream" is given to the safety of guests. So, the whole territory is under protection, and in each cottage an alarm button is installed, which allows residents to get help if necessary. Landscapes and attractions

The cottage village is conveniently located on the river. Neve. There are incredibly beautiful views, an abundance of clean air, beautiful nature - meadows, fields and forests. The length of the beach line is 500 m, which allows you to swim, sunbathe, ride a boat or fish for your pleasure. Connoisseurs of historical monuments can visit the legendary fortress "Nut", take a tour of the water to about. Balaam or Solovkov. Only one and a half kilometers from the base is the famous Lake Ladoga.





Housing stock

Spacious and beautiful buildings welcome guests to the recreation center "Dragoon Stream". Cottages are designed for 6-10 people, they are all the same in layout and type of structure. The first floor of the houses includes all the necessary household appliances and a dining table. The kitchen in the cottage is combined with the living room, there is furniture and accessories for relaxing: sofa, TV, coffee table. Also in the houses there is a bathhouse. The bedroom with a double bed, cabinets and bedside tables is also located on the ground floor. Also, the cottages have terraces with the necessary outdoor furniture for evening gatherings with friends and relatives. Near the house there is a platform on which there is a barbecue for the preparation of barbecue. On the second floor of the cottage there is a relaxation room with a sofa, a coffee table and various decorative elements. There are also two more bedrooms for four people. There is a bathtub on each floor of the cottage.





Cuisine, restaurant, menu

The Dragoon Stream recreation center is rightly proud of its excellent cuisine. The restaurant, which seats up to 700 people, is divided into the following areas: large hall, small hall, summer terrace and tents. All of them are decorated in the same way as the cottages of the village - in the style of an alpine chalet. The halls have a pleasant atmosphere, good repair, large panoramic windows, looking through which you can see and feel all the beauty of the Neva River. Here you can hold any event. There is also a veranda, a large room for dancing and animation, a stage with musical equipment. The restaurant is famous for its varied cuisine that everyone will like. So, here you will be offered a menu consisting of dishes of European and Caucasian cuisines.





A variety of services, room prices

The cost of renting cottages depends on what type of house you choose - regular or luxury. The price includes parking, security, bed linen, hygiene products, barbecue, bathhouse. In the cottage village "Dragoon Stream" there is everything for outdoor activities: table tennis, futsal, playgrounds, karaoke. If you are a fishing enthusiast, then in this place you can do your hobby. It offers vacationers fishing tackle and berths, which in the cottage village there are about 60 pieces. For outdoor enthusiasts, sports equipment is offered year-round. The base has all the conditions for a winter holiday: various Russian funs (sleigh rides, from the mountains, etc.), as well as classic sports activities (skiing, ice skating).





If you do not plan to spend the night here, then you will need to pay your stay in the amount of 500 rubles per adult and 250 rubles for a child aged seven to fourteen. In this case, you can stay on the territory of the "Dragoon stream" until 11 pm. Arriving in the village with animals is prohibited. However, an exception is made for small pets weighing up to 5 kilograms. For his accommodation will have to pay an additional 500 rubles per day.

How to get there

In order to get to the cottage village, you need to drive 36 kilometers along the Murmansk highway (25 kilometers intersection with the Ring Road). Turning right, you should follow the sign, after about a hundred meters you can see the exit. Having turned off the Murmansk highway, you will need to drive a bridge and overcome 2.5 kilometers to the railway crossing. After another three kilometers, you will see the second railway crossing. Before reaching it, turn right. You will see a forest road that leads to the cottage village.





Tourists reviews

Most of those who visited the recreation center "Dragoon Stream" receive a lot of positive emotions and want to return here again. Reviews of tourists for the most part are positive, and sometimes even enthusiastic. The guests consider the advantages of leisure in the village to be close to the settlements, comfortable accommodation, excellent cuisine, an abundance of active pastimes, the presence of a beach, as well as magnificent landscapes. Only some tourists were dissatisfied with the service and the lack of baby bedding. However, the most beautiful views, impeccably clean air, according to most vacationers, are much more important than minor flaws.

Finally

Summing up, we can say that the "Dragoon stream" (cottage village and recreation center) is a great place where you can have a great time both for a young couple and the whole family. Here every year many interesting events and events are organized. Gourmets will appreciate quality and varied food. The "Dragoon stream" has the necessary equipment for playing sports. Arriving here, you will marvel at the incredible beauty of nature.
