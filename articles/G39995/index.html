Philips steamer - which one to buy?

It's no secret that we are what we eat, and a person who eats only fried, fatty and baked can often be distinguished by his painful appearance. Modern scientists have long proved that when boiling and frying, almost all vitamins are removed from food, and the cells of our body, unfortunately, have not learned how to synthesize them over thousands of years of evolution. How do we process food in order to preserve the beneficial properties of products? The answer is simple: steam it. And the Philips double boiler will help us to prepare such dishes correctly.



But why exactly her? And all because the Philips company has been manufacturing high-quality kitchen equipment for many years, not only for the home, but also for professional kitchens around the world. Philips is a brand for which you do not have to overpay! But back to the most important thing, to our health.

Food prepared by steam is not only very useful, but also highly recommended for people suffering from problems with the gastrointestinal tract (gastritis, cholecystitis, gastric ulcer) and the cardiovascular system. The use of such food will help maintain or acquire an ideal figure, rejuvenate the skin, strengthen immunity, and preserve health.

Another important plus will be that dealing with such equipment is very simple, and besides, it will help you save a lot of time. Regardless of the type of food and the time of its processing, you can cook up to three different dishes and then wash only one double boiler. Isn't it wonderful?



But the market for such equipment is very large, and even if you limit yourself to choosing a brand, your eyes begin to run wide. The Philips double boiler produced by the company differs from other brands in its incredible build quality and will serve faithfully even to your children and grandchildren.

The main thing is to remember that even a cheap Philips double boiler is worth the money. The difference between the models is only in power, in the number of containers and the material of manufacture. Representatives of three different price categories of such goods as the Philips double boiler are compared below. Reviews about these models were the most striking and positive.

It can be concluded that the price of these products is very different. But this does not mean at all that the cheapest Philips double boiler is at the same time the worst. They just have a completely different field of application. For example, HD 9110 will be enough for yourself or two or three people and to get acquainted with the steam kitchen in general, HD 9140 is a technique for the whole family, and HD 9190 is the choice of a hostess who wants to feel like a master chef.

In the end, I want to note that the only fly in the ointment in all this will be the taste of the prepared dishes. It may seem unusual for some lovers of fried and greasy. But in just two weeks, when you get used to the taste of health, not a single living soul will be able to make you refuse to cook food for a couple.
