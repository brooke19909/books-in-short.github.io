The best hotels of Lviv: photos and reviews of tourists. Cheap Lviv hotels in the center

If you suddenly need to come to Lviv, then the best option would be to stay for the night in one of the many hotels in the city.

Lviv hotels will receive guests with a variety of requests

Of course, the most excellent hotels in Lviv are located in the city center. But it is also possible to find a good hotel if you walk a couple of minutes in the direction from the central part. Modest budget hotels can be found only on the outskirts of the city, but then it will be difficult for a tourist to quickly get to the main attractions of Lviv.

That is why for the guests of the city a hotel rating was compiled, which is based on the opinions of travelers. When choosing a hotel, it is necessary to study the reviews in order to avoid misunderstandings in choosing a place to stay or overnight. Below will be presented the hotels of Lviv (budget option), which have earned many positive reviews from tourists.

Golden Lion - low-cost housing in Lviv

Golden Lion is a budget hostel located in the historical part of the city. Next to it is the Latin Cathedral, the Opera House and Adam Mickiewicz Square. However, if you want to visit the famous Rynok Square, you can reach it on foot in just ten minutes.





Nutrition

Hotel "Golden Lion" (Lviv) offers its guests to use the shared kitchen, which is equipped so that it is easy to cook food on its own.

If you want to have a snack or dine with something tasty, then you can order food in any restaurant or cafe in the city. These catering establishments are not far from the hostel, and when you want to spend an evening in a relaxed atmosphere, you should visit one of the many bars and pubs and drink a glass of wine or tasty fresh beer.

Additional services

Hotel "Golden Lion" (Lviv) offers its guests high-speed Wi-Fi. When you urgently need to go to the airport, then for an additional fee you can order transport at the hotel, which will instantly deliver to the flight. All guests have the right to use free parking.

Rooms

The Golden Hotel (Lviv) - as the locals sometimes call the hostel - offers comfortable, bright rooms, but you must take into account the fact that the bathroom is on the floor. According to the reviews of the guests of the hostel, this moment does not overshadow the time spent in it. There are many more positive aspects. Convenient location, cleanliness and comfort, and most importantly - low cost of living. Many other hotels in Lviv in the city center have higher prices.

Hetman Hotel

The Getman Hotel has three stars, and it is a couple of minutes walk from the center, on Vladimir the Great. The difference of this hotel is that it is located in an ideal location, from where it is so easy to get to any corner of the city.

Accommodation

  • 53 double rooms, also standard class.
  • 16 economy double rooms.

Of course, they are all equipped according to their class. All rooms have: telephone, cable TV, refrigerator, shower room, but VIP rooms have a bathtub.





Almost all Lviv hotels have their own restaurants; Getman is no exception. Guests can enjoy delicious meals at the local cafe without leaving the hotel.

The hotel has round the clock hot and cold water.

Hotel "Georges"

Hotel George Hotel has a three-star category. It is located in the city center, namely on Adam Mickiewicz Square. Hotel "Georges" (Lviv) is located in an excellent location, from where you can easily and quickly get to any part of the city and to many local attractions. Near the hotel there are many different cafes, banks and public transport.





Rooms

The hotel has 80 rooms, which are mostly decorated in a classic style. There are:

  • double rooms of economy class;
  • standard double rooms with one berth;
  • standard double rooms with two beds;
  • Superior double rooms with one berth;
  • Superior double rooms with two beds.

All rooms have an exquisite design. They have classic furniture, paintings, lots of light, cable TV, telephone, bedside tables, as well as a modern bathroom, bathrobes and slippers.





The hotel offers all guests lunch, dinner and breakfast in its own restaurant. The restaurant gives guests the opportunity to enjoy amazing dishes of Ukrainian and European cuisine. At the time of booking a room, the client can choose the options of breakfast or half board.

The hotel offers a wide range of services, such as:

  • profitable rental of modern bicycles;
  • transfer rental;
  • ordering city tours;
  • booking and buying tickets for events;
  • table reservation in any catering establishments;
  • leisure activities - from riding to a professional photo shoot.

It is worth noting that the hotel offers a spacious and modernly equipped conference room, which can accommodate more than a hundred people.

History of George Hotel

The hotel was opened on Mickiewicz Square at the end of the 18th century. The hotel has been renamed several times throughout its history. Initially, the hotel was called De La Rus, and in the middle of the XIX century the institution was renamed in honor of the owner - Georges Hoffmann.

In 1899, the hotel building was rebuilt and the building style was changed to a more popular one - the Neo-Renaissance. Today the hotel has undergone several redevelopment and reconstruction.

Reviews of tourists indicate the special hospitality for which this hotel is famous. The food is delicious, the rooms are always clean, the staff is friendly and always ready to provide any help. Particularly pleased with the convenient location of the hotel and relatively inexpensive prices.

Hotel Lviv - cheap accommodation in the city center

The hotel is located in the city center, and nearby, 150 meters away is the famous Opera House. Budget tourists can safely choose the Lviv Hotel for accommodation. Reviews of tourists about this place are very diverse. The downside is that the level of service here is not at the highest level. But there are many positive points.

The hotel building is 8-story. There are several elevators and a large, spacious lounge.
In addition, the institution has a chic restaurant and a cozy cafe, a hall with various slot machines. There is also a disco bar, which is located on the ground floor and a couple of buffets on the 3rd and 6th floors, a hairdresser and even a modern sauna.
Not far from the establishment there is a paid parking lot.





Rooms

The hotel offers guests several options for spacious rooms, equipped with everything necessary for relaxation. The rooms are divided into economy class, standard and VIP class. Each of them has beds, furniture, but in economy rooms there are no bathrooms. The price of accommodation is quite affordable.

Budget tourists should pay attention to mini-hotels of Lviv. As a rule, the price for accommodation and additional services in such institutions is not very high.
