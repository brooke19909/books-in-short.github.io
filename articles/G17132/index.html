How to make call detailing on a megaphone without leaving your home

Every day, Megafon subscribers make thousands of calls and send even more SMS. And, of course, with such an intensity of conversations, tracking the movement of money on the balance sheet is very difficult. Therefore, most prefer to take details of their calls from the mobile operator. Usually they go directly to the office for this, which is not always convenient. And how to make call detailing without a personal presence, many simply do not know.



Of course, if the office is literally a stone's throw from home, it is much easier and more convenient to get to it. The main thing is not to forget your passport. But those who live away from the center often do not have such an opportunity. But since Megafon offers to make the details to all its subscribers, a variety of options have been developed and implemented for the inhabitants of the outback.

With the development of modern technology, the Internet has become available anywhere in the world. Therefore, many subscribers prefer to receive information about their calls by e-mail. In order for her to come to the post office every month, you only need to go to the office with a passport only once and write a statement. Starting next month, she will come. And you will no longer have to think about how to make details of calls on the Megaphone.



But this way of getting information about your calls has one drawback. She comes only once a month. And sometimes there are situations when you need information very urgently. In this case, the Personal Account or Service Guide will help the subscriber. In the "Personal Account" section you can order not only call details for the past month or see current charges, but also receive data on email about calls for the last day. Thus, knowing how to make details of calls on the Megaphone, you can find the forgotten number of the person who called the subscriber today or yesterday.

In addition to this service, the Service Guide also allows you to view information on-line about current charges. Of course, there is only general data. But right away you can, for example, see the charges for connected paid services or special calls. Sometimes this information is enough to resolve the situation. And this significantly saves not only time, but also protects the subscriber from empty worries.



And what if the subscriber is on the go and does not have access to the Internet, but you need to urgently find out information about recent calls? How to detail calls on a megaphone? For such cases, the Mobile Detailing service was conceived. It allows you to get information about the last five compounds for the current day. Moreover, the service is completely free. You just need to dial the combination * 105 * 105 # and select the desired menu item. In just a few minutes, detailed detail will come to the phone.

Only in one case, you still need to go over to the office for details. Sometimes a seal is required to confirm its authenticity. In all other cases, the modern client does not need to upholstery the thresholds of offices and wait for an employee to print it on a printer. The company carefully provided its customers with more than one option on how to make call details on Megafon without leaving home.
