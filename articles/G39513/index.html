Calangute

Formerly a small fishing village before hippies arrived, Calangute gradually became a popular holiday destination not only among them, but also among travelers from all over the world. The tourist season in Goa lasts from November to February, when the rainy season stops, and the air temperature +31 degrees is comfortable for residents from any part of the world.





The construction of the coast by hotels, as well as the improvement of the world-famous Calangute beach, has led to the fact that today it is the most visited tourist state of India. Hotel Ticlo Resort 2 * is located on the second line from the beach, but it is well known to those who prefer a relaxing family holiday away from beach parties. Hotel rooms

The hotel is a separate 4-story building with a crescent moon enveloping the pools located in the courtyard.

  • stocked with a refreshing and alcoholic drinks paid minibar;
  • shower, toilet, washbasin and hairdryer;

Rooms are cleaned upon delivery of the room key at the reception. Infrastructure in Ticlo Resort 2 * (Goa, India) offers the most necessary services for living, since the main life on the island occurs outside the hotels.

Calangute Beach

Since the main life of vacationers takes place on the beach, everything was created there so that a person stays on it for as long as possible.

The beach in Calangute occupies a sandy strip 7 km long. Convenient sunbeds are located along the entire coast, which can be used free of charge if you order something in the bar closest to them.





There are so many restaurants and bars on the coast that it seems that tourists come here for a drink and a snack. But not only this beach is famous for the whole world. Here is the majority of shops and stalls with food, fruits and drinks from those that are found throughout Goa as a whole.

If the Ticlo Resort 2 * restaurant offers local and partially international cuisine, then in the city or on the beach you can find dishes for every taste - from Italian pizza familiar to everyone to Tibetan dishes.

For vacationers who prefer a quiet family vacation, this hotel will become an "oasis" of peace and quiet. Moreover, its location is such that visitors can choose their own entertainment - gatherings in a bar on the beach, allowing you to enjoy the sunset and dawn, as well as numerous nightclubs and discos in the city, suitable for those who love nightlife.

Rest for sports lovers

In the hotel Ticlo Resort 2 * (north Goa) from the entertainment offered evening shows are available, and in the afternoon - table tennis.

All this because on the beach of Calangute there is a large selection of active sports for both beginners and "advanced" lovers. Since the refreshing breeze that causes waves blows in this part of Goa, those who love water sports come here.





The main influx of athletes to the beach falls in the afternoon, when the wind changes its direction and allows windsurfing, water skiing and parasailing. Parachuting above a body of water is a favorite form of relaxation not only among young people, especially since it is not expensive at all.

After playing beach volleyball or sailing, it is especially good to return to the hotel, guaranteeing peace and quiet - such as Ticlo Resorts 2 * (Goa). The reviews of those who rested in it, note the special tranquility and harmony of this place, so lovers of yoga and relaxing massage will also be happy to be here.

Excursions

  • You can book a trip to the 5th largest in the world Dudhsagar Falls;



  • you can‚Äôt visit India and leave without its amazing spices - an excursion to the Sahakari plantation, where they are grown, will be an unforgettable experience;
  • lovers of exotic fruits are waiting for the plantation where they are grown;

In addition to excursions around the island, the Ticlo Resort 2 * hotel offers a trip to Mumbai, where a huge number of inexpensive stores with a wide range of not only local textiles and jewelry, but also world brands.

For lovers of mysteries and mountains, an excursion to small Tibet awaits you with a visit to the valley of flowers. No less interesting is the abandoned ancient city of Hampi. The ruins of the 14th century in all its glory show the ancient history of Goa, when trade and crafts flourished here. It is hard to imagine that a few decades ago there were continuous jungles that carefully concealed the city for centuries.

It is important to remember when traveling in India that there are too many thieves and beggars, so you should monitor your belongings and wallet.
