Livadia is a sanatorium located in the Trinity Forest, a picturesque forest park zone of Kazan. general information

"Livadia" - a sanatorium located far from industrial enterprises on the banks of the river. Kazanka. It was opened in 1939. The name of the health resort is translated from Greek as "paradise". The institution has the status of medical and preventive.

Therapy

Sanatorium "Livadia" (Kazan) aims to use natural health factors when conducting preventive and therapeutic measures. Among the most effective therapeutic agents, local mineral water is isolated. This is a highly concentrated sodium chloride brine, which has a positive effect on people's health, especially with diseases of the musculoskeletal system, nervous and cardiovascular systems, gynecological and skin diseases, as well as failures in the metabolic process. The effectiveness of the remedy has been proven by scientists.



In addition, the sanatorium "Livadia" (photos are presented in the article) has a drinking mineral pump room with sulfate-magnesium-calcium water, which helps to get rid of stomach problems and ailments of the hepatobiliary system. Silt mud (sapropel) is delivered to the health resort from the Blue Lake. It is able to eliminate inflammation and pain.

Specialists of the institution pay no less attention to a comprehensive preventive examination of their patients. So, on the most modern equipment, ultrasound scans of internal organs, ECGs, etc. are performed. The health resort staff is highly professional. In combination with the latest diagnostic equipment, this allows you to tactically competently build the treatment process and prescribe an effective course of preventive therapy. Individual appointment of procedures ensures maximum results.
