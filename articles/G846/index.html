How to make an organza bow

Sometimes you need to urgently decorate a gift, but there is no bow at hand. What to do? You can quickly make a bow from organza. How to make it? Read about it below.





Simple bow

You can make such an article from a beautiful cut of lace or from organza. The bow is done in just 10 minutes. To do this, take a piece of fabric 10 x 10 cm. Then, pull it in the middle with a thread. To make the bow look more interesting, you can trim the edge with curly scissors. And so that the organza does not crumble and does not go, it is necessary to scorch the product with a lighter. The bow is ready, it remains to decorate it. To do this, beads, rhinestones or some small brooch should be glued to the center. Such a bow can be used not only for decorating a gift, but also for decorating clothes or shoes. And if necessary, this craft can be glued to a hairpin or hairpin.





Bow flower

Today it has become fashionable to do various crafts from organza. The fabric is fertile, easy to cut, and sticks without any problems. It is easy to make a bow from such material. Many small petals should be cut from organza. In shape, they should resemble a drop. After all the blanks are made, their edge must be scorched. The lower sharp part must be twisted into a weak tube and locked in this position. Similar actions are carried out with all the petals. Now you need to make the base. It should be cut out of cardboard and have the shape of a circle. When all the blanks are done, you can begin to manufacture a bow from organza. Glue 8 petals to the cardboard circle at an equal distance from each other. You need to do this with a glue gun. After placing on the circle the second row. Petals in it should already be less. Thus, row by row, you need to give the bow-flower volume. You can completely glue the center of the product, as well as glue the center of the beads or beads into the center.

Kanzashi Bow

With this product, you can decorate not only a box with a gift, but also a daughter's hairstyle on some matinee. A bow from organza with your own hands is done quite simply. It is necessary to make a base from a thick tape. Collect 4 drops from the workpiece and dress them with thread. The edges of the tape should be cut short. The second tier of the bow will consist of a thin ribbon. We collect it in drops and gently glue it to the base. The more you make such curls, the more beautiful the bow will be. The third tier will consist of the same tape as the first. It is necessary to roll a thick strip of organza with a tourniquet, fix it in this position with a lighter. We attach the third row of tapes by analogy with the second. An artificial flower, a beautiful brooch or any composition of beads or beads can crown the bow. In order for the bow to have its original appearance and not fall apart, it should be glued to a dense cardboard base. Thanks to this "bottom" the product will be easy to attach to any surface.
