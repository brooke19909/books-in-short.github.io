Half Moon 3 * Rimini and Ravenna (Rivazurra, Italy): reviews, photos

Going to relax on the Mediterranean coast, tourists often pay attention to the modern prestigious hotel complex Half Moon 3 * (Italy, Rimini and Ravenna, Rivadzurra). This compact establishment with a wonderful terrace and family management provides a budget vacation.

Families, young people and large friendly companies stay in his apartments. The dynamic area of ​​Rivadzurra is famous for its vibrant nightlife, which boils in a variety of entertainment venues, and a magnificent beach holiday.

Hotel location

The hotel complex Half Moon 3 *, as well as its two famous brothers, John and Montecarlo, is located in the center of a beautiful area of ​​the resort town of Rimini called Rivadzurra. Miramare Airport is a 5-minute taxi ride away. The hotel is surrounded by bars and restaurants. The train station is a 15-minute walk away.





6 kilometers from the hotel complex is the resort of Riccione. Most of the attractions of Rivadzurra are located within a 25-minute walk from the Hotel Half Moon 3 * (Rimini). It is adjacent to the outstanding beach holiday destinations of Rimini. Nearby is the amusement park Fiabilandia.

Hotel Description

The hotel is equipped with free parking, a solarium terrace, bike rental, and an elevator. Reception is open around the clock. Luggage storage space and a safe are provided. The hotel provides hotel transfer. It is equipped with a TV lounge, air conditioning.

Wi-Fi access is available in public areas. Family travelers are offered babysitting services. In the evening, live music is played at the hotel. The establishment has a lounge bar. Hotel Half Moon 3 * Rimini is located in the center of the spa town, in a building adjacent to it there is a disco.

Number of rooms

Hotel Half Moon 3 * (Rimini and Ravenna) offers guests to settle in 36 rooms of type Standart. Small apartments for one to three people are equipped with air conditioning, heating system, balcony, paid safe. The room has Internet access, satellite TV, kettle and a set for self-preparation of hot drinks.





In the small bathroom, guests use a shower with a curtain, a set of bath accessories, a hairdryer, a wash basin and a toilet. To clean up the apartment, a maid comes daily. Linen is changed on request.

Food in the restaurant

The cost of the tour includes two meals a day. Continental breakfasts are served in the restaurant, which includes hot drinks, juices, croissants, assorted meats and cheese. For dinner, there are three dishes to choose from, a vegetable buffet and fruit. In addition, the Half Moon 3 * Hotel (Rimini) has a 24-hour bar. In the restaurant, each room has its own table.





Beach vacation

The sandy beach is separated from the hotel by a road, 150 meters to go to it. On the well-groomed territory, sun loungers with umbrellas are built under a ruler. Beach accessories are paid by tourists on site. Here, on a schedule, beach animation takes place - exercise and aerobics.

Entertainment at the hotel

In the hotel Half Moon 3 * (Rimini) there is practically no entertainment. Guests are invited to take advantage of the TV lounge and fitness room, take walks and bicycles, go on excursions, the beach or in Rimini's entertainment venues. Tourists come here, having previously bought an excursion package, or find entertainment on their own.

Travelers inspect the palace complexes, take their photos, buy goods in boutiques and dine in restaurants, strolling along Cavour Square. Get acquainted with the history of the resort town, visiting the Arc de Triomphe of Augustus, the expositions of the Municipal Museum, the fortress and temple complex of Malatesta, the ancient Roman amphitheater, striking with the grandeur and grandeur of the ancient building.





Tourists with children have fun in the water park "Aquafan". Youth companies go for fun at the incendiary disco Walky Cup or in Piazzetta, where they spend nights of unbridled fun. In general, vacationers do not stay in the hotel Half Moon 3 * (Rimini). Photos of wonderful landscapes, cultural and historical monuments and amazing architectural objects in the parks "Italy in Miniature" and "San Marino", the historic quarters of Rimini with miniature streets are amazingly beautiful.

In the historical part of the town of Rimini, there is a magnificent little pizzeria where pastries are taken away. In this restaurant with a white bench at the exit, on Castelfidardo Street, real local pizza is sold at symbolic prices. Its amazing aroma and taste cannot be compared to the pizza that countless coastal cafes offer.

Family-run hotel reviews

Most tourists believe that the institution fully justifies its three stars. The staff and the owner of the hotel Giuseppe respond to the requests of the guests, are sympathetic to the problems that have arisen, and try to eliminate them. A guide to breaking the language barrier communicates with guests through the online translator Google.





The rooms, although small, are quite suitable for sleeping, restoring strength and putting oneself in order. They are quite comfortable and clean. Continental breakfasts are not plentiful, but no one is left hungry. For a meal, they cut real ham and bake delicate croissants. Dinner consists of soup, pasta, portioned meat or fish, fresh and stewed vegetables, fruits.

Tourists highlight two shortcomings at the hotel Half Moon 3 * (Italy). Reviews indicate noise in the evenings due to discos and cramped rooms without a refrigerator. For the rest, vacationers who leave positive comments about the hotel advise disappointed tourists not to make excessive demands on the establishment and comply with the rules of hospitality. A smile, friendly communication and symbolic tips guarantee a good location of the owner and staff, comfortable stay at the hotel.
