The Philips slow cooker can cook, stew, bake!

Slow cookers appeared relatively recently, but have already managed to gain popularity. Such a device will take its rightful place in your kitchen, as it replaces a stove, oven and even some other electrical appliances. There are various models of multicookers that differ in their characteristics and cost. Today I will tell you about what a Philips multicooker is, the manufacturer of which is one of the leaders in our market.

Philips HD 2139

Currently, the company produces 7 models of multicookers. All of them work on the same principle, which allows you to steam, boil, stew and bake. Each model has several programs, so you do not have to follow the cooking process, a smart technique will do everything by itself. Philips multicooker looks very stylish and modern. It will fit perfectly into any kitchen interior, will not take up much space. Maintenance is simple - the bowl does not burn and is easy to clean. Using the device is simple and clear: there is a menu with a small display on the case. But before you begin, you should definitely study the instructions, which contain a lot of useful information.

Philips HD 3027

The Philips slow cooker, depending on the model, may differ in the volume of the cooking bowl. The largest is designed for 5 liters, which is the best option for a family of 3 or more people. If we talk about the power of the device, then here the manufacturer offers various options. Undoubtedly, that multicooker, whose power is higher, will cook faster.

What can be cooked in such a device? Virtually everything. The company made sure to make its products as versatile as possible. The Philips slow cooker (reviews are confirmation of this) knows how to cook delicious cereals and soups, prepare meat dishes, homemade cakes and much more. Compared with a conventional stove, it does it many times faster, with almost no human involvement. Most of the models are equipped with a delayed start, the function of maintaining the temperature. All this facilitates the cooking process.

Philips HD 3038

The Philips slow cooker is an excellent choice for those who save their time, but also want to eat healthy and wholesome food. Such an appliance will make it possible to prepare delicious dishes for children, as well as for those who monitor their nutrition in order to lose weight. When exploring a multicooker, it is important to study all its characteristics, familiarize yourself with the rules of use, which will increase the life of the device. Well, there are already a myriad of recipes for a multicooker, so the choice of dishes for the family will not be difficult.
