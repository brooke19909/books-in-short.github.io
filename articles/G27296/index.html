Mobile technology. Which is better - iPad or Galaxy Tab?

Superiority is manifested in competition. Every day, every hour, every second in different markets there is a struggle between manufacturers for a leading position. In particular, there is a constant rivalry between the two companies in the mobile device market: Apple and Samsung. Today we will find out which is better - iPad or tablet.



Full comparison

We think that it will be best to divide the entire comparison process into several points in order to visually see the difference, as well as the advantages of a particular model. For comparison, the following models are taken : iPad 2 and Galaxy Tab 10.1.

1. Operating system

On the Galaxy Tab is Android 3.1, which is simply full of various utilities and settings, such as Google Maps, YouTube, Google Mail and so on. There is much more functionality than in iOS. An Android device can be adjusted in detail to your own use. But Android has its own trouble - sometimes it slows down and, of course, this system quickly drains the battery. IOS is much simpler and more understandable. It is easy to manage and does not require long-term setup. But basically all the content downloaded to such a system has a paid form.





2. "Iron"

Here we will talk about power, determining which is better - iPad or Galaxy Tab. Apple iPad 2 operates on a dual-core processor with a frequency of 900 Mhz and a graphics platform PoweVR, 512 Mb of RAM, allowing it to easily perform all kinds of actions without slowing down or freezing. Samsung has acquired a dual-core Nvidia Tegra processor with a frequency of 1 Ghz and a graphics platform ULP GPU, 1 GB of RAM, which gives the device much more power during labor-intensive processes. Drawing a conclusion about which tablets are better in terms of performance, we can call Galaxy Tab without a twinge of conscience.

3. Display

An important part of the device, especially when searching for the answer to the question of which is better - iPad or Galaxy Tab tablet. Samsung has a 10.1-inch TFT screen with a resolution of 800x1280 dpi. This is a very high quality and full display. The display on the iPad 2 also did not disappoint - a 9.7-inch TFT screen with a nice backlight with a resolution of 768x1024 dpi. The screen is very saturated and bright, working on such a display is quite comfortable and pleasant. When comparing these devices and finding the answer to the question of which is better - iPad or Galaxy tablet - in this section, it seems, there will be a draw. Because both displays look dignified and unusual.



4. Camera

Galaxy Tab is equipped with a wonderful 3.2 megapixel camera with the ability to record video with a resolution of 720 p. This is a very convenient camera with LED flash and autofocus. In addition, there is a 2 MP front camera that can be used for video calls. IPad 2 has at its disposal a much more modest camera - 0.7 megapixels. But it also has the ability to record video with a resolution of 720 p. Obviously, the choice of a tablet computer for a person who pays great attention to the camera will fall on the Galaxy Tab.

Summary

Yes, from the above it is clear that in almost all aspects the Samsung tablet looks more dignified, especially in terms of hardware. Despite this, the brainchild of Apple is a worthy choice among tablets, as it carries simplicity and ease of control. Both copies are excellent options that guarantee the user maximum return and efficiency.
