Entertainment in Phuket, which does not boil down to visiting local beaches

This island in the Andaman Sea is the main tourist center. Phuket, aimed at guests of all ages, is famous not only for its wonderful nature and curious sights. The resort, designed for a relaxed and active holiday, is also the territory of a wide variety of entertainment that will appeal to adults and children. No one will call the life of tourists boring, because an exotic island takes care of its guests and does everything to diversify their leisure.

Of course, being in a picturesque resort, I want to swim in the warm sea, sunbathe on a snow-white beach, get acquainted with unique architectural monuments, but the soul of a tourist requires fun and unforgettable impressions. Entertainment in Phuket will appeal to everyone, and especially extreme lovers will like it. Bungee jumping is an attraction for adults who want to tickle their nerves. The so-called bungee attracts the local population and vacationers, who do not need to provide any information to jump into the abyss.





The most famous place where a 50-meter springboard is installed above a beautiful lake is located near the Kathu (Kathu) area and is called Jungle Bungy Jump. Even a 13-year-old can jump down.

However, extreme entertainment in Phuket is unsafe and can lead to the death of vacationers. The second most popular venue was World Bungy Patong, where Thailand's highest springboard was located. There were quite serious restrictions on age and weight at this attraction, however, after a tragedy with a tourist who collapsed to the ground from a great height, in 2015 the tower for jumping in Patong was demolished.
