Developing the analytical mindset, do not forget about the innate features of a person

A person can live his whole life happily, fruitfully doing his favorite work, and never think about what kind of mindset God has awarded him. Some people recognize it as purely physical and consider it a waste of time to solve such problems. But there are people who are extremely dissatisfied with their choice, or are in a state of deciding what kind of activity to devote, if not their whole life, then the next few years.

Even if your choice fell on a profession that requires large physical expenses, then the search for its spiritual or intellectual component turns even boring and monotonous work into a meaningful pastime.

The analytical mindset competes with the humanitarian. Vivid "analysts" continuously analyze the surrounding reality, perceiving the processes around them from a point of view close to mathematical. They are interested in the relationships and relationships between various information elements. Experts are unanimous in the opinion that the candidates for the positions of managers, sales specialists, especially in terms of their development, IT specialists, logisticians, economists, marketers, analysts, consultants, must have an analytical mindset.

But indifference to humanitarian abilities and their development can play a cruel joke with representatives of professions of a pronounced technical and mathematical orientation. The humanitarian component of the mindset is innate for a person. A person is born with the predominant influence of the right hemisphere, which is responsible for the figurative perception of reality and imagination. The analytical mindset develops in the process of preschool education, as well as in the initial stage of schooling.

What does the analytical mindset mean without sufficiently developed imaginative thinking? If we use an analogue of building a house for a rough comparison of the educational process, then humanitarian abilities can be compared with a powerful foundation. The development of an analytical mind without a sufficient humanitarian base can lead to limited characteristics of the entire structure.
