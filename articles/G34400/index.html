"Victoria" (recreation center): description, contacts and reviews of tourists

The Black Sea coast is a storehouse of amazing and unique in nature resorts. If you plan to go on vacation to one of them, there is no better option than Victoria - a recreation center near Inal Bay. Why you should visit this place, read below.

Short description





The recreation center "Victoria" (Inal Bay) offers its guests a wonderful opportunity to relax in the lap of nature. Located on the territory of a mixed forest and next to the gentle sea, it will always attract groups of tourists. Here you can truly relax and take a break from the bustle of the city. This will be facilitated by picturesque nature, healthy and tasty home-made food, cozy wooden houses, spring spring water, clean forest and mountain air, historical monuments and a warm sea with many water attractions.

This place will spend an unforgettable vacation as single travelers, as well as families with children or the elderly. There is a children's and sports grounds, a table for tennis, a cafe and a dining room. Everyone will find entertainment at their camp site to their liking, after which they will not want to look for other places to spend their vacations.

Location

The recreation center "Victoria" (Inal) is located in the Tuapse district of the Krasnodar Territory, between the cities of Sochi and Gelendzhik, but at a fairly large distance from the railway and highways. This is a unique seven-hectare place where you can truly feel alone with nature and strengthen your immunity thanks to a healing mountain-climate, sources of blue clay, clean clear sea and spring water. For a city dweller, rest here will be truly paradise.

Nearby is the so-called climbing wall, where everyone can try to do mountain climbing. In addition, around the base there were Adyghe burials, which will be interesting for lovers of historical places, and at an altitude of seven hundred meters there is an observation deck with stunning views of the Greater Caucasus Range.





Walking or car trips to the most beautiful waterfalls, water park, dolmens and dolphinarium will not take much time. Having reached the promenade, the vacationer will see many cafes, restaurants, souvenir and grocery stores.

Beach

"Victoria" is a recreation center, which is located just three hundred meters from a fully equipped small pebble beach. There are changing cabins, umbrellas, a medical center and a booth for rescuers, as well as a place where you can rent sports water equipment. The cleanliness of the beach is monitored daily.

Fans of water entertainment will be pleased: here you can ride a jet ski, catamaran, yacht or boat, parachute jump, go diving.
