The most profitable deposit in the bank. The most profitable deposits in banks

Deposits is one of the most popular services offered by modern financial institutions. Deposits are the easiest investment format. All that is required of a person is to choose a suitable financial partner in the person of a large bank, take his savings and put into his account. At the end of the cooperation period, the amount of capital will be increased. The only question that needs to be solved on the eve of making a deposit is to determine what the most profitable deposit to the bank is at the moment.

General issues





Focusing on the term of capital placement on the bank account, you can distinguish term deposits and demand deposits. The latter format of programs provides the ability to withdraw funds from the account at any convenient time, but this advantage is to the detriment of the size of the interest rate. It, as a rule, will be minimal among all offers available on the market. We will indent and focus on the fact that the most profitable contribution to the bank involves the presence of large capital and a long term partnership with a financial institution. Small deposits for short periods cannot provide such a percentage of profitability. You should always take into account the rule: the more extensive access a bank customer has to his savings, the less he will have a level of profitability. The most profitable are deposits for a period of one year or more.

The most profitable deposit formats

The most profitable deposits in banks can be both savings and savings. Savings deposits cannot be replenished, and the initially assigned interest will be charged on the initial amount of capital. The second format of the program allows replenishment of the deposit, while the percentage will be calculated on the amount that will be on the account at a certain point in time.





The maximum increase in funds on the deposit guarantees a compound interest, also known as capitalization. Its essence lies in the fact that each month the base amount deposited on a deposit is recalculated taking into account interest, and in the subsequent period they are accrued not only on the size of the main deposit, but also on pre-accrued interest. The process is repeated constantly until the term of the deposit expires.

Estimated, indexed and OMS contributions

For some categories of customers, profitable deposits in St. Petersburg banks and other cities are settlement. The format of partnership with the bank provides for the possibility of topping up an account. The client receives a debit card. A prerequisite is the presence of the amount agreed upon when signing the partnership agreement on the account. Another interesting deposit option is a structured product. It can bring profitability several orders of magnitude higher than standard programs. This category of investment is considered to be highly risky, but with a successful combination of factors, the yield on the deposit can reach up to 20% in a few months. Another interesting and unconventional option to increase your funds is OMS (or depersonalized metal accounts). They can be very highly profitable, and in some situations the most profitable. Banks do not pay interest on such deposits, but a jump in the price of a precious metal in the international market during the deposit can provide a yield of up to 100% and higher. Interest rate - maximum attention

Profitable deposits with banks in Moscow and other cities are not those for which the maximum interest rate is offered. It is important to know that the Central Bank of the Russian Federation tightly controls this indicator, maintaining it at a more or less stable level. If a financial institution offers a rate several orders of magnitude higher than the average on the financial market, this is a signal that the financial institution has problems with liquidity. The interim administration is introduced into those institutions that try to attract investors with atypically advantageous offers.
