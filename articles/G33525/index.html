Quadrocopter Walkera QR W100S with HD camera (reviews)

Walkera is a renowned Hong Kong drone manufacturer. The brand has existed since 1994, therefore it is a true long-liver in this market. Walkera quadcopters are in great demand and have gained worldwide popularity.

general information

The great success of Walkera is associated with excellent value for money. The products of a Hong Kong manufacturer are characterized by modern functionality, which can be compared with the best world analogues.

The cost of quadrocopters Walkera is much lower than that of competitors, so the products can be purchased by people with a limited budget. The company offers a wide range of products, so customers can choose a quadrocopter that will best meet their objectives and personal preferences.

Features and Benefits

Quadrocopter Walkera is ideal for shooting from a height and long flights. The product can be used not only by professionals, but also by amateurs. Models are famous for ease of operation and ease of operation. Experts say that Walkera quadrocopters have excellent functional performance. Among the main advantages of this device are:

  • the presence of a GPS module;

The GPS module allows the quadrocopter to automatically return to the starting point, hover in the air, plot a route at given points, and much more. The quadcopter is made of high-strength and shock-resistant materials that provide the product with maximum protection against any mechanical damage. Quadrocopter Walkera QR X350 Premium belongs to the category of elite devices, so its cost is very high. The device has many functions, an excellent camera, a special modular system and automatic control.
