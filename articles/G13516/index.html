Hotel Barbara Tourist Apartments (Apts) (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): description, photos, reviews

Cyprus attracts fans of exotic holidays to its territory, who prefer luxurious living conditions and excellent service. All this is also available in a hotel called Barbara Tourist Apartments (Apts), which is located on this island. Every year different agencies sell a huge number of tickets to this place, and travelers resting on them are satisfied with the time spent.

general information

Barbara Tourist Apartments (Apts) is a three star hotel rated in accordance with international standards. It was erected in 1993 and since then continues to host travelers from different parts of the world on its territory. On its square several times in the history of its existence, large-scale repairs were carried out, during which technical equipment was updated, new opportunities for tourists were added. They are also aimed at maintaining an attractive general appearance of the hotel building.

Pets are strictly prohibited in his rooms and there are no facilities for disabled guests. In all other respects, the place is perfect for both youth and family vacations, as guests often mention in their reviews about it.



Location

The hotel is located in picturesque Cyprus, in the large resort town of Ayia Napa. There are a huge number of attractions, beautiful places and entertainment venues. Among them, nightclubs and restaurants occupy a significant niche, which travelers often mention in their reviews. They talk about the fact that they visit them quite often, and also share some features of the establishments.

The nearest beach is a 20-minute walk from Barbara Tourist Apartments (Apts), and three kilometers from this place is a large water park, which guests prefer to visit with their entire families - both adults and children have fun in it.

The airport is located in the large city of Larnaca, which is 60 kilometers from the hotel. In this regard, all vouchers necessarily include the payment of a transfer from the point of arrival to the place of rest.

Rooms

The room stock of Barbara Tourist Apartments Apts (Ayia Napa) is small - it includes only 39 apartments. Each of them offers quite modern and very comfortable living conditions, which tourists positively evaluate. All offered apartments are presented in one category. They have modern air conditioning, which is regulated by a central control point. According to the guests, the temperature regime is at an optimal level, which favorably affects the rest of the guests.

All rooms have a private bathroom, which has a standard set of plumbing, combined with a toilet. Cold and hot water is served around the clock. As for the furniture, the bathroom has a cupboard, in which a set of bath accessories is presented, consisting of a bathrobe, towel, gel and soap - this set is individual for every tourist.



Room features

All apartments have a standard furniture set, which includes a large double bed, designed for two adult guests. It has an orthopedic mattress complete with hypoallergenic linen, which is replaced once every four days. In some rooms, this bed is replaced with two single beds.

Near the bed there are two cupboards, and for storage of personal belongings of travelers there is a wardrobe, divided into several departments - it is made of light wood.

Near the window, which overlooks the urban surroundings, there is a sitting corner, represented by a combination of a sofa and a small round table. The guests talk about the fact that here you can drink a morning cup of coffee, prepared using an electrical substation, which is also installed in the room.

As for the technical equipment of the apartments, the apartments feature a plasma TV, which broadcasts two Russian-language channels. There is also a minibar and a safe - their use is paid.



Conditions for children

Travelers who prefer to relax with their children often speak well of the conditions that are provided for this category of guests at the Barbara Tourist Apartments (Cyprus) hotel. Among them, most often on the positive side, the possibility of free installation of a baby cot in the apartment, which is designed for guests aged 4 to 12 years, is considered. In catering establishments, they can accommodate in special rather comfortable chairs, as well as choose interesting dishes from the additional menu.

If desired, parents can send their children to a small pool, which is equipped in an aquazone on the outdoor terrace.

Nutrition

Tourists who live in Barbara Tourist Apartments (Cyprus / Ayia Napa) can afford to eat in a small restaurant, arranged for them in the main building. A rich buffet is served here daily for them, where original Mediterranean dishes are served. A huge number of traveler reviews addressed to the taste of the cooked food.

There is a small bar near the pool, which offers a huge selection of snacks and cocktails that attract the attention of true gourmets.

Services

Barbara Tourist Apartments (Apts) offers a range of facilities and services. Many of them are paid and are available upon request.

Very often, tourists rent cars and go on them on a short trip around the neighborhood. They also say in their reviews that a free parking space is provided for a rented car in a guarded parking lot located on site.

In this place for rest there is a laundry, where you can rent your clothes for washing. Throughout the territory there is access to wireless Internet, which can be used for a fee - the cost is about 350 rubles (5 euros) per day.



Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle in their reviews of Barbara Tourist Apartments Apts (Ayia Napa) very often note the presence of a large number of places for sports. Visiting each of them is paid.

Guests often drop in a table tennis club and billiards room. On a daily basis, you can rent a bicycle, which is used to travel around the resort region. Many tourists prefer to go on it to the sea.



Beach

The coastline is a twenty-minute walk from Barbara Tourist Apartments (Apts). Reviews often say that this section of the coast is clean. It is well-maintained for relaxation. The hotel does not provide a vehicle for transporting tourists to the sea - this is often viewed from the negative side by travelers in their reviews. Beach towels are also not provided. Umbrellas and sun loungers are available for rent on the public beach.

Prices

The pricing policy of the hotel is quite high, but this does not reduce the flow of guests. Judging by the reviews of Barbara Tourist Apartments (Cyprus), the average cost of a tour to this place is about 170,000 rubles for two guests. This price includes weekly room rental, as well as transportation costs.



Reviews

In the reviews of tourists it is often said that the hotel provides excellent service. Positive feedback can be found on the interior. Guests say that it is made in a noble combination of beige and light green colors. In such an atmosphere, you can pretty well rest and relax.

A huge number of guests respond positively that there are a considerable number of places for entertainment near the hotel. Some of them can be visited with the whole family, for example, a water park, which is located three kilometers from Barbara Tourist Apartments.

As for the service, it is provided at a fairly high level. The hotel has professional staff who are attentive to each guest. Most often this relates to attitudes towards children - it is special here, for the kids all the best conditions are created.

The restaurant serves excellent Mediterranean cuisine. According to tourists, staying here, you should certainly try seafood. The restaurant has a team of professional chefs who, with their abilities, can satisfy the needs of even the most demanding gourmets.

Very often, travelers are satisfied with the holidays spent here. They recommend this place to rest to their relatives and friends, showing bright photos and souvenirs that they brought from this wonderful country.
