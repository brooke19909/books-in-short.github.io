Hotel Yadis Djerba Golf Thalasso & SPA 5 * (Djerba, Tunisia): description, reviews

Do you prefer a measured beach holiday on the shores of the warm sea? Ever dreamed of visiting Tunisia? Prefer to stay in comfortable hotels with excellent service? Then be sure to pay attention to the five-star hotel Yadis Djerba Golf Thalasso & Spa, which is located on Djerba.





Description

The hotel in question is located on the seafront, half an hour from Djerba-Zarzis International Airport. You can drive to Houmt Souk in a quarter of an hour. The hotel is located in the Midoun tourist area, surrounded by golf courses.

The Yadis Djerba was built in 1992. In 2012, extensive repairs were carried out here. In total, the hotel has 330 rooms, located in six buildings, equipped with elevators for the convenience of guests. All apartments here are decorated in a combination of modern and traditional styles. The room fund consists of double rooms with a view of the hotel or the sea (it is possible to establish an extra bed) and luxurious suites. The apartments have the necessary furniture, TV (there are Russian-language channels), minibar, air conditioning, balcony or terrace, bathroom, safe and much more. Free Wi-Fi is always available throughout the hotel, including rooms and the beach.

Yadis Djerba offers guests beauty salons, a spa and thalassotherapy center, sports fields, golf courses, several indoor and outdoor pools with sun terraces, a tour desk, a 24-hour reception, luggage storage, laundry and dry cleaning, equipped with the latest technology conference room, doctor‚Äôs office, shop and much more. Dining at the hotel is organized in the format of ol inclusive. Breakfast, lunch and dinner can be in the main restaurant. It operates in buffet style. The hotel also has two restaurants with the possibility of ordering dishes on the menu: Omar Khayyam (Tunisian and Moroccan dishes) and Alcazar (Mediterranean cuisine).

Those who are used to relaxing in 4- and 5-star hotels, and moreover are not ready to put up with any shortcomings and difficulties. According to experienced travelers, when choosing a place to stay, you should definitely focus not only on the description of the hotel, but also on the reviews of its former guests. After all, tourists share true information about what they had to face, based on their own experience. This will help to draw up a more complete picture of a particular hotel and to understand whether it is suitable for you or whether it is worth continuing to search. Rating

The first impression of the hotel can be obtained by looking at the ratings given to it by the guests. As for the hotel we are considering, its rating, based on the opinion of tourists, is 4.2 points out of five maximum possible. It is worth noting that this is a very worthy indicator. Travelers especially appreciated the hotel rooms and facilities. The organization of stay and leisure of children caused some complaints. In general, most of the comments of our compatriots boil down to the fact that this hotel is very good. However, many believe that he still does not reach a few five stars, still deserving a confident four stars. Nevertheless, the money spent on living here is definitely worth it.

Service

As for the service in the hotel in question, the tourists had practically no complaints. So, according to the guests, the cleaning of the rooms here is carried out every day and make it very quality. Moreover, its quality is completely unaffected by the fact that you leave a tip to the maid or not. Towels (both for the bathroom and the beach) are updated every day. Maids change bedding several times a week. So guests have no complaints about cleanliness here.

Population

Many tourists in their reviews are pleased to note the fact that, despite the fact that officially room keys are only issued to arriving guests after 14:00, this rule is often not followed. Therefore, even if you arrive at the Yadis Djerba Golf Thalasso & Spa 5 * hotel early in the morning, then most likely you will be able to check into the room almost immediately, without having to wait for the set hour.





Food

Meals at Yadis Djerba are all-inclusive. Experienced travelers note that here it is very similar to what is offered in hotels in Egypt. In general, our compatriots have no complaints about catering. According to them, it is simply impossible to stay hungry here - at any time of the day you can easily find something to eat. And all the food is tasty, varied and of excellent quality. So, guests note that the menu always had fish and various seafood (shrimp, mussels, etc.), meat dishes, a lot of side dishes, salads, appetizers, desserts. In addition, the hotel has two restaurants working in a la carte format. Here you can order any dish from the menu. Tunisian and fish restaurants are available.

As for alcoholic beverages, then, according to the guests, their choice is quite wide. However, many say that local beers and wines are somewhat unusual for our compatriots. There were no complaints about stronger drinks. In addition, you can always ask the bartender to make you a cocktail to your taste.

Sea

One of the main advantages of Yadis Djerba Golf Thalasso & Spa 5 * (Tunisia, about Djerba), of course, is the presence of a large private beach. Despite the fact that the hotel is located on the first line, the journey from the main building to the sea takes about five minutes at a leisurely pace. The beach here is clean, with a fine pleasant snow-white sand. The entrance to the sea is gentle, to the depth you need to go forty meters. Therefore, it is quite safe to relax with children here. Algae are found near the coast. However, beach workers regularly cleanse them of water. There is a bar on the beach where you can enjoy drinks and snacks.



Entertainment

In general, according to tourists, this hotel is a great place for people who rely mainly on beach vacations. Swimming in the sea and sunbathing can be combined with playing sports - playing golf, soccer, volleyball, archery, visiting the fitness center. Also, guests really liked the large outdoor pool. It has different depths, so it will be comfortable and safe here for both small children and poorly swimming people, and everyone else.
