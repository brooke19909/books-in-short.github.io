Inimitable Spain. San Sebastian - the center of luxury and silence

One of the most interesting and, therefore, visited countries in Europe for a Russian tourist is precisely Spain. San Sebastian is a city that brings together the traditions of this state, ancient luxury and resort chic. At the same time, it can be a paradise for a person who likes to just spend time on the beach, and for a traveler who likes to see the sights and architectural monuments.



This city is located near the border where France and Spain intersect. For this reason, San Sebastian ‚Äúabsorbed‚Äù the culture and customs of both powers. It is this feature that it differs from other cities of the country, which is why there are so many tourists, as well as people trying to solve the mysteries of past years that Europe is fraught with.

This city received the status of a resort during the Second World War. Then the whole Spanish aristocracy gathered here to admire the ever-quiet and calm Bay of Biscay, endless green meadows and the grandeur of ancient castles. Now this resort is also designed mainly for European tourists, so when you go there, you are unlikely to meet fellow countrymen from Russia. Countries from which people gather there for the summer: Germany, France, England and Spain itself.



San Sebastian offers tourists several beaches that, as they say, for every taste. The most popular among them is Playa de la Concha, which is located in the very center of the bay. Here the vast majority of tourists prefer to spend time, since the sea in this place is always calm. Playa de Ondaretta is a small harbor in which there are always few vacationers. There is shallow sea, white sand and lush tropical flora. This place is considered ideal for families with children. And you can catch a high wave in the Basque country on the beach of Playa de Surriola. This place is one of the most luxurious and picturesque, which can only boast of Spain.



San Sebastian, a photo of which is presented in the article, is a place where you can taste incredibly delicious cuisine. Here, culinary traditions are based on Spanish recipes, but they have a pronounced shade characteristic of the Basques. The main dish to try is pinchos. In the rest of the country, it is called tapas, which means appetizer. However, cooked in this region, it has an extraordinary and very memorable taste.

San Sebastian, as we found out, is a city that attracts tourists with its grandeur, mystery and luxury. Here you can find fun entertainment, or retire to the beach or on one of the alleys in order to enjoy the silence and whisper of nature.
