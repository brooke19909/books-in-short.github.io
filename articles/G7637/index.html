Rest in the Urals: recreation facilities, prices, offers

Russia is a huge country with a unique nature. It is not surprising that today more and more tourists choose it is a domestic holiday. One of the best ways to pass the time is to spend a vacation in the Urals near picturesque lakes. In this region there are several thousand different reservoirs. In the Chelyabinsk region alone, there are about 3000 of them recorded. Naturally, natural reservoirs that have been formed for more than a thousand years attract tourists in summer and winter. A comfortable stay in the Urals provides a lot of recreation centers built on the local territory.

The recreation center "Crystal" (Lake Turgoyak)

Lake Turgoyak is not only a famous tourist attraction, but also a centuries-old natural monument that has preserved virgin landscapes. Many resort facilities are built on it, but I would like to draw attention to one of them - Crystal.

As a rule, all recreation centers (Urals) on Lake Turgoyak provide guests with the opportunity to engage in various types of recreation. You can come here with a tent or rent a cozy house. Also, tourists are invited to take a steam bath in the pool. For lovers of sunbathing, the beach is specially equipped, which is always maintained in perfect cleanliness. Those who love hiking are allowed to walk through the mysterious forests of the Urals.

I would like to note that at the Kristall recreation center, guests of the resort will be pleased with the price range (daily payment):

  • summer house for two with all amenities can be rented for 4000 rubles .;
  • economy class housing for 8 people - for 3000 rubles;
  • rent a gazebo will cost from 300 to 2500 rubles .;
  • You can set up a tent on the territory of this recreation center of the Urals for 350 rubles.

"Crystal" also provides rental of a large bath for 1,500 rubles. per day and paid parking worth 200 rubles.



The recreation center "Rainbow" on Lake Uvildy

One of the most picturesque places in the Urals is Lake Uvildy. This largest body of water not only offers a beautiful view of clear clear water and forests, but also has about 70 islands. Holidays in the Urals in the summer in these parts will amaze even the most demanding tourist.

On the shores of the lake is a beautiful recreation center "Rainbow". On its territory, water coolness, purity and transparency of air with the smells of needles and leaves are most harmoniously combined. The guests are offered the following services: comfortable cottages, summer and coastal houses, Russian and Finnish baths, winter entertainments.

On the territory of this recreation center of the Urals, accommodation prices are as follows:

  • on weekdays, a spacious cottage for 6 people can be rented for 4700 rubles .;
  • on holidays and weekends - for 7900 rubles.

The above amounts include breakfast.

Additional services:

  • car parking - 120 rubles .;
  • entrance to the territory of the base for guests who do not reside in it - 200 rubles. in a day;
  • rent of a Russian bath - 1300 rub. at one o'clock;
  • Finnish sauna - 900 rubles. (hourly payment).




Recreation center "Cote d'Azur" (Lake Sugoyak)

Lake Sugoyak is an original and unique lake, classified as a natural monument of regional significance. The Cote d'Azur base located here is oriented towards family health-improving vacations in the Urals. On its territory, guests have one- and two-story buildings and cozy cottages. Also at leisure services are a shop, a dining room, billiards, a sauna, a barbecue area, sports equipment, a playground, and areas for games. From June to September, you can rent a boat or a catamaran, fishing and diving equipment, a water park.

Price-list:

  • visit to the base without accommodation - 250 rubles .;
  • economy class rooms (one place) - 450 rubles .;
  • luxury cottage with a common room and a capacity of about 6 people will cost 7500 rubles .;
  • Check-in at the tent camp with your own tent - 300 rubles.

Meals are paid separately according to the ordering system (according to the menu).

Clear water and sky, fishing and hunting, a healthy vacation and magnificent views of the Ural flora - all this combined the recreation center "Rusich". It offers boat rental, a steam bath, excursions to local attractions, forest walks, and of course, new, fully equipped cottages.

The recreation center "Rodnichok" (Lake Elovoe)

Satisfying any aesthetic taste, the landscape of this resort is preserved in its original form, thanks to the reverent attitude of people and remoteness from densely populated industrial cities. The recreation center "Rodnichok" is equipped with a clean, pleasant beach, a football field, a children's area, a sauna. All guests are offered forest walks, excursions. It is important to note that the recreation center does not provide rental of houses or cottages.

The price list of the recreation center "Rodnichok":

  • a sauna with a pool - 1000 rubles .;
  • visit to the nursery under the supervision of a nanny - 100 rubles. at one o'clock;
  • The cost of a daily subscription to the water park on the water is 300 rubles.
