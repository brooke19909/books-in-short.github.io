Mersoy Bellavista Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Marmaris): description, service, reviews

The popular Turkish resort of Marmaris is located in the south-west of the country, on the coast of the Aegean Sea. During the season, this southern village is visited by 300-400 thousand tourists. And of course, for the convenience of vacationers, many hotels have been built here. Russian beach lovers, for example, can rent rooms in Marmaris, at the Mersoy Bellavista Hotel 4 *.

Where is

This four-star complex is located not directly in Marmaris itself, but about 9 km from it - in the picturesque village of Icmeler. From all sides, the Mersoy Bellavista Hotel 4 * is surrounded by mountains covered with luxurious tropical vegetation. The distance from this hotel to the seashore is only 200 m. That is, its guests can literally walk to the beach in 5 minutes.





Actually, the village of Icmeler is located in the bay. Therefore, the sea here is almost always calm. This explains the fact that this village is very popular primarily among lovers of a quiet beach holiday.

Mersoy Bellavista Hotel 4 *: description and features

This complex accepts guests in two multi-storey buildings. One of them is the main one, the other is optional. All the main infrastructure elements of the hotel are located in the courtyard. The territory of this hotel is not too big. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe complex is 2750 m 2 . But at the same time, the courtyard of the hotel looks very cozy and beautiful. Southern plants are planted at the hotel, and its bars and pavilions are decorated in the original "tropical" style.





This hotel was built relatively recently - in 2008. But the last reconstruction in it was carried out back in 2012. Therefore, tourists who rented rooms here should not count on too new furniture in their rooms, decoration and household appliances.

In addition to cash, for additional services at this hotel you can pay with cards. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. If you want to rent housing in this complex can also include people with disabilities. There is one room for disabled guests.

The main feature of this hotel is that only adult guests can be accommodated here. Children are accepted at the hotel only if they are already 16 years old.
