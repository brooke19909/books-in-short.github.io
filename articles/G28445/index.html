Original bright flowers for a birthday or a wedding today do not have to be bought in a store. A great gift in the form of a chic bouquet of paper will surely surprise your friends and acquaintances. It looks so natural that you can feel the delicate fragrance of a summer morning. Let's see how to make flowers out of paper?

How to make flowers from paper?

To create a beautiful gift you will need cardboard, corrugated paper, glue, scissors, wire, satin ribbon, rhinestones for decoration and patience.

Consider the process of creating flowers using the example of a rose.





  1. Take 5 small and 7 large petals, cutting them according to a pre-prepared template.
  2. Begin to form a bud by applying small petals to the center of the stamen, slightly stretching them. Attach the petals to the stamen, squeezing each new petal tightly. Overlay the next level so that it slightly overlaps the previous sheet.
  3. To attach the petals to the stamen, you need to stretch them a little, and then wrap the floral ribbons twice around the stamen. This should be done with each layer of petals. Finally, wrap the tape two more times.




Our rose is ready. Using different templates, you can make other crafts. Collect a few different colors, such as roses and magnolias.
