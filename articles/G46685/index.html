About company

The first Like Hostel was opened back in 2013 in Izhevsk. The founder of the company is the world famous businessman and coach, Ayaz Shabutdinov. Surprisingly, the first hostel did not even have beds! By the evening, after making an advance payment to the guests, the sleeping places were collected.

At the moment, Like is one of the fastest growing and most successful companies. Like Hostel in Moscow is a collection of luxury apartments that you will not find in many hotels where the cost of rooms is many times higher.

About service and services

The employees of Like Hostel in Moscow are proud that this hotel is considered one of the best in Russia and the CIS countries. Cozy beds made up with fresh linen are prepared for each visitor.
