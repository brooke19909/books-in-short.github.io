"Seaside-Galatea", Kirillovka. Kirillovka: recreation centers. "Seaside-Galatea": photo

How we all look forward to the summer! How do we dream to plunge into the gentle sea, lie on a warm sand. To do this, it is not necessary to go to distant lands, because in Ukraine there is a wonderful resort Kirillovka. Rest here has several advantages. In particular, relatively low housing prices, well-developed infrastructure of the village, excellent transport links. Recreation facilities at this resort for every taste and budget. Let's look at one of the best, whose name is "Seaside-Galatea."





Location

Very conveniently located resort complex "Seaside-Galatea." Kirillovka is a village in the Zaporozhye region, located on a picturesque peninsula. On the right lies Kosa Fedotova and the Utlyuk estuary, on the left is the Peresyp, framing the Molochny estuary. "Seaside-Galatea" is located approximately in the middle between them. You can get by car along the Simferopol highway before turning to Akimovka (the deer‚Äôs monument serves as a guide), and then to the left of the village. From Zaporozhye, Melitopol, Kharkov, Dnepropetrovsk there are regular bus services and minibuses. In addition, during the season from many nearby cities go minibuses on request. The ticket price is fixed. From remote regions, you can take a train to one of the above cities, and then transfer to a minibus. In Melitopol, they depart directly from the railway station without a schedule, that is, as the passenger compartment is full.



Description of the complex

"Primorskaya-Galatea" (Kirillovka) represents 11 modern buildings and one cottage. They are dispersed throughout the resort. At the entrance there is a spacious parking lot and administration building. Also on the territory there is a beautiful playground, a library, a cinema, a sports equipment rental, two cafes, two canteens, an Internet club, a sports area, a pharmacy, a first-aid post. As well as several outlets where you can buy food, souvenirs, various little things and accessories for a beach holiday. The territory of the complex is surrounded by greenery. There are many tiled walkways. The highlight is the chic flower beds, beautiful fountains and the valley of the Dwarves, where cute, skillfully made figures are waiting for kids and adults.



Rooms

The buildings of the Primorskaya-Galatea complex (Kirillovka) have a different design. Building number 8 is located near the parking lot, opposite the cafe "Seaside". Number 9 - next to the cafe "Galatea"; No. 7 near the administration. Number 2, as well as small buildings A and B, are around the playground. Buildings No. 5 and No. 6 are located in the green zone of the complex, No. 4 is not far from sports grounds, and No. 3 is ten meters from tennis and billiard tables. Closest to the sea are building number 1 and a cottage. Rooms of all categories are offered, from economy class with showers on the floor (scheduled) to suites with all amenities and a balcony. The rooms of the economy category, designed for one, two or three people, have refrigerators and a bathroom with a washbasin. There is no balcony or terrace, a TV, iron and an electric kettle can be rented. Suites and junior suites are equipped with all necessary electrical equipment, have spacious hygienic rooms with a toilet, washbasin and shower.

Nutrition

In the resort complex "Seaside-Galatea" (Kirillovka) there are two dining rooms, "Seaside" and "Galatea", as well as two cafes with identical names. As a rule, the price of a room includes three meals a day according to the system practiced in many resorts - a buffet. The menu includes first and second courses of meat, fish, vegetables, dairy products. On a side dish of porridge, pasta, potatoes. Living in standard-elite, deluxe and junior suites are provided with the opportunity to eat in separate cafes, which offer premium-menu dishes. For sweet for all categories of holidaymakers every day fresh pastries. Alcoholic beverages are not provided in the dining rooms. They can be purchased either at retail outlets of the complex, or at any of the many shops and bars of Kirillovka, as well as at the market where locals sell excellent house wines.





Beach

On the very shore of the endless blue sea is located the complex "Primorskaya-Galatea" (Kirillovka). The photos provide comprehensive information about the beach, which is less than a minute to go. The sand here is clean, the entrance to the water is shallow, the sea is warm and fairly clean. The depth at the edge, as almost on the entire coast of Azov, is small, so for vacationers with children there is a real paradise. Guests of the "Primorsky-Galatea" can use the sunbeds, umbrellas, a place under a canopy. For entertainment, water slides are installed here, inflatable "bananas", "tablets", jet skis, parachutes are provided. Since the sea is shallow, the water temperature here in early June reaches +20 ... + 22, and at peak season it rises to +26 ... + 28 degrees Celsius. In shallow water, even higher, so the parents of the kids may not be afraid that their children will catch a cold. But children can get sunburned, because there is practically no rain here in the summer, not a cloud in the sky, and the air temperature in the shade reaches +35 degrees Celsius.





Leisure

So that guests do not get bored, there is a cinema on the territory of the complex, where cartoons are played for children, and adults are offered a wide selection of films of all genres. Sports lovers will find well-equipped fitness areas (you can borrow equipment at the rental point for free). There is also the opportunity to play volleyball, tennis, billiards. For boat trips, boats and catamarans are issued. If after a sultry beach you just want to relax in the cool, in the complex in the shade of the alleys there are numerous benches and arbors. A special cosiness and charm to such a rest is given by fountains ringing and pleasing with freshness, decorated with beautiful sculptures. All this gives its guests the complex "Seaside-Galatea" (Kirillovka). 2014 prices vary by month, type of room and number of reserved days. In general, it is from 210 to 818 hryvnias per day per person. For an extra bed in a room (folding bed) for children under 12 years of age, the fee is 60% of the cost, for elders - 80%. Children under 4 years old have a free rest. In addition, there is a discount system of discounts.



Child care

Kids are always interested in relaxing at the seaside resort Galatea. Photos of tourists captured how their children frolic in wonderfully equipped playgrounds. There are slides, swings, and all kinds of stairs that kids like to climb so much. But that is not all. Adults can rent scooters, bicycles, electric cars and many, many toys for their heirs. In addition, on the territory there is a whole kids club, "Winnie's Friend", where older children, under the supervision of educators, are engaged in drawing, modeling from plasticine, create applications, play, and very young people do what they like in the presence of dads and mothers. But even that is not all. There is a computer room in Primorskaya Galatea where "advanced" children play a wide variety of games.



Recreation centers in Kirillovka

Recently, Kirillovka has been developing quite actively. Recreation centers here began to be built during the Soviet Union and continue to this day. How many of them, it is not possible to calculate, since they are occupied here by every free piece of land. Among them are inexpensive with minimal service and facilities. Housing there is provided in small houses without amenities, such as in Ivushka, or in converted carriages and cabins. But there are modern complexes built no earlier than 2000. Base "Bosphorus" provides very comfortable cottages with rooms equipped with split-system, refrigerator, small kitchen, shower room with toilet.
