Using Rep

In the modern world clothes from rep are very rare. This type of fabric is very popular among craftsmen who make jewelry, clothes or accessories with their own hands.

Reps are often used in the production of name tags, various trinkets, bookmark ribbons for photo albums or for stitching presentation materials. As elements of toys or in haberdashery, rep is no less popular.

Reps are often issued in the form of long narrow and wide ribbons. Using the silk screen printing method makes it possible to use this material as an advertisement. Printing is smooth, even. Using rep for creativity

To understand how the rep ribbon is used correctly, what it is, it is best to do something yourself. For example, you can make a hair ornament or gift wrapping.





Bows from rep ribbons are very popular in the manufacture of hair clips.

The technique of adding ribbons a lot, you can master any of them yourself.

To make bows from rep ribbons with your own hands, you will need:

  • rep ribbon of any color with or without ornament, 4x20 cm - 2 segments;
  • lace piece 2x20 cm - 2 pieces;
  • elastic band for hair of a dark color;
  • a bead for the middle or any decorative elements - twigs, flowers, beads;
  • a small piece of rep tape to secure the elastic;
  • needle with thread, scissors.

Lace is applied to the rep segment and carefully sewn. The ribbons must be sewn after this so that two rings are obtained. Fold in half, the rings overlap diagonally. Having picked up the tape in the middle, carefully sew it with a needle, then gum for hair is riveted . Wrapping the gum with the remaining piece of rep, fix the ring. It turns a hairpin. In the center, you can attach decorative elements that will decorate the finished product.

Also, the bow can be rolled up with a fork. To do this, a rep ribbon is wound on it, which is then decorated at will.
