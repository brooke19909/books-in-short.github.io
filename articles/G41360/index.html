Hotels in Esto-Sadka: list, photos and reviews

Whereas for some Russians, the onset of winter is associated with depression and severe frost, then for others it is the long-awaited period of skiing. Already, most of the fans of this sport pack bags and book hotel complexes at various ski resorts. At the same time, Russian tracks are incredibly popular, which are becoming better and better every year. Naturally, first of all, athletes and amateurs think about a trip to Sochi, where the small village of Esto-Sadok is located in the mountains.

The hotels of this ski paradise are distributed among three magnificent ski resorts, which provide their guests with a service of international standards. In the mountain village you can find absolutely everything in order to spend time interestingly and profitably: tracks equipped with the latest technology, entertainment and shopping centers, sightseeing programs and cozy cafes that serve surprisingly delicious local dishes. Today we will tell you a little about this beautiful place and help you navigate in Esto-Sadka hotels.





Brief description of the mountain village

Locals from Soviet times, wanting to ski, went to Esto-Sadok. Hotels marked with five stars, and modern routes were not there then. But this did not stop athletes from enjoying their vacation in this wonderful place.

Over the past ten years, the village has grown markedly. Now this is not a remote mountain village with eight hundred inhabitants, but a real center of winter recreation for Russians and foreign guests. The resort has especially developed on the eve of the Olympics, but even now the construction of infrastructure has not stopped. Every year Esto-Sadok is becoming more beautiful, which is confirmed by tourists who have come here for many years.

It is noteworthy that some vacationers get to this resort absolutely by accident. They simply drop out of curiosity after a tour of Krasnaya Polyana to Esto-Sadok. Hotels, cafes, pistes and the general atmosphere are so amazing that many spend their next vacation here.

Location of the resort area

Most lovers of skiing believe that Esto-Sadok is located in a unique climate zone, which ensures it is so popular among tourists from all over the world. The village itself and the ski slopes are protected by the ridges of the Caucasus, so there is almost never frost and piercing winds. In turn, through the gorge, the warm Black Sea air freely penetrates here, which makes the riding conditions even more comfortable. Professionals appreciate that here you can do what you love from December to May. All this time, tracks are waiting for athletes and amateurs who only master skiing. Indeed, many first time skiing in Krasnaya Polyana.

Esto-Sadok (resort hotels offer rooms of any price category) is located almost immediately after it. Local residents themselves cannot determine where the border between the two villages lies.

Most of the tourists, having arrived in Adler, do not stay in Sochi and immediately go to the resort. Travel time from the airport takes no more than forty minutes. However, please note that hotels in Esto-Sadka must be booked in advance. Experienced travelers do this even in the summer, when you can easily find the right price and comfort room in your favorite hotel complex. We will tell about the most popular of them in the following sections of the article.





Resort areas of Esto-Sadka

Before you start choosing a hotel complex or an apart-hotel in Esto-Sadka, you need to find out what zones the resort is divided into. After all, what routes you will ride directly depends on this. Of course, all of them are well equipped, but still some tourists have their own preferences.

There are three resort areas in the village:

  • "Rosa Khutor". This place is considered the best for snowboarders and freestylers, because for them there are as many as five parks of varying complexity. All are equipped with modern lifts and equipped with dozens of tracks, divided into several levels of difficulty.
  • GTZ Gazprom. The tracks and hotels of this resort are located at an altitude of one and a half thousand meters, so it is much quieter and calmer here than below. In addition, the slopes are ideal for those who prefer cross-country skiing and leisurely walks in the woodlands. The main highlight of this resort is the unique ring cable car, it stretches for more than five thousand meters.
  • The most luxurious hotels in Esto-Sadka are in Gorki Gorod . The most comfortable ski slopes are equipped here in the evening. In addition, they are the longest.

If you want to explore all the ski slopes on your own, then do not choose hotel complexes and apart-hotels in Esto-Sadka. In this case, it is best to stay in Krasnaya Polyana and from there go to conquer the mountain peaks.

Hotel complex "Flamingo"

This hotel offers rooms at a flexible price category. Apartments can be booked, for example, for seven hundred rubles. And you can spend almost five thousand rubles, getting a magnificent suite with breakfast included in the price.

The hotel can offer guests after a day of skiing on the slopes to take a steam bath or play billiards. Beginners who do not have their own equipment will rent it at the hotel. There is also a room for storage.

The hotel complex itself is quite small, but it has everything you need for a good vacation. Most tourists recommend this hotel for middle-class travelers. Hotel "Troika"

Despite having only three stars, the Troika Hotel (Esto-Sadok) often gets the highest rating on a five-point scale in reviews. This makes it very popular and in demand among lovers of skiing.

Guests in reviews write that there are cafes and restaurants around the complex, and the stop allows you to quickly leave for any point in Sochi. The hotel boasts regular customers who prefer this place when they come to Esto-Sadok.

Guests do not find cons in this hotel complex, they consider it one of the best and suitable for family vacations.
