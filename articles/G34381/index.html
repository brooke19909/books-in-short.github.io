Owl weave

So here we go. We offer the first scheme with the description of the ‚ÄúOwl‚Äù hat.





The whole pattern is made with knitting needles using the front (LP) and wrong (FR) loops. The former are indicated by a vertical line, the latter by a horizontal one.

The silhouette of an owl itself is only 8 loops and another 4 on each side must be done with the wrong side. This is necessary so that the owl looks as convex and effective as possible.

All rows are tied with alternating front and back loops in the order indicated in the scheme. And only three rows of all have additional features. At 23, 17 and 7, it is necessary to cross the loops. It is done like this:

  • 7th row, knit 4 FROM, then 2 PIECES, remove them on a pin or additional knitting needle and leave them behind the knitting fabric.
  • Knit the next 2 drugs, and then return the stitches from the pin to the main knitting needle and knit them.
  • Now repeat everything again, with the only difference being that the loops removed on the pin must be removed in front of the canvas.
  • Do the same in the 17th and 23rd rows.

Perhaps the first time you and not everything will work out. But when you understand the principle of weaving, you can independently change the pattern. If desired, the owl can be made a little larger or more embossed by adding several loops in the right places.

Jewelry for hats

When the knitted product is ready, it remains to decorate it a little. For the eye, you can take beautiful large buttons. The nose can be embroidered with black thread, or you can outline it with a triangular button.

Well and the most important decoration is the tassel ears. We cut the yarn into segments of 10-15 cm and collect two small bundles. We add each of them in half and tie a knot at the bend. It should make small brushes. Now we sew the ears to the corners of the cap.

Hat "Owl" without a pattern

But what about those for whom the Owl cap design seems too complicated? Often, beginning craftswomen, only having learned to knit the wrong and front loops, already want to create some kind of masterpiece for themselves. But skills, unfortunately, are still not enough. But what if there is no diagram describing the Owl pattern? Cap knitting does not work?





Do not despair! If you have a little lack of skill, you can simply connect the rectangle with an elastic band along the long side. Now fold and sew the short sides of the rectangle and the top. You got a hat-square, with an elastic band and with small protruding ears. Now it remains just to decorate it:

  • From dense felt, cut out the nose and large round eyes.
  • Sew them on the front of the product.
  • From the remnants of yarn of a contrasting (or matching) color, we cut the ears-brushes and fix them at the corners of the product.

That's all, the Owl hat is ready without using the scheme!
